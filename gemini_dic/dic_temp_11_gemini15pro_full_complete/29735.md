Catmull-Rom:/ˈkætmʌl ˈrɒm/ | adj. | 卡特穆尔-罗姆 | A type of spline used in computer graphics for smoothly interpolating between points. It's named after Edwin Catmull and Raphael Rom. |  The Catmull-Rom spline created a smooth curve through the data points. 卡特穆尔-罗姆样条在数据点之间创建了一条平滑的曲线。 | 近义词: spline, interpolation | 反义词: linear interpolation | 词性: adjective

Catmull-Rom:/ˈkætmʌl ˈrɒm/ | n. | 卡特穆尔-罗姆样条 | A specific type of cubic Hermite spline, guaranteeing the curve passes through all control points. | The artist used a Catmull-Rom spline to create the flowing lines in the design.  这位艺术家使用卡特穆尔-罗姆样条在设计中创建流畅的线条。| 近义词: cubic Hermite spline, interpolating spline | 反义词: linear interpolation, Bézier curve | 词性: noun
