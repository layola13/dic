mudar:/muˈdar/ (Brazilian Portuguese)| v.|改变，移动，迁移|To change something, to move something or someone from one place to another.|例句：Ele vai mudar de casa no próximo mês.  He will move house next month.


例句：Ela mudou de ideia sobre o projeto. She changed her mind about the project.|近义词：alterar, modificar, transferir, deslocar|反义词：manter, conservar, fixar|verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Eu mudo de roupa todos os dias. = I change clothes every day.
Ele muda o canal de televisão com frequência. = He changes the TV channel frequently.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Estou mudando de endereço. = I am changing my address.
Eles estão mudando os móveis de lugar. = They are moving the furniture.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Já mudei de emprego três vezes. = I have changed jobs three times already.
Ela já mudou o visual várias vezes. = She has changed her look several times already.

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Tenho mudado meus hábitos alimentares. = I have been changing my eating habits.
Ele tem mudado de humor constantemente. = He has been changing his mood constantly.

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Mudei de cidade no ano passado. = I moved to a new city last year.
Ela mudou o penteado para a festa. = She changed her hairstyle for the party.

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Estava mudando de casa quando você ligou. = I was moving house when you called.
Ele estava mudando o pneu do carro. = He was changing the car tire.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Eu já tinha mudado de ideia antes da reunião. = I had already changed my mind before the meeting.
Ela já tinha mudado de casa quando a conheci. = She had already moved house when I met her.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Tinha mudado meus hábitos de estudo por meses antes da prova. = I had been changing my study habits for months before the exam.
Ele tinha mudado de comportamento por semanas. = He had been changing his behavior for weeks.

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Mudarei de casa no próximo mês. = I will move house next month.
Ela mudará de emprego em breve. = She will change jobs soon.

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Estarei mudando de escritório na próxima semana. = I will be moving offices next week.
Eles estarão mudando os planos de viagem. = They will be changing their travel plans.

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Terei mudado de país até o final do ano. = I will have moved countries by the end of the year.
Ela terá mudado de carreira até lá. = She will have changed careers by then.

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Estarei mudando meus hábitos de vida por um ano inteiro. = I will have been changing my lifestyle for a whole year.
Ele estará mudando de cidade em cidade por vários meses. = He will have been moving from city to city for several months.


PHRASE->
mudar de ideia = to change one's mind
mudar de assunto = to change the subject
mudar de canal = to change the channel
mudar de roupa = to change clothes
mudar de vida = to change one's life


注意事项: This verb is primarily used in Brazilian Portuguese.  The equivalent in European Portuguese is "alterar" or "modificar".  While "mudar" exists in European Portuguese, it primarily refers to the act of moving from one place to another.
