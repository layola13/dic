adorableness:/əˈdɔːrəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
可爱；讨人喜欢
The quality of being adorable; extreme cuteness.
The puppy's adorableness was undeniable.  这只小狗的可爱程度是不可否认的。
Her adorableness captured everyone's hearts. 她的可爱俘获了所有人的心。
charm, sweetness, cuteness, loveliness
unattractiveness, unpleasantness, hideousness
noun
