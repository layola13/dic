bribees:/braɪˈbiːz/
n.
受贿者;  行贿对象
People who receive bribes; those who are bribed.
The investigation revealed a network of bribees across multiple government agencies. 调查揭露了一个跨越多个政府机构的受贿者网络。
The bribees were promised lucrative contracts in exchange for their cooperation.  这些受贿者被许诺，如果合作就能得到利润丰厚的合同。
近义词:recipients of bribes, those bribed
反义词:bribers
词性:名词
