nonsacrificing:/ˌnɒnˈsækrɪfaɪsɪŋ/
adjective
非牺牲的，不牺牲的
Not involving or requiring sacrifice.
She adopted a nonsacrificing approach to parenting, allowing her children ample freedom. 她采取了一种非牺牲式的育儿方式，给予孩子们充分的自由。
The company implemented a nonsacrificing strategy to expand its market share without compromising its core values.  这家公司实施了一种非牺牲的战略，在不扩大市场份额的同时不损害其核心价值观。
近义词:  uncompromising, non-renouncing
反义词:  sacrificial, self-denying
词性: adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sacrificing" (present participle of "sacrifice")
