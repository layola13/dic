acquainting:/əˈkweɪntɪŋ/
verb
使熟悉；使了解
the act of making someone aware of or familiar with something.
例句：I spent the first few days acquainting myself with the new software.  我花了头几天时间熟悉新软件。

例句：The museum offers guided tours acquainting visitors with the history of the city.  博物馆提供导游服务，让游客了解城市的历史。

近义词：familiarizing, informing, introducing
反义词：hiding, concealing
词性：v. (present participle of acquaint)
