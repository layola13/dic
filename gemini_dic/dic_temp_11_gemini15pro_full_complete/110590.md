limitingly:/ˈlɪmɪtɪŋli/
adverb|限制地，限定地|in a way that limits something; to a limited extent|例句：The study was limitingly focused on a small group of participants.  这项研究的重点被限制在一小组参与者身上。|例句：Resources were limitingly available, hindering the project's progress. 资源有限，阻碍了项目的进展。|近义词：restrictively, narrowly, confiningly|反义词：broadly, extensively, widely|词性: 副词

Etymology->
From "limiting" + "-ly."  "Limiting" comes from the verb "limit," which derives from the Latin "limitare," meaning "to bound, to confine."  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending.
