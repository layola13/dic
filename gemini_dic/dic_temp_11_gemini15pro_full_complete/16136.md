bangs:/bæŋz/
noun
额前短发，刘海
An area of short hair hanging over the forehead.
Her bangs covered her eyes. 她的刘海遮住了她的眼睛。
近义词:fringe
反义词:(No direct opposite, perhaps "long hair")
词性:n.
