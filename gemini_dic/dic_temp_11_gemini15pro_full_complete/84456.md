halolike:/ˈheɪloʊlaɪk/| adj.|像光环的，光环状的|Resembling or suggestive of a halo.  Similar in appearance to a halo, a ring of light.|The moon had a halolike ring around it. 月亮周围有一个光环状的环。|近义词：aureole-like, nimbus-like, radiant, glowing|反义词:dull, dark|词性:形容词

词源: From "halo" + "-like". "Halo" comes from the Greek word "halos," meaning "disk of the sun or moon."  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
