bumptiousnesses:/ˌbʌmpʃəsnəsɪz/
noun
自负；傲慢；狂妄
The quality of being bumptious; obtrusive self-assertiveness.
His bumptiousnesses alienated many of his colleagues. 他的傲慢疏远了他的许多同事。
Synonyms: arrogance, conceit, pomposity, presumptuousness, self-importance
Antonyms: humility, modesty, unassumingness
词性: 名词


bumptious:/ˈbʌmpʃəs/
adjective
傲慢的；自负的；狂妄的
Obtrusively and often noisily self-assertive.
He was a bumptious young man who thought he knew everything. 他是一个傲慢的年轻人，自以为无所不知。
Synonyms: arrogant, conceited, pompous, presumptuous, self-important
Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming
词性: 形容词
