fodor:/ˈfoʊdər/ | n. | 福多尔（姓氏）; (尤指食物的) 特殊气味,香味 |  A surname. Also, a distinctive smell, especially of food. | The restaurant was filled with the fodor of roasting meat. 餐厅里充满了烤肉的香味。 | 近义词: aroma, scent, smell | 反义词: odor (sometimes), stench | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fodor, from Old English *fōdor, from Proto-Germanic *fōdrą (“food, fodder”), from Proto-Indo-European *pā- (“to protect, feed”).  Cognate with Old Frisian fōder (“food, fodder”), Old Saxon fōdar (“food, fodder”), Old Dutch voeder, fōder (“food, fodder”), Old High German fuotar (“food, fodder”), Old Norse fóðr (“food, fodder, lining”), Gothic 𐍆𐍉𐌳𐍂 (fōdr, “food, fodder, sheath”).  More at fodder. The sense of "smell" developed from the smell of food.
