gastroenteritic:/ˌɡæstroʊˌentəˈraɪtɪk/| adj.|胃肠炎的；患胃肠炎的|Relating to or affected by gastroenteritis, an inflammation of the stomach and intestines.|He was diagnosed with a gastroenteritic infection. 他被诊断出患有胃肠炎。|近义词：intestinal, gastric|反义词: healthy|词性:形容词

gastroenteritis:/ˌɡæstroʊˌentəˈraɪtɪs/| n.|胃肠炎|Inflammation of the stomach and intestines, typically resulting from bacterial toxins or viral infection and causing vomiting and diarrhea.|The doctor diagnosed her with gastroenteritis. 医生诊断她患有胃肠炎。|近义词：stomach flu, intestinal flu|反义词: health|词性:名词
