formulates:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪts/
verb
制定；规划；构想；确切表达
creates or devises methodically (a strategy or a proposal).
The committee formulated a plan to raise money. 委员会制定了一个筹款计划。
Synonyms: devise, conceive, frame, develop, contrive, invent, originate, coin, create, produce, prepare, compose, draw up, work out, put together, make, fashion
Antonyms: destroy, ruin
词性:v.
