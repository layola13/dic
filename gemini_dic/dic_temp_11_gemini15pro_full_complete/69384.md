feebly:/ˈfiːbli/| adv.|微弱地，无力地|in a weak and ineffective way|She smiled feebly. 她无力地笑了笑。|Synonyms: weakly, faintly, powerlessly|Antonyms: strongly, powerfully, vigorously|adverb

feebly:/ˈfiːbli/| adj.(rare, literary) |虚弱的，衰弱的|lacking physical strength, especially as a result of age or illness|例句：a feeble old man 一位虚弱的老人|近义词：weak, frail, infirm|反义词: strong, robust, vigorous|adjective
