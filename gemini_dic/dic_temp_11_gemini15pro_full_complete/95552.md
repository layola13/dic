incogitativity:/ɪnˌkɒdʒɪtəˈtɪvɪti/
n.
缺乏思考；轻率；无深思熟虑
lack of thought; thoughtlessness; lack of reflection
His incogitativity led to a series of poor decisions. 他的轻率导致了一系列糟糕的决定。
Incogitativity can be dangerous when dealing with complex issues. 在处理复杂问题时，缺乏思考可能是危险的。
近义词：thoughtlessness, rashness, imprudence, heedlessness
反义词：thoughtfulness, prudence, circumspection, deliberation
词性: 名词
