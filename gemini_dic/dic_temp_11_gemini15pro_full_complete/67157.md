expositionally: /ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 说明地，解释地 | In a way that explains something clearly and thoroughly.  | The professor expositionally described the complex theory to the students. 教授向学生们详细地解释了这个复杂的理论。 |  近义词: explanatorily, clearly, explicitly, didactically | 反义词: implicitly, vaguely, obscurely | 词性: 副词

词源: From exposition + -al + -ly. Exposition comes from Latin expositio, from exponere "to put forth, set forth, explain," from ex- "out" + ponere "to put, place."
