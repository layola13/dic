ETYMOLOGY->
From French *météorologique*, from Ancient Greek μετεωρολογικός (meteōrologikós, “treating of meteors”), from μετέωρον (metéōron, “thing high up, meteor”) + -λογικός (-logikós, “relating to a particular science or art”);  ultimately from μετέωρος (metéōros, “raised from the ground, lofty”).
