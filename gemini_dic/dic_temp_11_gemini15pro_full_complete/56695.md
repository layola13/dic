doomed:/duːmd/
adjective
注定失败的；注定毁灭的；注定要遭难的
Certain to fail, suffer, or be destroyed.

The project was doomed from the start due to lack of funding.
由于缺乏资金，该项目从一开始就注定要失败。

Their marriage was doomed to failure.
他们的婚姻注定要失败。

doomed:/duːmd/
adjective
命定的；已注定的
Fated, preordained.

He felt doomed to repeat his father's mistakes.
他感到注定要重蹈覆辙。

The doomed ship sank beneath the waves.
这艘注定要沉没的船沉入了波涛之下。


近义词:fated, condemned, cursed, ill-fated, destined
反义词:fortunate, blessed, lucky, successful
词性:adjective
