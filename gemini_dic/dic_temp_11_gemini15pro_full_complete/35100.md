ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "geclāþod," past participle of "clāþian" (to clothe), from Proto-Germanic "*klæþijan," from "*klæþiz" (cloth).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*kle-," meaning "to stick, adhere."
