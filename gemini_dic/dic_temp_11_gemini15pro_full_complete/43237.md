crackles:/ˈkrækəlz/ (US), /ˈkrækəlz/ (UK)| n.|噼啪声，爆裂声|Short, sharp sounds, like the sound of something burning or frying, or the sound of dry leaves being crushed.|The doctor listened to her chest and heard crackles in her lungs. 医生听了她的胸部，听到了她肺部的噼啪声。

The fire crackled merrily in the fireplace. 火在壁炉里欢快地噼啪作响。|Synonyms:  crepitation, rales, clicking, rattling|Antonyms: silence, quiet|Noun
