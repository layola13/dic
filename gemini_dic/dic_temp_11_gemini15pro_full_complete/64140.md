equivalent:/ɪˈkwɪvələnt/| adj. n.|相等的，等价的；等价物|Something that is equivalent is equal in value, amount, function, meaning, etc. |例句：Ten thousand Yuan is roughly equivalent to 1,500 US dollars. 一万元人民币大约相当于1500美元。|近义词：equal, identical, similar, comparable, analogous|反义词:different, dissimilar, unequal, opposite|词性:形容词/名词
equivalent:/ɪˈkwɪvələnt/|n.|等价物|Something that has the same value, purpose, job etc as something else.|例句：Is there a French equivalent of the FBI?  法国有没有相当于联邦调查局的机构？|近义词：counterpart, parallel|反义词:opposite|词性:名词
