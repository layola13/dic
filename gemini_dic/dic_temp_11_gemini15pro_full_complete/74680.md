freed-up:/friːd ˈʌp/| adj.| 可用的，空闲的，腾出的 |Made available or no longer occupied; released from a previous commitment or constraint. |例句：The freed-up resources were used to fund new projects. 腾出来的资源被用来资助新项目。

例句：With the kids at school, I have a lot of freed-up time during the day. 孩子们上学后，我白天有很多空闲时间。 |近义词：available, free, spare, liberated, unoccupied |反义词: occupied, busy, engaged, unavailable |词性:形容词

词源:  "freed" is the past participle of the verb "free," which comes from Old English "freo," meaning "free, exempt, noble."  "Up" in this context acts as an intensifier and implies a complete freeing or opening up.
