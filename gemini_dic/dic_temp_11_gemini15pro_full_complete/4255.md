airs:/ɛərz/
n.
manner; appearance; affected manner; (plural) an affected manner; haughty behavior;
姿态；样子；做作的样子；（复数）装腔作势；傲慢的态度
She has such aristocratic airs. 她摆出一副贵族的样子。
近义词: mannerisms, affectations, pretenses
反义词: naturalness, sincerity, humility
词性: 名词

airs:/ɛərz/
n.
melody; tune; song; (often plural) a tune or melody
旋律；曲调；歌曲；（常用复数）曲调或旋律
He played some familiar airs on the piano. 他在钢琴上演奏了一些熟悉的曲子。
近义词: tunes, melodies, songs
反义词: dissonance, cacophony
词性: 名词

词源:
From Old French *airs (plural of air "air, melody, tune"), from Latin aer "air, atmosphere," from Greek aēr. The sense of "affected manner" developed from the notion of "putting on airs," as if one were superior to others.
