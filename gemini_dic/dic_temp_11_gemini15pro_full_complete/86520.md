heisting:/ˈhaɪstɪŋ/| n. |抢劫，盗窃|The act of robbing a place or person, typically in a daring or dramatic way.  |例句：The heisting of the museum was planned meticulously.  博物馆的抢劫经过了精心策划。

例句：They were arrested for heisting a jewelry store. 他们因抢劫珠宝店而被捕。|近义词：robbery, theft, burglary, stealing|反义词: returning, donating |词性: noun (gerund)
