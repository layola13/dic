bandeau:/ˈbændoʊ/ (US), /ˈbændəʊ/ (UK)| n.|头巾，发带，饰带|A narrow band worn around the head, especially by women, as a decoration or to keep the hair in place.|She wore a bright pink bandeau to keep her hair off her face. 她戴了一条亮粉色的发带，不让头发遮住脸。|近义词：headband, hairband, ribbon|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun

bandeau:/ˈbændoʊ/ (US), /ˈbændəʊ/ (UK)| n.|胸带，抹胸|A piece of women's clothing that covers the chest, often part of a swimsuit or evening dress.|The model wore a sequined bandeau top and a flowing skirt.  模特穿着一件亮片抹胸上衣和一条飘逸的长裙。|近义词：tube top, strapless top|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
