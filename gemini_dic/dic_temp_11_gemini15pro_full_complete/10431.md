applaudable:/əˈplɔːdəbl/
adjective
值得称赞的；令人钦佩的
deserving praise; commendable
Her dedication to her students is truly applaudable. 她对学生的奉献精神 truly 值得称赞。
His efforts to help the homeless are applaudable. 他帮助无家可归者的努力值得称赞。
近义词:praiseworthy, commendable, laudable, admirable
反义词:disgraceful, shameful, deplorable, reprehensible
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin applaudabilis, from Latin applaudere “to applaud” + -abilis “-able”
