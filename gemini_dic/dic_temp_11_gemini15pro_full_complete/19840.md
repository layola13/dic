binbashi:/bɪnˈbɑːʃi/| n. |营长，千夫长|A rank in the former Egyptian army, equivalent to major.  It was adopted from the Turkish word "binbaşı," meaning "head of a thousand."|He was promoted to the rank of binbashi after years of service. 他服役多年后被提升为营长。|Synonym: major, commander|Antonym: private, soldier|Noun
