intercrusting:/ˌɪntərˈkrʌstɪŋ/
n.
结壳；硬皮形成；嵌入；夹层
The formation of a hard crust or layer on or between something.
The intercrusting of minerals formed a beautiful pattern on the rock.  矿物质的结壳在岩石上形成了美丽的图案。
interstratification, layering, lamination
