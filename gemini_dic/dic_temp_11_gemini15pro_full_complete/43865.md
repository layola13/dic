crescent:/ˈkrɛsənt/| n. adj.|新月，月牙，新月形的|the curved shape of the moon when it is less than half full. Also describes something shaped like this. |A crescent moon hung in the sky. 一弯新月挂在空中。  |The bakery is famous for its crescent rolls. 这家面包店以其新月形面包卷而闻名。|近义词：meniscus, lunula (for the shape), waxing/waning (for the moon phase)|反义词: gibbous (for the moon phase), full moon|词性:名词，形容词
