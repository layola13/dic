escalate: /ˈɛskəleɪt/ (US) /ˈɛskəleɪt/ (UK)
verb
逐步升级；逐步扩大；加剧
become or cause to become more intense or serious.
The conflict escalated quickly into a full-blown war. 冲突迅速升级为全面战争。
The price of oil has escalated sharply in recent months.  近几个月来，石油价格急剧上涨。
synonyms: intensify, increase, accelerate, heighten, aggravate, worsen, deteriorate
antonyms: de-escalate, decrease, lessen, reduce, diminish, alleviate, improve
词性: 动词
