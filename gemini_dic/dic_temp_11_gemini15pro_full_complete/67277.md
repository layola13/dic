exsanguine:/ɪkˈsæŋɡwɪn/
adjective
血色全无的，苍白的；无血的
drained of blood; pale.
The soldier lay exsanguine on the battlefield.  这个士兵脸色苍白地躺在战场上。

近义词: pale, pallid, ashen, bloodless, anemic
反义词: ruddy, flushed, sanguine

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin exsanguis (“bloodless”), from ex (“out”) + sanguis (“blood”).
