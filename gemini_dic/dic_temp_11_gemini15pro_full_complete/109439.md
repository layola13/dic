leotard:/ˈliːətɑːrd/| n.|紧身衣|A close-fitting one-piece garment, typically made of stretchy material, worn by dancers, gymnasts, and acrobats.|The gymnast wore a sparkly leotard for her floor routine. 体操运动员穿着闪亮的紧身衣进行自由体操表演。|Synonyms: bodysuit, unitard|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps loose clothing)|noun
