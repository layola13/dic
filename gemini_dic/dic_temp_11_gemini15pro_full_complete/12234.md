asbo:/ˈæzbəʊ/ (UK) /ˈæzboʊ/ (US)| n. |反社会行为令 (Anti-Social Behaviour Order)|An order made by a court in the UK against a person who has been shown to have engaged in anti-social behaviour. It places restrictions on their behaviour, such as banning them from certain areas or associating with certain people.  It is no longer used in this form in the UK, having been replaced by other legal orders.|He was given an ASBO for persistent vandalism. 他因持续的故意破坏行为而被判处反社会行为令。|Synonym: Criminal Behaviour Order (CBO) (its modern equivalent), restraining order|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "commendation" or "award" for good behaviour)|Noun
