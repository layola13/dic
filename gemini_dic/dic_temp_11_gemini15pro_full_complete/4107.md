aimlessly:/ˈeɪmləsli/| adv.|漫无目的地，无目标地|Without purpose or direction; in a random or haphazard manner.|He wandered aimlessly through the streets. 他在街上漫无目的地游荡。|She scrolled aimlessly through social media. 她在社交媒体上漫无目的地浏览。|近义词：randomly, purposelessly, haphazardly, without direction|反义词: purposefully, deliberately, intentionally|词性:副词
Etymology: From aim + -less + -ly.  "Aim" comes from Old French "esmer" meaning "to estimate, point at."
