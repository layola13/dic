haruspicy:/həˈrʌspɪsi/ | n. | 占卜; 肝卜 | Divination of the future by inspecting the entrails of sacrificial animals, especially the liver. |  The Romans practiced haruspicy to determine the will of the gods. 罗马人通过肝卜来确定神的旨意。 |  The haruspicy revealed that the omens were unfavorable for war. 肝卜显示战争的预兆是不利的。 |  augury, divination, prophecy |  - | noun
