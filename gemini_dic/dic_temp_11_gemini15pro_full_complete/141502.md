opisthotic:/ˌɒpɪsˈθɒtɪk/ | adj. n. | 后耳的，后耳骨 | Situated behind the ear; relating to the opisthotic bone, a bone forming part of the posterior section of the otic capsule in many vertebrates. | The opisthotic bone forms a significant portion of the skull in many fish.  后耳骨在许多鱼类的头骨中占很大一部分。| The researcher identified the opisthotic bone in the fossil. 研究人员在化石中识别出了后耳骨。| 近义词: posterior auricular,  otocranial | 反义词:  anterior auricular | 词性: adjective, noun
