dictamnus:/dɪkˈtæmnəs/| n. |白鲜，也称燃烧灌木|A genus of flowering plants in the family Rutaceae, commonly known as burning bush, dittany, or gas plant, native to warm temperate regions of Eurasia.|The dictamnus in my garden is about to bloom. 我花园里的白鲜快要开花了。|近义词：burning bush, dittany, gas plant|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *dictamnus*, from Greek *δίκταμνον* (díktamnon), referring to a plant from Mount Dikte in Crete.
