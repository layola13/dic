mentorial:/mɛnˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
指导的，导师的
Relating to or characteristic of a mentor.  Providing guidance and support like a mentor.
She offered me some mentorial advice on my career path. 她就我的职业道路向我提供了一些指导性的建议。
The professor adopted a mentorial approach with his students.  这位教授对他的学生采取了导师式的方法。
近义词:  guiding, advisory, supportive, nurturing
反义词:  unsupportive, neglectful
词性: adjective
