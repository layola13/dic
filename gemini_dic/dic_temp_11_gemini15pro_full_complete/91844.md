hyperhilariousness:/ˌhaɪpərhɪˈleəriəsnəs/

极度滑稽；极度搞笑
The state of being extremely hilarious.
His hyperhilariousness had everyone in stitches. 他的极度滑稽让每个人都笑破了肚皮。

反义词：seriousness, solemnity
词性：名词

Etymology:
