non-recyclable:/ˌnɒn riːˈsaɪkləbl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːn riːˈsaɪkləbl/ (US)| adj.|不可回收的|Not able to be recycled or processed for reuse.|Many plastic bags are non-recyclable and end up in landfills. 许多塑料袋是不可回收的，最终被填埋。|These coffee cups are non-recyclable, so please dispose of them in the general waste bin. 这些咖啡杯是不可回收的，请将其丢弃在普通垃圾桶中。|non-reusable, disposable|recyclable, reusable|adjective

Etymology->
non-: prefix meaning "not" (from Latin non)
recyclable: from recycle + -able
