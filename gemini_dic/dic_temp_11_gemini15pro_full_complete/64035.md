epitheloid:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliɔɪd/ | adj. | 上皮样的，类上皮的 | Resembling or having the characteristics of epithelium. |  Epithelioid cells are often found in granulomas.  类上皮细胞常见于肉芽肿。| Similar:epithelial, epitheliomatous | Opposite: none | adjective

epitheloid:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliɔɪd/ | n. | 类上皮细胞 | A cell that resembles an epithelial cell. | The biopsy showed the presence of epithelioid cells. 活检显示存在类上皮细胞。 | Similar:epithelial cell | Opposite: none | noun
