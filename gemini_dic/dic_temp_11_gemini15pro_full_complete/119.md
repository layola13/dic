abalones:/ˈæbəˌloʊniz/ (US), /ˈæbəˌləʊniz/ (UK)| n.|鲍鱼|Abalones are large, edible sea snails with a single, flat shell, prized for their meat.|We had fresh abalones for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了新鲜的鲍鱼。|The restaurant is famous for its grilled abalones. 这家餐厅以烤鲍鱼闻名。|近义词：sea snails, shellfish|反义词: None|词性: Noun

词源: From Spanish *abulones*, plural of *abulón*, from Rumsen *aulón*.
