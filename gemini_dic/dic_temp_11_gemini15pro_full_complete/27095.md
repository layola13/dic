calculate /ˈkælkjuleɪt/
verb
计算，估算
determine (the amount or number of something) mathematically.
Example: He calculated the cost of the project. 他计算了项目的成本。

Example: Scientists have calculated that the earth is 4.6 billion years old. 科学家已经计算出地球有46亿年的历史。

近义词: compute, determine, estimate, figure, reckon
反义词: guess, estimate (Note: estimate can be both a synonym and antonym depending on context.  "Calculate" implies precision, while "estimate" can suggest approximation.)
词性: verb

PHRASE->
calculate on:  指望；依赖
Example:  I'm calculating on your support. 我指望你的支持。

calculate for:  为…作打算；预料
Example: We didn't calculate for such bad weather. 我们没有预料到这么糟糕的天气。

calculate something at:  估算…为
Example:  They calculated the damages at $1 million. 他们估算损失为100万美元。

calculate something out:  计算出
Example: I need to calculate out the exact measurements. 我需要计算出确切的尺寸。

calculate something up:  算出…的总数
Example:  Can you calculate up the total cost? 你能算出总成本吗？
