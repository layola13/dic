nonexplorative:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔːrətɪv/ | adj. | 非探索性的，非探究性的 | Not exploratory; not involving or characterized by exploration or investigation. | The study took a nonexplorative approach, focusing on confirming existing hypotheses rather than discovering new phenomena. 这项研究采取了非探索性的方法，侧重于证实现有假设，而不是发现新现象。|  The team adopted a nonexplorative strategy, relying on proven methods.  团队采用了非探索性策略，依靠行之有效的方法。 | Near synonyms: descriptive, confirmatory, conventional | Antonyms: exploratory, investigative, pioneering | word property: adjective
