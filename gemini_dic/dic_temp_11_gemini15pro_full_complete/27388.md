camellia: /kəˈmiːliə/ | n. | 山茶花；茶花 | An evergreen shrub or small tree of the tea family, native to Asia, with glossy leaves and showy, waxy flowers. |  The camellia is the state flower of Alabama.  山茶花是阿拉巴马州的州花。

 The garden boasted a wide variety of camellias, in shades of pink, red, and white.  花园里种着各种各样的山茶花，有粉红色、红色和白色的。 | 近义词: flower, blossom | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latinized form of the surname of Georg Josef Kamel (1661–1706), a Jesuit missionary and botanist who worked in the Philippines.
