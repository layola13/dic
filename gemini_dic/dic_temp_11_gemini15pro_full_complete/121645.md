misperform:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrm/ /ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrm/ | v. | 表现不佳，执行不力 | To perform poorly or below expectations; to fail to achieve the desired or expected results. | The company misperformed last quarter due to declining sales.  该公司由于销售额下降，上季度业绩不佳。| Synonyms: underperform, fail, falter | Antonyms: succeed, excel, flourish | verb

misperformance:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrməns/ /ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrməns/ | n. | 表现不佳，不佳表现 | The act or instance of misperforming; poor or inadequate performance. |  The misperformance of the team led to their elimination from the tournament.  该团队的不佳表现导致他们被淘汰出局。| Synonyms: underperformance, failure, shortfall | Antonyms: success, achievement, triumph | noun
