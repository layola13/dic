outscored:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrd/
verb
超过…的得分，比分高于
To score more points than (an opponent or another team) in a game or competition.
The Lakers outscored the Celtics in the fourth quarter. 湖人队在第四节超过了凯尔特人队的得分。
Our team outscored the opponents by 10 points.  我们的队伍比对手多得了10分。
synonyms: beat, defeat, triumph over, conquer, surpass
antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
