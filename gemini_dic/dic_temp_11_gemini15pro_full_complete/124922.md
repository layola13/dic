mudslide:/ˈmʌdslaɪd/| n. |泥石流，山体滑坡|A sudden flow of mud down a slope, often caused by heavy rain.  A disaster caused by a large mass of mud sliding down a mountain or hill.|例句：The mudslide destroyed several homes in the valley. 泥石流摧毁了山谷中的几栋房屋。

例句：The heavy rains triggered a devastating mudslide.  暴雨引发了毁灭性的泥石流。|近义词：landslide, debris flow|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun
