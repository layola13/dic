expendably:/ɪkˈspɛndəbli/
adjective|可牺牲地，可消耗地|In a way that can be sacrificed or used up and replaced.|The expendable parts of the rocket were jettisoned after launch. 火箭的可消耗部件在发射后被抛弃了。|近义词：dispensable, replaceable, nonessential|反义词：essential, indispensable, crucial|词性: adjective
词源: From expend + -able.  Ultimately from Latin expendere "to weigh out, pay out, disburse".
