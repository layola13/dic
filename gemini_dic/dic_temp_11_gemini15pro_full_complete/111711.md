loco:/ˈloʊkoʊ/| adj. |疯狂的，精神错乱的|Mentally deranged; insane.  Originates from Spanish, meaning "crazy."|He went loco after losing his job. 他失业后疯了。|Synonyms: crazy, insane, mad, nuts|Antonyms: sane, rational|adjective

loco:/ˈloʊkoʊ/| n. |疯子|A crazy person. Originates from Spanish, meaning "crazy."|He acted like a loco after winning the lottery. 他中彩票后像个疯子一样。|Synonyms: lunatic, madman, maniac|Antonyms: sane person|noun
