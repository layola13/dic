acculturating: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
逐渐适应文化, 文化适应
The process of adapting or adjusting to a new culture.
Many immigrants face the challenge of acculturating to a new society. 许多移民面临着适应新社会的挑战。
International students are acculturating to the American lifestyle. 国际学生正在适应美国的生活方式。
synonyms: adapting, adjusting, assimilating, integrating
antonyms: isolating, segregating, alienating
verb

acculturate: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪt/
verb
使适应文化, 文化适应
To adapt or adjust to a new culture.
Children acculturate more quickly than adults. 孩子比成年人适应文化的速度更快。
The company offers workshops to help new employees acculturate. 公司提供研讨会来帮助新员工适应文化。
synonyms: adapt, adjust, assimilate, integrate
antonyms: isolate, segregate, alienate
verb

acculturated: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪtɪd/
adjective
