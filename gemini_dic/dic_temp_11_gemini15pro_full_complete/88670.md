hoarstone:/ˈhɔːrstəʊn/| n. |界石，里程碑|A stone placed to mark a boundary, especially an ancient one.  It often carries historical or cultural significance.|例句：The hoarstone marked the edge of the king's territory. 这块界石标志着国王领土的边缘。

例句：The ancient hoarstone, weathered by centuries of rain, was a silent witness to history. 这块古老的界石，经受了几个世纪的风雨侵蚀，是历史的无声见证。|近义词：boundary stone, landmark, milestone|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hār stān* ("grey stone"), from *hār* ("grey," "hoary") and *stān* ("stone").
