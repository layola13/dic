armorican:/ˌɑːrˈmɒrɪkən/ | adj. n. | Armorican（指布列塔尼地区或其居民、语言或文化）; 布列塔尼人 |  Relating to or characteristic of Armorica, an ancient region in northwestern France corresponding roughly to Brittany. Also refers to the people, language, and culture of this region. | The Armorican peninsula is known for its rugged coastline. 布列塔尼半岛以其崎岖的海岸线而闻名。|  Breton, Celtic |  None | adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Armorica, from Gaulish *Aremorica (“coastal region”), from are- (“at, near, upon”) + mor- (“sea”) + -ica (suffix forming adjectives).
