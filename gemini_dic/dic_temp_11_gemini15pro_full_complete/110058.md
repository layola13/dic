licentious:/laɪˈsɛnʃəs/
adjective
放荡的；淫乱的；纵欲的
Disregarding accepted rules or conventions, especially in sexual conduct.
She led a licentious life, indulging in every kind of pleasure. 她过着放荡的生活，沉溺于各种享乐。
The licentious behavior of the emperor shocked the court.  皇帝放荡的行为震惊了朝廷。
synonyms: dissolute, debauched, promiscuous, immoral, wanton
antonyms: chaste, virtuous, moral, pure, decent
词性: 形容词
