apostille:/əˈpɒstiːl/ |n.|认证，加签；(尤指) 海牙认证|A certificate issued under the Hague Convention Abolishing the Requirement of Legalisation for Foreign Public Documents, which authenticates the signature and seal of a public official on a document.  It simplifies the process of using public documents internationally.|The document requires an apostille from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.|Synonyms: certification, authentication, legalization, attestation|Antonyms: invalidation, revocation|noun
