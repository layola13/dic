intergenic:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|基因间的|Located between genes on a chromosome.  Relating to or occurring within the regions between genes on a chromosome.|例句：Intergenic regions can contain regulatory elements. 基因间区域可能包含调控元件。

例句：Scientists are studying the function of intergenic DNA. 科学家正在研究基因间DNA的功能。|近义词：non-coding, extragenic|反义词：genic, intragenic|词性：形容词
