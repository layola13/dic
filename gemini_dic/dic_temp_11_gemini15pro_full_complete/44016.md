criminologist:/ˌkrɪmɪˈnɑːlədʒɪst/ /ˌkrɪmɪˈnɒlədʒɪst/| n. |犯罪学家|A person who studies crime and criminals.|例句：The criminologist studied the patterns of behavior among serial killers.  犯罪学家研究了连环杀手的行为模式。

例句：The criminologist testified in court about the likely profile of the perpetrator.  犯罪学家在法庭上作证，讲述了犯罪者的可能特征。|近义词：criminalist, penologist|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑law enforcement officer执法人员)|词性:noun
