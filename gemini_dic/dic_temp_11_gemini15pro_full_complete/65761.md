every: /ˈevri/ | determiner | 每个，每，所有的 | Used to refer to each member of a group or set, without exception. | Every student passed the exam. 每个学生都通过了考试。| 近义词: each, all | 反义词: none, no | 词性: determiner

词源: From Old English *æfre ælc "ever each," a compound of æfre "ever" and ælc "each."
