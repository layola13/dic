hygienist:/ˈhaɪdʒiənɪst/ | n. |保健员，卫生员|A person who is trained to clean teeth and give advice on dental care.|Example 1: The dental hygienist cleaned my teeth and gave me tips on flossing. 牙科保健员清洁了我的牙齿，并给了我一些使用牙线的建议。

Example 2:  I see a hygienist twice a year for a thorough cleaning. 我每年去看两次保健员进行彻底清洁。|Synonym: dental hygienist, oral hygienist | Antonym: N/A | Noun
