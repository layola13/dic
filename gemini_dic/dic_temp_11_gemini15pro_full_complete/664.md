abrood:/əˈbruːd/| adj.| 沉思的；担忧的；孵蛋的 |Deep in thought, often about something troubling; (of a bird) sitting on eggs to hatch them.|The hen sat abrood on her eggs. 母鸡伏在蛋上孵蛋。|She seemed abrood, lost in her own thoughts. 她看起来心事重重，沉浸在自己的思绪里。|近义词：pensive, thoughtful, meditative, contemplative (for the meaning "deep in thought"); brooding, incubating (for the meaning "sitting on eggs")|反义词：carefree, lighthearted (for "deep in thought")|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ābrōd "on brood," from ā- "on" + brōd "brood."
