narrow-mindedness:/ˌnæroʊˈmaɪndɪdnəs/| n.|心胸狭窄，偏狭|The narrow-mindedness of the committee prevented them from considering new ideas.  委员会的狭隘思想使他们无法考虑新的想法。|例句：His narrow-mindedness prevented him from seeing the other side of the issue. 他的心胸狭窄使他看不到问题的另一面。

|近义词：bigotry, intolerance, prejudice|反义词:open-mindedness, tolerance, broad-mindedness|词性:noun


narrow-minded:/ˌnæroʊˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|心胸狭窄的，偏狭的|She was criticized for her narrow-minded views on immigration. 她因为在移民问题上狭隘的观点而受到批评。|例句：It's narrow-minded to think that your way is the only right way.  认为你的方法是唯一正确的方法是狭隘的。|近义词：bigoted, intolerant, prejudiced|反义词:open-minded, tolerant, broad-minded|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From narrow + minded.  "minded"  suffix meaning "having a (specified kind of) mind".  "narrow" meaning limited in extent, amount, or scope.
