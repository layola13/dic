anticeremoniousness:/ˌæntɪˌsɛrɪˈmoʊniəsnɪs/
n.
缺乏礼仪；不拘礼节
The state of being opposed to or lacking in ceremony; informality.
His anticeremoniousness made some guests uncomfortable, while others appreciated his casual approach.  他的不拘礼节让一些客人感到不舒服，而另一些人则欣赏他随意的态度。
The meeting's anticeremoniousness surprised the foreign delegates.  会议的不拘礼节让外国代表感到惊讶。
近义词:informality, casualness, unpretentiousness, simplicity
反义词:formality, ceremoniousness, pomp, ritual
词性:noun
