overprolifically: /ˌoʊvərproʊˈlɪfɪkli/
adverb
过度多产地，过度繁殖地
Producing or reproducing offspring or works in excessive abundance.
The author has been publishing overprolifically lately, releasing a new book almost every month.  这位作者最近出版作品过多，几乎每个月都出一本新书。
The rabbits bred overprolifically, quickly overpopulating the small island. 兔子繁殖过度，迅速使小岛人口过剩。
近义词: excessively, abundantly, copiously
反义词: sparsely, sparingly, moderately
词性: adverb

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "prolific" (adjective meaning "producing much fruit or foliage or many offspring") + "-ally" (adverb suffix)
