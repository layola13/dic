beneficed:/ˈbɛnɪfɪst/| adj.|有俸禄的；享有圣俸的；|holding a benefice, especially a church living.|The beneficed clergy of the Church of England. 英国圣公会的有俸圣职人员。|近义词：entitled, privileged|反义词：unbeneficed|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *beneficed, from Anglo-Norman and Old French beneficie (“fief, ecclesiastical living”), from Late Latin beneficium (“fief, estate in land, privilege, kindness”), from Latin bene (“well”) + facere (“to do”).
