chestily:/ˈtʃɛstɪli/| adv.|骄傲地，趾高气扬地|In a manner suggesting self-importance or pride; pompously.|He chestily announced his victory. 他趾高气扬地宣布了他的胜利。|近义词：proudly, haughtily, arrogantly, pompously|反义词：humbly, modestly|词性:副词

词源:
From chest (n.) + -ly (adv.)  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kistiz "basket, box."  The adverbial suffix "-ly" derives from Old English "-lice," meaning "like," related to German "-lich."  The connection to "chest" arises from the puffed-out chest often associated with pride.
