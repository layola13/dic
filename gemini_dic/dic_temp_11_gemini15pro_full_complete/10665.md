approvedly:/əˈpruːvɪdli/
adverb
赞许地，认可地
in a way that shows approval
He nodded approvedly. 他赞许地点了点头。
近义词:approvingly, favorably
反义词:disapprovingly, critically
词性:副词

Etymology:
From approve + -ed + -ly.  "Approve" comes from the Old French *aprover, from Latin approbare "to prove, confirm, sanction," from ad- "to" + probare "to test, prove good," from probus "good, honest."
