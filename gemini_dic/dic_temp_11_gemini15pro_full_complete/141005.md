onerousness:/ˈoʊnərəsnəs/
n.
沉重；繁重；麻烦
The state or quality of being onerous; burdensomeness.
The onerousness of the task made him hesitate.  这项任务的繁重让他犹豫不决。
Synonyms: burdensomeness, difficulty, hardship, laboriousness, taxing, weightiness
Antonyms: easiness, lightness, simplicity
词性: 名词
