hornedness:/ˈhɔːrnɪdnɪs/| n. |有角的状态；角的特征|The state or quality of having horns.  The development or presence of horns. |The hornedness of the rhinoceros is a defining characteristic.  犀牛的有角状态是其一个显著特征。|The study examined the genetic basis of hornedness in sheep.  这项研究调查了绵羊有角状态的遗传基础。|近义词:cornification, horniness (in the context of animals developing horns) |反义词:polledness (lack of horns) |词性:noun
