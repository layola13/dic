cutaway:/ˈkʌtəweɪ/| n. adj.|剖面图，剖视图；插播镜头；燕尾服；|A cutaway is a drawing or model that shows the inside of something.  As an adjective, it describes something designed to be easily removed or opened.|The architect presented a cutaway of the building to show its internal structure.  The pilot escaped using the cutaway canopy of the jet.|近义词：cross-section, insert, breakaway|反义词: exterior, outside|noun
cutaway:/ˈkʌtəweɪ/| adj.|可裁剪的；|Designed to be cut away or detached, especially for quick release.|The aircraft has cutaway doors for emergency exits.|近义词：detachable, separable|反义词: fixed, attached|adjective
