conditionable:/kənˈdɪʃənəbl/
adj.
可调节的，可训练的
Susceptible to being conditioned; capable of being learned or modified by conditioning.
Classical conditioning experiments have shown that even involuntary reflexes are conditionable.  经典条件反射实验表明，即使是非自愿反射也是可以调节的。
Dogs are highly conditionable animals and can be trained to perform a variety of tasks. 狗是高度可训练的动物，可以被训练完成各种各样的任务。
近义词:trainable, modifiable, adaptable, malleable
反义词:incorrigible, unmodifiable, inflexible, resistant
词性:adjective
词源:From "condition" + "-able."  "Condition" comes from Old French "condicion," ultimately from Latin "condicio" (agreement, condition).
