imputably:/ɪmˈpjuːtəbli/
adjective
可归咎地，可归罪地
in a way that can be imputed or attributed to someone or something; ascribable.
The accident was imputably caused by the driver's negligence.  这起事故可归咎于司机的疏忽。
The delay was imputably due to unforeseen circumstances.  延误可归因于不可预见的情况。
近义词:attributably, ascribably, chargeable
反义词:unaccountably, inexplicably
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *imputabilis, from imputare "to attribute, ascribe, charge," from in- "in" + putare "to think."
