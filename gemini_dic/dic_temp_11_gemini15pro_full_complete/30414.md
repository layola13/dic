central:/ˈsɛntrəl/ (US), /ˈsentrəl/ (UK)| adj.|中心的，中央的，主要的，重要的|situated in the middle of something; of great importance|The central argument of the book is that poverty can be eradicated. 这本书的核心论点是贫困可以被根除。|Main, key, essential, chief, principal|Peripheral, outer, external, minor|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *central*, from Late Latin *centralis*, from Latin *centrum* ("center")
