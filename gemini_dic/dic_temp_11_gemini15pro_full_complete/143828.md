outpeople:/ˈaʊtˌpipəl/
verb
超越，胜过；在数量上超过
to surpass or exceed in number

例句：Their army outpeopled ours by a significant margin. 他们的军队在人数上大大超过了我们。

近义词：outnumber, surpass, exceed
反义词：be outnumbered by

词性：v.
