fraudulentness:/ˈfrɔːdʒələntnəs/
n.
欺诈性; 欺骗性
The quality or state of being fraudulent.
The fraudulentness of the claim was obvious.  这个索赔的欺诈性是显而易见的。
The report exposed the fraudulentness of the company's accounting practices. 报告揭露了该公司会计行为的欺骗性。
近义词: dishonesty, deceitfulness, deceptiveness, duplicity, trickery
反义词: honesty, integrity, truthfulness
词性: 名词

fraudulent:/ˈfrɔːdʒələnt/
adj.
欺诈的; 欺骗的
Characterized by, involving, or proceeding from fraud.
He was charged with fraudulent activities. 他被指控从事欺诈活动。
The company was found guilty of making fraudulent claims.  这家公司被判犯有欺诈索赔罪。
近义词: dishonest, deceitful, deceptive, duplicitous, tricky
反义词: honest, truthful, genuine
词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *fraudulentus, from Latin fraudulent-, stem of fraudāns, present participle of fraudāre "to cheat, defraud," from fraus "deceit, fraud."
