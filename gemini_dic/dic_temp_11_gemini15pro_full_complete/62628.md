enforcer:/ɪnˈfɔːrsər/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|执行者，强制执行人|Someone who enforces rules, laws, or decisions.  This can refer to a person in a position of authority, like a police officer, or someone who uses force or threats to ensure compliance.|The company hired enforcers to collect debts. 公司雇佣了执行者来收债。|Synonyms: executor, implementer, officer, agent|Antonyms: violator, lawbreaker|Noun

enforcer:/ɪnˈfɔːrsər/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|执行机构|An organization or group that enforces rules or laws.|The UN acted as an enforcer of the peace agreement. 联合国充当了和平协议的执行机构。|Synonyms: authority, regulator, agency|Antonyms:  rebel, dissident|Noun
