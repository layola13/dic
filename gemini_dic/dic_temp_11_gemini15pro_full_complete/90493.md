huckleberry:/ˈhʌkəlˌbɛri/| n.|越橘，野樱莓；无价值的东西；小人物|A small, round, dark blue berry that grows on bushes in North America.  Also, informally, a person of little importance or consequence.|We picked huckleberries in the woods all afternoon. 我们整个下午都在树林里采摘越橘。|近义词：bilberry, blueberry, whortleberry|反义词: N/A|noun
