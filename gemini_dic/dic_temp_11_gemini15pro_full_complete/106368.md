kombi:/ˈkɒmbi/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmbi/ (US)| n. |小型厢式货车；大众面包车|A small van, particularly a Volkswagen Transporter van.|例句：We rented a kombi for our surfing trip. 我们租了一辆大众面包车去冲浪旅行。

例句：The kombi was packed full of camping gear. 这辆小型厢式货车装满了露营装备。


近义词：van, minibus, minivan|反义词: -(not applicable for a vehicle)|词性:noun


词源：Shortened from the German word *Kombinationsfahrzeug*, meaning "combination vehicle".  This reflects the vehicle's dual-purpose nature, often used for both passengers and cargo.
