duffer:/ˈdʌfər/| n. |笨拙的人，不擅长的人；外行|A person who is not skillful at something; an incompetent person.|例句：He's a bit of a duffer at golf. 他打高尔夫球有点笨拙。

例句：I'm a real duffer when it comes to computers.  说到电脑，我真是个外行。|近义词：incompetent, bungler, klutz, awkward person|反义词:expert, professional, adept|词性:noun
