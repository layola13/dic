combatant: /ˈkɒmbətənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmbətənt/ (US)| adj.| 交战的，战斗的|Engaged in fighting|The combatant nations signed a truce. 交战各国签署了停战协议。|Synonyms: fighting, warring, belligerent|Antonyms: peaceful, non-violent|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English, from Old French *combatant, present participle of combatre (“to fight”), from Latin combattere, from com- (“together”) + battuere (“to beat”).
