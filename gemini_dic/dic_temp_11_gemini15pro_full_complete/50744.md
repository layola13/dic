derring-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋ ˈduː/
noun
勇敢的行为；大胆的举动；冒险精神
valiant deeds in undertaking perilous adventures.
He was known for his derring-do on the battlefield. 他因在战场上的英勇行为而闻名。

近义词:bravery, courage, valor, audacity, heroism
反义词:cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
词性:n.

Etymology:
From Middle English *derryng do*, from *derryng* (present participle of *durren*, "to dare") + *do*, "deed".  Essentially meaning "daring to do".
