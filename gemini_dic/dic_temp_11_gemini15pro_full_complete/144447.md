outswindle: /ˌaʊtˈswɪndl/
verb
欺骗；骗取
To cheat someone out of something, especially money, by using clever and dishonest methods.
He outswindled his business partner and took all the profits. 他骗取了他生意伙伴的钱，把所有的利润都拿走了。
The con artist outswindled the elderly woman out of her life savings.  这个骗子骗走了老妇人的毕生积蓄。
近义词: cheat, defraud, swindle, con
反义词:  be honest, be truthful

词性:  v.
