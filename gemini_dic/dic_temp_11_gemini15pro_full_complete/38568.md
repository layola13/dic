concoctor:/kənˈkɒktə(r)/ (US) /kənˈkɑːktər/ (UK)| n. |调制者；调配者；捏造者；编造者| someone who creates something, typically a dish or story, by combining various ingredients or elements. |The concoctor of this strange drink remains a mystery.  这种奇怪饮料的调配者仍然是个谜。|Synonyms: creator, inventor, maker, author, fabricator |Antonyms: destroyer, demolisher |noun


concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (US) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (UK)| v. |调制；调配；捏造；编造|to create or devise something, typically a dish or story, by combining various ingredients or elements. |She concocted a delicious soup from leftover vegetables. 她用剩下的蔬菜调制了一碗美味的汤。|Synonyms: create, devise, invent, make up, fabricate |Antonyms: destroy, demolish |verb
