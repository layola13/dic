cabassou:/kəˈbæsˌuː/
n.
巴西棕榈; 卡巴苏棕榈; 巴西蜡棕榈
A South American palm tree (Orbignya phyllacantha syn. O. speciosa, family Arecaceae) whose leaves yield a useful fibre and whose nuts yield an edible oil.
The cabassou palm provides a valuable source of oil for local communities.  卡巴苏棕榈为当地社区提供了宝贵的油脂来源。
The cabassou fibre is used in making rope and other products. 卡巴苏纤维用于制造绳索和其他产品。
Synonyms: babassu, cusi
Etymology:  From Portuguese *cabassú*, from Tupi *kawasú*.
