electrochemist: /ɪˈlɛktrəʊˈkɛmɪst/ (British) /ɪˈlɛktroʊˈkɛmɪst/ (American)| n.|电化学家|A scientist specializing in electrochemistry.|Michael Faraday is considered one of the greatest electrochemists of all time. 迈克尔·法拉第被认为是历史上最伟大的电化学家之一。|chemist, scientist| |noun
ETYMOLOGY->
electro- + chemist.  "electro-" comes from "electric," ultimately from Greek "elektron" meaning "amber" (because static electricity could be produced by rubbing amber).  "-chemist" from "chemistry,"  ultimately from Arabic "al-kīmiyā’."
