nonvalid:/nɒnˈvælɪd/ | adj. |无效的，不正确的| Not legally or officially acceptable; not valid. | Example: The ticket is nonvalid after the expiry date. 这张票在过期后就无效了。| Example:  His passport was declared nonvalid after it was discovered to be forged. 他的护照被发现是伪造的之后就被宣布无效了。 | Synonyms: invalid, void, null, unusable, ineffective | Antonyms: valid, legal, legitimate, effective, usable | Adjective

Etymology->
From non- + valid. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."  "Valid" comes from the Latin "validus," meaning "strong, powerful, effective."
