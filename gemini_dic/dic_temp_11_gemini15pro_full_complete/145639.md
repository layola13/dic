overcontented: /ˌōvər.kənˈten.tɪd/ | adj. | 过于满足的，过分自得的 | Excessively content; too satisfied, often to the point of complacency.  | He became overcontented with his life and stopped striving for improvement. 他对自己的生活过于满足，不再努力改进自己。 | Example: The overcontented cat just lay in the sun all day.  那只过分满足的猫整天就躺在太阳底下。 | Synonyms: complacent, self-satisfied, smug | Antonyms: dissatisfied, restless, ambitious | Word type: adjective
