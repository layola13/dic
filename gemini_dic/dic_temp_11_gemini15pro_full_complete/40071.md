contagioned:/kənˈteɪdʒənd/ | adj. | 被感染的，受传染的 | Affected by contagion; contaminated.  This word is archaic and rarely used. The standard modern form is "contagious." |  *Contagioned* areas were quarantined. (archaic - should be "Contagious areas were quarantined.") | 近义词: contagious, infectious, communicable | 反义词: immune, resistant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *contagionem*, accusative of *contagio* ("contact, infection"), from *contingere* ("to touch together"), from *com-* ("together") + *tangere* ("to touch").
