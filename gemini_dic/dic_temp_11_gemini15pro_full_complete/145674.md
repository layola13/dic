overcooks:/ˌoʊvərˈkʊks/| v. | 煮过头，烧过头 | To cook (food) for too long, thereby spoiling it. | I overcooked the chicken and it was dry. 我把鸡肉煮过头了，很干。| The steak was overcooked and tough. 牛排煮过头了，很硬。| 近义词: burn, scorch | 反义词: undercook | 动词
