otozoum:/ˈoʊtəˌzuːm/| n. |奥托佐姆（一种已灭绝的大型两栖动物）|A genus of extinct, very large amphibians of the suborder Labyrinthodontia that lived during the Late Triassic and Early Jurassic periods.  They are characterized by their massive size and distinctive footprints.|The otozoum left behind impressive footprints, giving us clues about its size and gait. 奥托佐姆留下了令人印象深刻的脚印，让我们得以了解它的大小和步态。

The discovery of otozoum fossils helped scientists understand the fauna of the Late Triassic. 奥托佐姆化石的发现帮助科学家了解了晚三叠世的动物群。|近义词:labyrinthodont (更广义的迷齿类动物)|反义词:N/A (已灭绝动物，无确切反义词)|词性:名词
