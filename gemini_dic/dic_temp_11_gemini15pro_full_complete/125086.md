mullers:/ˈmʌlərz/| n.|研磨器，磨碎器; (尤指)用于研磨颜料或香料的工具; 穆勒氏管|A muller is a hard, smooth tool, often made of stone or glass, used for grinding pigments or other substances into a fine powder.  It's often used in a shallow dish or slab.|The artist used a muller to grind the pigments for her paints. 艺术家用研磨器研磨颜料来制作她的颜料。|synonyms: grinder, pestle, pounder|antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct antonym for a tool)|noun
