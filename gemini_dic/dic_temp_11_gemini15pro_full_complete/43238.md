crackless:/ˈkræklɪs/
adjective
无裂纹的；完整的；完美的
Free from cracks; flawless.
The artisan presented a crackless porcelain vase. 工匠展示了一个完美无瑕的瓷花瓶。
近义词:flawless, intact, perfect, unblemished, undamaged
反义词:cracked, damaged, flawed, imperfect
