infrequent:/ɪnˈfriːkwənt/| adj.|罕见的，不常发生的|Not occurring often; rare.|例句：Infrequent visits.  拜访次数很少。|近义词：rare, uncommon, occasional|反义词：frequent, common, usual|词性:形容词
Etymology: From in- "not" + frequent.  Ultimately from Latin *frequens,* present participle of frequere "to crowd, throng, resort to, visit often."
