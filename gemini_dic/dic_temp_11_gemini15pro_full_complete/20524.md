bizarrely:/bɪˈzɑːrli/ | adv. | 奇怪地，古怪地 | in a bizarre manner; strangely; oddly. |  The statue was bizarrely dressed in a clown costume.  这尊雕像奇怪地穿着一件小丑服装。| synonym: strangely, oddly, unusually, eccentrically, weirdly | antonym: normally, ordinarily, typically, conventionally | adverb

bizarre:/bɪˈzɑːr/ | adj. | 奇异的，古怪的 | very strange or unusual. | The dream was so bizarre I couldn't make sense of it.  这个梦太奇怪了，我无法理解它。| synonym: strange, odd, unusual, eccentric, weird | antonym: normal, ordinary, typical, conventional | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bizarre*, from Italian *bizzarro* "irascible, quick-tempered," perhaps from Basque *bizar* "beard," thus meaning "bold, bristling." The sense shifted in French to "strange, odd."
