deuterated:/ˈdjuːtəreɪtɪd/
adj.
氘化的
containing deuterium in place of ordinary hydrogen.
Deuterated water is used in nuclear reactors. 氘化水用于核反应堆。
近义词:labelled with deuterium
反义词:non-deuterated
词性:形容词
