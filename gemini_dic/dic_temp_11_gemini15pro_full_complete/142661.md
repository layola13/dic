ostracodermi: /ˌɒstrəˈkəʊdɜːmiː/ /ˌɑːstrəˈkoʊdɜːrmaɪ/ | n. |  骨甲鱼纲（已灭绝） |  An extinct class of primitive jawless fish-like vertebrates of the Paleozoic Era, characterized by bony armor covering the head and part of the body. | The ostracoderms were among the earliest vertebrates. 骨甲鱼是最早的脊椎动物之一。|  Ostracoderms thrived in the Silurian period. 骨甲鱼在志留纪时期繁盛。| 近义词:  agnathans (无颌类), early vertebrates (早期脊椎动物) | 反义词:  gnathostomes (有颌类) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek óstrakon (shell) + dérma (skin).
