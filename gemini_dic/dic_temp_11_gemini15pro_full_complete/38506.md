conciliate: /kənˈsɪlieɪt/
verb
安抚，平息，调解
to stop someone from being angry or discontented; placate; pacify.
例句：She tried to conciliate the angry customer with a refund. 她试图用退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。

例句：The mediator conciliated the two warring factions. 调解员调解了两个交战派别。

近义词：appease, placate, mollify, soothe, pacify
反义词：aggravate, antagonize, provoke, incite
词性: verb

conciliated: /kənˈsɪlieɪtɪd/
adjective
被安抚的，被平息的
Having been pacified or appeased.
例句：The conciliated customer left the store satisfied.  被安抚的顾客满意地离开了商店。

例句：The two parties, now conciliated, were able to reach an agreement.  双方现在和解了，能够达成协议。

近义词：appeased, placated, mollified, soothed, pacified
反义词：aggravated, antagonized, provoked, incited
词性: adjective
