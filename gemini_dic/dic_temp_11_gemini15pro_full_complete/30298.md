cenobite:/ˈsɛnəˌbaɪt/| n. |禁欲者，修道士|A member of a religious order, especially one living in a monastery or convent.|例句：The cenobite dedicated his life to prayer and contemplation.  这位禁欲者毕生致力于祈祷和沉思。

例句：The cenobites lived a simple life, isolated from the outside world. 这些禁欲者过着简朴的生活，与世隔绝。|近义词：monk, nun, ascetic, hermit|反义词：hedonist, libertine|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coenobita, from Greek koinobios ‘living in a community’, from koinos ‘common’ + bios ‘life’.
