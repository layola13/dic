farmable:/ˈfɑːrməbl/| adj.|可耕作的，可养殖的|Suitable for farming; capable of being farmed.|The land is farmable, but it needs irrigation. 这块地可以耕种，但需要灌溉。|近义词：arable, cultivable|反义词：unfarmable, barren|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From farm + -able.  "Farm" comes from Old French "ferme" meaning "lease, rent, fixed payment," ultimately from Latin "firma" meaning "fixed, firm, steadfast." The suffix "-able" means "capable of being."
