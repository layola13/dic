knock-back:/ˈnɒkˌbæk/ (UK) /ˈnɑːkˌbæk/ (US)| n.|挫折，拒绝，回绝；(工程)反冲，后坐力|A setback or rejection; in engineering, recoil or kickback.|The planning application suffered a knock-back when the council refused permission.  规划申请遭到拒绝，这是一个挫折。|synonyms: setback, rejection, rebuff, refusal, blow, reverse, defeat, repulse; recoil, kickback|antonyms: success, acceptance, approval, advance|noun


knock-back:/ˈnɒkˌbæk/ (UK) /ˈnɑːkˌbæk/ (US)| adj.|令人沮丧的，令人失望的|(of an experience or piece of news) causing disappointment or discouragement.|The news of the job loss was a real knock-back. 失业的消息真是令人沮丧。|synonyms: disappointing, discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting, depressing, demoralizing|antonyms: encouraging, uplifting, inspiring|adjective
