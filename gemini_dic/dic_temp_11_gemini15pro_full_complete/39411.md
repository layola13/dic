conmen:/ˈkɒnmɛn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnmɛn/ (US)| n. |骗子；行骗者|A man who cheats or tricks people by gaining their trust and persuading them to believe something that is not true.|The conmen tricked her out of her life savings. 这些骗子骗走了她所有的积蓄。|近义词：fraudster, swindler, cheat, trickster|反义词:honest person|Noun
