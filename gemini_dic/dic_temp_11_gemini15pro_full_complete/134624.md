nonlyricisms:/ˌnɒnˈlɪrɪˌsɪzəmz/
noun
缺乏抒情性；非抒情性
The nonlyricisms of the report made it difficult to connect with emotionally. 这份报告缺乏抒情性，难以产生情感共鸣。
This scientific paper is characterized by its nonlyricisms. 这篇科学论文的特点是缺乏抒情性。
Near Synonyms:prosaicness, matter-of-factness, dryness
Antonyms: lyricism, poetry, emotionalism
词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "lyricism" (from "lyric," ultimately from Greek "lyrikos" relating to the lyre, a stringed instrument used to accompany poetry).  Thus, "nonlyricisms" refers to the absence of qualities associated with lyric poetry, such as emotional expression, imagery, and melodiousness.
