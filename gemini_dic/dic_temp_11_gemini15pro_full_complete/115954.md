marrieds: /ˈmærɪdz/ | n. | 已婚人士 |  Non-standard plural of "married".  While "married" can be used as an adjective preceding a noun (e.g., "married couples"), it is not typically used as a noun in standard English.  The standard way to refer to married people is "married couples," "married people," or "those who are married." |  "The marrieds were invited to a special dinner." (Non-standard) | Near synonyms: married couples, married people | Antonyms: singles, unmarried people | Noun
