bowlfuls:/ˈboʊlfʊlz/
noun
满满几碗
A quantity that fills several bowls.
She ate three bowlfuls of ice cream. 她吃了满满三碗冰淇淋。
heaping bowlfuls, several bowls
(no antonym applicable to this meaning)
