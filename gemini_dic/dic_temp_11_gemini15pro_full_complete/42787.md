couping:/ˈkuːpɪŋ/
n.
政变;颠覆;推翻
A sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government.
The military coup led to widespread unrest. 军队政变导致了广泛的动乱。
overthrow, seizure, takeover
restoration, reinstatement
noun
