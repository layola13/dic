detrainments:/diːˈtreɪnmənts/
| n. | 下车，下火车；（气象学）夹卷；（军事）部队下车 |  The process of removing passengers or goods from a train; In meteorology, the mixing of drier air into a cloud, leading to evaporation and a decrease in cloud liquid water content; In military contexts, the unloading of troops and equipment from a train. |  Heavy detrainments are expected during the rush hour. 预计在高峰时段下车人数众多。| 近义词：disembarkations, offloadings | 反义词: entrainments, boardings | 词性: 名词

detrainment:/diːˈtreɪnmənt/
| n. | 下车，下火车；（气象学）夹卷；（军事）部队下车 | The act of leaving a train; the process by which air is mixed into a cloud from the surrounding environment. | The detrainment of passengers was orderly and efficient. 乘客下车井然有序。| 近义词：disembarkation, offloading | 反义词: entrainment, boarding | 词性: 名词
