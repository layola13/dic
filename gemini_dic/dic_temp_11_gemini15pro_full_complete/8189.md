anti-discrimination:/ˌænti dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/ | adj. n.| 反歧视；反歧视的 |  Relating to or promoting the prevention of discrimination (unfair or prejudicial treatment of people and groups based on characteristics such as race, gender, age, or sexual orientation). | The company has a strong anti-discrimination policy.  这家公司有强有力的反歧视政策。| synonyms: equal opportunity, unbiased, impartial | antonyms: discriminatory, prejudiced, biased | adjective

anti-discrimination:/ˌænti dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n.| 反歧视 |  The act or practice of opposing discrimination. |  Anti-discrimination laws are essential for a just society. 反歧视法对一个公正的社会至关重要。| synonyms: equality, fairness, impartiality | antonyms: discrimination, prejudice, bias | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From anti- + discrimination. "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against" or "opposite of." "Discrimination" comes from the Latin word "discriminare," meaning "to distinguish between."
