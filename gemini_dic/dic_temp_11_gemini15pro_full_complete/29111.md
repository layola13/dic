cascade's:/kæˈskeɪdz/ | n. | 瀑布的；倾泻的； 一连串的|  The possessive form of "cascade," meaning belonging to or associated with a cascade. cascade:/kæˈskeɪd/ | n. | 瀑布；倾泻； 一连串 | A small waterfall, typically one of several that fall in stages down a steep rocky slope. A large number of things or people appearing or occurring in rapid succession. | The waterfall cascaded down the rocks. 瀑布倾泻而下。| synonyms: waterfall, torrent, avalanche, flood, deluge, series, succession | antonyms: trickle, drip | noun

cascade:/kæˈskeɪd/ | v. | 倾泻；瀑布似地流下；层叠 |  To fall or hang in copious or luxuriant quantities.  Arrange (things) in a series or sequence. |  Her hair cascaded down her back. 她的头发瀑布般披散在背上。| synonyms: pour, flow, gush, tumble, spill, overflow | antonyms: trickle, drip | verb
