depopulators:/diːˈpɒpjʊleɪtəz/
n.|人口减少者；使人口减少的事物|Those responsible for or the causes of depopulation.  The war and famine were major depopulators of the region. 战争和饥荒是该地区人口减少的主要原因。|
例句：The plague was a major depopulator of medieval Europe.  黑死病是中世纪欧洲人口大量减少的主要原因。

例句：Modern warfare, with its devastating potential, can be a significant depopulator.  现代战争及其破坏性的潜力，可能造成人口的大量减少。
近义词：depleters, decimators|
反义词：repopulators, colonizers|
词性：noun (plural)


depopulator:/diːˈpɒpjʊleɪtə/
n.|人口减少者；使人口减少的事物|Something that reduces population. |
例句：Disease acted as a depopulator in many ancient cities. 疾病在许多古代城市中导致人口减少。

例句：The volcano eruption proved to be a devastating depopulator for the island. 火山爆发对岛上的人口造成了毁灭性的打击，导致人口锐减。
近义词：reducer, diminisher|
反义词：increaser, enhancer|
词性：noun (singular)


ETYMOLOGY->
From depopulate + -or/-er. Depopulate from de- + populate. Populate from Latin populus "people".
