awkwarder:/ˈɔːkwərdər/ (US), /ˈɔːkwədə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更笨拙的，更尴尬的|Comparative form of "awkward".  More lacking in social graces or physical coordination; more difficult to handle or deal with.|例句：The silence grew awkwarder and awkwarder. 沉默变得越来越尴尬。|近义词：clumsier, more ungainly, more difficult|反义词:graceful, easier, more skillful|词性:adjective
