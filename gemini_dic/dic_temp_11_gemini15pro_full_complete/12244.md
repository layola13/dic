ascendancy:/əˈsɛndənsi/
noun
优势，支配地位，权势
A position of dominant power or influence.
The ascendancy of the new government brought about significant changes. 新政府的掌权带来了重大的变化。
The dictator's ascendancy over the people lasted for decades. 独裁者对人民的统治持续了几十年。
dominance, supremacy, control, power
subordination, weakness, impotence
n.
