ayuntamiento:/aˌʝuntaˈmjento/| n.|市政厅，市政府|A building or group of buildings that house the local government of a municipality.  It's the Spanish equivalent of "city hall" or "town hall".|The ayuntamiento is located in the main square. 市政厅位于主广场。|The mayor's office is in the ayuntamiento. 市长的办公室在市政厅里。|synonyms: city hall, town hall, municipal building, local government|antonyms: N/A|noun
