grainering:/ˈɡreɪnərɪŋ/| n.|木纹涂饰，仿木纹|The grainering on the door made it look like real wood. 门上的木纹涂饰使它看起来像真木。|近义词:woodgraining, faux bois|反义词: None|noun

Etymology:  From "grain" (referring to the texture of wood) + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-ing" (present participle/gerund suffix).  Essentially, the process of creating a grain-like appearance.
