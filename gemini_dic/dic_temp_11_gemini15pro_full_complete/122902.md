molossus:/məˈlɒsəs/ /məˈlɑːsəs/| n.|摩洛斯诗, 长长格|A metrical foot in poetry consisting of three long syllables.  It is a relatively rare foot in English poetry, creating a slow, ponderous rhythm.|The line "All the world's a stage" is an example of a molossus.|近义词:  (No direct synonym, but similar in effect to spondee)|反义词:  pyrrhic (two short syllables)|词性: noun
