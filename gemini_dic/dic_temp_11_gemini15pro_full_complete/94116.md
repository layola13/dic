impeccancy:/ɪmˈpekənsi/
n.
无罪；完美无缺；无瑕疵
The state or quality of being impeccable; faultlessness.
His impeccancy in dress and manners was remarkable. 他的穿着和举止无可挑剔。
Synonyms: faultlessness, flawlessness, perfection, immaculateness, purity
Antonyms: imperfection, flaw, defect, blemish, fault
词性: 名词
