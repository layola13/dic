ascription: /əˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. | 归因，归属，归功 | The act of attributing something to a cause or source.  The ascription of the painting to Rembrandt was later proven false. | 例句：The ascription of the poem to Shakespeare is still debated by scholars.  这首诗归属于莎士比亚的说法仍然受到学者们的争论。

例句：The ascription of blame for the accident is still under investigation.  事故责任的归属仍在调查中。

近义词：attribution, assignment, credit | 反义词：denial, rejection | 词性：noun
