ecotour:/ˈiːkoʊtʊr/ | n. | 生态旅游 | A tour of a natural habitat that is designed to have minimal impact on the environment and to educate visitors about the area's natural history. |  We took an ecotour of the rainforest. 我们参加了一个热带雨林的生态旅游。|  nature tour, green tour | mass tourism | noun

ecotourism:/ˈiːkoʊtʊrɪzəm/ | n. | 生态旅游 | Tourism directed toward exotic, often threatened, natural environments, especially to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife. | Ecotourism is becoming increasingly popular. 生态旅游正变得越来越受欢迎。 | nature tourism, green tourism | mass tourism | noun
