lactoses:/ˈlækˌtoʊsiz/| n. |乳糖（复数）|A disaccharide sugar (C12H22O11) present in milk. It is composed of glucose and galactose units.|Cow's milk contains about 4.7% lactose.  牛奶含有大约4.7%的乳糖。|Lactose intolerance is the inability to digest lactose. 乳糖不耐症是指无法消化乳糖。|synonyms: milk sugar|antonyms: None|noun
