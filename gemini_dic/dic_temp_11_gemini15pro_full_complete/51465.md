detonization:/ˌdiːtɒnɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌdiːtɑːnɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
去音调化；非音调化
The process of losing or removing the characteristic of having tones, particularly in phonetics and linguistics, where it refers to the loss of distinctive tones in a language or dialect.
The linguist explained the process of detonization in certain African languages.  这位语言学家解释了某些非洲语言中去音调化的过程。
The detonization of the language over time led to a simpler tonal system.  随着时间的推移，这种语言的去音调化导致了更简单的音调系统。
近义词: tone loss, tone reduction
反义词: tonogenesis
词性: n.
