dramaturgic:/ˌdræməˈtɜːrdʒɪk/ | adj. | 戏剧的；剧作的 | Relating to the art or technique of dramatic composition and theatrical representation. |  The play's dramaturgic structure was innovative and captivating. 这部戏剧的戏剧结构新颖而引人入胜。| synonyms: dramatic, theatrical, histrionic | antonyms: undramatic, nontheatrical | adjective

dramaturgical:/ˌdræməˈtɜːrdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 戏剧的；剧作的 | Relating to the art or technique of dramatic composition and theatrical representation. | The dramaturgical choices made by the director were bold and effective. 导演做出的戏剧选择大胆而有效。| synonyms: dramatic, theatrical, histrionic | antonyms: undramatic, nontheatrical | adjective


Etymology:  From French *dramaturgique*, from German *dramaturgisch*, from *Dramaturg* (dramaturge) + *-isch* (-ic). Ultimately from Ancient Greek *δρᾶμα* (*drama*, “action, play”) + *ἔργον* (*ergon*, “work”).
