dermopteran: /dɜːrˈmɒptərən/ | n. |  皮翼目动物 | An order of mammals also known as colugos or flying lemurs, characterized by a patagium (membrane of skin) that extends between their limbs and allows them to glide.  They are native to Southeast Asia.| The Philippine dermopteran is endangered due to habitat loss. 菲律宾皮翼目动物由于栖息地丧失而濒临灭绝。| synonym: colugo, flying lemur | antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific taxonomic group.  Could consider "terrestrial mammal" as a broad contrast.) | noun
