henpeckings:/ˈhenˌpekɪŋz/
|n.| 持续不断的抱怨和批评（尤指妻子对丈夫）; 惧内; 妻管严|The constant henpeckings from his wife made him miserable. 他妻子不断的抱怨和批评让他很痛苦。|例句：He couldn't stand the henpeckings anymore and moved out.  他再也受不了唠叨了，于是搬了出去。

例句：Years of henpeckings had eroded his self-confidence. 多年的唠叨已经侵蚀了他的自信心。|近义词：nagging, carping, scolding, badgering|反义词: praising, supporting|词性: 名词 (plural noun)
