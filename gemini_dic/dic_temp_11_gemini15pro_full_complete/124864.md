mucronulate:/mjuːˈkrɒnjʊleɪt/
adj.
微凸的，具小短尖的
Having a very small, sharp point; minutely mucronate.
The mucronulate leaf tip is a distinguishing feature of this species. 这种植物的叶子尖端有微小的凸起是其 distinguishing feature。
The fern's leaflets are mucronulate, ending in tiny points. 这种蕨类植物的小叶是微凸的，末端有小尖头。
近义词: mucronate, pointed, acute, cuspidate
反义词: obtuse, rounded
