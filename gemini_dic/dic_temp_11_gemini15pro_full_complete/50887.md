deserters:/dɪˈzɜːrtərz/
n.|逃兵，开小差者|People who abandon their military duty or post without permission.  那些未经许可擅离军事职守或岗位的人。|The army rounded up the deserters. 军队围捕了逃兵。|Synonyms: runaway, absconder, defector|Antonyms: loyalist, patriot|noun

deserter:/dɪˈzɜːrtər/
n.|背弃者，遗弃者|Someone who abandons a person, cause, or organization to which they owe allegiance or loyalty.  背弃对其负有忠诚或忠诚义务的人、事业或组织的人。 |He was branded a deserter by his former colleagues. 他被以前的同事称为叛徒。|Synonyms: traitor, renegade, turncoat|Antonyms: supporter, adherent|noun
