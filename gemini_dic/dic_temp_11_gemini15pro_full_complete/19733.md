billabong:/ˈbɪləbɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɪləbɔːŋ/ (US)| n. |死水潭，牛轭湖，干河床|A billabong is a stagnant pool of water left in a former river channel, typically formed when the river changes course or dries up. It's an Australian term.|例句：The kangaroos gathered around the billabong to drink.  袋鼠聚集在死水潭边喝水。

例句：The billabong was teeming with wildlife. 死水潭里充满了各种野生动物。


|近义词：oxbow lake, lagoon, backwater|反义词:river, stream|词性:noun
