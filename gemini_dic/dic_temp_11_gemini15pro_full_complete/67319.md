extemporaneous:/ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ /ɛkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/| adj.|即席的，临时的，无准备的|Spoken or done without preparation.|She delivered an extemporaneous speech that captivated the audience. 她发表了一场即兴演讲，吸引了全场观众。|近义词：impromptu, improvised, off-the-cuff, ad-lib|反义词：prepared, rehearsed, planned|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ex tempore*  meaning "out of the time/moment" + -aneous (forming adjectives).  "Ex tempore" itself comes from Latin *ex* (out of) + *tempore* (ablative of *tempus*, meaning "time").
