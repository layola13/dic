copo:/ˈkoʊpʊ/ (US), /ˈkɒpəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 杯子；高脚杯 |A drinking vessel, typically made of glass or plastic, often with a stem and base. |She poured the wine into a delicate copo. 她把酒倒进了一个精致的高脚杯里。|He raised his copo in a toast. 他举起酒杯祝酒。|synonyms: glass, goblet, chalice|antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  One could consider "bowl" or "plate" as alternative serving vessels.)|noun
