encolor:/ɪnˈkʌlər/ |v. |给...着色，染色| To give something color; to dye or tint something. |例句：She decided to encolor the fabric with natural dyes. 她决定用天然染料给织物上色。

例句：He encolored the old black and white photos. 他给旧的黑白照片上了色。|近义词：color, dye, tint, stain, pigment |反义词: bleach, fade |词性: 动词
