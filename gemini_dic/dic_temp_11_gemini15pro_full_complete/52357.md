dictatorian:/ˌdɪktəˈtɔːriən/ | adj. |独裁的，独裁者的| Relating to or characteristic of a dictator or dictatorship; tyrannical. |  The dictatorian regime suppressed all dissent.  独裁政权压制所有异议。|  The dictatorian policies led to widespread poverty. 独裁政策导致普遍贫困。 |Synonyms: tyrannical, despotic, autocratic, authoritarian | Antonyms: democratic, liberal, egalitarian | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dictātōriānus, from Latin dictātor (“dictator”) + -ānus.
