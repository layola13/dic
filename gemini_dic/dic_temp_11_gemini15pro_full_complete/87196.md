/wɒt/ | pron. | 什么 | Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | which, who | - | pronoun

/hwʌt/ | pron. | 什么 | Used to ask for information about something. | What do you want? 你想要什么? | which, who | - | pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? 你最近学了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? 她婚礼上穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? 他们在会议前讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我到达之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
