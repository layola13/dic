incogitantly:/ˌɪnkɒˈɡɪtəntli/ /ˌɪnkɑːˈɡɪtəntli/| adv.|不经思考地，无意地|Without thought; thoughtlessly. |She incogitantly blurted out the secret. 她无意中脱口说出了秘密。| He incogitantly left his keys in the car. 他不经意地把钥匙忘在了车里。|近义词：thoughtlessly, carelessly, heedlessly, unthinkingly|反义词：deliberately, thoughtfully, carefully, consciously|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incogitant- (stem of incogitans), present participle of incogitare (“not to think, not to consider”), from in- (“not”) + cogitare (“to think”).
