annulment:/əˈnʌlmənt/| n.|废除，取消，无效|The official annulment of a marriage.  婚姻的正式废除。|例句：

The couple sought an annulment of their marriage.
这对夫妇寻求废除他们的婚姻。

The annulment of the contract left both parties free from their obligations.
合同的废除使双方都摆脱了义务。

|近义词：cancellation, revocation, invalidation|反义词:validation, ratification, confirmation|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French anuller, from Latin annullare ‘reduce to nothing’, from ad- ‘to’ + nullus ‘none’.
词源: 来自中古英语晚期: 源自古法语 anuller，来自拉丁语 annullare “化为乌有”，来自 ad- “到” + nullus “无”。
