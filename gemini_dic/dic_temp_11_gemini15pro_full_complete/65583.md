evade:/ɪˈveɪd/| v. |逃避，躲避|To escape or avoid something, especially by trickery or cleverness.|He evaded the police by hiding in the woods. 他躲在树林里逃避警察。|The company evaded taxes by using offshore accounts.  该公司利用离岸账户逃税。|近义词：avoid, dodge, elude, escape, circumvent|反义词：confront, face, meet, encounter|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *evader (Modern French évader), from Latin ēvādere (“to go out, escape”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + vādere (“to go, walk”).
