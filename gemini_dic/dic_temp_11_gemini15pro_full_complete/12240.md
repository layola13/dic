ascend:/əˈsend/ /æˈsend/
verb
上升，攀登
to go up or climb
The hikers ascended the mountain.  登山者攀登了这座山。
The smoke ascended into the sky. 烟雾升腾到空中。

近义词: climb, rise, mount, soar
反义词: descend, fall, drop

词性: verb

词源: From Latin *ascendere, present active infinitive of ascendo (“I climb, mount, ascend”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + scando (“I climb”).
