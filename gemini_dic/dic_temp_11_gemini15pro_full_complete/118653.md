mestizas:/mɛˈstiːsəs/ | n. | 混血女性（尤指西班牙裔和美洲原住民后裔） | A woman of mixed race, especially of Spanish or Portuguese and Indigenous American ancestry. |  Maria, one of the mestizas in the village, was known for her beautiful embroidery. 村里的混血女性之一玛丽亚以她美丽的刺绣而闻名。


Luisa's grandmother, one of the town's oldest mestizas, shared stories of her ancestors. 路易莎的祖母，镇上年纪最大的混血女性之一，分享了她祖先的故事。



近义词: mixed-race woman, woman of mixed ancestry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym; perhaps "pure-blooded" in a specific racial context, but this is problematic and not recommended) | 词性: noun


词源: From Spanish *mestiza*, feminine of *mestizo*, from Late Latin *mixticius* "mixed," from Latin *mixtus* "mixed," past participle of *miscere* "to mix."
