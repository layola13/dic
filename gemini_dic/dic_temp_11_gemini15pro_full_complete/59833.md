ectothermic:/ˈektəʊθɜːmɪk/ | adj. | 变温的，冷血的 |  An animal whose body temperature varies with the temperature of its surroundings; cold-blooded. | Reptiles, like snakes and lizards, are ectothermic animals. 爬行动物，比如蛇和蜥蜴，是变温动物。|  Snakes bask in the sun to regulate their ectothermic body temperature. 蛇晒太阳来调节它们的体温。 | poikilothermic, cold-blooded | endothermic, warm-blooded, homeothermic | adjective
