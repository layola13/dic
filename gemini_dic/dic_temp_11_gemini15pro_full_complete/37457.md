commissary: /ˈkɒmɪsəri/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmɪseri/ (US) | n. | 軍糧供應官；小賣部；餐廳；伙食房 | a store that sells food and supplies, typically on a military base or in a prison; an officer in charge of supplies, especially food, for a military force |  The soldiers lined up at the commissary to buy snacks. 士兵們在小賣部排隊買零食。| synonyms: canteen, store, shop, provisioner | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "lack of supplies" as a concept) | noun

commissary: /ˈkɒmɪsəri/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmɪseri/ (US) | adj. | 代理的；委任的 | acting as a commissary; delegated | He was appointed commissary for the expedition. 他被任命為探險隊的軍需官。 | synonyms: deputy, acting, delegated | antonyms: principal, primary | adjective
