engagements:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/ /ɛnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/
n.
约定, 责任, 婚约
an agreement to do something or go somewhere at a fixed time.  A formal agreement to get married.  A battle or fight, especially a short one.
例句：I have a prior engagement this evening. 我今晚有个之前的约会。

例句：Their engagement was announced in the newspaper. 他们的订婚消息登报了。


近义词：appointments, commitments, promises, battles, conflicts
反义词：cancellations, withdrawals, retreats
词性：名词
