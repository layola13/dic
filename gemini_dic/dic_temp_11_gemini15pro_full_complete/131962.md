noncontroversially:/ˌnɑːnkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəli/ | adv. | 无争议地，毫无异议地 | In a way that does not cause disagreement or argument. | The matter was noncontroversially approved by the board. 董事会毫无异议地批准了这件事。 | synonym: indisputably, undoubtedly, unquestionably | antonym: controversially, disputably, debatably |词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + controversial + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Controversial" comes from Late Latin controversia, meaning "debate, dispute".
