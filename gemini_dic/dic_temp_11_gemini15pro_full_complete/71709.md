flavour:/ˈfleɪvər/ (US) /ˈfleɪvə/ (UK)| n. vt.| 味道，滋味；特色，风味；香料|a distinctive taste of a food or drink|The dish has a strong garlic flavour. 这道菜有大蒜味。|近义词：taste, aroma, scent, savor|反义词:blandness, insipidity|noun, verb

flavour:/ˈfleɪvər/ (US) /ˈfleɪvə/ (UK)| vt.|给...调味；加味于|to add flavour to food or drink|I flavoured the cake with vanilla extract. 我用香草精给蛋糕调味。|近义词：season, spice|反义词:None|verb
