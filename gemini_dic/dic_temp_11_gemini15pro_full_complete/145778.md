overdate:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪt/| vt. n.|过期；把日期填迟；过时的|To assign a date later than the actual date; to make out of date.|例句：I overdated the check by a week. 我把支票的日期写晚了一周。|例句：Don't overdate the check, or the bank may not honor it. 不要把支票日期写错，否则银行可能不予兑现。|近义词：postdate, antedate (as a verb)|反义词：predate (as a verb), current, contemporary (as an adjective)|词性：动词，名词


overdate:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪt/| n.|过时的；过期|Something that is past its due date; outmoded.|例句：The milk was an overdate. 牛奶过期了。|例句：These overdates need to be removed from the shelves. 这些过期的商品需要从货架上移除。|近义词：expiration, lapse, outdated|反义词：current, contemporary|词性：名词
