meteor:/ˈmiːtiər/ (US), /ˈmiːtiɔːr/ (UK)| n. |流星|A small body of matter from outer space that enters the earth's atmosphere, becoming incandescent as a result of friction and appearing as a streak of light.|A meteor flashed across the night sky. 一颗流星划过夜空。|近义词：shooting star, falling star|反义词: (无明确反义词)|noun
