jackboot:/ˈdʒækbuːt/| n.|长筒靴；杰克靴；(尤指)军用长筒靴|A heavy military boot that extends to the knee or higher, typically associated with oppressive regimes and military forces. |The soldiers wore shiny jackboots and carried rifles. 士兵们穿着锃亮的杰克靴，扛着步枪。|Synonyms: combat boot, riding boot|Antonyms: slipper, sandal|noun
