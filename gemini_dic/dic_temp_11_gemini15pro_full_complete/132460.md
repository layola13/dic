nondepression:/ˌnɑːndɪˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 非抑郁，无抑郁 | Absence of depression; a state of not being depressed. |  The study showed a period of nondepression following the therapy.  这项研究表明，治疗后有一段非抑郁期。|  The patient experienced a welcome period of nondepression after years of struggling.  经过多年的挣扎，病人经历了一段受欢迎的非抑郁期。 | 近义词: well-being, happiness, contentment | 反义词: depression, sadness, melancholy | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "depression" (noun).  "Non-" comes from Latin, while "depression" ultimately derives from Latin "depressus," past participle of "deprimere" (to press down).
