object:/ˈɒbdʒɪkt/ (British) /ˈɑːbdʒekt/ (American)| n. v.|物体，目标，宾语，反对|A material thing that can be seen and touched.|例句：The object of the game is to score the most points.  这个游戏的目标是得分最多。|近义词：thing, item, article, target, aim, goal|反义词:subject, idea|noun
object:/əbˈdʒekt/ (British) /əbˈdʒekt/ (American)| v.|反对，不赞成|Say something to express disapproval of or opposition to.|例句：I object to the way he treats his staff. 我反对他的员工待遇。|近义词：oppose, protest, disagree|反义词:agree, approve, support|verb
