chattering:/ˈtʃætərɪŋ/| adj.  n.|喋喋不休的，快速的连续的短促的声音| characterized by rapid, repetitive, and often meaningless talk;  the act of talking rapidly and continuously about unimportant things|The chattering of the monkeys was incessant. 猴子喋喋不休的声音不绝于耳。|近义词：babbling, jabbering, prattling|反义词：silent, quiet|词性: adjective, noun
chatter:/ˈtʃætər/| v.  n.| 喋喋不休，唠叨；（牙齿）打颤；（机器）发出震颤声|talk rapidly or incessantly about trivial matters; tremble rapidly and involuntarily, typically as a result of cold or fear; (of a machine) make a rapid succession of short percussive sounds|The children chattered excitedly about their presents. 孩子们兴奋地聊着他们的礼物。|近义词：babble, gabble, prattle|反义词：be quiet, be silent|词性: verb, noun
