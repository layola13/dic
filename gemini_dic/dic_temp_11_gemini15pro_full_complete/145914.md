overdilated:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度扩张的
Dilated beyond normal or desirable limits.  Enlarged excessively.
The patient's pupils were overdilated due to the medication.  病人的瞳孔由于药物作用而过度扩张。
近义词:  excessively dilated, widely dilated
反义词: constricted, narrowed
词性: adjective

overdilate:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈleɪt/
verb
过度扩张
To dilate beyond normal or desirable limits.
The drug can overdilate the blood vessels.  这种药物会使血管过度扩张。
近义词:  expand excessively, widen abnormally
反义词: constrict, narrow
词性: verb
