arguably:/ˈɑːrɡjuəbli/ | adv. | 可以说是，大概，|Used to say that something is possible or likely, although it may not be certain.  It introduces a statement which someone might disagree with. | He is arguably the best football player in the world. 他可以说是世界上最好的足球运动员。|  He is arguably the greatest writer of his generation. 他可以说是他那一代最伟大的作家。 | 近义词: possibly, probably, conceivably, plausibly | 反义词: definitely, undoubtedly, certainly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From argue + -ably.  "-ably" is a suffix used to form adverbs meaning "in a manner that can be...",  thus "arguably" means "in a manner that can be argued".
