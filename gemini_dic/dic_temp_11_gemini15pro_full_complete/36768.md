collusions:/kəˈluːʒənz/
n.
共谋，勾结
Secret or illegal cooperation or conspiracy, especially in order to cheat or deceive others.
The collusions between the two companies resulted in higher prices for consumers.  两家公司相互勾结，导致消费者价格上涨。
conspiracy, collaboration, connivance, complicity
honesty, openness, fairness
noun
