indeclinable:/ˌɪndɪˈklaɪnəbl/
adjective|不能变格的，不变形的|Not changing in grammatical form, such as case, gender, number, or tense.|例句：The word "sheep" is indeclinable, having the same form in singular and plural.  单词“sheep”是不能变格的，单复数形式相同。|例句：Many adverbs are indeclinable. 很多副词是不变形的。|近义词：invariant, uninflected|反义词：declinable, inflected|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *indeclinabilis, from in- "not" + declinabilis "capable of being inflected," from declinare "to bend, inflect," from de- "down" + clinare "to lean."
