friezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/| n. |壁带，带状装饰；（建筑物外墙的）横条装饰|A decorative band, especially near the top of a wall, often patterned with sculpture or painting.|The friezing on the ancient temple depicted scenes of battle.|近义词：band, border, molding, decoration|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly plainness or simplicity)|词性:noun
friezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/| adj. |极冷的|Extremely cold.|It's absolutely friezing outside today! 今天外面冷极了！|近义词：freezing, icy, arctic, bitter, frigid|反义词:hot, boiling, scorching|词性:adjective
