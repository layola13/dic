melasma: /məˈlæzmə/ | n. | 褐黄斑，黄褐斑 |  A common skin problem that causes brown or grayish-brown patches, usually on the face. |  Melasma is more common in women, especially during pregnancy. 妊娠期女性更容易出现黄褐斑。| synonyms: chloasma, pigmentation, hyperpigmentation | antonyms: hypopigmentation | noun
