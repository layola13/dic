antagonizable:/ænˈtæɡəˌnaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可对抗的，可被拮抗的 |  Capable of being antagonized; that can be opposed or neutralized. | The effect of the drug is antagonizable by another medication.  这种药物的作用可以被另一种药物拮抗。 | Synonyms: counteractable, neutralizable, reversible | Antonyms: irreversible, uncounteractable | adjective

antagonizable:/ænˈtæɡəˌnaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可引起敌意的，可被激怒的 | Capable of being made hostile or unfriendly. | His arrogant behavior is antagonizable to many people. 他傲慢的行为很容易引起很多人的敌意。 | Synonyms: provocative, irritating, exasperating | Antonyms: conciliatory, appeasing, placating | adjective
