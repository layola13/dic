gorgoneion:/ˌɡɔːrˈɡoʊniən/ | n. | 蛇发女怪头像; 女妖头像 | An image of a Gorgon's head, especially that of Medusa, used as an apotropaic symbol to avert evil. | The ancient Greeks often placed gorgoneions on their shields and armor to protect themselves in battle. 古希腊人经常把蛇发女怪头像放在盾牌和盔甲上，以在战斗中保护自己。 |  Example: The archaeologist unearthed a gorgoneion pendant believed to be from the 5th century BC. 考古学家出土了一个据信来自公元前5世纪的蛇发女怪头像吊坠。 | Synonyms: Gorgon's head, Medusa head | Antonyms: N/A | noun
