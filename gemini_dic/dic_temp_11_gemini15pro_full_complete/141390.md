operettists:/ˌɒpərˈetɪsts/ /ˌɑːpərˈetɪsts/
noun
轻歌剧作曲家/轻歌剧作家
A composer of operettas, or a group of composers of operettas.
The operettists of the early 20th century created a wealth of popular music.  20世纪早期的轻歌剧作曲家创作了大量流行音乐。
Synonyms: composers, writers, musicians
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider  "non-composers" or  "playwrights" if referring to writers of different genres)
词性: noun
