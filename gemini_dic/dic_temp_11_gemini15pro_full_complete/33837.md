circumlocutory: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈlɒkjʊtəri/ /ˌsɜːrkəmˈloʊkjʊtɔːri/
adjective
迂回的，绕圈子的
Expressing oneself in a roundabout way, using many words where fewer would do.
He gave a circumlocutory explanation of his lateness, avoiding the real reason. 他对迟到的解释很迂回，避开了真正的原因。

The politician gave a circumlocutory answer to the journalist's question.  这位政治家对记者的提问给出了一个迂回的答案。

近义词: indirect, evasive, periphrastic, roundabout
反义词: direct, straightforward, concise, succinct
词性: adjective
