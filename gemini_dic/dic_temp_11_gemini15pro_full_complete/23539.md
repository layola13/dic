brainily:/ˈbreɪnɪli/| adv.|聪明地，机智地|In a way that demonstrates intelligence and cleverness.  She solved the puzzle brainily.|近义词：intelligently, cleverly, smartly, wisely|反义词：stupidly, foolishly|词性:副词

词源:  Derived from "brain" (referring to the organ of thought) + "-ily" (adverbial suffix indicating manner).
