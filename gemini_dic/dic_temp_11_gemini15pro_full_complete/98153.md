insatiateness:/ɪnsəˈʃeɪʃəsnəs/
n.
贪得无厌，不知足
The state of being insatiable; inability to be satisfied.
His insatiateness for power led to his downfall. 他对权力的贪得无厌导致了他的垮台。

insatiateness:/ɪnˌseɪʃiəˈblənəs/
n.
贪得无厌，不知足
The state of being insatiable; inability to be satisfied.
Her insatiateness for knowledge led her to pursue multiple degrees. 她对知识的渴望让她去攻读多个学位。

近义词: greed, avarice, rapacity, gluttony, voracity
反义词: contentment, satisfaction, moderation, temperance
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insatiabilitas, from Latin insatiabilis (“insatiable”) + -itas (“-ity”).  Insatiable is formed from in- (“not”) + satiabilis (“able to be sated”), from satiare (“to sate”), from satis (“enough”).
