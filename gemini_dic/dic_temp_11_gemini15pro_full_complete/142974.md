outbake:/ˌaʊtˈbeɪk/
verb
烘烤得比…更好；在烘烤方面胜过
to bake for a longer time or with better results than someone else
例句：She outbaked all the other contestants with her delicious apple pie. 她用美味的苹果派烘烤得比所有其他参赛者都好。

例句：He tried to outbake his grandmother, but her cookies were still the best. 他试图烘烤得比他祖母更好，但她的饼干仍然是最好的。

近义词：surpass, excel
反义词：fail, lose to
词性：v.
