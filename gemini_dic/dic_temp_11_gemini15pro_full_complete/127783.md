neighborliness:/ˈneɪbərlinəs/| n.|睦邻友好；友善；亲切|The spirit of neighborliness is strong in this community.  这个社区的睦邻友好精神很强。|例句：Their neighborliness was heartwarming. 他们的友善令人感到温暖。

例句：We appreciate your neighborliness in helping us move in. 我们感谢你们在搬家时给予的帮助。|近义词：friendliness, amity, sociability, kindliness|反义词：hostility, unfriendliness, coldness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From neighbor + -liness.  Ultimately from Old English *nēahgebūr (near-dweller).
