amaranthus:/ˌæməˈrænθəs/| n. |苋菜，不凋花|A genus of plants, typically having small greenish or reddish flowers, often growing in dense clusters. Some species are cultivated for their edible seeds or leaves, while others are considered weeds.  The flowers are often used in dried arrangements.|The amaranthus in her garden grew tall and vibrant. 她花园里的苋菜长得又高又鲜艳。|Synonyms: pigweed, love-lies-bleeding, tassel flower|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

amaranth:/ˈæməˌrænθ/| n. |苋菜;紫红色;不凋花|A purplish-red color; a kind of imaginary flower that never fades. It is also used to refer to the amaranthus plant.|The artist used a deep amaranth for the background of the painting. 艺术家用了深紫红色作为画的背景。|Synonyms: magenta, fuchsia, purplish-red|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
