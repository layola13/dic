gurings:/ˈɡʊərɪŋz/
n.
斜撑，斜系绳
Diagonal bracing members, often used in scaffolding or other structures to provide stability.
The gurings on the scaffolding prevented it from collapsing. 脚手架上的斜撑防止它倒塌。
近义词:braces, supports, stays
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "weakening elements")
词性:noun
