cockles:/ˈkɒkəlz (UK) / ˈkɑːkəlz (US)| n. |鸟蛤，蛤蜊；内心深处；感情|A type of small, edible shellfish, also used figuratively to refer to the deepest feelings of the heart.|例句：We gathered cockles at low tide. 我们在退潮时收集了鸟蛤。
