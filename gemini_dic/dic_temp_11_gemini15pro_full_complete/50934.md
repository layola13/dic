desideration:/dɪˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/ |n.| 渴望；愿望；要求 |A desideratum is something that is needed or wanted. |例句：The desideratum of the project is to create a more sustainable community.  这个项目的目标是创建一个更可持续的社区。

例句：A fast internet connection is a desideratum for many people working from home.  对于许多在家工作的人来说，快速的互联网连接是必需的。|近义词：desire, wish, want, need, requirement |反义词：indifference, apathy |词性: noun

词源: From Latin dēsīderātum, neuter singular of dēsīderātus, past participle of dēsīderāre (“to long for, desire greatly”), from the prefix dē- + sīderāre (“to long for, expect”), probably from sīdus, sīderis (“star, constellation”).
