baignoire:/bɛˈɲwaʁ/ (French pronunciation)| n.|浴缸|A bathtub.  From the French word for "bathtub." It's often used in English when referring to a freestanding, claw-footed, or otherwise elegant tub, often implying a luxurious context.|She soaked in the antique baignoire, surrounded by candles. 她泡在古董浴缸里，周围点着蜡烛。|Synonym: bathtub, tub|Antonym: shower|Noun
