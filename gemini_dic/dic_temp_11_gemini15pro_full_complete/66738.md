exorcism:/ˈɛksɔːrsɪzəm/ | n. |驱魔，驱邪 |The act of expelling evil spirits from a person, place, or thing. |The priest performed an exorcism on the possessed man.  神父对被附身的人进行了驱魔。| 近义词: purification, cleansing | 反义词: possession |词性:名词

exorcism:/ˈɛksɔːrsɪzəm/ | n. |驱魔，驱邪 |The religious or spiritual practice of evicting demons or other spiritual entities from a person, or an area that is believed to be haunted. |The family called in a shaman to perform an exorcism on their house. 这家人请了一位萨满来为他们的房子驱邪。| 近义词: deliverance | 反义词:  invocation |词性:名词
