fungilliform:/fʌŋˈdʒɪlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 菌状的，蘑菇形的 | Shaped like a mushroom or fungus; having a rounded cap and a stalk. | The fungilliform papillae on the tongue are responsible for taste. 舌头上的菌状乳头负责味觉。 | The mushroom had a distinct fungilliform shape.  这个蘑菇有一个明显的菌状形状。 | Near synonyms: mushroom-shaped, umbonate, pileate | Antonyms: flat, planar | Adjective
