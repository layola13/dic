myxomatoses:/ˌmɪksəʊməˈtəʊsiːz/
noun
粘液瘤病
A highly infectious and usually fatal viral disease of rabbits, characterized by the formation of swellings in the skin and mucous membranes.
Myxomatosis decimated the rabbit population in Australia.  粘液瘤病使澳大利亚的兔子数量锐减。
近义词: rabbit fever
反义词: (None, as it's a specific disease)
词性: noun


myxoma:/mɪkˈsəʊmə/
noun
粘液瘤
A benign tumor of connective tissue containing gelatinous or mucoid material.
Cardiac myxomas are the most common primary tumors of the heart. 心脏粘液瘤是最常见的心脏原发性肿瘤。

词源:  From Greek *myxa* meaning "mucus" + *-oma* meaning "tumor, swelling" (for myxoma). Myxomatosis is derived from myxoma + *-osis* indicating a disease or condition.
