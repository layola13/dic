appointed: /əˈpɔɪntɪd/ | adj. | 指定的；约定的；任命的 |  Chosen for a particular job or position; fixed or set by authority or arrangement. |  She arrived at the appointed time. 她按约定时间到达。| synonyms: designated, assigned, nominated, chosen, selected | antonyms: unappointed, elected, dismissed | adjective

appointed: /əˈpɔɪntɪd/ | v. | 任命；指定；约定 | To choose someone officially for a job or role; to decide on a time or place for something to happen. | The committee appointed a new chairman. 委员会任命了一位新主席。 | synonyms: designate, assign, nominate, choose, select, commission, ordain, install | antonyms: dismiss, discharge, fire, remove | verb
