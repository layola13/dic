irrigation:/ˌɪrɪˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 灌溉 | The artificial application of water to land to assist in the production of crops. |  The irrigation system brought water to the dry fields. 灌溉系统将水输送到干旱的田地。

The farm uses a sophisticated irrigation system. 这个农场使用先进的灌溉系统。


| Synonym: watering, sprinkling | Antonym: drainage, dehydration | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *irrigationem, from irrigare "to water, moisten," from in- "in" + rigare "to water, moisten."
