controversy:/ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrsi/ /kənˈtrɒvəsi/| n. |争议，争论，辩论| prolonged public disagreement or heated discussion.| The new law sparked a controversy.  这项新法律引发了争议。| synonyms: dispute, debate, argument, disagreement, contention, quarrel, wrangle, feud| antonyms: agreement, accord, harmony, consensus| noun

controversial:/ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃl/ /ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃl/| adj. |有争议的，引起争论的| giving rise or likely to give rise to public disagreement.| The book proved to be highly controversial. 这本书被证明是极具争议的。| synonyms: disputed, debatable, contentious, at issue, moot, vexed| antonyms: undisputed, uncontroversial, accepted| adjective
