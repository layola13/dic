mosaic:/moʊˈzeɪɪk/ (US), /məʊˈzeɪɪk/ (UK)| n. adj.| 马赛克，镶嵌画；混合物，综合体 |A picture or pattern produced by arranging together small colored pieces of hard material, such as stone, tile, or glass. Also, a combination of diverse elements forming a more complex whole. |例句：The artist created a beautiful mosaic using tiny pieces of colored glass. 艺术家用彩色玻璃碎片创作了一幅美丽的马赛克画。

|例句：The city is a mosaic of different cultures and ethnicities. 这座城市是不同文化和种族的融合体。|近义词：collage, patchwork, blend, mixture|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity|词性: 名词, 形容词
