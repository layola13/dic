bloviate: /ˈbloʊvieɪt/ (US) /ˈbləʊvieɪt/ (UK)| vi.|夸夸其谈；高谈阔论|To talk at length, especially in an inflated or empty way.|He bloviated for hours about his latest business venture. 他就他最新的商业冒险夸夸其谈了几个小时。|Synonyms: rant, rave, spout, declaim|Antonyms: be concise, listen, be quiet|Verb

bloviation: /ˌbloʊviˈeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌbləʊviˈeɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|夸夸其谈；空洞的言辞|The act of bloviating; pompous or empty talk.|His speech was full of bloviation and lacked substance. 他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，缺乏实质内容。|Synonyms: bombast, rhetoric, verbosity|Antonyms: conciseness, brevity|Noun
