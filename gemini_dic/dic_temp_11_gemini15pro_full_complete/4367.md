alack:/əˈlæk/| interj.|哎呀；唉|An exclamation of sorrow, regret, or surprise.  Expressing mild dismay or regret.|例句：Alack, the day is already over! 哎呀，一天已经过去了！|近义词：alas, oh dear, woe is me|反义词:hurray, yahoo|词性:感叹词
词源: From Middle English *a lak*, equivalent to *a* (on, in) + *lak* (loss, fault, blame). Ultimately from Old Norse *lak*.
