monadelphous:/ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfəs/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdɛlfəs/| adj.|单体雄蕊的|Having all the stamens united into a single bundle or tube, as in many mallows.|Example: The hibiscus flower exhibits a monadelphous arrangement of stamens.  木槿花呈现出单体雄蕊的排列。|Synonym:  united stamens|Antonym:  (no single antonym, consider "free stamens" or "polyadelphous")|Part of Speech: Adjective


monadelphia:/ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfiə/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdɛlfiə/| n.|单体雄蕊|A botanical class in the Linnaean system characterized by flowers having monadelphous stamens.|Example:  Plants in the class Monadelphia share the characteristic of united stamens. 单体雄蕊纲的植物具有雄蕊联合的特征。|Synonym: (No direct synonym, refers to a specific Linnaean classification)|Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider "Diadelphia," "Polyadelphia," or other Linnaean classes)|Part of Speech: Noun
