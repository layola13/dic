fatherless:/ˈfɑːðərləs/| adj.|无父的，失去父亲的|Without a father.  This describes someone who has lost their father, typically through death, or who has never known their father.|The fatherless boy was taken in by his grandparents.  那个失去父亲的男孩被他的祖父母收养了。|He grew up fatherless in a small town. 他在一个小镇上无父地长大。|近义词：orphaned, parentless|反义词：fathered|词性:形容词
