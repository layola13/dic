gloom:/ɡluːm/| n.  |忧郁，阴暗，昏暗|A state of partial or total darkness; dimness; obscurity.  A feeling of depression or despondency.|The room was filled with gloom after the news.  房间里弥漫着消息传来后的忧郁气氛。|近义词：darkness, dimness, sadness, melancholy, despair|反义词：brightness, light, happiness, cheer|词性:noun

gloomy:/ˈɡluːmi/| adj. |黑暗的，阴沉的，忧郁的|Characterized by gloom; dimly lit; depressing or dismal.|The gloomy weather reflected his mood. 阴沉的天气反映了他的心情。|近义词：dark, dim, depressing, sad, pessimistic|反义词：bright, cheerful, optimistic, happy|词性:adjective
