misorganizes:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪz/
verb
组织混乱，管理不善
To organize something badly or inefficiently.
He misorganizes his desk so often that he can never find anything. 他经常把桌子弄得乱七八糟，什么也找不到。
The event was misorganized, resulting in chaos and confusion.  这次活动组织得很糟糕，导致一片混乱。
mismanage, disorganize
organize, systematize
词性: 动词
