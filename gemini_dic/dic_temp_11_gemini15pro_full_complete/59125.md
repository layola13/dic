dynamism:/ˈdaɪnəmɪzəm/ | n. | 活力，精力，劲头；动态，推动力 |  The quality of being characterized by vigorous activity and progress.  |  The dynamism of the city is attracting young people.  这座城市的活力吸引着年轻人。 | synonyms: energy, vigor, vitality, spirit, drive,  | antonyms: lethargy, inactivity, inertia, stagnation | noun

dynamical:/daɪˈnæmɪkl/ | adj. | 动态的，有活力的 | Characterized by continuous change, activity, or progress. | The Earth's climate is a dynamical system. 地球的气候是一个动态系统。 | synonyms: active, energetic, vigorous, robust  | antonyms: static, inert, passive | adjective
