haemocytotrypsis:/ˌhiːməsaɪˈtɒtrɪpsɪs/ | n. | 血细胞破裂；血细胞破碎 | The destruction of blood cells.  This can occur in various conditions, including some types of anemia. |  Haemocytotrypsis can lead to a decrease in red blood cells. 血细胞破裂会导致红细胞减少。|  Hemolysis, erythrocytolysis |  Haemocytogenesis | noun
