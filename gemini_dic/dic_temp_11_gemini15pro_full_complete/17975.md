befriendment:/bɪˈfrɛndmənt/
n.
交友，友好, 友好行为
the act of becoming friends with someone.
例句：Her befriendment of the lonely child was a kind gesture. 她与那个孤独的孩子交朋友是一个善意的举动。

例句：The befriendment program at the senior center helps combat loneliness.  老年中心的交友项目有助于对抗孤独。

近义词：friendship, companionship, amity
反义词：estrangement, alienation

词性: 名词

词源:  From befriend + -ment.  Befriend comes from the Old English word *befrēondan, meaning "to make a friend of".
