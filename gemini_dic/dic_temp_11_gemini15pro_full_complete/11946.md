arson:/ˈɑːrsən/ (US), /ˈɑːsən/ (UK)| n. |纵火(罪), 恶意纵火|The act of intentionally and maliciously setting fire to property.|例句：The fire was determined to be arson. 这场火灾被认定是人为纵火。

例句：He was charged with arson after burning down his neighbor's garage. 他因烧毁邻居的车库而被指控纵火罪。|近义词：incendiarism, firebombing|反义词:firefighting, fire prevention|词性:noun
