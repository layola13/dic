lonesomely:/ˈloʊnsəmli/ | adv. | 孤独地，寂寞地 | In a lonely manner; all alone. Feeling or experiencing loneliness. |  She sat lonesomely by the window, watching the rain. 她孤独地坐在窗边，看着雨。|  He wandered lonesomely through the empty streets. 他寂寞地在空旷的街道上徘徊。 | synonyms: solitarily, alone, forlornly | antonyms: together, companionably | adverb
