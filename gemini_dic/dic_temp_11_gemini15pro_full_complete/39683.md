considerable:/kənˈsɪdərəbl/| adj.|相当大的，相当多的，值得考虑的|Large in size, amount, or extent; significant; worthy of consideration.|例句：They invested a considerable amount of money in the project. 他们在该项目中投入了大量资金。|例句：The new policy has had a considerable impact on the economy. 新政策对经济产生了相当大的影响。|近义词：substantial, significant, sizable, large, ample|反义词：inconsiderable, insignificant, small, minor|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *considerabilis, from Latin considerare (“to consider”), from com- (“together”) + sidus (“star, constellation”). Originally meant "worthy of consideration, important."
