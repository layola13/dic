amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. |两栖动物，水陆两栖的|An animal that lives both on land and in water, such as a frog or newt.|The frog is an amphibian. 青蛙是两栖动物。|近义词：frog, toad, newt, salamander|反义词：mammal, reptile, bird, fish|noun, adjective

amphibious:/æmˈfɪbiəs/ | adj. |两栖的，水陆两用的|Relating to, living in, or suited for both land and water.|The Marines launched an amphibious assault.  海军陆战队发动了两栖攻击。|近义词：aquatic, semiaquatic|反义词：terrestrial|adjective
