biryanis:/bɪrˈjɑːniz/
noun
印度香饭，什锦饭
A mixed rice dish with meat, fish, or vegetables and spices, originating from the Indian subcontinent.
We ordered chicken biryanis for dinner. 我们晚餐点了鸡肉香饭。

近义词:pulao, pilaf
反义词:(No direct antonym, potentially plain rice)
词性:n.
