iodocresol: /ˌaɪoʊdoʊˈkriːsɒl/ (US), /ˌaɪəʊdəʊˈkriːsɒl/ (UK)| n. |碘甲酚|A chemical compound derived from cresol by the introduction of iodine. It is used as an antiseptic.| Iodocresol is commonly used in some throat lozenges. 碘甲酚通常用于一些喉咙含片中。|Synonyms: antiseptic, disinfectant|Antonyms: contaminant, pollutant|noun
