khaleesi:/kɑːˈliːsi/
n.
女王，卡丽熙 (Dothraki language)
A title of respect for a Dothraki warlord's wife in the fictional world of "A Song of Ice and Fire" and the television series "Game of Thrones."  It is roughly equivalent to "queen."
Daenerys Targaryen is often referred to as Khaleesi. 丹妮莉丝·坦格利安经常被称为卡丽熙。
The khaleesi rode her dragon into battle. 卡丽熙骑着她的龙投入战斗。

近义词:queen, ruler
反义词:subject, servant
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Dothraki language, constructed by George R.R. Martin for his "A Song of Ice and Fire" series.  Derived from "khal," meaning warlord.
