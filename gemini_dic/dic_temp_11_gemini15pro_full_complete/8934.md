antihierarchy:/ˈæntiˈhaɪərɑːrki/
noun| 反等级制度；反等级结构|Opposition to or rejection of hierarchy, especially in social or political structures.  It emphasizes equality and shared power.|The antihierarchy movement within the company sought to empower employees at all levels.|synonyms: egalitarianism, non-hierarchical structure, horizontal organization |antonyms: hierarchy, hierarchical structure, vertical organization|n.


antihierarchy:/ˈæntiˈhaɪərɑːrki/
adjective|反等级制度的|Characterized by opposition to hierarchy.|The antihierarchy nature of the online community fostered open communication and collaboration.|synonyms: egalitarian, non-hierarchical, horizontal |antonyms: hierarchical, vertical|adj.
