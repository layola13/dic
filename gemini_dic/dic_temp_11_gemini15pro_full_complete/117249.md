mechanic: /məˈkænɪk/ (US) /mɪˈkænɪk/ (UK)| n.| 机械师，技工 | A person skilled in the repair and maintenance of machinery, especially vehicles. |例句：The mechanic quickly diagnosed the problem with my car.  机械师很快就诊断出了我汽车的问题。

|例句：I took my bike to the mechanic to get it fixed. 我把自行车送去给机械师修理。
|近义词：technician, repairman, engineer, fitter |反义词: amateur, novice|词性: 名词


mechanic: /məˈkænɪkəl/ (US) /mɪˈkænɪkəl/ (UK)| adj.| 机械的，力学的| Relating to machinery or mechanics. | 例句：The car had a mechanical failure. 这辆车发生了机械故障。

|例句：She has a good understanding of mechanical principles. 她对力学原理有很好的理解。
|近义词：automated, motorized, robotic|反义词: manual, electronic|词性: 形容词
