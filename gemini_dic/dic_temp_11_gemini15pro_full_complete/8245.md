anti-indemnity:/ˌæntiɪnˈdɛmnɪti/ | adj. | 反赔偿的，反保障的 | Relating to a clause or provision that prohibits indemnification or compensation for loss or damage. |  The contract included an anti-indemnity clause, preventing either party from seeking compensation from the other.  合同中包含反赔偿条款，防止任何一方向另一方寻求赔偿。|  The anti-indemnity provision protected the company from liability. 反赔偿条款保护公司免于承担责任。| 近义词: non-indemnity,  exculpatory | 反义词: indemnity, compensatory | 词性: adjective
