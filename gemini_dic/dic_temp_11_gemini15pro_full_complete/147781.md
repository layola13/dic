overpromptly:/ˌōvərˈprɒmptˌli/ /ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmptˌli/ | adv. | 过于迅速地，过分及时地 |  Done with excessive or undue promptness.  | He replied overpromptly, making it seem like he was hiding something. 他回答得太快了，让人觉得他好像在隐瞒什么。 |  He overpromptly agreed to her request, raising her suspicions. 他答应她的请求太快了，引起了她的怀疑。 | 近义词：too quickly, hastily, prematurely | 反义词：slowly, tardily, late | 词性: 副词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + promptly (adverb meaning "without delay")
