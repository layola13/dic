hyperfastidious:/ˌhaɪpərˈfæˈstɪdiəs/| adj.| 过分讲究的，过分挑剔的 | Excessively meticulous or demanding; overly concerned with minor details or imperfections.  Characterized by excessive attention to detail and a tendency to find fault. |例句：He was hyperfastidious about his appearance, always ensuring his suit was perfectly pressed. 他对自己的外表极其挑剔，总是确保他的西装熨烫得一丝不苟。

例句：The hyperfastidious chef rejected any dish with the slightest imperfection. 这位过分讲究的厨师会拒绝任何有一点点瑕疵的菜肴。|近义词：fussy, meticulous, finicky, exacting, punctilious|反义词：careless, slovenly, uncritical, tolerant|词性: adjective

词源:
From hyper- "excessive" + fastidious "disgusted, squeamish, demanding."  Ultimately from Latin *fastus meaning "disdain, contempt"
