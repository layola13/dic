deductibilities:/dɪˌdʌktɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
可扣除额；可扣除项目
The quality or state of being deductible, especially from taxable income.  Amounts that can be deducted.
Many taxpayers are unaware of all the deductibilities available to them. 许多纳税人并不知道他们可以享受的所有扣除项目。
The new tax law expands the deductibilities for charitable donations. 新的税法扩大了慈善捐赠的扣除范围。
synonyms: deductions, allowances, write-offs
antonyms: non-deductible expenses, inclusions
