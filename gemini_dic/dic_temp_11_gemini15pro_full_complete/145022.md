overaffect:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɛkt/ | v. | 过分做作；装腔作势 | To display excessive or exaggerated emotion; to feign affection. | She overaffected a British accent, which sounded quite comical. 她刻意模仿英国口音，听起来很滑稽。|  He overaffected his grief, hoping to gain sympathy. 他装腔作势地悲伤，希望获得同情。 | 近义词：exaggerate, feign, pretend | 反义词：understate, downplay | 词性：动词

Etymology:
From over- + affect, from Latin affectus, past participle of afficere “to influence, act on,” from ad- “to” + facere “to do, make.”
