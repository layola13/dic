gorfly:/ˈɡɔːrflaɪ/| n.|牛虻；令人讨厌的人|A gorfly is a type of fly that bites livestock.  Figuratively, it refers to someone who persistently annoys or criticizes others in order to stimulate them into action.|The journalist was a gorfly to the corrupt politicians, constantly exposing their misdeeds.|近义词：gadfly, nuisance, pest, irritant|反义词:supporter, ally|noun
