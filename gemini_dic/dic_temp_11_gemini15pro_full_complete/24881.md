brucelloses:/bruːsəˈloʊsiːz/| n. |布鲁氏菌病|An infectious bacterial disease caused by bacteria of the genus Brucella, typically affecting cattle, swine, goats, sheep, and sometimes humans, characterized by recurrent fever, chills, sweating, headache, and muscular pain.|The farmer was concerned about brucelloses in his herd.  这位农民担心他的牛群感染布鲁氏菌病。|近义词:Malta fever, undulant fever, Mediterranean fever|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease.  One could consider "health" a broad antonym.)|词性:noun
