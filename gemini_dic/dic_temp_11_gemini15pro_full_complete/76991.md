gardenful: /ˈɡɑːrdənˌfʊl/ | n. | 一花园; 满园; 花园里的全部 | A gardenful is the amount that a garden can hold or the contents of a garden. |  The children picked a gardenful of strawberries. 孩子们摘了一花园的草莓。|  She brought in a gardenful of flowers. 她带进来满满一花园的花。| synonyms: abundance, profusion | antonyms: scarcity, lack | noun
