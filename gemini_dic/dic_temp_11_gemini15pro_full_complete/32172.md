chi's: /tʃiːz/
noun
气 (生命能量); 气功
(in Chinese thought) the circulating life energy that is believed to be inherent in all things; Qigong

Chi is a concept in traditional Chinese medicine and philosophy. It is believed to be a vital force that flows through the body and is essential for health and well-being.  Qigong is a system of coordinated body posture and movement, breathing, and meditation used for health, spirituality, and martial arts training.

例句：
He believes in the power of chi.
他相信气的力量。

She practices qigong every morning.
她每天早上练气功。


近义词: life force, vital energy, ki (Japanese), prana (Sanskrit)
反义词:  None (Chi represents a life force; there isn't a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
