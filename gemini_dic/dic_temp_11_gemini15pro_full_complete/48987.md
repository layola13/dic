deglaciation:/ˌdiːˌɡleɪsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |冰川消退，冰消期 |The gradual melting of glaciers and ice sheets, resulting in a rise in sea levels. | The deglaciation following the last ice age significantly altered the landscape.  上次冰河时代后的冰川消融极大地改变了地貌。| 近义词: glacial retreat, ice melt | 反义词: glaciation | 词性: 名词

deglaciation:/ˌdiːˌɡleɪsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |去冰作用，除冰 |The process of removing ice, especially from a road or aircraft. | Deglaciation of the aircraft's wings was necessary before takeoff.  起飞前必须清除飞机机翼上的冰。| 近义词: deicing | 反义词: icing, freezing | 词性: 名词
