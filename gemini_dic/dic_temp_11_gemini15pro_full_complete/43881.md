crestfallenness:/ˌkrɛstˈfɔːlənɪs/| n. |沮丧，垂头丧气|The state of being crestfallen; discouragement and dejection.|She couldn't hide her crestfallenness after losing the competition. 她输掉比赛后无法掩饰自己的沮丧。|Synonyms: dejection, despondency, discouragement, downheartedness|Antonyms: elation, joy, happiness|noun

crestfallen:/ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlən/| adj. |沮丧的，垂头丧气的|Sad and disappointed.|He looked crestfallen when he heard the bad news. 他听到坏消息后看起来很沮丧。|Synonyms: dejected, despondent, discouraged, downhearted|Antonyms: elated, joyful, happy|adjective
