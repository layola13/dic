cacklers:/ˈkæklərz/| n.|咯咯叫的人/动物（尤指鹅、母鸡）; 喋喋不休的人|People or animals, especially geese or hens, that cackle; also, people who talk incessantly and foolishly.|The cacklers in the barnyard announced the arrival of a fox.|近义词：gossips, chatterboxes, gabbers|反义词:quiet people, silent types|词性:noun
