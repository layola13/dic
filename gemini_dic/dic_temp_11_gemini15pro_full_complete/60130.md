effectively:/ɪˈfɛktɪvli/ /əˈfɛktɪvli/ | adv. |有效地，生效地|In a way that produces a desired result or effect.|例句：The new policy effectively reduced traffic congestion. 新政策有效地减少了交通拥堵。|近义词：efficiently, successfully, productively|反义词：ineffectively, inefficiently, unsuccessfully|词性:副词

effectively:/ɪˈfɛktɪvli/ /əˈfɛktɪvli/ | adv. |实际上，事实上|Used to indicate that something is true in practice, even if it may not be officially or legally so.|例句：He effectively runs the company, although his title is only Vice President.  虽然他的头衔只是副总裁，但他实际上在经营这家公司。|近义词：virtually, practically, essentially|反义词：nominally, theoretically|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *effectivus, from Latin effectus (“effect, result”), past participle of efficere (“to work out, accomplish”), from ex- (“out”) + facere (“to do, to make”).
