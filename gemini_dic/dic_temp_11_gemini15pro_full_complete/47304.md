de-integration:/diːˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃn/
n.|去整合，分解，拆分|The process of separating things that were previously integrated, often referring to systems or organizations.|The de-integration of the company led to several smaller, independent entities.  公司的去整合导致了几个更小、独立的实体。|disintegration, separation, breakup|integration, unification|noun
de-integration:/diːˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃn/
n.|反向整合，逆向整合|In business, the process of separating previously combined business functions or operations.|The company chose de-integration to focus on its core competencies. 公司选择反向整合以专注于其核心竞争力。|divestiture, decoupling|integration, vertical integration|noun
