averment:/əˈvɜːrmənt/
n.
断言，声明，主张
A positive statement or declaration, especially one made in pleading a cause.
The lawyer's averment was crucial to the case.  律师的声明对案件至关重要。

近义词：assertion, affirmation, declaration, statement, claim
反义词：denial, refutation, contradiction


词性: noun

词源: From Old French *averement (Modern French avèrement), from averer "to avow, to assert," from Latin advertere "to turn to," from ad- "to" + vertere "to turn."
