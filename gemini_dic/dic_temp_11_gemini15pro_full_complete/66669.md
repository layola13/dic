exocetus:/ˌɛksəˈsiːtəs/ | n. |飞鱼|A fish of the family Exocoetidae, found in warm seas, that can glide for short distances above the surface of the water using its long, winglike pectoral fins.|The exocetus glided above the waves. 飞鱼滑翔在波浪之上。|近义词：flying fish|反义词: - |词性:noun
