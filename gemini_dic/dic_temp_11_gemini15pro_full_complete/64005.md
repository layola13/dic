epistolography:/ɪˌpɪstəˈlɒɡrəfi/
n.
书信学;书信集
The study of letters, especially their authorship, date, authenticity, etc.  The collection of letters.
His research in epistolography revealed the true author of the anonymous letters. 他的书信学研究揭示了匿名信的真正作者。
