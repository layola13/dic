longbeak:/ˈlɒŋbiːk/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋbiːk/ (US)| n. |长喙鸟；长嘴鸟|A bird with a long beak.  A longbeak is a bird characterized by its long beak, which it uses for various purposes such as feeding, preening, and nest building.|例句：The longbeak probed the flower for nectar. 长喙鸟探寻花朵中的花蜜。

例句：The longbeak used its long beak to catch fish.  长喙鸟用它的长喙捕鱼。|近义词：wader, shorebird|反义词: N/A (No specific antonym for a physical characteristic)|词性:noun
