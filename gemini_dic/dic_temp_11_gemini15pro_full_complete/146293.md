overfattened: /ˌōvərˈfætnd/ | adj. | 过度肥胖的；肥育过度的 | Excessively fattened; fattened beyond what is considered healthy or desirable.  Made excessively fat, typically by overfeeding. | The farmer sold his overfattened pigs at the market.  农民在市场上卖掉了他的肥猪。|  The overfattened goose was barely able to waddle.  这只肥胖的鹅几乎走不动了。 | 近义词: obese, corpulent, overweight | 反义词: lean, thin, underweight | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
over- (prefix): Old English ofer, of German origin, related to over, meaning "above, beyond, across."
fatten (verb): Middle English fatten, from Old English fættan "to make fat," from fætt "fat."
-ed (suffix): Forms the past tense and past participle of many verbs.
