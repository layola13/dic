mipedes: /ˈmaɪpiːdz/ | n. | 千足虫; 马陆 | Millipedes are arthropods with two pairs of legs per body segment. | Example: The millipede curled up into a tight spiral when I touched it. 我碰到它的时候，千足虫蜷缩成了一个紧密的螺旋形。| Example:  Millipedes are often found in damp, dark places. 千足虫经常在潮湿阴暗的地方被发现。 | Synonyms: diplopod, thousand-legger | Antonyms: None | Noun
