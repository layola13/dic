bet-bottom-dollar:/bet ˈbɑːtəm ˈdɑːlər/ /bet ˈbɒtəm ˈdɒlə(r)/| idiom | 确信无疑，毫无疑问 | To be completely certain of something.  It implies a high degree of confidence. | I bet my bottom dollar that he'll win the election. 我确信他会赢得选举。 | synonyms: be sure, be certain, be positive, be convinced, have no doubt | antonyms: be unsure, be uncertain, doubt, question | idiom

bet-bottom-dollar:/bet ˈbɑːtəm ˈdɑːlər/ /bet ˈbɒtəm ˈdɒlə(r)/| idiom | 赌上全部家当，孤注一掷 | To risk everything on something. (Less common usage) | He bet his bottom dollar on that horse race and lost everything. 他在那场赛马中赌上了全部家当，结果输光了。| synonyms: risk everything, gamble everything, go all in | antonyms: play it safe, be cautious | idiom
