mudflats:/ˈmʌdflæts/ | n. |泥滩，潮滩|A stretch of muddy land left uncovered at low tide.|We walked across the mudflats at low tide, searching for shellfish. 我们在退潮时走过泥滩，寻找贝类。|Synonyms: tidelands, flats, salt marsh, slough|Antonyms: highlands, uplands|noun

mudflats:/ˈmʌdflæts/ | n. |泥滩，潮滩|Areas of soft, wet mud found near bodies of water, especially along coasts, that are exposed at low tide.|The mudflats are an important habitat for many species of birds.  泥滩是许多鸟类重要的栖息地。|Synonyms:  marshland, wetlands,  swamps|Antonyms: mountains, deserts|noun
