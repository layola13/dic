overperforming:/ˌoʊvərˈpərˌfɔːrmɪŋ/ /ˌəʊvəˈpəˈfɔːmɪŋ/| adj.|表现优异的，超出预期的|Performing better than expected or above the required standard.|The company has been consistently overperforming in the last few quarters.  这家公司在过去几个季度一直表现优异。|Synonyms: excelling, thriving, flourishing, outperforming|Antonyms: underperforming, failing, lagging|adjective

overperform:/ˌoʊvərˈpərˌfɔːrm/ /ˌəʊvəˈpəˈfɔːm/| v.|表现优异，超出预期|To perform better than expected or above the required standard.|The company overperformed in the last quarter, exceeding its sales targets. 这家公司上个季度表现优异，超出了销售目标。|Synonyms: excel, thrive, flourish, outperform|Antonyms: underperform, fail, lag|verb
