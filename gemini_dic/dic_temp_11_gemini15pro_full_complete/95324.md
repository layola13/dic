incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ | n. adj.|燃烧弹；煽动者；煽动性的|Something designed to cause fires, or someone who stirs up trouble or violence.  Figuratively, it can refer to something that inflames emotions or provokes strong reactions.|The arsonist used an incendiary device to start the fire. 纵火犯使用燃烧装置来纵火。|Synonyms: inflammatory, provocative, combustible, rabble-rousing|Antonyms: calming, soothing, peaceful, conciliatory|noun
incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ | adj.|煽动性的；放火的；燃烧的|Tending to stir up conflict or strong emotions; relating to or involving arson.|Her incendiary remarks caused a riot. 她煽动性的话引发了骚乱。|Synonyms: inflammatory, provocative, rabble-rousing|Antonyms: calming, soothing, peaceful, conciliatory|adjective
