cellulite: /ˈsɛljʊlaɪt/| n. |脂肪团，橘皮组织|A local unevenness of the skin, especially on women's thighs and buttocks, caused by fat deposits pressing against connective tissue.| Her cellulite was becoming more noticeable as she gained weight. 她的脂肪团随着体重增加变得越来越明显了。|Synonyms: orange peel, cottage cheese skin| Antonyms: smooth skin| Noun
