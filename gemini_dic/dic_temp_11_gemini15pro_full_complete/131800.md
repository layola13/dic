nonconscription:/ˌnɒnkənˈskrɪpʃən/ |n.| 非征兵制，免除兵役 |The country's policy of nonconscription meant a smaller standing army.  这个国家的非征兵制政策意味着常备军规模较小。|  Nonconscription is a system where military service is not compulsory. 非征兵制是指兵役非强制性的制度。| Example: The country adopted a policy of nonconscription after the war. 战后，这个国家采取了非征兵制政策。  The principle of nonconscription is central to their pacifist beliefs. 非征兵制原则是他们和平主义信仰的核心。|Synonym: exemption from military service, voluntary enlistment|Antonym: conscription, draft|noun
