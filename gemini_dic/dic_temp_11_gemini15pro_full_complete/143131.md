outcarols:/ˌaʊtˈkærəl/ | v. |以更高的声音唱圣诞颂歌;在唱圣诞颂歌方面胜过|To sing Christmas carols more loudly or better than someone else. |例句：The children tried to outcarol each other on Christmas Eve.  平安夜，孩子们试图比彼此唱圣诞颂歌的声音更大。

例句：She outcaroled everyone in the church choir with her beautiful soprano voice. 她用她优美的女高音在唱诗班里胜过了所有人。|近义词：outsing|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps be outsung) |词性:动词
