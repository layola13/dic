censor:/ˈsensər/ (US) /ˈsensə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|审查员，检查员；审查，检查|A person who examines books, films, etc., and removes anything considered offensive or harmful; to examine books, films, etc., and remove anything considered offensive or harmful.|The government censored the news reports before they were released. 政府在新闻报道发布前进行了审查。|近义词：examine, expurgate, redact, suppress|反义词：permit, allow, approve|词性: 名词, 动词
censor:/ˈsensər/ (US) /ˈsensə(r)/ (UK)| v.|审查，检查|To examine books, films, etc., and remove anything considered offensive or harmful.|They censored the letter before sending it. 他们在寄信前审查了信件。|近义词：examine, expurgate, redact, suppress|反义词：permit, allow, approve|词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cēnsor*, agent noun from *cēnsēre* "to assess, judge, or value," originally "to register."
