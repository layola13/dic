manito:/məˈniːtoʊ/| n.|密友，知己；(尤指青少年间的)秘密朋友；(在某些菲律宾土著文化中)护身符，神灵|A close friend or confidant; a term of endearment, especially among teenagers; (in some indigenous Filipino cultures) an amulet or spirit. |She's my manito, we tell each other everything. 她是我最好的朋友，我们无话不谈。|近义词：best friend, confidant, soulmate, BFF|反义词：enemy, foe|noun
