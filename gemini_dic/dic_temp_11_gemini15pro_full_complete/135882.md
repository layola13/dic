nonprovincial:/ˌnɑːnprəˈvɪnʃəl/ | adj. | 非狭隘的；见识广的；不局限于地方的 | Not limited to or characteristic of a province; sophisticated and worldly. |  She had a nonprovincial outlook on life, embracing diverse cultures and ideas. 她对生活有着非狭隘的眼光，包容不同的文化和思想。

Her nonprovincial tastes in art were reflected in her eclectic collection. 她对艺术的品味并不局限于地方特色，这体现在她兼收并蓄的收藏中。


近义词: cosmopolitan, sophisticated, worldly, broad-minded, open-minded | 反义词: provincial, narrow-minded, parochial, insular | 词性: adjective
