fay:/feɪ/
n.|仙女，小精灵|A fairy or elf; a mythical creature of folklore, often depicted as having magical powers.|She danced with the grace of a fay. 她像仙女一样优雅地跳舞。|近义词：fairy, elf, sprite, pixie|反义词:giant, ogre|词性:名词
fay:/feɪ/
v.|（古语）注定；赐予；清洁|To cleanse; to doom; to bestow.|His fate was fayed from the beginning. 他的命运从一开始就注定了。 (archaic usage, rarely used in modern English) |近义词：destine, ordain, decree (for "doom"); grant, bestow (for "bestow"); cleanse, purify (for "cleanse")|反义词:bless (for doom), withhold (for bestow), contaminate (for cleanse) |词性:动词
