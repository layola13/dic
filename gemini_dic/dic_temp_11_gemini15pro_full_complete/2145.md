adaunt:/əˈdɔːnt/| v. | 使气馁，使畏惧，使胆怯 | To make someone feel intimidated or discouraged. |  The sheer size of the task ahead adaunted her.  她被面前任务的艰巨性吓倒了。

The harsh criticism from the judges adaunted the young performer.  评委的严厉批评使这位年轻的表演者感到气馁。

| 近义词:intimidate, discourage, dishearten, dismay | 反义词:encourage, embolden, hearten | 词性:动词
