culmiferous:/kʌlˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
有茎的；产生茎的；禾本科的
Bearing culms or stems, especially those bearing grains.
The culmiferous plants, such as wheat and rice, are essential for human survival.  这些有茎的植物，例如小麦和水稻，对人类的生存至关重要。
Synonyms:  stemmed, stalky, gramineous
Antonyms:  acaulescent, stemless
