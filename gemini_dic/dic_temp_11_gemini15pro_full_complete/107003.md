lacrymatory:/ˈlækrɪməˌtɔːri/ /ˈlækrɪməˌtəri/
adjective|催泪的，引人落泪的|Causing tears; tearful.|The onion's lacrymatory effect made everyone in the kitchen cry. 洋葱的催泪效果让厨房里的每个人都哭了。|
lacrymatory:/ˈlækrɪməˌtɔːri/ /ˈlækrɪməˌtəri/
noun|催泪瓦斯，催泪弹|A substance that irritates the eyes and causes tears, such as tear gas.|The police used lacrymatory agents to disperse the crowd. 警方使用催泪瓦斯驱散人群。|
近义词:tear-inducing, tearful, weeping|
反义词:joyful, happy|


词性: adjective, noun
词源: From Latin *lacrimatorius, from lacrima (“tear”).
