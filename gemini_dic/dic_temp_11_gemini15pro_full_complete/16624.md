barong: /ˈbɑːrɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɑːrɔːŋ/ (US) | n. | 巴隆舞服；巴隆舞 | A traditional embroidered formal shirt worn by men in parts of the Philippines, especially during ceremonies and festivals. It is often highly decorated and considered a symbol of Filipino national dress. |  He wore a beautiful barong to the wedding.  他穿着一件漂亮的巴隆舞服参加婚礼。 |  The barong is a source of national pride. 巴隆舞服是民族自豪感的来源。| synonyms: formal shirt, ceremonial attire | antonyms: casual wear | noun
