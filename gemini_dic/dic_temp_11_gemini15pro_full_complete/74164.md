fountainhead:/ˈfaʊntɪnˌhed/| n.|源头，根源，发源地|A source, origin, or beginning, especially of a stream or river; a primary source of something.|例句：The novel traces the protagonist's journey back to the fountainhead of his inspiration. 这部小说追溯了主人公回到他灵感源头的旅程。

例句：The fountainhead of the river is high in the mountains. 这条河的源头在高山上。|近义词：source, origin, root, wellspring, beginning|反义词：end, conclusion, outcome|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fountainhed, *funtaynehed, equivalent to fountain +‎ head.  Ultimately from Latin fontāna (“spring, fountain”).
