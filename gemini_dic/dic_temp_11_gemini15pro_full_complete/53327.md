disaccommodation:/ˌdɪsəˌkɑːməˈdeɪʃən/
n.|失调；不适应；不配合；(眼睛)调节障碍|The process of losing a previously established accommodation; the inability of the eyes to adjust their focus.  This can manifest in various ways, such as difficulty focusing on near or far objects. |The elderly man experienced disaccommodation of his eyes, making it hard for him to read.  这位老人经历了眼睛调节障碍，使他难以阅读。|Near synonyms: maladaptation, imbalance, dysregulation|Near antonyms: adaptation, adjustment, accommodation|noun


disaccommodation:/ˌdɪsəˌkɑːməˈdeɪʃən/
n.|（社会学）对立；不和|A sociological term referring to a process of social distancing and opposition between groups, marked by a decline in cooperation and increased conflict. |The disaccommodation between the two communities led to increased tension and even violence. 这两个社区之间的对立导致紧张局势加剧，甚至出现暴力。|Near synonyms: discord, conflict, antagonism|Near antonyms: cooperation, harmony, agreement|noun
