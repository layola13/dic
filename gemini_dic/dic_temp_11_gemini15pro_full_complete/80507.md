good-natured:/ˌɡʊdˈneɪtʃərd/ | adj. | 温和的；和蔼的；心胸宽大的 | Having or showing a pleasant and kind disposition. | She's a good-natured girl, always willing to help. 她是一个温和的女孩，总是乐于助人。|  He responded to their teasing with good-natured humor. 他用和蔼的幽默回应了他们的戏弄。 | 近义词: amiable, affable, friendly, kind, pleasant | 反义词: ill-natured, bad-tempered, grumpy, irritable | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "good" + "natured". "Nature" in this context refers to one's inherent character or disposition. The word dates back to the late 16th century.
