conterraneous:/ˌkɒntəˈreɪniəs/ /ˌkɑːntəˈreɪniəs/
adjective
同时代的；同地区的
Existing or occurring at the same time; contemporary.  Living or existing in the same land or territory.
The conterraneous extinction of dinosaurs and other species suggests a common cause, like an asteroid impact.  恐龙和其他物种的同时灭绝表明了一个共同的原因，例如小行星撞击。
Shakespeare and Marlowe were conterraneous playwrights.  莎士比亚和马洛是同时代的剧作家。
contemporary, contemporaneous, coeval, synchronous
antediluvian, prehistoric
adjective
