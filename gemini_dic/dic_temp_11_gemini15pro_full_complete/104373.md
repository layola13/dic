kaba:/ˈkɑːbə/ | n. | 卡巴, 一种非洲长袍, 也指一种在匈牙利流行的辣椒粉 | A long, flowing robe worn in some African countries, also a type of paprika popular in Hungary. |  She wore a brightly colored kaba to the festival. 她穿着鲜艳的卡巴去参加节日。| The chef used kaba to add a smoky flavor to the dish. 厨师用卡巴给菜肴增添烟熏味。 | 近义词: robe, gown (for the garment), paprika (for the spice) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

kaba:/ˈkɑːbə/ | adj. |  (辣椒粉)辣的, 呛人的 | (of paprika) hot, pungent | The kaba was so strong it made me cough.  这卡巴太辣了，呛得我咳嗽。| This dish needs more kaba for a spicier kick. 这道菜需要更多卡巴来增加辣味。| 近义词: spicy, hot, pungent | 反义词: mild, bland | 词性: adjective
