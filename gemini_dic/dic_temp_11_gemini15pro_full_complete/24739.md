Also, inclined to sit on eggs to hatch them.| She gave him her broodest look. 她给了他她最忧郁的一瞥。| 近义词：gloomiest, sullest, most contemplative, most thoughtful| 反义词：cheeriest, happiest, most carefree| 词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *brōd "sitting on eggs", from brōd "brood, offspring".
