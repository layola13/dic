hero-worshipping:/ˈhɪəroʊ ˈwɜːrʃɪpɪŋ/
adjective
崇拜英雄的；英雄崇拜的
Excessively admiring someone.  Characterized by excessive admiration for someone, often treating them as a hero.
例句：He has a hero-worshipping attitude towards his favorite athlete.  他对最喜欢的运动员有一种英雄崇拜的态度。

例句：She's known for her hero-worshipping tendencies, especially towards rock stars. 她以英雄崇拜倾向而闻名，尤其是对摇滚明星。

近义词：idolizing, admiring, venerating
反义词：critical, skeptical, cynical
词性: adjective
