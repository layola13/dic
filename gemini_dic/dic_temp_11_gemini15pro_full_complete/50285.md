depictive:/dɪˈpɪktɪv/| adj.|描绘的，描述性的|Representing or portraying something visually or verbally.  Describing something in a way that creates a vivid picture in the mind.|The novel contained many depictive passages of life in the countryside.|近义词：descriptive, illustrative, representational, graphic, pictorial|反义词：nondescriptive, abstract|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *depictivus, from Latin depictus, past participle of depingere (“to depict”), from de- (“down, completely”) + pingere (“to paint”).
