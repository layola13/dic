antichristian:/ˌæntiˈkrɪstʃən/
adjective| 反基督的；反对基督教的|Opposed to or hostile toward Christianity or its principles.|His views were seen as antichristian by many religious leaders. 他的观点被许多宗教领袖视为反基督教的。|近义词：ungodly, impious, blasphemous|反义词：Christian, pious, devout|词性:形容词

antichristian:/ˌæntiˈkrɪstʃən/
noun| 反基督者；敌基督|A person who opposes or denies the divinity of Christ.|Many cults have been labeled as following an antichrist. 许多邪教都被贴上了追随敌基督的标签。|近义词：heretic, infidel, blasphemer|反义词：believer, Christian, follower of Christ|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Middle English *anticrist*, from Old French *antecrist*, from Late Latin *antichristus*, from Ancient Greek *ἀντίχριστος (antíkhristos)*.  *ἀντί (antí)* means "against" and *χριστός (khristós)* means "anointed," referring to Christ.
