forbidding:/fərˈbɪdɪŋ/| adj.|令人生畏的； forbidding|Unfriendly or threatening in appearance.  Evoking fear or dislike; looking dangerous or unpleasant.| The house had a forbidding appearance, with dark windows and overgrown vines. 这栋房子外观令人生畏，窗户黑漆漆的，藤蔓丛生。| The forbidding landscape of the desert stretched before them.  荒凉的沙漠景观展现在他们面前。| synonyms: menacing, daunting, intimidating, ominous, hostile| antonyms: welcoming, inviting, friendly, approachable| adjective

forbid:/fərˈbɪd/| vt.|禁止；不准|To refuse to allow (something). To command (someone) not to do something.|  My parents forbid me from going out late. 我父母不准我晚归。|  Smoking is forbidden in the hospital. 医院里禁止吸烟。| synonyms: prohibit, ban, disallow, outlaw| antonyms: permit, allow, authorize| verb
