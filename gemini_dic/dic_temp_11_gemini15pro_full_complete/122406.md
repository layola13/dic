mnemonically:/nɪˈmɒnɪkəli/ | adv. | 记忆地，助记地 |  By using a mnemonic device, such as a rhyme or acronym, to aid memory. Derived from "mnemonic," which traces back to the Greek word "mnēmōn," meaning "mindful." | She remembered the spelling mnemonically by associating it with a funny phrase. 她通过一个有趣的短语记忆了拼写。|Synonyms: memorably, by rote, by heart | Antonyms: forgetfully | adverb
