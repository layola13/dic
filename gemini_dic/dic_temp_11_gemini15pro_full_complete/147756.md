overprogramming:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræmɪŋ/
n.
过度编程
The act of writing excessive or unnecessarily complex code for a task.
Overprogramming can lead to software that is difficult to maintain and debug. 过度编程会导致软件难以维护和调试。
The software was bloated due to overprogramming.  这个软件由于过度编程而变得臃肿。
近义词：overengineering, code bloat
反义词：conciseness, minimalism
词性：名词
