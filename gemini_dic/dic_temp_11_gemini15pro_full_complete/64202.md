equicrural:/ˌiːkwɪˈkrʊərəl/
adjective
等腰的
Having two sides or legs of equal length.  Specifically referring to triangles having two equal sides.
An equicrural triangle has two sides of the same length.  一个等腰三角形有两条边长度相等。
The architect designed a roof with equicrural triangular sections. 建筑师设计了一个由等腰三角形部分组成的屋顶。
近义词:isosceles
反义词:scalene
词性:adjective
