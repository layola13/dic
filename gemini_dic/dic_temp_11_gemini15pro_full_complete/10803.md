aquatintist: /ˈækwəˌtɪntɪst/
noun
水族爱好者
A person who keeps and studies aquatic animals and plants, especially fish.
The aquatintist spent hours observing the behavior of his cichlids.  水族爱好者花了几个小时观察他的慈鲷鱼的行为。
The local aquatintist club is hosting a show this weekend.  当地的水族爱好者俱乐部这周末举办一场展览。

近义词: aquarist, fishkeeper
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes or avoids aquatic life)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aqua* (water) + *-tin-*,  combining form of *tenēre* (to hold) + *-ist* (one who does/studies).  Essentially, "one who holds/keeps water (creatures)."
