arabi:/ˈærəbi/ | adj. n.|阿拉伯的；阿拉伯人|Relating to the Arabs, their language, or their culture.  A person of Arab ethnicity or nationality.|例句：He speaks fluent Arabi. 他说一口流利的阿拉伯语。

例句：Many Arabis live in the Middle East. 很多阿拉伯人住在中东。|近义词：Arabian|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Arabi, from Latin Arabs, from Ancient Greek Ἄραψ (Araps), from a Semitic source. Ultimately from Akkadian arabū ("desert nomad, bedouin").
