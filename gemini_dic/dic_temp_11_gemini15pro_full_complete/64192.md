equestrienne:/ɪˌkwɛstriˈɛn/ /ɪˌkwɛstriˈən/
n.
女骑手
a female horse rider.
The equestrienne performed a difficult jump.  这位女骑手完成了一个高难度的跳跃。
近义词：horsewoman, rider
反义词：(None -  lacks a direct opposite.  One could consider "pedestrian" figuratively, but not literally.)
词性：noun

equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/ /ɪˈkwɛstriən/
adj.
骑马的；骑术的
relating to horse riding.
She demonstrated equestrian skills. 她展示了骑术技巧。
近义词：riding
反义词：(None -  lacks a direct opposite)
词性：adjective

equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/ /ɪˈkwɛstriən/
n.
骑手
a person who rides horses.
The equestrian entered the competition. 这位骑手参加了比赛。
近义词：horseman, rider, horsewoman
反义词：(None -  lacks a direct opposite.  One could consider "pedestrian" figuratively, but not literally.)
词性：noun

词源: From French *équestre* (masculine) and *équestrienne* (feminine), from Latin *equester*, meaning "horseman, knight," derived from *equus*, meaning "horse."
