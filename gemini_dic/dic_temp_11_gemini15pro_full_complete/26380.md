by-lining:/ˈbaɪlaɪnɪŋ/
n.
署名；冠名；副业
The act of providing a byline, or the byline itself.  A secondary job or activity undertaken in addition to one's main employment.
The article had a by-lining by a famous journalist. 这篇文章署了一位著名记者的名字。
He supplements his income with a little by-lining. 他做点副业来贴补收入。
近义词:credit, attribution, sideline
反义词:anonymity
词性:noun
