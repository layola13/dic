allegiance:/əˈliːdʒəns/
n.|忠诚，忠贞；效忠，拥戴|Loyalty or devotion to a person, group, or cause.|例句：
He swore allegiance to the king.
他宣誓效忠国王。

例句：
Her allegiance to the company never wavered.
她对公司的忠诚从未动摇。|近义词：loyalty, devotion, fidelity, commitment|反义词：treachery, disloyalty, betrayal|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "alegeance"  (homage, loyalty), from "alegier" (to bind by oath), from Latin "alligare" (to bind to).
