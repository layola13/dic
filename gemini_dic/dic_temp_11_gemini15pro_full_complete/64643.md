erythrogen:/ɪˈrɪθrədʒən/
noun
促红细胞生成素
A substance that stimulates the production of red blood cells.
Erythrogen is essential for the formation of red blood cells in the bone marrow. 促红细胞生成素对骨髓中红细胞的形成至关重要。
Athletes sometimes misuse erythrogen to enhance their performance.  运动员有时会滥用促红细胞生成素来提高他们的成绩。
Synonyms: erythropoietin, EPO
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but substances that inhibit red blood cell production could be considered)
