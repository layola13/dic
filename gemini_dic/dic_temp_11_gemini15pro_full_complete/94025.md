imparalleled:/ɪmˈpærəˌleld/
adjective
无与伦比的，空前的
Having no parallel; unmatched, unsurpassed.
Her talent is unparalleled in the music industry. 她的才华在音乐界是无与伦比的。
synonyms: unmatched, unrivaled, unsurpassed, peerless, incomparable, unequaled, matchless
antonyms: common, ordinary, mediocre, average, typical
adjective
