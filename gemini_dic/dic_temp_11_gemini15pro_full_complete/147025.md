overlash:/ˌoʊvərˈlæʃ/ | n. | 反冲；过度反应；强烈反应 | a strong reaction to something, especially a change, that goes further than is necessary or reasonable | The government's policies caused a public overlash. 政府的政策引起了公众的强烈反对。| 近义词：backlash, reaction, recoil | 反义词: acceptance, approval | 词性: 名词

overlash:/ˌoʊvərˈlæʃ/ | v. | 反冲；过度反应；强烈反应；（鞭子）抽打到 | (of a whip) to strike with the end after a stroke;  to react strongly to something, especially a change, and go further than is necessary or reasonable | The whip overlashed and caught the horse on the flank.  鞭子反抽打中了马的侧腹。| The market overlashed to the news. 市场对这一消息反应过度。|近义词：recoil, react, overreact | 反义词: underreact | 词性: 动词
