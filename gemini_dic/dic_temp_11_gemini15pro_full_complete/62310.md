endearedness:/ɪnˈdɪərdnəs/| n. |喜爱；受喜爱；亲爱|The quality of being endeared or loved. The state of being held dear.|Her endearedness to her grandchildren was evident in the way she interacted with them. 她对孙辈的喜爱在她与他们互动的方式中显而易见。|近义词：affection, fondness, love, charm|反义词：dislike, aversion, hatred|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From endear + -ness.  Endear comes from Middle English *enderen*, from *en-* (in, into) + *dere* (dear, precious).
