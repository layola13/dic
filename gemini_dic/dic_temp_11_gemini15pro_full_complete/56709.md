doomsayings:/ˈduːmseɪɪŋz/
noun
预言灾难；凶兆；悲观预言
Predictions of disaster; prophecies of doom.
The constant doomsayings about the economy are depressing.  对于经济的持续悲观预言令人沮丧。
Synonyms:  forebodings, prophecies of doom, pessimistic predictions, auguries, portents, warnings, alarms
Antonyms:  optimism, hope, positive predictions, reassurances
词性: 名词
