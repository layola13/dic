cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/| vt.|烧灼；烙；使麻木；使无感觉|To burn the skin or flesh of (a wound) with a heated instrument or caustic substance, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  |The doctor cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。|Synonyms: sear, burn, scorch|Antonyms: heal, soothe|verb

cauterized:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzd/| adj.|烧灼的；烙过的|Having been treated by cauterization.|The cauterized wound began to heal. 烧灼后的伤口开始愈合。|Synonyms: seared, burned, scorched|Antonyms: healed, soothed|adjective
