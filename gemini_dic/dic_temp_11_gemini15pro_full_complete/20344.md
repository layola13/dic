bismer:/ˈbɪzmər/| n.|野兽; 怪物; 粗野的人|A cruel, savage, or monstrous person or animal.  From Middle English *bismer, from Old Norse *bysmirr ("monster, prodigy"). Not in common modern usage; considered archaic or obsolete.|He was known as a bismer, feared by all who crossed his path. 他被称为野兽，所有与他作对的人都害怕他。|近义词：monster, beast, brute, savage|反义词：gentleman, civilized person|词性:名词
