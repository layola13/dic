It refers to the absence of extreme views or actions, favoring moderate or conservative approaches. |His nonradicalness in political matters often frustrated his more passionate colleagues. 他在政治问题上的温和态度常常使他那些更激进的同事感到沮丧。|Synonyms: moderateness, conservatism, centrism, mildness|Antonyms: radicalism, extremism, militancy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + radical + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "opposite of." "Radical" comes from Late Latin "radicalis," meaning "of or pertaining to the root," from Latin "radix," meaning "root."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
