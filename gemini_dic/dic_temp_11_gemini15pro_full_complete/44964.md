crystalling:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
结晶的; 正在形成晶体的
The process of a substance becoming a solid with a regular, repeating arrangement of atoms or molecules.  The solution was slowly crystalling.
The solution is crystalling as it cools. 溶液冷却时正在结晶。
The honey is crystalling in the jar.  蜂蜜在罐子里结晶了。
Synonyms: solidifying, hardening, congealing
Antonyms: melting, dissolving, liquefying
word type: adjective

crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/
verb
结晶; 使具体化
To cause something to become clear and specific or to take a definite form.
His vague ideas crystallized into a concrete plan. 他的模糊想法具体化成了一个具体的计划。
The cold weather crystallized the pond. 寒冷的天气使池塘结冰了。
Synonyms: solidify, concretize, materialize
Antonyms: melt, dissolve, liquefy
word type: verb
