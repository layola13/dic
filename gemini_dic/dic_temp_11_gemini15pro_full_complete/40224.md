contextualizations:/kənˌtɛkstʃʊələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 语境化；情境化 | The process of placing something within its historical, social, or cultural context. |  The historian's contextualizations of the ancient texts helped us understand their true meaning. 历史学家对古代文献的语境化帮助我们理解了它们的真正含义。 | synonyms: interpretations, explanations, analyses | antonyms: decontextualizations, abstractions | noun

contextualization:/kənˌtɛkstʃʊələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 语境化；情境化 | The act of placing something within its relevant context. | The teacher's contextualization of the novel helped the students understand its themes. 老师对小说的语境化帮助学生理解了它的主题。 | synonyms: placement, framing, situating | antonyms: decontextualization, abstraction | noun
