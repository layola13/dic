overextravagantly: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈstrævəɡəntli/
adverb
过于奢侈地，过分铺张地
in a way that is too extravagant; excessively
She dressed overextravagantly for the party, wearing a diamond tiara and a fur coat. 她为了派对穿着过于奢侈，戴着钻石王冠和皮草大衣。
The wedding was overextravagantly planned, with ice sculptures, a live band, and a five-course meal. 婚礼的计划过于奢侈，有冰雕、现场乐队和五道菜的大餐。

近义词: excessively, immoderately, lavishly, exorbitantly
反义词: moderately, frugally, economically, simply
词性: adverb
