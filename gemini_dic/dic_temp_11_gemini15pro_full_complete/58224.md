druggist:/ˈdrʌɡɪst/| n. |药剂师，药商|A person who is qualified to prepare and dispense drugs, especially a pharmacist.|例句：The druggist carefully measured the ingredients for the prescription. 药剂师仔细地称量处方中的成分。

例句：In the old days, people went to the druggist for all sorts of remedies. 过去，人们去找药剂师寻求各种治疗方法。|近义词：pharmacist, chemist, apothecary|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
