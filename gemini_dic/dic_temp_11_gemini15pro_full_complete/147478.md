overoptimistically: /ˌoʊvərˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 过分乐观地 | In a way that is excessively optimistic; too hopefully. | He overoptimistically predicted a quick victory. 他过分乐观地预测了一场速胜。|  He overoptimistically assumed he would get the job. 他过于乐观地认为自己会得到这份工作。 | 近义词: excessively optimistically, unduly optimistically, unrealistically optimistically | 反义词: pessimistically, realistically | 词性: 副词

overoptimistically: 词源来自 over- (过度) + optimistic (乐观的) + -ally (副词后缀).
