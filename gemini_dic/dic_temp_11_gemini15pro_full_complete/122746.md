moistful:/ˈmɔɪstfəl/
adjective
充满水分的；湿润的
Characterized by or saturated with moisture.  Considered nonstandard or humorous.
The baker described the cake as moistful, much to the amusement of his colleagues.  面包师把蛋糕描述为“湿润的”，这让他同事们感到好笑。
近义词:moist, damp, humid
反义词:dry, arid
词性:adjective
