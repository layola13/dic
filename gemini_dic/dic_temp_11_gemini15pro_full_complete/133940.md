nonhazardous:/ˌnɒnˈhæzərdəs/ |adj.|无害的，非危险的|Not dangerous; unlikely to cause harm.|This cleaning product is nonhazardous and biodegradable.  这种清洁产品是无害的，可生物降解的。|The toys are made of nonhazardous materials. 这些玩具由无害材料制成。|近义词：safe, harmless, innocuous|反义词:hazardous, dangerous, perilous|词性:形容词
词源: non- (not) + hazardous (dangerous)
