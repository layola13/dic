lymphocytotoxic: /ˌlɪmfəˌsaɪtəˈtɒksɪk/ (UK) /ˌlɪmfəˌsaɪtəˈtɑːksɪk/ (US) | adj. | 淋巴细胞毒性的 |  Destructive to lymphocytes.  This term refers to substances or processes that kill lymphocytes. |  The drug proved to be lymphocytotoxic in laboratory tests.  该药物在实验室测试中被证明具有淋巴细胞毒性。

The patient developed lymphocytotoxic antibodies after the transplant.  病人在移植后产生了淋巴细胞毒性抗体。 | 近义词:  cytotoxic, lymphotoxic | 反义词:  immunosuppressive (in some contexts - as it can prevent lymphocyte action) | 词性: adjective
