corroborates:/kəˈrɒbəreɪts/
verb
证实，确证
confirms or gives support to (a statement, theory, or finding).
The witness corroborated the boy's account of the accident.  证人证实了男孩对事故的描述。
confirm, verify, substantiate, validate
contradict, refute, deny
v.
