flat-mate:/ˈflætmeɪt/ (UK) /ˈflætˌmeɪt/ (US)| n.|室友，合租人|A person who shares a flat (apartment) with another person or people.|例句：My flat-mate and I share the cooking. 我和我的室友轮流做饭。

例句：I'm looking for a new flat-mate. 我正在寻找一个新的室友。

近义词：roommate, housemate|反义词:landlord, landlady|词性:noun
