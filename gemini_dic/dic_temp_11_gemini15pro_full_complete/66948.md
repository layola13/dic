experienceable:/ɪkˈspɪəriənsəbl/
adjective
可体验的，可经历的
Capable of being experienced; that can be known through experience.
This feeling of joy is experienceable by everyone.  这种喜悦的心情每个人都可以体验到。
The beauty of the sunset was truly experienceable. 日落的美丽是真正可以体验到的。
近义词: perceptible, tangible, sensible
反义词: imperceptible, intangible
词性: adjective
