abscess:/ˈæbses/ (US), /ˈæbses/ (UK)| n.|脓肿|A localized collection of pus in the tissues of the body, often caused by bacterial infection.|The doctor drained the abscess on his leg. 医生排出了他腿上的脓肿。|近义词：boil, ulcer, sore, infection|反义词: health|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "abscessus", past participle of "abscedere" (to go away, withdraw), from "ab-" (away from) + "cedere" (to go).  This refers to the accumulation of pus "going away" or separating from the healthy tissue.
