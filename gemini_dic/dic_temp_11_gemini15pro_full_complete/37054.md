comb:/kəʊm/ (British) /koʊm/ (American)| n. v.|梳子；梳理|A toothed device used for arranging or untangling hair.  To smooth, arrange, or untangle (hair or other fibers) with a comb.|She used a comb to smooth her hair. 她用梳子梳理头发。 He combed his hair neatly. 他把头发梳得整整齐齐。|近义词：brush, rake|反义词:tangle, snarl|名词, 动词
