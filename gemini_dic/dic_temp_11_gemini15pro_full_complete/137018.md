nonstaining:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪnɪŋ/
adjective
不染色的；不留污渍的
Not leaving stains; resistant to staining.
This marker is nonstaining, so you don't have to worry about it marking your clothes.  这种记号笔是不染色的，所以你不必担心它会弄脏你的衣服。
The tablecloth is treated with a nonstaining finish. 这块桌布经过了防污处理。
近义词:stain-resistant, non-marking
反义词:staining
