abstracted:/æbˈstræktɪd/
adjective
心不在焉的；出神的；抽象的
removed in thought from the immediate situation; lost in one's own mind.  Considered apart from concrete realities, specific objects, or actual instances.

He had an abstracted look on his face, as if he were thinking about something else entirely.  他脸上带着心不在焉的表情，好像在想完全不同的事情。

The artist's abstracted paintings used shapes and colors to evoke emotions rather than depict realistic scenes.  这位艺术家的抽象画运用形状和颜色来唤起情感，而不是描绘现实场景。

Synonyms: preoccupied, inattentive, distant, dreamy, theoretical, conceptual
Antonyms: attentive, focused, present, concrete, specific
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin abstractus, past participle of abstrahere "to draw away," from Latin abs-, variant of ab- "off, away from" + trahere "to draw."
