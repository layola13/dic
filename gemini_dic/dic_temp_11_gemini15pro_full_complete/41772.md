corruptions: /kəˈrʌpʃənz/
noun
腐败；堕落；贪污；贿赂
Dishonest or fraudulent conduct by those in power, typically involving bribery.
The investigation uncovered widespread corruption within the police force. 调查揭露了警察部队内部普遍存在的腐败现象。
Synonyms: dishonesty, fraud, bribery, venality, misconduct, wrongdoing
Antonyms: honesty, integrity, probity
