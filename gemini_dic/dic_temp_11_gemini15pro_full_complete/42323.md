counter-scale:/ˈkaʊntərˌskeɪl/ | adj. n.|反规模的，对应等级；反比例；对应等级|A counter-scale strategy is one that opposes the dominant scale of power or influence.|例句：The local community organized a counter-scale movement to resist the large corporation's takeover. 当地社区组织了一场反规模运动来抵制大公司的收购。

例句：They developed a counter-scale technology to address the limitations of the existing system. 他们开发了一种反规模技术来解决现有系统的局限性。|近义词：countervailing, opposing, resisting|反义词：supporting, reinforcing, conforming|词性:形容词, 名词
