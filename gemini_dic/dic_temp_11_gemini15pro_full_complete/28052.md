capella:/kəˈpɛlə/| n.|五车二（御夫座α星）; 清唱; 小教堂|A bright star, also known as Alpha Aurigae, in the constellation Auriga.  A piece of music sung a cappella is performed without any instrumental accompaniment. A small chapel.|例句：The capella shone brightly in the night sky. 五车二在夜空中闪耀着光芒。

例句：The choir sang a beautiful capella piece.  合唱团演唱了一首优美的无伴奏合唱曲。|近义词：chapel (for the small chapel meaning), a cappella (for the musical meaning)|反义词: (No direct antonym, context-dependent)|noun
