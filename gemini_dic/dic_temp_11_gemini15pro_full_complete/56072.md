docked:/dɑːkt/ (US) /dɒkt/ (UK)| v.  adj.|停靠，扣除，剪短|To bring a ship or boat into a dock; to deduct or subtract from something; shortened or clipped.|The ship docked at the harbor. 这艘船停靠在港口。|近义词：berthed, moored, secured, deducted, subtracted, trimmed, clipped|反义词: undocked, added|verb

docked:/dɑːkt/ (US) /dɒkt/ (UK)| adj.|（尾巴或耳朵）被剪短的|Having had a part cut off, especially a tail or ears.|The dog's ears were docked. 这只狗的耳朵被剪短了。|近义词：cropped, clipped, shortened|反义词: undocked, uncut|adjective
