flashmobbed:/ˈflæʃmɒbd/ (UK) /ˈflæʃmɑːbd/ (US)| v.|快闪突袭；快闪围观|A large public gathering at which people briefly perform an unusual or seemingly random act and then disperse, typically organized via the Internet or social media.| The unsuspecting shoppers were flashmobbed by a choir singing Christmas carols.  毫无戒心的购物者被一个唱圣诞颂歌的合唱团快闪突袭了。|Synonyms: surprised, ambushed, descended upon|Antonyms: planned, expected|verb (past tense)

flashmob:/ˈflæʃmɒb/ (UK) /ˈflæʃmɑːbd/ (US)| n.|快闪族；快闪行动|A large public gathering at which people briefly perform an unusual or seemingly random act and then disperse, typically organized via the Internet or social media.|A flashmob of dancers suddenly appeared in the town square. 一群快闪舞者突然出现在城镇广场上。|Synonyms: crowd, gathering, assembly|Antonyms:  individual, loner|noun
