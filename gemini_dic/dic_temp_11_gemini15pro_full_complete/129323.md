noxious: /ˈnɑːkʃəs/ | adj. | 有害的，有毒的 | harmful, poisonous, or very unpleasant. |  例句：The noxious fumes from the factory caused widespread illness. 工厂排放的有毒烟雾造成了广泛的疾病。

例句：The plant emits a noxious odor. 这种植物散发出一股难闻的气味。 | 近义词：toxic, poisonous, harmful, detrimental, dangerous | 反义词：harmless, beneficial, healthy, safe | 词性: adjective

词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin noxius ‘hurtful’, from noxa ‘harm’.
