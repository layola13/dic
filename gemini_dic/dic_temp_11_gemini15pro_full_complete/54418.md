disincorporated:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtɪd/
adjective
解散的，撤销法人资格的
Deprived of corporate status; no longer existing as a legal entity.
The company was disincorporated after years of financial struggles.  这家公司在多年的财务困境后被解散了。
The disincorporated town is now just a collection of houses.  这个被撤销法人资格的城镇现在只是一些房屋的集合。
Synonyms: dissolved, liquidated, defunct
Antonyms: incorporated, established, active
词性: adjective
