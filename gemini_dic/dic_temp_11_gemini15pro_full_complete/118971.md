metewand:/ˈmɛtɪwɒnd/ (UK) /ˈmiːtɪwɑːnd/ (US)| n. |测量杆，量尺|A rod or staff used for measuring, especially land. |例句：The surveyor used a metewand to measure the boundaries of the property.  测量员用测量杆测量了房产的边界。

例句：The ancient metewand was made of seasoned wood.  这根古老的测量杆是用干燥的木材制成的。


近义词：measuring rod, yardstick, ruler, staff|反义词: None|词性: Noun
