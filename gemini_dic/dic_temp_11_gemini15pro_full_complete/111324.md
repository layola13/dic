live-high-off-the-hog:/lɪv haɪ ɔf ðə hɔɡ/| idiom | 过着奢侈的生活；挥霍无度 | To live extravagantly, enjoying the best of everything.  This idiom refers to eating the best cuts of pork, which are higher up on the hog. |  They won the lottery and started living high off the hog. 他们中了彩票，开始过着奢侈的生活。|  live in luxury, live it up, indulge oneself | live frugally, live simply, scrimp and save | idiom
