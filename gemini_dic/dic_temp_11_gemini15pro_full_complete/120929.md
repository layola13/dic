miscegenation:/ˌmɪsɪdʒɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |种族混合，异族通婚|The term "miscegenation" is now largely considered offensive due to its historical association with racist ideologies that viewed interracial marriage as undesirable.  这个词现在很大程度上被认为是冒犯性的，因为它在历史上与种族主义意识形态有关，这些意识形态认为不同种族间的婚姻是不可取的。| In the past, laws against miscegenation were common in many parts of the United States. 过去，在美国许多地方，禁止种族通婚的法律很普遍。| 近义词: intermarriage, interracial marriage | 反义词: endogamy | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Latin *miscere* "to mix" + *genus* "race, kind".  源自拉丁语 *miscere* “混合” + *genus* “种族，种类”。
