dement:/dɪˈmɛnt/| v.|使精神错乱，使发狂|To deprive of reason or sanity; make insane.|The fever demented him.  发烧让他精神错乱。|The terrible experience demented her.  可怕的经历使她精神错乱。|近义词：maddened, crazed, deranged|反义词：sane, rational|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *dēmēns*, present participle of *dēmēntire* (“to be out of one's mind”), from *dē* (“out of, from”) + *mēns* (“mind”).
