dislocatedly: /dɪsˈloʊkətɪdli/ | adv. | 脱臼地，错位地 |  In a manner that is out of its usual or proper place, like a dislocated joint.  Describes something placed, arranged, or occurring in a way that is disordered or out of its natural order or connection. |  The narrative jumped dislocatedly between past and present, leaving the reader confused.  叙述在过去和现在之间错乱地跳跃，让读者感到困惑。 | The museum's artifacts were displayed somewhat dislocatedly, with no apparent chronological or thematic order.  博物馆的文物摆放得有些杂乱无章，没有明显的年代或主题顺序。 | 近义词:  incoherently, disconnectedly, haphazardly, randomly | 反义词:  coherently, connectedly, orderly, systematically | 词性: 副词
