cantatas:/kænˈtɑːtəz/ | n. |清唱剧; 康塔塔|A vocal composition, in several movements, for solo voices, chorus, and usually instrumental accompaniment, often based on a religious, dramatic, or lyrical text.| Bach's cantatas are masterpieces of the Baroque period. 巴赫的清唱剧是巴洛克时期的杰作。| The choir performed a beautiful cantata for Easter.  唱诗班在复活节表演了一首优美的清唱剧。| synonyms: oratorio, choral work | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps instrumental piece) | noun
