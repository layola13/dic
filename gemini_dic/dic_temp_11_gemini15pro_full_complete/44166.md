criticize: /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/ (US) /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/ (UK)
verb
批评，批判
To express disapproval of someone or something, to find fault with.
The teacher criticized the student for being late. 老师批评了学生迟到。
He criticized the government's handling of the economy. 他批评了政府对经济的处理方式。

近义词: condemn, censure, denounce, reprehend, blame, fault
反义词: praise, commend, laud, extol
词性: verb
