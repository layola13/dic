misattribute:/ˌmɪsəˈtrɪbjuːt/ /ˌmɪs əˈtrɪˌbjut/| vt.|错误地归因于；误认为…是…说的（或写的、做的等）|To wrongly say or believe that somebody is responsible for something|The painting was misattributed to Leonardo da Vinci for many years. 这幅画多年来一直被误认为是莱昂纳多·达·芬奇的作品。|The quote was misattributed to the wrong author. 这句话被错误地归因于另一位作者。|misassign, misconstrue|correctly attribute, rightly attribute|verb
