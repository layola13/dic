flume:/ˈfluːm/ | n. | 水槽，引水槽，峡谷 | A narrow channel or chute for carrying water, especially one used in mining or logging. |  The miners built a flume to carry water to the gold fields. 矿工们建造了一个水槽，将水输送到金矿区。

The flume carried logs down the mountainside. 引水槽将原木运下山坡。 | 近义词：channel, chute, trough, conduit | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *flum (unattested), from Latin flūmen (“river, stream”), from Proto-Italic *flōmen, from Proto-Indo-European *plew- (“to flow”).
