echinodermal:/ɪˌkaɪnəˈdɜːrməl/ |adj.|棘皮动物的；与棘皮动物有关的|Relating to or belonging to the Echinodermata, a phylum of marine invertebrates that includes starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers.|Starfish are echinodermal creatures. 海星是棘皮动物。|The echinodermal skeleton is made of calcium carbonate. 棘皮动物的骨骼由碳酸钙构成。|近义词: invertebrate (无脊椎动物的) |反义词: vertebrate (脊椎动物的) |词性: adjective

词源: From  Greek *ekhinos* "sea urchin, hedgehog" + *derma* "skin" + *-al*
