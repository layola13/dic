birkie: /ˈbɜːrki/ (UK) /ˈbɜːrki/ (US)| n. |家伙，小伙子（尤指苏格兰或英格兰北部的人）|A lively or cheeky young man, especially one from Scotland or northern England.  It can also be used affectionately or ironically.|He's a canny birkie, always looking for a bargain. 他是个精明的小伙子，总是想买便宜货。|近义词：chap, fellow, lad, bloke|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "old man" in some contexts)|noun
