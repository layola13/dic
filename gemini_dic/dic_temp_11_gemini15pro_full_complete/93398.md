illiquation:/ɪlɪˈkweɪʃən/ | n. | 渗透，渗入；（土壤中物质的）淋溶，淋溶作用 | The process of leaching or dissolving soluble substances from the upper layers of soil and depositing them in lower layers. | The illiquation of minerals from the topsoil can lead to nutrient depletion. 表土中矿物质的淋溶会导致养分枯竭。 | leaching, eluviation, percolation | deposition, accumulation | noun
