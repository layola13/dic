lychgate: /ˈlɪtʃɡeɪt/ | n. | 棂门，停柩门 | A roofed gateway to a churchyard, traditionally where a coffin awaits the clergyman before proceeding into the church for the funeral service. | The coffin rested under the lychgate while the mourners gathered. 灵柩停放在棂门下，哀悼者聚集在一起。 | The procession paused at the lychgate before entering the churchyard.  队伍在进入教堂墓地前在棂门处停了下来。 | 近义词: hearse house | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *liche-ȝate, from Old English *līc-geat (“corpse-gate”), from līc (“corpse, body”) + geat (“gate”).
