boil-down:/ˈbɔɪl daʊn/ (US), /ˈbɔɪl daʊn/ (UK)| phrasal verb | 归结为，浓缩，简化 | To reduce something, often liquid, by boiling; to summarize or simplify something complex. | The chef boiled the sauce down to intensify the flavor. 厨师将酱汁煮浓以增强风味。

| condense, summarize, reduce, abridge | expand, elaborate, amplify | verb phrase

boil-down (as a noun - less common): /ˈbɔɪl daʊn/ (US), /ˈbɔɪl daʊn/ (UK)| n. | 浓缩物，精华，概要 | The boil-down of the report is that we need to increase sales. 报告的概要是我们需要增加销售额。 | summary, gist, essence | expansion, detail | noun
