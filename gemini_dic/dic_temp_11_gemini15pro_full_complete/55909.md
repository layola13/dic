divulgater:/daɪˈvʌlɡeɪtər/ | n. | 泄露者；传播者 | someone who divulges; someone who reveals something previously unknown | The divulgater of the secret was never found.  秘密的泄露者一直没有找到。 | 近义词: revealer, discloser, informant | 反义词: concealer, suppressor | 词性: 名词

divulge:/daɪˈvʌldʒ/ | v. | 泄露；透露 | to make known (private or sensitive information) |  She refused to divulge the source of her information. 她拒绝透露消息来源。 | 近义词: reveal, disclose, leak, unveil | 反义词: conceal, suppress, hide | 词性: 动词
