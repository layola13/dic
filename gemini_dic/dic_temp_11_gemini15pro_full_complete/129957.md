non-Mediterranean:/ˌnɒnˌmedɪtəˈreɪniən/
adjective
非地中海的
Not characteristic of or relating to the Mediterranean Sea, its climate, or the countries bordering it.
The non-Mediterranean plants struggled in the hot, dry climate.  这些非地中海植物在炎热干燥的气候中挣扎求生。
The restaurant specializes in non-Mediterranean cuisine. 这家餐厅专门经营非地中海菜肴。
近义词: extra-Mediterranean, non-Mediterranean climate
反义词: Mediterranean
词性: adjective
