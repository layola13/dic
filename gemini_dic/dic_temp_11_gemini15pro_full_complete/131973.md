nonconversancy:/ˌnɒnkɒnvɜːrsənsi/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːnvɜːrsənsi/| n. |不熟悉，不精通|Lack of familiarity or expertise in a particular subject or area.|His nonconversancy with local customs led to some embarrassing situations. 他对当地习俗的不熟悉导致了一些尴尬的局面。|Example: Her nonconversancy with the software caused delays in the project. 她对该软件的不熟悉导致了项目延误。|Synonyms: unfamiliarity, inexperience, ignorance, lack of knowledge|Antonyms: familiarity, expertise, mastery, knowledge|noun

nonconversant:/ˌnɒnkɒnvɜːrsənt/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːnvɜːrsənt/| adj. |不熟悉的，不精通的|Not familiar or knowledgeable about something.|He was nonconversant with the latest developments in the field. 他不熟悉该领域的最新发展。|Example: She was nonconversant with the rules of the game. 她不熟悉游戏规则。|Synonyms: unfamiliar, inexperienced, ignorant, unacquainted|Antonyms: familiar, experienced, knowledgeable, acquainted|adjective
