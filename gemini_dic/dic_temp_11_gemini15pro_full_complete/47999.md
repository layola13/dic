decimetre:/ˈdɛsɪmiːtər/ (US) /ˈdɛsɪˌmiːtər/ (UK)| n. |分米|A unit of length in the metric system, equal to one tenth of a meter (10 centimeters).|The table is 10 decimetres wide.|近义词:10 centimeters|反义词: (No direct opposite, but consider units like kilometer, meter, millimeter)|词性:noun

decimetres:/ˈdɛsɪmiːtərz/ (US) /ˈdɛsɪˌmiːtərz/ (UK)| n. |分米|Plural form of decimetre, a unit of length equal to one tenth of a meter.|The bookcase is 20 decimetres tall.|近义词:200 centimeters|反义词:(No direct opposite, but consider units like kilometers, meters, millimeters)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *décimètre*, from *déci-* (one tenth) + *mètre* (meter).  Ultimately from Greek *deka* (ten) and *metron* (measure).
