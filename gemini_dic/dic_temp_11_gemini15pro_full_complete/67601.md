extravagantnesses:/ɪkˌstrævəɡəntnəsɪz/| n. | 奢侈，挥霍无度 |  The state or quality of being extravagant; excessive or unnecessary expenditure or display. | Her extravagantnesses eventually led to bankruptcy. 她的奢侈最终导致了破产。|  Luxury, lavishness, prodigality, excess | thrift, frugality, economy, moderation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From extravagant + -ness. Extravagant from Late Latin extravagant-, extravagans, present participle of extravagari "to wander outside or beyond," from Latin extra- "outside" + vagari "to wander."
