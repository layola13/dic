epigraphy: /ɪˈpɪɡrəfi/ | n. | 碑文研究; 金石学 | The study of inscriptions, especially those on stone or metal, as a means of understanding past cultures and events.  |  Example: The epigraphy of ancient Rome reveals much about their social and political structures. 古罗马的碑文研究揭示了他们社会和政治结构的许多信息。

Example: Her dissertation focused on the epigraphy of the Mayan civilization. 她的论文集中研究了玛雅文明的碑文。 |  Synonyms: inscription study, paleography | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
