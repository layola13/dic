homothermic:/ˌhōməˈθɜːrmɪk/ | adj. |恒温的；温血的 | Maintaining a relatively constant body temperature despite changes in the external environment. | Birds and mammals are homothermic animals. 鸟类和哺乳动物是恒温动物。|  Example: Unlike reptiles, homothermic animals can regulate their own body temperature.  不像爬行动物，恒温动物可以调节自己的体温。| synonyms: warm-blooded, endothermic | antonyms: poikilothermic, ectothermic, cold-blooded | adjective
