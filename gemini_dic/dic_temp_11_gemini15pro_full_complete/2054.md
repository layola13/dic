acupuncture: /ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃər/ (US) /ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃə/ (UK)| n.|针灸|A system of complementary medicine that involves pricking the skin or tissues with needles, used to alleviate pain and to treat various physical, mental, and emotional conditions. It originated in ancient China.|例句：Acupuncture is often used to treat back pain. 针灸常用来治疗背痛。

例句：She tried acupuncture to relieve her migraines. 她尝试针灸来缓解偏头痛。|近义词：acupressure, therapy|反义词: None|词性: noun
