diffident:/ˈdɪfɪdənt/| adj.|缺乏自信的；胆怯的；羞怯的|Lacking self-confidence; shy and timid.|She was diffident about speaking up in class.  她在课堂上不敢发言。|Synonyms: shy, timid, bashful, hesitant, reserved, insecure|Antonyms: confident, assertive, bold, outgoing|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diffīdēns*, present participle of diffīdere (“to distrust, lack confidence”), from dis- (“not, apart”) + fīdere (“to trust”).
