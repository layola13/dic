inter-coordinates:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪnɪts/ | adj. |相互协调的；互相配合的 |  Relating to or involving the coordination or interaction between different sets of coordinates or systems.  This term often appears in technical contexts, such as computer graphics, robotics, and geographic information systems, describing how different coordinate systems relate to each other. |  The software uses inter-coordinates transformations to seamlessly integrate data from different mapping systems. 该软件使用坐标间变换来无缝集成来自不同地图系统的数据。|  interrelated, coordinated, integrated | independent, separate | adjective

inter-coordinates:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪnɪts/ | n. |相互坐标；互相配合 | The set of relationships or transformations between different coordinate systems, allowing for data integration and consistent spatial referencing. | The inter-coordinates of the two systems were complex but essential for accurate analysis. 这两个系统的相互坐标很复杂，但对于准确分析至关重要。 | correlation, correspondence, mapping | disparity, divergence | noun
