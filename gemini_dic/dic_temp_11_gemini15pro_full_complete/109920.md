libelers:/ˈlaɪbələrz/| n.|诽谤者，造谣者|People who publish or spread false and malicious statements that damage someone's reputation.  They intentionally defame others through writing or other media.|The libelers were sued for spreading false information about the company. 这些诽谤者因为散布关于该公司的虚假信息而被起诉。|Synonyms: defamers, slanderers, calumniators|Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for someone who libels, the antonym would relate to the action, e.g., vindicator)|Noun
