antiutilitarian:/ˌæntijuːˌtɪlɪˈtɛəriən/
adjective
反功利主义的
opposed to or rejecting utilitarianism (the ethical theory that actions are right if they are useful or for the benefit of a majority).
He held a strongly antiutilitarian view, believing that some actions are inherently right or wrong regardless of their consequences.  他持有强烈的反功利主义观点，认为有些行为本身是对或错的，无论其后果如何。
The philosopher presented an antiutilitarian argument, challenging the idea that maximizing happiness is the ultimate moral goal. 这位哲学家提出了一个反功利主义的论点，挑战了将幸福最大化作为最终道德目标的观点。
近义词:nonutilitarian,  anti-utilitarian
反义词:utilitarian
词性:adjective
