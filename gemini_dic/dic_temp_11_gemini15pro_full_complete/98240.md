insensibly:/ɪnˈsɛnsəbli/| adv.|不知不觉地，无感觉地，迟钝地|Gradually and without being noticed; in a way that does not involve the senses.|例句：The days grew shorter insensibly. 白昼不知不觉地变短了。|近义词：gradually, imperceptibly, subtly, unconsciously|反义词：noticeably, consciously, perceptibly|词性:副词

insensibly:/ɪnˈsɛnsəbli/| adj.|(古用法) 无感觉的，麻木的|Lacking sensation or awareness.|例句：His hand grew insensibly numb from the cold. 他的手因寒冷而麻木了。|近义词：numb, unfeeling|反义词：sensitive, aware|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insensibilis, from in- "not" + sensibilis "perceptible," from sentire "to feel."
