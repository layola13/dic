funerary:/ˈfjuːnəreri/ | adj. | 葬礼的；丧葬的 | Relating to a funeral or burial. | The museum has a large collection of funerary artifacts. 博物馆收藏了大量的丧葬文物。|  The funerary rites were conducted with solemnity. 葬礼仪式庄严地进行。 | synonyms:  mortuary, sepulchral, burial, interment | antonyms: celebratory, festive | adjective

funerary:/ˈfjuːnərəri/ | n. | 丧葬品 | Objects associated with funerals or burials. |  The funerary included an elaborate sarcophagus. 这些丧葬品包括一个精心制作的石棺。 | (This noun usage is less common) | synonyms:  mortuary remains, burial objects | antonyms: N/A | noun
