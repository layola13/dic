mumblers:/ˈmʌmblər z/
n.
含糊不清说话的人；咕哝者
a person who mumbles; a person who speaks indistinctly.
The mumblers in the back of the room were hard to understand.
房间后面那些说话含糊不清的人很难听懂。
Synonyms: mutterer, murmurer
Antonyms: orator, speaker
noun
