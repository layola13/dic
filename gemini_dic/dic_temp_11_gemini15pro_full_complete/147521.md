overpartiality:/ˌoʊvərˌpɑːrʃiˈælɪti/
n.
过分偏袒；偏心；过分偏爱
Excessive favoritism or bias towards someone or something.
The judge was accused of overpartiality towards the defendant.  法官被指控对被告过分偏袒。
His overpartiality towards his youngest son caused resentment among his other children. 他对小儿子的过分偏爱引起了其他孩子的怨恨。

近义词: bias, favoritism, prejudice
反义词: impartiality, fairness, neutrality
词性: noun
