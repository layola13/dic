malaria: /məˈlɛəriə/ | n. | 疟疾 | An infectious disease characterized by cycles of chills, fever, and sweating, caused by a parasite that is transmitted by the bite of an infected mosquito. |  She contracted malaria while travelling in Africa.  她在非洲旅行时感染了疟疾。| synonyms: ague, fever, chills | antonyms: health, wellness | noun

malaria: /məˈlɛəriə/ | n. | 瘴气 | Noxious air or emanations formerly believed to cause disease, especially malaria. (Obsolete) |  The swamp was thought to be full of malaria.  人们认为沼泽里充满了瘴气。 | synonyms: miasma, bad air | antonyms: fresh air, pure air | noun
