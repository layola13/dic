multibuilding:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪldɪŋ/
adjective
多建筑的；多楼的
Relating to or consisting of multiple buildings.
The multibuilding complex houses several departments.  这个多建筑的综合大楼容纳了几个部门。
The university has a multibuilding campus. 这所大学有一个多建筑的校园。
Synonyms: multi-unit, complex
Antonyms: single-building, standalone
word type: adjective
