nonunison:/nɒnˈjuːnɪsən/ /nɑːnˈjuːnɪsən/
adjective
非齐奏的，不同音的
Not sounding at the same time or at the same pitch.  The singers performed a complex piece with nonunison harmonies. 歌手们表演了一首复杂的非齐奏和声作品。
例句：The nonunison chords created a dissonant yet intriguing effect. 非齐声的和弦创造了一种不和谐却又引人入胜的效果。

例句：The orchestra experimented with nonunison rhythms, giving the piece a unique texture.  管弦乐队尝试了非齐奏的节奏，赋予了这首曲子独特的质感。

近义词:discordant, asynchronous, out of sync
反义词:unison, harmonious, synchronous
词性:adjective
