breedy:/ˈbriːdi/| adj.|纯种的，优良品种的，有…特征的|Of or relating to a particular breed, especially of domestic animals; having the characteristics of a pure breed.  Showing the characteristics of one's ancestry or background.|例句：He owns a breedy-looking stallion. 他拥有一匹看起来像纯种的种马。|例句：She has a breedy elegance about her. 她有一种源于血统的优雅气质。|近义词：purebred, pedigreed, thoroughbred|反义词：mongrel, mixed-breed|词性:形容词
词源: From breed + -y.
