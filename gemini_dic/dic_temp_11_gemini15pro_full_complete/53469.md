disarmers:/dɪsˈɑːrmərz/ | n. |解除武装者；消除疑虑者；使人消气的话；缓和剂| People or things that disarm, either literally (taking away weapons) or figuratively (reducing hostility or suspicion). | Her smile was one of her most effective disarmers. 她的笑容是她最有效的缓和剂之一。| 近义词: conciliators, peacemakers, mediators | 反义词: agitators, provocateurs | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "disarm" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Disarm" comes from Old French "desarmer," from "des-" (opposite of) + "armer" (to arm), ultimately from Latin "arma" (arms, weapons).
