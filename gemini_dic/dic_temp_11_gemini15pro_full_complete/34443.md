clavecin:/ˈklævɪsɪn/ | n. |大键琴|A keyboard instrument that looks like a small grand piano but produces sound by plucking the strings rather than hammering them, popular especially in the 17th and 18th centuries.|  He played a beautiful piece on the clavecin. 他用大键琴演奏了一首优美的曲子。| synonym: harpsichord | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern piano) | noun
