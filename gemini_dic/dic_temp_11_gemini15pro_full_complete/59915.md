edental:/iːˈdɛntəl/
adjective
无齿的；缺齿的
lacking teeth; toothless
The edental old man gummed his bread.  这位无齿的老人用牙龈嚼面包。
近义词:toothless
反义词:dentate
词性:adjective


edental:/iːˈdɛntəl/
noun
无齿动物；缺齿动物
an animal that lacks teeth, such as an anteater.
The anteater is an edental. 食蚁兽是一种无齿动物。
近义词:toothless animal
反义词:dentate animal
词性:noun
