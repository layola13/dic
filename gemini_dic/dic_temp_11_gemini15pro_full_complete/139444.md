obsolescence:/ˌɒbsəˈlɛsəns/ /ˌɑːbsəˈlɛsəns/ | n. | 过时，陈旧，淘汰 |The state of being which occurs when an object, service, or practice is no longer wanted even though it may still be in good working order.  Becoming obsolete; passing out of use, going out of date. | Many once-popular kitchen appliances have now fallen into obsolescence. 许多曾经流行的厨房电器现在已经过时了。| 近义词：disuse, outmodedness, antiquity, superannuation | 反义词: currency, modernity, novelty | 词性:名词
