demilitarize:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/
|vt.|使非军事化；解除武装|To remove the military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a place or area, or to prohibit military activity there. |The treaty requires both countries to demilitarize the border zone.  该条约要求两国对边境地区进行非军事化。

The government decided to demilitarize the island. 政府决定将该岛非军事化。

|近义词：neutralize, disarm|反义词：militarize, arm|verb
