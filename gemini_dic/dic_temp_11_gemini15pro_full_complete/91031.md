huskers:/ˈhʌskərz/| n.|玉米脱壳机; 内布拉斯加州大学运动员(尤指橄榄球队员);|A husker is a machine or person that removes the husks from ears of corn.  Also, it is a nickname for athletes, especially football players, at the University of Nebraska. |The huskers worked tirelessly in the fields. 玉米脱壳机在田里不知疲倦地工作。|The Nebraska Huskers won the game. 内布拉斯加州大学运动员赢得了比赛。|近义词：corn-husker, shucker|反义词:|名词
