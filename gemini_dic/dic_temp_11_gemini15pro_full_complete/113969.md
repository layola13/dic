maggotiness:/ˈmæɡətɪnəs/
|n.|蛆虫滋生; 腐烂 |The maggotiness of the meat made me gag. 肉的腐烂让我作呕。| English explanation: The state or condition of being infested with maggots; putrefaction. |Example: The abandoned carcass exhibited advanced maggotiness.  被遗弃的尸体呈现出严重的腐烂。|Synonym: putrescence, decay, decomposition, rottenness|Antonym: freshness, purity, cleanliness|noun
