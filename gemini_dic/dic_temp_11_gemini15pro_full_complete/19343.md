bibliomania:/ˌbɪblɪəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 藏书癖；书籍收集狂 | An excessive fondness for acquiring and possessing books, often without regard to their content or usefulness.  | He had a bibliomania, his house overflowing with books he never read. 他有藏书癖，他的房子里堆满了从未读过的书。 | Example: His bibliomania led him to spend his life savings on a first edition. 他的藏书癖让他把毕生积蓄都花在了购买一本初版书上。 | Synonym: book collecting, bibliophilism | Antonym: bibliophobia (fear of books) | noun
