amygdaloid: /əˈmɪɡdəlɔɪd/ /əˈmɪɡdəˌlɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 杏仁状的；杏仁核 | Relating to or resembling an almond or the amygdaloid nucleus.  | The amygdaloid nucleus plays a crucial role in processing emotions. 杏仁核在处理情绪方面起着至关重要的作用。 | The doctor pointed to the amygdaloid area on the brain scan. 医生指着脑部扫描图上的杏仁状区域。 | 近义词: almond-shaped, tonsil-shaped | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun
