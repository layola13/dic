dishwasher:/ˈdɪʃˌwɔːʃər/ (US), /ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|洗碗工|A person who washes dishes.|例句：He got a summer job as a dishwasher. 他夏天找到了一份洗碗工的工作。|近义词：kitchen helper, busboy/girl|反义词: none|Noun
