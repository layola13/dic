imprudence:/ɪmˈpruːdəns/| n.|轻率，鲁莽|Lack of prudence; rashness.|His imprudence cost him dearly. 他的轻率让他付出了高昂的代价。|Synonyms: recklessness, foolishness, indiscretion, rashness|Antonyms: prudence, caution, wisdom, discretion|noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imprudentia, from Latin imprudens (not foreseeing, not provident, imprudent), from in- (not) + prudens (prudent).
