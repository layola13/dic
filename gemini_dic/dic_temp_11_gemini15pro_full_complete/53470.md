disarming:/dɪsˈɑːrmɪŋ/
adjective
解除武装的；使人消气的；迷人的
(of manner or behavior) tending to allay suspicion or hostility, especially through charm.
He gave a disarming smile. 他露出了令人放松警惕的微笑。
近义词:charming, winning, engaging, attractive, appealing, captivating, enchanting
反义词:alarming, off-putting, hostile, aggressive
词性:adjective

disarming:/dɪsˈɑːrmɪŋ/
verb
解除武装
take a weapon or weapons away from (a person, force, or country).
Guerrillas had been ordered to disarm. 游击队员被命令解除武装。
近义词:demilitarize, disable
反义词:arm, fortify
词性:verb
