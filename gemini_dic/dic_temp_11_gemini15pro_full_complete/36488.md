coleoptera:/ˌkoʊliˈɑːptərə/ | n. |鞘翅目（昆虫）| An order of insects having two pairs of wings, the outer pair being hard and shell-like, forming a protective cover for the inner pair.  This order includes beetles and weevils.| Beetles and weevils are examples of Coleoptera. 甲虫和象鼻虫是鞘翅目的例子。 |synonyms: beetle, weevil | antonyms: None (Coleoptera is a taxonomic order, not a descriptive term with a direct opposite) | noun
