gleaners:/ˈɡliːnərz/| n.|拾穗者；收集者|People who gather leftover grain or other crops after the harvest.  Those who collect information bit by bit.|The painting "The Gleaners" depicts three peasant women gleaning a field of wheat. 这幅画《拾穗者》描绘了三个农妇在麦田里拾穗。|近义词：collectors, gatherers, scavengers|反义词:sowers, planters|noun
