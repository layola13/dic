anormal:/æˈnɔːrməl/ | adj. |异常的，反常的| Deviating from what is standard or normal; unusual or atypical. | Her anormal behavior worried her parents. 她的异常行为使她的父母担忧。|The doctor noticed some anormal test results. 医生注意到了一些异常的测试结果。|近义词: unusual, atypical, aberrant, irregular, deviant |反义词: normal, typical, usual, standard |词性:形容词

词源:  From French anormal, from a- "not" + normal. Ultimately from Latin normalis.
