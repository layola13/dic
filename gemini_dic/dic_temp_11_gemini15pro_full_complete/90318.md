housewifery:/ˈhaʊswɪfri/ /ˈhaʊswaɪfəri/| n.|家务；家政；持家之道|The art or practice of managing a household.  The skills and knowledge required to run a home efficiently, including cooking, cleaning, sewing, and childcare.|She took pride in her housewifery and kept an immaculate home. 她为自己的家务技能感到自豪，把家里打理得一尘不染。|Synonyms: homemaking, domesticity, housekeeping|Antonyms: neglect, untidiness|noun
