breathing:/ˈbriːðɪŋ/ | n. |呼吸;  气息;  短暂的休息|The act of inhaling and exhaling air. |Her breathing was shallow and rapid. 她的呼吸又浅又快。| synonyms: respiration, inhalation, exhalation | antonyms: suffocation, asphyxiation |noun

breathing:/ˈbriːðɪŋ/ | adj. |呼吸的;  活的;  栩栩如生的|Related to the process of respiration; alive; lifelike. |A breathing sculpture. 会呼吸的雕塑 (比喻栩栩如生).| synonyms: living, alive, animate | antonyms: lifeless, inanimate, dead |adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "brēðing," derived from "brēðan" (to breathe), of Germanic origin.
