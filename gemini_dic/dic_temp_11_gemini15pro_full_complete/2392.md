adiposity:/ˌædɪˈpɒsɪti/ /ˌædɪˈpɑːsɪti/
n.
肥胖；脂肪过多
The state of being fat; obesity.
Example:  Childhood adiposity is a growing problem.  儿童肥胖是一个日益严重的问题。

Example: The study examined the link between adiposity and heart disease. 这项研究调查了肥胖与心脏病之间的联系。

近义词:obesity, corpulence, plumpness, stoutness
反义词:thinness, leanness, slenderness
词性:noun

词源:From Latin *adiposus (“fatty, greasy”), from adipis (“fat”) + -osus.
