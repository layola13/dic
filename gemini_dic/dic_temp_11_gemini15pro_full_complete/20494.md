bivalency:/ˌbaɪˈveɪlənsi/
n.|二价；双价；两难|The bivalency of antibodies is crucial for their function. 抗体的二价性对其功能至关重要.|例句：The bivalency of the antibody allows it to bind to two antigens simultaneously.  抗体的二价性使其能够同时结合两个抗原。|近义词：divalence|反义词: monovalency
词性: 名词

bivalency:/ˌbaɪˈveɪlənsi/
n.|二价性；二价|The bivalency of certain drugs can lead to complex interactions. 某些药物的二价性会导致复杂的相互作用。|例句：The bivalency of the receptor allows for cross-linking of signaling molecules. 受体的二价性允许信号分子的交联。|近义词： duality, ambivalence|反义词: univalence, monovalence
词性: 名词
