linguadental:/ˌlɪŋɡwəˈdɛntl/| adj.|舌齿音的；舌齿音|A sound produced with the tongue touching the teeth, such as /θ/ and /ð/ in English. |The sounds /θ/ and /ð/ are linguadental fricatives. /θ/和/ð/是舌齿摩擦音。|近义词:dental|反义词:None|词性:adjective
