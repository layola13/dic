outthrowing:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊɪŋ/
verb
超过…投掷；投掷得比…远
Throwing further or with more force than someone or something else.
例句：He was outthrowing all his competitors in the javelin event. 他在标枪比赛中投掷距离超过了所有对手。

例句：The young pitcher was outthrowing the veteran by a considerable margin.  这位年轻的投手比经验丰富的老投手投得远得多。
近义词：outslinging, surpassing
反义词：underthrowing
词性：动词
