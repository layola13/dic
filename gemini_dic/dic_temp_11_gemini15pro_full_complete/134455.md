nonjailable:/nɒnˈdʒeɪləbl/
adjective
不可监禁的
Not subject to imprisonment; that cannot or may not be jailed.
The offense was nonjailable, so he was released with a fine.  这个罪行是不可监禁的，所以他被罚款释放。
Parking violations are typically nonjailable offenses. 违规停车通常是不可监禁的违法行为。
Synonym:nonimprisonable, nonincarcerable
Antonym:jailable, imprisonable
词性:adjective
