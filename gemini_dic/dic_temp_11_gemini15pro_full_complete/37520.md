common-name:/ˈkɑːmən neɪm/ | adj. n.| 常见名称，俗名 |A name that is commonly used to refer to something, especially a plant or animal, as opposed to its scientific name. |  The common name for *Canis lupus familiaris* is dog.  *犬科动物*的俗称是狗。 | synonyms: vernacular name, trivial name, popular name | antonyms: scientific name, technical name, binomial name | adjective, noun
