chain-smokers:/ˈtʃeɪn ˌsmoʊkərz/| n.|连锁吸烟者，一支接一支吸烟的人|A person who smokes cigarettes continuously, lighting one after another.|He was a notorious chain-smoker, rarely seen without a cigarette in his hand. 他是个臭名昭著的老烟枪，手里很少有不夹着香烟的时候。|Synonym: heavy smoker, inveterate smoker|Antonym: non-smoker, occasional smoker|noun
