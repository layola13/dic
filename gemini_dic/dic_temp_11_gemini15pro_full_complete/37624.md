communist:/ˈkɒmjunɪst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmjənɪst/ (US)| n. adj.|共产主义者；共产主义的|A person who believes in or supports communism, a political and economic system in which the major productive resources in a society—such as mines, factories, and farms—are owned by the public or the state, and wealth is divided among citizens equally or according to individual need.|例句：He is a communist. 他是一名共产主义者。|近义词：Marxist, socialist, leftist|反义词：capitalist, conservative, right-winger|词性: noun, adjective
