adolescence:/ˌædəˈlesəns/ | n. | 青春期 | the period following the onset of puberty during which a young person develops from a child into an adult |  She struggled with the typical anxieties of adolescence. 她正经历着青春期常见的焦虑。| synonyms: youth, puberty, teens | antonyms: adulthood, infancy, childhood | noun

adolescences:/ˌædəˈlesənsiz/ | n. | 青春期（复数，罕用）| multiple periods of adolescence; usually used in a technical or specialized context referring to different groups or types of adolescence |  Researchers studied the adolescences of different cultural groups. 研究人员研究了不同文化群体的青春期。 | synonyms: youths, puberties (less common and often awkward) | antonyms: adulthoods, infancies, childhoods (less common and often awkward) | noun
