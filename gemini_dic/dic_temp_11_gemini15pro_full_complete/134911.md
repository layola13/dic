nonmusicalness:/ˌnɑːnˈmjuːzɪkəlnəs/
n.
缺乏音乐性，不懂音乐
lack of musical talent or appreciation; inability to appreciate or understand music
His nonmusicalness was apparent when he couldn't tell the difference between a violin and a viola. 他分不清小提琴和中提琴，可见他缺乏音乐性。
近义词：unmusicality, tone-deafness
反义词：musicality, musical talent
