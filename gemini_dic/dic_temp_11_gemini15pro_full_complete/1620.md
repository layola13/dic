acidly:/ˈæsɪdli/ | adv. | 酸溜溜地，尖刻地 | In a sharp, bitter, or sarcastic manner.  |  "She remarked acidly that his new suit looked cheap." 她尖刻地说他的新西装看起来很廉价。 |  bitterly, sharply, sarcastically, caustically |  sweetly, kindly, pleasantly | 副词

acidly:/ˈæsɪdli/ | adv. | 酸性地 |  Having the properties of an acid. (This meaning is less common in everyday usage.) | "The solution reacted acidly with the metal."  该溶液与金属发生酸性反应。| corrosively | mildly, neutrally, alkaline | 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From acid + -ly.  The word "acid" ultimately comes from the Latin "acidus," meaning "sour" or "sharp."
