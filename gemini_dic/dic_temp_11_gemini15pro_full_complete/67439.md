extinguishment:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃmənt/
n.
熄灭，消灭，扑灭
the act of extinguishing something, especially a fire
The fire department achieved rapid extinguishment of the blaze. 消防部门迅速扑灭了大火。
extinction, suppression, termination
ignition, kindling
noun
