azygous: /ˈeɪzɪɡəs/ /əˈzaɪɡəs/ | adj. | 不成对的，单一的 | Occurring singly; not one of a pair.  Relating to an anatomical structure that is not paired, having no corresponding part on the opposite side of the body. | The azygous vein is a major vessel in the thorax. 奇静脉是胸腔中的一条主要血管。|  The azygous lobe is a congenital anatomical variation of the lung. 奇静脉叶是肺的一种先天性解剖变异。| singular, unpaired | paired | adjective
