flailing:/ˈfleɪlɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|挥舞；挣扎；打；乱动|moving or swinging your arms or legs in a wild, uncontrolled way; failing or struggling to succeed or make progress. |The drowning man was flailing in the water. 溺水的人在水中挣扎。|近义词：thrashing, waving, struggling, failing|反义词:succeeding, thriving, controlled, still|verb, noun, adjective

flailing:/ˈfleɪlɪŋ/| adj.|失败的；挣扎的|failing or struggling to succeed or make progress. |The flailing company is trying to avoid bankruptcy.  这家处境艰难的公司正试图避免破产。|近义词：struggling, failing, floundering|反义词:thriving, successful, flourishing|adjective
