circumnutation:/ˌsɜːrkəmnyuˈteɪʃən/ | n. |  回旋转头运动; 环旋运动 | The circular or elliptical movement of the tip of a growing stem, shoot, or root.  This movement is caused by differential growth rates on opposite sides of the plant organ. |  The circumnutation of the vine allowed it to find a suitable support.  藤蔓的回旋转头运动使它能够找到合适的支撑。 |  rotary movement, circular motion, gyration | N/A | noun
