ambulatorial:/ˌæmbjʊləˈtɔːriəl/
| adj.|
|步行的，适合步行的，可走动的|
|Relating to walking; able to walk; of or for walking. |
|The patient was ambulatorial after the surgery.  病人手术后能够走动。|

|The ambulatorial patient was encouraged to take short walks around the hospital ward.  鼓励这位可以走动的病人绕着病房短距离散步。|
|近义词：walking, peripatetic, mobile, itinerant|
|反义词：bedridden, immobile, sedentary|
|词性: adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ambulatorius, from Latin ambulator ("walker"), from ambulo ("I walk"), from amb- ("around, about") + -ulo (frequentative suffix).
