gloriosa:/ɡlɔːriˈoʊsə/ /ɡlɔːrɪˈoʊsə/| n. |嘉兰百合，火焰百合|A genus of tuberous climbing plants with showy, lily-like flowers, typically red and yellow.  It is also known as flame lily, climbing lily, creeping lily, glory lily, fire lily, and tiger claw.|The gloriosa in her garden was a vibrant splash of color.|近义词：climbing lily, flame lily|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
词源: From Latin *gloriōsus*  "glorious, full of glory," from *glōria* "glory."
