indusiated: /ɪnˈdʌsɪeɪtɪd/
adjective
被毛覆盖的；有毛状物的
Covered with short, soft hairs or scales, like an indusium.
The indusiated fern frond unfurled slowly.  被毛覆盖的蕨类植物叶子慢慢展开。
The underside of the leaf was indusiated. 叶子的背面覆盖着细毛。
近义词: hairy, pubescent, downy
反义词: glabrous, smooth
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *indusiatus, past participle of *indusiare "to clothe in an indusium," from indusium "tunic, undergarment"
