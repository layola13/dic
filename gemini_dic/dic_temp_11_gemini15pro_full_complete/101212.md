invisibility:/ɪnˌvɪzəˈbɪləti/
n.
无形；隐形；看不见
The state of being invisible; not able to be seen.
The cloak granted him invisibility. 这件斗篷让他隐形了。
近义词：unseenness, imperceptibility
反义词：visibility, conspicuousness
词性：名词
