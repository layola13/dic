hypervirulently:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪrələntli/ | adv. | 极度强毒地，极度剧毒地 |  In a manner characterized by extreme virulence or pathogenicity; with exceptional ability to cause disease. | The bacteria spread hypervirulently through the population, causing widespread illness. 细菌在人群中极度强毒地传播，造成了大范围的疾病。| Near Synonyms: exceptionally virulently, highly aggressively (in terms of disease), extremely pathogenically | Near Antonyms: mildly, weakly (in terms of disease effects), avirulently | Part of Speech: Adverb
