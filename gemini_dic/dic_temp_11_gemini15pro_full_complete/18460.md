bemol:/ˈbeɪmɒl/ (US), /ˈbiːmɒl/ (UK)| n.| 降号; 降半音 |A symbol (♭) placed before a note to indicate that it is to be lowered in pitch by a semitone. |The bemol lowers the note by a half step. 降号将音符降低半音。| The composer added a bemol to the F. 作曲家在F上加了一个降号。| synonym: flat | antonym: sharp | noun
