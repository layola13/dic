hover:/ˈhʌvər/ (US), /ˈhɒvə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|盘旋，徘徊；犹豫不决|To remain in one place in the air. To remain poised uncertainly between two courses of action.|The hummingbird hovered over the flower.  蜂鸟在花朵上盘旋。|Synonyms: linger, float, drift, waver|Antonyms: settle, land, plummet|verb

hover:/ˈhʌvər/ (US), /ˈhɒvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|盘旋，徘徊|The state of remaining suspended.|The helicopter's hover was remarkably steady. 直升机的盘旋非常稳定。|Synonyms: suspension, poise|Antonyms: descent, landing|noun
