combed:/koʊmd/ | adj. | 梳理过的，整齐的 | Having had a comb passed through it; neat and tidy. |  Her hair was neatly combed. 她的头发梳得很整齐。 |  The farmer combed the wool. 农夫梳理羊毛。| synonyms: groomed, tidy, neat, arranged | antonyms: uncombed, messy, tangled, disheveled | word type: adjective/verb (past participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cambian, from Proto-Germanic *kambjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gombʰ- (“tooth, peg”). Cognate with Old High German camben (“to comb”), Old Norse kemba (“to comb”). Related to comb (n.).
