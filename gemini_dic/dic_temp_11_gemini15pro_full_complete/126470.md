mythologian:/ˌmɪθəˈloʊdʒən/
n.
神话学家；神话研究者
A scholar who studies myths and mythology.
The mythologian lectured on the symbolism in Greek myths.  这位神话学家就希腊神话中的象征意义进行了讲座。
