differentially:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəli/| adv.|区别地，不同地，差动地|In mathematics, it refers to an infinitesimal change in a variable.  More generally, it means in a way that distinguishes between things or people. |The two groups were treated differentially. 这两组受到了不同的对待。|Synonyms: distinctively, separately, unequally, variously|Antonyms: equally, uniformly, similarly|adverb

differentially:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəli/| adj.|差别的，特异的|Showing a difference; distinctive.  Often used in technical contexts. |Differentially expressed genes play a crucial role in disease development.  差异表达基因在疾病发展中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: distinctive, characteristic, peculiar|Antonyms: common, similar, uniform|adjective
