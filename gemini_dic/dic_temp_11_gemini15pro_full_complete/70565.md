fill-up:/ˈfɪlˌʌp/ | n. | 加满，充满；(加油站的)加油；(餐馆的)饱餐 | A fill-up is the act of filling something completely, especially a vehicle's fuel tank or a person's stomach. |  I stopped at the gas station for a fill-up. 我在加油站停下来加满了油。| synonyms: refill, top-up | antonyms: depletion, emptying | noun

fill-up:/ˈfɪlˌʌp/ | adj. | 令人饱腹的 |  A fill-up meal is one that leaves you feeling full and satisfied. | That all-you-can-eat buffet was a real fill-up!  那个自助餐真是让人吃得饱饱的！| synonyms: satisfying, filling | antonyms: unsatisfying, meager | adjective
