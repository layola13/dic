impelling:/ɪmˈpɛlɪŋ/ | adj. |  驱使的，迫使的，激励的 | Driving or urging forward; motivating. |  The impelling force of hunger drove the animals to search for food.  饥饿的驱动力迫使动物们去寻找食物。 | Synonyms: compelling, urging, driving, motivating, pushing, propelling | Antonyms: restraining, hindering, deterring | adjective

impel:/ɪmˈpɛl/ | v. |  驱使，迫使，激励 | Drive, force, or urge (someone) to do something. |  He felt impelled to help the poor. 他感到不得不帮助穷人。 | Synonyms: compel, force, oblige, require, urge, prompt, motivate | Antonyms: restrain, hinder, deter | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "impellere,"  from "in-" (in, into, on, onto, towards) + "pellere" (to push, drive).
