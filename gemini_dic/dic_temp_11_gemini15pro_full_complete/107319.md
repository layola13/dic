lambdacism:/ˈlæmdəˌsɪzəm/| n.|舌尖音化，l音化|The substitution of l for other sounds, especially r.  The pronunciation of r as l.|His lambdacism was noticeable whenever he tried to say "rabbit." 每当他试图说“兔子”时，他的舌尖音化就很明显。|近义词:lallation|反义词:rhotacism|词性:noun
