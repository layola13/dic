nonanalytically:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 非解析地；非分析地 | In a way that does not involve analysis; without analysis.  It describes a process or approach that does not rely on breaking down a complex whole into its constituent parts for examination. |  The problem was solved nonanalytically, through intuition. 这个问题是通过直觉而非分析解决的。| synonym: intuitively, holistically, synthetically | antonym: analytically, systematically | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + analytical + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Analytical" ultimately derives from the Greek "analuein," meaning "to unloose, resolve."
