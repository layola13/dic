impinguation:/ɪmpɪŋˈɡweɪʃən/| n. |肥胖，肥育|The process of making something fat or plump; the state of being fattened.  The impinguation of livestock for market is a common practice.|The farmer focused on the impinguation of his pigs before the winter months.  农民在冬季来临之前专注于育肥他的猪。|Near Synonyms: fattening, enriching, plumping|Near Antonyms: emaciation, thinning, weight loss|noun

impinguate:/ɪmˈpɪŋɡweɪt/| vt. |使肥胖，使肥沃|To make fat or plump; to fatten.  Specialized fertilizers are used to impinguate the soil.|The rich diet impinguated the already overweight dog.  丰富的饮食使本来就超重的狗更加肥胖。|Near Synonyms: fatten, enrich, nourish|Near Antonyms: emaciate, thin, starve|verb
