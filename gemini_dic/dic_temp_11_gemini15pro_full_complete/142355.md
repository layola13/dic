orthometry: /ɔːrˈθɒmɪtri/ /ɔːrˈθɑːmətri/ | n. |正射投影法，正射测量学| The science of accurate measurement of the earth's surface, especially as it relates to determining elevation and representing it on maps. |例句：Orthometry is crucial for creating accurate topographic maps. 正射测量学对于创建精确的地形图至关重要。

例句：The surveyor used orthometry to determine the exact height of the mountain. 测量员使用正射投影法确定了山的确切高度。|近义词：geodesy, surveying |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek orthos "straight, right, true" + metron "measure."
