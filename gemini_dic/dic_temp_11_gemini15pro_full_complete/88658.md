hoarier:/ˈhɔːriər (UK), ˈhɔːriər (US)| adj.|更灰白的，更老的，更令人害怕的|Covered with more hoar (white or gray hair-like growth); older and more venerable; more inspiring awe or fear.|The old man's beard was hoarier than I remembered.  这位老人的胡子比我记忆中的更灰白了。

The castle, with its hoarier towers, seemed more menacing under the stormy sky.  这座城堡，有着更古老的塔楼，在暴风雨的天空下显得更加危险。|近义词：grayer, older, more ancient, more venerable, more awe-inspiring|反义词：younger, newer, less ancient|词性:形容词
词源：From Middle English *horer, comparative of hor (“hoary”), from Old English hār (“hoary, gray-haired”), from Proto-Germanic *hairaz (“gray, hoary”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱey- (“gray”). Cognate with Dutch haar (“hair”), German Haar (“hair”).
