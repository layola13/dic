long-sightednesses:/ˌlɒŋˈsaɪtɪdnəsɪz/ /ˌlɔːŋˈsaɪtɪdnəsɪz/
noun
远视，目光长远
The state of being long-sighted.  The ability to see things clearly at a distance but not close up.
Example:  His long-sightednesses meant he needed glasses to read.  他的远视意味着他需要眼镜才能阅读。
Example:  She has inherited long-sightednesses from her father. 她遗传了她父亲的远视。
Synonyms: hyperopia, hypermetropia, farsightedness
Antonyms: short-sightedness, myopia, nearsightedness
