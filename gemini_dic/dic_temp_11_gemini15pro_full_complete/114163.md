mahogany:/məˈhɒɡəni/ (UK) /məˈhɑːɡəni/ (US)| n. adj.|桃花心木；红褐色|A type of tropical tree with reddish-brown wood, or the wood itself. Also, a reddish-brown color.|例句：The table is made of solid mahogany. 这张桌子是用实心桃花心木做的。

例句：He has a deep mahogany tan. 他晒成了深红褐色。|近义词：redwood, cedar (for the wood); maroon, auburn (for the color)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective

mahogany:/məˈhɒɡəni/ (UK) /məˈhɑːɡəni/ (US)| adj.|红褐色的|Having the color of mahogany wood.|例句：She wore a mahogany dress. 她穿着一件红褐色的连衣裙。

例句：His mahogany-colored eyes sparkled. 他红褐色的眼睛闪闪发光。|近义词：maroon, auburn, reddish-brown|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
