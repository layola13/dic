emotional: /ɪˈmoʊʃənəl/ (US), /ɪˈməʊʃənəl/ (UK) | adj. | 情绪化的，感情上的 | Relating to or characterized by emotion; readily affected by or expressing emotion. | She became very emotional when talking about her childhood. 她谈到童年时变得非常情绪化。|  He gave an emotional speech about the loss of his friend. 他就失去朋友一事发表了充满感情的演讲。 | sentimental, passionate, sensitive |  logical, rational, unemotional | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēmotionālis*, from Latin ēmovēre  "to move out, remove, agitate," from ex- "out" + movēre "to move".
