abirritant:/æbˈɪrɪtənt/
noun
减少刺激的;缓和刺激的;消刺激剂
An abirritant is a substance that diminishes irritation.
The doctor prescribed an abirritant cream for her rash. 医生给她开了个消炎药膏来治疗皮疹。
近义词:soother, demulcent, emollient, palliative
反义词:irritant, stimulant
词性:n.
