cheesiest:/ˈtʃiːziɪst/| adj.|最俗气的，最庸俗的|Superlative form of cheesy: most cheesy.  It describes something that is excessively sentimental, overly dramatic, or inauthentic in a way that is often considered embarrassing or tacky.  It often implies a lack of sophistication or good taste.|That was the cheesiest pick-up line I've ever heard. 那是我听过的最俗气的搭讪台词了。|Synonyms: corny, tacky, schmaltzy, hackneyed, clichéd|Antonyms: sophisticated, tasteful, refined, elegant|Etymology: From "cheese," likely referring to a quality of being overly rich or artificial, like processed cheese.|Part of Speech: Adjective
