biologically:/ˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ˌbaɪəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/
adjective
生物学地；从生物学角度
in a way that relates to the study of living things
例句：Biologically, humans and chimpanzees are closely related. 从生物学角度来看，人类和黑猩猩密切相关。

例句：The twins are biologically identical but have very different personalities. 这对双胞胎生物学上完全相同，但性格却截然不同。

近义词：naturally, organically
反义词：synthetically, artificially
词性：adverb

词源：
From biological + -ly.  Biological comes from biology + -ical. Biology comes from Greek bios "life" + -logy "study of".
