dictate:/dɪkˈteɪt/ | v. |口述；命令；规定|to say words for someone else to write down or to tell someone exactly what they must do or how they must behave | The doctor dictated a letter to his secretary. 医生向他的秘书口述了一封信。| 近义词: command, order, prescribe | 反义词: transcribe, obey | verb

dictated:/dɪkˈteɪtɪd/ | v. (past tense and past participle of dictate)|口述的；命令的；规定的 | having been said aloud for someone to write down or having been given orders | The dictated notes were then typed up. 口述的笔记随后被打印出来。 | 近义词: commanded, ordered, prescribed | 反义词: transcribed, obeyed | verb
