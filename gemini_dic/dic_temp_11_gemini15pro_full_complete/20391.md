bitches:/ˈbɪtʃɪz/| n.|母狗；(冒犯语)泼妇，婊子|A female dog.  A spiteful or unpleasant woman (offensive).|例句：The farmer had several bitches in his kennel. 农夫在他的狗舍里有几只母狗。

bitches:/ˈbɪtʃɪz/| v. (third-person singular simple present bitches, present participle bitching, simple past and past participle bitched)|抱怨，发牢骚|(informal) To complain. |例句：He's always bitching about the weather. 他总是抱怨天气。

例句：She was bitching about her boss to her coworkers.  她正在向同事抱怨她的老板。|近义词：complain, grumble, whine, moan|反义词：praise, compliment|词性:动词
