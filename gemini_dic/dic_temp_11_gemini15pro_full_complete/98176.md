inscriptive:/ɪnˈskrɪptɪv/
adjective
题写的；铭刻的；刻写的
Relating to, or suitable for inscription.
The inscriptive evidence on the tombstone helped identify the deceased. 墓碑上的题字有助于识别死者。
The archaeologist carefully examined the inscriptive markings on the ancient pottery. 考古学家仔细检查了古代陶器上的铭文标记。
近义词: engraved, inscribed
反义词: blank, uninscribed
词性: adjective

inscriptive:/ɪnˈskrɪptɪv/
noun
题词；铭文
An inscription. (This usage is less common than the adjective form)
The inscriptive on the base of the statue commemorated the founder. 雕像底座上的铭文是为了纪念创始人。（这种用法不如形容词形式常见）
The museum displayed several inscriptives from ancient Rome.  博物馆展出了几件来自古罗马的铭文。(这种用法不如形容词形式常见)
近义词: inscription, lettering
反义词:  (No direct antonym for the noun form)
词性: noun
