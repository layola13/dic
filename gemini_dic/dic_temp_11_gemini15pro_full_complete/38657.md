concussant:/kənˈkʌsənt/
adjective
震荡的，使脑震荡的
Having the power to shake violently; concussive.
The concussant force of the explosion shattered nearby windows. 爆炸的震荡力震碎了附近的窗户。
近义词：concussive, jarring, shocking
反义词：soothing, calming
词性：adjective
词源：From Latin concutere, from con- ‘together’ + quatere ‘to shake’.
