lambkin:/ˈlæmkɪn/| n. |小羊，羔羊，(尤指)可爱的幼畜|A young lamb, especially one that is considered cute or endearing. |The little lambkin frolicked in the meadow. 小羊在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：lamb, kid |反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 adult sheep 成年羊)|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lambken, *lambkyn, equivalent to lamb +‎ -kin. Cognate with Scots lambikie (“a little lamb”).
