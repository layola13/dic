glanderous:/ˈɡlændərəs/
adj.
诽谤的，恶意中伤的
Containing or involving slander; defamatory.
例句：His glanderous remarks about his opponent cost him the election. 他对对手的诽谤性言论让他输掉了选举。

例句：The newspaper published a glanderous article about the celebrity. 这家报纸发表了一篇关于这位名人的诽谤文章。

近义词：slanderous, defamatory, libelous, calumnious
反义词：complimentary, flattering, laudatory
词性: adjective
