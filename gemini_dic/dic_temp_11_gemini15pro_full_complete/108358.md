lavishers:/ˈlævɪʃərz/| n.|挥霍者；慷慨给予者|People who spend or give something freely or generously. |The lavishers showered the bride and groom with expensive gifts. 挥霍者们向新娘新郎赠送了昂贵的礼物。|近义词：spenders, givers, philanthropists|反义词：misers, hoarders|noun

lavish:/ˈlævɪʃ/| adj. vt.|奢华的；丰富的；慷慨给予| (adj.) Sumptuously rich, elaborate, or luxurious; (v.) To bestow something in generous or extravagant quantities upon.| (adj.) The lavish banquet included caviar and champagne. 奢华的宴会上有鱼子酱和香槟。 (v.)  They lavished attention on their guests. 他们对客人关怀备至。|近义词：(adj.) luxurious, opulent, extravagant; (v.) shower, bestow|反义词：(adj.) meager, sparse, frugal; (v.) stint, withhold|adjective, verb
