mendiant:/ˈmɑːndɪənt/ /ˈmɛndɪənt/| n.|乞丐；托钵僧；（尤指圣诞节期间吃的）杏仁巧克力糖|A beggar, especially a friar of the mendicant orders. A confection traditionally made of chocolate and decorated with dried fruit and nuts, typically eaten at Christmas.|例句：He gave some money to the mendiant. 他给了那个乞丐一些钱。

例句：She made mendiants for Christmas gifts. 她做了杏仁巧克力糖作为圣诞礼物。|近义词：beggar, pauper, mendicant friar (for person); confection, chocolate (for candy)|反义词:philanthropist, benefactor (for person)|词性:noun


mendiant:/ˈmɑːndɪənt/ /ˈmɛndɪənt/| adj.|行乞的；托钵的|Of or relating to begging or mendicancy.|例句：He lived a mendiant life. 他过着乞讨的生活。

例句：The mendiant friar relied on alms. 托钵僧依靠施舍生活。|近义词：begging, mendicant|反义词:wealthy, affluent|词性:adjective
