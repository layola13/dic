motley:/ˈmɒtli/ (UK) /ˈmɑːtli/ (US)| adj. n.|杂色的，混杂的；杂色衣服，混杂的一群人|A motley collection or group of things is made up of many different kinds of things.|例句：The market was a motley collection of stalls selling everything from fruit to furniture.  市场上各种各样的摊位都有，从水果到家具，应有尽有。

例句：She wore a motley assortment of jewelry. 她戴着各种各样的珠宝。|近义词：diverse, varied, heterogeneous, assorted, miscellaneous|反义词：uniform, homogeneous, similar|词性:形容词, 名词


motley:/ˈmɒtli/ (UK) /ˈmɑːtli/ (US)|n.|杂色衣服，混杂的一群人|A motley crew or group is made up of a strange mixture of people.|例句：A motley crew of musicians gathered for the jam session. 一群形形色色的音乐家聚集在一起进行即兴演奏。

例句：The team was a motley of experienced professionals and enthusiastic amateurs.  这个团队是由经验丰富的专业人士和充满热情的业余爱好者组成的杂牌军。|近义词：assortment, collection, mixture, array|反义词：group, team (when referring to a unified group)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *motteley*, from Old French *matelé*, past participle of *mateler* "to curdle," from *mate* "clot of milk, curd" (source also of English *mad*). The original sense referred to cloth woven with yarn dyed different colors.  The meaning later broadened to refer to any diverse or mixed group.
