baraesthesiometer:/ˌbærɪsˌθiːziˈɒmɪtər/ /ˌbærɪsˌθiːziˈɑːmɪtər/
n.
压觉计，触压觉计
An instrument for measuring sensitivity to pressure, especially of the skin.
The doctor used a baraesthesiometer to assess the patient's loss of sensation in their fingertips. 医生用压觉计评估病人指尖的感觉丧失。
Synonyms: pressure aesthesiometer, esthesiometer
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but conceptually: analgesic, anesthetic - things that *reduce* sensitivity)
noun
