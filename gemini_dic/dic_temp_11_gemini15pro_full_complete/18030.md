begnaw:/bɪˈnɔː/ /bɪˈnɑː/
verb
啃，咬，折磨
To bite or gnaw persistently, especially at something hard.  To worry or irritate persistently.
The dog begnawed the bone. 狗啃着骨头。
Guilt begnawed at her conscience. 内疚啃噬着她的良心。
近义词: gnaw, nibble, erode, corrode, worry, plague, torment
反义词: soothe, comfort, heal
词性: verb
