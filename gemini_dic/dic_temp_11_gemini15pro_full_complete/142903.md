out-peopled:/ˌaʊtˈpiːpəld/
verb
人口超过；人口多于
Having a larger population than another area or group.
The city was quickly out-peopled by the burgeoning suburb.  这个城市很快就被迅速发展的郊区在人口上超过了。
The small island nation was out-peopled by its larger neighbor. 这个岛国的人口少于它较大的邻国。
outnumber, surpass, exceed
be outnumbered by, be less populous than
