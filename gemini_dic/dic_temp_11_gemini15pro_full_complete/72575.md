fluxes:/ˈflʌksɪz/
n.| 流量; 变迁; 熔剂|A continuous moving on or passing by (as of a stream).|The magnetic flux is strongest at the poles. 磁通量在两极最强。|近义词：flows, currents, changes, variations|反义词:stasis, stability
fluxes:/ˈflʌksɪz/
v.| 熔化; 流动; 变化|To become liquid through heat; melt.|The artist fluxed the colors together. 艺术家将颜色熔合在一起。|近义词：melt, flow, vary, change|反义词:solidify, stabilize, freeze

PHRASE->
in flux:  处于不断变化的状态  The political situation is in flux. 政治局势动荡不安。
flux and reflux:  涨落; 来回流动  The tides are an example of flux and reflux. 潮汐是涨落的例子。
magnetic flux: 磁通量 The magnetic flux density is a measure of the strength of a magnetic field. 磁通密度是衡量磁场强度的指标。
flux density: 通量密度 The flux density of light decreases with distance from the source. 光的通量密度随着与光源距离的增加而减小。
flux of neutrons: 中子通量  The flux of neutrons in a nuclear reactor is carefully controlled. 核反应堆中的中子通量受到严格控制。
