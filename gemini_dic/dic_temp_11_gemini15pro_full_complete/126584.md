nagana:/nəˈɡɑːnə/ | n. |昏睡病，非洲锥虫病|A disease of cattle, horses, camels, etc., of tropical Africa, caused by trypanosomes. It is characterized by fever, lethargy, and edema, and often results in death.|The cattle were infected with nagana.|Synonyms: trypanosomiasis, sleeping sickness (in animals)|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun

naganas:/nəˈɡɑːnəz/ | n. |昏睡病，非洲锥虫病（复数）|Plural form of nagana, a disease of cattle, horses, camels, etc., of tropical Africa, caused by trypanosomes. |The farmer lost several cows to naganas.|Synonyms: trypanosomiases, sleeping sicknesses (in animals)|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun
