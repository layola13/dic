annalistically:/ˌænəlˈɪstɪkli/
adverb
编年史地；按年代顺序地
In a manner of relating to or resembling annals; chronologically.
He annalistically documented the history of the town, meticulously recording each year's events. 他按年代顺序记录了小镇的历史，一丝不苟地记录了每年的事件。
Synonym: chronologically, historically
Antonym: randomly, haphazardly
词性: 副词
