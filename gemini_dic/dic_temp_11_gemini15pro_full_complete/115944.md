marrer:/ˈmɑːrər/ (UK) /ˈmærər/ (US)| n.| 阻碍，障碍，麻烦|Something that spoils or prevents the success or enjoyment of something; a difficulty or problem.|The heavy rain put a marrer on our picnic plans. 大雨破坏了我们的野餐计划。|近义词：obstacle, hindrance, impediment, snag, hitch, setback|反义词:advantage, benefit|词性:名词

词源: From the Anglo-Norman French *marrer, from *marrir "to damage, spoil," from Late Latin *marrire, perhaps related to Latin marcere "to wither."
