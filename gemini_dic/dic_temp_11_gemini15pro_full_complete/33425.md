churchwarden: /ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdən/ | n. | 教会委员 | An elected lay officer in a parish church, responsible for the upkeep of the church building and its contents, and for certain secular matters. |  The churchwarden locked up after the service. 教堂仪式结束后，教会委员锁上了门。| The churchwardens are responsible for the church's finances. 教会委员负责教堂的财务。|Synonyms: vestryman, sidesman (in some contexts) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
