cookie:/ˈkʊki/ (US), /ˈkʊki/ (UK)| n. |饼干；小甜饼|A small, flat, sweet baked good, often containing chocolate chips, nuts, or other ingredients. |例句：I baked a batch of chocolate chip cookies. 我烤了一批巧克力饼干。

例句：She offered me a freshly baked cookie. 她给了我一块刚烤好的饼干。

近义词：biscuit (UK), cracker, treat|反义词: None|词性: Noun


cookie:/ˈkʊki/ (US), /ˈkʊki/ (UK)| n. |(计算机)小型文本文件|A small text file that a website stores on a user's computer to remember information about the user. |例句：The website uses cookies to track user preferences. 该网站使用 cookie 来跟踪用户偏好。

例句：Clear your browser's cookies to improve privacy. 清除浏览器的 cookie 以提高隐私性。

近义词：HTTP cookie, web cookie, browser cookie, internet cookie|反义词: None|词性: Noun
