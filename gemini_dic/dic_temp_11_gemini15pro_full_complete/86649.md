hellebore: /ˈhɛləˌbɔːr/ (US), /ˈhɛlɪbɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |嚏根草；圣诞玫瑰|A perennial plant of the buttercup family, typically having dark green, leathery leaves and clusters of greenish, purplish, or white flowers, which appear in winter.|例句：The hellebores are blooming beautifully in the garden this winter.  今年冬天花园里的嚏根草开得很漂亮。

例句：She planted hellebores for their winter interest. 她种植嚏根草是为了它们在冬天的观赏价值。|近义词：winter rose, Christmas rose|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *helleborus, from Latin helleborum, from Greek ἑλλέβορος (helléboros).  The Greek word is of uncertain origin, possibly from ἔλειν (elein, “to injure”) + βορά (bora, “food”), referring to its poisonous qualities.
