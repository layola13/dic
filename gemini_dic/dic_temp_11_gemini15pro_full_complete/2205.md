addle:/ˈædəl/| v. |使糊涂，使混乱；腐坏|To make someone confused and unable to think clearly; to become rotten (usually of eggs).|The heat addled my brain.  高温使我头脑昏沉。|The eggs were addled in the hot sun.  鸡蛋在烈日下腐坏了。|近义词：confuse, muddle, bewilder, rot (for eggs)|反义词：clarify, enlighten, freshen|词性：动词
