neuromere: /ˈnjʊərəˌmɪər/ | n. | 神经元节,体节 | One of a series of repeating segments in the developing hindbrain and spinal cord of vertebrates. These segments contribute to the organization of the nervous system. | The development of the cranial nerves is closely related to the neuromeres in the hindbrain.  颅神经的发育与后脑中的神经元节密切相关。

The researchers studied the expression of Hox genes in individual neuromeres.  研究人员研究了Hox基因在各个神经元节中的表达。| synonym: segment, metamere | antonym:  (No direct antonym exists for a structural unit like a neuromere.) | noun
