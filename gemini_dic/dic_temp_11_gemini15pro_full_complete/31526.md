charitable:/ˈtʃærɪtəbl/| adj.|慈善的；仁慈的；宽厚的|Relating to or connected with charity; showing kindness and tolerance in judging others; generous in giving to those in need.|例句：She made a charitable donation to the Red Cross. 她向红十字会做了慈善捐赠。|例句：He always takes a charitable view of people's mistakes. 他总是对人们的错误持宽容的态度。|近义词：benevolent, philanthropic, generous, kind, tolerant|反义词：uncharitable, mean, selfish|词性:形容词

charitable:/ˈtʃærɪtəbl/| n.|慈善团体，慈善事业（通常用复数形式）|An organization or cause that helps people in need.|例句：Many charitables benefit from the annual fundraising gala. 许多慈善机构受益于一年一度的募捐晚会。|例句：Proceeds from the event will go to local charitables. 活动的收益将捐给当地的慈善机构。|近义词：charities, non-profit organizations, philanthropic organizations|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 for-profit organizations)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *charitable*, from Old French *charitable*, from Late Latin *charitativus*, from Latin *charitas* ("charity") + *-ivus* (suffix forming adjectives of appurtenance). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*ǵʰer-* ("to like, desire").
