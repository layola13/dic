gastriloquy: /ɡæsˈtrɪləkwi/ | n. | 腹语术 | The art of speaking in such a way that the voice seems to come from somewhere other than the speaker, typically from a dummy or puppet. |  He amazed the audience with his gastriloquy, making the puppet seem to talk on its own. 他用腹语术使观众惊叹，使木偶看起来像是在自己说话。 | ventriloquism |  - | noun
