off-Broadway:/ˌɔːf ˈbrɔːdweɪ/ | adj. n. | 百老汇以外的, 非百老汇的；外百老汇演出；外百老汇剧院 |  Refers to professional theatrical productions in New York City that are presented in smaller theaters than those on Broadway, typically with fewer than 500 seats. These productions are often more experimental or intimate than large-scale Broadway shows. |  Example:  We saw a fantastic off-Broadway play last weekend.  我们上周末看了一场精彩的非百老汇戏剧。 | Synonym:  independent theater, experimental theater | Antonym: Broadway |  adjective, noun
