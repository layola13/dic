onto:/ˈɑːntuː/ /ˈɔːntoʊ/ | prep. adv. | 在...之上；到...之上；意识到；理解 |  Expressing movement towards a position on a surface or object. Also used figuratively to mean aware of or understanding something. | The cat jumped onto the table. 猫跳到桌子上。

He's really onto something with this new idea. 他这个新想法确实很有价值。 | 近义词：on, upon, aboard, aware of | 反义词：off | 词性: 介词, 副词
