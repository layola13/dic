coccyx:/ˈkɒksɪks/ /ˈkɑːksɪks/| n.|尾骨|The small triangular bone at the base of the spinal column in humans and some apes, formed of fused vestigial vertebrae.|He injured his coccyx in a fall.|近义词: tailbone|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coccygeus, from Greek kokkyx "cuckoo," because the bone resembles a cuckoo's beak.
