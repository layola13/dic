careworn:/ˈkerˌwɔːrn/ /ˈkerˌwɔrn/| adj.|忧心忡忡的，饱经风霜的|Showing the signs of worry and stress, typically having a lined face and tired eyes.|Her careworn face reflected the stress of the past few months. 她饱经风霜的脸上反映了过去几个月的压力。|Example: The refugees arrived at the camp looking tired and careworn. 难民们到达营地时，看起来疲惫不堪，饱经风霜。|Synonyms: worried, haggard, strained, anxious, drawn, tired, fatigued|Antonyms: relaxed, carefree, untroubled, refreshed|Word type: adjective
