melolonthidian:/ˌmɛləˈlɒnθɪdiən/| adj. n.|金龟子科的；金龟子|Relating to or belonging to the Melolonthidae family of beetles, commonly known as chafers. Also, a beetle of this family.|例句：The melolonthidian larvae are known to damage crops. 金龟子科的幼虫会损害农作物。

例句：The farmer found a melolonthidian beetle in his field. 农民在他的田里发现了一只金龟子。|近义词：chafer, scarab|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:
From Modern Latin *Melolonthidae*, from Melolontha, the type genus, from Ancient Greek μηλολόνθη (mēlolónthē) 'cockchafer' (probably from μῆλον (mêlon) 'apple, any tree-fruit' + λόνθη (lónthē) 'head').
