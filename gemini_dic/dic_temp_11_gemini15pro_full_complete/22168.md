bombast:/ˈbɒmbæst/ (US) /ˈbɑːmbæst/| n.|夸大的言辞；浮夸的风格|High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people.|His speech was full of bombast, but lacked any real substance. 他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，但缺乏实质内容。|synonyms: bluster, rhetoric, grandiloquence|antonyms: understatement, simplicity, conciseness|noun
bombastic:/bɒmˈbæstɪk/ (US) /bɑːmˈbæstɪk/| adj.|夸大的；言过其实的|Using high-sounding but meaningless language.|The politician's bombastic pronouncements failed to impress the voters.  这位政客夸夸其谈的声明未能打动选民。|synonyms: pompous, inflated, overblown|antonyms: understated, simple, modest|adjective
