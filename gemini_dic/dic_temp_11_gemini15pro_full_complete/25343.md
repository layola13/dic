build-up:/ˈbɪldʌp/ | n. |积累，增多，增强；宣传，炒作| a gradual increase in something, or an increase to a high level; the advertising and discussion relating to a particular event, especially in order to create excitement about it | The build-up of troops in the border area has increased tension. 边境地区部队的集结加剧了紧张局势。

The media build-up before the fight was intense.  比赛前的媒体宣传铺天盖地。 |synonyms: accumulation, increase, intensification, promotion, hype | antonyms: decrease, reduction, decline | noun

build-up:/ˈbɪldʌp/ | adj. |逐渐增强的，累积的| characterized by gradual increase or accumulation |  The build-up of pressure in the pipeline eventually caused it to burst. 管道中压力的逐渐累积最终导致其爆裂。| synonyms: cumulative, increasing, intensifying | antonyms: decreasing, declining | adjective
