flatuses:/ˈflætəsɪz/ /ˈflætjʊsɪz/
noun
肠胃胀气；放屁（尤指无声的）
The expulsion of gas from the rectum or anus.
Example:  The doctor asked the patient if he had experienced any abdominal pain or flatuses.  医生询问病人是否有腹部疼痛或胀气。

Example:  He excused himself for the embarrassing flatuses. 他为自己尴尬的放屁道歉。

Synonym: gas, wind, fart
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "retention of gas" as a contrasting concept)

词性: noun
