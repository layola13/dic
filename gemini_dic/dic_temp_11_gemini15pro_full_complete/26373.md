by and large:/baɪ ænd lɑːrdʒ/ | adv. |大体上，总的来说|Generally speaking; on the whole.  It refers to something that is true in general, despite any small exceptions.|By and large, the project was a success. 总的来说，这个项目是成功的。|Synonyms: generally, mostly, overall, on the whole, in general |Antonyms: specifically, particularly, exceptionally|adverb
