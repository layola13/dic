glitchy: /ˈɡlɪtʃi/ | adj. | 小故障的，有缺陷的 |  Experiencing small, temporary technical problems, especially in electronic equipment or software.  Characterized by brief, unexpected errors or malfunctions. |  The game is still glitchy and crashes sometimes.  这个游戏仍然有一些小故障，有时会崩溃。|  The website has been glitchy all day, making it difficult to shop.  网站一整天都断断续续的，很难购物。| 近义词：buggy, faulty, malfunctioning | 反义词：smooth, seamless, functional | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Derived from "glitch" (n.), which originated in the mid-20th century, possibly from Yiddish "glitsh" meaning "slippery place."
