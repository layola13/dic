compound:/ˈkɑːmpaʊnd/ (US) /ˈkɒmpaʊnd/ (UK)| adj. n. v.|复合的；化合物；混合物；复合词；加重；混合；妥协；和解|A compound is something made up of two or more separate elements.|例句：Water is a compound of hydrogen and oxygen. 水是氢和氧的化合物。|近义词：mixture, blend, composite, complex |反义词: simple, element, single |词性:形容词, 名词, 动词

compound:/kəmˈpaʊnd/ (US) /kəmˈpaʊnd/ (UK)| v.|加重；使恶化；混合；妥协；和解|To compound something means to make it worse or more severe, or to combine things.|例句：His financial problems were compounded by the loss of his job.  他的经济问题因失业而雪上加霜。|近义词：aggravate, worsen, exacerbate, combine |反义词: alleviate, improve, simplify |词性:动词
