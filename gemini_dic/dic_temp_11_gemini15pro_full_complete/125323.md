multijugate:/ˈmʌltɪˌdʒuːɡɪt/ | adj. |多偶的，多轭的| Having or consisting of many pairs of leaflets or similar parts.  Referring specifically to a leaf that is pinnately compound with four or more leaflets. | Example: The fern had delicate, multijugate fronds.  这种蕨类植物有纤细的、多偶的复叶。|Example: Multijugate leaves are a common characteristic of certain pea family members. 多偶叶片是某些豆科植物成员的共同特征。| Synonym:  pinnately compound, multipinnate | Antonym: simple, unifoliate | Word Type: Adjective
