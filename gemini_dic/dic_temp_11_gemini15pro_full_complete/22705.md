borehole:/ˈbɔːrhəʊl/ (UK) /ˈbɔːrhoʊl/ (US)| n. |钻孔，井眼|A hole drilled into the ground, typically for the extraction of water, oil, or natural gas, or for geological exploration.|例句：They drilled a borehole to extract samples of rock. 他们钻了一个钻孔以提取岩石样本。

例句：The borehole was over 100 meters deep. 这口井深一百多米。|近义词：well, shaft, pit|反义词: mound, hill |词性: noun
