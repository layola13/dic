national:/ˈnæʃ(ə)nəl/| adj.|国家的，民族的，国民的|Relating to a nation; characteristic of a particular nation; common to a whole nation.|The national anthem played as the athletes entered the stadium. 国歌在运动员进入体育场时奏响。|National parks are important for preserving natural beauty. 国家公园对于保护自然美景至关重要。|近义词：domestic, internal|反义词：international, foreign|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *nacional*, from Old French *nacional*, from Latin *nationalis* (“pertaining to birth or origin, national”), from *natio* (“birth, race, nation”), from *natus* (“born”), past participle of *nasci* (“to be born”).
