horsemanship:/ˈhɔːrsmənʃɪp/ | n. |骑术；马术|The art or skill of riding and managing horses.  The ability to ride a horse well. |She demonstrated impressive horsemanship in the competition. 她在比赛中展现了令人印象深刻的骑术。|Synonym: equitation, riding skill |Antonym: ineptitude (on horseback), clumsiness (on horseback) | noun
