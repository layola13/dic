ETYMOLOGY->
From liberal + -ize.  "Liberal" comes from the Latin "liberalis," meaning "befitting a free person." The suffix "-ize"  means "to make or become."  Therefore, "liberalize" means to make something more befitting a free person, or to make something less restricted.
