Celsius: /ˈselsiəs/ | adj. |摄氏的| Relating to the Celsius temperature scale, in which 0° represents the freezing point of water and 100° represents the boiling point.| Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius. 水在100摄氏度沸腾。|Synonym: centigrade|Antonym: Fahrenheit| adjective
