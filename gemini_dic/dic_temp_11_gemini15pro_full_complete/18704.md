berdash:/ˈbɜːrˌdæʃ/ | n. | 胡说八道，废话；荒唐事 | Meaningless or nonsensical talk or writing; a jumble of absurd or confusing ideas or things. |  He was talking absolute berdash. 他说的完全是胡说八道。 |  nonsense, gibberish, balderdash | sense, truth | noun

berdash:/bərˈdæʃ/ | v. |  装饰；点缀 |  To adorn or decorate, especially in a showy or excessive way. (This usage is less common in modern English.) |  She berdashed her hat with feathers and ribbons.  她用羽毛和丝带装饰她的帽子。| adorn, decorate, embellish | strip, simplify | verb
