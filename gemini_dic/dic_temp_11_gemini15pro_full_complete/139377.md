obsequiously:/əbˈsiːkwiəsli/ /ɒbˈsiːkwɪəsli/
adjective
谄媚地；奴颜婢膝地
in an obsequious manner; fawningly.  Characterized by or showing servile complaisance or deference; fawning.
He obsequiously opened the door for her. 他谄媚地为她开了门。
Synonyms: servilely, fawningly, sycophantically, subserviently, ingratiatingly
Antonyms: assertively, independently, defiantly, boldly, confidently
词性: adjective

obsequious:/əbˈsiːkwiəs/ /ɒbˈsiːkwɪəs/
adjective
谄媚的；奴颜婢膝的
Excessively eager to please or obey.  Marked by or exhibiting a fawning attentiveness.
He was surrounded by obsequious assistants. 他身边围绕着阿谀奉承的助手。
Synonyms: servile, fawning, sycophantic, subservient, ingratiating
Antonyms: assertive, independent, defiant, bold, confident
词性: adjective
