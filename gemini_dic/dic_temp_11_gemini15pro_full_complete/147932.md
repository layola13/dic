overremiss: /ˌoʊvərɪˈmɪs/ | adj. | 过分疏忽的，玩忽职守的 | Excessively negligent or remiss; very neglectful of duty. |  He was overremiss in his duties and was subsequently fired. 他玩忽职守，随后被解雇了。|  She was overremiss in not thanking her hosts for their hospitality. 她没有感谢主人的款待，真是太疏忽了。| 近义词: negligent, derelict, lax, remiss | 反义词: diligent, attentive, responsible | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "remiss" (from Latin *remissus*, past participle of *remittere* "to slacken, let go back")
