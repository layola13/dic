beadily:/ˈbiːdɪli/| adv.|锐利地；精明地；警惕地|In a sharp, observant, and watchful manner, often suggesting alertness or suspicion.  The detective looked at him beadily, suspecting he wasn't telling the whole truth.|例句：The hawk watched beadily from its perch.  老鹰警惕地从栖木上注视着。

例句：She eyed him beadily, trying to decipher his intentions. 她警惕地打量着他，试图揣摩他的意图。|近义词：sharply, keenly, watchfully, vigilantly, alertly|反义词：absentmindedly, inattentively, vaguely|词性:副词
