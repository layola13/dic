colure: /ˈkoʊljʊr/ | n. | 分至圈, 二至圈 | One of two great circles of the celestial sphere, one passing through the celestial poles and the equinoxes, the other through the celestial poles and the solstices. | The sun appears to cross the colure at the equinoxes and solstices. 太阳似乎在二分二至点穿过分至圈。| The star is located near the colure. 这颗恒星位于分至圈附近。 | 近义词: meridian (somewhat related, but not synonymous) | 反义词: N/A | noun
