beltfeed:/ˈbeltfiːd/
n.|输弹带; 弹链供弹; 链式输送|A mechanism that feeds ammunition into a firearm using a belt. |The machine gun used a beltfeed system. 机枪使用了输弹带系统。|Synonyms: ammunition belt, cartridge belt|Antonyms: manual feed, magazine feed
