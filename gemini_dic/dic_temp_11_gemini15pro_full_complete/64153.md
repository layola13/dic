equalizer:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzər/ | n. |均衡器，扳平比分者|A device that compensates for unwanted variations in frequency response.  A person or thing that equalizes. |The sound engineer adjusted the equalizer to improve the audio quality. 音响工程师调整了均衡器以提高音频质量。| The team's star player acted as an equalizer, scoring the tying goal in the final minutes.  球队的明星球员充当了扳平比分的角色，在最后几分钟打进了扳平比分的进球。| synonym: leveler, balancer, adjuster | antonym:  distorter, amplifier (in some contexts) | noun


equalize: /ˈiːkwəlaɪz/ | v. |使相等，扳平|To make equal; to become equal. |The new tax laws aim to equalize the wealth distribution. 新的税法旨在平衡财富分配。|  The score equalized in the second half of the game. 比分在下半场扳平了。| synonym: balance, level, even out | antonym:  differentiate, unbalance,  skew | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *equaliʒen*, from Old French *equaliser*, from Late Latin *aequalizāre*, from Latin *aequālis* ("equal").
