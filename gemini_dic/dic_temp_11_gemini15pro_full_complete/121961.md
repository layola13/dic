missionist:/ˈmɪʃənɪst/| n.|传教士，主张推行某种使命的人|A person who is sent on a mission, especially one sent to promote religious conversion in a foreign country. Also, a zealous advocate of a belief or cause.|例句：The missionist traveled to remote villages to spread the gospel.  这位传教士前往偏远村庄传播福音。

例句：He's a missionist for educational reform. 他是教育改革的积极倡导者。|近义词：missionary, evangelist, advocate, propagandist, zealot|反义词:opponent, antagonist|词性:noun
