nonruminants:/nɒnˈruːmɪnənts/ /nɑːnˈruːmɪnənts/| n. |非反刍动物|Animals that do not have a rumen, a specialized stomach compartment for digesting plant cellulose through microbial fermentation.|Horses and pigs are nonruminants. 马和猪是非反刍动物。|Synonym: monogastric animals|Antonym: ruminants|noun

nonruminant:/nɒnˈruːmɪnənt/ /nɑːnˈruːmɪnənt/| adj. |非反刍的|Relating to or characteristic of animals that do not have a rumen.|The digestive system of nonruminant animals is different from that of ruminants. 非反刍动物的消化系统与反刍动物不同。|Synonym: monogastric|Antonym: ruminant|adjective
