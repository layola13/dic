nonconsignments:/ˌnɒnkənˈsaɪnmənts/ | n. | 未寄售货物，非寄售商品 | Goods that are not sent on consignment, meaning the seller retains ownership until they are sold.  The buyer doesn't pay for them until they are sold to a third party. |  The warehouse was full of nonconsignments, waiting to be shipped directly to customers. 仓库里堆满了未寄售货物，等待直接运送给客户。

The company prefers nonconsignments to minimize financial risk.  该公司更喜欢非寄售货物以最大程度地降低财务风险。 | 近义词: non-consigned goods, direct sales | 反义词: consignments | 词性: 名词
