novelistic:/ˌnɑːvəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
小说体的；像小说的
characteristic of or suitable to novels; resembling or suggesting a novel.
Her writing style is very novelistic, full of vivid descriptions and intriguing characters. 她的写作风格非常具有小说性，充满了生动的描写和引人入胜的人物。
Synonyms: fictional, narrative, story-like, imaginative
Antonyms: factual, real, true, historical
词性: 形容词
