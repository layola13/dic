balanced:/ˈbælənst/| adj.|平衡的，均衡的，稳定的|Being in a state of equilibrium; having different parts or elements arranged in the correct proportions.|She maintains a balanced diet. 她保持均衡的饮食。|近义词：stable, equalized, symmetrical, harmonious|反义词：unstable, unequal, lopsided, biased|词性:形容词

balanced:/ˈbælənst/| v.|（使）平衡，（使）均衡|To bring into or maintain in a state of equilibrium.|The gymnast balanced gracefully on the beam. 体操运动员优雅地在平衡木上保持平衡。|近义词：stabilize, equalize, counteract|反义词：destabilize, unbalance|词性:动词
