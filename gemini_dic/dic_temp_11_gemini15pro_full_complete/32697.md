chlorate: /ˈklɔːreɪt/ (US), /ˈklɔːreɪt/ (UK)| n.|氯酸盐|A salt of chloric acid containing the ion ClO3-.|Potassium chlorate is a powerful oxidizing agent. 氯酸钾是一种强氧化剂。|近义词:oxidizing agent|反义词:reducing agent|noun

词源:
From French *chlorate*, from *chlore* (“chlorine”) + *-ate* (suffix forming nouns denoting salts of oxyacids). Ultimately from Ancient Greek *χλωρός* (khlōrós, “greenish-yellow”) (whence also chlorine).
