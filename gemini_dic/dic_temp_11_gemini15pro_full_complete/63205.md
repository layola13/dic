entente:/ɑːnˈtɑːnt/ /ɒnˈtɒnt/
noun
协议；谅解
a friendly understanding or informal alliance between states or factions.
The two countries formed an entente cordiale. 这两个国家达成了友好协议。

近义词:accord, agreement, alliance, understanding
反义词:discord, disagreement, hostility
