eelgrass:/ˈiːlɡræs/| n.|大叶藻，鳗草|A marine plant with long, ribbon-like leaves, found in shallow coastal waters.|The eelgrass beds provide a habitat for many marine animals. 大叶藻床为许多海洋动物提供了栖息地。|Synonym: seagrass, zostera|Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider terrestrial plants)|noun
