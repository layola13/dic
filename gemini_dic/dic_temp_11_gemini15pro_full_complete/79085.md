glacialist:/ˈɡleɪʃəlɪst/| n. |冰川学家|A scientist who studies glaciers.  A specialist in glaciology.|例句：The glacialist spent years studying the movement of the ice.  这位冰川学家花了数年时间研究冰的运动。

例句：The glacialist presented her findings on glacial melt at the conference.  这位冰川学家在会议上展示了她关于冰川融化的发现。|近义词：glaciologist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From glacial + -ist.  "Glacial" comes from the Latin "glacialis" meaning "icy," ultimately from "glacies" meaning "ice."  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is an expert in a particular field.
