blastocarpous: /ˌblæstəˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
胚胎外果实的，种子在果实外部生长的
Having the seeds developing outside the pericarp.
The blastocarpous fungi release their spores directly into the air. 这种胚胎外果实的真菌将孢子直接释放到空气中。
Near Antonyms: angiocarpous (被子果实的)
Etymology:  From Greek *blastos* (bud, sprout) + *karpos* (fruit).
