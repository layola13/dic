half-rotten:/ˌhæf ˈrɒtn/ | adj. | 半腐烂的；快要腐烂的 | Partially decayed or decomposed; starting to rot. | The fruit was half-rotten and smelled terrible. 水果半腐烂了，闻起来很难闻。|  The half-rotten wood crumbled in my hand.  半腐烂的木头在我手中碎裂了。 | Near synonyms: decaying, decomposing, putrefying, spoiling | Antonyms: fresh, sound, unspoiled | adjective
