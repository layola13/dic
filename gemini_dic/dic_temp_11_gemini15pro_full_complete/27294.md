calvessnout:/ˈkɑːvzˌsnaʊt/ | n. | 小牛鼻；金鱼草的一种 | A type of snapdragon (Antirrhinum majus), named for the resemblance of its flowers to a calf's nose. | The calvessnout in my garden is blooming beautifully.  我花园里的金鱼草开得很漂亮。| Synonyms: snapdragon, Antirrhinum | Antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:
From "calf" (young cow) + "snout" (nose of an animal).  The name refers to the flower's resemblance to a calf's nose.
