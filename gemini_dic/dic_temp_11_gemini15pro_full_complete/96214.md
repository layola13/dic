indexical:/ɪnˈdɛksɪk(ə)l/
indexical| adj. |指示的，索引的|Relating to or denoting a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used.|The word "here" is indexical, as its meaning depends on the speaker's location.|近义词:deictic, demonstrative|反义词:non-indexical|词性:形容词
indexical:/ɪnˈdɛksɪk(ə)l/
indexical| n. |指示词|A word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used.|Pronouns like "I" and "you" are examples of indexicals.|近义词:deictic, demonstrative|反义词:non-indexical|词性:名词
