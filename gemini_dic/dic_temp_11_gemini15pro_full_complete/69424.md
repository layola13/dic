例句：His feelingful speech resonated with the audience.  他充满感情的演讲引起了观众的共鸣。|近义词：emotional, sentimental, passionate, tender, heartfelt|反义词：unemotional, apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From feeling + -ful.  Feeling (n.) comes from Old English fēlan "to perceive by touch, examine by touch," later "to perceive mentally or emotionally."  The suffix -ful means "full of" or "characterized by."
