borax:/ˈbɔːræks/ (US) /ˈbɔːræks/ (UK)| n.|硼砂；四硼酸钠|A white crystalline compound, hydrated sodium borate, Na₂B₄O₇·10H₂O, used as a flux, in making glass, and as a cleansing agent.|Borax is often used in laundry detergents.|近义词：sodium borate, sodium tetraborate|反义词: None|noun
