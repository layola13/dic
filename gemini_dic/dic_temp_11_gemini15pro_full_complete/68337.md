fallout:/ˈfɔːlaʊt/ | n. | 放射性尘埃，后果，余波 | The radioactive dust that is produced by a nuclear explosion and falls to earth. The unpleasant results or effects of an action or event. |  The political fallout of the scandal was considerable. 这起丑闻造成的政治后果相当严重。 | synonyms: consequences, repercussions, after-effects, side effects | antonyms: cause, origin, source | noun

fallout:/ˈfɔːlaʊt/ | n. | 附带结果，副作用 |  An unexpected or incidental result, typically negative.  | There was considerable fallout from the decision to close the factory. 关闭工厂的决定带来了相当大的副作用。| synonyms: by-product, spin-off, side effect | antonyms: intended consequence, planned result | noun
