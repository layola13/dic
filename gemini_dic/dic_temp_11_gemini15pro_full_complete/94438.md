implorers:/ɪmˈplɔːrərz/
n.
恳求者，哀求者
People who implore; those who beg earnestly or desperately.
The implorers knelt before the king, begging for mercy. 恳求者跪在国王面前，乞求怜悯。
supplicants, petitioners, beggars
demanders, commanders
