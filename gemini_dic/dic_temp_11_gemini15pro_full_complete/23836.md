breadboard:/ˈbredbɔːrd/| n. | 面包板；实验电路板 |A thin plastic board with rows of holes into which component leads can be inserted to make temporary electrical connections.  |I prototyped the circuit on a breadboard before soldering it. 我在焊接之前在面包板上制作了电路原型。| The engineer tested the circuit on a breadboard. 工程师在面包板上测试了电路。 | synonyms: prototype board, circuit board | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "permanent circuit board" could be considered a contextual antonym) | noun
