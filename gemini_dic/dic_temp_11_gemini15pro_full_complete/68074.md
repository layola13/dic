faddishness:/ˈfædɪʃnəs/
n.
时尚；一时的流行；追求时尚
The quality of being faddish; frivolous and superficial enthusiasm for something new.
The faddishness of the latest diet trend is evident in the number of people following it.  最新的饮食潮流的流行程度从追随它的人数就可以看出。
Synonyms: trendiness, vogue, craze, mania
Antonyms: classicism, timelessness
noun
