mystifiedly:/mɪˈstɪfaɪdli/| adv.|迷惑地，茫然地|In a manner showing or suggesting bewilderment and confusion. |She looked at him mystifiedly, unsure of what he meant. 她迷惑不解地看着他，不确定他是什么意思。|Example 2:  He scratched his head mystifiedly, trying to figure out the puzzle. 他茫然地挠了挠头，试图解开这个谜题。|近义词：puzzledly, bewilderedly, perplexedly|反义词：knowingly, understandingly|词性: 副词

词源:
From the verb "mystify" + "-ed" (past participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Mystify" comes from the Late Latin "mystificare," meaning "to make mystical or obscure," from "mysticus" (mystical) + "-ficare" (to make).
