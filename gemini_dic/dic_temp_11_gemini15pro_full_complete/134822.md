nonministerial:/ˌnɒnmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/
adjective
非部长级的；非内阁的
Not headed or directly controlled by a government minister.
The nonministerial department is responsible for managing government properties.  这个非部长级部门负责管理政府财产。
The committee is a nonministerial body. 该委员会是一个非部长级的机构。
Synonyms: non-governmental, independent, autonomous
Antonyms: ministerial, governmental
词性: 形容词
