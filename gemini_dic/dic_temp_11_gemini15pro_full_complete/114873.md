mammonite:/ˈmæməˌnaɪt/
n.
拜金主义者;贪财的人
a person who is devoted to the pursuit of wealth; a materialist.
He was a mammonite, caring only for money and possessions. 他是个拜金主义者，只关心金钱和财产。
Synonyms: materialist, money-grubber, mercenary, plutocrat, capitalist
Antonyms: philanthropist, altruist
词性: 名词
