overpoise:/ˌoʊvərˈpɔɪz/| n. vt.|过度平衡，过分自信；使失去平衡；使过分自信|An overpoise of negativity can be detrimental to one's mental health. 过度的消极情绪会对一个人的心理健康有害。|He overpoised the vase, and it fell and shattered. 他把花瓶放得太偏，结果掉下来摔碎了。|近义词：overbalance, outweigh, conceit, arrogance|反义词：underestimate, modesty, humility|词性:名词,动词

Etymology:
From over- + poise.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much." "Poise" comes from Old French "pois" (weight), ultimately from Latin "pensum" (something weighed, weighed portion).  Therefore, "overpoise" literally means to weigh down excessively on one side.
