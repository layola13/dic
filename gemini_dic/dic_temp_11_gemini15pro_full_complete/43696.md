creditably:/ˈkrɛdɪtəbli/
adverb
值得称赞地，良好地
in a way that deserves praise or admiration
She performed creditably in the competition. 她比赛中表现值得称赞。
近义词：commendably, admirably, respectably
反义词：disgracefully, shamefully, poorly
词性: adverb

词源: From the Latin "creditus" (believed, trusted) -> Old French "credible" -> Middle English "credible" -> "creditable" (deserving of belief or praise)
