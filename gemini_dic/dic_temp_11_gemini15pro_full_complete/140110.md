off-putting:/ˌɒfˈpʊtɪŋ/ (UK), /ˌɔːfˈpʊtɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|令人不快的，令人反感的，倒胃口的|Unpleasant and discouraging; tending to repel or deter.|His off-putting manner made it difficult to make friends. 他令人反感的举止让他很难交到朋友。|The smell of the durian was off-putting to some people.  榴莲的味道让一些人感到反感。|近义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, repellent, distasteful, repulsive|反义词：attractive, appealing, pleasant, inviting|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "put off" meaning to discourage or repel. "Off" acts as an intensifier.  源于“put off”，意为劝退或击退。“Off”用作增强词。
