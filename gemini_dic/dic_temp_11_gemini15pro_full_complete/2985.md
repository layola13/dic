/ˈiːmeɪl/ | n. | 电子邮件 | Electronic mail; messages distributed by electronic means from one computer user to one or more recipients via a network. | Example: I sent him an email yesterday. 我昨天给他发了一封电子邮件。| Example: She checks her email every morning. 她每天早上查看电子邮件。| Synonym: e-mail, electronic mail | Antonym: snail mail (physical mail), letter | noun


/ˈiːmeɪl/ | vt. | 通过电子邮件发送 | To send a message by email. | Example: I'll email you the details later. 我稍后会把细节通过电子邮件发给你。| Example: Could you please email me the report? 你能把报告用电子邮件发给我吗？| Synonym: send an email, e-mail | Antonym: mail (physical mail), post | verb


Etymology:  A blend of *electronic* and *mail*.  First recorded in the early 1980s.
