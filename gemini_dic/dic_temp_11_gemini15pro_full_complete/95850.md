incorrigibleness:/ɪnˈkɒrɪdʒɪblənəs/
n.
不可救药, 顽固不化
The state of being incorrigible; incapable of being corrected or reformed.
His incorrigibleness made it impossible for him to learn from his mistakes. 他的不可救药使他无法从错误中吸取教训。
Synonyms: depravity, incorrigibility, perversity
Antonyms: corrigibility, reformability
noun


incorrigible:/ɪnˈkɒrɪdʒɪbl/
adj.
不可救药的, 顽固不化的
Not capable of being corrected or reformed.
He is an incorrigible liar. 他是个无可救药的骗子。
Synonyms: incurable, inveterate, hopeless
Antonyms: corrigible, reformable
adjective
