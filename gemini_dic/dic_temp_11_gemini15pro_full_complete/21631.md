blurredness:/ˈblɜːrdnɪs/ /ˈblɝːdnɪs/
n.
模糊, 模糊不清, 模糊的状态
The state of being blurred; lack of clarity or distinctness.
The blurredness of the photo made it difficult to identify the suspect. 照片的模糊不清使得难以辨认嫌疑人。
近义词:blur, fuzziness, indistinctness, haziness
反义词:sharpness, clarity, distinctness, focus
词性:名词
