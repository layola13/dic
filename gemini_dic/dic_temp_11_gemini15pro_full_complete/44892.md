cryogenics:/ˌkraɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪks/ | n. |低温学|The study of the production and behaviour of materials at very low temperatures.|The advancement of cryogenics has led to breakthroughs in various fields. 低温学的进步带来了各个领域的突破。|近义词: low-temperature physics|反义词: high-temperature physics|词性: 名词

cryogenicses: This word is not standard English.  The word "cryogenics" is already plural in meaning and does not typically take an "-es" ending.  Use "cryogenics" instead.


词源:  From Greek *kryos* (frost) + -genic (producing).
