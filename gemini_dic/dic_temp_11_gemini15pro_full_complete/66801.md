expandibility:/ɪkˌspændəˈbɪləti/| n. |可扩展性，可扩充性|The ability of something to be expanded or increased in size, scope, or capacity.|The company chose the software for its expandability and ease of use.  这家公司选择该软件是因为它的可扩展性和易用性。|近义词：scalability, flexibility, extensibility|反义词：inelasticity, rigidity, inflexibility|词性:noun
expand:/ɪkˈspænd/| v. |扩大，扩展|To increase in size, scope, or extent.|The company plans to expand its operations overseas. 该公司计划将其业务扩展到海外。|近义词：increase, enlarge, grow|反义词：contract, shrink, reduce|词性:verb
-able:/əbl/| suffix |可...的，能...的|Used to form adjectives meaning "capable of being," "suitable for being," or "tending to."|This sofa is expandable into a bed. 这张沙发可以展开成一张床。|近义词：capable, possible|反义词:impossible|词性:suffix
