hurlings:/ˈhɜːrlɪŋz/
n.
投掷，猛掷；辱骂，抨击（hurl的复数形式）
The hurlings of the protesters were met with police resistance.  抗议者的投掷物遭到了警察的抵抗。
The constant hurlings of insults were demoralizing.  不断的辱骂令人 demoralizing。
hurlings are actions of throwing something with force.
The athlete's powerful hurlings impressed the crowd.  运动员强有力的投掷给观众留下了深刻的印象。
近义词: throws, casts, pitches, abuses, criticisms
反义词: catches, receptions, praises
