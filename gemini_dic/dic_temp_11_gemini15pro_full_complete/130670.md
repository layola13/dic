nonadvocates:/ˌnɒnˈædvəkɪts/ | n. | 非倡导者；不提倡者 |  People who do not advocate or support a particular cause or policy.  Those who are not actively promoting or speaking in favor of something. |  The nonadvocates of the policy remained silent during the debate.  非该政策的支持者在辩论中保持沉默。

The meeting included advocates and nonadvocates of the new proposal.  会议包括了新提案的支持者和非支持者。| 近义词: opponents, dissenters, objectors | 反义词: advocates, supporters, proponents | 词性: 名词


词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
advocate (noun): From Latin advocatus, past participle of advocare "to summon, to call to one's aid," from ad- "to" + vocare "to call."
