flagitious:/fləˈdʒɪʃəs/
adjective
极恶的；凶恶的；罪大恶极的
Extremely wicked; villainous; heinous.
His flagitious crimes shocked the community. 他的滔天罪行震惊了整个社区。

flagitious:/fləˈdʒɪʃəs/
adjective
极其恶劣的
Outrageously wicked or evil.
The dictator's flagitious acts led to his downfall.  独裁者的罪恶行径导致了他的垮台。


近义词: wicked, evil, villainous, heinous, atrocious, nefarious, infamous
反义词: virtuous, righteous, noble, good, benevolent
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flagitiosus*, from flagitium (“shameful crime, scandal”), from *flagitare* (“to demand earnestly, importune”), frequentative of *flāgitāre* (“to demand”), from *flāgrāre* (“to burn”).
