brigadiership:/ˌbrɪɡəˈdɪərʃɪp/
noun
旅长职位; 准将军衔
The rank or position of a brigadier.  A brigadier is a rank in the army, typically between colonel and major general.
He was awarded a brigadiership after years of distinguished service.  他因多年杰出服役而被授予准将军衔。
The brigadiership came with a significant increase in responsibility.  准将军衔带来了更大的责任。
synonyms: command, leadership, captaincy, generalship
antonyms: subordination,  followership
