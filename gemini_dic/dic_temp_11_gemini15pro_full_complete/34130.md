cladophyll:/ˈklæd.əˌfɪl/ (US), /ˈklæd.ə.fɪl/ (UK)| n. |叶状茎；扁平茎|A flattened stem that resembles and functions as a leaf.  It is often photosynthetic and may bear flowers or fruit.|Example: The cactus pad is a type of cladophyll. 仙人掌的叶状茎是一种叶状茎。

Example:  The Butcher's Broom has cladophylls that look like leaves. 假叶树的叶状茎看起来像叶子。|近义词：phylloclade|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'leaf' in some contexts)|词性:noun
