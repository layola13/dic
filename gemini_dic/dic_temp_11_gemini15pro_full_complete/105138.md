ketch:/kɛtʃ/ | n. | 双桅帆船；小艇 | A two-masted fore-and-aft rigged sailing vessel with the mizzenmast stepped forward of the rudder post. |  The ketch sailed gracefully into the harbor.  双桅帆船优雅地驶入港口。

The fisherman used a small ketch to navigate the shallow waters. 渔夫用一艘小艇在浅水中航行。| 近义词：yawl, sailboat | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑ship, steamship等大型船舶) | 词性: 名词
