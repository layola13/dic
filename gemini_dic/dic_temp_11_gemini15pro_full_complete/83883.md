half-and-half:/ˌhæf ən ˈhæf/ | n. |一半一半；半对半；均分；各半|A mixture of equal parts of two things, typically milk and cream.| I take my coffee with half-and-half. 我喝咖啡要加一半牛奶一半奶油。|
half-and-half:/ˌhæf ən ˈhæf/ | adj. |一半一半的；均分的|Consisting of equal parts of two things.|It's a half-and-half solution. 这是一个各占一半的解决方案。|Synonyms: fifty-fifty, equal parts|Antonyms: whole, complete|noun, adjective
