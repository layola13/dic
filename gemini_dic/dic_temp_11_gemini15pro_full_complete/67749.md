eyebaths:/ˈaɪbɑːθs/
noun
洗眼液;洗眼杯
a solution used for washing the eyes, or a small vessel used to hold such a solution.
After working with chemicals, it's crucial to use eyebaths to rinse your eyes thoroughly.  在接触化学品后，使用洗眼液彻底冲洗眼睛至关重要。

The nurse instructed the patient to use the eyebaths three times a day. 护士指示病人每天使用洗眼杯三次。
synonyms: eye wash, eyewash, eye cup
