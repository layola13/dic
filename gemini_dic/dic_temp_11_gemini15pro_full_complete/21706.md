boarder:/ˈbɔːrdər/ (US) /ˈbɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|寄宿生；膳宿者；登机者；滑雪者，滑板者|A person who rents a room in someone's house and receives meals, or a person who pays for meals at a school or other institution, or someone who boards a vehicle, or someone who snowboards or skateboards. |例句：The boarder paid his rent weekly.  寄宿生每周支付房租。

例句：The boarders at the school had to follow strict rules. 学校的寄宿生必须遵守严格的规定。|近义词：lodger, resident, tenant, passenger, skier, skater|反义词:landlord, host|词性:名词

boarder:/ˈbɔːrdər/ (US) /ˈbɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|边界；边境|The edge or line that separates one country, area, or thing from another. |例句：The two countries share a common boarder. 这两个国家共享一个共同的边界。

例句：Guards patrol the boarder day and night. 卫兵日夜巡逻边界。|近义词：boundary, frontier, edge, limit, borderline|反义词:center, interior|词性:名词
