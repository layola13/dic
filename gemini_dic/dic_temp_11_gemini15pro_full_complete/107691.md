lankness:/ˈlæŋknəs/| n.|瘦削；细长；空虚|The lankness of his frame was apparent beneath his clothes. 他的衣服下显露出他身材的瘦削。|例句：The drought resulted in a lankness in the cattle. 干旱导致牛群消瘦。  The lankness of the fields after the harvest was striking. 收获后田野的空旷景象令人震惊。|近义词：thinness, slenderness, leanness, gauntness, emptiness, hollowness|反义词: fullness, plumpness, stoutness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lanke, from Old Norse *lankr (“slender, thin”), from Proto-Germanic *langaz (“long”).  Related to long. 最终源于原始印欧语 *dlonghos（“长”）。
