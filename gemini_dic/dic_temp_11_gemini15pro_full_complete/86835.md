hematothermal:/ˌhiːmətoʊˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. | 温血的；恒温的 | Relating to or characterized by the maintenance of a relatively constant body temperature despite changes in the surrounding environment.  This is achieved through internal metabolic processes. |  Example 1: Mammals and birds are hematothermal animals. 哺乳动物和鸟类是恒温动物。

Example 2: Hematothermal animals can remain active in a wide range of temperatures. 恒温动物可以在很广的温度范围内保持活跃。 |  Synonym: endothermic, warm-blooded, homeothermic, homothermal, homoeothermic | Antonym: poikilothermic, cold-blooded, ectothermic | 词性: adjective
