novitiateship:/nəˈvɪʃɪətˌʃɪp/| n.|见习期，学徒期，新手期|The period during which a novice or beginner learns the basic elements of a skill, trade, etc.  A period of being a novice.|She completed her novitiateship and became a full member of the order. 她完成了见习期，成为了该组织的正式成员。|Synonyms: apprenticeship, probation, traineeship, initiation|Antonyms: mastery, expertise|noun
