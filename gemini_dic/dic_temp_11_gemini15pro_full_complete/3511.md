aftertaste:/ˈæftərteɪst/| n.|余味，回味; 后遗症|a taste that stays in your mouth after you have swallowed something; the feeling or effects that remain after something has happened|The coffee had a bitter aftertaste. 这咖啡有苦涩的回味。|synonyms: after-effect, lingering flavor, residual taste|antonyms: foretaste, pre-taste|noun

aftertastes:/ˈæftərteɪsts/| n.|余味（复数）, 回味（复数）; 后遗症（复数）|plural form of aftertaste|The meal left pleasant aftertastes. 这顿饭留下了愉快的回味。|synonyms: after-effects, lingering flavors, residual tastes|antonyms: foretaste, pre-taste|noun
