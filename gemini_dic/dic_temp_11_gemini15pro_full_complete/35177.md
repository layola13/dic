cloyingly:/ˈklɔɪɪŋli/ | adv. |甜得发腻地，腻烦地 | Excessively sweet or sentimental to the point of being unpleasant. |  The romance novel was so cloyingly sweet that I couldn't finish it. 这部爱情小说甜得发腻，我读不下去。 | 近义词：sickly, excessively, oversweet | 反义词：subtly, delicately | 词性: 副词

cloy:/klɔɪ/ | v. |使吃腻；使厌烦 | To cause disgust or aversion by supplying too much of something originally pleasant, especially sweetness.  | The constant stream of compliments began to cloy.  源源不断的恭维话开始让人感到厌烦。 | 近义词：satiate, surfeit, glut | 反义词：stimulate, entice | 词性: 动词
