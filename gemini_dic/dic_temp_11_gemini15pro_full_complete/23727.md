brast:/bræst/
v.
爆裂，破裂
To burst violently; break open or apart.
The dam brast under the pressure of the floodwaters. 大坝在洪水的压力下决堤了。

近义词:burst, explode, rupture, shatter
反义词:mend, repair, fix

词性:v. (verb, 动词)

词源:Middle English *brasten, from Old Norse bresta; akin to Old English berstan to burst — more at burst
