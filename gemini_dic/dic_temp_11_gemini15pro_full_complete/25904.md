burn the midnight oil:/bɜːrn ðə ˈmɪdnaɪt ɔɪl/| idiom | 开夜车；熬夜学习或工作 | To work or study late into the night. | Example:  I had to burn the midnight oil to finish the project before the deadline. 我不得不熬夜完成这个项目，以便在截止日期前完成。

Example: She's been burning the midnight oil all week trying to catch up on her studies. 她整个星期都在熬夜学习，试图赶上进度。| 近义词: work late, stay up late, work overtime | 反义词: go to bed early, get a good night's sleep | 词性: idiom
