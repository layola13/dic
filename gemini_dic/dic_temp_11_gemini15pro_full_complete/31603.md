chasten:/ˈtʃeɪsn/| vt.|惩罚；磨炼；抑制；改正|To correct by punishment or suffering : to purify by affliction. To subdue or restrain. To cause to be more humble or restrained : to temper or moderate.|例句：The experience chastened him. 这次经历使他变得稳重了。

例句：She was chastened by the difficulties she faced. 她被她所面临的困难磨练了。|近义词：discipline, punish, subdue, humble, restrain|反义词:praise, commend, encourage, indulge|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chastnen*, from Old French *chastier*, from Latin *castigāre* (“to punish, correct, chastise”), from *castus* (“pure”).
