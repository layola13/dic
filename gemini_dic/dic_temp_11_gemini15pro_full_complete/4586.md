alcoholometer:/ˌælkəˈhɒləmɪtər/ | n. | 酒精比重计；酒精计 | An instrument used to measure the alcoholic strength of liquids, especially of mixtures of alcohol and water. |  The brewer used an alcoholometer to determine the alcohol content of the beer.  酿酒师使用酒精计来测定啤酒的酒精含量。

The customs officer checked the spirits with an alcoholometer. 海关官员用酒精计检查烈酒。| 近义词: hydrometer | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From alcohol + -meter (a suffix forming nouns referring to measuring instruments, from Greek metron "measure").
