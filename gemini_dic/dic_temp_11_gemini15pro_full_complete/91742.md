hyperdicrotic:/ˌhaɪpərdaɪˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. |过度重搏的|Characterized by a double pulse beat, where a secondary upstroke of the pulse wave is exaggerated.  This secondary wave occurs after the dicrotic notch and is more prominent than the primary wave. It indicates a low peripheral resistance in the arteries.|The patient presented a hyperdicrotic pulse, suggesting a condition of vasodilation.| 近义词:  bounding (pulse) | 反义词:  weak (pulse) |词性: 形容词
