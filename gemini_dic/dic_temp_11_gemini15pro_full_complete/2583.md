admonishment:/ˌædməˈnɪʃmənt/ | n. | 警告，劝告，训诫 |  An act of admonishing; a gentle or friendly reproof. A warning or caution. | The teacher gave the student an admonishment for talking in class. 老师警告学生不要在课堂上讲话。| synonyms: warning, caution, rebuke, reproof, reproach | antonyms: praise, commendation, approval | noun

admonish:/ədˈmɒnɪʃ/ | v. | 警告，劝告，训诫 | To warn or caution someone firmly. To advise or urge earnestly. |  The librarian admonished the students to be quiet. 图书管理员提醒学生保持安静。 | synonyms: warn, caution, rebuke, reproof, reproach, advise, counsel | antonyms: praise, commend, approve | verb
