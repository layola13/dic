cumulativeness:/ˈkjuːmjʊlətɪvnɪs/| n.|累积性，累加性|The property of increasing or accumulating gradually over time; the state of being cumulative.|The cumulativeness of knowledge allows us to build upon the discoveries of previous generations. 知识的累积性使我们能够在先人的发现基础上继续发展。|Synonyms: accumulation, aggregation, accretion|Antonyms: reduction, decrease, diminution|noun
