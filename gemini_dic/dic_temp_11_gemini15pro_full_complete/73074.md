footsie:/ˈfʊtsi/| n.|调情，玩暧昧; 轻浮的举动|A playful touching of feet under the table, often interpreted as flirting.  桌子底下用脚互相触碰，通常被理解为调情。|They were playing footsie under the table. 他们在桌子底下玩暧昧。|近义词：flirtation, dalliance|反义词:  aloofness, indifference|词性:noun

footsie:/ˈfʊtsi/| n.|英国富时100指数(FTSE 100 Index)的非正式说法|Informal term for the FTSE 100 Index, a share index of the 100 companies listed on the London Stock Exchange with the highest market capitalization.  伦敦证券交易所上市的市值最高的100家公司的股票指数的非正式说法。|The Footsie was down 100 points today.  今天富时指数下跌了100点。|近义词：FTSE 100, FTSE|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
