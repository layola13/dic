monogenesis:/ˌmɒnəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
单一起源论;单系发生
The formation of all living organisms from a single source.
Monogenesis is the theory that humans originated from a single pair of ancestors.  单一起源论认为人类起源于一对祖先。
The theory of monogenesis contrasts sharply with polygenesis. 单一起源论与多起源论形成鲜明对比。
Synonyms: single origin, common ancestry
Antonyms: polygenesis, multiple origins
