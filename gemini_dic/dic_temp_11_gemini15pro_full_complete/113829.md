madderwort:/ˈmædərˌwɜːrt/ | n. |茜草, 茜根 |A herbaceous plant (Rubia tinctorum) of the coffee family, formerly cultivated for a red dye obtained from its roots.| The madderwort was a valuable crop in ancient times. 茜草在古代是一种 ценная культура. | 近义词:madder | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant) | 词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *madderworte*, from Old English *mæderewyrt* (equivalent to madder + wort (“plant”)). Cognate with Middle Dutch *medewarte*, German *Mäderkraut*.
