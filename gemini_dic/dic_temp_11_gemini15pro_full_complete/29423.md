catagmatic: /ˌkætəˈɡmætɪk/
| adj. |  绝对的，无条件的; 肯定的，断然的 | Asserting or implying that something is invariably or necessarily the case; unconditionally affirmative; positive and dogmatic. |  His catagmatic pronouncements on the subject left no room for discussion. 他对这个问题的绝对断言没有留下任何讨论的余地。 | 近义词: absolute, unconditional, positive, dogmatic, assertive, peremptory | 反义词: conditional, qualified, tentative, hesitant, uncertain | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Greek *katagmatikos*, from *katagma* ‘predicate, assertion’, from *katagesthai* ‘to affirm, assert’.
