cupuliferous:/kjuːˌpjuːˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
有壳斗的，产生壳斗的
Bearing cupules; producing cup-shaped structures.
The oak tree, a cupuliferous species, produces acorns encased in cupules. 橡树是一种有壳斗的物种，它产生的橡子被包裹在壳斗中。
The beech tree is another example of a cupuliferous tree. 山毛榉是另一种有壳斗的树的例子。
Synonyms: acorn-bearing, nut-bearing (in the context of cupules)
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct antonym)
