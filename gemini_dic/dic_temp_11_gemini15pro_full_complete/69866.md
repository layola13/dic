ferruginated:/fəˈruːdʒɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
铁锈色的，锈迹斑斑的
covered with or containing rust; rust-colored.
The ferruginated surface of the old car showed its age.  这辆旧车锈迹斑斑的表面显示了它的年代。
The geologist examined the ferruginated rock sample. 地质学家检查了铁锈色的岩石样本。
Synonym:rusty, rust-colored, iron-stained
Antonym:polished, gleaming, pristine
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ferruginatus*, past participle of *ferruginare* "to make rusty," from *ferrugo* "rust," related to *ferrum* "iron."
