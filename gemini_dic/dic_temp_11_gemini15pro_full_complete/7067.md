anemonin:/əˈnɛmənɪn/
n.
海葵毒素
A poisonous compound, C10H8O4, found in various ranunculaceous plants, especially in the wood anemone (Anemone nemorosa), and having acrid properties.
Anemone pulsatilla contains anemonin. 白头翁含有海葵毒素。

近义词:protoanemonin (原海葵毒素 - a precursor)
反义词:None (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound.)
词性:noun
