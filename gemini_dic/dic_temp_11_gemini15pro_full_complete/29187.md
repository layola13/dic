cashierer:/kæˈʃɪərər/ | n. | 收银员；出纳员 |  A person who handles cash transactions, especially in a shop, bank, or business. | The cashierer scanned my groceries and told me the total. 收银员扫描了我的杂货，并告诉我总价。|  The cashierer counted the money carefully before handing over the change. 出纳员仔细数了钱，然后才把零钱交出来。 | Synonyms: teller, clerk, checkout operator | Antonyms: customer, client | Noun
