co-operative:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/| adj. n.|合作的，协作的；合作社|willing to help or do what people ask|例句：The witness was very co-operative. 证人非常合作。|近义词：collaborative, joint, combined|反义词:uncooperative, antagonistic|词性:形容词
co-operative:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/| n.|合作社，合作商店|a business or organization owned and run by the people involved, who share its profits|例句：She is a member of a housing co-operative. 她是一家住房合作社的成员。|近义词：collective, commune|反义词:corporation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cooperativus, from Latin cooperari (“to work together”), from co- (“together”) + operari (“to work”).
