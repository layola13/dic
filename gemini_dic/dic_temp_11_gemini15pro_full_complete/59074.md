dwindle:/ˈdwɪndl/| v. |减少，缩小，衰落|To become steadily less; to shrink. |The town's population has dwindled to a mere few hundred.  这个镇的人口已经减少到只有几百人。

The snow dwindled away as the temperature rose. 随着气温上升，雪逐渐融化消失了。|近义词：decrease, diminish, shrink, decline, contract|反义词：increase, grow, expand, rise|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dwinan "to vanish, disappear, waste away," from Proto-Germanic *dwinanan (source also of Old Norse dvina "to dwindle, pine away," Old Saxon dwīnan "to vanish," Old High German dwīnan "to languish, pine," German schwinden "to disappear, vanish, dwindle"), from PIE root *dheu- "to disappear, vanish, die out."
