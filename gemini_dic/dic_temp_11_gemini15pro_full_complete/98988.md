inter-variation:/ˌɪntərˌveəriˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 变异；内部差异 | The inter-variation within the data set was significant, indicating a wide range of responses.  数据集中内部差异很大，表明响应范围很广。 | Example:  The inter-variation in the results of the experiment was greater than expected. 实验结果的内部差异比预期的要大。

Inter-variation in temperature can affect the growth of plants. 温度的内部差异会影响植物的生长。| 近义词: variability, fluctuation, diversity | 反义词: uniformity, consistency, homogeneity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "inter-" (between, among) + "variation" (the act or process of varying). "Inter-" comes from Latin, and "variation" comes from Latin "variatio," from "variare" (to vary).
