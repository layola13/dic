maniform:/ˈmænɪfɔːrm/
adjective
多种形式的；多样的
Having many forms or shapes; multiform.
The artist's maniform sculptures explored various textures and materials.  这位艺术家的多种形式的雕塑探索了各种各样的纹理和材料。
近义词:multiform, diverse, varied, polymorphic
反义词:uniform, homogenous, consistent
词性:adjective
词源: From Latin *mani- "many"  + -form "having the form of"
