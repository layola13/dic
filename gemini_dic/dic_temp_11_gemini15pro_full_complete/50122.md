dentition:/dɛnˈtɪʃən/
n.
牙列；齿系
The arrangement or condition of the teeth in a particular species or individual.
The horse's dentition was examined to determine its age.  检查了马的牙列以确定它的年龄。

近义词:teeth, set of teeth
反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑edentulousness 无牙)
词性:noun
