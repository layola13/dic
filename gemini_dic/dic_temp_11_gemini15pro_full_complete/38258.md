comrades:/ˈkɒmrədz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmrædz/ (US)| n. |同志，同伴，战友|A person who shares one's activities or is a fellow member of an organization, especially a political one.|例句：The soldiers were comrades in arms during the war.  这些士兵在战争期间是战友。

例句：We are comrades in the struggle for social justice. 我们是争取社会正义的同志。

近义词：companions, friends, colleagues, associates, allies|反义词：enemies, opponents, foes|词性:名词

comrade:/ˈkɒmræd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmræd/ (US)| n. |同志，同伴，战友|Used as a form of address, especially in socialist or communist contexts.|例句：Comrade Lenin led the Bolshevik revolution. 列宁同志领导了布尔什维克革命。

例句：He addressed the crowd as "comrades". 他称呼人群为“同志们”。

近义词：friend, colleague, associate |反义词: enemy, opponent|词性:名词
