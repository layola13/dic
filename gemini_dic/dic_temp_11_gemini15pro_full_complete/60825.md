electropositivity:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌpɑːzəˈtɪvɪti/
n.|电正性|The tendency of an element to lose electrons and form positive ions.|Cesium has a high electropositivity. 铯具有很高的电正性。|Synonyms: electronegativity, cationic character|Antonyms: electronegativity|noun
