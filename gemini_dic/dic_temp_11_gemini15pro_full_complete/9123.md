antimusical:/ˌæntiˈmjuːzɪkəl/
adjective
反对音乐的；不懂音乐的；缺乏音乐感的
Opposed to, ignorant of, or lacking musical ability or appreciation.
He has some very antimusial opinions. 他对音乐有一些非常反对的观点。
近义词: unmusical, tone-deaf, philistine
反义词: musical, melodic, harmonious
词性: adjective

antimusical:/ˌæntiˈmjuːzɪkəl/
noun
不懂音乐的人
A person who is opposed to, ignorant of, or lacking musical ability or appreciation.
He's such an antimusial, he can't even clap in time. 他真是个不懂音乐的人，他连拍子都打不准。
近义词: philistine
反义词: musician, music lover
词性: noun
