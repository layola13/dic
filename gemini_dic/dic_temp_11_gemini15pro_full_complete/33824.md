circumgyration:/ˌsɜːrkəmˌdʒaɪˈreɪʃn/ /ˌsɝːkəmˌdʒaɪˈreɪʃn/
noun
旋转，绕转，回旋
The act of turning or whirling around; a circular or spiral motion.
The planets circumgyration around the sun. 行星绕太阳旋转。
