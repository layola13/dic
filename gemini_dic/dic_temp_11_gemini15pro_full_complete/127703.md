neglectedly: /nɪˈɡlɛktɪdli/ | adv. | 疏忽地，忽略地 | in a neglected manner; carelessly | The garden was neglectedly overgrown with weeds. 花园疏于打理，杂草丛生。|  The child's education had been sadly neglectedly.  这孩子的教育被可悲地忽视了。 | 近义词: carelessly, heedlessly, thoughtlessly | 反义词: carefully, attentively, diligently | 词性: 副词
词源: From neglect (v.) + -ed (forming past participles) + -ly (forming adverbs).  Ultimately from Latin *neclectus, past participle of neglegere "disregard," from nec "not" + legere "gather, choose."
