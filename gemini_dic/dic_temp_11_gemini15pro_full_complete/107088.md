laden:/ˈleɪdn/
|adj.|装满的，负载的|Loaded; burdened; weighed down.|The camel was laden with supplies for the journey. 这头骆驼驮着旅途所需的补给。|Synonyms: loaded, burdened, freighted, fraught|Antonyms: empty, unladen, unloaded|adjective


Etymology:
From Middle English *laden*, past participle of *laden* (“to load”), from Old English *lād* (“way, journey, course, carrying, load, conveyance”), from Proto-Germanic *laþą* (“invitation, journey”). Related to Old Norse *leið* (“way, journey”), Gothic *𐌻𐌰𐌹𐌸𐍃* (*laiþs*, “way”). More at load.
