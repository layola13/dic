handfastly:/ˈhændˌfæstli/ | adv. | 牢固地，坚定地；依古代婚俗| In a firm or secure manner, often in the context of a handfasting ceremony.  It implies a binding together, either literally or figuratively. | They were handfastly bound together in marriage. 他们依古代婚俗结为夫妇。 | 近义词: securely, firmly, tightly | 反义词: loosely, weakly | 词性: 副词

词源: From "handfast,"  meaning "to bind the hands together," especially in a marriage ceremony.  "Hand" + "fast" (meaning firm or secure). Old English *handfæst.
