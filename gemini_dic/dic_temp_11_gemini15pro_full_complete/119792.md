migrates:/ˈmaɪɡreɪts/| v.|迁移，移居，(鸟类)迁徙|(Of animals) to move from one region to another, especially regularly according to the seasons.|(Birds migrate south for the winter.) 鸟类冬天向南迁徙。|synonyms: relocate, move, resettle, journey, travel, emigrate, immigrate |antonyms: settle, stay, remain|verb
