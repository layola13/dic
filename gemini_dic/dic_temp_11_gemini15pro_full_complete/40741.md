conveyancings:/kənˈveɪənsɪŋz/
n.
财产转让，不动产转让手续
The legal process of transferring ownership of property from one person to another.
The conveyancing on the house took longer than expected.  这栋房子的产权过户手续比预期的要长。
近义词：property transfer, real estate transaction
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) property acquisition
词性: Noun


conveyance:/kənˈveɪəns/
n.
运输工具，交通工具；运送，输送；表达，传达
1. A means of transport, especially a vehicle.
2. The action of conveying; transmission.
3. The expression of an idea, feeling, or thought.
The company provides conveyance for its employees. 公司为员工提供交通工具。
His conveyance of the message was clear and concise. 他传达信息的方式清晰简洁。
近义词：transport, vehicle, transmission, communication
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) obstruction, suppression
词性: Noun


convey:/kənˈveɪ/
vt.
运送，输送；表达，传达；转让（财产）
1. Transport or carry to a place.
2. Communicate (a message or information).
3. Transfer the ownership of (property).
The train conveyed passengers from London to Edinburgh. 火车将乘客从伦敦送到爱丁堡。
He struggled to convey his feelings to her. 他努力地向她表达自己的感受。
The deed conveyed the property to the new owner. 契约将财产转让给了新的所有者。
近义词：transport, carry, communicate, transmit, transfer
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) withhold, suppress
词性: Verb

词源：From Old French conveier, from Late Latin *conviare "to accompany on the way," from Latin com- "with" + via "way."
