oncograph: /ˈɒŋkəɡrɑːf/ | n. |肿瘤描记器；癌细胞活动记录器| An instrument for recording variations in the dimensions of a part of the body, especially for detecting tumors.  It is a device used to measure and record changes in the size of a tumor. | The oncograph provided valuable data for monitoring the tumor's growth. 肿瘤描记器为监测肿瘤生长提供了宝贵的数据。| The doctor used an oncograph to track the patient's tumor. 医生使用肿瘤描记器来追踪病人的肿瘤。 |Synonyms:  tumor monitor, oncometer | Antonyms: N/A | noun
