nonhalogenated:/nɒnˌhælədʒəˈneɪtɪd/ /nɑːnˌhælədʒəˈneɪtɪd/| adj.|非卤化的|Not containing halogens (fluorine, chlorine, bromine, iodine, or astatine).  A nonhalogenated compound is one that does not include any of these elements.|This insulation uses a nonhalogenated flame retardant. 这种绝缘材料使用非卤化阻燃剂。|Near synonyms: halogen-free|Antonyms: halogenated|adjective
