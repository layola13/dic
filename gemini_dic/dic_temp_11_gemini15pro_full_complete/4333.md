Karakoram: /ˌkærəˈkɔːrəm/ | n. | 喀喇昆仑山脉 | A large mountain range spanning the borders of Pakistan, India, and China. It is home to K2, the second highest peak in the world. |  The Karakoram Highway connects Pakistan and China through the Karakoram mountains. 喀喇昆仑公路穿越喀喇昆仑山脉连接巴基斯坦和中国。

The Karakoram is a challenging environment for mountaineers. 喀喇昆仑山对于登山者来说是一个充满挑战的环境。 | 近义词: mountain range, Himalayas (partially) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific mountain range) | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Turkic *qara qorum "black gravel"
