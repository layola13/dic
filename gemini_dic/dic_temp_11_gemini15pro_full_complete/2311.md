adequative:/ˈædɪkwətɪv/
adj. |足够的，适当的|Sufficient; suitable; equal to the requirement; proportionate.|The resources available are not adequative to the needs of the program.  现有的资源不足以满足该计划的需求。|synonyms: sufficient, enough, appropriate, suitable|antonyms: inadequate, insufficient, deficient|adjective


adequative:/ˈædɪkwətɪv/
adj. |充分的，胜任的|Fully competent; able to perform a task or fulfill a role effectively.|His skills are adequative for the job. 他的技能足以胜任这份工作。|synonyms: competent, capable, proficient, qualified|antonyms: incompetent, incapable, unqualified|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adaequativus, from Latin adaequatus (past participle of adaequare “to make equal to”), from ad- “to” + aequare “to make equal,” from aequus “equal.”
