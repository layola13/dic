blunted:/ˈblʌntɪd/| adj.|变钝的，迟钝的，减弱的|Reduced in sharpness or intensity; made less effective.|The knife was blunted from years of use. 这把刀因多年使用而变钝了。|近义词：dulled, weakened, subdued|反义词:sharpened, intensified, heightened|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *blunten*, from Old French *blunt*, *blont* (“dull, weak”), of Germanic origin, related to Old Norse *blundr* (“blunder”).
