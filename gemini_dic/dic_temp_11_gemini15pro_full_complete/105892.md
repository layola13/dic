kittenishly: /ˈkɪtnɪʃli/ | adv. | 小猫般地，娇媚地 | In a way that is playful and flirtatious, like a kitten.  Resembling the playful or coy behavior of a kitten. | She smiled kittenishly and batted her eyelashes. 她娇媚地笑着，眨着眼睛。|  She spoke kittenishly, pretending to be shy. 她装作害羞，娇滴滴地说话。| 近义词: coyly, flirtatiously, playfully | 反义词: seriously, sternly, maturely | 词性: 副词
