dentation:/dɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 牙列; 齿系; 锯齿状 | The arrangement, type, and number of teeth in a given species at a given age.  The condition of having teeth.  A serrated edge, as of a leaf or tool. |  The horse's dentation showed it to be quite young. 这匹马的牙列表明它还很年轻。 | Synonyms: teeth, dentition, set of teeth | Antonyms: edentulousness | noun

dentation:/dɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 锯齿状 | A series of teeth-like projections. | The dentation on the leaf's edge helped identify the plant species. 叶子边缘的锯齿状帮助鉴定了植物种类。 | Synonyms: serration, indentation, notching | Antonyms: smoothness | noun
