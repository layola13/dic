cutch:/kʌtʃ/
n.
儿茶；黑儿茶浸膏；鞣革
A reddish-brown extract from acacia trees, used for tanning and dyeing.
The artisan used cutch to dye the leather a rich brown.  工匠用儿茶将皮革染成浓郁的棕色。

cutch:/kʌtʃ/
n.
（板球）击球手站立的位置
The area where a cricket batsman stands.
The groundsman carefully prepared the cutch.  场地管理员仔细地准备了击球手站立的位置。


近义词:catechu, tannin (for the extract); crease, wicket (for the cricket term)
反义词:None
词性:noun
