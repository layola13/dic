domain:/doʊˈmeɪn/ /dəˈmeɪn/| n.|领域，范围，域名|A domain is a specific sphere of knowledge, influence, or activity.|例句：She's an expert in the domain of astrophysics. 她在天体物理学领域是专家。|例句：The internet domain name is crucial for website traffic.  互联网域名对于网站流量至关重要。|近义词：field, area, realm, sphere, territory|反义词：None|词性：名词

domain:/doʊˈmeɪn/ /dəˈmeɪn/| n.|领地，产业|A domain can refer to a territory owned or controlled by a particular ruler or government.|例句：The king ruled over a vast domain. 国王统治着广阔的领土。 |例句：The company's domain includes several factories and warehouses.  这家公司的产业包括几家工厂和仓库。|近义词：territory, estate, property|反义词：None|词性：名词
