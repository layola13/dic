nonabsorptive:/ˌnɒnəbˈsɔːptɪv/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈsɔːrptiv/
adjective
非吸收性的
Not able to absorb or soak up (liquids or other substances).
The raincoat is made of a nonabsorptive material.  这件雨衣是用非吸收性材料制成的。
The wound was dressed with a nonabsorptive bandage.  伤口用非吸收性绷带包扎。
non-absorbing, impermeable, impervious
absorbent, porous, permeable
