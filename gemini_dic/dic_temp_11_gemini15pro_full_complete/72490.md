fluoresce:/flʊəˈrɛs/ /flɔːˈrɛs/| v. |发出荧光；发荧光|To emit light during exposure to radiation, and to cease emitting light when the radiation ceases.|The mineral fluoresces a bright orange under ultraviolet light. 这种矿物在紫外线下发出明亮的橙色荧光。|Synonyms: glow, shine, luminesce|Antonyms: darken, dim|verb
