esurient:/ɪˈsʊəriənt/ /ɛˈsjʊəriənt/| adj.|贪婪的；渴望的|Characterized by greediness or voracious desire, especially for food.|例句：The esurient wolf devoured the entire carcass. 贪婪的狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整具尸体。

例句：His esurient gaze lingered on the buffet table, laden with delicacies. 他贪婪的目光在摆满美味佳肴的自助餐桌上徘徊。|近义词：greedy, ravenous, voracious, insatiable, avaricious|反义词：satisfied, content, temperate, abstemious|词性:adjective

词源:From Latin esurient- (stem of esuriens), present participle of esurire ‘be hungry,’ from ed- ‘eat’ + an element related to Sanskrit āśu ‘quickly.’
