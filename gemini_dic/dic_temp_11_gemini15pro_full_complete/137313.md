nontactilities:/ˌnɒntækˈtɪlɪtiz/
n.
非触觉性，非触觉事物
Lack of tactile qualities or the inability to be perceived by touch.  Things that cannot be experienced through touch.
The artist explored the concept of nontactilities in their digital artwork, focusing on visual and auditory stimuli.  这位艺术家在他们的数字艺术作品中探索了非触觉性的概念，专注于视觉和听觉刺激。
The exhibit showcased nontactilities, with holographic projections and soundscapes replacing physical objects.  展览展示了非触觉性，用全息投影和音景取代了实物。
近义词:intangibilities, impalpabilities
反义词:tangibilities, palpabilities
词性:noun (plural)

词源:
"Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "tactile" (adjective meaning "pertaining to the sense of touch") + "-ity" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) + "-ies" (plural suffix).
