constrainers:/kənˈstreɪnərz/| n. |约束者，限制者|People or things that constrain or restrict something.|例句：Budget constraints are preventing us from hiring more staff. 预算限制使我们无法招聘更多员工。|近义词：restrictors, limiters, inhibitors|反义词:enablers, facilitators|Noun
