overemoting:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊtɪŋ/
verb
过度表演；表演过火
Expressing emotions in an exaggerated or excessive way, often appearing insincere or artificial.
The actor was overemoting, and it made the scene feel melodramatic.  这个演员表演过火，让整个场景感觉很做作。
She was overemoting so much that I found it hard to take her seriously. 她表演得太过火了，以至于我很难认真对待她。
近义词: overacting, exaggerating, hamming it up
反义词: underplaying, downplaying
词性: verb (动词)

overemote:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊt/
verb
过度表演；表演过火
To express emotions in an exaggerated or excessive way.
He tends to overemote when he's stressed. 他在压力大的时候容易表现得过火。
Don't overemote; just be yourself. 不要过度表演；做你自己就好。
近义词: overact, exaggerate, ham it up
反义词: underplay, downplay
词性: verb (动词)
