interdiscipline:/ˌɪntərˈdɪsəplɪn/ | n. | 交叉学科，跨学科 | A field of study that draws upon two or more disciplines. | Example: Bioinformatics is a growing interdiscipline combining biology and computer science. 生物信息学是一个结合生物学和计算机科学的新兴交叉学科。| Example:  She's pursuing an interdiscipline degree in environmental studies and economics. 她正在攻读环境研究和经济学的交叉学科学位。 | Synonym: cross-discipline, multidiscipline, transdiscipline | Antonym: monodiscipline | Noun
