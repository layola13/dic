hindsight:/ˈhaɪndsaɪt/| n.|后见之明，事后聪明|The wisdom that comes from having experienced something.  Understanding of a situation or event only after it has happened or developed.|With hindsight, I should have taken the job. 事后看来，我应该接受那份工作。|近义词：retrospect, afterthought|反义词:foresight, anticipation|词性:名词
