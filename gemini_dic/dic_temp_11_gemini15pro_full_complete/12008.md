sarcomere: /ˈsɑːrkəmɪr/ | n. | 肌节 | The basic contractile unit of striated muscle, extending from one Z disc to the next. |  A sarcomere contains the thick (myosin) and thin (actin) filaments needed for muscle contraction. 肌节包含肌肉收缩所需的粗（肌球蛋白）和细（肌动蛋白）肌丝。|  The arrangement of sarcomeres gives skeletal muscle its striated appearance. 肌节的排列赋予骨骼肌横纹外观。 |  myofibril, muscle segment |  None | noun
