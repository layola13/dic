azimuthal:/ˌæzɪˈmʌθəl/ | adj. | 方位角的，水平方向的 | Relating to or measured by an azimuth. |  The azimuthal quantum number describes the shape of an electron's orbital. 方位角量子数描述了电子轨道的形状。

The telescope measured the star's azimuthal coordinates. 望远镜测量了恒星的方位角坐标。 | 近义词: horizontal, angular | 反义词: vertical | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From azimuth + -al.  Azimuth comes from the Medieval Latin *azimut*, from the Arabic *as-sumūt*, meaning "the ways, the directions".
