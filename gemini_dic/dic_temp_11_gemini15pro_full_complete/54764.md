dispatchment:/dɪˈspætʃmənt/| n.|派遣；发送；急件|The dispatchment of troops to the border was swift. 部队迅速被派往边境。|例句：The dispatchment of the goods was delayed due to the storm.  货物发送因暴风雨而延误。

The dispatchment of the emergency medical team saved the climber's life.  紧急医疗队的派遣挽救了登山者的生命。|近义词：sending, shipment, delivery, transmission|反义词:receipt, arrival|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "dispatch," from Old French "despescher" (to hasten, expedite), ultimately from Latin "dis-" (apart, away) + "pactus" (agreed upon).
