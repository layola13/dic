appreciatory: /əˈpriːʃiətɔːri/ /əˈpriːʃiəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 感激的，赞赏的 | Showing or expressing gratitude or appreciation. |  She gave him an appreciatory smile. 她给了他一个感激的微笑。|  He sent an appreciatory letter to the company. 他给公司寄了一封感谢信。| 近义词: grateful, thankful, admiring | 反义词: critical, disapproving | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *appreciatorius, from Latin appretiare "to set a price on," from ad- "to" + pretium "price."  The meaning shifted from "estimating the value of" to "valuing highly."
