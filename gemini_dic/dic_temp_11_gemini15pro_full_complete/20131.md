bipartition:/ˌbaɪpɑːrˈtɪʃən/ /ˌbaɪpɑːrˈtɪʃn/| n.|二分，二分法，两部分|The bipartition of the city by the river created distinct social and economic zones. 这座城市被河流一分为二，形成了截然不同的社会和经济区域。|例句：The bipartition of India and Pakistan in 1947 led to widespread displacement and violence. 1947年印度和巴基斯坦的分裂导致大规模的流离失所和暴力。

例句：The algorithm performs a bipartition of the graph into two sets of vertices.  这个算法将图二分为两组顶点。|近义词：division, separation, dichotomy|反义词: union, merger|词性:noun

bipartite:/baɪˈpɑːrtaɪt/ /baɪˈpɑːrtaɪt/| adj.|二分的，由两部分组成的，双边的|A bipartite graph is a graph whose vertices can be divided into two disjoint and independent sets. 二部图是一种图，其顶点可以分为两个不相交且独立的集合。|例句：The agreement was a bipartite contract between the company and its employees. 该协议是公司与其员工之间的双边合同。

例句：The bipartite structure of the government ensures checks and balances. 政府的二元结构确保了制衡。|近义词：two-part, dual, twofold|反义词: unified, single|词性:adjective
