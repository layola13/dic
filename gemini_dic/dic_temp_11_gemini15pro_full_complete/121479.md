mislabelled:/ˌmɪsˈleɪb(ə)ld/
| adj. |贴错标签的；错误标记的|Incorrectly labeled.  Something is mislabelled when it has the wrong label or description attached to it.|The box of cereal was mislabelled as "oatmeal" when it actually contained cornflakes. 这盒麦片被错误地贴上了“燕麦片”的标签，实际上里面是玉米片。|mislabeled, wrongly labeled, incorrectly labeled|correctly labeled, accurately labeled|adjective

mislabel:/ˌmɪsˈleɪb(ə)l/
| vt. |贴错标签；错误标记|To put the wrong label on something; to label incorrectly.|The factory worker mislabelled several boxes of products, causing confusion in the warehouse. 工厂工人给几箱产品贴错了标签，导致仓库混乱。|misname, misidentify|correctly label, accurately label|verb
