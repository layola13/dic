gabled:/ˈɡeɪbəld/ | adj. | 有山墙的 | Having a gable or gables. A gable is the triangular upper part of a wall at the end of a ridged roof. |  The gabled roof of the cottage gave it a charming, rustic look. 小屋的山墙屋顶使它看起来很迷人，很乡村。 | The old house had a gabled roof and dormer windows. 这座老房子有山墙屋顶和老虎窗。 | synonyms: pitched, peaked | antonyms: flat | adjective
