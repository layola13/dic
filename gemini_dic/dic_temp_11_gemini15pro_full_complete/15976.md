baltimorean: /ˌbɔːltɪˈmɔːriən/ | n. adj. | 巴尔的摩人；巴尔的摩的 | A native or inhabitant of Baltimore, Maryland. Also used as an adjective to describe something related to Baltimore. |  例句：He's a Baltimorean, born and raised. 他是土生土长的巴尔的摩人。

例句：The Baltimorean accent is quite distinctive. 巴尔的摩口音很独特。 | 近义词：Baltimore resident, Marylander | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective

Etymology:  Derived from Baltimore + -an.  Baltimore itself is named after Cecil Calvert, 2nd Baron Baltimore, an English nobleman and the first Proprietor of the Province of Maryland.
