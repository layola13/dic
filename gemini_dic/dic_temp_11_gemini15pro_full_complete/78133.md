geometric mean:/ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk ˈmiːn/| n. |几何平均数|The average of a set of numbers calculated by multiplying them all together and then taking the nth root, where n is the total number of numbers.  It's used when dealing with values that are multiplicative rather than additive.|The geometric mean of 2, 4, and 8 is 4. (2 * 4 * 8 = 64; the cube root of 64 is 4). 2，4和8的几何平均数是4。|近义词:mean, average|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

geometric means:/ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk ˈmiːnz/| n. |几何平均数（复数）|The plural form of "geometric mean," referring to multiple instances of calculating this average.|The geometric means of several sets of data were compared in the study.  该研究比较了几组数据的几何平均数。|近义词:means, averages|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
