branchiopod:/ˈbræŋkiəpɒd/ (US) /ˈbræŋkɪəpɒd/ (UK)| n. |鳃足类动物|A small crustacean of the class Branchiopoda, characterized by having leaf-like appendages that serve as gills.|Fairy shrimps are branchiopods that inhabit temporary pools of water. 仙女虾是栖息在临时水池中的鳃足类动物。|The brine shrimp is a well-known branchiopod. 卤虫是一种著名的鳃足类动物。|近义词: crustacean 甲壳类动物|反义词: None|词性: Noun
