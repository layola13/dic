cypraea:/saɪˈpriːə/| n. |宝贝，海螺，子安贝 |A marine gastropod mollusk of warm seas, with a smooth, polished, brightly colored shell.  It was formerly widely used as money.|  The cypraea shell is highly prized by collectors.  这个宝贝壳深受收藏家的喜爱。| synonym: cowrie,  porcelain shell | antonym:  N/A (no direct opposite for a type of shell) | noun
