brachycephaly: /ˌbrækɪˈsɛfəli/ | n. | 短头畸形 | A congenital condition in which the skull is abnormally short from front to back, resulting in a flattened facial appearance. |  Brachycephaly can be caused by premature fusion of the coronal sutures.  短头畸形可能是由冠状缝过早融合引起的。

Brachycephaly is sometimes associated with developmental delays. 短头畸形有时与发育迟缓有关。 | 近义词: short-headedness | 反义词: dolichocephaly (长头畸形) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βραχύς (brakhús, “short”) + κεφαλή (kephalḗ, “head”).
