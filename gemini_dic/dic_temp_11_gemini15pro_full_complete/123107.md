mongrelizations:/ˌmɒŋɡrɪlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 混杂，杂交；掺杂；弄成不纯 | The process of making something impure or mixed, especially of mixed breed or origin.  It often carries a negative connotation, implying a degradation or debasement of something pure or original. | The mongrelizations of language through the incorporation of foreign words can lead to both enrichment and confusion. 语言通过融合外来词而变得混杂，这既可以丰富语言，也可能造成混乱。

The mongrelizations of architectural styles in the city created a unique, albeit chaotic, aesthetic. 城市中建筑风格的混杂创造了一种独特但又混乱的美感。| 近义词：hybridizations, mixtures, amalgamations, blends | 反义词：purifications, refinements | 词性: 名词(plural)
