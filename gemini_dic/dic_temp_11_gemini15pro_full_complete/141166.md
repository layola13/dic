ooespore: /ˈoʊəˌspɔːr/
n.
卵孢子
A thick-walled sexual spore that develops from a fertilized oosphere in some algae and fungi.
The ooespore is critical for the survival of the species during unfavorable conditions. 卵孢子对于物种在不利条件下的生存至关重要。
The researchers observed the development of ooespores under a microscope. 研究人员在显微镜下观察了卵孢子的发育。
近义词: zygote, zygospore
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider vegetative cell)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From oo- (combining form meaning "egg") + -spore (suffix meaning "seed" or "spore"). Ultimately from Greek *ōón* (egg) and *sporá* (seed).
