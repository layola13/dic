emblazoner:/ɪmˈbleɪzənər/ | n. |纹章绘制员；装饰者| A person who emblazons coats of arms or other heraldic devices, or who decorates something richly. | The king employed a skilled emblazoner to create a new coat of arms for his family. 国王聘请了一位技艺精湛的纹章绘制员为他家族创作新的纹章。 | Synonyms: decorator, ornamenter, adornist | Antonyms: vandal, defacer | noun

emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/ | vt. |绘制纹章；装饰；宣扬 | To adorn or decorate richly; to display prominently or vividly, especially with heraldic devices. |  The knight's shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有一头金狮。| Synonyms: decorate, adorn, ornament, celebrate | Antonyms: deface, obscure, conceal | verb
