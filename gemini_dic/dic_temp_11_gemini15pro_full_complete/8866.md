antifeudalist:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəlɪst/
noun|反封建主义者|A person who opposes feudalism.|The antifeudalists advocated for land reform and the abolition of serfdom.|近义词:revolutionary, radical, reformer|反义词:feudalist, reactionary, conservative|n.

antifeudalist:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəlɪst/
adjective|反封建的|Opposed to or against feudalism.|The antifeudalist movement gained momentum in the 18th century.|近义词:anti-feudal|反义词:feudal, pro-feudal|adj.
