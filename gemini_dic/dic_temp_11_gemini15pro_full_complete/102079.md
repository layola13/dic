isoplastic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈplæstɪk/ | adj. | 等塑性的，同种塑性的 |  Relating to or involving tissue transplantation between genetically identical individuals (like identical twins). It refers to grafts that are accepted by the recipient because the donor and recipient are genetically identical.  |  Isoplastic skin grafts between identical twins have a high success rate.  同卵双胞胎之间的等塑性皮肤移植成功率很高。 |  syngeneic, homoplastic | alloplastic, heteroplastic | adjective
