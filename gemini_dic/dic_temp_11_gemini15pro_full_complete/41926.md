cosmoline:/ˈkɒzməliːn/ (US) /ˈkɒzməˌliːn/ (UK)| n.|润滑脂，防锈油|A brownish, jelly-like petroleum-based substance used to protect metal from rust.|The engine parts were coated in cosmoline for long-term storage. 发动机零件涂上了润滑脂以便长期存放。|Synonyms: grease, lubricant, protectant|Antonyms: rust, corrosion|noun

cosmoline:/ˈkɒzməliːn/ (US) /ˈkɒzməˌliːn/ (UK)| vt.|涂润滑脂，涂防锈油|To apply cosmoline to something.|He cosmolined the gun before putting it away. 他把枪涂上防锈油才收起来。|Synonyms: grease, lubricate, protect|Antonyms: rust, corrode|verb
