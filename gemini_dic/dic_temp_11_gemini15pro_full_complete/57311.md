downswings:/ˈdaʊnswɪŋz/
noun
衰退；下降；低迷时期
A downward trend or period of reduced activity, especially in economic terms.
The company's profits suffered during the recent economic downswing.  这家公司的利润在最近的经济衰退期间遭受了损失。

The housing market is experiencing a downswing.  房地产市场正在经历一段低迷时期。

Synonym: decline, downturn, slump, recession
Antonym: upswing, boom, surge, increase
词性: 名词
