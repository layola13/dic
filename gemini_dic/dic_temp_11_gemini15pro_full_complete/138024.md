noose: /nuːs/
n.| 绞索；陷阱；束缚| A loop with a running knot, tightening as the rope or wire is pulled and typically used for hanging. | The cowboy lassoed the calf with a noose. 牛仔用套索套住了小牛。| synonyms: snare, trap, loop | antonyms: freedom, release | noun
