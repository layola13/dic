hyperpnoea:/ˌhaɪpə(r)pˈniːə/ /ˌhaɪpərˈniːə/
noun
呼吸过度，过度换气
Increased depth and rate of breathing.  An increased respiratory rate or tidal volume.  Breathing more air than normal.
The patient presented with hyperpnoea and tachycardia.  病人表现出呼吸急促和心动过速。
Hyperpnoea can be a symptom of anxiety.  过度换气可能是焦虑的症状。
