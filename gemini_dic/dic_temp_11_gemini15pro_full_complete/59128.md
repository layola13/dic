dynamistic: /daɪˈnæmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 有活力的，精力充沛的，强劲的 | Characterized by energy and vigor; forceful. |  He is a dynamic and enthusiastic leader. 他是一位充满活力和热情的领导者。| energetic, vigorous, forceful, powerful, active | lethargic, inactive, passive, apathetic, sluggish | adjective

Etymology:
From French *dynamistique*, from Ancient Greek *δύναμις* (*dýnamis*, “power”) + *-ikos* (*-ikos*, adjectival suffix).
