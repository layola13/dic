feebling:/ˈfiːblɪŋ/| n.|弱者；虚弱的人；幼小动物|a person or animal that is very weak, especially a baby|The little feebling struggled to keep up with its mother.  这只幼小的动物挣扎着跟上它的母亲。|近义词：weakling, infant, newborn|反义词: strong, robust|词性: noun
Etymology: From feeble + -ling (a suffix forming nouns meaning "one characterized by the quality of the stem," often depreciative).  Feeble itself comes from Old French feble, from Latin flebilis "lamentable," from flere "to weep."
