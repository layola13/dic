blackout:/ˈblækaʊt/| n.|停电；灯火管制；消息封锁；暂时性失忆|A period of darkness caused by a failure of the electrical power supply; a suppression of information, especially news.|例句：The blackout lasted for several hours. 停电持续了几个小时。|近义词：power outage, power failure, amnesia (for memory loss)|反义词:illumination, enlightenment|词性:名词

blackout:/ˈblækaʊt/| v.|使停电；封锁消息|To cause a blackout; to suppress information.|例句：The government blacked out all news of the disaster. 政府封锁了所有关于灾难的消息。|近义词：suppress, censor|反义词:reveal, publicize|词性:动词
