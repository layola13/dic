bravest:/ˈbreɪvɪst/| adj.|最勇敢的|Superlative form of "brave," meaning the most courageous or showing the most courage.|例句：He was the bravest soldier in the battalion. 他是营里最勇敢的士兵。

例句：She was the bravest of them all, facing the danger head-on. 她是最勇敢的，直面危险。|近义词：most courageous, most valiant, most daring|反义词：most cowardly, most timid, most fearful|词性:形容词

词源:
