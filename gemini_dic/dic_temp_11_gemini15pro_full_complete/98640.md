insubstantial:/ˌɪnsəbˈstænʃəl/| adj.|无实体的，非实质的，脆弱的，不坚固的|Lacking firmness, solidity, or strength; flimsy; lacking substance or reality; not important.|The evidence against him was insubstantial. 对他的证据不足。|Synonyms: flimsy, fragile, weak, tenuous, slight, negligible|Antonyms: substantial, strong, solid, firm, concrete|adjective

insubstantial:/ˌɪnsəbˈstænʃəl/| adj.|不重要的，无足轻重的|Lacking importance or significance.|The improvements they made were insubstantial. 他们的改进微不足道。|Synonyms: insignificant, unimportant, trivial, negligible, paltry|Antonyms: significant, important, substantial, considerable|adjective
