mating:/ˈmeɪtɪŋ/| n. |交配，配对|the act of animals coming together to breed|例句：The mating season for deer is in the fall. 鹿的交配季节在秋季。|近义词：breeding, coupling, pairing|反义词:separation|noun


mating:/ˈmeɪtɪŋ/| adj. |交配的|related to the act of animals coming together to breed|例句：Mating rituals can be quite elaborate in some species.  某些物种的交配仪式可能相当复杂。|近义词：breeding|反义词:non-breeding|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *maten, from Old English *matung, from Proto-Germanic *maitōną (“to measure”), from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (“to exchange, change”).  The sense shift from "measuring" to "mating" may be related to the idea of finding a suitable partner, or "measuring up".
