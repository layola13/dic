confoundable:/kənˈfaʊndəbəl/ | adj. | 可混淆的，易混淆的 |  Able to be confused with something else; capable of being mistaken for something else.  |  The results of the two studies are confoundable, making it difficult to draw clear conclusions. 这两项研究的结果容易混淆，难以得出明确的结论。 | Synonyms: mistakable, confusable | Antonyms: distinguishable, distinct | adjective

confoundable:/kənˈfaʊndəbəl/ | adj. | 可混淆的，易混淆的 | Susceptible to being confounded; especially : capable of being confounded with an extraneous variable. | The effects of the drug are easily confoundable with those of a placebo.  这种药物的效果很容易与安慰剂的效果混淆。 | Synonyms:  interchangeable, similar | Antonyms:  different, unique | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *confundibilis, from Latin confundere "to pour together, mix, confuse," from com- "together" + fundere "to pour."
