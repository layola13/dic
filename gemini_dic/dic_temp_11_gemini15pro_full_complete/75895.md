funded:/ˈfʌndɪd/ | adj. | 已拨款的，资金已到位 | Provided with funds; financed. | The project is fully funded.  这个项目资金已全部到位。| The funded research will focus on developing new energy sources. 这项已获资助的研究将专注于开发新能源。| 近义词：financed, endowed, subsidized, supported | 反义词：unfunded, bankrupt | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From fund (v.) + -ed.  Fund (v.) comes from the Latin "fundus" meaning "bottom, foundation, piece of land."
