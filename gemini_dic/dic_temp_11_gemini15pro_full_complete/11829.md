arrenotokous:/əˌrɛnəˈtɒkəs/ | adj. | 卵胎生的 | Producing young by means of eggs which are hatched within the body of the parent, as in certain reptiles and fishes. |  Example 1: The guppy is an arrenotokous fish, meaning the eggs hatch inside the female.  母孔雀鱼是一种卵胎生鱼类，这意味着鱼卵在雌鱼体内孵化。

Example 2: Some species of snakes are arrenotokous, giving birth to live young.  一些蛇类是卵胎生的，产下幼蛇。| 近义词: ovoviviparous | 反义词: oviparous (卵生的), viviparous (胎生的) | 词性: adjective
