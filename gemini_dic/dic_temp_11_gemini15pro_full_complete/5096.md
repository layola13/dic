allayment: /əˈleɪmənt/ | n. | 减轻，缓和，平静 | The act of allaying or the state of being allayed; alleviation, mitigation. |  The allayment of her fears brought a sense of peace.  减轻她的恐惧带来了一种平静的感觉。 | 近义词: alleviation, mitigation, relief, assuagement | 反义词: aggravation, exacerbation | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *alayment, equivalent to allay + -ment.  Allay comes from Old English *alecgan "to put down, abolish," from a- "down, away" + lecgan "to lay."
