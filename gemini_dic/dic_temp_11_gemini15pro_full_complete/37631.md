community:/kəˈmjuːnəti/ | n. | 社区，共同体，群体 | A group of people living in the same place or having a particular characteristic in common. |  例句：The local community came together to support the family. 当地社区团结起来支持这个家庭。

| 近义词：society, group, public, neighborhood | 反义词：individuality, isolation | 词性: 名词

community:/kəˈmjuːnəti/ | n. | 共有，共享 | A feeling of fellowship with others, as a result of sharing common attitudes, interests, and goals. | 例句：A sense of community is important for our well-being.  社群意识对我们的幸福很重要。


| 近义词：sharing, fellowship, unity | 反义词：division, discord | 词性: 名词
