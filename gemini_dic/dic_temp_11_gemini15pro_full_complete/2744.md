adulterating: /əˈdʌltəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
掺假，劣质化
making something poorer in quality by adding another substance
The company was accused of adulterating its products with cheaper ingredients.  该公司被指控在产品中掺杂廉价成分。
近义词: contaminating, debasing, degrading, polluting
反义词: purifying, refining
词性: Verb (present participle)

adulterate: /əˈdʌltəreɪt/
verb
掺假，劣质化
to make something impure or weaker by adding something of inferior quality
They adulterated the milk with water. 他们用水掺牛奶。
近义词: contaminate, debase, degrade, pollute
反义词: purify, refine
词性: Verb

adulteration: /əˌdʌltəˈreɪʃn/
noun
掺假，劣质化
the action of making something poorer in quality by adding another substance
The adulteration of food is a serious crime. 食品掺假是严重的犯罪行为。
近义词: contamination, debasement, degradation, pollution
反义词: purification, refinement
词性: Noun


词源:  From Latin *adulteratus, past participle of adulterare (“to corrupt, falsify, commit adultery”), from ad (“to”) + alter (“other”).
