fluoroid:/ˈflʊərɔɪd/| adj.| 氟化物的，似萤石的 |Relating to or resembling fluorite; containing fluorine.|This mineral exhibits a fluoroid structure. 这种矿物呈现出萤石结构。|近义词：fluoritic|反义词:|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From fluor- + -oid.  Fluor- comes from Latin fluor, meaning "a flowing," referring to the mineral fluorspar (fluorite) which was used as a flux in smelting.  -oid is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "having the form of."
