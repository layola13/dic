gave off:/ɡeɪv ɒf/ /ɡeɪv ɔːf/
verb phrase
发出，释放
emit or release something such as a smell, heat, or light.
The fire gave off a lot of smoke. 火释放出大量的烟雾。
The flowers gave off a sweet fragrance.  这些花散发着甜甜的香味。
近义词:emit, exude, radiate, release
反义词:absorb, conceal
词性:动词短语
