aerodynamically:/ˌɛːrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪkli/ | adv. | 空气动力地 | in a way that relates to aerodynamics; designed to reduce air resistance.  Relating to or involving the properties of moving air. | The car is aerodynamically designed to reduce drag. 这辆车采用空气动力学设计以减少阻力。|  The plane was aerodynamically unstable. 这架飞机在空气动力学上不稳定。 | Synonyms: stream-lined, sleek | Antonyms: clunky, unwieldy | adverb
