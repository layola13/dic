hasbin:/ˈhæzbɪn/| n. |哈希数据库 (通常指用于存储和检查文件哈希值的在线服务)|An online service that stores and allows users to check hashes of files, often used for malware analysis and data breach investigations.  It helps determine if a specific file has been previously identified as malicious or compromised.|Example:  Security researchers uploaded the malware sample to VirusTotal and Hasbin to see if it had been previously reported. 安全研究人员将恶意软件样本上传到VirusTotal和Hasbin，以查看它是否已被报告过。

Example:  Checking the leaked password hashes against Hasbin revealed that many users were reusing passwords across multiple platforms. 将泄露的密码哈希值与Hasbin进行比对后发现，许多用户在多个平台上重复使用密码。|近义词: hash database, hash checking service|反义词:  None (lacks a direct antonym)|词性:noun
