ammoniums: /əˈmoʊniəmz/
n.
铵盐（复数）
plural of ammonium: positively charged polyatomic ion NH₄⁺, or a cation formed by adding a proton to ammonia.  It is the conjugate acid of ammonia. Ammonium salts are also important nitrogenous fertilizers.
Ammonium chloride is used in dry cells. 氯化铵用于干电池中。

Ammonium nitrate is a commonly used fertilizer. 硝酸铵是一种常用的肥料。

Synonyms:  NH₄⁺ salts,  ammonium compounds
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound.  One could consider anions as a contrasting class of ions.)
词性: noun
