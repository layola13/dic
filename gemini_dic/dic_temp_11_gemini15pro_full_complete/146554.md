overgovern:/ˌoʊvərˈɡʌvərn/
|v.|过度管理，管制过严|To govern excessively; to regulate or control too much.|The city is overgoverned, with too many bureaucratic regulations hindering businesses. 这个城市管理过度，过多的官僚规定阻碍了企业发展.|The school was overgoverned, with rules for everything. 这所学校管理过度，对所有事情都有规定。|近义词: overregulate, micromanage|反义词: undergovern, deregulate|词性: 动词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "govern" (verb)
"over-" 表示“过度”，“过分”
"govern" 来自拉丁语 "gubernare"，意思是“引导”，“统治”
