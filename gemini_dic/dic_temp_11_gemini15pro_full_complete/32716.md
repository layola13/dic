chlorinators:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtərz/
noun
加氯器，氯化器
A device used to add chlorine to something, typically water, for the purposes of disinfection or sanitation.
The chlorinators in the pool kept the water clean and safe for swimming.  游泳池里的加氯器使水保持清洁，可以安全游泳。
synonyms: chlorination system, chlorine feeder, chlorine dispenser
antonyms: dechlorinator
n.
