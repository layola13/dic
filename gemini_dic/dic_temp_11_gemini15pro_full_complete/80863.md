gourmet:/ˈɡʊrmeɪ/ /ˈɡɔːrmeɪ/| n. adj.|美食家，美食的|A connoisseur of good food; a person with a refined palate. Also used to describe high-quality food and drink.|例句：He is a true gourmet, always seeking out the finest restaurants. 他是一位真正的美食家，总是寻找最好的餐厅。

例句：She prepared a gourmet meal for her guests. 她为客人准备了一顿美味佳肴。|近义词：epicure, connoisseur, foodie|反义词：glutton, gourmand (although these words can sometimes overlap in meaning)|词性: noun, adjective|词源：From French *gourmet*, originally meaning a wine merchant's assistant, from Old French *groumet*, possibly of Germanic origin.
