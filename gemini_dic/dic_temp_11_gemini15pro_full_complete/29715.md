catholicness:/ˌkæθəˈlɪknəs/
n.
天主教性；普遍性；包容性
The quality of being catholic, especially in terms of breadth or universality.
Her writings reflect a certain catholicness of taste, embracing various genres and styles. 她的作品反映出某种兼容并包的品味，涵盖了各种流派和风格。
catholicity, universality, comprehensiveness
narrowness, exclusiveness
