circumvent:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvent/ /ˌsərːkəmˈvent/| vt.|绕行，规避，回避|To find a way around (an obstacle, difficulty, etc.); to avoid (something) by artfulness or deception; to bypass or go around.|例句：The company circumvented the regulations by creating a subsidiary in a different country.  该公司通过在另一个国家创建子公司来规避这些规定。

例句：They circumvented the traffic jam by taking a back road. 他们绕道而行，避开了交通堵塞。|近义词：avoid, evade, bypass, dodge, sidestep|反义词：confront, face, meet, comply with|词性：动词
