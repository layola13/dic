ecologically:/ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ˌiːkəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/
adverb
生态地；从生态学的角度
in a way that relates to the relationship between living things and their environment
例句：It's ecologically sound to use recycled paper. 使用再生纸符合生态环保。

例句：The project is not ecologically sustainable.  这个项目在生态上不可持续。

近义词：environmentally, naturally
反义词：artificially

词性: adverb
