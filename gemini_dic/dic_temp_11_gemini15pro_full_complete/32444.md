chiming:/ˈtʃaɪmɪŋ/ | adj.  v. (present participle of chime) | 鸣响的；和谐的 |  making a ringing sound, typically like bells; harmonious or agreeing. | The chiming clock announced the hour. 鸣响的时钟报时了。 | Synonyms: ringing, resonant, harmonious, concordant | Antonyms: discordant, clashing | adjective

chiming:/ˈtʃaɪmɪŋ/ | v. (present participle of chime) | 鸣响；和谐 |  making a ringing sound, typically like bells; to be in agreement or harmony. | The bells were chiming in the distance. 远处钟声鸣响。| Synonyms: ringing, pealing, resounding, agreeing, harmonizing | Antonyms: clashing, disagreeing | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chimen, from Old North French chimer, of imitative origin.
