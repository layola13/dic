例句：The moribund economy showed no signs of recovery.  垂死的经济没有复苏的迹象。|近义词：dying, expiring, failing, declining, stagnant|反义词:thriving, flourishing, booming, vibrant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *moribundus*, present participle of *morī* "to die."
