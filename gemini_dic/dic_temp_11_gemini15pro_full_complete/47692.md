debunked:/diːˈbʌŋkt/
verb
揭穿；拆穿；驳斥
exposed as false; disproved.
例句：
The scientist debunked the myth that vaccines cause autism.  科学家驳斥了疫苗导致自闭症的谬论。

The documentary debunked the popular belief that the Earth is flat.  这部纪录片揭穿了地球是平的这一流行的错误观念。

近义词：disproved, refuted, exposed, invalidated
反义词：confirmed, validated, substantiated, proven
词性：verb (past participle of debunk)
