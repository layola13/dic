chichi:/ˈʃiːʃiː/ (US), /ˈʃɪːʃɪ/ (UK)| adj. n.| شیک的，别致的；乳房；虚饰，俗丽的装饰|Stylish or elegant, often in a frivolous or affected way; a woman's breast; showy or elaborate ornamentation.|例句：She wore a chichi little hat to the party. 她戴着一顶别致的小帽子去参加聚会。|近义词：stylish, elegant, fancy, chic, ornate|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性: adjective, noun
chichi:/ˈʃiːʃiː/ (US), /ˈʃɪːʃɪ/ (UK)| n.|乳房|A woman's breast. |例句：The baby suckled at its mother's chichi. 婴儿吮吸着妈妈的乳房。|近义词：breast, boob, tit (informal)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French chichi (stylish, elegant).  The origin of the French word is uncertain, possibly imitative of the sound of chattering or whispering. The meaning "breast" is likely a euphemism.
