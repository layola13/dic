candles:/ˈkændlz/ (US), /ˈkændəlz/ (UK)| n.|蜡烛|A cylinder or block of wax or tallow with a central wick that is lit to produce light as it burns.|The soft glow of the candles created a romantic atmosphere. 蜡烛柔和的光芒营造出浪漫的氛围。|近义词：tapers, lights|反义词: darkness, electric lights|noun
