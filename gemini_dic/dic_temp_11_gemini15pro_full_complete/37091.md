combine:/kəmˈbaɪn/ | v. n. | 结合，联合，化合 | To join or mix two or more things together. | The two companies will combine to form a larger corporation.  两家公司将合并成一家更大的公司。

combine:/kəmˈbaɪn/ | v. n. | 结合，联合，化合 | To possess or exhibit a combination of qualities or characteristics. | She combines intelligence with beauty. 她兼具智慧和美貌。


近义词: merge, blend, mix, unite, integrate
反义词: separate, divide, split
词性: verb, noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French combiner, from Latin combinare, from com- ‘together’ + bini ‘two by two’.
