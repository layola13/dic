furrow-like:/ˈfɜːroʊˌlaɪk/
adjective
沟状的；像犁沟的
Resembling a furrow; having the appearance of a long, narrow trench or groove.
The wrinkles on his forehead were deep and furrow-like. 他额头上的皱纹又深又像犁沟。
The tractor left furrow-like tracks in the mud. 拖拉机在泥地上留下了像犁沟一样的痕迹。
Synonyms: grooved, furrowed, channeled, wrinkled, lined
Antonyms: smooth, flat, even
adjective
