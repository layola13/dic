actualnesses:/ˌæktʃuˈælnəsəz/ | n. | 真实性；实际情况 | The state of being actual; reality.  This word is rarely used and considered non-standard.  It's formed by adding multiple suffixes to "actual," which is unnecessary.  Use "actuality" instead. |  I doubt the actualnesses of his claims. 我怀疑他所说事情的真实性. (This sentence is grammatically correct, but sounds awkward.  It's better to say: I doubt the actuality of his claims.) | 近义词: actuality, reality, factuality | 反义词: unreality, fiction, falsehood | 词性: noun
