hypomixolydian:/ˌhaɪpoʊmɪksoʊˈlɪdiən/| adj.|次混合利底亚调式|A musical mode derived from the Mixolydian mode by lowering the second degree by a semitone. It is a diatonic scale with a characteristic flattened seventh degree and also a flattened second degree.|The melody has a distinctly hypomixolydian feel.|近义词:Mixolydian (loosely)|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
