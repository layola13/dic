deaf's:/dɛfs/ | n. | 聋人的（财产、权利等）|  Possessive form of "deaf," referring to something belonging to or associated with deaf people.  While grammatically correct, it's often considered awkward and less common than "of the deaf" or "for the deaf." |  Example: The deaf's rights should be protected. 聋人的权利应该受到保护。 | Synonym: of the deaf, for the deaf | Antonym: hearing's | Noun
