cubicle: /ˈkjuːbɪkl/ (US) /ˈkjuːbɪkəl/ (UK)| n.|小隔间，小单间|A small partitioned-off area of a room, for example one containing a desk in an office.|Example: I work in a cubicle in a large open-plan office. 我在一个大型开放式办公室的小隔间里工作。

Example:  She decorated her cubicle with photos and plants. 她用照片和植物装饰了她的隔间。|近义词: compartment, booth, stall|反义词: open space, large room|词性: noun
