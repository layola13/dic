dorado:/dɔːˈrɑːdoʊ/ /dəˈrɑːdəʊ/
n.|鲯鳅鱼，金色的，理想中的黄金国|A large, powerful game fish found in tropical and subtropical waters, also known as mahi-mahi or dolphin fish. Also refers to a legendary city of gold sought by Spanish explorers in South America.|We caught a huge dorado while deep-sea fishing. 我们在深海捕鱼时钓到了一条巨大的鲯鳅鱼。|Synonyms: mahi-mahi, dolphin fish, dolphinfish|Antonyms: N/A|noun
dorado:/dɔːˈrɑːdoʊ/ /dəˈrɑːdəʊ/
adj.|金色的，金黄色的|Having a golden color.|The setting sun painted the clouds a vibrant dorado. 落日将云彩染成了鲜艳的金黄色。|Synonyms: golden, gold, aureate|Antonyms: silver, gray|adjective
