nonnationalisms:/nɒnˈnæʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
非民族主义，超越民族主义
Ideologies or movements that transcend or reject traditional nationalism, often emphasizing global citizenship or other forms of collective identity.
The rise of nonnationalisms poses a challenge to the traditional nation-state system. 非民族主义的兴起对传统的民族国家体系构成了挑战。
The conference explored various forms of nonnationalisms, including cosmopolitanism and environmentalism. 会议探讨了各种形式的非民族主义，包括世界主义和环境主义。
Synonyms: cosmopolitanism, globalism, internationalism, transnationalism
Antonyms: nationalism, patriotism, jingoism
