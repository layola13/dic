nonrupturable:/ˌnɒnˈrʌptʃərəbəl/ | adj. | 不可破裂的，不会断裂的 | Not capable of rupturing; unable to break or burst. | The company developed a nonrupturable material for use in high-pressure pipelines.  该公司开发了一种用于高压管道的不可破裂材料。

The nonrupturable membrane ensures the safety of the device.  这种不可破裂的薄膜确保了设备的安全。| 近义词: unbreakable, indestructible, shatterproof | 反义词: rupturable, fragile, breakable | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + rupturable (from Latin ruptūra, "a break" + -able, suffix meaning "capable of being")
