dziggetai: /ˈdzɪɡətaɪ/ /ˈzɪɡətaɪ/ | n. | 蒙古野驴，亚洲野驴 | A wild ass (Equus hemionus hemionus) native to Mongolia and northern China, having a sandy or grayish-brown coat.  It is one of the subspecies of the onager. |  The dziggetai is adapted to harsh desert conditions. 蒙古野驴适应恶劣的沙漠环境。

The dziggetai's population has declined drastically in recent years. 近年来蒙古野驴的数量急剧下降。


| 近义词：onager, Asiatic wild ass, kiang (for Tibetan wild ass) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "domesticated donkey") | 词性: noun
