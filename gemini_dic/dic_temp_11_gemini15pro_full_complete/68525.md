fanciest:/ˈfænsiɪst/| adj.|最花哨的，最精致的，最高档的|Superlative form of "fancy," meaning most elaborate, ornate, or sophisticated.|She wore her fanciest dress to the gala. 她穿着她最华丽的礼服去参加晚会。|The hotel had the fanciest lobby I'd ever seen. 这家酒店的大堂是我见过的最豪华的。|近义词：most elaborate, most ornate, most sophisticated, most stylish|反义词：plainest, simplest, most basic|词性:形容词
词源: From the Old French "fantasie" meaning "imagination, illusion, caprice."
