corrosives:/kəˈroʊsɪvz/
n.|腐蚀物，腐蚀剂|Substances that cause corrosion; materials that gradually destroy or damage a substance by chemical action.|The corrosives were stored safely in a locked cabinet. 腐蚀性物质被安全地存放在一个锁着的柜子里。|synonyms: acids, alkalis, oxidants|antonyms: inhibitors, neutralizers|noun

corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/
adj.|腐蚀性的，侵蚀性的|Having the power to gradually destroy or damage a substance by chemical action; tending to cause corrosion.|Sulfuric acid is a highly corrosive substance. 硫酸是一种高度腐蚀性的物质。|synonyms: caustic, erosive, destructive|antonyms: protective, preservative|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *corrosivus, from Latin corrodere "to gnaw to pieces," from com- "together" + rodere "to gnaw."
