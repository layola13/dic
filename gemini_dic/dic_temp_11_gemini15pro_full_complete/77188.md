gasped:/ɡæspt/ | v. | 喘气，倒抽一口气 | to inhale suddenly and sharply, as in shock or surprise. | She gasped when she saw the blood. 她看到血时倒抽了一口气。 | synonyms: inhaled sharply, breathed quickly, choked, panted | antonyms: exhaled, breathed slowly, sighed | verb

gasp:/ɡæsp/ | n. | 喘气，倒抽一口气 | a sudden, short intake of breath, as in shock or surprise. |  He let out a gasp of surprise. 他惊讶地倒抽了一口气。| synonyms: intake of breath, sharp breath, pant, wheeze | antonyms: exhalation, sigh | noun
