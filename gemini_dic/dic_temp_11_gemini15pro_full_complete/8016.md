anthracite-coals:/ˈænθrəsaɪt koʊlz/ | n. | 无烟煤 | A hard, compact variety of coal that has a high luster and burns with little flame or smoke. It is the highest rank of coal. | The blacksmith preferred anthracite coals for their intense heat. 铁匠更喜欢无烟煤，因为它们的热量高。 | The power plant used anthracite coals as its primary fuel source. 发电厂使用无烟煤作为主要燃料来源。 | synonyms: hard coal, black diamond, stone coal | antonyms: lignite, peat | noun
