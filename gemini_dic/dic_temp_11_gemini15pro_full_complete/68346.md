fall-to:/ˈfɔːl tuː/
verb phrase
开始认真地做某事；开始攻击；开始吃
begin to do something in a determined or enthusiastic way; begin to attack; begin to eat
The children fell to and ate everything on their plates. 孩子们开始狼吞虎咽地把盘子里的所有东西都吃光了。
The two armies fell to at dawn. 两军在拂晓开始交战。
synonyms: set to, get down to, tackle, attack, assault
antonyms:  delay, postpone, hesitate, hold back
