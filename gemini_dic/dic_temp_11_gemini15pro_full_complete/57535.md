drainage:/ˈdreɪnɪdʒ/| n.|排水; 排水系统; 排出物; 排水区域|The drainage system is very important for flood control. 排水系统对于防洪非常重要。|例句：The drainage in the area is poor, leading to flooding.  这个地区的排水很差，导致了洪水泛滥。

例句：The farmer improved the drainage of his fields.  这位农民改善了田地的排水系统。|近义词：draining, outflow, runoff|反义词:inflow, flooding|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *drenage, from *dreiner (Modern French drainer) "to drain," of uncertain origin, perhaps from Vulgar Latin *draginare, frequentative of Latin trahere "to draw, pull."
