mortaring:/ˈmɔːrtərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɔːtərɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 砌筑，用砂浆涂抹 | The act of applying mortar, a mixture of cement, sand, and water, to bricks, stones, or other building materials to bind them together. |  Example 1: The mortaring of the wall was completed quickly and efficiently.  墙体的砂浆涂抹工作快速高效地完成了。

 Example 2:  Poor mortaring can lead to structural weakness.  劣质的砂浆涂抹会导致结构脆弱。 | synonyms: cementing, plastering, rendering | antonyms: demolition, dismantling | noun
