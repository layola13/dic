nonsusceptibly:/ˌnɒnsəˈsɛptɪbli/ | adv. | 不易受影响地，无感觉地 | Not susceptible; in a nonsusceptible manner; without susceptibility. | The virus spread nonsusceptibly through the population, initially showing no noticeable symptoms. 病毒不易察觉地在人群中传播，最初没有明显的症状。 | The new coating made the metal nonsusceptibly to rust. 新涂层使金属不易生锈。 | 近义词：imperviously, invulnerably, resistantly | 反义词：susceptibly, vulnerably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
susceptible (adjective): from Late Latin susceptibilis, from Latin suscipere "to take up, undertake," from sub "up" + capere "to take"
-ly (suffix): adverbial suffix
