centerless:/ˈsɛntərlɪs/
adj.
无中心的，无轴的
lacking a center, especially lacking a center of rotation;
Centerless grinding is a machining process that uses abrasive grinding wheels to remove material from a workpiece. 无心磨削是一种使用砂轮从工件上去除材料的加工工艺。
近义词:axial, coreless
反义词:centered, axial
词性:形容词
