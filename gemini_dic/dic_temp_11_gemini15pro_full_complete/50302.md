depletable:/dɪˈpliːtəbl/
adjective
可耗尽的
able to be depleted; exhaustible.
例句：Fossil fuels are depletable resources.  化石燃料是可耗尽的资源。

例句：The earth's depletable resources must be managed carefully. 地球上可耗尽的资源必须谨慎管理。

近义词：exhaustible, finite, consumable
反义词：renewable, inexhaustible, infinite
词性: adjective


deplete:/dɪˈpliːt/
verb
耗尽，减少
reduce the amount of; exhaust the supply of.
例句：The prolonged drought has severely depleted water reserves. 长时间的干旱严重耗尽了水资源。

例句：Overfishing has depleted the fish population in the area. 过度捕捞耗尽了该地区的鱼类种群。

近义词：exhaust, drain, reduce, diminish
反义词：replenish, increase, augment
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (expressing removal or reversal) + pleten (to fill)  来自 de-（表示去除或逆转）+ pleten（填充）
