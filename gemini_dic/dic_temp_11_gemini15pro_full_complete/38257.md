comradery:/ˈkɑːmrɑːdəri/ /ˈkɒmrɑːdəri/
n.
同志情谊；友情
spirit of good fellowship
例句：There was a genuine sense of comradery among the team members.  团队成员之间有一种真诚的同志情谊。

例句：The soldiers developed a strong sense of comradery during the war.  士兵们在战争期间建立了深厚的同志情谊。

近义词：fellowship, camaraderie, friendship, esprit de corps
反义词：enmity, hostility, antagonism
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *camarade* (modern French *camarade*) "roommate, companion, comrade," from Spanish *camarada* "roommate," from Late Latin *camera* "chamber, room" (see chamber).  The sense of "fellow soldier" developed in French during the French Revolution.
