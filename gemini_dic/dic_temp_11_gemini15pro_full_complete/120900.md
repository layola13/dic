miscalculator:/ˌmɪskælkjəˈleɪtər/
|n.|误算者，计算错误的人|Someone who miscalculates or makes a mistake in calculation. |The miscalculator entered the wrong figures, leading to a significant financial error.  这个计算错误的人输入了错误的数字，导致了重大的财务错误。|Near synonyms: blunderer, estimator (when the miscalculation involves estimation) |Antonyms:  accurate calculator, precise estimator|noun
