demeanour:/dɪˈmiːnə(r)/ /dɪˈmenər/| n. |举止，行为，风度|Outward behavior; conduct; bearing.  The way a person behaves toward other people. |例句：


His quiet demeanour gave no hint of his violent past. 他的安静举止丝毫没有暗示他暴力的过去。


She maintained a professional demeanour throughout the meeting.  她在整个会议中保持着专业的举止。

|近义词：behavior, conduct, bearing, manner, deportment|反义词:misbehaviour, misconduct|词性:noun


demeanours:/dɪˈmiːnəz/ /dɪˈmenərz/| n. |举止（demeanour的复数形式）|Plural form of demeanour. Outward behaviors; conducts; bearings. The ways a person behaves toward other people. |例句：


Their respective demeanours couldn't have been more different.  他们各自的举止截然不同。

|近义词：behaviors, conducts, bearings, manners, deportments|反义词:misbehaviours, misconducts|词性:noun
