chirosophist:/ˈkaɪrəˌsɒfɪst/ /ˈkaɪroʊˌsɑːfɪst/ | n. |手相家，手相术士 | A person who practices chirosophy; one who claims to tell fortunes by examining the lines of the hand. |  He visited a chirosophist to learn about his future. 他拜访了一位手相家，想了解自己的未来。

The chirosophist examined her palm and predicted a long and prosperous life. 手相家仔细观察了她的手掌，预言她会长寿而且富裕。 | 近义词：palmist, hand reader | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *kheirosophistēs*, from *kheir* (hand) + *sophistēs* (wise man, expert).
