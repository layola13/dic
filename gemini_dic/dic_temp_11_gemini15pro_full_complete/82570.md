guard:/ɡɑːrd/ (US) /ɡɑːd/ (UK)| n. v.| 守卫，保卫；警卫；看守；护卫队|A person who protects or watches over something or someone.  A device designed to prevent injury or damage.|The museum hired a guard to protect the valuable artifacts. 博物馆雇佣了一名警卫来保护珍贵的文物。|近义词: protector, defender, watchman, custodian|反义词: attacker, assailant|noun
