cecidomyia:/ˌsɛsɪˈdɒmaɪə/ | n. |瘿蚊|A small, two-winged fly whose larvae cause galls to form on plants.|The cecidomyia infestation caused significant damage to the wheat crop. 瘿蚊侵扰对小麦作物造成了重大损害。|近义词: gall midge, gall gnat|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From New Latin, from Greek kēkis (gall) + myia (fly).
