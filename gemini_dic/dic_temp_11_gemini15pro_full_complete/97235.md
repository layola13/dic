inflection:/ɪnˈflɛkʃən/ | n. | 变格; 屈折变化; 音调变化 | A change in the form of a word (typically the ending) to express a grammatical function or attribute such as tense, mood, person, number, case, and gender. | The inflection of the verb indicates past tense.  动词的屈折变化表示过去时。

The inflection of his voice conveyed his doubt. 他说话的语调表达了他的疑惑。 | synonyms: conjugation, declension, variation, modulation, intonation | antonyms: uniformity, monotony | noun
