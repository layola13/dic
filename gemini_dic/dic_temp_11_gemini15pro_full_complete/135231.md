nonpalatability:/ˌnɒnpælətəˈbɪləti/ |n.|  难吃，不好吃；令人不快的 |The nonpalatability of the medicine made it difficult for the child to swallow.  这种药很难吃，孩子很难吞下去。| Near synonyms: unpalatability, distastefulness, unpleasantness | Antonyms: palatability, deliciousness, tastiness| noun

nonpalatabilities:/ˌnɒnpælətəˈbɪlətiz/ |n.| 难吃，不好吃；令人不快（复数）|The various nonpalatabilities of the experimental foods were recorded by the researchers. 研究人员记录了各种实验食物的难吃程度。|Near synonyms: unpalatabilities, distastefulnesses, unpleasantnesses | Antonyms: palatabilities, deliciousnesses, tastinesses |noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + palatable (pleasant to the taste) + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Palatable comes from Old French *palatable, from palat (palate) + -able (suffix meaning "capable of being").
