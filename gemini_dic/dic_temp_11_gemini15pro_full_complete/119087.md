metrician:/məˈtrɪʃən/
n.
韵律学家；诗体专家；精通韵律的人
A specialist in versification, prosody, and the metrical structure of verse.
The metrician analyzed the poem's complex rhythmic patterns. 这位韵律学家分析了这首诗复杂的节奏模式。
prosodist, versifier, rhythmist
