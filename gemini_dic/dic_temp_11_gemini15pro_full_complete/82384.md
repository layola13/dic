grubber:/ˈɡrʌbər/ | n. | 挖掘者；挖根工具；低传球 |  A person or tool that digs, especially one that digs up roots or something hidden in the ground. In sports, especially rugby and cricket, a grubber is a low, bouncing kick. | The gardener used a grubber to remove the weeds. 园丁用挖根工具清除杂草。 The scrum-half put in a clever grubber kick.  争球队的二传手踢出一个巧妙的低传球。 | 近义词: digger, excavator (for tools), cultivator (for tools), grubbing tool, dribbler (in sports) | 反义词: planter (opposite of removing plants), sower | 词性: 名词

grubber:/ˈɡrʌbər/ | v. | 挖掘；翻寻 | To dig, especially to dig up roots or something hidden in the ground. | He grubbed around in the dirt for his lost keys. 他在泥土里翻找丢失的钥匙。  She grubbed up the potatoes for dinner. 她挖出土豆准备晚餐。 | 近义词: dig, root, rummage, forage | 反义词: plant, bury | 词性: 动词
