czar's:/ˈzɑːrz/
词性:n.
中文翻译:沙皇的
英文解析:Possessive form of "czar," indicating ownership or belonging to a czar.  It's used as an adjective.
例句：The czar's palace was opulent. 沙皇的宫殿富丽堂皇。

例句：The czar's decree was absolute. 沙皇的法令是绝对的。
近义词:emperor's, monarch's, ruler's
反义词:subject's, citizen's
