masqueraders:/ˌmæs.kəˈreɪ.dərz/ | n. | 伪装者；化装者；假冒者 | People who wear masks or costumes to disguise themselves, often for a party or celebration. |  The masqueraders danced through the streets during the carnival.  狂欢节期间，化装者在街上跳舞。 | Synonyms:  disguisers, impersonators, pretenders, posers | Antonyms:  non-masqueraders (This is not a commonly used word, but it is the direct opposite) | noun
