aerobicist:/ɛˈroʊbɪsɪst/
n.
有氧运动教练，有氧健身教练
A person who teaches or leads aerobic exercises.
She's a certified aerobicist and teaches classes at the local gym. 她是一位经过认证的有氧运动教练，在当地一家健身房授课。
近义词：aerobics instructor, fitness instructor
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps) personal trainer specializing in weightlifting
词性：noun


aerobics:/ɛˈroʊbɪks/
n.
有氧运动
Exercises that strengthen the heart and lungs by making them work hard for several minutes or more.
I do aerobics three times a week to improve my cardiovascular health. 我每周做三次有氧运动来改善我的心血管健康。
近义词：cardio
反义词：anaerobics
词性：noun
