The gentleness of the climate. |She accepted a small douceur for her services. 她接受了一笔小小的酬金作为服务的报酬。|Synonyms: sweetness, gentleness, mildness, gratuity, tip, bribe|Antonyms: harshness, severity, unkindness|noun
