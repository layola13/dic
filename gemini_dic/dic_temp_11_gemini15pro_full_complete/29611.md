categorists:/ˈkætəɡərɪsts/
n.
分类学家；范畴论者
A person who studies or specializes in categories, especially in philosophy or mathematics, often referring to those who work with category theory.
Categorists often work on abstract mathematical structures. 分类学家经常研究抽象的数学结构。
The conference attracted categorists from around the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的范畴论者。
近义词:classifiers, taxonomists
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "generalists")
词性:noun
