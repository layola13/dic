dehydrogenation:/ˌdiːhaɪˌdrɒdʒəˈneɪʃən/ |n.|脱氢，脱氢作用|The process of removing hydrogen from a molecule.  This is a common reaction in organic chemistry and is often used in industrial processes.|The dehydrogenation of ethane produces ethene. 乙烷脱氢生成乙烯。|synonyms: oxidation, removal of hydrogen|antonyms: hydrogenation, addition of hydrogen|noun

dehydrogenate:/ˌdiːhaɪˈdrɒdʒəneɪt/ |v.|使脱氢|To remove hydrogen from a molecule.|Ethanol can be dehydrogenated to form acetaldehyde. 乙醇可以脱氢形成乙醛。|synonyms: oxidize|antonyms: hydrogenate|verb
