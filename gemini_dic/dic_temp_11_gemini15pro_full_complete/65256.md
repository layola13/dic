etymon:/ˈetɪmɒn/ (US) /ˈɛtɪˌmɑn/| n. |词源；词根；原义|A word or morpheme from which a later word is derived.  The fundamental linguistic unit from which other words are derived, whether by derivation, inflection, or compounding.|例句：The etymon of the word "electricity" is the Greek word "elektron," meaning amber. “electricity”一词的词源是希腊语“elektron”，意思是琥珀。

例句：Tracing the etymon of a word can reveal fascinating insights into its history and evolution. 追溯一个词的词源可以揭示其历史和演变的迷人见解。|近义词：origin, root, source, derivation|反义词: derivative|词性:noun
