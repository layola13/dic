effectible:/ɪˈfɛktɪbl/
adjective
可实行的，可完成的
Capable of being effected; achievable.
The plan is ambitious but effectible given sufficient resources.  这个计划雄心勃勃，但如果资源充足，是可以实现的。
The changes are small but effectible. 这些改变虽小，但行之有效。
近义词: achievable, feasible, practicable, realizable, viable
反义词: impossible, impracticable, unattainable, unachievable, unfeasible
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *effectibilis, from Latin effectus, past participle of efficere "to bring about, accomplish," from ex- "out" + facere "to do, make."
