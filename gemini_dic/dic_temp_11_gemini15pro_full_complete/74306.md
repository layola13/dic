fractiously:/ˈfrækʃəsli/ | adv. | 暴躁地；易怒地；倔强地 | In a way that expresses irritation or ill temper; readily angered; unruly. | The children were behaving fractiously due to lack of sleep. 孩子们因为缺乏睡眠而表现得暴躁不安。| The team argued fractiously about the best strategy. 团队成员就最佳策略进行了激烈的争论。 |  近义词:irritably, peevishly, quarrelsomely, cantankerously | 反义词:agreeably, amiably, cooperatively, peacefully | 词性:副词

fractious:/ˈfrækʃəs/ | adj. | 易怒的；暴躁的；倔强的 |  Easily irritated; bad-tempered; unruly. | The fractious child refused to go to bed.  这个暴躁的孩子拒绝上床睡觉。 |  The political climate was fractious and divisive. 政治气氛剑拔弩张，充满分歧。 | 近义词:irritable, grumpy, churlish, irascible | 反义词: docile, agreeable, pleasant, amiable |词性:形容词
