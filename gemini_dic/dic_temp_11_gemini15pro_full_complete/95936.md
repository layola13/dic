incubates:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪts/| v. |孵化，培养，孕育|to maintain (eggs, bacteria, embryos, etc.) at a suitable temperature for development; to develop slowly; foster|The mother bird diligently incubates her eggs. 母鸟勤奋地孵蛋。|近义词：develop, foster, nurture, cultivate|反义词: neglect, stifle|verb

incubates:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪts/| v. |酝酿，(疾病)潜伏|to develop slowly and secretly; to be in an early stage of development|He's incubating a new plan to boost sales. 他正在酝酿一个新的销售计划。|近义词：develop, brew, gestate|反义词: halt, stop|verb
