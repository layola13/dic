foetus's:/ˈfiːtəsɪz/
noun
胎儿的（所有格）
Possessive form of "foetus," referring to something belonging to or associated with a fetus.
The foetus's heartbeat was strong. 胎儿的心跳很强。
The doctor monitored the foetus's development. 医生监测胎儿的发育情况。
synonym: fetus's (American spelling), unborn baby's
antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider: infant's, baby's, newborn's - for after birth)
