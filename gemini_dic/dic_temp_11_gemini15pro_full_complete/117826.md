mellification:/ˌmelɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 甜化，蜜化；（比喻）使甜蜜，使愉快 | The process of making something sweet like honey, or figuratively, making something more pleasant or agreeable. | The mellification of her voice made the lullaby soothing.  她的声音变得甜美，使摇篮曲更加舒缓。| 近义词：sweetening, dulcification | 反义词：bittering, souring | noun
