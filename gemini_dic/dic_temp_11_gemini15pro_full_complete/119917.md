milk:/mɪlk/ | n.  v. |牛奶；挤奶|A white liquid produced by mammals to feed their young.  To draw milk from (a cow, goat, or other animal).| Cows produce milk. 奶牛产奶。| I milk the cows every morning. 我每天早上挤牛奶。 | 近义词:dairy (for the product), extract (for the verb) | 反义词: (no direct antonym for milk as a product. For the verb, perhaps "wean") | 词性: noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *meolc*, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *melk* and German *Milch*.
