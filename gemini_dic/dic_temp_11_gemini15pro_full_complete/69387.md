feedback: /ˈfiːdbæk/ | n. | 反馈，意见 |  Information about reactions to a product, a person's performance of a task, etc. which is used as a basis for improvement. | The teacher gave me some feedback on my essay. 老师就我的论文给我提了一些意见。 | synonyms: response, reaction, comment, input, review | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: lack of response, silence) | noun
