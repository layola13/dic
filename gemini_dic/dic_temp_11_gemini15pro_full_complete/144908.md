over-stored:/ˌoʊvərˈstɔːrd/| adj.|过度存储的，库存过剩的|Having more stored than is needed or can be used; excessively stocked.|The warehouse was over-stored with unsold merchandise. 仓库里积压了大量未售出的商品。|
The supermarket was over-stored with pumpkins after Halloween. 超市万圣节后南瓜库存过剩。|Synonyms: overstocked, glutted, saturated|Antonyms: understocked, depleted|adjective
