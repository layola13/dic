assurance:/əˈʃʊrəns/ | n. |保证，担保；自信，把握；（人寿）保险|A positive declaration intended to give confidence; a promise.  |例句：He gave me his assurance that the work would be completed on time. 他向我保证工作会按时完成。

例句：She spoke with assurance about the future of the company. 她自信地谈论了公司的未来。|近义词：certainty, confidence, guarantee, promise|反义词：doubt, uncertainty, insecurity|词性: noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin assurantia, from assurare ‘make safe, ensure’ (see assure).
