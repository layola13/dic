nonprotuberant:/nɒnproʊˈtʃuːbərənt/
adjective
不突出的，不隆起的
not protuberant; not bulging or swelling outward.
The cyst was nonprotuberant and difficult to detect by touch.  这个囊肿不突出，很难通过触摸检测到。
The wall was smooth and nonprotuberant.  墙壁光滑且不突出。

近义词:flat, even, smooth, level
反义词:protuberant, bulging, protruding, convex
词性:adjective
