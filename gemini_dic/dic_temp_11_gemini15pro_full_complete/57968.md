dripstone:/ˈdrɪpˌstoʊn/| n.|滴石,钟乳石|A deposit of calcium carbonate formed by the dripping of water, such as a stalactite or stalagmite.|Example: The cave was filled with fascinating dripstones of all shapes and sizes. 这个洞穴里充满了各种形状和大小的迷人滴石。|Example:  The dripstone formations created an otherworldly atmosphere in the cavern. 滴石的形成在洞穴中营造了一种超凡脱俗的氛围。|近义词：stalactite, stalagmite|反义词:|名词
