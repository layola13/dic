noninflammableness:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈflæməbəlnəs/
n.
不可燃性；不燃性
The quality of not being easily ignited and not burning rapidly if ignited.
The noninflammableness of the material made it safe for use in the factory.  这种材料的不可燃性使其可在工厂安全使用。
近义词:incombustibility, fire resistance
反义词:flammability, inflammability
词性:noun

noninflammable:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈflæməbəl/
adj.
不可燃的；不燃的
Not easily ignited and not burning rapidly if ignited.
Asbestos is a noninflammable material. 石棉是一种不可燃材料。
近义词:incombustible, fire-resistant
反义词:flammable, inflammable
词性:adjective
