collegiately:/kəˈliːdʒɪətli/
|adv.|
大学式地； collegiately|
Acting in a manner befitting a college student, especially in a positive sense, exhibiting qualities like camaraderie, intellectual curiosity, or a focus on academics and extracurricular activities.  像大学生一样行事，尤指在积极意义上，展现出同志情谊、求知欲或专注于学术和课外活动的品质。|
He debated the issue collegiately, presenting well-researched arguments and respecting opposing viewpoints. 他以大学生的方式辩论这个问题，提出了经过充分研究的论点，并尊重对方的观点。

She dressed collegiately in a sweater, jeans, and sneakers. 她穿着大学生的服装，毛衣、牛仔裤和运动鞋。

|近义词：academically, scholastically|
|反义词：unprofessionally, immaturely|
|词性：副词(adverb)|
