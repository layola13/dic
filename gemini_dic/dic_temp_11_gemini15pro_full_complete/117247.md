mech:/mɛk/| n.|机械, 机甲, 机修工 (非正式)|A shortened form of "mechanic" or "mechanical". Refers to anything related to machinery, mechanics, or sometimes, a mecha (a large, piloted robot).|The mech's engine roared to life.  机甲的引擎轰鸣着启动了。

The mech worked tirelessly on the car.  机修工不知疲倦地修着车。|近义词：mechanic, machine, robot (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|Noun
