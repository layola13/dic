merging:/ˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/| adj. n.|合并，融合|The act or process of combining or uniting two or more things into one.|The merging of the two companies created a larger, more powerful corporation. 两家公司的合并创造了一个更大、更强大的公司。|近义词：combining, uniting, integrating, blending|反义词：separating, dividing, splitting|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology: From Middle English mergen, from Old French mergier, from Late Latin mergere, from Latin mergō (“to dip, plunge, immerse, sink”).
