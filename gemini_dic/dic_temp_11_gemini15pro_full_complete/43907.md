creux:/krø/| adj.|空的，中空的，凹陷的|A creux is a hollow or sunken area. It can refer to a physical indentation or a feeling of emptiness.|The sculptor carved a creux in the stone. 雕塑家在石头上雕刻了一个凹陷。|The news of her death left a creux in my heart. 她去世的消息让我心里空落落的。|近义词：hollow, concave, sunken|反义词：convex, full, solid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French "creus," past participle of "crever" (to break, burst, hollow out), from Latin "crepare" (to crack, rattle, burst).
