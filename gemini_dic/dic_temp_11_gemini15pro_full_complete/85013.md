haranguing:/həˈræŋɪŋ/
harangue (verb):/həˈræŋ/
harangue (noun):/həˈræŋ/
词性: Verb (动词),  Noun (名词)
中文翻译: (v.) 长篇大论地演说，高谈阔论；(n.) 长篇大论，高谈阔论
英文解析: (v.) To deliver a long, pompous, or intense speech or lecture, often characterized by criticism or admonition. (n.) A long, pompous, or intense speech or lecture, often critical or admonitory.
例句: The coach harangued the team after their poor performance. 教练在球队表现糟糕后对他们进行了长篇大论的训斥。
例句: His harangue about the dangers of social media went on for hours. 他关于社交媒体危害的长篇大论持续了几个小时。
近义词: lecture, rant, diatribe, tirade, sermonize, declaim
反义词: discuss, converse, chat, whisper, praise
词性: v., n.


haranguing (present participle):/həˈræŋɪŋ/
词性:Present Participle (现在分词)
中文翻译:正在长篇大论地演说，正在高谈阔论
英文解析:The present participle form of the verb "harangue," used to describe the action of delivering a long, pompous speech or lecture.
例句: He was haranguing the crowd about the importance of voting. 他正在对人群高谈阔论投票的重要性。
例句: She was haranguing her children about the mess in their rooms.  她正在对她的孩子们大声斥责他们房间里的脏乱。
近义词: lecturing, ranting, scolding, berating
反义词: discussing, conversing, praising, encouraging
词性:present participle


词源: From French *harangue*, from Old Italian *arangare* "to speak in public," of Germanic origin.
