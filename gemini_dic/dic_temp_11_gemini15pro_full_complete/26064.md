bushman:/ˈbʊʃmən/| n.|布须曼人；居住在灌木丛中的人|A member of any of several indigenous hunter-gatherer peoples of southern Africa.|The bushman tracked the antelope for days.  布须曼人追踪羚羊好几天了。|近义词：hunter-gatherer, San|反义词:|n.

bushman:/ˈbʊʃmən/| n.|灌木丛居民；居住在偏远地区的人|Someone who lives in the bush or a remote area.|He lived like a bushman, far from civilization. 他像一个布须曼人一样生活，远离文明。|近义词：outback dweller, backwoodsman|反义词: city dweller|n.
