collapsing:/kəˈlæpsɪŋ/ | adj. | 倒塌的，崩溃的，萎陷的 | Present participle of collapse, meaning falling down or caving in. | The collapsing building was a hazard. 倒塌的建筑物很危险。| The collapsing economy caused widespread panic. 崩溃的经济引起了广泛的恐慌。|Synonyms: crumbling, failing, disintegrating | Antonyms: rising, strengthening, burgeoning | Verb (present participle)

collapse:/kəˈlæps/ | v. n. | 倒塌，崩溃；折叠 | From Latin *collapsus*, past participle of *collabi* "to fall together," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *labi* "to slip, slide, fall." | The old bridge collapsed into the river. 老桥坍塌到河里。|  He suffered a collapse from exhaustion. 他因疲惫而晕倒了。| Synonyms: fall, crumble, fail, give way | Antonyms: rise, build, strengthen | Noun/Verb
