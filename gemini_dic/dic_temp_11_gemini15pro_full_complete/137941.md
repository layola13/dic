nonwar:/ˌnɒnˈwɔːr/ /ˌnɑːnˈwɔːr/
adjective
非战争的
Relating to activities or situations that are not directly related to war or armed conflict.  Characterized by the absence of war.
The government increased its nonwar spending this year. 政府今年增加了非战争支出。
The ambassador worked tirelessly to find nonwar solutions to the conflict. 大使不知疲倦地为冲突寻找非战争解决方案。

近义词:peaceful, civilian, nonmilitary
反义词:war, military, warlike
词性:adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "war" (noun).  The prefix "non-" comes from Old French and ultimately from Latin "non".  "War" comes from Old English "werre" and is related to Old High German "werra".
