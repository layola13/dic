indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪndɪkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|指示器，指标，标志|Something that shows or suggests the state or level of something.|The unemployment rate is a key economic indicator. 失业率是一个关键的经济指标。|synonyms: sign, signal, measure, gauge|antonyms:  concealment,  obscurity|noun

indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪndɪkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|指示的，标示的|Serving as a sign or indication of something.|The indicator light flashed red. 指示灯闪烁着红光。|synonyms: indicative, suggestive, revealing|antonyms: concealing, hiding|adjective

Present Continuous:
