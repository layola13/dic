backdating:/ˈbækˌdeɪtɪŋ/
n.|回溯日期; 倒填日期|The act of putting a date on a document that is earlier than the current date.  This is sometimes done for fraudulent purposes, but can also be legitimate in certain circumstances.|The company was accused of backdating stock options.  该公司被指控倒填股票期权日期。|anachronism, retroaction|predating|noun
