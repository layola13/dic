glosser:/ˈɡlɒsə(r)/ (US) /ˈɡlɑːsər/ | n. |注释者；注解器； glossaries 的编写者|A glosser is someone who writes glosses, which are brief explanations or translations of words or phrases, often found in the margins of texts or in glossaries. |The medieval glosser provided helpful annotations to the complex Latin text. 中世纪的注释者对复杂的拉丁文本提供了有用的注释。

The glosser's work made the ancient manuscript accessible to modern readers. 注释者的工作使现代读者能够理解古代手稿。|近义词：annotator, commentator, explainer |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps obfuscator - someone who makes something unclear) |词性: noun
