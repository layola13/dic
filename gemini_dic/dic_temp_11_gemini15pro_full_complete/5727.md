alveolars:/ælˈviːələrz/ | n. | 肺泡；齿槽音 | The alveolars are tiny air sacs in the lungs where the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide takes place. 肺泡是肺部进行氧气和二氧化碳交换的微小气囊。|  The sounds /t/, /d/, and /n/ are alveolar consonants.  /t/、/d/和/n/是齿槽辅音。 | 近义词: air sacs (for lung alveoli) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

alveolar:/ælˈviːələr/ | adj. | 肺泡的；齿槽的 | Alveolar macrophages are immune cells found in the alveoli. 肺泡巨噬细胞是在肺泡中发现的免疫细胞。 |  The alveolar ridge is the part of the mouth where the tongue touches to produce alveolar sounds. 齿槽嵴是舌头接触产生齿槽音的口腔部位。| 近义词: pulmonary (for lung alveoli) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
