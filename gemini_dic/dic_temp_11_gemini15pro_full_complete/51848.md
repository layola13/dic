dexterously:/ˈdɛkstərəsli/| adv.|灵巧地，熟练地|In a skillful and clever way, especially in handling things.|She dexterously manipulated the controls of the complex machine. 她熟练地操纵着复杂机器的控制装置。|近义词：skillfully, adroitly, adeptly, nimbly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly|词性:副词

词源:
From Latin *dexter* "right, favorable, skillful" (source also of French *dextre*, Spanish *diestro*) + *-ous* (forming adjectives) + *-ly* (forming adverbs). The original sense was "on the right side," which was associated with skill and good fortune.
