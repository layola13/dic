noncovetous:/ˌnɒnˈkʌvɪtəs/
adjective
不贪婪的；不觊觎的
Not desiring something that belongs to another.
He led a simple, noncovetous life, finding joy in nature and friendship rather than material possessions. 他过着简朴、不贪婪的生活，在自然和友谊中寻找快乐，而不是物质财富。
The monk took a vow of poverty, embracing a noncovetous existence.  这位僧侣立誓要过贫穷的生活，拥抱一种不贪婪的存在。
近义词:content, satisfied, unacquisitive, unambitious
反义词:covetous, greedy, avaricious, grasping
词性:adjective
