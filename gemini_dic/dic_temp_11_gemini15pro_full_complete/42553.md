countermyth's:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɪθs/ | n. | 反驳神话; 反驳虚构故事;  |  A belief, idea, or story that counters a prevailing myth.  It's the possessive form of "countermyth," indicating belonging to or associated with a countermyth. | The countermyth's central argument was well-researched and compelling. 这个反驳神话的核心论点经过充分研究，很有说服力。| Near Synonyms: counter-narrative, rebuttal, debunking | Antonym: myth, fabrication | Noun
