ferrocyanide:/ˌfɛroʊˈsaɪənaɪd/ | n. |亚铁氰化物；六氰合铁(II)酸盐|A complex ion containing iron, carbon, and nitrogen, typically with four negative charges, or a salt of this ion. It forms brightly colored pigments with various metals.|Potassium ferrocyanide is used as an anticaking agent in table salt. 亚铁氰化钾用作食盐中的抗结块剂。|Synonym: hexacyanoferrate(II) |Antonym: ferricyanide |noun

ferrocyanide:/ˌfɛroʊˈsaɪənaɪd/ | adj. |亚铁氰化物的|Containing or relating to the ferrocyanide ion.|Ferrocyanide salts are often used in pigments. 亚铁氰化物盐常用于颜料中。|Synonym: hexacyanoferrate(II) |Antonym: ferricyanide |adjective
