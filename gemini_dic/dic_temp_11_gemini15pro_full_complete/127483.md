necessitude:/nɪˈsɛsɪˌtjuːd/ /nəˈsesɪˌtuːd/
n.
变迁；兴衰；沧桑
a change or variation in circumstances or fortune, typically one that is unwelcome or unpleasant.
例句：Life is full of vicissitudes. 人生充满了变迁。
例句：She has experienced the vicissitudes of life, from poverty to great wealth. 她经历了人生的沧桑，从贫穷到巨富。
近义词：change, fluctuation, variation, mutability, inconstancy
反义词：stability, constancy, permanence
词性：名词
