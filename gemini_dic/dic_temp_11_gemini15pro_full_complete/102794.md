jardine:/ˈdʒɑːrdɪn/ | n. |围场，畜栏；（尤指东南亚的）洋行|An enclosure for keeping or raising animals, especially in Southeast Asia. Also refers to a trading house, particularly in Southeast Asia.|The Jardines operated a large jardine in Hong Kong for the import and export of goods.  怡和洋行在香港经营着一个大型围场，用于进出口货物。

The Jardine Matheson Holdings is a famous jardine in Asia. 怡和 Matheson 控股公司是亚洲著名的洋行。 |近义词：enclosure, compound, trading house|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open field" for the enclosure sense) |词性: noun
