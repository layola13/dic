generalissimo:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈlɪsɪmoʊ/
n.
最高统帅，大元帅
The supreme commander of a combined military force, especially one who holds dictatorial powers.
Franco was proclaimed Generalissimo of Spain. 佛朗哥被宣布为西班牙的最高统帅。
synonyms: commander-in-chief, supreme commander, general, chief
antonyms: subordinate, follower
noun
