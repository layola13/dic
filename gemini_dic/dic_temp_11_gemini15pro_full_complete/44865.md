crux:/krʌks/| n.|关键，症结，核心|The crux of the matter is that we don't have enough money. 问题的症结在于我们没有足够的钱。|The crux of the argument was whether to raise taxes. 争论的焦点在于是否提高税收。|近义词：core, heart, essence, gist, kernel, nub|反义词: periphery, exterior, surface|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *crux* "cross, torture, affliction," also "a frame or other instrument of execution," figuratively "trouble, vexation, difficulty."
