nestling:/ˈnestlɪŋ/| n. |雏鸟; 婴儿; 幼小动物|A young bird that has not yet left the nest.  A baby or young child. A young animal.|例句：The nestlings chirped loudly, begging for food. 雏鸟大声唧唧喳喳地叫着，讨要食物。

例句：The mother bird carefully fed the nestlings. 母鸟细心地喂养雏鸟。|近义词：chick, fledgling, hatchling, infant, baby|反义词:adult|词性:noun
