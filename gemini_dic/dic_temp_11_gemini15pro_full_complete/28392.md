carbolize:/ˈkɑːrbəlaɪz/ | v. | 碳化，使碳化 | To heat a substance in the absence of air so that it decomposes and leaves a residue of carbon. |  The wood was carbolized in the intense heat. 木头在高温下碳化了。

The intense heat carbolized the organic material, leaving behind a black residue.  高温使有机物碳化，留下黑色残留物。| 近义词: carbonize, char | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 动词

词源: From carbon + -ize.  来自 carbon（碳） + -ize （使...化）
