kaleidoscopically:/kəˌlaɪdəˈskɒpɪkli/ | adv. | 万花筒般地；变化多端地 | In a manner resembling a kaleidoscope; with constant shifts of color, pattern, or form.  Characterized by a rapid and colorful succession of changing images or events. | The city lights shimmered kaleidoscopically in the rain-slicked streets. 城市灯光在湿漉漉的街道上万花筒般地闪烁。

The dancer's costume swirled kaleidoscopically as she spun across the stage.  舞者旋转穿过舞台时，她的服装万花筒般地旋转。 | Synonyms:  multifaceted, ever-changing,  variegated, shifting, iridescent | Antonyms:  monotonous, unchanging, static, uniform | 词性: 副词
