intermigrates:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪɡreɪts/ | v. |迁徙，互相迁移 | To migrate within a region or between regions; to move back and forth between different places. |  The butterfly population intermigrates between the mountains and the coast each year. 每年蝴蝶种群都会在山区和海岸之间迁徙。

The two tribes intermigrated across the valley, sharing resources and intermarrying. 这两个部落在山谷之间互相迁移，共享资源并通婚。 | 近义词: migrate, move, relocate | 反义词: settle, stay, remain |词性:动词
