micrometric:/ˌmaɪkrəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|微米的，测微计的|Relating to or measured in micrometers; extremely precise or accurate.|例句：Micrometric adjustments are essential for this experiment.  这个实验需要进行微米级的调整。

例句：The device makes micrometric movements, allowing for precise manipulation.  该设备进行微米级的运动，允许精确的操作。|近义词：minute, tiny, microscopic, precise, accurate|反义词：macroscopic, large, imprecise, inaccurate|词性:形容词

micrometer:/maɪˈkrɒmɪtər/| n.|测微计，千分尺|An instrument for measuring small distances or thicknesses.|例句：He used a micrometer to measure the thickness of the sheet of metal. 他用千分尺测量了金属板的厚度。

例句：The micrometer revealed the object was only a few microns wide. 千分尺显示该物体只有几微米宽。|近义词：calliper, gauge|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ruler" for a less precise instrument)|词性:名词

Etymology:
From micro- (small) + -metric (pertaining to measurement).  Ultimately from Greek *mikros* (small) and *metron* (measure).
