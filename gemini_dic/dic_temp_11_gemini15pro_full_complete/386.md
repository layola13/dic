abiogenesis:/ˌeɪbaɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
自然发生, 无生源说
The formation of living organisms from non-living matter; such genesis as does not involve the action of living parents.
Abiogenesis is the study of how life could have arisen from non-living matter.  自然发生学是研究生命如何从非生命物质中产生的学科。
Scientists are still researching different theories of abiogenesis. 科学家们仍在研究不同的自然发生理论。
近义词: spontaneous generation
反义词: biogenesis
词性: noun
