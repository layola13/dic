estate:/ɪˈsteɪt/ | n. | 财产，遗产，房地产，庄园 |  A person's estate consists of all the money and property that they leave behind them when they die. |  He left his entire estate to his daughter. 他把全部财产留给了女儿。| synonyms: property, assets, holdings, possessions | antonyms: debt, liability | noun

estate:/ɪˈsteɪt/ | n. | 地产，不动产 | An estate is a large area of land in the country, usually with a large house on it, or all the land and buildings owned by one person or organization. |  He owns a large estate in the countryside. 他在乡下拥有一大片地产。| synonyms: property, land, manor | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "rental property" for contrast) | noun
