giraffes:/dʒəˈræfs/ | n. | 长颈鹿 |  A tall African mammal with a very long neck and legs, a spotted coat, and small horns. |  We saw a herd of giraffes on safari. 我们在旅行中看到了一群长颈鹿。 |  Near synonyms: camelopard (obsolete) | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
