overprominently: /ˌoʊvərˈprɒmɪnəntli/ | adv. | 过于突出地，过分显著地 | In a way that is too noticeable or emphasized. |  He overprominently displayed his wealth, making others uncomfortable. 他过分炫耀自己的财富，让别人感到不舒服。|  The logo was overprominently placed on the shirt, making it look like a billboard.  商标过于突出地印在衬衫上，让它看起来像个广告牌。| 近义词: conspicuously, excessively, unduly | 反义词: subtly, discreetly, unobtrusively | 词性: 副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "prominent" (adjective meaning "standing out so as to be easily seen; important, well-known") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
