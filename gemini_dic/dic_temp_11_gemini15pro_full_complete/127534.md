necromorph:/ˈnɛkrəˌmɔːrf/| n. |尸变体，亡灵变体|A reanimated corpse that has been transformed into a monstrous creature, typically through infection or some form of dark magic.  A term popularized by the video game *Dead Space*.|The necromorphs overwhelmed the crew of the USG Ishimura. 尸变体淹没了石村号的船员。|The necromorphs swarmed the corridors, their grotesque forms a terrifying sight. 尸变体蜂拥在走廊里，它们怪诞的样子令人恐惧。|Synonyms: undead, zombie, monster, ghoul|Antonyms: living, alive|Noun
