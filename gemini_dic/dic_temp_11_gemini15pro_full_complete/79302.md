glazieries:/ˈɡleɪʒəriːz/
noun
玻璃厂, 玻璃制品
places where glass is made or repaired or sold; glass items collectively
The old city boasted several glazieries, each specializing in a different type of stained glass.  这座古城拥有几家玻璃厂，每家都专门制作不同类型的彩色玻璃。
Synonym: glassworks, glass factories
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "metalworks" or "woodworking shops" for contrasting materials)
词性: 名词
