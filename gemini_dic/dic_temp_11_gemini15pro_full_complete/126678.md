词源:  "Nambi" derives from the Nama word for "vast place," referring to the Namib Desert, a defining feature of the country.  Namibia gained independence from South Africa in 1990.


注意事项:

"Nambi" is an informal term, often used colloquially.  "Namibian" is the preferred and more formal term.  The term "Nambi" for the language is less common;  "Oshiwambo" is the most widely spoken language, although there are several recognized national languages.
