nonspeech:/ˈnɑːnˌspiːtʃ/ /ˈnɒnˌspiːtʃ/ | n. | 非言语; 非言语行为 | Communication that does not involve speaking, such as body language, facial expressions, and gestures. |  Her nonspeech cues, like a slight frown and tapping fingers, suggested she was impatient. 她的非言语暗示，比如轻微皱眉和敲击手指，表明她不耐烦。| synonyms: nonverbal communication, body language | antonyms: speech, verbal communication | noun

nonspeech:/ˈnɑːnˌspiːtʃ/ /ˈnɒnˌspiːtʃ/ | adj. | 非言语的 | Relating to communication that does not involve speaking. |  Nonspeech communication can often be more powerful than words. 非言语交流通常比言语更有力。| synonyms: nonverbal | antonyms: verbal, spoken | adjective
