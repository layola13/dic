floundered:/ˈflɑʊndərd/ /ˈflaʊndəd/| v.|挣扎；折腾；困难重重地前进；支吾；不知所措|To struggle clumsily or helplessly; to move with difficulty or agitation; to speak or act haltingly and confusedly.|例句：The company floundered in the recession.  这家公司在经济衰退中苦苦挣扎。|例句：He floundered for an answer to the unexpected question. 他对这个突如其来的问题不知所措，结结巴巴地回答。|近义词：struggle, thrash, wallow, stumble, falter|反义词:succeed, thrive, flourish
词性:动词
