insults:/ˈɪnˌsʌlts/ | n. | 侮辱，辱骂 |  Rude and disrespectful remarks or actions intended to offend or hurt someone's feelings.  | He hurled insults at the referee.  他辱骂裁判。 | synonyms: slurs, abuse, affronts, offenses | antonyms: compliments, praise | noun

insults:/ɪnˈsʌlts/ | vt. | 侮辱，辱骂 | To speak to or treat with disrespect, contempt, or abuse. |  His comments insulted my intelligence. 他的话侮辱了我的智力。 | synonyms: offends, affronts, slights | antonyms: compliments, praises | verb
