antinomian:/ˌæntɪˈnoʊmiən/ | n. adj. |反律主义者，反律主义的 | A person who believes that Christians are not bound by moral law, especially one who believes that salvation is attained solely through faith and divine grace.  |  The preacher was accused of being an antinomian because he suggested the Ten Commandments were merely suggestions.  这位传教士被指责为反律主义者，因为他暗示十诫仅仅是建议。| synonyms: libertine, freethinker, heretic | antonyms: legalist, moralist | noun, adjective

antinomism:/ˌæntɪˈnoʊmɪɪzəm/ | n. |反律主义 | The belief that Christians are released by grace from the obligation of observing the moral law. | Some forms of antinomism can lead to extreme behaviors.  某些形式的反律主义会导致极端行为。| synonyms: lawlessness, libertinism | antonyms: legalism, moralism | noun
