diiambus:/ˌdaɪˈæmbəs/ | n. |抑扬格| A metrical foot in poetry consisting of one short (or unstressed) syllable followed by one long (or stressed) syllable. |  The word "delight" is a diiambus.  “delight”这个词是一个抑扬格。 |  iamb, iambus |  | noun
