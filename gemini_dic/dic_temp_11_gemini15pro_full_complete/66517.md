词源:  From Latin exhalāre (“to breathe out, exhale”), from ex- (“out”) + halāre (“to breathe”).

注意事项:  Exhalence is a relatively uncommon word in modern English.  While both definitions relate to something emitted, the "fragrance" meaning is more common in literary contexts.
