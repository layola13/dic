enarch:/ˈenɑːrk/ | n. |地方行政长官|A governor or administrator of a province or district.|The enarch was responsible for collecting taxes and maintaining order in the province.  这位地方行政长官负责征税和维护该省的秩序。|近义词：governor, administrator, prefect |反义词:citizen, resident|Noun
enarch:/ˈiːnɑːrk/ | n. | (尤指法国的)高级文官；(毕业于法国国立行政学院的)高级行政官员|A senior civil servant, especially in France, a graduate of the École Nationale d'Administration (ENA).|Many enarchs go on to hold high positions in government and industry. 许多高级文官在政府和工业界担任要职。|近义词：bureaucrat, civil servant, mandarin |反义词:worker, laborer|Noun
