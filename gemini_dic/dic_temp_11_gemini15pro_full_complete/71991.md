flinders:/ˈflɪndərz/| n. |碎片，碎屑|Small broken pieces of something, especially wood or stone. |The floor was covered in flinders of glass after the window broke. 地板上到处都是玻璃碎片，窗户破了之后。|Synonyms: fragments, splinters, shards, pieces|Antonyms: whole, complete|noun

flinders:/ˈflɪndərz/| n. |(澳大利亚)弗林德斯山脉|A mountain range in South Australia.|We drove through the Flinders Ranges on our holiday. 我们在假期里开车穿越了弗林德斯山脉。|Synonyms: Flinders Ranges (specific)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
