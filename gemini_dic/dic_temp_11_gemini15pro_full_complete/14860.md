axonometric:/ˌæksənəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 轴测的，轴测投影的 | Relating to or designating a method of projection in which a three-dimensional object is represented by a drawing having all axes drawn to scale but with angles between them other than those in the object itself. |  Example: The architect used an axonometric projection to show the building's complex structure. 建筑师使用轴测投影来展示建筑的复杂结构。 | Example:  Axonometric drawings are commonly used in technical illustration. 轴测图通常用于技术插图。 |  Near synonyms:  isometric, dimetric, trimetric | Antonyms: perspective, oblique | adjective
