muriate:/ˈmjʊəriət/ | n. | 氯化物; 盐酸盐 | A chloride of a metal.  A term formerly used for any chloride, especially of a metal. Now usually restricted to salts of hydrochloric acid. |  The muriate of soda is common table salt. 食盐是普通的氯化钠。| synonym: chloride, hydrochloride | antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific chemical compound.  Could consider "base" as a chemical opposite to an acid salt.) | noun
