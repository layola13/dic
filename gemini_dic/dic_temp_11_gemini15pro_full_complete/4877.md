aligerous:/əˈlɪdʒərəs/
adjective
有翅膀的；翼状的；长翅膀的
Having wings; winged.
例句：
The aligerous insect flitted from flower to flower.
这只有翅膀的昆虫在花丛中飞来飞去。

The mythical creature was depicted as aligerous and fearsome.
这个神话生物被描绘成有翅膀的、可怕的样子。

近义词：winged, volant
反义词：wingless, apterous
词性：adjective

词源:
From Latin *aliger "winged," from ala "wing" + -ger "bearing, carrying."
