acculturate: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪt/ | v. | 适应文化，文化同化 | To adapt to a new culture; to assimilate or cause to assimilate to a different culture. |  Many immigrants find it difficult to acculturate to a new country. 许多移民发现很难适应一个新的国家。

The company offers training programs to help new employees acculturate quickly. 公司提供培训项目以帮助新员工快速适应文化。 | 近义词: assimilate, adapt, adjust | 反义词: isolate, alienate | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin 'ad-' (to, toward) + 'cultura' (cultivation).  The word literally means "to cultivate towards" a culture.
