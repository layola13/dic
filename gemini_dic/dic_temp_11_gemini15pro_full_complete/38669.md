词源:  From "condemn" + "-ably".  "Condemn" comes from Old French "condamner", ultimately from Latin "condemnare" (to condemn, sentence, doom), from "com-" (together, with) + "damnare" (to inflict loss upon).  "-ably" is a suffix forming adverbs meaning "in a manner that can be...".
