biome:/ˈbaɪoʊm/| n. |生物群落|A large community of plants and animals that occupies a distinct region. |例句：The tropical rainforest biome is known for its biodiversity.  热带雨林生物群落以其生物多样性而闻名。

例句：The desert biome is characterized by its arid conditions.  沙漠生物群落的特点是干旱。|近义词：ecosystem, community|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *bíos "life" + -ome. Coined by Frederic E. Clements in 1916.
