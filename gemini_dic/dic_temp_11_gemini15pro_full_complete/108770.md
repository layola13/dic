leasee:/lɛˈsiː/
n.
承租人，租户
A person or organization that holds property by lease.
The leasee is responsible for maintaining the property. 承租人有责任维护房产。

The leasee agreed to pay the rent on time.  承租人同意按时支付租金。


Synonym:
tenant, renter

Antonym:
lessor, landlord

词性:
noun
