finlike:/ˈfɪnlaɪk/
adjective
鳍状的；像鳍的
Resembling a fin, especially in shape or function.  Having the appearance or characteristics of a fin.
The creature had finlike appendages protruding from its back.  这个生物的背部长有鳍状附属物。
The kayaker used finlike paddles to propel himself through the water.  皮划艇运动员使用鳍状桨推动自己在水中前进。
Synonyms: fin-shaped, finned, flipper-like
Antonyms: finless
