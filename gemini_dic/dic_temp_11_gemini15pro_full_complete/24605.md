broil:/brɔɪl/ | v. n. | 烤，烧烤; 肉类烧烤 | To cook something by direct heat, usually on a grill or under a broiler.  |  例句：I broiled the steak for five minutes on each side. 我把牛排两面各烤了五分钟。

例句：We're going to broil some burgers for dinner. 我们晚餐要烤一些汉堡。

近义词：grill, barbecue, roast | 反义词：freeze, chill | 词性：动词，名词
