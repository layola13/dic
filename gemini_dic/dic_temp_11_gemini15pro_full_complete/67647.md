extrinsicalness:/ˌɛkstrɪnˈzɪkəlnəs/| n.|外在性，非固有性|The state or quality of being extrinsic; external origin or source.|The extrinsicalness of the reward system demotivated the employees, as they felt their work wasn't valued intrinsically.  这种外在的奖励制度降低了员工的积极性，因为他们觉得自己的工作本身没有得到重视。|Synonyms: externality, extraneousness, outsideness|Antonyms: intrinsicalness, inherence, innateness|noun

extrinsic:/ɛkˈstrɪnzɪk/| adj.|外在的，非固有的|Not part of the essential nature of someone or something; coming or operating from outside.|The extrinsic motivation of money is often not enough to sustain long-term happiness. 金钱这种外在的激励通常不足以维持长久的幸福。|Synonyms: external, outward, outer|Antonyms: intrinsic, inherent, innate|adjective
