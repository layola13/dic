nonreciprocity:/ˌnɒnˌresɪˈprɒsɪti/ | n. | 非互惠性，单向性 | Lack of reciprocity; the state of not being reciprocal or mutual.  A relationship or interaction where actions or feelings are not returned or reciprocated in kind. |  The nonreciprocity of their relationship was starting to bother her.  他们的关系不对等开始困扰她。 |  The trade policy suffered from nonreciprocity, with one country imposing tariffs while the other did not.  这项贸易政策受到不互惠的影响，一个国家征收关税，而另一个国家没有。 |  asymmetry, imbalance, inequality, one-sidedness | reciprocity, mutuality, exchange | noun
