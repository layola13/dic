outstung:/ˌaʊtˈstʌŋ/
verb
(outsting的过去式和过去分词) 刺痛胜过；蜇得更厉害
outstung someone with a witty retort. 用机智的反驳胜过某人。
He outstung his opponent in the debate.  他在辩论中驳倒了对手。
近义词: outdo, surpass, defeat, beat, triumph over
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb (动词)

词源: out- + sting.  "out-" prefix 表示超过，胜过. "sting" 指刺痛，蜇.
