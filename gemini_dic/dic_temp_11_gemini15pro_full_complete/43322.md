crammable:/ˈkræməbl/
adjective
可塞满的；能填鸭式学习的
Able to be crammed (filled tightly) or suitable for cramming (intense study).
This bag is very crammable, I can fit a lot of clothes in it.  这个包很能装，我可以装很多衣服进去。
He found the history course very crammable, managing to memorize a vast amount of information the night before the exam. 他发现历史课很适合突击学习，考试前一天晚上记住了大量的信息。
Synonyms: packable, stuffable, condensable
Antonyms: spacious, roomy
词性: 形容词
