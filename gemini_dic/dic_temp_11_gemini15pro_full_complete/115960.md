marron:/ˈmærɒn/ (UK), /məˈrɑːn/ (US)| n. adj.|栗色; 栗子; (法语)逃亡的黑奴|A reddish-brown colour, like that of a chestnut.  A large, edible chestnut.  (historical) A runaway slave (especially in French colonial areas).|例句：She wore a marron dress. 她穿着一件栗色连衣裙。

The marron glacés were delicious. 栗子糖真好吃。
