honorary:/ˈɒnərəri/ (UK) /ˈɑːnəreri/ (US)| adj.|荣誉的，名誉的|Conferred as an honor, without the usual requirements or duties.|She received an honorary doctorate from Harvard University. 她获得了哈佛大学的荣誉博士学位。|Example: He was given an honorary title. 他被授予了一个荣誉头衔。|Synonyms: titular, nominal, symbolic|Antonyms: earned, merited|adjective

honorary:/ˈɒnərəri/ (UK) /ˈɑːnəreri/ (US)| n.|荣誉获得者|A person who receives an honorary title or position.|The honorary members were invited to the gala. 荣誉会员被邀请参加了盛会。|Example: She is an honorary of the Royal Academy of Arts. 她是皇家艺术学院的荣誉会员。|Synonyms: recipient, awardee|Antonyms: - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *honorarius*, from Latin honor (“honor, office”).
