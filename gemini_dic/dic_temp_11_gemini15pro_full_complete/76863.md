gangliform:/ˈɡæŋɡlɪfɔːrm/| adj.|神经节状的，似神经节的|Resembling a ganglion in form. Having the form of a ganglion; arranged in a mass, like a ganglion.|The tumor was gangliform in appearance.|近义词：ganglionar, ganglioid|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From  gangli(o)-  +‎ -form.  Gangli(o)- comes from  ganglion, from Greek γάγγλιον (gánglion, “knot, tumor under the skin, nerve ganglion”). -form comes from Latin -formis, from forma (“form, shape”).
