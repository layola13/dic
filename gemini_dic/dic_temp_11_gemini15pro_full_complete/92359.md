hypochlorhydric:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌklɔːrˈhaɪdrɪk/
adjective
次氯酸缺乏的；胃酸过少的
deficient in hydrochloric acid (especially in the gastric juice)
The patient's hypochlorhydric condition made it difficult for him to digest proteins. 这位病人胃酸过少，难以消化蛋白质。
The doctor prescribed medication to address the patient's hypochlorhydric symptoms. 医生开了药来解决病人胃酸过少的症状。
近义词: achlorhydric
反义词: hyperchlorhydric


ETYMOLOGY->
From hypo- (meaning "under" or "deficient") + chlor- (referring to chlorine or chloride) + hydr- (referring to water or hydrogen) + -ic (forming an adjective).
