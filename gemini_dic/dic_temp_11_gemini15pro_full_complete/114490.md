malacopterygii:/ˌmæləˌkɒptəˈrɪdʒiːaɪ/ /ˌmæləˌkɑːptəˈrɪdʒiˌaɪ/
noun
辐鳍鱼纲
A large and diverse group of ray-finned fishes, characterized by having soft fin rays.  This taxonomic class includes the majority of bony fishes.
The salmon, trout, and tuna are all members of the Malacopterygii.  鲑鱼、鳟鱼和金枪鱼都是辐鳍鱼纲的成员。
近义词: ray-finned fishes (more general)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could contrast with Chondrichthyes - cartilaginous fishes)
词性: n
