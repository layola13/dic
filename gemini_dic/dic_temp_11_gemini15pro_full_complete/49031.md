degreasing:/diːˈɡriːsɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|脱脂，除油|We are degreasing the engine parts before reassembly. 我们正在重新组装发动机部件之前对其进行脱脂。|
degreasing:/diːˈɡriːsɪŋ/
adjective|脱脂的，去油的|Use a degreasing solvent to clean the surface. 使用脱脂溶剂清洁表面。|


Etymology:  From de- + grease + -ing.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse." "Grease" refers to oily or fatty matter. "-ing" forms the present participle of the verb or creates an adjective.

Synonyms: cleaning, scouring, washing, purging
Antonyms: greasing, oiling, lubricating
