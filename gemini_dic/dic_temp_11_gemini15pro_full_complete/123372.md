monomanias:/ˌmɒnəˈmeɪniəz/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmeɪniəz/
noun
偏执狂；狂热；着魔
An exaggerated or obsessive enthusiasm for or preoccupation with one thing.
His monomania for cleanliness bordered on the pathological. 他对清洁的狂热近乎病态。
obsessions, fixations, passions, infatuations, preoccupations
disinterest, apathy
n.
