hylopathism:/ˈhaɪləˌpæθɪzəm/ | n. |万物有灵论；泛灵论|The belief that all matter, especially plant life, has sensitivity or soul.  It attributes life and feeling to inanimate objects. |例句：Some indigenous cultures practice a form of hylopathism, believing that trees and rocks possess spirits.  一些土著文化实行一种万物有灵论，相信树木和岩石拥有灵魂。

例句：Hylopathism is a philosophical concept that can be difficult to grasp for those raised in a more materialistic worldview. 对于那些在更唯物的世界观中长大的人来说，万物有灵论是一个难以理解的哲学概念。|近义词：animism, panpsychism |反义词: materialism |词性: noun
