diplanar:/daɪˈpleɪnər/| adj.|双平面的，共面的|Having two planes; lying or consisting in two planes.  Relating to or situated in two planes.|The diplanar facets of the crystal reflected the light beautifully.  晶体的双平面刻面美丽地反射着光线。|近义词:coplanar|反义词:non-coplanar|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From "di-" (two) + "planar" (relating to a plane).  "Planar" ultimately derives from the Latin "planus" (flat, level).
