The architect's antimodern sensibilities were reflected in his preference for classical designs.  这位建筑师的反现代主义情感体现在他对古典设计的偏爱上。| 近义词：traditional, conservative, reactionary | 反义词：modern, modernist, contemporary | 词性: adjective
