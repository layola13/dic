marxists:/ˈmɑːksɪsts/| n.|马克思主义者|Followers of the political and economic theories of Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels.  These theories advocate for a classless society and the abolition of private property.|Many marxists believe that capitalism is inherently exploitative.|近义词：communists, socialists|反义词:capitalists|Noun
