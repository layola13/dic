artinesses:/ˈɑːrtinəsɪz/
noun
做作; 装腔作势
the quality or state of being arty, often in an affected or superficial way.  A deliberate display of artistic qualities, often seen as pretentious.
Her artinesses were evident in her bohemian clothing and constant pronouncements on modern art.  她穿着波西米亚风格的衣服，不断地对现代艺术发表高论，她的装腔作势显而易见。
affectations, pretensions, airs, posturing
genuineness, sincerity, naturalness
