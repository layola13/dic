mumpses:/ˈmʌmpsɪz/| n. |腮腺炎|An infectious disease characterized by swelling of the salivary glands, especially the parotid glands. It is caused by a virus.  This is the plural form of "mumps," which is already considered plural.  Using "mumpses" is extremely rare and generally considered incorrect.  |例句：He had the mumps as a child. 他小时候得过腮腺炎。(This is the standard usage. Avoid using "mumpses.")


|近义词：parotitis|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease)|词性: noun
