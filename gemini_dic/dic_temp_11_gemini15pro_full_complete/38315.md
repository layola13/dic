concealingly:/kənˈsiːlɪŋli/
adverb
隐蔽地，偷偷摸摸地
in a way that hides something
She smiled concealingly, hiding her true feelings. 她偷偷地笑了笑，掩饰着她的真实感受。
He glanced concealingly at the note in his hand. 他偷偷地瞥了一眼手中的纸条。
近义词:secretly, furtively, stealthily, covertly
反义词:openly, publicly, visibly
词性:adverb

conceal:/kənˈsiːl/
verb
隐藏，隐瞒
to hide something carefully
The trees concealed the house from view. 树木遮蔽了房子，使之无法看到。
He tried to conceal his disappointment. 他试图掩饰他的失望。
近义词:hide, disguise, mask, obscure
反义词:reveal, expose, show, uncover
词性:verb

词源:
From Late Latin *concelare, from Latin con- + celare "to hide".
