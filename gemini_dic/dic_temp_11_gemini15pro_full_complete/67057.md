explication:/ˌɛksplɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 解释，说明，阐释 | An explication is a detailed explanation or interpretation of a text, especially a literary one. | The professor's explication of the poem helped the students understand its complex themes. 教授对这首诗的讲解帮助学生理解了其复杂的主题。

The student provided a thorough explication of the philosophical concepts in the text. 学生对文本中的哲学概念进行了详尽的解释。| synonyms: explanation, interpretation, clarification, elucidation, analysis | antonyms: obfuscation, confusion, mystification | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin explicatio, from Latin explicare "to unfold, explain," from ex- "out" + plicare "to fold."
