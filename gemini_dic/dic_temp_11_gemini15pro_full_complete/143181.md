outcoaching: /ˌaʊtˈkoʊtʃɪŋ/
verb
在教练方面胜过
surpassing someone in coaching ability, typically in a sporting context.
Example: The team won because their coach outcoached the opposing team's coach.  这个队伍赢了是因为他们的教练在执教方面胜过了对方队伍的教练。

Example:  He outcoached his rival in the championship game. 他在冠军赛中在教练方面胜过了他的对手。

近义词: outmaneuvering, outsmarted (in a coaching context)
反义词: being outcoached
词性: verb
