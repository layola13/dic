ausonian:/ɔːˈsoʊniən/
noun
奥索尼亚人；意大利南部人
A native or inhabitant of Ausonia, an ancient name for Italy, especially its southern part.
The ausonians were known for their love of music and poetry. 奥索尼亚人以热爱音乐和诗歌而闻名。

反义词:None
