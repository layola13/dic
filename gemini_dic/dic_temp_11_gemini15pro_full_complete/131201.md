nonburnable:/ˌnɒnˈbɜːrnəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnˈbɜːrnəbl/ | adj. | 不可燃的；不易燃的 | Not capable of being burned or easily burned. | The building materials used were all nonburnable.  这栋建筑使用的材料都是不可燃的。 |  The safe is made of nonburnable materials. 这个保险箱是用不可燃材料制成的。 | 近义词: fireproof, incombustible, nonflammable | 反义词: flammable, combustible, burnable | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + burnable (adjective meaning "capable of being burned")
