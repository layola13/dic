ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  not, absence of  (Latin origin)
contemptuous (adjective): showing contempt; scornful (Latin origin - contemptus, past participle of contemnere "to scorn, despise")
-ness (suffix): forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition
