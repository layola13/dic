marler:/ˈmɑːrlər/ | n. | 马勒；泥瓦匠；泥水匠 | A person who works with marl (a type of soil).  A worker who spreads or applies marl to land. | The marler skillfully plastered the walls with the mixture. 泥瓦匠熟练地用混合物抹墙。| synonyms: bricklayer, mason, plasterer | antonyms: none | noun

marler:/ˈmɑːrlər/ | n. | 打架者；争吵者；爱吵架的人 | Someone who frequently engages in quarrels or fights. | He's known as a bit of a marler in the pub. 他在酒吧里是出了名的爱吵架的人。| synonyms: fighter, brawler, quarreler, arguer | antonyms: peacemaker, mediator | noun
