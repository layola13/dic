designating:/ˈdɛzɪɡˌneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 指定的，指派的 |  serving to indicate or specify; naming or pointing out; appointing | The mayor designating Smith as his successor. 市长指定史密斯为他的继任者。 |近义词: appointing, naming, nominating, assigning | 反义词: unspecific, general | 词性: adjective

designate:/ˈdɛzɪɡneɪt/ | vt. | 指定，指派；标明 | to indicate, point out; to appoint (someone) to a specified position |  The area was designated a national park.  这个地区被指定为国家公园。| 近义词: appoint, name, nominate, assign | 反义词: dismiss, remove | 词性: verb


Etymology:
From Late Latin *designatus, past participle of designare (“to mark out, designate”), from de- + signare (“to mark”), from signum (“mark, sign”).
