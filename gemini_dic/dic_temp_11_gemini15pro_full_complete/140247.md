offprinting:/ˈɔːfˌprɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 抽印本，单行本 | A separately printed copy of an article that originally appeared in a larger publication, such as a magazine or journal. |  Example: The author requested offprints of her article to distribute to colleagues. 作者要求提供她文章的抽印本分发给同事。

Example: The journal provided offprints to the authors for a small fee. 该期刊以少量费用向作者提供抽印本。 | Synonym: reprint, separate | Antonym: original publication | noun
