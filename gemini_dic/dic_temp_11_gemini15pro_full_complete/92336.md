hypo-allergenic:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌælərˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
低过敏性的；抗过敏的
Relatively unlikely to cause an allergic reaction.  Designed to reduce or minimize the possibility of an allergic reaction.
This lotion is hypo-allergenic and suitable for sensitive skin.  这款乳液是低过敏性的，适合敏感肌肤。
I always buy hypo-allergenic makeup because my skin is so reactive. 我总是买低过敏性化妆品，因为我的皮肤很容易过敏。
近义词:non-allergenic, sensitivity-free
反义词:allergenic
词性:adjective
