intertraded:/ˌɪntərˈtreɪdɪd/
verb
互相贸易，相互交易
engaged in trade with each other.
例句：The two countries have intertraded for centuries. 这两个国家已经互相贸易了几个世纪。

例句：The companies intertraded goods and services. 这些公司互相交易商品和服务。
近义词：traded mutually, exchanged goods,  conducted commerce
反义词：embargoed, boycotted
词性：v.
