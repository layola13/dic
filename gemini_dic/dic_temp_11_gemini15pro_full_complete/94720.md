impropriate:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət/ (US) /ˌɪnəˈprəʊpriət/ (UK)| adj.|不合适的，不恰当的|Not suitable or proper in the circumstances.|Wearing a bikini to a funeral is inappropriate. 穿比基尼参加葬礼是不合适的。|It is inappropriate to shout in a library. 在图书馆里大声喊叫是不合适的。|近义词：unsuitable, improper, unfit, unbecoming|反义词：appropriate, suitable, proper, fitting|词性:形容词

词源:
From in- (not) + appropriate.  The word "appropriate" comes from the Latin appropriatus, past participle of appropriare "to make one's own," from ad- "to" + proprius "one's own, special."
