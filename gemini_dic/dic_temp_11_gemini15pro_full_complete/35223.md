clueless:/ˈkluːləs/| adj.| 无能的，笨拙的；一窍不通的，毫无头绪的|Having no knowledge, understanding, or ability; totally bewildered.|例句：I'm completely clueless about cars. 我对汽车一窍不通。|近义词：ignorant, unaware, bewildered, lost, confused|反义词：knowledgeable, aware, informed, savvy, astute|词性:形容词
