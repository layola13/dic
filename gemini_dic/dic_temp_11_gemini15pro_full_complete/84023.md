half-educated:/ˌhæf ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd/
|adj.|半吊子; 一知半解的; 受过一点教育的|Having some education but not enough to be considered well-educated.  Lacking depth or thoroughness in knowledge or understanding.|He made some half-educated guesses about the history of the area. 他对该地区的历史做了一些一知半解的猜测。|semi-educated, poorly educated, partially educated|well-educated, knowledgeable, erudite|adjective
