north-westerly: /ˌnɔːθˈwɛstərli/ /ˌnɔːrθˈwɛstərli/
adjective
西北的；来自西北的
Coming from the northwest. The wind was north-westerly. 风来自西北方向。
例句：
A north-westerly wind was blowing. 西北风正在吹。
The ship sailed in a north-westerly direction. 船向西北方向航行。

近义词：northwestern
反义词：south-easterly

词性: adjective
