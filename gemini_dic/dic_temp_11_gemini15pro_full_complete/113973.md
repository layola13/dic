magnet:/ˈmæɡnɪt/| n. |磁铁，磁石；有吸引力的人或物|A piece of iron or other material that has its component atoms so ordered that the material exhibits properties of magnetism, such as attracting other iron-containing objects.|The metal shavings were drawn to the magnet. 金属屑被磁铁吸了过去。|synonyms: lodestone, electromagnet|antonyms: non-magnet|noun
