ornamentality:/ˌɔːrnəmɛnˈtælɪti/
n.|装饰性，观赏性|The quality of being ornamental; decoration.  The state or quality of serving as an ornament.|The architect emphasized the building's ornamentality over its functionality.  这位建筑师强调建筑的装饰性而非其功能性。|The ornamentality of the dress made it unsuitable for everyday wear. 这件衣服的装饰性使其不适合日常穿着。|近义词：decoration, embellishment, adornment|反义词：plainness, simplicity|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *ōrnāmentālitās, from Latin ōrnāmentum (“ornament”).
