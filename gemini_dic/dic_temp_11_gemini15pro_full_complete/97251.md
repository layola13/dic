inflection:/ɪnˈflɛkʃən/ (US), /ɪnˈflɛkʃn/ (UK)| n.|变形，屈折变化，音调变化|A change in the form of a word (typically the ending) to express a grammatical function or attribute such as tense, mood, person, number, case, and gender.|The inflection of the verb "to be" changes depending on the subject.|近义词：modulation, variation, conjugation, declension|反义词: uniformity|词性: noun

inflect:/ɪnˈflɛkt/ (US), /ɪnˈflɛkt/ (UK)| v.|使变形，使屈折变化，改变音调|Change the form of (a word) to express a grammatical function or attribute, typically tense, mood, person, number, case, and gender.|In English, we inflect verbs to show tense.|近义词：modulate, vary, conjugate, decline|反义词: maintain|词性: verb
