guacho:/ˈɡwɑːtʃoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡaʊtʃoʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.| 高乔人，流浪汉；孤儿；勇敢的，独立的 |A South American cowboy, typically of mixed Spanish and Indian descent, known for their horsemanship and independence. Can also refer to an orphan or someone who is alone and independent.  In some regions, it can also be used as an adjective meaning brave or independent. |He lived the life of a gaucho, roaming the pampas on horseback. 他过着高乔人的生活，骑着马在潘帕斯草原上漫游。|近义词：cowboy, horseman, orphan, independent, brave |反义词: dependent, cowardly|名词, 形容词
