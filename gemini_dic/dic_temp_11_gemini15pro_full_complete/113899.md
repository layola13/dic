madrigal:/ˈmædrɪɡəl/ | n. | 牧歌; 合唱曲; 小夜曲 | A part-song for several voices, especially one of the Renaissance period, typically unaccompanied and arranged in elaborate counterpoint. |  例句：The choir sang a beautiful madrigal. 合唱团唱了一首优美的牧歌。

例句：He composed several madrigals for the queen's birthday. 他为女王的生日创作了几首牧歌。 | 近义词：song, part-song, glee, air, chanson | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps cacophony if referring to the sound) | 词性:noun
