morselling:/ˈmɔːrsəlɪŋ/ | n. |分割，小块，少量|The act of dividing something into small pieces or portions. |She enjoyed morselling her food into tiny bites. 她喜欢把食物切成小块吃。| The artist was morselling clay for his next sculpture. 艺术家正在把黏土分割成小块，用于他的下一个雕塑作品。|近义词：dividing, fragmenting, portioning|反义词：combining, uniting, whole|词性:noun

词源: From the noun "morsel" (a small piece or amount of food), which comes from Old French "morcel" (a bite, mouthful), ultimately from Vulgar Latin *morsicare (to bite), from Latin mordere (to bite).
