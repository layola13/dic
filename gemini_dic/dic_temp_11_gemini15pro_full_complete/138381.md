notopodium:/ˌnoʊtəˈpoʊdiəm/| n. |疣足的背肢|The dorsal lobe of a parapodium in polychaete worms.  多毛类环节动物疣足的背肢|The bristle worm uses its notopodia for respiration and locomotion. 毛 bristle虫利用它的背肢进行呼吸和运动。|Synonyms: dorsal parapodium|Antonyms: neuropodium|noun
