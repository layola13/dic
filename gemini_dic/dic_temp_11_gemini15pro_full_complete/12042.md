articulateness:/ɑːrˈtɪkjələtnəs/| n.|表达清晰，清晰度|The articulateness of her speech impressed everyone. 她讲话的清晰度给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|Synonyms: eloquence, fluency, clarity, precision|Antonyms: inarticulateness, vagueness, ambiguity|noun
