amido:/əˈmiːdoʊ/ /ˈæmɪdoʊ/| adj. n.|酰胺的，酰氨基的；酰胺|An amido group is a functional group that contains a carbonyl group linked to a nitrogen atom.  酰氨基是一个包含与氮原子连接的羰基的官能团。|This compound contains an amido group.  这个化合物包含一个酰氨基。|近义词: carbamoyl (when attached to an amino group) |反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun

amido:/əˈmiːdoʊ/ /ˈæmɪdoʊ/| n.|酰胺|An amido is an organic compound derived from carboxylic acids or ammonia by replacement of the hydroxyl of an acid by the amino group. 酰胺是一种由羧酸或氨通过用氨基取代酸的羟基而衍生的有机化合物。|Acetamide is a simple amido. 乙酰胺是一种简单的酰胺。|近义词: acid amide|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
