buoying:/ˈbɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 使人振奋的，令人鼓舞的 | making someone feel more cheerful and hopeful | The good news was buoying.  好消息令人振奋。|  encouraging, uplifting, heartening | depressing, disheartening | adjective

buoy:/bɔɪ/ | n. | 浮标，航标 | a floating object anchored in water to mark a channel or warning of danger |  The ship steered clear of the buoy. 船避开了浮标。| marker, float |  N/A | noun

buoy:/bɔɪ/ | v. | 使浮起，使振作，支撑 | to keep afloat or support something, or to make someone feel more cheerful | The life jacket buoyed her up. 救生衣使她浮了起来。| support, uplift, encourage | depress, sink | verb
