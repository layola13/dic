breathability:/ˌbriːðəˈbɪləti/
noun
透气性
The ability of a fabric or material to allow air to pass through it.
This fabric offers excellent breathability, keeping you cool and comfortable. 这种面料透气性极佳，让您保持凉爽舒适。
porosity, permeability
impermeability, airtightness
