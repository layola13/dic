curvilinearity: /ˌkɜːrvɪlɪˈniːərɪti/ /ˌkɝːvɪlɪˈniːɛrɪti/ | n. | 曲线性；曲线状态 |The state or quality of being curvilinear; consisting of or bounded by curved lines. | The architect embraced curvilinearity in his design, incorporating flowing lines and rounded shapes.  这位建筑师在他的设计中采用了曲线性，融入了流畅的线条和圆润的形状。| synonym: curvature, crookedness | antonym: linearity, straightness | noun

curvilinear: /ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪniər/ /ˌkɝːvɪlɪˈniːɚ/ | adj. | 曲线的；弯曲的 |  Formed, bounded, or characterized by curved lines. | The artist's painting featured bold, curvilinear forms.  这位艺术家的画作以大胆的曲线形状为特色。| synonym: curved, bending, sinuous | antonym: linear, straight | adjective
