what:/wɒt/(British) /wʌt/(American) |pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| Synonyms: which, whatever | Antonyms:  N/A (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching? = 你在看什么？
What is he cooking? = 他在做什么菜？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten for lunch? = 她午饭吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before you started this job? = 你开始这份工作之前一直在做什么？
What had he been studying before he changed his major? = 他换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if we don't finish this project? = 如果我们不完成这个项目会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? = 到明年夏天他们将建成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习了什么多久？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我希望我知道他在想什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 这个问题正在被如何处理？


Interrogative Sentences:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？


Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you're saying. = 她不明白你在说什么。

PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the heck? = 搞什么鬼！/怎么回事？
what if = 如果...会怎么样？
what for = 为了什么？
no matter what = 无论如何
