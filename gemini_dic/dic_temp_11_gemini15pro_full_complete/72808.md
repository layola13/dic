folded-up:/ˌfōl.dɪdˈʌp/ | adj. | 折叠起来的 |  Something that is folded-up has been bent over on itself one or more times.  It implies a smaller, more compact state than its unfolded form. | I found the map folded-up in the glove compartment. 我在杂物箱里找到了折叠起来的地图。 | The folded-up laundry sat in the basket. 折叠好的衣物放在篮子里。 | compact, folded | unfolded, open | adjective
