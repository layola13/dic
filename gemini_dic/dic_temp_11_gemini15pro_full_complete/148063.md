oversanguine:/ˌoʊvərˈsæŋɡwɪn/| adj.|过于乐观的，过于自信的|Excessively confident; unduly optimistic; inclined to expect good outcomes.|例句：He was oversanguine about the company's prospects. 他对公司的前景过于乐观。|近义词：optimistic, bullish, confident, hopeful|反义词：pessimistic, gloomy, despondent, realistic|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *oversanguineus* "having too much blood," from *over-* "over" + *sanguineus* "of blood"
