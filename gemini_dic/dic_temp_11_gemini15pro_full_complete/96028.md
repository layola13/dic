indamaged:/ɪnˈdæmɪdʒd/
adjective
未损坏的，完好的
not damaged
The package arrived undamaged. 包裹完好无损地到达了。
近义词:undamaged, intact, unhurt, unscathed
反义词:damaged, broken, harmed, impaired
词性:adjective


词源: "in-" (not) + "damaged" (harmed or injured)
