courtepy: /ˈkɜːrtəsi/ (US), /ˈkɜːtɪsi/ (UK) | n. | 礼貌；客气；殷勤 | Politeness and respect; an act of politeness or respect. |  He treated her with great courtesy. 他对她非常有礼貌。| Synonyms: politeness, civility, respect, graciousness | Antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, disrespect, discourtesy | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "corteisie," meaning "courtesy, courtly behavior," derived from "cort" meaning "court," ultimately from Latin "cohors" meaning "enclosed space, cohort."
