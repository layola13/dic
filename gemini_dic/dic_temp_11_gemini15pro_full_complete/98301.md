insidiously:/ɪnˈsɪdiəsli/
adverb
阴险地；潜伏地；不知不觉地
in a gradual, subtle way, but with harmful effects.
The disease spreads insidiously. 这种疾病潜伏地传播。
insidiously | adverb | 阴险地；潜伏地；不知不觉地 |  The poison worked insidiously, slowly weakening him over months. | 毒药潜伏地起作用，在几个月内逐渐削弱了他。
Near synonyms: stealthily, subtly, cunningly, craftily, surreptitiously
Opposite: openly, overtly, blatantly
