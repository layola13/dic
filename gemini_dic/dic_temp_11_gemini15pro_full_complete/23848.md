breadless:/ˈbredlɪs/
adjective
无面包的；没有面包吃的；缺面包的
without bread; lacking bread.
The breadless family struggled to survive the harsh winter. 这户没有面包的人家艰难地熬过了严冬。
The refugees were often breadless and desperate. 难民们经常缺衣少食，绝望无助。

近义词:impoverished, destitute, needy
反义词:wealthy, affluent, prosperous
词性:adjective
