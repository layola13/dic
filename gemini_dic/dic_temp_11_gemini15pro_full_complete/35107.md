clotting:/ˈklɑːtɪŋ/ /ˈklɒtɪŋ/| n.|凝血，凝结|The clotting of blood is essential to stop bleeding. 血液凝固对于止血至关重要。|The doctor gave him medication to prevent blood clotting. 医生给他开了药以防止血液凝结。|近义词：coagulation, thickening|反义词:thinning, dissolving|词性:名词

clotting:/ˈklɑːtɪŋ/ /ˈklɒtɪŋ/| adj. |凝血的|Clotting factors are essential for blood coagulation. 凝血因子对血液凝固至关重要。|He has a clotting disorder that requires regular monitoring. 他有凝血障碍，需要定期监测。|近义词：coagulant, coagulative|反义词:anticoagulant|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *clotten, from Old English clott (“clod, lump”), from Proto-Germanic *klut- (“lump, ball”), from Proto-Indo-European *glew- (“to stick, adhere”). Cognate with Dutch klot (“lump, block”), German Klotz (“block, log”), dialectal German Klut (“lump, clod”). Related to clot.
