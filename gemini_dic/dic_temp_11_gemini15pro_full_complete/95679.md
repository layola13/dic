inconcluding:/ˌɪnkənˈkluːdɪŋ/
adjective
无结果的，不确定的
not leading to a definite result or conclusion
The investigation was inconclusive. 调查没有结果。
Synonyms:  indecisive,  uncertain,  ambiguous, open-ended
Antonyms: conclusive, decisive, definitive
词性: 形容词
