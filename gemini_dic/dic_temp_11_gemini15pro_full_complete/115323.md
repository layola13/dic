mannikins:/ˈmænɪkɪnz/ | n. |人体模型；时装模特儿；小人；侏儒|A life-size anatomical model of the human body, typically used for teaching purposes or displaying clothes; a small model of a human figure. |The fashion designer draped the fabric over the mannikins to see how it would fall. 时装设计师把布料披在人体模型上，看看它会怎么垂下来。

The store window displayed several mannikins dressed in the latest fashions.  商店橱窗里展示了几个穿着最新款时装的人体模型。|Synonyms: dummy, model, figure, mannequin |Antonyms: giant | Noun
