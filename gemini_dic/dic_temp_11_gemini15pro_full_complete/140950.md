one-shots:/ˈwʌn ˌʃɑːts/ | n. | 单发作品，一次性作品，单次尝试 |  A story, comic, or other creative work that is self-contained and not part of a series or ongoing narrative.  | This artist is known for their beautiful one-shots.  这位艺术家以其精美的单幅作品而闻名。 |  One-shot comics are often a good way for new creators to showcase their talent. 单幅漫画通常是新创作者展示才华的好方法。 |  standalone, independent, single issue  | series, sequel, ongoing | noun
