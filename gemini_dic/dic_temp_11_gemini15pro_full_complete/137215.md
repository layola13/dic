nonsuppressed:/ˌnɒnsəˈprɛst/ | adj. | 未抑制的，未被压制的 | Not suppressed; allowed to be expressed or operate freely. | Example: Nonsuppressed coughing can spread germs. 未抑制的咳嗽会传播病菌。 | Example: The nonsuppressed data showed the true extent of the problem. 未经抑制的数据显示了问题的真实程度。 | 近义词: unsuppressed, unrestrained, unchecked | 反义词: suppressed, restrained, controlled | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + suppressed, the past participle of suppress.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Suppress" comes from the Latin "suppressus," past participle of "supprimere," meaning "to press down, restrain, keep back."
