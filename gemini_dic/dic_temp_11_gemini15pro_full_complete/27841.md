canonically:/kəˈnɒnɪkli/| adv.|经典地，规范地，正统地|According to or ordered by canon law.  Following or in agreement with accepted, traditional, or authoritative rules or practices.|The book is canonically divided into three sections. 这本书按照经典被分为三个部分。|例句：The issue was canonically resolved by the council.  这个问题由委员会依照惯例解决了。|近义词：traditionally, authoritatively, orthodoxly, officially|反义词：unconventionally, heterodoxly, apocryphally|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *canonicus* (“relating to a rule”), from Ancient Greek *κανών* (*kanṓn*) (“rule, standard, list”), from *κάννα* (*kánna*) (“reed”), because reeds were used as measuring rods.
