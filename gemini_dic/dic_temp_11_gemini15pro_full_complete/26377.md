by-line:/ˈbaɪlaɪn/| n.|署名行，作者署名|A line at the beginning of a newspaper or magazine article that gives the writer's name.|The by-line read "By John Smith".|署名写着“约翰·史密斯撰写”。|attribution, credit|anonymity|noun

by-line:/ˈbaɪlaɪn/| n.|（广播电视节目的）插播广告语|A brief announcement identifying a sponsor of a program.|The by-line announced the sponsor of the show.|广告语宣布了节目的赞助商。|commercial, advertisement| |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "by," meaning "near" or "beside," + "line," referring to the line of text.  The term originally referred to a secondary line below a headline, but evolved to specifically mean the line attributing authorship.
