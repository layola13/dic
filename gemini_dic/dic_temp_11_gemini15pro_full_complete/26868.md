cajolery:/kəˈdʒəʊləri/ /kəˈdʒoʊləri/
n.|哄骗；劝诱；甜言蜜语|The salesman used cajolery to persuade her to buy the expensive car.  售货员用甜言蜜语劝她买了那辆昂贵的汽车。|例句：He tried to cajole her into going to the party with him. 他试图哄骗她和他一起去参加聚会。|近义词：persuasion, coaxing, flattery, blandishment|反义词：coercion, force, intimidation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle French *cajolerie*, from *cajoler* "to chatter like a jay," from Old French *gaioler*, of imitative origin.
