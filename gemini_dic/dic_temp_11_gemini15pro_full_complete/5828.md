amateurs:/ˈæmətərz/ (US), /ˈæmətəz/ (UK)| n.| 业余爱好者；外行|A person who engages in a pursuit, especially a sport, on an unpaid rather than a professional basis.|例句：The tournament is open to both amateurs and professionals. 这场锦标赛对业余选手和职业选手都开放。

例句：He's an amateur photographer, but his pictures are amazing. 他是一位业余摄影师，但他的照片令人惊叹。|近义词：nonprofessional, dilettante, novice, beginner|反义词：professional, expert, connoisseur|词性: 名词
