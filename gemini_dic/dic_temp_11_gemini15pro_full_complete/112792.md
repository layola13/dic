lubricous:/ˈluːbrɪkəs/| adj.|油滑的，光滑的；狡猾的，不诚实的|Having a slippery or smooth quality; also, shifty or tricky.|The politician's lubricous answers made it hard to trust him. 这位政客油嘴滑舌的回答让人难以相信他。|近义词：slippery, oily, smooth, evasive, shifty|反义词：rough, coarse, honest, straightforward|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *lubricus*, from *lubricāre* "to make smooth or slippery," related to *labor* "to slip, glide."
