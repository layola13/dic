fiestas:/fiˈɛstəz/
n.
festivals; celebrations
A time of public celebration, especially a religious festival.
The town held fiestas all summer long.  小镇整个夏天都在举行节日庆典。
synonyms: celebrations, festivals, holidays, fetes
antonyms: workday, mourning
noun
