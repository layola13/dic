lader:/ˈlædər/| n.|梯子；阶梯；途径；（紧身裤袜或长筒袜的）滑丝|A tool with two long pieces of wood or metal joined by short steps across them, used for climbing up and down walls, etc.|He climbed up the ladder to reach the roof. 他爬上梯子去够屋顶。|近义词：steps, staircase|反义词: descent, decline|noun
