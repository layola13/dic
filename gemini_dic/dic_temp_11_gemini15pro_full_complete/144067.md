outroguing: /ˌaʊtrəˈɡɪŋ/
adjective
超越的，胜过的，比...更有趣的
surpassing or exceeding others in intrigue or interest; more intriguing than.  This implies a comparative sense, meaning it's more intriguing than something else.  It's not simply "intriguing," but actively *out*-intriguing.  This word is not standard English and likely a neologism formed by adding the prefix "out-" to "intriguing."
She told an outroguing story about her travels in Nepal. 她讲述了一个关于她在尼泊尔旅行的引人入胜的故事，比其他故事都更有趣。
His new novel is outroguing his previous works. 他的新小说比他以前的作品更引人入胜。
近义词: captivating, fascinating, compelling, absorbing, engrossing
反义词: boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting, monotonous
词性: adjective
