nott:/nɒt/ | adv. | 不，不是 |  Used to form the negative of verbs, adjectives, and adverbs.  | It's not raining.  没有下雨。 | 近义词: no, ain't (informal) | 反义词: yes | 词性: 副词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I do not like spinach. = 我不喜欢菠菜。
She does not work here anymore. = 她不再在这里工作了。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
They are not playing outside. = 他们没有在外面玩。
He is not sleeping right now. = 他现在没在睡觉。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
We have not seen that movie yet. = 我们还没有看过那部电影。
She has not finished her homework. = 她还没有完成作业。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have not been feeling well lately. = 我最近感觉不太好。
They have not been studying hard enough. = 他们学习不够努力。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He did not go to the party. = 他没去参加派对。
We did not see her yesterday. = 我们昨天没见到她。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was not working when you called. = 你打电话时我没有工作。
They were not listening to the teacher. = 他们没有听老师讲课。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
She had not eaten before the meeting. = 会议前她没吃东西。
They had not arrived when we left. = 我们离开时他们还没到。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had not been sleeping well before the exam. = 考试前他一直睡不好。
We had not been expecting such a large crowd. = 我们没预料到会有这么多人。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will not go to school tomorrow. = 明天我不去上学。
They will not be here next week. = 他们下周不会在这里。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will not be working this time tomorrow. = 明天这个时候我不会在工作。
She will not be attending the meeting. = 她不会参加会议。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will not have finished the project by next week. = 下周我不会完成这个项目。
They will not have arrived by then. = 到那时他们不会到达。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will not have been living here for ten years. = 到明年，我就不会在这里住十年了。
By graduation, she will not have been studying medicine for five years. = 到毕业时，她就不会学医五年了。



虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would not do that. = 如果我是你，我就不会那样做。
I wish it were not raining. = 我希望没有下雨。

被动语态:
The decision was not made at the meeting. = 这个决定不是在会议上作出的。
The problem is not being solved effectively. = 这个问题没有得到有效解决。

疑问句:
Isn't it a beautiful day? = 今天天气不是很好吗？
Why didn't you tell me earlier? = 你为什么不早点告诉我？

否定句:
I do not want to go. = 我不想去。
She did not see the movie. = 她没看那部电影。


PHRASE->
not at all = 一点也不
not only...but also = 不仅...而且
not yet = 还没有
not really = 不完全是；并非如此
not bad = 不错


词源：From Old English *nāwiht, *nōwiht, from nā "no" + wiht "thing, creature".  Related to naught.
