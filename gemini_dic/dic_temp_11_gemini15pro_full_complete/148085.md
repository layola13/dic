overscepticalness:/ˌoʊvərˈskɛptɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 过度怀疑, 过分怀疑 | The state of being excessively skeptical; excessive skepticism. | His overscepticalness prevented him from seeing the genuine opportunity.  他的过度怀疑让他错失了真正的机会。 | 近义词: cynicism, distrust, incredulity | 反义词: credulity, gullibility, trust | noun


Etymology:

"over-" (Old English *ofer) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "sceptical" (from Greek "skeptikos" meaning "inquiring, reflective") + "-ness" (Old English *-nes(s), a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition).  Essentially, it signifies an excessive state of being skeptical.
