ghoul: /ɡuːl/ | n. |食尸鬼；盗墓者；贪婪残忍的人|An evil spirit or phantom, especially one supposed to rob graves and feed on corpses; a person morbidly interested in death or disaster; a ravenous person.|The old stories told of ghouls that haunted the graveyard.  那些古老的故事讲述了在墓地里出没的食尸鬼。| He acted like a ghoul, reveling in the tragedy of others. 他表现得像个食尸鬼，以他人的悲剧为乐。 |Synonyms: fiend, demon, monster, ogre, vampire|Antonyms: angel, saint|noun
