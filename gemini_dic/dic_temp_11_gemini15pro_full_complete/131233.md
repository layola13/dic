/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection | 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something; used to express surprise or disbelief. |

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner? = 你晚饭想吃什么？



近义词: which (in some contexts) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
