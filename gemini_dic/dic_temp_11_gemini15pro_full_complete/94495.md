importer's:/ɪmˈpɔːrtərz/
noun
进口商的；进口商所有的
belonging to or associated with an importer (a person or company that brings goods into a country from abroad for sale)
The importer's warehouse was full of goods from all over the world. 进口商的仓库里堆满了来自世界各地的货物。
synonyms: importer's company, importing firm
antonyms: exporter's, exporter's company
