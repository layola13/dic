hermitism:/ˈhɜːmɪtɪzəm/| n. |隐居；遁世；隐士主义|The practice of living as a hermit; seclusion from society for religious or spiritual reasons.  A system of religious or philosophical thought associated with hermits.|He embraced hermitism and lived in a remote cabin in the woods. 他信奉隐士主义，住在森林里一个偏僻的小屋里。|Synonyms: seclusion, solitude, asceticism, monasticism|Antonyms: sociability, gregariousness|noun

hermetic:/hɜːˈmɛtɪk/| adj. |密封的；炼金术的；深奥的|Tightly sealed; airtight. Relating to alchemy or the occult.  Difficult to understand; obscure.|The jar had a hermetic seal to keep the contents fresh.  这个罐子有一个密封的盖子以保持里面的东西新鲜。|Synonyms: airtight, sealed, impenetrable, esoteric, occult|Antonyms: open, permeable, exoteric|adjective
