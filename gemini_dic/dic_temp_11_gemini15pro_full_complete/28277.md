captivators:/ˈkæptɪveɪtərz/
n.
迷住者；令人着迷的人或物
Those who captivate or hold the attention of others by some special charm, power, or trait.
The captivating performance of the dancers held the audience spellbound. 舞蹈演员们迷人的表演让观众如痴如醉。
Synonyms: charmers, enchanters, fascinators, mesmerizers
Antonyms: bores, repellents
noun
