nonsweetest:/ˌnɒnˈswiːtɪst/ | adj. |最不甜的| Not at all sweet; least sweet.  This is the superlative form of 'nonsweet'. | The nonsweetest lemonade I've ever tasted was oddly refreshing.  我喝过的最不甜的柠檬水竟然异常清爽。| Near synonyms: unsweetest, least sweet, tartest, sourest | Near antonyms: sweetest, most sugary | Adjective
