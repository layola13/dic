ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *affodill, from Anglo-Norman affodile, asfodile, from Medieval Latin affodillus, a variant of asphodilus, from Latin asphodelus, from Ancient Greek ἀσφόδελος (asphódelos).  Influenced by the name Affodil, a pet form of the given name Aphrodite.
