lamprey:/ˈlæmpri/ (US), /ˈlæmprɪ/ (UK)| n. |七鳃鳗|A primitive eel-like fish with a sucker-like mouth lined with teeth. It attaches itself to other fish and sucks their blood.|例句：The lamprey is a parasitic fish. 七鳃鳗是一种寄生鱼类。

例句：Lampreys have been around for millions of years. 七鳃鳗已经存在了数百万年。|近义词：hagfish|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "host fish" in a parasitic context)|词性:noun
