kickbacks:/ˈkɪkbæks/| n.|回扣，佣金，强烈反应|A sum of money secretly paid to someone in exchange for a favor or service, typically involving bribery or corruption. Also, a strong negative reaction.|例句：He received kickbacks for awarding the contract to that company. 他因为把合同判给那家公司而收受了回扣。

kickbacks:/ˈkɪkbæks/| n.|后座力，反冲|A sudden recoil or backward movement.|例句：The rifle had a powerful kickback. 这支步枪的后座力很大。


近义词：bribe, commission, payoff, recoil, backlash|反义词:bonus, reward|词性:noun
