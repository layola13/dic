embarked:/ɪmˈbɑːrkt/ /ɛmˈbɑːrkt/ | v. | 上船；开始；从事 | to go aboard a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle, also to begin (a course of action). |  The passengers embarked at Southampton. 乘客们在南安普敦登船。| synonyms: board, commence, undertake | antonyms: disembark, conclude, abandon | verb

embark:/ɪmˈbɑːrk/ /ɛmˈbɑːrkt/ | n. | 登船，上飞机 | the act of embarking. (Less common usage) | The embark was delayed due to bad weather.  由于天气恶劣，登机被延误了。| synonyms: boarding, embarkation | antonyms: disembarkation, debarkation | noun
