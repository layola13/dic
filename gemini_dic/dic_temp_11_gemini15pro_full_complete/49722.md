democratising:/dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使民主化；民主化 | making something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions based on class, wealth, etc. |  Social media has democratizing access to a global audience. 社交媒体使接触全球受众变得民主化。| synonyms: opening up, empowering | antonyms: monopolizing, centralizing | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From democrat + -ise/-ize + -ing.  "Democrat" comes from French "démocrate" and ultimately from Greek "dēmokratia," meaning "rule by the people."  "-ise/-ize" is a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become." "-ing" is a suffix forming present participles.
