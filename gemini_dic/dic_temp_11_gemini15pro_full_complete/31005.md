chalklike:/ˈtʃɔːklaɪk/
adjective
像粉笔的; 白垩似的
Resembling chalk in appearance or texture.
The walls were painted a chalklike white.  墙被漆成了像粉笔一样的白色。
The soil was dry and chalklike. 土壤干燥，像粉笔一样。
近义词: chalky, powdery
反义词: smooth, glossy
词性: adjective
