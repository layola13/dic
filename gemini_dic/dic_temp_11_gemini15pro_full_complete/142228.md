ornithotomy:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒtəmi/ |n.|鸟类解剖；鸟体剖开术|The ornithotomy revealed the bird's cause of death. 鸟类解剖揭示了这只鸟的死因。|例句：The veterinarian performed an ornithotomy to determine the cause of the bird's illness. 兽医进行了鸟类解剖以确定这只鸟的病因。

例句：Ornithotomy is a specialized field of study. 鸟类解剖是一门专门的研究领域。|近义词：bird dissection, avian dissection|反义词：None|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὄρνιθος (órnithos, “bird”) +‎ -τομία (-tomía, “cutting”).
