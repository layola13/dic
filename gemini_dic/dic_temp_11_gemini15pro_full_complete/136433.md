nonrevelation:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛvəˈleɪʃən/
|n.|不揭露，不透露|The nonrevelation of the company's financial troubles led to a public outcry.  该公司财务困境的不披露导致公众哗然。|
|The witness's nonrevelation of key information hampered the investigation.  证人不透露关键信息阻碍了调查。|
|近义词：concealment, suppression, withholding|
|反义词：disclosure, revelation, divulgence|
|词性：名词|
|词源：non- (not) + revelation (the act of revealing)|
