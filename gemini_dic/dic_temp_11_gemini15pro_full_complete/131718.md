nonconfederations:/ˌnɒnkənˌfɛdəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 非邦联 |  A plural form of nonconfederation, referring to entities or groups that are not confederations. A confederation is a union of sovereign states, usually created by a treaty, that delegates certain limited powers to a central government.  Nonconfederations, therefore, are political entities that do not fit this specific structure. |  The historian compared the political structures of various nonconfederations throughout history. 这位历史学家比较了历史上各种非邦联的政治结构。 |  The report analyzed the economic performance of several nonconfederations in the region. 该报告分析了该区域几个非邦联的经济表现。 | synonyms: non-alliances, independent states | antonyms: confederations, alliances | noun
