biblicist:/ˈbɪblɪsɪst/| n.|圣经学者，圣经学家|A scholar specializing in the study of the Bible, especially its textual criticism and historical context.|例句：The biblicist dedicated her life to understanding the ancient scriptures.  这位圣经学者毕生致力于理解古代经文。

例句：The biblicist pointed out the subtle differences between the various translations.  圣经学者指出了不同译本之间的细微差别。|近义词：Bible scholar, scriptural scholar, theologian|反义词: None (缺乏明确的反义词)|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Bible + -ist.  "Bible" comes from Greek "biblia" (books), ultimately from "biblion" (book, paper).  "-ist" is a suffix denoting a person who practices or is an expert in a particular subject.
