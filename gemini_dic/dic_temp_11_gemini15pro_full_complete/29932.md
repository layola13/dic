cavalries: /ˈkævəlriz/ | n. |骑兵部队；装甲部队|  A group of soldiers who fought on horseback; now, often used to refer to armored forces. | The general inspected his cavalries before the battle. 将军在战斗前视察了他的骑兵部队。| The kingdom's cavalries were renowned throughout the land. 王国的骑兵部队闻名全国。 | 近义词:  troops, forces, armored units| 反义词: infantry | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cavalrye, from Italian cavalleria, from Late Latin caballarius "horseman," from Latin caballus "horse."
