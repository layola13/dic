dichromatic:/ˌdaɪkrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 双色的，二色的；二色性视觉的 | Having or exhibiting two colors; relating to or characterized by dichromatism (a deficiency of color vision in which only two colors can be distinguished). | The dichromatic bird flashed its red and blue wings.  这只双色鸟闪动着它的红蓝翅膀。| Synonyms: two-colored, bicoloured, bicolored | Antonyms: monochromatic, multicolored | adjective

dichromatic:/ˌdaɪkrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 双色的，二色的；二色性视觉的 |  A dichromatic solution changes color depending on the concentration. 这种二色性溶液的颜色会根据浓度而变化。 | Synonyms: two-toned, bicoloured, bicolored | Antonyms: monochromatic, multicolored | adjective
