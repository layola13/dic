outfaming: /ˌaʊtˈfeɪmɪŋ/
verb
胜过名声; 使名声黯然失色
surpassing someone in fame; eclipsing someone's reputation.
He outfamed his father, becoming a world-renowned scientist. 他比他父亲更有名，成为了一位世界知名的科学家。
The young artist quickly outfamed his mentor.  这位年轻的艺术家很快就比他的导师更有名了。
近义词: outshining, eclipsing, surpassing
反义词: overshadowed, eclipsed
