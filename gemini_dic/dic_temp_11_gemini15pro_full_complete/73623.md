foreword:/ˈfɔːrwɜːrd/ (US) /ˈfɔːwɜːd/ (UK)
n.
前言，序言
A short introductory section of a book, typically written by someone other than the author.
This edition includes a new foreword by the author's daughter.  这一版包含了作者女儿写的一篇新的前言。
