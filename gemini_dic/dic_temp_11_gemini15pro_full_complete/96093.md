indefensibly:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsɪbli/
adjective
无法辩护地，无可辩解地
not able to be defended or justified
His actions were indefensibly cruel. 他的行为残忍到无可辩解。
Synonyms: unjustifiable, inexcusable, unwarrantable
Antonyms: justifiable, defensible, excusable
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From in- (not) + defensible (capable of being defended) + -ly (adverbial suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin defendere (to defend).
