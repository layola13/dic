balize:/ˈbælɪz/ /bəˈlaɪz/ | n. vt.|航标，灯塔；设置航标|A floating or fixed object used to mark a channel or obstruction, or to indicate a position. |The ship steered clear of the balize marking the reef.  这艘船避开了标示暗礁的航标。| They balized the channel entrance. 他们在海峡入口处设置了航标。|近义词：beacon, buoy, marker|反义词:|n. & vt.
