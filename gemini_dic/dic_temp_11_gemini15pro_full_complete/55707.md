diurnation:/ˌdaɪərˈneɪʃən/
n.
白天活动;日间活动
the state of being active during the day.
Diurnation is a crucial aspect of the ecology of many species. 白天活动是许多物种生态学的一个重要方面。
Many desert animals have adapted to avoid diurnation during the hottest parts of the day. 许多沙漠动物已经适应了在一天中最热的时候避免白天活动。
近义词: daytime activity
反义词: nocturnality
词性: noun


Etymology: From Latin *diurnatio, from diurnus (“daily”) + -atio.
