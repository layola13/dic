The government imposed strict circumscriptions on freedom of speech. 政府对言论自由施加了严格的限制。

Her role in the project was carefully circumscribed. 她在项目中的角色被谨慎地限定了。

|近义词：limitation, restriction, definition, boundary, confinement|反义词：freedom, expansion, liberation, limitlessness|词性: 名词
