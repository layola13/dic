ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *golven, *gulven (“to swallow”), from Old Norse gulpa (“to gulp”), from Proto-Germanic *gulpōną (“to swallow, gulp”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʷelbh- (“to swallow, gulp”). Cognate with Dutch gulpen (“to gulp”), German gülpen (“to gush”).
