cynanthropy:/saɪˈnænθrəpi/ | n. |犬狂症，狂犬病；变形妄想症(认为自己是狗) |The condition of believing oneself to be a dog, or behaving like one.  It is a form of clinical lycanthropy, a rare psychiatric syndrome. |He suffered from cynanthropy and barked at the moon every night. 他患有犬狂症，每晚对着月亮吠叫。|  She exhibited symptoms of cynanthropy, walking on all fours and eating raw meat. 她表现出犬狂症的症状，四肢着地行走，吃生肉。| 近义词: lycanthropy (更广泛的变形妄想症) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun
