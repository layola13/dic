hedonic:/hɪˈdɑːnɪk/ |adj.| 享乐的，快乐的 | Relating to or characterized by pleasure; concerned with the pursuit of pleasure. |例句：Many purchases are driven by hedonic motives. 许多购物行为是由享乐动机驱动的。

例句：She leads a hedonistic lifestyle, indulging in every pleasure imaginable. 她过着享乐主义的生活，沉溺于一切可以想象的快乐。|近义词：pleasure-seeking, indulgent, luxurious, sybaritic |反义词：ascetic, self-denying, austere|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hēdonikos*, from *hēdonē* "pleasure."
