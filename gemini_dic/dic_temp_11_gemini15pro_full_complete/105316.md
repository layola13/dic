kiblah:/ˈkɪblə/ /ˈqɪblə/| n.|克尔白, 朝拜方向|The direction that should be faced when a Muslim prays.  It is toward the Kaaba in Mecca.|Muslims pray facing the kiblah. 穆斯林祈祷时面向克尔白。|The imam indicated the kiblah with his hand. 伊玛目用手指示克尔白的方向。|Synonyms: qibla, Qibla|Antonyms: N/A|noun
