non-subsidy:/nɒnˈsʌbsɪdi/ | adj. | 非补贴的 | Not receiving a subsidy; not related to or involving a subsidy.  A non-subsidy price reflects the true cost of a good or service without any government support. |  The non-subsidy price of gasoline is significantly higher. 非补贴汽油价格要高得多。 |  The farmers argued for non-subsidy pricing to ensure fair competition.  农民们主张非补贴定价以确保公平竞争。| Synonyms: unsubsidized, unsupported | Antonyms: subsidized, supported | adjective

non-subsidy:/nɒnˈsʌbsɪdi/ | n. | 非补贴 |  A product, service, or sector that does not receive government subsidies. | The growth of non-subsidy agriculture is encouraging. 非补贴农业的增长令人鼓舞。 |  The report analyzed the performance of non-subsidies in the renewable energy sector. 该报告分析了可再生能源领域非补贴的绩效。 | Synonyms: unsubsidized sector/product/service| Antonyms: subsidized sector/product/service | noun
