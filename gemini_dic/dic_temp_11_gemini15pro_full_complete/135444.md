nonpersuasive:/ˌnɒnpəˈsweɪsɪv/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈsweɪsɪv/
adjective
没有说服力的
not able to persuade someone to do or believe something.
The lawyer's closing argument was surprisingly nonpersuasive. 律师的结案陈词出人意料地没有说服力。
The evidence presented was nonpersuasive and the jury acquitted the defendant. 提出的证据没有说服力，陪审团宣判被告无罪。
近义词: unconvincing, ineffective, weak
反义词: persuasive, convincing, compelling
词性: adjective


词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
persuasive (adjective): From Late Latin persuasivus, from persuasus, past participle of persuadere "to persuade," from per- "thoroughly" + suadere "to advise."
