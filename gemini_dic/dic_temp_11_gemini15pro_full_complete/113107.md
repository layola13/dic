lunchrooms:/ˈlʌntʃruːmz/ /ˈlʌntʃˌrʊmz/
n.
午餐室，食堂
A room, especially in a school or place of work, set aside for eating lunch.
The students gathered in the lunchrooms to eat. 学生们聚集在食堂吃饭。
lunchroom, cafeteria, dining hall
canteen
noun
