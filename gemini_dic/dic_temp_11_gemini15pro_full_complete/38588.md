concordantly:/kənˈkɔːrdəntli/ /kɑːnˈkɔːrdəntli/| adv.| 一致地，和谐地，协调地 | in agreement, harmony, or conformity | The two studies' results, concordantly, showed a decrease in infections. 这两项研究的结果一致显示感染减少。|  The musicians played concordantly, creating a beautiful harmony. 音乐家们演奏得十分协调，创造出优美的和声。 | 近义词: harmoniously, consistently, agreeably, accordingly | 反义词: discordantly, inconsistently, differently | 词性: 副词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin concordant- ‘agreeing’, from the verb concordare (see concord).
