gesso:/ˈdʒɛsoʊ/ | n. | 石膏粉，底料 | A white paint mixture used to prepare surfaces, such as canvases or panels, for painting. | The artist applied a layer of gesso to the canvas before beginning to paint. 艺术家在开始绘画前在画布上涂了一层石膏粉。| synonyms: primer, ground | antonyms: N/A | noun

gesso:/ˈdʒɛsoʊ/ | v. |  用石膏粉涂底 | To apply gesso to a surface. | He gessoed the wooden panel before painting a portrait. 他在画肖像之前先用石膏粉涂在木板上。| synonyms: prime, prepare | antonyms: N/A | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *gesso* "gypsum, plaster of Paris," from Latin *gypsum*, from Greek *gypsos* "chalk, plaster."
