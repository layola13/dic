glandule:/ˈɡlændjuːl/| n. |小腺体|A small gland.  It is a diminutive of "gland".|The biologist identified a previously unknown glandule in the insect's abdomen. 生物学家在昆虫的腹部发现了一个以前未知的小腺体。|Synonyms: glandlet, acinus|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a small anatomical structure)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *glandule*, diminutive of *glande* (gland), from Latin *glans*, *glandis* (acorn, gland).
