manginesses:/ˈmeɪndʒɪnəsɪz/
|n.| 疥癣，污秽 |The state of being mangy; the condition of having mange.  A general state of dirtiness, shabbiness, or disorder.|The dog's manginesses were a sign of neglect. 这条狗的疥癣是它被忽视的迹象。|Synonyms: scabbiness, scruffiness, squalor|Antonyms: cleanliness, neatness|Noun
