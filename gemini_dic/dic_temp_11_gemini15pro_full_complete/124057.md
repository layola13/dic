morphogenetic:/ˌmɔːrfədʒəˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌmɔːrfoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|形态发生的，形态生成的|Relating to or involved in the development of form and structure in an organism.  The process by which an organism develops its shape.|例句：Morphogenetic proteins play a crucial role in embryonic development.  形态发生蛋白在胚胎发育中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Scientists are studying the morphogenetic processes that control the growth of plants. 科学家正在研究控制植物生长的形态发生过程。|近义词：formative, developmental|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
