heartburn:/ˈhɑːrtbɜːrn/| n.|胃灼热，烧心|A burning sensation in the chest, caused by acid refluxing from the stomach into the esophagus.|I ate too much spicy food and now I have heartburn. 我吃了太多辛辣的食物，现在胃灼热。|Synonyms: acid indigestion, pyrosis|Antonyms: comfort, relief|Noun

heartburn:/ˈhɑːrtbɜːrn/| n.|妒忌，羡慕|A feeling of envy or jealousy.|He felt a pang of heartburn when he saw his rival's success.  看到对手的成功，他感到一阵妒忌。|Synonyms: envy, jealousy, resentment|Antonyms: admiration, contentment|Noun
