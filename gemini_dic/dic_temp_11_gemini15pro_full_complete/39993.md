consultancies:/kənˈsʌltənsiz/
n.
咨询公司（复数）
Businesses that offer expert advice to other companies.
Many large companies use consultancies to help them improve their performance. 许多大公司聘请咨询公司来帮助他们提升业绩。
We hired a marketing consultancy to help us develop a new strategy. 我们聘请了一家营销咨询公司来帮助我们制定新的战略。
Synonyms: advisory firms, consulting firms
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "in-house teams")
