acinesia:/ˌeɪkaɪˈniːʒə, -ˈniːziə/ /ˌeɪsɪˈniːʒə, -ˈniːziə/
n.
运动不能，运动迟缓
Loss or impairment of the power of voluntary movement.
例句：Parkinson's disease is characterized by tremors, rigidity, and acinesia. 帕金森病的特征是震颤、僵硬和运动不能。

例句：The acinesia made it difficult for her to perform simple tasks like buttoning her shirt. 运动不能使她难以完成像扣衬衫这样简单的任务。

近义词：akinesia, hypokinesia, bradykinesia
反义词：hyperkinesia
词性：名词
