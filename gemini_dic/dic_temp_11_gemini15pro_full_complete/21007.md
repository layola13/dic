blessednesses:/ˈblesɪdnəsɪz/ /ˈblesɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
幸福；福祉；神恩；祝福
supreme happiness; spiritual joy.  The state of being blessed.
Example: The Beatitudes describe the blessednesses of those who are persecuted for righteousness' sake.  八福描述了那些为义受逼迫的人的福祉。
Example:  Many people seek blessednesses in material possessions, but true happiness often lies elsewhere. 许多人从物质财富中寻求幸福，但真正的幸福往往在别处。
Synonym: blessings, beatitudes, felicities, bliss
Antonym: curses, misfortunes, miseries
Noun
