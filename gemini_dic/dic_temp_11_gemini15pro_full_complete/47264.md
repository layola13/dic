de-Americanization:/diːəˌmɛrɪkənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 去美国化；非美国化 | The process of removing American influence or characteristics from something, such as a culture, product, or organization. |  The de-Americanization of global media is a complex process. 全球媒体的去美国化是一个复杂的过程。

The author argues for the de-Americanization of the school curriculum. 作者主张学校课程去美国化。 | 近义词：localization, indigenization | 反义词：Americanization | 词性：名词
