malinger:/məˈlɪŋɡər/| v. |装病；逃避责任|To pretend to be ill or incapacitated in order to avoid work or duty.|He malingered for weeks, claiming a back injury. 他装病几周，声称背部受伤。|例句：The soldier malingered to avoid combat.  这个士兵装病以逃避战斗。

例句：He was suspected of malingering to get out of military service. 他被怀疑装病以逃避兵役。

近义词：shirk, feign, dodge, pretend|反义词: persevere, attend |词性: verb

malingering:/məˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/| n. |装病；逃避责任（的行为）|The act of pretending to be ill or incapacitated in order to avoid work or duty.|His malingering was obvious to everyone. 他的装病对每个人来说都很明显。|例句：Malingering is a serious offense in the military. 在军队中，装病是严重的违纪行为。

例句：The doctor suspected malingering when the patient's symptoms didn't match any known illness. 当病人的症状与任何已知疾病都不相符时，医生怀疑是装病。
