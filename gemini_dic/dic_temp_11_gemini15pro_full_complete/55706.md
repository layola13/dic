diurnal: /daɪˈɜːrnl/ | adj. | 白天的；每日的 | Diurnal animals are active during the day. | The diurnal temperature variation can be significant in desert regions. 沙漠地区的昼夜温差可能很大。 | synonyms: daily, daytime | antonyms: nocturnal | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
