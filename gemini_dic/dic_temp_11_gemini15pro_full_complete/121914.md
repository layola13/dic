missend: /mɪsˈsɛnd/
verb
错发；发送失败
to send (something, such as an email) to the wrong person or address.
I missend the email to my boss instead of my colleague. 我把邮件错发给了老板，而不是我的同事。
The report was missend to the wrong department. 报告被错发到了错误的部门。
近义词: misdirect, misdeliver
反义词: deliver correctly, send correctly
词性: verb
