nonsanctification: /ˌnɒnsan(k)tɪfɪˈkeɪʃ(ə)n/ | n. | 未圣化，不神圣 | The state of not being sanctified; lack of holiness or religious consecration. |  The preacher spoke of the dangers of nonsanctification.  牧师谈到了未圣化的危险。 |  profaneness, ungodliness, impiety | sanctification, holiness, piety | noun

词源:
From non- + sanctification.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "sanctification" comes from the Latin "sanctificatio," meaning "a making holy."
