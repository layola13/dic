kilning:/ˈkɪlnɪŋ/ | n. |焙烧; 烘干|The process of drying and hardening something, especially grain or malt, in a kiln.  This involves heating the material to a specific temperature for a specific duration to reduce moisture content and induce chemical changes.|The kilning of the barley is crucial for the beer's flavor. 大麦的焙烧对啤酒的风味至关重要。|近义词：drying, baking, roasting|反义词：hydrating, soaking|词性：名词

词源: From the verb "kiln" + "-ing" suffix.  "Kiln" comes from Old English *cyline, from Latin culina "kitchen, cooking stove."
