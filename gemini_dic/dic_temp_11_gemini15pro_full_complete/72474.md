flunky:/ˈflʌŋki/| n.| 仆从，马屁精，走狗|A person who performs menial tasks or behaves servilely, especially to someone in authority.| He acts like the boss's flunky, always fetching coffee and agreeing with everything he says. 他表现得像老板的走狗，总是去拿咖啡，并且同意他说的每一句话。|Synonyms: lackey, servant, toady, yes-man|Antonyms: leader, boss, superior|noun

flunky:/ˈflʌŋki/| v.|拍马屁，卑躬屈膝|To behave servilely; to fawn.| He flunked around the professor, hoping for a better grade. 他对教授卑躬屈膝，希望能得到更好的成绩。|Synonyms: fawn, grovel, kowtow|Antonyms: defy, resist, challenge|verb
