duse:/djuːs/| n.|屁股；烟头；（苏格兰俚语）脸；家伙|A cigarette butt, especially one kept short for relighting later. Also (chiefly Scottish slang) the buttocks or face; a person.|He picked up the duse and relit it.|近义词：butt, fag-end, rump, backside, face, fellow|反义词: None|词性:名词

词源:
Likely a shortened form of "deuce" (meaning "two"), referring to the two ends of a cigarette.  Its usage to refer to the buttocks or face is of uncertain origin, possibly related to its shape or usage as a term of endearment/derision.
