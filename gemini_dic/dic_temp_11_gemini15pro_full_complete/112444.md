louchettes:/luˈʃɛts/
noun
护目镜，眼镜，(尤指)夹鼻眼镜
Spectacles, especially pince-nez.  A pair of eyeglasses, often of a specific old-fashioned type, held on the nose by a spring.
He adjusted his louchettes and peered at the document. 他调整了一下他的夹鼻眼镜，仔细地看着文件。
synonyms: spectacles, eyeglasses, pince-nez, glasses
antonyms: N/A
