dactyliology:/ˌdæktɪliˈɒlədʒi/
n.
指語學; 手語
the study of finger spelling and sign language.
Dactyliology is a crucial skill for those who are deaf or hard of hearing.  指語學對聾啞人或有聽力障礙的人來說至關重要。
The teacher used dactyliology to communicate with her deaf student. 老師使用指語與她耳聾的學生交流。
近义词:sign language, finger spelling
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


dactyliology:/ˌdæktɪliˈɒlədʒi/
n.
手語; 手語法
a method of communication using finger spelling and signs made with the hands.
She learned dactyliology to communicate with her deaf friend. 她學習了手語以便與她耳聾的朋友交流。
He was impressed by the speed and fluency of her dactyliology.  他對她手語的速度和流暢性印象深刻。
