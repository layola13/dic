bigotedly:/ˈbɪɡətɪdli/| adv.|偏执地，固执地|In a bigoted manner; with intolerance and prejudice.  Showing strong and unreasonable beliefs or opinions, especially about race, religion, or politics, and refusing to consider different views.|He bigotedly refused to listen to any opposing viewpoints. 他固执地拒绝听取任何反对意见。|Synonyms: intolerantly, narrowly, prejudicedly, dogmatically, fanatically|Antonyms: tolerantly, open-mindedly, fairly, impartially|Word type: Adverb

bigoted:/ˈbɪɡətɪd/| adj.|偏执的，固执的|Having strong, unreasonable beliefs or opinions, especially about race, religion, or politics, and refusing to consider different views.|He made some bigoted remarks about immigrants. 他对移民发表了一些偏执的言论。|Synonyms: intolerant, narrow-minded, prejudiced, dogmatic, fanatical|Antonyms: tolerant, open-minded, fair, impartial|Word type: Adjective

bigot:/ˈbɪɡət/| n.|偏执者，固执者|A person who has strong, unreasonable beliefs or opinions, especially about race, religion, or politics, and who refuses to consider different views.|He's a narrow-minded bigot who refuses to accept anyone different from himself. 他是一个心胸狭窄的偏执狂，拒绝接受任何与他不同的人。|Synonyms: fanatic, extremist, dogmatist|Antonyms: freethinker, liberal|Word type: Noun

Etymology: From Old French *bigot "religious hypocrite," of unknown origin.
