nontraditionalist:/ˌnɑːn.trəˈdɪʃ.ən.əl.ɪst/ | n. | 非传统主义者 | A person who rejects traditional beliefs, customs, or practices. |  Many nontraditionalists choose to home-school their children. 许多非传统主义者选择在家教育他们的孩子。 |  Modernist, innovator, reformist | Traditionalist, conservative | noun
nontraditionalist:/ˌnɒn.trəˈdɪʃ.ən.əl.ɪst/ | adj. | 非传统的 | Not adhering to tradition; innovative or modern. | The nontraditionalist approach to education emphasizes individualized learning. 这种非传统的教育方法强调个性化学习。 |  Modern, innovative, unconventional | Traditional, conventional, orthodox | adjective
