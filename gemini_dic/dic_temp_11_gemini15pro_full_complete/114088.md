magnificent:/mæɡˈnɪfɪs(ə)nt/| adj.|壮丽的，宏伟的，极好的|Impressively beautiful, elaborate, or extravagant; striking in appearance or effect.  Characterized by grandeur and impressiveness.|The Taj Mahal is a magnificent building. 泰姬陵是一座宏伟的建筑。|近义词：grand, splendid, glorious, impressive, majestic, superb|反义词：unimpressive, ordinary, mediocre, paltry|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle French *magnifique*, from Latin magnificus (“doing great things, magnificent, noble”), from magnus (“great, large, big”) + -ficus (akin to facere, “to do, to make”).
