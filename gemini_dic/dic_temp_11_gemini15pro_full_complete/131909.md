noncontiguous:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪɡjuəs/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈtɪɡjuəs/
adjective
非连续的
Not adjoining or adjacent; separated.  Elements that are not next to each other; not touching along a boundary or at a point.
The data is stored in noncontiguous blocks on the hard drive. 数据存储在硬盘的非连续块中。
The islands are noncontiguous, scattered across a vast ocean. 这些岛屿是非连续的，散布在广阔的海洋中。
近义词:disconnected, separate, disjointed, scattered
反义词:contiguous, adjacent, adjoining, connected
词性:adjective
