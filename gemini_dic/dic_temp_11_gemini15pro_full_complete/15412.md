bacteria's:/bækˈtɪəriəz/
n.|细菌的|The possessive form of "bacteria," indicating ownership or association.|
例句：The bacteria's cell wall protects it from the environment.  细菌的细胞壁保护它免受环境的影响。

例句：Studying the bacteria's DNA can help us understand its resistance to antibiotics. 研究细菌的DNA可以帮助我们了解它对抗生素的耐药性。
近义词：(None -  because it's a possessive form.  You could say "of the bacteria" or "belonging to the bacteria")
反义词：(None -  for the same reason as above)
词性:Possessive Noun
