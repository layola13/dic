词源:

From Middle English *noght, nowht, nawht, noȝt, from Old English nāwiht, nāuht (“nothing, naught, not, not at all, no, never”), from ne (“not”) + āwiht (“aught, anything”).  Compare Scots nocht, nout (“nothing”).  Related to naught.
