foreknowingly:/fɔːrˈnoʊɪŋli/
adv.
预先知道地，故意地
with previous knowledge of something, especially of something bad that is going to happen
例句：He foreknowingly lied to the police. 他明知故犯地向警察撒谎。

例句：She foreknowingly entered into a dangerous situation. 她明知山有虎，偏向虎山行。(比喻明知有危险，却偏要冒险)


近义词：deliberately, intentionally, wittingly, consciously
反义词：unknowingly, inadvertently, unconsciously
词性：副词
