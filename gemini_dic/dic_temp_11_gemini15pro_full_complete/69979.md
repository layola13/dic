festschriften:/ˈfɛstʃrɪftən/
n.
纪念文集，祝寿文集
A collection of essays or articles written by colleagues and admirers in honor of a scholar, often published on the occasion of a special anniversary such as retirement.
A Festschrift was published in her honor upon her retirement.  在她退休之际，出版了一本纪念文集来向她致敬。
commemorative volume, tribute
(Usually no antonym for this word)
noun
