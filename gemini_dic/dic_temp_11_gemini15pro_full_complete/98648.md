insufferable:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbl/ | adj. |无法忍受的，令人厌恶的 | Not to be endured; intolerable; extremely annoying or unpleasant. |  His arrogance is insufferable. 他的傲慢令人无法忍受。

The heat was insufferable that day. 那天的酷暑令人难以忍受。


| 近义词: intolerable, unbearable, unendurable, obnoxious, pompous | 反义词: tolerable, bearable, pleasant, agreeable | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *insufferable*, from Old French *insufferable*, from Medieval Latin *insufferabilis*, from Latin *in-* (not) + *sufferre* (to bear, endure).  The Latin *sufferre* is from *sub-* (under) + *ferre* (to carry, bear).
