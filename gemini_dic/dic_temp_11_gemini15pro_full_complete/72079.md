floatable:/ˈfloʊtəbl/| adj.|可漂浮的，能浮起的|Something that is floatable is able to float on water or another liquid.|The life jacket is floatable and will keep you safe in the water. 救生衣是可漂浮的，它能保证你在水中的安全。|The empty plastic bottles were surprisingly floatable.  这些空的塑料瓶出奇地能浮起来。|近义词：buoyant, afloat|反义词:sinkable, submersible|词性:adjective
词源: From float (v.) + -able.  来自动词float（漂浮）+形容词后缀-able（可...的）。
