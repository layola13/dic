outbulging:/ˌaʊtˈbʌldʒɪŋ/
adjective
向外膨胀的，凸出的
Curving or swelling outward; bulging.
The outbulging wall of the barn showed signs of needing repair.  谷仓向外膨胀的墙壁显示出需要维修的迹象。
The frog puffed its throat, creating an outbulging pouch.  青蛙鼓起喉咙，形成一个凸出的囊袋。
近义词: protruding, bulging, swelling
反义词: concave, indented, receding
词性: adjective

outbulge:/ˌaʊtˈbʌldʒ/
verb
向外膨胀，凸出
To bulge or swell outward.
The muscles in his arms outbulged as he lifted the heavy weight.  他举起重物时，手臂上的肌肉向外膨胀。
The damaged pipe began to outbulge, threatening to burst.  受损的管道开始向外膨胀，有爆裂的危险。
近义词: protrude, bulge, swell
反义词: recede, contract, shrink
词性: verb

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outward") + "bulge" (verb meaning "to swell or protrude").  "bulge" comes from the Old Norse word "bulki," meaning "cargo, swelling."
