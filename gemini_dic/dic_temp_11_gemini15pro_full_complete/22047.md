boldnesses:/ˈbəʊldnɪsɪz/
n.
大胆；鲁莽；冒失
The state or quality of being bold; audacity.
He showed considerable boldness in criticizing his superiors. 他在批评上司时表现出相当大的胆量。
近义词:audacity, nerve, courage, daring, bravery, fearlessness, intrepidity, mettle, spirit, pluck, grit, backbone, chutzpah
反义词:timidity, cowardice, fearfulness
词性:名词(plural)
