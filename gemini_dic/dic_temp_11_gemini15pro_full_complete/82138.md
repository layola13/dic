grommeting: /ˈɡrɒmɪtɪŋ/ | n. |索环固定; 装索环;  |The act of attaching grommets, small metal rings, to material, typically fabric or leather, to reinforce holes and prevent tearing. |Example 1: The grommeting on the sails ensured they could withstand strong winds.  帆上的索环固定确保它们能够承受强风。

Example 2:  The grommeting of the leather bag added a stylish touch and made it more durable. 皮包上的索环装饰增添了时尚感，也使其更加耐用。| 近义词: eyeleting | 反义词:  None | 词性: 名词
