ayahuasca:/ˌaɪəˈwɑːskə/ | n. | 南美卡皮木；死藤水 | A psychoactive brew made from the Banisteriopsis caapi vine and other plants, traditionally used by indigenous peoples of the Amazon basin for spiritual and healing purposes. |  Example 1: The shaman prepared the ayahuasca for the ceremony.  萨满为仪式准备了死藤水。

Example 2:  She experienced profound visions after drinking ayahuasca.  喝下死藤水后，她经历了深刻的幻觉。 | Synonym: yagé, caapi | Antonym: None | Noun
