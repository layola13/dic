confer:/kənˈfɜːr/ (US) /kənˈfəːr/ (UK)
verb
授予，给予；商讨，协商
To grant or bestow (a title, degree, benefit, or right); to have discussions; exchange opinions.
The university conferred an honorary degree on the renowned scientist.  大学授予这位著名科学家荣誉学位。
He conferred with his colleagues before making a decision. 他在做决定之前与同事们进行了协商。
近义词: bestow, grant, award, discuss, deliberate
反义词: withhold, deny, refuse
词性: verb

confer:/kənˈfɜːr/ (US) /kənˈfəːr/ (UK)
noun (rare)
会议，协商
A meeting or discussion.
They had a confer about the new project. 他们就新项目进行了一次会议。(这个用法比较少见)
近义词: meeting, discussion, conference
反义词: N/A (名词形式少用，故无明显反义词)
词性: noun
