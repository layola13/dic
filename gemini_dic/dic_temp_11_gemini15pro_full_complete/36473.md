colds:/koʊldz/| n. |感冒|A common illness that causes inflammation of the mucous membrane in the nose and throat, characterized by sneezing, a runny nose, and often a sore throat.  It's the plural form of "cold".|I've had three colds this winter. 我今年冬天已经感冒三次了。|近义词：flu, illness|反义词:health, wellness|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old English *ceald, from Proto-Germanic *kaldaz, from Proto-Indo-European *gel- (cold). The plural form is simply formed by adding -s.
