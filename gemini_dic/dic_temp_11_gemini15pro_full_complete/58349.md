dti:/ˌdiː tiːˈaɪ/
noun
Debt-to-income ratio
A personal finance measure that compares the amount of debt you have to your overall income.  It is expressed as a percentage.
例句：Lenders use your DTI to assess your ability to manage monthly payments and repay the money you borrow. 贷款人使用你的负债收入比来评估你管理每月还款和偿还借款的能力。

例句：A low DTI indicates good financial health.  低的负债收入比表明良好的财务状况。
synonym: debt ratio, debt service ratio
antonym: income-to-debt ratio (this isn't a standard term, but represents the inverse relationship)
词性:noun (abbreviation)
