cleanings:/ˈkliːnɪŋz/
noun
清洁；清洁工作；清除；净化
the act of making something clean or the process of becoming clean.

The spring cleanings are a yearly ritual. 春季大扫除是一年一度的惯例。

The dry cleanings were ready for pickup. 干洗的衣服可以取了。


Synonym: cleansing, purification, scrubbing, washing
Antonym: dirtying, soiling

词性: noun
