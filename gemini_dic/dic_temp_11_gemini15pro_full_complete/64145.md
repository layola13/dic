equalist:/ˈiːkwəlɪst/ | n. | 平等主义者；主张人人平等的人 | A person who believes in the principle of equality for all people, especially in political, social, or economic life. |  He considers himself an equalist and strives for a society where everyone has the same opportunities. 他自认为是一个平等主义者，并致力于建立一个每个人都有相同机会的社会。

| 近义词：egalitarian | 反义词：elitist, supremacist | 词性：noun


equalist:/ˈiːkwəlɪst/ | adj. | 平等主义的 | Relating to or characterized by the belief in the principle of equality for all people. | The organization promotes equalist policies that benefit all members of society.  该组织推行有利于所有社会成员的平等主义政策。

| 近义词：egalitarian | 反义词：elitist, discriminatory | 词性：adjective
