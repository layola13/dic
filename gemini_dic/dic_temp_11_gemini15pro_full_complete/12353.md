ashen:/ˈæʃən/| adj.|灰白色的，苍白的|Of or resembling ashes in color; pale.|Her face was ashen with fear. 她的脸色因恐惧而变得苍白。|近义词：pale, pallid, wan, gray|反义词：ruddy, flushed, rosy|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æscen, from æsce (“ash”). Cognate with Dutch asch, German Asche, Icelandic aska.
