now-that:/naʊ ðæt/ | conj. | 既然；由于；因为 |  Used to introduce a reason or explanation for something, especially something that has just happened or just been mentioned. | Now that I'm older, I appreciate my parents more. 既然我年纪大了，我更感激我的父母。| 近义词: since, because, seeing that, given that, inasmuch as | 反义词: although, though | 词性: 连词
