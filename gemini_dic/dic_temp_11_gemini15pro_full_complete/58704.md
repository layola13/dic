dumps:/dʌmps/| n.|垃圾场; 倾倒处; 沮丧; 忧郁|A place where rubbish or waste material is taken and left.|The city dumps are overflowing. 城市的垃圾场都快溢出来了。|近义词:tip, landfill, junkyard; depression, gloom|反义词: (for emotional state) happiness, cheerfulness|noun

dumps:/dʌmps/| v. (dumps, dumping, dumped)|倾倒; 丢弃; 甩掉(恋人); (向国外)倾销(商品)|To put something somewhere quickly and carelessly, or to get rid of something you do not want.|She dumped her old clothes in the bin. 她把旧衣服扔进了垃圾桶。|近义词:discard, dispose of, jettison, ditch (a lover)|反义词:keep, retain|verb
