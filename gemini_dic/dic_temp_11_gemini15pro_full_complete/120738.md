misanalysis:/ˌmɪsənˈælɪsɪs/| n.|错误分析，误判|An incorrect or faulty analysis of data, information, or a situation.  A misanalysis of the market led to the company's downfall.|The doctor's misanalysis of the symptoms led to the wrong treatment.|近义词：misinterpretation, misjudgment, blunder, error|反义词:correct analysis, accurate interpretation|词性:noun
