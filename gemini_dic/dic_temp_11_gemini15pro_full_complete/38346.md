concelebrate:/kənˈsɛləˌbreɪt/ /ˈkɑːnsɛləˌbreɪt/
verb
共同庆祝；一起举行（宗教仪式）
To celebrate together, especially a religious rite.
The couple concelebrated their 50th wedding anniversary with family and friends. 这对夫妇与家人和朋友共同庆祝了他们的金婚纪念日。
Several priests concelebrated the Mass. 几位神父共同主持了弥撒。
近义词: celebrate jointly, commemorate together
反义词: mourn, grieve
词性: verb
