misuse: /ˌmɪsˈjuːs/ (UK), /ˌmɪsˈjuːz/ (US)| vt.|滥用，误用；|He misused his power to get what he wanted. 他滥用权力来得到他想要的东西。| Synonyms: abuse, mishandle, misapply | Antonyms: use correctly, apply properly | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *misusen, from mis- + usen (“to use”), from Old French user, ultimately from Latin ūsārī (“to use”).
