CVV:/siː viː viː/ | n. | Card Verification Value (信用卡验证码) | A three- or four-digit security code printed on credit and debit cards, used to verify transactions and prevent fraud. | Example 1:  Please enter your CVV number to complete the purchase. 请输入您的CVV码以完成购买。

Example 2: The CVV is an important security feature to protect your card from unauthorized use. CVV是一项重要的安全功能，可以保护您的卡免遭未经授权的使用。 | 近义词: CVC (Card Verification Code), CID (Card Identification Number) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
