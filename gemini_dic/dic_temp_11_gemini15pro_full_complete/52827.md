dilution:/daɪˈluːʃn/ /dɪˈluːʃn/| n.|稀释，冲淡，淡化|The act of making a liquid thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent.  The act of reducing the strength or effectiveness of something.|The company decided on a dilution of its core brand.  公司决定淡化其核心品牌。|近义词：weakening, thinning, attenuation|反义词:concentration, strengthening|noun
