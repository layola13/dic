cartouche:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/| n. |涡卷形装饰; (古埃及象形文字的)椭圆形圈;  弹药盒; |A cartouche is an oval or oblong frame with a horizontal line at one end, indicating that it encloses the name of a royal personage. |The pharaoh's name was inscribed within a cartouche. 法老的名字刻在一个椭圆形圈内。|近义词：frame, oval, panel, tablet |反义词: N/A |noun
