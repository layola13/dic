clonking:/ˈklɒŋkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈklɑːŋkɪŋ/ (US)| adj.  n.|发出沉闷的金属撞击声的，沉闷的金属撞击声|A heavy dull metallic sound, like that produced by a large piece of metal striking another.  Also used to describe something making such a sound.|The old car was clonking as it went down the road. 那辆旧车在行驶时发出哐啷哐啷的声音。|近义词：clanging, banging, thumping|反义词: silent, quiet|词性: adjective, noun
