kiddies:/ˈkɪdiz/| n.|小孩，孩子们 (Informal)|A childish or affectionate way of referring to children. It's more informal and often used with younger children.|例句：The kiddies are having a great time at the party. 孩子们在派对上玩得很开心。|例句：I bought some candies for the kiddies. 我给孩子们买了一些糖果。|近义词：children, kids, tots, youngsters|反义词:adults, grown-ups|Noun
