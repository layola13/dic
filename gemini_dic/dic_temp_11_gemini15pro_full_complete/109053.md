legalist: /ˈliːɡəlɪst/ | n. |法家,墨守成规者|A person who adheres strictly to the law or legal principles, sometimes to an excessive degree. |例句：The legalist argued that the contract must be followed to the letter.  这位法学家认为合同必须严格遵守。

例句：He's such a legalist; he never bends the rules, even in emergencies. 他真是个墨守成规的人；即使在紧急情况下，他也从不违反规则。|近义词：stickler, formalist, rigorist |反义词: anarchist, rebel, nonconformist | 词性: noun
