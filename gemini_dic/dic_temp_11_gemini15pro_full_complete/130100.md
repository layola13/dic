non-prosecution:/ˌnɒnˌprɒsɪˈkjuːʃn/ | n. |不起诉，不予起诉|The act of not prosecuting someone for a crime, even if there is evidence that they committed it.  It's a decision made by law enforcement or prosecutorial authorities. |The prosecutor agreed to non-prosecution in exchange for the defendant's cooperation. 检察官同意不起诉，以换取被告的合作。

The non-prosecution agreement spared him from jail time.  不起诉协议使他免于牢狱之灾。 | Synonyms: immunity, pardon, clemency | Antonyms: prosecution, indictment, charge | noun
