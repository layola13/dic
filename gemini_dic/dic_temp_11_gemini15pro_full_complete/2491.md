ADME:/ˈædmiː/| n. |吸收、分布、代谢、排泄 (Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, and Excretion)|ADME describes the disposition of a pharmaceutical compound within an organism. It represents the four stages a drug goes through in the body.  The study of ADME is crucial in drug development.|The ADME properties of the new drug are promising.  这种新药的ADME特性很有希望。|近义词：pharmacokinetics|反义词: 无|名词
