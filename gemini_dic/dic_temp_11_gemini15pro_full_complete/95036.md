inacquiescent:/ˌɪnˌækwiˈɛsənt/
adjective
不顺从的，不默许的
unwilling to accept something quietly; not acquiescing.
Her inacquiescent nature made her a strong advocate for change. 她不顺从的天性使她成为变革的坚定拥护者。
The crowd grew increasingly inacquiescent as the speaker continued to evade their questions.  随着演讲者继续回避他们的问题，人群越来越不顺从。
近义词: resistant, defiant, opposed, dissenting, rebellious
反义词: acquiescent, compliant, submissive, yielding, agreeable
词性: adjective

词源:  From in- (not) + acquiescent (from Latin acquiescere, to rest, remain, find repose in).
