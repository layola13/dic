concorporation:/kənˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |合并，结合，混合| The act or process of uniting or combining into one body or mass. | The concorporation of several small businesses into one large company.  几家小企业合并成一家大公司。 | 近义词：amalgamation, merger, union, fusion, integration, combination | 反义词：separation, division, fission | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *concorporātiō*, from *concorporāre* ("to embody, incorporate"), from *con-* ("together") + *corpus* ("body").
