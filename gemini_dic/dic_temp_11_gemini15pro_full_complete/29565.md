catchpenny:/ˈkætʃˌpɛni/| n. | 廉价物品；骗钱的玩意儿；吸引人的东西| something designed to sell quickly and make a profit, but which is usually of poor quality |The market was full of catchpenny toys. 市场里充斥着廉价玩具。|Synonyms: gimmick, trinket, bauble, gewgaw|Antonyms:  treasure, heirloom, valuable|noun
catchpenny:/ˈkætʃˌpɛni/| adj. | 廉价的；骗钱的| designed to sell quickly and make a profit, but usually of poor quality | He makes a living selling catchpenny goods. 他靠卖廉价商品为生。|Synonyms: cheap, tawdry, shoddy|Antonyms:  expensive, high-quality, valuable|adjective
