lucimeter:/luːˈsɪmɪtər/
n.|光度计；照度计|An instrument for measuring the intensity of light. |The lucimeter showed that the light intensity was insufficient for optimal plant growth. 光度计显示光照强度不足以使植物最佳生长。|
近义词:photometer, light meter|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Latin *lūx* (light) + *-meter* (measure).
