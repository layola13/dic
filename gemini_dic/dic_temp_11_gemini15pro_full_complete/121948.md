mission:/ˈmɪʃən/| n.| 任务，使命，代表团 |A task or job that someone is given to do, especially one that involves travelling somewhere.|The doctor went on a mission to Africa. 这位医生去非洲执行一项任务。|近义词：task, assignment, duty, objective, goal, errand, quest, undertaking, expedition|反义词: leisure, vacation|noun

mission:/ˈmɪʃən/| n.| 使命，天职|A strongly felt aim, ambition, or calling.|Her mission in life is to help others. 她的人生使命是帮助他人。|近义词：calling, vocation, purpose|反义词: aimlessness|noun

mission:/ˈmɪʃən/| n.| 布道所，传教团|A group of people sent to a foreign country to teach their religion.|The mission provides food and shelter to the homeless. 这个布道所为无家可归的人提供食物和住所。|近义词：delegation, embassy, legation|反义词: N/A|noun
