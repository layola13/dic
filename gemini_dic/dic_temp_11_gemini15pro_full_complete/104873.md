keenness:/ˈkiːnnəs/
n.
敏锐, 热切, 渴望
The quality of being keen; eagerness or enthusiasm.
She approached the task with great keenness. 她以极大的热情投入这项工作。
近义词：eagerness, enthusiasm, avidity, zeal
反义词：apathy, indifference, reluctance
词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *kennesse*, from *kene* ("keen, brave, sharp") + *-nesse* (suffix forming abstract nouns).  The root *kene* comes from Old English *cēne* ("bold, brave, sharp"), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kōniz* ("knowing, wise, able").
