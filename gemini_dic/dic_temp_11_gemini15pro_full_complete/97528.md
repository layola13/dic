ingracious:/ɪnˈɡreɪʃəs/ | adj. | 不礼貌的，粗鲁的，不亲切的 | Lacking grace or charm in manner or action; discourteous. |  He made an ingracious remark about her dress. 他对她的着装做出了不礼貌的评价。|  rude, impolite, uncivil, disrespectful, ungracious |  gracious, polite, courteous, civil, respectful | adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + gracious (pleasing, courteous).  The word "gracious" ultimately derives from the Latin "gratia" meaning "grace, favor, thanks."
