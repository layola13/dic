nonconsents:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɛnts/
n.
未同意，不同意；未经同意的事情
Instances of refusing or withholding consent; actions taken without obtaining consent.
The report detailed numerous nonconsents regarding the use of personal data.  这份报告详细列出了许多关于使用个人数据的未同意案例。
The lawyer argued that the nonconsents invalidated the contract. 律师辩称，未经同意使合同无效。
近义词: dissents, refusals, objections
反义词: consents, agreements, approvals
词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "consent" (from Latin consentire, "to feel together," from com- "together" + sentire "to feel")
