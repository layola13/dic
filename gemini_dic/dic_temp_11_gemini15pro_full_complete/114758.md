malpractice: /ˌmælˈpræktɪs/ | n. |渎职；玩忽职守；不法行为| Improper, illegal, or negligent professional activity or treatment, especially by a medical practitioner, lawyer, or public official. | The doctor was sued for malpractice after a patient died during a routine surgery.  这位医生在一例常规手术中病人死亡后被控医疗事故。 |  The lawyer was accused of malpractice for failing to file the necessary paperwork on time.  这名律师因未能按时提交必要的文件而被指控玩忽职守。 |  negligence, misconduct, wrongdoing | good practice, due diligence | noun
