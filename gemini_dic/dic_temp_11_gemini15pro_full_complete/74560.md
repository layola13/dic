fraudulencies:/ˈfrɔːdʒələnsis/
noun
欺诈行为，诈骗行为
Instances of fraud or deception.  Acts of dishonesty intended to deceive.
The investigation uncovered numerous fraudulencies in the company's accounting practices. 这项调查揭露了公司会计实务中的多起欺诈行为。
synonyms:  frauds, deceptions, scams, cheats, swindles
antonyms:  honesties, integrities
n.
