nonrepeaters:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpiː.təz/
n.
非转发器; 非中继器; (尤指电信)单工通信设备;
Devices or systems that do not retransmit or relay signals, especially in telecommunications, often referring to single-hop communication.
The network uses nonrepeaters for direct communication between two points. 该网络使用非转发器在两点之间进行直接通信。
Specialized equipment is needed for long-distance communication without nonrepeaters.  长距离通信若没有非转发器则需要专用设备。

近义词:simplex devices, single-hop devices
反义词:repeaters, relays
