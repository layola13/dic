balkanization:/ˌbɔːlkənɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌbɑːlkənɪˈzeɪʃn/| n. |巴尔干化|The process of dividing a region or body into smaller, mutually hostile states or groups.|The balkanization of the former Yugoslavia led to years of conflict.|fragmentation, division, partition|unification, integration|noun
