falafel:/fəˈlɑːfəl/ (US), /fəˈlæfəl/ (UK)| n. |炸鹰嘴豆丸子，沙拉三明治|A Middle Eastern dish of spiced mashed chickpeas or fava beans formed into balls or patties and deep-fried, often served in pita bread.|Example 1:  I had a delicious falafel sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的炸鹰嘴豆丸子三明治。

Example 2:  The street vendor sells the best falafels in town.  这个街边小贩卖着城里最好吃的炸鹰嘴豆丸子。|近义词：fritter, croquette|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
