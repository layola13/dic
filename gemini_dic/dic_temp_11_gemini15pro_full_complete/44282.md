cronstedtite:/ˈkrɒnstɛdˌtaɪt/ | n. |克朗斯泰德矿|A dark greenish-black mineral, a hydrated iron silicate, belonging to the serpentine group. It is named after the Swedish mineralogist Axel Fredrik Cronstedt (1722–1765).|Cronstedtite is a relatively rare mineral found in metamorphic rocks. 克朗斯泰德矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于变质岩中。

Cronstedtite specimens are prized by collectors. 克朗斯泰德矿标本受到收藏家的珍视。|近义词:chamosite (a similar iron silicate mineral)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
