flounciest: /ˈflaʊnsiɪst/
adjective
最浮华的；最炫耀的
Superlative form of "flouncy":  most characterized by showy or elaborate ornamentation, often suggesting a frivolous or ostentatious display.
She wore the flounciest dress at the party, covered in ruffles and sequins. 她穿着晚会上最花哨的裙子，上面缀满了褶边和亮片。
The actress delivered her lines with the flounciest gestures, clearly playing to the audience.  这位女演员用最夸张的动作念台词，显然是在迎合观众。
近义词: showiest, fanciest, frilliest
反义词: plainest, simplest, most understated
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From flounce (verb) + -y (adjective suffix) + -est (superlative suffix). The verb "flounce" originated in the late 16th century, probably imitative of the sound of a skirt swishing.
