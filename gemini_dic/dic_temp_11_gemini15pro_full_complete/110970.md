liquid:/ˈlɪkwɪd/| n. adj.|液体，液体的|A substance that flows freely but is of constant volume, having a consistency like that of water or oil.|Water is a liquid. 水是液体。|The paint is still liquid. 油漆还是液态的。|近义词：fluid, molten, liquefied|反义词：solid, gaseous|名词，形容词
