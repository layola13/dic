fly-by:/ˈflaɪ baɪ/| n. |飞越；掠过；顺便访问|A flight past a point, especially close to a planet or moon, by a spacecraft to obtain scientific information.|The spacecraft made a fly-by of Jupiter.  宇宙飞船飞越了木星。|The president made a quick fly-by visit to the disaster area. 总统对灾区进行了短暂的访问。|近义词：flyover, pass, visit|反义词: landing| noun

fly-by:/ˈflaɪ baɪ/| adj. |飞越的；顺便访问的|Relating to or involving a fly-by.|a fly-by mission 飞越任务|The senator made some fly-by remarks about the economy. 参议员对经济状况作了一些简短的评论。|近义词：cursory, brief, passing|反义词: thorough, extensive| adjective
