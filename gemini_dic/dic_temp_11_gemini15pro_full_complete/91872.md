hyperinsulinaemia: /ˌhaɪpərɪnsjʊlɪˈniːmiə/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpərɪnsəˈliːniə/ (US)| n. |高胰岛素血症|A condition characterized by excessive levels of insulin circulating in the blood relative to the level of glucose.  It can be a sign of insulin resistance or a tumor of the insulin-producing cells of the pancreas.|Hyperinsulinaemia is a risk factor for type 2 diabetes. 高胰岛素血症是2型糖尿病的危险因素。|Hyperinsulinaemia can lead to weight gain. 高胰岛素血症会导致体重增加。|近义词:  insulin resistance (in some contexts)|反义词: hypoinsulinemia|词性: noun
