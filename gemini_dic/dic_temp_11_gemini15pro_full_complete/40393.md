contradistinctively:/ˌkɒntrədɪˈstɪŋktɪvli/ /ˌkɑːntrədɪˈstɪŋktɪvli/
adverb
独特地，鲜明地，对比鲜明地
in a way that contrasts clearly with something else
Her style of writing is contradistinctively different from her peers. 她的写作风格与她的同龄人截然不同。

The two cultures are contradistinctively different. 这两种文化截然不同。

synonym
distinctly, markedly, characteristically, uniquely, idiosyncratically, peculiarly

antonym
similarly, identically, indistinguishably
