misreliance:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪəns/| n.|过分依赖，过度信赖|The project's failure was due to a misreliance on outdated technology.  这个项目的失败是由于过分依赖过时的技术。|Example:  His misreliance on his GPS led him to a dead end. 他过度依赖GPS导航，结果把他带到了死胡同。|Synonyms: overreliance, overdependence, undue reliance|Antonyms: independence, self-reliance|noun
