foliature:/ˈfoʊliətʃər/ | n. | 叶子；簇叶; (书的)页码 | The arrangement, number, or type of leaves in a plant. Also, the representation of leaves, flowers, and branches in architecture, painting, etc. | The oak tree's foliature was dense and provided ample shade.  这棵橡树的叶子茂密，提供了充足的阴凉。|Synonyms: foliage, leaves, greenery | Antonyms: bareness, defoliation | noun
