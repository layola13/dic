diligency:/ˈdɪlɪdʒəns/
n.|勤奋，勤勉|Careful and persistent work or effort.|She completed the project with remarkable diligence.|近义词: assiduity, industry, application|反义词: laziness, negligence, indolence|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *diligence*, from Old French *diligence*, from Latin *diligentia*, from *diligēns* ("careful, diligent"), present participle of *diligō* ("esteem highly, value, love; attend to diligently, cultivate, cherish"), from *dis-* ("apart, asunder") + *legō* ("choose, gather, collect, read").
