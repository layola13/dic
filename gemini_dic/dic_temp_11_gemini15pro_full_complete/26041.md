bushel:/ˈbʊʃəl/ | n. |蒲式耳（容量单位）|A unit of dry measure equal to 4 pecks or 8 gallons (approximately 36 liters) used for grain, fruit, etc. |The farmer harvested 100 bushels of wheat this year. 今年农民收获了100蒲式耳小麦。| synonym: peck (although smaller unit), dry measure | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) | noun
