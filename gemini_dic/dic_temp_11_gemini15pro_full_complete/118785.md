metallurgical:/ˌmɛtəlˈɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
冶金的；冶金学的
Relating to the science or art of extracting metals from their ores, refining them, and preparing them for use.
The metallurgical properties of the alloy were carefully analyzed.  这种合金的冶金性能经过了仔细的分析。
Synonyms: metal-working, smelting, refining
Antonyms: non-metallurgical
