babiya:/bɑːˈbiːjə/
n.
baby girl, little girl (often used as a term of endearment)
宝贝女孩，小女孩（通常用作爱称）
The father called his daughter "babiya." 这位父亲叫他的女儿“宝贝”。
Synonyms: darling, sweetheart, sweetie, honey, babe
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider) adult, grown-up
词性: noun


babiya:/bɑːˈbiːjə/
interj.
an exclamation expressing surprise or delight
表达惊讶或喜悦的感叹词
"Babiya! Look at that beautiful sunset!" “宝贝！看那美丽的日落！”
Synonyms: Wow, Oh my, My goodness
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for interjections expressing emotion)
词性: interjection


Etymology:  Likely derived from "baby" with an added suffix for endearment.  The specific origin and suffix are uncertain, possibly influenced by regional dialects or other languages.
