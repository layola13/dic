brightens:/ˈbraɪtnz/| v.| (使)变亮，(使)鲜艳，(使)快乐|Makes something lighter or more cheerful; becomes lighter or more cheerful| The sun brightens the sky. 太阳照亮了天空。| synonyms: illuminates, lightens, cheers, enlivens | antonyms: dims, darkens, saddens, depresses | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *beorhtnian, from beorht "bright."  The "-en" suffix is a common way to form verbs from adjectives in English.
