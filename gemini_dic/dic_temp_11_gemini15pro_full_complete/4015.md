ahistoricities:/ˌeɪhɪˌstɔːrɪˈsɪtiz/ | n. | 非历史性，无视历史 | The lack of context and ahistoricities in the presentation made it difficult to understand the significance of the events. 演示中缺乏背景和对历史的无视使得难以理解事件的重要性。 |  Ahistoricities refer to instances of ignoring, distorting, or lacking awareness of historical context, often leading to misinterpretations of the past.  非历史性指的是忽略、歪曲或缺乏对历史背景的认识，往往导致对过去的误解。| Example 1: The movie's ahistoricities angered historians. 这部电影中对历史的歪曲激怒了历史学家们。

Example 2:  Her ahistoricities in analyzing the current political climate led to flawed conclusions.  她在分析当前政治气候时对历史的无视导致了错误的结论。| 近义词: anachronisms, inaccuracies, distortions | 反义词: historical accuracy, contextualization | 词性: 名词
