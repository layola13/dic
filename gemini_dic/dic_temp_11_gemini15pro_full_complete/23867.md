breadwinnings:/ˈbredˌwɪnɪŋz/
n.
生活费；收入；生计
the money that someone earns to support their family
例句：He is the sole breadwinner for his family. 他是一家人的唯一经济支柱。

例句：She worried about how she would earn her breadwinnings after losing her job. 她担心失去工作后如何维持生计。

近义词：income, earnings, livelihood, support
反义词：(No direct antonym.  Consider "expenses," "debts" as conceptually opposite.)
词性: noun (plural)
