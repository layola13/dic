gynandria:/ɡɪˈnændriə/
n.
雌雄同体，雌雄同株
The condition of having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual, especially in plants.
Orchids are well known for their gynandria.  兰花以其雌雄同体而闻名。
The gynandria of this species is a fascinating adaptation. 这个物种的雌雄同体是一种迷人的适应。
hermaphroditism, monoecism
dioecism
noun
