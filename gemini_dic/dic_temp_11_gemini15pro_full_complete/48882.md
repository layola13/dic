defragmenting:/diːfræɡˈmɛntɪŋ/
|v.| 碎片整理；消除分裂|The system is defragmenting the hard drive. 系统正在对硬盘进行碎片整理。|
|v.| 消除分裂；整理|The government is trying to defragment the divided society. 政府正在努力消除分裂的社会。|
|近义词：consolidating, reorganizing, optimizing|
|反义词：fragmenting, scattering, dividing|
|词性：动词|


defragmentation:/diːˌfræɡmɛnˈteɪʃn/
|n.| 碎片整理；消除分裂|Regular defragmentation can improve your computer's performance. 定期碎片整理可以提高计算机的性能。|
|n.| 消除分裂；整理|The defragmentation of the political landscape is a complex process. 政治格局的整理是一个复杂的过程。|
|近义词：consolidation, reorganization, optimization|
|反义词：fragmentation, scattering, division|
|词性：名词|


词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "removal" or "reversal") + "fragment" (from Latin "frangere" meaning "to break") + "-ing" (present participle suffix) / "-ation" (noun suffix).
