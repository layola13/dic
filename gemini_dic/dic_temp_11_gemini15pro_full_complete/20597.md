blackfly:/ˈblækflaɪ/| n.|黑蝇|A small, biting fly, often found near fast-flowing streams or rivers.  Their bites can be painful and itchy.  They are also vectors for certain diseases in some parts of the world. |The campers were plagued by blackflies. 露营者受到黑蝇的困扰。|Synonyms:  buffalo gnat, turkey gnat|Antonyms: None|Noun
