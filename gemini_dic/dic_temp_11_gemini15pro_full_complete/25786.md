bureaucratical:/ˌbjʊrəˈkrætɪkəl/ | adj. | 官僚主义的；繁文缛节的 | Relating to bureaucracy; excessively concerned with procedure and inflexible rules. | The process was slow and bureaucratical. 这个过程缓慢且官僚主义。| The application process was very bureaucratical, requiring multiple forms and signatures. 申请流程非常官僚，需要多种表格和签名。 |  近义词: bureaucratic, red-tape, officious | 反义词: efficient, streamlined, flexible | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bureaucratique*, from *bureaucratie* (bureaucracy) + *-ique* (adjective suffix). Ultimately from *bureau* (desk, office) + Greek *-kratia* (rule, power).
