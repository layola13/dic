frighty:/ˈfraɪti/| adj.|可怕的，吓人的|Causing or experiencing sudden fear; frightening.|例句：The frighty movie made me jump. 这部恐怖电影让我吓了一跳。|近义词：scary, frightening, terrifying|反义词：comforting, reassuring|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From fright + -y.  Fright from Middle English *frighten, from Old English *fyrhtan, from Proto-Germanic *furkhtjaną (“to frighten”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“high, elevated”).  The suffix -y forms adjectives.
