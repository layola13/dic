jesu:/ˈdʒiːzuː/
n.
Jesus
A name for Jesus, often used in hymns and prayers.
Jesu, joy of man's desiring.  耶稣，人类渴望的喜悦。
Synonym: Jesus, Christ
Antonym: None (as it's a proper noun)
noun
