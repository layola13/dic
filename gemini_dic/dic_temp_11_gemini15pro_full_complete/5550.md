alpine:/ˈælpaɪn/| adj.|高山的，阿尔卑斯山的|Relating to high mountains, especially the Alps.  Characteristic of a region above the timberline.|例句：We visited a charming alpine village. 我们参观了一个迷人的高山村庄。

例句：Alpine plants are adapted to harsh conditions. 高山植物适应恶劣的条件。|近义词：mountainous, highland|反义词：lowland|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *alpin*, from Latin *alpīnus* (“of the Alps”), from *Alpēs* (“the Alps”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂el- (“white, bright”)
