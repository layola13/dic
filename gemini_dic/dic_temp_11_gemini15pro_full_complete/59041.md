dwarfy:/ˈdwɔːrfi/ | adj. | 矮小的，矮墩墩的 | Like a dwarf; small and stunted.  Resembling or characteristic of a dwarf. | The bonsai tree was charmingly dwarfy. 这盆栽树矮墩墩的，十分可爱。| The dwarfy shrubs lined the pathway. 这些矮小的灌木排列在小路两旁。 | synonyms: diminutive, miniature, stunted, undersized | antonyms: giant, huge, immense, large, tall | adjective
