crapaudine:/kræpɔːˈdiːn/ /kræpəˈdin/| n.|枢轴承; 鱼肉块;  (烹饪) 蝴蝶鱼|A bearing in which a pivot rests and rotates. A method of preparing fish for cooking by cutting it almost through along the backbone and spreading it open like a book.  A method of butterflying a chicken by cutting it down the backbone.|例句1：The crapaudine in the windmill allowed the sails to turn with the wind. 风车里的枢轴承让帆叶可以随风转动。

例句2：She prepared the trout crapaudine, seasoning it with herbs and lemon. 她用香草和柠檬调味，把鳟鱼做成了蝴蝶状。|近义词：pivot bearing, bearing, butterfly (cooking)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
