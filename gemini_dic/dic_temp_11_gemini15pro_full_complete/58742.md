dungaree:/ˌdʌŋɡəˈriː/| n.|工作裤，粗布工装裤|A type of heavy cotton cloth, typically blue, and also clothing, especially overalls or trousers, made of this material.|He wore his dungarees to work in the garden. 他穿着他的工装裤在花园里干活。|近义词：overalls, jeans|反义词:|noun

dungarees:/ˌdʌŋɡəˈriːz/| n.|工装裤|Trousers, typically blue denim, usually with a bib and shoulder straps.|She bought a new pair of dungarees. 她买了一条新的工装裤。|近义词：overalls, bib overalls|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi डूंगरी (ḍūṅgrī), from Dongri, a village near Mumbai where the cloth was originally made.
