effectuating:/ɪˈfɛktʃueɪtɪŋ/
verb
使生效;实现;实行
bringing about; causing to happen; putting into effect
例句：The company is effectuating a new policy regarding vacation time. 公司正在实施一项新的休假政策。

例句：They are effectuating changes to improve the efficiency of the system. 他们正在实施改革以提高系统的效率。

近义词：implementing, realizing, achieving, executing, accomplishing
反义词：hindering, obstructing, preventing
词性：v.
