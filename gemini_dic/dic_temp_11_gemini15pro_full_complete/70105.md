feverwort:/ˈfiːvərwɜːrt/| n. |寒热草，香蜂草|A herbaceous plant (Tripterygium wilfordii) of the Celastraceae family, native to East Asia, traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat fever, inflammation, and other ailments.  It is also sometimes referred to as threewingnut.|The feverwort was blooming profusely in the garden.|Synonyms: threewingnut, Tripterygium wilfordii|Antonyms: N/A|noun
