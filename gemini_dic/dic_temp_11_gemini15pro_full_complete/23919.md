breakneck:/ˈbreɪkˌnɛk/| adj.| 危险的；飞快的；要命的|dangerously or extremely fast|例句：They drove at breakneck speed. 他们以惊人的速度开车。|近义词：dangerous, fast, rapid, swift, hasty, reckless, precipitate|反义词:slow, leisurely, cautious, safe|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the literal sense of "fast enough to break one's neck."  First recorded in the late 16th century.
