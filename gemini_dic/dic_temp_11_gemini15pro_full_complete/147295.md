overmodestly:/ˌoʊvərˈmɒdɪstli/| adv.|过度谦虚地|In an excessively modest manner; with undue humility. |She overmodestly described her achievements, downplaying her significant contributions. 她过度谦虚地描述了自己的成就，淡化了她重要的贡献。|He overmodestly attributed his success to luck, neglecting to mention his years of hard work. 他过分谦虚地将自己的成功归因于运气，而忽略了他多年的努力。|Synonym: excessively humbly, unduly modestly|Antonym: boastfully, arrogantly|Adverb
