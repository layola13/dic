excepting:/ɪkˈsɛptɪŋ/
preposition|除了...之外|excluding; not including. |Everyone went to the park excepting John. 除了约翰，大家都去了公园。|Synonyms: excluding, but, save, barring, besides, other than |Antonyms: including, with|词性:介词

excepting:/ɪkˈsɛptɪŋ/
conjunction|除了...之外|Used to introduce a statement that forms an exception to one just made.  |He liked all the subjects, excepting math. 他喜欢所有的科目，除了数学。|Synonyms: except, unless, save, but|Antonyms: including, with|词性:连词
