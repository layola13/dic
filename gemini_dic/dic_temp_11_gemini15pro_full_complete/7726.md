anonymization:/əˌnɒnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|匿名化，匿名处理|The process of removing identifying information from data.  This allows data to be used and shared without compromising individual privacy.|例句：Anonymization techniques are crucial for protecting user data. 匿名化技术对于保护用户数据至关重要。

例句：The researchers ensured the anonymization of all participant data before publishing their findings. 研究人员在公布他们的发现之前，确保了所有参与者数据的匿名化。|近义词：de-identification, pseudonymization|反义词：identification|词性:名词
