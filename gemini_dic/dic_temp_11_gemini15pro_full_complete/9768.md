antonomasia:/ˌæntənəˈmeɪʒə/ | n. | 换称，代称 | Substitution of a title, epithet, or descriptive phrase for a proper name, or of a proper name for a quality associated with it. |  Shakespeare is an antonomasia for a great writer.  莎士比亚是伟大作家的代称。

He’s a regular Einstein. 他真是个爱因斯坦（指他很聪明）. | 近义词：metonymy,  synecdoche | 反义词: N/A | noun
