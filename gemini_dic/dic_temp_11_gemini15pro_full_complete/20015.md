biolysis:/baɪˈɒlɪsɪs/
noun
生物分解；生物溶解
The process of decomposition or breakdown of organic matter by living organisms, especially bacteria.
Biolysis of pollutants can be an effective method of environmental remediation.  生物分解污染物可以成为一种有效的环境修复方法。
Biolysis offers a sustainable way to manage organic waste. 生物分解提供了一种可持续的有机废物管理方法。
decomposition, disintegration, breakdown
synthesis, formation
