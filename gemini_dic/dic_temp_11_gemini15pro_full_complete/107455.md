lampost:/ˈlæmpˌpəʊst/ (US) /ˈlæmpˌpəʊst/ (UK)| n. |路灯柱；灯杆|A tall pole with a light at the top, used to illuminate streets and other public areas.|例句：The lampost cast a dim glow on the wet street. 路灯在潮湿的街道上投下昏暗的光芒。

例句：He leaned against the lampost, waiting for his friend. 他靠在路灯柱上，等待他的朋友。

近义词：streetlight, light pole, lamp standard|反义词: (无直接反义词)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *lamp* + *post*.  "Lamp" ultimately derives from the Greek *lampas* (λαμπάς), meaning "torch". "Post" comes from the Latin *postis*, meaning "doorpost" or "post".
