lucerne:/luːˈsɜːn/ /luːˈsɝːn/| n.|紫花苜蓿；卢塞恩（瑞士城市）|A leguminous plant (Medicago sativa) with bluish-purple flowers and trifoliate leaves, grown extensively as fodder and as a soil-improving crop.  Also, a city in Switzerland. |例句：The farmer planted lucerne in the field.  农民在地里种了紫花苜蓿。

例句：Lucerne is a beautiful city in Switzerland. 卢塞恩是瑞士一座美丽的城市。|近义词：alfalfa|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
