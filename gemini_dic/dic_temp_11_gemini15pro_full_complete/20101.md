biosynthesis:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsɪnθəsɪs/
n.
生物合成
The formation of chemical compounds by living organisms.
The biosynthesis of proteins is a complex process. 蛋白质的生物合成是一个复杂的过程。
Scientists are studying the biosynthesis of new antibiotics. 科学家们正在研究新型抗生素的生物合成。
production, synthesis, formation
degradation, decomposition
noun
