collodion:/kəˈloʊdiən/
n.|火棉胶|A syrupy solution of nitrocellulose in ether and alcohol, which evaporates to leave a transparent film.|Collodion was once used as a surgical dressing. 火棉胶曾被用作外科敷料。|
近义词:nitrocellulose solution, pyroxylin solution|反义词:|词性:noun

collodion:/kəˈloʊdiən/
n.|火棉胶|A flammable, syrupy solution of cellulose nitrates in ether and alcohol, used to make photographic plates and as a coating or cement.|The old photographs were made using a collodion process. 这些老照片是用火棉胶工艺制作的。|
近义词:nitrocellulose solution, pyroxylin solution|反义词:|词性:noun
