archipelagos:/ˌɑːrkɪˈpɛləɡoʊz/ (US) /ˌɑːkɪˈpɛləɡəʊz/ (UK)| n. |群岛；列岛|A group or chain of islands clustered close together in a sea or ocean.|The Indonesian archipelago is known for its biodiversity. 印度尼西亚群岛以其生物多样性而闻名。|Example: The Philippines is an archipelago of over 7,000 islands. 菲律宾是一个由7000多个岛屿组成的群岛。|Synonyms: islands, island chain, island group|Antonyms: continent, mainland|noun
