brunette:/bruːˈnɛt/| n. adj.|深褐色头发的女子；深褐色的|A woman with dark brown hair; having dark brown hair.|例句：She's a tall, striking brunette. 她是一位高挑迷人的深褐发女子。|近义词：dark-haired, dark, raven-haired|反义词:blonde, fair-haired|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From French brunette, feminine of brunet (“brown, dark”), diminutive of brun (“brown”), from Frankish *brūn, from Proto-Germanic *brūnaz (“brown”).
