anthologizers:/ænˈθɑːləˌdʒaɪzərz/
n.
选集编纂者，选集编撰人
A person who compiles an anthology, a collection of selected literary pieces.
The anthologizers chose poems from a wide range of authors. 选集编撰者从众多作者中挑选诗歌。
compilers, editors
---
anthologize:/ænˈθɑːləˌdʒaɪz/
v.
编选集，将…编入选集
To compile an anthology; to include in an anthology.
They anthologized the best short stories of the year. 他们将年度最佳短篇小说编成选集。
compile, collect
---
词性: noun (anthologizers), verb (anthologize)
词源: From Greek 'anthologia' meaning "collection of flowers" ->  "collection of literary pieces" -> anthologize (verb) -> anthologizer (noun, agent suffix "-er").
