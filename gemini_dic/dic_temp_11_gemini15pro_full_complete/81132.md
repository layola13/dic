gramophonic:/ˌɡræməˈfɒnɪk/ | adj. | 留声机的；唱机的 | Relating to or characteristic of a gramophone; produced by a gramophone. | The gramophonic sound was distorted. 唱机的声音失真了。| The old gramophonic recording crackled.  老旧的留声机唱片发出噼啪声。 | 近义词: phonographic | 反义词: digital | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From gramophone + -ic.  Gramophone itself comes from Greek *gramma* (letter, writing) + *phone* (sound, voice).
