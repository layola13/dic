citron:/ˈsɪtrən/| n.|香橼；香橼树；香橼色|A pale yellow citrus fruit resembling a lemon, with a thick rind and fragrant peel.  The citron is one of the original citrus fruits from which all other citrus types developed through natural hybrid speciation or artificial hybridization.|The citron is used in many Asian cuisines.|近义词：lemon, citrus|反义词:|名词

citron:/ˈsɪtrən/| adj.|香橼色的；淡黄色的|Having a pale yellow colour.|Her dress was a delicate citron hue.|近义词：lemon-colored, yellowish|反义词:|形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *citron*, from Latin *citrus* (“citrus tree”), related to Greek *κέδρος (kédros)* (“cedar”).
