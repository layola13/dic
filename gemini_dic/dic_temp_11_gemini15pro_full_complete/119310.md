microbarograph:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈbærəˌɡræf/| n. |微气压计|An instrument that records minute changes in atmospheric pressure.|The microbarograph detected a subtle shift in air pressure before the storm. 微气压计在暴风雨来临前探测到气压的细微变化。|Synonyms: barograph, pressure recorder|Antonyms: N/A|noun
Etymology: micro- (small) + baro- (pressure) + -graph (writing instrument)
