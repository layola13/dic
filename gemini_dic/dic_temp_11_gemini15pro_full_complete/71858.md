fleshier:/ˈflɛʃɪər/| adj.|更多肉的；更丰满的|Having more flesh; plumper.|The tomatoes this year are fleshier than last year's. 今年的西红柿比去年的更多肉。|Synonyms: plumper, meatier, fuller|Antonyms: thinner, leaner, bonier|adjective

fleshier:/ˈflɛʃɪər/| adj.|更像肉的；肉感更强的|More like flesh in texture or appearance.|The fake steak had a surprisingly fleshier texture.  这块人造牛排的口感竟然很像肉。|Synonyms: meaty, pulpy|Antonyms: sinewy, bony|adjective
