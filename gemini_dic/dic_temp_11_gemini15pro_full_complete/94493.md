imported:/ɪmˈpɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 进口的，输入的 | Brought in from a foreign country. |  The store sells imported cheeses. 这家商店出售进口奶酪。|  Imported goods often face tariffs. 进口商品通常面临关税。| 近义词: foreign, overseas | 反义词: domestic, native, indigenous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the Latin "importare"  (to bring in), from "in-" (in) + "portare" (to carry).
