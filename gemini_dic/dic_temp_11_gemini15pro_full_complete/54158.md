disentanglement:/dɪsɪnˈtæŋɡlmənt/ | n. | 解开；理清；摆脱 |The act of disentangling something, or the state of being disentangled.  The process of separating things that have become joined or confused. |  The disentanglement of the two companies took months of negotiation.  两家公司的 disentanglement 花费了数月的谈判。

The archaeologist worked carefully on the disentanglement of the ancient necklace from the skeleton. 考古学家小心翼翼地将古代项链从骸骨上 disentanglement 出来。 | 近义词: separation, unraveling, extrication | 反义词: entanglement, involvement, complication | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + entangle + -ment (forming nouns of action or process). "Entangle" comes from the Old French "entangler" meaning "to entrap in nets".
