如果您想了解"what"这个词，我可以提供以下解析：

what: /wɑːt/ (US) /wɒt/ (UK) | pron. det. adj. adv. | 什么，多么，何等 | used to ask for information about people or things |

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？


近义词: which, how
反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word.)
词性: pronoun, determiner, adjective, adverb
