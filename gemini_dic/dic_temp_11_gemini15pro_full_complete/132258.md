nondecorously:/ˌnɒndɪˈkɔːrəsli/ | adv. | 不得体地，不雅观地 | In a way that lacks decorum; improperly or indecently. | She behaved nondecorously at the formal dinner, talking loudly and interrupting others.  她在那场正式晚宴上的举止不得体，大声喧哗还打断别人讲话。 | 近义词: indecorously, improperly, unseemly | 反义词: decorously, properly, appropriately | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + decorous (adjective meaning "in keeping with good taste and propriety") + -ly (adverb suffix)
