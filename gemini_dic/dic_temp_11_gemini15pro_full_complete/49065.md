dehumanizing:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/
adj.
使丧失人性的；非人化的
Depriving of human qualities; making somebody less human
例句：
The dehumanizing conditions in the prison led to a riot.  监狱里不人道的环境引发了一场暴动。

Working on the assembly line can be a dehumanizing experience. 在流水线上工作可能是一种失去人性的体验。

近义词：degrading, brutalizing, inhumane
反义词：humanizing, personalizing, individualizing
词性: adjective


dehumanize:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪz/
verb
使丧失人性
To deprive of human qualities, personality, or spirit.
例句：
War can dehumanize people, making them see the enemy as objects rather than individuals. 战争会使人丧失人性，使他们把敌人看作物体而不是个人。

The propaganda campaign sought to dehumanize the opposing side.  宣传活动试图使对方失去人性。

近义词：degrade, brutalize, objectify
反义词：humanize, personalize
词性: verb


词源:
From de- + humanize, from Late Latin humanizare "to make human," from Latin humanus "human" (see human).
