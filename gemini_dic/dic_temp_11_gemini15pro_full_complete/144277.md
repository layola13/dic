outsourcing:/ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋ/| n.|外包，外购|The practice of obtaining goods or services from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source.|Many companies are outsourcing their IT operations to reduce costs. 许多公司正在外包其IT运营以降低成本。|近义词: subcontracting, offshoring|反义词: insourcing|词性:名词
