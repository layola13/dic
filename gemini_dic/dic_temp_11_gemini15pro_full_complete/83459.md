habituation:/həˌbɪtʃuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 习惯化；适应 |  The process by which a response to a stimulus decreases with repeated exposure to that stimulus. |  After a few weeks, the dog showed habituation to the loud noises from the construction site.  几周后，这条狗对建筑工地传来的巨大噪音表现出了习惯化。 | acclimation, adaptation, adjustment | sensitization | noun
