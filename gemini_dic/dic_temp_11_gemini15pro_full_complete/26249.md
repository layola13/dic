例句：He called me a butthead for forgetting his birthday. 他因为我忘了他的生日叫我笨蛋。

近义词：jerk, idiot, moron, fool, dummy, dolt, bonehead, airhead, nimrod, knucklehead|反义词:genius, intellectual|词性:noun
