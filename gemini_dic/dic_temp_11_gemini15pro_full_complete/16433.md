bare-up: /beər ʌp/
| vi. | 振作起来；打起精神 | to become more cheerful or resilient, especially after a period of sadness or difficulty |  After the loss of his job, he struggled to bare up.  他失业后，努力振作起来。| 近义词: cheer up, perk up, brighten up | 反义词: break down, lose heart | 动词

bare-up: /beər ʌp/
| n. | (非正式) 争吵；争执 | a quarrel or fight |  They had a terrible bare-up last night.  他们昨晚大吵了一架。| 近义词: quarrel, argument, fight, row | 反义词: agreement, harmony, reconciliation | 名词

词源:  "bare up" 的动词含义来自 "bear up"，意思是 "to endure hardship or adversity with courage and resilience"。 "bear" 在这里的意思是 "to carry or support a heavy load"，引申为 "to endure"。 "up"  则增加了 "resisting collapse or giving way" 的含义。 作为名词，"bare-up" 可能是 "bear up" 的变体，也可能与 "flare-up"（突然爆发）有联系，表示情绪的突然爆发。
