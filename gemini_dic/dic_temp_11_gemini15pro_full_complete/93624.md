imbitterer:/ɪmˈbɪtərər/
n.|使人痛苦的人或事物|Something or someone that causes bitterness.
|The constant criticism from her boss was the imbitterer of her work life.|她老板不断的批评是她工作生活中痛苦的根源。
|近义词: bane, curse, affliction, scourge|
|反义词: comforter, solace, blessing|
|词性: 名词|

imbitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/
vt.|使怨恨；使痛苦|To make bitter; to arouse bitter feelings in.
|His betrayal imbittered their friendship.|他的背叛使他们的友谊变得痛苦。
|近义词: sour, poison, embitter, alienate|
|反义词: sweeten, improve, reconcile|
|词性: 动词|
