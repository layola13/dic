drippingly:/ˈdrɪpɪŋli/
adverb
滴水地；湿漉漉地
in a dripping manner; so wet as to drip
The rain was drippingly falling from the eaves. 雨水湿漉漉地从屋檐落下。
近义词：wetly, soggily
反义词：dryly
词性：副词

词源：From drip + -ing + -ly.  "drip" comes from Old English *dryppan, of Germanic origin.
