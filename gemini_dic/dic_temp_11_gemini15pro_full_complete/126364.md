myricin:/ˈmɪrɪsɪn/
noun
蜂蜡醇; 菌醇
A waxy substance consisting chiefly of myricyl palmitate, obtained from beeswax.  It is insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents.
Beeswax contains approximately 70-80% myricin.  蜂蜡含有大约70-80%的蜂蜡醇。
Myricin is used in some cosmetics and pharmaceuticals.  蜂蜡醇用于一些化妆品和药品。
synonyms: myricyl palmitate
antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
