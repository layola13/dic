myxomatized:/ˌmɪksəʊmətaɪzd/ | adj. |粘液瘤化的，患粘液瘤的 | Affected with myxomatosis.  Relating to or resembling myxoma.  |  The myxomatized rabbit population declined rapidly.  粘液瘤化的兔子数量迅速下降。

The farmer discovered a myxomatized rabbit in his field. 农民在他的田里发现了一只患粘液瘤的兔子。| 近义词: diseased, infected | 反义词: healthy, unaffected | 词性: 形容词


词源:  Derived from "myxoma" (a benign tumor of connective tissue) + "-atize" (to affect with or make into) + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  最终来自希腊语 "myxa" (mucus) + "-oma" (tumor suffix).
