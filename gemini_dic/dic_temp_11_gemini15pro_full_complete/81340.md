graphics: /ˈɡræfɪks/ | n. | 图形; 图表; 制图学; 图形艺术 |  Visual presentations of information, such as charts, diagrams, or images, often created or manipulated using computer software. graphics: /ˈɡræfɪks/ | adj. | 图形的; 图表式的 | Relating to visual presentations of information or the use of diagrams and images. |  He studied graphic design in college. 他在大学学习平面设计。 | synonyms: visual, pictorial, illustrative | antonyms: textual, verbal | adjective
