moky:/ˈmoʊki/| adj.| 冒烟的，烟雾弥漫的| Filled with or resembling smoke; smoky. | The air was thick and moky after the fire. 火灾过后，空气又浓又呛。 | 近义词：smoky, hazy, foggy | 反义词：clear, transparent | 词性:形容词

词源:  Likely a variant or dialectal form of "smoky," which comes from the Old English "smocian" (to smoke).
