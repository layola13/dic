overprized: /ˌoʊvərˈpraɪzd/ | adj. | 价格过高的，估价过高的 | Priced too high; costing more than it is worth. |  This restaurant is so overprized; the food is mediocre at best. 这家餐厅价格太高了；食物充其量也就是中等水平。| Example: The handbag was overprized, considering its poor quality.  这个手提包定价过高，考虑到它的质量很差。 | Synonyms: overpriced, exorbitant, expensive, inflated | Antonyms: underpriced, cheap, bargain, reasonable | adjective
