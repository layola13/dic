falser:/ˈfɔːlsər/| adj.|更假的，更不真实的|Comparative form of "false."  Indicates a greater degree of falsehood or untruthfulness.  It's used to compare two things, where one is more untrue than the other.|This claim is even falser than the last one. 这个说法比上一个更假。|近义词：more untrue, more deceptive, more fabricated|反义词: truer, more accurate, more genuine|词性:adjective
词源:From Middle English *falsere, comparative of fals, from Old French fals, faus, from Latin falsus, past participle of fallō (“deceive”).
