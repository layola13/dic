gyrfalcon:/ˈdʒɜːrfɔːlkən/ | /ˈdʒaɪrfɔːlkən/ | n. |  矛隼，猎隼 | A large, powerful falcon (Falco rusticolus) found in Arctic and subarctic regions, the largest falcon species. It is known for its speed and agility in flight, historically used in falconry. |  The gyrfalcon plummeted from the sky, seizing its prey in its talons.  矛隼从天而降，用爪子抓住了猎物。| synonym: falcon, hawk, peregrine | antonym: prey | noun
