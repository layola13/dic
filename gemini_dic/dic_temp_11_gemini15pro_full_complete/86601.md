heliolite:/ˈhiːliəˌlaɪt/| n. |日光石，太阳石|A translucent variety of oligoclase feldspar, exhibiting a reddish metallic glitter due to the presence of minute scales of hematite or goethite.|He found a beautiful piece of heliolite on the beach. 他在沙滩上发现了一块美丽的日光石。|近义词：sunstone|反义词: N/A|noun
