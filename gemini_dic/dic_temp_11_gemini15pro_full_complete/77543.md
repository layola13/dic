g'day:/ɡəˈdeɪ/
interjection
你好；日安 (Australian informal)
A greeting used in Australia.
G'day, mate! How are you doing? 你好，伙计！你好吗？
G'day, everyone! Welcome to the barbecue.  大家好！欢迎来到烧烤派对。
近义词: hello, hi, howdy (especially in American English)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a greeting.  One could consider farewells like goodbye as a contrasting concept.)
词性: interjection
