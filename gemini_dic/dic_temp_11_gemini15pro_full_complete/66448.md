exemplifier:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪər/ | n. | 例证者；示范者 | someone or something that serves as an example |  This painting is an exemplifier of the artist's skill. 这幅画是这位艺术家技巧的例证。| synonym: example, illustration, model | antonym: counter-example, exception | noun

exemplify:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪ/ | v. |  举例说明；示范；作为…的例子 | to be or give a typical example of something |  His works exemplify the best of 18th-century prose. 他的作品是18世纪散文的最佳范例。 | synonym: illustrate, demonstrate, represent | antonym: obscure, contradict | verb
