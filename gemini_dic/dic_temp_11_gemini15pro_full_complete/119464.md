micrometeorite: /ˈmaɪkroʊˈmiːtiːəˌraɪt/ | n. | 微陨石 | A very small meteorite, typically less than a millimeter in size, usually originating from comets or asteroids.  |  Micrometeorites constantly bombard the Earth's atmosphere.  微陨石不断轰击地球大气层。| synonym: cosmic dust, space dust | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "macro-meteorite" or "large meteorite") | noun
