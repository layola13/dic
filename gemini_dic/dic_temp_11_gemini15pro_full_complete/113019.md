lumbricoid:/ˌlʌmbrɪˈkɔɪd/| adj. n.|蛔虫样的；蛔虫|Resembling or pertaining to a roundworm, especially an intestinal roundworm of the genus Ascaris.|This parasite is lumbricoid in shape.  这种寄生虫的形状像蛔虫。|The veterinarian found lumbricoid worms in the dog's intestines.  兽医在狗的肠道里发现了蛔虫。|近义词：ascarid, helminthic, vermiform|反义词: None (lack of a direct opposite)|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: From Latin *lumbricus* (earthworm) + *-oid* (resembling).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wel-* (to turn, wind).
