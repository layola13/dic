diphthongises:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪzɪz/ /daɪˈfθɔŋɡaɪzɪz/
verb
使成双元音；把…变成双元音
changes a single vowel sound into a diphthong.
This dialect diphthongises the vowel in "house".  这种方言把“house”中的元音变成了双元音。
近义词:  vocalizes
反义词: monophthongizes
词性: v.
