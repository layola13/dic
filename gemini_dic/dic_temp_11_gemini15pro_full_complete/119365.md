microcontinental:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌkɑːntɪˈnɛntəl/ | adj. | 微陆块的；微大陆的 | Relating to or characteristic of a microcontinent; a fragment of continental crust that has become isolated from a larger continent. |  The Seychelles are a microcontinental fragment. 塞舌尔是一个微陆块碎片。 |  The geological history of this region suggests a microcontinental origin.  这个地区的地质历史表明它起源于微陆块。| Near synonyms: continental fragment, island arc,  | Antonyms: oceanic, abyssal | adjective
