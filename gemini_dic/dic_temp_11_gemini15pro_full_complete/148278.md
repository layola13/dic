oversoaked: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊkt/
adjective
过度浸泡的；湿透的
Excessively soaked; saturated beyond normal limits.
The towels were oversoaked and dripping onto the floor. 毛巾湿透了，水滴到了地板上。
The oversoaked fields were unsuitable for planting.  过度浸泡的田地不适合播种。
近义词: saturated, drenched, soaked, waterlogged
反义词: dry, arid, parched
词性: adjective

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "soaked" (past participle of "soak," meaning "to immerse in liquid")
