lacerating:/ˈlæsəreɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 撕裂的；痛苦的；尖刻的|Causing deep emotional or physical pain, tearing flesh.|The soldier suffered a lacerating wound in battle.  这个士兵在战斗中遭受了撕裂的伤口。

The movie's lacerating portrayal of grief resonated with many viewers.  这部电影对悲伤的深刻刻画引起了许多观众的共鸣。|近义词：agonizing, harrowing, excruciating,  tormenting,  searing|反义词：soothing, comforting, healing, pleasant|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin lacerare "to tear to pieces, mangle," from lacer "torn, mangled."
