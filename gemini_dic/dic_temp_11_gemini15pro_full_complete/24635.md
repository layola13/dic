brokerages:/ˈbroʊkərɪdʒɪz/
n.
经纪业务；佣金；经纪公司
The business of a broker; a fee or commission charged by a broker; a brokerage firm.
Many online brokerages offer low commission fees. 许多在线经纪公司提供低佣金费用。
The brokerage charged a 2% commission on the sale. 这家经纪公司收取了2%的销售佣金。
synonyms: commissions, fees, brokerage firms, brokerage houses
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but one could consider "direct sales" or "in-house sales" as conceptually opposite)
noun
