nonadventitiously: /ˌnɒnˌædvənˈtɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 非偶然地；固有地；天然地 | Not adventitious; inherent, intrinsic, essential.  Existing naturally and not because of some external factor. | The plant's deep roots are nonadventitiously formed, a characteristic of its species.  这种植物的深根是天然形成的，是其物种的特征。

The color of her eyes is nonadventitiously blue, not from colored contacts. 她的眼睛是天然的蓝色，不是因为戴了彩色隐形眼镜。 | 近义词: inherently, intrinsically, naturally, essentially | 反义词: adventitiously, extrinsically, accidentally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + adventitious (coming from outside; not inherent) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Adventitious ultimately comes from the Latin word "adventicius," meaning "coming from outside."
