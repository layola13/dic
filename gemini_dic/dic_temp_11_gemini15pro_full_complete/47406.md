deadliest:/ˈdedliɪst/| adj.|最致命的，死亡人数最多的|Causing or capable of causing the most deaths.  The deadliest animal in the world is the mosquito.|例句：The Black Death was one of the deadliest pandemics in history. 黑死病是历史上最致命的流行病之一。

例句：This is the deadliest weapon ever created. 这是有史以来最致命的武器。|近义词：most lethal, most fatal, most dangerous|反义词：safest, least harmful|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From Old English *dēadlīc (attested only as dēadlīce "mortally"), from dēad "dead" +‎ -līc "-ly". Cognate with Old Frisian dādlik, Old Saxon dōdlik, Middle Dutch dōdelijc (Dutch doodelijk), Old High German tōdlih (German tödlich), Old Norse dauðligr.
