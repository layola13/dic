gouts:/ɡaʊts/| n.|痛风发作; 一阵(强烈情感); (尤指坏事)一阵, 一场|A sudden outburst of something, especially of bad feeling or of something unpleasant.  A drop or spot, as of blood, rain, etc. A fit of ill temper. An attack of gout.|He suffers from gouts of arthritis. 他患有关节炎的阵痛发作。|近义词：attacks, bursts, fits, spells|反义词:relief, calmness|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *gote (Modern French goutte), from Late Latin gutta (“drop”). Related to guttate.
