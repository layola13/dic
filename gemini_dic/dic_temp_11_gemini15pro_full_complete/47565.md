debacle:/deɪˈbɑːkl/ /dɪˈbɑːkl/| n.|崩溃；惨败；溃败|A sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco.|The company's new product launch was a complete debacle.|近义词：fiasco, disaster, catastrophe, failure, collapse|反义词：success, triumph|词性:名词

debacle:/deɪˈbɑːkl/ /dɪˈbɑːkl/| n.|解冻；(尤指河流的)冰凌融化|The breakup of ice in a river.|The spring debacle caused flooding in the valley.|近义词：thaw, breakup|反义词：freeze|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French débâcle, from débâcler (“to unbar, clear”), from dé- (“un-”) + bâcler (“to bar, obstruct”). Ultimately from Frankish *baklan (“to bar”).
