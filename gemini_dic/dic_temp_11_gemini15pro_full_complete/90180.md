hotpressed:/ˈhɑːtˌprɛst/ | adj. | 热压的，热压成型的 | Made smooth and glossy by pressing between hot plates or rollers.  This process is often used for paper, cardboard, and wood products. | The hotpressed paper had a smooth, almost waxy surface. 热压纸表面光滑，几乎像蜡一样。 | The manufacturer uses a hotpressed method to create durable laminate flooring. 制造商使用热压方法来制造耐用的强化木地板。 | 近义词: calendered, pressed, smooth | 反义词: rough, textured | 词性: adjective

词源:  "hot" + "pressed" -  A straightforward compound word derived from the process of pressing something while hot.
