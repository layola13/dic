missell: /mɪsˈsɛl/ | v. | 射失，错过，未击中 | To fail to hit, reach, or attain something aimed at. |  He misselled the target by a mile. 他射失了目标，差之千里。|  She misselled the opportunity to study abroad. 她错过了出国留学的机会。 | 近义词：miss, fail, overlook | 反义词：hit, achieve, obtain | 词性: 动词

词源:  From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + sell (Old English sellan "to give," later "to give in exchange for money").  Originally meant "to mis-give" or "to give wrongly," later evolved to mean "to fail to hit" or "to miss."
