agorae:/ˈæɡɔːraɪ/
n.
集市，广场，古希腊的公民大会
A public open space used for assemblies and markets, especially in ancient Greece.
The philosophers would often debate in the agorae. 哲学家们经常在广场上辩论。
近义词：marketplace, forum, plaza
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "private space")
词性: noun
