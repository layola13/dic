backlighting:/ˈbækˌlaɪtɪŋ/| n.|背光；逆光；背景光|The backlighting made it difficult to see the subject's face. 背光使得很难看清拍摄对象的脸。|例句：The photographer used backlighting to create a dramatic effect. 摄影师用逆光来营造戏剧化的效果。

The backlighting of the stained glass window made the colors vibrant. 彩色玻璃窗的背光使颜色鲜艳。
