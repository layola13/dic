allowing:/əˈlaʊɪŋ/ | v. |允许，准许；考虑到|Present participle of "allow."  It means giving permission for something to happen or to take something into consideration. |例句：
The new rules are allowing more flexibility in working hours. 新规定允许工作时间更加灵活。

He succeeded by allowing himself to learn from his mistakes. 他允许自己从错误中学习，从而获得了成功。


|近义词：permitting, granting, enabling, acknowledging, considering |反义词: forbidding, prohibiting, denying, rejecting, disallowing |词性: Verb (present participle)
