galoshe:/ɡəˈlɑːʃ/ /ɡəˈlɒʃ/| n. |套鞋，橡胶套鞋|A waterproof overshoe, typically made of rubber.|例句：I put on my galoshes before going out in the rain.  我出门淋雨前穿上了套鞋。

例句：She wore bright yellow galoshes to keep her feet dry. 她穿着亮黄色的套鞋以保持双脚干燥。|近义词：overshoe, rubber, wellie|反义词: (无明确反义词)|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *galoche*, from Occitan *galòcha* "wooden shoe, clog", perhaps from Low Latin *calopedes* "shoe worn by a monk", from Greek *kálopodes* "wooden shoes".
