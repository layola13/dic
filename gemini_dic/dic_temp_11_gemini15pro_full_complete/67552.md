extraneousness:/ˌɛkˈstreɪniəsnəs/| n. | 外来性；无关性；不相干 |The extraneousness of the information made it difficult to understand the main point.  这些无关紧要的信息使得理解要点变得困难。|例句：The extraneousness of the details made the story confusing.  这些不相关的细节使故事变得令人困惑。|近义词：irrelevance, immateriality, unimportance, inapplicability |反义词：relevance, pertinence, importance, significance |词性:noun

extraneous:/ɛkˈstreɪniəs/| adj. | 外来的；无关的；不相干的 |  We need to eliminate all extraneous information from the report. 我们需要从报告中删除所有无关信息。 | 例句：Extraneous noises disturbed my concentration. 外来的噪音干扰了我的注意力。| 近义词：irrelevant, immaterial, unimportant, inapplicable | 反义词：relevant, pertinent, important, significant |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin extraneus "external, foreign," from Latin extra "outside of."  来自晚期拉丁语extraneus“外部的，外国的”，源自拉丁语extra“在……之外”。
