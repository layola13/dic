Etymology:
From Late Latin *barbarismus, from Ancient Greek βαρβαρισμός (barbarismós, “foreign mode of speech, barbarism”), from βαρβαρίζω (barbarízō, “to speak like a foreigner”), from βάρβαρος (bárbaros, “foreign, barbarous”).
