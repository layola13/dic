outspies:/ˌaʊtˈspaɪz/
verb
胜过…的侦察能力; 比…更善于侦察
to spy more effectively than
例句：The CIA outspied the KGB during the Cold War. 中央情报局在冷战期间的侦察能力胜过克格勃。

例句：She outspied all her rivals in the business world.  她在商界比所有竞争对手都更善于侦察。
近义词：surpasses in espionage, outmaneuvers in intelligence gathering
反义词：is outspied by, is surpassed in espionage by
词性：v.
