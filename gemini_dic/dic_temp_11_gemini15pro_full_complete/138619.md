nucleate:/ˈnjuːklɪeɪt/ | v. | 成核；使成核 | to form a nucleus; to gather or be gathered around a nucleus |  Dust particles can nucleate raindrops in clouds. 灰尘颗粒可以在云中使雨滴成核。 | Synonyms:  cluster, aggregate, coalesce | Antonyms: disperse, scatter | verb

nucleate:/ˈnjuːklɪeɪt/ | adj. | 有核的 | having a nucleus | The nucleated red blood cells are immature. 有核的红细胞是不成熟的。 | Synonyms: nuclear | Antonyms: anuclear | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nucleatus*, past participle of *nucleare* "to form a kernel," from *nucleus* "kernel, inner part"
