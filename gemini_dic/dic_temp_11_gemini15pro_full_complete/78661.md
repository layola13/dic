gieseckite: /ˈɡiːsɪkaɪt/ | n. |吉斯凯克特，一种假橄榄石 | A pseudomorph of biotite after nepheline, typically greenish-gray or brownish-green in color. It's a variety of pinite.  It is named after Sir Charles Giesecke, a German mineralogist. |  Gieseckite was found in the Greenland rocks. 吉斯凯克特在格陵兰的岩石中被发现。| The specimen contained gieseckite and other minerals.  该标本包含吉斯凯克特和其他矿物。 |Synonyms: pinite, liebenerite | Antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the surname of Sir Charles Lewis Giesecke (1761–1833), German-born mineralogist and naturalist +‎ -ite (suffix forming names of minerals).
