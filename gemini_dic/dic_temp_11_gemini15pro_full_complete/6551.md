amylolytic:/ˌæmɪloʊˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
淀粉分解的;  淀粉水解的
Relating to or causing the hydrolysis of starch.
Amylolytic enzymes break down starch into simpler sugars. 淀粉分解酶将淀粉分解成更简单的糖。
The amylase enzyme is amylolytic in its action. 淀粉酶在其作用中具有淀粉分解性。
近义词:starch-digesting, diastatic
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "anti-amylolytic" or words describing processes that synthesize starch)
