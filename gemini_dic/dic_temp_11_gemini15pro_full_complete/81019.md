graduality:/ɡrædʒuˈælɪti/
n.|逐渐性，渐进性|The graduality of the change made it almost imperceptible.  这种变化的逐渐性使得它几乎难以察觉。|例句：The graduality of the temperature increase is concerning. 气温的逐渐升高令人担忧。|近义词：progression, development, phasing, step-by-step process|反义词：suddenness, abruptness, immediacy|词性: noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *gradualitas, from Latin gradualis ("by degrees") + -ity.  Ultimately from Latin gradus ("step").
