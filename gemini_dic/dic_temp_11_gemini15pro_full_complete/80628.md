goosefish:/ˈɡuːsfɪʃ/| n. |鮟鱇|A type of fish known for its unusual appearance and predatory habits.|The goosefish camouflages itself on the ocean floor. 鮟鱇鱼在海底伪装自己。

例句：We caught a goosefish while deep-sea fishing. 我们在深海捕鱼时捕获了一条鮟鱇鱼。|近义词：angler, monkfish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: "goose" + "fish".  The name likely comes from the observation that geese sometimes prey on these fish, or from the fish's wide, gaping mouth resembling a goose's beak.
