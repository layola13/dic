de-democratize:/diː.dɪˈmɒkrətaɪz/ | v. | 去民主化，逆民主化 | To reverse the process of democratization; to take away democratic rights or freedoms. |  The military coup de-democratized the country, suppressing free speech and dismantling parliament.  军事政变使该国去民主化，压制言论自由并解散议会。 |  The new laws seemed to de-democratize the electoral process, making it harder for opposition parties to compete. 新法律似乎使选举过程去民主化，使反对党更难参与竞争。 | 近义词: undermine democracy,  authoritarianize,  tyrannize | 反义词: democratize, liberalize | 词性: 动词
