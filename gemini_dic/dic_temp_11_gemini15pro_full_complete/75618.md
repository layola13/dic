fugged:/ˈfʌɡd/| adj.|糟糕的，混乱的，被搞砸的|A slang term used to describe something that is messed up, ruined, or in a bad state.|例句：This project is completely fugged.  这个项目完全搞砸了。

例句：My computer is totally fugged; I can't even turn it on. 我的电脑彻底坏了；我甚至都开不了机。|近义词：screwed, messed up, ruined, broken, damaged|反义词：fixed, repaired, working, perfect|词性:形容词
词源:Likely a euphemism derived from "fucked," softening the harsher term while retaining its meaning of something being ruined or in a bad state.
