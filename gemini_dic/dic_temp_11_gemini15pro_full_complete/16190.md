banks:/bæŋks/| n. |银行；岸；堤；堆；大量|A financial institution that accepts deposits and makes loans.|例句：I need to go to the banks to deposit some money. 我需要去银行存一些钱。

|例句：Several international banks have their headquarters in this city.  许多国际银行的总部都在这座城市。
|近义词：financial institution, shore, riverside, mound, heap, pile, a lot, plenty|反义词：debtor (for financial institution),  |词性:名词

banks:/bæŋks/| v. |(使)倾斜；(使)堆积；筑(堤)；存(款)于银行|To tilt or cause to tilt; to pile up; to deposit money in a bank.|例句：The airplane banked sharply to the left. 飞机猛地向左倾斜。

|例句：She banks her salary every month. 她每个月都把工资存入银行。
|近义词：tilt, incline, lean, pile, heap, deposit|反义词：straighten (for tilt), withdraw (for deposit)|词性:动词

词源: From Old Norse *banki (“slope, ridge”), from Proto-Germanic *bankiz (“slope, mound”). Related to bench.
