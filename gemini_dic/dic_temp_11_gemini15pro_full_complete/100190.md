interrenal:/ɪntəˈriːnəl/ | adj. | 肾上腺的，肾间的 | Situated between the kidneys; relating to the adrenal glands, specifically the adrenal cortex in lower vertebrates. |  The interrenal tissue produces corticosteroids. 肾间组织产生皮质类固醇。 |  Interrenal cells are crucial for osmoregulation in fish. 肾间细胞对鱼类的渗透调节至关重要。 | synonyms: adrenal, suprarenal | antonyms: N/A | adjective
