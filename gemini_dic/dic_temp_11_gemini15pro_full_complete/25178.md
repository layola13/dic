bud:/bʌd/| n. vi.|芽，蓓蕾；花蕾；未成熟的人或物|A small swelling on a plant stem from which a shoot, leaf, or flower develops.  A person or thing that is not yet fully developed.|The roses are in bud. 玫瑰正在含苞待放。|近义词：sprout, shoot, germ|反义词:flower, blossom|noun
bud:/bʌd/| vi.|发芽，萌芽|To begin to grow or develop.|The trees are budding early this year. 今年树木发芽早。|近义词：sprout, germinate|反义词:wither, die|verb
