fruitarians:/fruːˈteəriənz/
|n.|果食主义者|People who eat only fruit.  Those who follow a diet consisting solely of fruits (including nuts and seeds, depending on the definition).|Example 1: The fruitarians held a festival celebrating the summer harvest. 果食主义者举办了一个庆祝夏季丰收的节日。

Example 2:  My friend is a fruitarian and never eats anything that comes from an animal. 我的朋友是一位果食主义者，从不吃任何来自动物的东西。|近义词:vegan, vegetarian|反义词:carnivore, omnivore|noun


Etymology:
From fruit + -arian (a suffix forming nouns meaning "one who adheres to or practices" a particular doctrine, system, etc.).  The word "fruit" comes from Old French, ultimately from Latin "fructus."
