antieconomic:/ˌæntiˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk/, /ˌæntiˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪk/ | adj. | 反经济的；不经济的 | Not economical; wasteful or unprofitable.  Preventing or hindering economic activity or progress. |  The government's antieconomic policies led to a recession. 政府的反经济政策导致了经济衰退。 |  uneconomical, wasteful, unprofitable, inefficient |  economical, profitable, efficient | adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposite of. From Greek _anti_.
economic (adjective): relating to the process or system by which goods and services are produced, sold, and bought. From Greek _oikonomikos_, from _oikos_ "house" + _nomos_ "managing."
