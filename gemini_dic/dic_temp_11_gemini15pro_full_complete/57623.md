draughtiest:/ˈdrɑːftiɪst/| adj.|通风最强的；最冷的|Superlative adjective of draughty: exposed to draughts of air; chilly.|The draughtiest room in the house was the attic. 房子里通风最强的房间是阁楼。|近义词：airiest, chilliest, coldest|反义词：warmest, snuggest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From *draught* + *-y* (suffix forming adjectives) + *-est* (suffix forming superlative adjectives).  *Draught* comes from Old English *dragan* "to draw, pull, drag".
