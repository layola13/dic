ionizations:/ˌaɪənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 电离，离子化 | The process by which an atom or molecule acquires a negative or positive charge by gaining or losing electrons | The ionization of the gas created a plasma.  气体的电离产生了等离子体。| synonyms: charging, electrification | antonyms: neutralization, deionization | noun
