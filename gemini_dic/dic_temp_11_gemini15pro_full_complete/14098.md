auteurisms:/oʊˈtɜːrɪzəmz/ | n. | 作者论风格，导演个人风格 |  Characteristic stylistic features or thematic obsessions of a film director, seen as analogous to those of an author of a literary work.  |  His later films are full of the auteurisms that made him famous.  他后期的电影充满了让他成名的作者论风格。 | synonyms:  idiosyncrasies,  mannerisms, stylistic trademarks, signatures | antonyms:  conventions, norms | noun
