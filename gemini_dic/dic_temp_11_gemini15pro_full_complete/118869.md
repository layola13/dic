metascientifically:/ˌmɛtəˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪkli/
adverb
元科学地
In a way that relates to the philosophical study of the nature, aims, methods, and limits of science.
Metascientifically examining the assumptions underlying scientific inquiry can reveal hidden biases. 元科学地检验科学探究背后的假设可以揭示隐藏的偏见。
Synonyms: philosophically, epistemologically
Antonyms: empirically, practically
词性: 副词
