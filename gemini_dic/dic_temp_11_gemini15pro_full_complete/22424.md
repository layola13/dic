bookbinding:/ˈbʊkˌbaɪndɪŋ/
noun
装订；书籍装帧
The art or process of binding books.
例句：The bookbinding on this old volume is exquisite. 这本古书的装订精美绝伦。

例句：He learned bookbinding from his grandfather. 他向他的祖父学习装订书籍。

近义词：binding
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "unbinding" in a metaphorical sense)
词性:n.
