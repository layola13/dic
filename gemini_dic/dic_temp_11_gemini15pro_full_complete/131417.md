nonclient:/ˌnɑːnˈklaɪənt/ | adj. | 非客户的，非委托人的 | Not belonging to or intended for the ordinary users or customers of a particular piece of software or hardware, but rather for the programmers or developers. |  The nonclient area of a window includes the title bar, menu bar, and borders.  窗口的非工作区包括标题栏、菜单栏和边框。| Near synonyms: internal, developer, system | Antonyms: client, user, customer | adjective

nonclient:/ˌnɑːnˈklaɪənt/ | n. | 非客户，非委托人 | A person who is not a client or customer. | The event was not open to nonclients.  该活动不对非客户开放。| Near synonyms: outsider, non-member | Antonyms: client, customer, member | noun
