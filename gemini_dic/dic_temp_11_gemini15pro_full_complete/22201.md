bombylious:/bɒmˈbɪliəs/| adj.|像熊蜂的，嗡嗡响的|Resembling or characteristic of a bumblebee; making a buzzing sound.|The bombylious insect hovered near the flower.  像熊蜂的昆虫在花朵附近盘旋。|Synonyms: buzzing, humming, droning|Antonyms: silent, quiet|adjective

bombylious:/bɒmˈbɪliəs/| adj.|吵闹的，喧闹的 (figurative, less common)|Loud and boisterous (used figuratively, and less common).|The bombylious crowd cheered for their team. 吵闹的人群为他们的球队欢呼。|Synonyms: loud, noisy, boisterous|Antonyms: quiet, peaceful|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bombylius, from Ancient Greek βομβύλιος (bombylios, “bumblebee”), from βόμβος (bombos, “a deep, hollow sound; buzzing”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰom- (“to hum, buzz”).
