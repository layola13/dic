canter:/ˈkæntər/ (US), /ˈkæntə(r)/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|慢跑，骑马慢跑，使慢跑|A canter is a three-beat gait of a horse, slower than a gallop but faster than a trot.  As a verb, it means to ride or cause a horse to canter.|The horse broke into a canter. 马开始慢跑。 |He cantered his horse along the beach. 他骑着马沿着海滩慢跑。|Synonyms: lope, jog (for horses), trot (slightly slower)|Antonyms: gallop, run (for horses), walk (much slower)|noun, verb

canter:/ˈkæntər/ (US), /ˈkæntə(r)/ (UK)| vi. vt.|慢跑，骑马慢跑，使慢跑|To move at a moderate gallop; especially of a horse.|The horse cantered around the field. 马绕着田野慢跑。|She cantered her pony along the path. 她骑着小马沿着小路慢跑。|Synonyms: lope, jog (for horses), trot (slightly slower)|Antonyms: gallop, run (for horses), walk (much slower)|verb
