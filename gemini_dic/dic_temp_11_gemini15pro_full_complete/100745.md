intransitives:/ɪnˈtrænsɪtɪvz/| adj. |不及物的|Not taking a direct object; designating a verb or verb construction that does not require or cannot take a direct object to complete its meaning.|The verb "sleep" is intransitive.  睡觉这个动词是不及物动词。|The birds fly south for the winter. 鸟儿们冬天飞往南方。|近义词：ergative|反义词:transitive|词性:形容词
