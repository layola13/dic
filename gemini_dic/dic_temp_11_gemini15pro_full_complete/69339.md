fedary:/ˈfɛdəri/ | n. | 同谋；共犯；帮凶 |  A person who is a partner or accomplice in a crime or wrongdoing. |  He was charged as a fedary in the robbery. 他被指控为抢劫案的同谋。| synonyms: accomplice, confederate, accessory, abettor | antonyms: opponent, adversary | noun

fedary:/ˈfɛdəri/ | adj. | (古)封建的；受封的  | Relating to or characteristic of feudalism or a feudal system. | The fedary lords held their lands from the king.  受封的领主从国王那里获得土地。(这个用法已过时，很少使用) | synonyms: feudal | antonyms: N/A | adjective
