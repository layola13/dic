agnosia:/æɡˈnoʊʒə/ /æɡˈnoʊziə/ | n. |失认症|Loss of the ability to interpret sensory stimuli, such as sounds or images, though the senses themselves are functioning normally. This is usually caused by brain damage.|
The stroke left him with agnosia, and he could no longer recognize familiar faces. 中风使他患上了失认症，他再也认不出熟悉的面孔了。

He suffered from visual agnosia and couldn't identify common objects. 他患有视觉失认症，无法识别常见的物体。

|近义词：inability to interpret sensations, sensory deficit|反义词: perception, recognition|词性: noun
