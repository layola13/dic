adenoid:/ˈædɪnɔɪd/ (US) /ˈædənɔɪd/ (UK)| n. adj.|腺样体; 增殖体; 鼻咽扁桃体|A mass of lymphoid tissue situated at the very back of the nose, where the nose joins the throat.  Serving a similar purpose to the tonsils, adenoids help the body fight infection.  They are largest in children, often shrinking considerably or disappearing altogether by adulthood.|例句：The doctor recommended removing the child's adenoids. 医生建议切除孩子的腺样体。

例句：He had his adenoids out when he was a child. 他小时候做了腺样体切除手术。|近义词：tonsil, pharyngeal tonsil|反义词: (无明确反义词)|词性: 名词、形容词 (罕用作形容词，意为“腺样的”)
