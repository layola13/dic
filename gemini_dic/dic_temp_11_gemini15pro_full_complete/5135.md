allegretto:/ˌæləˈɡrɛtoʊ/ | adj. adv. n. | 小快板，稍快的 | Moderately fast; quicker than andante but not as fast as allegro.  A piece of music in this tempo. | The second movement of the symphony is an allegretto.  这首交响曲的第二乐章是小快板。| 近义词: briskly, moderately fast | 反义词: lento, adagio | 词性: adjective, adverb, noun | 词源: Italian, diminutive of allegro 'cheerful, brisk' < Latin alacer 'lively'.
