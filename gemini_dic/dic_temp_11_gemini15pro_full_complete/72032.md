flippantness:/ˈflɪpəntnəs/| n. |轻率；轻浮；油嘴滑舌|The flippantness of his response angered the judge. 他的轻率回答激怒了法官。|例句：His flippantness about the situation was inappropriate. 他对这种情况的轻浮态度是不合适的。

例句：Her flippantness cost her the job. 她的轻浮让她丢了工作。|近义词：frivolity, levity, impertinence|反义词：seriousness, gravity, respectfulness|词性：名词

Etymology:
From flippant + -ness. Flippant from flip (v.) + -ant.  Flip (v.) probably imitative, perhaps related to Middle Dutch *flippen "to fly quickly."
