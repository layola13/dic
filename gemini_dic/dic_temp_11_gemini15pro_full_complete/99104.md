intercardinal:/ˌɪntərˈkɑːrdɪnl/
adjective
中间的，介于主方位之间的
Situated between the cardinal points.
例句：The intercardinal directions are northeast, southeast, southwest, and northwest. 中间方向是东北、东南、西南和西北。

例句：The wind blew from an intercardinal direction. 风从一个中间方向吹来。
近义词：intermediate, middle
反义词：cardinal
词性：adjective
