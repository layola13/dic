insectiferous:/ˌɪnsekˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
昆虫携带的；多昆虫的
Carrying or teeming with insects.
例句：The insectiferous jungle was teeming with mosquitoes.  这片充满昆虫的丛林里满是蚊子。

例句：The insectiferous plant attracted various insects. 这株携带昆虫的植物吸引了各种昆虫。

近义词：insect-ridden, infested
反义词：insect-free, clean

词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *insectífer*, from *insectum* (insect) + *-fer* (carrying).
