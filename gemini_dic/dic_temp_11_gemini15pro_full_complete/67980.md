facsimile:/fækˈsɪməli/ | n. | 传真，摹本，复制品 | An exact copy or reproduction of something, such as a document, book, or work of art. |  例句：This book contains a facsimile of the original manuscript. 这本书包含了原稿的复制品。

例句：Please send me a facsimile of the signed contract. 请把签署的合同的传真件发给我。


近义词：copy, replica, duplicate, reproduction | 反义词：original | 词性：noun

facsimilist:/fækˈsɪməlɪst/ | n. |  复制者，临摹者| A person who makes facsimiles. | 例句：The facsimilist meticulously recreated the ancient map.  这位复制者一丝不苟地临摹了这幅古老的地图。

例句：The museum hired a facsimilist to reproduce the damaged painting.  博物馆聘请了一位临摹者来复制这幅受损的画作。


近义词：copier, reproducer | 反义词：N/A | 词性：noun
