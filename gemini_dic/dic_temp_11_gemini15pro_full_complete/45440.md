accuracies:/ˈækjərəsiz/
|n.|准确度，精确性|The plural form of "accuracy," referring to multiple instances of being correct or precise.  It signifies the degree to which a result or measurement conforms to the correct value or a standard.|The accuracies of the measurements were verified by independent testing. 这些测量的准确性已通过独立测试验证。|近义词:precisions, exactnesses, correctness|反义词:inaccuracies, errors, imprecisions|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *accuratia, from Latin accūrātus (“prepared with care, exact, elaborate”), past participle of accūrō (“I take care of, attend to with care”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + cūra (“care”).
