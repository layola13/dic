gustily:/ˈɡʌstɪli/| adv.|猛烈地，阵风似地|Characterized by strong gusts of wind; in a gusty manner. |The wind blew gustily, rattling the windows.  风猛烈地吹着，窗子嘎嘎作响。|近义词：fiercely, violently, strongly|反义词:gently, calmly|词性:副词
词源: From gust + -ily.  From gust (n.), from Old Norse gustr "cold blast of wind."
