lumpinesses:/ˈlʌmpinəsɪz/
|n.|块状；肿块；粗糙|The lumpinesses in the sauce were unappetizing. 酱汁里的块状物让人倒胃口。|lumpiness的复数形式，表示多个块状或肿块。|The gravy had unpleasant lumpinesses. 肉汁里有令人不快的块状物。|Synonyms: bumps, lumps, nodules, swellings|Antonyms: smoothness, evenness|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From lump + -i- + -ness + -es.  "Lump" comes from Middle English lumpe, of uncertain origin, possibly Scandinavian. "-i-" is a connecting vowel. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being." "-es" is the plural suffix.
