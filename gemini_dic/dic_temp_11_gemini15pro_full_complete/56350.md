dogwood:/ˈdɒɡwʊd/ (British), /ˈdɑːɡwʊd/ (American)| n.|山茱萸，山茱萸木|A flowering tree or shrub of the genus Cornus, typically having small flowers surrounded by four large, showy bracts that are often mistaken for petals.  The wood is also known for its hardness.|The dogwood trees are in full bloom. 山茱萸树盛开着。|Synonym: cornel|Antonym: N/A|Noun


dogwood:/ˈdɒɡwʊd/ (British), /ˈdɑːɡwʊd/ (American)| adj.|山茱萸的，山茱萸木制的|Made from the wood of a dogwood tree.|He has a beautiful dogwood walking stick. 他有一根漂亮的山茱萸木手杖。|Synonym: cornelian (for the wood)|Antonym: N/A|Adjective
