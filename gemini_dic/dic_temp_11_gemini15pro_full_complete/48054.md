declaimers:/dɪˈkleɪmərz/
n.
雄辩家，朗诵者，高谈阔论者
a person who declaims; a person who speaks rhetorically and bombastically.
The declaimers filled the hall with their booming voices.  雄辩家们用洪亮的声音充满了大厅。
The political declaimers rallied the crowd. 政治演说家们激励了群众。
近义词: orators, speakers, rhetoricians
反义词: listeners, audience, silent ones
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin declamator, from Latin declamare (“to declaim”), from de- (“down, completely”) + clamare (“to cry out, shout”).
