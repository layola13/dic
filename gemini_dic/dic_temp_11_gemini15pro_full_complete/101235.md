inviting:/ɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 吸引人的，诱人的 | Appealing, attractive, tempting, and making one want to participate or be involved. |  The smell of freshly baked bread was inviting.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味很诱人。

The restaurant had an inviting atmosphere. 这家餐厅的氛围很吸引人。 | 近义词: appealing, attractive, tempting, alluring | 反义词: uninviting, repellent, off-putting | 词性: 形容词
