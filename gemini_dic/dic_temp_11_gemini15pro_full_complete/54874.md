displeasurably:/dɪsˈplɛʒərəbli/
adverb
不愉快地，令人不快地
in a way that causes displeasure or discomfort.
He frowned displeasurably at the news. 他不高兴地皱着眉头看着新闻。
The meeting ended displeasurably. 会议不欢而散。
近义词:unpleasantly, disagreeably
反义词:pleasurably, enjoyably
词性:副词
