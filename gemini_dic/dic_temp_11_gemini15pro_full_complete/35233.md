clumper:/ˈklʌmpər/| n.|丛生植物；形成团块之物；笨人|Something that clumps or forms clumps; a clumsy person. |The irises were clumpers and needed dividing every few years. 鸢尾花丛生，需要每隔几年分株一次。|Near synonyms: cluster, bunch,  |Antonyms: singleton, individual |noun

clump:/klʌmp/| n. vi. vt.|团块，丛，簇；沉重的脚步声；使成团块；结块|a compact mass; a heavy tread; to form into a clump; to tread heavily|The snow fell in soft clumps. 雪花纷纷扬扬地飘落。|Near synonyms: cluster, bunch, mass, lump, |Antonyms: scattering, dispersal |noun, verb
