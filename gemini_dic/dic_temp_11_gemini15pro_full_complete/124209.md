moshing: /ˈmɒʃɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːʃɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 猛撞，撞人舞 |The act of dancing in a violent or aggressive way in which participants push, shove, and slam into each other.| The concert was so wild, there was a huge mosh pit with everyone moshing. 这场音乐会太狂野了，有一个巨大的撞人舞池，每个人都在跳撞人舞。|近义词：slam dancing, body surfing|反义词: slow dancing|词性:名词

Etymology:  Back-formation from mosh pit, which is of uncertain origin, possibly related to the verb mash.
