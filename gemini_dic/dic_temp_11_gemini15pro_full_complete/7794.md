answering-back:/ˈɑːnsərɪŋ bæk/ /ˈænsərɪŋ bæk/ | n. |顶嘴，回嘴|The act of replying rudely or defiantly to someone in authority. |Example 1: The teacher didn't tolerate any answering-back from her students. 老师不容忍学生有任何顶嘴的行为。
