apotheosized: /əˈpɒθiːəˌsaɪzd/ | v. | 神化；将…尊奉为神；极度赞扬 | To elevate to the status of a god; to idolize or glorify to an excessive degree. | Elvis Presley was apotheosized by his fans.  埃尔维斯·普雷斯利被他的粉丝们奉为神明。|  exalted, glorified, deified | denigrated, vilified | Verb

apotheosis: /əˌpɒθiˈəʊsɪs/ | n. | 神化；典范；完美典型 | The elevation of someone to divine status; the highest point in the development of something; culmination or climax. |  The Roman emperors often claimed apotheosis after their deaths. 罗马皇帝通常在死后要求被神化。 | culmination, climax, peak, zenith | nadir, decline | Noun
