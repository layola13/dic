hellfires:/ˈhɛlˌfaɪərz/| n.|地狱之火；烈火；极大的痛苦|A destructive fire or attack, often used figuratively to describe intense suffering or punishment.  It evokes images of intense heat and torment associated with the Christian concept of hell.|The dictator rained hellfires down upon the rebel city. 独裁者向叛军城市倾泻炮火。|The loss of her child caused her unimaginable hellfires. 失去孩子给她带来了难以想象的痛苦。|Synonyms: inferno, blaze, conflagration|Antonyms: paradise, bliss, heaven|noun
