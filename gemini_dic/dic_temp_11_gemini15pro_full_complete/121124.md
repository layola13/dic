misdeed:/ˌmɪsˈdiːd/
n.
不端行为，恶行，罪行
An act that is criminal or bad.
He confessed to all his misdeeds. 他坦白了他所有的罪行。

近义词：crime, offense, wrongdoing, transgression, sin
反义词：good deed, virtue, kindness

词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *misdede, from mis- + deed.
