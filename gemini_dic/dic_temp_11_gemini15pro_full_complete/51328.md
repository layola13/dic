detat:/ˈdeɪtɑː/ /dɪˈtæt/
noun
分遣队, 支队, 小分队
A detachment is a group of soldiers, vehicles, ships, or aircraft, that have been sent away from the main military force, usually for a particular mission.

The army sent a small detachment to guard the bridge.
军队派出一支小分队守卫桥梁。

A detachment of marines was sent to secure the embassy.
一支海军陆战队分遣队被派去保卫大使馆。


近义词:unit, group, squad, contingent
反义词:main body, main force
词性:n.
