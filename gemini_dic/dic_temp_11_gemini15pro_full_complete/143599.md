outinfluenced:/ˌaʊtˈɪnflʊənst/
verb
超过…的影响力
To influence someone or something more strongly than another influence.
The charismatic leader outinfluenced his rivals and secured the nomination.  这位充满魅力的领导人对竞争对手的影响力更大，从而获得了提名。
The new marketing campaign completely outinfluenced the previous one, leading to a significant increase in sales.  新的营销活动的影响力完全超过了之前的活动，带来了销量的显著增长。
Synonyms: surpass, outweigh, eclipse, overshadow
Antonyms: be overshadowed by, be outweighed by
word type:verb
