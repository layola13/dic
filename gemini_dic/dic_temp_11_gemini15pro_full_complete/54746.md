disparity's:/dɪˈspærətiz/| n.| 差异，不等，悬殊 |The disparity's between the rich and the poor is a serious social problem. 贫富差距是一个严重的社会问题。| disparity's is the possessive form of "disparity", meaning "belonging to disparity" or "of disparity".  It refers to something that is associated with or caused by a difference or inequality. For example, "The disparity's impact on education" means the impact that the difference/inequality has on education.| The disparity's effects on healthcare access are devastating. 这种差异对医疗保健获取的影响是毁灭性的。| 近义词：difference, inequality, gap, imbalance | 反义词：similarity, equality, parity, balance | 词性: 名词 (possessive)
