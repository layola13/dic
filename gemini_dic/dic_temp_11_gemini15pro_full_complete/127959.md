neoprenes:/ˈniːəˌpriːnz/ | n. |氯丁橡胶；氯丁橡胶制品|A synthetic rubber that is resistant to oils, heat, and weathering, used in wetsuits, electrical insulation, and other products. |例句：Neoprenes are commonly used in wetsuits because of their excellent insulation properties. 氯丁橡胶通常用于潜水衣，因为它具有优异的隔热性能。

例句：The company manufactures a range of neoprenes for various industrial applications. 这家公司生产各种用于不同工业用途的氯丁橡胶。|近义词：polychloroprene, chloroprene rubber|反义词: None|词性: Noun
