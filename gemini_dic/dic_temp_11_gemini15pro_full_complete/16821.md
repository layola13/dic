basenet:/ˈbeɪsˌnɛt/| n.|基础网络，基准网络|A neural network architecture that is pre-trained on a large dataset and can be fine-tuned for specific tasks.  It serves as a starting point for transfer learning.|Example: We used ResNet50 as the basenet for our image classification model. 我们使用 ResNet50 作为我们图像分类模型的基础网络。|Example:  The basenet was trained on ImageNet and then fine-tuned on a smaller dataset of medical images.  这个基础网络在 ImageNet 上进行预训练，然后在较小的医学图像数据集上进行微调。|Synonym: base model, pre-trained model|Antonym: N/A|noun
