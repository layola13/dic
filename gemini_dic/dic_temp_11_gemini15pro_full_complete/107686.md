lankiest:/ˈlæŋkiɪst/| adj.|最高的，最瘦长的|Tall, thin, and ungainly.|例句：He was the lankiest boy in the class. 他是班上最高最瘦的男孩。|例句：The lanky teenager towered over his peers.  这个瘦高的少年比他的同龄人都高。|近义词：tall, gangly, spindly, lathy|反义词:short, stout, stocky|词性:形容词

词源:
From lank + -y + -est.  "Lank" comes from Middle English *lanke, from Old Norse *slǫkkr meaning "slack, loose."  The "-y" suffix forms adjectives meaning "having the quality of," and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
