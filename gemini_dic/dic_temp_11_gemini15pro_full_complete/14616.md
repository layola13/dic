avocat:/ˈævəkɑːt (US), ˈævəkət (UK)| n.| 牛油果; 律师|A creamy, green fruit with a large seed. Also, a lawyer in some French-speaking countries. |例句：I love avocado toast for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃牛油果吐司。

例句：In France, an avocat represents clients in legal matters. 在法国，avocat代表客户处理法律事务。|近义词：(fruit) alligator pear; (lawyer) attorney, lawyer, barrister, solicitor|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


avocat:/avɔka/ (French)| n.| 律师|A lawyer in France and other French-speaking countries. |例句：Mon avocat m'a conseillé de plaider non coupable.  My lawyer advised me to plead not guilty. 我的律师建议我做无罪辩护。
