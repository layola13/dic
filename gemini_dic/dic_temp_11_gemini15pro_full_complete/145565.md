overcomplacency:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmˈpleɪsənsi/
|n.|自满，过度自满
|A feeling of smug satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements.  An excessive or unwarranted feeling of contentment or self-satisfaction, often leading to a lack of awareness of potential problems or dangers.|
|The team's overcomplacency after their initial victory led to their downfall in the later rounds.  该队在最初的胜利后过于自满，导致他们在后来的几轮比赛中失利。|

|Their overcomplacency about security allowed the hackers to easily breach the system.  他们对安全的过度自满让黑客轻易地攻破了系统。|
|近义词：smugness, self-satisfaction, complacency|
|反义词：anxiety, vigilance, concern, worry|
|词性：noun|
