barometrograph:/ˌbærəˈmɒtrəɡrɑːf/| n.|气压计，气压记录器|An instrument that automatically records variations in atmospheric pressure.|The barometrograph showed a steady drop in pressure, indicating an approaching storm. 气压计显示气压持续下降，预示着暴风雨即将来临。|Synonyms: barograph|Antonyms: N/A|noun
