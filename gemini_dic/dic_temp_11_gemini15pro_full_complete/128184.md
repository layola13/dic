neuralgia:/nʊˈræl.dʒə/ /nʊˈræl.dʒi.ə/ | n. | 神经痛 |  A sharp, stabbing pain or burning sensation that occurs along a nerve.  | Example 1: The trigeminal neuralgia caused excruciating pain in her face.  她患有三叉神经痛，面部剧烈疼痛。

Example 2: He suffered from post-herpetic neuralgia after a bout of shingles. 他在带状疱疹发作后患上了带状疱疹后神经痛。 | 近义词: nerve pain | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of pain) | 词性: noun
