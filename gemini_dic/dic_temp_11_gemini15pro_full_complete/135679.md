nonprescient:/ˌnɒnˈprɛʃɪənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈprɛʃɪənt/
adjective
非先知的，没有预见的
Lacking foresight; unable to foresee the future.
The nonprescient CEO failed to anticipate the market crash.  这位没有预见的首席执行官未能预料到市场崩盘。
The economist's nonprescient analysis completely missed the impending recession. 这位经济学家的非先见性分析完全错过了即将到来的经济衰退。
Synonyms: unforeseeing, shortsighted, myopic, unprophetic
Antonyms: prescient, prophetic, farsighted, visionary
词性: adjective
