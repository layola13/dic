forebeam:/ˈfɔːrbiːm/
n.
前横梁，前车架
The part of a vehicle's chassis located at the front.
The forebeam of the truck was damaged in the collision.  卡车的前横梁在碰撞中受损了。
synonyms: front chassis member, front frame section
antonyms: rear beam, rear chassis member
noun
