galvanizer:/ˈɡælvənaɪzər/ | n. |镀锌工，电镀器|A person or company that galvanizes metal. | The galvanizer coated the steel with zinc to prevent rust.  镀锌工在钢上镀了一层锌以防生锈。 |  近义词:  plater, coater | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/ | v. |镀锌，激励，刺激 | To coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc. To shock or excite someone, typically into taking action. |  The news galvanized the public into action. 这条新闻激励了公众采取行动。 | 近义词: stimulate, excite, arouse, energize, inspire, stir, prod, spur, goad | 反义词: demotivate, discourage, depress | 词性: verb
