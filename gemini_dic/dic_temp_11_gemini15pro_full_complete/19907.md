binturong:/bɪnˈtʊərɒŋ/ (US) /bɪnˈtjʊərɒŋ/ (UK)| n.|熊狸|A nocturnal mammal (Arctictis binturong) of Southeast Asia, related to civets and genets, that has a prehensile tail and shaggy black fur.|The binturong, also known as the bearcat, is surprisingly agile in the trees. 熊狸，也被称为熊灵猫，在树上出奇的敏捷。|The binturong carefully climbed through the branches. 熊狸小心翼翼地在树枝间攀爬。|Near synonyms: bearcat, Asian bearcat|Antonyms: None|Noun
