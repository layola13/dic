lamellarly:/ləˈmelərli/
adv.
成薄片地，层状地
in the form of thin plates or layers; in a lamellar structure
The mineral cleaves lamellarly. 这种矿物成薄片状分裂。
近义词: layered, stratified, foliated
反义词: unlayered, massive
词性: 副词

lamella:/ləˈmelə/
n.
薄片，薄层
a thin layer, scale, or plate-like structure
The gills of a mushroom are composed of numerous lamellae.  蘑菇的菌褶由许多薄片组成。
近义词: layer, scale, plate
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "mass")
词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *lamella*, diminutive of *lamina* ("thin plate, leaf, layer").
