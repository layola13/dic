grumbly: /ˈɡrʌmbli/ (US) /ˈɡrʌmbli/ (UK)| adj.|脾气暴躁的，爱抱怨的|Inclined to grumble; complaining.|He was grumbly all morning because he didn't get enough sleep.|近义词：grouchy, grumpy, cantankerous|反义词：cheerful, good-natured|词性:形容词
Etymology: From grumble + -y.  Grumble comes from Middle English *grumbelen, frequentative of grumen "to murmur, complain," of imitative origin.
