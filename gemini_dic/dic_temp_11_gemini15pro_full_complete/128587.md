nice-as-pie:/naɪs əz paɪ/| adj.|非常友好的，和蔼可亲的，|Extremely pleasant and agreeable, often unexpectedly so.  Implies a superficial sweetness that may conceal other motives. |She was nice-as-pie to me, even after I spilled coffee on her. 她对我非常友好，即使我把咖啡洒在她身上之后也是如此。|近义词：sweet, pleasant, agreeable, amiable, charming|反义词:nasty, unpleasant, rude, mean|adjective
