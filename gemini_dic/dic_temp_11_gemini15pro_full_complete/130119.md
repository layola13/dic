non-random:/ˌnɒnˈrændəm/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈrændəm/ (UK)| noun.|非随机性|The state or quality of not being random.|The non-randomness of the data suggests a systematic bias.|Synonyms: intentionality, purposefulness|Antonyms: randomness, chance|noun
