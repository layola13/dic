ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin malignantia "malice, wickedness," from malignantem (nominative malignans), present participle of Latin malignari "to do maliciously, plot against," from male "badly" (from PIE root *mel- (1) "bad, badly") + gignere "to beget, produce" (from PIE root *gen- "give birth, beget").
