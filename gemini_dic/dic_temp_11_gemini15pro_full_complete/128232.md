neuroelectric:/ˌnʊroʊɪˈlɛktrik/ | adj. | 神经电的；神经电性的 | Relating to bioelectricity in the nervous system. | Neuroelectric signals are essential for brain function. 神经电信号对于大脑功能至关重要。|  Neuroelectric activity can be measured with an EEG. 可以用脑电图测量神经电活动。|  Synonyms: neuroelectrical, neuronal, neural | Antonyms: None | adjective

Etymology->
neuro- (prefix): from Greek "neuron," meaning "nerve"
electric (suffix): from Latin "electricus," meaning "of amber" (referring to static electricity)
