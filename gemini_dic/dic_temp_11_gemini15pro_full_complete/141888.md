ordaining:/ɔːrˈdeɪnɪŋ/
verb
任命；授予圣职；注定；命令
conferring holy orders upon someone.
例句：He was ordained as a priest last year. 他去年被任命为牧师。

例句：Fate had seemed to ordain that they would never meet again. 命运似乎注定他们永远不会再见面。


近义词：appoint, commission, decree, destine, order
反义词：dismiss, discharge
词性：v.
