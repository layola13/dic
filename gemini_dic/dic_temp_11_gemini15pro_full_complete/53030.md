diode:/ˈdaɪoʊd/ (US) /ˈdaɪəʊd/ (UK)| n. |二极管|A semiconductor device with two terminals, typically allowing current to flow in one direction only.  It acts as a one-way valve for electric current.|The diode allows current to flow from the anode to the cathode.  这个二极管允许电流从阳极流向阴极。|Synonyms: rectifier, valve|Antonyms: N/A|noun
