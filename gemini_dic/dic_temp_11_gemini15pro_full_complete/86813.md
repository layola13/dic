hematite:/ˈhiːmətaɪt/ (US), /ˈhɛːmətaɪt/ (UK)| n. |赤铁矿|A common iron oxide mineral, Fe2O3, reddish-brown to black or silver-gray in color, occurring in a variety of forms and being an important ore of iron.|The hematite deposit is a valuable resource for the region.|Synonyms: iron ore, red ochre, specularite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
