ambuscadoed:/ˌæmbəˈskeɪdəd/| v. |伏击，埋伏|To attack from a concealed position; to ambush.  It describes the action of having been ambushed.|例句：The soldiers were ambuscadoed in the valley. 士兵们在山谷中遭到伏击。

例句：The travelers were ambuscadoed by bandits. 旅行者被土匪伏击了。|近义词：ambushed, waylaid, trapped, attacked|反义词:protected, defended|词性:动词
