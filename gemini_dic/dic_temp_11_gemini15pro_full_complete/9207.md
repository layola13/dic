antiorthodoxies: /ˌæntiˈɔːrθədɒksiz/ (British) /ˌæntiˈɔːrθədɑːksiz/ (American)
|n.|非正统观念，异端| Beliefs or practices that challenge or oppose established orthodoxies.|The artist's work was a celebration of antiorthodoxies, rejecting traditional artistic conventions.|近义词: heterodoxies, dissents, heresies|反义词: orthodoxies, conformities|词性: noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
orthodox (adjective/noun): conforming to established doctrines, especially in religion (from Greek orthos 'right' + doxa 'opinion')
-y (suffix): forming nouns of quality or condition
-es (suffix): plural form
