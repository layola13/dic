canthoplasty:/ˈkænθəˌplæsti/| n.|外眦成形术|Surgical repair of the canthus, the corner of the eye.  It is a procedure to lengthen the palpebral fissure (eye opening) by surgical division of the outer canthus.|The patient underwent canthoplasty to correct her drooping eyelids.|近义词：canthotomy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek *kanthos* (corner of the eye) + *-plasty* (surgical repair or reconstruction).
