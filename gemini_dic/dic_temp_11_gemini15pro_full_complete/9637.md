antistrophon:/ænˈtɪstrəfən/ | n. | 反唱，反strophe |  A rhetorical device in which the speaker turns the tables on an opponent by using their own argument against them.  It's essentially taking someone's words and turning them back on them. |  "You accuse me of being too aggressive, but isn't it you who started this argument?" is an example of antistrophon. 你指责我太咄咄逼人，但不是你挑起这场争论的吗？这是一个反唱的例子。|  retort, counter-argument, rebuttal |  agreement, concession | noun
