malacologist:/ˌmæləˈkɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 软体动物学家 | A scientist who studies mollusks, a large phylum of invertebrate animals including snails, slugs, clams, mussels, octopuses, and squid. |  She is a renowned malacologist specializing in the study of land snails. 她是一位著名的软体动物学家，专门研究蜗牛。| synonym:  conchologist (more focused on shells),  invertebrate zoologist | antonym: N/A | noun
