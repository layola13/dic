nonreusable:/ˌnɒnriːˈjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 不可重复使用的 | Not able to be used again. |  Disposable nappies are convenient but nonreusable. 一次性尿布很方便，但不可重复使用。

This coffee cup is nonreusable. 这个咖啡杯是一次性的。 | 近义词：disposable, single-use | 反义词: reusable, recyclable | 词性:形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "reusable" (from "re-" (prefix meaning "again") + "use" + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being"))
