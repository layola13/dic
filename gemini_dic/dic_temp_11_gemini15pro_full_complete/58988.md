duty-bound:/ˈdjuːti baʊnd/| adj.|有义务的，义不容辞的|Morally or legally obligated to do something.  Bound by a sense of duty.|例句：As a doctor, she felt duty-bound to help the injured. 作为一名医生，她觉得有义务帮助伤者。

例句：He was duty-bound to report the crime to the police. 他有义务向警方报告这起犯罪行为。|近义词：obligated, compelled, required|反义词：free, exempt, optional|词性：形容词
词源：From "duty" (from Old French "deu," meaning "due" or "owed") + "bound" (from Old Norse "búinn," meaning "prepared" or "ready").  The sense evolved to mean "constrained by duty."
