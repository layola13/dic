bobs:/bɑːbz/ | n. | 短发，齐短发;  (垂钓用的)浮子，鱼漂 | Short haircuts, often cut straight around the head; floats used in fishing. |  She got a new hairstyle with cute bobs. 她剪了个可爱的波波头。| Synonym: bob haircuts, short hair | Antonym: long hair | noun

bobs:/bɑːbz/ | v. | (使)上下快速移动，(使)轻拍，轻叩; 剪短(头发) | To move quickly up and down; to tap or pat lightly; to cut hair short. | The fishing bob bobbed gently on the water. 鱼漂在水面上轻轻地上下摆动。 | Synonym: bounce, dip, dab, crop | Antonym: remain still, grow | verb
