mistuned:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːnd/ | /ˌmɪsˈtuːnd/ | adj. | 走调的，失谐的 | Incorrectly tuned; out of tune.  | The mistuned piano made the song sound awful. 走调的钢琴使这首歌听起来很糟糕。 | The guitarist played a few mistuned chords before realizing his instrument needed adjusting. 吉他手弹了几个走调的和弦才意识到他的乐器需要调整。 | 近义词: out of tune, discordant, inharmonious | 反义词: tuned, harmonious, in tune | 词性: 形容词

词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + tuned (past participle of "tune")
