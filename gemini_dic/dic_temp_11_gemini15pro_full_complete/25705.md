bunn:/bʌn/
n.|小圆面包，小甜饼|A small, sweet, usually round cake.|I had a delicious cinnamon bunn for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个美味的肉桂小圆面包。|近义词：roll, muffin, scone|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bunne*, perhaps from Old North French *buigne*, from Vulgar Latin *būgnea*, feminine of *būgneus*, from Latin *būlla* (“bubble, knob, boss”). Cognate with French *beignet* (“fritter”).
