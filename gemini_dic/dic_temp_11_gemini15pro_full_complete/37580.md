communalizing:/kəˈmjuːnəlaɪzɪŋ/

communalizing:/kəˈmjuːnəlaɪzɪŋ/
| adj. |  公有化的；共有的；集体化的 (Rarely used as an adjective) |  Relating to the act of communalizing. | The communalizing efforts faced strong opposition. 公有化努力遭遇强烈反对。| shared, collective, joint | private, individual | adjective
