contraindicative:/ˌkɒntrəˈɪndɪkətɪv/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈɪndɪkeɪtɪv/| adj.|禁忌的，不适宜的|Suggesting or indicating that a particular treatment or course of action is inadvisable.| This medication is contraindicated in patients with kidney disease. 这种药物肾病患者禁用。|近义词：inadvisable, inappropriate, unsuitable, unsuited|反义词：advisable, appropriate, suitable, indicated|词性:形容词
词源: contra- (against) + indicate (to show)

PHRASE->
contraindicated in (在...情况下禁忌): The medication is contraindicated in pregnancy. 这种药物在怀孕期间是禁忌的。
contraindicated for (对...禁忌):  Strenuous exercise is contraindicated for people with heart conditions. 剧烈运动对心脏病患者是禁忌的。
contraindicated by (被...禁忌): The surgery was contraindicated by the patient's low blood count. 病人的低血细胞计数使手术被禁忌。
absolutely contraindicated (绝对禁忌): This drug is absolutely contraindicated for patients with this specific allergy. 这种药物对有这种特定过敏症的患者绝对禁忌。
relatively contraindicated (相对禁忌): While not absolutely prohibited, the procedure is relatively contraindicated in this situation. 虽然不是绝对禁止，但在这种情况下，该手术是相对禁忌的。
