news-sheet:/ˈnjuːzˌʃiːt/ /ˈnuːzˌʃiːt/
n.
新闻简报；单页新闻；简报
A single sheet of paper giving news or information.
Example: The company distributed a news-sheet to all employees detailing the new policy. 公司向所有员工分发了一份新闻简报，详细说明了新政策。

Example:  The community group published a news-sheet to keep residents informed about local events.  社区团体出版了一份简报，让居民了解当地事件。

近义词: newsletter, bulletin, flyer, circular
反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a physical object)
词性: noun
