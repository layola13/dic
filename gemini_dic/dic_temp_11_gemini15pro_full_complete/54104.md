disembowelled:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəld/ | adj. |被剖腹的，被掏空的|  Having had the bowels removed; gutted.  Also used figuratively to mean thoroughly destroyed or devastated. |  The battlefield was a gruesome sight, littered with disembowelled soldiers. 战场上是一幅可怕的景象，到处都是被剖腹的士兵。| Synonyms: gutted, eviscerated, disemboweled | Antonyms: whole, intact | Word Type: adjective

disembowel:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/ | vt. |剖腹，掏空| To remove the bowels or entrails of; gut. Also used figuratively to mean to thoroughly destroy or devastate. |  The rebels disembowelled their captives. 叛军剖开了俘虏的肚子。 The company was financially disembowelled by the recession.  这家公司在经济衰退中元气大伤。| Synonyms: gut, eviscerate, disembowel | Antonyms:  repair, restore | Word Type: verb
