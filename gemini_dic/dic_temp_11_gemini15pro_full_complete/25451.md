bulldogger:/ˈbʊldɒɡər/ (US) /ˈbʊldɔːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.|牛仔竞技摔牛者；摔牛手|A rodeo performer who wrestles steers to the ground.|Example 1: The bulldogger quickly brought the steer down.  摔牛手迅速地把小公牛摔倒在地。

Example 2: He's a skilled bulldogger and has won many competitions.  他是一位技术娴熟的摔牛手，赢得了许多比赛。|近义词：steer wrestler|反义词: None|词性:noun
