out-measuring:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过（在测量方面）
surpassing in measurement; exceeding in size, quantity, or degree.
The new skyscraper out-measures all the other buildings in the city.  这栋新的摩天大楼比城市里所有其他建筑都高。
The athlete out-measured his competitors in the long jump.  这位运动员在跳远比赛中超过了他的竞争对手。
近义词: outstripping, exceeding, surpassing, outdoing
反义词: falling short of, lagging behind
词性: Verb

out-measuring:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərɪŋ/
adjective
胜过，超过（在测量方面）的
surpassing in measurement; exceeding in size, quantity, or degree.
The out-measuring performance of the new engine impressed everyone.  新引擎卓越的性能给大家留下了深刻印象。（这里out-measuring 作为形容词使用，虽然少见，但在特定语境下可以成立）
近义词: superior, greater, larger
反义词: inferior, smaller, lesser
词性: Adjective (less common)

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding) + "measuring" (present participle of "measure")
