conceiver:/kənˈsiːvər/
n.
构想者；设想者；发明者
Someone who conceives or invents something, especially an idea or plan.
例句：He was the conceiver of this innovative project. 他是这个创新项目的构想者。

例句：The conceiver of the airplane changed the world forever. 飞机的发明者永远地改变了世界。

近义词：creator, inventor, originator, designer, architect
反义词：imitator, copier

词性: noun
