conjunctionally:/kənˈdʒʌŋkʃənəli/| adv.|连接地，联合地|In grammar, relating to or functioning as a conjunction; joined together.  In a broader sense, it refers to things happening or existing in conjunction with each other, or operating together.|The two clauses were linked conjunctionally. 这两个从句是连接在一起的。|Synonyms: jointly, combinedly, connectedly|Antonyms: separately, independently, disjointedly|Adverb
