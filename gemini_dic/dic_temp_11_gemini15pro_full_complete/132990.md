nonenergetically:/ˌnɑːnˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪkli/
adverb|非能量地；不积极地；缺乏活力地|Without energy or enthusiasm; passively. |The task was performed nonenergetically, lacking any real effort.  这项任务执行得缺乏活力，没有任何真正的努力。| The team played nonenergetically, almost as if they didn't care about the outcome.  这支球队比赛缺乏活力，几乎就像他们不在乎结果一样。|  listlessly, apathetically, passively | energetically, actively, enthusiastically|词性:副词


Etymology->
The word "nonenergetically" is derived from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not"), the adjective "energetic" (meaning "possessing or exhibiting energy"), and the suffix "-ly" (forming adverbs).  "Energetic" itself comes from the Greek word "energeia" (meaning "activity, operation").
