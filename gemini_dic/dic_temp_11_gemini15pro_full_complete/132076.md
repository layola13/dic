noncreatives:/ˌnɑːnkriˈeɪtɪvz/
|n.|非创造性人群；缺乏创造力的人|People who are not considered creative or who work in jobs that don't require creativity.|The marketing campaign was criticized for being targeted towards noncreatives.|近义词:unimaginative people, conventional thinkers|反义词:creatives, innovators|词性:名词

Etymology: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "creative" (adjective meaning "having or showing an ability to make new things or think of new ideas").  The "s" makes it plural.
