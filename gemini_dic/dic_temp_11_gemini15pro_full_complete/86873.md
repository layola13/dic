hemiopia:/ˌhɛmiˈɒpiə/ (US) /ˌhɛmiˈəʊpiə/ (UK)| n. |偏盲，半盲|Blindness or defective vision in half of the visual field.|例句：The stroke caused hemiopia in his left eye.  中风导致他左眼偏盲。

例句：She experienced temporary hemiopia after the migraine. 偏头痛后，她经历了短暂的半盲。|近义词：hemianopsia, half-blindness|反义词: normal vision|词性:noun
