gomuti:/ɡəˈmuːti/| n. | 砂糖椰子，糖棕榈 | A type of palm tree (Arenga pinnata) native to Southeast Asia, valued for its sap, fiber, and various other products.  It is also known as the sugar palm or black fiber palm. |  The gomuti palm provides a strong fiber used for ropes and mats. 砂糖椰子提供了一种用于制作绳索和垫子的强韧纤维。

The sweet sap of the gomuti palm is used to make sugar and alcoholic beverages. 砂糖椰子的甜汁液被用来制作糖和酒精饮料。 | 近义词：sugar palm, black fiber palm | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific plant species) | 词性: noun
