byzantine:/bɪˈzæn.taɪn/ | adj. | 拜占庭式的，错综复杂的 | excessively complicated, typically involving a great deal of administrative detail | The tax code is so byzantine that it's almost impossible for the average person to understand. 税法如此复杂，普通人几乎不可能理解。|  The company's organizational structure is byzantine.  这家公司的组织结构错综复杂。 |  Synonyms: convoluted, intricate, complex, labyrinthine | Antonyms: simple, straightforward, clear | adjective
