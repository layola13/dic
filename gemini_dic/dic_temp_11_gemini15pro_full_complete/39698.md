considerers:/kənˈsɪdərərz/
noun|考虑者；斟酌者|People who consider something; those who think carefully about something.|The considerers weighed the pros and cons before making a decision. 考虑者在做决定之前权衡了利弊。|近义词：thinkers, ponderers, contemplators|反义词：impulsive people, reactors|词性:n.

Etymology:
From the verb "consider" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Consider" comes from the Old French "considerer,"  and ultimately from the Latin "considerare"  meaning "to look at closely, contemplate," literally "to observe the stars,"  from "com-" (together) + "sidus" (star, constellation).
