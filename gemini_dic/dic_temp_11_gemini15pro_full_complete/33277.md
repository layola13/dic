chrysology:/krɪˈsɒlədʒi/
n.
炼金术; 点金术
The study of gold and its properties, including its purification and uses, especially in alchemy.
Chrysology was a popular pursuit during the Renaissance.  文艺复兴时期，炼金术是一项很受欢迎的研究。
Chrysology is a fascinating, though often misunderstood, historical practice.  炼金术是一项迷人的，尽管经常被误解的历史实践。
近义词:alchemy
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek χρυσός (khrusós, “gold”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
