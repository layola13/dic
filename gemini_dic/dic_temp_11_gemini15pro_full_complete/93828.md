imminent:/ˈɪmɪnənt/| adj.|即将发生的，临近的|Likely to occur at any moment; impending.|例句：The dark clouds suggested that a storm was imminent. 乌云表明暴风雨即将来临。

例句：The announcement of the imminent arrival of the president caused a flurry of activity. 总统即将抵达的通知引起了一阵骚动。|近义词：impending, approaching, forthcoming, near, close|反义词：distant, remote, far off|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin imminēns, imminent- ‘overhanging, threatening’, present participle of imminēre, from in- ‘towards’ + minārī ‘to threaten’.
