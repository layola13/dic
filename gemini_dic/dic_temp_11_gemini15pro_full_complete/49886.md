demurely:/dɪˈmjʊərli/ | adv. | 娴静地，端庄地，矜持地 | In a reserved, modest, and shy manner, often suggesting a quiet charm or affected innocence.  | She smiled demurely and lowered her eyes. 她娴静地笑了笑，低下了眼睛。|  She demurely accepted the compliment. 她谦虚地接受了赞美。 | 近义词: modestly, shyly, reservedly, coyly | 反义词: boldly, immodestly, brazenly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Middle French *demourer "to linger, stay," from Latin demorari "to delay, linger," from de- "down" + morari "to delay." The original sense was "sober, grave," later developing into "modest, reserved."
