follow-ons:/ˈfɒləʊɒnz/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊɑːnz/ (US)| n.|后续行动，后续事件；（板球）后续局|Something that follows something else, especially as a consequence or result.  Often used in the plural. In cricket, it refers to an extra innings played if a team following on reaches a score within a certain margin of the team batting first.|The band announced two follow-on concerts after the success of their first performance. 乐队在首次演出成功后宣布了两场后续演唱会。

The team were forced to follow-on. 球队被迫进行后续局。|近义词：sequels, consequences, aftermath, aftereffects, repercussions (more formal)|反义词:precursors, antecedents|词性:名词
