biochar: /ˈbaɪoʊˌtʃɑːr/ | n. | 生物炭 |  A charcoal-like material produced from pyrolysis of biomass.  It is used to improve soil fertility and sequester carbon. | Biochar improves water retention in sandy soils. 生物炭提高了沙质土壤的保水性。| Biochar can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions. 生物炭有助于减少温室气体排放。 | synonyms: charcoal, biocoal | antonyms: N/A | noun
