fitna:/ˈfɪtnə/| n.|考验; 混乱; 叛乱; 煽动叛乱|A trial or ordeal. Discord, strife, civil strife, insurrection, temptation, seduction. Often used in a Muslim context referring to a period of civil war or chaos within the community.|The fitna that followed the Prophet's death led to the division of the Muslim community.|近义词：strife, discord, conflict, chaos, trial, ordeal, temptation|反义词：peace, harmony, unity, tranquility|noun
