lagomorpha:/ˌlæɡəˈmɔːrfə/ | n. |兔形目|An order of mammals including rabbits, hares, and pikas, distinguished by two pairs of upper incisors, one behind the other.| Rabbits and hares belong to the order Lagomorpha. 兔子跟野兔屬於兔形目。|近义词:  (No direct single-word synonym, but related terms include) leporids, rabbits, hares, pikas |反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek lagos "hare" + morphē "form".
