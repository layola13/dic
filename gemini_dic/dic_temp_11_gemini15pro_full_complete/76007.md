furcate:/ˈfɜːrkeɪt/| adj. v.|分叉的，叉状的；分叉|Divided or forked into two branches or parts. |The furcate branches of the tree reached towards the sky.  这棵树的分叉树枝伸向天空。| The river furcated into two smaller streams. 这条河分叉成两条小溪。|近义词：branched, forked, divided|反义词:undivided, single, unified|词性:形容词/动词

furcation:/fɜːrˈkeɪʃən/| n.|分叉，分歧|The point or area where something divides into two branches or parts. |The furcation of the road led to two different towns. 道路的分叉通向两个不同的城镇。|The surgeon pointed out the furcation of the artery in the diagram. 外科医生在图表中指出了动脉的分叉处。|近义词：branching, forking, division|反义词:junction, union, confluence|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *furcatus, past participle of furcare ‘to make forked,’ from furca ‘fork.’
