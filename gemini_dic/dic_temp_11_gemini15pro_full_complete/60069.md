edwardians: /ɛdˈwɔːrdiənz/
noun
爱德华七世时代的人们; 爱德华七世时代风格的(房屋、衣服等)
People who lived during the Edwardian era (1901-1910); things characteristic of that period.
The Edwardians were known for their elegant fashion.  爱德华七世时代的人们以其优雅的时尚而闻名。
Many Edwardian houses are still standing today. 许多爱德华七世时代的房屋至今仍然屹立不倒。
Synonym:  people of the Edwardian era, Edwardian-era
Antonym:  Victorians, Georgians
