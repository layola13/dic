overbait:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪt/ |v. |过度诱饵，下饵过多|To use too much bait, especially when fishing, often resulting in scaring away the fish. |He overbaited his hook and didn't catch anything. 他在鱼钩上放了太多饵，结果什么也没钓到。|The lake was overbaited, so the fish were wary.  湖里的饵料太多了，所以鱼都很警惕。|近义词: overfeed|反义词: underbait|词性:动词
