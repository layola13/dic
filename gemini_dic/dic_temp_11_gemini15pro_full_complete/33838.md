circummeridian:/ˌsɜːrkəmɪˈrɪdiən/| adj.|子午线的，经过子午线的，环绕子午线的|Relating to or situated near a meridian, especially the celestial meridian.|Astronomers use circummeridian observations to accurately determine the latitude of a location. 天文学家使用近子午线观测来精确地确定一个地点的纬度。|The circummeridian altitude was measured using a sextant. 使用六分仪测量了近子午线的高度。|近义词：meridian, meridional|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *circummeridianus*, from circum- (“around”) + meridianus (“of midday, meridian”).
