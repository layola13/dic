curvaciousness:/kɜːrˈveɪʃəsnəs/
n.|曲线美，丰满|The quality of being curvy; having a full, rounded figure.|She was admired for her curvaciousness. 她因其曲线美而备受赞赏。|近义词：shapeliness, voluptuousness|反义词：thinness, slenderness|词性:名词
Etymology: From 'curvaceous' + '-ness'. 'Curvaceous' comes from 'curve' (from Latin 'curvus', meaning "bent, curved") + '-aceous' (a suffix meaning "having the nature of, resembling").
