digamma:/dɪˈɡæmə/ | n. | 双伽玛/Ϝ |  The sixth letter of the early Greek alphabet, pronounced /w/, and later dropped.  It corresponds to the letter F in the Latin alphabet. |  The digamma fell out of use in the classical Greek alphabet. 双伽玛在古典希腊字母表中不再使用。 | The word "wine" in many Indo-European languages can be traced back to a root starting with the digamma.  许多印欧语系中“葡萄酒”一词可以追溯到一个以双伽玛开头的词根。 | 近义词: wau,  | 反义词:  (None - it's a letter) | noun
