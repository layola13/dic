consulter:/kɔ̃.syl.te/ (French), /kənˈsʌlt/ (English)| v. |咨询，查阅，商量|To seek advice or information from someone, or to refer to a book or other source for information.|例句：I consulted a doctor about my back pain. 我咨询了一位医生关于我的背痛。

例句：She consulted the dictionary for the meaning of the word. 她查字典找这个词的意思。


近义词：confer, discuss, refer to|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:verb

consulter (French):/kɔ̃.syl.te/| v. |咨询，查阅，商量|To seek advice or information from someone, especially a professional.|例句：J'ai consulté un avocat. (French)  I consulted a lawyer. 我咨询了一位律师。

例句：Il a consulté l'horaire des trains. (French) He consulted the train schedule. 他查阅了火车时刻表。

近义词：s'adresser à, interroger, se référer à (French)|反义词:ignorer, négliger (French)|词性:verb
