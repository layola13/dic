hallelujah:/ˌhælɪˈluːjə/ /ˌhæləˈluːjə/ | int. n.| 哈利路亚，赞美上帝|An exclamation of praise to God.| Hallelujah! He has risen! 哈利路亚！他复活了！|
例句：The choir sang "Hallelujah" at the end of the service.  唱诗班在礼拜结束时唱了“哈利路亚”。

例句：The news was met with cries of "Hallelujah!" 这消息受到了“哈利路亚”的欢呼。

近义词：praise, amen | 反义词: curse, blasphemy | 词性: interjection, noun
