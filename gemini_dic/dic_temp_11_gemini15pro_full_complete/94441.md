imploringly:/ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋli/
adverb
恳求地，哀求地
In a way that shows you are asking for something in a very sincere and emotional way.
She looked at him imploringly, hoping he would change his mind. 她恳求地看着他，希望他能改变主意。
He raised his hands imploringly to the heavens. 他恳求地向天举起双手。
近义词:beseechingly, pleadingly, supplicatingly
反义词:demandingly, imperiously
词性:adverb

词源:
From implore + -ing + -ly.  Implore comes from Old French *implorer, from Latin implōrāre "to invoke with tears," from in- "on, upon" + plōrāre "to weep, cry out."
