flapjacks:/ˈflæpˌdʒæks/ (US) /ˈflæpˌdʒæks/ (UK)| n. |煎饼，薄烤饼|A type of pancake, often made with oats.|例句：We had flapjacks for breakfast this morning.  今天早上我们吃了煎饼。

例句：The diner is famous for its fluffy flapjacks. 这家小餐馆以其蓬松的煎饼而闻名。


近义词：pancakes, griddlecakes, hotcakes |反义词: None (no direct opposite for a type of food)|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From "flap" (referring to the tossing or flipping action sometimes used in cooking) + "jack" (a term of endearment or diminutive suffix). The earliest recorded usage dates back to the early 17th century.
