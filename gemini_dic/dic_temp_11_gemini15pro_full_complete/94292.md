impertinent: /ɪmˈpɜːrtnənt/ (US) /ɪmˈpɜːtɪnənt/ (UK)
adjective
不恰当的，无礼的，鲁莽的
not showing proper respect; rude
例句：It is impertinent to ask such personal questions.  问这样的私人问题是不恰当的。

例句：He dismissed her question with an impertinent wave of his hand. 他轻蔑地挥了挥手，对她的问题不屑一顾。

近义词：rude, insolent, impudent, disrespectful, cheeky
反义词：polite, respectful, courteous, civil, appropriate
词性：adjective
