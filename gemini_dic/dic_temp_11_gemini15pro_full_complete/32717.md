chlorine:/ˈklɔːriːn/ (US) /ˈklɔːriːn/ (UK)| n.|氯|A common chemical element, a greenish-yellow gas, used for purifying water and making bleach and other chemicals.|Chlorine is commonly used in swimming pools to kill bacteria. 氯通常用于游泳池中以杀死细菌。|Synonyms: Cl (chemical symbol), halogen|Antonyms: None|noun

chlorine:/ˈklɔːriːn/ (US) /ˈklɔːriːn/ (UK)| adj.|含氯的|Containing or treated with chlorine.|The chlorine smell of the pool was strong. 游泳池里的氯气味很浓。|Synonyms: chlorinated|Antonyms: dechlorinated|adjective
