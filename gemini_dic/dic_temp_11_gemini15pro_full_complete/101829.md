irrigation:/ˌɪrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 灌溉 |The supply of water to land or crops to help growth, typically by means of channels.|  The farm uses a sophisticated irrigation system. 这个农场使用先进的灌溉系统。| 近义词: watering, sprinkling | 反义词: drainage, dehydration | noun

irrigate:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪt/ | vt. | 灌溉 |Supply water to (land or crops) in order to help growth, typically by means of channels.|  Farmers irrigate their crops during dry periods.  农民在干旱时期灌溉他们的庄稼。| 近义词: water, dampen | 反义词: drain, dry | verb

irrorate:/ˈɪrəˌreɪt/ | vt. | 洒水，喷洒；润湿 |To sprinkle or spray with liquid; moisten.|  The priest irrorated the congregation with holy water.  神父向会众洒圣水。| 近义词: sprinkle, spray, moisten | 反义词: dry, desiccate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Latin *irrigare*, from *in-* (in, on, upon) + *rigare* (to water, moisten).
