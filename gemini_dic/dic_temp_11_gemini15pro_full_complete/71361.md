flag-down:/ˌflæɡ ˈdaʊn/ | v. | 挥手示意(车辆)停下; 招手拦车 | To signal (a vehicle, especially a taxi) to stop by waving one's hand. |  I flagged down a taxi on the busy street. 我在繁忙的街道上招手拦了一辆出租车。

| Example:  The stranded motorist flagged down a passing car.  那个抛锚的司机挥手示意一辆过路的车停下。 | Synonym: hail, signal, stop | Antonym: ignore, pass by | verb

flag-down:/ˌflæɡ ˈdaʊn/ | n. | (挥手)拦车 | The act of signaling a vehicle to stop by waving. | The taxi driver noticed my flag-down and pulled over. 出租车司机注意到我挥手拦车，便靠边停车了。| Synonym: signal, hail | Antonym: N/A | noun
