haberdashery:/ˈhæbərdæʃəri/
n.
男子服饰用品;缝纫用品
Men's clothing and accessories; sewing notions.
He bought some needles and thread at the haberdashery. 他在男子服饰用品店买了一些针线。
Synonyms: notions, men's wear, accessories
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "women's wear" for contrast)
noun

haberdasher:/ˈhæbərdæʃər/
n.
男子服饰用品商
A dealer in men's clothing and accessories.
The haberdasher helped him choose a tie. 男子服饰用品商帮他选了一条领带。
Synonyms: clothier, outfitter
Antonyms: (No direct antonym)
noun


haberdash:/ˈhæbərdæʃ/
v.
(旧) 贩卖男子服饰用品
(Archaic) To deal in men's clothing and accessories.
He haberdashed for many years before retiring. 他退休前做了多年的男子服饰用品生意。
Synonyms: sell, vend, retail
Antonyms: buy, purchase
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *haberdassherie*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Old French, but no corresponding French word exists.  The second element may be related to *dasher*, meaning "a small dish or cup."
