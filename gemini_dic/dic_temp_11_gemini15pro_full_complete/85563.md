hated-on:/ˈheɪtɪd ɑːn/
adjective
被憎恨的，被讨厌的
Subjected to hate or intense dislike, often unfairly or excessively.
例句：The celebrity felt hated-on by the media after the scandal.  这位名人在丑闻曝光后感到被媒体憎恨。

例句：She was hated-on for her unconventional opinions. 她因为她非传统的观点而被讨厌。

近义词：vilified, maligned, despised, loathed
反义词：adored, loved, admired, respected
词性: adjective
