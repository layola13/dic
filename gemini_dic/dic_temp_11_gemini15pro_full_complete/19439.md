biconjugate:/baɪˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪt/ | adj. n. |双共轭的，双偶联的; 双共轭物|Having two conjugates, especially (of a complex number) having a conjugate equal to its negative.  (noun) A mathematical entity that is biconjugate to another. |The biconjugate of a complex number is the negative of its conjugate.  一个复数的双共轭是其共轭的负数。| The biconjugate gradient method is used to solve systems of linear equations. 双共轭梯度法用于求解线性方程组。|Synonym: dual, reciprocal (for noun) |Antonym:  N/A |adjective, noun
