mustered-out:/ˈmʌstərd aʊt/
adjective
退伍的，复员的
Discharged from military service.
例句：The mustered-out soldier returned to his hometown.  退伍的士兵回到了他的家乡。

例句：He felt lost and unsure of his future after being mustered-out.  退伍后，他对自己的未来感到迷茫和不确定。

近义词：discharged, demobilized
反义词：enlisted, drafted
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "muster out," meaning to formally discharge from military service. "Muster" originally referred to assembling troops for inspection or service, from Old French "mostre" (show, display).  "Out" indicates removal or completion of the process.
