noun
过度用肥皂清洗

Over-soaping can strip the skin of its natural oils. 过度用肥皂清洗会剥夺皮肤的天然油脂。
Excessive washing with soap can lead to skin problems.  过度用肥皂清洗会导致皮肤问题。
近义词: over-washing, excessive cleansing
反义词: under-washing
