nonautomatic:/ˌnɒnˌɔːtəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非自动的，手动 | Not automatic; requiring manual operation. |  This is a nonautomatic transmission, you have to shift gears yourself.  这是手动变速器，你得自己换挡。

|  The nonautomatic doors need to be opened and closed by hand.  这些非自动门需要手动开关。 | 近义词：manual, hand-operated | 反义词：automatic, self-acting | 词性：adjective

Etymology: non- + automatic
From non-  "not" + automatic "self-acting, having the power of self-motion"
