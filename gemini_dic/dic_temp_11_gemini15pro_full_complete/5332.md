alloy:/ˈælɔɪ/ (US), /ˈælɔɪ/ (UK)| n. vt.| 合金；混合物；降低…的纯度|A metal made by combining two or more metallic elements, especially to give greater strength or resistance to corrosion.  To mix (a metal) with another metal or other substance. |例句：
Steel is an alloy of iron and carbon. 钢是铁和碳的合金。

The company alloys copper with zinc to produce brass.  这家公司将铜与锌合金化生产黄铜。

近义词：mixture, blend, compound, composite|反义词: pure substance|词性: noun, verb
