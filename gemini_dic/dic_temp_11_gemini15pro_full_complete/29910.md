caution:/ˈkɔːʃən/ (US) /ˈkɔːʃn/ (UK)| n. vt.|小心，谨慎；警告，告诫|A warning against certain acts or practices.  A state of being careful and watchful.|例句：He exercised extreme caution when driving in the fog. 他在雾中开车格外小心。|近义词：carefulness, wariness, warning, admonition|反义词：recklessness, carelessness|noun
caution:/ˈkɔːʃən/ (US) /ˈkɔːʃn/ (UK)| vt.|警告，告诫|To warn or advise someone against something.|例句：The police cautioned him about speeding. 警察警告他超速行驶。|近义词：warn, advise, admonish|反义词：encourage|verb
