kiddie:/ˈkɪdi/| n. |小孩，儿童 (尤指幼儿)|A child, especially a young one.  A term often used in a playful or slightly condescending way. |The kiddies were splashing in the paddling pool. 孩子们在戏水池里嬉戏。|Synonyms: child, toddler, youngster, little one |Antonyms: adult, grown-up|noun
kiddie:/ˈkɪdi/| adj. |适合小孩的，幼稚的|Designed for or suitable for children, sometimes implying childishness or simplicity. |There are kiddie rides at the fair.  游乐场里有儿童游乐设施。|Synonyms: children's, juvenile, childish|Antonyms: adult, mature|adjective
