mid-point:/ˈmɪdˌpɔɪnt/| n.|中点，正中央|The midpoint of the line segment is where it is divided into two equal parts. 线段的中点是将其分成两等份的地方。|例句：The midpoint of the journey is often a good time to rest.  旅途的中点通常是休息的好时机。

mid-point:/ˈmɪdˌpɔɪnt/| n.|中间点，正中间|The midpoint of the negotiation was marked by a compromise.  谈判的中间点以妥协为标志。|例句：They met at the midpoint between the two cities. 他们在两座城市之间的中点相遇。 |近义词：center, middle, halfway point|反义词:end, beginning, extremity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mid + point.  Mid comes from Old English *midd, meaning "middle." Point comes from Old French point, ultimately from Latin punctum "prick, point."
