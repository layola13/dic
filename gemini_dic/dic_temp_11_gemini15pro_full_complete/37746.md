compass:/ˈkʌmpəs/ (US) /ˈkʌmpəs/ (UK)| n.|指南针，罗盘；圆规；范围，界限|An instrument used for navigation and orientation that shows the direction of magnetic north.  A tool used for drawing circles. The range or scope of something.|例句：The compass pointed north. 指南针指向北方。|例句：He used a compass to draw a perfect circle. 他用圆规画了一个完美的圆。|近义词：navigator,  range, scope,  calipers|反义词: |noun


compass:/ˈkʌmpəs/ (US) /ˈkʌmpəs/ (UK)| v.|包围，围绕；理解，领会|To surround or enclose. To understand or grasp fully.|例句：The mountains compass the valley. 群山环绕着山谷。|例句：I couldn't compass the meaning of the poem. 我无法理解这首诗的含义。|近义词：encircle, surround, comprehend, grasp|反义词:|verb
