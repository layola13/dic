monogenism:/ˈmɒnəʊˌdʒɛnɪzəm/ /ˈmɑːnəʊˌdʒɛnɪzəm/
n.
单一起源论
The theory that all human beings originated from a single common ancestor.
Monogenism contrasts sharply with polygenism.  单一起源论与多源论截然不同。
The theory of monogenism was central to many 19th-century debates about race.  单一起源论是19世纪许多关于种族的辩论的核心。
