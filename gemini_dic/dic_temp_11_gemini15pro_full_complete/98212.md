insectologist:/ˌɪnˌsɛkˈtɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌɪnˌsɛkˈtɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |昆虫学家|A scientist who studies insects.|例句：The insectologist discovered a new species of beetle in the rainforest.  这位昆虫学家在雨林中发现了一种新的甲虫。

例句：She consulted an insectologist about the infestation in her garden. 她向一位昆虫学家咨询了她花园里的虫害问题。|近义词：entomologist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Latin *insectum* ("insect") + *-ologist* ("one who studies")
