Etymology:
From the literal black boxes used in aircraft, which are actually bright orange for visibility in wreckage. The term is now used metaphorically to describe any system whose inner workings are hidden or not understood.  来自飞机上使用的字面意义上的黑匣子，实际上它们是亮橙色的，以便在残骸中可见。这个词现在被用作比喻，来描述任何内部运作被隐藏或不理解的系统。
