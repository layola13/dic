obloquy: /ˈɒbləkwɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːbləkwai/ (US)| n. | 辱骂；诽谤；恶名 |Strong public criticism or verbal abuse. |例句：He faced obloquy after the scandal broke.  丑闻曝光后，他面临着众人的谴责。

例句：The politician suffered obloquy for his controversial remarks.  这位政客因其有争议的言论而遭受了诽谤。|近义词：disgrace, dishonor, shame, reproach, condemnation, censure, vilification, slander, libel |反义词：praise, acclaim, honor, respect |词性: 名词
