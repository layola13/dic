overlearned:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrnd/| adj.|过度学习的，学习过度的|Learned or practiced to the point of automaticity; excessively learned.|Example: His overlearned responses made him inflexible in new situations. 他的过度学习的反应使他在新情况下不够灵活。|Example:  The pianist's overlearned scales allowed him to play them flawlessly without thinking.  钢琴家过度练习的音阶让他可以完美地演奏，无需思考。|Synonyms: ingrained, habituated, rote, automatic|Antonyms: unlearned, unfamiliar, novel|adjective

overlearn:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrn/| v.|过度学习|To learn something beyond the point of mastery.|Example:  She overlearned the vocabulary words, ensuring she wouldn't forget them during the test. 她过度学习了词汇，确保在考试期间不会忘记它们。|Example:  Athletes often overlearn basic skills to improve performance under pressure. 运动员经常过度学习基本技能以提高在压力下的表现。|Synonyms: drill, cram, practice excessively|Antonyms: skim, glance at, underlearn|verb
