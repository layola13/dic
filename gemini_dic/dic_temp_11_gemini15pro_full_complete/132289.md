nondefensibly:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪbli/ | adv. | 不可辩护地，无法辩解地 | Not able to be defended or justified. | His actions were nondefensibly cruel. 他的行为残酷得令人发指，不可辩护。 |  The politician's stance on the issue was nondefensibly weak.  这位政治家在这个问题上的立场弱得站不住脚，无法辩护。 | 近义词: unjustifiably, inexcusably, unwarrantably | 反义词: justifiably, excusably, defensibly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
defensible (adjective): from Old French defensable, from defendre "to defend," from Latin defendere "to ward off, protect."
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
