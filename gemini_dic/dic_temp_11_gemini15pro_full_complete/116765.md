mausolea:/ˌmɔːsəˈliːə/ | n. |陵墓，坟墓（mausoleum的复数形式）| A large stately tomb or building housing tombs, especially of a family or other group. |  The emperors' mausolea are popular tourist destinations. 皇帝们的陵墓是热门的旅游目的地。 |  The family's mausoleum was an elaborate structure.  这个家族的陵墓是一座精心设计的建筑。 | synonyms: tombs, sepulchers, crypts | antonyms:  None (lack of a direct opposite for a place of burial) | noun
