Present participle of equalize.  是动词equalize的现在分词形式。

synonyms: leveling, balancing, evening, standardizing

verb
使均衡，使相等

To make equal. 使平等。
They equalized the scores in the last minute of the game. 他们在比赛的最后一分钟扳平了比分。
synonyms: level, balance, even, standardize
antonyms: differentiate, vary, unbalance
