centerboard:/ˈsɛntərˌbɔːrd/| n. |中心板，中插板|A retractable keel or fin located amidships in a shallow-draft boat, lowered to provide lateral resistance and prevent leeway.|The sailor lowered the centerboard as the boat entered shallower water.  水手在船驶入较浅水域时放下了中插板。|近义词：keel, daggerboard|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

centerboard:/ˈsɛntərˌbɔːrd/| n. |中央板;活动龙骨|A fin-like device that can be pivoted up into a slot in a sailboat's hull, lowered into the water to provide lateral stability and reduced leeway.|The centerboard helps the boat sail closer to the wind.  中心板有助于帆船更贴近风航行。|近义词：leeboard, daggerboard|反义词: fixed keel|词性:noun
