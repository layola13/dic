cerotin:/ˈsɛrətɪn/ /ˈsɪərətɪn/| n. |鲸蜡醇，蜡醇|A white crystalline alcohol, C₂₆H₅₄O, found in beeswax and Chinese wax, and also occurring in various other waxes and higher plants.|Cerotin is a component of beeswax used in candle making. 鲸蜡醇是蜂蜡中用于制造蜡烛的一种成分。|Synonyms: ceryl alcohol, hexacosanol|Antonyms: None|Noun
