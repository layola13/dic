nut:/nʌt/ | n. | 坚果，螺母，难对付的人 |A hard-shelled dry fruit or seed with a separable rind or shell and interior kernel. Also, a metal block with a threaded hole through it, used to tighten a bolt.  A crazy or eccentric person. |  例句：I love to eat almonds, which are a type of nut. 我喜欢吃杏仁，它是一种坚果。

|例句：He tightened the nut with a wrench. 他用扳手拧紧了螺母。

|近义词：seed, kernel, bolt, crazy, eccentric |反义词: sane, normal |词性: noun


nut:/nʌt/ | v. | 采集坚果 |To gather nuts. | 例句：Squirrels nut in the autumn. 松鼠在秋天采集坚果。

|例句：We went nutting in the woods last weekend. 上周末我们去树林里采坚果了。

|近义词：gather, collect, forage |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: verb
