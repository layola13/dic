overaccumulating: /ˌoʊvərəˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度积累 |  The act of accumulating something in excess, beyond what is necessary or desirable.  It's the present participle form of the verb "overaccumulate". |  Companies are overaccumulating inventory due to decreased demand. 公司由于需求下降而过度积累库存。 | over-accumulating, excessively accumulating | under-accumulating, insufficiently accumulating | verb
