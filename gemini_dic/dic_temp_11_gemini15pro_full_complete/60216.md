effluviable:/ɪˈflʊviəbl/
adjective
可散发气味的; 可发散的
Capable of giving off an effluvium (a subtle or invisible exhalation or vapor).
The effluviable scent of the roses filled the garden. 玫瑰散发出的香味弥漫了整个花园。
近义词:odoriferous, fragrant, aromatic
反义词:odorless, scentless
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *effluviabilis, from Latin effluvius + -abilis.  Effluvius comes from effluere, meaning "to flow out."
