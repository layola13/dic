microorganisms:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈɔːrɡənɪzəmz/| n. |微生物|A microscopic organism, especially a bacterium, virus, or fungus.|Microorganisms play a crucial role in the ecosystem. 微生物在生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|synonyms: microbes, germs, bugs|antonyms: macroorganisms|noun

microorganism:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈɔːrɡənɪzəm/| n. |微生物|A microscopic organism, especially a bacterium, virus, or fungus.|Harmful microorganisms can cause diseases. 有害微生物会导致疾病。|synonyms: microbe, germ, bug|antonyms: macroorganism|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + organism.  "Micro-" comes from the Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Organism" comes from the Greek "organon" meaning "instrument" or "tool".
