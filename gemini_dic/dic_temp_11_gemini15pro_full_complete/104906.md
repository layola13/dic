keffiyeh:/kəˈfiːə/ | n. | 方格头巾 | A Middle Eastern headdress consisting of a square of cloth, usually cotton, folded and wrapped around the head, often held in place by a cord.  | He wore a traditional keffiyeh. 他戴着一条传统的方格头巾。|  Example: The keffiyeh protected his face from the sun and sand. 这条方格头巾保护了他的脸免受日晒和风沙。 | Synonym: shemagh, headscarf | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hat" in a Western context) | noun
