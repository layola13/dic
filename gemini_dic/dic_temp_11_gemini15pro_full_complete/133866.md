nongratuitously: /ˌnɒnɡrəˈtjuːɪtəsli/ | adv. | 非无偿地，并非无故地 |  Done or acting not for free; with a reason or purpose; deliberately.  It implies an action was performed with a specific intention, often negative or critical. | He nongratuitously insulted his colleague, clearly intending to provoke a reaction. 他故意侮辱了他的同事，显然是想激怒他。|  She nongratuitously mentioned his past failures, reminding everyone of his shortcomings. 她并非无故地提起他过去的失败，提醒大家他的缺点。 | synonym: deliberately, purposely, intentionally, pointedly | antonym: accidentally, unintentionally, gratuitously, needlessly | adverb
