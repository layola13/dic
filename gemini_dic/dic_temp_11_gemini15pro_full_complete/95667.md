inconcealable:/ˌɪnkənˈsiːləbl/
adjective
无法隐藏的，显而易见的
Not able to be hidden or disguised.
Her joy was inconcealable. 她的喜悦溢于言表。
Synonyms: obvious, evident, apparent, manifest, patent, unmistakable, undeniable
Antonyms: concealable, hidden, disguised
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + concealable (able to be concealed).  The root word "conceal" comes from the Old French "conceler," meaning "to hide."  Ultimately derived from the Latin "con-" (with, together) and "celare" (to hide).
