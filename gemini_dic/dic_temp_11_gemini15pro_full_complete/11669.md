armlocking:/ˈɑːrmlɒkɪŋ/ | n. | 擒拿; 臂锁 |The act of holding someone's arm in a way that prevents them from moving it, often as a method of restraint or in martial arts. |  He subdued the attacker with a swift armlocking maneuver. 他以迅雷不及掩耳的擒拿动作制服了袭击者。| synonym: grappling, hold, restraint | antonym: release, freedom | noun

armlock: /ˈɑːrmlɒk/ | n. vt. | 臂锁; 用臂锁制服 | A hold in which someone's arm is twisted or bent in a painful way, often used in wrestling or martial arts; to restrain someone using an armlock. | He put his opponent in an armlock. 他把对手置于臂锁之中。  The police officer armlocked the suspect. 警察用臂锁制服了嫌疑人。 | synonym:  grapple, hold, subdue | antonym: release, free | noun, verb
