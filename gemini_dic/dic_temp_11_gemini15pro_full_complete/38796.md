conductorial:/kənˌdʌkˈtɔːriəl/
adj.
指挥的；乐队指挥的；导电的
Relating to the act of conducting, especially of an orchestra or other musical group; relating to conduction, as of electricity or heat.
The conductorial gestures were precise and elegant. 指挥的动作精准而优雅。
Copper has excellent conductorial properties. 铜具有优良的导电性能。
近义词: conducting, directive, leading (for music); conductive (for electricity/heat)
反义词: non-conducting, insulating (for electricity/heat)
词性: adjective
