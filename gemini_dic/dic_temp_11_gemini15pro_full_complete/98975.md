inter-pollinates:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləneɪts/ | v. |异花授粉|To transfer pollen from one plant to another, especially of a different variety or species.  This often leads to hybridization.| The gardener inter-pollinates his orchids to create new varieties. 园丁对他的兰花进行异花授粉以创造新品种。| cross-pollinates, hybridizes | self-pollinates | verb
