free-for-all:/ˌfriː fər ˈɔːl/
n.|混战，多人乱斗；自由放任|A disorganized fight or competition involving many people; a situation lacking rules or control.|The Black Friday sale quickly descended into a free-for-all, with shoppers scrambling for the limited discounted items. 黑色星期五的促销活动迅速演变成一场混战，购物者争先恐后地抢购数量有限的打折商品。

|The meeting became a free-for-all, with everyone shouting their opinions at once. 会议变成了一场混战，每个人都在同时大声喊出自己的意见。
|近义词：brawl, melee, fracas, Donnybrook|反义词：order, control, peace|词性: 名词
