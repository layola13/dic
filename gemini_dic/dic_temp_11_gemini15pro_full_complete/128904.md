nimbyism: /ˈnɪmbɪɪzəm/ | n. |邻避症候群；邻避主义| NIMBY is an acronym for "Not In My Back Yard." It refers to the opposition by residents to proposed developments in their local area, even if those developments might benefit the wider community. | The proposed construction of a new prison was met with strong nimbyism from local residents.  当地居民强烈反对拟建的新监狱。|Example: The local community displayed strong nimbyism regarding the proposed wind farm, citing concerns about noise and visual impact. 当地社区对拟议的风电场表现出强烈的邻避主义，理由是担心噪音和视觉影响。| synonyms: localism, parochialism | antonyms: altruism, communitarianism | noun
