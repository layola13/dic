eurasiatio:/jʊˌreɪʒiˈeɪʃioʊ/ |n.| 欧亚主义，欧亚大陆主义|A geopolitical concept emphasizing the interconnectedness of Europe and Asia, particularly Russia's role as a bridge between the two continents. It often involves advocating for a distinct Eurasian civilization and opposing Western influence.| Eurasianism is a complex political ideology. 欧亚主义是一种复杂的政治意识形态。| The Eurasian Economic Union reflects some aspects of eurasiatio thinking.  欧亚经济联盟反映了欧亚主义思想的某些方面。 |Near Synonyms:  Pan-Eurasianism, Eurasian integration |Antonyms:  Westernization, Atlanticism |noun
