affectivity:/əˌfɛkˈtɪvɪti/
n.
情感性，感情
The quality or character of being affected or emotional; emotional responsiveness.
Her poems are full of affectivity and personal experience. 她的诗歌充满了情感和个人经历。

近义词: emotionality, feeling, sentiment, sensibility
反义词: apathy, indifference, coldness

词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *affectivitās, from Latin affectīvus (“relating to the affections”).  Equivalent to affect +‎ -ivity.
