overnourishment:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃmənt/ | n. | 营养过剩；营养过度 | The condition of having consumed more nutrients than the body needs, often leading to obesity and other health problems. |  Childhood overnourishment can lead to obesity in adulthood.  儿童期营养过剩会导致成年期肥胖。| Synonyms: overfeeding, hyperalimentation | Antonyms: malnutrition, undernourishment | noun


overnourished:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃt/ | adj. | 营养过剩的；营养过度的 |  Supplied with more nutrients than needed.  | The overnourished child struggled with his weight.  营养过剩的孩子与他的体重作斗争。| Synonyms: overfed | Antonyms: malnourished, undernourished | adjective
