bedevilment:/bɪˈdɛvəlmənt/
noun
折磨；困扰；纠缠
The state of being bedeviled; harassment or torment.
The project was plagued by constant bedevilments.  这个项目一直受到持续不断的困扰。
The bedevilment of technical glitches made the launch a disaster. 技术故障的困扰使得发布会成为一场灾难。

近义词:torment, affliction, harassment, plague, vexation
反义词:peace, tranquility, serenity, calm
词性:noun

Etymology:
From bedevil + -ment. Bedevil comes from Middle English *bedevelen*, from be- + devil.
