athrepsia:/əˈθrɛpsiə/| n. |营养不良，消瘦，衰弱|A failure of an infant to thrive, usually as a result of a nutritional deficiency.|The doctor diagnosed the baby with athrepsia due to prolonged malnutrition. 医生诊断婴儿因长期营养不良而患有营养不良症。|Synonyms: malnutrition, marasmus, wasting, cachexia|Antonyms: nourishment, health, vigor|noun


athreptic:/əˈθrɛptɪk/| adj. |营养不良的，消瘦的|Relating to or characterized by athrepsia.|The athreptic child was severely underweight. 这个营养不良的孩子体重严重不足。|Synonyms: malnourished, marasmic, wasted, cachectic|Antonyms: nourished, healthy, vigorous|adjective
