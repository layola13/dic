closehanded:/ˌkloʊsˈhændɪd/ | adj. |吝啬的，小气的| Unwilling to spend or give money; stingy; miserly. | He was known for being closehanded, rarely offering to pay for anything. 他以吝啬出名，很少主动付钱。|  She's so closehanded she squawks if she has to buy a postage stamp. 她非常小气，如果必须买邮票，她都会抱怨。| 近义词: stingy, miserly, tightfisted, parsimonious | 反义词: generous, openhanded, liberal | 词性: 形容词
