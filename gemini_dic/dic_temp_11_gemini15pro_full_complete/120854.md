misbehaved:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvd/| adj.|行为不端的，表现不好的|Behaved badly or improperly.  Having displayed bad manners or conduct.|The misbehaved child was sent to his room.  那个行为不端的孩子被送回了房间。|近义词:naughty, unruly, disobedient, wayward|反义词:well-behaved, obedient, good|词性:形容词

词源:
From mis- + behaved, past participle of behave.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating wrong or bad. "Behave" comes from the Old English "behabban," meaning "to contain oneself."
