humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n.  int.| 骗局，欺骗；废话，胡说；薄荷硬糖 |A deceptive trick, pretense, or falsehood; nonsense; a hard mint candy.|"That whole story about the ghost was a complete humbug." 整个关于鬼魂的故事完全是骗人的。|近义词：fraud, hoax, sham, nonsense, baloney|反义词：truth, honesty, reality|名词
humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| vt.| 欺骗，哄骗 |To deceive or trick.|"He humbugged me into believing he was a prince." 他骗我相信他是个王子。|近义词：deceive, trick, dupe, fool|反义词：be honest, tell the truth|动词
humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| int.| 呸！胡说！|Used to express disbelief or contempt.|"Humbug! I don't believe a word of it." 呸！我一个字也不信。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, fiddlesticks|反义词： (No direct antonym for an interjection)|感叹词
