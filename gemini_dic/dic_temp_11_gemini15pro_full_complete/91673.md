hypercivilizations:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɪvɪləˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |超级文明|Hypothetical civilizations that are vastly more advanced than present-day human civilization, often imagined to possess technologies and social structures beyond our current comprehension.|Scientists sometimes speculate about the possibility of encountering hypercivilizations in the vastness of space.| 近义词: advanced civilizations, super civilizations | 反义词: primitive civilizations | 词性: noun

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek *ὑπέρ (hupér) "over, beyond"
civilization (noun): from French civilisation, from Latin civilis "civil, relating to citizens," from civis "citizen"
