condominium:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ (US) /ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ (UK)| n. |公寓；一套公寓房；共同统治；共管|A building or complex of buildings containing a number of individually owned apartments or houses.|例句：They live in a condominium overlooking the ocean. 他们住在一栋可以俯瞰大海的公寓大楼里。

例句：The condominium has a swimming pool and a gym. 这栋公寓楼配有游泳池和健身房。

近义词：apartment, flat, unit|反义词:house, detached house|词性:noun

condominiums:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (US) /ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (UK)| n. |公寓（复数）；一套公寓房（复数）|Plural form of condominium.|例句：Many condominiums are being built in the city center. 市中心正在建造许多公寓楼。

例句：The condominiums offer stunning views of the mountains. 这些公寓可以欣赏到壮丽的山景。

近义词：apartments, flats, units|反义词:houses, detached houses|词性:noun
