castles: /ˈkɑːs(ə)lz/ (US) /ˈkɑːslz/ (UK)
| n. |城堡；宫堡|Large fortified buildings, typically of the medieval period, occupied by a ruler or powerful lord.|例句：The king lived in a grand castle overlooking the valley. 国王住在一座俯瞰山谷的宏伟城堡里。

例句：Many ancient castles are now popular tourist attractions. 许多古老的城堡现在是热门的旅游景点。

|近义词：fortresses, citadels, strongholds|
|反义词：huts, shacks|
|词性：noun|
