noncertificated:/ˌnɑːnsɜːrˈtɪfɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
未获得证书的；未经认证的
Not having a certificate or license, especially for teaching.
例句：The school hired several noncertificated teachers due to the teacher shortage.  由于教师短缺，学校雇佣了几名未获认证的教师。

例句：Noncertificated staff also play a vital role in the education system.  未获认证的员工在教育系统中也发挥着至关重要的作用。

近义词：uncertified, unlicensed
反义词：certified, licensed
词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + certificated (past participle of "certificate")
