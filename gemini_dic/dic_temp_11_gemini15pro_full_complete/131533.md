noncommemorative:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmɛməreɪtɪv/
adjective
非纪念性的
Not designed or intended to serve as a memorial or mark a special occasion.
This is a noncommemorative stamp, intended for everyday postage. 这是一枚非纪念邮票，用于日常邮寄。
The government issued a series of noncommemorative coins. 政府发行了一系列非纪念币。
Synonyms: ordinary, everyday, regular, standard
Antonyms: commemorative, memorial, celebratory
词性: 形容词
