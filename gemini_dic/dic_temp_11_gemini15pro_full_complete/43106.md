例句：She dismissed their concerns coxcombly, waving her hand dismissively. 她傲慢地驳回了他们的担忧，轻蔑地挥了挥手。


近义词：arrogantly, haughtily, superciliously, pompously|反义词:humbly, modestly|词性:副词
