overliberalities: /ˌoʊvərˌlɪbərˈælɪtiz/
noun
过度慷慨；过分大方
Excessive generosity or lavishness.
His overliberalities eventually led him to bankruptcy. 他的过度慷慨最终导致他破产。
Synonyms: extravagance, prodigality, lavishness, immoderation
Antonyms: stinginess, parsimony, frugality
n.

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "liberality" (generosity).  "Liberality" comes from the Latin "līberālitās,"  from "līberālis" (generous),  ultimately from "līber" (free).
