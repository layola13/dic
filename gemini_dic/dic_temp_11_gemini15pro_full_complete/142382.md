orthoselection:/ˈɔːrθoʊsɪˈlɛkʃən/| n. |定向选择|The process by which advantageous mutations become fixed in a population due to natural selection, leading to a directional change in the phenotype.|The long neck of the giraffe is a classic example of orthoselection. 长颈鹿的长脖子是定向选择的典型例子。|Orthogenesis, directional selection|Stabilizing selection, disruptive selection|noun
