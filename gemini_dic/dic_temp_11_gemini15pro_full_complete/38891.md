conferrers:/kənˈfɜːrərz/
noun
授予者；商议者；赋予者
Those who confer, or take part in a conference or discussion.
The conferrers met to discuss the terms of the agreement. 授予者们开会讨论协议的条款。
synonyms: discussants, participants, conferees, delegates
antonyms: recipients, receivers
