countercharges:/ˈkaʊntərˌtʃɑːrdʒɪz/ /ˈkaʊntərˌtʃɑːrdʒəz/
noun
反控诉，反指控
an accusation made in response to a previous accusation
例句：He filed countercharges against his accuser.  他对他原告提出了反诉。

例句：The company issued countercharges, denying all allegations of wrongdoing.  该公司提出反诉，否认所有不当行为的指控。

近义词：counteraccusations, counterclaims
反义词：charges, accusations
词性:n.
