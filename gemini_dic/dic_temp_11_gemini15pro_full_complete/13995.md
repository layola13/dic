auriflamme:/ˈɔːrɪˌflæm/| n.|金焰；金黄色火焰；王室的旗帜|A gold-colored flame or banner, especially the royal standard of France.|The auriflamme, once a sacred banner, became a symbol of French royalty.|近义词：banner, standard, pennant|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain flag")|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *oriflambe, from Medieval Latin auri flamma (“golden flame”).
