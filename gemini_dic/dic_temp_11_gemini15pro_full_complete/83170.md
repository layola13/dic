guttulous:/ˈɡʌtʃʊləs/ | adj. | 水滴状的；滴下的；多雨的 | Covered with or resembling drops of liquid; rainy.  | The guttulous leaves shimmered in the morning sunlight.  清晨的阳光下，水滴状的叶子闪闪发光。 |  The guttulous sky threatened a downpour.  多雨的天空预示着一场倾盆大雨。| 近义词: dripping, dewy, rainy | 反义词: dry, arid | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *guttulosus, from guttula (“little drop”), diminutive of gutta (“drop”).
