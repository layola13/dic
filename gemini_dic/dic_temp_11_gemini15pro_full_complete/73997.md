fortuity:/fɔrˈtuːɪti/ /fɔːrˈtuːɪti/
noun
偶然事件；意外；巧合
The occurrence of something by chance.  A lucky accident.
The meeting was a fortunate fortuity.  这次会面是一次幸运的巧合。
synonyms: chance, accident, coincidence, serendipity, fluke
antonyms: intention, plan, design, purpose
n.
