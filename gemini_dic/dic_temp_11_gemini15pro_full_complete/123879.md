morays:/ˈmɔːreɪz/ | n. | 海鳗 | Large, often brightly colored eels of warm seas, some of which are venomous.  They inhabit crevices in reefs and rocky areas. | Divers should be cautious around morays, as some species can be aggressive. 潜水员在海鳗周围应该小心，因为有些种类具有攻击性。|  The moray eel lurked in its crevice, waiting for unsuspecting prey. 海鳗潜伏在裂缝中，等待毫无戒心的猎物。 | Synonyms: eel, conger eel | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal) | Noun
