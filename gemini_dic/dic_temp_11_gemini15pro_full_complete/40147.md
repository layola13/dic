contemptibility:/kənˌtɛmptɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
可鄙性，轻蔑性
The contemptibility of his actions shocked everyone.
他的行为的可鄙性震惊了所有人。
His contemptibility earned him the scorn of his colleagues.
他的卑劣为他赢得了同事们的蔑视。
近义词：despicability, vileness, baseness
反义词：admirability, respectability, worthiness
词性：noun

contemptible:/kənˈtɛmptɪbl/
adj.
可鄙的，卑劣的
His contemptible behavior led to his dismissal.
他卑劣的行为导致他被解雇。
Lying is a contemptible act.
撒谎是一种卑鄙的行为。
近义词：despicable, vile, base, mean
反义词：admirable, respectable, worthy
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contemptibilitas, from Latin contemptibilis (contemptible) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  The Latin contemptibilis is from contemnere (to despise, scorn) + -bilis (suffix forming adjectives).  Contemnere is from com- (intensive prefix) + temnere (to despise).
