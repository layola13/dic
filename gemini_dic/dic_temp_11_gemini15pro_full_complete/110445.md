noun

The quality of being likeable or attractive.  This word is archaic and rarely used.
He possessed a certain likerousness that drew people to him. 他具有一种吸引人的特质。
近义词:likeability, attractiveness, charm, appeal

词性:n.


Etymology:
From "like" (meaning pleasing or agreeable) + "-er" (forming a comparative adjective) + "-ness" (forming a noun denoting a state or quality).
