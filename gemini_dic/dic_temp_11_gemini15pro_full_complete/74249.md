foxholes:/ˈfɒkshəʊlz/ (UK), /ˈfɑːkshəʊlz/ (US)| n. |散兵坑，散兵壕|A hole dug in the ground as a temporary protection for one or two soldiers from enemy gunfire or shelling.|The soldiers huddled in their foxholes, waiting for the barrage to end. 士兵们挤在散兵坑里，等待炮火停止。|例句：The soldiers dug foxholes for protection during the night. 士兵们在夜间挖了散兵坑以求保护。

例句：Enemy fire forced the troops to take cover in their foxholes. 敌人的炮火迫使部队躲进散兵坑里。|近义词：trenches, dugouts, bunkers|反义词: N/A |词性: 名词
