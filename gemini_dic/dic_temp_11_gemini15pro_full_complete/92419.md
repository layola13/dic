hypokinetic:/ˌhaɪpəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌhaɪpoʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
运动不足的；活动过少的
Characterized by or resulting from decreased movement or activity.
Hypokinetic diseases are conditions caused by too little exercise and movement. 运动不足的疾病是由缺乏锻炼和运动引起的。
People with sedentary jobs are at risk of developing hypokinetic diseases. 久坐不动的人有患运动不足疾病的风险。
sedentary, inactive
active, kinetic
