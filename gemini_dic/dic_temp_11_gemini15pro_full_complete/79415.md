glints:/ɡlɪnts/| n. v.|闪烁，闪光；闪现；流露|A glint is a small flash of light, typically reflected from a shiny surface.  A glint can also refer to a brief, fleeting expression or quality that is perceived.|The sunlight glints off the water. 阳光在水面上闪闪发光。|近义词：sparkle, flash, gleam, shimmer, glitter|反义词：dullness|词性:名词/动词
