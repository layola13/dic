harmonizers:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzərz/ /ˈhɑːrmənaɪzər/| n.|协调者，和声歌手|People or things that create harmony, especially in music.  Those who sing or play harmony parts.|The harmonizers blended their voices beautifully. 和声歌手们优美地融合了他们的声音。|Synonyms: singers, musicians, conciliators, mediators|Antonyms: dissenters, disputants|noun

harmonizer:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzər/ /ˈhɑːrmənaɪzər/| n.|协调者，和声歌手|A person or thing that harmonizes.|The software acts as a harmonizer, blending different audio tracks seamlessly.  这款软件充当协调器，无缝地融合不同的音轨。|Synonyms: coordinator, unifier, blender|Antonyms: disruptor, divider|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From harmonize + -er.  Harmonize comes from Middle French *harmoniser*, from Late Latin *harmonizare*, from Ancient Greek *ἁρμονίζειν (harmonizein)*, from *ἁρμονία (harmonia)* "harmony".
