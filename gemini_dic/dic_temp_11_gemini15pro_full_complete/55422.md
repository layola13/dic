distilleries:/dɪˈstɪləriz/| n.|酿酒厂，蒸馏厂|A place where alcoholic drinks, especially whisky or other spirits, are produced by distilling.|The Scottish Highlands are famous for their whisky distilleries. 苏格兰高地以其威士忌酒厂而闻名。|Example:  Many small craft distilleries have opened in recent years. 近年来，许多小型工艺酿酒厂开业了。|Synonyms: brewery, winery|Antonyms: None|Noun
