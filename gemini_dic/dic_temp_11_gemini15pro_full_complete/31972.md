chemistries:/ˈkɛmɪstriz/
n.
化学反应; 化学作用; (常复数)相互吸引(或排斥);
The two of them lacked chemistry from the start.  他们俩从一开始就缺乏化学反应（指缺乏吸引力）。
The chemistries between these two substances are complex. 这两种物质之间的化学反应很复杂。
Synonyms: reactions, interactions, rapport, affinity
Antonyms: incompatibility, repulsion
noun
