irrevocable:/ɪˈrɛvəkəbl/ |adj.|不可撤销的；不能改变的|Not able to be changed, reversed, or recovered.|例句：His decision to leave the company was irrevocable. 他离开公司的决定是不可撤销的。|例句：The damage done to the environment is often irrevocable. 对环境造成的损害往往是不可逆的。|近义词：unalterable, irreversible, final, permanent|反义词：reversible, changeable, revocable|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin irrevocabilis, from in- ‘not’ + revocabilis (from revocare ‘recall’).
