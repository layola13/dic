cowardliness:/ˈkaʊərdlɪnəs/| n.|怯懦，胆小|The soldier was dismissed from the army for cowardice.  这名士兵因怯懦而被军队开除。|近义词：timidity, pusillanimity, faintheartedness|反义词：bravery, courage, valor|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *`cowardlinesse`, from Old French `coardise`, from `coart`, variant of `couard` ("coward"), of uncertain origin. Possibly from Old French `coe` ("tail") (referring to an animal with its tail between its legs), or from Latin `cauda` ("tail").
