outjourneying:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɜːrniɪŋ/
|n.|外出旅行，远行|The act of traveling beyond one's usual environment, especially for a long period or a specific purpose.|He's on an outjourneying to discover himself.  他正在远行以探索自我。|近义词: expedition, journey, voyage, odyssey|反义词: homecoming, return|词性: 名词

词源:  From "out-" (meaning "outside, beyond") + "journeying" (present participle of "journey," from Old French "jornee" meaning "a day's travel").  由"out-"（意为"外面，超越"）+ "journeying"（"journey"的现在分词，源自古法语"jornee"，意为"一天的旅行"）组成。
