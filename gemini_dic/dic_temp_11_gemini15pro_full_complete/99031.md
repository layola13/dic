interallelic:/ˌɪntərəˈliːlɪk/ | adj. | 等位基因间的 | Occurring between different alleles of a gene.  Relating to or involving interaction between different alleles of the same gene. | Example: Interallelic complementation can restore gene function. 等位基因间互补可以恢复基因功能。 |  Example: Interallelic interactions can influence phenotype. 等位基因间的相互作用会影响表型。 | Synonym: trans- | Antonym: cis- | word type: adjective
