half-drunk:/ˌhɑːf ˈdrʌŋk/ | adj. | 微醉的，半醉的 | Slightly intoxicated; not completely drunk. | He was half-drunk and started singing loudly. 他半醉了，开始大声唱歌。|  She stumbled into the room, half-drunk and giggling. 她跌跌撞撞地走进房间，半醉半醒地笑着。| 近义词: tipsy, merry, slightly intoxicated | 反义词: sober, completely drunk | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
half (Old English healf) + drunk (past participle of drink - Old English drincan)
