microsurgeries:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɜːrdʒəriːz/
noun
显微外科手术（复数）
Microsurgeries are delicate operations performed using specialized instruments and microscopes.
例句：The hospital specializes in complex microsurgeries. 这家医院专门从事复杂的显微外科手术。

例句：Microsurgeries are often used to reattach severed limbs. 显微外科手术常用于重新连接断肢。
近义词：microsurgical procedures, microoperations
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly traditional surgery or macroscopic surgery)
词性: Noun (Plural)
