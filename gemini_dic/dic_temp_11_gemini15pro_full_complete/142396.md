orthotropic:/ˌɔːrθəˈtrɑːpɪk/ | adj. | 正交各向异性的 | Having physical properties that differ along three mutually perpendicular axes.  This means the material exhibits different properties in three perpendicular directions. |  The orthotropic nature of wood makes it strong along the grain but weaker perpendicular to it.  木材的正交各向异性使得它沿着纹理方向很强，但垂直于纹理方向则较弱。| Example: The orthotropic material was used in the bridge construction due to its directional strength properties.  由于其方向强度特性，该正交各向异性材料被用于桥梁建造。| Synonym: anisotropic | Antonym: isotropic | adjective
