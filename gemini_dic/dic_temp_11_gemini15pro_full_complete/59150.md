The museum houses a fascinating collection of dynastic artifacts.  这个博物馆收藏了引人入胜的王朝文物。| 近义词：hereditary, ancestral, patrimonial | 反义词：non-hereditary, elective | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dynastique*, from Late Latin *dynasticus*, from Greek *dunasteia* "lordship, power," from *dunastēs* "lord, master," from *dunamai* "to be able, to have power."
