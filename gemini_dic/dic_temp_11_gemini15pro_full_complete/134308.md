noninsertions:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsɜːʃənz/
noun
非插入
The absence of insertions or the act of not inserting something.  Specifically in genetics, it refers to the absence of a segment of DNA that is present in a reference sequence.
Noninsertions, along with deletions, are considered indels in genetic studies. 非插入和缺失在遗传学研究中被认为是插入缺失。
Near Synonyms: omissions, absences, gaps, deletions
Antonyms: insertions, additions
