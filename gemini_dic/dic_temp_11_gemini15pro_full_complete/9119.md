antimoralists:/ˌæntiˈmɔːrəlɪsts/
n.
反道德主义者
Those who oppose or reject traditional morality or moral principles.
The antimoralists argued that conventional morality was a tool of oppression. 反道德主义者认为传统的道德是压迫的工具。
The book was criticized by antimoralists for its conservative views.  这本书因其保守的观点而受到反道德主义者的批评。

immoralists, libertines, nonconformists
