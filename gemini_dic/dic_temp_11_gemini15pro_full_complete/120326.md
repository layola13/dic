minigolf:/ˈmɪniˌɡɒlf/ (UK), /ˈmɪniˌɡɑːlf/ (US)| n. |迷你高尔夫|A game played on a small course with obstacles, using a putter to hit a ball into a hole.|We played a round of minigolf on our vacation. 我们在假期玩了一轮迷你高尔夫。|Example: The family enjoyed a game of minigolf after dinner.  晚饭后，全家人玩了一场迷你高尔夫。|Synonym: miniature golf, putt-putt (especially US)|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "full-size golf")|Noun
