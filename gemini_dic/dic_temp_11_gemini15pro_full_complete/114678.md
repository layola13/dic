malignantly:/məˈlɪɡnəntli/
| adv. | 恶性地；怀有恶意地 | Showing great malevolence; viciously.  Characterized by or indicative of malignancy. | The tumor spread malignantly. 肿瘤恶性扩散。 | The rumors were spread malignantly to damage her reputation. 这些谣言被恶意散布，以损害她的名誉。 | 近义词: malevolently, viciously, wickedly, spitefully | 反义词: benignly, kindly, benevolently | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *malignant-, from Latin malignans, present participle of malignare "to do maliciously," from male "badly" + -gn- + -are (frequentative suffix).
