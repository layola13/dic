cannula:/ˈkæn.jʊ.lə/ | n. | 套管;插管 | A thin tube inserted into a vein or body cavity to administer medicine, drain fluid, or insert a surgical instrument. | The doctor inserted a cannula into the patient's vein to administer fluids. 医生将套管插入患者的静脉以输液。|  The surgeon used a cannula to drain fluid from the wound.  外科医生用套管排出伤口里的液体。 | catheter, tube | - | noun


cannular:/ˈkæn.jʊ.lər/ | adj. | 套管的;插管的 | Relating to or resembling a cannula. | The cannular procedure was performed successfully. 套管手术顺利完成。| The cannular insertion site was cleaned and bandaged. 插管部位已清洁并包扎。| tubular | - | adjective
