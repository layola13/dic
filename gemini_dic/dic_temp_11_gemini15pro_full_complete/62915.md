enliveners:/ɪnˈlaɪvənərz/
|n.|活跃气氛的人或物；使…更生动的事物|Something that enlivens; something that makes something more lively or interesting.|The bright colors of the flowers were enliveners in the otherwise drab room. 这些鲜艳的花朵为原本单调的房间增添了活力。|Synonyms: stimulants, animators, invigorators|Antonyms: depressants, dampeners|noun
