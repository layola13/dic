bestowable:/bɪˈstoʊəbl/
adjective
可授予的，可赋予的
Capable of being bestowed; that may be conferred or presented as a gift, honor, etc.
The medal of honor is bestowable upon those who demonstrate exceptional courage.  荣誉勋章可以授予那些表现出非凡勇气的人。
The king declared the land bestowable to his loyal knight. 国王宣布这块土地可以授予他忠诚的骑士。
近义词:conferrable, grantable, awardable
反义词:unworthy, undeserving
词性:adjective
