calumba:/kəˈlʌmbə/
n.
calumba root, colombo root
卡伦巴根，科伦坡根
The dried root of the calumba plant, used as a tonic and stomachic.
卡伦巴植物的干燥根，用作滋补剂和健胃剂。
Calumba is known for its bitter taste and is used in traditional medicine. 卡伦巴以其苦味而闻名，用于传统医学。
Synonyms: colombo root, Jatumba
Antonyms: None
Noun
