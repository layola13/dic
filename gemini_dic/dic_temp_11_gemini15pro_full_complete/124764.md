mrt:/ˌɛmˌɑːrˈtiː/ /ˌɛmˌɑːrˈtiː/ | n. | Mass Rapid Transit (地铁) |  An electric railway system in a metropolitan area, typically operating underground or elevated. |  Singapore's MRT system is very efficient.  新加坡的地铁系统非常高效。 | synonym: subway, underground, metro | antonym: bus, taxi | noun

mrt:/ˌɛmˌɑːrˈtiː/ /ˌɛmˌɑːrˈtiː/ | abbreviation | Magnetic Resonance Therapy (磁共振治疗) | A form of alternative medicine that claims to use pulsed electromagnetic fields to treat various health conditions. | Some clinics offer MRT as a treatment for arthritis. 一些诊所提供磁共振治疗关节炎。 | synonym: pulsed electromagnetic field therapy | antonym: conventional medicine | abbreviation
