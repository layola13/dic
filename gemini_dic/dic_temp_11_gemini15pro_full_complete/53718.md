disclosures:/dɪˈskloʊʒərz/
n.|披露；揭露|The act of making something known or public, especially something previously secret. |The company made several disclosures about its financial situation.  该公司公布了几个关于其财务状况的信息。|revelations, announcements, declarations|concealment, suppression|noun

disclosure:/dɪˈskloʊʒər/
n.|披露；揭露|The act of making something known or public, especially something previously secret. |The disclosure of his criminal record shocked everyone.  他的犯罪记录被披露后震惊了所有人。|revelation, announcement, declaration|concealment, suppression|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *disclosen*, from Anglo-French *desclosir* (to uncover, reveal), ultimately from Latin *discludere* (to open, uncover, reveal).
