fanner:/ˈfænər/ | n. | 扬谷扇, 鼓风机, 扇风的人 | A person or device that fans something, especially to winnow grain or to increase ventilation.  A fanner can also refer to a firearm that discharges multiple projectiles simultaneously. | The farmer used a fanner to separate the wheat from the chaff. 农民用扬谷扇把麦粒和谷壳分开。| Synonyms: winnower, blower, ventilator | Antonyms: collector | Noun
