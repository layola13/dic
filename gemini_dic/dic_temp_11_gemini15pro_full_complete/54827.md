dispersion:/dɪˈspɜːrʒən/ /dɪˈspɜːrʃən/| n.|分散，散布，传播；离差，色散|The dispersion of seeds is important for plant reproduction.  种子的散布对植物繁殖很重要。|例句：The dispersion of light through a prism creates a rainbow. 光线通过棱镜的色散形成了彩虹。|近义词：distribution, scattering, dissemination|反义词：concentration, gathering, collection|词性:名词

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ /dɪˈspɜːrs/| v.|分散，散布，传播|The police dispersed the crowd. 警察驱散了人群。|例句：The wind dispersed the clouds. 风吹散了云。|近义词：scatter, distribute, disseminate|反义词：gather, collect, assemble|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dispersio, from Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere "to scatter," from dis- "apart" + spargere "to strew, scatter."
