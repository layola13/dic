non-breeding:/ˌnɒnˈbriːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 非繁殖期的 | Not breeding; not involved in or related to the process of reproduction.  | Many birds adopt a different plumage during the non-breeding season. 许多鸟类在非繁殖季节会换上不同的羽毛。| The non-breeding plumage of the male is duller than the breeding plumage. 雄性非繁殖期的羽毛比繁殖期的羽毛暗淡。 |  Synonyms: quiescent, dormant | Antonyms: breeding, reproductive | adjective
