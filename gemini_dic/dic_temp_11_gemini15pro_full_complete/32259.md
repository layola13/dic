chic:/ʃiːk/
adjective
优雅的；时髦的；别致的
She looked chic in her new dress. 她穿着新连衣裙，看起来很时髦。
例句：The hotel had a chic, modern design. 这家酒店拥有时尚的现代设计。
例句：Her chic apartment was featured in a design magazine. 她那套别致的公寓被刊登在一本设计杂志上。
近义词：stylish, fashionable, elegant
反义词：frumpy, dowdy, shabby
词性:adj.

词源: French, from German schick 'skillful,' 'suitable.'
