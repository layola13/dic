galactopoietic:/ɡəˌlæktəpɔɪˈɛtɪk/
adjective
催乳的，促乳的
Stimulating or increasing the flow of milk.
Galactopoietic herbs can help nursing mothers increase their milk supply. 催乳草药可以帮助哺乳期妈妈增加乳汁分泌。
This medication has galactopoietic properties. 这种药物具有催乳特性。
近义词:lactation-stimulating, milk-producing
反义词:lactation-inhibiting
词性:adjective

词源:
From Greek *galakto-* (milk) + *-poietic* (producing).
