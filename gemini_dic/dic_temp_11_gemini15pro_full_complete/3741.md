agilely:/ˈædʒɪlli/ | adv. | 敏捷地，灵活地 | In a quick and nimble manner; nimbly. | The dancer moved agilely across the stage.  舞者在舞台上灵活地移动。| nimbly, swiftly, deftly, quickly | clumsily, awkwardly, stiffly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From agile + -ly.  Agile comes from French agile, from Latin agilis "nimble, quick," from agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
