galvanology:/ˌɡælvəˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |直流电疗法；电疗学|The study of the therapeutic application of direct current electricity.  It involves the use of a low-voltage direct current to stimulate muscles or nerves, often for pain relief or to improve muscle function.|He's studying galvanology to understand how electrical stimulation can help with pain management.| 近义词: electrotherapy, electrotherapeutics | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  *galvano-* (relating to electricity, after Luigi Galvani, Italian physician and physicist) + *-logy* (study of).  Galvani's experiments with frog legs led to the discovery of bioelectricity.
