frizzle:/ˈfrɪzl/ | v. n. |  卷曲；油炸至发出咝咝声；使卷曲；油炸至发出咝咝声 | To make something, especially hair, form tight curls, often by heat; to fry something until it is crisp and makes a sizzling sound. | The heat frizzled my hair.  高温把我的头发烤卷了。|  curl, crisp, sear | straighten, flatten | verb
frizzle:/ˈfrɪzl/ | n. |  卷曲；油煎声 | The act of frizzing or the state of being frizzed. |  There was a frizzle of bacon in the pan.  平底锅里的培根发出咝咝的油煎声。 | curl, crispness | smoothness, flatness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *frizer (compare modern French friser "to curl"), from Frankish *frisan, from Proto-Germanic *frisōną (“to curl, crisp”).
