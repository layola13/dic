cleanly:/ˈkliːnli/ | adv. |干净地，整洁地，彻底地| in a clean way; completely and effectively | She cleanly sliced the cake into eight pieces. 她干净利落地把蛋糕切成了八块。| 近义词：neatly, tidily, efficiently, completely, thoroughly | 反义词：messily, sloppily, incompletely | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *clenliche, from Old English clǣnlīċe (“purely, cleanly, splendidly, brightly, elegantly, clearly, innocently, righteously”), equivalent to clean +‎ -ly.
