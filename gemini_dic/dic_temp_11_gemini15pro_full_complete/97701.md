inimaginable:/ɪnɪˈmædʒɪnəbl/ | adj. | 不可想象的，难以想象的 | Not capable of being imagined or conceived; beyond the power of imagination. | The pain he suffered was unimaginable. 他遭受的痛苦难以想象。 |  The vastness of space is unimaginable.  太空的浩瀚是不可想象的。 |近义词：inconceivable, unthinkable, unbelievable | 反义词： imaginable, conceivable, thinkable | 词性: adjective

inimaginably:/ɪnɪˈmædʒɪnəbli/ | adv. | 不可思议地，难以想象地 | To an unimaginable degree; incredibly. | The universe is unimaginably vast. 宇宙浩瀚得不可思议。 | The task was unimaginably difficult. 这项任务难度超乎想象。 | 近义词： incredibly, unbelievably, inconceivably | 反义词： conceivably, imaginably | 词性: adverb
