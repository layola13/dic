haircloth:/ˈhɛəklɔːθ/ (US), /ˈhɛəklɒθ/ (UK)| n.| haircloth; sackcloth;  粗麻布; 苦衣|A coarse, rough fabric made of horsehair or camel hair, traditionally worn as a garment of penance or mourning.|The hermit wore a haircloth shirt as a sign of his devotion.|Synonyms: sackcloth, burlap|Antonyms: silk, satin|Noun
