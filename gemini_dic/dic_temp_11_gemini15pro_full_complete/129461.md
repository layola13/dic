non-adventurous:/ˌnɒn ədˈventʃərəs/ (US) /ˌnɒn ədˈventʃərəs/ (UK)| adj. |不爱冒险的；缺乏冒险精神的|Not willing to try new or difficult things; not involving excitement or danger.|She's a non-adventurous eater and sticks to plain food. 她饮食上不爱冒险，只吃清淡的食物。|He led a rather non-adventurous life. 他过着相当平淡的生活。|近义词：unadventurous, cautious, timid, conservative|反义词：adventurous, daring, bold, reckless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + adventurous (from adventure + -ous).  The word "adventure" ultimately comes from the Latin "adventura," meaning "a thing about to happen."
