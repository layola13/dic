co-obligor:/kəʊˈɒblɪɡər/ /koʊˈɑːblɪɡər/| n. |共同债务人，共同义务人|A person or entity who is jointly obligated with another party to fulfill a debt or other financial obligation.|例句：Both my husband and I are co-obligors on the mortgage. 我和我丈夫都是抵押贷款的共同债务人。

例句：The bank required a co-obligor on the loan because the student had no credit history. 由于学生没有信用记录，银行要求贷款有一个共同债务人。

|近义词：joint debtor, co-signer, guarantor|反义词:|词性:noun
