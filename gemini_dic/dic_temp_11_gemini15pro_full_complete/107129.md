ladyish:/ˈleɪdiɪʃ/| adj.|女子气的，女人似的；娇气的|Resembling or characteristic of a lady, often in a negative or overly refined way.  Suggesting an excessive concern with feminine propriety or delicacy, sometimes to the point of affectation.|She had a rather ladyish way of speaking, which some found affected. 她说话的方式有点女子气，有些人觉得很做作。|近义词：effeminate, womanish, prissy, dainty|反义词：masculine, manly, robust|词性:形容词
