commune:/ˈkɒmjuːn/ (UK) /kəˈmjuːn/ (US)| n. vi.|公社;密切交往|A group of people living together and sharing possessions and responsibilities.  To feel in harmony with; to communicate with someone or something in a spiritual way.|例句：The commune members shared all their resources. 公社成员共享所有资源。|近义词：collective, cooperative|反义词:individual, separate|词性:名词
