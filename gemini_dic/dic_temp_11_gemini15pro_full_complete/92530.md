hypoxias:/haɪˈpɒksiəz/
n.
缺氧（复数）
deficiency in the amount of oxygen reaching the tissues.
例句：Multiple hypoxias can lead to organ damage. 多次缺氧会导致器官损伤。

例句：The climbers experienced hypoxias at high altitude. 登山者在高海拔地区经历了缺氧。
近义词：anoxias, oxygen deficiencies
反义词：hyperoxias
词性：名词
