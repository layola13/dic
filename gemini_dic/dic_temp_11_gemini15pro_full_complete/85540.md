hatcheries:/ˈhætʃəriz/| n. |孵化场；孵化器|A place where eggs are hatched, especially fish eggs.  A building or enclosure for hatching eggs, typically those of fish or poultry.|The salmon hatcheries release millions of young fish into the river each year. 每年鲑鱼孵化场都会向河里放生数百万条幼鱼。|Synonyms: incubator, hatchery, breeding ground|Antonyms:  None (Difficult to have a direct antonym for a physical place)|noun
