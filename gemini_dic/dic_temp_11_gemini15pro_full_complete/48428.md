deduct:/dɪˈdʌkt/| vt.|扣除，减去|To subtract or take away from a total.|例句：They deducted the cost of the repairs from his salary. 他们从他的工资中扣除了修理费用。|近义词：subtract, withdraw, discount|反义词:add, increase|verb
deduction:/dɪˈdʌkʃən/| n.|扣除，减除；推论，演绎|The act or process of deducting; an amount that is deducted; the process of reasoning from general principles to specific instances.|例句：Tax deductions can save you money.  税收减免可以为你省钱。|近义词：subtraction, withdrawal, inference|反义词:addition, increase|noun
