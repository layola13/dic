overcares:/ˌoʊvərˈkɛrz/| v.|过分关心，过度照顾|To care for someone or something excessively, often to a detrimental degree.|She overcares for her plants, watering them so much that they drown. 她过分关心她的植物，浇水太多以至于它们淹死了。|近义词：fuss over, dote on, coddle|反义词:neglect, ignore|动词

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "care" (verb meaning "to feel concern or interest").  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or surplus of the action denoted by the base verb.  Thus, "overcare" literally means to care too much.
