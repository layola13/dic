heteromorphic:/ˌhɛtərəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|异形的，不同形态的|Occurring in two or more different forms, especially at different stages in the life cycle.|Example 1: The butterfly is a heteromorphic insect, with a larval stage (caterpillar) that is very different from the adult form. 蝴蝶是一种异形昆虫，其幼虫阶段（毛毛虫）与成虫形态截然不同。

Example 2:  Many plants exhibit heteromorphic leaves, with different shapes depending on their position on the stem. 许多植物表现出异形叶，其形状因其在茎上的位置而异。|近义词：polymorphic, allomorphic|反义词：monomorphic, isomorphic|词性: adjective
