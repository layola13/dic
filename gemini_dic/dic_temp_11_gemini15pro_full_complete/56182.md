dodecastyle: /ˌdoʊdɪkəˈstaɪl/ | n. | 十二柱式 | Having twelve columns in front, as of a temple.  A portico or façade with twelve columns. | The Parthenon, though technically octastyle, is nearly dodecastyle due to the close spacing of its columns.  帕特农神庙虽然严格来说是八柱式，但由于其柱子间距紧密，几乎是十二柱式。 | The temple was a magnificent dodecastyle structure, inspiring awe in all who beheld it. 这座寺庙是一座宏伟的十二柱式建筑，所有看到它的人都感到敬畏。 | 近义词: twelve-columned | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like tetrastyle (four columns) or hexastyle (six columns) depending on context) | 词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dōdeka* (twelve) + *stylos* (column)
