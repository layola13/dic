faucet:/ˈfɔːsɪt (US), ˈfɔːsɪt (UK)| n.|水龙头|A device that controls the flow of liquid, especially water, from a pipe.|例句：Turn off the faucet when you're done brushing your teeth. 刷完牙后关掉水龙头。

例句：The faucet is dripping, we need to fix it. 水龙头在滴水，我们需要修理它。|近义词：tap, spigot|反义词: None|词性:noun
