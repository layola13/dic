dimplier:/ˈdɪmpliər/ | adj. | 更多酒窝的，酒窝更深的 | Having more or deeper dimples. | Her dimplier smile made her even more charming. 她笑起来酒窝更深了，显得更加迷人。 |  近义词:  more dimpled | 反义词: less dimpled | 词性: 形容词

dimple:/ˈdɪmpl/ | n. | 酒窝；小凹陷 | A small hollow place, especially one that appears on a person's cheek when they smile, or on the surface of something. |  She has a dimple in her left cheek. 她的左脸颊上有一个酒窝。 | 近义词:  hollow, indentation | 反义词:  bump, bulge | 词性: 名词

词源:  From dimple + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dumpilaz.
