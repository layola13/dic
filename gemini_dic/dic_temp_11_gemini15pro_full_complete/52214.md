diathermaneity:/ˌdaɪəθɜːrˈmeɪnɪti/| n. |透热性；热透过性|The property of transmitting radiant heat; transparency to heat. |The diathermaneity of the material allowed the infrared radiation to pass through.  这种材料的透热性允许红外辐射穿过。|Synonyms: transmissivity, permeability, penetrability|Antonyms: opacity, impermeability|noun

diathermanous:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmənəs/| adj. |透热的；可让热通过的| Allowing radiant heat to pass through; transparent to heat. |Diathermanous materials are used in infrared heaters. 透热材料用于红外加热器。|Synonyms: transmissive, permeable, penetrable|Antonyms: opaque, impermeable|adjective
