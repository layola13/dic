conjoiner:/kənˈdʒɔɪnər/ | n. | 连接词，连接体 |A word or morpheme that connects words, phrases, clauses, or sentences.  A conjoiner joins together linguistic units.|  Conjoiners like "and" and "but" are essential for clear writing. 像“和”和“但是”这样的连接词对于清晰的写作至关重要。 | synonym: connective, conjunction, link, joiner | antonym: disjoiner, separator | noun
