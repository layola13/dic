calcite:/ˈkælˌsaɪt/ | n. |方解石|A common crystalline form of calcium carbonate, CaCO3, occurring in a variety of colors and forming rocks such as limestone and marble. | Calcite is a major component of limestone. 方解石是石灰石的主要成分。

Calcite crystals can be found in many caves. 在许多洞穴中可以发现方解石晶体。|近义词: aragonite, chalk (in some forms)|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)|词性: noun
