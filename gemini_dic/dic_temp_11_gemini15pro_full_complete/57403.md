dpi:/ˌdiː piːˈaɪ/| n. |dots per inch, 每英寸点数|A measure of printing or video display resolution.  It refers to the number of individual dots that can be placed in a line within the span of one inch.|The printer has a resolution of 600 dpi. 这台打印机的分辨率为600 dpi.|近义词：resolution, ppi|反义词:|noun
