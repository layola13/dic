installers:/ɪnˈstɔːlərz/ (US) /ɪnˈstɔːləz/ (UK)| n.|安装人员，安装程序|People or programs that install something, such as software or equipment.|The installers will be here tomorrow to set up the new air conditioning unit. 安装人员明天会来安装新的空调设备。|synonyms: fitters, erectors, setup crew|antonyms: removers, uninstallers|noun

installers:/ɪnˈstɔːlərz/ (US) /ɪnˈstɔːləz/ (UK)| n.|安装程序|A program that installs software.|I downloaded the installers for the new operating system. 我下载了新操作系统的安装程序。|synonyms: setup program, installation package|antonyms: uninstaller, removal program|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "install" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Install" comes from Old French "installer"  meaning "to establish, to put in place".  Ultimately derived from Latin "in-" (in, into) + "stallum" (a stall, place).
