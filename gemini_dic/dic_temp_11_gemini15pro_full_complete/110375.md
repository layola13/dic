lignin:/ˈlɪɡnɪn/ | n. |木质素|A complex organic polymer deposited in the cell walls of many plants, making them rigid and woody. |Lignin gives wood its strength. 木质素赋予木材强度。

Lignin is a major component of wood. 木质素是木材的主要成分。|近义词:xylogen |反义词:None |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lignum ("wood")
