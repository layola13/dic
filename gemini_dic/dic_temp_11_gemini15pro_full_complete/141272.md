opaque:/oʊˈpeɪk/ | adj. | 不透明的; 不透光的; 难理解的; 晦涩的 | Not able to be seen through; not transparent. Difficult to understand; obscure. |  例句1：The window was opaque with steam.  窗户因水蒸气而变得不透明。 | 例句2：His explanation was opaque to me. 他的解释让我难以理解。 | 近义词：obscure, unclear, impenetrable, nontransparent | 反义词：transparent, clear, translucent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From French opaque, from Latin opacus "darkened, shady, opaque," of uncertain origin.
