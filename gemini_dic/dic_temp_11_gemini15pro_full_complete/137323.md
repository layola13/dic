nontangential:/ˌnɒntænˈdʒɛnʃəl/
adjective
非切线的;直接相关的
Relating directly to the subject at hand; not digressing or irrelevant.
The professor's lecture was nontangential, focusing solely on the core concepts of the course. 教授的讲座没有偏题，只关注课程的核心概念。
Her questions were nontangential and helped to clarify the main points of the discussion.  她的问题切中要害，有助于澄清讨论的要点。
近义词:relevant, pertinent, germane, direct
反义词:tangential, irrelevant, digressive, circuitous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + tangential.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "tangential" refers to something that touches only lightly or is only slightly connected.  Therefore, "nontangential" means "not merely touching lightly," implying a direct and substantial connection.
