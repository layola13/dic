chivarras:/tʃɪˈvɑːrɑːs/| n. |墨西哥牛仔服; 刺绣皮裤|Embroidered leather trousers traditionally worn by Mexican horsemen.  They are often elaborately decorated and considered part of the charro outfit.|The charro proudly wore his chivarras.  这位墨西哥牛仔骄傲地穿着他的刺绣皮裤。|He danced in his chivarras, twirling and stomping his feet. 他穿着刺绣皮裤跳舞，旋转着、跺着脚。|Synonyms: charro pants|Antonyms: N/A|noun

chivarra:/tʃɪˈvɑːrɑː/| n. | practical joke; prank; hoax| A playful trick or mischievous act intended to deceive or amuse.|They played a chivarra on him by hiding his car keys. 他们跟他开了个玩笑，把他的车钥匙藏起来了。|The news report turned out to be a chivarra; it wasn't true at all. 这篇新闻报道原来是个恶作剧；它根本不是真的。|Synonyms: prank, joke, hoax, jape|Antonyms: seriousness, truth|noun
