metastatically:/ˌmɛtəˈstætɪkli/
| adv. |转移地，扩散地| In a way that involves metastasis; by metastasis.  The cancer had spread metastatically to his lungs.|
|例句：The cancer cells had spread metastatically throughout her body. 癌细胞已经扩散到她全身。|
|例句：The tumor was identified as metastatically spreading from the colon. 该肿瘤被确认为是从结肠转移扩散的。|
|近义词：diffusedly, disseminatedly|
|反义词: locally|
|词性: 副词|
|词源: From metastasis + -ically. Metastasis from Greek meta "change" + stasis "standing".|
