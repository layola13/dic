outgushes:/ˈaʊtɡʌʃɪz/

outgushes:/ˈaʊtɡʌʃɪz/
| v. |(情感、言语等)迸发，涌出|(To express (emotions, words, etc.) suddenly and unrestrainedly.)|His anger outgushed in a torrent of accusations. 他的愤怒化作一阵 accusations 的洪流迸发而出。|Synonyms: gush, pour, spill |Antonyms: suppress, repress|verb
