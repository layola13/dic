lever:/ˈliːvər/ (US) /ˈliːvə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|杠杆；控制杆；手段，方法|A rigid bar resting on a pivot, used to help move a heavy or firmly fixed load with one end when pressure is applied to the other.|She used a lever to pry open the crate. 她用杠杆撬开了板条箱。|近义词：crowbar, handle, tool, instrument, means, device|反义词: disadvantage|noun, verb
