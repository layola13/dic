inquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪər/ (US) /ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v.|询问，打听|To ask for information from someone.  It can be used in both formal and informal contexts, although it often carries a slightly more formal tone than "ask."|I inquired about the price of the car. 我询问了汽车的价格。|近义词：ask, question, investigate|反义词:answer, reply|verb
