influence:/ˈɪnfluəns/ (US), /ˈɪnflʊəns/ (UK)| n. vt.|影响；势力；影响力；产生影响的人或事|The weather influences our moods. 天气会影响我们的心情. |近义词：impact, effect, sway|反义词：insignificance, weakness|词性:名词,动词

influences:/ˈɪnfluənsɪz/ (US), /ˈɪnflʊənsɪz/ (UK)| n.|影响（influence的复数形式）|His father's influences shaped his career path. 他父亲的影响塑造了他的职业道路。|近义词：effects, impacts, factors|反义词：N/A (复数形式通常没有直接的反义词)|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *influence*, from Latin influentia (“a flowing in; influence”), from influens, present participle of influere (“to flow into”), from in- (“into, upon”) + fluere (“to flow”).
