neglectingly:/nɪˈɡlɛktɪŋli/ | adv. | 疏忽地，忽略地 | In a neglectful manner; with neglect. Showing or characterized by neglect. | He neglectingly left the door unlocked. 他疏忽地忘了锁门。| 近义词: carelessly, negligently, thoughtlessly | 反义词: carefully, attentively, diligently | 词性: 副词

词源: From neglect + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *neglegere*, from *nec* "not" + *legere* "to gather, collect, choose, read".
