overornamental:/ˌoʊvərˌɔːrnəˈmɛntəl/| adj.| 过于装饰的，过度装饰的| Excessively ornamented; decorated more than is necessary or tasteful.| The overornamental architecture of the building seemed gaudy and ostentatious. 这座建筑物过于装饰的建筑风格显得俗气而炫耀。|  The Christmas tree was overornamental, laden with so many baubles that the branches drooped. 圣诞树装饰得太过头了，挂满了饰品，树枝都垂下来了。 | 近义词：ostentatious, flamboyant, excessive, elaborate, fussy| 反义词：simple, plain, understated, minimalist| 词性: 形容词

overornamental:| /ˌoʊvərˌɔːrnəˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 过于装饰的，过度装饰的 | Formed from "over-" (meaning excessive) + "ornamental" (relating to decoration).  Indicates an excess of ornamentation, often to the point of being gaudy or tasteless.  | Etymology: "Over-" from Old English *ofer, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *uberi. "Ornamental" from Late Middle English, from Latin ornamentum "equipment, adornment," from ornare "to adorn, equip."|
