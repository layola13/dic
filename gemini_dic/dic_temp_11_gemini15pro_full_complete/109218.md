leig:/leɪɡ/ | n. | 臣下；效忠；忠诚；臣服 |  An archaic word meaning allegiance or loyalty.  Derived from Old French "liege" and ultimately from Frankish *letigi "free from obligation, attached to the king's service". In feudal times, a liege was a vassal who owed allegiance and service to a lord. |  He swore leig to the king. 他向国王宣誓效忠。| 近义词: allegiance, loyalty, fealty, fidelity, devotion | 反义词: treachery, disloyalty, betrayal | 词性: 名词
