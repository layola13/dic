abbots:/ˈæbəts/| n.| 男修道院院长；大寺院主持 |The abbots of the monasteries met to discuss the new religious decrees.  修道院的院长们开会讨论新的宗教法令。|  The new abbot was known for his piety and wisdom. 新院长以他的虔诚和智慧而闻名。| synonyms: superiors, heads, priors | antonyms: monks, subordinates | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *abbad*, from Late Latin *abbas*, from Greek *ἀββᾶς (abbas)*, from Aramaic *abba* meaning "father".
