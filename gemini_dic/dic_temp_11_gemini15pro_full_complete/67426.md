extinct:/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. | 灭绝的，绝种的 | No longer in existence; having no living members. | The dodo is an extinct bird. 渡渡鸟是一种已灭绝的鸟类。|  The dinosaurs went extinct millions of years ago. 恐龙在几百万年前灭绝了。| 近义词: vanished, defunct, died out | 反义词: extant, existing, living | 词性: adjective
词源: From Latin extinctus, past participle of extinguere "to extinguish, quench, put out, destroy."
