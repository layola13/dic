headhunter:/ˈhedˌhʌntər/| n.|猎头|A person who identifies and approaches suitable candidates employed elsewhere to fill business positions.|The headhunter called me about a job opportunity at a tech startup. 猎头打电话给我，说一家科技初创公司有一个工作机会。|Synonyms: recruiter, executive searcher|Antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym)|noun
