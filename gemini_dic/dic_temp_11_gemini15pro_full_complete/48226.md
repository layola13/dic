deconstruction:/ˌdiːkənˈstrʌkʃən/
n.|解构，拆析|A critical approach that analyzes the instability of meaning in texts.|Literary critics use deconstruction to interpret novels. 文学评论家使用解构主义来诠释小说。|analysis, interpretation|construction, synthesis|noun

deconstruct:/ˌdiːkənˈstrʌkt/
vt.|解构，拆析|To analyze a text or idea in order to reveal its underlying assumptions and contradictions.|The professor deconstructed the poem to show its hidden meanings. 教授解构了这首诗，以揭示其隐藏的含义。|analyze, dismantle|construct, synthesize|verb
