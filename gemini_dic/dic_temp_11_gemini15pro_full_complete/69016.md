fattens:/ˈfætənz/| v.|使发胖，使肥沃|Makes something become fatter or richer; increases the fat content of something.|The farmer fattens his pigs for market.  农民养肥了他的猪准备上市销售。|近义词：enriches, plumps, fattens up|反义词:thins, weakens, slims|词性:verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fættnian, from fætt (fat).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *faitaz (fat).
