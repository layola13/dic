nonassisters:/ˌnɒnəˈsɪstəz/
n.
非助攻者，不提供帮助的人
People who do not provide assistance or support, particularly in a sporting context where assists are measured statistically.
The coach was frustrated with the nonassisters on the team, as they weren't creating opportunities for their teammates to score.  教练对球队中不助攻的球员感到沮丧，因为他们没有为队友创造得分机会。
The nonassisters were criticized for their selfish play.  那些不助攻的球员因其自私的打法而受到批评。
Near Synonyms: non-contributors, unhelpful players
Antonyms: assisters, helpers, facilitators
