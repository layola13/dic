henna:/ˈhɛnə/ (US), /ˈhɛnə/ (UK)| n. |指甲花，散沫花，指甲花染料|A small shrub or tree of the loosestrife family, native to tropical Africa, Asia, and Australia, widely cultivated for its leaves, which yield a reddish-brown dye. Also, the dye itself.|She used henna to create intricate patterns on her hands. 她用指甲花在她手上绘制了复杂的图案。|近义词：dye, pigment|反义词: None|词性: Noun


henna:/ˈhɛnə/ (US), /ˈhɛnə/ (UK)| adj. |指甲花的，散沫花的|Made from or dyed with henna.|She wore a henna tattoo on her shoulder. 她在肩膀上纹了一个指甲花纹身。|近义词：reddish-brown|反义词: None|词性: Adjective


Etymology: From Arabic ḥinnā’ حناء.
