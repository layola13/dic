creators:/kriˈeɪtərz/
n.
创造者；创作者；创始人
People who create or originate something.
The creators of the video game designed a complex and immersive world.  这款电子游戏的创造者设计了一个复杂而身临其境的世界。
The creators of the new technology were awarded the Nobel Prize.  这项新技术的创造者获得了诺贝尔奖。
synonyms:  authors, designers, inventors, makers, originators, producers
antonyms:  destroyers, demolishes
noun
