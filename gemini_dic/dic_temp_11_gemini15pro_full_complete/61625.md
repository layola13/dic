emigrating: /ˈemɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
verb
移民，移居国外
the act of leaving one's own country to settle permanently in another;
Many people are emigrating from the country due to the ongoing conflict.  由于持续的冲突，许多人正在移居国外。
My grandparents were emigrating from Italy to America in the early 20th century. 我的祖父母在20世纪初从意大利移民到美国。
近义词: migrating, relocating, departing
反义词: immigrating, returning
词性: Verb (present participle)

emigrate: /ˈemɪɡreɪt/
verb
移民，移居国外
to leave one's own country in order to settle permanently in another;
He emigrated to Canada in search of better job opportunities. 他为了寻找更好的工作机会移民到加拿大。
She emigrated from Russia after the revolution.  她在革命后从俄罗斯移民。
近义词: migrate, relocate, depart
反义词: immigrate, return
词性: Verb
