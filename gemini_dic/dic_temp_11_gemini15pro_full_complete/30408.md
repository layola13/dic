centipede:/ˈsɛntɪpiːd/| n.|蜈蚣|A long, thin creature with many pairs of legs, typically one pair per body segment.|例句：I saw a centipede crawling across the bathroom floor. 我看到一只蜈蚣爬过浴室地板。

例句：Centipedes are known for their venomous bites. 蜈蚣以其有毒的叮咬而闻名。|近义词：myriapod|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centipeda, from centum (“hundred”) + pes (“foot”).
