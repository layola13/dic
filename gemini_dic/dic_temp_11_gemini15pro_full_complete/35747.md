cochineals:/ˌkɒtʃɪˈniːlz/| n. |胭脂虫，胭脂红|A scale insect (Dactylopius coccus) native to Mexico and Central America that feeds on cacti. Dried, crushed cochineals are used to produce a crimson dye called carmine.|Cochineals are used to produce a vibrant red dye. 胭脂虫被用来生产鲜艳的红色染料。|Synonym: carmine insect|Antonym: N/A|Noun

cochineal:/ˌkɒtʃɪˈniːl/| adj. |胭脂红的|Of a crimson color derived from cochineal insects.|She wore a cochineal dress to the party. 她穿着一件胭脂红的裙子去参加聚会。|Synonym: crimson, carmine|Antonym: N/A|Adjective
