introspected:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪd/ | v. | 内省的，反省的 |  examined one's own thoughts and feelings. | After the difficult conversation, she sat quietly and introspected.  在艰难的谈话之后，她静静地坐着反省。|  例句：He introspected deeply on his past mistakes. 他深刻地反省了自己过去的错误。| 近义词：reflective, contemplative, meditative, self-examining | 反义词：unreflective, outward-looking | 词性: 动词(past participle)

introspect:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛkt/ | v. | 内省，反省 |  examine one's own thoughts and feelings. |  I need some time to introspect before making a decision.  我需要一些时间反省一下再做决定。 | 例句：She spent hours introspecting about her life choices. 她花了几个小时反省她的人生选择。 | 近义词：reflect, contemplate, meditate, self-examine | 反义词：ignore, disregard | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin introspectus, past participle of introspicere "to look within," from intro- "inward" + specere "to look."
