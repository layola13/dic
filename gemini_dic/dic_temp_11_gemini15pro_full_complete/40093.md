contaminable:/kənˈtæmɪnəbl/
adjective
可污染的
Susceptible to contamination; capable of being contaminated.
The water supply is contaminable by industrial waste.  这供水系统容易被工业废料污染。
The open wound was highly contaminable.  这个开放性伤口很容易被感染。
近义词:pollutable, susceptible, vulnerable
反义词:impervious, resistant, protected
词性:adjective
