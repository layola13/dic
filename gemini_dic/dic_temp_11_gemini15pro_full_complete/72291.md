flounced:/flaʊnst/| v.| 猛然离去；暴跳如雷；装饰以荷叶边 | to go or move in an exaggeratedly impatient or angry manner; to decorate with a flounce | She flounced out of the room, slamming the door behind her. 她怒气冲冲地走出房间，砰地一声关上了门。| synonyms: stormed, swept, sashayed | antonyms: tiptoed, crept, glided | verb

flounce:/flaʊns/| n.| 荷叶边；猛然离去；暴跳如雷 | a wide ornamental strip of gathered or pleated material sewn by its inner edge to a skirt, etc., hanging loosely; an act of flouncing | The dress had a delicate flounce at the hem. 这条裙子的下摆有一圈精致的荷叶边。| synonyms: ruffle, frill, furbelow | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun
