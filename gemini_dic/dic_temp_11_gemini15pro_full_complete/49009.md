degraded:/dɪˈɡreɪdɪd/
adjective
堕落的，退化的，降级的
Reduced in rank, status, or character; lowered in quality or value.
The degraded image quality made it difficult to identify the suspect.  退化的图像质量使得难以识别嫌疑人。
The building had become degraded over the years due to neglect.  由于疏于照管，这座建筑多年来已经破败不堪。
近义词:debased, deteriorated, diminished, impaired, lowered, reduced
反义词:elevated, enhanced, improved, upgraded
词性:adjective

degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/
verb
降低，贬低，使降级；使退化，使恶化
To reduce someone's worth or value; to lower the quality of something.
The scandal degraded the politician's reputation. 这桩丑闻败坏了这位政治家的名声。
Exposure to sunlight can degrade certain plastics.  阳光照射会使某些塑料老化。
近义词:debase, demean, deteriorate, diminish, humble, lower, reduce
反义词:elevate, enhance, improve, promote, upgrade
词性:verb

词源: From Latin *degradare*, from de- ‘down’ + gradus ‘step, grade’.
