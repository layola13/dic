nondefamatory:/ˌnɒndɪˈfæmətri/ | /ˌnɑːndɪˈfæməˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 非诽谤性的 | Not defamatory; not containing or constituting defamation. |  The article was found to be nondefamatory and therefore legally permissible. 这篇文章被认定为非诽谤性，因此在法律上是允许的。

The lawyer ensured that all statements made were nondefamatory. 律师确保所有声明均不构成诽谤。| 近义词: harmless, innocent, inoffensive | 反义词: defamatory, libelous, slanderous | 词性: adjective
