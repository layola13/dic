chantable:/ˈtʃæntəbl/ | adj. | 可唱的；适合吟诵的 | Suitable for chanting or singing; easily chanted. | The melody was simple and chantable, perfect for a children's choir.  这旋律简单易唱，非常适合儿童合唱团。| The monks chanted chantable verses from the ancient scriptures.  僧侣们吟诵着来自古代经文的适合吟诵的诗句。 |  近义词: singable, melodious, tuneful | 反义词: unmelodious, unmusical | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From chant + -able.  "Chant" comes from Old French "chanter" (to sing), from Latin "cantare" (to sing).  "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being."
