illuminances:/ɪˌluːmɪnənsɪz/
n.
光照度（复数）
The total luminous flux incident on a surface, per unit area.
The illuminances of the two rooms were vastly different, one brightly lit, the other dim. 两个房间的光照度差异很大，一个灯火通明，另一个昏暗。
illuminance, light level, brightness
darkness, dimness
noun
