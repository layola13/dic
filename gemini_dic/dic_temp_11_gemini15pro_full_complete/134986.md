nonnaval:/nɒnˈneɪvəl/
adjective
非海军的，非舰队的
Not related to or connected with a navy or naval forces.
The army played a crucial role in the nonnaval defense of the country. 陆军在国家的非海军防御中发挥了至关重要的作用。
The government allocated a significant portion of the budget to nonnaval military expenses. 政府将很大一部分预算分配给了非海军军事开支。
近义词:civilian, military (in certain contexts)
反义词:naval
词性:adjective

词源: "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "naval" (from Latin "navis," meaning "ship").
