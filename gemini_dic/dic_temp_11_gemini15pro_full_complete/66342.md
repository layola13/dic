exculpations:/ˌɛkskʌlˈpeɪʃənz/
n.
辩解；开脱；免罪
the act of clearing someone of blame or guilt
The lawyer presented exculpations for his client's alleged involvement in the crime. 律师为他的客户涉嫌参与犯罪提出了辩解。
synonyms: acquittals, exonerations, justifications, defenses
antonyms: accusations, condemnations, incriminations
noun
