copperworm:/ˈkɑːpərˌwɜːrm/ | n. | 铜包线；铜线虫 |A larva of the click beetle, especially Agriotes spp., that is slender, hard-bodied, yellowish brown, and often destructive to crops. |  The copperworm has damaged the roots of several of our plants. 铜线虫损坏了我们几株植物的根部。|

The copperworms infested the potato field. 铜线虫侵扰了马铃薯田。 | 近义词: wireworm, click beetle larva | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词
