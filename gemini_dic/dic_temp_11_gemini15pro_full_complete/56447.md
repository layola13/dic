dolmens:/ˈdɒl.mɛnz/ (UK) /ˈdoʊl.mənz/ (US)| n. |石棚墓，支石墓|A megalithic tomb with a large flat stone laid on upright ones.|例句：The dolmens of Ireland are fascinating examples of prehistoric architecture. 爱尔兰的石棚墓是史前建筑的迷人例子。

例句：Researchers are studying the dolmens to understand the burial rituals of ancient cultures.  研究人员正在研究石棚墓，以了解古代文化的埋葬仪式。

近义词：megaliths, tombs, burial chambers|反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite)|词性: Noun
