giddy-up:/ˈɡɪdiˌʌp/ | int. | 加油；驾；快走（用于催促马匹前进） | Used to urge a horse to start or go faster. | Giddy-up, horsey!  加油，小马！ | Example: The cowboy yelled "Giddy-up!" and the horse galloped away. 牛仔喊了一声“驾！”，马就飞奔而去。 | Synonym:  go, mush, get up | Antonym: whoa, stop | interjection

giddy-up:/ˈɡɪdiˌʌp/ | n. | 催马前进的吆喝声 | The word used to urge a horse forward. |  With a "giddy-up," he spurred his horse onward. 他喊了一声“驾”，策马前进。 | Example: The sound of "giddy-up" echoed across the plains. “驾”的声音在平原上回荡。 | Synonym:  call, command | Antonym: N/A | noun
