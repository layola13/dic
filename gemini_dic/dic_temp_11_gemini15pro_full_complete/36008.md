codicil: /ˈkɒdɪsɪl/ (US) /ˈkəʊdɪsɪl/ (UK)| n. |遗嘱附录，附加条款|An addition or supplement that explains, modifies, or revokes a will or part of one.|Example: He added a codicil to his will, leaving his watch to his grandson. 他在遗嘱中添加了一个附录，把他手表留给了他的孙子。

Example:  The codicil clearly stated his wishes regarding the distribution of his art collection.  这份遗嘱附录清楚地说明了他关于其艺术收藏品分配的愿望。


|近义词：addendum, amendment, supplement, appendix|反义词: base document (although not a direct opposite)|词性: noun
