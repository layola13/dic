hedonism:/ˈhiːdənɪzəm/ | n. |享乐主义；快乐主义|The pursuit of pleasure; sensual self-indulgence.|例句：Their hedonism was evident in the lavish parties they threw. 他们的享乐主义从他们举办的奢华派对中可见一斑。

例句：He embraced hedonism and lived a life of pleasure. 他信奉享乐主义，过着快乐的生活。|近义词：pleasure-seeking, indulgence, epicureanism |反义词: asceticism, self-denial |词性: noun
词源: From Greek *hēdonē* "pleasure,"  from *hēdys* "sweet."
