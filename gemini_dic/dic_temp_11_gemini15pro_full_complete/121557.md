mismeter:/ˌmɪsˈmiːtər/
n.
错韵；韵律错误
A line of verse having an incorrect number of metrical feet or syllables, or a verse made up of such lines.
The poem contained a glaring mismeter in the third stanza. 这首诗的第三节中有一个明显的韵律错误。
近义词: metrical flaw, rhythmic error
反义词: perfect meter, correct rhythm
词性: noun
