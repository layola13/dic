misspace:/ˈmɪsˌspeɪs/ | n. | 错误空间; 拼写错误导致的空格;  |A misspace is an error where a space is either inserted incorrectly or omitted where it should be present. |  She made a misspace in her email address, so the message bounced back. 她在电子邮件地址中输入了一个错误的空格，所以邮件被退回了。| 近义词: typo, misspelling | 反义词: correct spacing | 词性: 名词
