nondigestiblenesses:/ˌnɒndaɪˈdʒɛstɪbəlnəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːndaɪˈdʒɛstɪbəlnəsɪz/
n.
无法消化；不消化性；难消化性
The state or quality of being nondigestible.  The extent to which something is not capable of being digested.
Certain types of fiber contribute to the nondigestiblenesses of food.  某些类型的纤维导致食物的难消化性。
The nondigestiblenesses of some artificial sweeteners can cause digestive discomfort. 一些人造甜味剂的无法消化性会导致消化不适。
近义词: indigestibility, non-digestibility
反义词: digestibility
词性: noun
