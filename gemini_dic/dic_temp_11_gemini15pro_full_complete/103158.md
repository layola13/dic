jerseyite:/ˈdʒɜːrziˌaɪt/
noun
新泽西州人；新泽西居民
A native or resident of the U.S. state of New Jersey.
He's a Jerseyite, born and bred. 他是土生土长的新泽西人。
synonyms: New Jerseyan
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, potentially resident of another state, e.g., New Yorker)
n.
