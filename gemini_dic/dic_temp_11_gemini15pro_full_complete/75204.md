froe:/froʊ/
n.
劈木楔，劈木工具
A wedge-shaped tool for splitting wood, especially shingles or staves.
He used a froe to split the logs into shingles. 他用劈木楔把原木劈成木瓦。
近义词: wedge, cleaver
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps something that joins instead of splits like "adhesive")
