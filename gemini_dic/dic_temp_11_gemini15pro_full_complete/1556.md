achievement: /əˈtʃiːvmənt/ | n. | 成就；完成；达到 | Something done successfully; something gained by working or trying hard. |  She felt a great sense of achievement when she finished the marathon. 她跑完马拉松后感到满满的成就感。

Her academic achievements were celebrated by the whole school. 她的学术成就受到了全校的赞扬。


近义词: accomplishment, attainment, feat, triumph, victory | 反义词: failure, defeat, setback | 词性: noun
