ladled:/ˈleɪdld/| v. |舀出；盛出(汤、菜等)|To serve or transfer (a liquid) with a ladle. |She ladled the soup into bowls. 她把汤舀进碗里。|The waiter ladled gravy over the mashed potatoes. 服务员把肉汁淋在土豆泥上。|近义词：spooned, served, dished out|反义词: poured (for liquids that don't require a ladle)|词性：动词
