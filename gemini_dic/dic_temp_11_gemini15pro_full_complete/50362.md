depopularizes:/dɪːˈpɒpjʊləraɪzɪz/
verb
使不再受欢迎；减少…的居民
To reduce the popularity of something or someone; to cause a place to lose population.
The rise of social media has depopularized traditional forms of advertising. 社交媒体的兴起使得传统的广告形式不再受欢迎。
Government policies have depopularized rural areas. 政府的政策使得农村地区人口减少。
synonyms: marginalize, unpopularize, diminish, reduce
antonyms: popularize, promote, boost, enhance
word type: verb
