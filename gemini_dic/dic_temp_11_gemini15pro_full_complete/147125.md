overloath:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊθ/| adj.|极不情愿的，非常不愿意的|Extremely unwilling; reluctant.|He was overloath to admit his mistake. 他极不情愿承认自己的错误。|Example: She was overloath to leave her family and move to a new city. 她非常不愿意离开家人搬到一个新的城市。|Synonyms: reluctant, unwilling, averse, disinclined|Antonyms: eager, willing, keen, enthusiastic|Etymology: From Old English *oferlāð "too loathsome," from *ofer- "over" + *lāð "loathsome."|Word Type: Adjective
