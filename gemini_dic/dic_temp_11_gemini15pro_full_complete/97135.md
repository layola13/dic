infiltration's:/ˌɪnfɪlˈtreɪʃənz/
n.
渗透，渗入；（尤指敌方的）潜入；浸润
The process of entering or gaining access to something (especially an organization, system, or territory) surreptitiously and gradually, often with harmful intent.
The infiltration's of enemy spies into the government was a serious security breach. 敌方间谍渗透到政府内部是一个严重的安保漏洞。
synonyms: penetration, permeation, incursion, infiltration
antonyms: expulsion, exclusion, withdrawal
noun
