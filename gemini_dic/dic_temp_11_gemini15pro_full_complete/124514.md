mounjaro:/ˌmaʊnˈdʒɑːroʊ/ | n. |蒙扎罗|A brand name for tirzepatide, a medication used for the treatment of type 2 diabetes. It is a glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) receptor agonist and a glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide (GIP) receptor agonist.|Mounjaro helps control blood sugar levels in adults with type 2 diabetes. 蒙扎罗有助于控制2型糖尿病成年人的血糖水平。|  Mounjaro is administered once weekly via subcutaneous injection. 蒙扎罗每周一次通过皮下注射给药。 |synonyms: tirzepatide (generic name) | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a brand name drug.  One could list medications with the opposite effect, like those that raise blood sugar, but that's not a true antonym.) | noun
