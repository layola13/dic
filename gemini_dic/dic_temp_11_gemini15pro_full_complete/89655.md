hooky:/ˈhʊki/| n.|逃学，旷课|The act of staying away from school or work without permission.|He played hooky and went to the beach. 他逃学去海滩了。|Synonyms: truancy, absenteeism|Antonyms: attendance, presence|noun

hooky:/ˈhʊki/| adj. (informal)|很棒的，吸引人的|Attractive or appealing, often in a slightly mischievous or playful way.|That new song has a hooky melody. 那首新歌的旋律很吸引人。|Synonyms: catchy, appealing, alluring|Antonyms: dull, boring, unappealing|adjective
