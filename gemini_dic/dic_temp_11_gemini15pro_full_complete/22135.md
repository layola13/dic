boltrope:/ˈboʊltˌroʊp/ | n. | 帆脚索，系帆的绳索 | A rope sewn to the edge of a sail for strength and to attach it to the spars of a ship. | The boltrope was frayed and needed replacing before the next voyage. 帆脚索磨损了，下次航行前需要更换。 |  The boltrope held the sail securely against the strong wind. 帆脚索牢牢地系住帆，抵御强风。| synonyms: footrope, leechline | antonyms: N/A | noun
