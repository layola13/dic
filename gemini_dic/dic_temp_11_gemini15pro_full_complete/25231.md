budless:/ˈbʌdlɪs/
adjective
无芽的；没有花蕾的
Without buds.  Lacking flower buds.
The budless branches reached towards the sky, stark against the winter gray.  光秃秃的树枝伸向天空，在冬日的灰暗中显得格外醒目。
Early frost left the rose bushes budless this spring. 早霜使得今年春天的玫瑰丛没有长出花蕾。
近义词: flowerless, bloomless
反义词: budding, flowering
词性: adjective
