confiscation:/ˌkɒnfɪˈskeɪʃn/ (US: /ˌkɑːnfɪˈskeɪʃn/) | n. | 没收，充没 | The act of taking something away from someone, especially as a punishment or because it is illegal. | The government ordered the confiscation of all illegal weapons. 政府下令没收所有非法武器。|  synonyms: seizure, appropriation, forfeiture, impoundment | antonyms: restitution, return, restoration | noun

confiscate:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪt/ (US: /ˈkɑːnfɪskeɪt/) | vt. | 没收，充公 | To officially take something away from someone, especially as a punishment or because it is illegal. |  Customs officials confiscated his passport. 海关官员没收了他的护照。| synonyms: seize, impound, appropriate | antonyms: return, restore | verb
