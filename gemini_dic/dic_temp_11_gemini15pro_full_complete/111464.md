lo-tech:/ˌloʊˈtɛk/ | adj. | 低技术的；技术含量低的 | Using simple or old technology; not advanced. |  Example:  The school's lo-tech computer lab was filled with outdated machines. 学校的低科技电脑实验室里摆满了过时的机器。

Example:  He prefers lo-tech solutions to complex problems. 他更喜欢用低技术方案来解决复杂问题。| 近义词: low-technology, simple, basic, rudimentary, primitive | 反义词: high-tech, advanced, sophisticated, cutting-edge | 词性: adjective
