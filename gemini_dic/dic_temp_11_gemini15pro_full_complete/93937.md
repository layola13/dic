immunodeficiency: /ˌɪmjʊnoʊdɪˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. | 免疫缺陷 | A state in which the immune system's ability to fight infections is compromised or entirely absent. |  People with immunodeficiency are more susceptible to infections. 免疫缺陷的人更容易感染。| synonyms: immune deficiency, immune suppression | antonyms: immunocompetence | noun

immunodeficiency: /ˌɪmjʊnoʊdɪˈfɪʃənsi/ | adj. | 免疫缺陷的 | Characterized by or relating to a deficiency in the immune response. |  Immunodeficiency disorders can be inherited or acquired. 免疫缺陷疾病可以是遗传的，也可以是后天获得的。| synonyms: immunodeficient, immunocompromised | antonyms: immunocompetent | adjective
