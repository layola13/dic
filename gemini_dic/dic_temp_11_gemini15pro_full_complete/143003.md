outbelched: /ˌaʊtˈbɛltʃt/
verb
大声地喷出; 比...喷出更多(更响)
to belch more loudly or forcefully than someone or something else.
The volcano outbelched smoke and ash. 火山喷出大量的烟雾和火山灰。
The dragon outbelched the other dragons in the competition.  在这场比赛中，这条龙比其他龙喷出的火焰更多。
近义词: erupt, spew, emit
反义词: inhale, absorb
词性: verb

词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "belch" (verb meaning to expel gas noisily from the stomach through the mouth)
