onsite:/ˈɑːnsaɪt/ /ˈɔːnsaɪt/| n.|现场工作，现场服务|Work or services performed at a specific location. |The onsite was completed within two days. 现场工作在两天内完成。|近义词：fieldwork |反义词:offsite work|noun
