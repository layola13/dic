overhomogenizes: /ˌoʊvərˌhoʊməˈdʒɛnaɪzɪz/ | v. | 过度均质化，过度同质化 | Makes something too uniform, eliminating variation or diversity.  | The policy overhomogenizes the city's cultural landscape, leading to a loss of unique local traditions.  这个政策使得城市文化景观过度同质化，导致独特的当地传统丧失。| The media's focus on a narrow range of issues overhomogenizes public opinion. 媒体对少数几个问题的关注导致公众舆论过度单一化。 | synonyms: standardizes, uniformizes, levels | antonyms: diversifies, varies, differentiates | verb
