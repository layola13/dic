courageousnesses:/kəˈreɪdʒəsnəsɪz/| n. |勇气，勇敢 (罕用，更常用courage)|The state or quality of being courageous; bravery.  This word is grammatically correct, but rarely used.  "Courage" or "bravery" are preferred. |He showed great courageousnesses in the face of danger. (Rarely used - prefer "He showed great courage/bravery in the face of danger.")|近义词：courage, bravery, valor, intrepidity|反义词: cowardice, fear, timidity|词性:名词

词源: From "courageous" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of state or quality). "Courageous" ultimately derives from the Old French "corage" (heart, spirit, courage) from Latin "cor" (heart).
