adumbrate: /əˈdʌmbreɪt/ | v. | 预示;  略微提及; 概括; 勾勒出…的轮廓 | to suggest or outline sketchily; to foreshadow; to partially disclose | The architect adumbrated the main features of his plan.  建筑师概述了他计划的主要特点。 | synonyms: outline, sketch, foreshadow, prefigure, intimate, suggest, indicate | antonyms: elaborate, detail, specify, clarify | verb

adumbrated: /əˈdʌmbreɪtɪd/ | adj. |  被概括的; 被预示的 | outlined sketchily; foreshadowed |  The adumbrated figure in the mist was barely visible.  雾中隐约可见的身影几乎看不见。 | synonyms: outlined, sketched, foreshadowed, intimated, suggested | antonyms: detailed, elaborated, specified, clarified | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adumbrare* "to sketch, represent faintly," from *ad-* "to" + *umbrare* "to shade," from *umbra* "shade, shadow."
