outwinging:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过（尤指在数量或程度上）
surpassing, exceeding (especially in number or extent)
The company's profits are outwinging those of its competitors. 该公司的利润超过了其竞争对手。
The team outwinged all expectations and won the championship.  这个团队超出了所有预期，赢得了冠军。
近义词: surpassing, exceeding, outstripping, outdoing
反义词: lagging behind, trailing, falling short of
词性: verb


outwinging:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋɪŋ/
adjective
胜过，超过（尤指在数量或程度上）的
surpassing, exceeding (especially in number or extent)
The outwinging success of the project surprised everyone.  该项目巨大的成功让所有人感到惊讶。
