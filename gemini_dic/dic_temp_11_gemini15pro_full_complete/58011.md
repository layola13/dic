drivve: /ˈdrɪv/ (This word likely intends to be "drive") | v. n. | 驾驶，开车；驱使，迫使；动力，干劲 | To operate and control the movement of a vehicle; to compel or urge; a strong urge or motivation. |

例句：
I will drive to the beach tomorrow.  我明天要开车去海滩。

He drove the car carefully. 他小心地驾驶着汽车。

近义词：operate, steer, propel, motivate, ambition
反义词：ride, follow, discourage, apathy
词性：verb, noun
