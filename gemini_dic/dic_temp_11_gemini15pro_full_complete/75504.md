fryer:/ˈfraɪər/ (US), /ˈfraɪə/ (UK)| n.|油炸锅；炸鸡店；要油炸的食物|A kitchen appliance used for deep frying food; a person or place that sells fried food, especially fried chicken; something that is to be fried.|例句：I bought a new air fryer. 我买了一个新的空气炸锅。|例句：We got takeout from the local chicken fryer. 我们从当地的炸鸡店买了外卖。|近义词：deep fryer, frying pan|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially 'oven' or 'grill' if contrasting cooking methods)|词性:noun
