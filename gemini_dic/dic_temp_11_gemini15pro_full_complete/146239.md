overexpressing:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛsɪŋ/
|v.|过表达；过度表达|The cells were overexpressing the protein. 这些细胞过度表达了这种蛋白质。|
|adj.|过表达的；过度表达的|The overexpressing cells were used in the experiment. 过表达的细胞被用于实验中。|
|Synonym:|overproducing, hyper-expressing|
|Antonym:|underexpressing, downregulating|
|Etymology:|over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + expressing (present participle of "express," from Latin expressus, past participle of exprimere "to press out, represent")|
