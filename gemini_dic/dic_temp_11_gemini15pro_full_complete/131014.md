nonaudiblenesses:/ˌnɒnˈɔːdəblnəsɪz/
noun
不可闻性；非听觉性
The state or quality of being nonaudible; inability to be heard.
Due to the nonaudiblenesses of the dog whistle, only the dog reacted. 由于狗哨的不可闻性，只有狗做出了反应。
近义词:inaudibility, unhearableness
反义词:audibility, hearableness
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not
audible (adjective): able to be heard
-ness (suffix): forms nouns indicating a state or quality
