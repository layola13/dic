lease:/liːs/
n.|租赁，租约|A contract allowing the use of property for a specified time in exchange for payment.|He signed a lease for a new apartment. 他签了一份新公寓的租约。|rental, agreement, tenancy|purchase, ownership|noun
lease:/liːs/
vt.|出租，租赁|To grant the use of property for a specified time in exchange for payment.|They leased the building to a new company. 他们把这栋楼租给了一家新公司。|rent, let, hire|buy, purchase|verb
