muzzleloader:/ˈmʌzəlˌloʊdər/ | n. | 前膛装填枪 | A firearm (typically a rifle or pistol) that is loaded through the muzzle. |  The frontiersmen relied on their muzzleloaders for hunting and defense.  拓荒者依靠他们的前膛枪狩猎和自卫。| synonyms: flintlock, musket | antonyms: breechloader | noun

muzzleloaders:/ˈmʌzəlˌloʊdərz/ | n. | 前膛装填枪（复数） |  Plural form of muzzleloader, referring to multiple firearms loaded through the muzzle. |  Muzzleloaders are still used today in historical reenactments and some types of hunting.  前膛枪至今仍在历史重演和某些类型的狩猎中使用。| synonyms: flintlocks, muskets | antonyms: breechloaders | noun
