non-compounder:/ˌnɒn kəmˈpaʊndər/ | n. | 非复利计算者；非累积者；不加重的人（或物）| Someone or something that does not compound (in various senses).  Specifically in finance, it refers to an investment or interest that does not compound, meaning the interest earned is not added to the principal to generate further interest. | Many retirees prefer non-compounder investments for a steady, predictable income stream. 许多退休人员更喜欢非复利投资，以获得稳定、可预测的收入来源。 | The doctor explained that the medication was a non-compounder and wouldn't interact negatively with her other prescriptions. 医生解释说，这种药物是非累积的，不会与她的其他处方产生负面影响。 | synonyms: simple interest investment, non-cumulative | antonyms: compounder, cumulative | noun
