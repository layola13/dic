confabulatory:/kənˈfæbjʊləˌtɔːri/ /kənˈfæbjəˌleɪtəri/
adjective
中文翻译：虚构的，编造的，错构的
英文解析：Relating to or characterized by confabulation, the act of fabricating or distorting memories, often without the intention to deceive.  It describes something that is made up, invented, or based on false memories.
例句：His confabulatory account of the incident was clearly a product of his illness.  他关于这起事件的虚构描述显然是他疾病的产物。

例句：The patient's confabulatory tendencies made it difficult to get a clear history. 病人编造的倾向使得很难获得清晰的病史。

近义词：fabricated, invented, fictitious, delusional, false, untrue
反义词：factual, true, real, genuine, authentic
词性：adjective
