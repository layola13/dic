hubbub:/ˈhʌbʌb/ (US), /ˈhʌbʌb/ (UK)| n.|喧闹，嘈杂，混乱|A loud, confused noise, as of many voices talking at once; a chaotic situation.|例句：The hubbub in the marketplace made it difficult to hear.  市场里的喧闹声让人难以听到对方说话。

例句：There was a great hubbub after the announcement of the winner. 宣布获胜者后，现场一片喧闹。

近义词：commotion, uproar, din, clamor, racket|反义词:silence, quiet, peace, tranquility|词性:noun
