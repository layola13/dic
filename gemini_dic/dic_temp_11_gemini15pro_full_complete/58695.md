dumpiness:/ˈdʌmpɪnɪs/
noun
矮胖；粗短；臃肿
The state or quality of being dumpy; short and thickset.
Her dumpiness made her look older than she was. 她的矮胖让她看起来比实际年龄大。
近义词: stoutness, chubbiness, thickness, stockiness
反义词: slenderness, slimness, thinness
词性: noun
