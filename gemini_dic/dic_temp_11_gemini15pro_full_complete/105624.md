kinetic-artists:/kɪˈnɛtɪk ˈɑːrtɪsts/| n.|动态艺术家|Artists who create art that moves or appears to move, often using motors, machines, or natural forces like wind or water.  Kinetic art incorporates real or apparent movement into the artwork.|The kinetic-artists' exhibition featured mesmerizing mobiles and sculptures powered by small motors. 动态艺术家们的展览以由小型电机驱动的迷人的动态雕塑和悬挂式作品为特色。|The museum's new wing is dedicated to the works of pioneering kinetic-artists.  博物馆的新侧厅专门展出动态艺术先驱的作品。|Synonyms: mobile artists, movement artists|Antonyms: static artists, traditional sculptors|noun
