changet: /ˈtʃeɪndʒt/ |v. | changed (过去式), changed (过去分词) |改变，变化|The weather has changed dramatically. 天气发生了剧烈的变化。| synonyms: alter, modify, transform, vary, convert, shift, switch, mutate | antonyms: remain, stay, preserve, fix, stabilize| verb

Etymology: From Middle English *chaungen, from Old French changier, from Late Latin cambiare "to barter, exchange," of uncertain origin.
