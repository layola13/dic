nonapprehension:/ˌnɒnˌæprɪˈhɛnʃən/| n.|不理解，未能理解，缺乏理解力|The student's nonapprehension of the complex theorem frustrated the teacher.  这个学生对复杂定理的不理解让老师很沮丧。|Etymology: From "non-" (not) + "apprehension" (understanding). "Apprehension" comes from the Latin "apprehendere," meaning "to grasp, seize, understand."|例句：His nonapprehension of the situation led to a series of mistakes. 他对情况的不理解导致了一系列错误。

His nonapprehension of the instructions resulted in a failed experiment. 他未能理解指示，导致实验失败。

|近义词：misunderstanding, incomprehension, lack of understanding|反义词:understanding, comprehension, grasp|词性:名词
