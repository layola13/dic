exhortingly:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtɪŋli/ | adv. | 劝勉地，激励地 | In a manner that urges or encourages strongly. | The coach spoke exhortingly to his team, urging them to give their all in the final quarter. 教练激励地对他的队员讲话，敦促他们在最后一节全力以赴。 | The teacher exhortingly reminded the students to study for the upcoming exam. 老师劝勉学生们为即将到来的考试学习。 |  近义词: encouragingly, urgingly, persuasively | 反义词: discouragingly, dissuasively | 词性: 副词

exhort:/ɪɡˈzɔːrt/ | v. | 劝告，规劝，激励 | To strongly encourage or urge (someone) to do something. |  He exhorted his colleagues to join the protest. 他规劝他的同事加入抗议活动。 | She exhorted her children to work hard in school. 她激励她的孩子们在学校努力学习。| 近义词: urge, encourage, advise | 反义词: discourage, dissuade | 词性: 动词
