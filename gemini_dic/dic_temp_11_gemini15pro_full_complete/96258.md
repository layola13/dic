indicia:/ɪnˈdɪʃɪə/ | n. (pl.) |标志，标记，迹象 |Marks, signs, or indications, especially those that point to something else. |The postage marks on the envelope are indicia of its having been mailed. 信封上的邮戳是它已被寄出的标志。|Synonyms: signs, symbols, markers, emblems, tokens, evidence, proof |Antonyms:  lack of evidence, absence of proof |noun

indicia:/ɪnˈdɪʃə/ | n. (pl.) | (法律) 权所有标志 | (Law) distinguishing marks of ownership or origin. |The indicia of ownership were clearly displayed on the deed. 所有权标志清楚地显示在契约上。|Synonyms: insignia, hallmarks |Antonyms: N/A |noun
