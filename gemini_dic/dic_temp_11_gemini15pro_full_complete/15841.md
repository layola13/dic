ballastage: /ˈbæləstɪdʒ/ | n. |压舱物，压舱|Material, such as gravel, sand, or iron, placed low in a vessel to improve its stability.|The ship took on ballastage in preparation for the transatlantic voyage. 这艘船为准备横渡大西洋航行而装载压舱物。|Synonyms: ballast, counterweight|Antonyms: buoyancy|noun


ballastage: /ˈbæləstɪdʒ/ | v. |给…装压舱物|To furnish with ballast.|They ballasted the ship with rocks and sand. 他们用岩石和沙子给船装压舱物。|Synonyms: stabilize, steady|Antonyms: destabilize|verb
