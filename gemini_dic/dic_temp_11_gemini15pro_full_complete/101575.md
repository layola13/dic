ironwort:/ˈaɪərnwɜːrt/ | n. | 铁线莲，夏枯草 | A perennial herb with small, purplish flowers, native to Europe and Asia. Used in traditional medicine. |  The ironwort grew wild along the edges of the forest. 铁线莲沿着森林边缘野蛮生长。|  The herbalist recommended ironwort tea for its calming properties.  草药师推荐铁线莲茶，因为它有镇静的作用。 | synonyms:  wood betony, Sideritis | antonyms: N/A | noun
