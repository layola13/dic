inturgescence:/ɪnˈtɜːrˈdʒɛsəns/ | n. | 肿胀，膨胀；充盈 | The state of being swollen or distended, especially due to an increase in internal pressure. |  The inturgescence of the cells caused the plant to stand upright.  细胞的膨胀使植物直立。| synonyms: swelling, distension, tumescence, engorgement | antonyms: shrinkage, contraction, deflation | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *inturgescentia, from Latin inturgescere "to swell up," from in- "in" + turgescere "to swell."
