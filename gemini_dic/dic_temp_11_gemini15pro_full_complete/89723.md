hopeless:/ˈhəʊplɪs/| adj.|绝望的，无望的，没有希望的|Without hope; giving no ground for hope; desperate.  Feeling or causing despair about something.|例句：The situation seemed hopeless.  情况似乎毫无希望。|例句：She felt hopeless about her future. 她对自己的未来感到绝望。|近义词：despairing, desperate, despondent|反义词：hopeful, optimistic, promising|词性:形容词
词源: From hope + -less.
