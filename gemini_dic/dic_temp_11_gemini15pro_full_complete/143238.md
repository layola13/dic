outdazzles:/ˌaʊtˈdæzəlz/ | v. | 使黯然失色，胜过 |  To surpass or eclipse someone or something in brilliance or showiness. | Her performance completely outdazzled the other contestants. 她的表演完全使其他参赛者黯然失色。 | The new stadium outdazzled all the others in the league.  这座新体育场使联盟中的所有其他体育场都黯然失色。 | 近义词：outshine, eclipse, surpass | 反义词：pale, diminish | 词性：动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "dazzle" (verb meaning to impress or overwhelm with brilliance)
