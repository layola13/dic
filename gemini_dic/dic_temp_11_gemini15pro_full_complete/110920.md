lipogrammatist:/ˌlɪpəˈɡræmətɪst/
n.
回避特定字母的作家; lipogram作家
A writer who deliberately avoids using a particular letter.
Example: The lipogrammatist painstakingly crafted a novel without using the letter 'e'.  这位回避特定字母的作家煞费苦心地创作了一部没有使用字母“e”的小说。

Example:  He challenged himself to become a lipogrammatist, writing a short story without the letter 'a'. 他挑战自己成为一个lipogram作家，写一个没有字母“a”的短篇小说。
Synonym: wordplayer, letter-avoider
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps) writer who uses all letters freely, unrestricted writer
词性: noun
