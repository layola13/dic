decimators: /ˈdesɪmeɪtərz/
n.
屠杀者，毁灭者，大幅削减者
Those who or that which decimates.  A person, group, or thing that causes great destruction or devastation.
The barbarians were the decimators of the Roman Empire.  这些野蛮人是罗马帝国的毁灭者。
The locusts were the decimators of the crops. 这些蝗虫是农作物的毁灭者。
destroyers, ravagers, annihilators
creators, preservers, builders
noun
