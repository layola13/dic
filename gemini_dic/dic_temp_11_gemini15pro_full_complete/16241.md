banstickle:/ˈbænˌstɪkəl/ | n. | 班氏鱼 |A small North American freshwater fish (genus *Campostoma*) with a sucker mouth and a fleshy protuberance on the chin, typically found in clear streams. |例句：The banstickle uses its sucker mouth to attach to rocks in fast-flowing streams. 班氏鱼用它的吸盘嘴附着在快速流动的溪流中的岩石上。

例句：Banstickles are known for their unique chin protuberance. 班氏鱼以其独特的下巴突起而闻名。|近义词：stoneroller, creek chub (related species)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
