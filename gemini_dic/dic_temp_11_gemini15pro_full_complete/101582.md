irpe:/ˈɪrpeɪ/ | n. | 个人所得税 (意大利) |  Acronym for "Imposta sul Reddito delle Persone Fisiche," which translates to "Personal Income Tax" in Italian. It is the equivalent of income tax in other countries. |  The irpe rate in Italy is progressive, meaning higher earners pay a higher percentage.  意大利的个人所得税税率是累进的，这意味着收入越高的人支付的百分比越高。 | Synonym: income tax (general term), personal income tax | Antonym: N/A | noun
