mature:/məˈtʃʊə(r)/
|adj.|成熟的；成年人的；到期的；|vt.& vi.|使…成熟；使…完善；|vi.|成熟；到期|The mature apple fell from the tree. 成熟的苹果从树上掉下来。|He has a mature outlook on life. 他的人生观很成熟。|The bond will mature next year. 债券将于明年到期。|Warm weather matured the grapes early.  温暖的天气使葡萄早熟。|近义词：ripe, grown-up, due|反义词：immature, young, unripe|词性: 形容词，动词

词源：From Middle English *matur*, from Old French *matur*, *meür*, from Latin *matūrus* (“ripe, timely, early, of ripe age”), from Proto-Italic *maturos*, from Proto-Indo-European *\*meh₂-tú-ros*.
