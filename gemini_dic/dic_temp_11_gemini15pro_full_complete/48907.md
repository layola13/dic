defray:/dɪˈfreɪ/
verb
支付，承担费用
to provide money to pay (the cost of something)
The company defrayed the costs of his trip. 公司支付了他旅行的费用。
近义词: pay, cover, bear, meet
反义词: charge, bill, invoice
词性: verb

defrays:/dɪˈfreɪz/
verb(第三人称单数)
支付，承担费用
to provide money to pay (the cost of something)
The scholarship defrays her tuition fees. 奖学金支付了她的学费。
近义词: pays, covers, bears, meets
反义词: charges, bills, invoices
词性: verb
