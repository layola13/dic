clericalize: /ˈklɛrɪkəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 使事务化；使文职化；使官僚化 | To make something more clerical; to increase the involvement of clerical staff or procedures; to make something more bureaucratic and focused on paperwork. | The company clericalized many of its processes, leading to increased paperwork and delays.  这家公司将其许多流程事务化，导致文书工作增加和延误。| The university clericalized the admissions process, requiring extensive documentation. 大学将招生过程事务化，需要大量的文件。 | synonyms: bureaucratize, formalize | antonyms: streamline, simplify | verb
