intercondenser:/ˌɪntərˈkənˈdɛnsər/ | n. | 中间冷凝器 | A condenser placed between other condensers, often used to cool the output of one condenser before it enters another, increasing overall efficiency. | The intercondenser lowered the temperature of the refrigerant before it entered the main condenser. 中间冷凝器降低了制冷剂进入主冷凝器之前的温度。 | The use of an intercondenser improved the overall efficiency of the refrigeration system. 使用中间冷凝器提高了制冷系统的整体效率。 | synonym: intermediate condenser | antonym: N/A | noun
