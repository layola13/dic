algaroba:/ˌælɡəˈroʊbə/ | n. | 角豆树；豆荚| A thorny tree (Prosopis pallida) native to South America and widely naturalized in other warm regions, having fragrant yellow flowers and long pods containing edible pulp. | The algaroba tree is highly invasive in some areas.  角豆树在某些地区具有很强的入侵性。

The algaroba pods are used as animal fodder. 角豆树的豆荚用作动物饲料。| 近义词:mesquite, kiawe | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *algaroba*, from Arabic *الخروب* (*al-kharrūb*), meaning "carob tree".
