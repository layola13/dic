async: /eɪˈsɪŋk/ | adj. | 异步的 | Not occurring at the same time.  Used in computer programming to describe operations that don't block the execution of other operations while they're running. | Example: The `async` keyword allows you to write non-blocking code.  `async`关键字允许你编写非阻塞代码。 | Example:  Async functions are crucial for responsive user interfaces. 异步函数对于响应式用户界面至关重要。 | 近义词: asynchronous, non-blocking | 反义词: synchronous, blocking | 词性: adjective
