neurofibrilla:/ˌnʊəroʊfɪˈbrɪlə/ /ˌnjʊərəʊfɪˈbrɪlə/ | n. | 神经原纤维 | A delicate threadlike structure found in the cytoplasm of a nerve cell, and believed to be involved in the transport of materials within the cell.  It is a component of the neuron's cytoskeleton and plays a role in maintaining the neuron's shape and transporting substances along the axon. |  Neurofibrillas are essential for neuronal function. 神经原纤维对神经元功能至关重要. |  The study showed abnormalities in the neurofibrillas of affected neurons.  这项研究显示受影响神经元的原纤维存在异常. |  nerve fiber, axon filament | None | Noun
