allotropize:/əˈlɒtrəpaɪz/
verb
使同素异形化; 以不同形式存在
to exist in different allotropic forms; to cause (an element) to change into an allotrope
Phosphorus can allotropize into several forms, including white and red phosphorus. 磷可以形成几种同素异形体，包括白磷和红磷。
近义词:transform, convert, modify
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "stabilize" in a chemical context)
词性:v.
