freebooter:/ˈfriːbuːtər/| n.|海盗，掠夺者|A pirate or plunderer, especially one who preys on shipping.|例句：The freebooters attacked the merchant ship and stole its cargo. 海盗袭击了商船并偷走了货物。

例句：The notorious freebooter terrorized the coastal towns for years.  臭名昭著的海盗恐吓了沿海城镇多年。


近义词：pirate, buccaneer, raider, plunderer, corsair|反义词:lawman, police officer|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch vrijbuiter, from vrij "free" + buiter "plunderer".  The term originally referred to Dutch privateers who operated in the Caribbean in the 17th century.
