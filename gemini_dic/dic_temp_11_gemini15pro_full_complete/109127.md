legionary: /ˈliːdʒənəri/ (US), /ˈliːdʒənəri/ (UK)| n. adj.| 军团士兵；军团的|A member of a legion, especially a Roman legion.  Also used as an adjective to describe something related to a legion.|例句：The legionary stood guard at the gate. 军团士兵在门口站岗。

例句：The legionary fortifications were impressive. 军团的防御工事令人印象深刻。

近义词：soldier, trooper, legionnaire|反义词:civilian|词性: noun, adjective

legionary: /ˈliːdʒənəri/ (US), /ˈliːdʒənəri/ (UK)| adj.| 军团的|Relating to a legion, especially a Roman legion.|例句：The legionary eagle was a symbol of Roman power.  军团的鹰是罗马力量的象征。

近义词：military, army|反义词:civilian|词性: adjective
