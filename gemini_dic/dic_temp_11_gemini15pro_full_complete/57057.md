doubtingly:/ˈdaʊtɪŋli/| adv.|怀疑地，疑惑地|In a manner expressing doubt or uncertainty.  Showing hesitation or lack of confidence.|She looked at him doubtingly, unsure if he was telling the truth. 她怀疑地看着他，不确定他是否在说实话。|Near Synonyms: hesitantly, uncertainly, skeptically, dubiously|Antonyms: confidently, assuredly, certainly|词性: 副词

Etymology: From the adjective "doubting" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Doubting" comes from the verb "doubt," which ultimately derives from Old French "douter," from Latin "dubitare" meaning "to hesitate, waver," related to "dubius" meaning "doubtful."
