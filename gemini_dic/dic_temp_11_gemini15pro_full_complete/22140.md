bolus:/ˈboʊləs/ (US) /ˈbəʊləs/ (UK)| n.|团块，大丸药，推注剂|A single dose of a drug or other substance given all at once. A mass of chewed food.|The doctor administered a bolus of morphine. 医生注射了一剂吗啡。|The snake swallowed the mouse in a single bolus. 蛇一口吞下了老鼠。|Synonyms: dose, lump, mass|Antonyms: trickle, drip|noun

bolus:/ˈboʊləs/ (US) /ˈbəʊləs/ (UK)| n.|食团|A mass of chewed food ready to be swallowed.|The bolus of food moved down his esophagus. 食团沿着他的食道下移。|She struggled to swallow the large bolus. 她费力地吞下大块食物。|Synonyms: mouthful, cud|Antonyms: N/A|noun
