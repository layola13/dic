antimorality:/ˌæntiˌmɔːˈrælɪti/
n.
反道德；非道德；逆道德
Opposition to or rejection of traditional moral principles or beliefs.
Some see the artist's work as a celebration of antimorality, while others see it as a critique of modern society.
有些人认为这位艺术家的作品是对反道德的颂扬，而另一些人则认为这是对现代社会的批判。

The protagonist's descent into violence and debauchery is a chilling depiction of antimorality.
主人公堕落到暴力和放荡的行为中，是对反道德的令人不寒而栗的刻画。

Synonyms: immorality, amorality, wickedness, depravity
Antonyms: morality, virtue, righteousness
