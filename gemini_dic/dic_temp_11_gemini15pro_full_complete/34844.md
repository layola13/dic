clinics:/ˈklɪnɪks/| n.|诊所；门诊部|A clinic is a place where people go to receive medical treatment, often for a specific condition or type of care. It is usually smaller than a hospital and may be run by a single doctor or a group of doctors.|She goes to the fertility clinic every week. 她每周都去生育诊所。| The clinic offers free vaccinations for children. 这家诊所为儿童提供免费疫苗接种。 |synonyms: medical center, health center, doctor's office, surgery (British English)|antonyms: hospital (sometimes, as clinics are often part of a hospital)|noun
