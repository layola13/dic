hyperscale:/ˈhaɪpərˌskeɪl/| adj.|超大规模的，超扩展的|Relating to computing systems that are designed to be very large and powerful, and that can be expanded easily as needed. |例句：Hyperscale data centers are essential for cloud computing. 超大规模数据中心对于云计算至关重要。

例句：The company is investing in hyperscale infrastructure to support its growing business.  该公司正在投资超大规模基础设施以支持其不断发展的业务。|近义词：massive, large-scale, web-scale|反义词:small-scale, limited|词性:形容词

Etymology:  "hyper-" (Greek:  ὑπέρ, hupér, meaning "over, beyond") + "scale" (from Old Norse "skál" meaning "bowl, scale").  The prefix "hyper-" intensifies the meaning of "scale," indicating a scale that is exceptionally large or beyond typical limits.
