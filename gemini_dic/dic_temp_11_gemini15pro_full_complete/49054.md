dehortative:/dɪˈhɔːrtətɪv/
adjective
劝阻的，阻止的
Trying to dissuade someone from doing something.
The teacher's dehortative speech discouraged students from cheating on the exam. 老师劝诫性的讲话劝退了学生考试作弊。
近义词: dissuasive, discouraging, admonitory
反义词: hortatory, encouraging, exhortative
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *dēhortātīvus, from dēhortārī (“to urge away from, dissuade”), from dē- (“away from”) + hortārī (“to urge, encourage”).
