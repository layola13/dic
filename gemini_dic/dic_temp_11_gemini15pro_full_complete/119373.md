microcosmography:/ˌmaɪkrəʊkɒzˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 小宇宙描绘；世态缩影 | A description of a small world, often representing the larger world or human nature in miniature.  |  John Earle's "Microcosmography" offers a witty portrayal of 17th-century characters.  约翰·厄尔的《小宇宙描绘》对17世纪的人物进行了机智的刻画。 | synonyms: character sketch, portrayal, depiction | antonyms: macrocosm | noun
