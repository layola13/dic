kiln:/kɪln/
|n.|窑，炉|A furnace or oven for burning, baking, or drying, especially one for firing pottery, bricks, or lime.|The bricks were hardened in the kiln. 砖块在窑中被硬化。|近义词：furnace, oven|反义词:|名词


词源:
From Old English *cyline, from Proto-Germanic *kulino, from Latin culina (“kitchen”). Cognate with Dutch kuil (“hole, pit”), German Kuhle (“hole, pit”).
