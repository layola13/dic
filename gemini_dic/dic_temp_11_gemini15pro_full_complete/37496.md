committers:/kəˈmɪtərz/
| n. |提交者，委托者，犯罪者|People who commit something, often code to a software project.  Those who pledge themselves to a cause or course of action. |The committers to the open-source project regularly review and merge code changes.  开源项目的提交者定期审查和合并代码更改。|Synonyms: contributors, developers, authors, perpetrators |Antonyms: abstainers, objectors|noun


committers:/kəˈmɪtərz/
| n. |犯罪者|People who perpetrate a crime. |The police are searching for the committers of the recent bank robbery. 警方正在搜捕最近银行抢劫案的犯罪者。|Synonyms: criminals, offenders, perpetrators, culprits |Antonyms: law-abiders, innocents|noun
