anti-Israelis:/ˌæntiˈɪzreɪliz/ | n. | 反以色列者；反对以色列的人 | People who oppose the policies and actions of the State of Israel. |  Many anti-Israelis believe the state should not exist. 许多反以色列者认为以色列国不应该存在。

Anti-Israelis protested the government's decision. 反以色列者抗议政府的决定。

| 近义词: Israel critics, opponents of Israel | 反义词: pro-Israelis, Zionist, supporters of Israel | 词性: 名词


**词源:**

"anti-" (prefix):  from Greek *anti* "against, opposite of, in place of, instead of"

"Israeli" (noun/adjective):  from Modern Hebrew *yisre'eli* "Israeli," relating to Israel.
