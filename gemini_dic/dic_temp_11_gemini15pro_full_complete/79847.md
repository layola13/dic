glyptographic:/ˌɡlɪptəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|雕刻文字的；雕文的|Relating to or consisting of carved or engraved writing or symbols. |例句：The archaeologist discovered a glyptographic inscription on the ancient tomb. 考古学家在古墓中发现了一段雕刻文字。

例句：The museum houses a collection of glyptographic artifacts from various civilizations.  这家博物馆收藏了来自不同文明的雕刻文字文物。|近义词：inscribed, engraved, carved|反义词：painted, drawn|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *glyptós* (carved) + *-graphikos* (relating to writing)
