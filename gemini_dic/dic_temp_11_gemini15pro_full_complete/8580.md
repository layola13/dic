anticlassicalists:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪsts/
|n.|反古典主义者|Those who oppose or reject the principles and aesthetics of classicism.|The anticlassicalists of the early 20th century challenged traditional forms and embraced experimentation.|近义词:modernists, avant-gardists|反义词:classicists|Noun

anticlassical:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪkəl/
|adj.|反古典的|Opposed to or rejecting the principles and aesthetics of classicism.|The anticlassical movement in art emphasized emotional expression over formal restraint.|近义词:modern, avant-garde|反义词:classical|Adjective
