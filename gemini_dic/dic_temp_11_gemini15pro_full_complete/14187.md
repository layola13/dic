auto-suggestible:/ˌɔːtəsəˈdʒɛstəbəl/ | adj. | 易受暗示的，易被自我暗示的 | Readily influenced by suggestion, especially self-suggestion.  Susceptible to accepting and acting upon ideas originating within oneself. |  He's very auto-suggestible, so be careful what you say around him. 他很容易受暗示，所以在他身边说话要小心。 |  Hypnotizable, impressionable, susceptible | Resistant, skeptical, impervious | adjective
