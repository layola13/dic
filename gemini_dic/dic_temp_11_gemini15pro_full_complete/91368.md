hydrostatic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪk/| adj.|静水的，流体静力学的|Relating to the pressure of liquids at rest or the study of such pressure.|例句：Hydrostatic pressure increases with depth. 静水压力随深度增加。

例句：The dam is designed to withstand hydrostatic forces. 大坝的设计是为了承受静水压力。|近义词：hydraulic|反义词:aerodynamic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + -static (relating to equilibrium).  Ultimately from Greek *hydōr* (water) and *statikos* (causing to stand).
