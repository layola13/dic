cheerleading:/ˈtʃɪəˌliːdɪŋ/
n.
啦啦队；啦啦队活动
The act of supporting a sports team, especially at a school or college, by organized cheering, chanting, and dancing.
She was captain of the cheerleading squad in high school.  她在高中时是啦啦队队长。
近义词: cheering, rooting, supporting
反义词: booing, heckling
