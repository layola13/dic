enhalo:/ɪnˈheɪloʊ/
verb
使笼罩荣光;使(尤指圣人头部)笼罩光环;褒奖；赞扬
to surround with or as if with a halo; glorify
The setting sun enhaloed the mountains. 落日给群山罩上了一层光晕。
The victory enhaloed the team. 胜利为这个团队笼罩了荣光。
glorify, exalt, extol, celebrate
denigrate, disparage, belittle
