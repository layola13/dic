outkeeper:/ˈaʊtkiːpər/ | n. | 门卫；看门人 | A person who guards a gate or entrance.  | The outkeeper refused him entry. 门卫拒绝让他进入。| Synonyms: gatekeeper, doorkeeper, guard | Antonyms:  intruder, trespasser | noun
