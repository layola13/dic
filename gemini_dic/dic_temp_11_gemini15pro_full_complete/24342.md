brimming:/ˈbrɪmɪŋ/| adj.|充满的，洋溢的|filled to the point of overflowing|例句：Her eyes were brimming with tears. 她的眼里充满了泪水。|例句：The cup was brimming with hot coffee. 杯子里盛满了热咖啡。|近义词：full, overflowing, teeming|反义词：empty, lacking|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *brymmen, from Old Norse *brymja (“to swell, teem”), from Proto-Germanic *brūmijaną (“to swell, roar”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrew- (“to swell, bubble, boil”). Cognate with Swedish brymma (“to swell”).
