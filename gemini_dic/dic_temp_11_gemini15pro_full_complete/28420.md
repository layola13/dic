carbonite:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪt/ | n. | 碳酸盐；碳酸铁矿；碳酸炸药 |A mineral consisting of a carbonate of a metal, such as calcite (calcium carbonate) or siderite (iron carbonate). Also, a type of explosive.  |  The miners discovered a vein of carbonite. 矿工们发现了一条碳酸盐矿脉。

The old mine was known for its rich deposits of carbonite.  这个老矿以其丰富的碳酸盐矿床而闻名。 | 近义词: carbonate (especially in technical contexts) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From carbon + -ite (suffix forming nouns denoting minerals, fossils, etc.). Ultimately from Latin *carbo, carbon- 'coal, charcoal'.
