intermuscular:/ˌɪntərˈmʌskjʊlər/| adj.|肌肉间的，肌间的|Situated between muscles.|The doctor administered the injection intermuscularly. 医生进行肌肉注射。|近义词:intramuscular|反义词:None|词性:adjective

Etymology->
From inter- + muscular.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," derived from Latin. "Muscular" relates to muscles, ultimately from Latin "musculus" (little mouse, muscle).
