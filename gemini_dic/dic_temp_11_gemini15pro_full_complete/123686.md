mooing:/ˈmuːɪŋ/ | n. |哞哞叫；牛叫声 |The sound a cow makes. |  The mooing of the cows could be heard across the field. 牛的哞哞叫声在田野里都能听到。 | 近义词: lowing, bellowing | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 silence 安静) | 词性: 名词

mooing:/ˈmuːɪŋ/ | adj. | 哞哞叫的 | Making the sound of a cow. | The mooing cows gathered near the fence. 哞哞叫的牛聚集在篱笆附近。| 近义词: lowing | 反义词: silent | 词性: 形容词


词源: From the verb "moo" + "-ing" suffix,  imitating the sound. 源自动词 "moo" + "-ing" 后缀，模仿声音。
