brumal:/ˈbruːməl/| adj.|冬天的；寒冷的|Of or relating to winter; wintry.  Characterized by the cold and gloom of winter.|例句：The brumal landscape was bleak and unforgiving. 冬天的景色荒凉而无情。

例句：Animals prepare for the brumal months by storing food and growing thicker coats. 动物们通过储存食物和长出更厚的皮毛来为寒冷的冬季做准备。|近义词：wintry, hibernal, wintery|反义词：summery, estival|词性：形容词
词源：From Latin brūmālis (“of the winter solstice”), from brūma (“winter solstice”).
