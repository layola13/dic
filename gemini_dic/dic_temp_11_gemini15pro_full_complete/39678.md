conserving:/kənˈsɜːrvɪŋ/
|v.|保存；保护；节省|The act of preserving something from loss, waste, or damage.|They are conserving energy by turning off lights when leaving a room. 他们在离开房间时关灯以节省能源。|preserving, protecting, safeguarding|wasting, squandering, depleting|Verb

conserving:/kənˈsɜːrvɪŋ/
|adj.|保护的；保存的|Serving to preserve something from loss, waste, or damage.|Conserving measures are being taken to protect endangered species. 正在采取保护措施来保护濒危物种。|protective, preservative|destructive, wasteful|Adjective
