congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (US)| v. |聚集，集合|To come together in a group or crowd.|例句：People congregated in the town square to hear the mayor speak. 人们聚集在市政广场听市长讲话。

例句：Students congregated outside the classroom before the exam. 学生们在考试前聚集在教室外面。


congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (US)| adj. |聚集的，集合的|(Rarely used) Gathered together into a crowd or mass.|例句：The congregate body of students listened attentively.  聚集在一起的学生们聚精会神地听着。(很少用)

例句：The congregate mass of protesters marched towards the capitol.  聚集在一起的抗议群众向国会大厦进发。(很少用)


congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (US)| n. |聚集的人群|(Rarely used) A group of people gathered together.|例句：The congregate was addressed by the king. 国王向聚集的人群发表了讲话。(很少用)

例句：A large congregate formed outside the stadium.  体育场外聚集了一大群人。(很少用)



近义词：assemble, gather, convene|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:主要作动词，偶尔作形容词或名词
