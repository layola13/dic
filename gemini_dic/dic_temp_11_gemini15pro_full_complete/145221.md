overbearingnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈbɛrɪŋnɪsɪz/
|n.|傲慢自大；专横跋扈；盛气凌人|The state or quality of being overbearing; arrogance or domineering behavior.  Excessive bossiness or assertiveness.|His overbearingnesses alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢态度疏远了他的同事。|Near Synonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, imperiousness, domineeringness, high-handedness|Near Antonyms: humility, modesty, meekness, deference, submissiveness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From overbearing + -ness.  Overbearing itself comes from over- + bearing, with "bearing" in the sense of "demeanor" or "conduct."
