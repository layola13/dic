devoutnesses:/dɪˈvaʊtnɪsɪz/| n. |虔诚，虔敬|The state or quality of being devout; deep religious devotion.|Her devoutnesses manifested in daily prayer and charitable acts. 她的虔诚体现在每天的祈祷和慈善行为中。|近义词：piety, religiousness, godliness|反义词：impiety, irreverence|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *devoutnes*, from Old French *devotnesse*, from *devot* (“devout”). Equivalent to devout +‎ -ness.
