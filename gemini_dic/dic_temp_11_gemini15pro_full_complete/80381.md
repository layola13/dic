golgotha: /ˈɡɒlɡəθə/ (British), /ˈɡɑːlɡəθə/ (American) | n. | 各各他，髑髅地 | A place of execution, specifically the place where Jesus was crucified.  It is often used figuratively to refer to a place of suffering or sacrifice. |  Pilgrims visit Golgotha to remember Jesus's sacrifice.  朝圣者前往各各他纪念耶稣的牺牲。

Golgotha is a symbol of suffering and redemption. 各各他是受难和救赎的象征。| synonym: Calvary, Skull | antonym:  (No direct antonym exists as it is a place name, but conceptually, perhaps "paradise" or "sanctuary") | noun
