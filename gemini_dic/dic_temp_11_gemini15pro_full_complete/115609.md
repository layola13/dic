marcasitic:/mɑːrˈkæsɪtɪk/ | adj. |  硫砷铁矿的；似硫砷铁矿的 | Relating to or resembling marcasite, a mineral form of iron sulfide (FeS2) that is similar to pyrite but has a different crystal structure. | The sample contained marcasitic crystals.  这个样本包含硫砷铁矿晶体。|  The rock displayed a marcasitic sheen. 这块岩石展现出硫砷铁矿般的光泽。| Synonym: pyritic | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-sulfidic) | adjective
