guanxi:/ɡwɑːnˈʃiː/
noun
关系,人脉,裙带关系
A Chinese term referring to interpersonal connections and social networks, often involving reciprocal favors and obligations.  It emphasizes building relationships based on trust, loyalty, and mutual benefit, which can be crucial for navigating social and business interactions in China.
He used his guanxi to secure the business deal. 他利用自己的人脉关系拿下了这笔生意。
近义词: connections, network, influence, leverage
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but a lack of guanxi could be implied)
词性:n.
