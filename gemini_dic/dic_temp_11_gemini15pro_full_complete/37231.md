comfortable: /ˈkʌmf(ə)təb(ə)l/ (UK) /ˈkʌmftərbəl/ (US)| adj.|舒适的，舒服的|Providing physical ease and relaxation; free from pain or constraint.|例句：The new sofa is very comfortable. 新沙发很舒服。|近义词：cozy, snug, relaxing, pleasant|反义词：uncomfortable, distressing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English confortable, from Old French confortable, from Late Latin confortābilis (“strengthening”), from Latin con- (“with, together”) + fortāre (“to strengthen”), from fortis (“strong”).
