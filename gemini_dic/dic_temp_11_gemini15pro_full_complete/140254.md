offset:/ˈɒfset (UK), ˈɔːfset (US)/| n. vt.| 抵消，补偿；偏离，偏移；支管；弯管；(印刷) 胶印；平版印刷 | An amount or distance by which something is out of line.  | The company's losses were offset by gains in other areas. 该公司的损失被其他领域的收益抵消了。 | synonym: compensate, counterbalance, balance out | antonym: augment, enhance, increase | noun
offset:/ˈɒfset (UK), ˈɔːfset (US)/| vt.| 抵消，补偿；偏离，偏移 | To balance one influence against an opposing influence, so that there is no great difference as a result. | The benefits offset the risks. 好处抵消了风险。| synonym: counter, counteract, neutralize | antonym: augment, enhance, increase | verb
