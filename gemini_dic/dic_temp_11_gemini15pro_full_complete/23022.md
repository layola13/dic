boulderers:/ˈboʊldərərz/
n.
攀岩者；抱石攀岩者
People who climb boulders.  Those who participate in the sport of bouldering.
The boulderers tackled the challenging rock face with skill and determination. 攀岩者们以技巧和决心应对了具有挑战性的岩壁。
Experienced boulderers can make quick ascents up seemingly impossible routes.  经验丰富的攀岩者可以快速攀登看似不可能的路线。
climbers, rock climbers
(No direct antonym, perhaps "non-climbers")
noun
