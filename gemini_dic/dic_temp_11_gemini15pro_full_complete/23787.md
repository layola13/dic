brawnily:/ˈbrɔːnɪli/
adverb
强壮地，有力地
in a brawny or muscular way; strongly and powerfully.
He brawnily lifted the heavy box. 他强壮地举起了沉重的箱子。
近义词:strongly, powerfully, muscularly, robustly
反义词:weakly, feebly
词性:adverb

词源:
From brawn + -ily.  Brawn comes from Old French "braon" meaning "fleshy part of the body, muscle," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*braunaz."  The suffix "-ily" forms adverbs from adjectives.
