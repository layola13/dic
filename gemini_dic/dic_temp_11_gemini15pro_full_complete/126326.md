myopically:/ˌmaɪˈɒpɪkli/ /ˌmaɪˈɑːpɪkli/| adv.| 近视地；目光短浅地 | lacking foresight or long-term perspective; in a short-sighted manner. |  He invested myopically, focusing only on short-term gains. 他目光短浅地投资，只关注短期收益。|  She myopically focused on the immediate problem and failed to see the bigger picture. 她近视地关注眼前的问题，而没有看到更大的局面。 | 近义词: shortsightedly, narrowly, unimaginatively | 反义词: farsightedly, broadly, wisely | 词性: 副词
