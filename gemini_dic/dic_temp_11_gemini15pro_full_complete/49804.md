demonomania:/ˌdiːmənəˈmeɪniə/| n. |邪魔狂；着魔；宗教狂|An excessive or unreasonable enthusiasm for religion or a particular religious practice.  A mental disorder in which the patient believes that he or she is possessed by demons.|He suffered from demonomania and believed evil spirits controlled him.|近义词：demonolatry, demonopathy|反义词：  (No direct antonym, consider terms like rationality, sanity)|词性: noun
