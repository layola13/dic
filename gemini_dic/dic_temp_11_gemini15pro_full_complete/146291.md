overfatigue:/ˌoʊvərˈfəˈtiːɡ/| n.|过度疲劳|Excessive tiredness, exhaustion.|She suffered from overfatigue after working for three days straight. 她连续工作三天后感到过度疲劳。|近义词：exhaustion, weariness, burnout|反义词:energy, vigor|noun

overfatigue:/ˌoʊvərˈfəˈtiːɡ/| vt.|使过度疲劳|To make excessively tired.|The long journey overfatigued the horses. 长途跋涉使马匹过度疲劳。|近义词：exhaust, wear out|反义词:energize, invigorate|verb
