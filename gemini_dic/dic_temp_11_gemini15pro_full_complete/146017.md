overdrain:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪn/ | v. | 排水过度，过度消耗 | To drain excessively, depleting resources or energy. | The farmer overdrained the soil, which led to poor crop yields. 农民过度排水，导致作物产量低。

The constant stress of the job was overdraining her.  这份工作的持续压力让她精疲力竭。| 近义词: exhaust, deplete, overtax | 反义词: replenish, nourish, conserve | 词性: 动词
