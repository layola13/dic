ooh:/uː/ |interj.|哦;啊（表示惊讶、疼痛、喜悦等）|An exclamation expressing surprise, pain, pleasure, or other emotions.| Ooh, that's a beautiful dress! 哦，那是一件漂亮的连衣裙！| 近义词: ah, oh | 反义词: (无明确的反义词) | 词性:感叹词

词源:  Imitative origin, representing a sound made with the lips rounded.  源于模仿，代表嘴唇圆润时发出的声音。
