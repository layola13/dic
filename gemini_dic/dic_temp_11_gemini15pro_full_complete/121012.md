miscoinages:/ˌmɪsˈkɔɪnɪdʒɪz/
| n. |错误的造词；杜撰词|An incorrect formation of a word; a neologism that doesn't follow standard rules of word formation. |Many miscoinages are quickly forgotten, but some become accepted over time. 很多杜撰词很快就被遗忘了，但也有一些随着时间的推移被接受了。|Synonyms: neologisms, barbarisms, solecisms |Antonyms: standard words, accepted words|noun
