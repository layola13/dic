brackeny: /ˈbrækəni/| adj.|蕨类植物丛生的，长满蕨类的|Covered with or resembling bracken.  A brackeny hillside. |例句：The path led through a brackeny area. 小路穿过一片蕨类植物丛生的区域。

例句：The hillside was brackeny and overgrown. 山坡上长满了蕨类植物，杂草丛生。|近义词：ferny, overgrown, weedy|反义词: clear, barren|词性: adjective

Etymology: From bracken + -y.  Bracken comes from Old Norse *brakni.
