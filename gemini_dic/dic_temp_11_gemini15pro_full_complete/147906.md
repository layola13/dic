overreflections:/ˌoʊvərɪˈflɛkʃənz/| n.|过度反思，过度反省|The act of reflecting excessively or too deeply on past experiences, often leading to anxiety or negative emotions.  It involves ruminating on past mistakes, failures, or embarrassing moments, often to an unhealthy degree.|She was trapped in a cycle of overreflections about her past relationship.|近义词：rumination, brooding, introspection|反义词:mindlessness, thoughtlessness|词性:noun
