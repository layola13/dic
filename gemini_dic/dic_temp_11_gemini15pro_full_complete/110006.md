librarianess:/laɪˈbreəriənɪs/
noun
女图书管理员
A female librarian.  This term is somewhat dated and considered less common than the gender-neutral term "librarian".
例句：The librarianess helped me find a rare book on medieval history. 女图书管理员帮我找到了一本关于中世纪历史的珍本书。

例句：My grandmother was a librarianess for over thirty years. 我的祖母做了三十多年的图书管理员。

近义词：librarian, library assistant
反义词: N/A (No direct opposite)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From librarian + -ess (a suffix used to form feminine nouns).  "Librarian" itself comes from Latin "librarius" (relating to books) from "liber" (book).
