doziest:/ˈdoʊziɪst/| adj.|最困倦的，最想睡的|Superlative form of dozy: most dozy.  Meaning the most sleepy, drowsy, or lethargic.|例句：He was the doziest one in the room after the long flight.  他坐了长途飞机后是房间里最困的人。

例句：She felt doziest in the afternoons. 她下午感觉最困。|近义词：sleepiest, drowsiest, most lethargic|反义词：most alert, most awake, liveliest|词性:形容词
词源: From doze (v.) + -y (adj. suffix) + -est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Old Norse *dusa "to be sluggish."
