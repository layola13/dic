hatchway:/ˈhætʃweɪ/| n. |舱口；天窗；地板门|An opening in a wall, ceiling, or floor, typically one with a hinged or sliding cover.|The sailors opened the hatchway to access the cargo hold below. 水手们打开舱口，进入下面的货舱。|The attic hatchway was small and difficult to climb through. 阁楼的舱口很小，很难爬过去。| synonyms: opening, trapdoor, access, doorway| antonyms: closure, seal| noun
