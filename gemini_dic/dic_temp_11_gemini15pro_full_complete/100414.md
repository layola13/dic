intertextually:/ˌɪntərˈtekstʃuəli/ | adv. |互文地，互文性地 | Relating to or involving a relationship between texts, especially literary ones.  The meaning of one text is shaped by its relationship to other texts. |  The novel intertextually references several classic works of literature. 这部小说互文地引用了几部经典文学作品。 |  The author uses language intertextually, drawing on popular culture and historical events. 作者互文地使用语言，借鉴了流行文化和历史事件。 | Synonyms: allusively, referentially | Antonyms: originally, independently | adverb
