dissociate:/dɪˈsoʊʃieɪt/ (US), /dɪˈsəʊsieɪt/ (UK)| v.  |分离，分开；游离；使脱离关系|To separate or become separated; to stop associating (oneself) with something.|He tried to dissociate himself from the scandal. 他试图把自己与丑闻撇清关系。|近义词：separate, detach, disconnect, disengage|反义词：associate, connect, unite, join|verb

dissociate:/dɪˈsoʊʃiət/ (US), /dɪˈsəʊsiət/ (UK)| adj. |分离的，分裂的|Not associated; separated.|a dissociate personality 分裂的人格|近义词：separate, detached, disconnected|反义词：associated, connected, united|adjective
