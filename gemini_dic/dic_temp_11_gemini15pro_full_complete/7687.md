anoint:/əˈnɔɪnt/
v.
涂油，膏抹
To smear or rub with oil, typically as part of a religious ceremony or ritual; to choose someone for a particular job or position.
The bishop anointed the new queen.  主教为新女王涂油。
He was anointed as the successor to the CEO.  他被指定为首席执行官的继任者。
近义词: consecrate, ordain, designate
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider perhaps "desecrate" in a specific context)
词性: Verb


词源:
From Old French enoint, past participle of enoindre, from Latin inungere "to smear on," from in- "in" + ungere "to anoint."
