hearkener:/ˈhɑːrkənər/ (US), /ˈhɑːkənə(r)/ (UK)| n. |倾听者；听众|One who listens attentively; a listener. |例句：The storyteller captivated the hearkeners with his tales of adventure.  说书人用他的冒险故事吸引了听众。

例句：Few hearkeners remained by the end of the lengthy speech.冗长的演讲结束时，几乎没有听众留下。|近义词：listener, auditor, audience, ear|反义词:speaker, orator|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *herkener*, from Old English *heorcniere*, equivalent to *hear* + *-ener*. Cognate with Dutch *horikenaar* (“eavesdropper”).
