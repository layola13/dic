out-streams:/ˈaʊtˌstriːmz/
noun
输出流
A sequence of data elements made available by an information producer, often a computer or person, and which is transferred to an information consumer.
Data out-streams from the sensor to the central processing unit. 数据从传感器输出到中央处理器。
outputs, effluence, discharge
intakes, influxes
n.
