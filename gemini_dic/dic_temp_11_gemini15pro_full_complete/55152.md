disseize:/dɪˈsiːz/ /dɪˈsaɪz/ | vt.|剥夺，夺取（尤指土地）|To deprive of rightful possession or ownership, especially of real property; to dispossess.|The king disseized the rebel lord of his lands. 国王剥夺了反叛领主的土地。|Synonyms: dispossess, expropriate, usurp, oust|Antonyms: invest, endow, grant|verb

disseizin:/dɪˈsiːzɪŋ/ /dɪˈsaɪzɪŋ/ | n.|剥夺，非法侵占|The act of disseizing.|The disseizin of the peasants' land caused widespread unrest.  对农民土地的剥夺引起了广泛的骚乱。|Synonyms: dispossession, expropriation, usurpation|Antonyms: investiture, endowment, grant|noun
