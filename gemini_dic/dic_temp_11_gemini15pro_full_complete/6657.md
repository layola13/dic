anaglyptographic:/ˌænəˌɡlɪptəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|雕刻的，浮雕的|Relating to or produced by anaglyptography, a process that creates a three-dimensional effect by using two slightly different images, one red and one cyan, which are superimposed and viewed through special glasses.|Anaglyptographic images were popular in the mid-20th century for viewing 3D movies and pictures.  雕刻图像在20世纪中期很流行，用于观看3D电影和图片。|Near Synonyms: stereoscopic, three-dimensional|Antonyms: two-dimensional, flat|adjective
