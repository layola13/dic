cantilena:/ˌkæntɪˈliːnə/ | n. | 歌唱；旋律；滑音；拖长的音节 | A smooth, flowing melody; a song, especially a long, elaborate one, often with a mournful quality.  A sustained or flowing melodic phrase, especially in vocal music. | The soprano's cantilena in the aria was breathtaking. 女高音在咏叹调中的旋律令人叹为观止。|  The violin's cantilena filled the concert hall. 小提琴悠扬的旋律充满了音乐厅。 |Synonyms: melody, tune, air, song, aria, theme | Antonyms: dissonance, cacophony | noun
