non-rhetorical:/ˌnɒn rɪˈtɒrɪkəl/
adjective
非修辞的；非反问的；真实的
Not rhetorical; meant to be taken literally and answered seriously.
"A non-rhetorical question requiring a real answer."  一个需要真正答案的非反问句。
Synonyms: genuine, sincere, earnest, serious, literal
Antonyms: rhetorical, insincere, sarcastic
词性: 形容词
