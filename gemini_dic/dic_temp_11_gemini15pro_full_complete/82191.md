grossers:/ˈɡroʊsərz/| n.|批发商；（尤指食品）批发商|Wholesalers, especially of food.|The grossers supplied the local markets with fresh produce.  这些批发商向当地市场供应新鲜农产品。|近义词：wholesalers, distributors|反义词: retailers|词性:名词
Etymology: From "gross," meaning "in bulk," referring to the large quantities handled by wholesalers.  源自“gross”，意为“大量”，指的是批发商处理的大批量货物。
