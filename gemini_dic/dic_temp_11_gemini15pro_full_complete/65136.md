ethidene:/ˈeθɪdiːn/| n.|亚乙基|A bivalent radical, CH3.CH<, derived from ethane by removal of two hydrogen atoms from the same carbon atom.  It forms the characteristic group of acetaldehyde and its derivatives.|The ethidene group is present in many organic compounds.|近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include vinyl and ethylene)|反义词: (No antonym)|词性:noun
