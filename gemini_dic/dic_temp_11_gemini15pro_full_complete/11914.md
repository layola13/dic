arroyo:/əˈrɔɪoʊ/ (US), /əˈrɔɪəʊ/ (UK)| n.|溪流，小河，旱谷|A watercourse in a dry region, often dry except during periods of rainfall.  A deep gully cut by an arroyo.|The hikers followed the arroyo hoping to find a spring.  徒步旅行者沿着旱谷走，希望能找到一眼泉水。|近义词：creek, gully, wadi, wash|反义词:ridge, hill|名词
