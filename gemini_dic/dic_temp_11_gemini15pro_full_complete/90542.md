huffman:/ˈhʌfmən/ | adj. n. | 霍夫曼（的）; 霍夫曼编码; |Relating to or denoting a method of lossless data compression in which variable-length codes are assigned to input characters, short codes being assigned to frequently occurring input characters and longer codes to less frequently occurring input characters.  It is named after David A. Huffman, who developed the technique in the 1950s. |Huffman coding is widely used in image and video compression. 霍夫曼编码广泛用于图像和视频压缩。|The Huffman tree is a binary tree used in Huffman coding. 霍夫曼树是霍夫曼编码中使用的二叉树。| 近义词: (As a coding method) lossless compression, entropy encoding | 反义词: lossy compression | 词性: Adjective, Noun
