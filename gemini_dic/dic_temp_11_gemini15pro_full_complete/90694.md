humdrummery:/hʌmˈdrʌməri/
|n.|单调乏味；无聊的事；平庸|A tedious, boring, or monotonous routine; a commonplace or unexciting activity.|The endless humdrummery of office life was starting to wear him down. 办公室生活的无休止的单调乏味开始让他感到疲惫不堪。|Synonyms: monotony, tedium, boredom, dullness, routine, banality|Antonyms: excitement, thrill, stimulation, novelty, originality|Noun
