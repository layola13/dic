deejays:/ˈdiːdʒeɪz/
n.
唱片骑师，音乐节目主持人
A person who plays recorded music, especially on the radio or at a club.
The deejays played a mix of old and new music.  唱片骑师播放了新旧音乐的混合曲目。
The club's deejay kept the party going all night.  俱乐部的唱片骑师让派对持续了整晚。
disc jockeys, DJs
(No real antonym for a profession)
noun
