on my part:/ɒn maɪ pɑːt/ /ɑːn maɪ pɑːrt/| adverbial phrase | 就我而言；在我看来；由我负责|Used to indicate that something is done or felt by the speaker specifically.  It emphasizes the speaker's individual role or perspective in a situation. | I apologize for any confusion on my part. 我就我引起的任何困惑道歉。|Synonyms: for my part, as for me, personally, from my perspective, on my behalf|Antonyms: on your part, on their part|phrase
