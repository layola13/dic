facultative:/ˈfækəlˌteɪtɪv/
adjective
兼性的；随意选择的；非强制的
Occurring optionally in response to particular environmental conditions.
Bacteria that are facultative anaerobes can switch between aerobic and anaerobic respiration. 兼性厌氧菌可以在有氧呼吸和无氧呼吸之间切换。
This bacterium is a facultative anaerobe, meaning it can survive with or without oxygen. 这种细菌是一种兼性厌氧菌，意味着它可以在有氧或无氧条件下生存。
近义词: optional, elective, discretionary
反义词: obligatory, compulsory, mandatory
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin facultātīvus, from facultās (“capability, opportunity”), from facilis (“easy”).
