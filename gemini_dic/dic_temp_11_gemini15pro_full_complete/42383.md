counterblockade:/ˈkaʊntərˌblɑːˈkeɪd/
noun
反封锁，对抗封锁
an action taken to prevent an enemy from blockading a port or other place by blockading their own ports
The British Navy implemented a counterblockade to disrupt enemy supply lines. 英国海军实施了反封锁，以扰乱敌人的补给线。
countermeasure, retaliation
blockade
