grosser:/ˈɡroʊsər/ (US), /ˈɡrəʊsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|杂货商；批发商|A person who buys and sells goods, especially food, in large quantities, typically to retailers.|例句：The grosser supplied the local shops with fresh produce.  这个批发商向当地商店供应新鲜农产品。

例句：He worked as a grosser for many years before opening his own store.  他做了多年的批发商，然后才开了自己的店。|近义词：wholesaler, distributor, supplier, merchant|反义词：retailer, consumer|词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *grossere, from Anglo-Norman grossour, grossier, from Old French grossier (“wholesale dealer”), from gros (“large, big; coarse”), from Late Latin grossus (“thick, coarse”).
