dilatator:/ˈdaɪleɪtər/ (US), /daɪˈleɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|扩张器, 扩张肌|A device or muscle that causes dilation (widening or expansion) of an opening or cavity in the body.|The surgeon used a dilatator to widen the patient's pupil.  外科医生用扩张器扩大病人的瞳孔。|Synonyms: expander, stretcher|Antonyms: constrictor, compressor|noun
