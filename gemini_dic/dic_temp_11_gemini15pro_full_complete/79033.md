give chase:/ɡɪv ˈtʃeɪs/| v.|追赶，追逐|To begin pursuing someone or something.|The police gave chase to the fleeing suspect. 警察追赶逃逸的嫌疑犯。|The dog gave chase to the squirrel. 狗追赶松鼠。|近义词：pursue, follow, chase after|反义词：flee, escape|词性:动词短语

词源: "give" comes from Old English "giefan," and "chase" from Old French "chacier," ultimately from Latin "captiare," meaning "to try to seize."  The combination "give chase" creates the sense of initiating a pursuit.
