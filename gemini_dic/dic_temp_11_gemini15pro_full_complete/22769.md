borshch:/ˈbɔːrʃt/ (US), /ˈbɔːʃt/ (UK)| n. |罗宋汤|A beet soup of Ukrainian origin, usually containing meat and vegetables such as cabbage, carrots, potatoes, onions, and garlic.|例句：My grandmother makes the best borshch in the world. 我祖母做的罗宋汤是世界上最好吃的。

例句：For dinner, we had a hearty bowl of borshch.  晚饭我们吃了一碗丰盛的罗宋汤。


近义词：beet soup, beetroot soup|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a contrasting dish like "gazpacho")|词性:noun
