drumbeatings:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːtɪŋz/
n.
鼓声，敲鼓声，（比喻）宣传，鼓吹
The constant drumbeatings from the protesters echoed through the streets.  抗议者的不断敲鼓声在街道上回荡。
The media's drumbeatings about the upcoming election were incessant. 媒体对即将到来的选举的宣传不绝于耳。
Example: The drumbeatings signaled the start of the ceremony. 鼓声标志着仪式的开始。
Example: The constant drumbeatings of negative news can be depressing.  不断涌现的负面新闻令人沮丧。
Synonyms: drumming, percussion, pounding, propaganda, promotion
Antonyms: silence, quiet, hush
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "drum" + "beating"
"drum" (n.) - Old English *drumme, of imitative origin.
"beating" (n.) - from "beat" (v.) - Old English *bēatan "to strike repeatedly," of imitative origin.
