mistlike:/ˈmɪstˌlaɪk/| adj.|像雾的，雾状的|Resembling or characteristic of mist; hazy.|The mistlike rain made it difficult to see. 像雾一样的雨使得很难看清东西。|近义词：hazy, foggy, misty|反义词:clear, sunny|形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mist + -like.  "Mist" comes from Old English *mist, of Germanic origin.  "-like" is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "resembling" or "having the characteristics of."
