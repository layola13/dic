claustrophobia:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbiə/
|n.|幽闭恐惧症|Claustrophobia is the fear of enclosed spaces. 幽闭恐惧症是对封闭空间的恐惧。|She suffers from claustrophobia and can't ride in elevators. 她患有幽闭恐惧症，不能乘坐电梯。|近义词: fear of enclosed spaces|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun


词源:  From the Latin "claustrum" (lock, bolt, bar, barrier) and Greek "phobos" (fear).
