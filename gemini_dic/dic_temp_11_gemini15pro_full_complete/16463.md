Derived from Old Norse *berserkr, meaning "bear-shirt" or "bear-skin," referring to warriors who wore bear skins in battle and entered a state of frenzied rage. |  He went completely baresark after losing his job. 他失业后完全暴怒了。 |  The baresark charged into battle, swinging his axe wildly.  狂战士冲入战场，疯狂地挥舞着斧头。 | Synonyms: frenzied, enraged, furious, berserk, amok | Antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil | adjective, noun
