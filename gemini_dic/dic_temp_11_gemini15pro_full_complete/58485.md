dudish:/ˈduːdɪʃ/| adj.| 花花公子的；纨绔子弟的；好打扮的；有男子气概的 | Characteristic of or resembling a dude; stylish, foppish, or masculine. | He had a rather dudish way of walking. 他走路的样子有点像花花公子。 | 近义词：dandyish, foppish, stylish, masculine | 反义词：unstylish, sloppy, feminine | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From dude + -ish.  The word "dude" originally referred to a sharply dressed man, particularly an Easterner visiting the American West.  The suffix "-ish" adds the meaning "characteristic of" or "resembling."
