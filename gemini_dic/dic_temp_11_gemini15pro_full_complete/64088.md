epode:/ˈɛpoʊd/ | n. | 抒情诗; 颂诗; 长短句诗; (古希腊合唱队)在舞台上来回走唱的颂歌 | A lyric poem, or one part of a lyric poem, in which a shorter verse follows a longer one.  An ode sung by the ancient Greek chorus while moving across the stage. |  The poet included an epode in his latest work. 这位诗人在他的最新作品中加入了一首长短句诗。 | synonyms: ode, lyric, poem, song | antonyms: prose, story | noun

epode:/ˈɛpoʊd/ | n. | (医学)发作的后期; (疾病)发作期 | (medicine) The latter part of a paroxysm; a fit or attack. | The epode of the seizure was marked by a deep sleep. 癫痫发作的后期以深度睡眠为特征。 | synonyms: after-stage, latter stage, decline | antonyms: onset, beginning | noun
