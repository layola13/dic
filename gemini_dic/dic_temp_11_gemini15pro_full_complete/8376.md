anti-racism:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 反种族主义 |  The active process of identifying and eliminating racism by changing systems, organizational structures, policies and practices and attitudes, so that power is redistributed and shared equitably. | Many organizations are implementing anti-racism training programs for their employees. 许多组织正在为其员工实施反种族主义培训计划。| synonyms: racial equality, racial justice | antonyms: racism, racial discrimination, prejudice, bigotry | noun

anti-racism:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪzəm/ | adj. | 反种族主义的 |  Related to or supporting the opposition to racism. |  She participated in an anti-racism protest. 她参加了一场反种族主义抗议活动。 | synonyms: anti-racist, non-racist | antonyms: racist, discriminatory | adjective
