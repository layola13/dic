meconidium: /ˌmɪkəˈnɪdiəm/ | n. | 分生孢子 | An asexual spore produced typically by fungi and some other organisms, usually at the tip of a specialized hypha or sporophore.  It is not enclosed in a sac. |  The meconidia of this fungus are characteristically spherical. 这种真菌的分生孢子呈典型的球形。| synonym: conidium, mitospore | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps sexual spore like ascospore or basidiospore could be considered a contrasting concept) | noun
