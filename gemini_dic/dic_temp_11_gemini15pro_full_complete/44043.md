crined:/kraɪnd/ | adj. | 有鬃毛的，有浓密毛发的 | Having a mane or long, thick hair. |  The crined horse pawed the ground. 那匹鬃毛浓密的马刨着地。 | 近义词：hairy, maned | 反义词:bald, hairless | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *crined, from crine + -ed, from Old French criné (“maned”), past participle of criner (“to mane”), from crin (“mane, hair”), from Frankish *krin, from Proto-Germanic *khrīnaną (“to shrink, shrivel”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱrī- (“to sieve, separate, distinguish”).  More distantly related to "grain" and "groin".
