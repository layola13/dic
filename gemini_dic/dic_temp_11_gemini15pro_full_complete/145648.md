overcontentment:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtɛntmənt/ | n. | 过分满足，过分知足 | A state of being excessively content or satisfied, often to the point of complacency or lack of ambition. |  He seemed to wallow in a state of overcontentment, oblivious to the problems around him.  他似乎沉溺于一种过度满足的状态，对周围的问题视而不见。

Overcontentment can sometimes lead to stagnation. 过度的满足有时会导致停滞不前。 | 近义词: complacency, satisfaction, smugness | 反义词: discontent, dissatisfaction, ambition | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From over- + contentment.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Contentment" comes from the Old French "contentement," ultimately derived from the Latin "contentus," meaning "contained" or "satisfied."
