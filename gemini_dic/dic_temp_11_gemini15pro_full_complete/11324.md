arear:/əˈrɪər/| n.|欠款，拖欠，逾期未付款|An amount of money that is owed and should have been paid earlier. |例句：He was in arear with his rent.  他拖欠房租。

例句：The company is taking steps to reduce its arears. 公司正在采取措施减少欠款。|近义词：debt, arrears, outstanding balance, due, unpaid|反义词：credit, advance, prepayment|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *arrier "behind, backward," from Latin ad "to" + retro "backward."  Originally used as an adverb and preposition meaning "behind" or "in the rear," the financial sense developed from the notion of being "behind" in payments.
