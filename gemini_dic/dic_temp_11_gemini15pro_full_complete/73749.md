forlorn:/fərˈlɔːrn/ /fɔːrˈlɔːrn/| adj.|绝望的，孤苦伶仃的，被遗弃的|Feeling or appearing miserable and lonely.|例句：The forlorn figure on the park bench seemed lost in thought. 公园长椅上那个孤单的身影似乎陷入了沉思。|例句：The house stood forlorn and empty after the family moved out.  家人搬走后，房子显得荒凉而空荡。|近义词：desolate, abandoned, lonely, miserable, wretched|反义词：cheerful, happy, hopeful|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *forloren, from Old English forloren (“lost, ruined, depraved, condemned, forfeited, abandoned”), past participle of forlēosan (“to lose, destroy, ruin, waste, forfeit”), from Proto-Germanic *farlusananą (“to lose”), from Proto-Indo-European *lews- (“to loosen, separate, divide”). Cognate with Scots forloin (“lost”), West Frisian ferlern (“lost”), Dutch verloren (“lost”), German verloren (“lost”), Danish forloren (“lost, forlorn”).
