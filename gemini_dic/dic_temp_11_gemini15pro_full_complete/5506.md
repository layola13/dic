alpenglow:/ˈælpənˌɡloʊ/| n.|日照金山，高山霞光|A reddish glow seen on high mountains after sunset or before sunrise, caused by the reflection of sunlight on snow or ice.|例句：The alpenglow on the Matterhorn was breathtaking.  马特洪峰上的日照金山令人叹为观止。

例句：We woke up early to witness the beautiful alpenglow. 我们早起是为了见证美丽的日照金山。|近义词：sunset glow, sunrise glow|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From German *Alpenglühen*, from *Alpen* (alps) + *glühen* (to glow).  Borrowed into English in the late 19th century.
