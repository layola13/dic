fling:/flɪŋ/ /flɪŋ/| v. n.|扔，投，猛冲，急冲；一时的放纵|He flung the door open and stormed out. 他猛地打开门，冲了出去。 |近义词：throw, hurl, cast, toss, indulge|反义词：catch, hold, refrain|词性：动词/名词

Etymology:
From Old Norse *flingja, from Proto-Germanic *flinganą, meaning "to throw, to fling".
