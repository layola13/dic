clinic:/ˈklɪnɪk/| n.|诊所，门诊部|A place where people go to receive medical treatment, especially when they are not admitted to a hospital.|She went to the clinic for a check-up. 她去诊所做了个检查。|synonyms: health center, medical center, doctor's office|antonyms: hospital (although sometimes used interchangeably)|noun

clinic:/ˈklɪnɪk/| n.|临床教学，临床实习|A session of instruction for medical students that involves examining and treating patients.|The medical students attended a clinic on dermatology. 医学生参加了皮肤病学的临床教学。|synonyms: practicum, seminar, workshop|antonyms: lecture, theory class|noun
