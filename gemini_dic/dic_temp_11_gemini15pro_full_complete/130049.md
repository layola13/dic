non-personal:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːsənəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrsənəl/
adjective
非个人的；非人称的；物性的
Not relating to or involving particular people; not showing any personal feelings.
例句：The letter was written in a formal, non-personal style. 这封信是用正式的、非个人风格写的。

例句：She tried to keep the discussion non-personal. 她尽量使讨论不涉及个人。

近义词：impersonal, objective, detached
反义词：personal, subjective, involved
词性：adjective
