fraudfully:/ˈfrɔːdjʊləli/
adverb
欺诈地
In a fraudulent manner; deceitfully.
He fraudulently obtained a visa. 他以欺骗手段获得了签证。
She fraudulently claimed benefits. 她以欺诈手段领取救济金。
近义词:dishonestly, deceitfully, deceptively, falsely
反义词:honestly, truthfully, legitimately
词性:adverb

词源:
From fraud + -ful + -ly.  "Fraud" comes from the Old French "fraude," which ultimately derives from the Latin "fraus," meaning "deceit, trickery, or damage."
