crescendo:/krəˈʃendoʊ/ (US) /krəˈʃendəʊ/ (UK)
n.|渐强；高潮；顶点|a gradual increase in loudness, or the highest point reached|The music reached a crescendo and then faded away. 音乐声逐渐增强，然后逐渐消失。|
近义词:climax, peak, culmination
反义词: diminuendo, decrescendo
词性:名词

crescendo:/krəˈʃendoʊ/ (US) /krəˈʃendəʊ/ (UK)
v.|（声音）逐渐增强；（事物）发展到高潮|to increase gradually in loudness or intensity|The applause crescendoed as the performers took their bows. 当演员鞠躬时，掌声逐渐增强。|
近义词:increase, intensify, build up
反义词:decrease, diminish, subside
词性:动词

Etymology: From Italian *crescendo*, present participle of *crescere* "to grow," from Latin *crescere* "to arise, grow."
