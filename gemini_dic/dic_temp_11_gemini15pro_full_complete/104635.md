karamazov:/ˌkærəˈmɑːzɔːf/ |n.|卡拉马佐夫（姓氏）|Referring to a character or theme related to Fyodor Dostoevsky's novel "The Brothers Karamazov".  It often implies complex moral dilemmas, philosophical struggles, and intense familial relationships. |The Karamazov brothers represent different aspects of human nature. 卡拉马佐夫兄弟代表了人性的不同方面。
