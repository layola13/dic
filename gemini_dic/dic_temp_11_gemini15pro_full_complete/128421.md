newfashioned:/ˌnjuːˈfæʃənd/| adj.|新式的，新潮的|Modern; recently come into fashion.  Characterized by a new fashion, especially one that is considered somewhat extreme or excessively modern.|She wore a newfashioned dress to the party. 她穿了一件新潮的连衣裙去参加派对。|近义词：modern, contemporary, stylish, trendy|反义词：old-fashioned, outdated, traditional|词性:形容词

词源:
From "new" + "fashioned" (past participle of "fashion," meaning "to shape or make").  Essentially, something newly made or styled.
