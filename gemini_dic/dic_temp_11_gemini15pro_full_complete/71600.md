flat-out:/ˈflæt ˈaʊt/| adv. adj.|完全地，彻底地；全力地，竭尽全力地|Completely; without qualification or reservation.  Used to emphasize the completeness or intensity of something.|He denied flat-out that he had ever met her. 他断然否认他见过她。|近义词：absolutely, completely, totally, utterly, entirely, outright|反义词：partially, hesitantly|词性: 副词，形容词

flat-out:/ˈflæt ˈaʊt/| adj.|直截了当的，坦率的|Direct and straightforward; blunt.|He gave a flat-out refusal. 他断然拒绝了。|近义词：blunt, straightforward, direct|反义词：evasive, indirect|词性: 形容词
