ductilimeter:/ˌdʌktɪˈlɪmɪtər/
noun
延性计，韧性计
An instrument for measuring the ductility of a material, especially of metals.
The engineer used a ductilimeter to assess the metal's suitability for drawing into wires.  工程师使用延性计来评估金属拉制成线的适用性。
Synonyms: ductility tester, tensometer
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a measuring instrument)
