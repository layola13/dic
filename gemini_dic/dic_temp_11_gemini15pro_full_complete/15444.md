bad-mouthers:/ˈbædˌmaʊðərz/
n.
诽谤者，说坏话的人
People who speak badly or critically of someone or something.
The bad-mouthers spread rumors about the company. 那些说坏话的人散播关于公司的谣言。
近义词：critics, detractors, slanderers
反义词：supporters, admirers, praisers
词性：名词(plural)

bad-mouth:/ˌbædˈmaʊθ/
v.
说坏话，诽谤
To criticize someone or something harshly.
He's always bad-mouthing his competitors. 他总是说他竞争对手的坏话。
近义词：criticize, denigrate, slander
反义词：praise, compliment, commend
词性：动词
