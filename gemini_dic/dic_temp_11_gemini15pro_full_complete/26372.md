by-jove:/baɪ ˈdʒoʊv/
interjection
哎呀；天哪
An exclamation of surprise or emphasis.
By Jove, that's a big fish! 哎呀，那条鱼真大！
近义词: by George, by God, goodness me, my goodness, my word, good heavens, gosh
反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection of surprise)
词性: interjection
