damageability:/ˌdæmɪdʒəˈbɪləti/| n. |损坏性，易损性|The damageability of the goods during transit is a major concern.  运输过程中货物的易损性是一个主要问题。|例句：The damageability of the new material is significantly lower than the old one.  新材料的损坏性明显低于旧材料。

例句：The insurance company assessed the damageability of the car after the accident.  保险公司在事故发生后评估了汽车的损坏性。|近义词：vulnerability, fragility, susceptibility|反义词：durability, resilience, robustness|词性:名词

词源: damage (n.) + -able (suffix) + -ity (suffix)  来自 damage（损害）+ -able（可...的）+ -ity（名词后缀，表示性质或状态）。
