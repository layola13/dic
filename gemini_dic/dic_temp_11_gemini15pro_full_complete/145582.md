overconcentrate: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt/ | v. | 过度集中；过分专注 | to concentrate too much on one thing, often to the exclusion of others |  He overconcentrated on his studies and neglected his health. 他过度专注于学习而忽略了健康。|  She tends to overconcentrate on details and misses the bigger picture. 她往往过度关注细节而忽略了大局。| 近义词: fixate, obsess | 反义词: neglect, disregard | 词性: 动词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "concentrate" (from Latin "concentrare," meaning "to bring to a center").
