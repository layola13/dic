circumterraneous:/ˌsɜːrkəm.təˈreɪ.ni.əs/
adjective
环绕地球的；地下的
Existing or operating under the earth's surface; encompassing the earth.
The circumterraneous caverns were home to a variety of strange creatures. 环绕地球的洞穴里栖息着各种各样的奇异生物。
The mole's circumterraneous tunnels extended for miles. 鼹鼠的地下隧道绵延数英里。
近义词:subterranean, underground
反义词:aboveground, surface
词性:adjective
