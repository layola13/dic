dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/ /dɪsˈmeɪ/| n. vt.|沮丧，灰心；使沮丧，使灰心|A feeling of unhappiness and disappointment.|The news of the defeat filled them with dismay.  战败的消息使他们充满了沮丧。|近义词：discouragement, disappointment, consternation, alarm|反义词：encouragement, hope, joy|词性:名词,动词

dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/ /dɪsˈmeɪ/| vt.|使沮丧，使灰心|To cause someone to feel unhappiness and disappointment.|The sudden drop in sales dismayed investors. 销售额的突然下降使投资者感到沮丧。|近义词：dishearten, discourage, daunt|反义词：encourage, hearten, cheer|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old French *desmayer "lose courage," from des- "away" +  Old French esmayer "dismay."
