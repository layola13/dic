intelligently:/ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəntli/| adv.|聪明地，明智地|In a way that shows intelligence; cleverly.  |She handled the situation intelligently. 她聪明地处理了这个情况。| synonym: cleverly, wisely, smartly, astutely, judiciously | antonym: foolishly, stupidly, unwisely, thoughtlessly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Late Latin *intelligenter, from Latin intelligens (present participle of intelligere “to understand”) + -ter (adverbial suffix).
