nineholes:/ˈnaɪnˌhoʊlz/
n.
九洞高尔夫球场; 九洞高尔夫球赛
A nine-hole golf course; a game of golf played on a nine-hole course.
例句：
We played nineholes this morning. 
我们今天早上打了九洞高尔夫球。

Let's go for nineholes after work.
下班后我们去打九洞高尔夫吧。

近义词:short course, executive course
反义词:eighteen holes, full course
