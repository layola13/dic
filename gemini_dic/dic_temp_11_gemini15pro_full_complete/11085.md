archaism:/ˈɑːrkeɪɪzəm/ | n. | 古旧；过时； archaism | the use of old or old-fashioned words | The author's frequent archaisms make the text difficult to understand. 作者频繁使用古语使得文本难以理解。| synonyms: antiquatedness, obsolescence | antonyms: modernity, currency | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *archaismus, from Ancient Greek ἀρχαϊσμός (arkhaïsmós, “an ancient or obsolete word or expression”), from ἀρχαῖος (arkhaîos, “ancient”) + -ισμός (-ismós).
