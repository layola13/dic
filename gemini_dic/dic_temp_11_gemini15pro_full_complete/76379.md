gads:/ɡædz/| n. |大量，许多 (尤用于表示惊奇或恼怒)|A large number or quantity of something (used especially to express surprise or annoyance).|例句：My gads, look at all this mess!  我的天哪，看看这一团糟！

例句：Good gads, what have you done? 天哪，你都做了些什么？|近义词：lots, heaps, tons, loads, oodles|反义词：few, little|词性:名词

gads:/ɡædz/| interj. |(表示惊奇或恼怒)天哪，哎呀|(expressing surprise or annoyance) an exclamation of surprise, dismay, etc.|例句：Gads! I've forgotten my keys!  哎呀！我忘了带钥匙了！

例句：Oh, my gads! I can't believe it.  哦，我的天哪！我真不敢相信。|近义词：goodness, heavens, gosh, gee|反义词： (无明确的反义词，因为是感叹词)|词性:感叹词
