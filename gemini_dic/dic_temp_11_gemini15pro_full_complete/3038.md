aerobiological:/ˌeəroʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
空气生物学的
Relating to the study of airborne biological particles, such as pollen, spores, and microorganisms.
Aerobiological studies are important for understanding the spread of allergens and diseases. 空气生物学研究对于了解过敏原和疾病的传播非常重要。
Aerobiological sampling methods are used to monitor air quality.  空气生物学采样方法用于监测空气质量。
近义词:airborne, atmospheric
反义词:None
词性:adjective

Etymology:
aero- (Greek aer, air) + biological
