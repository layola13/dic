echoing:/ˈekəʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɛkoʊɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |回响的，重复的| producing or characterized by echoes; repeating or imitating something, especially a sound or opinion. |The echoing halls of the castle amplified the slightest sound. 城堡里回响的大厅放大了最轻微的声音。| The politician's echoing statements lacked originality.  这位政客重复的声明缺乏原创性。|近义词：resonant, reverberating, repetitive, imitative|反义词: muffled, dampened, original|adjective

echoing:/ˈekəʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɛkoʊɪŋ/ (US)| n. |回声，共鸣| a sound or series of sounds caused by the reflection of sound waves from a surface back to the listener.|The echoing of his voice in the canyon was eerie. 他的声音在峡谷中的回声令人毛骨悚然。| The echoing of her mother's sentiments in her own beliefs was clear.  她母亲的观点在她自己的信仰中的共鸣很明显。|近义词：resonance, reverberation, repetition|反义词: silence, quiet|noun
