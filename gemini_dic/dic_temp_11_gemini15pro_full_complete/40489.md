词源:
From Late Latin contrītus, past participle of conterere (“to grind, bruise, crush”), from com- (“together”) + terere (“to rub, grind”).  来自晚期拉丁语 contrītus，是 conterere（“磨碎、擦伤、压碎”）的过去分词，源自 com-（“一起”）+ terere（“摩擦、磨碎”）。
