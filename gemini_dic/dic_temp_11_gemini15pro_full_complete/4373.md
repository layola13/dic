noun
敏捷, 活泼, 乐意
cheerful readiness, eagerness, and promptness
The team worked with alacrity to finish the project before the deadline.  团队以敏捷的速度赶在截止日期前完成了项目。
Her alacrity to help was truly inspiring. 她乐于助人的精神真是令人鼓舞。
Synonyms: eagerness, willingness, readiness, promptness, enthusiasm
Antonyms: reluctance, apathy, unwillingness, hesitation
