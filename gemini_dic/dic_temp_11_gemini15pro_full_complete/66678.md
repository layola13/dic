n.
出埃及记; 大批离去
Possessive form of exodus, referring to something belonging to or associated with a mass departure of people.

The exodus's main cause was the severe drought. 这次大批离去的主要原因是严重的干旱。
synonyms: departure, migration, emigration, flight
antonyms: arrival, influx, immigration, return
