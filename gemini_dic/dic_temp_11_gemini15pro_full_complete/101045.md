inveigle:/ɪnˈveɪɡl/ /ɪnˈviːɡl/
verb
引诱，诱骗
persuade (someone) to do something by means of deception or flattery.
例句：
He inveigled her into coming home with him. 他诱骗她跟他回家。

She was inveigled into joining the pyramid scheme. 她被诱骗加入了传销骗局。

近义词：cajole, coax, wheedle, entice, lure, tempt
反义词：dissuade, deter, discourage
词性：v.
