claret:/ˈklærɪt/ /ˈklɛrət/ | n. | 红葡萄酒；深紫红色 | A dry red wine from the Bordeaux region of France.  Also, a deep purplish-red color. |  例句：He ordered a bottle of claret with dinner.  他点了一瓶红葡萄酒佐餐。

例句：Her dress was a rich claret color. 她的裙子是浓郁的深紫红色。 | 近义词：red wine, Bordeaux, burgundy (for the wine); maroon, purple (for the color) | 反义词: white wine (for the wine) | 词性:noun


claret:/ˈklærɪt/ /ˈklɛrət/ | adj. | 深紫红色的 | Having the deep purplish-red color of claret. | 例句：The curtains were a claret color. 窗帘是深紫红色的。

例句：He wore a claret tie. 他戴着一条深紫红色的领带。 | 近义词：maroon, purple, burgundy | 反义词: (No direct opposite for a color, could consider contrasting colors like light green or yellow) | 词性:adjective

词源: From Old French *claret, diminutive of clair "clear", referring to the lighter color of the wine compared to darker reds.
