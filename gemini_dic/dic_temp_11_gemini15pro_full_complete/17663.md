beckon:/ˈbekən/ (US), /ˈbɛkən/ (UK)| v. |召唤；吸引；示意|to make a gesture with the hand, arm, or head to encourage someone to come nearer or follow|The flashing lights beckoned him closer. 闪烁的灯光吸引他走近。|近义词：summon, signal, invite, attract, lure|反义词：repel, deter, discourage|词性:动词

beckon:/ˈbekən/ (US), /ˈbɛkən/ (UK)| n. |召唤；吸引；示意|a gesture that summons or invites someone to come nearer|She felt the beckon of the open road. 她感受到了开阔道路的召唤。|近义词：summons, signal, invitation, attraction, lure|反义词：repulsion, deterrent|词性:名词
