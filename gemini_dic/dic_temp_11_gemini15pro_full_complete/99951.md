internuncius:/ɪntərˈnʌnsiəs/
noun
使者，信使，中间人
a messenger or envoy; a go-between.
The internuncius delivered the king's message to the neighboring kingdom. 国王的使者将国王的信送到了邻国。
The pope sent an internuncius to discuss the matter with the archbishop. 教皇派了一名使者与大主教讨论此事。
Synonyms: envoy, messenger, emissary, legate, delegate
Antonyms: recipient, receiver
n.
