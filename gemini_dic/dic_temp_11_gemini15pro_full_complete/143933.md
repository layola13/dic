outpursuing:/ˌaʊtpərˈsuːɪŋ/
|v.|超越追求，胜过|Surpassing in pursuit or effort; exceeding in diligence or zeal.|She is outpursuing her rivals in the race for the presidency. 她正在总统竞选中超越她的对手。|
近义词: outstripping, excelling, surpassing
|反义词: lagging, trailing|
词性: 动词 (present participle of outpursue)


outpursue:/ˌaʊtpərˈsuː/
|v.|追上，胜过|To pursue with greater speed or success; to surpass in any pursuit.|He managed to outpursue the other runners and win the race. 他成功地超越了其他选手并赢得了比赛。|
近义词: outrun, overtake, surpass
|反义词: lag behind, fall behind|
词性: 动词


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "pursue" (from Old French "porsuivre," from Latin "prosequi" - to follow forth, pursue)
