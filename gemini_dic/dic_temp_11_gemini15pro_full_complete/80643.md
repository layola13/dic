gophering:/ˈɡoʊfərɪŋ/
n.
忙于琐碎的事; 东奔西跑;  收集信息
The act of searching for information, especially online or through various sources, often likened to a gopher digging for food.
He spent hours gophering for information about his ancestors. 他花了几个小时四处搜寻关于他祖先的信息。
researching, searching, exploring, investigating
