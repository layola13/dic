outputter:/ˈaʊtpʊtər/| n.|输出器，输出装置|A device or program that outputs data or information.  It takes information from a computer and presents it in a usable form, such as text on a screen, sound from speakers, or a printed document. |The outputter sent the processed data to the printer.| 近义词:output device, output unit| 反义词:input device, input unit|noun

outputter:/ˈaʊtpʊtər/| n.|输出者，输出人员|A person or thing that produces or provides something, especially information. |The outputter of the report was responsible for ensuring its accuracy.| 近义词:producer, creator, author| 反义词:consumer, recipient|noun
