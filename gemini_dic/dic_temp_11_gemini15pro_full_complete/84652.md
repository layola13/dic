handball:/ˈhændˌbɔːl/ | n. |手球（运动）; 手球（球）;（某些运动中的）手球犯规|A sport resembling soccer, played indoors or outdoors on a court smaller than a soccer field, in which two teams of seven players try to throw or bat a small ball into a goal defended by a goalkeeper. Also, the ball used in this sport.  A foul in certain sports (like soccer or basketball) where a player illegally touches the ball with their hand or arm. | The handball team practiced hard for the upcoming tournament.  手球队为即将到来的锦标赛刻苦训练。

The new handball bounces really well. 这个新手球弹跳力很好。

The referee called a handball against the defender. 裁判判防守球员手球犯规。| 近义词: (for the sport) team handball,  (for the foul) handling | 反义词: (no single direct antonym - depends on context) | 词性: noun
