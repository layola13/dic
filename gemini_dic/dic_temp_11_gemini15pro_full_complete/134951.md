nonnarcotics:/ˌnɑːnɑːrˈkɑːtɪks/ | n. | 非麻醉药；非毒品 |  Medications that do not induce narcosis (a state of stupor, drowsiness, or unconsciousness).  They are not derived from opium or a related compound and do not have the addictive properties of narcotics. | The doctor prescribed nonnarcotics for her mild pain. 医生给她开了非麻醉药来治疗轻微疼痛。 |  The patient requested nonnarcotics pain relief. 病人要求非麻醉性止痛药。| synonyms: non-addictive analgesics, non-opioid pain relievers | antonyms: narcotics, opioids | noun

nonnarcotic: /ˌnɑːnɑːrˈkɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 非麻醉性的；非毒品的 | Not producing narcosis; not of or relating to narcotics. |  She was given a nonnarcotic analgesic after the procedure.  手术后，她服用了非麻醉性止痛药。| The dentist used a nonnarcotic anesthetic. 牙医使用了非麻醉性麻醉剂。| synonyms: non-addictive, non-opioid | antonyms: narcotic, opioid | adjective
