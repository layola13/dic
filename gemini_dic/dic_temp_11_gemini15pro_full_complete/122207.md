misutilization: /ˌmɪsˌjuːtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
误用，滥用
The misutilization of funds led to the project's failure.  资金的误用导致了项目的失败。
The report criticized the misutilization of resources. 报告批评了资源的滥用。
Etymology:  mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + utilization (from utilize, meaning "to make use of")  词源：mis-（前缀，意为“错误的”或“坏的”）+ utilization（来自utilize，意为“利用”）
Synonyms: misuse, abuse, misapplication
近义词：misuse, abuse, misapplication
Antonyms: proper use, appropriate application
反义词：proper use, appropriate application
