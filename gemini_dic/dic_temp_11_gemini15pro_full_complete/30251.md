cellulosities:/ˌsɛljʊˈlɒsɪtiz/
n.
纤维素性质；纤维素含量
The cellulosities of different plant species vary considerably. 不同植物种类的纤维素含量差异很大。
The study focused on the cellulosities of various wood samples.  这项研究的重点是各种木材样品的纤维素含量。
Near synonyms: cellulose content, cellulose levels
Antonyms: lignin content, hemicellulose content
noun
