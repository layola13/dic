odontophora:/ˌoʊdɒnˈtɒfərə/ | n. | 齿舌类动物 |  A taxonomic class within the phylum Mollusca, characterized by a toothed ribbon-like structure called a radula used for feeding. |  Odontophora includes snails, slugs, and other mollusks. 齿舌类动物包括蜗牛、蛞蝓和其他软体动物。 | synonyms:  Gastropoda (in some classifications) | antonyms: N/A | noun
