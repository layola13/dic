nondeviating:/ˌnɒndiːviˈeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不偏离的，不变的 | Not deviating; steady; consistent.  Maintaining a straight course or fixed direction; unwavering. | The missile followed a nondeviating path to its target. 导弹沿着一条不变的路径飞向目标。| The company maintained a nondeviating commitment to quality. 公司始终不渝地致力于质量。| 近义词：unswerving, steadfast, unwavering, undeviating | 反义词：deviating, varying, fluctuating, erratic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- (not) + deviating (present participle of deviate, from Latin *deviatus, past participle of *deviare "to turn aside," from de- "away" + via "way").
