indisposition:/ˌɪndɪspəˈzɪʃən/| n.|不舒服，略有不适，不情愿|A slight illness or a reluctance to do something.|例句：She couldn't attend the meeting due to a slight indisposition. 她因为略感不适而无法参加会议。

例句：He showed an indisposition to help with the project. 他表现出不愿意帮助这个项目。

|近义词：ailment, illness, sickness, reluctance, unwillingness|反义词: health, wellness, willingness, eagerness|词性:noun
