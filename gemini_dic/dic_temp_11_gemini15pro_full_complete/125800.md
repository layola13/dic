murkier:/ˈmɜːrkɪər (UK), ˈmɜːrkiər (US)| adj.|更阴暗的，更浑浊的，更模糊的|Comparative form of murky: more murky.  Meaning more dark and gloomy, more unclear or obscure.|The future of the company looks murkier than ever.  这家公司的前景比以往任何时候都更加黯淡。|The river is much murkier after the storm.  暴风雨过后，河水更加浑浊了。|近义词：darker, gloomier, more obscure, more opaque|反义词：brighter, clearer, more transparent|词性:形容词
murky:/ˈmɜːrki (UK), ˈmɜːrki (US)| adj.|阴暗的，浑浊的，模糊的|Dark and gloomy, especially due to thick mist or smoke; not clear or transparent; obscure or morally questionable.|The murky water of the swamp hid unseen dangers. 沼泽的浑浊的水中隐藏着看不见的危险。|His explanation was murky and confusing. 他的解释含糊不清，令人费解。|近义词：dark, gloomy, obscure, opaque|反义词：bright, clear, transparent|词性:形容词
