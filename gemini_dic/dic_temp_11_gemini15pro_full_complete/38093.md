compotation:/ˌkɒmpəˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 痛饮，狂饮 |  A feast or drinking bout; a revel.  The act of drinking together; a convivial drinking party. |  The king and his knights engaged in a night of compotation after their victory. 国王和他的骑士们在胜利后举行了通宵的狂欢痛饮。

 The annual compotation was a highlight of the village calendar.  一年一度的痛饮狂欢是村里日历上的亮点。| 近义词：carousal, revelry, bacchanalia, debauchery | 反义词：abstinence, sobriety, temperance | noun
