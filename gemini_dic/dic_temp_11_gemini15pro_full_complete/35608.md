coastal:/ˈkoʊstəl/ (US), /ˈkəʊstl/ (UK)| adj.|海岸的，沿海的|Relating to or located on or near a coast.|例句：The coastal region is known for its beautiful beaches. 这个沿海地区以其美丽的海滩而闻名。

例句：Coastal erosion is a serious problem in some areas.  海岸侵蚀在某些地区是一个严重的问题。

近义词：seaside, shore, littoral|反义词：inland, interior|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *coster, from Anglo-Norman coster, from Latin costa (“rib, side, flank, coast”), from Proto-Italic *kostā (“side”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kest- (“bone, skeleton”). Cognate with Old French coste (“rib, coast”), Italian costa (“coast, rib”), Portuguese and Spanish costa (“coast”), Romanian coastă (“rib”), Old Irish coss (“foot, leg”), Welsh coes (“leg”).
