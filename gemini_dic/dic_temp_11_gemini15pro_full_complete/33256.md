chrysalides: /krɪˈsælɪˌdiːz/
n.
蛹（chrysalis的复数）
The hard-shelled form of the pupa of certain insects, especially butterflies and moths.
例句：The chrysalides hung from the branches, shimmering in the sunlight. 蛹悬挂在树枝上，在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：After weeks of waiting, the chrysalides finally began to hatch.  经过几周的等待，蛹终于开始孵化了。

近义词：pupae, cocoons
反义词:larvae, caterpillars
词性:noun


chrysalis: /ˈkrɪsəlɪs/
n.
蛹
The hard-shelled form of the pupa of certain insects, especially butterflies and moths.
例句：The butterfly emerged from its chrysalis. 蝴蝶从蛹里钻了出来。

例句：The chrysalis provided protection for the developing butterfly. 蛹为发育中的蝴蝶提供了保护。

近义词：pupa, cocoon
反义词: larva, caterpillar
词性:noun


词源：From Latin *chrysallis, from Greek khrysallis, from khrysos "gold."  源自拉丁语 *chrysallis，来自希腊语 khrysallis，来自 khrysos “黄金”。
