infiltrate:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪt/| v. n.|渗透，潜入；（液体）渗入|To enter or gain access to (an organization, place, etc.) secretly and gradually, especially in order to acquire information or cause damage; (of a liquid) to permeate something by filtration. |The spy infiltrated the enemy's ranks.  间谍潜入了敌人的队伍。|synonyms: permeate, penetrate, infiltrate, pervade|antonyms:  exfiltrate, withdraw, retreat|verb

infiltrate:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪt/| n.|渗透；渗透物|The act of infiltrating; something that infiltrates. |The infiltration of enemy agents was a serious security breach. 敌方特工的渗透是严重的 Sicherheitslücke。|synonyms:  penetration, permeation|antonyms:  withdrawal, retreat|noun
