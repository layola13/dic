kabukis: /kəˈbuːkiz/ | n. | 歌舞伎 | A traditional Japanese form of theatre with highly stylized song, mime, and dance, usually performed by male actors. |  We saw a thrilling performance of kabuki in Tokyo. 我们在东京看了一场精彩的歌舞伎表演。 |  Kabuki is known for its elaborate costumes and makeup. 歌舞伎以其精致的服装和化妆而闻名。 | Synonyms: Japanese drama, traditional theatre | Antonyms: modern theatre | noun
