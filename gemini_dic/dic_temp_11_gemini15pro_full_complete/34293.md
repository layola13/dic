clarifier:/ˈklærɪfaɪər/| n.|澄清剂，净化器|A substance used to remove impurities or suspended particles from a liquid or gas, making it clear or transparent.|The water treatment plant uses a clarifier to remove sediment from the drinking water. 水处理厂使用澄清剂去除饮用水中的沉淀物。|synonyms: purifier, filter, cleaner|antonyms: contaminant, pollutant|noun

clarify:/ˈklærɪfaɪ/| vt.|澄清，阐明|To make (a statement or situation) less confused and more clearly comprehensible.|Could you clarify your earlier remarks? 你能澄清一下你之前说的话吗？|synonyms: explain, elucidate, explicate|antonyms: confuse, obscure, complicate|verb
