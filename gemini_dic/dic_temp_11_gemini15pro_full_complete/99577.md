interinfluenced:/ˌɪntərˈɪnfluənst/
verb
相互影响
Having a reciprocal effect on each other; mutually influencing.
The two cultures interinfluenced each other, leading to a unique blend of traditions. 这两种文化相互影响，形成了独特的传统融合。
The artists' styles interinfluenced each other, creating a new artistic movement.  这些艺术家的风格相互影响，创造了一个新的艺术运动。
近义词: interacted, affected, shaped, impacted
反义词: independent, separate, isolated
词性: verb

词源:
From inter- + influenced, past participle of influence.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "influence" meaning "to have an effect on."
