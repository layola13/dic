ctenostomata:/ˌtɛnəˈstɒmətə/| n. |栉口目动物|An order of aquatic invertebrates, commonly known as comb jellies, characterized by eight rows of comb-like plates used for locomotion.|Comb jellies, belonging to the phylum Ctenophora and the order Ctenostomata, are fascinating creatures of the deep sea.  栉水母，属于栉水母门和栉口目，是迷人的深海生物。|Synonym: comb jellies|Antonym: N/A|noun
