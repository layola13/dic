author:/ˈɔːθər/ (US) /ˈɔːθə(r)/ (UK)| n.|作者，作家|A person who writes books, articles, or other literary works.|例句：The author of the Harry Potter series is J.K. Rowling. 哈利波特系列丛书的作者是J.K.罗琳。

例句：She is a well-known author of children's books. 她是一位著名的儿童书籍作家。

近义词：writer, novelist, composer|反义词: reader, audience |词性: noun
