lying-in:/ˈlaɪɪŋ ɪn/
n.
分娩; 产褥期
the period of time that a woman spends in bed after giving birth to a child
After the lying-in, she felt much better.  产后，她感觉好多了。

近义词:confinement, postpartum period
反义词:None
词性:名词


lying-in:/ˈlaɪɪŋ ɪn/
adj.
产褥的
related to the period of time a woman spends in bed after giving birth
She needed lying-in care after the birth of her twins. 她生完双胞胎后需要产后护理。


近义词:postpartum, postnatal
反义词:None
