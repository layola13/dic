fibrinogen:/faɪˈbrɪnədʒən/ | n. | 纤维蛋白原 | A soluble protein present in blood plasma, from which fibrin is produced by the action of the enzyme thrombin. |  Elevated fibrinogen levels are associated with an increased risk of cardiovascular disease. 纤维蛋白原水平升高与心血管疾病风险增加相关。

Fibrinogen is essential for blood clotting. 纤维蛋白原对血液凝固至关重要。 | Near synonyms:  clotting factor,  plasma protein | Antonyms:  anticoagulant | Noun
