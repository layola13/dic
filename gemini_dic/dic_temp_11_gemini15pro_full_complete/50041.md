denotement:/ˌdɪnəʊtˈmɒnt/ /ˌdiːnoʊtˈmɛnt/| n. |结局，收场；（戏剧、小说等的）结局；结果|The denouement of the play was rather disappointing.  这出戏的结局相当令人失望。|The novel's denouement was predictable. 这部小说的结局是可以预见的。|近义词：conclusion, ending, resolution, outcome|反义词: beginning, start, introduction|noun

denotement:/ˌdɪnəʊtˈmɒnt/ /ˌdiːnoʊtˈmɛnt/| n. |表示；指示|His silence denoted agreement. 他的沉默表示同意。|The red flag denotes danger.  红旗表示危险。|近义词：signify, indicate, represent, mean|反义词: conceal, hide, obscure|noun
