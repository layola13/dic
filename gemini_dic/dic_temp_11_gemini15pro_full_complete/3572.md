agapes:/ˈæɡəˌpiːz/
|n.|爱餐；（基督教早期）爱宴；|A love feast; a communal meal, especially in the early Christian church, symbolizing fellowship and brotherly love.|"The early Christians celebrated agapes as a way to strengthen their bonds of community." 早期基督徒庆祝爱餐以加强他们的社区联系。|近义词：feast, banquet, communion|反义词：discord, disunity|词性：名词
agape:/ˈæɡəpiː/
|n.|博爱；无私的爱；|Unconditional or selfless love, especially as contrasted with erotic love or emotional affection.|"Agape is considered the highest form of love in many philosophical and religious traditions."  博爱在许多哲学和宗教传统中被认为是最高形式的爱。|近义词：charity, benevolence, altruism|反义词：selfishness, hatred|词性：名词
