countersink: /ˈkaʊntərˌsɪŋk/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəsɪŋk/ (UK)| v.| 使成沉头孔；用埋头螺钉固定| To cut a countersink in (a surface).| He countersank the screw holes so the heads would be flush. 他把螺丝孔做成了沉头孔，这样螺丝头就能与表面齐平了。|近义词: recess |反义词: protrude |词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + sink.  "Counter" in this context means "opposite" or "reciprocal," referring to the reversed conical shape of the hole compared to a regular sink (a basin or depression).
