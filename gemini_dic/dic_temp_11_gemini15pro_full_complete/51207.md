destinying:/ˈdɛstɪnɪŋ/
verb
注定;预定
The act of assigning or preordaining a fate or purpose to someone or something.
He felt that his life was destining him for greatness. 他觉得他的命运注定要成就一番伟业。
preordaining, fating, dooming
--- (No clear antonym exists, perhaps "randomizing" or "leaving to chance")
