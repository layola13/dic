noncomputation:/ˌnɒnkɒmpjuˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 非计算，非运算 |  The process or state of not performing a computation.  It often refers to tasks or aspects of a system that do not involve algorithmic processing or numerical calculation. |  The artist argued that true creativity resides in the realm of noncomputation. 这位艺术家认为真正的创造力存在于非计算的领域。| synonyms: non-calculation, non-processing | antonyms: computation, calculation | noun
