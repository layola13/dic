dump's:/dʌmps/ | n. | 垃圾场；废物堆； (informal) 沮丧；忧郁 |  A place where refuse is deposited and left.  Also, a state of depression or low spirits. |  The city dump's smell was unbearable.  城市的垃圾场气味难以忍受。 He was in the dumps after losing his job.  他失业后情绪低落。 | 近义词: junkyard, landfill, blues, doldrums | 反义词:  paradise, euphoria | 词性: 名词
