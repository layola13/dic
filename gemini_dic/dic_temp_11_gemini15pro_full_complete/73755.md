forlornness:/fərˈlɔːrnɪs/
n.
凄凉；孤寂；绝望
a state of sadness, loneliness, and hopelessness
The forlornness of the empty house was overwhelming. 空荡荡的房子弥漫着令人难以承受的凄凉。
近义词：desolation, loneliness, misery, hopelessness
反义词：happiness, cheerfulness, hopefulness
词性: 名词

forlornness:/fərˈlɔːrnɪs/
n.
被遗弃的状态；荒凉
a state of being abandoned or deserted; dreariness
The forlornness of the abandoned village was palpable.  废弃村庄的荒凉感显而易见。
近义词：abandonment, desolation, bleakness
反义词：inhabited, lively, cheerful
词性: 名词


词源：
From Middle English *forlornesse, from forloren (“lost, ruined, abandoned”) +‎ -ness, from Old English forloren (“lost, ruined, abandoned”).  More at forlorn.
