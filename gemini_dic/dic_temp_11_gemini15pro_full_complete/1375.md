accrued:/əˈkruːd/ | adj. | 已累计的，应计的 | accumulated or increased over time, especially because of interest | The accrued interest on the account was substantial.  账户上累计的利息相当可观。 | Example:  Interest accrued on the savings account.  储蓄账户上的利息增加了。| Synonyms: accumulated, gathered, amassed | Antonyms: decreased, diminished, reduced | Word Type: Adjective

accrue:/əˈkruː/ | v. | 累积，应计 | (of sums of money or benefits) be received by someone in regular or increasing amounts over time. |  Interest will accrue on the account at a rate of 5% per annum.  该账户将以每年 5% 的利率累积利息。 | Example:  Benefits accrue to society from education. 教育使社会受益。| Synonyms: accumulate, collect, gather | Antonyms: decrease, diminish, dwindle | Word Type: Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *accruen*, from Old French *acru*, past participle of *acroisstre* "to increase, grow," from Latin *accrescere* "to increase," from *ad-* "to" + *crescere* "to grow."
