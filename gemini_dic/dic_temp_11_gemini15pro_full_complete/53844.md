discontent:/dɪskənˈtɛnt/ | adj. | 不满的，不满足的 | not content or satisfied; dissatisfied | Many voters are discontent with the current political situation. 许多选民对目前的政治局势感到不满。 | synonyms: dissatisfied, disgruntled, malcontent | antonyms: content, satisfied, happy | adjective
