backbiter:/ˈbækˌbaɪtər/| n. | 背后诽谤者，说坏话的人 | A person who says mean or unpleasant things about someone behind their back. |  She's known as a backbiter, so be careful what you tell her. 她以爱说人坏话著称，所以小心你告诉她的事。

He's a backbiter and a gossip, so I wouldn't trust him. 他是个背后诽谤者和爱嚼舌根的人，所以我不会相信他。 | 近义词： slanderer, defamer, gossipmonger | 反义词:  supporter, admirer | 词性: noun
