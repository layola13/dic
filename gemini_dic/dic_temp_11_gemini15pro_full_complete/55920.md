divvied:/dɪˈvaɪd/| v. |分摊，分配|To share or distribute something among a group of people.|They divvied up the prize money equally among the winners. 他们将奖金平均分配给了获胜者。|We divvied the pizza between us. 我们把披萨分了。|近义词：split, share, distribute, allocate|反义词: hoard, keep, monopolize|词性:动词

词源:  "Divvy" is a back-formation from the word "dividend," which comes from the Latin "dividendum," meaning "something to be divided."  The verb "divvy" emerged in the late 19th century.
