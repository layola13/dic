mustarder:/ˈmʌstərdər/ | n. |芥末瓶;芥末罐;芥末粉研磨器| A container for holding or grinding mustard. |  She reached for the silver mustarder and added a dollop to her sandwich. 她伸手拿起银芥末瓶，在她的三明治上加了一团芥末。 | synonym: mustard pot, mustard mill | antonym: N/A | noun

Etymology->
From "mustard" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting instruments or containers).  "Mustard" ultimately comes from the Latin "mustum" (must, new wine) and "ardens" (burning), referring to the pungent taste.
