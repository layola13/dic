dulcino:/duːlˈtʃiːnoʊ/ /dʌlˈtʃiːnoʊ/
noun
一种虚构的甜味剂；理想化的爱人；梦想中的情人;
A fictional sweetening agent; an idealized lover; a dream lover.
Don Quixote was infatuated with his dulcino, Dulcinea. 堂吉诃德迷恋着他的梦中情人杜尔西内娅。
Synonyms: sweetheart, beloved, darling, ideal, fantasy
Antonyms: enemy, foe, rival
