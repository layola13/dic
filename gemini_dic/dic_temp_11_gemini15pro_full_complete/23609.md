branch:/bræntʃ/ | n. | 分支；树枝；分部；部门 | A part of something larger, like a tree limb or a company division. | The bank has branches all over the country.  这家银行在全国各地都有分行。| synonyms: limb, offshoot, division, department | antonyms: trunk, main, root | noun

branch:/bræntʃ/ | v. | 分支；出现分歧 | To divide into separate parts. | The road branches off to the left. 这条路向左分叉。| synonyms: diverge, fork, separate | antonyms: converge, join, unite | verb
