ascensions: /əˈsɛnʃənz/
n.
上升，登高，提升
The act of ascending; rising or going up.
The Feast of the Ascension celebrates Christ's ascension into heaven.  耶稣升天节庆祝基督升天。
Hot air balloon ascensions are a popular tourist attraction.  热气球升空是一项很受欢迎的旅游活动。
ascents, climbs, risings
descents, declines, falls
