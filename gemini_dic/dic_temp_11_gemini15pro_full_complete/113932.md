muffler:/ˈmʌflər/ (US) /ˈmʌflə(r)/ (UK)| n.|消声器；围巾；头巾|A device used to reduce noise, especially on a car's exhaust system; a scarf worn around the neck for warmth.|例句：The car's muffler needs to be replaced. 汽车的消声器需要更换。|近义词：silencer, scarf (in some contexts)|反义词:amplifier (for the noise-reducing sense)|noun
muffler:/ˈmʌflər/ (US) /ˈmʌflə(r)/ (UK)| v.|消音；减弱声音；使声音低沉|To reduce the noise of something.|例句：She muffled her cough with her hand. 她用手捂住嘴咳嗽。|近义词：mute, deaden, suppress|反义词:amplify|verb
