balafon:/ˈbæləfɒn/ (UK), /ˈbæləfɑːn/ (US)| n. |巴拉风，木琴|A West African xylophone with calabash resonators under each bar. It is played with mallets.|The balafon player captivated the audience with his intricate melodies. 巴拉风演奏者用他复杂的旋律吸引了观众。|Synonym: xylophone, marimba|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "silent instrument")|Noun
