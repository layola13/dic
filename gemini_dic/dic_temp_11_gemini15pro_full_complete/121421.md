misinformants:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmənts/ | n. | 误导者；提供错误信息的人 | People who give incorrect information, either intentionally or unintentionally. | The journalists realized they had been misled by misinformation from several misinformants. 记者们意识到他们被几个误导者提供的错误信息误导了。| The article was retracted after the editor discovered the key sources were misinformants. 在编辑发现关键消息来源是误导者之后，这篇文章被撤回了。| synonyms: liars, deceivers, fabricators | antonyms: informants, truth-tellers | noun
