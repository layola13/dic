noncontemporaneousness:/nɒnkənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsnəs/ | n. | 非同时性；不同时发生 | The noncontemporaneousness of the data made it difficult to draw accurate conclusions.  数据非同时性使得难以得出准确的结论。 | Example: The noncontemporaneousness of the two events made it impossible to establish a causal link. 这两个事件的不同时性使得建立因果关系成为不可能。| Synonym: asynchrony, non-simultaneity | Antonym: contemporaneity, simultaneity | noun

noncontemporaneous:/nɒnkənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ | adj. | 非同时的；不同时的 | The noncontemporaneous nature of the data made analysis challenging. 数据的非同时性使得分析具有挑战性。 | Example: The researcher compared noncontemporaneous accounts of the historical event. 研究人员比较了对该历史事件的不同时描述。 | Synonym: asynchronous, non-simultaneous | Antonym: contemporaneous, simultaneous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + contemporaneous + -ness (state of being). Contemporaneous comes from Latin contemporaneus, from con- (together) + tempus (time).
