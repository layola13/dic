brachygraphy:/brəˈkɪɡrəfi/ | n. |速记，速记法 |The art or practice of writing in shorthand.  It involves using symbols and abbreviations to represent words and phrases, allowing for faster writing. |She studied brachygraphy to keep up with the fast-paced lectures. 她学习速记以便跟上快节奏的讲课。| synonym: shorthand, stenography | antonym: longhand | noun
