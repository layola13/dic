legatee:/ˈlɛɡəˈtiː/ /ləˈɡeˈtiː/| n.|继承人，受遗赠人|A person who receives a legacy (a gift of personal property) in a will.|Example 1:  She was named as the sole legatee in her aunt's will. 她被指定为她姨妈遗嘱中的唯一继承人。

Example 2: The legatee received a substantial sum of money.  这位继承人得到了一大笔钱。|Synonym: inheritor, beneficiary, heir|Antonym: testator (遗嘱人), grantor (让与人)|noun
