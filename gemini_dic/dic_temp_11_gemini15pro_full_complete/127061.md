natalities:/nəˈtælɪtiz/
noun
出生率
the ratio of live births to the population of a particular area during a particular period of time
The natalities in the region have been declining steadily for the past decade.  该地区出生率在过去十年中一直在稳步下降。

Synonym: birth rate, fertility rate
Antonym: mortality rate
