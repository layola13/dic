cavesson:/ˈkævəsən/ | n. | 马笼头；鼻革 | A type of halter or noseband used on horses, typically for lunging or training.  It fits over the horse's nose and is often used with a lunge line. |  The trainer attached the lunge line to the cavesson.  训练员把调教绳系在马笼头上。| Synonyms: lunging cavesson, noseband | Antonyms: bridle | noun

cavesson:/ˈkævəsən/ | n. |  (尤指调教马匹用的) 无嚼口的笼头 | A headstall without a bit, especially one used for training horses. | The young horse was trained using a cavesson. 这匹小马是用无嚼口笼头训练的。| Synonyms:  lunge cavesson,  headcollar (British) | Antonyms: bitted bridle | noun
