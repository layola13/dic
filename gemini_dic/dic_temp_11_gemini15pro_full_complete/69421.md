feeling-for:/ˈfiːlɪŋ fɔːr/ | n. | 同情，理解，体谅;  触觉，感觉 | An intuitive understanding and sensitivity towards someone or something.  The ability to perceive something through touch. | She had a real feeling-for the suffering of others. 她对别人的痛苦有真切的感受。He lost the feeling-for his craft after the accident. 事故之后，他失去了对手艺的感觉。 | 近义词：empathy, compassion, sympathy, intuition, touch, sense | 反义词：apathy, indifference, insensitivity | 词性: 名词


词源:  From the verb "feel" (Old English *fēlan "to perceive by touch") + "for" (Old English *fore "before, in front of," also "on account of, because of").  The sense evolved from physically feeling for something to emotionally understanding someone's situation.
