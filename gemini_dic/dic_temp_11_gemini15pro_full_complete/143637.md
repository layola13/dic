outjuts:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌts/
outjut: v.
outjut: 伸出，突出
outjut: to stick out further than something else; to protrude.
outjut: The rocky cliff outjuts into the sea.  这块岩石峭壁伸入海中。

outjut: project, protrude, extend, jut out
outjut: recede, retract, withdraw

outjut: verb
