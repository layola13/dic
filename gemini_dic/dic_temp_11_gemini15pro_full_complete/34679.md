clerisy: /ˈklɛrɪsi/ | n. |知识阶层；文人阶层；知识分子|A body of learned persons, especially the clergy or educated class.  A group of writers and intellectuals who uphold or propagate a particular ideology or belief system.|例句：The nation's clerisy debated the merits of the new policy.  这个国家的知识阶层就新政策的优劣进行了辩论。

例句：The clerisy often influences public opinion. 知识分子阶层经常影响公众舆论。|近义词：intelligentsia, intellectuals, literati, academics |反义词:illiterate, uneducated |词性:noun
