noncorrespondent:/ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈspɒndənt/ | adj. | 不一致的，不符的，不相符的 | Not matching or agreeing; not corresponding. | The figures quoted in the two reports are noncorrespondent. 这两份报告中引用的数字不一致。 | The bank statements and the company's records were noncorrespondent, raising concerns about potential fraud. 银行对账单和公司的记录不符，引发了对潜在欺诈的担忧。 |  Near synonyms:  discrepant, divergent, inconsistent, dissimilar | Antonyms:  corresponding, consistent, matching, congruent | adjective
