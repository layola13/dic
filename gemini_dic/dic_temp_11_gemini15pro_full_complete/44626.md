It can refer to several different species, including Queen Anne's Lace (Daucus carota).| The meadow was dotted with delicate crowflowers.|近义词：Queen Anne's Lace, wild carrot, Bishop's lace |反义词: (No direct antonym, as it is a specific type of flower) |词性:noun
