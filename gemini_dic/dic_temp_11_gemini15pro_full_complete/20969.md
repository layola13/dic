blench:/blɛntʃ/ | v. | 畏缩，退缩；（脸色）变白 | to suddenly become pale, usually because you are shocked or frightened |  He blenched at the sight of the blood. 他看到血吓得脸色发白。 | synonyms: flinch, recoil, blanch | antonyms:  embolden, face, confront | verb

blenched:/blɛntʃt/ | adj. | 变得苍白的，退缩的 | having suddenly become pale, usually because of shock or fright | His blenched face showed how terrified he was. 他苍白的脸色表明他有多害怕。 | synonyms: pale, white, ashen | antonyms: flushed, ruddy | adjective
