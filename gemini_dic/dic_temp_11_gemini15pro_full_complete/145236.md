overbite:/ˈoʊvərˌbaɪt/| n.|覆咬合，龅牙|A condition in which the upper teeth protrude significantly over the lower teeth when the jaws are closed.|She has a noticeable overbite.|Synonyms: malocclusion, buck teeth|Antonyms: underbite|Noun

overbite:/ˈoʊvərˌbaɪt/| v.|（牙齿）覆咬合|To have the upper teeth protruding significantly over the lower teeth when the jaws are closed.|His upper teeth overbite his lower ones considerably.|Synonyms: overlap|Antonyms: underbite|Verb
