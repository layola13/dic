confident:/ˈkɑːnfɪdənt/ /ˈkɒnfɪdənt/| adj. |自信的；确信的|Feeling or showing certainty of one's abilities or character.  Having trust or faith in something.|She was confident that she would pass the exam. 她确信自己会通过考试。|Synonyms: assured, self-assured, positive, optimistic, certain, sure, convinced|Antonyms: unsure, uncertain, hesitant, doubtful, insecure, timid|adjective
