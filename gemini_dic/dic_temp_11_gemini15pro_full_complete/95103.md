例句：He was struck by the inanity of the television commercials. 他被电视广告的空洞无聊所震惊。

近义词：emptiness, hollowness, meaninglessness, futility, triviality |反义词:  significance, meaningfulness, substance, depth |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inanitas, from Latin inanis (“empty, void, vain”), from in- (“not”) + -anis (akin to anus (“old woman”), with connotations of uselessness).
