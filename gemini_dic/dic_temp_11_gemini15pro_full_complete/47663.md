debriefed:/diːˈbriːft/
verb
汇报；向…汇报；听取…的汇报
To question (someone, typically a pilot or astronaut) about a completed mission or undertaking.
The pilots were debriefed after every mission.  每次任务结束后，飞行员都要进行汇报。

debrief:/diːˈbriːf/
noun
汇报；行动情况汇报会
A meeting for giving and receiving information after a mission or project.
The team held a debrief to discuss the results of the project.  团队举行了一次汇报会，讨论项目的结果。
