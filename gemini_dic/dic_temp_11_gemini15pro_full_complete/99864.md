intermunicipal:/ˌɪntərˌmjuːˈnɪsɪpəl/
adjective
市政当局间的，跨市政的
Existing or occurring between municipalities.
The intermunicipal bus service connects several towns in the region. 该跨市政巴士服务连接了该地区的几个城镇。
近义词:intercity, regional
反义词:intramunicipal, local
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + municipal.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," "among," or "reciprocal." "Municipal" comes from the Latin word "municipalis," meaning "pertaining to a self-governing town."
