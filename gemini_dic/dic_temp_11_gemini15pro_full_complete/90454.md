huapan:/ˈwɑːpən/ | n. |  瓦潘; (墨西哥的一种浅底平锅) | A huapan is a shallow, flat-bottomed pan used in Mexican cuisine, typically made of clay or metal. It's often used for cooking sopes, tlacoyos, and other traditional dishes. | She cooked the sopes on a hot huapan.  她在一个热的瓦潘上烤索普饼。 |  近义词: comal (though comals can be larger and more concave), griddle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific cooking tool) | 词性: 名词
