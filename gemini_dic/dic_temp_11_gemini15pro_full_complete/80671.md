gore:/ɡɔːr/ (UK), /ɡɔːr/ (US)| n. v.| 血块；血污；三角形布料； (v.) 用角刺；戳|Blood that has been shed, especially as a result of violence. (n.) To pierce or stab with a horn or tusk. (v.)|例句：The movie was full of gore.  这部电影充满了血腥。  He gored the bull with his sword. 他用剑刺伤了公牛。|近义词：(n.) blood, carnage, slaughter; (v.) pierce, stab|反义词: (n.) peace, tranquility |词性:名词, 动词
