coelodont:/ˈsiːləˌdɑːnt/ | adj. n. |腔齿类动物的；腔齿类动物|An animal with hollow teeth, specifically referring to an extinct group of reptiles.  Relating to or belonging to the Coelodonta (woolly rhinoceros).| Example: Coelodonts were a diverse group of reptiles. 腔齿类动物是爬行动物的一个多样化的群体。

Example: The museum displayed a Coelodont skull.  博物馆展出了一个腔齿动物的头骨。|近义词: extinct reptile |反义词: extant animal |词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek koilos "hollow" + odont- "tooth".
