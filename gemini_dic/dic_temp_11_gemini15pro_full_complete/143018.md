outbitch:/ˌaʊtˈbɪtʃ/
verb
胜过；比…更泼妇
To outdo someone in being bitchy or behaving in a spiteful, malicious, or overly aggressive manner, especially a woman.
She tried to outbitch her rival, but ended up looking ridiculous. 她试图比她的对手更泼妇，但最终看起来很可笑。
My sister can outbitch anyone I know. 我妹妹比我认识的任何人都泼妇。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, surpass, outdo, defeat
Antonyms: concede, yield, submit
词性: 动词
