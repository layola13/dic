approves:/əˈpruːvz/| v.| 批准，认可，赞成|To officially accept or allow something.|The committee approves the new budget. 委员会批准了新的预算.|Synonym: accepts, sanctions, authorizes, endorses|Antonym: disapproves, rejects, opposes|Verb (third person singular present)

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *approven*, from Old French *aprover*, from Latin *approbare* (“to prove, test, approve”), from *ad-* (“to”) + *probare* (“to test, try, prove”), from *probus* (“good, honest”).
