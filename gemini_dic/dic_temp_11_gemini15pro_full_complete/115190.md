manichord:/ˈmænɪkɔːrd/ | n. | 古钢琴，曼尼考德琴 | A medieval stringed keyboard instrument, the strings of which are plucked by plectra activated from a keyboard.  It is a precursor to the harpsichord and clavichord. | The museum's collection includes a rare and beautifully preserved manichord. 博物馆的收藏包括一台稀有且保存完好的曼尼考德琴。 | The manichord produced a delicate, ethereal sound. 曼尼考德琴发出一种精致空灵的声音。 | Synonym: clavichord, virginal | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern piano as a contrasting instrument) | Noun
