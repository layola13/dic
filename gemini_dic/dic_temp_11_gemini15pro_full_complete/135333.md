nonpenal:/ˌnɒnˈpiːnəl/
adjective
非刑罚的
Not subject to criminal punishment.
The committee recommended nonpenal sanctions for minor offenses.  委员会建议对轻微违法行为处以非刑罚处罚。
Nonpenal measures, such as community service, were considered.  考虑了非刑罚措施，例如社区服务。
Synonyms: noncriminal, civil, administrative
Antonyms: penal, criminal
