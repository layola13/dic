nonexcessively:/ˌnɑːnɪkˈsɛsɪvli/
adverb
不过分地，适度地
Not to an excessive degree; moderately.
She exercised nonexcessively, careful not to strain her muscles. 她适度地锻炼，小心不要拉伤肌肉。
The food was seasoned nonexcessively, allowing the natural flavors to shine through.  食物的调味不过分，让天然的味道得以散发。
近义词: moderately, reasonably, temperately
反义词: excessively, immoderately, exorbitantly
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non
excessive (adjective):  meaning "too much"  来自拉丁语 excessivus,  excedere 的过去分词, 意思是 "to go beyond"
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs from adjectives
