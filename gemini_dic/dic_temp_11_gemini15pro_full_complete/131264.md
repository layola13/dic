noncategoricalness:/ˌnɒnkætəˈɡɒrɪkəlnəs/| n.|非绝对性，非范畴性|The noncategoricalness of the diagnosis made it difficult to prescribe a specific treatment.  诊断的非绝对性使得难以开出具体的治疗方案。|The noncategoricalness of his response left me unsure of his true intentions. 他的回答模棱两可，让我不确定他的真实意图。|近义词：ambiguity, vagueness, indeterminacy|反义词：certainty, definiteness, precision|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + categorical + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Categorical" comes from the Late Latin "categoricus," meaning "affirmative, predicative," from Greek "katēgorikos," related to "katēgorein" meaning "to accuse, to speak against, to preach," from "kata-" (down, against) + "agora" (public assembly).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
