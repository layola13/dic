nonreplaceable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈpleɪsəbl/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈpleɪsəbl/| adj.|不可替代的，无法取代的|Not capable of being replaced.|This antique vase is nonreplaceable.  这个古董花瓶是不可替代的。|The original documents are nonreplaceable. 原始文件是不可替代的。|近义词：irreplaceable, invaluable, essential|反义词：replaceable, dispensable|词性:形容词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + replaceable (from replace + -able)
