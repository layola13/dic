词源： crescent (n.) + -like (suffix)

crescent: from Old French *cressant, present participle of creistre "to grow" (from Latin crescere), used as a noun. Originally "increasing (moon)," it later acquired the specific sense of the waxing moon.
-like: Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-liko- "having the form or nature of, resembling."
