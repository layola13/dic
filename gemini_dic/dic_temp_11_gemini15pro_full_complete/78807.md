gimblet:/ˈɡɪmblət/ | n. | 手钻，钻孔器 | A small tool with a pointed screw at one end and a handle at the other, used for boring holes. | He used a gimblet to make a hole for the screw. 他用手钻钻了一个螺丝孔。|  I used a gimblet to start the pilot hole for the larger drill bit. 我用手钻钻了一个导向孔，以便使用更大的钻头。 | 近义词: awl, drill, auger | 反义词:  (无明显的反义词) | 词性: noun
