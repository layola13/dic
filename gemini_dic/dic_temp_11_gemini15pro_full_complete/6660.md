anagoge:/ˈænəɡɒdʒi/ (US) /ænəˈɡoʊdʒiː/ (UK)| n. |神秘的解释，精神上的解释，寓意解经|A mystical or spiritual interpretation of a text, especially of Scripture, that finds a higher meaning than the literal or historical one.  An anagoge unveils the hidden spiritual or moral meaning beneath the literal surface.|例句：The anagoge of the story of the exodus is seen as a representation of the soul's journey to God.  出埃及记故事的寓意被看作是灵魂走向上帝之旅的象征。

例句：Medieval scholars often employed anagoge to interpret biblical texts. 中世纪学者经常用寓意解经来解释圣经文本。|近义词：allegory, spiritual interpretation, mystical interpretation|反义词: literal interpretation|词性: noun
