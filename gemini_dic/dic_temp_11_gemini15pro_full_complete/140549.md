olfactorily:/ɑːlˈfæktərəli/
adverb
嗅觉上地
Relating to the sense of smell; in terms of smell.
The hound tracked the deer olfactorily.  猎犬靠嗅觉追踪鹿。
近义词: by smell, through smell
反义词: None (Lack of a direct antonym for a sensory process adverb)
词性: adverb

olfactory:/ɑːlˈfæktəri/
adjective
嗅觉的
Relating to the sense of smell.
The olfactory nerve transmits smells to the brain. 嗅觉神经将气味传递到大脑。
近义词: smelling, aromatic, fragrant
反义词: anosmic (related to the loss of smell)
词性: adjective
