gulflike:/ˈɡʌlfˌlaɪk/| adj.|像海湾的；像深渊的|Resembling a gulf, especially in depth, vastness, or separation.|The canyon was gulflike in its immensity.  这个峡谷的广阔无垠就像海湾一样。|The chasm between the two families was gulflike.  两个家庭之间的隔阂如同深渊。|近义词：abyssal, cavernous, vast, immense|反义词：shallow, narrow, small|词性:形容词
