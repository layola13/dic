civilisation:/ˌsɪvələˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌsɪvɪlɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|文明，文化；开化，教化|The stage of human social development and organization that is considered most advanced.|例句：The impact of technology on modern civilisation. 科技对现代文明的影响。|例句：The rise and fall of civilisations. 文明的兴衰。|近义词：culture, society|反义词：barbarity, savagery|词性:名词
