drop-behind:/drɑːp bɪˈhaɪnd/ | adj. | 落后的，拖后的 |  Failing to keep up with others or a schedule.  Someone or something that is drop-behind is lagging. | The runner started to drop-behind in the last lap of the race.  赛跑者在最后一圈开始落后。

The project is drop-behind schedule due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，该项目落后于计划。 | 近义词: lagging, straggling, trailing | 反义词: leading, ahead, advanced | 词性:形容词

词源:  "Drop" comes from Old Norse *droppa "to drop, drip." "Behind" comes from Old English behindan, from bi "by" + hindan "from behind."  The combination "drop-behind" signifies the act of falling back or becoming positioned behind others.
