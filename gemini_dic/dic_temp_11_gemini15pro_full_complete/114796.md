maltworm: /ˈmɔːltˌwɜːrm/ (UK) /ˈmɔːltˌwɝːm/ (US)| n. |酒鬼，嗜酒者|A person who drinks a lot of alcoholic beverages, especially beer or ale.  It implies heavy drinking and often carries a negative connotation. | He's become a real maltworm since he lost his job. 他失业后变成了一个真正的酒鬼。|Synonyms: drunkard, alcoholic, tippler, sot, inebriate|Antonyms: teetotaler, abstainer|noun
