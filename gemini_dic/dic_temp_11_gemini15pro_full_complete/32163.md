chewy:/ˈtʃuːi/| adj.|耐嚼的，有嚼劲的|Having a texture that requires considerable chewing; requiring much chewing before it can be swallowed. |This caramel is very chewy.  这种焦糖很耐嚼。|The steak was tough and chewy. 这块牛排又硬又难嚼。|近义词：tough, gummy, rubbery|反义词:tender, soft, melt-in-your-mouth|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From chew + -y.  chew 来自古英语 ceowan “咀嚼”。
