caued:/kɔːzd/ | v. | 造成的，引起 |Caused is the past tense and past participle of the verb "cause." It means to make something happen or to be the reason for something. | The heavy rain caused flooding. 大雨造成了洪水。|  The virus caused a pandemic. 病毒引起了大流行。 | synonyms: created, produced, generated, brought about, led to, resulted in, effected, occasioned | antonyms: prevented, stopped, averted, hindered, obstructed | verb
