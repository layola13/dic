healz: /hiːlz/
verb
治愈，痊愈
To make or become healthy or whole again.
She hoped the fresh air would heal her lungs. 她希望新鲜空气能治愈她的肺病。
近义词: cure, mend, remedy
反义词: injure, harm, wound
词性: verb

词源: From Old English *hælan, from Proto-Germanic *hailjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *kail- “whole, uninjured.” Cognate with German heil “whole, healthy,” Dutch heel “whole,” Gothic hails “whole, sound.”
