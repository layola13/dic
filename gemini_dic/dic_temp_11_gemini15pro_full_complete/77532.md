词源:  "Gbop" likely originated as an onomatopoeia, mimicking the sound of a quick, light tap or knock.  It's closely related to "bop," which has roots in jazz music and also describes a quick, rhythmic movement or sound.  "Gbop" may be a variation or intensification of "bop."
