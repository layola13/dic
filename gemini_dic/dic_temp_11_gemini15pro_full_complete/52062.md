dialogistical:/ˌdaɪəˌlɒˈdʒɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 逻辑的，辩证的；与逻辑或辩证法有关的 | Relating to logic or dialectic; argumentative. |  The dialogistical process helped them arrive at a sound conclusion.  辩证的过程帮助他们得出了一个合理的结论。|  The philosopher engaged in a dialogistical exchange with his students. 哲学家与他的学生进行了辩证交流。 | 近义词: logical, dialectical, argumentative, reasoned | 反义词: illogical, irrational, emotional | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *dialektikos* "of conversation, argumentative," from *dialegesthai* "to converse."
