omnivore:/ˈɑːmnɪvɔːr/ | n. |杂食动物；无所不知的人 | An animal or person that eats food of both plant and animal origin. |  Bears are omnivores, eating berries and fish. 熊是杂食动物，吃浆果和鱼。 | synonyms: generalist, all-eater | antonyms: herbivore, carnivore | noun

omnivorous:/ɑːmˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. |杂食的；什么都读的 | (of an animal) feeding on food of both plant and animal origin. |  Pigs are omnivorous animals. 猪是杂食动物。| synonyms: all-devouring, indiscriminate | antonyms: herbivorous, carnivorous | adjective
