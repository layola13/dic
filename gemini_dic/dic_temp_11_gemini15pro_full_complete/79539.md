glochidiate: /ɡloʊˈkɪdiˌeɪt/
adjective
钩毛状的；有钩毛的
Covered with barbed bristles or hairs; barbed.
例句：The cactus is glochidiate, so be careful when handling it.  这种仙人掌有钩毛，所以处理它时要小心。

例句：The glochidiate leaves of the plant can cause skin irritation.  这种植物的钩毛状叶子会引起皮肤刺激。

近义词：barbed, bristly, prickly
反义词：smooth, soft

词性：adjective

词源：From Modern Latin *glochidiatus, from Latin glochis (“point, barb of an arrow”), from Ancient Greek γλωχίς (glōkhís, “point of an arrow, barb”).
