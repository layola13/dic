interfriction:/ˌɪntərˈfrɪkʃən/
n.
内摩擦，内部摩擦
Friction between different parts or elements within a single entity or system.
The interfriction within the team hindered their progress.  团队内部的摩擦阻碍了他们的进步。
Interfriction between the moving parts caused the machine to overheat.  运动部件之间的内部摩擦导致机器过热。
近义词: internal friction, inner friction
反义词: cohesion, unity
词性: noun

词源:
From inter- + friction.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally." "Friction" from Late Middle English: from late Latin frictio(n- ), from Latin fricare ‘to rub.’
