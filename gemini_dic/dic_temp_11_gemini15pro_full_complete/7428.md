animosity:/ˌænɪˈmɒsɪti/ | n. | 敌意，仇恨，憎恶 | A strong feeling of dislike or hatred. |  She felt a surge of animosity towards her boss. 她对老板感到一阵强烈的敌意。

The animosity between the two families has lasted for generations.  两个家族之间的仇恨持续了几代人。| 近义词：hostility, antagonism, rancor, enmity, bitterness | 反义词：friendliness, goodwill, amity, affection | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *animositas, from Latin animosus (“spirited, bold, courageous; full of animosity”), from animus (“mind, soul, spirit, life, courage; feeling, passion, wrath”).
