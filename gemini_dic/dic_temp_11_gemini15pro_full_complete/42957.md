coverers:/ˈkʌvərərz/
noun
覆盖者；遮盖物；报道者
People or things that cover something.  Those who report on an event or topic.
The coverers quickly spread the tarpaulin over the damaged roof.  覆盖者们迅速地把防水布铺在受损的屋顶上。
Reporters and photographers were the main coverers of the political summit.  记者和摄影师是这次政治峰会的主要报道者。
Synonyms: protectors, shielders, reporters, journalists
Antonyms: exposers, revealers
