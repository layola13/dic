nietzschean:/ˈniːtʃiən/ | adj. n. | 尼采式的；尼采的；尼采哲学的；尼采的信徒 | Of or relating to the German philosopher Friedrich Nietzsche, his philosophy, or his followers.  Characterized by a rejection of traditional morality and an emphasis on the will to power. |  His novels are full of nietzschean ideas about the will to power. 他的小说充满了关于权力意志的尼采式思想。 |  Nietzsche's philosophy is often described as nietzschean. 尼采的哲学常被描述为尼采式的。 | Synonyms: nihilistic, existentialist, iconoclastic | Antonyms: conventional, traditional, orthodox | adjective, noun
