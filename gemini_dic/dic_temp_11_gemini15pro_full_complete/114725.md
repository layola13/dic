mallow:/ˈmæloʊ/ (US) /ˈmæloʊ/ (UK)| n.|锦葵；蜀葵；药蜀葵|A herbaceous plant with hairy stems, lobed leaves, and showy flowers, typically pink or purple.  Several kinds are grown as ornamentals, and some are edible.|The marsh mallow grows wild in damp areas.  药蜀葵生长在潮湿的地区。|近义词：hollyhock (especially for ornamental kinds), hibiscus|反义词: N/A|noun

mallow:/ˈmæloʊ/ (US) /ˈmæloʊ/ (UK)| adj.|锦葵色的；淡紫色的|Of a pale purplish colour.|She wore a mallow dress to the party. 她穿着一件淡紫色的连衣裙去参加派对。|近义词：lavender, lilac, mauve|反义词: N/A|adjective
