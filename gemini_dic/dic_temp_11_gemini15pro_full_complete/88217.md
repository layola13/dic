hinderer:/ˈhɪndərər/| n. |阻碍者，妨碍者|Someone or something that hinders or obstructs progress or movement.|The heavy traffic was a hinderer to our journey.  交通拥堵阻碍了我们的旅程。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, obstructionist, barrier|反义词：facilitator, helper, aid, assistant|词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb "hinder" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Hinder" comes from the Old English "hindrian," meaning "to keep back, restrain."
