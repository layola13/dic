cylindrometric:/ˌsɪlɪndrəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
圆柱测量的，圆柱测定法的
Relating to or used in cylindrometry.  Pertaining to the measurement of the curved surface of a cylinder.
Cylindrometric measurements are crucial for accurate engine performance analysis. 圆柱测量对于精确的发动机性能分析至关重要。
The cylindrometric device provided precise readings.  圆柱测量装置提供了精确的读数。
Near Synonyms: cylindrical, mensurative
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
