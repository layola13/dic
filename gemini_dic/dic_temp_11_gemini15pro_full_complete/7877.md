antecommunion:/ˌæntiːkəˈmjuːnjən/
n.
圣餐前
The part of the Eucharist prior to reception of the elements.
He attended the antecommunion service. 他参加了圣餐前的仪式。

近义词:pre-communion service
反义词:postcommunion
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antecommunio, from ante- + communio (“communion”).
