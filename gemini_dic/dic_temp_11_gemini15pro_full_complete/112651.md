lowbrowism:/ˈloʊbraʊɪzəm/
n.
低俗，庸俗；迎合低级趣味
Unsophisticated or crude cultural tastes, values, or behavior.
Lowbrowism is becoming increasingly prevalent in television programming.  电视节目越来越低俗。
The critic condemned the film for its blatant lowbrowism.  评论家谴责这部电影公然迎合低级趣味。
近义词: vulgarity, tackiness, philistinism
反义词: highbrowism, sophistication, refinement
词性: noun

lowbrow:/ˈloʊbraʊ/
adj.
低俗的，庸俗的
Not highly intellectual or cultured.
The movie was criticized for its lowbrow humor.  这部电影因其低俗的幽默而受到批评。
She considered reality TV to be lowbrow entertainment. 她认为真人秀电视节目是低俗的娱乐。
近义词: vulgar, tacky, unsophisticated
反义词: highbrow, sophisticated, refined
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "lowbrow" (adj.), coined in the late 19th century in reference to the perceived shape of the skull of less intelligent people (a pseudoscientific theory). The "-ism" suffix transforms the adjective into a noun denoting a doctrine, belief, or practice.
