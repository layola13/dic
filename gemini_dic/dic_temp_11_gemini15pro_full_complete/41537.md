coronerships:/ˈkɔːrənərʃɪps/
n.
验尸官职位；验尸官任期
the office or term of office of a coroner
The coronership had been vacant for several months.  验尸官职位空缺了好几个月。
The election for the coronership was fiercely contested. 验尸官职位的选举竞争激烈。
近义词: coroner's office
反义词:  (No direct antonym.  One might consider related antonyms like "life" or "survival" in contrast to the coroner's dealing with death, but these are not direct antonyms for the office itself.)
