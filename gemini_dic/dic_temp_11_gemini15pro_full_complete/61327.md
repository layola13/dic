embassies: /ˈembəsiːz/
n.
大使馆（复数）
the official residences or offices of ambassadors in foreign countries.
The embassies of several countries are located in the capital city.  几个国家的大使馆都位于首都。
Many embassies issued travel warnings to their citizens. 许多大使馆向其公民发布了旅行警告。
近义词:consulates, legations
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑用host countries 主办国)
词性: noun
