divestible:/daɪˈvɛstɪbl/ | adj. | 可剥夺的，可出售的 | Capable of being divested; that can be sold off or otherwise disposed of. | The company is selling off its non-core, divestible assets.  这家公司正在出售其非核心、可剥离的资产。|  The government is considering making some of its land holdings divestible. 政府正在考虑将其部分土地持有量出售。 | synonyms: disposable, saleable, alienable | antonyms:  inalienable, non-transferable | adjective
