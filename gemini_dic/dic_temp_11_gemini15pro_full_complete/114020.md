magnanimity:/ˌmæɡnəˈnɪməti/
n.|宽宏大量；慷慨|The quality of being generous and forgiving, especially toward a rival or someone less powerful than oneself.|He showed great magnanimity in forgiving his enemies. 他以宽宏大量的气度原谅了他的敌人。|
近义词：generosity, forbearance, benevolence, charity|
反义词：pettiness, meanness, vindictiveness, spite|
词性: noun

magnanimous:/mæɡˈnænɪməs/
adj.|宽宏大量的；慷慨的|Having or showing a generous and forgiving spirit, especially toward a rival or someone less powerful than oneself.|It was a magnanimous gesture on her part to offer help to her competitor. 她主动向竞争对手提供帮助，这是一种宽宏大量的姿态。|
近义词：generous, forgiving, benevolent, charitable|
反义词：petty, mean, vindictive, spiteful|
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *magnanimitas*, from Latin magnanimus (“great-souled, noble, generous”) (from magnus (“great”) + animus (“mind, soul”)).
