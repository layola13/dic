mothernudger:/ˈmʌðərˌnʌdʒər/| n. |母亲般的唠叨者；爱管闲事的人|Someone, especially a mother, who persistently reminds or encourages someone to do something.  A person who habitually prods or nags, especially a mother.  Derived from "mother" and "nudger".|例句：My aunt is such a mothernudger, always reminding me to eat my vegetables. 我的阿姨真是个爱唠叨的人，总是提醒我要吃蔬菜。

例句：He hates being treated like a child by his mothernudger of a sister. 他讨厌被他那爱管闲事的姐姐像孩子一样对待。|近义词：nag, pest, busybody, meddler|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) easygoing person, laid-back person|词性: noun
