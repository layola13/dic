lepidomelane:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒməleɪn/ | n. |黑云母|A black mica containing iron and magnesium, common in igneous and metamorphic rocks. | Lepidolite alters to lepidomelane.  锂云母蚀变为黑云母。

Lepidomelane is a common mineral in granites. 黑云母是花岗岩中的常见矿物。| 近义词:biotite, annite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *λεπίς* (lepis, “scale, flake”) + *μέλας* (melas, “black”).  由希腊语 *λεπίς* (lepis, “鳞片”) + *μέλας* (melas, “黑色”) 组成。
