franchisal: /frænˈtʃaɪzəl/
n.
特许经营权；特许经营的商品或服务
A license granted by a franchisor to a franchisee, allowing the franchisee to operate a business using the franchisor's brand and business model.  It can also refer to the business itself or the goods or services offered.
McDonald's is a popular franchisal. 麦当劳是一个受欢迎的特许经营品牌。
The franchisal offers a wide variety of products and services.  这个特许经营店提供各种各样的产品和服务。

近义词: franchise, concession
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "independent business" or "proprietorship")
