barbered:/ˈbɑːrbərd/ |adj.|理过发的，修剪过的|Having had one's hair cut and styled by a barber.|He looked very smart and barbered. 他看起来很精神，像是刚理过发。|近义词:groomed, trimmed, neat|反义词:unbarbered, shaggy, unkempt|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "barber" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Barber" comes from Old French "barbier," from Late Latin "barbatus," meaning "bearded," from Latin "barba," meaning "beard."
