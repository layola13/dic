atolia: /əˈtoʊliə/ | n. | 阿托利亚（古希腊的一个地区）| Atolia was a region of ancient Greece, located in the central part of the country. It was bordered by Epirus to the north, Thessaly to the northeast, Boeotia and Locris to the east, Phocis and Doris to the southeast, and the Ionian Sea to the west. |  Alexander the Great conquered Atolia. 亚历山大大帝征服了阿托利亚。| synonym: Aetolia (alternative spelling) | antonym: N/A | noun
