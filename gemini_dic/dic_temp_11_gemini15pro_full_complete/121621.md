mispackaging:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒɪŋ/
n.
错误包装，包装不当
The act or process of packaging something incorrectly, or the state of being packaged incorrectly.
The mispackaging of the product led to customer complaints.  产品包装错误导致顾客投诉。

近义词: incorrect packaging, faulty packaging, improper packaging
反义词: correct packaging, proper packaging
词性: noun


mispackaged:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒd/
adj.
包装错误的
Incorrectly packaged.
The mispackaged goods had to be returned to the supplier. 包装错误的货物必须退回给供应商。

近义词: incorrectly packaged, wrongly packaged
反义词: correctly packaged, properly packaged
词性: adjective


词源:  mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + packaging (from package + -ing)
