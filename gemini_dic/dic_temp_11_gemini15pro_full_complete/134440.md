nonirrigating:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非灌溉的
Not irrigated; not requiring or receiving irrigation.
The nonirrigating farmland relies on rainfall for moisture. 这片非灌溉农田依靠降雨获得水分。
Near synonyms: dryland, unirrigated, rainfed
Antonyms: irrigated, watered
