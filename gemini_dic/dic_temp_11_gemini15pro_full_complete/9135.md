antimystics:/ˌæntiˈmɪstɪks/
| n. | 反神秘主义者 | People who oppose mysticism or are skeptical of its claims. | The antimystics argued that spiritual experiences could be explained by psychological phenomena. 反神秘主义者认为，精神体验可以用心理学现象来解释。 |  | skeptics, rationalists, empiricists | mystics, spiritualists | noun
antimystical:/ˌæntiˈmɪstɪkəl/
| adj. | 反神秘主义的 | Opposed to or skeptical of mysticism. | He took an antimystical approach to religious experience. 他对宗教体验采取了一种反神秘主义的态度。|  | skeptical, rational, empirical | mystical, spiritual | adjective
