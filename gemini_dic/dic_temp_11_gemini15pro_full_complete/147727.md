overprinting:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 套印，叠印 | The process of printing one color on top of another, often used to create specific color effects or to add information such as a date or price. | The overprinting of the red ink on the blue created a vibrant purple. 红色墨水套印在蓝色上，形成了鲜艳的紫色。 | superimposition, surprinting | underprinting | noun
