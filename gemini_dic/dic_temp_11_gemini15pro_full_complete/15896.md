balloted:/ˈbælətɪd/| v.|投票表决（ballot的过去式和过去分词）|The union members balloted for strike action. 工会成员投票表决是否采取罢工行动。|近义词：voted, polled|反义词：abstained|词性:动词
ballot:/ˈbælət/| n. v.|投票；投票表决；投票总数|They cast their ballots in secret. 他们秘密地投了票。|近义词：vote, poll|反义词：abstention|词性:名词/动词

词源: From Old French *ballotte (diminutive of balle "ball"), from Italian ballotta, diminutive of balla "ball." Originally referred to a small ball used in secret voting.
