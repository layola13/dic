genii:/ˈdʒiːniaɪ/ | n. |精灵，守护神；天才，天赋|Plural of genius.  A supernatural creature, often depicted as a spirit residing in a lamp or bottle, that can grant wishes. Also, a person of exceptional intellectual or creative power.| Example: The genii emerged from the lamp in a puff of smoke. 精灵从灯里冒出一股烟雾。| Example: Mozart was considered one of the musical genii of his time. 莫扎特被认为是他那个时代音乐天才之一。| 近义词: spirits, djinn, geniuses| 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "ordinary person", "mediocrity") |词性: noun


genie:/ˈdʒiːni/ | n. |精灵，守护神|A supernatural creature, often depicted as a spirit residing in a lamp or bottle, that can grant wishes.|Example: Rub the lamp and a genie will appear. 摩擦神灯，精灵就会出现。|Example:  The genie granted him three wishes. 精灵满足了他三个愿望。|近义词: djinn, spirit| 反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
