inestimableness:/ɪnˈɛstɪməblənəs/| n.|无价, 不可估量|The inestimableness of a mother's love. 母爱的无价。|近义词：pricelessness, invaluable, immeasurable|反义词:worthlessness, valueless|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From inestimable + -ness.  Inestimable from in- (not) + estimable (capable of being estimated).  Estimable from Old French estimable, from Latin aestimabilis, from aestimare (to estimate).
