gluttonous:/ˈɡlʌtənəs/ | adj. | 贪吃的；贪婪的；暴食的 | Excessively greedy; given to overindulgence in food or drink. | He has a gluttonous appetite and can eat an entire pizza by himself. 他胃口很大，可以一个人吃掉一整个披萨。|  She is gluttonous for gossip. 她对八卦如饥似渴。 | synonyms: greedy, voracious, ravenous, insatiable | antonyms: temperate, moderate, abstemious | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gluttonosus, from Latin gluttio (“I gulp down, swallow, devour”), from glutto (“glutton”), from glutire (“to swallow”).
