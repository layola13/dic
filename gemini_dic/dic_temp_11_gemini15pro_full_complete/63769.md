epicoracoid: /ˌɛpɪˈkɔːrəkɔɪd/
n.
喙突上骨；上喙骨
A small bone or cartilage attached to the coracoid in the pectoral girdle of many amphibians, reptiles, birds, and monotremes.  It is located above the coracoid.
The frog's pectoral girdle includes an epicoracoid cartilage.  这只青蛙的肩带包含一块喙突上软骨。
Synonyms: supracoracoid
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical feature)
