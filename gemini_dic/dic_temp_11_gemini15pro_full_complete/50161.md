denyingly:/dɪˈnaɪɪŋli/
| adv. | 否认地，否定地 | in a way that shows you are denying something;  in a manner that suggests denial or refusal. | He shook his head denyingly. 他否认地摇了摇头。 |Synonyms: negatively, dismissively, contradictorily | Antonyms: affirmatively, approvingly, positively
|词性: 副词

denying:/dɪˈnaɪɪŋ/
| adj. | 否认的，否定的 | refusing to admit or accept something |  His denying look made her doubt herself. 他否认的表情让她开始怀疑自己。 | Synonyms: negative, dismissive, contradictory | Antonyms: affirmative, approving, positive
|词性: 形容词

deny:/dɪˈnaɪ/
| vt. | 否认，拒绝 | state that something is not true; refuse to admit or accept something | He denied stealing the money. 他否认偷了钱。 | Synonyms: contradict, refute, disavow | Antonyms: admit, confess, acknowledge
|词性: 动词
