what:/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) |pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么，|Used to ask for information about something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which|反义词: (无直接反义词)|pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚晚餐做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done with all the money? = 他把所有的钱都拿去做什么了？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading all afternoon? = 她整个下午都在看什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was he thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间他在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed prior to the announcement? = 宣布之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你打算做什么？
What will the company do to improve sales? = 公司将采取什么措施来提高销售额？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by the time the deadline arrives? = 截止日期到来之前，他们将建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? = 到2033年，你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she gets her PhD? = 到她获得博士学位时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
