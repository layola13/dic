fair-mindedly:/ˌfeərˈmaɪndɪdli/ | adv. | 公正地，公平地 | In a way that is fair and unbiased; without prejudice. | He listened fair-mindedly to both sides of the argument before making a decision. 他在做决定之前公平地倾听了争论的双方。|  impartially, objectively, justly, equitably |  biasedly, unfairly, unjustly, prejudicially | adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From fair-minded + -ly. Fair (from Old English fæger "beautiful, pleasant, fitting") + minded (from mind + -ed).
