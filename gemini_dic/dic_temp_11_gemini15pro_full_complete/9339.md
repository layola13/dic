antipredator:/ˈæntiˈprɛdətər/ | adj. n.| 反捕食的，反捕食者；反捕食性适应| Relating to or denoting adaptations or strategies used by prey animals to avoid being captured and eaten by predators.  |  The bright coloration of the poison dart frog is an antipredator adaptation.  毒镖蛙鲜艳的颜色是一种反捕食的适应。 |  The gazelle's speed is a key antipredator defense.  瞪羚的速度是其主要的防御捕食者的策略。 |  defensive, protective | prey | adjective, noun
