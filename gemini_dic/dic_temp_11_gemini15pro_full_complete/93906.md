immortification:/ˌɪˌmɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
克制；禁欲；抑制；苦行
The act of subduing the passions and appetites, especially by abstinence or self-inflicted pain or discomfort.
He practiced immortification by fasting and sleeping on a hard floor. 他通过禁食和睡硬板床来实行克己。
self-denial, abstinence, asceticism, mortification
indulgence, gratification, hedonism
noun
