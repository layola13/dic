conclusively:/kənˈkluːsɪvli/
adverb
最终地；决定性地
Putting an end to doubt or question; decisively.
The study conclusively demonstrated the link between smoking and lung cancer.  这项研究最终证明了吸烟和肺癌之间的联系。
The evidence conclusively proves his guilt.  证据确凿地证明了他的罪行。
近义词: definitively, decisively, finally, irrefutably, categorically
反义词: inconclusively, vaguely, ambiguously
词性: adverb
词源: From Late Latin *conclusivus, from Latin concludere (“to conclude”) + -ivus.
