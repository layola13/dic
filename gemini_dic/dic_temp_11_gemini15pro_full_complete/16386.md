barbing:/ˈbɑːrbɪŋ/ | n. |倒钩，倒刺；(尤指在铁丝网上的)刺铁丝；(鱼钩上的)倒钩 |A sharp projection pointing away from the main point, as on a fishhook or arrowhead, designed to prevent easy extraction.|The fishhook has a barbing to prevent the fish from escaping. 鱼钩上有倒钩，防止鱼逃脱。|近义词：barb, point, spike|反义词: smoothness|词性:名词

barbing:/ˈbɑːrbɪŋ/ | adj. |带倒钩的；有倒刺的 |Having barbs or sharp points.|Barbing wire is used to secure the perimeter. 带刺的铁丝网用来保护周边安全。|近义词：barbed, pointed, spiked|反义词: smooth|词性:形容词
