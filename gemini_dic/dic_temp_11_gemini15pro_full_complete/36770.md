collusively:/kəˈluːsɪvli/| adv.|串通地，勾结地|In a manner involving secret or unlawful cooperation, especially to cheat or deceive others.|The two companies collusively fixed prices to eliminate competition. 这两家公司串通操纵价格以消除竞争。|Synonyms: conspiratorially, secretly, underhandedly, fraudulently|Antonyms: openly, honestly, transparently|词性: 副词

collusion:/kəˈluːʒən/| n.|串通，勾结|Secret or unlawful cooperation, especially to cheat or deceive others.|The investigation uncovered evidence of collusion between the company and government officials. 调查发现了该公司与政府官员勾结的证据。|Synonyms: conspiracy, connivance, complicity|Antonyms: honesty, integrity, transparency|词性: 名词

collusive:/kəˈluːsɪv/| adj.|串通的，勾结的|Involving secret or unlawful cooperation, especially to cheat or deceive others.|The collusive agreement between the two companies was designed to keep prices artificially high. 这两家公司之间的串通协议旨在人为地抬高价格。|Synonyms: conspiratorial, clandestine, fraudulent|Antonyms: open, honest, aboveboard|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *colludere* "to play together," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *ludere* "to play" (see ludicrous). The notion is of "playing the same game" as someone else, hence "conspiring, intriguing."
