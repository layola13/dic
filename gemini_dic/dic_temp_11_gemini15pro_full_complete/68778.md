farsighted:/ˈfɑːrˈsaɪtɪd/ /ˈfɑːrˌsaɪtəd/| adj.|有远见的；远视的|Able to see things clearly that are far away but not things that are close; wise and able to understand what will happen in the future.|例句：She's farsighted and always plans for the future. 她很有远见，总是为未来做计划。

例句：He wears glasses because he is farsighted. 他戴眼镜是因为他远视。|近义词：prescient, visionary, long-sighted|反义词：shortsighted, myopic, nearsighted|词性:adjective
