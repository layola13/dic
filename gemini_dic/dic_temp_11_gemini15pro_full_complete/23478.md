bragless:/ˈbræɡləs/| adj.|不吹嘘的，谦虚的|Not given to bragging; modest.  Free from boasting or bragging. Characterized by a lack of bragging. |She was bragless about her accomplishments, preferring to let her work speak for itself. 她对自己的成就毫不夸耀，更喜欢让自己的工作来说话。|例句：He's a bragless hero, quietly doing good deeds without seeking recognition. 他是一位不爱吹嘘的英雄，默默地做好事，不求认可。|近义词：modest, humble, unassuming, unpretentious|反义词：boastful, bragging, arrogant, conceited|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From brag + -less.  "Brag" comes from Middle English *braggen*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to a Scandinavian word meaning "to move briskly" or "to swagger."  The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
