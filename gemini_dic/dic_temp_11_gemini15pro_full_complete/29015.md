cartographers:/kɑːrˈtɑːɡrəfərz/ | n. | 制图师，制图员 | A person who makes maps. |  Early cartographers often relied on celestial navigation to determine location. 早期的制图师通常依靠天文导航来确定位置。|  mapmakers, chart-makers | opposites not typically used for professions, could consider "misinformer" in a negative context. | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French cartographe, from carto- + -graphe (as in -grapher).  Ultimately from Greek khartēs "papyrus sheet, leaf of paper" and graphein "to write".
