bedrop:/bɪˈdrɒp/ /bɪˈdrɑːp/
verb
点缀；用…点缀
To cover with drops of something; to sprinkle or adorn with drops.
The grass was bedropped with dew. 草地上点缀着露珠。
The artist bedropped the canvas with paint. 艺术家在画布上点缀着颜料。
sprinkle, dot, speckle, bespatter

v.
