fusionist:/ˈfjuːʒənɪst/| n.|融合主义者；混合主义者|A person who advocates or practices fusion, especially in politics, religion, or the arts.|The fusionist movement aimed to combine elements of different artistic styles.|近义词：synthesizer, integrator, combiner|反义词：purist, separatist|词性:noun

Etymology:
From fusion + -ist.  Fusion comes from Late Latin *fūsiōn- (stem of fūsiō ), from Latin fūsus, past participle of fundere "to melt, pour, cast."
