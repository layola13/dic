lithophyse:/ˈlɪθəˌfaɪz/| n. |石泡; 石花; |A hollow, often concentrically layered, nodular or irregular mass of secondary minerals, such as agate, calcite, or chalcedony, occurring in volcanic or other igneous rocks.|The geologist discovered a beautiful lithophyse filled with amethyst crystals. 地质学家发现了一个充满紫水晶晶体的美丽的石泡。|近义词:amygdule, geode|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps solid rock or dense rock)|noun
