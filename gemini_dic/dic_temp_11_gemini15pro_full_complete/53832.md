disconsent:/ˌdɪskənˈsent/
n.|不赞成，异议|Lack of agreement or harmony; dissent.|例句：There was widespread discontent among the workers. 工人们普遍不满。|近义词：disagreement, dissent, discord, opposition|反义词：agreement, consent, concord, harmony|词性:名词
disconsent:/ˌdɪskənˈsent/
v.|不赞成，不同意|To withhold assent or approval; disagree.|例句：He disconsented to the proposed changes. 他不同意提议的修改。|近义词：oppose, object, disagree, dissent|反义词：agree, consent, approve, concur|词性:动词
