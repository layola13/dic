half-ingenious:/ˌhæf ɪnˈdʒiːniəs/
adjective
半巧妙的，略微独创的
Partially or somewhat ingenious; clever or inventive to a limited degree.  Not fully or completely ingenious.
His solution to the problem was half-ingenious, relying on a trick that wouldn't always work. 他解决这个问题的方法有点巧妙，依靠一个并不总是奏效的技巧。
It was a half-ingenious plan, but ultimately flawed. 这计划略微有点巧妙，但最终还是有缺陷的。
near synonyms: semi-ingenious, partially clever, somewhat inventive
antonyms: uninspired, unimaginative, conventional
词性: 形容词
