ourology:/jʊˈrɒlədʒi/ /ʊˈrɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 泌尿学 | The branch of medicine and surgery concerned with the urinary tract in both sexes and the genital tract in the male. | He specializes in urology. 他专攻泌尿学。|  Urology plays a crucial role in men's health. 泌尿学在男性健康中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: None (as it's a specific medical field) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *urologie*, from *uro-* (from Greek *οὖρον* (ouron) "urine") + *-logie* (from Greek *-λογία* (-logia) "study of").
