competer:/kəmˈpiːtər/ (BrE) /kəmˈpiːtər/ (AmE)| v. |竞争，对抗|To strive to gain or win something by defeating or establishing superiority over others who are trying to do the same.|例句：Several companies are competing for the contract.  几家公司正在竞争这份合同。|近义词：contend, rival, vie|反义词：cooperate, yield|词性:动词
competer:/kəmˈpiːtər/ (BrE) /kəmˈpiːtər/ (AmE)| n. |竞争者|One who competes, especially in a commercial market.|例句：New competitors are entering the market. 新的竞争者正在进入市场。|近义词：rival, contender, competitor|反义词：collaborator, partner|词性:名词

词源: From Latin competere "to coincide, agree; be suitable; strive after," from com- "together" + petere "to seek, go after, aim at, attack."
