dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| n.|销钉，木钉，暗榫|A short wooden or metal rod used for fastening things together, typically by fitting into a corresponding hole in each piece.|例句：He used dowels to reinforce the joints of the chair. 他用销钉加固了椅子的接合处。

例句：The shelves are held together with dowels and glue.  这些架子是用销钉和胶水粘合在一起的。|近义词：peg, pin, rod|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "adhesive" if considering the function)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Low German *dōvel, diminutive of *dōve "peg, dowel," related to Middle Dutch dovel.
