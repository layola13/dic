nidulite: /ˈnɪdjʊˌlaɪt/ | n. | 妮珠岩 | A rare phosphate mineral, Ca₂(UO₂)(PO₄)₂·10H₂O, occurring as yellow to greenish-yellow tabular crystals. | Nidulite is a secondary mineral formed by the alteration of uraninite.  妮珠岩是一种稀有的磷酸盐矿物，由晶质铀矿蚀变形成的次生矿物。| Synonym: none (too specific) | Antonym: none (not applicable) | noun
