joker:/ˈdʒoʊkər/| n.|小丑，玩笑者，鬼牌|A playing card, usually depicting a jester or clown, that can have any value in some card games.  A person who tells jokes or plays pranks. A tricky or unpredictable factor or element.|He’s a real joker – always cracking jokes. 他真是个爱开玩笑的人 – 总是讲笑话。|Synonyms: clown, comedian, prankster, trickster, wild card|Antonyms: serious person, bore|noun

joker:/ˈdʒoʊkər/| n.|小丑，玩笑者，鬼牌|An unpredictable or disruptive element in a situation. A person who acts foolishly or irresponsibly.|The new tax law is a real joker in the pack – nobody knows how it will affect businesses.  新的税法真是个变数 – 没人知道它会如何影响企业。|Synonyms: wild card, unknown quantity, unpredictable factor|Antonyms: predictable factor, known quantity|noun
