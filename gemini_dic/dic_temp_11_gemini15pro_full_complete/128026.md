neptunist:/ˈnɛptjuːnɪst/| n.|海王星论者；水成论者|A proponent of Neptunism, a now-discredited scientific theory that held that all rocks were formed from water.|The neptunist argued that granite could not be of volcanic origin.|近义词:Wernerian (referring to Abraham Gottlob Werner, a prominent Neptunist)|反义词:plutonist, vulcanist|词性:noun
