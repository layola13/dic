dinging:/ˈdɪŋɪŋ/ | v. | 持续响铃；不断唠叨；反复灌输 |  making a ringing sound repeatedly; persistently nagging or reminding someone; repeatedly instilling an idea | The doorbell was dinging incessantly. 门铃响个不停。| 近义词：ringing, chiming, nagging, harping, repeating | 反义词: silent, quiet | 词性: 动词

dinging:/ˈdɪŋɪŋ/ | n. | 叮当声；唠叨 | the sound of a bell ringing repeatedly; persistent nagging | The constant dinging of the alarm clock woke me up. 闹钟不断叮当响把我吵醒了。| 近义词：ringing, chime, nagging, harping | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "ding," which is imitative of the sound of a bell.
