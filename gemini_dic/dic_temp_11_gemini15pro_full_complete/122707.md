moha:/ˈmoʊhɑː/| n.| 妄想，迷妄；愚蠢，愚昧 |A delusion or infatuation; foolishness or stupidity.| He was filled with moha, believing he could fly. 他充满了妄想，认为自己能飞。|Synonyms: delusion, infatuation, folly, stupidity| Antonyms: wisdom, clarity, sense, rationality|noun

moha:/ˈmoʊhɑː/| n.| (佛教) 迷惑，执迷 |(Buddhism) Delusion, attachment| Moha is one of the three poisons in Buddhism.  在佛教中，迷惑是三毒之一。|Synonyms: delusion, attachment, craving| Antonyms: enlightenment, detachment, wisdom|noun
