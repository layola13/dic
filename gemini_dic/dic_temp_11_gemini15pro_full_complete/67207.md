expressionlessnesses:/ɪkˈspreʃənləsnɪsɪz/
|n.|毫无表情；缺乏表情|The expressionlessnesses of the masked figures were unsettling.  戴着面具的人毫无表情，令人不安。|The multiple expressionlessnesses of the statues created a strange atmosphere in the museum.  博物馆里众多雕像毫无表情，营造出一种奇异的氛围。|近义词：blankness, impassivity, apathy|反义词：expressiveness, animation, vivacity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From expressionless + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality) + -es (plural suffix).  Expressionless itself comes from expression + -less (suffix meaning "without").  Ultimately from Latin expressus, past participle of exprimere "to press out, represent."
