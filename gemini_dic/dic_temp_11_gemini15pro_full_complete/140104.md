off-loaded:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdəd/ | v. | 卸载，卸货，卸下; 转移（负担，责任等）| to remove a load from something, especially a vehicle or ship; to transfer (a burden, responsibility, etc.) to someone else | The cargo was off-loaded at the dock. 货物在码头被卸下。| unload, discharge, transfer | load, burden | verb

off-loaded:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdəd/ | adj. | 已卸载的 | having had a load removed | The off-loaded truck drove away. 卸完货的卡车开走了。| unloaded, empty | loaded, full | adjective
