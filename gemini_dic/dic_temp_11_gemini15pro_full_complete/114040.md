magnet:/ˈmæɡnɪt/| n.|磁铁，磁石，有吸引力的人或物|A magnet is an object that produces a magnetic field. This magnetic field is invisible but is responsible for the most notable property of a magnet: a force that pulls on other ferromagnetic materials, such as iron, and attracts or repels other magnets.|The fridge door is held shut by a magnet.| 近义词: lodestone| 反义词: nonmagnet| noun
