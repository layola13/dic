adipoma:/ˌædɪˈpoʊmə/ /ˌædɪˈpəʊmə/ | n. |脂肪瘤|A benign tumor made up of fatty tissue.  A growth of fat cells in a thin, fibrous capsule, usually found just beneath the skin.| Doctors diagnosed the lump on her back as an adipoma. 医生诊断她背上的肿块是脂肪瘤。| The surgeon successfully removed the adipoma.  外科医生成功地切除了脂肪瘤。 | synonym: lipoma, fatty tumor | antonym: N/A | noun
