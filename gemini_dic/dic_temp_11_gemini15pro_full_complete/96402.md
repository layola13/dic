indispensable:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbl/ | adj. n. | 不可缺少的；必需品 |Something that is indispensable is so important or useful that it is impossible to manage without it. |  Water is indispensable to life. 水是生命中不可缺少的。| essential, crucial, vital, necessary | dispensable, unnecessary | adjective

indispensables:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəblz/ | n. | 必需品 |  Plural form of indispensable, referring to essential items or people. | Pack only the indispensables for your camping trip.  只打包露营旅行的必需品。| essentials, necessities, requisites | nonessentials, luxuries | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from late Latin indispensabilis, from in- ‘not’ + dispensabilis (see dispensable).
