hawaiians:/həˈwaɪjənz/
|n.|夏威夷人；夏威夷土著|People who are native to or reside in the Hawaiian Islands.  Descendants of the Polynesian voyagers who settled the islands.  Can also refer to the culture and traditions of these people.| Many Hawaiians are proud of their Polynesian heritage. 许多夏威夷人为他们的波利尼西亚血统感到自豪。|natives, islanders|mainlanders|noun
