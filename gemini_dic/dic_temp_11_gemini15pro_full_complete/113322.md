luxation:/lʌkˈseɪʃən/
noun
脱臼，错位
dislocation of a bone from its joint
The skier suffered a luxation of his shoulder.  这位滑雪者肩膀脱臼了。

近义词: dislocation, subluxation
反义词: reduction (指复位)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *luxationem, accusative of *luxatio (“dislocation”), from Latin luxare (“to dislocate”), from luxus (“dislocated, out of joint”).
