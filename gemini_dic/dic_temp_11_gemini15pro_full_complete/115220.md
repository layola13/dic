manifestoing:/ˈmænɪˌfɛstoʊɪŋ/
v.
展现，显现，表明；使...显现
The illness first manifested itself in severe headaches.  这种疾病最初表现为严重的头痛。
She manifested little enthusiasm for the proposal. 她对这项提议几乎没有表现出热情。
Synonyms: demonstrating, exhibiting, revealing, showing, displaying
Antonyms: concealing, hiding, suppressing
词性: 动词 (verb)
