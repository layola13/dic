misintending:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛndɪŋ/
|v.| 误解；曲解；怀有恶意 | To understand or interpret (something, such as a person's actions or words) wrongly, especially in a negative way; to have harmful intentions toward.| She misintended his silence as disapproval. 她误以为他的沉默是不赞成。 |  misinterpret, misconstrue, misjudge| intend correctly, understand correctly | verb
