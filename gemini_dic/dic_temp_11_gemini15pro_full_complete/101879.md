ischiocerite:/ˌɪskiˈɒsəraɪt/| n.|坐节基节；坐节基肢|The ischiocerite is the second segment of the antennal peduncle in decapod crustaceans.  它是十足目甲壳类动物触角柄的第二节。|The ischiocerite of this shrimp species is unusually long.  这种虾的坐节基肢异常长。|Synonyms: second antennal segment, basal antennal segment|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, potentially distal antennal segments could be considered somewhat opposite)|noun
