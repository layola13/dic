aeromagnetometer:/ˌɛroʊmæɡnɪˈtɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌɛərəʊmæɡnɪˈtɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |航磁仪，航空磁力计|An instrument used in aircraft to measure variations in the Earth's magnetic field.|例句：The aeromagnetometer detected a significant magnetic anomaly. 航磁仪探测到一个明显的磁异常。|近义词：magnetometer, magnetic gradiometer|反义词: N/A|noun

Etymology: aero- (Greek, "air") + magnetometer (magnet + -meter, "measuring instrument")
