otherwhile:/ˈʌðərˌ(h)waɪl/| adv.|偶尔，有时，间或|At otherwhile, he would walk for hours. 有时，他会散步几个小时。|例句：I see him otherwhile at the library. 我偶尔在图书馆看到他。|例句：Otherwhile she preferred to stay home and read. 有时候她更喜欢待在家里看书。|近义词：sometimes, occasionally, now and then|反义词:always, constantly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ōðerhwīl, from *ōðer "other" + *hwīl "while, time."  The sense developed from "at another time" to "at times."
