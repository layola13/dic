nubbin:/ˈnʌbɪn/| n.|小块，小结，小瘤，残株|a small lump or piece of something, especially one that is left after the main part has been removed or used up|The corn had several nubbins on it that were too small to eat. 玉米上有几个太小而不能吃的小玉米棒。|近义词：lump, knob, stub, stump|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "whole")|词性:noun

nubbin:/ˈnʌbɪn/| n.|（尤指人或动物的）小而圆的突出部分|a small rounded protuberance, especially on a person or animal|The frog had strange little nubbins on its back.  这只青蛙背上有奇怪的小疙瘩。|近义词：lump, knob, protuberance|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
