aphotic:/eɪˈfɒtɪk/ | adj. | 无光的，无光区的 | Relating to the zone of a body of water that receives no sunlight. |  The aphotic zone of the ocean is a mysterious and largely unexplored realm. 海洋的无光区是一个神秘且很大程度上未被探索的领域。

The aphotic depths of the cave were home to strange, bioluminescent creatures.  洞穴无光的深处是奇异的生物发光生物的家园。 | 近义词：dark, lightless, sunless | 反义词：photic, light, bright | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *ἀ- (a-, “without”) + *φῶς (phôs, “light”).
