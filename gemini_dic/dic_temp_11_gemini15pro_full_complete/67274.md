exquisitely:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪtli/ | adv. | 精致地；优美地；敏锐地 | In a delicate and refined manner; with great beauty and skill; acutely. | The cake was exquisitely decorated with delicate sugar flowers.  蛋糕用精致的糖花装饰得非常漂亮。|  She dresses exquisitely. 她穿着非常优雅。 |  Synonym: beautifully, delicately, elegantly, perfectly, refinedly | Antonym: crudely, roughly, coarsely | adverb

exquisite:/ˈɛkskwɪzɪt/ | adj. | 精致的；优美的；剧烈的 | Extremely beautiful and delicate; intensely felt. | The museum houses an exquisite collection of porcelain.  博物馆收藏了精美的瓷器。| The pain was exquisite. 疼痛剧烈。| Synonym: beautiful, delicate, elegant, fine, perfect | Antonym: crude, rough, coarse, ugly | adjective
