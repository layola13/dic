hypervoluminously:/ˌhaɪpərˌvɒljuːmɪnəsli/
adverb
极大量地，超级大量地
in an extremely voluminous way; to a very great extent or degree.
She wrote hypervoluminously, filling notebooks with her thoughts and observations. 她写作量极大，用她的想法和观察填满了笔记本。
The volcano erupted hypervoluminously, spewing ash and lava for miles. 火山猛烈喷发，喷出的火山灰和熔岩绵延数英里。
Synonyms: profusely, copiously, abundantly, excessively
Antonyms: sparsely, meagerly, sparingly
词性: 副词
