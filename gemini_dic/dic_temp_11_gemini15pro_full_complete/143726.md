outlive:/ˌaʊtˈlɪv/| v. | 比...活得长; 比...持久; 经受住;|To live longer than someone or something else; to survive or last longer than something.|例句：She outlived her husband by twenty years. 她比她丈夫多活了二十年。

例句：The old house has outlived several owners. 这栋老房子比好几任主人都活得久。|近义词：survive, outlast, last longer than|反义词：predecease|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ūtlifian (unattested), from *ūt (out) + *lifian (to live). Cognate with Dutch overleven (to survive), German überleben (to survive).
