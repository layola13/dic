void:/vɔɪd/| adj. n. vt.|空的，无效的；空虚；使无效|
(adj.) Completely empty; having no legal force or effect; (n.) an empty space; (vt.) to make invalid; to empty|
(adj.) The contract was void from the start.  合同从一开始就无效。
(n.)  She felt a void in her life after her children left home. 她孩子离家后，她感到生活中一片空虚。
(vt.) The court voided the contract. 法院宣布该合同无效。|近义词：(adj.) invalid, null, empty; (n.) emptiness, vacuum; (vt.) invalidate, annul, nullify|反义词：(adj.) valid, full; (n.) fullness|形容词, 名词, 动词
