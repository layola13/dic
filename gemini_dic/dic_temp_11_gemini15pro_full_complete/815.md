abstemiousness:/ˌæbstɪˈmiːəsnəs/| n.|节制；禁欲；克制|The state of being abstemious; moderation, especially in eating and drinking.  It implies a conscious and deliberate restraint from indulging in pleasures, particularly those related to food and drink.|His abstemiousness was remarkable considering the abundance of food at the feast. 考虑到宴会上丰富的食物，他的节制令人瞩目。|近义词：moderation, temperance, sobriety, restraint|反义词：gluttony, indulgence, excess|词性:名词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *abstemiosus* "abstaining from wine," from Latin *abstemius* "sober, temperate," from *ab- "away from" + *temetum* "intoxicating drink."
