anaesthetized:/əˈniːsθətaɪzd/ | adj. | 麻醉的，失去知觉的 | Deprived of feeling or sensation, especially so as to allow a medical operation.  From Greek roots *an-* (without) + *aisthesis* (sensation).| The patient was anaesthetized before the surgery. 病人在手术前被麻醉了。 | The anaesthetized dog lay motionless on the operating table.  被麻醉的狗一动不动地躺在手术台上。 | synonyms: numb, insensible, unconscious | antonyms: conscious, aware, sensitive | adjective
