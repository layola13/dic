euthanasia:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/ /ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒiə/
n.
安乐死
The painless killing of a patient suffering from an incurable and painful disease or in an irreversible coma. The practice is illegal in most countries.
例句：The debate about euthanasia continues to be a sensitive and complex one.  关于安乐死的争论仍然是一个敏感而复杂的问题。

例句：Some people believe euthanasia is a compassionate choice for those suffering unbearable pain.  有些人认为安乐死对于那些遭受难以忍受的痛苦的人来说是一个富有同情心的选择。

近义词：mercy killing, assisted suicide
反义词：dyathanasia (allowing death to occur naturally)
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek, eu- (good) + thanatos (death).  来自希腊语，eu-（好的）+ thanatos（死亡）。
