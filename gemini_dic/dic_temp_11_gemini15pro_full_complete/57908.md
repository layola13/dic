driftwood:/ˈdrɪftˌwʊd/| n. |浮木；流木|Wood that has been washed ashore or is floating in the sea or a river.|例句：The beach was littered with driftwood. 海滩上到处都是浮木。

例句：We gathered driftwood to build a fire. 我们收集了一些浮木来生火。|近义词：flotsam, wreckage|反义词: None|词性:noun
