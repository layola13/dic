adjective

The balefullest of omens, a black raven, circled overhead.
例句：With a balefullest glare, the villain revealed his wicked plan.  恶棍用最凶恶的一瞥，揭示了他的邪恶计划。

近义词：most ominous, most sinister, most menacing
反义词：auspicious, promising, fortunate
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From bale (evil, woe) + -ful (full of) + -est (superlative suffix).  The word "bale" comes from Old English *balu, meaning "evil, woe, mischief."
