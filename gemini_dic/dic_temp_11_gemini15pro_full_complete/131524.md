noncomicalities:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪkəlɪtiz/
noun
缺乏幽默; 严肃的事情; 不好笑的事情
The absence of humor or amusement; things that are not funny.
His speech was filled with noncomicalities about the current economic crisis. 他的演讲充满了关于当前经济危机的严肃内容，一点也不好笑。
Synonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gravity
Antonyms: humor, comedy, levity
