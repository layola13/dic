neutralizes:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
抵消；使中和；使无效
makes something ineffective or harmless by applying an opposite force or effect
Baking soda neutralizes stomach acid. 小苏打可以中和胃酸。
synonyms: counteracts, offsets, balances, cancels out
antonyms: activates, enhances, strengthens
词性: 动词
