in-leaf:/ˈɪnˌliːf/ | adj. | 叶内的，叶中的 |  Existing or occurring within a leaf.  |  Example: The in-leaf miners cause significant damage to the plant. 叶内潜叶虫对植物造成重大损害。 | Example: The in-leaf structure is complex and fascinating. 叶内结构复杂而迷人。 | 近义词: intrafolial | 反义词: extrafolial | 词性: adjective

词源: "in-" (prefix meaning "within, inside") + "leaf" (noun).
