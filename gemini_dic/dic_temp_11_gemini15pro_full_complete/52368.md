dictional:/ˈdɪkʃənəl/| adj.|字典的；词汇的；措辞的|Relating to diction, or choice of words, especially with regard to correctness, clearness, or effectiveness.|Her diction was impeccable, showcasing her vast vocabulary and command of the English language. 她的措辞无可挑剔，展现了她丰富的词汇量和对英语的掌握。|synonyms: lexical, verbal, vocabulary-related|antonyms: non-lexical, non-verbal|adjective
