laggy:/ˈlæɡi/| adj.|迟缓的，卡顿的|Responding slowly or with delays, often due to network issues or insufficient processing power.|The game was laggy because my internet connection was poor. 游戏很卡，因为我的网络连接很差。|My computer is laggy today. 我的电脑今天很卡顿。|近义词：slow, sluggish, choppy|反义词:fast, responsive, smooth|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "lag" + "-y". "Lag" originates from Old Norse "laggr" meaning "hanging behind".
