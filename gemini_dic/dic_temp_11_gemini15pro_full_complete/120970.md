mischief-making:/ˈmɪstʃɪfˌmeɪkɪŋ/
noun
恶作剧，捣乱，调皮捣蛋
The act of causing trouble or annoyance, often in a playful or mischievous way.
The children were punished for their mischief-making in the classroom. 孩子们因为在教室里捣乱而受到惩罚。
mischief, prankishness, troublemaking
obedience, helpfulness
