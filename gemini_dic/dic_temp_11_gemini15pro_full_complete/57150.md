dowagerism:/ˈdaʊədʒərɪzəm/
n.
守寡，寡妇身份；老年妇女的专横跋扈
The state or condition of being a dowager, especially an elderly woman of elevated social standing who exhibits arrogance or domineering behavior.
The queen mother's dowagerism made life difficult for the young king's wife.  太后专横跋扈，让年轻国王的妻子日子不好过。
Synonyms: imperiousness, haughtiness, arrogance, domineeringness
Antonyms: humility, modesty, meekness


ETYMOLOGY->
From dowager + -ism.  Dowager comes from Old French douagiere, from douage (dower), from Latin dotare (to endow).
