film-goer:/ˈfɪlmˌɡəʊər/ (UK), /ˈfɪlmˌɡoʊər/ (US)| n.|电影观众；常看电影的人|A person who regularly attends the cinema.|She's a regular film-goer and sees almost every new release. 她是一位经常去看电影的人，几乎每部新上映的电影都会去看。|Synonym: moviegoer, cinemagoer, movie fan, cinephile|Antonym: (No direct antonym, but one could consider someone who rarely or never goes to the cinema.)|noun
