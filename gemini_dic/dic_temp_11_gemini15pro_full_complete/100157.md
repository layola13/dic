interrater:/ˌɪntərˈreɪtər/| adj.| 评分者间的，评价者间的 |Relating to or involving the consistency of ratings or judgments made by different raters or observers.| The study examined interrater reliability between the three judges. 这项研究检验了三位评委之间的评分者信度。|近义词：interobserver, between-rater|反义词：intra-rater|词性:形容词
