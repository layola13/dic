changlong:/ˈtʃɑːŋlɒŋ/ (approximation based on Pinyin, no standard English pronunciation)| n.|长龙 (chánglóng)|A long line or queue of people or things, often used in Chinese context, literally meaning "long dragon".|例句：There was a changlong outside the popular restaurant.  热门餐馆外面排起了长龙。

例句：The changlong of cars stretched for miles on the highway. 高速公路上排起了长龙，绵延数英里。|近义词：queue, line, procession|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "short line" or "no line") |词性:Noun
