free-based:/ˈfriː beɪst/ | adj. | 游离碱的；(指可卡因)已从盐酸盐中提纯的 |  Freebasing refers to the process of converting a drug, typically cocaine, from its salt form (cocaine hydrochloride) to its freebase form. This is done by dissolving the drug in water, adding an alkali (like ammonia or baking soda), and then extracting the pure cocaine base with a nonpolar solvent. This process results in a smokable form of the drug with a higher concentration and a more intense high. |  The police found evidence of free-based cocaine in the apartment. 警方在公寓里发现了游离碱可卡因的证据。 |  The documentary showed the dangers of using free-based drugs. 这部纪录片展示了吸食游离碱毒品的危害。 | Near Synonyms: pure, processed (in this specific context), crack (often used interchangeably, though not technically the same) | Antonyms: hydrochloride (specifically referring to the salt form), unprocessed (in this context) | Adjective
