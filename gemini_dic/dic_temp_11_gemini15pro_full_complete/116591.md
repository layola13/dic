matriculants:/məˈtrɪkjʊlənts/
n.
新生；入学的学生； matriculants
People who have been admitted to a college or university and enrolled.
This year's matriculants come from all over the world. 今年的新生来自世界各地。
The university welcomed its new matriculants with a convocation ceremony.  大学以开学典礼欢迎新生入学。
synonyms: freshmen, entrants, incoming students
antonyms: graduates, alumni
noun
