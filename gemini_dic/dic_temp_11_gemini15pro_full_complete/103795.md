jousts:/dʒaʊsts/ | n. v. | 竞技; 争论，辩论 | A combat between two mounted knights or men-at-arms using lances. Also, any vigorous competition or contest. | The knights jousts for the queen's favor. 骑士们为了赢得女王的欢心而进行比武。 |  He jousts with his rival for the top position. 他与对手争夺最高职位。 | 近义词: tournaments, combats, contests, competitions, battles, clashes, disputes, arguments | 反义词: agreements, compromises, peace | 词性: 名词, 动词

joust:/dʒaʊst/ | v. | 竞技; 争论，辩论 | To engage in a joust; to compete vigorously or contend. | The knights joust in the arena. 骑士们在竞技场上比武。|  The two candidates joust over economic policy. 两位候选人就经济政策展开辩论。 | 近义词: compete, contend, vie, battle, clash, dispute, argue | 反义词: agree, compromise, yield | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "joster" (to joust, tilt, frolic), from Vulgar Latin *juxtare (to approach, join), from Latin "iuxta" (near, next to).
