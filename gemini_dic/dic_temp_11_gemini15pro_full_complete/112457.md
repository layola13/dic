loudmouthing:/ˌlaʊdˈmaʊθɪŋ/ | n. | 大声喧哗；夸夸其谈 | The act of speaking loudly and boastfully, often about oneself or one's accomplishments. | He's always loudmouthing about his achievements, which is annoying.  他总是吹嘘自己的成就，很烦人。| 近义词：boasting, bragging, showing off | 反义词：modesty, humility | 词性：名词

loudmouth:/ˈlaʊdˌmaʊθ/ | n. |  爱吹牛的人；大声说话的人 | A person who talks loudly and boastfully. |  Don't be such a loudmouth; no one likes a braggart.  别这么大声嚷嚷，没人喜欢吹牛大王。 | 近义词：braggart, boaster | 反义词：quiet person, introvert | 词性：名词

loudmouth:/ˌlaʊdˈmaʊθ/ | v. | 大声说；夸夸其谈 | To speak loudly and boastfully. | He loudmouthed about his new car to anyone who would listen.  他逢人便吹嘘他的新车。 | 近义词：boast, brag | 反义词：be modest, downplay | 词性：动词
