noncoagulation:/ˌnɒnkoʊˌæɡjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 不凝固，非凝固 |The noncoagulation of the blood sample caused difficulties in the analysis.  血液样本的非凝固给分析带来了困难。 |  non-clotting, incoagulability | coagulation | noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
coagulation (noun): from Late Latin "coagulatio," from "coagulare" meaning "to curdle," from "co-" (together) + "agere" (to drive, to do).
