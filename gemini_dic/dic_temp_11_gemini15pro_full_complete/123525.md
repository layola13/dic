monotonousness:/məˈnɑːtənəsnəs/
|n.|单调；千篇一律|The monotony of the desert landscape was oppressive. 沙漠景观的单调令人压抑。|
|例句：The monotony of his job was starting to wear him down. 他工作的单调乏味开始让他感到疲惫不堪。|
|例句：She longed to escape the monotonous routine of her daily life. 她渴望摆脱日常生活单调乏味的惯例。|
|近义词：tedium, dullness, repetitiveness, uniformity|
|反义词：variety, diversity, excitement, change|
|词性：名词|
|词源：From French *monotonie*, from Late Latin *monotonia*, from Greek *monotōnia*  "sameness of sound," from *monos* "single" + *tonos* "tone."|
