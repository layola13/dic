inanimate:/ɪˈnænɪmət/| adj.|无生命的，无生气的|Not alive, not having the characteristics of life; showing no signs of life; lacking energy or spirit.|例句：Rocks and minerals are inanimate objects.  岩石和矿物是无生命的物体。|例句：He lay inanimate on the floor. 他一动不动地躺在地板上。|近义词：lifeless, inert, dead|反义词：animate, living, alive|词性：形容词

词源：
From Late Middle English: from Latin *inanimatus, from in- ‘not’ + animatus ‘animated’.
