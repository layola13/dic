ocellary: /ˈɒsələri/ (US) /ˈɒsɪləri/ (UK) | adj. | 眼點的；單眼的 | Relating to or resembling an ocellus; having ocelli. |  The butterfly's wings had beautiful ocellary markings.  蝴蝶的翅膀上有漂亮的眼點圖案。 |  Synonyms: eye-spotted, ocellated | Antonyms: eyeless, blind | adjective

ocellary: /ˈɒsələri/ (US) /ˈɒsɪləri/ (UK) | n. | 單眼 | A simple eye or eyespot; an ocellus. (Less common usage) | The ocellary on the caterpillar helped it detect light. 毛毛蟲身上的單眼幫助它探測光線。 | Synonyms: ocellus, eyespot | Antonyms: compound eye | noun
