etiquettical:/ˌetɪˈketɪkəl/
adjective
礼仪的；礼节的
Relating to etiquette; prescribed by social conventions.
She made an etiquettical blunder by arriving late to the formal dinner. 她因迟到正式晚宴而犯了一个礼仪上的错误。
It's considered etiquettical to send a thank-you note after receiving a gift.  收到礼物后寄送感谢卡被认为是礼貌的。
近义词: polite, courteous, proper, decorous
反义词: impolite, rude, uncivil, improper
词性: adjective

词源:  From French *étiquette* (label, ticket) + -al.  Originally, etiquette referred to the cards or tickets given to visitors at court ceremonies outlining the proper forms of behavior.
