candid:/ˈkændɪd/| adj.|坦率的，直言不讳的；率直的；公正的；偷拍的|Characterized by openness and sincerity; free from reservation, disguise, or subterfuge; straightforward; impartial; (of a photograph) taken informally, typically without the subject's knowledge.|例句：He gave a candid interview about his experiences. 他坦率地讲述了自己的经历。

例句：The photographer captured a candid shot of the children playing. 摄影师抓拍到了孩子们玩耍的画面。


近义词：frank, honest, straightforward, open, forthright, unbiased, impartial, unposed|反义词：deceptive, evasive, secretive, biased, posed|词性:形容词
