dingdong:/ˈdɪŋdɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈdɪŋdɑːŋ/ (US)| n. int. adv.|叮咚（门铃声）；反复无常|A ringing sound, like that of a doorbell. Also, can describe something constantly changing or fluctuating.|例句：The doorbell went "dingdong". 门铃响了“叮咚”。

例句：The stock market has been dingdong all week. 这周股市一直在波动。|近义词：ring, chime, jingle, fluctuate, waver |反义词:stable, constant|noun, interjection, adverb
