addressor's:/əˈdrɛsər's/
noun
发言者的；致辞者的；发信人的
belonging to or associated with the addressor (the person who delivers a speech or writes a letter)
The addressor's tone was formal and respectful. 发言者的语气正式且恭敬。
The addressor's handwriting was difficult to decipher. 发信人的笔迹难以辨认。

antonyms: addressee's, recipient's
