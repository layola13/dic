deduction:/dɪˈdʌkʃən/
n.
扣除;推演;演绎;推理
the process of reaching a decision or answer by thinking about the known facts, or the decision that is reached
例句：
The detective's deduction that the killer knew the victim proved to be correct. 侦探推断凶手认识受害者，结果证明是正确的。

All we can do is make deductions from the available evidence. 我们所能做的就是从现有证据中进行推断。

近义词：inference, conclusion, reasoning
反义词：induction
词性：名词

deduction:/dɪˈdʌkʃən/
n.
扣除额;减免额
an amount that is or can be subtracted from a total
例句：
He claimed deductions for travel expenses on his tax return. 他在纳税申报单上要求扣除差旅费。

Tax deductions are available for charitable contributions.  慈善捐款可以免税。

近义词：subtraction, reduction
反义词：addition
词性：名词
