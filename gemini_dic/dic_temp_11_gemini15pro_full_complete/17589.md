beautification:/ˌbjuːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |美化；装饰；修饰|The beautification of the city included planting trees and flowers.  这个城市的美化工程包括种植树木和花卉。|例句：The beautification project transformed the neglected park into a vibrant community space.  美化项目将荒废的公园变成了一个充满活力的社区空间。

例句：The beautification of the town square is scheduled to begin next month. 城镇广场的美化工程计划下个月开始。|近义词：embellishment, adornment, enhancement, improvement|反义词：uglification, disfigurement|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: beautification, via Old French from late Latin *beautificatio(n-), from beautificare ‘make beautiful’ (see beautify).
