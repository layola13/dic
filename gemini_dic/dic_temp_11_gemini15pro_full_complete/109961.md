liberated:/ˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/
adjective
解放的，自由的
freed from traditional social constraints or outdated attitudes, especially relating to sex.
例句：
She felt liberated after quitting her stressful job. 她辞掉压力大的工作后感到解脱了。

The liberated woman enjoyed her newfound independence.  这位获得解放的妇女享受着她新获得的独立。

近义词：freed, emancipated, unrestrained, uninhibited
反义词：oppressed, restricted, confined, repressed
词性：adjective

liberate:/ˈlɪbəreɪt/
verb
解放，释放
set (someone) free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression.
例句：
The army liberated the prisoners of war. 军队解放了战俘。

They fought to liberate their country from foreign rule. 他们为把自己的国家从外国统治下解放出来而战斗。


近义词：free, release, emancipate, deliver
反义词： imprison, enslave, oppress, confine
词性：verb


词源：From Latin liberatus, past participle of liberare "to free," from liber "free."
