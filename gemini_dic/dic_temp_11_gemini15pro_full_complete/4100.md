aimer's: /ˈeɪmərz/
|n.| 爱好者们的；追求者们的 |The possessions of an aimer, typically used in the plural to refer to a group of people who love or admire someone or something.|  This website is dedicated to all the aimer's of classic literature.  这个网站是献给所有古典文学爱好者的。| 近义词: admirers, devotees, fans | 反义词: haters, detractors | 词性: 名词 (plural)


Etymology:
From French 'aimer' (to love) + '-er' (suffix forming agent nouns) + '-s' (plural marker).  The suffix '-er' is similar to the English '-er' in words like 'teacher' or 'baker'. The '-s' indicates the plural form.
