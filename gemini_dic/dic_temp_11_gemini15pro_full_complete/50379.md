deportees:/ˌdiːpɔːrˈtiːz/
|n.|被驱逐出境者；被遣返者
|People who have been deported from a country.|
|The deportees were sent back to their country of origin.|被驱逐出境者被遣送回原籍国。

|The government is planning to deport a group of illegal immigrants.  These deportees will be returned to their home countries.|政府正计划驱逐一批非法移民。这些被驱逐者将被遣返回他们的祖国。
|近义词: exiles, refugees|
|反义词:  immigrants, citizens|
|词性: noun (plural)|



ETYMOLOGY->
From French *déporté*, past participle of *déporter* "to deport," from Latin *deportare* "to carry away," from *de-* "away" + *portare* "to carry."
