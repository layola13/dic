exemplifier:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪər/ | n. | 例证者；示例者 |  Someone or something that serves as a good example.  | This painting is an exemplifier of the artist's early style. 这幅画是这位艺术家早期风格的例证。| synonym: example, illustration, instance | antonym: counter-example, exception | noun

exemplifier:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪər/ | n. |  例证；范例 |  Something that exemplifies a quality or characteristic. | The company's success is an exemplifier of the power of innovation.  这家公司的成功是创新力量的例证。 | synonym: paradigm, model, archetype | antonym: anomaly, aberration | noun
