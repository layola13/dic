cliffs:/klɪfs/ | n. |悬崖，峭壁 | Steep, high rocks, especially along a coast. | The cliffs overlooking the ocean were breathtaking.  悬崖俯瞰着大海，景色令人叹为观止。|  The hiker carefully navigated the treacherous cliffs.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地在险峻的悬崖上行走。 | synonyms: bluffs, precipices, crags, escarpments | antonyms: valleys, plains | noun

Etymology: From Old English *clif, from Proto-Germanic *kliban, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kleib- "to lean, incline."
