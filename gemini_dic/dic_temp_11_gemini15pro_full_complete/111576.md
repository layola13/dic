lobosa:/ləˈboʊsə/ | n. | 叶足纲，变形虫纲 | A large group of amoeboid protozoa, characterized by lobose pseudopods, which are blunt, fingerlike projections of the cytoplasm used for movement and engulfing food. | Lobosa are commonly found in freshwater and soil habitats. 叶足纲常见于淡水和土壤栖息地。| synonyms: Amoebida, Tubulinea | antonyms: N/A | noun
