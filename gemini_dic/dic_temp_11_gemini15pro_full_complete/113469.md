lyrate: /ˈlaɪreɪt/ (US), /ˈlʌɪərət/ (UK)| adj.| 琴形的，七弦琴状的|Shaped like a lyre; having a rounded shape that is broader at the base than at the top, like a lyre.|The lyrate leaves of the fig tree are distinctive. 无花果树琴形的叶子很独特。|近义词: lyre-shaped, panduriform|反义词: N/A|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin *līrātus, from līra (“lyre”) + -ātus (past participle suffix).
