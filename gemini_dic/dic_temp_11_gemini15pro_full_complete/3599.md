age-groups:/ˈeɪdʒ ɡruːps/| n.|年龄段，年龄组|A set of people of similar ages, often used for statistical analysis or marketing.  These groups are often categorized based on specific age ranges. |The marketing campaign targets several age-groups, from teenagers to senior citizens. 这个营销活动针对从青少年到老年人的几个年龄段。|synonyms: age brackets, demographics, cohorts|antonyms: N/A|noun
