ironworker:/ˈaɪərnˌwɜːrkər/ (US), /ˈaɪənˌwɜːkə/ (UK)| n.|钢铁工人|A person who erects or dismantles iron or steel structures, such as bridges and skyscrapers.|例句：The ironworker skillfully welded the two pieces of metal together.  钢铁工人熟练地将两块金属焊接在一起。|例句：An experienced ironworker can earn a good salary. 一位经验丰富的钢铁工人可以挣到不错的薪水。|近义词：steelworker, metalworker, construction worker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: iron + worker.  "iron" comes from Proto-Germanic *isarnan. "worker" comes from Proto-Germanic *wurkijaną.
