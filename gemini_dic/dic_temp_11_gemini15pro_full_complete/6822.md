anary:/ˈeɪnəri/ | n. |金鸟，金丝雀|A canary is a small, bright yellow or orange songbird, originally from the Canary Islands. It is often kept as a pet. |例句：The canary sang a beautiful song. 金丝雀唱了一首优美的歌。

例句：My grandmother used to keep a canary in a cage. 我的祖母过去常常在笼子里养一只金丝雀。|近义词：songbird, finch|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Canary Islands, where the bird is native.  Ultimately from Latin *canariae insulae* "islands of dogs", from *canis* "dog".  The name refers to the large dogs found on the islands, not the birds.
