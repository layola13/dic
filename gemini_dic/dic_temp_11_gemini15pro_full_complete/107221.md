laid-up:/leɪd ˈʌp/| adj.|被搁置的，卧床不起的，停止使用的|Confined to bed or inactivity because of illness or injury; (of a ship or vehicle) not in use.|例句：He's been laid-up with the flu for a week. 他因为流感已经卧床一星期了。

The ship has been laid-up for repairs. 这艘船已经停止使用进行维修了。|近义词：confined, bedridden, inactive, out of service|反义词:active, healthy, in service|词性:形容词

词源:  From the phrasal verb "lay up," meaning to store or confine.  "Lay" in this context comes from Old English "lecgan," meaning "to put, place, cause to lie down."  "Up" indicates a state of completion or confinement.
