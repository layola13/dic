anaks: /ˈænæks/
n.
sons, male children (plural of anak in Indonesian/Malay)
Sons; male offspring.  "Anak" is the singular form in Indonesian and Malay, and "anaks" is its plural form, though it's not standard English.
The anaks helped their father on the farm.  这些儿子们在农场帮助他们的父亲。
近义词: sons, children, offspring
反义词: daughters (specifically), parents (generally)
词性: Noun
