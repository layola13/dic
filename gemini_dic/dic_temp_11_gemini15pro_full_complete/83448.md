habited:/ˈhæbɪtɪd/
adjective
有人居住的；有动物栖息的
Inhabited means lived in or occupied by people or animals.
The island is inhabited by a variety of rare birds.  这个岛屿栖息着各种各样的珍稀鸟类。
The house appeared to be uninhabited. 这房子似乎无人居住。
近义词:occupied, populated, settled
反义词:uninhabited, deserted, vacant
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *habitātus, past participle of habitāre (“to inhabit, dwell”), from habitus (“condition, appearance, dress, attire”), ultimately from habēre (“to have, hold”).
