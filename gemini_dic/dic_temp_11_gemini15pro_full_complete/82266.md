groundlessness:/ˈɡraʊndləsnəs/
n.|无根据；无基础|The groundlessness of her fears became apparent. 她的恐惧毫无根据，这一点变得显而易见。|Etymology: From groundless + -ness. Groundless from ground + -less.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grunduz ("ground").|例句：The lawyer demonstrated the groundlessness of the accusation. 律师证明了指控是毫无根据的。

例句：He dismissed the rumors as pure groundlessness. 他认为这些谣言纯属无稽之谈。|近义词：baselessness, unfoundedness, falsity|反义词：validity, truth, substantiation|词性: 名词
