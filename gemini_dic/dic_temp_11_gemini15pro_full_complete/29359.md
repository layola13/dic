castrated:/ˈkæstreɪtɪd/
adjective
阉割的
Deprived of the testicles (of a male animal or person).
例句：The castrated bull became docile. 这头阉割过的公牛变得温顺了。

例句：The farmer castrated the piglets. 农民阉割了小猪。

近义词：gelded, neutered, emasculated
反义词：intact, uncastrated
词性: adjective
