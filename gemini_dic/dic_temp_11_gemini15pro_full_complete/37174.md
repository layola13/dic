come-yourself:/kʌm jɔːrˈsɛlf/ | adj. | 自助的;自助的;自己动手的 |  Describing something that is done by oneself, without professional or outside help.  It emphasizes personal involvement in the process. |  We had a come-yourself barbecue at the park. 我们在公园里举办了一场自助烧烤。 |  DIY, self-service |  professionally done, catered | adjective
come-yourself:/kʌm jɔːrˈsɛlf/ | n. | 自助餐;自助活动 | An event or meal where individuals serve themselves. | The church hosted a come-yourself dinner to raise money. 教堂举办了一场自助晚餐来筹款。 | buffet, potluck, self-service | catered event, formal dinner | noun
