lowbrow:/ˈloʊbraʊ/| n. adj.|低俗的人；低俗的|A person with little taste or intellectual interest; not intellectual or cultured. |例句：The movie was criticized for its lowbrow humor. 这部电影因其低俗的幽默而受到批评。

例句：He considered reality TV to be lowbrow entertainment. 他认为真人秀是低俗的娱乐。|近义词：uncultured, philistine, vulgar, tasteless|反义词：highbrow, sophisticated, cultured, refined|词性: 名词，形容词
