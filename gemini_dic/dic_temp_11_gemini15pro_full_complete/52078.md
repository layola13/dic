dialytic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|透析的；辩证的；分离的|Relating to dialysis (medical); pertaining to dialectic (logic/philosophy); separating or loosening.|The doctor recommended dialytic therapy for the patient with kidney failure. 医生建议对肾衰竭患者进行透析治疗。|近义词：dialysing, analytic, separative|反义词：synthetic, unifying|词性:形容词

dialytic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɪtɪk/| n.|透析物；分解剂|A substance used in dialysis.|The dialytic helped to remove waste products from the blood.  这种透析物帮助清除了血液中的废物。|近义词：dialysate|反义词:|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dialektikos* (pertaining to dialectic), from *dialegesthai* (to converse, discuss), from *dia* (through, across) + *legein* (to speak). The medical sense relating to dialysis is a later development, drawing on the idea of separation or filtering.
