exotics:/ɪɡˈzɑːtɪks/ | n. | 外来物；异国情调的事物；外来品种；舶来品 |  Something unusual and interesting, typically from or related to a distant foreign country. | The market offers a wide array of exotics, from rare fruits to handcrafted jewelry.  这个市场提供各种各样的外来商品，从稀有水果到手工珠宝。 | synonyms: rarities, curiosities, novelties, foreign goods, imported goods | antonyms: commonplace items, ordinary things, natives, indigenous species | noun

exotic:/ɪɡˈzɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 外来的；异国情调的；奇异的 | Originating in or characteristic of a distant foreign country; strikingly unusual or strange in effect or appearance.  |  She wears exotic perfumes. 她使用异国情调的香水。 | synonyms: foreign, unusual, striking, outlandish, bizarre | antonyms: native, indigenous, common, ordinary, familiar | adjective
