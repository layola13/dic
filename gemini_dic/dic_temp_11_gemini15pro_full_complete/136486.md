nonrhythmic:/nɑːnˈrɪðmɪk/ /nɒnˈrɪðmɪk/
adjective
非节奏性的，无节奏的
Lacking rhythm or regularity.
Her breathing was nonrhythmic and shallow. 她的呼吸没有节奏，很浅。
non-metrical, arrhythmic, irregular
rhythmic, regular
adjective
