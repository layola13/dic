infightings:/ˈɪnˌfaɪtɪŋz/
noun
内斗，内讧
Struggles or conflicts within a group or organization.
The party was weakened by internal infightings.  该党因内部斗争而被削弱。
The infightings within the company prevented them from making progress. 公司内部的斗争阻碍了他们的进步。

近义词:squabbles, bickering, feuding, internal conflicts, internal strife, power struggles
反义词:cooperation, collaboration, unity, harmony, teamwork


词性:n. (noun)
