confessorial: /kənˈfɛʃənəl/ | adj. |忏悔的；坦白的|Relating to or characteristic of confession, especially of private matters or sins.  Characterized by or prone to revealing private thoughts and feelings. |例句：The book has a confessional tone, revealing the author's deepest insecurities. 这本书的语气是忏悔的，揭示了作者内心深处的不安全感。

例句：He adopted a confessional tone as he spoke about his past mistakes. 他在谈到过去的错误时，采取了一种忏悔的语气。|近义词：frank, candid, open, intimate, revealing|反义词：secretive, reserved, guarded|词性: adjective
