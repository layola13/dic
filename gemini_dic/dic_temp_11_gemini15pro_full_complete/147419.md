overnourishment:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃmənt/ | n. | 营养过剩；过度营养 | The state of being supplied with more nutrients than needed, leading to obesity or other health problems. | Childhood overnourishment can lead to health issues later in life.  儿童营养过剩会导致日后的健康问题。 | Synonyms: overfeeding, hyperalimentation | Antonyms: undernourishment, malnutrition | noun

overnourish:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃ/ | vt. |  营养过剩；过度喂养 | To provide with excessive nutrients. | Some parents unintentionally overnourish their children.  有些父母会在无意中过度喂养他们的孩子。 | Synonyms: overfeed | Antonyms: undernourish | verb
