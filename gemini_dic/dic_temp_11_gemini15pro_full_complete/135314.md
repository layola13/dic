nonpatriotically: /ˌnɒnˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪkli/
| adv. |不爱国地|In a manner that lacks patriotism; showing a lack of love or devotion to one's country.| He acted nonpatriotically by burning the flag. 他通过焚烧国旗表现得毫不爱国。|
|Synonym: disloyally, treasonously, unpatriotically|Antonym: patriotically, loyally|
|词性: 副词 (Adverb)|
