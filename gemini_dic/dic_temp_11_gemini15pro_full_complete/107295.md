lamaistic:/ˌlɑːməˈɪstɪk/
adjective
喇嘛教的；喇嘛主义的
Relating to or characteristic of Lamaism, a form of Buddhism practiced in Tibet and Mongolia.
The temple was decorated with lamaistic symbols and artwork. 这座寺庙装饰着喇嘛教的符号和艺术品。
Monks chanted lamaistic scriptures in the temple. 僧侣们在寺庙里诵读喇嘛教经文。
近义词:Tibetan Buddhist,
反义词:(None - lacks a direct opposite)
词性:adjective

lamaistic:/ˌlɑːməˈɪstɪk/
noun
喇嘛教；喇嘛主义
Lamaism, a form of Buddhism practiced in Tibet and Mongolia.
He is a scholar of Lamaistic philosophy. 他是喇嘛教哲学的学者。
The museum has a large collection of Lamaistic art.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的喇嘛教艺术品。
近义词:Tibetan Buddhism
反义词:(None - lacks a direct opposite)
词性:noun


词源:  From Lama + -istic.  "Lama" comes from Tibetan "bla ma" meaning "superior one" or "guru."  "-istic" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of."
