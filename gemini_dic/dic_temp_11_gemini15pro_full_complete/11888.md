arrogate: /ˈærəˌɡeɪt/
| v. | 擅取；霸占；僭越 | To take or claim something without justification. |  The king arrogated the right to dissolve parliament. 国王僭越了解散议会的权力。 | 近义词: usurp, seize, appropriate, assume, claim | 反义词: relinquish, cede, surrender | 词性: 动词


词源:  From Latin arrogare "to claim for oneself," from ad- "to" + rogare "to ask."  来自拉丁语 arrogare “为自己要求”，来自 ad- “to” + rogare “to ask”。
