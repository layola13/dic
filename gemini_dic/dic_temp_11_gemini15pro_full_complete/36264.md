cogwheel:/ˈkɒɡwiːl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡwiːl/ (US)| n. |齿轮|A wheel with teeth around its edge, designed to mesh with the teeth of another wheel or part to transmit motion.|The cogwheels in the clock mechanism work together perfectly. 时钟装置中的齿轮完美地协同工作。|Synonyms: gear, gearwheel, sprocket|Antonyms: N/A|noun
