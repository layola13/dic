antireflection:/ˌæntiɹɪˈflɛkʃən/| adj.|抗反射的，防反射的|Reduces or prevents reflection of light.  It is often used to describe coatings applied to lenses or screens.|The camera lens has an antireflection coating to improve image quality. 相机镜头有抗反射涂层以提高图像质量。|Synonyms: nonreflective, non-reflecting|Antonyms: reflective|adjective


antireflection:/ˌæntiɹɪˈflɛkʃən/| n.|抗反射|The property of reducing or preventing reflection of light.|The antireflection of the screen is impressive. 屏幕的抗反射性能令人印象深刻。|Synonyms: nonreflection|Antonyms: reflection|noun
