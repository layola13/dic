agreed: /əˈɡriːd/ | adj. | 同意的，一致的 | Having the same opinion; consenting or concurring. |  We agreed on a price for the car. 我们就汽车的价格达成了一致。 | synonyms: consented, concurred, assented | antonyms: disagreed, dissented, opposed | adjective

agreed: /əˈɡriːd/ | v. | 同意，赞成 |  To have the same opinion; to consent or concur.  | They agreed to meet the following week. 他们同意下周见面。 | synonyms: consented, concurred, assented | antonyms: disagreed, dissented, opposed | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *agreen, from Anglo-Norman agreer, from Old French agreer (“to please, consent, agree”), from a- (“to”) + greer (“to please, accept”), from Late Latin grātum (“pleasing, welcome”), neuter of Latin grātus (“pleasing, thankful, grateful”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵwerH- (“to favor, like”).
