embargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. vt.|禁运，禁止贸易；禁止发表或公开|An official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.|The US imposed an embargo on trade with Cuba. 美国对古巴实施了贸易禁运。|Synonyms: ban, prohibition, restriction|Antonyms: allowance, permission|Noun, Verb

embargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| vt.|禁止发表或公开|To officially ban the publication or release of something.|The government embargoed the report until after the election. 政府禁止在选举后公布该报告。|Synonyms: suppress, withhold, censor|Antonyms: release, publish, disclose|Verb
