nonpropitiable:/ˌnɒnprəˈpɪʃɪəbl/
adjective
不可讨好的，无法平息的
not capable of being propitiated or appeased
Her nonpropitiable fury made everyone in the room tremble. 她无法平息的愤怒让房间里的每个人都颤抖。
Near Synonyms: implacable, unappeasable, relentless, inexorable
Antonyms: appeasable, placable, forgiving, merciful
词性: adjective
