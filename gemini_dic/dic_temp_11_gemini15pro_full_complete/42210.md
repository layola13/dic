councils:/ˈkaʊnslz/| n.|委员会，理事会，会议|A group of people elected or appointed to make decisions or give advice on a particular subject.|The city council voted to approve the new budget. 市议会投票通过了新的预算。|synonyms: committees, boards, assemblies|antonyms: individuals, single person|noun


councils:/ˈkaʊnslz/| n.|顾问，忠告|advice; guidance|He sought counsel from his lawyer. 他向他的律师寻求建议。|synonyms: advice, guidance, consultation|antonyms:discouragement, dissuasion|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conceil*, from Latin *concilium* "assembly, meeting, council," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *calare* "to call, summon" (see calendar).
