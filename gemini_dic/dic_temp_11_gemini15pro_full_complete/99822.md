intermingle:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡl/
verb
混合，交织
mix together or with something else
The flavors intermingle to create a unique taste. 各种味道混合在一起，创造出独特的口味。
The two rivers intermingle near the ocean. 两条河流在靠近大海的地方交汇融合。
近义词:mix, blend, merge, combine, fuse
反义词:separate, divide, segregate
词性:v.

intermingle:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡl/
noun
混合，交织
the act of mixing together or with something else
The intermingle of cultures created a vibrant community.  文化交融创造了一个充满活力的社区。
The intermingle of different styles of music made for an interesting concert. 不同音乐风格的融合，造就了一场有趣的音乐会。
近义词:mixture, blend, fusion, combination
反义词:separation, division, segregation
词性:n.

词源: From inter- + mingle, from Old English mengan "to mix."
