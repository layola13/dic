homograph:/ˈhɒməɡrɑːf/ (US) /ˈhɑːməɡræf/| n. |同形异义词|A word that is spelled the same as another word but has a different meaning and sometimes a different pronunciation.|The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are homographs.|近义词:homonym|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

homograph:/ˈhɒməɡrɑːf/ (US) /ˈhɑːməɡræf/| adj. |同形的|Having the same spelling as another word but a different meaning and sometimes a different pronunciation.|Lead (the metal) and lead (to guide) are homographic words.|近义词:homonymous|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
