jacobitism:/ˈdʒeɪkəˌbaɪˌtɪzəm/
n.
詹姆斯党主义；詹姆斯二世党人的拥护
The principles and practices of the Jacobites; adherence to the cause of the exiled Stuarts, especially James II and his descendants.
The museum's exhibit detailed the history of Jacobitism in Scotland.  博物馆的展览详细介绍了苏格兰詹姆斯党主义的历史。
近义词:Stuart supporter
反义词:Hanoverianism
