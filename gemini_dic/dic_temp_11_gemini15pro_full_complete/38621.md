concretize:/ˈkɒŋkrɪtaɪz/ /ˈkɑːŋkrɪtaɪz/| v. |使具体化，实现，落实|To make something concrete or definite; to give form or substance to an idea or plan.|例句：The government needs to concretize its plans for economic reform. 政府需要将其经济改革计划具体化。|近义词：realize, materialize, solidify, actualize|反义词：abstract, generalize|词性:动词

concretize:/ˈkɒŋkrɪtaɪz/ /ˈkɑːŋkrɪtaɪz/| n. |具体化，实现|The act of making something concrete or definite.|例句：The concretization of his plans took several years.  他的计划的具体化花了数年时间。|近义词：realization, materialization, solidification, actualization|反义词：abstraction, generalization|词性:名词
