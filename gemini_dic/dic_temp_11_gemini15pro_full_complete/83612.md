haematothermal:/ˌhiːmətoʊˈθɜːrməl/ /ˌhɛmətoʊˈθɜːməl/ | adj. | 温血的；热血的 | Relating to or characterized by warm-bloodedness, having a body temperature regulated by internal metabolic activity.  |  Haematothermal animals, like mammals and birds, can maintain a stable body temperature. 温血动物，如哺乳动物和鸟类，可以保持稳定的体温。| synonyms: warm-blooded, endothermic | antonyms: cold-blooded, ectothermic | adjective
