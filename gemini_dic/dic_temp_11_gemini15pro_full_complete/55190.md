dissentient:/dɪˈsɛnʃənt/
adjective
持异议的；不同意的
expressing disagreement with the majority opinion.
Two members dissented from the majority decision. 两个成员对多数人的决定表示异议。
dissenting, disagreeing, objecting
agreeing, concurring, assenting
adjective

dissentient:/dɪˈsɛnʃənt/
noun
持异议者；反对者
a person who expresses disagreement with the majority opinion.
The dissentients were outnumbered. 持异议者的人数少于多数。
objector, dissenter, protester
supporter, conformist, adherent
noun
