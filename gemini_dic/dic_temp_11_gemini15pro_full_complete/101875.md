ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ischiadicus, from Greek ἰσχιαδικός (iskhiadkos), from ἰσχίον (iskhion, “hip joint, hip”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱeHi- (“to divide, separate”).
