fish-farmed:/ˈfɪʃ fɑːrmd/ | adj. | 鱼塘养殖的 | Raised or grown in fish farms.  This refers to fish that are commercially raised in enclosures, such as tanks or ponds, rather than caught in the wild. | Example: Fish-farmed salmon is often less expensive than wild-caught salmon. 鱼塘养殖的三文鱼通常比野生三文鱼便宜。| Example:  The demand for fish-farmed products is increasing globally. 全球对鱼塘养殖产品的需求正在增长。| 近义词: aquaculture, farm-raised | 反义词: wild-caught | 词性:形容词
