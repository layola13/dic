interinhibition:/ˌɪntərɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/
|n.|相互抑制|The process by which the activation of one neuron suppresses the activity of another.|The interinhibition between the two muscle groups allows for smooth, coordinated movement.|suppression, interference|facilitation, excitation|noun
