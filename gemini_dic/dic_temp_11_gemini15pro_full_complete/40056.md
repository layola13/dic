consumptives:/kənˈsʌmptɪvz/
| n. |肺痨患者；痨病患者| People suffering from pulmonary tuberculosis (consumption). |  In the 19th century, consumptives often sought treatment in sanatoriums with fresh air.  19世纪，肺结核患者经常在空气新鲜的疗养院寻求治疗。| 近义词:tubercular patients, TB sufferers | 反义词:healthy individuals | 词性:noun


consumptive:/kənˈsʌmptɪv/
| adj. | 患肺痨的；消费的| Relating to or affected with pulmonary tuberculosis (consumption); tending to consume or waste. | The consumptive cough was a telltale sign of the disease.  肺痨的咳嗽是这种疾病的明显征兆。|  The country's consumptive habits are depleting its natural resources.  该国的消费习惯正在耗尽其自然资源。|近义词:(for disease) tubercular, (for consumption) wasteful, extravagant | 反义词:(for disease) healthy, (for consumption) thrifty, frugal | 词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *consumptivus, from Latin consumptus (past participle of consumere "to consume"), from com- "together" + sumere "to take."
