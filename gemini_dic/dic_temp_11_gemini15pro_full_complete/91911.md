hyperlustrously:/ˌhaɪpərˈlʌstrəsli/
adverb
极度光亮地，非常有光泽地
With an exceedingly bright luster; very lustrously.  It describes something shining with an intense or exaggerated brilliance.
The newly polished car gleamed hyperlustrously in the sunlight.  新抛光的汽车在阳光下闪闪发光。
The gem hyperlustrously reflected the light, captivating everyone in the room.  宝石反射出极强的光芒，吸引了房间里所有人的目光。
近义词: brilliantly, dazzlingly, radiantly, gleamingly
反义词: dully, dimly
词性: adverb

词源:
"Hyper-" (Greek):  meaning "over, above, beyond"
"Lustrous" (Latin "lustrare"): meaning "to brighten, illuminate"
"-ly" (Old English "-lice"): adverbial suffix
