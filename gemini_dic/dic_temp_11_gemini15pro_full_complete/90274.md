housekeeper:/ˈhaʊskiːpər/| n.|管家，保洁员|A person employed to manage a household and its staff, or to clean and maintain a house or hotel room.|The housekeeper keeps the entire estate spotless.  这位管家把整个庄园都打扫得一尘不染。|The hotel housekeeper changed the sheets and towels. 酒店的保洁员更换了床单和毛巾。|synonyms: maid, domestic, cleaner, servant|antonyms: employer, owner|noun
