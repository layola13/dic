filthied:/ˈfɪlθid/ | v. | 使污秽；弄脏 | To make something filthy; to defile or contaminate something. | The scandal filthied his reputation.  这桩丑闻玷污了他的名誉。|  The mud filthied her new white dress. 泥巴弄脏了她崭新的白裙子。 | 近义词: soiled, dirtied, polluted, contaminated, defiled | 反义词: cleaned, purified, sanitized | 词性: 动词

词源: From the adjective "filthy" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Filthy" comes from Old English "filthig," meaning "foul, dirty."
