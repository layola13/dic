ephippial:/ɛˈfɪpiəl/ | adj. |  休眠卵的，鞍状的 | Relating to or resembling an ephippium, a protective structure for eggs or embryos in certain invertebrates, especially daphnia. | Daphnia produce ephippial eggs during times of environmental stress.  水蚤在环境压力下会产休眠卵。| Synonyms: resting, dormant | Antonyms: active, reproductive | Adjective

ephippium:/ɛˈfɪpiəm/ | n. | 休眠卵，鞍状物 | A thickened, chitinous, often pigmented shell or capsule enclosing the winter or resting eggs of certain invertebrates, such as daphnia and rotifers; also, any similar structure. | The ephippium protects the eggs from harsh conditions. 休眠卵保护卵免受恶劣条件的影响。 | Synonyms: resting egg case, protective shell | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms related to active reproduction like "egg sac" in contrast to a dormant structure) | Noun
