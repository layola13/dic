minuted:/ˈmɪnɪtɪd/| adj.|记录在案的；详细记录的|Recorded in or as minutes; recorded in detail.|The meeting was minuted carefully, with all decisions and discussions documented. 会议被仔细记录下来，所有决定和讨论都已存档。|The agreement was fully minuted. 该协议被完整记录在案。|近义词：recorded, documented, logged|反义词: unrecorded, undocumented|词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "minute" (meaning "to record in minutes"), which comes from the noun "minute" (meaning "a brief record"), ultimately from Latin "minuta" (plural of "minutum," meaning "small part").
