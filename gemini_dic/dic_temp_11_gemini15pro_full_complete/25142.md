buckle:/ˈbʌkəl/ (US), /ˈbʌkl/ (UK)| n. v.|扣环；扣住；垮掉；屈服|A metal fastening for a belt or strap, typically one in which a pin on one part is passed through a loop or other opening on the other.|He buckled his belt. 他扣上了皮带。|近义词：clasp, fastener, clip|反义词：unbuckle|词性:名词,动词


buckle:/ˈbʌkəl/ (US), /ˈbʌkl/ (UK)| v.|扣住；垮掉；屈服|To fasten or be fastened with a buckle; to bend or collapse under pressure or strain.|The bridge buckled under the weight of the truck. 桥在卡车的重压下垮掉了。|近义词：fasten, secure, collapse, yield|反义词：unbuckle, straighten|词性:动词
