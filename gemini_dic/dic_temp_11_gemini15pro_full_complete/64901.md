essentialized:/ɪˈsɛnʃəlaɪzd/
| adj.|本质化的，被简化的，被概括的|Reduced to its essence; treated as if representing the essence of something.  The complex issue was essentialized for the sake of brevity.|The history of the conflict was essentialized into a simple narrative of good versus evil.  这场冲突的历史被简化为一个简单的善恶叙事。|simplified, reduced, epitomized|complex, detailed, nuanced|adjective
