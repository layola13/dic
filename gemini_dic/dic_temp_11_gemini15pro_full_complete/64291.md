equity:/ˈekwɪti/ | n. |公平，公正；股权，权益；净值|The value of a property after all charges have been paid is called equity.  房产在支付所有费用后的价值称为净值。|例句：He sold his equity in the company. 他卖掉了他在公司的股份。

例句：They are campaigning for equity in the workplace. 他们在为工作场所的公平而奔走。|近义词：fairness, justice, impartiality, right, ownership, stake |反义词：injustice, unfairness, bias, debt |词性:名词

词源: From Old French "equite"  and directly from Latin "aequitas" meaning  "evenness, uniformity, fairness, equal right". Ultimately from "aequus" (equal).
