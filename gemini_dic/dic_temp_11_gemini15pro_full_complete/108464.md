lay-down-law:/ˈleɪˌdaʊn ˈlɔː/ | adj. | 严格的，不容置疑的 | A lay-down-law situation is one where the rules or outcome are very clear and cannot be argued with.  It implies a situation where the outcome is predetermined or obvious. |  It was a lay-down-law case; the evidence was overwhelming. 这是个铁证如山的案子；证据确凿。|  The champion was a lay-down-law favorite to win the match.  这位冠军是这场比赛的必然赢家。|  certain, definite, obvious, undeniable | debatable, disputable | adjective
