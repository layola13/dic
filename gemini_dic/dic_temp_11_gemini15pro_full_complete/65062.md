eternalizing:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使永恒化，使不朽
The artist sought to eternalize the fleeting beauty of nature in his paintings.  这位艺术家试图在他的画作中使稍纵即逝的自然美永恒。
Making a time capsule is a way of eternalizing memories. 制作时间胶囊是使记忆永恒的一种方式。
immortalizing, perpetuating, memorializing
