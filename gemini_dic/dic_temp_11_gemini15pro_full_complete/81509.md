gravid: /ˈɡrævɪd/ | adj. | 怀孕的；充满的 | Pregnant; carrying young or developing eggs.  Also, figuratively, filled with something. |  The gravid mare was due to foal any day.  怀孕的母马随时都可能分娩。| Synonyms: pregnant, expecting, teeming, laden | Antonyms: barren, infertile, empty | adjective

gravid: /ˈɡrævɪd/ | adj. | 沉重的；装满的 |  Full of something, often in a way that seems heavy or excessive. | The air was gravid with the scent of jasmine. 空气中弥漫着浓浓的茉莉花香。 | Synonyms: heavy, laden, charged, pregnant | Antonyms: light, empty, unburdened | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gravidus*, from *gravis* "heavy".
