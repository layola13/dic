dynamo:/ˈdaɪnəmoʊ/ (US), /ˈdaɪnəməʊ/ (UK)| n.|发电机;精力充沛的人|A machine that generates electricity; a forceful, energetic person. |例句：The dynamo powering the town failed. 为这个小镇供电的发电机坏了。

例句：She's a real dynamo in the office, always getting things done. 她是办公室里的一个真正的精力充沛的人，总是把事情做好。|近义词：generator, powerhouse, livewire|反义词：sluggard, drone|noun
