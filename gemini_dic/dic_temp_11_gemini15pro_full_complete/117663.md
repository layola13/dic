mehndi: /ˈmɛndi/ (US), /ˈmɛndi/ (UK)| n.| 指甲花染料，指甲花纹身| A paste made from the dried leaves of the henna plant, used for temporary skin decoration.| She had intricate mehndi designs on her hands and feet for the wedding. 她为了婚礼在手脚上画了复杂的指甲花图案。| 近义词: henna | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Hindi मेंहदी (meṃhdī), from Sanskrit मेहन्दी (mehandi).
