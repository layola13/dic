codec:/ˈkoʊdɛk/| n. |编码解码器|A device or program that compresses data to enable faster transmission and decompresses received data.|Example: This video requires a specific codec to play.  这个视频需要特定的编解码器才能播放。|Synonym: coder, decoder|Antonym: N/A|noun


codec:/ˈkoʊdɛk/| n. |编码解码器|A device or program that encodes and decodes digital data, especially for audio or video signals.|Example:  I need to install a new codec to watch this movie. 我需要安装一个新的编解码器才能观看这部电影。|Synonym: encoder/decoder|Antonym: N/A|noun
