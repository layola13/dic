fatalist:/ˈfeɪtəlɪst/ | n. | 宿命论者；听天由命的人 | A person who believes that events are predetermined and inevitable. | He accepted his fate with the quiet resignation of a fatalist. 他以宿命论者的平静顺从接受了自己的命运。| synonyms: determinist, predestinarian | antonyms: optimist, free-thinker | noun

fatalistic:/ˌfeɪtəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 宿命论的；听天由命的 | Relating to or characteristic of fatalism. |  She had a fatalistic attitude about the future. 她对未来抱有宿命论的态度。| synonyms: resigned, defeatist, pessimistic | antonyms: optimistic, hopeful | adjective

fatally:/ˈfeɪtəli/ | adv. | 致命地；宿命地 | In a way that causes death or failure. |  He was fatally injured in the accident. 他在事故中受了致命伤。| synonyms: mortally, lethally, critically | antonyms: harmlessly, slightly | adverb
