formalistically:/fɔːrməˈlɪstɪkli/
adv.
形式上地，拘泥形式地
In a formalistic manner, adhering strictly to prescribed forms or conventions, often at the expense of substance or meaning.
The problem was approached formalistically, with little regard for practical considerations.  这个问题处理得很拘泥形式，几乎没有考虑实际情况。
The artist's later work became increasingly formalistic and lost its emotional impact.  这位艺术家后期的作品越来越拘泥形式，失去了情感冲击力。

近义词:ceremoniously, rigidly, technically
反义词:substantively, meaningfully, practically
词性:adverb
