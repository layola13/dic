globalistic:/ˌɡloʊbəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
全球主义的
Relating to or characteristic of globalism.  Focusing on economic and cultural interconnectedness across the world, often with the implication of promoting a unified world order.
The speaker delivered a globalistic speech, emphasizing the need for international cooperation. 演讲者发表了一篇全球主义的演讲，强调了国际合作的必要性。
The company adopted a globalistic approach to marketing, targeting consumers in multiple countries with the same campaign.  这家公司采取了一种全球化的营销方式，以同一活动针对多个国家的消费者。
近义词: international, cosmopolitan, global
反义词: nationalist, isolationist, local
词性: adjective
