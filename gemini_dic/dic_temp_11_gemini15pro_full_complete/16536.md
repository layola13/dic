Berkshire: /ˈbɜːrkʃɪr/ (US) /ˈbɑːkʃɪr/ (UK)| n. |伯克郡|A county in southern England.|例句：He lives in Berkshire. 他住在伯克郡。|例句：Berkshire is known for its beautiful countryside. 伯克郡以其美丽的乡村而闻名。|近义词：county, shire|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


Etymology: From Old English Bearrocscir  meaning "Berroc's shire",  likely referring to a bare patch of ground where box trees grew.
