ditrochee:/ˈdaɪtrəʊkiː/ | n. | 双音步 | A metrical foot consisting of two trochees.  A trochee is a metrical foot consisting of a stressed syllable followed by an unstressed syllable. |  The word "poetry" itself is a ditrochee. |  Synonyms:  (No exact synonyms, but related terms include) trochaic dimeter, double trochee | Antonyms:  (No antonyms) | noun
