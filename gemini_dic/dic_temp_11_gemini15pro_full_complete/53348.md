disadvantageously:/ˌdɪsədvænˈteɪdʒəsli/
adverb|不利地，劣势地|In a way that causes disadvantage; in a way that is not favorable.|His small stature disadvantaged him disadvantageously in basketball. 他矮小的身材在篮球方面对他很不利。|
Synonyms: unfavorably, detrimentally, badly, poorly, adversely
Antonyms: advantageously, favorably, beneficially, profitably, helpfully
词性: 副词


disadvantage:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒ/
noun|缺点，劣势|A condition or circumstance that puts one in a worse position or at a lower level.|One disadvantage of living in the city is the high cost of housing. 住在城市的一个缺点是住房成本高。|
Synonyms: drawback, handicap, detriment, weakness, shortcoming
Antonyms: advantage, benefit, asset, strength, plus
词性: 名词


disadvantage:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒ/
verb|使处于劣势，使不利|Put at a disadvantage.|His lack of experience disadvantaged him in the job market. 他缺乏经验使他在就业市场上处于劣势。|
Synonyms: handicap, hinder, hamper, obstruct, impede
Antonyms: advantage, benefit, aid, assist, help
词性: 动词
