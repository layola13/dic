overcast:/ˈoʊvərˌkæst/| adj. n.|阴天的，阴暗的；阴天，乌云|covered with clouds; dull or gloomy|例句：The sky was overcast and threatened rain. 天空阴沉，像是要下雨。|近义词：cloudy, dull, gloomy|反义词：sunny, bright, clear|词性:形容词, 名词

overcasts:/ˌoʊvərˈkæsts/| v. (third-person singular present)|遮蔽，使阴暗|(of clouds) cover the sky so as to make it dark; overshadow|例句：A dark cloud overcasts the sky. 一片乌云遮蔽了天空。|近义词：clouds, darkens, obscures|反义词：brightens, clears|词性:动词
