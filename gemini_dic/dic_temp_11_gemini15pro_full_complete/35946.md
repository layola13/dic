coddlers:/ˈkɒdləz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːdlərz/ (US)| n.|娇惯者，溺爱者，呵护者|A person who coddles or treats someone with excessive care and attention, often preventing them from developing independence.|The child was raised by coddlers and never learned to do anything for himself.  这个孩子是被娇惯着长大的，从来没学过自己做任何事。|近义词：pamperers, overprotectors|反义词: disciplinarians|Noun
