drogher:/ˈdroʊɡər/| n. |小型拖船，驳船；一种西非海岸的单桅帆船|A small vessel used for carrying goods, often towed by a larger ship.  Historically, also a type of West African single-masted sailing vessel.|The drogher was laden with supplies for the island. 这艘驳船装载了岛上的补给品。|Synonyms: barge, lighter, coaster|Antonyms: tugboat (in the sense that a tugboat pulls, while a drogher is pulled)|noun
drogher:/ˈdroʊɡər/| n. |西非海岸的一种单桅帆船|A type of single-masted sailing vessel used along the West African coast.|The drogher sailed down the coast, carrying goods to various ports. 这艘单桅帆船沿着海岸航行，将货物运送到各个港口。|Synonyms: sailing vessel, ship, boat|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps steamship for a contrasting vessel type)|noun
