grocer:/ˈɡroʊsər/ (US) /ˈɡrəʊsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|杂货商；食品商|A grocer is a person who owns or works in a shop that sells food and small household goods.|例句：The local grocer sells fresh produce. 当地的杂货商出售新鲜农产品。

例句：She bought a loaf of bread from the grocer. 她从杂货店老板那里买了一条面包。


近义词：shopkeeper, retailer, merchant|反义词:|词性：noun
