intersale:/ˈɪntərseɪl/| n.|季中促销，季中销售|A sale of merchandise between regular sales seasons, typically at reduced prices.  A sale of goods or services within a single company or organization, often between different departments.|The department store held an intersale to clear out summer inventory before the fall fashions arrived. 百货公司在秋季时装到货前举行了季中促销，以清理夏季库存。| The intersale of software licenses between company divisions streamlined operations and reduced costs. 公司各部门之间软件许可证的内部销售简化了运营并降低了成本。|近义词：mid-season sale, clearance sale|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


词源:  "inter-" (between) + "sale"
