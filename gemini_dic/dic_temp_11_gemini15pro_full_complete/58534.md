duffing:/ˈdʌfɪŋ/ | adj. | 达芬方程的；与达芬方程有关的 | Relating to or characterized by the Duffing equation, a nonlinear second-order differential equation used to model certain oscillatory systems. | The researchers analyzed the duffing oscillator to understand its chaotic behavior. 研究人员分析了达芬振子以了解其混沌行为。 |  The team studied the duffing system's response to various forcing frequencies.  团队研究了达芬系统对各种强迫频率的响应。| 近义词: nonlinear, oscillatory | 反义词: linear, stable | 词性: adjective

詞源: Named after Georg Duffing (1861–1944), a German engineer who studied nonlinear oscillations.
