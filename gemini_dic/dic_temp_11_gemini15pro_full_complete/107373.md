lamentable:/ˈlæm(ə)ntəb(ə)l/| adj.|令人惋惜的， regrettable; deplorable; distressing|Causing or expressing distress and regret.|The lamentable state of the economy is a serious concern.  令人惋惜的经济状况是一个严重的问题。|The team's lamentable performance led to their elimination from the tournament.  该队令人遗憾的表现导致他们被淘汰出局。|近义词：regrettable, deplorable, unfortunate, distressing, sad, pitiful|反义词：fortunate, lucky, commendable, praiseworthy|词性:adjective
词源:From late Middle English: from Old French lamentable, from lamenter ‘to lament’, from Latin lamentari .
