lavatory:/ˈlævəˌtɔːri/ (US) /ˈlævətri/ (UK)| n.|厕所，盥洗室|A room or compartment with a toilet and sink, chiefly on trains, ships, and aircraft; also, a toilet in a house.  A lavatory is typically a smaller room primarily intended for urination and defecation, and may or may not include a sink for handwashing.  In British English, "lavatory" is a more formal term for "toilet."|The train's lavatory was out of order. 火车上的厕所坏了。|
