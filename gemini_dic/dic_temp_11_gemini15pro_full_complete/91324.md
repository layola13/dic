hydrophid:/ˈhaɪdrəfɪd/ | n. |海蛇|A venomous aquatic snake of the family Hydrophiidae, found chiefly in the Indian and Pacific Oceans. |例句：The hydrophid, with its flattened tail, is well adapted to marine life.  海蛇，凭借其扁平的尾巴，非常适应海洋生活。

例句：Hydrophids are known for their potent venom. 海蛇以其强效的毒液而闻名。 |近义词：sea snake, sea serpent|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps terrestrial snake) |词性:noun
