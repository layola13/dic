-er:/ər/|/ suffix|人或物；比较级后缀；反复做…的人|A suffix that forms nouns designating persons or things, often from verbs (e.g., writer, painter, computer). It can also form the comparative degree of adjectives (e.g., smaller, taller). Finally, it can denote someone who performs an action repeatedly (e.g., complainer, sleeper).|He's a fast learner. 他学东西很快。|Suffixes like -ist, -or, -ant|N/A|suffix
