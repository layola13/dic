bloodily:/ˈblʌdɪli/
adverb
血腥地，残忍地
in a bloody or violent manner.
The battle was fought bloodily. 这场战斗打得血腥残酷。
近义词：violently, brutally, savagely
反义词：peacefully, gently
词性: adverb
词源: From blood + -ily.
