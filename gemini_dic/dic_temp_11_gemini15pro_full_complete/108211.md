laudation: /lɔːˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 赞扬，称赞；赞美词，颂词 | An act of praising, especially publicly and formally. | The president delivered a laudation for the retiring senator. 总统发表了对退休参议员的颂词。|  The audience erupted in laudation at the end of the performance.  演出结束时，观众爆发出热烈的赞扬。 | 近义词: praise, acclaim, commendation, tribute | 反义词: criticism, condemnation, censure | 词性: noun
