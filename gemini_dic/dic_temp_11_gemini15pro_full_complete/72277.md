flotant:/flɔ.tɑ̃/| adj. | 浮动的，漂浮的；不固定的；可变的 | Floating or able to float; not fixed; variable. | The exchange rate is kept *flotant*.  汇率保持浮动。 | The company has a large amount of *flotant* capital. 这家公司有大量的流动资金。 | 近义词: floating, adrift, fluctuating, variable | 反义词: fixed, stable, constant | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From French *flotant*, present participle of *floter* "to float," from Latin *flotāre* "to float," frequentative of *flare* "to blow."
