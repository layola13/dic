depressurises:/diːˈprɛʃəraɪzɪz/
verb
使减压; 降低压力
To reduce the pressure in (something, such as an aircraft cabin or a sealed container).
The pilot depressurises the cabin before landing.  飞行员在着陆前降低机舱压力。
The chamber depressurises slowly to avoid damage. 为了避免损坏，压力室缓慢减压。
synonyms: decompress, vent, release pressure
antonyms: pressurize, compress
词性: 动词
