miniaturized:/ˈmɪniətʃəraɪzd/ | adj. |小型化的，微型化的 | Made smaller than usual; reduced in size. | Example 1: Scientists have developed miniaturized sensors for medical implants. 科学家们已经开发出用于医疗植入物的小型传感器。

Example 2: The company specializes in miniaturized electronics for portable devices. 该公司专门生产用于便携式设备的小型电子产品。 | Synonyms: smaller, reduced, compact, tiny, microscopic | Antonyms: enlarged, magnified, expanded, maximized | Word Type: Adjective
