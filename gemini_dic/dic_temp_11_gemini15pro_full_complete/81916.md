grillage:/ˈɡrɪlɪdʒ/ | n. |格栅，格架，栅板结构|A framework of crossing beams or timbers, often forming a foundation.  A structure of metal beams and rods fastened together in a grid pattern, used as a foundation or to reinforce concrete. |The bridge's foundation was a complex grillage of steel beams. 这座桥的基础是一个复杂的钢梁格栅。|The grillage distributed the weight of the building evenly across the soft ground.  格栅结构将建筑物的重量均匀地分布在松软的地面上。| synonyms: framework, lattice, grid, network | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps: solid, continuous) | noun
