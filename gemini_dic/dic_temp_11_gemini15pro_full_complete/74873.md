frescoist:/ˈfrɛskoʊɪst/
noun
壁画家
An artist who paints frescoes.  A fresco is a painting done rapidly in watercolour on wet plaster on a wall or ceiling, so that the colours penetrate the plaster and become fixed as it dries.
The frescoist spent years perfecting his technique.  这位壁画家花费了数年时间完善他的技艺。
Michelangelo was a renowned frescoist, responsible for the Sistine Chapel ceiling. 米开朗基罗是一位著名的壁画家，创作了西斯廷教堂的天花板壁画。
Synonyms: muralist, painter
Antonyms: N/A
