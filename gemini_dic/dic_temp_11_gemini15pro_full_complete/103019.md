jejunum:/dʒɪˈdʒuːnəm/ |n.|空肠|The jejunum is the middle section of the small intestine, between the duodenum and the ileum. It plays a vital role in the absorption of nutrients from digested food.|The doctor explained that the problem was located in her jejunum. 医生解释说问题出在她的空肠。|Synonyms: midgut (somewhat broader term), middle part of small intestine|Antonyms: N/A (anatomical structures generally don't have direct antonyms)|Noun
