admonitor:/ˌædməˈnɪtər/ | v. | 劝告，警告，提醒 | To warn or advise someone earnestly. |  He admonished his son to be careful with his money. 他告诫儿子要谨慎用钱。

He was admonished by his teacher for being late. 他因为迟到而受到了老师的警告。 | synonyms: warn, caution, advise, reprove, rebuke | antonyms: praise, commend, encourage | verb

Etymology: From Latin *admonēre*  (to remind, advise, warn), from *ad- (to)* + *monēre* (to warn).
