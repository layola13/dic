geyseral:/ˈɡaɪzərəl/| adj.|间歇泉似的，间歇泉的|Resembling or characteristic of a geyser; erupting or gushing like a geyser.  Like a geyser, the fountain geyserally erupted, sending a plume of water high into the air.|The pressure built up until the water geyserally erupted from the vent.|近义词：eruptive, gushing, spouting|反义词： trickling, dripping|词性:形容词

词源: From *geyser* + *-al*.  *Geyser* comes from Icelandic *geysir*, meaning "gusher."  The suffix *-al* forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of".
