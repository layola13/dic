epiphysial: /ˌɛpɪˈfɪzɪəl/ /ˌɛpɪˈfɪsiəl/ | adj. | 骨骺的 | Relating to the epiphysis, a part of a long bone where growth occurs. |  The epiphysial plate is responsible for bone lengthening during childhood.  骨骺板负责儿童时期的骨骼增长。 |  synonyms:  growth plate, physis | antonyms: diaphysial | adjective

epiphyseal: /ˌɛpɪˈfɪzɪəl/ /ˌɛpɪˈfɪsiəl/ | n. | 骨骺 | The end part of a long bone, initially growing separately from the shaft. | Damage to the epiphyseal can stunt growth. 对骨骺的损伤会阻碍生长。 | synonyms: physis, growth plate | antonyms: diaphysis | noun
