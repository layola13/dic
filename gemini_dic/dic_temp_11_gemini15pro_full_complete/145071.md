overanalyze:/ˌoʊvərˈænəˌlaɪz/| v. |过度分析|To analyze something excessively, often to the point of finding meanings that are not intended or real.  To think too much about something.|She tends to overanalyze every little thing he says, leading to misunderstandings. 她倾向于过度分析他说的每句话，导致误解。|近义词：overthink, dissect, scrutinize|反义词: skim, glance at, overlook|词性:动词

Etymology:
From over- + analyze.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Analyze" comes from the French analyser, from Greek analyein "to loosen, dissolve, resolve," from ana- "up, throughout" + lyein "to loose."
