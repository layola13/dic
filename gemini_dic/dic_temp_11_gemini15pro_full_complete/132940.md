nonembryonically:/ˌnɒnˌɛmbrɪˈɒnɪkli/
adjective
非胚胎地
Not related to or derived from an embryo.  Developing or occurring outside of the embryo.
Nonembryonic stem cells can be derived from adult tissues.  非胚胎干细胞可以从成人组织中获得。
These tumors arise from nonembryonic tissues. 这些肿瘤起源于非胚胎组织。
近义词:extraembryonic, postembryonic
反义词:embryonic
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not".  Indicates negation.
embryonic (adjective): From French embryonique, from Late Latin embryon-, from Greek embryon "embryo".
-ally (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.  Indicates manner or degree.
