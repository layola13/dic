gadded:/ˈɡædɪd/
verb
闲逛，游荡，四处游玩
spent time visiting a lot of different places for pleasure
She gadded about Europe for two months after graduation. 她毕业后在欧洲游玩了两个月。
近义词：roamed, wandered, gallivanted
反义词：stayed, remained, settled
词性：v.

gadding:/ˈɡædɪŋ/
noun
闲逛，游荡
the activity of visiting a lot of different places for pleasure
Her constant gadding about kept her from finishing her work. 她总是四处闲逛，导致工作无法完成。
近义词：roaming, wandering, gallivanting
反义词：staying, remaining, settling
词性：n.

Etymology:
From Middle English *gadden, possibly from Old Norse *gatha (“to go”). Related to Old English gæð (“company, association”).
