metastasize:/məˈtæs.tə.saɪz/ /ˈmet̬.ə.stəˌsaɪz/| v.i.|转移，扩散| (of a cancer) spread to other sites in the body by metastasis. |The cancer has metastasized to his lungs. 癌症已经转移到他的肺部了。|近义词：spread, diffuse, disseminate|反义词:localize, contain|verb

metastasis:/məˈtæs.tə.sɪs/ /meˈtæs.tə.sɪs/| n.|转移，扩散|The spread of a disease-producing agency (such as cancer cells or bacteria) from the initial or primary site of disease to another part of the body.|The doctor discovered metastasis in the patient's liver. 医生在病人的肝脏里发现了转移灶。|近义词：spread, diffusion, dissemination|反义词:localization, containment|noun
