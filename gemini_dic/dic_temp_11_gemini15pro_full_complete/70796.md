fingerling:/ˈfɪŋɡərlɪŋ/| n. |鱼苗，幼苗；小东西|A young fish, especially one less than a year old.  A small, immature version of something. |例句：The pond was stocked with trout fingerlings.  池塘里放养了鳟鱼鱼苗。

例句：She carefully transplanted the tomato fingerlings into larger pots. 她小心翼翼地把番茄幼苗移植到更大的花盆里。|近义词：fry, seedling, sapling, young, juvenile|反义词: adult, mature|词性:noun
