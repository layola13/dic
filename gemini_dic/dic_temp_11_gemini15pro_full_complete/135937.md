nonpunitory:/nɒnˈpjuːnɪtəri/
adjective

Not intended as punishment; not punitive.
The school adopted a nonpunitory approach to discipline, focusing on restorative justice. 学校采取了一种非惩罚性的纪律措施，重点是恢复性司法。
The program offers nonpunitory measures for first-time offenders.  该计划为初犯提供非惩罚性措施。
synonym:nonpunitive, remedial, corrective, rehabilitative
antonym:punitive, disciplinary, retributive
词性:adjective
