bustard:/ˈbʌstərd/ (US), /ˈbʌstɑːd/ (UK)| n. |鸨|A large, heavy-bodied bird of the family Otididae, found in open country in the Old World.  These birds are known for their elaborate mating displays.| We saw a bustard strutting across the savanna. 我们看到一只鸨在稀树草原上昂首阔步。| The male bustard performed an elaborate courtship display. 雄鸨进行了一场精心设计的求偶表演。|近义词: (no direct equivalent, but similar birds might include) crane, stork |反义词: (no antonym)|noun
