didynamous:/daɪˈdɪnəməs/| adj.|二强的，两强雄蕊的|Having four stamens, two of which are long and two short, as in the flowers of the mint family (Lamiaceae).|例句：The flowers of the catnip plant are didynamous. 猫薄荷的花是二强的。

例句：Didynamous stamens are a characteristic feature of many plants in the mint family. 二强雄蕊是薄荷科许多植物的一个特征。|近义词：tetradynamous (somewhat similar but with six stamens)|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, could consider flowers with equal length stamens)|词性: adjective
