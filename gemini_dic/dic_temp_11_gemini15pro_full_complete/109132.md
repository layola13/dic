legionnaires:/ˌliːdʒəˈnɛərz/
n.
退伍军人，军团士兵
Members of a legion, especially former soldiers.
The American Legion is a veterans' organization. 美国退伍军人协会是一个退伍军人组织。
veterans, ex-soldiers
civilians
noun


legionnaire:/ˌliːdʒəˈnɛər/
n.
退伍军人，军团士兵
A member of a legion, especially a former soldier.
He was a proud legionnaire, having served his country with distinction. 他是一位自豪的退伍军人，曾以杰出的表现为国家服务。
veteran, ex-soldier
civilian
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *légionnaire*, from Latin *legionarius* (“of a legion”), from *legio* (“legion”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\leg-* (“to collect, gather”).
