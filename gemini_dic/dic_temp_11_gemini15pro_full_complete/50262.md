depersonalising:/diːpɜːrsənəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使人失去个性化的；非人化的
The process of depriving someone of their personality or individuality, making them feel like an object or less than human.
The long hours and repetitive work can be depersonalising for factory workers.  长时间重复性的工作会使工厂工人失去个性。
The hospital environment can feel depersonalising for patients. 医院的环境会让病人感觉没有人情味。
近义词:dehumanizing, alienating
反义词:personalizing, individualizing
词性: adjective

depersonalise:/diːpɜːrsənəlaɪz/
verb
使失去个性；使无人情味
To deprive someone of their personality or individuality.
The company's strict dress code depersonalises its employees.  公司的严格着装规定使员工失去了个性。
Bureaucracy can depersonalise the relationship between doctor and patient.  官僚作风会使医生和病人之间的关系变得没有人情味。
近义词:dehumanize, objectify
反义词: personalize, humanize
词性: verb

词源: de- (Latin prefix meaning "down, away from, off") + personal + -ize (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or cause to be")
