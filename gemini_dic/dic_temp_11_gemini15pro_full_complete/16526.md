barkentine:/ˈbɑːrkənˌtiːn/ | n. | 三桅帆船 | A sailing vessel with three masts; square-rigged on the foremast and fore-and-aft rigged on the main and mizzen masts. | The barkentine sailed gracefully into the harbor.  三桅帆船优雅地驶入港口。

The old barkentine was beautifully restored.  这艘古老的三桅帆船被精美地修复了。 | 近义词: barquentine | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 steamship (蒸汽船) 表示不同动力类型的船) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From *barquentine*, from *barque* + *-entine*. Ultimately from the Old French *barque*.
