outdazzled: /ˌaʊtˈdæz(ə)ld/
verb
使…眼花缭乱；胜过；使相形见绌
to impress or excite someone very much, especially with your skill or ability, so that they cannot think clearly
The fireworks outdazzled all the other displays. 这些烟火使所有其他表演都黯然失色。
Synonyms: outshine, eclipse, surpass
Antonyms:  pale in comparison, be overshadowed
词性：动词
