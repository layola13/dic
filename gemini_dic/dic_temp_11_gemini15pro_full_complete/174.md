abate:/əˈbeɪt/ | v. | 减轻，减少，缓和 | To lessen in intensity or degree.  | The storm began to abate.  暴风雨开始减弱。 |  subside, lessen, diminish, decrease, mitigate | increase, intensify, worsen | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abatre "to beat down, cast down, demolish," from a- "down" + batre "to beat."  The sense shifted from "beat down (literally)" to "beat down (figuratively, as in a price)" to "reduce in intensity."
