ale:/eɪl/ | n. | 麦芽酒;淡色啤酒 | A type of beer, usually pale in color, made with top-fermenting yeast. |  例句：He ordered a pint of ale. 他点了一品脱麦芽酒。| 近义词：beer, brew | 反义词: (无明确反义词，可考虑lager，拉格啤酒，作为对比) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old English *alu, from Proto-Germanic *alō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂elut- (“bitter”).
