derailments:/dɪˈreɪlmənts/
n.
脱轨；出轨；偏离正轨
the act of a train coming off the rails; a situation in which something fails or becomes unsuccessful
Several train derailments have occurred this year due to extreme weather. 今年由于极端天气发生了几起火车脱轨事故。
The project suffered several derailments due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，该项目遭遇了几次偏离正轨。
synonyms:wrecks, crashes, failures, setbacks
antonyms:successes, achievements
