metamictness:/ˌmɛtəˈmɪktnəs/| n. |非晶质；蜕晶质；变晶质|The state of a mineral having lost its crystalline structure due to radiation damage, typically from alpha particles emitted by radioactive elements within the mineral itself.|The metamictness of the zircon crystals allowed scientists to date the rock formation.  锆石晶体的非晶质状态使科学家能够确定岩石的形成年代。|Near synonyms: amorphism, disorder|Antonym: crystallinity|noun


metamictization:/ˌmɛtəmɪktɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非晶化；蜕晶化；变晶化 | The process by which a mineral loses its crystalline structure due to radiation damage. |The metamictization of the mineral was caused by alpha decay. 这种矿物的非晶化是由α衰变引起的。|Near synonyms: amorphization|Antonym: crystallization|noun
