curate:/ˈkjʊreɪt/| v. n.|策划，策展；精选；组织|To select, organize, and present online content, typically for a specific audience or purpose.  To act as a curator.|The museum curated an exhibition of modern art.  博物馆策划了一个现代艺术展览。|近义词：organize, compile, assemble, select|反义词:disorganize, scatter|verb

curate:/ˈkjʊreɪt/| n.|策展人|A person who selects, organizes, and looks after items in a collection, such as in a museum or art gallery.|The curate gave a tour of the new exhibition.  策展人带领参观了新的展览。|近义词：curator, compiler, organizer|反义词: N/A|noun
