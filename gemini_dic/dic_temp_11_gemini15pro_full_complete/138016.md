noonday: /ˈnuːndeɪ/ | n. adj. | 正午,中午 | The period of midday; noon.  It can also function as an adjective, meaning "of or relating to noon." |  The noonday sun was scorching. 正午的阳光 scorching. | Synonym: midday, noon | Antonym: midnight | noun

noonday: /ˈnuːndeɪ/ | adj. | 正午的 | Of or relating to noon. | We rested in the shade during the noonday heat. 我们在正午的酷热中在阴凉处休息。| Synonym: midday | Antonym: midnight | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *nōntīd, from nōn "noon" (from Latin nona (hora) "ninth (hour)," originally 3 p.m., later noon) + tīd "time, hour."
