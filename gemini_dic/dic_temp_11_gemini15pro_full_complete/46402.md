daffiest:/ˈdæfiɪst/
adjective
最愚蠢的；最傻的
superlative adjective of daffy: most daffy.  Meaning extremely silly or foolish.
That's the daffiest thing I've ever heard!  那是我听过的最愚蠢的事情！
He made the daffiest excuse for being late. 他编了一个最傻的迟到借口。
synonym: silliest, craziest, goofiest, most foolish
antonym: wisest, sanest, most sensible
词性: 形容词
