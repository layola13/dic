diastasis:/daɪˈæstəsɪs/
noun
分离，分裂，舒张
Separation of normally joined parts.  Specifically, a separation of the rectus abdominis muscles, often occurring during pregnancy.
The diastasis of the rectus abdominis muscles can cause a bulge in the abdomen. 腹直肌分离会导致腹部隆起。
After giving birth, she noticed a diastasis recti.  生完孩子后，她注意到腹直肌分离。
Synonyms: separation, split, gap, fissure
Antonyms: union, joining, connection
词性: 名词
