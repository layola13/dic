ironheads:/ˈaɪərnˌhɛdz/| n.|顽固分子，铁头，死硬派|People who are stubborn or inflexible in their opinions or ways.  They are often resistant to change or new ideas.  The term suggests a metaphorical "hard head" like iron, unable to be persuaded or influenced.|Those ironheads refused to consider any new proposals.|近义词：diehards, hardliners, reactionaries, conservatives, stick-in-the-muds|反义词: liberals, progressives, reformers, open-minded people|noun
