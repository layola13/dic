deviating:/ˈdiːvieɪtɪŋ/| adj.|偏离的，背离的|Straying from a norm, course, or standard.|例句：The airplane is deviating from its planned flight path. 飞机偏离了预定的航线。|近义词：diverging, straying, swerving, veering|反义词：conforming, following, adhering|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Latin *deviatus*, past participle of *deviare* "to turn aside," from *de-* "away" + *via* "way, road."
