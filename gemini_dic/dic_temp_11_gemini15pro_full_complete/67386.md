exteriors:/ɪkˈstɪəriərz/
noun
外部；外观；外景
The exteriors of the houses were all painted white. 这些房子的外部都被漆成了白色。
The film's exteriors were shot in Morocco. 这部电影的外景是在摩洛哥拍摄的。
Synonyms: outsides, facades, surfaces
Antonyms: interiors
词性: 名词
