founderous:/ˈfaʊndərəs/| adj.| 易倒塌的，不牢固的，不稳定的 | Prone to collapsing or giving way; unstable. | The old bridge was founderous and unsafe to cross.  这座老桥摇摇欲坠，过桥不安全。| The building's founderous foundation made it susceptible to earthquakes. 这栋建筑不牢固的地基使其容易受到地震的影响。| 近义词: rickety, shaky, unstable, dilapidated, ramshackle | 反义词: stable, sturdy, solid, firm | 词性: 形容词

词源: From *fondre* (Old French) meaning "to melt, dissolve,"  from Latin *fundere* "to pour, melt."  The original meaning referred to the "melting away" or collapsing of something.
