convexed /kɒnˈvekst/ | adj. | 凸面的，凸起的 | Having an outline or surface curved like the exterior of a circle or sphere. | The mirror was convexed, giving a distorted reflection.  镜子是凸面的，反射出扭曲的影像。| Example: The convexed lens magnified the image. 凸透镜放大了图像。 | Synonyms: bulging, rounded, protuberant | Antonyms: concave, indented, hollow | adjective

convexed /kənˈvekst/ | adj. | 拱形的，穹形的 | Arched or vaulted. |  The ceiling was beautifully convexed. 天花板呈美丽的拱形。| Example:  The convexed shape of the roof allowed rain to run off easily.  拱形的屋顶使雨水容易流走。| Synonyms: arched, vaulted, domed | Antonyms: flat, level | adjective


词源: From Latin convexus "vaulted, arched," past participle of convehere "to bring together," from com- "together" + vehere "to carry."
