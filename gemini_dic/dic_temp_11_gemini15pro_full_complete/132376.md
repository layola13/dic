nondeliveries:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪv(ə)riz/
noun
未送达的货物/邮件；未交付
Instances where goods or services were not delivered as expected.
The company apologized for the nondeliveries and promised to rectify the situation.  公司为未送达的货物道歉，并承诺纠正这种情况。
