half-criticalness:/ˌhæf krɪtɪkəlnɪs/ | n. | 半批评性；略带批评的态度 | A state of being moderately or partially critical; a disposition to find some fault but not be overly critical. | Her half-criticalness of the performance was appreciated; it was honest without being harsh. 她对表演略带批评的态度受到赞赏；这是诚实的，但并不苛刻。| 近义词: mild criticism, measured disapproval, qualified approval | 反义词:  wholehearted praise, enthusiastic endorsement, uncritical acceptance | 词性: noun

Etymology:
half- (Old English healf) + criticalness (critical (from Late Latin criticus) + -ness (Old English -nes))
