chutes:/ʃuːts/ | n. | 斜槽，滑道，瀑布 | A sloping channel or slide for conveying things to a lower level.  Also, a waterfall. | The laundry chutes make it easy to get dirty clothes downstairs.  洗衣滑槽可以轻松地将脏衣服送到楼下。

The kayaker navigated the dangerous chutes of the river. 皮划艇运动员在危险的河道中航行。 |  slides, channels, ramps, waterfalls, cascades |climbs, ascents | noun
