concolorous: /kənˈkʌlərəs/ | adj. | 同色的，单色的 | Of the same color throughout; uniformly colored. Having the same color. |  The concolorous plumage of the bird made it difficult to spot against the similarly colored foliage.  这只鸟的单色羽毛使它很难在颜色相似的树叶中被发现。| The artist used a concolorous palette of blues for the sky.  这位艺术家用了一系列同色系的蓝色来描绘天空。| synonyms: monochromatic, uniform, self-colored, solid-colored | antonyms: multicolored, variegated, polychromatic | adjective
