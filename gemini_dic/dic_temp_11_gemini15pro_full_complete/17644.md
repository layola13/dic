Bechdel: /ˈbɛkdəl/ (US), /ˈbɛktəl/ (UK)| adjective, proper noun (often used as adjective)|贝克德尔|Refers to the Bechdel test, a measure of the representation of women in fiction. It asks whether a work features at least two women who talk to each other about something other than a man.|The movie easily passes the Bechdel test.| 近义词: feminist, representation test| 反义词: N/A| adjective, proper noun
