karat:/ˈkærət/ /ˈkærɪt/| n.|克拉（黄金纯度的单位）; 开（宝石的重量单位）|A unit of fineness for gold equal to 1/24 part of pure gold.  A unit of weight for precious stones, equal to 200 milligrams.|This ring is made of 18-karat gold.  这枚戒指是由18克拉黄金制成的。|The diamond weighs three karats. 这颗钻石重三克拉。|Synonym: carat (Note: 'carat' is the more common spelling for the weight of gemstones)|Antonym: N/A|noun


karats:/ˈkærəts/ /ˈkærɪts/| n.|克拉（黄金纯度的单位，复数）; 开（宝石的重量单位，复数）|Plural form of karat.|These earrings are 24 karats.  这些耳环是24克拉的。|How many karats are these diamonds? 这些钻石是多少克拉的？|Synonym: carats|Antonym: N/A|noun
