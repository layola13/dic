blipping:/ˈblɪpɪŋ/| n. v.| 短促的信号；暂时消失；快速移动；忽明忽暗|A momentary flashing or beep, often indicating a signal or location. Also refers to a brief period of disappearance or a quick movement. |The radar screen showed a blip, then it was gone. 雷达屏幕上出现了一个光点，然后消失了。|Synonyms: flash, beep, flicker, glitch, blink, dart, zip|Antonyms: continuous, steady, permanent|noun
