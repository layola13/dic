aerogramme: /ˈɛərəɡræm/ /ˈeɪrəɡræm/
noun
航空信，航空邮件纸
A sheet of lightweight paper that folds into an envelope and is sent by airmail.
She sent an aerogramme to her family while she was traveling abroad. 她出国旅行时，用航空信给她家人寄了一封信。

aerogramme: /ˈɛərəɡræm/ /ˈeɪrəɡræm/
noun
航空邮件
Mail sent by aerogramme.
The aerogramme arrived within a week. 航空邮件一周内就到了。

Etymology:  From French *aérogramme*, from *aéro-* (from Greek *aḗr* 'air') + *-gramme* (from Greek *grámma* 'something written or drawn').

Synonyms: air letter, airmail letter

Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  One could consider "surface mail" as an antonym in terms of delivery method.)
