overgloomy:/ˌoʊvərˈɡluːmi/
adj.
过于忧郁的，过度悲伤的
Excessively gloomy; extremely depressed or pessimistic.
The overgloomy weather reflected his mood. 天气过于阴沉，反映了他的心情。
The film's overgloomy atmosphere made me want to leave. 电影过于悲伤的氛围让我想要离开。
近义词:melancholy, despondent, dejected, pessimistic
反义词:cheerful, optimistic, joyful, lighthearted
词性:形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "gloomy" (adjective meaning "dark or poorly lit, especially so as to appear depressing or frightening; causing or feeling depression or despondency").
