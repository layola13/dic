ining:/ˈaɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 内衬; 衬里 | A layer of material placed inside something else, typically for warmth, comfort, or protection. | The coat had a warm fur lining. 这件外套有一个温暖的毛皮衬里。| lining | exterior, covering | noun


Etymology: From Middle English *linyng, *lynynge, equivalent to line +‎ -ing.  Ultimately from Latin *līnea (“line, string”).
