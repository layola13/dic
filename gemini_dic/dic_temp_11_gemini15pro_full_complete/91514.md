hyoganoidei:/ˌhaɪoʊɡəˈnɔɪdiˌaɪ/
|n.|硬鳞鱼类|A group of ray-finned fishes characterized by ganoid scales.  These are bony, diamond-shaped scales. This group includes gars and bowfins.|The hyoganoidei are an ancient group of fish.|近义词:ganoid fishes|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially teleosts as a contrasting group)|noun
