bestow:/bɪˈstoʊ/
verb
授予，给予；放置，安放
to give something as a gift or honor, or to put something in a particular place
The Queen bestowed him with a knighthood. 女王授予他骑士爵位。

He bestowed a loving glance upon his wife. 他深情地看了他的妻子一眼。

Synonyms: confer, grant, award, present, give
Antonyms: withhold, deprive, refuse, take, remove
词性: 动词
