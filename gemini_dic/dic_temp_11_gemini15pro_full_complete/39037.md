conflictions:/kənˈflɪkʃənz/| n. |冲突，矛盾|A situation in which two or more ideas, beliefs, or opinions are incompatible or contradictory.|The report revealed deep conflictions within the organization.|近义词：disagreements, clashes, disputes, contradictions|反义词：agreements, harmonies, accords|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *conflicten*, from Latin *conflictus*, past participle of *confligere* "to strike together, contend, fight."
