brainstormings:/ˈbreɪnˌstɔːrmɪŋz/
noun
头脑风暴，集体讨论；集思广益
the process of getting a group of people together to think of new ideas
例句：We had a brainstorming session to come up with a new marketing campaign. 我们举行了一次头脑风暴会议，想出一个新的营销方案。

例句：The brainstorming session generated several innovative ideas.  这次头脑风暴会议产生了一些创新想法。

近义词：idea generation, discussion, collaboration, deliberation
反义词：procrastination, inaction
