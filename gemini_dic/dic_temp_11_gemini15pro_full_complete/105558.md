kimonoed:/kɪˈmoʊnoʊd/
adjective
穿着和服的
dressed in a kimono
The kimonoed woman walked gracefully through the garden. 穿着和服的女子优雅地走过花园。
近义词:gowned, robed
反义词: undressed, naked
词性: adjective

词源:  From kimono + -ed.  "Kimono" itself comes from Japanese 着物 (kimono), literally "wearing thing," from 着る (kiru, "to wear") + 物 (mono, "thing").
