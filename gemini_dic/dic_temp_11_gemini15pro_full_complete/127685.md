negational:/nɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 否定的，反面的 | Relating to or expressing negation; denying something. |  The negational statement contradicted the previous claim. 否定的陈述与之前的声明相矛盾。|  The lawyer presented a negational argument. 律师提出了一个反面的论点。 | 近义词: negative, denying, contradictory | 反义词: affirmative, positive, confirming | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *negationalis, from negatio (“denial”), from negare (“to deny”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₁neḱ- (“not”).
