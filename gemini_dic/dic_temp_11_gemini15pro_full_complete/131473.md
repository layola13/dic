noncohesiveness:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhiːsɪvnəs/
noun
不凝聚力；不粘结性；不内聚性
Lack of cohesiveness; inability to stick or hold together, literally or figuratively.
The noncohesiveness of the group ultimately led to its downfall.  这个团队缺乏凝聚力，最终导致了它的垮台。
The noncohesiveness of the sand made it impossible to build a sandcastle. 沙子的不粘结性使得建造沙堡成为不可能。
incoherence, disunity, fragmentation
cohesion, unity, solidarity
noun
