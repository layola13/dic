key-up:/ˈkiː ʌp/| phrasal verb | 键入，输入；启动，准备 | To type something into a computer or other device; to make something ready to start. |  He keyed up the information into the database. 他把信息输入了数据库。 |近义词: input, enter, type, prepare | 反义词: delete, erase | verb

key-up:/ˈkiː ʌp/| adjective | 紧张的，兴奋的 |  Feeling excited or nervous, usually before an important event. |  She was keyed up before her big presentation.  她在重要的演讲前感到很紧张。|近义词: nervous, excited, tense, anxious | 反义词: relaxed, calm | adjective
