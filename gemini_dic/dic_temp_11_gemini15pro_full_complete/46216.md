cysticercus:/ˌsɪstɪˈsɜːrkəs/ | n. |囊尾蚴，囊虫 |The larval stage of a tapeworm, consisting of a fluid-filled sac containing the invaginated scolex.  |  The pig was infected with cysticerci. 这头猪感染了囊尾蚴。 | synonym: bladder worm | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a biological term like this) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cysticercus, from Ancient Greek kystikerkos (κυστίκερκος), from kystis (κύστις) "bladder, cyst" + kerkos (κέρκος) "tail."
