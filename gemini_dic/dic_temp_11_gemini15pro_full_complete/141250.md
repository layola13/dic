opacular:/oʊˈpækjələr/| adj.|不透明的，浑浊的|Resembling an opaque spot or covered by an operculum.  Similar to or resembling an operculum; serving as an operculum. |The opacular membrane prevented light from entering the fish's gill chamber.  这层不透明的膜阻止了光线进入鱼的鳃腔。|The bacterial suspension was opacular, making it difficult to see through.  细菌悬液是不透明的，难以看穿。|Synonyms: opaque, cloudy, turbid, nontransparent |Antonyms: transparent, clear, translucent|adjective
