microanalysis:/ˌmaɪkroʊəˈnælɪsɪs/| n. |微量分析；显微分析|The microanalysis of the sample revealed traces of arsenic. 样品的微量分析显示出痕量的砷。|例句：The microanalysis of the soil helped determine its nutrient content.  土壤的微量分析帮助确定了它的营养成分。

例句：Microanalysis is crucial in forensic science for identifying minute particles. 在法医学中，微量分析对于识别微小颗粒至关重要。|近义词：microscopy, minute analysis|反义词:macroanalysis|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + analysis.  "micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Analysis" comes from Greek "analusis"  meaning  "a breaking up, loosening, releasing".
