autodigestion:/ˌɔːtəʊdaɪˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 自体消化，自我消化 | The process by which an organism's tissues are broken down by its own enzymes. |  Pancreatitis can lead to autodigestion of the pancreas. 胰腺炎会导致胰腺的自我消化。


Autodigestion can occur after death.  死后会发生自体消化。
