crank-down:/ˈkræŋk daʊn/
crank-down|n.|强制执行，取缔，压制|The government imposed a crank-down on illegal immigration. 政府对非法移民进行了强制取缔。|例句：The police initiated a crank-down on drug trafficking in the city. 警察在该市对贩毒活动展开了打击。

crank-down|v.|强制执行，取缔，压制|The authorities are cranking down on unauthorized street vendors. 当局正在取缔未经授权的街头小贩。|例句：The school is cranking down on student absences. 学校正在严格管理学生的缺勤。

crank-down|adj.|强制性的，压制的|The new crank-down measures have been met with resistance.  新的压制措施遭到了抵制。|例句：The crank-down policies on free speech are concerning. 对言论自由的压制政策令人担忧。


近义词:clampdown, crackdown, suppression
反义词:liberation, deregulation
词性:名词, 动词, 形容词

词源: "crank" (旋转把手) + "down" (向下)，原指通过转动把手向下压紧或收紧某物，引申为压制或取缔。
