heroic:/hɪˈroʊɪk/ (US) /hɪˈrəʊɪk/ (UK)| adj.|英雄的；英勇的；崇高的|Having the characteristics of a hero or heroine; very brave. Showing great courage.|例句：He made a heroic attempt to rescue the drowning child. 他英勇地试图救起溺水的孩子。|例句：Her heroic deeds during the war earned her many medals. 她在战争中的英雄事迹为她赢得了许多奖章。|近义词：brave, courageous, valiant, daring, bold|反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle French *héroïque* and directly from Late Latin *heroicus*, from Latin *heros* ("hero") + *-icus*. Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἥρως* (*hḗrōs*, “hero, warrior”).
