hectoringly:/ˈhɛktərɪŋli/| adv.|威吓地，专横地，盛气凌人地|In a bullying or domineering way, often by nagging or scolding.  Derived from the name "Hector," a Trojan warrior known for his strength and courage, but often portrayed as a bully in later works.|She spoke to the staff hectoringly, demanding immediate results. 她用威吓的语气对员工说话，要求立即出结果。|例句：He hectoringly demanded that she complete the task immediately.  他气势汹汹地要求她立即完成任务。

例句：She hectoringly criticized his every move, making him feel incompetent. 她对他的每一个举动都横加指责，让他觉得自己很无能。|近义词：bullyingly, domineeringly, overbearingly, tyrannically|反义词: gently, kindly, politely|词性: 副词
