continued:/kənˈtɪnjud/| adj. v. |继续的，持续的| Carrying on after an interruption; prolonged. |He continued to work even though he was tired. 即使很累，他仍然继续工作。|Synonyms:  resumed,  proceeded,  persisted,  extended,  prolonged| Antonyms: ceased, stopped, halted, discontinued, interrupted|Adjective


Etymology: From Middle English *continuen*, from Old French *continuer*, from Latin *continuāre* (“to join together, make continuous”), from *continuus* (“continuous”).
