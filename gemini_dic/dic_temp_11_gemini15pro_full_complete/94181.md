imperativeness:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪvnəs/| n.|必要性，紧迫性，强制性|The imperativeness of the situation demanded immediate action.  形势的紧迫性要求立即采取行动。| The imperativeness of evacuating the building was clear.  撤离大楼的必要性显而易见。|近义词：urgency, necessity, obligation|反义词:unimportance, optionality|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From imperative + -ness.  来自 imperative（必要的） + -ness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
