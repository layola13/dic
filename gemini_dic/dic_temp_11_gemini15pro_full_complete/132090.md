noncreditably:/nɑːnˈkrɛdɪtəbli/
adverb
不值得称道地，不光彩地
in a manner that does not bring credit or honor; disreputably.
He behaved noncreditably during the meeting, interrupting and insulting other attendees. 他在会议上的行为不值得称道，打断并侮辱了其他与会者。
The project was completed noncreditably, with numerous errors and omissions. 这个项目完成得很不光彩，有很多错误和遗漏。
近义词:disgracefully, shamefully, dishonorably
反义词:creditably, honorably, respectably
词性:adverb

词源:
From non- (not) + creditably (in a manner deserving of credit).  "Credit" ultimately derives from the Latin "creditum," meaning "something entrusted, a loan."
