outcourts:/ˈaʊtkɔːrts/
n.
外围球场；室外球场
Tennis outcourts are usually less prestigious than the main show courts.  网球外围球场通常不如主要的表演场地那么有名望。
The outcourts were packed with spectators watching the qualifying matches. 外围球场挤满了观看资格赛的观众。
synonyms: outside courts, secondary courts
antonyms: center court, main court, show court
