lobeless:/ˈloʊbləs/| adj.|无裂片的，无耳垂的|Lacking lobes; without lobes.  Specifically referring to a leaf, petal, or other plant part that does not have rounded or projecting divisions.|The lobeless leaves of the plant were a key identifying feature.|近义词:undivided, entire|反义词:lobed, divided|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "lobe" + "-less". "Lobe" comes from Old French "lobe"  meaning "pod, husk", ultimately from Late Latin "lobus" meaning "pod, husk".  "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without".
