diamagnet:/ˈdaɪəˌmæɡnɪt/| n.|抗磁体；反磁质|A material that creates a magnetic field in opposition to an externally applied magnetic field.|Bismuth is a diamagnet. 铋是一种抗磁体。|The sample exhibited strong diamagnetic properties. 该样品表现出很强的抗磁性。|Synonyms: non-magnetic material|Antonyms: paramagnet, ferromagnet|noun

diamagnetic:/ˌdaɪə.mæɡˈnet.ɪk/| adj.|抗磁的；反磁性的|Relating to diamagnetism; having the property of diamagnetism.|The levitating frog demonstrated the diamagnetic properties of water.  悬浮的青蛙展示了水的抗磁性。|Gold is a diamagnetic metal. 金是一种抗磁性金属。|Synonyms: non-magnetic|Antonyms: paramagnetic, ferromagnetic|adjective
