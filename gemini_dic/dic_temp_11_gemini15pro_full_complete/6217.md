ammunition:/ˌæmjuˈnɪʃn/ | n. | 弹药，军火 | Materials used in the discharge of firearms, such as bullets, shells, cartridges, or explosive missiles. Also, figuratively, facts or information used to support an argument or attack a position. | The soldiers were running low on ammunition. 士兵们的弹药快用完了。|  The lawyer gathered all the ammunition she could find to build her case. 律师收集了她能找到的所有信息来支持她的案子。| synonyms: weaponry, ordnance, armament, munitions, materiel,  evidence, arguments | antonyms: peace, disarmament | noun
