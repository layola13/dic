iglesias:/iˈɡlesiəs/ | n. | churches; church (especially in Spanish-speaking contexts) | A building used for public Christian worship. Derived from the Spanish word "iglesia," ultimately from the Latin "ecclesia" meaning "assembly" or "church." |  The small town had two iglesias. 这个小镇有两座教堂。| synonyms: churches, chapels, cathedrals | antonyms:  None (lack of a direct opposite for a physical building) | noun
