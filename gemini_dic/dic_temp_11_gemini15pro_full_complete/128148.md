netted:/ˈnɛtɪd/| adj. v.|用网覆盖的；净赚的|Covered with a net; having gained a particular amount of money as clear profit after deductions.|The company netted a profit of $1 million. 这家公司净赚了100万美元。|Synonyms: covered, earned, gained|Antonyms: lost, gross|adjective, verb

netted:/ˈnɛtɪd/| v.|净赚；用网捕；(足球)踢进球门|To earn or receive as clear profit after deductions; to catch with a net; (in soccer, etc.) to kick or hit (the ball) into the goal|He netted a small fortune from the sale. 他从这次出售中净赚了一小笔钱。|Synonyms: earned, gained, captured|Antonyms: lost, forfeited|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From net + -ed.  "Net" itself comes from Old English *nett, from Proto-Germanic *natjan, of uncertain origin.
