candelabra: /ˌkændəˈlɑːbrə/ (singular), /ˌkændəˈlɑːbrə/ or /ˌkændəˈlɑːbriː/ (plural)| n. |枝形烛台；烛台|A large, branched holder for several candles or lamps.|The dining room was lit by a magnificent silver candelabra. 餐厅里点着一盏华丽的银质烛台。|Synonyms: chandelier, candlestick, sconce|Antonyms: lamp, lightbulb|Noun

candelabras: /ˌkændəˈlɑːbrəz/ or /ˌkændəˈlɑːbriː/ | n. |枝形烛台；烛台（复数）|Plural form of candelabra.|The candelabras on the mantelpiece added a touch of elegance to the room. 壁炉架上的烛台给房间增添了一丝优雅。|Synonyms: chandeliers, candlesticks, sconces|Antonyms: lamps, lightbulbs|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *candelabro*, from *candela* "candle" (from Latin *candēla*) + *-bro* (from Latin *-brum*, a suffix denoting an instrument).
