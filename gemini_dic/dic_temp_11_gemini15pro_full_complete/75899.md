fundholding:/ˈfʌndˌhəʊldɪŋ/| n.|基金持有; 基金管理; 预算持有|The practice of allocating budgets to organizations, particularly in healthcare, allowing them to manage their own finances.|The hospital gained greater autonomy under the new fundholding scheme. 这家医院在新基金持有计划下获得了更大的自主权。|Synonyms: budget management, budget control, financial management|Antonyms: centralized budgeting|noun
