epiphyte:/ˈɛpɪˌfaɪt/ | n. | 附生植物 | A plant that grows on another plant, but is not parasitic, deriving its moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. |  The orchid is an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree branches high in the rainforest canopy.  这株兰花是一种附生植物，附着在雨林树冠高处的树枝上。


Epiphytes, such as certain orchids and bromeliads, thrive in the humid rainforest environment.  某些兰花和凤梨等附生植物在潮湿的雨林环境中茁壮成长。| 近义词: air plant | 反义词: terrestrial plant | 词性: noun
