diverging:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒɪŋ/| adj.|分叉的，岔开的，相异的，背道而驰的|moving, going, or extending in different directions from a common point; becoming or making something become different|The diverging paths led to two different villages. 这条岔路通往两个不同的村庄。|synonyms: separating, branching, forking, parting, splitting|antonyms: converging, meeting, uniting, intersecting, merging|adjective


diverge:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒ/| v.|分叉，岔开，相异，背道而驰|to move, go, or extend in different directions from a common point; to become or make something become different|The paths diverged at the fork in the road. 路径在岔路口分叉。|synonyms: separate, branch, fork, part, split|antonyms: converge, meet, unite, intersect, merge|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dīvergere* "to bend apart, turn aside," from *dī-* "apart, asunder" + *vergere* "to bend, incline."
