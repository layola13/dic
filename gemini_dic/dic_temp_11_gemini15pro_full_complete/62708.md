engineless:/ˈendʒɪnlɪs/
adjective
无引擎的；无发动机的
lacking an engine.
The engineless glider soared silently through the air.  这架无引擎的滑翔机静静地在空中翱翔。
The museum displayed several engineless aircraft, including early gliders.  博物馆展出了几架无引擎的飞机，包括早期的滑翔机。
Synonyms:  unpowered, motorless
Antonyms:  powered, motorized
詞性: adjective
