gonozooid:/ˌɡɒnəˈzoʊɔɪd/ /ˌɡɑːnəˈzoʊɔɪd/| n. |生殖个体，生殖体|A member of a hydroid colony that produces gametes (sex cells).  It is a specialized individual within the colony responsible for sexual reproduction. |例句：The gonozooids release sperm and eggs into the water for fertilization. 生殖体将精子和卵子释放到水中进行受精。

例句：The colony consisted of feeding individuals and gonozooids. 这个群体由摄食个体和生殖个体组成。|近义词：reproductive polyp, gonophore|反义词:None (No direct opposite exists for a specialized biological term.)|词性:noun
