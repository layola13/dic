barak: /ˈbɑːræk/ (Hebrew: בָּרַךְ)| v. |祝福，赐福|To bless; to invoke divine favor upon someone or something.  Derived from the Hebrew word בָּרַךְ (bārak).|May God barak you with good health. 愿上帝保佑你身体健康。|The priest baraked the congregation. 牧师祝福了会众。|近义词: bless, consecrate|反义词: curse|词性: verb
