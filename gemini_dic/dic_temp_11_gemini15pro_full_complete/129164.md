no-smoking: /ˌnoʊ ˈsməʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 禁止吸烟的 |  A compound adjective meaning that smoking is not allowed in a particular place. It's formed by the adverb "no" and the present participle "smoking". |  There is a no-smoking sign in the restaurant. 餐厅里有一个禁止吸烟的标志。|  The hospital has a strict no-smoking policy.  这家医院有严格的禁烟政策。| 近义词: nonsmoking, smoke-free | 反义词: smoking-permitted | 词性: adjective

no-smoking: /ˌnoʊ ˈsməʊkɪŋ/ | n. | 禁烟 | The prohibition of smoking, typically indicated by a sign or policy. | The no-smoking rule is strictly enforced here. 这里的禁烟规定严格执行。|  The no-smoking area is clearly marked. 禁烟区有明确的标识。| 近义词: smoking ban, prohibition of smoking | 反义词: smoking allowance | 词性: noun
