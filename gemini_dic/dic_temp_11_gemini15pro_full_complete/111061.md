listenability:/ˌlɪsənəˈbɪləti/| n.|可听性，易听度|The quality of being easy and pleasant to listen to.  It refers to how agreeable and engaging audio content is for a listener.|This podcast has high listenability due to the host's soothing voice and interesting stories.  这个播客由于主持人的舒缓的声音和有趣的故事而具有很高的可听性。|近义词：audibility, accessibility, appeal|反义词：unpleasantness, jarringness|词性: noun
