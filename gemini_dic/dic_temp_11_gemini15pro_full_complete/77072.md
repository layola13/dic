garotte:/ɡəˈrɒt/ /ɡəˈrɑːt/| n. vt.|绞杀，勒死，扼杀; 绞索，勒索|A garotte is a weapon, usually a cord or wire, used to strangle someone.  绞索是一种武器，通常是一根绳索或金属丝，用来勒死人。|The victim was garotted with a piece of wire. 受害者被一根金属丝勒死了。|近义词：strangle, choke, suffocate|反义词:resuscitate, revive|词性:名词,动词

garotte:/ɡəˈrɒt/ /ɡəˈrɑːt/| vt.|勒索，敲诈|To extort money from someone by threats or violence. 通过威胁或暴力向某人勒索钱财。|They garotted the businessman for a million dollars. 他们勒索了商人一百万美元。|近义词：extort, blackmail|反义词:donate, give|词性:动词
