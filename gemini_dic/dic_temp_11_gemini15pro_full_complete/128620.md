nickel-and-dime: /ˌnɪkəl ən ˈdaɪm/ | v. |斤斤计较; 为小钱争执；在小事上精打细算; |To treat someone unfairly in order to make small amounts of money, or to haggle over small sums.|  The landlord is always nickel-and-diming us about the rent.  房东总是在房租上跟我们斤斤计较。| 近义词: scrimp, economize, pinch pennies, haggle, quibble | 反义词: splurge, be generous | 词性: verb

nickel-and-diming: /ˌnɪkəl ən ˈdaɪmɪŋ/ | n. |斤斤计较的行为; 为小钱争执的行为；在小事上精打细算的行为| The act of being frugal or stingy, especially with small amounts of money.| All this nickel-and-diming is getting ridiculous.  所有这些斤斤计较的行为都变得荒谬了。| 近义词: haggling, quibbling, penny-pinching | 反义词: generosity, extravagance | 词性: noun
