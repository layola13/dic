disciplinarians:/ˌdɪsəplɪˈnɛəriənz/
n.|训导员，纪律执行者|People who believe in or enforce strict discipline.  Those who maintain order and obedience to rules.|The school employed strict disciplinarians to maintain order in the classrooms. 学校雇佣了严格的训导员来维持课堂秩序。|Synonyms: enforcers, regulators, sticklers, taskmasters|Antonyms: permissives, libertarians|noun

discipline:/ˈdɪsəplɪn/
n.|纪律，学科，训练|A system of rules of conduct. A branch of knowledge or learning.|The teacher maintained discipline in the classroom. 老师维持了课堂纪律。|Synonyms: order, control, training, instruction|Antonyms: chaos, disorder, neglect|noun
discipline:/ˈdɪsəplɪn/
vt.|训练，惩罚|To train someone to follow rules or a code of behavior. To punish someone for wrongdoing.|The coach disciplined the players for breaking curfew. 教练因为球员违反宵禁而惩罚他们。|Synonyms: train, punish, correct, chastise|Antonyms: reward, praise, indulge|verb
