discontentful:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntfʊl/
adjective
不满的；不满足的
Characterized by discontent; dissatisfied.
The discontentful workers went on strike. 不满的工人们举行了罢工。
近义词:discontented, dissatisfied, malcontent, disgruntled
反义词:content, satisfied, happy, pleased
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From discontent + -ful.  Discontent itself comes from the Old French descontentement, ultimately from Latin dis- "not" + contentus "content."
