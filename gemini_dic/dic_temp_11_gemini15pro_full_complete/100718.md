intramedullary:/ˌɪntrəˈmɛdʌˌlɛri/ | adj. | 髓内的；骨髓内的 | Situated within the medulla or bone marrow. |  An intramedullary rod was inserted to stabilize the fractured femur.  一根髓内棒被插入以稳定骨折的股骨。 | Synonyms: intraosseous, endosteal | Antonyms: extramedullary | adjective

intramedullary:/ˌɪntrəˈmɛdʌˌlɛri/ | n. | 髓内钉；髓内装置 | A rod or device inserted into the medullary cavity of a bone. | The surgeon opted for an intramedullary nail to fix the broken tibia.  外科医生选择髓内钉来固定断裂的胫骨。| Synonyms: medullary nail, bone nail | Antonyms: external fixator | noun
