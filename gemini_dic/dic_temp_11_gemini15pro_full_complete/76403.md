gaganaw:/ɡʌɡənɔː/ | n. | astronaut, cosmonaut |  A person trained to travel in spacecraft. |  Example: The gaganaw skillfully piloted the spacecraft.  这位宇航员熟练地驾驶着宇宙飞船。| Synonym: astronaut, cosmonaut | Antonym: N/A | Noun


Etymology: From Hindi गगनयात्री (gaganayātrī), from गगन (gagan, “sky”) + यात्री (yātrī, “traveller”). Ultimately from Sanskrit गगन (gagana) and यात्रिन् (yātrin).
