aforecited:/əˈfɔːrˈsaɪtɪd/
adjective
前述的，上述的
mentioned earlier in a text or speech.
The aforementioned article discusses this issue in detail.  上述文章详细讨论了这个问题。
The costs, as aforecited, are prohibitive.  如前所述，费用高得令人望而却步。
synonym: aforementioned, aforesaid, previously mentioned
antonym: following, subsequent
词性: adjective
