inexpiableness:/ˌɪnɪkˈspiːəbəlnəs/
n.
不可原谅；不可解释；无法辩解
The quality or state of being inexpiable; inability to be expiated or atoned for.
His inexpiable crime led to a life sentence.  他不可饶恕的罪行导致了终身监禁。
The inexpiable mystery of the missing jewels haunted the family for years.  丢失珠宝的无法解释的谜团困扰了这个家庭多年。
近义词: unforgivableness, unpardonableness
反义词: forgivableness, pardonableness
词性: noun
