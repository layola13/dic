liquefying:/ˈlɪkwɪˌfaɪɪŋ/| v. |液化，使液化|The process of liquefying gas involves cooling it to extremely low temperatures.  将气体液化的过程包括将其冷却到极低的温度。|例句：The intense heat was liquefying the metal.  强烈的热量正在熔化金属。

例句：They are liquefying the natural gas for easier transportation. 他们正在将天然气液化以便于运输。

近义词：melting, dissolving, thawing|反义词：solidifying, freezing|词性：动词 (present participle of liquefy)


词源: From late Middle English: from late Latin liquefacere, from Latin lique- ‘liquid’ + facere ‘to make.’
