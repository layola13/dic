captive:/ˈkæptɪv/ (US) /ˈkæptɪv/ (UK)| adj. n.|被俘虏的，囚禁的；俘虏|Someone or something that is captive is being held prisoner or is trapped in a place they cannot escape from.  A captive animal is kept in a zoo or park.|The soldiers were held captive for three months.  这些士兵被俘虏了三个月。|Synonyms: imprisoned, confined, jailed, captured, enslaved|Antonyms: free, liberated, released|adjective

captive:/ˈkæptɪv/ (US) /ˈkæptɪv/ (UK)| n.|俘虏|A person or animal held prisoner.|The captives were released unharmed.  俘虏们被安然无恙地释放了。|Synonyms: prisoner, detainee, hostage|Antonyms: captor, guard, jailer|noun
