jabber:/ˈdʒæbər/| v. n.|喋喋不休，快而含糊不清地说；急促而含糊不清的话|To talk rapidly and excitedly but without making much sense; rapid, unclear talk.|He jabbered away in a foreign language. 他用一种外语喋喋不休。|近义词：chatter, babble, prattle|反义词:articulate, enunciate|词性:动词,名词
jabber:/ˈdʒæbər/| n.|快而含糊不清的话|Rapid, unclear talk.|I couldn't understand a word of his jabber. 我一句也听不懂他含糊不清的话。|近义词：chatter, babble, prattle|反义词:articulation, enunciation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From perhaps imitative of the sound of rapid talk.  Possibly related to gabble.
