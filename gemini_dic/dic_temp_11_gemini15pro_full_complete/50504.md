deprivable:/dɪˈpraɪvəbl/
adjective
可剥夺的; 可被夺走的
able to be taken away from someone or something.
例句：Citizens are protected from having their basic rights deprivable. 公民的基本权利受保护，不会被剥夺。

例句：His driving license is deprivable if he gets another speeding ticket. 如果他再收到一张超速罚单，他的驾照可能会被吊销。

近义词：removable, revocable, forfeitable, withdrawable
反义词：inalienable, unalienable, non-forfeitable

词性: adjective
词源: From deprive + -able.  Deprive comes from Old French depriver, from Latin deprivare "to bereave, rob, dispossess," from de- "away" + privare "to deprive," from privus "one's own, private."
