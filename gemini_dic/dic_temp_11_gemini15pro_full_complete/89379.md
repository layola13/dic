homophone:/ˈhɒməfəʊn/ (UK) /ˈhɑːməfoʊn/ (US)| n.|同音词|A word that is pronounced the same as another word but differs in meaning, and may also differ in spelling.|The words "too" and "two" are homophones.|近义词: homonym|反义词: heteronym|词性:noun
