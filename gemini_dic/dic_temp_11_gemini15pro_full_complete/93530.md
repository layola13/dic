imaginable:/ɪˈmædʒɪnəbl/| adj.|可以想象的，可能的|Possible to be imagined or conceived.|例句：Every imaginable difficulty arose.  出现了所有可以想象的困难。|例句：The hotel offers every imaginable comfort. 这家酒店提供了所有可以想象的舒适设施。|近义词：conceivable, possible, thinkable|反义词：inconceivable, unimaginable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *imaginable, from Late Latin imaginabilis (“that can be imagined”), from Latin imaginari (“to imagine, form an image, picture to oneself”), from imago (“image, likeness, form, appearance”), from Proto-Indo-European *ey- (“to go”).
