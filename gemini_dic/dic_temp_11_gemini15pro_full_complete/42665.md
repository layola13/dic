counterstrategists:/ˌkaʊntərˈstrætədʒɪsts/
|n.|反战略家，对策制定者
|People who devise counterstrategies; those who plan and implement actions to neutralize or overcome an opponent's strategy.|
|The counterstrategists spent weeks analyzing the enemy's plans and developing a response.| 反战略家们花了数周时间分析敌人的计划并制定应对措施。

|The team of counterstrategists worked tirelessly to anticipate the competitor's next move.|  反战略家团队不知疲倦地工作，以预测竞争对手的下一步行动。
|Synonyms: tacticians, strategists, planners|
|Antonyms: followers, reactors|
|noun|

Etymology:
counter- (prefix): against, opposite  (from Latin "contra")
strategist (noun):  a person skilled in strategy (from Greek "stratēgos," meaning "general")
