chop-down:/ˌtʃɒp ˈdaʊn/ (UK), /ˌtʃɑːp ˈdaʊn/ (US)| adj. |砍倒的；砍伐的|An adjective describing something that has been felled by chopping, typically a tree.|The chop-down Christmas tree filled the room with a festive scent. 那棵砍倒的圣诞树让房间里充满了节日的气息。|近义词：felled, cut down|反义词:standing, upright|词性:形容词

chop-down:/ˌtʃɒp ˈdaʊn/ (UK), /ˌtʃɑːp ˈdaʊn/ (US)| v. |砍倒；砍伐|To fell something by chopping, usually a tree.|They chopped down the tree to make way for a new building.  他们砍倒了那棵树，以便为新建筑腾出空间。|近义词：fell, cut down|反义词:plant|词性:动词


词源:  From the verb "chop" (meaning to cut with repeated blows) and the adverb "down" (indicating direction towards the ground).
