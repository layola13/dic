fourling: /ˈfɔːrlɪŋ/ | n. | 四胞胎之一；四倍 | One of four offspring born at the same birth; something fourfold. |  Example: Each fourling was surprisingly healthy despite being premature.  每个四胞胎都出奇地健康，尽管是早产儿。

Example: The farmer proudly displayed the fourling lambs. 农民自豪地展示了四胞胎羊羔。| 近义词: quadruplet | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From four + -ling (a diminutive suffix). 源自 four + -ling（指小后缀）。
