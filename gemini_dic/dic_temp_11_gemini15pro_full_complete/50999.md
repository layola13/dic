desirousnesses:/dɪˈzaɪrəsnɪsɪz/
n.
渴望，愿望，欲望
The state of having or expressing desires, especially multiple or strong desires.
His desirousnesses for fame and fortune ultimately led to his downfall. 他对名利和财富的渴望最终导致了他的垮台。
Synonyms: desires, longings, yearnings, cravings, hankerings
Antonyms: apathy, indifference, contentment
