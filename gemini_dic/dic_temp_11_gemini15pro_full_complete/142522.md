ossiculated:/ˈɒsɪkjʊleɪtɪd/ /ˈɑːsɪkjəleɪtɪd/
adjective
骨化的；变成骨的
Having been converted to bone; bony.
The ossiculated skeleton of the ancient fish was remarkably well-preserved.  这条古代鱼的骨化骨骼保存得非常完好。
ossified, hardened, calcified
fleshy, cartilaginous
词性: 形容词
