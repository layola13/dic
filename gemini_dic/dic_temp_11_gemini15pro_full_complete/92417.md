hypogynous:/ˌhaɪpəˈdʒaɪnəs/
adj.
下位的，子房上位花的
(of a flower) having the other floral parts inserted below the gynoecium, especially on a convex receptacle.
The flower is hypogynous, meaning the sepals, petals, and stamens are attached below the ovary.  这朵花是下位花的，意思是萼片、花瓣和雄蕊都着生于子房下方。
The lily has a hypogynous flower. 百合花是子房上位花。
近义词: superior (ovary)
反义词: epigynous, perigynous
词性: adjective
