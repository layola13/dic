certificator:/sərˈtɪfɪkeɪtər/ |n.| 认证员，鉴定人 |A person who certifies something; one who grants a certificate. |The certificator verified the authenticity of the document. 认证员核实了文件的真实性。|Synonyms: certifier, authenticator, validator|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "falsifier," "fraudster" as conceptually opposite) |Noun
