ill-disposedness:/ˌɪl dɪˈspoʊzdnəs/
noun
不友好；恶意；敌意；不情愿
The state of being ill-disposed; unwillingness; reluctance; malevolence.
His ill-disposedness towards the project was obvious from the start. 他从一开始就对这个项目表现出不情愿。
Near synonyms: hostility, animosity, antagonism, antipathy, malevolence, unkindness, unfriendliness, malice, spite, venom, acrimony, resentment, rancor, ill will, bad blood, aversion
Antonyms: benevolence, goodwill, kindness, friendliness
n.
