blowby:/ˈbloʊbaɪ/| n.|窜气；漏气|The escape of combustion gases past the piston rings into the crankcase of an internal combustion engine.|The mechanic noticed significant blowby and determined the piston rings needed replacing. 技工注意到明显的窜气，并确定需要更换活塞环。|Synonyms: leakage, escape, seepage|Antonyms: seal, containment|noun

blowby:/ˈbloʊbaɪ/| n.|冲击波|The sudden rush of air caused by an explosion or rapid movement.|The blowby from the explosion shattered nearby windows. 爆炸产生的冲击波震碎了附近的窗户。|Synonyms: blast, rush, surge|Antonyms: vacuum, stillness|noun
