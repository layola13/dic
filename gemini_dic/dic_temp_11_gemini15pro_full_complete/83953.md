half-consciously:/ˌhæfˈkɒnʃəsli/ (UK) /ˌhæfˈkɑːnʃəsli/ (US)
adverb
半意识地；半清醒地
partly conscious; not fully aware
He half-consciously reached for his phone. 他半意识地伸手去拿手机。
Near synonyms: vaguely, dimly, slightly
Opposite: consciously, fully aware
词性: 副词

half-consciously:/ˌhæfˈkɒnʃəsli/ (UK) /ˌhæfˈkɑːnʃəsli/ (US)
adjective (less common)
处于半昏迷状态的
in a state of partial consciousness
She was in a half-conscious state after the accident. 事故发生后，她处于半昏迷状态。
Near synonyms: semiconscious, dazed
Opposite: conscious, fully aware
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'half' + 'conscious' + '-ly'. 'Half' means one of two equal parts. 'Conscious' comes from Latin 'conscius' meaning "aware, knowing".  '-ly' is a suffix used to form adverbs.
