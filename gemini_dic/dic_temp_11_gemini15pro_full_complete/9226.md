antiparallel:/ˌæntiˈpærəlel/| adj.|反向平行的，反平行的|Running in opposite directions, but parallel.  Two strands are antiparallel if they run parallel to each other but with opposite alignments (directions).|The two strands of a DNA molecule are antiparallel.|Synonyms: oppositely directed, inversely oriented|Antonyms: parallel, aligned|adjective
