dither:/ˈdɪðər/| n.|犹豫，踌躇；抖动，颤动|A state of indecision or trepidation.|He was in a dither about what to do next. 他对下一步该做什么感到不知所措。|Synonyms: hesitation, indecision, vacillation|Antonyms: decision, resolution|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *didderen*, frequentative of an unrecorded verb *diden*, probably of imitative origin. Related to dodder.
