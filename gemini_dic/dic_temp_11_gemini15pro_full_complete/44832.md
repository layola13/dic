crustaceans:/krʌˈsteɪʃənz/ | n. |甲壳类动物|Aquatic arthropods with a hard exoskeleton, typically including crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles.|例句：Crustaceans are a major food source for many people. 甲壳类动物是许多人的主要食物来源。

例句：Many crustaceans live in the ocean. 很多甲壳类动物生活在海洋里。


|近义词：shellfish|反义词: (No direct opposite, but perhaps terrestrial animals) |词性: noun
