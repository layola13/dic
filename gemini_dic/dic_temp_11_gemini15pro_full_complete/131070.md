nonbaryonic:/ˌnɒnbæriˈɒnɪk/
adjective
非重子的
Not composed of baryons (a type of subatomic particle including protons and neutrons which comprise ordinary matter).
Dark matter is believed to be nonbaryonic. 暗物质被认为是非重子的。
Synonyms: non-baryonic
Antonyms: baryonic
词性: adjective
