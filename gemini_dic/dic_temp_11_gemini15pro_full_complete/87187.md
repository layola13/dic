herdsmanship: /ˈhɜːrdzmənʃɪp/ | n. | 牧畜；畜牧业；牧人的技艺 | The art or skill of managing livestock, especially cattle or sheep. | His herdsmanship was renowned throughout the region, his cattle always healthy and productive.  他的牧畜技术闻名整个地区，他的牛总是很健康，产量很高。 | Example: The success of the ranch was due in large part to the excellent herdsmanship of the cowboys.  这个牧场的成功很大程度上归功于牛仔们出色的牧畜技术。| Synonyms:  animal husbandry, stockmanship, shepherding | Antonyms: neglect, mismanagement | noun
