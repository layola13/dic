blowlamp:/ˈbləʊˌlæmp/ (UK) /ˈbloʊˌlæmp/ (US)| n. |喷灯，吹管|A device that produces a very hot flame by mixing air with a flammable gas, typically paraffin (kerosene), and used for soldering, brazing, or heating.|例句：The plumber used a blowlamp to solder the pipes together. 水管工用喷灯把管道焊接在一起。

例句：He used a blowlamp to melt the ice on the driveway. 他用喷灯融化了车道上的冰。|近义词：blowtorch, torch|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
