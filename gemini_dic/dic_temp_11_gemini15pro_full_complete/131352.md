nonchivalrous:/ˌnɒnˈʃɪv(ə)l.rəs/ (US: /ˌnɑːnˈʃɪv(ə)l.rəs/)
adjective
不骑士精神的，无骑士风度的
Not behaving in a way that is traditionally considered polite and kind, especially towards women.
He made a nonchivalrous remark about women drivers. 他对女司机说了一句不绅士的话。

近义词:ungallant, discourteous, rude, impolite, unmannerly
反义词:chivalrous, gallant, courteous, polite

词性:adjective

词源: From non- + chivalrous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Chivalrous" comes from Old French "chevalerie" meaning "knighthood".
