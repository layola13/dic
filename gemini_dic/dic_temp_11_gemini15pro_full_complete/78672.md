gift-vouchers:/ˈɡɪft ˈvaʊtʃəz/| n.|礼券|A gift voucher is a piece of paper that can be exchanged for goods or services up to a specified monetary value. It's often given as a gift.|例句：I received some gift vouchers for my birthday. 我生日收到了一些礼券。

例句：She used her gift vouchers to buy a new dress. 她用她的礼券买了一件新裙子。|近义词：gift certificates, gift cards|反义词:|noun
