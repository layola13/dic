original:/əˈrɪdʒənəl/ | adj. n. | 原始的；原创的；原件；|Something original is not a copy but is created for the first time.  Something original is present from the beginning; first or earliest.| The original painting is worth thousands of dollars. 这幅原画价值数千美元。| 近义词：initial, primary, innovative, creative | 反义词：copied, imitated, unoriginal | 词性: adjective, noun

词源:  From Old French *original, from Late Latin originalis (“originating, primary”), from Latin origo (“origin, source, beginning”).
