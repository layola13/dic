词源:  From Middle English *drouȝthe, *drougthe, from Old English drūgaþ (“dryness, drought”), from Proto-Germanic *drūgaz (“dry”). Cognate with Scots drouth (“thirst, dryness, drought”), West Frisian droechte (“drought”), Dutch droogte (“drought”), German Dürre (“drought, aridity”), Danish tørke (“drought”), Swedish torka (“drought”), Icelandic þurrkur (“drought”).  Related to dry.
