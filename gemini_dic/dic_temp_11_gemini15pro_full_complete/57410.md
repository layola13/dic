dr:/dɔːr/ (BrE) /dɔːr/ (AmE)| abbreviation| 博士;  医生 |Abbreviation for doctor.  Used before a name to indicate that someone is a doctor. |Example: Dr. Smith will see you now. 史密斯医生现在就见你。

Example: I have an appointment with Dr. Lee. 我和李医生有个约会。|Synonym: physician (for medical doctor)|Antonym: N/A (Abbreviations don't typically have antonyms)| abbreviation
