goodwill:/ˈɡʊdˈwɪl/| n.| 商誉；友好；善意|the established reputation of a business regarded as a quantifiable asset and calculated as part of its value when it is sold.  A friendly disposition; kindness.|例句：The company's goodwill is its most valuable asset.  这家公司的商誉是其最有价值的资产。

例句：He bears no ill will towards his former rivals. 他对以前的对手并无恶意。|近义词：benevolence, kindness, amity|反义词： hostility, animosity, malice|词性:名词
