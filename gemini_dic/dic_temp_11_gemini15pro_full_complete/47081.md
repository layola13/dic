datsun:/ˈdætsən/| n. |达特桑|A brand of Japanese automobiles, formerly produced by Nissan.  Originally based on designs licensed from Austin Motor Company in the UK, Datsun became known for its affordable and reliable cars. Nissan phased out the Datsun brand in 1986, but revived it in 2013 for budget-focused vehicles, primarily in emerging markets. The brand was discontinued again in 2022.|He learned to drive in his father's old Datsun.|Synonym: Nissan (in later years), Japanese car|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps American car as a contrasting example)|noun
