countersunk:/ˈkaʊntəˌsʌŋk/ /ˈkaʊntɚˌsʌŋk/| adj. v. n.|埋头的，锥形的；使埋头；埋头孔|A countersunk screw is one that has a conical head that fits into a corresponding conical hole so that the screw head is flush with the surface.|The screws are countersunk for a smooth finish. 这些螺丝是埋头的，以获得光滑的表面。|recessed, embedded|protruding|adjective, verb, noun
countersink:/ˈkaʊntəˌsɪŋk/ /ˈkaʊntɚˌsɪŋk/| n. v.|埋头孔；埋头钻|A countersink is a conical hole cut into a material to accommodate a countersunk screw. |He used a countersink to create the holes for the screws. 他用埋头钻钻孔来装螺丝。|recess|protrusion|noun, verb

词源: counter- (against, opposite) + sunk (past participle of sink)
