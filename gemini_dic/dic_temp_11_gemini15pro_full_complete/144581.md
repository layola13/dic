outwatches:/ˌaʊtˈwɒtʃɪz/
verb
监视得比…久；比…更警觉
observes or stays awake longer than someone else.
He outwatched his rivals and finally saw the rare bird. 他比他的竞争对手观察得更久，最终看到了那只珍稀鸟类。
近义词: outlasts, surpasses,
反义词: loses to, gives up,
