humanest:/ˈhjuːməˌnɪst/ | adj. | 最人道的，最仁慈的 | Showing the most kindness and concern for others; most humane. |  He took the humanest possible course of action. 他采取了最人道的行动。|  The shelter provides the humanest care for abandoned animals. 这家收容所为被遗弃的动物提供最人道的照顾。 | 近义词:  compassionate, merciful, kind, benevolent | 反义词:  cruel, inhumane, barbaric | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From 'humane' + '-est' (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin 'humanus'.
