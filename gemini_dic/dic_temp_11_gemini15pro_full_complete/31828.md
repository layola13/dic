chee:/tʃiː/ | interj. | 嗨；你好 | An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey".  It is primarily used in Singapore and Malaysia. | Chee, how's it going? 嗨，最近怎么样？ | 近义词: hi, hey, hello | 反义词: goodbye, farewell | 词性:感叹词
词源: Believed to be derived from Hokkien Chinese, possibly related to the word "chi̍t" (一), used as a casual greeting.
