helmsman: /ˈhɛlmzmən/ (US), /ˈhɛlmzmən/ (UK)| n. |舵手，掌舵人|A person who steers a ship or boat.|The helmsman steered the ship through the storm. 舵手驾驶着船只穿过风暴。|近义词：steerer, pilot, navigator|反义词: passenger, crew (not direct opposites, but represent different roles on a ship)|noun
