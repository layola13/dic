irruption:/ɪˈrʌpʃən/ | n. | 突然闯入，爆发，喷发 | A sudden, often violent, entrance or outburst; a bursting in or forth. |  Example 1: The irruption of the protestors into the building caught the security guards off guard. 抗议者突然闯入大楼，让保安措手不及。

Example 2:  The volcanic irruption sent ash and lava spewing into the sky. 火山爆发，喷出火山灰和熔岩。 | Synonyms: incursion, invasion, eruption, outburst | Antonyms: retreat, withdrawal | noun
