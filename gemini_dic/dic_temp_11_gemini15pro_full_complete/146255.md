overextravagant: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈstrævəɡənt/ | adj. | 过度奢侈的，过分铺张的 | Spending more than is reasonable or necessary; excessively lavish. |  She threw an overextravagant party for her daughter's sixteenth birthday, complete with a live band and a catered five-course meal. 她为女儿的十六岁生日举办了一个过于奢侈的派对，包括现场乐队和五道菜的餐饮服务。

The overextravagant decorations made the small apartment look cluttered. 过于奢华的装饰使这间小公寓看起来很杂乱。


| 近义词: lavish, extravagant, immoderate, excessive | 反义词: economical, frugal, moderate, thrifty | 词性: adjective
