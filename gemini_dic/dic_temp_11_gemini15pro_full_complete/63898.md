epinikian: /ɛpɪˈnɪkiən/
adjective
赞美诗的；颂歌的；凯旋的
Of, relating to, or composed in the style of an epinikion.  A poem celebrating a victory.
The poet composed an epinikian ode for the victorious athlete.  诗人创作了一首赞美诗来歌颂这位获胜的运动员。
近义词: triumphal, celebratory, laudatory
反义词: mournful, elegiac
词性: adjective
