baria:/ˈbɛəriə/| n.|巴里亚（压力单位）|A unit of pressure equal to 100,000 pascals.  It is named after the Italian physicist Evangelista Torricelli, who invented the barometer. Although not an SI unit, the baria is permitted for use with the SI. |The pressure at the bottom of the ocean was measured in barias. 海底的压力是用巴里亚来测量的。|Synonyms: barye|Antonyms: N/A|noun

baria:/ˈbɛəriə/| n.|重子|An obsolete CGS unit of pressure equal to one dyne per square centimeter. |The old textbook mentioned pressure in barias. 那本旧教科书提到了以巴里亚为单位的压力。|Synonyms: barye|Antonyms: N/A|noun
