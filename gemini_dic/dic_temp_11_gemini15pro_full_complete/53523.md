disastrousness:/dɪˈzæstrəsnəs/
n.|灾难性，灾难的程度|The disastrousness of the flood was evident in the widespread destruction.  这场洪水的灾难性在广泛的破坏中显而易见。|例句：The disastrousness of the hurricane shocked the nation.  飓风的灾难性震惊了全国。|近义词：calamitousness, catastrophism|反义词：fortunateness, auspiciousness|词性:名词

Etymology: From disastrous + -ness.  Disastrous comes from disaster, which ultimately derives from the Old Italian word disastro, meaning "ill-starred" or "unlucky," from dis- "bad" + astro "star."
