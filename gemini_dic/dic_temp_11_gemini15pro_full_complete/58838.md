duplexes:/ˈduːpleksɪz/ /ˈdjuːpleksɪz/
noun
复式公寓；双拼房屋
A duplex is a building divided into two separate dwelling units, either side-by-side or stacked one above the other.
The duplexes on this street are expensive.  这条街上的复式公寓很贵。

近义词:two-family house, semi-detached
反义词:single-family home, detached house
词性:n. (noun)
