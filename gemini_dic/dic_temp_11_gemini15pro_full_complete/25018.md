brutish:/ˈbruːtɪʃ/| adj.|野蛮的；残忍的；粗暴的|Resembling or characteristic of a brute; cruel, savage, or violent.|He gave a brutish laugh. 他发出一阵野蛮的笑声。|近义词：savage, brutal, cruel, barbaric, vicious, inhuman, beastly, animalistic|反义词：gentle, kind, humane, civilized, compassionate|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From brute + -ish.  Brute comes from the Old French word "brute," meaning "dull, insensitive," ultimately from Latin "brutus," meaning "heavy, dull, stupid, irrational."
