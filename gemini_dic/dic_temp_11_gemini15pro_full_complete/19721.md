bilateral:/baɪˈlætərəl/| adj.|双边的，双方的|Involving two sides or parties.  Relating to or affecting two sides of the body.|例句：The two countries signed a bilateral trade agreement.  两国签署了一项双边贸易协定。|例句：Bilateral hearing loss can make it difficult to understand speech in noisy environments. 双耳听力损失会使在嘈杂环境中理解语音变得困难。|近义词：mutual, reciprocal, two-sided|反义词：unilateral, one-sided|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *bi* (two) + *lateralis* (of the side)
