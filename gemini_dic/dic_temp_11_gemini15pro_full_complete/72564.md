fluty:/ˈfluːti/| adj.|像长笛的，笛声的|Resembling the sound of a flute; clear, high-pitched, and sweet.|Her voice was light and fluty. 她的声音轻柔悦耳，像笛声一样。|近义词：melodious, sweet, clear, high-pitched|反义词：raspy, hoarse, deep|词性:形容词
词源: From flute + -y. Ultimately from Old French flaüte and Latin flatus (“a blowing”).
