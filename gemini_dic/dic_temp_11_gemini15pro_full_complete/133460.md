nonfeasibility:/ˌnɒnˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/
|n.|不可行性，不可实现性|The nonfeasibility of the project became apparent after a thorough analysis.  这个项目在彻底分析后显现出不可行性。|The committee concluded the nonfeasibility of implementing the proposed changes within the given budget. 委员会得出结论，在给定的预算内实施拟议的变更不可行。|近义词：impracticability, impossibility, unworkability|反义词：feasibility, possibility, practicability|词性:名词

nonfeasible:/ˌnɒnˈfiːzəbl/
|adj.|不可行的，办不到的|Due to limited resources, the plan was deemed nonfeasible. 由于资源有限，该计划被认为不可行。|Constructing a bridge across the strait proved nonfeasible with current technology.  用现有技术建造一座跨越海峡的大桥被证明是不可行的。|近义词：impractical, impossible, unattainable|反义词：feasible, possible, achievable|词性:形容词
