furrier:/ˈfɜːriər/ | n. |皮草商；毛皮匠| A person who makes, repairs, or sells fur garments. |  The furrier carefully examined the mink coat for any imperfections. 皮草商仔细检查了貂皮大衣，看看有没有什么瑕疵。

|近义词: fur dealer, pelter |反义词: None|词性: Noun


furrier:/ˈfɜːriər/ | adj. (comparative furrier, superlative furriest) |多毛的；毛茸茸的| Covered with fur; furry. | The cat's furrier tail twitched playfully. 猫毛茸茸的尾巴顽皮地抽搐着。

|近义词: hairy, fluffy, downy |反义词: bald, smooth |词性: Adjective
