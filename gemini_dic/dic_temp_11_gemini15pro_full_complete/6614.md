anacrotism: /əˈnækrətɪzəm/ | n. | 上升支附加波，回升支切迹 | A notch on the ascending limb of a pulse tracing.  A small secondary upstroke on the ascending limb of the arterial pulse tracing. | The anacrotism in the pulse tracing suggested a problem with the patient's aortic valve.  脉搏描记图上的上升支附加波提示患者的主动脉瓣有问题。

Anacrotism can be observed in some patients with aortic stenosis. 在一些主动脉瓣狭窄患者中可以观察到上升支附加波。| 近义词: notch, incisura | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "smooth pulse tracing") | 词性: noun
