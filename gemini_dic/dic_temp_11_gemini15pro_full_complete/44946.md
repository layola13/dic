cryptology:/krɪˈtɒlədʒi/ (US) /krɪpˈtɑːlədʒi/ (UK)| n. |密码学|The study of codes, ciphers, and other methods of concealing information, including both cryptography (making codes) and cryptanalysis (breaking codes).|例句： Cryptology plays a crucial role in modern cybersecurity. 密码学在现代网络安全中扮演着至关重要的角色。

|例句：He dedicated his life to the study of cryptology. 他毕生致力于密码学的研究。|近义词： cryptography, cryptanalysis|反义词: None|Noun
