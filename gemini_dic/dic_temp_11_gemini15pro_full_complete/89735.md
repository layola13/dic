hopkins:/ˈhɑːpkɪnz/ | n. | 霍普金斯 | Usually a surname, derived from a patronymic meaning "son of Hobkin," a diminutive of Robert. 例句：My neighbor's last name is Hopkins. 我的邻居姓霍普金斯。
