effectual:/ɪˈfektʃuəl/
adjective
有效的，奏效的
Producing or capable of producing a desired effect.
His efforts to improve the situation were effectual. 他努力改善情况，并且奏效了。

The medicine was effectual in relieving her pain.  这种药有效地缓解了她的疼痛。

近义词:effective, efficient, potent, successful
反义词:ineffective, futile, useless
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *effectualis, from Latin effectus (“effect”) + -alis (“-al”).
