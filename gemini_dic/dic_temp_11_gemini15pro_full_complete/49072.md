dehydrates:/diːˈhaɪdreɪts/
verb|脱水，使脱水|removes water from; causes to lose water|The sun dehydrates the body. 太阳使身体脱水。|近义词：dries, evaporates|反义词：hydrates, moistens
verb|词性

dehydrates:/diːˈhaɪdreɪts/
noun|脱水物|a substance that has had water removed|Dried fruits are essentially dehydrates. 干果本质上是脱水物。|近义词：dessicants|反义词：N/A
noun|词性

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reduce") + hydrate (from Greek hydōr "water").
