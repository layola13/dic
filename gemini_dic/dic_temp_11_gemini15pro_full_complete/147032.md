overlauded:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔːdɪd/| adj.|过誉的，吹捧过度的|Praised too much; excessively praised.  The movie was overlauded by critics, but I found it quite boring.|例句：The overlauded author's new book was a disappointment.  这位被过誉的作家的新书令人失望。

例句：He felt overlauded for a relatively simple achievement.  他觉得自己的成就相对简单，却受到了过多的赞扬。|近义词：overpraised, overhyped, overrated|反义词：underrated, undervalued|词性:形容词
