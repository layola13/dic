draa:/drɑː/| n.|干河床，旱谷|A dry riverbed or wadi, especially in North Africa.|The camels trekked across the draa, their hooves sinking into the sand.  骆驼跋涉过干河床，它们的蹄子陷在沙子里。|例句：The ancient draa was once a flowing river. 这条古老的干河床曾经是一条奔流的河流。|近义词：wadi, arroyo, wash|反义词:river, stream|词性:名词
Etymology: From Arabic درعة (draʻa), meaning "female camel".  词源：源自阿拉伯语 درعة (draʻa)，意思是“母骆驼”。
