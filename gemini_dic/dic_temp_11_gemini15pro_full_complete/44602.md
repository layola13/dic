crowd-pleasers:/ˈkraʊdˌpliːzərz/
noun
取悦大众的事物；迎合大众口味的作品
Something designed to gain popular approval, typically an entertainer or type of entertainment.
The concert featured a mix of original songs and crowd-pleasers. 这场音乐会融合了原创歌曲和一些大众喜闻乐见的曲目。
Synonyms: popular favorites, hits, chart-toppers
Antonyms: niche appeal, avant-garde, experimental
