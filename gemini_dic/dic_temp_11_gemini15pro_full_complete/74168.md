fountainlife: /ˈfaʊntɪnˌlaɪf/ | n. |  生命之泉；青春活力；长生不老 | A state of prolonged youthfulness, vitality, and health, often associated with longevity. |  The company claims its new treatment can offer fountainlife to its clients.  这家公司声称它的新疗法可以为客户提供生命之泉。 | 近义词： longevity, vitality, rejuvenation, immortality | 反义词： aging, decline, mortality | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
"fountain" (from Old French "fontaine," ultimately from Latin "fons" meaning "spring, source") + "life" (from Old English "līf" meaning "life, existence"). The term evokes the image of a magical fountain that restores youth and grants immortality.
