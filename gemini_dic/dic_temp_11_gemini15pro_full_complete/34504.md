clean-living:/ˌkliːnˈlɪvɪŋ/
adjective
洁身自好的；生活习惯良好的
Having a lifestyle that is considered morally healthy, avoiding excessive drinking, smoking, and other such habits.
He was known for his clean-living habits and disciplined lifestyle. 他以洁身自好的习惯和自律的生活方式而闻名。
She attributed her longevity to clean living and a positive attitude. 她将自己的长寿归功于良好的生活习惯和积极的态度。
近义词:wholesome, moral, upright, virtuous
反义词:dissolute, debauched, immoral
词性:adjective
