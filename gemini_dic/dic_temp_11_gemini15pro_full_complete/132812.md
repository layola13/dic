noneclectic:/ˌnɒnɪˈklɛktɪk/
adjective
非折衷的，非兼容并蓄的
Not eclectic; not drawing on or made up of elements from various sources.
Her noneclectic taste in music limited her to only classical composers. 她对音乐非兼容并蓄的品味使她只局限于古典作曲家。
Near synonyms: narrow, limited, restricted, specific, unvaried
Antonyms: eclectic, diverse, varied, broad, wide-ranging
词性: 形容词
