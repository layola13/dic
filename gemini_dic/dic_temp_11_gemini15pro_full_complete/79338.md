gleed:/ɡliːd/
n.
炽热的煤炭;余烬
A glowing coal; an ember.
The blacksmith stoked the gleeds in the forge.  铁匠在锻炉里拨弄着炽热的煤炭。
The dying gleeds of the campfire glowed in the twilight. 篝火的余烬在暮色中闪烁。
近义词:ember, coal, cinder
反义词:ash

词性:n. (noun)

词源：
From Middle English *glede, from Old English glēd (“ember, burning coal”), from Proto-Germanic *glōdiz (“glowing coal”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- (“to shine, glow, burn”). Cognate with Scots gled (“live coal”), West Frisian gleed (“ember”), Dutch gloed (“glow, ardour”), German Glut (“glow, embers”), Danish glød (“glow, embers”), Swedish glöd (“glow, embers”), Icelandic glóð (“glow, embers”).
