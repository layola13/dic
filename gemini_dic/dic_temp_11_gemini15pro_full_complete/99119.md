interceders:/ˌɪntərˈsiːdərz/| n. |调解人，说情者，代祷者|A person who intervenes on behalf of another, especially by prayer or entreaty.|例句：The interceders prayed for peace between the warring nations. 调解员们为交战国之间的和平祈祷。

例句：Many interceders lifted their voices for the sick child. 许多代祷者为生病的孩子祈祷。|近义词：mediator, advocate, negotiator, pleader|反义词:opponent, adversary|词性:noun
