morass:/məˈræs/ /mɔːˈræs/| n. |沼泽；困境；僵局|An area of muddy or boggy ground. A complicated or confused situation.|例句：The army became bogged down in the morass. 军队陷入了沼泽。

例句：The project is stuck in a morass of bureaucracy.  这个项目陷入了官僚主义的泥潭。|近义词：swamp, bog, marsh, mire, quagmire, predicament, dilemma|反义词:solid ground, clarity, solution|词性:noun
