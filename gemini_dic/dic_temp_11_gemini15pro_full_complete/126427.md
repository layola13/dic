mystical:/ˈmɪstɪkəl/| adj.|神秘的， mystical experience 神秘的体验|Relating to mystics or religious mysticism.|The old house had a mystical atmosphere. 这座老房子有一种神秘的气氛。|近义词：spiritual, occult, esoteric, supernatural|反义词：mundane, ordinary, commonplace|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin mysticus, from Greek mystikos ‘of secret rites’, from mystēs ‘initiate’.
