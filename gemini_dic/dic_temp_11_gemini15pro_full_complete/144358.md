outsteal:/ˌaʊtˈstiːl/
verb
胜过；超过（在偷窃方面）
To surpass or exceed in stealing; to steal more or better than.
Robin Hood outstole even the most cunning of thieves. 罗宾汉偷窃的技艺甚至超过了最狡猾的盗贼。
近义词:outdo, surpass, excel, outmaneuver
反义词:be outdone, be surpassed
词性:v.
