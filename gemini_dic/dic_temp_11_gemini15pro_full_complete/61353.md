ember:/ˈɛmbər/| n.|余烬， embers(复数)；|A small piece of burning or glowing coal or wood in a dying fire. 火快要熄灭时剩下的燃烧着或发着热的煤块或木块。|The last embers of the campfire glowed in the darkness.  篝火的余烬在黑暗中闪烁。|近义词：spark, cinder, coal|反义词:ash|名词
