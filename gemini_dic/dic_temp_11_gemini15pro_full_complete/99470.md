interferant:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərənt/ | n. |干扰物，干扰因素|A substance that interferes with a process or reaction.|例句：The experiment was compromised by the presence of an interferant.  实验因存在干扰物而受到影响。

例句：Identifying the interferant was crucial to improving the accuracy of the test.  识别干扰物对于提高测试的准确性至关重要。|近义词：inhibitor, impediment, obstacle |反义词:catalyst, facilitator |词性:noun

interfering:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərɪŋ/ | adj. |干扰的，干涉的|Preventing or hindering something.|例句：Interfering signals made it difficult to receive the radio broadcast. 干扰信号使接收无线电广播变得困难。

例句：His interfering mother-in-law was a constant source of frustration. 他爱管闲事的岳母是他持续的挫败感来源。|近义词：meddling, intrusive, obstructive |反义词:helpful, supportive |词性:adjective

Etymology:
From the verb "interfere," which comes from the Old French "entreferir" (to strike each other), from "entre-" (between) + "ferir" (to strike). Ultimately from Latin "ferire" (to strike).
