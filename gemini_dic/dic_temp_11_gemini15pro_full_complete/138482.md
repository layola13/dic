novelty:/ˈnɒvəlti/ (UK) /ˈnɑːvəlti/ (US)| n.|新奇; 新奇的事物; 新颖小巧而廉价的物品|Something new and unusual, or different from what is usual or expected.  The quality of being new, original, or unusual.|例句：The novelty of the situation soon wore off.  情况的新鲜感很快就消失了。|例句：She loves the novelty of gadgets. 她喜欢新奇的小玩意儿。|近义词：originality, freshness, innovation, uniqueness|反义词:familiarity, staleness, conventionality|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *noveleté, from Latin novitas (“newness”), from novus (“new”).
