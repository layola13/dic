nonsympathetic:/ˌnɑːnsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
不表同情的，不同情的，冷漠的
Lacking in sympathy and understanding; uncaring.
例句：She was nonsympathetic to his plight, simply telling him to get over it. 她对他的困境漠不关心，只是告诉他要克服它。

例句：The judge gave him a nonsympathetic look and handed down a harsh sentence.  法官面无表情地看了他一眼，并作出了严厉的判决。

近义词：unsympathetic, indifferent, callous, uncaring
反义词：sympathetic, compassionate, understanding, caring
词性: adjective


词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
sympathetic (adjective): From Late Latin sympathetikos, from Greek sympathetikos, from sympathein "to feel with," from syn- "with" + pathos "suffering."
