demobbed:/ˌdiːˈmɒbd/ (UK), /ˌdiːˈmɑːbd/ (US)| adj.| 复员的，退伍的|Discharged from military service.|例句：He was demobbed after the war.  他战后复员了。

例句：Many demobbed soldiers struggled to find work. 许多退伍士兵难以找到工作。

近义词：discharged, mustered out|反义词：enlisted, conscripted|词性:形容词

词源: demobbed 是 demobilized 的缩写形式，demobilize 来自 de- (表示“离开，除去”) + mobilize (动员)。
