canteen:/kænˈtiːn/| n.|食堂，小卖部|A canteen is a place where food and drinks are sold, often in a factory, school, or office. |The school canteen serves hot lunches. 学校食堂供应热午餐。|
The soldiers lined up at the canteen for their evening meal. 士兵们在食堂排队等候晚餐。|
Synonyms: cafeteria, mess hall, snack bar|
Antonyms: restaurant (more formal, diverse menu)|noun
