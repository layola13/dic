octogenary: /ˌɒktəˈdʒiːnəri/ /ˌɑːktəˈdʒenəri/
n. adj.
八十岁的；八十年代
A person who is between 80 and 89 years old; the period of time between a person's 80th and 89th birthdays.
He celebrated his octogenary birthday with his family. 他和家人一起庆祝了他的八十岁生日。
My grandmother is an octogenary, full of wisdom and stories. 我的祖母是一位八十多岁的老人，充满了智慧和故事。
近义词: octagenarian
反义词: None
词性: noun, adjective
