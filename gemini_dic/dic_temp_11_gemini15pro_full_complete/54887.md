displume:/dɪˈpluːm/
verb
剥夺(荣誉)；使丧失(羽毛)；使丢脸
deprive of feathers; degrade; humiliate
The scandal displumes the politician of his reputation. 这桩丑闻损害了这位政治家的名誉。
synonyms:degrade, humble, humiliate, shame, disgrace, dishonor, discredit, strip, deprive
antonyms:honor, praise, extol, elevate
词性:v.
