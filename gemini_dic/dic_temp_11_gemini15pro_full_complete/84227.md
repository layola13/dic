half-semitic:/ˌhæf səˈmɪtɪk/ | adj. | 半闪米特的 | Partially or incompletely Semitic, referring to languages or peoples showing some but not all characteristics of Semitic origin. |  Some scholars classify certain languages as half-Semitic due to their mixed influences. 一些学者将某些语言归类为半闪米特语，因为它们受到混合的影响。

|  The origins of the Cushitic languages, sometimes considered half-Semitic, are still debated.  库希特语系的起源，有时被认为是半闪米特语，仍然存在争议。| 近义词: partially Semitic | 反义词: fully Semitic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
"half-" (Old English *healf) + "Semitic" (from Shem, one of Noah's sons, traditionally considered the ancestor of the Semitic peoples)
