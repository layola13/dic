languorous:/ˈlæŋɡərəs/ | adj. | 慵懒的，倦怠的 | Characterized by a languid, dreamy mood; lacking in energy; pleasantly lazy and relaxed. |  The cat stretched out in a languorous pose.  这只猫慵懒地伸展着身体。| Synonyms: indolent, listless, lethargic | Antonyms: energetic, vigorous, lively | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *langoureux*, from Latin *languor* ("faintness, weariness").
