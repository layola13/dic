ETYMOLOGY->
From messiness + -es (plural suffix). Messiness is derived from messy + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). Messy originates from mess (a state of disorder, dirt, or confusion) + -y (suffix forming adjectives). The origin of "mess" can be traced back to Old French mes, meaning "portion of food," and ultimately to Late Latin missus, "a course at a meal."
