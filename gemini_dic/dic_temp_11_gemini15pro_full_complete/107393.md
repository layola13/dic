laminable:/ˈlæmɪnəbl/
adjective
可压延的；可叠层的
Capable of being rolled or pressed into thin sheets or layers.
Aluminum is a highly laminable metal. 铝是一种高度可压延的金属。
Near Synonyms: rollable, malleable, ductile
Antonyms: non-malleable, brittle
Etymology: From laminate + -able. Ultimately from Latin *lamina, "thin plate," from PIE root *lembh- "to hang limp."
