nonapplicablenesses:/ˌnɒnəˈplɪkəbəlnəsɪz/
noun
不适用性；不适用之处
The state or quality of not being applicable; instances of not being applicable.
Due to the unique circumstances, the usual rules and their nonapplicablenesses were discussed. 由于情况特殊，讨论了通常规则及其不适用性。
The lawyer argued the nonapplicablenesses of certain clauses in the contract. 律师辩称合同中的某些条款不适用。
近义词:inapplicability, irrelevance, unsuitability
反义词: applicability, relevance, suitability
词性:noun

nonapplicability:/ˌnɒnəˌplɪkəˈbɪləti/
noun
不适用性
The state or quality of not being applicable.
The nonapplicability of the law in this case is clear.  在这种情况下，法律的不适用性是显而易见的。
He demonstrated the nonapplicability of the rule to the current situation. 他证明了这条规则不适用于当前的情况。
近义词:irrelevance, unsuitability
反义词: applicability, relevance, suitability
词性:noun
