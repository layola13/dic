endeictic:/ɛnˈdaɪktɪk/ |adj.|指示的，指示性的|Relating to or denoting a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used, e.g., *here*, *you*, *me*, *that*, *now*.|Example:  "This" is an endeictic word because its meaning depends on what the speaker is pointing to. “这”是一个指示词，因为它的含义取决于说话者所指的对象。

Example: The use of "here" in the sentence "I am here" is endeictic, as it refers to the speaker's current location.  在“我在这里”这个句子中，“这里”的用法是指示性的，因为它指的是说话者当前的位置。|近义词:demonstrative, indexical|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
