naysayer:/ˈneɪˌseɪər/| n.|反对者，唱反调的人|A person who habitually expresses negative views or opposes suggestions. |例句：The naysayers were proved wrong when the project was a resounding success.  反对者在项目取得巨大成功时被证明是错误的。

例句：He ignored the naysayers and pursued his dream. 他忽略了反对者，继续追求自己的梦想。


|近义词：pessimist, skeptic, doubter, objector, critic|反义词: optimist, supporter, proponent|词性:noun
