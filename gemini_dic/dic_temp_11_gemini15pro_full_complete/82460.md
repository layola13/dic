grumpier:/ˈɡrʌmpiər/ | adj. | 更脾气暴躁的，更爱抱怨的 | Comparative form of grumpy: more grumpy.  More bad-tempered and irritable. | Example: He seemed even grumpier than usual this morning. 他今天早上似乎比平时更暴躁。 | Example:  The longer the line got, the grumpier the customers became.  队伍越长，顾客就越不耐烦。| Synonyms: more irritable, more bad-tempered, crankier, more churlish, more sullen | Antonyms: happier, cheerier, more pleasant, more agreeable | Derivation: From grumpy + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from grump (n.) + -y (adjective suffix). Grump (n.) is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
