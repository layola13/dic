antiparallels:/ˌæntiˈpærəˌlɛlz/ |n. |反平行线，反平行| Lines or segments that are equally inclined to a given line or segment, but in opposite directions.  Two lines are antiparallel with respect to two other lines if the angle between the first pair is equal to the angle between the second pair, but with opposite signs. In a quadrilateral, if two opposite sides are antiparallel, it is called an antiparallelogram. | The two base angles of an isosceles triangle are antiparallel with respect to the sides. 等腰三角形的两个底角相对于两边是反平行的。

The diagonals of an antiparallelogram are antiparallels with respect to the sides. 反平行四边形的对角线相对于边是反平行的。 | near synonyms: antiparallel lines| antonyms: parallel lines | noun
