modellers:/ˈmɒdələz (UK) /ˈmɑːdələrz (US)| n. | 建模者，建模师 |  People who create models, often mathematical or computational, to represent and understand complex systems or processes. | The modellers used sophisticated software to predict the impact of climate change.  建模人员使用复杂的软件来预测气候变化的影响。| synonyms:  simulators, analysts, designers | antonyms:  observers, measurers | noun
