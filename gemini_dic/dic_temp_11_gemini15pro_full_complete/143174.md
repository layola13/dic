outclimb:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪm/ | v. | 胜过攀登，爬得比…高 | To climb higher or faster than someone or something else. | The experienced climber easily outclimbed the novice.  经验丰富的登山者轻松地超过了新手。|  The young ibex outclimbed its mother on the steep cliff face. 小野山羊在陡峭的悬崖面上爬得比它妈妈还高。 | 近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo | 反义词：lag behind, trail | 词性：动词
