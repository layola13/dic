chrysalises: /krɪˈsælɪsiːz/
n.
蛹（复数）
The hard-shelled form that some insects take before they become adults.  This stage is between the larva and the adult stage.
The butterfly emerged from its chrysalis. 蝴蝶从它的蛹中羽化出来。
The chrysalises hanging from the branch were shimmering in the sunlight.  悬挂在树枝上的蛹在阳光下闪闪发光。
近义词: pupae, cocoons (for moths)
反义词: larva, adult, imago
词性: Noun (plural)

chrysalis: /ˈkrɪsəlɪs/
n.
蛹（单数）
The hard-shelled form that some insects take before they become adults.  This stage is between the larva and the adult stage.
The butterfly emerged from its chrysalis. 蝴蝶从它的蛹中羽化出来。
A chrysalis is a stage of transformation. 蛹是蜕变的一个阶段。
近义词: pupa, cocoon (for moths)
反义词: larva, adult, imago
词性: Noun (singular)

词源:
From Latin *chrysalis, from Greek khrysallis (χρυσαλλίς), from khrysos (χρυσός, "gold"), because of the golden color of some butterfly pupae.
