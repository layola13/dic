extrapolatory:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪtɔːri/
adjective
外推的，推断的
Based on extending a known series of facts or data beyond the area that is certainly known; conjectural.
例句：
The economist made extrapolatory predictions about the future of the market based on current trends.
经济学家根据当前趋势对市场未来做出了推断性预测。

His extrapolatory remarks about the company's future profits were met with skepticism.
他对公司未来利润的推断性言论遭到了质疑。


近义词：conjectural, speculative, predictive, projected
反义词：interpolatory, factual, empirical
词性：adjective
