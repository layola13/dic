nomarch:/ˈnoʊmɑːrk/ | n. | 省长，方伯 | A governor of a nome (province) in ancient Egypt. |  The nomarch controlled the province's resources and administered justice.  这位方伯控制着该省的资源并执行司法。 | Example: The nomarch was responsible for collecting taxes.  这位方伯负责收税。| Synonyms: governor, prefect, administrator | Antonyms: subject, citizen | noun

nomarch:/ˈnoʊmɑːrk/ | n. | 州长；地方长官 | A governor of a nomos (administrative division) in modern Greece. | The nomarch oversaw the regional government and implemented national policies.  这位州长负责监督地方政府并执行国家政策。 | Example: The nomarch addressed the citizens about the new infrastructure project.  州长向市民们讲述了新的基础设施项目。 | Synonyms: governor, administrator | Antonyms:  citizen, resident  | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek νόμαρχος (nómarkhos), from νόμος (nómos, “district, province, law”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule”).
