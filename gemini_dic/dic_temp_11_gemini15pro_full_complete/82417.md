gruelling:/ˈɡruːəlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɡruːəlɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 艰苦的，令人筋疲力尽的|Extremely tiring and demanding; exhausting.|The marathon was a gruelling experience. 马拉松是一次令人筋疲力尽的经历。|近义词：exhausting, arduous, strenuous, demanding|反义词：easy, effortless, simple|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "gruel" (to punish or exhaust), ultimately from Old French "grueler" (to torment).
