discarder:/dɪˈskɑːrdər/ | n. | 丢弃者；抛弃者 |  A person or thing that discards something. Someone who throws something away or gets rid of it. | The city council is looking for ways to reduce the amount of waste produced by discarders. 市议会正在寻找减少丢弃者产生的废物量的方法。

The discarder left the unwanted furniture on the curb. 丢弃者把不要的家具放在路边。 | 近义词：rejector, dumper | 反义词：keeper, hoarder | 词性：名词
