fixings:/ˈfɪksɪŋz/
n.
配料，配件，装饰
the ingredients or equipment needed for a particular dish or activity.
Example:  She bought all the fixings for a Thanksgiving feast. 她买了感恩节盛宴的所有配料。

Example: He had all the fixings for a model airplane. 他拥有制作模型飞机的所有配件。
synonyms: ingredients, accessories, trimmings, equipment, paraphernalia
antonyms:  lack, deficiency, shortage
noun

fixings:/ˈfɪksɪŋz/
n.
（尤指不诚实的）安排，修理
(especially dishonest) arrangements or repairs
Example: He was accused of fixing the election. 他被指控操纵选举。

Example:  They were suspected of fixing the prices. 他们被怀疑操纵价格。
synonyms: manipulation, rigging,  machinations, scheme, plot
antonyms: fairness, honesty, integrity
noun
