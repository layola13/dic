cuffless:/ˈkʌflɪs/
adjective
无袖口的，无翻边的
Without cuffs, typically referring to shirts or trousers.
She prefers cuffless trousers for a more streamlined look. 她更喜欢无袖口的裤子，看起来更简洁。
The cuffless shirt was perfect for the hot weather. 这件无袖口的衬衫非常适合炎热的天气。
Near Synonyms: uncuffed, cuff-free
Opposite: cuffed
词性: 形容词
