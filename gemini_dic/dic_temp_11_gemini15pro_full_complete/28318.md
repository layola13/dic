caracol:/kɑːrəˈkɒl/ (US) /ˈkærəˌkɒl/ (UK)| n.|蜗牛；螺旋形物|A terrestrial mollusk with a spiral shell.  A snail.  Can also refer to something spiral shaped.|例句：The caracol slowly made its way across the leaf. 蜗牛慢慢地爬过叶子。

例句：The staircase wound upwards in a caracol. 楼梯盘旋而上。|近义词：snail, conch, helix|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

caracol:/kɑːrəˈkɒl/ (US) /ˈkærəˌkɒl/ (UK)| n.|卡拉科尔舞|A type of partnered dance of Cuban origin.|例句：They danced the caracol with grace and precision. 他们优雅而精准地跳着卡拉科尔舞。

例句：The band played a lively tune for the caracol. 乐队为卡拉科尔舞演奏了一曲欢快的旋律。|近义词：dance|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
