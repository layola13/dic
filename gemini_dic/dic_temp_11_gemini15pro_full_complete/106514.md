| Synonym:  citadel, fortress, Russian government | Antonym: N/A | Noun


kremlin: /ˈkrem.lɪn/ | adj. (Figurative, less common) |  克里姆林宫的；俄罗斯政府的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Kremlin or the Russian government. | The Kremlin's policies have been criticized internationally. 克里姆林宫的政策受到了国际社会的批评。

| Synonym: Russian governmental, Moscow's central government | Antonym: N/A | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY ->
From Russian Кремль (Kremlʹ), meaning "fortress" or "citadel".  Ultimately from a Proto-Slavic root *kreml’ meaning "fortress, castle."
