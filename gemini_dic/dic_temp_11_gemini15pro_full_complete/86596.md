heliograph:/ˈhiːliəɡrɑːf/ /ˈhiːlioʊɡræf/| n. |日光反射信号机；日光通信|A device that uses mirrors to flash sunlight, used for signaling.|The soldiers used a heliograph to communicate with headquarters across the vast desert.|Synonym:  helioscope,  semaphore |Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider means of communication that don't rely on light, like radio)|noun
heliographs:/ˈhiːliəɡrɑːfs/ /ˈhiːlioʊɡræfs/| n. |日光反射信号机（复数）|Multiple devices that use mirrors to flash sunlight, used for signaling.|Several heliographs were positioned along the mountain range to relay messages.|Synonym: heliotropes (less common)|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun
