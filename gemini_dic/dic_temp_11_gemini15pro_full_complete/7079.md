anaerobic:/ˌænəˈrəʊbɪk/ (UK), /ˌænəˈroʊbɪk/ (US)| adj.| 厌氧的，无氧的|Relating to, involving, or requiring an absence of free oxygen.|Anaerobic bacteria thrive in environments without oxygen. 厌氧细菌在无氧环境中 thrive.|近义词:anoxic|反义词:aerobic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek an- "without" + aero- "air" + -bios "life" + -ic.
