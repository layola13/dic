apelike:/ˈeɪplaɪk/| adj.|像猿的，类人猿的|Resembling an ape, especially in appearance or behavior.|例句：The creature had an apelike stance and long, powerful arms.  这个生物有着像猿一样的姿态和长而有力的胳膊。

例句：His apelike grin made me uncomfortable. 他像猿猴一样的笑容让我很不舒服。|近义词：simian, monkeylike, anthropoid|反义词:humanlike, humanoid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
ape (n.) + -like (suffix)

ape (n.) Old English *apa (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *apon (source also of Old Norse api, Middle Dutch ape, Old High German affo), of uncertain origin. Perhaps from Vulgar Latin *simia, alteration of Latin simia "ape," from Greek simos "snub-nosed."

-like (suffix) Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-liko (source also of Old Saxon -lik, Old Frisian -lik, Old Norse -ligr, Dutch -lijk, Old High German -lih, German -lich, Gothic -leiks), from PIE *-likos.
