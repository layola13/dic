figurehead:/ˈfɪɡjərˌhɛd/| n. |名义领袖；傀儡；船首像|A person who appears to be the leader of an organization but who has no real power. A carved figure, usually of a woman, fixed to the prow of a ship.|The company president was merely a figurehead, with the real power residing in the board of directors.  这家公司的总裁只是一个有名无实的傀儡，真正的权力掌握在董事会手中。

The old sailing ship had a beautiful figurehead of a mermaid. 这艘古老的帆船船首有一个漂亮的美人鱼雕像。|Synonyms: puppet, nominal leader, front, token, ornament|Antonyms: leader, authority, boss, chief, decision-maker|noun
