condone:/kənˈdoʊn/ | v. | 宽恕；原谅；容忍（尤指不好的事情） | to accept or allow behavior that is considered morally wrong or offensive. |  She could not condone his actions. 她无法容忍他的行为。 |  The school does not condone bullying. 学校不容忍欺凌行为。 | synonyms: overlook, excuse, pardon, forgive, tolerate | antonyms: condemn, denounce, censure | verb
