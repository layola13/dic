gazehound:/ˈɡeɪzhaʊnd/| n. |猎兔犬，视力敏锐的人|A tall, slender dog of an ancient breed, noted for its keen eyesight and used for hunting by sight rather than scent.  A person with exceptionally keen eyesight.|例句：The gazehound, with its sleek coat and piercing gaze, was a magnificent sight.  这只猎兔犬有着光滑的皮毛和锐利的目光，看起来非常壮观。

例句：He was a gazehound when it came to spotting errors in the text.  他在发现文本错误方面眼光敏锐。|近义词：greyhound, sighthound, whippet|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
