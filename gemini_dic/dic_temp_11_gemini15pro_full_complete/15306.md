backplate:/ˈbækpleɪt/| n.|背板，底板|A plate that forms the back of something, providing support or protection.|The backplate of the watch was engraved with a special message. 手表的背板上刻着一条特殊的留言。|Synonyms: backing, support, plate|Antonyms: front, face|noun

backplate:/ˈbækpleɪt/| n.|护背，防护背板|A piece of armor or protective gear worn on the back.|The knight's backplate protected him from arrows. 骑士的护背保护他不受箭的伤害。|Synonyms: armor, protection, shield|Antonyms: front, face|noun
