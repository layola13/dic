cwm:/kuːm/ (Welsh pronunciation) /kwʊm/ (anglicized pronunciation)| n. |冰斗; 圈谷|A steep-walled hollow on a mountainside, shaped like an amphitheater or bowl, typically formed by glacial erosion.|例句：The hikers reached the cwm after a strenuous climb.  徒步旅行者在艰苦的攀登后到达了冰斗。

例句：The cwm was filled with wildflowers in the spring. 春天，冰斗里开满了野花。|近义词：cirque, corrie|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "ridge" or "peak" in a descriptive sense)|词性:noun
