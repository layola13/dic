dto:/ˌdēˌtēˈō/ | n. | 数据传输对象 | Data Transfer Object.  A simple object used to encapsulate data for transfer between layers or systems, often used to reduce the number of remote calls. | The DTO simplifies data exchange between the client and the server.  这个DTO简化了客户端和服务器之间的数据交换。 |  data structure, value object | N/A | noun
