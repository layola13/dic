eulogistically:/ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪkli/
| adv. |颂赞地，赞美地|Speaking or writing in a way that praises someone or something highly.  Expressing high praise or commendation.|He spoke eulogistically about his mentor, praising her wisdom and guidance. 他颂扬了他的导师，赞美她的智慧和指导。|Synonyms:  laudingly, praisingly, commendably, approvingly|Antonyms: critically, disparagingly, negatively|Word type: Adverb
