aquatics:/əˈkwætɪks/ | n. |水上运动，水生生物|Relating to water; living in or practiced on water.  Activities performed in or on water, such as swimming, diving, and water polo.  Can also refer to aquatic animals or plants.|例句：She enjoys aquatics, especially swimming and diving. 她喜欢水上运动，尤其是游泳和跳水。|例句：The local university has a strong aquatics program. 这所地方大学有一个很强大的水上运动项目。|近义词：water sports, swimming, diving, water activities|反义词:|n.
