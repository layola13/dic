antifluoridation:/ˌæntiˌflɔːrɪˈdeɪʃən/
n.
反对氟化作用；反氟化物措施
Opposition to the addition of fluoride to public water supplies.
The town held a referendum on antifluoridation. 该镇就反对氟化物措施举行了全民公决。

近义词:opposition to fluoridation
反义词:pro-fluoridation
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
fluoridation (noun) : the addition of fluoride to a substance, especially public water supplies. (from fluorine + -idation)
