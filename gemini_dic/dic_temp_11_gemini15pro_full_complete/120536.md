miquelet:/ˌmɪkəˈlɛt/
n.
miquelet lock; miquelet gun; a soldier armed with a miquelet
A type of firearm lock, also the gun using that lock, and a soldier armed with such a weapon.  The miquelet lock was a development of the earlier snaplock and was common in Spain and the Mediterranean region.
The bandits were armed with rusty miquelets. 土匪们装备着生锈的米克莱特枪。
The museum displayed a collection of antique miquelets. 博物馆展出了一批古董米克莱特枪。
近义词:snaphaunce, flintlock
词性:noun
