mufti:/ˈmʌfti/| n.|便服，便衣；教法学家|A Muslim legal expert who is empowered to give rulings on religious matters.|The Grand Mufti issued a fatwa against the practice.  大穆夫提发布了一项反对这种做法的宗教法令。|Synonyms: civilian clothes, plain clothes (for the clothing sense); jurist, scholar, expert (for the religious sense)|Antonyms: uniform, military attire (for the clothing sense)|noun


muftis:/ˈmʌftiz/| n.|便服，便衣；教法学家（复数）|Plural form of mufti, referring to Muslim legal experts.|The muftis of several countries condemned the violence.  几个国家的教法学家谴责了暴力行为。|Synonyms: civilian clothes, plain clothes (for the clothing sense); jurists, scholars, experts (for the religious sense)|Antonyms: uniforms, military attires (for the clothing sense)|noun
