indefensiblenesses:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbəlnəsɪz/
|n.|无可辩解，无力反驳|The indefensiblenesses of his position became increasingly apparent. 他的立场越来越明显站不住脚。|
|例句：The report exposed the indefensiblenesses of the company's environmental practices.  这份报告揭露了该公司环境实践中站不住脚的地方。|

|例句：The indefensiblenesses of her argument were clear to everyone in the room. 她论证的无力反驳之处，房间里每个人都清楚。|
|近义词：vulnerability, weakness, flaws, shortcomings|
|反义词：strength, soundness, validity, justifiability|
|词性: noun (plural)|
