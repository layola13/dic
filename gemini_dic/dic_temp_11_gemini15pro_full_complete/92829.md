iconology:/ˌaɪkəˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |图像学；图像志; 图像符号学|The study of the meaning of images, symbols, and allegories, especially in art.  It goes beyond simple description and considers the cultural, social, and historical context of the work to understand its deeper significance.|例句：The iconology of the painting reveals a complex interplay of religious and political themes. 这幅画的图像学揭示了宗教和政治主题的复杂交织。

例句：Her dissertation explored the iconology of medieval manuscripts. 她的论文探讨了中世纪手稿的图像学。|近义词：semiotics, symbolism, iconography |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *iconologie*, from Greek *eikōn* (image) + *-logia* (study).
