de-litters:/diːˈlɪtərz/
verb
清除垃圾；清理废弃物
Removes litter from (a place).
The volunteers de-littered the park after the festival. 志愿者们在节日后清理了公园的垃圾。
The city council has started a campaign to de-litter the streets. 市议会已经开始了一项清理街道垃圾的运动。
clean up, tidy up
litter, pollute
