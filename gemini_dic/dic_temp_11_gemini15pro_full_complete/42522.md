counterlathing:/ˈkaʊntərˌlæθɪŋ/
n.|反向条板；挂瓦条|A system of narrow strips of wood fixed to rafters or ceiling joists to provide a surface for attaching tiles, slates, or other roofing materials.|The roof was constructed using counterlathing to provide better ventilation. 屋顶使用了反向条板以提供更好的通风。|近义词:furring strips, battens|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源:
"counter-" (prefix meaning "against, opposite") + "lathing" (noun from "lathe," meaning "thin strip of wood")
