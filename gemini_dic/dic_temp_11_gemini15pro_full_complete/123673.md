moodily:/ˈmuːdɪli/| adv.|闷闷不乐地，忧郁地|In a way that shows you are unhappy or annoyed.|He stared moodily out the window. 他闷闷不乐地盯着窗外。|例句：She walked moodily through the park. 她忧郁地走过公园。|近义词：sullenly, glumly, morosely|反义词：cheerfully, happily|词性：副词
Etymology: From mood + -ily. "Mood" comes from Old English "mōd" meaning "mind, feeling, heart, spirit, courage, arrogance."
