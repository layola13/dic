nitraniline:/ˌnaɪtrəˈænɪliːn/| n. |硝基苯胺|An aromatic amine compound derived from aniline by replacing one or more hydrogen atoms of the benzene ring with a nitro group (NO₂).  There are three isomers: ortho-nitraniline, meta-nitraniline, and para-nitraniline.|Para-nitraniline is used in the production of certain dyes. 对硝基苯胺用于生产某些染料。|Synonyms: nitroaniline, amino-nitrobenzene|Antonyms:  (No direct chemical antonyms exist)|noun
