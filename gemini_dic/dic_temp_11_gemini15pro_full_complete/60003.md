editorializers:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəˌlaɪzərz/
n.
评论员；社论撰写人
People who express opinions, often in a critical or biased way, especially in the media.  Those who write editorials or offer commentary.
The editorializers on the cable news networks often present one-sided arguments.  有线新闻网上的评论员经常提出片面的论点。
The newspaper's editorializers were quick to condemn the politician's actions.  该报的社论作者迅速谴责了这位政客的行为。
commentators, pundits, analysts
reporters, journalists (in a neutral sense)
noun
