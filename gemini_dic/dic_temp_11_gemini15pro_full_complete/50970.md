desilverization:/diːˌsɪlvəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|脱银; 除银|The process of removing silver from a substance, typically lead. 从物质中去除银的过程，通常指铅。|例句：The desilverization of lead is an important step in lead refining.  铅的脱银是铅精炼中的一个重要步骤。|例句：The new desilverization technique significantly increased efficiency. 新的脱银技术显著提高了效率。|近义词：silver removal, silver extraction|反义词: silver plating, silvering|词性: 名词
