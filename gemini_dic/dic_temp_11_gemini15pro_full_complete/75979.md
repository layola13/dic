funnelform:/ˈfʌn(ə)lˌfɔːrm/ | adj. | 漏斗形的，漏斗状的 | Shaped like a funnel; tapering towards one end. | The flower had a funnelform corolla.  这朵花的花冠呈漏斗形。| The artist used a funnelform mold to create the sculpture. 艺术家用一个漏斗形的模具来创作雕塑。| 近义词: funnel-shaped, infundibuliform | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider shapes like cylindrical, spherical) | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "funnel" + "-form." "Funnel" comes from the Old French "funel," ultimately from the Latin "infundibulum," meaning "funnel." "-form" is a suffix derived from Latin "-formis," meaning "having the form of."
