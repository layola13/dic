co-eds:/ˈkoʊ ɛdz/| n.|男女同校的学生（尤指女生）|Students, especially female students, at a co-educational institution.|The university has a large number of co-eds. 这所大学有大量的男女同校的学生。|synonyms: female students (in a co-ed institution), women students (in a co-ed institution)|antonyms: male students (in a single-sex institution),  female students (in a single-sex institution)|noun


co-ed:/ˈkoʊ ɛd/ | adj. |男女同校的|Educated together, regardless of gender.|My brother goes to a co-ed school. 我弟弟上的是男女同校的学校。|synonyms: coeducational, mixed-sex|antonyms: single-sex, all-male, all-female|adjective


Etymology: Shortened from "co-education," with "co-" being a combining form meaning "together" or "jointly."  "Education" comes from the Latin "educatio," meaning "a bringing up, a rearing, training."
