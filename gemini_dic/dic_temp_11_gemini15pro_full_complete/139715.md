ocelot:/ˈɑːsəlɑːt/ /ˈɔːsəlɒt/| n.|豹猫; 矮豹;|A wild cat of Central and South America, with a spotted and striped coat.|The ocelot is a nocturnal animal, rarely seen during the day. 豹猫是一种夜行动物，白天很少见到。|Synonyms: dwarf leopard, margay|Antonyms: None|Noun
