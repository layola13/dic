overpraising: /ˌoʊvərˈpreɪzɪŋ/
|n.| 过度称赞，过分夸奖
|The act of praising someone or something too much, beyond what is deserved or warranted.|
|Her constant overpraising of her son made him arrogant.| 她对儿子不断的过度称赞让他变得傲慢。
|近义词：excessive praise, effusive praise, gushing, adulation|
|反义词：understatement, criticism, disparagement|
|词性: 动名词 (gerund) derived from the verb "overpraise"|


overpraise: /ˌoʊvərˈpreɪz/
|vt.| 过度称赞，过分夸奖
|To praise someone or something too much, beyond what is deserved or warranted.|
|The coach overpraised the team's performance, even though they barely won.| 教练过分夸奖了球队的表现，尽管他们勉强获胜。
|近义词：extol, laud excessively, gush over|
|反义词：understate, criticize, disparage|
|词性: verb (transitive)|
