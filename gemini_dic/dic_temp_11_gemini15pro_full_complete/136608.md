nonsaponifiable:/ˌnɒnsəˌpɒnɪˈfaɪəbl/ /ˌnɑːnsəˌpɑːnɪˈfaɪəbl/| adj.|不可皂化的|Not capable of being saponified; that is, not converted into soap by reaction with an alkali such as sodium hydroxide or potassium hydroxide.  Specifically applied to the fraction of fats and oils that does not form soaps during saponification.|The nonsaponifiable fraction of shea butter contains valuable antioxidants. 乳木果油的不可皂化部分含有珍贵的抗氧化剂。|insoluble, nonhydrolyzable|saponifiable|adjective
