blackmail:/ˈblækmeɪl/| n.|敲诈，勒索; 勒索所得之款|Money or concessions extorted by blackmail.|The blackmailers demanded $1 million.|近义词：extortion, coercion|反义词: donation, gift|词性:名词(n.)
