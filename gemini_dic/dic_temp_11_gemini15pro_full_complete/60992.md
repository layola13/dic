eligibility:/ˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪləti/| n.|资格，合格，被选举资格|The state of having the right to do or obtain something because of meeting the appropriate requirements.|例句：He checked his eligibility for the contest. 他检查了他是否有资格参加比赛。|例句：Her eligibility for the scholarship was questioned. 她的奖学金资格受到了质疑。|近义词：qualification, suitability, fitness|反义词：ineligibility, disqualification|词性:名词

词源: From Late Middle English: from Old French eligibleté, from late Latin eligibilitas, from Latin eligibilis ‘eligible’
