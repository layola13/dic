hypersusceptibilities:/ˌhaɪpərsəˌsɛptəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 超敏性，过敏性 |  The state of being unusually or excessively sensitive to something, especially a substance such as a drug or allergen.  This term often refers to an exaggerated immune response. |  The patient exhibited hypersusceptibilities to various environmental allergens.  该患者对各种环境过敏原表现出超敏反应。 | hypersensitivities, sensitivities | tolerances, resistances | noun
