knitters:/ˈnɪtərz/
n.
编织者；针织者
People who knit, creating garments or other items by interlocking loops of yarn.
The knitters meet every Thursday evening to share patterns and socialize.  编织者们每周四晚上聚会，分享图案并进行社交。
The local yarn store is a popular gathering place for knitters.  当地的毛线店是编织者们喜爱的聚集地。
knitter, crocheter, needleworker
non-knitter
noun
