indorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ /ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/| n.|认可，支持，背书，签注|An act of giving public approval or support to someone or something.  The signature on the back of a check.|例句：The product received a celebrity endorsement.  该产品获得了名人的代言。|近义词：approval, support, backing, sanction, ratification, confirmation|反义词：opposition, disapproval, rejection|noun
