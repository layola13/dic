overrent:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛnt/ | vt. | 过度收租；收取过高的租金 | To charge someone too much for rent. | The landlord overrented the apartment, taking advantage of the housing shortage. 房东过度收取了公寓的租金，利用了住房短缺的局面。

He felt he was being overrented for a small, dingy apartment. 他觉得他为一套又小又脏的公寓付了过高的租金。 | 近义词: overcharge | 反义词: undercharge | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "rent" (noun meaning "payment for the use of property").  The prefix "over-" is from Old English "ofer," and "rent" is from Old French "rente," ultimately from Latin "reddita" ("things that are given back," from "reddere" "to give back").
