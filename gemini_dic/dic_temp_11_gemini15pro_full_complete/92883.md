idealistically:/ˌaɪdiəˈlɪstɪkli/
adverb
理想主义地，理想化地
In a way that is based on ideals rather than practical considerations.
He idealistically believed he could single-handedly change the world. 他理想化地认为他可以单枪匹马地改变世界。
She idealistically envisioned a world without poverty. 她理想化地设想了一个没有贫困的世界。
Synonyms: naively, romantically, unrealistically, impractically
Antonyms: pragmatically, realistically, practically
词性: 副词
