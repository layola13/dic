evaporates:/ɪˈvæpəreɪts/
verb
蒸发，挥发
turns from liquid into vapor.
The water evaporates quickly in the hot sun.  太阳下的水蒸发得很快。

近义词:vaporizes, dissipates, dries up
反义词:condenses, solidifies
词性:v.
