odiousnesses:/ˈoʊdiəsnəsɪz/
noun
令人憎恶；可恶；可憎
The state or quality of being odious; hatefulness.
The odiousnesses of slavery are well documented.  奴隶制的种种可憎之处都有详细的记录。
近义词: hatefulness, repulsiveness, offensiveness, abhorrence, detestation
反义词: pleasantness, attractiveness, agreeableness, loveliness
词性: noun

odious:/ˈoʊdiəs/
adjective
可憎的；讨厌的；令人作呕的
Extremely unpleasant; repulsive.
The dictator's odious regime was finally overthrown.  独裁者令人憎恶的政权终于被推翻了。
近义词: hateful, repulsive, offensive, abhorrent, detestable
反义词: pleasant, attractive, agreeable, lovely
词性: adjective
