denoiser:/diːˈnɔɪzər/| n. |去噪器，降噪器|A device or software program designed to reduce or eliminate noise from a signal, such as audio, video, or images.|例句：The photographer used a denoiser to clean up the grainy images. 摄影师使用去噪器来清理颗粒状的图像。

例句：This new denoiser plugin significantly improves the audio quality. 这个新的去噪器插件显著提高了音频质量。|近义词：noise reducer, noise suppressor, filter|反义词:noise amplifier, noise generator|词性:noun
