oscillate:/ˈɒsɪleɪt/ (UK), /ˈɑːsɪleɪt/ (US)| v. |摆动，振动， колебаться(Russian), osciller(French)|To swing back and forth repeatedly between two points, positions, or states.|The pendulum oscillated back and forth. 钟摆来回摆动。|近义词：swing, vibrate, fluctuate, waver|反义词：stabilize, steady|词性:动词

oscillate:/ˈɒsɪleɪt/ (UK), /ˈɑːsɪleɪt/ (US)| n. |振荡，摆动|An act of oscillating; a vibration.|The oscillation of the stock market made investors nervous. 股市的震荡让投资者感到紧张。|近义词：vibration, fluctuation, swing|反义词：stability, steadiness|词性:名词
