outwell:/ˌaʊtˈwɛl/| v.|涌出，流出；溢出|To flow out; to well out.|Tears outwelled from her eyes.  眼泪从她的眼中涌出。|近义词：gush, overflow, spill, pour|反义词: seep in, trickle in|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old English *ūtwellan, from *ūt (out) + *wellan (to well, to boil, to spring up).
