half-made:/ˌhɑːfˈmeɪd/ | adj. | 半成品的，未完成的 | Something that is half-made is only partly completed. | The dress was half-made, with pins still holding the seams together. 这件衣服只做了一半，接缝处还用别针别着。|  The project was half-made when the funding was cut.  这个项目做到一半时资金就被削减了。| Near synonyms: unfinished, incomplete, partially completed | Near antonyms: finished, complete, whole | adjective
