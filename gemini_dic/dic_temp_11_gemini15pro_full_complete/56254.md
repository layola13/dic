dogberry:/ˈdɒɡbəri/ (US) /ˈdɔːɡˌberi/ (UK)| n.|山楂；（因莎士比亚剧作而出名的）道格贝里式人物；愚蠢自负的低级官员|A foolish, self-important, and officious person, like the character Dogberry in Shakespeare's *Much Ado About Nothing*.|例句：He acted like a complete dogberry, misusing words and misunderstanding everything. 他表现得像个十足的道格贝里，用词不当，什么都理解错。

例句：The local councilor was a real dogberry, full of pompous pronouncements and empty promises.  这位地方议员是个十足的道格贝里，满口浮夸的声明和空洞的承诺。

近义词：buffoon, clown, fool, simpleton|反义词:sage, genius|词性:noun
