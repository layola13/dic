incorrupt:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌpt/
adjective
不腐败的，正直的，清廉的
Not susceptible to corruption, especially bribery; honest.
The judge was known for his incorrupt nature.  法官以其清廉的本性而闻名。
He was an incorrupt official in a corrupt government. 他在一个腐败的政府中是一位清廉的官员。

近义词:honest, upright, honorable, virtuous, ethical, principled
反义词:corrupt, dishonest, crooked, unethical, unprincipled
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *incorruptus*, from in- ‘not’ + corruptus (see corrupt).
