hypoblastic:/ˌhaɪpəˈblæstɪk/
adjective
下胚层的，下胚叶的
Relating to or derived from the hypoblast, the inner layer of cells in the early embryo.
The hypoblastic layer of the embryo eventually contributes to the formation of the yolk sac.  下胚层最终会促成卵黄囊的形成。
The researchers studied the differentiation of hypoblastic cells in vitro. 研究人员在体外研究了下胚层细胞的分化。
近义词: endodermal, entodermal
反义词: epiblastic
词性: adjective
