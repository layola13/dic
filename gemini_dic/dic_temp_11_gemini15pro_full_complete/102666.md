jambing:/ˈdʒæmɪŋ/
Verb
塞紧; 挤满; 卡住
The action of jamming something, especially a part of the body, into a small space.
He was jambing clothes into his suitcase. 他正把衣服塞进手提箱里。
Synonyms:  cramming, stuffing, packing, wedging
Antonyms:  removing, extracting, pulling out
词性: 动词
