cow:/kaʊ/
n.
母牛，奶牛
A fully grown female animal of a domesticated breed of ox, kept to produce milk or beef.
The farmer milked the cow every morning.  农民每天早上挤牛奶。
近义词:heifer (young female cow), bovine
反义词:bull (male cow)
词性:noun


cow:/kaʊ/
vt.
恐吓，威胁
To frighten someone into doing something using threats or violence.
The bully tried to cow the smaller children into giving him their lunch money.  恶霸试图恐吓较小的孩子，让他们把午餐钱给他。
近义词:intimidate, frighten, bully
反义词:embolden, encourage
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cou*, from Old English *cū* (female cow), from Proto-Germanic *kōuz* (cow), from Proto-Indo-European *gʷōws* (cow, ox). Cognate with Dutch *koe*, German *Kuh*, Latin *bōs* (ox, cow).
