cornicle:/ˈkɔːrnɪk(ə)l/ | n. | 角状管; 蜜管 | A small tube-like projection on the abdomen of aphids and some other insects, through which they excrete a sugary fluid called honeydew. | Aphids use their cornicles to secrete a sticky substance that deters predators. 蚜虫用它们的角状管分泌一种粘性物质来阻止捕食者。| synonym: siphon, tube | antonym: N/A | noun
