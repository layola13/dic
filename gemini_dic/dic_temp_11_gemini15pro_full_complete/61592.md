emeritus:/ɪˈmɛrɪtəs/| adj.|退休的，荣誉退休的|Having retired but retaining an honorary title corresponding to that held immediately before retirement.|Professor Jones is now emeritus professor of physics.  琼斯教授现在是物理学荣誉退休教授。|Retired, honorary|Working, active|adjective

emerita:/ɪˈmɛrɪtə/| adj.|退休的，荣誉退休的 (用于女性)|Having retired but retaining an honorary title corresponding to that held immediately before retirement (used for women).|Dr. Smith is now emerita professor of biology. 史密斯博士现在是生物学荣誉退休教授。|Retired, honorary|Working, active|adjective
