assimilator:/əˈsɪməleɪtər/| n.|同化者；吸收者；同化器|Someone or something that assimilates, absorbs, or incorporates something else into itself.|The company is a powerful assimilator of new ideas and technologies. 这家公司是一个强大的新思想和新技术的吸收者。|近义词：absorber, integrator, incorporator|反义词: segregator, separator|noun

assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/| v.|同化；吸收；消化|To absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture.|Immigrants often struggle to assimilate into a new culture. 移民通常很难融入新的文化。|近义词：absorb, integrate, incorporate|反义词: segregate, separate|verb
