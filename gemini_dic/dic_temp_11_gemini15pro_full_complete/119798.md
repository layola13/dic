migrator:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtər/| n.|迁徙者，移民|A person or animal that migrates.  A person who moves from one place to another, especially in order to find work or better living conditions.|Many birds are migrators, flying south for the winter. 许多鸟类都是迁徙者，冬天飞往南方。|The seasonal migrators followed the harvest north.  季节性移民随着收成向北迁移。|近义词：immigrant, emigrant, traveler, nomad|反义词:resident, settler|noun
