bragged:/bræɡd/ | v. | 吹嘘，夸耀 | To speak with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or qualities. | He bragged about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。| Synonyms: boasted, crowed, vaunted | Antonyms: downplayed, minimized | verb

bragged:/bræɡd/ | v. | 吹嘘，夸耀 |  Past tense and past participle of "brag."  It means to talk boastfully about something.  | She bragged about her high score on the test.  她吹嘘自己考试得了高分。 | Synonyms: boasted, gloated, preened | Antonyms:  downplayed, understated | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *braggen*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *bragn* (“melody, song”), or a variant of *crack*.
