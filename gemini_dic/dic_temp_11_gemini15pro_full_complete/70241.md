fictionalised: /ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 虚构化的，小说化的 |  Made into or presented as fiction; given a fictional character.  Based on real events but with changes made to make it more like a story. | The movie is a fictionalised account of his life. 这部电影是对他人生的虚构描述。 |  fictionalized, novelized, dramatized, romanticized | factual, historical, true | adjective

fictionalised: /ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzd/ | v. | 将…小说化; 为…创作小说 | To make something into fiction, often by changing or adding details. | The author fictionalised the story of his grandfather's life. 作者将祖父的人生故事小说化了。 | fictionalize, novelize, dramatize | document, report | verb
