lumbago:/lʌmˈbeɪɡoʊ/ (US) /lʌmˈbeɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. |腰痛|A sudden pain or stiffness in the lower back.|例句：He suffers from lumbago. 他患有腰痛。

例句：Gardening often gives me lumbago.  园艺活经常让我腰痛。


|近义词：backache, lower back pain|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "comfort," "ease," or "well-being" as antonyms to the state of pain.) |词性: noun
