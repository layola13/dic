force-out:/ˈfɔːrs aʊt/| n.|强制出局，逼退|A play in baseball in which a runner is retired because a fielder holding the ball touches a base to which the runner is forced to run.|The shortstop made a quick throw to second for the force-out.|近义词：putout, retirement|反义词: safe|词性:名词

force-out:/fɔːrs ˈaʊt/| vt.|强制出局，逼退|To cause a runner to be retired in baseball by holding the ball and touching a base to which the runner is forced to run.|The pitcher forced out the runner at second.|近义词：retire, put out|反义词: advance|词性:动词
