grainiest:/ˈɡreɪnɪɪst/| adj.|颗粒最粗的，最粗糙的|Having the most prominent grains; most granular. The image was grainy, almost pixelated, but it was the grainiest one they had.|例句：The grainiest sand is often used for industrial purposes. 颗粒最粗的沙子通常用于工业用途。

例句：The photographer preferred the grainiest film for its artistic effect. 摄影师偏爱颗粒最粗的胶片，因为它具有艺术效果。|近义词：coarsest, roughest, granular, gritty|反义词：smoothest, finest, softest|词性:形容词
词源:From grain + -y + -est.  "grain" comes from Old French "grein," ultimately from Latin "granum."  "-y" forms adjectives indicating having the quality of, and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
