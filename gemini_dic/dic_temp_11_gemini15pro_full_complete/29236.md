cassation:/kəˈseɪʃən/ | n. |撤销，废除；最高上诉法院|The court of cassation overturned the lower court's ruling. 最高上诉法院推翻了下级法院的裁决。| annulment, revocation, abrogation | ratification, confirmation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cassation*, from *casser* "to break", from Latin *quassare* "to shake, shatter", frequentative of *quatere* "to shake".
