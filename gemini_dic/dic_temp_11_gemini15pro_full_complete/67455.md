exogenous:/ɛkˈsɒdʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 外生的，外源的 | Originating or produced from outside; not inherent or internal. |  Exogenous factors, such as sunlight and rainfall, affect plant growth. 外部因素，例如阳光和降雨，会影响植物的生长。| Example: The exogenous application of growth hormones can stimulate plant development.  外源生长激素的施用可以刺激植物发育。 | synonyms: external, outer, outside, extrinsic | antonyms: endogenous, internal, intrinsic | adjective
