miscible:/ˈmɪsɪb(ə)l/| adj.|可混合的，互溶的|Capable of being mixed together; forming a homogeneous solution.|例句：Water and alcohol are miscible liquids. 水和酒精是互溶的液体。

例句：Oil and water are not miscible. 油和水不能互溶。|近义词：mixable, blendable, soluble (especially with liquids)|反义词：immiscible, insoluble|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *miscibilis, from Latin miscēre "to mix".
