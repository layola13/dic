infraorbital:/ˌɪnfrəˈɔːrbɪtəl/
adjective

Located beneath the orbit (eye socket).
The infraorbital nerve supplies sensation to the lower eyelid, cheek, and upper lip.  眶下神经支配下眼睑、脸颊和上唇的感觉。
The surgeon made an infraorbital incision.  外科医生做了眶下切口。
Synonyms: suborbital
Antonyms: supraorbital
word class: adjective
