federating:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪŋ/
v.|联合，组成联邦|The act of uniting separate entities, such as states or organizations, into a federation.  |Several small businesses are federating to increase their buying power.  几个小企业联合起来以提高他们的购买力。|uniting, combining, joining, amalgamating|separating, dividing, splitting|verb
federating:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪŋ/
adj.|联合的，联邦制的|Descriptive of the act of uniting or the state of being united in a federation. |They are forming a federating body to represent all the regional groups. 他们正在组建一个代表所有区域团体的联合机构。|united, allied, combined, joint|separate, independent, individual|adjective
