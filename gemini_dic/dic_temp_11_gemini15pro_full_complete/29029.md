cartoonery:/kɑːrˈtuːnəri/
noun
滑稽模仿；卡通风格；卡通作品
the exaggerated representation of someone or something, usually in a humorous way; cartoons or the art of creating them
The play was full of cartoonery, with exaggerated characters and slapstick humor. 这部戏充满了滑稽模仿，夸张的人物和闹剧式的幽默。
synonyms: caricature, parody, burlesque, lampoon, satire, mockery
antonyms: realism, seriousness, accuracy
