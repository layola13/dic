bipedalism:/baɪˈpɛdəlɪzəm/| n.|双足行走|The condition of having two feet and habitually walking upright.  The habitual locomotion of animals using only two rear limbs.|Human bipedalism is a defining characteristic that separates us from other primates.|Synonym:bipedality|Antonym:quadrupedalism|noun
