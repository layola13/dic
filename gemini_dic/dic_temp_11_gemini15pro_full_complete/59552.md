eavesdropped:/ˈiːvzdrɒpt/ (US) /ˈiːvzdrɒpt/ (UK)| v. | 偷听；窃听 | To secretly listen to a conversation.|  I eavesdropped on their conversation and learned they were planning a surprise party. 我偷听了他们的谈话，得知他们正在计划一个惊喜派对。|  She eavesdropped on her parents' argument from her bedroom. 她从卧室里偷听了父母的争吵。|  Synonyms: overhear, listen in, spy | Antonyms: ignore, disregard | verb
