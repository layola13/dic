meekly: /ˈmiːkli/ | adv. | 温顺地，顺从地 | In a quiet, gentle, and submissive manner; humbly. | She meekly accepted his criticism. 她温顺地接受了他的批评。| meekly apologized for the interruption. 他温顺地为打扰道歉了。| 近义词: humbly, submissively, gently, quietly | 反义词: boldly, defiantly, aggressively | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *mekeli, from Old Norse *mjúkliga (“softly, meekly”), from Proto-Germanic *mjuklīkō (“softly”), derived from *mjuk (“soft”). Cognate with Swedish mjukligen (“softly, meekly”), Danish *mjøklig (“soft”).  Related to meek.
