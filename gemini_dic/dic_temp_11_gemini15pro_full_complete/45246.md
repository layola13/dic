cultch:/kʌltʃ/
n.
牡蛎养殖场; 牡蛎壳; 贝壳碎片
Material, such as oyster shells, used to form a bed for oyster propagation. Also, any rough material used to collect oyster spat.
Oyster farmers spread cultch on the seabed to encourage oyster larvae to settle and grow.  牡蛎养殖者将贝壳碎片撒在海床上，以鼓励牡蛎幼虫定居和生长。
The old oyster shells provide cultch for new oysters to grow on.  旧牡蛎壳为新牡蛎的生长提供了附着物。
Synonyms: oyster bed, spat collector, substrate
Antonyms: None
