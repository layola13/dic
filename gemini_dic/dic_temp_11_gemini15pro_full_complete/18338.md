belligerence:/bəˈlɪdʒərəns/| n.|好战性，交战状态，斗争性|A hostile or warlike attitude or nature; the act of warfare.|His belligerence surprised everyone, as he was usually so mild-mannered. 他的好战性让每个人都感到惊讶，因为他通常都非常温文尔雅。|Example: The country's belligerence led to war.  这个国家的好战性导致了战争。|Synonyms: aggressiveness, hostility, pugnacity, combativeness|Antonyms: peacefulness, pacifism, friendliness, docility|noun

belligerent:/bəˈlɪdʒərənt/| adj.|好战的，交战的|Inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness, hostility, or combativeness.|Her belligerent attitude made it difficult to work with her. 她好斗的态度使人很难与她共事。|Example: The belligerent nations finally agreed to a ceasefire. 交战国最终同意停火。|Synonyms: aggressive, hostile, pugnacious, combative|Antonyms: peaceful, pacifistic, friendly, docile|adjective
