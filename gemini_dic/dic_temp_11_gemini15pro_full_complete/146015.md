overdrafting:/ˈoʊvərˌdræftɪŋ/
n.
透支
The act of withdrawing more money from a bank account than is available, resulting in a negative balance.
He was charged a fee for overdrafting his account.  他因透支账户而被收取费用。

overdraft:/ˈoʊvərˌdræft/
n.
透支
An amount of money withdrawn from a bank account that is greater than the balance, resulting in a negative balance.
I had to pay an overdraft fee. 我不得不支付透支费。
v.
透支
To withdraw more money from a bank account than is available.
I accidentally overdrafted my account when I bought groceries. 我买杂货时不小心透支了我的账户。



近义词：overdrawing
反义词：depositing


词性: noun, verb (depending on context - overdrafting is the noun, overdraft can be noun or verb)
