nonactualness:/ˌnɒnˈæktʃuəlnəs/
n.
非现实性；非实际性；虚构性
The state or quality of not being actual or real; hypotheticality.
The author explored the nonactualness of the utopian society in his novel.  作者在他的小说中探讨了乌托邦社会的非现实性。

The nonactualness of the proposed solution made it impractical.  提出的解决方案的非实际性使其无法实施。

近义词:unreality, hypotheticality, fictionality
反义词:reality, actuality, factuality
词性:noun

词源: non- (not) + actual + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality)  来自non-（不）+ actual（实际的）+ -ness（构成名词的后缀，表示状态或性质）
