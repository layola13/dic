dispeople:/dɪsˈpiːpəl/
|vt.|使（某地）人口减少；驱逐人口|To deprive (a region) of inhabitants; depopulate.|The invading army dispeopled the countryside, forcing villagers to flee their homes.|depopulate, evacuate|populate, inhabit|verb
dispeople:/dɪsˈpiːpəl/
|n.|（被驱逐的）人们|People who have been displaced or driven from their homes.|The dispeople wandered the land, searching for a new place to settle.|refugees, exiles|settlers, residents|noun
