microdont:/ˈmaɪkroʊdɑːnt/| adj. n.|小齿的，小齿动物|Having small teeth; an animal with small teeth. |例句：The microdont shrew has remarkably small teeth. 小齿鼩鼱的牙齿非常小。

例句：Microdont fossils are rare and valuable to paleontologists. 小齿动物化石很稀有，对古生物学家来说很有价值。|近义词：small-toothed|反义词: macrodont (large-toothed)|词性: adjective, noun


词源:  From micro- (small) + -dont (tooth). Ultimately from Greek *mikrós* (small) and *odoús* (tooth).
