lumped:/lʌmpt/| adj.|成块的，总括的，集中在一起的|treated as a single entity, without considering individual differences|The expenses were all lumped together in one bill.  费用都集中在一张账单里。|aggregated, combined, collective|separate, individual|adjective
lump:/lʌmp/| n.|块，肿块|a compact mass of a substance, especially one without a definite shape|She found a lump in her breast. 她发现乳房里有个肿块。|mass, chunk, clod|speck, grain|noun
lump:/lʌmp/| v.|使成块状，集中放置|put or treat (a number of things) together|We can lump these small orders together and ship them all at once. 我们可以把这些小订单集中起来一起发货。|combine, group, consolidate|separate, divide|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lumpen*, from or akin to Middle Low German *lump*, *lumpe* (“lump, clod, mass”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly ultimately from a Proto-Germanic *\*lumpaz*, from Proto-Indo-European *\*lewb-* (“to peel, strip, bark”).
