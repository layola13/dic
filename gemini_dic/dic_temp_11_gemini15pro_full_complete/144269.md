outsophisticated:/ˌaʊtsəˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.| 过于世故的，矫揉造作的，不自然的 |  Surpassing or exceeding in sophistication, often to the point of being unnatural or affected.  Appearing overly refined or worldly in a way that is not genuine. | Her outsophisticated mannerisms seemed forced and unnatural. 她过于世故的举止显得做作而不自然。 | Synonyms: affected, pretentious, artificial, mannered, contrived | Antonyms: natural, genuine, unaffected, artless, simple | adjective
