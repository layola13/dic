occasioned:/əˈkeɪʒənd/
verb
引起，造成，使发生
caused or brought about something
The heavy rain occasioned the flood.  暴雨造成了洪水。
Her sudden departure occasioned much speculation. 她的突然离开引起了很多猜测。
近义词:caused, brought about, led to, precipitated, prompted
反义词:prevented, averted, hindered
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *occasionatus, past participle of occasionare "to cause, bring about," from Latin occasio "opportunity, occasion" (see occasion).
