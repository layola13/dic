incantator:/ɪnˈkænteɪtər/ | n. | 施咒者，念咒者 | A person who incants; a sorcerer or wizard. | The incantator muttered the words of power, and the earth trembled. 施咒者低声念着咒语，大地颤抖起来。 |  incantation, magician, sorcerer, wizard, witch |  None | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin incantator, from Latin incantare "to enchant, charm," from in- "in, on" + cantare "to sing."
