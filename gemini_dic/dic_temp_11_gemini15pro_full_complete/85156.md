hardtop:/ˈhɑːrdtɒp/ (UK), /ˈhɑːrdtɑːp/ (US)| n. adj.|硬顶，硬顶汽车，硬顶敞篷车|A car with a rigid, non-detachable roof, or the roof itself, often used in contrast to a convertible.  A hardtop can also refer to a boat with a rigid roof.|例句：He bought a classic hardtop convertible. 他买了一辆经典的硬顶敞篷车。|近义词：coupe, closed car|反义词：convertible, soft-top|n.
hardtop:/ˈhɑːrdtɒp/ (UK), /ˈhɑːrdtɑːp/ (US)| adj.|硬顶的|Having a fixed, rigid roof.|例句：The hardtop version of the car was more popular in colder climates. 这款车的硬顶版本在气候较冷的地区更受欢迎。|近义词：fixed-roof|反义词：convertible, soft-top|adj.
