overcommunication:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度沟通；过度交流 | The act of communicating too much, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency.  |  Example: Overcommunication can sometimes lead to confusion rather than clarity. 过度沟通有时会导致混乱而不是清晰。 | Synonyms: oversharing, excessive communication | Antonyms: undercommunication, lack of communication | noun

overcommunication:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度沟通；过度交流 |  Sometimes, less is more, and overcommunication can overwhelm the recipient. 有时，少即是多，过度沟通会让接收者不知所措。 | Synonyms: information overload, excessive communication | Antonyms: concise communication, brevity | noun
