bandlike: /ˈbændˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 带状的，像带子一样的 | Resembling a band; having the form of a long, narrow strip. | The clouds were bandlike across the sky.  云在天空中呈带状。| Similar: ribbonlike, striplike, beltlike | Opposite: shapeless, irregular | adjective

Etymology:
band + -like. "Band" comes from Old Norse "band," meaning "a bond, tie, fetter."  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-lic."
