deflator:/dɪˈfleɪtər/ | n. | 通货紧缩因素；平减指数 | Something that deflates or reduces inflation, typically a decline in spending or an increase in taxes. |  The government implemented several deflators to curb rising inflation. 政府实施了几项平减措施以抑制通货膨胀。| synonyms: moderator, reducer, damper | antonyms: inflator, stimulant | noun

deflators:/dɪˈfleɪtərz/ | n. | 通货紧缩因素（复数）; 平减指数（复数） | Plural form of "deflator," referring to multiple factors that deflate or reduce inflation. | Several economic deflators were at play, contributing to the slowing economy.  几个经济平减因素在起作用，导致经济放缓。| synonyms: moderators, reducers, dampers | antonyms: inflators, stimulants | noun
