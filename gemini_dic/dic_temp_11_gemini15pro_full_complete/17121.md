batten:/ˈbætən/ (US) /ˈbætən/ (UK)|n. v.|板条，压条；使…肥沃；养肥；大吃大喝|A long, flat strip of wood or metal used especially for fastening things together or as a support.|The stage crew used battens to secure the backdrop.  舞台工作人员用板条固定背景幕布。|近义词:strip, lath, plank|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 动词

batten:/ˈbætən/ (US) /ˈbætən/ (UK)|v.|使…肥沃；养肥；大吃大喝|To thrive, especially at the expense of another; to feast.|The pigs battened on the scraps from the kitchen. 猪靠厨房的残羹剩饭养肥了。|近义词: thrive, flourish, feast|反义词: starve, deprive|词性:动词

词源: From Old North French *baton, from Vulgar Latin *bastō (“stick”), from Late Latin bastum (“pack-saddle made of bast”), from bast (“inner bark of trees”).
