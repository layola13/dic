lubbard:/ˈlʌbərd/| n.|懒汉，笨蛋|A lazy, clumsy person.  A person who is idle and gluttonous.|He was such a lubbard, he slept through his alarm and missed work. 他真是个懒汉，睡过了闹钟，错过了工作。|Synonyms: loafer, idler, slug, drone, sluggard|Antonyms: hard worker, diligent person|Noun

Etymology->
From Middle English *lobard*, from *lobbe* (“lout”) + *-ard* (pejorative suffix).
