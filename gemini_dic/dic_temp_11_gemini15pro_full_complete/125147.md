multi-tool:/ˈmʌltiˌtuːl/ /ˈmʌltaɪˌtuːl/| n.|多功能工具|A hand tool that combines several individual functions in a single unit. | He always carries a multi-tool on his camping trips. 他在露营旅行时总是随身携带一把多功能工具。|Synonyms: multi-purpose tool, Swiss Army knife|Antonyms: single-purpose tool|noun

multi-tool:/ˈmʌltiˌtuːl/ /ˈmʌltaɪˌtuːl/| adj.(rarely used) |多功能的|Having multiple tools or functions. |While not a common usage, one might describe a particularly versatile device as "multi-tool" in nature. 虽然不常用，但人们可能会将一种特别通用的设备描述为“多功能工具”性质。 |Synonyms: multipurpose, versatile|Antonyms: specialized, single-function|adjective
