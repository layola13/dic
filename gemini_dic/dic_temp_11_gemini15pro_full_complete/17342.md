beached:/biːtʃt/
adjective
搁浅的，被冲上岸的
(of a boat or sea creature)  unable to move off a beach after the tide has gone out, or left on a beach by the tide.
The beached whale was a sad sight.  搁浅的鲸鱼是一幅悲伤的景象。
近义词:stranded, grounded
反义词:afloat, launched
词性:adjective

beached:/biːtʃt/
verb
使搁浅，使冲上岸
to cause (a boat or sea creature) to become unable to move off a beach after the tide has gone out, or to leave (a boat) on a beach.
The storm beached several boats.  暴风雨使几艘船搁浅了。
近义词:strand, ground
反义词:launch, float
词性:verb
