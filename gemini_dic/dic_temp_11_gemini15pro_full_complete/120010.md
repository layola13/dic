milliner:/ˈmɪlɪnər/| n.|女帽商；女帽制造者|A person who designs, makes, or sells hats, especially women's hats.|例句：The milliner created a stunning hat for the wedding.  这位女帽商为婚礼制作了一顶漂亮的帽子。

例句：She visited the milliner to find the perfect hat for the races. 她去拜访了女帽商，想为赛马会找到一顶完美的帽子。|近义词：hatter, hat maker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mylloner, from Milaner, from Milan, Italy, where hats were fashionable.  来自于中古英语 *mylloner，源自 Milaner，指来自意大利米兰，那里帽子很时尚。
