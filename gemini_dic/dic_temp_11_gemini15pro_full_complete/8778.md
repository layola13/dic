antiecclesiastical:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪkəl/
adj.
反对教会的；非教会的
Opposed to the church or its practices; secular.
The writer's antiecclesiastical views were evident in his satirical portrayal of the clergy.  这位作家的反教会观点在他对神职人员的讽刺性描写中显而易见。
The museum housed a collection of antiecclesiastical art, including works that challenged religious dogma.  博物馆收藏了一批反教会艺术品，包括一些挑战宗教教义的作品。
近义词:anticlerical, secular, irreligious
反义词:religious, pious, devout
词性:adjective

词源:
anti- (prefix meaning "against") + ecclesiastical (adjective relating to the church)
