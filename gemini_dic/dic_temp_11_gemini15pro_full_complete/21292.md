bloodroot:/ˈblʌdruːt/| n.|血根草；美洲血根草|A North American plant (Sanguinaria canadensis) with a reddish root and sap, and white flowers. It is also known for its medicinal properties.|The bloodroot bloomed early this spring.  今年春天血根草开得很早。|Synonyms:  bloodwort, tetterwort|Antonyms:  None (specific plant, no direct opposite)|noun

bloodroot:/ˈblʌdruːt/| n.|血根碱|The orange-red sap of the bloodroot plant, used as a dye and in traditional medicine.|Bloodroot extract was used by some Native American tribes as a dye.  一些美洲原住民部落用血根草提取物作为染料。|Synonyms:  sanguinarine|Antonyms:  None (specific chemical compound)|noun
