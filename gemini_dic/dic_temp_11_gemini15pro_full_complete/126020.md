muss:/mʌs/| n. |混乱，脏乱;  (尤指头发的)蓬乱; 争吵，打架|A state of disorder or untidiness, especially of the hair; a brawl or fight.|The room was in a complete muss after the party. 房间在派对后一片狼藉。|近义词：mess, disarray, clutter, brawl, fight|反义词：order, tidiness, neatness|词性:名词

muss:/mʌs/| vt. |弄乱，弄脏; 粗暴地对待|To make untidy or messy; to handle roughly.|Don't muss my hair! 别弄乱我的头发！|近义词：dishevel, rumple, tousle, manhandle|反义词：neaten, tidy, arrange|词性:动词
