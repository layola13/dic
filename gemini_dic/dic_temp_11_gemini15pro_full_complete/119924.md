milkily:/ˈmɪlkɪli/| adv.|乳状地；乳白色地；柔和地|Resembling milk in color or consistency; softly, gently.|The moon shone milkily through the clouds. 月光柔和地透过云层。|近义词：softly, gently, palely|反义词:harshly, brightly|词性:副词
Etymology: From milk + -y + -ly.  "Milk" comes from Old English *meolc (West Saxon), milc (Anglian), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *miluk-. The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of," while "-ly" forms adverbs.
