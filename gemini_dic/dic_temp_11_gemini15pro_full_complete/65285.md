euclase:/ˈjuːkleɪz/| n. |绿柱石|A rare beryllium aluminum silicate mineral, BeAlSiO₄(OH), occurring in light-colored prismatic crystals. It is valued as a gemstone.|例句：The museum displayed a beautiful specimen of euclase. 博物馆展出了一块美丽的绿柱石标本。

例句：Euclase is a relatively rare mineral sought after by collectors. 绿柱石是一种相对稀有的矿物，受到收藏家的追捧。|近义词：beryl (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *euclase*, from Ancient Greek εὖ (eû, “well, good”) + κλάσις (klásis, “fracture, breaking”), referring to its easy cleavage.
