intercurl:/ˌɪntərˈkɜːrl/| v.|互相卷曲，交织卷曲|To curl or twist together; to intertwine.|The vines intercurled around the trellis. 藤蔓在棚架上相互缠绕。|近义词：intertwine, interweave, entwine|反义词:untangle, straighten|词性:动词

Etymology:
From inter- + curl, from Middle English curlen, from Old North French curler, from Vulgar Latin *corlare, from Latin corollarium "garland, crown," from corolla "small crown," diminutive of corona "crown."
