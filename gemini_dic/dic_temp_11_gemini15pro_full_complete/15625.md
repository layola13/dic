PHRASE->
bain de soleil (n.): sunbath 日光浴
bain marie (n.):  a double boiler; a pan of hot water in which a container is placed for slow cooking.  隔水炖锅；双层锅
bain-marie cooker:  a container holding hot water into which a pan is placed for slow cooking. 双层锅炉
bain de foule (n.):  walkabout; an informal stroll among a crowd of people, especially by a politician.  (尤指政治人物在人群中的)非正式走访，与民众见面
bain public (n.): public baths 公共浴室


注意事项: 
bain 在现代英语中很少使用，bath 是更常见的词汇。 Bain 主要用于法语或一些专业的烹饪术语中。
