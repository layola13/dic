cleated:/ˈkliːtɪd/| adj.|带防滑钉的，有防滑装置的|Furnished with cleats, especially for traction.  A cleat is a metal or plastic projection on the sole of a shoe or boot, used to improve grip on soft or slippery surfaces.|The football player wore cleated shoes for better traction on the field. 足球运动员穿着带防滑钉的鞋子以便在球场上获得更好的抓地力。|近义词：studded, spiked|反义词:smooth, flat|词性:形容词
