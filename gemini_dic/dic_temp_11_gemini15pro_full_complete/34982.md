cloggiest:/ˈklɒɡiɪst/ /ˈklɑːɡiɪst/| adj.|最阻塞的，最堵塞的|superlative adjective of cloggy; most cloggy|The cloggiest drain I've ever seen was completely blocked with hair and grease. 我见过的最堵塞的下水道完全被头发和油脂堵住了。|The narrow, winding streets were the cloggiest during rush hour. 在高峰时段，狭窄蜿蜒的街道是最拥堵的。|近义词：most blocked, most congested, most obstructed|反义词: clearest, freest, most open|词性:形容词

cloggy:/ˈklɒɡi/ /ˈklɑːɡi/| adj.|阻塞的，堵塞的|thick and sticky; tending to clog|The batter was too cloggy, so the pancakes were dense. 面糊太稠了，所以煎饼很厚。|The old pipes were cloggy with rust and sediment. 旧管道被铁锈和沉淀物堵塞了。|近义词：blocked, congested, obstructed|反义词: clear, free, open|词性:形容词

clog:/klɒɡ/ /klɑːɡ/| n. v.|木屐；障碍；堵塞；穿木屐|a shoe with a thick wooden sole; something that obstructs; to block or become blocked|She wore clogs in the garden. 她在花园里穿木屐。|The drain was clogged with hair. 下水道被头发堵塞了。|近义词：(n.) obstruction, blockage; (v.) block, obstruct|反义词: (v.) clear, unblock|词性:名词，动词

词源: From Middle English *clogge, from Old Norse *kloggi (“block of wood, log”), from Proto-Germanic *klukkaną (“to lump, to clot”).
