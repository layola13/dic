death-rattle:/ˈdeθ rætl/| n.|临终喘息，垂死挣扎|A gurgling or rattling sound sometimes heard in the throat of a dying person, caused by the passage of air through accumulated mucus.|The old man's death-rattle signaled the end was near. 老人临终的喘息预示着他的生命即将结束。|Synonyms:  death throes,  agonal breathing,  terminal respiration|Antonyms:  healthy breathing,  normal respiration|noun


death-rattle:/ˈdeθ rætl/| v.|发出临终喘息|To make a gurgling or rattling sound in the throat while dying.|He lay in bed, death-rattling for hours. 他躺在床上，发出临终喘息声，持续了几个小时。|Synonyms:  gasp,  choke,  struggle for breath|Antonyms:  breathe easily,  respire normally|verb
