billed:/bɪld/
|v.|开账单；宣传；把…宣传为| to charge or present a bill to someone; to advertise or describe someone or something in a particular way |  The plumber billed us for $200. 水管工给我们开了200美元的账单。| The movie was billed as a romantic comedy. 这部电影被宣传为一部浪漫喜剧。| synonyms: charged, invoiced, advertised | antonyms: paid, compensated | verb

billed:/bɪld/
|adj.|有喙的| having a beak | The billed bird pecked at the seeds. 那只长着喙的鸟啄食着种子。| synonyms: beaked | antonyms: beakless | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *billen*, from Old English *bill*, meaning "beak, bill." The financial sense developed from the practice of writing debts on slips of parchment.
