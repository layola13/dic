caster:/ˈkæstər (US), ˈkɑːstər (UK)| n.|脚轮；施法者；调味瓶|A small wheel attached to the bottom of a piece of furniture so that it can be moved easily.  Also, someone who casts spells, or a container with a perforated top for sprinkling sugar, pepper, etc.|例句：The caster on the chair is broken. 椅子上的脚轮坏了。

例句：He plays a caster in the online game. 他在网络游戏中扮演一个施法者。


近义词：wheel, roller, sorcerer, wizard, shaker, sprinkler|反义词:|词性:noun


caster:/ˈkæstər (US), ˈkɑːstər (UK)| n.|演员；投掷者|Someone who casts something, such as a fishing line or a vote.|例句：He's a good caster and can throw the line a long way. 他是一个优秀的投掷者，可以把鱼线抛得很远。

例句：Every caster has the right to vote. 每个投票者都有投票的权利。

近义词：thrower, pitcher, voter|反义词:|词性:noun
