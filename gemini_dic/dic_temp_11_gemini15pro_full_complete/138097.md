normativism:/ˈnɔːrmətɪvɪzəm/
n.
规范主义，唯规范主义
The doctrine that norms or standards determine the value of something.
Normativism in ethics emphasizes moral rules and duties. 伦理学中的规范主义强调道德规则和义务.
synonym: prescriptivism
antonym: descriptivism
noun
