bandle:/ˈbændl/| n. v. |捆, 束, 扎, 襁褓|A bandle is a small bundle, especially of sticks or straw.  As a verb, it means to tie or wrap something into a bandle.|例句：She bandled the sticks together for the fire. 她把柴火捆在一起准备生火。|近义词：bundle, sheaf, bunch|反义词: untie, separate |词性:noun, verb

bandle:/ˈbændl/| v. | 襁褓, 用襁褓裹|To wrap a baby in swaddling clothes.|例句：The baby was bandled snugly in a warm blanket.  婴儿被紧紧地裹在一张温暖的毯子里。|近义词：swaddle, wrap|反义词: unwrap |词性:verb
