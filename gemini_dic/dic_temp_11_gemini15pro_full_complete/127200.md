naturalize:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪz/ | v. | 使归化；移植；吸收 |To grant full citizenship to a foreigner. To introduce and establish (a plant or animal) in a new region or environment so that it flourishes as if native.  To adopt (a foreign word or practice) into a language or culture.|He was naturalized as a US citizen last year. 他去年加入美国国籍。 The plant has become naturalized in the area. 这种植物已在该地区归化。| 近义词: acclimatize, adapt, assimilate | 反义词: repatriate |词性: 动词
