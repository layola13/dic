fretter:/ˈfrɛtər/| n. v.|磨损者;烦躁的人;焦躁不安;|Someone who worries or frets constantly.|He's a terrible fretter - always worrying about something. 他是个非常爱烦躁的人——总是担心一些事情。|近义词：worrier, fusser|反义词：calm person, relaxed individual|词性:名词,动词

fretter:/ˈfrɛtər/| v.|磨损;焦躁不安;烦躁;腐蚀;|To worry or be anxious about something.|She fretted constantly about her children. 她一直担心她的孩子们。|近义词：worry, agonize|反义词：relax, calm down|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fretan "to devour, consume," from Proto-Germanic *frataną "to gnaw, rub," from PIE *gʰer- "to rub, scrape."  The sense of "to worry" developed from the idea of being "gnawed at" by anxiety.
