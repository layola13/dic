ect:/ɪkt/| abbreviation |等等，及其他|Short for "et cetera," meaning "and other things" or "and so forth."|例句：The symptoms include fever, chills, headache, ect. 症状包括发烧、发冷、头痛等等。|近义词：etc., and so forth, and so on|反义词:|abbreviation
