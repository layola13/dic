demotes:/dɪˈmoʊts/| v. |降级，降职|To lower in rank or position; relegate. |The manager demoted him from senior manager to assistant manager. 经理把他从高级经理降为助理经理。| He was demoted for his poor performance.  由于表现不佳，他被降职了。| synonyms: relegate, downgrade, lower, reduce| antonyms: promote, elevate, upgrade, advance| verb
