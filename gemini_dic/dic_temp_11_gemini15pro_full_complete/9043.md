antimechanist:/ˌæntiˈmɛkənɪst/ | n. | 反机械论者；非机械论者 |  A person who opposes the philosophical doctrine of mechanism, which explains natural phenomena in terms of purely mechanical principles, especially as applied to living organisms. | The antimechanist argued that life was more than just a complex machine.  反机械论者认为，生命不仅仅是一台复杂的机器。|  vitalist, organicist | mechanist, reductionist | noun
