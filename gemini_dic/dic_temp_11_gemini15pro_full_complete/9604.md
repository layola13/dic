antispeculative:/ˌæntiˈspɛkjʊlətɪv/
adjective
反投机的；防止投机的
Designed to prevent or discourage speculation, especially in finance.
The government introduced antispeculative measures to curb the housing bubble. 政府出台了反投机措施以抑制房地产泡沫。
The central bank implemented antispeculative policies to stabilize the currency market. 中央银行实施了反投机政策以稳定货币市场。
近义词: non-speculative, precautionary
反义词: speculative
