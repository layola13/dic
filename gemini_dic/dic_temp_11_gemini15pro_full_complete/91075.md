hutlike:/ˈhʌtlaɪk/
adjective
像小屋的；简陋的
Resembling a hut; small and simple.
The hermit lived in a hutlike dwelling in the woods.  隐士住在森林里一个像小屋一样的住所。
The building was small and hutlike. 这座建筑物小巧，像个小屋。
similar, basic, primitive
grand, luxurious
adjective
