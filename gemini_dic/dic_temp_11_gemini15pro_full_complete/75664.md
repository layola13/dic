fulcrum:/ˈfʊlkrəm/ /ˈfʌlkrəm/| n. |支点；杠杆支点；关键 |A thing that plays a central or essential role in an activity, event, or situation.|The fulcrum of the lever shifted, causing the load to fall. 杠杆的支点移动了，导致负载掉落。|synonyms: pivot, axis, center, support|antonyms: periphery, edge, extremity|noun

fulcra:/ˈfʌlkrə/ /ˈfʊlkrə/| n. |支点（fulcrum 的复数）|The plural form of fulcrum, referring to multiple points of support or leverage.|The seesaw balanced perfectly on its fulcra. 跷跷板完美地平衡在它的支点上。|synonyms: pivots, axes, centers, supports|antonyms: peripheries, edges, extremities|noun
