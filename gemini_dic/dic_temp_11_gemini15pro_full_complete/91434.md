hygienes:/ˈhaɪdʒiːnz/
noun
卫生；卫生学；保健法
conditions or practices conducive to maintaining health and preventing disease, especially through cleanliness.
例句：Poor hygiene can lead to illness.  不良的卫生习惯会导致疾病。

例句：Dental hygiene is important for preventing cavities.  牙齿卫生对于预防蛀牙很重要。
近义词：cleanliness, sanitation
反义词：filth, dirtiness
词性：n.



ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hygiène*, from Ancient Greek *ὑγιεινή (hugieinḗ)*  meaning "healthful," from *ὑγιής (hugiḗs)* meaning "healthy, sound." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *aug-/* meaning "to increase, grow."
