earshot:/ˈɪrʃɒt/ (UK), /ˈɪrʃɑːt/ (US)| n. |听力范围，听距|The distance within which a sound can be heard. |She whispered, so only those within earshot could hear her. 她低声说话，所以只有在她听力范围内的人才能听到。|例句：He was just within earshot of the conversation. 他正好在谈话的听力范围内。

例句：The children were playing out of earshot. 孩子们在听不到的地方玩耍。


近义词：hearing distance, range of hearing|反义词: out of range|词性:noun
