localization:/ˌloʊkəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 本地化；局限；定位 |The process of adapting a product or content to a specific locale or market.  The act of determining the location of something. |  The company invested heavily in the localization of its software for the Chinese market.  该公司对其软件的中文本地化投入了巨资。

The localization of the pain was difficult. 疼痛的定位很困难。 | 近义词：adaptation, translation, pinpoint | 反义词：globalization, internationalization | 词性: noun

词源: From the Late Latin word *localis* (of a place), derived from *locus* (place).  The suffix "-ization" denotes the process of making or becoming.
