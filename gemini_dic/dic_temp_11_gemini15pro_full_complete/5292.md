allotment: /əˈlɒtmənt/ (UK), /əˈlɑːtmənt/ (US)| n.|分配；份额；小块菜园；拨款|A share or portion of something allocated to someone or something.  The process of allocating something.|例句：Each family on the list received an allotment of land. 名单上的每个家庭都获得了一块土地。

例句：The allotment of funds for the project has been approved.  该项目的资金分配已获批准。|近义词：allocation, share, portion, ration, assignment|反义词:confiscation, seizure|词性:noun

allot: /əˈlɒt/ (UK), /əˈlɑːt/ (US)| vt.|分配；拨给|To give or apportion something to someone as a share or task.|例句：The teacher allotted each student a specific task. 老师给每个学生分配了特定的任务。

例句：They allotted three days for the trip. 他们为这次旅行分配了三天时间。|近义词：allocate, assign, distribute, apportion|反义词:withhold, deny|词性:verb
