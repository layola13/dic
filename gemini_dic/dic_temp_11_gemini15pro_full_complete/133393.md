nonfact:/ˈnɑːnfækt/
n.
非事实；虚构的事物
Something that is not factual or true; a fabrication.
The story he told was a complete nonfact. 他讲的故事完全是虚构的。
近义词:fiction, falsehood, fabrication, lie, untruth
反义词:fact, truth, reality
词性:名词
