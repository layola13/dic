doac:/ˈdoʊæk/ | n. | 直接口服抗凝药 (Direct Oral Anticoagulant) | A type of medication that inhibits blood clotting.  These are taken by mouth. |  The doctor prescribed a DOAC to prevent blood clots. 医生开了直接口服抗凝药来预防血栓。 | Synonyms:  oral anticoagulant | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) procoagulant | noun
