berried:/ˈber.id/ /ˈbe.rid/
adjective
浆果状的；覆有浆果的
Covered with berries; having berries.
The path led through a berried bush.  这条小路穿过一片结满浆果的灌木丛。
近义词:fruited, berry-laden
反义词:bare, barren
词性:adjective

berried:/ˈber.id/ /ˈbe.rid/
verb (past participle of berry)
采集浆果
To gather berries.
We've berried all afternoon and have filled two baskets. 我们采集了一下午的浆果，装满了两个篮子。
近义词:picked, harvested, gathered
反义词:planted, sowed
词性:verb
