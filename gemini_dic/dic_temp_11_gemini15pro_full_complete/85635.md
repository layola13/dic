haunching:/ˈhɔːntʃɪŋ/| v.|蹲伏，弓着背，蜷缩|The cat was haunching in the corner, ready to pounce.  猫蜷缩在角落里，准备扑出去。|近义词：crouching, squatting, hunkering|反义词：standing, straightening|词性:动词
词源: From haunch (n.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and gerunds), from Old French hanche "hip," from Frankish *hanka, from Proto-Germanic *hankō "hip, ham."
