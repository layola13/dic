debarment:/dɪˈbɑːrmənt/
n.
禁止；剥夺权利；阻止；停职
The act of debarring or the state of being debarred; exclusion, prohibition.
He faced debarment from the legal profession.  他面临着被剥夺律师资格的处境。
Synonyms: exclusion, prohibition, disqualification, preclusion, suspension
Antonyms: inclusion, admittance, reinstatement, permission
noun

debar:/dɪˈbɑːr/
vt.
禁止；阻止；剥夺…的权利
To officially prevent someone from doing something, especially from having a particular job.
The doctor was debarred from practicing medicine. 该医生被禁止行医。
Synonyms: prohibit, prevent, forbid, preclude, exclude
Antonyms: permit, allow, authorize, enable
verb
