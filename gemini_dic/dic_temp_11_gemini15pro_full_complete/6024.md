ameliorating:/əˈmiːliəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
改善，改进
making something bad or unpleasant better
The new policy is aimed at ameliorating the housing crisis. 这项新政策旨在改善住房危机。
improving, enhancing, bettering
worsening, exacerbating, aggravating
v.
