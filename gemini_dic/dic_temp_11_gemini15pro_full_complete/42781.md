coupar:/ˈkuːpər/| n.|箍桶匠；箍桶业；桶匠铺|A person who makes or repairs barrels, casks, tubs, and other similar wooden vessels.|He learned the cooper's trade from his father. 他从他父亲那里学到了箍桶的手艺。|近义词：barrel maker, cask maker|反义词:|n.

Etymology:
From Middle English *coupere*, from Old French *cupere*, from Late Latin *cuparius*, from Latin *cupa* ("tub, cask, vat").
