julienne:/ˌdʒuːliˈɛn/ | adj. n. v. |细条状的，切成细条；细条状食品；切成细条 |Cut into long thin strips. |例句：The carrots were julienned and added to the salad. 胡萝卜被切成细条，加到了沙拉里。

例句：She ordered the julienne potatoes. 她点了细条状的土豆。|近义词：shred, slice, dice |反义词: chop, mince |词性:adjective, noun, verb

词源: From French, named after Jules Gouffé (1807–1877), a French chef.
