如果你是想了解疑问词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what /wɒt; hwɒt/ (strong form) /wət/ (weak form)
|pron. det. adv.| 什么 | used to ask for information about something |

Example:
What is your name?
你叫什么名字？

What time is it?
现在几点了？

Synonym: which, how

Antonym: (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word.)


词性:
pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->


一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living?
你做什么工作？

What does she like to eat?
她喜欢吃什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing right now?
你现在在做什么？

What is he working on?
他正在做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done today?
你今天做了什么？

What has she accomplished this year?
她今年完成了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all afternoon?
你整个下午都在做什么？

What has he been working on lately?
他最近一直在忙什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday?
你昨天做了什么？

What happened last night?
昨晚发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang?
电话响的时候你在做什么？

What was she thinking about?
她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left?
你离开之前做了什么？

What had they discussed prior to the meeting?

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow?
你明天要做什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
