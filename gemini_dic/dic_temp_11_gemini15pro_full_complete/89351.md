homolographic:/ˌhɒməˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌhoʊməˈɡræfɪk/
|adj.|同形异义的|Having the same spelling as another word but a different meaning and sometimes a different pronunciation.|The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are homolographic.|Synonyms: homographic, homonymous|Antonyms: heterographic, heteronymous|Adjective


homograph:/ˈhɒməɡrɑːf/ /ˈhoʊməɡræf/
|n.|同形异义词|A word that is spelled the same as another word but has a different meaning and sometimes a different pronunciation.|The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are homographs.|Synonyms: homonym, homophone (sometimes)|Antonyms: heteronym, heterograph|Noun
