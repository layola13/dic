alumina:/əˈluːmɪnə/
n.
氧化铝
A white crystalline oxide of aluminum.
Aluminum oxide, a chemical compound with the formula Al₂O₃.  It is amphoteric, meaning it can react as both an acid and a base.
例句：Alumina is a major component of bauxite. 氧化铝是铝土矿的主要成分。

例句：The ceramic is made of high-purity alumina.  这种陶瓷由高纯度氧化铝制成。

近义词：aluminum oxide
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)
词性: noun
