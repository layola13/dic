merciless:/ˈmɜːrsɪləs/ | adj. |无情的，残忍的 | Showing no mercy or pity; cruel. |  The merciless dictator showed no compassion for his people.  无情的独裁者对他的子民毫无怜悯之心。

The merciless sun beat down on the desert travelers.  无情的太阳炙烤着沙漠中的旅行者。

| 近义词: cruel, ruthless, pitiless, inhumane, barbarous, savage | 反义词: merciful, compassionate, kind, humane | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mercilees*, from Old French *merciles*, from *merci* ("mercy") + *-les* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "without"). Equivalent to *mercy* + *-less*.
