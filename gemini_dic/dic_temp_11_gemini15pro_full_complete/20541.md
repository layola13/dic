blabbermouth:/ˈblæbərmæʊθ/ (US) /ˈblæbəmaʊθ/ (UK)| n.|饶舌者，长舌妇，大嘴巴|A person who talks too much and reveals secrets or private information.|例句：He's such a blabbermouth; he told everyone our secret. 他真是个大嘴巴，把我们的秘密告诉了所有人。

例句：Don't tell her anything confidential; she's a blabbermouth.  别告诉她任何机密，她是个长舌妇。

|近义词：gossip, chatterbox, windbag|反义词: reticent person, clam|词性:noun
