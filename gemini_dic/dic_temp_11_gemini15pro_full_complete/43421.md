crapaud:/kræˈpoʊd/ | n. |蟾蜍; (尤指) 大蟾蜍 |A large toad, especially a marine toad. |例句：The crapaud hopped across the damp forest floor. 大蟾蜍跳过潮湿的森林地面。|例句：Local legends spoke of a giant crapaud guarding a hidden treasure. 当地传说讲述一只巨大的蟾蜍守护着隐藏的宝藏。|近义词：toad, bullfrog |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
词源: From French *crapaud*, from Old French *crape*, *crespe* (“toad”), of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Provençal *escarpat*, *escarpan* (“lizard”), from Late Latin *scarpa* (“cliff”).
