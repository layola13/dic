compoundable:/kəmˈpaʊndəbəl/ | adj. |可组合的，可混合的，可调和的，(罪行)可按罚金处理的 |Able to be combined or settled; (of an offense) punishable by a fine. |例句：The ingredients are easily compoundable into a smooth paste. 这些成分很容易混合成光滑的糊状物。

例句：The offense is compoundable by paying a small fine.  该罪行可以通过支付少量罚款来和解。|近义词：combinable, mixable, reconcilable, settleable |反义词：uncompoundable, irreducible |词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *compounabill, from Anglo-Norman compounable, from compoun- (stem of compondre ‘to compound’), from Latin componere ‘to put together’ + -able.
