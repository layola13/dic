abseil: /ˈæbseɪl/ (UK), /æpˈseɪl/ (US)| v. n.|下降；绳降|To descend a rock face or other near-vertical surface by using a doubled rope coiled round the body and fixed at a higher point.|The climbers abseiled down the cliff face. 攀岩者们沿悬崖面绳降而下。|近义词：rappel, descend, lower|反义词：ascend, climb|词性：动词，名词

abseil: /ˈæbseɪl/ (UK), /æpˈseɪl/ (US)| n.|绳降|The act of abseiling.|The abseil was the most challenging part of the climb.  绳降是攀登过程中最具挑战性的部分。|近义词：rappel, descent|反义词：ascent, climb|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *abseilen*, from *ab* ("down") + *seil* ("rope").
