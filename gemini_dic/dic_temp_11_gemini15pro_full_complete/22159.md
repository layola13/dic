bombarding:/bɒmˈbɑːdɪŋ/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdɪŋ/

|v.|攻击，痛斥|He was bombarded with questions from the reporters. 他被记者们连珠炮似的提问轰炸。|
|近义词： shelling, attacking, battering, assaulting, peppering|
|反义词： protecting, defending|

bombardment:/bɒmˈbɑːdmənt/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdmənt/
|n.|轰炸，炮击；大量提问；连续发送信息|The city suffered a heavy bombardment. 这座城市遭受了猛烈的轰炸。|
|n.|攻击，痛斥|He faced a constant bombardment of criticism. 他面临着持续不断的批评。|
|近义词： shelling, attack, barrage, assault|
|反义词： defense, protection|
|词性：名词|



Etymology: From the French word "bombarder," from Italian "bombardare," from "bombarda" (a type of cannon), ultimately from Late Latin "bombus" (a humming sound), imitative of the sound of an explosion.
