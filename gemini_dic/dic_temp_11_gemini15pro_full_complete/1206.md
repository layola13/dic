accommodate: /əˈkɑːmədeɪt/ (US) /əˈkɒmədeɪt/ (UK)

|phrasal verb|提供；供应；|to provide someone with something, typically something helpful or needed|The bank accommodated us with a loan. 银行为我们提供了贷款。|synonyms: provide, supply, furnish|antonyms: deprive, withhold|phrasal verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accommodatus, past participle of accommodare "to make fit, adapt," from Latin ad- "to" + commodare "to make fit, lend," from commodus "suitable, convenient."


注意事项:

"accommodate with" 虽然可以表达“提供”的意思，但不如 "provide with," "supply with," 或 "furnish with" 常用。 在现代英语中，"accommodate" 更常用于表示“容纳”或“适应”。
