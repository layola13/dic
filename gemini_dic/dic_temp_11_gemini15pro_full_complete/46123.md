cymatium:/saɪˈmeɪʃiəm/| n. |波形飾, 顶饰, 弯檐|A molding which crowns a cornice or other architectural member; also, any wave-like, crowning member or molding.|The cymatium of the temple was intricately carved.  寺庙的顶饰雕刻精美。|The cymatium protected the wall from rain damage.  弯檐保护墙壁免受雨水侵蚀。|Synonyms: cornice, molding, crest, fillet|Antonyms: base, foundation|noun
