misgauge:/ˌmɪsˈɡeɪdʒ/
verb
错误估计；判断失误
to estimate or judge something wrongly
He misgauged the strength of the wind and capsized the boat. 他错误估计了风力，导致船翻了。
The company misgauged the public's interest in the new product. 这家公司错误估计了公众对新产品的兴趣。
miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate
judge correctly, estimate accurately
v.
