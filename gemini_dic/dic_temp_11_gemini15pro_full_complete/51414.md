deterrence:/dɪˈtɜːrəns/| n.|威慑，制止，阻止|The country's nuclear arsenal serves as a deterrence against foreign aggression. 这个国家的核武库是对外来侵略的威慑。|例句：The presence of police officers acted as a deterrence to crime. 警察的出现对犯罪起到了威慑作用。|近义词：prevention, dissuasion, intimidation|反义词：encouragement, inducement|noun

deter:/dɪˈtɜːr/| v.|威慑，吓住，阻止|High prices deter many young people from buying houses. 高昂的价格让很多年轻人不敢买房。|例句：The heavy fines should deter motorists from speeding. 严厉的罚款应该能阻止驾车者超速行驶。|近义词：discourage, prevent, hinder|反义词：encourage, promote|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēterrentia, from Latin dēterreō ("I deter, frighten off"), from dē ("away, from") + terreō ("I frighten").
