biophor:/ˈbaɪəfɔːr/| n.|生物体，生物活体，活体;|A hypothetical smallest unit of living matter. A microscopic aggregate of molecules capable of growth and reproduction.|Biophors, according to Weismann, are the bearers of the individual hereditary qualities.  根据魏斯曼的说法，生物体是个人遗传素质的载体。|The theory of biophors was popular in the late 19th century. 生物体理论在19世纪后期很流行。|近义词：organism, living being|反义词:inanimate object|noun
