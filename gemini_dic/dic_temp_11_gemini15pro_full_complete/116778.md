mava:/ˈmɑːvə/ /ˈmeɪvə/ | n. | 移动平均; 移动平均线 |  (Short for "moving average," often used in finance and statistics to smooth out price fluctuations and identify trends.)  It helps to identify the direction of a trend. | The mava for the last three months shows a steady increase in sales.  过去三个月的移动平均线显示销售额稳步增长。| synonym: moving average | antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "raw data" or "individual data point" could be considered as contrasting concepts) | noun
