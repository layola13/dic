misreformed:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
改革不当的，改革失败的
incorrectly or unsuccessfully reformed
The misreformed economic policies led to widespread hardship. 改革不当的经济政策导致了普遍的困境。
The misreformed legal system failed to protect the rights of citizens.  改革失败的法律体系未能保护公民的权利。
Near Synonyms: botched, bungled, flawed, faulty
Antonyms: successful, effective, well-reformed
Word Origin: "mis-" (prefix meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + "reformed" (past participle of "reform")
