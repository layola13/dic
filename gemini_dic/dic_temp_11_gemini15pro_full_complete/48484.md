deepavali:/ˌdiːpəˈvɑːli/ | n. | 排灯节，印度教节日 | Deepavali, also known as Diwali, is one of the most important festivals in Hinduism, Jainism, and Sikhism, celebrated with lights and fireworks.  排灯节，也称为光明节，是印度教、耆那教和锡克教最重要的节日之一，人们用灯和烟花庆祝。 |  We celebrate Deepavali every year. 我们每年都庆祝排灯节。 |  Deepavali is a festival of lights and joy. 排灯节是一个充满光明和欢乐的节日。 | synonyms: Diwali, Festival of Lights | antonyms: N/A | noun
