miniaturizes:/ˈmɪnɪətʃəraɪzɪz/
verb
使小型化；微型化
makes something much smaller than it usually is.
The company miniaturizes electronic components for use in mobile phones.  这家公司将电子元件小型化，用于手机。
miniaturize, shrink, reduce, compress
enlarge, expand, magnify
v.
