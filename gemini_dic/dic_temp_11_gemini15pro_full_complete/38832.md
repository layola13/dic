confab:/ˈkɒnfæb/ (UK) /kənˈfæb/ (US)| n. |闲谈，非正式会议|an informal private conversation or discussion|例句：We had a confab about the upcoming project. 我们就即将开展的项目进行了非正式会谈。

例句：They held a confab to discuss their travel plans. 他们开了一个非正式会议讨论旅行计划。


近义词：chat, discussion, meeting, talk|反义词:silence, quiet|词性:noun
