encephalotomy:/ɛnˌsefəˈlɒtəmi/ | n. | 脑切开术 |  Surgical incision into the brain.  The operation of cutting into the brain, usually to sever brain tissue. |  Encephalotomy was once a common treatment for severe mental illness. 脑切开术曾经是治疗严重精神疾病的常见方法。| lobotomy, craniotomy |  N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + kephal- + -tomy.  En-  means "in" or "within". Kephal- comes from the Greek kephalē, meaning “head.” -tomy is a suffix denoting "incision" or "cutting into".
