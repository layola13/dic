misworshipping:/ˌmɪsˈwɜːrʃɪpɪŋ/
verb
错误地崇拜；崇拜偶像
The act of worshipping falsely or giving reverence to something that is not worthy of worship.
He was accused of misworshipping pagan gods. 他被指控错误地崇拜异教神。
Near Antonyms: worshipping correctly, revering appropriately
Antonyms:  None (The direct opposite would be "not worshipping" which doesn't convey the nuance of incorrect worship)
词性: verb
