noncommunicativeness:/nɒnkəmˈjuːnɪkətɪvnəs/
n.
不善交际；沉默寡言
The state or quality of being uncommunicative; unwillingness or inability to communicate effectively.
His noncommunicativeness made it difficult to understand his feelings. 他的不善言辞使人难以理解他的感受。
Synonyms: reticence, reserve, taciturnity, uncommunicativeness
Antonyms: communicativeness, loquacity, talkativeness
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + communicative + -ness (state of being).  The word "communicative" comes from Late Latin communicativus, from communicat-, past participle stem of communicare "to share, impart," from communis "common."
