dnn:/diː ɛn ɛn/ |abbr.| 深度神经网络 (Deep Neural Network)| A deep neural network (DNN) is an artificial neural network (ANN) with multiple layers between the input and output layers. | Deep neural networks are typically used for complex tasks such as image recognition and natural language processing. 深度神经网络通常用于图像识别和自然语言处理等复杂任务。 | 近义词: ANN (Artificial Neural Network) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "shallow neural network" in a comparative context) |abbr.
