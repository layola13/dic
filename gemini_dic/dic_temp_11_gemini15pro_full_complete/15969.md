balter:/ˈbɔːltər/| vi.|欢快地跳舞，雀跃|to dance artlessly without particular grace or skill but usually with enjoyment; to skip or romp about|例句：The children baltered around the maypole. 孩子们绕着五月柱欢快地跳舞。|例句：He baltered across the field, delighted with the sunshine. 他兴高采烈地在田野里雀跃，享受着阳光。|近义词：cavort, frolic, gambol, romp, skip|反义词：mope, sulk|词性:动词
