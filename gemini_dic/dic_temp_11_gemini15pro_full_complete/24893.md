bruise:/bruːz/| n. vt. |瘀伤，擦伤；挫伤；（水果等）碰伤；（精神上的）打击；|An injury appearing as an area of discolored skin on the body, caused by a blow or impact rupturing underlying blood vessels.|She fell down the stairs and got a bruise on her leg. 她从楼梯上摔下来，腿上碰伤了。|近义词：contusion, injury, lesion|反义词: heal|n.

bruise:/bruːz/| vt. |使出现伤痕；擦伤；挫伤；（精神上）打击；|To inflict a bruise or bruises on.|He bruised his knee playing football. 他踢足球时擦伤了膝盖。|近义词：injure, hurt, damage|反义词: heal|vt.
