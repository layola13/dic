imbue:/ɪmˈbjuː/ /ɪmˈbju/| vt.|灌输，使充满，浸透|To inspire or permeate with a feeling or quality.|例句：The artist imbued the painting with a sense of melancholy. 艺术家为这幅画注入了忧郁的情感。

例句：Her poetry is imbued with a deep love of nature. 她的诗歌充满了对大自然的热爱。

近义词：infuse, instill, permeate, saturate, impregnate|反义词：deprive, divest, drain|词性：动词
