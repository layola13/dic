bathymetrical:/ˌbæθɪˈmetrɪkl/
adjective
水深的；测深的
relating to or involving the measurement of the depths of the oceans or other large bodies of water
例句：Bathymetrical surveys are essential for safe navigation.  测深测量对于安全航行至关重要。

例句：The bathymetrical data revealed a deep trench in the ocean floor.  水深数据揭示了海底深处的一条海沟。
近义词：depth-related, bathygraphic
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "topographic" for land elevation)
词性:adjective

bathymetry:/bəˈθɪmətri/
noun
测深学；水深测量
the measurement of the depths of the oceans or other large bodies of water
例句：Bathymetry helps us understand the topography of the ocean floor. 测深学帮助我们了解海底地形。

例句：Modern bathymetry uses sonar technology for accurate measurements. 现代测深学使用声纳技术进行精确测量。
近义词：depth sounding, hydrography
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性:noun
