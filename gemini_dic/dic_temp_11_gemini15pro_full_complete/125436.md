multiplanetary:/ˌmʌltiˈplænɪtəri/ | adj. |多行星的；涉及多个行星的|  Relating to or involving more than one planet.  |  Example 1:  Scientists are exploring the possibility of multiplanetary life.  科学家们正在探索多行星生命存在的可能性。

Example 2:  A multiplanetary species is one that can survive and thrive on multiple planets.  多行星物种是指能够在多个行星上生存和繁衍的物种。 | 近义词: interplanetary | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "single-planet" or "Earth-bound") | 词性: adjective
