fecundate:/ˈfekəndeɪt/ | vt. | 使受精；使肥沃；使多产 | To make fertile or productive; to impregnate. | The farmer fecundated his fields with manure.  农民用肥料使田地肥沃。

The pollen fecundated the ovules. 花粉使胚珠受精。| 近义词: fertilize, impregnate, enrich | 反义词: sterilize, barren | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Latin *fecundātus*, past participle of *fecundāre* (“to make fruitful”), from *fecundus* (“fruitful”).
