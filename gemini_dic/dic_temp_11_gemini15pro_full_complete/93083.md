idrialite:/ɪˈdriəˌlaɪt/| n. |伊德里亚石|A rare hydrocarbon mineral found in mercury deposits, typically occurring as small, yellowish crystals. It is named after Idrija, Slovenia, where it was first discovered.|Idrialite is often associated with cinnabar. 伊德里亚石通常与辰砂伴生。|The geologists discovered a new vein of idrialite in the mine. 地质学家在矿井中发现了一条新的伊德里亚石矿脉。|Synonyms: None (highly specific mineral)|Antonyms: None (highly specific mineral)|noun
