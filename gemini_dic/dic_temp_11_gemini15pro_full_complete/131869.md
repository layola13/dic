nonconsumptively:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsʌmptɪvli/ | adv. | 非消耗性地 |  In a way that does not involve consuming or using up a resource. | Researchers observed the gorillas nonconsumptively, taking care not to disturb their natural habitat. 研究人员非消耗性地观察大猩猩，小心地不打扰它们的自然栖息地。 |  non-destructively, sustainably | consumptively | adverb

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + consumptive (adjective meaning "tending to consume") + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "consume" comes from the Latin "consumere," meaning "to use up completely."
