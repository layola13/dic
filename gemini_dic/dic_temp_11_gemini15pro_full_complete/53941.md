discover:/dɪˈskʌvər/ | v. | 发现，发觉 | To find unexpectedly or during a search; to obtain sight or knowledge of for the first time. | Columbus discovered America. 哥伦布发现了美洲。

Scientists discovered a new species of frog. 科学家们发现了一种新的青蛙物种。

近义词: find, unearth, detect, locate, uncover | 反义词: hide, conceal, cover, lose | 词性: verb
