loveable:/ˈlʌvəbl/ (US), /ˈlʌvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可爱的，讨人喜欢的|Inspiring love or affection. Easy to love.|She has such a loveable personality. 她有着如此可爱的性格。|近义词：adorable, charming, sweet, endearing|反义词：unlovable, detestable, hateful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From love + -able.  The suffix "-able" means "capable of being," so "loveable" means "capable of being loved."
