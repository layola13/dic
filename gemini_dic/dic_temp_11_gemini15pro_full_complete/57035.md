doublethreaded:/ˈdʌbəlˈθrɛdɪd/
adjective
双线的；双股的
Having two threads or strands twisted together.
The fabric was strong, made of doublethreaded yarn.  这种织物很结实，是用双股纱线制成的。
The rope was doublethreaded for extra strength.  这绳子是双股的，为了增加强度。
synonym:double-stranded, two-ply
antonym:single-threaded, single-strand
词性:adjective
