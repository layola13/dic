disfavor:/dɪsˈfeɪvər/
n.|不喜爱，不赞成，失宠|lack of favor; disapproval|His latest novel has fallen into disfavor with the critics. 他最新的小说不受评论家欢迎。|近义词：disapproval, dislike, disesteem, unpopularity|反义词：favor, approval, popularity|词性:名词
Etymology: From dis- "not, opposite of" + favor. Ultimately from Latin favēre "to be favorable."
