acroterial:/ˌækrəˈtɪəriəl/
adjective
acroterial的，acroteria的
Relating to or resembling an acroterion.  An architectural ornament placed at the apex or corner of a pediment.
The temple's pediment displayed elaborate acroterial sculptures of griffins.  这座寺庙的山形墙上展示了精致的狮鹫形雕塑。
近义词: architectural, ornamental
反义词: plain, unadorned
词性: adjective

acroterium:/ˌækrəˈtɪəriəm/
noun
acroterium，（山形墙顶端或转角处的）雕饰
An architectural ornament placed at the apex or corner of a pediment.
The acroteria on the Parthenon were damaged by weathering.  帕特农神庙上的雕饰被风化破坏了。
近义词: finial, ornament
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider: base, foundation)
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *acroterium, from Greek akrōtērion "extremity," from akros "highest, outermost."
