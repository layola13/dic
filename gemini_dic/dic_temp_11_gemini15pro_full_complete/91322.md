hydrophane:/ˈhaɪdrəˌfeɪn/ | n. | 水蛋白石 | A translucent opal that becomes transparent or translucent when immersed in water. | The hydrophane absorbed the water and became clear.  水蛋白石吸收了水分，变得透明了。 | synonym: opal, hyalite | antonym: opaque stone | noun

hydrophane:/ˈhaɪdrəˌfeɪn/ | adj. | 吸水的 |  Having the property of becoming transparent or translucent when immersed in water. | This type of opal is hydrophane. 这种蛋白石是吸水的。 | synonym: absorbent, water-absorbing | antonym: waterproof, water-repellent | adjective
