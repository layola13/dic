censoriousnesses:/ˌsensɔːriəsnəsəz/
n.
挑剔, 吹毛求疵
The state of being excessively critical or fault-finding.
Her censoriousnesses about the food made everyone uncomfortable. 她对食物的挑剔让每个人都不舒服.
近义词: criticism, fault-finding, carping, nitpicking
反义词: tolerance, acceptance, leniency, forbearance
词性: 名词


censoriousness:/ˌsensɔːriəsnəs/
n.
挑剔, 吹毛求疵
The state of being excessively critical or fault-finding.
His censoriousness made it difficult to work with him. 他吹毛求疵的性格让人很难与他共事。
近义词: criticism, fault-finding, carping, nitpicking
反义词: tolerance, acceptance, leniency, forbearance
词性: 名词


词源:
From Late Latin *censoriosus, from Latin censor "critic, judge of morals" (see censor (n.)) + -ious. The noun is from 16c.
