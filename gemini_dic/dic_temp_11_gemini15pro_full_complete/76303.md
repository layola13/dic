gabbles:/ˈɡæblz/ | v. | 喋喋不休，急促而不清楚地说 | Speaks rapidly and indistinctly, often about trivial matters. | The auctioneer gabbled so fast I couldn't understand a word. 拍卖师说得太快了，我一个字也听不懂。 |  He gabbled excitedly about his new toy. 他兴奋地絮叨着他的新玩具。 | 近义词：babble, chatter, prattle | 反义词：articulate, enunciate | 词性: 动词

gabble: /ˈɡæbl/ | n. |  快而不清楚的话，喋喋不休 | Rapid, indistinct speech. |  I couldn't make sense of the gabble coming from the next room. 我听不懂从隔壁房间传来的叽里呱啦的声音。 | The excited gabble of the children filled the air. 孩子们兴奋的喋喋不休充满了空气。 | 近义词：babble, chatter, prattle | 反义词：articulation, enunciation | 词性: 名词
