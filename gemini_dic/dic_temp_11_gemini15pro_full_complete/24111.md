brewed:/brʊd/| v. adj.|酿造的，冲泡的|Past participle of "brew."  Meaning: (v) to make (beer, ale, etc.) by steeping, boiling, and fermentation. (adj) made by brewing.  Origin: Old English *brēowan "to brew."|例句：I brewed a pot of coffee this morning. 我今天早上煮了一壶咖啡。

The tea is freshly brewed.  这茶是新泡的。|近义词：fermented, steeped, infused (for the verb); prepared, made (for the adjective)|反义词:(For verb) N/A (no direct opposite for the act of brewing). (For adjective) instant, pre-made|词性：verb (brewed is the past tense and past participle form), adjective
