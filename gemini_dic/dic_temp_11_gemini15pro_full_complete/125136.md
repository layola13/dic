multi-platinum:/ˌmʌltiˈplætɪnəm/
adjective
多白金的
Certified as having sold a specified number of copies (usually one million or more, depending on the territory) of an album or single, typically one million copies in the US and 800,000 copies in the UK.
The band's multi-platinum album cemented their place in music history.  这个乐队的多白金专辑巩固了他们在音乐史上的地位。
Her latest album went multi-platinum in just a few weeks. 她的最新专辑在短短几周内就获得了多白金销量。
Synonyms:  best-selling, chart-topping, hit
Antonyms:  unpopular, unsuccessful, flop
