overplayed:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪd/| adj.|过度演奏的，表演过火的，滥用的，陈腐的|Played or performed too much; overemphasized or overused, resulting in a loss of effectiveness or appeal.|例句：That song is so overplayed, I'm tired of hearing it. 那首歌被播放得太多了，我都听腻了。|近义词：overused, hackneyed, stale, trite|反义词:fresh, original, novel, underplayed|词性:形容词

overplayed:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪd/| adj.|过度表演的|Acted in an exaggerated or unconvincing manner.|例句：His performance was overplayed and melodramatic. 他的表演太夸张，太戏剧化了。|近义词：exaggerated, hammy, theatrical|反义词:understated, subtle, nuanced|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "too much") + "played" (past participle of "play").  来自 "over-"（前缀，意为“过度”）+ "played"（动词 "play" 的过去分词）。
