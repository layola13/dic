mitu:/ˈmiːtuː/ |pron.|什么，什么东西 (古英语)| Something; what.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. | Example: "Mitu segst þu?" (Old English) - "What sayest thou?" (Modern English) - 你说什么？ | 近义词: what | 反义词: N/A | 词性: pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you see? = 你看到了什么？
What does he want? = 他想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they watching? = 他们在看什么？
What is she cooking? = 她在煮什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has he done? = 他做了什么?

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has she been working on? = 她一直在忙什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What did she buy? = 她买了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing yesterday? = 你昨天在做什么？
What was he thinking about? = 他在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed? = 他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? =  被打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before changing her major? =  转专业之前她一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will they eat for dinner? = 他们晚餐要吃什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will he be studying next semester? = 他下学期会学什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next month? = 到下个月你将会完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们将会取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past five years? = 过去五年你一直在做什么？ (用于将来的时间点回顾过去)
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么(多久)了？

虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would say it. = 如果我知道该说什么，我就会说。
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我希望我知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？


疑问句:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What is going on here? = 这里发生了什么事？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He doesn't understand what I mean. = 他不明白我的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ...怎么样?
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？

注意事项:  "mitu" 虽然词源上与"what"相关，但它在现代英语中已经不再使用。  上面提供的例句和用法说明是基于"what"的，因为"mitu"本身缺乏现代语境。
