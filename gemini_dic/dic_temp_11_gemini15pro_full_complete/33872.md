circumspect:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌspekt/ | adj. | 谨慎的，慎重的，考虑周全的 | Careful to consider all circumstances and possible consequences; cautious. |  He was circumspect in his dealings with the media, careful not to reveal too much information. 他在与媒体打交道时非常谨慎，注意不要透露太多信息。

She gave a circumspect answer, aware of the potential legal ramifications. 她给出了一个谨慎的回答，意识到潜在的法律后果。 | 近义词: cautious, prudent, wary, discreet, judicious | 反义词: reckless, careless, impulsive, rash, imprudent | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin circumspectus, past participle of circumspicere (“to look around, consider”), from circum (“around”) + specere (“to look”).
