antisecrecy:/ˌæntiˈsiːkrəsi/
n.|反保密，反对保密|Opposition to secrecy, especially in government or business.|The antisecrecy movement gained momentum after the Snowden revelations. 斯诺登爆料后，反保密运动势头增强。|近义词：transparency, openness, disclosure|反义词：secrecy, confidentiality, concealment|词性:名词


Etymology:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
secrecy (noun): the state of being secret or kept hidden (from Late Latin secretum "a secret")
