chirurgical:/kərˈɜːrdʒɪkəl/| adj.|外科的；手术的|Relating to surgery; surgical.  Derived from the Late Latin word *chirurgicus*, meaning "surgical," which itself comes from the Greek word *kheirourgikos*, from *kheirourgia* "surgery," from *kheir* "hand" + *ergon* "work."|例句：The chirurgical procedure was successful. 外科手术很成功。

例句：He had a chirurgical scar on his knee. 他的膝盖上有一个手术疤痕。|近义词：surgical, operative|反义词：medical (in a broader sense, not directly related to surgery), therapeutic (when referring to non-surgical treatments)|词性：adjective
