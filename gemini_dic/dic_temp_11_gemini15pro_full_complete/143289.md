outdrags:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡz/
verb
胜过，超过（尤指在耐力方面）
outlasts or surpasses, especially in endurance
The Kenyan runner outdragged his competitors in the final lap.  肯尼亚选手在最后一圈超过了他的对手。
近义词: outlasts, surpasses, beats, defeats
反义词: loses to, is surpassed by
词性: 动词
