hydrofluorocarbons:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌflʊərəˈkɑːrbənz/| n.|氢氟碳化物|A group of man-made organic compounds that contain hydrogen, fluorine, and carbon atoms. They are used as refrigerants, propellants, and blowing agents.|Hydrofluorocarbons are potent greenhouse gases and contribute to climate change. 氢氟碳化物是强效的温室气体，导致气候变化。|synonyms: HFCs|antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but alternatives include natural refrigerants like propane and isobutane)|noun
