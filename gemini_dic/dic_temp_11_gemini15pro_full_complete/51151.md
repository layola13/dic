ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *desponsationem, accusative singular of desponsatio (“a betrothal”), from desponsare (“to betroth”), from de- (“un-”) + sponsare (“to promise solemnly, betroth”), from sponsus (“bridegroom; surety, sponsor”), from spondere (“to pledge oneself, promise solemnly”).
