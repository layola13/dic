iconodule:/ˈaɪkənəˌduːl/ /aɪˈkɑːnəˌduːl/
noun
破坏偶像者，圣像破坏者
a person who attacks or criticizes cherished beliefs or institutions.  Someone who destroys religious images or opposes their veneration.
The professor, known as an iconodule in the department, challenged the traditional theories.  这位教授在系里被称为圣像破坏者，他挑战了传统理论。
The iconodule smashed the statue of the saint.  这个圣像破坏者砸碎了圣人的雕像。
iconoclast, critic, dissenter
traditionalist, conformist
n.
