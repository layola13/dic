nonfelicities:/ˌnɑːnfəˈlɪsətiz/
noun
不幸;不恰当的表达;错误
plural of nonfelicity:  instances of being unsuitable or inappropriate, especially infelicitous expressions.
His speech was marred by several nonfelicities of phrasing.  他的演讲因措辞不当而大打折扣。
Synonyms:  infelicities, improprieties, gaucheries, solecisms
Antonyms:  felicities, proprieties, aptness
词性: 名词(plural)
