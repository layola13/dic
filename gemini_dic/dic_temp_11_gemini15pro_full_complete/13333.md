-ative:/ətɪv/ | adj. | 倾向于...的; 表示有...倾向的 |  Forming adjectives meaning "tending to," "having the nature of," or "relating to."  It derives from the Latin suffix "-ativus," which has the same meaning. |  A talkative person tends to talk a lot. 健谈的人倾向于说很多话。|  The drug has a curative effect.  这种药有疗效。|  curative,  talkative,  authoritative | passive, inert | suffix
