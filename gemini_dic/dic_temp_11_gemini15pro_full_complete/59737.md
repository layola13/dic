eco-label:/ˈiːkəʊˌleɪbəl/ /ˈɛkoʊˌleɪbəl/ | n. |生态标签，环保标签 |A label which indicates that a product or service meets certain environmental standards, often awarded by a third-party organization.| This product carries an eco-label, showing its commitment to sustainability.  这个产品带有生态标签，表明其对可持续发展的承诺。|Synonyms: green label, environmental label |Antonyms: N/A| noun

eco-label:/ˈiːkəʊˌleɪbəl/ /ˈɛkoʊˌleɪbəl/ | v. |标以生态标签 |To mark a product or service with an eco-label.| The company decided to eco-label its entire range of cleaning products.  该公司决定将其所有清洁产品系列都贴上生态标签。|Synonyms: certify, endorse (in an environmental context) |Antonyms: N/A| verb
