germanicism:/ˈdʒɜːrmənɪsɪzəm/
n.|日耳曼主义；德语借词|A word, phrase, or idiom borrowed from the German language or constructed on German linguistic patterns.  It can also refer to a German custom or trait. |The word "rucksack" is a germanicism that has become common in English.  这个词“rucksack”（背包）是一个在英语中变得常见的德语借词。|近义词：loanword, borrowing|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
