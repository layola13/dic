in any event:/ɪn ˈɛni ɪˈvɛnt/| adv.|无论如何，不管怎样|Means "regardless" or "no matter what happens."  It's used to introduce a conclusion or course of action that doesn't depend on the outcome of a previous event or situation.|In any event, I'll be there. 无论如何，我都会在那里。|Synonyms: anyway, anyhow, regardless, in any case|Antonyms: specifically, particularly|adverb
