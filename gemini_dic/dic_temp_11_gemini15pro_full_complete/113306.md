luting:/ˈluːtɪŋ/| n.|粘固，粘接|The luting of the crown was successful.  牙冠的粘固很成功。|例句：The dentist used a special cement for luting the bridge. 牙医用一种特殊的粘固剂来粘接牙桥。

The luting agent ensured a strong bond between the restoration and the tooth. 粘固剂确保了修复体和牙齿之间的牢固结合。|近义词：cementing, bonding, fixing|反义词: removal, detachment|词性:noun

词源: From the Late Latin "luta" (mud, clay),  then to Old French "luter" (to close with clay),  eventually leading to the English "lute" and "luting."
