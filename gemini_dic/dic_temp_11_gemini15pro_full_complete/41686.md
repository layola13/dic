correspondingly:/ˌkɔːrɪˈspɑːndɪŋli/ /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndɪŋli/
adverb|相应地，对应地|In a way that matches or relates to something else. |As the cost of living rises, wages will correspondingly increase.  随着生活成本的上升，工资也会相应增加。|
correspondingly: Prices have risen, and correspondingly, demand has fallen. 价格上涨了，相应地需求下降了。
近义词: accordingly, consequently, therefore, thus, similarly
反义词: inversely, oppositely
词性: adverb

词源: From correspond + -ing + -ly.  Correspond comes from the French correspondre, which in turn comes from the Latin correspondere, meaning "to answer to," from com- "together" + respondere "to answer."
