organizable:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzəbl/
adjective
可组织的，可安排的
Capable of being organized or arranged in a systematic way.
The meeting notes were detailed and easily organizable into an action plan. 会议记录很详细，很容易整理成行动计划。
The data is highly organizable and can be easily sorted into different categories. 这些数据非常易于组织，可以轻松地分类到不同的类别中。
近义词: manageable, orderable, structurable, systematizable
反义词: disorganized, chaotic, messy
