ballahoo:/ˌbæləˈhuː/| n.|喧闹，叫卖；大肆宣传；小帆船|A loud outcry; clamor; a small sailing vessel.|The street vendor made a great ballahoo to attract customers. 街头小贩大声叫卖以吸引顾客。|近义词：clamor, uproar, hubbub, din, hullabaloo, sloop|反义词:silence, quiet|noun

ballahoo:/ˌbæləˈhuː/| v.|大声叫卖；大肆宣传|To shout or cry out; to advertise or publicize something loudly and insistently.|The carnival barker ballahooed about the amazing feats of the performers.  嘉年华的吆喝者大声宣传表演者的惊人技艺。|近义词：tout, promote, publicize, clamor, yell|反义词:whisper, conceal|verb
