barway:/ˈbɑːrweɪ/| n.|栅栏路，围栏路，用栅栏围起来的路|A path or road enclosed by a fence or barrier, often providing access to fields or other rural areas.|例句：The barway led to a secluded meadow. 这条栅栏路通向一片僻静的草地。

例句：We parked the car by the barway and walked across the field. 我们把车停在栅栏路旁，然后穿过田野。|近义词：track, path, lane, trail|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源：Likely a compound of "bar" (meaning a barrier or obstruction) and "way" (meaning a path or road).  Its usage is primarily regional, common in certain dialects of English, particularly in rural areas.
