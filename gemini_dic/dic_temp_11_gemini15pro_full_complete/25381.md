bulblet:/ˈbʌlb.lət/ | n. | 小鳞茎；球芽；珠芽 | A small bulb, often produced in the axils of leaves or flowers of a plant, capable of developing into a new plant. |  The lilies produce bulblets at the base of their stems. 这些百合在茎的底部会长出小鳞茎。| synonyms: bulbil, offset, clove, propagule | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a physical object like a bulblet) | noun
