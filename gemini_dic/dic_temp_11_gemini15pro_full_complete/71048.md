first-foot:/ˈfɜːrstˌfʊt/ | n. | 开年第一客 | The first person to enter a house on New Year's Day, traditionally thought to bring good or bad luck depending on their characteristics. |  It's considered good luck if your first-foot is a dark-haired man.  开年第一客如果是黑头发的男人，就被认为是好运。

My grandmother always makes sure a dark-haired man is the first-foot in her house on New Year's Day. 我祖母总是确保新年第一天在她家里的开年第一客是一位黑发男子。 | 近义词: first-footer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
