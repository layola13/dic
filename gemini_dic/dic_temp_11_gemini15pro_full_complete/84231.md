half-seriousness:/ˌhæfˈsɪəriəsnəs/
|n.|半认真；亦庄亦谐|A state between seriousness and playfulness, often suggesting a mixture of genuine concern and lightheartedness.|He delivered the news with a tone of half-seriousness, making it hard to tell if he was joking or not. 他用半开玩笑的语气宣布了这个消息，让人很难判断他是否在开玩笑。|Near synonyms: semi-seriousness, partial seriousness, playful seriousness|Near antonyms: complete seriousness, total levity, utter frivolity|noun
