hexabasic:/ˌhɛksəˈbeɪsɪk/
adjective
六元的；六碱的
Containing six atoms of hydrogen replaceable by basic atoms or radicals.
The hexabasic acid forms a series of salts. 这种六元酸能形成一系列的盐。
近义词:  polybasic (多碱的)
反义词: monobasic (一元的)
词性: adjective
