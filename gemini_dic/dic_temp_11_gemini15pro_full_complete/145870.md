overdescribing:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/
verb
过度描述
Providing excessive detail in a description, often to the point of being tedious or unnecessary.
She was overdescribing her symptoms to the doctor, making it difficult to determine the actual problem. 她向医生过度描述了她的症状，使得很难确定真正的问题所在。
The tour guide was overdescribing every single painting, and we quickly lost interest. 导游对每一幅画都过度描述，我们很快就失去了兴趣。
近义词: overexplaining, elaborating excessively
反义词: summarizing, being concise
词性: verb (present participle)
