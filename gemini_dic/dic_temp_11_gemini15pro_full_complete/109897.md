liang:/ljɑːŋ/ | n. | 两 (a Chinese unit of weight, approximately 50 grams) | A traditional Chinese unit of weight, roughly equivalent to 50 grams. It is commonly used to measure ingredients in cooking and traditional medicine. |  Example:  The recipe calls for two liang of ginger.  配方需要两两生姜。 | Synonym: tael (although the exact weight can vary regionally) | Antonym: N/A | noun

liang:/ljɑːŋ/ | num. | 二，两 (two) |  Used in certain dialects or contexts to represent the number two.  | Example: Liang ge ren. 两个人。(Two people.) | Synonym: èr (二) | Antonym: N/A | numeral
