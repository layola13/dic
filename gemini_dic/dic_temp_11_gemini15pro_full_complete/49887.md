demureness:/dɪˈmjʊrnəs/
|n.|端庄，娴静，严肃，矜持
|The quality of being demure; reserved, modest, and shy behavior.|
|Her demureness and quiet grace made her stand out in the crowd. 她的端庄娴静使她在人群中脱颖而出。|
|近义词: modesty, shyness, reserve, reticence|
|反义词: boldness, brazenness, immodesty|
|词性: noun|


demure:/dɪˈmjʊr/
|adj.|端庄的，娴静的，严肃的，矜持的
|Reserved, modest, and shy in appearance or behavior.|
|She gave him a demure smile. 她给了他一个矜持的微笑。|
|近义词: modest, shy, reserved, reticent|
|反义词: bold, brazen, immodest|

词源:
From Middle French *demuere (“staying, abiding”), present participle of demourer (“to stay, remain”), from Late Latin demorari (“to linger, delay”), from Latin de- + morari (“to delay”). Related to English demur.  来自中古法语 *demuere（“停留，逗留”），是 demourer（“停留，逗留”）的现在分词，源自晚期拉丁语 demorari（“逗留，延迟”），来自拉丁语 de- + morari（“延迟”）。与英语 demur 相关。
