hypotricha:/ˌhaɪpəˈtrɪkə/ | n. | 下毛类 | A group of ciliate protozoa characterized by cilia primarily on their ventral surface, used for locomotion.  They are commonly found in freshwater habitats. | Example: Hypotricha are fascinating organisms to observe under a microscope.  下毛类在显微镜下是迷人的生物。

Example:  Some species of Hypotricha can move remarkably quickly.  一些下毛类物种可以移动得非常快。 | 近义词: ciliates (a broader category) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源:  From Greek *ὑπό* (hupó, “under”) + *θρίξ* (thríx, “hair”).
