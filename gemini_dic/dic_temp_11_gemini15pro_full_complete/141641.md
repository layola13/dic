He optimises the website for search engines. 他为搜索引擎优化网站。

Present Continuous:
The team is optimising the software for better user experience.  该团队正在优化软件以获得更好的用户体验。
They are optimising the marketing campaign to reach a wider audience. 他们正在优化营销活动以覆盖更广泛的受众。

Present Perfect:
The company has optimised its supply chain to reduce costs.  该公司已经优化了其供应链以降低成本。
He has optimised his code for faster execution. 他已经优化了他的代码以加快执行速度。

Present Perfect Continuous:
They have been optimising the website for months.  他们几个月来一直在优化网站。

PHRASE->
optimise for:  优化...以适应... (optimise for performance 为了性能优化)
optimise with:  用...优化 (optimise with new technology 用新技术优化)
fully optimise:  完全优化 (fully optimise the system 完全优化系统)
optimise further:  进一步优化 (optimise further to improve efficiency 进一步优化以提高效率)
optimise and improve:  优化和改进 (optimise and improve the process 优化和改进流程)

词源: From Latin optimus (“best”) + -ize.
