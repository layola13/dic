jaw-breaking:/ˈdʒɔːˌbreɪkɪŋ/
adjective
令人费解的；拗口的；难读的
(of a word or phrase) very long and difficult to pronounce.
Example:  The medical students were struggling with the jaw-breaking terminology.  医学生们正在努力学习拗口的术语。

Example: She recited a jaw-breaking poem with perfect fluency. 她流利地背诵了一首拗口的诗。

synonym: difficult, complicated, tongue-twisting, unpronounceable
antonym: easy, simple, pronounceable
词性: adjective
