dramaturgies:/ˌdræməˈtɜːrdʒiz/
n.
戏剧理论; 剧作法; 戏剧性; 戏剧创作技巧

例句：He studied the dramaturgies of ancient Greek theatre. 他研究了古希腊戏剧的剧作法。

近义词：dramatics, theatrics, playwriting, dramatic literature
