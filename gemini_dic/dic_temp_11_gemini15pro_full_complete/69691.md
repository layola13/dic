fenestrule:/ˈfɛnɪstruːl/| n.|小窗，透孔，气孔|A small window-like opening, especially in a bryozoan (苔藓虫) skeleton or a sponge.|例句：The fenestrule in the bryozoan skeleton allows for water flow. 苔藓虫骨骼中的小窗允许水流通过。

例句：The sponge's fenestrules are vital for filter feeding. 海绵的小窗对其滤食至关重要。|近义词：aperture, opening, pore|反义词:closure, blockage|词性:noun
