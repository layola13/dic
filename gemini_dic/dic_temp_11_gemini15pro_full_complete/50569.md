Simple Present:
Der Zug fährt jeden Tag. = The train runs every day.
Die Sonne scheint. = The sun is shining.

Present Continuous:
(German uses present tense for ongoing actions)
Er liest gerade ein Buch. = He is currently reading a book.
Sie arbeitet im Garten. = She is working in the garden.

Present Perfect:
Er hat das Buch gelesen. = He has read the book.
Sie ist nach Hause gegangen. = She has gone home.

Present Perfect Continuous:
(German doesn't have a direct equivalent, uses present perfect or other constructions)

Sie hat die ganze Zeit gelernt. = She has been studying the whole time.

Past Continuous:

Past Perfect:

Sie war nach Hause gegangen, bevor er anrief. = She had gone home before he called.
