creaming:/ˈkriːmɪŋ/ | n. | 打奶油; 提取精华;  | The act of beating cream until it becomes thick and fluffy. Also, the act of taking the best part of something. | She was creaming the butter and sugar for the cake. 她正在为蛋糕打发黄油和糖。|  The company is creaming the best graduates from universities. 这家公司正在从大学里挑选最优秀的毕业生。 | 近义词: skimming, besting | 反义词: worst, dregs | 词性: noun (名词)

creaming:/ˈkriːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 奶油状的; 极好的 | Resembling cream in consistency or color; exceptionally good. | The soup had a rich, creaming texture. 这汤口感浓郁，呈奶油状。|  They had a creaming time at the party. 他们在派对上玩得很开心。 | 近义词: creamy, excellent, superb | 反义词: lumpy, awful, terrible | 词性: adjective (形容词)
