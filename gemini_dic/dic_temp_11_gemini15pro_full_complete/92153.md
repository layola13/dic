hypersensuousness:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnʃuəsnəs/| n. |过度感性，极度感性|The state of being excessively or abnormally sensitive to sensuous impressions.|Her poetry is filled with a hypersensuousness that borders on the mystical. 她的诗歌充满了近乎神秘主义的极度感性。|Near synonyms: hypersensitivity, oversensitivity, heightened sensitivity|Antonyms: insensitivity, numbness, apathy|noun
