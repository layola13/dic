domite:/ˈdoʊmaɪt/| n.|白云石; 菱镁矿|A white or gray mineral consisting of calcium magnesium carbonate, used as an ornamental building stone and in fertilizers and refractories.  一种由碳酸钙镁组成的白色或灰色矿物，用作装饰性建筑石材以及肥料和耐火材料。|The cathedral was built from domite.  这座大教堂是用白云石建造的。|Synonyms: dolomite, magnessian limestone|Antonyms: N/A|noun
