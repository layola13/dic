abactinal:/ˌæbˈæktɪnəl/
adj.
背部的，远离口的
Situated away from the mouth; on the opposite side from the mouth, especially in echinoderms.
The abactinal surface of a starfish is covered in spines.  海星的背部表面覆盖着棘刺。
The abactinal shields of the brittle star were brightly colored.  蛇尾海星的背部骨板颜色鲜艳。
近义词: dorsal, posterior
反义词: ventral, anterior
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *abactus, past participle of abigō (“to drive away”) + -īnālis.
