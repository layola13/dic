bog-in:/ˈbɒɡ ɪn/ /ˈbɑːɡ ɪn/| phrasal verb | 开始认真工作；深陷于 | To start working seriously on something, often after a period of delay or hesitation; to become deeply involved in something. |例句：


Let's bog in and get this project finished.
让我们开始认真工作，完成这个项目。


After a long break, they bogged in and finished the report in record time.
休息了很长时间后，他们开始认真工作，并以创纪录的时间完成了报告。|近义词：get down to it, get cracking, get stuck in, dive in|反义词: procrastinate, delay, put off|词性: 动词短语
