nonclimbing:/ˌnɒnˈklaɪmɪŋ/
adjective
非攀爬的，不攀爬的
Not able to or designed for climbing.
The researchers studied the nesting habits of nonclimbing birds. 研究人员研究了不会攀爬的鸟类的筑巢习性。
The zoo features a special enclosure for nonclimbing reptiles. 动物园有一个专门的围栏，用于饲养不攀爬的爬行动物。
近义词:ground-dwelling, terrestrial
反义词:climbing, scansorial
词性:adjective
