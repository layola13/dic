interinserted:/ˌɪntərɪnˈsɜːrtɪd/
adjective
相互插入的，互相穿插的
inserted between or among other things.
The chapters are interinserted with anecdotes. 章节中穿插着奇闻轶事。
近义词: interleaved, interspersed, interwoven
反义词: separated, detached
词性: adjective

词源:
From inter- + insert.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Insert" comes from the Latin "inserere," meaning "to put in."
