arteriole:/ɑːrˈtɪəriˌoʊl/ (US) /ɑːˈtɪəriˌəʊl/ (UK)| n. |小动脉|A small branch of an artery leading into capillaries.|The arterioles constrict to reduce blood flow. 小动脉收缩以减少血流。|近义词: small artery |反义词: venule|noun
