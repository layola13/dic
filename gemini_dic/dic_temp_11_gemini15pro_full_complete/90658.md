humation:/hjuːˈmeɪʃən/ |n.|土葬，埋葬|The practice of burying a corpse.  The humation took place in the family cemetery.|例句：His humation was a simple, private affair. 他的葬礼简单而私密。

例句：The family chose humation over cremation. 这家人选择了土葬而不是火葬。|近义词：burial, interment, inhumation|反义词：cremation, exhumation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *humationem, from Latin humus (“ground, earth”) + -ationem.
