disputatious:/ˌdɪspjuːˈteɪʃəs/
|adj.|爱争论的；好辩的|Inclined to dispute; argumentative. |He became increasingly disputatious as he grew older. 他年纪越大就越爱争论。|Synonyms: argumentative, contentious, quarrelsome, combative|Antonyms: agreeable, amiable, conciliatory, peaceable|adjective

disputatious:/ˌdɪspjuːˈteɪʃəs/
|adj.|引起争议的|Likely to cause an argument. |The disputatious nature of the topic made for a lively debate.  这个话题本身就容易引起争议，所以引发了激烈的辩论。|Synonyms: controversial, debatable, contentious|Antonyms: uncontroversial, undisputed, accepted|adjective
