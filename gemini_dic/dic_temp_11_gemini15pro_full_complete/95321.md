incedingly:/ɪnˈsiːdɪŋli/| adv.|越来越，日益|Meaning "in an increasing manner," used to describe something that is becoming greater in intensity, frequency, or amount.|The music grew incedingly louder as the orchestra reached the crescendo.  音乐随着乐队达到高潮而越来越响亮。|The situation became incedingly complex as more factors came into play. 随着更多因素的加入，情况变得越来越复杂。|近义词: increasingly, progressively, more and more|反义词: decreasingly, less and less|词性:副词
Etymology: From "incede" (to walk, advance) + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "incedere" (to go in, advance).
