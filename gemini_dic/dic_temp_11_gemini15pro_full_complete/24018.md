breeches:/ˈbrɪtʃɪz/| n. |马裤；短裤|Short trousers, typically worn for riding or as part of a historical costume.  They usually extend to just below the knee.|The rider wore a smart pair of breeches. 骑手穿了一条漂亮的马裤。|Synonyms: jodhpurs, knickerbockers|Antonyms: trousers, pants|noun

breeches:/ˈbrɪtʃɪz/| n. |裤子（古英语）|An archaic term for trousers or pants.|In Shakespeare's time, men wore breeches. 在莎士比亚时代，男人们穿马裤。|Synonyms: trousers, pants|Antonyms: skirt, dress|noun
