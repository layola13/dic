coughed:/kɒft/ (UK) /kɔːft/ (US)| v.|咳嗽|Past tense of "cough," meaning to expel air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound.|He coughed loudly during the concert. 他在音乐会上大声咳嗽。|She coughed into her handkerchief. 她用手帕捂着嘴咳嗽。|近义词：spluttered, choked, hacked|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑相关的呼吸动作，例如) inhaled, breathed|词性:动词
