oscillator:/ˈɒsɪleɪtə(r)/ | n. | 振荡器，振动器 | A device that produces continuous oscillations or vibrations. | The oscillator in the radio generates the carrier wave.  收音机里的振荡器产生载波。| synonym: vibrator, generator | antonym: damper, stabilizer | noun


oscillate:/ˈɒsɪleɪt/ | v. | 振荡，摆动；犹豫 | To swing back and forth with a steady rhythm; to waver between different opinions or actions. | The pendulum oscillated back and forth. 钟摆来回摆动。 The market oscillated between gains and losses. 市场在涨跌之间波动。 | synonym: swing, vibrate, fluctuate, waver | antonym: stabilize, steady, remain constant | verb
