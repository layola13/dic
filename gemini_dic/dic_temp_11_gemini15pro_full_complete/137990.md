noodle:/ˈnuːdl/| n.|面条；笨蛋|A thin strip of pasta or a similar flour paste, eaten with a sauce or in a soup.  (A silly or foolish person - informal).|例句：I love eating noodles. 我喜欢吃面条。

例句：He's a bit of a noodle. 他有点傻。|近义词：pasta, ramen, spaghetti (for food); fool, idiot (for person - informal)|反义词:genius (for person - informal) |词性:noun
