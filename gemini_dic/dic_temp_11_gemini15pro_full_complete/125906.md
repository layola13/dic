musculospiral:/ˌmʌskjʊloʊˈspaɪrəl/ | adj. | 肌旋的，桡神经的 | Relating to or affecting both muscles and a spiral structure, specifically the radial nerve.  |  The musculospiral nerve controls the muscles that extend the wrist and fingers. 桡神经控制手腕和手指的伸展肌肉。 |  The patient experienced musculospiral paralysis after the injury. 病人在受伤后经历了桡神经麻痹。 |Synonyms: radial | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "ulnar" for the opposite nerve) | adjective

musculospiral:/ˌmʌskjʊloʊˈspaɪrəl/ | n. | 桡神经 | The radial nerve, which supplies muscles in the arm and forearm. | Damage to the musculospiral can result in wrist drop. 桡神经损伤会导致腕下垂。 | The surgeon carefully repaired the damaged musculospiral.  外科医生仔细地修复了受损的桡神经。 | Synonyms: radial nerve | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
