imprudent:/ɪmˈpruːdənt/| adj.|不明智的，轻率的，鲁莽的|Not showing care for the consequences of an action; rash.|例句：It was imprudent of you to invest all your savings in one company. 你把所有积蓄都投资在一家公司上是不明智的。

例句：Her imprudent remarks caused a great deal of offense. 她轻率的言论引起了很大的反感。|近义词：unwise, foolish, reckless, injudicious|反义词：wise, prudent, cautious, judicious|词性:形容词
