nonatmospheric:/ˌnɒnˌætməsˈfɪrɪk/
adjective
非大气的；不在大气层内的
Not relating to, existing in, or caused by the atmosphere.
例句：Nonatmospheric engines, such as rocket engines, carry their own oxygen supply.  非大气发动机，例如火箭发动机，携带自身的氧气供应。

例句：The spacecraft used nonatmospheric propulsion to navigate in space.  航天器使用非大气推进系统在太空中航行。

近义词：extraterrestrial, space-based
反义词：atmospheric

词性: adjective
