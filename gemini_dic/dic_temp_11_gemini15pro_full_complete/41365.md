corkiness:/ˈkɔːrkɪnɪs/
n.
软木塞味，木塞味；呆滞；迟钝
The dryness and corkiness in the wine were unpleasant.  这酒的干涩和木塞味令人不快。
The corkiness of the material made it ideal for insulation. 这种材料的软木质感使其成为理想的隔热材料。
His corkiness made it difficult for him to understand complex instructions. 他的迟钝使他难以理解复杂的指令。
Synonyms: mustiness, staleness, dullness, slowness
Antonyms: freshness, liveliness, sharpness, quickness
noun
