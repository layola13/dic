concerted:/kənˈsɜːrtɪd/ | adj. | 协调的，一致的，共同完成的 | Planned or performed in cooperation with others; deliberate and determined. |  例句：The students made a concerted effort to finish the project on time. 学生们齐心协力按时完成了项目。

例句：The government launched a concerted campaign against drug trafficking. 政府发起了打击贩毒的联合行动。| 近义词：joint, collaborative, combined, cooperative, united | 反义词：uncoordinated, disorganized, separate, individual | 词性: adjective

词源: Late 16th century: past participle of concert ‘arrange by agreement’, from French concerter, from Italian concertare, based on Latin concertare ‘sing or play together’, from con- ‘together’ + certare ‘contend’.
