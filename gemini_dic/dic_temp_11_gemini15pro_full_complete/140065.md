oestrogens: /ˈɛstrədʒənz/ | n. | 雌激素 | A group of steroid hormones that promote the development and maintenance of female characteristics of the body. |  High levels of oestrogens are essential for normal reproductive function in women.  女性正常的生殖功能需要高水平的雌激素。| synonyms: estrogen | antonyms: androgens | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek οἶστρος (oîstros, “gadfly, frenzy, desire”) + -gen, from Ancient Greek -γενής (-genḗs, “producing, born of”).  The term reflects the link between oestrogens and sexual receptivity in some mammals.
