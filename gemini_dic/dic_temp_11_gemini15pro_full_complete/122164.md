mistrustless:/mɪsˈtrʌstləs/
adj.
无猜忌的；信任的
Free from mistrust; trustful.
例句：In those days, children lived in a relatively mistrustless world. 在那些日子里，孩子们生活在一个相对没有猜忌的世界。

例句：The team fostered a mistrustless environment where everyone felt safe sharing their ideas.  团队营造了一个没有猜忌的环境，每个人都觉得可以安全地分享他们的想法。

近义词：trusting, confiding, unsuspicious
反义词：mistrustful, suspicious, wary
词性：形容词


词源：
"mistrustless" is formed by combining the noun "mistrust" with the suffix "-less," which indicates the absence of something.  "Mistrust" itself comes from the Middle English "mistrusten," combining "mis-" (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") and "trusten" (meaning "to have confidence in").
