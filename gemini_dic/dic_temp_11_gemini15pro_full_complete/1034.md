acceleratingly:/əkˈsɛləreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
加速地，越来越快地
in a way that becomes faster or more frequent
The car sped acceleratingly down the hill. 汽车沿着山坡加速行驶。
近义词:increasingly, rapidly, swiftly
反义词:decreasingly, slowly
词性:副词

Etymology:
From accelerate + -ing + -ly.  "Accelerate" comes from Late Latin *acceleratus, past participle of accelerare "to hasten," from Latin ad- "to" + celer "swift."
