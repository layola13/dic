alligatored:/ˈælɪɡeɪtərd/
adjective
像鳄鱼皮的；（尤指鞋子或手提包）鳄鱼皮制的
Covered with alligator skin, especially (of shoes or handbags) made of alligator hide.
例句：She carried an alligatored handbag. 她提着一个鳄鱼皮手提包。

例句：He wore alligatored shoes to the gala. 他穿着鳄鱼皮鞋去参加晚会。

近义词：crocodile-skin, alligator-hide
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective


词源： alligator + -ed.  From the noun "alligator" meaning a large reptile of the crocodile family, native to the Americas and China, with a broad snout and powerful jaws, plus the suffix "-ed" which forms adjectives meaning 'having, covered with, or resembling'.
