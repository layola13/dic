barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 气压的；晴雨表的 | Relating to atmospheric pressure or a barometer; indicating changes in atmospheric pressure. | The barometric pressure dropped significantly before the storm.  暴风雨前气压显著下降。|  synonyms: atmospheric, pressure-related | antonyms: N/A | adjective


barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 晴雨表似的; (喻)预示...的 | (figurative) Indicative or predictive of something. |  The drop in consumer confidence is a barometric reading of the state of the economy. 消费者信心下降是经济状况的晴雨表读数。| synonyms: indicative, predictive, foreshadowing | antonyms: N/A | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *barométrique*, from *baromètre* (“barometer”) + *-ique* (suffix forming adjectives), from Ancient Greek *βάρος* (báros, “weight”) + *μέτρον* (métron, “measure”).
