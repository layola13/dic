emissive:/ɪˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
发射的;发光的
emitting light; radiating
The emissive properties of the material make it ideal for use in display screens. 这种材料的发光特性使其非常适合用于显示屏。
近义词：radiant, luminous, bright
反义词：absorbent, dark, non-luminous
词性: adjective
词源：From Latin ēmittō (“send forth, let go, discharge”), from ē- (“out of, from”) + mittō (“send, let go”).
