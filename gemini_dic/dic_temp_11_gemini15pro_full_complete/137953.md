nonwetted:/ˌnɒnˈwɛtɪd/ | adj. | 未润湿的，非湿润的 | Not wetted; not having been made wet.  This refers to surfaces that repel liquids, or liquids that do not adhere to surfaces. | The nonwetted surface of the lotus leaf repels water. 荷叶未润湿的表面排斥水。|  The duck's feathers are nonwetted due to their oily coating.  鸭子的羽毛由于油性涂层而未被润湿。 |  hydrophobic, water-repellent, dry | wetted, soaked | adjective
