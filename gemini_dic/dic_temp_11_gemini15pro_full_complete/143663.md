outlabour:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbər/
verb
胜过工作; 比...更努力工作
To work harder or longer than someone else.
He outlaboured everyone on the team, but they still lost. 他比队里任何人都更努力，但他们还是输了。
近义词: outwork, surpass, outperform
反义词: underperform, lag behind
词性:v.


outlabour:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbər/
noun
过度工作; 过度劳累
Excessive work or exertion.
The outlabour involved in building the pyramids must have been immense. 建造金字塔所付出的劳动一定是巨大的。
近义词: toil, exertion, overwork
反义词: rest, leisure, inactivity
词性:n.



词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding, surpassing) + "labour" (work).  From the Old French "labourer" and ultimately from the Latin "laborare" (to work).
