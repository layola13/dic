deuces:/ˈduːsɪz/ /ˈdjuːsɪz/
deuces|n.|二，一对二，再见|Used to express farewell or departure, often in a casual or playful way. It originates from the card game meaning two.  It can also refer to the number two in dice.|"Later, deuces!" he called as he left the room. 他离开房间时喊道：“回头见！”|近义词：goodbye, farewell, see ya, peace out|反义词:hello, greetings|词性:名词
