ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lāþ, from Proto-Germanic *laiþaz (“hateful, loathsome”). Cognate with Old Norse leiðr (“loathsome, hateful”), Old High German leid (“sorry, grievous, hateful”), Gothic 𐌻𐌰𐌹𐌸𐍃 (laiþs, “evil”). More at loathe.
