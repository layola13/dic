almoner: /ˈɑːmənər/ | n. |施舍者；救济品分发者；医院的社会工作者 | A person who distributes alms or charity, especially one employed by a hospital to investigate the financial circumstances of patients and arrange for assistance where necessary. | The almoner visited the patients to assess their needs.  施舍者探访了病人以评估他们的需求。|  The hospital almoner helped the family apply for financial aid. 医院的社会工作者帮助这个家庭申请了经济援助。 | Synonyms: chaplain, social worker | Antonyms: miser | noun
