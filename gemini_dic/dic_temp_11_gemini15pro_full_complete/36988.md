columbarium:/kɒləmˈbeəriəm/ |/kəˈlʌmbɛəriəm/| n. |骨灰安置所；存放骨灰盒的建筑物或房间|A place for storing urns containing the cremated remains of the dead, typically in niches within a wall or building. |例句：The family visited the columbarium to pay their respects. 家人们前往骨灰安置所表达他们的敬意。

例句：The columbarium was a peaceful and serene place. 骨灰安置所是一个宁静祥和的地方。|近义词：mausoleum, ossuary|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "cemetery" or "graveyard" as locations for in-ground burial)|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *columbārium*  "dovecote,"  from *columba* "dove."  The resemblance of the niches for urns to the nesting boxes in a dovecote led to the term's adoption for this purpose.
