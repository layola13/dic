diffluent:/ˈdɪfluənt/| adj.|流动的，溶解的|Flowing away, dissolving; readily dissolving.  Relating to a stream that flows out of a larger body of water.|The diffluent glacier carved a new path through the valley. 这条流动的冰川在山谷中开辟了一条新路。|Synonyms: flowing, dissolving, liquefying|Antonyms: solidifying, coagulating, hardening|adjective

diffluent:/ˈdɪfluənt/| n.|支流|A stream that flows out of a larger body of water.|The Amazon has many diffluents that feed into the Atlantic. 亚马逊河有许多支流流入大西洋。|Synonyms: tributary, branch, offshoot|Antonyms: mainstream, main channel|noun
