inventrix:/ɪnˈvɛntrɪks/
noun
女发明家
A female inventor.
Example:  Hedy Lamarr, the inventrix of frequency-hopping spread spectrum, was also a famous Hollywood actress. 海蒂·拉玛，跳频扩频技术的女发明家，也是一位著名的
好莱坞女演员。

Example: The inventrix patented her groundbreaking invention in renewable energy.  这位女发明家为她在可再生能源方面的突破性发明申请了专利。
synonym: inventor, innovator, creator
antonym:  -- (No direct antonym for a person)
词性: noun
