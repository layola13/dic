horsebacks:/ˈhɔːrsbæks/| n.|马背（复数，罕用）|The term "horsebacks" is grammatically correct, but it's extremely uncommon.  It refers to the backs of multiple horses.  It's much more common to use "on horseback" to describe the act of riding. |He imagined warriors on horsebacks charging across the plains. 他想象着战士们骑着马冲过平原。| 近义词: saddles (马鞍,  more focused on the equipment), mounts (坐骑, broader term) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps  "on foot") | noun
