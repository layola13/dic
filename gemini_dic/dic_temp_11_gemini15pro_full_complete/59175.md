dysfunctioning:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/
adjective
功能失调的；失灵的
Not operating normally or properly.
The family was clearly dysfunctional.  这个家庭显然功能失调。
The equipment is old and dysfunctional.  这设备老旧且功能失调。
malfunctioning, broken, impaired, failing
functioning, working, healthy, normal
