endorhiza:/ɛndəˈraɪzə/| n. |内根菌|A fungus that grows within the root of a plant, forming a symbiotic relationship. |例句：The endorhiza plays a crucial role in nutrient uptake for the host plant. 内根菌在宿主植物的养分吸收中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Researchers are studying the benefits of different types of endorhiza for agricultural crops. 研究人员正在研究不同类型的内根菌对农作物的益处。|近义词：mycorrhiza|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑pathogenic fungi致病真菌) |词性:noun
