maslin:/ˈmæslɪn/| n.|混播谷; 混播谷物; 大麦小麦混合物; (尤指)大麦燕麦混合物;|A mixture of grains, especially wheat and rye, formerly sown together.|The farmer sowed the field with maslin.|近义词:mixed grain, mixed corn|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *maslin, from Vulgar Latin *misculinum, alteration of Latin miscellaneum “mixed,” neuter of miscellaneus. Ultimately from miscēre “to mix.”
