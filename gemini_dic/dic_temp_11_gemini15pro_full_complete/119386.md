microdiorite:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈdaɪəˌraɪt/| n. |微闪长岩|A dioritic rock of fine-grained texture.  It is an intrusive igneous rock. It is essentially a diorite with a finer grain size.|The geologist identified the sample as a microdiorite due to its texture and mineral composition.|近义词：microgabbro, dolerite (in some cases)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but contrasting rocks could be granite, rhyolite depending on composition) |noun
