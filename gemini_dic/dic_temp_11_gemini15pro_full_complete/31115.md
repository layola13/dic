chance:/tʃæns/ /tʃɑːns/| n.  机会, 可能性;  v. 碰巧, 偶然发生; adj. 偶然的| a possibility of something happening; an occasion that allows something to be done; to happen or meet by accident|例句：There is a chance of rain today. 今天有可能下雨。|近义词：opportunity, possibility, likelihood, probability; happen upon, stumble upon|反义词：impossibility, improbability; plan, intend|词性: 名词, 动词, 形容词

chance:/tʃæns/ /tʃɑːns/| v. 碰巧, 冒险|to risk something|例句：He chanced a look at the forbidden book. 他冒险看了一眼那本禁书。|近义词：risk, gamble, venture|反义词：avoid, refrain|词性: 动词

chance:/tʃæns/ /tʃɑːns/| adj. 偶然的|not planned or expected|例句：It was a chance meeting that changed my life.  一次偶然的相遇改变了我的人生。|近义词：accidental, fortuitous, random|反义词：planned, deliberate, intentional|词性: 形容词
