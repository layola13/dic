lend an ear:/lɛnd ən ɪr/
|idiomatic phrase|倾听；仔细听|To listen attentively and sympathetically to someone.|例句：Could you lend an ear? I need to talk to someone. 你能听我说说吗？我需要跟人聊聊。|例句：She always lends an ear to her friends' problems. 她总是倾听朋友们的问题。|近义词：listen, pay attention, heed|反义词：ignore, disregard|phrase
