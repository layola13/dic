encumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/| n.|累赘，妨碍，阻碍；债务，抵押|Something that encumbers; a burden or impediment.|The high taxes are an encumbrance on the economy. 高额税收是经济的负担。|Synonyms: burden, hindrance, impediment, obstacle, obstruction |Antonyms: aid, assistance, help, advantage, benefit|noun


encumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/| n.|债务，抵押（尤指房产的）|A claim or liability attached to property.|There were several encumbrances on the property, including a mortgage and a lien.  这处房产上有几项抵押，包括抵押贷款和留置权。|Synonyms: lien, mortgage, charge |Antonyms: asset|noun
