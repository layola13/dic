moratorium:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ | n. | 暂停，延期，冻结 | A legally authorized period of delay in the performance of a legal obligation or the payment of a debt. | The bank declared a moratorium on loan repayments for businesses affected by the flood. 银行宣布对受洪灾影响的企业暂停偿还贷款。| suspension, postponement, deferment | resumption, continuation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *moratorium*, neuter of *moratorius* ("of delaying"), from *morari* ("to delay").
