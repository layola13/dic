breadbasket:/ˈbredˌbæskɪt/ /ˈbredˌbɑːskɪt/
n.
粮仓；产粮区；面包篮
a region that produces a large amount of grain; a basket for serving bread
The Midwest is often referred to as the breadbasket of America. 美国中西部通常被称为美国的粮仓。
The hostess brought a breadbasket filled with warm rolls to the table. 女主人拿着一篮子热面包卷来到餐桌旁。
granary, food bowl
desert, barren land
noun
