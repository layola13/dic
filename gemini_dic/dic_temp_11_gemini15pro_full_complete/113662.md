machinists:/məˈʃiːnɪsts/
n.|机械师，机械工|A person who operates or repairs machinery, especially metalworking machinery.  A skilled worker who uses machine tools to make or repair metal parts.|The machinists carefully calibrated the lathe before starting the job. 机械师在开始工作前仔细校准了车床。|synonyms: mechanics, technicians, operators, engineers (in some contexts)|antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "unskilled laborer" as a contrast)|noun
