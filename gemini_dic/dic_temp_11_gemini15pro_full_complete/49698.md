demobbing:/diːˈmɒbɪŋ/
n.
复员，遣散（军人）
The process of discharging soldiers from military service.
The demobbing of troops after the war was a slow process. 战后部队的复员是一个缓慢的过程。
discharge, disbandment,  mustering-out
enlistment, recruitment, mobilization
noun
