blouse: /blaʊs/ (US), /blaʊz/ (UK)| n. | 女衬衫，罩衫 | A woman's shirt, typically a loose-fitting garment worn over other clothes. | She wore a silk blouse with her jeans. 她穿着一件丝绸衬衫搭配牛仔裤。| synonyms: shirt, top, tunic | antonyms: trousers, pants | noun

blouse: /blaʊs/ (US), /blaʊz/ (UK)| v. | 使…膨胀，使…鼓起 (较少用) | To gather or puff out loosely. | The wind bloused her skirt. 风吹得她的裙子鼓了起来。| synonyms: billow, puff | antonyms: flatten, deflate | verb
