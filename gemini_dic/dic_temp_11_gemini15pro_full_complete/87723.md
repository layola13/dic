hexene:/ˈhɛksiːn/| n. |己烯|An alkene with the formula C6H12.  It refers to any of several isomeric hydrocarbons, all of which contain one double bond in their six-carbon chain.|1-Hexene is an important industrial chemical used in the production of polyethylene. 己烯是一种重要的工业化学品，用于生产聚乙烯。| Isomers of hexene include 1-hexene, 2-hexene, and 3-hexene. 己烯的异构体包括1-己烯、2-己烯和3-己烯。|alkene, olefin|alkane|noun
