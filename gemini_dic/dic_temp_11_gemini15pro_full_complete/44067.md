crinkle:/ˈkrɪŋkəl/ (US), /ˈkrɪŋkl/ (UK)| n. v. |皱纹，褶皱；沙沙声|A small crease or wrinkle; a rustling sound.|The crinkle of the wrapping paper told me there was a present inside. 包装纸的沙沙声告诉我里面有礼物。|近义词：wrinkle, crease, rumple, rustle|反义词：smooth, flatten|词性:名词,动词

crinkle:/ˈkrɪŋkəl/ (US), /ˈkrɪŋkl/ (UK)| v. |(使)起皱，(使)发出沙沙声|To become or cause to become covered with small creases or wrinkles; to make a rustling sound.|The leaves crinkled under my feet. 叶子在我脚下发出沙沙声。|近义词：wrinkle, crease, rumple, rustle|反义词：smooth, flatten|词性:动词

词源:  From Middle English *crinklen, frequentative of *cringen (“to shrink, cringe”), from Old Norse kringja (“to bend in a circle”).
