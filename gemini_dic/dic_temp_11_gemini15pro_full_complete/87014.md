henrys:/ˈhenriz/| n.|亨利（电感单位）|The inductance of the coil is measured in henrys.  线圈的电感以亨利为单位。|例句：The inductor has an inductance of 10 henrys.  这个电感器有10亨利的电感。

例句：The unit of inductance is the henry. 电感的单位是亨利。

近义词: (No direct synonym for a unit, but related terms include inductance, reactance)|反义词: (No antonym for a unit)|词性:noun
