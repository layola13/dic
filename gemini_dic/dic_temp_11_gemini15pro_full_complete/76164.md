fusillade:/ˌfjuːsɪˈleɪd/ | n. | 齐射；（批评、问题等的）连发，猛烈攻击 | A fusillade is a rapid discharge of firearms or outburst of something.  A fusillade of arrows rained down upon the castle. 一阵箭雨射向城堡。 | 例句：The candidate faced a fusillade of questions from the reporters. 候选人面临记者连珠炮似的提问。 | 近义词：barrage, volley, outburst, salvo | 反义词：trickle, dribble | 词性：名词

词源: From French *fusillade*, from *fusiller* "to shoot," from *fusil* "musket," ultimately from Late Latin *focile* "steel for striking fire, flint," from Latin *focus* "hearth, fireplace."
