bifurcation:/ˌbaɪfərˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 分叉，分歧 | the division of something into two branches or parts | The bifurcation of the river created two distinct waterways. 这条河的分叉形成了两条截然不同的水道。 | synonyms: division, split, fork, branching | antonyms: convergence, union, merging | noun

bifurcate:/ˈbaɪfərˌkeɪt/ | v. | 分叉，分歧 | to divide into two branches or parts | The road bifurcates at the end of the street. 这条路在街道尽头分叉。 | synonyms: divide, split, fork, branch | antonyms: converge, unite, merge | verb
