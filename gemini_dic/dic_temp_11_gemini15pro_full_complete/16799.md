baseboard:/ˈbeɪsbɔːrd/| n. |踢脚板，护壁板|A board that runs along the bottom of an interior wall, covering the joint between the wall and the floor.|The baseboards in the old house were intricately carved. 老房子里的踢脚板雕刻精美。|The contractor installed new baseboards in the living room. 承包商在客厅安装了新的踢脚板。|Synonyms: skirting board, mopboard|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "crown molding" as an opposite architectural feature)|Noun
