bloody-mindedness:/ˌblʌdiˈmaɪndɪdnəs/
|n.|顽固; 固执; 死板; 不妥协|A deliberate and stubborn determination to do what one wants, regardless of the consequences or the opinions of others.  It suggests an unreasonable or perverse obstinacy.|He showed sheer bloody-mindedness in refusing to compromise.  他拒绝妥协，表现得十分固执。|
近义词:stubbornness, obstinacy, pigheadedness, intransigence,  willfulness
|反义词:compliance, acquiescence, cooperation, flexibility|
