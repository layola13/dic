minority: /maɪˈnɒrəti/ (UK), /maɪˈnɔːrəti/ (US) | n. | 少数；少数民族；未成年 | A smaller number or part, especially a group of people differing from others in race, religion, language, or political persuasion. |  Ethnic minorities make up 10% of the population. 少数民族占人口的10%。| synonyms: few, small number, handful | antonyms: majority, mass, multitude | noun

minority: /maɪˈnɒrəti/ (UK), /maɪˈnɔːrəti/ (US) | adj. | 少数的；次要的 | Relating to or belonging to a minority group.  |  This is a minority view within the party. 这是党内的少数派观点。| synonyms: lesser, smaller, minor | antonyms: major, main, principal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *minorité, from Latin minoritās (“lesser number, smaller part”), from minor (“smaller”).
