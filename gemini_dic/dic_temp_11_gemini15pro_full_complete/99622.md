interjurisdictional:/ˌɪntərˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃənəl/ | adj. | 跨司法管辖区的 | Relating to or involving different legal or administrative jurisdictions. |  The interjurisdictional task force is investigating the cross-border crime.  跨司法管辖区工作组正在调查这起跨境犯罪。|  cross-border, interstate, multi-jurisdictional | intrajurisdictional, local | adjective
Etymology: inter- (between) + jurisdiction + -al (suffix forming adjectives)
From Latin *inter* (between) and *jurisdictio* (administration of justice)
