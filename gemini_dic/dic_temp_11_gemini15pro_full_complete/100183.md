interrelated:/ˌɪntərɪˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
相互关联的，互相联系的
Having a mutual or reciprocal relation.  Different parts of the system are all interrelated.
The problems of poverty and unemployment are often interrelated. 贫困和失业问题常常相互关联。
近义词: interconnected, interdependent, linked, related
反义词: independent, separate, unrelated
词性: adjective

词源: inter- (between) + related (connected)  From Latin "inter" (between) + "relatus" (carried back, related).
