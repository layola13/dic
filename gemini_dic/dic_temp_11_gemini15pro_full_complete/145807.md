overdedicate: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪkeɪt/
verb
过度奉献，过分投入
To dedicate excessively or beyond what is reasonable or healthy.
He overdedicated himself to his work and neglected his family. 他过度投入工作而忽略了家人。
She overdedicated herself to the cause, sacrificing her own well-being. 她过分投入这项事业，牺牲了自己的健康。
近义词: overwork, overexert
反义词: neglect, underdedicate
词性: verb

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "dedicate" (from Latin "dedicare," meaning "to declare, to devote").
