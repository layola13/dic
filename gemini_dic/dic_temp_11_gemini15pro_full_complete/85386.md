hartbeest:/ˈhɑːrtbiːst/| n. |狷羚|A large African antelope with a reddish-brown coat, long legs, and curved horns.|例句：The hartbeest grazed peacefully on the savanna. 狷羚在热带草原上平静地吃草。

例句：We saw a herd of hartbeest during our safari. 在我们狩猎旅行中，我们看到了一群狷羚。|近义词：antelope, hartebeest|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Afrikaans hartebeest, from hart "hart" + beest "beast."
