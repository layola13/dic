hydrometeor:/ˈhaɪdrəˈmiːtiər/ | n. |水凝物；大气中的水分|Any product of the condensation or sublimation of atmospheric water vapor, whether formed in the free atmosphere or at the earth's surface. This includes things like rain, snow, hail, fog, dew, frost, etc.|The pilot encountered severe hydrometeors during the flight. 飞行员在飞行中遇到了严重的水凝物。|Synonyms: precipitation, condensation, atmospheric water|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "dry air" or "anhydrous conditions")|Noun
