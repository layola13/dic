commensation: /kɒmɛnˈseɪʃən/ (British) /kɑːmɛnˈseɪʃən/ (American)
n.
开始，着手
the act of beginning something.
例句：
The commencement of the project has been delayed.
该项目的开始已被推迟。

The commencement ceremony was held in the auditorium.
毕业典礼在礼堂举行。


近义词：beginning, start, initiation, outset
反义词：conclusion, end, termination
词性: 名词


commence: /kəˈmɛns/ (British) /kəˈmɛns/ (American)
v.
开始，着手
begin; start.
例句：
The meeting will commence at 9 a.m. sharp.
会议将于上午9点准时开始。

We will commence building the new house next spring.
我们将在明年春天开始建造新房子。

近义词：begin, start, initiate
反义词：end, finish, conclude, terminate
词性: 动词
