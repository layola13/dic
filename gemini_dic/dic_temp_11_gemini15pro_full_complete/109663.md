leucocytic:/ˌljuːkəˈsɪtɪk/
adjective
白细胞的;与白细胞有关的
Relating to or involving leukocytes (white blood cells).
The leucocytic count is high, indicating an infection. 白细胞计数很高，表明有感染。
Synonyms: leukocytic
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective

leucocytic:/ˌljuːkəˈsɪtɪk/
adjective
白细胞增多的
Characterized by an increase in the number of leukocytes in the blood.
The patient presented with leucocytic infiltration in the affected tissue. 病人患处组织出现白细胞浸润。
Synonyms: leukemic, leucocytotic
Antonyms: leukopenic
词性: adjective
