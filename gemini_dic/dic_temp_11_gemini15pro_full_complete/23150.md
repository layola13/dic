boutique:/buːˈtiːk/| n.|精品店；时装店|A small shop selling fashionable clothes, jewellery, or other luxury goods.|She bought a beautiful scarf from a boutique in Paris. 她从巴黎的一家精品店买了一条漂亮的围巾。|Synonyms: shop, store, outlet|Antonyms: department store, supermarket|noun

boutique:/buːˈtiːk/| adj.|精品的；小规模而时尚的|Relating to or suggesting a small, fashionable shop.|They stayed in a boutique hotel. 他们住在一个精品酒店。|Synonyms: stylish, chic, exclusive|Antonyms: mass-market, generic|adjective
