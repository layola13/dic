indefinable:/ˌɪndɪˈfaɪnəbl/
adjective
难以定义的；无法解释的；模糊不清的
Not able to be defined or described exactly.

Her beauty is indefinable.
她的美丽难以言喻。

There's an indefinable something about him that makes me uneasy.
他身上有种难以名状的东西让我感到不安。

Synonym:indescribable, inexpressible, vague, elusive, subtle
Antonym:definable, describable, explicit, clear, concrete
词性:形容词
