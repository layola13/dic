counterclaimed:/ˌkaʊntərˈkleɪmd/
verb
反诉，反驳
to assert a claim against an opposing party in a legal action, especially in response to a previous claim.
The company counterclaimed, arguing that the supplier had breached the contract.  该公司提起反诉，辩称供应商违反了合同。
The defendant counterclaimed for damages.  被告反诉要求赔偿损失。
Synonym:  offset, retort, rejoin
Antonym:  accept, concede
word type: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + claimed, past participle of claim (v.).
