inclination:/ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃn/| n.|倾向，爱好，斜坡|A feeling that makes you want to do something.  A slope or the angle at which something slopes.|She has an inclination to overspend. 她有过度消费的倾向。The roof has a steep inclination. 屋顶很陡。|近义词：tendency, propensity, leaning, slope, gradient|反义词：aversion, disinclination|noun
