overloftily: /ˌoʊvərˈlɔːftɪli/ | adv. | 傲慢地，高傲地 | In a haughty and condescending manner, as if looking down on others. | He spoke overloftily to the waiter, as if he were addressing a servant. 他傲慢地对服务员说话，就好像在对仆人说话一样。| She dismissed my suggestion overloftily, making it clear she thought it was beneath her. 她傲慢地驳回了我的建议，明确表示她认为这 beneath her. | 近义词: haughtily, arrogantly, superciliously, disdainfully | 反义词: humbly, modestly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "overloft" + "-ly." "Overloft" is formed from "over-" (above) + "loft" (an upper room or floor). The sense evolved from the physical position of being higher up to the metaphorical sense of being superior.
