chirped:/tʃɜːrpt/ | v. | 吱吱叫，唧唧喳喳地说 | to make a short, high-pitched sound like a bird | The bird chirped merrily in the tree. 小鸟在树上欢快地啁啾。| synonyms: twitter, peep, cheep | antonyms: croak, growl | verb

chirped:/tʃɜːrpt/ | adj. | 吱吱叫的，唧唧喳喳的 | uttered in a short, high-pitched tone | She delivered the news in a chirped, excited voice. 她用一种兴奋的、尖锐的声音宣布了这个消息。| synonyms: twittering, peeping, cheeping | antonyms: croaking, growling | adjective
