dissolved:/dɪˈzɑːlvd/ /dɪˈzɒlvd/
adjective|溶解的，解散的|Broken up or brought to an end.|The company was dissolved after the scandal.  这家公司在丑闻之后解散了。|dispersed, broken up|united, combined
verb (past participle of dissolve)|溶解，解散|To become incorporated into a liquid so as to form a solution; to cause to disperse or disappear; to bring to an end.|The sugar dissolved in the water. 糖溶解在水中。|liquefy, melt|solidify, freeze
word origin|From Latin dissolvere, from dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen"|
