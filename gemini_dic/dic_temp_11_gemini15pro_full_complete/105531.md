kilowatt-hour:/ˈkɪləwɑːt aʊər/ | n. |千瓦时|A unit of energy equal to the work done by one kilowatt acting for one hour.  It is used to measure electrical energy consumption. |The average household uses about 900 kilowatt-hours of electricity per month.|Synonyms: kWh (abbreviation), Unit of energy |Antonyms: None | Noun
