invitation: /ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 邀请，邀请函 |  The act of inviting someone to go somewhere or to do something; a written or spoken request to someone to go somewhere or to do something. | I received an invitation to their wedding. 我收到了他们婚礼的邀请。|  She extended an invitation to dinner. 她发出了晚餐邀请。 | synonym: request, summons, call | antonym: refusal, rejection | noun

invitation: /ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 诱惑，吸引 | Something that encourages you to do something, especially something wrong or unwise. |  The open door was an invitation to thieves.  敞开的门对小偷来说是一种诱惑。| Leaving the key in the car is an invitation to disaster. 把钥匙留在车里会招来灾祸。 | synonym: enticement, temptation, lure | antonym: deterrent, discouragement | noun
