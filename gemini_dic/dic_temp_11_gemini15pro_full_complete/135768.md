nonprofit:/nɒnˈprɒfɪt/ (UK) /nɑːnˈprɑːfɪt/ (US)| adj. n.|非营利的；非盈利组织|Not established for the purpose of making a profit; not entered into for money.|例句：The Red Cross is a nonprofit organization. 红十字会是一个非营利组织。|近义词：charitable, not-for-profit, philanthropic|反义词：for-profit, commercial|词性:形容词/名词
Etymology: non- + profit. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Profit" comes from Old French and ultimately Latin, referring to gain or advantage.
