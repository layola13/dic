bullfighting: /ˈbʊlˌfaɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 斗牛 | The sport of fighting bulls, especially in Spain, Portugal, and Latin America. | Bullfighting is a controversial tradition in Spain. 斗牛在西班牙是一个备受争议的传统。 |  Many tourists travel to Spain to witness bullfighting. 许多游客前往西班牙观看斗牛。 | 近义词: tauromachy | 反义词: animal welfare | 词性: noun

词源: bull + fight + -ing

bull (n.): from Old English *bula, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bul, German Bulle ‘bull, castrated bull’, also to bullock.

fight (v.): from Old English feohtan ‘contend’, of West Germanic origin; related to Dutch vechten and German fechten.

-ing (suffix): forming nouns from verbs, denoting an action or process, or its result.
