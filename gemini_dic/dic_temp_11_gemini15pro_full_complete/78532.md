ghostfish:/ˈɡoʊstˌfɪʃ/| n. |鬼鱼，幽灵鱼|A type of fish known for its pale, translucent appearance, often found in caves or deep waters. It refers to several different species, most notably the cavefish *Astyanax mexicanus*.|例句：The ghostfish drifted eerily through the dark cave. 鬼鱼怪异地在黑暗的洞穴中漂流。

例句：Researchers are studying the ghostfish to understand how animals adapt to extreme environments. 研究人员正在研究鬼鱼，以了解动物如何适应极端环境。|近义词：cavefish, blindfish (sometimes)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "ghost" + "fish". "Ghost" refers to the pale, almost translucent appearance of these fish.  "Fish" denotes the animal class.
