ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *nave* "ship, vessel, nave of a church," from Latin *navis* "ship."  The architectural term arose from the resemblance of a church's central area to the hull of a ship, perhaps reinforced by the idea of the Church as a vessel of salvation.
