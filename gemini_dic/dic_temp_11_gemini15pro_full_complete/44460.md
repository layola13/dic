cross-vault:/ˈkrɒs vɔːlt/ (US) /ˈkrɔːs vɔːlt/ (UK)| n.| 交叉拱顶 |A vault formed by the intersection of two barrel vaults of equal size which create four angled groins.| The cathedral boasted a magnificent cross-vault.  这座大教堂拥有宏伟的交叉拱顶。|The church features a series of elegant cross-vaults. 这座教堂的特点是一系列优雅的交叉拱顶。 |Synonyms: groin vault, quadripartite vault | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps flat ceiling) | noun

cross-vault:/ˈkrɒs vɔːlt/ (US) /ˈkrɔːs vɔːlt/ (UK)| v.| 建造交叉拱顶 |To construct a vault formed by the intersection of two barrel vaults.| They cross-vaulted the chapel ceiling. 他们为小教堂的天花板建造了交叉拱顶。|The builders skillfully cross-vaulted the nave. 建筑工人巧妙地为中殿建造了交叉拱顶。|Synonyms:  construct a groin vault, build a quadripartite vault | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | verb
