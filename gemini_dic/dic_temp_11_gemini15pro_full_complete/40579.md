conundrum:/kəˈnʌndrəm/ | n. |难题，谜题|A confusing and difficult problem or question.|例句：The scientists were faced with a conundrum they couldn't solve. 科学家们面临着一个他们无法解决的难题。

例句：She was struggling with the conundrum of how to balance work and family. 她正在努力解决如何平衡工作和家庭的难题。|近义词：puzzle, riddle, enigma, problem, dilemma|反义词:solution, answer|词性:名词
