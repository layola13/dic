mariposa:/ˌmɑːrɪˈpoʊsə/ (US), /ˌmærɪˈpəʊsə/ (UK)| n. |蝴蝶|A flying insect with large, often brightly colored wings. From the Spanish word meaning "butterfly."|例句：I saw a beautiful mariposa fluttering in the garden. 我看到一只美丽的蝴蝶在花园里飞舞。

例句：The children chased the mariposas through the field. 孩子们在田野里追逐蝴蝶。

近义词：butterfly, moth (although moths are typically nocturnal)|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
