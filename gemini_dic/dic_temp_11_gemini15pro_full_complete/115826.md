marketablenesses:/ˌmɑːrkɪtəˈbɪlnɪsɪz/ | n. |适销性，市场性 |The quality of being easy to sell or being in demand.  This refers to how easily something can be sold or marketed due to its desirable qualities, usefulness, or appeal to potential buyers. |  The marketableness of her skills made her a sought-after candidate. 她的技能的市场性使她成为炙手可热的候选人。|  The new phone's marketableness was boosted by its innovative features.  这款新手机的市场性因其创新功能而得到提升。 | 近义词: salability, merchantability, marketability | 反义词: unsalability, unmarketability | 词性: noun
