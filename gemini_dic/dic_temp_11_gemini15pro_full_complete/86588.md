heliogram:/ˈhiːliəɡræm/| n.|日光信号，日光电报|A message transmitted by reflecting sunlight with a mirror, typically using Morse code.|例句：The heliogram flashed across the valley, carrying a vital message. 日光信号闪过了山谷，传递着一条重要的信息。|例句：Rescuers used a heliogram to signal their location to the search plane. 救援人员使用日光信号向搜索飞机示意他们的位置。|近义词：signal, message|反义词: None|noun
