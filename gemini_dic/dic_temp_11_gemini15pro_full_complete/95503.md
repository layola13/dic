inclinatory:/ɪnˈklaɪnətɔːri/ /ɪnˈklaɪnəˌtɔri/
adjective
倾斜的; 有…倾向的
Having a tendency or inclination towards something.
He had an inclinatory attitude towards accepting the offer.  他倾向于接受这个提议。
The terrain has an inclinatory slope, making it difficult to climb.  这片地形倾斜，很难攀爬。
近义词: inclined, disposed, prone, tending
反义词: averse, disinclined, opposed
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inclinatorius, from Latin inclinare (“to lean, bend, incline”), from in- (“in, into”) + clinare (“to bend, lean”).
