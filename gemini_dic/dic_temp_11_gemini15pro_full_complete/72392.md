fluctuation:/ˌflʌktʃuˈeɪʃn/| n. |波动，起伏，涨落|An irregular rising and falling in number or amount; a variation.|The fluctuation in the stock market made investors nervous. 股票市场的波动让投资者感到紧张。|近义词：variation, oscillation, swing, instability|反义词：stability, constancy|词性:noun

fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/| v. |波动，起伏，涨落|To rise and fall irregularly in number or amount.|Temperatures have fluctuated dramatically this month. 这个月气温波动很大。|近义词：vary, oscillate, swing|反义词：stabilize, remain constant|词性:verb

词源:  From Latin fluctuatus, past participle of fluctuare "to wave, undulate, fluctuate," from fluctus "wave, billow," related to fluere "to flow."
