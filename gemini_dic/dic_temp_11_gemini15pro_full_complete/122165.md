mistrusts:/mɪsˈtrʌsts/ | v. | 不信任，怀疑 |  To doubt the honesty or reliability of; regard with suspicion. | She mistrusts anyone who offers easy solutions. 她不信任任何提供简单解决方案的人。| He mistrusts politicians and their promises. 他不信任政客和他们的承诺。 | synonyms: doubts, suspects, distrusts | antonyms: trusts, believes, has faith in | verb


mistrust:/mɪsˈtrʌst/ | n. | 不信任，怀疑 | Lack of trust or confidence. | There is a deep mistrust between the two communities. 这两个社群之间存在着根深蒂固的不信任。|  His mistrust of authority stems from his childhood experiences. 他对权威的不信任源于他的童年经历。| synonyms: suspicion, distrust, doubt | antonyms: trust, confidence, faith | noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *mistrusten*, from mis- + trusten (“to trust”). Equivalent to mis- +‎ trust.  The noun is derived from the verb.
