monotrematous:/ˌmɒnəˈtriːməs/ | adj. | 单孔目动物的 | Belonging or pertaining to the Monotremata, an order of primitive mammals that lay eggs instead of giving birth to live young, such as the echidna and the platypus. |  The platypus, a monotrematous mammal, is native to Australia.  鸭嘴兽，一种单孔目哺乳动物，原产于澳大利亚。 |  egg-laying, oviparous | viviparous | adjective
