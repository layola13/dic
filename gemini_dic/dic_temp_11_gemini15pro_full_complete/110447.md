likert: /ˈlɪkərt/ | adj. | 李克特式的；李克特量表的 | relating to a psychometric scale in which respondents specify their level of agreement or disagreement on a symmetrical agree-disagree scale for a series of statements. |  The survey used a five-point Likert scale to measure customer satisfaction.  该调查使用五点李克特量表来衡量客户满意度。 | 近义词: psychometric, rating | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

likert scale: /ˈlɪkərt skeɪl/ | n. | 李克特量表 | a psychometric scale in which respondents specify their level of agreement or disagreement on a symmetrical agree-disagree scale for a series of statements. | The Likert scale allowed participants to express nuanced opinions. 李克特量表允许参与者表达细致的意见。 | 近义词: rating scale, psychometric scale | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
