carabid:/ˈkærəbɪd/| n.|步甲，步甲虫|A beetle of a large family (Carabidae) that includes the ground beetles, tiger beetles, and bombardier beetles, many of which are predatory.|例句：The carabid beetle is a beneficial insect in the garden.  花园里的步甲虫是一种益虫。

例句：Researchers are studying the predatory behavior of carabids. 研究人员正在研究步甲的捕食行为。|近义词：ground beetle, tiger beetle (for some species)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology->
From New Latin *Carabidae, from Ancient Greek κάραβος (kárabos, “horned beetle, stag beetle”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- (“horn”).
