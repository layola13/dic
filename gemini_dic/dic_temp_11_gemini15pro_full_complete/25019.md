brutishly:/ˈbruːtɪʃli/
adverb
野蛮地；残忍地
in a brutish manner; cruelly.
He was brutishly murdered. 他被残忍地杀害了。
Synonyms: savagely, cruelly, barbarously, viciously, brutally
Antonyms: gently, kindly, humanely
词性: 副词
