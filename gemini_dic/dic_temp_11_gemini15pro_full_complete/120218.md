mines:/maɪnz/| n.| 矿井；矿山；地雷；矿藏；宝藏|a pit or excavation in the earth from which mineral substances are taken; an explosive device placed just below the surface of the ground, detonated by contact or proximity with a person or vehicle; a rich source of a particular quality or substance|The miners worked in dangerous mines. 矿工们在危险的矿井里工作。|近义词：pits, quarries, collieries, explosives, bombs|反义词: - |词性:名词

mines:/maɪnz/| v. (third-person singular simple present mines, present participle mining, simple past and past participle mined)| 开采；布雷|extract or obtain (coal or other minerals) from a mine; lay explosive mines in (an area)|They mined for gold in the mountains. 他们在山里开采黄金。|近义词：excavate, extract, quarry, exploit|反义词: - |词性:动词
