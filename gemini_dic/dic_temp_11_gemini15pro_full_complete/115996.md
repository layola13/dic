marshalled:/ˈmɑːrʃəld/ (US) /ˈmɑːʃəld/ (UK)| v. adj.|排列，整理；集结；控制；引导|to arrange or assemble (something, such as troops or facts) in order|The troops were marshalled on the parade ground. 部队在阅兵场上集结。|Synonyms: assemble, organize, arrange, gather, muster, array, align, order, deploy|Antonyms: disperse, scatter, disorganize|verb
