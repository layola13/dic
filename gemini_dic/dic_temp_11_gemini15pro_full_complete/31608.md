chastise:/ˈtʃæstaɪz/ /tʃæˈstaɪz/
verb
惩罚，责罚
rebuke or reprimand severely.
The teacher chastised the student for being late. 老师责罚了迟到的学生。
He was chastised for his dishonesty. 他因为不诚实而受到惩罚。
synonym:reprimand, rebuke, scold, punish, discipline, castigate, censure
antonym:praise, commend, reward, encourage
词性:v.
