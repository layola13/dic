mzee:/mˈzeɪ/| n.|尊敬的老人；长者；酋长|A term of respect for an older man, especially a leader or elder in a community.  It comes from Swahili.|例句：The mzee offered wise counsel to the young warriors.  这位长者向年轻的战士们提供了明智的建议。

例句：The village mzee called for a meeting. 村里的长者召集了一次会议。|近义词：elder, patriarch, chief, senior|反义词:youth, youngster|词性:noun
