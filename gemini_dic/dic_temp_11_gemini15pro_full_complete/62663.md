engage:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/
|v.|参与；从事；吸引|To participate or become involved in something; to occupy or attract the attention or interest of someone or something.|He engaged in a lively debate with his colleagues. 他和同事们进行了热烈的辩论。|participate, involve, occupy, attract|disengage, withdraw, ignore, repel|verb
