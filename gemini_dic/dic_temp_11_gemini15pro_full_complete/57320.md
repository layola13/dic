downtowners:/ˌdaʊnˈtaʊnərz/
noun
市中心居民；在市中心工作的人
People who live or work in the downtown area of a city.
The downtowners often complain about the lack of parking. 市中心居民经常抱怨停车位不足。
The downtowners enjoy the convenience of living close to work and entertainment. 在市中心工作的人享受着靠近工作场所和娱乐场所的便利。
synonyms: city dwellers, urbanites
antonyms: suburbanites, country folk
n.
