inaudible: /ɪnˈɔːdəbl/ (British) /ˌɪnˈɑːdəbl/ (American) | adj. |  听不见的；难以理解的 | Not able to be heard or understood clearly. |

Example 1: The recording was so faint it was almost inaudible.  录音太微弱了，几乎听不见。

Example 2: Her whisper was inaudible over the noise of the traffic.  在交通噪音中，她的低语听不见。


Synonym: unheard, imperceptible, faint, muffled

Antonym: audible, clear, distinct, loud

词性: 形容词 (adjective)
