词源：From Denmark + -s (plural marker).  The name "Denmark" originates from the Old Norse *Danmǫrk, meaning "the march of the Danes".  "Dane" likely refers to the Dani, a North Germanic tribe mentioned in 6th-century sources. "Mark" signifies a borderland or frontier.
