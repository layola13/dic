obstipation:/ˌɒbstɪˈpeɪʃən/| n. |便秘；严重便秘|Severe constipation, especially when impacted fecal matter blocks the bowel.|The patient presented with obstipation and abdominal distension. 病人表现为便秘和腹胀。|近义词：constipation, costiveness|反义词：diarrhea|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin obstīpātiōn- (stem of obstīpātiō), from obstīpātus (past participle of obstīpāre ‘to become numb or stiff, be astounded’), from ob- (against) + stīpāre ‘to press, pack closely’.
