fallfish:/ˈfɔːlfɪʃ/| n. | 秋鱼 | A North American freshwater fish (Semotilus corporalis) of the minnow family, found in clear streams. It is silvery with a dusky back and fins, and a reddish spot at the base of the tail. |  The fallfish is the largest minnow native to eastern North America. 秋鱼是北美东部最大的本土鲤科鱼类。

 The fallfish is a popular game fish for anglers. 秋鱼是垂钓者喜爱的游钓鱼类。 | 近义词: chub, dace | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) | 词性: noun
