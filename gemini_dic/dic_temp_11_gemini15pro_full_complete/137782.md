nonvegetativenesses:/ˌnɒnˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪtɪvnəsɪz/
n.
无生命状态(复数)
The state or quality of being nonvegetative; lack of vegetative functions.
Due to severe brain damage, the patient exhibited multiple nonvegetativenesses, including the inability to breathe independently and lack of responsiveness to external stimuli. 由于严重的脑损伤，病人表现出多种无生命状态，包括无法自主呼吸和对外界刺激没有反应。
Near synonyms:inanimateness, lifelessness
Antonyms: vitality, aliveness
noun


nonvegetativeness:/ˌnɒnˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/
n.
无生命状态
The state or quality of being nonvegetative; lack of vegetative functions.
Prolonged nonvegetativeness can lead to irreversible organ damage. 长期无生命状态会导致不可逆的器官损伤。
Near synonyms:inanimateness, lifelessness
Antonyms: vitality, aliveness
noun


nonvegetative:/ˌnɒnˈvɛdʒɪteɪtɪv/
adj.
无生命的，非植物性的
Not vegetative; lacking the characteristics of plant life; inanimate.
The patient remained in a nonvegetative state for several weeks. 病人持续几周处于无生命状态。
Near synonyms:inanimate, lifeless
Antonyms: vegetative, animate, living
