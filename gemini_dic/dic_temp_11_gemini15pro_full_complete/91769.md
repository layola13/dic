|v.|过分强调|The politician was accused of hyperemphasizing the threat to gain votes.  这位政治家被指责为了获得选票而过分强调了威胁。|  He kept hyperemphasizing his role in the project, which annoyed his colleagues. 他一直过分强调自己在项目中的作用，这让他的同事们很恼火。|overemphasizing, overstressing|understating, downplaying|verb

Etymology:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, above, beyond
"emphasize" (from French "emphasiser"): to give emphasis to; stress
