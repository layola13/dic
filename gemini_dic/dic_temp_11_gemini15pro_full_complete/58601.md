dulled:/dʌld/| adj.|钝的，迟钝的，无光泽的，暗淡的|Made less sharp, bright, or sensitive; weakened.|The knife was dulled from years of use. 这把刀因为多年的使用而变钝了。|近义词：blunted, subdued, muted, weakened|反义词：sharpened, brightened, heightened|词性:形容词

dulled:/dʌld/| v.|使钝，使迟钝，使无光泽，使暗淡|(past tense and past participle of dull) To make less sharp, bright, or sensitive; to weaken.|The constant noise dulled my senses.  持续不断的噪音使我的感官变得迟钝。|近义词：blunted, subdued, muted, weakened|反义词：sharpened, brightened, heightened|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *dullen*, from Old English *dol*, meaning “foolish, stupid”. The meaning shifted to "blunt" and then to "make less intense".
