cubation: /kjuːˈbeɪʃən/
n.
孵化; 培养; 潜伏期
the act of sitting on eggs to keep them warm until they hatch; the development of ideas, thoughts, or plans; the period between infection and the appearance of signs of a disease.
The cubation period for chickens is 21 days.  小鸡的孵化期是21天。
The project is still in its cubation phase.  这个项目仍处于酝酿阶段。
The cubation period for this virus is about two weeks. 这种病毒的潜伏期大约是两周。
incubation, brooding, gestation, development
dormancy, inactivity
noun
