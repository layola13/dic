malanders:/ˈmæləndərz/| n.|马的膝盖疮|A dry scurf on the inside of the forelegs of horses, just above the knee, often accompanied by cracks and fissures.  It is considered a type of eczema. |The old horse suffered from malanders, making it difficult for him to bend his knees.|近义词:grease, scratches, sallenders|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "healthy skin/coat")|noun
