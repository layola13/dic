mayduke:/ˈmeɪˌduːk/| n.|五月公爵（一种欧洲甜樱桃的品种）|A variety of sweet cherry, typically ripening in May.|The mayduke cherries are ripe and ready to be picked. 五月公爵樱桃熟了，可以采摘了。|近义词：cherry|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词

词源： "May Duke" cherry's name likely comes from its ripening time in May and "Duke," possibly referring to a noble person or a large size.  It's a long-standing variety, with mentions dating back to the 17th century.
