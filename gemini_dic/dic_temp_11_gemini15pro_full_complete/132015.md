noncorrelation:/ˌnɒnkɒrəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 无关；无关联；不相关 | Absence of a linear relationship between two variables; the condition in which two things do not correlate. | Example 1: The study found a noncorrelation between coffee consumption and heart disease.  这项研究发现咖啡的摄入量与心脏病无关。

Example 2:  There is a clear noncorrelation between shoe size and intelligence. 鞋码和智力之间明显没有关联。| Synonyms: independence, unrelatedness, dissociation | Antonyms: correlation, relationship, association | noun
