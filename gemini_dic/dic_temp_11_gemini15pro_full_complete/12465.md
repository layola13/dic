aspalathus:/ˌæspəˈleɪθəs/
noun
南非茶，博士茶
A shrub-like legume native to South Africa, whose fermented leaves are used to make a caffeine-free herbal tea, also called rooibos.
Aspalathus linearis is a popular tea in South Africa. 南非茶在南非很受欢迎。
synonym:rooibos
antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific plant.  Could consider "caffeinated tea" as a contrasting beverage.)
词性: noun
