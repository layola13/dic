ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *episcopālis, from Latin episcopus (“bishop”), from Ancient Greek ἐπίσκοπος (epískopos, “overseer, guardian, bishop”), from ἐπί (epí, “on, upon, over”) + σκοπός (skopós, “watcher, guard”).
