lyencephala:/laɪɛnˈsɛfələ/
n.
rhombencephalon; hindbrain
The lyencephala is the posterior part of the brain, composed of the metencephalon (cerebellum and pons) and myelencephalon (medulla oblongata).
The lyencephala controls vital functions such as breathing and heart rate.
The doctor explained that damage to the lyencephala can be life-threatening.
近义词:hindbrain, rhombencephalon
反义词:proencephalon (forebrain)
词性:noun
