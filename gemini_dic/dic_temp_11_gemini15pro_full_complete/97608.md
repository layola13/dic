inhalant:/ɪnˈheɪlənt/ | n. | 吸入剂；可吸入物 | A substance that is inhaled, often for its intoxicating effects. |  Example: The fumes from the inhalant made him dizzy.  吸入剂的烟雾使他头晕。| Example:  Glue sniffing is a dangerous form of inhalant abuse. 吸食胶水是一种危险的滥用吸入剂的形式。 | Synonyms:  aerosol, vapor,  fumes | Antonyms: exhalant | noun
