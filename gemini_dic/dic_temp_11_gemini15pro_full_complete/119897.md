militarizations: /ˌmɪlɪtərɪˈzeɪʃənz/
| n. |军事化|The process of equipping and organizing something, like a country, region, or group, for military purposes.  It can also refer to the increasing influence of the military on a society's culture and policies.|The militarization of the border has significantly increased tensions between the two countries. 边境的军事化大大加剧了两国之间的紧张局势。|militarization is the noun form of militarize|demilitarization, civilianization
