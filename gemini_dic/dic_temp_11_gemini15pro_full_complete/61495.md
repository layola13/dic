embroideries:/ɪmˈbrɔɪd(ə)riz/
noun
刺绣品；绣花；刺绣工艺
Decorative needlework.

The cushion covers were adorned with intricate embroideries.
这些靠垫套装饰着复杂的刺绣。

synonyms: needlework, needlepoint, fancywork, crewelwork, cross-stitch
antonyms: plainness, simplicity


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "embrouder," meaning "to decorate with needlework," from "en-" (in, on) + "brouder" (to embroider), from Frankish "*brod," a pointed instrument.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*brozdą" (point, prickle).
