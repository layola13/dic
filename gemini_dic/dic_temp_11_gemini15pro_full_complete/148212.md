overshot:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɑːt/ /ˌəʊvəˈʃɒt/ | v. n. adj. | 超过，过冲，上射式水车 | to go further than intended; to shoot or pass over or beyond |  The car overshot the junction and ended up in a field. 汽车超过了路口，最终停在了田里。 | 近义词：exceed, surpass, overshoot, go beyond | 反义词: undershoot, fall short | verb

overshot:/ˈoʊvərʃɑːt/ /ˈəʊvəʃɒt/ | n. | 上射式水车 | a water wheel that is driven by water flowing over the top | The old mill still used an overshot water wheel. 这座古老的磨坊仍在使用上射式水车。 | 近义词：water wheel | 反义词: N/A | noun

overshot:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɑːt/ /ˌəʊvəˈʃɒt/ | adj. | 上射式的 | relating to or denoting an overshot water wheel | The mill had an overshot design. 这座磨坊采用上射式设计。 | 近义词：N/A | 反义词: undershot | adjective
