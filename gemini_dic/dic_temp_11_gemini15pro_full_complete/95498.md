inclinable:/ɪnˈklaɪnəbl/| adj.|可倾斜的，易于…的，有…倾向的|Having a natural tendency or disposition to do something; readily tending or inclined; capable of being inclined or bent.|The seat back is inclinable to three different positions. 座椅靠背可以倾斜到三个不同的位置。|近义词：disposed, prone, liable, susceptible|反义词：averse, resistant, unwilling|词性:形容词
inclinable:/ɪnˈklaɪnəbl/| adj.|倾向…的|Tending toward a particular belief or opinion, favorably disposed.|She is inclinable to agree with your proposal. 她倾向于同意你的提议。|近义词：leaning, tending, inclined|反义词：opposed, against|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inclinabilis, from Latin inclinare (“to bend, incline”), from in- (“in, into”) + clinare (“to lean, bend”).
