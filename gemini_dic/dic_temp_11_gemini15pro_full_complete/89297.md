homoeopathic:/ˌhəʊmiəˈpæθɪk/ /ˌhoʊmiəˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 同种疗法的；顺势疗法的 |  Relating to or involving the medical system of homeopathy, which treats disease by giving patients extremely small doses of natural substances that in larger doses would produce symptoms of the disease. |  She's taking a homoeopathic remedy for her allergies. 她正在服用顺势疗法药物治疗过敏。| Synonyms: homeopathic | Antonyms: allopathic, conventional | adjective


homoeopathy:/ˌhəʊmiˈɒpəθi/ /ˌhoʊmiˈɑːpəθi/ | n. | 同种疗法；顺势疗法 | A system of medicine based on the belief that a substance that causes certain symptoms in a healthy person can cure similar symptoms in someone who is sick. |  He believes in homeopathy and uses it to treat his family. 他相信顺势疗法，并用它来治疗他的家人。 | Synonyms: homeopathy | Antonyms: allopathy, conventional medicine | noun
