overhomogenizing: /ˌoʊvərˌhoʊməˈdʒɛnaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度均质化 | Making something too uniform or similar, eliminating variation or diversity. | The city council's policies are overhomogenizing the urban landscape, leading to a loss of unique local character. 城市议会的政策使得城市景观过度均质化，导致当地独特特征的丧失。|  The constant stream of globalized media is overhomogenizing cultures around the world.  持续的全球化媒体流正在使世界各地的文化过度均质化。| 近义词: standardizing, uniforming | 反义词: diversifying, varying | 词性: 动词 (present participle)


Etymology:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "homogenize" (from "homo-" meaning "same" + "-gen" meaning "kind" + "-ize" meaning "to make").
