have-a-hand-in:/hæv ə hænd ɪn/| idiom |参与；插手；对…有影响|To be involved or influential in something, especially in bringing something about.|例句：I suspect she had a hand in his dismissal. 我怀疑她插手了他的解雇。|例句：He had a hand in designing the new bridge.  他参与了新桥的设计。|近义词：be involved in, participate in, contribute to, influence|反义词：abstain from, stay out of|词性: idiom
