conveyances:/kənˈveɪənsɪz/
n.
运输工具；转让；传达
The act of conveying; transmission; communication.  Something that serves to convey; especially : a vehicle or means of transportation.  The legal process of transferring property from one owner to another.
The company provides conveyances for its employees.  该公司为其员工提供交通工具。
The lawyer handled the conveyances of the property. 律师处理了房产的转让。
近义词:transport, vehicle, transfer, transmission
反义词:reception
