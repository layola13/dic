delightous: /dɪˈlaɪtəs/ | adj. | 美味的，令人愉悦的 |  "Delightous" is not a standard English word.  It seems to be a blend of "delightful" and "delicious." While sometimes used informally, it's generally considered nonstandard and should be avoided in formal writing.  Use "delightful" for general pleasure and "delicious" for food. |  (Non-standard usage) "The cake was delightous."  蛋糕非常美味。 | 近义词：delicious, delightful, enjoyable, pleasant | 反义词：unpleasant, distasteful, disgusting | 词性: 形容词
