auntie:/ˈænti/ (US) /ˈɑːnti/ (UK)| n.|阿姨；姑妈；伯母；舅妈|A term of endearment for a woman who is not one's mother, often used for a close family friend or an older woman one respects. It can also refer to one's mother's sister, father's sister, or the wife of one's uncle.|例句：My auntie baked me a cake for my birthday. 我阿姨为我生日烤了一个蛋糕。

例句：She calls all the older women in the neighborhood "auntie." 她称呼邻里所有年长的女性为“阿姨”。

近义词：aunt, |反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun
