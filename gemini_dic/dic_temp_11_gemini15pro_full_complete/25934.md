burnout:/ˈbɜːrnaʊt/ (US) /ˈbɜːnaʊt/ (UK)| n.|精疲力竭；倦怠|A state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged or excessive stress.|She's suffering from burnout after working too many hours. 她因工作时间过长而身心俱疲。|近义词：exhaustion, fatigue, breakdown|反义词：energy, enthusiasm|词性:名词

burnout:/ˈbɜːrnaʊt/ (US) /ˈbɜːnaʊt/ (UK)| adj.|燃尽的；烧坏的|Burnt out or exhausted.|The burnout car was towed away. 烧毁的汽车被拖走了。|近义词：exhausted, spent, burnt-out|反义词：functional, working|词性:形容词
