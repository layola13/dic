nevermind:/ˌnevərˈmaɪnd/| int. |没关系；不要紧；别介意|Used to tell someone not to worry about something because it is not important.| "I forgot to bring your book." "Nevermind, I have another copy." “我忘了带你的书。” “没关系，我有另一本。” |Synonyms: it's okay, it doesn't matter, don't worry about it|Antonyms:  It's important, pay attention to it|interjection
