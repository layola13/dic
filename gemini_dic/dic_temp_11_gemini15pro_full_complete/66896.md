expedite:/ˈekspədaɪt/| v. | 加快；促进；迅速处理 | To make a process happen more quickly. |例句：The company took steps to expedite the delivery of goods. 公司采取措施加快了商品的交付。|例句：We need to expedite the approval process. 我们需要加快审批流程。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, quicken, speed up |反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct |词性:动词
词源：From Latin expedire "extricate, disengage, free from difficulties," from ex- "out" + ped- "foot," from pes (genitive pedis) "foot".  The original notion is of freeing one's feet from entanglements.
