obsessors:/əbˈsɛsərz/| n. |痴迷者，困扰者|People who are obsessed with something or someone.  Those who persistently preoccupy someone's thoughts.|例句：The paparazzi are often seen as obsessors of celebrities. 狗仔队经常被视为痴迷于名人的困扰者。

例句：She couldn't escape her obsessors, who constantly sent her messages. 她无法摆脱那些不断给她发信息的痴迷者。|近义词：fanatics, devotees, stalkers|反义词:critics, detractors|词性:noun
