abacination:/ˌæbæsɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
放弃，舍弃；计算的技巧
The act of abandoning, relinquishing, or renouncing. Skill in calculation.
He demonstrated his abacination by quickly solving the complex equation. 他通过快速解出复杂的方程式展示了他的计算技巧。
The monk's abacination of worldly possessions was complete.  这位僧侣完全放弃了世俗的财产。
近义词: renunciation, abandonment, relinquishment, abnegation, forsaking
反义词: acquisition, acceptance, embrace
词性: noun
