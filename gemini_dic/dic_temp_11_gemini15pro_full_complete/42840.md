coursework:/ˈkɔːrswɜːrk/ (US) /ˈkɔːswɜːk/ (UK)| n. |课程作业；功课| Academic work done during a course of study, typically assessed to count towards a final grade or mark. |例句：The coursework for this semester includes writing three essays and completing a final project. 这学期的课程作业包括写三篇论文和完成一个期末项目。

例句：She spent the weekend catching up on her coursework. 她周末都在赶她的课程作业。

近义词：assignments, homework, studies, schoolwork|反义词: leisure, recreation|词性: noun
