evictor:/ɪˈvɪktər/| n.|驱逐者，赶走的人|A person who evicts someone, especially a landlord or their agent.  The evictor gave the family only a week to leave the apartment.|例句：The evictor arrived with a court order. 驱逐者带着法院命令来了。

例句：The family resented the evictor for forcing them out of their home. 这家人怨恨驱逐者强迫他们离开家园。|近义词：landlord, owner, lessor|反义词:tenant, renter|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin evictus, past participle of evincere "to overcome, conquer, prevail," from ex- "out" + vincere "to conquer."  The noun form "evictor" is derived from the verb "evict."
