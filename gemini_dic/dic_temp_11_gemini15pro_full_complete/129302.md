noetic:/noʊˈɛtɪk/| adj.|直觉的；理智的；纯理智的|Relating to pure intellect or understanding, especially as distinct from sense perception or emotions.  Derived from Greek "nous" meaning "mind" or "intellect."|例句：Noetic sciences explore the nature of consciousness.  心灵科学探索意识的本质。

例句：She had a noetic understanding of the complex problem. 她对这个复杂的问题有一种直觉的理解。|近义词：intellectual, mental, cognitive, rational|反义词：emotional, sensual, instinctive|词性：adjective
