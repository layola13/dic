beadlike:/ˈbiːdlaɪk/| adj.|像珠子一样的，珠状的|Resembling beads in shape, size, or arrangement.  Small, round, and arranged in a line or cluster like a string of beads.|The dew hung on the spiderweb in beadlike droplets. 露珠像珠子一样挂在蜘蛛网上。|The child strung beadlike berries onto a piece of grass.  孩子把像珠子一样的浆果串在一根草上。|spherical, globular, pearl-like|amorphous, shapeless|adjective
