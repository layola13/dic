dextrousness:/dɛkˈstɛrəsnəs/
n.
灵巧，熟练
Skill and ease in using the hands or body; agility.
Her dextrousness on the piano is remarkable. 她在钢琴上的灵巧令人惊叹。
近义词: adroitness, deftness, skill, agility, proficiency
反义词: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptitude
词性: noun

dexterity:/dɛkˈstɛrɪti/
n.
灵巧，熟练
Skill and ease in using the hands or body; agility.
The surgeon's dexterity was evident in the delicate operation.  这位外科医生在精细的手术中展现出了他的灵巧。
近义词: adroitness, deftness, skill, agility, proficiency
反义词: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptitude
词性: noun

dexterous:/ˈdɛkstərəs/
adj.
灵巧的，熟练的
Showing or having skill, especially with the hands.
He's a dexterous woodcarver. 他是一位技艺精湛的木雕家。
近义词: adroit, deft, skillful, agile, proficient
反义词: clumsy, awkward, inept
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dexter* "right, right-hand," figuratively "favorable, propitious," from PIE *deks- "right; south." The connection with "right hand" likely arose because most people are right-handed.
