hyperconfidently:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnfɪdəntli/ (US) /ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnfɪd(ə)ntli/ (UK)| adv.|过度自信地；极度自信地|In a manner exhibiting excessive confidence, often to the point of arrogance or unrealistic self-assurance.|He hyperconfidently predicted his victory, despite all evidence to the contrary. 他过度自信地预测自己会获胜，尽管所有证据都表明相反。|Synonyms: overconfidently, cockily, arrogantly|Antonyms: hesitantly, timidly, diffidently|adverb

Etymology:
From hyper- + confident + -ly.  "Hyper-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "over, above, beyond," and "confident" comes from Latin "confidentem," meaning "trusting, bold."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
