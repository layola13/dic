glyoxime:/ɡlaɪˈɒksiːm/| n. |乙二肟|An organic compound, specifically a dioxime of glyoxal, often used in analytical chemistry for the detection and determination of nickel.|Nickel glyoxime is a bright red precipitate. 镍乙二肟是一种鲜红色的沉淀物。|近义词:dimethylglyoxime (DMG)|反义词:None|noun
