anthropophagite:/ˌænθrəˈpɒfədʒaɪt/ | n. |食人者，食人族|A person who eats human flesh; a cannibal.|例句：In some cultures, anthropophagites were feared and ostracized. 在某些文化中，食人者令人恐惧并被排斥。

例句：Ancient texts detail gruesome rituals practiced by anthropophagites. 古代文献详细记载了食人者进行的可怕仪式。|近义词：cannibal, man-eater|反义词:vegetarian, vegan|词性:noun
Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀνθρωποφάγος (anthrōpophagos, “man-eating”), from ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + φαγεῖν (phageîn, “to eat”).
