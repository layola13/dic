metempirics:/ˌmɛtɛmˈpɪrɪks/| n. |形而上学经验论；超验经验论|A branch of philosophy that explores the nature of reality and knowledge, combining metaphysical speculation with empirical observation.|例句：Metempirics attempts to bridge the gap between abstract philosophical concepts and concrete experience. 形而上学经验论试图弥合抽象哲学概念与具体经验之间的差距。

例句：Some philosophers argue that metempirics provides a more holistic understanding of the world. 一些哲学家认为，形而上学经验论提供了一种对世界更全面的理解。|近义词：metaphysics, epistemology, transcendentalism|反义词:empiricism, positivism|词性:noun
