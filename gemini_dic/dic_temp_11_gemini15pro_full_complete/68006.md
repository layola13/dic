factive:/ˈfæktɪv/| adj.|事实的；预设的；以事实为基础的|An adjective describing a verb or other predicate that presupposes the truth of its complement clause.|例句：The verb "know" is a factive verb, because if you say "I know that it is raining," you are presupposing that it is, in fact, raining. 你说“我知道正在下雨”，你预设了事实上正在下雨。“知道”是一个事实动词。

例句：Her claim that she was innocent was based on a factive premise. 她声称自己无罪是基于一个事实前提。|近义词：presuppositional, assertive|反义词:non-factive|词性:adjective
