bizarro:/bɪˈzɑːroʊ/ (US), /bɪˈzɑːrəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|奇异的，古怪的；怪人|Very strange or unusual.|例句：That's a bizarro story! 那真是个怪诞的故事！|例句：He's a real bizarro. 他真是个怪人。|近义词：weird, strange, odd, eccentric, bizarre|反义词：normal, ordinary, conventional|词性：形容词，名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *bizarro* "irascible," probably from Basque *bizkar* "back," thus "obstinate."  The current meaning developed in American slang, popularized by DC Comics' "Bizarro Superman."
