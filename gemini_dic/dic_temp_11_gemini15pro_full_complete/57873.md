dribbled:/ˈdrɪbld/| v. |运球，滴下，少量给予|moved (a ball) by repeated slight touches or taps, as in basketball or soccer; let fall in drops; dispensed little by little|He dribbled the basketball down the court. 他运球沿着球场前进。 The rain dribbled down the windowpane. 雨水顺着窗玻璃滴下来。 She dribbled a little olive oil onto the salad.  她在沙拉上滴了几滴橄榄油。|近义词：trickled, dripped, dispensed|反义词：poured, gushed|词性:动词
