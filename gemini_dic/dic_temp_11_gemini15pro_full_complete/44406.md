cross-legged:/ˌkrɒsˈlɛɡɪd/ (UK) /ˌkrɔːsˈlɛɡɪd/ (US)| adj.|盘腿的，交叉腿的|Sitting with legs crossed, typically at the ankles.|She sat cross-legged on the floor. 她盘腿坐在地板上。|He listened attentively, sitting cross-legged on the cushion. 他盘腿坐在垫子上，专心听着。|近义词：cross-legged, with legs crossed|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) straight-legged, with legs extended|词性: adjective
