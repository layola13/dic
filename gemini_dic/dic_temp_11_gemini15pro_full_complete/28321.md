caracara:/ˌkærəˈkɔːrə/| n.|卡拉卡拉鹰|Any of several long-legged, heavy-bodied birds of prey (family Falconidae) of the Americas, especially the crested caracara ( *Caracara cheriway*), having dark plumage, a bare face, and a habit of walking and running on the ground.|We spotted a caracara scavenging on the beach.|近义词:falcon, caracara|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
