fissirostral:/fɪsɪˈrɒstrəl/ | adj. | 深裂喙的；剪嘴的 | Having a deeply cleft beak, like swallows or nightjars.  Characterized by a beak that is deeply divided or split. | Example: The fissirostral bird caught an insect mid-flight. 这只深裂喙的鸟在飞行中抓住了一只昆虫。

Example:  Nightjars are fissirostral birds known for their nocturnal habits. 夜鹰是深裂喙的鸟类，以其夜行习性而闻名。 | 近义词: cleft-beaked | 反义词:  (No direct antonym; could consider "short-beaked" depending on context) | 词性: adjective
