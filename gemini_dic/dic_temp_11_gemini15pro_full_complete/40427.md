contrariness:/ˌkɑːntrɛrɪnəs/ | /ˌkɒntrɛrɪnəs/ | n. | 倔强; 反抗; 乖张 | A disposition to oppose and contradict; perverseness.  A state of being contrary; opposition. | Her contrariness made it difficult to work with her. 她的倔强使得很难和她一起工作。 | The child's contrariness was evident in his refusal to eat his vegetables.  这个孩子的倔强表现在他拒绝吃蔬菜。 | synonyms: perverseness, obstinacy,  willfulness, defiance, disobedience | antonyms: obedience, compliance, agreeableness, docility | noun
