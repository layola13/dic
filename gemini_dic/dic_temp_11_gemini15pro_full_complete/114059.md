magnetitic:/ˌmæɡnɪˈtɪtɪk/
adjective
磁铁的；磁性的
Of or relating to magnetite; containing magnetite.
The ore is highly magnetitic. 这种矿石磁性很强。

The magnetitic properties of the rock made it useful for navigation.  这块岩石的磁性使其可用于导航。
synonym: magnetic, ferrous
antonym: non-magnetic, diamagnetic
词性: adjective
