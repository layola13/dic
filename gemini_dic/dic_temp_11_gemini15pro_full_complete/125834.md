murre:/ˈmɜːr/ (US), /mʌr/ (UK)| n.|海鸠|A seabird of northern and Arctic waters, typically with a dark head and back and a white breast.  It lays its single egg on bare rock ledges.|Thousands of murres nest on the cliffs each year. 每年都有成千上万的海鸠在悬崖上筑巢。|Synonyms: guillemot, auk|Antonyms: (None specific, could consider land birds)|noun

murre:/ˈmɜːr/ (US), /mʌr/ (UK)| v.|低沉地发出咕噜声|To make a low, continuous, murmuring sound.|The murres murred softly to each other. 海鸠们互相轻声低语。|Synonyms: murmur, coo, croon|Antonyms: shout, yell|verb
