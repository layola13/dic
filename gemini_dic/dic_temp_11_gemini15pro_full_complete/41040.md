copernican:/ˌkoʊˈpɜːrnɪkən/ | adj. | 哥白尼的；日心说的 | Relating to Nicolaus Copernicus or his astronomical theory that the earth rotates daily on its axis and revolves yearly around the sun, which is at the center of the universe. |  例句：The Copernican theory revolutionized astronomy. 哥白尼的理论彻底改变了天文学。 | 近义词：heliocentric | 反义词：geocentric | 词性: adjective

copernican:/ˌkəˈpɜːnɪkən/ | n. | 哥白尼学说信奉者 | A believer in the Copernican theory. | 例句：He was a Copernican in his views of the universe. 他在宇宙观上是一位哥白尼学说的信奉者。| 近义词：heliocentrist | 反义词：geocentrist | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Nicolaus Copernicus (1473-1543), Polish astronomer + -an.
