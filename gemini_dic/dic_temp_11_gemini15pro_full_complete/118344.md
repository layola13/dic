meridionally:/ˌmɛrɪˈdiənəli/| adv.|南北地，经线方向地|In a north-south direction, along a meridian. |例句：The mountains run meridionally. 这些山脉南北走向。|例句：The wind blew meridionally across the plains. 风沿着经线方向吹过平原。|近义词：north-south, longitudinally|反义词：latitudinally, east-west|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *merīdiōnālis, from Latin merīdiānus (“of or belonging to midday, southern”), from merīdiēs (“midday, noon, south”).
