虚拟语气:
Wenn jeder mithelfen würde, ginge es schneller. If everyone would help, it would go faster.

被动语态:
Von jedem wird erwartet, dass er pünktlich ist.  Everyone is expected to be on time. (Literally: From each is expected that he is punctual).

否定句:

PHRASE->
(Phrases with "jeder" are not common in the way phrases with "what" are.  "Jeder" functions more like "each" or "every".)

(Example usages resembling phrases, demonstrating the distributive nature of "jeder")

Jeder für sich. Every man for himself.
Jeder auf seine Weise. Everyone in their own way.
Mit jedem Tag. With each day.


注意事项:
"Jeder" is always singular, even when referring to a plural group.  It emphasizes the individual members within that group.  It doesn't have a plural form.  "Alle" (all) is used when referring to the group as a whole.
