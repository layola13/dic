ointment:/ˈɔɪntmənt/ | n. | 药膏；软膏；油膏 | A smooth oily substance that is rubbed on the skin to heal it or make it softer. |  I applied some ointment to the burn. 我在烧伤处涂了一些药膏。

The doctor prescribed an ointment for her rash. 医生给她开了治疗皮疹的药膏。 | 近义词：cream, balm, salve | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps irritant if the ointment causes a negative reaction) | noun
