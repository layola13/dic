lambskinnet:/ˈlæmˌskɪnɪt/
n.
羔羊皮; 羔羊皮衣;
A soft, flexible leather made from the skin of a lamb.  A garment made from this leather.
例句：She wore a beautiful lambskinnet coat. 她穿着一件漂亮的羔羊皮外套。

例句：The lambskinnet gloves were incredibly soft. 这副羔羊皮手套柔软无比。

近义词：lambskin, sheepskin
反义词: (无直接的反义词，可以考虑使用材质对比，例如：synthetic leather 人造革)
词性: noun
