barrage:/bəˈrɑːʒ/ /bəˈrɑːʒ/| v. | 以密集炮火攻击；（用问题、批评等）猛攻某人 |To barrage someone is to bombard them with something, usually questions or criticisms. |He was barraged with questions from the reporters.  他被记者的问题连珠炮轰。 |The celebrity was barraged with criticism after the scandal.  丑闻之后，这位名人受到了猛烈的批评。|synonyms: bombard, pepper, inundate, overwhelm | antonyms: shield, protect | verb
