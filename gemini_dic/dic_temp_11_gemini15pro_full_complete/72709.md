focused:/ˈfoʊkəst/ (US) /ˈfəʊkəst/ (UK)| adj.|专注的，集中的|Giving a lot of attention to one particular thing|例句：She was focused on her work and didn't hear the phone. 她专注于工作，没有听到电话响。

例句：The company is focused on developing new products. 这家公司专注于开发新产品。


|近义词：concentrated, attentive, intent|反义词：distracted, unfocused, scattered|词性：形容词
