nonvisual:/ˌnɑːnˈvɪʒuəl/
adjective
非视觉的；非直观的
Not relating to or perceived by sight.
例句：The teacher used nonvisual teaching methods for the blind students. 老师对盲人学生使用了非视觉的教学方法。

例句：Nonvisual learners may struggle with traditional textbook-based learning. 非视觉学习者可能会在传统的基于教科书的学习中感到吃力。

synonym:auditory, tactile, olfactory, gustatory, kinesthetic
antonym:visual, visible

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + visual.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "visual" relates to sight.
