observer:/əbˈzɜːrvər/ (US) /əbˈzɜːvə/ (UK)| n.|观察者；观察员；遵守者|A person who watches or notices something; someone who is a specialist in a particular subject and who closely follows its development; someone who obeys a law, rule, or custom.|The United Nations sent observers to monitor the elections. 联合国派遣观察员监督选举。|synonyms: spectator, onlooker, viewer, witness, monitor, superintendent|antonyms: participant, doer, actor|noun

observer:/əbˈzɜːrvər/ (US) /əbˈzɜːvə/ (UK)| n.|观察家；评论员|Someone who watches or comments on something.|Political observers predict a close election. 政治观察家预测这是一场势均力敌的选举。|synonyms: analyst, commentator, pundit|antonyms: participant, doer, actor|noun
