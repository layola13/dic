barangaria:/ˌbærənˈɡɑːriə/
n.
ostentatious display of power, arrogance, extravagant behavior
An overbearing display of superiority and contempt for those considered inferior.  It often involves excessive spending and a disregard for the needs of others.
The king's barangaria was evident in his lavish feasts and disregard for the suffering of his people. 国王的骄奢淫逸体现在他奢华的宴会和对人民苦难的漠视上。
Synonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, ostentation, extravagance, pomp
Antonyms: humility, modesty, simplicity, frugality
noun


Etymology:
From Old French *baranguier, from Occitan baranharia "boasting, bragging," from baranh "boastful talk, bragging."
