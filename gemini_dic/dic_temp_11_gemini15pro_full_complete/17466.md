beardedness:/ˈbɪrdɪdnəs/
n.
胡须茂盛,留胡子的状态
The state or quality of having a beard.
His beardedness made him look older than he was. 他的胡须让他看起来比实际年龄大。
His  bushy beardedness gave him a distinguished air. 他浓密的胡须让他显得气宇轩昂。
hirsutism, beard growth
clean-shavenness, lack of a beard
noun
