archprelate: /ˌɑːrtʃˈprɛlɪt/ | n. |大主教；总主教|A chief prelate; a bishop of the highest rank. | The archprelate presided over the important religious ceremony. 总主教主持了重要的宗教仪式。| The archprelate delivered a moving sermon. 总主教发表了一篇感人的布道。 |Synonyms: archbishop, primate, metropolitan | Antonyms: layman, parishioner | noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *archeprelat, from Old French archeprelat, from Medieval Latin archipraeletus, from archi- + praeletus (“prelate”).
