cariboo:/ˈkærɪbuː/| n.|驯鹿|A large North American deer (Rangifer tarandus), found in Canada, Alaska, and northern parts of some US states. It has large antlers in both sexes. Also called reindeer (especially when domesticated).|例句：The caribou migrate south in the winter. 驯鹿在冬天向南迁徙。

例句：The herd of caribou crossed the frozen river.  一群驯鹿穿过了冰冻的河流。


|近义词：reindeer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
