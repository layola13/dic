not lift a finger:/nɑːt lɪft ə ˈfɪŋɡər/ | idiom | 不动一根手指；袖手旁观 | To do nothing to help; to remain idle and not assist with a task. | Example: He didn't lift a finger to help with the move, just sat there watching us struggle. 他搬家时一点忙也不帮，就坐在那儿看着我们忙活。 | Example:  She expected me to do all the cleaning myself, she wouldn't lift a finger! 她指望我自己做所有的清洁工作，她根本就不帮忙！| Synonym: do nothing, sit idle, be inactive, not contribute | Antonym: help, assist, pitch in, lend a hand | idiom
