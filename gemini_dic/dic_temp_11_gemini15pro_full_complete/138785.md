numinousness:/ˌnuːmɪˈnɒsnəs/| n.|神圣感；超自然感；神秘感|A sense of the presence of something greater than ourselves, often associated with religious or spiritual awe.  The feeling of being in the presence of something holy or sacred.|The numinousness of the ancient temple filled her with awe. 古老寺庙的神圣感让她充满敬畏。|synonyms: holiness, sacredness, divinity, spirituality, transcendence|antonyms: profanity, secularity, worldliness|noun
