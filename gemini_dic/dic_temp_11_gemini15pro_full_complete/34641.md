clenbuterol:/klɛnˈbjuːtərɒl/ (UK) /klɛnˈbjutərɔːl/ (US)| n. |克伦特罗|A bronchodilator and decongestant with performance-enhancing effects. It is used illegally by some athletes to increase muscle mass and reduce body fat.| Athletes caught using clenbuterol face suspension from competition. 运动员如果被发现使用克伦特罗，将面临禁赛。|Synonyms: Spiropent, Ventipulmin|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a specific drug.  One might consider drugs that *decrease* muscle mass or *increase* body fat, but these wouldn't be true opposites.)|noun
