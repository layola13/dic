despot:/ˈdespɒt/ (UK), /ˈdespɑːt/ (US)| n.|暴君，专制君主|A ruler or other person who holds absolute power, typically one who exercises it in a cruel or oppressive way.|The despot ruled his country with an iron fist.  这个暴君用铁腕统治他的国家。|Synonyms: tyrant, dictator, autocrat, oppressor|Antonyms: democrat, liberator|Noun
