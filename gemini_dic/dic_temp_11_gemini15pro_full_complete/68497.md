famously:/ˈfeɪməsli/| adv.|著名地，出名地|In a way that is widely known. |例句：The restaurant is famously expensive. 这家餐厅以昂贵闻名。|近义词：notedly, notoriously, prominently|反义词: obscurely, anonymously|词性:副词
词源: From famous + -ly.  "famous" comes from the Latin "famosus," meaning "renowned, celebrated."
