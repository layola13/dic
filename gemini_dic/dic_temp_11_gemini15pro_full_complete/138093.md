normative:/ˈnɔːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 规范的；标准的；指示的 | Relating to or deriving from a standard or norm, especially of behavior.  Describing what should be the case rather than what actually is. |  The teacher explained the normative rules of grammar. 老师讲解了规范的语法规则。| Synonyms: standard, regular, typical, prescriptive | Antonyms: abnormal, atypical, deviant, irregular | adjective

normative:/ˈnɔːrmətɪv/ | n. | 规范；标准 | A standard or norm, especially of behavior.  (Less common usage) | Company policies set the normative for employee conduct. 公司政策为员工行为设定了规范。(Less common) | Synonyms: standard, benchmark, criterion | Antonyms: anomaly, deviation, exception | noun
