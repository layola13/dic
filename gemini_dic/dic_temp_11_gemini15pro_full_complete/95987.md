incurability:/ˌɪnkjʊərəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可治愈，无法治愈|The incurability of the disease caused great despair.  这种疾病的不可治愈性令人非常绝望。|例句：The doctor confirmed the incurability of his condition. 医生确认了他的病情无法治愈。|近义词：irremediability, hopelessness, terminality|反义词: curability, remediability|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *incurabilite, from Late Latin incurabilitās (“incurableness”), from Latin incūrābilis (“incurable”). Equivalent to incurable +‎ -ity.
