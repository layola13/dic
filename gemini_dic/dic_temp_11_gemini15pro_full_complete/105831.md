kitbags: /ˈkɪtbæɡz/ | n. | 行囊，工具袋 | A kitbag is a large, strong bag used to carry clothes, equipment, and other personal belongings, typically by soldiers, travelers, or athletes. | The soldier packed his kitbag with essentials for the mission. 士兵把任务必需品装进了他的行囊。|  He carried his cricket kitbag to the nets for practice. 他提着板球工具袋去网练习。 | synonyms: duffel bag, holdall, rucksack | antonyms: N/A | noun
