listenability:/ˌlɪsənəˈbɪləti/| n.|可听性；易懂性|The listenability of a podcast is crucial for its success.  播客的可听性对其成功至关重要。|例句：The speaker's clear articulation enhanced the listenability of the lecture. 演讲者清晰的表达提高了讲座的可听性。

例句：The podcast's high listenability is due to its engaging content and clear audio. 该播客的高可听性归功于其引人入胜的内容和清晰的音频。


近义词：clarity, comprehensibility, intelligibility|反义词:incomprehensibility, complexity|词性:noun
