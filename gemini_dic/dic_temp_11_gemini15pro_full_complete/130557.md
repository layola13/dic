nonacquisitively:/ˌnɒnˌækwɪˈzɪtɪvli/
adverb
非获取性地；不热衷于获取地
In a manner that is not focused on acquiring possessions or material wealth; without the desire to possess or acquire.
He lived a simple, nonacquisitive life, content with the basic necessities.  他过着简朴的、非获取性的生活，满足于基本必需品。
She traveled nonacquisitively, preferring experiences to souvenirs. 她旅行时不热衷于获取物品，更喜欢体验而不是纪念品。
近义词: non-materialistically, simply, modestly, unambitiously
反义词: acquisitively, greedily, materialistically
词性: adverb
词源: non- (not) + acquisitive (given to acquiring) + -ly (adverb suffix)
