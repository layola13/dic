nonmarine:/nɒn.məˈriːn/ /nɑːn.məˈriːn/| adj.|非海洋的，陆生的|Not of or relating to the sea; terrestrial.|例句：Nonmarine rocks are formed on land.  非海洋岩石是在陆地上形成的。|例句：The study focused on nonmarine fossils. 这项研究集中于非海洋化石。|近义词：terrestrial, land, earthly|反义词：marine, oceanic, aquatic|词性：形容词

Etymology->
non-  + marine
non-:  prefix meaning "not" (from Latin non "not").
marine: from Old French marin "of the sea," from Latin marinus "of the sea," from mare "sea."
