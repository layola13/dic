bazillion:/bəˈzɪljən/
|n.|无数，极大量|An extremely large, indeterminate number.  A humorous exaggeration. |例句：I've told you a bazillion times to clean your room! 我已经告诉你无数次要打扫你的房间了！

|例句：There were a bazillion people at the concert last night. 昨晚的音乐会人山人海。|近义词：zillion, gazillion, jillion, umpteen, countless, innumerable|反义词:few, little|词性:名词
