haematoplastic:/ˌhiːmətəˈplæstɪk/| adj.|造血的，促进血液形成的|Relating to or involved in the formation of blood cells.  This term describes substances or processes that contribute to the production of blood components.|Haematoplastic growth factors are essential for blood cell production. 造血生长因子对血细胞生成至关重要。| synonyms: hematopoietic, blood-forming | antonyms:  hemostatic (stops bleeding, opposite of forming blood) | adjective
