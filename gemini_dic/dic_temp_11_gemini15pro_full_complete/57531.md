dragun:/ˈdræɡən/| n.|龙;  喷水灭火车;  凶恶的人;  严厉的女人|A mythical creature, typically depicted as a large, serpentine reptile with wings, claws, and the ability to breathe fire. Also, a fierce or intimidating person, especially a woman. |例句：The knight slew the dragon. 骑士杀死了那条龙。

例句：She's a real dragon when she's angry. 她生气的时候像条母老虎。 |近义词：monster, beast, wyvern, hydra (mythical creatures); shrew, harpy (for a fierce woman)|反义词: (No direct opposite for a mythical creature.  For a person, perhaps) angel, lamb|词性: noun
