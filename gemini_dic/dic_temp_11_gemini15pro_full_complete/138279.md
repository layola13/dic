notarised:/ˈnəʊtəraɪzd/ | adj. | 公证的；经公证的 |  Officially certified by a notary public. | Example: The contract was notarised before being submitted to the court.  合同在提交法院之前经过了公证。| Example:  She needed a notarised copy of her birth certificate. 她需要一份经公证的出生证明副本。 | Synonyms: certified, authenticated, attested | Antonyms: uncertified, unauthenticated | word property: adjective

notarise:/ˈnəʊtəraɪz/ | v. | 公证；证明 | To certify or attest to (a document) formally by a notary public. | Example:  I need to have this document notarised. 我需要公证这份文件。| Example: The lawyer notarised the signature on the will. 律师公证了遗嘱上的签名。 | Synonyms: certify, authenticate, attest | Antonyms:  invalidate | word property: verb

notarisation:/ˌnəʊtəraɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 公证；认证 | The act of notarising a document.  | Example: The notarisation of the document cost $25.  这份文件的公证费用为25美元。 | Example:  Proof of notarisation is required for this application. 此申请需要公证证明。 | Synonyms: certification, authentication, attestation | Antonyms: invalidation | word property: noun
