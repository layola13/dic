cultural:/ˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|文化的，与文化有关的|Relating to the ideas, customs, and social behaviour of a society or a particular group of people.|例句：The city has a rich cultural heritage. 这座城市有着丰富的文化遗产。|近义词：artistic, civilizing|反义词：natural, uncultured|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *culturalis*, from Latin cultura (“cultivation, agriculture; care, culture”), from cultus (“tended, cultivated, tilled; cherished, honored, adored”), past participle of colō (“I till, cultivate, inhabit; cherish, care for, respect”).
