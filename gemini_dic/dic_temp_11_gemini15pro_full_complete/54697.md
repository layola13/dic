disorganization's:/dɪˌsɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |缺乏组织，混乱状态|The disorganization's impact on the project was significant.  这个项目的混乱状态造成了重大影响。|近义词：disorder, chaos, mess, confusion|反义词：organization, order, tidiness, system|noun

Etymology:
From dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + organization + -'s (possessive suffix).  "Organization" comes from Old French "organizacion" and ultimately from Latin "organizare" meaning "to arrange, organize."
