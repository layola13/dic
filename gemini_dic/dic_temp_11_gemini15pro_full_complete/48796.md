deflexed:/dɪˈflɛkst/| adj.|向下弯曲的，偏斜的|Bent or turned downwards or aside.|The deflexed leaves of the plant drooped towards the ground. 这种植物向下弯曲的叶子垂向地面。|The bird's wings were slightly deflexed in flight. 这只鸟的翅膀在飞行中稍微向下弯曲。|近义词：bent, curved, declined, downturned|反义词：erect, upright, straight|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin deflectere, from de- + flectere "to bend."
