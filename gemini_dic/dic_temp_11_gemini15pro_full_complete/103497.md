jockeyship:/ˈdʒɒkɪʃɪp/ | n. |骑术；骑师的技能；驾驶技术；操控能力|The skill in riding or handling something, especially a horse or vehicle, with expertise.  It can also refer to maneuvering for advantage or a position. |He demonstrated impressive jockeyship in the Formula One race. 他在一级方程式赛车中展现了令人印象深刻的驾驶技术。|Synonyms: skill, expertise, mastery, proficiency, adroitness, dexterity |Antonyms: incompetence, clumsiness, ineptitude|noun
