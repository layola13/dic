crankly:/ˈkræŋkli/| adj.|暴躁地；古怪地|Irritable; ill-tempered; cranky.|例句：He answered crankily, clearly not in the mood to talk. 他暴躁地回答，显然没有心情说话。|例句：The old woman gave a crankly laugh. 老妇人古怪地笑了笑。|近义词：cranky, irritable, grumpy, crotchety|反义词：good-natured, pleasant, cheerful|词性:形容词

crankly:/ˈkræŋkli/| adv.|暴躁地；古怪地|In a cranky or irritable manner.|例句： "Leave me alone," he said crankily. “让我一个人待着，”他暴躁地说。|例句：The door creaked crankily as she pushed it open. 她推开门时，门发出古怪的吱吱声。|近义词：irritably, grumpily, peevishly|反义词：pleasantly, cheerfully|词性:副词
