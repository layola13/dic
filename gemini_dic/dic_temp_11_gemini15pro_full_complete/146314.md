overfeeds:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːdz/| v. |过量喂食，喂得太多|to feed (someone or something) more than is necessary or desirable|例句：She overfeeds her cat, which is why it's overweight. 她给她的猫喂得太多，这就是它超重的原因。|近义词：overstuff, gorge|反义词: starve, underfeed|词性:动词

overfeeds:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːdz/| n. |过量喂食（名词罕用）|The act of overfeeding.|例句：The overfeeds led to the fish's death. 过量喂食导致了鱼的死亡。|近义词：overstuffing, gorging|反义词: starvation, underfeeding|词性:名词
