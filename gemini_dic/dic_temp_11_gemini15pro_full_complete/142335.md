orthognathic: /ˌɔːrθəɡˈnæθɪk/
adjective
正颌的，颌骨正常的
Relating to or having a normal jaw structure, where the teeth and jaws are properly aligned.
例句：Orthognathic surgery can correct jaw misalignment. 正颌外科手术可以矫正颌骨错位。

例句：She had orthognathic surgery to improve her bite and facial profile. 她做了正颌外科手术来改善咬合和面部轮廓。

近义词：eugnathic
反义词：retrognathic, prognathic
词性：adjective
