例句：We saw a group of hot-tubbers laughing and chatting in the backyard. 我们看到一群泡热水澡的人在后院里笑着聊天。|近义词：spa-goers, bathers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) non-bathers|词性:Noun
