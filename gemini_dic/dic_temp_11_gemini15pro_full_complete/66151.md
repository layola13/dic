例句：After hours of excerebration, she finally found a solution to the complex problem.  经过数小时的彻底思考，她终于找到了解决这个复杂问题的方法。|近义词：evisceration, disembowelment (for the literal meaning), contemplation, deliberation (for the figurative meaning)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "implantation" for the literal meaning, "rashness" for the figurative meaning)|Noun
