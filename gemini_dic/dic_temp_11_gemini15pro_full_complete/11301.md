ardently:/ˈɑːrdəntli/
adverb
热烈地，热情地
With great passion and enthusiasm; fervently.
She ardently defended her beliefs. 她热烈地捍卫自己的信仰。
He ardently pursued his goals. 他热情地追求自己的目标。
Synonyms: passionately, fervently, eagerly, zealously, enthusiastically
Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently, coldly, halfheartedly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ardentli, derived from Old French ardamment, from ardent (“burning, passionate”), from Latin ardens, present participle of ardēre (“to burn”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂es- (“to burn, glow”).
