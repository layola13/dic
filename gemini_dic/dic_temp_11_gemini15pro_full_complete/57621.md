draughthouse:/ˈdrɑːfthaʊs/ | n. |屠宰场；厕所；透风的地方|A building where animals are slaughtered.  A place that is drafty or poorly insulated. A crude term for a toilet. |The old draughthouse on the edge of town was finally demolished. 城镇边缘的老屠宰场终于被拆除了。

The cabin was a draughthouse in the winter.  这间小屋在冬天透风得很厉害。|近义词：abattoir, slaughterhouse, restroom, toilet, lavatory |反义词: airtight (for drafty sense) |词性: 名词
