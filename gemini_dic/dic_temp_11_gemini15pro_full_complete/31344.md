characteristical: /ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪkəl/
| adj. | 特有的，典型的 | Showing the qualities that are typical of a particular person, thing, or group. | This behavior is characteristical of adolescents. 这种行为是青少年特有的。| 近义词: typical, characteristic, representative | 反义词: atypical, unusual, uncharacteristic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the noun "characteristic" + suffix "-al" meaning "relating to".  The noun "characteristic" derives from Late Latin *characteristicus*, from Greek kharaktēristikos, from kharaktērizein "to characterize," from kharaktēr "engraving tool, distinguishing mark, character."
