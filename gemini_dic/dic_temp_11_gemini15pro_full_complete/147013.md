overlap:/ˌəʊvəˈlæp/| v. n. |重叠，交叠；部分一致，部分相同| to extend over and cover a part of something else; or to have elements in common.| The shingles on the roof overlap each other. 屋顶上的瓦片互相重叠。| synonyms: coincide, intersect, | antonyms: diverge, separate | verb

overlap:/ˈəʊvəlæp/| n. |重叠，交叠；重叠部分；共同点| the amount by which two things or activities cover the same area or time or have features in common.| There is considerable overlap between the two subjects. 这两个学科之间有相当多的重叠部分。| synonyms: intersection, convergence, common ground | antonyms: divergence, separation | noun
