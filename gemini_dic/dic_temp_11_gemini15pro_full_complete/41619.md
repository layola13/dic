corrals:/kəˈrɑːlz/
n.|畜栏，围栏|An enclosure for livestock, especially cattle or horses.|The cowboys herded the cattle into the corrals.|近义词：pens, enclosures, stockyards|反义词: open field
词性: 名词

corrals:/kəˈrɑːlz/
v.|把…关进畜栏，围住|To enclose or confine in a corral.|They corralled the sheep for shearing.|近义词：pen, enclose, round up|反义词: release, free
词性: 动词

词源: From Spanish *corral*, ultimately from Latin *currāre* "to run".
