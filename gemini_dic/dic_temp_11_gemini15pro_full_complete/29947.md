caved:/keɪvd/
verb|塌陷，坍塌；屈服，屈从|Collapsed inward, yielded or submitted (often followed by "in").|The roof of the old mine caved in. 老矿的屋顶塌陷了。|yield, collapse, buckle, give way|resist, withstand|v.
caved:/keɪvd/
adjective|凹陷的，坍塌的|Having a concave or hollowed-out shape.|The accident left a caved-in section of the road. 事故导致一段道路凹陷。|concave, hollowed, sunken|convex, protruding|adj.
