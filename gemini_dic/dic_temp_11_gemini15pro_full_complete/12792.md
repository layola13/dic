It takes time to assimilate all the information from a lecture.  需要时间来吸收讲座中的所有信息。 |  integrate, absorb, incorporate, digest | differentiate, separate, distinguish | verb

assimilate: /əˈsɪməleɪt/ | n. | 被同化的事物 | Something that has been absorbed or integrated.  This usage is less common than the verb form. | The community welcomed the assimilate from other countries.  社区欢迎来自其他国家的被同化者。 | absorption, integration | differentiation, separation | noun
