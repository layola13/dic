bloatedness:/ˈbloʊtɪdnəs/| n.|腹胀，肿胀|The feeling of being uncomfortably full, especially in the stomach or abdomen.  A state of being swollen with fluid or gas.|After Thanksgiving dinner, I experienced a feeling of bloatedness.  感恩节晚餐后，我感到腹胀。|近义词：swelling, distension, inflation|反义词:flatness, emptiness|名词
