alters:/ˈɔːltərz/ (US) /ˈɒltəz/ (UK)| v. |改变，更改|To change something, typically slightly, or to cause something to change.|例句：She altered the dress to fit her better. 她修改了连衣裙以更好地适合自己。

例句：The city's landscape has been drastically altered by new construction. 新的建筑极大地改变了这座城市的景观。

近义词：change, modify, adjust, adapt, amend

反义词：preserve, maintain, keep, retain

词性：动词
