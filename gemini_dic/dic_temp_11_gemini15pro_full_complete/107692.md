n.
瘦弱；细长；虚弱
The state of being lank or thin, often implying weakness or emaciation.
His lanknesses was apparent after his long illness.  他久病之后，身体明显瘦弱。
The lanknesses of the trees made the forest look eerie.  树木的细长让森林看起来很怪异。
synonyms: thinness, slenderness, leanness, emaciation, gauntness
antonyms: stoutness, plumpness, robustness, chubbiness
