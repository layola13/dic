conceded:/kənˈsiːdɪd/
verb
承认，让步
admitted something is true, often unwillingly.
例句：He conceded that he had made a mistake. 他承认他犯了一个错误。

例句：The government conceded defeat. 政府承认失败。

近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, allow
反义词：deny, dispute, refuse
词性：v.


conceded:/kənˈsiːdɪd/
adjective
被承认的，被让步的
something that has been granted or yielded.  Usually used in a passive construction.
例句：The goal was conceded to be valid.  这个进球被认为是有效的。

例句：The point is conceded. 这点是被承认的。

近义词：acknowledged, admitted, granted
反义词：denied, disputed, contested
词性：adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "concedere" (to yield, grant, allow), from "con-" (together, with) + "cedere" (to go, yield).
