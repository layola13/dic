nonreproductively:/ˌnɑːnˌriːprəˈdʌktɪvli/
adverb|非繁殖地，非生殖地|in a way that does not involve reproduction; without reproducing.|Some organisms exist nonreproductively for a period of their life cycle.|近义词: asexually, vegetatively|反义词: reproductively|词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
reproductive (adjective): from Late Latin *reproducere "to lead back, bring forth again," from re- "back, again" + producere "to bring forth, produce"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
