outshine:/ˌaʊtˈʃaɪn/
verb
胜过，比...更出色
To be much more impressive, successful, or skilled than someone or something else.
例句：He easily outshone his rivals in the competition. 他在比赛中轻松胜过对手。

例句：Her performance outshone all the others. 她的表演比其他所有人都出色。

近义词：surpass, eclipse, excel, outdo, outstrip
反义词：pale in comparison, be inferior to
词性：verb
