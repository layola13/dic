barbadian:/bɑːrˈbeɪdiən/
noun|巴巴多斯人；巴巴多斯的|A person from Barbados or relating to Barbados.|例句：Many Barbadians work in the tourism industry. 许多巴巴多斯人在旅游业工作。|近义词：Bajan|反义词:|n.

barbadian:/bɑːrˈbeɪdiən/
adjective|巴巴多斯的|Relating to Barbados.|例句：Barbadian rum is world-famous. 巴巴多斯朗姆酒世界闻名。|近义词:|反义词:|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Barbados + -ian.  Barbados is believed to be derived from the Portuguese *os barbudos* "the bearded ones," referring either to the fig trees with aerial roots or to the indigenous peoples.
