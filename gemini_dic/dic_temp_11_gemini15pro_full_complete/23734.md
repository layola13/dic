brattice: /ˈbrætɪs/ (US), /ˈbrætɪs/ (UK) | n. v. | 隔板，隔墙；用隔板隔开 | A partition, especially one in a mine, to control ventilation or prevent the spread of fire. |  The miners erected a brattice to direct the airflow. 矿工们竖起隔板来引导气流。 | They bratticed the tunnel to prevent the spread of smoke. 他们用隔板隔开隧道以防止烟雾蔓延。 | synonyms: partition, barrier, bulkhead, screen | antonyms: opening, gap | noun, verb
