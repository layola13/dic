antidepressant:/ˌæntidiˈprɛsənt/ | n. adj. |抗抑郁药，抗抑郁的|A medication used to treat depression by affecting neurotransmitters in the brain. It can also be used as an adjective to describe something that alleviates depression.|She was prescribed an antidepressant to help manage her symptoms. 她被开了抗抑郁药来帮助控制她的症状。| The therapist recommended some antidepressant techniques, like exercise and meditation.  治疗师推荐了一些抗抑郁技巧，比如锻炼和冥想。|Synonyms: mood elevator, thymoleptic|Antonyms: depressant|Noun, Adjective
