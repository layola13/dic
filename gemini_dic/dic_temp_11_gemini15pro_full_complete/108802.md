leatherhead:/ˈleðərˌhed/| n.|皮革头，傻瓜，笨蛋|A person regarded as stupid or thick-headed.  Originally, a derogatory term for someone from Leatherhead, a town in Surrey, England.|He acted like such a leatherhead when he locked his keys in the car. 他把钥匙锁在车里，真是个笨蛋。|Synonyms: idiot, fool, dolt, dunce, nitwit, blockhead, dimwit, simpleton|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, mastermind|Noun
