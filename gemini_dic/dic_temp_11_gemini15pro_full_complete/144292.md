outspied:/ˌaʊtˈspaɪd/
verb
胜过…的侦察；在侦察方面超过
To surpass in spying; to outdo in espionage.
The CIA outspied the KGB during the Cold War. 中央情报局在冷战期间在侦察方面胜过了克格勃。
Synonyms: outmaneuvered, outsmarted, surpassed
Antonyms: outwitted, defeated
