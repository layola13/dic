despotism:/ˈdɛspətɪzəm/ | n. | 专制，暴政 | The exercise of absolute power, especially in a cruel and oppressive way. |  The country suffered under his cruel despotism.  这个国家在他的残酷专制下饱受苦难。 | synonyms: tyranny, dictatorship, autocracy, absolutism | antonyms: democracy, freedom, liberty | noun

despotic: /dɪˈspɒtɪk/ | adj. | 专制的，暴虐的 | Of or relating to a despot; tyrannical. | The king's despotic rule led to widespread unrest. 国王的专制统治导致了 widespread 的动荡。| synonyms: tyrannical, dictatorial, autocratic, authoritarian | antonyms: democratic, liberal, egalitarian | adjective
