exsiccate: /ˈɛksɪkeɪt/ | v. | 使干燥，使脱水；压制（植物标本）| To dry completely; to preserve (a plant specimen) by drying. |  The botanist exsiccated the rare flower for his collection.  植物学家将这朵珍稀的花干燥后纳入他的收藏。| 近义词: dehydrate, desiccate, dry | 反义词: hydrate, moisten | 词性: 动词
Etymology: From Latin *exsiccātus*, past participle of *exsiccāre* "to dry up," from *ex-* "out" + *siccāre* "to dry," from *siccus* "dry."
