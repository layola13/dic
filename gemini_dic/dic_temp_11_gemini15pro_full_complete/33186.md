chromolithographic: /ˌkroʊməlɪˈθəɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 彩色石印的 | Relating to or produced by chromolithography, a method of printing in color from several stones. | The antique book contained beautiful chromolithographic illustrations. 这本古董书包含美丽的彩色石印插图。| Near synonyms: color-printed, multicolored, polychromatic | Antonym: monochrome | adjective

chromolithography: /ˌkroʊməlɪˈθɑːɡrəfi/ | n. | 彩色石印术 | The process of producing multi-color prints by using a series of lithographic stones, each for a different color.  | Chromolithography was a popular printing technique in the 19th century. 彩色石印术是19世纪流行的印刷技术。| Near synonyms: color printing, lithography | Antonym: monochrome printing | noun
