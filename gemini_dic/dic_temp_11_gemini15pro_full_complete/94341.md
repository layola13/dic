impingement:/ɪmˈpɪndʒmənt/ | n. | 碰撞，冲击，侵犯， impingement syndrome(撞击综合征) |The impingement of the bone on the nerve caused considerable pain. 骨头压迫神经引起剧烈疼痛。| Synonyms: impact, collision, encroachment, infringement | Antonyms: avoidance, retreat | noun

impinge:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ/ | v. | 撞击，冲击，侵犯 | The noise of the traffic impinged on my concentration. 交通噪音干扰了我的注意力。| Synonyms: encroach, infringe, intrude, impose | Antonyms: respect, uphold | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Middle English *empingen, from Old French empingier, from en- + pingier (“to prick, sting”).  The noun "impingement" derives from the verb "impinge".
