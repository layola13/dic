nonperseverances:/ˌnɒnpɜːrsɪˈvɪərənsɪz/
n.
缺乏毅力，不能坚持
lack of perseverance; inability to continue working on a task or project until it is completed
His lack of nonperseverances led to the project's failure. 他缺乏毅力导致了项目的失败。
The team's nonperseverances resulted in a loss. 团队的缺乏毅力导致了失败。
Synonyms: instability, inconsistency, vacillation, irresolution, fickleness
Antonyms: perseverance, persistence, tenacity, determination, steadfastness
noun
