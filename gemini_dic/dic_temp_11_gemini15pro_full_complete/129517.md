non-attendance:| /ˌnɒn əˈtɛndəns/ /ˌnɑːn əˈtɛndəns/| n. |未出席；缺席|The state of not attending something; absence. It's formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "attendance".  The word "attendance" comes from the Old French "atendance," from "atendre" meaning "to wait for," ultimately from Latin "attendere" meaning "to stretch toward, give heed to."|His non-attendance at the meeting was noted. 他在会议上的缺席被注意到了。|Her continued non-attendance at school is worrying. 她持续旷课令人担忧。|近义词：absence, absenteeism|反义词：attendance, presence|词性:名词
