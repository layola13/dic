annular:/ˈæn.jʊ.lər/ | adj. | 环形的，环状的 | Relating to or shaped like a ring; forming a ring. | The annular eclipse created a stunning ring of fire around the moon. 日环食在月亮周围形成了一个令人惊叹的火环。 | The planet Saturn has beautiful annular rings. 土星拥有美丽的环状环。| synonyms: ring-shaped, circular, ringlike | antonyms: linear, straight | adjective
