multi-party:/ˌmʌltiˈpɑːrti/ | adj.| 多方的，多党制的 | involving several or many different parties or groups |  The multi-party negotiations were complex and challenging. 多方谈判复杂而具有挑战性。|  The agreement was reached after lengthy multi-party discussions.  该协议是经过长时间的多方讨论后达成的。 |  multilateral, multinational | single-party, bilateral | adjective

multi-party:/ˌmʌltiˈpɑːrti/ | n.| 多党制 | a system of government in which several political parties compete for power | The country transitioned to a multi-party system after years of one-party rule.  该国在经历了多年的“一党制”之后过渡到了多党制。 | The multi-party system encourages a wider range of political views. 多党制鼓励更广泛的政治观点。 | coalition government | dictatorship, one-party system | noun
