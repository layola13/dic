diagnosing:/ˈdaɪəɡnoʊzɪŋ/| v.|诊断，判断|The doctor is diagnosing the patient's illness. 医生正在诊断病人的病情。|例句：They are diagnosing the cause of the engine failure. 他们正在诊断引擎故障的原因。

例句：The mechanic is diagnosing the problem with my car.  机修工正在诊断我的车的问题。|近义词：analyzing, examining, identifying, investigating|反义词: overlooking, ignoring|词性: 动词(present participle of diagnose)


Etymology: From Late Latin *diagnōsis*, from Ancient Greek διάγνωσις (diágnōsis, “distinction, discrimination, decision”), from διαγιγνώσκειν (diagignṓskein, “to distinguish, discern, decide”), from διά (diá, “through”) + γιγνώσκειν (gignṓskein, “to learn, to know”).
