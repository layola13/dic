antistyle:/ˈæntiˌstaɪl/
n.
反风格；刻意避免风格
A deliberate avoidance of or reaction against a prevailing style, especially in the arts.
Her antistyle of dressing made her stand out from the crowd. 她刻意反潮流的穿着让她在人群中脱颖而出。
synonyms: unconventional style, nonconformity, rejection of style
antonyms: style, conformity, traditional style
noun
