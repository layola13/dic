ochrous:/ˈoʊkrəs/| adj.|赭色的，黄褐色的|Of a pale brownish-yellow color.|The walls were painted an ochrous hue, reminiscent of desert sand.|近义词：yellowish-brown, buff, tawny, sand-colored|反义词：azure, cerulean|词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin ōchra (“yellow ochre”), from Ancient Greek ὤχρα (ṓkhra).
