deontology:/ˌdiːɒnˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 道义论；义务论 |  The study of the nature of duty and obligation.  It is an ethical theory that uses rules to distinguish right from wrong.  |  Example:  Kant's deontology emphasizes the importance of universal moral rules. 康德的义务论强调普遍道德规则的重要性。

Example:  Deontology focuses on the rightness or wrongness of actions themselves, as opposed to the rightness or wrongness of the consequences of those actions.  道义论关注的是行为本身的对错，而不是这些行为后果的对错。 | 近义词:  ethics, moral philosophy | 反义词: consequentialism, teleology | 词性: noun
