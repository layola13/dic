nunatak: /ˈnuːnətæk/ (US), /ˈnʊnətæk/ (UK)| n. |冰原岛峰；冰原石山 |A peak of rock projecting above a surface of inland ice or snow, especially in Greenland. |例句：The climbers used the nunatak as a base camp. 登山者用冰原岛峰作为大本营。

例句：Several nunataks dotted the vast expanse of the ice sheet.  几座冰原岛峰点缀着广阔的冰盖。|近义词：peak, summit, outcrop |反义词: valley, glacier |词性:noun
