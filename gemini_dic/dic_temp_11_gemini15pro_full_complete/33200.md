chromosphere: /ˈkroʊməˌsfɪr/ | n. | 色球（太阳大气层的一层） | The chromosphere is a layer in the Sun's atmosphere between the photosphere and the corona.  It is characterized by its reddish color, which is visible during a total solar eclipse. |  During a total solar eclipse, the chromosphere can be seen as a reddish glow around the edge of the Moon. 在日全食期间，色球层可以看作是月亮边缘周围的红色光芒。

The chromosphere is where solar flares and prominences originate. 色球是太阳耀斑和日珥的起源地。
