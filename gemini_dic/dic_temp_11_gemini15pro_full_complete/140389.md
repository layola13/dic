oils:/ɔɪlz/
n.
油；油类；油画颜料
A viscous liquid derived from petroleum, especially for use as a fuel or lubricant. Also, paint made with oil.
The car needs an oil change. 这辆车需要换油。
The artist used vibrant oils to create the masterpiece.  这位艺术家用鲜艳的油彩创作了这幅杰作。
synonyms: lubricants, fuels, petroleum, paints
antonyms: water, solids
