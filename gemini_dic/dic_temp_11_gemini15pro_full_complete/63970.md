episodically:/ˌɛpɪˈsɒdɪkli/ /ˌɛpɪˈsɑːdɪkli/
adverb|间歇地；偶尔地；阶段性地|Occurring in episodes; not continuously.|例句：The illness manifested itself episodically. 这种疾病间歇性地发作。|例句：He worked on the project episodically, whenever he had free time.  他一有空就断断续续地做这个项目。|近义词：intermittently, sporadically, occasionally|反义词：continuously, constantly, regularly|词性:副词
词源: From the Greek word "epeisodion" meaning "coming in besides" or "insertion", later evolving into the Late Latin "episodium" and then the English "episode".  The suffix "-ically" transforms it into an adverb.
