dumfounder:/dʌmˈfaʊndər/ | v. | 使…惊呆；使…目瞪口呆 | To greatly astonish or amaze someone, to the point of leaving them speechless or confused. | The magician's sudden disappearance dumbfounded the entire audience. 魔术师突然消失使全场观众惊呆了。

The news of her sudden marriage dumbfounded her family. 她突然结婚的消息使她的家人目瞪口呆。 | synonyms: astound, amaze, bewilder, stun, shock | antonyms: enlighten, clarify, comfort, reassure | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dumb (unable to speak) + confound (to confuse or perplex).  Originally meant "to make dumb with astonishment."  Dates back to the mid-16th century.
