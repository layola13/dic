glump:/ɡlʌmp/
noun
闷闷不乐；沮丧
a state of low spirits and boredom
例句：She sat in a glump, staring out the window. 她闷闷不乐地坐在那里，望着窗外。
例句：He's been in a real glump ever since he lost his job. 自从失业以来，他一直很沮丧。
近义词：sulk, mope, frown, gloom
反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, joy
词性:n.


glump:/ɡlʌmp/
verb
使沮丧；使闷闷不乐
to cause someone to feel glum
例句：The rainy weather glumped everyone.  下雨的天气让每个人都很沮丧。
例句：Losing the game really glumped him. 输掉比赛真的让他很沮丧。
近义词：depress, sadden, deject
反义词：cheer, brighten, enliven
词性:v.
