hardy:/ˈhɑːdi/ (US) /ˈhɑːdi/ (UK)| adj.|强壮的，耐寒的，勇敢的|Able to survive difficult conditions, especially cold weather; strong and healthy; brave and determined.|例句：These plants are hardy enough to survive the winter. 这些植物足够耐寒，可以过冬。|例句：The fishermen are a hardy lot, used to battling the elements. 这些渔民是一群坚强的人，习惯于与恶劣天气搏斗。|近义词：robust, resilient, tough, strong, courageous|反义词：weak, fragile, delicate, timid|词性:形容词

词源: From Old Norse *harðr (“hard”), from Proto-Germanic *harduz (“hard”). Cognate with Old English heard (“hard”).
