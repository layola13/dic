human:/ˈhjuːmən/ (US) /ˈhjuːmən/ (UK)| adj. n.| 人类的；人；|A member of the species Homo sapiens, characterized by superior intelligence, articulate speech, and erect carriage.|例句：It's only human to make mistakes.  犯错误是人之常情。|近义词：mortal, person, individual|反义词：animal, inhuman|词性：形容词, 名词
Etymology: From Middle English *humaine*, from Old French *humain*, from Latin *hūmānus* (“of or belonging to man”), from *homo* (“man, human being”).
