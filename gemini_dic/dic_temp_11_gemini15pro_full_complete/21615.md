bluntish:/ˈblʌntɪʃ/| adj.|钝的，有点迟钝的|Moderately blunt; somewhat dull.|The knife was bluntish and couldn't cut the vegetables properly. 这把刀有点钝，不能把蔬菜切好。|近义词：dull, obtuse|反义词:sharp, keen|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->From blunt + -ish.  Blunt itself comes from Old Norse *bluntr, meaning "weak, dull." The suffix -ish forms adjectives meaning "somewhat" or "having the quality of."
