What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？

What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? 他用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

What had happened before the police arrived? 警察到达之前发生了什么事？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？
