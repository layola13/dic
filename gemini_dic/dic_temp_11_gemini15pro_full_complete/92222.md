hypothetical:/ˌhaɪpəˈθetɪk(ə)l/ |adj.|假设的，假定的|Based on an assumption or guess; used to describe something that has not actually happened but is considered as a possibility.|例句：Let's consider a hypothetical situation where the company goes bankrupt.  让我们考虑一个公司破产的假设情况。

例句：A hypothetical question is a good way to start a discussion. 一个假设性的问题是开始讨论的好方法。|近义词：theoretical, assumed, supposed, conjectural|反义词：actual, real, factual, proven|词性: adjective
