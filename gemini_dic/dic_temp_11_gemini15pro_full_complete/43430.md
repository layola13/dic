crappily:/ˈkræpɪli/| adv.|糟糕地，劣质地|In a crappy manner; poorly; badly.  It describes something done in a low-quality, unsatisfactory, or shoddy way.|The movie was crappily made, with poor acting and a weak plot. 这部电影拍得很烂，演技差，剧情也弱。|Synonyms: poorly, badly, shoddily, inadequately, inferiorly|Antonyms: well, excellently, superbly, skillfully|Word Type: Adverb
