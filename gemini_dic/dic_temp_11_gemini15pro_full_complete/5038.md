all intents and purposes:/ɔːl ɪnˈtɛnts ænd ˈpɜːrpəsɪz/ |phrase|实际上，几乎所有方面| For all intents and purposes, the project is complete. 实际上，这个项目已经完成了。| English explanation: This phrase means "in every practical sense" or "virtually." It is used to indicate that something is true in all important respects, even if not completely or technically true. It is often misspelled as "for all intensive purposes."| Example 1: For all intents and purposes, he is the owner of the company.  实际上，他是这家公司的所有者。

Example 2: The building is, for all intents and purposes, finished. 这栋楼实际上已经完工了。|Synonym: effectively, practically, virtually|Antonym: theoretically, hypothetically|phrase
