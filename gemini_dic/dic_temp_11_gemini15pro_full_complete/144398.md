outstripped:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪpt/
verb
超过，胜过
to go faster or further than someone or something else
例句：The demand for new housing has outstripped supply.  对新住房的需求已经超过了供应。

例句：His achievements have outstripped all expectations. 他的成就已经超出了所有人的预期。

近义词：surpassed, exceeded, overtaken, eclipsed
反义词：lagged behind, trailed, fallen behind
词性：v.
