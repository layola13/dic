boardsailing:/ˈbɔːrdˌseɪlɪŋ/
n.
帆板运动，帆板，风帆冲浪
The act of riding on a board with a sail attached, powered by the wind.
He enjoys boardsailing on the lake every summer. 他每年夏天都喜欢在湖上玩帆板运动。
