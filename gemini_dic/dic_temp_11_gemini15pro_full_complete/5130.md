allegorization:/ˌælɪɡərɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 寓言化；讽喻化 |The process of turning something into an allegory, where characters, events, or other elements represent abstract ideas or moral qualities. | The allegorization of the story made it more accessible to children.  这个故事的寓言化使它更容易为孩子们所接受。 |  symbolism, metaphorization | literalization | noun

allegory:/ˈælɪɡɔːri/ | n. | 寓言；讽喻 | A story, poem, or picture that can be interpreted to reveal a hidden meaning, typically a moral or political one. | The novel is an allegory for the political struggles of the time. 这部小说是当时政治斗争的寓言。| fable, parable | literal account | noun
