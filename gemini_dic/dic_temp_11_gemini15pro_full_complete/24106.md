brevity:/ˈbrevəti/| n.|简洁，简短|Conciseness of expression; shortness or briefness.|The report is a model of brevity and clarity. 这份报告是简洁明了的典范。|synonyms: conciseness, succinctness, terseness, pithiness|antonyms: verbosity, prolixity, lengthiness, wordiness|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *brevitās*, from *brevis* ("short").
