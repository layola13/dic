belligerent:/bəˈlɪdʒərənt/ | adj. n.|好战的，交战的；交战国，交战者| Inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness, hostility, or combativeness.  Engaged in war.|例句：His belligerent attitude made it difficult to work with him. 他的好战态度使人很难与他共事。|近义词：aggressive, hostile, warlike, combative|反义词：peaceful, conciliatory, amicable|词性:adjective

belligerent:/bəˈlɪdʒərənt/ | n.|交战国，交战者| A nation or person engaged in war or conflict.|例句：The belligerents finally agreed to a ceasefire. 交战双方最终同意停火。|近义词：combatant, warrior|反义词：pacifist|词性:noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin belligerent- ‘waging war’, from the verb belligerare, from bellum ‘war’ + gerere ‘wage’.
