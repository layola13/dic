calvinist:/ˈkælvɪnɪst/| n. adj.|加尔文主义者，加尔文派的|A follower of the theological doctrines of John Calvin, emphasizing predestination, the sovereignty of God, and the authority of the Bible.  A person who believes in Calvinism.  (As an adjective) Relating to or characteristic of Calvinism.|例句：He was a devout Calvinist. 他是一个虔诚的加尔文主义者。|近义词：Protestant, Reformed|反义词:Arminian|词性:noun, adjective


calvinistic:/ˌkælvɪˈnɪstɪk/| adj.|加尔文主义的|Relating to or characteristic of Calvinism or its adherents.|例句：The church followed a strict Calvinistic doctrine.  这个教会遵循严格的加尔文主义教义。|近义词：Reformed, Puritanical|反义词:Arminian|词性:adjective
