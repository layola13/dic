mocap: /ˈmoʊˌkæp/ | n. | 动作捕捉 | Motion capture, the process of recording the movements of people or objects for use in computer animation or other digital applications. | The movie used mocap to create realistic dinosaur movements.  这部电影使用动作捕捉技术来创造逼真的恐龙动作。 | Synonyms:  performance capture, motion tracking | Antonyms:  N/A | Noun

mocap: /ˈmoʊˌkæp/ | adj. | 动作捕捉的 | Relating to or created using motion capture. |  The mocap suit allowed the actor's movements to be translated to the computer. 这套动作捕捉服可以让演员的动作被转换成电脑数据。 | Synonyms:  motion-captured | Antonyms:  N/A | Adjective
