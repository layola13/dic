assonance:/ˈæsənəns/ | n. |  类韵；音谐；半谐音 |  The repetition of vowel sounds to create internal rhyming within phrases or sentences. |  Example: The rain in Spain falls mainly on the plain. (Repetition of the "ai" sound) 西班牙的雨主要下在平原上。（重复“ai”音）

Example:  Try to light the fire. (Repetition of the "i" sound)  试着点火。（重复“i”音）


| 近义词:  rhyme, resonance,  | 反义词: dissonance | 词性: noun
