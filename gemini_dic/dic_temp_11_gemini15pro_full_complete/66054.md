exasperate:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪt/
verb
激怒，使恼怒
irritate intensely; infuriate.
例句：Her constant complaining exasperated me. 她不断的抱怨让我很恼火。

例句：The children's incessant bickering exasperated their parents. 孩子们不停的争吵激怒了他们的父母。


近义词：irritate, annoy, infuriate, provoke, vex
反义词：soothe, calm, placate, appease
词性:v.

exasperating:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人恼火的
intensely irritating; infuriating.
例句：It was an exasperating delay. 这是一个令人恼火的延误。

例句：His exasperating habit of interrupting drove everyone crazy. 他那令人恼火的打断别人说话的习惯让每个人都抓狂。

近义词：irritating, annoying, infuriating, frustrating
反义词：soothing, calming, pleasing
词性:adj.

exasperation:/ɪɡˌzæspəˈreɪʃn/
noun
恼怒，激怒
a feeling of intense irritation or annoyance.
例句：She sighed in exasperation. 她恼怒地叹了口气。

例句：He threw his hands up in exasperation. 他恼怒地举起了双手。

近义词：irritation, annoyance, frustration, anger
反义词：calm, patience, composure
词性:n.
