concreteness:|n.|具体性，实在性|The quality of being concrete; consisting of material things.  Being specific and definite rather than general or abstract.|例句：The artist preferred the concreteness of sculpture to the more ethereal nature of painting.  这位艺术家更喜欢雕塑的具体性，而不是绘画更为飘渺的本质。|近义词：solidity, tangibility, definiteness|反义词：abstractness, vagueness, ambiguity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English concretenes, from Late Latin concrētēnēs, from concrētus (past participle of concrēscere ‘grow together, harden’), from con- ‘together’ + crescere ‘grow’.
