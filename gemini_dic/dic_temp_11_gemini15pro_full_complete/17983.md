beg:/beɡ/ | v. |乞求；请求；恳求 | to ask someone earnestly or humbly for something. | He begged for forgiveness. 他乞求原谅。| synonyms: implore, plead, beseech | antonyms: demand, command | verb

beg:/beɡ/ | v. |乞讨 | to ask people for money or food, especially in the streets. | The homeless man begged for money. 这个无家可归的人乞讨钱财。| synonyms: panhandle, solicit | antonyms: give, donate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English beggen, from Old North French begart (“mendicant, beggar”), probably of Germanic origin. Related to Old English bēgan ("to bend, bow").  The original sense was probably "to crouch or cower," hence "to humble oneself."
