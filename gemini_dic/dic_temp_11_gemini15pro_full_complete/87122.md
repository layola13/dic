herbalists:/ˈɜːrbəlɪsts/ /ˈhɜːrbəlɪsts/
noun
草药师；草药学家
A person who is expert in the use of herbs, especially medicinal herbs.
例句：The herbalists recommended a tea made from chamomile and lavender for relaxation.  草药师推荐了一种用甘菊和薰衣草制成的茶来放松身心。

例句：Many herbalists believe that natural remedies can be as effective as conventional medicine. 许多草药师认为天然药物可以和传统药物一样有效。
近义词：herbal practitioner, botanist, naturopath
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) conventional doctor, allopath
词性: noun
