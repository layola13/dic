out-boxing:/ˌaʊtˈbɒksɪŋ/ | n. | 拳击策略，以智取胜 | The act of outmaneuvering an opponent in boxing, focusing on skill and strategy rather than brute force.  |  The boxer's out-boxing frustrated his larger opponent.  这位拳击手的灵活战术挫败了他的大块头对手。 | synonyms: outmaneuvering, outsmarting | antonyms: brawling, slugging | noun

out-boxing:/ˌaʊtˈbɒksɪŋ/ | v. | 以智取胜，智胜（对手）| To defeat an opponent in boxing by using superior skill and strategy rather than brute force. | He out-boxed his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮以智取胜击败了对手。 | synonyms: outsmart, outmaneuver, outwit | antonyms: overpower, overwhelm | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "boxing" (the sport).  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating superiority in the action of boxing.
