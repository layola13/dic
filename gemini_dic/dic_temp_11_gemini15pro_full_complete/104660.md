karl:/kɑːrl/ | n. | 卡尔（男子名） | A masculine given name of Scandinavian origin, equivalent to Charles. | Karl is a common name in Germany. 卡尔在德国是一个常见的名字。| Karl Marx was a German philosopher. 卡尔·马克思是一位德国哲学家。 | 近义词: Charles | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From the Old Norse name *Karlar, meaning "free man."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *karlaz "free man," from PIE *ker- "man."
