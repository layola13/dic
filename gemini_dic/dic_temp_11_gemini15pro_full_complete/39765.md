consolidate:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ | v. | 巩固，合并 | The two companies consolidated to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并组成了一家更大的公司。 | strengthen, reinforce, combine, merge | weaken, separate, divide | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin consolidare "to make solid, make firm, strengthen," from com- "together" (see com-) + solidare "to make solid," from solidus "solid".
