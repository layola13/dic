Inocarpin
A toxic flavone found in the heartwood of *Dalbergia spp*. (especially *D. oliveri*)
Inocarpin is a toxic flavone found in the heartwood of several Dalbergia species, particularly Dalbergia oliveri.  It is known for its insecticidal properties.
Studies have shown that inocarpin exhibits strong insecticidal activity against certain insect pests.
