nonreviewable:/ˌnɑːnriˈvjuːəbl/ | adj. | 不可审查的，不可复议的 | Not subject to review or appeal. | The judge's decision is nonreviewable. 法官的决定是不可复议的。 | The contract states that certain decisions are nonreviewable. 合同规定某些决定是不可审查的。 | 近义词: final, conclusive, unappealable | 反义词: reviewable, appealable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + reviewable.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Reviewable" comes from the verb "review" which ultimately derives from the Old French "reveue," meaning "a looking over again."
