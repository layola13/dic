boughty: /ˈbaʊti/
adjective
爱买的；喜欢购物的
given to buying; fond of shopping.
She's a very boughty person, always coming home with new clothes. 她是个很爱买东西的人，总是买新衣服回家。
近义词: acquisitive, consumerist, materialistic
反义词: frugal, thrifty
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "bought" (past participle of "buy") + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives indicating a tendency or inclination).  The word is relatively recent and informal.
