non-disciplinary:/ˌnɒnˈdɪsɪplɪnəri/ | adj. | 非纪律性的，非惩戒性的 | Not related to discipline or punishment; often used in an academic context to describe actions or measures that are not intended as punishment. | The university issued a non-disciplinary warning to the student. 大学向该学生发出了非纪律性警告。 |  The professor gave the student a non-disciplinary advisory. 教授给了学生一个非惩戒性的建议。 | 近义词: non-punitive, corrective | 反义词: disciplinary, punitive | 词性: adjective

non-disciplinary:/ˌnɒnˈdɪsɪplɪnəri/ | adj. | 非学科的，跨学科的 | Not limited to a single academic discipline; interdisciplinary.  | The research project was non-disciplinary, involving experts from various fields.  这个研究项目是跨学科的，涉及各个领域的专家。 | The conference encouraged non-disciplinary collaboration.  会议鼓励跨学科合作。 | 近义词: interdisciplinary, multidisciplinary, cross-disciplinary | 反义词: disciplinary, subject-specific | 词性: adjective

词源:  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Disciplinary" comes from the Latin word "disciplina," meaning "instruction, knowledge," and later, "training, discipline."  因此，“non-disciplinary”表示“非训练”或“非惩戒”的。
