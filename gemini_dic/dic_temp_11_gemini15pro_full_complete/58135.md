drosera:/ˈdrɒsərə/ (US) /ˈdrɒsərə/ (UK)| n. |茅膏菜，毛毡苔|A carnivorous plant with sticky hairs on its leaves that trap and digest insects.|例句：The drosera caught a fly on its sticky tentacles. 茅膏菜用它粘性的触须捕获了一只苍蝇。

例句：Many species of drosera are cultivated for their unusual beauty. 许多种类的茅膏菜因其奇特的美丽而被栽培。|近义词：sundew|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Modern Latin *drosera*, from Ancient Greek δρόσος (drósos, “dew”).  The name refers to the glistening, dew-like secretions on the plant's tentacles.
