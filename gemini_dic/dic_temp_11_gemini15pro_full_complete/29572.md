catchweed:/ˈkætʃˌwiːd/| n. |猪殃殃；拉拉藤|A plant of the genus *Galium aparine*, with small hooked bristles that cling to clothing or fur. It is considered a weed in many areas. |例句：The catchweed clung to my socks as I walked through the field. 我走过田野时，猪殃殃粘在我的袜子上。

例句：Catchweed can be a nuisance in gardens. 猪殃殃在花园里可能很讨厌。|近义词：cleavers, goosegrass, stickyweed, gripgrass|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
