funambulate:/fjuːˈnæmbjʊleɪt/
|v.|走钢丝；(尤指)在高空走钢丝；冒险行事|To walk on a rope, especially at a great height; to act in a risky or precarious manner.|He funambulated across the canyon on a tightrope. 他在峡谷上走钢丝。|Synonyms: tightrope walk, walk a tightrope, balance, tread carefully|Antonyms: stumble, fall, falter|verb

funambulist:/fjuˈnæmbjʊlɪst/
|n.|走钢丝者；(尤指)在高空走钢丝者；冒险行事的人|A person who walks on a rope, especially at a great height; someone who takes risks.|The funambulist captivated the audience with his daring feats. 走钢丝者以其大胆的技艺吸引了观众。|Synonyms: tightrope walker, acrobat, risk-taker, daredevil|Antonyms: cautious person, careful person|noun

funambulatory:/fjuːˈnæmbjʊlətɔːri/
|adj.|走钢丝的；(尤指)在高空走钢丝的；冒险的|Relating to tightrope walking; risky, precarious.|His funambulatory skills were honed through years of practice. 他多年的练习磨练了他的走钢丝技巧。|Synonyms: tightrope-walking, acrobatic, risky, precarious|Antonyms: safe, secure, cautious|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
