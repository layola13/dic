missing:/ˈmɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 丢失的，缺少的 | absent; not present when expected or needed |  The missing piece of the puzzle was finally found.  丢失的拼图碎片终于被找到了。 | lost, absent, lacking | present, found | adjective

missing:/ˈmɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 失踪人口，失踪的东西 | something or someone that cannot be found  | The police are searching for the missing. 警察正在寻找失踪人员。 | loss, absence | presence, discovery | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *missinge, present participle of missen (“to fail to hit, miss, lack”), from Old English missan (“to miss, fail, err”), from Proto-Germanic *missjaną (“to go astray, err”), from Proto-Indo-European *meit- (“to change, exchange, move”).
