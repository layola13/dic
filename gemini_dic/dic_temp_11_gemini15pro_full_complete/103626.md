n.
托梁，搁梁，用托梁支撑
The act or process of constructing with joists.  The joists support the floor.
The contractor began the joisting for the second story. 承包商开始了二楼的托梁施工。
The joisting in this old house is starting to rot.  这栋老房子的托梁开始腐烂了。

joist:/dʒɔɪst/
n.
托梁，搁梁
A horizontal structural member that supports a floor or ceiling.  Floor joists are typically made of wood or steel.
The builder installed the joists for the new deck.  建筑工人安装了新平台的托梁。
The joists in the basement were infested with termites.  地下室的托梁被白蚁侵蚀了。
beam, girder, support
foundation, floor
noun
