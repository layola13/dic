incompleteness:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːtnəs/ | n. |不完整，不完全|The incompleteness of the data made it difficult to draw any firm conclusions.  数据的完整性导致难以得出任何确定的结论。|例句：The incompleteness of the fossil record makes it hard to trace the evolution of certain species. 化石记录的不完整性使得追踪某些物种的进化变得困难。

例句：The feeling of incompleteness haunted her, even after finishing the project. 即使完成了项目，不完整的感觉仍然困扰着她。|近义词：inadequacy, deficiency, imperfection|反义词:completeness, wholeness|词性:noun

incompleteness:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːtnəs/ | n. | 未完成 |  His sudden departure left a sense of incompleteness in the project. 他的突然离开给项目留下了一种未完成的感觉。|例句：The incompleteness of her explanation left me with many questions. 她解释的不完整性给我留下了许多问题。

例句：There's a sense of incompleteness in the story without a proper ending.  这个故事没有一个合适的结局，有一种不完整的感觉。 | 近义词：unfinishedness, lack of completion|反义词: completion, fulfillment| 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + complete + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  "Complete" ultimately comes from Latin "complētus," past participle of "complēre" (to fill up).
