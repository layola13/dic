nonirritant:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪtənt/| adj.|无刺激性的；不刺激的|Not causing irritation or inflammation to the skin or other tissues.|This cream is nonirritant and suitable for sensitive skin.  这款面霜无刺激性，适合敏感肌肤。|Synonyms: soothing, gentle, mild|Antonyms: irritant, irritating, harsh|adjective

nonirritant:/ˌnɑːnˈɪrɪtənt/| n.|无刺激物|A substance that does not cause irritation or inflammation.|This new formula is a nonirritant and safe for babies.  这个新配方是一种无刺激物，对婴儿安全。|Synonyms: demulcent, emollient|Antonyms: irritant, stimulant|noun
