bankroller: /ˈbæŋkˌroʊlər/ | n. | 资助者，出资人 | A person who provides financial backing for a project or venture, especially a gambling venture. |  The millionaire bankrolled the young artist's exhibition.  这个百万富翁资助了这位年轻艺术家的展览。

| Synonyms: financier, backer, sponsor, patron | Antonyms: debtor, borrower | noun


bankroll: /ˈbæŋkˌroʊl/ | vt. | 资助，为…提供资金 | To provide financial backing for. | He bankrolled the entire expedition. 他资助了整个探险。| Synonyms: finance, fund, subsidize, support | Antonyms: defund | verb
