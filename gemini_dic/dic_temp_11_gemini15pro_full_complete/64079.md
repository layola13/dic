epizootic:/ˌepɪzuːˈɒtɪk/| adj. n.|动物流行病的；动物流行病|An epizootic is a disease that appears as a new case in a given animal population, during a given period, at a higher-than-expected rate.  It is analogous to an epidemic in humans.|The epizootic decimated the rabbit population.  这场动物流行病使兔子数量锐减。|近义词：epidemic, pandemic, outbreak|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"endemic"表示地方性的，与大规模流行相对)|词性: adjective, noun


epizootic:/ˌepɪzuːˈɒtɪk/| n.|动物流行病|An epizootic is a widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in animals at a particular time.|The recent epizootic of avian flu has caused significant concern. 最近禽流感的爆发引起了极大的关注。|近义词：epidemic, pandemic, outbreak|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"endemic"表示地方性的，与大规模流行相对)|词性: noun
