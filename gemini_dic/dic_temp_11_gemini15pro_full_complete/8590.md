anticlerical:/ˌæntiˈklerɪkl/ | adj. | 反教权主义的，反对神职人员的 | Opposed to the power or influence of the clergy in secular matters. |  The French Revolution had strong anticlerical elements. 法国大革命具有强烈的反教权主义元素。

Anticlerical sentiment was widespread in the 19th century. 反教权情绪在19世纪很普遍。 |  Near synonyms: secular, anti-church, irreligious | Near antonyms: clerical, religious, devout | adjective
