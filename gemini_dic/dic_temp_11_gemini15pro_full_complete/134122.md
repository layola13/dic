nonimpeachability:/ˌnɒnɪmˌpiːtʃəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可弹劾；无过失；无可指责 | The state of being nonimpeachable; immunity from impeachment.  | The president asserted his nonimpeachability in the face of the scandal.  总统面对丑闻声称自己不可弹劾。|  immunity, unimpeachability | impeachability, vulnerability | noun

nonimpeachable:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpiːtʃəbl/ | adj. | 不可弹劾的；无过失的；无可指责的 | Not liable to impeachment; free from guilt or error. |  His conduct in office was deemed nonimpeachable. 他的任职行为被认为是无可指责的。 | unimpeachable, irreproachable, blameless | impeachable, culpable, blameworthy | adjective
