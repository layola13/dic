megacoulomb:/ˈmɛɡəˌkuːlɒm/ |ˈmɛɡəˌkuːlɑːm/ |n.| 兆库仑 |A unit of electric charge equal to one million coulombs.  It is a measure of the quantity of electricity. |The lightning strike delivered several megacoulombs of charge.  闪电释放了数兆库仑的电荷。

The battery stores a total of 5 megacoulombs.  电池总共储存了5兆库仑的电量。|近义词: None (Units are generally unique)|反义词: None (Units don't typically have opposites)|词性:noun

Etymology:
mega- (from Greek μέγας megas 'great') + coulomb (named after French physicist Charles-Augustin de Coulomb)
