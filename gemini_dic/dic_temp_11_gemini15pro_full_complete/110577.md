limited:/ˈlɪmɪtɪd/| adj.|有限的；受限制的；缺乏想象力的|Restricted in size, extent, or number; confined within limits. Also, lacking breadth of view or understanding; narrow-minded.|例句：The company has limited resources.  这家公司的资源有限。

例句：We had limited time to complete the project. 我们完成这个项目的时间有限。

近义词：restricted, confined, narrow, finite, scant, meager|反义词：unlimited, vast, extensive, boundless, infinite, ample|词性：形容词
