non-provided:/ˌnɒn prəˈvaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 未提供 | Not given or supplied; withheld.  This term is most commonly associated with web analytics data that is not shared due to privacy concerns. | Example:  The keyword data for this traffic is marked as "non-provided."  这个流量的关键词数据被标记为“未提供”。 | Example:  A significant portion of our search traffic is now non-provided, making keyword analysis more challenging.  我们很大一部分搜索流量现在都是未提供的，这使得关键词分析更具挑战性。 | Synonym:  unavailable, undisclosed, withheld | Antonym:  provided, supplied, available | adjective
