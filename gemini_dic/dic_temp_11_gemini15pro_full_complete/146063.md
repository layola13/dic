overeagerness:/ˌoʊvərˈiːgərnəs/| n. |过度热情，过分渴望|The child's overeagerness to please sometimes led to mistakes.  这个孩子过分渴望取悦别人，有时会导致犯错。| English Explanation: Excessive eagerness; being too keen or enthusiastic. | Example: His overeagerness to impress the boss made him seem insincere. 他过分渴望给老板留下好印象，反而显得不真诚。|Synonyms:  impatience, zeal, fervor|Antonyms: apathy, indifference, reluctance| Noun

overeager:/ˌoʊvərˈiːɡər/| adj. |过度热情的，过分渴望的|The overeager fans crowded the stage. 过度热情的歌迷挤满了舞台。|English Explanation: Excessively eager; too keen or enthusiastic.|Example:  She was overeager to start her new job and arrived an hour early on her first day. 她过分渴望开始她的新工作，第一天上班就提前一个小时到达。|Synonyms:  impatient, zealous, fervent|Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, reluctant|Adjective
