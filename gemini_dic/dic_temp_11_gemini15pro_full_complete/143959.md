outquestioned:/ˌaʊtˈkwɛstʃənd/
verb
胜过…的提问；问得…哑口无言
To question someone more thoroughly or persistently than another person.
The lawyer outquestioned the witness, revealing inconsistencies in his testimony. 律师问倒了证人，揭露了他证词中的矛盾之处。
out-argue, outwit, surpass
