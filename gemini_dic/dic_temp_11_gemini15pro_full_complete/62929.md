enmeshing:/ɪnˈmɛʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 纠缠的，使卷入的 |  Involved in a complicated or difficult situation, often in a way that makes it hard to escape.  |  The two families were enmeshed in a bitter feud.  这两个家庭卷入了一场激烈的世仇。 |  entangled, intertwined, involved | detached, separate | adjective

enmesh:/ɪnˈmɛʃ/ | vt. |  使纠缠，使卷入 | To involve someone or something in a complicated or difficult situation, often in a way that makes it hard to escape. | His business dealings enmeshed him in a web of deceit. 他的商业交易使他陷入欺骗的网络。 | entangle, involve, trap | extricate, disentangle | verb
