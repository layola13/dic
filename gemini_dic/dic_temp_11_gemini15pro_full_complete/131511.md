noncolorings:/nɒnˈkʌlərɪŋz/ | n. | 非着色，未着色（通常指图论中的点不染色）| The study of noncolorings in graph theory explores configurations where vertices are intentionally not assigned colors according to specific rules.  图论中对非着色的研究探索了顶点根据特定规则故意不分配颜色的配置。|  Graph theorists are interested in the properties of graphs with specific noncolorings. 图论学家对具有特定非着色的图的性质感兴趣。| Near synonyms: uncolorings, non-assignments (of color) | Antonyms: colorings, color assignments | noun
