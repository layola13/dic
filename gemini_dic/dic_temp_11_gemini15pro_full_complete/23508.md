braille: /breɪl/ /breɪl/ | n. |盲文；布莱叶盲文|a system of writing for blind people in which raised dots represent letters of the alphabet.  It was invented by Louis Braille.|例句：She learned to read Braille when she was five. 她五岁时学会了阅读盲文。

例句：The book is available in Braille. 这本书有盲文版。|近义词：blind writing|反义词: print (印刷字体)|词性:noun
