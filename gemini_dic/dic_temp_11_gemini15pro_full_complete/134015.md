nonhouseholder:/ˌnɑːnˈhaʊshəʊldər/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈhaʊshəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| n.|非户主，非家庭户成员|A person who is not the head of a household or family. This term is often used in census data and sociological studies. | Example: The survey showed a growing number of nonhouseholders living in urban areas. 这项调查显示，越来越多的非户主居住在城市地区。

|Example:  Many young nonhouseholders choose to live with roommates to share expenses. 许多年轻的非户主选择与室友合住以分担费用。
|Synonym: lodger, tenant, roomer, boarder|Antonym: householder, homeowner, head of household|noun
