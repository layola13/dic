grumping:/ˈɡrʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 抱怨，发牢骚 | The constant grumping about the weather is getting tiresome.  不停地抱怨天气让人厌烦。| Example: He kept grumping about the cold weather. 他不停地抱怨寒冷的天气. | Synonyms: complaining, whining, moaning, grousing | Antonyms: praising, complimenting, applauding | Noun

grumpy: /ˈɡrʌmpi/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的，爱抱怨的 |  Don't be so grumpy!  别这么暴躁！ | Example:  She was grumpy after a bad night's sleep. 她一晚没睡好，脾气很暴躁。| Synonyms: irritable, bad-tempered, crabby, surly | Antonyms: cheerful, happy, good-natured | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From grump (n.) + -ing. Grump (n.) is of uncertain origin, possibly related to grumble.
