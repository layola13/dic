genericness:/dʒəˈnɛrɪknəs/| n.|一般性，普遍性，通俗性|The genericness of the plot made the movie predictable.  情节的通俗性使得这部电影可以预见。|例句：The genericness of the product's packaging made it difficult to stand out on the shelves.  产品包装的普遍性使其难以在货架上脱颖而出。|近义词：generality, commonness, universality|反义词:specificity, uniqueness, particularity|词性:名词

Etymology: From generic + -ness.  Generic comes from Late Latin *genericus, from Latin genus "birth, race, stock, kind," from PIE *gene- "to give birth, beget."
