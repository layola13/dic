copartnerships:/koʊˈpɑːrtnərʃɪps/
n.
合伙企业；共同合伙关系
A copartnership is a business organization in which two or more individuals agree to share in the profits or losses of a business venture.
The two lawyers formed a copartnership.  这两个律师成立了一个合伙企业。
The family runs the farm as a copartnership.  这家人以合伙企业的方式经营农场。
synonyms: partnership, alliance, association, collaboration
antonyms: sole proprietorship, corporation


词性: noun
