creeled:/kriːld/
verb
装入鱼篓, 用鱼篓装
past tense and past participle of creel
The fisherman creeled his catch. 渔夫把捕到的鱼装进了鱼篓。


creel:/kriːl/
noun
鱼篓, 鱼篮
A wicker basket for carrying fish.
The fisherman carried his creel on his back. 渔夫背着鱼篓。
近义词:basket, container
反义词:(none - a creel is a specific type of container, it doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性:noun
