n.|陪审团遴选；评审团遴选|The process of selecting a jury or panel of judges. |The empanelling of the jury took several hours. 陪审团的遴选花了几个小时。|selection, formation, choosing|dismissal, discharge|noun
empanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/
empanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/
vt.|遴选（陪审团）；选任（评审团）|To select and swear in a jury or panel of judges. |The judge empanelled a jury of twelve people. 法官遴选了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。|select, appoint, choose|dismiss, discharge|verb
