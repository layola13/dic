featherness:/ˈfeðərnɪs/
n.
轻盈；羽毛般的状态
The lightness and softness characteristic of feathers.
The featherness of the down comforter made it incredibly comfortable.  羽绒被的轻盈使其非常舒适。
lightness, fluffiness, airiness
heaviness, denseness, solidity
