divulges:/daɪˈvʌldʒɪz/| v.|泄露，透露|To make known (private or sensitive information).|例句：The company divulged its plans for a new product.  公司透露了其新产品计划。

例句：She refused to divulge the source of her information. 她拒绝透露消息来源。

|近义词：reveal, disclose, unveil, leak|反义词：conceal, hide, suppress|词性：动词
