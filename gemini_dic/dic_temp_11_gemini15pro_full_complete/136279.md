nonreplicated:/ˌnɒnˈrɛplɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
未复制的，未重复的
Not replicated; not reproduced or copied.
The nonreplicated study found a significant effect.  这项未重复的研究发现了一个显著的影响。
The results of the nonreplicated experiment were inconclusive. 未重复实验的结果是不确定的。
near synonyms: unique, singular, one-of-a-kind, unrepeated
antonyms: replicated, duplicated, copied, reproduced
词性: 形容词
