fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒɪtɪv/| n. adj.|逃亡者，逃犯；短暂的，易逝的|A person who has escaped from a place or is in hiding, especially to avoid arrest or persecution. Also, lasting for a short time; fleeting.|例句：The fugitive was apprehended after a lengthy chase.  这名逃犯在长时间的追捕后被逮捕了。|近义词：escapee, runaway, deserter, refugee, fleeting, ephemeral, transient|反义词:captive, permanent, lasting|词性:noun, adjective

fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪv/| adj.|逃亡的；短暂的，易逝的|Lasting for a short time; fleeting; (of thoughts, feelings, or sensations) elusive and difficult to define or describe.|例句：A fugitive thought crossed her mind. 一个 fleeting 的念头闪过她的脑海。|近义词：fleeting, ephemeral, transient, elusive|反义词:permanent, lasting|词性:adjective
