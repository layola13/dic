empir:/ɛmˈpɪr/ | n. | 经验，实践 | Based on observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic. |  例句：The theory is supported by empir evidence.  这个理论是由经验依据支持的。| 近义词：experience, observation, practice | 反义词：theory, speculation | 词性：名词

empir:/ɛmˈpɪr/ | adj. | 经验主义的，以经验为依据的 |  Relying on or derived from observation or experiment. | 例句：Empir data is crucial for scientific research.  经验数据对于科学研究至关重要。| 近义词：experiential, observational, practical | 反义词：theoretical, speculative | 词性：形容词

词源: From Late Latin *empiricus, from Ancient Greek ἐμπειρικός (empeirikós, “experienced”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + πεῖρα (peîra, “trial, experiment”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *per- (“to try, risk”).
