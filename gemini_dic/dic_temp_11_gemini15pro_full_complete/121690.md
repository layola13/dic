mispositioning:/ˌmɪspəˈzɪʃənɪŋ/
noun
错误放置，位置不当
the act of putting something in the wrong place, or the fact of being in the wrong place
The product's mispositioning in the market led to poor sales.  该产品在市场上的错误定位导致销售不佳。
The mispositioning of the microphone caused feedback during the concert.  麦克风位置摆放错误导致音乐会期间出现反馈。
misplacement, malposition
correct positioning, proper placement
n.
