ethicists:/ˈɛθɪsɪsts/
n.
伦理学家；道德学家
A person who specializes in ethics; a moral philosopher.
Ethicists are debating the implications of this new technology. 伦理学家正在讨论这项新技术的含义。

Ethicists often disagree on the best course of action. 伦理学家对于最佳行动方案经常意见不一。

近义词: moralists, moral philosophers
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 immoral people 不道德的人)
词性: noun
