nonappeasable:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːzəbl/
adjective
不可安抚的，无法平息的
Not able to be appeased, pacified, or satisfied.
His nonappeasable hunger for power led to his downfall. 他对权力永不满足的渴望导致了他的垮台。
The baby's nonappeasable crying worried the new parents. 婴儿不停的哭闹让新手父母很担心。
近义词: implacable, unyielding, relentless
反义词: appeasable, placable, conciliatory
词性: adjective
词源: From non- (not) + appeasable (able to be appeased), from appease (from Old French apaisier, from a- (to) + pais (peace)).
