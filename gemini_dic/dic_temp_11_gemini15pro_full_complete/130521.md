nonaccords:/ˌnɒnəˈkɔːdz/ | n. |不一致，不符合|Lack of agreement or harmony; things that do not accord. |The nonaccords between the two versions of the contract caused confusion. 这两份合同版本之间的不一致之处引起了混乱。| The numerous nonaccords in their testimonies made it difficult to determine the truth. 他们证词中的诸多不一致之处使得难以确定真相。 | 近义词: disagreements, discrepancies, disparities, inconsistencies | 反义词: agreements, accords, harmonies, consistencies | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "accord" (from Old French "acorder," meaning "to agree").  The word "accord" ultimately derives from Latin "ad" (to) + "cor" (heart).
