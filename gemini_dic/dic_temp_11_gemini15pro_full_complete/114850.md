mammiform:/ˈmæmɪfɔːrm/
adjective
乳房状的，乳头状的
Resembling a breast or nipple in shape.
The cactus had mammiform protuberances.  这种仙人掌有乳头状的突起。
近义词: breast-shaped, nipple-shaped, mamillary
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., flat, concave)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin mamma ("breast") + -form ("having the form of").
