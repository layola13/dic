overillustrates:/ˌoʊvərˈɪləˌstreɪts/ | v. | 过度图解；用过多插图说明 | To illustrate excessively; to use too many illustrations to explain something. | The textbook overillustrates the basic concepts, making it cluttered and confusing. 这本教科书对基本概念的图解过多，使其显得杂乱无章，令人困惑。 The author overillustrates his points, making the article visually overwhelming. 作者过度图解了他的观点，使文章在视觉上显得过于繁复。 | overemphasizes, overstates | understates | verb
