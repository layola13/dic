gooselike:/ˈɡuːslaɪk/
adjective
像鹅的；鹅似的
Resembling a goose in appearance, behavior, or sound.
Her walk was stiff and gooselike. 她走路僵硬，像鹅一样。
Near synonyms:  anserine, goosey
Antonyms: graceful, elegant
词性: adjective
