archaeography:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒɡrəfi/
n.
考古学
the study of ancient documents and the methods used to write them
Archaeography helps us understand how writing evolved in different cultures.  考古学帮助我们理解文字在不同文化中的演变。
The archaeography of medieval manuscripts is a complex field. 中世纪手稿的考古学是一个复杂的研究领域。
