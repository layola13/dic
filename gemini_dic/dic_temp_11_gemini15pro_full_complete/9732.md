antivariolous: /ˌæntivaɪˈɒləs/ /ˌæntivaɪˈɑːləs/
adjective
抗天花的
preventing or curing smallpox.  Relating to or used against smallpox.
例句：Antivariolous vaccination was introduced in the late 18th century.  抗天花疫苗接种是在18世纪后期引入的。

例句：The doctor administered an antivariolous serum. 医生给病人注射了抗天花血清。

近义词：anti-smallpox
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From anti- + variola (smallpox) + -ous.  "Anti-" signifies "against" or "opposed to."  "Variola" is the Latin word for smallpox.  "-ous" is a suffix forming adjectives and meaning "possessing, full of, or characterized by."
