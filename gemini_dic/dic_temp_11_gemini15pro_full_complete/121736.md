mispronounce: /ˌmɪsprəˈnaʊns/ | v. |  错误地发音；读错 | to pronounce a word or name wrongly. |  I always mispronounce his name. 我总是把他的名字念错。 | misspeak, misstate | pronounce correctly | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + pronounce.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong" or "badly." "Pronounce" comes from the Old French "prononcer,"  ultimately from Latin "pronuntiare"  meaning "to proclaim" or "to utter."
