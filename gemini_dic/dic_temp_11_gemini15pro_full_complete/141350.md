operant:/ˈɒpərənt/ (US) /ˈɒpərənt/ (UK)| adj. n.|操作性的，操作性的反应; 操作者|Influencing or controlling behavior by reinforcing a desired response. A response that is emitted and controlled by its consequences.|例句：Operant conditioning is a type of learning where behavior is controlled by consequences. 操作性条件反射是一种通过结果控制行为的学习类型。

例句：The operant behavior of pressing the lever was reinforced by the delivery of food. 按压杠杆的操作性行为因食物的提供而得到强化。|近义词：instrumental, effective, active|反义词：passive, unresponsive|词性：adjective, noun

operant:/ˈɒpərənt/ (US) /ˈɒpərənt/ (UK)| n.|操作者, 手术者|A person who operates or performs a function.|例句：The skilled operant controlled the machinery with precision. 熟练的操作员精确地控制着机器。

例句：He was the chief operant in the complex surgery. 他是这次复杂手术的主刀医生。|近义词：operator, worker, performer|反义词：N/A|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *operantem*, present participle of *operari* "to work," from *opera* "work."
