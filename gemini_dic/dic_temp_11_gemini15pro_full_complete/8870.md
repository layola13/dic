antifilibuster:/ˌæntiˈfɪlɪbʌstər/
noun
反阻挠议案者；反阻挠议案的
a person who opposes the use of the filibuster, a tactic used in the US Senate to delay or block a vote on a bill.  A measure intended to counter or limit the use of filibusters.
The senator was a staunch antifilibuster, arguing that it undermined the democratic process.  参议员是一位坚定的反阻挠议案者，认为它破坏了民主进程。
The new rules included an antifilibuster provision designed to expedite voting. 新规则包含一项旨在加快投票的反阻挠议案条款。
Synonyms: obstructionist (for a person), procedural reform (for a measure)
Antonyms: filibusterer, obstructionist (for a person)
