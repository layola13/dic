cereous:/ˈsɪəriəs/ | adj. |蜡质的；蜡状的；苍白的|Resembling wax in appearance, consistency, or color; pale.|The cereous coating on the leaves made them look artificial. 叶子上的蜡质涂层使它们看起来像是人造的。|近义词:waxy, waxen, waxlike|反义词:fleshy, ruddy|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *cereus* "of wax," from *cera* "wax."
