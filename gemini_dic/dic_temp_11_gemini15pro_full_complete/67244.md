expulsions:/ɪkˈspʌlʃənz/
n.
驱逐，逐出，开除
the act of forcing someone to leave a place, school, organization, etc.
The expulsions of the diplomats caused an international incident. 这些外交官的驱逐引发了一场国际事件。
deportation, ejection, removal
admission, inclusion
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Late Middle English: from Old French expulsion, from Latin expulsio(n-), from the verb expellere (see expel).
