jackpot:/ˈdʒækˌpɒt/ (UK) /ˈdʒækˌpɑːt/ (US)| n.|头奖，累积奖金，意外的收获|a large amount of money that you can win in a game of chance, lottery, etc., especially one that has been increasing because no one has won it yet. Also, a sudden, large gain or stroke of luck.|She won the jackpot in the lottery. 她中了彩票头奖。|Synonyms: bonanza, windfall, winnings|Antonyms: loss, failure|noun
