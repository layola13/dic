kistful: /ˈkɪstfʊl/
noun
一满盒子;一满箱
A kistful of treasures.  一箱子宝藏。
She brought a kistful of old photographs to show us. 她带来满满一箱老照片给我们看。
Synonyms: chestful, boxful
Antonyms: empty
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Scots *kist (“chest, box”) +‎ -ful. Ultimately from Old Norse kista (“chest, box”).
