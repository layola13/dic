causation:/kɔːˈzeɪʃən/
n.
因果关系，原因
The act or process of causing something to happen or exist.
Heavy smoking is a major causation of lung cancer. 大量吸烟是肺癌的主要病因。
Synonyms: causality, origin, source, genesis, reason
Antonyms: effect, result, consequence
词性: 名词
