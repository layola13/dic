outthwacking: /ˌaʊtˈθwækɪŋ/
verb
痛击，狠狠地打
Striking or hitting with great force, surpassing someone else in the act of thwacking.
He was outthwacking his opponent in the badminton match. 他在羽毛球比赛中狠狠地击败了对手。
Synonyms:  outbeating, thrashing, clobbering, trouncing, walloping
Antonyms: losing to, yielding to
词性: 动词
