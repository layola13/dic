monophthongal:/ˌmɒnəfˈθɒŋɡəl/ | /ˌmɑːnəfˈθɑːŋɡəl/ | adj. | 单元音的 |  Consisting of a single vowel sound; (of a vowel) not changing in quality during its articulation. |  The word "bet" has a monophthongal vowel sound.  "bet"这个单词有一个单元音。 |  pure, single | diphthongal | adjective
