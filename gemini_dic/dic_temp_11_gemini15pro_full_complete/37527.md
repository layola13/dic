commonality:/ˌkɒməˈnæləti/ (US) /ˌkɑːməˈnæləti/ (UK)| n. |共性，共同点，共通性|A feature or trait shared by two or more people or things.|例句：There is a commonality of purpose among the team members.  团队成员之间目标一致。

|例句：The study explores the commonalities between the two cultures.  这项研究探讨了两种文化之间的共同点。

|近义词：similarity, likeness, resemblance, shared characteristic, common ground, affinity|反义词：difference, dissimilarity, divergence, distinction|词性:noun


commonality:/ˌkɒməˈnæləti/ (US) /ˌkɑːməˈnæləti/ (UK)| n. |普遍性，常见性|The state of being common or widespread.|例句：The commonality of this disease is alarming. 这种疾病的普遍性令人担忧。

|例句：The commonality of smartphones has changed the way we communicate. 智能手机的普及改变了我们的沟通方式。


|近义词：prevalence, ubiquity, frequency|反义词：rarity, infrequency, uncommonness|词性:noun
