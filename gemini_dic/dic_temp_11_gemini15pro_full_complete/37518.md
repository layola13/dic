common-grounds:/ˈkɒmən ɡraʊndz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmən ɡraʊndz/ (US)| n. |共同点；共同立场；共识|A basis of mutual interest or agreement.|例句：They found common ground in their love of music. 他们在对音乐的热爱中找到了共同点。|例句：Despite their differences, they were able to find common ground on the issue. 尽管存在分歧，他们还是能够在这个问题上找到共同点。|近义词：shared beliefs, common interest, mutual understanding, similar views|反义词：disagreement, conflict, opposing views|词性:名词

词源:  "common" comes from Old French "comun," ultimately from Latin "communis" meaning "shared by all or many." "Grounds" comes from Old English "grund" referring to the surface of the earth and later to a foundation or basis.
