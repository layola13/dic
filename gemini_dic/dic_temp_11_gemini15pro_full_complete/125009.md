mugwumperies:/ˈmʌɡwʌmpəriz/
noun
故作清高;自命清廉的行为
The behavior or attitudes of mugwumps, characterized by an air of moral superiority and disdain for practical politics.
His mugwumperies alienated many of his former supporters. 他的故作清高疏远了他以前的许多支持者。
Synonyms: sanctimoniousness, holier-than-thou attitudes, self-righteousness
Antonyms: pragmatism, realism, cynicism
