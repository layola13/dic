hypermobilities:/ˌhaɪpərmoʊˈbɪlətiz/| n.|过度活动性，关节过度活动|Excessive range of motion in joints, often exceeding the normal physiological limits.  This can be a symptom of certain medical conditions.|She suffers from hypermobilities in her fingers and wrists, making it difficult to play the piano.|近义词：flexibility, laxity|反义词: stiffness, rigidity|词性:noun
