hyperrealist:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlɪst/ | n. adj. | 超现实主义者；超现实主义的 |  An artist or writer who depicts reality in an extremely detailed and precise manner, often exceeding the accuracy of a photograph.  A style of painting and sculpture resembling a high-resolution photograph. | The hyperrealist paintings were so lifelike, it was hard to believe they weren't photographs. 这些超现实主义绘画栩栩如生，很难相信它们不是照片。|  The hyperrealist sculptures captured every wrinkle and pore on the subject's face. 这些超现实主义雕塑捕捉到了对象脸上的每一条皱纹和毛孔。 | 近义词: photorealist, superrealist | 反义词: impressionist, abstract | 词性: noun, adjective

hyperrealistic:/ˌhaɪpərˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 超现实主义的 | Marked by extreme realism in detail. | The hyperrealistic details in the movie made it incredibly immersive. 电影中超现实的细节使它令人难以置信的沉浸。 | The video game boasted hyperrealistic graphics that blurred the lines between virtual and real.  这款电子游戏拥有超逼真的画面，模糊了虚拟与现实之间的界限。 | 近义词: photorealistic, superrealistic | 反义词: impressionistic, abstract | 词性: adjective
