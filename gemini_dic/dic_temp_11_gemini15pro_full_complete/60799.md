electronically:/ɪˌlekˈtrɒnɪkli/ /ɪˌlekˈtrɑːnɪkli/
adverb|电子地；以电子方式|By means of electronic devices or systems. |例句：The data was electronically transmitted. 数据以电子方式传输。|例句：The form can be submitted electronically.  该表格可以电子方式提交。|近义词：digitally, online, virtually|反义词:manually, physically|词性:adverb
词源:electronic + -ally.  From electronic, from electr- + -ic.
