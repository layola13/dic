fro:/froʊ/| adv.| 从；往；回；离去 | away from; back.|He waved as he rode off into the sunset, never to return fro. 他骑马向夕阳走去，挥着手，永远不再回来了。 | 近义词: away, back | 反义词: to, toward | 词性: 副词

词源:  Middle English fra, from Old Norse frá, from Proto-Germanic *fram, from Proto-Indo-European *pro-, meaning "forward," "forth."  Over time, the sense shifted to "away from" or "from."
