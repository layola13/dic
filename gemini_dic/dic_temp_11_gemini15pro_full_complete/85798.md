hazardable:/ˈhæzərdəbəl/
adjective
危险的;有害的
Capable of posing a hazard; dangerous.
The chemical is hazardable and should be handled with care.  这种化学品有危险，应小心处理。
近义词: dangerous, risky, perilous, hazardous
反义词: safe, secure
词性: adjective

词源:  From hazard + -able.  Hazard ultimately comes from Arabic  az-zahr (meaning "the die").
