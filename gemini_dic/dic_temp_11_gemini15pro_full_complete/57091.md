doughtiest:/ˈdoʊtiɪst/
adjective
最勇敢的，最强悍的
Most valiant or courageous; strongest.
The doughtiest warriors of the kingdom assembled for the battle. 王国里最勇敢的战士们集结起来准备战斗。
The doughtiest knight faced the dragon alone.  最强悍的骑士独自面对恶龙。
synonyms: bravest, most valiant, most courageous, stoutest, strongest
antonyms: cowardly, fearful, timid, weak
词性: adjective


doughty:/ˈdaʊti/
adjective
勇敢的，强悍的
Brave and strong.
He was a doughty warrior, feared by all his enemies. 他是一位勇敢的战士，令所有敌人闻风丧胆。
She showed a doughty spirit in the face of adversity.  面对逆境，她展现了坚韧不拔的精神。
synonyms: brave, valiant, courageous, stout, strong
antonyms: cowardly, fearful, timid, weak
词性: adjective
