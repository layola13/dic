maximumly:/ˈmæksɪməli/| adv.|最大限度地，极度地|Not considered standard English. Formed by adding the adverbial suffix "-ly" to the adjective "maximum."  While grammatically understandable, it is generally avoided in formal writing and speech.  Use "maximally" instead.|*Example (non-standard): He tried maximumly to achieve his goals. (他尽最大努力去实现他的目标。)* *Better Example: He tried maximally to achieve his goals. (他尽最大努力去实现他的目标。)*|近义词：maximally, to the fullest extent, utterly, completely|反义词: minimally, slightly, hardly|词性:Adverb (non-standard)
