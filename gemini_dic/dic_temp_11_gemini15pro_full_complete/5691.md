aluminated:/əˈluːmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
镀铝的；涂铝的；用铝处理的
Coated or treated with aluminum.
The aluminated surface of the mirror reflected the light brightly.  镀铝的镜面将光线明亮地反射出来。
The telescope's mirror was aluminated for better reflectivity.  望远镜的镜面镀了铝，以提高反射率。
Near Synonyms: aluminum-coated, aluminum-plated, metallized
Antonyms: uncoated, untreated
