outwaters:/ˈaʊtˌwɔːtərz/ |n.|外海，远海；离岸较远的水域|The ship sailed into the outwaters.  这艘船驶向了远海。| English explanation: The areas of the sea or ocean that are far from the shore or coast.  It refers to waters beyond the territorial limits of a country or those further out from the coastline than inshore waters. | Example: The fishing boat ventured into the outwaters in search of larger fish.  这艘渔船冒险驶入远海去寻找更大的鱼。|Synonym: open sea, deep sea, high seas|Antonym: inshore waters, coastal waters|noun
