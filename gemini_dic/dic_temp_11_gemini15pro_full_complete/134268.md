noninfluences:/nɒnˈɪnfluənsɪz/| n.|无影响力; 非影响因素|Factors or elements that do not exert any impact or sway on a particular outcome or situation.  The report identified several noninfluences on the market fluctuations.|The study found several noninfluences on the final result.  这项研究发现了几个对最终结果没有影响的因素。|近义词: irrelevancies, non-factors|反义词: influences, factors|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Old French non- and directly from Latin non "not."
influence (noun): From Old French influence "flowing in; influx," from Medieval Latin influentia "a flowing in," especially of celestial or supernatural forces, from Latin influere "to flow into," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + fluere "to flow."
