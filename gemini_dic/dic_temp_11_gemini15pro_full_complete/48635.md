defender:/dɪˈfɛndər/ (US) /dɪˈfɛndə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 防御者，拥护者，辩护者 |Someone who protects something or someone from attack or criticism. |例句：He was a strong defender of human rights. 他是人权的坚定捍卫者。

例句：The team's defenders were unable to stop the opposing team's forwards. 球队的后卫无法阻止对方球队的前锋。

近义词：protector, guardian, advocate, supporter |反义词：attacker, assailant, opponent, critic |词性: noun
