heliotropic:/ˌhiːliəˈtrɑːpɪk/| adj.|向日性的，向光的|Turning or growing toward the sun.  Relating to or exhibiting heliotropism.|例句：The sunflower is a heliotropic plant.  向日葵是一种向日性植物。

例句：The heliotropic movement of the plant allows it to maximize its exposure to sunlight.  植物的向日运动使其能够最大限度地暴露在阳光下。|近义词：phototropic|反义词:aphototropic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hēlios* (sun) + *trope* (a turning)
