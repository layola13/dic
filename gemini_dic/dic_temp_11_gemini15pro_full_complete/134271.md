noninformation:/ˌnɒnɪnfəˈmeɪʃən/
|n.|无信息；无效信息|Meaningless or useless data that does not add to understanding or knowledge.  It can also refer to data that actively obscures or misleads. |The report was full of noninformation, offering no real insights. 这份报告充满了无信息，没有提供任何真正的见解。|Synonyms:  useless data, noise, meaningless data, irrelevant information|Antonyms: information, knowledge, data, intelligence|noun
