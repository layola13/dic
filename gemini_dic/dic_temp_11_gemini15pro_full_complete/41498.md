cornuto:/kɔrˈnuːtoʊ/ (US), /kɔːˈnjuːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|戴绿帽子的（男人），通奸的，（意大利语）|A man whose wife has been unfaithful to him.  Can also be used as an adjective to describe such a man, or something related to the situation.  Derived from the Italian word for "horned," referring to the cuckold's symbolic horns.|

例句：
He was called a cornuto after his wife's affair.
他妻子有外遇后，他被称为戴绿帽子的。

His friends jokingly referred to him as a cornuto.
他的朋友们开玩笑地称他为戴绿帽子的。

近义词：cuckold, cheated on (husband)|
反义词: faithful husband|
词性: noun, adjective|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *cornuto*, past participle of *cornutare* "to give horns to," from *corno* "horn," from Latin *cornu*. The image of horns is associated with cuckoldry across many cultures, likely stemming from the behavior of some horned animals.
