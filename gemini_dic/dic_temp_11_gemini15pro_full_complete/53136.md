diplomacy: /dɪˈploʊməsi/ (US) /dɪˈpləʊməsi/ (UK)| n.|外交，外交手腕|The art and practice of conducting negotiations between nations. Skill in handling affairs without raising hostility.|例句：The government resorted to diplomacy to resolve the international crisis. 政府采取外交手段来解决这场国际危机。

例句：She showed great diplomacy in handling the difficult situation.  她在处理这个棘手的情况时展现了高超的外交手腕。


近义词：tact, negotiation, statesmanship|反义词: hostility, aggression, belligerence|词性:noun
