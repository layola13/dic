nectareal:/nɛkˈtɛəriəl/ |adj.| 花蜜的；甘美的；甜蜜的|Resembling or suggesting nectar; sweet or delicious.  Pertaining to or resembling nectar.|The nectareal wine was a perfect accompaniment to the dessert.  这甘美的葡萄酒是甜点的完美搭配。|近义词:sweet, delicious, honeyed, ambrosial|反义词:bitter, sour, acrid|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *nectaralis, from Latin nectar + -alis. Ultimately from Greek νέκταρ (néktar, “drink of the gods”).
