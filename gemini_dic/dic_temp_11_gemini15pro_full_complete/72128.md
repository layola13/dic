flooded:/ˈflʌdɪd/| adj. v.|被淹没的，泛滥的|Covered or overwhelmed with water; also, overwhelmed with a large quantity of something.|The basement was flooded after the heavy rain. 地下室在大雨后被淹没了。|近义词：inundated, overwhelmed, swamped|反义词:dry, arid|verb (past participle of flood)|The river flooded the town. 河水淹没了城镇。

flooded:/ˈflʌdɪd/| adj. v.|充满的，涌现的|Filled or overwhelmed with a large amount of something (not necessarily water).|I was flooded with emails after the announcement. 公告发布后，我的邮箱收到了大量的电子邮件。|近义词：awash, overflowing, teeming|反义词:empty, lacking|verb (past participle of flood)|Memories flooded back as I looked at the old photos.  看着老照片，回忆涌上心头。
