hyperesthesia:/ˌhaɪpərɪsˈθiːʒə/ /ˌhaɪpərɪsˈθiːziə/| n. |感觉过敏，感觉异常|A condition of abnormally increased sensitivity to stimuli, especially of the sense of touch or pain.|She experienced hyperesthesia after the surgery, with even light touches causing intense pain. 她手术后出现了感觉过敏，即使是轻微的触摸也会引起剧烈的疼痛。|近义词：hypersensitivity, allodynia|反义词：hypoesthesia, anesthesia|noun
