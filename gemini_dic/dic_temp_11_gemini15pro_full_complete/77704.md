geminy:/ˈdʒɛmɪnaɪ/| n.|双子座；双子宫；一对；一对相似或相同的事物|A pair of twins; two people or things regarded as a pair.  It is also one of the constellations of the zodiac.|例句：The twins are Geminis. 这对双胞胎是双子座。

例句：My car and my best friend's car are practically geminis. 我的车和我最好的朋友的车简直一模一样。|近义词：twins, pair, couple, duo|反义词:|noun
