destructure:/ˌdiːˈstrʌktʃər/ /ˌdiːˈstrʌktʃɚ/ | v. |解构，分解|To break down a complex data structure into its individual parts, typically assigning those parts to separate variables.|
const { name, age } = person; // Destructures the 'name' and 'age' properties from the 'person' object.
从 person 对象中解构出 'name' 和 'age' 属性。

let [first, second] = array; // Destructures the first two elements of the array.
解构数组的前两个元素。

|近义词：decompose, break down, unpack|反义词：construct, compose, assemble|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "undo") + structure.  The prefix "de-" signifies reversal or undoing, while "structure" refers to something that is built or organized. Therefore, "destructure" literally means to undo or break apart a structure.
