nyctalopia:/ˌnɪktəˈloʊpiə/ | n. |夜盲症|Inability to see clearly in dim light or at night, often due to a vitamin A deficiency. |  He suffered from nyctalopia and couldn't drive at night. 他患有夜盲症，晚上不能开车。| synonym: night blindness | antonym: - | noun

Etymology: From Greek *nýx* (night) + *aláoō* (I am blind) + *-ia*.
