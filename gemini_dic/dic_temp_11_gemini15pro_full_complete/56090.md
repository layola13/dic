docquet:/ˈdɒkɪt/ | n. |摘要; 签署; 认证; (正式文件的)简要内容; | A docquet is a summary of the contents of a legal document, especially one kept in a court of law. | The clerk prepared the docquet for the judge. 书记员为法官准备了摘要。| synonym: abstract, summary, digest | antonym:  elaboration, expansion | noun

docquet:/ˈdɑːkɪt/ | v. |签署; 认证; |To docquet a document means to officially record or summarize it. | The lawyer docqueted the agreement. 律师签署了协议。| synonym: endorse, sign, certify, validate | antonym: invalidate, reject, deny | verb
