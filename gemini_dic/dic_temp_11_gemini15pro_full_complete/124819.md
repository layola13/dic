muciparous:/mjuːˈsɪpərəs/
adj.
产生粘液的;分泌粘液的
Producing or secreting mucus.
The muciparous glands in the frog's skin help keep it moist.  青蛙皮肤上的粘液腺帮助它保持湿润。
近义词: mucous, mucose
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "dry" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective
