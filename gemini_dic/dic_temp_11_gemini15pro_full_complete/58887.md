durbar:/ˈdɜːrbɑːr/ (US), /ˈdɜːbɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.|正式会见; 朝觐; (尤指印度旧时王公的)接见会; |A durbar was held to celebrate the king's birthday. |例句：The Maharaja held a durbar to receive the visiting dignitaries.  大君举行朝觐，接见来访的贵宾。
