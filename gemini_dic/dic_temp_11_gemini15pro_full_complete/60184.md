efficaciously:/ˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəsli/
adverb
有效地；奏效地
in a way that produces the desired result or effect.
The problem was dealt with efficaciously.  这个问题得到了有效处理。
The medicine worked efficaciously.  这药效果很好。
近义词: effectively, successfully, potently
反义词: ineffectively, inefficiently, unsuccessfully
词性: adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *efficaciōsus, from Latin efficax (“effective”) + -ōsus.
