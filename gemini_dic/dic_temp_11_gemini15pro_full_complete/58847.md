duplicately:/ˌduːplɪkətli/| adv.|重复地，双重地|in a duplicate manner; involving duplication.  The information was duplicately stored on two separate servers.|近义词：redundantly, doubly|反义词:singly, uniquely|词性:副词

词源: From the Latin word "duplicatus," the past participle of "duplicare," meaning "to double."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "duplicate" into an adverb.
