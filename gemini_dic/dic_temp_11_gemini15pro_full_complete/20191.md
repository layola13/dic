bird-brained:/ˈbɜːrdˌbreɪnd/ | adj. | 愚蠢的，头脑简单的 | Stupid, silly, or scatterbrained, like a bird.  |  Example:  He made a bird-brained decision to invest all his money in a failing company. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定，把所有的钱都投资到了一家快要倒闭的公司。

Example: Don't listen to his bird-brained ideas; they'll never work.  别听他那些愚蠢的想法，它们永远不会奏效。 |  Synonyms: silly, foolish, stupid, witless, daft, dizzy, flighty | Antonyms: intelligent, clever, smart, wise, sensible | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "bird" + "brain,"  referring to the perceived small size of birds' brains, hence a lack of intelligence.
