counterstrategy:/ˈkaʊntərˌstrætɪdʒi/| n. |对策；反战略|A plan designed to counteract or defeat an opponent's strategy.|例句：The company developed a counterstrategy to compete with their rival's new product.  这家公司制定了一个对策来与竞争对手的新产品竞争。

例句：Their counterstrategy involved a surprise attack. 他们的对策包括一次突袭。|近义词：counterplan, countermeasure, countertactic |反义词:strategy|词性:名词
