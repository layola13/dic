executor:/ɪɡˈzekjətər/ (US), /ɛɡˈzɛkjʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|执行者，遗嘱执行人|A person or institution appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of their will.|The executor of the will was responsible for distributing the assets. 遗嘱执行人负责分配资产。|Synonyms: administrator, trustee, enforcer|Antonyms: beneficiary, heir|noun
