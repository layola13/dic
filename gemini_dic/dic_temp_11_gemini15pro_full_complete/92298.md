hyping:/ˈhaɪpɪŋ/
verb
炒作；大肆宣传
Promoting or publicizing intensely, often exaggerating the merits of something.
They are hyping their new product as the best thing since sliced bread. 他们把他们的新产品吹捧成自切片面包以来最好的东西。
近义词: promoting, publicizing, boosting, exaggerating
反义词: downplaying, understating
词性: verb

hyped:/haɪpt/
adjective
兴奋的；被大肆宣传的
Excited; having been heavily promoted.
I'm so hyped for the concert tonight! 我对今晚的音乐会感到非常兴奋！
近义词: excited, enthusiastic, thrilled
反义词: bored, unenthusiastic, apathetic
词性: adjective

hype:/haɪp/
noun
炒作；大肆宣传
Excessive publicity and promotion.
There's a lot of hype surrounding the new movie. 这部新电影被大肆宣传。
近义词: publicity, promotion, ballyhoo
反义词: understatement
词性: noun


词源:  Shortening of hyperbole (夸大其词), from Greek hyperbolē.
