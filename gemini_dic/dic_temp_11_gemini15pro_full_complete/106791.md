labialization:/ˌleɪbiələɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |唇化，唇音化|The act of making a sound by rounding the lips, as in the pronunciation of [w] or [ʊ].  It's a phonetic process that involves adding lip rounding to a sound that is normally not pronounced with rounded lips.|The labialization of the consonant gives it a softer sound. 这个辅音的唇音化使它的发音更柔和。|近义词:rounding|反义词:unrounding|词性:noun

labialize:/ˈleɪbiəlaɪz/ | v. |使唇化，使成唇音|To pronounce (a sound) with the lips rounded.|He labialized the vowel by rounding his lips. 他通过撅嘴使元音唇化。|近义词:round|反义词:unround|词性:verb

labialized:/ˈleɪbiəlaɪzd/ | adj. |唇化的，唇音化的|Pronounced with the lips rounded.|The "kw" sound in "quick" is a labialized consonant. “quick”中的“kw”音是一个唇音化的辅音。|近义词:rounded|反义词:unrounded|词性:adjective
