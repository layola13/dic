burdensomely:/ˈbɜːrdənsəmli/
adverb
沉重地；繁重地
in a burdensome manner; so as to be difficult to bear.
The regulations were applied burdensomely to small businesses.  这些规章对小企业来说执行起来很繁重。
The heat and humidity made the journey burdensomely slow.  高温和潮湿使得旅程变得异常缓慢。
近义词:oppressively, onerously, heavily
反义词:lightly, easily
词性:adverb

詞源: From burdensome + -ly.  burden (n.)  from Old English byrthen "load, weight, charge, duty," from Proto-Germanic *burthinjo- (source also of Old Norse byrthr, Old Saxon burthina, Middle Dutch borden, Old High German burdi, German Bürde "burden").
