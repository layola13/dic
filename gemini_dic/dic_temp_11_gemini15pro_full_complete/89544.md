honorarium:/ˌɑːnəˈrɛəriəm/ | /ˌɒnəˈrɛəriəm/
n. |酬金，谢礼|A payment given for professional services that are rendered nominally without charge. |The speaker received a small honorarium for his presentation.|Synonym: fee, stipend, payment, compensation|Antonym:  charge, debt, expense
词性: 名词
