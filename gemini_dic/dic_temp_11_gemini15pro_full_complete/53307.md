disability's:/ˌdɪsəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |残疾（状况）的；残障（人士）的|  The possessive form of "disability," referring to something that belongs to or is associated with a disability. disability:/ˌdɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |残疾；残障；无能；无力| A physical or mental condition that limits a person's movements, senses, or activities. |  She has a learning disability. 她有学习障碍。| Synonym: impairment, handicap, incapacity | Antonym: ability, capability | Noun
