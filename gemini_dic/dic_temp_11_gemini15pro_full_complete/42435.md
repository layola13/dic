counterdeclarations:/ˌkaʊntərˌdɛkləˈreɪʃənz/
|n.|反声明，对抗性声明|Statements made in opposition to previous declarations, often to refute or deny them.|The government issued counterdeclarations to the accusations of corruption. 政府发布了反声明来驳斥腐败指控。|Synonyms: rebuttals, denials, contradictions|Antonyms: affirmations, confirmations|noun
