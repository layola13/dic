idolise:/ˈaɪdəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɪdəˌlaɪz/ (US)| vt.|崇拜；把…当作偶像|To admire, revere, or love someone or something to an excessive degree.|She absolutely idolises her older brother. 她绝对崇拜她的哥哥。|
近义词: worship, adore, venerate, exalt|反义词: despise, loathe, abhor|词性: verb

idolization:/ˌaɪdəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌɪdələˈzeɪʃən/ (US)| n.|崇拜；偶像化|The act of idolizing someone or something.|The band enjoys widespread idolization among teenagers.  这个乐队在青少年中广受崇拜。|
近义词: worship, adoration, reverence|反义词: disdain, contempt|词性: noun
