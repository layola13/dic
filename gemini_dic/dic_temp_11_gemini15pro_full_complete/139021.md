oafish:/ˈoʊfɪʃ/| adj.|笨拙的，愚蠢的|Resembling or characteristic of an oaf; clumsy, stupid, or unrefined.|He made an oafish attempt to catch the ball and tripped over his own feet. 他笨手笨脚地试图接住球，结果被自己的脚绊倒了。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, gawky, ungainly, lumbering, loutish, boorish|Antonyms: graceful, elegant, refined, sophisticated|Adjective


oaf:/oʊf/| n.| 笨蛋，蠢货|A stupid, clumsy, or awkward person.|He's such an oaf, always bumping into things. 他真是个笨蛋，总是撞到东西。|Synonyms: lout, boor, churl, yokel, clodhopper|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, sophisticate|Noun
