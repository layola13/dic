lowbrow:/ˈloʊbraʊ/| adj.|低俗的，庸俗的；知识水平低的|Relating to or suitable for a person with little taste or intellectual interest.|That lowbrow comedy really wasn't my cup of tea. 那部低俗的喜剧真的不合我的口味。|Synonyms:uncultured, unrefined, vulgar, tasteless, philistine|Antonyms:highbrow, sophisticated, refined, intellectual, cultured|adjective

lowbrow:/ˈləʊˌbraʊ/| noun.|低级趣味的人；庸俗的人|A person with little taste or intellectual interest.|He dismissed his critics as lowbrows. 他把批评他的人斥为庸俗之辈。|Synonyms:philistine, yahoo|Antonyms:intellectual, highbrow|noun
