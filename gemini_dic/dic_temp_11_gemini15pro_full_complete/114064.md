magnetized:/ˈmæɡnəˌtaɪzd/ | adj. | 已磁化的，带磁性的 | Having been made magnetic; imbued with magnetic properties. | The magnetized needle pointed north. 磁化后的指针指向北方。|  The iron filings were magnetized by the powerful magnet. 铁屑被强力磁铁磁化了。| 近义词: magnetic | 反义词: demagnetized | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From magnetize + -ed, from magnet + -ize. Ultimately from Latin magnes, from Greek Μαγνήτης λίθος (Magnētēs lithos, “Magnesian stone”), referring to magnetite, a magnetic iron ore.
