clinium: /ˈklɪniəm/ | n. |斜面; 倾斜面; 床; |A couch or bed, especially one used in ancient Rome for reclining at meals.  A sloping surface or incline.|The Romans would recline on a clinium while feasting.  罗马人在宴饮时会斜躺在clinium上。| synonyms: couch, bed, chaise lounge, sofa, divan | antonyms: chair, seat (implying upright posture) | noun
