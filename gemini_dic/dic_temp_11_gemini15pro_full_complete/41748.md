corrugated:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. |波纹的；波状的|Having parallel ridges and furrows, like a washboard. |The roof was made of corrugated iron. 屋顶是用波纹铁皮做的。|
corrugated:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. |起皱的|Wrinkled or furrowed. |His brow was corrugated in a frown. 他皱着眉头。|

近义词: ridged, grooved, furrowed, wrinkled, creased
反义词: smooth, flat, even

词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *corrugatus*, past participle of corrugare (“to wrinkle”), from com- (“together”) + rugare (“to wrinkle”), from ruga (“wrinkle”).
