aponeurosis: /ˌæpəˈnʊərəsɪs/, /ˌæpəˈnjʊərəsɪs/ | n. | 腱膜 | A sheet of pearly-white fibrous tissue that takes the place of a tendon in sheetlike muscles having a wide area of attachment. | Example 1: The surgeon carefully repaired the aponeurosis during the hernia operation.  外科医生在疝气手术中仔细地修复了腱膜。

Example 2:  Damage to the abdominal aponeurosis can cause significant pain. 腹部腱膜的损伤会导致剧烈的疼痛。 | Synonym: fascia, tendon | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a body part) | noun
