demilance:/ˈdɛmɪlæns/ | n. | 轻骑枪，半截枪 | A light lance about five feet long, formerly used by light cavalry.  A lance shorter and lighter than a full-sized lance, often used by light cavalry. |  The demilance was the preferred weapon of the  16th-century Polish hussars.  波兰16世纪的骠骑兵喜欢使用轻骑枪。 | The hussar lowered his demilance and charged. 那名骠骑兵放低轻骑枪冲锋陷阵。 | 近义词: lance, spear | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'sword' as a contrasting close-combat weapon) | 词性: noun
