inebriate:/ɪnˈiːbriˌeɪt/ /ɪˈniːbrieɪt/| v.  n.  adj.|使醉；醉汉；醉酒的|To make drunk; to intoxicate. A person who is drunk. Intoxicated.|例句：The strong liquor inebriated the guests quickly.  烈酒很快让客人们醉了。


例句：He was arrested for driving while inebriated. 他因醉酒驾驶被捕。


近义词：intoxicate, drunk, tipsy|反义词:sober, temperate|词性:verb, noun, adjective

inebriate:/ɪnˈiːbriˌeɪt/ /ɪˈniːbrieɪt/| n.|醉汉|A person who is habitually intoxicated.|例句：The inebriate stumbled down the street. 醉汉在街上踉跄而行。


例句：The police picked up the inebriate and took him to the station. 警察把醉汉带到了警察局。


近义词：drunkard, alcoholic|反义词:teetotaler|词性:noun

inebriate:/ɪnˈiːbriˌeɪt/ /ɪˈniːbrieɪt/| adj.|醉酒的|Intoxicated; drunk.|例句：He became quite inebriated after the party. 派对结束后他醉得很厉害。


例句：Her inebriated state made her unable to walk straight. 她醉酒的状态使她无法直线行走。


近义词：drunk, intoxicated, tipsy|反义词:sober|词性:adjective
