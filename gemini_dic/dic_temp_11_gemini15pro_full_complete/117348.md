mediaevalist:/ˌmiːdiˈiːvəlɪst/ |/ˌmɛdiˈiːvəlɪst/| n. |中世纪研究者；中古史学家|A specialist in the history and culture of the Middle Ages.|例句：The mediaevalist spent years studying ancient manuscripts.  这位中世纪研究者花了数年时间研究古代手稿。|例句：She is a renowned mediaevalist specializing in 14th-century literature. 她是一位著名的中世纪研究者，专攻 14 世纪文学。|近义词：medieval scholar, medieval historian|反义词:modernist, contemporary historian|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Medieval + -ist.  "Medieval" comes from Latin "medium aevum" (middle age).
