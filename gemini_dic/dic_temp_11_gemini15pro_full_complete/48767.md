deflagration:/ˌdefləˈɡreɪʃn/ | n. |爆燃；快速燃烧|A rapid combustion, slower than detonation, in which the flame front proceeds through the material at subsonic speed. |The deflagration of the fireworks created a beautiful display. 烟花的爆燃产生了一场美丽的景象。|例句：The deflagration of the gunpowder caused a loud bang. 火药的爆燃发出一声巨响。|近义词：combustion, burning, explosion|反义词：detonation|词性：名词
