corymbose:/kɔːˈrɪmboʊs/ | adj. |伞房状的，聚伞花序的 | Resembling a corymb; arranged in a corymb.  A corymb is a flat-topped or convex flower cluster in which the individual flower stalks arise at different points on the main stem, with the outer flowers opening first. | The yarrow plant has corymbose white flowers.  蓍草开有伞房状的白色小花。 |  Synonyms: corymbous, umbellate | Antonyms: racemose, spicate | Adjective

corymbous:/kɔːˈrɪmbəs/ | adj. |伞房状的，聚伞花序的 |  Same as corymbose.  | The plant displays corymbous inflorescences.  这种植物展现出伞房花序。 | Synonyms: corymbose, umbellate | Antonyms: racemose, spicate | Adjective
