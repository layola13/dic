nonsententiousness:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈtɛnʃəsnəs/
n.
缺乏说教；不爱用格言警句
lack of sententiousness; avoidance of pompous or moralizing pronouncements.
Her nonsententiousness was refreshing in a world full of platitudes.  在一个充满陈词滥调的世界里，她不爱说教的特点令人耳目一新。
nonsententiousness: Her writing style is marked by its clarity and nonsententiousness. 她的写作风格以清晰和不爱说教为特点。

近义词:  artlessness, naturalness, simplicity, unpretentiousness
反义词:  sententiousness, pomposity, moralizing, preachiness
词性: noun
