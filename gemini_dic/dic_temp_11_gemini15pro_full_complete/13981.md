auric:/ˈɔːrɪk/| adj.|金的，金色的；含金的；炼金术的|Relating to gold; containing gold.  Derived from the Latin word "aurum" meaning "gold".|The auric sheen of the sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues.|Synonyms: golden, gold-colored, aurous|Antonyms: silver, argent|adjective

auric:/ˈɔːrɪk/| n.|耳廓|The external part of the ear.|He suffered an injury to his auric cartilage.|Synonyms: pinna, auricle|Antonyms: N/A|noun
