cloudlifter:/ˈklaʊdˌlɪftər/| n. |云提升器，话筒放大器|A device used to boost the signal of low-output microphones, particularly ribbon microphones.|I use a Cloudlifter with my ribbon mic to get a stronger signal. 我用云提升器来增强我的铝带麦克风的信号。|近义词：microphone preamp, mic booster|反义词: attenuator|名词
