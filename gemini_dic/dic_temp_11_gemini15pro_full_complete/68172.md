faint-heartedly:/ˌfeɪntˈhɑːrtɪdli/| adv.|怯懦地，胆怯地|In a manner lacking courage or confidence.|He approached the task faint-heartedly, fearing failure. 他怯懦地开始了这项任务，害怕失败。|She faint-heartedly agreed to go, even though she didn't want to. 她胆怯地同意去了，尽管她并不想去。|近义词：timidly, cowardly, nervously|反义词：boldly, courageously, confidently|词性:副词

faint-hearted:/ˌfeɪntˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|胆小的，怯懦的|Lacking courage or confidence.|He's too faint-hearted to try anything new. 他太胆小了，不敢尝试任何新事物。|She gave a faint-hearted smile. 她胆怯地笑了笑。|近义词：timid, cowardly, fearful|反义词：bold, courageous, brave|词性:形容词

词源:  From "faint" (lacking strength or courage) + "hearted" (having a specified kind of heart).
