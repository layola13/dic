flummery:/ˈflʌməri/ | n. | 糊状甜食；空洞的恭维话；胡言乱语 |A sweet, soft food made with flour, milk, eggs, etc. Empty compliments or flattering nonsense. Meaningless or insincere talk. |例句：She served a delicious flummery for dessert. 她用甜点招待了一份美味的糊状甜食。

例句：His speech was full of political flummery. 他的演讲充满了政治上的空洞恭维。|近义词：nonsense, balderdash, flattery, blandishments, sweet talk |反义词：truth, sincerity, honesty |词性: noun
