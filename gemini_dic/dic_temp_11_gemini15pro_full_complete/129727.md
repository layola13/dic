non-endorsement:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdɔːsmənt/ | n. | 不认可，不背书 |  The company issued a statement of non-endorsement of the candidate's views. | Example:  His silence on the matter was taken as non-endorsement of the policy.  他的对这件事的沉默被认为是不认可这项政策。
