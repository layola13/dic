layaway:/ˈleɪəˌweɪ/ | n. | 分期付款;预留商品 | A system of paying for goods in installments, where the goods are kept by the store until the final payment is made. | I put the new TV on layaway. 我把新电视机分期付款了。| I'm buying a new bike on layaway. 我正在分期付款买一辆新自行车。 | 近义词: installment plan, payment plan | 反义词: outright purchase | noun
