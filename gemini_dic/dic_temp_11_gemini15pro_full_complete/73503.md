foreseeing:/fɔːrˈsiːɪŋ/
adjective
预见的，有远见的
able to predict what will happen or be needed in the future.
The company was foreseeing in its investment in new technology.  这家公司在新技术上的投资很有远见。
She had the foreseeing wisdom to invest her money wisely. 她具有预见的智慧，明智地投资了她的钱。
近义词：predictive, anticipatory, visionary
反义词：shortsighted, myopic
词性：adjective
