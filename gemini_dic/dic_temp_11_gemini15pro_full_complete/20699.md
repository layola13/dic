ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blæd, from Proto-Germanic *bladą (“leaf, blade”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₁- (“to blow, swell, bloom”). Cognate with Old Saxon blad (“leaf, blade”), Old Norse blað (“leaf”), Dutch blad (“leaf, sheet, blade”), German Blatt (“leaf, sheet”), Gothic 𐌱𐌻𐌰𐌳 (blad, “leaf”).
