composes:/kəmˈpoʊzɪz/
verb
组成，构成
to form by putting together; constitute
The committee is composed of ten members. 委员会由十名成员组成。

verb
创作（音乐、诗歌等）
to create or produce (a musical or literary work)
Mozart composed his first symphony at the age of eight. 莫扎特八岁时创作了他的第一部交响曲。

verb
使平静；使镇定
to calm or quiet (one's mind, features, etc.)
She composed herself before entering the room.  她进房间前镇定了一下自己。

近义词: constitute, form, create, write, calm, soothe
反义词: decompose, destroy, agitate
词性: verb
