cathedrals:/kəˈθiːdrəlz/
n.
大教堂（复数）
A cathedral is a large and important church, often the main church of a particular area or diocese.  It is usually where a bishop is based.
The cathedrals of Europe are magnificent examples of Gothic architecture. 欧洲的大教堂是哥特式建筑的宏伟典范。
churches, basilicas
chapels, shrines
noun
