bhagwan:/bʌɡˈwɑːn/| n.|神，上帝，主|A term of respect for a god or a very holy person, primarily used in Hinduism.  It is often used to address or refer to deities like Vishnu, Krishna, Shiva, and others.|例句：The devotees prayed to Bhagwan for blessings. 信徒们向神祈祷以求保佑。

例句：They consider their guru to be a bhagwan. 他们认为他们的古鲁是一位神。|近义词：God, Lord, Deity, Divinity|反义词:Demon, Devil|词性:noun
