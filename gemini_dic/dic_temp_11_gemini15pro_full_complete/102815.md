jargonists:/ˈdʒɑːrɡənɪsts/
n.
行话使用者；术语使用者
A person who uses a lot of jargon, especially obscure or technical terms.
The jargonists in the meeting made it difficult for the layperson to understand what was being discussed.  会议上的那些行话使用者让外行人很难理解他们讨论的内容。
近义词: specialists, experts, professionals, insiders
反义词: laypersons, amateurs, novices
