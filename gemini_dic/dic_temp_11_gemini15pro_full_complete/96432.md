indistinction:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkʃən/ | n. | 模糊；不清晰；无差别 | lack of distinction; lack of clarity or sharpness |  The indistinction between their political positions made it difficult to choose between the candidates.  他们的政治立场模糊不清，难以在候选人之间做出选择。 | Example: The photo was blurred, resulting in an indistinction of features.  照片很模糊，导致五官看不清。| Synonym: blur, vagueness, confusion, obscurity, anonymity | Antonym: clarity, distinction, sharpness, definition, precision | noun

indistinct:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. | 模糊的；不清楚的 | not clearly marked or defined | Example: His speech was indistinct and difficult to understand. 他的讲话含糊不清，难以理解。 | Synonym: blurred, vague, faint, fuzzy, muffled | Antonym: clear, distinct, sharp, precise, definite | adjective
