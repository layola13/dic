joggler:/ˈdʒɑːɡlər/ | n. | 玩杂耍的人；变戏法的人；骗子；耍花招的人 | A person who juggles objects, often as a form of entertainment.  Someone who deftly handles multiple tasks or responsibilities. Can also refer to someone who deceives or manipulates. | He's a skilled juggler, able to keep five balls in the air at once. 他是一个熟练的杂耍演员，可以同时让五个球在空中飞舞。|  The project manager is a real juggler, handling multiple deadlines and client demands.  项目经理真是个高手，能同时处理多个截止日期和客户需求。 | synonyms:  multitasker, manipulator, con artist, trickster | antonyms:  idler, slacker | noun

joggler:/ˈdʒɑːɡlər/ | v. | 玩杂耍；耍弄；欺骗；歪曲 |  To manipulate objects skillfully, typically for entertainment. To manage multiple tasks or responsibilities simultaneously. Can also mean to deceive or manipulate someone. | He juggled three flaming torches, much to the delight of the crowd. 他耍弄着三个燃烧的火把，令观众非常高兴。 | She juggled work, family, and her studies with remarkable efficiency. 她以惊人的效率兼顾工作、家庭和学习。| synonyms: manipulate, handle, manage, deceive | antonyms: neglect, ignore | verb
