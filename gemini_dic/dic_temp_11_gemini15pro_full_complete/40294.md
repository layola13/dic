continuos: This is a misspelling. The correct spelling is **continuous**.

continuous:/kənˈtɪnjuəs/| adj.|连续的，持续的|Forming an unbroken whole; without interruption.|The continuous noise from the construction site was driving me crazy.  建筑工地的持续噪音让我快疯了。|近义词：uninterrupted, incessant, constant, perpetual|反义词:intermittent, sporadic, discontinuous|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *continuus*, from Latin *continēre* "to hold together," from *com-* "together" + *tenēre* "to hold."
