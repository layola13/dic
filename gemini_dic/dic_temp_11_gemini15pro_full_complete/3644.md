agency: /ˈeɪdʒənsi/ | n. | 代理；代理处；中介；作用；力量 |  A business or organization that provides a particular service, typically one that acts as an intermediary between two parties. Also, the capacity, power, or instrumentality of a particular agent in producing a specified effect. |  She found a job through an employment agency. 她通过一家职业介绍所找到了一份工作。

The agency of the river in shaping the landscape is evident.  河流对地貌的塑造作用是显而易见的。 | synonyms: office, bureau, business, company, firm, organization, operation, service, representative, intermediary, action, effect, influence, power | antonyms: inaction, inactivity, powerlessness | noun
