cirque:/sɜːrk/ (US) /səːk/ (UK)| n. |冰斗，圆形剧场|A steep-walled bowl-shaped hollow in a mountain, formed by glacial erosion.|例句：The hikers marveled at the magnificent cirque carved by ancient glaciers.  徒步旅行者对古代冰川雕刻出的壮丽冰斗感到惊叹。

例句：The cirque was filled with wildflowers in the spring. 春天，冰斗里开满了野花。

|近义词：corrie, cwm|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
