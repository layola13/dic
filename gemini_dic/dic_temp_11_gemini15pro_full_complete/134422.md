nonirate:/nɒnˈaɪreɪt/
adjective
无愤怒的；平静的
not feeling or showing anger; calm
He remained nonirate despite the provocation. 他尽管被激怒了，仍然保持平静。
近义词:calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene, composed
反义词:angry, irate, furious, enraged, wrathful
词性:adjective

词源:
"Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "irate" (from Latin "iratus," past participle of "irasci" meaning "to be angry")
