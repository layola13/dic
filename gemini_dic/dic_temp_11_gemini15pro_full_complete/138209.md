nosey:/ˈnəʊzi/ (UK) /ˈnoʊzi/ (US)| adj.|爱管闲事的，好打听的|Inclined to pry into other people's affairs; inquisitive.|例句：She's a bit nosey, always wanting to know everyone's business. 她有点爱管闲事，总是想知道每个人的事。

例句：Don't be so nosey! It's none of your concern. 别这么八卦！这不关你的事。|近义词：inquisitive, curious, prying, meddlesome|反义词：uninterested, indifferent|词性:形容词
