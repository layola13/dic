exposure:/ɪkˈspəʊʒər (UK), /ɪkˈspoʊʒər (US)| n.|暴露，接触，曝光，揭露|The prolonged exposure of the skin to the sun can cause sunburn. 长时间暴露在阳光下会导致晒伤。|例句：

Children's exposure to violence on television is a growing concern.
儿童接触电视暴力是一个日益严重的问题。

He risked exposure to the virus by working with infected patients.
他冒着接触病毒的风险与感染病人一起工作。

|近义词：uncovering, revelation, disclosure, unveiling|反义词：concealment, protection, shelter|词性:noun
