indice:/ˈɪndɪsiː/ (also /ˈɪndaɪsiː/) | n. |指数，指标，索引，迹象|A sign or indication of something.  A number or formula used to measure change in a particular area, such as prices, wages, or the environment.| The consumer price indice rose by 0.5% last month. 消费者物价指数上个月上涨了0.5%。| synonyms: indicator, measure, sign, symptom | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "counter-indicator" or describing the *lack* of an indice) | noun
