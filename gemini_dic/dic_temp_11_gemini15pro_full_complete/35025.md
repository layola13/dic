cloot:/kluːt/
n.|蹄; (尤指牛、羊等的)偶蹄; 布; (尤指作裹尸布的)一块布|A cloot is a hoof, especially of a cow, sheep, or similar animal. It can also refer to a piece of cloth, particularly a shroud.|He carefully examined the cloot of the injured horse. 他仔细检查了受伤马的蹄子。|The body was wrapped in a simple cloot. 尸体裹在一块简单的布里。| 近义词:hoof, cloth, shroud|反义词:|名词

cloot:/kluːt/
v.|用布包裹|To wrap or cover with cloth, especially a shroud.|They clooted the body before burial. 他们下葬前用布包裹了尸体。|She clooted the wound with a clean bandage. 她用干净的绷带包扎了伤口。|近义词:wrap, shroud, cover|反义词:uncover|动词
