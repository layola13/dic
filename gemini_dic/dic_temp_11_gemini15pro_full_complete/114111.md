magnum opus:/ˌmæɡnəm ˈoʊpəs/ | n. | 杰作；代表作 | A large and important work of art, music, or literature, especially one regarded as the most important work of an artist or writer. |  My novel is my magnum opus – ten years in the making. 我的小说是我的杰作——耗时十年创作。| Synonyms: masterpiece, chef-d'œuvre, masterwork, pièce de résistance | Antonyms: failure, flop | noun
