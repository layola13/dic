grovelled: /ˈɡrɒvəld/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːvəld/ (US)| v. | 卑躬屈膝，匍匐，奴颜婢膝 | To behave in a subservient or demeaning manner, often to gain favor or forgiveness. |  He grovelled at the king's feet, begging for mercy. 他匍匐在国王脚下，乞求宽恕。

The defeated boxer grovelled before the champion.  被打败的拳击手在冠军面前卑躬屈膝。

近义词: crawl, fawn, cringe, kowtow | 反义词:  stand up to, defy, resist |词性: 动词
