注意事项:
The term "Burmas" is considered outdated and potentially offensive.  Always use "Burmese" (as an adjective) or "people from Myanmar" when referring to people from Myanmar.  Using "Burma" instead of "Myanmar" can also be politically sensitive, although both are used.  It is generally recommended to use "Myanmar".
