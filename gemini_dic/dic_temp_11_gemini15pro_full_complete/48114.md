declivity:/dɪˈklɪvɪti/
noun
下坡；斜坡
A downward slope.
The hikers struggled up the steep declivity.  徒步旅行者艰难地爬上陡峭的斜坡。
The ball rolled down the declivity.  球滚下山坡。
synonyms: slope, incline, gradient, descent, downslope
antonyms: acclivity, ascent, rise, upgrade
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dēclīvitās (“sloping downwards”), from dēclīvis (“sloping downwards”), from dē (“down”) + clīvus (“slope”).
