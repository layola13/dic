exorcismal:/ˈɛksɔːsɪzməl/ | adj. |驱魔的，驱邪的 | Relating to or characteristic of exorcism.  | The priest performed exorcismal rites.  神父举行了驱魔仪式。 |  近义词:purifying, cleansing | 反义词: demonic,  unholy | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exorcismalis, from exorcismus (“exorcism”) + -alis (adjective suffix).  Exorcismus is from Ancient Greek ἐξορκισμός (exorkismós), from ἐξορκίζω (exorkízō, “I put a spell on, conjure”), from ἐξ- (ex-, “out”) + ὅρκος (hórkos, “oath”).
