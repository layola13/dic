coinventor:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛntər/| n. |共同发明人，共同发明者|A person who invents something jointly with another person or other people.| Marie Curie and her husband, Pierre, were coinventors of radium and polonium. 居里夫人和她的丈夫皮埃尔是镭和钋的共同发明人。|collaborator, joint inventor|  (No single antonym. Could be sole inventor)|noun
