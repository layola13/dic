conserve:/kənˈsɜːrv/ (US), /kənˈsɜːv/ (UK)| v. n.|保护，保存；节省，节约|To protect something from harm or destruction; to avoid wasteful or excessive consumption.|We must conserve our natural resources for future generations. 我们必须为子孙后代保护我们的自然资源.|synonyms: preserve, protect, save, safeguard|antonyms: waste, squander, deplete|verb

conserve:/ˈkɒnsɜːv/ (UK) /kənˈsɝːv/ (US)| n.|蜜饯，果酱|A sweet food made by preserving fruit with sugar.|My grandmother makes delicious strawberry conserve. 我的祖母做美味的草莓酱。|synonyms: jam, preserve, jelly|antonyms: N/A|noun
