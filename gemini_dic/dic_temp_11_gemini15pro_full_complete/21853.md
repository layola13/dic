bodices:/ˈbɒdɪsɪz/
n.
紧身胸衣（bodice的复数）
A close-fitting upper garment for women, covering the chest and back to the waist, typically worn as part of a dress or as lingerie.
The bodices of the dancers' costumes were intricately embroidered. 舞蹈演员服装的紧身胸衣绣工精美。

The queen wore a gown with a jeweled bodice. 女王穿着一件镶有珠宝的紧身胸衣的礼服。

近义词:corsets, stays
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑loose-fitting garments 宽松的服装)
词性: 名词(plural noun)
