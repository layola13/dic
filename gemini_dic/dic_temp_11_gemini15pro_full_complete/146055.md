overdubbing:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌbɪŋ/ | n. |配音；叠录；覆录|The process of recording additional sound onto an existing recording, especially in filmmaking or music production.  Adding a new soundtrack or dialogue over the original one.|The film used overdubbing for the foreign language versions. 这部电影在外语版本中使用了配音。| synonyms: dubbing, rerecording | antonyms: original recording | noun

overdub:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌb/ | vt. |为…配音；在…上加录（音响等）；叠录|To record additional sound onto an existing recording.|They overdubbed the dialogue in English. 他们用英语配上对白。| synonyms: dub, rerecord | antonyms: record originally | verb
