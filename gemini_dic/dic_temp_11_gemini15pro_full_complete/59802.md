ecphrastic:/ɛkˈfræstɪk/
adjective
描述艺术作品的；生动描述的
Relating to or involving the verbal description of a work of visual art.
The poem is an ecphrastic response to a painting by Monet. 这首诗是对莫奈一幅画的描述性回应。
The novel included several ecphrastic passages describing the protagonist's art collection. 这部小说包含几段描述主人公艺术收藏的生动段落。
Synonyms: descriptive, vivid, evocative
Antonyms: inexpressive, inarticulate
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ekphrastikos*, from *ekphrasein* "to describe vividly," from *ek-* "out" + *phrazein* "to tell, declare."
