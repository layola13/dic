floodgate:/ˈflʌdɡeɪt/| n.|防洪闸门; 闸门; (感情等的)发泄途径|A gate used to control the flow of water, especially in a flood barrier.  Figuratively, something that, if opened, allows a large quantity of something else to emerge.|The government's decision to relax planning laws opened the floodgates for developers. 政府放宽规划法的决定为开发商打开了方便之门。|Synonyms: barrier, sluice, gate, valve|Antonyms: opening,  inlet|noun

floodgate:/ˈflʌdɡeɪt/| n.| (感情等的)发泄途径|Something that, if opened, allows a large quantity of something else, especially emotions or information, to emerge.|Her tears opened the floodgates of emotion. 她的眼泪打开了情感的闸门。|Synonyms: outlet, vent, release|Antonyms: suppression, repression|noun
