carcinology:/ˌkɑːrsɪˈnɑːlədʒi/ /ˌkɑːrsɪnˈɑːlədʒi/
noun
甲壳动物学
The scientific study of crustaceans.
Carcinology is a specialized branch of zoology. 甲壳动物学是动物学的一个专门分支。
She is a renowned carcinologist specializing in deep-sea crabs. 她是一位著名的甲壳动物学家，专门研究深海蟹。
近义词:crustaceology,malacostracology
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider fields studying other organisms like entomology or ornithology.)
词性:n.
