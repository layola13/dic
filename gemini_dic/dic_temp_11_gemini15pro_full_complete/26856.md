caisson:/ˈkeɪsɒn/ (UK), /ˈkeɪsɑːn/ (US)| n.|沉箱；弹药箱；（尤指运送棺材的）炮车|A large watertight chamber used for construction work underwater or as a foundation.|例句：The workers entered the caisson to begin repairs on the bridge foundation. 工人们进入沉箱开始桥梁基础的维修工作。

例句：The ammunition was stored in a secure caisson. 弹药存放在一个安全的弹药箱里。|近义词：cofferdam, chamber, box|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open structure" in some contexts)|noun
