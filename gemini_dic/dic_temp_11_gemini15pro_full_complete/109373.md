lensless:/ˈlɛnzlɪs/
adjective
无透镜的
Without a lens.
A lensless camera uses computational imaging techniques to produce images. 无透镜相机使用计算成像技术来生成图像。
The researchers developed a lensless microscope that is small enough to be implanted in the body. 研究人员开发了一种无透镜显微镜，其体积小到可以植入体内。
Synonym: afocal
Antonym: lensed
词性: 形容词
