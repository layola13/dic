miniskirt:/ˈmɪniˌskɜːrt/ (US), /ˈmɪniˌskɜːt/ (UK)| n. |迷你裙|A very short skirt, typically one reaching several inches above the knee.|She wore a denim miniskirt. 她穿了一条牛仔迷你裙。|近义词：short skirt|反义词:maxi skirt|词性:名词

Etymology:
mini- (prefix meaning "small") + skirt.  The term "miniskirt" originated in the 1960s in London.  It was named for its drastically shorter length compared to the prevailing skirt styles of the time.  "Mini" was already a popular slang term for small cars (like the Mini Cooper) and other small items.
