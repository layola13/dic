neoner:/ˈniːɑːnər/ | n. |  新手；网络新手 | Someone new to the internet or a particular online community, often characterized by naivete or lack of understanding of online norms and etiquette. |  Example: He posted way too much personal information – what a neoner! 他发布了太多的个人信息——真是个网络菜鸟！

Example:  The forum is full of neoners asking basic questions.  这个论坛上到处都是问基本问题的网络新手。 | Near synonyms: newbie, noob, beginner, rookie, greenhorn | Antonyms: expert, veteran, pro, guru | Noun
