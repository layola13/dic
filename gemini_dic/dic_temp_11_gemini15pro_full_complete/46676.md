damper's:/ˈdæmpərz/
n.
阻尼器，减震器，调节器，（钢琴等乐器的）制音器
A device that reduces vibrations or oscillations, or regulates airflow.
The damper's effectiveness was compromised by rust.  阻尼器的效用因生锈而受损。
The piano tuner adjusted the dampers.  钢琴调音师调整了制音器。
Synonyms: muffler, mute, absorber, control
Antonyms: amplifier, accelerator
词性: 名词 (Noun)
