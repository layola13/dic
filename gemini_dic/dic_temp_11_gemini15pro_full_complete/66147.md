exceptive:/ɪkˈsɛptɪv/ | adj. | 例外的；除外的 | Forming an exception; excepting. Making or constituting an exception.  | This rule is exceptive to the general principle. 这条规则是普遍原则的例外。 |  All members are included, exceptive of those who have resigned. 所有成员都包括在内，除了那些已经辞职的。 | 近义词: exceptional, excluding, except, excepting | 反义词: inclusive, including | 词性: adjective

exceptive:/ɪkˈsɛptɪv/ | n. | 例外 | Something that is excepted. |  There are few exceptives to this rule. 这条规则几乎没有例外。 | The exceptives to the policy are clearly stated. 该政策的例外情况已明确说明。 | 近义词: exception | 反义词: norm, rule | 词性: noun
