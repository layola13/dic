overrestraints:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstreɪnts/| n. |过度限制，过度约束|Excessive restraints or limitations; too many rules or restrictions. |The overrestraints placed on the company by the government stifled innovation. 政府对公司施加的过度限制扼杀了创新。|
The overrestraints on children's playtime can hinder their social development. 对孩子们游戏时间的过度限制会阻碍他们的社会发展。|
近义词：restrictions, limitations, constraints, curbs|反义词:freedom, liberty, license|词性:名词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "restraint" (from Old French restreinte, from Latin restringere "to bind back, hold in check").
