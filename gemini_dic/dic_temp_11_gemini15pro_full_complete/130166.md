non-returnable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈtɜːnəbl/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈtɜːrnəbl/| adj.|不可退货的，不可退还的|Not able to be returned, especially of goods to a shop.|This ticket is non-returnable. 这张票是不能退的。|近义词：nonrefundable|反义词: returnable|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + returnable (adjective from the verb "return")  词源: non-（前缀，意思是“不”）+ returnable（形容词，来自动词“return”）
