goud:/ɣut/ (Dutch)| adj.| good, golden |Good, of high quality; made of gold.  This word is primarily used in Dutch.  It doesn't exist in standard English.  If you encountered it in an English text, it's likely borrowed directly from Dutch. | Dat is een goud idee! (Dutch) = That's a good idea! | 近义词：(In Dutch) goed, uitstekend (excellent) | 反义词：(In Dutch) slecht (bad)| adjective
