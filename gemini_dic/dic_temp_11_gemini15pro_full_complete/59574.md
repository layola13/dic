ebonies:/ˈɛbəniz/
n.
黑檀树; 黑檀木;  (黑人妇女的)乌黑秀发
a very dark black color, like the wood of the ebony tree.  Ebony is also used to refer to objects made from ebony wood, or to the ebony tree itself, especially several tropical trees of the genus Diospyros.  Figuratively, it can refer to the dark black hair of a black woman.
例句：The antique table was made of polished ebonies. 这张古董桌子是用抛光的黑檀木制成的。

例句：Her ebonies cascaded down her back. 她的乌黑秀发倾泻而下。
近义词：blackwood, darkwood
反义词：ivory, bone (referring to color or material)
词性：noun
