bitmap:/ˈbɪtmæp/ | n. | 位图 | A digital image composed of a matrix of dots called pixels. Each pixel is assigned a specific color and location, which together form the complete image. |  The image was saved as a bitmap.  这张图片保存为位图格式。| synonyms: raster image, pixel map | antonyms: vector graphic | noun

bitmap:/ˈbɪtmæp/ | adj. | 位图的 | Relating to or characteristic of a bitmap image. |  A bitmap image is made up of pixels.  位图图像由像素组成。 | synonyms: raster, pixel-based | antonyms: vector | adjective
