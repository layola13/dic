helltown:/ˈhɛlˌtaʊn/
n.
地狱小镇;鬼城; 非常糟糕或令人不快的地方
A place perceived as dreary, desolate, or frightening; a place with a bad reputation.
例句：They described the abandoned mining town as a real helltown. 他们把那个废弃的矿镇描述成一个真正的地狱小镇。

例句：After the factory closed, the town became a helltown with high unemployment and crime. 工厂倒闭后，这个镇变成了一个失业率高、犯罪率高的鬼城。

近义词：ghost town, badlands, slum
反义词：paradise, utopia
词性：名词
