come-to-naught:/kʌm tə nɔːt/
verb phrase
化为乌有；失败；落空
to fail to produce a successful result
All his schemes came to naught. 他所有的计划都落空了。
Synonyms: fail, fall through, come to nothing, miscarry
Antonyms: succeed, flourish, thrive
