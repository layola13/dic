intermediae:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdi.iː/
adjective
中间的，中级的
situated or occurring between two extremes or in a middle position or state.
The reaction occurs via an intermediate compound. 该反应通过一种中间化合物发生。
近义词:middle, intermediate, median, average, mean
反义词:extreme, ultimate, terminal
词性:adjective

intermedia:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdi.ə/
noun
中间物，媒介物
a thing that conveys something else, such as a message or information.
The enzyme acts as an intermedia in the reaction. 这种酶在反应中充当媒介物。
近义词:medium, agent, vehicle, intermediary
反义词:extremity
词性:noun

词源: From Latin intermedius, from inter- 'between' + medius 'middle'.
