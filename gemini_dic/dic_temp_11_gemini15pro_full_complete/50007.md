denning:/ˈdɛnɪŋ/| n. |冬眠；兽穴|The act of a wild animal, especially a bear, using or resting in its den.  A bear's denning period is crucial for its survival during winter.|The bear was denning in a cave high up in the mountains.  那只熊在高山上的一个洞穴里冬眠。|近义词：hibernating, sheltering|反义词:awakening|noun

denning:/ˈdɛnɪŋ/| v. |（野生动物，尤指熊）在兽穴中过冬；冬眠|The action of an animal, especially a bear, using or resting in its den, typically during the winter.|The bears are denning for the winter. 熊正在冬眠过冬。|近义词：hibernating, holing up|反义词:emerging|verb
