hawala:/həˈwɑːlə/ | n. | 哈瓦拉，一种非正式的资金转移系统 | An informal money transfer system based on trust and often used in the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia, operating outside of traditional banking systems. It involves a network of brokers (hawaladars) who facilitate the transfer of funds through promises and record-keeping rather than physical movement of cash. | Example 1:  The migrant workers used hawala to send money back to their families.  移民工人使用哈瓦拉系统把钱寄回家。

Example 2:  Hawala is often preferred for its speed and lower fees compared to traditional banking.  与传统银行相比，哈瓦拉因其速度快、费用低而更受青睐。 | Synonym:  underground banking, informal remittance system | Antonym:  formal banking system | Noun
