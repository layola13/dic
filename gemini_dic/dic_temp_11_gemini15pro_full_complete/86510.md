heirloom:/ˈɛərˌluːm/| n.|传家宝，祖传遗物|A valuable object that has belonged to a family for several generations.|My grandmother left me a diamond ring as an heirloom. 我的祖母留给我一枚钻石戒指作为传家宝。|Synonyms: inheritance, legacy, heritage|Antonyms: new purchase, modern acquisition|Noun

heirloom:/ˈɛərˌluːm/| adj.|祖传的，世代相传的|Passed down in a family through succeeding generations.|This heirloom tomato variety has been in my family for over 100 years.  这种祖传的番茄品种在我家已经有一百多年了。|Synonyms: ancestral, inherited, traditional|Antonyms: modern, contemporary, new|Adjective
