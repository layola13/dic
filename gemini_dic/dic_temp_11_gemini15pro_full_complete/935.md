abyssal:/əˈbɪsl/| adj.|深渊的；深不可测的|Relating to or denoting the depths of the ocean (2,000-6,000 metres down), where sunlight does not penetrate.|The anglerfish lives in the abyssal zone of the ocean.  琵琶鱼生活在海洋的深渊带。|近义词：profound, deep, unfathomable|反义词：shallow, superficial|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *abyssal*, from Latin *abyssus*, from Greek *ábyssos* meaning "bottomless," from *a-"* (without) + *byssos* (bottom).
