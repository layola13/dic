augur:/ˈɔːɡər/| v.  n.|预示，预兆; 占卜师|
(v.) To predict, especially from signs or omens; to be a sign or omen of.
(n.) A soothsayer; a prophet.|
(v.) The flight of birds augured ill for their journey. 鸟儿的飞行预示着他们的旅程不顺利。
(n.) The augur interpreted the emperor's dream. 占卜师解释了皇帝的梦。|近义词：(v.)foretell, presage, portend, foreshadow (n.) soothsayer, prophet, seer|反义词: (v.) belie, contradict|verb, noun
