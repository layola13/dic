bronzing:/ˈbrɒnzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːnzɪŋ/ (US)| n. |古铜色，晒黑，青铜化|The process of becoming bronze or bronze-colored, either by tanning, applying a cosmetic product, or developing a bronze patina on a metal.|She achieved a deep bronzing after a week in the Bahamas. 她去了巴哈马一周后，晒成了很深的古铜色。|近义词：tanning, sunbathing|反义词：blanching, whitening|词性:名词

bronzing:/ˈbrɒnzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːnzɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |青铜色的，用于晒黑的|Having a bronze color or used for creating a bronze color.|She used a bronzing lotion to achieve a sun-kissed look. 她用了一种美黑乳液来打造阳光亲吻的效果。|近义词：bronze, tanned|反义词：pale, white|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bronze + -ing.  "Bronze" ultimately comes from the Old Persian word *birinj*, meaning "copper".
