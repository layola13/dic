outplays:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪz/
verb
以更高的技巧战胜，胜过；在比赛中胜过
to defeat (someone) by playing with greater skill or cleverness
例句：She completely outplayed her opponent in the tennis match. 她在网球比赛中完全胜过了她的对手。

例句：The experienced team outplayed the newcomers, winning by a large margin.  经验丰富的团队胜过了新来者，以很大的优势获胜。

近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, defeat, beat, triumph over
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
