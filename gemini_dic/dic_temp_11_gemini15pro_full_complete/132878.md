nonelasticities: /ˌnɒnɪˌlæˈstɪsɪtiz/ /ˌnɑːnɪˌlæˈstɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 非弹性 | The lack of elasticity; the state or quality of being nonelastic.  A measure of how unresponsive the quantity demanded (or supplied) is to a change in price, income, or other determinant.|  High nonelasticities of demand for certain goods make them good targets for taxation.  某些商品的需求非弹性很高，使它们成为征税的好目标。| inelasticity, rigidity | elasticity, flexibility | noun
