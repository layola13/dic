overpackaging:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒɪŋ/
noun
过度包装
Excessive use of packaging materials, often beyond what is necessary to protect or contain a product.
The company has been criticized for its overpackaging of products.  这家公司因产品过度包装而受到批评。
Many consumers are concerned about the environmental impact of overpackaging. 许多消费者担心过度包装对环境的影响。
excessive packaging, wasteful packaging
minimal packaging, eco-friendly packaging
