lest:/lɛst/
conjunction
唯恐，以免
for fear that; so as to prevent
例句：She spoke softly lest she should wake the baby. 她轻声说话，以免吵醒婴儿。

例句：He gripped his son's hand lest he get lost. 他紧紧抓住儿子的手，以免他走丢。

近义词：in case, for fear that, so that...not
反义词:  (lest 没有直接的反义词，表达相反意思需要用肯定句式)
词性: conjunction


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *þȳ læs þe, *þȳ læs, from Proto-Germanic *þiz laisizai.  Related to less. Originally meaning "by that less that."
