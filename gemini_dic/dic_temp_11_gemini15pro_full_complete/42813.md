courageous:/kəˈreɪdʒəs/| adj.|勇敢的，有胆量的|Possessing or showing courage; brave.|例句：She was courageous enough to stand up to the bully. 她勇敢地站出来反抗恶霸。|近义词：brave, valiant, bold, daring, fearless, intrepid, heroic|反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful, afraid|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Middle English *coragious, from Old French corageus (“brave, spirited”), from corage (“heart, spirit, courage”), from Vulgar Latin *corāticum (“of the heart”), from Latin cor (“heart”).
