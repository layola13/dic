index-linked:/ˈɪndɛks lɪŋkt/| adj.|指数挂钩的，与指数相关的|Linked to an index, usually a price index, so that the value rises and falls as the index rises and falls. |例句：Index-linked investments protect savers against inflation. 指数挂钩的投资可以保护储户免受通货膨胀的影响。

例句：Many pensioners rely on index-linked pensions to maintain their standard of living. 许多养老金领取者依靠与指数挂钩的养老金来维持他们的生活水平。|近义词：inflation-protected, inflation-adjusted|反义词:fixed-rate|词性:形容词
