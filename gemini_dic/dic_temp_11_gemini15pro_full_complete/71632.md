flatlong:/ˈflætˌlɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈflætˌlɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|单间公寓；一室公寓|A self-contained flat, typically on a single floor of a larger building, comprising a combined living room and bedroom, a separate kitchen, and a bathroom.|I'm living in a flatlong near the city center. 我住在市中心附近的一套单间公寓里。|Synonym: studio apartment, one-bedroom apartment|Antonym: house, multi-bedroom apartment|Noun
