hardwood:/ˈhɑːrdˌwʊd/| n. |硬木；阔叶树木材|Wood from a broad-leaved tree (such as oak, ash, or elm) as distinguished from a conifer.|The floor is made of beautiful hardwood.| 近义词:timber, lumber|反义词:softwood|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hard + wood.  "Hard" meaning dense and firm, and "wood" referring to the material of trees.
