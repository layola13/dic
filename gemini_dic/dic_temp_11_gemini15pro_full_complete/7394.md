animatedly:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪdli/
adverb
活泼地，生气勃勃地
in a lively and enthusiastic manner, full of life and energy
She spoke animatedly about her trip to Italy. 她充满活力地讲述了她的意大利之旅。
The children were talking animatedly about the upcoming holiday. 孩子们兴致勃勃地谈论着即将到来的假期。
近义词: enthusiastically, excitedly, spiritedly, vibrantly, energetically
反义词: listlessly, apathetically, dully, lifelessly
词性: adverb

词源: From the Latin word "anima" meaning "soul" or "breath," which became "animatus" meaning "alive" or "spirited". The English word "animate" derives from this, and "animatedly" is the adverbial form.
