gonorrhoea:/ˌɡɒnəˈrɪə/ (UK) /ˌɡɑːnəˈriːə/ (US)| n. |淋病|A sexually transmitted bacterial infection caused by Neisseria gonorrhoeae, characterized by inflammation of the genital mucous membrane, often with purulent discharge.|Gonorrhoea can cause serious health problems if left untreated.  淋病如果不及时治疗会导致严重的健康问题。|Gonorrhoea is a common sexually transmitted infection (STI). 淋病是一种常见的性传播感染。|近义词:clap (informal), the drip (informal)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease, but one could consider "health" in a broad sense) |词性:noun
