mosa:/ˈmoʊzə/ | n. | Mosaic | A picture or pattern produced by arranging together small colored pieces of hard material, such as stone, tile, or glass. | Example: The artist created a beautiful mosa depicting a Roman scene.  艺术家创作了一幅描绘罗马场景的美丽马赛克。| Synonym: tessellation, inlay | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "monochrome" if contrasting with the multicolored nature of mosaics) | noun
