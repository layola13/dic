hyalotype: /ˈhaɪəloʊˌtaɪp/ | n. | 透明体，玻璃体模型 | A clear, glass-like representation of the vitreous humor of the eye.  It is formed when the vitreous humor detaches from the retina and shrinks, leaving a mold of its former shape. |  The ophthalmologist examined the patient's hyalotype after the posterior vitreous detachment. 眼科医生在玻璃体后脱离后检查了患者的透明体。|  The hyalotype showed signs of retinal tears. 透明体显示出视网膜撕裂的迹象。 | synonyms: vitreous cast, vitreous mold | antonyms: N/A | noun
