menologium:/ˌmenəˈloʊɡiəm/
n.
圣人传, 圣人行状录
A book containing accounts of the lives of the saints, arranged according to the calendar day of their commemoration.
The priest consulted the menologium to learn more about the saint of the day.  神父查阅了圣人传，想了解更多关于今日圣徒的信息。
The menologium detailed the miracles performed by Saint Nicholas. 圣人传详细记载了圣尼古拉斯所行的奇迹。
Synonyms: martyrology, calendar of saints
Antonyms: None
Noun
