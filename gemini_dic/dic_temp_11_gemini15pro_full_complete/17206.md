bauxitic:/bɔːkˈsɪtɪk/
adjective
铝土矿的;似铝土矿的
Relating to, resembling, or containing bauxite.
The bauxitic soil is rich in aluminum. 这种铝土矿土壤富含铝。
The company mines bauxitic ore. 这家公司开采铝土矿石。
Synonyms: aluminous, lateritic
Antonyms: non-bauxitic
