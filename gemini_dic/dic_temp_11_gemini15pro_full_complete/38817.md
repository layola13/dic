cone:/koʊn/ (US) /kəʊn/ (UK)| n.|锥形，圆锥体；球果；蛋卷筒|A solid or hollow object that tapers from a circular or roughly circular base to a point.|The ice cream dripped down the cone. 冰淇淋顺着蛋卷筒滴下来。|

The pine tree had many cones. 松树上有很多球果。|近义词:pyramid, taper|反义词:sphere, cube|词性:noun


cone:/koʊn/ (US) /kəʊn/ (UK)| v.|使成锥形|To give a cone shape to something.|They coned the traffic using orange markers. 他们用橙色标记物将交通限制在锥形区域内。|近义词:taper, narrow|反义词:expand, widen|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cone*, from Latin *conus*, from Greek *kōnos* meaning "cone, pine cone".
