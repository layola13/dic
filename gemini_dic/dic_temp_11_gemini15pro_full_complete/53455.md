disapprove:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːv/ | v. | 不赞成，反对 | to have or express an unfavorable opinion of |  I strongly disapprove of his actions. 我强烈反对他的行为。

The school disapproves of students smoking. 学校不赞成学生吸烟。

| 近义词: object to, condemn, deprecate, criticize | 反义词: approve, endorse, support, commend | 词性: 动词
