forswore:/fɔːrˈswɔːr/
verb
放弃，发誓放弃
renounced; repudiated (something, especially under oath)
He forswore all worldly possessions. 他放弃了所有的世俗财产。
近义词: renounce, abjure, repudiate
反义词: affirm, uphold, embrace
词性: verb (past tense of forswear)
