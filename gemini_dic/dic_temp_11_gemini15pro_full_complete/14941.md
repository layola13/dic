azureous:/ˈæʒərəs/| adj.|天蓝色的，蔚蓝色的|Of a light, purplish shade of blue, resembling the clear sky.|The azureous waters of the Mediterranean Sea sparkled in the sunlight. 地中海蔚蓝的海水在阳光下波光粼粼。|近义词：azure, cerulean, sky-blue|反义词:dark, black|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *azureus*, from Medieval Latin *azureus*, from Arabic *lāzaward*, from Persian *lāžaward* (lapis lazuli). Ultimately from Sanskrit *rājavarta* (king's enclosure).
