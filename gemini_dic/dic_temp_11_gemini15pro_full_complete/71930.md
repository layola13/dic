flicked:/flɪkt/| v. |轻弹，轻拂，快速地移动|A quick, light movement, often with a finger.  She flicked the dust off her jacket. 她轻弹掉夹克上的灰尘。|近义词：flip, whisk, dart, flit |反义词: press, hold |verb

flicked:/flɪkt/| v. |轻击，轻抽|To hit something lightly with a whip, stick, or finger. The rider flicked the horse with his whip. 骑手用鞭子轻抽马。|近义词：tap, strike, whip |反义词: caress, stroke |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Proto-Germanic *flikōną (“to move quickly”), from Proto-Indo-European *pleḱ- (“to plait, to turn”). Related to flitch.
