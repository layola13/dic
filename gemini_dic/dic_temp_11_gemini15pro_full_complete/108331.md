lavage:/ləˈvɑːʒ/ (US), /ˈlævɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. |灌洗，洗，冲洗|The act or process of washing or cleansing a body cavity, such as the stomach or bowel.|Gastric lavage was performed to remove the ingested toxins. 对病人进行了胃灌洗以清除摄入的毒素。|Synonyms: washing, cleaning, rinsing|Antonyms: soiling, dirtying|noun

lavage:/ləˈvɑːʒ/ (US), /ˈlævɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. |洗涤液，灌洗液|A solution used for washing or irrigating a body cavity.|The lavage fluid was analyzed for the presence of bacteria.  对灌洗液进行了细菌检测。|Synonyms: wash, solution, irrigant|Antonyms: N/A|noun
