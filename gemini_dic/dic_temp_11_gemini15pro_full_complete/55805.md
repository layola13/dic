divertive: /daɪˈvɜːrtɪv/ /dɪˈvɜːrtɪv/
adjective
娱乐的，消遣的，转移注意力的
Tending to divert or amuse; entertaining.
例句：
The game proved to be a divertive pastime.  这个游戏被证明是一种娱乐性的消遣。

She found his stories highly divertive. 她觉得他的故事非常有趣。

近义词：amusing, entertaining, diverting, distracting
反义词：boring, tedious, monotonous, dull
词性：adjective


词源:
From Late Latin dīvertīvus, from Latin dīvertō (I turn aside, divert).  See divert.
