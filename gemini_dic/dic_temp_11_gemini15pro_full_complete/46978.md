darwinian:/dɑːrˈwɪniən/ | adj. | 达尔文的；达尔文主义的；适者生存的 | Of or relating to Charles Darwin, his theories, or his followers, especially the theory of evolution by natural selection. |  Darwinian evolution is a cornerstone of modern biology. 达尔文进化论是现代生物学的基石。

The company operates on a Darwinian principle, where only the strongest survive.  这家公司奉行适者生存的达尔文式原则。| 近义词：evolutionary, natural selection | 反义词: creationist | 词性:形容词

词源: From the name of Charles Darwin (1809-1882), English naturalist who proposed the theory of evolution by natural selection.
