contortionist:/kənˈtɔːrʃənɪst/ | n. | 柔术演员，扭曲主义者 | A person, especially a performer, who can twist their body into unusual positions. | The contortionist amazed the audience with her incredible flexibility.  柔术演员以她惊人的柔韧性令观众赞叹不已。| synonym: acrobat, gymnast | antonym: stiff person | noun

contortionists:/kənˈtɔːrʃənɪsts/ | n. | 柔术演员们，扭曲主义者们 | Plural form of contortionist.  | The contortionists performed a series of breathtaking bends and twists.  柔术演员们表演了一系列令人叹为观止的弯曲和扭转动作。| synonym: acrobats, gymnasts | antonym: stiff people | noun
