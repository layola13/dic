misthrow:/ˌmɪsˈθroʊ/
verb
投掷失误，投偏
To throw (something) inaccurately or unsuccessfully.
The quarterback misthrew the ball, and the receiver couldn't catch it.  四分卫投球失误，接球手没能接住。
miscast, fumble
hit, catch
