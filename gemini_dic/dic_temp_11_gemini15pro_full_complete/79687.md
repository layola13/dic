glower:/ˈɡlaʊər/ (US) /ˈɡlaʊə(r)/ (UK)| vi. n.| 怒视，瞪眼；阴沉地盯着|To look or stare angrily or sullenly.|He glowered at her across the table. 他隔着桌子怒视着她。|近义词：scowl, glare, frown|反义词：smile, beam|词性:动词, 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *glouren, *glowren, probably of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch gluren (“to leer, stare”), German glotzen (“to stare”).
