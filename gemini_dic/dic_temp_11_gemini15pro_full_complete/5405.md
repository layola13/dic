almanzors:/ˌælˈmænzɔːrz/ | n. | 阿尔曼佐尔人，阿尔曼佐尔王朝 |  A medieval Berber dynasty that ruled parts of North Africa and the Iberian Peninsula.  They are also referred to as the Almohads. | The Almanzors built many impressive mosques and palaces. 阿尔曼佐尔人建造了许多令人印象深刻的清真寺和宫殿。| The Almohad Caliphate, under rulers like Ya'qub al-Mansur, experienced a golden age. 在像雅各布·阿尔曼苏尔这样的统治者的领导下，阿莫哈德哈里发经历了一个黄金时代。 | synonyms: Almohads | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific dynasty.  Could consider opposing forces/dynasties of the time.) | noun
