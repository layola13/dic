lavine: /ləˈviːn/ | n. | 雪崩 | A large mass of snow, ice, and rocks falling rapidly down a mountainside. | The skiers were caught in a sudden lavine. 滑雪者突然遭遇了雪崩。 |  The avalanche roared down the mountain. 雪崩咆哮着冲下山。 | 近义词: avalanche, snowslide | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用stability表示山体的稳定) | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *lavine*, from Franco-Provençal *lavina*, from Vulgar Latin *labīna* (“landslide”), from Latin *lābēs* (“a sliding, falling down”).
