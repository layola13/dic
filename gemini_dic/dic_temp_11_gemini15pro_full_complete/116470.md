material:/məˈtɪəriəl/ /məˈtɪriəl/| n. adj.|材料，物质；素材，资料；布料|a solid substance from which things can be made or which has a particular use|例句：The building materials are expensive. 建筑材料很贵。|近义词：substance, fabric, stuff, data, information|反义词:immaterial, spiritual|noun

material:/məˈtɪəriəl/ /məˈtɪriəl/| adj.|物质的；重要的；实质性的|relating to physical objects, as opposed to spiritual or intellectual matters; important; essential; relevant|例句：Material possessions do not bring happiness. 物质财富并不能带来幸福。|近义词：physical, tangible, important, significant, relevant|反义词:immaterial, spiritual, unimportant, insignificant, irrelevant|adjective
