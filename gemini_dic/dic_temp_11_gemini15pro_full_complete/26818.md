cageyness:/ˈkeɪdʒinəs/
n.
谨慎, 拘谨, 吞吞吐吐
The quality of being cautious, secretive, and unwilling to give information.
He answered questions with his characteristic cageyness. 他以其特有的谨慎回答了问题。
近义词：reticence, reserve, guardedness, secrecy
反义词：openness, frankness, candor
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From cagey + -ness.  "Cagey" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "cagey" meaning wary or shrewd, perhaps influenced by the image of a caged animal being wary of its surroundings.
