equability:/ˌiːkwəˈbɪləti/
n.
平静；均衡；稳定性
The equability of his temperament made him a good mediator.
他的性情平和使他成为一名优秀的调解员。
Evenness of temper or behavior; composure; calmness.  A state of being unchanging; uniformity.
The equability of the tropical climate.
热带气候的稳定性。
近义词:stability, steadiness, evenness, composure, calmness, uniformity
反义词:instability, volatility, variability, unevenness, agitation


equabilities:/ˌiːkwəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
平静；均衡；稳定性（复数）
The equabilities of the climate in this region are remarkable.
这个地区气候的稳定性非常显著。
Plural form of equability, referring to multiple instances of evenness, calmness, or stability.
The report highlighted the equabilities of temperature and rainfall throughout the year.
该报告强调了全年气温和降雨量的稳定性。
近义词:stabilities, steadinesses, evennesses, composures, calmness, uniformities
反义词:instabilities, volatilities, variabilities, unevennesses, agitations


词源:
From Late Latin aequabilitatem, from aequabilis "equable," from aequare "to make equal," from aequus "equal."
