nean:/niːn/ | adj. | 吝啬的，卑鄙的; 平均的; (统计)平均数的|  Mean can describe someone who is unkind, selfish, or unwilling to share. It can also refer to an average value in mathematics or statistics. | He was a mean and spiteful man. 他是一个刻薄而恶毒的人。| The mean temperature for July is 25 degrees Celsius. 七月份的平均气温是25摄氏度。| synonyms: stingy, miserly, ungenerous, average| antonyms: generous, kind, lavish, above average | adjective
