mis-sampling:/ˌmɪsˈsæmplɪŋ/
n.|错误采样，抽样偏差|The mis-sampling of the population skewed the results of the study.  人口抽样偏差导致研究结果出现偏差。|
近义词: sampling bias, sampling error
反义词: representative sampling, accurate sampling
词性: 名词


Etymology:
mis- (prefix):  From Old English *mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly, badly, astray," from Proto-Indo-European *mei- (1) "to change, go, move."  Meaning "bad, wrong."
sampling (noun): from sample (verb) "to take a sample of," 1827, from sample (n.).
