gasoline:/ˈɡæsəliːn/ (US) /ˈpɛtrəl/ (UK)| n.|汽油|A flammable liquid derived from petroleum, used as fuel in internal combustion engines.|例句：I need to fill up the car with gasoline. 我需要给车加满汽油。

例句：The price of gasoline has gone up again. 汽油价格又上涨了。|近义词：petrol (UK), fuel|反义词: none|词性: noun
