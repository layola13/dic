knack:/næk/
n.
诀窍，技巧
A special skill or ability that you have naturally or can learn easily.
She has a knack for making people feel comfortable. 她有一种让人感到舒适的诀窍。
talent, gift, aptitude, skill
ineptitude, inability

knacks:/næks/
n.
诀窍，技巧（knack的复数形式）
Special skills or abilities that you have naturally or can learn easily.
He has a few knacks that make him a valuable employee. 他有一些诀窍，这使他成为一名宝贵的员工。
talents, gifts, aptitudes, skills
ineptitudes, inabilities
