descension:/dɪˈsɛnʃən/
n.
下降，下倾;屈尊，俯就
an act of descending; the process of descending.  A coming down from a higher place to a lower one. Also: an instance of this.  Condescension.
The hikers began their slow descension from the mountain peak.  徒步旅行者开始从山顶缓慢下降。
synonyms: descent, decline, sinking, drop, fall
antonyms: ascent, rise, climb
