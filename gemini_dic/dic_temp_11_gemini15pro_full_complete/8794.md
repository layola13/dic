antielitist:/ˌæntiɪˈliːtɪst/
adjective
反精英主义的；反对精英主义的
Opposed to or hostile towards elitism or elites.
The candidate ran on an antielitist platform, appealing to working-class voters.  这位候选人以反精英主义的纲领进行竞选，吸引了工人阶级的选民。
The antielitist movement gained traction as people grew tired of being governed by a small, privileged group. 反精英主义运动随着人们厌倦了被一小群特权阶层统治而获得了支持。
synonyms: egalitarian, populist, democratic
antonyms: elitist, aristocratic, snobbish
