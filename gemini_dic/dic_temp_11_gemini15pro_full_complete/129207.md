noblest:/ˈnoʊblɪst/| adj.|最高贵的，最崇高的|Having or showing fine personal qualities or high moral principles and ideals.|He made the noblest sacrifice for his country. 他为了国家做出了最崇高的牺牲。|近义词：grandest, worthiest, most honorable|反义词：basest, meanest, vilest|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *noblest*, superlative of *noble*, from Old French *noble*, from Latin *nobilis* (“noble, well-known, famous, excellent, splendid, magnificent”), from *nosco* (“I get to know”).
