bondes:/ˈbɒndɪz/| n.|农奴，佃农|A peasant who is legally bound to the land and subject to the will of its owner.|The bondes worked hard to cultivate the lord's land. 佃农们辛勤耕耘着领主的土地。|Synonyms: peasant, serf, villein|Antonyms: freeman, lord, landowner|noun

bondes:/ˈbɒndɪz/| n.|债券（bond的复数形式，较少用）|Multiple bonds issued by a government or corporation to raise money.|The company issued bondes to finance its expansion. 公司发行债券为其扩张融资。|Synonyms: bonds, securities, debentures|Antonyms: loans, credits|noun
