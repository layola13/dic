criticist: /ˈkrɪtɪsɪst/ | n. |批评家，评论家| A person who judges the merits of literary, artistic, or musical works, especially one who does so professionally. | The critic praised the film's innovative cinematography.  这位影评人称赞了这部电影的创新摄影。 | Synonyms: reviewer, judge, assessor, evaluator | Antonyms: admirer, supporter, advocate | noun
