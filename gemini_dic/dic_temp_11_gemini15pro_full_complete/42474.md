counterfeisance:/ˌkaʊntərˈfiːzəns/
n.|伪造；赝品；假冒|The act of counterfeiting; forgery.  The creation of a fraudulent imitation. A spurious imitation or copy.|The police uncovered a counterfeisance operation producing fake designer handbags.|近义词：forgery, imitation, fake, sham, fraud|反义词：authenticity, genuineness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *contrefaisance, from contrefaire "to imitate," from contre- "against" + faire "to make."
