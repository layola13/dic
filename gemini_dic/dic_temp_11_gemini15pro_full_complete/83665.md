haffle:/ˈhæfəl/ | v. | 犹豫，支吾，吞吞吐吐 | To speak or act hesitantly or evasively, especially when trying to avoid committing oneself. |  He haffled over the question, unwilling to give a direct answer. 他对这个问题支吾其词，不愿给出直接的答案。|  She haffled for a moment before admitting she'd lost the money. 她犹豫了一会儿才承认她把钱弄丢了。 | synonyms: hesitate, prevaricate, equivocate, stammer, stutter | antonyms:  decide, assert, declare | verb
