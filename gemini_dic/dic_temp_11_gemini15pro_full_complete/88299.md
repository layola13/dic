hipip:/ˈhɪpɪp/| interj.|加油！万岁！|An exclamation of encouragement or celebration, often repeated three times.|Hipip! Hipip! Hooray! 加油！加油！万岁！|近义词：hurrah, hooray|反义词:boo|词性:感叹词

词源:  Likely a playful variation or extension of "hip," which itself is an exclamation of enthusiasm.  The addition of the reduplicated "ip" intensifies the feeling.
