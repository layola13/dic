feigned:/feɪnd/| adj. | 假的，虚假的，装出的 | Pretended; simulated; not genuine.  Giving a false appearance of; insincere. | She gave a feigned smile to hide her disappointment. 她假装微笑来掩饰她的失望。|  He feigned illness to avoid going to school. 他装病不去上学。 |  simulated, pretended, affected, artificial, sham | genuine, sincere, real, authentic | adjective
