intragastrically:/ˌɪntrəˈɡæstrɪkli/
adverb
胃内地
within the stomach; administered through or applied within the stomach.
The drug was administered intragastrically.  这种药物是胃内给药的。
The patient was fed intragastrically. 病人被胃内喂食。
Synonyms:  intragastric,  by stomach tube
Antonyms:  intravenously, intramuscularly
词性: adverb
