conservatory:/kənˈsɜːrvətɔːri/ (US) /kənˈsɜːvətri/ (UK)| n.|温室；音乐学院|A room with a glass roof and walls, attached to a house, used as a sun lounge or for growing plants.  A school specializing in one of the fine arts, especially music.|例句：The conservatory was filled with exotic plants. 温室里满是奇异的植物。

例句：She studied piano at the conservatory. 她在音乐学院学习钢琴。

近义词：greenhouse, glasshouse (for plant growing); music school, academy (for music education)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


conservatory:/kənˈsɜːrvətɔːri/ (US) /kənˈsɜːvətri/ (UK)| adj.(罕见)|保存的，保护的|Relating to or promoting conservation.|例句：Conservatory measures were put in place to protect the endangered species.  实施了保护措施以保护濒危物种。(注意：此用法相对罕见，通常用 conservation 或 conservative)|近义词：preservationist, conservationist|反义词:destructive|词性: adjective (rare)
