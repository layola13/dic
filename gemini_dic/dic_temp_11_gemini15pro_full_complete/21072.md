blindnesses:/ˈblaɪndnɪsɪz/
noun
失明（复数）
The state of being unable to see.  The plural form emphasizes multiple instances or types of blindness, perhaps referring to different individuals or different causes of blindness.
Many organizations are working to cure preventable blindnesses.  许多组织正在努力治愈可预防的失明。
The conference addressed the challenges of living with various blindnesses.  这次会议讨论了各种失明带来的生活挑战。
Near Synonyms:  sightlessness, vision impairment, visual disabilities
Antonyms: sight, vision, clear-sightedness
