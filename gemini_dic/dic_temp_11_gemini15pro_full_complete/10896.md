arafat:/ˈærəfæt/ | n. | 阿拉法特（沙特阿拉伯麦加附近的一座小山，伊斯兰教朝圣的重要地点）| Arafat is a plain about 20 km (12 mi) east of Mecca, in Saudi Arabia, where Muslim pilgrims gather during Hajj, the annual Islamic pilgrimage.  It is the location of the Mount Arafat, also known as the Mount of Mercy (Jabal ar-Rahmah). |  Pilgrims gather on the plain of Arafat during Hajj. 朝圣者在朝觐期间聚集在阿拉法特平原。

During the Hajj, Muslims spend a day on Arafat in prayer and contemplation. 在朝觐期间，穆斯林会在阿拉法特度过一天，进行祈祷和沉思。| 近义词: (No direct synonyms, but related terms include: Mount of Mercy, Jabal ar-Rahmah) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
