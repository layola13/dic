cbd:/siː biː diː/ | n. | 大麻二酚;  中央商务区 | cannabidiol (CBD) is a chemical in the Cannabis sativa plant, also known as marijuana or hemp. More than 80 chemicals, known as cannabinoids, have been identified in the cannabis plant. CBD is a non-psychoactive cannabinoid, meaning it doesn't cause a high. |  CBD oil is becoming increasingly popular for its potential health benefits.  大麻二酚油因其潜在的健康益处而越来越受欢迎。 |Central Business District (CBD) refers to the commercial centre of a city. 中央商务区指的是一个城市的商业中心。 | synonyms: (for cannabidiol) cannabinoid; (for Central Business District) downtown, city centre | antonyms:  (for cannabidiol) THC (tetrahydrocannabinol - the psychoactive component of cannabis); (for Central Business District) suburbs, outskirts | noun
