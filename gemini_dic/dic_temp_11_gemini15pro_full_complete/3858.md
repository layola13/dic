agoraphobics: /ˌæɡərəˈfəʊbɪks/ | n. | 广场恐惧症患者 | People who suffer from agoraphobia, a fear of open or crowded spaces. |  Agoraphobics often find it difficult to leave their homes. 广场恐惧症患者经常发现很难离开家。|  Agoraphobics sometimes experience panic attacks in crowded places. 广场恐惧症患者有时会在拥挤的地方经历恐慌症发作。 | synonyms: sufferers of agoraphobia | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a person, could consider "sociable people" but it's not a perfect opposite) | noun


agoraphobic: /ˌæɡərəˈfəʊbɪk/ | adj. | 患广场恐惧症的；广场恐惧症的 | Relating to or suffering from agoraphobia. | The agoraphobic patient rarely left his apartment. 这位广场恐惧症患者很少离开他的公寓。 | She became agoraphobic after a traumatic experience in a crowded mall. 在一次拥挤的商场里经历了创伤性事件后，她患上了广场恐惧症。| synonyms: claustrophobic (similar but refers to enclosed spaces), anxiety-ridden | antonyms:  outgoing, sociable | adjective
