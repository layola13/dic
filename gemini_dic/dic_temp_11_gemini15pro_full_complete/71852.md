flesh-out:/ˈflɛʃ aʊt/| vt.|充实，完善|To add more details or information to something, making it more complete or substantial. |The author fleshed out the story with vivid descriptions of the setting.  作者用对场景的生动描述充实了故事。

The screenwriter fleshed out the characters' backstories to make them more believable. 编剧充实了角色的背景故事，使他们更可信。|近义词：expand, elaborate, develop, enrich, enhance|反义词：abridge, condense, shorten, summarize|词性:动词
