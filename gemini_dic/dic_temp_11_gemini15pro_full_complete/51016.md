deskilling:/diːˈskɪlɪŋ/
n.
减少技能；去技能化
The process of reducing the level of skill needed to do a job, often by introducing technology.
Automation has led to the deskilling of many factory jobs. 自动化导致许多工厂工作岗位技能降低。
The introduction of new machinery resulted in the deskilling of traditional crafts. 新机器的引进导致传统工艺的技能下降。
近义词：degradation, devaluation
反义词：upskilling, reskilling
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning 'reduce' or 'remove') + skill + -ing (suffix forming a noun from a verb).
