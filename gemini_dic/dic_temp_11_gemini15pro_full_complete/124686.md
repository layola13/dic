movie-goer:/ˈmuːviˌɡəʊər/ (UK) /ˈmuːviˌɡoʊər/ (US)| n. |电影观众，常看电影的人|A person who frequently attends movies.|She's a regular movie-goer and sees almost every new release. 她是一位经常去看电影的人，几乎每部新上映的电影都会去看。|
Movie-goers lined up for hours to see the premiere.  电影观众排了好几个小时的队去看首映。|
Synonyms: film buff, cinephile, movie fan|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use "homebody" or someone who avoids movies)|Noun
