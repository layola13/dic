engorgement:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒmənt/ | n. | 充血；肿胀；饱食 |The act of engorging or the state of being engorged; fullness.  A condition of excessive fullness or distension, especially due to an accumulation of blood or other fluids. |  Breast engorgement is common in new mothers.  新妈妈的乳房充血很常见。 |  The river was engorged with floodwater. 河水因洪水而暴涨。 | synonyms: congestion, swelling, distension, fullness, plethora | antonyms: depletion, emptiness | noun

engorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/ | v. |  (使)充盈；(使)饱胀；狼吞虎咽 | To cause to swell with blood, fluid, or food; to fill to excess.
