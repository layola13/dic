baud:/bɔːd/ | n. | 波特 | A unit of signaling speed equal to the number of discrete signal events per second.  It is the reciprocal of the duration of the shortest signaling element. | The modem operated at 1200 bauds.  调制解调器以1200波特的速度运行。 | synonyms: signal rate, modulation rate | antonyms: N/A | noun
