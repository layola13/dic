fire-extinguisher: /ˈfaɪər ɪkˌstɪŋɡwɪʃər/ | n. |灭火器|A portable device used to put out small fires, typically by ejecting a jet of water, foam, or other fire-retardant substance.|Keep a fire extinguisher in the kitchen for safety. 在厨房里放一个灭火器以保安全。|Synonyms: fire-fighting apparatus, extinguisher |Antonyms: accelerant, fuel | noun
