over-mixes:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪksɪz/
verb
过度混合
mixes something too much, typically ingredients in cooking or baking.
She over-mixed the batter, and the cake was tough. 她把面糊搅拌过度了，蛋糕很硬。
The DJ over-mixed the tracks, and the transition was jarring.  DJ过度混合了音轨，过渡很不和谐。
