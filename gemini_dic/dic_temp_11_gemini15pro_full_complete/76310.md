gabelle:/ɡəˈbɛl/ | n. | 食盐税；（尤指）法国旧时的食盐专卖税 | a tax on salt, especially the former French government monopoly on salt | The gabelle was a deeply unpopular tax in pre-revolutionary France. 法国大革命前，食盐税非常不得人心。| synonym: salt tax, excise, levy | antonym: subsidy, tax exemption | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gabelle*, from Old Italian *gabella*, from Arabic *qabāla* ("acceptance, receipt, tax"). Ultimately from the root *q-b-l* ("to receive, accept").
