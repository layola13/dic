disfigure:/dɪsˈfɪɡjər/ /dɪsˈfɪɡər/
verb
损毁…的外形；使…变丑
To spoil the appearance of something or someone, especially their face, by damaging it.
The acid disfigured her face. 酸液毁了她的容貌。
Synonyms: deform, mar, mutilate, spoil
Antonyms: beautify, enhance, improve
词性: 动词

disfigurement:/dɪsˈfɪɡjərmənt/ /dɪsˈfɪɡjərmənt/
noun
毁容；外形的损毁
The act of disfiguring something or someone, or the state of being disfigured.
He suffered permanent disfigurement in the accident.  他在事故中永久毁容。
Synonyms: deformity, blemish, defect
Antonyms: beauty, perfection
词性: 名词
