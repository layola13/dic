anti-physically:/ˌæntiˈfɪzɪkli/
adjective
反物理地；违反物理规律地
Contrary to the laws of physics; impossible or improbable according to established physical principles.
His claims about teleportation seemed anti-physical, defying our current understanding of the universe. 他关于瞬间移动的说法似乎违反物理规律，挑战了我们目前对宇宙的理解。
The magician's trick appeared anti-physical, as if he'd bent the very fabric of reality. 魔术师的戏法看起来违反物理定律，就好像他扭曲了现实的结构一样。
Synonyms: unnatural, supernatural, paranormal, impossible, improbable
Antonyms: physical, natural, normal, possible, probable
词性: 形容词
