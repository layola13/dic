befogging:/bɪˈfɒɡɪŋ/
v.
使困惑，使模糊
causing confusion or obscurity
The dense fog was befogging the road ahead. 浓雾使前方的道路模糊不清。
近义词:confusing, obscuring, bewildering, mystifying
反义词:clarifying, illuminating, elucidating
词性:动词(present participle of befog)


befogged:/bɪˈfɒɡd/
adj.
困惑的，模糊的
confused and unclear
I was completely befogged by the complex instructions.  我完全被复杂的指令弄糊涂了。
近义词:confused, bewildered, perplexed, disoriented
反义词:clear, lucid, coherent
词性:形容词(past participle of befog)



词源:
From be- + fog.  由be- + fog组成。
