confederacy: /kənˈfɛdərəsi/ | n. | 联盟，邦联 | a league or alliance, especially of states |  The Confederate States of America was a confederacy of Southern states during the American Civil War. | 近义词: alliance, league, federation, coalition | 反义词: disunity, division | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *confœderatia, from Latin confœderatus (“united by treaty”), past participle of confœderare, from con- (“together”) + fœderare (“to make a treaty”), from fœdus (“treaty”). Doublet of federation.
