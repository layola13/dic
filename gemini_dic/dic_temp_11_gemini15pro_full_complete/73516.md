foreshine:/ˈfɔːrʃaɪn/| n.|前兆，预兆；先兆 |A subtle indication of something to come; a presage.|A strange foreshine of coming events troubled him. 一种对即将发生的事情的奇怪预感困扰着他。|近义词：presage, omen, portent, foreshadowing|反义词：aftermath, consequence|词性：名词
Etymology: From 'fore-' (before) + 'shine' (to emit light), suggesting a light shining before something happens.
