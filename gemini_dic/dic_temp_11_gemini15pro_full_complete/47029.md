dasyure:/ˈdæsijʊər/ | n. | 袋鼬 | A small, carnivorous marsupial native to Australia and New Guinea. | The dasyure is a nocturnal hunter. 袋鼬是一种夜行动物。|  近义词：quoll, marsupial | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些非食肉动物，如 kangaroo 袋鼠) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Dasyurus, from Ancient Greek δασύς (dasús, “hairy, shaggy”) + οὐρά (ourá, “tail”).
