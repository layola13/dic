exarc:/ˈeksɑːrk/ | n. |总督；地方官；外主教|A governor or prefect in the Byzantine Empire, especially ruling one of the provinces remote from the capital. |The exarch of Ravenna held considerable power in Italy. 拉文纳总督在意大利拥有相当大的权力。|近义词：governor, prefect, viceroy|反义词:citizen, subject|noun
