emulatory:/ˈɛmjʊləˌtɔːri/| adj.|竞争的；模仿的；仿效的|Inclined or designed to emulate; imitating.|Her emulatory zeal drove her to surpass her older sister in every field. 她好胜的热情驱使她在各个领域都超过她的姐姐。|近义词：imitative, competitive, vying|反义词：original, innovative|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin aemulatorius, from Latin aemulari "to rival, to emulate," from aemulus "rivaling, emulous."
