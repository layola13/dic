noneffusive:/ˌnɒnɪˈfjuːsɪv/
adjective
不热情洋溢的；含蓄的；内敛的
Not effusive; restrained or reserved in emotional expression.
He gave a noneffusive welcome, merely nodding as she entered. 他只是在她进来时点了点头，表示欢迎，并不热情。
Synonyms: reserved, restrained, subdued, undemonstrative, quiet
Antonyms: effusive, gushing, demonstrative, enthusiastic, passionate
词性: 形容词
