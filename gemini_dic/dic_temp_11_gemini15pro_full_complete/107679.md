lanigerous:/ləˈnɪdʒərəs/
adjective
多毛的；产毛的
Bearing wool or wool-like hairs.
The lanigerous plant was covered in soft, white hairs.  这种产毛的植物覆盖着柔软的白毛。
The caterpillar was lanigerous, its body covered in fine, silky hairs. 这条毛毛虫身上长满了细细的丝状绒毛。
synonym: woolly, hairy, fleecy
antonym: hairless, glabrous
