effeminate:/ɪˈfɛmɪnət/| adj.|女人气的，柔弱的，缺乏男子气概的|Having or showing characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly.|例句：He had an effeminate manner that some people found off-putting. 他有一种女人气的举止，有些人觉得很不舒服。

例句：His high-pitched voice and delicate gestures struck some as effeminate.  他尖细的声音和优雅的姿态让一些人觉得他很女性化。|近义词：womanish, unmanly, sissy|反义词：masculine, manly, virile|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *effeminatus*, past participle of *effeminare* (“to make womanish, weaken”), from *ex-* (“out”) + *femina* (“woman”).
