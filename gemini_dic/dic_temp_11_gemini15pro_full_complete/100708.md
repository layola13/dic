intrafascicular:/ˌɪntrəfəˈsɪkjʊlər/| adj.|束内的；束间的|Situated or occurring within a fasciculus (a small bundle or cluster, especially of nerve or muscle fibers).|Intrafascicular injection is a method of local anesthesia.  束内注射是一种局部麻醉方法。|Intrafascicular electrodes can selectively stimulate nerve fibers. 束内电极可以选择性地刺激神经纤维。|近义词:intrabundle|反义词:extrafascicular|词性:adjective
