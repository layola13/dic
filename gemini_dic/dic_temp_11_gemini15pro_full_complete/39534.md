conquistador:/kɒŋˈkɪstədɔːr/ (UK), /kɑːŋˈkɪstədɔːr/ (US)| n.|征服者，侵略者|A conqueror, especially one of the Spanish conquerors of Mexico and Peru in the 16th century.|例句：The conquistadors were driven by a thirst for gold and glory. 征服者的动力是对黄金和荣耀的渴望。|例句：Hernán Cortés was a famous conquistador who conquered the Aztec Empire. 埃尔南·科尔特斯是一位著名的征服者，他征服了阿兹特克帝国。|近义词：conqueror, invader, victor|反义词：defeated, vanquished|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *conquistador*, from *conquistar* "to conquer," from Latin *conquistare*, from *com-* "together" + *quaerere* "to seek, gain."
