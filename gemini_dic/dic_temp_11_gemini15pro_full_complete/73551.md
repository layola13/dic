forestaff:/ˈfɔːrstæf/| n.|前杆，十字测天仪|A simple instrument used for taking the altitude of the sun or stars, consisting of a staff with a crosspiece sliding along it.|He used a forestaff to navigate by the stars.|近义词:cross-staff, Jacob's staff|反义词:None|noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *forstaf, from Old English *fōrstæf, from *fōr "before, in front" + *stæf "staff".  The crosspiece was held *before* the eye.
