ludicrousness:/ˈluːdɪkrəsnəs/ | n. | 荒谬，滑稽 |The ludicrousness of the situation made everyone burst out laughing. | The sheer ludicrousness of his suggestion left me speechless. | absurdity, silliness, ridiculousness | seriousness, sensibleness | noun

Etymology:
From ludicrous + -ness.  Ludicrous comes from the Latin word *lūdicrus*, meaning "sportive, playful," from *lūdus*, meaning "play, game, sport."  The meaning shifted over time from "playful" to "laughable" and then to "absurd."
