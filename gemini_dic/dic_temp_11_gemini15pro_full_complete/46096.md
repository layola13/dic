cyder:/ˈsaɪdər/ | n. | 苹果酒 | An alcoholic drink made from fermented apple juice.  It is similar to but distinct from hard cider in the US which tends to be stronger. |例句：He enjoyed a glass of refreshing cyder. 他享用了一杯清爽的苹果酒。

例句：The local pub serves a variety of cyders. 当地的酒吧提供各种各样的苹果酒。| 近义词：cider, apple wine | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *cidre* and Anglo-Norman *cidre*, from Latin *sicera*, from Ancient Greek *σῐ́κερα* (*síkera*), from Akkadian *šikaru* (beer, strong drink). Ultimately from a Semitic root related to Hebrew *שׁכר* (*shakar*).
