dispensable:/dɪˈspɛnsəbl/
adjective
可有可无的；非必要的
not necessary; that can be done without
Many factory jobs have become dispensable due to automation. 由于自动化，许多工厂工作变得可有可无。
synonyms: unnecessary, nonessential, inessential, expendable
antonyms: essential, indispensable, necessary, crucial
词性: 形容词
