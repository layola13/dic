ethernets:/ˈiːθərˌnɛts/ | n. | 以太网 | A system for connecting a number of computer systems to form a local area network, with protocols to control the passing of information and to avoid simultaneous transmission by two or more systems.  It is usually referred to in the singular, as Ethernet. | Our office uses Ethernet for its network connectivity. 我们的办公室使用以太网进行网络连接。| Near Synonyms:  network, LAN (local area network) | Antonyms:  (Generally, Ethernet doesn't have direct antonyms, but you could consider contrasting technologies like "dial-up" or "wireless" as conceptually opposite in some contexts.) | noun
