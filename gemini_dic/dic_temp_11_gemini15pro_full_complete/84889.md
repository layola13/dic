hangworthy:/ˈhæŋˌwɜːrði/
adjective
该绞死的；罪大恶极的；严重的
Deserving hanging; (of an offense) so serious as to merit the death penalty.
His crimes were deemed hangworthy by the court. 法庭认为他的罪行应该处以绞刑。
Near Synonyms: capital, punishable by death, atrocious, heinous, egregious, flagrant
Antonyms:  trivial, minor, venial, excusable, forgivable
词性: 形容词
