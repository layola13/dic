druidical: /druːˈɪdɪk(ə)l/
adjective
德鲁伊教的；与德鲁伊教有关的；类似德鲁伊教的
Relating to the Druids, their religion, or their practices; resembling or characteristic of the Druids.
The ancient stones formed a druidical circle.  这些古老的石头形成了一个德鲁伊教的环形。
The ceremony had a distinctly druidical feel.  这个仪式有一种明显的德鲁伊教的感觉。
近义词: pagan, ancient, Celtic
反义词: modern, contemporary
词性: adjective

词源:  From Druid + -ical.  Druid comes from Gaulish and ultimately Proto-Celtic, possibly meaning "knowing the oak tree."
