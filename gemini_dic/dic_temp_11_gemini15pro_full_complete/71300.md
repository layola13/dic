fixture:/ˈfɪkstʃər/ (US), /ˈfɪkstʃə/ (UK)| n.| 固定装置; 设备; 家具; (体育)预定的比赛|A fixture is something that is fixed or attached to a building or piece of land, such as a bath or toilet, or something that is fixed or attached as a permanent part of a piece of furniture. It can also refer to a sports match or event that has been arranged for a particular date and time.|例句：The price of the house includes all fixtures and fittings.  这栋房子的价格包括所有固定装置和配件。|近义词：appliance, fitting, equipment, apparatus|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
fixture:/ˈfɪkstʃər/ (US), /ˈfɪkstʃə/ (UK)| n.| (商店或公司里的)长期雇员|A fixture can refer to a person who is well established in a particular place or situation.|例句：He's been a fixture in the company for over 20 years. 他在公司工作了20多年，是个老员工了。|近义词：regular, mainstay|反义词: newcomer, transient|词性: noun
