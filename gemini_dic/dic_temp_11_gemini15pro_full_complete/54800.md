dispensers:/dɪˈspɛnsərz/
n.
分配器；药剂师；施舍者
A machine or container that distributes something, often in measured amounts.  A person who gives out something.
The cafeteria has new soap dispensers.  自助餐厅有新的皂液器。
He worked as a dispenser in a pharmacy. 他在药房当药剂师。
distributors, vendors, providers
conservers, hoarders
noun
