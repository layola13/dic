aladdin:/əˈlædɪn/| n.|阿拉丁|A character from a Middle Eastern folk tale, a young man who finds a magic lamp containing a genie that grants him wishes.|例句：Aladdin rubbed the lamp and a genie appeared. 阿拉丁擦了擦神灯，一个精灵出现了。

例句：The story of Aladdin is a popular children's tale. 阿拉丁的故事是一个很受欢迎的儿童故事。|近义词：genie, hero|反义词:villain|Noun
