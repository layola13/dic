ascertainer:/æsərˈteɪnər/
n.
确定者；查明者；探明者
a person who ascertains something.
The ascertainer of the facts presented his findings to the court.  事实查明者向法庭提交了他的调查结果。
近义词: investigator, researcher, inquirer
反义词: obfuscator, concealer
