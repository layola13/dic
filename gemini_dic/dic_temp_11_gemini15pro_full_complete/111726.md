locomotor:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtər/| adj. n.|运动的；运动器官|Relating to movement or locomotion; a locomotor organ.|The snail's locomotor system consists of a muscular foot.  蜗牛的运动系统由肌肉发达的腹足组成。

The child's locomotor skills are developing rapidly.  这个孩子的运动技能发展迅速。|近义词：mobile, motile|反义词：immobile, sessile|词性: adjective, noun

locomotion:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊʃən/| n.|运动，移动|The act or power of moving from place to place.|The amoeba's locomotion is achieved through pseudopods. 变形虫通过伪足进行运动。

Birds use wings for locomotion. 鸟类用翅膀移动。|近义词：movement, travel|反义词：stasis, immobility|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *locomōtōrius*, from *locomōtus* ("moved from its place"), past participle of *locomōvō* ("move from its place, dislodge, remove"), from *locus* ("place") + *movō* ("move").
