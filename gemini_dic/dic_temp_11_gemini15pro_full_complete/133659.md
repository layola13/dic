nonfortification:/ˌnɑnfɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
不设防，不 укрепление
The nonfortification of the border led to increased smuggling. 边境不设防导致走私活动增加。
non-fortification of the city walls left them vulnerable.  城市城墙未设防使他们容易受到攻击。
近义词: undefendedness, vulnerability, weakness
反义词: fortification, strengthening, reinforcement
词性: 名词


词源:
non- (prefix) + fortification (noun)

non-: from Latin *non < ne (“not”) + oinom (“one; a, an”)

fortification: from Old French fortification, from Late Latin fortificationem (nominative fortificatio) “a strengthening, making strong,” noun of action from past-participle stem of fortificare “to make strong, fortify,” from Latin fortis “strong” + facere “to make.”
