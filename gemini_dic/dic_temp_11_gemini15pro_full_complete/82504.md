gryphon:/ˈɡrɪfən/ /ˈɡrɪfɪn/| n. |狮鹫；格里芬|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle.|例句：The gryphon is a popular figure in heraldry. 狮鹫是纹章学中的一个流行形象。

例句：Legends say the gryphon guarded treasures of gold. 传说狮鹫守护着黄金宝藏。|近义词：griffin, hippogriff|反义词:  (无明确反义词，因为是神话生物)|词性:noun
