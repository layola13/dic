miscellanarian: /ˌmɪsələˈnɛəriən/ | n. | 杂集作者；杂学家；涉猎广泛的人 | A person who studies or collects miscellaneous items or information; a person with varied interests or knowledge. | He was a true miscellanarian, with interests ranging from ancient history to modern art. 他是一位真正的杂学家，兴趣范围从古代历史到现代艺术。 |  Synonyms: polymath, generalist, dilettante | Antonyms: specialist | noun

miscellany: /mɪˈsɛləni/ | n. | 杂集；混合物；各种各样 | A collection of different items or a mixture of various things. | The antique shop was a miscellany of old furniture, books, and trinkets. 这家古董店里有各种各样的旧家具、书籍和小饰品。| Synonyms: assortment, medley, potpourri | Antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity | noun
