codependency:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.|相互依赖，共依存|A condition in which a person is excessively reliant on another person, typically one with an addiction or illness, and their lives become intertwined.|Her codependency on her alcoholic husband was damaging to both of them. 她对她酗酒丈夫的共依存关系对他们两人都有害。|Example:  His codependency on his mother prevented him from developing healthy relationships.  他对母亲的依赖使他无法发展健康的人际关系。

|Synonyms: interdependence, reliance, attachment|Antonyms: independence, autonomy, self-reliance|noun
