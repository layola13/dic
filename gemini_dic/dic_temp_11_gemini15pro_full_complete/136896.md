nonsocieties:/ˌnɒn səˈsaɪətiz/ |n.| 非社会；非社会群体|Groups of people who do not form or belong to a recognized society, often lacking shared norms, values, or institutions.  They might be isolated groups, or people living outside the structures of mainstream society.|Example:  Researchers studied the behavior of nonsocieties in remote areas to understand human social development. 研究人员研究了偏远地区非社会群体的行为，以了解人类的社会发展。

Example: The book explored the challenges of integrating nonsocieties into the larger global community. 这本书探讨了将非社会群体融入更大全球社区的挑战。| 近义词: non-states, outcast groups |反义词: societies, communities| 词性: 名词
