headhunt:/ˈhedˌhʌnt/ | n. |猎头；物色人才；挖墙脚|The practice of identifying and approaching suitable candidates employed elsewhere to fill a business position.|例句：Headhunting has become a common practice in many industries. 猎头在很多行业已经成为一种常见的做法。|近义词：recruitment, poaching, scouting|反义词：dismissal, firing|词性：名词

词源：Compound of *head* and *hunt*.  Originally referred to the practice of some indigenous cultures of collecting the heads of enemies as trophies.  The modern usage, referring to recruitment, emerged in the mid-20th century, implying a similar pursuit of desirable individuals.
