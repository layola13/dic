opisthoglypha:/ˌɒpɪsˈθɒɡlɪfə/ | n. | 后沟牙蛇类 | A suborder of snakes characterized by having enlarged, grooved fangs located near the rear of the maxilla (upper jaw). These fangs are used to inject venom into prey. |  Opisthoglyphous snakes, such as the boomslang, possess rear fangs. 后沟牙蛇类，例如非洲树蛇，拥有后沟牙。|  近义词: rear-fanged snakes | 反义词: proteroglypha (前沟牙蛇类), solenoglypha (管牙蛇类), aglypha (无毒牙蛇类) | 词性: 名词
