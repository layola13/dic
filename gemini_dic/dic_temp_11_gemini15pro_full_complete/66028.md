exanimate:/ɪɡˈzænɪmət/ | adj. | 无生命的；失去知觉的；缺乏生气的 | Lifeless; deprived of consciousness or animation; spiritless. | The explorers discovered an exanimate body frozen in the ice. 探险者们发现了一具冻在冰里的尸体。| The old house had an exanimate, almost ghostly feel. 这座老房子有一种毫无生气，几乎像鬼魂一样的感觉。 | 近义词: lifeless, inanimate, dead, inert, spiritless | 反义词: animate, living, lively, energetic, vibrant | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin exanimatus ‘deprived of breath’, from the verb exanimare, from ex- ‘out of’ + anima ‘breath, soul’.
