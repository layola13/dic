nonpastorals:/nɒnˈpɑːstərəlz/ | n. | 非田园作品；非牧歌式作品 | Works that do not idealize or romanticize rural life, often depicting the harsher realities of the countryside or focusing on urban themes. |  The poet's recent collection of nonpastorals explores the complexities of modern urban life. 这位诗人最近的非田园作品集探讨了现代都市生活的复杂性。| near synonyms:  realistic depictions, gritty portrayals, urban narratives | antonyms: pastorals, idylls, bucolics | noun
