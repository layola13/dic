ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēdecorōsus*, from *dēdecor* (disgrace, shame) + *-ōsus* (suffix forming adjectives).  The Latin *dēdecor* is from *dē-* (down, away from) + *decor* (ornament, grace, beauty).
