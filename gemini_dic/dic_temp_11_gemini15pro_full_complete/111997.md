lonely: /ˈloʊnli/ (US) /ˈləʊnli/ (UK) | adj. | 孤独的，寂寞的 | Feeling sad because one is alone or isolated. |  She felt incredibly lonely after her husband died. 她丈夫去世后，她感到无比孤独。

| I get lonely when I travel for work. 我出差的时候会感到孤独。

|近义词：isolated, alone, solitary, lonesome |反义词：accompanied, together, sociable, gregarious | 词性: 形容词
