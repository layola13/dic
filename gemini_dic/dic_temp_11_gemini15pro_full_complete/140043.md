oenophile:/ˈiːnəfaɪl/ /ˈiːnəˌfaɪl/| n. |葡萄酒爱好者；品酒行家|A person who is knowledgeable about and enjoys wine.|She's a true oenophile, with a cellar full of vintage wines. 她是一位真正的葡萄酒爱好者，酒窖里收藏了大量的年份葡萄酒。|Example:  He considers himself an oenophile and loves to visit vineyards. 他自认为是葡萄酒鉴赏家，喜欢参观葡萄园。

|Synonyms: wine lover, wine connoisseur, wine buff|Antonyms: teetotaler, abstainer|Noun
