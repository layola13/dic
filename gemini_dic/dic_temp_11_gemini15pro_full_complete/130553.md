nonacquiescence:/ˌnɒnˌækwiˈɛsəns/ | n. | 不默许，不服从，不认可 | Refusal to accept or comply with something; the act of not agreeing or giving consent. |  The government's nonacquiescence to the court ruling led to further legal battles. 政府对法院裁决的不默许导致了进一步的法律斗争。

|  His nonacquiescence to the new policy resulted in his dismissal. 他不服从新政策导致了他的解雇。 | 近义词：disagreement, dissent, objection, refusal, resistance | 反义词：acquiescence, agreement, compliance, consent, acceptance | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- "not" + acquiescence (from Latin acquiescere "to rest, be quiet, find rest in," from ad- "to" + quiescere "to be quiet," from quies "rest, quiet")
