outcoaches:/ˌaʊtˈkoʊtʃɪz/
verb|以更好的教练方式胜过；在教练方面超越|To surpass someone in coaching ability; to be a better coach than someone else.|例句：Team A outcoached Team B in the final game, leading to their victory. A队在决赛中以更优秀的教练策略战胜了B队，最终赢得了胜利。

例句：She outcoached all her competitors and led her team to an undefeated season. 她以出色的执教能力超越了所有对手，带领她的团队取得了赛季全胜。|近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, outperform|反义词：be outcoached, be surpassed, be outperformed|词性:动词
