aquatint:/ˈækwətɪnt/ | n. | 水彩画，铜版腐蚀制版法 | A method of etching that produces a tonal effect, rather than lines, by using powdered resin. | The artist used aquatint to create a misty effect in the background of the print. 艺术家用水彩画法在版画的背景中营造了一种朦胧的效果。 | Synonym: etching, engraving | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "lithograph" as a different printmaking technique) | noun


aquatint:/ˈækwətɪnt/ | v. | 用铜版腐蚀制版法制作 | To create a print using the aquatint method. |  He aquatinted the plate to achieve a subtle gradation of tones. 他用铜版腐蚀制版法制作版面，以达到微妙的色调渐变。 | Synonym: etch, engrave | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | verb
