cinderellas:/ˌsɪndəˈrɛləz/| n. |灰姑娘们；(比喻)被低估或被忽视的人或事物|Refers to individuals or entities, often underestimated or overlooked, who achieve unexpected success or recognition, alluding to the fairytale character Cinderella. |Many of these small businesses are the Cinderellas of the tech world, quietly innovating and disrupting established players. 这些小型企业中的许多都是科技界的灰姑娘，默默地进行创新并颠覆老牌企业。|近义词:underdogs, dark horses|反义词:favorites, frontrunners|词性:名词
