complication:/ˌkɑːmplɪˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkɒmplɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |并发症；复杂化；混乱|A circumstance that complicates something; a difficulty.  A secondary disease or condition aggravating an already existing one. |The surgery went well, but there were some post-operative complications. 手术进行得很顺利，但术后出现了一些并发症。

The bad weather added a further complication to our journey. 恶劣的天气给我们的旅程增加了更多麻烦。|近义词：difficulty, problem, snag, obstacle, hurdle, impediment, setback, twist, hitch, intricacy |反义词：simplification, ease, solution |词性:名词
