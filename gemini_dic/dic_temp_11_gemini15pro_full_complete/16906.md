basker:/ˈbæskər/ (US), /ˈbɑːskər/ (UK)| n.| 晒太阳的人，取暖的人|A person who lies or sits in a warm place, especially in the sun, to enjoy its warmth.|The basker lay on the beach, soaking up the sun's rays.  晒太阳的人躺在沙滩上，尽情享受阳光。| The lizard was a basker, enjoying the heat of the rock.  蜥蜴喜欢晒太阳，享受着岩石的热度。| synonyms: sunbather, lounger, idler| antonyms: worker, hustler| noun

bask:/bæsk/ (US), /bɑːsk/ (UK)| v.| 晒太阳，取暖，沉浸于|To lie or sit enjoying warmth, especially sunshine; to revel in something pleasant.|The cats basked in the afternoon sun. 猫咪在午后的阳光下晒太阳。| She basked in the admiration of her fans. 她沉浸在粉丝们的赞美中。| synonyms: revel, wallow, luxuriate| antonyms: freeze, shiver| verb
