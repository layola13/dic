memorizers:/ˈmɛməˌraɪzərz/| n. |记忆者，背诵者|People who memorize things, often referring to students learning by rote.  Those who dedicate themselves to committing information to memory. |Example 1: The memorizers in the class could recite the entire poem. 班里的背诵者可以背诵整首诗。

Example 2:  Many memorizers find flashcards a helpful tool. 许多记忆者发现抽认卡是一个有用的工具。|Synonyms: learners, students, rote learners, memorists |Antonyms:  forgetters |noun
