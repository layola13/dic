diacid:/ˌdaɪˈæsɪd/| n.|二元酸|A diacid is an acid that has two acidic hydrogen atoms in its molecule.  These hydrogens can react with bases to form two kinds of salts.|Tartaric acid is a diacid found in grapes.|近义词：diprotic acid|反义词:|n.
