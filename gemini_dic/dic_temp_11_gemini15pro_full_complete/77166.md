gaslighting:/ˈɡæsˌlaɪtɪŋ/
n.
煤气灯效应；心理操控
a form of psychological manipulation in which a person or group covertly sows seeds of doubt in a targeted individual or group, making them question their own memory, perception, or judgment.
She was the victim of gaslighting by her abusive partner. 她遭受了伴侣的心理操控。
manipulation, abuse, brainwashing
empowerment, support, validation
noun

gaslights: /ˈɡæsˌlaɪts/
v. (third-person singular simple present gaslights, present participle gaslighting, simple past and past participle gaslighted)
对…进行心理操控
to manipulate someone psychologically by making them question their own sanity.
He gaslights her by constantly denying things he said or did. 他通过不断否认自己说过或做过的事情来对她进行心理操控。
manipulates, abuses, brainwashes
supports, empowers, validates
verb
