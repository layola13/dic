disapprovingly:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvɪŋli/
adverb
不赞成地
in a way that shows you do not approve of something or someone.
She looked at him disapprovingly. 她不赞成地看着他。
Near Synonyms: critically, censoriously, scornfully
Antonyms: approvingly, supportively
词性: 副词

disapprovingly:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvɪŋli/
词源:  From disapprove + -ing + -ly.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Approve" comes from the Old French "aprover," meaning "to prove, demonstrate, or find good."  Therefore, "disapprovingly" literally means "in a manner of not finding good."
