ornithodelphia:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈdɛlfiə/
noun
单孔目动物
A subclass of mammals, the Prototheria, characterized by laying eggs.  They are represented by the echidnas and the platypus.
The platypus and echidnas are the only surviving members of the Ornithodelphia.  鸭嘴兽和针鼹是单孔目动物中仅存的成员。
Prototheria, monotremes
