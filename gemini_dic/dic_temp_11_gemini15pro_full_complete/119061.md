metonymical:/ˌmetəˈnɪmɪkəl/| adj.|转喻的，借喻的|Relating to or characteristic of metonymy, a figure of speech that replaces the name of something with the name of something else with which it is closely associated.|The White House announced a new policy today. (White House is used metonymically to refer to the US presidential administration.) 白宫今天宣布了一项新政策。（白宫在这里转喻指美国总统行政部门。）|

The use of "suits" to refer to businessmen is metonymical. 用“西装”来指代商人是转喻的用法。|近义词：figurative, symbolic, metaphorical|反义词：literal|词性:adjective
