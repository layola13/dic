embroid:/ɪmˈbrɔɪd/ /ɛmˈbrɔɪd/| vt.| 刺绣，修饰 | To decorate cloth by sewing patterns on it with thread. | She embroidered a beautiful design on the cushion. 她在一个垫子上绣了一个漂亮的图案。|  The tablecloth was embroidered with flowers. 桌布上绣着花朵。 | 近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish | 反义词: simplify, strip | 词性：动词

词源:
From Middle English *embrouden*, from Old French *embrouder*, *embrordre*, *embroder*, from *en- + *brouder* (“to embroider”), of Germanic origin, from Proto-Germanic *brōdōną* (“to braid”). Cognate with Dutch *borduren* (“to embroider”), German *borden* (“to embroider, edge, trim”).
