defit:/dɪˈfɪt/ | v. | 击败，战胜；使无效，挫败 | to defeat or frustrate; to thwart. | He defitted his opponent in the chess match. 他在象棋比赛中击败了对手。

The lawyer defitted the prosecution's case by proving the evidence was inadmissible. 律师通过证明证据不可采纳而挫败了控方的案件。 | 近义词：conquer, vanquish, overcome, foil, thwart | 反义词：lose to, surrender to, yield to | 词性：动词

词源：From Old French *desfaire, from des- (expressing reversal) + faire (to do).  Ultimately from Latin dis- (apart, not) + facere (to do, make).
