communicant:/kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/
n.|领圣餐者；交流者|A person who receives Holy Communion.  A person who communicates. |例句：The communicant knelt at the altar rail. 领圣餐者跪在圣坛栏杆前。

例句：He was a frequent communicant with the spirit world. 他经常与精神世界交流。|近义词：partaker, participant, communicator|反义词:non-communicant|noun


communicant:/kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/
adj.|交流的；传达的|Communicating; imparting.  |例句：The communicant artery supplies blood to the heart. 交通动脉向心脏供血。

例句：Her communicant nature made her a popular friend. 她善于沟通的天性使她成为受欢迎的朋友。|近义词：communicative, expressive|反义词:uncommunicative|adjective
