disinclined:/ˌdɪsɪnˈklaɪnd/
adjective
不愿意的，不情愿的
Not wanting to do something; averse.
She was disinclined to leave the party. 她不愿离开派对。
近义词: reluctant, unwilling, averse, loath
反义词: inclined, willing, eager, keen
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + inclined (from Latin inclinare, "to bend, lean").
