exhibit:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ (US) /ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.|展览品，展出；展示，表现|An exhibit is a displayed object, often in a museum or art gallery. To exhibit something means to show it publicly.|The museum is exhibiting a collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts.  博物馆正在展出一批古埃及文物。|synonyms: display, show, present, demonstrate|antonyms: conceal, hide|noun, verb

exhibit:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ (US) /ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ (UK)| vt.|展示，表现|To exhibit a particular quality or trait means to show it openly.|He exhibited great courage in the face of danger.  面对危险，他表现出了极大的勇气。|synonyms: display, show, manifest, reveal|antonyms: conceal, hide|verb
