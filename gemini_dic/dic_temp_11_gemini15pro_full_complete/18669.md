PHRASE->
While less common, "bepraise" can be used with phrases like:
bepraise someone for something (因某事赞扬某人)
bepraise someone's efforts/work/achievements (赞扬某人的努力/工作/成就)
bepraise someone highly (高度赞扬某人)
bepraise someone to the skies (极力赞扬某人) -  This is less common in modern usage.
bepraise something as groundbreaking/innovative (称赞某事物为开创性的/创新的)

注意事项:
"bepraise" is relatively formal and less common than "praise".
