benefiter's:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtərz/
n. |受益者（的）|Someone or something that receives an advantage or good effect from something. |The benefiter's smile showed their appreciation. 受益者的微笑表达了他们的感激之情。|近义词：recipient, receiver, gainer|反义词: loser, contributor, donor|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:
From "benefiter" + "'s" (possessive suffix).
"Benefiter" comes from the verb "benefit" + "-er" (agent suffix).
"Benefit" comes from Old French "bienfet" (good deed, benefit), from "bien" (good) + "fet" (deed), from Latin "factum" (something done).
