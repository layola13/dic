downwardness:/ˈdaʊnwərdnəs/
n.
向下, 下降趋势, 颓势
A downward trend or tendency; a decline.
The downwardness of the economy is worrying. 经济的下降趋势令人担忧。
The graph shows a marked downwardness in sales. 图表显示销售额明显下降。

近义词:decline, descent, downturn, fall, slump
反义词:upwardness, ascent, rise, increase
