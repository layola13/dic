lambastes:/læmˈbeɪsts/
verb
严厉批评，抨击
criticizes someone or something very strongly
The critic lambasted the movie for its poor plot and weak acting. 影评人严厉批评这部电影的糟糕情节和糟糕的演技。

The coach lambasted the team for their lackluster performance. 教练严厉批评了球队缺乏生气的表现。

Synonyms: criticize, denounce, condemn, castigate, berate, excoriate
Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, applaud
词性: 动词
