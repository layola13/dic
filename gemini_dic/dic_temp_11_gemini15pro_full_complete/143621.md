outjinx:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɪŋks/
verb
胜过(某人)的厄运;比(某人)更幸运
to surpass someone in the use of jinxes or spells; to be more fortunate than someone else.
He outjinxed his rival by finding a four-leaf clover before the big game. 他在重要比赛前找到一片四叶草，运气胜过了他的对手。
近义词: outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词: be cursed, be unlucky
词性: 动词
