nondefeat:/nɒn.dɪˈfiːt/
n.|非失败，不败|The state of not being defeated; the avoidance or prevention of defeat.|Their strategy aimed at nondefeat rather than outright victory. 他们的战略旨在避免失败，而不是 outright victory.|近义词：victory, triumph, success|反义词：defeat, loss, failure|词性:名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "defeat" (from Old French "defete," ultimately from Latin "disfacere" - to undo, destroy).
