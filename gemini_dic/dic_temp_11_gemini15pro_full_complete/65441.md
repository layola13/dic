eupryion:/juːˈpraɪɒn/| n.|优普莱温，适温期|A period of mild weather in winter, especially in ancient Greece.|例句：The eupryion was a welcome respite from the cold winter months.  适温期是寒冷冬季中受欢迎的短暂休息。

例句：Ancient Greeks looked forward to the eupryion for planting and other outdoor activities.  古希腊人期待着适温期进行种植和其他户外活动。|近义词：thaw, mild spell|反义词：frost, cold snap|词性:名词

词源: From Ancient Greek εὐπρύϊος (euprúïos, “at dawn, early”), from εὖ (eû, “well, good”) + πρωΐ (prōḯ, “morning”).
