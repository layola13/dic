marinorama: /ˌmærɪnəˈrɑːmə/ /ˌmærɪnəˈræmə/
| n. |海景画；海景模型；海景馆|A pictorial representation of a marine scene, often encompassing a wide view and sometimes displayed in a circular panorama.  A three-dimensional representation of a marine scene, typically enclosed in a glass case. A building or other structure housing such a display.|

例句：
The marinorama at the museum transported us to the depths of the ocean.
博物馆里的海景模型把我们带到了海洋深处。

The artist's marinorama captured the beauty of the coastline.
艺术家的海景画捕捉到了海岸线的美丽。

近义词：seascape, diorama, panorama
反义词: None

词性: noun
