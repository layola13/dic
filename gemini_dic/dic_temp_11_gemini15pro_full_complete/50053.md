denouncers:/dɪˈnaʊnsərz/
n.
谴责者；告发者
People who publicly declare that someone or something is wrong or evil.
The denouncers of the regime were imprisoned.  这些政权的谴责者被监禁了。
近义词: critics, accusers, informers
反义词: supporters, defenders, apologists
词性: 名词复数

denouncer:/dɪˈnaʊnsər/
n.
谴责者；告发者
A person who publicly declares that someone or something is wrong or evil.
He was a vocal denouncer of the government's policies. 他直言不讳地谴责政府的政策。
近义词: critic, accuser, informer
反义词: supporter, defender, apologist
词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "denounce," which comes from Old French "denoncier"  and ultimately from Latin "denuntiare," meaning "to announce, proclaim, threaten."
