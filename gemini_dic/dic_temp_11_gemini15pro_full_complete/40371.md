contradictable:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəbl/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktəbl/| adj.|可反驳的，可驳斥的|Capable of being contradicted or denied; disputable.|His statement was easily contradictable. 他的说法很容易被反驳。|Synonyms: refutable, disputable, deniable, challengeable|Antonyms: irrefutable, undeniable, indisputable, unquestionable|adjective

contradict:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkt/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkt/| v.|反驳，驳斥|To assert the contrary of; deny the truth of (a statement).|The evidence contradicts his alibi.  证据反驳了他的不在场证明。|Synonyms: deny, refute, gainsay, oppose|Antonyms: confirm, support, corroborate, verify|verb

contradiction:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkʃn/| n.|矛盾，反驳|A combination of statements, ideas, or features of a situation that are opposed to one another.|There is a contradiction between what he says and what he does. 他的言行不一。|Synonyms: inconsistency, discrepancy, conflict, paradox|Antonyms: agreement, consistency, harmony, concord|noun
