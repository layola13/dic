albumin:/ˈælbjʊmɪn/
n.
白蛋白
A simple form of protein that is soluble in water and coagulable by heat, such as that found in egg white, milk, and blood serum.
Albumin levels in his blood were dangerously low. 他的血液中白蛋白水平低得危险。

albumin:/ˈæl.bjʊ.mɪn/
n.
白蛋白
A water-soluble protein found in blood plasma, egg white, milk, and other animal and vegetable tissues. It is essential for maintaining the osmotic pressure of the blood and transporting various substances.
The doctor checked her albumin levels as part of the routine blood test. 医生检查了她的白蛋白水平，作为常规血液检查的一部分。


近义词:protein, globulin (部分近义词，因为球蛋白是另一种类型的蛋白质)
反义词:None (白蛋白没有直接的反义词)
词性:noun
