classing-up:/ˈklæsɪŋ ˈʌp/
|v.|使高级化，使更有品位|Making something more elegant or sophisticated.|She's been classing up her apartment with new furniture and artwork. 她一直在用新的家具和艺术品提升她公寓的档次。|例句：He tried classing up the burger joint by adding tablecloths. 他试图通过添加桌布来提升汉堡店的档次。|近义词：improving, upgrading, refining, elevating|反义词：cheapening, degrading, vulgarizing|词性:动词

classing-up:/ˈklæsɪŋ ˈʌp/
|adj.|提升档次的，使更有品位的|(Used as an adjective, though less common)  Relating to the act of making something more elegant or sophisticated.|She made some classing-up changes to her wardrobe. 她对自己的衣橱进行了一些提升档次的改变。|例句：The restaurant underwent a classing-up renovation. 这家餐厅进行了一次提升档次的翻新。|近义词：improving, upgrading, refining, elevating|反义词：cheapening, degrading, vulgarizing|词性:形容词
