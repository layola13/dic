fucked-up:/ˌfʌkt ˈʌp/| adj.|糟糕的，混乱的，不正常的|Damaged, ruined, or in a state of disrepair; also, very unpleasant or upsetting.  It can also describe a person who is mentally or emotionally disturbed.|The printer is completely fucked-up. (打印机完全坏了。)

|近义词：screwed-up, messed-up, broken, damaged, ruined, chaotic, dysfunctional, troubled, disturbed|反义词：functional, working, orderly, normal, healthy|词性:形容词
fucked-up:/ˌfʌkt ˈʌp/| n.|混乱，糟糕的情况|A situation that is chaotic, dysfunctional, or problematic.|Dealing with this fucked-up situation is exhausting. (处理这种糟糕的局面令人筋疲力尽。)|近义词：mess, disaster, chaos|反义词：order, normalcy|词性:名词

词源：The term "fucked-up" originated as slang, combining the past participle of the vulgar verb "fuck" (meaning to damage, ruin, or spoil) with the adverb "up" (intensifying the meaning).  It gained widespread use in the 20th century.
