curtsying: /ˈkɜːtsiɪŋ/ /ˈkɜːrtsiɪŋ/ | n. | 行屈膝礼 | The act of performing a curtsy. |  Example: Her curtsying was elegant and graceful. 她行屈膝礼的姿势优雅而得体。

Example: The little girl practiced her curtsying before meeting the queen. 小女孩在见女王之前练习了屈膝礼。


近义词: bowing, genuflecting | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered a sign of respect, so a lack of respect could be an antonym conceptually) | 词性: noun (gerund)

词源: From the verb "curtsy," which is a shortening of "courtesy." Ultimately from Old French "curteisie," meaning "courtly behavior."
