decalcification:/diːˌkælsifiˈkeɪʃən/
n.
脱钙，去除钙盐
The process of removing calcium salts from bone or other tissue.
Bone decalcification is a crucial step in preparing specimens for microscopic examination.  骨脱钙是制备显微镜检查标本的关键步骤。
The archaeologist used a solution for decalcification of the ancient bones. 考古学家使用了一种溶液来对古代骨骼进行脱钙。
demineralization, calcium removal
