nonenumerative:/ˌnɒnɪˈnjuːmərətɪv/ | adj. | 非枚举的，非列举的 | Not listing items one by one; not exhaustive.  It refers to a list or description that does not attempt to mention every single item or possibility, but rather provides a general overview or representative sample. | This list of potential side effects is nonenumerative and may not include all possible reactions.  这份潜在副作用的清单并非详尽无遗，可能未包含所有可能的反应。

This guide provides a nonenumerative overview of key features.  本指南提供了主要功能的非详尽概述。 | 近义词: non-exhaustive, incomplete, partial | 反义词: exhaustive, complete, comprehensive | 词性: adjective
