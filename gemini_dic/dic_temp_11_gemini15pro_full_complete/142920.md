out-steps:/ˌaʊtˈstɛps/
verb
超越，胜过，比...做得更好
surpasses or exceeds in performance or action.
He out-stepped his rivals in the competition. 他在比赛中胜过了他的对手。
She consistently out-steps her colleagues in sales. 她的销售业绩一直超过她的同事。
Synonym: surpasses, exceeds, outdoes, outperforms
Antonym: underperforms, lags behind
v.
