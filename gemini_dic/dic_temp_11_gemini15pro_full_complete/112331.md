lorenzo:/ləˈrɛnzoʊ/ (US), /ləˈrɛntsəʊ/ (UK)| n. |洛伦佐|A masculine given name of Italian origin, derived from the Roman cognomen Laurentius, meaning "from Laurentum" (a city near Rome).  It is associated with the laurel plant, a symbol of victory and honor.|Lorenzo is a common name in Italy. 洛伦佐在意大利是一个常见的名字。|Lorenzo de' Medici was a powerful Italian statesman and patron of the arts. 洛伦佐·德·美第奇是一位强大的意大利政治家和艺术赞助人。|近义词:Laurence, Lawrence, Laurentius|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
