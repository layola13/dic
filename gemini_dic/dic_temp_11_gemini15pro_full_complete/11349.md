arenicolite:/ˌærɪˈnɪkəlaɪt/ |n.|海滩沙虫；沙栖动物的化石; a fossil of a marine worm that burrowed in sand |The arenicolite fossils were found in the sandstone cliffs. 在砂岩峭壁中发现了沙栖动物的化石。| 近义词:  lugworm, sandworm | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *arenicola* (sand-dweller) + -ite (suffix indicating a fossil). The Latin word *arenicola* is derived from *arena* (sand) + *-cola* (dweller).
