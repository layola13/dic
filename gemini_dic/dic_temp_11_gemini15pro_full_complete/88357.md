hippopotamus:/ˌhɪpəˈpɑːtəməs/ (US) /ˌhɪpəˈpɒtəməs/ (UK)| n. |河马|A large, thick-skinned, semiaquatic African mammal with short legs, a massive head, and a large mouth.| We saw a hippopotamus wallowing in the mud. 我们看到一只河马在泥里打滚。|Synonyms: hippo, river horse|Antonyms: None|Noun

hippopotami:/ˌhɪpəˈpɑːtəˌmaɪ/ (US) /ˌhɪpəˈpɒtəˌmaɪ/ (UK)| n. |河马（复数）|The plural form of hippopotamus.|  The hippopotami grazed peacefully on the riverbank. 河马在河岸上平静地吃草。|Synonyms: hippos, river horses|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἱπποπόταμος (hippopótamos, “river horse”), from ἵππος (híppos, “horse”) + ποταμός (potamós, “river”).
