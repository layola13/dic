murals:/ˈmjʊrəlz/ | n. | 壁画 | Large paintings applied directly to a wall or ceiling surface. | The murals depict scenes from local history.  这些壁画描绘了当地历史的场景。 | frescoes, paintings | graffiti (can be considered a type of mural but often unauthorized) | noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *murale, from Latin murus “wall.”
