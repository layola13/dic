back-alley:/ˌbækˈæli/| adj. n.|后巷的，不正当的；后巷|A back alley is a narrow, often dark and dirty street behind buildings, typically associated with illegal or illicit activities.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something shady or disreputable.|He grew up in the back alleys of Naples. 他在Naples的后巷长大。|近义词：backstreet, side street, alleyway|反义词:main street, avenue|词性:形容词, 名词
