koftas:/ˈkɒftəz/ (UK), /ˈkoʊftəz/ (US)| n. |肉丸，烤肉串|Small balls or fingers of minced meat, often spiced, usually cooked by frying or grilling.  Origin: from Hindi _kōftā_, from Persian _kūfta_ 'pounded, beaten'.|例句：We had koftas and rice for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了肉丸和米饭。

例句：The lamb koftas were served with a cooling yogurt dip.  羊肉丸配上清爽的酸奶蘸酱。|近义词：meatballs, rissoles|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


kofan:/ˈkoʊfɑːn/ (US)|n.| 棺材;灵柩|A chest or box in which a corpse is buried or cremated. Origin: from Greek _kophinos_ 'basket'.|例句：The coffin was carried into the church. 棺材被抬进了教堂。

例句：The family gathered around the coffin to say their final goodbyes. 家人们聚集在棺材周围做最后的告别。|近义词：casket|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
