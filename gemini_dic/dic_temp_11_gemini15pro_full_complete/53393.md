disalliege:/ˌdɪsəˈliːdʒ/
verb
解除效忠；背叛
To renounce allegiance to; abandon or betray.
The knight disallieged himself from the king. 骑士宣布不再效忠国王。
The soldiers disallieged themselves from their commander. 士兵们背叛了他们的指挥官。

近义词:rebel, revolt, mutiny, desert
反义词:support, obey, be loyal
