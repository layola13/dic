interculturally:/ˌɪntərˈkʌltʃərəli/
adv.|跨文化地|In a way that relates to or involves different cultures.|She works interculturally, bridging communication gaps between teams from different countries. 她跨文化地工作，弥合来自不同国家的团队之间的沟通差距。|近义词:cross-culturally|反义词:intraculturally|词性:副词

词源:
inter- (prefix): between, among (来自拉丁语 inter)
cultural (adjective): relating to culture (来自拉丁语 cultura)
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
