courts-martial: /ˈkɔːrts ˈmɑːrʃəl/ (US), /ˈkɔːts ˈmɑːʃəl/ (UK)| n.|军事法庭|A judicial court for trying members of the armed forces accused of offenses against military law.|The soldier faced a courts-martial for desertion.  这名士兵因擅离职守而面临军事法庭的审判。|Synonyms: military tribunal, court of inquiry|Antonyms: civilian court|Noun
