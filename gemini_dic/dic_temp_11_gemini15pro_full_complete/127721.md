negligences:/ˈneɡlɪdʒənsɪz/
n.
疏忽，玩忽职守，粗心大意
failure to take proper care in doing something.
His negligence resulted in the accident. 他的疏忽导致了这场事故。
近义词：carelessness, neglect, oversight, inattention, disregard
反义词：carefulness, diligence, attention, heedfulness
词性: 名词(plural)


negligence:/ˈneɡlɪdʒəns/
n.
疏忽，玩忽职守，粗心大意
failure to take proper care in doing something.
The fire was a result of negligence. 这场火灾是由于疏忽造成的。
近义词：carelessness, neglect, oversight, inattention, disregard
反义词：carefulness, diligence, attention, heedfulness
词性: 名词(singular)


词源:  From Old French negligence, from Latin negligentia (“negligence, carelessness”), from negligens (“negligent”), present participle of negligō (“neglect, disregard”), from nec (“not”) + legō (“gather, choose, read”).
