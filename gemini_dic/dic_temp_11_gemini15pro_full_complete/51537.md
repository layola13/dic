deturbate:/diːˈtɜːrbeɪt/
verb
使不安；使心神不宁；使烦恼
to make someone feel anxious and uneasy.

例句：
The news of the impending storm deturbated the residents of the coastal town.
即将来临的风暴的消息使沿海城镇的居民感到不安。

The constant noise from the construction site deturbated her concentration.
建筑工地持续不断的噪音扰乱了她的注意力。

近义词：
agitate, upset, disturb, unsettle, discompose

反义词：
calm, soothe, pacify, tranquilize

词性：v.
