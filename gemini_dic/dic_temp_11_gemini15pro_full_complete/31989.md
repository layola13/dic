chemotherapies:/ˌkiːmoʊˈθɛrəpiz/| n. |化疗（复数）|The treatment of disease by the use of chemical substances, especially the treatment of cancer by cytotoxic and other drugs.|She underwent several cycles of chemotherapies to combat the cancer. 她接受了几个疗程的化疗来对抗癌症。|Near synonyms: chemotherapy treatments, chemo (informal), drug therapies|Antonym: None (no direct opposite for a medical treatment)|Noun

Etymology:  From "chemo-" (chemical) + "therapy" (treatment).  "Chemo-" ultimately derives from Greek "khemeia" (alchemy), and "therapy" from Greek "therapeia" (healing, medical treatment).
