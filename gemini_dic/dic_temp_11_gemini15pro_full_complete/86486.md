heightens:/ˈhaɪtnz/| v. |加剧，增强，提高|To make something greater, stronger, or more intense.|The crisis heightens the need for international cooperation. 这场危机加剧了国际合作的需要.| intensifies, increases, escalates| diminishes, reduces, weakens|verb
Etymology-> From Middle English heghten, from Old English hēahtan (“to heighten, exalt”), from Proto-Germanic *hauhtjaną (“to make high”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kawk- (“high”). Cognate with Dutch hechten (“to fasten, attach”), German heften (“to attach, fasten”).
