dasi:/ˈdɑːsi/| n.|女仆，侍女 (尤指印度教寺庙中的)|A female servant or attendant, especially in a Hindu temple.|The dasi lit the lamps in the temple.  侍女在寺庙里点燃了灯。|synonyms: maid, servant, attendant|antonyms: master, mistress|noun
