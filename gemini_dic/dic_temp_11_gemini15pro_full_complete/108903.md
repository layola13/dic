ledgeless:/ˈledʒləs/
adjective
无边的；无檐的；无脊的
Lacking a ledge or edge; having no distinct or supporting edge.
The ledgeless pool seemed to stretch out infinitely.  无边的泳池似乎无限延伸。
The climbers faced a dangerous, ledgeless cliff.  攀登者面临着危险的、无边无际的悬崖。
近义词:rimless, edgeless, unbounded
反义词:edged, rimmed, bounded
词性:adjective
