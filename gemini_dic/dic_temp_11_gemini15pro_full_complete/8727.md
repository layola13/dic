antide:/ˈæntaɪd/| n.|抗体|A blood protein produced in response to and counteracting a specific antigen. Antibodies combine chemically with substances which the body recognizes as alien, such as bacteria, viruses, and foreign substances in the blood.|The body produces antibodies to fight infection.|近义词:immunoglobulin|反义词:antigen|词性:noun
