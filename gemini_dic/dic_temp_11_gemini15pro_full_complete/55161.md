dissemble:/dɪˈsembəl/ | v. | 掩饰；假装；装作 | To conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs. |  He dissembled his fear with a smile. 他用微笑掩饰了他的恐惧。

She dissembled her anger by speaking calmly. 她强作镇定掩饰她的愤怒。

| 近义词: disguise, conceal, feign, mask, pretend | 反义词: reveal, disclose, expose, be honest | verb
