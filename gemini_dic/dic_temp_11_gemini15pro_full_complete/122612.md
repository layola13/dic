modernnesses:/ˈmɒdərnɪsnɪsɪz/ /ˈmɑːdərnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
现代性（复数）
The state or quality of being modern, often used in the plural to refer to multiple instances or aspects of modernity.
The architect embraced the modernnesses of the city while respecting its historical core.  这位建筑师在尊重城市历史核心的同时，也欣然接受了城市的现代性。
The exhibition explored the conflicting modernnesses of the early 20th century.  展览探讨了20世纪早期相互冲突的现代性。
近义词: modernities, contemporariness
反义词: antiqueness, archaism
词性: n.

Etymology:
From modern + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Modern ultimately derives from Latin *modernus, meaning "of the present time," from modo, meaning "just now."
