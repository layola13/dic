gestural:/ˈdʒɛstʃərəl/| adj.|姿势的；手势的|Relating to, or characterized by, gestures. |She gave a gestural indication of agreement. 她做了一个表示同意的手势。|The conductor’s gestural communication with the orchestra was mesmerizing. 指挥家用手势与乐队交流，令人着迷。|近义词：nonverbal, expressive, communicative|反义词:verbal, spoken|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From gesture + -al.  Ultimately from Latin *gerere "to bear, carry, perform"
