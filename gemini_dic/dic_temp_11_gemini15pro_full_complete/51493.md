detractious:/dɪˈtrækʃəs/
adjective
诋毁的；贬低的；诽谤的
Tending to detract, especially from the reputation of something or someone.  Serving to diminish or take away.
Her detractious remarks about her colleagues damaged her own reputation. 她对同事的诋毁性言论损害了她自己的声誉。
Synonyms: disparaging, defamatory, slanderous, denigrating, belittling
Antonyms: complimentary, flattering, laudatory, praising
词性: adjective
