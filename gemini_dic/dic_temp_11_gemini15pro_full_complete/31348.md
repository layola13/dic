characteristics:/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪks/ | n. | 特征，特性 |  A distinguishing feature or quality, especially one that helps to identify a person or thing. |  One of the characteristics of this breed of dog is its long, silky coat.  这种狗的一个特点是它长而光滑的皮毛。| traits, features, attributes | opposites, differences | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *characteristicus*, from Greek kharaktēristikos, from kharaktērizein "to engrave, characterize," from kharaktēr "engraving tool, distinguishing mark, character," from kharassein "to scratch, engrave."
