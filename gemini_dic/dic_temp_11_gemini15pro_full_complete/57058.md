doubtingnesses:/ˈdaʊtɪŋnəsɪz/
noun
疑惑，怀疑，不确定
the state of being full of doubt; uncertainty
His doubtingnesses about the project caused delays.  他对这个项目的疑惑导致了延误。
近义词: doubts, uncertainties, hesitations, misgivings
反义词: certainties, convictions, assurances
词性: noun (plural)
