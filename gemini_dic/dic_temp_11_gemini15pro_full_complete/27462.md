campagna:/kæmˈpɑːnjə/ /kæmˈpeɪnjə/
n.
campaign; countryside; rural area
A military campaign; an organized course of action to achieve a goal; the countryside.
The Italian army launched a campagna against the Austrians. 意大利军队对奥地利人发动了一场战役。
We spent a relaxing week in the Italian campagna. 我们在意大利乡村度过了一个轻松的星期。
Synonyms: campaign, drive, crusade, expedition, countryside, rural area
Antonyms: city, urban area
noun
