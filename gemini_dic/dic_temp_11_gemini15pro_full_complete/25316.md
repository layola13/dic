bugle: /ˈbjuːɡl/ (US) /ˈbjuːɡəl/ (UK)| n. v.| 军号，喇叭；吹号|A brass instrument like a small trumpet, without valves, used for military signals.|The bugle sounded, signaling the start of the day. 军号声响起，标志着一天的开始。|He bugled reveille. 他吹响了起床号。|近义词：trumpet, horn|反义词: (n.) silence (referring to the absence of sound)|词性: noun, verb

bugle: /ˈbjuːɡl/ (US) /ˈbjuːɡəl/ (UK)| v.| 吹号|To play a bugle.|The soldier bugled the troops awake. 士兵吹号叫醒军队。|近义词：trumpet, sound|反义词:  (v.) silence, mute|词性: verb
