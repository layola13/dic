edictal:/iˈdɪktəl/| adj.| 口述的，命令的| Relating to or delivered by edict; declarative.| The king issued an edictal decree banning public gatherings. 国王颁布了一项禁止公众集会的法令。|近义词：decreed, proclaimed, ordained, mandated|反义词：requested, petitioned, suggested|词性:形容词

词源:  From Latin *edictalis*, from edictum (“edict”) + -alis (adjective suffix).
