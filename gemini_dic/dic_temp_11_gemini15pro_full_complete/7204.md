anglemeter:/ˈæŋɡəlˌmiːtər/
noun
测角仪，角度计
An instrument for measuring angles.
The surveyor used an anglemeter to determine the precise angle of the slope. 测量员使用测角仪来确定斜坡的精确角度。
Synonyms: protractor, goniometer, inclinometer
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a measuring instrument)
n.
