begrime: /bɪˈɡraɪm/
verb
弄脏，使污秽
to make something dirty with grime
例句：
The mechanic's hands were begrimed with oil.
技工的双手沾满了油污。

His clothes were begrimed after working in the garden all day.
在花园里工作了一整天后，他的衣服脏兮兮的。

近义词：soil, dirty, stain, besmirch
反义词：clean, cleanse, purify
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + grime.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over." Grime comes from Middle English *grime*, from Old Norse *gríma* "mask, hood."  The original sense likely referred to something covering or obscuring something else, which later evolved to mean dirt or soot.
