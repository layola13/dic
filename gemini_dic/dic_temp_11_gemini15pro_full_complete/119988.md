milliary:/ˈmɪliəri/ | adj. | 粟粒状的；无数的；微小的 | Characterized by tiny lesions resembling millet seeds; extremely numerous; very small. |  The doctor observed milliary tuberculosis in the patient's lungs. 医生在病人的肺部观察到粟粒性肺结核。

milliary:/ˈmɪliəri/ | n. | 粟粒疹 | A condition characterized by the eruption of numerous tiny vesicles. | The baby developed a milliary rash on its face. 婴儿脸上起了粟粒疹。

Etymology: From Latin *miliarius* ("containing a thousand," from *mille* "thousand"), later associated with *milium* ("millet"). The medical sense arose from the resemblance of the lesions to millet seeds.
