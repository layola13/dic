expeditionist:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃənɪst/
n.
探险家，远征队员
A person who participates in or leads expeditions, especially to remote or unexplored regions.
例句：The expeditionist spent months preparing for his journey to the Antarctic.  这位探险家花了几个月的时间准备他的南极之旅。

例句：The renowned expeditionist gave a lecture about her discoveries in the Amazon rainforest.  这位著名的探险家做了一个关于她在亚马逊雨林的发现的讲座。
近义词：explorer, adventurer, traveler
反义词：homebody, stay-at-home
词性：noun
