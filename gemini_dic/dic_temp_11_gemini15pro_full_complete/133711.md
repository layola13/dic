nonfrigidness:/nɒnˈfrɪdʒɪdnəs/
n.
non-frigidity; warmth.
lack of coldness; warmth, especially of manner.  The nonfrigidness of her greeting surprised him after their last encounter.
Near synonyms:  warmth, cordiality, geniality, affability
Antonyms: coldness, frigidity, aloofness, iciness
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix meaning "not") + frigid (from Latin frigidus, meaning "cold") + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality)
