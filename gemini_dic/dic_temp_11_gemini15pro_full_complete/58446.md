ducks:/dʌks/
n.
鸭子（复数）
Plural form of "duck," referring to more than one duck.
The ducks are swimming in the pond.  鸭子们正在池塘里游泳。
The farmer raises ducks for eggs and meat.  这位农民养鸭子是为了获取蛋和肉。
近义词:waterfowl
反义词:(none specific, possibly other types of birds like chickens or geese if contrasting farm animals)
词性:noun


duck:/dʌk/
n.
鸭子
A waterbird with a broad blunt bill, short legs, webbed feet, and a waddling gait.
A duck quacked loudly. 一只鸭子大声地叫着。
The children fed the ducks in the park. 孩子们在公园里喂鸭子。
近义词:drake (male), hen (female)
反义词:(none specific, possibly other types of birds)
词性:noun


duck:/dʌk/
v.
躲避，闪避
To lower the head or body suddenly, especially to avoid being hit or seen.
He ducked under the low-hanging branch. 他躲开了低垂的树枝。
She ducked her head to avoid being seen. 她低下头以免被看见。
近义词:dodge, avoid, evade
反义词:confront, face
词性:verb


Etymology:
From Old English *duce, related to Old Norse dūka "to dive, duck," from Proto-Germanic *dūkōną.
