fearfulest:/ˈfɪərfələst/ | adj. |最害怕的，极度恐惧的 |  Superlative form of fearful: most fearful.  It is an archaic and non-standard form, rarely used in modern English. |  He had the fearfulest dreams after watching the horror movie. 他看完恐怖电影后做了最可怕的梦。 | 近义词: most fearful, terrified, petrified | 反义词: fearless, brave, courageous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *ferfulest, from Old English *fǣrfullost, superlative of fǣrfull (“fearful”).
