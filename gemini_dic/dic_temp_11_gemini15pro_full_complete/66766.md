exost:/ˈɛksɒst/ | n. |骨疣，外生骨疣 |A benign bony outgrowth, usually capped with cartilage, projecting from the surface of a bone. | 例句：The doctor diagnosed the bump on my knee as an exost.  医生诊断我膝盖上的肿块是骨疣。

例句：Multiple exostoses can cause pain and limit joint movement. 多发性骨疣会导致疼痛并限制关节活动。| 近义词：osteochondroma | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially osteolysis - bone resorption) |词性: noun
