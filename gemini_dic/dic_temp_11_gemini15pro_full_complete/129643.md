non-corporation:/ˌnɑːn kɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 非公司，非法人团体 | An entity that is not incorporated as a company and therefore does not have the same legal status or protections. It can include partnerships, sole proprietorships, and other unincorporated associations. | Example 1:  The local bakery operates as a non-corporation, with the owners sharing profits and liabilities directly.  当地面包店作为非公司运营，店主直接分享利润和承担责任。

Example 2:  Many non-corporations are small businesses or family-owned enterprises. 许多非公司是小企业或家族企业。| Synonym: unincorporated business, unincorporated association | Antonym: corporation, incorporated company | noun
