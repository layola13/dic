feel-up-to:/fiːl ˈʌp tuː/| phrasal verb | 觉得能够胜任，有心情做 | To have the energy or desire to do something.  | Example: I don't feel up to going out tonight. 我今晚不想出去。| Synonym: capable of, inclined to, in the mood for | Antonym: incapable of, disinclined to, not in the mood for | phrasal verb

feel-up-to:/fiːl ˈʌp tuː/| phrasal verb | 胜任 | To be competent or capable enough to do something. | Example: Do you feel up to the challenge? 你能胜任这项挑战吗？| Synonym: competent for, capable of, equal to | Antonym: incompetent for, incapable of, unequal to | phrasal verb
