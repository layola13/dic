impugners:/ɪmˈpjuːnərz/| n.|质疑者；非难者；攻击者|People who challenge the truth or validity of something, often by casting doubt on someone's motives or character.  They attack or oppose something, especially someone's honesty or reputation.|The politician's integrity was questioned by several impugners. 这位政治家的正直受到了几位质疑者的质疑。|Critics, dissenters, challengers, questioners, attackers, disputants|Supporters, defenders, upholders|noun
