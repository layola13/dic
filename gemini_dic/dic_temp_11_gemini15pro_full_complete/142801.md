ouranography: /ˌʊərəˈnɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 天象学，天空描述 | The study or description of the heavens. |  Ouranography is a less common term than uranography, which refers to the mapping of the celestial bodies.  天象学是一个不太常见的术语，与uranography相比，后者指的是绘制天体图。 |  Ancient Egyptians practiced a form of ouranography, observing and recording the movements of stars. 古埃及人实行一种天象学，观察并记录星星的运动。 |Synonym: uranography | Antonym:  N/A | noun
