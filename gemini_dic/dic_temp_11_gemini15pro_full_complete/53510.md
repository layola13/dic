disassociate:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃieɪt/ /dɪsəˈsəʊsieɪt/
verb
分离；脱离；不相往来
To separate from something; to end a connection or association with something.
He tried to disassociate himself from the scandal. 他试图撇清自己与这桩丑闻的关系。
dissociate, disconnect, separate
associate, connect, join
