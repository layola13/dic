burnouts:/ˈbɜːrnaʊts/
noun
精疲力竭，倦怠
the state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged or excessive stress.
Many teachers are suffering from burnout. 许多老师都感到精疲力竭。
exhaustion, fatigue, weariness
energy, enthusiasm
n.

burnout:/ˈbɜːrnaʊt/
noun
烧毁，烧坏
an instance of something being destroyed by fire.
The burnout destroyed the entire building.  这场火灾烧毁了整栋建筑。
fire, combustion, conflagration
extinguishment, preservation
n.
