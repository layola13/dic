overappreciation: /ˌoʊvərəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/
n.|过高评价；过分赏识|The overappreciation of the company's stock led to a market bubble.  这家公司股票的过高评价导致了市场泡沫。|例句：His overappreciation of his own abilities led him to make some poor decisions. 他对自己能力的过高评价导致他做出了一些糟糕的决定。|近义词：overvaluation, overestimation|反义词: undervaluation, underestimation|词性:名词

overappreciate: /ˌoʊvərəˈpriːʃiˌeɪt/
v.|过高评价；过分赏识|Investors overappreciated the potential of the new technology. 投资者过高估计了这项新技术的潜力。|例句：Don't overappreciate the importance of this meeting; it's just a routine check-in. 不要过分高估这次会议的重要性；这只是一次例行检查。|近义词：overvalue, overestimate|反义词: undervalue, underestimate|词性:动词
