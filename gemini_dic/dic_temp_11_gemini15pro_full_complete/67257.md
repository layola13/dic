expurgating:/ˈekspərɡeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|删除，清除（不当内容）|Removing or altering parts of a text or other work that are considered offensive or unsuitable.|The editor was tasked with expurgating the offensive language from the novel. 编辑的任务是从小说中删除冒犯性的语言。|The censors expurgated all references to the rebellion from the history books. 审查员从历史书中删除了所有关于叛乱的内容。|近义词：censoring, bowdlerizing, purging, sanitizing|反义词：including, adding, restoring|词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *expurgatus*, past participle of *expurgare* "to clean out, purify," from *ex-* "out" + *purgare* "to cleanse."
