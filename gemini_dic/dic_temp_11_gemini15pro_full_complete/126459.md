mythes:/mɪθs/| n.|神话，传说，虚构的故事|A traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events. |The ancient Greeks had many myths about their gods and goddesses. 古希腊人有很多关于他们神灵的传说。|synonyms: legend, fable, lore, saga, folklore|antonyms: reality, truth, fact|noun


myths:/mɪθs/| n.|错误的观念，谬见，迷思|A widely held but false belief or idea.|There are many myths about healthy eating. 关于健康饮食有很多错误的观念。|synonyms: misconception, fallacy, delusion, untruth|antonyms: truth, reality, fact|noun
