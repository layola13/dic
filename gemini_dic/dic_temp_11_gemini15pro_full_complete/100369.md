interstrand:/ˌɪntərˈstrænd/| adj.|链间的，股间的|Existing or occurring between strands, especially of a DNA or RNA molecule.|例句：Interstrand crosslinking can damage DNA and lead to cell death.  链间交联会损害DNA并导致细胞死亡。

例句：The interstrand hydrogen bonds hold the two strands of the DNA double helix together. 链间的氢键将DNA双螺旋的两条链连接在一起。|近义词：intermolecular|反义词：intrastrand|词性：形容词
