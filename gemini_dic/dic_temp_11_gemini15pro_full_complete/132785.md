nonductility:/ˌnɒndʌkˈtɪləti/
n.|非延展性，不延展性|The nonductility of the material made it unsuitable for the project.  这种材料的非延展性使其不适合该项目。|The nonductility of cast iron makes it prone to cracking under stress. 铸铁的非延展性使其在压力下容易开裂。|Synonyms: brittleness, fragility, inflexibility|Antonyms: ductility, malleability, flexibility|noun

nonductile:/ˌnɒndʌkˈtaɪl/
adj.|非延展性的，不延展的|A nonductile material is likely to fracture under stress. 非延展性材料在压力下容易断裂。|The bridge collapsed due to the use of nonductile steel beams.  桥梁倒塌是由于使用了非延展性钢梁。|Synonyms: brittle, fragile, inflexible|Antonyms: ductile, malleable, flexible|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + ductile + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  "Ductile" comes from Latin "ducere" meaning "to lead" or "to draw out".
