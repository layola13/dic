butcherers:/ˈbʊtʃərərz/
n.
屠夫们，刽子手们
People who slaughter animals for meat, or, figuratively, those who kill cruelly and indiscriminately.
The butcherers prepared the meat for the market. 屠夫们准备把肉送到市场上去。
The dictator and his butcherers were responsible for the deaths of thousands. 独裁者和他的刽子手们对成千上万人的死亡负有责任。
synonyms: slaughterers, killers, murderers, executioners
antonyms: protectors, rescuers, saviors
noun
