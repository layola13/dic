gynodioecious:/ˌɡaɪnodaɪˈiːʃəs/
adjective
雌雄异株的
Having female and hermaphrodite flowers on separate plants.
Gynodioecy is a dimorphic breeding system in which individual plants in a population are either female or hermaphroditic.
Some plant species, like the thyme *Thymus vulgaris*, are gynodioecious. 一些植物物种，例如百里香*Thymus vulgaris*，是雌雄异株的。
The gynodioecious nature of the plant allows for a mixed mating strategy.  这种植物的雌雄异株特性允许混合交配策略。
synonym: dioecious (in a broader sense)
antonym: hermaphroditic (in a narrower sense), monoecious
词性:形容词
