cady:/ˈkædi/| n.|球童；助理；年轻女子（非正式）|A person who carries a golfer's clubs and provides other assistance during a round of golf; also, informally, a young woman.|例句：He hired a cady to carry his clubs. 他雇了一个球童来背他的球杆。

例句：She's a bright young cady. 她是一个聪明伶俐的年轻女子。|近义词：assistant, helper, girl, young woman|反义词:None|词性:noun
