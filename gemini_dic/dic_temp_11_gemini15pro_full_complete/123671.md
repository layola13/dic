moodies:/ˈmuːdiz/| n.| 心情不好的人；情绪化的人|People who are moody, experiencing fluctuating or unpredictable moods.  These individuals are often characterized by emotional instability and shifts in temperament.|"The moodies in the office can be difficult to work with." 办公室里那些情绪化的人很难共事。|近义词：grumps, sulkers, whiners|反义词：optimists, cheerful people|noun
