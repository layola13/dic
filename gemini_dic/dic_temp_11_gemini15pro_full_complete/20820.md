blastema: /blæsˈtiːmə/ | n. | 芽基; 母体; 再生芽 | A mass of undifferentiated cells capable of growth and differentiation, especially one that forms a new plant or animal part. |  The blastema gives rise to the regenerating limb. 芽基会生长成新的肢体。

The regeneration of the tail depends on the formation of a blastema.  尾巴的再生依赖于芽基的形成。| 近义词: germ, primordium, rudiment | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Blastem*, from Ancient Greek *βλάστημα* (blástēma, “sprout, shoot, bud”), from *βλαστάνω* (blastánō, “to sprout, bud”).
