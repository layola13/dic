nonpejoratively:/ˌnɒnpɪˈdʒɔːrətɪvli/ | adv. | 无贬义地；不带贬损意味地 |  In a way that does not express or imply a negative judgment or criticism.  Used to indicate that a word or phrase typically considered offensive is being used in a neutral or descriptive manner. | He used the term "naive" nonpejoratively, simply meaning lacking in experience. 他用“天真”一词并无贬义，只是指缺乏经验。 | neutrally, objectively, impartially | pejoratively, derogatorily, disparagingly | adverb
