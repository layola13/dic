electioneering:/ɪˌlɛkʃəˈnɪərɪŋ/
n.
竞选活动，拉票活动
the activity of trying to persuade people to vote for a particular political party or candidate
The candidate's aggressive electioneering tactics alienated many voters.  这位候选人咄咄逼人的竞选策略疏远了许多选民。

近义词: campaigning, canvassing, lobbying
反义词: apathy, indifference
