disembowelling:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəlɪŋ/
noun
取出内脏; 开膛破肚
The act of removing the internal organs, especially the intestines, from a body.
The disembowelling of the fish was a messy but necessary task.  取出鱼的内脏是一项凌乱但必要的任务。
evisceration, gutting
--
verb
(disembowel) /dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/
取出…的内脏; 开膛破肚
To remove the internal organs, especially the intestines, from a body.
The killer disembowelled his victims.  凶手取出受害者的内脏。
eviscerate, gut
