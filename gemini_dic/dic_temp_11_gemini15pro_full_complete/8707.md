anticriticalness:/ˌæntiˈkrɪtɪkəlnəs/
noun
反对批评；不接受批评
The state or quality of being opposed to or resistant to criticism.  A lack of receptiveness to critical evaluation or feedback.
His anticriticalness made it difficult for the team to improve their project.  他的不接受批评的态度使得团队难以改进他们的项目。
noncriticalness, defensiveness
criticalness, receptiveness
词性: 名词
