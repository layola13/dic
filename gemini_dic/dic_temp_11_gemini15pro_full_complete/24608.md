broilers:/ˈbrɔɪlərz/| n. |肉鸡，供烤焙的鸡|A young chicken raised specifically for meat.  These chickens are typically raised in controlled environments and reach slaughter weight quickly. |例句：The farmer raises thousands of broilers.  这个农民饲养了数千只肉鸡。

例句：Broilers are a popular source of protein. 肉鸡是蛋白质的流行来源。|近义词：roasters, fryer chickens|反义词:layer hens (蛋鸡)|词性:noun
