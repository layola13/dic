fossilation:/fɒsɪˈleɪʃən/ /fɑːsɪˈleɪʃən/
n.|化石作用，石化作用|The process of becoming a fossil; fossilization involves the gradual replacement of organic materials with minerals, preserving the original structure of the organism.|The dinosaur skeleton underwent fossilation over millions of years.  这具恐龙骨架经过数百万年的化石作用。|petrification, mineralization|decomposition, decay|noun
