nonhomogeneity:/ˌnɒnhəmədʒəˈniːəti/ | n. | 非均匀性，不均匀性 |  Lack of uniformity; the state of not being homogeneous.  The condition of a substance or system in which its components are not evenly distributed, leading to variations in properties or composition across different locations. | The nonhomogeneity of the soil caused problems for the construction project. 土壤的非均匀性给建筑工程带来了问题。| The nonhomogeneity of the mixture made it difficult to analyze. 混合物的非均匀性使其难以分析。 |  heterogeneity, disuniformity, unevenness, variability | homogeneity, uniformity, evenness | noun
