chorally:/ˈkɔːrəli/ | adv. |合唱地，齐声地| In a manner resembling a chorus; performed by a chorus or choir. | The hymn was sung chorally by the congregation.  会众齐声唱起了赞美诗。| 近义词: in unison, together, collectively | 反义词: individually, separately | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *choralis, from Latin chorus (“chorus, choir”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek χορός (khorós, “chorus, choral dance”).
