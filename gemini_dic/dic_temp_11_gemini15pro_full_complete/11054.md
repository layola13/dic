arch-foe:/ˈɑːrtʃˌfoʊ/ (US) /ˈɑːtʃˌfəʊ/ (UK)
n.|主要敌人；宿敌|A principal enemy; someone who has been an enemy for a long time.
|例句：The two politicians were arch-foes, constantly battling each other in the media. 这两位政治家是宿敌，经常在媒体上互相攻击。

|例句：He considered his former business partner his arch-foe. 他把他以前的生意伙伴视为他的主要敌人。
|近义词：nemesis, archenemy, adversary, rival, opponent
|反义词：ally, friend, supporter
|词性:noun

Etymology:
"arch-" (prefix): from Greek "arkhos," meaning "chief," "principal," or "ruler."
"-foe": from Old English "fāh," meaning "hostile," "inimical," or "fated to die."
