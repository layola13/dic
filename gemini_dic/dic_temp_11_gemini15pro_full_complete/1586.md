achromatic:/ˌeɪkrəˈmætɪk/| adj.|消色差的，无色的|Transmitting light without separating it into constituent colors; free from color; colorless. |The telescope uses an achromatic lens to minimize chromatic aberration.|Synonyms: colorless, uncolored, neutral, monochrome |Antonyms: chromatic, colorful, colored|adjective
