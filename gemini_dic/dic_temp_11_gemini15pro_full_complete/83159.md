gutterpup:/ˈɡʌtərˌpʌp/| n. |流浪狗，卑贱的人，无赖|A dog of mixed or unknown breed, often found living on the streets; a person considered to be of low social standing or morally reprehensible.|The abandoned gutterpup scavenged for scraps of food. 这只被遗弃的流浪狗在垃圾堆里寻找食物残渣。|近义词：mongrel, cur, stray, outcast|反义词:pedigree dog, aristocrat|词性:名词
