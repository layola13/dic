benefitting:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtɪŋ/
verb
受益; 得益于
Present participle of benefit.  It describes the act of receiving an advantage or good effect from something.
The new regulations are benefitting small businesses. 新规定使小型企业受益。
Many people are benefitting from the new healthcare system. 许多人正受益于新的医疗保健系统。
synonyms: profiting, gaining, improving
antonyms: harming, damaging, hindering
词性: verb (动词现在分词)
