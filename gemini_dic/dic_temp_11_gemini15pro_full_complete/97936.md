innovates: /ˈɪnəveɪts/
verb
创新，改革
introduces new methods, ideas, or products.
The company constantly innovates to stay ahead of the competition.  这家公司不断创新以保持竞争优势。
Tesla innovates in the electric vehicle market. 特斯拉在电动汽车市场进行创新。

synonyms: creates, pioneers, develops, introduces, modernizes
antonyms: stagnates, imitates, copies, preserves, maintains
词性: verb (动词)
