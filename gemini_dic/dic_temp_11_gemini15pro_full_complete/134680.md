nonmarketables:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪtəbəlz/
|n.|滞销品；非上市证券；非市场化产品|Goods or securities that cannot be readily sold or traded in a public market.|The company had a large inventory of nonmarketables that were difficult to liquidate. 这家公司有大量难以清算的滞销品。|Synonyms: unsalable, unmarketable, illiquid|Antonyms: marketable, liquid, salable
|noun|

nonmarketable:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪtəbəl/
|adj.|非市场化的；无法出售的；滞销的|Not easily sold or traded in a public market.|The nonmarketable assets were a drag on the company's balance sheet. 这些无法出售的资产拖累了公司的资产负债表。|Synonyms: unsalable, unvendible, illiquid|Antonyms: marketable, liquid, salable
|adjective|
