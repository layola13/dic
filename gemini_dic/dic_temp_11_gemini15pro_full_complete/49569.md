demented:/dɪˈmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 痴呆的，精神错乱的 | Driven mad; insane. Showing the effects of dementia, especially memory loss. |  The demented old man wandered the streets.  这个痴呆的老人在街上游荡。 |  insane, mad, crazy, deranged | sane, lucid, rational | adjective

demented:/dɪˈmɛntɪd/ | adj. |  疯狂的，失去理智的 |  Behaving irrationally due to anger, excitement, etc.  | She was demented with worry. 她担心得快要疯了。 |  frantic, frenzied, beside oneself | calm, composed, collected | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēmentātus, past participle of dēmentāre (“to make mad”), from Latin dē (“out of, from”) + mēns (“mind”).
