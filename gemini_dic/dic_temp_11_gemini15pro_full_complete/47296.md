de-icer:/dɪˈˈaɪsər/
noun|除冰器，除冰剂|A substance used to remove ice or prevent ice from forming.|The pilot sprayed de-icer on the wings before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前在机翼上喷洒了除冰剂。|Synonyms: antifreeze, thawing agent|Antonyms: ice, freezing agent|n.
de-icer:/dɪˈˈaɪsər/
verb|除冰|To remove ice or prevent ice from forming.|We need to de-ice the car before we can drive. 我们需要先给车除冰才能开车。|Synonyms: defrost, thaw|Antonyms: freeze, ice up|v.
