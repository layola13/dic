crocus: /ˈkroʊkəs/ (US) /ˈkrəʊkəs/ (UK)| n.|番红花，藏红花|A small, spring-flowering plant of the iris family, with brightly coloured, cup-shaped flowers.|例句：The crocuses are blooming in the garden. 番红花在花园里盛开了。

例句：She planted some crocus bulbs in the fall. 她在秋天种了一些番红花球茎。

近义词：spring flower, bulb|反义词:|词性: noun
