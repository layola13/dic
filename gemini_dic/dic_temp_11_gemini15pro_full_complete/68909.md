fast:/fæst/| adj. adv.|快的，迅速的；紧紧地；坚固的|Moving or capable of moving at high speed; firmly fixed or attached; abstaining from food.|例句：The car is very fast. 这辆车很快。|近义词：quick, rapid, swift, speedy|反义词：slow, sluggish|词性:形容词/副词
fast:/fæst/| v.|禁食，斋戒|Abstain from all or some kinds of food or drink, especially as a religious observance.|例句：Muslims fast during Ramadan. 穆斯林在斋月期间禁食。|近义词：abstain|反义词：feast|词性:动词
fast:/fæst/| n.|禁食，斋戒|An act or period of abstaining from all or some kinds of food or drink.|例句：She observed a strict fast for a week. 她进行了一周严格的禁食。|近义词：abstinence|反义词：feast|词性:名词

**词源:**

From Old English *fæst*, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *vast* and German *fest*.
