armiferous:/ɑːrˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
持有武器的；有防御器官的
Bearing arms or weapons; furnished with defensive organs.
The armiferous knight rode into battle. 持有武器的骑士骑马投入战斗。
近义词: armed, weaponed, fortified
反义词: unarmed, defenseless
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *armifer*, from *arma* "arms" + *-fer* "bearing".
