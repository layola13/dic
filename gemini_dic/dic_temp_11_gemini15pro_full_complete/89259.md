hominoid:/ˈhɒmɪnɔɪd/ | n. adj. |类人猿；类人猿的|A primate of the superfamily Hominoidea, such as a gibbon, orangutan, gorilla, chimpanzee, or human.|例句：Chimpanzees and gorillas are hominoids. 黑猩猩和大猩猩是类人猿。|例句：The study of hominoid evolution is crucial to understanding human origins. 对类人猿进化的研究对于理解人类起源至关重要。|近义词：ape, anthropoid|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-hominoid primate") |词性: noun, adjective
