dreamland:/ˈdriːmlænd/| n.|梦境，梦幻之地，理想国|A state of sleep or of being lost in dreams; a place or state of unrealistic or fantastic imagery or illusion.|She drifted off to dreamland. 她渐渐进入了梦乡。|Synonyms: fantasy, dream world, never-never land, utopia|Antonyms: reality, actuality|noun

dreamland:/ˈdriːmlænd/| n.|极乐世界，美好的地方|A place or situation considered to be ideal or perfect.|For antique car lovers, this show is dreamland. 对于古董车爱好者来说，这场展览简直就是天堂。|Synonyms: paradise, heaven, eden|Antonyms: hell, nightmare|noun
