congeneracy:/kənˈdʒɛnərəsi/ | n. |同类性，同源性，同属性|The state or property of belonging to the same genus or kind.  Sharing a common ancestor or origin.|The congeneracy of the two species was evident in their similar skeletal structures. 这两个物种的同源性在其相似的骨骼结构中显而易见。|近义词：similarity, affinity, kinship, relatedness |反义词: dissimilarity, difference, unrelatedness |词性: noun

congener:/ˈkɒndʒɪnə(r)/ | n. |同类，同属，同源物|A thing of the same kind or nature as another.  A member of the same genus or group, especially as related to chemical substances or biological species.|Ethanol and methanol are congeners, both being types of alcohol. 乙醇和甲醇是同类物，都是醇类。|近义词：relative, analogue, counterpart |反义词: - |词性: noun
