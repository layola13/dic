cames:/keɪmz/ | n. | 织布工；梳毛机; (尤指旧时)梳理羊毛者 | A worker who prepares wool for spinning, especially one using a hand-operated comb. |  The cames carefully combed the wool before spinning. 梳毛工在纺纱前仔细地梳理羊毛。 | 近义词: wool comber, carder | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *kemben, from Old English *cemban (“to comb”), from Proto-Germanic *kambijaną (“to comb”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰembh- (“to bite, chew; tooth, nail”). Cognate with Scots kame (“to comb”), Saterland Frisian kamje (“to comb”), West Frisian kemme (“to comb”), Dutch kammen (“to comb”), German kämmen (“to comb”), Danish kæmme (“to comb”), Swedish kamma (“to comb”), Icelandic kemba (“to comb”). Related to comb.
