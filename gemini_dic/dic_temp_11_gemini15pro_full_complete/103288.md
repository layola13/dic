Example 2: Her jibbah flowed gracefully in the desert breeze. 她的吉贝服在沙漠的微风中优雅地飘动。 | Near Synonyms: djellaba, abaya, kaftan, robe | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing or tight-fitting clothing) | Noun
