graille:/ɡʁaj/ (French)| n.|食物; 粗劣的食物; 伙食|Food; grub; (derogatory) low-quality food.  It often implies eating greedily or messily.|例句：Ils ont englouti toute la graille.  They gobbled up all the food.|近义词：nourriture, bouffe (informal), victuailles (formal)|反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps "jeûne" - fast)|词性:noun
