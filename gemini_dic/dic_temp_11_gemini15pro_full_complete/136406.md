nonretaliation:/ˌnɒn rɪˌtæliˈeɪʃən/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˌtæliˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 不报复，不打击报复 |  The absence of retaliation or revenge; a policy or practice of refraining from retaliating.  | The company has a strict policy of nonretaliation against employees who report misconduct.  这家公司对举报不当行为的员工有严格的不报复政策。 |  non-reprisal, forbearance | reprisal, retaliation, revenge | noun

nonretaliation:/ˌnɒn rɪˌtæliˈeɪʃən/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˌtæliˈeɪʃən/ | adj. | 不报复的 |  Characterized by the absence of retaliation. (Less common usage as an adjective) | The company implemented nonretaliation measures to protect whistleblowers.  公司实施了不报复措施来保护举报人。 | non-reprisal | reprisal | adjective
