nonartistically:/ˌnɑːnɑːrˈtɪstɪkli/
adverb
非艺术地；缺乏艺术性地
Not in an artistic way; lacking artistic skill or sensitivity.
She arranged the flowers nonartistically, simply bunching them together. 她很不艺术地插花，只是简单地把它们捆在一起。
Near Synonyms: clumsily, crudely, inartistically, tastelessly, unskillfully
Antonyms: artistically, skillfully, tastefully
词性: 副词
