archenteron:/ˌɑːrkˈɛntərɒn/ (US) /ˌɑːkˈɛntərən/ (UK)| n. |原肠|The primitive digestive cavity of an embryo at the gastrula stage, formed by invagination of the blastula.  It is the precursor to the gut. |The archenteron eventually develops into the digestive tract of the animal.|Synonym: gastrocoel|Antonym:  (No direct antonym)|noun
