frogged:/frɑːɡd/
verb
(使)疲惫不堪，(使)筋疲力尽
exhausted, worn out, especially after strenuous activity.
The hikers were completely frogged after their three-day trek in the mountains.  徒者们在山上跋涉三天后筋疲力尽。
Near Synonyms: exhausted, drained, spent, beat, wiped out
Antonyms: energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性: 动词

frogged:/frɑːɡd/
adjective
疲惫不堪的，筋疲力尽的
exhausted, worn out, especially after strenuous activity.
My legs were frogged after running the marathon. 我的腿在跑完马拉松后疲惫不堪。
Near Synonyms: exhausted, drained, spent, beat, wiped out
Antonyms: energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性: 形容词
