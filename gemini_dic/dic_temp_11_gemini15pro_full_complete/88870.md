hoisters:/ˈhɔɪstərz/
n.
起重机操作员，吊车操作员
People who operate hoists, lifting devices used to raise or lower heavy objects.
The hoisters carefully lifted the steel beams into place.  起重机操作员小心地将钢梁吊到位。
The experienced hoisters ensured the safe transport of the cargo.  经验丰富的起重机操作员确保了货物的安全运输。
Synonyms: crane operators, lift operators
Antonyms: N/A
