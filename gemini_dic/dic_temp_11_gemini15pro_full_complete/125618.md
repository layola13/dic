multiyear:/ˌmʌltiˈjɪr/| adj.|多年的，持续数年的|Spanning or extending over several years.  Formed by combining "multi-" (meaning many or multiple) with "year".  Essentially means "many years."|The government announced a multiyear plan to improve infrastructure. 政府宣布了一项为期多年的基础设施改善计划。

The study is a multiyear project involving researchers from several universities.  这项研究是一个涉及多所大学研究人员的多年项目。|近义词：long-term, continuing, ongoing|反义词：short-term, annual|词性:形容词
