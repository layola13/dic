overcuriousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊrɪəsnɪsɪz/| n. | 过分好奇，过度好奇 |The state of being excessively curious.  An instance of being overcurious. |  Her overcuriousnesses led her to snoop through her friend's diary. 她的过度好奇让她偷看了朋友的日记。|近义词：inquisitiveness, nosiness |反义词: apathy, indifference |词性: Noun
词源: From "over" (excessive) + "curious" (eager to know or learn) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).
