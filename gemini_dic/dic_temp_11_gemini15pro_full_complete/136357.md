nonresonant:/nɒnˈrɛzənənt/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈrɛzənənt/ (US)| adj.|非共振的，非谐振的|Not resonant; not exhibiting resonance.  A nonresonant system does not amplify or oscillate at a particular frequency.|The nonresonant frequency response of the system was flat across the measured range. 系统的非共振频率响应在测量范围内是平坦的。|The bridge is designed to be nonresonant to prevent oscillations caused by wind. 这座桥被设计成非共振的，以防止风引起的振动。|近义词:non-vibrating, undamped (in some contexts)|反义词:resonant, resonant vibrating|词性:形容词
