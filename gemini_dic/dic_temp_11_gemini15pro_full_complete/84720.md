handily:/ˈhændɪli/| adv.|容易地，方便地，熟练地|easily, conveniently, skillfully; in a handy manner.  It refers to doing something with ease and efficiency.|She handily defeated her opponent in the election. 她轻松地在选举中击败了对手。|近义词：easily, conveniently, effortlessly, skillfully|反义词:awkwardly, clumsily, with difficulty|副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From handy + -ly.  "Handy" comes from Old English *handi "at hand, ready," related to hand.
