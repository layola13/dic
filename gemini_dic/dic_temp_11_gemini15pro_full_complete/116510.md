mateship:/ˈmeɪtʃɪp/ | n. | 友情；伙伴关系；同志情谊 |  The quality of being mates; comradeship or friendship, especially between men;  a bond of loyalty and mutual support. It emphasizes equality, loyalty, and friendship.  |  Example 1: Their mateship was forged in the trenches of World War I.  他们的友谊是在一战的战壕里建立起来的。

Example 2:  Mateship is a core value in Australian culture.  伙伴关系是澳大利亚文化的核心价值观。|  Synonyms:  companionship, camaraderie, fellowship, friendship, brotherhood  | Antonyms:  enmity, hostility, antagonism | noun
