culturing:/ˈkʌltʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 培养；培育；教养 |The process of growing something, usually cells or bacteria, in a controlled environment.  It can also refer to the development of a particular skill or quality.|  例句：The culturing of bacteria in the lab requires sterile conditions.  实验室细菌培养需要无菌环境。|近义词：cultivation, growing, development, nurturing |反义词：neglect, suppression |词性:noun (gerund or verbal noun)

culturing:/ˈkʌltʃərɪŋ/ | adj. | 培养的;栽培的| Related to the process of culturing. | 例句：The culturing medium was contaminated. 培养基被污染了。 | 近义词：cultivating, growing | 反义词： N/A | 词性:adjective (present participle)
