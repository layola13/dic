kro:/kroʊ/ | n. | crow | 乌鸦 | A large, black bird with a harsh cry. |  Example: A kro perched on the branch. 一只乌鸦栖息在树枝上。

Example: The kro's caw echoed through the forest. 乌鸦的叫声在森林中回荡。 | 近义词: raven | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: noun
