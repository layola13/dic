outwiggle: /ˌaʊtˈwɪɡəl/
verb
胜过…的扭动；比…更灵活地扭动
To wiggle more or more effectively than someone or something else.
The baby outwiggled his older brother and escaped from the playpen.  婴儿扭动得比他哥哥更厉害，从游戏围栏里逃了出来。
The worm outwiggled the hook and swam away.  虫子扭动着逃脱了鱼钩，游走了。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, surpass, beat
Antonyms: lose to, be outdone by
词性: 动词
