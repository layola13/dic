disguisers:/dɪsˈɡaɪzərz/
n.
伪装者；掩饰者
People who disguise themselves or things that disguise something. Those who change their appearance or alter something to conceal its true nature.
The disguisers were so good, no one recognized them. 伪装者们非常出色，没有人认出他们。
The magician is a master of disguisers. 魔术师是伪装大师。
camouflagers, concealers
