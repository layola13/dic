din-dins:/ˈdɪn dɪnz/
noun
晚餐,晚饭 (Informal, childish term for dinner)
A childish or informal way to refer to dinner.
例句：
The din-dins is ready! 晚饭准备好了！

Time for din-dins, kiddo!  小朋友，晚饭时间到了！

近义词：dinner, supper, tea (regional)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but conceptually: breakfast, lunch, brunch)
词性:noun
