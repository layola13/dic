helicoid:/ˈhɛlɪkɔɪd/ | n. adj. | 螺旋面，螺旋形的 | A surface generated by a line that revolves around an axis while moving uniformly along the axis.  A shape resembling a helix. |  The architect designed a striking helicoid staircase.  这位建筑师设计了一个引人注目的螺旋楼梯。 | The DNA molecule has a helicoid structure. DNA分子具有螺旋结构。|  spiral, helical | planar, flat | noun, adjective
