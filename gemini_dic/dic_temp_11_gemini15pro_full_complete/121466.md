misjudgment:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒmənt/| n.|错误判断，判断失误|An act of judging wrongly or incorrectly.|Firing her was a serious misjudgment. 解雇她是一个严重的错误判断。|It was a misjudgment on my part to trust him.  相信他是我的判断失误。|近义词：miscalculation, error, mistake|反义词:accurate judgment, sound judgment|词性:noun

词源:
From mis- + judgment.  "Mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly".  "Judgment" comes from Old French "jugement".
