constructor:/kənˈstrʌktər/ | n. | 构造器，构造函数 | A constructor is a special method within a class that is automatically called whenever a new object of that class is created. It is used to initialize the object's data members. | The constructor initialized the object's name and ID.  构造函数初始化了对象的名称和ID。| synonym: initializer, builder | antonym: destructor | noun

constructor:/kənˈstrʌktər/ | n. | 建造者，施工者 | A person or company that builds or constructs something, typically buildings or large structures. | The constructor finished the bridge ahead of schedule.  建造者提前完成了桥梁的建造。| synonym: builder, developer, erector | antonym: demolisher, destroyer | noun
