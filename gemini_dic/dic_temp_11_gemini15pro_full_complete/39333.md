conjecturer:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərər/| n.|推测者，猜想者|A person who forms conjectures or makes guesses.|The conjecturer proposed a possible solution, but lacked evidence.|近义词：guesser, speculator, hypothesizer, theorizer|反义词：knower, expert|词性:noun

conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/| n. v.|推测，猜想|An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information.|He conjectured that the painting was a forgery. 他推测这幅画是赝品. |近义词：guess, speculation, hypothesis, theory|反义词：fact, knowledge, certainty|词性:noun, verb
