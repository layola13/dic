nonfrigidly:/ˌnɒnˈfrɪdʒɪdli/
| adv. |不冷淡地，热情地|Not in a frigid or emotionally cold manner; warmly, affectionately.|She greeted him nonfrigidly, with a warm hug and a kiss on the cheek. 她热情地迎接他，给他一个温暖的拥抱和脸颊上的吻。|He spoke nonfrigidly about his family, showing genuine affection.  他热情地谈论他的家人，流露出真挚的爱意。| 近义词:warmly, affectionately, cordially, passionately|反义词:frigidly, coldly, indifferently|词性:副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "frigid" (adjective meaning "extremely cold in temperature; lacking warmth or ardor") + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  “non-”（表示“不”的前缀）+ “frigid”（形容词，意为“温度极低；缺乏温暖或热情”）+ “-ly”（副词后缀）。
