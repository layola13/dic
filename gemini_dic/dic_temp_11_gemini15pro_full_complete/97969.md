inobedience:/ˌɪnəˈbiːdiəns/
n.|不服从，违抗|The child's disobedience angered his parents.  这个孩子的忤逆激怒了他的父母。|例句：His disobedience resulted in his expulsion from school. 他的不服从导致他被学校开除。|近义词：defiance, noncompliance, rebelliousness|反义词:obedience, compliance, submission|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French inobedience, from Latin inobedientia, from inobedient- ‘disobedient’.
