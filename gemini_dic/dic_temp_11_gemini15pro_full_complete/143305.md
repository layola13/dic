outdrink:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪŋk/
verb
喝赢，比…更能喝
To drink more alcohol than someone else.
He can outdrink anyone in the bar. 他能喝过酒吧里的任何人。
synonyms: outmatch, surpass, best
antonyms: be outdrunk by
