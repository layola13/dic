curtains:/ˈkɜːrtnzn/ (US) /ˈkɜːtnz/ (UK)| n.|窗帘，帷幕；终结，结束|A piece of cloth intended to cover a window, stage, etc.  It can also metaphorically refer to the end of something.|例句：She drew the curtains to block out the sunlight. 她拉上窗帘挡住阳光。

例句：It's curtains for their relationship. 他们的关系结束了。|近义词：drapes, blinds, shades, the end, finale|反义词:beginning, start|noun
