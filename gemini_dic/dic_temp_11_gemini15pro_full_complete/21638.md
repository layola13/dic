blurring:/ˈblɜːrɪŋ/ /ˈblɝːɪŋ/
n.| 模糊，模糊化|The blurring of the lines between work and personal life. 工作和个人生活界限的模糊。|例句：The photograph was ruined by the blurring caused by the camera shake.  照片被相机的抖动造成的模糊毁了。|近义词：obfuscation, smudging, dimming|反义词：sharpening, focusing, clarifying|词性:名词

blurring:/ˈblɜːrɪŋ/ /ˈblɝːɪŋ/
adj.| 模糊的|a blurring effect 模糊效果|例句：The fast-moving car was a blurring image.  快速行驶的汽车是一个模糊的影像。|近义词：indistinct, hazy, fuzzy|反义词：sharp, clear, distinct|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From blur (v.) + -ing (present participle suffix).  Blur itself comes from Middle English blurre, of uncertain origin.
