basera:/ˈbeɪsərə/ | n. |基地，总部|A place used as a center of operations.|The company established its basera in the city center.  这家公司把总部设在市中心。|近义词: base, headquarters, center, post |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性:名词

Etymology: From Hindi बसेरा (baserā), meaning "dwelling, lodging, settlement."  Ultimately from Sanskrit वास (vāsa) meaning "dwelling."
