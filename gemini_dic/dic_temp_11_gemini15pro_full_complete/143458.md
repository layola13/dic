outfroths:/aʊtˈfrɒθz/ | v. | 吐出泡沫; 比...泡沫更多 | To produce or emit froth in greater abundance than something else.  The beer outfroths from the tap.  啤酒从龙头里涌出泡沫。| Synonyms: foams over, effervesces | Antonyms: flattens, settles | verb

outfroths:/aʊtˈfrɒθz/ | v. |  胜过; 超越 (比喻) | To surpass or exceed someone or something, especially in exuberance or enthusiasm. |  His performance outfrothed all the others. 他的表演热情洋溢，胜过所有其他人。 | Synonyms: outdoes, surpasses, excels | Antonyms: underperforms, lags behind | verb
