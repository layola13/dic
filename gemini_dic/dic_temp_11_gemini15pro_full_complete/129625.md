non-contagious:/ˌnɒn.kənˈteɪ.dʒəs/ (US) /ˌnɑːn.kənˈteɪ.dʒəs/| adj.|非传染性的|Not capable of being transmitted by contact.  It describes something that cannot be spread from one person to another through direct or indirect physical contact.|The common cold is contagious, but arthritis is non-contagious.|Synonyms: noncommunicable, noninfectious|Antonyms: contagious, communicable, infectious|adjective
