charbroiler: /ˈtʃɑːrˌbroɪlər/ | n. | 炭火烤炉，烧烤炉 | A grill that uses charcoal or gas as a heat source to cook food. |  This steak was cooked on a charbroiler. 这块牛排是用炭火烤炉烤的。| The restaurant just bought a new charbroiler. 这家餐厅刚买了一个新的烧烤炉。 | synonyms: grill, broiler, barbecue | antonyms: oven, microwave | noun


charbroilers: /ˈtʃɑːrˌbroɪlɚz/ | n. | 炭火烤炉（复数）, 烧烤炉（复数） | Plural form of charbroiler, referring to multiple grills that use charcoal or gas as a heat source. | We cleaned all the charbroilers after the barbecue.  烧烤结束后，我们清洗了所有的烧烤炉。 |  They sell various sizes of charbroilers at the store.  这家商店出售各种尺寸的烧烤炉。 | synonyms: grills, broilers, barbecues | antonyms: ovens, microwaves | noun


Etymology: char + broiler. "Char" refers to the partially burned wood or charcoal, and "broiler" refers to a device for grilling food.
