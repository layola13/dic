dissentingly:/dɪˈsɛntɪŋli/
adverb
不同意地，持异议地
in a dissenting manner; expressing disagreement
He dissentingly shook his head at the proposal. 他对这个提议不同意地摇了摇头。
She dissentingly voted against the motion. 她投了反对票，表示不同意这项动议。

近义词: disagreeingly, opposingly, objectionably, controversially
反义词: agreeably, approvingly, concurringly, supportively
词性: adverb

词源:
From dissent + -ing + -ly.  Dissent comes from Old French dissentir, from Latin dissentire, from dis- "apart" + sentire "to feel."
