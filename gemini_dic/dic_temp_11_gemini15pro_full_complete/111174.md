lithogenesis:/ˌlɪθəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
岩石成因；岩石生成
The formation of rock, the processes that result in the formation of rocks.
The geologist studied the lithogenesis of the sedimentary rocks.  这位地质学家研究了沉积岩的成因。
lithification, petrogenesis, rock formation
