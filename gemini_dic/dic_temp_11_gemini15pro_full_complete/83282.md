gynaeceum:/ˌɡaɪnɪˈsiːəm/ /ˌdʒaɪnɪˈsiːəm/ | n. | 雌蕊群；妇女公寓 |The innermost part of a flower, consisting of the carpels; in ancient Greece, the women's apartments, or the part of a house reserved for women. | The botanist examined the gynaeceum of the orchid.  植物学家检查了兰花的雌蕊群。|  The women gathered in the gynaeceum to spin and weave. 妇女们聚集在妇女公寓里纺纱织布。 | synonyms: pistil, carpel | antonyms: androecium | noun
