nonbusiest:/ˌnɒnˈbɪziɪst/ | adj. | 最不繁忙的 | Not the busiest; less busy than all others being compared.  This word is grammatically correct, though "least busy" is more common and sounds more natural. | The library was nonbusiest on Tuesday mornings. 图书馆在星期二早上最不繁忙。| The north entrance was nonbusiest during rush hour. 在高峰时段，北入口是最不繁忙的。 |  least busy, quietest | busiest, most crowded | adjective
