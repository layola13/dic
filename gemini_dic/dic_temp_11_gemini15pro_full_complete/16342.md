barbarossa: /ˌbɑːrbəˈrɒsə/ (UK), /ˌbɑːrbəˈroʊsə/ (US) | n. | 巴巴罗萨 |  German code name for the Axis invasion of the Soviet Union in World War II, launched on June 22, 1941.  It translates to "Redbeard" in Italian. |  Operation Barbarossa was a turning point in World War II. 巴巴罗萨行动是二战的转折点。 | near synonym: invasion, attack | antonym: defense, retreat | noun
