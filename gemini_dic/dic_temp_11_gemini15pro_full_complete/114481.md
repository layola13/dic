malacissant:/ˌmæləˈsɑːsɒ̃/ | adj. |  使变软的，使变潮湿的 | Making soft or tender, especially by the application of moisture.  This term is primarily used in the context of cooking and baking, referring to the softening effect of liquids on dough or other food substances. | The malacissant effect of the milk made the dough easier to knead. 牛奶的软化作用使面团更容易揉捏。| The humid air had a malacissant effect on the bread, making it slightly soggy. 潮湿的空气使面包变软，有点湿漉漉的。 | softening, tenderizing, moistening | hardening, drying, firming | adjective
