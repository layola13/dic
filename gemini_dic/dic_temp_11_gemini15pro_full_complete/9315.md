antipoetical:/ˌæntipoʊˈetɪkəl/
adjective
反诗意的；非诗性的；缺乏诗意的
Not poetical; unpoetical; unimaginative; prosaic.  Lacking the qualities or characteristics of poetry; not conducive to or suggestive of poetry.
His antipoetical language made it difficult for readers to connect with the text emotionally. 他那缺乏诗意的语言使得读者难以与文本产生情感共鸣。
The accountant’s dry, antipoetical description of the budget cuts left the employees feeling demoralized.  会计师对预算削减的枯燥、缺乏诗意的描述让员工感到士气低落。

近义词:prosaic, mundane, unimaginative, unpoetic
反义词:poetical, poetic, imaginative, lyrical, romantic


词源:
From anti- + poetical.  The prefix "anti-" means "against" or "opposite of."
