numberer's:/ˈnʌmbərərz/
n.
编号员的，编号人的
Possession form of "numberer," referring to something belonging to or associated with a person who numbers things.
The numberer's pen ran out of ink. 编号员的钢笔没墨了。
近义词:counter's, enumerator's
反义词: N/A
词性:noun, possessive


词源:
From "number" (v.) + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns) + "-'s" (possessive suffix). "Number" ultimately derives from Old French "nombre" and Latin "numerus."
