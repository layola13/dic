non-summons:/nɒnˈsʌmənɪz/ |n.| 非传唤；未传唤 |Absence of a summons; failure to summon. | The case was dismissed due to non-summons of key witnesses.  由于关键证人未被传唤，案件被驳回。 | Example: The non-summons of expert witnesses weakened the prosecution's case. 未传唤专家证人削弱了控方的论点。| Synonym: non-citation,  lack of summons | Antonym: summons, citation | noun
