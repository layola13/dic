monanthous:/məˈnænθəs/
| adj. |单花的|Having only one flower.  A plant that produces only one flower.|The orchid is monanthous, producing a single, exquisite bloom each year.  这株兰花是单花的，每年只开一朵精致的花。|uniflorous, single-flowered|multiflorous, polyanthous|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *monos* (single) + *anthos* (flower)
