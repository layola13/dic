frugalness:/ˈfruːɡəlnəs/
n.
节俭，俭省
The quality of being frugal; thriftiness.
Her frugalness allowed her to save a significant portion of her income. 她的节俭使她能够节省很大一部分收入。

近义词:thrift, economy, parsimony
反义词:extravagance, lavishness, prodigality
词性:名词


词源:
From Middle English *frugalnesse, equivalent to frugal +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Latin frūgālis (“useful, profitable, economical, temperate”), from frūx (“fruit, produce, profit”).
