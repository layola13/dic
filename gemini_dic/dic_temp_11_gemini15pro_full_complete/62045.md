encamps:/ɪnˈkæmps/ | v. |扎营，宿营| to set up a camp; to pitch tents for a temporary stay | The army encamped near the river.  军队在河边扎营。|  The travelers encamped for the night in a sheltered valley.  旅行者们在一个避风的峡谷里扎营过夜。|近义词：camp, bivouac, pitch tents|反义词：decamp, break camp|词性: 动词

encampment:/ɪnˈkæmpmənt/ | n. |营地，宿营地|the act of encamping or the state of being encamped; a camp | The refugees set up an encampment on the edge of the city. 难民们在城市边缘建立了一个营地。| The summer encampment for the scouts was in the mountains. 童子军的夏季营地在山上。| 近义词：camp, campsite, bivouac|反义词：N/A|词性: 名词
