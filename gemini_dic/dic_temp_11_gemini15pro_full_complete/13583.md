attentions:/əˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. | 注意；关注；注意力；殷勤；关怀 |  The act of carefully thinking about, listening to, or watching someone or something. |  He showered her with attentions. 他对她关怀备至。 | 近义词: care, concern, consideration, regard, solicitude | 反义词: neglect, disregard | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *atencion, from Latin attentionem (nominative attentio) "a stretching out, attention," noun of action from past-participle stem of attendere "to stretch toward, give heed to," from ad- "to" + tendere "to stretch."
