non-Aryan:/ˌnɒn ˈɛəriən/ | adj. | 非雅利安人的 | Not belonging to the group of people who speak Indo-European languages, especially not of Nordic or Germanic origin. Historically used in a discriminatory and racist manner by the Nazi regime to classify and persecute people deemed "inferior." |  The Nazi regime persecuted those deemed non-Aryan. 纳粹政权迫害那些被认为是非雅利安人的人。 | Synonyms: non-Indo-European, Gentile (in Nazi usage) | Antonyms: Aryan, Indo-European (in Nazi usage) | adjective
