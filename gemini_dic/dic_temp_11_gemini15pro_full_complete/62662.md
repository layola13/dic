engage:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ (US); /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ (UK)| v. | 参与，从事；雇佣；吸引（注意力）；与…交战；订婚 | To involve someone in an activity; to hire someone; to attract someone's attention; to begin fighting with someone; to become engaged to be married. |例句：The speaker engaged the audience with his humor. 演讲者用他的幽默吸引了观众。 | 近义词：involve, employ, attract, occupy, fight, betroth | 反义词：disengage, detach, ignore, release, retreat | verb

engage:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ (US); /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. | 约定，契约 | A formal agreement to do something. |例句：They made an engage to meet for lunch. 他们约定一起吃午饭。|近义词：agreement, commitment, contract|反义词：disagreement, refusal| noun
