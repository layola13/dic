conceders:/kənˈsiːdərz/
n.|让步者，承认者|People who concede something (e.g., a point in an argument, a game, or an election).|The conceders in the debate acknowledged the strength of their opponents' arguments.|近义词：acknowledgers, admitters, yielders|反义词：deniers, disputants|词性:noun (plural)

concede:/kənˈsiːd/
v.|承认，让步，认输|To admit that something is true or valid, often reluctantly; to surrender or yield.|He conceded the election after the final vote count. 他在最终计票后承认败选。|近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, yield|反义词：deny, dispute, contest|词性:verb
