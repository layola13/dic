multimammate:/ˌmʌltiˈmæmeɪt/ | adj. n. | 多乳头的，多乳头的动物 | Having multiple mammary glands (more than the usual number for a given species). Also used as a noun to refer to an animal with multiple mammary glands. | The multimammate mouse is a common research subject. 多乳头小鼠是一种常见的实验对象。| The multimammate rat is known for its high reproductive rate. 多乳头大鼠以其高繁殖率而闻名。 | synonyms: polymastic | antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "typical mammary count" but this is not a single word) | adjective, noun

Etymology:
From multi- (many) + Latin mamma (breast) + -ate (suffix forming adjectives).
