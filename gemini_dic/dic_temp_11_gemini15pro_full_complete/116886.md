maybloom:/ˈmeɪbluːm/| n.|五月花；春花；青春|A flower that blooms in May; a symbol of spring and youth. |The maybloom is a sign that summer is coming. 五月花是夏天即将到来的标志。| The meadows were dotted with mayblooms. 草地上点缀着五月花。 |近义词：spring flower, blossom|反义词:winter flower|词性:名词

词源:
May + bloom.  "May" refers to the month, and "bloom" refers to the act of flowering.  The word is a descriptive compound noun.
