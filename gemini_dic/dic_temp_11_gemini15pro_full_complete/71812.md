fleecily:/ˈfliːsɪli/| adv.|柔软地，羊毛般地|In a manner resembling fleece; softly, woollily.  It describes something that feels or looks like fleece, emphasizing a soft and woolly texture.|The snow fell fleecily, covering the landscape in a soft white blanket. 雪柔柔地飘落，大地盖上了一层柔软的白毯。|近义词：softly, gently, woollily|反义词:roughly, harshly|词性:副词

词源: From fleece + -ily.  "Fleece" comes from Old English *flēos, related to Old High German flōz "fleece."
