hogwash:/ˈhɒɡwɒʃ/ (UK), /ˈhɑːɡwɑːʃ/ (US)| n. |胡说八道；废话；无稽之谈|Nonsense; worthless or insincere talk.  Originally, hogwash referred to kitchen swill fed to pigs. This literal meaning evolved metaphorically to represent worthless ideas or words.|That's complete hogwash! He's just trying to scare you.|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, balderdash, bunk, claptrap, drivel, twaddle|反义词：truth, fact, reality|词性:名词
