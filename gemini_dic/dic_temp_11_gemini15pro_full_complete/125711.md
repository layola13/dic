municipalism:/mjuːˌnɪsɪˈpælɪzəm/
n.
市政主义;地方自治主义
A system of political organization based on municipality autonomy and citizen participation.
The candidate's platform focused heavily on municipalism, promising greater local control over resources and decision-making.
候选人的政纲重点关注市政主义，承诺加强地方对资源和决策的控制。

Barcelona's vibrant neighborhoods are a testament to the city's strong tradition of municipalism.
巴塞罗那充满活力的街区证明了这座城市强大的市政主义传统。

近义词:localism, decentralization, home rule
反义词:centralism, statism
