aggregatory:/ˈæɡrɪɡətɔːri/ | adj. n.|聚集的，集合的；总数，合计|Formed by aggregation; collective.  A total or aggregate.|例句：The aggregatory effect of small changes can be significant. 小变化的累积效应可能很重要。

例句：The museum displayed an aggregatory of ancient artifacts. 博物馆展出了一批古代文物。|近义词：cumulative, collective, combined, total, aggregate|反义词：individual, separate, discrete|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *aggregatorius, from Latin aggregatus (past participle of aggregare, "to add to a flock"), from ad- ("to") + gregare ("to herd"), from grex ("flock").
