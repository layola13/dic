occasional:/əˈkeɪʒənəl/ | adj. | 偶然的，临时的 | Happening or done sometimes but not often or regularly. | She makes occasional visits to her family. 她偶尔去看望她的家人。|  We make occasional trips to the beach. 我们偶尔去海滩旅行。| 近义词: infrequent, sporadic, irregular | 反义词: frequent, regular, constant | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin occasionalis, from occasio ‘occasion’.
