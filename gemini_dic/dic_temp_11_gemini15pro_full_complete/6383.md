amphidromical:/ˌæmfɪˈdrɒmɪk(ə)l/
adjective
双潮的，无潮的
Relating to a point in a tidal system where the tide does not rise or fall.  The tidal range increases with distance from this point.
例句：The amphidromic point in the North Sea is located near the coast of Norway. 北海的无潮点位于挪威海岸附近。

例句：Amphidromic systems are characterized by rotating tidal currents. 双潮系统以旋转的潮流为特征。

近义词: tideless
反义词: tidal
