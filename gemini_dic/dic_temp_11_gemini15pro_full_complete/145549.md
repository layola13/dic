overcommunicates:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪts/
verb
过度沟通，沟通过多
communicates excessively, often to the point of redundancy or being overwhelming.
She overcommunicates every decision, sending countless emails and holding multiple meetings. 她对每个决定都过度沟通，发送无数电子邮件并举行多次会议。
He tends to overcommunicate when he's nervous, repeating the same information over and over. 当他紧张的时候，他倾向于过度沟通，一遍又一遍地重复相同的信息。
近义词: overshares, overexplains
反义词: undercommunicates, withholds information
词性: 动词
