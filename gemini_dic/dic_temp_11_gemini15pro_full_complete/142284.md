orseille:/ɔːrˈseɪ/ /ɔːrˈseɪj/
n.
紫红色染料；法国紫色；地衣红
A purplish-red dye obtained from various lichens.
The dye gives a purplish red colour. 这种染料会产生紫红色。
Orseille was a popular dye in the Middle Ages.  地衣红在中世纪是一种很流行的染料。
