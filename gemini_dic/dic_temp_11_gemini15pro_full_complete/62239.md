encrusted:/ɪnˈkrʌstɪd/
adjective
镶嵌的，覆盖着的
Covered with a hard crust or layer of something.
The old ship's hull was encrusted with barnacles.  旧船的船体上覆盖着藤壶。
The jeweler showed me a beautiful encrusted dagger.  珠宝商给我看了一把镶嵌着宝石的匕首。
近义词: coated, covered, layered
反义词: bare, clean, unadorned
词性: adjective
词源: From en- (meaning "in" or "on") + crust + -ed (past participle suffix). Ultimately from Latin crusta ("crust, shell").
