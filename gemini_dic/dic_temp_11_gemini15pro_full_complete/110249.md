lifesaving:/ˈlaɪfˌseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
救命的；救生的
Designed to save or protect people's lives.
例句：The doctor's quick thinking proved to be lifesaving.  医生的快速反应被证明是救命的。

例句：The coastguard performed a lifesaving rescue in the stormy seas.  海岸警卫队在暴风雨的大海中进行了一次救生救援。

近义词：life-preserving, essential, crucial
反义词：life-threatening, fatal, deadly
词性：adjective


lifesaving:/ˈlaɪfˌseɪvɪŋ/
noun
救生；救命
The action or skill of saving lives, especially from drowning.
例句：He learned lifesaving techniques as a boy scout.  他小时候在童子军里学过救生技巧。

例句：The organization provides lifesaving training and equipment to communities.  该组织为社区提供救生培训和设备。

近义词：rescue, first aid, emergency aid
反义词：endangerment, hazard, peril
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"life" (Old English līf) + "saving" (present participle of "save" from Old French sauver, ultimately from Latin salvare "to save")
