olefinic:/ˌoʊləˈfɪnɪk/ | adj. |烯烃的，含烯烃的|Relating to or containing an olefin (another term for alkene), a hydrocarbon with a carbon-carbon double bond.| Ethylene is a simple olefinic compound. 乙烯是一种简单的烯烃化合物。 |  Olefinic bonds are reactive. 烯烃键具有反应活性。|Synonyms: alkenyl, unsaturated | Antonyms: saturated, paraffinic | adjective
