be-of-service:/biː əv ˈsɜːrvɪs/
adjective phrase
乐于助人; 为…效劳
Being useful or helpful to someone.

例句：
He is always eager to be of service to others.
他总是热心帮助别人。

The librarian was happy to be of service in finding the book I needed.
图书管理员很乐意帮助我找到我需要的书。

近义词：helpful, accommodating, obliging, useful
反义词：unhelpful, inconsiderate

词性: adjective phrase
