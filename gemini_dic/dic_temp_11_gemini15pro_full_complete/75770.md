fulsome:/ˈfʊlsəm/ | adj. | 过分恭维的；令人生厌的；过多的；肥沃的 | excessively complimentary or flattering, to the point of being insincere or offensive; disgustingly excessive; copious; (archaic) fertile |  He gave a fulsome speech praising the dictator. 他发表了一通令人作呕的演讲，赞扬了独裁者。 | synonyms: excessive, extravagant, overdone, immoderate, inordinate, copious, profuse | antonyms: restrained, moderate, understated, meager, scanty | adjective

fulsome:/ˈfʊlsəm/ | adj. | 过多的；大量的 | of large size or quantity; generous or abundant | The harvest was fulsome this year. 今年的收成很丰盛。 | synonyms: copious, abundant, plentiful, ample, profuse | antonyms: meager, scanty, sparse, scarce | adjective
