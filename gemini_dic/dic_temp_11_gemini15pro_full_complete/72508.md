fluorous:/ˈflʊərəs/| adj.|含氟的，氟化的|Relating to or containing fluorine, especially in a higher oxidation state than in fluoride.  Characterized by the presence of fluorine atoms bonded to carbon. |例句：Fluorous solvents are used in some chemical processes. 含氟溶剂用于某些化学过程中。

例句：Fluorous tags can be used in separation techniques. 含氟标签可用于分离技术。|近义词：fluorinated|反义词: non-fluorous|词性: adjective
