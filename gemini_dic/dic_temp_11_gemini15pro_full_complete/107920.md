larviparous: /lɑːrˈvɪpərəs/ | adj. | 幼体产的；胎生的 | Producing larvae instead of eggs.  Animals that are larviparous give birth to live larvae which have developed inside the parent's body. |  Many species of flies are larviparous. 许多种类的苍蝇都是幼体产的。

The tsetse fly is larviparous, giving birth to a single fully developed larva. 采采蝇是幼体产的，一次产下一只完全发育的幼虫。 | 近义词: viviparous (胎生的) | 反义词: oviparous (卵生的) | 词性: adjective
