nonspiritedness:/ˌnɒnˈspɪrɪtɪdnəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈspɪrɪtɪdnəs/| n. |缺乏活力；没精神；沮丧|Lack of spirit, animation, or enthusiasm.  A state of being depressed or discouraged.|His nonspiritedness was apparent in his slumped posture and quiet voice. 他的无精打采从他低垂的姿势和低沉的声音中显露出来。|Near synonyms: listlessness, apathy, lethargy, languor, dejection, depression|Antonyms:  enthusiasm, energy, vigor, liveliness, spirit, animation|Noun
