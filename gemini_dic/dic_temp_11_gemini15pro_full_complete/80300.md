golan:/ˈɡoʊlɑːn/
n.
戈兰（地名）
A rocky plateau in southwestern Syria.
The Golan Heights are a disputed territory. 戈兰高地是一个有争议的领土。

近义词:heights (高地)
反义词:valley (山谷)
词性:noun (名词)
