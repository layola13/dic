frivolously: /ˈfrɪvələsli/ | adv. | 轻浮地，轻率地 | In a frivolous manner; not serious or sensible. | She frivolously spent her entire paycheck on designer shoes. 她轻浮地把整张工资都花在了名牌鞋上。|  He dismissed the issue frivolously, not realizing its importance. 他轻率地驳回了这个问题，没有意识到它的重要性。 | 近义词: carelessly, thoughtlessly, giddily, superficially | 反义词: seriously, thoughtfully, prudently, soberly | 词性: 副词

frivolous: /ˈfrɪvələs/ | adj. | 轻浮的，轻率的；琐碎的 | Not having any serious purpose or value. |  She made a frivolous complaint about the noise. 她对噪音提出了一个琐碎的抱怨。 |  It was a frivolous lawsuit that wasted the court's time.  这是一场浪费法庭时间的琐碎诉讼。 | 近义词: trivial, petty, insignificant, silly | 反义词: serious, important, significant, weighty | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin *frivolus "friable, crumbling, slight, worthless," from friare "to rub, break, crumble"
