inhumation:/ˌɪnhjuːˈmeɪʃn/ | n. | 土葬，埋葬 | The act of burying a dead body in the ground. |  His family chose inhumation over cremation. 他的家人选择了土葬而不是火葬。

The archaeologist discovered evidence of ancient inhumation rituals. 考古学家发现了古代土葬仪式的证据。

| 近义词: burial, interment | 反义词: cremation, exhumation | 词性: noun
