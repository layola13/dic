interpervading:/ˌɪntərpərˈveɪdɪŋ/
adjective
相互渗透的，弥漫的
mutually permeating or pervading throughout.
The interpervading scent of woodsmoke filled the air.  空气中弥漫着木柴燃烧的气味。
Synonyms: permeating, pervading, suffusing, encompassing
Antonyms: separate, distinct, isolated
词性: adjective
