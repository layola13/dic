cunette:/kjuːˈnet/ /kjuːˈnɛt/ | n. |排水沟，边沟，路缘石下的排水沟|A ditch, especially one at the side of a road, for drainage.  A small channel or ditch running along the side of a road or other paved area to carry away rainwater.|The cunette was filled with leaves and debris.  边沟里满是树叶和碎屑。|Synonyms: ditch, gutter, channel, trench|Antonyms: ridge, mound|noun
