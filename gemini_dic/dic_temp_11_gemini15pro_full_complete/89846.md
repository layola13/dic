hornowl:/ˈhɔːrnˌaʊl/ | n. | 角鸮，大雕鸮 | A large owl of the genus *Bubo*, characterized by ear tufts.  These owls are known for their deep hooting calls and powerful hunting abilities. |  The hornowl swooped down and caught a mouse. 角鸮俯冲下来，抓住了一只老鼠。|  The haunting call of the hornowl echoed through the forest. 大雕鸮令人难忘的叫声在森林中回荡。 | synonyms: eagle owl, great horned owl | antonyms: N/A | noun
