buffaloing:/ˈbʌfəloʊɪŋ/
verb
恐吓，欺骗
The salesman tried buffaloing me into buying a car I didn't need.
这个销售员试图恐吓我买一辆我不需要的车。
词源: From "buffalo," referring to the powerful and intimidating nature of the animal. 最初来源于“buffalo（水牛）”，指的是这种动物强大和令人生畏的本性。
例句：
He tried buffaloing his way through the crowd. 他试图在人群中横冲直撞。
She was buffaloed into signing the contract. 她被强迫签署了合同。
近义词: intimidating, bullying, coercing, pressuring
反义词: persuading, coaxing, reasoning
词性: v.
