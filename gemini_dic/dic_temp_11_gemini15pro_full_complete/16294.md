baptizing:/ˈbæptaɪzɪŋ/
verb, present participle
施洗，为…施洗礼
The act of performing the Christian rite of baptism.  The priest was baptizing the baby.  牧师正在为婴儿施洗。
例句：
He was baptizing people in the river. 他在河里为人施洗。
They are baptizing their child next Sunday. 他们下周日要为孩子施洗。
近义词：christening, immersing
反义词：None
词性：Verb (present participle)


baptize:/ˈbæptaɪz/
verb
施洗，命名
To perform the Christian rite of baptism.  The child was baptized John.  孩子被命名为约翰。
例句：
They baptized their daughter Sarah. 他们给女儿施洗，取名莎拉。
John the Baptist baptized Jesus in the River Jordan.  施洗约翰在约旦河为耶稣施洗。
近义词：christen, immerse
反义词：None
词性：Verb

词源：From Old French baptiser, from Late Latin baptizare, from Greek baptizein "to immerse, dip, wash," from baptos "dipped, dyed," verbal adjective of baptein "to dip, dye, wash."
