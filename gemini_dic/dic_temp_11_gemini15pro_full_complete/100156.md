interran:/ˌɪntəˈræn/ | adj. | 内陆的，相互斗争的 | Existing or occurring between different parts of a country or continent; engaged in conflict or strife with each other. | The interran conflicts weakened the empire. 内陆冲突削弱了帝国。|  The interran trade routes were vital to the economy. 内陆的贸易路线对经济至关重要。 | 近义词: inland, interior, domestic, internecine | 反义词: coastal, external, foreign | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *interrānus, from inter- ‘between’ + terra ‘land, earth’.
