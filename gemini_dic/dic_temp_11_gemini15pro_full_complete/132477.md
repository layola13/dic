nonderogatorily:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɒɡətərɪli/ | adv. | 非贬义地 |  In a way that does not disparage or belittle.  Expressing or characterized by absence of derogation. | He mentioned his former employer nonderogatorily, focusing on the positive aspects of his time there. 他非贬义地提到了他的前任雇主，重点讲述了他在那里工作期间的积极方面。| Synonyms: respectfully, neutrally, objectively | Antonyms: disparagingly, contemptuously, pejoratively | word type: adverb

Etymology:
From non- + derogatory + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Derogatory" comes from the Late Latin "derogatorius", meaning "detracting", from the Latin "derogare",  "to repeal (part of a law), detract from". "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
