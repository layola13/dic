degrass:/diːˈɡræs/| v. |除油，脱脂|To remove grease or fat from something.|例句：We need to degrass the engine before we can repair it. 我们需要在修理发动机之前先除油。

例句：The chef degrassed the stock for a clearer soup. 厨师将高汤脱脂，以便汤更清澈。|近义词：degrease|反义词: grease, oil|词性:动词

词源：de- (prefix meaning "remove") + grass (referring to fat, grease, especially from cooking)
