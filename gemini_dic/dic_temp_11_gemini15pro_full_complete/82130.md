grok:/ɡrɑːk/ | v. | 理解；深刻理解；彻底领悟；心领神会 | To understand something deeply and intuitively. | Example:  She really groks the importance of community. 她真正理解了社群的重要性。

Example: After years of study, he finally grokked the complexities of quantum physics.  经过多年的学习，他终于彻底理解了量子物理学的复杂性。

| 近义词: understand, comprehend, grasp, fathom, perceive, intuit | 反义词: misunderstand, misinterpret |词性: 动词
