heteroclitous:/ˌhɛtərɒklɪtəs/
adj.
混杂的，不同种类的
Composed of different kinds or dissimilar elements.
例句：The collection of furniture was a heteroclite assortment of styles and periods. 这套家具是各种风格和年代的混杂组合。

例句：The heteroclite group of travelers included students, businesspeople, and retirees. 这群形形色色的旅行者包括学生、商人以及退休人员。

近义词：miscellaneous, diverse, varied, assorted
反义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar

词性: adjective

词源: From Ancient Greek ἑτερόκλιτος (heteróklitos, “irregularly inflected”), from ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) + κλίνω (klínō, “I bend, incline”).
