drizzliest:/ˈdrɪzliɪst/| adj.|毛毛雨最大的，下毛毛雨最多的|Characterized by the most frequent or heaviest drizzle.  The drizzliest day I've ever experienced.|It was the drizzliest afternoon of the entire trip.  整个旅程中最毛毛雨的下午。|近义词：wettest, dampest, rainiest|反义词：driest, sunniest|词性:形容词
词源: From drizzle + -y + -est.  Drizzle comes from the Old English word *drisle, frequentative of *drison "to fall in fine drops."
