diplopoda:/dɪˈplɒpədə/| n. |倍足纲动物; 千足虫; 马陆;|A class of arthropods characterized by having two pairs of legs on most body segments, commonly known as millipedes.|Millipedes, members of the class Diplopoda, play an important role in breaking down leaf litter in forest ecosystems. 千足虫，也就是倍足纲动物，在森林生态系统中分解落叶方面起着重要作用。

Diplopoda are often confused with centipedes, but they are distinct groups of arthropods. 倍足纲动物常与蜈蚣混淆，但它们是不同的节肢动物类群。|近义词：millipede|反义词: None|词性:noun
