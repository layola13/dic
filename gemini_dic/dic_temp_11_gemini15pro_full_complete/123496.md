monothalmic:/ˌmɒnəˈθælmɪk/
adj.
单眼的
Having or relating to only one thalamus (a structure in the brain).  The thalamus relays sensory information and regulates sleep, alertness, and wakefulness.
Monothalmic animals rely primarily on other senses for spatial awareness.  单眼动物主要依靠其他感官来感知空间。
The study focused on the effects of monothalmic lesions on sleep patterns. 这项研究的重点是单侧丘脑损伤对睡眠模式的影响。
近义词: one-eyed (referring to vision), unilateral (referring to one side)
反义词: binocular, bilateral
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mono-" (Greek: μόνος, mónos, "alone", "single") + "thalamic" (referring to the thalamus).
