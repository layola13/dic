devotional:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəl/| adj. n.|虔诚的，祈祷的；灵修书籍|Characterized by or expressing devotion; a short religious service, especially one held in the home.|She reads a devotional every morning before starting her day. 她每天早上开始新的一天之前都会阅读灵修书籍。|近义词：religious, pious, prayerful, spiritual|反义词：irreligious, impious, secular|词性:adjective, noun
devotional:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəl/| n.|灵修；祈祷式|A short act of private worship; a form of prayer or worship.|The family gathered for their evening devotional. 全家聚集在一起进行晚间祈祷。|近义词：prayer, worship, meditation|反义词：blasphemy, sacrilege|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *devotionalis, from Latin devotio (“dedication, consecration”), from devovere (“devote, dedicate”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *de- (“down, away”) + *wowe- (“to vow”).
