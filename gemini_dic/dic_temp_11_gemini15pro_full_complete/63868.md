epilepsy: /ˈɛpɪˌlɛpsi/ | n. | 癫痫（单数）| A neurological disorder marked by sudden recurrent episodes of sensory disturbance, loss of consciousness, or convulsions, associated with abnormal electrical activity in the brain. | Epilepsy can be controlled with medication. 癫痫可以通过药物控制。| synonyms: seizure disorder, convulsion | antonyms: none | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin epilepsia, from Greek epilepsia "a seizure," from epileptein "to seize upon, attack," from epi "upon" + lambanein "to take."
