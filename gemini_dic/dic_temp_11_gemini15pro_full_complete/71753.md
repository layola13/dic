flea:/fliː/
n.
跳蚤
A small, wingless, bloodsucking insect that lives on animals and humans.
The dog was scratching constantly because it had fleas.  这只狗不停地抓挠，因为它身上有跳蚤。

近义词:parasite
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flēah, from Proto-Germanic *flauhaz, from Proto-Indo-European *pléws (“flea”). Cognate with Old Norse fló, Dutch vloo, German Floh.
