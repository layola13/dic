orohippus:/ˌɔːroʊˈhɪpəs/ | n. |始祖马|An extinct genus of early horse, considered a transitional form between *Eohippus* and *Epihippus*. It lived in the Eocene epoch. |Orohippus was slightly larger than Eohippus and had four toes on its front feet and three on its back feet. 始祖马比始马略大，前脚有四个脚趾，后脚有三个脚趾。|Orohippus fossils have been found in North America. 在北美发现了始祖马的化石。|近义词:Eohippus, Epihippus (as related genera)|反义词:Modern horse (in terms of evolutionary stage)|词性:名词
