bossiness:/ˈbɒsɪnəs/ /ˈbɑːsinəs/| n. |专横,跋扈,爱指挥人|The quality of being bossy; overbearing or domineering behavior.|Her bossiness made it difficult for others to contribute to the project. 她的专横跋扈使得其他人难以对项目做出贡献。|Example: He couldn't stand his boss's bossiness any longer. 他再也无法忍受老板的专横了。|Synonyms: domineeringness, imperiousness, authoritarianism|Antonyms: submissiveness, meekness, humility|Noun
