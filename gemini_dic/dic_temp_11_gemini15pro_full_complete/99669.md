interlinear:/ˌɪntərˈlɪniər/| adj.|行间的，逐行对照的|Placed or printed between the lines of a text, typically a translation of the text.|Example: The book includes an interlinear translation of the ancient poem. 这本书包含了这首古诗的逐行对照翻译。|Example: The student used an interlinear Bible to help understand the original Hebrew.  这个学生使用了一本逐行对照的圣经来帮助理解原始希伯来语。|Synonyms: line-by-line, parallel, juxtaposed|Antonyms: non-interlinear, separate
