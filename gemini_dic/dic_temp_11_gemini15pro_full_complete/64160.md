equally:/ˈiːkwəli/| adv.|平等地，同样地|in the same degree or manner; to the same extent.|The two teams were equally matched. 这两支队伍势均力敌。|近义词: evenly, fairly, uniformly|反义词: unevenly, unfairly, disproportionately|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *equaly*, from Old French *egalment* (Modern French *également*), from Late Latin *aequalmente*, from Latin *aequalis* ("equal") + *-mente* (adverbial suffix).
