overlegislate:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛdʒɪsleɪt/
verb
过度立法
To create an excessive number of laws, often to the point of inefficiency or counterproductivity.
The government has been criticized for overlegislating in recent years. 政府近年来因过度立法而受到批评。
Congress tends to overlegislate when public outcry is high. 当公众强烈抗议时，国会往往会过度立法。
overregulate, overgovern
