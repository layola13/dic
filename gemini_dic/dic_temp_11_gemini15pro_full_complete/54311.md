dishabituate:/dɪshəˈbɪtʃueɪt/
verb
使戒除习惯；使不再习惯
To cause (someone) to lose a habit or the effects of habituation.  To free from a habit.
The therapist helped the patient dishabituate to nicotine.  治疗师帮助病人戒除尼古丁。
synonyms: break a habit, wean, decondition
antonyms: habituate, accustom
词性: 动词
