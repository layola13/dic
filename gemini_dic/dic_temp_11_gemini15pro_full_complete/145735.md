overcriticize:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/ |v. |过度批评，吹毛求疵|To criticize someone or something too much or too harshly.|He tends to overcriticize his employees, which demotivates them. 他总是过度批评他的员工，这让他们很沮丧。|She overcriticized the painting, focusing on minor flaws. 她对这幅画吹毛求疵，专注于一些细小的瑕疵。|Synonyms: nitpick, carp, cavil, faultfind |Antonyms: praise, commend, laud|verb
