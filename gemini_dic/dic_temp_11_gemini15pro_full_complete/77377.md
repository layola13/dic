gauntest:/ˈɡɔːntɪst/ | adj. | 最憔悴的，最瘦骨嶙峋的 |  Superlative form of "gaunt," meaning extremely thin and bony, especially because of suffering, hunger, or age.  It describes a state of extreme thinness, often implying an unhealthy or undesirable appearance. |  He looked gauntest after his illness, his cheekbones protruding sharply. 他生病后看起来最憔悴，颧骨突出得很厉害。| synonyms: thinnest, boniest, leanest, skinniest, emaciated, haggard | antonyms: plumpest, heaviest, fleshiest, stoutest | adjective
