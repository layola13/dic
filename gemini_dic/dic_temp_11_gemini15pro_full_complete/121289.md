misfields:/ˈmɪsˌfiːldz/
|v. (third-person singular simple present misfields, present participle misfielding, simple past and past participle misfielded)|失误接球，漏接|To fail to field (a ball) cleanly in a sport such as baseball or cricket.|The outfielder misfielded the fly ball, allowing the winning run to score. 外野手漏接了高飞球，让对手得分赢得了比赛。|Example 2:  He misfielded the grounder and the runner reached first base. 他漏接了地滚球，跑垒员到达了一垒。|近义词：fumble, muff, bobble|反义词:catch, field cleanly|词性:动词
