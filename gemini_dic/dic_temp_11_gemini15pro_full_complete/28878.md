carpologist:/kɑːrˈpɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 果实学家；果树学家 | A scientist who studies fruits and fruit growing. | Example: The carpologist studied the different varieties of apples grown in the orchard. 果树学家研究了果园里种植的各种苹果。

Example:  The carpologist is an expert in the cultivation of mangoes. 这位果实学家是芒果种植方面的专家。| Synonym: pomologist, fruit scientist | Antonym: N/A | Noun
