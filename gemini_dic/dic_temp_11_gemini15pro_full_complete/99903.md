internationalism:/ɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/
n.|国际主义|The principle of cooperation among nations, for the promotion of their common good, sometimes as a political movement advocating a world federation.|例句：Internationalism promotes peace and understanding between different cultures. 国际主义促进了不同文化之间的和平与理解。

例句：The organization's internationalism is reflected in its diverse membership.  该组织的国际主义体现在其多元化的成员构成中。

|近义词：globalism, cosmopolitanism, multilateralism|反义词：nationalism, isolationism|词性: noun
