biohazardous:/ˌbaɪoʊˈhæzərdəs/
adjective
生物危害的
Containing or posing a risk of containing a biological agent or organism, especially one that constitutes a threat to human health or the environment.
The biohazardous waste must be disposed of properly.  生物危害废物必须妥善处理。
The lab is equipped to handle biohazardous materials.  该实验室配备了处理生物危害材料的设备。
近义词:dangerous, harmful, toxic, contaminated
反义词:safe, harmless, non-toxic
词性:adjective

词源: bio- (life) + hazardous (dangerous)  来自希腊语 bios (life) 和 hazard (danger).
