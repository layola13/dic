graphicnesses:/ˈɡræfɪknɪsɪz/
|n.|
|图解性；形象性；生动性|
|The quality of being graphic or vivid in representation or description.  It refers to the degree to which something is depicted or described in a visually striking or detailed manner, often with a focus on realism or explicitness.|
|The graphicnesses of the war photos shocked the public. 战争照片的逼真程度震惊了公众。|
|vividness, explicitness, detail, realism|
|vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity|
|noun|
