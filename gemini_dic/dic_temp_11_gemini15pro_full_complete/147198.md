overmagnify: /ˌoʊvərˈmæɡnɪfaɪ/
verb
过度放大；过分夸大
to magnify something excessively; to exaggerate the importance or significance of something.
The media tends to overmagnify the risks of air travel. 媒体往往过分夸大航空旅行的风险。
He overmagnified the importance of his role in the project. 他过分夸大了他在项目中的作用。
近义词: exaggerate, overstate, amplify, inflate
反义词: understate, minimize, downplay
词性: verb

Etymology:
From over- + magnify.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Magnify" comes from the Latin "magnificare," meaning "to make large" or "to extol."
