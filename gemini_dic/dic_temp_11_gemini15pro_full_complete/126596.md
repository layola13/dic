naggy:/ˈnæɡi/| adj.|唠叨的，不停抱怨的|Complaining or criticizing frequently, especially about trivial matters.|She has a naggy cough that won't go away. 她一直咳嗽不停，好不了。|近义词：carping, complaining, fault-finding, harassing|反义词：tolerant, patient|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "nag," which likely originated from Old Norse "nagga" (to gnaw, bite).  The sense of persistent complaining developed later.
