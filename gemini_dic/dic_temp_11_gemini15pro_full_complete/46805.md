danker:/ˈdæŋkər/| adj.|更潮的，更酷的，高质量的（尤指大麻）|Excellent, high quality, especially of marijuana; cool, awesome.|例句：That new strain is danker than the last one. 那个新品种比上一个更棒。|例句：This party is way danker than I expected.  这个派对比我想象的要酷得多。|近义词：awesome, cool, excellent, top-notch, primo (especially for marijuana)|反义词：lame, weak, poor quality|词性:形容词

词源:
Dank originally meant “unpleasantly moist or humid.”  Over time, it began to be used to describe high-quality marijuana, likely due to its sticky, moist texture. From there, it broadened to become a general term of approval, especially among younger generations.
