obolus: /ˈɒbələs/ (US) /ˈɒbʊləs/ (UK)| n. |古代希腊钱币；小额捐款；微不足道的数量|A small ancient Greek coin, originally made of bronze or copper; a small sum of money; a very small amount. |例句：He donated a mere obolus to the charity. 他只给慈善机构捐了一点钱。

例句：The obolus was a common unit of currency in ancient Greece. 奥波勒斯是古希腊常见的货币单位。

近义词：mite, pittance, trifle|反义词: fortune, wealth|词性: noun
