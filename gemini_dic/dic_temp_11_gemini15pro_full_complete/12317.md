ascus:/ˈæskəs/ (pl. asci /ˈæsaɪ/) | n. | 子囊 | A saclike structure characteristic of Ascomycetes (sac fungi), in which sexually produced spores (ascospores) are formed. | The asci of the morel are cylindrical and contain eight ascospores.  羊肚菌的子囊呈圆柱形，包含八个子囊孢子。| synonym: sporangium | antonym: N/A | noun
