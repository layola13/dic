bromide:/ˈbroʊmaɪd/ | n. |溴化物；镇静剂；陈词滥调|A chemical compound of bromine with another element.  A commonplace or hackneyed statement; a platitude.|例句：Potassium bromide is used as a sedative. 溴化钾用作镇静剂。

|例句：His speech was full of bromides about the importance of hard work. 他的演讲充满了关于努力工作重要性的陈词滥调。|近义词：platitude, cliché, banality|反义词：originality, novelty|词性:noun

bromides:/ˈbroʊmaɪdz/ | n. |溴化物；镇静剂；陈词滥调（复数）|Plural form of bromide.  Refers to multiple chemical compounds of bromine, or multiple commonplace statements.|例句：These bromides are commonly used in photography. 这些溴化物常用于摄影。
