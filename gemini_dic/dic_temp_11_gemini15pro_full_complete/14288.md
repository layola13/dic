自身免疫性疾病
Conditions or diseases caused by autoimmunity, where the body's immune system attacks its own healthy tissues.
Autoimmunities such as rheumatoid arthritis and lupus can cause significant pain and disability.  自身免疫性疾病，如类风湿性关节炎和狼疮，会导致严重的疼痛和残疾。
Autoimmunities are often treated with immunosuppressants.  自身免疫性疾病通常用免疫抑制剂治疗。
