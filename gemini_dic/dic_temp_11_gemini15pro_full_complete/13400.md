atomises:/ˈætəmaɪzɪz/
verb
使…雾化；使…原子化
reduces (something) to fine particles or spray.
The perfume atomises as it is sprayed. 香水喷洒时雾化了。
synonyms: vaporizes, sprays, mists
antonyms: condenses, solidifies
词性: 动词


atomises:/ˈætəmaɪzɪz/
verb
彻底摧毁；消灭
completely destroys (something).
The bomb atomised the city. 炸弹彻底摧毁了这座城市。
synonyms: obliterates, annihilates, destroys
antonyms: creates, builds, constructs
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *atomiser*, from *atome* (atom) + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs from nouns).  Ultimately from Greek *atomos* (indivisible).
