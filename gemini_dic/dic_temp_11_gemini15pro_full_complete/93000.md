idiopathic:/ˌɪdiəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|特发性的，原因不明的|Relating to or denoting any disease or condition that arises spontaneously or for which the cause is unknown.|例句：The doctor diagnosed the patient with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. 医生诊断该病人患有特发性肺纤维化。|例句：Idiopathic hypersomnia is a neurological disorder characterized by excessive daytime sleepiness. 特发性嗜睡症是一种以白天过度嗜睡为特征的神经系统疾病。|近义词：cryptogenic, essential, primary|反义词:secondary, acquired|词性:adjective
词源: From Greek *idios* (“one's own, personal, private”) + *pathos* (“suffering, disease”).
