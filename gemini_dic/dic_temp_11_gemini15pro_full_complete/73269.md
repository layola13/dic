foreappointment:/fɔːrəˈpɔɪntmənt/
n.
预先约定；预定
an arrangement or agreement made in advance
例句：He made a foreappointment with the doctor. 他与医生预先约定了时间。
例句：The foreappointment ensured a smooth process. 预约确保了流程顺利进行。
近义词：prearrangement, prior arrangement, booking, reservation
反义词：impromptu, unplanned, spontaneous
词性: 名词
