natality:/neɪˈtælɪti/
n.
出生率
The ratio of live births to total population.
The natality rate has been declining in recent years.  近年来出生率一直在下降。
近义词:birth rate, fertility rate
反义词:mortality rate
词性:noun

natality:/nəˈtæləti/
n.
出生
The birth of offspring. (Less common usage)
The scientist studied the factors affecting natality in the deer population.  科学家研究了影响鹿群出生的因素。
近义词:birth, parturition
反义词:mortality, death
词性:noun
