fight-with-windmills:/faɪt wɪð ˈwɪndmɪlz/ |idiomatic expression|与风车作战；做徒劳的斗争|To fight imaginary enemies or to pursue a hopeless cause.  It refers to a pointless or futile struggle against something that cannot be defeated.|例句：He was fighting with windmills, trying to convince the committee to fund his impossible project. 他在做无用功，试图说服委员会资助他那不可能实现的项目。

例句：Trying to change his mind is like fighting with windmills.  试图改变他的想法就像与风车作战一样徒劳。|近义词：tilt at windmills, chase rainbows, pursue a lost cause|反义词：face reality, be practical, address real problems|phrase
