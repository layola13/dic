bunked:/bʌŋkt/
verb
睡（通常指在临时的地方）; 逃课; 旷课
slept, especially in a makeshift bed;  skipped classes; played truant
The hikers bunked down in a cave for the night.  徒步旅行者在山洞里过夜。

We bunked off school to go to the beach. 我们逃学去海滩了。

synonym: doss, kip, skip, ditch
antonym: attend, present
