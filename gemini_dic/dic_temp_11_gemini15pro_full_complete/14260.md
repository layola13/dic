autofocuses:/ˌɔːtəʊˈfəʊkəsɪz/ | v. | 自动对焦 |  (of a camera or other device) automatically adjusts the focus of its lens. | My phone camera autofocuses quickly, so I rarely miss a shot. 我的手机摄像头自动对焦很快，所以我很少错过镜头。| The camera autofocuses on the subject's face. 相机自动对焦在主体的脸上。 | synonyms: self-focuses, automatically focuses | antonyms: manually focuses | verb (present tense, third person singular)
