This discutient cream helps reduce swelling.  这种消散性乳膏有助于消肿。(n.)  The doctor prescribed a discutient for the bruise. 医生给瘀伤开了消散药。 | (adj.)  dispersing, resolvent, dissipating | (adj.)  aggravating, worsening | adjective, noun
