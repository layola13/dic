guerrilla:/ɡəˈrɪlə/ (US), /ɡəˈrɪlə/ (UK)| n. |游击队员，游击战|A member of a small independent group taking part in irregular fighting, typically against larger regular forces.|例句：The guerrillas ambushed the army convoy. 游击队员伏击了军队的车队。

例句：The guerrilla war lasted for decades. 这场游击战持续了几十年。

近义词：partisan, insurgent, rebel, freedom fighter|反义词:soldier, regular|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *guerrilla*, diminutive of *guerra* "war", from West Germanic *werra* "confusion, strife, discord, war" (compare with Old Saxon *werra*, Old High German *werra*, German *wirren* "to confuse, entangle"), from Proto-Germanic *werzō "confusion". Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *wers- "to mix, confuse".
