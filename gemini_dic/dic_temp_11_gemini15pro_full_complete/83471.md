hacienda:/ˌhɑːsiˈɛndə/ |n.|  大庄园；种植园|A large estate or plantation, especially in Spanish-speaking countries.|The family owned a vast hacienda in Mexico. 这家人在墨西哥拥有一个广阔的大庄园。|Example:  The old hacienda had been in the family for generations.  这个古老的庄园世代相传在这个家族里。

|Example:  They converted the hacienda into a luxury hotel. 他们把庄园改建成了一家豪华酒店。|Synonyms: estate, plantation, ranch, farm|Antonyms:  smallholding, cottage|Noun
