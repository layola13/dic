metrology:/mɪˈtrɒlədʒi/ /məˈtrɑːlədʒi/
n.|计量学；度量衡学|The science of measurement, embracing both experimental and theoretical determinations at any level of uncertainty in any field of science and technology.|例句：The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is a leader in metrology research. 美国国家标准与技术研究院 (NIST) 是计量学研究的领导者。|例句：Advances in metrology have enabled more precise manufacturing processes. 计量学的进步使得更精确的制造工艺成为可能。|近义词：measurement science|反义词:|词性:noun


metrologies:/mɪˈtrɒlədʒiz/ /məˈtrɑːlədʒiz/
n.|计量学；度量衡学（metrology 的复数）|Plural form of metrology, referring to different systems or areas of measurement science.|例句：Various metrologies are employed in different industries, each tailored to specific needs. 不同的行业采用不同的计量学，每一种都根据特定需求量身定制。|例句：The conference will cover a range of metrologies, including optical, mechanical, and electrical. 会议将涵盖一系列计量学，包括光学、机械和电学。|近义词：measurement sciences|反义词:|词性:noun
