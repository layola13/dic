dirigible:/ˈdɪrɪdʒəbəl/ /ˈdɪrɪdʒɪbəl/
n.|飞艇；可操纵的|A dirigible is a type of airship, a lighter-than-air aircraft that can be steered. |The dirigible floated gracefully across the sky.  飞艇优雅地飘过天空。|Synonyms: airship, zeppelin, blimp|Antonyms: airplane, helicopter|noun


dirigible:/dəˈrɪdʒəbəl/ /daɪˈrɪdʒəbəl/
adj.|可操纵的；可控制的|Something dirigible is capable of being steered or directed.|The new drone was highly dirigible, able to navigate tight spaces. 新的无人机具有很高的可操纵性，能够在狭窄的空间中航行。|Synonyms: steerable, controllable, maneuverable|Antonyms: unsteerable, uncontrollable, fixed|adjective
