excusatory:/ɪkˈskjuːsətɔːri/
adjective
辩解的；道歉的；歉意的
Intended to justify or apologize for an action or remark.
He offered an excusatory smile. 他带着歉意的微笑。
近义词: apologetic, justifying, defensive
反义词: accusatory, blaming
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *excusatorius, from Latin excusatus (past participle of excusare ‘to excuse’), from ex- ‘out’ + causa ‘cause, reason’.
