olmec: /ˈɔːlmek/ | n. adj. | 奥尔梅克人；奥尔梅克文化的 |  A member of an ancient Mesoamerican civilization that flourished on the southern Gulf Coast of Mexico from roughly 1200 to 400 BC. Also used to describe the art and culture of this civilization.  | The Olmec colossal heads are a remarkable feat of ancient engineering. 奥尔梅克巨型头像体现了古代工程的非凡技艺。 |  Mesoamerican, ancient |  Modern, contemporary | noun, adjective
