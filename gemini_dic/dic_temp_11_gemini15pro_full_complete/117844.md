mellone:/məˈloʊni/ | n. | 甜瓜 (尤指哈密瓜) | A large, sweet melon with a pale green rind and orange flesh, originally from Central Asia. |  Example: The ripe mellone was juicy and fragrant.  成熟的甜瓜多汁又芳香。 | Example:  We enjoyed slices of chilled mellone for dessert. 我们享用了切好的冰镇甜瓜作为甜点。 | Synonyms: cantaloupe, muskmelon | Antonyms: None | Noun
