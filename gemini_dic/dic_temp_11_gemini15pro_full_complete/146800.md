overimaginativenesses:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmædʒɪnətɪvnəsəz/ | n. | 过度想象力（复数）| The quality of having an excessively or unrestrained imagination, often leading to unrealistic or fanciful thoughts or ideas.  It's the plural form of "overimaginativeness". |  Her overimaginativenesses often led her to believe in fantastical creatures. 她的过度想象力常常让她相信奇幻生物的存在。| Synonyms: fantasies, flights of fancy, daydreams | Antonyms: practicality, realism | Noun
