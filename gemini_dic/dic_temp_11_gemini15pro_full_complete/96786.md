ineradicable:/ˌɪnɪˈrædɪkəbl/
adjective
不能根除的，无法消除的
Not able to be eradicated; impossible to destroy or remove.
The memory of the trauma was ineradicable.  这创伤的记忆无法磨灭。
Her influence on her students was profound and ineradicable. 她对学生的影响是深刻而无法消除的。
Synonyms: permanent, ingrained, indelible, fixed, enduring
Antonyms: eradicable, temporary, fleeting, ephemeral
词性: 形容词
