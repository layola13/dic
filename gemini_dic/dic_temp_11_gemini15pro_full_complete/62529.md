endurer:/ɪnˈdjʊərər/| n.|忍受者，忍耐者|Someone who endures or suffers something patiently.|例句：He was an endurer of hardship. 他是一个忍受苦难的人。|例句：She proved to be an endurer, facing her illness with courage. 她被证明是一位坚忍不拔的人，勇敢地面对自己的疾病。|近义词：sufferer, tolerator, patient|反义词:quitter, complainer|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Middle English *endurer, from Anglo-Norman endurer, endurour, from Old French endurer (“to endure, tolerate”). Equivalent to endure +‎ -er.
