churnable:/ˈtʃɜːrnəbl/
adj.
可流失的，易变动的
able to be churned; likely to churn (change providers/companies).  Specifically refers to customers who are likely to stop using a service or switch to a competitor.
例句：The company is trying to reduce its churnable customer base.  该公司正试图减少其易流失的客户群。

例句：The telecom industry is known for its high churnable rate. 电信行业以其高客户流失率而闻名。

近义词：volatile, fickle, unstable, fluid
反义词：loyal, stable, committed, sticky
词性：adjective
