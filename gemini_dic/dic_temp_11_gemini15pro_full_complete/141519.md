oppa:/ˈopɑː/| n.|哥哥 (用于女性称呼比自己年长的男性)|A Korean term of address used by females to refer to older males, typically close friends or brothers, but can also be used in a flirtatious way.|例句：Oppa, can you help me with this? 哥哥，你能帮我一下吗？

例句：She called out to her oppa across the street.  她在街对面喊她的哥哥。

|近义词：(Context-dependent) brother, hyung (male equivalent)|反义词: None|词性:Noun
