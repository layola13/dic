nativism:/ˈneɪtɪvɪzəm/| n.|本土主义；地方主义|A policy of favoring native inhabitants as opposed to immigrants.|The rise of nativism in the 20th century led to stricter immigration laws. 20世纪本土主义的兴起导致了更严格的移民法。|近义词：xenophobia, isolationism|反义词:internationalism, cosmopolitanism|词性:noun


Etymology: From Latin *nativus* "native, natural" (from *natus*, past participle of *nasci* "to be born"), + -ism.
