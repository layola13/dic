heartland:/ˈhɑːrtlænd/ (US) /ˈhɑːtlənd/ (UK)| n.|中心地带；腹地；核心地区|The central or most important part of a country, area, or field of activity, typically considered as being its vital center and source of its strength and identity.|The Midwest is often referred to as the heartland of America. 美国中西部通常被称为美国的心脏地带。|synonyms: center, core, hub, nucleus|antonyms: periphery, outskirts, fringe|noun
