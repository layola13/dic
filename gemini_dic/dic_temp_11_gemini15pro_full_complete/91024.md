hushest:/ˈhʌʃɪst/| adj.|最安静的，最寂静的|Superlative form of "hushed," meaning characterized by the greatest degree of silence or quiet.|The hushest sound of the forest was broken by the snap of a twig. 森林里最寂静的声音被一根树枝的折断声打破了。|近义词：quietest, stillest, silentest|反义词：loudest, noisiest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *hushtest, superlative of *hushed, from hush (v.). Ultimately from a sound-imitating base.
