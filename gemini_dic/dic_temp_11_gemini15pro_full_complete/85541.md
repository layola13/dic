hatchers:/ˈhætʃərz/| n.|孵化器; 孵化者|People who or things that hatch something, especially eggs.  Those who devise or invent something new.|The hatchers carefully monitored the temperature and humidity in the incubation room. 孵化员仔细监测孵化室的温度和湿度。

The tech company is known for its innovative hatchers who constantly develop new software. 这家科技公司以其不断开发新软件的创新孵化者而闻名。|近义词：incubators, breeders, originators, inventors|反义词:destroyers|noun
