didelphous:/daɪˈdɛlfəs/
adjective
双雄蕊的；二体雄蕊的
Having two groups or bundles of stamens.  Specifically, having stamens united by their filaments into two groups.
Example: The didelphous flower has two distinct stamen bundles.  这种双体雄蕊的花有两束明显的花丝。
Example:  Didelphous stamens are a characteristic feature of certain plant families. 双体雄蕊是某些植物科的特征。
Synonym: diadelphous
Antonym: None (This is a highly specific botanical term; a direct antonym doesn't exist.)
词性: adjective
