exsanguinity:/ˌɛksæŋˈɡwɪnɪti/
n.
失血; 血虚; 苍白
The state of being exsanguinated; the condition of having lost a large amount of blood.
The victim's exsanguinity was the cause of death. 受害者因失血过多而死。

Exsanguinity led to the patient's demise. 病人因失血而死。

近义词:bloodlessness, anemia, pallor
反义词: plethora,ruddy
词性:noun
