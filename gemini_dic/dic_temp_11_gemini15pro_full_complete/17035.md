batful:/ˈbæt.fʊl/| adj.|满满一球棒的，大量的|A batful is an informal measurement meaning the amount that a baseball bat can hold.  It implies a large, imprecise quantity.|He scooped a batful of candy into his bag. 他往袋子里装了满满一球棒的糖果。|Synonyms: loads, heaps, plenty|Antonyms: little, few|Adjective
