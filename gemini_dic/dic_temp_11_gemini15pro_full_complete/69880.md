The ferryland offered stunning views of the surrounding islands. 渡口提供了周围岛屿的壮丽景色。| 近义词: ferry terminal, dock, pier, wharf | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ferry + land.  Ferry (from Old English *ferian, from Proto-Germanic *farjaną, meaning "to carry, convey") + land (from Old English land, meaning "land, territory").
