interrupt:/ˌɪntəˈrʌpt/ (US) /ˌɪntəˈrʌpt/ (UK)| v. n.|打断，中断；插嘴；阻断|To stop the continuous progress of (an activity or process).|The loud noise interrupted my sleep.  喧闹声打断了我的睡眠。|Synonyms: disturb, disrupt, break off|Antonyms: continue, proceed|verb

interrupt:/ˌɪntəˈrʌpt/ (US) /ˌɪntəˈrʌpt/ (UK)| n.|中断，打断|An interruption.|The frequent interrupts made it difficult to concentrate.  频繁的中断使人难以集中注意力。|Synonyms: disruption, break|Antonyms: continuation|noun

Etymology: From Latin *interrumpere*, from *inter-* (“between”) + *rumpere* (“to break”).
