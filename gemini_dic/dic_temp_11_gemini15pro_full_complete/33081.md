chrismal: /ˈkrɪzməl/ | adj. n.| 圣油的；圣油；圣膏|Relating to chrism, a consecrated oil used in various Christian ceremonies, especially confirmation, baptism, and ordination; the chrism itself.|The bishop blessed the chrismal oil. 主教祝福了圣油。|The chrismal was applied to the forehead of the newly baptized. 圣油涂抹在新受洗者的额头上。|近义词：consecrated, sacred|反义词：profane, secular|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *chrismale, from Latin chrisma ("anointing, unction, chrism"), from Ancient Greek χρῖσμα (khrîsma, “anointing”), from χρίω (khríō, “I anoint”).
