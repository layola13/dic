firstling:/ˈfɜːrstlɪŋ/ | n. | 初生的；最早的；初熟的果实 |  The first offspring of an animal, or the first fruits or flowers of a season.  A firstling can also refer to something that is the first of its kind. |  The farmer offered the firstling of his flock to the gods.  农民将他的羊群中头生的羊献给了神。 | synonyms: firstborn, first fruit, first produce | antonyms: last-born, latest | noun
