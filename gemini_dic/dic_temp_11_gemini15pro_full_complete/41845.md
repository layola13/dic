corybantic:/ˌkɔːrɪˈbæn.tɪk/ | adj. | 狂乱的，狂热的，激动不已的 | Characterized by or resembling a wild, frenzied revelry, especially of emotional or religious nature.  Originating from the Corybantes, priests of Cybele, known for their ecstatic dances and music. | The crowd's reaction to the winning goal was corybantic.  人群对制胜球的反应是狂热的。 | The music inspired a corybantic dance.  这音乐激发了一场狂乱的舞蹈。 | 近义词: frenzied, ecstatic, wild, unrestrained, boisterous | 反义词: calm, peaceful, subdued, restrained, quiet | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Corybanticus, from Ancient Greek Κορύβας (Kórýbas), a name for priests of Cybele, whose rites were characterized by wild, ecstatic dancing.
