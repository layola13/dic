disinvest:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛst/ | v. |撤资，减少投资| To withdraw investment from (something).  The process of selling off assets, such as stocks or bonds, or reducing spending on something.|  The company disinvested from the failing business.  这家公司从失败的业务中撤资。|The government disinvested in education. 政府减少了对教育的投资。| 近义词: divest, withdraw, pull out | 反义词: invest, fund, finance | verb
