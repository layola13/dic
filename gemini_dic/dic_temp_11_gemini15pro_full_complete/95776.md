incontiguous:/ˌɪnkənˈtɪɡjuəs/ | adj. | 不连续的，不相邻的 | Not touching; not in physical contact; separated. | The houses on the street are incontiguous, with large gaps between them. 街上的房子不连续，彼此之间有很大的空隙。| The data is stored in incontiguous blocks on the hard drive. 数据存储在硬盘上不连续的块中。 |  nonadjacent, discontinuous, separate, detached | adjacent, contiguous, adjoining, abutting | adjective
