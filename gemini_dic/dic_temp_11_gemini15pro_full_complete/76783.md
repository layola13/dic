gamification:/ˌɡeɪmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
游戏化
The application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts.
Many companies are using gamification to increase employee engagement. 许多公司正在使用游戏化来提高员工敬业度。

Companies use gamification to encourage customer loyalty.  公司使用游戏化来鼓励顾客忠诚度。

近义词：ludification
反义词： (No direct antonym, possibly "seriousness" or "formality" in specific contexts)


词性: noun

词源:  "gamification" is a relatively recent term, coined in the early 2000s. It combines "game" + "-ify" (meaning to make into) + "-cation" (a suffix forming nouns of action or process).  Therefore, it literally means "the process of making something into a game."
