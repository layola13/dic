evolvability:/ɪˌvɒlvəˈbɪləti/| n.|进化性，演化能力|The capacity of a biological system to evolve.  This encompasses the ability to generate heritable phenotypic variation and the potential for this variation to be acted upon by natural selection.|The evolvability of a species is crucial to its long-term survival. 一个物种的进化能力对其长期生存至关重要。|近义词：adaptability, plasticity|反义词：rigidity, inflexibility|词性：noun
evolve:/ɪˈvɒlv/| v.|进化，发展，逐渐形成|Develop gradually, especially from a simple to a more complex form.|The company has evolved from a small startup to a multinational corporation. 这家公司已经从一家小型初创企业发展成为一家跨国公司。|近义词：develop, progress, adapt|反义词：devolve, regress, stagnate|词性：verb
