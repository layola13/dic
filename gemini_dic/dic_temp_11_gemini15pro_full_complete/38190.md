compulsively:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvli/| adv.|强迫地，不由自主地|In a compulsive manner; characterized by obsession or irresistible impulse.|He compulsively washes his hands every few minutes. 他每隔几分钟就强迫性地洗手。|近义词:obsessively, involuntarily, uncontrollably|反义词:willingly, voluntarily|词性:副词
Etymology: From compulsive + -ly. Compulsive comes from Late Latin compuls- "driven together," from the past participle stem of compellere "to compel."
