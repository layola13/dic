isozooid:/ˌaɪsəˈzoʊɪd/
n.
同形体；同形个员；同形群体
An individual zooid in a colony of polymorphic animals that is similar in form to other members of the colony.
Individual isozooids within a colony can specialize in different functions, such as feeding or reproduction.  群体中的单个同形个员可以专门从事不同的功能，例如进食或繁殖。
Isozooids are identical polyps that comprise the main body of a soft coral. 同形体是组成软珊瑚主体的相同息肉。
近义词: zooid, polyp
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly heterozooid for polymorphic colonies)
词性: noun
