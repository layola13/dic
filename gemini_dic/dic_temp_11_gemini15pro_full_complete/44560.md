crotchet:/ˈkrɒtʃɪt/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːtʃɪt/ (US)| n.| (音乐)四分音符；怪念头；奇想；钩针|A crotchet is a musical note with the time value of a quarter of a whole note. It can also refer to an odd or whimsical notion.|She played a series of crotchets on the piano. 她在钢琴上演奏了一连串的四分音符。|He has a crotchet about always wearing green socks. 他有个怪念头，总是穿绿袜子。|synonyms: quarter note, whim, fancy, quirk|antonyms: (For musical meaning) whole note, half note|noun
