biosystem:/ˈbaɪoʊˌsɪstəm/
n.
生物系统
A complex network of interacting biological entities, including organisms, their physical environment, and the processes that connect them.
The Amazon rainforest is a complex biosystem. 亚马逊雨林是一个复杂的生物系统。

The coral reef is a delicate biosystem. 珊瑚礁是一个脆弱的生物系统.


近义词:ecosystem, biological system
反义词:None
词性:noun
