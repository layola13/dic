gablelike:/ˈɡeɪblˌlaɪk/
adjective
像山墙的，三角形的
Resembling a gable; shaped like a gable.
The gablelike roof of the small cabin provided a picturesque view. 小木屋的山墙状屋顶提供了一幅如画的景色。
The barn had a gablelike facade.  谷仓有一个像山墙一样的正面。
近义词:gabled, triangular, peaked
反义词:flat, curved
