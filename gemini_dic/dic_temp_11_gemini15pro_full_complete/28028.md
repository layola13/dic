capacitate:/kəˈpæsɪteɪt/
verb
使能够；使有能力
To give someone the ability or power to do something.
The training capacitated her to do the job.  这个培训使她能够胜任这项工作。

近义词:enable, empower, qualify
反义词:disable, incapacitate
词性:v.

词源: From Late Latin *capacitatus, past participle of *capacitare (“to make capable”), from Latin capax (“capable”).
