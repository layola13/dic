miscoordinate:/ˌmɪskəʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ | v. | 协调不良，配合失调 | To fail to coordinate properly; to cause a lack of coordination. |  The dancer's arms and legs seemed to miscoordinate, making her stumble. 舞蹈演员的手臂和腿似乎配合失调，使她踉跄了一下。

The construction project was miscoordinated, leading to delays and cost overruns.  这个建筑项目协调不当，导致了延误和成本超支。 | Near synonyms:  mismanage, bungle, fumble, muddle | Antonyms: coordinate, synchronize, harmonize | verb
