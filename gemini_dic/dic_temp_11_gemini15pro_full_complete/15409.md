baconian:/beɪˈkoʊniən/
adjective
培根的；培根哲学的；归纳法的
Of or relating to Francis Bacon or his philosophy; inductive.
例句：His scientific method was strictly Baconian, emphasizing observation and experimentation. 他的科学方法严格遵循培根的理论，强调观察和实验。

例句：The Baconian method of scientific inquiry involves rigorous observation and inductive reasoning. 培根的科学探究方法包括严谨的观察和归纳推理。

近义词：inductive, empirical
反义词：deductive, theoretical
词性: adjective
