clunkyly:/ˈklʌŋkɪli/| adv.|笨拙地，笨重地|Moving or operating in a clumsy and awkward way, often making a lot of noise.|The robot moved clunkily across the room.  机器人笨拙地穿过房间。|The old car clunkily made its way up the hill.  这辆旧车笨重地爬上山坡。|近义词：awkwardly, clumsily, heavily|反义词：gracefully, smoothly, elegantly|词性:副词

clunky:/ˈklʌŋki/| adj.|笨拙的，笨重的|Clumsy, awkward, and heavy; not graceful or elegant.|The software felt clunky and outdated.  这个软件感觉笨重且过时。|She wore clunky platform shoes.  她穿着笨重的厚底鞋。|近义词：awkward, clumsy, cumbersome|反义词：graceful, elegant, streamlined|词性:形容词

Etymology: From *clunk* + *-y* (adjective suffix) + *-ly* (adverb suffix).  *Clunk* is imitative in origin, referring to a dull, heavy sound.  词源：来自 *clunk* + *-y* （形容词后缀） + *-ly* （副词后缀）。 *Clunk* 是拟声词，指的是沉闷的、沉重的声音。
