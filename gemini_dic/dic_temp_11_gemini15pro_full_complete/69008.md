fatted:/ˈfætɪd/ | adj. | 肥育的；养肥的 | Made fat by special feeding, typically for slaughter. | The farmer brought a fatted calf to the feast. 农民带了一头肥育的小牛来赴宴。 | The fatted pig was ready for roasting. 养肥的猪准备好了可以烤了。| 近义词：plump, fleshy, well-fed | 反义词：lean, thin, emaciated |词性: 形容词
