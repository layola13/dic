lenticellate:/lɛnˈtɪsɪˌleɪt/ | adj. | 具皮孔的；有皮孔的 | Having lenticels; covered with lenticels. |  The young branches are lenticellate.  这些幼枝有皮孔。 | Synonym: porous, aerated | Antonym:  impermeable | adjective

lenticel:/ˈlɛntɪsl/ | n. | 皮孔 | A small, raised pore in the bark of woody stems and roots that allows gases to pass through. | The trunk was covered with lenticels.  树干上布满了皮孔。| Synonym: pore, stoma | Antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lenticella, diminutive of Latin lens, lent- (“lentil”).  The lenticels resemble the shape of lentils.
