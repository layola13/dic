antimusicalnesses:/ˌæntiˈmjuːzɪkəlnəsɪz/
|n.|非音乐性，缺乏音乐性|The state or quality of being unmusical; lacking musical qualities.  A collection of instances of being unmusical.|His amusicalnesses grated on her nerves. 他的缺乏音乐性让她很烦。|近义词：unmusicality, dissonance, discord|反义词：musicality, harmony, euphony|词性:noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek)
musical (adjective): relating to music (from Latin "musicus")
-ness (suffix): forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition
