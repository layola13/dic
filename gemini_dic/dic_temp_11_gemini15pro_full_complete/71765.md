fleak:/fliːk/
noun
小斑点，微粒，少量
a small speck, particle, or bit
例句：There's a fleak of dust on your coat. 你外套上有个小灰尘。

例句：She added a fleak of pepper to the soup. 她往汤里加了一点胡椒粉。

近义词：speck, particle, bit, dot, mote
反义词：(No clear antonym, possibly "lot," "abundance")
词性：noun
