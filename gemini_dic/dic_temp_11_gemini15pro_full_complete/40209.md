contesters:/kənˈtɛstərz/
n.
参赛者，竞争者，争辩者
People who compete or contend for something; those who take part in a contest or dispute.
The contest attracted contestants from all over the country.  这场比赛吸引了来自全国各地的参赛者。
Several contestants argued with the judge's decision.  几位参赛者对裁判的决定提出了异议。
competitors, rivals, participants, contenders, disputants
collaborators, allies
noun
