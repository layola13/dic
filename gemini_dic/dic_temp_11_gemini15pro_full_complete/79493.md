globalizing: /ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈɡləʊbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb, adjective|全球化（的）|The process of making something worldwide in scope or application.  Becoming worldwide in scope or application.|例句：The company is globalizing its operations.  这家公司正在将其业务全球化。|近义词：internationalizing, expanding, spreading|反义词:localizing, restricting|词性: verb, adjective
Etymology: From globe + -ize + -ing.  "Globe" ultimately comes from Latin "globus," meaning "sphere."  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become." "-ing" is a suffix that can indicate a present participle or gerund.
