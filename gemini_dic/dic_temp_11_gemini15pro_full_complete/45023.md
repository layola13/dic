ctenoid:/ˈsiːtənɔɪd/| adj. n.|栉状的，栉鳞鱼|Having or covered with ctenoid scales; belonging to or characteristic of the Ctenoidei, a group of fishes having such scales.|例句：The perch has ctenoid scales.  鲈鱼有栉鳞。|例句：Ctenoid scales are rough to the touch. 栉鳞摸起来很粗糙。|近义词：scaly, rough|反义词：smooth|词性:adjective, noun

ctenoid:/ˈsiːtənɔɪd/| n.|栉鳞鱼|A fish having ctenoid scales.|例句：Many marine fish are ctenoids. 许多海洋鱼类是栉鳞鱼。|例句：Ctenoids are a diverse group of fishes. 栉鳞鱼是一个多样化的鱼类群体。|近义词：fish|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of fish)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ktenoeidēs* "comb-like," from *kteis* (genitive *ktenos*) "comb" + *-oeidēs* "-oid."
