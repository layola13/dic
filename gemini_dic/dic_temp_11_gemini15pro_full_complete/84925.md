hanse: /ˈhɑːnzə/ | adj. | 汉萨的 | Relating to the Hanseatic League. |  Lübeck was a powerful Hanseatic city. 吕贝克是一个强大的汉萨城市。| 近义词: Hanseatic | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective
