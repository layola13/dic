overidentified:/ˌōvər aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd/
adjective
过度认同的；过分认同的
excessively identified, especially with another person or group, often to the point of losing one's own separate identity.  This often occurs in psychology, particularly in relation to family dynamics or with a fictional character.
The young boy overidentified with the superhero in the movie, wearing the costume everywhere he went.  这个小男孩对电影里的超级英雄过度认同，走到哪都穿着那套服装。

She overidentified with her mother's struggles, to the detriment of her own mental health. 她过分认同她母亲的挣扎，损害了她自己的心理健康。

近义词: overly identified, excessively empathetic
反义词: detached, indifferent
词性: adjective

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "identified" (past participle of "identify," from Latin "identificare," meaning "to make identical").
