asp:/æsp/ | n. | 毒蛇; 白杨 | A venomous snake, especially of the cobra family, found in Africa and Asia. Also, a type of poplar tree. |  The asp is a symbol of death in ancient Egyptian art.  埃及古代艺术中，毒蛇象征着死亡。| synonym: viper, adder (for snake); poplar (for tree) | antonym:  N/A | noun

asp:/æsp/ | n. | (computing) Active Server Page | A Microsoft technology for generating dynamic web pages. | The website was built using ASP.  这个网站是用ASP搭建的。| synonym: ASP.NET, PHP, JSP | antonym:  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æsp, from Proto-Germanic *aspis, from Latin aspis, from Greek ἀσπίς (aspis), meaning "shield" (referring to the shape of the Egyptian cobra's hood). For the tree, the origin is uncertain, possibly related to Greek αἴγειρος (aigeiros), meaning "poplar."
