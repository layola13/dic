multanimous:/ˌmʌlˈtænɪməs/ | adj. | 大气的，宽宏大量的，慷慨的 | Having a great soul; magnanimous. Showing or suggesting generosity or nobility of mind or character.  | Example: His multanimous gesture of donating a large sum to charity was widely praised. 他慷慨地向慈善机构捐赠巨款的举动广受赞誉。

| Example:  The multanimous king pardoned the rebels. 宽宏大量的国王赦免了叛军。 | 近义词: magnanimous, generous, noble, charitable, benevolent | 反义词: petty, mean, stingy, selfish, ungenerous | 词性: 形容词
