mandragora: /mænˈdræɡərə/ | n. | 曼德拉草，风茄 | A plant with a forked root resembling a human form, historically believed to have magical powers and to shriek when pulled from the ground. |  The mandragora root was highly prized in medieval times for its supposed medicinal and magical properties.  曼德拉草根在中世纪因其所谓的药用和神奇特性而备受珍视。

The witch used mandragora in her potion. 女巫在她的药水中使用了曼德拉草。 | 近义词: mandrake | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
