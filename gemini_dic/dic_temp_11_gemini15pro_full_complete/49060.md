dehumanization:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌdiːhjuːmənɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
非人化; 去人性化
The process of depriving a person or group of positive human qualities.
The Nazis' propaganda engaged in the systematic dehumanization of Jews, portraying them as vermin. 纳粹的宣传对犹太人进行了系统的非人化，将他们描绘成害虫。

近义词:brutalization, degradation
反义词:humanization
词性:noun


dehumanize:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪz/ /ˌdiːhjuːmənɪz/
vt.
使失去人性
To deprive of human qualities, personality, or spirit.
War can dehumanize people, making them indifferent to suffering. 战争会使人失去人性，使他们对苦难漠不关心。

近义词:degrade, brutalize
反义词:humanize
词性:verb


词源:
From de- + humanize, from human + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin *humanus*, from homo “man.”
