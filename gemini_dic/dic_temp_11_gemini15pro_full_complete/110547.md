limenean:/lɪˈmiːniən/
adjective
与门槛有关的；与阈值有关的
Relating to a threshold or limen, especially in psychology, referring to the point at which a stimulus is just strong enough to be perceived.
The limenean value for the detection of the bitter taste of quinine varies widely among individuals.  个体对奎宁苦味的检测阈值差异很大。
Near Antonyms: subliminal
