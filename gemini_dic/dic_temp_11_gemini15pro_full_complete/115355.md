manorial:/məˈnɔːriəl/
adjective
庄园的；领地的
Relating to a manor or its lord.
The manorial system was the basic economic arrangement of medieval Europe.  欧洲中世纪的基本经济安排是庄园制度。
The historian described the manorial courts and their customs.  历史学家描述了庄园法庭及其习俗。
近义词:feudal, landed, estate
反义词:non-feudal, urban
词性:adjective
