nondepletive:/ˌnɒndɪˈpliːtɪv/
adjective
非消耗性的；非递减的
Not depleting or reducing something; not being used up.
Solar energy is a nondepletive resource.  太阳能是一种非消耗性能源。
Using a nondepletive method of fishing ensures the fish population remains healthy. 使用非消耗性捕鱼方法确保鱼类种群保持健康。
近义词: renewable, sustainable, inexhaustible
反义词: depletive, consumable, exhaustible
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + depletive.  Depletive comes from the Latin word "deplere" meaning "to empty out."
