afternoons:/ˌæftərˈnuːnz/
n.
下午；午后
the part of the day between noon and evening.
例句：
I spend my afternoons reading.
我下午都在读书。

We often go for walks in the afternoons.
我们经常下午去散步。

近义词：
p.m., midday
反义词:
mornings

词性:
名词(plural noun)

词源:
From Middle English *afternone, *afternon, from Old English æfternōn (“afternoon”), from æfter (“after”) + nōn (“noon”). Cognate with Scots efternuin, efternune (“afternoon”), West Frisian efternon (“afternoon”), Dutch namiddag (“afternoon”), German Nachmittag (“afternoon”), Danish eftermiddag (“afternoon”), Swedish eftermiddag (“afternoon”), Icelandic eftir hádegi (“afternoon”).
