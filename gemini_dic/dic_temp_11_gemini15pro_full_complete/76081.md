furred:/fɜːrd/
adjective
有毛皮的，覆有毛皮的
covered with fur
The cat is a small furred animal. 这只猫是一只小的有毛动物。
The actress wore a furred coat.  这位女演员穿着一件毛皮大衣。
近义词: hairy, furry
反义词: hairless, bald
词性: adjective

词源: From the noun "fur" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Fur" comes from Old French "forre" (sheath, lining), from Frankish or a similar West Germanic source.
