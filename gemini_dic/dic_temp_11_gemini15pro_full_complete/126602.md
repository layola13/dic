词源:  The Negroni is named after Count Camillo Negroni, who allegedly asked for his Americano cocktail (Campari, sweet vermouth, and soda water) to be strengthened with gin instead of soda water.  内格罗尼以卡米洛·内格罗尼伯爵的名字命名，据说他要求用金酒代替苏打水来加强他的美式鸡尾酒（金巴利、甜味苦艾酒和苏打水）。
