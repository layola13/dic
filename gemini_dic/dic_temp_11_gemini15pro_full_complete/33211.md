chronicities:/krɒnɪˈsɪtiz/ /krɑːnɪˈsɪtiz/
n.
慢性；长期性；长期持续的状态
The study examines the chronicities of various diseases and their impact on the healthcare system.  这项研究调查了各种疾病的长期性和它们对医疗保健系统的影响。
The chronicities of poverty and unemployment in the region are major concerns. 该地区长期存在的贫困和失业问题是主要关切。
例句：The chronicities of her condition made it difficult to treat. 她的病情长期存在，难以治疗。
近义词：persistence, duration, continuance
反义词：transience, brevity
词性：名词


词源:
From chronic + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) + -ies (plural suffix). Ultimately from Greek khronikos “of time” (from khronos “time”) + -ity + -ies.
