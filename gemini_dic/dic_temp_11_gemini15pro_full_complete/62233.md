encroachingly:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃɪŋli/ | adv. | 侵入地，侵占地 | In a way that gradually intrudes on or takes over something else. |  The city lights spread encroachingly into the countryside. 城市的灯光侵入式地蔓延到乡村。 |  The desert is encroachingly upon the fertile land. 沙漠正逐渐侵蚀肥沃的土地。 | 近义词: invasively, intrusively, gradually | 反义词: recedingly, withdrawingly | 词性: 副词

encroach:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ | v. | 侵占，侵入 | Intrude on (a person's territory or a thing considered to be a right). | The suburbs encroached further into the rural areas. 郊区进一步侵占了农村地区。 |  Don't encroach on my privacy. 不要侵犯我的隐私。 | 近义词: intrude, infringe, impinge | 反义词: retreat, withdraw | 词性: 动词
