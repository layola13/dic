gadere: /ˈɡæðər/ (sometimes /ˈɡeɪdər/) | v. | 聚集，收集，推测，认为 | To bring together into one place or collection; to infer or conclude. |  Example 1: The farmer gathered the sheep into the pen. 农民把羊聚集到羊圈里。

Example 2:  I gather you won't be joining us for dinner. 我想你不会和我们一起吃晚饭了。 | 近义词: assemble, collect, amass, infer, deduce | 反义词: disperse, scatter, dissipate | 词性: verb
