auteurist:/ˌɔːtəˈrɪst/
n.
auteur理论支持者，作者论者
A filmmaker whose personal influence and artistic control over a movie are so great that the filmmaker is regarded as the author of the movie.
例句：The auteurist approach to film criticism emphasizes the director's unique vision.  作者论的电影批评方法强调导演独特的视野。

例句：Many consider Quentin Tarantino an auteurist due to his distinct style and recurring themes. 许多人认为昆汀·塔伦蒂诺是一位作者论电影导演，因为他独特的风格和反复出现的主题。

近义词：director, filmmaker
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "collaborator" in a contrasting sense)
词性: noun
