fend:/fɛnd/
verb
抵挡，挡开; 照顾自己; 独立生活
to defend oneself from a blow, attack, or attacker
例句：He raised his hand to fend off the blow. 他举起手来挡住这一击。
例句：The company is having to fend off a hostile takeover bid. 这家公司不得不抵御恶意收购。
近义词：defend, protect, guard, resist, repel
反义词：submit, surrender
词性：verb

fend for oneself:/fɛnd fɔːr wʌnˈsɛlf/
verb phrase
照顾自己; 独立生活
to take care of oneself without help from anyone else
例句：He left home at 16 and had to fend for himself. 他16岁就离开了家，不得不自己照顾自己。
例句：She’s been fending for herself since her parents died. 自从父母去世后，她就一直靠自己生活。
近义词：manage, cope, survive, look after oneself
反义词：depend on others
词性：verb phrase
