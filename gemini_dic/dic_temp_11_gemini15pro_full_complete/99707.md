interloper:/ˈɪntərˌloʊpər/ (US), /ɪnˈtɜːləʊpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |入侵者，干涉者，闯入者|Someone who becomes involved in a place or situation where they are not wanted or are considered not to belong.|The meeting was interrupted by an interloper who had no business being there.  会议被一个与会议无关的闯入者打断。|Synonyms: intruder, trespasser, meddler, interferer|Antonyms: host, owner, resident|Noun
