misremembered:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbərd/ | v. | 记错，误记 | To remember incorrectly; have a false memory of. | I misremembered the date of the meeting and arrived a day late. 我记错了会议日期，晚到了一天。| Example: She misremembered the lyrics to her favorite song. 她记错了她最喜欢的歌的歌词。 | Synonyms: misrecollect, mistake, distort, confuse | Antonyms: remember, recall, recollect | verb
