compellability:/kəmˌpɛləˈbɪləti/ | n. |可强制性，可强迫性 |The quality or state of being compellable; the ability to compel someone to do something, especially to give evidence in a court of law. | The witness's compellability was questioned by the defense. 证人的可强制性受到了辩方的质疑。| synonym: coercibility, enforceability | antonym:  non-compellability, optionality | noun


compel:/kəmˈpɛl/ | v. |强迫，迫使 |To force or oblige (someone) to do something. | The law compels drivers to wear seatbelts.  法律强制驾驶员系安全带。 | synonym: force, oblige, coerce | antonym: allow, permit | verb
