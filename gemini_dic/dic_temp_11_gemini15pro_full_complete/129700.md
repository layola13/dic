non-domesticated:/ˌnɒn dəˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 非家养的，野生的 | Not tamed or cultivated; wild.  Referring to animals or plants that have not been bred or adapted for human use. |  Example: Non-domesticated animals can be dangerous.  非家养的动物可能很危险。

| Example: The study focused on the behavior of non-domesticated felines. 这项研究的重点是非家养猫科动物的行为。 | Synonym: wild, feral, untamed | Antonym: domesticated, tame, cultivated | Adjective
