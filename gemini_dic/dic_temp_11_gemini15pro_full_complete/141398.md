ophicleide:/ˈɒfɪklaɪd/ | n. | 低音铜管乐器;奥菲克莱德 |A keyed brass instrument similar to a tuba, popular in the 19th century.  It had a wide conical bore and a cup-shaped mouthpiece. | The ophicleide had a powerful sound, but was difficult to play in tune. 奥菲克莱德的声音很洪亮，但很难演奏得音准。| The ophicleide was eventually replaced by the tuba. 奥菲克莱德最终被大号取代了。| synonym:keyed bugle, tuba| antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps flute or piccolo as a contrasting high-pitched instrument) | noun
