congruity: /kənˈɡruːɪti/ | n. | 一致性；适合性；和谐；全等 | the quality of agreeing; being suitable and appropriate; harmony;  a point of agreement |  There is a definite congruity between the two stories. 这两个故事之间肯定有相似之处。 |  agreement, consistency, harmony, accord, compatibility |  incongruity, disparity, discrepancy, incompatibility | noun

congruent: /ˈkɒŋɡruːənt/ | adj. | 一致的；适合的；全等的 | agreeing; corresponding;  (of figures) identical in form; coinciding exactly when superimposed |  The two triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形是全等的。| similar, analogous, corresponding, matching | incongruent, disparate, dissimilar, different | adjective
