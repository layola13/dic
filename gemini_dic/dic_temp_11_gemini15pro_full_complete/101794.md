irrigate:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪt/| v. |灌溉，冲洗|To supply water to (land or crops) in order to help growth, typically by means of channels.|The farmer irrigated his fields. 农民灌溉了他的田地。|water, moisten|drain, dry|verb
irrigate:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪt/| v. |冲洗（伤口等）|To wash out (a body cavity or wound) with water or a medicated fluid.|The nurse irrigated the wound with saline solution. 护士用生理盐水冲洗伤口。|cleanse, flush|infect, contaminate|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin irrigare "to water, moisten," from in- "in, into" + rigare "to water, moisten."
