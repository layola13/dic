jougs:/dʒuːɡz/ | n. | 铁枷，颈枷 | An iron collar, fastened by a short chain to a wall or post, formerly used as a punishment and restraint, especially in Scotland. |  The jougs were a common sight outside churches in the 16th and 17th centuries.  16和17世纪，教堂外经常能看到颈枷。| 近义词: stocks, pillory | 反义词: freedom, liberty | noun
