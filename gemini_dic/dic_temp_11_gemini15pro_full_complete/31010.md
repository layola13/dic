challan:/tʃəˈlɑːn/ | n. | 罚单，传票，挑战 | An official form, especially one used as a receipt or authorization. In South Asia, it often refers to a traffic ticket or a fine. |  The traffic police issued him a challan for speeding. 交警给他开了一张超速罚单。| synonyms: ticket, summons, citation | antonyms: acquittal, pardon | noun

challan:/tʃəˈlɑːn/ | vt. |  挑战，召唤，传唤 | To issue a challan to someone; to challenge someone. | The officer challaned the driver for reckless driving.  警察以鲁莽驾驶为由传唤了司机。| synonyms: summon, challenge, cite | antonyms: dismiss, ignore | verb
