corvee: /kɔːrˈveɪ/ (US) /kɔːˈveɪ/ (UK)| n.|徭役；强制劳动|Unpaid labor imposed by a feudal lord on his tenants, or a day’s unpaid labor for a public purpose.|The peasants resented the corvee, as it took them away from their own fields. 农民们憎恨徭役，因为它让他们无法耕种自己的田地。|The villagers had to perform corvee to maintain the roads. 村民们必须服徭役来维护道路。|Synonyms: forced labor, statute labor, compulsory labor|Antonyms: voluntary work, free labor|noun
