cheque:/tʃek/ (British English), check:/tʃek/ (American English)| n.|支票|A written order to a bank to pay a stated sum from the drawer's account.|I wrote him a cheque for £50. 我给他写了一张50英镑的支票。|Synonyms: draft, order, bill of exchange|Antonyms: cash, deposit|noun


cheque:/tʃek/ (British English), check:/tʃek/ (American English)| v.|用支票支付|To pay with a cheque.|I chequed £100 out of my account. 我从我的账户里支取了100英镑。|Synonyms: pay by check|Antonyms: pay cash|verb
