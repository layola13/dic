dusken:/ˈdʌskən/ | v. | 变昏暗，(使)朦胧 | To become or make dim or shadowy, especially as twilight approaches. | The light duskened as the sun began to set.  随着太阳开始落山，光线变暗了。| 近义词: darken, dim, fade | 反义词: brighten, lighten | 词性: 动词

dusken:/ˈdʌskən/ | n. | 薄暮，黄昏 | The time of day immediately following sunset.  |  The dusken sky was a beautiful mix of orange and purple. 黄昏的天空是橙色和紫色的美丽混合。| 近义词: twilight, dusk, evening | 反义词: dawn, daybreak | 词性: 名词
