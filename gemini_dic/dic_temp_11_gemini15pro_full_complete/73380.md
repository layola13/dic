forehanded:/fɔːrˈhændɪd/ | adj. |有远见的；精明的；提前准备好的；熟练的| Characterized by foresight and prudence; provident; skillful and resourceful.| She was forehanded enough to bring an umbrella. 她很有远见，带了一把伞。| 近义词: provident, prudent, thrifty, farsighted, prepared | 反义词: improvident, imprudent, shortsighted, unprepared | 词性: adjective

forehanded:/fɔːrˈhændɪd/ | adj. |（网球等）正手的| Executed with the palm of the hand turned forward.|  He hit a powerful forehanded shot. 他打了一个强有力的正手球。| 近义词: right-handed (in some contexts) | 反义词: backhanded | 词性: adjective
