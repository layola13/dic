bendy: /ˈbɛndi/ | adj. | 易弯曲的；柔韧的 | Easily bent; flexible. | The toy was made of bendy plastic. 这个玩具是用可弯曲的塑料制成的。 |  The gymnast was incredibly bendy and could contort her body into amazing shapes. 体操运动员非常柔韧，可以把她的身体扭曲成各种惊人的形状。| 近义词: flexible, pliable, supple | 反义词: rigid, stiff, inflexible | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bend (v.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  The suffix "-y" is added to verbs or nouns to form adjectives meaning "having the quality of" or "full of."  The verb "bend" comes from Old English *bendan, of Germanic origin.
