carbuncle: /ˈkɑːrbʌŋk(ə)l/ (US) /ˈkɑːbʌŋkəl/ (UK)| n.|痈; 红宝石; 炭疽|A painful, localized bacterial infection of the skin and underlying tissues, larger than a boil. Also, a deep red gemstone, especially a garnet.  In older usage, could also refer to anthrax.|

例句：
The doctor diagnosed the painful swelling on his neck as a carbuncle.  医生诊断他脖子上的疼痛肿胀是痈。

She wore a necklace with a large, glittering carbuncle. 她戴着一条镶嵌着闪亮大红宝石的项链。


近义词：boil, abscess, ruby, garnet|
反义词:  (For the medical meaning) healthy skin|
词性: noun
