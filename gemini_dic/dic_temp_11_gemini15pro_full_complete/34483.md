clayiest:/ˈkleɪɪɪst/ | adj. | 最黏土的，含黏土最多的 |  Superlative form of clayey: most clayey.  Containing the greatest amount of clay. | The clayiest soil in the garden was difficult to work with. 花园里黏土最多的土壤很难耕作。|  sticky, gummy, dense | loose, sandy, porous | adjective

Etymology:
From clay + -y + -est.  "Clay" comes from Old English "clæg" and Proto-Germanic "*klajjaz."  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."  "-est" is a superlative suffix.
