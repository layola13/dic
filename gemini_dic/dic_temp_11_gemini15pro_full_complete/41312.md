corecipient:/ˌkɔːrɪˈsɪpiənt/ | n. |共同收件人；联名收件人|A person who receives something jointly with another person or other people.|The email was sent to me and my colleague as corecipients. 这封邮件是发给我和我的同事作为共同收件人的。|近义词：joint recipient, fellow recipient|反义词:sender, originator|词性:名词

Etymology:  From 'co-' (together, joint) + 'recipient' (receiver).  'Recipient' comes from Latin 'recipere' (to receive).
