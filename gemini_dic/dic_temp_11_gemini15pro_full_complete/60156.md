effeminate: /ɪˈfɛmɪnət/ | adj. |  柔弱的，女人气的；缺乏男子气概的 | Having or showing characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly. |  He had an effeminate manner that some people found off-putting.  他有一种女人气的举止，让一些人感到反感。 | Synonyms: womanish, unmanly, sissy, girly | Antonyms: manly, masculine, virile | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *effeminātus*, past participle of effēmināre (“to make womanish, weaken”), from ex (“out of, from”) + fēmina (“woman”).
