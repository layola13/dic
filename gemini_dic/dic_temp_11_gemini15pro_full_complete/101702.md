irrepealability:/ˌɪrɪˌpiːləˈbɪləti/| n.|不可废止性；不可撤销性|The quality of being impossible to repeal or revoke.|The irrepealability of the law ensured its long-term impact.  这项法律的不可废止性确保了其长期影响。|Synonyms: irreversibility, finality, immutability|Antonyms: repealability, revocability|noun
