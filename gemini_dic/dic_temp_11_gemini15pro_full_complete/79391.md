ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glīdan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *glīdaną (“to glide, slide, slip”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰleydʰ- (“to slip, slide”). Cognate with Dutch glijden (“to glide, slide”), German gleiten (“to glide, slide”), Icelandic glía (“to gleam”). The noun is derived from the verb.
