accessorizes: /əkˈsesəraɪzɪz/
verb
装饰，配饰
adds accessories to something, typically clothing or a room, to enhance its appearance.
She accessorizes her outfits with colorful scarves and jewelry. 她用色彩鲜艳的围巾和珠宝来装饰她的服装。
synonyms: adorn, embellish, enhance, ornament, decorate
antonyms: strip, simplify
词性: 动词 (verb)
