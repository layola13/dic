gliadin:/ˈɡlaɪədɪn/| n.|麦胶蛋白|A protein component of gluten, found in wheat and other cereals. It is responsible for some of the adverse health effects associated with gluten intolerance, such as celiac disease.|例句：Gliadin is one of the main components of gluten that triggers an immune response in individuals with celiac disease. 麦胶蛋白是麸质的主要成分之一，会在乳糜泻患者体内引发免疫反应。

例句：The presence of gliadin in food products can be detected using specific laboratory tests.  食品中麦胶蛋白的存在可以通过特定的实验室测试来检测。|近义词：gluten protein, prolamin|反义词: None|词性:noun
