nonimmigrants:/ˌnɑːnˈɪmɪɡrənts/ |n.|非移民|People who are allowed to enter and stay in a country for a specific purpose and limited period, such as tourists, students, or temporary workers. |The US issues visas to nonimmigrants for various reasons, including tourism and business.| Synonyms: aliens, visitors, foreigners (in this context) | Antonyms: immigrants, citizens, residents | noun
