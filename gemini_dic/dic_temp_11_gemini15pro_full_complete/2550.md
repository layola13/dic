admissibility:/ədˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可采性，可受理性| The admissibility of evidence is a matter for the judge to decide. 证据的可采性由法官决定。|例句：The admissibility of the evidence was questioned by the defense.  辩方质疑证据的可采性。

The lawyer challenged the admissibility of the confession. 律师对供词的可采性提出了质疑。 | 近义词：acceptability, allowability | 反义词：inadmissibility, unacceptability | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Middle English *admissibilite, from Late Latin admissibilitās (“admissibility”), from Latin admissus, past participle of admittō (“I admit, receive, let in”). Equivalent to admissible + -ity.
