hyperpatriotic:/ˌhaɪpərˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
极端爱国的
Excessively or fanatically patriotic. Showing extreme or excessive patriotism.
例句：His hyperpatriotic speeches alienated even some of his supporters. 他的极端爱国主义演讲甚至疏远了他的一些支持者。

例句：The hyperpatriotic fervor in the country led to a rise in xenophobia.  这个国家极端的爱国热情导致了仇外心理的上升。

近义词：ultranationalistic, jingoistic, chauvinistic
反义词：unpatriotic, disloyal
词性: adjective
