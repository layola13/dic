nuttiest:/ˈnʌtiɪst/
adjective
最疯狂的，最古怪的
most crazy or eccentric
例句：That's the nuttiest idea I've ever heard! 那是我听过的最疯狂的想法！
例句：He had the nuttiest collection of old cars. 他收藏了一堆最古怪的老爷车。
近义词：craziest, wackiest, most bizarre
反义词：sanest, most rational
词性: adjective

nutty:/ˈnʌti/
adjective
疯狂的，古怪的;坚果味的
crazy, eccentric; containing or tasting of nuts
例句：He has some nutty ideas about politics. 他对政治有一些古怪的想法。
例句：This cake has a nutty flavor. 这个蛋糕有坚果的味道。
近义词：crazy, eccentric, bizarre, wacky
反义词：sane, rational, normal
词性: adjective

nut:/nʌt/
noun
坚果;螺母,螺帽;疯子
a hard-shelled dry fruit; a metal block with a threaded hole; a crazy person
例句：Almonds and walnuts are my favorite nuts. 杏仁和核桃是我最喜欢的坚果。
例句：I need a nut and bolt to fix this shelf. 我需要一个螺母和螺栓来修理这个架子。
例句：He's a complete nut! 他是个十足的疯子！
近义词：(for crazy person) lunatic, madman, nutcase
反义词：(for nut as food) None. (for crazy person) sane person
词性: noun
