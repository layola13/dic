decrepitly:/dɪˈkrɛpɪtli/
adverb
衰弱地，破旧地
in a decrepit manner; showing the effects of age or wear
例句：The old man walked decrepitly across the street. 老人步履蹒跚地走过街道。

例句：The decrepitly furnished apartment felt cold and unwelcoming.  陈设破旧的公寓让人感到阴冷而不受欢迎。

近义词：frailly, feebly, weakly
反义词：strongly, robustly, vigorously
词性：adverb

词源：From Latin *decrepitus, past participle of decrepare (“to crackle, break down”), from de- (“down, away”) + crepare (“to crack, rattle, creak”).
