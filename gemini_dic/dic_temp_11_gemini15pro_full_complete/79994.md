go-in-for:/ɡəʊ ˈɪn fɔː(r)/ /ɡoʊ ˈɪn fɔːr/
phrasal verb
参加；从事；爱好；赞成
enter a competition, examination, etc.; have as a hobby or interest; like or habitually do.
He went in for the 100-meter race. 他参加了100米赛跑。
She doesn't go in for gossiping. 她不喜欢闲聊。
近义词:participate in, engage in, indulge in, be fond of, support
反义词:avoid, abstain from, dislike, oppose
词性:phrasal verb
