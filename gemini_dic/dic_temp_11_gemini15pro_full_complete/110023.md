libriform:/ˈlɪbrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
纤维状的，似木质纤维的
Resembling a wood fiber; specifically : designating elongated, thick-walled cells in the xylem of woody plants
The libriform fibers provide structural support to the plant. 这些木质纤维为植物提供结构支撑。
The cross section revealed the presence of libriform cells. 横截面显示存在木质纤维细胞。
近义词:fibrous, woody
反义词:non-fibrous, soft
词性:adjective
