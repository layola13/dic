admonitioner:/ˌædməˈnɪʃənər/ | n. | 劝诫者；告诫者 |  A person who admonishes; one who gives a warning or reprimand. | The admonitioner spoke sternly to the children about their misbehavior. 劝诫者严厉地斥责了孩子们的不良行为。 |  Near Synonyms: reprover, rebuker, censor, critic | Near Antonyms: praiser, flatterer | Noun
