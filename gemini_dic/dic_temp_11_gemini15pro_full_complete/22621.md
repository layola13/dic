booziness:/ˈbuːzinəs/| n.|醉酒，酗酒|The state of being boozy; alcoholic quality.|The booziness of the party was evident from the loud music and spilled drinks. 派对上的醉态从大声的音乐和洒出的饮料中显而易见。|近义词：drunkenness, intoxication|反义词：sobriety|词性:名词

Etymology:  From boozy + -ness.  Boozy itself comes from booze, which is of uncertain origin, possibly from Middle Dutch *būsen "to drink heavily".
