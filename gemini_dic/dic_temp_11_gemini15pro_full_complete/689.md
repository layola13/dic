abscond:/əbˈskɒnd/ /æbˈskɑːnd/| v. |潜逃，逃遁；(携款)潜逃|To leave hurriedly and secretly, typically to avoid detection of or arrest for an unlawful action such as theft.|The cashier absconded with the day's receipts.  出纳携当日收款潜逃了。|近义词：flee, escape, vanish, disappear, bolt|反义词:remain, stay, surrender|词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *abscondere*, from *abs-* (away, from) + *condere* (to put together, hide, store).
