calibrators:/ˈkælɪbreɪtərz/
n.
校准器，校准仪，校准员
A device or person used to standardize or adjust something, especially a measuring instrument.
The technician used a set of calibrators to ensure the accuracy of the pressure gauge.  技术员使用一套校准器来确保压力表的精度。
The calibrators were essential for maintaining the precision of the laboratory equipment. 这些校准器对于维持实验室设备的精度至关重要。
synonyms:  standards, gauges, adjusters, regulators
antonyms:  distorters, misaligners
