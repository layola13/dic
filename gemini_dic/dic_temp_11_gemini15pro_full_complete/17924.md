beet:/biːt/ | n. | 甜菜; 深红色 | A round root vegetable with dark red flesh. |  例句：I added some roasted beets to the salad. 我在沙拉里加了一些烤甜菜。

例句：The beet is a good source of vitamins and minerals. 甜菜富含维生素和矿物质。

近义词：beetroot (especially British English) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a vegetable) | 词性: noun


beet:/biːt/ | n. | (指脸的) 深红色, 紫红色 | A deep red or purplish colour of the face. | 例句：His face was beet red with embarrassment. 他的脸因尴尬而涨得通红。

例句：She turned beet red when he complimented her. 当他称赞她时，她脸变得通红。

近义词：crimson, scarlet, flushed | 反义词: pale, white | 词性: noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bēte, from West Germanic *bētō, from Proto-Germanic *baitō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeyt- (“to bite”).  Related to Dutch biet and German Bete. The original meaning referred to the leaves, which were used as a vegetable. The root developed later.
