accost: /əˈkɒst/ (British) /əˈkɔːst/ (American)| v. |搭讪，上前攀谈；质问，责问| To approach and speak to someone, often in a bold or aggressive way. |  The reporter accosted the mayor as he left City Hall.  记者在市长离开市政厅时上前质问他。

The beggar accosted me for money. 乞丐向我乞讨钱。| 近义词：approach, confront, hail, address, challenge | 反义词：avoid, shun, ignore |词性：动词
