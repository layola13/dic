jeopardising:/ˈdʒɛpədaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
危害，危及
putting something at risk
The company's expansion plans are jeopardising its financial stability. 该公司的扩张计划正在危及其财务稳定。
synonyms: endangering, risking, imperilling
antonyms: protecting, safeguarding
verb

jeopardize:/ˈdʒɛpədaɪz/
Verb
危害，危及
putting something at risk
His reckless driving jeopardized the lives of his passengers.  他鲁莽的驾驶危及了乘客的生命。
synonyms: endanger, risk, imperil
antonyms: protect, safeguard
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French jeu parti "divided game," a term from chess meaning a game in which the chances are even; hence, "a risky or dangerous situation."
