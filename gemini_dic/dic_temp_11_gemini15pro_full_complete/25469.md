bulletin:/ˈbʊlətɪn/ (US), /ˈbʊlətɪn/ (UK)| n.|公告；简报；报告；新闻快报|A short official statement or broadcast summary of news.|The company released a bulletin about the merger.|announcement, notice, report, communiqué|dispatch, memo|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *bullettin*, from Middle French *bulletin*, diminutive of *bulle* (“papal bull, official document”), from Latin *bulla* (“bubble, knob, seal”). Doublet of *bull*.
