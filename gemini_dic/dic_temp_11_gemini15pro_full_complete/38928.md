confestly:/kənˈfɛstli/ | adv. |坦白地，公开地 | Openly and frankly; avowedly. | He confessedly had little knowledge of the subject. 他坦白地承认自己对这个主题知之甚少。|  He confessedly broke the rules. 他公开承认违反了规则。 | 近义词: frankly, openly, avowedly, admittedly | 反义词: secretly, covertly, privately | 词性: 副词

词源:  From confess + -ly.  "Confess" comes from Old French confesser, from Latin confessus, past participle of confiteri "to confess," from com- "together" + fateri "to acknowledge."
