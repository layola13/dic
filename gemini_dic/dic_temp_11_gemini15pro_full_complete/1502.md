acetifier:/əˈsɛtɪfaɪər/
noun
醋化器，酸化器
A device or apparatus used to acetify liquids, especially for turning wine into vinegar.
This acetifier helps to speed up the vinegar-making process.  这个醋化器有助于加快制醋过程。
The traditional acetifier used wood shavings to harbor the bacteria.  传统的醋化器使用木屑来 harbor 细菌。
synonyms: vinegar generator, acidifier
antonyms: neutralizer, alkalizer
