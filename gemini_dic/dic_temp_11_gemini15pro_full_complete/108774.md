leaseholds:/ˈliːshəʊldz/ (UK), /ˈliːshoʊldz/ (US)
n.
租赁产权;租赁权
A leasehold is the ownership of a temporary right to hold land or property, especially in return for rent.
The company sold its leaseholds and moved out.  这家公司卖掉了它的租赁产权，搬了出去。

The leaseholds were renewed for another 99 years. 这些租赁产权又续了99年。
近义词:tenancy, lease
反义词:freehold
