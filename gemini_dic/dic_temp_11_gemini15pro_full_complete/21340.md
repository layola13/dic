blooming:/ˈbluːmɪŋ/| adj.|盛开的；繁荣的；非常的（用于加强语气）| flourishing, thriving; used for emphasis, often expressing annoyance|The garden is full of blooming flowers. 花园里开满了鲜花。| synonyms: flourishing, thriving, blossoming, in full bloom | antonyms: withered, dying, fading, declining | adjective

blooming:/ˈbluːmɪŋ/| n.|开花；盛开|the state of having flowers open; the time when this happens|The blooming of the cherry blossoms is a beautiful sight.  樱花盛开是一道美丽的风景。| synonyms: flowering, blossoming | antonyms: withering, fading | noun
