flocks:/flɑːks/ | n. | 群,羊群,鸟群,人群,棉束,毛束 |A number of birds or animals of one kind that keep or feed together or are herded together. A large number of people or things. A lock of wool or hair. |  The shepherd led his flocks to the pasture. 牧羊人把他的羊群带到牧场。| synonyms: herds, swarms, crowds, throngs, masses | antonyms: individual, single, lone | noun

flocks:/flɑːks/ | v. | 聚集,成群结队,涌向 | To gather or move in a flock or crowd. | People flocked to the beach on the hot summer day. 炎热的夏日，人们涌向海滩。| synonyms: gather, congregate, assemble, converge | antonyms: disperse, scatter, separate | verb
