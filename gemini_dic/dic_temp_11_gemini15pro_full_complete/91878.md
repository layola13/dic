hyperintelligence:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/| n.|超智能|Extreme intelligence far surpassing that of any human or even the smartest existing AI. It often connotes an intellect capable of solving problems currently beyond human comprehension.|The concept of hyperintelligence is explored in science fiction, often depicting entities capable of manipulating reality itself.|近义词：superintelligence, ultra-intelligence|反义词: stupidity, unintelligence|词性: noun

Etymology:
From hyper- + intelligence.  "Hyper-" is a prefix derived from Greek meaning "over, beyond, excessive,".  "Intelligence" comes from Latin "intelligentia" meaning "understanding, ability to learn".
