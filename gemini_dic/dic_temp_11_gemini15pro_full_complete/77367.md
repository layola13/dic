gauge:/ɡeɪdʒ/| n. v.|测量仪器，规格，标准，估计，判断|A measuring instrument or a standard of measurement. As a verb, it means to measure or estimate something, or to assess or judge something.|例句：Use a rain gauge to measure rainfall.  使用雨量计来测量降雨量。

例句：He tried to gauge her reaction to the news. 他试图揣摩她对这个消息的反应。|近义词：measure, assess, estimate, judge, appraise, evaluate|反义词:misjudge, miscalculate|词性:noun, verb
