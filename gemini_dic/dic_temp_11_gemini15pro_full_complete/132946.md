nonemergent:/ˌnɑːnɪˈmɜːrdʒənt/ | adj. | 非紧急的，非急诊的 | Not requiring immediate or urgent attention; not an emergency. |  The doctor determined that the patient's condition was nonemergent and could wait until morning. 医生判断病人的情况并非紧急，可以等到早上。

The nonemergent nature of the call allowed the dispatcher to prioritize other, more urgent requests.  这个电话的非紧急性质使得调度员可以优先处理其他更紧急的请求。 | 近义词: routine, non-urgent, elective | 反义词: emergent, urgent, critical | 词性: adjective


词源:

From non- + emergent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "emergent" comes from the Latin "emergere," meaning "to rise out," hence referring to something that suddenly appears and requires immediate attention.
