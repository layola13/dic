ascertain:/ˌæsərˈteɪn/ | v. | 查明，确定，弄清 | To find something out for certain; make sure of. |  The police are trying to ascertain the facts of the case. 警方正试图查明案件真相。

The doctor ascertained that the patient had a broken leg. 医生确定病人腿断了。

| 近义词：determine, establish, verify, confirm | 反义词：disprove, refute, invalidate | 词性: 动词
