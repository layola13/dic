distanced:/ˈdɪstənst/ | adj. |疏远的，有距离的| Emotionally or physically separated or removed. |  They remained distanced from each other after the argument. 争吵之后，他们彼此保持距离。|近义词：remote, aloof, detached, estranged|反义词：close, intimate, connected|词性:形容词
distanced:/ˈdɪstənst/ | v. |拉开距离，使疏远 (past tense of distance) | To create emotional or physical separation. | He distanced himself from the toxic relationship. 他让自己远离了那段有害的关系。|近义词：separate, withdraw, detach|反义词：approach, engage, connect|词性:动词
