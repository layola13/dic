gemmule:/ˈdʒɛmjuːl/| n.|微芽，芽球，胞芽|A small bud or gemma; an aggregation of cells set apart in many invertebrates to serve in asexual reproduction; a hypothetical particle formerly believed to carry genetic information.|The sponge releases gemmules that can survive harsh conditions and later develop into new sponges. 海绵释放出能够在恶劣条件下生存并随后发育成新海绵的芽球。|Synonyms: bud, gemma, propagule|Antonyms: N/A|noun

gemmule:/ˈdʒɛmjuːl/| n.|（泛指）微小的颗粒，微粒|A minute granule, particle, or bud.|In pangenesis theory, gemmules were thought to be the carriers of hereditary traits. 在泛生论中，微芽被认为是遗传性状的载体。|Synonyms: granule, particle, speck|Antonyms: N/A|noun
