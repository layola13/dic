outloose: /aʊtˈluːs/
adjective
自由自在的，不受约束的
free from restraint or control.
The dogs were turned outloose in the park. 狗被放出来在公园里自由奔跑。
近义词: free, unrestrained, liberated, unfettered
反义词: restrained, confined, restricted
词性: adjective

outloose: /aʊtˈluːs/
verb
释放，放开
to set free or release.
They outloosed the prisoners. 他们释放了囚犯。
近义词: release, liberate, free, unleash
反义词: confine, restrain, imprison
词性: verb

词源:  "Out-" (meaning "out, forth, completely") + "loose" (meaning "not tight or fixed").  The combination intensifies the sense of "loose" to mean completely free or unrestrained.
