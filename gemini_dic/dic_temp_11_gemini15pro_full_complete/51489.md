detracting:/dɪˈtræktɪŋ/
adjective
贬低的，减损的
reducing the value or quality of something
The constant noise was detracting from my enjoyment of the concert.  持续不断的噪音降低了我对音乐会的享受。
近义词: diminishing, lessening, belittling
反义词: enhancing, improving, augmenting
词性: adjective

detract:/dɪˈtrækt/
verb
减损，贬低
to make something seem less valuable or less good
These small faults, however, do not detract from the overall quality of the book. 然而，这些小缺点并没有降低这本书的整体质量。
近义词: diminish, lessen, belittle
反义词: enhance, improve, augment
词性: verb


词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin *detract- ‘drawn down’, from the verb detrahere, from de- ‘down’ + trahere ‘to draw’.
