magniloquent:/mæɡˈnɪləkwənt/| adj.|夸夸其谈的，辞藻华丽的|Speaking or expressed in a lofty or pompous style; using high-flown language.|例句：The mayor delivered a magniloquent speech about the city's bright future. 市长就该市光明的前景发表了慷慨激昂的演讲。

例句：His magniloquent style of writing often obscured the meaning of his work. 他华丽的写作风格常常掩盖了他作品的意义。|近义词：bombastic, grandiloquent, pompous, lofty, orotund, eloquent|反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious, modest, humble|词性:形容词
