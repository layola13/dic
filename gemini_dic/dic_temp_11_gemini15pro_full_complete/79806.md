glycocin:/ˌɡlaɪkoʊˈsaɪsɪn/
noun|糖精，一种蛋白质|A bacteriocin produced by a strain of Streptococcus milleri active against Streptococcus mutans. It is being investigated as a potential agent for preventing dental caries.|Glycocin was shown to inhibit the growth of Streptococcus mutans. 糖精被证明可以抑制变形链球菌的生长。|Synonyms: bacteriocin, antimicrobial peptide|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific bacteriocin)|n.
