irrevocable:/ɪˈrɛvəkəbl/ | adj. | 不可撤销的；不能取消的 | Not able to be changed, reversed, or recovered. |  The judge's decision is irrevocable. 法官的判决是不可撤销的。

His words had an irrevocable effect on her life. 他的话对她的人生产生了不可磨灭的影响。


近义词: unalterable, irreversible, final, permanent | 反义词: revocable, reversible, changeable | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin irrevocabilis, from in- ‘not’ + revocabilis (from revocare ‘recall’).
