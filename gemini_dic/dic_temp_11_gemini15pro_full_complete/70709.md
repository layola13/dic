financier:/fɪˈnænsiər/ /faɪˈnænsiər/
n.|金融家；财政家|A person who deals with large sums of money, especially in investments or banking.|例句：The financier was known for his shrewd investments. 这位金融家以其精明的投资而闻名。|例句：Several financiers were involved in the deal.  几位金融家参与了这笔交易。|近义词：investor, banker, capitalist|反义词: debtor, borrower|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *financier*, from *finance* "finance," ultimately from Latin *finis* "end, settlement, payment."
