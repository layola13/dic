half:/hæf/ | n. adj. adv. | 一半，半个 | One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole. | I ate half the pizza. 我吃了一半披萨。 | Cut the apple in half. 把苹果切成两半。 | 近义词: moiety, one-half, 50% | 反义词: whole, double | 词性: noun, adjective, adverb

accustomed:/əˈkʌstəmd/ | adj. | 习惯的，通常的 | Customary; usual. |  He is accustomed to getting up early. 他习惯早起。 |  She gave him her accustomed smile. 她给了他她惯常的微笑。| 近义词: used to, familiar with, habituated to | 反义词: unaccustomed, unfamiliar with | 词性: adjective
