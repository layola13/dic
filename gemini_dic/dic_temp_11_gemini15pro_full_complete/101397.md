what:/wɒt/ (UK) /hwʌt/ (US)| pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么，多么 | Used to emphasize the degree or amount of something. | What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！ | such, so | - | determiner


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she study at university? 她在大学里学什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV right now? 你现在在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? 他今晚在做什么晚餐？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? 她今年完成了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? 她对你说了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when you saw her? 你看到她的时候她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for so long? 他们一直在讨论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting next month? 他们下个月的会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by next week? 到下周他们将完成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to do, I would tell you. 如果我知道该怎么做，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. 我希望我知道她在想什么。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What time is the meeting? 会议是什么时间？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. 我不知道该说什么。
She didn't tell me what she wanted. 她没有告诉我她想要什么。



PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/你好吗？
What the hell! 搞什么鬼！
What if we go to the beach? 如果我们去海滩怎么样？
What for? 为了什么？
No matter what happens, I'll be there for you. 无论发生什么，我都会在你身边。


词源：From Old English _hwæt_, neuter of _hwā_ "who," which also functioned as an interrogative and relative pronoun meaning "what," from Proto-Germanic *_hwat_*, from PIE *_kwod_*.  Related to who, which.
