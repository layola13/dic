entrepreneurially:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːriəli/
adverb
企业家式地；富有创业精神地
In an entrepreneurial way; with the spirit and characteristics of an entrepreneur.
She approached the problem entrepreneurially, seeking innovative solutions and taking calculated risks. 她以企业家的方式处理这个问题，寻求创新的解决方案并承担可计算的风险。
He managed his finances entrepreneurially, always looking for new opportunities to invest and grow his wealth. 他以企业家的方式管理自己的财务，总是寻找新的投资机会来增加财富。
近义词: innovatively, proactively, creatively, resourcefully
反义词: conservatively, cautiously, passively
词性: adverb
词源: From entrepreneur + -ial + -ly. Ultimately from the French entreprendre "to undertake".
