overrealisms: /ˌoʊvərˈriːəlɪzəmz/
noun
超现实主义（复数）
The philosophical or artistic representation of an often-disturbing reality in which the subjective consciousness plays a crucial role.  It emphasizes the imagination and dreams as a more real or truer understanding of the world than the perceived objective reality.
The artist's overrealisms were unsettling yet captivating.  这位艺术家的超现实主义作品令人不安却又引人入胜。
Synonyms: surrealisms, hyperrealisms
Antonyms: realisms, naturalisms
