libelled:/ˈlaɪbəld/ (US) /ˈlaɪbəld/ (UK)| v. |诽谤，中伤|To publish or broadcast false statements that damage someone's reputation.|The newspaper libelled the politician by claiming he had accepted bribes. 这家报纸声称这位政治家收受贿赂，对他进行了诽谤。|Near Synonyms: defamed, slandered, maligned, calumniated|Antonyms: praised, lauded, commended|Verb

libelled:/ˈlaɪbəld/ (US) /ˈlaɪbəld/ (UK)| adj. |被诽谤的|Having been the subject of a libel.|The libelled businessman sued the newspaper for damages.  这位被诽谤的商人起诉了这家报纸，要求赔偿损失。|Near Synonyms: defamed, slandered|Antonyms: praised, lauded|Adjective
