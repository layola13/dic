centipede:/ˈsɛntɪpiːd/| n. |蜈蚣|A predatory myriapod invertebrate with numerous body segments, each bearing a single pair of legs.  Centipedes are known for their venomous bites.|例句：I saw a centipede crawling across the bathroom floor. 我看到一只蜈蚣爬过浴室地板。

例句：The centipede's venom can be painful, but it's rarely fatal to humans. 蜈蚣的毒液会很痛，但很少对人类致命。|近义词：myriapod, chilopod|反义词: None (no direct opposite for an animal)|词性:noun
