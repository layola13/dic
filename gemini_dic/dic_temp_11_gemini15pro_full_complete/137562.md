nontransitives:/ˌnɒnˈtrænzɪtɪvz/ | n. | 非及物动词 | Verbs that do not take a direct object.  They describe actions that do not affect or act upon someone or something else. | Example: The baby slept soundly.  婴儿睡得很香。| Example: The sun rises in the east. 太阳从东方升起。 | Synonyms: intransitive verbs | Antonyms: transitive verbs | Noun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The bird sings beautifully.  这只鸟歌唱得很优美。
He walks to work every day. 他每天步行上班。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The children are playing in the park. 孩子们正在公园里玩耍。
She is sleeping right now. 她现在正在睡觉。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The flowers have bloomed. 花已经开了。
He has traveled all over the world. 他已经周游了世界。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have been working on this project for months. 他们已经在这个项目上工作了好几个月了。
She has been studying English for years. 她已经学习英语很多年了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The dog barked loudly.  狗大声地叫。
The plane landed safely. 飞机安全着陆。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The rain was falling heavily. 雨下得很大。
He was sleeping when I called. 我打电话时他正在睡觉。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The snow had stopped before we left. 我们离开之前雪已经停了。
The movie had started by the time we arrived. 我们到达时电影已经开始了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been hiking for hours before they reached the summit. 他们在到达山顶之前已经徒步旅行了好几个小时。
She had been practicing the piano for weeks before the concert. 她在音乐会前已经练习钢琴好几个星期了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The sun will rise tomorrow. 明天太阳会升起。
The train will arrive at 6 pm. 火车将在下午6点到达。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be sleeping at this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候我会在睡觉。
They will be traveling to Europe next summer. 明年夏天他们将去欧洲旅行。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have finished the book. 到下周，我将完成这本书。
By the time you arrive, I will have cooked dinner. 等你到达时，我将已经做好晚餐。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been living here for five years. 到明年，我将已经在这里住了五年了。
By the time she graduates, she will have been studying medicine for six years. 到她毕业时，她将已经学了六年的医学。

虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would fly. 如果我是一只鸟，我会飞。
I wish I could speak French fluently. 我希望我能流利地说法语。

被动语态 (不适用):  非及物动词没有被动语态。

疑问句:
Did he arrive on time? 他准时到达了吗？
Is she sleeping? 她正在睡觉吗？

否定句:
The bird doesn't sing at night. 这只鸟晚上不唱歌。
He didn't sleep well last night. 他昨晚没睡好。
