hyperflexibility:/ˌhaɪpərˌflɛksɪˈbɪlɪti/| n. |过度柔韧性；超柔韧性|The ability of a joint to move beyond its normal range of motion. This can be a normal variation or a sign of an underlying medical condition.|She has hyperflexibility in her shoulders, allowing her to perform impressive contortionist tricks. 她肩膀过度柔韧，可以表演令人印象深刻的柔术技巧。|近义词：laxity, looseness, suppleness|反义词：stiffness, rigidity|noun
