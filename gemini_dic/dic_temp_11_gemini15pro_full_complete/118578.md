mesostate:/ˈmesəʊˌsteɪt/ | n. | 中间态，介稳态 | An intermediate state between two other more stable states, often used in chemistry and physics.  A mesostate is a transient, relatively long-lived intermediate in a chemical reaction. | The reaction proceeds through a series of mesostates before reaching the final product.  这个反应在达到最终产物之前会经历一系列中间态。 | intermediate, transition state | stable state | noun
