brogues: /broʊɡz/ (US) /brəʊɡz/ (UK)
n.
布洛克鞋，粗革皮鞋
A sturdy leather shoe, typically with decorative perforations and serrations along the seams.
He wore a pair of brown brogues to the wedding.  他穿着一双棕色布洛克鞋去参加婚礼。
The brogues were scuffed from his long walk.  他的布洛克鞋因为走了很长的路而磨损了。
近义词:wingtips, oxfords
反义词:sneakers, slippers
词性:名词


brogue: /broʊɡ/ (US) /brəʊɡ/ (UK)
n.
口音，方言
A strong regional accent, particularly associated with Ireland or Scotland.
His Irish brogue was charming. 他的爱尔兰口音很迷人。
She could easily identify the Scottish brogue. 她可以很容易地辨认出苏格兰口音。
近义词:accent, dialect
反义词:standard pronunciation
词性:名词


词源:  From Irish *bróg, from Old Irish brócc (“shoe”). Related to Gaelic bròg (“shoe”), Welsh brog (“shoe”), and perhaps to Old Norse brók (“trousers”).
