filmy:/ˈfɪlmi/| adj.|薄膜的；朦胧的；模糊不清的|Covered with or resembling a thin film; thin and translucent; vague or indistinct.|例句：The old photographs had a filmy quality. 这些老照片有一种朦胧感。

例句：Her eyes were filmy with tears. 她的眼里噙满了泪水。|近义词：gauzy, sheer, diaphanous, translucent, hazy, misty, blurry, vague, indistinct|反义词：thick, opaque, clear, distinct, sharp|词性:形容词
