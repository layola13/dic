nonstringent:/ˌnɒnˈstrɪndʒənt/ | adj. | 不严格的，不苛刻的 | Not strict or severe; lenient.  | The rules for this competition are nonstringent, allowing for a wide range of entries.  这项比赛的规则不严格，允许各种各样的参赛作品。 | Example: The laboratory used nonstringent sterilization procedures.  实验室采用了不严格的灭菌程序。| Synonyms: lenient, lax, flexible, tolerant, permissive | Antonyms: stringent, strict, rigorous, demanding, exacting | adjective
