need-blind:/ˌniːdˈblaɪnd/ | adj. |  不考虑经济需求的；助学金需求无关的 |  Referring to a college or university admissions policy where an applicant's financial need is not considered when deciding whether to admit them. |  Harvard has a need-blind admissions policy.  哈佛大学的招生政策是不考虑经济需求的。 |  Example: The university's need-blind admission policy ensures that all applicants have an equal opportunity, regardless of their financial background. 这所大学的助学金需求无关的招生政策确保所有申请人都有平等的机会，无论其经济背景如何。| Synonyms:  need-unaware | Antonyms: need-aware, need-based | adjective

need-blind:/ˌniːdˈblaɪnd/ | adj. |  对经济需求视而不见的；无视经济需求的 |  Ignoring or unaware of someone's financial need.  |  He seemed need-blind to her struggles as a single mother. 他似乎对她作为单亲妈妈的困境视而不见。 | Example: His need-blind approach to charity work meant he often donated to causes that didn't truly benefit those in need. 他对慈善工作无视经济需求的做法意味着他经常向那些并不能真正帮助有需要的人的机构捐款。| Synonyms: inconsiderate, oblivious | Antonyms:  considerate, aware | adjective
