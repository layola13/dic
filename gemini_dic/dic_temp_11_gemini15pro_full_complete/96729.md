inefficiently:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃəntli/ | adv. | 无效率地，效率低地 | Not achieving maximum productivity; wasting or failing to make the best use of time or resources. | He worked inefficiently, taking twice as long as necessary. 他工作效率低下，花了比必要时间多一倍的时间。| The company was run inefficiently, leading to significant losses.  这家公司经营效率低下，导致了重大损失。 | inefficiently, unproductively, wastefully | efficiently, effectively, productively | adverb
