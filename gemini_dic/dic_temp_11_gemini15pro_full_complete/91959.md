hypermodest:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɒdɪst/ (US) /ˌhaɪpərˈmɑːdɪst/| adj. | 过分谦虚的，极度谦逊的 | Excessively modest; overly humble.  Characterized by an exaggerated sense of humility, often to the point of downplaying one's accomplishments or abilities. |  He gave a hypermodest account of his role in the project, downplaying his significant contributions. 他对自己在项目中的作用做了过分谦虚的描述，淡化了他重要的贡献。

He was hypermodest about his achievements, attributing his success to luck. 他对自己的成就非常谦虚，把成功归因于运气。

| 近义词: excessively modest, overly humble, self-deprecating, unassuming | 反义词: boastful, arrogant, conceited, immodest | 词性: adjective
