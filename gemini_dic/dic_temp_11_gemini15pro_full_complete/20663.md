blacksnake:/ˈblækˌsneɪk/ | n. |黑蛇，黑游蛇|A nonvenomous snake, typically black in color, found in North America.  Specifically, it often refers to the black rat snake (Pantherophis obsoletus).|例句：A blacksnake slithered across the path. 一条黑蛇蜿蜒穿过小路。

例句：The farmer found a blacksnake in his barn.  农民在谷仓里发现了一条黑蛇。|近义词：rat snake, pilot snake (specifically for black rat snakes)|反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for an animal name)|词性:noun
