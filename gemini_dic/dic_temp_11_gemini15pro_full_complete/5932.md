ambivalent:/æmˈbɪvələnt/ | adj. |矛盾的， ambivalent |Having mixed feelings or contradictory ideas about something or someone. | She was ambivalent about accepting the job offer. 她对接受这份工作犹豫不决。| I felt ambivalent about leaving home; excited but also sad. 我对离家感到矛盾；既兴奋又悲伤。 |近义词：uncertain, unsure, doubtful, hesitant, indecisive |反义词：certain, sure, decided, resolute, unequivocal |词性: 形容词

ambivalence:/æmˈbɪvələns/ | n. |矛盾心理，犹豫不决 |The state of having mixed feelings or contradictory ideas about something or someone. |  His ambivalence towards the project was evident. 他对这个项目的矛盾心理显而易见。 | She expressed ambivalence about her future career path. 她对未来的职业道路表达了犹豫不决的态度。 | 近义词：uncertainty, doubt, hesitation, indecision | 反义词：certainty, decisiveness, resolution |词性: 名词
