boxings:/ˈbɒksɪŋz/
n.
拳击比赛; 拳击运动
The act or sport of fighting with the fists, especially according to prescribed rules.
The boxing match was intense and exciting. 这场拳击比赛紧张刺激。
近义词：fights, matches
反义词：peace, harmony
词性：名词

Etymology: From the verb "box," from Old English "boxian" (to strike), of Germanic origin.
