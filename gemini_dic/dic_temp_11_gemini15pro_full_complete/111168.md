lithocyst: /ˈlɪθəsɪst/
noun
石细胞
A specialized cell, often found in the flesh of pears and other fruits, that has a thick, hard cell wall containing lignin.
Pears are gritty because they contain many lithocysts.  梨子吃起来沙沙的，因为它们含有许多石细胞。
sclereid, stone cell
