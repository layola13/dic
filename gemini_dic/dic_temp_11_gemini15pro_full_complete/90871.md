hunker down:/ˈhʌŋkər daʊn/
verb
蹲下；躲避；准备长期抵抗
To settle in for a period of confinement, inactivity, or siege.  To crouch down or take shelter.
例句：
The family hunkered down in the basement during the tornado.  
家人在龙卷风期间躲在地下室里。

They hunkered down for a long winter.
他们做好准备过一个漫长的冬天。


近义词：settle in, dig in, hole up, hunker, shelter, take cover
反义词：emerge, go out, venture out
词性：verb
