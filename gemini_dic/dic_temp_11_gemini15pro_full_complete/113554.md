macaque:/məˈkɑːk/ | n. |猕猴|A type of monkey found in various parts of Asia and North Africa, typically with a short tail and cheek pouches.|例句：The macaque snatched the banana from the tourist. 猕猴从游客手中抢走了香蕉。

例句：Researchers study macaques to learn more about primate behavior. 研究人员研究猕猴以了解更多关于灵长类动物行为的信息。|近义词：monkey, primate|反义词: N/A |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *macaque*, from Portuguese *macaco* "monkey,"  ultimately of Bantu origin (cf. Kongo *makaku*, Kimbundu *makaku*).
