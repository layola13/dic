frigid:/ˈfrɪdʒɪd/| adj.|寒冷的；冷淡的；性冷感的|Extremely cold; lacking warmth or enthusiasm; sexually unresponsive.|例句：The frigid air made my fingers numb. 寒冷的空气使我的手指麻木了。|近义词：cold, icy, freezing, frosty, chilly, glacial, arctic, wintry, aloof, distant, unresponsive, passionless|反义词：warm, hot, passionate, ardent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *frigidus*, from *frigere* ("to be cold").
