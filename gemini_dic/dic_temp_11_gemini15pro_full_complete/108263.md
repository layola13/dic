launder:/ˈlɔːndər/ (US), /ˈlɔːndə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|洗涤；清洗；洗钱|To wash and iron clothes or linens; to conceal the source of illegally obtained money. |例句：She laundered the sheets and towels. 她洗了床单和毛巾。


例句：They were accused of laundering drug money. 他们被指控洗钱。|近义词：wash, clean, cleanse, rinse (for washing); conceal, disguise (for illegal money)|反义词：soil, dirty (for washing); expose, reveal (for illegal money)|词性：动词, 名词


launder:/ˈlɔːndər/ (US), /ˈlɔːndə(r)/ (UK)| n.|洗涤；洗涤物|The act of laundering or the clothes or linens that have been laundered. |例句：The hotel offers a laundry service. 这家酒店提供洗衣服务。


例句：A pile of dirty laundry lay on the floor. 一堆脏衣服在地板上。|近义词：washing, laundry load|反义词：N/A|词性：名词


词源: From Old French "lavandrie" meaning "wash-house," derived from "laver" meaning "to wash," ultimately from Latin "lavāre" meaning "to wash."
