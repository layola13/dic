cabazite:/ˈkæbəˌzaɪt/ | n. |菱沸石|A mineral of the zeolite group, a hydrated calcium aluminosilicate occurring in white, pink, or yellow rhombohedral crystals.|Cabazite is often found in volcanic rocks. 菱沸石常在火山岩中发现。|chabazite|None|Noun
