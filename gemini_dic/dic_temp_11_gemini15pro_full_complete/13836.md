auditory:/ˈɔːdɪtɔːri/ (US) /ˈɔːdɪtri/ (UK)| adj.|听觉的；耳朵的|Relating to the sense of hearing or the organs of hearing.|例句：The auditory nerve connects the inner ear to the brain.  听神经连接内耳和大脑。

例句：Auditory processing disorder can make it difficult to understand speech.  听觉处理障碍会使理解言语变得困难。|近义词：aural, acoustic|反义词:visual|词性:adjective
