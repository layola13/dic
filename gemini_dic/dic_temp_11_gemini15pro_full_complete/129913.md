non-judgemental:/ˌnɒnˈdʒʌdʒˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 无批判的；不带评判的；不加评判的 | Not judging or criticizing people |  She tried to create a non-judgemental atmosphere where everyone felt comfortable sharing their opinions. 她试图营造一个不带评判的氛围，让每个人都能自在地分享他们的观点。|  She listened with a non-judgemental ear. 她带着不加评判的态度倾听。 | accepting, tolerant, open-minded, understanding | judgemental, critical, censorious | adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not"
judgemental (adjective): from judgement (noun) + -al (suffix). Judgement from Old French jugement.
