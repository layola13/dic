igloo:/ˈɪɡluː/| n.|冰屋，雪屋|A dome-shaped Inuit house made of blocks of packed snow.|The Inuit people build igloos for shelter during the winter. 因纽特人在冬天建造冰屋作为住所。|近义词：snow house, snow hut|反义词:house, building|名词
