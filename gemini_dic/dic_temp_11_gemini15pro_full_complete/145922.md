overdilutes:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈluːts/
verb
过度稀释
To dilute something excessively, beyond what is desirable or effective.
The company overdiluted its brand by licensing it to too many low-quality products.  公司通过将其品牌授权给太多低质量产品而过度稀释了其品牌价值。
