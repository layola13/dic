bring justice:/brɪŋ ˈdʒʌstɪs/ |v. |伸张正义，主持公道|To cause justice to be done; to ensure that someone is punished for a crime or wrongdoing, or that a victim receives due compensation or recognition.|例句：The police are determined to bring justice to the victims of the crime. 警方决心为犯罪受害者伸张正义。

例句：They fought to bring justice to those wrongly accused. 他们为那些被冤枉的人伸张正义。|近义词：enforce justice, administer justice, right a wrong, avenge, redress |反义词: perpetuate injustice, obstruct justice, cover up |词性:动词
