counterdistinction:/ˌkaʊntər dɪˈstɪŋkʃən/
|n.|对比; 对照; 相对的区分|A distinction made by contrasting one thing with another.|The argument is made by counterdistinction to the opposing view.  这个论点是通过与相反的观点进行对比而提出的。|
| |The leader emphasized the importance of teamwork, in counterdistinction to individual effort. 领导强调了团队合作的重要性，与个人努力形成对比。 |
| |contrast, juxtaposition| |noun|
