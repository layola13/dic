castrates:/ˈkæˌstreɪts/| v.|阉割；删除；使无力|Removes the testicles of (a male animal or person); deprives of power or vigor.|例句：The farmer castrates the bull calves. 农民阉割了公牛犊。

例句：The government's policies have castrated the power of the unions. 政府的政策削弱了工会的力量。|近义词：geld, neuter, emasculate|反义词: empower, strengthen|词性:动词

castrated:/ˈkæˌstreɪtɪd/| adj.|被阉割的；被删除的；无力的|Having been castrated; deprived of power or vigor.|例句：The castrated bull was docile. 被阉割的公牛很温顺。

例句：The censored version of the film felt castrated and incomplete. 电影的审查版本感觉被阉割了，不完整。|近义词：gelded, neutered, emasculated|反义词: empowered, strengthened|词性:形容词


词源: From Latin castrāre, frequentative of castrāre (“to prune, to castrate”), from caster (“camp”). The original meaning referred to the Roman practice of punishing soldiers by castration.
