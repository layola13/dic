granulite:/ˈɡrænjuːlaɪt/| n. |麻粒岩|A metamorphic rock formed at high temperature and pressure, typically consisting of granular minerals such as feldspar, quartz, and garnet.|The geologist identified the sample as a granulite. 地质学家将样品鉴定为麻粒岩。|Example: The ancient mountain range was composed primarily of granulite.  这座古老的山脉主要由麻粒岩构成。|Synonyms: gneiss (sometimes), high-grade metamorphic rock|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could contrast with low-grade metamorphic rocks like slate or phyllite)|noun
