mingling:/ˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
n.|混合，交际，交往|The party was a great opportunity for mingling with new people.|这个聚会是与新朋友交流的好机会。|
mingling:/ˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
v.|（使）混合，（使）相交往|He mingled with the crowd at the reception.|他在招待会上与人群混在一起。|
mingling:/ˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
adj.|混合的，交际的|a mingling of different cultures|不同文化的融合|


Etymology->
From Middle English *minglen, menglen, from Old English mengan (“to mix, mingle, stir”), from Proto-Germanic *mangōną (“to mix, knead”), from Proto-Indo-European *meyg- (“to mix, knead”). Cognate with West Frisian mingelje (“to mix, mingle”), Dutch mengen (“to mix”), German mengen (“to mix”), Swedish mänga (“to mix”).
