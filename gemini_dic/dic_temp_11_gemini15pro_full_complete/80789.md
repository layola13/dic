gotcha:/ˈɡɒtʃə/ (US) /ˈɡɒtʃə/ (UK)|n.|陷阱，圈套；明白了|An unexpected or hidden problem or difficulty, often designed to trick or catch someone out; an informal way of saying "I understand" or "I've got you".|The contract was full of gotchas that cost us a fortune.  合同里充满了陷阱，让我们损失了一大笔钱。|synonyms: trap, pitfall, snare, catch, trick, I understand, I get it|antonyms: straightforwardness, honesty, clarity|noun

gotcha:/ˈɡɒtʃə/ (US) /ˈɡɒtʃə/ (UK)|interj.|明白了|Informal way of saying "I understand" or "I've got you".| "Got it?" "Gotcha." “明白了？” “明白了。”|synonyms: I understand, I get it, OK, alright|antonyms: I don't understand|interjection
