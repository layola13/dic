demurest:/dɪˈmjʊərist/| adj.|最端庄的，最娴静的|reserved, modest, and shy (especially of a woman)|She gave him a demurest smile. 她给了他一个最娴静的微笑。|近义词：modest, shy, reserved, bashful, coy|反义词：bold, immodest, brazen, forward|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle French *demourer* "to stay, remain, linger," from Latin *demorari* "to delay, linger." The original sense was "sober, grave," later evolving to "modest, reserved."  "Demure" is the base adjective, and "demurest" is the superlative form, meaning "most demure."
