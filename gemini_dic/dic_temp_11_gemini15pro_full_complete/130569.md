nonactivations:/ˌnɑːnˌæktɪˈveɪʃənz/ | n. | 非激活，未激活 | Absence of activations; instances where something is not activated or switched on.  This often refers to neurons or genes that are not expressing themselves. | The study measured neuronal nonactivations during the control task.  这项研究测量了对照任务中神经元的非激活状态。

The experiment showed significant nonactivations of certain genes in the presence of the inhibitor.  实验表明，在抑制剂存在的情况下，某些基因的非激活状态显著。 | 近义词:inactivations, deactivations | 反义词: activations | 词性: 名词
