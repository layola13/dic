outgnaw:/aʊtˈnɔː/
verb
咬穿，啃穿，超过...的啃咬能力
To gnaw through from the outside; to surpass in gnawing.
The mice outgnawed the other animals in the competition to see who could chew through the thickest piece of wood. 老鼠在啃咬最厚的木头的比赛中，咬穿能力超过了其他动物。
The persistent dripping water eventually outgnawed a hole in the stone. 不断滴落的水最终在石头上啃出了一个洞。
Synonyms: erode, corrode, wear away
Antonyms: build, construct, strengthen
词性: 动词
