attorney-general:/əˈtɜːrni ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəl/
n.
首席检察官；司法部长
The chief law officer of a country or state.
The Attorney General announced a new initiative to combat cybercrime. 司法部长宣布了一项打击网络犯罪的新举措。

The attorney general addressed the nation on the issue. 首席检察官就这个问题向全国发表了讲话。

近义词:chief law officer, legal advisor
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps defendant in some contexts)
词性: Noun

首席检察官们；司法部长们

The attorneys general released a joint statement. 首席检察官们发表了联合声明。
