concinnous:/kənˈsɪnəs/| adj.|和谐的，优美的，精巧的|Harmonious, elegant, and skillfully put together; characterized by neatness and precision.  It often implies a pleasing arrangement or organization of parts, suggesting both beauty and skillful execution.|Her concinnous prose was a delight to read. 她的优美散文令人赏心悦目。|近义词：harmonious, elegant, well-proportioned, graceful, neat, precise|反义词：disorderly, clumsy, awkward, inelegant, ungainly|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *concinnus* "well-put-together," from *com-* "together" + *cinnus* "mixed, mingled."
