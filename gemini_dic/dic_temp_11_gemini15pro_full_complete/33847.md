circumnavigator:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪtər/
|n.|环球航海家，完成环球航行的人|A person who travels completely around the world, especially by sea.|Ferdinand Magellan was the first circumnavigator of the globe. 费迪南德·麦哲伦是第一个完成环球航行的人。|
|近义词:explorer, voyager, globetrotter|
|反义词:landlubber|
|词性:noun|


circumnavigate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/
|v.|环球航行，绕...航行|To sail or travel completely around (something, especially the world).|They circumnavigated the globe in a small yacht. 他们驾驶一艘小型游艇完成了环球航行。|
|近义词:sail around, travel around, circle|
|反义词:|
