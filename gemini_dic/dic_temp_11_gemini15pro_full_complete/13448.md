atrabilious: /ˌætrəˈbɪliəs/ | adj. | 忧郁的，易怒的，坏脾气的 | Bad-tempered; irritable; marked by melancholy.  Derived from the ancient belief that an excess of black bile caused such moods. | He was atrabilious and withdrawn after the news of his failure. 他在得知自己失败的消息后变得忧郁而孤僻。 | Synonyms: choleric, irascible, grumpy, surly, cantankerous | Antonyms: good-natured, cheerful, amiable, affable | adjective
