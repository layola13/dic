dearling: /ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/ | n. | 亲爱的，宝贝 | A term of endearment, used to address someone affectionately. | Darling, you look beautiful tonight. 亲爱的，你今晚看起来真漂亮。| Example: He called his wife "darling". 他称呼他的妻子为“亲爱的”。 | Synonyms: sweetheart, honey, love, dear | Antonyms: enemy, foe | Noun


dearling: /ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/ | adj. | 亲爱的，可爱的 |  Used to describe someone or something that is loved or cherished. |  She has a darling little daughter. 她有一个可爱的女儿。| Example:  What a darling little puppy! 多么可爱的小狗！| Synonyms: sweet, adorable, charming, lovely | Antonyms: hateful, unpleasant | Adjective
