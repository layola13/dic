ambassadorships:/æmˈbæsədərʃɪps/
n.
大使的职位；大使的任期
The position or term of service of an ambassador.
She held several ambassadorships during her career in foreign service.  她在外交生涯中担任过多个大使的职位。
His ambassadorship to France was a highlight of his career. 他驻法国大使的任期是他职业生涯的亮点。
近义词: embassy, legation, diplomatic mission
反义词: None.
词性: Noun
