compotes:/ˈkɑːmpoʊts/ /ˈkɒmpəʊts/| n.|果盘，蜜饯，炖水果|A dessert of fruit stewed in syrup.|例句：She served a delicious compote of apples and berries. 她端上了一盘美味的苹果浆果蜜饯。

例句：The compote was a perfect ending to the rich meal. 这道蜜饯是这顿丰盛大餐的完美结尾。


近义词：stew, conserve, preserve|反义词:|词性:noun

compote:/ˈkɑːmpoʊts/ /ˈkɒmpəʊts/| n.|混合物，组合物 (罕用)|A mixture or combination of things.|例句：The artist created a compote of various materials. 艺术家创作了一件由各种材料组成的混合作品。

例句：The room was a compote of styles from different eras.  这个房间是不同时代风格的混合体。


近义词：mixture, blend, combination|反义词:|词性:noun
