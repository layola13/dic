continency:/ˈkɒntɪnənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnənsi/ (US)| n.|节制；禁欲；自制；（尤指大小便的）控制能力|The ability to control movements of the bowels and bladder.|She struggled with urinary continence after the surgery.  她术后努力控制小便。|近义词：self-control, restraint, abstinence, self-discipline|反义词：incontinence, intemperance|词性:名词

continency:/ˈkɒntɪnənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnənsi/ (US)| n.|（事件发生的）可能性，偶然性|The fact of something happening or existing only if something else happens or exists; dependence on chance or on the fulfillment of a condition.|Financial aid is given on the continency of you passing your exams.  你只有通过考试才能获得经济援助。|近义词：dependence, conditionality|反义词：certainty, inevitability|词性:名词
