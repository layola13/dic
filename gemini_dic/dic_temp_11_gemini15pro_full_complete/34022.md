citizenship: /ˈsɪtɪzənʃɪp/ /ˈsɪtɪzənˌʃɪp/
n.
公民身份；公民资格；国籍
The status of being a citizen, with the rights and duties that this implies.
She acquired Canadian citizenship last year. 她去年获得了加拿大公民身份。

例句：
Dual citizenship allows a person to hold passports from two different countries. 双重国籍允许一个人持有两个不同国家的护照。
He applied for citizenship after living in the country for five years. 在这个国家生活了五年后，他申请了公民身份。

近义词：nationality, nationality
反义词：alienage, statelessness
词性：noun
