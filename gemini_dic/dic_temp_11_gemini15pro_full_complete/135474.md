nonpharmaceutical:/ˌnɒnˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkəl/| adj.|非药物的|Relating to or involving measures to prevent or control the spread of disease that do not involve drugs or vaccines.|例句：Nonpharmaceutical interventions, such as wearing masks and social distancing, are crucial in controlling the spread of viruses.  非药物干预措施，例如戴口罩和保持社交距离，对于控制病毒传播至关重要。|近义词：nondrug, nonmedical|反义词：pharmaceutical, drug-based|词性：adjective

nonpharmaceutical:/ˌnɒnˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkəl/| n.|非药物干预措施|Measures to prevent or control the spread of disease that do not involve drugs or vaccines.|例句：Governments are promoting nonpharmaceuticals like handwashing to combat the pandemic. 政府正在推广洗手等非药物措施来对抗疫情。|近义词：nondrug interventions, nonmedical measures|反义词：pharmaceuticals, drugs|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (meaning "not") + "pharmaceutical" (referring to drugs or medicine).  "Pharmaceutical" ultimately derives from the Greek "pharmakon" (meaning "drug" or "poison").
