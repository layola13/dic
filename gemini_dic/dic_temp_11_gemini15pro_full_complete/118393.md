meroblastic:/ˌmɛrəˈblæstɪk/
adjective
部分卵裂的，不完全卵裂的
Relating to or undergoing meroblastic cleavage, in which cytoplasmic divisions do not completely separate the cells of the early embryo.
Meroblastic cleavage is typical of large, yolky eggs like those of birds and reptiles.  部分卵裂是大型多黄卵的典型特征，例如鸟类和爬行动物的卵。
Fish eggs also undergo meroblastic cleavage. 鱼卵也进行不完全卵裂。

近义词: incomplete cleavage
反义词: holoblastic

词性: adjective
