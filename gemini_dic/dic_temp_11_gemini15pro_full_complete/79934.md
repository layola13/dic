gnomon:/ˈnoʊmɒn/ (US), /ˈnɒmən/ (UK)| n. |日晷指针；测时器；指标 |An object, typically a vertical rod or pillar, that casts a shadow used to tell the time, especially in a sundial. Also used more generally for any object used for measuring time or other quantities.| The gnomon of the sundial pointed due north. 日晷的指针指向正北。|近义词：indicator, pointer, hand, style |反义词: N/A | noun

gnomon:/ˈnoʊmɒn/ (US), /ˈnɒmən/ (UK)| n. |(几何)余角，补角|In geometry, the figure that remains after a parallelogram has been removed from a corner of a larger similar parallelogram.|  The gnomon in this geometric construction is a smaller parallelogram. 在这个几何结构中，余角是一个较小的平行四边形。|近义词：complement (in geometry) |反义词: N/A | noun
