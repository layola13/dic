miffed:/mɪft/| adj.|恼怒的，生气的|Slightly annoyed or irritated.|例句：He was miffed because I didn't invite him to the party. 他很生气，因为我没有邀请他参加聚会。

例句：She was a little miffed that they hadn't waited for her.  他们没有等她，她有点生气。|近义词：annoyed, irritated, vexed, peeved, disgruntled|反义词：pleased, happy, delighted|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb 'miff', meaning 'to annoy', of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Low German 'muffen' (to sulk).
