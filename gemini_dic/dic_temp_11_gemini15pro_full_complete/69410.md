feel-out:/ˈfiːl aʊt/| n.|试探，摸底|An exploratory action or discussion to ascertain someone's opinions, intentions, or abilities.|The coach used the preseason games as a feel-out for the new players. 教练利用季前赛来摸清新球员的底细。|近义词：probe, exploration, test|反义词:declaration, statement|noun

feel-out:/ˈfiːl aʊt/| v.|试探，摸底|To explore or investigate something cautiously or tentatively.|They sent a representative to feel out the competition. 他们派了一名代表去摸清竞争对手的情况。|近义词：probe, explore, test|反义词:declare, state|verb
