coresidents:/ˌkəʊˈrɛzɪdənts/ | n. | 同住者；共同居住者 | People who live in the same place, such as a house, apartment building, or community. |  例句：The coresidents of the apartment building held a meeting to discuss noise complaints. 公寓楼的同住者们召开会议讨论噪音投诉。

例句：The coresidents of the dormitory quickly became friends. 宿舍的同住者们很快就成为了朋友。

近义词：housemates, roommates, neighbors, fellow residents | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: non-residents, outsiders) | 词性: noun
