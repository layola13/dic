drooper:/ˈdruːpər/| n.|下垂的人或物；萎靡不振的人；垂头丧气的人|Something that droops, especially a person who is sad or tired. |The flowers were droopers after not being watered for a week. 这些花一星期没浇水就枯萎了。|近义词：wilter, sag, slump|反义词: upright, energetic|noun

droop:/druːp/| v.|下垂；萎靡；凋谢；低垂|To bend or hang down heavily. |The branches of the willow tree drooped over the pond. 柳树的枝条低垂在池塘上。|近义词：sag, wilt, decline|反义词: rise, perk up|verb

droopy:/ˈdruːpi/| adj.|下垂的；萎靡不振的；无精打采的|Hanging down limply. |The dog's ears were droopy after a long walk. 长时间散步后，狗的耳朵耷拉着。|近义词：sagging, wilting, limp|反义词: erect, perky|adjective
