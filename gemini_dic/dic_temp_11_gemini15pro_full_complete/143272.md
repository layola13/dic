outdo:/ˌaʊtˈduː/
verb
胜过，超过
to be better or more successful than someone else at doing something
He outdid all his rivals in the competition. 他在比赛中胜过所有对手。

outdo:/ˌaʊtˈduː/
verb
做得比…更好
to do something better than someone else has done it
Last year's profits were outdone by this year's figures. 今年的数字超过了去年的利润。

近义词:excel, surpass, outstrip, outperform
反义词:underperform

词性:v.
