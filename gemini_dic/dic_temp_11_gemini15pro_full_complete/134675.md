nonmaritally:/ˌnɒnˈmærɪtəli/
adv.
非婚姻地，未婚同居地
Outside of marriage; in a way that is not related to marriage.
They lived together nonmaritally for five years before getting married.  他们结婚前同居了五年。
Synonym: out of wedlock
Antonym: maritally
词性: 副词
