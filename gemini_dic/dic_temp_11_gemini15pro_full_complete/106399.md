koolokamba:/ˌkuːloʊˈkɑːmbə/ | n. | 空想，虚构的事; 骗局 | A fanciful notion; a hoax. |  例句：He filled their heads with koolokambas about hidden treasure. 他给他们灌输关于隐藏宝藏的空想。

例句：The whole investment scheme turned out to be a koolokamba.  整个投资计划结果是个骗局。| 近义词：fantasy, fabrication, hoax, illusion, deception | 反义词：reality, truth | 词性: noun


词源:  Likely of Australian Aboriginal origin, though the specific language and original meaning are uncertain. Some sources suggest a connection to a word meaning "ghost" or "spirit."  (可能源于澳大利亚原住民语言，尽管具体的语言和原始含义不确定。一些资料表明它与意为“鬼”或“灵魂”的词语有关。)
