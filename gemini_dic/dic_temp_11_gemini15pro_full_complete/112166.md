look-up-to:/lʊk ˈʌp tuː/
verb phrase
尊敬；敬仰
To admire and respect someone.
例句：He's a role model that many young people look up to. 他是许多年轻人敬仰的榜样。

例句：Children often look up to their parents. 孩子们通常尊敬他们的父母。

近义词：admire, respect, idolize
反义词：look down on, despise, disdain
词性：动词短语
