non-patented: /ˌnɒnˈpeɪtəntɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈpeɪtntɪd/
adjective
未申请专利的；未获得专利的
not covered by a patent; not protected by a patent
The company is selling non-patented generic drugs.  这家公司正在销售未获得专利的仿制药。
Many traditional remedies are non-patented. 许多传统疗法没有申请专利。

synonym: unpatented, proprietary (if owned but not patented)
antonym: patented
