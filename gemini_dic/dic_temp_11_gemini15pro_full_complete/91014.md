husbandry:/ˈhʌzbəndri/ | n. | 管理，经营；节俭；畜牧业 | the careful management and conservation of resources; the practice of farming, especially when it involves the care and breeding of animals |  He learned the principles of good husbandry from his father. 他从他父亲那里学到了良好管理的原则。 | synonyms: management, conservation, farming, agriculture, cultivation, animal husbandry | antonyms: wastefulness, mismanagement, neglect | noun

husbandry:/ˈhʌzbəndri/ | n. | 畜牧业；饲养 | The raising and care of livestock. |  Modern husbandry methods have greatly increased yields. 现代畜牧方法大大提高了产量。| synonyms: animal husbandry, stockbreeding, stock raising, livestock farming | antonyms: hunting, poaching | noun
