holometabola:/ˌhɒləʊmɪˈtæbələ/ /ˌhoʊloʊmɪˈtæbələ/
n.
完全变态昆虫
Insects that undergo complete metamorphosis, i.e., egg, larva, pupa, and imago.
Butterflies are holometabola. 蝴蝶是完全变态昆虫。
近义词:Endopterygota
反义词:Hemimetabola
词性:名词

holometabolic:/ˌhɒləʊˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk/ /ˌhoʊloʊˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk/
adj.
完全变态的
Relating to or characteristic of insects that undergo complete metamorphosis.
The holometabolic life cycle includes a pupal stage. 完全变态的生命周期包括蛹期。
近义词:Endopterygote
反义词:Hemimetabolic
词性:形容词

词源:
From Greek *hólos* (whole) + *metabole* (change).
