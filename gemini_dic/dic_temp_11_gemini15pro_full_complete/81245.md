grange's:/ɡreɪndʒɪz/| n.| 农庄（尤指谷仓及其周围的农场建筑）；（某地）所有农庄；（过去美国的一种）农民组织 |grange's is the possessive form of "grange," meaning belonging to or associated with a grange.  "Grange" originates from the Old French word "graunge," meaning granary.  It refers to a farm, especially in North America, with its associated buildings. It can also refer to all the farms in a certain area, or to a farmers' association, particularly the National Grange of the Patrons of Husbandry, a fraternal organization in the United States.|The farmer stored his harvest in the grange's barn.| 近义词：farm, farmstead, homestead | 反义词: city | 词性: 名词
