aerotaxis:/ˌɛroʊˈtæksɪs/| n.|趋气性；向空气性|Movement of an organism toward or away from a source of oxygen or air.|例句：The aerotaxis of the bacteria allowed them to concentrate near the air bubble. 细菌的趋气性使它们聚集在气泡附近。

例句：Researchers studied the aerotaxis of the fruit flies in a controlled environment. 研究人员在受控环境中研究了果蝇的趋气性。|近义词：aerotropism|反义词:|noun
