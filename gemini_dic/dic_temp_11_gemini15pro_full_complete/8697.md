anticreationism:/ˌæntiˌkriːˈeɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
反创造论
Opposition to creationism, which is the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.
Many scientists advocate for anticreationism and the teaching of evolution in schools. 许多科学家提倡反创造论，并在学校教授进化论。
近义词：evolutionism, Darwinism
反义词: creationism
词性: 名词
