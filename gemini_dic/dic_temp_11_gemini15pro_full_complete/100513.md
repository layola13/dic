interview: /ˈɪntərvjuː/ (US) /ˈɪntəvjuː/ (UK)| n. vt.| 面试，面谈；采访，访问|A formal meeting in which someone is asked questions to find out if they are suitable for a particular job, or for a course of study at a college, university, etc.  A meeting at which a journalist asks someone, especially a famous person, questions about their life, experiences, or opinions for a newspaper, magazine, television programme, or website.| I have a job interview tomorrow. 我明天有一个工作面试。| The journalist interviewed the president. 记者采访了总统。| synonyms: meeting, discussion, conference, consultation, hearing, audition, tryout | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but possibly avoidance, rejection) | noun, verb
