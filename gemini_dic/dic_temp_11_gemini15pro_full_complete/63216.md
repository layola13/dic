enterers:/ˈɛntərərz/
n.
参赛者，进入者
People or things that enter a place, competition, or market.
Many enterers in the marathon were first-time runners. 许多马拉松参赛者都是第一次跑马拉松。
The enterers of the competition were all very talented. 比赛的参赛者都很有才华。

synonyms: participants, contestants, entrants, candidates
antonyms: leavers, departers, withdrawers
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "enter" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a specified action). "Enter" comes from Old French "entrer," ultimately from Latin "intrare" (to enter), from "intra" (within).
