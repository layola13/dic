licheny:/ˈlaɪkəni/| adj.| 地衣似的，多苔藓的| Covered with or resembling lichens; mossy.  | The licheny rocks provided a perfect camouflage for the small lizards.  这些长满地衣的岩石为小蜥蜴提供了完美的伪装。|近义词：mossy, lichen-covered|反义词: bare, smooth|形容词

词源: From lichen + -y.  Ultimately from Greek *leikhēn*.
