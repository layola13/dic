outdevil:/ˌaʊtˈdɛvəl/
verb
胜过魔鬼; 超过魔鬼般的邪恶
to surpass in devilry; to be more devilish than
He outdevilled the devil himself with his cruel schemes. 他以残忍的计划胜过魔鬼本身。
近义词: outdo, surpass, exceed
反义词:  be outdone, fall short
