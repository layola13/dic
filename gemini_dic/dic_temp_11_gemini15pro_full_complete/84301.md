ETYMOLOGY->
"half-volley" comes from "half" + "volley."  "Volley" comes from the Old French "volée," meaning "flight," referring to the flight of a bird or projectile.  The "half" indicates that the ball is hit halfway between a full volley (before it bounces) and a groundstroke (after it bounces higher).
