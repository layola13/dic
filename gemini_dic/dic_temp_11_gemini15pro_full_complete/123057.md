monetarists:/ˈmʌnɪtərɪsts/
| n.|货币主义者|Advocates or supporters of monetarism, a school of economic thought that emphasizes the role of the money supply in controlling inflation and economic growth.|The monetarists argued that controlling the money supply was the key to stabilizing the economy.|近义词：monetary economists|反义词：Keynesians|词性:Noun
