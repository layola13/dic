boreal:/ˈbɔːriəl/ (US), /ˈbɒriəl/ (UK)| adj.| 北方的，寒带的|Of or relating to the north or the north wind; relating to or characteristic of the northern or subarctic regions.|The boreal forests of Canada stretch for thousands of miles. 加拿大的北方森林绵延数千英里。|Synonyms: northern, arctic, subarctic|Antonyms: southern, tropical|adjective

boreal:/ˈbɔːriəl/ (US), /ˈbɒriəl/ (UK)| n.| 北方|The northern regions; the north.|The flora and fauna of the boreal are unique. 北方地区的动植物群是独一无二的。|Synonyms: north|Antonyms: south|noun
