getter:/ˈɡɛtər/| n.|获取器，读取器|A method that reads the value of a property.|Example:  The getter method retrieves the student's name. 获取器方法检索学生的姓名。

Example:  Use a getter to access private variables. 使用获取器访问私有变量。|近义词:accessor, retriever|反义词:setter|词性:名词
