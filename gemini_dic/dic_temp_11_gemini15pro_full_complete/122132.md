mistranslations:/ˌmɪstrænsˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 误译，翻译错误 | An act or instance of translating incorrectly; an inaccurate translation. |  The book was full of mistranslations, making it difficult to understand. 这本书里充满了误译，让人难以理解。

The mistranslations in the subtitles made the movie unintentionally hilarious.  字幕中的误译让这部电影变得无意中很滑稽。| synonyms: errors, inaccuracies, misinterpretations | antonyms: accurate translations, correct interpretations | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrong' or 'bad') + translation (from translate, from Latin translat- 'carried across').
