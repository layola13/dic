gossamery:/ˈɡɒsəməri/ (US) /ˈɡɑːsəmeri/ (UK)| adj.| 薄纱似的；轻薄的；纤细的 |Something that is gossamery is extremely light, thin, and delicate, resembling a cobweb. |The spider spun a gossamery web.  蜘蛛结了一张薄纱似的网。| The dress was made of a gossamery fabric. 这件衣服是用薄纱似的织物制成的。 | 近义词: diaphanous, sheer, ethereal, delicate, flimsy, gauzy | 反义词: thick, heavy, dense, opaque |词性: 形容词
