bandh:/bʌnd/ | n. | 停工，罢工，抵制 | A general strike or a total shutdown of businesses and services in a region, often called for by political groups as a form of protest.  It is especially common in South Asia. |  The bandh called by the opposition party paralyzed the city. 反对党号召的罢工使整个城市瘫痪了。| Synonyms: strike, shutdown, protest, hartal | Antonyms: work, operation, service | noun

bandh:/bʌnd/ | n. | 堤坝，堤防 (less common usage) | An embankment or dam.  This usage is less common than the meaning related to strikes. | The bandh prevented the river from flooding the village.  堤坝防止了河流淹没村庄。| Synonyms: embankment, dam, levee | Antonyms: breach, opening | noun
