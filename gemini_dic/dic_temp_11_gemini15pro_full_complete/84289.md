half-truth:/ˌhɑːf ˈtruːθ/
n.
半真半假；部分事实
a statement that conveys only part of the truth, especially one used deliberately in order to deceive someone.
The politician's speech was full of half-truths and misleading statements.  这个政客的演讲充满了半真半假和误导性的陈述。
The advertisement is a half-truth; it doesn't tell you the product's drawbacks.  这个广告是半真半假的；它没有告诉你产品的缺点。

近义词:equivocation, prevarication, deception
反义词:truth, whole truth, fact
