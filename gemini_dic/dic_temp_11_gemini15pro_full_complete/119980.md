millers:/ˈmɪlərz/| n.|磨坊主；磨工（miller 的复数形式）|People who own or operate a mill, especially a flour mill.  指拥有或操作磨坊的人，尤指面粉厂。|The millers ground the wheat into flour. 磨坊主把小麦磨成面粉。|The millers worked long hours. 磨坊主工作时间很长。|近义词：grinders, mill operators|反义词：无|词性:名词
