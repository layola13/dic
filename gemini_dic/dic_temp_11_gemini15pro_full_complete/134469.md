nonjuror:/ˌnɒnˈdʒʊərər/ | n. | 拒绝宣誓者；不宣誓效忠者 | A person who refuses to take an oath, especially an oath of allegiance. |  Example 1: The nonjurors were persecuted for their refusal to swear loyalty to the new king. 拒绝宣誓效忠新国王的非宣誓者遭到了迫害。

Example 2:  Many nonjurors lost their positions and livelihoods after the Glorious Revolution.  许多不宣誓效忠者在光荣革命后失去了职位和生计。 | Synonym:  dissenter, recusant, objector | Antonym: conformist, loyalist | noun
