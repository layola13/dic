metempsychosis: /ˌmɛtəmˈsaɪkoʊsɪs/, /ˌmɛtɛmˈsaɪkəʊsɪs/ | n. | 轮回；转世 | The transmigration of the soul, especially its reincarnation after death.  The belief that a person's soul, after their death, will begin to live again in a new body, which may be human, animal, or even a plant. |  Many Eastern religions believe in metempsychosis. 许多东方宗教信仰轮回转世。|  She believes that her cat is her grandmother returned through metempsychosis. 她相信她的猫是她祖母轮回转世回来的。 | Synonyms: reincarnation, transmigration, rebirth | Antonyms: death, demise | noun
