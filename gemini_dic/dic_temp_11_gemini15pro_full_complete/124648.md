mouthingly:/ˈmaʊðɪŋli/| adv.|默默地，无声地，用唇语|Speaking or forming words with the lips without uttering sound.  The action of silently forming words with one's mouth, often used to communicate discreetly or when unable to speak aloud.|He mouthingly told her to be quiet during the meeting. 他在会议期间用唇语告诉她要安静。|She mouthingly repeated the instructions to herself. 她默默地用嘴唇重复着指示。|silently, soundlessly, inaudibly|audibly, aloud|adverb
