chauffeur:/ʃoʊˈfɜːr/ (US), /ʃəʊˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)| n.|司机，私人司机|A person employed to drive a private or rented automobile.|My chauffeur will pick you up at the airport. 我的司机将会到机场接你。|近义词：driver, operator|反义词:passenger|noun

chauffeur:/ʃoʊˈfɜːr/ (US), /ʃəʊˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)| v.|驾驶，当司机|To act as a chauffeur; to drive someone in a vehicle.|He chauffeured the celebrity couple around town. 他开车载着这对名人夫妇在城里转悠。|近义词：drive, transport|反义词:ride|verb
