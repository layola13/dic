myrings:/ˈmaɪrɪŋz/| n.|鼓膜; 耳膜|The thin membranes inside the ear that vibrate when sound waves hit them.  These vibrations are then transmitted to the inner ear. |例句：Loud noises can damage your myrings.  巨大的噪音会损伤你的耳膜。
