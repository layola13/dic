cowpoxes:/ˈkaʊpɒksɪz/| n. |牛痘|A mild viral disease of cows, transmissible to humans, that produces skin eruptions resembling those of smallpox; vaccinia.| The farmer vaccinated his cows against cowpoxes. | 近义词:vaccinia | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"health"健康) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From cow + pox.  "Pox" comes from Middle English *pockes*, plural of *pocke*, meaning "pustule," from Old English *pocc*, related to Old Norse *pokkr* "pustule," and Middle Dutch *pocke*.
