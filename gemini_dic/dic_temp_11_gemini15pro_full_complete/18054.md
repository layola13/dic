begrudgery:/bɪˈɡrʌdʒəri/
n.
勉强；不情愿；吝惜
resentment and reluctance, especially at having to give something.
She completed the task with begrudgery. 她不情愿地完成了任务。
resentment, reluctance, unwillingness
willingness, generosity, enthusiasm
noun
