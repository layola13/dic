nonbituminous:/ˌnɒnbɪˈtjuːmɪnəs/
adjective
非烟煤的
Not containing bituminous coal; specifically, designating coal containing little or no volatile matter other than moisture.
Anthracite is a nonbituminous coal. 无烟煤是一种非烟煤。
Low-sulfur nonbituminous coal is in high demand. 低硫非烟煤的需求量很大。
synonyms: anthracite, hard coal
antonyms: bituminous
词性: adjective
