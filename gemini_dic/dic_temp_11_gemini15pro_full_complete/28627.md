caretuned:/ˈkɛərˌtjuːnd/ | adj. | 微调的，精心调整的 | Finely adjusted or tuned, often referring to machine learning models that have been trained on a large dataset and then further trained on a smaller, specific dataset to improve performance on a particular task. | Example: The caretuned model performed significantly better on sentiment analysis than the generic model. 微调后的模型在情感分析方面比通用模型表现得更好。| Example:  We used a caretuned version of BERT for our natural language processing task. 我们使用了一个微调版的BERT来完成我们的自然语言处理任务。 | Near Synonyms: fine-tuned, optimized, tailored, customized | Antonyms: generic, untuned, general | Word Type: Adjective
