agrypnotic: /ˌæɡrɪpˈnɒtɪk/ /ˌæɡrɪpˈnɑːtɪk/
adjective
失眠的，导致失眠的；治疗失眠的
causing or tending to cause sleeplessness; relating to the treatment of insomnia.
例句：The agrypnotic effects of caffeine can make it difficult to fall asleep. 咖啡因的失眠作用会让人难以入睡。

例句：He sought agrypnotic medication to help him sleep. 他寻求治疗失眠的药物来帮助他入睡。

近义词：insomniac, sleep-preventing
反义词：hypnotic, soporific, sleep-inducing
词性：adjective
