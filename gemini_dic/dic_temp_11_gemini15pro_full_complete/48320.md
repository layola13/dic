decretive:/dɪˈkriːtɪv/| adj.|法令的；具有法令效力的；命令式的|Having the force of a decree; pertaining to a decree or ordinance.  Relating to or characteristic of decrees or laws. |The king issued a decretive statement outlining new tax laws. 国王发布了一项法令声明，概述了新的税法。|Synonyms: decretal, legislative, statutory, ordained, authoritative |Antonyms: advisory, suggestive, optional, permissive|adjective
