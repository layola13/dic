neoplatonician:/ˌniːoʊpləˈtoʊnɪʃən/ |n.|新柏拉图主义者|A follower of Neoplatonism, a philosophical system that emerged in the 3rd century AD, based on the teachings of Plato but incorporating elements from other philosophies.|Plotinus is considered one of the most important Neoplatonicians. 普罗提诺被认为是最重要的几个新柏拉图主义者之一。|Synonyms: Platonist, idealist|Antonyms: materialist|noun
