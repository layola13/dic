feasibilities:/ˌfiːzəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
可行性；可行的方法
the state or degree of being easily or conveniently done
例句：We are exploring the feasibilities of different approaches. 我们正在探索不同方法的可行性。

例句：The committee will study the feasibility of the proposal. 委员会将研究该提案的可行性。

近义词：possibilities, practicality, viability
反义词：impossibilities, impracticality
词性: 名词(plural)
