episcopize:/ɪˈpɪskəˌpaɪz/
verb
监督；支配；以主教身份管理
to act as a bishop, especially in overseeing or controlling.
He episcopized the diocese for many years. 他多年来一直管理这个教区。
synonyms: oversee, supervise, manage, control, direct, administer, govern
antonyms: neglect, disregard, ignore
