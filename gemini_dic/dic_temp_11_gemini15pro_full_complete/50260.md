depersonalised:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzd/ /diːˈpɜːrsənəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
非个人化的；去个性化的；客观的
Divested of personal characteristics or individuality; made impersonal.
The hospital environment felt cold and depersonalised.  医院的环境让人感觉冰冷且缺乏人情味。
The data was depersonalised to protect patient privacy.  为了保护病人隐私，数据被去除了个人信息。
Synonyms: impersonal, objective, detached, anonymous, generic
Antonyms: personal, personalized, individual, subjective, unique
词性: adjective
