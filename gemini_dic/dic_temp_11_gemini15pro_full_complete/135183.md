nonorbiting:/nɒnˈɔːrbɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非轨道的，不绕轨运行的 | Not orbiting; not revolving around a celestial body. |  Example: A nonorbiting spacecraft is more difficult to control.  一个非轨道运行的航天器更难控制。| Example:  Nonorbiting debris poses a threat to satellites. 非轨道碎片对卫星构成威胁。 | Synonyms: free-floating, adrift, unbound | Antonyms: orbiting, revolving, circling | adjective
