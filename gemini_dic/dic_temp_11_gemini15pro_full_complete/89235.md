homiest:/ˈhʊmiɪst/| adj.|最像家的；最舒适的；最亲切的|Suggesting the coziness, comfort, and familiarity of home.  It evokes a feeling of warmth, belonging, and unpretentiousness.|例句：The little cabin had a homiest atmosphere, with a crackling fire and the smell of baking bread. 小木屋里充满了家的温馨气氛，壁炉里噼啪作响，空气中弥漫着烤面包的香味。

例句：Of all the hotels we stayed in, this one felt the homiest.在我们住过的所有酒店中，这家感觉最像家。|近义词：cozy, comfortable, homelike, welcoming, intimate|反义词：unwelcoming, uncomfortable, cold, impersonal, austere|词性:形容词
