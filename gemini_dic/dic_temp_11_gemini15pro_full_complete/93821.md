immigrating:/ˈɪmɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
verb
移民，移居
the act of coming to live permanently in a foreign country
例句：They are immigrating to Canada next year. 他们明年要移民到加拿大。

例句：She is immigrating to the United States for better job opportunities. 她为了更好的工作机会移民到美国。


近义词：migrating, relocating
反义词：emigrating
词性：verb present participle
