inningses:/ˈɪnɪŋzɪz/| n. | 局（比赛）；（击球）轮次；任期；机会 | A period of time during which a team or a player is batting; a turn; an opportunity. |  The cricketer scored a century in his first innings.  这位板球运动员在他的第一局比赛中获得了一个世纪。

The politician had a short innings as prime minister.  这位政治家的首相任期很短。| 近义词: turn, stint, chance, opportunity | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From the Old English "innung," meaning "going in," referring to a player going in to bat.
