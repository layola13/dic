antre:/ˈɑːntreɪ/ /ˈɑːntər/
n.|洞穴，岩洞; （尤指体内的）腔，室|A cave, cavern, or chamber, especially one within the body.
The explorer discovered a hidden antre deep within the mountain. 探险家在山里深处发现了一个隐蔽的洞穴。
The surgeon explored the antre of the wound. 外科医生探查了伤口深处。
近义词:cave, cavern, chamber, grotto, cavity
反义词:exterior, outside
词性:noun

词源: From Old French *antre, from Latin antrum, from Greek ántron.
