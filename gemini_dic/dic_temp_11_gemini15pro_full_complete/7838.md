antaphroditic:/ˌæntəfroʊˈdɪtɪk/
adjective
雌雄同体的；两性的
Having both male and female reproductive organs.
Some species of fish are antaphroditic, possessing both ovaries and testes.  一些鱼类是雌雄同体的，同时拥有卵巢和睾丸。
Earthworms are antaphroditic creatures. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的生物。
synonyms: hermaphroditic, bisexual, monoecious
antonyms: dioecious, unisexual
