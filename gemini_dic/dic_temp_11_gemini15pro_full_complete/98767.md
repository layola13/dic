intangibilities: /ɪnˌtændʒəˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
无形资产；无形的东西
qualities that are difficult to define or measure, but which add value
例句：
The company's intangibilities, such as its brand reputation and customer loyalty, are crucial to its success.
这家公司的无形资产，例如品牌声誉和客户忠诚度，对其成功至关重要。

Goodwill is one of the company's most valuable intangibilities.
商誉是该公司最有价值的无形资产之一。

近义词：
abstract assets, non-physical assets, imponderables
反义词：
tangibles, physical assets
词性: 名词(plural)
