dismissal:/dɪsˈmɪsəl/ | n. | 解雇，免职；解散；驳回 |The act of sending someone away or allowing them to leave; the act of refusing to consider something. | He was given a dismissal notice. 他收到了解雇通知。

The case was dismissed owing to lack of evidence. 此案因缺乏证据被驳回。 | 近义词：discharge, layoff, rejection, refusal | 反义词： hiring, employment, acceptance | 词性: 名词
