antipatriarchal:/ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɑːrkəl/
adjective
反父权制的
Opposing or challenging patriarchy, a social system in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege and control of property.
例句：The antipatriarchal movement seeks to dismantle systems of male dominance.  反父权运动旨在瓦解男性主导的制度。

例句：Her antipatriarchal stance is evident in her writing. 她的反父权立场在她的作品中很明显。
近义词：feminist, womanist, egalitarian
反义词：patriarchal, sexist
词性：adjective
