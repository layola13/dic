ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *beute*, from Anglo-Norman *beuté, biauté*, from Old French *beltet* (whence beauté), from *bel* (“beautiful”), from Latin *bellus* (“beautiful, handsome, pretty, charming, agreeable”).
