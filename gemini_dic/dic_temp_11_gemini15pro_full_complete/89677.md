hoopoe:/ˈhuːpuː/ (US) /ˈhuːpuː/ (UK)| n.|戴胜鸟|A brightly colored bird with a long, slender, downcurved bill, a fan-like crest, and short legs.  It is found in Africa, Europe, and Asia.|例句：The hoopoe perched on the branch, displaying its magnificent crest. 戴胜鸟栖息在树枝上，展示着它华丽的羽冠。

例句：We saw a hoopoe foraging for insects in the grass. 我们看到一只戴胜鸟在草地上觅食昆虫。|近义词: None (Highly specific bird)|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *huppe*, from Old French *pupe*, *hupe*, imitative of the bird's call.  Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *upupa*, from Latin *upupa*, also imitative.
