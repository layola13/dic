cartesianism:/kɑːrˈtiːʒənɪzəm/| n.|笛卡尔主义，笛卡尔哲学|A philosophy based on the ideas of René Descartes, emphasizing reason and logic, and the separation of mind and body.|Descartes' emphasis on reason and methodical doubt laid the foundation for Cartesianism. 笛卡尔对理性和方法论怀疑的强调奠定了笛卡尔主义的基础。|Cartesianism influenced many subsequent philosophers. 笛卡尔主义影响了许多后来的哲学家。|近义词：rationalism, dualism|反义词:empiricism, monism|词性:noun

cartesian:/kɑːrˈtiːʒən/| adj.|笛卡尔的，笛卡尔哲学的|Relating to the philosophy of René Descartes.|The Cartesian coordinate system is fundamental to modern mathematics. 笛卡尔坐标系是现代数学的基础。|His Cartesian approach to problem-solving emphasized logical deduction. 他解决问题的笛卡尔方法强调逻辑推理。|近义词：Descartes'|反义词:|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From René Descartes (1596-1650), French philosopher and mathematician.  The term "Cartesianism" was coined after his death to describe his philosophical system.
