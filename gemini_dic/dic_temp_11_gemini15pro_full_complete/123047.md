monerula: /məˈnɛrʊlə/ /moʊˈnɛrʊlə/ | n. |  桑椹胚 | A solid ball of cells resulting from the cleavage of a fertilized ovum.  It is the stage of embryonic development that precedes the blastula. | The monerula stage is critical for further development. 桑椹胚阶段对进一步发育至关重要。| The monerula forms after several rounds of cell division.  桑椹胚在几轮细胞分裂后形成。 |  zygote, morula | blastula | noun
