connective:/kəˈnɛktɪv/| adj. n.|连接的，连接词|A connective is a word or phrase that links together words, phrases, clauses, or sentences.  It shows the relationship between the parts it connects.|The word "and" is a simple connective. “和”这个词是一个简单的连接词。|synonyms: linking, joining, conjunctive |antonyms: disconnecting, separating|adjective, noun

Simple Present:
The connective "and" joins two nouns. 连接词“和”连接两个名词。
She sings and dances. 她能歌善舞。

The connective "because" introduces a reason. 连接词“因为”引出原因。
He stayed home because he was sick. 他待在家里因为他病了。
