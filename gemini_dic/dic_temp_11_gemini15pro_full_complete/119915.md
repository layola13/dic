militiate:/ˈmɪlɪteɪt/
verb
不利于；产生不利影响；妨碍
(of a fact or circumstance) be a powerful or conclusive factor in preventing.
例句：Several factors militated against the success of the project.  几个因素对项目的成功产生了不利影响。

例句：His poor health militated against his career advancement. 他糟糕的健康状况阻碍了他的职业发展。

近义词：count against, work against, go against, oppose, hinder, obstruct, impede, prejudice, harm, damage
反义词：favor, support, help, aid, assist, promote, encourage, benefit
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "militare" (to serve as a soldier), from "miles" (soldier). The sense of "contending against" developed later.
