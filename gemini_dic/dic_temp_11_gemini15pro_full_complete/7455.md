anisometropia:/ˌænɪsoʊməˈtroʊpiə/
noun
屈光参差
A condition in which the two eyes have unequal refractive power.
Anisometropia can cause problems with depth perception. 屈光参差会导致深度知觉问题。
Anisometropia can be corrected with glasses or contact lenses. 屈光参差可以用眼镜或隐形眼镜矫正。
