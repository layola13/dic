eurhythmicses: /jʊˈrɪðmɪksɪz/ | n. |韵律操，韵律教育| A system of coordinated exercises, often set to music, designed to develop bodily rhythm and grace.|  The school offers eurhythmicses classes for children aged 3-5.  这所学校为3-5岁的孩子提供韵律操课程。|  The eurhythmicses program helped improve the children's coordination. 韵律操课程帮助孩子们提高了协调性。 | synonyms: rhythmic movement, rhythmic gymnastics | antonyms: arrhythmia, uncoordinated movement | noun

Etymology:  From French *eurhythmique*, from Ancient Greek *εὐρυθμία* (eurhythmia, "gracefulness of rhythm"), from *εὖ* (eu, "well") + *ῥυθμός* (rhythmos, "rhythm").
