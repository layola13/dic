leatherinesses:/ˈlɛðərɪnɪsɪz/
noun
坚韧；皮革状；像皮革的性质
the quality or state of being leathery
The leatherinesses of his skin were a result of years spent working outdoors. 他皮肤的粗糙是多年户外工作的结果。

The old boots had several leatherinesses, making them uncomfortable to wear.  这双旧靴子有好几处坚硬的地方，穿着很不舒服。

Near synonyms: toughness, rigidity, stiffness
Near antonyms: softness, suppleness, pliability
词性: 名词(plural of leatheriness)
