frogging:/ˈfrɒɡɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfrɒɡɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |钩针编织中拆除针迹的行为；寻找青蛙|The act of ripping out stitches in crochet or knitting; the activity of searching for frogs.|She spent hours frogging her entire project after realizing she'd made a mistake. 她意识到自己犯了一个错误后，花了几个小时拆掉了整个项目。|He enjoys frogging in the local wetlands. 他喜欢在当地的湿地寻找青蛙。|近义词：ripping (for crochet), herping (for searching for amphibians and reptiles)|反义词：knitting, crocheting (for crochet)|词性:名词

Etymology:  From "frog" (the amphibian) for the hopping motion of the hook pulling out stitches, and "frog" (the amphibian) for the act of searching for them. 源于“frog”（两栖动物），指钩子拉出针迹时的跳跃动作，以及“frog”（两栖动物），指寻找它们的行为。
