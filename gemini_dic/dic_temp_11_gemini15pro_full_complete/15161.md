back-projection:/ˌbæk prəˈdʒekʃən/ | n. | 反投影，逆投影 | The process of projecting an image onto a surface from behind, often used in cinematography and medical imaging.  It involves reconstructing an image from its projections. | The film used back-projection to create the illusion of the actors driving a car. 这部电影使用了反投影技术来制造演员们开车的错觉。

Back-projection was commonly used in older films to simulate outdoor scenes. 在老电影中，反投影通常被用来模拟户外场景。| synonyms: back projection, image reconstruction | antonyms:  N/A | noun
