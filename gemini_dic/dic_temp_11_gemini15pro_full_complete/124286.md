mother:/ˈmʌðər/ (US) /ˈmʌðə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|养育；像母亲般关怀|To care for someone as a mother would.|例句：She mothered the orphaned kittens. 她像母亲一样照顾这些孤儿小猫。|近义词：nurture, care for|反义词:neglect|verb
