heisenberg:/ˈhaɪzənbɜːrɡ/
n.
海森堡, 海森堡不确定性原理
Werner Karl Heisenberg, a German theoretical physicist known for the Heisenberg uncertainty principle.
Heisenberg's uncertainty principle states that the position and the velocity of an object cannot both be measured exactly, at the same time. 海森堡不确定性原理指出，一个物体的速度和位置不可能同时被精确测量。
The lecture covered Heisenberg's contributions to quantum mechanics.  讲座涵盖了海森堡对量子力学的贡献。
近义词: physicist (物理学家)

词性: noun (名词)
