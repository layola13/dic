intraterritorial:/ˌɪntrəˌterɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
区域内的，领土内的
Existing or occurring within a territory, especially within the territorial limits of a given state or country.
The intraterritorial trade regulations are complex.  区域内的贸易法规很复杂。
The government imposed intraterritorial restrictions on the movement of goods. 政府对货物移动施加了区域内的限制。
近义词:domestic, internal, within
反义词:extraterritorial, international, external
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From intra- (within) + territorial (relating to territory).  "Intra-" comes from Latin, meaning "within," and "territorial" comes from the Late Latin "territorium," meaning "territory."
