orchestrate:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪt/ |v. |精心安排；策划；(尤指)幕后操纵 |to carefully organize or plan something, especially something complicated, so that it works successfully| The politician orchestrated a smear campaign against his rival.  这位政治家精心策划了一场针对其竞争对手的诽谤运动。|  The general orchestrated a complex military operation. 将军精心策划了一次复杂的军事行动。| synonyms: arrange, organize, coordinate, manage, stage-manage, mastermind, engineer, contrive, devise, plan, plot, scheme, maneuver, manipulate | antonyms:  improvise, wing it | verb
