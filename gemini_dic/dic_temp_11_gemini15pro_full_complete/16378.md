例句：The city's barbican was once a formidable obstacle. 这座城市的外围工事曾经是一道难以逾越的障碍。|近义词：fortification, rampart, bulwark, bastion, outwork|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "weak point" or "vulnerability")|词性: noun
