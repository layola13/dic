incongenial:/ˌɪnkənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 不合的；不相宜的；令人不愉快的 | Not agreeable, suitable, or pleasant; not compatible. |  The work environment was incongenial to his artistic temperament.  工作环境与他的艺术气质格格不入。| 近义词:unpleasant, disagreeable, unsuitable, incompatible, uncongenial | 反义词: congenial, agreeable, pleasant, suitable, compatible | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + congenial (agreeable, having the same nature), from Latin com- (together) + genius (guardian spirit, innate character).
