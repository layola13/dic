co-sleeping:/ˈkoʊ sliːpɪŋ/| n. |共同睡眠，同床共枕|The practice of sleeping in the same bed with another person, typically a parent with a child.|例句：Co-sleeping can be a controversial topic. 同床共枕可能是一个有争议的话题。|近义词：bed-sharing|反义词：solitary sleep|词性：名词
