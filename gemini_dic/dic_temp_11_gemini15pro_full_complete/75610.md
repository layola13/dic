fugacity:/fjuːˈɡæsɪti/| n.|逸度，逃逸性|The fugacity of a gas is a measure of its tendency to escape from a solution.|The fugacity of carbon dioxide in seawater is an important factor in ocean acidification. 海水中二氧化碳的逸度是海洋酸化的一個重要因素。|Synonyms: volatility, escaping tendency|Antonyms: stability, inertness|noun
