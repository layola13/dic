overdiversified: /ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度多元化的 |  Having too much diversity, especially in investments, to the point where it becomes detrimental.  This often leads to increased complexity, higher costs, and diluted returns. |  The company's portfolio was overdiversified, spreading their resources too thin and hindering overall growth.  该公司投资组合过度多元化，资源分散，阻碍了整体增长。 | synonyms: overextended, spread too thin | antonyms: focused, concentrated, specialized | adjective
