burred:/bɜːrd/ /bɝːd/| adj.| 模糊的，(声音)低沉不清的，(边缘)不清晰的|  Having rough or prickly edges, especially of a plant or seed; indistinct or unclear.| The photo was blurred because the camera moved. |照片模糊不清，因为相机动了。|近义词：fuzzy, indistinct, unclear, muffled (for sound)|反义词：sharp, clear, distinct, crisp|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *burren, from Old English *burrian (“to push forth buds, put forth prickly burs”), from Proto-Germanic *burzijaną (“to bristle”), from *burz- (“bristle, spine”). Cognate with Dutch burren (“to stick out, stand on end”), German burren (“to stick out”). Related to bur.
