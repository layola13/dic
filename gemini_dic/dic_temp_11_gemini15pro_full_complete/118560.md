mesopodium:/ˌmesəˈpoʊdiəm/
noun
中足，间足
The mesopodium is the middle region of a limb or appendage, located between the propodium (proximal part) and the metapodium (distal part).
The horse's mesopodium consists of the carpal bones in the forelimb and the tarsal bones in the hindlimb. 马的中足由前肢的腕骨和后肢的跗骨组成。
The mesopodium of a bird's wing includes the radius and ulna. 鸟翅膀的中足包括桡骨和尺骨。
mid-limb, middle part of a limb
propodium, metapodium
