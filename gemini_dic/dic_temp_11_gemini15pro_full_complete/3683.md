aggravate:/ˈæɡrəveɪt/
verb
加剧，恶化
To make a problem or situation worse.
The humid weather aggravated her eczema. 潮湿的天气加剧了她的湿疹。
worsen, exacerbate, intensify
alleviate, improve
v.


aggravator:/ˈæɡrəveɪtər/
noun
加重者，加剧者
A person or thing that makes a problem or situation worse.
Stress is a major aggravator of heart disease. 压力是心脏病的主要加重因素。
worsening factor, exacerbating factor
alleviating factor, improving factor
n.

vet:/vɛt/
noun
兽医
A doctor who treats animals.
We took our sick dog to the vet. 我们把生病的狗带去看兽医。
veterinarian
n.

vet:/vɛt/
verb
审查，检查
To examine something or someone carefully to make sure that they are acceptable or suitable.
The committee will vet the applicants. 委员会将审查申请人。
examine, scrutinize, check
n./v.
