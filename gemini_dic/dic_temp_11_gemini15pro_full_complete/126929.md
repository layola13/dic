narkiest:/ˈnɑːrkiɪst/| adj.|最爱告密的，最爱抱怨的|Most inclined to inform on others or complain, especially about petty offenses.|例句：He's the narkiest person I know; he'll tell the teacher about anything. 他是我认识的最爱告密的人；他会把任何事都告诉老师。

例句：She's always so narkiest about the smallest things. 她总是对最小的事情都抱怨不停。|近义词：whiny, complaining, gossipy, tattling|反义词：tolerant, forgiving, easygoing|词性:形容词

nark:/nɑːrk/| n. vi. vt.|告密者，告发；向…告密|A person who informs on others, especially to the police; to inform on someone.|例句：He narked on his classmates for cheating on the test. 他向老师告发他的同学考试作弊。

例句：Don't be a nark; just mind your own business.  别做告密者；管好你自己的事。|近义词：informer, snitch, rat|反义词：protector, defender|词性:名词，动词
