electrophorus:/ɪˈlɛktrəfɔːrəs/
n.|起电盘|A device used to generate static electricity, typically consisting of a dielectric plate and a metal plate with an insulated handle.|The physics teacher used an electrophorus to demonstrate static electricity.|electroscope, capacitor, generator| N/A |noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἤλεκτρον (ḗlektron, “amber”) + -φόρος (-phóros, “-bearing”).
