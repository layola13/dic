inholder:/ˈɪnhəʊldər/ (US) /ˈɪnhəʊldər/ (UK)| n.|旅店老板，客栈老板|A person who owns or manages an inn.|例句：The inholder welcomed the weary travelers with a warm fire and a hearty meal.  客栈老板以温暖的炉火和丰盛的餐点迎接疲惫的旅客。

例句：The inholder's hospitality was renowned throughout the region.  这位旅店老板的好客远近闻名。|近义词：innkeeper, publican, hotelier|反义词: guest, lodger|词性:noun
词源: From Middle English *inhaldere, equivalent to inn +‎ holder.
