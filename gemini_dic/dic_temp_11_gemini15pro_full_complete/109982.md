libertine:/ˈlɪbərˌtiːn/ | n. | 放荡不羁的人；浪荡子 | a person, especially a man, who behaves without moral principles or a sense of responsibility, especially in sexual matters. |  The aging libertine finally settled down and got married.  这个老浪荡子最终安定下来结婚了。 | synonyms: debauchee, rake, profligate, reprobate, dissolute person | antonyms: prude, ascetic, puritan | noun

libertine:/ˈlɪbərˌtiːn/ | adj. | 放荡的；浪荡的 |  characterized by a disregard of morality, especially in sexual matters. | He lived a libertine lifestyle, indulging in every excess. 他过着放荡的生活，沉溺于各种放纵。 | synonyms: dissolute, debauched, profligate, licentious, immoral | antonyms: moral, chaste, virtuous, upright | adjective
