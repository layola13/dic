equiradical:/ˌiːkwɪˈrædɪkəl/ | adj. | 等根的；同根的；根次相同的 | Having the same root or radical.  Specifically in mathematics, having the same index or degree of a root. | The equations x² - 4 = 0 and x² - 9 = 0 are equiradical because they both involve square roots. 这两个方程式 x² - 4 = 0 和 x² - 9 = 0 是等根的，因为它们都涉及平方根。 | Synonyms:  similar, like, analogous | Antonyms: dissimilar, unlike, different | adjective
