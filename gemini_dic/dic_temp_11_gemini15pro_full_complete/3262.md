affirmably:/əˈfɜːməbli/
adverb
可肯定地，断然地
in a way that can be stated positively.
The existence of extraterrestrial life cannot be affirmably stated.  外星生命的存在无法断言。
The witness affirmably identified the suspect. 目击者肯定地指认了嫌疑人。
近义词:definitely, positively, assertively, categorically
反义词:doubtfully, uncertainly, ambiguously
词性:adverb

词源: From affirm (v.) + -ably (adv. suffix).  Affirm comes from Old French afermer and ultimately from Latin affirmare, from ad- "to" + firmare "to strengthen".
