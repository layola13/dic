non-leveraged:/ˌnɒnˈliːvərɪdʒd/ /ˌnɑːnˈlevərɪdʒd/| adj.|无杠杆的，无负债经营的|Not using borrowed capital or debt.|Example:  A non-leveraged buyout is financed entirely by equity. 一项无杠杆收购完全由股权融资。

Example: They prefer a non-leveraged investment strategy. 他们更喜欢无杠杆投资策略。|Synonyms: debt-free, unleveraged, ungeared|Antonyms: leveraged, geared|Adjective
