disarmer:/dɪsˈɑːrmər/
n.|解除武装者；使人消气的人；令人消除敌意的事物|something that removes or reduces hostility or suspicion|Her smile was a total disarmer.|近义词:pacifier, conciliator, charmer|反义词:agitator, provocateur|词性:名词

disarm:/dɪsˈɑːrm/
v.|解除武装；使息怒；消除敌意；使人倾倒；|to take weapons away from someone; to make someone feel less angry or hostile; to charm someone|Her charm disarmed all her critics.|近义词:neutralize, appease, captivate|反义词:arm, provoke, antagonize|词性:动词
