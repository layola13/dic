marquessates:/ˈmɑːkwɪsɪts/ | n. |侯爵爵位；侯爵领地|The territory or rank of a marquess.  A marquessate is a region ruled by a marquess.| He inherited the marquessate from his father.| 近义词: marquisate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Old French *marquesat, from marques (“marquess”) + -at (suffix forming nouns denoting office, dignity, or jurisdiction), ultimately from Frankish *marka (“frontier”).
