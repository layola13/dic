mousetrap:/ˈmaʊsˌtræp/| n.|捕鼠器；圈套|A device for catching mice, or figuratively, a tricky situation.|His business proposal seemed like a mousetrap. 他的商业提案像是一个圈套。|Near synonyms: snare, trap, gin|Antonyms: freedom, liberation|Noun
