as a matter of fact:/æz ə ˈmætər əv fækt/
adverbial phrase|事实上，其实，说真的|Used to introduce a statement that contradicts something previously said or implied, or adds to something already mentioned.|例句：I thought he was from Canada, but as a matter of fact, he's American. 我以为他来自加拿大，但事实上他是美国人。|近义词：actually, in fact, in reality, to tell the truth|反义词:theoretically, hypothetically|词性:adverbial phrase
