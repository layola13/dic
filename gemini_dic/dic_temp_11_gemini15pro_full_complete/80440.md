gondwanas:/ɡɒndˈwɑːnəz/ |n.|冈瓦纳大陆（复数）| Gondwanas refers to Gondwana, a supercontinent that existed from the Neoproterozoic (about 550 million years ago) until the Jurassic (about 180 million years ago).  It is often used in the plural in geological contexts when discussing different stages or fragments of the landmass. |The breakup of Gondwanas led to the formation of several continents. 冈瓦纳大陆的分裂导致了几个大陆的形成。| The flora and fauna of Gondwanas were unique. 冈瓦纳大陆的动植物是独一无二的。 | synonym: Gondwana, supercontinent | antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially Laurasia, another ancient supercontinent) | noun
