drearisome:/ˈdrɪəriːsʌm/| adj.| 沉闷的，阴郁的，令人沮丧的 | Characterized by or causing gloom or depression; dreary. | The drearisome weather made us all feel melancholic.  阴沉沉的天气让我们都感到忧郁。| 近义词：dreary, gloomy, depressing, dismal, bleak | 反义词：cheerful, bright, lively, exciting | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From *dreary* + *-some*.  Dreary itself comes from Old English *dreorig* "sad, mournful," related to *dreor* "gore, blood," suggesting the original sense was "bloody," hence "cruel, grievous." The suffix *-some* forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "tending to."
