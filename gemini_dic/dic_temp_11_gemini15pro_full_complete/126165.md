muttoned:/ˈmʌtənd/ | adj. | 沉默的，缄默的；（尤指绵羊或山羊）阉割的 |  Reduced to silence; silenced.  Also, castrated (especially of sheep or goats). | The boy remained muttoned throughout the interrogation.  这个男孩在整个审讯过程中都保持沉默。|  The farmer had his flock of sheep muttoned. 这位农民阉割了他的羊群。 | 近义词: silent, speechless, dumb, mute | 反义词: talkative, loquacious, vocal | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the past participle of *mutton*, used as a verb meaning "to silence" or "to castrate."  The verb "mutton" itself is likely derived from the noun "mutton" (sheep meat), perhaps with a sense of rendering the animal silent or docile.
