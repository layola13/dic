addressee's:/ˌædrɛˈsiːz/
noun
收件人的
belonging to the addressee; of the addressee.  The possessive form of "addressee," indicating ownership or relation.
The addressee's name was clearly printed on the envelope.  信封上清楚地印着收件人的名字。
Synonyms: recipient's, receiver's
Antonyms: sender's, addresser's
