christlikeness:/ˈkraɪstˌlaɪknəs/
n.
基督般的品格，像基督一样
The quality of resembling Christ in character or spirit.
She radiated a christlikeness that inspired everyone around her. 她散发着基督般的气质，激励着周围的每一个人。
Near Synonyms: piety, holiness, godliness, devoutness, saintliness
Antonyms: ungodliness, wickedness, impiety, sinfulness
