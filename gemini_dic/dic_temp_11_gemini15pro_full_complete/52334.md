dichotomous: /daɪˈkɑːtəməs/ | adj. | 二分的，二叉的 | Divided or dividing into two sharply distinguished parts or classifications. |  The survey presented a dichotomous choice: agree or disagree.  该调查提出了一个二分法的选择：同意或不同意。 | Example: The plant exhibits dichotomous branching.  这种植物呈现出二叉分枝。| Synonyms: binary, bifurcate, forked | Antonyms: continuous, unified, blended | Adjective
