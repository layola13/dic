decentralization:/ˌdiːsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 分散化，去中心化 | The process of distributing power or control away from a central authority. |  The company is undergoing a period of decentralization, giving more autonomy to regional offices.  这家公司正在经历一个权力下放的时期，赋予区域办事处更大的自主权。

The decentralization of power led to more efficient local governance. 权力下放导致地方治理更加高效。 | synonyms: distribution, dispersal, diffusion | antonyms: centralization, consolidation | noun
