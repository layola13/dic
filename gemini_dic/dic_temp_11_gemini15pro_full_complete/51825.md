dewinesses:/ˈdjuːɪnəsɪz/
n.
露水般的状态; 清新湿润
The state or quality of being dewy; freshness and moistness.
The dewinesses of the morning air filled her lungs.  清晨空气中露水般的清新湿润充满了她的肺。
近义词: freshness, moisture, dampness
反义词: dryness, aridity
词性: 名词

dewy:/ˈdjuːi/
adj.
露水般的; 带有露水的; 清新湿润的
Covered with or resembling dew; fresh and moist.
The dewy grass glistened in the early morning sun.  沾满露水的草地在清晨的阳光下闪闪发光。
Her dewy complexion made her look youthful. 她露水般的肤色使她看起来很年轻。
近义词: moist, damp, fresh
反义词: dry, arid
词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *dewy, from Old English dēawig (“dewy”), equivalent to dew + -y. Cognate with West Frisian dauwich (“dewy”), Dutch dauwig (“dewy”), German tauig (“dewy”).
