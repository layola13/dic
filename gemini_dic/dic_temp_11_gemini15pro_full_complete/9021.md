antimalarial:/ˌæntiməˈleriəl/| n.|抗疟药|A drug used to prevent or cure malaria.|例句：He took antimalarials throughout his stay in the tropics. 他在热带地区逗留期间一直服用抗疟疾药物。|近义词：anti-malaria medication, antimalarial drug|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + malarial.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to". "Malarial" comes from "malaria," ultimately from Italian "mala aria" meaning "bad air".
