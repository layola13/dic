amarant:/ˈæmərænt/ | n. adj.|苋菜，不凋谢的| An herbaceous plant of the genus Amaranthus having dense green, red, or purplish flowers, several kinds of which are cultivated as pot herbs, cereals, or ornamentals. Also, an imaginary flower that never fades. | The poet described an amarant that symbolized eternal love. 诗人描述了一种象征永恒爱情的苋菜花。| 近义词：unfading, everlasting, immortal, pigweed| 反义词：fading, transient, ephemeral |词性: 名词, 形容词
