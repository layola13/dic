indeterminatenesses:/ɪnˌdɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪtnɪsɪz/

A plurality of indeterminate things or factors.|Due to the indeterminatenesses of the situation, we couldn't make a decision. 由于情况的不确定性，我们无法做出决定。|The future is full of indeterminatenesses, making planning difficult. 未来充满了不确定性，使得规划变得困难。|Synonym: uncertainties, ambiguities, vagueness|Antonym: certainties, precision, clarity|noun
