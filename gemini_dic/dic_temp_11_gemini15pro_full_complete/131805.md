nonconsensual:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɛnʃuəl/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsɛnʃuəl/| adj.|未经同意的，非自愿的|Not agreed to or done with consent; forced or coerced.|例句：She reported a nonconsensual sexual encounter to the police. 她向警方报告了一起未经她同意的性接触。|例句：The video depicted a nonconsensual kiss, sparking outrage online.  视频中描绘了一个未经同意的吻，在网上引发了愤怒。|近义词：forced, coerced, involuntary, against one's will|反义词：consensual, voluntary, agreed upon|词性:adjective

nonconsensual:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɛnʃuəl/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsɛnʃuəl/| n.|未经同意的事情|An instance of something not agreed to or done with consent.|例句：The lawyer argued that the act constituted a nonconsensual taking of property.  律师辩称，该行为构成未经同意地侵占财产。|例句：Several nonconsensuals were reported to the authorities.  一些未经同意的事情被报告给了当局。|近义词：violation, infringement, encroachment|反义词：agreement, consent, accord|词性:noun (rarely used)
