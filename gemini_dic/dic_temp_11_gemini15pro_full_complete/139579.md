obviate:/ˈɒbviˌeɪt/ (US) /ˈɒbvieɪt/ (UK)| vt.|排除；避免；使不必要|To remove a difficulty, especially so that action to deal with it becomes unnecessary.|The new medical treatment obviates the need for surgery.  这种新的治疗方法避免了手术的需要。|

The invention of the smartphone obviated the need for separate cameras, music players, and GPS devices. 智能手机的发明使单独的相机、音乐播放器和GPS设备变得不必要。

|Synonyms: prevent, preclude, avert, forestall|Antonyms: necessitate, require, cause, create|Verb|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin obviāre (“to meet, withstand, prevent”), from ob (“against, in the way of”) + viāre (“to go, travel”), from via (“way, road”).
