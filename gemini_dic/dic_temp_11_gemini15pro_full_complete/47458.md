deal with:/diːl wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 处理，应付，涉及 | to take action to solve a problem; to handle or manage a situation or person; to be about a particular subject |  The book deals with the issue of climate change. 这本书论述了气候变化的问题。| synonyms: handle, manage, address, cope with | antonyms: ignore, avoid, neglect | verb phrase

deal with:/diːl wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 与…打交道；与…做生意 | to interact or do business with someone | I don't like dealing with difficult customers. 我不喜欢与难缠的顾客打交道。 | synonyms: interact with, transact with, negotiate with | antonyms: avoid, shun | verb phrase
