clockwork:/ˈklɒkwɜːk/ (UK) /ˈklɑːkwɜːrk/ (US)| n. adj.|发条装置；钟表机构；精密的；规律的|A mechanism, especially of a clock or toy, that operates by means of a spring and a series of cogs.  Figuratively, it refers to something that is regular, predictable, or precise like a clock.|The toy soldier marched across the table, powered by clockwork.  这个玩具士兵在发条装置的驱动下走过桌子。|Synonyms: mechanism, machinery, device, apparatus, system; (adj.) regular, precise, predictable, methodical, systematic|Antonyms: (adj.) erratic, unpredictable, random, chaotic|noun, adjective
