congealedness:/kənˈdʒiːldnɪs/
n.
凝固；凝结
The state of being congealed; thickness, solidity.
The congealedness of the gravy made it difficult to pour. 肉汁凝固了，很难倒出来。
coagulation, solidification, thickness
fluidity, liquidity
noun
