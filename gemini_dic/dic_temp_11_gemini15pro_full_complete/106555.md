krooman:/ˈkruːmən/| n. |苦力;搬运工|A derogatory term for a South Asian or East Asian laborer, particularly one employed in manual labor during the colonial era.  Derived from the Tamil word "kurumban," referring to a member of a tribal group in southern India.|The old docks were full of kroomen unloading cargo from the ships.  老码头上满是苦力在从船上卸货。|Synonyms: coolie, laborer, dockworker|Antonyms: employer, manager|noun
