hemimetabolic:/ˌhɛmiˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk/
adjective
半变态的
Undergoing incomplete metamorphosis, in which the immature stages (nymphs) resemble the adult form.
例句：Grasshoppers are hemimetabolic insects. 蚱蜢是半变态昆虫。

例句：The hemimetabolic development of cockroaches involves several nymphal stages. 蟑螂的半变态发育包括几个若虫阶段。


近义词：paurometabolous
反义词：holometabolic
词性: adjective
