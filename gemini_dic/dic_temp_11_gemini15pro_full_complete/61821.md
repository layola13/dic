empirical:/ɪmˈpɪrɪkl/ /ɛmˈpɪrɪkl/ | adj. | 经验主义的；以经验为依据的 | Based on observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic. |  例句：Empirical evidence suggests that regular exercise can improve cardiovascular health.  经验表明，经常锻炼可以改善心血管健康。

例句：The researchers conducted an empirical study to test their hypothesis. 研究人员进行了一项实证研究来检验他们的假设。

近义词：observed, experimental, practical, factual, pragmatic | 反义词：theoretical, hypothetical, conjectural, speculative | 词性：adjective
