ambassadrix: /æmˈbæsədrɪks/
noun
女大使；女代表
A female ambassador or representative.
The ambassadrix negotiated a peace treaty between the two warring nations. 女大使在两个交战国之间谈判达成了和平条约。
近义词: ambassador, envoy, representative, delegate
反义词: None (This word emphasizes the female gender of the role, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun

词源:
The word "ambassadrix" is a feminized form of "ambassador." "Ambassador" comes from the Old French word "ambassadeur," ultimately derived from the Late Latin "ambactiator," meaning "messenger" or "servant."  The "-trix" suffix is a feminine agentive suffix, used to form nouns denoting female agents. It has Latin origins.
