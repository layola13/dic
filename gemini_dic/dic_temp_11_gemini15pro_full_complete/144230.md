outslide: /ˈaʊtslaɪd/
verb
向外滑动；滑出
to slide out or cause something to slide out.
The drawer outslid smoothly.  抽屉平稳地滑了出来。
The file outslid from the cabinet. 文件从柜子里滑了出来。
近义词: slide out, eject, extend
反义词: slide in, retract
词性: verb
