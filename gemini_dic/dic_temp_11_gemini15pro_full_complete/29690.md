cathetus: /kəˈθiːtəs/ | n. | 垂线；直角三角形的直角边 |A cathetus (plural: catheti) is either of the two shorter sides of a right triangle, or, in an oblique triangle, the altitude on any side.  | The theorem states that the square of the hypotenuse is equal to the sum of the squares of the two catheti.  该定理指出，斜边的平方等于两条直角边的平方和。|  The length of one cathetus is 3 cm and the other is 4 cm. 一条直角边的长度是3厘米，另一条是4厘米。 | synonym: leg, side | antonym: hypotenuse | noun
