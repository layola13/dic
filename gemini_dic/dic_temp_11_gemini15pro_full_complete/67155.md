ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English exposicioun, from Old French exposition, from Latin expositiō (“a setting forth, explanation”), from expositus, past participle of expōnō (“I set forth, explain”), from ex- (“out”) + pōnō (“I put, place”).
