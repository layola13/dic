accredits:/əˈkrɛdɪts/
verb
授权；鉴定；委派；正式认可
officially recognizes someone or something as having a particular status or being qualified to perform a particular activity
The agency accredits schools that meet its standards.  该机构认可符合其标准的学校。
synonyms: authorize, certify, endorse, license, recognize, validate
antonyms: disqualify, reject, revoke
词性: 动词
