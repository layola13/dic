Habsburgs:/ˈhæpsbɜːrɡz/
proper noun
哈布斯堡家族
A royal German family that provided rulers for several European states from the 15th to the 20th century.
The Habsburgs ruled Austria for over six centuries. 哈布斯堡王朝统治奥地利超过六个世纪。
近义词: House of Habsburg
反义词: N/A
词性: proper noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Habsburg*, from the name of their original castle, *Habichtsburg* ("Hawk's Castle"), in Switzerland.
