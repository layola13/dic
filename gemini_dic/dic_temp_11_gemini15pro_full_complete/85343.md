harpylike:/ˈhɑːrpiˌlaɪk/| adj.|像鸟身女妖的，凶恶的，贪婪的|Resembling or characteristic of a harpy, especially in being predatory, cruel, or rapacious.|Her harpylike laughter echoed through the halls. 她的笑声像鸟身女妖一样回荡在大厅里。|The CEO's harpylike demands for increased profits left the employees demoralized.  这位CEO对利润增长的贪婪要求让员工们士气低落。|近义词：vicious, rapacious, ravenous, predatory, greedy|反义词：kind, benevolent, generous|词性:形容词

词源: From harpy + -like.  Harpy comes from the Latin "harpyia," from the Greek "harpyia," meaning "snatchers," plural of "harpyx," from "harpazein," meaning "to snatch."
