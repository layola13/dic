methought:/məˈθɔːt/
verb (past tense, archaic)
我以为；我料想
It seemed to me; I thought.
例句：Methought I saw my late father in my dream. 我梦见我已故的父亲。

例句：Methought the lady protested too much. 我觉得这位女士抗议得太过分了。

近义词：I thought, it seemed to me
反义词：I knew, I was certain
词性：动词
