nocuous:/ˈnɒkjuəs/ | adj. | 无害的，无毒的 | Not harmful or poisonous; harmless. |  The herbs used in the tea are completely nocuous.  这种茶里使用的草药是完全无害的。|  Example: The spider, despite its alarming appearance, is actually nocuous. 这只蜘蛛虽然外表吓人，但实际上是无害的。 | Synonyms: harmless, safe, innocuous, inoffensive | Antonyms: harmful, noxious, poisonous, toxic, dangerous | adjective

词源:  From Latin nocuus (“harmful”), from nocēre (“to harm”).  The English word acquired the opposite meaning through use in ironic contexts. 来自拉丁语 nocuus（“有害的”），来自 nocēre（“伤害”）。英语单词通过在讽刺语境中的使用获得了相反的含义。
