lily-white:/ˈlɪli waɪt/| adj.|纯白的；洁白的；（尤指）苍白的；（比喻）清白的，无瑕疵的|Of a pure white color; (especially) pale; (figuratively) pure, unblemished.  The term carries connotations of innocence, sometimes artificial or affected, and historically has been associated with racial purity in a derogatory context.|Her skin was lily-white, a stark contrast to her dark hair. 她的皮肤洁白无瑕，与她乌黑的头发形成鲜明对比。

The candidate tried to present a lily-white image, denying any past wrongdoings.  这位候选人试图展现出一个清白的形象，否认任何过去的不当行为。|近义词：pure white, snow-white, spotless, immaculate|反义词：dirty, stained, tainted, impure|词性:adjective
