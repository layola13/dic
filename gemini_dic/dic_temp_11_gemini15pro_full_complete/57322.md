downtrend:/ˈdaʊntrend/| n.|下降趋势；低迷的趋势|A downward trend, as in prices, sales, etc.  在价格、销售额等方面的下降趋势。|The stock market has been in a downtrend for the past six months. 股市在过去六个月里一直处于下跌趋势。|近义词：decline, decrease, slump, downturn|反义词:uptrend, increase, rise|词性:名词

Etymology:  "down-" + "trend". "Down-" indicates a downward direction or movement, and "trend" refers to a general direction in which something is developing or changing.
