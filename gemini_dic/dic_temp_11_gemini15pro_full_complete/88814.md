hog-like:/ˈhɔːɡˌlaɪk/| adj.|像猪一样的；贪婪的；肮脏的|Resembling a hog in appearance, behavior, or habits; greedy; filthy.|例句：The man had a hog-like appetite, devouring everything on the table.  这个人像猪一样贪吃，吞掉了桌上所有的东西。

例句：His hog-like manners disgusted everyone at the dinner party. 他像猪一样的举止让宴会上每个人都感到恶心。|近义词：piggish, swinish, gluttonous, greedy, filthy|反义词：refined, dainty, abstemious, clean|词性:形容词
