delusory:/dɪˈluːsəri/ /dɪˈluːsɔːri/| adj.| 欺骗性的；虚假的；迷惑的 |Based on or producing illusion; deceptive.|  The oasis in the desert proved to be delusory. | 近义词: deceptive, misleading, fallacious, illusory, spurious | 反义词: genuine, real, true, authentic | 词性: adjective

Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *delusorius, from Latin delus- ‘mocked’, from the verb deludere.
