hutong:/ˈhuːtɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈhuːtɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|胡同|A narrow lane or alleyway, typically found in traditional residential areas of Chinese cities, especially Beijing.|We strolled through the charming hutongs of Beijing. 我们漫步在北京迷人的胡同里。|Synonyms: alleyway, lane, byway|Antonyms: avenue, boulevard|noun
