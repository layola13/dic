funnier:/ˈfʌniər/ | adj. | 更滑稽的，更有趣的 |Comparative form of "funny." Used to describe something that is more humorous or amusing than something else. | That joke was funnier than the last one. 那个笑话比上一个更有趣。|  His impression of the teacher was even funnier. 他模仿老师的样子更滑稽了。 | 近义词: more amusing, more humorous, more comical | 反义词: less funny, more serious, less amusing | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *funni*, from *fun* (pleasure, amusement) + *-y* (adjective suffix). The comparative form *-er* is a standard Germanic suffix.
