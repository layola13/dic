excessing:/ɪkˈsɛsɪŋ/
verb
过度；过量；超过
going beyond the limits or exceeding what is necessary or desirable
The company was excessing its inventory. 公司库存过剩。
近义词: exceeding, surpassing, overdoing
反义词: lacking, insufficient, moderate
词性: v.
