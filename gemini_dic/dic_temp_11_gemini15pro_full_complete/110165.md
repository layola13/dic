liegest:/ˈliːdʒɪst/
adjective
最忠诚的；最忠实的 (古语，已废弃)
Most loyal or faithful.
例句：The liegest of his followers remained by his side. 他最忠实的追随者仍然在他身边。
例句：He swore to be the king's liegest subject. 他发誓要做国王最忠诚的臣民。
近义词：loyal, faithful, devoted, steadfast
反义词：disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous
词性：adjective

词源:
From Middle English *legest, *leigest, from Anglo-Norman lige, leige (“liege, sovereign, vassal”), ultimately from Frankish *letig (“free from obligation, released from service”), from Proto-Germanic *lētijaz (“released”), from *lētaną (“to let, allow”).
