hands-on:/ˌhændzˈɒn/ /ˌhændzˈɑːn/ | adj. |亲自动手的，实际操作的 | involving or requiring personal participation; characterized by active involvement |  This workshop provides hands-on experience in software development.  这个研讨会提供软件开发的实践经验。| practical, active, involved | theoretical, passive, uninvolved | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "hands on" literally meaning having one's hands physically on something, implying direct involvement.  The hyphenated form "hands-on" emerged as an adjective in the mid-20th century.
