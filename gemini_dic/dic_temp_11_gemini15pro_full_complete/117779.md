melek:/ˈmeɪlek/| n.|天使，信使|A supernatural being, often depicted with wings, believed to act as an intermediary between God and humanity.  A messenger or representative of God.|例句：The melek appeared in a dream. 天使出现在梦中。|例句：In some cultures, meleks are believed to be guardian spirits. 在某些文化中，人们相信天使是守护神。|近义词：angel, seraph, cherub|反义词：demon, devil|名词
