leaches:/ˈliːtʃɪz/| n.|水蛭; 榨取他人钱财者|Blood-sucking worms that live in water.  A person who extorts money from others. |The leeches were clinging to his legs after he swam in the pond. 他在池塘里游泳后，水蛭 clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clinging clung to his legs. |The landlord was accused of being a leech, constantly raising the rent. 房东被指责是吸血鬼，不断提高租金。|近义词：parasites, bloodsuckers, freeloaders, sponges|反义词: benefactor, donor|noun

leaches:/ˈliːtʃɪz/| v.| (水蛭)吸(血); 榨取; 滤掉|To drain (something) away gradually.  (Of a leech) to suck blood.|The chemicals leached into the groundwater. 化学物质渗入了地下水。|近义词：drain, seep, bleed, extort|反义词: replenish, nourish|verb
