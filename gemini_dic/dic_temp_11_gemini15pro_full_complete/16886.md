basine:/ˈbeɪsɪn/| n.|盆；脸盆；水池|A wide, shallow container used for washing, especially the face and hands.  A basin can also refer to a geographic area drained by a river and its tributaries.|She filled the basine with warm water. 她把盆里装满了温水。|Synonyms: bowl, sink, washbasin|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "mountain peak" in a geographical context)|noun
