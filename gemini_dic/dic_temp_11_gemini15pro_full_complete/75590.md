fuel:/ˈfjuːəl/ (US), /ˈfjuːl/ (UK)| v. |给…加燃料；刺激；<比喻>火上浇油|To supply or power (an engine, fire, etc.) with fuel; to sustain or stimulate (a feeling or activity).|The rumors fueled her anxiety. 这些谣言加剧了她的焦虑。|synonyms: power, energize, stimulate, feed, stoke |antonyms: extinguish, dampen, suppress|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feuaile, *fouaille "firewood, kindling," from Vulgar Latin *focalia, neuter plural of *focalis "of a hearth," from Latin focus "hearth, fireplace."
