accommodateness:/əˌkɒməˈdeɪtnəs/ | n. |随和；乐于助人；通融；适应性|The hotel staff were known for their accommodateness and willingness to help guests.|例句：Her accommodateness made her a popular colleague. 她的随和让她成为一位受欢迎的同事。

例句：The accommodateness of the furniture impressed the interior designer. 家具的适应性给室内设计师留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：amiability, obligingness, flexibility, adaptability |反义词：inflexibility, rigidity, unhelpfulness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From accommodate + -ness.  Accommodate comes from Late Latin accommodatus, past participle of accommodare "to fit, adapt," from ad- "to" + commodare "to make fit," from commodus "suitable, convenient," from com- "together" + modus "measure, manner."
