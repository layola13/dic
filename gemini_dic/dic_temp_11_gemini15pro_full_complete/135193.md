nonoriental:/ˌnɒnɔːriˈentl/
adjective
非东方的
Not of, from, or characteristic of the Orient.
例句：Nonoriental art often focuses on different themes than Oriental art.  非东方艺术的主题通常与东方艺术不同。

例句：She studied nonoriental philosophy for her dissertation. 她为了写论文研究了非西方哲学。

近义词：Western, Occidental
反义词：Oriental, Eastern
词性：adjective
