flexitime: /ˈfleksɪtaɪm/ | n. | 弹性工作时间 | A system of working a set number of hours with the starting and finishing times chosen within agreed limits by the employee. | Example 1: Flexitime allows me to avoid rush hour traffic. 弹性工作时间让我可以避开高峰时段的交通拥堵。

Example 2: The company offers flexitime to all its employees. 这家公司为所有员工提供弹性工作时间。| 近义词: flexible working hours, flextime | 反义词: fixed hours, nine-to-five | 词性: noun
