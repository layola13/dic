nonthinker:/ˌnɒnˈθɪŋkə(r)/ | n. |不思考者，不动脑筋的人|A person who does not think critically or creatively; someone who accepts information passively without questioning or analyzing it.|He dismissed his critics as mere nonthinkers, incapable of grasping the brilliance of his plan. 他把批评他的人斥为不动脑筋的人，认为他们无法理解他计划的精妙之处。| 近义词：conformist, follower, sheep| 反义词：thinker, intellectual, innovator |词性:名词
