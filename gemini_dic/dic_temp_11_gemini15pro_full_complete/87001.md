henotheism:/ˈhɛnəʊθiːɪzəm/ | n. | 唯一神崇拜;单一神崇拜 |The belief in and worship of a single god while accepting the existence or possible existence of other deities.|  Ancient Egyptians practiced a form of henotheism, focusing on a particular deity at different times and places while acknowledging the existence of other gods. 古埃及人实行一种唯一神崇拜的形式，在不同时间和地点专注于一个特定的神，同时承认其他神的存在。


Henotheism is different from monotheism, which posits the existence of only one god. 唯一神崇拜不同于一神教，一神教认为只有一个神存在。

|Synonyms: monolatry|Antonyms: polytheism, atheism|Noun|
