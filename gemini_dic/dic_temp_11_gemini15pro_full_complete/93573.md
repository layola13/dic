imago:/ɪˈmɑːɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪˈmeɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 成虫；意象；理想中的形象 |An imago is the final, adult, and sexually mature stage of an insect after metamorphosis.  An imago is also a mental image, especially of a loved person. | The butterfly emerged from its chrysalis as a beautiful imago. 蝴蝶从蛹中羽化成美丽的成虫。


She had an imago of her father as a strong and silent protector. 她心目中父亲的形象是一位坚强而沉默的保护者。 | synonyms: adult, mature form, image, likeness, impression, concept | antonyms: larva, pupa, immature form | noun
