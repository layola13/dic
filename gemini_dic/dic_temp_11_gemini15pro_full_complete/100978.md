invaginated:/ɪnˈvædʒɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
内陷的，套叠的
folded inward to form a cavity or pouch; sheathed.
The invaginated cell membrane formed a vesicle. 内陷的细胞膜形成了囊泡。

The surgeon repaired the patient's invaginated intestine.  外科医生修复了病人内陷的肠子。


近义词:inverted, recessed, introverted, tucked in
反义词:evaginated, protruded, extroverted


词性: adjective


词源: From Latin invaginatus, past participle of invaginare "to sheathe," from in- "in" + vagina "sheath."
