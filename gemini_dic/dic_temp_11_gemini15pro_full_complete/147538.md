overpay:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪ/ /ˌəʊvəˈpeɪ/ | vt. vi. n.| 多付，多给;超额付给;工资过高 | to pay someone too much money or more than is deserved | Example: I think I overpaid for that car. 我觉得我那辆车买贵了。 | Example:  Some athletes are ridiculously overpaid. 有些运动员的薪水高得离谱。 | Synonyms: overcompensate, reward excessively | Antonyms: underpay, shortchange | verb

overpay:/ˈoʊvərpeɪ/ /ˈəʊvəpeɪ/ | n.| 多付的钱 | an excessive payment | Example: The overpay was refunded to the customer. 多付的钱款已退还给顾客。 | Example:  There was a significant overpay in her last paycheck. 她上一张工资单上多付了一大笔钱。 | Synonyms: overpayment | Antonyms: underpayment | noun
