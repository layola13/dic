dressing-rooms:/ˈdrɛsɪŋ ruːmz/ /ˈdrɛsɪŋ rʊmz/
n.
更衣室；化妆室
A room, especially in a theatre or television studio, for changing clothes and applying make-up.
The actors were getting ready in their dressing-rooms.  演员们正在更衣室里做准备。
The star has a luxurious dressing-room backstage.  这位明星在后台有一个豪华的更衣室。
近义词：changing rooms, fitting rooms

词性：名词
