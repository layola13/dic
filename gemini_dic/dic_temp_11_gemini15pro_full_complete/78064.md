geochemical:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈkɛmɪkəl/| adj.|地球化学的|Relating to the chemical composition of the Earth and its rocks and minerals.|例句：Geochemical analysis revealed the presence of gold in the rock sample. 地球化学分析揭示了岩石样本中存在金。|例句：The geochemical properties of the soil affect the types of plants that can grow there. 土壤的地球化学性质影响了可以在那里生长的植物种类。|近义词：geological, mineralogical|反义词: None|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "geo-" (meaning earth) + "chemical". "Geo" comes from the Greek word "gē" (γῆ), meaning "earth, land, ground." "Chemical" derives ultimately from the Arabic word "al-kīmiyā’" (الكيمياء), meaning "alchemy."
