discernment:/dɪˈsɜːnmənt/ | n. |识别力，洞察力，辨别力|The ability to judge well; the ability to distinguish between truth and falsehood, right and wrong, or good and bad.|She showed great discernment in choosing her friends. 她择友很有眼光。|近义词: insight, perception, judgment, discrimination|反义词: naivety, blindness, ignorance|词性:名词

discernment:/dɪˈsɜːrnmənt/ | n. |识别；辨别；觉察|The act of recognizing or understanding something.|His discernment of the subtle differences in the wines was impressive. 他对葡萄酒中细微差别的辨别能力令人印象深刻。 |近义词: recognition, understanding, identification|反义词: confusion, misinterpretation|词性:名词
