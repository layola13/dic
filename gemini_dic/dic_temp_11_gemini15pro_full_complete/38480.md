conchite: /ˈkɒŋkaɪt/ (US) /ˈkɒŋtʃaɪt/ (UK)| n.| 贝壳岩，贝壳灰岩|A sedimentary rock formed by the cementation of shells or shell fragments.|The ancient city walls were constructed of conchite.|近义词：shell limestone, coquina|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider rocks formed without shells like granite or basalt) |词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *concha* (shell) + *-ite* (suffix forming names of rocks and minerals).
