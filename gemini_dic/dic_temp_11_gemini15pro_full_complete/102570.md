jaggy:/ˈdʒæɡi/| adj.|锯齿状的，参差不齐的|having sharp, pointed, irregular edges or teeth; unevenly notched or toothed|The coastline was jaggy and dramatic.  海岸线崎岖不平，景色壮观。|The torn piece of paper had a jaggy edge. 那张撕破的纸边缘参差不齐。|近义词：rough, uneven, serrated, ragged|反义词：smooth, even|词性:形容词

词源:
From the noun "jag" + "-y," a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."  "Jag" itself likely originated from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Old Norse "jǫggr," meaning a sharp point or barb.
