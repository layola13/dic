literalism:/ˈlɪtərəlɪzəm/| n. |字面主义，拘泥字句|Adherence to the exact letter or meaning, especially without regard to the intended spirit.|He interpreted the poem with a dull literalism. 他对这首诗的解释呆板而拘泥于字面。|近义词：literalness, exactness, precision|反义词：figurativeness, interpretation, symbolism|词性: noun
