moisturizing: /ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 保湿的，滋润的 |  Having the effect of making something less dry, especially skin. | This moisturizing cream keeps my skin soft.  这款保湿霜让我的皮肤保持柔软。 | synonyms: hydrating, emollient, softening | antonyms: drying, dehydrating | adjective


moisturize: /ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪz/ | v. |  滋润，保湿 | To make something less dry, especially skin. |  I moisturize my face every day. 我每天都给我的脸保湿。| synonyms: hydrate, soften | antonyms: dry, dehydrate | verb

Etymology:
From *moist* (adj.) + *-ize* (suffix forming verbs).  *Moist* comes from Old French *moiste*, ultimately from Latin *musteus* "fresh, new, young."
