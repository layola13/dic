gooiest:/ˈɡuːiɪst/| adj.|最粘的|Superlative form of gooey: most gooey.  It means extremely sticky, soft, and viscous.|The caramel was the gooiest I've ever tasted.  这种焦糖是我尝过最粘稠的。|The child's hands were gooiest after playing with slime.  这孩子玩了粘液后，手黏糊糊的。| 近义词: stickiest, tackiest, gummiest|反义词: driest, firmest|词性: 形容词
