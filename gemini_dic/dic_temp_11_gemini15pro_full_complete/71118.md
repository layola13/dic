fishful:/ˈfɪʃfʊl/
adjective
充满鱼的;鱼多的
Full of fish; abounding in fish.
The river was fishful, promising a good catch. 这条河里鱼很多，预示着会有好收成。
Near synonyms: teeming with fish, abounding with fish, rich in fish
Antonyms: fishless, barren of fish
词性: 形容词
