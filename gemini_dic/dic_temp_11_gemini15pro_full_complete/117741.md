melanochroite:/məˈlænəkrɔɪt/
noun
红铅矿，铬铅矿
A rare mineral consisting of lead chromate, typically occurring as bright red prismatic crystals.
Melanochroite is a rare and beautiful mineral sought after by collectors. 红铅矿是一种稀有而美丽的矿物，受到收藏家的追捧。
Melanochroite crystals can be found in oxidized lead deposits. 红铅矿晶体可以在氧化的铅矿床中找到。
Synonyms: chrome lead ore, phoenicochroite
Antonyms: N/A (lacks a direct opposite)
词性: noun
