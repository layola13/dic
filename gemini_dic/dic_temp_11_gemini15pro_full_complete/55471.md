distortion:/dɪˈstɔːrʃn/ | n. | 扭曲，变形；失真；曲解 |  A change in the shape, sound, or appearance of something so that it is strange or unclear. | The loud music caused a distortion of the sound.  这嘈杂的音乐导致了声音的失真。| synonyms: deformation, warping, contortion, perversion, misrepresentation | antonyms: accuracy, clarity, truth, precision | noun


distort:/dɪˈstɔːrt/ | vt. | 扭曲，变形；使失真；曲解 | To change the shape, sound, or appearance of something so that it is strange or unclear. |  The media distorted the facts. 媒体歪曲了事实。 | synonyms:  misrepresent, twist, falsify, warp, deform | antonyms: clarify, represent accurately, purify | verb
