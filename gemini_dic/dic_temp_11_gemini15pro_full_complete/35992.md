codevelop:/ˌkəʊdɪˈveləp/ | v. | 共同开发；合作研制 | To develop something jointly with another or others. |  Example: We are codeveloping a new software with a company in Japan. 我们正在与一家日本公司共同开发一款新软件。

Example:  They codeveloped a vaccine for the virus. 他们共同研制了一种病毒疫苗。

Synonym: collaborate, co-create, joint develop, partner | Antonym:  develop independently, solo develop | verb
