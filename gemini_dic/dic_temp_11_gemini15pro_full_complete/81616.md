grebes:/ɡriːbz/ | n. | 鸊鷉 | Any of various diving birds of the family Podicipedidae, having lobed toes, short wings, and a rudimentary tail.  | Example: We saw several grebes diving for fish on the lake. 我们看到几只鸊鷉在湖里潜水捕鱼。 | Example: The grebes built their nest right on the water. 鸊鷉就在水面上筑巢。 | Synonym: dabchick | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species) | noun
