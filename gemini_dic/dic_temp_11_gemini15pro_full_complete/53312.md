disablements:/dɪsˈeɪblmənts/
n.
残疾，缺陷，障碍
Conditions that limit a person's movements, senses, or activities.
Many elderly people suffer from disablements that restrict their mobility. 许多老年人患有残疾，限制了他们的行动能力。

The disablements caused by the accident made it impossible for him to work.  事故造成的残疾使他无法工作。

近义词:disabilities, impairments, handicaps
反义词:abilities, capabilities

词性:名词(plural noun)
