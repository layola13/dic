fortunateness:/ˈfɔːrtʃənətnəs/| n.|幸运，好运|The state of being fortunate; good fortune.|His fortunateness allowed him to escape the burning building. 他的幸运让他逃出了燃烧的建筑物。|近义词：luck, luckiness, felicity, serendipity|反义词：misfortune, unluckiness, adversity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fortunate + -ness.  Fortunate comes from Latin *fortunatus*, past participle of *fortunare* "to make fortunate," from *fortuna* "fortune, chance, luck."
