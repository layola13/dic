ladyfingers:/ˈleɪdiˌfɪŋɡərz/ | n. | 手指饼干；秋葵 | A small, oblong, sweet sponge cake or biscuit used in desserts such as tiramisu and charlottes. Also refers to the okra vegetable. |  Example: I need ladyfingers to make tiramisu. 我需要手指饼干来做提拉米苏。 | Example:  We grew ladyfingers in our garden this summer. 我们今年夏天在花园里种了秋葵。 | Synonym:  Savoiardi (for the cake), okra, gumbo | Antonym: N/A | Noun
