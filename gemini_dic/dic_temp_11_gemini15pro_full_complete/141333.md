opennesses:/ˈoʊpənɪsɪz/
n.
开放性；坦率；开阔
The quality of being open, receptive, or accessible.
Her openness about her struggles inspired others. 她坦诚地讲述自己的奋斗经历，激励了其他人。

opennesses:/ˈoʊpənɪsɪz/
n.
公开性；透明度
The quality of being honest and transparent.
The government promised openness in its dealings. 政府承诺在其交易中保持公开透明。

The quality of being spacious or expansive.
