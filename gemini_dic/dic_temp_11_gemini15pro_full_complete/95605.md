incommunicability:/ˌɪnkəˌmjuːnɪkəˈbɪləti/
n.|无法沟通，不善言辞|The state of being unable to communicate effectively.  The quality of being too complex or subtle to be communicated.|His incommunicability made it difficult to work with him. 他的不善言辞使得很难和他共事。|近义词：uncommunicativeness, aloofness, reticence, isolation|反义词：communicativeness, openness, expressiveness|词性:名词

incommunicable:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkəbl/
adj.|无法沟通的，难以表达的|Not able to be communicated to others.|His feelings were so deep and personal that they seemed incommunicable. 他的感受如此深刻和私人，以至于似乎无法表达。|近义词：inexpressible, unutterable, ineffable|反义词：communicable, expressible, utterable|词性:形容词
