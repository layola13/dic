maguey:/məˈɡeɪ/| n. |龙舌兰，剑麻|A succulent plant, native to Mexico, of the agave family, from which pulque, mescal, and tequila are made.  The leaves also yield a fiber used for rope and other products.|The maguey plant is essential to many Mexican communities. 龙舌兰对许多墨西哥社区至关重要。|Synonym: agave, century plant|Antonym: N/A|noun


maguey:/məˈɡeɪ/| n. |龙舌兰纤维|Fiber obtained from the leaves of the maguey plant.|Maguey fiber is incredibly strong and durable. 龙舌兰纤维非常结实耐用。|Synonym: agave fiber, sisal (sometimes, though sisal is specifically from Agave sisalana)|Antonym: N/A|noun
