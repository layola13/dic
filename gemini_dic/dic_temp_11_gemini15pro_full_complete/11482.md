arhizous:/eɪˈraɪzəs/ | adj. | 无根的，缺乏真根的 | Lacking true roots.  |  Example: Some parasitic plants are arhizous, obtaining nutrients directly from their host.  一些寄生植物是无根的，直接从宿主那里获取营养。

Example:  Arhizous fungi obtain nutrients through absorption. 无根真菌通过吸收获得营养。 | 近义词: rootless | 反义词: rhizomatous, rooted | 词性: adjective
