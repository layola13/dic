fictionalize:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪz/| v. |使小说化；虚构|To create a fictional account of something real; to make something into fiction.|例句：The movie fictionalizes the life of a famous singer.  这部电影将一位著名歌手的生活小说化了。

例句：He fictionalized his travel experiences in his latest novel. 他在他最新的小说中虚构了他的旅行经历。

|近义词：novelize, dramatize, invent, fabricate|反义词：document, report, factualize|词性：动词
