backlashers:/ˈbækˌlæʃərz/
n.
强烈反对者，抵制者
People who react negatively or with hostility to a social or political change.
The backlashers protested loudly against the new law. 抵制者大声抗议这项新法律。
opponents, dissenters, critics
supporters, advocates
