dug-up:/ˌdʌɡ ˈʌp/
adjective
挖出的；翻出的；被挖掘的
Unearthed or excavated; discovered after having been buried or hidden.
The archaeologists discovered a dug-up treasure chest. 考古学家发现了一个挖出来的宝箱。
synonyms: unearthed, excavated, exhumed, disinterred
antonyms: buried, hidden, concealed
词性:形容词

dug-up:/ˌdʌɡ ˈʌp/
adjective
(非正式)翻旧账的；提起不愉快往事的
(Informal) Referring to old, unpleasant matters that are brought back to attention.
They had a dug-up argument about something that happened years ago. 他们翻出了一件多年前发生的旧账，吵了一架。
synonyms: resurrected, revived, rehashed
antonyms: forgotten, buried (figuratively)
词性:形容词
