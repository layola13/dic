degassing:/diːˈɡæsɪŋ/
n.|脱气; 除气|The process of removing dissolved gases from a liquid or a solid.|例句：Degassing the molten metal is crucial for producing high-quality castings. 将熔融金属脱气对于生产高质量铸件至关重要。|近义词：outgassing, venting|反义词：absorption, saturation
词性:名词

degassing:/diːˈɡæsɪŋ/
v.|脱气; 除气|The act of removing dissolved gases from a liquid or a solid.|例句：They are degassing the water to remove dissolved oxygen.  他们正在对水进行脱气以去除溶解氧。|近义词：outgassing, venting|反义词：absorption, saturating
词性:动词
