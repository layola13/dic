lamelliform:/ləˈmɛlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 薄片状的，层状的 | Having the form of a thin plate or lamella.  Resembling a lamella or consisting of lamellae/lamellas. | The fungus had a lamelliform cap. 这种真菌的菌盖呈薄片状。|Synonyms: laminar, lamellar, foliated, scaly | Antonyms: amorphous, shapeless | adjective
