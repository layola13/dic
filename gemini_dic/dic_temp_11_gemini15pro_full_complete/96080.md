indefatigablenesses:/ɪnˌdɪˈfætiɡəbəlˌnɛsɪz/| n. |不倦,不知疲倦|The quality of being tireless and persistent.|Her indefatigableness in pursuing her goals was truly inspiring. 她追求目标不知疲倦的精神着实令人鼓舞。|近义词:tirelessness, persistence, perseverance|反义词:fatigue, weariness, lethargy|词性:名词

词源: From indefatigable + -ness.  Indefatigable comes from French *indéfatigable*, from in- "not" + defatigable "tiring," from Latin defatigare "to tire out," from de- "thoroughly" + fatigare "to weary."
