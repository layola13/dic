anthropotomy:/ˌænθrəˈpɒtəmi/ | n. |人体解剖；人体解剖学|The study of human anatomy, especially by dissection.  The practice of dissecting human bodies. | He studied anthropotomy in medical school. 他在医学院学习了人体解剖学。|  The professor of anthropotomy demonstrated the procedure. 解剖学教授演示了操作过程。| 近义词:anatomy, dissection | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑synthesis-合成) | 词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + -τομία (-tomía, from τέμνω (témnō, “to cut”)).
