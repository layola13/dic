obscurest:/əbˈskjʊərɪst/
adjective
最模糊的；最费解的；最鲜为人知的
most obscure; not discovered or known about; uncertain
The awards ceremony highlighted some of the obscurest corners of musical history.  该颁奖典礼重点介绍了音乐史上一些最鲜为人知的角落。
近义词: most hidden, most unclear, most cryptic, most enigmatic
反义词: clearest, most obvious, most transparent, most well-known
词性: adjective


obscure:/əbˈskjʊr/
adjective
模糊的；费解的；鲜为人知的
not discovered or known about; uncertain
His origins and parentage are obscure. 他的出身和父母不详。
近义词: unclear, uncertain, unknown, vague
反义词: clear, obvious, well-known, famous
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin obscurus "dark, dim, dusky; hidden, secret, unknown," figuratively "insignificant, humble," from ob "over" (see ob-) + -scurus "covered," from PIE root *(s)keu- "to cover, conceal."
