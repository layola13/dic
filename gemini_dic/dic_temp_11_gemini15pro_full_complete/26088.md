busies:/ˈbɪziz/| v. (third-person singular simple present busies, present participle busying, simple past and past participle busied)|使忙碌，使奔走|to occupy or engage (oneself) actively; involve (oneself): He busied himself with packing. 他忙于打包。|He busies himself with gardening. 他忙于园艺。|近义词：occupies, engages, absorbs, engrosses|反义词：idles, rests, relaxes|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *bisien, from Old English *bysigian, from bysig (“busy”).  Cognate with West Frisian bisigje (“to occupy, busy”), Dutch bezig zijn (“to be busy”), German besichtigen (“to inspect, visit”).
