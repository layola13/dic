mothball:/ˈmɔːθbɔːl/ (US), /ˈmɒθbɔːl/ (UK)| n. vt.| 樟脑丸；封存；搁置|A small, white ball containing a chemical with a strong smell, used to keep moths away from clothes.  (As a verb, it means to store something and not use it for a long period of time.)|例句：She put mothballs in her closet to protect her sweaters. 她把樟脑丸放进衣橱里以保护她的毛衣。

例句：The project was mothballed due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被搁置了。|近义词：preserve, store, protect (for the noun); shelve, postpone, suspend (for the verb)|反义词:use, activate, resume (for the verb)|词性:noun, verb
