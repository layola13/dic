lukewarmnesses:/ˌluːkˈwɔːrmnəsɪz/
n.
缺乏热情, 冷淡, 不冷不热
lack of enthusiasm; tepidity.
His lukewarmness about the project was obvious. 他对这个项目缺乏热情是显而易见的。
近义词:apathy, indifference, coolness
反义词:enthusiasm, fervor, passion
词性:名词

词源:
From lukewarm + -ness.  Lukewarm itself comes from Middle English *lewkewarm, from lew (tepid) + warm.
