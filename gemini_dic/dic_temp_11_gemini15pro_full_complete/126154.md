mutt:/mʌt/| n. |杂种狗，笨蛋，傻瓜 |A dog of mixed breed.  Informally, it can also refer to a foolish or contemptible person. |例句：That mutt of yours chewed up my slippers! 你那只杂种狗咬坏了我的拖鞋！

例句：He called me a mutt, but I didn't take it personally. 他叫我笨蛋，但我没往心里去。|近义词：mongrel, cur, fool, idiot |反义词:purebred (for dogs) |词性: noun
