discordant:/dɪˈskɔːrdənt/ | adj. | 不和谐的，不一致的 | Lacking harmony or agreement; conflicting. | The discordant notes of the untuned piano made her wince.  走调的钢琴发出不和谐的音符，让她感到畏缩。 | synonyms: clashing, conflicting, dissonant, inharmonious, jarring | antonyms: harmonious, agreeable, concordant, consonant | adjective

discordant:/dɪˈskɔːrdənt/ | n. | 不和谐的事物 | Something that is discordant or disagrees. (Rare Usage) |  The discordant element in the painting ruined the overall effect.  画作中不和谐的元素破坏了整体效果。 | synonyms: clash, conflict, dissonance, inharmony | antonyms: harmony, agreement, concordance, consonance | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *discordant-, present participle of discordāre "to disagree," from dis- "apart" + cordāre "to be of one heart," from cor "heart."
