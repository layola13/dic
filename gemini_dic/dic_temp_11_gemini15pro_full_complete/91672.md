hypercivilization:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|超级文明|A hypothetical civilization vastly more advanced than any known to exist, often possessing technologies and capabilities beyond our current understanding.|Scientists theorize about the potential characteristics of a hypercivilization. 科学家们对超级文明的潜在特征进行了理论推测。|The discovery of Dyson spheres could be evidence of a hypercivilization. 发现戴森球可能是超级文明存在的证据。|Synonyms: advanced civilization, super civilization, transhuman civilization|Antonyms: primitive civilization|noun
