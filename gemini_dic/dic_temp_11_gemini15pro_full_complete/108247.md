laumontite:/ˈlɔːmɒntaɪt/| n. |浊沸石|A hydrous calcium aluminum silicate mineral that occurs as vitreous white to pink or reddish prismatic crystals. It dehydrates readily in dry air, becoming powdery and opaque.|Laumontite is often found in volcanic rocks. 浊沸石常在火山岩中发现。|近义词:leonhardite (partially dehydrated laumontite)|反义词: None (Specific antonyms for mineral names are uncommon)|词性: Noun
