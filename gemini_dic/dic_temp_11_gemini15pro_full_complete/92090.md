hyperrealized:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪzd/
adjective
超真实的，极度逼真的
Characterized by extreme realism or detail, often to the point of artificiality or exaggeration.
The video game boasted hyperrealized graphics, making the characters look almost like photographs. 这款电子游戏以其超真实的画面而自豪，使得角色看起来几乎像照片一样。

The movie's hyperrealized violence made it difficult to watch.  这部电影中极度逼真的暴力场面让人难以观看。

近义词:ultra-realistic, photorealistic, lifelike, vivid
反义词:unrealistic, stylized, abstract, impressionistic
词性:adjective
