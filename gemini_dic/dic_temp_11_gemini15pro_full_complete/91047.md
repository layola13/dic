husseini: /ˌhʊˈseɪni/ | adj. n. | 侯赛因的；侯赛尼派信徒 | Relating to or supporting Husayn ibn Ali, a grandson of the Prophet Muhammad, especially in the context of Shia Islam. Also, a follower of Husayn ibn Ali. |  Example 1: The Husseini procession commemorates the martyrdom of Imam Husayn. 侯赛因游行是为了纪念伊玛目侯赛因的殉难。

Example 2:  Many Husseini banners were displayed during Ashura.  在阿舒拉节期间，许多侯赛因的旗帜被展示出来。 | Near synonyms: Shi'a, Shiite | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but potentially Sunni in a broad sectarian context, though this is an oversimplification) | word type: adjective, noun
