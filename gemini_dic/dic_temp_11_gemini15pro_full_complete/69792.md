fern:/fɜːrn/ (US) /fɜːn/ (UK)|n.|蕨类植物|A flowerless plant that has feathery or leafy fronds and reproduces by spores instead of seeds.|The forest floor was covered with ferns.|Synonyms: bracken, pteridophyte|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plant type.  Could consider "flowering plant")|noun
