architecturalization: /ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n. |架构化，体系化|The process of organizing something into a structured system or framework, often resembling the design and construction of a building.  |The architecturalization of the company's IT infrastructure improved efficiency.  公司IT基础设施的架构化提高了效率。|Synonyms: structuring, systematization, organization, formalization |Antonyms: disorganization, chaos, disorder
词性: 名词
