identically:/aɪˈdɛntɪkli/
adverb
相同地；一致地
in exactly the same way
The twins were dressed identically. 这对双胞胎穿得一模一样。
Synonyms: similarly, alike, uniformly
Antonyms: differently, dissimilarly, uniquely
word type: adverb
