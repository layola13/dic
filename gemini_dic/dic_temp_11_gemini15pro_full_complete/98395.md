insoluble:/ɪnˈsɒljʊbl/ (US) /ɪnˈsɒljəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不溶的，无法解决的|Not able to be dissolved in a liquid; (of a problem or difficulty) too difficult to be solved.|例句：The precipitate was insoluble in water. 沉淀物不溶于水。|近义词：indissoluble, unsolvable|反义词：soluble, solvable|词性:形容词

insoluble:/ɪnˈsɒljʊbl/ (US) /ɪnˈsɒljəbl/ (UK)| adj.|无法解决的| (of a problem or difficulty) too difficult to be solved.|例句：The problem seemed insoluble. 这个问题似乎无法解决。|近义词：unsolvable, intractable|反义词：solvable, resolvable|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insolubilis, from in- "not" + solubilis "that can be loosened, dissolved," from solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
