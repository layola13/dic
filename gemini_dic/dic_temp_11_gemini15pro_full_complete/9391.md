antiqueness:/ænˈtiːkwɪtnəs/| n.|古老，古董，古代|The antiquity of the artifact makes it extremely valuable. 这件文物的古老使其极具价值。|例句：The museum is known for its collection of Roman antiquities.  这家博物馆以其收藏的罗马古物而闻名。

例句：She was fascinated by the antiquity of the castle. 她被城堡的古老所吸引。|近义词：ancientness, oldness, antiquity|反义词：modernity, newness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Middle English:  antiquite, from Old French antiquité, from Latin antiquitas, from antiquus ‘ancient’.
