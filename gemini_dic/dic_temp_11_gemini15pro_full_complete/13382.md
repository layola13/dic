atokous: /əˈtōkəs/
atokous: adj.
atokous:  无性的；不产幼体的
atokous: Not producing offspring; viviparous. Specifically, designating certain polychaete worms that lack gonads and reproduce by epitoky.
atokous: The atocous form of the worm remains in the burrow.  这种无性繁殖的蠕虫停留在洞穴里。
atokous:  Near-synonym: infertile, sterile, barren
atokous:  Near-antonym:  fertile, fecund, prolific
atokous: adjective
