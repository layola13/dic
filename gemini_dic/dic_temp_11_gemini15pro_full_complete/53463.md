disarm:/dɪsˈɑːrm/ | v. |解除武装；使息怒；缓和；消除|To deprive of weapons; to make less hostile or less dangerous.|The police disarmed the robber. 警察解除了抢劫犯的武装。|synonyms: demilitarize, neutralize, appease, conciliate|antonyms: arm, fortify, provoke, aggravate|verb

disarm:/dɪsˈɑːrmɪŋ/ | adj. |使人消除敌意的；令人放松的|Having the quality of inspiring trust or putting one at ease.|He had a disarming smile. 他有着令人放松的微笑。|synonyms: charming, winning, engaging|antonyms: alarming, threatening, off-putting|adjective
