huffiest:/ˈhʌfiɪst/| adj.|最生气的，最恼火的|Superlative form of huffy: most huffy.  Meaning extremely offended or annoyed.|She gave me the huffiest look I've ever seen after I accidentally spilled coffee on her new dress. 她在我不小心把咖啡洒到她的新裙子上后，给了我我见过的最恼火的眼神。|Synonyms: angriest, most annoyed, most indignant, most offended, crossest|Antonyms: calmest, most pleased, happiest|adjective
