logcock:/ˈlɒɡkɒk/ (British) /ˈlɔːɡkɑːk/ (American)| n. |啄木鸟|A bird with a strong beak for drilling holes in tree trunks, typically to extract insects or sap.  It's also known as a woodpecker.|例句：The logcock hammered at the tree trunk. 啄木鸟敲击着树干。

例句：We heard the distinctive drumming of a logcock in the woods. 我们听到树林里啄木鸟独特的敲击声。|近义词：woodpecker|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
