nicknames:/ˈnɪkˌneɪmz/| n. |昵称，绰号|A familiar or humorous name given to a person or thing instead of or as well as their real name. |例句：He has a lot of nicknames, but his favorite is "Ace". 他有很多昵称，但他最喜欢的是“Ace”。

例句：Her real name is Elizabeth, but everyone calls her Liz, which is a common nickname. 她真正的名字是伊丽莎白，但大家都叫她利兹，这是一个常见的昵称。|近义词：monikers, aliases, sobriquets|反义词：formal names, given names|词性: noun
