barich: /ˈbærɪtʃ/ | adj. | 荒芜的，贫瘠的 |  Barren or unproductive; lacking vegetation or life.  Originating from the Old French word "barage," meaning "barren land."  It later evolved into "barich" in Middle English. | The barich landscape offered little sustenance for the travelers.  这片荒芜的土地几乎无法为旅行者提供食物。| The barich soil yielded a meager harvest. 贫瘠的土壤只产出了微薄的收成。 | synonyms: barren, sterile, desolate, infertile | antonyms: fertile, productive, lush, abundant | word type: adjective
