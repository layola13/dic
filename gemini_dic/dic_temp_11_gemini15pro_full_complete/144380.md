outstrain:/ˌaʊtˈstreɪn/
verb
过度拉紧；过度劳累
to strain or exert (oneself, one's powers, etc.) to the utmost; overexert.
He outstrained his voice trying to be heard over the noise. 他过度用力喊叫，试图盖过噪音。
近义词: overexert, overstrain, strain
反义词: relax
