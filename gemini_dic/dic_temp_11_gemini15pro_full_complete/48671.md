deference:/ˈdefərəns/ (US), /ˈdɛfərəns/ (UK)| n.|尊重，顺从，敬重|Respectful submission or yielding to the judgment, opinion, will, etc., of another.|Example: He showed deference to his elders. 他对长辈表示尊重。|Example:  In deference to her wishes, they held the meeting in the library. 出于对她的尊重，他们在图书馆举行了会议。|近义词：respect, reverence, courtesy, submission, compliance|反义词：disrespect, defiance, disobedience|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deferance* (Modern French *déférence*), from Late Latin *deferentia* "yielding, respect," from Latin *deferentem* (nominative *deferens*), present participle of *deferre* "to carry down, bring down, offer, convey, transfer, yield, submit," from *de-* "down, away" + *ferre* "to carry, bear."
