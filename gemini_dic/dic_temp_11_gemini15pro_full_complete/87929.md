hieronymite:/ˈhaɪərənɪmaɪt/ /ˈhɪərənɪmaɪt/
|n.|修道士；隐士；耶罗尼米特会修士|a member of a religious order founded by St. Jerome, especially the Spanish order (Order of St. Jerome) noted for its scholarship.|The Hieronymites were known for their beautiful illuminated manuscripts.  耶罗尼米特会修士以其精美的泥金装饰手抄本而闻名。|synonyms: monk, hermit, recluse|antonyms: extrovert, socialite
|noun|
