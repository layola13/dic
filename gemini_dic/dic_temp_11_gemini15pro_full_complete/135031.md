nonneutrality:/ˌnɑːnnuːˈtrælɪti/
n.
不中立，非中立性
lack of neutrality; the state of not being neutral
The country's nonneutrality in the conflict drew criticism.  这个国家在冲突中的不中立立场招致批评。
The journalist's nonneutrality was evident in her reporting. 这名记者在报道中明显地表现出不中立。
Synonyms: partisanship, bias, favoritism
Antonyms: neutrality, impartiality, objectivity
noun
