circumvallate: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvæleɪt/ /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvælət/

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin circumvallatus, past participle of circumvallare (“to surround with a rampart”), from circum (“around”) + vallare (“to wall”), from vallum (“wall, rampart”).
