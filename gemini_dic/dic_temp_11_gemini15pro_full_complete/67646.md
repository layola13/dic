extrinsically: /ɛkˈstrɪnzɪkli/ /ɪkˈstrɪnzɪkli/
| adv. | 外在地；外部地；非本质地 | From outside; externally; not inherently.  |  His motivation was extrinsically driven by the promise of a bonus.  他的动力来自外部承诺的奖金。|
extrinsically: /ɛkˈstrɪnzɪkli/ /ɪkˈstrɪnzɪkli/
|  | 外在地；外部地；非本质地 |  The value of a college degree is often seen extrinsically, in terms of earning potential. 大学学位的价值通常从外部来看，就其赚钱潜力而言。| 近义词：externally, outwardly | 反义词：intrinsically, inherently | 词性: 副词
