chickaree: /ˈtʃɪkəriː/ (US), /ˈtʃɪkəriː/ (UK)| n.|美洲红松鼠，赤松鼠|A North American squirrel (Tamiasciurus hudsonicus) with reddish-brown fur, a bushy tail, and a distinctive chattering call.  It is known for its energetic behavior and habit of storing food for the winter.|例句：The chickaree chattered angrily from high in the pine tree.  美洲红松鼠在高高的松树上愤怒地吱吱叫。

例句：We saw a chickaree burying nuts in the garden. 我们看到一只赤松鼠在花园里埋坚果。|近义词：red squirrel, pine squirrel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
