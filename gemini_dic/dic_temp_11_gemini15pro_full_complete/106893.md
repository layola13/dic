laccic:/ˈlæksɪk/ | adj. | 虫胶的；由虫胶制成的 | Relating to lac, a resinous secretion of lac insects, used to make shellac and other products. |  This laccic dye gives a beautiful crimson color.  这种虫胶染料呈现出美丽的深红色。 | Synonyms: resinous, shellac | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps synthetic) | adjective


laccic:/ˈlæksɪk/ | n. | 虫胶;紫胶 | Lac, a resinous secretion of lac insects.  | The laccic was harvested and processed to make shellac.  虫胶被收集起来并加工成虫胶漆。 | Synonyms: lac, shellac | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun


Etymology: From Latin *laccicus, from lacca (“lac”).  Ultimately from Sanskrit लक्ष  (lakṣa, “one hundred thousand”), referring to the large number of insects involved in producing lac.
