hurriedness:/ˈhɜːridnɪs/ /ˈhʌridnɪs/| n. |匆忙，仓促|The hurriedness of his departure suggested something was wrong. 他的匆忙离去表明出了什么问题。|例句：The hurriedness of her packing made her forget her passport. 她打包的匆忙让她忘记了护照。|近义词：haste, rush, hurry|反义词：leisureliness, calmness, deliberation|词性:名词

Etymology: From "hurried" (past participle of "hurry") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  "Hurry" originates from the Middle English "hurrien," of uncertain origin, possibly related to a Scandinavian source.
