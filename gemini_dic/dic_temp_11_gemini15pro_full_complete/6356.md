amphibiological:/ˌæmfɪbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkl/
adjective
两栖的，水陆两用的
Relating to or adapted for both land and water; able to live both on land and in water.
The frog is an amphibiological animal. 青蛙是一种两栖动物。
amphibious, aquatic, terrestrial
N/A
adjective

amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/
noun
两栖动物
A cold-blooded vertebrate animal of a class that comprises the frogs, toads, newts, salamanders, and caecilians. They are distinguished by having an aquatic gill-breathing larval stage followed (typically) by a terrestrial lung-breathing adult stage.
Frogs and toads are amphibians. 青蛙和蟾蜍是两栖动物。
