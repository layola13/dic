nonbreeders:/ˌnɒnˈbriːdərz/ | n. | 非繁殖者，不育动物 | Animals that are not breeding, either temporarily or permanently.  This could be due to age, health, social status, or environmental factors. |  Many seabirds are nonbreeders in their first year. 许多海鸟在它们的第一年是不繁殖的。

The zoo keeps a separate enclosure for nonbreeders. 动物园为非繁殖动物设置了单独的围栏。 | synonyms: non-reproductive animals, barren animals | antonyms: breeders, reproductive animals | noun
