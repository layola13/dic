druggist:/ˈdrʌɡɪst/| n.|药剂师；药商|A druggist is a person who is qualified to prepare and dispense medicinal drugs.|例句：The druggist prepared the prescription carefully. 药剂师仔细地配好了处方。

例句：The old druggist knew all his customers by name. 老药剂师知道所有顾客的名字。|近义词：pharmacist, chemist, apothecary|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
