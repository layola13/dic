frutage:/ˈfruːtɪdʒ/
n.
水果拼盘; 水果甜点; 水果（总称）
A dessert made of mixed fruit, or fruit in general.
The frutage was a colorful mix of berries and melon.  水果拼盘是由各种浆果和甜瓜混合而成的。
We had a delicious frutage for dessert. 我们吃了一份美味的水果甜点。
近义词:fruit salad, fruit compote, fruit
反义词:(No direct antonym, potentially "vegetables")
