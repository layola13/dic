overmasterfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈmæstərfʊlnəs/
|n.|专横跋扈；盛气凌人|The king's overmasterfulness alienated his subjects. 国王的专横跋扈疏远了他的臣民。|English Explanation:  An excessive or tyrannical assertion of power or control; domineering behavior. It implies a desire to dominate or control others completely, often in a harsh or arrogant manner.|例句：His overmasterfulness made him unpopular. 他的专横跋扈让他不受欢迎。

|例句：Her overmasterfulness stifled creativity in the team. 她的专横跋扈扼杀了团队的创造力。
|近义词：domineeringness, imperiousness, tyranny, despotism, authoritarianism|反义词：humility, modesty, submissiveness, meekness|词性：名词
