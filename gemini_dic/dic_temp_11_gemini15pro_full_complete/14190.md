autobahn:/ˈɔːtoʊbɑːn/ (US), /ˈɔːtəʊbɑːn/ (UK)| n.|高速公路|A German expressway, typically one with multiple lanes and no speed limit (or a recommended speed limit only).|We drove on the autobahn at 180 kilometers per hour. 我们以每小时180公里的速度在高速公路上行驶。|Synonym: expressway, freeway, motorway|Antonym: local road|Noun
