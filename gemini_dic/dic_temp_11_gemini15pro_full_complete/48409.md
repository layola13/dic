dedicators:/ˈdɛdɪˌkeɪtərz/
n.
奉献者，献身者
People who dedicate something, especially a book, building, or work of art, to someone.
The dedicators of the new hospital wing were thanked for their generosity.  新的医院侧楼的捐赠者因其慷慨而受到感谢。
supporters, contributors, donors
opponents, detractors
noun
