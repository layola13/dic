hypanthium:/haɪˈpænθiəm/| n. | 花筒，被丝托 | The cup-shaped part of a flower formed by the fusion of the bases of the sepals, petals, and stamens.  It is sometimes called a floral cup or floral tube. | The rose has a well-developed hypanthium. 玫瑰有一个发达的花筒。|  floral tube, calyx tube | N/A | noun
