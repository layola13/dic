nonchallengers:/ˌnɒnˈtʃælɪndʒəz/
noun
不挑战者；不竞争者；不提出异议者
Those who do not challenge or compete; those who do not raise objections.
Many nonchallengers simply accepted the new policy. 许多不挑战者只是简单地接受了新政策。
The nonchallengers remained silent during the debate.  在辩论中，不挑战者保持沉默。
Synonyms: conformists, acquiescers, passivists
Antonyms: challengers, competitors, objectors
n.
