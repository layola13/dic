monogamist:/məˈnɒɡəmɪst/ /məˈnɑːɡəmɪst/
n.
一夫一妻制者；单配偶制者
A person who believes in or practices monogamy, the practice of having only one spouse or sexual partner at a time.
例句：He is a monogamist and believes in the sanctity of marriage. 他是一夫一妻制者，相信婚姻的神圣性。

例句：She's always been a monogamist, unlike her sister who prefers open relationships.  她一直是一夫一妻制者，不像她姐姐更喜欢开放式关系。


近义词：none (The closest synonym would describe the *practice*, like "monogamous person")
反义词：polygamist, bigamist
词性: noun
