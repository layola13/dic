manageresses:/ˈmænɪdʒərəsɪz/
noun
女经理们；女性管理人员
A female manager.  This term is now widely considered outdated and sexist, and "managers" is the preferred term regardless of gender.
例句：The manageresses of the two branches met to discuss strategy.  两个分公司的女经理们开会讨论战略。

例句：In the past, some companies referred to female managers as manageresses, but this term is now outdated. 过去，一些公司将女性经理称为manageresses，但现在这个词已经过时了。

近义词：managers, female managers, supervisors, executives, administrators
反义词：employees, subordinates, staff
词性：noun (plural)
