expressway:/ɪkˈsprɛsweɪ/| n. |高速公路，快速路|A major highway designed for high-speed traffic, typically with controlled access and multiple lanes in each direction. |We took the expressway to get to the city quickly. 我们走高速公路很快就到了市区。|The new expressway has significantly reduced travel time. 新的高速公路大大缩短了旅行时间。|近义词：highway, freeway, motorway, thruway|反义词: side road, back road |noun
