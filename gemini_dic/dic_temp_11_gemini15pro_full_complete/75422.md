frugivores:/ˈfruːdʒɪvɔːrz/
| n. |食果动物|Animals that primarily eat fruit. |Bats and certain primates are frugivores.  蝙蝠和某些灵长类动物是食果动物。|Synonyms: fruit-eater|Antonyms: carnivore, herbivore|noun


frugivorous:/fruːˈdʒɪvərəs/
| adj. |食果的|Feeding on fruit. |Many birds are frugivorous. 许多鸟类是食果的。|Synonyms: fruit-eating|Antonyms: carnivorous, herbivorous|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frux* (fruit) + *-vorus* (eating), from *vorare* (to devour).
