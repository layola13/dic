midpoint:/ˈmɪdpɔɪnt/| n. |中点，正中央|The midpoint of a line segment is the point that divides it into two equal parts.  线段的中点是将其分成两等分的点。|The midpoint of the journey is where we'll stop for lunch. 旅途的中点是我们停下来吃午饭的地方。|近义词：center, middle, halfway point|反义词:end, extremity|noun
