knowledge:/ˈnɒlɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈnɑːlɪdʒ/ (US)| n. |知识，学识，了解，知道| The understanding and awareness of facts, information, descriptions, or skills acquired through experience or education.|She has a wide knowledge of music. 她对音乐有广泛的了解。|近义词：understanding, comprehension, awareness, expertise, information, learning, scholarship|反义词：ignorance, unawareness|词性:名词

词源:
Middle English: knowleche, from knowen ‘to know’ + -leche. The change in the ending was due to association with words such as hawleche, and the spelling with dg was influenced by acknowledge.
