BioNTech: /ˈbaɪoʊnˌtɛk/ | n. | 生物新技术公司（Biopharmaceutical New Technologies的缩写）| A German biotechnology company that develops and manufactures immunotherapies for cancer and other diseases.  It is most well-known for its mRNA-based COVID-19 vaccine developed in partnership with Pfizer. | BioNTech's COVID-19 vaccine was the first to be authorized for use in the Western world. 生物新技术公司的COVID-19疫苗是第一个在西方世界获得授权使用的疫苗。|  Moderna, Pfizer |  N/A | noun

Etymology:
BioNTech is a portmanteau of **Biopharmaceutical New Technologies**.  "Biopharmaceutical" combines "bio-" (life) and "pharmaceutical" (relating to medicinal drugs). "New" and "Technologies" are standard English words.
