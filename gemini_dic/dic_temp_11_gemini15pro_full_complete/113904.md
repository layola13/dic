madrigalism:/ˈmædrɪɡəlɪzəm/
n.
狂想曲主义;牧歌体裁;牧歌式作品
A style of musical composition, especially vocal music, characteristic of the madrigal; a short lyrical poem suitable for being set to music in this style.
Example:  The composer's latest work is a beautiful example of madrigalism.  作曲家最新的作品是狂想曲主义的一个美丽范例。

Example: Her poetry often exhibits a delicate madrigalism. 她的诗歌经常展现出一种精致的牧歌体裁。

Synonym:  madrigal, part-song, glee
Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider atonal music, or perhaps spoken word poetry as a contrasting form)
词性: noun
