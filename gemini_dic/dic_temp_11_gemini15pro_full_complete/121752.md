mispublished:/ˌmɪsˈpʌblɪʃt/
verb
错误地出版；刊登错误
published incorrectly or with errors.
The article was mispublished with several factual inaccuracies. 这篇文章出版时有几处事实错误。
近义词: misprinted, published incorrectly
反义词: published correctly, printed accurately
词性:Verb (过去分词)
