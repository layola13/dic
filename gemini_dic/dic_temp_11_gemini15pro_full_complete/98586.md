intuitively:/ɪnˈtuːɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 直觉地；凭直觉地 | By instinct; without conscious reasoning.  |  She intuitively knew the answer. 她凭直觉就知道答案。 |例句：He intuitively grasped the meaning of the poem. 他凭直觉领会了这首诗的含义。| 近义词：instinctively, naturally, spontaneously | 反义词：logically, rationally, consciously | 词性:副词

intuitively:/ɪnˈtuːɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 直觉地；凭直觉地 | Based on feelings rather than facts or proof. |  He intuitively understood her feelings. 他直觉地理解了她的感受。 |例句：Intuitively, I felt that something was wrong. 直觉上，我觉得有些不对劲。| 近义词：instinctually, viscerally | 反义词：deliberately, analytically | 词性:副词
