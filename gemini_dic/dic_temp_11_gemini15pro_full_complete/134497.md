nonlegislative:/ˌnɑːnˈledʒɪsleɪtɪv/
adjective

Not involved in or relating to the making of laws.
The committee has a nonlegislative function. 该委员会具有非立法职能。
Near Synonyms: non-lawmaking, executive, administrative
Antonyms: legislative, lawmaking
词性: adjective
