heavenly-minded:/ˈhɛvənliˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|天堂般的; 心系天堂的; 重视精神生活的|Having thoughts focused on heaven or spiritual matters rather than earthly concerns.  Someone who is heavenly-minded is more concerned with religious or spiritual matters than with material or worldly things.|She was a quiet, heavenly-minded woman, always ready to help those in need. 她是一位安静的、心系天堂的女人，总是乐于帮助那些需要帮助的人。

He was so heavenly-minded he forgot to pay his bills. 他如此专注于精神世界，以至于忘记了付账单。|近义词：spiritual, pious, devout, godly|反义词：worldly, materialistic, earthly, carnal|词性:adjective

词源:
"Heavenly" comes from Old English "heofonlic," meaning "of or belonging to heaven." "Minded" comes from Old English "gemyndig," meaning "mindful" or "remembering."  The combined term "heavenly-minded" emphasizes a focus on spiritual matters related to heaven.
