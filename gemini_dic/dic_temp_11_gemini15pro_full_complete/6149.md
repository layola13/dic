amicably:/ˈæmɪkəbli/
adverb
友好地；和睦地
In a friendly and peaceful manner; without disagreement or rancor.
The two parties settled their differences amicably.  双方友好地解决了分歧。
The divorce was settled amicably, with both parties agreeing on custody arrangements.  离婚是友好解决的，双方就监护权安排达成了一致。
近义词:peacefully, harmoniously, agreeably, cordially
反义词:hostilely, unpleasantly, disagreeably, acrimoniously
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *amicabilis (“friendly”), from Latin amicus (“friend”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning “able to be,” “worthy of being”).
