dottard:/ˈdɒtərd (UK), ˈdɑːtərd (US)| n.|老糊涂，老顽固|An old person, especially one who is considered to be weak or senile.|The dottard wandered off again and got lost in the park.  那个老糊涂又走丢了，在公园里迷路了。|Synonyms:  codger, fool, simpleton|Antonyms:  genius, sage, savant|noun
Etymology-> From Middle English *dotard, from doten (“to be foolish or senile”) + -ard.
