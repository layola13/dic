invests:/ɪnˈvɛsts/| v. |投资，投入，授予，赋予| puts (money) into financial schemes, shares, property, or a commercial venture with the expectation of achieving a profit. Also means to devote (time, effort, or energy) to a particular undertaking with the expectation of a worthwhile result.| He invests his money wisely in the stock market. 他明智地把钱投资于股市。| synonyms: puts money into, spends money on, funds, finances, backs, supports, endows, furnishes, vests, empowers | antonyms: divests, withdraws, liquidates | verb
