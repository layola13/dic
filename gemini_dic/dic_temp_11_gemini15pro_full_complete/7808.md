antacid:/ænˈtæsɪd/ | n. adj. |抗酸剂；抗酸的|A substance that neutralizes stomach acidity. |She took an antacid to relieve her heartburn. 她服用抗酸剂来缓解胃灼热。|The doctor prescribed an antacid medication. 医生开了抗酸药物。| 近义词: antacid, antiacidic, acid neutralizer |反义词: acid, acidic |词性: noun, adjective

词源:  From anti- + acid.  "Anti-" meaning against and "acid" referring to stomach acid.
