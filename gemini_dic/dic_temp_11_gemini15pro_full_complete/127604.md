needfulnesses:/ˈniːdfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
必需品；必要性；贫困
The state or quality of being needful; necessity.  The condition of being in need; poverty.
The needfulnesses of life, such as food and shelter, must be met first.  生活必需品，如食物和住所，必须首先得到满足。
Synonyms: necessities, essentials, requirements, exigencies, poverty, indigence, want
Antonyms: luxuries, nonessentials, superfluities, affluence, wealth, prosperity
