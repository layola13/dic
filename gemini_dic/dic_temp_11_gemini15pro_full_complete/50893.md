desertification:/dɪˌzɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.| 荒漠化 |The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture. |Example 1: Desertification is a serious environmental problem in many parts of the world.  荒漠化是世界许多地区的一个严重环境问题。

Example 2: The increasing desertification of the area is threatening the livelihoods of local farmers.  该地区日益严重的荒漠化正在威胁当地农民的生计。|近义词：aridification,  desertization |反义词:  afforestation, land reclamation |词性: noun
