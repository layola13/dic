nonassimilating:/ˌnɒnəˈsɪməleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不吸收的，不同化的 | Not absorbing or integrating into the dominant culture or environment.  Refusing to adopt or conform to the customs, attitudes, etc., of a dominant social group or nation. | The nonassimilating nature of some immigrant groups can lead to social tensions.  一些移民群体的不融合性会导致社会紧张。| The nonassimilating bacteria remained distinct in the culture. 这些不融合的细菌在培养基中保持着独立性。 | 近义词: unintegrating, unadapting, segregating | 反义词: assimilating, integrating, adapting | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- + assimilating (present participle of assimilate).  "Assimilate" comes from Late Latin assimilatus, past participle of assimilare "to make like," from ad- "to" + similis "like."
