overreserved:/ˌoʊvərɪˈzɜːrvd/
adjective
过度拘谨的，过于保守的
Having made too many reservations or commitments; booked beyond capacity.  Excessively restrained or reserved in manner.
The restaurant was overreserved, and they had to turn away several parties.  这家餐馆预订太多了，不得不拒绝几桌客人。
He was so overreserved that he barely spoke a word all evening. 他太拘谨了，整个晚上几乎没说一句话。
近义词: overbooked, oversubscribed, shy, reticent, withdrawn
反义词: outgoing, gregarious, extroverted
词性: adjective

overreserved:/ˌoʊvərɪˈzɜːrvd/
verb (past participle)
被过度预订的
The past participle of "overreserve," indicating a state of being booked beyond capacity.
The flight was overreserved, and some passengers had to be bumped.  这趟航班被过度预订了，一些乘客不得不被安排到其他航班。
The hotel rooms were overreserved due to a computer glitch.  由于电脑故障，酒店房间被过度预订了。
近义词: overbooked, oversubscribed
反义词: underbooked, available
词性: verb (past participle)


词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much") + "reserve" (verb meaning "to keep back or set aside for future use").
