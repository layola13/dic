giantism:/ˈdʒaɪənˌtɪzəm/| n.|巨人症；巨大发育；|Excessive growth and height significantly above average, usually caused by overproduction of growth hormone in childhood.|His giantism made him stand out in a crowd. 他的巨人症使他在人群中很显眼。|近义词：acromegaly, gigantism|反义词:dwarfism|noun
