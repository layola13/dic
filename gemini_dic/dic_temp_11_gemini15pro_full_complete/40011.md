consults:/kənˈsʌlts/ /kənˈsʌlts/ | v. | 咨询，商量，查阅 |  She consults a doctor about her persistent headaches. 她向医生咨询她持续的头痛问题。| seek advice from, refer to | disregard, ignore | verb
consults:/kənˈsʌlts/ /kənˈsʌlts/ | n. | 咨询，磋商  (通常用复数形式) | The project required several consults with experts in the field.  这个项目需要多次咨询该领域的专家。 | consultations, meetings | disagreements, disputes | noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English *consulten*, from Latin *cōnsultāre* (“to deliberate, consult”), frequentative of *cōnsulere* (“to consult, take counsel”), from *com-* (“together”) + *salīre* (“to leap, spring”).  The noun form emerged later.
