attunement:/əˈtuːnmənt/
n.|协调，一致；调和；和谐|The process of bringing into harmony; a state of harmonious or sympathetic relationship.|She felt a deep attunement with nature. 她感觉与大自然深深地融为一体。|近义词：harmony, resonance, rapport, sympathy|反义词：discord, dissonance, conflict|词性: 名词

attunement:/əˈtuːnmənt/
n.|调音，校准|The act of tuning or adjusting something, especially a musical instrument.|The piano needed attunement after being moved. 钢琴搬运后需要调音。|近义词：tuning, adjustment, calibration|反义词：detuning|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From at- + tune + -ment.  "Tune" ultimately comes from the Latin "tonus," meaning "tone" or "sound."
