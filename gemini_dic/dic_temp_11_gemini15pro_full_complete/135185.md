nonorchestrally:/ˌnɒnɔːˈkɛstrəli/
adverb
非管弦乐队地; 非用管弦乐队演奏地
Without the use of an orchestra; not in an orchestral manner.
The piece was performed nonorchestrally, with only a piano and a flute.  这首曲子是非管弦乐演奏的，只用了一架钢琴和一支长笛。
The composer experimented with nonorchestral sounds, incorporating electronic instruments and found objects.  这位作曲家尝试了非管弦乐的声音，融入了电子乐器和现成物品。
Synonyms:  without an orchestra, unaccompanied (in some contexts)
Antonyms:  orchestrally, symphonically
word type: adverb
