campanologies:/ˌkæmpəˈnɒlədʒiz/
|n.|钟学；敲钟术|The study of bells, their history, tuning, and ringing techniques, especially change ringing.|He's an expert in campanologies and can identify the age of a bell by its sound. 他是钟学专家，能通过声音辨别钟的年代。|Synonym: bell-ringing |Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps silence)|Noun
