disheritance:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/| n.|剥夺继承权，取消继承资格|The disheritance of his eldest son caused a rift in the family.  他剥夺了长子的继承权，导致家庭关系破裂。|例句：His disheritance left him with nothing. 他被剥夺了继承权，一无所有。|近义词：exclusion from inheritance, deprivation of inheritance|反义词: inheritance, bequeathal|noun

disherit:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪt/| vt.|剥夺继承权，取消继承资格|He disinherited his daughter after she eloped.  在他女儿私奔后，他剥夺了她的继承权。|例句：The king disinherited his rebellious son. 国王剥夺了叛逆儿子的继承权。|近义词：cut off, disown|反义词: bequeath, endow|verb
