honouree: /ˌɒnəˈriː/ (UK) /ˌɑːnəˈriː/ (US)| n.| 受奖人；领奖人；被表扬的人 |A person who is honoured or awarded something. |例句：The honourees were all given a medal. 所有受奖人都获得了一枚奖牌。|例句：The honouree gave a short acceptance speech. 领奖人发表了简短的获奖感言。|近义词：recipient, awardee, laureate|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From honour + -ee (suffix indicating the recipient or object of an action). Ultimately from Latin honor.
