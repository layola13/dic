emblematically:/ˌɛmbləˈmætɪkli/
adv.
象征性地，作为象征
Serving as a symbol; symbolically.
The eagle emblematically represents the United States.  这只鹰象征性地代表了美国。
The dove is emblematically associated with peace. 鸽子象征性地与和平联系在一起。
近义词:symbolically, representatively, typically
反义词:literally, concretely
词性:副词

词源:
From emblem + -atic + -ally.  Ultimately from the Greek word *ἔμβλημα* (émblēma), meaning "something inserted," hence "inlaid ornament," "mosaic," "emblem."
