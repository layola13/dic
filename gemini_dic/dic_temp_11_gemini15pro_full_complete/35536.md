coak:/kəʊk/ | n. |焦炭; 焦块|A hard, grey substance that is left after coal has been heated and the gases have been removed.  It is used as a fuel and in making steel.| The factory uses coak to power its furnaces. 这家工厂用焦炭为其熔炉提供动力。| synonyms:  coke, carbon, char | antonyms: fuel (in a broader sense, since coke *is* a fuel)| noun
