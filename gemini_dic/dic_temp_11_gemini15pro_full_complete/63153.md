ensues:/ɪnˈsuːz/
verb
随之而来；接着发生
follows as a consequence or result.
After the heavy rain, flooding ensued.  大雨过后，发生了洪水。
The police officer fired a warning shot, and chaos ensued. 警察鸣枪示警，随后一片混乱。
近义词: follows, results, develops, arises, proceeds, occurs, happens
反义词: precedes, antecedes
词性：v.
