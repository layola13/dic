acidiferous:/ˌæsɪˈdɪfərəs/
adjective
产酸的；含酸的
Producing or containing acid.
The acidiferous glands in the stomach secrete hydrochloric acid. 胃里的产酸腺体会分泌盐酸。
The chemist analyzed the acidiferous solution. 化学家分析了这种含酸的溶液。

近义词:acidic, acid-forming, acid-producing
反义词:alkaline, basic

词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *acidifer, from acidus (“sour, sharp, acid”) + -fer (“-bearing”).
