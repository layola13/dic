nondeterrent:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːrənt/ | adj. | 非威慑性的，不具威慑力的 | Not serving to deter; not discouraging or preventing something. | The current penalties for speeding are nondeterrent; drivers continue to ignore them.  目前的超速惩罚措施并不具备威慑力；司机们依然无视它们。|  The prison sentence proved nondeterrent, and he reoffended within months of his release. 监狱刑罚证明没有威慑力，他在获释后的几个月内就再次犯罪。|Synonyms: ineffective, weak, feckless, impotent | Antonyms: deterrent, effective, strong, powerful | adjective
