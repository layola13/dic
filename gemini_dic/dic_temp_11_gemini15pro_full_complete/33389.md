chunters:/ˈtʃʌntəz/ (British English) | n. |抱怨者，牢骚满腹的人|People who complain habitually, especially about trivial matters.  They often express their dissatisfaction in a low, monotonous tone.|The old man was one of those chunters who always found something to moan about.  那个老头就是那种牢骚满腹的人，总能找到抱怨的东西。|grumblers, complainers, whiners|contented people|noun


chunter:/ˈtʃʌntə(r)/ (British English) | v. |抱怨，嘀咕，发牢骚|To complain or grumble, typically in a low, monotonous tone.|He chuntered about the price of petrol all the way to the station. 他一路上都在抱怨汽油价格。|grumble, complain, moan, whine|praise, commend|verb
