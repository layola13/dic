flabellation:/ˌflæbəˈleɪʃən/
n.
扇动，煽动; 用扇子扇;
the act of fanning or flapping.
The flabellation of the bird's wings created a gentle breeze. 鸟儿翅膀的扇动产生了一阵微风。

The constant flabellation of the peacock's tail feathers was mesmerizing.  孔雀不断扇动尾羽的动作令人着迷。


近义词:fanning, waving, flapping
反义词:stillness, motionlessness
词性:noun
