epigaea:/ˌɛpɪˈdʒiːə/| n. | trailing arbutus, mayflower | A small, flowering plant ( *Epigaea repens*) that grows close to the ground and has fragrant pink or white flowers, blooming in early spring. |  例句：The epigaea is one of the first wildflowers to bloom in spring.  春天里最早开花的野花之一就是匍匐香柏。

例句：We found a patch of epigaea blooming in the woods. 我们在树林里发现了一片盛开的匍匐香柏。| 近义词：mayflower, ground laurel | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun


Etymology: From Greek *epi* (“upon”) + *gaia* or *ge* (“earth”), referring to its low-growing habit.
