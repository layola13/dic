intimating:/ˈɪntɪmeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |暗示，提示|To make known subtly and indirectly; hint. |He intimated that he would resign if his demands were not met. 他暗示如果他的要求得不到满足，他就会辞职。|She intimated her disapproval with a raised eyebrow. 她扬起眉毛暗示了她的不满。|Synonyms: suggest, imply, hint, allude to |Antonyms: declare, state, announce, proclaim |verb
