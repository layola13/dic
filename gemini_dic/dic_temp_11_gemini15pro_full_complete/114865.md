mammology:/məˈmɑːlədʒi/
noun
哺乳动物学
The study of mammals.
She specialized in mammalogy, focusing on the behavior of primates. 她专门研究哺乳动物学，重点研究灵长类动物的行为。
近义词:  theriology
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性:  n.

Etymology:  From Modern Latin *mammalogia*, from mammal + -logy.  Ultimately from Latin mamma ("breast") + Greek -λογία (-logia, “study of”).
