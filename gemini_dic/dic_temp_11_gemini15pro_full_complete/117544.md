mega:/ˈmɛɡə/| adj. n.|巨大的，百万的；兆|A combining form meaning “great,” “large,” “a million,” used in the formation of compound words: megabyte; megaton.|例句：A megastar is a very famous and successful performer. 巨星是一位非常有名且成功的表演者。

例句：The company launched a mega-advertising campaign. 该公司发起了一场大型广告宣传活动。|近义词：huge, enormous, gigantic, immense, vast|反义词：micro, tiny, small|词性：形容词，名词

Etymology->
From Greek *megas* "great"
