gnatworm:/ˈnætˌwɜːrm/| n. |蚊蚋幼虫；摇蚊幼虫|The larva of a gnat, especially a midge. It is often aquatic and used as fish bait. | Fishermen often use gnatworms as bait. 渔民经常用摇蚊幼虫作诱饵。|The gnatworms wriggled in the muddy water. 摇蚊幼虫在浑浊的水里蠕动。|近义词：bloodworm, midge larva|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider adult gnat/midge)|noun

词源:
gnat + worm.  "Gnat" comes from Old English "gnæt," and "worm" comes from Old English "wyrm."
