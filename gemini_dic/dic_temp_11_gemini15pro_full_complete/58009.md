强劲地，有力地
With force and energy; compellingly.
The music pulsed drivingly, urging everyone to dance.  音乐强劲地跳动着，促使每个人都去跳舞。
She spoke drivingly about the need for change. 她强劲地谈到了变革的必要性。
近义词: forcefully, powerfully, compellingly, energetically, vigorously
反义词: weakly, feebly, gently
