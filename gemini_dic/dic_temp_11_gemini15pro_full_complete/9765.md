antoninus:/ˌæntəˈnaɪnəs/ | n. | 安东尼努斯（古罗马皇帝） | A Roman emperor.  Marcus Aurelius Antoninus (121-180 AD) is considered one of the most important Stoic philosophers. | Example: Antoninus Pius was known for his just and peaceful reign. 安东尼努斯·庇护以其公正与和平的统治而闻名。 | Example: The Meditations of Marcus Aurelius Antoninus offer profound insights into Stoic philosophy. 马可·奥勒留·安东尼努斯的《沉思录》对斯多葛哲学提供了深刻的见解。 | 近义词: emperor (皇帝), ruler (统治者) | 反义词: subject (臣民) | 词性: noun (名词)

词源: Derived from the Roman family name *Antoninus*.  The origin of the name is uncertain, but it may be related to the Etruscan name *Antonius*. 源于罗马家族姓氏*Antoninus*。该姓氏的起源不确定，但可能与伊特鲁里亚语的姓氏 *Antonius* 有关。
