nurser:/ˈnɜːrsər/ (US), /ˈnɜːsə/ (UK)| n.|保育员，育婴员；托儿所；苗圃|A person who takes care of babies or young children, especially one who is trained and employed to do this.  Also, a place where young children or plants are cared for.|The nurser rocked the baby gently. 保育员轻轻地摇着婴儿。|Synonyms: caregiver, childminder, nanny, nursery school teacher|Antonyms: none|Noun
