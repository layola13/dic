burkina:/bɜːrˈkiːnə/
n.
布基纳法索 (旧称上沃尔特)
A landlocked country in West Africa, Burkina Faso was formerly called Upper Volta.
Burkina Faso is a landlocked country in West Africa. 布基纳法索是一个位于西非的内陆国家。
The capital of Burkina Faso is Ouagadougou. 布基纳法索的首都是瓦加杜古。

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Moore words *burkina* ("upright people," singular *burkin’bi*) and *faso* ("fatherland"), adopted in 1984 upon renaming from Upper Volta.  The name *Burkina* is from the Moore language spoken by the Mossi people, and *Faso* is from the Dyula language.
