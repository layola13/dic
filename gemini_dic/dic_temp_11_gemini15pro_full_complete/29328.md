castigators:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪtərz/
noun
惩罚者；严厉批评者
People who criticize or punish someone harshly.
The politician faced a barrage of questions from the castigators in the media.  这位政治家面临来自媒体上批评者们的连番质问。
synonyms: critics, censurers, reprovers, rebukers, chastisers
antonyms: praisers, commenders, flatterers
词性: 名词

castigate:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪt/
verb
严厉批评；惩罚
To criticize or punish someone harshly.
The teacher castigated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师严厉地批评了考试作弊的学生。
synonyms: criticize, censure, reprove, rebuke, chastise
antonyms: praise, commend, flatter
词性: 动词
