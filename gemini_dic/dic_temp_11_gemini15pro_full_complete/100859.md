introspect:/ˌɪntrəˈspekt/ | v. | 内省，反省 | To examine or observe one's own mental and emotional processes. |  He introspected on his past mistakes and vowed to do better. 他反省了过去的错误，并发誓要做得更好。 | She introspected deeply before making her final decision.  她在做出最终决定之前进行了深刻的反省。 | 近义词: contemplate, reflect, ponder, meditate | 反义词: ignore, disregard | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin introspectus, past participle of introspicere "to look within," from intro- "inwardly" + specere "to look."
