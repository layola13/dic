copublishing:/koʊˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ/
| n. | 共同出版；联合出版 | The act of publishing something together with another publisher. |  Example:  The small press explored copublishing with a larger company to reach a wider audience. 小出版社探索与一家更大的公司共同出版，以接触更广泛的读者。| Synonym: joint publication, collaborative publishing | Antonym: sole publishing | noun

copublish:/koʊˈpʌblɪʃ/
| v. | 共同出版；联合出版 | To publish something jointly with another publisher. | Example: They decided to copublish the book with a university press. 他们决定与一家大学出版社共同出版这本书。 | Synonym: jointly publish, collaborate on publishing | Antonym: solely publish | verb
