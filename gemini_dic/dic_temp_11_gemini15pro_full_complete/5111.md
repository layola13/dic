allegement: /əˈledʒmənt/ | n. | 减轻，缓解，缓和; (痛苦等)的减轻; 主张，断言; | An allegation is a claim or assertion that someone has done something illegal or wrong, typically one made without proof.  An *allegement* is the act of making such a claim, or the claim itself (though this usage is less common).|  The newspaper published an allegement of corruption against the mayor. 这家报纸发表了一篇指控市长腐败的文章。 | Near synonyms: assertion, claim, declaration. | Antonyms: denial, refutation, retraction. | noun
