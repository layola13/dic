nonmendicant: /ˌnɒnˈmɛndɪkənt/ | adj. n.| 非乞讨的；非托钵僧的；自食其力的(人) | Not begging; not living by asking for alms; self-supporting.  A person who does not beg. |  Example: The nonmendicant monks supported themselves through farming. 这些非托钵僧的僧侣通过耕作养活自己。 | Example:  He lived a nonmendicant life, earning his living as a carpenter. 他过着自食其力的生活，以木匠为生。 | 近义词：self-supporting, independent, self-reliant | 反义词: mendicant, begging, indigent | 词性: adjective, noun

词源:  From non- + mendicant.  "Mendicant" comes from Late Latin mendicantem (nominative mendicans), present participle of mendicare "to beg," from mendicus "beggar."
