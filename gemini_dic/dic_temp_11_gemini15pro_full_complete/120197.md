minefields:/ˈmaɪnˌfiːldz/
noun
雷区；充满危险的境地
An area of land or water that contains mines.  A situation that is potentially dangerous or risky.
The peace talks are a political minefield. 和谈是一个政治雷区。
Negotiating a salary increase can be a minefield. 谈判加薪可能是一个雷区。
synonyms:danger zone, hazard, risk, peril
antonyms:safe zone, haven, sanctuary
n.
