mousk: /mʌsk/ | n. |麝香; 麝香鹿| A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance which is produced by a male musk deer. It is used in perfumes. |The perfume had a strong scent of mousk. 这款香水有浓烈的麝香味。|近义词: civet, ambergris |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其气味相反的词，例如：freshness 清新) | 词性: 名词
