outweave: /ˌaʊtˈwiːv/
verb
交织，编织出;胜过(织布)
To weave something elaborately or surpass in weaving.  The intricate tapestry was outwoven with threads of gold and silver. 这幅复杂的挂毯用金线和银线交织而成。
The skilled artisan outwove all his competitors.  这位技艺精湛的工匠胜过了他所有的竞争对手。
Synonym: interweave, outdo
Antonym: unravel, disentangle
词性: 动词
