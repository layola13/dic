antiliberalistic:/ˌæntiˌlɪbərəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反自由主义的
Opposed to the principles of liberalism, especially in political or economic contexts.
The senator's antiliberalistic stance alienated many moderate voters.  参议员的反自由主义立场疏远了许多温和派选民。
His antiliberalistic views were often met with strong opposition.  他的反自由主义观点经常遭到强烈反对。
近义词: illiberal, reactionary, conservative
反义词: liberal, progressive
词性: adjective
