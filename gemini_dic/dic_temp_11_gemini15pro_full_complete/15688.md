bakshish:/bækˈʃiːʃ/| n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially in some Middle Eastern and Asian countries; also, a bribe.|例句：He offered the border guard a small bakshish to speed up the process. 他给了边防警卫一点小费，以加快通关速度。

例句：She received a generous bakshish for her excellent service at the hotel.  她在酒店提供了优质的服务，收到了丰厚的小费。|近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Persian بخشش (bakhshesh), meaning "gift" or "forgiveness".  Ultimately from the verb بخشیدن (bakhshidan), "to give" or "to forgive".
