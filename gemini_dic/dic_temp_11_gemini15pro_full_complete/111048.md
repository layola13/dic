lissencephala:/ˌlɪsənˈsefələ/ | n. | 平滑脑，无脑回畸形 | Lissencephaly is a rare, gene-linked brain malformation characterized by the absence of normal convolutions (folds) in the cerebral cortex and an abnormally thick cortex. | Lissencephaly is a serious neurological disorder that can cause severe developmental delays. 平滑脑是一种严重的神经系统疾病，会导致严重的生长发育迟缓。 |  The child diagnosed with lissencephaly required constant medical care.  被诊断患有平滑脑的儿童需要持续的医疗护理。 | Synonyms: agyria | Antonyms:  N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
