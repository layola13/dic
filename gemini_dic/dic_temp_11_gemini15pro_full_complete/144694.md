ovalbumin:/ˌoʊvəlˈbjuːmɪn/| n. |卵白蛋白|The primary protein constituent of egg white, comprising approximately 54% of the total protein content. It plays a crucial role in egg white's gelling properties and is often used in research and various food applications.|Researchers studied the effect of heat on ovalbumin's structure. 研究人员研究了热对卵清蛋白结构的影响。|Synonyms: egg white protein|Antonyms: None|noun
