monarchs:/ˈmɒnəks/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnɑːrks/ (US)| n.|君主，帝王|A sovereign head of state, especially a king, queen, or emperor. |例句：The monarchs of Europe met to discuss the crisis. 欧洲的君主们开会讨论危机。

例句：  Many monarchs throughout history have been overthrown. 历史上许多君主都被推翻了。|近义词：ruler, sovereign, king, queen, emperor|反义词：subject, citizen|词性:noun
