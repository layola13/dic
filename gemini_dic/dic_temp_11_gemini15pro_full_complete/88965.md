holidayed:/ˈhɒlədeɪd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːlədeɪd/ (US)| v.|度假|Spent time on holiday; vacationed.  The word originates from "holy day," referring to religious festivals, which later evolved to encompass general breaks from work. The "-ed" suffix indicates the past tense form of the verb.|例句：They holidayed in Spain last summer. 去年夏天他们在西班牙度假。|近义词：vacationed, travelled, toured|反义词:worked|词性:动词
