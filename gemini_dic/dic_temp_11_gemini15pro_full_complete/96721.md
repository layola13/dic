inefficaciously:/ˌɪnɪfɪˈkeɪʃəsli/
adverb
无效地；无用地；徒劳地
in a way that does not produce the desired effect or achieve the intended result.
The medicine was administered inefficaciously, and the patient's condition worsened.  这种药用得无效，病人的病情恶化了。
The team worked inefficaciously, failing to meet the deadline.  团队工作效率低下，未能按期完成任务。
近义词:fruitlessly, futilely, unsuccessfully, ineffectively, uselessly
反义词:effectively, efficiently, successfully
词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *inefficax, from in- "not" + efficax "effective," from efficere "to work out, accomplish," from ex- "out" + facere "to do, make."
