affricated:/ˈæfrɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.| 塞擦音化的，发生塞擦音的|Of or relating to a speech sound that begins as a stop and releases as a fricative, such as the ch in "church" or the j in "judge".  指一个语音，开始是塞音，释放是擦音，例如“church”中的“ch”或“judge”中的“j”。|The "ch" sound in "chair" is affricated. “chair”中的“ch”音是塞擦音。|近义词:fricative, sibilant|反义词:plosive, stop|词性:形容词

affricate:/ˈæfrɪkət/| n.| 塞擦音|A consonant sound that begins as a stop and releases as a fricative, such as the ch in "church" or the j in "judge". 指一个辅音，开始是塞音，释放是擦音，例如“church”中的“ch”或“judge”中的“j”。|The sounds /tʃ/ and /dʒ/ are affricates. /tʃ/ 和 /dʒ/ 是塞擦音。|近义词:fricative, sibilant|反义词:plosive, stop|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *affricātus, past participle of affricāre (“to rub against”), from ad (“to”) + fricāre (“to rub”).  来自晚期拉丁语 *affricātus，affricāre（“摩擦”）的过去分词，来自 ad（“到”）+ fricāre（“摩擦”）。
