devolute:/ˌdiːvəˈluːt/ | adj. | 下放的，移交的 | Transferred or delegated to a lower level or authority. | The king devolved some of his power to the local governors. 国王将部分权力下放给地方官员。 | The company devolved responsibility for customer service to a new department. 公司将客户服务的责任移交给了一个新部门。 | 近义词: delegate, transfer, devolve | 反义词: centralize, retain | 词性: adjective

devolute:/ˈdɛvəˌlut/ | v. |  (使)下放，(使)移交 |  To transfer or delegate (power, responsibility, etc.) to a lower level or authority. | The government devolved the power to set tax rates to the states. 政府将制定税率的权力下放给各州。|  The manager devolved some of his tasks to his assistant. 经理将他的部分任务移交给了他的助手。 | 近义词: delegate, transfer, entrust | 反义词: centralize, retain | 词性: verb

词源: From Latin devolutus, past participle of devolvere "to roll down, transfer," from de- "down" + volvere "to roll."
