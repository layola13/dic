heterochronic:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrɒnɪk/
adjective
异时性的，发育异时的
Relating to a change in the timing of developmental events in an organism compared to its ancestors.
Heterochronic mutations can alter the rate or timing of developmental processes. 异时性突变可以改变发育过程的速度或时间。
The heterochronic shift in flowering time allows the plant to adapt to a new climate.  开花时间的异时性转变使植物能够适应新的气候。
近义词: allochronic, heterochronous
反义词: isochronic, synchronous
词性: adjective

heterochrony:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrɒni/
noun
异时性，发育异时
A developmental change in the timing or rate of events, leading to changes in size and shape.
Heterochrony plays a significant role in the evolution of new species. 异时性在新物种的进化中起着重要作用。
The study of heterochrony helps us understand the diversity of life on Earth. 对异时性的研究有助于我们理解地球上生命的的多样性。
近义词: allochrony, heterochronism
反义词: isochrony, synchrony
词性: noun
