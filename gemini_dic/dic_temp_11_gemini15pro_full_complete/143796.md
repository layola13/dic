outnumbers:/ˌaʊtˈnʌmbərz/
verb
在数量上超过
exceeds in number
The protesters outnumbered the police. 抗议者人数超过了警察。
Synonyms: surpasses, exceeds, outweighs
Antonyms: is outnumbered by, is fewer than
verb
