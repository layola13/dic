hypermasculinity:/ˌhaɪpərˌmæskjʊˈlɪnɪti/
|n.|
|超级男子气概；极端男子气概|
|Exaggerated adherence to traditional masculine gender roles, emphasizing strength, aggression, and dominance.|
|The film glorifies hypermasculinity and violence. 这部电影美化了极端男子气概和暴力。|

|The study examined the link between hypermasculinity and crime. 这项研究调查了极端男子气概和犯罪之间的联系。|

|近义词: machismo, exaggerated masculinity|
|反义词: femininity,  femininity|
|词性: noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, above, beyond"
masculinity (noun): from Late Middle English, from Old French masculinité, from Latin masculinus "male, masculine," from masculus "male"
