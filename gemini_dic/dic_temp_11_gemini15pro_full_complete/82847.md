gulfy:/ˈɡʌlfi/| adj.|贪婪的，狼吞虎咽的|Having a large appetite and eating greedily; ravenous.  Characterized by a large or deep chasm or gulf.|He had a gulfy appetite after the long hike.  远足后，他的胃口大开。|近义词：voracious, ravenous, gluttonous|反义词: abstemious, temperate|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "gulf" (meaning a deep chasm) + "-y" (forming adjectives).  The imagery suggests a vast emptiness that needs to be filled, hence the connection to a large appetite.
