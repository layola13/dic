forespeaking:/fɔrˈspiːkɪŋ/ |v. |预言，预示；预先约定；抢先发言| To predict or foretell something; to speak for someone else, often without their permission; to reserve or bespeak something in advance. |The witch forespoke a great famine. 女巫预言了一场大饥荒。|Synonyms: predict, foretell, prophesy, anticipate, forecast, divine, augur, portend, presage, betoken. |Antonyms:  retrospect,  review, hindsight|verb
forespeaking:/fɔrˈspiːkɪŋ/ |n. |预言；预示|The act of predicting or foretelling something. |Her forespeaking of the disaster proved accurate. 她对灾难的预言被证明是准确的。|Synonyms: prediction, prophecy, forecast. |Antonyms: hindsight, review.|noun
