holloway:/ˈhɒləweɪ/ (UK), /ˈhɑːloʊweɪ/ (US)| n. |小路，山谷中的小路，通常两边有堤岸或树篱|A sunken road or track, typically one bordered by banks or hedges. |We walked along the holloway, shaded from the sun by the trees. 我们沿着这条两边有树遮荫的小路走。|The children played in the holloway, building forts in the banks. 孩子们在小路里玩耍，在堤岸上建堡垒。|近义词：lane, path, track, byway|反义词：ridge, hilltop|词性: 名词

词源: From Old English *holhweg*, from *holh* "hollow" + *weg* "way".  Meaning a road in a hollow or valley.
