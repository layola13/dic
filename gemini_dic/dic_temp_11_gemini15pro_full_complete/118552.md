mesophloeum: /ˈmeɪzəʊfliːəm/ | n. | 中树皮 | The middle bark of a tree, located between the outer bark (periderm) and the inner bark (phloem).  It is primarily composed of parenchyma cells. | The mesophloeum of the giant redwood is incredibly thick, providing insulation and protection. 巨型红杉的中间树皮非常厚，提供隔热和保护作用。 | 近义词: None (highly specific term) | 反义词: None (highly specific term) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *mésos* (middle) + *phlóios* (bark).
