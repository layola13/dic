firesides:/ˈfaɪərsaɪdz/| n.|炉边；壁炉旁；家庭夜晚|The area around a fireplace, often seen as a symbol of warmth, comfort, and family.  It can also refer to an informal gathering or atmosphere of friendly conversation.|We spent many happy evenings sitting by the fireside. 我们在炉边度过了许多愉快的夜晚。|Near synonyms: hearth, fireplace, fire, home|Antonyms: outdoors, outside|Noun
