mispay: /ˌmɪsˈpeɪ/ | n. vt. | 错误付款，支付错误 | An incorrect payment, either paying the wrong amount or to the wrong person. | The company mispaid their employees last month and had to issue corrections. 公司上个月员工工资发错了，不得不进行更正。| The bank mispaid the invoice, sending the money to the wrong account. 银行支付发票时出错，把钱汇到了错误的账户。 | 近义词: overpay, underpay, incorrect payment | 反义词: correct payment | 词性: noun, verb

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning wrong or bad) + "pay" (verb meaning to give money for goods or services).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss." "Pay" comes from Old French "paier" meaning "to pacify, appease, satisfy a debt."
