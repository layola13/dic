legitimation: /lɪˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 合法化；认可 | The act of making something legitimate. |  The legitimation of his claim took years.  他的主张的合法化花了数年时间。 | The new law provides legitimation for the government's actions. 新法律为政府的行为提供了合法性。 | validation, authorization, sanction | invalidation, prohibition, condemnation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin legitimatus, past participle of legitimare “to make lawful,” from Latin legitimus “lawful,” from lex (genitive legis) “law.”
