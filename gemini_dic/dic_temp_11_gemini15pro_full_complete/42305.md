counter-opening:/ˈkaʊntərˌoʊpənɪŋ/ | n. |还盘；反建议|An offer made in response to a previous offer, typically in a negotiation or bargaining situation.  It signifies a rejection of the initial offer and proposes alternative terms.|例句：His counter-opening was a shrewd move, securing a better deal. 他的还价是一步高明的棋，确保了更好的交易。

例句：The seller rejected the buyer's initial offer and presented a counter-opening. 卖方拒绝了买方的最初报价，并提出了还价。|近义词：counteroffer, counterproposal |反义词:initial offer, original offer |词性:名词
