multiflagellated:/ˌmʌltiˈflædʒəleɪtɪd/
adjective
多鞭毛的
Having many flagella (whip-like appendages used for locomotion).
Example: Multiflagellated bacteria are common in aquatic environments. 多鞭毛细菌在水生环境中很常见。
Example: The multiflagellated protozoan moved rapidly through the water.  这种多鞭毛原生动物在水中快速移动。
Synonym: polyflagellate
Antonym: uniflagellate, biflagellate
word type: adjective
