airfreighted:/ˈɛərˌfreɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)|空运的|Transported by aircraft.  Derived from "air" + "freight" + "-ed".  "Freight" comes from the Middle Dutch word "vrecht" meaning "freight, cargo, load."|The flowers were airfreighted from Holland. 这些花是从荷兰空运过来的。|Shipped by air, flown|Shipped by sea, trucked|verb
