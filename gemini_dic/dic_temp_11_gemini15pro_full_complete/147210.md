overmannered: /ˌoʊvərˈmænərd/ | adj. | 过分拘礼的，矫揉造作的 | Excessively polite or refined in a way that seems artificial or affected.  Showing too much politeness in a way that is not natural. |  He was so overmannered it was almost comical. 他太拘谨了，简直滑稽。|  She gave an overmannered laugh. 她矫揉造作地笑了笑。 | 近义词: affected, pretentious, artificial, stiff, formal | 反义词: natural, genuine, informal, casual | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "mannered" (adjective derived from "manner," meaning "a way of acting or behaving").
