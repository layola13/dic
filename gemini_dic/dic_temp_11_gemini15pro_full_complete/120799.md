misassembled:/ˌmɪsəˈsɛmbəld/
| adj. | 错误组装的，装配错误的 | Incorrectly put together; assembled in the wrong way. | The toy was misassembled, so it wouldn't work.  这个玩具装错了，所以不能用。 | The furniture was delivered misassembled, which caused a lot of frustration. 家具送到时装配错误，这让人很沮丧。 | incorrectly assembled, wrongly put together | correctly assembled, properly assembled | adjective

misassemble:/ˌmɪsəˈsɛmbəl/
| vt. | 装配错误，组装错 | To assemble something incorrectly. | I misassembled the bookshelf, and now it's wobbly. 我把书架装错了，现在它摇摇晃晃的。 |  I tried to misassemble the puzzle pieces to make it more challenging. 我试着把拼图碎片装错，让它更有挑战性。| assemble incorrectly, put together wrongly | assemble correctly, put together properly | verb
