nontactically:/nɒnˈtæktɪkli/
adverb|非战术地；非策略地|Without tactical or strategic considerations; in a way that is not related to tactics or strategy.|He approached the problem nontactically, focusing on the immediate needs rather than long-term goals. 他非策略性地处理这个问题，专注于眼前的需要而不是长期目标。|
The company nontactically invested in a failing business, leading to significant losses. 该公司非策略性地投资于一家失败的企业，导致重大损失。|
Synonyms:  unstrategically, aimlessly, haphazardly, randomly
Antonyms: strategically, tactically, deliberately, purposefully
词性: 副词
