galvanoglyphy:/ɡælˌvænəˈɡlɪfi/ | n. | 電鑄版術，電版印刷術 |  The art or process of producing plates for printing by electrotyping.  It involves depositing a thin layer of metal onto a mold or matrix using electrolysis.  This creates a durable replica that can be used for printing. |  Galvanoglyphy was a popular printing technique in the 19th century. 電鑄版術是19世紀流行的印刷技術。 | synonym: electrotyping | antonym: N/A (No direct antonym exists as it's a specific technical process) | noun
