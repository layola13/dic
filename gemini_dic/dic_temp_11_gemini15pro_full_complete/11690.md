armourers:/ˈɑːmərərz/
noun
军械师，盔甲制造者
A person who makes or repairs armour.
The armourers worked diligently to prepare the knights for battle. 军械师们勤奋地工作，为骑士们备战。
Synonyms: armorers, weaponsmiths, blacksmiths
Antonyms: None.
n.
