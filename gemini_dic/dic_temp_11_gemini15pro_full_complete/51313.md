detainder:/dɪˈteɪnər/| n.|拘留令，扣押; 阻止离去或行动之物|A writ authorizing the further detention of someone already in custody.  A detainer is a request filed by a criminal justice agency with the institution in which a prisoner is incarcerated, asking the institution either to hold the prisoner for the agency or to notify the agency when release of the prisoner is imminent.|The immigration authorities placed a detainer on him. 移民局对他发出了拘留令。|Synonyms: warrant, hold, custody|Antonyms: release, liberation|noun
