fieldworkers:/ˈfiːldˌwɜːrkərz/ (US), /ˈfiːldˌwɜːkəz/ (UK)| n. |野外工作人员，现场工作人员|People who work in the field, often doing research, manual labor, or providing services.  These could be agricultural workers, researchers collecting data, social workers visiting clients, etc.|The fieldworkers collected data on the local bird population.|近义词：field hands, researchers, laborers, outreach workers|反义词: office workers|Noun
