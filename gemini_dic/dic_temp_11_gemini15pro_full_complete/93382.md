illiberally:/ɪˈlɪbərəli/
adverb
狭隘地；不自由地；吝啬地
In a manner that is not liberal; in a narrow-minded, bigoted, or stingy way.
He treated his employees illiberally, offering low wages and long hours. 他对待员工很刻薄，工资低，工时长。
The politician illiberally dismissed the concerns of the protesters.  这位政客狭隘地驳回了抗议者的担忧。


近义词:narrowly, bigotedly, meanly, stingily
反义词:liberally, generously, tolerantly, open-mindedly
词性:adverb

词源:
From il- (not) + liberal + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin liber (free).
