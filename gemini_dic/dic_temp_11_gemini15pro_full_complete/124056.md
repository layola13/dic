morpho:/ˈmɔːrfoʊ/ | n. | 形态，词素 |  A morpheme, especially one considered in relation to other morphemes in a word.  A combining form meaning “form” or “shape,” used in the formation of compound words: morphology. |  The morpho "bio" indicates something related to life.  “bio”这个词素表示与生命有关。 | 近义词: form, shape | 反义词:  (None - Abstract concept) | 词性: noun
