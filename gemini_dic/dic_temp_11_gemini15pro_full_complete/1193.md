acclivity: /əˈklɪvɪti/
n.
上坡，斜坡
An upward slope.
The car struggled up the acclivity. 汽车艰难地爬上斜坡。

近义词: ascent, incline, slope, rise, grade
反义词: declivity, descent, decline

词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *acclivitas, from acclivis (“sloping upwards”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + clivus (“slope”).
