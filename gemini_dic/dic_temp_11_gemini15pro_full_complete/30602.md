cerebralist:/ˈsɛrəbrəlɪst/| n.| 大脑至上主义者；理性主义者|A person who emphasizes intellectual activity over emotions or physicality; an intellectual.| He's such a cerebralist, he analyzes everything to death and never just enjoys the moment. 他真是个理性主义者，什么事都分析得透彻，从来不懂得享受当下。|近义词：intellectual, rationalist, highbrow|反义词：emotionalist, sentimentalist|词性:noun
cerebral:/ˈsɛrəbrəl/| adj.| 大脑的；理智的；智力的|Relating to the cerebrum or the brain; intellectual rather than emotional or physical.|  Chess is a very cerebral game.  国际象棋是一项非常需要智力的游戏。|近义词：intellectual, mental|反义词：emotional, instinctive|词性:adjective

词源: From Latin cerebrum ("brain") + -al ("relating to") + -ist ("one who practices or believes in").
