idolising:/ˈaɪdəlaɪzɪŋ/
Verb, present participle|崇拜；偶像化|The act of admiring someone excessively and treating them as an idol. |She was idolising a famous pop star. 她正在崇拜一位著名的流行歌星。|Near synonyms: worshipping, adoring, revering|Antonyms: criticizing, condemning, disparaging|v.

idolising:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
Adjective|崇拜的；偶像化的|Characterized by excessive admiration and treatment as an idol. |Her idolising gaze made him uncomfortable. 她崇拜的目光让他感到不舒服。|Near synonyms: worshipping, adoring, revering|Antonyms: critical, condemning, disparaging|adj.
