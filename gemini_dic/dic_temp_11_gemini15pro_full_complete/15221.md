backed-out:/ˌbækt ˈaʊt/ | adj. | 撤销的，取消的，退出(协议、安排等)的 |  Withdrawn from a commitment, promise, or agreement.  |  The investor backed out of the deal at the last minute. 投资者在最后一刻退出了这笔交易。 | withdrawn, reneged, cancelled | committed, involved | adjective

backed-out:/ˌbækt ˈaʊt/ | v. | 退出，撤回 |  To withdraw from a commitment, promise, or agreement. | They backed out of the contract after discovering hidden clauses.  他们发现隐藏条款后就退出了合同。 | withdraw, renege, retreat | commit, proceed | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "back" (meaning to reverse or retreat) + "out" (meaning away from or from within).  The combination emphasizes the act of reversing a course of action and withdrawing from something.
