beamers:/ˈbiːmərz/| n.|投影仪，（尤指）幻灯机|A beamer is a device, especially a slide projector, that projects images onto a screen. |例句：The professor used a beamer to show us the slides. 教授用投影仪给我们展示了幻灯片。

|例句：The beamers in the conference room weren't working. 会议室里的投影仪坏了。|近义词：projector, slide projector|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps screen as it receives the projection)|词性:noun (plural)
