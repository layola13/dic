crotchetily:/ˈkrɒtʃɪtɪli/ | adv. | 乖戾地，古怪地 | In a way that is bad-tempered, irritable, and eccentric. | He answered me crotchetily, clearly annoyed by the interruption. 他乖戾地回答我，显然是被打扰了。| 近义词: irritably, grumpily, peevishly | 反义词: pleasantly, amiably, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

crotchety:/ˈkrɒtʃɪti/ | adj. | 乖戾的，古怪的 | Bad-tempered, irritable, and eccentric. | My grandfather is getting quite crotchety in his old age. 我的祖父年纪大了，变得相当古怪。 | 近义词: irritable, grumpy, cantankerous | 反义词: good-natured, affable, agreeable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From crotch (a whim or fancy) + -ety (a suffix forming adjectives indicating a quality).  "Crotch" in this sense is obsolete and unrelated to the anatomical term.  Ultimately from Old French "croc" (hook).
