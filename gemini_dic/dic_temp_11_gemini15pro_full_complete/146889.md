overinhibited:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd/| adj.|过度抑制的，过分拘谨的|Excessively restrained or reserved in behavior or expression, often due to psychological factors.|She was so overinhibited that she could barely speak in public. 她太拘谨了，以至于几乎无法在公共场合讲话。|近义词:repressed, shy, withdrawn, reserved, inhibited|反义词:uninhibited, outgoing, extroverted, expressive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "inhibited" (from Latin "inhibere" meaning "to restrain, hold back").
