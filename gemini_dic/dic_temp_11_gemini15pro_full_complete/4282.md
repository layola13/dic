airway:/ˈɛəˌweɪ/ (US), /ˈeəˌweɪ/ (UK)| n.|呼吸道；航线；航空公司|The passage by which air reaches the lungs.  A route regularly used by aircraft.|例句：The doctor checked the patient's airway to ensure it was clear. 医生检查了病人的呼吸道，确保它是畅通的。

例句：The airway between London and New York is one of the busiest in the world.  伦敦和纽约之间的航线是世界上最繁忙的航线之一。|近义词：passage, route, airline, carrier|反义词:blockage (for respiratory sense)|n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From air + way.  The respiratory sense dates from the late 19th century.  The aviation sense dates from the early 20th century.
