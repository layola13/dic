erythrocytes:/ɪˈrɪθrəˌsaɪts/| n.|红细胞|Red blood cells are essential for carrying oxygen from the lungs to the body's tissues. 红细胞对于将氧气从肺部输送到身体组织至关重要。|A decrease in erythrocytes can lead to anemia. 红细胞减少会导致贫血。|Synonyms: red blood cells|Antonyms: None|Noun
