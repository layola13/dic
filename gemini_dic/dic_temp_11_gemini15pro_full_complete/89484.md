honeying:/ˈhʌniɪŋ/| v. |奉承；说甜言蜜语；（对…）甜言蜜语|To flatter or cajole someone with sweet words and insincere compliments.  It implies excessive flattery, often with an ulterior motive.|例句：He was honeying the boss in hopes of a promotion.  他为了升职而奉承老板。

例句：She was honeying him up to get him to agree to her plan. 她用甜言蜜语哄他同意她的计划。|近义词：flattering, cajoling, sweet-talking, buttering up|反义词：criticizing, condemning|词性:动词
