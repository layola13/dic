outachieved:/ˌaʊtəˈtʃiːvd/
verb
超越；胜过；比…更出色
To perform better than someone or something.
He outachieved his classmates in math. 他在数学方面超过了他的同学们。
The new product outachieved all expectations in its first year.  这款新产品第一年的业绩超出了所有预期。
surpass, outperform, excel, exceed
underperform, lag behind
