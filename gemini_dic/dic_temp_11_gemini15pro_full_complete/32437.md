chimera: /kaɪˈmɪərə/ /kɪˈmɪərə/ | n. |  嵌合体；虚构的怪物；幻想；妄想 | A fire-breathing female monster with a lion's head, a goat's body, and a serpent's tail in Greek mythology.  A thing that is hoped or wished for but in fact is illusory or impossible to achieve. | Example 1: The scientists created a chimera by combining the DNA of two different species. 科学家们通过结合两种不同物种的DNA创造了一个嵌合体。

Example 2:  His dream of becoming a rock star was just a chimera. 他成为摇滚明星的梦想只是一个幻想。| 近义词: illusion, fantasy, delusion, monster | 反义词: reality | 词性: noun
