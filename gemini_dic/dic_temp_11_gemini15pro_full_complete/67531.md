extraditing:/ˈɛkstrəˌdaɪtɪŋ/
verb
引渡
the action of extraditing a person accused or convicted of a crime.
The government is seeking the extraditing of the fugitive from justice. 政府正在寻求将逃犯引渡回国受审。

extradite:/ˈɛkstrəˌdaɪt/
verb
引渡
hand over (a person accused or convicted of a crime) to the jurisdiction of another country or state.
The suspect was extradited to the US to face trial.  嫌疑犯被引渡到美国受审。


近义词:deporting, handing over, transferring
反义词:harboring, sheltering, protecting
词性:verb (present participle)
