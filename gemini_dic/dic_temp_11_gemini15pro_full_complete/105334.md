kick-in-the-pants:/ˌkɪk ɪn ðə ˈpænts/
noun
刺激；激励；警告
a strong warning or criticism that motivates someone to take action or improve their behavior
He needs a real kick in the pants to get him moving. 他需要真正的刺激才能让他行动起来。
motivation, incentive, push, jolt, spur
deterrent, discouragement, hindrance
