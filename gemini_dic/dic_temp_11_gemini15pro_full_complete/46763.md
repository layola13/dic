danewort:/ˈdeɪnˌwɜːrt/| n.|矮接骨木; 欧洲接骨木|A dwarf elder (Sambucus ebulus), native to Europe and parts of Asia and Africa. It has a strong, unpleasant smell and was traditionally used for medicinal purposes.|The danewort grew thick along the edges of the forest. 矮接骨木沿着森林边缘茂密地生长着。|近义词：dwarf elder, walewort, elder|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
