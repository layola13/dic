kwanzaa: /kwɑːnˈzɑː/
| n. |宽扎节|A seven-day festival celebrated by many African Americans from December 26 to January 1, based on African harvest celebrations and emphasizing the unity of African people.|We celebrate Kwanzaa every year. 我们每年都庆祝宽扎节。|Synonyms: harvest festival, celebration|Antonyms: N/A|noun
