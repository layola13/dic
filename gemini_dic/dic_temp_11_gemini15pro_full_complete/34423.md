claudius:/ˈklɔːdiəs/| n.|克劳狄斯 (罗马皇帝或男子名)|A Roman emperor, the fourth of the Julio-Claudian dynasty. Also a male given name.|例句：Claudius was the emperor who succeeded Caligula. 克劳狄斯是卡利古拉之后的皇帝。

例句：My uncle's name is Claudius. 我叔叔的名字是克劳狄斯。|近义词：(For the name) Claude,  (For the emperor)  no direct synonym|反义词:None|词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Roman clan name *Claudius*, possibly derived from Latin *claudus* meaning "lame" or "crippled."
