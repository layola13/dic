decorative:/ˈdekərətɪv/| adj.|装饰性的，装璜的|Serving no practical purpose but to look attractive. |The vase was purely decorative.  这个花瓶纯粹是为了装饰。|The decorative carvings on the furniture were exquisite. 家具上的装饰性雕刻精美绝伦。|近义词：ornamental, embellishing, adorning|反义词:functional, plain, austere|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin decorativus, from decorare ‘decorate’.
