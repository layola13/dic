irritability:/ˌɪrɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 易怒，烦躁 |The state of being easily annoyed or irritated. | Her irritability was evident in her sharp tone. 她尖锐的语气显露出她的烦躁。 |  He suffered from headaches and irritability due to lack of sleep. 由于缺乏睡眠，他饱受头痛和易怒的困扰。 | 近义词：irascibility, testiness, peevishness, cantankerousness | 反义词：patience, tolerance, calmness, placidity | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin irritabilitatem, from Latin irritabilis "irritable," from irritare "to excite, provoke."
