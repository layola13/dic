evaluation:/ɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 评估，评价 | The process of determining the significance, worth, or condition of usually by careful appraisal and study. |  The teacher gave an evaluation of each student's progress. 老师对每个学生的进步进行了评估。

The program underwent a thorough evaluation before being implemented.  这个项目在实施前经过了彻底的评估。 | synonyms: assessment, appraisal, judgment, review | antonyms: disregard, neglect | noun
