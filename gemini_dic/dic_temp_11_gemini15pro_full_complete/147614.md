overplenteous:/ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪəs/| adj.|过多的，过剩的|Excessively plentiful; superabundant.|The harvest was overplenteous this year, leaving the farmers with more crops than they could sell.|近义词：excessive, abundant, superfluous, copious|反义词:scarce, meager, insufficient|词性:形容词
Etymology: From over + plenteous.  "Over" signifies "excessively," and "plenteous" means "abundant."  Ultimately derived from Latin "plenus" (full).
