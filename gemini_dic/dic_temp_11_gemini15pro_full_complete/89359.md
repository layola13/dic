homomorphy:/ˌhōməˈmɔːrfi/ /ˌhoʊməˈmɔːrfi/ | n. |同形异义；同形性|A correspondence between the forms of two items, such as words, without a corresponding correspondence of meaning.|The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are examples of homomorphy.| 近义词: homonymy, homography| 反义词: polysemy |词性:noun
