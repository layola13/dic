burkini:/bɜːrˈkiːniː/ /bərˈkiːni/
n.
布基尼泳衣
A swimsuit that covers the whole body except the face, hands, and feet, designed to comply with Islamic dress codes.
She wore a burkini at the beach. 她穿着布基尼泳衣在海滩上。
Muslim swimsuit, full-body swimsuit
Bikini, swimsuit
noun
