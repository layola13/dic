dunam:/ˈdunəm/| n. |度南（面积单位）|A unit of area equal to 1,000 square meters (about 1/4 acre).|例句：The farmer owned ten dunams of land.  这位农民拥有十度南的土地。

例句：They purchased a plot of five dunams for their vineyard.  他们购买了一块五度南的土地来种植葡萄园。|近义词：acre, hectare|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
