but:/bʌt/
conj. | 但是，然而，可是 | used to introduce a phrase or clause contrasting with what has already been mentioned |  He's intelligent but lazy. 他很聪明，但是懒惰。|  yet, however, although | and | conjunction

but:/bʌt/
prep. | 除了，除...之外 | except; apart from | Everyone but me went to the party. 除了我，大家都去参加聚会了。| except, besides | including | preposition

but:/bʌt/
adv. | 仅仅，只不过 | only | He is but a child. 他只不过是个孩子。| only, just, merely |  | adverb
