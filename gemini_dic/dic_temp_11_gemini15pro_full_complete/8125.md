anti-austrians:/ˌæntiˈɔːstriənz/ | n. | 反奥地利学派人士 |  Those who oppose the theories of the Austrian School of economics, a school of thought that emphasizes individual action, private property, and free markets. |  The anti-austrians argue that government intervention is necessary to stabilize the economy. 反奥地利学派人士认为政府干预对于稳定经济是必要的。|  critics of Austrian economics, opponents of Austrian school, interventionists | free market advocates, Austrian economists | noun
