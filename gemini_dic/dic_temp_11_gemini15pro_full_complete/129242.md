noctule:/ˈnɒktjʊːl/ (UK) /ˈnɑːktʃuːl/ (US)| n.|夜蝠;大夜蝠|A large bat (Nyctalus noctula) common in Europe.|例句：The noctule is known for its high-pitched calls. 夜蝠以其高亢的叫声而闻名。

例句：Noctules are often seen flying high at dusk.  夜蝠经常在黄昏时分高飞。

近义词：bat|反义词:|词性:noun
