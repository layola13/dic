dodecasyllable:/ˌdoʊˌdekəˈsɪləbəl/ /ˌdəʊˌdekəˈsɪləbəl/
noun
十二音节的词，十二音节诗行
A word or line of verse having twelve syllables.
The poet employed dodecasyllables throughout the epic poem.  这位诗人整部史诗都使用了十二音节的诗行。
近义词: alexandrine (specifically for a line of iambic hexameter)
反义词: monosyllable
