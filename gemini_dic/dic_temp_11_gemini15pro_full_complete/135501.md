nonphysician:/ˌnɑːnfɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 非医生; 非医务人员 |  A person who is not a physician or medical doctor.  |  Example: The nonphysician staff at the hospital includes nurses, technicians, and administrative personnel.  医院的非医务人员包括护士、技术员和行政人员。 | Example:  Many nonphysicians work in healthcare settings. 许多非医务人员在医疗机构工作。 | Synonyms: non-doctor, paramedic (sometimes), healthcare professional (broader) | Antonyms: physician, doctor, medical doctor | noun
