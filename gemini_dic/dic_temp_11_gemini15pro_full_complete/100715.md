intralingual:/ˌɪntrəˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj.|语内的，内部语言的|Existing or occurring within a single language; involving only one language.  Relating to or operating within the same language.|The intralingual translation helped clarify the nuances of the original English text.|近义词：monolingual|反义词：interlingual|词性: adjective
