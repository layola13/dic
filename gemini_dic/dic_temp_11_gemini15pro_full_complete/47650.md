debonairness:/ˌdebənˈɛrnəs/
noun
儒雅；温文尔雅；落落大方
A sophisticated charm and confidence in manner and appearance.
He exuded an air of debonairness that charmed everyone in the room. 他举手投足间流露出的儒雅气质，让房间里的每个人都为之倾倒。
Synonyms: sophistication, charm, grace, elegance, urbanity, polish, suavity
Antonyms: awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucheness, boorishness
