front-desks: /ˈfrʌnt dɛsks/ | n. |前台，接待处| The area in a hotel, office, etc., where guests or customers are received.  It often involves a counter or desk where staff interact with people entering the building. |  The front-desks at the hotel were always busy.  酒店的前台总是很忙。| synonyms: reception desk, reception area, information desk, concierge desk | antonyms: back office, behind the scenes | noun


front-desk: /ˈfrʌnt dɛsk/ | adj. |前台的，接待处的 | Relating to or involved in the reception or front desk area of a business or organization. | The front-desk staff were very helpful. 前台的工作人员非常乐于助人。| synonyms: reception, front office | antonyms: back office, administrative | adjective
