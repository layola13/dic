morphologization:/ˌmɔːrfəlɒdʒaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌmɔːrfəlɑːdʒɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 形态化，形态学化 | The process by which a word or phrase becomes a morpheme (the smallest meaningful unit in a language).  It involves a grammatical element becoming bound to another element and losing its independent status. | The morphologization of "going to" into "gonna" is a common example in English. “going to”演变成“gonna”是英语中形态化的一个常见例子。 |  grammaticalization, lexicalization | - | noun
