condemn:/kənˈdɛm/| vt.|谴责，判刑，宣告…不适用|to express complete disapproval of; censure.  To declare (someone) to be guilty of a crime and sentence them to punishment. To officially declare (something, especially a building) to be unfit for use.|The government condemned the terrorist attack. 政府谴责了这次恐怖袭击。|synonyms: censure, criticize, denounce, convict, sentence|antonyms: praise, commend, acquit, exonerate|verb

condemn:/kənˈdɛm/| vt.|迫使…接受不好的事物|to force someone to accept a difficult or unpleasant situation.|His illness condemned him to a life of pain. 他的疾病使他不得不忍受痛苦的生活。|synonyms: doom, sentence|antonyms: liberate, free|verb
