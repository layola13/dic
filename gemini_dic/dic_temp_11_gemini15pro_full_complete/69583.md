felspathic:/fɛlˈspæθɪk/
adjective
长石质的，含长石的
Containing feldspar; of or relating to feldspar.
The geologist identified the rock as felspathic. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为长石质的。
The pottery is made from a felspathic clay. 这陶器是用一种含长石的粘土制成的。
近义词:feldspathic, feldspar-rich
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
