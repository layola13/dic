murenger:/ˈmjʊərɪndʒər/| n.| 城墙管理员，城墙修缮者| A person responsible for maintaining the walls of a city or fortified place. | The murenger inspected the city walls for any signs of damage. 城墙管理员检查了城墙是否有任何损坏的迹象。|  The murenger ordered repairs to a section of the wall that had begun to crumble. 城墙管理员下令修复一段开始坍塌的城墙。 |Synonym: wall-keeper, waller | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "saboteur" if considering someone who *destroys* walls) | noun
