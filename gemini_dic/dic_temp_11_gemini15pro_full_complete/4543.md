alcalimeter:/ˌælkəlˈɪmɪtər/ | n. |碱量计；碱度计 |An instrument used to determine the amount of alkali present in a solution, especially the amount of alkali or carbonate in an aqueous solution.| The chemist used an alcalimeter to measure the alkalinity of the solution. 化学家用碱量计测量溶液的碱度。|Synonyms: alkalimeter, alkalimetry |Antonyms: acidimeter |noun
