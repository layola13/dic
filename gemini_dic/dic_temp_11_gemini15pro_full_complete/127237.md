nauseative:/ˈnɔːzieɪtɪv/ /ˈnɔːʒəˌteɪtɪv/| adj.| 恶心想吐的，令人作呕的 |Causing nausea; nauseating.  | The smell of the rotting food was nauseative.  腐烂食物的气味令人作呕。| 近义词: nauseating, sickening, disgusting, revolting | 反义词: pleasant, agreeable |词性:形容词

nauseated:/ˈnɔːzieɪtɪd/ /ˈnɔːʒəˌteɪtɪd/| adj.| 感觉恶心的，想吐的 | Affected with nausea; inclined to vomit. |  I felt nauseated after riding the roller coaster. 坐过山车后，我感到恶心。 | 近义词: sick, queasy, nauseous | 反义词: well, healthy |词性:形容词


词源: From Latin *nauseosus, from nausea (“seasickness”), from Greek ναυσία (nausía, “seasickness”), from ναῦς (naûs, “ship”).
