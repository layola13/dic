bighorn:/ˈbaɪɡˌhɔːrn/ | n. | 大角羊 | A wild sheep (Ovis canadensis) of mountainous regions of western North America, having large, curving horns. | We saw a bighorn ram standing majestically on the cliff. 我们看到一只雄性大角羊雄伟地站在悬崖上。| Example: The bighorn sheep navigated the steep terrain with ease. 大角羊轻松地穿过了陡峭的地形。 | Synonym: mountain sheep | Antonym: N/A | noun
