lobeliaceous:/ˌloubɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
半边莲科的；属于半边莲科的
Relating to or characteristic of the Lobeliaceae family of plants, which includes lobelia, isotoma, and other herbaceous plants.
The small, blue flowers are characteristic of many lobeliaceous plants. 这些蓝色的小花是许多半边莲科植物的特征。
Lobeliaceous plants are often found in damp or wet habitats. 半边莲科植物通常生长在潮湿或多水的环境中。
synonym:lobelioid
antonym: N/A
词性: adjective
