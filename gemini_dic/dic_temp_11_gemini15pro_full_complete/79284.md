glauconite:/ˈɡlɔːkəˌnaɪt/ | n. |海绿石|A dull green mineral, a hydrous silicate of iron and potassium, found extensively in sedimentary deposits and used as a water softener and fertilizer.  |Glauconite is often found in marine sandstones. 海绿石经常在海相砂岩中发现。|Glauconite grains were observed in the thin section. 在薄片中观察到了海绿石颗粒。|Synonyms: greensand, celadonite |Antonyms: N/A |noun
