icosahedron: /ˌaɪkɒsəˈhiːdrən/ /ˌaɪkɑːsəˈhiːdrən/ | n. |二十面体|A polyhedron with twenty faces, typically equilateral triangles.|The virus's protein shell forms an icosahedron. 病毒的蛋白质外壳形成了一个二十面体。|Synonym: polyhedron|Antonym: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek εἴκοσι (eíkosi, “twenty”) + ἕδρα (hédra, “seat, base, face”).
