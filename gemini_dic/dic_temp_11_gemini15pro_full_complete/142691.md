other-fish-fry:/ˈʌðər fɪʃ frʌɪ/ | n. |其他的事情；其他的鱼要炸；其他的麻烦事| Something else entirely; a different matter altogether; other concerns or priorities.  It implies a disinterest or lack of involvement in the current topic. | "I can't help you with that, I have other fish to fry." 我帮不了你，我还有别的事情要做。| "He's not interested in our project; he has other fish to fry." 他对我们的项目不感兴趣；他另有要务。| Synonyms: other priorities, other matters, other concerns | Antonyms:  N/A (This phrase doesn't have a direct opposite.) | noun phrase
