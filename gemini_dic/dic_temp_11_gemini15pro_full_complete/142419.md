oryx: /ˈɔːrɪks/ | n. | 羚羊，大羚羊 | A large, heavily built antelope of arid regions of Africa and Arabia, having long, straight, or slightly curved horns. |  The oryx is well adapted to desert life.  大羚羊非常适应沙漠生活。

The oryx can survive for weeks without drinking water. 大羚羊可以几周不喝水而存活。| 近义词: antelope | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
