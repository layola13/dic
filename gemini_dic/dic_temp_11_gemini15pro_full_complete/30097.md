cebo:/ˈsɛboʊ/ (US), /ˈsiːboʊ/ (UK)| n.|诱饵; 吸引物|Bait; something used to lure or attract, especially animals or fish. |The fisherman used worms as cebo for the fish. 渔夫用虫子作鱼饵。|
近义词:bait, lure, decoy, enticement|反义词:deterrent, repellent|词性:noun

cebo (Spanish):/ˈseβo/ (Spanish)| n.|肥肉；油脂; (figuratively) incentive, motivation |Animal fat; tallow; (figuratively) something that incites or motivates. |El cebo para el perro fue un jugoso hueso.  The bait for the dog was a juicy bone.|
近义词:(for fat) grasa, sebo; (for incentive) incentivo, motivación|反义词:(for incentive) disuasión|词性:noun
