disacidify:/ˌdɪsəˈsɪdɪfaɪ/
|v.|去酸，脱酸|To remove acid from something; to reduce the acidity of something.|The library is disacidifying its old books to preserve them. 图书馆正在对它的旧书进行脱酸处理以保存它们。|The process is used to disacidify polluted lakes.  这个过程用于降低受污染湖泊的酸度。|neutralize, deacidify|acidify|verb
