buzzard:/ˈbʌzərd/ | n. | 秃鹫；贪婪的人 | A large hawk-like bird of prey that feeds chiefly on carrion.  A person who preys on others; a profiteer. |  The buzzard circled overhead, searching for carrion. 秃鹫在头顶盘旋，寻找腐肉。| synonyms: vulture, hawk, raptor, predator, profiteer | antonyms: prey, victim | noun

buzzard:/ˈbʌzərd/ | n. | 笨蛋；傻瓜 (Informal, chiefly US) | A stupid or foolish person. | He called me a buzzard, but I know he didn't mean it. 他叫我笨蛋，但我知道他不是认真的。| synonyms: fool, idiot, dolt, dunce | antonyms: genius, smart person | noun
