crossroads:/ˈkrɒsrəʊdz/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsroʊdz/ (US)| n. |十字路口；转折点|A place where two or more roads meet; a point at which a crucial decision must be made.|We are at a crossroads in our history. 我们正处在历史的十字路口。|The company is at a crossroads and must decide whether to expand or downsize.  这家公司正处于一个十字路口，必须决定是扩张还是缩小规模。|近义词：intersection, juncture, turning point|反义词:dead end, continuation|词性:名词
