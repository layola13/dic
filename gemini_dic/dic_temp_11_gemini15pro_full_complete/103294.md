jibe:/dʒaɪb/ | vi. n.| 符合；一致；嘲讽；奚落 | to agree; to be in accord;  a taunting or sarcastic remark |  
The two accounts of the accident didn't jibe. 这两份事故报告不一致。

His story jibes with hers. 他的说法与她的一致。

He made a jibe about her weight. 他嘲笑了她的体重。

| 近义词：agree, correspond, conform, taunt, sneer | 反义词：disagree, clash, conflict | 词性: 动词(vi.), 名词(n.)

jibe (US also gybe):/dʒaɪb/ | vi. n.| (航海) 順風改變航向；(航海) 順風改变航向的動作；嘲讽；奚落 | (nautical) to shift a sail from one side of a vessel to the other when running before the wind; the act of jibing a sail; a taunting or sarcastic remark |  
The captain jibed the mainsail. 船长调整了主帆的航向。

The sudden jibe caused the boat to lurch violently. 突如其来的改变航向使船猛烈倾斜。

He made a cruel jibe at her expense. 他以她为代价，说了一句刻薄的讥讽话。


| 近义词：tack (v.), shift (v.), taunt (n.), sneer (n.) | 反义词： N/A | 词性: 动词(vi.), 名词(n.)
