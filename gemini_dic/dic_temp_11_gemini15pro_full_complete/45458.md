curation:/kjʊˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 策展，管理，精选 | The act of carefully selecting and organizing items or content for presentation, typically in a museum, art gallery, or online platform.  | The museum's curation of ancient artifacts was impressive.  博物馆对古代文物的策展令人印象深刻。 | synonyms: selection, organization, compilation, assembly | antonyms: neglect, disregard | noun

curation:/kjʊˈreɪʃən/ | n. | (尤指网络内容的) 组织, 整理 | The process of gathering, organizing, and presenting digital content. |  The website's curation of user-generated content is excellent.  该网站对用户生成内容的组织非常出色。 | synonyms: management, administration, supervision | antonyms: chaos, disorder | noun
