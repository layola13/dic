happy-go-luckiness:/ˌhæpi ɡoʊ ˈlʌkinəs/ | n. | 无忧无虑；随遇而安 |  The state of being happy-go-lucky; carefree unconcern. |  Her happy-go-luckiness allowed her to breeze through life's challenges. 她随遇而安的性格使她能够轻松应对生活中的挑战。 |  carefreeness, lightheartedness, nonchalance | anxiety, worry, concern | noun

Etymology:  Derived from the adjective "happy-go-lucky," which itself originated in the late 19th century.  "Happy-go-lucky" likely comes from a dialectal expression meaning "easy-going" or "carefree."
