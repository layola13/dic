de-coding:/diːˈkoʊdɪŋ/
n.|解码，译码|The process of converting a coded message into intelligible language.  The act of interpreting or understanding the meaning of something.|例句：De-coding the enemy's messages was crucial to the war effort.  译码敌人的信息对战争的努力至关重要。

例句：De-coding the complex scientific jargon was a challenge for the journalist.  对于记者来说，解码复杂的科学术语是一个挑战。|近义词：deciphering, interpreting, decrypting|反义词：encoding, encrypting|词性:名词

de-code:/diːˈkoʊd/
v.|解码，译码|To convert (a coded message) into intelligible language. To interpret or understand the meaning of something.|例句：The spy was able to de-code the secret message.  间谍能够解码秘密信息。

例句：Researchers are trying to de-code the human genome.  研究人员正在试图解码人类基因组。|近义词：decipher, interpret, decrypt|反义词：encode, encrypt|词性:动词
