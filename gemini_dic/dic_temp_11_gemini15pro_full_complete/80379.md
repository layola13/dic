golfo-de-mexicos:/ˈɡɔlfoʊ de ˈmɛhɪkoʊs/
noun
Gulf of Mexico(s) (Usually singular: Golfo de México)
Body of water bordered by the United States, Mexico, and Cuba.
The oil spill in the Golfo de México caused significant environmental damage. 墨西哥湾的石油泄漏造成了严重的破坏。
synonyms: Gulf of Mexico, Mexican Gulf
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a geographical location)
