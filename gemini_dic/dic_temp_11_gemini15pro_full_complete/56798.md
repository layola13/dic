dore:/dɔːr/ /dɔːɹ/ | n. | 金龟子；吉丁虫；雄蜂 | A golden or metallic-colored beetle, especially a scarab beetle or a jewel beetle; a male bee. | The dore flew around the flower. 金龟子绕着花飞舞。| 近义词：beetle, scarab | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

dore:/dɔːr/ /dɔːɹ/ | adj. | 金色的；镀金的 | Made of gold or gilded.  (Archaic) | He wore a dore crown. 他戴着一顶金色的王冠。 | 近义词：golden, gilded, gold | 反义词: silver | 词性: 形容词


词源:
From Old French *doré, past participle of dorer “to gild,” from Late Latin deaurare, from Latin de- “down, completely” + aurare “to gild,” from aurum “gold.”
