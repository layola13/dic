flow-chart:/ˈfloʊ tʃɑːrt/ /ˈfləʊ tʃɑːrt/| n.|流程图|A flow-chart is a diagram of the sequence of movements or actions of people or things involved in a complex system or activity.| The flow-chart clearly illustrates the steps involved.  流程图清楚地说明了所涉及的步骤。|近义词：diagram, chart|反义词: None|词性:名词

**词源:**

"Flow-chart" is a compound word formed from "flow" and "chart." "Flow" comes from Old English "flōwan," meaning "to flow." "Chart" comes from Old French "charte," meaning "card" or "map," ultimately from Latin "charta," meaning "paper" or "papyrus."
