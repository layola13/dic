leggy:/ˈleɡi/| adj.|长腿的，腿细长的|Having long and slender legs; often used to describe a woman or girl in an attractive way.  Characterized by long stems or stalks, especially disproportionately so.|例句：The model was tall and leggy.  这个模特又高又长腿。

例句：The leggy flowers swayed in the breeze.  这些长茎的花在微风中摇曳。|近义词：long-legged, lanky, spindly|反义词：stocky, stubby|词性:形容词

leggy:/ˈleɡi/| adj.|（植物）细长茎的；长茎的|Having long and thin stems. Often used to describe plants.|例句：The tomatoes grew leggy in the low light. 由于光线不足，西红柿长得茎细长。

例句：The leggy seedlings needed to be transplanted. 这些细长茎的幼苗需要移植。|近义词：spindly, spindling, stalky|反义词：bushy, compact|词性:形容词
