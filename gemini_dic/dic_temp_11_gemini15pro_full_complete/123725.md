moons:/muːnz/
n.
卫星；月亮
Natural satellites of planets.  A celestial body that orbits a planet.
例句：Jupiter has many moons. 木星有很多卫星。

例句：The moons of Jupiter were first observed by Galileo. 木星的卫星最早是由伽利略观测到的。

近义词：satellites
反义词：（无直接反义词）
词性：名词
