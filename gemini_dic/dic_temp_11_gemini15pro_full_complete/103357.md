jiminy:/ˈdʒɪmɪni/| int.|天哪！哎呀！|A mild oath or exclamation of surprise or alarm.  It is a euphemism for "Jesus," used to avoid taking the Lord's name in vain.|Jiminy! I almost forgot my keys! 天哪！我差点忘了我的钥匙！|近义词：gosh, gee, heavens|反义词: (No direct antonym for an exclamation)|词性: interjection
