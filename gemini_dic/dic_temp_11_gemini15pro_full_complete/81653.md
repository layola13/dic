noun
希腊性;希腊精神;希腊风格

Her paintings capture the greekness of the Aegean islands. 她的画作捕捉到了爱琴海诸岛的希腊风情。
Synonyms:Hellenism, Greek character, Greek spirit
Antonyms: Non-Greekness, foreignness
