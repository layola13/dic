fossick:/ˈfɒsɪk/ (UK) /ˈfɑːsɪk/ (US)| v. n.|翻找；搜寻；少量发现；（尤指在被他人丢弃或忽视的地方）搜寻，翻找|To search for something, especially by rummaging, often in a place where others have discarded or overlooked items.  Also, to discover something in small quantities.|He was fossicking around in the attic for old photographs. 他在阁楼里翻找旧照片。|Synonyms: rummage, scavenge, hunt, search, explore|Antonyms:  discard, ignore, neglect|verb, noun

fossick:/ˈfɒsɪk/ (UK) /ˈfɑːsɪk/ (US)| n.|翻找；少量发现之物|The act of fossicking or something found while fossicking.|She made a good fossick at the flea market. 她在跳蚤市场淘到不少好东西。|Synonyms: search, rummage, exploration|Antonyms:  disposal, abandonment|noun
