dominions:/dəˈmɪnjənz/
n.|统治，领土，支配|Territories or areas under the control of a ruler or government.  A sphere of influence or control.|The British dominions once covered a quarter of the globe. | 近义词: territories, realms, domains, possessions, dependencies | 反义词: subjection, subordination | 词性: noun

dominion:/dəˈmɪnjən/
n.|统治，领土，支配|Control or the power to rule; sovereignty.|The king held dominion over the entire kingdom. | 近义词: rule, control, sway, authority, power | 反义词: subjection, subordination | 词性: noun
