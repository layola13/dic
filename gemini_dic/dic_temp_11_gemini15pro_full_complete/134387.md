nonintimidating:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪŋ/| adj.|不令人生畏的，不吓人的|Not causing fear or intimidation; approachable and easy to talk to.  Derived from "intimidate" with the prefix "non-" indicating negation.|例句：The teacher created a nonintimidating atmosphere in the classroom. 老师在教室里营造了一种轻松的氛围。|例句：He has a nonintimidating manner, which puts people at ease. 他有一种不令人生畏的态度，让人感到轻松自在。|近义词：approachable, friendly, welcoming, reassuring|反义词：intimidating, threatening, daunting, forbidding|词性:形容词
