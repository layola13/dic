nontruths:/nɒnˈtruːθs/ /nɑːnˈtruːθs/
noun
不实之词；谎言
Statements that are not true; lies.
The politician was accused of spreading nontruths during the campaign.  这个政客被指控在竞选期间散布不实之词。
He told so many nontruths it was hard to know what to believe. 他说了这么多谎话，很难知道该相信什么。
Synonyms: lies, falsehoods, untruths, fabrications, misrepresentations
Antonyms: truths, facts, realities
