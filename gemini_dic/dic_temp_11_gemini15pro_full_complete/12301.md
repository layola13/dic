asclepiadaceous:/əˌskliːpiːəˈdeɪʃəs/
adjective
萝藦科的；像萝藦的
Relating to or characteristic of the milkweed family (Asclepiadaceae), typically having milky sap and often showy flowers.
The asclepiadaceous plant produced a sticky white sap.  这种萝藦科植物会分泌一种粘稠的白色汁液。
The botanist specialized in asclepiadaceous species.  这位植物学家专门研究萝藦科植物。
近义词:milkweed-like

词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Asclepiadaceae*, from Asclepias (the type genus of the milkweed family), named after Asclepius, the Greek god of medicine, + -aceae (a suffix used to form the names of plant families).
