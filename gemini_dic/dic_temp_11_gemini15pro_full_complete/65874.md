evolution:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃən/ (US), /ˌiːvəˈluːʃən/ (UK)| n. |进化，演变，发展|The process by which different kinds of living organisms are thought to have developed and diversified from earlier forms during the history of the earth.|例句：The evolution of the internet has changed the way we communicate. 互联网的进化改变了我们交流的方式。|近义词：development, progression, growth, advancement|反义词：regression, devolution, stagnation|noun

evolution:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃən/ (US), /ˌiːvəˈluːʃən/ (UK)| n. |进化论|The theory that all living things developed from earlier simpler forms of life.|例句：Darwin's theory of evolution is widely accepted by the scientific community. 达尔文的进化论被科学界广泛接受。|近义词：Darwinism|反义词：creationism|noun
