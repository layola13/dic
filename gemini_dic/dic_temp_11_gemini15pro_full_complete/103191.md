jesuitocracy:/ˈdʒɛzjuɪtɒkrəsi/
noun
耶稣会统治；耶稣会士的政治权力
Government by the Jesuits, or a government unduly influenced by them.  A state or organization controlled or unduly influenced by Jesuits.
The historian argued that the 17th-century Spanish monarchy was essentially a jesuitocracy.  这位历史学家认为，17世纪的西班牙君主制本质上是耶稣会统治。
The novel depicted a dystopian future under a jesuitocracy. 这部小说描绘了一个在耶稣会统治下的反乌托邦未来。
Synonyms:  clericalism, theocracy, hierocracy
Antonyms: secularism, democracy
