dissentiate:/dɪˈsɛnʃɪeɪt/
verb
持异议；不同意；不赞成
To disagree with or reject a prevailing sentiment, belief, or theory.
The board dissentiated from the CEO's proposal. 董事会不同意CEO的提案。
He dissentiated from the majority opinion. 他不同意多数人的意见。
近义词: disagree, dissent, object, oppose
反义词: agree, concur, assent, approve
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Latin dissentiāre (“to differ in sentiment, disagree”), from dis- (“apart, asunder, away”) + sentiāre (“to feel, think, perceive”).
