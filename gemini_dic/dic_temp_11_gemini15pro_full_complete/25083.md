bubbles:/ˈbʌbəlz/ (US), /ˈbʌblz/ (UK)| n. |泡泡；气泡；泡沫；幻想；（漫画中的）对话气泡|Small, round pockets of air or gas in a liquid or a solid substance. Also can refer to a short-lived or unrealistic idea or plan.|The children were blowing bubbles in the park. 孩子们在公园里吹泡泡。|Synonyms: foams, froth, spheres, orbs|Antonyms: solids, liquids|noun

bubbles:/ˈbʌbəlz/ (US), /ˈbʌblz/ (UK)| v. |起泡；冒泡；发出汩汩声；兴奋；洋溢|To form or produce bubbles; to show enthusiasm or excitement.|The champagne bubbled in the glass. 香槟在杯子里冒泡。|Synonyms: fizz, effervesce, froth, sparkle|Antonyms: flatten, deflate|verb
