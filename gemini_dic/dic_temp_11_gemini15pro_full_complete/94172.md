impennes:/ɪmˈpɛnz/ | adj. | 无飞羽的，不会飞的 | Lacking flight feathers; incapable of flight.  This term is specifically used for birds and refers to the complete absence or reduction of flight feathers rendering them flightless.  It is derived from the French "impenne," meaning "featherless." | Penguins are impennes birds, perfectly adapted for swimming and diving.  企鹅是不会飞的鸟，非常适合游泳和潜水。| The dodo was an impenne bird that became extinct in the 17th century.  渡渡鸟是一种不会飞的鸟，在17世纪灭绝了。 | Synonym: flightless | Antonym: volant, capable of flight | adjective
