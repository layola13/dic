antiphony:/ænˈtɪfəni/ | n. |对唱，轮唱; 交互应答; 对照; |A responsive alternation between two groups especially of singers.  A musical piece in which two groups of singers or instruments answer each other.  A thing that contrasts or corresponds with another.|The antiphony between the two choirs created a beautiful, ethereal sound. 两个唱诗班的交相呼应创造出一种优美飘渺的声音。|Synonyms: response, reply, rejoinder, riposte, counterpoint|Antonyms: unison, harmony, agreement, concord|noun
