mislead:/ˌmɪsˈliːd/ /ˌmɪsˈlid/| vt.|误导，欺骗|to cause someone to believe something that is not true|The advertisement misleads the public about the effectiveness of the product.  这则广告误导了公众对产品有效性的认识。

The politician misled the voters with false promises.  这个政客用虚假的承诺误导了选民。|近义词：deceive, trick, fool, dupe|反义词:guide, enlighten, inform|词性:动词
