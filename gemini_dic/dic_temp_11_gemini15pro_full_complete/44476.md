crossborder:/ˈkrɒsˌbɔːdə(r)/ /ˈkrɑːsˌbɔːrdər/| adj.|跨境的，跨国界的|Relating to or involving activity that takes place across a border between two countries.|例句：Crossborder trade has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来跨境贸易显著增长。

例句：The company specializes in crossborder mergers and acquisitions. 这家公司专门从事跨境并购。
|近义词：international, transnational, global|反义词：domestic, national|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross + border.  "Cross" meaning "across" or "going from one side to another" has its roots in Old Norse "kross,"  related to Old English "crux" ultimately from Latin "crux" (cross). "Border" meaning "the edge or boundary of a land or area" comes from Old French "bordure,"  related to "bord" (edge).
