imperialize:/ɪmˈpɪəriəlaɪz/ | v. | 帝国化，使帝国主义化 | To extend the rule or authority of an empire over foreign countries or territories; to influence strongly or dominate. |  The British Empire imperialized many countries in Asia and Africa.  英国帝国在亚洲和非洲将许多国家帝国化了。

The company was accused of trying to imperialize the local market.  该公司被指控试图控制当地市场。 | 近义词: colonize, conquer, subjugate, dominate | 反义词: liberate, free, emancipate | 词性: 动词
