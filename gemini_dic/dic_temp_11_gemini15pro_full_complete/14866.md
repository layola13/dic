注意事项: 
"Ay" as an interjection is more common in American English. "Aye" is a variant spelling, particularly in British English, and is more frequently used for the meaning "yes," especially in nautical or voting contexts.  The pronunciation is generally the same for both spellings, regardless of meaning.
