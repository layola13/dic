noncommissioned:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪʃənd/ (US) /ˌnɒnkəˈmɪʃnd/ (UK)| adj.|非委任的，未授衔的|Not holding a commission; relating to or denoting a rank of noncommissioned officer.|例句：He is a noncommissioned officer in the army. 他是军队中的一名士官。|近义词：enlisted, non-officer|反义词：commissioned, officer|词性：形容词

Etymology->
From non- + commissioned.  Commissioned comes from the Old French word *comission and ultimately from the Latin *commissio, meaning "a sending, committing, entrusting."  The "non-" prefix simply negates the meaning.
