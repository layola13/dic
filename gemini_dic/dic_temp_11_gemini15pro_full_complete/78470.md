geyserich:/ˈɡaɪzərɪtʃ/ | adj. | 间歇泉的；由间歇泉形成的 |  Formed by or containing deposits from a geyser. | The geyserich landscape was otherworldly. 间歇泉的地貌如同另一个世界。| 近义词:geyserite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From geyser + -rich (suffix meaning "abounding in").  The word "geyser" itself comes from Icelandic "geysir," meaning "gusher."
