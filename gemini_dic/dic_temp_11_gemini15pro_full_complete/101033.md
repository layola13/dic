invection:/ɪnˈvɛkʃən/| n.|谩骂；恶言；痛斥|A strong verbal attack against someone or something; harsh or abusive language.|He launched into a bitter invection against the government's policies. 他对政府的政策进行了猛烈的抨击。|近义词：abuse, vituperation, diatribe, denunciation, condemnation, reproach, censure, criticism, stricture,  |反义词:praise, commendation, compliment|noun

invective:/ɪnˈvɛktɪv/| adj.|谩骂的；恶言的|Characterized by harsh or abusive language.|Her invective remarks shocked the audience. 她恶毒的言论震惊了观众。|近义词：abusive, vituperative, censorious, critical, harsh, scathing, virulent|反义词:complimentary, laudatory, flattering|adjective
