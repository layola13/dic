dissociating: /dɪˈsoʊʃieɪtɪŋ/ /dɪˈsəʊsieɪtɪŋ/
verb.
分离，游离，分裂
The process of separating something into its constituent parts.  Also, in psychology, a mental process of disconnecting from one's thoughts, feelings, memories, or sense of identity.
The chemist is dissociating the compound into its elements.  化学家正在将化合物分解成其组成元素。
He dissociated himself from the extremist group. 他脱离了极端组织。
synonyms: separating, disconnecting, detaching, disuniting
antonyms: associating, connecting, uniting, joining
verb

dissociate: /dɪˈsoʊʃieɪt/ /dɪˈsəʊsieɪt/
verb.  noun.
分离，游离，分裂;  游离基
The act of separating or the state of being separated. Can also refer to a free radical (chemistry).
The company chose to dissociate itself from the controversial spokesperson. 公司选择与这位备受争议的发言人撇清关系。
synonyms: separate, disconnect, detach, disunite
antonyms: associate, connect, unite, join
verb, noun
