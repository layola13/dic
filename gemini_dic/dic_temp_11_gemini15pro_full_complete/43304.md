cragginess:/ˈkræɡɪnɪs/
n.
崎岖，陡峭
The state of being craggy; ruggedness.
The cragginess of the mountains made the climb difficult. 山脉的崎岖使得攀登变得困难。
近义词：ruggedness, steepness, unevenness
反义词：smoothness, flatness
词性：名词

craggy:/ˈkræɡi/
adj.
崎岖的，陡峭的
Having many crags; rugged and uneven.
The craggy cliffs dropped sharply to the sea. 崎岖的悬崖峭壁直落入海。
近义词：rocky, rugged, steep
反义词：smooth, flat
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From crag + -y.  Crag from Middle English cragge, probably of Celtic origin.
