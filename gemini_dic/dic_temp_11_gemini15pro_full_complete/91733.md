例句：The chef created a hyperdelicious meal that left everyone speechless.  这位厨师做了一顿超级美味的饭菜，让每个人都赞不绝口。|近义词：exquisite, scrumptious, divine, delectable, ambrosial|反义词：disgusting, unappetizing, revolting, nauseating|词性:形容词
