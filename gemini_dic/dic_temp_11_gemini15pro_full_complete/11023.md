arc's:/ɑːrks/ | n. | 弧的，弧线 |  Possessive form of "arc," meaning belonging to or associated with an arc.  It indicates ownership or a close relationship with an arc. | The arc's brightness surprised the onlookers. 弧线的亮度让旁观者感到惊讶。 | Synonyms: curve's, bend's | Antonyms: straight line's | noun


Etymology: From the Latin "arcus," meaning "bow" or "arch."
