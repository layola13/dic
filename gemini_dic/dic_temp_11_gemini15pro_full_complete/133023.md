nonentrant:/ˌnɑːnˈɛntrənt/ /ˌnɒnˈɛntrənt/| adj. n.|非进入者，非参赛者|Not entering; not entered. nonentrant:/ˌnɑːnˈɛntrənt/ /ˌnɒnˈɛntrənt/| n.|非进入者，非参赛者|A person who is not entered in a competition or a member of a particular group or profession.|Example: The nonentrants were not allowed into the awards ceremony. 非参赛者不允许进入颁奖典礼。|近义词：outsider, nonmember, nonparticipant|反义词: entrant, participant, member|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (not) + "entrant" (one who enters).  "Entrant" comes from the French "entrant" (entering), present participle of "entrer" (to enter), ultimately from Latin "intra" (within).
