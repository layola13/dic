adorned:/əˈdɔːrnd/
adjective
装饰的，装扮的
decorated; embellished.  Something adorned is enhanced with decorative elements.
The bride's hair was adorned with flowers.  新娘的头发上饰有鲜花。
Synonyms: decorated, embellished, ornamented, beautified, bedecked
Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned, bare
词性: adjective

adorn:/əˈdɔːrn/
verb
装饰，装扮
to make something more beautiful by adding something to it.
She adorned herself with jewels. 她用珠宝装饰自己。
Synonyms: decorate, embellish, ornament, beautify, bedeck
Antonyms: strip, bare
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *adornen*, from Old French *adorner* "to adorn, decorate, embellish," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) "to" + *orner* "to equip, furnish, arrange," of uncertain origin.
