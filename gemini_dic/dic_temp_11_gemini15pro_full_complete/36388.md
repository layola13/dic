coinvent:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛnt/| vt.|共同发明，联合发明|To invent something together with another person or people.|Alexander Graham Bell is credited with coinventing the telephone.  亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔被认为是电话的共同发明者。

The Wright brothers are known for coinventing the airplane. 莱特兄弟以共同发明飞机而闻名。|近义词：collaborate on an invention, jointly invent|反义词:|v.
