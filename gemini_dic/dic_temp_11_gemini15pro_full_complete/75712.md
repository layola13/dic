full-fat:/ˌfʊlˈfæt/ | adj. |全脂的，未脱脂的 | Containing all the fat naturally present; not reduced in fat content. |  I prefer full-fat milk in my coffee. 我喜欢在咖啡里加全脂牛奶。 |  I always buy full-fat yogurt. 我总是买全脂酸奶。 | 近义词: whole-fat, regular | 反义词: low-fat, skimmed, nonfat, reduced-fat | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Compound of "full" + "fat". "Full" meaning complete or containing the maximum amount, and "fat" referring to the fatty component of food.
