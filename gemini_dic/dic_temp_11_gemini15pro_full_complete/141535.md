opportune:/ˌɒpəˈtjuːn/ (US) /ˌɑːpərˈtuːn/| adj.|合适的，恰当的，适时的|Suitable or convenient for a particular purpose; occurring at an appropriate time.|例句：It was an opportune moment to raise the subject. 那是提出这个问题的适当时机。|近义词：favorable, auspicious, timely, convenient, felicitous|反义词:inopportune, unfortunate, unfavorable|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *opportunus* (“before the port, convenient”), from *ob* (“before, towards”) + *portus* (“port, harbor”).  The original sense referred to a ship having a favorable wind for entering a port.
