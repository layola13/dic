overcooling:/ˌoʊvərˈkuːlɪŋ/
noun
过度冷却，过冷
The process of cooling something below its normal operating temperature or freezing point without it solidifying.
The engine suffered damage due to overcooling. 引擎由于过度冷却而受损。
overchilling, supercooling
overheating
