moralized:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzd/ | v. adj.| 道德化的，说教的 |To imbue with morality; to explain or interpret morally. As an adjective, it describes something that has been made moral or used to teach a moral lesson. | The author moralized the story by adding a clear message about the importance of honesty.  作者通过添加关于诚实重要性的清晰信息，使故事具有道德意义。 |  The moralized fable taught children about the consequences of greed.  这则道德化的寓言教导孩子们贪婪的后果。 | 近义词:didactic, preachy, sermonizing | 反义词: amoral, immoral |词性: 动词/形容词
