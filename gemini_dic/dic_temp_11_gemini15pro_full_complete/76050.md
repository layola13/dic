furl:/fɜːrl/ | n. | furlong 的缩写;收拢，卷起 |A measure of length, equal to one eighth of a mile (201.17 meters). Also, the act of rolling or folding something up, such as a sail or a flag. |The yacht was a mile off the coast, six furlongs out. 那艘游艇离海岸一英里，即六弗隆。|近义词：roll up, fold, furlong |反义词:unfurl, spread, open| noun

furl:/fɜːrl/ | vt. | 收拢，卷起（尤指帆、旗等）| To roll or fold something up neatly.  |They furled the sails before the storm. 他们在暴风雨来临前收起了帆。|近义词：roll up, fold, stow |反义词:unfurl, spread, open| verb
