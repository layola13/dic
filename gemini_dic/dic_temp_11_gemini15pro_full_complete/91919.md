hypermanics:/ˌhaɪpərˈmænɪks/ | n. | 狂躁症患者（复数） | People experiencing a hypermanic episode, a period of abnormally elevated mood, energy, and activity.  This term is less common than "people with hypermania." |  Hypermanics often experience racing thoughts and decreased need for sleep. 狂躁症患者经常思绪飞奔，睡眠需求减少。|  Support groups can be helpful for hypermanics and their families. 支持小组对狂躁症患者及其家人很有帮助。| 近义词:  people with hypermania, people experiencing a manic episode | 反义词: people with depression | 词性: 名词 (plural noun)
