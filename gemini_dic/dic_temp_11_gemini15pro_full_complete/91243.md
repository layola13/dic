hydrocyanide:/ˌhaɪdrəˈsaɪənaɪd/| n. |氰化氢|A highly poisonous, volatile, colorless liquid, HCN, used in the manufacture of acrylic fibers, plastics, and other products and as a fumigant. It is also released in the burning of certain plastics. |例句：Hydrocyanide is a deadly poison. 氰化氢是一种致命毒药。

例句：Hydrocyanide gas is used in some industrial processes. 氰化氢气体用于一些工业过程。|近义词：prussic acid |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: hydro- + cyanide.  "Hydro-" denotes the presence of hydrogen, while "cyanide" refers to the CN− anion.
