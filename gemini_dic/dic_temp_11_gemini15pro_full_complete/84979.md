happily:/ˈhæpɪli/ | adv. | 幸福地，快乐地 | In a happy way; with pleasure; gladly. | She smiled happily. 她幸福地笑了。|  happily ever after 幸福地生活在一起 | 近义词: cheerfully, joyfully, merrily, gladly | 反义词: sadly, unhappily, miserably | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *happeli, from Old English *happolīċe (“happily, by chance”), equivalent to happy +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots happilie (“happily”), Dutch happelijk (“happily, fortunately”), German happig (“happily”).
