alienee:/ˌeɪliəˈniː/
n.
受让人，接受财产转让者
The person to whom property is transferred.
The alienee is responsible for paying property taxes from the date of transfer.  受让人从转让之日起负责缴纳房产税。

The alienee signed the deed, officially accepting ownership of the property. 受让人签署了契约，正式接受了房产的所有权。

近义词:grantee, transferee, recipient
反义词:alienor, grantor, transferor
词性:noun


词源:
From Anglo-French *aliené, past participle of aliener, from Late Latin alienare “to estrange, transfer,” from Latin alienus “of another.”
