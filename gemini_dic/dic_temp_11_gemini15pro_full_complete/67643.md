extrinsic:/ɛkˈstrɪnsɪk, ɛkˈstrɪnzɪk/
adjective
外在的，外来的，非固有的
not inherent or essential
例句：
Extrinsic motivation relies on external rewards. 外在的动机依赖于外部奖励。

The extrinsic muscles of the hand are located in the forearm. 手的外部肌肉位于前臂。

近义词：external, outward, outer, superficial, extraneous
反义词：intrinsic, inherent, internal, inner

词性: adjective
