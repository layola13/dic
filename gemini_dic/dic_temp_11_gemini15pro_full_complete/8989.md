antiliberalness:/ˌæntiˈlɪbərəlnəs/
n.
反自由主义，非自由主义
The state of being opposed to liberal principles or policies.
His antiliberalness manifested in his opposition to free speech. 他对言论自由的反对表现出他的反自由主义。
The growing antiliberalness of the country is concerning.  这个国家日益增长的反自由主义令人担忧。
Synonyms: illiberalism, intolerance, authoritarianism
Antonyms: liberalism, tolerance, open-mindedness
noun
