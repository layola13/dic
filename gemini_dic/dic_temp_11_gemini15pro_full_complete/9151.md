antinaturalist:/ˌæntiˈnætʃrəlɪst/
| adj.|反自然主义的|Opposed to naturalism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy.  Rejecting the view that everything can be explained in terms of natural laws and processes.|His antinaturalist stance led him to believe in the supernatural. 他反自然主义的立场使他相信超自然现象。|
|Synonyms: non-natural, supernatural, metaphysical|
|Antonyms: naturalist, naturalistic|
|adjective|


antinaturalism:/ˌæntiˈnætʃrəlɪzəm/
| n.|反自然主义|The philosophical doctrine opposing naturalism, especially the belief that there are supernatural or spiritual realities not explicable by natural laws.|Antinaturalism gained traction in some intellectual circles during the late 19th century.  反自然主义在19世纪后期的一些知识界获得了关注。|
|Synonyms: supernaturalism, non-naturalism|
|Antonyms: naturalism|
|noun|
