imperative:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/| adj. n.|必要的，紧急的；命令的；祈使语气|Of vital importance; crucial.  Expressing a command.|例句：It is imperative that we act now. 我们现在必须采取行动。|近义词：essential, crucial, vital, urgent, mandatory, compulsory|反义词:optional, unnecessary|词性:adjective

imperative:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/| n.|命令；祈使语气；必要的事|A command or order.  The imperative mood (grammar). Something that is essential or urgent.|例句：He spoke with the imperative tone of a king. 他以国王的命令语气说话。|近义词：command, order, decree, directive|反义词:request, plea|词性:noun
