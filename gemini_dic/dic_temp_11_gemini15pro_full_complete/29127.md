caseworker:/ˈkeɪsˌwɜːrkər/| n. |个案工作者，社会工作者|A social worker who is responsible for managing the cases of individuals or families needing support, typically involving welfare, healthcare, or social services. |例句：The caseworker visited the family weekly to assess their progress.  个案工作者每周拜访这个家庭以评估他们的进展。

例句：My caseworker helped me find affordable housing. 我的个案工作者帮助我找到了经济适用房。|近义词：social worker, care manager, support worker|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "opponent" in a legal context if the caseworker represents one side.)|词性:noun
