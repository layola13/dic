corraled:/kəˈrɔːld/ |v. |被聚集；被围拢|gathered or confined (animals or people) into an enclosure.|The cowboys corralled the cattle for branding. 牛仔们把牛群赶到一起准备烙印。|The police corralled the protesters in the square. 警察把抗议者围堵在广场上。|近义词：gather, round up, assemble, collect, herd|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:动词

词源:
From Spanish *corral* "yard, pen," from Old Spanish *currale*, perhaps ultimately from Late Latin *currale* "stable for horses," from Latin *currere* "to run."
