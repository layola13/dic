orthotopic:/ˌɔːrθəˈtɑːpɪk/ /ˌɔːθəˈtɒpɪk/
adjective
原位的，同位移植的
Located or occurring in the normal or usual position.  Relating to transplantation of tissue or an organ to its normal anatomical location.
例句：The patient underwent orthotopic liver transplantation.  病人接受了原位肝移植。

例句：Orthotopic heart transplantation is the most common type of heart transplant. 原位心脏移植是最常见的心脏移植类型。

近义词：autochthonous, indigenous
反义词：heterotopic, ectopic
词性：adjective
