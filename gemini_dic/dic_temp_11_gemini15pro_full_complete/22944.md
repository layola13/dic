bottleneck: /ˈbɒtlənɛk/ (British) /ˈbɑːtlənɛk/ (American) | n. | 瓶颈；障碍；阻塞点 | A bottleneck is a narrow or restricted section of a road, passage, or process that limits the overall flow or capacity.  It can also refer to a situation that slows down progress or creates delays. | The construction work is creating a bottleneck on the highway.  施工正在高速公路上造成瓶颈。| synonyms: obstruction, impediment, constriction, hindrance | antonyms:  expedite, advance, accelerate | noun

bottleneck: /ˈbɒtlənɛk/ (British) /ˈbɑːtlənɛk/ (American) | vt. | 限制；阻碍 | To bottleneck something means to restrict its flow or progress. | The lack of funding bottlenecked the project. 缺乏资金阻碍了该项目。| synonyms: restrict, impede, hamper, obstruct | antonyms: expedite, facilitate, accelerate | verb
