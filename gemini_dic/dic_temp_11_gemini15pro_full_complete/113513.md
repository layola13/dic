maa:/mɑː/ | n. | 妈妈 (Informal, childish) | A child's word for mother. | Example: Maa, can I have a cookie? 妈妈，我可以吃块饼干吗？| Example: The little girl cried out for her maa. 小女孩哭着喊妈妈。| Synonym: mom, mommy, mama, mother | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a familial term) | Noun

Etymology:  Likely a variant of "mama," which is a common baby-talk word for mother found in many languages.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *méh₂tēr (mother).
