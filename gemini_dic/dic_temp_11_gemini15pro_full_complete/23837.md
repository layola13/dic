breadboarded:/ˈbrɛdbɔːrdɪd/
verb |  用面包板搭建的，在面包板上测试的 | Assembled or tested on a breadboard (a solderless device for making temporary circuits). | The circuit was breadboarded before being soldered onto a PCB.  电路在焊接到印刷电路板之前先在面包板上进行了测试。 | prototyped, tested, mocked up | finalized, produced | v.
