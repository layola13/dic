bluegrass:/ˈbluːɡræs/| n. adj.|蓝草音乐；蓝草；|A type of country music characterized by fast tempos and the prominent use of stringed instruments, especially the banjo, fiddle, guitar, mandolin, and double bass.  It originated in the Appalachian Mountains of the United States.|例句：I went to a bluegrass festival last weekend. 我上周末去了一个蓝草音乐节。|近义词：country music, mountain music|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps popular music, rock music as contrasting genres) |词性: noun, adjective
bluegrass:/ˈbluːɡræs/| adj.|蓝草的|Relating to or characteristic of bluegrass music.|例句：That's a great bluegrass band. 那是一支很棒的蓝草乐队。|近义词： (No direct synonym for the adjective form) |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the state of Kentucky, nicknamed the "Bluegrass State" because of the bluish-green color of the grass Poa pratensis, which is prevalent there. The music genre originated in this region.
