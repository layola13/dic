foreshadower:/fɔːrˈʃædəʊər/ /fɔːrˈʃædoʊər/ | n. |预兆者，预示者 |Someone or something that foreshadows a future event. | The dark clouds were a foreshadower of the coming storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。| synonym: harbinger, precursor, omen, portent | antonym:  aftermath, consequence | noun

foreshadow:/fɔːrˈʃædəʊ/ /fɔːrˈʃædoʊ/ | v. |预示，预兆 |To be a warning or indication of (a future event). | The author foreshadowed the tragic ending in the first chapter. 作者在第一章就预示了悲剧的结局。| synonym: prefigure, presage, augur, portend | antonym:  conceal, hide | verb
