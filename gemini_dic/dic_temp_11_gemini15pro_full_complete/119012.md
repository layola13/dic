methodism:/ˈmɛθədɪzəm/ | n. |卫理公会主义，循道主义|A Protestant denomination of Christianity, characterized by its emphasis on personal holiness, social justice, and evangelical zeal. | John Wesley founded Methodism in the 18th century.  约翰·卫斯理在18世纪创立了卫理公会。

Methodism stresses the importance of good works. 卫理公会强调善行的重要性。


|近义词：Wesleyanism|反义词: None|词性:Noun
