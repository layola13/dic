counter-advertising's:/ˌkaʊntər ˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋz/ | n. | 反广告，对抗性广告 |  Advertising that takes a position contrary to an advertising message that precedes it. It may be used to refute claims made in other advertising or to promote a different point of view. | Example: The counter-advertising's campaign aimed to debunk the myths perpetuated by the tobacco industry. 反广告活动旨在揭穿烟草业造成的种种神话。| Example:  Counter-advertising's effectiveness is often debated, but it can be a powerful tool for social change.  反广告的效果经常受到争议，但它可以成为社会变革的有力工具。| Synonym: counter-message, rebuttal advertising | Antonym:  supporting advertising,  pro-advertising | noun
