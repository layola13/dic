defrayers:/dɪˈfreɪərz/
|n.|支付者，承担费用者
|People who defray costs; those who provide funds to cover expenses.|
|The defrayers of the expedition's costs were a group of anonymous donors.|匿名捐赠者承担了这次探险的费用。
|The company sought out defrayers for the new project.|公司为新项目寻找出资者。

|近义词：financers, sponsors, backers, underwriters
|反义词：debtors, recipients

词性: n. (noun)

词源:  From the verb "defray" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Defray" comes from Old French "desfraier" meaning "to pay expenses," ultimately from Latin "dis-" (apart, away) + "frangere" (to break).  The idea is of breaking up or removing a burden (of expense).
