frontager:/ˈfrʌntɪdʒər/ | n. |临街业主，沿街地主| An owner of property fronting on a street or other public place. |  The frontagers complained about the noise from the new nightclub. 临街业主抱怨新夜总会发出的噪音。|  The frontagers were responsible for maintaining the sidewalk in front of their properties.  沿街地主负责维护他们房产前面的人行道。| synonym:  abutter, riparian owner | antonym:  N/A | noun
