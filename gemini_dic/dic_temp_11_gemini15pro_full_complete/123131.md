monism: /ˈmɒnɪzəm/ (US) /ˈmoʊnɪzəm/| n. |一元论|The doctrine that reality is ultimately made up of only one essence.  | Spinoza's philosophy is often described as a form of monism. 斯宾诺莎的哲学常被描述为一种一元论的形式。| 近义词：singularity, oneness | 反义词: dualism, pluralism | noun

monistic: /ˈmɒnɪstɪk/ (US) /ˈmoʊnɪstɪk/ | adj. |一元论的|Relating to or characterized by monism. | A monistic worldview sees everything as interconnected. 一元论的世界观认为一切都是相互联系的。| 近义词：unified, singular | 反义词: dualistic, pluralistic | adjective
