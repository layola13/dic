overdedication:/ˌoʊvərˌdedɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
过度投入，过度奉献
Excessive dedication to a cause, activity, or person, often to the detriment of other aspects of life.
His overdedication to his work led to burnout. 他对工作的过度投入导致了倦怠。
近义词: overcommitment, workaholism
反义词:  undercommitment, apathy, negligence
词性:  n.


Etymology:
From over- + dedication.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Dedication" comes from the Late Latin word "dedicatio," meaning "a setting apart, consecration," derived from the verb "dedicare," meaning "to dedicate, consecrate."
