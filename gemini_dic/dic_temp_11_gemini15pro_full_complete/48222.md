deconstruct:/ˌdɪːkənˈstrʌkt/ | v. | 解构，拆析 | To analyze (something, such as a text or work of art) by examining and interpreting its various aspects, especially with a view to revealing its inherent contradictions or hidden meanings. |  The professor deconstructed the poem, revealing its hidden political message. 教授解构了这首诗，揭示了其中隐藏的政治信息。

The critic deconstructed the film, analyzing its use of symbolism and cinematic techniques.  评论家解构了这部电影，分析了它对象征主义和电影技术的运用。

| 近义词: analyze, dissect, examine, interpret | 反义词: construct, synthesize, build |词性:动词
