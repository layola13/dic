belfry: /ˈbelfri/ (US), /ˈbelfri/ (UK)
n.
钟楼，钟塔
A bell tower; a room or framework in which a bell is hung, typically at the top of a church.
The bells pealed from the belfry. 钟声从钟楼里传出来。
The old church belfry offered a stunning view of the town.  古老的教堂钟楼提供了令人惊叹的城镇景色。

近义词: bell tower, campanile, steeple
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
