enterplead:/ˌɛntərˈpliːd/ | v. |互相辩护，相互起诉；提出交互诉讼|To plead against each other; to file cross-suits.|The two companies enterpleaded, each accusing the other of breach of contract.  这两家公司互相起诉，互相指责对方违反合同。| Example:  The insurance companies enterpleaded to determine who was liable for the damages.  保险公司互相起诉，以确定谁应该对损失负责。 |Synonyms: cross-plead, interplead|Antonyms: N/A|verb
