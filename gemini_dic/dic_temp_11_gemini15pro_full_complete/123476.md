monostrophic:/ˌmɒnəˈstrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 单层的，单旋回的 | Relating to or characterized by a single layer or a single cycle.  Specifically in meteorology, referring to a single-layer model of atmospheric flow, where the atmosphere is treated as a single layer of fluid. |  Monostrophic winds are often observed near coastlines.  单层风常在海岸线附近观察到。|  The monostrophic balance is a simplified model used in meteorology. 单层平衡是气象学中使用的简化模型。 | single-layer, one-layer | multi-layered, multi-cyclic | adjective
