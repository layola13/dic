nonreciprocal:/ˌnɒnrɪˈsɪprəkəl/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈsɪprəkəl/| adj. n.|非互惠的，非对等的；非互反的；单向的；非互惠元件|Not mutual; not operating or felt equally by both sides; one-way.|例句：The relationship between the two countries is nonreciprocal.  这两个国家之间的关系是不对等的。|近义词：one-sided, unilateral, asymmetric|反义词:reciprocal, mutual, two-sided|adjective

nonreciprocal:/ˌnɒnrɪˈsɪprəkəl/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈsɪprəkəl/| n.|非互惠元件；单向元件|A device that does not behave identically when its terminals are interchanged.|例句：A diode is a nonreciprocal element. 二极管是一个非互惠元件。|近义词：unidirectional device|反义词:reciprocal element|noun
