overpolish:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlɪʃ/ /ˌəʊvəˈpɒlɪʃ/ |v. |过度抛光；过分润饰|To polish something excessively, often to the point of detracting from its appearance or value.|He overpolished the car, and now the paint looks thin in places. 他把车过度抛光了，现在油漆有些地方看起来很薄。|Synonyms: overrefine, overdo|Antonyms: dull, roughen|verb

overpolish:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlɪʃ/ /ˌəʊvəˈpɒlɪʃ/ |n. |过度抛光；过分润饰（的状态）|The state of being excessively polished.|The overpolish on the furniture made it look cheap. 家具过度抛光，使它看起来很廉价。|Synonyms: overrefinement|Antonyms: dullness, roughness|noun
