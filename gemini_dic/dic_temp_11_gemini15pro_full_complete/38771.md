conduce:/kənˈd(j)uːs/
verb
导致；有助于；促成
To lead or contribute to a particular result.
Regular exercise conduces to good health.  规律的运动有助于身体健康。
The talks conduced to a settlement of the dispute.  会谈促成了争端的解决。
Synonyms: contribute, lead, promote, facilitate
Antonyms: hinder, obstruct, impede
