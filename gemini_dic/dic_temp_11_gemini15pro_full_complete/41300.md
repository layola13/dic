cordonnet:/ˌkɔːrdəˈneɪ/ | n. | 花边，细绳， cordon bleu菜肴上的装饰 | A thin string or braid, especially one used as a trimming on clothing or as a decorative tie for food, particularly associated with the presentation of cordon bleu dishes. | The chef tied the chicken breasts with a delicate cordonnet before frying them.  厨师在煎鸡胸肉之前用细绳捆扎好。| Synonyms: string, braid, ribbon, tie | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

cordonnet:/ˌkɔːrdəˈneɪ/ | n. | (建筑)凸圆线脚 |  A small molding, often used in classical architecture. | The architect included a delicate cordonnet along the edge of the frieze.  建筑师沿着楣饰的边缘加了一条精致的凸圆线脚。| Synonyms: molding, trim | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
