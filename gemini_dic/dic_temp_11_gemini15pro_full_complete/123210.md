monociliated:/ˌmɒnəˈsɪlieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 单纤毛的 | Having a single cilium. |  This organism is monociliated, possessing only one hair-like appendage.  这种生物是单纤毛的，只有一个毛发状附属物。 | Example 2: Monociliated cells play a crucial role in sensing fluid flow. 单纤毛细胞在感知流体流动中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: uniflagellate | 反义词: multiciliated | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "mono-" (Greek: μόνος, mónos, "single, alone") + "ciliated" (from Latin: cilium, "eyelash").
