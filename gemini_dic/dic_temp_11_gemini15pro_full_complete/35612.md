coaster:/ˈkoʊstər (US) / ˈkəʊstə(r) (UK)| n.|过山车；杯垫；沿海航行的船|A coaster is a small mat or tray placed under a drink to protect the surface of a table. It can also refer to a roller coaster or a ship that travels along a coast.|She put her glass on a coaster. 她把杯子放在杯垫上。

The children rode the roller coaster five times. 孩子们玩了五次过山车。|近义词： placemat (for the mat sense), roller coaster, drinks mat|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun


coaster:/ˈkoʊstər (US) / ˈkəʊstə(r) (UK)| v. | 沿海岸航行；滑行|To coaster means to sail along a coast or to move smoothly and easily, often downhill.|The ship coasted along the shore. 这艘船沿着海岸航行。

The sled coasted down the hill. 雪橇滑下山坡。|近义词：sail, glide, drift|反义词: climb, ascend|verb
