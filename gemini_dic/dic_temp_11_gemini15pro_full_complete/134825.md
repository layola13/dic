nonminority:/ˌnɑːnmaɪˈnɔːrəti/ | adj. | 非少数民族的；多数的 | Not belonging to a minority group; constituting or relating to the majority of a population. |  Example:  The nonminority population of the city voted in favor of the new law.  该市非少数民族人口投票赞成新法律。

Example: Nonminority students make up the majority of the student body. 非少数族裔学生占学生主体的大多数。| 近义词: majority, dominant | 反义词: minority | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + minority.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "minority" refers to a smaller group within a larger population.
