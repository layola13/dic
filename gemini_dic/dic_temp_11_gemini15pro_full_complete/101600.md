irrationalness:/ɪˌræʃəˈnælɪsnəs/| n.|不理性，非理性|The state or quality of being irrational; lack of reason or logic.|His irrationalness led him to make poor decisions. 他的不理性导致他做出糟糕的决定。|近义词：illogicality, unreasonableness, absurdity|反义词: rationality, reasonableness, logic|词性:noun
irrational:/ɪˈræʃənəl/ | adj.|不合理的，非理性的|Not logical or reasonable.|Her fear of flying is completely irrational. 她对飞行的恐惧完全是非理性的。|近义词：illogical, unreasonable, absurd|反义词: rational, reasonable, logical|词性:adjective
rational:/ˈræʃənəl/ | adj.|理性的，合理的|Based on or in accordance with reason or logic.|There must be a rational explanation for his behavior. 他的行为一定有合理的解释。|近义词：logical, reasonable, sensible|反义词: irrational, unreasonable, illogical|词性:adjective
