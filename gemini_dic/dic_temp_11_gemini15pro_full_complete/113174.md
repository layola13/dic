lurching:/ˈlɜːrtʃɪŋ/ (US) /ˈlɜːtʃɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 摇晃的，蹒跚的，突然倾斜的 | Moving in a sudden, unsteady, and uncontrolled way. | The ship was lurching violently in the storm.  这艘船在暴风雨中剧烈摇晃。|近义词：staggering, swaying, reeling, faltering |反义词: steady, smooth, balanced |词性:形容词

lurch:/lɜːrtʃ/ (US) /lɜːtʃ/ (UK)| n. vi. | 突然倾斜，蹒跚，踉跄 | A sudden unsteady movement or tilt; to make a sudden unsteady movement. | The bus lurched forward, throwing the passengers off balance. 公共汽车突然向前倾斜，使乘客失去平衡。|近义词：stagger, sway, reel, falter |反义词: steady, smooth, balance |词性:名词，动词
