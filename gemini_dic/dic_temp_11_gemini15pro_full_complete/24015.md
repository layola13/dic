ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *brech, from Old English *brēc (“breeches, trousers”), from Proto-Germanic *brōkiz (“trousers”), from Proto-Indo-European *bhr̥g- (“to cover, protect”). Cognate with Old High German bruoh (“trousers”), Old Norse brōk (“trousers”). Related to break.  源自中古英语 *brech，来自古英语 *brēc（“马裤，裤子”），来自原始日耳曼语 *brōkiz（“裤子”），来自原始印欧语 *bhr̥g-（“覆盖，保护”）。与古高地德语 bruoh（“裤子”）、古诺尔斯语 brōk（“裤子”）同源。与 break 相关。
