latitat: /ˈlætɪtæt/| n.|潜逃令；躲藏；潜逃|A writ by which a person is summoned to answer in personal actions in the Court of King’s Bench, and by which, if he does not appear, an attachment may issue against him.|He was issued a latitat for failing to appear in court. 他因未出庭而被签发了潜逃令。|近义词：abscondence, flight, hiding|反义词: appearance, presence|noun
