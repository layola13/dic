ordinate:/ˈɔːrdɪnət/ | n. | 纵坐标；坐标 |  The ordinate is the vertical coordinate on a graph. | The point (2,3) has an ordinate of 3.  (点(2,3)的纵坐标是3。) | synonyms: y-coordinate, vertical coordinate | antonyms: abscissa, x-coordinate | noun

ordinates:/ˈɔːrdɪnɪts/ | n. | 纵坐标（复数）;坐标（复数） | These ordinates represent the values of the function. | The ordinates on this graph show the population growth over time. (这张图上的纵坐标显示了人口随时间的增长。) | synonyms: y-coordinates, vertical coordinates | antonyms: abscissas, x-coordinates | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ordinatus, past participle of ordinare (“to set in order, arrange”), from Latin ordo (“order, rank”).
