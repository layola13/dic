minimising:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪŋ/
verb
最小化，使减到最小
present participle of minimise (UK), minimizing (US)  The act of reducing something to the smallest possible amount or degree.
The company is minimising its environmental impact. 公司正在将其对环境的影响降到最低。
近义词: reducing, decreasing, lessening
反义词: maximizing, increasing, enlarging
词性: verb (present participle)
