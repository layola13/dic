erectly:/ɪˈrɛktli/| adv.|竖直地，笔直地，正直地|in a straight upright position; in a direct and honest way|He stood erectly, shoulders back. 他站得笔直，肩膀向后.|近义词：uprightly, vertically, straightforwardly|反义词: crookedly, dishonestly, deviously|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *erectli, from Old French erectement, from Latin ērectē. Ultimately from Latin ērigō (“I raise up”).
