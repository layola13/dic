fuels:/ˈfjuːəlz/ (US), /ˈfjuːlz/ (UK)| n. |燃料|Material such as coal, gas, or oil that is burned to produce heat or power.|The rising cost of fuels is a major concern for businesses.  燃料成本上涨是企业关注的主要问题。|synonyms: energy sources, combustibles, propellants|antonyms: none|noun


fuels:/ˈfjuːəlz/ (US), /ˈfjuːlz/ (UK)| v. |给…加燃料；激起|To supply something with material that can be burned to produce heat or power; to make something stronger, especially a feeling or a type of behaviour.|The power plant is fueled by coal.  这家发电厂以煤炭为燃料。
The protests were fueled by anger over the government's policies. 抗议活动是由对政府政策的愤怒激起的。|synonyms: power, energize, incite, stimulate|antonyms: extinguish, dampen, suppress|verb
