nontheocratical:/ˌnɒnθiːəˈkrætɪkəl/ | adj. | 非神权政治的 | Not pertaining to or characteristic of a theocracy; not ruled by a religious elite.  | The government adopted a nontheocratical approach, separating church and state. 政府采取了非神权政治的方式，政教分离。 | Example:  The new constitution established a nontheocratical republic. 新宪法建立了一个非神权共和国。| Synonyms: secular, laic, lay | Antonyms: theocratic, hierocratic | adjective
