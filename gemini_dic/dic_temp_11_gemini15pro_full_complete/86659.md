hellenize:/ˈhɛlɪnaɪz/ /ˈhɛlənɪz/| v. |使希腊化，希腊化|To make Greek or to adopt Greek customs, language, or culture.|Alexander the Great hellenized much of the ancient world. 亚历山大大帝使古代世界的大部分地区希腊化。|The Romans hellenized many of the conquered territories. 罗马人使许多被征服的领土希腊化。|近义词：Grecize, acculturate|反义词: barbarize|词性:动词
