anxiety: /æŋˈzaɪəti/ | n. | 焦虑，忧虑，担心 | A feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease, typically about an imminent event or something with an uncertain outcome. | She felt a surge of anxiety before the exam. 她考试前感到一阵焦虑。 |  He suffers from social anxiety. 他患有社交焦虑症。 | 近义词: worry, concern, apprehension, nervousness, angst | 反义词: calmness, tranquility, serenity, composure | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *anxietās, from anxius (“troubled, uneasy”), ultimately from angō (“to strangle, throttle, torment”).
