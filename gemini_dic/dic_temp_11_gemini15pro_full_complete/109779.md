levite:/ˈliːvaɪt/| n.|利未人，利未支派的成员|A member of the tribe of Levi, one of the twelve tribes of Israel, traditionally assigned to priestly duties in the Jewish religion.  Specifically, a Levite was a descendant of Levi, but not a descendant of Aaron (the priests were descendants of Aaron).|The levites assisted the priests in the Temple. 利未人在圣殿里协助祭司。

The book of Numbers details the duties of the levites. 民数记详细记载了利未人的职责。|近义词：priest (in a broader sense)|反义词: (None -  lacks a direct opposite.  One could argue "layperson" in a religious context.)|词性:noun
