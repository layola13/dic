agronomic:/ˌæɡrəˈnɑːmɪk/ |adj.|农艺学的，农学的|Relating to the science of soil management and crop production.|例句：The agronomic properties of the soil were carefully analyzed. 土壤的农艺性质经过仔细分析。

例句：Agronomic research has led to increased crop yields. 农艺研究带来了作物产量的提高。|近义词：agricultural,farming|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *agronomique*, from Greek *agros* (field) + *nomos* (law, management).
