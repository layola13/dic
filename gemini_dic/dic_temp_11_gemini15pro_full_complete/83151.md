guttated:/ˈɡʌteɪtɪd/
adjective
滴出的；泌出的
covered with or exuding droplets of liquid, especially as a result of guttation.
例句：The leaves were guttated with dew. 叶子上滴着露水。

例句：Guttated droplets were observed on the leaf margins. 在叶缘观察到泌出的液滴。

近义词：exuded, secreted, dripping
反义词：absorbed
