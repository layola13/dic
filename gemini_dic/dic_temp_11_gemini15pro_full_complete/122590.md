modernised:/ˈmɒdənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪzd/ (US)| v. | 使现代化 | To make something more modern; to update or improve something to suit contemporary needs or standards. | The factory has been modernised to increase efficiency. 工厂已经现代化以提高效率。

The government modernised the education system. 政府使教育系统现代化了。


近义词: updated, renovated, improved, upgraded
反义词: outdated, antiquated, obsolete
词性: verb (past participle)


Etymology:
From modern + -ise/-ize.  "Modern" comes from Late Latin *modernus, from Latin modo "just now."
