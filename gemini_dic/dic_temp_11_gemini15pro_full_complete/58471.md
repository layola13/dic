dud:/dʌd/
noun
无用物，废物；衣着过时的人
something that fails to work or is otherwise unsatisfactory or worthless.
The fireworks were a dud, none of them went off. 这些烟花是哑炮，一个都没响。
synonyms: failure, flop, bust
antonyms: success, hit, triumph
词性: 名词

dud:/dʌd/
adjective
衣着过时的；失效的，不成功的
failing to work or meet expectations; unsatisfactory.
The movie was a dud. 这部电影很烂。
synonyms: unsuccessful, ineffective, useless
antonyms: successful, effective, useful
词性: 形容词
