overdigested:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈdʒɛstɪd/ |adj.|过度消化的|broken down by digestion more than normally.  The food was overdigested, losing most of its nutrients.|The baby's food was overdigested, making it too runny. 婴儿的食物被过度消化了，变得太稀了。|近义词: overcooked, overprocessed|反义词: undercooked, underdigested|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "digest" (from Latin <em>digerere</em>, meaning "to separate, distribute, dissolve").
