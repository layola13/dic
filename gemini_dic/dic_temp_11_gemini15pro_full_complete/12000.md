arthrodia:/ɑːˈθrəʊdiə/
n.
关节窝
A gliding joint; a diarthrosis in which the articular surfaces are nearly flat and admit of only gliding motion.
The carpal bones of the wrist form an arthrodia.  腕骨形成关节窝。

arthrodia:/ɑːˈθrəʊdiə/
n.
平面关节
A type of joint where the bones meet at flat or nearly flat surfaces, allowing them to glide against each other.
The acromioclavicular joint is an example of an arthrodia. 肩锁关节是平面关节的一个例子。


Etymology->
From Greek *arthrōdia*, plural of *arthrōdion*, diminutive of *arthron* "joint".

Synonyms->
gliding joint, plane joint

Antonyms->
(No direct antonym for a type of joint.  Could consider joints that allow a wider range of motion, like ball-and-socket joints.)
