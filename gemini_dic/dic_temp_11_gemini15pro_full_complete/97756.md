initiating:/ɪˈnɪʃɪeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
开始的，创始的
beginning, starting something
The initiating phase of the project is crucial.  项目启动阶段至关重要。
synonyms:  commencing, inaugural, introductory, pioneering
antonyms: concluding, ending, finishing, terminating
词性: adjective


initiating:/ɪˈnɪʃɪeɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle of initiate)
开始，发起
the act of beginning something
He is initiating a new program to help the homeless. 他正在启动一个新的项目来帮助无家可归者。
synonyms:  beginning, commencing, starting, launching
antonyms:  ending, finishing, stopping, concluding
词性: verb (present participle)


词源:  From Late Latin initiatus, past participle of initiare "to begin, originate," from Latin initium "beginning," from in- "in" + ire "to go."
