combativeness:/kəmˈbætɪvnəs/| n.|好斗，斗争性，战斗性|The quality of being eager to fight or argue.|His combativeness made him a formidable opponent in debates.  他的好斗性使他成为辩论中的劲敌。|Synonyms: aggressiveness, belligerence, pugnacity|Antonyms: peacefulness, docility, passivity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From combative + -ness. Combative from French combatif, from combattre "to fight," from Late Latin combattere, from Latin com- "together" + battuere "to beat."
