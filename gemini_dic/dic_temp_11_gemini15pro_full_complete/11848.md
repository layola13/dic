arrha:/ˈɑːrə/| n.|定金，订金，保证金|A deposit or earnest money given to bind a bargain or contract.  It signifies a part payment or pledge to ensure fulfillment of an obligation.|The buyer offered an arrha of $1,000 to secure the purchase of the property. 买家提供了1000美元的定金以确保购买该房产。|近义词：deposit, earnest money, down payment|反义词: N/A|名词
