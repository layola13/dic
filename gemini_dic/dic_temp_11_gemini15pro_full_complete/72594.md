fly-by-night:/ˈflaɪ baɪ ˈnaɪt/| adj. n.|不可靠的，不诚实的；夜间飞行；只做短期生意的人；骗子|A fly-by-night person or business is unreliable and likely to disappear or cheat people, especially by going away with their money. | Example:  Be wary of fly-by-night operations that promise quick riches.  小心那些承诺快速致富的短期生意。

Example: They hired a fly-by-night contractor who took their money and ran.  他们雇了一个骗子承包商，他拿了他们的钱就跑了。|近义词：unreliable, untrustworthy, shady, dishonest, irresponsible |反义词:reliable, trustworthy, reputable, honest, responsible |词性:形容词，名词
