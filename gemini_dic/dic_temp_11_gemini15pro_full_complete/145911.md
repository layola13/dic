overdignify: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 过分美化；过分抬举；过分重视 | To treat (something) with more dignity or seriousness than it deserves. | He tends to overdignify his minor ailments, complaining loudly about a simple headache. 他总是把他的小病夸大，一个简单的头痛也大声抱怨。 |  The museum overdignified the rather mediocre collection of pottery.  博物馆过分抬高了这批相当普通的陶器收藏。 | 近义词: exaggerate, aggrandize, overemphasize | 反义词: downplay, minimize, understate | 词性: 动词
