compensated:/ˈkɒmpənseɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpənseɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|已补偿的，已赔偿的|Having been paid or given something in return for a loss, service, or injury.|例句：He was fully compensated for his injuries. 他因伤得到了全额赔偿。|近义词：reimbursed, paid, indemnified, remunerated|反义词:uncompensated, unpaid|adjective

compensate:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpənseɪt/ (US)| v.|补偿，赔偿|To give something to someone in return for something they have lost or suffered.|例句：The company compensated her for the damage to her car. 公司赔偿了她汽车的损失。|近义词：reimburse, pay, indemnify, remunerate|反义词: penalize, fine|verb
