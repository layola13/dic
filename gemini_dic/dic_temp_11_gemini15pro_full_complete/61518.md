embryogenesis:/ˌɛmbriəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌɛmbriːoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
胚胎发生；胚胎形成
The process by which an embryo forms and develops.  This involves cell division, cell differentiation, and the formation of tissues and organs.
例句：The study of embryogenesis helps us understand how complex organisms develop from a single fertilized egg.  研究胚胎发生有助于我们理解复杂生物体是如何从单个受精卵发育而来的。

例句：Scientists are studying the role of genes in embryogenesis. 科学家正在研究基因在胚胎发生中的作用。

近义词：embryonal development,  embryo development

词性: noun
