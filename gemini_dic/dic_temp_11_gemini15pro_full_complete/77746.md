gender-bending:/ˈdʒɛndərˌbɛndɪŋ/
| adj. n.|性别模糊的，性别颠覆的|Relating to or involving the adoption of the dress or behavior of the opposite sex, especially for theatrical or other entertainment purposes.  Challenging or blurring traditional gender roles and expectations.|例句：The play featured a gender-bending performance where a male actor played a female character.  这出戏以性别颠覆的表演为特色，由一位男演员扮演女性角色。

|例句：Gender-bending fashion is becoming increasingly popular, with people embracing androgynous styles. 模糊性别界限的时尚越来越流行，人们开始接受中性风格。|近义词：androgynous, gender-fluid, gender-nonconforming, transgender|反义词:traditional, conventional|词性:adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From "gender" + "bending".  "Gender" refers to the socially constructed roles, behaviors, expressions, and identities of girls, women, boys, men, and gender diverse people. "Bending" implies a distortion or manipulation of something, in this case, gender norms.
