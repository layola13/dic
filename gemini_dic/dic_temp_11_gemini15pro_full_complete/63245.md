enterprised: /ˈentərpraɪzd/
adjective
有进取心的，有事业心的，有创业精神的
Characterized by a readiness to undertake new projects or ventures, especially business ventures; willing to take risks.
He was an enterprised young man, always looking for new opportunities. 他是一个有进取心的年轻人，总是寻找新的机会。
She's an enterprised businesswoman who built her company from the ground up.  她是一位有事业心的女商人，白手起家建立了自己的公司。
近义词: ambitious, resourceful, energetic, driven, proactive
反义词: unambitious, lazy, passive, inactive
词性: adjective
词源: From the verb "enterprise" + "-ed," meaning "characterized by enterprise."  The verb "enterprise" comes from Old French "entreprendre" (to undertake), ultimately from Latin "inter" (between) + "prendere" (to take).
