diesinking:/ˈdaɪˌsɪŋkɪŋ/
n.|落料加工，型腔加工；冲模制造|The process of creating a die or mold, typically by machining a cavity into a block of metal.  This process is used to create molds for casting or dies for stamping or forging.|Diesinking is a crucial process in the manufacture of many products. 落料加工是许多产品制造中的关键工序。|
|近义词：die making, mold making, cavity sinking|反义词:|词性:noun
