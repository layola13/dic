authorized: /ˈɔːθəraɪzd/ (US) /ˈɔːθərʌɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|授权的，批准的|Having official permission or approval.|例句：Only authorized personnel are allowed in this area. 只有授权人员才能进入该区域。|例句：The authorized biography of the president was released last month. 总统的授权传记已于上个月发布。|近义词：approved, sanctioned, licensed, permitted|反义词：unauthorized, forbidden, prohibited|词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "authorize," which comes from Old French "autoriser"  and ultimately from Latin "auctorizare," meaning "to give authority to."
