intercomplimentary:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːmplɪˈmentəri/ | adj. |相互补充的，相互完善的 | Serving to complement each other; mutually completing. | The two systems are intercomplimentary, each supplying what the other lacks. 这两个系统相互补充，彼此提供对方所缺乏的东西。| The intercomplimentary nature of their skills made them a powerful team. 他们的技能互补，使他们成为一支强大的团队。 |  近义词：complementary, synergistic, mutually beneficial | 反义词：conflicting, contradictory, incompatible | 词性:形容词

词源:
"inter-" (prefix) from Latin, meaning "between, among, mutually"
"complimentary" (adjective) from Latin "complementum" meaning "that which fills up or completes"
