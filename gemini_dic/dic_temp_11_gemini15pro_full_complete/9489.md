antiromanticisms:/ˌæntiˌroʊˈmæntɪsɪzəmz/
|n.|反浪漫主义，反浪漫情怀|The rejection of or opposition to romanticism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy.  It often emphasizes realism, skepticism, or irony.|His antiromanticisms manifested in his preference for stark realism in film. 他对电影中赤裸裸的现实主义的偏爱，体现了他的反浪漫主义情怀。|近义词: cynicism, pragmatism, realism|反义词: romanticism, idealism, sentimentalism|词性: noun


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to
romanticism (noun): a movement in the arts and literature emphasizing inspiration, subjectivity, and the primacy of the individual
-ism (suffix): forming nouns denoting action, practice, or condition
