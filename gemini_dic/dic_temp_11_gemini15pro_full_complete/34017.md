citizenish:/ˈsɪtɪzənɪʃ/
adjective
平民的，市民的，像市民的
Resembling or characteristic of a citizen; especially exhibiting the qualities or characteristics of a good citizen.  Having the qualities of a citizen, often in a positive sense, suggesting civic-mindedness or public spirit.
He displayed a citizenish concern for the park's upkeep. 他表现出市民对公园维护的关心。
Near synonyms: civic-minded, public-spirited, community-minded
Antonyms: uncivic, apathetic, selfish
词性: adjective
