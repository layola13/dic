dolling:/ˈdɒlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈdɒlɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |打扮；装扮；打扮得漂漂亮亮的人（尤指女孩或年轻女子）|The act of dressing someone, especially a child, in fine or fancy clothes. Can also refer to a person, especially a girl or young woman, who is dressed up in a particularly attractive way. |She spent hours dolling her daughter up for the party. 她花了几个小时为女儿打扮去参加聚会。|近义词：dressing, adorning, preening, beautifying, a doll (informal)|反义词:undressing, stripping|noun

dolling:/ˈdɒlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈdɒlɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |打扮；装扮|Present participle of "doll," meaning to dress someone up, usually in an attractive or elaborate manner. |She was dolling herself up for a night out.  她正在打扮自己准备晚上出去。|近义词：dressing, adorning, preening, beautifying|反义词:undressing, stripping|verb
