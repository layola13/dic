babbler:/ˈbæblər/| n.|喋喋不休的人，饶舌的人；爱说闲话的人|A person who talks incessantly and often foolishly or indiscreetly. |The child was a natural babbler, always chattering away.  这个孩子天生爱说话，总是叽叽喳喳的。|Synonyms: chatterbox, chatterer, prattler, gabbler|Antonyms: reticent person, quiet person|noun

babbler:/ˈbæblər/| n.|（尤指亚洲的）鸣禽科鸟|A kind of bird, especially an Asian one, belonging to the family Timaliidae. |We saw several different babblers on our birdwatching trip. 在我们的观鸟之旅中，我们看到了几种不同的鸣禽科鸟类。|Synonyms: songbird, passerine|Antonyms: (No specific antonym, could use a non-vocal bird like a vulture)|noun
