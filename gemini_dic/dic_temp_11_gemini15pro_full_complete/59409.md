earthed:/ɜːrθt/ /ɝːθt/
adjective
接地的；接地的；与大地连接的
Connected to the earth, especially so as to allow electricity to flow to the ground.
The appliance must be properly earthed.  这个电器必须正确接地。
The lightning rod is earthed to protect the building. 避雷针接地以保护建筑物。
grounded, connected (to earth)
unearthed
adjective
