colophony:/ˈkɒləfəni/ (US) /ˈkɑːləfoʊni/| n. |松香，树脂|A translucent, brittle resin obtained from the distillation of turpentine oleoresin from pine trees. It is used in varnishes, adhesives, and soldering fluxes, as well as in various other applications.|The violinist rubbed colophony on his bow to improve the grip on the strings. 小提琴家用松香擦拭琴弓以增加与琴弦的摩擦力。

|近义词：rosin, resin|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *colophone*, from Latin *colophonia rēsīna* "resin from Colophon," from Colophon, an ancient Greek city in Ionia famed for its resin.
