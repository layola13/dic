gusty:/ˈɡʌsti/ | adj. | 阵风的；刮风的 | Characterized by strong and sudden gusts of wind. |  The weather was gusty, making it difficult to control the kite. 天气多风，很难控制风筝。

The ship struggled through the gusty seas. 船在狂风巨浪中艰难航行。


近义词: windy, blustery, squally | 反义词: calm, still | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *gusti, from Old Norse gustr (“gust of wind”), from Proto-Germanic *gustuz (“blast, gust”), from Proto-Indo-European *gheus- (“to taste, choose”).  The original sense likely related to a sudden blast or puff of air.
