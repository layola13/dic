krystal:/ˈkrɪstəl/| n. adj.|水晶; 晶体; 克里斯特尔(女子名)|A solid material whose constituents (such as atoms, molecules, or ions) are arranged in a highly ordered microscopic structure, forming a crystal lattice that extends in all directions. Also, a female given name.|例句：The krystal vase sparkled in the sunlight. 水晶花瓶在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：Krystal is my best friend. 克里斯特尔是我最好的朋友。|近义词：crystal, quartz|反义词:amorphous|noun, adjective
