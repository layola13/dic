interdictors:/ˈɪntərˌdɪktərz/ | n. |禁令者，阻碍者|People or things that prohibit or forbid something; those who issue or enforce an interdict. |The interdictors prevented the shipment of arms to the warring nation.  禁令者阻止了向交战国运送武器。| The airport interdictors confiscated the smuggled goods. 机场拦截人员没收了走私货物。 |Synonyms: prohibitors, obstructors, blockers |Antonyms: facilitators, enablers |noun

interdictor:/ˈɪntərˌdɪktər/ | n. |禁令者，阻碍者|A person or thing that prohibits or forbids something; one who issues or enforces an interdict. |The interdictor stood firm in his decision to prevent the illegal activity.  禁令者坚定地执行阻止非法活动的决定。|The software acts as an interdictor against malware. 该软件充当了阻止恶意软件的拦截器。|Synonyms: inhibitor, obstructor, preventer |Antonyms:  promoter, enabler |noun
