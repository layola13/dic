overimitative:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
过度模仿的
Disposed to imitate the actions of others excessively, even when those actions are not relevant or appropriate.
Children are often overimitative, copying the behaviors of adults even when they don't understand the purpose.  孩子们经常过度模仿，即使他们不理解目的，也会模仿成人的行为。
Young learners can be overimitative, adopting incorrect pronunciations from their peers. 年轻的学习者可能过度模仿，从同伴那里学到不正确的发音。
近义词:mimic, copycat, ape
反义词:independent, original, innovative
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "imitative" (inclined to imitate), from "imitate" (from Latin imitatus, past participle of imitari "to copy, portray"), ultimately from PIE root *aim- "to copy."
