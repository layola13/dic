dad:/dæd/
n.|爸爸；爹爹|A male parent.|My dad taught me how to ride a bike. 我的爸爸教我骑自行车。
近义词:father, papa, pop
反义词:mom, mother
词性:noun

dad:/dæd/
v.|（儿语）叫爸爸|To call one's father "dad".|The baby learned to dad early. 宝宝很早就学会叫爸爸了。

反义词:N/A

词源:
