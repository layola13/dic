disorganized:/dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/
adjective
混乱的，无序的
lacking organization; not properly planned or arranged
Her notes were disorganized and difficult to follow. 她的笔记杂乱无章，难以理解。
The project was disorganized and fell behind schedule.  这个项目组织混乱，落后于进度。
近义词: messy, cluttered, chaotic, disordered
反义词: organized, orderly, neat, tidy
词性: adjective

disorganise:/dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪz/
verb
打乱，扰乱
to destroy the organization or order of something
The strike disorganized the company's production schedule. 罢工打乱了公司的生产计划。
Constant interruptions disorganise my work.  不断的打扰扰乱了我的工作。
近义词: disrupt, disturb, upset
反义词: organize, order, arrange
词性: verb
