masaba:/məˈsɑːbə/| n.|马萨巴（一种东非部落的传统服饰，通常色彩鲜艳，图案大胆）; (Uganda) a rectangular piece of printed cotton cloth, brightly coloured and boldly patterned, traditionally worn wrapped around the body.|Masaba is a popular clothing style in East Africa. 马萨巴在东非是一种流行的服装风格。|She wore a vibrant masaba to the festival. 她穿着鲜艳的马萨巴服饰参加节日。|近义词：kanga, kitenge (though these can have different specific meanings and regional variations)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "Western clothing" in a specific context)|词性:noun
