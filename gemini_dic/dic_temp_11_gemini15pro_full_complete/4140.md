air-mailing:/ˈerˌmeɪlɪŋ/
n.
air mailing
寄航空邮件
the act of sending something by airmail
例句：Air-mailing the package will ensure it arrives quickly.  寄航空邮件可以确保包裹快速到达。

例句：She considered air-mailing the documents but decided against it due to the cost. 她考虑过空邮这些文件，但由于成本原因决定不这么做。

近义词：airmail, sending by air
反义词：surface mail, sending by sea

词性：noun (gerund or present participle)

词源：From "air mail" + "-ing" suffix, denoting an action or process.  "Air mail" is a compound noun from "air" (referring to travel by aircraft) and "mail" (letters and parcels sent by post).  "Air" ultimately comes from Old French "air" and Latin "aer," while "mail" comes from Old French "male" and ultimately from Frankish "malha" meaning "bag, wallet."
