inlaying:/ɪnˈleɪɪŋ/
n.
镶嵌; 嵌饰
The act of setting pieces of wood, metal, etc., into a surface to create a decorative pattern.
The craftsman specialized in inlaying mother-of-pearl into ebony boxes.  工匠专门在乌木盒子上镶嵌珍珠母。
inlay, embedding, insertion
removal, extraction
noun


inlay:/ˈɪnleɪ/
v.
镶嵌; 嵌饰
To set pieces of wood, metal, etc., into a surface to create a decorative pattern.
He inlaid the table with intricate patterns of silver wire. 他在桌子上镶嵌了复杂的银丝图案。
embed, insert, set
remove, extract
verb
