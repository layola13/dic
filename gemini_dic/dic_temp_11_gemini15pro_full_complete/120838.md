misaward:/ˌmɪsəˈwɔːrd/
|n.|错误判决，误判|An incorrect or unjust award (of something like a prize, contract, or legal decision).|The misaward of the contract led to a lengthy legal battle.  错误的合同判决导致了漫长的法律诉讼。|The judge's misaward caused outrage among the public. 法官的误判引起了公众的愤怒。|近义词：wrongful award, unjust award, improper award|反义词：rightful award, just award, proper award|词性:noun


Etymology:
mis- (prefix) + award (noun)

"mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "badly," or "wrongly."

"award" comes from the Old North French word "awarder," meaning "to adjudge, determine."
