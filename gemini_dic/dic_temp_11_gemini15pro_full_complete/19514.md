bier:/bɪr/
n.
棺材；柩；担架
A stand on which a corpse or coffin is placed before burial.
The body lay in state on a bier in the church. 遗体停放在教堂的棺材架上供人瞻仰。

近义词:coffin, casket
反义词:None
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bǣr (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *bāriz, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- (“to carry”). Cognate with Dutch baar (“bier”), German Bahre (“stretcher, bier”).
