flakelike:/ˈfleɪklaɪk/| adj.|薄片状的，像雪片的|Resembling or consisting of flakes; flaky.  像薄片或由薄片组成；片状的。|The pastry was flakelike and buttery.  这糕点呈薄片状，而且黄油味很浓。|近义词：flaky, scaly, laminar|反义词:solid, compact|词性:形容词
