demotic:/dɪˈmɒtɪk/ /dɪˈmɑːtɪk/| adj. n.|通俗的，民众的；通俗体，世俗体|Relating to or characteristic of ordinary people.  A simplified form of ancient Egyptian writing.|The demotic script was used for everyday purposes.  这种世俗体文字用于日常目的。|近义词：popular, common, colloquial, vernacular|反义词：hieratic, esoteric, learned|词性: adjective, noun
