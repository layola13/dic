catonian:/kəˈtoʊniən/ | adj. | 加图式的，加图派的；禁欲主义的；简朴的 | Of or relating to Cato the Younger (95-46 BC), a Roman statesman known for his conservative and austere principles. Characterized by simplicity, austerity, and moral strictness. |  He lived a Catonian life, shunning luxury and embracing simplicity. 他过着加图式的生活，避免奢华，崇尚简朴。 | synonyms: austere, spartan, ascetic, simple, frugal | antonyms: luxurious, extravagant, indulgent, hedonistic | adjective
