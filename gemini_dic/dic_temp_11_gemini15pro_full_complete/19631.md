bijugate:/baɪˈdʒuːɡət/| adj.|双轭的，成对的|Having two yokes or pairs; paired.  Specifically in botany, having two pairs of leaflets. |Example 1: The bijugate leaves of the plant were a distinctive characteristic.  这种植物的双叶复叶是一个显著的特征。

Example 2: The scientist examined the bijugate structure under the microscope. 科学家在显微镜下检查了这种成对的结构。|近义词：paired, double, twofold|反义词: single, solitary|词性:adjective
词源: From Latin *bi-jugatus*, from bi- (“two”) + jugatus (“yoked”), past participle of jugare (“to yoke”).
