aurora:/əˈrɔːrə/ | n. |极光，曙光，黎明|A natural electrical phenomenon characterized by the appearance of streamers of reddish or greenish light in the sky, especially near the northern or southern magnetic pole.| We were lucky enough to see the aurora borealis. 我们很幸运地看到了北极光。| The aurora australis lit up the Antarctic sky. 南极光照亮了南极的天空。 | synonym:polar lights, northern lights (borealis), southern lights (australis), dawn | antonym:sunset, dusk | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Aurora, the Roman goddess of the dawn.
