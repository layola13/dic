词源:
From nameless + -ly.  Nameless comes from Old English *nama-lēas, from nama "name" + -lēas "-less".  The suffix -ly derives from Old English -līce, an adjectival suffix meaning "like, similar to, having the qualities of," which became an adverbial suffix in Middle English.
