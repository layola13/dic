exhalation:/ˌɛks(h)əˈleɪʃ(ə)n/
n.
呼气；呼出的气体
The exhalation of breath is a natural process. 呼气是一个自然的过程。
The doctor listened to the patient's exhalation. 医生听了病人的呼气。
近义词：breath, expiration, outbreath
反义词：inhalation, inspiration
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exhalatio, from Latin exhalare "to breathe out," from ex- "out" + halare "to breathe."
