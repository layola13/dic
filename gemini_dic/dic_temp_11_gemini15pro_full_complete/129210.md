nobley:/ˈnəʊbli/| adj.|高贵的，贵族的，崇高的|Of, relating to, or belonging to the nobility.  Characterized by high moral qualities; virtuous.|He behaved in a nobley fashion, putting others before himself. 他表现得高贵，把别人放在自己前面。|近义词：aristocratic, dignified, honorable, noble|反义词：base, ignoble, common, vulgar|词性:形容词

词源: From *noble* (adjective) + *-y* (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Latin *nobilis* (“well-known, famous, celebrated, high-born, of noble birth”).
