calumniation:/kəˌlʌm.niˈeɪ.ʃən/
n.
诽谤，诬蔑
the act of saying false and unpleasant things about someone in order to damage their reputation
例句：He was the target of calumniation by his political enemies. 他是他政敌诽谤的目标。

例句：The newspaper article was a vicious calumniation of the actor's character. 这篇报纸文章是对这位演员品格的恶意诽谤。

近义词：slander, defamation, libel, aspersion, vilification
反义词：praise, commendation, extolment
词性：noun
