maintaining:/meɪnˈteɪnɪŋ/
|v.|维护，保持；维持；坚持；断言；供养；|Keeping something in good condition or at a certain level.|例句：Maintaining a healthy lifestyle is important. 保持健康的生活方式很重要。|近义词：preserving, upholding, sustaining, supporting|反义词：neglecting, abandoning|词性:动名词(gerund)
词源: From Middle English *maintenen*, from Anglo-Norman *mainteignir, maintenir*, and Old French * maintenir*  "to hold in one's hand, maintain, support, defend, protect", from Latin *manu tenere* "to hold in the hand".
