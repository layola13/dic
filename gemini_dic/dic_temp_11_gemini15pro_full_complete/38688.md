condense:/kənˈdɛns/ | v. | 浓缩，凝结，精简 | to make something more dense or compact; to reduce the volume of something; to make something shorter by removing unnecessary parts | The steam condensed into water droplets on the cold window. 蒸汽在冰冷的窗户上凝结成水滴。 |  The author condensed his 500-page manuscript into a 200-page book.  作者将其500页的手稿精简为200页的书。 | synonyms: compress, concentrate, shorten, abridge | antonyms: expand, dilute, lengthen | verb
