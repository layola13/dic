adherer:/ədˈhɪrər/| n. |拥护者；追随者；信徒|Someone who adheres to a particular belief, cause, or leader. |例句：The Dalai Lama has many adherents worldwide. 达赖喇嘛在世界各地有很多追随者。

例句：She is a strict adherent to the rules. 她严格遵守规则。|近义词：follower, supporter, devotee, advocate|反义词：opponent, dissenter, critic|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *adhaerēns*, present participle of *adhaerēre* "to stick to," from ad- "to" + haerēre "to stick."
