cycloscope: /ˈsaɪkləskəʊp/ | n. | 环视镜；循环放映机 | An optical device used for viewing pictures arranged in a circle and appearing successively in the field of view, as if in motion.  A device that allows a viewer to see a series of images arranged in a circle, giving the impression of movement. |  The cycloscope displayed a series of images that simulated a horse race. 环视镜展示了一系列模拟赛马的图像。 | synonyms: panorama, cyclorama, diorama | antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a device with this specific function) | noun
