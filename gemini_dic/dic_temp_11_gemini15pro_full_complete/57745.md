dreamery: /ˈdriːməri/ (US), /ˈdriːməri/ (UK)| n. | 梦工厂，幻想世界，白日梦 | A place or state of mind characterized by fanciful or dreamy thoughts or ideas. |  She retreated into her own private dreamery, filled with visions of faraway lands. 她退缩到自己私密的幻想世界里，充满了对遥远土地的憧憬。

The old abandoned theater became a dreamery for the children who played there.  这座废弃的老剧院成了孩子们玩耍的幻想世界。| 近义词：fantasy, reverie, daydream | 反义词：reality, actuality | 词性: noun
