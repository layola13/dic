gigging:/ˈɡɪɡɪŋ/ | n. | 演出；演奏（尤指爵士乐或流行音乐）；临时工作 | The act of performing music, especially jazz or popular music, in public for money; also, working at a series of temporary jobs. |  He makes a living by gigging in local pubs. 他靠在当地酒吧演出谋生。 |  performing, playing, working (temporary jobs) | settling down, permanent employment | noun

gigging:/ˈɡɪɡɪŋ/ | adj. (less common) |  参加演出的；做零工的 | Relating to or involving gigging (noun). | He has a gigging schedule that takes him all over the country.  他的演出安排让他走遍全国。| performing, working (temporary jobs) | unemployed, idle | adjective
