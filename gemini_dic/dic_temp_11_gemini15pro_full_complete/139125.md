obeisant:/oʊˈbeɪsənt/ /əˈbiːsənt/ | adj. | 顺从的，恭顺的 | Showing respect and obedience; dutiful. |  The obeisant servant bowed low before the king. 恭顺的仆人在国王面前深深鞠躬。

The young student was obeisant to his teacher.  年轻的学生对他的老师很恭敬。


近义词: respectful, deferential, submissive, compliant, docile
反义词: disrespectful, defiant, rebellious, insubordinate
词性: adjective

词源:
From Old French *obeissant*, present participle of obeir (“to obey”), from Latin oboedīre, from ob- (“towards”) + audīre (“to hear, listen to”).
