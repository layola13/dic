forgeful:/ˈfɔːrdʒfəl/| adj.|健忘的，疏忽的|Often forgetting things; characterized by neglect or inattention.  Relating to or characterized by an act of forging.| He was forgeful of his promises. 他忘记了他的承诺。|近义词：forgetful, oblivious, negligent, remiss|反义词：mindful, attentive, careful|词性:形容词
词源: From "forge" (to create or shape, often metal) + "-ful" (suffix indicating abundance or tendency).  While seemingly related to "forgive" etymologically, its meaning and usage are distinct.
