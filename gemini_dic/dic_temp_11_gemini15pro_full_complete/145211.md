overbashful: /ˌoʊvərˈbæʃfəl/ | adj. | 过分害羞的，极其腼腆的 | Excessively bashful; extremely shy. | She was so overbashful that she could barely speak to anyone at the party. 她太害羞了，在派对上几乎不能和任何人说话。|  He gave an overbashful smile and quickly looked away. 他害羞地笑了笑，迅速把目光移开。 | 近义词: shy, timid, diffident, self-conscious | 反义词: bold, confident, outgoing, extroverted | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "bashful" (adjective meaning "shy").  "Bashful" comes from the verb "bash," which originally meant to be ashamed or abashed.
