flanked:/flæŋkt/ | v. | 侧翼，包围，位于…的侧面 | To be situated on each side of something or someone. |  The president, flanked by his advisors, entered the room. 总统在他的顾问的陪同下走进了房间。

The building is flanked by two towers.  这座建筑的两侧是两座塔。| 近义词：bordered, surrounded, escorted, accompanied | 反义词：unsupported, alone, exposed | 词性：动词(past participle)


flanked:/flæŋkt/ | adj. | 位于…的侧面的,被...包围的 |  Situated on each side of something or someone. | The flanked position of the soldiers gave them a tactical advantage. 士兵们位于侧翼的位置给了他们战术上的优势。

The house, flanked by tall trees, felt secluded and peaceful.  这所房子，两边都是高大的树木，感觉僻静而平和。 | 近义词：sided, bordered, enclosed | 反义词：exposed, open, central | 词性：形容词
