counterlath:/ˈkaʊntərˌlæθ/| n. |反向板条，挂瓦条|A narrow strip of wood fixed to rafters or ceiling joists and to which laths are nailed for supporting plaster or tiles.  It creates a space for air circulation behind the tiles or plaster.|The roofer installed the counterlath before laying the tiles. 屋顶工人在铺瓦之前安装了反向板条。

The counterlath helps prevent moisture buildup. 反向板条有助于防止湿气积聚。|近义词：furring strip, batten|反义词:|名词
