compendium:/kəmˈpɛndiəm/| n.|概要，纲要，汇编|A brief, comprehensive summary of a larger work or of a field of knowledge.|This book is a compendium of useful information on gardening. 这本书是园艺方面有用信息的汇编。|synonym: summary, abstract, epitome, digest|antonym: expansion, elaboration|noun

compendium:/kəmˈpɛndiəm/| n.|手册，指南|A collection of concise but detailed information about a particular subject, often intended for quick reference.|The style guide is a compendium of rules for writers.  这本风格指南是为作家提供的规则汇编。|synonym: handbook, manual, guidebook|antonym: N/A|noun
