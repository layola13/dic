appellate:/əˈpɛlɪt/ /əˈpɛlət/ | adj. | 上诉的，受理上诉的 | Relating to appeals; having the power to review and change the decisions of a lower court. | The appellate court overturned the lower court's ruling. 上诉法院推翻了下级法院的裁决。 |  The defendant has the right to file an appellate brief. 被告有权提交上诉摘要。 |近义词：reviewing, higher, superior |反义词: original, lower |词性: adjective

appellate:/əˈpɛlɪt/ /əˈpɛlət/ | n. | 上诉人 | A person who appeals a legal decision to a higher court. | The appellate argued that the trial court had erred in its interpretation of the law.  上诉人辩称初审法院对法律的解释有误。 | The appellate filed a motion for a new trial. 上诉人提出新的审判动议。 |近义词：appellant, petitioner |反义词: respondent, appellee |词性: noun
