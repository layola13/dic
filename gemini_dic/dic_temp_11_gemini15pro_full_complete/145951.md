overdiscusses:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌsɪz/
verb
过度讨论，过分讨论
to discuss something excessively or to an unnecessary degree
They overdiscussed the issue and failed to reach a decision. 他们过度讨论了这个问题，未能达成决定。
The committee overdiscussed the minor details, wasting valuable time. 委员会过度讨论了细枝末节，浪费了宝贵的时间。
近义词: analyze excessively, belabor,  overanalyze
反义词: underdiscuss, skim over, gloss over
词性: verb
