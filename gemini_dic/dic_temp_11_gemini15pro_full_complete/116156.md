mascle: /ˈmæskəl/ | n. | 菱形, 菱形饰, (纹章学) 镂空菱形 | A lozenge-shaped figure, voided, used in heraldry.  A mascle is always pierced or voided. It differs from a fusil, which is longer and narrower. | The knight's shield bore a mascle argent on a field sable. 骑士的盾牌上有一个银色的菱形图案，底色是黑色。|  lozenge, rhombus | N/A | noun
