convey:/kənˈveɪ/ /kənˈveɪ/| vt.|传达，表达，运送|to make (an idea, impression, or feeling) known or understandable to someone; to take or carry (someone or something) from one place to another|The song conveys a sense of longing. 这首歌表达了一种渴望的情绪。 The train conveys passengers from one city to another. 火车把乘客从一个城市运送到另一个城市。|近义词：communicate, transmit, transport, carry|反义词：misrepresent, withhold, obstruct|verb

conveyance:/kənˈveɪəns/ /kənˈveɪəns/| n.|运送，运输工具，表达|the action or process of transporting someone or something from one place to another; a vehicle or method of transport|The conveyance of goods by sea is cheaper than by air. 海上运输货物比空运便宜。  His conveyance was a battered old jeep. 他的交通工具是一辆破旧的吉普车。|近义词：transportation, transport, vehicle|反义词：N/A|noun
