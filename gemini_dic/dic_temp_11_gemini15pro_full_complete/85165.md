hardwoods:/ˈhɑːrdˌwʊdz/| n.|硬木|The wood of broad-leaved, mostly deciduous trees as distinguished from that of conifers.  Broad-leaved trees that lose their leaves annually.|
This table is made of beautiful hardwoods.|近义词：timber, lumber|反义词:softwoods|Noun
