abdication:/ˌæbdɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
放弃，退位，辞职
the act of formally giving up the position of king, queen, or emperor, or another similar position of power
例句：The king's abdication caused a political crisis. 国王的退位引发了一场政治危机。

例句：His abdication of responsibility shocked everyone. 他放弃责任的行为震惊了所有人。

近义词：resignation, renunciation, relinquishment
反义词：accession, assumption, coronation
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abdicatio, from abdicare (to disown, renounce, abdicate), from ab- (away from) + dicare (to proclaim, dedicate).
