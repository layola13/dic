bowstrung:/ˈboʊstrʌŋ/| adj.|紧张的，神经紧绷的，弦绷紧的|Stretched tight like a bowstring; hence, nervously strained.  Being in a state of tension or strain, like a tightly drawn bowstring.|He was bowstrung with anxiety waiting for the test results. 他焦虑地等待着测试结果，神经紧绷。|近义词：tense, strained, nervous, anxious|反义词:relaxed, calm|词性:形容词

Etymology:
bowstring (n.)  Old English *bogestreng, from bog "bow" + streng "string".  The adjective is a later formation.
