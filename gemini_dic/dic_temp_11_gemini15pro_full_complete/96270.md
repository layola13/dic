indicter:/ɪnˈdaɪtər/| n.|控告者；起诉者；告发者|A person who formally accuses another of a crime.|The grand jury acted as indicter in the case. 大陪审团在此案中充当了起诉人。|Synonyms: accuser, prosecutor, complainant|Antonyms: defendant, accused|Noun
