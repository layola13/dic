hemitrope:/ˈhɛmɪˌtroʊp/| n.|半晶|A crystal twinned in such a manner that one half of the crystal appears to be rotated 180 degrees about an axis perpendicular to a plane of twinning.|The hemitrope was clearly visible under the microscope. 在显微镜下，半晶清晰可见.|Synonyms: twinned crystal|Antonyms: single crystal|noun


hemitropic:/ˌhɛmɪˈtrɒpɪk/| adj.|偏向性的|Having different properties in different directions; anisotropic.|The hemitropic material exhibited different optical properties depending on the direction of the light. 这种偏向性材料根据光的方向表现出不同的光学特性。|Synonyms: anisotropic|Antonyms: isotropic|adjective
