caterwaul:/ˈkætərˌwɔːl/| n. vi.|猫叫春声，嚎叫；喧闹的抗议|A harsh cry or howl, like that of a cat in heat; also, a loud, shrill, and discordant noise. |The alley cats caterwauled all night. 巷子里的猫整夜嚎叫。|近义词：shriek, howl, wail, yowl|反义词：purr, whisper, murmur|词性:名词,动词

caterwaul:/ˈkætərˌwɔːl/| vi.|发出猫叫春声，嚎叫；喧闹地抗议|To make a harsh cry or howl, like that of a cat in heat; to protest or complain noisily. |The protesters caterwauled their disapproval of the new policy. 抗议者大声疾呼反对新政策。|近义词：shriek, howl, wail, yowl|反义词：purr, whisper, murmur|词性:动词
