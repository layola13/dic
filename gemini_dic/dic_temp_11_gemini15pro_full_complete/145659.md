overcontributing:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtrɪbjʊtɪŋ/
|adj.|过度贡献的；贡献过多的|Contributing more than is necessary or expected.  It implies an excess of effort or resources dedicated to something, often to the detriment of other areas or the contributor themselves.|She was overcontributing to the project, neglecting her other responsibilities.|近义词: overexerting, overdoing, overworking|反义词: undercontributing, neglecting|词性:形容词

overcontribute:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtrɪbjuːt/
|v.|过度贡献；贡献过多|To contribute more than is necessary or expected.|He overcontributed to his retirement fund, leaving himself short of cash.|近义词: overdo, overexert|反义词: undercontribute, neglect|词性:动词
