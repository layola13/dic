foraying:/ˈfɒreɪɪŋ/ /ˈfɔːreɪɪŋ/
verb
突袭；初次尝试；涉足
making a raid or brief invasion
例句：The company is foraying into the European market.  这家公司正在进军欧洲市场。

例句：She made a foray into acting but decided it wasn't for her. 她尝试了表演，但觉得不适合自己。

近义词：venturing, exploring, branching out, dabbling
反义词：retreating, withdrawing

词性：v. 动名词形式


foray:/ˈfɒreɪ/ /ˈfɔːreɪ/
noun
突袭；初次尝试
a sudden attack or incursion into enemy territory, especially to obtain something; a raid
例句：The soldiers made a foray into enemy territory. 士兵们对敌方领土进行了突袭。

例句：His foray into politics was short-lived. 他短暂地涉足了政界。

近义词：raid, incursion, attack, venture
反义词：retreat, withdrawal

词性：n.


词源: From Old French *forrier “to forage, pillage,” from forrer “outside."  源自古法语 *forrier “觅食，掠夺”，来自 forrer “外面”。
