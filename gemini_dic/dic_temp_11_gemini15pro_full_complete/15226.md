backface:/ˈbækfeɪs/| n. |背面，反面（尤指多边形的）|The backface of a polygon is the side that is not visible from the camera.  多边形的背面是从摄像机看不到的那一面。|例句：In computer graphics, backface culling is an optimization that discards polygons facing away from the viewer. 在计算机图形学中，背面剔除是一种优化，它丢弃了背对观察者的多边形。

例句：The artist painted a different scene on the backface of the canvas. 艺术家在画布的背面画了不同的场景。|近义词：reverse, underside|反义词: front, obverse|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From back + face.  "Back" meaning the rear or further side, and "face" referring to a surface.
