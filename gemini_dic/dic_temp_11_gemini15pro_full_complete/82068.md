grittily:/ˈɡrɪtɪli/
adverb
毅然地；坚韧地；不屈不挠地
In a determined and courageous manner, especially in the face of pain or difficulty.  Showing strength of character and resilience.
He grittily fought his way back from injury. 他顽强地从伤病中恢复过来。
近义词: resolutely, determinedly, stubbornly, persistently, tenaciously
反义词: easily, weakly, timidly
词性: adverb


grittily:/ˈɡrɪtɪli/
词源:  From the adjective "gritty" + the adverbial suffix "-ly". "Gritty" comes from "grit" (n.), meaning small hard particles, especially of sand or stone.  The metaphorical sense of courage and resolve developed from the idea of having a personality like grit - hard, unyielding, and resistant to wear.
词性: adverb
中文翻译: 毅然地；坚韧地；不屈不挠地
英文解析:  In a determined and courageous manner, especially when facing hardship or pain. Demonstrating resilience and strength of character.
例句: The boxer grittily held on until the final round.  拳击手顽强地坚持到了最后一轮。
例句: She grittily pursued her goals despite numerous setbacks. 她不顾重重挫折，毅然追求自己的目标。
近义词: determinedly, resolutely, persistently, tenaciously
反义词: timidly, weakly, hesitantly
