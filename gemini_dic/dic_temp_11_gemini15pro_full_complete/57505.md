dragnet:/ˈdræɡnɛt/| n.|搜捕；拉网式搜查；警网|A system of coordinated measures for apprehending (especially criminals). A method of searching for someone or something by covering a wide area systematically.|The police launched a dragnet to find the escaped prisoner. 警方展开拉网式搜查以寻找逃犯。|synonyms: roundup, search, manhunt, sweep|antonyms: release, liberation|noun

dragnet:/ˈdræɡnɛt/| n.|拖网|A large net drawn along the bottom of the sea or a riverbed. |Fishermen use dragnets to catch large quantities of fish.  渔民使用拖网捕捞大量鱼类。|synonyms: fishing net, trawl net|antonyms: N/A|noun
