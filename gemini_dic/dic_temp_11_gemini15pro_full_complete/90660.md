humbag:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n.| 骗局，花招；冒牌货| something that is not what it seems to be and is designed to deceive people; something that is not genuine |  The weight-loss pills were a total humbug. 这些减肥药完全是个骗局。| synonyms: hoax, sham, fraud, fake, deception | antonyms: truth, reality, genuineness | noun

humbag:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| vt.| 欺骗，哄骗| to deceive or trick someone | The salesman tried to humbug me into buying a more expensive car.  销售员试图哄骗我买一辆更贵的车。| synonyms: deceive, trick, dupe, cheat, swindle | antonyms: be honest, be truthful | verb
