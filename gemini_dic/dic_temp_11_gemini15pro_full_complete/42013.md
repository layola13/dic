costermonger:/ˈkɒstəˌmʌŋɡə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːstərˌmʌŋɡər/| n. |水果小贩；蔬菜小贩；街头小贩|A person who sells fruit, vegetables, or other goods from a stall or barrow in the street.|The costermongers were shouting out their prices to attract customers. 水果小贩们大声叫卖，以吸引顾客。|Synonyms: street vendor, hawker, peddler|Antonyms: shopkeeper, retailer|noun
