childly:/ˈtʃaɪldli/| adj.|孩子气的，幼稚的|Resembling or characteristic of a child, especially in immaturity or innocence.  Having qualities (such as innocence or naivety) that are considered typical of a child, especially a young one.|He showed a childly delight in the new toy. 他对新玩具表现出孩子般的喜悦。|Synonyms:  immature, juvenile, puerile, infantile|Antonyms: mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
