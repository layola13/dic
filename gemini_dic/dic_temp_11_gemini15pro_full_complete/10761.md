aquacultured:/ˈækwəkʌltʃərd/
adjective
水产养殖的
Cultivated in water; farmed in water.
例句：Aquacultured salmon is widely available in supermarkets.  超市里到处都有养殖的三文鱼。

例句：The aquacultured prawns were larger than the wild ones. 养殖的虾比野生的要大。

近义词：farmed, cultivated
反义词：wild-caught, wild
词性：adjective
