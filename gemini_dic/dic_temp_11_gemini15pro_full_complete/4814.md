algum:/aɫˈɡũ/ (Portuguese)| determiner, pronoun |一些，某个，任何一个|Used in Portuguese to express some, any, a certain, or a few. It can function as both a determiner and a pronoun.|例句：Algum dia eu vou viajar para o Brasil. (Someday I will travel to Brazil.)|近义词：(depending on context) algum, certo, qualquer|反义词:nenhum (none)|词性:Determiner, Pronoun


Etymology: From Old Portuguese *algum, from Latin *alicunus, from aliquis (“someone, anyone”).

注意事项:

"Algum" changes its form according to gender and number (e.g., alguma, alguns, algumas).  This entry focuses on the masculine singular form.  Its usage and meanings can be nuanced depending on the context and whether it precedes a noun or stands alone. It's important to consult a Portuguese grammar resource for a comprehensive understanding.
