go-up-in-smoke:/ɡəʊ ʌp ɪn sməʊk/ | idiom |化为乌有，付诸东流| To be destroyed or ruined; to come to nothing. | All our hard work went up in smoke when the computer crashed. 我们所有的努力都付诸东流了，因为电脑崩溃了。|Synonyms: evaporate, vanish, disappear, fail, fall through | Antonyms: succeed, materialize, flourish | idiom

go-up-in-smoke:/ɡəʊ ʌp ɪn sməʊk/ | idiom |化为乌有，付诸东流| To be wasted or lost, especially money or effort. |  His dreams of owning a business went up in smoke after the economic downturn.  他拥有自己企业的梦想在经济衰退后化为乌有。|Synonyms: be wasted, be squandered, be lost | Antonyms: be saved, be preserved, be gained | idiom
