blazonments: /ˈbleɪzənmənts/
n.
纹章装饰; 紋章的描绘; 公开宣扬;
Formal descriptions of coats of arms, or the act of displaying or proclaiming something widely.
The book contained detailed blazonments of the royal family's crests. 这本书详细描述了皇室的纹章。
The company's success was blazoned across the newspapers.  这家公司的成功被各大报纸广泛报道。
Synonyms: depictions, portrayals, proclamations, announcements
Antonyms: concealments, suppressions, secrets
noun
