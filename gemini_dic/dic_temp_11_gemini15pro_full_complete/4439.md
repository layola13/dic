n.
过程，状态，结果
The process, state, or result of an action, typically indicated by the suffix "-ation".
The revelation caused a sensation in the media.  这个爆料引起了媒体的轰动。
近义词:process, procedure, action, operation
反义词:inaction, cessation
词性:名词

词源:
From Latin -ātiō, -ātiōnis, from past participle stem of verbs ending in -āre.  源自拉丁语 -ātiō, -ātiōnis，来自以 -āre 结尾的动词的过去分词词干。
