fluids:/ˈfluːɪdz/| n.| 流体；液体 |Substances that can flow and take the shape of their container, such as liquids and gases. |Hydraulic systems use fluids to transmit power. 液压系统使用流体来传递动力。

The doctor advised him to drink plenty of fluids. 医生建议他多喝水。 |近义词：liquids, gases |反义词:solids |词性:noun (plural)

fluid:/ˈfluːɪd/| n. adj.| 流体；液体；流畅的 |A substance that can flow and take the shape of its container, such as a liquid or gas; smooth and graceful. |Water is a fluid. 水是一种流体。

Her movements were fluid and graceful. 她的动作流畅而优雅。|近义词：liquid, flowing, smooth |反义词:solid, rigid, stiff |词性:noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from late Latin fluidus ‘flowing’, from Latin fluere ‘to flow’.
