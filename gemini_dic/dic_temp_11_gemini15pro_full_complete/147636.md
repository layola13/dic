overpolemically:/ˌoʊvərˌpəˈlɛmɪkli/ | adv. | 过于论战地，过于争辩地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive argumentation or disputation, often to the point of being aggressive or unnecessarily combative. | His response was overpolemically charged, alienating even those who agreed with his general point.  他过于论战的回应疏远了那些即使同意他大体观点的人。 | 近义词: contentiously, aggressively, belligerently | 反义词: diplomatically, conciliatorily, amicably | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "polemical" (adjective meaning "relating to or involving strongly critical, controversial, or disputatious writing or speech") + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Polemical" ultimately derives from the Greek "polemikos" (warlike).
