fallency: /ˈfælənsi/ | n. | 谬误，谬论 | An instance of flawed reasoning or an invalid argument. |  Example: The politician's argument was based on a fallency, easily debunked by fact-checkers.  政客的论点是基于谬误，很容易被事实核查员揭穿。

Example:  It's a common fallency to assume that correlation equals causation. 假设相关性等于因果关系是一种常见的谬误。 | 近义词: error, fallacy, misconception, delusion | 反义词: truth, fact, reality, accuracy | 词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *fallentia, from Latin fallens, present participle of fallere "to deceive." Related to false.
