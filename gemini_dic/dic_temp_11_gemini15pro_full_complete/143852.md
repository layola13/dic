outplayed:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪd/
verb
以更好的技巧或策略击败，胜过
defeated by using superior skill or strategy
例句：She completely outplayed her opponent in the chess match. 她在国际象棋比赛中彻底击败了对手。

例句：The team was outplayed and outscored in the final. 该队在决赛中技不如人，比分落后。

近义词：defeated, beaten, surpassed, outmaneuvered, outsmarted
反义词：lost to, defeated by

词性: verb
