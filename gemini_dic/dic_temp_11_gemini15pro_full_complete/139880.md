ocypodian:/ˌɒsɪˈpoʊdiən/ | adj. |招潮蟹的；沙蟹的|Relating to or characteristic of ocypodes, a genus of crabs that includes ghost crabs and fiddler crabs.|These ocypodian crabs are known for their quick movements across the sand. 这些招潮蟹以在沙滩上快速移动而闻名。|近义词:brachyuran (短尾蟹的) |反义词: N/A |词性:形容词


词源: From Modern Latin *Ocypoda*, from Greek *ōkypous*, meaning "swift-footed" (from *ōkus* "swift" + *pous* "foot") + -ian (suffix forming adjectives).
