hessian:/ˈhɛsiən/| n. adj.|粗麻布，粗麻布制的|A strong, coarse fabric made from jute or hemp, used for sacks, upholstery, etc.  A person from Hesse in Germany.|例句：The potatoes were delivered in hessian sacks. 土豆是用粗麻布袋装运的。|例句：The hessian curtains gave the room a rustic feel.  粗麻布窗帘给房间带来了一种乡村气息。|近义词：burlap, jute|反义词:silk, satin|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology: From Hesse, a region in Germany where the fabric was originally produced.
