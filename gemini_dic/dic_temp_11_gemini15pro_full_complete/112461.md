loudspeaker:/ˌlaʊdˈspiːkər/ /ˌlaʊdˈspikər/| n.|扬声器，喇叭|A device that converts electrical signals into sound and projects it at a higher volume.|The band's music was blasted through the loudspeakers. 乐队的音乐通过扬声器播放出来。|synonyms: speaker, amplifier, bullhorn|antonyms: microphone, earphone|noun

loudspeaker:/ˌlaʊdˈspiːkər/ /ˌlaʊdˈspikər/| n.|扩音器|A device, especially in a public address system, that converts electrical signals into sound and projects it at a higher volume.|The announcement was made over the loudspeaker.  公告是通过扩音器发布的。|synonyms: PA system, public address system|antonyms: microphone, earphone|noun
