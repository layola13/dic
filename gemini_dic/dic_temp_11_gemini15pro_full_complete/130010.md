non-objective:/ˌnɒn əbˈdʒɛktɪv/
adjective
非客观的; 非表象的; 抽象的
Not representing or based on external reality or nature.  Relating to abstract art, especially painting, that does not depict recognizable scenes or objects.
His paintings are mostly non-objective, focusing on color and form rather than representation. 他的绘画大多是非客观的，注重色彩和形式而不是具象描绘。
近义词: abstract, nonrepresentational
反义词: objective, representational
词性: adjective
