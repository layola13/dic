nymph:/nɪmf/| n.|仙女，幼虫|A mythological spirit of nature imagined as a beautiful maiden inhabiting rivers, woods, or other locations. Also, the immature form of some insects.|例句：Greek mythology is full of stories about nymphs and their interactions with mortals. 希腊神话中充满了仙女和她们与凡人互动的故事。
