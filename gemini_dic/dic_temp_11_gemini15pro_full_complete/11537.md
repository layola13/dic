arisen:/əˈrɪzn/
|v.|出现，发生，产生|Past participle of arise.  Having come into being or existence.|A new problem has arisen. 一个新问题出现了。| 近义词：emerged, occurred, appeared|反义词: subsided, disappeared|词性:动词


Etymology:
From Middle English *arisen*, from Old English *ārīsan* (“to arise”), from Proto-Germanic *uzrīsaną (“to arise”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂rews- (“to rise”). Cognate with Dutch *rijzen* (“to rise”), German *reisen* (“to travel”), Old Norse *rísa* (“to rise”).
