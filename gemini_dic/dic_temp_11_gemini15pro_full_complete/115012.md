mandatory:/ˈmændətɔːri/ (US) /ˈmændətri/ (UK)| adj.|强制的，义务的，必须的|Required by law or rules; compulsory.|例句：Attendance at the meeting is mandatory.  出席会议是强制性的。|例句：Wearing a helmet is mandatory for all motorcycle riders. 所有摩托车骑手都必须戴头盔。|近义词：compulsory, obligatory, required, essential|反义词：optional, voluntary, elective|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *mandatorius, from Latin mandatum (“a mandate, order”), from mandare (“to entrust, order”).
