bypassers:/ˈbaɪˌpæsərz/| n. |旁观者|People who observe a situation but do not get involved.|The bypassers simply watched the accident unfold. 旁观者只是看着事故发生。|Synonyms: observers, witnesses, spectators|Antonyms: participants, rescuers|noun
