additives:/ˈædɪtɪvz/
noun
添加剂
Substances added to something in small quantities, typically to improve or preserve it.
Many processed foods contain chemical additives. 许多加工食品都含有化学添加剂。
synonyms:  adjuncts, supplements, ingredients
antonyms: base, natural, pure
