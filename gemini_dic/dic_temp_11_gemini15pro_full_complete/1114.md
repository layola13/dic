accessive:/əkˈsɛsɪv/| adj.|易接近的；可得到的；易懂的；平易近人的；（价格）不贵的；过度的；过分的|easily reached or entered; easy to understand or obtain;  (of a price) not high; excessive |The information is easily accessible online.  这些信息在网上很容易找到。|近义词：approachable, available, attainable, affordable, reasonable, excessive, immoderate|反义词：inaccessible, unavailable, unattainable, unaffordable, expensive, moderate|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin accessivus, from accedere ‘to approach’ (see accede).
