osteal:/ˈɒstiəl/ (US) /ˈɑːstiəl/| adj.|骨的，骨质的|Relating to bone; bony.|Example: Osteal tissue is the hard, dense connective tissue forming the skeleton. 骨组织是构成骨骼的坚硬致密的结缔组织。|Example:  The osteal density decreases with age. 骨密度随着年龄的增长而降低。|Synonym: bony, osseous|Antonym: nonosseous|词性:形容词
