internationalist: /ɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪst/ | n. adj. | 国际主义者；国际主义的 | A person who advocates or believes in internationalism; also used as an adjective to describe something related to or supporting internationalism.  Internationalism is the principle of cooperation among nations, for the promotion of their common good, sometimes as contrasted with nationalism or patriotism. |  Example:  He was a staunch internationalist, believing in global cooperation. 他是一位坚定的国际主义者，相信全球合作。

| Example: The internationalist organization promotes peace and understanding between nations.  这个国际主义组织促进各国之间的和平与理解。 | Near synonyms: cosmopolitan, globalist, humanist | Antonyms: nationalist, isolationist, patriot | noun, adjective
