metazoa:/ˌmɛtəˈzoʊə/ | n. | 后生动物 |  A major division of the animal kingdom that comprises all animals except protozoa and sponges.  Metazoans are multicellular organisms with differentiated tissues. |  例句：Metazoa are characterized by their complex organization. 后生动物的特点是它们的复杂组织。

例句：The study of metazoa is essential for understanding animal evolution.  研究后生动物对于理解动物进化至关重要。 | 近义词：animal | 反义词：protozoa | 词性：名词
