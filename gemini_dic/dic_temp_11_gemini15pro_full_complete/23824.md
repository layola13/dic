breached:/britʃt/ | v. | 违反，破坏，突破 | to break through, or otherwise violate a law, promise, agreement, or code of conduct |  The hackers breached the company's security system. 黑客入侵了公司的安全系统。

The dam was breached and the town was flooded.  大坝决堤，城镇被淹没了。| 近义词:violated, broken, infringed, contravened | 反义词:obeyed, upheld, honored, complied with | 词性:动词

breached:/britʃt/ | adj. | 被突破的，被违反的 | having been broken through or violated | The breached contract led to a lawsuit.  被违反的合同导致了一场诉讼。

The breached dam caused widespread flooding. 被破坏的大坝造成了大范围的洪水。| 近义词:violated, broken, compromised | 反义词:intact, secure, unbroken | 词性:形容词


词源: From Old French *brechier, from breche "a break, breach," from Frankish *breka or some other Germanic source, from Proto-Germanic *brekan "to break."
