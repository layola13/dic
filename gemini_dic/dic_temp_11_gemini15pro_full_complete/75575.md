fuddled:/ˈfʌdld/| adj.|困惑的，糊涂的，醉醺醺的|Confused and unable to think clearly, especially as a result of drinking alcohol.|例句：He was completely fuddled after drinking too much wine. 他喝了太多酒，完全糊涂了。

例句：The complicated instructions left her feeling fuddled. 复杂的说明让她感到困惑。|近义词：confused, bewildered, muddled, tipsy, intoxicated|反义词：clear-headed, sober, lucid|词性:形容词

fuddle:/ˈfʌdl/| vt.|使迷惑，使糊涂，灌醉|To make someone confused and unable to think clearly.|例句：The long journey and lack of sleep completely fuddled him. 长途旅行和缺乏睡眠让他完全糊涂了。

例句：Don't fuddle me with all these technical details. 不要用这些技术细节把我搞糊涂。|近义词：confuse, bewilder, muddle, intoxicate|反义词：clarify, enlighten|词性:动词


词源:
From Middle English *fudlen, frequentative of Old English fudulian (“to act foolishly, be frivolous”), of unknown origin.
