autochthon:/ˈɔːtɒkθən/ (UK) /ɔːˈtɑːkθən/ (US)| n.|土著，原住民|A person, plant, or animal that is indigenous to a place; native.|The autochthons of Australia are the Aboriginal people.|Synonyms: native, indigenous, aboriginal|Antonyms: immigrant, alien, foreigner|noun

autochthonous:/ɔːˈtɒkθənəs/ (UK) /ɔːˈtɑːkθənəs/ (US)| adj.|土生的，原产的|Originating or formed in the place where found; indigenous.|The autochthonous flora of the island is unique.|Synonyms: native, indigenous, endemic|Antonyms: foreign, introduced, exotic|adjective
