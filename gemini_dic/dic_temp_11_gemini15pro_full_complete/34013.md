citizen: /ˈsɪtɪzn/ (US) /ˈsɪtɪzən/ (UK)| n.|公民；市民；居民|A legally recognized subject or national of a state or commonwealth, either native or naturalized.|She's a British citizen. 她是一位英国公民。|Synonyms: national, resident, inhabitant|Antonyms: foreigner, alien|noun


citizen: /ˈsɪtɪzn/ (US) /ˈsɪtɪzən/ (UK)| adj.|市民的；公民的|Relating to the citizens of a place.|Citizen participation is crucial for a healthy democracy. 公民参与对于健康的民主至关重要。|Synonyms: civic, municipal|Antonyms: non-citizen, foreign|adjective
