immaculately:/ɪˈmækjələtli/| adv.|完美无瑕地，洁净地|In a manner that is perfectly clean, neat, or tidy; without any flaws or mistakes.|The house was immaculately clean. 房子一尘不染。|近义词：perfectly, flawlessly, spotlessly|反义词：sloppily, messily|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *immaculatus, from in- "not" + maculatus "spotted, stained," past participle of maculare "to spot, stain," from macula "spot, stain."
