guyu: /ɡuːˈjuː/ | n. | 谷雨 (Grain Rain) | Guyu is the sixth of the 24 solar terms in the traditional Chinese lunisolar calendar. It signifies the arrival of ample rainfall, essential for the growth of crops. It marks the end of spring and the beginning of summer. |  Guyu is an important time for farmers in China. | 近义词: (No direct English equivalent, can be related to) spring showers, rain season | 反义词: drought | 词性: Noun
