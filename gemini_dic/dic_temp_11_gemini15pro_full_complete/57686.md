drawling:/ˈdrɔːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 慢吞吞的，拖长的 | Speaking in a slow, lazy way, often prolonging the vowels. |  He had a slow, drawling Southern accent. 他说话慢吞吞的，带着南方口音。| 近义词：slow, lazy, prolonged | 反义词：rapid, quick, brisk | 词性：形容词

drawl:/drɔːl/ | v. n. |  慢吞吞地说，拖长声音；慢吞吞的说话方式 |  To speak slowly and lazily, often prolonging the vowels. | He drawled his words in a relaxed manner. 他慢条斯理地说着话。| 近义词：drone, elongate | 反义词：clip, rush | 词性：动词/名词

词源: From Middle English *drawlen, frequentative of drawen (“to draw”).
