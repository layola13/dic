ommateal:/ɒˈmeɪtiəl/ | adj. | 复眼的，小眼的 | Relating to an ommatidium, which is a single unit of a compound eye found in insects and crustaceans.  Each ommatidium contributes a small part of the overall image. | The fly's ommatea allowed it to see a wide range of motion.  苍蝇的复眼使它能够看到大范围的运动。 | The bee has thousands of ommatea in each compound eye. 蜜蜂的每只复眼里都有成千上万的小眼。 | compound, multifaceted | simple | adjective
