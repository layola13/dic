beseeched:/bɪˈsiːtʃt/
verb
恳求，哀求
To ask (someone) urgently and fervently to do something; implore; entreat.
The villagers beseeched the king for help. 村民恳求国王的帮助。
implore, entreat, beg, plead
demand, command
