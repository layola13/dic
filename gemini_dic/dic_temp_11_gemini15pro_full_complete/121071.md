misconstruer: /ˌmɪskənˈstruːər/ | n. | 误解者，曲解者 | Someone who misconstrues something; someone who interprets something incorrectly. | He was a misconstruer of good intentions, always suspecting ulterior motives. 他总是误解别人的好意，总是怀疑别人别有用心。| 近义词: misinterpreter, distorter | 反义词: interpreter, explainer | 词性: noun

misconstrue: /ˌmɪskənˈstruː/ | vt. | 误解，曲解，歪曲 | To interpret (something, such as a word or action) wrongly. |  She misconstrued his silence as anger. 她把他的沉默误解为生气。| 近义词: misinterpret, misunderstand, misapprehend | 反义词: understand, comprehend, grasp | 词性: verb
