knolling:/ˈnɒlɪŋ/| n.|排列整理，平铺展示|The process of arranging related objects in parallel or 90-degree angles as a method of organization.  This arrangement is often photographed and shared online.|He spent an hour knolling his tools before starting the project. 他在开始项目前花了一个小时排列整理他的工具。|近义词：arranging, organizing, tidying|反义词: scattering, cluttering|noun

knolling:/ˈnɒlɪŋ/| v.|排列整理，平铺展示|To arrange items neatly at right angles, usually for a photograph.|She knolled all her art supplies on the table. 她把所有的美术用品都整齐地摆放在桌子上。|近义词：arrange, organize, tidy|反义词: scatter, clutter|verb
