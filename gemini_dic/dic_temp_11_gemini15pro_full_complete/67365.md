extensive:/ɪkˈstɛnsɪv/| adj.|广泛的，大量的，广阔的|Covering a large area; having a great range.|例句：She has extensive knowledge of music. 她对音乐有着广泛的了解。|例句：The house has extensive grounds. 这房子有广阔的庭院。|近义词：wide, broad, large, comprehensive, vast|反义词：limited, narrow, restricted, small|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *extensus*, past participle of *extendere* "to stretch out," from *ex- "out" + *tendere* "to stretch."
