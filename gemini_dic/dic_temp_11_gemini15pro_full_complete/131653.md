noncomputerized:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpjuːtəraɪzd/
adjective
非计算机化的；非电算化的
Not computerized; done or operated without the use of a computer.
例句：They still use a noncomputerized system for tracking inventory.  他们仍然使用非计算机化的系统来跟踪库存。

例句：The library's card catalog is noncomputerized.  图书馆的卡片目录是非计算机化的。
近义词：manual, analog, traditional
反义词：computerized, digital, automated
词性: adjective
