hymenopteran:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərən/ (US) /ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərən/ (UK)| n.|膜翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Hymenoptera, including bees, wasps, ants, and sawflies, characterized by two pairs of membranous wings.|例句：The bee is a common hymenopteran. 蜜蜂是一种常见的膜翅目昆虫。

例句：Many hymenopterans are social insects. 许多膜翅目昆虫是社会性昆虫。|近义词：insect|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be non-insect)|词性:noun
