incompliant:/ɪnkəmˈplaɪənt/
adj.
不服从的，不顺从的
Not compliant; failing or refusing to comply with a rule, request, or command.
The student was incompliant with school rules and constantly got into trouble.  这个学生不遵守校规，经常惹麻烦。
The incompliant patient refused to take his medication.  不顺从的病人拒绝服药。
近义词:disobedient, noncompliant, resistant, rebellious, defiant
反义词:compliant, obedient, submissive, docile
词性:形容词
词源:in- (not) + compliant (yielding; obeying)
