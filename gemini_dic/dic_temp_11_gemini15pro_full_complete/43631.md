creational:/kriˈeɪʃənəl/| adj.|创造的，创建的|Relating to creation or the act of creating something; pertaining to the origination of something new.|例句：Creational design patterns are essential for building flexible and maintainable software. 创造性设计模式对于构建灵活且可维护的软件至关重要。|近义词：productive, formative, generative|反义词：destructive, ruinous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *creationale, from Latin creatio (“creation”), from creare (“to create”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“to grow”).
