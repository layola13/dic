naggingnesses:/ˈnæɡɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
反复唠叨，持续抱怨
The state of being characterized by persistent nagging or complaints.
Her naggingnesses about the dishes were starting to annoy him. 她对洗碗的唠叨开始让他恼火。
近义词：carping, complaining, faultfinding
反义词：praise, compliment
词性：名词

词源: From "nag" (verb) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ness" (noun-forming suffix) + "-es" (plural suffix).  The verb "nag" comes from Old Norse *nagga "to gnaw, bite, grumble."
