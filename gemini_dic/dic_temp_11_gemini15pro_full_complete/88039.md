highbrow:/ˈhaɪbraʊ/| n. |知识分子，文化人|A person who has or affects superior intellectual or cultural interests and tastes.|She considers herself a highbrow and looks down on popular culture. 她自认为是个知识分子，瞧不起流行文化。|近义词：intellectual, cultured, sophisticated|反义词：lowbrow, philistine|Noun

highbrow:/ˈhaɪbraʊ/| adj. |高雅的，曲高和寡的|Appealing to or designed for highly cultured or intellectual people.|The museum's new exhibit is a bit too highbrow for the average visitor.  博物馆的新展览对普通游客来说有点曲高和寡。|近义词：intellectual, sophisticated, refined|反义词：lowbrow, popular, unsophisticated|Adjective
