jacobean:/ˌdʒækəˈbiːən/ | adj. n. | 雅各布王朝的；雅各布一世时代的；雅各布党人；雅各布派诗人 | Relating to the reign of James I of England (1603-1625); a supporter of James II after he was deposed in 1688; a style of architecture, furniture, etc. from this period. | The jacobean era was a golden age for English literature. 雅各布时代是英国文学的黄金时代。 |  Synonyms: seventeenth-century, Renaissance (for the artistic style) | Antonyms: modern, contemporary | adjective, noun

jacobean:/ˌdʒækəˈbiːən/ | n. | 雅各布党人；雅各布派诗人 | A supporter of James II after he was deposed in 1688; a poet of the Jacobean era. |  Many Jacobeans fled to France after the Glorious Revolution. 光荣革命后，许多雅各布党人逃往法国。| Synonyms: Jacobite | Antonyms: Williamite | noun
