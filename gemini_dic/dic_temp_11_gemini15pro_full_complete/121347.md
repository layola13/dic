misgoverned:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvənd/ | adj. | 管理不善的，治理不当的 | Ruled or managed badly or wrongly. | The country was misgoverned for decades, leading to widespread poverty.  这个国家几十年来管理不善，导致普遍贫困。|  The misgoverned city was plagued by crime and corruption. 这个治理不善的城市充斥着犯罪和腐败。 |Synonyms: mismanaged, misruled, ill-governed | Antonyms: well-governed, well-managed, well-run | Word type: Adjective

Etymology:
From mis- + governed, past participle of govern.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly." "Govern" comes from Old French governer, ultimately from Latin gubernare "to steer, guide, direct, govern."
