balsamaceous:/ˌbɔːlsəˈmeɪʃəs/| adj.|香脂的，香胶的，有香脂味的|Having the qualities of balsam; yielding or resembling balsam; fragrant.|The air was thick with a balsamaceous scent. 空气中弥漫着香脂的气味。|Synonyms: aromatic, fragrant, balmy, resinous, spicy|Antonyms: acrid, fetid, odorless|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *balsamāceus, from Latin balsamum (“balsam”) + -āceus.
