indisposed:/ˌɪndɪˈspəʊzd/ (UK), /ˌɪndɪˈspoʊzd/ (US)| adj.|不舒服的，不愿意的|Slightly unwell or reluctant to do something.|例句：I'm feeling a bit indisposed today. 我今天感觉有点不舒服。

例句：He was indisposed to accept the invitation. 他不愿意接受邀请。

近义词：unwell, ill, sick, reluctant, unwilling|反义词:well, healthy, willing, eager|词性:adjective
