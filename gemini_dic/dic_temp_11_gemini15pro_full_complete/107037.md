lactobacillus:/ˌlæktəʊbəˈsɪləs/ | n. | 乳酸杆菌 | A rod-shaped bacterium of a genus that produces lactic acid, especially in fermented milk products.  It is commonly found in yogurt and other fermented foods. | Lactobacillus is crucial for maintaining a healthy gut flora. 乳酸杆菌对维持健康的肠道菌群至关重要。

Lactobacillus strains are used in probiotic supplements. 乳酸杆菌菌株被用于益生菌补充剂。| 近义词: lactic acid bacteria | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
