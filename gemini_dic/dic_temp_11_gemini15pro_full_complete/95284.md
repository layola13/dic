incarcerate:/ɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪt/| vt.|监禁，关押|To confine someone in prison; to imprison.|例句：The government incarcerated the political dissidents. 政府监禁了政治异议人士。

例句：He was incarcerated for his involvement in the robbery. 他因参与抢劫而被监禁。

近义词：imprison, jail, confine, detain|反义词:liberate, release, free|词性:动词
