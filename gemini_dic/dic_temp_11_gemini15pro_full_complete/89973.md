horsewhipped:/ˈhɔːrsˌwɪpt/| v. |用马鞭抽打；严厉斥责|To whip with a horsewhip; to flog or punish severely; to criticize harshly.|The slave was horsewhipped for disobeying his master.  这个奴隶因不服从主人而被鞭打。|

The politician was horsewhipped in the press for his controversial remarks.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论而受到媒体的严厉批评。|近义词：flog, lash, scourge, castigate, reprimand|反义词：praise, commend|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From horse + whip + -ed.  "Whip" comes from Middle English wippen. "Horse" comes from Old English hors.
