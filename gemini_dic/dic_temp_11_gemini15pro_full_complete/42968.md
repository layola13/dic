covert:/ˈkʌvərt/ (US), /ˈkəʊvɜːt/ (UK)| adj. n.|秘密的，隐蔽的；隐蔽处，藏身处|Concealed; secret; disguised.  As a noun, it refers to a hiding place, especially one in undergrowth.|例句：The spy went on a covert mission.  间谍执行了一项秘密任务。

例句：The fox disappeared into its covert in the woods. 狐狸消失在树林里的藏身之处。|近义词：clandestine, surreptitious, hidden, secret, stealthy|反义词：overt, open, public, unconcealed|词性: adjective, noun
covert:/ˈkʌvərt/ (US), /ˈkəʊvɜːt/ (UK)| n.| 隐蔽处，藏身处|A hiding place, especially one in undergrowth.|例句：The deer sought covert in the dense thicket.  鹿在茂密的灌木丛中寻找藏身之处。
