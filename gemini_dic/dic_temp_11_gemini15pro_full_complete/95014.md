inabilities:/ˌɪnəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
无能；无力；不能
lack of ability to do something
His inability to accept criticism led to many disagreements. 他无法接受批评，导致了很多分歧。
Synonyms:incompetences, weaknesses, disabilities, failings, shortcomings
Antonyms:abilities, capabilities, talents, skills, strengths
noun
