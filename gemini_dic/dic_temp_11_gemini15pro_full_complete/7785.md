answerable-to:/ˈɑːnsərəbl tuː/
adjective
对…负责的，应向…汇报的
Responsible to someone or some organization for one's conduct or decisions.
The CEO is answerable to the board of directors. 首席执行官对董事会负责。
Synonyms: accountable, responsible, liable
Antonyms: unaccountable, irresponsible
词性: 形容词
