nonsimilarly:/nɒnˈsɪmələli/ | adv. | 不同地，不相似地 | In a way that is not similar; differently. | The two groups reacted nonsimilarly to the stimulus. 这两组对刺激的反应不同。| The twins, despite their identical looks, behaved nonsimilarly in social situations. 这对双胞胎尽管长得一模一样，但在社交场合的行为却截然不同。 | 近义词: differently, dissimilarly, distinctly, uniquely | 反义词: similarly, identically, alike | 词性: 副词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + similar + -ly (adverb suffix)
