herby:/ˈhɜːrbi/ (US), /ˈhɜːbi/ (UK)| adj.|香草味的；草本的|Containing or tasting of herbs.|The soup had a wonderfully herby flavour. 这汤有一种很棒的草本香味。|The stew was richly herby and savory. 这道炖菜香草味浓郁，味道鲜美。|近义词：aromatic, fragrant, savory|反义词：bland, tasteless|词性：形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From herb + -y.  "Herb" comes from Old French "erbe" from Latin "herba" meaning "grass, green crops, herbage". The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of".
