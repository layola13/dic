muxy: /ˈmʌksi/ | adj. | 黏糊糊的；泥泞的 | Covered in or full of thick, sticky mud. | The ground was muxy after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，地面泥泞不堪。 |  The path was so muxy, my boots got stuck. 小路太泥泞了，我的靴子陷进去了。 | 近义词：muddy, slushy, miry | 反义词：dry, firm, solid | 词性：形容词

词源:  Likely a variant of "mucky," which is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "muck" (animal dung or decaying matter).
