beguiler:/bɪˈɡaɪlər/ /bɪˈɡɑɪlər/| n. |欺骗者，诱惑者|Someone who beguiles; a charmer who uses their skill to deceive others. |She was a beguiler, charming her way into people's lives and then disappearing with their money. 她是一个骗子，用她的魅力进入人们的生活，然后带着他们的钱消失了。|近义词：deceiver, charmer, trickster, enchanter, temptress|反义词：honest person, dupe|词性:noun

beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/ /bɪˈɡɑɪl/| vt. |欺骗，诱惑，使着迷|To charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way. To trick (someone) into doing something. To pass (time) pleasantly. |He beguiled them with his charm and false promises. 他用他的魅力和虚假的承诺欺骗了他们。

The beautiful scenery beguiled us during the long journey.  美丽的景色使我们在漫长的旅途中着迷。|近义词：deceive, charm, enchant, mislead, tempt, seduce|反义词：disenchant, repel, bore|词性:verb
