life-giving:/ˈlaɪfˌɡɪvɪŋ/
adjective
赋予生命的；给予生命的
Giving life, invigorating, or essential to life.
The life-giving properties of water are essential for all living things.  水赋予生命的特性对所有生物都至关重要。

The desert oasis provided life-giving water to the weary travelers.  沙漠绿洲为疲惫的旅行者提供了生命之水。

synonym: invigorating, vital, essential, restorative, reviving, animating
antonym:  deadly, lethal, destructive, debilitating
