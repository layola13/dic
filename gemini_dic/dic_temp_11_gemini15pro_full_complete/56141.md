documentary: /ˌdɒkjʊˈmentəri/ (UK) /ˌdɑːkjəˈmentəri/ (US) | adj. | 纪实的；文件的 | Consisting of or based on official documents. |  The evidence presented was purely documentary. 提出的证据完全是纪实的。| synonyms: factual, recorded, archival | antonyms: fictional, fabricated, invented | adjective
