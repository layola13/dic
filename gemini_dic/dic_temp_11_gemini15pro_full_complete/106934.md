lachrymate: /ˈlækrɪmeɪt/ (US), /ˈlækrɪmət/ (UK)| v. | 流泪，哭泣 | To secrete tears; weep. |  She lachrymated when she heard the sad news. 她听到这个悲伤的消息后流下了眼泪。

The movie's ending caused many in the audience to lachrymate.  这部电影的结局让许多观众流泪。

近义词: weep, cry, sob, tear up

反义词: laugh, rejoice

词性: verb

词源: From Latin *lacrimāre, present active infinitive of lacrimō (“I weep, cry, shed tears”), from lacrima (“tear”).
