overcherishing:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɛrɪʃɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度珍爱，过分爱护
The act of cherishing something or someone to an excessive degree, often to the point of hindering its growth or independence.
He overcherished his only child, preventing her from experiencing the necessary challenges of life.  他过分溺爱他唯一的孩子，阻止她经历生活中必要的挑战。
She overcherished the memory of her late husband, unable to move on with her life. 她过分珍爱已故丈夫的记忆，无法继续她的生活。
近义词: overprotecting, coddling, smothering
反义词: neglecting, ignoring
词性: v.
