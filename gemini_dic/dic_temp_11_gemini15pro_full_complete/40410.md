contramure:/ˌkɒntrəˈmjʊə(r)/ | n. | 外郭墙，外护墙 | An outer wall or rampart built in front of and protecting another wall, especially of a fortified place. |  The castle's contramure provided an additional layer of defense against siege weapons. 城堡的外郭墙提供了一层额外的防御，以抵御攻城武器。

The contramure was breached by the enemy's cannon fire.  敌人的炮火摧毁了外郭墙。 | 近义词: outer wall, rampart, bulwark, fortification | 反义词: inner wall | 词性: 名词
