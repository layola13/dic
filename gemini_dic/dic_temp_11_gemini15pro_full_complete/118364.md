meritocracies: /ˌmerɪtˈɑːkrəsiːz/ | n. |精英管理的社会; 英才教育制度; 贤能统治; 能者为领袖的制度 | a system in which people get success or power because of their abilities, not because of their money or social position | The company claims to be a meritocracy, but promotions seem to be based on favoritism.  这家公司声称是精英管理，但晋升似乎是基于偏袒。|  The ideal of a meritocracy is rarely achieved in practice. 精英管理的理想在实践中很少实现。 | synonyms:  system of merit,  talent pool  | antonyms:  aristocracy,  oligarchy | noun
