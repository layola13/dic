chuncha:/ˈtʃʌntʃə/| n.|春茶|Spring tea, referring to the first flush of tea leaves harvested in the spring.  It is considered to be of higher quality due to its delicate flavor and aroma.|Example: This year's chuncha has a unique floral fragrance. 今年的春茶有着独特的的花香。|近义词：first flush, spring harvest|反义词:autumn tea, winter tea|Noun
