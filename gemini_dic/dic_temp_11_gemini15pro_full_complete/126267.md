myeloma: /ˌmaɪəˈloʊmə/ (US) /ˌmaɪəˈləʊmə/ (UK)| n. |骨髓瘤|A malignant tumor of the bone marrow. It is a type of cancer that develops from plasma cells in the bone marrow.  These plasma cells are a type of white blood cell that produces antibodies to help fight infection. In myeloma, these cancerous plasma cells multiply uncontrollably and crowd out healthy blood cells in the bone marrow.|Myeloma is a serious type of blood cancer. 骨髓瘤是一种严重的血液癌症。|Multiple myeloma is often treated with chemotherapy. 多发性骨髓瘤通常用化疗治疗。|近义词:cancer, tumor, neoplasm|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease)|noun
