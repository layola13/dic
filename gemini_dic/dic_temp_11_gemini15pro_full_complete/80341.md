goldhorn:/ˈɡoʊldˌhɔːrn/| n. |金角山羊(或羚羊)|A real animal, specifically a species of goat-antelope ( *Naemorhedus caudatus*) found in East Asia, with horns that appear golden in certain light. |The zoo's newest exhibit featured a pair of beautiful goldhorn.  动物园最新的展览展出了一对美丽的金角山羊。|近义词：long-tailed goral|反义词: (No direct antonym) |noun
