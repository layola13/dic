intreatful:/ɪnˈtriːtfʊl/| adj.|恳求的，哀求的|Characterized by earnest entreaty; beseeching.|She cast an intreatful look at her father, hoping he would change his mind.|近义词：beseeching, imploring, supplicating|反义词：demanding, assertive|词性:形容词
词源: From in- + treat (verb) + -ful.  Ultimately from Latin *tractare*, frequentative of trahere "to draw, pull."
