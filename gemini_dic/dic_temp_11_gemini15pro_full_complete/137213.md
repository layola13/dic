nonsuppositional:/ˌnɒnsʌpəˈzɪʃənəl/ |adj.|非假设的，不基于假设的|Not based on or involving supposition; not hypothetical.  It describes something that is accepted as fact or presented as being objectively true, without relying on assumptions or hypothetical scenarios.|The lawyer presented nonsuppositional evidence to the jury.|Near synonyms: factual, objective, empirical, concrete, real|Antonyms: hypothetical, conjectural, assumed, supposed, presumptive
词性: 形容词
