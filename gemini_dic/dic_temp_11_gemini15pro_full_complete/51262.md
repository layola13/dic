desubsidizing:/diːˈsʌbsɪdaɪzɪŋ/
verb
取消补贴，停止资助
The act of removing or reducing a subsidy.  It refers to the process of gradually or completely eliminating financial assistance provided by a government or other entity.
The government is desubsidizing the farming industry. 政府正在取消对农业的补贴。
The desubsidizing of the coal industry led to mine closures. 取消对煤炭行业的补贴导致了矿山关闭。
Synonyms: defunding, withdrawing funding, phasing out subsidies
Antonyms: subsidizing, funding, supporting
v.
