词源：
"out-" (prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding") + "stay" (verb meaning "remain")

用法说明：
Outstaying is the present participle form of the verb "outstay". It is used to describe the act of staying longer than permitted or desired.  It can also be used as an adjective, though less commonly.
