interhaemal:/ˌɪntərˈhiːməl/
adjective
血管间的，血窦间的
Situated or occurring between blood sinuses or blood vessels.
The interhaemal spaces are filled with connective tissue.  血管间的空隙充满了结缔组织。
Interhaemal arches support the gills.  血管弓支撑着鳃。
近义词: intervascular
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered intravascular - within blood vessels)
词性: adjective
