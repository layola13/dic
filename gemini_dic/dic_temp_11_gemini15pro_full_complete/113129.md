lungs:/lʌŋz/
noun
肺
The main organs of respiration in humans and other air-breathing vertebrates, located in the chest cavity.
He took a deep breath, filling his lungs with fresh air. 他深深地吸了一口气，让新鲜空气充满了肺部。
synonyms: respiratory organs, breathing organs
antonyms: N/A
