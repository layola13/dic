adoptability:/əˌdɑːptəˈbɪləti/
n.|可收养性，可采纳性|The quality of being able to be adopted or accepted.|The agency assessed the adoptability of the children.  该机构评估了这些儿童的可收养性。|近义词：acceptability, suitability|反义词：unsuitability, unacceptability|词性:名词

Etymology:
From adopt + -able + -ity.  "adopt" comes from the Latin "adoptare," meaning "to choose for oneself."  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being," and "-ity" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
