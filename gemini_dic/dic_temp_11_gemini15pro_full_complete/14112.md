authentication:/ɔːˌθɛntɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 认证，鉴定；证实 | The process of verifying the identity of a user, device, or other entity.  |  例句：Two-factor authentication provides an extra layer of security.  双因素认证提供额外的安全层。

例句：The painting's authentication was confirmed by experts.  这幅画的真伪已被专家证实。| 近义词：verification, validation, confirmation | 反义词：denial, repudiation | noun
