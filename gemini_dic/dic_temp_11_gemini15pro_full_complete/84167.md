half-praising:/ˌhɑːfˈpreɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 半赞扬的，略带赞扬的 | Expressing mild or qualified praise; praising with reservations. | He gave a half-praising, half-critical review of the performance. 他对这场演出做出了半褒半贬的评价。 |  Example 2: Her half-praising smile made him unsure of her true feelings. 她略带赞扬的微笑让他不确定她的真实感受。 | Near Synonyms: lukewarm, faint praise, qualified approval, grudging admiration | Antonyms: wholeheartedly praising, effusive, enthusiastic, damning, criticizing | adjective
