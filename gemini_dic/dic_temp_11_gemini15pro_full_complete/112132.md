loob:/luːb/
noun
loop, noose, snare
A loop of rope.  一根绳圈。
The dog slipped its lead and ran off, its leash trailing in a loob. 狗挣脱了牵引绳跑掉了，皮带拖在地上形成一个环。
Synonyms: circle, ring, hoop, loop, noose
Antonyms: line, straight
词性: 名词
