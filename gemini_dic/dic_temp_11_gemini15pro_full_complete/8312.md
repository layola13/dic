anti-oxygenator:/ˈæntiˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtər/ |n.|抗氧化剂；除氧器|A substance that inhibits oxidation or removes oxygen.|The anti-oxygenator protected the metal from rusting. 抗氧化剂保护金属免受生锈。|The system uses an anti-oxygenator to remove dissolved oxygen from the water. 该系统使用除氧器去除水中的溶解氧。|synonyms: antioxidant, deoxygenator, oxygen scavenger|antonyms: oxidant, oxygenator|noun
