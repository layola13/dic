neuterer:/ˈnjuːtərər/ | n. | 阉割者；去势者 |  A person who castrates (especially an animal). | The farmer hired a neuterer to castrate his bull. 农民雇了一个阉割者来阉割他的公牛。|  Synonyms: castrator, gelder | Antonyms: breeder | noun


neuter:/ˈnjuːtər/ | v. | 阉割；去势 | To castrate or remove the sex organs of (an animal). | The vet neutered the cat to prevent unwanted kittens. 兽医阉割了这只猫，以防止不必要的幼猫出生。| Synonyms: castrate, geld, spay (for females) | Antonyms: breed | verb
