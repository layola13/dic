depthen:/ˈdɛpθən/ | v. | 加深，使加深 | To make something deeper or more profound. | The crisis deepened his understanding of human nature. 这场危机加深了他对人性的理解。 |  The artist deepened the shadows in the painting to create a more dramatic effect. 艺术家加深了画作中的阴影，以创造更戏剧性的效果。 | 近义词: intensify, enhance, strengthen, deepen | 反义词: lessen, reduce, weaken, shallow | 词性: 动词

词源: From "depth" + "-en" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become").  Derived from Old English "deop" (deep).
