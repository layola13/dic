misreflected:/ˌmɪsrɪˈflektɪd/
adjective
反射错误的；曲解的
incorrectly reflected, giving a false impression
The curved mirror misreflected her image, making her look much thinner than she was. 扭曲的镜子错误地反射了她的形象，使她看起来比实际瘦得多。
misrepresented, distorted
distorted, warped, misrepresented, falsified
accurately reflected, faithfully represented
adjective
