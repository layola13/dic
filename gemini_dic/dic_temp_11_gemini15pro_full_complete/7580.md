annotatory:/ˈænəteɪtɔːri/ | adj. |注释的，解释的 | Serving to annotate; explanatory.  | The annotatory notes in the textbook were very helpful. 教科书中的注释非常有帮助。 | 近义词: explanatory, interpretive, commentative | 反义词: uninformative, unexplained | 词性: 形容词

annotatory:/ˈænəteɪtɔːri/ | n. |注释，注解 | A note that annotates; an annotation. (This usage is less common) |  The editor compiled a comprehensive annotatory on the author's work. 编辑汇编了关于作者作品的全面注释。(较少用) | 近义词: annotation, commentary, explanation | 反义词:  (noun form less common, so antonyms are less relevant) | 词性: 名词


词源: From the Late Latin *annotatorius*, from annotatus, past participle of annotare "to note, mark," from ad- "to" + notare "to note."
