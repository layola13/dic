cascalho:/kɐʃˈkaʎu/ (Brazilian Portuguese)| n.| 碎石，砾石，沙砾 |Small stones, gravel, or pebbles, often used in construction or as a ground covering. | The road was covered in cascalho. 这条路铺满了碎石。|  The builder used cascalho to create a stable base for the patio.  建筑工人用碎石为露台建造一个稳定的基座。 | synonyms: gravel, pebbles, stones, rubble | antonyms:  sand, silt, clay | noun
