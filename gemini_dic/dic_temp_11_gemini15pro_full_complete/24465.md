britishes: /ˈbrɪtɪʃɪz/ | n. (Non-standard, rarely used) |英国人（复数）|  A non-standard and rarely used plural form of "British," sometimes encountered informally.  It attempts to create a plural for a noun that functions both as an adjective and a noun, but the standard plural is simply "British." |  "I met a couple of Britishes on my travels."
