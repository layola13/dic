camel-like:/ˈkæməlˌlaɪk/ | adj. |像骆驼的；耐劳的| Resembling a camel, especially in being able to endure hardship or go without water for a long time. |  The plant has a camel-like ability to survive in arid conditions. 这种植物具有像骆驼一样在干旱环境中生存的能力。|  The nomads, camel-like in their endurance, crossed the desert.  这些游牧民像骆驼一样耐力十足，穿越了沙漠。 |Synonyms: hardy, resilient, tough, enduring | Antonyms: fragile, weak, delicate | adjective
