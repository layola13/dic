imbrangle:/ɪmˈbræŋɡl/ | vt. | 使纠缠；使卷入纷争；使复杂化 | To involve in a complicated or confusing situation; to entangle. |  The politician imbrangled himself in a scandal involving campaign funds.  这位政治家卷入了一场与竞选资金有关的丑闻。

The legal jargon imbrangled the already complex issue further.  法律术语使已经很复杂的问题更加复杂化了。 | 近义词：entangle, embroil, complicate, confuse | 反义词：disentangle, extricate, simplify, clarify | 词性：动词

词源:  From Late Latin *imbrangari, perhaps from in- + branga "claw, branch."  源自晚期拉丁语 *imbrangari，可能来自 in- + branga “爪子，树枝”。
