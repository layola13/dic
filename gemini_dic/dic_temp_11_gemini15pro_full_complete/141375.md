operatorless:/ˈɒpəreɪtərlɪs/
adj.
无操作员的；无人操作的
lacking a human operator; automatic
The operatorless system efficiently managed the entire production line.  这个无人操作的系统有效地管理了整个生产线。
Operatorless vehicles are being tested in several cities.  无人驾驶车辆正在几个城市进行测试。
Synonyms: automated, automatic, robotic, unmanned, driverless
Antonyms: manual, operated, staffed
