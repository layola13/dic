baste:/beɪst/| v.|涂油；润湿；狠揍；粗缝；拷问|To moisten (meat) with melted fat or juices during cooking; to sew with long, loose stitches; to criticize harshly or attack verbally.|例句：The chef basted the turkey with butter. 厨师用黄油给火鸡涂油。

例句：She basted the hem of the dress before sewing it properly. 她在正式缝制之前，先粗缝了一下裙子的下摆。|近义词：moisten, brush, daub, sew, tack, criticize, lambaste|反义词:dry|词性:动词

basted:/ˈbeɪstɪd/ |adj.|涂过油的；粗缝的；挨打的|Covered with fat or juices during cooking; sewn with long, loose stitches; having been beaten or attacked verbally. |例句：The basted chicken smelled delicious. 涂过油的鸡闻起来很香。

例句：He felt basted after the harsh criticism from his boss.  被老板严厉批评后，他感觉受到了打击。|近义词：moistened, brushed, daubed, sewn, tacked|反义词:dry|词性:形容词
