ensnarement:/ɪnˈsneərmənt/
n.
陷阱，圈套；诱捕；束缚
the state of being caught in a trap or difficulty.
The rabbit escaped the ensnarement of the hunter's net. 兔子逃脱了猎人网的陷阱。
近义词: trap, entanglement, capture, snare
反义词: release, liberation, freedom
词性: 名词


ensnarement:/ɪnˈsneərmənt/
n.
诱惑，引诱
the act of tempting someone into sin or wrongdoing.
He warned of the ensnarement of false promises. 他警告人们要小心虚假承诺的诱惑。
近义词: temptation, seduction, allurement
反义词: resistance, repulsion
词性: 名词


词源:
From en- + snare + -ment.  "Snare" comes from Old English *snerian "to tie, bind," from Proto-Germanic *snarjan.
