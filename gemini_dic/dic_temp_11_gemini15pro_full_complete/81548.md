grayest:/ˈɡreɪɪst/| adj.|最灰色的|Superlative form of gray.  Having the most gray color; most lacking in color or brightness.|The gravestones were the grayest objects in the dreary cemetery.|近义词：darkest, dullest, bleakest|反义词:brightest, lightest, most colorful|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *greiest, *greyest, from Old English grǣgest, superlative form of grǣg (“gray”). Cognate with Old Norse grár (“gray”), Old High German grāo (“gray”), Latin rāvus (“grayish”), Ancient Greek γλαυκός (glaukós, “gray, bluish-green”).
