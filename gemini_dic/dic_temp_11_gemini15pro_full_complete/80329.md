goldens:/ˈɡoʊldənz/| n.|金色的东西；金鱼；金毛犬（复数）|Golden things; goldfish; Golden Retrievers (plural)|The goldens in the pond shimmered in the sunlight. 池塘里的金鱼在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：goldfish, retrievers (depending on context)|反义词:(No direct antonym, depends on context - perhaps "browns" for goldfish)|词性:名词

词源:
From "golden" + "-s" (plural suffix). "Golden" comes from Old English "golden," derived from "gold."
