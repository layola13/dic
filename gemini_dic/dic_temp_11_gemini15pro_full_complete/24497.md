broached:/broʊtʃt/| v. |提出，开始讨论；打开，开启（尤指酒桶等容器）；|To raise (a sensitive or difficult subject) for discussion.|He finally broached the subject of divorce. 他终于提出了离婚的话题。|近义词：raise, introduce, open, tap|反义词：avoid, evade, ignore|词性:动词

broached:/broʊtʃt/| v. |(用尖锐工具)刺穿，戳破| To pierce (something, such as a cask) in order to draw out liquid.|They broached a keg of beer. 他们打开了一桶啤酒。|近义词：pierce, puncture, tap|反义词：seal, close|词性:动词
