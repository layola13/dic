nutmeg:/ˈnʌtˌmeɡ/ (US), /ˈnʌtmeɡ/ (UK)| n.|肉豆蔻，肉豆蔻树|The hard, aromatic seed of a tropical tree, used as a spice.|例句：Grate some fresh nutmeg over the eggnog. 在蛋酒上磨一些新鲜的肉豆蔻。|近义词：mace (referring to the outer covering of the nutmeg seed)|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a spice. Could consider contrasting flavors like pepper or cinnamon depending on the context.)|词性: noun


nutmegs:/ˈnʌtˌmeɡz/ (US), /ˈnʌtmeɡz/ (UK)| n.|肉豆蔻（复数）|Plural form of nutmeg, referring to multiple nutmeg seeds.|例句：The recipe calls for two whole nutmegs.  这个食谱需要两个完整的肉豆蔻。|近义词：mace (plural: maces, but only when referring to the outer covering, not the seed itself)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, similar to the singular form.)|词性: noun
