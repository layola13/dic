contingential: /ˌkɒntɪˈdʒɛnʃəl/ | adj. |  偶然的；依情况而定的；可能的；附带的 | Depending on circumstances; accidental; incidental. |  The meeting is contingential on everyone's agreement.  会议是否举行取决于每个人的同意。 | Example: Payment is contingential on fulfillment of certain conditions. 付款取决于某些条件的履行。 | Synonyms: conditional, dependent, subject to, contingent | Antonyms: absolute, unconditional, certain, inevitable | adjective
