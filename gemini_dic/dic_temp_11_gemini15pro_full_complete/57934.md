drinkable:/ˈdrɪŋkəbl/| adj.|可饮用的，适于饮用的|Suitable for drinking; potable.|The water from the stream was clear and drinkable. 小溪里的水清澈可饮用。|近义词：potable, safe to drink|反义词：undrinkable, non-potable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From drink + -able.  "drink" comes from Old English "drincan," meaning "to swallow liquid."  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin meaning "capable of being."
