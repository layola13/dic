geldings:/ˈɡɛldɪŋz/| n. |阉割的马；骟马|A male horse that has been castrated. |例句：The farmer had several geldings in his stable. 农民的马厩里有几匹骟马。

例句：Geldings are generally more docile than stallions. 骟马通常比种马更温顺。|近义词：castrated horse, neutered horse|反义词:stallion, stud|词性:noun
