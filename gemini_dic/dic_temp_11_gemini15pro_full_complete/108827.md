leavenless:/ˈlɛvənlɪs/
adjective
无酵的，未发酵的
Not containing leaven (a substance, typically yeast, that causes fermentation and expansion of dough).
They ate leavenless bread during Passover.  他们在逾越节期间吃无酵饼。
The recipe calls for leavenless crackers.  这个食谱需要无酵饼干。
