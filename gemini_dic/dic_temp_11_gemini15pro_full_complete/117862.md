melodic:/məˈlɒdɪk/ (UK) /məˈlɑːdɪk/ (US)| adj.|旋律的；悦耳的|Relating to melody; tuneful; pleasant to listen to.|Her voice was soft and melodic. 她的声音柔和而悦耳。|The song has a simple but melodic tune. 这首歌的曲调简单却很悦耳。|近义词：tuneful, musical, harmonious, sweet-sounding|反义词：discordant, unmusical, harsh|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek "melos" (song) + "-ikos" (relating to).
