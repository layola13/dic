glamourises:/ˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪz/
glamorizes:/ˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使...更有魅力；美化；给...增添魅力
makes something seem more attractive, exciting, or interesting than it is
The magazine glamourises the life of a movie star. 这本杂志美化了电影明星的生活。
glamorizes, romanticizes, idealizes
deglamorizes, disparages, belittles
v.
