confabbing:/kənˈfæbɪŋ/ | n. | 闲聊；虚构；(尤指老年痴呆症患者)虚构记忆 | The act of chatting or having an informal discussion, especially about unimportant matters. In a medical context, it refers to the fabrication of memories, often seen in patients with dementia. |  "They were confabbing about their weekend plans." 他们正在闲聊他们的周末计划。| synonyms: chatting, gossiping, fabricating, inventing | antonyms: being silent, truth-telling | noun

confab:/ˈkɒnfæb/ /kənˈfæb/ | n. vi. | 闲谈；虚构 | An informal discussion or chat. As a verb, it means to engage in such a discussion.
