bronco:/ˈbrɒŋkəʊ/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːŋkoʊ/ (US)| n. |野马; (尤指)未驯服的马;  bronco(尤指北美西部) |A bronco is a wild or half-tamed horse of the western US.|He rode a bucking bronco. 他骑着一匹暴跳的野马。|近义词:mustang, wild horse|反义词:tame horse|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *bronco*, meaning "rough, coarse," ultimately from Late Latin *broccus*, "projecting, prominent."
