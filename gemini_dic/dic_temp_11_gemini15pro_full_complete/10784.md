aquarian:/əˈkwɛəriən/ | n.  |养鱼爱好者；水族馆工作人员 |A person who keeps or cares for an aquarium. |例句：He's a keen aquarian and has several tanks at home. 他是一位热衷于养鱼的人，家里有几个鱼缸。

|例句：The aquarian at the zoo is responsible for the health of the marine life. 动物园里的水族馆工作人员负责海洋生物的健康。
|近义词：fishkeeper, aquarist |反义词: None |词性: Noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aquārius* (“of water, pertaining to water”), from *aqua* (“water”).  The astrological sense comes from the constellation Aquarius, the "water bearer."
