basked:/bæskt/
verb
晒太阳，享受温暖或愉悦
To lie exposed to warmth and light, typically from the sun, for relaxation and enjoyment; to revel in something pleasant.
The cat basked in the sun. 猫在阳光下晒太阳。
She basked in the admiration of her fans. 她沉浸在粉丝们的赞赏中。
synonyms:  revel, luxuriate, wallow, savor
antonyms:  shiver, freeze, suffer
word type: verb
