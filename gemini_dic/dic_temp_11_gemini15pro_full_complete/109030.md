leg-pullers:/ˈleɡˌpʊlərz/
n.
开玩笑的人; 搞恶作剧的人
People who tease or play jokes on others, especially by telling them untrue stories.
"Don't believe a word they say, they're just a bunch of leg-pullers."  别相信他们说的任何一句话，他们只是一群爱开玩笑的人。
jokers, pranksters, teasers
serious people, earnest people
noun
