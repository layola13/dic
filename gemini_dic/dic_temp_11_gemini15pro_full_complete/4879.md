alight /əˈlaɪt/ | v. | 降落，下（车、马等）；偶然发现；容光焕发 |  To descend and settle, as from a bird from flight or a person from a vehicle; to come down; to land. Also, to find or come upon something unexpectedly; to happen upon.  | The butterfly alighted on a flower. 蝴蝶落在了一朵花上。 | descend, land, dismount | ascend, mount, board | verb

alight /əˈlaɪt/ | adj. | 点燃的；容光焕发的；明亮的 | Lighted up; burning.  | The room was alight with candles. 房间里点满了蜡烛，灯火通明。 | lit, illuminated, ablaze | dark, dim, extinguished | adjective
