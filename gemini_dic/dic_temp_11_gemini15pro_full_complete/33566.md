cil:/sɪl/ | n. |纤毛，睫毛|A short, hairlike vibrating structure found on some cells, particularly in the respiratory tract, that helps to move mucus and other substances.|The cilia in her lungs helped to clear out the dust. 她肺部的纤毛帮助清除了灰尘。|近义词:flagellum, hair|反义词: None|词性: Noun

词源: From Latin *cilium* "eyelid, eyelash," then in post-classical Latin also "hairlike appendage of certain microorganisms."
