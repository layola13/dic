leisureless:/ˈliːʒərləs/| adj.|不停歇的，没有闲暇的|Without leisure; incessantly occupied; busy.|She led a leisureless life, always working and never relaxing. 她过着没有闲暇的生活，总是工作，从不放松。| He's been leisureless since starting his new business.  自从开始了他的新生意，他就一直没有闲暇时间。|近义词：busy, occupied, industrious, tireless|反义词: leisurely, relaxed, idle|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From leisure + -less.  "Leisure" comes from Old French "leisir" meaning "permission, opportunity," ultimately from Latin "licēre" meaning "to be permitted."  The suffix "-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without."
