eudaemonist:/ˌjuːdɪˈmiːnɪst/ | n. | 快乐主义者；幸福主义者 | A person who believes that the highest good is happiness and that it should be the aim of all human activity.  A follower of eudaemonism. |  He lived his life as a eudaemonist, always seeking joy and fulfillment. 他以快乐主义者的身份度过一生，始终追求快乐和满足。| Example:  She considered herself a eudaemonist, prioritizing well-being above all else. 她认为自己是一个快乐主义者，将幸福置于一切之上。 | Synonyms: hedonist, epicurean | Antonyms: ascetic, stoic | Noun
