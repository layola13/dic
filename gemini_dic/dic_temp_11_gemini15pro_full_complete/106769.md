labaris:/ləˈbɑːrɪs/ | n. | 拉巴里斯双刃斧, 双面斧 | A double-edged ax, often associated with ancient Minoan culture and religious rituals. | Example: The archaeologist unearthed a labaris, suggesting a Minoan presence at the site. 考古学家出土了一把拉巴里斯双刃斧，表明该遗址存在米诺斯文明的痕迹。

Example: The labaris was a symbol of power and authority in Minoan society. 拉巴里斯双刃斧是米诺斯社会权力和权威的象征。 | Near synonyms: double axe, two-headed axe | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
