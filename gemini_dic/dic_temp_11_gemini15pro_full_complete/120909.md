miscarriage:/ˈmɪskærɪdʒ/| n. |流产；失败；误送|The spontaneous or induced expulsion of a fetus from the womb before it is viable.|She suffered a miscarriage in her first trimester. 她怀孕三个月时流产了。|Synonyms: abortion (induced), spontaneous abortion, stillbirth (late-term)|Antonyms: live birth, successful pregnancy|noun


miscarriage:/ˈmɪskærɪdʒ/| n. |失败；失误|Failure to achieve the intended purpose.|The project was a complete miscarriage. 这个项目彻底失败了。|Synonyms: failure, fiasco, debacle|Antonyms: success, triumph, achievement|noun
