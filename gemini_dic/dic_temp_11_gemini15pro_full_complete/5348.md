alluding:/əˈluːdɪŋ/
verb
暗示, 间接提到
suggesting or calling attention to indirectly; hinting at
He was alluding to the recent scandal. 他在暗示最近的丑闻。
Synonyms: hinting, suggesting, implying, intimating, referencing
Antonyms: stating, declaring, specifying, explaining, elaborating
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin alludere "to play with, jest, mock," from ad- "to" + ludere "to play."  The sense evolution is from "play with words" to "hint at."
