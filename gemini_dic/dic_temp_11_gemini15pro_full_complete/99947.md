internuncial:/ˌɪntərˈnʌnʃəl/
adjective
中间神经元的；介于神经元之间的
Connecting two or more neurons; specifically applied to neurons in the central nervous system that connect sensory and motor neurons.
The internuncial neurons relay signals between afferent and efferent neurons.  中间神经元在传入神经元和传出神经元之间传递信号。
Internuncial pathways in the spinal cord allow for rapid reflexes.  脊髓中的中间神经通路可以实现快速反射。
近义词:connecting, intermediary, relay
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "terminal" or "end")
词性: adjective
