crayonists:/ˈkreɪənɪsts/
n.
蜡笔画家；用蜡笔作画的人
Someone who uses crayons, especially an artist.
The crayonists' exhibition showcased a variety of colorful landscapes. 蜡笔画家们的展览展示了各种色彩丰富的风景。
The young crayonists filled the paper with vibrant hues.  年轻的蜡笔画家们用鲜艳的色彩填满了纸张。
近义词：crayon artist, artist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


crayon:/ˈkreɪən/
n.
蜡笔；彩色铅笔
A stick of colored wax or chalk, used for drawing.
Children love to draw with crayons. 孩子们喜欢用蜡笔画画。
She bought a box of 64 crayons. 她买了一盒64色的蜡笔。
近义词：colored pencil, wax crayon
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

-ist:/ɪst/
suffix
表示"...人"，"...主义者"
A suffix that forms nouns indicating a person who practices or is concerned with something, or adheres to a certain doctrine or system.
Examples: artist, pianist, socialist. 例如：艺术家，钢琴家，社会主义者。
近义词： N/A
反义词: N/A
词性: suffix

词源：
crayon (n.) - from French "crayon," originally "pencil, chalk," from "craie" (chalk), from Latin "creta" (Cretan earth, chalk).
-ist (suffix) - from Greek "-istes," a suffix forming agent nouns.
