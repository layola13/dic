banshee:/ˈbænʃiː/ (US), /ˈbænʃiː/ (UK)| n.|女妖，报丧女妖|A female spirit in Irish folklore whose wailing warns of an impending death in a family.|The old woman swore she heard a banshee wailing in the night. 老妇人发誓她晚上听到了女妖的哀号。|Synonyms: spirit, wraith, phantom, ghost|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "living being," "mortal")|noun

banshees:/ˈbænʃiːz/ (US), /ˈbænʃiːz/ (UK)| n.|女妖们，报丧女妖们|Plural form of banshee, referring to multiple female spirits in Irish folklore whose wailing warns of impending deaths.|Legend has it that banshees haunt the old castle. 传说女妖们经常出没在那座古老的城堡里。|Synonyms: spirits, wraiths, phantoms, ghosts|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "living beings," "mortals")|noun
