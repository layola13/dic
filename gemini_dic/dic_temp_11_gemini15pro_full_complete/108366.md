lavishnesses:/ˈlævɪʃnəsɪz/
n.
奢侈，奢华（复数）
The lavishnesses of the royal wedding were breathtaking.  皇家婚礼的奢华令人叹为观止。
He couldn't hide his lavishnesses on clothing. 他无法掩饰在穿着上的奢靡。
近义词: extravagances, luxuries
反义词: austerities, frugalities
词性: 名词

词源: From lavish (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Lavish itself comes from Old French lavasse ("deluge, downpour"), suggesting an overflowing abundance.
