balneatory: /ˌbælnɪˈeɪtəri/ /ˌbɑːlnɪˈeɪtəri/
adjective
洗浴的；温泉疗养的
Relating to baths or bathing; suitable for bathing.
The ancient Romans were famous for their balneatory practices. 古罗马人以其洗浴习俗而闻名。
The hotel boasted a balneatory spa with various mineral pools. 这家酒店拥有一个带有各种矿物池的温泉浴场。
近义词: therapeutic, medicinal, curative
反义词: none
词性: adjective

balneatory: /ˌbælnɪˈeɪtəri/ /ˌbɑːlnɪˈeɪtəri/
noun
浴室；浴场
A bath or bathing place.
The balneatory was a popular social gathering place in ancient Rome. 在古罗马，浴场是一个受欢迎的社交场所。
The town's balneatory offered a variety of treatments and massages.  该镇的浴场提供各种护理和按摩服务。
近义词: bathhouse, spa, baths
反义词: none
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *balneātōrius*, from *balneāre* "to bathe," from *balneum* "bath," from Greek *balaneion* "bath, bathhouse."
