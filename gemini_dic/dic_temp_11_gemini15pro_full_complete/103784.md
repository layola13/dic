journeyman:/ˈdʒɜːrniːmən/ | n. | 熟工；雇工；有经验的工人；|A skilled worker who has successfully completed an apprenticeship but is not yet a master.  A worker who is competent at a particular trade or craft. | He worked as a journeyman carpenter for several years before opening his own shop. 他做了几年的熟练木匠才开了自己的店。|Synonyms: artisan, craftsman, tradesman, skilled worker, qualified worker | Antonyms: apprentice, novice, beginner, amateur | noun

journeyman:/ˈdʒɜːrniːmən/ | adj. | 熟练的；平庸的；缺乏独创性的 |  Competent but not especially brilliant or innovative; routine, mediocre. | He gave a journeyman performance, nothing particularly inspiring. 他的表演很平庸，没有什么特别鼓舞人心的。| Synonyms: average, ordinary, mediocre, run-of-the-mill | Antonyms: exceptional, outstanding, brilliant, innovative | adjective
