jubilee:/ˈdʒuːbɪliː/ /ˈdʒuːbəliː/
n.
禧年，周年纪念，欢乐的节日
a time of celebration or rejoicing, especially one marking a special anniversary.
The Queen's Golden Jubilee was celebrated with street parties and parades. 女王的五十周年庆典以街头派对和游行来庆祝。

The town celebrated its bicentennial jubilee with a week-long festival.  这个镇以一周的节日庆祝其二百周年纪念。

近义词：celebration, anniversary, festival, feast
反义词：mourning, grief
