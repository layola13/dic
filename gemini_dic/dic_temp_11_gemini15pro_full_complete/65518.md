euryalida:/ˌjʊəriˈælɪdə/ | n. | 蛇尾纲 | An order of brittle stars (class Ophiuroidea) characterized by a large, flat disc and often branched arms.  These brittle stars are also known as basket stars. |  Euryalida are found in deep ocean habitats. 蛇尾纲生物发现于深海栖息地。|  Euryalida often use their branched arms to capture plankton. 蛇尾纲生物经常用它们的分支臂来捕捉浮游生物。 | synonyms: basket stars | antonyms: N/A | noun
