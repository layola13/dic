autotrophic:/ˌɔːtəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 自养的 | An organism that is able to form nutritional organic substances from simple inorganic substances such as carbon dioxide. | Example 1: Plants are autotrophic organisms, producing their own food through photosynthesis. 植物是自养生物，通过光合作用产生自己的食物。

Example 2: Autotrophic bacteria play a crucial role in the nitrogen cycle. 自养细菌在氮循环中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: self-nourishing, self-feeding | 反义词: heterotrophic | 词性: adjective
