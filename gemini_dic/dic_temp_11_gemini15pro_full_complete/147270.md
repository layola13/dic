overmined:/ˌoʊvərˈmaɪnd/ | adj. | 过度开采的 | Excessively mined; mined to the point of depletion or exhaustion. | The overmined coalfields are now barren landscapes.  过度开采的煤田现在变成了荒芜的景象。 | The once-lush forests were overmined for timber, leaving behind a desolate wasteland.  曾经茂密的森林因木材过度开采而变成荒凉的荒地。 | 近义词: depleted, exhausted, overexploited | 反义词: unexploited, preserved, conserved | 词性: 形容词

overmined: /ˌoʊvərˈmaɪnd/ | v. (past participle) | 过度开采 |  Past participle of "overmine," meaning to mine excessively. | The area had been overmined for decades, leaving it scarred and unproductive.  这个地区几十年来一直被过度开采，留下了伤痕累累、毫无生产力的景象。| The mountain was overmined for its rare minerals, causing significant environmental damage. 这座山因其稀有矿物而被过度开采，造成了严重的破坏。 | 近义词: depleted, exhausted, overexploited | 反义词: unexploited, preserved, conserved | 词性: 动词(过去分词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "mine" (verb meaning "to extract minerals"). The prefix "over-" intensifies the action of "mine," indicating excessive or destructive extraction.
