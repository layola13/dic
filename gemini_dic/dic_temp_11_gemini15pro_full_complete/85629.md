haum:/hɔːm/ | n. | home; dwelling place | A traditional Inuit snow house. |  The family spent the winter in their warm and comfortable haum. | 近义词: igloo, snow house, dwelling, abode | 反义词: outdoors | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Inuktitut  ᐃᒡᓗ (iglu, “house”), from ᐃᒡᓗᒃ (igluk, “snow house”). Haum is a variant used in some dialects.
