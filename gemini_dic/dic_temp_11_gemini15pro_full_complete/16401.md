barcarolle:/ˌbɑːrkəˈrəʊl/ /ˌbɑːrkəˈroʊl/| n.|船歌；威尼斯民歌|A typically Venetian folk song traditionally sung by gondoliers, or a piece of music imitating this style, usually in 6/8 time and a swaying rhythm.|
例句：The pianist played a beautiful barcarolle. 钢琴家演奏了一首优美的船歌。
例句：The gentle rocking of the gondola inspired the composer to write a barcarolle. 平缓摇曳的贡多拉激发了作曲家创作一首船歌的灵感。

|近义词：gondola song, boat song|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps dirge or lament as contrasting musical forms)|Noun
