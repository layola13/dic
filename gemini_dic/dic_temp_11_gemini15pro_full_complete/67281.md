exsanguious:/ɛkˈsæŋɡwɪəs/
adjective
血色苍白的；毫无血色的；贫血的
Pale, bloodless; drained of blood.
The patient appeared exsanguious after the significant blood loss.  病人大量失血后显得脸色苍白。
The long illness left him looking exsanguious and weak.  长期的疾病使他看起来面色苍白，身体虚弱。

近义词: pale, pallid, ashen, wan, bloodless, anemic
反义词: ruddy, flushed, sanguine

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin exsanguis (“bloodless”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + sanguis (“blood”).
