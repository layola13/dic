noncustomary:/nɒnˈkʌstəməri/ | adj. | 非惯例的，非常规的 | Not according to the usual practice; unusual or uncommon. | This noncustomary procedure caused some confusion among the staff.  这个非常规的程序在员工中引起了一些困惑。 |  The CEO made the noncustomary decision to work alongside the employees. 首席执行官做出了一个不寻常的决定，与员工一起工作。 | 近义词: unusual, uncommon, atypical, irregular, abnormal | 反义词: customary, usual, common, typical, normal | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
customary (adjective): from "custom" (meaning a traditional and widely accepted way of behaving or doing something) + "-ary" (suffix forming adjectives)
