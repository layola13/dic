flushers:/ˈflʌʃərz/
noun
冲洗器；脸红的人
People who flush (cause redness in the face, usually from embarrassment or exertion).  It can also refer to devices that flush something, like toilet flushers.
The embarrassment made the flushers in the room even more uncomfortable.  尴尬使得房间里脸红的人更加不舒服。
The plumbers installed new flushers in all the toilets. 水管工在所有的厕所里都安装了新的冲水器。
synonyms: reddeners, blushers (for people); flushing devices, rinsing mechanisms (for devices)
antonyms: palers (for people);
n.
