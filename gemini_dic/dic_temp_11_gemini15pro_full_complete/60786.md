electrometallurgical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌmɛtəlˈɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
电冶金的
Relating to the use of electricity in metallurgy, typically for the extraction or refining of metals.
Electrometallurgical processes are crucial for the production of aluminum. 电冶金工艺对铝的生产至关重要。
The development of new electrometallurgical techniques has revolutionized the industry. 新型电冶金技术的发展彻底改变了这个行业。
近义词:electrolytic
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional metallurgical")
词性:adjective
