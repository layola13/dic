arboreal:/ɑːrˈbɔːriəl/
adjective
树栖的；树木的
Living in trees; relating to trees.
The monkey is an arboreal creature, spending most of its life in the branches. 猴子是一种树栖动物，一生中的大部分时间都在树枝上度过。

Squirrels are arboreal animals, adept at climbing and leaping among trees. 松鼠是树栖动物，擅长在树木间攀爬和跳跃。

arbrous, dendritic
terrestrial, aquatic
