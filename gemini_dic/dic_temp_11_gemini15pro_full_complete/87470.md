hesitate:/ˈhezɪteɪt/| v. |犹豫，迟疑|To pause before saying or doing something, especially because you are nervous or not certain.|She hesitated before answering the question. 她回答问题前犹豫了一下。|He hesitated for a moment, then knocked on the door. 他犹豫了一会儿，然后敲了敲门。|近义词：pause, waver, falter|反义词：decide, resolve, proceed|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Latin *haesitare*, frequentative of haerere "to stick, adhere, hold fast."
