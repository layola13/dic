nonfluently:/nɑːnˈfluːəntli/ /nɒnˈfluːəntli/| adv. |不流利地|In a manner that is not smooth or flowing, especially in speech or writing; with hesitation or difficulty.| She spoke English nonfluently, often pausing to search for the right words. 她英语说得不流利，经常停下来寻找合适的词语。|He read the complex text nonfluently, stumbling over several unfamiliar words. 他磕磕绊绊地读着复杂的文本，被几个不熟悉的单词绊倒了。|近义词：haltingly, hesitantly, awkwardly|反义词: fluently, smoothly, effortlessly|词性:副词
