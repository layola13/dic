liquefy:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪ/ /ˈlɪkwəˌfaɪ/| n. |液化|The process of changing something into a liquid state.|The liquefaction of natural gas allows for easier transport. 天然气的液化使其更容易运输。|synonyms: melting, dissolving, thawing |antonyms: solidification, freezing, hardening |noun
