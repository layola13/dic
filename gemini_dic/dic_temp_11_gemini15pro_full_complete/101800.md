irrigatory:/ˈɪrɪɡətɔːri/
adjective
灌溉的; 用于灌溉的
Relating to or used for irrigation.
The irrigatory canals bring water to the crops. 灌溉渠将水输送到农作物。
The farmer installed an irrigatory system in his fields. 农民在他的田里安装了灌溉系统。
近义词:  irrigation, watering
反义词: draining
词性: adjective

irrigatory:/ˈɪrɪɡətɔːri/
noun
灌溉设备; 灌溉系统
A device or system for irrigation.
The irrigatory was damaged in the storm. 灌溉设备在暴风雨中受损了。
The new irrigatory helped increase crop yields. 新的灌溉系统帮助提高了作物产量。
近义词: irrigation system, watering system
反义词: drainage system
词性: noun
