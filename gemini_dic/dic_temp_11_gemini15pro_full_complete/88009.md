high-spiritedly:/ˌhaɪˈspɪrɪtɪdli/
adv.
兴高采烈地，精神饱满地
In a lively and enthusiastic manner, full of energy and good spirits.
The children played high-spiritedly in the park. 孩子们在公园里兴高采烈地玩耍。
They celebrated their victory high-spiritedly. 他们兴高采烈地庆祝他们的胜利。
近义词: enthusiastically, cheerfully, energetically, exuberantly
反义词: dejectedly, listlessly, apathetically
词性: 副词 (adverb)
词源: high-spirited (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix)  "High-spirited" combines "high" (meaning elevated or intense) with "spirited" (meaning having or showing vigor or courage).  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective into an adverb.
