fabliau: /ˈfæbliˌoʊ/ (US), /ˈfæbliəʊ/ (UK)
n.
滑稽故事，粗俗故事
A short, metrical tale, usually comic and often bawdy, that flourished in France in the 12th and 13th centuries.
The fabliau often featured cuckolded husbands, clever wives, and trickster figures. 这个滑稽故事通常以戴绿帽子的丈夫、聪明的妻子和骗子形象为特色。
The Canterbury Tales includes several fabliaux. 《坎特伯雷故事集》包含几个滑稽故事。
近义词: tale, story, anecdote, yarn
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'tragedy' in terms of tone)
词性: noun
