outsteals:/ˌaʊtˈstiːlz/
|v.|偷垒次数超过; 偷垒胜过|He outsteals everyone on the team. 他在队里偷垒次数超过任何人。|例句：The shortstop outsteals all other players in the league.  这个游击手的偷垒次数超过联盟中所有其他球员。

例句：Despite his smaller size, he consistently outsteals the bigger players.  尽管他个子较小，但他经常在偷垒方面胜过那些更大的球员。|近义词：outperforms, surpasses, excels|反义词: underperforms, lags behind|词性：动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "steal" (verb referring to stealing a base in baseball).  由表示“超过、胜过”的前缀 "out-" 和表示棒球中“偷垒”的动词 "steal" 组成。
