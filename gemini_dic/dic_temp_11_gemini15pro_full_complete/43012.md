cowbell:/ˈkaʊˌbɛl/| n.|牛铃；牛颈铃|A bell, typically a large one, hung around the neck of a cow so that the animal can be easily located.|The rhythmic clang of the cowbell added a rustic touch to the music. 牛铃有节奏的叮当声给音乐增添了乡村气息。|Synonyms: bell, chime|Antonyms: silence|noun
