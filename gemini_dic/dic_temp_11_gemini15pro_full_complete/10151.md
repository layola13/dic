apologizes:/əˈpɒlədʒaɪzɪz/
verb
道歉
expresses regret for something that one has done wrong.
She apologizes for the mistake. 她为这个错误道歉。
He apologizes for being late. 他为迟到道歉。
synonyms: says sorry, expresses regret, makes amends, asks for forgiveness
antonyms: blames, accuses, justifies, defends
词性: 动词(Third-person singular present tense)
