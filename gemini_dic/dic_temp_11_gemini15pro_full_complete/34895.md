clipt:/klɪpt/ | v. | 剪辑，修剪 | to shorten something, especially by cutting | I clipt the article from the newspaper. 我从报纸上剪下了这篇文章。|  trimmed, cut, snipped | extend, lengthen | verb
clipt:/klɪpt/ | adj. |  修剪过的，简洁的 | cut short or clipped | He had a clipt beard. 他留着修剪过的胡须。| trimmed, neat, concise | overgrown, lengthy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English clippen, from Old English clyppan (“to embrace, clasp, hug”), from Proto-Germanic *klippaną (“to press, pinch, grip”).  Related to clip (n.).  The meaning shifted from "embrace" to "cut short" likely through the intermediate sense of "pinch off."
