noneffusively:/ˌnɒnɪˈfjuːsɪvli/
adv.
不热情地，冷淡地
in a way that is not effusive; in a restrained or reserved manner.
She thanked him noneffusively for his help. 她冷淡地感谢了他的帮助。
近义词:reservedly, restrainedly, undemonstratively, quietly
反义词:effusively, enthusiastically, warmly
词性:副词

noneffusive:/ˌnɒnɪˈfjuːsɪv/
adj.
不热情的，冷淡的
not effusive; restrained or reserved in expression.
His noneffusive response made her feel unwelcome.  他不热情的回应让她感到不受欢迎。
近义词:reserved, restrained, undemonstrative, quiet
反义词:effusive, enthusiastic, warm
词性:形容词


词源:
noneffusive 由否定前缀 non- 和 effusive 构成。
effusive 源自拉丁语 effusus，过去分词 effundere，意为“倒出，流出”。
