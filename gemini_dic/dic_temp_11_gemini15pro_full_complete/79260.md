glassite:/ˈɡlæsˌaɪt/| n.|格拉斯派信徒；格拉斯主义者|A member of a strict Scottish Protestant sect founded by John Glas (1695–1773), which rejected national churches and stressed the independence of individual congregations.|John Glas's followers, known as Glassites, faced persecution for their beliefs.  约翰·格拉斯的追随者，被称为格拉斯派信徒，因为他们的信仰而面临迫害。|The Glassites practiced communal living and strict adherence to biblical principles. 格拉斯派信徒实行集体生活并严格遵守圣经原则。|近义词:Sanctificationist, Independent|反义词:None|词性:Noun
