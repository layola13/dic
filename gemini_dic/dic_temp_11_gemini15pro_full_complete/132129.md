nonculminating:/ˌnɒnˈkʌlmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非最终的；非高潮的；不达顶点的
Not reaching a climax or peak; not culminating.
The preliminary rounds of the competition are nonculminating, serving only to narrow down the field of participants.  预赛并非最终的，只是为了缩小参赛者的范围。

The senator's speech was a rambling, nonculminating discourse that left the audience confused. 参议员的演讲冗长而没有高潮，让听众感到困惑。

近义词:anticlimactic, inconclusive, non-decisive, preparatory
反义词:culminating, climactic, decisive, conclusive
词性:adjective
