imparidigitate:/ɪmˌpærɪˈdɪdʒɪteɪt/
adjective
奇数羽状的;单数羽状的
Having an odd number of digits (fingers or toes) on a hand or foot, typically with the central digit being the longest.
The cassowary is an imparidigitate bird, having three toes on each foot.  食火鸡是一种奇数羽状的鸟类，每只脚上有三个脚趾。
Synonym: perissodactyl
Antonym: artiodactyl
