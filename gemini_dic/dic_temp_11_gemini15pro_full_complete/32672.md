chivalrousness:/ˈʃɪvəlrəsnəs/
n.
骑士精神；侠义；殷勤
The quality of being chivalrous, characterized by nobility, honor, courtesy, and valor, especially toward women.
He demonstrated his chivalrousness by opening the door for her and offering her his coat. 他为她开门并把外套给她，展现了他的骑士精神。

近义词:gallantry, courtesy, nobility, courtliness
反义词:rudeness, discourtesy, boorishness
词性:noun

chivalry: /ˈʃɪvəlri/
n.
骑士制度，骑士精神；彬彬有礼
The medieval system or institution of knighthood. The combination of qualities expected of an ideal knight, especially courage, honor, courtesy, justice, and a readiness to help the weak.
The knight displayed chivalry in the tournament. 骑士在比赛中展现了骑士精神。

近义词:gallantry, valor, courtesy
反义词:cowardice, rudeness
词性:noun

chivalrous: /ˈʃɪvəlrəs/
adj.
骑士的；勇敢的；侠义的；对女人彬彬有礼的
Marked by honor, courtesy, and valor; knightly.
His chivalrous behavior impressed everyone. 他侠义的行为给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。

近义词:gallant, courteous, noble, courtly
反义词:rude, discourteous, boorish
词性:adjective
