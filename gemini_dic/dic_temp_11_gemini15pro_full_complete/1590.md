achroous:/ˈeɪkrʊəs/| adj.|无色的，透明的|Lacking color; colorless.  Derived from Greek *ἀ- (a-, "not") + *χρῶμα (chrōma, "color").|The achroous liquid was difficult to see in the glass beaker.  这种无色液体在玻璃烧杯中很难看到.|Synonyms: colorless, transparent, unpigmented|Antonyms: colored, pigmented, chromatic|adjective
