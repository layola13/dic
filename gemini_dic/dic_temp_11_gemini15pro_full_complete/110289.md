ligatured:/ˈlɪɡətʃərd/ | adj. |  连字的；绑扎的 | Joined or tied together, especially letters; combined into a ligature.  |  The manuscript was written in beautiful calligraphy, with many ligatured letters.  这份手稿是用漂亮的书法写成的，有很多连字字母。 |  joined, bound, tied, connected | separate, detached, unbound | adjective

ligature:/ˈlɪɡətʃər/ | n. | 结扎线；连字；绑带 |  Something used to bind or tie, such as a thread, cord, or wire; a character consisting of two or more letters united, as æ, œ. | The surgeon used a ligature to tie off the bleeding artery.  外科医生用结扎线扎住出血的动脉。 |  bond, tie, connection, ligatured (adj.) | separation, detachment | noun
