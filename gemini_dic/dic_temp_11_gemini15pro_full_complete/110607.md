limonin:/ˈlɪmənɪn/ | n. |  柠檬苦素 | A bitter, white, crystalline substance found in citrus fruit, especially in the seeds, and also formed in the tissues of some plants when they are damaged. It is a triterpenoid lactone. |  Limonin is responsible for the delayed bitterness in some citrus juices. 柠檬苦素是造成一些柑橘类果汁延迟苦味的原因。| The researchers are investigating ways to reduce limonin levels in citrus fruits. 研究人员正在研究降低柑橘类水果中柠檬苦素含量的方法。| 近义词: nomilin (another citrus limonoid) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym due to its specific chemical nature. Sweetness could be considered a contrasting sensory quality.) | 词性: noun
