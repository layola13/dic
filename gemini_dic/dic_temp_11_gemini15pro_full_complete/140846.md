on-the-bum:/ɒn ðə bʌm/ (British English) /ɑːn ðə bʌm/ (American English)
adjective|流浪的，乞讨的；失业的；闲逛的|Unemployed and living by begging or casual earnings; wandering aimlessly.|He's been on the bum for months, ever since he lost his job. 他失业好几个月了，自从他丢了工作就一直流浪街头。|Synonyms: down and out, destitute, broke, penniless|Antonyms: employed, affluent, prosperous|adjective

on-the-bum:/ɒn ðə bʌm/ (British English) /ɑːn ðə bʌm/ (American English)
adverb|流浪地，乞讨地；失业地；闲逛地|In a state of unemployment, poverty, and wandering.|He travelled on the bum around Europe for a year. 他以流浪的方式在欧洲游荡了一年。|Synonyms: aimlessly, idly, haphazardly|Antonyms: purposefully, diligently, systematically|adverb
