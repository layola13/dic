entailment:/ɪnˈteɪlmənt/ (US), /ɪnˈteɪlmənt/ (UK)| n.| 蕴涵；必需的结果；继承 |Something that is entailed is a necessary part or consequence of it.  |Building a new factory will entail taking on extra staff.  建设新工厂将需要额外雇用员工。|近义词：consequence, result, implication, necessity|反义词：option, choice|词性:名词

entailments:/ɪnˈteɪlmənts/ (US), /ɪnˈteɪlmənts/ (UK)| n.| 蕴涵（entailment 的复数形式）|The plural form of entailment, referring to multiple necessary parts or consequences. |The project has several entailments, including hiring new staff and purchasing new equipment.  这个项目有几个必需的结果，包括雇用新员工和购买新设备。|近义词：consequences, results, implications, necessities|反义词：options, choices|词性:名词
