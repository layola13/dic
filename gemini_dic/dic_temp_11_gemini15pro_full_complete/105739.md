kipped:/kɪpt/
verb
睡（尤指在不舒服的地方）,逃学
slept, especially in an uncomfortable place;  (British informal) played truant from school.
例句：We kipped on the floor for a few hours. 我们在地板上睡了几个小时。

例句：He kipped school to go to the football match. 他逃学去看足球比赛了。
近义词：slept, dozed, bunked (off), skipped
反义词：stayed awake, attended

kipped:/kɪpt/
verb
(past tense and past participle of kip)
睡过(kip的过去式和过去分词)
The past tense and past participle form of the verb "kip".
例句：I kipped at my friend's house last night. 我昨晚在我朋友家睡了。

例句：We had kipped in a tent the previous weekend.  我们上周末在帐篷里睡过。
近义词：slept
反义词：stayed awake

词源：kip来源于斯堪的纳维亚语，最初指妓院或廉价旅馆，后来引申为在这些地方睡觉，最终演变为一般的“睡觉”的意思。在英国俚语中，kip还可以指“逃学”。
