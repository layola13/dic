jockeyism: /ˈdʒɒkiɪzəm/ /ˈdʒɑːkiɪzəm/
n.
骑师行为；骑师职业；骑马技术；(尤指为获取优势的)不择手段，耍手腕，玩弄技巧
The art or practice of riding horses in races, especially as a professional jockey.  Also: dishonest or unfair behaviour, especially in order to gain an advantage.
The investigation revealed widespread jockeyism in the awarding of government contracts.  这项调查揭露了政府合同授予过程中普遍存在的不正当竞争行为。
synonyms: trickery, maneuvering, manipulation, chicanery, sharp practice, gamesmanship
antonyms: fairness, honesty, sportsmanship
noun
