commutative:/kəˈmjuːtətɪv/| adj.|交换的，可交换的|Relating to or involving substitution or exchange of one thing for another; allowing or involving the performance of a mathematical operation in any order without affecting the result.|Addition is commutative: 5 + 2 = 2 + 5. 加法是可交换的：5 + 2 = 2 + 5.|Synonyms: interchangeable, permutable, invertible|Antonyms: noncommutative, non-interchangeable|adjective
