laissez-faire:/ˌleseɪˈfɛər (US), ˌlɛseɪˈfɛː (UK)| n. adj.|放任主义；自由放任|A policy or attitude of letting things take their own course without interfering.|The government adopted a laissez-faire approach to the economy. 政府对经济采取了放任主义的态度。|synonyms: nonintervention, free enterprise, deregulation|antonyms: interventionism, regulation, control|noun
laissez-faire:/ˌleseɪˈfɛər (US), ˌlɛseɪˈfɛː (UK)| adj.|自由放任的|Characterized by a policy or attitude of letting things take their own course without interfering.|A laissez-faire attitude towards parenting. 对育儿采取放任自流的态度。|synonyms: noninterventional, hands-off|antonyms: interventionist, controlling|adjective
