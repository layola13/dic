antitheistic:/ˌæntiθiˈɪstɪk/
adjective
反神论的；反对神论的
Opposed to the belief in the existence of a god or gods.
His antitheistic views were not popular in the small, religious town.  他在宗教氛围浓厚的小镇里，他的反神论观点并不受欢迎。
The philosopher presented a series of antitheistic arguments. 这位哲学家提出了一系列反神论的论点。

synonym: atheistic, godless
antonym: theistic, religious
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + theistic.  Anti- (prefix meaning "against") + theistic (relating to the belief in God).
