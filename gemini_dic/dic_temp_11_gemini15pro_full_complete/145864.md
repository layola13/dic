overderisively: /ˌoʊvər dəˈraɪsɪvli/
| adv. | 过分嘲笑地，过分轻蔑地 |  in an excessively derisive manner; mockingly and scornfully to an exaggerated degree.  | He laughed overderisively at her suggestion, making her feel small. 他过分嘲笑她的建议，让她感到渺小。|  He overderisively dismissed her concerns as trivial. 他过分轻蔑地驳回了她的担忧，认为这些担忧微不足道。 | 近义词:  scornfully, mockingly, contemptuously, disdainfully | 反义词: respectfully, seriously, appreciatively | 词性: 副词

词源:

"over-" (prefix): excessive, too much 过度的
"derisive" (adjective): expressing contempt or ridicule 嘲笑的，嘲弄的
"-ly" (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives  副词后缀
