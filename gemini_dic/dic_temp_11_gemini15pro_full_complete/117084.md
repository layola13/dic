meadowwort:/ˈmedəʊwɜːt/ | n. | 草地香薷; 水苏 | A plant of the genus *Filipendula*, typically growing in meadows and having clusters of small white or pink flowers. | The meadowwort bloomed profusely in the damp field. 草地香薷在潮湿的田野里茂盛地开着花。| The meadowwort added a touch of delicate beauty to the landscape. 草地香薷给景色增添了一丝精致的美。 | synonyms: meadowsweet, queen-of-the-meadow | antonyms: N/A | noun
