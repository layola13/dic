noncohesivenesses:/ˌnɒnkoʊˈhiːsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
缺乏凝聚力；不团结
the state of lacking cohesiveness; the inability of parts to stick together or to form a united whole.
The noncohesivenesses of the team led to their defeat.  团队缺乏凝聚力导致了他们的失败。
The report identified several noncohesivenesses within the organization. 报告指出了组织内部存在的几个不团结的因素。
Synonyms: disunity,  incoherence, fragmentation, division, discord
Antonyms: unity, cohesion, solidarity, harmony, agreement
noun
