lysis: /ˈlaɪsɪs/
n.
分解；溶解；(精神病的)情感宣泄
destruction or dissolution of something, typically cells or tissues
例句：
The lysis of red blood cells is called hemolysis.
红细胞的溶解称为溶血。

The doctor explained that the virus caused the lysis of the infected cells.
医生解释说，病毒导致了受感染细胞的溶解。

近义词：decomposition, disintegration, breakdown, rupture
反义词：synthesis, fusion

词源:
From Ancient Greek λύσις (lúsis, “a loosening, releasing”), from λύω (lúō, “I loosen, release”).
