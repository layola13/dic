ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēsōlātiōnem, accusative singular of dēsōlātiō (“a laying waste, devastation”), from Latin dēsōlātus, past participle of dēsōlāre (“to lay waste, abandon”), from dē- (“thoroughly”) + sōlāre (“to make lonely”), from sōlus (“alone”).
