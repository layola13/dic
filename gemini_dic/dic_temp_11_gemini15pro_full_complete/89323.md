homogenetic:/ˌhɒməʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌhoʊmoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. |同源的，同基因的|Having the same origin; derived from the same source or ancestor.  Relating to or exhibiting homogeneity in genetic structure.|The study revealed homogenetic patterns in the DNA of the isolated population. 这项研究揭示了孤立人群DNA中的同源模式。|Synonyms: homologous, cognate, related|Antonyms: heterogenetic, unrelated|adjective
