indiscriminate:/ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnət/| adj.|不加选择的，不加区分的，滥杀滥伤的|Done at random or without careful judgment.|The indiscriminate use of pesticides can harm the environment.  滥用杀虫剂会危害环境。|例句：The bomber launched an indiscriminate attack on the city.  轰炸机对这座城市发动了不加区分的袭击。

例句：The indiscriminate killing of civilians is a war crime.  滥杀平民是战争罪。|近义词：random, haphazard, unselective, aimless|反义词：selective, discriminating, careful, discerning|词性:adjective

词源: From Latin indiscriminatus, from in- ‘not’ + discriminare ‘to distinguish,’ from discrimen ‘separation, distinction.’
