mollification:/ˌmɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
软化；缓和；平息
the act of making something softer or less severe
The mollification of her anger was evident in her softened tone.  她语气缓和表明她怒气已消。

近义词: appeasement, mitigation, pacification, alleviation
反义词: aggravation, exacerbation, intensification

词性: noun
