ogle:/ˈoʊɡəl/ | v. | 凝视，盯着看 | to stare at someone in a way that is considered rude or unpleasant | He kept ogling the waitress. 他一直盯着女服务员看。 | 近义词: stare, gawk, leer | 反义词: glance, glimpse | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Dutch *oogelen "to eye," from oog "eye."  Related to German äugen "to eye."
