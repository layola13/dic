meninges:/məˈnɪndʒiːz/
noun
脑膜（复数）
The three membranes (dura mater, arachnoid, and pia mater) that line the skull and vertebral canal and enclose the brain and spinal cord.
Meningitis is an inflammation of the meninges. 脑膜炎是脑膜的炎症。
synonyms:  meninx (singular), membrane
antonyms:  None (no direct anatomical opposite)
n.


meninx:/ˈmiːnɪŋks/
noun
脑膜（单数）
Any of the three membranes that envelop the brain and spinal cord in vertebrates.
The meninx is crucial for protecting the central nervous system. 脑膜对于保护中神经系统至关重要。
synonyms:  meninges (plural), membrane
antonyms: None (no direct anatomical opposite)
n.
