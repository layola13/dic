compatible-with:/kəmˈpætəbl wɪð/ | adj. | 兼容的；可共存的 | Able to exist or occur together without conflict.  Something is compatible with something else if it can function or exist alongside it without issues. | This software is compatible with all versions of Windows.  这个软件与所有版本的Windows兼容。

This printer is compatible with both Mac and PC operating systems. 这台打印机兼容Mac和PC操作系统。


近义词: consistent with, congruent with, interoperable, agreeable | 反义词: incompatible, conflicting, inconsistent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *compatibilis, from compati, from com- ‘together’ + pati ‘suffer’.
