attributer's:/əˈtrɪbjʊtərz/
noun
归因者(的)
The word "attributer's" is the possessive form of "attributer," meaning belonging to or associated with someone who attributes something.  An attributer is a person who ascribes a quality or characteristic to someone or something.
The attributer's opinion was biased. 归因者的观点是有偏见的。
近义词:ascriber's, imputer's
反义词:None
