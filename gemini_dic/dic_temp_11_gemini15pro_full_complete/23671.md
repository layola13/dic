brank: /bræŋk/
n.
辔头，马嚼子；一种用于惩罚的头部器具
A scold's bridle; an instrument formerly used for punishing scolds, consisting of an iron framework which enclosed the head, having a gag or sharp bit of iron which entered the mouth and restrained the tongue.
The brank was used to silence gossips and nagging wives.  这个马嚼子是用来让爱嚼舌根的人和唠叨的妻子闭嘴的。
bridle, halter, gag
muzzle
