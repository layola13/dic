enactive:/ɪˈnæktɪv/ /iˈnæktɪv/ | adj. |   enacting; characterized by action, especially physical action; based on or involving action. | relating to or based on direct experience or interaction with one's environment; involving action. | Enactive learning involves hands-on activities and experiences.  实操性学习包含动手活动和经验。 | 近义词: active, dynamic, practical, experiential | 反义词: passive, theoretical, abstract |词性: adjective

enactive:/ɪˈnæktɪv/ /iˈnæktɪv/ | adj. | 生成的；制定（或颁布）的 |  bringing something into existence, such as a law. | The enactive phase of the project was completed successfully.  该项目的制定阶段顺利完成。 | 近义词:  legislative,  constitutive | 反义词:  repealing, abolishing |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *enactivus, from enactus, past participle of enagere "to drive or push in," from in- "in" + agere "to drive, do, act."
