bureaucracy: /bjʊˈrɒkrəsi/ | n. | 官僚主义，官僚机构 | A system of government in which most of the important decisions are made by state officials rather than by elected representatives.  A complex administrative structure characterized by many rules and regulations. | The company was bogged down in bureaucracy.  这家公司陷入了官僚主义的泥潭。

The government bureaucracy is hindering progress on the project. 政府的官僚作风阻碍了项目的进展。 | 近义词: red tape, officialdom, administration | 反义词: democracy, meritocracy | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *bureaucratie*, from *bureau* ("desk," also "office") + *-cratie* (from Ancient Greek *kratía* ("power," "rule")).
