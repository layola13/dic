isogloss:/ˈaɪsəɡlɒs/ (US) /ˈaɪsəɡlɒs/ (UK)| n. |等语线|A line on a map marking the boundary of an area within which a particular linguistic feature occurs.|The isogloss separating the use of "pop" and "soda" runs through the Midwest.|近义词:isoline, boundary line|反义词:N/A|noun
