infecund:/ɪnˈfiːkənd/ | adj. |不孕的，不肥沃的 |Not fertile; unable to produce offspring (of people, animals, or plants).  |例句：The infecund soil yielded a meager harvest.  贫瘠的土地只收获了微薄的收成。|例句：The couple struggled with infecundity for years. 这对夫妇多年来一直为不孕不育而苦恼。|近义词：barren, sterile, infertile |反义词：fertile, fecund, productive |词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *infecundus*, from *in-* (not) + *fecundus* (fruitful, fertile). *Fecundus* is related to *fe-* (to suckle, produce) and thus to words like "female" and "fetus".
