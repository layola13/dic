multitracked:/ˈmʌltiˌtrækɪd/ | adj. |多轨录制的|Recorded on multiple tracks, allowing for separate recording and mixing of different instruments or vocals.|The song features a multitracked guitar solo, creating a rich and layered sound. 这首歌以多轨录制的吉他独奏为特色，创造了丰富而多层次的声音。|Synonyms: multi-channel, layered|Antonyms: single-track, monaural|adjective
