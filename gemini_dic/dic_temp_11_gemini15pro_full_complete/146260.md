overexuberantly:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzjuːbərəntli/
adverb
过于热情地；过分兴奋地
In a manner that shows excessive enthusiasm or excitement.
The fans cheered overexuberantly after their team won the championship.  球迷们在他们的球队赢得冠军后欢呼雀跃。
He waved his hands overexuberantly, nearly knocking over a vase. 他过于热情地挥舞着双手，差点打翻一个花瓶。

近义词: enthusiastically, excitedly, boisterously, ebulliently
反义词: apathetically, listlessly, indifferently
词性: adverb

词源:
From over- + exuberant + -ly.  "Over-" prefix meaning "excessive" + "exuberant" meaning "full of energy and excitement" + "-ly" adverb suffix.  源自 over-（表示“过度”的前缀） + exuberant（意为“充满活力和兴奋”） + -ly（副词后缀）。
