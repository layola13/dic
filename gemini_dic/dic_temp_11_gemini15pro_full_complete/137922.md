nonvolcanic:/ˌnɑːn.vɑlˈkæn.ɪk/
adjective
非火山的
Not of or pertaining to volcanoes; not produced by volcanoes.
The island's nonvolcanic origin makes its unique rock formations even more fascinating.  这个岛屿的非火山起源使其独特的岩层更加引人入胜。
The region's landscape is shaped by nonvolcanic forces like erosion and glacial activity. 该地区的地貌是由侵蚀和冰川活动等非火山力量塑造的。
synonyms:  nonigneous, amagmatic
antonyms: volcanic, igneous
