fibrillation:/ˌfɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 颤动，纤维性颤动 |  A rapid, irregular, and unsynchronized contraction of muscle fibers, especially of the heart (atrial fibrillation) or other muscles. |  His heart went into fibrillation. 他的心脏发生了纤颤。 |  tremor, quiver, flutter |  stability, stillness | noun

fibrillate:/ˈfɪbrɪleɪt/ | v. | 颤动 |  To undergo or cause to undergo fibrillation. | The heart muscle began to fibrillate uncontrollably. 心肌开始不受控制地颤动。 |  twitch, quiver, flutter |  stabilize, still | verb
