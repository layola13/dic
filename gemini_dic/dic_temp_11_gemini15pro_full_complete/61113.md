elogium:/ɪˈloʊdʒiəm/ /jʊˈloʊdʒiəm/
n.
颂词；赞词；悼词
A formal expression of praise; a eulogy.
He delivered a moving elogium at his friend's funeral. 他在朋友的葬礼上致了感人的悼词。
近义词：eulogy, panegyric, tribute, encomium
反义词：criticism, condemnation, denunciation
词性: noun
