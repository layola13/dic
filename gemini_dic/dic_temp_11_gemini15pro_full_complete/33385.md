chunky:/ˈtʃʌŋki/| adj.|厚实的，粗短的，块状的|Thick, short, and solid.  Relating to or resembling chunks.|She wore a chunky knit sweater. 她穿着一件厚实的针织毛衣。|近义词：thick, bulky, stocky, hefty|反义词:thin, slender, slim|词性:形容词
Etymology: From chunk + -y.  "Chunk" meaning a thick, solid piece, likely of imitative origin.
