deceivable:/dɪˈsiːvəbl/
adjective
可欺骗的，容易受骗的
capable of being deceived or misled.
例句： Appearances can be deceivable. 外表可能是骗人的。

例句：He had a deceivable smile. 他的笑容很 deceptive.


近义词：misleading, deceptive, fallacious
反义词：honest, trustworthy, reliable
词性: adjective
