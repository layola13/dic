breadline:/ˈbredlaɪn/ (US) /ˈbredlaɪn/ (UK)| n. |贫困线；靠救济度日；领取救济食品的长队|A line of people waiting to receive free food or other necessities, typically because they are very poor.|例句：Millions of families are living on the breadline. 数百万家庭生活在贫困线上。

例句：The recession has pushed many more people to the breadline. 经济衰退使更多的人陷入贫困。

近义词：poverty line, poverty level|反义词: affluence, wealth |词性: noun
