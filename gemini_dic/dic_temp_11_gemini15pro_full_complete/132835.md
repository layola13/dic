noneditorial:/ˌnɒnˌedɪˈtɔːrɪəl/
adjective
非编辑的；非社论的
Not connected with the writing or editing of material for publication; not relating to the expression of opinions or interpretations.
The noneditorial sections of the newspaper, such as the classified ads, are handled by a separate department.
报纸的非编辑部分，例如分类广告，由一个单独的部门处理。
Near synonyms: non-journalistic, non-opinion, factual, objective
Antonyms: editorial, journalistic, subjective, opinionated
词性: adjective
