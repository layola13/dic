一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The sun rises in the east.  太阳从东方升起。
He works at a bank. 他在银行工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is reading a book now. 她现在正在读书。
They are playing football in the park. 他们在公园里踢足球。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have finished my homework. 我已经完成了我的家庭作业。
She has traveled to many countries. 她去过很多国家旅行。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been studying English for three years. 我一直在学习英语三年了。
He has been working on this project for months.  他几个月来一直在做这个项目。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He went to the cinema last night. 他昨晚去看电影了。
She ate an apple for breakfast. 她早餐吃了一个苹果。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was watching TV when the phone rang. 电话响的时候我正在看电视。
She was cooking dinner when he arrived. 他到达时她正在做晚饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had finished my work before he came. 在他来之前我已经完成了我的工作。
She had already left when I arrived. 当我到达时她已经离开了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been studying for two hours when he called. 他打电话时我已经学习了两个小时。
She had been working on that project for months before it was finished. 在项目完成之前，她已经做了好几个月了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will go to the beach tomorrow. 我明天会去海滩。
She will visit her parents next week. 她下周会去看望她的父母。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be watching TV this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候我会在看电视。
They will be having a meeting at 10 am.  他们上午10点将举行会议。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished my homework by tonight. 我今晚之前会完成我的家庭作业。
She will have graduated by next year. 她明年就会毕业。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

He will have been working at this company for ten years by next month. 到下个月他就在这家公司工作十年了。

注意事项:

*  Enim is primarily used in formal or literary contexts. 在现代英语中很少使用，主要用于正式或文学语境。
*  It usually appears after the first word or phrase of a sentence. 它通常出现在句子的第一个单词或短语之后。
