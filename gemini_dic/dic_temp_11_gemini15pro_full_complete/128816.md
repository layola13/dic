nightlike:/ˈnaɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像夜晚的，夜间的
Resembling or characteristic of night; nocturnal.
The owl, a nightlike creature, emerged from its daytime slumber.  猫头鹰，一种像夜晚的生物，从白天的睡眠中醒来。
The dense fog created a nightlike atmosphere even though it was midday. 浓雾即使在中午也营造出一种像夜晚的氛围。
近义词: nocturnal, dark, shadowy
反义词: daylike, diurnal, bright
词性: adjective
