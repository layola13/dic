meddlingly:/ˈmedlɪŋli/
adverb
爱管闲事地，多管闲事地
In a manner that interferes in other people's affairs without being asked or needed.
She meddlingly rearranged the furniture in her friend's apartment. 她未经允许就爱管闲事地重新布置了朋友公寓里的家具。
He meddlingly offered unsolicited advice. 他多管闲事地主动提出不必要的建议。
近义词: intrusively, officiously, interferingly
反义词: respectfully, discreetly

词性: adverb

词源: From Middle English *medlen, from Old French medler (compare modern French mêler), from Vulgar Latin *misculāre, from Latin misceō (“I mix, mingle”). More at mix. The suffix -ly transforms the adjective meddling into an adverb.
