absent-minded:/ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪd/
adjective
心不在焉的，健忘的
Having or showing a habitually forgetful or inattentive disposition.
He's so absent-minded, he's always losing his keys. 他太心不在焉了，总是丢钥匙。
My professor is quite absent-minded and often forgets what he's talking about mid-lecture. 我的教授非常健忘，经常在讲课中途忘记自己在说什么。
近义词:forgetful, distracted, preoccupied, inattentive
反义词:attentive, mindful, alert, focused
词性:adjective

absent-mindedly:/ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪdli/
adverb
心不在焉地
In an absent-minded manner.
She absent-mindedly stirred her coffee while staring out the window. 她心不在焉地搅拌着咖啡，眼睛盯着窗外。
He absent-mindedly patted his pockets, searching for his phone. 他心不在焉地拍了拍口袋，寻找他的手机。
近义词: distractedly, inattentively, vaguely
反义词: attentively, mindfully, deliberately
词性:adverb

absent-mindedness:/ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪdnəs/
noun
心不在焉，健忘
The state of being absent-minded.
His absent-mindedness led to several missed appointments. 他的心不在焉导致他错过了好几个约会。
Her absent-mindedness is a constant source of amusement for her friends. 她的健忘经常让她的朋友觉得好笑。
近义词: forgetfulness, distraction, preoccupation, inattention
反义词: attentiveness, mindfulness, alertness, focus
词性:noun
