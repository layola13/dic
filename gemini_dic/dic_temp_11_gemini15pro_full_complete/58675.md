dummy:/ˈdʌmi/| adj.|假的，虚拟的|Something that is dummy is not real or genuine, but is used for practice or testing.  假的不是真的，而是用于练习或测试。|例句：He practiced his presentation with a dummy audience. 他对着虚拟观众练习他的演讲。|近义词：fake, imitation, simulated, mock|反义词：real, genuine, authentic|词性:形容词
