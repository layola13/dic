countervallation:/ˌkaʊntərˌvæˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 对垒；围攻工事；包围 | An artificial barrier, such as a ditch and rampart, formed around a besieged place to prevent the besieged from making sallies.  |  The Roman army built a massive countervallation around the city.  罗马军队在城市周围建造了巨大的包围工事。 | Synonyms: circumvallation, siegeworks, blockade | Antonyms: sally, sortie | noun

countervallation:/ˌkaʊntərˌvæˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 防御工事；对抗工事 |  A defensive structure built to protect against attacks. | The countervallation prevented the enemy from advancing. 防御工事阻止了敌人的前进。 | Synonyms: rampart, fortification, bulwark | Antonyms: breach, gap | noun
