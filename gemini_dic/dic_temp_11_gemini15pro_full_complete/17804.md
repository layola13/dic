bedrolls:/ˈbedroʊlz/
noun
床上用品；铺盖卷；卧具
A portable roll of bedding, typically consisting of a blanket or sleeping bag, often used for camping or other outdoor activities.
The cowboys unrolled their bedrolls under the starry sky. 牛仔们在星空下铺开他们的铺盖卷。

近义词:sleeping bags, bedding
反义词:(无明确的反义词)
词性:noun
