conservative:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/(UK) /kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/(US)| n.|保守主义者|A person who is averse to change and holds to traditional values and attitudes, typically in relation to politics.|He's a staunch conservative. 他是一个坚定的保守主义者。|近义词：traditionalist, right-winger|反义词：liberal, radical, progressive|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
