army: /ˈɑːrmi/ (US) /ˈɑːmi/ (UK)| n. |军队，陆军；大群，大批|A large organized body of armed personnel trained for war, especially on land.  A large number of people or things.|例句：The army marched towards the border. 军队向边境进发。

The boy saw an army of ants.  男孩看到了一大群蚂蚁。|近义词：troops, forces, legion, multitude, host|反义词:civilian, individual|noun


Etymology->
From Old French *armee “army, armed expedition,” noun use of past participle of armer “to arm,” from Latin armare "to arm," from arma "arms, weapons."
