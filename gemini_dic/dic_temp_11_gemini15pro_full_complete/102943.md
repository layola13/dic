jazzest:/ˈdʒæzɪɪst/
adj.
最爵士的; 最具爵士乐风格的
Most jazzy; having the most characteristics of jazz music.
That's the jazzest melody I've ever heard. 那是至今为止我听过的最爵士的旋律。
近义词:jazzy, swingy
反义词:unjazzy, square
词性:形容词

词源:  "jazzest" is the superlative form of the adjective "jazzy." "Jazzy" itself originated from "jazz," a musical genre that emerged in the late 19th and early 20th centuries in African American communities in New Orleans, United States. The exact origin of the word "jazz" is uncertain, but it is believed to have connections to West African and Creole slang.
