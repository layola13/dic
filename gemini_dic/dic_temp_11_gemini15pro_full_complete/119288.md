micro-encapsulate:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | v. | 微囊化；包入微囊 | To enclose (a substance, such as a drug or flavoring agent) in a minute capsule. | The company micro-encapsulates the pesticides within a biodegradable shell.  这家公司将杀虫剂微囊化在一个可生物降解的外壳内。|  The vitamins are micro-encapsulated to protect them from degradation. 这些维生素被微囊化以防止其降解。 | encapsulate, encase | release | Verb


micro-encapsulation:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˌkæpsjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 微囊化；微胶囊化 | The process of enclosing a substance in a minute capsule. | Micro-encapsulation allows for controlled release of the active ingredient. 微囊化允许活性成分的控释。 |  The micro-encapsulation of fragrances is common in the perfume industry. 香料的微囊化在香水行业很普遍。 | encapsulation |  - | Noun
