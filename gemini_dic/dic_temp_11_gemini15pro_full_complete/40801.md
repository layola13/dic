convokers:/kənˈvoʊkərz/| n.|召集人，召集者|People who call a meeting or assembly together are the convokers.  召集会议或集会的人是召集者。|The convokers of the conference decided to postpone it due to the pandemic.  会议的召集人决定因疫情推迟会议。|synonyms: organizers, conveners, summoners|antonyms: attendees, participants|noun
