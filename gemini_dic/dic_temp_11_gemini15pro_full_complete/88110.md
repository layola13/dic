hijab:/hɪˈdʒɑːb/ /hɪˈdʒæb/| n.|头巾，面纱 (尤指穆斯林妇女佩戴的)|A head covering worn in public by some Muslim women.|She wears a hijab as a symbol of her faith. 她戴着头巾作为她信仰的象征。|Synonyms: headscarf, veil|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps uncovered hair)|noun

hijab:/hɪˈdʒɑːb/ /hɪˈdʒæb/| n.|遮蔽，掩盖|The act of covering or concealing something.|The hijab provides a sense of privacy and modesty.  头巾提供了一种隐私和端庄的感觉。|Synonyms: covering, concealment|Antonyms: exposure, revelation|noun
