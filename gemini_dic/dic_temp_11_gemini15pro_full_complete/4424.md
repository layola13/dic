alarumed:/əˈlɑːrmd/
|adj.|惊恐的，担心的|Filled with alarm; frightened or worried.|例句：She was alarmed by the news. 她被这个消息吓坏了。|近义词：frightened, worried, anxious, apprehensive, panicked|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed, tranquil|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "alarm" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Alarm" comes from the Italian "all'arme" (to arms!), indicating a call to prepare for danger.
