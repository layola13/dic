nontransportability:/ˌnɒntrænspɔːrtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可移植性，不可转移性 | The inability to generalize or apply findings, conclusions, or methods from one context or setting to another. | The researchers acknowledged the nontransportability of their findings to other cultural contexts. 研究人员承认他们的发现不能推广到其他文化背景。| The nontransportability of the software limited its usefulness.  该软件的不可移植性限制了它的实用性。|  near synonyms: non-generalizability, inapplicability, context-dependence  | antonyms: universality, generalizability, transportability | noun
