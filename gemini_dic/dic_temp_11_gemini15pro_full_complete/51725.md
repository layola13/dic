devocalize:/diːˈvoʊkəlaɪz/
verb
使失声，使不能发声；消除…的声音
To deprive of voice or vocal sound; to make voiceless.
The surgery devocalized the singer, ending his career.  手术使这位歌手失声，结束了他的职业生涯。
The city council passed a law to devocalize leaf blowers, making them quieter. 市议会通过了一项法律，以消除吹叶机的噪音，使其更安静。
synonyms: silence, mute, hush
antonyms: amplify, vocalize
词性: 动词
