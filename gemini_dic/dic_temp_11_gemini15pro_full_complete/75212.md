froggish:/ˈfrɒɡɪʃ/ (US) /ˈfrɑːɡɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|像青蛙的；呆滞的；冷漠的|Resembling a frog in appearance or behavior; sluggish; apathetic.|He sat there with a froggish expression, staring blankly at the wall. 他带着呆滞的表情坐在那里，茫然地盯着墙。|近义词：sluggish, apathetic, inert, phlegmatic|反义词：energetic, lively, animated|词性:形容词
词源: from frog + -ish.  From Middle English *froggyssh, equivalent to frog + -ish.
