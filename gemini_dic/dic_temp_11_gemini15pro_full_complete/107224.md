laika:/ˈlaɪkə/| n. |莱卡（苏联太空犬）|A Soviet dog, the first animal to orbit the Earth.|Laika was sent into space aboard Sputnik 2. 莱卡乘坐斯普特尼克2号被送入太空。|Synonyms: cosmonaut (loosely), astronaut (loosely)|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian лайка (lajka), a general term for several breeds of Spitz-type hunting dog.
