misjudged:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒd/
verb
判断错误；误判
formed by adding the prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") to the verb "judge"
I misjudged the distance and drove off the road. 我错误地判断了距离，把车开出了路。
The police misjudged the situation and used excessive force. 警方错误地判断了情况，过度使用了武力。
synonyms: miscalculated, underestimated, overestimated, misinterpreted
antonyms: judged correctly, assessed accurately, understood
verb
