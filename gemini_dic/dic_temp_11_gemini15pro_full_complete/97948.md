innoxious:/ɪˈnɒksəs/ | adj. | 无害的；无毒的 | Not harmful or offensive. | The spider may look scary, but it's completely innoxious.  这只蜘蛛可能看起来很吓人，但它完全无害。 |  The berries are innoxious, you can eat them. 这些浆果无害，你可以吃它们。 | 近义词: harmless, safe, benign | 反义词: harmful, noxious, dangerous, toxic | 词性: adjective
词源: From Latin innoxius "harmless," from in- "not" (see in- (1)) + noxius "harmful," from noxa "harm, injury."
