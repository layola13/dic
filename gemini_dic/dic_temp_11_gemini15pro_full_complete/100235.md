interrunning:/ˌɪntərˈrʌnɪŋ/
adjective
交错的，交织的
extending or occurring between or among other things.  Formed from "inter-" (between) + "running".
The interrunning vines created a dense canopy. 交错的藤蔓形成了茂密的树冠。
Near synonyms: interwoven, intertwined, interlaced
Antonyms: separate, detached, discrete
词性: 形容词
