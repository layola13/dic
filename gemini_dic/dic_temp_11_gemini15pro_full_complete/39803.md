consortion:/kənˈsɔːrʃən/ /kənˈsɔːrʃn/
n.
联合，合伙，财团
a group of companies or people who join together to carry out a particular activity, especially a large financial operation
例句：The construction project was undertaken by a consortion of local businesses.  这个建筑项目是由当地企业联合承办的。

例句：An international consortion of banks financed the deal. 一个国际银行财团资助了这笔交易。

近义词：alliance, coalition, partnership, syndicate, consortium
反义词：None (No direct opposite exists.  The absence of a consortion could be competition or individual action.)
词性：noun
