modular:/ˈmɒdjʊlə(r) (UK), ˈmɑːdʒələr (US)| adj.|模块化的，模数的|Designed with standardized units or sections for easy construction, modification, or flexibility.|The company uses a modular design for its furniture, allowing customers to customize their pieces.|Synonyms: component, sectional, prefabricated, standardized, unit, block|Antonyms: monolithic, integrated, unified|adjective
