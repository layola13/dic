cowier:/ˈkaʊɪər/| adj.|更胆小的，更怯懦的|Comparative form of "cowy," meaning more timid or easily frightened.  It describes someone who displays a greater degree of fear or lack of courage.|He became cowier after the incident.  事件发生后，他变得更加胆怯了。|The cowier dog hid behind the tree.  那只更胆小的狗躲在树后面。|近义词：more timid, more fearful, more cowardly|反义词：braver, bolder, more courageous|词性:形容词
