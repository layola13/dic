nudism:/ˈnjuːdɪzəm/| n. |裸体主义|The practice of going without clothes, especially in a social setting where others are also nude, for reasons of health, freedom, or aesthetic appreciation. |例句：Some people believe nudism promotes a healthy body image.  有些人认为裸体主义能促进健康的身体形象。

例句：The resort has a private beach for nudism.  这个度假胜地有一个供裸体主义者使用的私人海滩。|近义词：naturism|反义词: clothedness, conservatism|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *nudisme*, from *nu* (“nude”) + *-isme*. Ultimately from Latin *nūdus* (“naked, bare”).
