holdable:/ˈhoʊldəbl/| adj.|可握住的，可携带的|Something that is holdable is able to be held in the hand. |例句：The camera is small and holdable, making it perfect for travel.  这款相机小巧便携，非常适合旅行。

例句：The baby's toy is soft and holdable.  婴儿的玩具柔软且易于抓握。|近义词：graspable, manageable, portable|反义词:unwieldy, cumbersome|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From "hold" (verb) + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").  The word "hold" comes from Old English "haldan," meaning "to grasp, keep, possess." The suffix "-able" ultimately derives from Latin "-abilis."
