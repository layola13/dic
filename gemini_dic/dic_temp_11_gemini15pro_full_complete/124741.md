mpla: /ˈɛmˌpiːˌɛlˌeɪ/ | abbreviation |  多协议标签交换 (Multiprotocol Label Switching) | MPLS is a networking technology that routes data from one network node to the next based on short path labels rather than long network addresses. | Example: MPLS provides a fast and efficient way to forward network traffic.  多协议标签交换提供了一种快速有效的转发网络流量的方法。 | Example: Many large companies use MPLS for their wide area networks. 许多大公司在其广域网中使用 MPLS。| Synonym: label switching | Antonym: traditional IP routing | noun
