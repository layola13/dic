crackdown:/ˈkrækdaʊn/| n.|镇压；打击；取缔|A sudden increase in official activity against something that is considered wrong or harmful.|The government launched a crackdown on illegal immigration. 政府对非法移民发起了一场打击。|近义词：suppression, repression, clampdown|反义词:leniency, tolerance|名词

crack down:/kræk daʊn/| phrasal verb.|镇压；打击；取缔|To start dealing with someone or something much more strictly.|The police are cracking down on drug dealers. 警方正在严厉打击毒贩。|近义词：suppress, repress, clamp down|反义词:ignore, condone|动词
