nonedibility:/ˌnɒnˌedɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可食用性
The quality or state of being unfit or unsuitable for consumption as food.
The mushroom's bright red color signaled its nonedibility.  这种蘑菇鲜红的颜色表明它不可食用。
The nonedibility of certain wild berries can pose a danger to hikers.  某些野生浆果的不可食用性会对徒步旅行者构成危险。
