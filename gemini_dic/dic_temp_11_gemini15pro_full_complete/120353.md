minimises:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
最小化，使减到最小
reduces something to the smallest possible amount or degree.
The company minimises its costs by outsourcing production. 公司通过外包生产来最大限度地降低成本。
近义词: reduces, decreases, lessens
反义词: maximises, increases, expands
词性: verb (third-person singular present tense)
