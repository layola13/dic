blow-a-kiss:/ˌbləʊ ə ˈkɪs/| n.|飞吻|An affectionate gesture made by kissing one's hand and then blowing on it, as if transferring the kiss to someone else.|She blew a kiss to her baby as she left the room. 她离开房间时给了宝宝一个飞吻。|Near synonyms: air kiss, thrown kiss|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a rude gesture)|noun

blow-a-kiss:/ˌbləʊ ə ˈkɪs/| v.|送飞吻|To perform the action of blowing a kiss.|He blew a kiss to the cheering crowd. 他向欢呼的人群送飞吻。|Near synonyms: send a kiss, air kiss|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a rude gesture)|verb
