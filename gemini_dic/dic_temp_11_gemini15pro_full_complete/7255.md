anglocentric:/ˈæŋɡloʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 以英国为中心的；英国中心主义的 | Focusing on England or Britain to the exclusion of other cultures or perspectives; considering England or Britain as the central fact or most important element. |  Many older history books have an anglocentric view of the world. 许多古老的历史书都以英国为中心的视角看待世界。

The curriculum was criticized for its anglocentric bias.  该课程因其以英国为中心的偏见而受到批评。 | 近义词：British-centered, Anglophile | 反义词：multicultural, global | 词性: 形容词
