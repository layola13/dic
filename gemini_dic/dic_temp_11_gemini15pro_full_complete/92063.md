hyperpromiscuous:/ˌhaɪpərprəˈmɪskjuəs/
|adj.|过度滥交的，极度混杂的|Excessively promiscuous; engaging in or characterized by a great deal of sexual activity with many partners.|She was labeled hyperpromiscuous after rumors of her numerous affairs spread.|近义词：promiscuous, wanton, dissolute|反义词：chaste, virtuous|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
hyper- (Greek): prefix meaning "over, excessive," from *hupér "over, beyond"
promiscuous (Latin): from promiscuus "mixed, indiscriminate," from pro- "forth" + miscere "to mix"
