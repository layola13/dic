crt:/siːɑːrˈtiː/ /ˈkætˌθoʊd ˈreɪ ˈtjuːb/
n.|阴极射线管|Cathode Ray Tube. A vacuum tube that contains one or more electron guns and a phosphorescent screen, and is used to display images.  It modulates, accelerates, and focuses electron beams onto the screen to create the images.|The old television used a CRT. 那台旧电视使用阴极射线管。|近义词:monitor, screen, display|反义词:LED, LCD|名词

crt:/siːɑːrˈtiː/
abbr.|证书|Certificate.  Often used as an abbreviation for various types of certificates, particularly in IT contexts, such as X.509 certificates used for SSL/TLS.|He renewed his SSL crt. 他更新了他的SSL证书。|近义词:certification, document, credential|反义词:None|缩写
