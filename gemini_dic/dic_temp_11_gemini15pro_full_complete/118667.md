meta-talk: /ˈmɛtəˌtɔːk/ | n. | 元语言；关于语言的讨论 | Discussion about the language being used, rather than about the topic being discussed. | "The lecturer paused for some meta-talk, explaining the etymology of the term he was using."  演讲者暂停了一下，进行了一些元语言的讨论，解释了他正在使用的术语的词源。| synonyms: metalanguage, discourse about language | antonyms: object language (the language being discussed, not the language used to discuss it) | noun

meta-talk: /ˈmɛtəˌtɔːk/ | n. | 元交际; 关于交际本身的交际 | Communication about the communication itself, rather than about the topic of the communication. | "Their conversation was filled with meta-talk, constantly analyzing how they were interacting." 他们的对话充满了元交际，不断分析他们是如何互动的。 | synonyms: metacommunication | antonyms:  object communication (communication about something other than the communication itself) | noun
