iberians:/aɪˈbɪəriənz/
noun
伊比利亚人
People who live in or originate from the Iberian Peninsula (Spain and Portugal).
The Iberians were known for their seafaring skills. 伊比利亚人以其航海技术而闻名。
Ancient Iberians traded with the Phoenicians. 古代伊比利亚人与腓尼基人进行贸易。
