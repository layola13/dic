fanner's:/ˈfænərz/
noun
农民的；簸谷者的；鼓风机的
belonging to or associated with a fanner (someone who uses a fan or a winnowing machine, especially to separate grain from chaff).  Derived from "fanner" + possessive suffix "-'s."
The fanner's hat protected him from the sun. 农民的帽子保护他免受阳光照射。
近义词:agricultural worker's, harvester's
反义词:None
词性:Possessive noun
