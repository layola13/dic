diffusers:/dɪˈfjuːzərz/| n.|扩散器，漫射器|A device that spreads something, such as light, heat, or sound, over a wider area.|例句：The photographer used a diffuser to soften the light. 摄影师使用了一个漫射器来柔化光线。

例句：Essential oil diffusers can make a room smell wonderful. 精油扩散器可以让房间充满香味。|近义词：spreaders, dispersers|反义词：concentrators|词性:名词
