impatientness:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənsnəs/| n.|不耐烦，急躁|The state of being unable to wait patiently or tolerate delay; restlessness or irritability.|His impatience was evident in his constant fidgeting. 他的不耐烦表现在他不停地坐立不安。|Near synonyms: restlessness, irritability, edginess, fretfulness|Near antonyms: patience, tolerance, forbearance|noun

impatient:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənt/| adj.|不耐烦的，急躁的|Not patient; easily annoyed by someone's mistakes or because you have to wait|He was impatient to get started. 他急于开始。|Near synonyms: restless, irritable, edgy, fretful|Near antonyms: patient, tolerant, forbearing|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French impacient, from in- ‘not’ + patient.
