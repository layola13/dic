juggle:/ˈdʒʌɡ(ə)l/| v. n.|玩杂耍；尽力应付；篡改(数字等)|To continuously toss into the air and catch (several objects) so as to keep at least one in the air while handling the others.|He was juggling three balls at once. 他同时在玩三个球的杂耍。|synonyms:manipulate, handle, manage|antonyms:neglect, ignore|verb

juggle:/ˈdʒʌɡ(ə)l/| n.|玩杂耍；欺骗；困难局面|The act of juggling; a difficult situation requiring skillful manipulation.|It's a real juggle to balance work and family life. 平衡工作和家庭生活真是一个难题。|synonyms:trick, deception, manipulation|antonyms:honesty, truthfulness|noun
