buttonwood:/ˈbʌtnˌwʊd/| n.|美桐树，纽扣树，梧桐|A North American plane tree (Platanus occidentalis) with wood used for buttons, boxes, and furniture.  It is also known as the American sycamore or the American planetree.  Historically, buttonwood trees were significant as the site where early Wall Street traders gathered.|例句：The buttonwood tree provided shade for the early Wall Street traders.  美桐树为早期的华尔街交易员提供了树荫。

例句：Buttonwood is a fast-growing tree. 美桐树是一种生长迅速的树木。|近义词：sycamore, plane tree, American planetree|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
