matily:/ˈmeɪtɪli/| adv.|友好地，亲切地，像伙伴一样|In a friendly, matey way, like close friends.|They greeted each other matily, clapping each other on the back. 他们友好地互相问候，互相拍着对方的背。|近义词:friendlily, amicably, fraternally|反义词:unfriendlily, coldly, hostilely|词性:副词

词源: From "matey" + "-ly."  "Matey" itself comes from "mate," which has its roots in Middle Low German "māte," meaning "companion, comrade."
