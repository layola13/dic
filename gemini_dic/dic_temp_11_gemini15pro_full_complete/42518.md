counterirritate:/ˌkaʊntərˈɪrɪteɪt/
verb
对抗刺激；以刺激疗法治疗
To apply an irritant to the skin in order to relieve inflammation or pain in a deeper part of the body.
The doctor counterirritated the patient's sore muscle with a menthol rub. 医生用薄荷醇擦剂对病人疼痛的肌肉进行对抗刺激。
Synonyms: counteract, relieve, soothe
Antonyms: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate
