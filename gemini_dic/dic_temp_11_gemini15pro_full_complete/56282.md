doggerel:/ˈdɒɡərəl/ (US) /ˈdɑːɡərəl/| n.|打油诗，蹩脚的诗|Crude, poorly written verse, often humorous or irregular in rhythm and rhyme.  It is characterized by its simple, often clichéd language and its lack of artistic merit.|Example 1:  The greeting card was full of sentimental doggerel. 贺卡上充满了感伤的打油诗。

Example 2: His attempt at a love poem ended up as embarrassing doggerel. 他尝试写一首爱情诗，结果变成了一首令人尴尬的打油诗。|近义词：verse, jingle, rhyme, limerick|反义词:poetry, epic, sonnet|词性:noun
