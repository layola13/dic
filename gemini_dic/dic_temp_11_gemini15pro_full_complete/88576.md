hither-and-thither:/ˈhɪðər ənd ˈðɪðər/| adv.|到处；来回地；四处|Moving from one place to another, often in a disorganized or random way.|The butterflies flitted hither and thither in the garden. 蝴蝶在花园里飞来飞去。|Synonyms: back and forth, to and fro, here and there, everywhere|Antonyms: nowhere, in one place|adverb

hither:/ˈhɪðər/| adv.|到这里；向这里|To or toward this place.|Come hither, my child. 孩子，到这儿来。|Synonyms: here, to this place|Antonyms: thither, thence|adverb

thither:/ˈðɪðər/| adv.|向那里；往那边|To or toward that place.|He went thither and yon. 他四处奔波。|Synonyms: there, to that place|Antonyms: hither, hence|adverb
