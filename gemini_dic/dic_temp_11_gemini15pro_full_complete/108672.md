leag:/liːɡ/ | n. | 牧场；草地；休耕地 | A leag is a tract of open land, typically used for pasture or left fallow. | The farmer grazed his sheep on the leag. 农民在他的草地上放羊吃草。 |  The wildflowers bloomed beautifully in the leag. 野花在休耕地上开得很漂亮。 | synonym: meadow, pasture, field | antonym: city, urban area | noun

词源：From Old English *lēah (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *lauhaz.
