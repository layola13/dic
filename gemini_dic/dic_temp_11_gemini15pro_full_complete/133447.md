nonfatality:/ˌnɑːnfəˈtælɪti/ | n. | 非致命事件；非致命性；非死亡人数 | An outcome of an accident or incident that does not result in death. |  The report highlighted the high number of nonfatalities in car accidents involving airbags. 该报告强调了安全气囊相关的车祸中大量的非致命事件。| Near misses, injuries | Fatality, death | noun

nonfatal:/ˌnɑːnˈfeɪtl/ | adj. | 非致命的 | Not causing or resulting in death. | The illness proved to be nonfatal, and the patient made a full recovery. 这种疾病被证明是非致命的，病人完全康复了。| Nonlethal, harmless | Fatal, lethal, deadly | adjective
