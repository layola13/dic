dwarfishnesses:/ˈdwɔːrfɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
矮小，侏儒；矮小状态
The state or quality of being dwarfish; unusually small size.
The dwarfishnesses of the bonsai trees were carefully cultivated. 这些盆景的矮小状态是精心培育的。
近义词: shortness, smallness, diminutiveness
反义词: tallness, largeness, giantness
词性: noun (plural)

dwarf:/dwɔːrf/
n.
侏儒，矮子
A person, animal, or plant that is much smaller than the usual size of its kind.
Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs  白雪公主和七个小矮人
近义词: midget, pygmy
反义词: giant
词性: noun
