disinhibited:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd/
adjective
无拘无束的；放纵的；不受拘束的
Lacking restraint or inhibition.  Free from or no longer subject to inhibition.
He became loud and disinhibited after drinking too much. 他喝多了以后变得大声喧哗，毫无顾忌。
uninhibited, unrestrained, unconstrained
inhibited, restrained, reserved
