outwalked:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːkt/
verb
胜过…的步行速度；比…走得远；走得比…久
walked farther or for a longer time than.
I easily outwalked him on the trail. 我在小路上轻松地超过了他。
outwalk, outdistance, outrun
