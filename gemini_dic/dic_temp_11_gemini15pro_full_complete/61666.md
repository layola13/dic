emitting:/ɪˈmɪtɪŋ/
|v. |发出，散发，发射|Producing and discharging something, such as light, heat, sound, or a smell.|The volcano was emitting smoke and ash. 火山正在喷发出烟雾和火山灰。|synonyms: radiating, discharging, releasing, exuding|antonyms: absorbing, concealing, suppressing|verb

emit:/ɪˈmɪt/
|v. |发出，散发，发射|Produce and discharge something, such as light, heat, sound, or a smell.|The factory emits a lot of pollution. 这家工厂排放大量污染物。|synonyms: radiate, discharge, release, exude|antonyms: absorb, conceal, suppress|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin emittere "send forth," from ex- "out" + mittere "to send."
