forestall:/fɔːrˈstɔːl/ /fɔːrˈstɑːl/
verb
预防，阻止
to prevent something from happening by acting first
The company took steps to forestall a hostile takeover.  该公司采取措施防止恶意收购。
They forestalled criticism by offering a full explanation in advance. 他们事先提供完整的解释以避免批评。
prevent, anticipate, preclude, obviate
allow, permit, encourage, facilitate
词性：动词
