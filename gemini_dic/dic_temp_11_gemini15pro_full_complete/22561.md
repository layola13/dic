boot-up:/ˈbuːt ʌp/ (US), /ˈbuːt ʌp/ (UK)| n. |启动，开机|The process of starting a computer system.|例句：The boot-up process took several minutes. 开机过程花了数分钟。

例句：A slow boot-up can be frustrating.  启动慢会让人很沮丧。

近义词：startup, start, launch|反义词:shutdown, power down|词性:noun


boot-up:/ˈbuːt ʌp/ (US), /ˈbuːt ʌp/ (UK)| v. |启动，开机|To start a computer system.|例句：I booted up my computer and checked my email. 我启动了电脑，查看了我的电子邮件。

例句：It took a while to boot up the old system. 启动旧系统花了一段时间。


近义词：start up, power on, switch on|反义词:shut down, power off, switch off|词性:verb


词源: "Boot" comes from the phrase "to pull oneself up by one's bootstraps," meaning to achieve something seemingly impossible.  In computing, it refers to the process of loading the operating system, which initially seems like a complex task for a machine to perform on its own.
