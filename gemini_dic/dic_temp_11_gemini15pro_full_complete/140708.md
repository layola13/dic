omnidirectional:/ˌɒmniˈdɪˈrekʃənəl/ | adj. | 全向的，各个方向的 | Receiving signals from or transmitting in all directions.  Able to pick up signals from or send signals in all directions. |  The security camera has an omnidirectional microphone, so it can pick up sounds from anywhere in the room.  安全摄像机有一个全向麦克风，所以它可以接收房间里任何地方的声音。

The antenna is omnidirectional, radiating power equally in all directions. 这个天线是全向的，向各个方向均匀地辐射能量。 | 近义词: all-directional, multidirectional | 反义词: directional, unidirectional | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

omni- (prefix): all, every (from Latin omnis)
directional (adjective): relating to or indicating direction
