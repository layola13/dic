grief:/ɡriːf/ | n. | 悲痛，悲伤，忧伤 | Deep sorrow, especially that caused by someone's death. | She was overcome with grief after her husband's death. 她丈夫去世后她悲痛欲绝。| synonyms: sorrow, sadness, anguish, distress | antonyms: joy, happiness, elation | noun

grief:/ɡriːf/ | v. | 使悲伤，使悲痛 | To cause grief to; to mourn for.  | It grieved her to see her children fighting. 看到她的孩子们打架让她很伤心。 | synonyms: sadden, distress, upset | antonyms: gladden, please, delight | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *grief* (noun), from *grever* "to burden, aggrieve, harm," from Late Latin *gravare* "to burden," from Latin *gravis* "heavy."
