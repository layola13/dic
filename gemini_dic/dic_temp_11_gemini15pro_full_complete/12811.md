assistful:/əˈsɪstfəl/| adj.|有帮助的；乐于助人的|Ready and willing to help; helpful.|She was very assistful in helping me organize the event. 她非常乐于助人，帮我组织了这次活动。|Synonyms: helpful, supportive, cooperative, obliging|Antonyms: unhelpful, uncooperative, obstructive|adjective

Etymology->
From assist + -ful.  "Assist" ultimately comes from Latin *assistere*, meaning "to stand by".
