nonsoberest:/nɒnˈsəʊbərɪst/ | adj. | 最不清醒的 |  This is a non-standard word formed by adding the superlative suffix "-est" to "nonsober" (meaning not sober). It implies being the furthest from a state of sobriety, or the most intoxicated. |  He was, by far, the nonsoberest person at the party. 他是聚会上迄今为止最不清醒的人。|  most intoxicated, drunkest, most inebriated | soberest, clearest | adjective
