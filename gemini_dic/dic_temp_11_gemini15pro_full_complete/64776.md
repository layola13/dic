Etymology: From Old French *esgard (“regard, look, attention”), from es- (“out”) + garder (“to guard”). Ultimately from Frankish *wardōn, from Proto-Germanic *wardōną (“to watch, guard”). Cognate with Old High German wartōn (“to watch”), Old English weardian (“to watch, guard”).
