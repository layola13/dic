deagon: /ˈdɛəɡən/ | n. | 十边形; 十角形 | A polygon with ten sides and ten angles. | Example: A regular decagon has all sides and angles equal. 正十边形的所有边和角都相等。

Example: The architect incorporated a decagon into the building's design.  这位建筑师将一个十边形融入到建筑设计中。| 近义词: ten-sided figure, ten-angled figure | 反义词:  (No direct opposite for a geometric shape) | 词性: noun
