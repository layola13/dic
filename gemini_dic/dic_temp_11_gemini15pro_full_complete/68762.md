farrago:/fəˈrɑːɡoʊ/ (US), /fəˈreɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 混乱；混杂；杂烩 | A confused mixture; a medley. |例句：The play was a farrago of clichés and predictable plot twists.  这出戏是老套桥段和可预测情节转折的大杂烩。

例句：His speech was a farrago of ill-connected ideas and unsubstantiated claims. 他的演讲是一堆毫无关联的想法和未经证实的断言的杂烩。

近义词：medley, mixture, jumble, hodgepodge, mishmash, potpourri | 反义词：order, organization, system |词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin, literally ‘mixed fodder’.
