imperiality:/ɪmˌpɪəriˈælɪti/| n.|帝国主义，帝国性|The concept of imperiality encompasses the ideology and practices of empires, including expansionism, colonialism, and domination. 帝国主义的概念包含了帝国的意识形态和实践，包括扩张主义、殖民主义和统治。|The historian analyzed the rise and fall of imperiality in the 19th century.  这位历史学家分析了19世纪帝国主义的兴衰。|近义词：imperialism, colonialism|反义词：anti-imperialism, independence|noun
