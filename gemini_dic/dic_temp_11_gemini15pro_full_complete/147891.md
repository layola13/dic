overrealistically:/ˌoʊvərˌriːəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 过于现实地，过分逼真地 |  Representing something in a way that is more real or detailed than it actually is or needs to be. | The movie portrayed the violence overrealistically, making it difficult to watch.  这部电影把暴力描绘得过于逼真，让人难以观看。 |  The video game rendered the graphics overrealistically, making the characters look almost lifelike. 这款电子游戏将画面渲染得过于逼真，使角色看起来栩栩如生。 |Synonyms: excessively realistically, hyperrealistically | Antonyms: unrealistically, fancifully | Word Type: Adverb
