arashiyama:/ɑːrɑːʃiˈjɑːmə/ | n. |嵐山 (あらしやま)|A district on the western outskirts of Kyoto, Japan, known for its scenic beauty, particularly during the cherry blossom and autumn leaf seasons.  It features the Togetsukyo Bridge, Tenryuji Temple, and bamboo groves.|  We visited Arashiyama in the autumn and the foliage was stunning. 我们在秋天参观了岚山，那里的紅葉美不胜收。|  Synonym:  scenic spot, tourist destination | Antonym: urban area | noun
