bass: /beɪs/ | n. adj. | 低音；鲈鱼 |  The lowest adult male singing voice. / A type of fish. |  The bass line of the song is very strong. 这首歌的低音线条非常强劲。/ He caught a large bass in the lake. 他在湖里钓到了一条大鲈鱼。 | 近义词：(for sound) baritone, low, deep; (for fish) perch, sea bass | 反义词：(for sound) treble, soprano, high;  | 词性: 名词, 形容词

bass: /bæs/ | n. | 低音；低音乐器；鲈鱼 |  The lowest part in musical harmony. / A double bass. / A type of fish. |  He plays bass in a rock band. 他在一个摇滚乐队里弹贝斯。/ She ordered grilled sea bass for dinner. 她点了烤海鲈鱼作为晚餐。 | 近义词：(for instrument) double bass, bass guitar; (for fish) perch, sea bass | 反义词：(for sound) treble, soprano, high;  | 词性: 名词


词源: Both pronunciations ultimately derive from the Old French word *bars*, meaning "perch" (a kind of fish). The musical sense developed later, referring to the lowest part of the harmony.
