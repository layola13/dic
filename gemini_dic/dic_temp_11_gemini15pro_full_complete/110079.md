lichenographic:/ˌlaɪkənəˈɡræfɪk/ |adj.| 地衣学的；用地衣描述的|Relating to lichenography, the study of lichens. |Example:  Lichenographic studies can reveal information about air pollution. 地衣学研究可以揭示有关空气污染的信息。

Example: The lichenographic diversity of the region is remarkable. 该地区的地衣多样性非常显著。|近义词:  cryptogamic (relating to cryptogams, a group including lichens)|反义词: N/A|词性:  adjective
