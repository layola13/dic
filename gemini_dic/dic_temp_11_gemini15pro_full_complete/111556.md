lobbyist:/ˈlɑːbiɪst/ (US) /ˈlɒbiɪst/ (UK)| n. |游说者，说客|A person who tries to influence legislation on behalf of a special interest or group.|The tobacco lobbyists spent millions trying to defeat the bill. 烟草游说者花费数百万美元试图阻止该法案通过。|Synonyms: advocate, influencer, persuader, representative|Antonyms: opponent, adversary|noun
