indiscreetly:/ˌɪndɪˈskriːtli/
adverb
轻率地，不谨慎地
in a way that is not careful about what you say or do, and that is likely to offend or upset people
She talked indiscreetly about her colleagues. 她轻率地谈论她的同事。
近义词: carelessly, thoughtlessly, unwisely, imprudently
反义词: discreetly, cautiously, prudently, carefully
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + discreet + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Discreet comes from Latin discretus, past participle of discernere (to distinguish, separate).
