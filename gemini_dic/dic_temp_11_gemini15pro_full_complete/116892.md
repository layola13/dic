mayen: /ˈmeɪən/ | n. |  Swiss mountain pasture; a mountain meadow used for grazing in summer | A mayen is a high-altitude pasture in the Swiss Alps where cattle are taken to graze during the summer months. |  The farmer led his cows up to the mayen for the summer.  农民夏天把他的牛赶到高山牧场。 | synonyms: alp, pasture, meadow | antonyms: valley, lowland | noun
