opine:/əˈpaɪn/| v. |表达意见；想；认为|To express an opinion.|He opined that the movie was overrated. 他认为这部电影被高估了.|近义词：comment, suggest, declare, assert, maintain|反义词：withhold, refrain|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin opinari (“to think, suppose, judge, be of opinion”), from opīnor (“I think”).
