collatable:/kəˈleɪtəbl/| adj.|可排序的，可整理的|Able to be collated; capable of being arranged in a proper sequence or order.|The documents are collatable and ready to be bound. 这些文件可以整理，并准备好装订。|近义词:sortable, orderable, arrangeable|反义词:uncollatable, disordered|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *collātus, past participle of collāre "to compare," from com- "together" + lātus, past participle of ferre "to bring, carry."  The English word "collate" arose in the 15th century, and "collatable" followed later.
