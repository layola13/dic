backseat:/ˈbækˌsiːt/ (US), /ˈbækˌsiːt/ (UK)| n.|后座，次要位置，不重要的地位|The children were arguing over who got to sit in the backseat. 孩子们在争论谁坐在后座。|A position of less power or influence. 指权力或影响力较小的位置。|He took a backseat in the negotiations. 他在谈判中处于次要地位。|近义词:back row, passenger seat (in a car), subordinate position|反义词:front seat, driver's seat, leading position|词性:名词

backseat:/ˈbækˌsiːt/ (US), /ˈbækˌsiːt/ (UK)| adj.|后座的|The backseat window was broken. 后座车窗破了。|Relating to or located in the back seat of a vehicle. 与车辆后座相关或位于车辆后座。|She always prefers to ride backseat. 她总是喜欢坐后座。|近义词:rear|反义词:front|词性:形容词
