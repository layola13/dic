gremlin: /ˈɡrɛmlɪn/ (US), /ˈɡrɛmlɪn/ (UK)| n. |小鬼，捣蛋鬼，（机械故障的）神秘原因|A mischievous sprite or imaginary being blamed for mechanical or electronic malfunctions, especially in aircraft.|The pilot blamed gremlins for the engine trouble. 飞行员把发动机故障归咎于小鬼捣乱。|Near synonyms: imp, sprite, goblin, elf|Antonyms: angel, guardian angel|Noun
