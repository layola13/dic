aflame:/əˈfleɪm/| adj.|燃烧的，着火的；激动的，兴奋的|Burning; ablaze; figuratively, excited or passionate.|例句：The building was aflame after the lightning strike.  大楼在雷击后着火了。

例句：Her face was aflame with excitement. 她的脸因兴奋而通红。|近义词：ablaze, burning, alight, fiery, ignited, blazing, flaming|反义词：extinguished, unlit, quenched|词性:形容词


词源: From a- + flame.  "A-" is a prefix meaning "on" or "in a state of".
