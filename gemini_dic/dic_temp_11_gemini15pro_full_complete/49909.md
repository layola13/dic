demystifying:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪɪŋ/
|v.|使不再神秘；阐明；揭秘|The book aims at demystifying the complex world of finance. 这本书旨在揭开复杂金融世界的神秘面纱。|近义词：clarifying, explaining, simplifying, elucidating|反义词：obscuring, confusing, mystifying|词性: Verb, present participle of demystify

demystify:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪ/
|vt.|使不再神秘；阐明；揭秘|The speaker tried to demystify the process of applying for a mortgage. 演讲者试图阐明申请抵押贷款的过程。|近义词：clarify, explain, simplify, elucidate|反义词：obscure, confuse, mystify|词性: Verb

Etymology:
From de- + mystify, from French mystifier, from mystique (“mystery”) + -fier (from Latin -ficāre). Ultimately from Ancient Greek μυστικός (mustikós, “secret, mystic”).
