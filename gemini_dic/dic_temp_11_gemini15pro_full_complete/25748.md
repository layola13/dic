burara: /bʊˈrɑːrɑː/
adjective
邋遢的，不整洁的 (Japanese origin)
untidy, messy, disheveled, slovenly.  This word is borrowed from Japanese.
例句：Her room is always burara. 她的房间总是乱糟糟的。

例句：He has a burara appearance.  他的外表很邋遢。

近义词：messy, untidy, slovenly, disheveled, scruffy
反义词：tidy, neat, organized, clean


词性: adjective

词源: From Japanese ぶらぶら (burubura, “dangling; idly; aimlessly”), from the sound of something dangling.
