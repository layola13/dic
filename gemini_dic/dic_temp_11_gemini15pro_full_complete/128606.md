niches:/ˈnɪtʃɪz/ /ˈniːʃɪz/
n.| 壁龛；合适的位置；商机；生态位|A shallow recess, especially one in a wall to display a statue or other ornament. A comfortable or suitable position in life or employment. A specialized segment of the market for a particular kind of product or service.  The relational position of a species or population in its ecosystem.|She found her niche in teaching. 她找到了自己在教学中的位置。|Synonyms: alcove, corner, nook, place, position, slot, market, segment|Antonyms:  misplacement, disadvantage|Noun
