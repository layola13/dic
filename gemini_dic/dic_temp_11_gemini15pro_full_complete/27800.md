cannonball:/ˈkænənˌbɔːl/| n. |炮弹；急速跳水；|A heavy metal ball fired from a cannon.  从大炮发射出的重金属球。|The cannonball whizzed past his head. 炮弹嗖地一声从他头上飞过。|Synonyms: projectile, shot, shell|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical object, could consider "peace" as an antonym in a figurative sense)|noun
cannonball:/ˈkænənˌbɔːl/| vi. |像炮弹一样跳入水中；|To jump into the water headfirst with knees bent and arms wrapped around the legs.  双膝弯曲，双臂抱住双腿，头朝下跳入水中。|He cannonballed into the pool, splashing everyone. 他像炮弹一样跳进游泳池，溅了每个人一身水。|Synonyms: plunge, dive|Antonyms: surface, emerge|verb
