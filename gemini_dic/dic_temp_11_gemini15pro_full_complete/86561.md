helichrysum:/ˌhɛlɪˈkraɪsəm/ | n. |蜡菊，永久花|A plant of the daisy family, typically with yellow, papery flowers that retain their colour when dried.  It is native to Africa, Madagascar, Australasia, and Eurasia.  The name comes from the Greek words "helios" (sun) and "chrysos" (gold), referring to the color of the flowers.|例句：Helichrysum is often used in dried flower arrangements. 蜡菊常用于干花插花。

例句：The helichrysum oil has a sweet, honey-like aroma. 蜡菊精油有一种甜美的蜂蜜香味。|近义词：strawflower, immortelle |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
