leveled:/ˈlevəld/ | adj. | 平整的，水平的，同等的，被夷为平地的 | made flat or level; even, uniform, or equal | The ground was leveled for the new building.  这块地被夷平以建造新楼。 | synonyms: flat, even, smooth, uniform, equal | antonyms: uneven, bumpy, sloping, unequal | adjective

leveled:/ˈlevəld/ | v. | 夷平，使平等，瞄准，对准 | to make something flat or level; to make something equal; to aim something (like a gun or criticism) at someone or something | He leveled the gun at the target. 他把枪瞄准了目标。 | synonyms: flatten, even, smooth, equalize, aim, direct | antonyms: raise, elevate, tilt, differentiate | verb
