lavas:/ˈlɑːvəz/| n. |熔岩（复数）|Molten rock that erupts from a volcano or fissure, or the solid rock formed when it cools and hardens.|The lavas flowed down the mountainside. 熔岩顺着山坡流了下来。|The volcano erupted, sending lavas and ash into the air. 火山爆发，将熔岩和火山灰喷向空中。|近义词：magma, molten rock|反义词:|noun
