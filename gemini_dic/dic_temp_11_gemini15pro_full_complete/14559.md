averts:/əˈvɜːrts/ /əˈvɜːrts/| v. |避免，防止，转移|turns away or prevents something from happening|例句：He averted a disaster by braking quickly. 他紧急刹车避免了一场灾难。

例句：She averted her gaze from the disturbing scene. 她把目光从令人不安的景象中移开。

近义词：prevents, avoids, forestalls, obviates, staves off|反义词:causes, incites, provokes, encourages|词性:动词
