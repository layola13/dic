nationalizing:/ˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
国有化
The act of transferring privately owned businesses or industries to government ownership and control.
The government is nationalizing the railways. 政府正在将铁路国有化。
The country nationalized all foreign-owned oil companies.  该国将所有外资石油公司国有化。
Synonyms:  socializing, collectivizing
Antonyms: privatizing, denationalizing
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From nationalize + -ing.  Nationalize comes from nation + -al + -ize.  Nation ultimately derives from Latin "natio" (birth, race, nation).
