forbearing:/fɔːrˈbeərɪŋ/
adjective
宽容的；忍耐的；克制的
patient and tolerant, especially of annoying behaviour
例句：She was forbearing with the children. 她对孩子们很宽容。
例句：He showed great forbearance in dealing with the difficult customer.  他在处理难缠的顾客时表现出了极大的耐心。
近义词：patient, tolerant, understanding, lenient, indulgent
反义词：impatient, intolerant, unforgiving, harsh, severe
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forberand*, present participle of *forberen*, from Old English *forberan* (“to forbear, refrain from, endure, tolerate”), from *for-* (prefix meaning “away, without, against”) + *beran* (“to bear, carry, endure”). Cognate with Dutch *verberen* (“to conceal, suppress”), German *ertragen* (“to bear, endure”), Gothic *����������������* (usbeiran, “to endure, bear”).
