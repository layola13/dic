beanfeast: /ˈbiːnˌfiːst/ | n. | 盛宴，宴会，庆祝活动 | A celebratory feast or party, often provided by an employer for their employees.  Originally, it referred to an annual dinner given by an employer to their workers. | The company's annual beanfeast is always a highlight of the year. 公司的年度宴会一直是一年中的亮点。|  The office beanfeast was a raucous affair. 办公室的宴会是一场喧闹的聚会。| synonyms: feast, banquet, celebration, party, bash | antonyms: wake, funeral | noun
