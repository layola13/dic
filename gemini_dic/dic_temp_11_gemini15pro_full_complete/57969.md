drist:/drɪst/ | n. |  尘土，飞尘 | Fine, powdery dust; grit. |  The wind whipped up a drist of snow. 风卷起了一阵雪尘。 | 近义词: dust, grit, powder | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑cleanliness, purity) | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse *drystr, meaning "drizzle" or "dust."  The connection likely comes from the fine, almost mist-like quality of the dust.
