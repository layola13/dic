baccivorous: /ˌbæsɪˈvɔːrəs/
adjective

The baccivorous bird flitted from branch to branch, feasting on the ripe berries.  这只食浆果的鸟在枝头跳来跳去，尽情享用成熟的浆果。

Synonyms: berry-eating, frugivorous
Antonyms: carnivorous, herbivorous (excluding berries)
word type: adjective
