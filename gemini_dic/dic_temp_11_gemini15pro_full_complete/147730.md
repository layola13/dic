overprize: /ˌoʊvərˈpraɪz/
verb
过高评价；过分重视；给予过高的评价
To value or reward excessively.
The painting was overprized at the auction, selling for three times its estimated worth. 这幅画在拍卖会上被高估了，售价是其估价的三倍。
Overprizing material possessions can lead to unhappiness. 过分重视物质财富会导致不快乐。
synonyms: overvalue, overestimate, overrate
antonyms: undervalue, underestimate, underrate
v.
