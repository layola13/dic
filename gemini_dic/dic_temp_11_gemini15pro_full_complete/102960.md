一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Je parle français. (I speak French.)
Je mange une pomme. (I eat an apple.)

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
This tense doesn't exist in French in the same way as English.  The present tense is often used with an adverb to express ongoing action.
Je suis en train de manger. (I am eating. Literally, "I am in the process of eating.")
Je lis un livre maintenant. (I am reading a book now.)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
J'ai mangé une pomme. (I have eaten an apple.)
J'ai fini mon travail. (I have finished my work.)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Similar to the present continuous, this is usually expressed with the present tense and an adverb.
Je lis depuis une heure. (I have been reading for an hour.)
Je travaille sur ce projet depuis des semaines. (I have been working on this project for weeks.)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Je parlai français. (I spoke French - literary, less common in spoken French)
J'ai parlé français. (I spoke French - common in spoken French using passé composé)


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Je lisais un livre. (I was reading a book. - imperfect tense, describes ongoing past action)
Je mangeais une pomme. (I was eating an apple. - imperfect tense)

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
J'avais mangé une pomme. (I had eaten an apple.)
J'avais fini mon travail. (I had finished my work.)

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
This is generally conveyed using the imperfect tense and an adverbial phrase.
Je lisais depuis une heure quand il est arrivé. (I had been reading for an hour when he arrived.)
Je travaillais sur ce projet depuis des semaines avant la présentation. (I had been working on this project for weeks before the presentation.)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Je parlerai français. (I will speak French.)
Je mangerai une pomme. (I will eat an apple.)

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This is usually expressed with the future tense and an adverb.
Je serai en train de manger demain à midi. (I will be eating tomorrow at noon.)
Je lirai ce livre demain soir. (I will be reading this book tomorrow night.)

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
J'aurai mangé une pomme. (I will have eaten an apple.)
J'aurai fini mon travail. (I will have finished my work.)

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
This tense doesn't have a direct equivalent and is usually conveyed with the future perfect and an adverbial phrase.
J'aurai travaillé sur ce projet pendant un an en juin prochain. (I will have been working on this project for a year next June.)
Dans deux heures, j'aurai lu pendant trois heures. (In two hours, I will have been reading for three hours.)

虚拟语气:
Il faut que je parte. (I must leave - subjunctive mood expressing necessity)
Je souhaite que tu sois heureux. (I wish you to be happy - subjunctive mood expressing a wish)

被动语态:
The passive voice is less common in French than in English.
Le repas est préparé par ma mère. (The meal is prepared by my mother.)
La chanson a été écrite par un chanteur célèbre. (The song was written by a famous singer.)

疑问句:
Est-ce que je peux entrer ? (May I come in?)
Que fais-tu ? (What are you doing?)

否定句:
Je ne parle pas français. (I do not speak French.)
Je ne mange pas de pomme. (I do not eat an apple/apples.)
