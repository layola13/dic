dowdyisms:/ˈdaʊdiɪzəmz/
noun
过时的衣着；古板的举止
Outmoded or unfashionable clothes or behavior; dowdiness.
Her dowdyisms made her look much older than she was. 她的衣着打扮让她看起来比实际年龄老得多。
近义词：frumpiness, shabbiness, dowdiness
反义词：stylishness, chic, fashionableness
词性：n.


Etymology:
From dowdy + -ism.  Dowdy itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "dowd," meaning "insignificant woman" or "dull, spiritless person."  The "-ism" suffix denotes a characteristic practice, style, or belief.
