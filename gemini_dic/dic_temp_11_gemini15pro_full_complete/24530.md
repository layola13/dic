broadens:/ˈbrɔːdnz/| v.|拓宽，扩大，变宽|Makes something wider or more extensive; increases the scope or range of something.|Travel broadens the mind. 旅行使人开阔眼界.|近义词：expands, widens, increases, extends|反义词：narrows, restricts, limits|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *brodenen*, from Old English *brādian* (“to broaden, widen, spread out”), from Proto-Germanic *brōdjaną (“to broaden”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreh₁dʰ- (“broad”).  Cognate with Scots braid (“broad”), West Frisian breidzje (“to broaden”), Dutch breiden (“to broaden, widen, knit”), German breiten (“to spread, extend”), Danish brede (“to spread”). Related to broad.
