isatide: /ˈɪsəˌtaɪd/
pronoun|等潮时，平潮期|The period of time during which the height of the tide remains stationary, occurring between high and low tide.|The boat was grounded at isatide. 船在平潮期搁浅了。|Synonym: slack water, stand of the tide|Antonym: ebb tide, flood tide|n.
