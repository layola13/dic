avadavat:/ˌævədəˈvɑːt/ | n. |红梅花雀，红雀|A small, brightly colored finch (Amandava amandava) native to India and Southeast Asia, popular as a cage bird.  It is known for its vibrant red plumage, especially in males during breeding season.|The avadavat is a popular cage bird due to its beautiful song. 红梅花雀因其优美的歌声而成为一种受欢迎的笼鸟。

The avadavat's vibrant red plumage makes it easily recognizable.  红梅花雀鲜艳的红色羽毛使它很容易被辨认。|近义词：red munia, strawberry finch|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
