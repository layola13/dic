epichordal:/ˈɛpɪˌkɔːrdl/
adjective
脊索上的，脊索背面的
Located above or dorsal to the notochord.
The epichordal cartilage is a small, paired cartilage found in some vertebrates, located dorsal to the notochord. 脊索上软骨是一些脊椎动物体内发现的小而成对的软骨，位于脊索的背面。
Synonym: dorsal to notochord
Antonym: ventral to notochord
词性: adjective
