gerbil:/ˈdʒɜːrbl/ (US), /ˈdʒɜːbɪl/ (UK)| n. |沙鼠，沙漠仓鼠|A small rodent, typically found in desert or semi-desert regions, known for its long hind legs and hopping ability. It is often kept as a pet.|例句：The gerbil escaped from its cage. 沙鼠从笼子里逃跑了。

例句：Gerbils are social animals and prefer to live in groups. 沙鼠是群居动物，喜欢群居。

近义词：desert rat, jird|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
