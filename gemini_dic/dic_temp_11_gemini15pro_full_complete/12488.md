aspiration:/ˌæspəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 抱负，渴望；吸气 | A strong desire to achieve something high or great; the action of drawing breath. |  He has aspirations to become a doctor. 他渴望成为一名医生。 | synonyms: ambition, goal, desire, yearning, inhalation | antonyms: apathy, indifference, exhalation | noun

aspiration:/ˌæspəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 送气音 |  The pronunciation of a consonant with a strong puff of air, as in the English sounds /p/, /t/, and /k/. | The word "cat" begins with an aspirated consonant.  单词“cat”以送气辅音开头。| synonyms:  plosive | antonyms: unaspirated | noun
