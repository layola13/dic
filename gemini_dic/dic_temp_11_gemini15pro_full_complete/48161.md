decommissioned:/ˌdiːkəˈmɪʃənd/
v.|使退役，使停止使用|To officially take something out of service, especially military equipment or a nuclear power station.|The battleship was decommissioned in 1992. 这艘战列舰于1992年退役。|retired, withdrawn, deactivated|commissioned, activated|verb


decommissioned:/ˌdiːkəˈmɪʃənd/
adj.|退役的，停止使用的|Officially taken out of service.|The decommissioned power plant is being dismantled.  这已退役的发电厂正在被拆除。|retired, obsolete, defunct|operational, active|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + commission (verb meaning "to give someone authority to do something").
