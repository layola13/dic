melanesia:/ˌmɛləˈniːʒə/ | n. | 美拉尼西亚 |A subregion of Oceania extending from New Guinea island in the southwest to the Arafura Sea, and eastward to Fiji.  It includes the countries of Papua New Guinea, Fiji, Solomon Islands, Vanuatu, and New Caledonia (France).|The unique cultures of Melanesia are fascinating to study.  美拉尼西亚独特的文化值得研究。|Melanesia is known for its diverse languages and traditions. 美拉尼西亚以其多样的语言和传统而闻名。| 近义词: (No direct synonym, could use "South Pacific Islands" in some contexts) | 反义词:  None | noun
