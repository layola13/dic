fungi:/ˈfʌŋɡaɪ/ or /ˈfʌndʒaɪ/| n. | 真菌（复数） | Plural form of fungus. | Example: Many fungi are beneficial to the environment. 许多真菌对环境有益。| Synonym: molds, mildews, yeasts (for certain types)| Antonym: (No direct antonym) | Noun
