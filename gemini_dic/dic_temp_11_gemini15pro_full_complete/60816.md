electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪt/ /ɪˈlɛktroʊˌpleɪt/| v. n. adj. |电镀；镀层|To coat (a metal) with another metal by electrolysis.  To cover with a thin layer of metal using electricity.|  We electroplated the spoons with silver. 我们给勺子镀了银。 |近义词：plate, coat, cover |反义词:strip, uncover| verb

electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪt/ /ɪˈlɛktroʊˌpleɪt/| v. n. adj. |电镀；镀层|A thin coating of metal deposited by electroplating.| The electroplate on the faucet was starting to peel. 水龙头上的镀层开始剥落。|近义词：plating, coating|反义词:base metal| noun

electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪt/ /ɪˈlɛktroʊˌpleɪt/| v. n. adj. |电镀的|Made by electroplating; plated.|  an electroplate teapot. 一个电镀的茶壶。|近义词：plated|反义词:unplated| adjective
