busbars:/ˈbʌsbɑːrz/ (US), /ˈbʌsbɑːz/ (UK)| n.|汇流条|A metallic conductor, typically copper or aluminum, that serves as a common connection for multiple circuits within an electrical distribution system.  It is designed to carry a substantial current and distribute it to various branches of the circuit.|The busbars in the power distribution panel were carefully inspected for signs of overheating.|Synonyms: conductor bar, distribution bar, bus|Antonyms: insulator|noun
