asterisk: /ˈæstərɪsk/ (US), /ˈæstərɪsk/ (UK) | n. | 星号；星形符 | A symbol (*) used to mark a footnote, omission, or other reference. |  Example: Please see the table below for details, marked with an asterisk*.  请参阅下表，详情标有星号。

Example:  He added an asterisk to the page to indicate a footnote. 他在页面上添加了一个星号以指示脚注。 | 近义词: star, marker | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a symbol) | 词性: noun
