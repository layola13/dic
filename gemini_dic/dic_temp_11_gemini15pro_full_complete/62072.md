enceladus:/ɛnˈsɛlədəs/| n.|土卫二|A moon of Saturn, known for its icy surface and geysers that erupt water vapor and other volatiles.|例句：Enceladus is a fascinating moon for scientists to study. 土卫二是科学家们研究的一颗引人入胜的卫星。

例句：The geysers on Enceladus suggest the possibility of a subsurface ocean. 土卫二上的间歇泉表明其地下可能存在海洋。|近义词：Saturnian moon, icy moon|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Latin *Enceladus*, from Ancient Greek Ἐγκέλαδος (*Enkélados*), one of the Gigantes in Greek mythology.
