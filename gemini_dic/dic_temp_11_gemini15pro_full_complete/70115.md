fey:/feɪ/
adjective
给予超自然力量的；命中注定的；古怪的；快要死的
giving an impression of vague unworldliness and mystery, especially suggesting a magical quality; fated to die; whimsical; otherworldly; doomed

She had a fey, ethereal beauty. 她有一种神秘飘渺的奇异之美。

He gave a fey smile. 他露出了一个古怪的笑容。

There was a fey quality to her beauty. 她的美带有一种超凡脱俗的气质。

近义词: whimsical, ethereal, otherworldly, elfin, magical, enchanted, strange, eccentric, quirky
反义词: mundane, ordinary, earthly, realistic
