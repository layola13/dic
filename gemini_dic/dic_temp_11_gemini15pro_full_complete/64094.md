eponymically:/ˌɛpəˈnɪmɪkli/
adv.
以名命名地; 与名字有关地
Relating to or named after a person or thing, often a fictional character or brand name.
The disease was eponymously named after the doctor who first discovered it.  这种疾病以第一个发现它的医生的名字命名。
近义词:nominally, titularly
反义词:anonymously
词性:副词

Etymology:
From eponym + -ic + -al + -ly.  Eponym comes from the Greek *epónymos*, meaning "giving a name," from *epí* ("upon") + *ónyma* ("name").
