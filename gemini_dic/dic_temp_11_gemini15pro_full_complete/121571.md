misnavigation:/ˌmɪsnævɪˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 错误导航，导航错误 | The misnavigation of the ship led them off course. 船舶的导航错误使他们偏离了航线。| The misnavigation caused the drone to crash. 导航错误导致无人机坠毁。 | 近义词：misdirection, misguidance | 反义词：correct navigation, proper guidance | 词性: 名词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "navigation" (from Latin "navigationem," noun of action from past participle stem of "navigare" "to sail, navigate," from "navis" "ship" + "-igare" frequentative suffix of uncertain origin)
