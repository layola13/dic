nonadaptational:/ˌnɒnˌædæpˈteɪʃənl/ | adj. | 非适应性的 | Not related to adaptation; not contributing to survival or reproductive success. | The peacock's elaborate tail feathers might be a nonadaptational byproduct of other genetic changes.  孔雀精心制作的尾羽可能是非适应性基因变化的副产品。

The bright coloration of some poisonous frogs could be a nonadaptational result of their diet.  一些毒蛙的鲜艳颜色可能是它们饮食的非适应性结果。| Near synonyms: maladaptive, non-adaptive | Antonyms: adaptive, adaptational | word type: adjective
