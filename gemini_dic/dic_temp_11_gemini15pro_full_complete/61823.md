empiricalness:/ɪmˈpɪrɪkəlnəs/
|n.|经验性，实证性|The empiricalness of the study's findings is unquestionable. 这项研究结果的经验性毋庸置疑。|例句：The empiricalness of his approach ensured reliable results. 他的经验方法确保了结果的可靠性。  The scientist emphasized the empiricalness of their research, relying solely on observable data.  这位科学家强调他们研究的经验性，完全依赖于可观察的数据。|近义词：factuality, practicality, observability|反义词：theoreticity, abstractness|词性:名词

empirical:/ɪmˈpɪrɪkəl/
|adj.|经验主义的，以经验为依据的|Empirical evidence supports the theory. 经验证据支持该理论。|例句：The study was based on empirical data collected over a period of five years. 这项研究基于五年来收集的经验数据。 Empirical observation is crucial in scientific research. 在科学研究中，经验观察至关重要。|近义词：experiential, observed, practical|反义词：theoretical, hypothetical|词性:形容词
