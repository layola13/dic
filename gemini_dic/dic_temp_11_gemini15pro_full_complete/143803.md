outorganizing: /ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
胜过组织；在组织方面超越
surpassing in organizing; organizing more effectively than.
Company A is outorganizing Company B by implementing a more efficient supply chain.  公司A通过实施更有效的供应链，在组织方面胜过公司B。
The new startup is outorganizing its established competitors. 这家新兴公司在组织方面超越了其老牌竞争对手。
近义词: outmaneuvering, outperforming, surpassing
反义词: disorganizing, mismanaging
