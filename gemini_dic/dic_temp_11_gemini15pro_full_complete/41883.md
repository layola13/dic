coshering: /ˈkɒʃərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkoʊʃərɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 犹太教的洁食屠宰；犹太教的洁食认证|The act of preparing food according to Jewish dietary laws.  It involves slaughtering animals and processing food in a specific manner to ensure it is kosher.|例句：The coshering of the meat took several hours.  肉的洁食处理花了几个小时。

例句：He learned the art of coshering from his father. 他从他父亲那里学到了洁食屠宰的技艺。


近义词：Shechita (specifically for slaughtering), Kashering (can be used interchangeably),  Ritual slaughter|反义词: Trefa (non-kosher), Unkosher|词性:noun
