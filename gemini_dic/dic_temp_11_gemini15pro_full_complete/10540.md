appraised:/əˈpreɪzd/
v. |估价，评价|To assess the value or quality of something.
|The antique vase was appraised at $5,000.  这个古董花瓶被估价为5000美元。|
|The teacher appraised the student's work. 老师评价了学生的作品。|
|assess, evaluate, estimate|
|criticize, condemn|
