moodier:/ˈmuːdiər/ | adj. | 心情更易变化的，更加喜怒无常的 | Comparative form of "moody," meaning more susceptible to changes in mood, more temperamental. | He's been much moodier lately, snapping at everyone.  他最近情绪更加反复无常，对每个人都大发脾气。| The weather has been moodier than usual this spring. 今年春天天气比往常更加变化无常。 |  近义词: more temperamental, more irritable, more sullen | 反义词: more cheerful, more stable, more even-tempered | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "moody" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Moody" comes from Middle English "modi," meaning "proud, haughty," later evolving to its current meaning related to fluctuating moods.
