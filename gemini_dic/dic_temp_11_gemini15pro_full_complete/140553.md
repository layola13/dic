olibanum:/ˈoʊlɪbænəm/ /ˈɒlɪbænəm/| n. |乳香|A fragrant gum resin obtained from certain trees and burnt as incense.|The air was thick with the scent of olibanum.|近义词: frankincense|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *olibanum*, from Old French *oliban*, from Medieval Latin *olibanum*, from Late Latin *libanum*, from Greek *λίβανος (líbanos)*.
