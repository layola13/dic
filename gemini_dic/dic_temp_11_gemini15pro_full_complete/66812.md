expanding: /ɪkˈspændɪŋ/
adjective
扩展的，扩大的，膨胀的
Increasing in size, range, or amount.
The company is expanding its operations into new markets.  公司正在将其业务扩展到新的市场。
The universe is constantly expanding. 宇宙在不断膨胀。
近义词: growing, increasing, enlarging, developing, stretching, widening, spreading, burgeoning
反义词: shrinking, contracting, diminishing, decreasing, reducing
