as-yet:/ˌæz ˈjɛt/ | adj. | 至今，迄今为止 |  Used to say that something has not happened up to the present time, but that it is expected to happen in the future. |  例句：The as-yet unpublished report is highly critical.  至今尚未发表的报告是高度批判性的。 | 近义词：still, so far, hitherto, thus far | 反义词: already, now | 词性: adjective
