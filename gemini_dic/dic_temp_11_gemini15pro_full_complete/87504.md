heteroclite:/ˈhɛtərəˌklaɪt/| adj. n.|不规则的，不规则变化的；不规则名词或动词|Deviating from common forms or rules; irregular; anomalous.  A word that deviates from the normal pattern of inflection.|This archaic verb form is a true heteroclite.  这个古老的动词形式是一个真正的不规则变化。|Synonyms: anomalous, abnormal, irregular, atypical, aberrant, deviant, eccentric, unconventional, unorthodox, idiosyncratic|Antonyms: regular, normal, standard, typical, conventional|adjective
heteroclite:/ˈhɛtərəˌklaɪt/| n.|不规则名词或动词|A noun or verb that does not follow the regular pattern of inflection.|The word "children" is a heteroclite. “children”这个词是一个不规则名词。|Synonyms: anomaly, irregularity|Antonyms: regularity|noun
