cottrel:/ˈkɒtrəl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtrəl/ (US)| n. | 铁环扣；钩环；系链 |A metal loop or ring, especially one used for fastening or attaching something.  It can also refer to a shackle used to restrain a prisoner.| The cottrel on the dog's collar broke, and he ran away. 狗项圈上的铁环断了，它跑了。|Synonyms: ring, loop, shackle, fastener |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "release" in the context of restraint)|noun
