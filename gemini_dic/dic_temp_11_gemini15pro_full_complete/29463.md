catalyzes:/ˈkætəlaɪzɪz/
verb
催化
to cause or accelerate (a reaction) by acting as a catalyst
例句：The enzyme catalyzes the breakdown of starch into sugars. 这种酶催化淀粉分解成糖。

例句：Sunlight catalyzes the production of vitamin D in the skin. 阳光催化皮肤中维生素D的产生。

近义词：accelerates, facilitates, promotes, speeds up
反义词：inhibits, hinders, retards, slows down
词性：v.
