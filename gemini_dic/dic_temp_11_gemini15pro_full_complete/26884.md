cakewalking:/ˈkeɪkwɔːkɪŋ/| n. adj. |轻而易举的事，容易的事；轻松的胜利|Something that is very easy to do or achieve.|Winning the competition was a cakewalk for her.  对她来说，赢得比赛轻而易举。|synonyms: easy, simple, effortless, a breeze, a cinch, a piece of cake|antonyms: difficult, challenging, arduous, strenuous|noun, adjective

cakewalk:/ˈkeɪkwɔːkɪŋ/| n. |一种舞蹈|A strutting dance performed by couples, popular among African Americans in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, often judged for prizes.|They performed a lively cakewalk at the community celebration. 他们在社区庆祝活动上表演了一段活泼的Cakewalk舞。|synonyms: dance, strut|antonyms: N/A|noun
