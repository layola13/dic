kitu:/ˈkiːtuː/| n.|东西，物品(Swahili)|A thing or object.  This word is borrowed from Swahili. |例句：Lete kitu hicho hapa. (Swahili) Bring that thing here. 把那东西带到这里来。

例句：Nina kitu cha kukwambia. (Swahili) I have something to tell you. 我有件事要告诉你。|近义词：thing, item, object|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

注意事项:
