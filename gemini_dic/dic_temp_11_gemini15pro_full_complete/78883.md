ginned:/dʒɪnd/
adjective|轧过的，去籽的|separated from the seeds, as cotton fiber.|The ginned cotton was ready to be spun into yarn. 轧过的棉花准备纺成纱线。|例句：The farmer sold his ginned cotton to the textile mill. 农民把轧过的棉花卖给了纺织厂。

例句：Ginned cotton is much easier to process than raw cotton.  轧过的棉花比原棉更容易加工。

近义词：processed, cleaned|反义词:unginned, raw|词性:adjective
词源:From the past participle of *gin*, which itself comes from engine.  Originally referred to the machine used for separating cotton fibers from their seeds.
