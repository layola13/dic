gavotted:/ɡævˈɒtɪd/ | v. |加沃特舞步移动，跳加沃特舞|Moved or danced in the manner of a gavotte. |She gavotted across the room, her skirt swirling around her. 她跳着加沃特舞步穿过房间，裙子在她周围旋转。| The dancers gavotted gracefully to the music. 舞者们优雅地跳着加沃特舞，伴着音乐。|近义词：danced, skipped, pranced|反义词：walked, plodded|词性: 动词

Etymology: From French *gavotter*, frequentative of *gavot*, from Gavot, a native of the Pays de Gap in the Dauphiné, whose dance this was.
