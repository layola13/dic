liaises:/liˈeɪzɪz/| v.|联络，联系，协调|Acts as a link between two or more groups, organizations, or individuals.|She liaises with the marketing team to ensure the project is on track. 她与营销团队联络，以确保项目按计划进行.|Synonyms: coordinates, communicates, connects, networks|Antonyms: separates, isolates, divides|Verb (third-person singular simple present liaises, present participle liaising, simple past and past participle liaised)

liaison:/liˈeɪzɒn/ /liˈeɪzən/| n.|联络，联系，联络人|Communication or cooperation which facilitates a close working relationship between people or organizations.|He served as a liaison between the two warring factions. 他充当了两个交战派别之间的联络人。|Synonyms: contact, intermediary, link, connection|Antonyms: separation, division|Noun
