gelatification:/dʒəˌlætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
胶凝化，凝胶化
The process of becoming a gel.  The transformation of a sol to a gel.
The gelatification of the mixture occurred after it cooled. 混合物冷却后发生了胶凝化。
gelation, solidification
liquefaction, melting
noun
