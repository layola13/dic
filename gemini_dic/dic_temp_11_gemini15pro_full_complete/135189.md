nonorganic:/ˌnɒnɔːrˈɡænɪk/ | adj. | 非有机的，无机的 | Not produced by or involving living organisms.  Relating to or denoting compounds not containing carbon (other than carbonates, bicarbonates, and cyanides). |  Example: Nonorganic fertilizers can deplete the soil over time.  非有机肥料会随着时间的推移耗尽土壤肥力。

Example:  The artist used nonorganic materials like plastic and metal in her sculpture.  这位艺术家在她的雕塑中使用了塑料和金属等非有机材料。 | 近义词: inorganic, synthetic, artificial | 反义词: organic, natural | 词性: adjective
