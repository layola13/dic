indeed:/ɪnˈdiːd/ | adv. | 的确，确实 | used to emphasize a statement or response |  It was a very good book indeed. 这确实是一本好书。| 近义词: truly, certainly, actually, really | 反义词: doubtfully, questionably | 词性:副词

indeed:/ɪnˈdiːd/ | adv. | 甚至，而且 | used to introduce a further and stronger point |  He was very tired, indeed he was exhausted. 他非常累，事实上他已经筋疲力尽了。| 近义词: furthermore, moreover | 反义词: however | 词性:副词

词源: From Old English *in dede* "in deed, in fact," from *in* "in" + *dēd* "deed."
