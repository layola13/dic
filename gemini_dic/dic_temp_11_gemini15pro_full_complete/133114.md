nonerudition:/ˌnɒnˌɛrʊˈdɪʃən/
n.
lack of learning; ignorance
The student's nonerudition was evident in his inability to answer even the simplest questions.  这个学生缺乏学识，即使是最简单的问题也无法回答。
His nonerudition on the subject was surprising. 他对这个主题的无知令人惊讶。
Near synonyms: ignorance, illiteracy, unlearnedness, lack of knowledge
Antonyms: erudition, knowledge, learnedness, scholarship
