gutturize:/ˈɡʌtəˌraɪz/ | v. | 使发喉音；用喉音发音 | To pronounce a sound using the throat as the primary articulator.  |  The speaker gutturized the consonants in the word, giving it a harsh sound.  说话者用喉音发音了这个词中的辅音，使它听起来很刺耳。 |  Synonym: gutturalize | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "pronounce clearly") | verb

gutturized:/ˈɡʌtəˌraɪzd/ | adj. |  用喉音发音的 | Pronounced using the throat as the primary articulator. | The gutturalized consonants gave the language a distinctive quality.  用喉音发音的辅音赋予了这门语言一种独特的特质。 | Synonym: guttural | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | adjective

词源： From Late Latin *gutturālis, from Latin guttur ("throat").
