menders:/ˈmɛndərz/
| n. |修理工，修补者|People who mend things; those who repair or fix something.|The menders worked tirelessly to repair the damaged bridge. 修理工们不知疲倦地修补被毁坏的桥梁。|近义词：repairers, fixers, restorers|反义词：breakers, destroyers|词性:名词

词源:
From mend (v.) + -er (suffix forming agent nouns).  "Mend" comes from the Old English *emendan, from Proto-Germanic *andamjaną (“to amend, correct”), from *anda- (“opposite, against”) + *manjaną (“to think, remember”).  The "-er" suffix signifies a person who performs the action of the verb.
