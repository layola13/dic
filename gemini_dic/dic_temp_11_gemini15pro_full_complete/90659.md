humbaba:/ˈhʊmbɑːbɑː/ /ˈhʌmbɑːbɑː/
noun|胡巴巴|A monstrous giant of immense strength and ferocity in Mesopotamian mythology, especially known from the Epic of Gilgamesh. He is the guardian of the Cedar Forest and is slain by Gilgamesh and Enkidu.|Gilgamesh and Enkidu faced the terrible Humbaba in the Cedar Forest. 吉尔伽美什和恩奇都雪松林里遭遇了可怕的胡巴巴。|Synonyms: monster, giant, ogre|Antonyms: hero, protector|n.
