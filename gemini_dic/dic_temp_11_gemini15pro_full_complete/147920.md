overregulations:/ˌoʊvərˌreɡjʊˈleɪʃənz/
noun
过度监管
Excessive regulations or rules.
The overregulations in the industry stifle innovation.  这个行业的过度监管扼杀了创新。
The city's overregulations are driving businesses away. 城市的过度监管正在赶走企业。
近义词: overgovernance, excessive regulation
反义词: deregulation, underregulation
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "regulation" (from Latin "regulare," to control).  "over-"（前缀，意为“过度的，太多的”）+ "regulation"（源自拉丁语 "regulare"，控制）。
