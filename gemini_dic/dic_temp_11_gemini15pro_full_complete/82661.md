guerrillas: /ɡəˈrɪləz/ /ɡəˈriləz/ | n. | 游击队员；游击队 | Members of an irregular military force, often fighting against a larger, more established army. |  The guerrillas ambushed the government troops in the mountains. 游击队员在山里伏击了政府军。

The guerrillas were hiding in the dense jungle.  游击队员藏在茂密的丛林里。

| 近义词: partisans, insurgents, freedom fighters | 反义词: regular army, conventional forces | 词性: 名词
