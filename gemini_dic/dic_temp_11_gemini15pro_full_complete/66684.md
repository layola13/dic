exogen:/ˈeksədʒən/| n. |外生变量；外生基因|Something that originates externally.  A variable whose value is determined outside the model. A gene originating in another organism.|例句：The model uses rainfall as an exogen.  这个模型使用降雨量作为外生变量。

例句：The exogen was introduced into the plant's genome.  这个外生基因被引入到植物的基因组中。|近义词：external factor, independent variable|反义词：endogen, internal factor|词性:noun
