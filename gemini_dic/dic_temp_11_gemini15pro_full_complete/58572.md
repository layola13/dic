dulcetness:/ˈdʌlsɪtnəs/ | n. | 甜美，悦耳，动听 | The state or quality of being dulcet; sweetness of sound. | The dulcetness of her voice soothed the crying baby. 她甜美的声音安抚了哭闹的婴儿。 | Synonyms: sweetness, melodiousness, euphony | Antonyms: harshness, dissonance, cacophony | noun

Etymology:  From Middle English *dulcetnes, from dulcet +‎ -ness.  Dulcet itself comes from Old French doucet "sweet," from Vulgar Latin *dulcitius, a diminutive of Latin dulcis "sweet."
