outsourcing:/ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋ/
n.
外包；业务外包
The practice of obtaining goods or services from an external supplier, often in another country, instead of producing them internally.
Many companies are outsourcing their IT operations to save money. 许多公司将IT业务外包以节省成本。
outsource:/ˈaʊtsɔːrs/
v.
外包；将…外包
To obtain goods or services from an external supplier, often in another country, instead of producing them internally.
The company decided to outsource its customer service operations.  这家公司决定将其客户服务业务外包。
offshoring, subcontracting
insourcing
noun, verb
