iine:/iːne/ | adj. |好的，不错的 (Japanese slang) | "Iine" is a Japanese slang word derived from "ii ne," which literally translates to "good, isn't it?" It's used online and in casual conversation to express approval or liking, similar to "good," "nice," or "cool" in English.  It's frequently used in social media as a way to express a "like." | Example 1:  A: I got a new bike!  B: Iine! (不错！) | Example 2: This cake is iine! (这个蛋糕真不错！)| 近义词: good, nice, cool, great, awesome (English),  いいね (ii ne - Japanese) | 反义词: bad, terrible, awful | 词性: adjective (used as an interjection)
