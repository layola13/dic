introverting: /ɪntrəˈvɜːtɪŋ/ | v. | 使内向；使内翻；使反转 | Turning something inwards or making someone more introverted. |  She found the constant social interaction introverting and longed for some solitude. 她发现不断的社交互动让她变得内向，渴望独处。

The design of the building, with its small windows and enclosed spaces, felt introverting. 这栋建筑的设计，配上小窗户和封闭的空间，让人感觉内向。| 近义词：internalizing, withdrawing | 反义词：extroverting | 词性: 动词
