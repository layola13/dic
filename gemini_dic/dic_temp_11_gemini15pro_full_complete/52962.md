dingiest:/ˈdɪndʒɪɪst/| adj.|最肮脏的，最昏暗的|Superlative adjective of dingy: most dingy.  It means extremely gloomy, dirty, and shabby.|The dingiest corner of the basement was filled with cobwebs. 地下室最肮脏的角落里满是蜘蛛网。|Synonyms: grimiest, filthiest, shabbiest, darkest, gloomiest|Antonyms: brightest, cleanest, tidiest, most immaculate|adjective
