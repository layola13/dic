arbiters:/ˈɑːrbɪtərz/ (US) /ˈɑːbɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|仲裁人，裁决人，权威人士|A person who settles a dispute or has ultimate authority in a matter.|The arbiters of fashion have decided that purple is the color this spring. 时尚界的权威人士已经决定紫色是今年春天的流行色。|Synonyms: judge, mediator, umpire, referee, adjudicator|Antonyms: disputant, litigant|noun

arbiters:/ˈɑːrbɪtərz/ (US) /ˈɑːbɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|仲裁者，公断人|Someone chosen to settle a disagreement.|The two countries chose an independent body of arbiters to resolve the border dispute. 这两个国家选择了一个独立的仲裁机构来解决边界争端。|Synonyms: judge, mediator, arbitrator|Antonyms: disputant|noun
