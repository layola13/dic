montanist:/ˈmɒntənɪst/ | n. | 蒙坦主义者；孟他努斯派信徒 | A follower of Montanus, a 2nd-century Phrygian prophet who claimed to have direct revelations from the Holy Spirit, emphasizing ecstatic prophecy and ascetic practices. |  The Montanists believed in the imminent Second Coming of Christ.  蒙坦主义者相信基督即将第二次降临。| Synonyms:  prophet, enthusiast, sectarian | Antonyms:  orthodox, conventional | noun

montanistic:/ˌmɒntəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 蒙坦主义的；孟他努斯派的 | Of or relating to Montanism, a 2nd-century Christian sect. |  The Montanistic movement was characterized by its emphasis on prophecy and asceticism. 蒙坦主义运动的特点是强调预言和禁欲主义。 | Synonyms: prophetic, ecstatic, ascetic | Antonyms: orthodox, conventional | adjective
