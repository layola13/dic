antioptimists:/ˌæntiˈɒptɪmɪsts/ | n. |反乐观主义者；悲观主义者|  A person who expects things to turn out badly; a pessimist. | The antioptimists predicted the company would fail, but it thrived. 反乐观主义者预测公司会倒闭，但它却蓬勃发展。| Near synonyms: pessimists, cynics | Antonyms: optimists, idealists | Noun
