bear:/ber/ (US) /beə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |熊; 忍受; 承担; 结果实; 生（孩子）|A large, heavy mammal with thick fur and sharp claws.  To carry or support the weight of something. To endure or tolerate something difficult. To produce or give birth to. |例句：The bear hibernates in winter. 熊在冬天冬眠。/  She couldn't bear the pain any longer. 她再也无法忍受疼痛了。|近义词：(n.) grizzly, polar bear, panda; (v.) endure, tolerate, carry, support |反义词: (v.) refuse, reject|词性:名词,动词

bear:/ber/ (US) /beə(r)/ (UK)| v. |结果实|To produce fruit or other crops. |例句：The apple tree bears fruit every year. 这棵苹果树每年都结果。 / The tree bore many apples this year. 这棵树今年结了很多苹果. |近义词：produce, yield|反义词: barren|词性:动词
