didelphid:/daɪˈdɛlfɪd/ | n. |负鼠；负鼠科动物|A marsupial mammal of the family Didelphidae, found in the Americas and characterized by a prehensile tail, pointed snout, and, in some species, a pouch.|例句：The didelphid used its prehensile tail to hang from the tree branch.  负鼠用它能缠绕的尾巴挂在树枝上。

例句：Researchers studied the behavior of several didelphid species in the Amazon rainforest. 研究人员研究了亚马逊雨林中几种负鼠的行为。|近义词：opossum, possum |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Didelphis, genus name, from Ancient Greek  δίς (dís, “twice, double”) + δελφύς (delphús, “womb, uterus”).  Referring to the double uterus characteristic of marsupials.
