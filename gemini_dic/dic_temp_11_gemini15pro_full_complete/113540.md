macabre:/məˈkɑːbrə/ /məˈkɑːbri/| adj.|可怕的，恐怖的，令人毛骨悚然的|Suggesting the horror of death and decay; gruesome.|例句：The movie was filled with macabre imagery of ghosts and zombies. 这部电影充满了鬼魂和僵尸的恐怖意象。

例句：She told a macabre story about a haunted house. 她讲了一个关于鬼屋的恐怖故事。|近义词：gruesome, morbid, ghastly, eerie, creepy|反义词：pleasant, cheerful, delightful|词性: 形容词

Etymology: French, from *Macabré, perhaps from danse Macabré, Dance of Death, perhaps of unknown origin, perhaps from Arabic maqābir cemeteries
