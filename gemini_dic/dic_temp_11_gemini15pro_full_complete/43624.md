creates: /kriˈeɪts/
verb
创造，创作；产生，引起
brings something new into existence; causes something to happen
The artist creates beautiful sculptures.  艺术家创作美丽的雕塑。
The new law creates more problems than it solves. 新法律带来的问题比它解决的问题更多。
synonyms: produces, generates, makes, forms, originates, composes, designs, invents, builds, constructs
antonyms: destroys, demolishes, ruins, eliminates, eradicates
词性: 动词 (verb)

词源:
From Middle English *creat, from Latin creō (“I create, produce, bring forth, beget, make”), from Proto-Italic *kreō, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- (“to grow”).
