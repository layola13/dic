nonconsultatory:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌltətəri/
adjective
非咨询性的；未经协商的
Not involving consultation; made or done without consultation.
The CEO made a nonconsultatory decision that angered the board members. 首席执行官做出了一个未经协商的决定，激怒了董事会成员。
The government imposed nonconsultatory regulations on the industry. 政府对该行业施加了未经协商的法规。
Synonyms: undemocratic, arbitrary, unilateral, high-handed
Antonyms: consultative, collaborative, democratic, participatory
词性: 形容词
