gossipy: /ˈɡɒsɪpi/ (UK), /ˈɡɑːsəpi/ (US) | adj. | 爱说闲话的；爱聊八卦的 | Fond of gossip; given to spreading rumors or talking about the private matters of others. | She's always been a bit gossipy, but she's never been malicious. 她一直有点爱说闲话，但她从没恶意中伤过别人。|  He has a gossipy nature and can't keep a secret. 他天生爱八卦，守不住秘密。 | Synonyms: chatty, talkative, nosy, inquisitive | Antonyms: discreet, reserved, reticent | adjective
