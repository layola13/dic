macs:/mæks/| n.|媒体访问控制地址（Media Access Control Address）|A Media Access Control address (MAC address) is a unique identifier assigned to network interfaces for communications on the data link layer of a network segment.|Every network device has a unique MAC address. 每个网络设备都有一个唯一的MAC地址。|近义词：physical address, hardware address|反义词: None|noun
