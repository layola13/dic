aming: /ˈeɪmɪŋ/
|v.| 瞄准; 针对; 力求达到; | aiming is the present participle of the verb "aim". It means directing or positioning something (like a weapon or a remark) towards a target, or striving to achieve a specific goal. |He was aiming a rifle at the target. 他正用步枪瞄准目标。| synonyms: targeting, directing, striving, intending | antonyms: ignoring, neglecting
|verb|
