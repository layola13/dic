misspender:/mɪˈspendər/| n. |挥霍者，浪费者|A person who spends money wastefully or unwisely. |He was a misspender of his talents and opportunities. 他浪费了自己的才华和机会。|Synonyms: squanderer, prodigal, waster|Antonyms: saver, economizer|Noun


misspend:/mɪsˈspend/| v. |浪费，挥霍|To spend (time or money) unwisely or wastefully.|He misspent his youth in idle pursuits. 他虚度了青春。|Synonyms: squander, waste, dissipate|Antonyms: save, conserve, economize|Verb
