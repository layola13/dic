auricled:/ˈɔːrɪkld/ /ˈɑːrɪkld/| adj.|有耳廓的；有耳状突起的|Having auricles or ear-shaped appendages.  Relating to or resembling an auricle, especially of the heart.|The auricled tree frog has distinctive ear-like projections on its head.  这种有耳廓的树蛙头部有独特的耳状突起。|Synonyms: eared, auriculate|Antonyms: earless|adjective
