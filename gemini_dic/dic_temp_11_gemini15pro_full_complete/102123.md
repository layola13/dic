isotrimorphic:/ˌaɪsoʊtraɪˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 同质三形的；同晶三型的 | Having three crystal forms that are chemically identical but have different physical properties, such as different melting points or densities. | Example:  Sulfur is isotrimorphic, exhibiting rhombic, monoclinic, and amorphous forms. 硫是同质三形的，表现为斜方晶系、单斜晶系和无定形三种形式。| Example: Isotrimorphic substances can be challenging to identify through simple observation. 同晶三型的物质很难通过简单的观察来识别。 | Synonym:  trimorphic (though less specific) | Antonym:  monomorphic | word type: adjective
