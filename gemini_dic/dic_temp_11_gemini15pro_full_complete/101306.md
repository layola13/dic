inward:/ˈɪnwərd/ (US), /ˈɪnwəd/ (UK)| adj. adv.|向内的，内心的；内向地，向内|directed or proceeding toward the inside; relating to the mind or spirit|She felt an inward sense of peace. 她感到内心平静。|synonyms: inner, internal, inside, interior|antonyms: outward, external, outside, exterior|adjective

inward:/ˈɪnwərd/ (US), /ˈɪnwəd/ (UK)| adv.|向内，在内|towards the inside|The door opens inward. 门向内开。|synonyms: inwardly, internally|antonyms: outwardly, externally|adverb

inwards:/ˈɪnwədz/ (UK)| adv.|向内|towards the inside (chiefly British)|The door opens inwards.  门向内开。|synonyms: inward, inwardly, internally|antonyms: outwards, outwardly, externally|adverb
