backstreet:/ˈbækˌstriːt/ (US), /ˈbækˌstriːt/ (UK)| n. |后街，偏僻街道|A narrow, quiet street, typically one located away from the main thoroughfares of a town or city.|例句：We parked in a backstreet to avoid the traffic. 我们把车停在一条后街上以避开交通拥堵。|例句：The shop is located on a quiet backstreet. 这家商店位于一条安静的后街上。|近义词：alley, side street, lane|反义词：main street, avenue|词性:名词

Etymology:
From 'back' + 'street'. 'Back' in this context implies something secondary or less important than the 'main' street.
