needn't:/ˈniːdnt/ |modal verb|不需要|Contraction of "need not," indicating lack of necessity or obligation.|You needn't worry about that. 你不用担心那个。|近义词:don't have to, don't need to|反义词:must, have to|词性:modal verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
You needn't come early today. 你今天不必早来。
They needn't pay for the tickets. 他们不必付门票钱。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
You needn't be working so hard. 你不必这么辛苦地工作。
She needn't be worrying about it. 她不必担心这件事。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
You needn't have brought a gift. 你不必带礼物来。
He needn't have apologized. 他不必道歉。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
You needn't have been waiting so long. 你不必等这么久。
They needn't have been working all night. 他们不必整夜工作。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

She needn't have told him about it. 她不必告诉他这件事。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:

被动语态: (Needn't is rarely used in the passive voice)


疑问句:

否定句:

PHRASE->
