glottal:/ˈɡlɒtl/ (UK) /ˈɡlɑːtl/ (US)| adj.|声门的；喉音的|Relating to the glottis (the opening between the vocal cords).|The singer produced a series of glottal stops during the performance. 歌手在表演中发出了一系列的喉音。|Synonyms: guttural, laryngeal|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider sounds produced by other parts of the vocal tract like "nasal")|adjective

glottal stop:/ˈɡlɒtl stɒp/ (UK) /ˈɡlɑːtl stɑːp/ (US)| n.|喉塞音|A consonant sound produced by closing the glottis (the opening between the vocal cords) and then releasing it.  Represented by ʔ in the International Phonetic Alphabet.|The word "uh-oh" often includes a glottal stop. “uh-oh”这个词通常包含一个喉塞音。|Synonyms: (No direct synonyms, can describe it as a type of "consonant")|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)|noun
