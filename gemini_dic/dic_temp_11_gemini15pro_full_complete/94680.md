imprisonable:/ɪmˈprɪzənəbl/
adjective
可监禁的
Liable to imprisonment.
His actions were deemed imprisonable. 他的行为被认为是可以判处监禁的。
The offense is imprisonable for up to five years.  这项罪行可判处最高五年的监禁。
近义词:jailable, incarcerable
反义词:non-imprisonable

词性:adjective

词源:  From imprison + -able.  Imprison comes from Old French emprisoner, from en- "in" + prison.  The suffix -able derives from Latin -abilis, meaning "capable of being."
