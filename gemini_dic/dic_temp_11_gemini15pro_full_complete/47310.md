de-license:/diːˈlaɪsəns/
verb
取消…的许可证，撤销…的执照
To revoke or remove a license from someone or something, typically a business or activity.
The city decided to de-license all street vendors without permits. 市政府决定取消所有无证街头摊贩的执照。
The government is considering de-licensing certain professions to reduce bureaucracy. 政府正在考虑取消某些行业的执照要求以减少官僚作风。
Synonyms: deregulate, revoke, withdraw
Antonyms: license, authorize, permit
v.
