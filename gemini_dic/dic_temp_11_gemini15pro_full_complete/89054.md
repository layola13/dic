holohedral:/ˌhɒləˈhiːdrəl/ /ˌhoʊləˈhiːdrəl/ | adj. |全对称的；全面的| Exhibiting all the faces required by the full symmetry of the crystal system.  A crystal having the full number of planes required by its symmetry. |  Example: The holohedral form of the cubic system has 48 faces. 立方晶系的全对称形式有48个面。

Example:  Diamond crystals are often found in holohedral forms. 金石的晶体常以全对称的形式被发现。| 近义词:isometric, symmetrical| 反义词:anisotropic, asymmetrical|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hólos* (whole) + *hédra* (seat, base, face)
