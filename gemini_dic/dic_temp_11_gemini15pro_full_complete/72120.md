floggers:/ˈflɒɡəz/ (UK) /ˈflɑːɡərz/ (US)| n.| 鞭打者；惩罚者；严厉批评者 |Someone who flogs, especially as a punishment.  A person who whips another, especially as punishment; one who beats with or as if with a flog. Also, one who criticizes harshly.|The slave owner was a cruel flogger.  奴隶主是一个残忍的鞭打者。

The movie critic was known as a flogger of bad films.  这位影评人以严厉批评烂片而闻名。|近义词：whipper, scourger, critic, castigator|反义词：protector, defender|noun
