doubting:/ˈdaʊtɪŋ/| adj.|怀疑的，疑惑的| feeling or expressing uncertainty about something| Example:  A doubting look crossed his face. 他脸上掠过一丝怀疑的神色。

Example:  She had a doubting heart. 她有一颗怀疑的心。| 近义词：skeptical, suspicious, uncertain, questioning|反义词：trusting, believing, certain, confident|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *doutinge*, from Anglo-French *douter*, from Latin *dubitare* "to hesitate, waver, doubt," literally "to be in two minds," from *dubius* "doubtful," literally "of two minds," related to *duo* "two."
