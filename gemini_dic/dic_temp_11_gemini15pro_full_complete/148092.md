overscrupulous:/ˌoʊvərˈskruːpjələs/
adjective
过分谨慎的；过于拘泥的
Having or showing a tendency to be too concerned with avoiding doing wrong.
He was so overscrupulous that he wouldn't even tell a white lie. 他非常谨慎，甚至连善意的谎言都不肯说。
近义词: conscientious, meticulous, fastidious
反义词: unscrupulous, careless, negligent
词性: adjective

overscruple:/ˌoʊvərˈskruːpəl/
noun
过分顾虑；过分谨慎
Excessive concern with fine points of conscience or propriety.
He had no overscruples about using any methods to achieve his aims. 他为了达到目的，不择手段，毫无顾忌。
近义词: compunction, qualm, hesitation
反义词: recklessness, disregard, indifference
词性: noun

词源:
From over- + scruple (from Latin scrupulus ‘small sharp stone’, hence ‘anxiety, uneasiness’).
