macaroni:/ˌmækəˈroʊni/ (US) /ˌmækəˈrəʊni/ (UK)| n.|通心粉；空心面|A tubular pasta made with durum wheat. It is commonly cut in short lengths and is often used in dishes such as macaroni and cheese.|例句：I love macaroni and cheese. 我喜欢通心粉加奶酪。

|例句：She made a delicious macaroni salad. 她做了一份美味的通心粉沙拉。

|近义词：pasta, noodles|反义词:|词性:名词
