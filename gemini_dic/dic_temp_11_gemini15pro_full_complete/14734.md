awe-inspiring:/ˈɔːɪnspaɪərɪŋ/
adjective
令人惊叹的；令人敬畏的
Inspiring a feeling of awe; extremely impressive.
The Grand Canyon is an awe-inspiring sight. 大峡谷是一个令人惊叹的景象。
Synonyms: breathtaking, magnificent, stunning, wondrous, impressive, remarkable, spectacular
Antonyms: unimpressive, ordinary, mundane
词性: 形容词
