nominating:/ˈnɑːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 提名的；任命的 | Present participle of "nominate" meaning to propose or formally enter as a candidate for election or for an honour or award. |  The nominating committee will meet next week. 提名委员会将于下周召开会议。| Synonyms: proposing, suggesting, recommending | Antonyms: rejecting, dismissing, opposing | Verb (present participle)

nominate:/ˈnɑːmɪneɪt/ | vt. | 提名；任命；指定 | To propose or formally enter as a candidate for election or for an honour or award. | I nominate John for president. 我提名约翰为主席。| Synonyms: appoint, designate, propose | Antonyms: reject, dismiss, oppose | Verb
