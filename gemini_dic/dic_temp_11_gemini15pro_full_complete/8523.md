anticausotic:/ˌæntikaʊˈstɔːtɪk/
adjective
抗腐蚀的; 防腐蚀的
Preventing or counteracting burning or corrosion, especially by a caustic alkali.
This special paint is anticausotic and protects the metal from rust. 这种特殊的油漆具有抗腐蚀性，可以保护金属免受锈蚀。
近义词:anticorrosive, rustproof
反义词:corrosive, caustic
词性:adjective
