etheriform:/ˈiːθərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
醚状的；像乙醚的
Resembling ether, especially in lightness or volatility.
The substance had an etheriform quality, light and airy. 这种物质具有醚状的性质，轻盈而飘渺。
The gas, colorless and etheriform, quickly dissipated.  这种无色醚状的气体迅速消散了。
近义词: ethereal, airy, volatile
反义词: dense, heavy, solid
词性: adjective

词源:
From ether + -iform.  Ether comes from Late Latin aether, from Greek aithēr, meaning "upper air."  The suffix -iform means "having the form of."
