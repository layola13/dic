ethiopic:/ˌiːθiˈɒpɪk/ | adj. n.|埃塞俄比亚的；埃塞俄比亚人；埃塞俄比亚语|Relating to Ethiopia, its people, or their language.  It can be used as an adjective or a noun referring to the ancient Ge'ez language.| Ethiopia has a rich ethiopic culture. 埃塞俄比亚有着丰富的埃塞俄比亚文化。|He studies Ethiopic literature. 他研究埃塞俄比亚文学。|近义词：Ethiopian|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *Æthiopicus, from Ancient Greek Αἰθιοπικός (Aithiopikós). Ultimately from Αἰθίοψ (Aithíops, “Ethiopian”).
