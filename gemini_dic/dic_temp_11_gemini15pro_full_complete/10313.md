appeachment:/əˈpiːtʃmənt/
n.
弹劾，控告
The act of impeaching a public official before an appropriate tribunal.
The House of Representatives voted to begin impeachment proceedings against the president. 众议院投票决定启动对总统的弹劾程序。

The president is facing impeachment over allegations of corruption. 总统因腐败指控面临弹劾。

近义词:indictment, accusation, charge
反义词:acquittal, exoneration
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French empeeschement, from empeescher "to prevent, hinder," from em- "in" + pescher "to hinder."
