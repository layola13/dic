delectable:/dɪˈlɛktəbl/
adjective
令人愉快的；美味的
Highly pleasing; delightful, especially delicious.
例句：The restaurant served a delectable chocolate mousse. 这家餐厅供应美味的巧克力慕斯。
例句：The aroma of freshly baked bread was delectable. 新烤面包的香味令人愉悦。
近义词：delicious, delightful, enjoyable, palatable, savory, luscious
反义词：unpleasant, distasteful, unappetizing, repulsive
词性：形容词
词源：From Late Latin *dēlectabilis, from Latin dēlectāre (“to delight, to please”), from dē- + lactare (“to entice, to allure”).
