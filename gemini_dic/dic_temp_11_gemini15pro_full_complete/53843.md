disconsonant:/dɪsˈkɑːnsənənt/ | adj. | 不和谐的，不一致的 | Lacking harmony; incongruous; clashing.  |  The disconsonant chords created a jarring effect.  不和谐的和弦产生了刺耳的效果。 |  The modern furniture felt disconsonant with the antique decor of the room.  现代家具与房间的古董装饰格格不入。 | 近义词: discordant, incongruous, incompatible, jarring, clashing | 反义词: harmonious, consonant, congruent, compatible, agreeable | 词性: 形容词

disconsonance:/dɪsˈkɑːnsənəns/ | n. | 不和谐，不一致 |  Lack of harmony; discord. | The disconsonance between her words and actions was obvious. 她的言行不一致显而易见。 |  There's a noticeable disconsonance between the government's promises and its policies. 政府的承诺和政策之间存在明显的矛盾。 | 近义词: discord, disagreement, incongruity, discrepancy, clash | 反义词: harmony, consonance, agreement, congruence, accord | 词性: 名词
