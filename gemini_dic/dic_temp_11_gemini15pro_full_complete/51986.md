diagenesis:/ˌdaɪəˈdʒenɪsɪs/| n.|成岩作用|The physical and chemical changes occurring during the conversion of sediment to sedimentary rock.|Diagenesis transforms loose sediment into hard rock.  成岩作用将松散的沉积物转化为坚硬的岩石。

Diagenesis includes processes such as compaction and cementation. 成岩作用包括压实和胶结等过程。|近义词：lithification|反义词:|名词
