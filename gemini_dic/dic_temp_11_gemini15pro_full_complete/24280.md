brigading:/brɪɡˈeɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 在线串联，网络围攻 | The act of organizing a group of people to vote or comment on a website or online forum with the intention of influencing the outcome or manipulating public opinion. |  The forum was accused of brigading after a sudden influx of negative reviews.  论坛在突然涌入大量负面评论后被指责为网络围攻。

The politician's supporters were accused of brigading the online poll to make it seem like he had more support than he actually did.  这位政治家的支持者被指控串通网络投票，使其看起来比实际获得的支持更多。| 近义词:  astroturfing, raiding, swarming, vote brigading | 反义词:  organic growth, grassroots movement | 词性: 名词
