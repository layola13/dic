forerider:/ˈfɔːrˌraɪdər/ (US) /ˈfɔːˌraɪdə/ (UK)| n.|先驱，前兆，预兆|Someone or something that comes before and indicates the approach of another, more important person or thing.  A forerunner; a harbinger.|例句：The light shower was a forerider of the storm that followed.  这场小阵雨是随后暴风雨的前兆。

例句：Increased tourism is often a forerider of economic development. 旅游业的增长通常是经济发展的前兆。|近义词：precursor, forerunner, harbinger, herald|反义词: follower, successor |词性:noun
