muffled:/ˈmʌfəld/ | adj. | 闷的，低沉的，模糊不清的 | (of a sound) quiet and unclear because it has been blocked by something |  The muffled sound of voices came from the next room. 隔壁房间传来低沉的说话声。 | muted, hushed, subdued | loud, clear, distinct | adjective

muffle:/ˈmʌfəl/ | vt. | 使声音降低；裹住；抑制 |  to make a sound quieter and less clear; to wrap or cover something for warmth or protection | She muffled her cough with her hand. 她用手捂住嘴咳嗽。 | mute, deaden, suppress | amplify, enhance, release | verb
