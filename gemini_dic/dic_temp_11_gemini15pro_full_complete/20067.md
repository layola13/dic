biopsychical:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsaɪkɪkəl/ | adj. | 生物心理的 | Relating to or involving both biological and psychological factors. |  Her research takes a biopsychical approach to understanding depression. 她的研究采用生物心理的方法来理解抑郁症。|  The biopsychical model of illness suggests that both physical and mental factors play a role.  疾病的生物心理模型表明，生理和心理因素都起作用。 | 近义词: biopsychological | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
bio- (from Greek bios, meaning "life") + psychical (from Greek psykhikós, meaning "of the soul or mind")
