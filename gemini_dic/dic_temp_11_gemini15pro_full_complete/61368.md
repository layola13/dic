embiotocoid: /ɪmˌbaɪəˈtɒkɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 胎生鲈鱼科的；胎生鲈鱼科的鱼 |  Relating to or denoting the Embiotocidae, a family of perciform fishes of the Pacific coast of North America and East Asia that bear live young. |  The embiotocoid fishes are known for their unique reproductive strategy.  胎生鲈鱼科鱼类以其独特的繁殖策略而闻名。|  Embiotocoids are viviparous. 胎生鲈鱼是胎生的。 | synonym: surfperch | antonym: oviparous | adjective, noun
