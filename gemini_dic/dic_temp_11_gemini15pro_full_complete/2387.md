adipose:/ˈædɪˌpoʊs/ (US), /ˈædɪpəʊs/ (UK)| adj.|脂肪的；肥胖的|Relating to animal fat; consisting of, resembling, or containing fat.  Used to describe body tissue that stores fat.|The adipose tissue around his midsection had increased significantly. 他腰部的脂肪组织明显增加了。|Synonyms: fatty, obese, corpulent, fleshy|Antonyms: lean, thin, slender|adjective

adiposity:/ˌædɪˈpɒsɪti/ (US), /ˌædɪˈpɒsɪti/ (UK)| n.|肥胖；脂肪过多|The condition of being obese; excessive accumulation of body fat.|His adiposity put him at risk for several health problems. 他的肥胖使他面临多种健康问题的风险。|Synonyms: obesity, corpulence, fatness|Antonyms: thinness, leanness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adipōsus* (full of fat), from *adeps* (fat, lard).
