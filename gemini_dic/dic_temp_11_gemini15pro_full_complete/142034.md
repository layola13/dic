ETYMOLOGY->
From  "organo-" (relating to organs) + "-graphic" (writing or recording).  "Organo-" ultimately comes from the Greek "organon" (instrument, tool, organ). "-graphic" comes from the Greek "-graphikos" (of or for writing), from "graphein" (to write).
