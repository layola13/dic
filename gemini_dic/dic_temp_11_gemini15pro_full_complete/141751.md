orangery:/ˈɒrɪndʒəri/ /ˈɔːrɪndʒəri/ |n.| 橘园，柑橘温室|A building with large windows, often attached to a house, used for growing orange trees and other plants. | The Duke built a magnificent orangery on the grounds of his estate. 公爵在他的庄园里建造了一座宏伟的橘园。

orangery:|n.| 橘园，柑橘温室|A heated greenhouse, especially one used in the past for growing citrus trees. |  We enjoyed afternoon tea in the orangery. 我们在橘园里享用了下午茶。
