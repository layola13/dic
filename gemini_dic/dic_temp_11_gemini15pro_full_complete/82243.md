groundable:/ˈɡraʊndəbl/| adj.|可禁足的，可接地的|Susceptible to being grounded, as in punishment or for safety reasons.  This can refer to a person (usually a child) who can be punished by being grounded, or to an electrical device or system that can be connected to the ground.|例句：His behavior was so bad he was groundable. 他的行为太糟糕了，可以被禁足了。

例句：The electrical system is groundable for safety.  为了安全起见，该电气系统是可接地的。|近义词：punishable, restrictable (for people); earthing, grounding (for electrical systems)|反义词: ungroundable (rare), exempt (for people)|词性: adjective
