fireworks:/ˈfaɪərwɜːrks/ (US), /ˈfaɪəwɜːks/ (UK)| n. |烟花；烟火；烟火表演；激烈争论|A device containing gunpowder and other combustibles that burns with a brilliant display of colored fire, used typically for celebration or entertainment. Also, a spectacular display of such devices. Can also refer to a brilliant or dazzling display, or a heated argument or angry exchange.|例句：The fireworks display on the Fourth of July was spectacular. 七月四日的烟花表演非常壮观。

例句：Their argument ended in a display of fireworks. 他们的争论以激烈的争吵告终。|近义词：pyrotechnics, firecrackers|反义词: None (no direct antonym)|词性: Noun
