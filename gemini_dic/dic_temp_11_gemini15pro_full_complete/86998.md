henogenesis:/ˌhɛnəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
雌性生殖; 单性生殖
The development of an ovum without fertilization, as in certain insects and other invertebrates.
例句：Henogenesis is a form of asexual reproduction. 单性生殖是一种无性繁殖的形式。

例句：Some species of aphids reproduce through henogenesis. 一些种类的蚜虫通过单性生殖繁殖。
近义词：parthenogenesis, thelytoky
反义词：sexual reproduction, arrhenotoky
词性：名词
