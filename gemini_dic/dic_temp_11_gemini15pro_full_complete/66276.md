excommune:/ˌɛksˈkɒmjuːn/
n.
前公社成员，脱离公社者

例句：The excommune member struggled to adapt to life in the city.  这位前公社成员努力适应城市生活。

例句：Many excommunes returned to their families after the commune disbanded. 许多前公社成员在公社解散后回到了他们的家庭。

近义词：former member, defector
反义词: member, resident
