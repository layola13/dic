noneconomy:/ˌnɒnɪˈkɒnəmi/ |n.| 非经济性，不经济 |Lack of economy or thrift; wastefulness. A situation, activity, or process that is not economical.| The project was a complete noneconomy, wasting both time and resources.  这个项目完全不经济，浪费了时间和资源。 |Synonyms: extravagance, waste, uneconomicalness| Antonyms: economy, thrift, frugality | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "economy" (from Old French "economie" and ultimately from Greek "oikonomia," meaning "household management").
