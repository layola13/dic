nongracious:/nɒnˈɡreɪʃəs/
adjective
不亲切的，粗鲁的，不礼貌的
lacking grace or charm; not polite or friendly.
He gave a nongracious reply to her question. 他对她的问题给出了一个不礼貌的回答。
Synonyms: rude, impolite, discourteous, ungracious, churlish
Antonyms: gracious, polite, courteous, civil, pleasant
词性: 形容词
