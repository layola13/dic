drillability:/ˌdrɪləˈbɪləti/| n.|可钻性，钻探性|The drillability of rock is an important factor in determining the cost and efficiency of drilling operations. 岩石的可钻性是决定钻探作业成本和效率的重要因素。|例句：The drillability of the formation was tested before drilling commenced.  在开始钻探之前测试了地层的可钻性。

例句：The new drill bit improved the drillability of the hard rock significantly. 新的钻头显著提高了硬岩的可钻性。|近义词：boreability, penetrability|反义词: impenetrability |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From drill + -ability.  "Drill" comes from Middle Dutch "drillen," meaning "to bore, pierce." "-ability" is a suffix denoting the capacity or possibility of being acted upon in a specified way.
