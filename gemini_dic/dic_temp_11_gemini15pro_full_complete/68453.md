familiar:/fəˈmɪliər/ (US), /fəˈmɪljə(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.|熟悉的；常见的；随便的；亲近的|Well known from long or close association; easily recognized.|例句：The street was familiar to me. 这条街我很熟悉。|近义词：known, common, close, intimate|反义词：unfamiliar, strange, foreign|词性:形容词

familiar:/fəˈmɪliər/ (US), /fəˈmɪljə(r)/ (UK)| n.|熟人；密友|A demon supposedly attending and obeying a witch, often said to assume the form of an animal.|例句：He is an old familiar of mine. 他是我的老熟人。|近义词：friend, acquaintance|反义词：stranger|词性:名词
