graininesses:/ˈɡreɪninɪsɪz/
n.
颗粒状，粒度
The state or quality of being grainy; the presence of grains or granules.
The photograph suffered from excessive graininess due to the high ISO setting.  由于ISO设置过高，照片颗粒感过强。

近义词:granularity, coarseness, roughness
反义词:smoothness, fineness

词性:noun (plural)
词源: From grainy + -ness.  Ultimately from grain.
