expendability:/ɪkˌspɛndəˈbɪləti/
n.
可牺牲性，可消耗性
The expendability of low-ranking soldiers in wartime is a tragic reality.  战时低级别士兵的可牺牲性是一个悲惨的现实。
The expendability of certain types of equipment makes them cost-effective.  某些类型设备的可消耗性使其具有成本效益。
近义词: dispensability, replaceability
反义词: indispensability, irreplaceability
词性: noun

Etymology: From expendable + -ity.  Expendable comes from expend (from Latin expendere, meaning "to weigh out, pay out") + -able.
