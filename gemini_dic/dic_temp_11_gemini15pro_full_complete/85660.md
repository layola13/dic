Etymology: From Old French *hault bois (“high wood”), referring to its high pitch and wooden construction. Ultimately from Latin altus (“high”) and bos, bovis (“ox, cow; of an ox, ox-hide”).  Originally referred to a loud shawm, later settling on the oboe.
