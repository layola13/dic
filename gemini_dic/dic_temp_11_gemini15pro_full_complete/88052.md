highfalutin:/ˌhaɪfəˈluːtɪn/ | adj. | 夸大其词的；装腔作势的；高傲的 | Pompous or pretentious, especially in language; characterized by an inflated or affected style of speech or writing. |  He delivered a highfalutin speech that no one could understand. 他发表了一篇夸夸其谈的演讲，没有人能理解。

She uses such highfalutin language in her poetry. 她在诗歌中使用如此浮夸的语言。


近义词: pompous, pretentious, bombastic, inflated, ostentatious | 反义词: simple, unpretentious, modest, down-to-earth | 词性: adjective
