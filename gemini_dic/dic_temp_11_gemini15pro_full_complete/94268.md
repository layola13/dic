impersonal:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənl/| adj.|客观的，非个人的，没有人情味的|Not influenced by, showing, or involving personal feelings; lacking human warmth or emotional involvement.|The hospital has a rather impersonal atmosphere. 这家医院的气氛相当没有人情味。|Synonyms: objective, detached, neutral, unemotional, cold, distant, aloof, indifferent|Antonyms: personal, subjective, emotional, warm, friendly|adjective

impersonal:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənl/| adj.|非人称的|(of a verb or pronoun) having no specified or personal subject, typically used in formal contexts.|The report was written in an impersonal style. 这份报告是用一种非人称的风格写的。|Synonyms: generic, general, universal|Antonyms: personal, specific|adjective
