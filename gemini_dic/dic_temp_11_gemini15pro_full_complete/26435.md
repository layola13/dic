bypass:/ˈbaɪpæs/ (US), /ˈbaɪpɑːs/ (UK)| n. vt.| 旁路，绕行；分流术；旁通管|A road or route that avoids a congested area, typically a town or city center. A surgical operation to divert the flow of blood or other bodily fluid.|The new bypass has reduced traffic congestion in the town center. 这条新的旁路减少了市中心的交通拥堵。|synonyms: detour, alternative route, workaround|antonyms: direct route, main road|noun

bypass:/ˈbaɪpæs/ (US), /ˈbaɪpɑːs/ (UK)| vt.| 绕过，避开；忽略|To avoid something by going around it.|He bypassed the usual channels and went straight to the CEO. 他绕过了通常的渠道，直接去找了首席执行官。|synonyms: circumvent, avoid, evade|antonyms: confront, address, face|verb
