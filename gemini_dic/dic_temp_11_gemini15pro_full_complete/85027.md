She faced numerous harassments after reporting the incident.  举报事件后，她面临着无数的骚扰。|近义词：persecution, intimidation, bullying, torment, abuse|反义词: encouragement, support|词性:名词

词源:  From the French word "harasser," meaning "to set a dog on" (16th century).  源自法语单词 "harasser"，意为“放狗咬”（16世纪）。
