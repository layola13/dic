all-time:/ˈɔːltaɪm/
adjective
空前的；创纪录的
Of all time; the best, worst, etc. ever.
Michael Jordan is considered an all-time great basketball player. 迈克尔·乔丹被认为是史上最伟大的篮球运动员。
近义词: record-breaking, unparalleled, unsurpassed
反义词: average, mediocre, ordinary
词性: adjective
