mussing:/ˈmʌsɪŋ/| v. |弄乱，揉乱|To disarrange or rumple, especially hair.|She was mussing her hair in frustration. 她沮丧地揉乱了自己的头发。|近义词：rumpling, dishevelling, tousling|反义词：smoothing, tidying, arranging|词性:动词
Etymology: From the Middle English *musen*, perhaps of Scandinavian origin, related to Norwegian *mysja* "to crumple".
