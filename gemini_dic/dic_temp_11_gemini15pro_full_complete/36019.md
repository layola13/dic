codilla:/kəˈdɪlə/ | n. | 鱼干碎片，鳕鱼干 | Dried flakes of cod, especially those remaining after the fish has been salted and dried.  | The fishermen used the codilla to make fertilizer.  渔民用鳕鱼干碎片制作肥料。 | 近义词: flakes, scraps | 反义词: (None -  lacks a direct opposite) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *codillo*, diminutive of *codo* "elbow, joint of meat," from Latin *cubitus* "elbow." The meaning shifted to refer to pieces of dried fish, perhaps because they resemble bent elbows.
