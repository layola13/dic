drivepipe: /ˈdraɪvpaɪp/ | n. | 驱动管，打入管 | A heavy steel pipe driven into the earth to provide a casing for a well or to support foundations. |  The construction crew used a drivepipe to create a stable foundation for the building.  施工队使用打入管为建筑物建造稳定的地基。| Example: They inserted a drivepipe to reach the water table. 他们插入驱动管以到达地下水位。 | Synonyms: casing, well pipe, piling | Antonyms: N/A | noun

drivepipe: /ˈdraɪvpaɪp/ | v. | 打入（管子） | To insert a drivepipe into the earth. |  They drivepiped through the layers of rock to access the oil deposit.  他们用打入管穿过岩层以获取石油矿藏。| Example:  The workers drivepiped several feet into the ground. 工人们将管子打入了地下几英尺。 | Synonyms:  sink, implant, embed | Antonyms: extract, remove | verb
