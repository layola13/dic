adventure:/ədˈvɛntʃər/ (US), /ədˈvɛntʃə/ (UK)| n. vi. |冒险; 奇遇; 冒险经历; 投机活动|An unusual and exciting, typically hazardous, experience or activity.|例句：They set off on a daring adventure. 他们开始了大胆的冒险。|近义词：journey, expedition, quest, undertaking|反义词: safety, security|noun
adventure:/ədˈvɛntʃər/ (US), /ədˈvɛntʃə/ (UK)| vi. |冒险; 去(历险); 碰运气|Engage in daring or risky activity.|例句：He adventured into the forest. 他冒险进入森林。|近义词：explore, venture, gamble, risk|反义词: retreat, withdraw|verb
