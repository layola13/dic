counterrioter:/ˌkaʊntərˈraɪətər/ | n. | 反暴乱者；对抗暴乱的人 | A person who opposes or confronts rioters. |  The counterrioters clashed with the original protesters. 反暴乱者与最初的抗议者发生了冲突。 |  The police struggled to distinguish between the rioters and the counterrioters. 警方难以区分暴乱者和反暴乱者。 | synonyms: opponent, adversary, antagonist | antonyms: rioter, insurgent, rebel | noun
