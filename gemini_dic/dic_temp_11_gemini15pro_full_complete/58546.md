dugout: /ˈdʌɡaʊt/ (US), /ˈdʌɡaʊt/ (UK)
n.
防空洞，掩体; 独木舟; 球员休息区;
A shelter dug in the ground, especially one used by troops; a canoe hollowed from a log; a sheltered bench for baseball players.
The soldiers took cover in the dugout during the bombardment. 士兵们在轰炸期间躲进了防空洞。
The baseball team retreated to the dugout between innings.  棒球队在局间休息时退回到球员休息区。

近义词: shelter, trench, canoe, dugout canoe, bench, dug-out
反义词: mound (for baseball dugouts)
词性: noun
