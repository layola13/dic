non-controlled:/ˌnɒn kənˈtrəʊld/ | adj. | 非受控的，未加控制的 | Not controlled or regulated; operating freely or autonomously. |  The experiment involved a non-controlled environment, leading to unpredictable results.  实验涉及一个非受控环境，导致结果不可预测。 | Example: The non-controlled spread of the virus caused widespread panic.  非受控的病毒传播引起了广泛恐慌。| Synonyms: unregulated, unrestricted, free, autonomous, independent | Antonyms: controlled, regulated, restricted, managed, governed | Adjective

Etymology:
"Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not."
"Controlled" comes from the verb "control," which ultimately derives from the Old French "contreroller" (to check or verify accounts).
