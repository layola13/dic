epispermic:/ˌɛpɪˈspɜːrmɪk/
adjective
外种皮的，种皮外的
Relating to or situated on the outside of the seed coat.
The epispermic hairs aid in seed dispersal.  这些外种皮的毛有助于种子传播。
Near Synonyms: extra-testa, supra-testa
Antonyms: endospermic, intra-testa
词性: adjective
