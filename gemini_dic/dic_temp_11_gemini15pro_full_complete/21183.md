block-up:/ˌblɒk ˈʌp/ (UK) /ˌblɑːk ˈʌp/ (US)| v. |阻塞，堵塞|To obstruct or fill a passage or opening so that nothing can pass through.|The drain was blocked up with leaves.  下水道被树叶堵塞了。

The heavy snow blocked up all the roads. 大雪阻塞了所有的道路。


|近义词：obstruct, clog, plug, barricade|反义词:clear, open, unblock|词性:动词
