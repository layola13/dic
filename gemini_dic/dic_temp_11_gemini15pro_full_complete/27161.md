call-ups: /ˈkɔːl ʌps/ (US) /ˈkɔːl ʌps/ (UK)
n.| 征召；召集；调遣 |The army issued call-ups to reservists. 军队向预备役人员发出征召令。|
call-ups:
英文解析:  An act of summoning someone to military service, or to join a higher level of a sports team.  Can also refer to a summoning to participate in some event or activity.
例句：Several young players received call-ups to the national team. 几名年轻球员被召入国家队。

call-ups:
近义词：summons, draft, mobilization, promotion
反义词: demobilization, discharge
词性: noun
