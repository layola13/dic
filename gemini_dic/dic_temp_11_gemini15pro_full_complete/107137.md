ladylike:/ˈleɪdɪlaɪk/| adj.|淑女的；像女士的|Behaving in a way that is considered appropriate for a woman; polite, graceful, and refined.  Exhibiting qualities traditionally associated with a well-bred and elegant woman.|She has very ladylike manners. 她举止非常淑女。|Synonyms: refined, graceful, elegant, polite, courteous, demure, well-bred, proper|Antonyms: unladylike, uncouth, vulgar, coarse, boisterous, rough, tomboyish|adjective
