mistiness:/ˈmɪstɪnəs/| n.|薄雾笼罩，朦胧，模糊|The mistiness of the morning gave the landscape an ethereal quality. 早晨的薄雾使景色显得飘渺。|近义词：fogginess, haziness, murkiness|反义词：clarity, clearness|词性:名词
Etymology: From misty + -ness.  Misty comes from Middle English *misti, from Old English *mistig, from mist.
