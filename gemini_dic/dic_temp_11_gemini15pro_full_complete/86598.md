heliogravure:/ˌhiːliəˈɡrævjʊər/ |n.|凹版照相术；影印版画|A printing process that involves etching an image onto a copper plate, which is then used to print high-quality reproductions.  It's known for its rich tones and fine detail.|The museum exhibited a collection of exquisite heliogravures.  博物馆展出了一批精美的影印版画。|Synonyms: photogravure, rotogravure|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps simpler printing methods like lithography or offset printing)|Noun
