cuddled:/ˈkʌdld/| v. |拥抱；依偎；蜷缩|held close in one's arms as a way of showing love or affection. |The child cuddled the teddy bear.  孩子抱着泰迪熊。|
The kittens cuddled together for warmth. 小猫们依偎在一起取暖。|近义词：snuggled, embraced, hugged|反义词: separated, distanced|词性:动词

cuddled:/ˈkʌdld/| adj. |依偎的，拥抱的|being held close in one's arms as a way of showing love or affection. |The cuddled couple looked happy.  依偎在一起的夫妇看起来很幸福。|
The cuddled kittens were fast asleep.  依偎在一起的小猫们很快就睡着了。|近义词：snuggling, embracing, hugging|反义词: separated, distanced|词性:形容词
