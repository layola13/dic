afrikanerism:/ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnərɪzəm/ | n. |南非荷兰语的特征；南非荷兰语的词汇或表达方式；南非荷兰人的文化、习俗或特征| A custom, characteristic, or expression of Afrikaners, especially a word or phrase peculiar to Afrikaans. |  The use of "bakkie" for pickup truck is a common Afrikanerism.  用“bakkie”来表示皮卡车是一个常见的南非荷兰语表达。|Synonyms: Afrikaans idiom, Afrikaner characteristic, Boere characteristic|Antonyms: N/A|noun

afrikanerism:/ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnərɪzəm/ | n. |南非荷兰人的种族隔离政策；南非荷兰人的种族优越感|The policy of racial segregation and discrimination enforced in South Africa by Afrikaners; a belief in the superiority of Afrikaners. | Apartheid was a system built upon Afrikanerism. 种族隔离是一个建立在南非荷兰人种族主义基础上的制度。|Synonyms: apartheid, racial segregation, white supremacy|Antonyms: racial equality, integration|noun
