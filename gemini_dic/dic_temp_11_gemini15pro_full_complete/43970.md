cricks:/krɪks/| n.| 肌肉痉挛；僵硬；落枕|A painful stiffness in a muscle or joint, typically in the neck or back.|例句：I woke up with a crick in my neck. 我醒来时脖子落枕了。

例句：I've got a crick in my back from gardening.  我园艺劳动后背部僵硬了。|近义词：spasm, stiffness, twinge, kink|反义词: flexibility, looseness|词性:名词

crick:/krɪk/| n.| 小溪；小河（主要用于英国方言）|A small stream (chiefly British dialect).|例句：The children played by the crick. 孩子们在小溪边玩耍。

例句：We crossed the crick on stepping stones. 我们踩着石头过了小河。|近义词：stream, brook, rivulet|反义词: river|词性:名词
