methodological:/ˌmɛθədəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 方法论的，方法的 | Relating to the system of methods used in a particular area of study or activity. |  The study employed a rigorous methodological approach. 这项研究采用了严格的方法论方法。| 近义词: systematic, organized, procedural | 反义词: disorganized, haphazard, unsystematic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From late Latin methodologia, from Greek methodos ‘way of teaching or inquiry’ + -logia ‘-logy’.
