indophenol:/ˌɪndoʊˈfiːnɒl/ | n. |靛酚|A class of organic compounds, typically intensely colored, used as dyes and indicators.  They are formed by the condensation of a phenol with a nitroso compound or a quinoneimine.|The indophenol test is used to detect the presence of ammonia. 靛酚测试用于检测氨的存在。|近义词: dye, indicator|反义词: N/A|noun
