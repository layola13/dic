non-territorial:/ˌnɒnˌterɪˈtɔːriəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˌterɪˈtɔːriəl/ (UK)| adj.|非领土的，非地域性的|Not limited or restricted to a particular territory or geographic area; not exhibiting territorial behavior.|Many bird species are non-territorial outside of the breeding season.|近义词：nonlocal, cosmopolitan, widespread|反义词: territorial, local, regional|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + territorial.  "Territorial" ultimately derives from the Latin "terra" meaning "earth" or "land."
