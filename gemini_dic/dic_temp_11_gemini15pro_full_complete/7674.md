anodizer:/ˈænəˌdaɪzər/ | n. | 阳极氧化器，阳极氧化处理装置 | A device or apparatus used for anodizing metals, particularly aluminum.  It typically consists of a tank containing an electrolytic solution and a power supply to create the necessary electrical current. | The company invested in a new anodizer to improve the durability of their aluminum products. 这家公司投资了一个新的阳极氧化器，以提高其铝制品的耐用性。|  The anodizer was carefully calibrated to ensure a consistent coating thickness.  阳极氧化器经过仔细校准，以确保涂层厚度一致。| 近义词: anodizing equipment, anodizing plant | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

anodize:/ˈænəˌdaɪz/ | v. | 阳极氧化，阳极化 | To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide film by electrolysis. | The aluminum parts were anodized to prevent corrosion.  铝制零件经过阳极氧化处理以防止腐蚀。|  They anodize the metal to give it a harder, more durable surface. 他们对金属进行阳极氧化处理，使其表面更硬更耐用。| 近义词:  oxidize, plate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb


词源:  From anode + -ize.  "Anode" comes from Greek anodos "a going up, way up," from ana- "up" + hodos "way."
