capitalising:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/| v.|大写；利用；使资本化|The author is capitalising all the nouns in the title. 作者将标题中所有名词都大写了。|Synonyms: uppercase, writing in capitals|Antonyms: lowercasing, writing in lowercase|verb

Etymology: From capitalize + -ing, from capital + -ize, from Late Latin capitalis "of the head," from Latin caput "head."
