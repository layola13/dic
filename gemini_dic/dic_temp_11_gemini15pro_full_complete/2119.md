adaptational:/ˌædæpˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
适应的，改编的
Relating to or characterized by adaptation, especially the adaptation of a work of art from one medium to another.
The adaptational changes made to the novel for the film were significant.  这部小说改编成电影时，做了很大的改编。
The play is an adaptational work based on a short story. 这部戏剧是根据一个短篇小说改编的。
近义词: adapted, derivative
反义词: original, unadapted
词性: adjective
