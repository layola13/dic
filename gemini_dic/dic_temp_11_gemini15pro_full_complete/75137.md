frisky:/ˈfrɪski/| adj.|活泼的，欢闹的|Playful and full of energy.|The frisky puppy chased its tail around the room. 活泼的小狗在房间里追着自己的尾巴跑。|The frisky lambs frolicked in the meadow. 活泼的小羊在草地上嬉戏玩耍。|近义词：playful, lively, energetic, spirited|反义词：lethargic, sluggish, apathetic|词性:adjective

frisky:/ˈfrɪski/| adj.|（尤指性方面）轻佻的，嬉戏的|(especially of a person) Behaving in a playful way, especially in a way that suggests sexual desire.|He gave her a frisky pat on the bottom. 他轻佻地拍了拍她的屁股。|She felt frisky and flirted with the bartender. 她感到轻佻，并与酒保调情。|近义词：playful, flirtatious, mischievous|反义词：serious, reserved, demure|词性:adjective
