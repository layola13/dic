inviolable:/ɪnˈvaɪələbəl/
adjective
不可侵犯的；神圣的
secure from violation or profanation
例句：
The treaty guarantees the inviolable right to free speech.
该条约保障不可侵犯的言论自由权利。

Everyone has the inviolable right to protect themselves from harm.
每个人都有不可侵犯的权利来保护自己免受伤害。

近义词：sacred, sacrosanct, untouchable, invulnerable
反义词：violable, vulnerable


词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin inviolabilis, from in- ‘not’ + violare ‘violate’.
