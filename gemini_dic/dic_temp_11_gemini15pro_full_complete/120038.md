millipede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ (US), /ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ (UK)| n. |千足虫，马陆|A small creature with a long, thin body and many legs, belonging to the class Diplopoda.|例句：I saw a millipede crawling across the garden path. 我看到一只千足虫爬过花园小路。

例句：Millipedes are important decomposers in the ecosystem. 千足虫是生态系统中重要的分解者。

近义词：diplopod|反义词: None|词性: noun
