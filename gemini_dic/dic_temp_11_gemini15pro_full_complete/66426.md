exegesis:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːsɪs/| n.|注释，解释，诠释|A critical explanation or interpretation of a text, especially of scripture.|The professor's exegesis of the poem helped the students understand its complex meaning. 教授对这首诗的诠释帮助学生理解了其复杂的含义。|例句：His exegesis of the ancient text was thorough and insightful. 他对古代文本的解释透彻而富有洞察力。

例句：The rabbi offered a learned exegesis of the Torah passage. 拉比对律法段落进行了博学的解释。

|近义词：interpretation, explanation, exposition, commentary|反义词：misinterpretation, distortion|词性: noun
