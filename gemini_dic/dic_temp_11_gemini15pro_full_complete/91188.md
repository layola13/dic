hydrations:/haɪˈdreɪʃənz/
n.
水合作用；水合；水合物
The process of causing something to absorb water.  The process of combining a substance chemically with water molecules. The state of being combined with water molecules.
Hydration is important for athletes.  对运动员来说，水合作用很重要。
Hydration of the cement caused it to harden. 水泥的水合作用使其硬化。
The doctor stressed the importance of hydration, especially during the summer months. 医生强调了水合作用的重要性，尤其是在夏季。

近义词:  watering, moistening
反义词: dehydration
词性: 名词
