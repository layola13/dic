outinventing:/ˌaʊtɪnˈvɛntɪŋ/
verb
胜过发明；在发明方面超越
surpassing in inventiveness; inventing something even better than what already exists
Tesla aimed at outinventing its competitors in the electric vehicle market.  特斯拉的目标是在电动汽车市场上超越其竞争对手的发明。
Companies are constantly striving to outinvent each other in the tech industry. 各家公司都在科技行业不断努力超越彼此的发明。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, eclipse
反义词: lag behind, fall behind
词性: verb
