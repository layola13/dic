dictation:/dɪkˈteɪʃn/
n.|听写，口述|the action of saying words aloud to be typed, written down, or recorded|The students listened carefully during the dictation.|transcription, recital|reading|noun
dictation:/dɪkˈteɪʃn/
n.|命令，指示|an authoritative order or command|The workers followed the manager's dictation.|command, order, decree|request, plea|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dictatio, from dictare (to say often, dictate), from Latin dicere (to say).
