incontrollable:/ˌɪnkənˈtroʊləbl/| adj.|无法控制的，抑制不住的|Not able to be controlled.|Her laughter was uncontrollable. 她的笑声无法控制。|The pain was so intense it was uncontrollable. 疼痛剧烈得无法控制。|近义词:unmanageable, rampant, irrepressible|反义词:controllable, manageable, restrained|词性:形容词
词源:in- (not) + controllable (able to be controlled)  Ultimately from Latin "contra" (against) and "rotula" (little wheel).
