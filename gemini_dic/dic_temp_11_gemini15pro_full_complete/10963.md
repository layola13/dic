arbitrament: /ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪmənt/ | n. |仲裁,裁决,决断|The matter was settled by arbitrament. 这件事通过仲裁解决了。| An independent body was brought in for the arbitrament of the dispute. 一个独立机构被引入来仲裁这场纠纷。| 近义词: adjudication, arbitration, decision, determination, judgment, ruling | 反义词: indecision | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Old French *arbitrement, from arbitrer (“to judge, arbitrate”), from Latin arbitrārī (“to give judgment, witness, examine, judge”), from arbiter (“judge, witness, spectator”).
