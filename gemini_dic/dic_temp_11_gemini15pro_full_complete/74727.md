freers:/ˈfriːərz/| n.| 释放者；获得自由的人|People who have been freed or liberated.  Those who advocate for freedom. |The freers celebrated their newfound liberty with joyful songs. 释放者们用欢快的歌声庆祝他们新获得的自由。| The group of freers advocated for the rights of all people. 这群争取自由的人倡导所有人的权利。| synonyms: liberators, emancipators, advocates | antonyms: captors, oppressors, enslavers | noun

词源:

From "free" (adjective) + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons or things performing an action).  "Free" comes from Old English "freo" meaning "free, exempt, noble, joyful,"  related to Old High German "frī" (free). The "-er" suffix is a common Germanic suffix used to form agent nouns.
