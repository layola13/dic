didgeridoo:/ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː/| n. |迪吉里杜管|A long wooden wind instrument of the Australian Aborigines, traditionally made from a hollowed-out eucalyptus branch.|例句：He played a haunting melody on the didgeridoo. 他用迪吉里杜管吹奏了一曲萦绕心头的旋律。

例句：The didgeridoo's deep drone resonated through the valley. 迪吉里杜管低沉的嗡嗡声在山谷中回荡。|近义词: drone pipe (虽然声音类似，但并非真正的近义词，因为它们是不同的乐器)|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词)|词性: noun
