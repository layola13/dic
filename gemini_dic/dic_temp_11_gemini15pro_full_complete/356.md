abiders:/əˈbaɪdərz/| n. |遵守者，居留者，容忍者|People who abide by rules or laws, or those who dwell in a place.  Those who tolerate something.|例句：The abiders of the law shall be rewarded. 遵守法律的人将得到奖励。|近义词：residents, inhabitants, dwellers, adherents, followers, tolerators|反义词:violators, offenders, transgressors|noun
