causticily:/kɔːˈstɪkəli/
adv.
刻薄地，挖苦地
in a bitter, sarcastic manner.
She replied caustically to his rude remark. 她刻薄地回应了他粗鲁的言论。
Synonyms:  bitingly, sarcastically, acrimoniously, mordantly, sharply, cuttingly
Antonyms:  kindly, gently, sweetly, politely, respectfully
词性: 副词


caustic:/ˈkɔːstɪk/
adj.
腐蚀性的；刻薄的
able to burn or corrode organic tissue by chemical action; sarcastic in a scathing and bitter way.
The chemical is highly caustic. 这种化学物质具有强腐蚀性。
His caustic wit made him many enemies. 他刻薄的机智使他树敌众多。
Synonyms:  corrosive, abrasive, biting, acrid, mordant, trenchant
Antonyms:  mild, gentle, soothing, bland, kind
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *causticus, from Greek kaustikos "burning," from kaiein "to burn."
