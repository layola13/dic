budging:/ˈbʌdʒɪŋ/| v.|移动; 让步; 改变主意|yielding, giving way, changing one's mind|The stubborn mule refused to budge. 那头固执的骡子拒绝移动。|synonyms: moving, shifting, yielding, compromising|antonyms: resisting, staying, holding firm|verb

budging:/ˈbʌdʒɪŋ/| adj.|微小的，几乎没有的 (通常用于否定句)|very small or slight (typically used in negative constructions)|There hasn't been a budging inch of progress in the negotiations.  谈判进展甚微。|synonyms: minimal, slight, insignificant|antonyms: significant, substantial, considerable|adjective
