doublespeak:/ˈdʌbəlˌspiːk/| n.|双言巧语；模棱两可的话；|language that deliberately disguises, distorts, or reverses the meaning of words. Doublespeak is most closely associated with political language.|The government engaged in doublespeak when they called the invasion a "liberation." 政府将入侵称为“解放”，这是一种双重语言。|近义词：equivocation, euphemism, jargon, gobbledygook|反义词：plain speaking, straightforwardness|noun

doublespeak:/ˈdʌbəlˌspiːk/| n.|双重语言；|deliberately euphemistic, ambiguous, or obscure language.|The company's press release was full of doublespeak, making it difficult to understand their true intentions.  公司的新闻稿充满了双重语言，很难理解他们的真实意图。|近义词：obfuscation, prevarication|反义词：clarity, transparency|noun
