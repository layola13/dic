antifur:/ˌæntiˈfɜːr/
n., adj.
反皮草; 反皮草的
A person or group who opposes the wearing of animal fur.  Also used as an adjective to describe something related to the opposition of wearing fur.
例句：The antifur activists protested outside the fashion show. 反皮草活动家在时装秀外抗议。

例句：She joined an antifur organization. 她加入了一个反皮草组织。
近义词：animal rights, anti-cruelty
反义词：pro-fur
词性：名词, 形容词
