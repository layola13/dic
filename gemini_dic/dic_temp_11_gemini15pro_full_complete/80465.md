goniometric:/ˌɡoʊniəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|测角的，量角的|Relating to or involving the measurement of angles.|Goniometric measurements are crucial in surveying. 测角测量在测量工作中至关重要.|Synonyms: angular, trigonometric|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

goniometry:/ˌɡoʊniˈɒmɪtri/ |n.|测角法，角度测量|The science or art of measuring angles, especially as applied to joints or crystals.|Goniometry is used to assess the range of motion of a joint. 测角法用于评估关节的活动范围.|Synonyms: angle measurement|Antonyms: N/A|noun
