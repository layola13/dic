custodial:/kʌˈstəʊdiəl/ (US) /kʌˈstɒdiəl/ (UK)| adj.|保管的；监护的；拘留的|Relating to or involving the legal right to keep and care for someone, especially a child, or to keep and protect something.|The court awarded her custodial rights to the children. 法院授予了她孩子的监护权。|Synonyms: protective, guardian, supervisory|Antonyms: non-custodial, neglectful|adjective

custodial:/kʌˈstəʊdiəl/ (US) /kʌˈstɒdiəl/ (UK)| n.|保管人；监护人|A person who has custody or guardianship.|He works as a custodial staff in the museum. 他在博物馆做保管员工作。|Synonyms: guardian, keeper, custodian|Antonyms: ward, charge|noun
