overcame:/ˌoʊvərˈkeɪm/ | v. |克服，战胜|defeated, conquered, triumphed over something. |  He overcame his fear of public speaking. 他克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。

She overcame many obstacles to achieve her goals. 她克服了许多障碍才实现了自己的目标。 | 近义词：conquered, defeated, triumphed over, surmounted | 反义词：succumbed to, yielded to, gave in to |词性：动词
