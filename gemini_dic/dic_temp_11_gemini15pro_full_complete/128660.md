nicolaitan:/ˌnɪkəˈleɪɪtən/ | n. | 尼古拉派信徒；尼古拉主义者 | A follower of the doctrines of the Nicolaitans, an early Christian sect mentioned in Revelation 2:6 and 15.  They are condemned for their immoral practices, often interpreted as sexual immorality and eating food sacrificed to idols. | Example:  The early church struggled against the influence of the Nicolaitans. 早期教会与尼古拉派的影响作斗争。

Example:  Some scholars believe the Nicolaitans were a Gnostic sect. 一些学者认为尼古拉派是一个诺斯底教派。 | Synonym: heretic, sectarian | Antonym: orthodox believer | noun
