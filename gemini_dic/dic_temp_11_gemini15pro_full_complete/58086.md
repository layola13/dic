droopingness:/ˈdruːpɪŋnəs/
n.|下垂；萎靡不振|The droopingness of the flowers indicated they needed water.  花朵的萎靡表明它们需要浇水。|例句：The droopingness of his eyelids suggested he was tired. 他的眼皮下垂表明他很疲倦。|近义词：droop, wilt, sag, decline|反义词：firmness, strength, vigor|词性:名词

Etymology: From "drooping" + "-ness". "Drooping" comes from the Middle English "droupen,"  from Old Norse "drupa," meaning "to droop, sink, fall." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
