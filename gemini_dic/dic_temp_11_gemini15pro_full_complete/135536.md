nonpliantly:/nɒnˈplaɪəntli/
adverb|不顺从地，顽固地|in a way that is not compliant; stubbornly or rigidly. |The child nonpliantly refused to eat his vegetables.  这个孩子执拗地不肯吃蔬菜。|近义词:stubbornly, rigidly, unyieldingly, inflexibly|反义词: compliantly, obediently, submissively, cooperatively
词性: 副词
