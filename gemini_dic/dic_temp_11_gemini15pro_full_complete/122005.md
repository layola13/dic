misstamp:/ˌmɪsˈstæmp/
n.|错误盖章；盖错邮戳|An incorrect stamp or a stamp affixed incorrectly. |The letter was returned due to a misstamp. 这封信由于盖错邮戳被退回了。|近义词:error, mistake|反义词:correct stamp|词性:名词
vt.|盖错邮戳|To stamp incorrectly. |I misstamped the envelope and had to buy a new stamp. 我把信封盖错了邮戳，不得不买一张新的。|近义词:stamp incorrectly|反义词:stamp correctly|词性:动词
