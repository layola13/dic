contentable:/kənˈtɛntəbl/ | adj. | 心满意足的，容易满足的 | Easily satisfied; content. | She leads a contentable life in the countryside. 她过着心满意足的乡村生活。|  He seemed quite contentable with his simple meal.  他似乎对他的简餐感到相当满足。 | 近义词: content, satisfied, cheerful, happy | 反义词: discontented, unsatisfied, restless, greedy | 词性: adjective

词源: From content + -able.  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being."
