facilitative:/fəˈsɪlɪˌteɪtɪv/| adj.|促进的，便利的，使容易的|Making something easier or more likely to happen.  It describes something that helps a process or activity run more smoothly.|The teacher created a facilitative environment for learning. 老师创造了一个有助于学习的环境。|近义词：helpful, conducive, enabling, supportive|反义词：inhibitive, hindering, obstructive|词性:形容词
词源: From facilitate (verb) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin facilis "easy."
