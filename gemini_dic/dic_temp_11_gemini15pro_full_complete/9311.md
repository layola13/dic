antipodal:/ænˈtɪpədəl/
adjective
正好相反的；对跖的
Situated on opposite sides of the earth; diametrically opposed.
例句：The antipodal points on a sphere are as far apart as possible.  球体上的对跖点相距最远。

例句：Their political views are antipodal. 他们的政治观点截然相反。

近义词：opposite, contrary, diametrically opposed
反义词：similar, identical, same
词性：adjective


antipodes:/ænˈtɪpəˌdiːz/
noun
对跖点；对跖地
Places on opposite sides of the earth.
例句：Australia is often referred to as the antipodes of Britain. 澳大利亚常被称为英国的对跖点。

例句：The expedition reached the antipodes after months of arduous travel.  经过数月的艰苦跋涉，探险队到达了对跖地。

近义词：opposite side, other side of the world
反义词：same location
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antipodes, from Greek antipodes (plural), "having feet opposite," from anti- "opposite" + pous "foot" (genitive podos).
