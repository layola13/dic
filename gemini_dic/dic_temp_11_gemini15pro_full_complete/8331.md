anti-philosophical:/ˌæntiˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkəl/ |adj.| 反哲学的，反对哲学的 | Opposed to or rejecting philosophy or philosophical methods. |  He took an anti-philosophical stance, preferring practical solutions over abstract theories. 他采取了一种反哲学的立场，更喜欢实际的解决方案而不是抽象的理论。|  His anti-philosophical attitude made him skeptical of any theoretical explanations. 他反哲学的态度使他对任何理论解释都持怀疑态度。 |  unphilosophical, non-philosophical | philosophical | adjective
