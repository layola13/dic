inflating:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/
v. |膨胀；充气；抬高（物价）；（使）自负|increasing in size or volume, especially by filling with air or gas; exaggerating the importance or value of something. |The government's spending is inflating the economy. 政府的支出正在刺激经济增长。|synonyms: expanding, enlarging, swelling, pumping up, blowing up|antonyms: deflating, contracting, shrinking|verb

inflating:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/
adj. |膨胀的；充气的；自负的|increasing in size or volume; filled with air or gas; having an exaggerated sense of self-importance. |The inflating balloon soon burst.  膨胀的气球很快爆了。|synonyms: expanding, enlarging, swelling, pumped up, blown up|antonyms: deflating, contracting, shrinking|adjective
