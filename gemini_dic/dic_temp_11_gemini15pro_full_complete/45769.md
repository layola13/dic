cushion:/ˈkʊʃən/ (US), /ˈkʊʃn/ (UK)| n. vt.|垫子，缓冲物；缓和，减轻|A soft bag filled with something soft, used for sitting or leaning on. To make something less severe or unpleasant.|She sat on a cushion on the floor. 她坐在地板上的一个垫子上。|近义词：pillow, pad, buffer|反义词:aggravate, worsen|词性:名词, 动词

Etymology: From Old French *coissin, from Vulgar Latin *coxinum, from Latin coxa "hip, thigh".
