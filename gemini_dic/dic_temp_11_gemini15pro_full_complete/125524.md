multisiliquous:/ˌmʌltiˈsɪlɪkwəs/| adj.|多荚的，多果实的|Having many siliques (dry, dehiscent seed pods with a thin partition).  Specifically refers to plants in the mustard family (Brassicaceae) that produce multiple elongated seed pods.|The researcher studied the multisiliquous structure of the Arabidopsis plant. 研究人员研究了拟南芥的多果结构。|Synonyms: multicapsular, polycarpellary|Antonyms: unisiliquous (if such a word existed), oligocarpellary|adjective
