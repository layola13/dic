newsmen:/ˈnjuːzmən/ (US), /ˈnjuːzmən/ (UK)| n. |新闻记者（尤指男性）|A man who works as a journalist, especially one who gathers news for newspapers, magazines, radio, or television.  This term is somewhat dated and considered less gender-neutral than "journalist" or "reporter".|例句：The newsmen crowded around the president for a statement.  新闻记者们围着总统要一份声明。

例句：The newsmen were scrambling to get the latest scoop on the scandal.  新闻记者们争先恐后地想获得这起丑闻的最新内幕。|近义词：journalist, reporter, correspondent|反义词: None (No direct opposite; perhaps "citizen," "bystander")|词性: Noun
