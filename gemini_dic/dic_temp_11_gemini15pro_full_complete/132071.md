noncranking:/ˌnɑːnˈkræŋkɪŋ/
adjective
非启动的，不转动的
Not related to cranking or starting an engine.  This term usually describes a condition where the engine is not being cranked or rotated by the starter motor.
The noncranking current draw was within specifications. 非启动电流消耗在规格范围内。
The mechanic diagnosed a noncranking condition due to a faulty starter.  机械师诊断出由于起动机故障导致的非启动状态。
nonstarting, non-running
cranking, running
