accessorization:/əkˌsesərɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
配饰，装饰；添加配件
The accessorization of her outfit was perfect. 她的服装配饰非常完美。
She spent hours on the accessorization of her wedding dress. 她花了几个小时为她的婚纱搭配配饰。
Synonym: adornment, ornamentation, embellishment
Antonym: simplification, stripping down
noun
