hypertoxic:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɒksɪk/ /ˌhaɪpərˈtɑːksɪk/| adj.|极毒的，剧毒的|Extremely toxic; excessively poisonous.|The waste from the chemical plant is hypertoxic and must be handled with extreme caution. 化工厂的废料剧毒，必须极其小心地处理。|The venom of this spider is hypertoxic, capable of killing a human in minutes. 这种蜘蛛的毒液剧毒无比，几分钟内就能杀死一个人。| 近义词:supertoxic, extremely toxic, highly toxic| 反义词:nontoxic, harmless|词性:形容词
