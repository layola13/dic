holostean:/ˈhɒləstiən/ (US) /ˈhɒləstiən/ (UK)| n. adj.|全骨鱼类，全骨鱼类的|A primitive bony fish of the subclass Holostei, such as a gar or bowfin, intermediate between the Chondrostei and the Teleostei.|Gars and bowfins are examples of holostean fish.  雀鳝和弓鳍鱼是全骨鱼类的例子。|The holostean skeleton is less cartilaginous than that of the chondrosteans. 全骨鱼类的骨骼比软骨硬鳞鱼类的骨骼软骨少。|近义词:ganoid (adj. pertaining to ganoid fishes)|反义词:teleost (n.  a teleost fish)|词性:noun, adjective
