disregardant:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdənt/
adjective
漠视的，不顾的
Showing a complete lack of concern or respect.
He was disregardant of the rules and regulations. 他对规章制度漠不关心。

She was utterly disregardant of his feelings. 她完全不顾他的感受。

近义词:  disrespectful, inconsiderate, negligent, heedless, unmindful
反义词: respectful, considerate, attentive, mindful
词性: adjective
词源: From disregard + -ant.
