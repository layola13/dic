dullify: /ˈdʌlɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使迟钝；使沉闷；使呆滞 | To make something less intense, interesting, or lively; to make someone less alert or sensitive. | The medication dullified his senses.  这种药物使他的感觉迟钝了。

The hot weather dullified everyone. 炎热的天气使每个人都昏昏沉沉的。| 近义词: blunt, deaden, weaken, enervate, subdue | 反义词: sharpen, stimulate, invigorate, enliven | 词性: 动词
