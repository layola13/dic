invasive:/ɪnˈveɪsɪv/
adjective
侵入的；侵略性的
tending to spread prolifically and undesirably or harmfully.
The invasive species of plant is crowding out the native plants. 这种入侵植物正在排挤本地植物。
近义词:aggressive, intrusive, pervading
反义词:noninvasive, harmless, benign
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *invāsivus, from Latin invādere “to invade, attack, enter, fall upon,” from in- “in, into” + vādere “to go, walk, step, stride.”
