nonrequirement:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈkwaɪərmənt/ | n. | 非必要条件，非强制性要求 | Something that is not required or mandatory. |  The submission of a photograph is a nonrequirement for this application. 提交照片不是此应用程序的必要条件。 | The dress code is casual; a tie is a nonrequirement. 着装要求是休闲的；领带不是必需的。 |  近义词：option, nonessential, unnecessary | 反义词：requirement, necessity, essential | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + requirement (from Old French requerement, from requerir "to require")
