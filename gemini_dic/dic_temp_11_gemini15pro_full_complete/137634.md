nontyrannic: /nɒn-tɪˈrænɪk/ /nɑːn-tɪˈrænɪk/
adjective
非暴政的; 不专横的
Not tyrannical; not oppressive or unjustly severe.
The new government promised a nontyrannic rule, respecting the rights of all citizens. 新政府承诺实行非暴政统治，尊重所有公民的权利。
The organization promotes nontyrannic leadership and encourages collaboration.  该组织提倡非专制的领导方式，并鼓励合作。
近义词: democratic, liberal, tolerant, lenient, gentle
反义词: tyrannical, oppressive, dictatorial, authoritarian, despotic
词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (Latin, not) + "tyrannic" (from Greek "tyrannos," meaning "absolute ruler").
