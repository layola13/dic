be-in-for:/biː ˈɪn fɔːr/
verb phrase
即将经历，即将面临
be about to experience something, typically something unpleasant
例句：I think we're in for a storm. 我想我们快要遇到暴风雨了。

例句：He's in for a shock when he sees how much it costs. 当他看到价格时，他会大吃一惊。
近义词：expect, anticipate, about to experience
反义词: avoid, escape
词性: verb phrase

PHRASE->
be in for it:  注定要受惩罚或批评  He's in for it now! He broke his mom's favorite vase. 他现在要倒霉了！他打碎了他妈妈最喜欢的花瓶。
be in for a treat: 将会享受一段美好的时光 You're in for a treat! This restaurant is amazing. 你会有口福的！这家餐馆很棒。
be in for a shock/surprise: 将会感到震惊/惊喜 He was in for a shock when he saw the price. 当他看到价格时，他大吃一惊。
be in for a long wait/ride etc.: 将会等待/经历很长时间 We're in for a long wait at the airport. 我们要在机场等很长时间。
be in for some trouble: 将会遇到麻烦 I think we're in for some trouble if we don't finish this project on time. 如果我们不按时完成这个项目，我想我们会遇到一些麻烦。
