bucketing:/ˈbʌkɪtɪŋ/| n.|分组，分桶，分类|The process of grouping items into different categories or "buckets."  It's often used in data analysis and programming.|Data bucketing is crucial for analyzing large datasets. 数据分组对于分析大型数据集至关重要。|
The company uses bucketing to categorize customer feedback. 这家公司使用分组来分类客户反馈。|grouping, categorizing, classifying|sorting, individualizing|noun
