dinkier:/ˈdɪŋkiər/| adj.|更小的，更精致的|Smaller or more compact than the usual or standard size, often implying a sense of stylishness or cuteness.  It is the comparative form of "dinky."|That dinkier sports car is perfect for zipping around the city. 那辆更小巧的跑车非常适合在城市里穿梭。|Synonyms: smaller, compact, miniature, petite, tiny|Antonyms: larger, bigger, huge, enormous|adjective

dinky:/ˈdɪŋki/| adj.|小的，精致的|Small and often stylish or cute.|She drove a dinky little convertible. 她开着一辆小巧玲珑的敞篷车。|Synonyms: small, compact, miniature, petite, tiny|Antonyms: large, big, huge, enormous|adjective
