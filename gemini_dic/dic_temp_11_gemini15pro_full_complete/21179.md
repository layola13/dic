blocage:/blɔˈkaʒ/| n.|阻塞，封锁，障碍|A blockage is something that is stopping something else from moving through or along something else.  It can also refer to a mental or emotional obstacle.|The road was closed due to a blockage caused by a fallen tree.  道路因倒下的树木造成的阻塞而关闭。|近义词：obstruction, barrier, deadlock, impasse, standstill|反义词:opening, access, breakthrough|noun
