donnism:/ˈdɒnɪzəm/| n.| 唐尼主义（指爱尔兰诗人William Butler Yeats的戏剧风格）|A style of dramatic verse characterized by rhetorical extravagance and elaborate imagery, associated with the Irish poet William Butler Yeats.| Yeats's later plays are often cited as examples of donnism. 叶芝后期的戏剧经常被引用为唐尼主义的例子。| 近义词: Yeatsism, aestheticism | 反义词:  (No clear antonym exists) | 词性: noun

词源: Derived from the surname of William Butler Yeats (1865-1939), Irish poet and playwright.
