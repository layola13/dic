equiparable:/ɪˈkwɪpərəbl/
adj.
可比较的，相等的
capable of being compared; equal or similar
The two cars are equiparable in terms of performance. 这两辆车的性能不相上下。
近义词：comparable, similar, equivalent, analogous
反义词：incomparable, dissimilar, unequal, different
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *aequiparabilis, from Latin aequus (“equal”) + parabilis (“comparable”), from parare (“to prepare, make ready, provide”). Thus literally "equally preparable."
