overabusiveness:/ˌoʊvərəˈbjuːsɪvnəs/| n. |过度虐待，过度辱骂|The state of being excessively abusive; extreme or excessive abusiveness.  This word describes a pattern of behavior, not just isolated incidents.|Her overabusiveness towards her staff led to several resignations. 她对员工的过度辱骂导致多人辞职。|近义词：maltreatment, mistreatment, cruelty, abuse|反义词: kindness, respect, consideration|noun
