gastroscope:/ˈɡæstrəˌskoʊp/ | n. |胃镜|An instrument used for visually examining the stomach.|The doctor used a gastroscope to examine the patient's stomach lining. 医生用胃镜检查了病人的胃粘膜。|近义词:endoscope (内窥镜 - 更广义)|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun

Etymology:  From gastro- (combining form meaning "stomach") + -scope (suffix meaning "instrument for viewing").  Ultimately from Greek *gastēr* (stomach) and *skopein* (to look at).
