avise:/əˈvaɪz/| vt.|通知，告知|To inform or notify someone.|He was avised of the meeting's postponement. 他被告知会议延期了。|
例句：The lawyer avised his client to plead guilty.  律师建议他的客户认罪。

例句：We were avised of the change in schedule. 我们被告知了时间表的变更。

近义词：inform, notify, apprise, tell|反义词: withhold, conceal|词性: 动词(vt.)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aviser (“to consider, deliberate, inform”), from Vulgar Latin *advidere, from Latin ad (“to”) + vidēre (“to see”).
