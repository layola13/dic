invaluableness:/ɪnˈvæljuəbəlnəs/
n.
无价；极宝贵
The state or quality of being invaluable; preciousness beyond estimation.
The invaluableness of his advice was clear when the project succeeded. 他的建议的宝贵之处在项目成功时显而易见。
近义词: pricelessness, worth, value, importance
反义词: worthlessness, insignificance


ETYMOLOGY->
From invaluable + -ness.  Invaluable itself comes from in- (not) + valuable.  Ultimately from Latin *valere (to be strong).
