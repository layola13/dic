nonenticingly:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtaɪsɪŋli/| adv.| 不吸引人地，引不起兴趣地 |  Lacking the power to entice or attract; in a way that does not tempt or allure. | The food was presented nonenticingly, just slopped onto the plate.  食物摆放得很不吸引人，只是随便倒在盘子里。| The gray, rainy day felt nonenticingly dreary. 灰蒙蒙的下雨天让人提不起兴趣，感到沉闷。 | 近义词: unattractively, uninterestingly, dully, blandly, vapidly | 反义词: enticingly, attractively, alluringly, temptingly, captivatingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From non- (not) + enticing (tempting, alluring) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Enticing" ultimately comes from the Old French word "enticier" meaning to set on fire or to incite.
