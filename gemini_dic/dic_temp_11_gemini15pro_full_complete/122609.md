modernizing:/ˈmɑːdərnaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈmɒdənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
现代化;使现代化
the process of adapting something to modern needs or habits by adopting modern techniques or technology.
Many countries are modernizing their industries. 许多国家正在使其工业现代化。
synonyms: updating, innovating, renovating, improving, developing, upgrading, reforming, overhauling, transforming, revolutionizing
antonyms: stagnating, regressing, deteriorating, declining, decaying
verb

modernize:/ˈmɑːdərnaɪz/ /ˈmɒdənaɪz/
verb
现代化;使现代化
adapt (something) to modern needs or habits, typically by installing modern equipment or adopting new techniques.
They are determined to modernize the factory. 他们决心要使工厂现代化。
synonyms: update, innovate, renovate, improve, develop, upgrade, reform, overhaul, transform, revolutionize
antonyms: stagnate, regress, deteriorate, decline, decay
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From modern + -ize + -ing.  Modern comes from Old French moderne, ultimately from Latin modernus "of the present time," from modo "just now."  The suffix -ize is used to form verbs meaning "to make or become (something)," and -ing is a present participle suffix.
