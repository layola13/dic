crave:/kreɪv/| v.|渴望，热求|To have a strong desire for something.|She craved chocolate. 她渴望巧克力。|I crave your forgiveness. 我渴望得到你的原谅。|近义词：desire, yearn for, long for, hanker after|反义词：abhor, detest, loathe|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old English *cravian, from Proto-Germanic *krabōną (“to ask, demand, beg”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰrebʰ- (“to seize, take, grasp”).
