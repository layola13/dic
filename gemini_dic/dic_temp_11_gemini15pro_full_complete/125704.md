mungo:/ˈmʌŋɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈmʌŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. |貓鼬，蒙哥|A small, slender mammal of the mongoose family, native to southern Europe and Asia, having a grayish-brown coat and a long tail. It is noted for its ability to kill venomous snakes.|The mungo quickly dispatched the cobra. 貓鼬迅速殺死了眼鏡蛇。|近义词：mongoose, meerkat|反义詞: N/A|noun
