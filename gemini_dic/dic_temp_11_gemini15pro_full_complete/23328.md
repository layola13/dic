boyishly:/ˈbɔɪɪʃli/| adv.|孩子气地，像男孩一样|In a manner characteristic of or resembling a boy; in a youthful, energetic, and sometimes mischievous way.|He grinned boyishly. 他孩子气地咧嘴一笑。|近义词：youthfully, childishly, playfully|反义词：maturely, womanly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From boy + -ish + -ly.  "Boy" comes from Middle English *boi, of uncertain origin. "-ish" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "of the nature of," "like," "somewhat," from Old English -isc. "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives, from Old English -lice.
