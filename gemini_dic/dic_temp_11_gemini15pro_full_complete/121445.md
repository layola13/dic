misinterpretation:/ˌmɪsɪnˌtɜːrprəˈteɪʃn/ | n. |误解，曲解|An act of misinterpreting something; a wrong or inaccurate interpretation.|例句：His words were open to misinterpretation. 他的话容易被误解。

例句：The misinterpretation of the data led to flawed conclusions. 对数据的误解导致了错误的结论。

近义词：misunderstanding, misconstruction, misreading|反义词：accurate interpretation, correct understanding|词性：名词
