archaic:/ɑːrˈkeɪɪk/ | adj. | 古老的；过时的；古代的 | Very old or old-fashioned. |  例句：The company's computers are archaic.  这家公司的电脑很老旧。 | 近义词：ancient, obsolete, outdated, antiquated | 反义词：modern, contemporary, current | 词性: 形容词

archaic:/ɑːrˈkeɪɪk/ | adj. | 古风的；古体的（尤指词语） | (of a word or a style of language) no longer in everyday use but sometimes used to impart an old-fashioned flavour. | 例句： ‘Thou art’ is an archaic form of ‘you are’. ‘Thou art’是‘you are’的古体。 | 近义词：obsolete, antiquated | 反义词：modern, contemporary | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *archaicus, from Greek arkhaïkos "ancient, old-fashioned," from arkhaios "from the beginning, primitive," from arkhe "beginning."
