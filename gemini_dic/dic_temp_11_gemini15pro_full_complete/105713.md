kinrede:/ˈkɪnˌred/
noun
亲属关系；血缘关系；家族
A group of people related by blood or marriage.  A close connection by blood or common origin.
例句：He felt a strong kinrede with the people of the village.  他感觉和这个村子里的人们有着很强的亲缘关系。

例句：There's a definite kinrede between the two families. 这两个家族之间肯定有血缘关系。

近义词：kinship, family, relatives, relations
反义词：stranger
