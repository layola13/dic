monotonicity:/ˌmɒnətəˈnɪsɪti/ /ˌmɑːnətəˈnɪsəti/| n. |单调性，单调乏味|The monotonicity of the landscape made the journey tedious.  这单调的景色使旅程乏味。|近义词: dullness, tediousness, uniformity|反义词: variety, diversity|noun


monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ /ˈmɑːnəˌtoʊn/| n. adj. |单调，单调的|The lecturer's monotone voice made it difficult to stay awake. 演讲者单调的声音让人很难保持清醒。|近义词: tedious, dull, boring|反义词: varied, interesting, exciting|noun, adjective


monotonous:/məˈnɒtənəs/ /məˈnɑːtənəs/| adj. |单调的，乏味的|The monotonous routine of factory work can be demoralizing. 工厂工作的单调程序可能会令人沮丧。|近义词: tedious, dull, repetitive|反义词: varied, interesting, stimulating|adjective
