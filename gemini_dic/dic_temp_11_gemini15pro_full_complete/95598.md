incommodation:/ɪnˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən/ |n.|不便，麻烦，打扰；不适；不舒服|The noise caused great incommodation to the residents. 噪音给居民带来了极大的不便。|近义词:inconvenience, discomfort, nuisance|反义词:convenience, comfort, ease|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *incommodatio*, from Latin *incommodus* "inconvenient," from *in-* "not" + *commodus* "convenient, suitable."
