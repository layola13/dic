nonemphatical:/ˌnɒnɪmˈfætɪkəl/| adj.|非强调的，不加重的|Not emphatic; without emphasis.  Expressing something in a way that does not draw particular attention to it.|He spoke in a nonemphatical tone, simply stating the facts. 他以一种非强调的语气说话，只是简单地陈述了事实。|Synonyms: unemotional, neutral, matter-of-fact, objective, subdued|Antonyms: emphatic, stressed, forceful, highlighted, prominent|Adjective
