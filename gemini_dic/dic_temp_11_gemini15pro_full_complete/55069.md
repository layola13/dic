disregardful:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdfəl/
adjective
漠视的，不顾的，不尊重的
Showing a lack of consideration or respect; ignoring.
He was disregardful of his wife's feelings. 他漠视妻子的感受。
She was utterly disregardful of the consequences. 她完全不顾后果。
近义词：inconsiderate, disrespectful, negligent, unmindful
反义词：considerate, respectful, mindful, attentive
词性：adjective
词源：From disregard + -ful. Disregard comes from the Old French desregarder, meaning "to look away from."
