demographers:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfərz/ (UK), /dɪˈmɑːɡrəfərz/ (US)| n. |人口学家；人口统计学家|Experts who study demographics, the statistical characteristics of human populations.  They analyze factors like birth rates, death rates, migration patterns, and age distributions.|Demographers predict a population decline in many developed countries. 人口学家预测许多发达国家的人口将会下降。|synonyms: population scientists, population analysts|antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym exists)|noun
