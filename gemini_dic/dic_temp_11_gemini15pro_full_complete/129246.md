nocturnally:/nɑːkˈtɜːrnəli/ | adv. |夜间地；在夜里|Happening, done, or active at night.|Bats fly nocturnally.  蝙蝠在夜间飞行。|
|近义词: at night, by night|反义词: diurnally|词性: 副词|

nocturnal:/nɑːkˈtɜːrnəl/ | adj. |夜间的；夜间活动的|Of, relating to, or occurring in the night.|Owls are nocturnal birds. 猫头鹰是夜行动物。|
|近义词: nightly|反义词: diurnal|词性: 形容词|
