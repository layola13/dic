otolaryngologists:/ˌoʊtəˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːlədʒɪsts/ |n.|耳鼻喉科医生|A medical specialist who diagnoses and treats diseases and disorders of the ear, nose, and throat (ENT).|例句：I need to see an otolaryngologist about my persistent sore throat.  我需要去看耳鼻喉科医生，因为我的喉咙一直很痛。

例句：The otolaryngologist performed surgery to remove the patient's tonsils. 耳鼻喉科医生为病人做了扁桃体切除手术。

|近义词：ENT doctor, ENT specialist, ear, nose, and throat doctor|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From oto- + laryng- + -ologist.  "Oto-" comes from Greek "ōtos," meaning "ear." "Laryng-" comes from Greek "larynx," referring to the voice box. "-ologist" denotes a specialist in a particular field of study.
