nonfragilenesses:/ˌnɒnfrəˈdʒɪlɪtɪsɪz/
noun
非脆弱性；抗脆性
The quality or state of not being fragile; resilience to shocks and stressors.  This word emphasizes not just surviving adversity, but thriving and improving because of it.
The system demonstrated remarkable nonfragilenesses in the face of the cyberattack.  该系统在面对网络攻击时表现出非凡的抗脆性。
The nonfragilenesses of the economy allowed it to quickly recover from the recession. 经济的抗脆性使其能够迅速从衰退中复苏。
Synonyms: resilience, robustness, antifragility
Antonyms: fragility, vulnerability, weakness
