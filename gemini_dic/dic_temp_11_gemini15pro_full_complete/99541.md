intergovernmental:/ˌɪntərɡʌvərnˈmentl/
adjective
政府间的；跨政府的
Relating to or involving relations between governments of different countries.
The intergovernmental panel on climate change released a new report. 政府间气候变化专门委员会发布了一份新报告。
The project requires intergovernmental cooperation. 这个项目需要政府间的合作。
近义词: international, multinational
反义词: domestic, national
词性: adjective

词源:
From inter- + governmental.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "governmental" relates to government.
