disinterest:/dɪsˈɪntrɪst/
| n.| 公正无私;不感兴趣;冷漠 |Lack of interest or involvement; impartiality.|  The judge showed complete disinterest in the case, ensuring a fair trial.  法官对这个案子表现出完全的公正无私，确保了审判的公平。|Synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, objectivity, detachment, indifference, apathy | Antonyms:  interest, involvement, bias, partiality, prejudice| noun


disinterested:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪd/
| adj.|  公正无私的;不感兴趣的;冷漠的 |  Not influenced by personal advantage or prejudice; unbiased.| A disinterested observer is needed to mediate the dispute. 需要一个公正无私的观察员来调解这场纠纷。  | Synonyms: impartial, unbiased, neutral, objective, detached, indifferent, uninterested, apathetic | Antonyms: interested, biased, partial, prejudiced, involved | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *désintéressé*, past participle of *désintéresser*, from *dés-* (prefix indicating negation) + *intéresser* (to interest).  Ultimately from Latin *interesse* (to be of importance).
