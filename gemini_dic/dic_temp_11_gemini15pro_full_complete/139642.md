occulted: /əˈkʌltɪd/ /ɑːˈkʌltɪd/
verb
掩蔽，遮掩
Hidden; concealed.  The moon occulted the star. 月亮遮蔽了那颗星星。
The star was occulted by the moon. 这颗星星被月亮遮住了。
Synonyms: eclipsed, hidden, obscured, covered
Antonyms: revealed, uncovered, exposed
