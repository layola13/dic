emphysematous:/ˌɛmfɪˈsiːmətəs/| adj.|肺气肿的，气肿的|Affected by emphysema; swollen or inflated with air or gas.|The patient presented with emphysematous lungs.| 近义词：inflated, swollen, distended| 反义词：deflated, collapsed| 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin emphysematosus, from Greek emphysema (inflation), from emphysan (to inflate), from en- (in) + physan (to blow).
