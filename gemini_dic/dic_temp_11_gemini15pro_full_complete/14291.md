autokinesis:/ˌɔːtəʊkɪˈnɪːsɪs/ /ˌɑːtoʊkɪˈniːsɪs/ | n. | 自主运动 | The apparent movement of a stationary point of light in the dark.  The illusion that a stationary point of light in a dark room is moving. | Example 1: The pilot experienced autokinesis and mistook a star for a moving aircraft. 飞行员经历了自主运动，误将一颗星星当作移动的飞机。

Example 2:  In the experiment, participants were asked to describe the autokinesis they perceived. 在实验中，参与者被要求描述他们感知到的自主运动。| 近义词: apparent motion, illusory motion | 反义词: actual motion, real movement | 词性: noun
