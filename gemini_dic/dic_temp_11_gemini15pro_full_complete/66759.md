exosmose:/ˌeksɒzˈməʊsɪs/ /ˌɛksoʊzˈmoʊsɪs/
noun
外渗，外渗透
The outward passage of a fluid through a semipermeable membrane.
Example 1: The exosmose of water from the plant cells caused them to wilt. 植物细胞的水分外渗导致它们枯萎。
Example 2: The experiment demonstrated the process of exosmose through the artificial membrane.  实验演示了通过人工膜的外渗过程。
synonym: exosmosis, outward osmosis
antonym: endosmosis, inward osmosis
词性: n.
