colestaff:/ˈkoʊlˌstæf/
noun
甘蓝茎; 羽衣甘蓝茎
The thick stem of a cabbage, kale, or similar plant.
The colestaff can be used in soups or stews. 甘蓝茎可以用来做汤或炖菜。
synonyms: cabbage stalk, kale stalk
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, possibly cabbage leaf, kale leaf)
n.
