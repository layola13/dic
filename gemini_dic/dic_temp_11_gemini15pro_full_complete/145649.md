overcontentments:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtɛntmənts/
|n.|过度满足；过分的满意|A state of being excessively content or satisfied, often to the point of complacency.|His overcontentments with his current situation prevented him from seeking further improvement. 他对自己现状的过度满足阻碍了他寻求进一步的提升。|Near Synonyms: complacency, smugness, self-satisfaction|Antonyms: dissatisfaction, discontent, restlessness|noun

Etymology:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "contentment" (from "content," from Old French "content," ultimately from Latin "contentus," past participle of "continere" meaning "to contain, hold together").  The suffix "-ment" forms nouns from verbs.
