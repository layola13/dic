hitchiness:/ˈhɪtʃɪnəs/
n.
不流畅，不顺畅，结结巴巴
The hitchiness in his speech betrayed his nervousness. 他讲话的不流畅暴露了他的紧张。
The hitchiness of the animation was distracting.  动画的卡顿让人分心。
Unevenness, jerkiness, irregularity, faltering
Smoothness, fluidity, evenness
noun
