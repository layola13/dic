hurrier:/ˈhɜːriər/| n.| 赶快的人；匆忙的人|A person who hurries; someone moving quickly.|The hurrier tripped over his own feet in his haste. 匆忙的人因为太着急而绊倒了。|Seeing the time, the hurrier grabbed his bag and dashed out the door. 看到时间后，那个匆忙的人抓起包冲出了门。|近义词：rusher, speeder|反义词：dawdler, lingerer|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "hurry," which comes from the Middle English "hurrien," of uncertain origin, possibly related to the Old French "harier" (to harass).
