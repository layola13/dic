electrocute:/ɪˈlɛktrəkjuːt/ | vt. |电击致死；以电刑处死 | To kill someone by passing a strong electric current through their body. | The murderer was electrocuted for his crimes. 谋杀犯被电击处死。

The faulty wiring electrocuted the dog.  错误的线路导致狗被电死。


Synonym: execute, kill | Antonym: revive, resuscitate | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- + execute.  "Electro-" comes from Greek "elektron" meaning "amber" (referring to static electricity). "Execute" comes from Latin "executus," past participle of "exequi" meaning "to follow out, perform."
