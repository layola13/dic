misguided:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪdɪd/| adj.|误导的；被误导的；不明智的|Having or showing faulty judgment or reasoning.|例句：His misguided attempts to help only made things worse. 他想帮忙，但方法不对，反而把事情弄糟了。

例句：The company's misguided marketing campaign alienated many customers. 该公司误导性的营销活动疏远了许多顾客。

近义词：mistaken, erroneous, ill-advised, unwise, foolish|反义词：wise, sensible, sound, judicious, well-advised|词性:形容词
