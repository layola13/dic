exhilaratingly:/ɪɡˈzɪləreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
令人振奋地；欢欣鼓舞地
in a way that makes one feel very happy, animated, or elated
The music was exhilaratingly fast-paced.  这音乐节奏快得令人振奋。
The rollercoaster ride was exhilaratingly fun. 过山车真是刺激好玩。
近义词: excitedly, thrillingly, joyfully
反义词: depressingly, dully, boringly
词性: adverb
词源: From exhilarate + -ing + -ly.  Exhilarate comes from Latin exhilaratus, past participle of exhilarare "to gladden, cheer," from ex- "out" + hilarare "to cheer," from hilaris "cheerful."
