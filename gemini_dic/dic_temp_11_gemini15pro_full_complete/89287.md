homodynamous:/ˌhɒməˈdaɪnəməs/ | adj. | 同名的，同音异义的 | Having the same name or sounding alike but having different meanings. | Example 1:  The words "bow" (to bend at the waist) and "bow" (a weapon used with arrows) are homodynamous.  鞠躬的“bow”和弓箭的“bow”是同音异义词。
Example 2: Several homodynamous species exist within this genus, making identification challenging.  在这个属中存在几个同名的物种，使得鉴定具有挑战性。 | Synonym: homonymous, homophonous | Antonym: heteronymous | 词性: 形容词
