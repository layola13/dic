ditheistic:/ˌdaɪˈθiːɪstɪk/
adjective
二神论的
Believing in two gods, especially two equal and opposing gods.
The ancient religion of Zoroastrianism is sometimes described as ditheistic because of its focus on the opposing forces of good and evil.  古代琐罗亚斯德教有时被描述为二神论，因为它关注善恶的对立力量。
Ditheistic beliefs are less common than monotheistic or polytheistic ones. 二神论信仰不如一神论或多神论信仰普遍。
Near Synonyms:dualistic
Antonyms:monotheistic, polytheistic
词性:adjective
