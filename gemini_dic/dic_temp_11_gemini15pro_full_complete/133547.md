nonfinding:/ˈnɒnˌfaɪndɪŋ/
noun
未发现；未查明；无结果
A nonfinding is a determination that something has not been found or proven.
The audit resulted in a nonfinding for fraud.  审计结果是没有发现欺诈行为。
The investigation concluded with a nonfinding.  调查以未查明告终。
near synonyms:  null result, negative result, lack of evidence, absence of proof
antonyms: discovery, finding, positive result
n.
