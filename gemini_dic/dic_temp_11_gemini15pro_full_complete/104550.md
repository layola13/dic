Also used to describe someone who acts recklessly or takes extreme risks. |  The kamikaze pilot crashed into the aircraft carrier. 神风敢死队飞行员撞上了航空母舰。|  He took a kamikaze approach to his investments, risking everything. 他对投资采取了亡命之徒般的方法，孤注一掷。 | 近义词: suicide bomber, daredevil | 反义词: coward | 词性: 名词 (singular)


Etymology: From Japanese 神風 (kamikaze, literally "divine wind"), referring to a typhoon that dispersed the Mongol fleets attacking Japan in 1281.
