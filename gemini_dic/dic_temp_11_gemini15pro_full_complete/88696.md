hobbit:/ˈhɒbɪt/| n.|霍比特人|A member of a fictional race of short, hairy-footed people in stories by J.R.R. Tolkien.|例句：Bilbo Baggins was a hobbit. 比尔博·巴金斯是一个霍比特人。|例句：Hobbits are known for their love of food and comfort. 霍比特人以热爱美食和舒适的生活而闻名。|近义词：halfling, dwarf (in some contexts)|反义词:giant (loosely)|词性:noun
