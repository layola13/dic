legalizers:/ˈliːɡəˌlaɪzərz/
noun
合法化者，支持合法化的人
People who advocate for making something legal, often referring to substances like marijuana or activities like gambling.
The legalizers argue that marijuana has medicinal benefits and should be available to patients. 合法化支持者认为大麻具有药用价值，应该提供给病人。
The legalizers of gambling believe it can boost the economy and provide entertainment.  赌博合法化支持者认为它可以促进经济发展并提供娱乐。
synonyms: proponents, advocates, supporters
antonyms: prohibitionists, opponents
