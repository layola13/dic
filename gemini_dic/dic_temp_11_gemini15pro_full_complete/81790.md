grenade:/ɡrəˈneɪd/ | n. | 手榴弹；榴弹 | A small bomb thrown by hand or launched mechanically. | The soldier pulled the pin and threw the grenade. 士兵拔掉保险销，扔出了手榴弹。| Example: Enemy forces attacked with grenades and small arms fire. 敌军用手榴弹和小武器开火攻击。| Synonyms: bomb, explosive | Antonyms: peace, safety | noun
