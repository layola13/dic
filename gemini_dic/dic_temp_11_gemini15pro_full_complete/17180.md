batwing:/ˈbætˌwɪŋ/| adj.|蝙蝠翼状的|Shaped like the wing of a bat.|She wore a batwing top. 她穿着一件蝙蝠衫。|近义词:wing-shaped, flappy|反义词:(no direct antonym) |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bat + wing.  The word "bat" comes from Old English "bætt," probably related to Old Norse "ledhrblaka" (leather flapper). "Wing" comes from Old English "winge," related to Old Norse "vængr."
