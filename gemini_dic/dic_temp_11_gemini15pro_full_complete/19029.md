betake:/bɪˈteɪk/
verb
前往；致力于；专心于
to go to a place, especially for rest or shelter; to apply or devote oneself
He betook himself to his studies. 他专心于学习。
近义词：resort, apply, dedicate
反义词：abandon, forsake
词性：v.

Etymology:
From Middle English *bitaken*, from Old English *betācan* (“to entrust, commit, hand over, deliver, assign, bequeath, give, grant, apply, devote”), from *be-* (prefix meaning “about, around, on all sides, completely”) + *tācan* (“to show, teach, assign, direct, order”). Cognate with Dutch *betekenen* (“to signify, mean, denote”), German *bezeichnen* (“to mark, denote, label”), Gothic *����������������* (biteihan, “to proclaim, announce”).
