nonreactor:/ˌnɒnriˈæktər/ |n.|非反应堆;非反应者|Something that is not a reactor, typically referring to a facility or substance that does not participate in a nuclear reaction.  It can also refer to a person or entity that does not respond or react to a stimulus.|The facility was classified as a nonreactor and therefore exempt from certain regulations.  这个设施被归类为非反应堆，因此免除某些法规。|The nonreactor material showed no signs of radiation.  这种非反应堆材料没有显示出任何辐射迹象。|Synonym: non-reactive (adjective), inert|Antonym: reactor, reactive|noun
