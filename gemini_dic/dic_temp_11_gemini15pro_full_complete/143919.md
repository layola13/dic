outprize: /ˌaʊtˈpraɪz/
verb
胜过，超越（尤指在比赛中）
to surpass in value or excellence, especially in competition
He outprized all his rivals in the contest. 他在比赛中胜过所有对手。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip, outrival
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
