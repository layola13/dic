overdependence:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈpɛndəns/| n.|过度依赖，过分依赖|Excessive reliance on someone or something; a state where one's ability to function is significantly impaired without the presence or support of another person or thing.|Her overdependence on her parents hindered her personal growth. 她对她父母的过度依赖阻碍了她的个人成长。|Overdependence on social media can lead to feelings of inadequacy. 过度依赖社交媒体会导致不安全感。|近义词：reliance, dependence|反义词：independence, self-reliance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From over- + dependence. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Dependence" comes from the Old French word "dependence" and ultimately from the Latin "dependere," meaning "to hang down from" or "to rely on."
