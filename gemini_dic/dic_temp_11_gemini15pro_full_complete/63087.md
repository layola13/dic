enseal:/ɪnˈsiːl/| vt.|密封，盖章|To enclose something in a seal, thereby protecting or authenticating it.|The documents were carefully ensealed in an envelope. 文件被小心地密封在一个信封里。|近义词：seal, close, secure|反义词:open, unseal|verb

enseal:/ɪnˈsiːl/| vt.|加封，盖章于|To affix a seal to something.|He ensealed the letter with wax. 他用蜡封住了信。|近义词：stamp, seal|反义词:open, break the seal|verb
