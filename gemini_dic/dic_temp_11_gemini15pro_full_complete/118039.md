menacing:/ˈmenɪsɪŋ/| adj.|威胁的，险恶的|Suggesting the presence of danger; threatening.|The dog growled menacingly, its teeth bared. 那条狗发出威胁的低吼，露出了牙齿。|Synonyms: threatening, intimidating, ominous, forbidding|Antonyms: comforting, reassuring, welcoming|adjective

menace:/ˈmenɪs/| n. v. |威胁，恐吓；威胁，恐吓|A person or thing that is likely to cause harm; a threat or danger. To threaten, especially in a vague or indirect way.|The rising crime rate is a menace to society.  犯罪率上升是对社会的威胁。  He menaced me with a knife. 他用刀子威胁我。|Synonyms: threat, danger, peril, hazard (n.); threaten, intimidate, bully (v.)|Antonyms: safety, security, protection (n.); protect, defend, safeguard (v.)|noun, verb
