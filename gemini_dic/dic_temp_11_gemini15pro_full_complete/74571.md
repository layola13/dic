fraught:/frɔːt/
adjective
充满…的；焦虑的
(of a situation or course of action) filled with or likely to result in (something undesirable)
The atmosphere was fraught with tension. 气氛非常紧张。

近义词:filled with, full of, charged with, laden with, replete with
反义词: devoid of, lacking
