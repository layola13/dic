confirmator:/kənˈfɜːrmətər/ | n. | 确认者；批准者；证实者 |  A person who confirms something; someone who establishes the truth or validity of something. | The bishop acted as the confirmator for the young people joining the church. 主教担任了加入教会的年轻人的确认者。| Synonyms: validator, ratifier, corroborator, authenticator | Antonyms: denier, refuter, contradictor | noun


confirmator:/kənˈfɜːrmətər/ | n. | 批准者 |  A person who approves or sanctions something.  | The president acted as the final confirmator for the new policy. 总统是新政策的最终批准者。| Synonyms: approver, sanctioner, authorizer | Antonyms: objector, opponent | noun
