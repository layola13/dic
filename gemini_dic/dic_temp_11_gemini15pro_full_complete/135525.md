nonpleading:/nɑːnˈpliːdɪŋ/
adjective
不答辩的，不抗辩的
Relating to or being a party to a lawsuit that does not file a pleading in response to a complaint.  A nonpleading defendant is one who has not filed an answer or other responsive pleading to the complaint.
The nonpleading defendant did not appear in court.  未答辩的被告没有出庭。
The plaintiff sought a default judgment against the nonpleading defendants. 原告寻求对未答辩的被告进行缺席判决。
Near Synonyms: defaulting, unresponsive
Antonyms: pleading, responsive
