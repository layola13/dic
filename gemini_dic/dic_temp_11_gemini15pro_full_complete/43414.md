crantara: /krænˈtɑːrə/ | n. | 悬崖，峭壁; (苏格兰) 险峻的岩石 | A high, steep rock face, especially in Scotland. |  The climber scaled the perilous crantara with ease.  登山者轻松攀上了危险的悬崖峭壁。 |  The crantara offered a breathtaking view of the coastline. 峭壁提供了令人叹为观止的海岸线景色。| 近义词: cliff, precipice, crag, bluff | 反义词: abyss, chasm | 词性: 名词


词源: From Scottish Gaelic *creanntara*, a rocky height, possibly related to *creag*, rock.
