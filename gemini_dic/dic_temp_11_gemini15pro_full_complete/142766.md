ottomanlike: /ˈɒtəmənlaɪk/ | adj. | 像奥斯曼式的，像土耳其式软垫长椅的 | Resembling an ottoman, a type of upholstered seat or low footstool without a back or arms, typically padded and covered in fabric or leather, often used in Middle Eastern and North African cultures. | The room was furnished with ottomanlike cushions scattered across the floor. 房间里摆放着像奥斯曼式样的靠垫，散落在地板上。| The low, plush sofa had an ottomanlike appearance.  这张矮矮的豪华沙发看起来像个奥斯曼长椅。 |  Similar: cushioned, plush, upholstered, squat | Antonym: austere, stark, rigid | adjective
