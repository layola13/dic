barmiest: /ˈbɑːmiɪst/ | adj. |  最疯狂的，最愚蠢的 |  Superlative adjective of "barmy," meaning extremely foolish or eccentric.  Originates from "barm," the froth on fermenting malt liquor, implying a lightheaded or foolish state. | That's the barmiest idea I've ever heard! 那是我听过的最疯狂的想法！|  He had the barmiest grin on his face. 他脸上露出了最愚蠢的笑容。 | Synonyms: craziest, maddest, daftest | Antonyms: sanest, most sensible | adjective
