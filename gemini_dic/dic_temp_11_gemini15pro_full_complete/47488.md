deaneries:/ˈdiːnərɪz/
n.
主教管区；院长管区；教务长管辖区
The administrative district of a dean, especially in the Church of England.
The deaneries of the diocese met to discuss the new policies.  主教管区的全体教长开会讨论新政策。
deanery:/ˈdiːnəri/
n.
主教管区；院长管辖区；教务长管辖区
The administrative district of a dean.
He was appointed to the deanery of York. 他被任命为约克主教管区的教长。
