mhm:/mˈhʌm/ /mˈhm/ | interjection | 嗯，是的，表示同意或理解 | An informal expression of agreement, acknowledgement, or understanding.  It's often used in spoken conversations and represents a non-verbal sound like a hum. | Example 1: "Are you coming to the party tonight?" "Mhm, definitely." 你今晚来参加派对吗？嗯，当然。

Example 2: "Mhm, that makes sense." 嗯，这很有道理。| Synonym: yes, yeah, uh-huh, okay | Antonym: no, nah, nope | part of speech: interjection
