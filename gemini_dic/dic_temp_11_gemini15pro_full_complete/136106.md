nonrecyclables:/ˌnɒnriːˈsaɪkləblz/ (US) /ˌnɒnriːˈsaɪkləblz/ (UK)
noun
不可回收物
Materials that cannot be recycled or reused.
The city has separate bins for recyclables and nonrecyclables. 城市设置了不同的垃圾桶来分别收集可回收垃圾和不可回收垃圾。
Many nonrecyclables end up in landfills. 许多不可回收物最终被填埋。
non-reusable, non-recoverable
recyclables, reusable materials
