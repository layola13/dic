need-to-know:/ˌniːd tə ˈnoʊ/ | adj. | 需要知道的，必知的 | Essential information or knowledge that someone must have. | Example: The CEO only shared need-to-know information with the board members. 首席执行官只与董事会成员分享了必要的信息。 | Example:  This document contains all the need-to-know details about the project.  这份文件包含了所有关于这个项目的必要细节。 | Synonym: essential, crucial, vital, critical | Antonym: unimportant, trivial, insignificant | adjective
