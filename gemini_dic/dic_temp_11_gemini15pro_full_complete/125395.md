multinodous:/ˌmʌltiˈnoʊdəs/
| adj. |多节的；多瘤的；多结的|Having many nodes or swellings.|The doctor discovered a multinodous goiter in the patient's neck. 医生在病人的脖子上发现了一个多节的甲状腺肿。|Synonyms: nodular, lumpy, bumpy, knobbed, gnarled|Antonyms: smooth, even, uniform|adjective

multinodular:/ˌmʌltiˈnɒdjʊlə(r)/
| adj. |多节的；多瘤的；多结的|Having many nodules or small, rounded lumps.|The radiologist identified a multinodular lesion on the patient's lung. 放射科医生在病人的肺部发现了一个多结的病灶。|Synonyms: nodular, lumpy, bumpy, knobbed, gnarled|Antonyms: smooth, even, uniform|adjective
