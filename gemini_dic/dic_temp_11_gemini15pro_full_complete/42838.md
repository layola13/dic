coursewares:/ˈkɔːrswɛrz/
n.
课件；课程软件
Educational materials designed for use in a course of study, often digital.
The professor uploaded the coursewares to the online learning platform. 教授将课件上传到在线学习平台。
The students accessed the coursewares through the university's website. 学生通过大学网站访问课件。
近义词：course materials, learning materials, teaching aids, educational resources
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "lack of resources")
