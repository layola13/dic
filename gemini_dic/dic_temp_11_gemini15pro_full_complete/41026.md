cope-in:/koʊp ˈɪn/| phrasal verb | 融入；参与；加入；处理；应付 | To involve or include someone in something, or to deal with a difficult situation. |

例句：
The new employee struggled to cope in with the fast-paced environment.
新员工很难适应快节奏的环境。

例句：
They coped in their neighbors in the party planning.
他们让邻居参与了派对的策划。

近义词：involve, include, manage, handle, deal with
反义词：exclude, leave out
词性：动词短语


词源：
"Cope" comes from Old French "couper" meaning "to strike, hit."  "In" is a common preposition indicating inclusion or involvement. The phrasal verb "cope in" combines these to signify managing or dealing with something effectively, often by including others or oneself in a process or situation.
