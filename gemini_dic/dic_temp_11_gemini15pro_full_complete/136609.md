nonsatiation:/ˌnɒnseɪˈʃeɪʃn/ /ˌnɑːnseɪˈʃeɪʃn/
n.
不满足，未饱和
The state of not being satiated; lack of satiety.
The nonsatiation of consumer demand drives economic growth. 消费者需求的未饱和推动了经济增长。
insatiability, unsatiatedness
satiation, satisfaction
noun
