malpractice: /mælˈpræktɪs/ | n. |渎职；玩忽职守；医疗事故| Improper, illegal, or negligent professional activity or treatment, especially by a medical practitioner, lawyer, or public official. |  The lawyer was sued for malpractice.  这位律师因玩忽职守而被起诉。| synonyms: negligence, misconduct, dereliction of duty | antonyms: due diligence, proper conduct, professionalism | noun

malpractice: /mælˈpræktɪs/ | n. |  医疗事故；治疗不当 | Failure by a health professional to meet accepted standards of diagnosis, treatment, or care, resulting in injury to the patient. | The doctor was accused of malpractice after the patient died.  该医生在病人死后被指控医疗事故。| synonyms: medical negligence, medical error, substandard care | antonyms: proper medical care, due diligence in medical practice | noun
