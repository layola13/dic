nah:/nɑː/| interjection|不，没有，不是|An informal way of saying no.  It expresses a lack of interest or disagreement.|例句：
"Want to go to the movies?" "Nah, I'm tired."
“想去电影院吗？” “不，我累了。”

例句：
"Is that your car?" "Nah, it's my brother's."
“那是你的车吗？” “不，是我兄弟的。”|近义词：no, nope, uh-uh|反义词:yeah, yes, yep|词性:interjection
