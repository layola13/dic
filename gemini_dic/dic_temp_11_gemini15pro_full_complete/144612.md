outwhirling:/ˌaʊtˈhwɜːrlɪŋ/
|n.|外旋，向外旋转|The outwhirling of the helicopter blades created a powerful downdraft. 直升机桨叶的外旋产生强大的下沉气流。|
|v.|向外旋转|The leaves outwhirled in the gust of wind.  树叶在阵风中向外旋转。|
|英文解析|Outwhirling combines "out" (indicating outward direction) and "whirling" (rotating rapidly). It describes a motion of spinning or rotating outwards from a central point.|
|近义词|rotating, spinning, whirling, revolving|
|反义词|inwhirling|
|词性|Noun, Verb|
|词源|From "out-" + "whirl" + "-ing"|
