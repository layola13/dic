attractable:/əˈtræktəbl/
adjective
可吸引的；有吸引力的
Capable of being attracted; attractive.
Iron filings are attractable to a magnet. 铁屑会被磁铁吸引。
近义词: appealing, alluring, magnetic, charming
反义词: repellent, repulsive, unappealing, unattractive
词性: adjective

attractable:/əˈtræktəbl/
adjective
易受…影响的；易患…疾病的
Susceptible or vulnerable to something.
Some people are more attractable to certain diseases than others.  有些人比其他人更容易患某些疾病。
近义词: susceptible, vulnerable, prone, liable
反义词: resistant, immune, impervious
词性: adjective
