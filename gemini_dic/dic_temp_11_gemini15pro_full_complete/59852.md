ectosteal:/ˌɛktoʊˈstiːəl/
adjective
外骨骼的; 由外胚层形成的
Relating to or derived from the ectoderm, the outermost layer of cells or tissue of an embryo in early development.
The ectosteal layer gives rise to the skin and nervous system. 外胚层会形成皮肤和神经系统。
The shark's exoskeleton is composed of ectosteal material. 鲨鱼的外骨骼是由外胚层物质组成的。
Synonyms: exoskeletal, dermal
Antonyms: endoskeletal
