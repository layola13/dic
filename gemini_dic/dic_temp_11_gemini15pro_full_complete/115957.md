marries:/ˈmæriz/ (US) /ˈmærɪz/ (UK)| v.|结婚，嫁，娶|To take (someone) as a husband or wife.  To perform the ceremony of marriage for (a couple).|John marries Mary next month. 约翰下个月要和玛丽结婚。|The priest married the couple in a small chapel. 牧师在一个小教堂里为这对新人举行了婚礼。|synonyms: weds, espouses|antonyms: divorces, separates|verb
