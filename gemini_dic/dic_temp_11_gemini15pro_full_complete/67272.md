exquisitism:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪtɪzəm/| n. |讲究；精致；唯美主义|Excessive refinement and fastidiousness, especially in literary or artistic taste.|His art collection reflects his exquisitism and attention to detail. 他的艺术收藏反映了他的讲究和对细节的关注。|近义词：refinement, elegance, sophistication, fastidiousness|反义词：crudeness, coarseness, simplicity, artlessness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *exquisitisme*, from exquisit + -isme.  Ultimately from Latin *exquisītus* (“carefully sought out”), past participle of *exquīrō* (“seek out carefully, examine, inquire into”), from *ex* (“out”) + *quaerō* (“seek, ask”).
