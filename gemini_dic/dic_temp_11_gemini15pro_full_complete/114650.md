malfunctioning:/mælˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/
adjective
故障的，失灵的
Not working or operating correctly.
The malfunctioning robot caused a delay in production.  故障的机器人导致生产延误。
The technician quickly identified the malfunctioning component.  技术人员迅速确定了故障的部件。
近义词:broken, faulty, defective, inoperative
反义词:working, functioning, operational
词性:adjective

malfunction:/mælˈfʌŋkʃən/
noun
故障，失灵
A failure to function normally.
The system malfunction caused a power outage.  系统故障导致停电。
There was a malfunction in the engine.  发动机发生了故障。
近义词:failure, breakdown, glitch, error
反义词:success, operation
词性:noun

malfunction:/mælˈfʌŋkʃən/
verb
发生故障，失灵
To fail to function normally.
The computer started to malfunction after the power surge.  电脑在电压激增后开始出现故障。
The brakes malfunctioned, causing the accident.  刹车失灵，导致了事故。
近义词:fail, break down, crash
反义词:work, function, operate
词性:verb


词源:
mal- (bad) + function
