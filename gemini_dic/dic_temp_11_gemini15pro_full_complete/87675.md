hexagynia:/ˌhɛksəˈɡɪniə/
noun
六雌蕊植物
A plant having six pistils or styles in each flower.
Example: The flower exhibits hexagynia, with six distinct styles emerging from the ovary. 这朵花展现了六雌蕊的特征，子房中长出了六个不同的花柱。
