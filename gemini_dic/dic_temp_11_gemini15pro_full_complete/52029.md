dialectic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪk/ | n. adj.|辩证法，辩证的|A method of argument that involves some sort of contradictory process between opposing sides.|Socrates was a master of dialectic. 苏格拉底是辩证法大师。|synonyms: argumentation, debate, reasoning|antonyms: agreement, harmony|noun

dialectic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪk/ | adj.|辩证的|Relating to dialectic or employing it.|He has a dialectic approach to problem-solving. 他用辩证的方法解决问题。|synonyms: logical, analytical, rational|antonyms: illogical, irrational|adjective
