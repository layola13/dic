espinel:/ˌɛspɪˈnɛl/ | n. |尖晶石；尖晶石色；尖晶石激光器 |A hard, glassy mineral consisting of an oxide of magnesium and aluminum, typically occurring as variously colored octahedral crystals.  A synthetic ruby substitute. A laser that uses synthetic spinel as its amplifying medium.| The ring featured a beautiful red espinel. 这枚戒指上有一颗美丽的红色尖晶石。 | synonyms: spinel ruby, balas ruby, ruby spinel | antonyms: N/A | noun
