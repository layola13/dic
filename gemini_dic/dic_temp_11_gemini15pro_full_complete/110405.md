likability:/ˌlaɪkəˈbɪləti/| n.|好感度，受欢迎程度|The quality of being pleasant and easy to like.  It refers to how agreeable, pleasant, and appealing someone or something is to others.|Her likability made her a popular choice for class president. 她很受欢迎，所以被选为班长。|近义词：appeal, charm, charisma, popularity|反义词：unpopularity, offensiveness|noun
