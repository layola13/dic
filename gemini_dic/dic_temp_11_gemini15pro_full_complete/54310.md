dishabited:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhæbɪtɪd/
adjective
无人居住的；荒废的
Deprived of inhabitants; deserted.
The dishabited village stood silent and empty.  这个无人居住的村庄寂静而空旷。
The earthquake left the city dishabited. 地震过后，这座城市变得无人居住。
Synonyms: deserted, abandoned, uninhabited, desolate
Antonyms: inhabited, populated, occupied
