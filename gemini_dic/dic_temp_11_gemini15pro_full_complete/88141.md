hilarities:/hɪˈlærətiz/
noun
欢闹，欢笑，滑稽场面
noisy fun or laughter
The party was full of hilarities.  派对上充满了欢声笑语。
synonyms: amusement, laughter, mirth, merriment, gaiety
antonyms: sadness, gloom, seriousness
n.

Etymology:
From Late Latin *hilaritas, from Latin hilaris (“cheerful, merry”), from Proto-Italic *hilaːris, probably from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to shout, resound; to incline, bend”).
