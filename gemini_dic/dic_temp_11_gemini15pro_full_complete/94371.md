implanter:/ɪmˈplæntər/ | n. | 植入者，移植者 |  A person or device that implants something.  A surgeon who implants medical devices is an implanter.  Also refers to a machine used in semiconductor manufacturing to implant ions into a material. | The implanter skillfully inserted the pacemaker.  植入者熟练地插入了起搏器。

The ion implanter is crucial for creating precise doping profiles in semiconductors. 离子注入机对于在半导体中创建精确的掺杂分布至关重要。 | 近义词: inserter, injector | 反义词: remover, extractor | 词性: noun
