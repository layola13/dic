demonstrationist:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃənɪst/
|n.|示威者，游行者|A person who participates in demonstrations, especially one who regularly or habitually does so.  Someone who engages in public protests or rallies.|例句：The demonstrationists marched to the capitol building. 示威者向国会大厦进发。

例句：The police were prepared for the arrival of the demonstrationists. 警察为示威者的到来做好了准备。
|近义词：protester, demonstrator, marcher, rallyist, activist|反义词：conformist, bystander|词性: 名词
