greyly:/ˈɡreɪli/| adv.|灰白地，阴沉地|In a grey or dull manner; bleakly or drearily.|The sky lowered greyly over the city. 天空阴沉沉地笼罩着城市。|近义词：drably, gloomily, somberly|反义词：brightly, cheerfully|词性:副词

Etymology:
From grey + -ly.  "Grey" comes from Old English *grǣg, from Proto-Germanic *grēwaz.  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial suffix, derived from Old English -līce.
