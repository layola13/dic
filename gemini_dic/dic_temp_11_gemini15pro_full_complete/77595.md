geekdom: /ˈɡiːk.dəm/
noun
极客的世界；极客文化；痴迷于某事的状态
The state of being a geek, or the realm of geeks.  A place or community of geeks. An obsession or enthusiasm for a particular subject.
例句：He found his place in the geekdom of computer programming. 他在计算机编程的极客世界找到了自己的位置。

例句：Her geekdom extends beyond computers to encompass all things sci-fi. 她的极客爱好不仅仅局限于电脑，还包括所有科幻的东西。

近义词：nerdiness, fandom, obsession, enthusiasm
反义词：normality, mainstream

词性: n.
