neurotendinous:/ˌnjʊərəˈtɛndɪnəs/
adjective
神经腱的
Relating to both nerves and tendons.  Specifically, referring to the Golgi tendon organ, a proprioceptive sensory receptor organ that senses changes in muscle tension.
The neurotendinous junction is where the muscle fibers meet the tendon.  神经腱连接处是肌纤维与肌腱相遇的地方。
The neurotendinous reflex helps protect muscles from excessive force. 神经腱反射有助于保护肌肉免受过度用力。
Synonyms:  musculotendinous
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective
