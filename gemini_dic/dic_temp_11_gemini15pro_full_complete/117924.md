melton:/ˈmɛltən/| n.|麦尔登呢，一种厚实紧密的羊毛织物|A heavy, tightly woven, smooth-faced woolen cloth used for coats, jackets, etc.  A type of heavy, smooth woolen cloth used especially for making overcoats.|例句：He wore a melton overcoat. 他穿着一件麦尔登呢大衣。

例句：Melton cloth is known for its durability. 麦尔登呢以其耐用性而闻名。|近义词：broadcloth, kersey, beaver cloth|反义词:|noun
