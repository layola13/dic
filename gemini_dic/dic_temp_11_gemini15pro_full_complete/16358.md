barbecuing: /ˈbɑːrbɪˌkjuːɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɑːbɪˌkjuːɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|烧烤，户外烤肉|The act of cooking food on a barbecue grill, often outdoors.| We were barbecuing steaks and burgers. 我们在烤牛排和汉堡。|Synonyms: grilling, cookout|Antonyms:  baking, roasting|gerund/present participle

Etymology: From barbecue (n.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles).  The word "barbecue" itself comes from the Taino word "barabicu," meaning a framework of sticks used for cooking meat.
