craver:/ˈkreɪvər/| n. |渴望者；贪求者|A person who strongly desires or yearns for something.|He's a craver of knowledge and always eager to learn new things. 他是一个求知若渴的人，总是渴望学习新事物。|近义词:addict, devotee, enthusiast, fiend, fan, aficionado|反义词:hater, detractor|词性:noun
Etymology: From the verb "crave" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Crave" comes from Old English "crafian" meaning "to ask, demand, beg," with cognates in other Germanic languages.
