orbitosphenoid:/ˌɔːrbɪtəˈsfɪnɔɪd/ | n. | 蝶骨眼眶部 |  A paired bone or cartilage forming part of the lateral wall of the braincase, located posterior to the presphenoid. | The orbitosphenoid forms part of the eye socket.  蝶骨眼眶部形成了眼窝的一部分。 |  The paleontologist identified the fossil by its distinctive orbitosphenoid. 古生物学家通过其独特的蝶骨眼眶部识别了这块化石。 | sphenoid bone, lateral wall of the braincase |  (no commonly used antonym) | noun
