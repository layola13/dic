etiquette:/ˈetɪkət/ (US), /ˈetɪket/ (UK)| n. |礼仪，礼节|The rules of correct or polite behavior in society or among members of a particular profession or group. |She has impeccable etiquette. 她礼仪无可挑剔。|Synonym: decorum, protocol, manners, propriety|Antonym: rudeness, impoliteness|Noun
