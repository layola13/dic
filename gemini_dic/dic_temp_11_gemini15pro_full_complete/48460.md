deeming:/ˈdiːmɪŋ/
verb
认为，视为
Considering or judging something in a particular way.
The government deemed the area unsafe. 政府认为该地区不安全。
Synonyms: considering, regarding, judging, esteeming
Antonyms: disregarding, ignoring
verb
