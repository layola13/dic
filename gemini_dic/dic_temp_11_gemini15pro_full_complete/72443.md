fluke:/fluːk/| n.|侥幸；意外的成功；意外；偶然事件；(锚的)爪；侥幸击中(或达到的)球；(鱼的)尾鳍|An unlikely chance occurrence, especially a surprising piece of luck.|He won by a sheer fluke. 他完全是侥幸获胜。|Synonyms:accident, coincidence, stroke of luck|Antonyms:intention, plan|noun

fluke:/fluːk/| v.|侥幸成功；意外击中(或达到)|To achieve or gain something by luck rather than skill.|He fluked the exam. 他考试侥幸过关。|Synonyms:chance, luck into|Antonyms:earn, work for|verb

注意事项:
fluke作名词时，指“意外的成功、侥幸”，强调偶然性和运气成分。作动词时，指“侥幸成功”，强调没有付出相应的努力或技能。
