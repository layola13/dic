conventional:/kənˈvenʃənəl/| adj.|传统的，常规的，惯例的|Based on or in accordance with what is generally done or believed.|例句：Her views on marriage are rather conventional. 她对婚姻的看法相当传统。|例句：It was a conventional wedding ceremony. 那是一个传统的婚礼仪式。|近义词：traditional, customary, usual, orthodox, standard|反义词：unconventional, unorthodox, non-traditional, radical, innovative|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French *conventionel, from late Latin *conventionalis, from Latin conventio(n-) ‘meeting, agreement’, from convenire (see convene).
