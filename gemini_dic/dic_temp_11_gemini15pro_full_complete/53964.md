discredits:/dɪˈskredɪts/| v. (third person singular simple present discredits, present participle discrediting, simple past and past participle discredited)|使失去信誉；败坏…的名声|to harm the reputation of, especially by making people doubt someone or something|The scandal discredited the politician. 这桩丑闻败坏了这位政治家的名声。|synonyms: disgrace, dishonor, shame, tarnish, smear, defame|antonyms: honor, praise, commend, extol|verb

discredit:/dɪˈskredɪt/| n.|名誉丧失；丢脸；信誉丧失|loss or damage to someone's reputation; something that causes this|The allegations brought discredit upon the entire organization. 这些指控给整个组织带来了耻辱。|synonyms: disgrace, dishonor, shame, stigma, reproach|antonyms: honor, praise, credit, esteem|noun
