misdirection:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛkʃən/ | n. |误导，转移注意力|the act of directing someone to a wrong place or in a wrong direction.  The magician's skillful misdirection fooled the entire audience.| The magician used misdirection to conceal his trick. 魔术师用误导来掩盖他的戏法。| synonyms: deception, distraction, diversion | antonyms: guidance, direction | noun
