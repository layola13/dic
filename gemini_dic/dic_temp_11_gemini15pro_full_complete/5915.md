ambiparous:/æmˈbɪpərəs/
adjective
两性生殖的；左右手都善用的
(of an animal) bearing both male and female reproductive organs.  Able to use both hands with equal skill.
例句：Some species of fish are known to be ambiparous, possessing both male and female reproductive organs.  有些鱼类是众所周知的两性生殖的，同时拥有雄性和雌性生殖器官。

例句：An ambiparous surgeon is a valuable asset to any operating room.  一个双手都善用的外科医生对任何手术室来说都是宝贵的资产。
近义词：ambidextrous, bisexual (in the biological sense)
反义词：unilateral
