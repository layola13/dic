gazel:/ɡəˈzɛl/ | n. |瞪羚，一种小羚羊；(尤指波斯语或乌尔都语)抒情诗，一种格律诗|A slender, graceful antelope of the genus Gazella, found in open country in Africa and Asia, known for its leaping ability. Also, a lyric poem with a fixed form, especially in Persian or Urdu literature. |例句：The gazelle leaped gracefully across the savanna. 瞪羚优雅地跃过大草原。

例句：He wrote a beautiful gazelle in the traditional style. 他用传统风格写了一首优美的抒情诗。 |近义词：antelope, springbok (for the animal); lyric, ode (for the poem)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French gazelle, from Arabic *ghazal*, from *ghazal* "to flirt, to spin (thread)", because of its graceful movements. The poetic sense comes from the subject matter of love and beauty often found in such poems.
