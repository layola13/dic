in bulk:/ɪn bʌlk/
| adv. adj.|大量，批量；散装|Buying in bulk is often cheaper.  批量购买通常更便宜。|We purchased the screws in bulk. 我们批量购买了螺丝。|近义词：wholesale, in large quantities, by the ton|反义词:individually, separately|词性:副词，形容词
词源: From Middle English *bulke (“heap, mass”), from Old Norse *bulki (“cargo, heap”), from Proto-Germanic *bulkijǭ (“pile, heap”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰulǵ- (“to swell, bulge”).
