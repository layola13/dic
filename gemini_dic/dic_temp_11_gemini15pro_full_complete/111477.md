loaders:/ˈloʊdərz/| n.|装载机；装货机；加载器|A machine or person that loads something, especially a large vehicle such as a truck or ship, or a device that loads data or programs into a computer.|The loaders were busy filling the trucks with gravel. 装载机正忙着往卡车上装砾石。|synonyms: loading machines, excavators, front-end loaders|antonyms: unloaders, dischargers|noun
