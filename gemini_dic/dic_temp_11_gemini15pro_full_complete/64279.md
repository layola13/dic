equitabilities: /ˌɛkwɪtəˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
公平性；公正性；合理性
The quality of being fair and impartial; fairness.
The equitabilities of the new tax system are being debated.  新的税收制度的公平性正在被辩论。
近义词: fairness, justice, impartiality, evenness
反义词: unfairness, injustice, bias, partiality
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源:  From Late Middle English *equitablete, from Old French equitableté, from Latin aequitabilitas, from aequitabilis (“equitable”). Equivalent to equitable +‎ -ity.
