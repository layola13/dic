limitary:/ˈlɪmɪtəri/
adjective
限制性的；界限的
Restricting or confining within limits.
The fence served as a limitary boundary, preventing the animals from wandering too far.  这栅栏起到了限制边界的作用，防止动物走得太远。
The government imposed limitary measures on trade. 政府对贸易实施了限制性措施。
近义词: restrictive, confining, limiting
反义词: expansive, unlimited, boundless
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *līmitārius, from līmit-, stem of līmes (“limit, boundary”).
