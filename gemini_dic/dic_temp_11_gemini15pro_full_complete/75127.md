frisket:/ˈfrɪskɪt/| n.|遮蔽膜，保护膜|A thin sheet of metal, plastic, or paper used to mask off areas of a drawing or painting that are not to be worked on.|The artist used a frisket to protect the edges of the design while she airbrushed the background.|近义词：mask, stencil, template|反义词:|noun
