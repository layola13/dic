cherishable:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃəbl/
adjective
值得珍惜的
Worth keeping and treasuring; deserving to be cherished.
She gave him a cherishable gift on their anniversary. 她在他俩的周年纪念日送给他一份值得珍惜的礼物。
The memories we made together are cherishable. 我们共同创造的回忆是值得珍惜的。
synonyms: precious, treasured, valued, beloved, dear
antonyms: worthless, unimportant, trivial
