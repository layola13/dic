gutturo:/ɡʊˈtʊroʊ/ /ɡʌˈtʊəroʊ/
adjective
喉音的; 粗声的; 浊音的
produced in the throat; harsh-sounding; guttural.
A guttural sound emanated from the back of his throat.  他喉咙深处发出一种粗哑的声音。
近义词:throaty, harsh, rasping, gravelly
反义词:clear, melodious, smooth, soft
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *gutturālis, from Latin guttur (“throat”).
