multilobed:/ˌmʌltiˈləʊbd/ | adj. | 多裂的；多瓣的 | Having multiple lobes or rounded divisions. | The multilobed leaves of the oak tree are easily recognizable. 橡树的多裂叶子很容易辨认。| The doctor examined the patient's multilobed liver. 医生检查了病人多叶的肝脏。 |  synonym: multipartite, polylobate | antonym: entire, undivided | adjective
