fanatical:/fəˈnætɪkl/ | adj. | 狂热的，入迷的 | Marked by excessive enthusiasm and uncritical devotion. | He's a fanatical supporter of the local football team. 他是当地足球队的狂热支持者。

She's fanatical about keeping her house clean. 她对保持房子清洁非常狂热。

近义词: obsessive, zealous, fervent, ardent | 反义词: moderate, apathetic, indifferent | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from French fanatique, or from late Latin fanaticus ‘inspired by a deity, frenzied’, from Latin fanum ‘temple’. The original sense was ‘possessed by a god or demon’, hence ‘mad, frantic’.
