cuddly:/ˈkʌdli/| adj.|可爱的，令人想拥抱的|Soft and designed to be hugged or snuggled.|The kitten was so cuddly I couldn't resist picking it up.  这只小猫太可爱了，我忍不住把它抱起来。|近义词：huggable, snuggly, soft, cozy|反义词:unpleasant, prickly, cold|词性:形容词
Etymology: From cuddle + -y.  Cuddle comes from the Middle English word *cudden, meaning "to hug."
