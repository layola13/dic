oilskinned:/ˈɔɪlˌskɪnd/| adj.|油性皮肤的，涂油防水的|Having skin that is oily or appears to be oiled; made of oilskin (waterproofed or oiled cloth).|The oilskinned fisherman braved the stormy seas.  涂油防水衣的渔夫冒着暴风雨出海。|The sailor wore an oilskinned jacket. 水手穿着一件油布夹克。|近义词：waterproof, water-repellent, greasy|反义词：dry, absorbent|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From oil + skinned.  Oil refers to the oil used to treat the fabric, and skinned refers to being covered or coated like skin.
