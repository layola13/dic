moated:/ˈmoʊtɪd/| adj.|有护城河的；壕沟围绕的|Surrounded by a moat (a deep, wide ditch, often filled with water, surrounding a castle, fortification, or town as a defense against attack).|例句：The moated castle stood on a hill overlooking the valley.  那座带护城河的城堡矗立在可以俯瞰山谷的山丘上。

例句：They lived in a grand, moated manor house.  他们住在一座宏伟的有护城河的庄园里。|近义词：protected, fortified, defended|反义词:unprotected, undefended|词性:adjective
词源:From Middle English *motyd, from Old French moté (“moated”), past participle of moter (“to provide with a moat”), from mote (“moat”).
