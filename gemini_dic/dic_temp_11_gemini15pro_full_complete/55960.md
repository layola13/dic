djs:/ˈdiːdʒeɪz/ (plural)| n.|DJs (disc jockeys)|A person who plays recorded music, typically at a party or in a club or at a radio station.| Example: The DJs at the club played a mix of electronic music.  这家俱乐部的DJ播放了各种电子音乐。|Example:  Many radio DJs have their own shows. 很多电台DJ都有自己的节目。|Synonym:  disc jockey, mixer, selector|Antonym: N/A|noun
