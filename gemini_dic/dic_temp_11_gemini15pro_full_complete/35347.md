co-estate:/koʊ.ɪˈsteɪt/
n.
共同财产; 共有地产
A co-estate is property owned jointly by two or more people.
John and his sister inherited their parents' house as a co-estate.
约翰和他的妹妹共同继承了父母的房子。
The siblings were co-estates in the family business.
兄弟姐妹们是家族企业的共同业主。
Synonyms: joint property, joint ownership, shared ownership
Antonyms: sole ownership, individual ownership
