demonic:/dɪˈmɒnɪk/ /diˈmɑːnɪk/| adj.|恶魔的；魔鬼似的；着魔的|Of or like a demon; possessed or influenced by a demon; intensely energetic and driven.|例句：He had a demonic gleam in his eyes. 他的眼中闪现着恶魔般的光芒。

例句：She worked with a demonic energy. 她以一种着了魔般的精力工作。

近义词：fiendish, devilish, satanic, infernal, hellish|反义词：angelic, divine, heavenly|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *daemonicus, from Latin daemon “demon” (from Greek daimōn) + -icus.
