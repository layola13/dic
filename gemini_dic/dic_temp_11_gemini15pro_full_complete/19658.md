bilaminar:/baɪˈlæmɪnər/
adjective
双层的，两层的
Composed of two layers, laminae, or membranes.
The bilaminar disc is a crucial stage in early embryonic development.  双胚层胚盘是早期胚胎发育的关键阶段。
The bilaminar embryonic disc gives rise to the amniotic cavity and yolk sac. 双层胚胎盘形成羊膜腔和卵黄囊。
synonym: two-layered, double-layered
antonym: monolayer, single-layered
词性: adjective
