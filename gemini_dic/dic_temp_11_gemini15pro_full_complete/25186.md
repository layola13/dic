buddha:/ˈbʊdə/| n.|佛陀，佛|A person who has attained Buddhahood.|例句：The Buddha taught the Four Noble Truths. 佛陀传授了四圣谛。

例句：Many people visit the temple to pray to the Buddha. 很多人去寺庙拜佛。|近义词：Enlightened One, Tathagata|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Sanskrit *buddha*, meaning "awakened one" or "enlightened one".  The Sanskrit term is derived from the root *budh-* meaning "to awaken," "to know," or "to become aware."
