monkflower:/ˈmʌŋkˌflaʊər/ | n. | 僧帽花；猴面花 | A plant of the genus *Mimulus*, especially *Mimulus guttatus* (yellow monkey flower), with yellow flowers that resemble a monkey's face.  The name "monkflower" likely comes from the resemblance of the flower to a monk's cowl or hood. | The monkflower thrives in moist environments. 僧帽花在潮湿的环境中生长茂盛。

The vibrant yellow monkflower brightened the meadow.  鲜艳的黄色僧帽花使草地更加明亮。 | 近义词：monkey flower, mimulus | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
