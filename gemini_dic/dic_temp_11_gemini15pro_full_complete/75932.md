fungicides:/ˈfʌndʒɪsaɪdz/ | n. |杀菌剂|A substance that destroys fungi or inhibits their growth.| Farmers use fungicides to protect crops from fungal diseases. 农民使用杀菌剂来保护农作物免受真菌病害。|Synonyms: antimycotic, antifungal |Antonyms: fertilizer (in a broad sense, as it promotes growth, although not directly related) | noun


Etymology: From the Latin *fungus* (mushroom, fungus) + *-cide*  (killer, from Latin *caedere*, to kill).
