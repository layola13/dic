ictic:/ˈɪktɪk/| adj.|抽搐的，痉挛的|Relating to or characterized by a tic; spasmodic.|例句：The patient exhibited ictic movements of the face.  病人面部表现出抽搐的动作。|例句：Ictic symptoms can be distressing and disruptive. 抽搐症状可能令人痛苦和造成干扰。|近义词：spasmodic, convulsive|反义词: relaxed, steady|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *ictique*, from Greek *iktikos* “fit for striking, of the nature of a stroke,” from *iktos* “stroke, blow.”
