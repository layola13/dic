megafauna:/ˈmɛɡəˌfɔːnə/ | n. | 巨型动物群 | Large or giant animals of a particular region or time period. |  The Pleistocene megafauna included mammoths and saber-toothed cats. 更新世的巨型动物群包括猛犸象和剑齿虎。

The Australian megafauna has suffered greatly since the arrival of humans. 自从人类到来之后，澳大利亚的巨型动物群遭受了巨大的损失。 | 近义词: large animals, giant animals | 反义词: microfauna | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *megas* "great" + *fauna* "animal life".
