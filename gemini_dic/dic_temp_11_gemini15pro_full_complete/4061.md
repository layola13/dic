aiel:/ˈaɪ.iːl/
n.
艾伊尔人 (Aiel)
A fictional group of nomadic people in Robert Jordan's "The Wheel of Time" fantasy series. They are fierce warriors, especially the women, and have a strong sense of honor and duty.
The Aiel are renowned for their skill with the spear.  艾伊尔人以其精湛的枪法而闻名。
Rand al'Thor discovered his Aiel heritage.  兰德·艾索发现了他的艾伊尔血统。
近义词:  clansmen, tribespeople (in a general sense)
反义词:  (No direct antonym; could consider settled peoples or enemies within the series)
词性: noun
