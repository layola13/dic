gaudiness:/ˈɡɔːdinəs/| n.|俗丽；艳俗；炫耀|The gaudiness of the decorations was overwhelming.  装饰的艳俗令人难以忍受。|例句：The gaudiness of the carnival costumes was a feast for the eyes. 嘉年华服装的艳丽是一场视觉盛宴。

例句：She found the gaudiness of the Las Vegas strip rather off-putting. 她觉得拉斯维加斯大道的俗气令人反感。|近义词：garishness, flashiness, showiness|反义词：simplicity, understatement, subtlety|词性:名词

词源:From gaudy + -ness.  来自gaudy（华而不实的）+ -ness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。 Ultimately from Anglo-Norman gaudi, gaudie (“rejoicing, finery”), and akin to Old French gaudir (“to rejoice”). 最终来自盎格鲁-诺曼语的gaudi, gaudie（“欢庆，华丽的服饰”），与古法语gaudir（“欢欣”）相似。
