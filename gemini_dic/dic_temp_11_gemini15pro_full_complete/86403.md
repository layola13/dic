hedonistic:/ˌhiːdəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. |享乐主义的；快乐主义的|Characterized by the pursuit of pleasure; sensually self-indulgent. |  They lead a hedonistic lifestyle, focused on enjoying themselves. 他们过着享乐主义的生活方式，专注于享受自己。| synonyms: pleasure-seeking, self-indulgent, luxurious, sybaritic | antonyms: ascetic, austere | adjective
