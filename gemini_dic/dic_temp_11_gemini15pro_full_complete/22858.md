boswellism: /ˈbɒzwɛlɪzəm/ /ˈbɑːzwɛlɪzəm/
n.
波斯威尔主义；对伟人崇拜和记录其言行的行为
The practice of detailed biographical recording, especially of conversations, as notably exemplified by James Boswell in his "Life of Samuel Johnson."
He kept a detailed journal for years, a clear case of Boswellism. 他多年来坚持写详细的日记，这显然是波斯威尔主义的表现。
近义词: hero-worship, idolatry, biographer
反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可考虑apathy, indifference)
词性: noun

词源:  Named after James Boswell (1740-95), the Scottish biographer of Samuel Johnson. 源于詹姆斯·波斯威尔（1740-95），他是塞缪尔·约翰逊的苏格兰传记作家。
