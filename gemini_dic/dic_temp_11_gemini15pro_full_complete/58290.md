drused:/druːzd/ | adj. | 晶簇状的；表面有晶簇的 | Covered with small crystals. | The drused surface of the amethyst geode glittered in the light. 紫水晶晶洞的晶簇状表面在光下闪闪发光。| Example 2: The mineral specimen was described as being heavily drused with quartz crystals.  该矿物标本被描述为密布着石英晶体晶簇。 | Near Synonyms: crystal-covered, studded | Antonyms: smooth, unadorned | Word Type: Adjective

druse:/druːs/ | n. | 晶簇 | A crust of small crystals lining a cavity in rock. |  The druse inside the geode was a breathtaking sight. 晶洞内部的晶簇景象令人叹为观止。| Example 2: The geologist carefully chipped away at the rock to reveal the sparkling druse within. 地质学家小心翼翼地凿开岩石，露出了里面闪闪发光的晶簇。| Near Synonyms: crystal cluster, crystal lining | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: void, empty cavity) | Word Type: Noun

词源: From German *Druse, possibly from Low German drusen “to grow thick, sprout,” from Proto-Germanic *drus- “thicket, sprout.”  Related to Old English drysne "lees, dregs" and German drusig "dregsy."
