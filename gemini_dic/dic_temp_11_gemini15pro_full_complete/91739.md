hyperdevelopment:/ˌhaɪpərdiˈvɛləpmənt/ | n. |过度发展，超速发展|Excessive or accelerated development, often beyond what is considered sustainable or beneficial.| The hyperdevelopment of coastal areas has led to environmental damage.  沿海地区的过度开发导致了环境破坏。| The city's hyperdevelopment has resulted in a housing crisis. 这座城市的过度发展导致了住房危机。|近义词：overdevelopment, rapid growth, accelerated development |反义词: underdevelopment, stagnation |词性: noun

Etymology:  From hyper- "over, excessive" + development. The prefix "hyper-" comes from Greek "hyper" meaning "over, beyond." "Development" comes from the Old French "desveloper"  meaning "to unfold, unwrap."
