drearinesses:/ˈdrɪriːnɪsɪz/
n.
沉闷，乏味，单调
The state of being dreary; dismalness.  A long succession of dreary days.
The drearinesses of small-town life eventually drove her to the city. 小镇生活的单调乏味最终迫使她去了城市。
近义词:monotony, dullness, tedium, boredom
反义词:excitement, liveliness, vibrancy
词性:名词 (plural noun)

dreariness:/ˈdrɪriːnɪs/
n.
沉闷，乏味，单调
The state of being dreary; dismalness. A long succession of dreary days.
The dreariness of the winter months can be depressing. 冬日月份的沉闷可能会令人沮丧。
近义词:monotony, dullness, tedium, boredom
反义词:excitement, liveliness, vibrancy
词性:名词 (singular noun)

dreary:/ˈdrɪri/
adj.
沉闷的，阴沉的，令人沮丧的
Causing dejection; gloomy or dismal. Dull, bleak, and lifeless; depressing.
The weather was dreary and rainy. 天气阴沉多雨。
近义词:dull, gloomy, dismal, bleak
反义词:bright, cheerful, lively, exciting
词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *dreri, from Old English *drēorig (“sad, mournful, gory, bloody”), from Proto-Germanic *drauzigaz (“mournful, sad”).
