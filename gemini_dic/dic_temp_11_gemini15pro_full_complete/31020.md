challengingly:/ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋli/
adv.
具有挑战性地，艰难地
In a way that presents a challenge; difficultly.
The task was challengingly difficult, pushing the team to their limits.  这项任务极具挑战性的难度，将团队推向了极限。
The mountain rose challengingly before them. 这座山巍峨地耸立在他们面前，充满挑战。
近义词: arduously, difficultly, demanding, laboriously
反义词: easily, simply, effortlessly
词性: 副词

challenge:/ˈtʃælɪndʒ/
n. 挑战，质疑 vt. 向…挑战
Something that tests strength, skill, or ability.  To invite someone to compete.
He accepted the challenge to climb the mountain. 他接受了攀登这座山的挑战。
I challenge you to a game of chess. 我向你挑战一盘棋。
近义词: dare, provocation, contest
反义词: acceptance, agreement
词性: 名词，动词

challenging:/ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/
adj. 挑战性的，考验能力的
Testing one's abilities; demanding.
It was a challenging but rewarding experience. 这是一次充满挑战但又收获颇丰的经历。
The course proved to be very challenging. 这门课程被证明是非常具有挑战性的。
近义词: demanding, difficult, stimulating
反义词: easy, simple, undemanding
词性: 形容词
