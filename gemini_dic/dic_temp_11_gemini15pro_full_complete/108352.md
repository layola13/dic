laverock: /ˈlɑːrvək/ (UK), /ˈlɑːrvək/ (US) | n. | 云雀 | A small brown and grey bird that sings while flying high in the air, especially a skylark. | Example: The laverock ascended into the sky, filling the air with its melodious song. 云雀飞上天空，用它悠扬的歌声充满了空气。

Example:  He listened to the laverock's joyful trill as he walked through the fields. 他穿过田野时，聆听着云雀欢快的颤音。 | 近义词: skylark | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
