back-breaking:/ˈbækˌbreɪkɪŋ/
adjective
极其辛苦的，累断腰的
(of manual labor) requiring great physical exertion; very tiring.
例句：
Digging ditches is back-breaking work.
挖沟渠是极其辛苦的工作。

例句：
She worked long, back-breaking hours on the farm.
她在农场长时间辛苦地工作。

近义词：exhausting, grueling, strenuous, arduous, laborious
反义词：easy, effortless, simple

词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"back" + "breaking"  The word combines "back" referring to the spine, and "breaking" suggesting the intensity of the physical exertion as if it could break one's back.
