comedia:/kəˈmiːdiə/ /kəˈmidiə/
n.
喜剧; 喜剧性;
A comedia is a play, movie, or other performance that is intended to be funny.  It's derived from the Spanish word "comedia," which originally referred to any play with a happy ending, not just humorous ones. Eventually, the meaning narrowed to focus primarily on humorous works.
例句：
The comedia was a huge success, with audiences laughing throughout the performance.
这部喜剧非常成功，观众从头笑到尾。

例句：
She starred in several comedias during the Golden Age of Spanish theatre.
在西班牙戏剧的黄金时代，她主演过几部喜剧。

近义词：comedy, play, farce
反义词：tragedy, drama
