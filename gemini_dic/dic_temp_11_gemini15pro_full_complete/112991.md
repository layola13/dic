lumachel:/ˈluːməʃəl/ /ˈlʌməkɛl/
n.
贝壳大理石；贝壳化石石灰岩
A decorative building stone consisting of shells and shell fragments cemented together, often exhibiting iridescent colors.
This fireplace is made of lumachel, a beautiful and rare stone.  这个壁炉是用贝壳大理石做的，一种美丽而稀有的石头。

The ancient Romans used lumachel to decorate their villas. 古罗马人用贝壳化石石灰岩装饰他们的别墅。

近义词:shell limestone, coquina
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps granite or marble if contrasting decorative stones)
词性:noun
