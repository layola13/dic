crackest:/ˈkrækɪst/| adj.|最裂的；最脆的；最棒的（俚语）|Superlative form of "crack," meaning most cracked, crisp, or excellent (in slang).|例句：That's the crackest joke I've ever heard! 这是我听过的最好笑的笑话！

例句：He has the crackest voice, it's so unique. 他的声音最独特了，非常特别。|近义词：best, finest, top-notch, excellent (depending on context)|反义词：worst, poorest, flawed (depending on context)|词性:形容词

词源: From "crack," of uncertain origin, possibly imitative. The "-est" suffix forms the superlative.
