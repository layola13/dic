counterman:/ˈkaʊntərmæn/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəmæn/ (UK)| n. |柜台服务员，柜台售货员|A man who serves customers at a counter, especially in a shop or restaurant.|例句：The counterman handed me my sandwich. 柜台服务员把我的三明治递给了我。

例句：I asked the counterman for a pack of cigarettes. 我向柜台服务员要了一包香烟。

近义词：clerk, server, attendant|反义词:customer|词性:noun
