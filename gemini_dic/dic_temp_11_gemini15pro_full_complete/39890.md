constituting:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtɪŋ/| v.|构成，组成，建立，任命|A constitution is a set of fundamental principles or established precedents according to which a state or other organization is governed.|Twelve months constitute a year. 十二个月构成一年.|Synonyms: form, compose, make up, comprise, establish, create, set up, appoint|Antonyms: destroy, dissolve, dismantle|verb

constituting:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtɪŋ/| adj.|构成的，组成的|Serving to form something.|The act constituting the commission was passed in 1990.  设立该委员会的法案于1990年通过。|Synonyms: component, formative, essential|Antonyms: nonessential, inessential|adjective
