discoverable:/dɪˈskʌv(ə)rəbl/
adjective
可发现的；可查明的；可探知的
capable of being discovered
The truth is discoverable. 事实是可以查明的。

The hidden entrance to the cave was barely discoverable.  洞穴隐藏的入口几乎难以发现。

近义词:detectable, locatable, findable, traceable
反义词:undiscoverable, hidden, concealed
词性:adjective
