mesosternum:/ˌmiːzəʊˈstɜːrnəm/| n. |中胸骨|The middle part of the sternum, between the manubrium and the xiphoid process.  It is the largest part of the sternum in most vertebrates. |The surgeon carefully accessed the patient's mesosternum during the procedure.  外科医生在手术过程中小心翼翼地触及病人的中胸骨。

The mesosternum plays a crucial role in protecting the heart and lungs. 中胸骨在保护心脏和肺部方面起着至关重要的作用。 |近义词: gladiolus (anatomy term)|反义词: None|词性: noun
