antiphonal:/ænˈtɪfənəl/ | adj. | 对唱的，轮唱的 | Sung, recited, or played alternately by two groups, especially in church services or musical performances. | The antiphonal chanting of the monks created a mystical atmosphere.  僧侣们的对唱吟诵营造了一种神秘的氛围。| Example: The two choirs sang in antiphonal response.  两个唱诗班以对唱的方式应答。| Synonyms: responsive, alternating, reciprocal | Antonyms: unison, simultaneous | adjective

antiphonal:/ænˈtɪfənəl/ | n. | 对唱圣歌集 | A book containing antiphons.  | The choirmaster consulted the antiphonal to select the appropriate music.  唱诗班指挥查阅了对唱圣歌集，以选择合适的音乐。 | Synonyms: antiphonary | Antonyms: N/A | noun
