anti-polish:/ˌæntiˈpɒlɪʃ/ | adj. | 反抛光的，防抛光的，抗抛光的 | Opposing or preventing polishing; designed to resist polishing.  Relates to substances or treatments that inhibit the development of a polished surface. | This anti-polish treatment will keep the wood looking natural. 这种抗抛光处理将使木材保持自然的外观。

The car wax has anti-polish properties, so it won't become overly shiny.  这款车蜡具有抗抛光特性，因此不会变得过于闪亮。 | 近义词: matte, non-glossy, non-reflective | 反义词: polishing, glossy, reflective | 词性: 形容词
