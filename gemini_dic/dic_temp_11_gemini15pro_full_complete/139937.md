odometer:/oʊˈdɑːmɪtər/ (US) /əʊˈdɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |里程表；里程计|An instrument for measuring the distance traveled by a vehicle.|The odometer showed that the car had driven over 100,000 miles. 里程表显示这辆车已经行驶了超过10万英里。|Synonyms: milometer, trip meter|Antonyms: None|noun
