greenstone:/ˈɡriːnˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈɡriːnˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |绿岩，绿玉|A hard, dark green metamorphic rock, often used by the Maori of New Zealand to make tools and ornaments.|The Maori used greenstone to create beautiful carvings. 毛利人用绿岩制作美丽的雕刻。|Synonyms: nephrite, jade|Antonyms: N/A|noun

greenstone:/ˈɡriːnˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈɡriːnˌstəʊn/ (UK)| adj. |绿岩的，绿玉的|Made of or resembling greenstone.|The greenstone pendant was a treasured heirloom. 这块绿玉吊坠是一件珍贵的传家宝。|Synonyms: jade, nephritic|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
