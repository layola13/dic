laminates:/ˈlæmɪneɪts/| n. v.|层压材料，层压板；层压，使成层状|Thin sheets of material bonded together, typically wood, plastic, or metal.  The act of bonding such sheets together. |The table was made of laminates. 这张桌子是用层压材料制成的。|近义词：veneers, composites, overlays|反义词:solids, monoliths|词性:名词复数；动词第三人称单数


laminate:/ˈlæmɪneɪt/| v. n. adj.|层压，使成层状；层压材料；层压的|To bond thin sheets of material together. A material made by bonding thin sheets together. Made by bonding thin sheets together.|They laminate the wood to make it stronger. 他们把木头层压以使其更坚固。|近义词：bond, ply, veneer|反义词:separate, divide|词性:动词；名词；形容词

词源: From the Latin word "lamina" meaning "thin plate" or "layer."
