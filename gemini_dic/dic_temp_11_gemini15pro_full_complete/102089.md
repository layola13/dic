isopycnic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈpɪknɪk/ | adj. | 等密度的，同密度的 | Having the same or equal density. |  Centrifugation isopycnic separation relies on density differences of the particles. 离心等密度分离依赖于颗粒的密度差异。|  Isopycnic centrifugation is a useful technique for separating macromolecules. 等密度离心是分离大分子的有用技术。 |  isodense, equidense | antonym: heteropycnic(rarely used) | adjective

isopycnic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈpɪknɪk/ | n. | 等密度线 | A line or surface of equal or constant density. | The isopycnic surfaces in the ocean are often sloped. 海洋中的等密度面通常是倾斜的。 |  The isopycnics showed a clear stratification in the lake. 等密度线显示了湖泊中清晰的分层。| isodense line, equidensity line | antonym: heteropycnic line(rarely used) | noun
