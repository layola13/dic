noncircumstantially:/ˌnɒnsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəli/ | adv. | 非偶然地，不考虑具体情况地 | Without considering the specific circumstances; directly, simply. |  He addressed the issue noncircumstantially, stating the facts without embellishment. 他直接地谈论了这个问题，只陈述了事实，没有添油加醋。 |  She noncircumstantially denied the accusations. 她毫不犹豫地否认了这些指控。 | 近义词: directly, simply, straightforwardly, plainly | 反义词: circumstantially, indirectly, evasively | 词性: 副词
