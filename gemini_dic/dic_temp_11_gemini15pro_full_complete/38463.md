concessory:/kənˈsesəri/ | adj. |特许的， concessionary | relating to or involving a concession | The government offered concessory rates on electricity for senior citizens. 政府为老年人提供优惠电价。 | concessional, preferential | standard, regular | adjective

concessionary:/kənˈseʃənəri/ | n. |特许经营者， concessionaire | a person or company to whom a concession has been granted | The concessionary operates the food stalls at the stadium.  特许经营者在体育场经营食品摊位。 | concessionaire, franchisee |  | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concessionarius, from Latin concessio (“a granting, yielding”), from concedere (“to yield, grant”), from com- (“together”) + cedere (“to go, yield”).
