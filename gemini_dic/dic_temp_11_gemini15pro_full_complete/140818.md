on-level:/ˈɑːn ˈlɛvəl/| adj.|水平相当的，程度相符的；达到标准水平的|At or appropriate to the expected level of ability or achievement.  Matching the academic performance expected of a student in their current grade.  Neither below nor above average. |The on-level math class is challenging but manageable for most students.  这门水平相当的数学课对大多数学生来说既有挑战性，但也是可控的。

The teacher provided on-level reading material for the students. 老师为学生提供了符合他们水平的阅读材料。|近义词：standard, appropriate, suitable, average|反义词: advanced, below-level, remedial|词性:形容词
