debarred:/dɪˈbɑːrd/
verb
被禁止的，被阻止的
officially prevented from doing something
例句：He was debarred from entering the country.  他被禁止进入该国。

例句：She was debarred from practicing law after being convicted of fraud. 她因被判犯有欺诈罪而被禁止从事律师职业。

近义词：banned, prohibited, excluded, precluded
反义词：permitted, allowed, authorized
词性：verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + bar, from Old French barrer "to bar," from barre "bar."
