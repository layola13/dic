frithstool:/ˈfrɪθstuːl/
noun
庇护所，圣座
a seat or chair in a church, especially near the altar, offering privilege of sanctuary to those fleeing from justice.
King Athelstan granted frithstool privilege to several churches in England.  埃塞尔斯坦国王授予英格兰几座教堂圣座特权。
sanctuary, asylum, refuge
exposure, vulnerability
