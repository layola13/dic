It corresponds roughly to the Gregale.|The caecias blew fiercely, whipping the waves into a frenzy. 凯基亚斯风猛烈地吹着，把海浪搅得狂怒。|近义词：gregale, northeast wind|反义词:southwest wind|词性:noun
