overmodulate:/ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdʒəleɪt/ /ˌəʊvəˈmɒdjʊleɪt/| v. | 过度调制；过调 | To apply excessive modulation to (a signal).  This results in distortion and can cause interference with adjacent channels. | The radio signal was overmodulated, causing the audio to sound distorted. 无线电信号过度调制，导致音频失真。| 近义词: distort, overdrive | 反义词: undermodulate | 词性: 动词


overmodulation:/ˌoʊvərˌmɑːdʒəˈleɪʃən/ /ˌəʊvəˌmɒdjʊˈleɪʃən/| n. | 过度调制；过调 | The act or process of overmodulating. |  Overmodulation can cause interference with other signals. 过度调制会对其他信号造成干扰。| 近义词: distortion, overdrive | 反义词: undermodulation | 词性: 名词
