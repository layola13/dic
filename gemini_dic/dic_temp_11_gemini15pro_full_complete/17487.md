bearing-up:/ˈbɛərɪŋ ʌp/
v.|振作；支撑；承受住|To endure hardship or stress with strength and resilience. bearing-up:/ˈbɛərɪŋ ʌp/
n.|支撑；振作|The act of enduring hardship or stress with strength and resilience. The act of supporting or holding something up.|Her bearing-up during the crisis was admirable. 她在危机期间的坚强令人钦佩。 The bearing-up of the roof is crucial for the safety of the building. 屋顶的支撑对建筑物的安全至关重要。|近义词:endurance, resilience, support|反义词:collapse, surrender|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "bear" (Old English beran) meaning "to carry, endure" + the adverb/preposition "up" (Old English up, uppe) indicating direction or maintenance of a position.
