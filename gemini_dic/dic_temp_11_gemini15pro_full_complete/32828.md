choking-off:/ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ ˈɔːf/
|phrasal verb|抑制，扼杀|The government is choking off the supply of drugs. 政府正在抑制毒品供应。|例句：The sanctions are choking off the country's economy. 制裁正在扼杀该国的经济。|近义词：suppressing, stifling, strangling, thwarting|反义词：encouraging, promoting, fostering|词性:动词短语

choking-off:/ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ ˈɔːf/
|adjective|令人窒息的，抑制性的|The choking-off effect of the new regulations is stifling innovation. 新法规的抑制作用正在扼杀创新。|例句：The choking-off policies have led to a decline in investment. 抑制性政策导致了投资下降。|近义词：suppressive, restrictive, inhibiting|反义词：promotional, encouraging, supportive|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->

From "choke" (v.) + "off" (adv.).  "Choke" comes from Old English *ceocian "to choke," related to Dutch kichen "to pant, gasp," German keuchen "to gasp." "Off" comes from Old English of "away, from, off." The combination "choke off" creates the meaning of stopping or suppressing something.
