nonemotionally:/ˌnɒnɪˈməʊʃənəli/ | adv. | 无感情地，不动感情地 | without emotion; in a way that does not show feelings. | She reported the news nonemotionally, as if reading from a script. 她不动声色地报道了这则新闻，就像照本宣科一样。| He spoke nonemotionally about the accident. 他语气平静地讲述了这起事故。 | 近义词: impassively, apathetically, indifferently | 反义词: emotionally, passionately, feelingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + emotional + -ly (adverb suffix)
