attendant:/əˈtɛndənt/| n. adj.|服务员，侍者；出席者；伴随的|A person who attends or assists another person, especially as a job; a person who is present at a particular place or event. |The flight attendant offered us drinks.  航班服务员给我们提供了饮料。|Synonyms: assistant, helper, server, aide, participant, attendee |Antonyms: absentee, no-show|noun

attendant:/əˈtɛndənt/| adj.|伴随的，随之而来的|Accompanying or resulting from something else. |There are many problems attendant on running a small business. 经营小企业有很多随之而来的问题。|Synonyms: accompanying, associated, related, connected, consequent |Antonyms: unrelated, detached, separate|adjective
