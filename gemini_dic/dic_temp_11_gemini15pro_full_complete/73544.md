foreslow:/fɔːrˈsloʊ/| v. |预见…而放慢速度；预先减速|To reduce speed in anticipation of something. |例句：The driver foreslowed as he approached the intersection. 司机在接近十字路口时放慢了速度。

例句：Seeing the children playing in the street, she foreslowed. 看到孩子们在街上玩耍，她放慢了车速。|近义词：decelerate, slow down, reduce speed |反义词:accelerate, speed up |词性:动词

词源:  From fore- + slow.  "Fore-" is a prefix meaning "before" or "beforehand."  Therefore, "foreslow" literally means to slow down beforehand.
