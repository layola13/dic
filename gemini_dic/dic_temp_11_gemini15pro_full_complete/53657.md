dischurch:/dɪsˈtʃɜːrtʃ/
verb
逐出教会,褫夺教籍
To expel from church membership; to deprive of church privileges.
He was dischurched for his heretical beliefs. 他因异端信仰被逐出教会。
近义词:excommunicate
反义词:ordain
词性:v.
