anti-knock:/ˌæntiˈnɒk/ (UK) /ˌæntiˈnɑːk/ (US)| adj. |抗爆的|Resistant to knocking or detonation (especially of gasoline).|High-octane gasoline is anti-knock. 高辛烷值汽油是抗爆的。|Synonyms: detonation-resistant, non-knocking|Antonyms: knocking, detonating|adjective

anti-knock:/ˌæntiˈnɒk/ (UK) /ˌæntiˈnɑːk/ (US)| n. |抗爆剂|A substance added to gasoline to prevent knocking.|This fuel contains an anti-knock additive.  这种燃料包含一种抗爆添加剂。|Synonyms: octane booster, anti-detonant|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but a fuel without anti-knock would be prone to knocking)|noun
