outsourced:/ˈaʊtsɔːrst/ | v. | 外包；委外 | To obtain (goods or a service) from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source. | Many companies outsource their IT operations to specialized firms. 许多公司将IT业务外包给专业公司。| The company outsourced its customer service to a call center in India.  这家公司将其客户服务外包给印度的一个呼叫中心。| 近义词: subcontracted, contracted out | 反义词: insourced | 词性: 动词

outsourcing:/ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 外包；委外 | The practice of obtaining goods or a service from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source. | Outsourcing can help companies reduce costs. 外包可以帮助公司降低成本。| The outsourcing of manufacturing jobs has become a controversial issue. 制造业岗位的外包已成为一个有争议的问题。| 近义词: subcontracting | 反义词: insourcing | 词性: 名词
