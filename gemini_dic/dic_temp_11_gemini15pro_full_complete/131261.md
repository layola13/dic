noncatarrhal:/ˌnɑːnkəˈtɑːrəl/
adjective
非卡他性的
Not relating to or affected by catarrh (inflammation of the mucous membranes).
The patient presented with a noncatarrhal cough.  病人表现出非卡他性咳嗽。
近义词: noninflammatory
反义词: catarrhal
词性: adjective
