ceduous:/ˈsiːdʒuəs/| adj.|落叶的；脱落的；凋落的|Falling off at maturity, as petals, leaves, or fruit.|The ceduous leaves of the tree carpeted the ground in autumn. 秋天，落叶树的叶子铺满了地面。|近义词：deciduous, caducous|反义词：evergreen, persistent|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *cēduus, from cēdō (“I yield, withdraw, fall off”).
