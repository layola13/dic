agree-to:/əˈɡriː tuː/
verb phrase
同意，答应
to consent or accede to something
The company agreed to the terms of the contract.  公司同意了合同条款。
They agreed to meet again next week. 他们同意下周再见面。

近义词:accept, consent to, accede to
反义词:refuse, reject, decline
词性:动词短语
