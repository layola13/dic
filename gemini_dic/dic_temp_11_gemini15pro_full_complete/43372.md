craniology: /kreɪniˈɒlədʒi/ /kreɪniˈɑːlədʒi/
n.
颅骨学
The scientific study of the shape and size of skulls, especially human skulls.
Craniology was once used to try to determine intelligence and character based on skull measurements. 颅骨学曾被用来试图根据头骨的尺寸来确定智力和性格。
The museum has an extensive collection of skulls for craniology research.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的头骨，用于颅骨学研究。
近义词: phrenology
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but related fields might be neurology, psychology)
词性: noun
