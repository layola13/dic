harmonise:/ˈhɑːmənaɪz/ (US) /ˈhɑːmənaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使和谐，使协调，使相配|to bring into or cause to be in harmony; to make things consistent or compatible|例句：The government's policies don't always harmonise with its public pronouncements. 政府的政策并不总是与其公开声明相符。

例句：The architect harmonised the old building with the new extension. 建筑师使旧建筑与新扩建部分协调一致。|近义词：coordinate, blend, reconcile, unify, integrate|反义词：clash, conflict, disagree, jar|词性：动词
