entreative:/ɪnˈtriːtɪv/ /ɛnˈtriːtɪv/
adjective
恳求的；祈求的
Of or relating to entreaty; pleading; beseeching.
She cast an entreative glance at her father, hoping he would change his mind. 她恳求地看了她父亲一眼，希望他能改变主意。
Near Synonyms:  pleading, supplicating, imploring, beseeching, begging
Antonyms: demanding, commanding, ordering, dictatorial
词性: adjective
