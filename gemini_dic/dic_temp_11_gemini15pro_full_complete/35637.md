coatroom:/ˈkoʊtruːm/ (US), /ˈkəʊtruːm/ (UK)| n. |衣帽间，存放衣物的地方|A room (often in a public building) for leaving coats and other outdoor clothing.|例句：Please leave your coats and umbrellas in the coatroom. 请把你的外套和雨伞放在衣帽间。|例句：The coatroom was crowded with guests' belongings. 衣帽间里挤满了客人的物品。|近义词：cloakroom, checkroom|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From coat + room.  First recorded in the early 19th century.
