cystoplast: /ˈsɪstəˌplæst/
noun
膀胱成形术；膀胱修补术
A piece of tissue used for cystoplasty, usually taken from the patient's own body.  A surgical procedure to increase the size of the bladder, often using a piece of intestine.
例句：Cystoplasty is a surgical procedure used to increase bladder capacity. 膀胱成形术是一种用来增加膀胱容量的外科手术。

例句：The surgeon performed a cystoplast using a section of the patient's bowel.  外科医生用病人一部分肠道进行了膀胱成形术。
近义词: bladder augmentation, bladder reconstruction
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but conceptually the opposite could be bladder reduction surgery)
词性: n.
