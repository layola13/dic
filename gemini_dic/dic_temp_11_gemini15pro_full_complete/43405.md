crank: /kræŋk/
noun|曲柄；古怪的人；坏脾气的人|A handle attached to a shaft and used for turning it. A person with strange or eccentric ideas. A bad-tempered person.|例句：He turned the crank to start the engine. 他转动曲柄启动了引擎。

例句：He's a bit of a crank, always going on about conspiracy theories. 他有点古怪，总是谈论阴谋论。|近义词：handle, eccentric, grouch|反义词：normal person, amiable person|n.

crank: /kræŋk/
verb|转动曲柄；启动（引擎）；（使）恼怒；（使）变得暴躁|To turn a crank. To start (an engine) by turning a crank. To make (someone) annoyed or bad-tempered.|例句：She cranked the engine and the car sputtered to life. 她发动了引擎，汽车突突地发动起来了。

例句：The constant noise was starting to crank him up. 持续不断的噪音开始让他烦躁起来。|近义词：turn, start, irritate|反义词：stop, soothe|v.
