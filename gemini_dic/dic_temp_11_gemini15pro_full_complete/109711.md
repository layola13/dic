levees:/ˈlɛviz/ (US), /ˈlɛviːz/ (UK)| n. |堤坝，防洪堤|An embankment built to prevent the overflow of a river.|The levees protecting New Orleans failed during Hurricane Katrina.  新奥尔良的堤坝在卡特里娜飓风期间决堤了。|Synonyms: embankments, dikes, floodwalls|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "breach" as the opposite action/result)|Noun


levees:/ˈlɛviz/ (US), /ˈlɛviːz/ (UK)| n. |征税，征兵|The act of levying (collecting) a tax or compulsory service.|The king ordered levees of troops and taxes to fund the war.  国王下令征兵和征税以资助战争。|Synonyms:  levy, tax, conscription|Antonyms: exemption, remission|Noun
