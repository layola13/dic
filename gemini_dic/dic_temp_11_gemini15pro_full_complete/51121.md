despiser:/dɪˈspaɪzər/
n.|轻蔑者；鄙视者|A person who feels contempt or disdain for someone or something.|He is a despiser of all forms of hypocrisy. 他鄙视一切形式的虚伪。|Synonyms: scorner, contemner, disdainer|Antonyms: admirer, respecter|Noun
