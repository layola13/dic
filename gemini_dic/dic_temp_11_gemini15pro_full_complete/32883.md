chondritic:/kɒnˈdrɪtɪk/
adjective
球粒状陨石的
Relating to or resembling a chondrite (a stony meteorite containing small mineral granules called chondrules).
This meteorite is classified as chondritic due to the presence of chondrules. 这块陨石由于含有球粒而被归类为球粒陨石。
The chondritic composition of the meteorite suggests it originated in the early solar system.  这块陨石的球粒状成分表明它起源于早期太阳系。
近义词: stony (when referring to meteorites)
反义词: achondritic
词性: adjective
