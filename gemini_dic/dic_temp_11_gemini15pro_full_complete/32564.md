chips:/tʃɪps/| n.|薯条，炸薯片，芯片，筹码|Thinly sliced pieces of potato fried until crisp.  Small discs used in gambling.  Tiny pieces of semiconductor material.|例句：Fish and chips is a classic British dish. 鱼和薯条是经典的英国菜。

例句：He lost all his chips at the poker game. 他在扑克游戏中输光了所有筹码。|近义词：fries (for food), crisps (for food - UK), counters (for gambling), microchips (for electronics)|反义词:None (meaning dependent)|词性:noun

chips:/tʃɪps/| n.|木屑|Small pieces of wood broken off from a larger piece.|例句：The carpenter swept the chips off the floor. 木匠把地上的木屑扫干净了。

例句：Wood chips can be used for gardening. 木屑可以用于园艺。|近义词：shavings, splinters|反义词:None|词性:noun
