brightly:/ˈbraɪtli/| adv.|明亮地，鲜明地，欢快地|Shining with light; vividly; cheerfully.|The sun shone brightly. 太阳明亮地照耀着。|Synonyms: brilliantly, vividly, radiantly, cheerfully|Antonyms: dimly, faintly, dully|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *beorhtlīce (brightly), from beorht (bright) + -līce (ly).
