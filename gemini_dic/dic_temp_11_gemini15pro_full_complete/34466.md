clavy: /ˈklævɪ/
n.
clavichord; keyboard instrument
A clavy is a keyboard instrument, specifically a clavichord.
He played a beautiful melody on the clavy. 他在古钢琴上演奏了一曲优美的旋律。
The clavy's delicate sound filled the room. 古钢琴柔和的声音充满了整个房间。

近义词: clavichord, keyboard instrument
反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clave, from Old French clavie ("key (of an instrument)"), from Latin clavis ("key").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kleh₂w- (“hook, peg”).
