mappable:/ˈmæpəbəl/| adj.|可绘制地图的，可映射的|Something that is mappable can be represented on a map.  It can also refer to something that can be systematically assigned or correlated.|The terrain was rough but still mappable.  这片地形崎岖不平，但仍然可以绘制地图。|Synonyms: chartable, plottable, representable|Antonyms: unmappable, immeasurable|adjective
