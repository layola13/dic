overcompensated: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmpənˌseɪtɪd/
adjective
过度补偿的，矫枉过正的
Having compensated excessively for something perceived as a deficiency or undesirable trait.  Going beyond what is necessary to correct a deficiency or perceived flaw.
He bought a huge, flashy car, which seemed overcompensated for his lack of self-confidence. 他买了一辆巨大的、炫耀的汽车，这似乎是为了弥补他缺乏自信。

The government's overcompensated reaction to the crisis caused more problems than it solved. 政府对危机的过度反应造成的麻烦比解决的还要多。

Synonym: overdone, excessive, extravagant
Antonym: undercompensated, inadequate, insufficient
词性: adjective
