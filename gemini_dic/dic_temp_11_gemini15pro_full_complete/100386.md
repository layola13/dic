interstructure:/ˈɪntərˌstrʌktʃər/| n.|相互结构，互构性|A complex system of interconnected parts or elements that form a whole, especially within or between different structures or organizations.|The interstructure of the global economy is becoming increasingly complex. 全球经济的相互结构正变得越来越复杂。|近义词：framework, infrastructure, network|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "disconnection," "fragmentation")|词性:noun

词源: "inter-" (between) + "structure" (arrangement of parts)
