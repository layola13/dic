franklin:/ˈfræŋklɪn/| n.|弗兰克林（人名；美国货币单位）|A surname, and also a unit of currency equal to one hundredth of a pound sterling (used in some former British colonies).|Benjamin Franklin was one of the Founding Fathers of the United States.  本杰明·富兰克林是美国的开国元勋之一。

Franklin is a common surname in the United States.  富兰克林是美国一个常见的姓氏。|近义词: (as a unit of currency) cent, penny |反义词:  N/A |词性:noun


franklin:/ˈfræŋklɪn/| adj.|坦率的，直率的|Characterized by directness and honesty in speech or action.  This meaning is now archaic and rarely used.|He gave a franklin answer to the judge. 他给了法官一个坦率的回答。(古语用法，现在很少使用)

She spoke with franklin candor. 她说话坦率真诚。(古语用法，现在很少使用) |近义词:frank, candid, direct, forthright|反义词:dishonest, evasive, indirect|词性:adjective
