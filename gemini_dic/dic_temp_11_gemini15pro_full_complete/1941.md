actinozoal:/ˌæktɪnəˈzoʊəl/| adj.| 珊瑚虫类的；放线菌的|Relating to or characteristic of Actinozoa, a group of aquatic invertebrates including corals and sea anemones.  Also, relating to or caused by actinomycetes (branching filamentous bacteria).|The actinozoal skeleton forms the basis of coral reefs. 珊瑚虫类的骨骼形成了珊瑚礁的基础。| The infection was determined to be actinozoal in nature. 感染被确定为放线菌引起的。|近义词：coral, anthozoan (for the animal sense); actinomycetic (for the bacterial sense)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Actinozoa + -al.  Actinozoa comes from Greek *aktīn* (ray) + *zōion* (animal), referring to the radial symmetry of these animals.
