mayhem:/ˈmeɪhem/| n. |混乱，骚乱，浩劫|violent or damaging disorder; chaos|The hurricane caused mayhem in the small coastal town. 飓风给沿海小镇带来了浩劫。|近义词：chaos, havoc, disorder, turmoil|反义词:order, peace, tranquility|名词

词源: From Old French *mahaing, probably from mahain (“defect, blemish”), which is itself of uncertain origin.
