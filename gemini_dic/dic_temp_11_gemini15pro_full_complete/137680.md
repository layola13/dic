nonuniversals:/ˌnɑːnˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəlz/
noun|非普遍性；非共性|Characteristics or features that are not shared by all cultures or languages.|Many linguists are interested in studying linguistic nonuniversals to understand the diversity of human language. 许多语言学家对研究语言的非共性很感兴趣，以理解人类语言的多样性。|近义词:particularities, idiosyncrasies|反义词:universals, commonalities|n.
