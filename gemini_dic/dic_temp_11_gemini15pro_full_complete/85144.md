hardpan:/ˈhɑːrdpæn/ (US) /ˈhɑːdpæn/ (UK)| n. | 硬磐，坚硬土层 | A dense, compacted layer of soil, often cemented by clay, iron oxide, or silica, that is difficult for roots to penetrate.  | The hardpan beneath the topsoil prevented proper drainage.  硬土层下面的表土阻止了正常的排水。

The drought worsened the hardpan, making it nearly impossible to plant anything.  干旱使硬土更加坚硬，几乎无法种植任何东西。 | 近义词：claypan, caliche, duripan | 反义词: topsoil | 词性: 名词

hardpan:/ˈhɑːrdpæn/ (US) /ˈhɑːdpæn/ (UK)| adj. |  (比喻) 顽固的，不妥协的 |  Uncompromising and inflexible. |  He has a hardpan attitude about changing his mind. 他对改变主意持顽固的态度。

She remained hardpan in her refusal to cooperate. 她仍然顽固地拒绝合作。| 近义词：inflexible, unyielding, obstinate | 反义词: flexible, yielding | 词性: 形容词
