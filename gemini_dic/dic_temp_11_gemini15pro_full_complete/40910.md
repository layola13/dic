cooldowns:/ˈkuːldaʊnz/| n.|冷却时间，冷却期|A period of time required for something to cool down, often used in the context of video games or machinery. Also refers to a period of time after strenuous activity where the body returns to a normal state.|The engineer recommended a five-minute cooldown period between tests.  工程师建议两次测试之间有五分钟的冷却时间。|synonyms: recovery period, rest period, waiting period|antonyms: warm-up, preparation|noun
