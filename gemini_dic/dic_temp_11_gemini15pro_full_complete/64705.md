escarpment:/ɪˈskɑːrpmənt/| n. |悬崖，陡坡|A long, steep slope, especially one at the edge of a plateau or separating areas of land at different heights.|例句：The escarpment offered a breathtaking view of the valley below.  悬崖提供了下面山谷的壮丽景色。

例句：The hikers carefully navigated the treacherous path along the escarpment.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地在悬崖边上危险的小路上行走。|近义词：cliff, precipice, bluff|反义词:slope, incline|词性:noun
