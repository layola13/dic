nightingales:/ˈnaɪtɪŋɡeɪlz/| n. |夜莺（复数）|A small migratory thrush of Europe, noted for its rich melodious song, heard chiefly at night.  A nightingale is a bird famous for its beautiful singing, especially at night.|例句：The nightingales were singing in the woods. 夜莺在树林里歌唱。

例句：We heard the beautiful song of the nightingales. 我们听到了夜莺优美的歌声。|近义词：songbird|反义词:crow (in terms of song quality, not species)|词性:noun
