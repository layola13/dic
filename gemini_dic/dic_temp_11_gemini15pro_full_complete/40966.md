coopered:/ˈkuːpərd/ | adj. | 修补过的；装桶的 | Made or repaired by a cooper (a maker or repairer of casks and barrels); also, stored or treated in a cask or barrel. |  The coopered barrel held fine wine. 修补过的酒桶装着上好的葡萄酒。| Near synonyms: mended, patched, restored, barreled | Antonyms: broken, damaged, unrepaired | adjective

coopered:/ˈkuːpərd/ | v. | 修补（桶）；装桶 | Past participle of "cooper," meaning to make or repair casks and barrels, or to store in them. | The wine was coopered in oak barrels for six months.  这葡萄酒在橡木桶中存放了六个月。 | Near synonyms: mended, patched, restored, barreled | Antonyms: break, damage | verb
