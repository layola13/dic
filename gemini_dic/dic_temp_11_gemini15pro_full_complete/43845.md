crepuscular:/krɪˈpʌskjələr/| adj.|暮色的；朦胧的；黄昏的|Relating to twilight; dim; occurring or active during twilight.|Bats are crepuscular creatures, flying around as the sun sets. 蝙蝠是暮色动物，在太阳落山时四处飞翔。|The crepuscular light cast long shadows across the lawn. 暮色中的光线在草坪上投下长长的影子。|近义词：twilight, dusky, nocturnal|反义词：diurnal, daytime|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *crepusculum* ("twilight, dusk"), diminutive of *creper* ("dark, obscure").
