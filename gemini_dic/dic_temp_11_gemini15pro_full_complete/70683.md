finalising: /ˈfaɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 完成；最后确定；定稿 | The process of completing, putting the finishing touches on something, or making a final decision about something. |  We are finalising the details of the contract. 我们正在敲定合同的细节。

|  They were finalising the arrangements for the wedding. 他们正在最后确定婚礼的安排。 | 近义词: completing, concluding, finishing | 反义词: beginning, starting, initiating | 词性: 动词(现在分词)

Etymology: From 'finalise' + '-ing'. 'Finalise' is a back-formation from 'finalisation', which is from 'final' (from Old French and ultimately Latin 'finalis') + '-isation'.
