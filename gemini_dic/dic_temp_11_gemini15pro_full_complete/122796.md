moldering:/ˈmoʊldərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈməʊldərɪŋ/ (UK)| adjective|腐朽的，腐烂的，衰败的|Slowly decaying or disintegrating, especially because of dampness or neglect; becoming stale or outdated.|The moldering books in the attic were covered in dust. 阁楼里腐烂的书籍上满是灰尘。|Synonyms: decaying, rotting, decomposing, crumbling, disintegrating, putrefying, festering, stagnant|Antonyms: fresh, new, thriving, flourishing|词性:形容词

molder:/ˈmoʊldər/ (US) /ˈməʊldər/ (UK)| verb|腐烂，腐朽，衰败|To decay or disintegrate slowly, especially because of dampness or neglect; to become stale or outdated.|The fruit began to molder in the bowl. 水果开始在碗里腐烂。|Synonyms: decay, rot, decompose, crumble, disintegrate, putrefy, fester|Antonyms: thrive, flourish|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *moldren, from Old English molde "dust, earth."
