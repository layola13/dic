apothegmatical: /ˌæpəθɪɡˈmætɪkəl/ | adj. | 格言式的；警句的；简洁精辟的 | Of the nature of an apothegm; terse and pithy; sententious.  |  His apothegmatical style makes his writing memorable. 他的格言式风格使他的作品令人难忘。 |  Her speech was full of apothegmatical pronouncements. 她的演讲充满了格言式的声明。 |  terse, pithy, concise, succinct, epigrammatic | verbose, prolix, rambling | adjective

apothegm: /ˈæpəθɛm/ | n. | 格言；箴言；警句 | A short, pithy, instructive saying. |  He is fond of quoting apothegms. 他喜欢引用格言。 |  The book is full of wise apothegms. 这本书充满了智慧的格言。 |  maxim, adage, proverb, saying |  diatribe, rant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *apothegmaticus, from Greek apothegma (ἀπόθεγμα) meaning "something set apart," from apotithenai (ἀποτιθέναι) meaning "to put away, deposit, store," from apo- (ἀπο-) "away" + tithenai (τιθέναι) "to put, place."
