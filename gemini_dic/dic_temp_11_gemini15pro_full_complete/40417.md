contrapuntal:/ˌkɒntrəˈpʌntl/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈpʌntl/| adj.|对位的，复调的|Relating to or characterized by counterpoint, a musical technique involving the combination of two or more independent melodic lines.|The composer's contrapuntal skill is evident in the intricate interplay of the various instrumental parts.|近义词：polyphonic|反义词：homophonic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *contrapunto*, from *contra* ("against") + *punto* ("point," in music "note").
