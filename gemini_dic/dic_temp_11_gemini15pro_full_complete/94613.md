impregnate:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪt/ | v. adj. | 使充满；使饱和；浸渍；使怀孕 | To fill something completely with a particular substance or quality; to make a woman or female animal pregnant. |

例句：
The air was impregnated with the smell of pine.
空气中充满了松树的气味。

The cloth was impregnated with a waterproof solution.
这块布浸透了防水溶液。

近义词：saturate, permeate, infuse, imbue, fertilize | 反义词：empty, drain, deplete | 词性: 动词, 形容词

impregnated:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 充满的；饱和的；浸渍的 | Filled thoroughly; saturated; permeated. |

例句：
The wood was impregnated with preservative.
木材浸透了防腐剂。

She found an impregnated sponge left in the sink.
她在水槽里发现了一块浸满水的海绵。

近义词：saturated, permeated, infused, imbued | 反义词：empty, drained, depleted | 词性: 形容词
