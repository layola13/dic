elephantoidal:/ˌɛlɪfænˈtɔɪdəl/| adj.|象形的，像象的|Resembling an elephant, especially in size or shape.  Having massive or elephant-like proportions.|例句：The elephantoidal statue dominated the square.  象形的雕像矗立在广场上。

例句：He had elephantoidal limbs, thick and heavy. 他的四肢粗壮如象腿。|近义词：massive, enormous, gigantic, colossal|反义词：tiny, small, miniature|词性:adjective

elephantoid:/ˌɛlɪfænˈtɔɪd/| n.|象|A person with elephantiasis.|例句：The doctor diagnosed him as an elephantoid. 医生诊断他患有象皮病。

例句：Historical accounts describe elephantoids suffering from social isolation. 历史记载描述了象皮病患者遭受社会隔离的痛苦。|近义词：(for the disease) elephantiasis|反义词：N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From elephanto- + -idal.  "Elephanto-" comes from the Greek "elephas" (elephant), and "-idal" is a suffix meaning "like" or "resembling."
