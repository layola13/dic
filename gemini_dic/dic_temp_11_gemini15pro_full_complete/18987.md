bestorm:/bɪˈstɔːrm/
verb
猛烈攻击，痛斥
attack fiercely, criticize severely
The critics bestormed the new play.  评论家们猛烈抨击了这出新戏。
近义词: assail, lambaste, excoriate, berate
反义词: praise, laud, commend
词性: verb


词源: be- + storm
"be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over."
"storm" refers to a violent disturbance of the atmosphere.
