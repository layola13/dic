outtinkling: /ˌaʊtˈtɪŋklɪŋ/
verb
发出比...更清脆的叮当声
surpassing or exceeding in tinkling sound; tinkling louder, clearer, or more musically than something else.
The tiny bells on her bracelet outtinkled the wind chimes in the breeze. 她手镯上的小铃铛在微风中发出比风铃更清脆的叮当声。
The stream, as it danced down the mountain, outtinkled the birdsong.  当溪流沿着山坡流淌时，它的叮当声盖过了鸟鸣。
Near synonyms: outsounding, exceeding, surpassing
Antonyms:  hushed, muted, muffled, quieter
词性: 动词
