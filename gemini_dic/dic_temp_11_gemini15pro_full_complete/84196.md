half-reclined:/ˌhæf rɪˈklaɪnd/
adjective
半躺的，半倚的
partly reclined; lying back at an angle
She was sitting half-reclined on a chaise lounge, reading a book. 她半躺在躺椅上看书。
The Buddha statue was in a half-reclined posture. 这尊佛像呈半卧姿势。
Near Antonyms: upright, erect, standing
Etymology: half + reclined (from recline, from Latin reclinare, from re- "back" + clinare "to lean")
