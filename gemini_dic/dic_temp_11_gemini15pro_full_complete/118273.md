merchantmen:/ˈmɜːrtʃəntmən/ | n. | 商船，商船队 | A ship used in commerce; a trading vessel. | The merchantmen sailed across the ocean carrying valuable cargo. 商船载着贵重货物横渡海洋。 |  Synonyms: trading ship, cargo ship, freighter | Antonyms: warship, pirate ship | noun

merchantmen:/ˈmɜːrtʃəntmən/ | n. | 商人（古语，现已少用）|  A merchant, especially one who owns or operates merchant ships. |  In days of yore, the merchantmen controlled the flow of goods.  在过去，商人控制着货物的流通。| Synonyms: trader, dealer, businessman | Antonyms: customer, buyer, consumer | noun
