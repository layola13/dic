bonobos:/bəˈnoʊboʊz/ /ˈbɒnəˌbəʊz/
n.
倭黑猩猩
A kind of great ape, closely related to the chimpanzee, known for its peaceful and matriarchal social structure.
Bonobos are known for their peaceful nature. 倭黑猩猩以爱好和平的天性而闻名。
近义词: pygmy chimpanzee
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑使用其他灵长类动物，例如 "chimpanzee"黑猩猩，来表示对比)
词性: noun
