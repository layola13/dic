kinetogenesis:/ˌkaɪnɪtəˈdʒenɪsɪs/
noun
动细胞生成, 运动发生
The formation or production of movement.  Specifically, the development of movement in an organism or embryo.
The kinetogenesis of the embryo was studied in detail.  胚胎的运动发生被详细研究。
