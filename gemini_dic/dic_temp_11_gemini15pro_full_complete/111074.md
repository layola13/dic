listeric:/lɪˈstɪrɪk/| adj.|李斯特菌的；由李斯特菌引起的|Relating to or caused by Listeria, a type of bacteria that can cause food poisoning.|The patient suffered from listeric meningitis.  病人患有李斯特菌性脑膜炎。| Listeria monocytogenes infection |Bacterial, microbial|adjective
Etymology:  Derived from "Listeria," the genus of bacteria named after Joseph Lister, a pioneer of antiseptic surgery.
