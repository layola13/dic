calamity:/kəˈlæməti/ | n. |灾难；灾祸 | An event causing great and often sudden damage or distress; a disaster. | The earthquake was a calamity for the entire region. 地震对整个地区来说是一场灾难。| synonym: disaster, catastrophe, tragedy | antonym: blessing, fortune, windfall | noun
