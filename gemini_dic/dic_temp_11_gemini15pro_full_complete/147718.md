overpresumptive:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmptɪv/| adj.|过分自以为是的，专横的|Excessively or unwarrantedly presumptuous; too forward or bold in behavior or attitude. Characterized by excessive presumption or confidence in one's own abilities or opinions, often to the point of arrogance.|例句：His overpresumptive manner alienated his colleagues. 他过分自以为是的态度疏远了他的同事。

例句：It was overpresumptive of him to assume he would get the promotion without even applying. 他想当然地认为不用申请就能升职，这太自以为是了。|近义词：arrogant, presumptuous, conceited, haughty, overweening, overconfident|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, diffident|词性:形容词
