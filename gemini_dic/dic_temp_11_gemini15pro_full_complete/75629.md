fugitiveness:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪvnəs/| n.|逃亡，短暂，易逝|The fugitiveness of life makes every moment precious.  生命的短暂让每一刻都弥足珍贵。|The fugitiveness of the scent made it difficult to track.  这气味的易逝使得追踪变得困难。|近义词：transience, ephemerality, impermanence|反义词：permanence, stability|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *fugitifenes, from Old French *fugitifnes,  from Latin *fugitīvus (“fleeing, fugitive”) + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *fugiō (“to flee”).
