arose:/əˈroʊz/
verb
出现，发生，起源于
came into being; emerged; originated
A problem arose during the project. 项目期间出现了一个问题。
The opportunity arose for her to travel abroad. 她有了出国旅行的机会。
synonyms: emerged, appeared, occurred, stemmed, resulted
antonyms: subsided, disappeared, vanished
词性: 动词
