adiaphory:/ˌeɪdiˈæfəri/ /ˌædiˈæfəri/ | n. | 中立之事；无关紧要之事；可有可无之事 | A matter considered to be morally neutral, neither right nor wrong, or of little importance.  |  Example: The choice of what to eat for lunch was considered an adiaphory by the monk.  这位僧侣认为午餐吃什么都无所谓。

Example: Whether to use a blue or black pen is a pure adiaphory. 使用蓝色或黑色钢笔完全是无关紧要的。 | Synonym:  indifferent, neutral, nonessential, unimportant, trifling | Antonym: essential, crucial, vital, important, significant | noun
