curiosities:/ˌkjʊəriˈɑːsətiz/ | n. | 好奇心，奇事，珍品 |  A state of being curious or something that arouses curiosity; rare or unusual objects considered interesting or valuable. |  She had a natural curiosity about the world around her. 她对周围的世界充满好奇心。

The museum displayed many curiosities from around the world. 博物馆展出了来自世界各地的许多珍品。 | 近义词：rarities, oddities, wonders, marvels | 反义词: commonalities, ordinariness | 词性: 名词(plural)

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French curiosité, from Latin curiositas, from curiosus ‘careful, inquisitive’ (see curious).
