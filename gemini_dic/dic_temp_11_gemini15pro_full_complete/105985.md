knackered:/ˈnækəd/ (UK) /ˈnækərd/ (US)| adj.|筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的|Exhausted, very tired.  Originally meaning "broken down" (like a horse).|例句：I'm absolutely knackered after that run. 我跑完步后累极了。

例句：He was knackered after working a double shift.  他上完两班倒后累坏了。

近义词：exhausted, tired, worn out, shattered, beat, spent|反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated|词性:形容词
