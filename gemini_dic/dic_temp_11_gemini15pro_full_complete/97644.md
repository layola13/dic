inheritor:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtər/ | n. |继承人|A person who inherits something, especially property, a title, or a responsibility.|例句：He is the sole inheritor of his father's fortune. 他是他父亲财产的唯一继承人。

例句：The museum is the inheritor of a rich artistic tradition.  这家博物馆继承了丰富的艺术传统。

近义词：heir, successor, beneficiary|反义词: predecessor, testator |词性: noun
