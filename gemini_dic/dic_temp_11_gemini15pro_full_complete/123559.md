monsignors:/ˌmɒnsɪˈnjɔːz/ /ˌmɑːnsɪˈnjɔːrz/ | n. |大人们；阁下们；主教们| A respectful title given to a person of high rank in the Roman Catholic Church, especially one who has been honored by the Pope. | The monsignors gathered for the annual conference. 主教们聚集在一起参加年度会议。|  The Pope addressed the assembled monsignors. 教皇向聚集的主教们发表了讲话。 | synonyms: prelates, ecclesiastics | antonyms: laymen | noun
