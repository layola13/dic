arctically:/ˈɑːktɪkli/ | adv. | 极寒地；冷漠地 | In a manner characteristic of the Arctic; extremely cold; frigidly; unfeelingly. | The wind blew arctically across the plains.  风冰冷地吹过平原。| 近义词：frigidly, glacially, coldly | 反义词：warmly, tropically | 词性：副词

词源: From Arctic + -ally.  Arctic comes from the Greek arktos, meaning "bear," referring to the constellation Ursa Major (Great Bear), which is near the North Pole.
