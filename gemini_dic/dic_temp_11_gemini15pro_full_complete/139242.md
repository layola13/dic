obligati's: /ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 义务，责任 |  A duty or commitment. Something that you must do because of a law, rule, promise, etc. It derives from the Latin word "obligatio," meaning "a binding." |  例句：She felt a strong obligation to help her family. 她感到有强烈的义务帮助她的家人。

例句：He has several financial obligations to meet each month. 他每个月都要履行几项财务义务。

近义词：duty, responsibility, commitment, liability
反义词：option, choice, freedom, exemption
词性: 名词
