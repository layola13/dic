circumfuse: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈfjuːz/ /ˌsər-kəm-ˈfyüz/
verb
环绕；包围；浇灌
To pour or spread something, especially liquid, all around something.  To surround; encompass.
The gardener circumfused the roses with water. 园丁给玫瑰浇水。
The mountain was circumfused with clouds. 山被云雾环绕。
近义词: surround, encompass, encircle, envelop
反义词: expose, uncover
词性: v.

Etymology: From Latin circumfundere (circum-, "around" + fundere, "to pour").
