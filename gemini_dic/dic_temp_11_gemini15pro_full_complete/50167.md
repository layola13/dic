deodar:/ˌdiːoʊˈdɑːr/| n. |雪松，喜马拉雅雪松|A large evergreen coniferous tree native to the western Himalayas, having drooping branches and long needles.  It is prized for its durable and fragrant wood.|例句：The deodar is the national tree of Pakistan.  雪松是巴基斯坦的国树。

例句：The scent of deodar filled the mountain air. 雪松的香味弥漫在山间空气中。|近义词：cedar, pine|反义词:|noun
