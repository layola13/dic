enantiomorphous:/ɪˌnæntiəˈmɔːrfəs/ | adj. | 对映体的，互为镜像的 | Existing in two forms that are mirror images of each other but cannot be superimposed; chiral. |  The two enantiomorphous crystals refracted light differently. 这两个对映体晶体以不同的方式折射光线。 | synonyms: chiral, mirror-image, non-superimposable | antonyms: achiral, superimposable | adjective


enantiomorph:/ɪˌnæntiəˈmɔːrf/ | n. | 对映体 | Each of a pair of molecules that are mirror images of each other. | The two enantiomorphs have different biological activities.  这两个对映体具有不同的生物活性。| synonyms: enantiomer, optical isomer, chiral molecule | antonyms: - | noun
