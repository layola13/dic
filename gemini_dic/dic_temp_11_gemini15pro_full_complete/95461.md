incitable:/ɪnˈsaɪtəbl/ | adj. | 易激动的，易煽动的 | Easily roused to excitement or action; easily provoked. | The incitable crowd was quick to anger. 易怒的人群很快就生气了。| The politician delivered an incitable speech that roused the crowd to a frenzy. 这位政治家发表了一场煽动性的演讲，使人群陷入疯狂。 | 近义词: excitable, inflammable, provocative | 反义词: calm, placid, impassive | 词性: adjective

incitable:/ɪnˈsaɪtəbl/ | adj. | 易被激怒的 | Easily angered or provoked.  |  He has a very incitable temperament and is easily offended. 他性情暴躁，很容易被冒犯。 | Even a small disagreement can make him incitable. 即使是小分歧也能让他发怒。| 近义词: irritable, touchy, testy | 反义词: patient, tolerant, forbearing | 词性: adjective
