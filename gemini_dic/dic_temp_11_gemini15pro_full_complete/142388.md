orthotectonic: /ˌɔːrθoʊˈtɛktɒnɪk/
adjective
正造山运动的；地壳垂直运动的
Relating to or designating vertical movements of the earth's crust.
The orthotectonic movements caused the uplift of the mountain range. 正造山运动导致了山脉的隆起。
The geologist studied the orthotectonic features of the region. 地质学家研究了该地区的正造山运动特征。
近义词:  epeirogenic, vertical
反义词: orogenic
词性: adjective
