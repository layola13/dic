flat-top:/ˈflætˌtɒp/ (UK), /ˈflætˌtɑːp/ (US)| n. adj.|平顶，平头，平顶发型|A flat-top is a type of haircut where the hair on the top of the head is cut to a uniform length, creating a flat surface.|He sported a meticulously groomed flat-top. 他留着一个精心修剪的平头。|Synonyms: crew cut, butch cut, platform cut|Antonyms: long hair, shaggy hair|noun, adjective

flat-top:/ˈflætˌtɒp/ (UK), /ˈflætˌtɑːp/ (US)| adj.|平顶的，平头的|Having a flat top surface.|The aircraft carrier has a flat-top deck.  这艘航空母舰有一个平顶甲板。|Synonyms: level, even|Antonyms: sloped, angled|adjective
