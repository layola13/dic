isogonic:/ˌaɪsəˈɡɒnɪk/ | adj. | 等磁偏角的，等偏线的 |  Relating to or denoting lines on a map connecting points of equal magnetic declination. |  An isogonic chart shows lines of equal magnetic declination. 等磁偏角图显示了磁偏角相同的线。| The isogonic lines on the map helped the navigators. 地图上的等偏线帮助了航海家。 |Synonyms: isomagnetic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "anisogonic" - not having equal magnetic declination) | adjective
