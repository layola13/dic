corrugant:/ˈkɔːrəɡənt/ /ˈkɑːrəɡənt/ | adj. | 波纹状的，起皱的 |  Wrinkled or furrowed; having a wavy surface. | The caterpillar's skin was corrugant, like a tiny accordion.  毛毛虫的皮肤是波纹状的，像个小手风琴。|  Synonyms: wrinkled, furrowed, creased, ridged, grooved | Antonyms: smooth, flat, even | adjective

corrugated:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːrəɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 波纹状的，起皱的 | Shaped into alternating ridges and grooves. | Corrugated cardboard is commonly used for packaging. 瓦楞纸板通常用于包装。| Synonyms: wrinkled, furrowed, creased, ridged, grooved, corrugant | Antonyms: smooth, flat, even | adjective


*Note: While "corrugant" exists and is a synonym of "corrugated," "corrugated" is significantly more common and widely understood.*

Etymology: From Latin "corrugatus," past participle of "corrugare" (to wrinkle), from "com-" (together) + "ruga" (wrinkle).
