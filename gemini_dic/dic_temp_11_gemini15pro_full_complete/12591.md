assagai's:/ˈæsəˌɡaɪz/
n.
标枪（复数）
A slender, iron-tipped spear, typically used by Southern African peoples.
The Zulu warriors raised their assagais in a show of defiance.  祖鲁勇士们举起标枪以示反抗。
