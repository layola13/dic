filoplumaceous:/ˌfɪləʊpluːˈmeɪʃəs/
adjective
丝状羽的，毛状羽的
Having or resembling filoplumes; covered with filoplumes.  Filoplumes are hairlike feathers with a few short barbs or barbules at the tip. They are sensory feathers believed to help birds sense the position of their contour feathers.
例句：The kiwi's plumage is filoplumaceous, giving it a shaggy appearance.  几维鸟的羽毛是丝状的，使它看起来毛茸茸的。

例句：The filoplumaceous feathers around the bird's eyes may help it detect air currents.  鸟类眼睛周围的丝状羽毛可以帮助它们探测气流。

近义词: hairlike, downy, filamentous
反义词: pennaceous (referring to the structured, flight feathers)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *filum* (thread) + *pluma* (feather) + *-aceous* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "having the nature of," "resembling").
