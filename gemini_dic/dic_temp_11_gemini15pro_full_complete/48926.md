deftly:/ˈdeftli/ | adv. | 灵巧地，熟练地 | In a skillful, nimble, or clever way. | She deftly caught the falling vase. 她灵巧地接住了掉落的花瓶。| skillfully, nimbly, adroitly, expertly | clumsily, awkwardly | adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From deft + -ly. Deft comes from Middle English *deft, from Old English *gedæfte (meaning “mild, gentle, meek”), related to Old Frisian *defte and Middle Dutch *defte.  The meaning shifted to "apt, fitting" and then to "skillful, dexterous."
