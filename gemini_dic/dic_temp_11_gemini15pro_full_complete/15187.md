backbone:/ˈbækboʊn/ (US) /ˈbækbəʊn/ (UK)| n.|脊椎; 支柱; 骨干; 决心|The central feature or principal support of something.|例句：The farming community is the backbone of this country.  农业群体是这个国家的支柱。

例句：He doesn't have the backbone to stand up to his boss. 他没有勇气反抗他的老板。|近义词：spine, support, core, foundation, courage, resolve|反义词：weakness, cowardice|词性: noun
