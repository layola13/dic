eyewitnessing:/ˈaɪˈwɪtnɪsɪŋ/
n.
目击，见证
The act of seeing something happen, especially a crime or accident.
I gave the police my account of the eyewitnesssing of the robbery.  我向警方讲述了我目击抢劫案的经过。
observation, witnessing, seeing
ignorance, unawareness
noun
