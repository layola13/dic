cogman:/ˈkɒɡmən/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡmən/ (US)| n. |齿轮工，装配工；（尤指蒸汽机车的）司炉|A person who works with or maintains cogs and gears, especially a stoker on a steam locomotive.|The old cogman skillfully adjusted the gears of the massive engine. 老齿轮工熟练地调整着大型发动机的齿轮。|Synonyms: mechanic, engineer, stoker|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "supervisor" if considering a hierarchy)|noun
