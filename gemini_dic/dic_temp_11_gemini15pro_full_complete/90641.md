humanize:/ˈhjuːmənaɪz/ /ˈhjuːmənɑɪz/ | vt. |使人性化，使通情达理；赋予人性|To make something more humane or to give something human qualities.|例句：They tried to humanize the prison system. 他们试图使监狱系统更加人性化。

例句：The author humanized the characters in the novel. 作者赋予了小说中的人物人性。

近义词：civilize, personalize, soften|反义词：dehumanize, brutalize|词性：动词
