outswim:/ˌaʊtˈswɪm/
verb
游得比…快；比…游得远
to swim faster or further than someone or something else
例句：He can outswim anyone on the team. 他比队里任何人都游得快。

例句：She easily outswam her competitors in the race.  她在比赛中轻松地游赢了她的对手。

近义词：outpace, surpass, beat
反义词：be outswum by
词性：verb
