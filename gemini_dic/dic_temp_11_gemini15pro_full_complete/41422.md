cornerer:/ˈkɔːrnərər/ | n. | 垄断者; 囤积者; 逼至绝境的人 | Someone who corners a market or commodity. A person who forces someone into a corner or a difficult situation. |  The cornerer had bought up all the available wheat, driving up prices. 这个垄断者买断了所有可用的麦子，抬高了价格。|  He felt like a cornerer, trapped with no way out. 他感觉自己像被逼到墙角的人，无路可逃。| monopolist, hoarder | competitor | noun
