nonembellished:/ˌnɒnɪmˈbɛlɪʃt/ | adj. | 未加修饰的；朴素的；未经润色的 |  Not embellished; plain or simple.  Lacking extra decoration or elaboration. |  She told the story in a nonembellished, factual way. 她以一种未加修饰、实事求是的口吻讲述了这个故事。 | synonyms: plain, simple, unadorned, unembellished, austere, stark | antonyms: embellished, ornate, decorated, elaborate, flowery | adjective
