embroiled:/ɪmˈbrɔɪld/| adj.|卷入的，纠缠不清的|involved deeply in an argument, conflict, or difficult situation|例句：The two countries were embroiled in a bitter trade dispute. 这两个国家卷入了一场激烈的贸易纠纷。|例句：He became embroiled in a scandal that cost him his job. 他卷入了一场丑闻，让他丢了工作。|近义词：involved, entangled, mired|反义词:uninvolved, detached, extricated|词性:形容词
词源:From Old French *embroillier (meaning "to confuse, entangle"), from en- ("in") + brooillier ("to muddy, stir up"), from broil ("mud, dirt").
