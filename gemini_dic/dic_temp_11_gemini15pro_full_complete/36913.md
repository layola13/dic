colorizing:/ˈkʌləˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
着色；彩色化
The process of adding color to something, typically a black and white photograph or film.
They are colorizing old black and white movies. 他们正在给黑白老电影上色。
近义词: coloring, tinting, painting
反义词: bleaching, desaturating
词性: verb
