nonperiodicals:/ˌnɒnˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkəlz/ | n. | 非期刊，非定期刊物 | Publications that are not issued on a regular schedule, such as books, reports, and pamphlets. |  The library has a vast collection of nonperiodicals, including rare manuscripts and first editions. 图书馆收藏了大量的非期刊，包括珍稀的手稿和初版书籍。 | Synonyms: non-serials, monographs | Antonyms: periodicals, serials, magazines, journals | noun
