limpkin:/ˈlɪmpkɪn/ | n. | 跛行鸟，叫鹤 | A large wading bird (Aramus guarauna) native to the Americas, with long legs and toes, brown plumage, and a distinctive loud, wailing call. It feeds primarily on apple snails. |  We saw a limpkin probing the mud for snails. 我们看到一只跛行鸟在泥里探寻蜗牛。

|  The limpkin's call echoed through the swamp. 跛行鸟的叫声在沼泽中回荡。 | 近义词: snail kite, crying bird (due to its call) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From its perceived limping gait.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *limpaną "to limp."
