brachyurous:/brækɪˈjʊərəs/| adj.|短尾的|Of or relating to brachyuran crabs; having a short abdomen, typically folded under the thorax.  Characterized by a short, broad carapace and a reduced abdomen folded beneath the thorax.|The brachyurous crabs are a diverse group.|近义词:short-tailed|反义词:long-tailed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *brakhus* "short" + *oura* "tail."
