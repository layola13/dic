microbiologists:/ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒɪsts/
noun
微生物学家
Scientists who study microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, algae, fungi, and some types of parasites.
Microbiologists are studying the new virus. 微生物学家正在研究这种新病毒。
近义词:bacteriologists, virologists
反义词:N/A
词性:n
