flavorings:/ˈfleɪvərɪŋz/ | n. | 调味品；香料 | Substances added to food or drink to give it a particular flavor. | The chef used a variety of flavorings to enhance the dish.  厨师用各种调味品来提升菜肴的味道。| seasonings, spices, condiments, additives |  (No direct antonym, perhaps "natural ingredients" in some contexts) | noun

flavorings:/ˈfleɪvərɪŋz/ | n. | 调味剂 |  Substances that add flavor to something, like food or drink. |  Artificial flavorings are often used in processed foods. 加工食品中经常使用人造香料。|  seasonings, spices, condiments | plain, bland, natural | noun

Etymology:  From "flavor" + "-ing" (suffix indicating the result of an action or process). "Flavor" ultimately comes from the Old French "flaur" (smell, scent), related to the Latin "flare" (to blow).
