outwin:/ˌaʊtˈwɪn/
verb
战胜，赢过
to defeat (someone) in a competition, especially a sporting event.
例句：
Team A outwon Team B in the final match.
A队在决赛中战胜了B队。

He outwon his rivals in the election.
他在选举中战胜了对手。

近义词：defeat, beat, conquer, triumph over, vanquish
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
