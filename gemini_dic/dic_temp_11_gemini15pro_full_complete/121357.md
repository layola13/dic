misgrading:/mɪsˈɡreɪdɪŋ/
n.
错误评分，评分不当
the act of grading something incorrectly, either giving it a higher or lower grade than it deserves.
The student complained about the misgrading of his exam.  这个学生抱怨他的考试评分不当。
The misgrading led to a lower GPA than he expected.  评分错误导致他的平均绩点比预期的低。
incorrect grading, erroneous grading
accurate grading, correct grading
noun
