crackling:/ˈkræklɪŋ/| adj. n.|噼啪作响的，爆裂声|A crackling sound is a series of short, sharp sounds, like something burning in a fire.|The crackling fire warmed the room. 噼啪作响的火温暖了房间。|近义词：crepitating, snapping, popping|反义词：silent, quiet|词性：形容词/名词

crackling:/ˈkræklɪŋ/| n.|爆裂声，噼啪声|The sound of something crackling.|We could hear the crackling of the fire. 我们可以听到火的噼啪声。|近义词：crepitation, snapping, popping|反义词：silence, quiet|词性：名词


词源: From Middle English *craklinge,  present participle of craklen (“to crackle”), frequentative of craken (“to crack”), from Old English cracian (“to crack”).
