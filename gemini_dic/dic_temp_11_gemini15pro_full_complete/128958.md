ninnyish:/ˈnɪniɪʃ/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻气的|Resembling or characteristic of a ninny; silly or foolish.|He made a ninnyish remark that embarrassed everyone. 他说了一句傻话，让大家都尴尬。|近义词：silly, foolish, stupid, inane, asinine|反义词：wise, intelligent, sensible, smart|词性:形容词
Etymology: From ninny + -ish.  "Ninny" comes from the Spanish word "niño" (child), which was often used in a derogatory sense to refer to a simple or foolish person. The suffix "-ish" denotes resembling or characteristic of.
