ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English cristallise, from Medieval Latin crystallīzāre, from Latin crystallus (“crystal”) (from Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krústallos)) + -īzāre (suffix forming verbs).

PHRASE->
crystallize out: 析出结晶  The salt crystallized out of the solution. 盐从溶液中析出结晶。
crystallize into:  具体化为，形成  His vague ideas crystallized into a concrete plan. 他模糊的想法形成了一个具体的计划。
crystallize around:  围绕…形成，以…为中心  Public opinion crystallized around the need for change. 公众舆论围绕着变革的需求而形成。
crystallize a decision:  做出明确的决定  The meeting helped to crystallize a decision on the matter.  会议帮助就此事做出了明确的决定.
crystallize one's thoughts:  使某人的想法清晰  Writing in a journal helps to crystallize one's thoughts.  写日记有助于使人的想法清晰。
