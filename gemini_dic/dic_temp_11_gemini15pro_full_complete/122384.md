mle:/ˌɛm ɛl ˈiː/ | n. | 最大似然估计 (Maximum Likelihood Estimation) |  A method of estimating the parameters of a statistical model, given observations.  It finds the parameter values that maximize the likelihood function, which measures the probability of observing the data given the parameters. | The researchers used MLE to estimate the parameters of the growth model. 研究人员使用最大似然估计来估计增长模型的参数。 | synonyms: maximum likelihood, ML | antonyms: N/A | noun

mle:/ˌɛm ɛl ˈiː/ | abbreviation | 最大似然估计 (Maximum Likelihood Estimation) | Short for Maximum Likelihood Estimation. |  MLE is a common method used in statistics. 最大似然估计是统计学中常用的方法。| synonyms: maximum likelihood, ML | antonyms: N/A | abbreviation
