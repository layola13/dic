exploitational:/ˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃənəl/| adj.|剥削的，利用的|Taking unfair advantage of a person or situation for personal gain.  Characterized by or involving exploitation.|例句：The company's business practices were deemed highly exploitational, taking advantage of cheap labor in developing countries.  这家公司的商业行为被认为是高度剥削性的，利用了发展中国家的廉价劳动力。

例句：The documentary exposed the exploitational conditions in the mining industry. 这部纪录片揭露了采矿业剥削性的工作环境。|近义词：abusive, predatory, selfish, manipulative|反义词：fair, ethical, just, equitable|词性:adjective
