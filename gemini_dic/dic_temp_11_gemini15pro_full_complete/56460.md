dolorousness:/ˈdoʊlərəsnəs/, /ˈdɒlərəsnəs/
n.
悲痛，忧伤
The state of being dolorous; grief, sorrow, or misery.
Her dolorousness was palpable after the loss of her beloved pet. 她失去了心爱的宠物后，悲痛之情溢于言表。
The dolorousness of the occasion was marked by the mournful music.  这个场合的悲痛气氛被哀伤的音乐所烘托。
近义词：sorrow, grief, sadness, misery, anguish
反义词：joy, happiness, elation, cheerfulness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *dolorousnes*, from Old French *doleros*, from Latin *dolōrōsus* (“full of pain”), from *dolor* (“pain, grief”).  The suffix *-ness* forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
