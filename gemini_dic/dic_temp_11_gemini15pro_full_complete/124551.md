mounted:/ˈmaʊntɪd/| adj. |安装的，骑马的，镶嵌的|fixed or set up on something, riding a horse, set in a frame or setting |例句：The museum displayed a mounted dinosaur skeleton. 博物馆展出了一具安装好的恐龙骨架。|近义词：fixed, attached, installed, riding, framed |反义词:dismounted, unmounted| adjective
mounted:/ˈmaʊntɪd/| v. |登上，骑上，增加，上演|get up on, increase, stage a play or event |例句：He mounted his horse and rode away. 他骑上马，策马而去。|近义词：climbed, ascended, increased, escalated, staged |反义词:dismounted, descended, decreased| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mounten*, from Old French *monter* (to mount, ascend, climb, rise), from Vulgar Latin *montare* (to climb, go up), from Latin *mons, montem* (mountain).
