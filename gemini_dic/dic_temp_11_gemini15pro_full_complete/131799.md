nonconscription:/ˌnɒnkənˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. | 免役，非征兵 | The state of not having conscription; the absence of compulsory military service. |  Switzerland has a policy of nonconscription, relying instead on a militia system. 瑞士实行免役政策，依靠民兵制度。| synonyms: exemption from military service, voluntary military service | antonyms: conscription, draft, compulsory military service | noun
