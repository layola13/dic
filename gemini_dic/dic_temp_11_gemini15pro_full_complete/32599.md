chiropodists:/kɪˈrɒpədɪsts/ /kɪˈrɑːpədɪsts/
n.
足病医生；足病治疗师
A chiropodist is a specialist who treats conditions of the feet.
例句：I went to see a chiropodist about my ingrown toenail. 我去看足病医生治疗我的嵌甲。

例句：The chiropodist recommended special insoles for my shoes. 足病医生建议我在鞋子里放特殊的鞋垫。

近义词：podiatrist
反义词：(None -  There isn't a direct opposite profession)
词性：noun
