dauntless:/ˈdɔːntləs/| adj.|无畏的，勇敢的|Showing fearlessness and determination.|例句：The dauntless firefighters rushed into the burning building. 无畏的消防员冲进了燃烧的建筑物。

例句：With dauntless courage, she faced her fears. 她以无畏的勇气面对她的恐惧。|近义词：fearless, intrepid, courageous, valiant, bold|反义词：fearful, timid, cowardly, apprehensive|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *dauntles, from daunten + -less. Equivalent to daunt +‎ -less.
