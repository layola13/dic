intextured:/ɪnˈtɛkstʃərd/
adjective
有纹理的；交织的
woven or incorporated into a texture; having a woven or interwoven texture.
The artist intextured various materials into the tapestry, creating a richly layered piece. 艺术家将各种材料交织到挂毯中，创造出一件层次丰富的作品。
近义词: textured, woven, interwoven
反义词: smooth, plain
词性: adjective
