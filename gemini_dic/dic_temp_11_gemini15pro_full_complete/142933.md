out-travel:/ˌaʊtˈtrævl/
verb
超越旅行；比…旅行更多；旅行距离超过
to travel further or more widely than someone or something else
He out-traveled all his colleagues, visiting over 30 countries in a year. 他比所有同事都旅行得更多，一年内访问了 30 多个国家。
She out-traveled her budget and had to borrow money. 她超出了旅行预算，不得不借钱。
近义词: outdo, surpass, exceed
反义词: be out-traveled by
