capnomancy: /ˈkæpnəmænsi/ | n. | 占卜术(通过观察烟雾) | Divination by observing the smoke or movements of smoke. | The ancient Greeks practiced capnomancy to predict the outcome of battles. 古希腊人通过观察烟雾来预测战争的结果。|  Capnomancy was sometimes used in conjunction with other forms of divination.  占卜术有时与其他形式的占卜一起使用。 |  augury, divination, soothsaying | N/A | noun
