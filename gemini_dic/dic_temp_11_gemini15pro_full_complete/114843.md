mammee:/məˈmiː/ | n. | 玛米果，热带水果 | A tropical American tree (Mammea americana) of the family Calophyllaceae having edible fruit; also, its fruit. |  The mammee is a sweet and aromatic fruit. 玛米果是一种香甜的水果。

The mammee is popular in the Caribbean. 玛米果在加勒比地区很受欢迎。 | 近义词：apricot (in terms of flavor and texture), mamey sapote | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
