flourishing:/ˈflɜːrɪʃɪŋ/ /ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋ/
adjective|繁荣的，兴旺的|Growing or developing successfully; thriving.|例句：The company is flourishing under the new management.  这家公司在新管理层下蓬勃发展。|近义词：thriving, prosperous, booming|反义词：declining, failing, struggling|词性: adjective
flourishing:/ˈflɜːrɪʃɪŋ/ /ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋ/
noun|繁荣，兴盛|A period of thriving or prosperity.|例句：The Renaissance was a flourishing of art and literature. 文艺复兴时期是艺术和文学的繁荣时期。|近义词：prosperity, boom, success|反义词：decline, failure, struggle|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *floriss-, present participle stem of florir "to blossom, bloom, flourish," from Latin florere "to bloom, blossom, flower, be in flower, flourish, prosper," from flos (genitive floris) "flower"
