nonmunicipal:/ˌnɑːnˌmjuːˈnɪsɪpəl/
adjective
非市政的
Not relating to or controlled by a municipality (a city or town with its own local government).
The report analyzed nonmunicipal water usage in the region.  该报告分析了该地区非市政用水情况。
The nonmunicipal organization provided aid to the victims. 这个非市政组织向受害者提供了援助。
Synonyms: private, nongovernmental, independent
Antonyms: municipal, public, city, town
词性: adjective
