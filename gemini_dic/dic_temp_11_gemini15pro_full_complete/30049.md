ccd:/siː siː diː/ | n. | 电荷耦合器件 (Charge-Coupled Device) | A semiconductor device used for the movement of electrical charge, usually from within the device to an area where the charge can be manipulated, for example conversion into a digital value.  It is used in digital cameras, scanners and video cameras. | This new telescope uses a highly sensitive CCD.  这台新望远镜使用高灵敏度的CCD。| synonym: image sensor | antonym: N/A | noun
