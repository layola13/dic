hindrances:/ˈhɪndrənsɪz/| n. |障碍，阻碍|Something that makes it more difficult for you to do something or for something to develop.|The main hindrance to development is the lack of technical expertise.|近义词：obstacles, impediments, barriers, obstructions|反义词：advantages, aids, benefits|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hindrer (Modern French gêner) "to hinder, impede," from Frankish *hindōn, from Proto-Germanic *hindōną (source also of Old High German hintōn "behind," Old Norse hindri "hinderer," Old English hindan "behind, from behind").  The original sense was "to put or keep behind."
