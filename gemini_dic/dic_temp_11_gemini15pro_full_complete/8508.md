antiburgher:/ˈæntiˌbɜːrɡər/ | n. | 反对市政合并者；(尤指苏格兰教会)反对教会受国家控制者 | A member of a Scottish Presbyterian sect (18th century) that opposed the connection between church and state, especially the power of the civil magistrate to appoint ministers to a vacant charge. |  The antiburghers broke away from the established Church of Scotland.  反对市政合并者脱离了苏格兰教会。| synonyms: dissenter, seceder, nonconformist | antonyms: conformist, establishmentarian | noun
