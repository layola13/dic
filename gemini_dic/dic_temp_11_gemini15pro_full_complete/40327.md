contra-indication:/ˌkɒntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
禁忌症; 禁忌
A contraindication is a specific situation in which a drug, procedure, or surgery should not be used because it may be harmful to the person.
例句：There is no contraindication to giving the patient painkillers.  给病人服用止痛药没有禁忌症。

例句：Pregnancy is a contraindication for this medication. 怀孕是服用这种药物的禁忌症。

近义词：prohibition, restriction, caution
反义词：indication
词性：名词
