condemn:/kənˈdɛm/ | vt. |谴责，判刑，宣告…不适用|To express strong disapproval of; to pronounce guilty and sentence to punishment; to declare unfit for use.|The judge condemned the thief to ten years in prison. 法官判处小偷十年监禁。|Synonyms: denounce, censure, sentence, doom|Antonyms: praise, commend, acquit, exonerate|verb

condemned:/kənˈdɛmd/ | adj. |被谴责的，被判刑的，被宣告不适用的|Having been declared unfit for use or habitation; sentenced to a particular punishment, especially death.|The condemned building was scheduled for demolition. 这栋被宣告不适用的建筑物预定要拆除。|Synonyms: doomed, damned, convicted|Antonyms: praised, commended, acquitted|adjective
