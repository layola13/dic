infestuous:/ɪnˈfɛstʃuəs/| adj.|充满害虫的；滋生害虫的；蔓延成灾的|Overrun with or tending to breed infestations, typically of insects or vermin.|The basement was dark and infestuous, filled with cockroaches and rats.  地下室阴暗潮湿，到处是蟑螂和老鼠。|The abandoned building quickly became infestuous with mice and other vermin. 这栋废弃的建筑很快就被老鼠和其他害虫 infest 了。|近义词：infested, overrun, swarming, teeming|反义词：clean, hygienic, pest-free|词性:形容词
