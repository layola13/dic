eyeing:/ˈaɪɪŋ/
verb
注视，打量
looking at something or someone with interest, often because you want to have it or them
She was eyeing the cake hungrily. 她贪婪地盯着蛋糕。
The cat was eyeing the bird in the tree.  猫盯着树上的鸟。
watching, observing, gazing, staring, scrutinizing
ignoring, overlooking, neglecting
v.
