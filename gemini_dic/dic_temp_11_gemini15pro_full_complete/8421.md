anti-whaling:/ˌæntiˈweɪlɪŋ/
adjective
反捕鲸的
Opposed to the hunting of whales.
The anti-whaling group protested the Japanese fleet.  反捕鲸组织抗议日本捕鲸船队。
The activist gave an impassioned anti-whaling speech.  这位活动家发表了充满激情的反捕鲸演讲。
近义词:conservationist, preservationist
反义词:pro-whaling
词性:adjective
