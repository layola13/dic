nonformalistic:/ˌnɒnfɔːməˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非形式主义的
Not adhering to or concerned with formalism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy; informal.  Focusing on content and meaning rather than strict adherence to rules and conventions.
His nonformalistic approach to painting allowed for greater freedom of expression. 他非形式主义的绘画方法允许更大的表达自由。
The critic praised the author's nonformalistic style, appreciating its natural flow and emotional depth.  评论家称赞作者非形式主义的风格，欣赏其自然流畅和情感深度。

近义词:informal, unconventional, unstructured
反义词:formalistic, conventional, rigid
词性:adjective
