flay:/fleɪ/
verb
剥皮；严厉批评
To strip off the skin or outer covering of something; to criticize harshly.
The hunter flayed the deer. 猎人剥了鹿的皮。
The critic flayed the new movie in his review.  评论家在他的评论中严厉批评了这部新电影。
近义词: skin, excoriate, criticize, lambaste
反义词: praise, commend
词性: verb

flay:/fleɪ/
noun
剥皮；严厉批评 (较少用作名词)
The act of stripping off the skin or outer covering; a harsh criticism.
The flay of the animal was quick and efficient.  剥这只动物的皮很快也很有效率。(较少用)
His flay of the government's policies was scathing. 他对政府政策的严厉批评是尖刻的。(较少用)
近义词: stripping, excoriation, criticism, lambasting
反义词: praise, commendation
词性: noun
