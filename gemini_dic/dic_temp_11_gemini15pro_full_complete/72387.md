fluctuant:/ˈflʌktʃuənt/| adj.|波动的，起伏的，涨落的|Varying or unstable; rising and falling irregularly.|The patient's temperature was fluctuant throughout the day. 病人的体温整天都在波动。|Synonyms: variable, unstable, wavering, shifting, oscillating|Antonyms: stable, constant, steady, fixed, unvarying|adjective

fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/| v.|波动，起伏，涨落|To change continually; rise and fall irregularly.|Stock prices fluctuated wildly throughout the year. 股票价格全年剧烈波动。|Synonyms: vary, oscillate, waver, swing, change|Antonyms: stabilize, remain constant, hold steady|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fluctuans, present participle of fluctuare "to wave, undulate," from fluctus "wave, billow."
