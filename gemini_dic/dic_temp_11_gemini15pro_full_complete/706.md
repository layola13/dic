absent-mindedness:/ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪdnəs/
|n.|心不在焉，健忘|The state of being forgetful or preoccupied.|His absent-mindedness led him to leave his keys in the car. 他的心不在焉导致他把钥匙忘在了车里。|近义词：forgetfulness, inattention, preoccupation, distraction|反义词：attentiveness, mindfulness, alertness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From absent + minded + -ness.  "Absent" traces back to Latin "absens," present participle of "abesse" (to be away), from "ab" (away) + "esse" (to be).  "Minded" comes from Old English "gemyndig" (mindful). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or condition.
