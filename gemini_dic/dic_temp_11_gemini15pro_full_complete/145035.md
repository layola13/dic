overafflicts: /ˌoʊvərəˈflɪkt/
verb
过度折磨，使痛苦不堪
To cause excessive suffering or pain; to afflict too much.
The weight of his responsibilities overafflicted him.  他身上的责任让他不堪重负。
The prolonged drought overafflicted the farmers and their crops. 长期干旱使农民和他们的庄稼苦不堪言。
近义词:  overburden, overwhelm, oppress
反义词:  comfort, soothe, relieve
词性: verb
