cyclical: /ˈsaɪklɪkl/ | adj. | 循环的，周期性的 | Happening in cycles; recurring. | The cyclical nature of the seasons.  季节的周期性。|  The company's profits are cyclical, rising and falling with the economy.  公司的利润是周期性的，随着经济的起伏而涨落。 | periodic, recurrent, recurring | non-cyclical, linear | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cyclicus, from Greek kuklikos “circular,” from kuklos “circle, wheel, cycle.”
