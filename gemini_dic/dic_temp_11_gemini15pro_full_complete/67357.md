extensile:/ɪkˈstɛnsaɪl/ /ɛkˈstɛnsaɪl/| adj.|可伸展的，可延长的|Capable of being extended or stretched out.|The chameleon has an extensile tongue. 变色龙的舌头可以伸展。|近义词：protractile, extensible, telescopic|反义词:non-extensile, inextensible, retractile|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *extendere* (to stretch out) + -ile (capable of being).
