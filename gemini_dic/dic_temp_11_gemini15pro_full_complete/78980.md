ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Greek γύρος (gýros, “circle, rotation”), from Ancient Greek γῦρος (gûros). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʷelh₁- (“to revolve, turn”).  Borrowed into English in the mid-20th century.
