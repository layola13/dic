consequently:/ˈkɒnsɪkwəntli/ (US) /ˈkɑːnsɪkwentli/| adv.|因此，所以|As a result; therefore.  It introduces a result or conclusion based on what has been stated previously.|例句：He lost his job, and consequently, he had to move out of his apartment. 他丢了工作，因此不得不搬出公寓。|近义词：therefore, thus, hence, accordingly, as a result|反义词:nevertheless, however, nonetheless|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *consequenter, from Latin consequens, present participle of consequi ("to follow").
