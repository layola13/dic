fisticuffs:/ˈfɪstɪˌkʌfs/| n.| 拳斗，互殴|A fight or brawl involving the use of fists. | They settled their argument with fisticuffs in the alleyway. 他们在小巷里用拳头解决了争论。|Example 2: The two boys engaged in fisticuffs after school. 两个男孩放学后进行了拳击。|Synonym: brawl, fight, scuffle, melee|Antonym: peace, truce, agreement|noun
