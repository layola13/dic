despumation:/ˌdiːspjuːˈmeɪʃən/ |n.|去沫，除沫，消泡|The despumation process removes unwanted foam from liquids. 去沫过程去除液体中不需要的泡沫。|例句：Despumation is crucial in the production of certain foods and beverages.  去沫在某些食品和饮料的生产中至关重要。

例句：Effective despumation prevents overflow and ensures product quality. 有效的去沫可防止溢出并确保产品质量。|近义词：foam removal, defoaming|反义词：foaming, aeration|词性：名词

词源:  From Latin *despumationem, from despumat- “skimmed, froth removed”.  *despumatus is the past participle of despumare “to skim, remove froth” from de- + spumare “to foam.” Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *(s)pewh₂-, to spit, foam.
