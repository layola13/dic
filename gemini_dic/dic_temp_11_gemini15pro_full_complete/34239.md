clans:/klænz/
n.
家族，氏族；部落；集团
A group of families, especially in Scotland, who originally came from the same ancestor and are considered as a unit.  A group of people with a shared interest.
例句：The clans gathered for the annual Highland games.  各氏族聚集起来参加一年一度的高地运动会。

例句：The gaming clan dominated the online tournament.  这个游戏战队称霸了线上锦标赛。

近义词：tribes, groups, families, societies
反义词：individuals

词性: 名词
