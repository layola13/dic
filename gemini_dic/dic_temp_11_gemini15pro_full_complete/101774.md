irreturnable:/ˌɪrɪˈtɜːrnəbl/ | adj. | 不可退货的，不可归还的 | Not able to be returned. |  This software is irreturnable once the seal is broken.  这软件一旦开封就不能退货了。

The tickets are irreturnable and non-refundable.  这些票既不能退也不能退款。

Synonyms: non-returnable, nonrefundable, unredeemable, no-return
Antonyms: returnable, refundable, redeemable

Etymology:  "ir-" (not) + "returnable" (able to be returned).  "Return" comes from Old French "retourner," meaning "to turn back."
