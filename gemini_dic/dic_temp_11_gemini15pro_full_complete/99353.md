interdentil:/ˌɪntərˈdɛnt(ə)l/| adj. n.|齿间的，齿间音|A sound produced by placing the tip of the tongue between the upper and lower teeth, such as the "th" sounds in "thin" and "this".  It can also refer to the space between two teeth.|例句：The "th" sound in "thin" is an interdental fricative. “thin”中的“th”音是一个齿间摩擦音。

例句：He has a large interdentil space between his two front teeth. 他的两颗门牙之间有一个很大的齿间隙。|近义词：interdental (mostly used as adjective) |反义词: None (no direct opposite for a sound or anatomical location)|词性: adjective, noun
