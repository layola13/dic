outvanish:/ˌaʊtˈvænɪʃ/
verb
超越消失；消失得更彻底
To disappear more completely or for a longer duration than something else.
The magician outvanished his rival, making the other's disappearance seem amateurish.  魔术师的消失术比他的对手更高超，使对手的消失显得拙劣。
Ancient civilizations have outvanished, leaving behind only ruins and artifacts. 古老的文明已经彻底消失，只留下废墟和文物。
近义词:eclipse, surpass, outdo
反义词:appear, materialize
词性:v.
