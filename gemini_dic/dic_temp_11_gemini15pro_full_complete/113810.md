mad-as-a-hatter:/ˌmæd əz ə ˈhætər/| adj.|疯疯癫癫的；古怪的；精神错乱的|This idiom refers to someone who is completely crazy or behaves in a very strange way.  It originated from the 18th and 19th centuries when hat makers often suffered from mercury poisoning due to the use of mercury in felt processing, leading to neurological damage and erratic behavior.|He's been acting mad as a hatter ever since he lost his job. 他自从失业后就举止疯疯癫癫的。|Synonyms: crazy, insane, demented, eccentric, bonkers|Antonyms: sane, rational, sensible|adjective
