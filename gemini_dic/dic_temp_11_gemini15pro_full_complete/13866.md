augmenter:/ɔːɡˈmɛntər/ | n. | 增益剂，增强器 |Something that augments; an increasing agent. |  The new software is an augmenter for productivity.  这款新软件可以提高生产力。 | synonyms: enhancer, booster, amplifier | antonyms: reducer, diminisher | noun

augmenter:/ɔːɡˈmɛnt/ | v. |  增加，增大，增强 | To make larger; enlarge in size, extent, or quantity; increase. | The company augmented its staff by hiring ten new employees. 公司增加了十名新员工，扩大了员工队伍。| synonyms: increase, enlarge, expand, amplify | antonyms: decrease, reduce, diminish, lessen | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French augmenter (verb), augment (noun), from Latin augmentare "to increase, enlarge, enrich," from augmentum "an increase, growth," from augere "to increase, make big, enlarge, enrich."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *aug- "to increase."
