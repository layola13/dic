lexeme:/ˈlɛksiːm/| n.| 词位，词素 |A lexeme is a unit of lexical meaning that exists regardless of any inflectional endings it may have or the number of words it may contain. It is the abstract unit which underlies all its inflected variants.|The lexeme "run" includes "runs," "running," and "ran."|近义词: word, vocabulary item, lexical unit|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a linguistic term)|词性:noun
