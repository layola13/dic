misunion: /ˌmɪsˈjuːnjən/ | n. | 错位愈合，不良愈合 | An incorrect or faulty union, especially of fractured bone.  The bones didn't heal properly, resulting in a misunion. 骨头没有正确愈合，导致了错位愈合。 | Example 1: The misunion of his leg caused him chronic pain. 他的腿错位愈合导致他长期疼痛。

Example 2:  The doctor had to re-break the bone to correct the misunion. 医生不得不重新打断骨头来纠正错位愈合。 | Near synonyms: malunion, faulty union, improper healing | Antonyms: proper healing, correct union | noun
