dioeciously: /daɪˈiːʃəsli/ /daɪˈiːsiəsli/
adjective
雌雄异株地
Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.  Plants that are dioecious have separate male and female plants.
Holly trees are dioeciously, meaning that some trees have only male flowers and others have only female flowers. 冬青树是雌雄异株的，这意味着有些树只有雄花，而另一些树只有雌花。
The ginkgo tree reproduces dioeciously. 银杏树是雌雄异株繁殖的。
近义词:  heterosexual (in a broader biological context)
反义词: monoecious, hermaphroditic
词性: adjective
