hemstitched:/ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃt/| adj.| 抽纱边的；褶边的|A decorative stitch used to finish a hem, creating a row of small loops or openwork along the edge. It's often seen on handkerchiefs, napkins, and other fine linens.| The hemstitched edges of the handkerchief were delicate and beautiful. 手帕的抽纱边精致美观。|近义词：embroidered, scalloped, fringed|反义词: plain|词性:adjective

hemstitching:/ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃɪŋ/| n.| 抽纱；褶边|The act or process of creating a hemstitch.| The hemstitching on the tablecloth was done by hand. 桌布上的抽纱是手工做的。|近义词：embroidery, needlework|反义词: None|词性:noun
