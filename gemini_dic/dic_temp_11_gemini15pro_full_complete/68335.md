fall-on:/ˈfɔːl ɒn/| n.|意外收获；意外所得；负担；累赘|Something unexpected that comes to one's advantage or disadvantage.  Something that is burdensome or unwanted. |The inheritance was a welcome fall-on. 这笔遗产是一笔受欢迎的意外之财。 |近义词：windfall, bonus, godsend, burden, nuisance|反义词: loss, detriment|noun

fall-on:/ˈfɔːl ɒn/| adj.|意外发生的；偶然获得的|Happening or acquired unexpectedly.  |The fall-on profits helped the company recover. 意外的利润帮助公司复苏了。 |近义词：unexpected, accidental, fortuitous|反义词: planned, expected|adjective
