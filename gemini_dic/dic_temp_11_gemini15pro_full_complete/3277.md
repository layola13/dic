affirming: /əˈfɜːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 肯定的，确认的；积极的，乐观的 |  Providing positive feedback or encouragement; supportive; validating. | She gave him an affirming nod. 她肯定地点了点头。|  The experience was deeply affirming for her. 这段经历对她来说是非常积极的。 | 近义词: positive, supportive, encouraging, validating | 反义词: negative, discouraging, undermining | 词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "affirm," from Latin *affirmare*, from ad- "to" + firmare "to strengthen," from firmus "strong."
