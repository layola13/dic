calycine:/ˈkælsɪnaɪn/ /ˈkælsɪn/| adj.|杯状的；花萼状的；由花萼形成的|Relating to or resembling a calyx (the sepals of a flower); cup-shaped.  Formed from or by a calyx.|The calycine scales protected the developing bud. 这些杯状鳞片保护着发育中的芽。|近义词：cup-shaped, cuplike, calyx-like|反义词:|adjective

calycine:/ˈkælsɪnaɪn/ /ˈkælsɪn/| n.|由花萼形成的结构|A structure formed from a calyx.|The fruit had a persistent calycine at its base. 果实的底部有一个宿存的花萼结构。|近义词：calyx|反义词:|noun

词源: From Late Latin *calycinus, from Latin calyx, calicis “cup, calyx."
