awayness:/əˈweɪnəs/
n.
远离; 分离感;  疏离感
A state of being away or distant, either physically or emotionally.  A feeling of detachment or disconnection.
She felt a strange awayness from her surroundings after the accident.  事故发生后，她对周围环境感到一种奇怪的疏离感。
近义词：detachment, remoteness, distance, alienation
反义词： closeness, nearness, connection, involvement
词性: 名词

awayness:/əˈweɪnəs/
n.
心不在焉; 出神
A state of being mentally distant or preoccupied.
His awayness during the meeting was evident to everyone. 他在会议期间的心不在焉显而易见。
近义词：absent-mindedness, distraction, preoccupation
反义词： attentiveness, presence, focus
词性: 名词
