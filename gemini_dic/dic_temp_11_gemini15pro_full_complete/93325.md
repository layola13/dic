ill-temperedly: /ɪlˈtɛmpədli/
adverb
暴躁地，脾气坏地
in a bad-tempered or irritable way
He slammed the door ill-temperedly. 他暴躁地摔门。
She ill-temperedly snapped at the waiter. 她没好气地对服务员厉声说道。
近义词: angrily, irritably, grumpily, crossly
反义词: good-naturedly, pleasantly, cheerfully
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From ill-tempered + -ly.  "Ill-tempered" itself comes from "ill" (meaning bad or harmful) + "tempered" (referring to one's disposition or state of mind). "Tempered" ultimately derives from the Latin "temperare" (to mix, blend, regulate).
