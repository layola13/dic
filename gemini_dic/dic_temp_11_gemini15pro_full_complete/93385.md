illicit:/ɪˈlɪsɪt/| adj.|违法的，不正当的|Forbidden by law, rules, or custom. |例句：The illicit trade in endangered species is a serious problem.  非法买卖濒危物种是一个严重的问题。|例句：He was involved in illicit activities such as drug smuggling. 他参与了诸如毒品走私之类的非法活动。|近义词：illegal, unlawful, illegitimate, prohibited|反义词:legal, lawful, legitimate|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *illicitus*, from in- ‘not’ + licitus ‘permitted’.
