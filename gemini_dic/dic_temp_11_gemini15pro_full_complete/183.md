abay:/əˈbeɪ/ | n. | 阿巴亚 | An abaya is a simple, loose over-garment, essentially a robe-like dress, worn by some Muslim women in parts of the Middle East, including Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates.  |  Many women choose to express their personal style through different colors and embellishments on their abayas. 许多女性选择通过不同颜色和装饰的阿巴亚来表达她们的个人风格。 | 近义词：jilbab, chador | 反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific garment.) | noun
