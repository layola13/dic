misrepresentative:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 不具有代表性的；歪曲的；误传的 | Giving a false or misleading representation of something or someone. |  This small sample is misrepresentative of the population as a whole. 这个小样本并不能代表总体情况。| synonyms: misleading, distorted, inaccurate, biased, fallacious, deceptive, untrue, false | antonyms: representative, accurate, truthful, genuine, authentic | adjective

misrepresentation:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. |  歪曲；曲解；不如实陈述 | The action or offence of giving a false or misleading account of the nature of something. | The advertisement was a gross misrepresentation of the facts.  这个广告严重歪曲了事实。| synonyms: distortion, falsification, perversion, travesty, caricature,  misstatement | antonyms: representation, portrayal, depiction, account | noun
