escortage:/ˈeskɔːrtɪdʒ/
n.
护送；护卫队
the act of escorting someone or something; a group of people or vehicles that escort someone or something
The president's escortage included several police motorcycles. 总统的护卫队包括几辆警用摩托车。

The queen's escortage made its way through the crowded streets. 女王的护卫队穿过拥挤的街道。
近义词: escort, guard, convoy
反义词: None
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *escortage*, from escorter (to escort), from Old French esquarter (to quarter, lodge), of Germanic origin.
