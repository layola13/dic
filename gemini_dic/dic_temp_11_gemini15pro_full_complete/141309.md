open-plan:/ˈoʊpən plæn/ (US), /ˈəʊpən plæn/ (UK)| adj.|开放式的|Characterized by the absence of internal walls dividing a floor space into separate rooms. |例句：We work in an open-plan office. 我们在开放式办公室工作。|近义词：open, spacious|反义词：closed-plan, partitioned|adjective

open-plan:/ˈoʊpən plæn/ (US), /ˈəʊpən plæn/ (UK)| n.|开放式布局|A layout or design characterized by the absence of internal walls.|例句：The open-plan design makes the apartment feel much bigger. 开放式布局让公寓感觉更大。|近义词：open layout, open space|反义词：closed plan, partitioned space|noun
