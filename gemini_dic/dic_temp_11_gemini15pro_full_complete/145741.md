overcrowd:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊd/| adj. |过于拥挤的|Filled with too many people or things.|The overcrowded train made it difficult to breathe. 拥挤的火车让人难以呼吸.| congested, packed, jammed| deserted, empty, spacious|adjective
