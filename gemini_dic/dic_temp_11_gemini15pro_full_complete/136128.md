nonreflecting:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈflektɪŋ/ | adj. | 非反射的，不反射的 | Not reflecting light or other radiation; preventing reflection. | Example: They used a nonreflecting coating on the lens. 他们在镜头上使用了非反射涂层。| Example:  Nonreflecting glass is used in picture framing to reduce glare.  非反射玻璃用于相框中以减少眩光。|Synonyms: non-reflective, anti-reflective, matte | Antonyms: reflecting, reflective, glossy | adjective
