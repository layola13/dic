academician: /ˌækədəˈmɪʃən/ | n. |院士；学会会员| A member of an academy, especially a society of scholars, artists, or scientists. |例句：He is a respected academician in the field of physics. 他是物理学界受人尊敬的院士。

例句：She was elected an academician of the Royal Academy of Arts. 她被选为皇家艺术学院院士。|近义词：scholar, intellectual, expert|反义词: amateur, layman|词性:noun

词源: From French *académicien*, from *académie* ("academy") + *-ien* (suffix forming agent nouns).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek *Ἀκαδήμεια* (*Akadḗmeia*, “Academy, Plato's philosophical school”).
