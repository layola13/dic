docibleness:/ˈdɒsəbəlnəs/ /ˈdɑːsəbəlnəs/| n. |温顺；驯服；可教性|The quality of being docile; readily instructed or guided; teachableness. |Her docibleness made her an ideal student. 她的温顺使她成为一个理想的学生。| synonyms: tractability, malleability, compliance, obedience, submissiveness| antonyms: intractability, stubbornness, defiance, rebelliousness| noun


docile:/ˈdɒsaɪl/ /ˈdɑːsəl/| adj. |温顺的；驯服的；易控制的|Easily taught or managed; submissive to instruction.|The docile horse was easy to ride. 这匹温顺的马很容易骑。|synonyms: compliant, obedient, manageable, tame, tractable|antonyms: unruly, defiant, intractable, stubborn, rebellious| adjective
