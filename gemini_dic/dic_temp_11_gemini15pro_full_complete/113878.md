madnesses:/ˈmædnɪsɪz/
n.
疯狂，精神错乱
The state of being mad; insanity.  A display of extremely foolish behavior.
He descended into madness after the loss of his family. 在失去家人之后，他陷入了疯狂。
The party was a scene of sheer madness. 派对现场一片疯狂。
lunacy, insanity, dementia, mania
sanity, rationality
noun
