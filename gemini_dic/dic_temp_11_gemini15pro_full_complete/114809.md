mammal:/ˈmæməl/ | n. | 哺乳动物 |A warm-blooded vertebrate animal of a class that is distinguished by the possession of hair or fur, the secretion of milk by females for the nourishment of the young, and (typically) the birth of live young. |例句：The blue whale is the largest mammal on Earth. 蓝鲸是地球上最大的哺乳动物。|近义词：mammalian creature |反义词:invertebrate |词性:noun
