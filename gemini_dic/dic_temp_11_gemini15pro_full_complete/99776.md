intermediatory:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiətəri/ | adj. | 中间的；媒介的 | Serving as a medium or agent; intervening; intermediate. | The ambassador played an intermediatory role in the negotiations. 大使在谈判中扮演了中间人的角色。 |  The company acts as an intermediatory between buyers and sellers. 这家公司充当买家和卖家之间的中介。 | 近义词: intermediate, mediating, intervening | 反义词: direct, immediate | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *intermediātōrius, from Latin intermedius ("intermediate").
