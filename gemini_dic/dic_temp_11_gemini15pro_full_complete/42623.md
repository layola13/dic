counterretaliation:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˌtæliˈeɪʃən/
|n.|反报复，反击|an act of retaliation in response to an earlier retaliatory action.|例句：The government threatened counterretaliation against any further attacks. 政府威胁要对任何进一步的袭击进行反报复。|例句：Their counterretaliation was swift and decisive. 他们的反击迅速而果断。|近义词：revenge, reprisal, retribution|反义词：forgiveness, pardon|词性: 名词


counterretaliation:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˌtæliˈeɪʃən/
|词源|From counter- + retaliation.  "Counter-" comes from Old French "contre-" meaning "against," ultimately from Latin "contra." "Retaliation" comes from Late Latin "retaliationem," noun of action from past participle stem of "retaliare" meaning "to repay in kind," from "re-" (back, again) + "talis" (such).|
