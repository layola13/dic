medallist:/ˈmedlɪst/ (US) /ˈmedəlɪst/ (UK)| n. |奖牌获得者，勋章获得者|A person who has been awarded a medal, especially in a sporting competition.|The Olympic medallist waved to the crowd. 奥运奖牌获得者向人群挥手致意。|Synonyms: winner, champion, victor|Antonyms: loser, runner-up|noun
