equipoised:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɔɪzd/
adjective
平衡的，均衡的
being in equipoise; evenly balanced or balanced in weight.
例句：The dancer remained equipoised on one leg.  舞者用一条腿保持平衡。

例句：The two teams were equipoised in skill.  两队的实力不相上下。

近义词：balanced, even, stable, poised
反义词：unbalanced, uneven, unstable, lopsided
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From equi- (equal) + poised.
