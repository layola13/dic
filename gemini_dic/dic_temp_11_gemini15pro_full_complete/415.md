abjure:/əbˈdʒʊr/| vt.|放弃，发誓放弃，公开放弃|To solemnly renounce or reject something, especially a belief, cause, or claim.|He abjured his former allegiances. 他放弃了他以前的效忠。|近义词：renounce, relinquish, forswear, recant|反义词：affirm, embrace, uphold|verb

abjure:/əbˈdʒʊr/| vt.|避免，避开|To avoid or shun something.|She abjured all forms of violence. 她避免了所有形式的暴力。|近义词：avoid, shun, eschew|反义词：embrace, welcome|verb
