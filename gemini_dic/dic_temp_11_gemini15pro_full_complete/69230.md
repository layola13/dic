fearsomeness:/ˈfɪərsəmnəs/| n. |可怕; 令人畏惧|The fearsomeness of the tiger's roar made the explorers freeze in their tracks.  老虎吼叫的可怕程度让探险家们吓得呆在原地。|近义词：terribleness, formidableness, dreadfulness|反义词: gentleness, mildness, harmlessness|词性:名词
Etymology: From fearsome + -ness.  Fearsome derives from Old English *fērsam "causing fear," from *fēr "fear" (see fear (n.)) + -some.
