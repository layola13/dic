half-moon:/ˈhæfˌmuːn/ (US), /ˈhɑːfˌmuːn/ (UK)| n.|半月，新月；半月形|A half-moon is the shape of the moon when it is half illuminated, appearing as a semicircle. It also refers to things shaped like a half-moon.|The flag features a half-moon against a blue background. 这面旗帜的图案是在蓝色背景上的一个半月形。|crescent, semicircle|full moon|noun
