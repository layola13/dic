noticed: /ˈnoʊtɪst/ (US) /ˈnəʊtɪst/ (UK)| v. |注意；注意到；察觉到| to become aware of something by seeing, hearing, smelling, touching, or feeling it | I noticed a strange smell in the room. 我注意到房间里有一股奇怪的味道。|  I noticed him leaving the building. 我注意到他离开了大楼。 | perceive, observe, detect, spot | ignore, overlook, miss | verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *noticen, from Anglo-Norman noticer, from Latin notāre (“to mark, note, observe”), from nota (“mark, note”).
