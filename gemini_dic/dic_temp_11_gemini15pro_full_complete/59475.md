easement:/ˈiːzmənt/| n.|地役权，缓和，减轻|A right to cross or otherwise use someone else's land for a specified purpose.|The farmer granted an easement to the power company to run a power line across his field.  农民授予电力公司在其田地上架设电线的通行权。|近义词：right of way, access, relief, mitigation|反义词: obstruction, hindrance|noun

easement:/ˈiːzmənt/| n.|缓和，减轻|The act of making something less painful or difficult.|The medicine provided some easement of his pain. 这药减轻了他的一些痛苦。|近义词：relief, alleviation, mitigation|反义词: aggravation, intensification|noun
