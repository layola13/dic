metafictionist:/ˌmetəˈfɪkʃənɪst/
n.
元小说作家
A writer of metafiction, which is fiction that draws attention to its own constructedness.
Example: The metafictionist blurred the lines between reality and fiction in her novel.  这位元小说作家在小说中模糊了现实与虚构的界限。
Example:  As a metafictionist, he often incorporated the act of writing itself into his narratives. 作为一名元小说作家，他经常将写作本身融入到他的叙事中。
Synonym: postmodernist, experimental writer, self-reflexive writer
Antonym: realist, traditionalist
