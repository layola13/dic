bishoplike: /ˈbɪʃəplaɪk/
adjective
像主教的；主教似的
Resembling or characteristic of a bishop, especially in manner or appearance.
He had a bishoplike gravity about him. 他身上有一种主教般的庄重感。
近义词: episcopal, pontifical, priestly
反义词:  secular, lay
词性: adjective

词源:
bishop (n.) + -like (suffix).  "Bishop" comes from Old English "bisceop," from Late Latin "episcopus," and ultimately from Greek "episkopos" meaning "overseer."  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
