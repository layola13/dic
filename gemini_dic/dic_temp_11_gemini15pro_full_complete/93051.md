idolater:/ˈaɪdəˌleɪtər/| n.|偶像崇拜者，盲目崇拜者|A person who worships idols or someone/something other than God.|例句：He was accused of being an idolater for worshipping statues. 他因崇拜雕像而被指责为偶像崇拜者。

例句：Modern society creates idolaters of celebrities. 现代社会造就了对名人的盲目崇拜者。|近义词：worshipper, devotee, fan, admirer|反义词：iconoclast|词性: noun
