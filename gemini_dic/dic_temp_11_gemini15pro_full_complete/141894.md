ordeal:/ɔːrˈdiːl/ /ɔːrˈdiːl/

| | |例句：The interview was a real ordeal. 面试真是一场煎熬。|
| | |例句：Losing his job was a major ordeal for him and his family. 失去工作对他和他的家人来说是一场巨大的磨难。|
| | |近义词：trial, tribulation, hardship, test|
| | |反义词：comfort, ease, pleasure|
| | |词性：名词|
