overoffending:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度冒犯的；过分得罪人的 |  Causing offense to an excessive degree; going too far in causing someone to feel upset, insulted, or hurt. |  His overoffending jokes made everyone uncomfortable. 他的过分冒犯的笑话让每个人都不舒服。|  Synonyms: overly offensive, excessively offensive, too offensive | Antonyms: inoffensive, harmless, polite | adjective

overoffending:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɛndɪŋ/ | n. | 过度冒犯的行为 | The act of causing offense to an excessive degree. | Her overoffending led to her being asked to leave the party. 她过分的冒犯行为导致她被要求离开派对。 | Synonyms: excessive offense, over-the-top offense | Antonyms: politeness, respect, courtesy | noun
