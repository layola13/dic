curdled:/ˈkɜːrdld/ (US), /ˈkɜːdəld/ (UK)| adj.|凝固的，变质的|Having turned into curds; solidified or thickened; also used figuratively to describe something that has soured or gone bad.|The milk curdled after we left it out overnight.  牛奶在我们隔夜放置后凝固了。|The sight of the accident curdled my blood.  看到事故现场我的血液都凝固了。|Synonyms: coagulated, clotted, thickened, soured (figurative)|Antonyms: liquid, fluid, smooth, sweet (figurative)|Adjective
