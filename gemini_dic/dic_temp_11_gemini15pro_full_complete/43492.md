craterlike:/ˈkreɪtərˌlaɪk/| adj.|像火山口的，环状的|Resembling a crater, typically having a bowl-shaped depression.|The meteorite impact left a craterlike impression in the desert. 陨石撞击在沙漠中留下了一个像火山口一样的凹陷。|近义词：cratered, bowl-shaped, concave|反义词:convex, domed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From 'crater' + '-like'.  'Crater' comes from the Greek word 'kratēr', meaning "mixing bowl," referring to the bowl-shaped cavity of a volcano.  '-like' is a suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
