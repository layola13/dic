circumundulate: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈʌndjʊleɪt/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈʌndʒəleɪt/
verb
环绕波动，环绕起伏
to flow or extend in waves or sinuous curves around something.  The river circumundulates the valley.
The river circumundulates the valley. 这条河流环绕着山谷蜿蜒流淌。
The path circumundulates the hill, offering stunning views at every turn. 这条小路环绕着山丘蜿蜒而上，每一步都能欣赏到迷人的景色。

近义词: meander, wind, encircle, surround
反义词: straighten
