hardening:/ˈhɑːrdnɪŋ/ /ˈhɑːrdnɪŋ/ | n. |硬化，强化|The process of making something harder or more resistant.  The hardening of steel involves heating and rapid cooling.|The concrete needs time for hardening.| 近义词:strengthening, toughening, reinforcing|反义词:softening, weakening|词性:名词

hardening:/ˈhɑːrdnɪŋ/ /ˈhɑːrdnɪŋ/ | adj. |变硬的，强化的|Becoming harder or more resistant. Hardening concrete is vulnerable to cracking.|The hardening agent is added to the resin.|近义词:solidifying, setting|反义词:softening|词性:形容词
