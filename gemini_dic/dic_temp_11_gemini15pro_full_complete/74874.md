frescoists:/ˈfrɛskoʊɪsts/
noun
壁画画家
Artists who paint frescoes (murals done on wet plaster).
The frescoists of the Renaissance were highly skilled. 文艺复兴时期的壁画画家技艺高超。
muralists, painters
sculptors
