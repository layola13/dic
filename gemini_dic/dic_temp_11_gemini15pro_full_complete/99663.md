interlabeling:/ˌɪntərˈleɪbəlɪŋ/
|n.|相互标记，互相标记|The process of assigning labels to each other, typically in a collaborative or comparative context.  This can involve annotating data, categorizing items, or tagging features in a shared space or system, enabling a mutual understanding and agreement on the meaning and significance of the labeled elements.|Researchers engaged in interlabeling of the images to ensure consistency in their analysis. 研究人员对图像进行相互标记，以确保分析的一致性。|近义词：cross-labeling, collaborative labeling, joint annotation|反义词：independent labeling, solo annotation|词性: noun
