actualise:/ˈæktʃʊəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈæktʃuəˌlaɪz/ (US)| v. |实现，实行，使…具体化|To make something real or to put something into action; to fulfil a potential. |She finally actualised her dream of opening a bakery. 她终于实现了开面包店的梦想。

He has the potential to be a great leader, but he needs to actualise it. 他有潜力成为一位伟大的领导者，但他需要将其实现出来。|近义词：realize, achieve, fulfil, implement|反义词：negate, prevent, hinder|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin actualis "active, practical," from Latin actus "a doing, a driving, an impulse," from agere "to drive, lead, act."  The English suffix "-ise" (or "-ize") is used to form verbs and means "to make or cause to be."
