infinitesimalities:/ɪnˌfɪnɪtəˈsɪmələtiz/ | n. | 无穷小量；无限小的东西 |  Extremely small quantities; quantities approaching zero.  Values that are so small they are effectively zero. |  Calculus deals with infinitesimalities to determine the slope of a curve. 微积分处理无穷小量来确定曲线的斜率。| The theory involved considering infinitesimalities of time.  该理论涉及考虑无限小的时间。 | synonyms:  minima,  infinitesimals | antonyms: infinities | noun
