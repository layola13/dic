doublecrosser:/ˌdʌbəlˈkrɒsər/ (US) /ˌdʌbəlˈkrɒsə(r)/ (UK)| n. |出卖朋友者；背叛者；骗子|A person who deceives or betrays someone, especially someone who trusts them.| He thought his partner was loyal, but he turned out to be a doublecrosser. 他以为他的伙伴很忠诚，结果却是个背叛者。| 近义词：traitor, betrayer, deceiver, cheat | 反义词：loyalist, ally | 词性：名词

doublecross:/ˌdʌbəlˈkrɒs/ (US) /ˌdʌbəlˈkrɒs/ (UK)| v. |背叛；欺骗|To deceive or betray someone, especially someone who trusts you.|  She doublecrossed him by revealing his secret to the police. 她向警察透露了他的秘密，背叛了他。| 近义词：betray, deceive, cheat | 反义词：support, help | 词性：动词
