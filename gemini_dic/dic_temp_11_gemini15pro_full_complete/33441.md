churme:/tʃɜːrm/ | n. | 喧闹声，嘈杂声；鸟鸣声 | A confused noise or murmur; the sound of birds twittering. | The churme of the city kept him awake all night. 城市的喧闹声让他整夜无法入睡。| 近义词: noise, din, clamor, hubbub, twittering | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: 名词

词源:  Middle English *chirm, from Old French *chirmer (to chirp, twitter). Related to chirm (v.).
