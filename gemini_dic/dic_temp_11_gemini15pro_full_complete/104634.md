ETYMOLOGY->
From Turkish *Karaman*, named after the Karamanids, a Turkic dynasty that ruled the region in the 13th-15th centuries. 源自土耳其语 *Karaman*，以 13-15 世纪统治该地区的突厥王朝卡拉曼王朝命名。
