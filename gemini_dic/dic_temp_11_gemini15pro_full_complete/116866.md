maxis:/ˈmæksiːz/
noun
极大值；最大限度；最大值；极大
The point at which something is largest.
The company reached its maxis in the 1990s. 这家公司在20世纪90年代达到了顶峰。
maximum, peak, apex, summit, acme
minimum, nadir, bottom
