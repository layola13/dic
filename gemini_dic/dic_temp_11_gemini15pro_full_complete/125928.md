mushier:/ˈmʌʃiər/ (US), /ˈmʌʃɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更糊状的，更感伤的|Softer and more pulpy than normal; excessively sentimental.|The overripe bananas were too mushier for banana bread.  这些过熟的香蕉太糊了，不适合做香蕉面包。| The movie was a bit too mushier for my taste. 这部电影对我来说有点太煽情了。|近义词：softer, pulpier, sentimental, mawkish|反义词：firmer, harder, unsentimental|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mush + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  "Mush" originates from the Old French word "mosche," meaning "soft, swampy ground."
