impr:/ɪmˈprʊv/| v. |改进，改善|To make or become better.|She's been working hard to improve her French. 她一直在努力提高她的法语水平。|The weather has improved considerably. 天气已经明显好转了。|近义词: enhance, ameliorate, better, upgrade|反义词: worsen, deteriorate, decline|词性:动词
词源: From Middle French *emprover, from en- (“in-”) + prover (“to prove, test”).  Ultimately from Latin probare (“to test, prove”).
