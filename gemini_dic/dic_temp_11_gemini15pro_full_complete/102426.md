jackaroo:/ˌdʒækəˈruː/
noun
澳大利亚的农场工人，牧场学徒
A young man working on a sheep or cattle station to gain practical experience.
Jackaroos learn the skills necessary to manage large properties.  澳大利亚农场工人学习管理大型地产所需的技能。

He started out as a jackaroo on a remote cattle station. 他最初在一个偏远的养牛场当农场工人。

近义词:farmhand, station hand, stockman
反义词:None (No direct opposite)
词性:n.
