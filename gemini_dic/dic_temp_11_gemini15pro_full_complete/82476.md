grungeish:/ˈɡrʌndʒɪʃ/| adj.|垃圾摇滚风格的；颓废派的；邋遢的|Resembling or characteristic of grunge, a style of rock music characterized by distorted electric guitars and lyrics expressing disillusionment and apathy, or the related fashion style of unkempt clothing.|Her grungeish look involved ripped jeans and an oversized flannel shirt.|近义词：sloppy, unkempt, messy, disheveled, scruffy, grungy|反义词：neat, tidy, clean, polished, sophisticated|词性:形容词
