misproposal:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊzl/
n.
错误的提议，不当的建议
An incorrect or unsuitable proposal; a proposal that is flawed or inappropriate.
He made a misproposal that was quickly rejected by the board. 他提出了一个很快被董事会否决的错误建议。
Near Synonyms:  bad proposal, flawed proposal, inappropriate proposal, unsuitable proposal
Antonyms: good proposal, sound proposal, appropriate proposal, suitable proposal
词性: noun
