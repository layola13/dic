nonserious:/ˌnɒnˈsɪəriəs/ (British), /ˌnɑːnˈsɪriəs/ (American)| adj.|不严肃的，非认真的，轻松的|Not serious; frivolous or lighthearted.|例句：They exchanged nonserious banter. 他们互相开了些轻松的玩笑。|近义词：frivolous, lighthearted, playful, casual, unimportant|反义词：serious, grave, important, significant|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + serious.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not."  "Serious" comes from the Latin "serius" meaning "grave, weighty, important."
