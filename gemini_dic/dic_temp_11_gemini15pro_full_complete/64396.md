erethism:/ˈɛrɪθɪzəm/ | n. |兴奋过度; 兴奋易怒; 水银中毒|A state of abnormal irritability or sensitivity to stimulation, often associated with mercury poisoning.  A heightened state of excitement or sensitivity.|

例句：
The erethism manifested as tremors and emotional instability.
这种兴奋过度表现为震颤和情绪不稳定。

The patient exhibited signs of erethism, including anxiety and restlessness.
病人表现出兴奋易怒的迹象，包括焦虑和不安。


近义词：irritability, excitability, sensitivity, restlessness
反义词：calmness, tranquility


词性: noun


词源：From Greek *erethismos*, from erethizein 'to irritate, excite'.
