dallier:/ˈdæliər/
noun
闲荡者，游手好闲的人
A person who dallies; one who lingers or delays.
He was a dallier, never finishing a task on time. 他是个游手好闲的人，从不按时完成任务。
近义词: lingerer, loiterer, idler, procrastinator
反义词: hustler, go-getter

dallier:/ˈdæliər/
verb
闲荡，游荡，拖延
To linger; to delay or waste time.
She dallied in the garden, enjoying the flowers. 她在花园里闲逛，欣赏着鲜花。
近义词: linger, loiter, tarry, dawdle
反义词: hasten, hurry, rush


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dalien, from Old French dalier "to chat, converse, entertain," of uncertain origin.
