nondefiner:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈfaɪ.nər/ | n. | 非定义者，未下定义者 |  Someone or something that does not define or establish a clear boundary or category. |  The artist is a nondefiner, refusing to be categorized by any single genre. 这位艺术家是一个非定义者，拒绝被任何单一类型所归类。|  nonconformist, individualist |  conformist, traditionalist | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "define" (from Latin "definire" - to limit, determine) + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns).
