boldness:/ˈboʊldnɪs/ | n. |大胆；勇敢；冒失|The quality of being bold; willingness to take risks and act decisively; courage and confidence.|She showed boldness in confronting her boss. 她勇敢地面对她的老板。|近义词：courage, bravery, audacity, daring, nerve|反义词：timidity, cowardice, fearfulness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *boldnes*, equivalent to bold +‎ -ness.
