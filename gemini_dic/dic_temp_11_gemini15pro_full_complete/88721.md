hobit:/ˈhɒbɪt/ (British) /ˈhɑːbɪt/ (American)| n.|霍比特人|A fictional humanoid race in J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy world, smaller than dwarves and characterized by hairy feet and a love of comfort.| Bilbo Baggins was a hobbit who lived in the Shire. 比尔博·巴金斯是一个住在夏尔的霍比特人。| The hobbits enjoyed a peaceful life in their hobbit-holes. 霍比特人在他们的霍比特洞里过着平静的生活。 | synonyms: halfling (though Tolkien distinguished between the two), little people| antonyms: giant, ogre| noun
