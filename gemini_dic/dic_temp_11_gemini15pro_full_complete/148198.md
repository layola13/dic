overshipment: /ˌoʊvərˈʃɪpmənt/ | n. | 过量装运，过量出货 | the act of shipping more goods than were ordered or more than are needed. |  The overshipment caused storage problems for the small warehouse.  过量装运给小型仓库带来了存储问题。 | Example: Due to a clerical error, an overshipment of ten thousand units was sent to the retailer.  由于文书错误，零售商收到了过量一万件的货物。| Synonyms: excess shipment, surplus shipment | Antonyms: undershipment, shortage | noun
