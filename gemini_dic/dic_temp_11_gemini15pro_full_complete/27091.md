calicoback:/ˈkælɪkoʊbæk/| adj.|印花棉布背的；有印花棉布背衬的|A calicoback refers to something, usually a book, that has a backing or cover made of calico, a plain-woven cotton fabric.  It often implies a simple, inexpensive, or homemade binding.|The antique book had a faded calicoback.|近义词:clothbound, fabric-covered|反义词:leather-bound, hardcover|词性:adjective
