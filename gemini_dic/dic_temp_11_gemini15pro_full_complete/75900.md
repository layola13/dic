fundi:/ˈfʊndiː/ (UK), /ˈfʌndiː/ (US)| n.|专家，行家；内行|An expert or skilled person in a particular field or activity.  A person who is extremely knowledgeable and enthusiastic about a particular subject or activity.|例句：He's a real fundi when it comes to computers.  他是个真正的电脑专家。|例句：She's a fundi about vintage cars. 她对老爷车很在行。|近义词：expert, specialist, connoisseur, aficionado|反义词：amateur, novice|词性: noun

词源: From Swahili *fundi*, from *m-fundi* "craftsman," from *ku-funda* "to learn, to teach."
