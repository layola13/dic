overembroiders:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈbrɔɪdərz/
verb
过度刺绣，过分修饰
Embroidering something excessively, adding too much decoration.
The designer overembroidered the dress, making it look gaudy.  设计师在裙子上绣得太多，使它看起来很俗气。

She overembroidered the story, adding unnecessary details that made it unbelievable. 她过分修饰了故事，添加了不必要的细节，使它难以置信。

近义词:overdecorate, embellish excessively
反义词:understate, simplify
词性:v
