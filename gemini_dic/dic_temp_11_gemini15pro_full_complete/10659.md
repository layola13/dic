approvals:/əˈpruːvəlz/
n.
批准；认可；赞同
the act of officially agreeing to something
The project has received approval from the board. 该项目已获得董事会的批准。

例句：
The new drug is awaiting FDA approval.  这种新药正在等待FDA的批准。

The proposal needs the approval of the CEO before it can be implemented.  这项提议需要首席执行官的批准才能实施。

近义词：
acceptance, consent, sanction, ratification, endorsement
反义词：
disapproval, rejection, denial, veto

词性:
名词 (Noun)


词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French aprover, based on Latin approbare, from ad- ‘to’ + probare ‘prove, test’.
