kaftan:/ˈkæftæn/ (US), /ˈkæftɑːn/ (UK)| n.|长袍，卡夫坦|A long, loose robe with wide sleeves, often worn in Middle Eastern countries.|She wore a brightly colored kaftan to the beach party. 她穿着色彩鲜艳的卡夫坦去参加海滩派对。|Synonyms: robe, gown, tunic|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "tight-fitting clothes")|noun
