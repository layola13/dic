interpolable:/ɪnˈtɜːpələbl/ | adj. |可插值的，可内插的 | Able to be interpolated; capable of being estimated or calculated by interpolation. | The data points are dense enough to be interpolable.  这些数据点足够密集，可以进行插值。| Synonyms: calculable, estimable, inferable | Antonyms: non-interpolable | adjective
