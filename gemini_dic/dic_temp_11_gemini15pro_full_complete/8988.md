antiliberally:/ˌæntiˈlɪbərəli/
adverb
反自由地，非自由主义地
In a manner opposed to liberal principles or policies.
He antiliberally condemned the progressive social reforms. 他以反自由主义的方式谴责了进步的社会改革。
The politician antiliberally rejected the proposal for universal healthcare.  这位政客以反自由主义的立场拒绝了全民医保的提议。
Synonyms: illiberally, undemocratically, restrictively
Antonyms: liberally, democratically, progressively
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  + liberally (adverb): in a liberal manner
