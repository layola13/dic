fumaric:/fjuːˈmærɪk/ | adj.| 富马酸的；反丁烯二酸的 | relating to or derived from fumaric acid | Fumaric acid is used in the food industry. 富马酸用于食品工业。 |  近义词: butenedioic (technical term) | 反义词: maleic (its isomer) | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *fūmāricus (“of smoke”), from fūmus (“smoke”).  Fumaric acid was originally isolated from the plant Fumaria officinalis, which was burned to produce a smoky, acidic vapor.
