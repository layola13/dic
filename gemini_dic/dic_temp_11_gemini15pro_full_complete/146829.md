overimpressionable:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛʃənəbəl/ | adj. | 过于易受影响的，过于敏感的 | Easily impressed or influenced; highly susceptible to external stimuli or suggestion. |  Teenagers are often overimpressionable and easily swayed by peer pressure. 青少年通常很容易受影响，容易受到同伴压力的左右。

She's overimpressionable and takes everything to heart. 她过于敏感，什么事都往心里去。|  impressionable, susceptible, sensitive | insensitive, resistant, impervious | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From over- + impressionable.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Impressionable" comes from the verb "impress" (from Latin *impressare, "to press into"), combined with the suffix "-able," meaning "capable of being."
