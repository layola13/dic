flywheel:/ˈflaɪˌwiːl/ | n. | 飞轮 | A heavy revolving wheel in a machine that helps to increase the machine's momentum and provide greater stability or a more constant speed.  It stores rotational energy. | The flywheel helps to smooth out the power delivery of the engine. 飞轮有助于平滑发动机的动力输出。

The potter's wheel uses a flywheel to maintain a constant speed. 陶工的轮子使用飞轮来保持恒定的速度。 | 近义词：inertia wheel, momentum wheel | 反义词：brake | 词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fly + wheel.  The "fly" in flywheel refers to the rapid rotation of the wheel.
