nontabulated:/ˌnɒnˈtæbjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未列表的，未制成表格的 | Not arranged or recorded in a table or list. | The raw data is nontabulated, making analysis difficult. 原始数据未列表，使得分析困难。|  The survey results were nontabulated, presented as a series of narratives instead.  调查结果未制成表格，而是以一系列叙述的方式呈现。 |近义词：unlisted, unclassified, disorganized |反义词: tabulated, listed, organized |词性: adjective
