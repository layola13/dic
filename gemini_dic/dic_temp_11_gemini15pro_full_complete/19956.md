bioeconomy:/ˈbaɪoʊɪˌkɑːnəmi/ | n. | 生物经济 | An economy based on the sustainable production and utilization of renewable biological resources, including crops, forests, marine resources, and microorganisms, to produce food, energy, materials, and other products. |  The development of the bioeconomy is crucial for a sustainable future. 生物经济的发展对可持续未来至关重要。

The bioeconomy offers a promising alternative to fossil fuels. 生物经济为化石燃料提供了一个有希望的替代方案。 | 近义词: bio-based economy | 反义词: fossil fuel economy | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
bio- (life) + economy (management of resources) From Greek bios "life" + oikonomia "household management"
