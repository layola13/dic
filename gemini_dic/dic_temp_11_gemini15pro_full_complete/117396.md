medicares:/ˈmɛdɪˌkɛərz/
n.
医疗保险（Medicare 的复数形式，通常指多种 Medicare 计划或多人享有的 Medicare）
Medicare is a US government health insurance program for people aged 65 or older. Medicares are available with different coverage options. (Medicare 是美国政府为 65 岁及以上人群提供的健康保险计划。有多种 Medicare 计划可供选择。)
Medicare plans, Medicare benefits, health insurances
Lack of health insurance
