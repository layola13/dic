epizootic:/ˌɛpɪzoʊˈɒtɪk/ | adj. n. | 动物流行病的；动物流行病 | Affecting many animals of one kind at the same time; an outbreak of disease that spreads quickly and widely among animals. |
The epizootic outbreak of avian flu led to the culling of millions of chickens.  禽流感疫情导致数百万只鸡被扑杀。

The farmer was concerned about the potential for an epizootic. 农民担心可能会爆发动物流行病。 |
近义词: epidemic (among humans), pandemic, outbreak | 反义词: sporadic, isolated | 词性: adjective, noun

epizootic:/ˌɛpɪzoʊˈɒtɪk/ | n. | 动物流行病 | An outbreak of disease that spreads quickly and widely among animals. |
The epizootic decimated the local deer population.  这场动物流行病使当地鹿的数量锐减。

The rapid spread of the disease indicated an epizootic was underway. 疾病的迅速传播表明一场动物流行病正在发生。 |
近义词: epidemic (among humans), pandemic, outbreak | 反义词: sporadic, isolated | 词性: noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From French *épizootique*, from Greek *epizōotikos* "prevailing among animals," from *epi* "upon" + *zōon* "animal."
