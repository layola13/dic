happy-hours:/ˈhæpi ˌaʊərz/ | n. |欢乐时光；特价时段|A period of time, usually in the late afternoon or early evening, when alcoholic drinks are sold at reduced prices in bars and restaurants.|例句：Let's go to happy hour after work. 下班后我们去喝一杯吧。

例句：The happy hours at this bar are from 5 pm to 7 pm.  这家酒吧的欢乐时光是从下午5点到7点。

|近义词：cocktail hour|反义词:|词性:名词
