brahmanism:/ˈbrɑːmənɪzəm/
n.
婆罗门教
A historical religion of India, based on the Vedas and the caste system, that developed into Hinduism.
Brahmanism was the dominant religion in India before Buddhism.  婆罗门教是佛教兴起前印度的主要宗教。
The caste system was a central feature of Brahmanism. 种姓制度是婆罗门教的核心特征。
Hinduism, Vedic religion
Atheism, Buddhism
