词源：
From Old French cruel (12c., Modern French cruel), from Latin crudelis "hard-hearted, unfeeling, harsh, severe, fierce," from crudus "bloody, raw" (see crude). The sense evolution is perhaps via "bloody-minded." Related: Cruelly; cruelness.
