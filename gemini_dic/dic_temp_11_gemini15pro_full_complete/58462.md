ductility:/dʌkˈtɪləti/
n.|延展性，韧性|The ductility of a metal is its ability to be drawn into wires without breaking.  金属的延展性是指其被拉成线材而不发生断裂的能力。|例句：Copper has high ductility, making it ideal for wiring. 铜具有很高的延展性，使其成为理想的电线材料。

|近义词：malleability, plasticity, flexibility|反义词：brittleness, fragility|词性:noun

ductile:/ˈdʌktaɪl/
adj.|可延展的，韧性的|Ductile materials can be easily molded into different shapes. 可延展的材料可以很容易地塑造成不同的形状。|例句：Gold is a highly ductile metal. 黄金是一种延展性很高的金属。|近义词：malleable, pliable, flexible|反义词：brittle, fragile|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ductilitas, from Latin ductilis (“ductile”), from ducere (“to lead, draw”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *ductilitas，来自拉丁语 ductilis（“可延展的”），来自 ducere（“引导，拉”）。
