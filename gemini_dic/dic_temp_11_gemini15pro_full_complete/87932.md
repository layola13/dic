hierophantic:/ˌhaɪərəˈfæn.tɪk/ | adj. | 神圣的，显圣的 |  Relating to or denoting a manifestation of the sacred.  Revealing something holy or sacred. | The hierophantic nature of the ancient rituals filled the participants with awe. 古老仪式的显圣性质使参与者充满了敬畏。 |The shaman performed a hierophantic dance to invoke the spirits. 萨满跳了一支显圣的舞蹈来召唤神灵。 | 近义词: sacred, holy, divine, numinous | 反义词: profane, secular, mundane | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Greek hieros (sacred) + phainein (to show).  来自希腊语hieros（神圣的）+ phainein（展示）。
