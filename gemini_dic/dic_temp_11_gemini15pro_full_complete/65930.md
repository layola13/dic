exacerbatingly:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪtɪŋli/
adverb
加剧地，恶化地
making a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse
The situation was exacerbatingly slow.  情况恶化地缓慢。
The noise was exacerbatingly loud. 噪音大得令人难以忍受。
Synonyms:  worseningly, aggravatingly, intensifyingly
Antonyms: soothingly, calmingly, alleviatingly
词性: 副词
