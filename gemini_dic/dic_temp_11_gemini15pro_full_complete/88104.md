highwayman:/ˈhaɪweɪmən/| n. |拦路强盗，公路劫匪|A robber who attacks travellers on a highway.|例句：The highwayman held up the stagecoach. 拦路强盗拦截了公共马车。

例句：Highwaymen were a common danger in the 18th century.  18世纪，拦路强盗是一个常见的危险。

近义词：bandit, robber, outlaw, brigand|反义词:lawman, policeman|词性:noun
