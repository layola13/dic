cabbler:/ˈkɒblə(r)/ (US: /ˈkɑːblər/)| n.|修鞋匠，补鞋匠|A person who mends shoes.|例句：The cobbler repaired my worn-out boots. 补鞋匠修好了我破旧的靴子。

例句：I took my shoes to the cobbler for new heels. 我把鞋子拿到鞋匠那里去换新鞋跟。

近义词：shoemaker, shoe repairer|反义词:|词性：noun

词源：From Middle English *cobelere, from Old French cobelier, from Latin *copularius (“cup maker”), from copula (“bond, tie”).  Originally referring to a maker of cups and then of wooden vessels or tubs before shifting to a mender of shoes.
