deflagrate:/ˈdefləˌɡreɪt/| vi.|爆燃，迅速燃烧|To burn suddenly and violently, especially with a bright flame and crackling sound.|The fireworks deflagrated in a shower of sparks. 烟花爆燃，火花四溅。|近义词：explode, combust, detonate|反义词：extinguish, smother|Verb

词源: From Latin *deflagratus*, past participle of *deflagrare* "to burn down," from *de-* "down" + *flagrare* "to blaze, burn."
