consentingly:/kənˈsɛntɪŋli/
adv.
同意地，默认地
in a consenting manner; willingly.
She consentingly agreed to his proposal. 她欣然同意了他的求婚。
近义词: willingly, readily, voluntarily
反义词: unwillingly, reluctantly, forcibly
词性: 副词

词源: From consent + -ing + -ly.  Consent ultimately derives from Latin consentire "to feel together," from com- "together" + sentire "to feel."
