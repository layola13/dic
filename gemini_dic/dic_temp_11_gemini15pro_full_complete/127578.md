nectarines:/ˈnɛktəriːnz/
n.
油桃（复数）
A small, smooth-skinned variety of peach.
例句：I bought a bag of fresh nectarines at the market. 我在市场买了一袋新鲜的油桃。

例句：Nectarines are sweeter than peaches. 油桃比桃子甜.

近义词：peaches, apricots
反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of fruit)
词性: noun
