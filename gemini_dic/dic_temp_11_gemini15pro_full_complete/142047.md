organophosphates:/ɔːrˌɡænəˈfɒsfeɪts/ | n. |有机磷酸酯；有机磷酸盐|A class of organic chemicals containing phosphorus. These compounds are often used as pesticides, herbicides, and nerve agents. |Organophosphates are widely used in agriculture to control pests. 有机磷酸酯广泛用于农业害虫防治。

Organophosphates can be extremely toxic to humans. 有机磷酸酯对人类有剧毒。 |Synonyms:  OP compounds,  phosphoric acid esters |Antonyms: None (No direct chemical antonym, though some substances act as antidotes to their toxic effects) | Noun
