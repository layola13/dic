coppering:/ˈkɒpərɪŋ/ | n. |镀铜; 铜锈; 铜绿;  (马蹄)铜色化 |The process of coating something with copper, or the appearance of copper rust or verdigris.  The discoloration of a horse's hoof to a copper-like color, often a sign of disease. | The old statue showed signs of coppering.  这古老的雕像显现出铜绿的痕迹。 | 近义词:  plating, coating, rusting, verdigris | 反义词: stripping, cleaning | 词性: 名词

coppering:/ˈkɒpərɪŋ/ | adj. |镀铜的; 铜锈的; 铜绿的; (马蹄)铜色化的|  Covered with or resembling copper, or having the appearance of copper rust or verdigris. Discolored to a copper-like color (referring to a horse's hoof). | The coppering roof shimmered in the sunlight.  镀铜的屋顶在阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词: copper-plated, copper-colored, rusty, verdigrised | 反义词: clean, uncoated | 词性: 形容词
