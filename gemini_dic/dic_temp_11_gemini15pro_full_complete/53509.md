disassimilative:/ˌdɪsɪˈsɪmɪlətɪv/ | adj. | 异化的，异化作用的 | Relating to or characterized by dissimilation, a process in which similar sounds become less similar. | The dissimilative loss of one of the 'r' sounds in the word 'February' is a common example in spoken English.  二月这个词在英语口语中一个“r”音的异化消失是一个常见的例子。 | Similar: divergent, differentiating | Opposite: assimilative, convergent | adjective


disassimilation:/ˌdɪsɪˌsɪməˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 异化，异化作用 | The process by which similar sounds in a word become less similar. | The pronunciation of "February" as "February" is an example of dissimilation. “February”发音成“February”是异化的一个例子。 | Similar: differentiation, divergence | Opposite: assimilation, convergence | noun
