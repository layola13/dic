dismounted:/dɪˈsmaʊntɪd/ | v. adj. |下马；拆卸；卸下|get off a horse, bicycle, etc.; take apart (a piece of equipment) | The knight dismounted his horse. 骑士下马了。|近义词：alight, disembark, dismantle|反义词:mount, assemble|词性:动词/形容词
dismounted:/dɪˈsmaʊntɪd/ | adj. |已下马的；已拆卸的|having got off a horse, bicycle, etc.; having been taken apart | The dismounted gun was transported by truck. 拆卸下来的枪支用卡车运输。 | 近义词：unmounted, disassembled | 反义词: mounted, assembled | 词性:形容词

词源:  From Old French *desmonter (unattested) or directly from Medieval Latin dismontare "to descend from a horse, dismantle," from Latin dis- "apart, away" + montare "to mount, climb," from mons "mountain."
