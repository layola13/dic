nonascetic: /ˌnɒnəˈsɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非禁欲主义的，非苦行的 | Not ascetic; not characterized by or suggesting the practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons. |  He lived a nonascetic life, enjoying good food and wine.  他过着非禁欲的生活，享受美食和美酒。 |  non-austere, indulgent, worldly, hedonistic | ascetic, austere, abstinent | adjective

Etymology:
From non- + ascetic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "ascetic" comes from the Greek "askētikos," meaning "rigorous, practicing self-discipline."
