insecurenesses:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊrɪtɪz/
n.|不安全感；不安；无把握|A feeling of lacking confidence and not being sure of your own abilities or worth. |She has many insecurities about her appearance. 她对外表有很多不安全感。|
近义词: anxieties, doubts, worries|反义词: confidence, security, self-assurance|词性:名词(plural)

insecurity:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊrɪti/
n.|不安全；不安；无把握|Lack of confidence and not being sure of your own abilities or worth.|He spoke with a slight insecurity in his voice.  他说话的声音略带一丝不安。|近义词: anxiety, doubt, worry|反义词: confidence, security, self-assurance|词性:名词

词源:  From in- (not) + secure + -ity (noun suffix). Ultimately from Latin *sēcūrus* (free from care).
