amitsu: /ɑːˈmɪtsuː/
noun
阿密束
A Japanese word meaning "honeydew" (specifically, a type of melon).  It is also sometimes used to refer to other sweet, juicy fruits or foods.
例句：The amitsu was perfectly ripe and sweet.  这个蜜瓜熟得恰到好处，非常甜。

例句：She brought a box of amitsu as a gift. 她带了一盒蜜瓜作为礼物。
近义词：honeydew melon, cantaloupe
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fruit)
词性: noun
