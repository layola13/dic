nonexploitative:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔɪtətɪv/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔɪtətɪv/ (UK)
adjective
非剥削性的
Not exploiting others, especially not making profit unfairly from their work.
例句：They promote nonexploitative working conditions for all employees.  他们提倡为所有员工提供非剥削性的工作环境。

例句：The company prides itself on its nonexploitative business practices.  这家公司以其非剥削性的商业行为为荣。

近义词：fair, just, ethical, equitable
反义词：exploitative, abusive, predatory
词性: adjective
