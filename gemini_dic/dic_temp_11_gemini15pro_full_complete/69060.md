ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *falteren*, frequentative of *falten* (“to fold, stumble, fall”), from Old English *fealdan* (“to fold”) and *fallan* (“to fall”), both from Proto-Germanic *falþaną* (“to fold”). Related to Dutch *vouwen* (“to fold”) and German *falten* (“to fold”). The sense development is from "stumble and almost fall" to "hesitate."
