millenniarism:/mɪˈleniərɪzəm/
n.|千禧年主义；千年至福说|The belief in a future period of one thousand years of peace and happiness, especially on earth.|The preacher's sermons were filled with millenniarism, predicting the imminent end of the world and the rise of a new divine kingdom. |牧师的布道充满了千禧年主义，预言世界末日即将到来，一个新的神圣王国即将崛起。|
millenniarism:/mɪˈleniərɪzəm/
noun|千禧年主义；千年至福说|The doctrine of or belief in millenarianism. |The book explored the history of millenniarism in various cultures. |这本书探讨了不同文化中千禧年主义的历史。|
近义词:chiliasm, millennialism|
反义词:|无明确反义词|
词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *millenarismus, from Latin mīllēnārius (“containing a thousand”).
