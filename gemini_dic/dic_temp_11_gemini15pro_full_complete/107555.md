landloper:/ˈlændˌloʊpər/| n. |流浪汉；漂泊者；游民|A person who travels from place to place without a settled home or regular work.|The landloper wandered from town to town, seeking odd jobs.|Synonyms: vagrant, vagabond, tramp, drifter, nomad, wanderer|Antonyms: settler, resident, homeowner|noun
