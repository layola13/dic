miasmology:/ˌmaɪæzˈmɒlədʒi/
n.
瘴气学;  关于瘴气的学说
The study of miasmas, or noxious exhalations from decaying organic matter, formerly believed to cause disease.
The doctor, steeped in miasmology, believed the swamp fumes were the source of the illness.  这位深谙瘴气学的医生认为沼泽散发的气体是疾病的根源。
近义词:obsolete medical theories (已淘汰的医学理论)
反义词:germ theory (细菌理论)
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Greek *miasma* "stain, pollution" + -logy
