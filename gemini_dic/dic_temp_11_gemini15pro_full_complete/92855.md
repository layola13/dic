icv: /ˈaɪ siː viː/ | abbreviation | 心血管的 (cardiovascular) |  ICV is an abbreviation for Intracranial Volume, meaning the volume within the skull. It is also sometimes used informally as an abbreviation for cardiovascular. |  The doctor said the patient's ICV was normal. 医生说病人的颅内容积正常。|  The study focused on ICV health. (informal usage) 这项研究的重点是心血管健康。(非正式用法) | synonyms: (for cardiovascular) cardiac, circulatory | antonyms: (none for abbreviation) | abbreviation
