hypostatize:/haɪˈpɒstətaɪz/ | v. | 使抽象化，使具体化；假设，假定 | To treat or regard (something abstract) as a concrete reality. To attribute real existence to (a concept). |  He tends to hypostatize the forces of history. 他倾向于将历史的力量具体化。 |  reify, embody, personify, objectify | abstract | verb

hypostatization:/haɪˌpɒstətɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 抽象化，具体化；假设，假定 | The act of hypostatizing something, or the state of being hypostatized. | The hypostatization of abstract concepts can lead to confusion. 将抽象概念具体化会导致混淆。| reification, embodiment, personification, objectification | abstraction | noun
