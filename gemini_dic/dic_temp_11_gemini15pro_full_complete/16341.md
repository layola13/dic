barbarize:/ˈbɑːrbəˌraɪz/| v. |使野蛮化，使粗俗化|To make or become barbarous; to cause to decline from a civilized state to a more primitive one.|The constant warfare has barbarized the region, eroding its cultural traditions.  不断的战争使该地区野蛮化，侵蚀了其文化传统。| The influx of tourists has barbarized the once pristine beaches.  大量游客的涌入使曾经原始的海滩变得粗俗不堪。| 近义词: brutalize, savage, corrupt, debase | 反义词: civilize, refine, cultivate |词性: 动词
