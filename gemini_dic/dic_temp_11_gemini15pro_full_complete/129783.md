non-fictional:/ˌnɒnˈfɪkʃənəl/ |adj.|非虚构的，纪实的|Based on real events or facts, not imagined or invented.| Example: I prefer non-fictional books, especially biographies. 我更喜欢非虚构类的书籍，尤其是传记。| Example:  She's writing a non-fictional account of her travels in South America. 她正在写一本关于她在南美旅行的纪实作品。 |Synonyms: factual, true, real, authentic, genuine|Antonyms: fictional, imaginary, invented, made-up|Word type: adjective

non-fiction:/ˌnɒnˈfɪkʃən/ |n.|非小说，非虚构文学| Prose writing that is based on facts, real events, and real people, such as biography or history.| Example:  The library has a large section devoted to non-fiction. 图书馆有一个很大的区域专门用于非虚构类书籍。 | Example:  He's a well-known author of non-fiction. 他是一位著名的非虚构类作家。| Synonyms: factual writing, documentary|Antonyms: fiction|Word type: noun
