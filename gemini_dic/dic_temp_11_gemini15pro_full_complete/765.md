absolvatory:/əbˈzɒlvətəri/
adjective
赦免的，免罪的
Serving to absolve; containing or granting absolution.
The priest gave an absolvatory prayer. 神父做了一次赦免的祷告。
近义词: forgiving, pardoning, exculpatory
反义词: condemnatory, incriminating
词性: adjective

absolvatory:/əbˈzɒlvətəri/
noun
赦免
A statement granting a pardon or remission of sins.
The court issued an absolvatory regarding his involvement in the crime. 法庭发布了一份关于他参与犯罪的赦免声明。
近义词: pardon, remission, acquittal
反义词: conviction, condemnation
词性: noun
