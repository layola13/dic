casemented:/ˈkeɪsməntɪd/
adjective
装有窗扇的；有窗框的
Fitted with casements; having casements.
The casemented windows offered a picturesque view of the garden. 装有窗扇的窗户提供了花园如画的景色。
The old house had charming casemented windows. 这座老房子有着迷人的窗框窗户。
近义词: windowed, glazed
反义词: windowless
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From casement + -ed.  Casement itself comes from Old French *casement, from caser "to frame, enclose."
