capsquare:/ˈkæpˌskwɛər/ | n. |平方上限; 上限平方 |A function that takes a vector and returns the square of its magnitude.  It is often used in optimization problems where one wants to minimize or maximize the magnitude of a vector. | The capsquare of the vector (3, 4) is 25.  (3, 4) 向量的平方上限是 25。 |近义词: magnitude squared, squared norm |反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "square root of magnitude") |词性: noun
