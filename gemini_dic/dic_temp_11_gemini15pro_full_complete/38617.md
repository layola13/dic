concretive:/kənˈkriːtɪv/
adjective
具体的；凝固的；团结的
Making something concrete or solid; uniting into a solid mass.  Relating to the solidification of fluids.
例句：The concretive power of shared experience brought the community closer. 共同经历的凝聚力使社区更加紧密。

例句：The artist used a concretive material to create the sculpture.  艺术家用一种凝固性材料来创作雕塑。

近义词：coagulative, solidifying, cementing, unifying, binding
反义词：dissolving, separating, fluid, liquid
词性: adjective


concretive:/kənˈkriːtɪv/
noun
凝固剂
A substance that causes concretion or solidification.
例句：The scientist added a concretive to the mixture to accelerate the hardening process. 科学家在混合物中加入了一种凝固剂来加速硬化过程。

例句：The concretive used in the construction process was a new type of cement.  建筑过程中使用的凝固剂是一种新型水泥。

近义词：coagulant, setting agent, binder
反义词：solvent, thinner
词性: noun
