overdub:/ˈoʊvərˌdʌb/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈdʌb/ (UK)| n. vt.|配音；叠录；覆录|To record additional sound (such as music or vocals) on an existing recording.|The singer overdubbed her vocals to make them sound richer. 歌手叠录了她的歌声，使它们听起来更丰富。|近义词：dub, rerecord|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'original recording')|词性: noun, verb

overdubs:/ˈoʊvərˌdʌbz/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈdʌbz/ (UK)| n.|配音；叠录；覆录（复数）|Multiple recordings of additional sound layered onto an existing track.|The song features several overdubs of the guitar solo. 这首歌的特点是吉他独奏的几段叠录。|近义词：dubs, rerecordings|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun

词源： "Over-" (meaning "on top of" or "in addition to") + "dub" (shortened from "double," originally referring to copying a film or recording).  The term evolved to specifically describe adding sound to an existing recording.
