gabionnade:/ˌɡæbiəˈnɑːd/ |n.|石笼挡土墙；用石笼加固的工事|A defensive work of gabions. A structure made of gabions, often used for retaining walls or erosion control. | The engineers constructed a gabionnade to protect the bridge from the river's current. 工程师们建造了一道石笼挡土墙来保护桥梁免受河水冲刷。 | synonym: gabion wall, retaining wall,revetment | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "open bank," "unprotected slope") | noun
