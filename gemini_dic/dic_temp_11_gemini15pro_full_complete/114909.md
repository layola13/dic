manacle: /ˈmænəkl/ (US) /ˈmænəkəl/ (UK)| n. v.| 手铐，束缚；给…上手铐，束缚 |A metal band, chain, or shackle for fastening someone's hands or ankles.  To restrain with manacles. | The prisoner's hands were bound with manacles. 囚犯的双手被手铐铐住了。| The police manacled the suspect. 警察给嫌疑犯戴上了手铐。| 近义词：handcuffs, shackles, fetters, chains, restrain, confine | 反义词：free, liberate, release | 词性: noun, verb

Etymology:
From Old French *manacle, from Vulgar Latin *manicla, from Latin manicula "little hand," diminutive of manus "hand."
