barista:/bəˈriːstə/ /bɑːˈriːstə/| n.|咖啡师，咖啡调配师|A person who makes and serves coffee (especially in a coffee shop).|例句：The barista skillfully poured latte art onto the cappuccino. 咖啡师熟练地将拿铁艺术倒在卡布奇诺上。

例句：I asked the barista for a recommendation and they suggested a cold brew. 我向咖啡师征求建议，他们推荐了冷萃咖啡。|近义词：coffee maker, barman (if serving alcoholic drinks as well)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps customer)|词性:noun
