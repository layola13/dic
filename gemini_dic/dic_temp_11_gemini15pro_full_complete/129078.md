nitromagnesite:/ˌnaɪtrəʊˈmæɡnɪˌsaɪt/ | n. | 硝镁石 | A mineral consisting of hydrous magnesium nitrate, typically occurring as white efflorescent crystals.  A naturally occurring hydrated magnesium nitrate mineral. | Nitromagnesite is often found in caves. 硝镁石经常在洞穴中被发现。 | Nitromagnesite can be used as a fertilizer. 硝镁石可以用作肥料。 |  Synonyms:  magnesium nitrate hydrate, nitro-chalcite | Antonyms: None | noun
