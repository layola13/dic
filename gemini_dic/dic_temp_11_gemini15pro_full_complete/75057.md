frightening:/ˈfraɪtnɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人恐惧的；骇人的 | Causing fear or alarm; terrifying. | The movie was so frightening that I couldn't sleep. 这部电影太吓人了，我睡不着。|  The storm was a frightening experience. 这场暴风雨是一次可怕的经历。 | 近义词：scary, terrifying, horrifying, alarming | 反义词：comforting, reassuring, calming | 词性：形容词

frighten:/ˈfraɪtn/ | v. | 使惊恐；吓唬 | To make someone feel fear; to scare. |  Don't frighten the children with ghost stories. 不要用鬼故事吓唬孩子们。 | The loud noise frightened the dog. 巨大的噪音吓到了那只狗。 | 近义词：scare, terrify, alarm | 反义词：comfort, reassure, calm | 词性：动词
