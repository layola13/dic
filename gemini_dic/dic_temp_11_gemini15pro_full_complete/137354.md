nontaxonomically:/ˌnɒnˌtæksəˈnɒmɪkli/ | adv. | 非分类学地 |  In a way that is not based on taxonomy or classification.  It refers to grouping or considering things without strict adherence to established scientific classifications. |  The museum displayed the artifacts nontaxonomically, grouping them by color and material rather than by their historical period.  博物馆以非分类学的方式展示了这些文物，按颜色和材质而不是历史时期进行分组。| Synonyms:  nonsystematically,  informally | Antonyms: taxonomically, systematically | adverb
