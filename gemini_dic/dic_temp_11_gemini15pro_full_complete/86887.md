hemisection:/ˌhemiˈsekʃən/ | n. | 半切; 半断 | The surgical cutting of half of a structure, such as the spinal cord. |  A hemisection of the spinal cord can result in loss of sensation and motor function on one side of the body. 脊髓半切可导致身体一侧感觉和运动功能丧失。 | bisection, transection |  | noun
