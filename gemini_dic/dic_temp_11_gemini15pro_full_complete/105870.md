kitish:/ˈkɪtɪʃ/| adj.|媚俗的，庸俗的|Excessively ornate or sentimental, considered to be in poor taste.|例句：Her house was filled with kitish ornaments. 她的房子里摆满了庸俗的装饰品。

例句：The film was criticized for its kitish portrayal of romance. 这部电影因其对浪漫爱情的庸俗刻画而受到批评。|近义词：tacky, gaudy, tasteless, vulgar, cheap, garish|反义词：tasteful, refined, elegant, sophisticated, understated|词性:形容词

词源: From German *kitsch*, meaning "trash".  Ultimately probably related to Yiddish *kitsh*, meaning "to cheat".
