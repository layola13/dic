anti-oriental:/ˌænti-ɔːriˈentl/
adjective
反东方的，反东方人的
Opposed or hostile to the peoples, cultures, or customs of the Orient (East Asia).
His anti-oriental sentiments were evident in his writings.  他反东方的观点在他的著作中很明显。
The politician's anti-oriental rhetoric fueled racial tensions.  这位政客的反东方言论加剧了种族紧张局势。
Synonyms: racist, prejudiced, xenophobic, bigoted
Antonyms: pro-oriental, tolerant, accepting, inclusive
词性: adjective
