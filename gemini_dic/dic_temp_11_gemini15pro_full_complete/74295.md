fractionalist:/ˈfrækʃənəlɪst/
noun
分裂主义者；细分主义者
A person who advocates for fractional ownership or fractionalization, especially in financial markets.  Someone who promotes dividing assets or systems into smaller units.
例句：The fractionalist argued that dividing the company into smaller, independent units would increase its overall value. 分裂主义者认为，将公司分成更小的独立单位会增加其整体价值。

例句：Some fractionalists believe that fractional ownership of real estate can make luxury properties more accessible to a wider range of investors. 一些细分主义者认为，房地产的部分所有权可以让更广泛的投资者更容易获得豪宅。
近义词：separatist, splitter, divisionist
反义词：unitarian, integrationist
词性:n.
