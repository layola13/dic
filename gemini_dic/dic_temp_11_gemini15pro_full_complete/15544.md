baggies:/ˈbæɡiz/| n. |小塑料袋；宽松的短裤|Small plastic bags (often used for storing drugs); loose-fitting shorts.|例句：He kept his lunch in a baggie. 他把午饭放在一个小塑料袋里。

例句：He wore baggies to the beach. 他穿着宽松短裤去了海滩。|近义词：plastic bags (for small bags), loose shorts|反义词: - |名词

baggies:/ˈbæɡiz/| n. |宽松的裤子（尤指运动短裤）|Loose-fitting pants, especially athletic shorts.|例句：She wore her baggies to the gym. 她穿着宽松的运动裤去了健身房。

例句：The kids were all wearing baggies at the park.  孩子们在公园里都穿着宽松的裤子。|近义词：shorts, sweatpants|反义词: tight pants|名词
