hierarchism: /ˈhaɪərɑːrkɪzəm/ | n. |等级主义，等级制度 |A belief in or adherence to a hierarchy or hierarchical system.  The belief that social structures should be organized based on a hierarchy, often with a rigid system of power and authority. |  Example 1: Hierarchism is deeply embedded in the company's culture, making it difficult for employees to voice their concerns.  公司文化中根深蒂固的等级主义使得员工难以表达自己的担忧。

Example 2:  The hierarchism of the feudal system placed the king at the top and the peasants at the bottom. 封建制度的等级体系将国王置于顶端，将农民置于底层。 |Synonyms: hierarchy, stratification, ranking, pecking order | Antonyms: egalitarianism, equality, democracy | noun
