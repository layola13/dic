nonnegligible:/ˌnɒnˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbl/ | adj. |不可忽略的，不容忽视的 |Not negligible; large enough to be important or to have a noticeable effect. |The impact of the new policy was nonnegligible. 新政策的影响不容忽视。|  Example: The study found a nonnegligible correlation between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究发现吸烟和肺癌之间存在不可忽视的相关性。|Synonyms: significant, substantial, appreciable, considerable| Antonyms: negligible, insignificant, trivial, minor | adjective
