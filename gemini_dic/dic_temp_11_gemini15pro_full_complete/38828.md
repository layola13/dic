cones:/kəʊnz/ (UK) /koʊnz/ (US)| n.|锥体; 圆锥体; (尤指松树的)球果; |A cone is a solid or hollow three-dimensional object that has a circular base and a single vertex. Cones can also refer to the cone-shaped fruits of certain trees, especially conifers like pine trees.|The pine tree was covered in cones. 那棵松树上长满了球果。|Synonyms: pyramid, vertex, strobilus (technical)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

cones:/kəʊnz/ (UK) /koʊnz/ (US)| v. (third-person singular simple present cones, present participle coning, simple past and past participle coned)|使成锥形;|To shape something like a cone.  This is less common than the noun form.|The potter coned the clay on the wheel.  陶工在轮子上把黏土做成锥形。|Synonyms: taper, narrow, point|Antonyms: broaden, widen, flatten|Verb
