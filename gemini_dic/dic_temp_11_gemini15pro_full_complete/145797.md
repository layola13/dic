overdecadently:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkədəntli/
| adv. | 过于颓废地，过度放纵地 | Excessively, immoderately, or luxuriously indulgent, often to the point of being morally objectionable or wasteful. | He lived overdecadently, squandering his fortune on lavish parties and expensive wines. 他生活极其颓废，把自己的财产挥霍在奢华的派对和昂贵的葡萄酒上。 |  The desserts were presented overdecadently, piled high with whipped cream and drizzled with chocolate. 甜点摆放得过于奢华，堆满了鲜奶油，淋上了巧克力。 | synonym:  luxuriously, extravagantly, immoderately, excessively | antonym:  moderately, frugally, abstemiously, simply | 词性: 副词
