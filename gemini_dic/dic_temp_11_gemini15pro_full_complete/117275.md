mechanization:/ˌmekənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |机械化 |The process of introducing machines or automatic devices to do work previously done by humans or animals. |The mechanization of agriculture has greatly increased crop yields. 农业机械化极大地提高了农作物产量。| 近义词: automation, industrialization | 反义词: manual labor, hand labor | 词性: noun

mechanize:/ˈmekənaɪz/ | v. | 机械化 |To equip with machinery, especially to replace human or animal labor. |The factory was mechanized to increase production. 工厂机械化以提高产量。|近义词: automate, motorize |反义词:  de-mechanize (less common) |词性: verb
