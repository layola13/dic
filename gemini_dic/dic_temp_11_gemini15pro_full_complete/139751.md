一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The clock strikes twelve o'clock. 时钟敲响十二点。
The train leaves at eight o'clock every morning. 火车每天早上八点出发。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
It's approaching five o'clock.  快到五点了。
The bells are ringing, it must be six o'clock. 钟声响起，一定是六点了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
It has just turned ten o'clock. 刚到十点。
The meeting has run past two o'clock. 会议开过了两点。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
We have been waiting since eight o'clock. 我们从八点起就一直在等。
She has been working since seven o'clock this morning. 她从今天早上七点就开始工作了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The alarm went off at six o'clock. 闹钟在六点响了。
The film started at eight o'clock. 电影八点开始。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
At seven o'clock, I was having dinner. 七点钟的时候，我正在吃晚饭。
She was sleeping at ten o'clock last night. 昨晚十点她在睡觉。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
By nine o'clock, I had finished my work. 到九点钟，我已经完成了我的工作。
They had left by six o'clock. 他们在六点之前离开了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
By five o'clock, I had been working for eight hours. 到五点钟，我已经工作了八个小时。
She had been waiting for two hours by the time he arrived at seven o'clock.  到他七点到达时，她已经等了两个小时。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The meeting will start at two o'clock. 会议将在两点开始。
I will be there by five o'clock. 我会在五点之前到那里。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

This time tomorrow, I will be traveling to London. 明天这个时候，我将在去伦敦的路上。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
