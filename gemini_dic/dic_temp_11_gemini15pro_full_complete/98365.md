insisture: /ɪnˈsɪstʃər/
n.
坚持, 执着
The quality or state of insisting; insistence.
His insisture on the matter finally convinced them. 他对这件事的坚持最终说服了他们。
近义词: insistence, persistence, perseverance
反义词: acquiescence, yielding, submission
词性: 名词

词源:  From insist + -ure,  modeled after words like capture, closure, etc. Insist itself comes from Latin insistere "to stand upon, persist, persevere," from in- "on, upon" + sistere "to cause to stand," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm."
