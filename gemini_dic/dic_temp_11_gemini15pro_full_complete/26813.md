cageless:/ˈkeɪdʒləs/| adj.|无笼的，自由的|Without a cage; not confined to a cage.  It describes something or someone that is not restricted or confined by a cage, either literally or figuratively. |The zoo is known for its cageless enclosures, allowing animals to roam more freely.  这个动物园以其无笼的围栏而闻名，让动物们可以更自由地活动。|近义词：free, unrestrained, liberated, unconfined|反义词: caged, confined, imprisoned|词性:形容词
