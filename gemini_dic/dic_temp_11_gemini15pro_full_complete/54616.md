dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/
verb
肢解，分割
To cut or tear (a body) into pieces; mutilate.
The serial killer dismembered his victims and buried the parts in different locations.  连环杀手肢解了他的受害者，并将尸块埋在不同的地方。
dissect, mutilate
assemble, unite

dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/
verb
分割，分裂 (国家、组织等)
To divide into parts; break up; separate.
The empire was dismembered after the war. 战后，帝国被分裂了。
divide, separate, break up
unite, integrate
