nonexcusable:/ˌnɒnɪkˈskjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 不可原谅的，不可宽恕的 | Not justifiable or excusable; unforgivable. | His behavior was completely nonexcusable. 他的行为完全不可原谅。|  His lateness to the funeral was nonexcusable. 他葬礼迟到是不可原谅的。| 近义词: unforgivable, unjustifiable, indefensible, inexcusable | 反义词: excusable, justifiable, forgivable, understandable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- (not) + excusable (able to be excused). The root "excuse" comes from the Latin "excusare," meaning "to free from blame."
