maverick:/ˈmævərɪk/| n. adj.|特立独行的人，持不同意见者；不墨守成规的，标新立异的|A person who independently takes an original or unorthodox line of thought or action.  Also used as an adjective to describe such a person or their actions. |例句：He was considered a maverick in the field of physics. 他被认为是物理学界的特立独行之人。|近义词：nonconformist, rebel, individualist, dissenter, iconoclast|反义词:conformist, follower, traditionalist|词性:名词, 形容词
etymology: From Samuel Maverick (1803-1870), a Texas rancher who did not brand his cattle.|词源：来自Samuel Maverick (1803-1870)，一位不给牛打烙印的德克萨斯州牧场主。
