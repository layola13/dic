caiman:/ˈkeɪmən/| n. |凯门鳄|A semiaquatic reptile resembling an alligator, but with a broader, shorter head and bony plates on its belly.  Native to Central and South America.|Caymans inhabit swamps and rivers. 凯门鳄栖息在沼泽和河流中。|The caiman lurked in the murky water. 凯门鳄潜伏在浑浊的水中。|近义词:alligator, crocodile|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Spanish *caimán*, from Carib *kaiman*.
