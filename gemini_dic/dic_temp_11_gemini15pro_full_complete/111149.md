litheness:/ˈlaɪθnəs/| n. |轻盈，柔软，灵活性|The gymnast displayed incredible litheness in her routine.  体操运动员在她的表演中展现了惊人的灵活性。|近义词：flexibility, suppleness, agility|反义词：stiffness, rigidity|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *lithenesse, from Old English *līþnes (“mildness, gentleness”), from līþe (“gentle, mild; pleasant, agreeable; soft, tender; supple”) + -nes.  Cognate with Old High German lindī (“softness, gentleness”), from lindi (“soft, mild”).  The sense development from “mildness” to “suppleness” occurred in Middle English.
