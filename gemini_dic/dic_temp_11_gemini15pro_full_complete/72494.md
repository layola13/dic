fluorescently:/flɔːˈresəntli/ /flʊəˈresəntli/
adverb
荧光地；发荧光地
Emitting or reflecting light by fluorescence; in a fluorescent manner.
The bacteria were fluorescently labeled so they could be easily tracked.  这些细菌被荧光标记，以便于追踪。
The jellyfish pulsed fluorescently in the dark depths. 水母在黑暗的深处发出荧光脉冲。
近义词: brightly, vividly, brilliantly
反义词: dimly, faintly
词性: adverb

词源:  From the adjective "fluorescent" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Fluorescent" comes from "fluorescence," which is derived from "fluorspar" (a mineral that exhibits fluorescence) and "-escence" (a suffix indicating a process or state).
