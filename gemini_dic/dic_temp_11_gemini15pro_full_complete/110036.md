licences:/ˈlaɪsənsɪz/ (British English), /ˈlaɪsənsəz/ (American English)| n.|许可证，执照|A document giving official permission to do something.|He doesn't have a driver's licence. 他没有驾驶执照。|Synonyms: permits, authorization, certifications|Antonyms: prohibitions, bans|Noun

licence:/ˈlaɪsəns/ (British English), /ˈlaɪsənsəz/ (American English)| n.|许可，特许|Permission to do something.|The restaurant applied for a licence to sell alcohol. 这家餐厅申请了售酒许可证。|Synonyms: permission, authorization, permit|Antonyms: prohibition, ban|Noun

license:/ˈlaɪsəns/ (American English)| v.|许可，授权|To give someone official permission to do something.|The state licenses teachers. 该州给教师颁发许可证。|Synonyms: authorize, permit, allow|Antonyms: prohibit, forbid, ban|Verb
