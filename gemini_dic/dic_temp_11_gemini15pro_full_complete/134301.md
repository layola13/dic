noninjury:/ˌnɑːnˈɪndʒəri/
noun
无伤害；无损伤
The accident resulted in noninjury to the driver.  这起事故没有对司机造成伤害。
The report confirmed the noninjury status of all passengers. 报告确认所有乘客均未受伤。
Synonyms: safety, harmlessness
Antonyms: injury, harm, damage
