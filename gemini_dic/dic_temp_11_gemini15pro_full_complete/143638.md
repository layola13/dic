outjutted:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌtɪd/
verb
突出；伸出
extend or protrude beyond a surface or line.
The rocky cliffs outjutted into the stormy sea.  悬崖峭壁伸入波涛汹涌的大海。
The balcony outjutted from the second floor.  阳台从二楼伸出来。
project, protrude, jut out, stick out, extend
recede, retract, sink
v.
