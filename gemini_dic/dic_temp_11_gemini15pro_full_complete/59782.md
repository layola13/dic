ecoterrorism:/ˈiːkoʊˈtɛrərɪzəm/
n.|生态恐怖主义|The use of violence or sabotage to achieve environmentalist aims.|Ecoterrorism is a serious threat to businesses that exploit natural resources. 生态恐怖主义是对开发自然资源的企业的严重威胁。|Example: The group was accused of ecoterrorism after sabotaging the logging equipment.  这个组织在破坏伐木设备后被指控犯有生态恐怖主义罪行。

Example:  Some activists believe ecoterrorism is the only way to protect endangered species. 一些激进分子认为生态恐怖主义是保护濒危物种的唯一途径。|Synonyms: environmental terrorism, green terrorism|Antonyms: environmental conservation, ecological preservation|noun


Etymology:
eco- (from Greek oikos "house, dwelling, home") + terrorism
