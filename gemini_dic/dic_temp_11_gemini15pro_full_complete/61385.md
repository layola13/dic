emblazonments: /ɪmˈbleɪzənmənts/ | n. | 纹章装饰, 辉煌的装饰; 详细的描述 |  Noun form of emblazon, referring to the act of adorning something with heraldic devices or brightly colored decorations, or to a vivid or detailed description of something. | The knight's shield bore elaborate emblazonments. 骑士的盾牌上有精致的纹章。| The poet filled his verses with emblazonments of the natural world. 诗人用对自然世界的详细描述充满了他的诗句。 | synonyms: decorations, adornments, embellishments, ornamentations, depictions, portrayals | antonyms: plainness, simplicity, understatement | noun
