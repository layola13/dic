earthwork: /ˈɜːθwɜːk/ (UK) /ˈɜːrθwɜːrk/ (US)
n. (plural earthworks)
土方工程；土方；土木工程
A construction made of earth, especially one made for defense.
Ancient earthworks mark the site of a former settlement. 古代的土方工程标志着一个以前定居点的位置。
The earthworks protected the castle from attack. 土方工事保护城堡免受攻击。
synonyms:  rampart, fortification, embankment, mound, ditch
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider "natural landscape" or "depression")
noun
