clinodiagonal:/ˌklaɪnoʊdaɪˈæɡənəl/| adj.|斜对角线的；倾斜对角线的|A line or plane that is obliquely across a crystal.  A clinodiagonal is an oblique diagonal axis in the monoclinic system.|The clinodiagonal axis is a unique axis in monoclinic crystals.|近义词:oblique diagonal|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From *clino- + diagonal, from Greek *klino* (to lean, slope) + *dia* (through) + *gonia* (angle).
