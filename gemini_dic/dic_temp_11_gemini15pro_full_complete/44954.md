cryptosporidium: /ˌkrɪptoʊspɔːˈrɪdiəm/ | n. |隐孢子虫|A parasitic protozoan of the genus Cryptosporidium that causes diarrhea.  It can be transmitted through contaminated water or food. | Cryptosporidium is a common cause of waterborne illness. 隐孢子虫是水传播疾病的常见原因。| Cryptosporidium infections can be particularly severe in individuals with weakened immune systems.  隐孢子虫感染在免疫系统较弱的人群中可能特别严重。 |Synonyms:  None (scientific name)| Antonyms: None | Noun
