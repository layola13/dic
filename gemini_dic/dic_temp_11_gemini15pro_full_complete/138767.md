numero:/ˈnuːmərəʊ/ (UK), /ˈnuːməroʊ/ (US)| n.|数字，号码|A numero is a number.  A numero is often used in a more formal or technical context, or when referring to a specific identifying number. |My library card numero is 2749. 我的借书证号码是2749。|Synonyms: number, figure, digit|Antonyms: N/A (Numbers don't have direct opposites)|Noun
