gimcrackeries: /ˌɡɪmˈkrækəriz/ | n. | 小玩意儿；廉价的装饰品；花哨的小玩意 | Showy but useless ornaments; cheap trinkets.  | The shelves were cluttered with gimcrackeries.  架子上堆满了廉价的小玩意儿。 | Synonyms: trinkets, baubles, knick-knacks, gewgaws, fripperies | Antonyms: necessities, essentials | noun

Etymology: From gimcrack, likely a playful alteration of whimwham.
