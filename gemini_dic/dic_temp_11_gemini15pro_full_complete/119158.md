mews:/mjuːz/
verb
（猫）咪咪叫；
(of a cat) make a characteristic high-pitched crying sound.
例句：The kitten mewed pitifully, lost and hungry. 小猫可怜地喵喵叫着，又饿又迷路。

例句：The cat mewed at the door, wanting to be let in.  猫在门口喵喵叫，想进来。

近义词：meow, purr (when content)
反义词: hiss, growl
词性:v.
