factitively:/ˈfæktɪtɪvli/
adverb|事实地；实际地；构成事实地|In a way that establishes something as a fact; in a factitive manner.  Expressing or implying causation of a fact or state.|She factitively claimed he was the thief, leading to his arrest. 她断定他是小偷，导致他被捕。|Near Synonyms: causatively, effectively|Antonyms: hypothetically, conjecturally|词性: 副词
