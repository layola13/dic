chalkboards:/ˈtʃɔːkbɔːrdz/
n.
黑板
A large, smooth, dark surface, typically framed, used in schools and other institutions for writing or drawing on with chalk.
The teacher wrote the equations on the chalkboard.  老师把方程式写在黑板上。

近义词:blackboard, whiteboard
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "screen" or "projector" in a modern context)
词性:noun
