nonforgiving:/ˌnɑːnfərˈɡɪvɪŋ/
adjective
无情的，不宽恕的
Unwilling or unable to forgive.  Not allowing or making allowance for error or weakness.
例句：He has a nonforgiving nature.  他生性无情。

例句：She gave him a nonforgiving look. 她给了他一个不饶恕的眼神。
近义词：unforgiving, relentless, implacable, ruthless
反义词：forgiving, merciful, lenient
词性: adjective
