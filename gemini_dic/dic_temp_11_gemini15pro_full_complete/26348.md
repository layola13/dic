buzzwords:/ˈbʌzwɜːdz/ (US), /ˈbʌzwɜːdz/ (UK) | n. | 流行语；时髦词；行话 |  Words or phrases that become very popular for a short time, often related to a particular industry or field. They are often used to impress or sound knowledgeable, even if the speaker doesn't fully understand their meaning. |  The presentation was full of buzzwords like "synergy" and "paradigm shift." 演示文稿充满了“协同效应”和“范式转变”之类的流行语。| synonyms: jargon, slang, trendy phrases, catchphrases, vogue words | antonyms: standard vocabulary, plain language, simple terms | noun
