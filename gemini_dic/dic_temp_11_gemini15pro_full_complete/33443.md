churn-out:/ˈtʃɜːrn aʊt/| v.|大量生产；粗制滥造|To produce something quickly and in large quantities, often of low quality.|The factory churns out thousands of plastic toys every day.  这家工厂每天生产数千个塑料玩具。|Example: The studio churned out dozens of low-budget horror movies every year.  这家工作室每年都会粗制滥造几十部低成本恐怖电影。|Synonyms: mass-produce, crank out, generate|Antonyms: craft, hand-make|verb
churn-out:/ˈtʃɜːrn aʊt/| n.|大量生产；粗制滥造|A large quantity of something produced quickly, often of low quality.|There's a constant churn-out of cheap novels from that publisher.  那个出版社不断地大量生产廉价小说。|Example: The constant churn-out of reality TV shows is overwhelming.  真人秀节目的不断涌现令人应接不暇。|Synonyms: mass production, output|Antonyms: limited edition, handcrafted item|noun
