boatyards:/ˈboʊtjɑːrdz/
n.
船坞；修船厂
An area of water where boats or ships are built or repaired.
例句：The boatyards were busy with activity as workers prepared the yachts for the summer season.  船厂里一片繁忙景象，工人们正准备着夏季的游艇。

例句：Many skilled craftsmen work in the boatyards along the coast. 许多熟练的工匠在沿海的船厂工作。

近义词：shipyard, marina, dockyard
反义词: None

词性: noun
