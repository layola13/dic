interclouded:/ˌɪntərˈklaʊdɪd/
adjective
间有云的；部分多云的
partially covered with clouds; partly cloudy
The sky was interclouded, with patches of blue showing through. 天空间有云，可以看到几片蓝色。
The afternoon was interclouded and cool. 下午天气部分多云，凉爽宜人。
synonyms: partly cloudy, broken, variably cloudy
antonyms: clear, cloudless, sunny
词性: 形容词
