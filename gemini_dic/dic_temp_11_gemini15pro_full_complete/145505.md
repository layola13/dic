overcloys:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪz/ | v. | 过分腻味；使生腻；使过饱 | To make someone feel sick or disgusted by providing too much of something rich or sweet; to satiate to the point of disgust or aversion. | The richness of the dessert overcloyed after a few bites.  这道甜点太腻了，吃几口就觉得恶心。| Example:  He overcloyed himself with rich food. 他吃了太多油腻的食物，感到恶心。| Synonyms: satiate, surfeit, glut, cloy | Antonyms: stimulate, whet, tantalize | Verb

overcloy:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪz/ | n. | 过分腻味；过饱 | The state of being overcloyed; excessive sweetness or richness. | The overcloy of the perfume made her head ache.  这香水的浓烈气味让她头疼。| Example:  The film suffered from an overcloy of sentimentality. 这部电影过于煽情，让人感到腻味。| Synonyms: satiety, surfeit, glut | Antonyms: lack, deficiency, scarcity | Noun
