butur: /ˈbuːtər/ | n. | 黄油;奶油 | A pale yellow edible fatty substance made by churning cream and used as a spread or in cooking. |  例句：He spread butter on his toast. 他在吐司上涂了黄油。

例句：She used butter to make the cake. 她用黄油做蛋糕。

近义词：margarine, shortening | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "oil" in some cooking contexts) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *butere (unattested), from Late Latin butyrum, from Ancient Greek βούτυρον (boútyron), of uncertain origin, perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *gʷow- (“cow”) + *tyros (“cheese”).
