checklisted:/ˈtʃɛklɪstɪd/
adjective
已列入清单的，已核对的
Having been checked off on a checklist; verified as complete or satisfactory.
The pilot checklist was completed before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前完成了检查清单。
Synonyms: verified, confirmed, completed
Antonyms: incomplete, unchecked, pending
词性:形容词

checklisted:/ˈtʃɛklɪstɪd/
verb (past participle)
列入清单，核对
To mark (an item) on a checklist as having been verified or completed.
All the necessary items were checklisted before the launch. 发射前所有必要的项目都已核对。
Synonyms: ticked off, marked off, signed off
Antonyms: omitted, missed, overlooked
词性:动词(过去分词)
