nozzle:/ˈnɒzl/ (UK) /ˈnɑːzl/ (US)|n.|喷嘴; 管嘴|A projecting spout, tube, or similar device, by which a flow of liquid or gas may be directed or controlled.|The fireman attached the nozzle to the hose and aimed it at the fire. 消防员把喷嘴连接到水龙带上，瞄准了火灾。|Synonyms: spout, jet, tip, aperture|Antonyms: inlet, intake|noun
