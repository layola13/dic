knickknack: /ˈnɪknæk/ | n. | 小玩意儿；小摆设；廉价的装饰品 | A small, inexpensive ornament or trinket. |  例句：She collects knickknacks from her travels. 她收集旅行带回来的小玩意儿。

例句：The shelves were filled with knickknacks and souvenirs. 架子上摆满了小摆设和纪念品。

近义词：trinket, ornament, curio, bauble | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "essential" or "necessity") | 词性：noun
