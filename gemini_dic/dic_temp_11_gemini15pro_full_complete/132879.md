nonelasticity:/ˌnɒnɪˌlæsˈtɪsɪti/
|n.|无弹性，非弹性
|The nonelasticity of demand for certain goods means price increases do not significantly affect consumption.|某些商品的需求缺乏弹性意味着价格上涨不会显著影响消费。
|The nonelasticity of the rope made it unsuitable for bungee jumping.|这条绳子没有弹性，不适合蹦极跳。
|inelasticity, rigidity|
|elasticity, flexibility|
|noun|
