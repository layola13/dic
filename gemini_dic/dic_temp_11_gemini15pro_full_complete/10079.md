apocryphalist:/əˈpɒkrɪfəlɪst/
n.
伪经作者；伪经信奉者；虚构故事作者
A person who writes apocryphal books or accepts them as genuine; a teller of fictitious stories.
The apocryphalist filled his narratives with fantastical creatures and impossible feats.  这个伪经作者在他的叙述中充满了奇幻的生物和不可能的壮举。
The historian dismissed the claims of the apocryphalist as unsubstantiated legends.  历史学家认为伪经作者的主张是没有根据的传说，予以驳回。
近义词:fabricator, liar, storyteller, fabulist
反义词:historian, chronicler
词性:noun

apocryphal:/əˈpɒkrɪf(ə)l/
adj.
伪经的；可疑的；杜撰的
Of doubtful authenticity; spurious.
Many apocryphal stories surround the life of this famous historical figure.  许多可疑的故事围绕着这位著名的历史人物的一生。
The document's apocryphal nature made it inadmissible as evidence.  这份文件的可疑性质使其无法作为证据被采纳。
近义词:dubious, spurious, questionable, fictitious, fabricated
反义词:authentic, genuine, verifiable
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *apocryphus, from Greek apókryphos “hidden, spurious,” from apokrýptein “to hide away,” from apo- “away” + krýptein “to hide.”
