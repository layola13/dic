anomuran:/ˈænəˌmʊrən/ | n. |异尾下目动物；歪尾下目动物|A group of crustaceans, including hermit crabs and squat lobsters, characterized by an abdomen that is usually asymmetrical and often soft or folded beneath the cephalothorax. |Hermit crabs are a well-known example of anomurans. 寄居蟹是异尾下目动物的一个众所周知的例子。|Near synonyms: decapod, crustacean |Antonyms:  (No clear antonym for a taxonomic group)|noun

anomuran:/ˌænəˈmʊrən/ | adj. |异尾下目的；歪尾下目的|Relating to or characteristic of anomurans. |The anomuran abdomen is often soft and tucked under the body. 异尾下目动物的腹部通常是柔软的，并且卷缩在身体下方。|Near synonyms: decapod, crustacean |Antonyms: (No clear antonym for a taxonomic group)|adjective
