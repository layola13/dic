outbidder:/ˌaʊtˈbɪdər/| n. |出价更高者，胜过他人出价者|Someone who offers a higher price than someone else at an auction or in a competitive bidding process. |例句：The outbidder offered $10,000 more than the original bid. 出价更高者比原始出价高出1万美元。

例句：The painting was eventually sold to the outbidder for a record price. 这幅画最终以创纪录的价格卖给了出价最高的人。

近义词：higher bidder, overbidder|反义词:underbidder|词性:noun
