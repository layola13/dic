cyclone-collector:/ˈsaɪkloʊn kəˈlɛktər/| n.|旋风收集器，旋风除尘器|A device that uses centrifugal force to separate particles from a gas stream.|The cyclone-collector efficiently removes dust from the workshop air. 旋风收集器有效地清除了车间空气中的灰尘。|Synonyms: cyclone separator, centrifugal separator|Antonyms: N/A|noun

cyclone:/ˈsaɪkloʊn/| n.|旋风，气旋，飓风|A system of winds rotating inwards to an area of low barometric pressure.|The cyclone caused widespread damage. 气旋造成了大范围的破坏。|Synonyms: hurricane, typhoon, tornado|Antonyms: anticyclone|noun
