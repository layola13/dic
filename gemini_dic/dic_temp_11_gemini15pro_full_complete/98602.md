instructing:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪŋ/
verb
指示，指导
the act of teaching or giving directions
The teacher was instructing the students on how to write a research paper. 老师正在指导学生如何写研究论文。
teaching, coaching, tutoring, training, educating
misinforming, misleading
v.
