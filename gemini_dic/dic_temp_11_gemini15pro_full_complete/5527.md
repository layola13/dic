alphabetism: /ˈælfəbɪˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 字母主义，字母崇拜 | The excessive reverence or reliance on the letters of the alphabet, often in mystical or occult contexts.  A belief in the inherent power or meaning of letters. | He practiced a form of alphabetism, believing that chanting certain letters could unlock hidden powers.  | 近义词: letter worship, graphemysticism | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps: illiteracy, ignorance) | noun
