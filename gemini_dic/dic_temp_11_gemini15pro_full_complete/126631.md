nails:/neɪlz/ | n. |指甲；钉子|Hardened keratin plates covering the upper surface of the fingertips, or a thin, pointed piece of metal used to fasten things together.|She painted her nails red. 她把指甲涂成了红色。|He hammered the nail into the wall. 他把钉子钉进墙里。|近义词：(for fingernail) claw, talon; (for metal nail) pin, tack, spike |反义词: (无明确反义词) | noun

nails:/neɪlz/ | v. |钉住；抓住；揭露|To fasten something with nails; to catch or arrest someone; to expose or reveal something. |He nailed the sign to the post. 他把牌子钉在柱子上。|The police nailed the thief. 警察抓住了小偷。|The reporter nailed the corrupt politician. 记者揭露了腐败的政客。|近义词: (for fasten) fix, attach, secure; (for catch) apprehend, capture; (for expose) reveal, uncover, expose |反义词: (for fasten) detach, remove; (for catch) release | verb
