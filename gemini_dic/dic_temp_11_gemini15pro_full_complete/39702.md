consign:/kənˈsaɪn/ | vt. |交付；托运；寄售；打发；放逐 |to deliver something to a person's custody, typically in order for it to be sold |例句：The goods were consigned to the warehouse. 货物被运到仓库。

例句：She consigned her old clothes to a charity shop. 她把旧衣服送到慈善商店寄售。|近义词：deliver, commit, entrust, relegate, banish |反义词：retain, keep, withhold| verb


consign:/kənˈsaɪn/ | vt. |交付；托运；寄售；打发；放逐 |to put someone or something in an unpleasant place or situation |例句：He was consigned to a life of poverty. 他被注定过贫困的生活。

例句：The defeated army was consigned to oblivion.  战败的军队被遗忘。|近义词：commit, condemn, relegate |反义词：retrieve, rescue, save| verb
