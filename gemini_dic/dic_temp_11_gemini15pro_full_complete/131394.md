noncivilizable:/ˌnɒnˌsɪvɪˈlaɪzəbl/
adjective
不可开化的; 难以教化的
Not capable of being civilized; uncivilizable.  Lacking the capacity for becoming or being made civilized.
Some anthropologists argued that certain tribes were noncivilizable due to their isolated existence.  一些人类学家认为，某些部落由于与世隔绝而无法开化。
The report described the behavior of the rioters as noncivilizable and barbaric.  该报告将暴徒的行为描述为野蛮和不可开化的。
近义词:uncivilized, barbaric, savage, primitive
反义词:civilized, cultured, refined
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + civilizable (from civilize, from French civiliser, from Latin civilis "relating to a citizen, civil," from civis "citizen")
