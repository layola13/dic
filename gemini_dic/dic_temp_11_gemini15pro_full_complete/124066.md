morphologize: /mɔːrˈfɒlədʒaɪz/ /mɔːrˈfɑːlədʒaɪz/ | v. |  形态分析，词形变化 | To analyze or describe the forms of words, especially the formation of compound words or the internal changes that take place in the forms of words. | Linguists morphologize words to understand their structure. 语言学家对单词进行形态分析以了解它们的结构。|  We can morphologize the word "unbreakable" into "un-", "break", and "-able".  我们可以将“unbreakable”这个词形态分析为“un-”、“break”和“-able”。 | analyze, parse, decompose | synthesize, combine | Verb
