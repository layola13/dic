geosynclinal:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsɪŋklɪnəl/
geosynclinal:| adj. n.|地槽的；地槽|A geosyncline is a large, long-lived, subsiding trough in the Earth's crust within which large amounts of sediment accumulate.  地槽是地壳中一个巨大、长期存在的下沉槽，其中积累了大量的沉积物。|The Appalachian Mountains were formed from a geosynclinal. 阿巴拉契亚山脉是由地槽形成的。|近义词：basin, trough|反义词: arch, dome|词性: adjective, noun
geosyncline:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsɪŋklʌɪn/
geosyncline:| n.|地槽|A geosyncline is a large, long-lived, subsiding trough in the Earth's crust within which large amounts of sediment accumulate.  地槽是地壳中一个巨大、长期存在的下沉槽，其中积累了大量的沉积物。|The sediments in the geosyncline were eventually folded and uplifted to form mountains.  地槽中的沉积物最终褶皱隆起形成山脉。|近义词：basin, trough|反义词: arch, dome|词性: noun
