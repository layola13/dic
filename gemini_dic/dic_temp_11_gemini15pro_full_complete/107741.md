laparoscopist:/ˌlæpəˈrɒskəpɪst/
n.
腹腔镜医师
A surgeon who performs laparoscopy, a minimally invasive surgical procedure.
The laparoscopist skillfully inserted the instruments through small incisions. 腹腔镜医师熟练地将器械插入小切口。
Dr. Smith, a renowned laparoscopist, performed the surgery.  著名的腹腔镜医师史密斯医生进行了手术。
surgeon,  endoscopic surgeon
--- (No commonly used antonym)
noun


Etymology:
From laparoscopy + -ist.  Laparoscopy comes from  laparo- (from Greek *lapar-, meaning "flank, loin") + -scopy (from Greek skopein, meaning "to look at").
