outscore:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːr/
verb
以较多得分胜过；比分超过
To defeat (an opponent) by scoring more points.
The Lakers outscored the Celtics in the final quarter. 湖人队在最后一节得分超过了凯尔特人队。
outscore:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːr/
verb
胜过；超过
To surpass or exceed in scoring.
She outscored everyone on the math test. 她在数学考试中得分超过了所有人。
outscore:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːr/
近义词: outpoint, beat, defeat, surpass, exceed
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
