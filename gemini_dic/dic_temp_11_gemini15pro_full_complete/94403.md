implicated:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
牵涉的，涉及的
involved in or connected to something, especially something bad
例句：Several officials were implicated in the scandal.  几位官员被牵涉进这桩丑闻。

例句：He was implicated in the robbery. 他与这起抢劫案有牵连。

近义词：involved, incriminated, entangled
反义词：exonerated, cleared

词性: adjective

词源: From Latin implicare ("to enfold, entangle, involve"), from in- ("in, on, upon") + plicare ("to fold").
