morsel:/ˈmɔːrsəl/ (US) /ˈmɔːsəl/ (UK)| n. |小块; 少量; 一口; 片段|A small piece or amount of food; a small piece or amount of something, especially something abstract.|

例句：
He ate a morsel of cheese.  他吃了一小块奶酪。

She offered me a morsel of information. 她给了我一点儿信息。


近义词：bit, piece, fragment, scrap, snippet, crumb

反义词:chunk, lot, abundance

词性:noun
