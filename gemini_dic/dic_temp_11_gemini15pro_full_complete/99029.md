interagrees:/ˌɪntərəˈɡriːz/ | v. | 互相同意，达成一致 | To reach a mutual agreement or understanding; to concur. |  The two parties interagreed on the terms of the contract. 双方就合同条款达成了一致。

The countries interagreed to cooperate on environmental issues. 这些国家同意在环境问题上合作。 | 近义词: concur, agree, coincide | 反义词: disagree, differ, clash | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Inter-" (between) + "agree" (to have the same opinion).  The prefix "inter-" suggests a reciprocal action between multiple parties.
