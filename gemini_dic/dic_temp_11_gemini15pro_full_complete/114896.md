man-hour:/ˈmænˌaʊər/| n. |工时；人时|A unit of work representing one worker working for one hour.  The amount of work that one person can do in one hour. |It took 100 man-hours to complete the project. 完成这个项目花了100个工时。|Synonyms: labor-hour, work-hour, person-hour|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider concepts like idleness, inactivity)|noun
