nonanatomic:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈtɒmɪk/
adjective
非解剖的；非解剖学的
Not related to anatomy; not based on anatomical structure or function.
The device uses a nonanatomic approach to stabilize the fracture.  这个装置采用非解剖学方法来稳定骨折。
The procedure involves a nonanatomic reconstruction of the ligament.  该手术涉及韧带的非解剖重建。
synonyms: non-anatomical, nonstructural
antonyms: anatomic, anatomical
adjective
