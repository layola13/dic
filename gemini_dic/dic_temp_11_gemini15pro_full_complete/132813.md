noneclectically:/ˌnɒnɪˈklɛktɪkli/
adverb|非折衷地，非选择性地|Not in an eclectic way; without eclecticism.  This means not selecting or choosing from various sources or styles.|She furnished her apartment noneclectically, sticking to a minimalist Scandinavian theme.|近义词: uniformly, homogeneously, consistently|反义词: eclectically, diversely|词性: 副词
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "eclectic" (from Greek "eklektikos" meaning "selective") + "-ally" (adverb suffix)
