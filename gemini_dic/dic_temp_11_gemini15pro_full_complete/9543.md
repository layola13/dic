antisentimentally:/ˌæntiˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəli/
| adv. | 反感伤地，不感情用事地 | In a manner rejecting or opposing sentimentality; without showing or expressing exaggerated or affected emotion. | He spoke antisentimentally about the loss, focusing on the practicalities. 他反感伤地谈到损失，专注于实际情况。|  She looked at the old photos antisentimentally, acknowledging the past without dwelling on it. 她不感情用事地看着老照片，承认过去，但并不沉溺其中。| 近义词: pragmatically, realistically, unsentimentally | 反义词: sentimentally, emotionally, mawkishly | 词性: 副词
