lei:/ˈleɪ/| n.|花环，花圈|A garland or wreath of flowers or leaves, given as a symbol of affection or greeting, especially in Hawaii.|例句：She wore a beautiful lei around her neck. 她脖子上戴着一个漂亮的花环。

例句：Visitors to Hawaii are often greeted with a lei. 到夏威夷的游客通常会收到花环。|近义词：garland, wreath|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
