evangelizer:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzər/ | n. | 福音传道者；推广者；热心支持者 | Someone who spreads the gospel or promotes something enthusiastically. |  Billy Graham was a famous evangelizer. 比利·格雷厄姆是一位著名的福音传道者。

Steve Jobs was an evangelizer for Apple products. 史蒂夫·乔布斯是苹果产品的热心推广者。| 近义词: advocate, promoter, missionary | 反义词: opponent, critic | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *evangelizator, from evangelizare "to preach the gospel," from Greek euangelizesthai.
