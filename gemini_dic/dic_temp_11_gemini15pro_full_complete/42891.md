courtyards: /ˈkɔːrtjɑːrdz/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrtjɑːrdz/ (US)| n. |庭院，院子|An enclosed area, often rectangular, open to the sky and surrounded by buildings or walls.  A courtyard can provide a private outdoor space within a larger building or complex.|The hotel has a beautiful central courtyard filled with flowers.|Synonyms: patio, quadrangle, piazza, plaza|Antonyms: field, meadow|Noun
