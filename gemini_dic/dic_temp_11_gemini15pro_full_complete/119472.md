micrometry: /maɪˈkrɒmɪtri/ /maɪˈkrɑːmɪtri/ | n. | 测微术，显微测量法 | The process of making very precise measurements of small distances or sizes using a microscope and calibrated instruments. |  The micrometry revealed the specimen was only 10 microns wide.  测微术显示标本只有10微米宽。|  microscopy, micromensuration |  macrometry | noun
