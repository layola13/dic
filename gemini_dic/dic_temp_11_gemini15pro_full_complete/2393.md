adipose:/ˈædɪˌpoʊs/| adj.|脂肪的，肥胖的|Relating to animal fat; fatty.  Containing or composed of fat.|例句：Adipose tissue is a specialized connective tissue that functions as the major storage site for fat in the form of triglycerides. 脂肪组织是一种特化的结缔组织，是甘油三酯形式脂肪的主要储存场所。

例句：People with more adipose tissue around their middles are at greater risk for heart disease.腹部脂肪较多的人患心脏病的风险更大。|近义词：fatty, obese, corpulent|反义词：lean, thin|词性:adjective

adiposity:/ˌædɪˈpɒsɪti/| n.|肥胖，脂肪过多|The state of being fat or obese; excessive fatness.|例句：Childhood adiposity is a growing problem worldwide. 儿童肥胖症是一个日益严重的全球性问题。

例句：Increased adiposity is associated with a higher risk of developing type 2 diabetes. 肥胖增加与患2型糖尿病的风险较高有关。|近义词：obesity, corpulence, fatness|反义词：leanness, thinness|词性:noun
