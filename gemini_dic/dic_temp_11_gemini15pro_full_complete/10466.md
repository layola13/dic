applicable:/ˈæplɪkəbl/ (US) /əˈplɪkəbl/ (UK)| adj.|适用的，可应用的|Relevant or appropriate; capable of being applied.|This rule is applicable to all students. 这条规则适用于所有学生.|
applicable:| adj.|适用的，可应用的|Able to be applied or used in a particular situation.|The offer is applicable to all new customers.  这个优惠适用于所有新客户.|
applicable:| adj.|适用的，可应用的|Suitable or relevant.|The same principles are applicable to both cases. 相同的原则适用于这两种情况.|
applicable:|adj.|适用的|Having relevance or being appropriate.|These regulations are applicable to everyone. 这些规定适用于每个人。|
applicable:|adj.|可应用的|Capable of being applied.|Is this offer applicable online?  这个优惠可以在线使用吗？|

近义词:relevant, appropriate, suitable, pertinent, germane
反义词:inapplicable, irrelevant, inappropriate

词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin applicabilis, from applicare ‘fold, apply’.
