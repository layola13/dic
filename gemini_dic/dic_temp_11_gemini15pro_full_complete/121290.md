misfile:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪl/| vt. n.|错误归档，归错档案|To file something incorrectly, putting it in the wrong place or category. |Example 1: I misfiled the report under the wrong client's name. 我把报告错误地归档到另一个客户名下了。

Example 2: The clerk misfiled the documents, causing a significant delay.  办事员把文件归档错了，导致了严重的延误。|近义词：misplace, miscategorize|反义词: file correctly, categorize correctly|词性:动词, 名词

misfile:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪl/| n.|错误归档的文件|A file that has been filed incorrectly. |Example 1: The misfile was eventually found after a thorough search.  经过彻底的搜索，终于找到了归档错误的文件。

Example 2: Several misfiles were discovered during the audit. 在审计过程中发现了几份归档错误的文件。|近义词：misplaced file, incorrectly filed document|反义词: correctly filed document|词性:名词
