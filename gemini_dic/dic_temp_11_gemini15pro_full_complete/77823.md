generationism:/ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 世代主义 | The belief that characteristics of a person or group are determined by the period in which they were born. |  Generationism can lead to stereotypes and misunderstandings between age groups.  世代主义会导致刻板印象和不同年龄群体之间的误解。 |  cohort theory, generationalism | ageism | noun
Etymology:  From "generation" + "-ism"
