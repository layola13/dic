amphicoelous: /æmˌfɪˈsiːləs/ | adj. | 双凹的 | Having vertebrae that are concave at both ends (referring to the centrum, the main body of the vertebra). |  Amphicoelous vertebrae are characteristic of some fish and amphibians.  双凹椎骨是某些鱼类和两栖类的特征。| Example: The fossil showed evidence of amphicoelous vertebrae, suggesting it was an ancient fish. 这块化石显示出双凹椎骨的证据，表明它是一种古老的鱼类。| Synonym: biconcave | Antonym: procoelous, opisthocoelous | adjective
