nontenantable:/ˌnɒnˈtɛnəntəbəl/ | adj. | 不可租赁的，不宜居住的 | Not fit to be rented or lived in, usually due to disrepair or unsafe conditions. | The apartment was declared nontenantable after the fire. 这套公寓在火灾后被宣布为不可租赁的。|  The building was deemed nontenantable due to severe structural damage. 由于严重的结构损坏，该建筑被认为不适合居住。 | synonyms: uninhabitable, unlivable, unusable | antonyms: habitable, livable, rentable | adjective
