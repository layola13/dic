overmerits: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪts/
verb
过分赞扬；过誉
to praise or reward someone more than they deserve
He felt that the book had been overmerited by the critics.  他觉得评论家对这本书的评价过高了。

overmerits: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪts/
noun (plural)
过分的优点；过度的功绩 (rare usage, often considered non-standard)
Excessive merits or attributes attributed to someone or something.
The speaker dwelt on the overmerits of the candidate, making the audience skeptical. 演讲者对候选人的优点夸大其词，让听众产生了怀疑。


近义词: overpraise, overestimate, exaggerate, extol
反义词: underestimate, undervalue, depreciate
词性: verb, noun (rare)
