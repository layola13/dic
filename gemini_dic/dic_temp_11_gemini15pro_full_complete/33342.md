chuko:/ˈtʃuːkoʊ/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A foolish or naive person.  Often used playfully or affectionately. |"Don't be such a chuko, you'll figure it out."  别犯傻了，你会想出来的。|"He's a bit of a chuko, but he means well." 他有点傻，但他是好意的。|近义词：fool, idiot, dope, ditz|反义词: genius, smart, intelligent|词性:noun
