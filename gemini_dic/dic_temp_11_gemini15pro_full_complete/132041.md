noncorrupters:/ˌnɒnkəˈrʌptərz/
n.
不腐败者，清廉者
People who do not engage in corrupt practices, maintaining integrity and honesty.
The noncorrupters in the government are working hard to expose the fraud. 政府中不腐败的人正在努力揭露欺诈行为。
synonyms:  moralists,  uprights,  principled individuals
antonyms:  corrupters,  grafters,  dishonest individuals
noun
