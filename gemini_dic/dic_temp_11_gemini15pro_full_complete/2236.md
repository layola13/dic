adducent:/əˈdʌsənt/
adjective
内收的； приводящий
drawing towards a center or median line.
The adducent muscles of the thigh bring the legs together. 大腿内收肌使双腿并拢。
近义词: adductor, convergent
反义词: abducent, divergent
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *addūcēns*, present participle of addūcere (“to lead to, bring to, draw together”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + dūcere (“to lead”).
