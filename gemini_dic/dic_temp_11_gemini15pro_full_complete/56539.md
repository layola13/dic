dominating:/ˈdɒmɪneɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|支配，统治 (rarely used as a noun)|The act of dominating.|例句：The dominating of the market by a single company.  一家公司对市场的支配。|近义词：control, rule, dominance|反义词：submission, subordination|词性:名词
