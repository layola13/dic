outstartle:/ˌaʊtˈstɑːrtl/
verb
惊吓过度；比…更令人吃惊
To startle more greatly than; to surprise or frighten to a greater degree than.
He outstartled her with a sudden shout. 他突然大喊一声，把她吓了一大跳。
Synonyms: surprise, amaze, astonish, shock
Antonyms: comfort, reassure, soothe
词性: 动词
