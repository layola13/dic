ferus:/ˈfɛrʊs/| adj.|野生的，未驯服的，凶猛的|wild, untamed, savage; existing in a natural state, especially as a plant or animal.  It also carries a connotation of fierceness or untamed spirit, often applied to animals but sometimes metaphorically to people or their emotions. |The ferus cat hissed at the approaching human.  这只野猫对靠近的人嘶嘶作响。|Synonyms: wild, feral, savage, untamed, undomesticated|Antonyms: tame, domesticated, civilized, cultivated|adjective
