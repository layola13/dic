elayl: /ˈɛθɪl/ | n. | 乙烯, 乙炔 | An old name for ethene, a hydrocarbon gas, C2H4, that is the first member of the alkene series. It is produced by the cracking of petroleum and is used in the manufacture of plastics. | Elayl is a key component in the production of polyethylene. 乙烯是聚乙烯生产中的关键成分。| Elayl was once used as an anesthetic. 乙烯曾经用作麻醉剂。 | Synonyms: ethene, ethylene | Antonyms: None | noun
