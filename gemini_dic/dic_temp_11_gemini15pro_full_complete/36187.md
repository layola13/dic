cofound:/koʊˈfaʊnd/ | vt. | 共同创立；共同创办 | To establish something (like a business) jointly with another or others. |  Example 1: Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak cofounded Apple Computer. 史蒂夫·乔布斯和史蒂夫·沃兹尼亚克共同创立了苹果电脑公司。

Example 2:  She cofounded the charity with her brother. 她和她兄弟共同创办了这个慈善机构。 | 近义词: establish together, create jointly, set up together | 反义词: dissolve, dismantle | 词性: 动词
