napping:/ˈnæpɪŋ/| n. |小睡，打盹|The act of sleeping for a short period, typically during the day.|例句：I enjoy napping in the hammock on a warm afternoon. 我喜欢在温暖的午后在吊床上小睡一会儿。

例句：The baby is napping in her crib. 宝宝正在婴儿床里小睡。

|近义词：dozing, resting, sleeping|反义词:waking, working|词性:noun
