oped:/ˈoʊpɛd/ | n. | 评论版; 评论文章 | An op-ed (originally opposite the editorial page) is an opinion piece typically published in a newspaper or magazine. |  The newspaper published an op-ed by a leading economist on the current financial crisis.  该报发表了一位著名经济学家就当前金融危机的评论文章。 | synonym: opinion piece, commentary | antonym: news report | noun

oped:/ˈoʊpɛd/ | v. | 撰写评论文章 | To write and publish an op-ed. |  She oped in the New York Times about the importance of education reform.  她在《纽约时报》上发表了关于教育改革重要性的评论文章。 | synonym: comment, editorialize | antonym: report | verb
