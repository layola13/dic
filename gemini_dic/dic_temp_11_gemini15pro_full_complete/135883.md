nonprovincially:/ˌnɒnprəˈvɪnʃəli/
adverb
非狭隘地；不局限于一省地；不偏狭地
Not limited to or characteristic of a province; having a broad or worldly outlook.
She approached the issue nonprovincially, considering its global implications. 她以非狭隘的眼光处理这个问题，考虑到了它的全球影响。
