catamarans: /ˌkætəməˈrænz/ | n. |双体船，帆船|A boat with two parallel hulls.  A catamaran is a multi-hulled watercraft featuring two parallel hulls of equal size. It is a geometrically stabilized craft, deriving its stability from its wide beam, relative to its length.|We sailed around the bay on a catamaran. 我们乘双体船绕着海湾航行。|Synonyms: multi-hull, twin-hull |Antonyms: monohull, single-hull|noun
