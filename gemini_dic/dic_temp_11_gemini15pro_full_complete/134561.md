nonlitigious:/ˌnɒnlɪˈtɪdʒəs/
adjective
非诉讼的
Not involving or characterized by litigation.
例句：They opted for a nonlitigious approach to resolve the dispute. 他们选择了一种非诉讼的方式来解决纠纷。

例句：Mediation is a nonlitigious method of dispute resolution. 调解是一种非诉讼的纠纷解决方法。

近义词：amicable, peaceful, conciliatory
反义词：litigious, contentious, adversarial
词性：adjective

词源：
From non- + litigious.  "Litigious" comes from the Latin word "litigiosus," meaning "quarrelsome" or "disputatious," which in turn derives from "litigium," meaning "lawsuit" or "dispute."
