notum:/ˈnəʊtəm/| n. |背板；背甲|The notum is the dorsal part of an insect's thorax. 背板是昆虫胸部的背面。|The beetle's brightly colored notum served as a warning to predators.  这只甲虫颜色鲜艳的背板是对捕食者的警告。|Synonyms: tergum, dorsal plate|Antonyms: sternum, ventral plate|noun
