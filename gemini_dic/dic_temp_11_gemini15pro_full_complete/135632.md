nonpracticing:/ˌnɒnˈpræktɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 非执业的，没有从事…的 | Not actively engaged in a profession, especially law or medicine, despite having been qualified to do so. |  He's a nonpracticing lawyer, now working in finance. 他是一位非执业律师，现在从事金融工作。|  She's a nonpracticing doctor who now writes medical thrillers. 她是一位非执业医生，现在写医学惊悚小说。| Synonyms: inactive, retired, dormant | Antonyms: practicing, active, working | adjective
