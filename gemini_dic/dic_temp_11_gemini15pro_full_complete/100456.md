intertwine:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪn/| v.|交织，缠绕，紧密联系|To twist or twine together; interweave.  To be or become mutually involved.|
The destinies of these two families are intertwined. 这两个家族的命运交织在一起。
Her fate is intertwined with his. 她的命运与他的命运紧密相连。
|近义词：interweave, interlace, entwine, tangle|反义词:separate, disentangle, unravel|词性:动词

intertwines:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪnz/| v.|交织，缠绕，紧密联系（第三人称单数形式）|Third-person singular simple present indicative form of intertwine.|
The ivy intertwines around the oak tree. 常春藤缠绕在橡树上。
The plot of the novel intertwines the lives of several characters. 小说的情节将几个角色的生活交织在一起。
|近义词：interweaves, interlaces, entwines, tangles|反义词:separates, disentangles, unravels|词性:动词
