conservatories:/kənˈsɜːrvətɔːriz/ |kənˈsɜːrvəˌtɔːriz/ | n. |温室；音乐学院；音乐学校|A conservatory is a building made mostly of glass, used for growing plants that need warmth and protection. conservatory:/kənˈsɜːrvətɔːri/ |kənˈsɜːrvəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 保存的；保护的| Relating to or promoting conservation, especially of the natural environment. |  Conservatory measures were put in place to protect the endangered species.  实施了保护措施来保护濒危物种。 | 近义词：preservation, protective | 反义词: destructive | 词性: adjective
