gup:/ɡʌp/ | n. | 小道消息，流言蜚语 | Informal term for gossip or inside information. |  Example: Have you heard any gup about the new CEO? 你听说过关于新任首席执行官的小道消息吗？| Example:  He's always full of gup about the office romances. 他总是充满关于办公室恋情的八卦。| 近义词: gossip, rumor, hearsay | 反义词: fact, truth | 词性: noun

词源:  Likely of Hindi origin, from the word "gap," meaning "chat" or "gossip."
