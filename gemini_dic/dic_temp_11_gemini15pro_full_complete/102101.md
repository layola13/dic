isostatic:/ˌaɪsəˈstætɪk/| adj.|地壳均衡的，等静压的|Relating to or characterized by isostasy, a state of gravitational equilibrium between Earth's lithosphere and asthenosphere such that the tectonic plates "float" at an elevation that depends on their thickness and density.|The Earth's crust is in isostatic equilibrium.  地球的地壳处于均衡状态。|The isostatic uplift of the land after the ice melted was significant. 冰川融化后，陆地发生了显著的均衡抬升。|近义词：balanced, equilibrated|反义词: unbalanced, nonequilibrated|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From iso- (Greek: equal) + static (Greek: standing).  Comes from the Greek words "isos" meaning "equal" and "stasis" meaning "standing still".
