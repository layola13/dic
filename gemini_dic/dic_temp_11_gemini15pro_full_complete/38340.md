conceive:/kənˈsiːv/| v.|构想，设想；怀孕|To form an idea or plan in the mind; to become pregnant.|She conceived a plan to start her own business. 她构想了一个创办自己企业的计划。|Synonyms: imagine, envision, devise, formulate, think up|Antonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret|verb

conceived:/kənˈsiːvd/ | v. | (conceive的过去式和过去分词) 构想，设想；怀孕 | Past tense and past participle of conceive. | The project was conceived during a brainstorming session. 这个项目是在一次头脑风暴会议中构思出来的。 | Synonyms: imagined, envisioned, devised, formulated| Antonyms: misunderstood, misinterpreted|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concipere, from Latin concipere "to take in and hold, conceive, become pregnant," from com- "together" (see com-) + capere "to take, seize" (see capable).
