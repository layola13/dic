hinter:/ˈhɪntər/| n.| 后面，偏僻的地方; (尤指瑞士或奥地利的)山区; 内陆; 腹地; (森林或山脉的)深处|A remote or less developed area, typically in the mountains (especially of Switzerland or Austria); the hinterland; the interior or remote parts (of a forest or mountain range).|The village is located in the hinterland, far from any major cities. 这个村庄位于腹地，远离任何主要城市。|Synonyms: backcountry, outback, wilderness, hinterland|Antonyms: foreland, front, forefront|noun

hinter:/ˈhɪntər/| adj.| 后面的，偏僻的|Situated in the hinterland; remote.|The hinter regions of the country are sparsely populated.  这个国家的偏远地区人口稀少。|Synonyms: remote, backwoods, outlying, secluded|Antonyms: urban, central, metropolitan|adjective
