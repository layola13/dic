ensignship: /ˈɛnsɪnʃɪp/ | n. |  旗手职位；旗手身份 | The position or status of an ensign. | He held the ensignship for two years. 他担任了两年的旗手。 | 近义词: flagbearership | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "ensign" (flag or standard-bearer) + "-ship" (suffix denoting status or office).  "Ensign" comes from Old French "enseigne"  ultimately from Latin "insignia" (plural of "insigne," meaning "distinguishing mark, emblem, sign").
