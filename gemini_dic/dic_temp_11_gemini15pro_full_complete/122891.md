mollycoddler:/ˈmɒlɪˌkɒdlə(r)/ (US) /ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdlər/| n. |娇惯的人；溺爱孩子的人；宝贝蛋|A person who pampers or treats someone with excessive indulgence, especially a child.|He's a real mollycoddler with his children, always giving them whatever they want.|近义词：pamperer, spoiler, cosseter|反义词:disciplinarian, martinet|词性:noun

mollycoddler:| n. |娇生惯养的人；饭来张口的人|Someone who is used to being pampered and indulged and is unable to cope with hardship.|She's become a bit of a mollycoddler since she moved back in with her parents.|近义词：softie, weakling|反义词:tough guy, independent person|词性:noun
