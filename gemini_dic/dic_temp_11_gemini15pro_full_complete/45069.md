cubicity:/kjuːˈbɪsɪti/
n.
立方性，三次方
The cubicity of a cube is the cube of its side length.
立方体的立方性是其边长的立方。
The concept of cubicity is important in understanding three-dimensional space.
立方性的概念对于理解三维空间很重要。
