invigorators:/ɪnˈvɪɡəˌreɪtərz/| n.|鼓舞人心的人或物；使人精神焕发的人或物|Something that invigorates; a stimulant.|The coach's pep talk was a real invigorator for the team. 教练的激励讲话对球队来说真是一个鼓舞。|The fresh mountain air was an invigorator after the stuffy city.  在闷热的城市待过之后，清新的山间空气令人精神焕发。|Synonyms: stimulants, tonics, energizers, refreshers|Antonyms: depressants, sedatives, enervators|noun
