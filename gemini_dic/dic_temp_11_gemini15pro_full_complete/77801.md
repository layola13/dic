generalizer: /ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzər/ | n. | 概括者，推广者 |  A person or thing that generalizes, forming general concepts or principles from particular instances.  |  He's a generalizer, preferring to talk about broad trends rather than specific details. 他是一个概括者，喜欢谈论广泛的趋势而不是具体的细节。|  The new software is a powerful generalizer, able to learn from a small dataset and apply its knowledge to new situations.  这款新软件是一个强大的概括器，能够从小数据集学习并将知识应用于新情况。 | synonyms: abstractor, synthesizer, theorist | antonyms: specialist, particularizer | noun
