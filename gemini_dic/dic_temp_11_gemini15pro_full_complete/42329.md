counter-surveillance:/ˌkaʊntər sərˈveɪləns/ | n. |反监视，反侦察|The act of detecting and preventing surveillance.  It involves identifying and neutralizing attempts by others to gather information or monitor activities.|The company invested heavily in counter-surveillance measures to protect its trade secrets.  这家公司大力投资反侦察措施以保护其商业机密。|
|synonyms: counterintelligence, counterspying|antonyms: surveillance, espionage|noun|

counter-surveillance:/ˌkaʊntər sərˈveɪləns/ | n. |反监视，反侦察|Methods and techniques used to thwart surveillance attempts.  This can include using encrypted communications, detecting hidden cameras, and employing other security measures.|Their counter-surveillance training included recognizing and avoiding surveillance techniques. 他们的反侦察训练包括识别和避免侦察技术。|synonyms: counterintelligence, counterspying|antonyms: surveillance, espionage|noun|
