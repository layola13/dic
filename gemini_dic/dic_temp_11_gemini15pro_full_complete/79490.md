ETYMOLOGY->
From globe + -ize + -ed.  "Globe" comes from the Latin "globus," meaning "sphere" or "round mass."  "-ize" is a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become," and "-ed" is a suffix that creates the past participle form of a verb, often used as an adjective.
