inadherent:/ˌɪnædˈhɪərənt/
adjective
不粘附的，非附着的
not sticking to something; not adhering.
The bandage was inadherent and fell off.  绷带没有粘住，掉了下来。
近义词:nonadherent, loose, detached, unattached
反义词:adherent, attached, clinging, sticking
词性:形容词

词源:
From in- "not" + adherent (from Latin adhaerere "to stick to").
