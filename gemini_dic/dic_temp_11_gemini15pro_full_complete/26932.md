calanus:/ˈkælənəs/| n. |桡足类动物（哲水蚤）|A small crustacean of the subclass Copepoda, abundant in plankton.|Calanus is a key component of the marine food web. 哲水蚤是海洋食物网的关键组成部分。|Synonyms: copepod|Antonyms: None|Noun

calanus:/ˈkælənəs/| n. |哲水蚤属|A genus of copepods, specifically referring to Calanus.|The species Calanus finmarchicus is particularly important in North Atlantic ecosystems. 哲水蚤属的finmarchicus种在北大西洋生态系统中尤为重要。|Synonyms: copepod (more general)|Antonyms: None|Noun
