discombobulated:/ˌdɪskəmˈbɒbjʊleɪtɪd/ /ˌdɪskəmˈbɑːbjəleɪtɪd/
adjective
困惑的，糊涂的，不知所措的
Confused and disoriented.
例句：I was so discombobulated after the long flight that I couldn't remember where I parked my car. 长途飞行后，我感到非常困惑，以至于不记得我把车停在哪里了。

例句：He was completely discombobulated by the unexpected news.  这突如其来的消息让他完全不知所措。

近义词：confused, bewildered, disoriented, flustered, disconcerted
反义词：composed, calm, collected, organized

词性: adjective
