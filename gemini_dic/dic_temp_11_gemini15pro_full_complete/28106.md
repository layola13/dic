capitalistically:/ˌkæpɪtəˈlɪstɪkli/
adverb|资本主义地，以资本主义的方式|In a way that is based on the principles of capitalism; in a way that prioritizes profit and private ownership.|例句：The company operates capitalistically, focusing on maximizing shareholder value.  这家公司以资本主义方式运营，专注于最大化股东价值。|例句：The resources were distributed capitalistically, favoring those with more capital. 资源以资本主义方式分配，有利于那些拥有更多资本的人。|近义词：commercially, profitably|反义词：socialistically, communistically|词性:副词
