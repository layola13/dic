bequeath:/bɪˈkwiːð/ /bɪˈkwiːθ/| vt.|遗赠；把…传下去|To leave (property) to someone by means of a will.  To hand down or pass on.|He bequeathed his art collection to the museum. 他把他的艺术收藏品遗赠给了博物馆.|近义词：leave, will, bestow, impart|反义词:inherit, receive|verb
