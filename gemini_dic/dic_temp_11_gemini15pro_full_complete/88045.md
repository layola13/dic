higher-up:/ˈhaɪərˌʌp/| adj.|高级的，上层的|Of or relating to the higher levels of an organization or hierarchy.|He has some higher-up connections in the government. 他在政府有一些高层关系。|近义词：senior, superior, high-ranking, upper-level|反义词:junior, lower-level, subordinate|adjective
