buttering:/ˈbʌtərɪŋ/
n.|涂黄油;奉承;拍马屁|The act of spreading butter on something. Also, figuratively, the act of flattering someone excessively.|He was buttering his toast. 他正在给吐司抹黄油。|近义词：flattering, praising, fawning|反义词：criticizing, condemning|词性:名词/动名词
buttering:/ˈbʌtərɪŋ/
v.|涂黄油;奉承;拍马屁|Present participle of "butter".  To spread butter on something. Also, figuratively, to flatter someone excessively.|He was buttering up the boss hoping for a promotion. 他为了升职而拍老板马屁。|近义词：flattering, praising, fawning|反义词：criticizing, condemning|词性:动词
