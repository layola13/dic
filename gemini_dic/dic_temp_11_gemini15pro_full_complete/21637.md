blurrinesses:/ˈblɜːrinɪsɪz/
noun
模糊不清; 模糊之处 (plural of blurriness)
The state or quality of being blurry.
The blurrinesses in the photo made it difficult to identify the suspect.  照片的模糊之处使得难以识别嫌疑人。
近义词: fuzziness, indistinctness, haziness
反义词: clarity, sharpness, distinctness
词性: noun
