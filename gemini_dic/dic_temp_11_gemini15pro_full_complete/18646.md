benzoin:/ˈbɛnzoʊɪn/ | n. |安息香，苯偶姻|A fragrant resin obtained from the bark of certain trees of the genus *Styrax*, used in perfumes and medicine.  It also refers to an aromatic ketone used in organic synthesis.|The perfume had a distinct note of benzoin. 这款香水有明显的安息香味道。|近义词:gum benjamin, styrax|反义词: None|noun
