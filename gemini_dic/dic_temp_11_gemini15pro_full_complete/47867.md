deceiver:/dɪˈsiːvər/| n.|欺骗者，骗子|A person who deceives others. |He was a deceiver and a thief. 他是一个骗子和小偷。|The con artist was a master deceiver, preying on vulnerable people.  这个骗子是一个老练的欺诈者，专门欺骗弱势群体。|近义词：liar, fraud, cheat, imposter|反义词：honest person, truth-teller|词性:noun
