overknowing:/ˌoʊvərˈnoʊɪŋ/| adj.|过于世故的，自以为是的，装懂的|Affecting to know more than one does; pretentious in displaying knowledge.  Pretending to have greater knowledge or understanding than is actually possessed;  excessively sophisticated or worldly-wise, especially in a way that is irritating or superficial.| He had an overknowing smirk that made me want to punch him. 他脸上挂着一种自以为是的假笑，让我真想揍他一顿。|近义词：pretentious, condescending, supercilious, smug, arrogant|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性:adjective

词源: "over" (excessively) + "knowing" (having knowledge).  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or surplus.
