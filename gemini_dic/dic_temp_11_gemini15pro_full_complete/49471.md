deluging:/ˈdɛl.juː.dʒɪŋ/ /dɪˈluː.dʒɪŋ/| v.|淹没，泛滥，使充满|The town was deluged by floodwaters. 这个小镇被洪水淹没了。|近义词：flooding, overwhelming, inundating|反义词:draining, drying|Verb (present participle)

deluge:/ˈdɛl.juːdʒ/ /dɪˈluː.dʒ/| n. v.|洪水，暴雨；泛滥，涌入|The deluge caused widespread damage. 洪水造成了广泛的破坏. /  A deluge of complaints followed the announcement. 公告发布后，投诉蜂拥而至。|近义词：flood, torrent, downpour|反义词:trickle, drought|Noun, Verb

词源: From Old French *deluge, from Latin diluvium "flood, inundation," from diluere "to wash away," from dis- "away" + luere "to wash."
