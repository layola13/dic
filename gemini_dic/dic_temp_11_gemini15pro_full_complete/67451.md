extirpators:/ɪkˈstɜːrpəteɪtərz/| n. |根除者，消灭者|People who completely destroy or remove something, especially something harmful or unwanted.  Those who root out or eradicate.|例句：The extirpators of the invasive species worked tirelessly to remove every last plant.  这些入侵物种的根除者不知疲倦地工作，以清除每一株植物。

例句：The government acted as extirpators of corruption, seeking to eliminate it from all levels of society. 政府扮演着腐败根除者的角色，力求将其从社会各阶层清除出去。|近义词：destroyers, eradicators, eliminators|反义词: preservers, protectors|词性:noun
