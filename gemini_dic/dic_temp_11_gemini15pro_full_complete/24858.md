brownout:/ˈbraʊnaʊt/| n.|电压骤降；灯泡变暗；部分停电|A reduction in voltage in an electrical power supply.  A brownout is a drop in voltage in an electrical power supply system. It is less severe than a blackout, which is a total loss of power. Brownouts can cause flickering or dimming of lights, and can also cause damage to electronic equipment.|The frequent brownouts were damaging our computers. 频繁的电压骤降正在损坏我们的电脑。|The brownout caused the lights to flicker. 电压骤降导致灯光闪烁。|近义词：voltage drop, power dip, dimming|反义词:power surge, blackout|词性:名词

Etymology:  brown + out.  "Brown" refers to the dimming of lights, and "out" signifies a reduction in power.
