distorts:/dɪˈstɔːrts/| v. |扭曲，歪曲|to change the shape, sound, or appearance of something so that it is strange or unclear|例句：The loudspeaker distorts at high volume.  高音量时扬声器会失真。

The heat distorted the image reflected in the water. 热量使水中反射的图像变形了。|近义词：twist, warp, deform, misrepresent, falsify|反义词：clarify, represent accurately, preserve|词性：动词
