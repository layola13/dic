handbrake:/ˈhændbreɪk/ (UK) /ˈhændbreɪk/ (US)| n.|手刹; 手闸|A lever used to keep a vehicle stationary, typically by locking the rear wheels.|Example:  Remember to put on the handbrake when parking on a hill. 记住在山上停车时要拉上手刹。

Example: The handbrake failed, and the car rolled down the hill. 手刹失灵了，车子从山上滚了下来。


|近义词：parking brake, emergency brake|反义词: accelerator, gas pedal |词性: noun
