boilermaking:/ˈbɔɪlərmeɪkɪŋ/| n.|锅炉制造；锅炉制造业|The art and trade of fabricating, assembling, and erecting boilers and other closed vessles.|Boilermaking is a demanding trade requiring skilled welders. 锅炉制造是一项需要熟练焊工的高要求行业。|synonyms: metal fabrication, welding, plate work|antonyms: N/A|noun
