monographing:/ˈmɒnəˌɡrɑːfɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːnəˌɡræfɪŋ/
|n.| 专题论文写作；专题研究 |The act of writing or researching a monograph.|  He devoted years to monographing the history of the Byzantine Empire. 他多年致力于研究拜占庭帝国的历史.| 近义词: researching, studying, writing, composing | 反义词: neglecting, ignoring |词性: 动名词 (gerund)

monographs:/ˈmɒnəˌɡrɑːfs/ /ˈmɑːnəˌɡræfs/
|n.| 专题著作；专题论文|A detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it.|  The library has a large collection of monographs on various scientific topics.  图书馆收藏了大量关于各种科学主题的专题著作。| 近义词: treatise, dissertation, study, paper | 反义词: overview, summary |词性: 名词 (noun)

monographer:/məˈnɒɡrəfər/ /məˈnɑːɡrəfər/
|n.| 专题论文作者；专题研究者|A person who writes or researches a monograph.| She is a renowned monographer in the field of medieval literature. 她是中世纪文学领域著名的专题论文作者。| 近义词: researcher, scholar, writer, author | 反义词: amateur, dabbler |词性: 名词 (noun)


词源:  From mono- (one) + -graph (writing), ultimately from Greek monos (single) + graphein (to write).
