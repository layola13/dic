noncondescending:/ˌnɒnkɒndɪˈsendɪŋ/ | adj. | 不居高临下的，没有优越感的，平易近人的 |  Not patronizing; treating others as equals, even if they are less knowledgeable or experienced. | The professor had a noncondescending way of explaining complex theories, making them accessible to everyone.  教授有一种不居高临下的方式来解释复杂的理论，使每个人都能理解。

She appreciated his noncondescending attitude, as it made her feel comfortable asking questions. 她很欣赏他不居高临下的态度，这让她可以放心地提问。 | 近义词: approachable, respectful, egalitarian, unpatronizing | 反义词: condescending, patronizing, supercilious, haughty | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + condescending (showing a patronizingly superior attitude).  "Condescend" comes from the Latin "condescendere," meaning "to descend, stoop, lower oneself."
