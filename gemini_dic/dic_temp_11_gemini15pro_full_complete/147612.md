overplenitude:/ˌoʊvərˈplɛnɪtjuːd/
noun
过度丰富，过剩
an excessive abundance; superfluity
The farmer had an overplenitude of tomatoes from his garden this year. 今年农民的菜园里西红柿产量过剩。

The overplenitude of information online can be overwhelming. 网上信息的过剩可能会让人不知所措。

Near synonyms:  superabundance, plethora, profusion, surplus, glut, excess
Near antonyms: scarcity, dearth, lack, deficiency, insufficiency
