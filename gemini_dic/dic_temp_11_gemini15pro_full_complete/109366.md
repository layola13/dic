lens:/lɛnz/| n.|透镜，镜头，晶状体|A piece of transparent material (such as glass) that has one or two curved surfaces and is used to make an image clearer, smaller, or bigger, or to focus light rays. Also, the transparent part of the eye that focuses light onto the retina.|The camera lens was scratched. 相机的镜头被刮花了。|synonyms: optic, glass|antonyms: --|noun

lens:/lɛnz/| v.|给…装透镜|To provide with a lens or lenses.|They lensed the new documentary with high-quality cameras. 他们用高质量的摄像机拍摄了这部新的纪录片。|synonyms: focus|antonyms: --|verb
