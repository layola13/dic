chainwork:/ˈtʃeɪnwɜːrk/| n.|链上工作量，累计工作量|A measure of the total computational effort expended in mining a blockchain. It represents the cumulative difficulty of all blocks in the chain up to a certain point.  It's often used to determine the "longest chain" in blockchain systems, which is considered the valid chain.|The chainwork of Bitcoin's blockchain is constantly increasing. 比特币区块链的链上工作量在不断增加。|Synonyms: cumulative work, total work|Antonyms: N/A|noun

chainwork:/ˈtʃeɪnwɜːrk/| n.|链式作业，连锁工作|A series of tasks or operations that are linked together, where the output of one stage becomes the input for the next.|The assembly line is a classic example of chainwork.  装配线是链式作业的典型例子。|Synonyms:  assembly line, production line, workflow, sequence|Antonyms: N/A|noun
