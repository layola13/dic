词源:  From distract + -ed + -ness. Distract comes from the Latin *distractus*, past participle of distrahere "to draw apart, divide, separate, distract," from dis- "apart" + trahere "to draw."  -ed forms the past participle and -ness forms a noun indicating a state or quality.
