anti-littering:/ˌæntiˈlɪtərɪŋ/
adjective
反乱扔垃圾的; 禁止乱扔垃圾的
Opposed to or designed to prevent littering.
The city council implemented an anti-littering campaign. 市议会开展了一场反乱扔垃圾的运动。
The park has prominent anti-littering signs. 公园里竖立着醒目的禁止乱扔垃圾的标志。
近义词: anti-litter,  no-littering
反义词: littering, pro-littering (rarely used)
词性: adjective

anti- /ˈænti/ (prefix) against; opposed to
litter /ˈlɪtər/ (noun) rubbish such as paper, cans, and bottles left lying in an open or public place.
-ing (suffix) forms present participles and gerunds of verbs, and also some adjectives.
