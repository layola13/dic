fairhood:/ˈfeərhʊd/
n.
公平；正义；正直
The state or quality of being fair.
The knight was known for his fairhood and bravery.  这位骑士以其公正和勇敢而闻名。
近义词:fairness, justice, equity, righteousness
反义词:unfairness, injustice, inequity, wickedness
词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *fairehede, equivalent to fair +‎ -hood. Cognate with Scots fairheid (“beauty, fairness, honesty”). More at fair, -hood.  来自中古英语 *fairehede，相当于 fair + -hood。 与苏格兰语 fairheid（“美丽、公平、诚实”）同源。 更多信息请参见 fair、-hood。
