feminises:/ˈfɛmɪnaɪzɪz/
verb
使女性化,使具有女性特征
To make something or someone more feminine or to adopt feminine characteristics.
The author feminises the main character in his latest novel.  作者在他最新的小说中使主要角色女性化。
Society often feminises men who express emotions openly.  社会常常将公开表达情感的男性女性化。
