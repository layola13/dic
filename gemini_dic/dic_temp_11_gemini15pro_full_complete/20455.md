bitterroot:/ˈbɪtəˌruːt/ | n. | 苦根 | A perennial herb (Lewisia rediviva) native to western North America, having a fleshy root and showy rose-colored flowers. Also: the edible root of this plant. | The Bitterroot Mountains divide Montana and Idaho. | 近义词:  Lewisia rediviva (scientific name), sand rose, portulaca | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
