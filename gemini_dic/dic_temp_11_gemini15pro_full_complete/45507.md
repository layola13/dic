cure-alls:/ˈkjʊrˌɔːlz/
noun
万能药; 万灵丹
A cure-all is a medicine or product that is claimed to cure a wide range of illnesses and problems.
Many people are skeptical of cure-alls, believing that there is no single solution for every ailment.
There's no such thing as a cure-all for every social ill.  没有一种万能药可以治愈所有社会弊病。
Synonyms: panacea, remedy, elixir, nostrum
Antonyms:  (Cure-alls doesn't have a direct antonym, but one could consider specialized treatments or targeted therapies as conceptually opposite.)
