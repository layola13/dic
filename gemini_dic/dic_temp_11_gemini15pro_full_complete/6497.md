amuck:/əˈmʌk/| adv.| 狂乱地，疯狂地；杀人狂地 |In a state of murderous frenzy. |The man ran amuck, attacking everyone in sight.  那个男人狂暴地跑着，攻击所有看得见的人。|近义词：frenziedly, wildly, berserk, violently |反义词：calmly, peacefully |词性: 副词

amucks:/(Rarely used as a plural noun)/| n.| (罕用复数形式) 杀人狂暴状态 |A state of murderous frenzy. | Historical texts sometimes refer to groups of people running "amucks."  历史文献有时会提到一群人“狂暴地”奔跑。|近义词：frenzy, rampage, riot |反义词：peace, tranquility |词性: 名词 (罕用)

词源:  From the Malay word "amuk" meaning "attacking furiously," often in a homicidal rage. 源自马来语单词 "amuk"，意思是“疯狂攻击”，通常指杀人狂怒。
