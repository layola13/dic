词源:  "Front" and "house" are both Old English words. "Front" from *fronti, akin to Old High German frunt and Latin frons. "House" from hūs, akin to Old High German hūs and Greek keuthō. The combined term "front-of-house" emerged later, likely in the context of theatres and hospitality.
