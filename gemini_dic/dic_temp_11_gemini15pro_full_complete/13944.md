auntly:/ˈɑːntli/ | adj. |像阿姨的；慈爱的；有母亲风范的|Like an aunt; kind and motherly. |She gave me some auntly advice. 她给了我一些像阿姨一样的建议。|My auntly concern made me call him. 我出于阿姨般的关心给他打了电话。|近义词：motherly, matronly |反义词: uncaring, indifferent |词性:形容词

Etymology: From "aunt" + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives). "Aunt" comes from Old French "ante," ultimately from Latin "amita" (father's sister).
