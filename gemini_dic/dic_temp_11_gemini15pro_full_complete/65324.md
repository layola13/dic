eugenin: /juːˈdʒɛnɪn/ | n. |优金蛋白;丁香酚;丁子香酚|A crystalline phenolic compound, C₁₀H₁₂O₂, obtained from cloves, allspice, and cinnamon leaves, isomeric with eugenol and used as an antiseptic and local anesthetic.|Eugenin is found in clove oil and is used in some perfumes. 优金蛋白存在于丁香油中，并用于一些香水。|Synonyms:  4-allyl-2-methoxyphenol, caryophyllene, eugenol|Antonyms:  N/A | noun
