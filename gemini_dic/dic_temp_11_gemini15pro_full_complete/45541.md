curiosity:/ˌkjʊrɪˈɒsɪti/ (US) /ˌkjʊriˈɑːsəti/| n. |好奇心；求知欲；奇物；珍品|A strong desire to know or learn something. |例句：Her natural curiosity led her to explore the abandoned house. 她天生的好奇心驱使她去探索那栋废弃的房子。

例句：The antique shop was full of curiosities.  这家古董店里摆满了奇珍异宝。|近义词：inquisitiveness, interest, wonder|反义词：apathy, indifference|词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *cūriōsitās*, from Latin *cūriōsus* (“careful, diligent, inquisitive”), from *cūra* (“care”).
