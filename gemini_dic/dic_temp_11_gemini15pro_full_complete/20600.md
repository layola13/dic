blackguard:/ˈblækɡɑːrd/| n. |恶棍，无赖，流氓|A person, particularly a man, who behaves in a dishonest or contemptible way; a scoundrel.| He was a blackguard who cheated everyone he met. 他是个骗子，欺骗他遇到的每一个人。|Synonyms: villain, rogue, scoundrel, rascal, knave|Antonyms: gentleman, hero, saint|noun

blackguard:/blækˈɡɑːrd/| vt. |辱骂，诽谤|To abuse or vilify someone with coarse language.|The newspaper blackguarded the politician with false accusations. 这家报纸用虚假的指控诽谤这位政治家。|Synonyms: revile, abuse, vilify, slander, defame|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud|verb
