USAGE->

Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影是怎么说的？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten for dinner? = 她晚饭吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before you called me? = 你打电话给我之前一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do this weekend? = 你这个周末要做什么？
What will she bring to the picnic? = 她会带什么去野餐？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be wearing to the wedding? = 她婚礼上会穿什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将学到什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for the past five years? = 过去五年你一直在做什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she gets her PhD? = 到她获得博士学位时，她将学习什么专业多久了？
