impli:/ɪmˈplaɪ/ | v. |暗示，意味|To suggest or hint at something without stating it directly.|Her silence implied consent. 她的沉默暗示了同意。|He implied that I was lying. 他暗示我在撒谎。|近义词：suggest, hint, intimate|反义词：state, declare|词性:动词
词源: From Late Latin implicare "to involve, entangle, enfold," from Latin in- "in" + plicare "to fold"
