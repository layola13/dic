matched:/mætʃt/ | adj. | 相配的，匹配的，相称的；敌得过的 |  suitable, fitting, corresponding; equal in quality or strength | The curtains and carpet are perfectly matched.  窗帘和地毯非常相配。 | synonyms: corresponding, compatible, suited, harmonious, similar, like, alike, twin, identical, duplicate | antonyms: mismatched, unsuited, incompatible, dissimilar, different, contrasting, clashing | adjective


Etymology: From the Old French *macher "to spot, stain," of uncertain origin.  The meaning shifted to "to make equal or similar."
