overawed:/ˌəʊvərˈɔːd/| adj.|敬畏的，慑服的| Filled with respect and wonder, often mixed with a slight fear; intimidated.|  The young musician was overawed by the presence of the famous conductor.| 近义词: intimidated, awestruck, daunted| 反义词: unimpressed, bold, audacious|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + awe.  "Awe" comes from Old Norse "agi," meaning "terror, dread."  The prefix "over-" intensifies the feeling, suggesting an overwhelming sense of awe.
