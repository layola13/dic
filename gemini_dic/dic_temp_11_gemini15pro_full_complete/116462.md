mateless:/ˈmeɪtləs/| adj.|无伴侣的，独身的|Without a mate; solitary; unpaired.  Lacking a partner or companion, especially a romantic or sexual one.|The mateless bird sang a mournful song.  那只没有伴侣的鸟唱着一首悲伤的歌。|Synonyms: single, unmated, unpaired, lone, solitary|Antonyms: mated, paired, coupled, married|adjective
