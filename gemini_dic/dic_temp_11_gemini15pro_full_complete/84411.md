hallowe'ens:/ˌhæləˈwiːnz/
n.
Hallowe'ens
万圣节节日（复数形式，指多个万圣节或万圣节的多个场合）
The Hallowe'ens of my childhood were always filled with fun. 我童年时代的万圣节总是充满乐趣。
We've had some great Hallowe'ens parties over the years. 多年来，我们举办了一些很棒的万圣节派对。
近义词: All Hallows' Eve (更正式), All Saints' Eve (更正式)
反义词: 无
词性: 名词


词源:  From "Hallow Eve," the evening before All Hallows' Day (All Saints' Day).  "Hallow" is an archaic word meaning "holy person" or "saint."  The "een" is a shortening of "even," meaning evening.
