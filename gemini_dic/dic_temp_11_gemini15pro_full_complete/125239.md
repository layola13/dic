multiday:/ˈmʌltiˌdeɪ/ /ˈmʌl.ti.deɪ/| adj.|多日的，持续数日的|Lasting or occurring over multiple days. |We went on a multiday hike through the mountains. 我们进行了一次为期数天的徒步旅行，穿越了群山。|近义词：extended, prolonged|反义词:single-day, one-day|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many") + "day" (from Old English "dæg").  The prefix "multi-" indicates something involving many or multiple instances of the following element.
