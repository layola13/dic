huers:/ˈhʊər(z)/| n.|呼喊者；叫喊的人（尤指叫渔船回港的人）|People who shouted to guide fishing boats back to shore, especially in Cornwall, England.|The huers would stand on the cliffs and shout to the pilchard boats when they sighted the shoals. 当他们看到鱼群时，呼喊者会站在悬崖上向沙丁鱼船呼喊。|近义词：shouters, callers|反义词:listeners|noun
