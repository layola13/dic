eruginous:/ɪˈruːdʒɪnəs/
adjective
绿锈色的，铜绿色的
Resembling the color of verdigris (greenish-blue coating formed on copper or brass).
The old statue was covered in an eruginous patina.  这古老的雕像覆盖着一层铜绿。
近义词:verdigris,aeruginous
反义词:bright, shiny


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aerūginōsus, from aerūgō (“rust of copper, verdigris”), from aes (“copper, bronze”).
