field-worker:/ˈfiːld ˌwɜːrkər/ (US), /ˈfiːld ˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 田野工作人员；户外工作人员；实地考察人员 | A person who works in the fields, doing agricultural or other outdoor work, or who conducts research in a natural environment. | The field-workers were out harvesting the crops.  田野工作人员正在收割庄稼。|  The field-worker collected data on the local bird population.  实地考察人员收集了当地鸟类种群的数据。 |Synonyms: farmhand, laborer, researcher, surveyor | Antonyms: office worker, administrator | noun
