overcharitable: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃærɪtəbl/
adjective
过度慈善的，过分慷慨的
Excessively charitable; too lenient or forgiving.
She was perhaps overcharitable in her assessment of his motives. 她对他的动机评价或许过于宽容了。
近义词: overgenerous, too lenient, forgiving, indulgent
反义词: uncharitable, unforgiving, harsh, stringent
词性: adjective


overcharitable: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃærɪtəbl/
中文翻译: 过度慈善的，过分慷慨的
英文解析: Excessively charitable; too lenient or forgiving.  Being charitable to an excessive degree, often to the point of being unwise or naive.
例句: He was overcharitable with his praise, even for mediocre work.  他即使对平庸的作品也过分赞赏。

例句: Her overcharitable nature often led her to be taken advantage of. 她过度慈善的天性经常导致她被人利用。
近义词: overgenerous, excessively lenient, too forgiving, indulgent
反义词: uncharitable, unforgiving, harsh, judgmental, stringent
词性: adjective
词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "charitable" (from Old French "charitable," ultimately from Latin "caritas" meaning "charity, love")
