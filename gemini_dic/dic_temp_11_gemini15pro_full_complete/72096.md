floccose:/ˈflɒkəʊs/ (UK) /ˈflɑːkəʊs/ (US)| adj.|絮状的；绒毛状的|Covered with tufts of soft woolly hairs.  Resembling wool.  Having a fluffy or woolly appearance.|The fungus had a floccose texture. 这种真菌的质地呈绒毛状。|Synonyms: woolly, downy, fluffy, fleecy|Antonyms: smooth, glabrous, bare|adjective

floccose:/ˈflɒkəʊs/ (UK) /ˈflɑːkəʊs/ (US)| adj.|絮凝的|Characterized by or resembling flocks or flocs, as of a precipitate.|The solution formed a floccose precipitate. 溶液形成了絮状沉淀。|Synonyms: flocculent|Antonyms: dispersed, dissolved|adjective
