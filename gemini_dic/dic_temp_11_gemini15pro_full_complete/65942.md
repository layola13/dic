ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *exactest*, superlative of *exact*, from Latin *exactus*, past participle of *exigō* (“I demand, require, drive out, measure, weigh, complete”), from *ex* (“out of, from”) + *agō* (“I drive, lead, do, act”).
