dollop:/ˈdɒləp/ (UK), /ˈdɑːləp/ (US)| n. |一小团，一小块，（尤指食物的）一勺，一团|A small amount of a soft or sticky substance. |She added a dollop of cream to her coffee. 她往咖啡里加了一团奶油。|
dollop:/ˈdɒləp/ (UK), /ˈdɑːləp/ (US)| n. |少量，一点儿|A small quantity of something. |He has a dollop of talent. 他有一点儿天赋。|
dollop:/ˈdɒləp/ (UK), /ˈdɑːləp/ (US)| v. |用勺舀，盛|To serve or add a dollop of something. |She dolloped some whipped cream onto the pie. 她往馅饼上舀了一些鲜奶油。|
