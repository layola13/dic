例句：She delivered a high-flown speech about the importance of art. 她发表了一篇关于艺术重要性的浮夸的演讲。|近义词：bombastic, pretentious, flowery, inflated|反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious, down-to-earth|词性:形容词
