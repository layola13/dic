brace:/breɪs/| n. vt.|支撑物，支架；牙箍；括号；准备，防备|A device that clamps things tightly together or that gives support to a weak or injured part of the body.|The doctor put a brace on her injured knee. 医生在她的受伤膝盖上戴上了支架。|近义词：support, prop, splint, prepare, strengthen|反义词: weaken|noun
brace:/breɪs/| vt.|支撑；加固；使防备；使振作|To make something stronger or more stable, or to prepare yourself for something difficult or unpleasant.|He braced himself for the impact. 他做好准备迎接撞击。|近义词：support, prop, splint, prepare, strengthen|反义词: weaken|verb
