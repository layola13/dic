lay-by:/ˈleɪ baɪ/ (UK) /ˈleɪ baɪ/ (US)
n.|路边停车带，路边休息区|A paved area beside a main road where vehicles can stop temporarily.|We pulled into a lay-by to admire the view. 我们把车开进了一个路边停车带欣赏风景。|Synonyms: lay-bye (UK), turnout, pull-off, rest area|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "through road" or "highway")|noun
lay-bys:/ˈleɪ baɪz/ (UK) /ˈleɪ baɪz/ (US)
n.|路边停车带（复数）|Plural of lay-by, paved areas beside a main road where vehicles can stop temporarily.|There are several lay-bys along this stretch of road. 这段路边有几个停车带。|Synonyms: lay-byes (UK), turnouts, pull-offs, rest areas|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
