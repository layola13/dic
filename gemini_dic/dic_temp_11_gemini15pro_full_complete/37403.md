commercialising: /kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 商业化，商品化 | The act of turning something into a product or service that can be bought or sold. |  They are commercialising a new type of software. 他们在将一种新型软件商业化。| 近义词：marketing, monetizing, merchandising | 反义词：decommercializing | Verb

commercialise: /kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/ | v. | 商业化，商品化 | To make something into a product or service that can be bought or sold. | The university patented the discovery and commercialised it. 这所大学为这项发现申请了专利并将其商业化。 | 近义词：market, monetize, merchandise | 反义词：decommercialize | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From commercial + -ize.  The word "commercial" comes from the Late Latin "commercialis," meaning "of or pertaining to trade."  The suffix "-ize" (or "-ise") is used to form verbs meaning "to make or become" something.
