counterinterpretations:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 反释，对抗性解释 |  Explanations that oppose or contradict a prevailing interpretation.  It involves offering a different understanding of something, often challenging the established or dominant view. |  Historians offered several counterinterpretations of the event, challenging the traditional narrative. 历史学家对这一事件提出了几种反释，挑战了传统的说法。

The defense lawyer presented a counterinterpretation of the evidence, suggesting the defendant's innocence.  辩护律师对证据提出了反释，暗示被告是无辜的。| 近义词: alternative interpretations, opposing interpretations, rebuttals, refutations | 反义词: standard interpretations, accepted interpretations, established interpretations | 词性: 名词
