outpatients:/ˈaʊtˌpeɪʃənts/
noun
门诊病人
Patients who receive treatment at a hospital or clinic without being admitted for an overnight stay.
The hospital has a large number of outpatients. 这家医院有大量的门诊病人。
The new wing of the hospital is dedicated to outpatients. 医院的新翼楼专门用于门诊病人。
synonyms: clinic patients, non-resident patients
antonyms: inpatients, hospitalized patients
