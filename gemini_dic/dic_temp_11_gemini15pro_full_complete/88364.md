hippurite:/ˈhɪpjuːˌraɪt/| n. | Hippurites(复数)|An extinct rudist bivalve mollusk of the Cretaceous period, having a conical lower shell and a smaller lid-like upper shell.  |The hippurites fossil was remarkably well-preserved. 这块 Hippurites 化石保存得非常好。|近义词：rudist, bivalve|反义词: None (extinct organism)|noun
