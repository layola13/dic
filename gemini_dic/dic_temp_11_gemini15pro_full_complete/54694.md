disorganic:/ˌdɪsɔːrˈɡænɪk/
adjective
无机的；非有机的
Not having the structure or characteristics of living organisms; inorganic.
例句：Disorganic substances do not contain carbon. 无机物不含碳。

例句：The chemist studied the properties of disorganic compounds. 化学家研究了无机化合物的性质。

近义词：inorganic, non-organic, lifeless, inanimate
反义词：organic, living, animate
词性：adjective


词源：
From dis- + organic.  The prefix "dis-" denotes negation or reversal.
