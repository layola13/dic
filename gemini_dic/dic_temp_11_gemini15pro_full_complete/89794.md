horizontals:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntlz/ | n. |水平线，水平面，横线；水平位置|Lines that are parallel to the horizon are called horizontals.  平行于地平线的线称为水平线。|The artist used strong horizontals to create a sense of calm.  这位艺术家用强烈的水平线来营造一种平静的感觉。|近义词：level lines, horizontal lines|反义词：verticals, perpendiculars|词性：名词

Etymology: From the Late Latin *horizontalis*, from Latin *horizon*, from Ancient Greek ὁρίζων (horízōn, “bounding, defining”), present participle of ὁρίζω (horízō, “I bound, limit”), from ὅρος (hóros, “boundary, limit, landmark”).
