epoch:/ˈiːpɒk/ (US) /ˈepɒk/ (UK)| n. |时代，纪元，时期|A period of time in history or a person's life, typically one marked by notable events or particular characteristics.|例句：The invention of the printing press marked a new epoch in human history. 印刷机的发明标志着人类历史的新纪元。


例句：The Victorian epoch was a time of great social change. 维多利亚时代是一个社会大变革的时期。


近义词：era, age, period, time|反义词:instant|词性:noun
