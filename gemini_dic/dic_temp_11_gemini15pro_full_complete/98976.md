inter-pollinating:/ˌɪntərˈpɒləˌneɪtɪŋ/ /ˌɪntərˈpɑːləˌneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
相互授粉的
The act of transferring pollen from one flower to another of a different plant, especially of a different variety.
例句：The inter-pollinating varieties of apple trees produced a new hybrid fruit.  相互授粉的苹果树品种培育出了一种新的杂交水果。

例句：Bees play a crucial role in inter-pollinating many different species of plants. 蜜蜂在许多不同种类植物的相互授粉中起着至关重要的作用。


近义词：cross-pollinating, hybridizing
反义词：self-pollinating
词性：adjective
