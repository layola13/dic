dolcest:/ˈdoʊlstɪst/ | adj. | 最甜的;最悦耳的 |  Superlative form of "dolce" meaning "sweet" or "soft" in Italian, used to describe something exceptionally sweet in taste, sound, or manner. | Her voice was the dolcest I had ever heard. 她的声音是我听过的最甜美的。| 近义词: sweetest, most melodious, most pleasant | 反义词: harshest, most bitter, most unpleasant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Italian "dolce" (sweet) + "-est" (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Latin "dulcis".
