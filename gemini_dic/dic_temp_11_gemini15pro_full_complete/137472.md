nontolerance:/nɒnˈtɒlərəns/ (US) /nɑːnˈtɑːlərəns/| n. |不宽容，不容忍|Lack of tolerance; refusal to tolerate other beliefs, practices, or behaviors, especially those that are different from one's own.|例句：The school had a policy of nontolerance for bullying. 学校对欺凌行为采取零容忍政策。|近义词：intolerance, bigotry, prejudice|反义词:tolerance, acceptance, forbearance|词性:名词

词源:  From non- + tolerance.  "Tolerance" comes from the Latin "tolerantia," meaning "endurance, a bearing, a supporting."
