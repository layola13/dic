ears:/ɪrz/ /ɪərz/| n. |耳朵；听力；听觉；穗 |The organs of hearing and balance, located on either side of the head. Also, the seed-bearing part of a cereal plant, such as wheat or corn.|She listened with attentive ears. 她专心听着。|Synonyms: hearing, auditory sense, lugs (informal),  spikes (for plants)|Antonyms: deafness (loss of hearing) |noun
