debasednesses:/dɪˈbeɪsɪdnəsɪz/
noun
堕落；卑鄙；贬低
The state of being debased; a decline in moral or artistic standards.
The debasednesses of the regime were well documented.  这个政权的堕落行为都有详细记录。
The museum curator lamented the debasednesses of modern art.  博物馆馆长对现代艺术的堕落表示惋惜。
synonyms: degradation, corruption, depravity, perversion, vileness
antonyms: nobility, virtue, purity, integrity, refinement
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

Etymology:
From debase + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Debase itself comes from the Old French *debaser, from de- (down) + bas (low).
