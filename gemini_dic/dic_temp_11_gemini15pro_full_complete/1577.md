achromatic:/ˌeɪkroʊˈmætɪk/| adj.|无色的，消色的|Relating to, or denoting lenses that transmit light without separating it into constituent colors; free from color; colorless.|The scientist used an achromatic lens for the experiment.  科学家用消色差透镜做实验。|Synonyms: colorless, uncolored, neutral, monochrome|Antonyms: chromatic, colorful, colored|adjective

achromatic:/ˌeɪkroʊˈmætɪk/| n.|无色|Something that is achromatic; a gray scale.|The artist primarily worked in achromatics, exploring the subtleties of light and shadow.  这位艺术家主要使用无色系，探索光影的微妙之处。|Synonyms: grayscale, monochrome|Antonyms: color, hue|noun
