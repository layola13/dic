half-angry:/ˌhæf ˈæŋɡri/

angry:/ˈæŋɡri/ | adj. | 生气的，愤怒的 | Feeling or showing strong annoyance, displeasure, or hostility. | She was angry with him for being late. 她因为他迟到而生他的气。| He gets angry easily. 他很容易生气。| 近义词：furious, enraged, indignant | 反义词：calm, happy, pleased | 词性: 形容词
