bonny:/ˈbɒni/ (UK) /ˈbɑːni/ (US)| adj.|美丽的；健康的；漂亮的|Pleasing to the eye; attractive; healthy-looking, especially of children.|例句：She has a bonny baby girl. 她有一个漂亮的宝贝女儿。

例句：The bonny lass caught everyone's attention.  这位美丽的少女吸引了所有人的注意。

近义词：pretty, beautiful, attractive, handsome, fair, lovely, charming, pleasant, agreeable|反义词：ugly, unattractive, plain, homely, unpleasant|词性：形容词
