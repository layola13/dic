computerizes:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzɪz/
|v.|计算机化，使…计算机化|To equip with or convert to computers, often replacing manual systems.|The company computerizes its payroll system.  公司将其工资系统计算机化。|automates, digitalizes|manualizes|verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From computer + -ize.  Computer from compute (from Latin computare "to count, sum up, reckon") + -er (agent suffix).  -ize from Greek -izein.
