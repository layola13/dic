nonfarmer:/ˌnɒnˈfɑːmə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɑːrmər/
noun
非农民；非农业人口
A person who does not engage in farming as a profession.
The majority of the population in this area are nonfarmers. 这个地区的大多数人口是非农民。
synonyms: city dweller, urbanite, townsman, townswoman
antonyms: farmer, agriculturalist
n.
