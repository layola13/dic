nonparties:/ˌnɒnˈpɑːrtiz/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɑːrtiz/
n.
非当事人；非签约方；局外人
People or entities not involved in a particular legal case, agreement, or dispute.
The nonparties to the agreement were not bound by its terms.  协议的非签约方不受其条款约束。
The judge ordered the nonparties to leave the courtroom. 法官命令局外人离开法庭。
outsiders, bystanders
parties, participants
noun
