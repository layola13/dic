cross-checkers:/ˌkrɔsˈtʃɛkərz/ | n. | 复核员，核对员 | People who verify information or data by checking it against multiple sources.  Those who double-check something for accuracy. | The cross-checkers ensured that all the data in the report was accurate.  复核员确保报告中的所有数据都是准确的。

The cross-checkers compared the two lists to find any discrepancies. 核对员比较了两个列表，以发现任何差异。 | synonyms: verifiers, validators, auditors | antonyms:  N/A (lack of a direct antonym for a person performing this action) | noun
