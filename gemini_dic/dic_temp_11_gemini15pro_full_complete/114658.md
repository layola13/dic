malabar:/ˌmæləˈbɑːr/ | adj. n. | 马拉巴尔的；马拉巴海岸 | Relating to the Malabar Coast of southwest India. Also, a type of spinach. |  The Malabar Coast is known for its beautiful beaches. 马拉巴海岸以其美丽的海滩而闻名。| The malabar spinach is a fast-growing vine.  马拉巴菠菜是一种生长迅速的藤蔓植物。 | synonyms: coastal (as an adjective referring to the region), Vine Spinach (for the plant) | antonyms: inland (as an adjective referring to the region) | adjective, noun
