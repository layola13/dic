defeatment:/dɪˈfiːtmənt/
n.
失败，击败
The state of being defeated.
His defeatment in the election was a crushing blow. 他在选举中的失败是一个沉重的打击。
近义词：defeat, loss, failure
反义词：victory, triumph, success
词性：名词

词源：From defeat + -ment.  Ultimately from the Old French *desfaire* "to undo".
