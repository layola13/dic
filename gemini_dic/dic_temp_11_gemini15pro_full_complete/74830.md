freneticisms:/frəˈnɛtɪsɪzəmz/
|n.| 狂乱，疯狂行为；狂热举动 |A state of intense activity and excitement, often characterized by wild or disordered behavior.  Repeated or habitual displays of frenetic behavior.|The freneticisms of the stock market during the crash were alarming. 股市崩盘期间的疯狂举动令人担忧。|Synonyms: frenzy, mania, hysteria, delirium|Antonyms: calmness, tranquility, peacefulness|Noun

frenetic:/frəˈnɛtɪk/
|adj.| 狂乱的，疯狂的|Fast and energetic in a rather wild and uncontrolled way.|Her frenetic schedule left her exhausted. 她疯狂的日程安排让她筋疲力尽。|Synonyms: frantic, frenzied, hectic, wild|Antonyms: calm, relaxed, peaceful|Adjective
