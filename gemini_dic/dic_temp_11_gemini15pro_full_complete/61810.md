emphysemic:/ˌɛmfɪˈsiːmɪk/
adjective
肺气肿的
Relating to or affected with emphysema, a lung condition characterized by abnormal enlargement of the air spaces in the lungs, resulting in shortness of breath.
His emphysemic lungs struggled to take in enough air. 他那患有肺气肿的肺部难以吸入足够的空气。
emphysematous, pulmonary
healthy, normal
