opacities:/oʊˈpæsɪtiz/| n. |不透明度，浑浊度|The state or quality of being opaque; lack of transparency.  The presence of opaque areas in a normally transparent structure.|The doctor noted some opacities in the patient's lungs on the X-ray. 医生在X光片上注意到病人肺部有一些浑浊.|近义词：cloudiness, haziness, obscurity|反义词:transparency, clarity|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *opacitas*, from Latin *opacus* ("shaded, dark, opaque").
