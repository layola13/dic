argot:/ˈɑːrɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈɑːɡɒt/ (UK)| n. |隐语，黑话，行话|The special vocabulary of a particular group, such as criminals or soldiers, often used to keep conversations secret from outsiders. |例句：The thieves used argot to discuss their plans without the police understanding them. 小偷们用黑话讨论他们的计划，以防警察听懂。

例句：The argot of the jazz musicians included terms like "cat" for a musician and "gig" for a performance.  爵士乐手的行话包括像 "cat" 指音乐家，"gig" 指演出这样的术语。|近义词：jargon, slang, cant, vernacular|反义词:standard language, formal language|词性:noun
