editorialize: /ˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪz/ | v. |发表社论；评论；|to express opinions rather than just report facts, especially in a way that is considered inappropriate|The reporter was criticized for editorializing in his news story.  这名记者因在他的新闻报道中发表评论而受到批评。|Synonyms: comment, opine, criticize, pontificate|Antonyms: report, narrate|verb

editorialize: /ˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪz/ | v. |发表社论；评论；| to offer one's opinion; to inject personal opinions into an ostensibly objective account|She began to editorialize about the situation, adding her own interpretation to the facts. 她开始对情况发表评论，在事实中加入了自己的解释。|Synonyms:  commentate,  expound,  moralize|Antonyms: report, narrate|verb
