gripe:/ɡraɪp/| n. v.|抱怨，牢骚；肠绞痛|A gripe is a complaint about something.|例句：He had a gripe about the service at the restaurant. 他对餐厅的服务颇有微词。|例句：She's always griping about something. 她总是抱怨个不停。|近义词：complaint, grumble, moan|反义词：praise, compliment|词性:名词,动词
gripes:/ɡraɪps/| n.|抱怨，牢骚|Gripes are complaints.|例句：My main gripe about the hotel was the noise. 我对这家酒店的主要抱怨是噪音。|例句：Air travelers have a legitimate gripe about baggage fees.  航空旅客对行李费有合理的抱怨。|近义词：complaints, grievances|反义词：praises, compliments|词性:名词
