cleistogamic:/ˌklaɪstəˈɡæmɪk/ | adj. |闭花受精的|Of or relating to cleistogamy; self-pollinating and fertilizing within closed, unopened flowers.|例句：Cleistogamic flowers are often small and inconspicuous. 闭花受精的花通常小而不起眼。

例句：Violets produce both cleistogamic and chasmogamous flowers. 紫罗兰会产生闭花受精和开放受精的花。|近义词：self-pollinating, autogamous|反义词：chasmogamous|词性：形容词

Etymology: From Greek *kleistos* "closed" + *gamos* "marriage"
