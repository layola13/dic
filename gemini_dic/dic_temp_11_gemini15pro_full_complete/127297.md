navigator:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtər/| n.|航海家，领航员，导航仪|A person who directs the route or course of a ship, aircraft, or other form of transportation, especially by using instruments and maps.  A device or system that uses signals from satellites to show the position of something (such as a car or ship).|例句：The navigator plotted a course through the treacherous waters. 领航员在危险的水域中绘制了一条航线。

例句：The car's navigator helped us find our way through the unfamiliar city. 汽车的导航仪帮助我们在陌生的城市里找到了路。|近义词：pilot, guide, director, helmsman|反义词:passenger, follower|词性:noun
