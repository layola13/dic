levana:/ləˈvɑːnə/ | n. | 月亮女神; 月亮 |  A name of Hebrew origin meaning "moon" or "white". It is often associated with the moon goddess in ancient mythology. |  Levana was worshipped as a powerful deity. 月亮女神被崇拜为强大的神。 The name Levana evokes images of the moon.  莱瓦娜这个名字让人联想起月亮的形象。 | 近义词: Luna, Selene | 反义词: Sol (sun) | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Hebrew לָבָנָה (levanah) meaning "white", "moon".  Ultimately from the Semitic root *lbn meaning "white".
