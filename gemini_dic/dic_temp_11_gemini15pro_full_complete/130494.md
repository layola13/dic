nonaccenting:/ˌnɒnˈæksentɪŋ/ | adj. | 非重读的，无重音的 | Not having or placing stress or accent (on a syllable, word, etc.).  It refers to a syllable, word, or part of a word that is not pronounced with stress or emphasis. | The nonaccenting syllable in the word "banana" is the second "a".  单词"banana"中的第二个"a"是非重读音节。| unstressed, unaccented | stressed, accented | adjective
