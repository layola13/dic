handman:/ˈhændmæn/| n.|杂工，修理工|A person, typically a man, employed to do a variety of odd jobs, typically around the home.|例句：We hired a handyman to fix the leaky faucet. 我们雇了一个杂工来修理漏水的水龙头。

例句：The handyman did a great job repairing the fence. 修理工把篱笆修得很好。|近义词：repairman, fixer, odd-jobber, maintenance man|反义词: specialist, expert|词性: noun
