overhunt:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌnt/| v. |过度捕猎|To hunt a species excessively, to the point of endangering its population.|The overhunting of whales in the 19th century nearly drove them to extinction. 19世纪对鲸鱼的过度捕猎几乎使它们灭绝。

The local tribes overhunted the deer, leading to a sharp decline in their numbers.  当地部落过度捕猎鹿，导致鹿的数量急剧下降。 |近义词：overexploit, deplete|反义词:conserve, protect|词性:动词

overhunt:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌnt/| n. |过度捕猎|The act of hunting a species excessively.|The overhunt led to a scarcity of game in the region. 过度捕猎导致该地区猎物稀少。

The overhunt of the passenger pigeon contributed to its extinction. 旅鸽的过度捕猎导致了它的灭绝。|近义词：overexploitation, depletion|反义词:conservation, protection|词性:名词
