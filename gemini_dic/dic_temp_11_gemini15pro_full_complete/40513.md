control:/kənˈtroʊl/ (US) /kənˈtrəʊl/ (UK)| n. vt.|控制；管理；抑制；克制|To have power over something or someone, or to manage a situation.|The pilot lost control of the aircraft. 飞行员失去了对飞机的控制。|近义词：manage, regulate, govern, direct, command, dominate, restrain, curb, suppress|反义词:release, lose, surrender|noun, verb

control:/kənˈtroʊl/ (US) /kənˈtrəʊl/ (UK)| n. vt.|控制装置；调节器|A device used to operate a machine or system.|The temperature control is broken. 温度控制器坏了。|近义词：regulator, switch, lever, button|反义词: N/A|noun

Etymology: From Old French *contrerolle (noun), contreroller (verb), from Medieval Latin contra- + rotulus "roll," referring to a duplicate register used to verify accounts.
