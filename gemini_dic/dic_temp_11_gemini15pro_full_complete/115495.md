manuring:/məˈnjʊərɪŋ/| n.|施肥，上肥|The act of applying manure to soil to enrich it.  |例句：Regular manuring is essential for healthy plant growth. 定期施肥对植物的健康生长至关重要。|近义词：fertilizing, composting|反义词:depleting|noun

manure:/məˈnjʊər/| vt.|施肥，给…施肥|To apply manure to (land).|例句：He manured the field before planting the seeds. 他在播种前给田地施了肥。|近义词：fertilize, compost|反义词: N/A|verb
