flowable:/ˈfloʊəbl/| adj. n.|可流动的，流体；流动资产|Something that is flowable is capable of flowing, typically like a liquid or gas.  It can also refer to assets that can be easily converted to cash. |The paint was too thick and not flowable enough for the sprayer.  这油漆太稠了，不适合喷雾器使用。

The company's flowable assets decreased significantly in the last quarter. 该公司流动资产在上个季度大幅减少。|近义词：fluid, liquid, pourable|反义词:solid, viscous, immobile|词性:adjective, noun
词源:From flow + -able, meaning "capable of flowing."  源自 flow + -able，意思是“能够流动的”。
