hopeless:/ˈhoʊplɪs/ (US) /ˈhəʊpləs/ (UK)| adj.|绝望的，无望的；无能的，无可救药的|Feeling or causing despair about something; incapable of improvement or success.|例句：The situation seemed hopeless.  情况似乎毫无希望。|近义词：desperate, despairing, incurable|反义词：hopeful, promising, optimistic|词性:形容词
hopelessness:/ˈhoʊpləsnəs/ (US) /ˈhəʊpləsnəs/ (UK)| n.|绝望，无望|The state of being without hope.|例句：A sense of hopelessness pervaded the room. 房间里弥漫着绝望的气氛。|近义词：despair, desperation|反义词：hope, optimism|词性:名词
