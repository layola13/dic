fox:/fɒks/ (UK) /fɑːks/ (US)| n.|狐狸|A carnivorous mammal of the dog family with a pointed muzzle and bushy tail, proverbial for its cunning.|The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.  这只敏捷的棕色狐狸跳过了懒惰的狗。|Synonyms: vixen (female fox), reynard|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an animal)|Noun


fox:/fɒks/ (UK) /fɑːks/ (US)| vt.|欺骗，迷惑|To deceive or trick someone.|He foxed his opponents with a clever move. 他用一个聪明的举动迷惑了他的对手。|Synonyms: trick, deceive, outwit|Antonyms: be honest, be truthful|Verb
