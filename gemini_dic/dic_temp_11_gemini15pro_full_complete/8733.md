antidemocratic:/ˌæntiˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 反民主的；反对民主的 | Opposed to or violating the principles of democracy. |  The regime employed antidemocratic tactics to suppress dissent.  该政权采用反民主的策略来压制异议。| synonyms: undemocratic, authoritarian, totalitarian, despotic, tyrannical | antonyms: democratic, egalitarian, liberal | adjective

antidemocratic:/ˌæntiˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ |  n. | 反民主者；反民主行为 | A person or action that opposes democracy. |  His antidemocratic views led to his expulsion from the party. 他的反民主观点导致他被开除党籍。| synonyms: autocrat, dictator, tyrant | antonyms: democrat, liberal | noun
