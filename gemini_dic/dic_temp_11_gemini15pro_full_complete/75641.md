fuguing:/ˈfjuːɡɪŋ/
adj.
赋格的，模仿赋格曲式的
Resembling or characteristic of a fugue, a musical form in which a theme is introduced by one voice or instrument and then repeated and developed by others.
The composer experimented with fuguing melodies in his latest symphony.  作曲家在他最新的交响曲中尝试了赋格旋律。
近义词:contrapuntal, canonic
反义词:homophonic
词性:adjective
