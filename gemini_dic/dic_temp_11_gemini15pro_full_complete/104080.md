jump's:/dʒʌmps/ | n. | 跳跃（的动作或高度）；暴涨；突升；惊跳 |  The possessive form of "jump," indicating ownership or belonging.  It's less common than "jump" as a noun or verb, but can be used to refer to the specific jump of someone or something. | The cat's jump was impressive.  这只猫的跳跃令人印象深刻。| synonyms: leap, spring, bound | antonyms: fall, drop | noun
