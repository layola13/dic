meaninglessness:/ˈmiːnɪŋləsnəs/
n.
无意义；空虚
lack of meaning or purpose
The meaninglessness of life without love.  没有爱的生命是空虚的。

The meaninglessness of the ritual. 这仪式毫无意义。

近义词:futility, emptiness, pointlessness, purposelessness, insignificance
反义词:meaningfulness, significance, purpose, value
词性:noun
