accompanies:/əˈkʌmpəniz/
|v.|陪伴，伴随；与…同时发生；为…伴奏|goes somewhere with someone; happens at the same time as something else; plays a musical instrument while someone else sings or plays|The pianist accompanied the singer. 钢琴家为歌手伴奏。|attend, escort, companion|leave, abandon|verb

accompanies:/əˈkʌmpəniz/
|v.|伴奏|plays a musical instrument while someone else sings or plays|She accompanied him on the piano. 她在钢琴上为他伴奏。|play along with, support musically|play solo|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acompaignier, from compaignie (“company”), from Late Latin companio (“companion, one who shares bread”).
