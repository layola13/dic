formulaic:/ˌfɔːrmjəˈleɪɪk/ | adj. | 公式化的，刻板的 | characterized by or conforming to a set formula or style, lacking originality or individuality | The plot of the movie was predictable and formulaic. 这部电影的情节是可以预测的，而且公式化。 | 近义词: clichéd, stereotypical, unoriginal, predictable, stock, conventional, routine, trite | 反义词: original, innovative, creative, imaginative, fresh, novel, unique | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *formulaicus*, from Latin formula (“formula, rule, method, pattern”) + -icus.
