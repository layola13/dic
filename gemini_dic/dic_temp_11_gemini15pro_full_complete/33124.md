christlike:/ˈkraɪstlaɪk/| adj.|像基督的，基督般的|Resembling or characteristic of Jesus Christ, especially in showing kindness, humility, and forgiveness.|She showed christlike compassion to the homeless man. 她对那个无家可归的人展现了基督般的同情心。|近义词：charitable, compassionate, benevolent, humane|反义词：cruel, heartless, uncharitable|词性:形容词
词源: Christ (基督) + like (像) - 源于古英语 Crist + lic.
