limacina:/ˌlɪməˈsaɪnə/ | n. | 海蝴蝶，一种翼足类软体动物 | A small, free-swimming sea snail of the family Limacinidae, with a thin, transparent shell and wing-like parapodia used for swimming.  It is a type of pteropod. | 例句：The limacina fluttered through the water, its delicate shell shimmering. 海蝴蝶在水中飘动，它精致的壳闪闪发光。

例句：Researchers are studying the effects of ocean acidification on limacina populations.  研究人员正在研究海洋酸化对海蝴蝶种群的影响。 | 近义词：sea butterfly, pteropod | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *limax, limacin- (“slug”) +‎ -ina.  Relating to the slug-like body within the shell.
