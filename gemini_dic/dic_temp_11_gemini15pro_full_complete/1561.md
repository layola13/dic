achieving: /əˈtʃiːvɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
实现;  取得;  获得
the process of successfully completing something or getting a good result
She finally succeeded in achieving her ambition to become a doctor. 她最终成功地实现了成为一名医生的抱负。
近义词: accomplishing, attaining, realizing, gaining, obtaining
反义词: failing, missing, losing

词性: v.
词源: From Old French achever "to finish, complete, carry out," from a- "to" + chief "head, end," from Latin caput "head."  The sense shifted from "coming to a head" to "accomplishing."
