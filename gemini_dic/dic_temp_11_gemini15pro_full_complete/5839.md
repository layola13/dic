amaz:/əˈmeɪz/ | v. | 使惊奇，使惊愕 | To surprise or astonish someone greatly. | Example: The magician's tricks amazed the audience. 魔术师的戏法使观众惊叹。

Example:  Her knowledge of the subject amazed me. 她对这个学科的了解令我惊讶。

近义词: astonish, surprise, astound, bewilder, daze

反义词: bore, tire, disappoint

词性: verb (动词)
