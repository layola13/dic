bibliolatry:/ˌbɪbliˈɒlətri/ | n. | 偶像崇拜；圣经崇拜 | Excessive reverence for the Bible or other sacred texts, often treating them as literally infallible.  |  Example 1: His bibliolatry prevented him from accepting any scientific findings that contradicted his interpretation of scripture. 他对圣经的盲目崇拜使他无法接受任何与他对经文的解释相矛盾的科学发现。

Example 2:  Some critics accuse certain religious groups of bibliolatry, claiming they prioritize the letter of the law over its spirit.  一些批评家指责某些宗教团体对圣经盲目崇拜，声称他们将法律条文置于其精神之上。 |  Synonyms:  scripture worship, idolization of the Bible | Antonyms:  sacrilege, blasphemy | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βιβλίον (biblíon, “book”) + -λατρεία (-latreía, “worship”).
