invidious:/ɪnˈvɪdiəs/| adj.|令人反感的；引起不满的；有害的|Likely to arouse or incur resentment or anger in others.  Unpleasant, awkward, or undesirable.|例句：She was placed in the invidious position of having to choose between her career and her family. 她处于不得不在事业和家庭之间做出选择的尴尬境地。|例句：Making invidious comparisons between the two groups can only exacerbate tensions. 在两组之间进行令人反感的比较只会加剧紧张局势。|近义词：offensive, unpleasant, objectionable, discriminatory|反义词：favorable, pleasant, complimentary|词性:形容词
