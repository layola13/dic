kers:/kɜːrz/ | n. | Kinetic Energy Recovery System | A system for recovering the kinetic energy of a moving vehicle under braking, typically storing this energy and making it available for later use when accelerating. | The Formula 1 car uses kers to boost acceleration out of corners.  一级方程式赛车使用动能回收系统来提高出弯时的加速度。 | 近义词: regenerative braking | 反义词: N/A | noun
