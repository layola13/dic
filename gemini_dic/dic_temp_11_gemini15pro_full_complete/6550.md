amylolysis: /ˌæmɪˈlɒlɪsɪs/ (US) /ˌæmɪˈlɒlɪsɪs/ (UK)
noun
淀粉分解；淀粉水解
The process of breaking down starch into simpler sugars, such as glucose, through the action of enzymes like amylase.
Example 1: Amylolysis is an important process in digestion. 淀粉分解是消化过程中的一个重要过程。

Example 2: Salivary amylase begins the process of amylolysis in the mouth. 唾液淀粉酶在口腔中启动淀粉分解的过程。
Synonym: starch digestion, starch hydrolysis
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider processes like starch synthesis)
