non-brahmanic:/nɒn-brɑːˈmænɪk/ | adj. | 非婆罗门的，非婆罗门教的 |  Not belonging to or associated with the Brahman caste or Brahmanical tradition in Hinduism.  It refers to individuals or groups outside the highest priestly caste, or practices and beliefs not aligned with Brahmanical orthodoxy. |  The study focused on non-Brahmanic religious traditions in ancient India.  这项研究的重点是古印度的非婆罗门宗教传统。| Near synonyms: non-Brahmin, Shramana (sometimes), anti-Brahminical | Antonyms: Brahmanic, Brahmin | Adjective
