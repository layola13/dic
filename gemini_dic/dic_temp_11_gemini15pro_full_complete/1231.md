accompany: /əˈkʌmpəni/ | v. | 陪伴，伴奏，伴随 | To go somewhere with someone or to exist or happen at the same time as something else. |  She accompanied her grandmother to the doctor. 她陪奶奶去看医生。

The pianist accompanied the singer. 钢琴家为歌手伴奏。| 近义词: escort, attend, follow, supplement | 反义词: leave, abandon | 词性: 动词

accompany: /əˈkʌmpəni/ | n. | 伴奏，伴随物 | (Rarely used as a noun) The act of accompanying or something that accompanies. | The wine makes a perfect accompaniment to the meal.  这酒是这顿饭的完美搭配。| 近义词: complement, addition | 反义词: N/A (noun form rarely used) | 词性: 名词 (罕用)
