ministryship:/ˈmɪnɪstriʃɪp/
n.
牧师的职位；牧师的职务；牧师的任期
The position, duties, or term of a minister, especially a Christian minister.

He began his ministryship in a small rural church.
他在一个乡村小教堂开始了他的牧师生涯。

Her ministryship was marked by her compassionate outreach to the community.
她的牧师任期以她对社区的同情和帮助而著称。

近义词: pastorate, ministry
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑非宗教职业或职位，例如) laity, secular employment


词性: noun


词源:  ministry + -ship

ministry:  From Old French *ministrie, from Late Latin ministerium ("service, office, ministry"), from Latin minister ("servant, attendant, assistant").

-ship: Old English -scipe (also -sciepe, -scipe, -scyp), a suffix forming abstract nouns denoting condition, state, office, dignity, etc. Cognate with Old Saxon -skipi, Old Frisian -skip, Dutch -schap, Old High German -scaf, German -schaft, Old Norse -skapr.
