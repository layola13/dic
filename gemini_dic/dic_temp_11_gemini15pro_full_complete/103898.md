judaizer:/ˈdʒuːdəˌaɪzər/| n.| 犹太化者；犹太教的拥护者 |A person who advocates or promotes the adoption of Jewish customs, beliefs, or practices, especially by non-Jews.|  Many early Christians were accused of being Judaizers by those who believed in a complete break from Jewish tradition.  许多早期基督徒被那些相信与犹太传统完全决裂的人指责为犹太化者。| synonyms: Jewish proselytizer, Hebraist | antonyms: Hellenizer, anti-Semite | noun
