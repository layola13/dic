arbitrarily:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtrɛrəli/| adv.|任意地，随意地，武断地|Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system.|He arbitrarily decided to change the meeting time. 他随意地决定更改会议时间。|Example: The books were arranged arbitrarily on the shelves. 书架上的书是随意摆放的。|Synonyms: randomly, capriciously, whimsically, haphazardly|Antonyms: systematically, methodically, rationally, logically|词性:副词
