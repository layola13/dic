impalements:/ɪmˈpeɪlmənts/| n.|刺穿，穿刺；被刺穿的状态|The museum displayed gruesome impalements from medieval times. 博物馆展示了中世纪时期令人毛骨悚然的穿刺刑具。|近义词：perforations, piercings, stabbings|反义词: removals, extractions|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "impale," derived from Old French "empaler," meaning "to put on a stake," ultimately from Latin "in" (in) and "palus" (stake).
