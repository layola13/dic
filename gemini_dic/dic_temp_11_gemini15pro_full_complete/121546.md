mismates:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪts/
| n.|配错之物；不匹配的人或物|Things or people that are not well matched or suited to each other. |The box contained a collection of mismates - single socks, odd gloves, and unmatched buttons.|近义词：mismatches, oddments|反义词: matches, pairs|词性:名词

mismate:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪt/
| vt.|错误地匹配；配错|To match incorrectly or unsuitably.|He mismated the wires, causing the circuit to malfunction.|近义词：mismatch, mispair|反义词: match, pair|词性:动词

mismated:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪtɪd/
| adj.|配错的；不匹配的|Incorrectly or unsuitably matched.|The mismated couple argued constantly.|近义词：mismatched, ill-matched|反义词: matched, well-matched|词性:形容词
