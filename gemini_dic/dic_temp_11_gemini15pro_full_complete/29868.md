causes:/ˈkɔːzɪz/ (US) /ˈkɔːzɪz/ (UK)| n. |原因，起因|The underlying causes of the problem are complex. 这个问题的根本原因很复杂。|Example: The heavy rain caused flooding in the city.  暴雨导致城市发生洪水。|Synonyms: reasons, origins, sources, factors|Antonyms: effects, results, consequences|noun

causes:/ˈkɔːzɪz/ (US) /ˈkɔːzɪz/ (UK)| v. |引起，导致|Smoking causes lung cancer. 吸烟会导致肺癌。|Example:  The accident caused a lot of damage. 事故造成了很大的损失。|Synonyms: produces, leads to, results in, brings about|Antonyms: prevents, stops, avoids|verb
