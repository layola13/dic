backpack: /ˈbækˌpæk/ (US) /ˈbækˌpæk/ (UK)| n.|背包|A bag carried on the back, typically made of cloth or other flexible material and supported by straps over the shoulders, used to carry supplies or personal belongings.|I packed my clothes and sleeping bag in my backpack. 我把衣服和睡袋装进了我的背包。|synonyms: rucksack, knapsack, packsack|antonyms: suitcase, briefcase|noun
