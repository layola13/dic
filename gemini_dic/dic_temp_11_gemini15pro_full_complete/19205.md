bewrought:/bɪˈrɔːt/
adjective
精工细作的；（感情上）激动的，兴奋的
deeply affected or wrought up emotionally; elaborately or intricately fashioned
例句：She had a bewrought expression on her face. 她脸上带着激动的神情。

例句：The gate was bewrought with intricate designs. 大门上装饰着复杂的图案。
近义词：elaborate, ornate, wrought, agitated, distressed
反义词：simple, plain, calm, composed
词性：adjective
