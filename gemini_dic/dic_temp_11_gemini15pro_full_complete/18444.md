bemaul: /bɪˈmɔːl/
verb
弄脏，涂抹，诽谤
to smear or stain something, often with something sticky or dirty; to criticize someone harshly and unfairly.
The child bemauled his new shirt with chocolate. 孩子把他的新衬衫弄脏了巧克力。
His reputation was bemauled by the scandalous accusations. 他的名誉被那些诽谤性的指控玷污了。
近义词: smear, stain, besmirch, slander, defame
反义词: clean, cleanse, purify, praise, commend
词性：v.
