baldachin: /ˈbɔːldəkɪn/ | n. | (建筑)  Baldachin (拱门或祭坛上方类似华盖的装饰) | An ornamental structure resembling a canopy, especially one over an altar or doorway. | The church's altar was adorned with a carved wooden baldachin. 教堂的祭坛装饰着一个雕刻的木制华盖。

The entrance to the palace was marked by a grand baldachin supported by marble columns. 宫殿的入口处有一个由大理石柱支撑的宏伟华盖。 | 近义词: canopy, awning, covering | 反义词: (No direct antonym - consider "exposed" as a contextual opposite) | 词性: noun


词源: From Italian *baldacchino*, from *Baldacco*, a variant of *Baghdad*, from where rich fabrics used for such canopies were imported.  源自意大利语 *baldacchino*，来自 *Baldacco*，是 *Baghdad*（巴格达）的变体，这种华盖所用的华丽织物是从巴格达进口的。
