nonsurety:/ˌnɑːnˈʃʊrəti/ | adj. | 非担保的，无担保的 | Not secured by a surety or guarantor; not involving a surety.  Relating to a debt or obligation that is not backed by a surety. | The loan was nonsurety, meaning no one else was responsible for repaying it if I defaulted.  这笔贷款是无担保的，这意味着如果我违约，没有其他人有责任偿还。| Near synonyms: unsecured, unguaranteed | Antonyms: surety, secured, guaranteed | adjective

nonsurety:/ˌnɑːnˈʃʊrəti/ | n. | 非担保债务 | A debt or obligation that is not backed by a surety.  |  Nonsureties are considered higher risk for lenders. 非担保债务对贷款人来说风险更高。 | Near synonyms: unsecured debt, unguaranteed obligation | Antonyms: surety bond, secured debt, guaranteed obligation | noun
