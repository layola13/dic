osteogen:/ˈɒstiədʒɛn/ | n. | 成骨细胞，骨原 | A substance that stimulates the formation of bone. |  Osteogen is important for bone growth and repair. 成骨细胞对骨骼生长和修复很重要。|  bone morphogenetic protein (BMP), growth factor | antonym not applicable | noun


osteogenic:/ˌɒstiəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 成骨的，生骨的 | Relating to or causing the formation of bone. | Osteogenic cells are responsible for bone formation. 成骨细胞负责骨骼的形成。| bone-forming | antonym not applicable | adjective


Etymology:
From osteo- (bone) + -gen (producing).  The prefix "osteo-" comes from the Greek "osteon" meaning "bone".  The suffix "-gen" comes from the Greek "-genes" meaning "born" or "produced".
