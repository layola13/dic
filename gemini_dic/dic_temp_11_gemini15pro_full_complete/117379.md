mediatorship:/ˌmiːdiˈeɪtərʃɪp/ | n. |调解；调停；斡旋|The act of mediating; the role or position of a mediator.  The process by which a third party helps two or more parties resolve a dispute or conflict.|The UN played a crucial role in the mediatorship between the warring factions. 联合国在交战各派之间的调解中发挥了至关重要的作用。

The mediatorship of the community elders brought an end to the feud.  社区长老的调解结束了这场不和。 |Synonyms: mediation, intervention, conciliation, arbitration|Antonyms: conflict, dispute, disagreement|noun
