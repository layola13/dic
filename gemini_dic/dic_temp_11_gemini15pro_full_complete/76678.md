galvanograph:/ɡælˈvænəɡræf/ | n. | 电流曲线图 | A curve or record produced by a galvanograph. | The galvanograph showed a steady decrease in current over time. 电流曲线图显示电流随时间稳定下降。| Synonyms: graph, chart, record | Antonyms: None | noun
