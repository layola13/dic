hypostasize:/haɪˈpɒstəsaɪz/ /haɪˈpɑːstəsaɪz/| v. |假设；赋予实体；使具体化|To attribute real existence to (an abstract concept); reify. To posit as a distinct entity or substance.|Philosophers often hypostasize abstract concepts like justice and truth. 哲学家们经常将正义和真理之类的抽象概念实体化。|The artist hypostasized his emotions in his paintings.  这位艺术家在他的绘画中具体化了他的情感。|reify, embody, personify, materialize| - |verb
