anglicises:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
使英语化;英国化
To make or become English in form or character.
Many foreign words have been anglicised over time. 许多外来词随着时间的推移已经被英语化了。
The restaurant anglicises its menu to appeal to a wider audience.  这家餐馆将其菜单英语化以吸引更广泛的顾客。
synonyms: adapt, modify, translate
antonyms: foreignize, localize
