edgebone:/ˈɛdʒˌboʊn/
n.
aitchbone, rump bone
The edgebone is a cut of beef from the rump area.
The butcher prepared a large edgebone for the Sunday roast.  屠夫为周日的烤肉准备了一大块牛臀骨。
Synonyms: aitchbone, rump bone, rump roast
