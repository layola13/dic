inexcitable:/ˌɪnɪkˈsaɪtəbl/
adjective
不易兴奋的；不易激动的
not easily excited; calm
例句：He has an inexpressive and inexpcitable personality. 他性格木讷，不易激动。

例句：She remained inexpcitable even during the emergency. 即使在紧急情况下，她也保持冷静。

近义词：calm, composed, impassive, imperturbable, phlegmatic, serene, tranquil
反义词：excitable, nervous, agitated

词性: adjective
词源:  in- (not) + excitable (capable of being excited).  From Latin excitabilis, from excitare "to rouse, excite."
