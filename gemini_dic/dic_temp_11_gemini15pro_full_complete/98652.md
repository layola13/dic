insufficiency:/ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. |不足，缺乏，不充分 |The insufficiency of funds prevented the project from being completed. |例句：The insufficiency of evidence led to the acquittal of the defendant.  证据不足导致被告被判无罪。

例句：There is an insufficiency of qualified teachers in the area.  该地区合格教师不足。 |近义词：lack, shortage, deficiency, inadequacy |反义词: sufficiency, abundance, surplus |词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French insuffisance, from insufficient, or from late Latin insufficientia, from insufficient- ‘not sufficient’.
