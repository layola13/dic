foraminifera:/ˌfɔːrəmɪˈnɪfərə/ |n. (plural)|有孔虫|A single-celled planktonic animal with a perforated chalky shell through which slender protrusions of protoplasm extend.  These shells form thick ocean-floor deposits.|例句：Foraminifera are important indicators of past climates. 有孔虫是过去气候的重要指标。

例句：The study of foraminifera helps us understand ocean ecosystems. 对有孔虫的研究有助于我们了解海洋生态系统。|近义词：None (highly specialized term)|反义词: None|词性: Noun


Etymology:
From Modern Latin *Foraminifera*, neuter plural of *foraminifer* (“bearing holes or foramina”), from Latin *foramen* (“hole, opening”) + *-fer* (“bearing”).
