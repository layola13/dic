amoralist:/ˈeɪˈmɔːrəlɪst/ /əˈmɒrəlɪst/
n.
不道德的人；没有道德观念的人
A person who disregards moral principles or believes that morality is not relevant to human behavior.
He lived his life as an amoralist, unconcerned with the consequences of his actions. 他过着不道德的生活，不关心自己行为的后果。
近义词：nihilist, immoral person, libertine
反义词：moralist, ethicist
