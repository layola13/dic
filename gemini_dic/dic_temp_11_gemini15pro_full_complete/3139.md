aestheticism:/iːsˈθɛtɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 审美主义；唯美主义 | A doctrine that the principles of beauty are central to other, especially moral, principles; devotion to beauty in art, literature, etc. |  The artist's work was a clear example of aestheticism, prioritizing beauty above all else.  这位艺术家的作品是审美主义的鲜明例证，将美置于一切之上。| synonyms:  art for art's sake, aesthetic movement,  | antonyms: philistinism, utilitarianism | noun
