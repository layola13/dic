dullsville:/ˈdʌlzvɪl/| n.|沉闷乏味的地方；无聊的场合|A fictional place characterized by extreme boredom and lack of excitement.  It's used figuratively to describe a boring situation or location.|This town is total dullsville on a Sunday.  这个小镇星期天真是无聊透顶。|Synonyms:  nowheresville, sticks, backwater|Antonyms:  exciting place, metropolis, hotspot|noun
