demarcator:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪtər/ (US), /dɪˈmɑːkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 分界线，分界符，界标 |Something that marks a boundary or limit. |The river serves as a natural demarcator between the two countries. 这条河流是两国之间的天然分界线。|Synonyms: boundary, divider, separator, delimiter |Antonyms: connector, unifier|noun

demarcator:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪtər/ (US), /dɪˈmɑːkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 划分器，区分器 |A device or symbol used to separate or distinguish different parts of something. |The program uses special characters as demarcators between data fields.  这个程序使用特殊字符作为数据字段之间的分隔符。|Synonyms: separator, delimiter, divider |Antonyms: integrator, combiner|noun
