hypertechnical:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɛknɪkəl/| adj.|过度技术化的，过于专业的|Excessively or unnecessarily technical; using jargon or overly specialized language that is difficult for a layperson to understand.|例句：The manual was so hypertechnical that even the engineers struggled to understand it. 这本手册过于技术化，即使是工程师也难以理解。

例句：His explanation was hypertechnical and went over the heads of most people in the room. 他的解释过于专业，让房间里的大多数人无法理解。|近义词：overly technical, esoteric, jargonistic, specialized|反义词：simple, basic, layman's, accessible|词性:形容词
