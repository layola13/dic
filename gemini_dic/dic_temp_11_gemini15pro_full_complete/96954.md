infallibles:/ɪnˈfælɪblz/| n. |不会犯错的人（或事物）; 绝对可靠的人（或事物）| People or things that are incapable of making mistakes or being wrong. It is the plural form of "infallible." |The Pope is considered by Catholics to be infallible in matters of doctrine.  天主教徒认为教皇在教义问题上是不会犯错的。

The guidebook claimed to be an infallible guide to the city's best restaurants.  该指南声称是这座城市最佳餐厅的绝对可靠指南。|近义词：unerring, faultless, impeccable, perfect|反义词：fallible, errant, flawed, imperfect|词性: 名词
