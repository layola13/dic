halcyon:/ˈhælsiən/ (US), /ˈhælsiən/ (UK)| adj. n.|平静的，宁静的；太平鸟|Characterized by happiness, great success, and prosperity; idyllically calm and peaceful; a mythical bird identified with the kingfisher, said to breed in a nest floating on the sea and to have the power to calm the wind and waves while it was nesting.|例句：The halcyon days of summer. 夏日宁静的日子。|例句：She recalled the halcyon days of her youth. 她回忆起她年轻时平静的日子。|近义词：peaceful, tranquil, serene, calm, golden|反义词：turbulent, chaotic, stormy, troubled|词性:形容词, 名词
