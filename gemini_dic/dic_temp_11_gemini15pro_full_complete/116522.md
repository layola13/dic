matha: /ˈmɑːθə/ | n. | monastery, hermitage (especially in Buddhism) | A matha is a Hindu or Buddhist monastery.  It often serves as a center for religious learning and practice. |  Example 1: The young monk traveled to a remote matha in the Himalayas to deepen his spiritual practice. 年轻的僧侣前往喜马拉雅山一个偏远的寺院加深他的修行。

Example 2:  The matha provided food and shelter for pilgrims traveling to the sacred site.  寺院为前往圣地的朝圣者提供食物和住所。| Synonym: monastery, ashram, hermitage | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "secular building") | noun
