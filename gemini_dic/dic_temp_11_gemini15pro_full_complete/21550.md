bluely:/ˈbluːli/| adv.| 蓝色地，忧郁地 |  In a blue or bluish manner; sadly; gloomily. | The sky shone bluely after the storm.  暴风雨过后，天空呈现出蓝色。She stared bluely out the window, lost in thought. 她忧郁地凝视着窗外，陷入了沉思。 | 近义词: sadly, gloomily, dejectedly | 反义词: brightly, cheerfully, happily | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "blue" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The word "blue" itself has a complex etymology, ultimately deriving from Proto-Germanic *blǣwaz.
