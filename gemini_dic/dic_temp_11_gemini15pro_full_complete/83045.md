gurdwaras: /ˈɡʊrdwɑːrəz/
n.
锡克教寺庙
A place of worship for Sikhs.
Many Sikhs gather at gurdwaras on Sundays. 很多锡克教徒星期天聚集在寺庙里。
The gurdwara provides free meals to everyone, regardless of their faith.  寺庙向所有人提供免费餐食，无论他们的信仰如何。
synonyms: Sikh temple, Sikh shrine
antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a place of worship)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Punjabi ਗੁਰਦੁਆਰਾ (gurdwārā), from guru (“guru”) + dwārā (“door, doorway, gate”).
