magnetises:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪzɪz/
verb
使磁化; 吸引
To make something magnetic; to strongly attract someone.
The iron filings were magnetised by the bar magnet. 铁屑被条形磁铁磁化了。

His charisma magnetises people to him. 他的人格魅力深深吸引着人们。

Synonym: attract, mesmerize, captivate, charm
Antonym: repel, deter
word type: verb
