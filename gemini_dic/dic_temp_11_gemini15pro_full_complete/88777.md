hodgepodge:/ˈhɒdʒpɒdʒ/ /ˈhɑːdʒpɑːdʒ/| n. |大杂烩；混合物|A confused mixture or collection of different things.|The room was a hodgepodge of furniture from different eras. 房间里摆放着不同年代的家具，像个大杂烩。|例句：The script was a hodgepodge of clichés and borrowed ideas.  剧本是些陈词滥调和借用观点的大杂烩。

例句：Her essay was a hodgepodge of unsupported assertions. 她的文章是一堆未经证实的断言的杂烩。|近义词：mixture, medley, jumble, assortment, miscellany|反义词: uniformity, consistency|词性:名词
