benzoic:/bɛnˈzoʊɪk/| adj.|苯甲酸的；安息香的|Relating to or derived from benzoic acid.|Benzoic acid is a white crystalline solid.|近义词: benzene carboxylic|反义词: None|词性: adjective
Etymology: From *benzoin* + *-ic*.  *Benzoin* comes from  Medieval Latin *benzoinum*, from Arabic *lubān jāwī*  meaning "Javanese frankincense."
