coscripting:/ˈkoʊˌskrɪptɪŋ/| n.|共同脚本编写，协同脚本编写|The process of writing a script collaboratively, often involving multiple writers or incorporating contributions from various sources.  It emphasizes a shared creative process and integration of diverse perspectives.|Example 1: Coscripting allowed the team to blend their individual strengths and create a more dynamic and engaging narrative. 共同脚本编写让团队能够融合各自的优势，创造出一个更具活力和吸引力的叙事。

Example 2: The film's success was attributed in part to the effective coscripting process, which brought together experienced writers and fresh perspectives. 这部电影的成功部分归功于有效的共同脚本编写过程，它汇集了经验丰富的作家和新鲜的视角。|近义词：collaborative writing, joint scripting|反义词:solo writing, individual writing|词性:noun
