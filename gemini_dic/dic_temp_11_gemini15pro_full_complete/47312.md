de-licenses:/ˌdiːˈlaɪsənzɪz/
verb
取消许可，撤销执照
To remove or revoke a license from someone or something.
The government de-licensed several radio stations. 政府取消了几个广播电台的执照。
The city de-licensed the taxi driver after several complaints.  在接到几起投诉后，市政府吊销了出租车司机的执照。
deregulate, dismantle, abolish
license, authorize, permit
