continuable:/kənˈtɪnjuəbl/
adjective
可继续的，可延续的
able to be continued
The story is continuable in a sequel.  这个故事可以在续集中继续。
The project is continuable with further funding.  有了进一步的资金，这个项目就可以继续下去。
近义词:extendable, resumable,  prolongable
反义词:unfinishable, interminable, endless (These are antonyms in the sense that they imply something cannot be *finished*, whereas 'continuable' implies something *can* be continued, but isn't necessarily finished.)
词性:adjective
