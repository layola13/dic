confirmational:/ˌkɑːnfərˈmeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 确认的，证实的 | Relating to or serving as confirmation. | The judge requested confirmational evidence. 法官要求提供确凿的证据。| The company sent a confirmational email after I placed the order. 我下单后，公司发来了一封确认邮件。 |  近义词: validating, corroborative, verifying, substantiating | 反义词: disproving, refuting, contradictory | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From confirmation + -al.  Confirmation comes from Late Middle English: from Old French confirmacion, from late Latin confirmatio(n-), from the verb confirmare (see confirm).
