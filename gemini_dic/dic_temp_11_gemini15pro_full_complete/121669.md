misplaned:/mɪsˈpleɪnd/
|v.|错误地计划；计划不周|The project was misplanned from the start and ran over budget.  这个项目从一开始就计划不周，导致预算超支。|
|v.|
|English explanation: To plan something badly or incorrectly.|
|Example 1:  They misplanned the trip and ended up lost in the woods. 他们计划旅行不周，结果在树林里迷路了。|
|Example 2:  The misplanned marketing campaign resulted in a significant loss of revenue.  计划不周的营销活动导致了巨大的收入损失。|
|Synonym: miscalculate, bungle, mishandle|
|Antonym:  well-planned, organize, strategize|
|Word type: verb (past tense and past participle of misplan)|
