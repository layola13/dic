excruciating:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪtɪŋ/ /ɛkˈskruːʃiˌeɪtɪŋ/| adj.|极度痛苦的；折磨人的|Extremely painful; agonizing.|The pain in my back was excruciating. 我的背疼得厉害。|Synonyms: agonizing, torturous, unbearable, intense, severe|Antonyms: mild, slight, bearable, pleasant, soothing|adjective


excruciate:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪt/ /ɛkˈskruːʃiˌeɪt/| vt.|（使）受酷刑，折磨|To inflict severe pain or torture on (someone).|The memories of the accident continued to excruciate him. 事故的记忆不断地折磨着他。|Synonyms: torment, torture, agonize|Antonyms: soothe, comfort, relieve|verb
