inaccessible:/ˌɪnækˈsesəbl/ | adj. | 难以到达的，难以理解的，难以接近的 | Not easily reached or approached; difficult to understand or appreciate. |  例句：The remote village was inaccessible by road during the winter. 冬天，这个偏远的村庄公路不通。

例句：His complex philosophical ideas were inaccessible to most people. 他复杂的哲学思想对大多数人来说难以理解。


近义词：unreachable, unapproachable, impassable, impenetrable | 反义词：accessible, reachable, approachable | 词性:形容词
