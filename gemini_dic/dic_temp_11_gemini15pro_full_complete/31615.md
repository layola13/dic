chastities: /ˈtʃæstɪtiz/
noun
贞洁，纯洁
the state or quality of being chaste; abstention from unlawful sexual intercourse.
The novel explores the theme of female chastities in a patriarchal society. 这部小说探讨了父权社会中女性贞洁的主题。
Chastities were highly valued in Victorian England. 在维多利亚时代的英格兰，贞操是非常珍贵的。
synonyms: purity, virtue, innocence, celibacy
antonyms: promiscuity, lewdness, impurity
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chastites*, from Old French *chasteté*, from Latin *castitās*, from *castus* ("pure, chaste").
