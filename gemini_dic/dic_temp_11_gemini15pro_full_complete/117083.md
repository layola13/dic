meadowsweet:/ˈmedəʊswiːt/ (US) /ˈmedəʊswiːt/ (UK)| adj. |香甜的，如草地般芬芳的|Having a sweet fragrance reminiscent of meadows.|The meadowsweet scent of the air was intoxicating.|近义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling|反义词:foul, acrid|词性:adjective
