interconvertible:/ˌɪntər.kənˈvɜː.tə.bəl/ | adj. | 可互相转换的，可互相变换的 | Able to be changed from one form to another and back again. |  Glucose and fructose are interconvertible sugars. 葡萄糖和果糖是可以互相转换的糖。

The two forms of energy are interconvertible.  这两种能量形式可以互相转换。

interconvertible:|近义词：transformable, interchangeable, mutable |反义词: fixed, immutable, unchangeable | 词性: adjective

Etymology: inter- (between) + convert (to change) + -ible (capable of being)
