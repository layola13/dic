desegmentation:/diːˌsɛɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/
n.|去分段，取消分段|The desegmentation of the hard drive improved computer performance. |例句：Desegmentation of the computer's hard drive can improve its speed.  取消计算机硬盘的分段可以提高其速度。

例句：The desegmentation process can take several hours. 去分段过程可能需要几个小时。|近义词：consolidation, integration, unification|反义词：segmentation, fragmentation|词性: 名词

Etymology:  de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + segmentation (from segment, ultimately from Latin segmentum "a piece cut off")
