chaplet:/ˈtʃæplət/| n. |花环，念珠，一系列|A circular garland or wreath for the head; a string of beads, especially one used for counting prayers; a series of connected items resembling a string of beads.|She wore a chaplet of flowers in her hair. 她头上戴着花环。|近义词：wreath, garland, rosary, string, series|反义词: None|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "chapelet" (little hat, garland), diminutive of "chapel" (hat, garland), from Late Latin "capellus" (hood, cowl), diminutive of Latin "cappa" (cape, hood).  The sense of "string of beads" arose from the resemblance to a garland.
