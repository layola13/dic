mosotho:/məˈsuːtuː/ /məˈsuːθoʊ/
n.|莫索托人；塞索托语|A member of a Bantu people of Lesotho and South Africa. Also, the Bantu language of the Sotho. |Many Mosotho people live in Lesotho. 许多莫索托人居住在莱索托。|The Mosotho language is closely related to Setswana. 塞索托语与茨瓦纳语密切相关。|近义词:Basotho|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|词性:名词
