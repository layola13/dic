nonvitriolic:/ˌnɒnˈvɪtriˈɒlɪk/
adjective
温和的，不刻薄的
Not harsh or corrosive in tone; lacking in vitriol.  Free from bitter criticism or malice.
She responded with a nonvitriolic, even conciliatory, tone. 她以温和的，甚至是安抚的语气回应。
The debate, while passionate, remained remarkably nonvitriolic. 辩论虽然激烈，但仍然保持着显著的温和。
近义词:gentle, mild, kind, inoffensive, conciliatory
反义词:vitriolic, acrimonious, caustic, bitter, scathing
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
vitriolic (adjective): derived from "vitriol," originally referring to sulfuric acid, later extended to mean "bitterly scathing."
