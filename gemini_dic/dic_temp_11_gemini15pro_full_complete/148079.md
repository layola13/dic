oversaucy:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːsi/
adjective
过于轻佻的，过分 impertinent 的
Excessively bold, impudent, or flippant.
例句：His oversaucy remarks to the teacher earned him a detention.  他过于轻佻地对老师说话，结果被留堂。

例句：The oversaucy waiter kept interrupting our conversation.  那个过于轻佻的服务员不停地打断我们的谈话。

近义词：impudent, impertinent, bold, brazen, cheeky, flippant, forward, presumptuous, saucy
反义词：respectful, polite, courteous, deferential, humble, modest


词性: adjective
