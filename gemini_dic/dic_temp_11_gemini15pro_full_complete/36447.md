cold-blooded:/kəʊldˈblʌdɪd/ /koʊldˈblʌdɪd/| adj.|冷血的，残酷的|Lacking emotion or pity; without warm feelings; cruel.  Literally, having blood that varies in temperature with the surrounding environment, as with reptiles, fishes, and amphibians.|The murderer was described as a cold-blooded killer.|近义词：heartless, ruthless, unfeeling, pitiless|反义词：warm-hearted, compassionate, kind|词性:形容词

cold-blooded:/kəʊldˈblʌdɪd/ /koʊldˈblʌdɪd/| n.|冷血动物|An animal whose blood temperature varies with the surrounding environment, such as a reptile, fish, or amphibian.|Snakes are cold-blooded animals.|近义词：poikilotherm|反义词：warm-blooded/homeotherm|词性:名词
