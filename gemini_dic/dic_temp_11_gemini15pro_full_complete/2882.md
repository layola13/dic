advertent:/ədˈvɜːrtənt/
adjective
留意的；注意的；故意的
Paying attention; heedful; intentional.
She made an advertent decision to pursue a career in medicine. 她刻意决定从事医学事业。
近义词:attentive, mindful, deliberate, conscious
反义词:inadvertent, careless, negligent, unconscious
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin advertens, present participle of advertere "to turn toward," from ad- "to" + vertere "to turn."
