equivocating: /ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtɪŋ/
verb.
含糊其辞，支吾其词
using ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.
He was equivocating about his role in the affair. 他对他在这件事中的角色含糊其辞。
近义词：prevaricating, hedging, evading, dodging
反义词：being forthright, being direct, being honest


equivocate: /ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪt/
verb.
含糊其辞，支吾其词
use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.
The defendant equivocated when asked about his whereabouts on the night of the crime. 当被问及犯罪当晚的行踪时，被告含糊其辞。
近义词：prevaricate, hedge, evade, dodge
反义词：be forthright, be direct, be honest


equivocation: /ɪˌkwɪvəˈkeɪʃn/
noun.
含糊其辞，模棱两可的话
the use of ambiguous language to conceal the truth or to avoid committing oneself; prevarication.
Her equivocation on the subject made him suspicious.  她在这个问题上的含糊其辞让他起了疑心。
近义词：prevarication, hedging, evasion, dodging
反义词：directness, honesty, forthrightness

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin aequivocatio(n-), from aequivocus ‘ambiguous’ (see equivocal).
