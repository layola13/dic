collectively:/kəˈlɛktɪvli/| adv.|共同地，集体地，总体地|in a way that involves or considers a group of people or things as a whole.|例句：The farmers collectively own the land. 农民们共同拥有这片土地。|例句：Collectively, they decided to boycott the meeting.  他们集体决定抵制会议。|近义词：together, jointly, combined, unitedly|反义词:individually, separately, singly|词性:副词
词源: From Latin collectivus, from collectus (past participle of colligere 'gather together') + -ive.
