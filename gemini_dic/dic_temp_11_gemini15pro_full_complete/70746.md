fine-tuning:/ˌfaɪnˈtjuːnɪŋ/ | n. | 微调，精调 | The process of making small adjustments to something to improve its performance or accuracy. |  The model needed some fine-tuning to improve its accuracy.  这个模型需要一些微调来提高其准确性。| 近义词: adjustment, refinement, calibration | 反义词: coarsening, roughing | 词性: 名词

fine-tuning:/ˌfaɪnˈtjuːnɪŋ/ | v. | 微调，精调 | To make small adjustments to something to improve its performance or accuracy. | They fine-tuned the engine to get better gas mileage. 他们微调了引擎以获得更好的油耗。 | 近义词: adjust, refine, calibrate | 反义词: coarsen, rough | 词性: 动词
