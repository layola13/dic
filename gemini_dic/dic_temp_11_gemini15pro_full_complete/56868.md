dory: /ˈdɔːri/ (US) /ˈdɔːri/ (UK)| n. | 多莉鱼，黄尾雀鲷，小船 | A small, flat-bottomed boat.  A brightly colored tropical fish, also known as a surgeonfish. | 例句：The fisherman rowed his dory back to shore. 渔夫划着他的小船回到了岸边。

dory: /ˈdɔːri/ (US) /ˈdɔːri/ (UK)| adj. | 昏沉的，迷糊的，傻乎乎的 (Informal, primarily US) |  Mentally confused or absent-minded. | 例句：I felt a bit dory after hitting my head. 我撞到头后感觉有点昏沉。

例句：He had a dory look on his face after the long flight. 长途飞行后，他脸上露出傻乎乎的表情。| 近义词：dazed, confused, foggy | 反义词: alert, sharp | 词性: adjective
