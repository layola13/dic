noncontumacious:/ˌnɒnkɒntjuːˈmeɪʃəs/
adjective
不抗拒的，不蔑视的，顺从的
Not contumacious; not stubbornly disobedient or rebellious.
The defendant's noncontumacious behavior impressed the judge.  被告不抗拒的行为给法官留下了深刻的印象。
The witness offered a noncontumacious apology to the court.  证人向法庭表达了顺从的歉意。
近义词: compliant, obedient, submissive, yielding
反义词: contumacious, defiant, rebellious, resistant
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *non contumax, from Latin non "not" + contumax "stubborn, rebellious".
