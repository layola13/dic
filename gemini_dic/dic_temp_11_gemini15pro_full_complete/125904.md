musculophrenic:/ˌmʌskjʊloʊˈfrɛnɪk/ |adj.|肌膈的|Relating to both the muscles and the diaphragm.  Formed from the combination of "musculo-" (relating to muscles) and "-phrenic" (relating to the diaphragm).|The musculophrenic artery supplies blood to the diaphragm and intercostal muscles. 肌膈动脉为膈肌和肋间肌供血。|The musculophrenic nerve innervates portions of the diaphragm and intercostal muscles. 肌膈神经支配部分膈肌和肋间肌。|phrenic, diaphragmatic| N/A|adjective
