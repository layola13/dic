jocko:/ˈdʒɒkoʊ/ (US), /ˈdʒɒkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|水手，猴子，大猩猩|A slang term for a sailor, also used to refer to a monkey or gorilla.|The old jocko climbed the mast with ease.  老水手轻松地爬上了桅杆。|近义词：sailor, monkey, ape, gorilla|反义词:landlubber (for sailor)|noun
