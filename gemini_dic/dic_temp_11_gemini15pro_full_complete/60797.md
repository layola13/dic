electroneutrality: /ɪˌlektrəʊnjuːˈtrælɪti/ | n. |电中性 |The state of having equal numbers of positive and negative charges, resulting in no net charge. |The principle of electroneutrality dictates that solutions must have a balance of positive and negative charges.  电中性原理规定溶液必须具有正负电荷的平衡。

The electroneutrality of the compound is maintained by the equal number of cations and anions. 该化合物的电中性是通过相同数量的阳离子和阴离子来维持的。 | 近义词: neutrality | 反义词: charged state | 词性: noun
