convalescence:/ˌkɒnvəˈles(ə)ns/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈles(ə)ns/ | n. | 康复期;恢复期 |The period of gradual recovery of health and strength after an illness or injury. |  After her surgery, she spent a month in convalescence.  手术后，她花了一个月的时间康复。|  recuperation, recovery, rehabilitation | relapse, deterioration | noun

convalescent:/ˌkɒnvəˈles(ə)nt/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈles(ə)nt/ | adj. | 康复中的 |Recovering from an illness or injury. | He is still convalescent after his heart attack. 他心脏病发作后仍在康复中。| recovering, recuperating, healing | sick, ill | adjective
