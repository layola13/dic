overenviously:/ˌoʊvərˈɛnviəsli/
adverb
过度羡慕地
in a way that shows too much envy
He stared overenviously at his neighbor's new car. 他过度羡慕地盯着邻居的新车。
She overenviously eyed her friend's designer handbag. 她嫉妒地盯着她朋友的名牌手提包。
Synonyms: jealously, covetously, resentfully
Antonyms: generously, admiringly, approvingly
词性: 副词
