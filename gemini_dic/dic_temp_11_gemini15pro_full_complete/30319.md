censoriously:/ˌsensɔːriəsli/
adverb
批判地，吹毛求疵地
In a manner that expresses disapproval or criticism, especially of someone's morals or behavior.
She looked at him censoriously, clearly disapproving of his attire. 她批判性地看了他一眼，显然不赞成他的穿着。
He censoriously commented on her lifestyle choices. 他吹毛求疵地评论她的生活方式选择。
近义词: critically, disapprovingly, judgmentally, faultfindingly
反义词: approvingly, supportively, uncritically

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *censoriosus, from Latin censor "censor" (from censēre "to assess, judge") + -orius.
