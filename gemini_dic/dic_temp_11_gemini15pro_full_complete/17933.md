beetlers:/ˈbiːtlərz/| n.|甲虫|Beetles are insects with hard, shell-like wing covers.|例句：The beetles crawled slowly across the leaf. 甲虫缓慢地爬过树叶。

例句：Many different species of beetles inhabit this forest.  这片森林里栖息着许多不同种类的甲虫。|近义词：insects, bugs|反义词:|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bītela, from Proto-Germanic *bītilą ("biter"), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeyd- ("to bite, split, cleave").  Related to "bite."  The plural form "beetles" is regular.
