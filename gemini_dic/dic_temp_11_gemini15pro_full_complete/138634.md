nucleotide:/ˈnjuːkliəˌtaɪd/ /ˈnuːkliəˌtaɪd/| n.|核苷酸|A compound consisting of a nucleoside linked to a phosphate group. Nucleotides form the basic structural unit of nucleic acids such as DNA.|DNA is made up of nucleotides. DNA由核苷酸组成。|近义词: base, monomer|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for nucleotide as a chemical compound.)|词性:noun
