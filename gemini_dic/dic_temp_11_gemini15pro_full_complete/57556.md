dramatic:/drəˈmætɪk/| adj.|戏剧的，引人注目的，急剧的|Relating to drama or the performance or study of drama. Striking in appearance or effect. Sudden and noticeable.|例句：The play had a dramatic ending.  这戏剧的结局引人注目。|近义词：theatrical, striking, impressive, sudden, sharp|反义词：undistinguished, unimpressive, gradual|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin dramaticus, from Greek dramatikos, from drama (see drama).
