lovebird:/ˈlʌvbɜːrd/| n.|爱情鸟，鹦鹉的一种|A small parrot of the genus *Agapornis*, typically brightly coloured and having a short tail.  These birds are known for forming strong pair bonds.|例句：The lovebirds were cuddling in their cage.  爱情鸟在笼子里依偎在一起。

例句：She bought a pair of lovebirds for her anniversary. 她为她的周年纪念买了一对爱情鸟。|近义词：parakeet, parrot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *love* + *bird*.  The name comes from the birds' strong pair bonds and the affectionate behavior they display towards their mates.
