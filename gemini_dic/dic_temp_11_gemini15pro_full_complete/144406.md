outstrode:/ˌaʊtˈstrəʊd/
verb
大步超过，阔步行走
walked with long steps so as to pass or get ahead of.
He outstrode his companions easily. 他轻松地超过了他的同伴们。
Synonyms: outpaced, outran, overtook, surpassed
Antonyms: lagged behind, trailed
词性: 动词
