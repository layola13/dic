nonprominences:/nɒnˈprɒmɪnənsɪz/
|n.|非突出部分；不显著之处|Areas of low relief or nonprominences on a surface.|The map highlighted the nonprominences in the terrain.|Synonym: depressions, hollows, concavities|Antonym: prominences, protuberances, projections
|noun|

nonprominence:/nɒnˈprɒmɪnəns/
|n.|非突出；不显著|The state of not being prominent or noticeable.|The nonprominence of the issue surprised everyone.|Synonym: insignificance, obscurity, unimportance|Antonym: prominence, salience, importance
|noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + prominence.  "Prominence" comes from Latin "prominentia,"  meaning "a standing out," from "prominere"  "to jut out, project," from "pro-" ("forward") + "minere" ("to project").
