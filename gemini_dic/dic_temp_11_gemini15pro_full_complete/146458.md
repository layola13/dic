overfrightened: /ˌoʊvərˈfraɪtnd/
adjective
过度惊吓的，吓坏了的
Excessively frightened; terrified.  This word emphasizes a state of fear beyond normal fright.
The overfrightened child clung to his mother.  吓坏了的孩子紧紧 clinging to his mother.
The dog, overfrightened by the fireworks, hid under the bed. 被烟花吓坏了的狗躲在床底下。
近义词: terrified, petrified, horror-stricken
反义词: calm, composed, unafraid
词性: adjective

overfrighten: /ˌoʊvərˈfraɪtn/
verb
过度惊吓，吓坏
To frighten excessively; to terrify.
The loud noise overfrightened the horses. 巨大的噪音吓坏了马匹。
Don't overfrighten the children with ghost stories. 不要用鬼故事吓坏孩子们。
近义词: terrify, petrify, scare
反义词: calm, soothe, comfort
词性: verb


词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "frightened" (past participle of "frighten," from Old English *fyrhtan "to frighten")
