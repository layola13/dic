hemitone:/ˈhemɪˌtəʊn/ (UK), /ˈhemɪˌtoʊn/ (US)| n.|半音|A semitone. The smallest interval used in Western music.  It is the difference in pitch between two adjacent notes on a piano keyboard (including the black keys).|The piece is written in C major, but with frequent hemitones creating a melancholic feel.|近义词:semitone, half step, half tone|反义词:whole tone, whole step|词性:noun

hemitonic:/ˌhemɪˈtɒnɪk/ (UK), /ˌhemɪˈtɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.|半音的|Relating to or consisting of semitones.|The hemitonic scale is often used in jazz and blues music.|近义词:semitonal|反义词:whole-tone|词性:adjective
