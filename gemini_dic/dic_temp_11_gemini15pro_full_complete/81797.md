grenadier:/ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪər/| n. |掷弹兵|A soldier equipped and trained to throw grenades. Also, a member of a regiment formerly armed with grenades.  Historically, a tall, strong soldier selected for his height and strength.|The grenadier was decorated for bravery. 这名掷弹兵因勇敢而被授勋。|Synonyms:  guardsman, trooper|Antonyms: civilian, noncombatant|Noun
