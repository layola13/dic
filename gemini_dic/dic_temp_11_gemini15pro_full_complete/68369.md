fallibly:/ˈfælɪbli/| adv.|易错地；可能出错地|in a way that is not perfect and is likely to make mistakes|Humans, being fallible, are prone to errors. 人类容易犯错，因此容易出错。|Example:  He spoke fallibly, admitting he didn't have all the answers. 他说得不确定，承认自己并非掌握所有答案。

Example: Even the most experienced doctors can diagnose fallibly. 即使是最有经验的医生也可能做出错误的诊断。|近义词：erroneously, imperfectly|反义词：infallibly, perfectly|词性:副词
