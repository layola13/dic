n.
日内瓦主义；加尔文主义；日内瓦学派
The doctrines and practices of Calvin or his followers.  加尔文或其追随者的教义和实践。
Geneva was a center of Calvinism, and the city's religious and political life was deeply influenced by Genevanism. 日内瓦是加尔文主义的中心，这座城市的宗教和政治生活深受日内瓦主义的影响。
Synonyms: Calvinism,  the Reformed tradition
Antonyms: Arminianism, Catholicism
