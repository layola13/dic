calippic: /kəˈlɪpɪk/ | n. | 讽刺诗，尖刻的攻击 | A short poem or writing characterized by bitter invective or malicious satire. |  His latest blog post was a pure calippic against the current political climate.  他的最新博客文章是对当前政治气候的纯粹讽刺。| Example: The disgruntled employee penned a scathing calippic against his former boss.  这位心怀不满的员工写了一篇尖刻的讽刺诗来抨击他的前任老板。 | Synonyms: diatribe, invective, philippic, tirade, harangue | Antonyms: eulogy, panegyric, encomium | noun
