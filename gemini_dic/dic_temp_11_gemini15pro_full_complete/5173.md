alleviation:/əˌliːviˈeɪʃən/
n.
减轻，缓和
the act of making something bad less severe
The alleviation of poverty is a key goal for the government. 政府的主要目标是减轻贫困。

近义词:relief, mitigation, ease, reduction
反义词:aggravation, exacerbation, intensification
