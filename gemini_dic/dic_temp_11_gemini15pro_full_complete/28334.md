caramelizations:/ˌkærəməlɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|焦糖化（作用），焦糖化产物|The process of caramelizing sugar, or the brown substances produced by this process.  The caramelizations on the crème brûlée were perfect.|The chef explained the science behind the caramelizations of onions.|近义词:browning, roasting|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用未焦糖化的 raw, uncooked 等)|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French carameliser, from caramel + -iser (verbal suffix).  Ultimately from Spanish caramelo, from Portuguese caramelo "burnt sugar," of uncertain origin.
