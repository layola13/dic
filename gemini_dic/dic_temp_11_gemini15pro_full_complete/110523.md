limbmeal:/ˈlɪmˌmiːl/| n. |肢解; 碎尸|The act of dismembering a body, especially as a form of violence or ritual.  The word evokes a gruesome image of a body torn apart, often associated with cannibalism or extreme violence.|The detective found evidence of limbmeal at the crime scene. 侦探在犯罪现场发现了肢解的证据。|The ancient tribe practiced limbmeal as part of their burial rituals.  这个古老的部落将肢解作为他们丧葬仪式的一部分。|近义词：dismemberment, mutilation|反义词: wholeness, integration|词性: noun


词源:  A compound word formed from "limb" (meaning a body part, especially an arm or leg) and "meal" (meaning a portion of food).  The combination creates a visceral image of a body being treated as food, implying a violent and dehumanizing act.
