dondo:/ˈdɒndoʊ/ (US), /ˈdɒndəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 咚咚声;  (非洲)一种双头鼓|A dull, resonant sound, like a drum; A type of double-headed drum used in Africa.|The dondo beat resonated through the village. 咚咚鼓声在村子里回荡。|The musicians played a rhythmic tune on the dondo.  音乐家们用咚咚鼓演奏了一曲节奏感强的曲子。|Synonyms: thump, thud, beat|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun
