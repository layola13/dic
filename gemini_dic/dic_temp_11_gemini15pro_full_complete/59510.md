eastnesses:/ˈiːst.nəs.ɪz/
noun
东部特征，东方特性
The quality of being east or eastern.  This word is rarely used and considered non-standard.
Due to its rare usage, providing natural examples is difficult. A contrived example could be:  "The eastnesses of the region are evident in the architecture and cuisine." 该地区的东方特征在建筑和美食中显而易见。
Near synonyms: eastern qualities, oriental characteristics
Antonyms: western qualities, occidental characteristics
