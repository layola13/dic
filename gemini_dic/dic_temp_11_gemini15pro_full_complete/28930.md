carrions:/ˈkæriənz/
n.
腐肉，动物尸体
decaying flesh of dead animals
Vultures were circling above the carrions. 秃鹫在腐肉上空盘旋。
近义词：carcasses, remains
反义词:  (No direct antonym for decaying flesh, could consider "living flesh")
