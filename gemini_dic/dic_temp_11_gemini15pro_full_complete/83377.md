gyrose:/ˈdʒaɪˌroʊs/ /ˈdʒaɪrəʊs/| adj.|旋转的，回旋的，螺旋形的|Marked by convex curves, sinuous lines, or spiral arrangement.  Having a circular or spiral form or motion.|The gyrose movements of the dancer were mesmerizing. 舞者的旋转动作令人着迷。|近义词：spiral, coiled, convoluted, winding, twisting|反义词:straight, linear|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *gyrosus, from Greek gyros "circle, ring."
