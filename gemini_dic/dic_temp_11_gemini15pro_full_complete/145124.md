overargumentativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌɑːrɡjʊˈmɛntətɪvnəs/| n. |过度争辩; 过于好辩|The tendency to argue excessively or unnecessarily.  A disposition to dispute or disagree, often to an excessive degree. |His overargumentativeness made it difficult to have a productive conversation. 他过度争辩使得很难进行富有成效的对话。|Synonyms: contentiousness, disputatiousness, quarrelsomeness|Antonyms: agreeableness, amiability, cooperativeness|noun
