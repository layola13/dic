measurability:/ˈmɛʒərəˈbɪləti/
n.
可衡量性；可测性
The quality of being measurable; the extent to which something can be measured.
The measurability of student learning outcomes is crucial for evaluating the effectiveness of educational programs.  学生学习成果的可衡量性对于评估教育项目的有效性至关重要。
The researchers questioned the measurability of happiness. 研究人员质疑幸福的可衡量性。

近义词:quantifiability, calculability
反义词:immeasurability, intangibility
词性:noun
