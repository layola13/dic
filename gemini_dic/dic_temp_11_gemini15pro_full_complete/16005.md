PHRASE->
ban on something | 对某事的禁令 | There is a ban on smoking in restaurants. 餐馆禁止吸烟。
lift the ban | 解除禁令 | The government decided to lift the ban on imported goods. 政府决定解除对进口商品的禁令.
impose a ban | 实施禁令 |  The city imposed a ban on overnight parking.  该市实施了夜间停车禁令。
under a ban | 在禁令之下 |  The sale of alcohol is under a ban.  酒类销售被禁止。
ban from something/doing something | 禁止某人做某事/禁止进入某地|He was banned from driving for six months. 他被禁止驾驶六个月。
