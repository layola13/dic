irreverent:/ɪˈrevərənt/ | adj. |不敬的，无礼的|Showing a lack of respect for people or things that are generally taken seriously.|  She cast an irreverent eye on the assembled dignitaries. 她对聚集的要人们投以不敬的目光。|近义词：disrespectful, impious, profane, blasphemous|反义词：reverent, respectful, pious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *irreverent-, from Latin irreverens, from in- ‘not’ + reverens, reverent- ‘revering’ (see reverent).
