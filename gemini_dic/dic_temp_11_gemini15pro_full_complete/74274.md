注意事项:

词源: fuzzy + -ness

Fuzziness is derived from the adjective "fuzzy," which itself likely originates from the Low German word "fussig," meaning "loose" or "spongy."  The "-ness" suffix transforms the adjective into a noun, indicating the state or quality of being fuzzy.
