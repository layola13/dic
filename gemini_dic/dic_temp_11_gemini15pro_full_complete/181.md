abaxial:/ˌæbˈæksiəl/
adjective
背轴的，远轴的
Situated away from the axis of an organ or organism; relating to the underside of a leaf.
The abaxial surface of a leaf is typically lighter in color than the adaxial surface. 叶子背轴面通常比近轴面颜色浅。
The botanist examined the abaxial side of the leaf to identify the species.  植物学家检查了叶子的背面以识别物种。
近义词: dorsal
反义词: adaxial
词性: adjective
