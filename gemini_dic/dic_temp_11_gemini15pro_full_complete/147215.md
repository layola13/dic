overmasterful: /ˌoʊvərˈmæstərfl/ | adj. | 专横的，盛气凌人的，不可抗拒的 | Excessively masterful; domineering.  Having overwhelming power or influence. | He had an overmasterful personality that made it difficult to work with him. 他专横跋扈的个性使人难以与他共事。 | The overmasterful urge to create drove him day and night.  不可抗拒的创作冲动驱使着他日夜不停地工作。 | 近义词: domineering, dictatorial, tyrannical, overpowering, overwhelming | 反义词: submissive, meek, humble, gentle | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + masterful
