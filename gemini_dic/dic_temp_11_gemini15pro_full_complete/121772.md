misrate:/ˌmɪsˈreɪt/ | v. | 错误地评估；低估；轻视 | To underestimate or undervalue; to assign too low a rate or value to. |  He misrated the importance of the meeting and arrived late. 他低估了会议的重要性，来晚了。

The company misrated the demand for their new product and produced too few units.  这家公司错误地估计了他们新产品的需求，生产得太少了。 | 近义词: undervalue, underestimate, misjudge, miscalculate | 反义词: overrate, overestimate, overvalue | verb
