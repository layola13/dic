concurs:/kənˈkɜːrz/
verb
同意，一致
agrees or expresses agreement with someone or something
The report concurs with previous findings.  该报告与先前的调查结果一致。
The two doctors concurred that the patient needed surgery. 两位医生一致认为病人需要手术。
近义词:agrees, coincides, corresponds
反义词:disagrees, differs, dissents
词性:v.
