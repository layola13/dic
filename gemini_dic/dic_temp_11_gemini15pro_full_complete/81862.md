gridlocked:/ˈɡrɪdˌlɒkt/ (US) /ˈɡrɪdˌlɒkt/ (UK)| adj. |交通堵塞的，陷入僵局的|A situation where streets are so full of traffic that nothing can move.|The city centre was completely gridlocked during rush hour. 市中心在高峰时段完全堵塞了。|The negotiations became gridlocked over the issue of pay.  谈判在薪酬问题上陷入了僵局。|Synonyms: congested, jammed, stalled, deadlocked|Antonyms: flowing, free-flowing|adjective
