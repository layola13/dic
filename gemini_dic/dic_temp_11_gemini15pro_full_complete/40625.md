convenors:/kənˈviːnəz/
| n. |召集人，会议召集人|A person who convenes or calls people together for a meeting or activity.|The convenors of the conference welcomed the participants from all over the world. 会议的召集人欢迎来自世界各地的与会者。|近义词：organizers, coordinators, chairmen, chairwomen, chairpersons|反义词: participant, attendee | noun

convenor:/kənˈviːnə(r)/
| n. |召集人，会议召集人|A person who convenes or calls people together for a meeting or activity.|The convenor scheduled the next meeting for Tuesday afternoon. 召集人将下次会议安排在星期二下午。|近义词：organizer, coordinator, chairman, chairwoman, chairperson|反义词: participant, attendee | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "convene" + "-or" (agent suffix).  "Convene" comes from the Old French "convenir,"  from Latin "convenīre" ("to come together," "assemble"), from "com-" ("together") + "venīre" ("to come").
