during:/ˈdjʊərɪŋ/| prep.|在…期间，在…的时候|Indicates a period of time within which something happens.|He worked hard during the summer. 他在夏天努力工作。|例句：She slept during the whole movie. 她看电影时睡了整场。|近义词：throughout, for, in, within, amidst|反义词：before, after|词性:介词

词源: From Middle English *duringe, present participle of duren (“to last, endure”), from Old French durer, from Latin dūrō (“to harden, to last, to endure”).
