decollated:/dɪˈkɒleɪtɪd/
adjective
斩首的，被砍头的
Having had the head cut off; beheaded.
The decollated body of the king was displayed as a warning to rebels. 国王的断头尸体被展示出来以警告叛乱分子。
Synonyms: beheaded, decapitated
Antonyms: intact, whole
词性: adjective

verb (past participle)
斩首，砍头
Behead. (This usage is less common than the adjective form)
He was decollated for treason. 他因叛国罪被斩首。 (This usage is less common)
Synonyms: beheaded, decapitated
Antonyms: spared, saved
