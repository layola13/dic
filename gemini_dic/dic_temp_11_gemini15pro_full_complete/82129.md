grokking:/ˈɡrɑːkɪŋ/| n. |深刻理解，彻底领悟|To understand something deeply and intuitively; to empathize with something so completely that you become one with it.|例句：After spending weeks with the code, I finally started grokking how it worked.  在花了数周时间研究代码后，我终于开始彻底理解它是如何工作的。

例句：She grokked the complexities of the situation immediately. 她立刻就明白了情况的复杂性。

|近义词：understanding, comprehending, grasping, internalizing, empathizing|反义词:misunderstanding, misinterpreting|词性:noun (derived from verb "grok")

grok:/ɡrɑːk/| v. |深刻理解，彻底领悟|To understand something deeply and intuitively; to empathize with something so completely that you become one with it.|例句：I didn't really grok what he was saying until he explained it again. 直到他再次解释，我才真正理解他在说什么。

例句：Experienced programmers can grok new languages quickly. 有经验的程序员可以很快地理解新的语言。

|近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp, internalize, empathize|反义词:misunderstand, misinterpret|词性:verb
