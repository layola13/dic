annats:/ˈænəts/| n. | 首年俸，第一年收入 | First year's profits or revenues, especially of a spiritual benefice. Historically, it referred to the first year's income of a church office given to the Pope. |  The annats from the newly appointed bishop were substantial. 新任命的主教的首年俸相当可观。| The king claimed the annats of vacant bishoprics. 国王要求获得空缺主教职位的第一年收入。 | synonyms: first fruits, income, revenue | antonyms: expenses, losses | noun
