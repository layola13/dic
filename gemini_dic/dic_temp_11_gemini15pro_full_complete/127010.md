nascency:/ˈneɪsənsi/ /ˈnæsənsi/| n.| 初生，起源，萌芽 |The nascency of the internet led to a boom in technology. 互联网的兴起导致了技术的蓬勃发展。| The company is still in its nascency.  这家公司仍处于初创阶段。 |  近义词：beginning, birth, origin, infancy, inception | 反义词：end, decline, maturity, death | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *nascentia, from nascens, present participle of nasci (“to be born”).
