constrained:/kənˈstreɪnd/| adj.|受约束的，被迫的，拘谨的|Limited or restricted, not free to act.  Forced or compelled to do something.  Showing constraint or reserve.|例句：His movements were constrained by the tight ropes. 他的行动被紧紧的绳索束缚住了。|例句：She felt constrained to accept the offer, even though she didn't really want to. 即使她并非真的想要，她也感到被迫接受了这个提议。|近义词：restricted, limited, forced, obliged, compelled, inhibited, restrained|反义词：free, unconstrained, uninhibited, relaxed|词性:形容词
