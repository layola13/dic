nontemporizingly:/nɒnˈtɛmpəraɪzɪŋli/
adv.
直截了当地，不拖延地
Without delay or evasion; directly and frankly.
The CEO nontemporizingly addressed the rumors of layoffs. 首席执行官直截了当地回应了裁员的传闻。
He nontemporizingly rejected the offer. 他直截了当地拒绝了这份提议。
近义词: directly, forthrightly, frankly, unequivocally, unhesitatingly
反义词: evasively, indirectly, hesitantly, procrastinatingly
词性: 副词

词源:
From non- + temporize + -ing + -ly.  "Temporize" comes from the Late Latin temporizāre, "to comply with the time," from Latin tempus, tempor- "time."  The prefix "non-" negates the verb, indicating a lack of temporizing (delaying).
