bardlike:/ˈbɑːrdlaɪk/| adj.|像吟游诗人的；诗意的|Resembling or characteristic of a bard; poetic.  Evoking the image or qualities of a traditional poet, especially in eloquence, musicality, or romanticism.|His bardlike pronouncements captivated the audience. 他那吟游诗人般的发言吸引了观众。|近义词：poetic, lyrical, rhapsodic, eloquent, musical|反义词：prosaic, mundane, unimaginative|词性:形容词
