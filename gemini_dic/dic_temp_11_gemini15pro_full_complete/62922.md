enlumine:/ɪˈluːmɪn/ /ɛnˈluːmɪn/ | vt.|照亮，启发；用鲜艳色彩装饰|To decorate (a book, manuscript, etc.) by adding elaborate designs and illustrations, often using gold or silver. To make something clearer or easier to understand.|The artist enlumined the manuscript with intricate gold leaf designs. 艺术家用复杂的金色叶子图案装饰了手稿。|近义词：illuminate, brighten, adorn, decorate|反义词：darken, obscure|动词
