It describes the digestive action of the stomach involving both hydrochloric acid and pepsin. |  Chloropeptic remedies may be helpful for indigestion. 氯消化性药物可能有助于消化不良。| Synonym: digestive, gastric | Antonym: anti-digestive | adjective
