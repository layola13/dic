cocounseling:/ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/
noun
共同咨询；朋侣咨询；互惠咨询
A form of peer counseling where two people take turns being the counselor and the client, providing each other with emotional support and helping each other work through personal issues.
例句：Cocounseling can be a valuable tool for personal growth. 共同咨询是个人成长的宝贵工具。

例句：They met weekly for cocounseling sessions. 他们每周见面进行共同咨询。
近义词：peer counseling, reciprocal counseling
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially: professional counseling, therapy)
词性:n.
