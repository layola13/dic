cabbage:/ˈkæbɪdʒ/ | n. | 卷心菜，甘蓝 | A leafy green, red (purple), or white (pale green) biennial plant grown as an annual vegetable crop for its dense-leaved heads. | Example: We had corned beef and cabbage for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了咸牛肉和卷心菜。

Example:  The cabbage patch was teeming with butterflies.  卷心菜地里满是蝴蝶。
