damodar:/ˈdɑːmoʊdɑːr/| n.|达摩达尔河，黑天神（Krishna的别名）|A major river of eastern India, rising in the Chota Nagpur plateau. Also, a name for Krishna, a Hindu deity.|例句：The Damodar River flows through the states of Jharkhand and West Bengal. 达摩达尔河流经贾坎德邦和西孟加拉邦。

例句：Damodar is another name for Krishna, the eighth avatar of Vishnu. 达摩达尔是黑天神的另一个名字，毗湿奴的第八个化身。|近义词：Krishna, Dark One (as an epithet)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
