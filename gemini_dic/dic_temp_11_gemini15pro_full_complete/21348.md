bloomy:/ˈbluːmi/| adj.|盛开的，繁花似锦的；青春焕发的；面色红润的|Covered with blooms; flourishing; having a healthy, rosy complexion.|例句：The bloomy meadows in springtime are a sight to behold. 春天盛开的草地是一道美丽的风景。|例句：Her bloomy cheeks suggested a youthful exuberance. 她红润的脸颊显示出青春的活力。|近义词：flowering, blossoming, flourishing, rosy, radiant|反义词：withered, faded, pale, pallid|词性：形容词

Etymology: From bloom + -y.  "Bloom" comes from Old Norse *blōm, meaning "flower, blossom."
