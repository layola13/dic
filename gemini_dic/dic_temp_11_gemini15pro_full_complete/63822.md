epididymitis:/ˌepɪdɪdɪˈmaɪtɪs/
n.
附睾炎
Inflammation of the epididymis, a tube located at the back of the testicle that stores and carries sperm.
He was diagnosed with epididymitis after experiencing scrotal pain.  他经历了阴囊疼痛后被诊断出患有附睾炎。
Epididymitis can be caused by bacterial infections or STIs. 附睾炎可能是由细菌感染或性传播感染引起的。
近义词:orchitis (睾丸炎),  testicular inflammation (睾丸炎症)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a medical condition like this)
词性:noun
