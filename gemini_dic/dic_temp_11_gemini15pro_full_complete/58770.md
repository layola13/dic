dunlin:/ˈdʌnlɪn/ | n. | 敦林;濱鷸 | A small wading bird (Calidris alpina) with a long, slightly downcurved bill, found in Arctic and subarctic regions. It breeds in the tundra and winters in coastal areas further south.  | I observed a flock of dunlins probing the mudflats for food. 我观察到一群滨鷸在泥滩上觅食。 |  We saw several dunlins foraging along the shoreline during our walk. 我们散步时看到几只滨鷸在海岸线上觅食。| 近义词:sandpiper, stint | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific bird species) | 词性:noun
