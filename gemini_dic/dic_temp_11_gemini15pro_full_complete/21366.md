blossomy: /ˈblɒsəmi/ (UK) /ˈblɑːsəmi/ (US) | adj. | 盛开的；多花的；像花的 | Covered in blossoms; resembling blossoms; flourishing. |  The cherry trees were blossomy and beautiful in the spring. 春天，樱花树开满了花，非常漂亮。| The fields were blossomy with wildflowers. 田野里开满了野花。 | 近义词: blooming, flowery, blossoming | 反义词: barren, leafless | 词性: 形容词
词源: From blossom + -y.  "blossom" comes from Old English *blōstm, from a Germanic base also found in Old High German bluost "blossom."
