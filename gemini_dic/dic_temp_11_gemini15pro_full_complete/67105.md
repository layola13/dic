explores:/ɪkˈsplɔːrz/
verb
探索，探究，考察
investigates systematically
The scientist explores the Amazon rainforest.  科学家探索亚马逊雨林。
近义词: investigates, researches, examines, discovers
反义词: ignores, neglects
词性: verb
词源: From Old French *explorer, from Latin explorare "search out, investigate," perhaps from ex- "out" + plorare "to cry out, weep, wail; bewail, lament."  The original notion perhaps being "to cry out and search for."
