greenlit: /ˈɡriːnlɪt/ /ˈɡriːnlɪt/ | v. adj.|批准，准许；被批准的，被准许的 | To give permission for something to happen, especially a movie or television show, to be made.  Derived from the practice of using a green light as a signal to proceed.  | The studio has finally greenlit the sequel.  电影制片厂终于批准了续集的拍摄。 | Synonyms: approve, authorize, sanction | Antonyms: reject, refuse, deny | verb

greenlit: /ˈɡriːnlɪt/ /ˈɡriːnlɪt/ | adj.|批准的，被准许的 | Having received permission to proceed. | The greenlit project is scheduled to begin filming next month.  获得批准的项目计划下个月开始拍摄。| Synonyms: approved, authorized, sanctioned | Antonyms: rejected, refused, denied | adjective
