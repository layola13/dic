kilo's:/ˈkiːləʊz/ | n. |公斤（的）|  The possessive form of kilo, indicating ownership or belonging.  A less common and generally informal way to express kilograms belonging to someone or something. | Example:  That's three kilo's of apples. 那是三公斤苹果。 | Synonym: kilograms (belonging to) | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement, but conceptually: less than a kilogram) | noun
