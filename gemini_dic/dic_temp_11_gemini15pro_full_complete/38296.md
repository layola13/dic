concave:/ˈkɑːnkeɪv/ /kɒnˈkeɪv/| adj. n.|凹的，凹面；凹形|Curved inwards, like the inner surface of a bowl.  A surface that curves inwards.|The mirror had a concave surface. 这面镜子有一个凹面。|近义词：hollow, recessed, indented|反义词:convex, bulging, protuberant|词性:形容词, 名词

concaves:/ˈkɑːnkeɪvz/ /kɒnˈkeɪvz/| n.|凹面（concave 的复数形式）|The plural form of "concave," referring to multiple inward curves or hollows.|The machine uses rotating concaves to separate the grain. 这台机器使用旋转的凹面来分离谷物。|近义词：hollows, depressions, indentations|反义词:convexities, bulges, protuberances|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *concavus, from Latin con- ‘together’ + cavus ‘hollow.’
