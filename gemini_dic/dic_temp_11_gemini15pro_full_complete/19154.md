bewailed:/bɪˈweɪld/
verb
恸哭，哀悼
express great sadness or disappointment about something.
She bewailed the loss of her beloved pet. 她为失去心爱的宠物而恸哭。
The nation bewailed the death of its leader.  举国上下哀悼领袖的逝世。

Synonyms: lament, mourn, grieve, deplore
Antonyms: rejoice, celebrate
词性: 动词
