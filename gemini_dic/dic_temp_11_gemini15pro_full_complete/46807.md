dankish:/ˈdæŋkɪʃ/| adj.|微湿的，阴冷的|Slightly damp and chilly; unpleasantly humid.|The basement had a dankish smell, like mildew and old earth. 地下室有一股潮湿的气味，像是霉菌和陈土的味道。| The air in the cave was dankish and cold.  洞穴里的空气阴冷潮湿。|近义词：damp, clammy, musty|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词

词源:
From dank + -ish.  Dank itself comes from Middle Low German or Middle Dutch *dank.
