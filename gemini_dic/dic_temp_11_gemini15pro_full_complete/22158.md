bombardiers:/ˌbɒm.bəˈdɪəz/ /ˌbɑːm.bərˈdɪrz/| n. |轰炸员，投弹手|A member of a bomber crew responsible for aiming and releasing bombs.|The bombardiers carefully calculated their target before releasing the payload. 轰炸员在投放有效载荷之前仔细计算了他们的目标。|例句：The skilled bombardiers ensured the bombs hit their targets accurately.  熟练的轰炸员确保炸弹准确击中目标。

例句：During World War II, bombardiers played a crucial role in strategic bombing campaigns. 在二战期间，轰炸员在战略轰炸行动中发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：bomber crews, aircrews|反义词:None|词性:Noun
