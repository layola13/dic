forbidder:/fərˈbɪdər/| n.|禁止者，阻止者|Someone who forbids something.  The forbidder of the activity was unpopular.  那个禁止这项活动的人不受欢迎。|Synonyms:  prohibitor, interdictor|Antonyms:  permitter, authorizer|noun

forbid:/fərˈbɪd/| v.|禁止，阻止|To command (someone) not to do something.  I forbid you to go. 我禁止你去。|Synonyms:  prohibit, ban, disallow|Antonyms:  permit, allow, authorize|verb
