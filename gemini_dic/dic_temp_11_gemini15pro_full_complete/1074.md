acceptableness:/əkˌsɛptəbəlˈnɛs/ |n.|可接受性，合意性|The quality of being acceptable or satisfactory.|The acceptableness of the proposal was debated for hours.  这个提议的可接受性被讨论了好几个小时。|近义词：acceptability, suitability, appropriateness|反义词：unacceptability, unsuitability, inappropriateness|词性: 名词

Etymology: From acceptable + -ness.  Acceptable derives from the Latin *acceptabilis*, from acceptāre "to accept," from ad- "to" + capiō "to take."
