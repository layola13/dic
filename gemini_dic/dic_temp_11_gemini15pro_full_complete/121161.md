misdiagnose: /ˌmɪsˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊz/ (US) /ˌmɪsˌdaɪəɡˈnəʊz/ (UK)| vt.|误诊|To diagnose incorrectly; to give a wrong diagnosis for (an illness or other condition).|The doctor misdiagnosed her illness as the flu. 医生误诊了她的病，认为是流感。|The specialist misdiagnosed the patient's condition, leading to inappropriate treatment.  专家误诊了病人的病情，导致了不恰当的治疗。| 近义词: misidentify, mistake|反义词: diagnose correctly|词性: verb
