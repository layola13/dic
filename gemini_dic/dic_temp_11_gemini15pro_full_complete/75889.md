fundamentalist:/ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst/| n. adj.|原教旨主义者；基要主义的|A person who believes in the strict, literal interpretation of religious texts.  A person who adheres strictly to the basic principles of any subject or discipline.|例句：The fundamentalists protested against the new curriculum. 原教旨主义者抗议新课程。|近义词：dogmatist, traditionalist, orthodox|反义词：liberal, modernist, reformer|词性: noun, adjective

fundamentalist:/ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst/| adj.|基要主义的|Adhering strictly to the basic principles of any subject or discipline.|例句：He has a fundamentalist approach to economics. 他对经济学采取基要主义的方法。|近义词：strict, rigid, inflexible|反义词：flexible, adaptable, pragmatic|词性: adjective
