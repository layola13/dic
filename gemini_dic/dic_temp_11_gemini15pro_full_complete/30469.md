centrifuges:/ˈsɛntrɪˌfjuːdʒɪz/| n.|离心机|A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.|The lab uses centrifuges to separate blood components. 实验室使用离心机分离血液成分。|近义词：separator, extractor|反义词:|noun

centrifuge:/ˈsɛntrɪˌfjuːdʒ/| v.|离心分离|To rotate something at high speed to separate its components.|They centrifuged the sample to isolate the DNA. 他们对样本进行离心分离以提取DNA。|近义词：spin, whirl|反义词:|verb
