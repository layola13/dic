megagrants:/ˈmɛɡəɡrænts/ | n. |巨额赠款|Large grants, typically given by governments or foundations to support significant projects, often in research or education.|The university received a megagrant to fund its groundbreaking research on artificial intelligence.|Synonyms: substantial grants, major grants |Antonyms: small grants, minor grants |noun

megagrants:/ˈmɛɡəɡrænts/ | n. |巨额拨款|Large sums of money granted, especially by a government, for a particular purpose.|The government awarded megagrants to several universities for scientific research.|Synonyms:  large grants, massive grants |Antonyms: small grants, micro-grants |noun
