muesli: /ˈmjuːzli/ (UK), /ˈmjuːsli/ (US)| n. | 木斯利，瑞士燕麦片 | A breakfast cereal consisting of rolled oats, nuts, dried fruits, and sometimes bran, typically eaten with milk or yogurt. | I had muesli and yogurt for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上吃了木斯利和酸奶当早餐。|  Example: She prefers muesli to other breakfast cereals because it's healthier. 她比起其他早餐麦片更喜欢木斯利，因为它更健康。|Synonym: granola, cereal| Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider cooked breakfast items like eggs, bacon)| Noun
