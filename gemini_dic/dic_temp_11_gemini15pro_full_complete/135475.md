nonpharmaceutical:/ˌnɒnfɑːməˈsuːtɪkəl/
adjective
非药物的
Not involving or relying on drugs or pharmaceutical products.
例句：
Nonpharmaceutical interventions like handwashing are crucial in preventing the spread of infection.
非药物干预措施，如洗手，对于预防感染的传播至关重要。

The government is promoting nonpharmaceutical measures to combat the flu.
政府正在推广非药物措施来对抗流感。

近义词：nondrug
反义词：pharmaceutical

词性: adjective
