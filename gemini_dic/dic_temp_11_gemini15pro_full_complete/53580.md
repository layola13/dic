disbowel:/dɪsˈbaʊəl/ | vt. | 取出内脏；剖腹；使…失去力量 | To remove the bowels or entrails of; eviscerate.  Figuratively, to deprive of essential content or effectiveness. | The gruesome scene showed the soldier disboweling his enemy.  这可怕的场景显示了士兵剖开了敌人的肚子。

The new regulations have disbowelled the power of the local council. 新规定使得地方议会失去了权力。 | 近义词：eviscerate, disembowel, gut | 反义词：N/A | 词性：动词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing reversal) + bowel (intestines).  Ultimately from Latin *botellus (sausage, intestine).
