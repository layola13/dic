aphakial: /eɪˈfeɪkiəl/ | adj. | 无晶状体的 | Relating to or having the condition of aphakia, the absence of the lens of the eye. |  After cataract surgery, the patient's eye was aphakial.  术后，病人的眼睛处于无晶状体状态。| Example: The aphakial eye requires a strong corrective lens.  无晶体眼睛需要强矫正镜片。| Synonyms: lensless | Antonyms: lenticular | adjective

aphakia: /əˈfeɪkiə/ | n. | 无晶状体 | Absence of the lens of the eye. | The aphakia was corrected with an intraocular lens.  无晶状体通过人工晶状体得到矫正。| Example:  Aphakia can result from trauma or congenital defects. 无晶状体可能是外伤或先天性缺陷造成的。| Synonyms: absence of lens | Antonyms: presence of lens | noun
