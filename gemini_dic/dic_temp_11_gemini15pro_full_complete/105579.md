kindhearted:/ˌkaɪndˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|仁慈的，好心的|Having a kind and sympathetic nature; compassionate.|She's a kindhearted woman who always helps those in need. 她是一位心地善良的女人，总是帮助那些需要帮助的人。|近义词：compassionate, benevolent, charitable, sympathetic, warmhearted|反义词：cruel, heartless, unkind, ruthless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From kind +‎ hearted.  Kind meaning "caring, gentle, considerate" dates back to Old English *gecynde meaning "natural, innate, inborn." Hearted meaning "having a specified kind of heart" dates back to the late 14th century.
