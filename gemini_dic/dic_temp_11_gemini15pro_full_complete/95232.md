inburst:/ˈɪnbɜːrst/| n.|爆发，突然增长|A sudden bursting in or into something; a sudden increase or intensification.|例句：There was an inburst of light as the door opened. 门打开时，光线突然涌入。

例句：The company experienced an inburst of new customers after their successful advertising campaign.  公司在成功的广告宣传活动后，迎来了新客户的激增。|近义词：upsurge, surge, outbreak, burst, eruption|反义词: decline, decrease, reduction|词性:名词

词源: in- (prefix meaning "in, into") + burst (noun meaning "a sudden outbreak")
