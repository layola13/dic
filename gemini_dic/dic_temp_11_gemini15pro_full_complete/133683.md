nonfraudulency:/ˌnɑːnˌfrɔːdʒəˈlɛnsi/ | n. | 无欺诈; 真实性; 诚实 |  The state or quality of not being fraudulent; genuineness; honesty. | The nonfraudulency of the transaction was confirmed by the bank.  这笔交易的真实性得到了银行的确认。 | synonyms: honesty, integrity, probity, veracity, trustworthiness | antonyms: fraudulence, dishonesty, deceit, deception | noun

nonfraudulent:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɔːdʒələnt/ | adj. | 不欺诈的; 真实的; 诚实的 | Not fraudulent; genuine; honest. | The contract was deemed nonfraudulent by the court. 法院认为该合同是真实的。 | synonyms: honest, genuine, authentic, legitimate, valid | antonyms: fraudulent, dishonest, deceptive, bogus, fake | adjective
