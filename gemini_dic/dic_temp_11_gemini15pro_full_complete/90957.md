hurrahed:/hʊˈreɪd/ /hʊˈrɑːd/| v. |欢呼，喝彩|To express enthusiastic approval or congratulations, typically by shouting "hurrah."|The crowd hurrahed as the team scored the winning goal.  人群为球队攻入制胜一球而欢呼。|The soldiers were hurrahed by the crowds as they returned home. 士兵们回家时受到群众的热烈欢迎。|近义词：cheered, acclaimed, applauded|反义词：booed, jeered|词性：动词

词源: From the exclamation "hurrah," of uncertain origin, possibly from a Slavic or Germanic source.
