interregimental:/ˌɪntərˌrɛdʒɪˈmɛntəl/
adjective
团际的，不同兵团之间的
Existing or occurring between regiments.
例句：Interregimental sports competitions foster camaraderie among soldiers from different units. 团际体育比赛促进了来自不同单位的士兵之间的友谊。

例句：The interregimental rivalry added an extra layer of excitement to the annual military exercises.  团际竞争为年度军事演习增添了额外的刺激。
近义词：interunit, between regiments
反义词：intraregimental
词性: adjective
