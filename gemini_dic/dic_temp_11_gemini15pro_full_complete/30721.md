ceruminous:/səˈruːmɪnəs/ /sɪˈrumɪnəs/
adjective
耳垢的；产生耳垢的
relating to or consisting of earwax.
Earwax, also known by the medical term cerumen, is a brown, orange, red, yellowish or gray waxy substance secreted in the ear canal of humans and other mammals.
例句：Ceruminous glands secrete a waxy substance.  耳垢腺分泌一种蜡状物质。

例句：Excessive ceruminous buildup can cause hearing problems. 耳垢堆积过多会导致听力问题。

近义词：waxy, earwax-producing
反义词：None

词性: adjective
