outstudying: /ˌaʊtˈstʌdiɪŋ/
verb
比...学习更努力
Surpassing someone in studying; studying more diligently or effectively than another.
He was outstudying everyone in his class, determined to get the top grade. 他比班上任何人都努力学习，决心获得最高分。
She outstudied her classmates and aced the final exam. 她学习比同学们更努力，并且在期末考试中得了高分。
近义词: outworking, surpassing
反义词: slacking, neglecting
