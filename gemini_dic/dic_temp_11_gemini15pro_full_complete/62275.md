encyclopedism:/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪzəm/ | n. | 百科全书主义；百科全书式知识； | A system of organizing knowledge that resembles an encyclopedia, often characterized by comprehensiveness and superficiality.  A belief in or advocacy of such a system. | He criticized the encyclopedism of the curriculum, arguing that it lacked depth. 他批评了课程的百科全书式，认为它缺乏深度。| The sheer encyclopedism of his knowledge was astounding. 他渊博的知识令人震惊。 | synonyms: comprehensiveness, polymathy | antonyms: specialization, narrowness | noun
