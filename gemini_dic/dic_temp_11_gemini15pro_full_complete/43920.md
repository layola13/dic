crew:/kruː/
n.|全体船员，全体机组人员，工作人员，一帮人，一伙人|A group of people who work together, especially one that operates a ship or aircraft, or a group of people working on a film or television programme. |The ship's crew was very experienced. 这艘船的船员经验非常丰富。

The film crew was setting up for the next scene.  电影摄制组正在为下一个场景做准备。

近义词:team, staff, group, gang, band
反义词:passengers, audience
词性:名词
