dilapidator:/dɪˈlæpɪdeɪtər/
n.| dilapidator | someone who causes a building or property to fall into ruin or disrepair | The dilapidator was sued by the property owners for neglecting necessary repairs.  这个 dilapidator 被业主起诉，因为他疏于必要的维修。| synonyms: vandal, spoiler, ruiner | antonyms: restorer, renovator | noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *dilapidator, from Latin dilapidare "to squander, scatter like stones," from dis- "apart" + lapidare "to throw stones," from lapis "stone."
