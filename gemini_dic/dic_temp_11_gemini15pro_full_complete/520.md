absolute:/ˈæbsəluːt/
adjective|绝对的，完全的，无条件的；专制的，不受任何限制的|Completely and totally; without limit or restriction; autocratic, despotic.
|He ruled with absolute power. 他拥有绝对的权力。
|The show was an absolute disaster. 演出完全是一场灾难。

synonym:complete, total, utter, perfect, pure, unqualified, unconditional, unrestricted, unlimited, outright, downright, thorough, thoroughgoing, consummate, sheer, unmitigated, undiluted
antonym:relative, qualified, conditional, limited, restricted


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English absolut, from Latin absolūtus (“loosened from, detached, complete, perfect, finished”), past participle of absolvere (“to loosen, detach, acquit, finish”), from ab- (“away from”) + solvere (“to loosen, detach, untie”).
