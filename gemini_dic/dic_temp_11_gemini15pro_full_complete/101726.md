irresistabilities:/ˌɪrɪˌzɪstəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |无法抗拒的事物；诱惑力|The irresistibilities of the chocolate cake were too much for him to overcome.  巧克力蛋糕的诱惑力让他无法抗拒。|例句：The irresistibilities of the city drew her in.  这座城市的魅力吸引了她。

The many irresistibilities of the buffet made it impossible to stick to my diet.  自助餐的诸多诱惑让我无法坚持我的饮食计划。|近义词：allurements, temptations, attractions|反义词:repulsiveness,  unattractiveness|词性:名词

Etymology: From "irresistible" + "-ity" (forming abstract nouns of quality) + "-ies" (plural suffix).  "Irresistible" comes from "ir-" (not) + "resist" (from Latin "resistere," to stand back, withstand) + "-ible" (capable of).
