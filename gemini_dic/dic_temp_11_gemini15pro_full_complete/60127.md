effecting:/ɪˈfɛktɪŋ/
v.
实现；产生；造成
Bringing about; causing.
The new CEO is effecting major changes in the company. 新任首席执行官正在公司进行重大变革。
Synonyms: achieving, accomplishing, realizing, executing, performing, implementing, bringing about
Antonyms: hindering, obstructing, preventing, impeding
词性: 动词

注意事项:
"Effecting" is often confused with "affecting". "Affecting" means to influence or to produce a change in something. "Effecting" means to cause something to happen; to bring about.  This distinction is crucial for proper usage.
