nonscented:/ˌnɒnˈsɛntɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛntɪd/| adj.|无香味的，无气味的|Having no scent or fragrance.  Lacking added perfume or fragrance.|例句：She prefers nonscented lotions for sensitive skin. 她偏爱用于敏感肌肤的无香味乳液。

例句：The hospital uses nonscented soap. 医院使用无香味肥皂。


近义词：unscented, fragrance-free, odorless|反义词:scented, fragrant|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
scent (noun/verb): From Old French sentir "to feel, perceive, smell," from Latin sentire "to feel, perceive, find out," related to sensus "feeling, sense."
-ed (suffix):  Forms the past participle of verbs or adjectives meaning "having, characterized by."
