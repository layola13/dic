dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ /dɪˈluːt/
verb
稀释；冲淡；削弱
To make a liquid thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent. To reduce the strength or effectiveness of something.
The company diluted its brand by offering cheaper products.  这家公司通过提供更便宜的产品削弱了其品牌。
The artist diluted the paint with water.  这位艺术家用水稀释了颜料。
近义词:thin, weaken, water down, adulterate
反义词:concentrate, strengthen, thicken
词性:v.

dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ /dɪˈluːt/
adjective
稀释的；冲淡的
Thinned or weakened by adding water or another solvent.
The dilute solution was not strong enough to clean the stain.  这种稀释的溶液不足以清除污渍。
He gave a dilute explanation of the complex issue. 他对这个复杂的问题作了一个简略的解释。
近义词:thin, weak, watered-down
反义词:concentrated, strong, thick
词性:adj.
