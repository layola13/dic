greatness:/ˈɡreɪtnəs/| n. |伟大，杰出，巨大|The state of being great; eminence or distinction.|His greatness as a leader was undeniable. 他作为领袖的伟大是不可否认的。|synonyms: eminence, distinction, magnitude, grandeur, excellence|antonyms: insignificance, mediocrity, inferiority|noun

Etymology: From Middle English *gretnes*, from Old English *grēatnes*, equivalent to *great* + *-ness*. Cognate with Scots *gritness* (“greatness”).
