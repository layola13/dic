**Note:** "interpols" itself isn't a standard English word. It seems to be a conjugated form of "interpol" which, while referring to the organization, is more commonly used as a verb meaning "to insert something between other things," especially in text.  The correct plural for the organization would simply be "Interpol".  The provided analysis covers the various forms of "interpol".
