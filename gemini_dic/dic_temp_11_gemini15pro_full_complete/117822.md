mellay:/ˈmeɪleɪ/| n.|混战，格斗，乱斗|A confused fight, skirmish, or scuffle; a tumultuous mingling.|The knights charged into the mellay, swords clashing against shields.|近义词：brawl, fray, fracas, melee, tussle|反义词：peace, truce, calm|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle French *meslee* (modern French *mêlée*), past participle of *mesler* "to mix," from Vulgar Latin *misculāre*, from Latin *miscellus* "mixed."
