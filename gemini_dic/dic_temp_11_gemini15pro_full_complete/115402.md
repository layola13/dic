mansuete:/mænˈswiːt/ /mænˈsjuːɪt/| adj.|温顺的，驯服的，柔和的|Gentle and tame; not wild or violent.  Originating from Latin *mansuetus*, meaning "tame, gentle, mild."  It's related to the word "manus" (hand) and suggests an animal that can be handled easily.|The farmer had a mansuete horse that even children could ride.|近义词：tame, gentle, docile, meek, mild|反义词：wild, fierce, aggressive, unruly|词性:形容词
