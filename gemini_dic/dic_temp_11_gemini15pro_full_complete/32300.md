achieve: /əˈtʃiːv/
verb
实现，达到，完成
succeed in doing or getting something, typically by effort, skill, or courage.
He achieved his ambition to become a doctor. 他实现了成为一名医生的抱负。
The company has achieved considerable success in this market. 该公司在这个市场取得了相当大的成功。
近义词: accomplish, attain, reach, fulfill, realize
反义词: fail, forfeit

词性: verb
词源: From Old French achever “to finish, complete, carry out, come to an end,” from a- (from Latin ad “to”) + chief “head, end,” from Latin caput “head.”
