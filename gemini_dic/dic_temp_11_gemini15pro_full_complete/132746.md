nondocumented's:/ˌnɒnˈdɒkjʊmɛntɪdz/ | n. | 无证人员的家属（或财产） |  Possessions or family members of a person who does not have the legal documents required for residence or employment in a particular country.  This word is grammatically unusual and generally considered informal or non-standard. It is formed by adding the possessive suffix -'s to "nondocumented," which itself is an adjective.  |  The nondocumented's children were also at risk of deportation.  无证人员的孩子也有被驱逐出境的危险。 |  Synonyms: (No direct equivalent, rephrase as "belongings of undocumented immigrants," "families of undocumented immigrants") | Antonyms: (None) | noun

nondocumented:/ˌnɒnˈdɒkjʊmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 无证件的；未登记的|  Not having the necessary official documents, especially those proving identity, citizenship, or legal residence/employment. | The nondocumented worker was afraid to go to the hospital.  这位无证工人不敢去医院。| Synonyms: illegal, unauthorized, undocumented | Antonyms: legal, documented, authorized | adjective
