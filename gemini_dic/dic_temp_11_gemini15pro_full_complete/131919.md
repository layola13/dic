noncontingent:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪndʒənt/
| adj. | 无条件的，非偶然的 | Not dependent on something else; not contingent. |  Her salary is noncontingent, meaning she receives it regardless of her performance. 她的薪水是无条件的，这意味着无论她的表现如何，她都会收到。 |  The noncontingent nature of the law makes it universally applicable.  该法律的无条件性质使其普遍适用。 |  absolute, unconditional, independent, guaranteed | conditional, dependent, contingent | adjective
