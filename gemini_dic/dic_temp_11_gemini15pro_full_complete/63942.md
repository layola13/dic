episcopacy:/ɪˈpɪskəpəsi/
n.
主教制；主教的职位；主教的任期
Government by bishops; the office of a bishop; the term of a bishop's office.
The Church of England maintains episcopacy.  英国教会维持主教制。
Synonyms: bishopric, prelacy
Antonyms: congregationalism
