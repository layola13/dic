infallibly:/ɪnˈfælɪbli/| adv.|无误地，绝对可靠地|in a way that is always right and never wrong; without making mistakes|例句：The test infallibly predicts the presence of the disease.  这项测试能准确无误地预测疾病的存在。

例句：He infallibly knew what she was thinking. 他总是能准确无误地知道她在想什么。


近义词：unerringly, certainly, reliably, accurately, faultlessly|反义词：erroneously, inaccurately, fallibly|词性:副词


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin infallibiliter, from infallibilis (see infallible).
