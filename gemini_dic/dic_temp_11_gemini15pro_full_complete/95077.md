inaffability:/ˌɪnˌæfəˈbɪləti/
n.
难以亲近；不和蔼；粗鲁无礼
The quality of being unapproachable and unfriendly; aloofness or surliness.
His inaffability made it difficult for people to approach him. 他的不友善让人难以接近他。
近义词：unfriendliness, aloofness, coldness, surliness, hostility
反义词：affability, friendliness, warmth, approachability, sociability
词性：名词

inaffable:/ˌɪnˈæfəbl/
adj.
难以亲近的；不和蔼的；粗鲁无礼的
Not friendly or approachable; aloof or surly.
He had an inaffable manner that discouraged conversation.  他态度冷淡，令人不愿与之交谈。
近义词：unfriendly, aloof, cold, surly, hostile
反义词：affable, friendly, warm, approachable, sociable
词性：形容词

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French inaffable, from Latin inaffabilis, from in- ‘not’ + affabilis (from affari ‘address’).
