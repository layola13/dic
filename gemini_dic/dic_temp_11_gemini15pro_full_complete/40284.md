continuedness:/kənˈtɪnjuːdnəs/| n.|持续性，连续性|The state or quality of being continued; uninterrupted succession or duration.|The continuedness of the policy ensured stability in the market. 该政策的持续性确保了市场的稳定性。|continuity, persistence|discontinuity, interruption|noun

continue:/kənˈtɪnjuː/| v.|继续，持续|To persist in an activity or process; to resume after interruption.|The rain continued all night. 雨持续了一整夜.  He continued reading after a short break. 短暂休息后，他继续看书。|persist, proceed, resume|stop, cease, halt|verb
