Control of a political organization or machine by a boss. |The city was plagued by bossism and corruption for decades. 这座城市数十年来一直饱受老板作风和腐败的困扰。|The investigation exposed the widespread bossism in the construction industry.  调查揭露了建筑行业普遍存在的老板作风。|近义词：dictatorship, tyranny, authoritarianism, despotism|反义词：democracy, egalitarianism|词性:名词
