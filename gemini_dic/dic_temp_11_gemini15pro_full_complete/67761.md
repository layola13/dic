eyeflap:/ˈaɪˌflæp/| n.|眼罩; 遮眼罩|A piece of material, often leather, that is fitted over a horse's eye, usually to prevent it from seeing sideways and becoming startled.|The racehorse wore an eyeflap to help it focus on the track ahead. 赛马戴着眼罩，以帮助它专注于前方赛道。|Synonyms: blinker, blinder, winkers|Antonyms: N/A|noun
