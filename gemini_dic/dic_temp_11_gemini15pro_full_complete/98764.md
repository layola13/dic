intakes:/ˈɪnteɪks/| n.|摄入量，吸入量，录取人数|An amount taken in.  The process of taking something in.|例句：The daily intake of calories should be controlled.  每日卡路里摄入量应该控制。|近义词：consumption, ingestion|反义词:output, excretion|词性:名词

intakes:/ˈɪnteɪks/| n.|（尤指机构的）新生，新成员|A group of people who have recently joined an organization or institution, especially a school or college.|例句：The university's intake of students has increased this year.  今年大学的招生人数增加了。|近义词：newcomers, entrants|反义词:graduates, leavers|词性:名词
