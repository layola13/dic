eagle: /ˈiːɡəl/ (US) /ˈiːɡl/ (UK)| n.|鹰|A large bird of prey with a hooked beak and powerful talons, known for its sharp eyesight and soaring flight.  It is often used as a symbol of strength, freedom, and national power.|The bald eagle is a symbol of the United States. 白头鹰是美国的象征。|The eagle soared high above the mountains.  鹰在群山上空翱翔。|近义词：hawk, falcon, raptor|反义词: dove (symbolically)|词性: noun
