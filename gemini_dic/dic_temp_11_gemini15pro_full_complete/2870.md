adverse:/ˈædvɜːs/ (US) /ˈædvɜːs/ (UK)| adj.|不利的，有害的，相反的|Preventing success or development; harmful; unfavorable.|例句：Adverse weather conditions forced the cancellation of the flight.  恶劣的天气条件迫使航班取消。

例句：He had an adverse reaction to the medication. 他对药物产生了不良反应。


近义词：unfavorable, detrimental, negative, hostile, contrary|反义词：favorable, beneficial, positive, helpful, supporting|词性：形容词

adversest:/ˈædvɜːsɪst/ (US) /ˈædvɜːsɪst/ (UK) |adj.|最不利的，最有害的 (adverse的最高级)|Most unfavorable, most harmful.| 例句：This was the adversest weather I had ever experienced. 这是我经历过的最恶劣的天气。

例句：They overcame the adversest of conditions to achieve their goal.  他们克服了最不利的条件，实现了目标。

近义词：most unfavorable, most detrimental, most negative |反义词：most favorable, most beneficial, most positive| 词性：形容词


词源: From Latin *adversus* (“turned towards, opposite, unfavorable, hostile, adverse”), past participle of *advertere* (“to turn towards”), from *ad-* (“to, towards”) + *vertere* (“to turn”).
