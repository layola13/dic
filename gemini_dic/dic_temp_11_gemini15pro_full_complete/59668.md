echinoderms:/ɪˈkaɪnəˌdɜːrmz/
|n.|棘皮动物|A marine invertebrate of the phylum Echinodermata, such as a starfish, sea urchin, or sea cucumber, having a radiating arrangement of parts and a skeleton of calcareous plates.|Starfish and sea urchins are echinoderms. 海星和海胆是棘皮动物。|Synonyms:  marine invertebrates|Antonyms: vertebrates|noun
