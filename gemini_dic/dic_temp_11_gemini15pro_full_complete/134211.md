nonindigenous:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdɪdʒɪnəs/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈdɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
非本土的，非原生的，外来的
Not native or original to a particular place; introduced from elsewhere.
The nonindigenous plant species are crowding out the native flora.  这些非本土植物物种正在排挤本土植物群。
The island's ecosystem is threatened by nonindigenous predators.  该岛的生态系统受到外来捕食者的威胁。

近义词:foreign, alien, exotic, introduced, non-native
反义词:indigenous, native, endemic
词性:adjective
