counter-indicate:/ˌkaʊntərˈɪndɪkeɪt/| v. |禁忌; 禁用; 指明禁忌|To make (a treatment or procedure) inadvisable.|The patient's history of bleeding counter-indicates the use of this medication.  这位病人有出血史，禁用这种药物。|Synonyms: proscribe, forbid, advise against|Antonyms: indicate, suggest, recommend|verb

counter-indication:/ˌkaʊntərˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |禁忌症|A factor that makes a treatment or procedure inadvisable.|High blood pressure is a counter-indication for this drug. 高血压是这种药物的禁忌症。|Synonyms: contraindication|Antonyms: indication|noun
