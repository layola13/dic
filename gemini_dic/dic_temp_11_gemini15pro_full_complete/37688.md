companion:/kəmˈpænɪən/ (US), /kəmˈpænɪən/ (UK)| n.|同伴，伴侣，指南|A person or animal with whom one spends a lot of time or with whom one travels.|She went travelling with a companion. 她和一个同伴去旅行了。|Synonyms: friend, partner, associate, comrade, mate|Antonyms: enemy, foe, opponent|noun

companion:/kəmˈpænɪən/ (US), /kəmˈpænɪən/ (UK)| adj.|同行的，陪伴的|Existing or occurring together.|Companion volume 同配套的书|Synonyms: accompanying, attendant, related|Antonyms: separate, detached|adjective
