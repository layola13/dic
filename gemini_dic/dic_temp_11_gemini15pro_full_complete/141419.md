ophiuran:/ˌɒfiˈjʊərən/ | n. | 蛇尾纲动物；海蛇尾 | Any of various echinoderms of the class Ophiuroidea, having a body with a central disk and five slender, flexible arms.  These creatures are closely related to starfish. |  Example: The ophiuran quickly retreated into its crevice when the diver approached.  海蛇尾在潜水员靠近时迅速退回到裂缝中。

Example:  Researchers studied the regenerative abilities of the ophiuran's arms. 研究人员研究了海蛇尾腕的再生能力。 |  Synonyms: brittle star, serpent star | Antonyms:  N/A | Noun
