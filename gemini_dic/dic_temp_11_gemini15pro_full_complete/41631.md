correctibility:/ˌkɔːrɛktɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
可纠正性；可改正性
The quality of being able to be corrected or rectified.
The software has high correctibility, allowing users to easily fix errors.  这个软件有很高的可纠正性，允许用户轻松修复错误。

The correctibility of the data is crucial for accurate analysis.  数据的可纠正性对于准确分析至关重要。

近义词: amendability, rectifiability, revisability
反义词: incorrigibility, uncorrectability


词性: noun
