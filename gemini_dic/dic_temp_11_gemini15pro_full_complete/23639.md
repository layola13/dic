brand:/brænd/
n.|品牌，商标，烙印|A type of product manufactured by a particular company under a particular name.|This is a very popular brand of coffee. 这是一款非常受欢迎的咖啡品牌。|近义词：make, trademark, label|反义词：generic|词性:noun
brand:/brænd/
vt.|给…打上烙印，铭刻于，加污名于|To mark with a hot iron, typically with a symbol of ownership.|The rancher branded his cattle. 牧场主在他的牛身上烙上了印记。|近义词：mark, stigmatize, label|反义词：unbrand|词性:verb
