outshapes:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪps/
verb
胜过；优于；在…方面超过
surpass in shape or form; excel
The new model outshapes its predecessors in terms of fuel efficiency.  这款新车型在燃油效率方面胜过以前的型号。
outshines, surpasses, exceeds, betters
lags behind, falls short of
v.
