nonconsequentialness:/ˌnɒnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəlˈnəs/ |n.| 无关紧要；不重要；不后果性 |The nonconsequentialness of his actions surprised everyone. 他的行为无关紧要，让每个人都感到惊讶。|The theory emphasizes the nonconsequentialness of individual actions in the grand scheme of things.  该理论强调个体行为在宏大计划中的无关紧要性。|近义词：insignificance, immateriality, irrelevance|反义词：significance, importance, consequence|词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + consequential + -ness (suffix)
* non-:  a prefix meaning "not"
* consequential: following as a result or effect
* -ness: a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, expressing a state or quality
