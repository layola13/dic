lobotomizing: /ˌloʊbəˈtɒmaɪzɪŋ/ (US), /ˌlɒbəˈtɒmaɪzɪŋ/ (UK)
| v. | 切除脑白质；对…进行脑叶切除手术 | Performing a lobotomy, a surgical procedure that involves severing connections in the brain's prefrontal cortex. | The doctor was lobotomizing the patient to treat their severe mental illness.  医生正在对病人进行脑叶切除手术以治疗他们的严重精神疾病。| Synonyms:  performing a lobotomy,  carrying out a lobotomy | Antonyms:  healing, curing | Verb (present participle)

lobotomy: /ləˈbɒtəmi/ (US), /ləˈbɒtəmi/ (UK)
| n. | 脑叶切除术 | A surgical procedure that involves severing connections in the brain's prefrontal cortex. |  The lobotomy left the patient with severe personality changes. 脑叶切除术使患者的人格发生了严重改变。 | Synonyms: leucotomy, prefrontal lobotomy | Antonyms: therapy, medication | Noun
