ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *letten*, from Old English *lǣtan* (“to let, allow, leave, depart, give up, cause, consider”), from Proto-Germanic *lētaną* (“to let, leave, allow”), from Proto-Indo-European *leH₁- (“to let go, slacken”). Cognate with West Frisian *litte* (“to let”), Dutch *laten* (“to let”), German *lassen* (“to let, leave”), Danish *lade* (“to let, load”), Swedish *läta* (“to let, sound”), Icelandic *láta* (“to let”).
