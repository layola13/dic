concertinaing:/ˌkɒnsəˈtiːnɪŋ/ /ˌkɑːnsərˈtiːnɪŋ/
Verb
(使)像六角手风琴一样折叠；(使)压缩
compressing or collapsing something into folds like those of a concertina.
The collision concertinaed the front of the car. 这次碰撞使车的前部折叠起来。
Synonyms:  folding, compressing, collapsing, crumpling, crushing
Antonyms: expanding, stretching, unfolding
词性: 动词
