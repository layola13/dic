overassisting:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɪstɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度帮助; 过度协助
Providing too much assistance, often to the point of hindering someone's development or independence.
The teacher was overassisting the students, preventing them from learning to solve problems on their own. 老师过度帮助学生，阻止了他们学习自己解决问题。
Overassisting can stifle creativity and problem-solving skills. 过度协助会扼杀创造力和解决问题的能力。
Near Synonyms:  overhelping, coddling, babying
Antonyms: neglecting, ignoring
