conciator:/kənˈsɪlieɪtər/| n.|调解人，调停人|A person who acts as a mediator between two disputing parties.|例句：The conciliator helped the two sides reach a compromise. 调解人帮助双方达成了妥协。

例句：The experienced conciliator successfully resolved the long-standing dispute between the labor union and the management. 经验丰富的调解人成功解决了工会和管理层之间长期的争端。


近义词：mediator, arbitrator, peacemaker, intermediary|反义词:agitator, instigator|词性:noun
