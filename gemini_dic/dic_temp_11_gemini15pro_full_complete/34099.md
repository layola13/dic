civilnesses:/ˈsɪvəlnəsɪz/
noun
礼貌；客气；彬彬有礼(复数)
The act of being polite; courtesy; acts of politeness.
He treated everyone with the utmost civilnesses. 他以最大的礼貌对待每个人。
近义词:courtesies, politenesses, civilities
反义词:rudeness, impoliteness, incivility
词性:n. (noun)


Etymology:
From Middle English *civilnesse, from Old French civilite (“courtesy, politeness, good manners”), from Latin cīvīlitās (“citizenship, courtesy”), from cīvīlis (“civil”), from cīvis (“citizen”).
