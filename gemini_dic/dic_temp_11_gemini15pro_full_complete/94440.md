imploring:/ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋ/
adjective
恳求的；哀求的
Making an earnest or desperate plea.
The child gave her mother an imploring look.  孩子恳求地望着她的母亲。
Near Synonyms:  beseeching, pleading, supplicating, entreating
Antonyms: demanding, commanding, ordering
词性: adjective

imploring:/ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋ/
verb
恳求；哀求
To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.
He was imploring her to stay. 他恳求她留下。
Near Synonyms:  beseech, plead, supplicate, entreat
Antonyms: demand, command, order
词性: verb
