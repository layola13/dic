machines:/məˈʃiːnz/| n.|机器；机械装置|A piece of equipment with several moving parts that uses power to do a particular type of work.|The factory is full of noisy machines. 工厂里满是嘈杂的机器。|近义词：equipment, apparatus, appliance, device, mechanism|反义词:tool (sometimes), hand tools|noun

machines:/məˈʃiːnz/| v. |用机器制造|To make or shape something using a machine.|The parts are machined to a very high tolerance. 这些零件是用机器加工到非常高的公差的。|近义词：manufacture, produce, fabricate, construct|反义词:handcraft|verb
