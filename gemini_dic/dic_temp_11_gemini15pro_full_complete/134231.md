noninert:/ˌnɒnɪˈnɜːrt/
adjective
非惰性的
Not inert; chemically reactive.
例句：Noninert gases can react with other substances. 非惰性气体可以与其他物质发生反应。

例句：The noninert atmosphere of early Earth played a crucial role in the development of life.  早期地球的非惰性大气在生命发展中起到了至关重要的作用。

近义词：reactive, active
反义词：inert, inactive
词性：adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "inert" (from Latin "iners," meaning "idle, inactive").
