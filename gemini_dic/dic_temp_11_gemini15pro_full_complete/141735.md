orana:/ɔːˈrɑːnə/ |interj. n.| 你好；一种问候语 |A Tahitian greeting meaning "hello" or "welcome".  It is also used as a noun referring to the greeting itself. |Example: Orana, friend! 你好，朋友！

|Example:  They greeted us with a warm "orana." 他们热情地用“orana”来问候我们。 |Synonym: hello, ia ora (another Tahitian greeting)| Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a greeting.  Perhaps "farewell") | Interjection, Noun
