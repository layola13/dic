ignoramus:/ˌɪɡnəˈreɪməs/
n.| 无知的人；愚人|A person who is ignorant or uninformed; a dunce.
|He's an ignoramus when it comes to art. 他对艺术一窍不通。
|近义词：dunce, fool, idiot, simpleton|
|反义词：scholar, expert, savant, intellectual|
|词性: noun|

ignoramus:/ˌɪɡnəˈreɪməs/
adj.| 无知的；愚蠢的|Ignorant or uninformed.
|His ignoramus comments about the situation were embarrassing. 他对情况的无知评论令人尴尬。
|近义词：uninformed, uneducated, ignorant, foolish|
|反义词：knowledgeable, informed, wise, intelligent|
|词性: adjective (less common)|
