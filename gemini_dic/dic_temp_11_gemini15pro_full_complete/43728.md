creedlessness:/ˈkriːdləsnəs/
n.
lack of creed; absence of religious belief.
缺乏信仰；没有宗教信仰。
His creedlessness made him an outsider in the deeply religious community.  他的缺乏信仰使他在这个宗教氛围浓厚的社区中显得格格不入。
近义词：unbelief, nonbelief, atheism, irreligion, skepticism
反义词：faith, belief, piety, religiousness
词性：noun

creed:/kriːd/
n.
a system of Christian or other religious belief; a faith.
基督教或其他宗教信仰体系；一种信仰。
The Nicene Creed is a statement of Christian belief. 尼西亚信经是基督教信仰的陈述。
近义词：doctrine, dogma, tenet, credo
反义词：disbelief, doubt, skepticism
词性：noun

-less:/ləs/
suffix.
without; lacking.
没有；缺乏。
Hopeless, helpless, careless.  没有希望的，无助的，粗心的。
近义词：-free, -devoid, un-
反义词：-ful, -rich
词性：suffix

词源：
Creedlessness is formed from "creed" + "-less."  "Creed" comes from the Old English "crēda" (belief, statement of belief), which is related to the Latin "credo" (I believe). The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and indicates a lack or absence of something.
