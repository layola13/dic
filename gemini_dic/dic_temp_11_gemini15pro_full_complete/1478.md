acervuline:/əˈsɜːrvjʊlaɪn/
adjective
簇生的，堆积的
Growing in dense clusters or heaps; aggregated.
The acervuline growth of the fungus covered the tree branch.  这种真菌簇生的生长覆盖了树枝。
The acervuline barnacles encrusted the rocks. 藤壶密密麻麻地附着在岩石上。
近义词: clustered, aggregated, heaped, dense
反义词: sparse, scattered, dispersed
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acervulinus, from acervus ("heap, pile") + -inus (adjectival suffix).
