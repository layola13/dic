overpedaling: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛdəlɪŋ/
verb
过度踩踏板; 过度用力骑车
Applying excessive force to the pedals of a bicycle, often resulting in inefficient use of energy and reduced speed.
The cyclist was overpedaling up the hill, wasting energy that could have been conserved.  骑自行车的人在上坡时过度踩踏板，浪费了可以节省的能量。
He was overpedaling so much that his legs started to cramp. 他过度踩踏板，以至于腿开始抽筋。
Near Synonyms:  overexerting, pushing too hard, straining
Antonyms:  pacing oneself, conserving energy
