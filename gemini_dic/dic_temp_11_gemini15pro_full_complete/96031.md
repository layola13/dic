indazole: /ɪnˈdeɪzɒl/ (US) /ɪnˈdeɪzɒl/ (UK)| n. |吲唑|A colorless crystalline solid, a heterocyclic aromatic organic compound, isomeric with benzopyrazole.  It is a bicyclic compound consisting of benzene fused to pyrazole.|Indazole derivatives are found in certain pharmaceuticals.|近义词: benzopyrazole (isomer, not synonym in the strictest sense)|反义词: N/A|noun
