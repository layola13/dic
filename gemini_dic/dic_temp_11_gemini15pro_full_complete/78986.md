girthed:/ɡɜːrθt/
|v. |(用带子)围…的周长；束缚|To measure the circumference of something with a girth; to encircle or bind with a girth.|The knight girthed his sword around his waist. 骑士把剑束在腰间。|近义词：encircle, encompass, surround, band, belt|反义词:unbind, unfasten|动词
girthed:/ˈɡɜːrθt/
|adj. |有…围长的|Having a specified girth.|a tree girthed ten feet. 一棵周长十英尺的树。|近义词：broad, thick, wide|反义词:narrow, thin, slender|形容词
