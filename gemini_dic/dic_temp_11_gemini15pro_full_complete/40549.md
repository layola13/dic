controvert:/ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrt/ /ˈkɒntrəvɜːt/| vt.|反驳，驳斥，争论|To argue against something; to dispute or deny.|The lawyer controverted the witness's testimony. 律师驳斥了证人的证词。|近义词：dispute, challenge, oppose, rebut, refute|反义词：accept, concede, confirm, support|verb

controverted:/ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrtɪd/ /ˈkɒntrəvɜːtɪd/| adj.|有争议的，被驳斥的|Disputed or denied.|The controverted election results led to a recount.  有争议的选举结果导致了重新计票。|近义词：disputed, challenged, debated|反义词：accepted, undisputed, acknowledged|adjective
