neurasthenically:/ˌnʊərəsˈθɛnɪkli/ /ˌnjʊərəsˈθɛnɪkli/ | adv. | 神经衰弱地；神经质地 | In a manner characteristic of neurasthenia, exhibiting symptoms of fatigue, anxiety, and often depression.  | She sighed neurasthenically and sank onto the sofa. 她神经质地叹了口气，瘫倒在沙发上。 |  Example: He complained neurasthenically about the slightest noise. 他神经衰弱般地抱怨着最轻微的噪音。| 近义词: nervously, anxiously, weakly, neurotically | 反义词: calmly, robustly, energetically | 词性: 副词

词源: From neurasthenia + -ically.  Neurasthenia comes from Greek *neuron* (nerve) + *astheneia* (weakness).
