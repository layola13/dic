over-talks:/ˌoʊvərˈtɔːks/ | v. | 打断别人说话，抢话 | To speak over someone else, interrupting them and preventing them from finishing their thoughts. | He over-talks everyone in meetings, making it hard to have a productive discussion. 他在会议上总是打断别人说话，很难进行有效的讨论。|  She over-talks so much that I can barely get a word in edgewise. 她总是抢话，我几乎插不上嘴。 | interrupts, dominates the conversation | listens attentively, lets others speak | verb
