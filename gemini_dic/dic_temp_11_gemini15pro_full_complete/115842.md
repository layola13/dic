marking:/ˈmɑːrkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |标记，评分，记号，斑纹，污点|A mark or a series of marks made on a surface.  The process of making such marks. The evaluation or grading of a student's work.|
The teacher spent hours marking exam papers. 老师花了几个小时批改试卷。
The bird's distinctive markings made it easy to identify.  这种鸟独特的斑纹使它很容易辨认。
|近义词：label, sign, grade, score, notation, symbol,  characteristic,  feature|反义词: unmarked, plain, clean |词性: noun
