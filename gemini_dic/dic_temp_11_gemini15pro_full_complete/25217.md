budgerigar:/ˈbʌdʒərɪˌɡɑːr/ (US) /ˈbʌdʒərɪɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. |虎皮鹦鹉|A small, long-tailed parrot, usually light green with yellow and black markings, native to Australia and popular as a cage bird.  It's often called a "budgie" for short.|例句：My budgerigar learned to say "hello." 我的虎皮鹦鹉学会说“你好”。

|例句：The budgerigar chirped happily in its cage.  虎皮鹦鹉在笼子里欢快地鸣叫。|近义词：parakeet, budgie|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


budgies:/ˈbʌdʒiz/|n.|虎皮鹦鹉（复数）|Plural form of "budgerigar," referring to multiple of these small parrots.|例句：The pet store had many different colored budgies. 这家宠物店有许多不同颜色的虎皮鹦鹉。

|例句：We watched the budgies fly around the aviary. 我们看着虎皮鹦鹉在鸟舍里飞来飞去。|近义词：parakeets|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


词源:

From Gamilaraay *bachiri-gar  meaning "good bird" or "good food," perhaps referring to its use as a food source by Indigenous Australians.  Shortened to "budgerigar" and then further to "budgie."
