disseizor:/dɪˈsiːzər/| n. |非法剥夺者；强占者|A person who ousts someone else from real property without lawful authority and claims ownership.|例句：The disseizor claimed the land as his own, even though he had no legal right to it.  非法剥夺者声称这块土地是他的，即使他没有合法权利。

例句：The court ruled against the disseizor and ordered him to return the property to its rightful owner. 法院判决非法剥夺者败诉，并命令他将财产归还原主。|近义词：usurper, intruder, squatter|反义词:owner, proprietor|词性:noun
