costal:/ˈkɒstəl/ (US) /ˈkɔːstl/ (UK)| adj.|肋骨的；靠近肋骨的|Relating to the ribs or the sides of the body between the ribs and the pelvis.|The doctor examined the patient's costal cartilage. 医生检查了病人的肋软骨。|Example: The intercostal muscles are located between the ribs. 肋间肌位于肋骨之间。

Example:  Costal pain can be a symptom of various conditions. 肋骨疼痛可能是各种疾病的症状。|Synonyms: rib-related,  thoracic|Antonyms:  abdominal,  vertebral|adjective
