bailed:/beɪld/| v.|保释，帮助…摆脱困境，舀出（水），跳伞|To release (someone) from custody by paying bail.  To remove (water) from a boat by scooping. To abandon a situation, person, or activity. To parachute from an aircraft.|The judge bailed him out. 法官保释了他。 |The boat was leaking so badly they had to bail constantly.  船漏得很厉害，他们不得不不停地往外舀水。 |He bailed on the project at the last minute. 他在最后一刻放弃了这个项目。 |The pilot bailed out of the burning plane. 飞行员从燃烧的飞机上跳伞逃生。|近义词：rescued, saved, scooped, abandoned, parachuted |反义词: imprison, confine, join |词性:动词

bailed:/beɪld/| n.|保释金|Money paid to secure someone's release from custody pending trial.|He was released on bail. 他被保释出狱了。|近义词:bond|反义词:|词性:名词
