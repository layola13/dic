invaginate:/ɪnˈvædʒɪneɪt/| v. |内陷，套入|To fold inwards, forming a pouch or cavity; to sheathe or enclose (one part) within another. |The tissue invaginated to form a pocket.  组织内陷形成一个囊袋。|The developing embryo invaginates to form the gut.  发育中的胚胎内陷形成肠道。|近义词：introvert, infold, pockets|反义词：evert, protrude|verb

invagination:/ɪnˌvædʒɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |内陷，套入|The action or process of being invaginated; the state of being invaginated. |The invagination of the cell membrane formed a vesicle. 细胞膜的内陷形成了一个囊泡。|Gastrulation involves the invagination of the blastula.  原肠胚形成涉及到囊胚的内陷。|近义词：intussusception, infolding|反义词：evagination, protrusion|noun
