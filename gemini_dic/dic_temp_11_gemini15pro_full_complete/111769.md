lodging:/ˈlɒdʒɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈlɑːdʒɪŋ/ (US)| n. |住宿，寄宿；住所；（射入口）卡住|A place to sleep or stay temporarily, or the act of providing such a place.|We found cheap lodging at a nearby hostel. 我们在附近的一家旅社找到了便宜的住宿。|Synonyms: accommodation, shelter, housing, quarters|Antonyms: eviction, homelessness|noun

lodging:/ˈlɒdʒɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈlɑːdʒɪŋ/ (US)| v. |（使）固定；存放；提出（申诉等）|To stay temporarily in a particular place.|He lodged with a family while he was a student. 他上学时寄宿在一个家庭里。|Synonyms:  stay, board, reside, quarter|Antonyms: leave, depart|verb
