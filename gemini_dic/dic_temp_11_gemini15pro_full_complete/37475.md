commissively:/kəˈmɪsɪvli/
adv.
承诺地，带有承诺意味地
in a way that commits you to something or involves making a promise
He spoke commissively about his future plans. 他承诺性地谈论了他未来的计划。
近义词：obligingly,  promisingly
反义词：noncommittally, evasively
词性: 副词
