nonsecretory:/ˌnɑːnˈsiːkrɪˌtɔːri/
adjective
非分泌性的
Not producing or releasing secretions.
The nonsecretory cells of the stomach lining protect it from acid. 胃壁的非分泌性细胞保护它免受酸的侵蚀。
