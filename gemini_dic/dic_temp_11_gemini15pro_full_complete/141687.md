opulency: /ˈɒpjʊlənsɪ/ (US) /ˈɒpjʊlənsɪ/ (UK)
n.
富裕，豪华，奢侈
Great wealth or luxuriousness.
The opulency of the ancient Roman emperors is legendary. 古罗马皇帝的奢华生活是传奇。
The hotel was decorated with remarkable opulency. 这家酒店装饰得非常豪华。
近义词: luxury, lavishness, affluence, richness, grandeur
反义词: poverty, austerity, simplicity, modesty
词性: 名词

opulent: /ˈɒpjʊlənt/ (US) /ˈɒpjʊlənt/ (UK)
adj.
富裕的，豪华的，奢侈的
Characterized by rich abundance; luxurious.
They lived in an opulent mansion. 他们住在一座豪华的宅邸里。
She wore an opulent fur coat. 她穿着一件奢华的皮草大衣。
近义词: luxurious, lavish, sumptuous, rich, affluent
反义词: poor, simple, modest, austere
词性: 形容词
