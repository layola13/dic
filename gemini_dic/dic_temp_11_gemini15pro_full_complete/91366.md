hydrospheric:/ˌhaɪdrəˈsfɪrɪk/ |adj.|水圈的；水界的|Relating to the hydrosphere, which is the total amount of water on a planet. |The hydrospheric conditions are crucial for marine life. 水圈条件对海洋生物至关重要。| The report detailed the hydrospheric effects of climate change.  这份报告详细说明了气候变化对水圈的影响。|近义词:aquatic, marine|反义词:terrestrial|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From hydro- + sphere + -ic.  "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" meaning "water". "Sphere" comes from Greek "sphaira" meaning "ball" or "globe".  "-ic" is a suffix forming adjectives.
