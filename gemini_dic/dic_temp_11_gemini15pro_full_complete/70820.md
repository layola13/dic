finicality:/fɪnɪˈkæləti/ | n. | 过分讲究；吹毛求疵；苛求 | Excessive attention to detail and correctness, often in a petty or ostentatious way; fastidiousness. |  She was known for her finicality in matters of dress. 她以衣着讲究而闻名。

Finicality can be a barrier to true enjoyment. 吹毛求疵会阻碍真正的享受。 | 近义词: fastidiousness, meticulousness, punctiliousness, scrupulousness | 反义词: carelessness, negligence, sloppiness | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From finical + -ity.  "Finical" comes from the now obsolete adjective "fine" (meaning refined, elegant, subtle) + -ical.  The sense shifted over time from "refined" to "overly refined" and then to "fussy and overly particular."
