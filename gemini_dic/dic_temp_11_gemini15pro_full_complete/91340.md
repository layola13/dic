hydropiper:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpaɪpər/ | n. |水蓼,辣蓼|A type of aquatic plant known for its peppery taste. It belongs to the knotweed family (Polygonaceae) and is found in wetland habitats.  |例句：Hydropiper is often used in traditional medicine. 水蓼常用于传统医学。

例句：The hydropiper grew abundantly along the riverbank. 水蓼沿着河岸茂盛地生长。|近义词：water pepper, smartweed, marshpepper |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
Etymology: From the Greek "hydro" (water) + "piper" (pepper), referring to its aquatic habitat and peppery flavor.
