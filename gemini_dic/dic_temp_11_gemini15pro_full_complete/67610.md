extravenate:/ɪkˈstrævəneɪt/
verb
泄出；逸出血管外
(of fluids, especially blood) to escape from a blood vessel or the lymphatic system into the tissues.
The drug caused the blood to extravenate.  这种药物导致血液逸出血管外。
近义词:exude, leak, seep
反义词:infuse, inject
词性:v.
