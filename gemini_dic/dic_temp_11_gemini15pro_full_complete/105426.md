kidney:/ˈkɪdni/ (US), /ˈkɪdni/ (UK)| n. |肾脏|Either of two bean-shaped organs in the abdominal cavity of vertebrates that excrete urine.|My uncle needs a kidney transplant. 我的叔叔需要肾移植。|The kidneys filter waste products from the blood. 肾脏过滤血液中的废物。|近义词:renal (adj. related to kidneys)|反义词:None (Organs don't typically have antonyms)|词性:noun
