cinematics:/ˌsɪnəˈmætɪks/ | n. |电影艺术；电影学；运动学|The study of motion without reference to the forces that cause the motion.  The art or technique of making films, especially in relation to their visual aspects.  | Example 1: The cinematics of the car chase were breathtaking.  这汽车追逐场景的电影艺术令人叹为观止。

Example 2: He studied cinematics in college. 他在大学学习电影学。 |Synonym: filmmaking, moviemaking, motion picture art|Antonym: N/A | Noun
