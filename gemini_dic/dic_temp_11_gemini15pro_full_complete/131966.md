Etymology:
From non- + contumaciousness.  Contumacious from Late Latin contumacia "obstinacy," from Latin contumax "stubborn, insolent," from com-, intensive prefix (see com-), + tumere "to swell" (see thumb).  The -ness suffix forms nouns from adjectives.
