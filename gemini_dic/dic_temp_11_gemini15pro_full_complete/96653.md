industrialisms: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəmz/
n.
工业主义，工业制度
The social and economic systems characteristic of industrialized societies, often associated with mass production, mechanization, and urbanization.
The negative effects of industrialisms on the environment are well documented. 工业主义对环境的负面影响有充分的文献记载。
The rise of industrialisms led to significant social and economic changes. 工业主义的兴起导致了重大的社会和经济变革。
synonyms: industrialization, modernization, mechanization
antonyms: agrarianism, traditionalism
noun
