ombre:/ˈɒmbreɪ/ (US), /ˈɒmbrə/ (UK)| adj. n.|渐变的，阴影; 渐变色|A gradual blending of one color hue to another, usually moving tints and shades from light to dark.  It can refer to hair coloring, fabric dyeing, or other decorative arts.|Her hair was styled in a fashionable ombre, going from dark brown at the roots to a light blonde at the tips. 她的头发被染成了时髦的渐变色，从发根的深棕色到发梢的浅金色。|Synonyms: graduated, shaded, dip-dyed|Antonyms: uniform, solid, monochromatic|adjective, noun

ombre:/ˈɒmbreɪ/ (US), /ˈɒmbrə/ (UK)| n.|一种纸牌游戏|(Archaic) A card game popular in the 17th and 18th centuries.|They spent the evening playing ombre. 他们晚上在玩ombre纸牌游戏。|Synonyms: card game (general)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
