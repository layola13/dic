manongi: /məˈnɒŋɡi/ | n. | 马农吉果（一种非洲水果）| A type of African fruit, scientifically known as *Ricinodendron rautanenii*, also called Manketti nut. It's prized for its oil-rich nuts and versatile uses. | Example: Manongi oil is used in cooking and cosmetics.  马农吉油用于烹饪和化妆品。

| Example: The manongi tree is drought-resistant and vital to local ecosystems. 马农吉树具有抗旱性，对当地生态系统至关重要。 | Synonyms: Manketti nut, Mongongo nut | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
