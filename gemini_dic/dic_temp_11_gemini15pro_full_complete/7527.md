annex:/əˈnɛks/ | n. | 附加物，附件；附属建筑物 |An annex is a building that is added to a larger building. | The library has built a new annex to house its growing collection. 图书馆新建了一个附属建筑来存放日益增长的藏书。| synonyms: addition, extension, wing, appendage | antonyms: detachment, separation | noun
annex:/əˈnɛks/ | vt. | 附加，吞并 |To annex something means to add it to something else, often by force. | Russia annexed Crimea in 2014.  俄罗斯在2014年吞并了克里米亚。| synonyms: seize, conquer, appropriate, acquire | antonyms: relinquish, surrender, cede | verb
