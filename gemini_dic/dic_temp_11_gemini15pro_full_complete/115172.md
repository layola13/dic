manic:/ˈmænɪk/ | adj.|躁狂的；狂热的；着迷的|Showing wild, apparently deranged excitement and energy.|He was in a manic state, laughing and shouting.|近义词:frantic, frenzied, hysterical|反义词:calm, peaceful, relaxed|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *manicus*, from Latin *maniacus* "mad, insane," from *mania* "madness," from Greek *mania* "madness, frenzy, enthusiasm."
