amplification:/ˌæmplɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 放大；扩大；增强 | the act of increasing the strength or importance of something | The amplification of the music was too loud.  音乐放大得太响了。| synonyms: enlargement, expansion, augmentation, intensification | antonyms: reduction, diminution, decrease | noun

amplification:/ˌæmplɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 详述；补充说明 | additional information to make something clearer or more complete |  The lawyer's amplification of the key points helped the jury understand the case. 律师对关键点的补充说明帮助陪审团理解了案情。| synonyms: elaboration, explanation, clarification, illustration | antonyms: abridgement, condensation, summary | noun
