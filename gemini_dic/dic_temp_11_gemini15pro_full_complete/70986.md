firestones:/ˈfaɪərstəʊnz/ (UK) /ˈfaɪərstəʊnz/ (US)| n. |火石;打火石;燧石;（Firestone）凡士通（轮胎品牌）|Pieces of hard rock used for producing sparks, especially for lighting fires, or (Firestone) a brand of tires. |例句：The pioneers used firestones to start their campfires. 先驱者们用火石点燃篝火。|例句：He bought a new set of Firestone tires for his truck. 他给他的卡车买了一套新的凡士通轮胎。|近义词：flint, lighter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fȳrstan (attested only as fȳrstæne, dative singular), from fȳr (“fire”) + stan (“stone”).  Cognate with Old Saxon fūrsten, Old High German fūristān.
