decreasing:/dɪˈkriːsɪŋ/
adjective
减少的，递减的
becoming smaller or less
The company reported decreasing profits.  这家公司报告利润下降。
The decreasing temperature made us feel cold.  下降的温度让我们感到寒冷。
近义词: declining, diminishing, reducing, lessening
反义词: increasing, rising, growing, expanding
词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "decrease," which comes from the Old French "decreistre" (modern French "décroître"), from the Latin "decrescere," meaning "to grow less," from "de-" (down, away) and "crescere" (to grow).
