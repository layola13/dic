dementednesses:/dɪˈmɛntɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
痴呆，精神错乱（的状态）
The state of being demented; severe mental impairment.
His dementednesses led him to believe he was Napoleon. 他的痴呆让他相信自己是拿破仑。
近义词: insanities, madness, lunacy
反义词: sanity, lucidity
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From demented + -ness.  Demented itself comes from Old French dementer "to make mad," from Latin dementare, from de- "out of" + mens "mind."
