brainish:/ˈbreɪnɪʃ/
adjective
愚蠢的，没脑子的，轻率的
Silly or scatterbrained; lacking in sense or judgment.
He made a rather brainish decision to invest all his money in that failing company. 他把所有的钱都投资在那家倒闭的公司上，真是个愚蠢的决定。
近义词：silly, foolish, stupid, unintelligent, unwise, imprudent, rash
反义词：wise, intelligent, sensible, prudent
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From "brain" + "-ish," a suffix indicating resemblance or having the quality of. The suffix "-ish" derives from Old English "-isc."
