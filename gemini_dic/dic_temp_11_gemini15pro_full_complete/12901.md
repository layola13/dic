assurable:/əˈʃʊrəbl/ | adj. | 可担保的，可保证的 |  Able to be assured; capable of being insured. | The risk is assurable at a reasonable premium.  这种风险以合理的保费是可以承保的。 | Example: His future success is assurable given his talent and dedication. 他的天赋和奉献精神保证了他未来的成功。| Synonyms: insurable, guaranteed, certain, secure | Antonyms: uninsurable, uncertain, risky |adjective
