fredericton:/ˈfrɛdrɪktən/| n. |弗雷德里克顿|The capital city of the province of New Brunswick, Canada, located on the Saint John River. |Fredericton is known for its beautiful riverfront and Victorian architecture. 弗雷德里克顿以其美丽的河滨和维多利亚式建筑而闻名。|近义词:capital (as in capital city)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: Named after Prince Frederick, Duke of York and Albany, the second son of King George III.
