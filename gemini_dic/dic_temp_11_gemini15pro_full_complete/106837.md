laboriously:/ləˈbɔːriəsli/
adverb
辛苦地，费力地
in a way that takes a lot of time and effort
She laborediously climbed the steep staircase. 她费力地爬上陡峭的楼梯。
laboriously:/ləˈbɔːriəsli/
adverb
吃力地
with difficulty and requiring a lot of effort
He laboriously explained the complex process. 他吃力地解释了这个复杂的过程。

近义词:strenuously, arduously, painstakingly, toilsomely
反义词:easily, effortlessly, readily

词源:From Late Latin *laboriōsus, from Latin labor (“work, toil, hardship”) + -ōsus.
