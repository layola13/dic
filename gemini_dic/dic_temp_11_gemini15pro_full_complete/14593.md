avidnesses:/ˈævɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
渴望，热心，热情
The intensity of someone's desire or enthusiasm for something.
He showed great avidness for learning new things. 他对学习新事物表现出极大的热情。
近义词: eagerness, keenness, enthusiasm, zeal
反义词: apathy, indifference, reluctance

词性:名词(plural of avidness)

词源: From avid + -ness.  Avid traces back to the Latin "avidus," meaning "eager" or "greedy."  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
