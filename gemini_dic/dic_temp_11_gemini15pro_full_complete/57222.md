downfallen:/ˈdaʊnˌfɔːlən/| adj.|堕落的，落魄的，垮台的|Having lost one's power, wealth, or status; ruined.|The downfall of the corrupt politician was celebrated by the public. 腐败政客的垮台受到了公众的庆祝。|Synonyms: ruined, degraded, overthrown, destitute, bankrupt|Antonyms: prosperous, successful, thriving|adjective

downfall:/ˈdaʊnˌfɔːl/| n.|垮台，衰败，堕落|A sudden loss of power, status, or wealth; a sudden and complete failure or ruin.|The scandal led to the downfall of the company.  这起丑闻导致了公司的垮台。|Synonyms: ruin, collapse, demise, failure|Antonyms: success, triumph, rise|noun
