bankability:/ˌbæŋkəˈbɪləti/
n.
可融资性，银行接受度
The quality of being likely to bring in profits and therefore be attractive to banks and investors.
The project's bankability was in question due to the uncertain economic climate.  由于经济环境不确定，该项目的可融资性受到质疑。
Synonyms: viability, feasibility, financeability
Antonyms: unviability, infeasibility
