morpheming:/mɔːrˈfiːmɪŋ/ | n. | 词素分析，形态分析 | The process of breaking down words into their smallest meaningful units (morphemes). |  Example: Morpheming the word "unbreakable" reveals the morphemes "un-", "break", and "-able".  词素分析“unbreakable”这个词揭示了词素“un-”，“break”和“-able”。 | Example:  Understanding morpheming can help improve vocabulary and reading comprehension. 了解词素分析有助于提高词汇量和阅读理解能力。 | synonyms: morphological analysis, decomposition (of words) | antonyms: word formation, synthesis (of words) | noun
