bapu:/ˈbɑːpuː/| n.|巴普，圣雄甘地|A respectful term of address for Mahatma Gandhi, meaning "father" or "revered elder" in Gujarati and other Indian languages.|Example:  Bapu inspired millions with his message of nonviolence. 巴普以其非暴力信息激励了数百万人。|Example: Many Indians still revere Bapu as the father of the nation. 许多印度人仍然尊崇巴普为国父。| 近义词:father, Mahatma, Gandhi| 反义词: (No direct antonym; conceptually, perhaps "enemy" or "opponent")|词性:名词
