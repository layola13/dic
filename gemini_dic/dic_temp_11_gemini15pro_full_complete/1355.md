accumulate:/əˈkjuːmjəleɪt/ /əˈkjuːmjʊleɪt/| v. |积累，积聚|to gather or collect something in an increasing quantity over a period of time|Snow accumulated on the roof. 屋顶上积起了雪。|synonyms: gather, collect, amass, stockpile|antonyms: disperse, dissipate, squander, deplete|verb

accumulate:/əˈkjuːmjəleɪt/ /əˈkjuːmjʊleɪt/| adj. |累积的|increasing by successive additions|The accumulated snow blocked the doorway. 积雪堵住了门口。|synonyms: cumulative, amassed, gathered|antonyms: dispersed, dissipated|adjective
