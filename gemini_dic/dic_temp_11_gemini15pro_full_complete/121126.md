misdeem:/ˌmɪsˈdiːm/
verb
判断错误；误判；认为…不好
To judge wrongly; to have an erroneous opinion of.
He misdeems their silence as agreement. 他误以为他们的沉默就是同意。
Synonyms: misjudge, misinterpret, misconstrue
Antonyms: judge correctly, understand, comprehend
词性: 动词
