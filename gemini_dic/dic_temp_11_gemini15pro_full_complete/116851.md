maximist:/ˈmæksɪmɪst/| n. |最大化主义者；极端主义者|A person who holds extreme views and advocates maximal demands or courses of action. |例句：He's a maximist in his political views, always pushing for the most radical changes. 他政治观点极端，总是推动最激进的变革。

例句：She's a design maximist, filling her home with bold colors and patterns. 她是一位设计上的极端主义者，用大胆的色彩和图案装饰她的家。|近义词：extremist, radical, zealot|反义词:minimalist, moderate|词性:noun
