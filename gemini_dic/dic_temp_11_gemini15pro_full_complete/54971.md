disproportionately: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːʃənətli/ | adv. | 不成比例地，不相称地 |  in a way that is too large or too small in relation to something else |  Minority groups are disproportionately represented in the prison population. 少数族裔在监狱人口中所占比例过高。 | The small town benefited disproportionately from the government funding. 这个小镇从政府资助中获得了不成比例的好处。 |  disproportionally, unevenly, unfairly | proportionately, equally, fairly | adverb
