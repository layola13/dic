jigging:/ˈdʒɪɡɪŋ/ | n. v. |跳吉格舞; 用鱼钩钓鱼; 上下急动|A lively dance, usually in triple time, or the music for such a dance; the act of fishing with a jig. |例句：He was jigging on the deck, excited about the catch. 他在甲板上跳着吉格舞，对捕到的鱼感到兴奋。

例句：They were jigging for cod off the coast of Newfoundland. 他们在纽芬兰海岸用鱼钩钓鳕鱼。|近义词：dancing, bobbing, fishing, jerking|反义词:stillness, inactivity|词性:名词, 动词
