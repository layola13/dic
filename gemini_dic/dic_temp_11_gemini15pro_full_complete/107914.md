larvalia: /lɑːrˈveɪliə/
noun
幼虫期，幼体期
The stage in an animal's lifecycle between hatching from the egg and becoming an adult; the larval stage.
The larvalia of butterflies can last from a few weeks to several months. 蝴蝶的幼虫期可以持续几周到几个月。
Larvae, young, juvenile
Adult, mature
n.
