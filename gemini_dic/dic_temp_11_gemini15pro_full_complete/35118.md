clouder:/ˈklaʊdər/| n.|云的形成者; 聚集者; 使模糊不清的人或物|Something that forms clouds or makes something cloudy.  It can also refer to a person or thing that obscures or confuses something. |The pollution acted as a clouder, obscuring the view of the mountains. 污染像云一样遮蔽了山景。|近义词：obscurer, confuser|反义词：clarifier|词性:Noun
