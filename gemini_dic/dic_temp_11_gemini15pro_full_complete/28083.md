capillariness:/ˌkæpɪˈlærɪnɪs/
n.
毛细作用，毛细现象
The property of a liquid rising or falling in a narrow tube due to surface tension.
Water's capillariness allows it to be drawn up into the roots of plants. 水的毛细作用使它能被吸收到植物的根部。

The capillariness of the soil helps to retain water. 土壤的毛细作用有助于保持水分。

近义词：capillary action, capillary attraction
反义词：(None - this is a physical property)
词性：noun
