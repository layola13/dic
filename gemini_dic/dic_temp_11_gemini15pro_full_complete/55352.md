distal:/ˈdɪst(ə)l/| adj.|末梢的，远端的|Situated away from the center of the body or from the point of attachment.|The distal end of the femur connects to the knee joint. 股骨远端与膝关节相连。|Example: The pain was located in the distal phalanges of her fingers. 她的疼痛位于她手指的远端指骨。|Synonyms: peripheral, outermost, terminal|Antonyms: proximal, central, medial|adjective

distal:/ˈdɪstl/| adj.|末梢的，远端的|Situated farthest from the point of attachment or origin, as of a limb or bone.|The distal end of the ulna is at the wrist. 尺骨远端在手腕处。|Example:  Distal symptoms of the disease include numbness in the toes.  这种疾病的远端症状包括脚趾麻木。|Synonyms: outermost, furthest, endmost|Antonyms: proximal, closest, nearest|adjective
