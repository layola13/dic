noncontagious:/ˌnɑːnkənˈteɪdʒəs/ /ˌnɒnkənˈteɪdʒəs/
adj.
非传染性的
Not capable of being transmitted by contact.
The doctor assured her that the rash was noncontagious. 医生向她保证皮疹不会传染。

近义词:noncommunicable, noninfectious
反义词:contagious, communicable, infectious
词性:adjective


词源:
From non- + contagious.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."  "Contagious" comes from the Latin "contagiosus," meaning "touching," from "contingere," meaning "to touch."
