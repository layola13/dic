nonprohibitive:/ˌnɒnprəˈhɪbɪtɪv/ | adj. |非禁止性的；价格适中的；不太昂贵的 |Not prohibitive;  allowing or encouraging something to be done because it does not prevent it.  Relatively inexpensive or affordable.| The cost of the software was nonprohibitive, so we decided to purchase it.  该软件的成本不算太高，所以我们决定购买。| Synonyms: affordable, reasonable, moderate, inexpensive, cheap, economical | Antonyms: prohibitive, exorbitant, expensive, costly, dear | adjective
