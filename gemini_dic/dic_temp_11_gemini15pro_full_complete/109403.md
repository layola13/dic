lentous:/ˈlɛntəs/ | adj. | 缓慢的，粘稠的 | Slow and viscous; sluggish. | The lentous flow of the lava made it difficult to escape.  熔岩的缓慢流动使得难以逃脱。| The lentous syrup took forever to pour. 粘稠的糖浆要倒好久。 | 近义词: viscous, sluggish, slow, tardy | 反义词: rapid, quick, fast | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *lentus* "tough, sticky, slow," from PIE root *len-/*lī- "flexible, pliant, sticky."
