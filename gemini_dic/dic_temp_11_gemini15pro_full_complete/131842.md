nonconstants:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnstənts/ | n. | 非恒量，非常数 | Quantities that are not constant; variables.  Values that can change within a given context, such as in a program or mathematical equation. | Example: In this equation, x and y are nonconstants, while 5 is a constant.  在这个等式中，x和y是非恒量，而5是常量。| Synonyms: variables, parameters | Antonyms: constants | noun
