noncharitably:/ˌnɒnˈtʃærɪtəbli/
adverb
无情地，刻薄地
in a way that is not charitable; unkindly or unsympathetically.
He noncharitably assumed she was lying. 他不客气地认为她在撒谎。
Synonyms: unkindly, unsympathetically, meanly, cruelly, harshly, callously, heartlessly, ruthlessly, mercilessly, pitilessly, inhumanely, brutally, viciously, spitefully, malevolently, malignantly, venomously
Antonyms: charitably, kindly, sympathetically, generously
