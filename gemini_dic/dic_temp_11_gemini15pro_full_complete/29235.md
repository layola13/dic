cassate:/kəˈsɑːt/ /kæˈseɪt/
noun
蜜饯水果糕点；蜜饯水果
A Neapolitan ice cream dessert consisting of layers of various flavors and candied fruit.
The cassate was rich and decadent, layered with pistachio, chocolate, and candied cherries. 这款卡萨塔冰淇淋蛋糕香浓醇厚，由开心果、巧克力和蜜饯樱桃层层叠加而成。
synonyms:Neapolitan ice cream, spumoni, frozen dessert
antonyms:N/A
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *cassata*, from Arabic *qaššāṭah* (meaning "bowl").
