coachloads:/ˈkoʊtʃloʊdz/
n.
满载旅客的游览车；很多；大批
A large number of people, as many as would fill several coaches.
Coachloads of tourists arrive every day. 每天都有很多游客到来。
近义词:busloads, crowds, masses
反义词:handful, few
词性:名词


coachload:/ˈkoʊtʃloʊd/
n.
一游览车的人
A group of people who travel together in a coach.
A coachload of schoolchildren visited the museum. 一车小学生参观了博物馆。
近义词:busload
反义词:individual
词性:名词
