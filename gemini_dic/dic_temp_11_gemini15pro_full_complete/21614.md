ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blunt "dull, obtuse," of uncertain origin, perhaps from or related to Old Norse blunda "to doze, close the eyes," or possibly from a Proto-Germanic *blunza-, from PIE root *bhel- (2) "to swell," with a semantic development of "swollen," hence "dull, insensitive."
