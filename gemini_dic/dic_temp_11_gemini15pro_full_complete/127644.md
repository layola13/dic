needly:/ˈniːdli/| adj.|多刺的，针状的|Covered with needles; resembling needles.  |例句：The cactus was covered in needly spines. 仙人掌上覆盖着针状的刺。|近义词：prickly, spiny, thorny|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *nedli, from Old English nǣdlic (“needly, pointed”), equivalent to needle +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots nedlie (“needly”).
