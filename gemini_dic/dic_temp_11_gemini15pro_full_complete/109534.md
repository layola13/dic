lessee:/leˈsiː/ /ləˈsiː/
n.
承租人，租户
a person who holds the lease of a property; a tenant.
The lessee is responsible for maintaining the property in good condition. 承租人有责任维护好房屋的状况。
tenant, renter
landlord, lessor
noun
