aileron:/ˈeɪləˌrɒn/ (US) /ˈeɪləˌrɒn/ (UK)| n. |副翼|A hinged control surface on the trailing edge of an aircraft wing, used to control roll.|The pilot used the ailerons to bank the plane. 飞行员使用副翼使飞机倾斜。|The ailerons helped the aircraft make a smooth turn. 副翼帮助飞机顺利转弯。|近义词:control surface|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *aileron* "little wing," diminutive of *aile* "wing," from Latin *ala*.
