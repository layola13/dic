godwit:/ˈɡɒdwɪt/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdwɪt/ (US)| n.| 杓鷸 |A large wading bird with a long bill, often found in coastal areas.| We saw a flock of godwits probing the mudflats for food. 我们看到一群杓鷸在泥滩上觅食。|
The godwit used its long bill to search for invertebrates in the sand.  这只杓鹬用它的长喙在沙子里寻找无脊椎动物。|
Synonyms: curlew, sandpiper |Antonyms: N/A |noun
