disciplinable:/ˈdɪsəplɪnəbl/
adjective
可训练的；可管教的；遵守纪律的
Capable of being disciplined; amenable to discipline.
例句：He is a disciplinable child who responds well to clear expectations. 他是一个可管教的孩子，对明确的期望反应良好。

例句：She has a disciplinable mind and can focus on complex tasks for extended periods. 她有着自律的头脑，可以长时间专注于复杂的任务。

近义词：trainable, teachable, manageable, controllable
反义词：unruly, incorrigible, intractable, wild
词性: adjective


词源:
From discipline + -able.  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to."  "Discipline" comes from the Latin word "disciplina," meaning "instruction, knowledge."
