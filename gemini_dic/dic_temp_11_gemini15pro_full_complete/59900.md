eddyless:/ˈedɪlɪs/
adj.
无涡流的；平静的
Without eddies; smooth-flowing.
The river flowed eddyless and silent. 这条河平静地、静静地流淌。
近义词:smooth, calm, still, tranquil
反义词:turbulent, swirling, agitated
词性:形容词

词源:
"eddyless" comes from "eddy" + "-less". "Eddy" originates from Old Norse "iða" meaning "whirlpool".  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking".
