non-technical:/ˌnɒnˈtɛknɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɛknɪkəl/ | adj. | 非技术性的，通俗的 | Not related to the technical details or aspects of a subject; easy to understand for people without specialized knowledge. | This is a non-technical guide to computers for beginners.  这是一本面向初学者的计算机非技术指南。

The book explains complex scientific concepts in non-technical language. 这本书用通俗易懂的语言解释了复杂的科学概念。

| 近义词：simple, easy-to-understand, layman's, popular | 反义词: technical, specialized, complex | 词性: adjective
