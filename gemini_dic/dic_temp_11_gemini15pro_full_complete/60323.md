egocentrism: /ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/ /ˌɛɡəʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/ | n. | 自我中心主义;  |The inability to see a situation from another person's point of view.  The assumption that others share one's own perspectives and experiences. |  Example: Her egocentrism prevented her from seeing how her actions affected others. 她的自我中心主义使她看不到自己的行为是如何影响别人的。

Example:  The child's egocentrism is developmentally normal at this age.  这个孩子在这个年龄段的自我中心主义在发展上是正常的。 | Near synonyms: self-centeredness, narcissism, self-absorption | Antonyms:  altruism, empathy, other-centeredness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *égocentrisme*, from *ego* (Latin *ego* "I") + *centre* (Latin *centrum* "center") + *-isme* (-ism). Coined by Jean Piaget.
