irrigators:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪtərz/
n.
灌溉者；灌溉器
People or devices that supply water to land or crops.
The irrigators worked tirelessly to water the fields during the drought.  灌溉者们在干旱期间不辞辛劳地给田地浇水。
Modern irrigators are much more efficient than traditional methods.  现代灌溉器比传统方法效率更高。
近义词:waterers
反义词:drainers
词性:名词


irrigator:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪtər/
n.
灌溉者；灌溉器

The irrigators were essential for maintaining the green lawns.  灌溉器对于维护绿色的草坪至关重要。
