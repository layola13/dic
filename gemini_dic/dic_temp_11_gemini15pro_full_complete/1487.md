acetabuliform:/æsɪˈtæbjʊlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
杯状的，髋臼状的
Shaped like an acetabulum (the socket of the hip joint).
The acetabuliform cavity provided a secure fit for the femoral head.  这个杯状的空腔为股骨头提供了一个安全的契合。
The fossilized bone displayed an acetabuliform structure. 这块骨骼化石呈现出杯状结构。
近义词: cup-shaped, concave
反义词: convex
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *acetabulum* (vinegar cup) + *-formis* (having the form of).  源自拉丁语 *acetabulum*（醋杯）+ *-formis*（具有...形状的）。
