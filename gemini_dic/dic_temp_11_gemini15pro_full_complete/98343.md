insinuator:/ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪtər/| n. |  insinuator (noun) | someone who insinuates; someone who subtly introduces something into conversation or someone's mind | He was a master insinuator, subtly planting doubts in the minds of his rivals. 他是一个散布谣言的高手，巧妙地在对手心中播下怀疑的种子。 |  The insinuator spread rumors about the company's financial troubles.  这个造谣者散布了关于公司财务问题的谣言。 | synonyms:  hint dropper, innuendo user,  rumormonger, gossipmonger,  backbiter, slanderer | antonyms:  frank person, forthright person,  straightforward person | noun
