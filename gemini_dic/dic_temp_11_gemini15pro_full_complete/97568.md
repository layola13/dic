ingrowing:/ɪnˈɡrəʊɪŋ/ (US: /ˈɪnˌɡroʊɪŋ/)
adjective
向内生长的；嵌入肉体的
growing inwards, especially into the flesh
My ingrowing toenail is very painful. 我的嵌甲很疼。
近义词: incarnated, embedded
反义词: outgrowing
词性: adjective


ingrown:/ˈɪnˌɡroʊn/ (US: /ɪnˈɡroʊn/)
adjective
向内生长的; 嵌生的; 内向的
growing inwards, especially of a toenail; firmly established and difficult to change
She had surgery to remove an ingrown toenail. 她做了手术切除了嵌甲。 He's very shy and ingrown. 他很害羞，很内向。
近义词: introverted, withdrawn (for the meaning "内向的")  incarnated, embedded (for the meaning "嵌生的")
反义词: extroverted (for the meaning "内向的")
词性: adjective
