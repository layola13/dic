duct tape: /ˈdʌkt teɪp/ (US) /ˈdʌkt teɪp/ (UK)| n.|管道胶带，强力胶带|A strong, cloth-backed, waterproof adhesive tape. It was originally developed for sealing heating ducts, hence the name.|I used duct tape to repair the tear in the tent. 我用管道胶带修补了帐篷上的裂口。|近义词：gaffer tape, duck tape|反义词: None|名词
