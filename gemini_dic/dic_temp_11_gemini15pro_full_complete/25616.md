bumpkinish: /ˈbʌmpkɪnɪʃ/
adjective
土气的，粗俗的
Having the characteristics of a bumpkin; unsophisticated, awkward, or socially inept.
He looked rather bumpkinish in his ill-fitting suit. 他穿着不合身的西装，看起来很土气。

She gave a bumpkinish laugh that made everyone uncomfortable. 她发出一声土气的笑声，让每个人都感到不舒服。

近义词:rustic, unsophisticated, awkward, clumsy, boorish, uncouth, countrified
反义词: sophisticated, urbane, polished, refined, elegant
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bumpkin + -ish.  Bumpkin's origin is uncertain, possibly from Middle Dutch *bommekijn "little barrel," referring to a clumsy or awkward person.
