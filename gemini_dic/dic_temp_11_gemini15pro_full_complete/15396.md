backwashing: /ˈbækˌwɒʃɪŋ/ /ˈbækˌwɑːʃɪŋ/
n.
反冲洗，逆洗
The process of cleaning a filter by reversing the flow of water through it.
Backwashing your pool filter helps remove debris and keep the water clean.  反冲洗你的泳池过滤器有助于去除碎屑并保持水质清洁。
Backwashing the filter is essential for maintaining water quality.  反冲洗过滤器对于保持水质至关重要。
cleaning, flushing
contamination

词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From back + wash + -ing.  Back in the sense of reverse, and wash referring to the cleansing action of water.
