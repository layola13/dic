creatinine: /kriˈætɪnin/ /kriˈætɪˌniːn/ | n. |肌酐|A waste product produced by muscles and excreted in urine. It is used as a marker of kidney function. |High creatinine levels may indicate kidney problems. 肌酐水平高可能表明肾脏有问题。|Creatinine is a breakdown product of creatine phosphate. 肌酐是磷酸肌酸的分解产物。|Synonym:  None (Creatinine is a specific chemical compound)|Antonym: None|Noun
