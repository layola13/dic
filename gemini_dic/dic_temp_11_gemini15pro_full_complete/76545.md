gallantry: /ˈɡæləntri/ | n. | 勇敢；殷勤；骑士精神 |Politeness and attentiveness to women; courageous behavior, especially in battle. | He showed great gallantry in rescuing the child from the burning building. 他在从着火的大楼里救出孩子时表现出了极大的勇敢。| 近义词: bravery, courage, chivalry, courtesy, valor | 反义词: cowardice, rudeness, discourtesy | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *galanterie "courtesy, valor," from galant "gallant," from Italian galante "courteous, brave," ultimately from a Germanic source.
