deliciously:/dɪˈlɪʃəsli/| adv.|美味地，可口地|In a very pleasant or enjoyable way, especially concerning taste.|The cake was deliciously moist and flavorful.  蛋糕美味可口，湿润而香浓。|Synonyms:  tastily, savorily, appetizingly|Antonyms: unpleasantly, distastefully, nastily|词性: 副词

delicious:/dɪˈlɪʃəs/| adj.|美味的，可口的|Highly pleasant to the taste.|The food was absolutely delicious.  这食物绝对美味。|Synonyms: tasty, savory, palatable|Antonyms: unappetizing, distasteful, nasty|词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *delicios (Modern French délicieux), from Latin dēliciōsus (“delightful”), from dēlicia (“delight”), from dēlicere (“to allure, entice”), from dē- (“from”) + lacere (“to entice, allure”).
