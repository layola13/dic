convertist:/ˈkɒnvɜːtɪst/ | n. | 皈依者;  改变信仰者 | A person who has been converted to a particular religion or belief. |  She was a convertist to Buddhism after years of searching for spiritual meaning. 她多年来一直在寻找精神意义，后来皈依了佛教。| synonyms: convert, proselyte | antonyms: apostate, heretic | noun

convertist:/kənˈvɜːrtɪst/ | n. | (尤指商业)转变者，转型者 | A person who changes or causes something to change from one form or use to another, especially in business.  | The company is looking for convertists who can adapt to the changing market. 这家公司正在寻找能够适应不断变化的市场的转型者。 | synonyms: innovator, reformer, transformer | antonyms: traditionalist, conservative | noun
