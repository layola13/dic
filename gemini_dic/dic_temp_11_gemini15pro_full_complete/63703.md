ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ /ɪˈfiːmərəl/ | adj. | 短暂的，转瞬即逝的 | Lasting for only a short time; transient. |  The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral.  樱花的美丽是短暂的。

The ephemeral joys of childhood. 童年转瞬即逝的快乐。

Synonyms: fleeting, transient, momentary, short-lived, brief, temporary

Antonyms: enduring, lasting, permanent, perpetual, eternal

词性: adjective
