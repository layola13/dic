burgles:/ˈbɜːrɡəlz/
verb|入室盗窃，偷窃|Enters a building illegally with intent to commit a crime, especially theft.|The thieves burgled the house and stole jewelry.  小偷入室盗窃，偷走了珠宝。|近义词：robs, steals, pilfers, breaks into|反义词:protects, guards|词性:v.
Etymology: From burglar + -s (third-person singular present tense). Ultimately from Anglo-Norman burgler, from Old French burgier.
