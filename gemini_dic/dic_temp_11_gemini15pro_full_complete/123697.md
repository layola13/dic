mooncake:/ˈmuːnˌkeɪk/| n.|月饼|A rich, round pastry traditionally eaten in China and other Asian countries during the Mid-Autumn Festival. It typically contains a sweet filling, such as red bean paste or lotus seed paste, and may also include a salted duck egg yolk.|例句：We ate mooncakes while watching the full moon. 我们一边赏月一边吃月饼。

例句：Mooncakes are a traditional food for the Mid-Autumn Festival. 月饼是中秋节的传统食物。
