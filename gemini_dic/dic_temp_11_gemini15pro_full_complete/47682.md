debuggability:/ˌdɪˌbʌɡəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可调试性 | The ease with which a program can be debugged.  The quality of a system that makes it easy to identify and correct defects. | This new software framework increases the debuggability of our code.  这个新的软件框架提高了我们代码的可调试性。 | synonyms: testability, maintainability | antonyms: complexity, opacity | noun
