imaginal:/ɪˈmædʒɪnəl/ | adj. | 想象的，成虫期的 | Relating to or occurring during the final stage of an insect's metamorphosis, the adult stage; of, relating to, or involving imagination | The butterfly is in its imaginal stage. 蝴蝶正处于成虫期。|  The child's imaginal world was full of magical creatures.  这个孩子的想象世界充满了神奇的生物。 | synonyms: adult, mature,  developed, fanciful, visionary | antonyms: larval, pupal, immature, undeveloped, realistic | adjective
