elaidin:/ɪˈleɪɪdɪn/
noun
反油酸甘油酯;硬脂精
A solid fat created by the hydrogenation of certain liquid unsaturated fats.  It is the trans isomer of olein.
Elaidin is produced by the action of nitrous acid on oleic acid.  反油酸甘油酯是由亚硝酸作用于油酸而产生的。

The elaidin test can differentiate between drying and non-drying oils. 反油酸甘油酯试验可以区分干性油和非干性油。

Synonyms: trans fat, hydrogenated fat
Antonyms: olein, unsaturated fat
