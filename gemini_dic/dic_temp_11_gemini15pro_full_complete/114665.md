malices:/ˈmælɪsɪz/
n.
恶意，怨恨
ill will; spite; the desire to harm someone
The defendant was motivated by pure malice.  被告的动机纯粹是恶意。
malice aforethought  预谋
近义词: spite, animosity, malevolence, rancor, venom, ill will
反义词: benevolence, goodwill, kindness
词性: noun (plural)


malice:/ˈmælɪs/
n.
恶意，怨恨
the intention or desire to do evil; ill will
She accused him of acting out of malice. 她指责他是出于恶意行事。
近义词: spite, animosity, malevolence, rancor, venom, ill will
反义词: benevolence, goodwill, kindness
词性: noun (singular)
