hereditable:/hɪˈrɛdɪtəbl/
adjective
可遗传的，可继承的
Capable of being inherited or passed down from one generation to the next.
Her eye color is hereditable, just like her mother's. 她的眼睛颜色是遗传的，就像她母亲的一样。
Genes determine which traits are hereditable. 基因决定哪些性状是可遗传的。
synonym: inheritable, transmissible, genetic
antonym: acquired, non-hereditary
词性: adjective
