bibliomaniacal: /ˌbɪbliəʊˈmeɪniəkəl/ | adj. | 藏书癖的；嗜书如命的 | Excessively fond of acquiring and possessing books, often regardless of their content or readability.  Characterized by an extreme passion for collecting books. | He has a bibliomaniacal obsession with first editions. 他对初版书有一种藏书癖般的痴迷。 | Synonyms: bibliophilic, bookish | Antonyms: indifferent (to books), apathetic (to reading) | adjective

bibliomania: /ˌbɪbliəʊˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 藏书癖； bibliomania | An excessive fondness for acquiring and possessing books, often regardless of their content or readability. | His bibliomania led him to spend a fortune on rare books. 他的藏书癖使他在珍本书上花了一大笔钱。 | Synonyms: book madness | Antonyms: indifference (to books) | noun
