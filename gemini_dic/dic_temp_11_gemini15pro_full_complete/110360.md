lightships:/ˈlaɪtˌʃɪps/
n.
灯船
A ship equipped with a bright light, anchored at a specific location to warn other ships of hazards or to mark a channel.
The lightship marked the entrance to the harbor. 灯船标志着港口的入口。

The lightship was a welcome sight after the storm.  暴风雨过后，看到灯船让人欣慰。

近义词：beacon, buoy
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "unmarked hazard")
词性: noun
