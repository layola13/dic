antiecclesiastic:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪk/
adjective
反对教会的；非教会的
Opposed to the church or ecclesiastical authority; secular.
His antiecclesiastic views often put him at odds with the local clergy. 他反对教会的观点经常使他与当地神职人员发生冲突。

antiecclesiastic:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪk/
noun
反对教会者
A person who opposes the church or ecclesiastical authority.
The antiecclesiastics rallied against the church's growing influence in the government. 反对教会的人们集会反对教会在政府中日益增长的影响力。


近义词:anticlerical, secular, irreligious
反义词:religious, pious, devout
词性:adjective, noun
