frontispiece: /ˈfrʌntɪspiːs/ | n. |卷首插画，扉页|An illustration facing the title page of a book.| The frontispiece of the book was a beautiful engraving. 这本书的卷首插画是一幅精美的雕刻画。| synonyms: illustration, plate, engraving, picture | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "back cover" in a loose sense) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *frontispice*, from Medieval Latin *frontispicium* "facade of a building," from Latin *frons* "forehead, front" + *specere* "to look."  The meaning shifted from "facade" to "illustrated leaf preceding the title page" in the 17th century.
