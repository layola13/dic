dipyridine:/ˌdaɪˈpɪrɪdɪn/ | n. | 二吡啶，联吡啶 | A chemical compound with two pyridine rings linked together. It exists in several isomeric forms. |  Dipyridine is often used as a ligand in coordination chemistry. 二吡啶常在配位化学中用作配体。|  The complex formed between iron and dipyridine exhibits intense color. 铁和二吡啶形成的络合物呈现出强烈的颜色。 | synonyms: bipyridine, bipyridyl | antonyms: N/A | noun
