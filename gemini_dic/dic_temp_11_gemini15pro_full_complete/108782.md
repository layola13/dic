leashing:/ˈliːʃɪŋ/
n.
拴住，束缚，控制
the act of putting a leash on an animal or person, or the state of being restrained by a leash.
The dog park requires leashing of all dogs.  狗公园要求所有狗都必须拴上狗绳。
The leashing of his creativity stifled his artistic expression.  束缚他的创造力扼杀了他的艺术表达。
restraint, control, confinement
freedom, liberation, release
noun
