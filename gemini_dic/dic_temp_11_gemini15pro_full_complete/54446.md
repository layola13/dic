disinherit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪt/ | vt. |剥夺继承权，取消继承资格|To prevent someone, usually a family member, from inheriting property or money.|He disinherited his son for marrying against his wishes. 他因为儿子违背他的意愿结婚而剥夺了他的继承权。| 近义词: cut off, exclude | 反义词: bequeath, endow | Verb

disinherit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪt/ | vt. |剥夺继承权，取消继承资格|To deprive of a rightful inheritance.|She was disinherited by her father. 她被她父亲剥夺了继承权。| 近义词: deprive, divest | 反义词:  bestow, grant | Verb
