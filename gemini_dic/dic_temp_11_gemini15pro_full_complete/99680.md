interlinked:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋkt/ | adj. | 互相连接的，相互关联的 | connected together, especially by a computer network or system | The two systems are interlinked so that data can be easily shared. 这两个系统相互连接，以便轻松共享数据。 |  The global economy is a complex web of interlinked markets. 全球经济是一个由相互关联的市场组成的复杂网络。 | synonyms: interconnected, interwoven, linked, related | antonyms: separate, disconnected, independent | adjective

interlink:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋk/ | v. |  (使)互相连接，(使)相互关联 | to connect things together, especially by a computer network or system | The new software interlinks all the departments in the company.  新软件将公司所有部门连接起来。 |  These issues are complex and interlink in many ways. 这些问题很复杂，并在许多方面相互关联。 | synonyms: connect, join, link, relate | antonyms: separate, disconnect, divide | verb
