nonlogicalities:/ˌnɒnˌlɒdʒɪˈkælɪtiz/
noun
不合逻辑之处；不合逻辑的想法
The argument was riddled with nonlogicalities.  这个论点充满了不合逻辑之处。
nonlogicalities are often the source of humor in absurdist theater.  不合逻辑之处常常是荒诞派戏剧的幽默来源。
Synonym:  inconsistencies, fallacies, absurdities
Antonym:  logic, reason, rationality
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

Etymology:  non- + logical + -ity + -es
"Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."
"Logical" comes from the Late Latin "logicalis,"  from the Greek "logikos," meaning "of or pertaining to reason or speech."
"-ity" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
"-es" is a plural suffix.
