cupidinous:/kjuːˈpɪdɪnəs/| adj.|好色的；贪恋酒色的；渴望的|Characterized by or expressing lustful desire; lecherous. Driven by strong desires, especially sexual ones.|例句：He cast a cupidinous glance at the waitress. 他对女服务员投以贪婪的目光。|例句：The cupidinous king was known for his many mistresses.  这位好色的国王以他的众多情妇而闻名。|近义词：lustful, lecherous, lascivious, carnal, desirous|反义词：chaste, virtuous, ascetic, platonic|词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *cupidus* ("desirous, eager, passionate"), from *cupere* ("to desire"). Related to *cupido* ("desire, longing, passion; Cupid").
