achromatically:/ˌeɪkrəˈmætɪkli/| adv.|无色地，非彩色地|Without color; in a way that relates to shades of gray, black, and white.|The artist rendered the portrait achromatically, focusing on form and texture rather than color.  这位艺术家以非彩色的方式绘制了这幅肖像，专注于形式和纹理而不是颜色。|Example: The data was presented achromatically to avoid distracting the audience with unnecessary color. 数据以非彩色的方式呈现，以避免不必要的颜色分散观众的注意力。|Synonyms:  monochromatically, neutrally|Antonyms: chromatically, colorfully|Part of Speech: Adverb
