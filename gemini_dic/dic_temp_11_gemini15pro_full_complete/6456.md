amplify-on:/ˈæmplɪfaɪ ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 详述，进一步阐述 | To expand or elaborate on something that has already been said, providing more details or information. |  Example:  The professor amplified on the topic of quantum physics, explaining the concepts in greater detail. 教授详细阐述了量子物理学的主题，更详细地解释了这些概念。

Example: Could you amplify on your earlier statement about the project's budget? 你能详细说明一下你之前关于项目预算的陈述吗？ | 近义词: elaborate on, expand on, expound on | 反义词: summarize, condense, abbreviate | 词性: 动词短语
