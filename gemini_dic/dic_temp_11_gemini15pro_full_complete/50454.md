depreciators:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtərz/ | n. |贬值者；折旧者| People or things that cause something to lose value. | The constant negative reviews from online depreciators hurt the company's image.  网上贬低者的持续负面评论损害了公司的形象。| Synonyms: detractors, critics, belittlers | Antonyms:  promoters, advocates, supporters | Noun


depreciate:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/ | v. |贬值；折旧；轻视|  1. To diminish in value over time. 2. To belittle or undervalue. |  1.  The car depreciated quickly after we bought it. 我们买了车之后，它很快就贬值了。 2. He tends to depreciate the contributions of others. 他倾向于轻视别人的贡献。 | Synonyms: devalue, decline, undervalue, belittle | Antonyms: appreciate, increase, enhance, value | Verb
