nonreferences:/ˌnɒnˈrɛfərənsɪz/ | n. | 非引用;不涉及 |  Lack of references or citations, typically in academic or legal contexts.  The absence of mentions or allusions to something. | The article's nonreferences to previous research made it seem less credible. 这篇文章没有提及之前的研究，使得它看起来不那么可信。 |  synonyms: omissions, exclusions | antonyms: citations, references | noun
