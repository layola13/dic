crybabying:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbiɪŋ/
n.|爱哭鬼行为，哭诉，抱怨|The constant crybabying was getting on everyone's nerves.  不断的哭诉让每个人都很烦。|English Explanation: The act of complaining or whining excessively, like a baby crying. |例句：Stop your crybabying and deal with the problem! 别再哭哭啼啼了，去解决问题吧！|近义词：whining, complaining, griping|反义词:bravery, stoicism|词性:noun
