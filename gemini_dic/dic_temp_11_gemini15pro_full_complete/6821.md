anarthrous:/ænˈɑːrθrəs/| adj.|无冠词的，不用冠词的|Lacking a definite or indefinite article.  It refers to a noun or noun phrase that appears without a determiner (like "the," "a," or "an").  In Greek grammar, it specifically refers to nouns without the definite article.|The word "God" is often anarthrous in theological texts.|Synonyms: articleless, determinerless|Antonyms: articular (though rarely used)|adjective
