convince:/kənˈvɪns/| vt.|说服，使确信|To persuade someone to believe something or to do something.|例句：I managed to convince him to come with us. 我设法说服了他和我们一起来。

例句：She convinced me that I was wrong. 她使我确信我错了。

|近义词：persuade, assure, satisfy|反义词：dissuade, discourage|词性:动词

词源:  From Old French *convincre (Modern French convaincre), from Latin convincō (“I overcome, convict, prove wrong, refute”), from con- (“together, with”) + vincō (“I conquer, overcome”).
