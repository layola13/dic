moulter:/ˈmoʊltər (US), ˈməʊltər (UK)| n.|换羽的动物; 蜕皮的动物|An animal that is moulting, shedding its feathers, skin, or other outer covering.|The moulter was barely recognizable as a hawk with its patchy feathers.|近义词：shedder|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用“growing”表示正在生长新的覆盖物)|词性:noun

moulting:/ˈmoʊltɪŋ (US), ˈməʊltɪŋ (UK)| n.|换羽; 蜕皮|The process of shedding feathers, skin, or other outer covering.|The bird's moulting season is in late summer.|近义词：shedding, ecdysis|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun

molt:/moʊlt (US), məʊlt (UK)| v. |换羽; 蜕皮|To shed feathers, skin, or other outer covering.|The snake molts its skin several times a year.|近义词：shed, exuviate|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mutian, from Proto-Germanic *mutijaną, related to Old Norse mūta "to change."  The spelling with -l- influenced by moult (n.), from Old French mue "change, moulting," from muer "to change, moult," from Latin mutare "to change."
