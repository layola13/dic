coopers:/ˈkuːpərz/| n.|桶匠，制桶工人；桶匠铺|A cooper is someone who makes or repairs wooden barrels or tubs.|The coopers were busy repairing the old wine barrels. 桶匠们正忙着修理旧的酒桶。|近义词：barrel maker|反义词:|n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *couper*, from Old French *cupier*, from Late Latin *cuparius*, from Latin *cupa* ("tub, cask, vat").
