ETYMOLOGY->
From "flow" (meaning a smooth, continuous movement) + "diagram" (a simplified drawing showing the components of something).  "Flow" comes from Old English "flōwan," while "diagram" comes from Greek "diagramm," from "diagraphein" meaning "to mark out by lines."
