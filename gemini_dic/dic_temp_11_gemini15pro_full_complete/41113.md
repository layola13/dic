coproducer:/ˌkoʊprəˈduːsər/ | n. | 共同制片人 |  A person who shares responsibility with one or more other people for the production of a film, play, broadcast, or other creative work. |  Example: The coproducer worked closely with the director throughout the filming process. 共同制片人自始至终与导演紧密合作拍摄。

Example: She was listed as a coproducer on the album credits. 她在专辑的制作名单上被列为共同制片人。 |  Synonym: joint producer, co-maker | Antonym: N/A | noun
