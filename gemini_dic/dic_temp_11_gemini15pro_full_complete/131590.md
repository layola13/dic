noncompeting:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpiːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非竞争性的 | Not competing; not involved in competition.  Relating to goods or services that are not directly competitive with each other because they serve different needs or appeal to different markets. |  Example 1: Noncompeting goods, like cars and computers, are unlikely to be affected by price changes in the other market. 非竞争性商品，例如汽车和电脑，不太可能受到对方市场价格变化的影响。

Example 2: The two companies operate in noncompeting markets. 这两家公司在非竞争性市场中运营。 | Near Synonyms: noncompetitive, complementary | Opposite: competing, rival | adjective
