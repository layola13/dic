occiduous:/ɒˈsɪdʒuəs/
adj.
落叶的;脱落的
(of a tree or shrub) shedding its leaves annually.
例句：Oak trees are occiduous, losing their leaves in autumn. 橡树是落叶树，在秋天落叶。

例句：The occiduous forest blazed with color in the fall. 秋天，落叶林色彩斑斓。
近义词：deciduous
反义词：evergreen
词性：adjective
