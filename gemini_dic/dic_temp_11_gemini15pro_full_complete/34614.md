cledgy:/ˈklɛdʒi/
adjective
多峭壁的; 岩石突兀的
Characterized by or having clegs or cliffs; craggy.
The cledgy coastline offered stunning views.  多峭壁的海岸线提供了令人惊叹的景色。
The climbers scaled the cledgy face of the mountain. 登山者攀登了岩石突兀的山峰。
近义词:craggy, rocky, cliffy
反义词:smooth, flat
词性:adjective

词源: From "cleg" + "-y".  "Cleg" comes from Old Norse "kleggi" meaning a rock.
