leprechaun: /ˈlɛprəkɔːn/ | n. | 小妖精，矮妖 | A small, mischievous sprite in Irish folklore, typically having magical powers and guarding a treasure of gold. |  Example: Legend says that if you catch a leprechaun, it will grant you three wishes.  传说如果你抓到一个小妖精，它会满足你三个愿望。

Example: The children were searching for leprechauns at the end of the rainbow. 孩子们在彩虹的尽头寻找小妖精。 | Synonym: fairy, pixie, elf, sprite | Antonym: giant | noun
