moonshots:/ˈmuːnʃɒts/ (US) /ˈmuːnʃɒts/ (UK)| n.| 登月，远大目标，雄心勃勃的项目 |A project that is very ambitious and difficult to achieve, but which if successful would be a major breakthrough. |Example:  Investing in renewable energy is one of the government's key moonshots. 投资可再生能源是政府的关键登月计划之一。

Example:  The company is known for its moonshots, like developing self-driving cars. 这家公司以其雄心勃勃的项目而闻名，比如开发自动驾驶汽车。|近义词：ambitious projects, long shots, groundbreaking projects|反义词:small steps, incremental progress|词性:名词
