badland:/ˈbædˌlænd/| n.|荒地；劣地|A region of barren, heavily eroded land with clay soil and little vegetation, typically in arid or semi-arid climates. |The movie was filmed in the badlands of South Dakota. 这部电影是在南达科他州的荒地拍摄的。|The explorers struggled to cross the treacherous badlands. 探险者们艰难地穿越了险恶的荒地。|近义词：wasteland, desert, barren|反义词:fertile land, grassland|名词

词源:
From bad + land, referring to the difficulty of traversing such terrain.  源自 bad + land，指的是穿越这种地形很困难.
