coeval:/koʊˈiːvəl/ /kəʊˈiːvəl/
adjective
同龄的；同时代的；同时期的
Of the same age, date, or duration; contemporary.
例句：The poet was coeval with the dramatist.  这位诗人和剧作家是同一时代的。

例句：The two ancient civilizations were coeval. 这两个古老的文明是同时代的。

近义词：contemporary, synchronous, concurrent
反义词：noncontemporary, asynchronous, earlier, later
词性: adjective
