nuzzling: /ˈnʌzlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈnʌzlɪŋ/ (UK)
|n. |依偎；挨近；轻蹭|The act of rubbing one's nose or face against something affectionately.|The cat was nuzzling against its owner's leg. 猫在主人的腿上蹭来蹭去。|近义词：cuddling, snuggling, nestling|反义词: separating, distancing|词性: 名词/动名词

nuzzle: /ˈnʌzl/ (US), /ˈnʌzl/ (UK)
|v. |用鼻子轻触；依偎；挨近|To rub or push gently against something with the nose or face.|The horse nuzzled my hand looking for an apple. 马用鼻子轻触我的手，想找苹果吃。|近义词：cuddle, snuggle, nestle|反义词: separate, distance|词性: 动词
