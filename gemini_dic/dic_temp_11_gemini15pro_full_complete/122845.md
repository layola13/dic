词源: From the Persian word "yāsmin" via Arabic "yāsamīn" and ultimately from Sanskrit "yāsmin."  The English word "jasmine" shares this etymology.  "Moli" is the Chinese name for jasmine and has been adopted into English primarily to refer to jasmine tea.
