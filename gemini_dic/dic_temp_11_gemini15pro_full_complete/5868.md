ambassador: /æmˈbæsədər/ | n. | 大使；代表；使节 | A diplomat of the highest rank, sent by one country to another as its official representative.  |  The ambassador presented his credentials to the president. 大使向总统递交了国书。 | Synonyms: envoy, diplomat, legate, emissary | Antonyms:  None (This word doesn't have a direct antonym.) | noun

ambassador: /æmˈbæsədər/ | n. | （某物的）代表，代言人 | A person who represents or promotes a particular organization, group, or brand. | She's an ambassador for the new line of athletic wear. 她是新系列运动服的代言人。 | Synonyms: representative, spokesperson, advocate | Antonyms:  None (This word doesn't have a direct antonym.) | noun
