lairdship:/ˈleɪrdʃɪp/| n.|领主地位；领主的身份；领地|The position, status, or estate of a laird (a Scottish landowner).|He inherited the lairdship from his father. 他从他父亲那里继承了领主的地位。|The lairdship includes a large manor house and several farms.  这片领地包括一座很大的庄园宅邸和几个农场。|近义词：estate, domain, lordship|反义词: servitude, tenancy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *laird* + *-ship*.  *laird* is from Middle English *laverd, loverd*, from Old English *hlāford* (meaning "lord"), from *hlāf* ("loaf") + *weard* ("keeper, guardian").  *-ship* is a suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
