momentousness:/moʊˈmɛntəsnəs/| n.|重大，重要性|The momentousness of the occasion was not lost on anyone. 没有人没有意识到这个场合的重要性。|The signing of the peace treaty was an event of great momentousness. 签署和平条约是一件具有重大意义的事件。|近义词：importance, significance, gravity, weight|反义词：insignificance, triviality|词性:名词
Etymology: From momentous + -ness. Momentous is from Latin moment- (stem of momentum “movement, moving power,” from movēre “to move”) + -ous.
