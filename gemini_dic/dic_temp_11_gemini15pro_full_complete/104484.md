kakapo:/ˈkɑːkəpəʊ/ (UK), /ˈkɑːkəpoʊ/ (US)| n. |鸮鹦鹉|A large, flightless, nocturnal parrot of New Zealand, with greenish plumage, a distinctive facial disc of sensory feathers, and a low booming call. It is critically endangered.|例句：The kakapo is a unique and fascinating bird. 鸮鹦鹉是一种独特而迷人的鸟类。

例句：Conservation efforts are underway to save the kakapo from extinction.  人们正在努力保护鸮鹦鹉免于灭绝。|近义词：parrot (general term), owl parrot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Māori *kākāpō*, from *kākā* (“parrot”) + *pō* (“night”).
