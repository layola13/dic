honest:/ˈɑːnɪst/ | adj. | 诚实的，正直的 | Free from fraud or deception; truthful. | She is an honest woman. 她是一位诚实的女性。|  He gave an honest account of what happened. 他如实讲述了事情的经过。 | 近义词：truthful, sincere, upright, frank, candid | 反义词：dishonest, deceitful, untruthful, lying | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *honest*, from Old French *honeste*, from Latin *honestus* (“honorable, respectable, dignified”), from *honos* (“honor, reputation”).
