aeriform:/ˈɛərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
气态的，空气状的
Having the form or nature of air; gaseous.
例句：The aeriform substance floated gently upwards.  气态物质轻轻地向上飘浮。

例句：The scientist studied the properties of the aeriform compound.  科学家研究了这种气态化合物的性质。

近义词：gaseous, airy, ethereal
反义词：solid, liquid, dense
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *aeriformis, from aer ("air") + -formis ("-form").
