opisthocoelous: /ˌɒpɪsˈθoʊˈsiːləs/ | adj. | 后凹的 | Having centra (the main body of a vertebra) that are concave posteriorly and convex anteriorly.  This refers to the shape of the vertebrae in some animals. | The opisthocoelous vertebrae of some amphibians allow for greater flexibility in the neck. 一些两栖动物的后凹椎骨使颈部更加灵活。| 近义词: concave posteriorly | 反义词: procoelous | 词性: adjective
