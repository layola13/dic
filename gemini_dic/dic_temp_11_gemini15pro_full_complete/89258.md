hominins:/ˈhɒmɪnɪnz/ |n.| 人族，人类 |A member of the subtribe Hominina, which includes modern humans and all our immediate ancestors (including members of the genera *Homo*, *Australopithecus*, *Paranthropus*, and *Ardipithecus*).| Early hominins used tools. 早期人族使用工具。| synonyms: hominids, humans, human ancestors | antonyms: (no direct antonym, perhaps other primates) | noun
