flexural:/ˈflekʃərəl/| adj.|弯曲的，挠曲的|Relating to or involving bending.|The flexural strength of concrete is crucial for building bridges.  混凝土的抗弯强度对于桥梁建设至关重要。|Example: The engineer calculated the flexural stress on the beam. 工程师计算了梁上的弯曲应力。|Synonyms: bending, flexible, pliant|Antonyms: rigid, inflexible, stiff|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *flexūrālis, from Latin flexūra (“a bending”), from flexus, past participle of flectere (“to bend”).
