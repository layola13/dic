curatorial:/kjʊərəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
策展的；馆长的
Relating to a curator or their duties.  Pertaining to the selection, organization, and care of a collection, especially in a museum or art gallery.
The museum's curatorial staff is highly knowledgeable.  博物馆的策展人员知识渊博。
The curatorial decisions were controversial. 策展决定备受争议。
Synonyms:  museum, gallery, archival, collection-based
Antonyms:  unorganized, chaotic, haphazard
词性: adjective
