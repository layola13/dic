nonpliably:/nɒnˈplaɪəbli/
adverb
不灵活地，顽固地
in a nonpliable manner; inflexibly; stubbornly.
The metal sheet resisted being bent, behaving nonpliably.  这块金属板难以弯曲，表现得很不灵活。
The old man nonpliably refused to change his mind. 老人固执地拒绝改变主意。
近义词:inflexibly, rigidly, unyieldingly
反义词:flexibly, pliably, yieldingly
词性:adverb

nonpliant:/nɒnˈplaɪənt/
adjective
不灵活的，顽固的
not pliant; inflexible; stubborn.
The nonpliant material made it difficult to create the desired shape. 这种不灵活的材料使得难以创造出想要的形状。
His nonpliant attitude frustrated the negotiators. 他不妥协的态度让谈判人员感到沮丧。
近义词:inflexible, rigid, unyielding
反义词:flexible, pliant, yielding
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + pliant, from Old French pliant, present participle of plier "to bend," from Latin plicare "to fold."
