demain:/dəmɛ̃/| adv.|明天|Tomorrow; in the future.  It comes from Old French and ultimately from Latin. |See you demain! 明天见！|Synonyms: tomorrow, shortly, soon|Antonyms: yesterday, previously|Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *demain, from Vulgar Latin *de mane (“from morning”), from Latin de (“from”) + mane (“morning”).
