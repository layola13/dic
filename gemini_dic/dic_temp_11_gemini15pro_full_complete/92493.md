hypothenar:/ˌhaɪpəˈθiːnər/ | n. | 小鱼际，小鱼际肌 | The fleshy prominence on the palm of the hand at the base of the little finger.  It is formed by the intrinsic muscles of the hand. |  The hypothenar muscles control the movement of the little finger. 小鱼际肌控制小指的运动。| The hypothenar eminence is less prominent than the thenar eminence. 小鱼际隆起不如大鱼际隆起明显。 | synonyms: hypothenar eminence, hypothenar prominence | antonyms: thenar | noun
