one-sided:/ˌwʌnˈsaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 片面的，单方的，不公平的 | Favoring one person or group over others; unfair or biased. | The media coverage of the election was one-sided and only presented one candidate's perspective. 媒体对选举的报道是片面的，只呈现了一位候选人的观点。

The judge's decision seemed one-sided, as he clearly favored the prosecution.  法官的判决似乎有失公允，因为他明显偏袒控方。


近义词：biased, prejudiced, unfair, partial, lopsided | 反义词：balanced, impartial, fair, objective, two-sided | 词性：adjective
