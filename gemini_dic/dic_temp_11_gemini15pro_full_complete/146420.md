overfoolishly: /ˌoʊvərˈfuːlɪʃli/
adverb
过于愚蠢地
in an excessively foolish manner
He overfoolishly invested all his money in a single stock. 他过于愚蠢地把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。
She overfoolishly trusted a stranger with her life savings. 她过于愚蠢地把一生的积蓄托付给一个陌生人。
Synonym: excessively foolishly, ridiculously, absurdly, preposterously
Antonym: wisely, prudently, judiciously
词性: adverb

词源:
over- (prefix):  from Old English *ofer- "over, above, beyond," from Proto-Germanic *ubar (source also of Old Saxon obar, Old Frisian over, Old Norse yfir, Dutch over, Old High German ubar, German über, Gothic ufar), from PIE *uper "over, above" (source also of Sanskrit upari "over, above, upon," Avestan upairi, Greek hyper, Latin super, Old Church Slavonic vuz-, Lithuanian viršùs "top").

foolish (adjective): from Old English folisc "foolish," from fol "fool" + -isc.

-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives.
