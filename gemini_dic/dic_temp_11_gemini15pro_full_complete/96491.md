indivisibly:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪzəbli/
adv.
不可分割地
in a way that cannot be divided
例句：The two countries are indivisibly linked by history and culture. 这两个国家被历史和文化不可分割地联系在一起。

例句：These concepts are indivisibly connected. 这些概念是不可分割地联系在一起的。

近义词：inseparably, unitedly
反义词：separately, divisibly
词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + divisible (capable of being divided) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *in- (not) + dividere (to divide) + -ibilis (capable of being) + -ly.
