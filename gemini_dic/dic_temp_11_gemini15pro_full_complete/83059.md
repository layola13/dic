gurjun:/ˈɡɜːrdʒən/ | n. | 古云香脂，木油，古云树 | A reddish-brown oleoresin obtained from various trees of the genus Dipterocarpus, native to Southeast Asia. It is used in varnishes, paints, and as a wood preservative. |  The gurjun oil is used in traditional medicine.  古云香脂在传统医学中使用。

Gurjun trees are a valuable source of timber. 古云树是重要的木材来源。 | 近义词:keruing (克鲁因木，同属龙脑香科)| 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi and Urdu *gurjún*, from Sanskrit *garjana* "roaring" (referring to the sound the tree makes in the wind).
