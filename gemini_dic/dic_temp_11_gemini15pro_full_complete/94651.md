impressionist:/ɪmˈprɛʃənɪst/
n.|印象派艺术家；印象主义者|An artist who paints in the style of Impressionism. | Monet is a famous impressionist. 莫奈是一位著名的印象派艺术家。| synonym: Impressionistic painter, plein air painter | antonym: realist, classicist | noun
impressionistic:/ɪmˈprɛʃənˈɪstɪk/
adj.|印象派的；印象主义的|Relating to or characteristic of Impressionism, an artistic style that emphasizes the subjective impression of a scene or subject, typically using short brushstrokes and vivid colors. | The painting had a dreamy, impressionistic quality. 这幅画有一种梦幻般的印象派特质。| synonym: evocative, suggestive, atmospheric | antonym: realistic, detailed, precise | adjective
impressionism:/ɪmˈprɛʃənɪzəm/
n.|印象派；印象主义|A style of painting developed in France in the late 19th century, characterized by short brushstrokes and an emphasis on capturing the fleeting effects of light and color. | Impressionism revolutionized the art world. 印象派彻底改变了艺术世界。| synonym: Impressionist movement, plein air painting | antonym: realism, classicism | noun
