autotheism:/ˌɔːtəʊˈθiːɪzəm/
n.
自我神化；将自己视为神
The belief that oneself is God or equivalent to God.
He explored the concept of autotheism in his philosophical writings. 他在他的哲学著作中探讨了自我神化的概念。

Autotheism is a complex philosophical concept. 自我神化是一个复杂的概念。

近义词：self-deification, apotheosis of self
反义词：atheism, nontheism
词性：noun
