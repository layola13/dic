corinthianize:/kəˌrɪnθiənaɪz/
verb
科林斯化；模仿科林斯风格；放荡；奢侈
to imitate or adopt the luxurious or licentious lifestyle associated with ancient Corinth.
The wealthy merchant corinthianized his home with lavish decorations and imported marble.  这位富商用奢华的装饰和进口大理石将他的家科林斯化。
The emperor was criticized for corinthianizing the imperial palace with excessive opulence.  皇帝因过度奢华地科林斯化皇宫而受到批评。
近义词:luxuriate, indulge, debauch
反义词:simplify, economize, abstain
词性:v.

corinthianizing:/kəˌrɪnθiənaɪzɪŋ/
noun
科林斯化；模仿科林斯风格的行为
The act of imitating or adopting the luxurious or licentious lifestyle associated with ancient Corinth.
The corinthianizing of the city's elite was a sign of their increasing wealth and declining morals. 城市精英的科林斯化是他们日益增长的财富和道德沦丧的标志。
Critics condemned the corinthianizing of public spaces as a waste of resources. 批评人士谴责公共场所的科林斯化是浪费资源。
近义词:debauchery, extravagance, luxury
反义词:austerity, simplicity, moderation
词性:n.

词源: From Corinth, an ancient Greek city known for its wealth and luxurious lifestyle.  来自科林斯，一个以财富和奢华生活方式闻名的古希腊城市。
