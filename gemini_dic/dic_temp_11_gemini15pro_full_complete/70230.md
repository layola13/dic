fickle:/ˈfɪk(ə)l/| adj.|善变的，反复无常的|Changing frequently, especially as regards one's loyalties, interests, or affection.|例句：He was fickle in his affections, falling in and out of love easily. 他用情不专一，很容易坠入爱河，也很容易变心。

例句：The fickle weather made it difficult to plan a picnic.  变化无常的天气使得计划野餐很困难。

近义词：capricious, erratic, mercurial, volatile, whimsical|反义词：constant, faithful, loyal, steadfast, steady|词性:形容词

fickle:/ˈfɪk(ə)l/| adj.|易变的，不稳定的|Liable to sudden unpredictable change.|例句：The stock market can be fickle and unpredictable. 股票市场可能变幻莫测。

例句：Public opinion can be fickle, shifting with the latest news.  公众舆论可能反复无常，随着最新消息而变化。

近义词：changeable, variable, unsteady|反义词：stable, consistent|词性:形容词
