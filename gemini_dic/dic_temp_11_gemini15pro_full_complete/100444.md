intertrial:/ˌɪntərˈtraɪəl/
adjective
试验间的，两次试验之间的
Occurring between trials, especially in a psychological experiment.
The intertrial interval was 30 seconds. 两次试验之间的间隔为30秒。
Synonyms: between-trials
Antonyms: intratrial
词性: adjective
