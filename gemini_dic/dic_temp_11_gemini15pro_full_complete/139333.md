obovate:/ˈoʊboʊveɪt/
adjective
倒卵形的
Having a shape resembling an egg with the broader end at the apex.
The leaf is obovate, broadest near the tip.  叶子是倒卵形的，靠近尖端最宽。
obovate leaves have their widest point near the rounded apex. 倒卵形的叶子在靠近圆形顶点的地方最宽。
synonyms: obovoid, inversely ovate
antonyms: ovate, lanceolate
词性: adjective
