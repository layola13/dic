dupe:/djuːp/ /duːp/| n. vt.|欺骗；骗子；易受骗的人|a person who is easily tricked or fooled.  To deceive; trick.|例句：The con man duped several elderly people out of their savings. 那个骗子骗走了几个老人的积蓄。
He was duped into investing in a fraudulent scheme. 他被骗投资了一个欺诈性的计划。|近义词：fool, deceive, trick, con, swindle|反义词: undeceive, enlighten|词性:n. vt.
dupe:/djuːp/ /duːp/| n.|复制品|a copy or replica, especially one that is intended to deceive. |例句：The painting was a near-perfect dupe of the original. 这幅画几乎是原作的完美复制品。
The counterfeit money was a clever dupe. 这假钞是一张巧妙的复制品。|近义词：copy, replica, imitation, counterfeit|反义词: original, authentic|词性:n.
