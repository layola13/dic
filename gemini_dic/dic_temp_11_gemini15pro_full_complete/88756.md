hockle:/ˈhɒkəl/ (UK), /ˈhɑːkəl/ (US)| v. | 欺骗，诈骗；使跛；弯曲 | To disable or hamstring (usually an animal, sometimes a person) by cutting the tendons of the hock; to cheat or swindle. | 
The farmer hockled the horse to prevent it from wandering off.  农民将马的腿筋割断，以防止它走失。

The con man hockled the elderly woman out of her life savings. 骗子骗走了老妇人的毕生积蓄。


| 近义词: hamstring, cripple, disable, cheat, swindle | 反义词: enable, heal, cure, repair |词性: 动词|
