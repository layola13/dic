impending:/ɪmˈpɛndɪŋ/| adj.|即将发生的，迫近的|about to happen; imminent|例句：The impending storm worried the sailors. 即将到来的暴风雨让水手们担忧。

例句：She felt a sense of impending doom. 她有一种不祥的预感。

近义词：imminent, approaching, forthcoming, nearing|反义词：distant, remote, past, bygone|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *impendens, present participle of impendere (“to overhang, be imminent, threaten”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + pendere (“to hang, be suspended”).
