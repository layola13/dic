monochromous:/ˌmɒnəˈkrəʊməs/ /ˌmɑːnəˈkroʊməs/| adj.|单色的，单频的|Having or consisting of one color or hue.  Relating to radiation of a single wavelength or frequency.|The artist is known for his monochromous paintings, using only shades of blue.  这位艺术家以其单色画而闻名，只使用蓝色调。|The laser emitted a monochromous beam of light. 激光器发出单色光束。|近义词：monochromatic, one-color, single-hued|反义词：polychromatic, multicolored, varicolored|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek mónos (μόνος, “alone, single”) +  khrôma (χρῶμα, “color”).
