languishers:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃərz/
n.
憔悴的人，萎靡不振的人
people who are experiencing stagnation and a lack of progress in their lives, often feeling joyless and aimless.
Many employees are languishers, feeling stuck in their current roles. 许多员工都处于萎靡不振的状态，感觉被困在目前的职位上。
synonyms: idlers, dawdlers, sufferers
antonyms: achievers, thrivers, dynamos
noun

languish:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ/
v.
变得衰弱；失去活力
to lose or lack vitality; to grow weak or feeble.
The flowers languished in the hot sun. 花朵在烈日下枯萎了。
synonyms: weaken, deteriorate, decline
antonyms: thrive, flourish, prosper
verb
