bet:/bet/ (UK) /bɛt/ (US)| n. v.| 打赌，赌注|An agreement in which people try to guess what will happen and the person who guesses wrong has to give something, such as money, to the person who guesses right. | I bet you ten dollars that he'll be late. 我赌十美元他会迟到。| synonyms: wager, gamble, stake| antonyms: abstain, decline| noun, verb

bet:/bet/ (UK) /bɛt/ (US)| v.|  敢说，确信|Used to express your belief that something is true or likely to happen. | I bet she's at home now. 我敢说她现在在家。| synonyms: believe, suppose, assume| antonyms: doubt, question| verb
