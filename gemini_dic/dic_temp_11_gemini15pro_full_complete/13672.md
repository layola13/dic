attractive:/əˈtræktɪv/ | adj. | 吸引人的，有魅力的 | Pleasing or appealing to the senses. | She has an attractive personality. 她拥有迷人的个性。|  He found the offer financially attractive. 他觉得这份工作在经济方面很有吸引力。 | 近义词: appealing, charming, alluring, captivating, magnetic | 反义词: unattractive, unappealing, repulsive, repellent | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin attractivus, from attrahere ‘draw to oneself’, from ad- ‘to’ + trahere ‘draw’.
