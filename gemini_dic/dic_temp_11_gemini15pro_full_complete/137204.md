nonsupplementary:/nɒnˌsʌplɪˈmentəri/ | adj. | 非补充的，不补充的 | Not supplementary; not serving to supplement something else.  Specifically (of two angles): not adding up to 180 degrees. |  The two angles are nonsupplementary, as their sum is less than 180 degrees. 这两个角是非补充的，因为它们的和小于180度。 |  noncomplementary, disjunctive | supplementary, complementary | adjective
