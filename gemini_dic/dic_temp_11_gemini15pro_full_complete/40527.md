controllerships:/kənˈtroʊlərʃɪps/
n.
主计长职位；主计长任期
The position or term of office of a controller.
The company is seeking a qualified candidate for the controllership.  该公司正在寻找合格的候选人担任主计长。
She held the controllership for five years. 她担任了五年的主计长。
近义词:comptrollership, controller's office
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps lack of control or oversight)
词性: noun
