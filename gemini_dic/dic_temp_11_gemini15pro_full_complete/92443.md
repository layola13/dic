hypophosphite:/ˌhaɪpəʊˈfɒsfaɪt/| n. |次磷酸盐|a salt of hypophosphorous acid (H3PO2).  It contains the hypophosphite anion (H2PO2−).|Sodium hypophosphite is used as a reducing agent in electroless nickel plating. 次磷酸钠在化学镀镍中用作还原剂。|近义词:phosphite (though chemically different)|反义词:N/A|noun

hypophosphite:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈfɒsfaɪt/| n. |次磷酸酯|An ester of hypophosphorous acid.|Certain hypophosphites are used in organic synthesis. 某些次磷酸酯用于有机合成。|近义词:phosphite ester (though chemically different)|反义词:N/A|noun
