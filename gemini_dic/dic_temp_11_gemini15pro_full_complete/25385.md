bulbosity:/bʌlˈbɒsɪti/
noun
球茎状，膨胀，肿胀
The state or quality of being bulbous; a swollen or rounded shape.
The bulbosity of the onion made it difficult to chop.  洋葱的球茎状使它很难切碎。
The plant's bulbosity was a sign of disease.  这种植物的肿胀是疾病的征兆。
近义词: swelling, protuberance, roundness
反义词: slenderness, thinness
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bulbous + -ity.  Bulbous ultimately derives from the Latin "bulbus," meaning "onion."
