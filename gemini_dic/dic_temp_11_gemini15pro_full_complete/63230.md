enterocoele:/ˈentərəˌsiːl/| n. |肠体腔|A body cavity formed by the outpouching of the primitive gut (archenteron).  A coelom formed by the outpouching of a mesodermal sac from the archenteron/primitive gut.  Animals with this type of coelom are known as enterocoelomates. |The starfish is an example of an enterocoele. 海星是肠体腔动物的一个例子。|Synonym:  enterocoel, archicoelomate |Antonym: schizocoel, pseudocoelomate|noun
