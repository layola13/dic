hirling:/ˈhɜːrlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhɜːlɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|雇工，佣工；受雇于不光彩工作的人|A person who is hired, especially for unskilled labor, often with connotations of being easily replaceable or exploited. |The farmer hired several hirlings to help with the harvest. 农场主雇佣了几个短工帮忙收割。

The ruthless CEO treated his employees like expendable hirlings.  这位无情的CEO把他的员工当作可随时替换的雇工。|近义词：worker, laborer, employee, hand, drudge|反义词：employer, boss, manager|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hirlinge, from hiren (“to hire”), from Old English hyrian (“to hire”).
