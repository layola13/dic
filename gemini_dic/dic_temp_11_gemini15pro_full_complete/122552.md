modelling:/ˈmɒdlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəlɪŋ/ (US)| n. |建模，造型，模特工作|The process of creating a simplified representation of something, often for the purpose of understanding, analyzing, or predicting its behavior.  It can also refer to working as a model.|
She studied financial modelling. 她学习了金融建模。
He started his career in modelling. 他开始了他的模特生涯。|近义词：simulation, representation, posing|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


modelling:/ˈmɒdlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəlɪŋ/ (US)| v. |模仿;塑造;当模特|present participle of *model* - To create a model of something.  It can also mean to work as a model.|
He's modelling the new uniform. 他在展示新制服。
She's modelling herself on her older sister.  她正在模仿她的姐姐。|近义词：imitating, shaping, posing|反义词: N/A|词性:verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "model," which comes from Old French "modelle" and Italian "modello," ultimately derived from Latin "modulus" meaning "a small measure" or "standard."
