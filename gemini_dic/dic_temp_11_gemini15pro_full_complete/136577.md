nonsalutariness:/ˌnɒnsæljuːˈtɛrɪnəs/
n.
无益; 不利; 不健康
lack of salutariness; the state of being detrimental to health or well-being.
His nonsalutariness was evident in his constant negativity and unhealthy habits. 他的不健康状态体现在他持续的消极情绪和不健康的习惯中。

近义词:unhealthiness, harmfulness, detriment, noxiousness
反义词:salubrity, healthfulness, wholesomeness
