dilated:/daɪˈleɪtɪd/ /dɪˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
扩张的；扩大的；放大的
Made wider or larger; expanded.
Her pupils were dilated. 她的瞳孔放大了。

dilated:/daɪˈleɪtɪd/ /dɪˈleɪtɪd/
verb
（使）扩大，（使）膨胀，（使）扩张
To make or become wider, larger, or more open.
The drug dilates the blood vessels. 这种药能扩张血管。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dilatatus, past participle of dilatare "to spread out," from dis- "apart" + latus "wide."

SYNONYMS->
expanded, enlarged, widened, distended, stretched

ANTONYMS->
contracted, constricted, narrowed, shrunk
