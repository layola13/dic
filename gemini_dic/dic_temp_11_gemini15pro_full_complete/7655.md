annunciation:/əˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/| n. |宣告，宣布；（尤指）天使报喜|the act of announcing something, especially the announcement of the Incarnation by the angel Gabriel to the Virgin Mary|例句：The Annunciation is celebrated on March 25th. 天使报喜节在3月25日庆祝。

例句：The painting depicts the annunciation to Mary. 这幅画描绘了天使向玛丽报喜的情景。|近义词：declaration, proclamation, announcement|反义词:concealment, suppression|词性:noun

annunciate:/əˈnʌnsiˌeɪt/| v. |宣告，宣布；清晰地发音|to announce something clearly and distinctly; to pronounce clearly|例句：The angel Gabriel annunciated God's message to Mary. 天使加百列向玛丽宣告了上帝的旨意。

例句：He annunciated each word carefully. 他仔细地念出每一个字。|近义词：declare, proclaim, pronounce|反义词:mumble, slur|词性:verb
