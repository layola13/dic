marblings:/ˈmɑːrblɪŋz/
n.
大理石花纹，纹状物, 大理石纹理
the patterns or streaks of different colors like those found in marble.
The artist achieved beautiful marblings in the clay.  艺术家在黏土中实现了美丽的大理石花纹。
The marblings in the rock face were quite striking. 岩壁上的大理石纹理非常醒目。
streaks, patterns, veins
plainness, uniformity
noun
