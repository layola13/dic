bach:/bɑːx/ |n.|巴赫; (尤指) 約翰·塞巴斯蒂安·巴赫|(Johann Sebastian Bach, 1685-1750) a German composer, organist, harpsichordist, violist, and violinist of the Baroque period. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest composers of all time.  He enriched established German styles through his skill in counterpoint, harmonic and motivic organisation
