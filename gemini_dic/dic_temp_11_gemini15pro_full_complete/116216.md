masoner:/ˈmeɪsənər/| n.|石匠，泥瓦匠|A person skilled in building with stone or brick.|例句：The masoner carefully placed each brick, creating a sturdy wall. 石匠小心翼翼地放置每一块砖，砌成了一堵坚固的墙。

例句：The experienced masoner pointed out the flaws in the foundation. 经验丰富的石匠指出了地基的缺陷。|近义词：bricklayer, stonemason, builder|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑demolition worker)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "masson"  and Anglo-Norman "mascun", ultimately from Medieval Latin "macio" (hammer).
