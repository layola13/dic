goose-stepping:/ˈɡuːsˌstɛpɪŋ/
noun
正步走，鹅步
A stiff-legged marching style in which the legs are swung high and kept straight.  Originates from Prussian military drills.
The soldiers were goose-stepping across the parade ground. 士兵们在阅兵场上正步走。
近义词:marching, parading
反义词:strolling, ambling
词性:n.


goose-step:/ˈɡuːsˌstɛp/
verb
正步走
To march with a stiff-legged gait, swinging the legs high and keeping them straight.
The troops goose-stepped past the reviewing stand. 部队正步走过检阅台。
近义词:march, parade
反义词:stroll, amble
词性:v.
