factoring:/ˈfæktərɪŋ/| n.|因式分解；保理|The process of finding the factors of a number or expression.  It involves breaking down a mathematical expression into simpler components.|He learned about factoring in algebra class. 他在代数课上学了因式分解。|synonyms: factorization, decomposition|antonyms: expansion, multiplication|noun


factoring:/ˈfæktərɪŋ/| n.|（应收账款）保理|A financial transaction and a type of debtor financing in which a business sells its accounts receivable (invoices) to a third party called a factor at a discount.|The company used factoring to improve its cash flow. 这家公司用保理来改善现金流。|synonyms: accounts receivable financing, invoice discounting|antonyms: lending, borrowing|noun
