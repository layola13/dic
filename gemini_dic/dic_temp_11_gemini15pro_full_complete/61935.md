emulge:/ɪˈmʌldʒ/| v. |乳化|To combine two immiscible liquids, such as oil and water, into a stable mixture, often with the aid of an emulsifier.|The chef emulsified the oil and vinegar to make a vinaigrette.|Synonyms: blend, mix, combine, homogenize|Antonyms: separate, divide|verb
