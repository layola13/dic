cripplehood:/ˈkrɪpəlˌhʊd/| n.|残疾状态，残废状态|The state of being a cripple; disability.  A period of time spent as a cripple.|He spent his childhood in cripplehood after a debilitating illness. 他在一次使人衰弱的疾病后，童年是在残疾中度过的。|Near synonyms: disability, infirmity, impairment, invalidism|Near antonyms: health, wellness, able-bodiedness|noun
