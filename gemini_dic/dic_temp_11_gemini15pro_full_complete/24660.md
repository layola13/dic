bromeliaceous: /ˌbroʊˌmiːliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
凤梨科的；属于凤梨科的
Relating to or characteristic of the Bromeliaceae family of plants, which includes pineapples, Spanish moss, and many other tropical and subtropical species.
The bromeliaceous plants in her garden were thriving in the humid climate. 她花园里的凤梨科植物在潮湿的气候中茁壮成长。
The greenhouse contained a stunning collection of bromeliaceous epiphytes. 温室里收藏了令人惊叹的凤梨科附生植物。
近义词:  pineapple family (非正式)

bromeliad: /broʊˈmiːliæd/ | n. | 凤梨科植物 | A plant of the Bromeliaceae family, typically having short stems and stiff, spiny leaves. Many are epiphytic. |  She had a collection of bromeliads on her porch. 她在门廊上收集了一些凤梨科植物。 | 近义词: air plant (某些品种) | 反义词: (无明确的反义词) | 词性: noun
