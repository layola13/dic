jaculate: /ˈdʒækjʊleɪt/ | v. | (突然)喷出，射出，发出(尤指精液或带毒液的唾液) | To eject (semen or a venomous fluid, for example) suddenly and forcibly. | The snake jaculated venom at its attacker. 蛇向攻击者喷射毒液。| 近义词: eject, spurt, squirt | 反义词: absorb, withdraw | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin jaculātor, agent noun from jaculārī (“to hurl, throw a javelin”), from jaculum (“javelin, dart”), from jacere (“to throw”).
