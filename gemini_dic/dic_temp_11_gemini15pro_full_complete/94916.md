in-house:/ˌɪnˈhaʊs/ | adj. adv.|内部的，在机构内的；内部地，在机构内|Done, made, or provided within an organization, rather than by an outside company or organization.|例句：We have an in-house team of designers. 我们有一个内部的设计团队。|近义词：internal, domestic|反义词:external, outsourced|词性:形容词/副词

in-house:/ˌɪnˈhaʊs/| n.|内部，机构内部|The resources or expertise available within an organization.|例句：We decided to keep the project in-house. 我们决定把这个项目留在内部做。|近义词：internally|反义词:externally|词性:名词
