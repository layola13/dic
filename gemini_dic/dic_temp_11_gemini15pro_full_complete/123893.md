morcha:/ˈmɔːrtʃə/ | n. | 行军，队伍，前线，阵线 | A procession, especially one of a political or military nature; a front or battle line.  From Hindi मोरचा (morcā), ultimately from Portuguese *morcha "breastwork". |  The farmers' morcha slowly made its way to the capital.  农民的队伍慢慢地向首都进发。| The army held the morcha against the enemy attack.  军队坚守阵线抵御敌人的进攻。| synonyms: procession, march, parade, demonstration, front, frontline | antonyms: retreat, withdrawal | noun
