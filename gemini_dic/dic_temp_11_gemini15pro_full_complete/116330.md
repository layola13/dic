masterliness:/ˈmɑːstərlɪnɪs/| n.|精湛；娴熟；大师风范|The masterliness of her performance was breathtaking. 她的表演技巧精湛，令人叹为观止。|近义词：skill, expertise, proficiency, mastery|反义词：ineptitude, incompetence|词性: 名词
Etymology: From masterly + -ness.  "masterly" comes from "master" (meaning "having control or skill") + "-ly" (forming an adjective). Ultimately from Latin "magister" (teacher, master).
