braver:/ˈbreɪvər/| adj.|更勇敢的|Comparative form of "brave."  Indicates a greater degree of courage or fearlessness than another person or thing.|He was braver than I expected. 他比我想象的更勇敢。|近义词：more courageous, bolder, more daring|反义词:more cowardly, more timid, more fearful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *bravere*, comparative of *braf*, from Old French *brave*, ultimately of uncertain origin. Possibly from Italian *bravo* ("wild, savage"), or from Latin *barbarus* ("barbarian").
