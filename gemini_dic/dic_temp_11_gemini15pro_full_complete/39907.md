constitutiveness:/kənˌstɪtʃuːtɪvnəs/
n.
构成性；组成本质
The property of being constitutive or essential to something.  A quality that is inherent and defining.
The constitutiveness of language to human thought is a central theme in philosophy.  语言对人类思维的构成性是哲学中的一个中心主题。

近义词: essentiality, inherentness, innateness, intrinsicness
反义词:  extrinsicness, superficiality
词性: noun
