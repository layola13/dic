karyokinetic:/ˌkærioʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 核分裂的，核运动的 | Relating to or involving karyokinesis, the division of the cell nucleus during mitosis.  | The karyokinetic process is essential for cell replication. 核分裂过程对细胞复制至关重要。| Synonym: mitotic | Antonym: amitotic | adjective
