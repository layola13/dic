frier:/ˈfraɪər/ | n. | 油炸食品；油炸锅；油炸工 | Someone or something that fries food.  A person who fries food, especially as a job.  A deep pan, especially one with a wire basket, used for frying food in hot oil or fat.|  The frier quickly cooked the french fries to a golden brown. 油炸工迅速地把薯条炸成了金黄色。 | 近义词: fryer | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *friere, from Old French frire (“to fry”), from Latin frīgō (“to roast, fry, parch”).
