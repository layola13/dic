ledges:/ˈlɛdʒɪz/
n.
壁架；突出物；暗礁
A narrow horizontal surface projecting from a wall, cliff, or other surface.
The climber rested on a small ledge.
登山者在一个小壁架上休息。

近义词:shelf, projection, overhang, rim, brink
反义词:abyss, chasm, drop-off
词性:名词
