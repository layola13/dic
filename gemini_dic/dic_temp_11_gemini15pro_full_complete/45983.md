cyanopathy:/ˌsaɪəˈnɒpəθi/ | n. | 氰化物中毒，蓝藻病 | A disease caused by cyanide poisoning or by toxins produced by blue-green algae. | Example 1: The fish kill was attributed to cyanopathy resulting from an algal bloom.  鱼类死亡被归因于藻华导致的蓝藻病。

Example 2:  The researchers are studying the effects of cyanopathy on marine life. 研究人员正在研究蓝藻病对海洋生物的影响。 | Synonym: cyanide poisoning, blue-green algae poisoning | Antonym:  health, wellness | noun
