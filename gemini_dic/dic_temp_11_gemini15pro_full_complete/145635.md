overconsumes:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːmz/
verb
过度消耗，过量消费
To consume an excessive amount of something, typically beyond what is healthy or necessary.
He overconsumes sugar and processed foods. 他过度食用糖和加工食品。
The average American overconsumes resources compared to people in many other countries.  与许多其他国家的人相比，美国人平均过度消耗资源。

overconsume (present participle): /ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːmɪŋ/
verb
过度消耗，过量消费
The act of consuming something in excess.
Overconsuming sugary drinks can lead to health problems. 过量饮用含糖饮料会导致健康问题。
They are overconsuming resources at an alarming rate. 他们正在以惊人的速度过度消耗资源。

overconsumption:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsʌmpʃən/
noun
过度消耗，过量消费
The act or instance of overconsuming something.
Overconsumption of alcohol is detrimental to one's health.  过量饮酒有害健康。
The report highlights the environmental problems caused by overconsumption in developed countries. 该报告强调了发达国家过度消费造成的环境问题。


近义词: overindulge, overspend, overuse
反义词: underconsume, conserve, save
词性: verb (overconsumes), verb (overconsume), noun (overconsumption)
