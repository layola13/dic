amphicoelian: /ˌæmfɪˈsiːliən/
adjective
双凹的
Having a centrum (the central part of a vertebra) that is concave at both ends.
Amphicoelian vertebrae are found in some fish and amphibians.
Fish with amphicoelian vertebrae can move their bodies more flexibly in water.  鱼类拥有双凹椎骨能够在水中更灵活地活动身体。
Fossil records show that some extinct reptiles also had amphicoelian vertebrae. 化石记录显示一些已灭绝的爬行动物也拥有双凹椎骨。
synonym: biconcave
antonym: procoelous, opisthocoelous
词性: adjective
