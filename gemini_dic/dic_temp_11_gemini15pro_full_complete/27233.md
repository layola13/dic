calmly: /ˈkɑːmli/ (US) /ˈkɑːmli/ (UK) | adv. | 平静地；镇定地 | In a calm manner; without agitation or strong emotion. | She calmly explained the situation to the angry customer. 她平静地向愤怒的顾客解释了情况。| He calmly accepted the bad news. 他平静地接受了这个坏消息。 | 近义词: peacefully, quietly, serenely, composedly | 反义词: frantically, hysterically, nervously, anxiously | 词性: 副词

词源: From calm + -ly.  "Calm" comes from Middle French "calme" and ultimately from Late Latin "cauma," meaning "heat of the day, midday calm." The suffix "-ly" is used to form adverbs from adjectives.
