deary:/ˈdɪəri/ /ˈdɪri/| n. adj.|亲爱的，宝贝；可爱的|A term of endearment, often used in a slightly patronizing or humorous way.  Can also be used as an adjective meaning "sweet" or "dear."|例句：Oh deary me, what a mess! 哦，天哪，真是一团糟！

|近义词：dear, darling, sweetheart, honey, love|反义词: (No direct opposite, depends on context - perhaps "enemy," "stranger")|词性:noun, adjective
