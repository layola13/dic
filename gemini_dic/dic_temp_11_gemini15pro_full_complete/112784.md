lubricative: /ˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 润滑的；滑溜的；油滑的；圆滑的 | Reducing friction; oily or slippery. Also, (figuratively) smooth-talking, evasive. | The mechanic applied a lubricative oil to the gears. 机械师给齿轮涂上了润滑油。| The salesman's lubricative manner made me distrust him.  这个推销员油嘴滑舌的态度让我不信任他。 | synonyms: slippery, oily, greasy, smooth, slick, evasive | antonyms: rough, coarse, abrasive, sticky | adjective

lubricant: /ˈluːbrɪkənt/ | n. | 润滑剂；润滑油 | A substance, such as oil or grease, used to reduce friction between moving parts. |  This lubricant is specifically designed for high-performance engines. 这种润滑剂是专门为高性能发动机设计的。 |  The company produces a range of industrial lubricants. 这家公司生产一系列工业润滑剂。 | synonyms: oil, grease, emollient | antonyms: abrasive, desiccant | noun
