antimaterialistic:/ˌænti-məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/
adj.
反物质主义的；不重物质的
Opposed to or rejecting materialism, the belief that material possessions are the most important things in life.
She lives a simple, antimaterialistic life, focused on experiences rather than possessions. 她过着简单、不重物质的生活，注重体验而非拥有。
He gave up his high-paying job for a more fulfilling, antimaterialistic lifestyle. 他放弃了高薪工作，去追求更充实、不重物质的生活方式。
近义词:non-materialistic, spiritual, ascetic
反义词:materialistic, consumerist, greedy
词性:adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
materialistic (adjective): believing that having money and possessions is the most important thing in life (from Late Latin materialis, meaning "relating to matter")
