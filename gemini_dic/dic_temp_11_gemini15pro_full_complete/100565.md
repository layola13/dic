interwove:/ˌɪntərˈwoʊv/ | v. |交织，穿插，混合|To weave or mix together.|The story interwove elements of fantasy and reality.  这个故事交织着幻想和现实的元素。|例句：The tapestry interwove threads of gold and silver.  挂毯交织着金线和银线。

例句：Her life was interwoven with the history of the town.  她的生活与小镇的历史交织在一起。|近义词：intertwined, interlaced, blended, mingled|反义词：separated, disentangled|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *interweven, from Old English interwefan (“to interweave”), equivalent to inter- + weave.  来自中古英语 *interweven，源自古英语 interwefan（“交织”），相当于 inter- + weave。
