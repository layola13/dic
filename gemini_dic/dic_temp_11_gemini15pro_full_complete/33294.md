chubbed:/ˈtʃʌbd/| adj.|胖乎乎的，圆胖的|Pleasantly plump; slightly fat.  Describes someone, usually a child, who is slightly overweight in a cute or endearing way.|The baby had chubbed cheeks and dimpled hands.  婴儿有着胖乎乎的脸颊和带酒窝的小手。|近义词：plump, chubby, portly, rotund|反义词:thin, skinny, slender|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From *chub*, meaning a small, thick-bodied fish, likely referring to the similar shape.  The word's use to describe people emerged later.
