cajon:/kəˈhɒn/ | n. | 卡洪（一种打击乐器）| A box-shaped percussion instrument played by slapping the front or sides with the hands. | He played a rhythmic beat on the cajon. 他用卡洪演奏了一段节奏感很强的节拍。| The cajon added a unique percussive element to the music. 卡洪为音乐增添了独特的打击乐元素。 | 近义词: box drum, percussion instrument | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | noun
