mimics:/ˈmɪmɪks/ | n.  |模仿;模拟;拟态|  Something that mimics something else copies it. | The robot mimics human movements.  这个机器人模仿人类的动作。| synonyms: imitates, copies, apes | antonyms: originates, creates | noun

mimics:/ˈmɪmɪks/ | v.  |模仿;模拟;拟态| To copy someone's behavior or speech, usually to make people laugh. |  He mimics his teacher perfectly. 他完美地模仿了他的老师。| synonyms: imitates, copies, apes | antonyms: originates, creates | verb
