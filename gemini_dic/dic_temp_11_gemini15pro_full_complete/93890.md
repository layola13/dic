immortalising:/ɪˈmɔːtəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使不朽，使永垂不朽
The artist immortalized her beauty in a painting.  这位艺术家在一幅画中使她的美永垂不朽。
Present participle of immortalize.  immortalize的现在分词形式。
例句：
He immortalized his name by his contributions to science. 他对科学的贡献使他的名字永垂不朽。
The writer immortalized the events of the war in his novel.  这位作家在他的小说中使战争事件永垂不朽。
近义词：memorializing, commemorating, eternizing
反义词：forgetting, ignoring, obscuring
词性：v.
