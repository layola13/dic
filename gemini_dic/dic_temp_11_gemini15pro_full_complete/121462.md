misjudgers:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒərz/
n.
误判者，判断错误的人
People who make incorrect judgments or assessments of someone or something.
The misjudgers underestimated the team's potential and were proven wrong. 误判者低估了团队的潜力，最终被证明是错误的。
He proved the misjudgers wrong by winning the championship. 他通过赢得冠军证明了那些误判他的人是错误的。
近义词:misinterpreters, miscalculators
反义词:good judges, astute observers
词性:名词(plural)
