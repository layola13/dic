denationalize:/ˌdiːˈnæʃənəlaɪz/
v.
使非国有化；剥夺国籍
To transfer (an industry or enterprise) from government to private ownership or control.  To deprive of nationality or citizenship.
The government plans to denationalize the railway system. 政府计划将铁路系统私有化。
The dictator denationalized his political opponents and exiled them. 独裁者剥夺了其政敌的国籍并将他们流放。
Synonym: privatize, disenfranchise
Antonym: nationalize, naturalize
词性: 动词 (verb)
