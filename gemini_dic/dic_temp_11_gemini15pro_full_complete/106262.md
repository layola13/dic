knowledgeably: /ˈnɒlɪdʒəbli/ /ˈnɑːlɪdʒəbli/ | adv. | 博学地，知识渊博地 |  In a manner that demonstrates possession of knowledge; in an informed way. | She spoke knowledgeably about the history of art. 她对艺术史侃侃而谈。| 近义词: learnedly, eruditely, scholarly, wisely | 反义词: ignorantly, foolishly | 词性: 副词

knowledgeably: /ˈnɒlɪdʒəbli/ /ˈnɑːlɪdʒəbli/ | adv. | 知识丰富地，有见识地 | Showing or suggesting extensive knowledge; learned. | He discussed the topic knowledgeably. 他对这个话题进行了知识丰富的讨论。| 近义词: expertly, proficiently, skillfully | 反义词: inexpertly, clumsily | 词性: 副词


词源:

From knowledge + -ably.  Knowledge comes from Middle English knowleche, from Old English cnāwlīċ, from cnāwan (to know).
