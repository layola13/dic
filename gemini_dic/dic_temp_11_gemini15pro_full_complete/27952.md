cantones:/kænˈtoʊniːz/
| adj.|广东的；广东人的；广东话的|Relating to or characteristic of Canton (now Guangzhou), its people, or their language.|The cantones opera is very popular. 广东粤剧非常受欢迎。|近义词：Guangdong, Cantonese|反义词:|形容词


cantones:/kænˈtoʊniːz/
| n.|广东话；广东人|The language spoken in Canton (now Guangzhou) and surrounding areas, belonging to the Yue branch of the Chinese-Tai family.|She speaks fluent Cantones. 她讲一口流利的广东话。|近义词：Cantonese|反义词:|名词

Etymology: From Latin *Cantonensis*, derived from Canton (now Guangzhou).
