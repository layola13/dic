collegial:/kəˈliːdʒiəl/
adjective
学院的；大学的；共同掌权的；合作的
Relating to or involving shared responsibility, as among colleagues; characterized by or promoting collegiality.
The department has a very collegial atmosphere.  该系有非常合作的氛围。
The decision was made after collegial discussion.  这个决定是经过共同商议后做出的。
近义词:collaborative, cooperative, joint
反义词:autocratic, dictatorial, hierarchical
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin collegialis (“of or belonging to a college”), from Latin collega (“colleague”) + -alis.
