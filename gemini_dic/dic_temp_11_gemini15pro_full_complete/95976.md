incumbent-on:/ɪnˈkʌmbənt ɑːn/ | adj. | 义不容辞的；必须做的；负有责任的 | Binding, obligatory; resting as a duty or obligation on someone. | It is incumbent on all citizens to vote. 所有公民都有投票的义务。 | It is incumbent on the government to protect its citizens. 政府有责任保护其公民。 | 近义词：obligatory, mandatory, required, necessary, binding, compulsory | 反义词：optional, unnecessary | 词性：形容词

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin incumbent- ‘lying or leaning on’, from the verb incumbere, from in- ‘upon’ + -cumbere ‘lie’.  The original sense was ‘resting (as a duty) on’, later ‘currently holding a specified office’.
