elaborates:/ɪˈlæbəreɪts/ | v. | 详细说明，阐述 |  develops or presents (a theory, policy, or system) in detail. | The author elaborates on the theme of love in the final chapter. 作者在最后一章详细阐述了爱情的主题。 | expands, develops, explains, amplifies | shortens, summarizes, condenses | verb

elaborate:/ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ | adj. | 复杂的，精心制作的 | involving many carefully arranged parts or details; detailed and complicated in design and planning.  | They had created an elaborate system of tunnels. 他们建造了一个复杂的隧道系统。| complex, complicated, detailed, intricate | simple, basic, plain | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *elaboratus, past participle of elaborare (“to work out, produce by labor”), from Latin e- (“out, from”) + laborare (“to labor, work”).
