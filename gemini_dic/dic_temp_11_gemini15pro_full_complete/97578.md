ingurgitation:/ɪnˌɡɜːrdʒɪˈteɪʃn/
n.|吞咽；狼吞虎咽；暴饮暴食|The act of swallowing greedily or excessively.  The rapid ingestion of large quantities of something, especially food or drink.|He was reprimanded for his ingurgitation of the entire cake. 他因狼吞虎咽地吃掉整个蛋糕而受到斥责。|近义词：gorging, guzzling, devouring|反义词: abstinence, moderation|词性:noun

ingurgitate:/ɪnˈɡɜːrdʒɪteɪt/
v.|吞咽；狼吞虎咽；暴饮暴食|To swallow greedily or excessively; to gulp down.|The python ingurgitated the rat whole.  蟒蛇囫囵吞下了老鼠。|近义词：gorge, guzzle, devour|反义词: abstain, moderate|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin ingurgitatio, from ingurgitare "to engulf, swallow down," from in- "in" + gurges "whirlpool, abyss."
