asbestiform:/æsˈbɛstɪfɔːrm/
adjective
石棉状的，像石棉的
Resembling asbestos in appearance or form.  Having a fibrous structure similar to that of asbestos.
This mineral has an asbestiform habit. 这种矿物具有石棉状结构。
The material displayed asbestiform properties under the microscope.  在显微镜下，该材料显示出石棉状特性。
近义词: fibrous, asbestos-like
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "non-fibrous" or "compact")
词性: adjective
