jazzed:/ˈdʒæzd/| adj.|兴奋的，激动的|Feeling excited and enthusiastic.|I'm so jazzed about the upcoming concert! 我对即将到来的演唱会感到非常兴奋！|近义词：excited, thrilled, enthusiastic, exhilarated|反义词：bored, apathetic, unenthusiastic|adjective

Etymology:  Derived from "jazz," the musical genre, originally meaning "to enliven or energize" (early 20th century).  The sense of excitement likely comes from the energetic and improvisational nature of jazz music.
