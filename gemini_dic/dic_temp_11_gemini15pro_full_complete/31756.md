cheating:/ˈtʃiːtɪŋ/| adj. |欺骗的，作弊的|Relating to or involving cheating.|He used a cheating method to win the game. 他用作弊的方法赢得了比赛。|近义词：dishonest, fraudulent, deceitful|反义词：honest, fair|adjective

词源：From Old French *cheater (modern French acheter) "to buy, acquire, procure," probably a blend of two words: Late Latin accaptare "to buy" and Old North French achapter "to bargain, haggle." The meaning shifted from "acquiring something unfairly" to "acting dishonestly" in general.
