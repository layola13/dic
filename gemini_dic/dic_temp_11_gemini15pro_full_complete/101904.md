isidorian:/ˌɪsɪˈdɔːriən/
adjective
伊西多尔派的；伊西多尔著作的
Relating to Isidore of Seville (c. 560 – 636), a scholar and archbishop of Seville.
Isidorian decretals are collections of canon law attributed to Isidore, but now considered to be forgeries. 伊西多尔教令集是归于伊西多尔的法规汇编，但现在被认为是伪造的。
The Isidorian Renaissance refers to a period of renewed interest in the works of Isidore during the Carolingian Renaissance. 伊西多尔文艺复兴是指加洛林文艺复兴时期人们对伊西多尔作品重新燃起的兴趣。
近义词:Visigothic (in a broad sense)
反义词:None
