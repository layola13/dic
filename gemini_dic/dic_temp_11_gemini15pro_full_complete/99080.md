interbasin:/ˌɪntərˈbeɪsɪn/ | adj. | 流域间的，盆地间的 | Existing or occurring between basins, especially river basins. | The interbasin water transfer project aims to alleviate water shortages in the northern region.  流域间调水工程旨在缓解北部地区的水资源短缺。 | The interbasin agreement regulates water usage among the states. 流域间协议规范了各州之间的用水。 | 近义词: transbasin | 反义词: intrabasin | 词性: adjective

interbasin:/ˌɪntərˈbeɪsɪn/ | n. | 流域间地区 | The area between basins. |  The interbasin is a rich ecosystem supporting diverse wildlife.  流域间地区是一个丰富的生态系统，支持着多样化的野生动物。|  Mineral deposits are often found in the interbasin. 矿藏经常在流域间地区被发现。 | 近义词: interbasin area | 反义词:  intrabasin area | 词性: noun
