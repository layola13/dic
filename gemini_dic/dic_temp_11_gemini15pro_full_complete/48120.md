deco:/ˈdɛkoʊ/
noun
装饰艺术, 装饰风格
Decorative style, often characterized by geometric shapes, bold colors, and luxurious materials.
The hotel's lobby was a masterpiece of Art Deco.  这家酒店的大堂是装饰艺术的杰作。
synonyms: Art Deco, ornamentation, embellishment
antonyms: plainness, simplicity
n.

deco:/ˈdɛkoʊ/
adjective
装饰艺术风格的
Relating to or characteristic of Art Deco.
She wore a deco-inspired dress to the party. 她穿着一件装饰艺术风格的连衣裙去参加派对。
synonyms: Art Deco, ornamental, embellished
antonyms: plain, simple
adj.
