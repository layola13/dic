noncharitable:/ˌnɒnˈtʃærɪtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈtʃerɪtəbl/ | adj. | 无慈善心的，不仁慈的，刻薄的 | lacking in charity; unkind; unsympathetic |  He made several noncharitable remarks about his ex-wife. 他对他的前妻说了几句刻薄的话。 |  unsympathetic, unkind, uncharitable, ungenerous, mean-spirited | charitable, benevolent, kind, generous, compassionate | adjective
Etymology: non- + charitable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."  "Charitable" comes from the Old French "charitable" and ultimately from the Latin "caritas," meaning "charity" or "dearness."
