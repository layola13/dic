leipothymic:/ˌlaɪpəˈθaɪmɪk/
adjective
轻度抑郁的，情绪低落的
Relating to or characteristic of leipothymia; mildly melancholic.
She felt leipothymic after her favorite pet died. 她最爱的宠物去世后，她感到有些情绪低落。
The patient reported feeling leipothymic for the past few weeks. 病人报告说过去几周一直感到情绪低落。
近义词:depressed, melancholic, low-spirited
反义词:euphoric, cheerful, elated
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *leipothymia* "faintness of spirit," from *leipos* "lacking" + *thymos* "spirit."
