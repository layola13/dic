kirpans:/ˈkɪrpɑːnz/
n.
(锡克教徒佩戴的)短剑,匕首
Ceremonial daggers worn by Sikhs.
Sikhs are allowed to carry kirpans as a religious symbol. 锡克教徒可以携带短剑作为宗教象征。
The kirpan is one of the five Ks of Sikhism. 短剑是锡克教五K之一。
Synonym: dagger, knife
Antonym: (This word doesn't have a direct opposite since it's a specific religious object.  Perhaps "peace symbol" could be considered a thematic antonym.)
