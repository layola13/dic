A misgraft can refer to a physical graft that fails to take or a metaphorical graft, implying corruption or abuse of authority. | The apple tree misgraft produced no fruit. 苹果树嫁接失败，没有结出果实。 The mayor was accused of misgraft, using public funds for his private mansion. 市长被指控滥用职权，挪用公款修建私人豪宅。 | 近义词: corruption, embezzlement, malfeasance, abuse | 反义词:  proper management, integrity, honesty | 词性: 名词 & 动词
