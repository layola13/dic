dumbfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
惊呆的，目瞪口呆的
So amazed or confused that one is unable to speak or react.
She was dumbfounded by the news. 她被这个消息惊呆了。
The audience was dumbfounded by the magician's trick.  观众被魔术师的戏法惊呆了。
近义词: astonished, speechless, stunned, bewildered
反义词: unsurprised, unfazed, composed


词性: adjective
