antischolastically:/ˌæntiˌskəˈlæstɪkəli/
adv.
反学术地，非学究式地
in a manner opposed to scholarly methods or principles; in a way that rejects traditional academic values.
He argued antischolastically, prioritizing practical experience over formal education.  他以反学术的方式进行辩论，将实践经验置于正规教育之上。
The artist approached the subject antischolastically, rejecting traditional art historical interpretations. 这位艺术家以非学究式的方式处理这个主题，拒绝了传统的艺术史解释。
Synonyms:  unacademically, informally, non-traditionally, practically
Antonyms:  academically, scholastically, formally, traditionally
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + scholastic (relating to school or scholarship) + -ally (adverbial suffix).  源自 anti-（反对） + scholastic（与学校或学术相关的） + -ally（副词后缀）。
