high-resolution:/ˌhaɪ ˌrezəˈluːʃən/ | adj. |高分辨率的；高清晰度的|Having or producing images that are sharp and detailed because they contain a large number of pixels.|The photographer used a high-resolution camera to capture the stunning landscape. 摄影师使用高分辨率相机拍摄了令人惊叹的风景。|近义词：high-definition, sharp, clear, detailed|反义词：low-resolution, blurry, fuzzy|词性:形容词
词源: "high" + "resolution". "Resolution" comes from the Latin "resolvere," meaning "to loosen, dissolve, or solve." In the context of images, it refers to the ability to separate and distinguish small details.
