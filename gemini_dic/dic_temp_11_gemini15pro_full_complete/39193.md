congenialness:/kənˌdʒiːniˈælnəs/
|n.|
|合宜；适意；意气相投； congeniality|
|The congenialness of the atmosphere made everyone feel relaxed. 氛围的融洽让每个人都感到放松。|
|例句：The congenialness of the small town was one of its greatest attractions.  这个小镇的友善氛围是它最大的吸引力之一。

|例句：She appreciated the congenialness of her colleagues and the supportive work environment. 她很感激同事们的友善和支持性的工作环境。|
|近义词：agreeableness, pleasantness, compatibility, affinity, rapport, harmony|
|反义词：disagreeableness, unpleasantness, incompatibility, discord, antagonism|
|词性: 名词|


congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/
|adj.|
|意气相投的；性格相似的；合得来的；适宜的；宜人的|
|We spent a congenial evening with friends. 我们和朋友们度过了一个愉快的夜晚。|
|例句：They found the climate congenial to their health. 他们发现这里的气候对他们的健康有益。

|例句：He was a congenial host, making everyone feel welcome. 他是一位和蔼可亲的主人，让每个人都感到宾至如归。|
|近义词：agreeable, pleasant, compatible, sympathetic, kindred, amicable|
|反义词：disagreeable, unpleasant, incompatible, uncongenial, hostile|
|词性: 形容词|


词源:
From Latin *congenialis, from com- "with, together" + genialis "of the same family," from genius "guardian deity."
