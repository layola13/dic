metallochromy:/məˌtæləˈkrəʊmi/
noun
金属显色；金属指示剂显色法
The study or use of metallochromic indicators.  The phenomenon whereby a dye changes color when a metal ion binds to it.
Metallochromy is used in analytical chemistry to detect the presence of metal ions.
金属显色用于分析化学中检测金属离子的存在。
Metallochromic indicators are crucial for complexometric titrations.
金属显色指示剂对于络合滴定至关重要。
Synonyms: metal ion indicator, chelometric indicator
Antonyms: N/A
