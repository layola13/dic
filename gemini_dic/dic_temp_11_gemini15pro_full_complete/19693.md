bilgy:/ˈbɪldʒi/| adj.| 胀气的，充满液体的，污秽的|Swelling, filled with liquid, often foul-smelling; nauseated.|The bilgy smell coming from the basement made me gag. 地下室里传来的污秽气味让我作呕。|近义词：foul, fetid, nauseating, putrid|反义词:fresh, clean, aromatic|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'bilge' (the lowest compartment of a ship's hull, often containing stagnant water and waste), from Old North French 'bouge' (leather bottle, swelling), ultimately from Latin 'bulga' (leather bag).  The adjectival form '-y' is a common suffix indicating having the quality of.
