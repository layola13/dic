eaglets:/ˈiːɡləts/| n. |小鹰，雏鹰|A young eagle.  A baby eagle before it has fledged.|The eagle soared above its eaglets in the nest.  老鹰在巢穴上方的雏鹰上空翱翔。|近义词：young eagles, baby eagles|反义词:adult eagles, mature eagles|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *eglete, from Old French aiglete, diminutive of aigle ("eagle"), from Latin aquila ("eagle").
