overregulation:/ˌōvərˌreɡjuˈleɪʃn/ | n. |过度监管，过度管制| The act of regulating something too much, which can stifle innovation and create unnecessary burdens. | Excessive government overregulation can stifle economic growth. 政府过度监管会扼杀经济增长。|近义词：overcontrol, excessive regulation |反义词:deregulation, underregulation | 词性:名词

overregulation:/ˌoʊvərˌreɡjuˈleɪʃn/ | n. |过度监管，过度管制| The process of controlling something too strictly, often resulting in unintended negative consequences. |  Overregulation of the financial sector can lead to reduced lending.  对金融部门的过度监管会导致贷款减少。 | 近义词：micromanagement, excessive control | 反义词: liberalization, deregulation | 词性:名词


词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive," "too much") + "regulation" (from Latin "regula," meaning "rule")
