micro-evolution:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃən/
|n.|微进化，微演化|Evolutionary change within a species or small group of organisms, especially over a short period.|The development of antibiotic resistance in bacteria is an example of micro-evolution.  细菌对抗生素产生抗药性是微进化的一个例子。|
|近义词: intraspecific evolution|反义词: macro-evolution|名词|

micro-evolution:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃən/
|n.|微进化|The gradual change in the genetic makeup of a population over time, typically referring to changes within a species rather than the emergence of new species.|The peppered moth's color change during the Industrial Revolution is a classic example of micro-evolution.  工业革命期间胡椒蛾的颜色变化是微进化的一个典型例子。|
|近义词: small-scale evolution|反义词: macro-evolution|名词|
