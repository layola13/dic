hyperemotional:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənəl/
adjective
过度情绪化的；感情用事的
Excessively emotional; prone to displaying exaggerated or heightened emotional responses.
She's been very hyperemotional lately, crying at the drop of a hat. 她最近情绪非常激动，动不动就哭。
The movie's hyperemotional scenes resonated with the audience.  这部电影中过度煽情的场景引起了观众的共鸣。

近义词:overemotional, overly sensitive, dramatic, histrionic
反义词:stoic, impassive, unemotional, calm
词性:adjective
