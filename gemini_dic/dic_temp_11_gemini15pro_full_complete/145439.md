overcherish: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃɛrɪʃ/
verb
过度珍爱，过分爱护
To cherish excessively; to love or value too much.
She overcherished her only child, shielding him from the realities of the world. 她过分溺爱她唯一的孩子，把他与世界的现实隔绝开来。
He overcherished the memory of his late wife, unable to move on with his life. 他过分珍视亡妻的记忆，无法继续自己的生活。
近义词: overprotect, coddle, pamper, spoil
反义词: neglect, ignore, disregard
词性: verb

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "cherish" (from Old French "cherir," meaning "to hold dear")
