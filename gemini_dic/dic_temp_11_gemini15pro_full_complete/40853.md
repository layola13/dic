cooee:/kʊˈiː/ | int. n. v. |招呼声；呼喊；库伊鸟叫声| A long, high-pitched call used to attract attention, especially in the Australian outback. | Example: He let out a cooee to signal his location. 他发出一声库伊来表明他的位置。| Example:  She heard a faint cooee in the distance. 她听到远处传来微弱的库伊声。 | 近义词: shout, yell, call, holler | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性: interjection, noun, verb
词源: From Dharuk *guwiː
