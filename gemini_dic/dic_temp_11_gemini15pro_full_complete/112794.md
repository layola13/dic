lubrification:/ˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|润滑，加润滑油|The lubrication of the engine is essential for its proper functioning. 发动机的润滑对其正常运转至关重要。|Lubrication of the gears reduced friction and wear. 齿轮的润滑减少了摩擦和磨损。|Synonyms: oiling, greasing|Antonyms: friction, abrasion|noun
