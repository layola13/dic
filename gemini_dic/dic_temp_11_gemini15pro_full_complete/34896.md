clique:/klik/ | n. | 小圈子，派系 | A small, exclusive group of people, often with shared interests or status, who tend to socialize among themselves and exclude others. |  She belonged to the popular clique in high school. 她高中时属于受欢迎的小圈子。

There's a clique at work that always goes out for lunch together. 公司里有个小圈子总是结伴出去吃午饭。

| 近义词: circle, group, coterie, inner circle | 反义词: outsiders | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *clique*, "latch, handle of a latch," figuratively "a group of people," probably of imitative origin. The sense evolution likely went from the sound of a latch clicking shut (suggesting exclusivity) to a group closed off to outsiders.
