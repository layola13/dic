achenial:/əˈkiːniəl/| adj.|瘦果的|Relating to or resembling an achene, a small, dry, one-seeded fruit that does not open to release the seed.|The dandelion's achenial fruit is dispersed by the wind.|近义词:indehiscent|反义词:dehiscent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Modern Latin *achenialis*, from achene + -ial.  The word "achene" itself comes from Greek *a-*, "not" + *chaínein*, "to gape".
