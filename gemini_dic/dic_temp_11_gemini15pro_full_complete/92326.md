hypnotizability:/ˌhɪpnəˌtaɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可催眠性；受催眠性 | The quality or degree to which a person is susceptible to being hypnotized.  |  Her hypnotizability was surprisingly high, allowing her to quickly enter a deep trance. 她惊人的易受催眠性使她能够迅速进入深度催眠状态。 | synonyms: suggestibility, susceptibility | antonyms: resistance, immunity | noun

hypnotizability:/ˌhɪpnəˌtaɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可催眠性；受催眠性 | The extent to which an individual can be hypnotized. | Some people have low hypnotizability and are difficult to hypnotize. 有些人的可催眠性很低，很难被催眠。 | synonyms: responsiveness to hypnosis | antonyms: unresponsiveness to hypnosis | noun
