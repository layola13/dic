collocating:/ˈkɒləkeɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləkeɪtɪŋ/ (US)| v. |搭配; 共现|The process of words appearing together frequently in a language.  It refers to the tendency of certain words to occur together regularly. |Example: "Heavy rain" is a common collocation in English.  暴雨在英语中是一个常见的搭配。

Example:  The words "make" and "decision" collocate frequently.  “make”和“decision”这两个词经常搭配出现。|近义词：combining, associating, pairing |反义词: separating, isolating |词性: Verb (present participle)
