giraffoid:/dʒɪˈræfɔɪd/| adj. n.|长颈鹿似的，长颈鹿科动物|Resembling a giraffe; belonging to the Giraffidae family.  A giraffoid animal.|例句：The okapi, though shorter, is a giraffoid animal. 尽管霍加狓矮一些，但它是一种长颈鹿科动物。

例句：The animal had a distinctly giraffoid appearance with its long neck and spotted coat.  这种动物有着明显的长颈鹿特征，长长的脖子和斑点皮毛。|近义词：giraffe-like|反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider "short-necked")|词性:adjective, noun
