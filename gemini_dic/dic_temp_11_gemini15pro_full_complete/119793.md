migrating:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| v. adj.|迁移，移居；（鸟类）迁徙|present participle of migrate|The birds are migrating south for the winter. 鸟儿正在向南迁徙过冬。|Synonyms: relocating, moving, traveling, shifting |Antonyms: settling, staying, remaining|verb

migrating:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|迁移的，迁徙的|used as an adjective to describe something that is in the process of migrating|Migrating birds often fly in large flocks. 迁徙的鸟类经常成群结队地飞行。|Synonyms: migratory, mobile, nomadic|Antonyms: settled, stationary, resident|adjective
