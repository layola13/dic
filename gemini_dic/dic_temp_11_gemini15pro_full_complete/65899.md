evs:/iːviːˈɛs/| n. |electric vehicles (电动汽车)|Electric vehicles are becoming increasingly popular due to their environmental benefits. 电动汽车因其环境效益正变得越来越受欢迎。|例句：The government is offering incentives for people to buy EVs. 政府正在为购买电动汽车的人们提供奖励。

evs:/iːviːˈɛs/| n. |extravehicular activities (舱外活动)|Extravehicular activities are essential for maintaining and repairing spacecraft. 舱外活动对于维护和修理航天器至关重要。|例句：The astronaut performed a series of EVAs during the mission. 宇航员在任务期间进行了一系列舱外活动。

近义词：(For electric vehicles) electric cars, battery-powered cars, zero-emission vehicles
近义词：(For extravehicular activities) spacewalks
