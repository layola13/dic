acardiac: /eɪˈkɑːrdiæk/ | adj. | 无心的；无心脏的 | Lacking a heart, especially congenitally.  Relating to or affecting individuals born without a heart. |  The acardiac twin was dependent on its sibling for circulation.  这个无心双胞胎依赖其兄弟姐妹的血液循环生存。 | Synonyms: heartless (figurative), heart-deficient | Antonyms: healthy, normal | Adjective


Etymology:  From a- (without) + cardiac (relating to the heart), ultimately from Greek *kardíā (heart).
