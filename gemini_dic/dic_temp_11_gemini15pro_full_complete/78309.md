gerocomy: /dʒɛrɒˈkɒmi/
n.
老年医学;老年病学
The study of aging and the problems of the aged.
Gerocomy is a growing field as the population ages.  老年医学是一个随着人口老龄化而不断发展的领域。
The conference focused on advances in gerocomy.  这次会议的重点是老年医学的进步。
近义词: geriatrics
反义词: pediatrics
词性: noun


Etymology:  From Greek gēras (old age) + -komia (care).
