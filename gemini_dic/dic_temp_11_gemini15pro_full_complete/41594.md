n.
尸体（复数）
The plural form of "corpse," referring to the dead bodies of multiple humans or animals.

corpses:/ˈkɔːrpsɪz/
n.
尸体
The dead body of a human or animal.
The police found the corpse in the woods. 警方在树林里发现了尸体。

近义词:bodies, remains, cadavers
反义词:living, alive


词性:名词(plural)
词源:From Old French *cors, from Latin corpus "body."
