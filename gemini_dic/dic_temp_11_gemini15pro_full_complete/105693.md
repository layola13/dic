kink:/kɪŋk/ | n. | 扭结，弯曲；怪癖，癖好 | A sharp twist or bend in something flexible; a peculiarity of behavior; a quirk. |  There's a kink in the hose. 水管里有个扭结。| synonyms: twist, bend, curl, quirk, eccentricity | antonyms: straightness | noun
