overfee: /ˈoʊvərfiː/
noun
额外费用；过高的费用
An overfee is a charge that is excessive or beyond what is considered reasonable.
The customer complained about the overfee on their bill.  顾客抱怨账单上的额外费用。
The hotel charged an overfee for using the internet.  这家酒店对使用互联网收取过高的费用。
synonyms: surcharge, extra charge, excessive fee
antonyms: discount, rebate, reduction
n.
