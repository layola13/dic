high-fidelity:/ˌhaɪ fɪˈdelɪti/ | adj. | 高保真的 | Reproducing sound with little distortion, giving a result very similar to the original. | The new headphones offer high-fidelity sound reproduction. 这款新耳机提供高保真音质。 | Synonyms: high-quality, accurate, precise | Antonyms: low-fidelity, distorted, muffled | adjective

high-fidelity:/ˌhaɪ faɪˈdelɪti/ | n. | 高保真度 | The quality of being high-fidelity. | The audiophiles were impressed by the high-fidelity of the recording. 音响发烧友对录音的高保真度印象深刻。| Synonyms: accuracy, precision | Antonyms: distortion, muffling | noun
