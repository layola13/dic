magnoliaceous:/ˌmæɡˌnoʊliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
木兰科的；似木兰的
Relating to or resembling the magnolia family of plants (Magnoliaceae).
The garden boasts a stunning collection of magnoliaceous trees.  这个花园里生长着一系列令人惊叹的木兰科树木。
The creamy white flowers are characteristic of magnoliaceous species.  乳白色的花朵是木兰科植物的典型特征。
近义词: magnolia-like
反义词: (No specific antonym -  could potentially list families dissimilar to Magnoliaceae, but it's not a true opposite)
词性: adjective
