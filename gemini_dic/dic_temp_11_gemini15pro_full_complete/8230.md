anti-heroine:/ˈæntiˌhɛrəʊɪn/ /ˈæntiˌhɛroʊɪn/| n. |反女主角，非典型女主角|A female protagonist who lacks the conventional qualities of a hero, such as courage, morality, or idealism.  She may be flawed, unconventional, or even villainous, but still sympathetic or compelling in some way.|Example: Lisbeth Salander from "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo" is a compelling anti-heroine. 莉丝贝丝·莎兰德出自《龙纹身的女孩》是一个引人注目的反女主角。

Example:  Fleabag from the eponymous TV show is a modern anti-heroine, relatable in her flaws and struggles.  电视剧《伦敦生活》中的弗里巴格是一个现代的反女主角，她的缺点和挣扎让人产生共鸣。|近义词：protagonist, female lead, unconventional heroine|反义词:heroine, ingenue|词性:noun
