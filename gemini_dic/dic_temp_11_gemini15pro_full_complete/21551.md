blueness:/ˈbluːnɪs/
n.
蓝; 忧郁
The blueness of the sky was breathtaking.  天空的湛蓝令人叹为观止。
A feeling of blueness overcame her. 一种忧郁的感觉笼罩着她。
Etymology: From blue + -ness.  源自 blue + -ness.
Synonyms: azure, cerulean, sapphire (for the color); sadness, melancholy, depression (for the feeling)
Antonyms: whiteness, redness, yellowness (for the color); happiness, joy, elation (for the feeling)
