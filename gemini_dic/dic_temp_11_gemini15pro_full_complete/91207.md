hydriodate:/ˌhaɪdraɪˈoʊdeɪt/ | n. |碘酸盐;氢碘酸盐|A salt of hydriodic acid.  A compound containing the anion IO₃⁻ or the anion HI₂⁻. |Potassium hydriodate is used in iodimetry. 碘酸钾用于碘量法。|Synonyms: iodate |Antonyms: None | noun

hydriodate:/ˌhaɪdraɪˈoʊdeɪt/ | v. |用氢碘酸处理|To treat or combine with hydriodic acid. |The alkene was hydriodated to form an alkyl iodide.  该烯烃经氢碘酸处理形成烷基碘。|Synonyms: treat with hydriodic acid |Antonyms: None | verb
