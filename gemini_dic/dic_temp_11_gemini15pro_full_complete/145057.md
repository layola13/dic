overambition: /ˌōvəræmˈbɪʃən/ | n. | 过度雄心，野心过大 | The state of being excessively ambitious; an ambition that is too great or unrealistic. | His overambition led him to make several poor business decisions. 他的野心过大，导致他做了几个糟糕的商业决策。| 近义词:  excessive ambition, vaulting ambition, megalomania | 反义词:  lack of ambition, modesty, contentment | 词性: 名词

overambitious: /ˌōvəræmˈbɪʃəs/ | adj. | 野心过大的，过度雄心勃勃的 | Excessively ambitious; having ambitions that are too great or unrealistic.  | The overambitious project ultimately failed due to lack of funding.  这个野心勃勃的项目最终因为缺乏资金而失败了。| 近义词: aspiring, ambitious, aggressive | 反义词: unambitious, modest, content | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "ambition" (from Old French "ambicion," ultimately from Latin "ambitio" meaning "a going around," hence "canvassing for votes," then "eager desire for preferment").
