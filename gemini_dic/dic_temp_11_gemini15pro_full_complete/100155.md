interramal:/ˌɪntərˈreɪməl/| adj.|校内的，学院内的|Situated within the branches of a ramified structure, especially a branching nerve or blood vessel; or occurring between the branches of such a structure. Also, relating to or involving only members of the same school or college.|The interramal ganglion is a small structure located within the mandible.|近义词：intramural, internal, inner|反义词: extramural, external, outer|词性: adjective
