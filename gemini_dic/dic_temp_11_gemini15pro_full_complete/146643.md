overhardness:/ˈoʊvərˈhɑːrdnəs/| n.|过度硬化，过硬|Excessive hardness; hardness beyond what is desirable or necessary.|The overhardness of the steel made it brittle and prone to cracking. 钢的过度硬化使其变脆，容易开裂。|近义词：brittleness, rigidity|反义词: softness, flexibility|词性:名词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "hard" (Old English heard "hard, firm, solid, severe, difficult," from Proto-Germanic *harduz "hard") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), from Proto-Germanic *-nassu-, abstract noun suffix).
