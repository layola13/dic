gazump:/ɡəˈzʌmp/ | v. | 哄抬物价；抬高要价(尤指在双方即将成交之际)；毁约加价 | To raise the price of something (especially a house) that has already been agreed upon, often at the last minute. |  The seller gazumped us by accepting a higher offer from someone else. 卖家接受了别人更高的报价，哄抬了价格。| synonyms: outbid, overbid | antonyms: underbid, undercut | verb

gazumping:/ɡəˈzʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 哄抬物价；抬高要价(尤指在双方即将成交之际)；毁约加价 | The act of gazumping. | Gazumping is a common practice in a competitive housing market. 在竞争激烈的房地产市场中，哄抬房价是一种常见的做法。| synonyms: outbidding, overbidding | antonyms: underbidding, undercutting | noun
