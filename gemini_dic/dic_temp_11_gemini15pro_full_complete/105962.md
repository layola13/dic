klutz:/klʌts/| n.|笨蛋，笨手笨脚的人|A clumsy, awkward person.  Someone who often drops things or bumps into things.|He's such a klutz; he spilled coffee all over himself. 他真是个笨蛋，把咖啡洒得满身都是。|Synonyms: butterfingers, clod, oaf|Antonyms: graceful, coordinated, deft|Noun
