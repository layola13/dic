dab:/dæb/| v. n.|轻拍，轻涂；少量；少量涂抹|To press against something lightly several times with a piece of cloth or other soft material, usually in order to clean or dry it, or to apply a substance.|She dabbed at her eyes with a tissue. 她用纸巾轻拭双眼。|近义词：pat, touch, blot|反义词：smear, rub|词性：动词/名词

dab:/dæb/| n.|少量；轻拍；内行人|A small amount of something.|She put a dab of perfume behind her ears. 她在耳后涂了一点香水。|近义词：bit, drop, speck|反义词：lot, abundance|词性：名词

dab:/dæb/| n.|内行；专家|An expert in a particular activity.|He's a dab hand at cooking. 他是个烹饪高手。|近义词：expert, professional, proficient|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner|词性：名词

词源:  Probably of imitative origin.
