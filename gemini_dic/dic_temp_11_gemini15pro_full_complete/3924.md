centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ | n. v. | 离心机，离心分离机；用离心机分离 | A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids. |

例句：
The lab technician used a centrifuge to separate the blood components. 实验室技术员用离心机分离血液成分。

The mixture was centrifuged at high speed. 混合物被高速离心。


近义词：separator, extractor | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, verb

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ | v. | 用离心机分离 | To rotate something at high speed in a centrifuge. |

例句：
The researchers centrifuged the sample to isolate the DNA. 研究人员将样品离心以分离DNA。

They centrifuged the solution to remove the precipitate.  他们将溶液离心以去除沉淀物。


近义词：spin, whirl, rotate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb
