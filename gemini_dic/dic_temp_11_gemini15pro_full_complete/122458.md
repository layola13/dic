mobilisation:/ˌmoʊbələˈzeɪʃən/ (US), /ˌməʊbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|动员; 调动;|The mobilisation of the army took several weeks.  军队的动员花了几个星期。|近义词: activation, rallying, assembly, deployment|反义词: demobilisation, disbandment|Noun

Etymology: From French mobiliser, from mobile (“movable”) + -iser (suffix forming verbs).  Ultimately from Latin mobilis (“movable”).
