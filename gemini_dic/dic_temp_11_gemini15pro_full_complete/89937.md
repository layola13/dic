horsehead:/ˈhɔːrsˌhed/ (US), /ˈhɔːsˌhed/ (UK)| n. |马头|The part of a horse's head in front of the neck, including the ears, eyes, nose, and mouth.  A cloud of gas and dust in space resembling the head of a horse. |The horsehead sculpture was incredibly lifelike.  那马头雕塑栩栩如生。The Horsehead Nebula is a famous dark nebula. 马头星云是一个著名的暗星云。|近义词：equine head,  (for nebula) dark nebula |反义词: N/A |noun
