nonintellectualness:/ˌnɑːnˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlnəs/
n.
lack of intellect; absence of intellectual pursuits
The nonintellectualness of the movie disappointed the critics.  这部电影缺乏智慧，令评论家们失望。
nonintellectualness is often associated with a focus on practical matters rather than abstract ideas.  非智力性通常与注重实际事务而非抽象概念有关。
Synonyms: unintelligence, simplemindedness, shallowness
Antonyms: intelligence, intellectuality, brilliance
词性: noun
