imperium:/ɪmˈpɪəriəm/| n.|统治权，帝国；绝对权力|Supreme power; dominion; empire.  The power or authority of a sovereign state over its territory and people.|The Roman imperium stretched across much of Europe and North Africa.|Synonyms: dominion, sovereignty, sway, authority, command, control, power, rule, ascendancy, supremacy, mastery|Antonyms: subjection, servitude, weakness, impotence|Noun
