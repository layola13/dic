lapelled:/ləˈpɛld/| adj.| 有翻领的；带翻领的|Having a lapel or lapels (the part of a garment that folds back from the front neckline and is continuous with the collar).|He wore a smart, lapelled jacket. 他穿着一件时髦的翻领夹克。|Synonyms: collared |Antonyms: lapel-less (rarely used)|adjective
