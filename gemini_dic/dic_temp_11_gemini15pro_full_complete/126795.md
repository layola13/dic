nanotechnological:/ˌnænəʊˌtɛknəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌnænoʊˌtɛknəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
纳米技术的
Relating to nanotechnology, the branch of technology that deals with dimensions and tolerances of less than 100 nanometers, especially the manipulation of individual atoms and molecules.
This new material is the result of nanotechnological advances. 这种新材料是纳米技术进步的成果。
Nanotechnological research has led to breakthroughs in medicine. 纳米技术研究已在医学领域取得突破。
近义词: nanoscale, nanoscopic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "macroscale" or "conventional" in some contexts)
词性: adjective
