conspicuous:/kənˈspɪkjuəs/ | adj. | 显眼的，引人注目的 | Standing out so as to be clearly visible; attracting notice or attention. |  Her bright red dress made her conspicuous in the crowd.  她的鲜红色连衣裙使她在人群中很显眼。 | synonyms: noticeable, prominent, obvious, striking, outstanding | antonyms: inconspicuous, unnoticeable, subtle, hidden, obscure | adjective

conspicuous:/kənˈspɪkjuəs/ | adj. | 显著的，重要的 | Clearly visible or observable; attracting attention because of a striking quality or feature. | His absence was conspicuous at the meeting. 他在会议上的缺席很引人注目。 | synonyms:  pronounced, marked, distinct, evident, manifest | antonyms: insignificant, unimportant, trivial, minor, negligible | adjective
