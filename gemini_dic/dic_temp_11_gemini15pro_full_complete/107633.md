what /wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) |pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

**中文翻译:**  什么，多么，何等

**英文解析:** Used to ask for information about something; used to express surprise or disbelief.

**例句:**

What is your name?
你叫什么名字？

What do you want to eat?
你想吃什么？

What a beautiful day!
多么美好的一天！

What did you say?
你说什么？


**近义词:** which, how

**反义词:**  (What 没有直接的反义词，其意义取决于上下文)

**词性:** pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
