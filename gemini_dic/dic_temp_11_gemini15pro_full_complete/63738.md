epical: /ˈɛpɪk(ə)l/
adjective
史诗般的；壮丽的；宏大的
Relating to or characteristic of an epic or epics.  Of, relating to, or having the characteristics of an epic poem. Extending beyond the usual or ordinary especially in size or scope.
例句：The battle was of epical proportions. 这场战斗规模宏大。

例句：He told an epical tale of adventure. 他讲述了一个史诗般的冒险故事。

近义词：heroic, grand, majestic, monumental, epic
反义词：mundane, ordinary, trivial, insignificant

词性: adjective

词源: From epic + -al.  Ultimately from the Greek word *epos* meaning "word, story, poem."
