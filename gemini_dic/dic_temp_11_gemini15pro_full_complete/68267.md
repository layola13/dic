fakeers:/fəˈkɪərz/| n. |托钵僧；苦行者|A Muslim or Hindu religious ascetic who lives solely on alms.|例句：The fakeers wandered from village to village, begging for food. 托钵僧们从一个村庄游荡到另一个村庄，乞讨食物。

例句：He lived a simple life, like a fakeer, detached from material possessions.  他过着简朴的生活，像个苦行者，远离物质财富。|近义词：ascetic, mendicant, mystic|反义词:materialist, hedonist|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Urdu فقیِر (faqīr), from Arabic فقير (faqīr, “poor, needy”), from فقر (faqr, “poverty”). Ultimately from Proto-Semitic *faqīr- (“poor”).
