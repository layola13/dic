hippophagism:/ˌhɪpəˈfædʒɪzəm/
n.
食马肉；嗜食马肉
The practice of eating horseflesh.
Hippophagism was common in some ancient cultures.  古代一些文化中食用马肉很普遍。
The prevalence of hippophagism declined after the introduction of Christianity. 基督教传入后，吃马肉的现象减少了。
synonyms: horse-eating
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps vegetarianism or abstinence from horse meat)
noun
