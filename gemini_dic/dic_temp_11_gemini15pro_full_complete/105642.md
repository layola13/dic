kingdom: /ˈkɪŋdəm/ (UK), /ˈkɪŋdəm/ (US)| n. |王国；领域；界|A country ruled by a king or queen; a realm or domain.|The United Kingdom comprises England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland.  英国由英格兰、苏格兰、威尔士和北爱尔兰组成。|The animal kingdom is vast and diverse. 动物王国广阔而多样。|近义词：realm, domain, empire, monarchy|反义词: republic, democracy|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cyningdōm (attested only as cyningdōmes, genitive singular), from cyning "king" (see king) + -dōm "-dom".
