fielden:/ˈfiːldən/ | adj. | 在田野里的；野生的 | Covered with or situated in fields; wild.  | The fielden flowers were beautiful. 田野里的花很漂亮。 | The fielden mice scurried through the grass. 田鼠在草丛中窜来窜去。 | 近义词: rural, rustic, agrarian | 反义词: urban, domestic | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old English *feldin, from feld (field) + -in (suffix forming adjectives). Related to Old Frisian feldin.
