counterstrategist:/ˈkaʊntərˈstrætɪdʒɪst/
n.|反战略家；对抗战略家|A person who devises strategies to counter those of an opponent.|The general brought in a skilled counterstrategist to anticipate the enemy's next move. 将军请来了一位技艺高超的反战略家来预测敌人的下一步行动。|Synonyms: tactician, strategist, planner|Antonyms:  dupe, pawn|noun
