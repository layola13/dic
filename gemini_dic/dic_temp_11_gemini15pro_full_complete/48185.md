decomposite:/ˌdiːkəmˈpɒzɪt/| adj. |分解的，合成的（较少用）|Made up of separate parts or elements; compound.|A decomposite number can be divided by numbers other than 1 and itself. 一个合数可以被除1和它本身以外的数字整除。|This is a decomposite material made from various recycled plastics. 这是一种由各种回收塑料制成的复合材料。|近义词：composite, compound|反义词: simple, prime (for numbers)|词性:形容词
