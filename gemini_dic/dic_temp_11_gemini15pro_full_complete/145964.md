overdistorts:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈstɔːrts/
verb
过度扭曲；过分歪曲
to distort excessively; to twist or misrepresent something beyond recognition.
The media overdistorted the politician's words, making it seem like he supported the opposite of his actual stance. 媒体过度歪曲了这位政客的话，使他看起来像是支持与他实际立场相反的观点。
The funhouse mirror overdistorted my reflection, making me look incredibly tall and thin.  哈哈镜过度扭曲了我的影像，让我看起来又高又瘦。

SYN-> exaggerate, misrepresent, warp, falsify
ANT->  represent accurately, portray truthfully, depict faithfully
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "distort" (from Latin distortus, past participle of distorquere "to twist different ways," from dis- "apart, away" + torquere "to twist")
