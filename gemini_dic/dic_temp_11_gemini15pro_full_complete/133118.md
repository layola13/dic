nonescalating: /ˌnɒnˈɛskəleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非升级的，不升级的 | Not becoming more intense or serious; not escalating. | The government pursued a nonescalating policy during the crisis. 政府在危机期间奉行不升级政策。| The two countries engaged in nonescalating border skirmishes. 两国进行了非升级的边境小冲突。 | 近义词: de-escalating, controlled, contained, limited | 反义词: escalating, intensifying, worsening | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "escalating" (from "escalate," derived from Latin "scala" meaning "ladder," referring to a gradual increase).
