nonimitativeness:/ˌnɒnɪmɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/
n.
非模仿性；独创性
The nonimitativeness of the artist's style is remarkable.
这位艺术家的风格独树一帜。
nonimitativeness is a valuable trait in artistic creation.
非模仿性在艺术创作中是一个宝贵的特质。
近义词: originality, creativity, uniqueness, individuality
反义词: imitation, copying, mimicry
词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + imitative (adjective meaning "copying or following a model") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)  Ultimately from Latin *imitatus*, past participle of imitari "to copy, imitate."
