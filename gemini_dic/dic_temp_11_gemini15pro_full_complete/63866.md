epigynous:/ɪˈpɪdʒɪnəs/ |adj.|上位花的，子房下位的|Having the perianth and stamens attached to or near the top of the ovary, so that the ovary appears to be below them; inferior.|Example 1: The apple blossom is epigynous, with the ovary situated below the other floral parts. 苹果花是上位花的，子房位于其他花部下方。

Example 2: Epigynous flowers are common in the rose family.  上位花在蔷薇科中很常见。|Synonym: inferior (ovary)|Antonym: hypogynous, perigynous|Adjective
