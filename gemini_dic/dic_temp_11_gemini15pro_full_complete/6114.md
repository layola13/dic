ametabolian:/ˌeɪmɛtəˈbɒliən/
adjective
无变态的; 不完全变态的
Undergoing little or no metamorphosis.  Developing directly into the adult form without a larval stage.
Silverfish are ametabolous insects.  衣鱼是不完全变态的昆虫。
近义词:ametabolic
反义词:holometabolous, hemimetabolous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From a- (not) + Greek metabolē (change) + -ian (adjectival suffix).
