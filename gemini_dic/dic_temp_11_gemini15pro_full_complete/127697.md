negator:/ˈnɪɡeɪtər/| n.|否定者；反驳者；否定词|A person or thing that negates something.  Something that denies or refutes.  A word or phrase that expresses negation, such as "not" or "no."|例句：The lawyer acted as a negator to the plaintiff's claims.  律师对原告的主张进行了反驳。|近义词：denier, contradicter, refuser, opponent|反义词：affirmer, supporter, proponent|词性: noun
