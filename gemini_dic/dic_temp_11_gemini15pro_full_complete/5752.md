amacratic: /ˌæməˈkrætɪk/
adjective
无统治的; 无政府主义的; 非官僚政治的
Relating to or advocating amacracy, a society without rulers or government.  Characterized by a lack of hierarchical structure and centralized authority.
例句：The group embraced an amatocratic philosophy, believing that true freedom could only exist without government.  这个团体信奉无政府主义哲学，认为只有没有政府才能存在真正的自由。

例句：Their amatocratic ideals led them to establish a commune where decisions were made collectively. 他们的无政府主义理想使他们建立了一个集体决策的公社。
近义词：anarchic, anarchist, lawless, ungoverned
反义词：governed, hierarchical, authoritarian, bureaucratic
词性：adjective
