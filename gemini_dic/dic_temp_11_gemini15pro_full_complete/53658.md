disci:/ˈdɪsaɪ/ /ˈdɪski/ | n. | disciple; follower; learner | A person who believes in the teachings of a leader, especially a religious or political one.  A pupil or student. |  Example 1: The Buddha had many disci who followed his teachings. 佛陀有很多弟子追随他的教诲。

Example 2:  He considered himself a disci of the great philosopher. 他认为自己是那位伟大哲学家的弟子。 | Synonyms: follower, adherent, pupil, student, learner | Antonyms: leader, teacher, mentor | noun
