hercogamous:/ˌhɜːrkəˈɡæməs/
adjective
雌雄异熟的
Having male and female reproductive organs maturing at different times, thus preventing self-fertilization.
Many orchid species are hercogamous, ensuring cross-pollination. 许多兰花种类都是雌雄异熟的，以确保异花授粉。
Hercogamous flowers have evolved mechanisms to prevent self-pollination. 雌雄异熟的花进化出了防止自花授粉的机制。
近义词:dichogamous
反义词: homogamous
词性: adjective
