look-after:/lʊk ˈɑːftər/ | phrasal verb | 照顾，照看 | To take care of someone or something. |  I need to look after my sick grandmother. 我需要照顾我生病的祖母。

|  Could you look after my plants while I'm away? 我不在的时候你能帮我照看一下我的植物吗？ | 近义词: care for, take care of, tend | 反义词: neglect, ignore | 词性: 动词短语


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old English "locian" (to look) and "æfter" (after).  The combination signifies directing one's gaze towards something with the intention of caring for it.
