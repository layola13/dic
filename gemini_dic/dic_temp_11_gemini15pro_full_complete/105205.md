keylogger:/ˈkiːˌlɒɡər/ (US) /ˈkiːˌlɒɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.|键盘记录器|A keylogger is a type of surveillance software or hardware that records every keystroke made on a computer or other device.|A keylogger can be installed on a computer without the user's knowledge, allowing someone to steal passwords and other sensitive information. 键盘记录器可以在用户不知情的情况下安装在计算机上，从而窃取密码和其他敏感信息。|Synonyms: keystroke logger, keyboard capture software|Antonyms: anti-keylogger software|Noun
