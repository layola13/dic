brachiopods:/ˈbrækɪəˌpɒdz/| n. |腕足动物|Marine animals with two shells and a stalk-like pedicle, superficially resembling clams but internally quite different.|Brachiopods are often found as fossils in ancient rocks. 腕足动物经常以化石的形式在古老的岩石中被发现。|近义词：lamp shells|反义词:|名词

brachiopod:/ˈbrækɪəˌpɒd/| n. |腕足动物|A single organism of the brachiopod group.|This brachiopod is remarkably well-preserved.  这只腕足动物保存得非常好。|近义词：lamp shell|反义词:|名词
