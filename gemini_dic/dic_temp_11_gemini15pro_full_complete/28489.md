carcinomas:/kɑːrsɪˈnoʊməz/
noun
癌(carcinoma 的复数)
A carcinoma is a cancer that begins in the skin or in tissues that line or cover internal organs. 癌是一种起源于皮肤或覆盖内部器官的组织中的癌症。
Lung carcinomas are the leading cause of cancer deaths worldwide. 肺癌是全球癌症死亡的主要原因。
Synonyms: cancers, tumors, malignancies
Antonyms: healthy tissue
