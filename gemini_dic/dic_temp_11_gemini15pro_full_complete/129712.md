non-egocentric:/ˌnɒn iːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 非自我中心的；不以自我为中心的 | Not focused primarily on oneself or one's own needs and views; considering the feelings and needs of others. |  She displayed a non-egocentric attitude by considering everyone's opinion. 她通过考虑每个人的意见展现了非自我中心的态度。 |  altruistic, selfless, considerate, empathetic | egocentric, selfish, self-centered | adjective
Etymology->
non-: prefix meaning "not" from Latin *non
ego-: from Latin ego "I"
centric: from Latin centrum "center" + -ic, suffix forming adjectives.
