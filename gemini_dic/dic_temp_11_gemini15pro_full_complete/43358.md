cranelike:/ˈkreɪnˌlaɪk/| adj.|像鹤的，鹤立鸡群的|Resembling a crane, especially in being tall and slender.|例句：She has a cranelike neck. 她有着像鹤一样修长的脖子。|例句：He stood there, cranelike, observing the scene below. 他鹤立鸡群地站在那里，观察着下面的景象。|近义词：tall, slender, lanky, gangly|反义词：short, stocky|词性:形容词

词源: crane + like.  "crane" refers to the bird, and "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling."  Therefore, "cranelike" literally means "resembling a crane."
