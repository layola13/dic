chignon:/ˈʃiːnjɒn/ (US) /ʃiːˈnjɔːŋ/ (UK)|n.|发髻，盘在脑后的发髻|A chignon is a hairstyle in which the hair is arranged in a knot or roll, typically worn at the back of the head.|She wore a beautiful chignon at the wedding. 她在婚礼上梳了一个漂亮的发髻。|Synonyms: bun, knot, updo|Antonyms: loose hair|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chignon*, meaning "nape of the neck," ultimately from Old French *caignon*.
