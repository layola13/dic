cartoonists: /kɑːrˈtuːnɪsts/
noun
漫画家
A person who creates cartoons.
The newspaper employed several talented cartoonists.  这家报纸雇佣了几位才华横溢的漫画家。
Cartoonists often use satire to make their point. 漫画家经常用讽刺来表达他们的观点。

近义词: comic strip artists, illustrators, animators
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "realist" in an artistic context)
词性: noun (plural)
