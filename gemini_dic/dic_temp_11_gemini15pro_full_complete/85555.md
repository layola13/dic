hatchling:/ˈhætʃlɪŋ/| n.| 刚孵化的动物(尤指鸟类或爬行动物); 雏鸟; 雏龟 |A hatchling is a young animal that has recently emerged from its egg, especially a bird or reptile. |The turtle hatchlings scrambled towards the sea.  这些小海龟争先恐后地爬向大海。| The birdwatchers observed the hatchlings leaving the nest.  鸟类观察员观察到雏鸟离开了巢穴。| synonyms: chick, nestling, fledgling, newborn| antonyms: adult, mature| noun
