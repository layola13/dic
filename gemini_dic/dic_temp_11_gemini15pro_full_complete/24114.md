breweries: /ˈbruːəriz/ /ˈbrʊrəriz/
| n. |啤酒厂（brewery 的复数形式）|Places where beer is commercially made.| San Diego has many excellent breweries. 圣地亚哥有很多很棒的啤酒厂。|Many craft breweries have opened in recent years. 近年来，许多精酿啤酒厂开业。|近义词：brewpubs, beer gardens|反义词: 无|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *brēowhūs, from *brēowan (to brew) + hūs (house).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *breuwaną.
