gunnysacks:/ˈɡʌniˌsæks/| n.|麻袋，粗麻布袋|A gunnysack is a large sack made of burlap, a coarse, woven fabric made from jute or hemp. It is typically used for storing and transporting agricultural products like grains, potatoes, or coffee beans. |The farmer filled the gunnysacks with potatoes. 农民把麻袋装满了土豆。|
|近义词：burlap sacks, hessian sacks|反义词:|noun
