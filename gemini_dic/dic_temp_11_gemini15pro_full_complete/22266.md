boneheadednesses:/ˌboʊnˈhɛdɪdnəsɪz/
n.
愚蠢的行为；笨拙；傻事
A plural form of boneheadedness, referring to multiple instances of stupidity or foolish behavior.
That series of boneheadednesses cost him his job. 那一系列愚蠢的行为让他丢了工作。
Synonyms: stupidities, follies, blunders, idiocies
Antonyms: wisdom, intelligence, brilliance
noun

boneheadedness:/ˌboʊnˈhɛdɪdnəs/
n.
愚蠢的行为；笨拙；傻事
The quality of being boneheaded; stupidity.
His boneheadedness is astounding. 他的愚蠢令人震惊。
Synonyms: stupidity, folly, foolishness, idiocy
Antonyms: wisdom, intelligence, brilliance
noun

boneheaded:/ˈboʊnˌhɛdɪd/
adj.
愚蠢的；笨拙的；傻的
Stupid; foolish.
That was a boneheaded move. 那是一个愚蠢的举动。
Synonyms: stupid, foolish, idiotic, silly
Antonyms: wise, intelligent, brilliant, smart
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
boneheaded (adj.) 1883, American English, from bonehead + -ed.  The image is of someone who has bone where brains should be.
