muellerian:/mjuːˈlɪəriən/ | adj. | 米勒管的，缪勒氏管的 | Relating to the Müllerian ducts, embryonic structures that develop into the female reproductive tract in mammals. |  The Muellerian ducts differentiate into the fallopian tubes, uterus, cervix, and upper vagina. 米勒管分化成输卵管、子宫、子宫颈和上阴道。 |  The physician identified a Muellerian cyst during the ultrasound. 医生在超声波检查中发现了一个米勒氏囊肿。 | Synonyms: paramesonephric | Antonyms: Wolffian | adjective
