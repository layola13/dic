lah:/lɑː/ | interj. | 啦; 咯 | Used to emphasize a statement or question, or to express agreement or acknowledgement. Similar to "right," "okay," or "you know." | It's hot today, lah. 今天很热，啦。 | 近义词: right, okay, you know, yeah | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection like this) | 词性: interjection


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
It's late, lah. = 很晚了，啦。
You understand, lah? = 你明白啦？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm going now, lah. = 我现在要走了，啦。
He's coming, lah. = 他要来了，啦。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I've finished my work, lah. = 我已经完成我的工作了，啦。
We've eaten already, lah. = 我们已经吃过了，啦。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've been waiting for hours, lah. = 我已经等了好几个小时了，啦。
They've been studying all day, lah. = 他们一整天都在学习，啦。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I told you, lah. = 我告诉过你了，啦。
He went home, lah. = 他回家了，啦。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was sleeping, lah. = 我在睡觉，啦。
She was working, lah. = 她在工作，啦。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already left, lah. = 我已经离开了，啦。
They had finished eating, lah. = 他们已经吃完了，啦。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been waiting for a long time, lah. = 我已经等了很久了，啦。
They had been working on the project for months, lah. = 他们已经在这个项目上工作好几个月了，啦。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will go tomorrow, lah. = 我明天会去，啦。
She will be there, lah. = 她会在那里，啦。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be studying tomorrow, lah. = 我明天会在学习，啦。
They will be working on the project, lah. = 他们会在做这个项目，啦。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished by tomorrow, lah. = 我明天之前会完成，啦。
They will have left by then, lah. = 到那时他们会已经离开了，啦。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been working on this for a year by then, lah. = 到那时我将已经做这件事一年了，啦。
They will have been living there for five years, lah. = 到那时他们将已经在那里住了五年了，啦。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would tell you, lah. = 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你的，啦。
If I were you, I would do it, lah. = 如果我是你，我会做的，啦。

被动语态:
It was done already, lah. = 已经做完了，啦。
It will be finished tomorrow, lah. = 明天会完成的，啦。

疑问句:
You understand, lah? = 你明白啦？
You coming, lah? = 你来吗，啦？

否定句:
I don't know, lah. = 我不知道，啦。
I'm not going, lah. = 我不去，啦。


PHRASE->
Come on, lah! = 快点啦！
Don't worry, lah! = 别担心啦！
Just do it, lah! = 就去做吧，啦！
It's okay, lah! = 没关系啦！
Hurry up, lah! = 快点啦！

注意事项:

"Lah" is primarily used in Singaporean and Malaysian English.  It doesn't have a direct equivalent in other dialects and can be difficult to translate precisely.  It often conveys a sense of informality and can be used to soften a statement, express impatience, or seek confirmation.
