destroy:/dɪˈstrɔɪ/ | vt. | 杀死 | to kill (someone or something) | The hunter destroyed the deer. 猎人杀死了那只鹿。| synonyms: kill, eliminate, exterminate | antonyms: revive, resurrect, save | verb
