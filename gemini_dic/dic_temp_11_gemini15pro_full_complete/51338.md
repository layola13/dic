detectible:/dɪˈtɛktəbl/| adj.|可检测的，可发现的|able to be discovered or noticed|例句：The change in temperature was barely detectable. 温度的变化几乎无法察觉。|例句：There is a detectable amount of iron in the water. 水中含有可检测量的铁。|近义词：perceptible, noticeable, observable, discernible|反义词：undetectable, imperceptible, invisible|词性:形容词
词源: From detect + -able.  Detect comes from the Late Latin dētectus, past participle of dētegō (“I uncover, expose”), from dē- (“un-, off, from”) + tegō (“I cover”).
