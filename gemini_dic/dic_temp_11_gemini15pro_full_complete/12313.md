ascribing: /əˈskraɪbɪŋ/
verb
归因；把…归于
The act of attributing something to a cause, source, or author.
He was ascribing his success to hard work. 他将他的成功归因于努力工作。
Synonyms: attributing, imputing, assigning, crediting
Antonyms: denying, rejecting, disavowing
v.

commonly ascribed (普遍认为): The discovery is commonly ascribed to Marie Curie.  这一发现通常被认为是玛丽·居里的功劳。
