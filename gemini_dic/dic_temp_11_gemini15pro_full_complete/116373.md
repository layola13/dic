mastiff:/ˈmæstɪf/ | n. |獒犬，猛犬|A large, powerful breed of dog with a short, smooth coat, originally bred for guarding and fighting. | The mastiff barked deeply at the intruder.  獒犬对着入侵者狂吠。| 近义词:dog, hound | 反义词:None (No single antonym for a breed of dog) | 词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mastin (Modern French mâtin), from Vulgar Latin *mansutinus, of uncertain origin, possibly from Latin mansuetus "tame, gentle" (source of English mansuetude), ironically, or perhaps from Latin masticare "to chew," or from a Germanic source.
