flipside:/ˈflɪpsaɪd/| n.|反面，另一方面，负面|the opposite side of something, especially a record; the less good or less popular side of something|例句：Every coin has two sides, and we must consider the flipside of this decision. 每个硬币都有两面，我们必须考虑这个决定的反面。|例句：The flipside of the success was the pressure he was under.  成功的反面是他承受的压力。|近义词：reverse, other side, downside, drawback|反义词: obverse, upside, advantage|词性:名词
