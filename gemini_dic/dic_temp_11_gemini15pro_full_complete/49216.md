delectations:/ˌdiːlɛkˈteɪʃənz/
noun
乐趣， delights
great pleasure or enjoyment.
The chef prepared a feast of culinary delectations.  厨师准备了一场烹饪乐趣的盛宴。
Synonyms: pleasures, enjoyments, delights, gratifications
Antonyms: miseries, displeasures, sorrows
