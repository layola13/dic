incessantly:/ɪnˈsesəntli/| adv.|不停地，不断地|Without ceasing; constantly.  It describes an action that continues without interruption.|The rain fell incessantly for three days. 雨不停地下了三天。|Synonyms: constantly, perpetually, continually, unremittingly|Antonyms: intermittently, occasionally, sporadically|adverb
