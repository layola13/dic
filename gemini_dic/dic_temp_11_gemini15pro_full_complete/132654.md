nondiscussible:/ˌnɒndɪˈskʌsəbəl/ |adj.|不可讨论的，禁止讨论的|Not open to discussion or debate; forbidden to be discussed.|This matter is nondiscussible. 这件事是不可讨论的。|The terms of the contract are nondiscussible. 合同条款不可讨论。|近义词:unmentionable, taboo, forbidden, off-limits|反义词:discussable, open, negotiable|词性:形容词
词源:non- (not) + discussable (able to be discussed).  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "discussable," creating the meaning "not able to be discussed."
