caveating:/ˈkeɪviˌeɪtɪŋ/
v.| 附带警告；提出限制条件| Adding a caveat or proviso to a statement. |  He accepted the offer, caveating that he would need to review the contract. 他接受了这个提议，但提出需要审查合同。|  qualifying, stipulating, restricting, limiting | accepting, endorsing | verb

caveat:/ˈkeɪviæt/
n.| 警告；限制条件；告诫| A warning or proviso of specific stipulations, conditions, or limitations.| The caveat is that this offer is only valid for a limited time.  需要注意的是，此优惠仅在有限的时间内有效。 | warning, proviso, caution, admonition |  acceptance, endorsement | noun
