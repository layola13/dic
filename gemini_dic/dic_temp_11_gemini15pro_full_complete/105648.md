kingest:/ˈkɪŋɪst/ | adj. | 最像国王的；最具王者风范的 |  Superlative form of "kingly," meaning most like a king, most regal, or most fit for a king. |  He had the kingest bearing of all the men in the room. 他是房间里所有人中最有王者风范的。 |  The kingest lion ruled the pride with wisdom and strength.  最有王者风范的狮子以智慧和力量统治着狮群。| 近义词：most regal, most majestic, most stately | 反义词：least regal, most humble, most common | 词性：形容词

词源：From "king" + "-est" (superlative suffix). 源自“king”（国王） + “-est”（最高级后缀）。
