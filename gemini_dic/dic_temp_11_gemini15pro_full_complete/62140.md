enclaved:/ɪnˈkleɪvd/ | adj. |被包围的，被封锁的 | Surrounded by another territory; enclosed.  A country, or especially part of a country, that is enclaved is surrounded by another country. |  The small republic was enclaved within the larger nation's borders.  这个小共和国被包围在更大的国家边界内。|  The village is enclaved within enemy territory. 这个村庄被包围在敌人的领土内。 |  surrounded, enclosed, landlocked, embedded |  exposed, open | adjective

enclave:/ˈɛnkleɪv/ | n. |飞地，被包围的领土 | A portion of territory within or surrounded by a larger territory whose inhabitants are culturally or ethnically distinct. A distinct region or community enclosed within a larger territory. | Campione d'Italia is an Italian enclave within Switzerland.  坎皮奥内迪塔利亚是瑞士境内的一块意大利飞地。|  The city has several ethnic enclaves. 这座城市有几个少数民族聚居区。| territory, region, district, area, community |  mainland | noun
