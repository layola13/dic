mewses:/ˈmjuːzɪz/ | n. | 缪斯女神们; 灵感来源 | Muses are goddesses in Greek mythology who preside over the arts and sciences.  They are often invoked as a source of inspiration. | The poet prayed to the Muses for inspiration.  诗人向缪斯女神祈祷以获得灵感。 |  He found his muses in the natural beauty of the countryside. 他在乡村的自然美景中找到了灵感。 | synonyms: inspirations, influences | antonyms: distractions, hindrances | noun
