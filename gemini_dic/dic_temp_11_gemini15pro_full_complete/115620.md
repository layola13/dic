marcescible:/mɑːrˈsesɪbl/ | adj. | 凋而不落的，枯而不落的 | Withering but not falling off; persistent |  The oak tree is marcescible, its brown leaves clinging to the branches throughout winter. 橡树的叶子是枯而不落的，棕色的叶子整个冬天都 clinging在树枝上。

The beech tree also has marcescible leaves, providing a rustling sound in the winter wind.  山毛榉树也有枯而不落的叶子，在冬天的风中发出沙沙的声音。| 近义词: persistent,  withered,  dead,  dry | 反义词: deciduous,  falling,  abscise | 词性: 形容词


词源:

From Latin *marcescere, from marcere "to wither, droop," related to mārciō "I bruise, crush."
