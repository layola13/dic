expurgate: /ˈɛkspərɡeɪt/ | vt. | 净化；删去（不当之处）| To remove matter thought to be objectionable or unsuitable from (a book, play, etc.). | The editor expurgated the novel before its release. 编辑在小说发行前删去了其中不当的内容。|  The publisher expurgated all references to sex and violence from the book. 出版商从书中删除了所有关于性和暴力的内容。 | 近义词: censor, bowdlerize, purge | 反义词: include, insert, add |词性: 动词

expurgated: /ˈɛkspərɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. |  净化过的；删节过的 | Having been subject to expurgation. | The expurgated edition of the play was released for school performances.  该剧的删节版是为了学校演出而发行的。 |  They read an expurgated version of the novel in class. 他们在课堂上读了这部小说的删节版。 | 近义词: censored, bowdlerized, purged | 反义词: uncensored, unexpurgated, complete | 词性: 形容词
