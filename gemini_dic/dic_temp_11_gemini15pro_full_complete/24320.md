brilliancy:/ˈbrɪljənsi/ /ˈbrɪliənsi/| n.|光辉；辉煌；才华；杰出|The brilliance of the diamond dazzled her. 钻石的光辉使她眼花缭乱。|近义词：radiance, splendor, luminosity, genius, intelligence|反义词：dullness, mediocrity|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle French *brilliance* (French *brillance*), from *brilliant* + *-ance*.
