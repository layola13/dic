bureaucratic: /ˌbjʊərəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 官僚的，官僚主义的 | Relating to bureaucracy; rigidly adhering to rules and formalities, often resulting in inefficiency and inflexibility. | The bureaucratic process made it difficult to get anything done. 这个官僚程序使得很难完成任何事情。| Synonyms: administrative, official, governmental, red-tape | Antonyms: efficient, streamlined, flexible | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bureaucratique*, from *bureau* (desk, office) + *-cratique* (related to *-cracy*, rule). Ultimately from Late Latin *burra* (coarse woolen cloth, desk covering).
