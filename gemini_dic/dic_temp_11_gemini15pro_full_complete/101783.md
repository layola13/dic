irreversibleness:/ˌɪrɪˈvɜːrsəbəlˌnɛs/ | n. | 不可逆性，不可挽回性 | The quality or state of being irreversible; inability to be reversed. | The irreversibleness of the decision weighed heavily on her mind.  这个决定的不可逆转性让她心沉重。 | synonyms: finality, immutability, permanence | antonyms: reversibility, changeability, mutability | noun


irreversible:/ˌɪrɪˈvɜːrsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可逆的，不可挽回的 | Not able to be reversed or undone. | The damage to the environment is irreversible. 对环境的损害是不可逆转的。 | synonyms: permanent, unchangeable, irrevocable | antonyms: reversible, changeable, revocable | adjective


Etymology:
From in- (not) + reversible (able to be reversed)  来自 in-（不）+ reversible（可逆的）
reversible from re- (back, again) + vertere (to turn)  reversible 来自 re-（返回，再次）+ vertere（转动）
