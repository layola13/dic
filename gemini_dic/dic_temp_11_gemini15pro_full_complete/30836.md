chabouk: /ʃæˈbuːk/ | n. |  鞭子；皮鞭 | A whip, typically made of leather or hide, used for flogging or urging on animals. | The rider cracked his chabouk to urge the horse forward. 骑手挥舞着鞭子催促马匹前进。 | synonyms: whip, lash, scourge, flagellum | antonyms: reward, caress | noun
