exclamatorily:/ˌɛkskləˈmeɪtərɪli/ | adv. | 感叹地，呼喊地 | In a manner that expresses strong emotion, typically surprise, anger, or excitement.  | "Stop!" she shouted exclamatorily. “停下！”她大声喊道。 |  "Get out!" he yelled exclamatorily. “出去！”他叫喊道。| 近义词: excitedly, emphatically, vehemently | 反义词: calmly, quietly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From the Latin word "exclamare" meaning "to cry out" plus the suffix "-ory" (forming adjectives) and "-ly" (forming adverbs).
