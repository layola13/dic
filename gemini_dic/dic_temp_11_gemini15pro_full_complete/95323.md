incend:/ɪnˈsend/ | v. | 点燃，燃烧 | To set on fire; to kindle. | The logs incend quickly with a little kindling. 木柴加了一点引火物后很快就燃烧起来了。 |  The arsonist attempted to incend the building. 纵火犯试图点燃大楼。 | 近义词: ignite, kindle, inflame | 反义词: extinguish, quench | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *incendere, from in- "in, on" + candēre "to shine, glow."
