lycanthropy:/ˌlaɪˈkænθrəpi/ | n. | 变狼狂；狼人 | The mythical ability or power to transform into a wolf, or the folklore surrounding such transformations.  The condition of being a werewolf. |  He wrote a chilling tale of lycanthropy and the supernatural. 他写了一个关于狼人和超自然现象的令人毛骨悚然的故事。 |  Near synonyms:  werewolfism, shape-shifting | Antonym:  N/A | Noun
