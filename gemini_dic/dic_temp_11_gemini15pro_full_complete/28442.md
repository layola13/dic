carboy:/ˈkɑːrˌbɔɪ/ | n. |大玻璃瓶；大肚瓶 | A large glass bottle with a narrow neck, encased in wicker or other protective material, used for holding and transporting liquids, especially acids. | The chemist carefully poured the acid into the carboy. 化学家小心翼翼地把酸倒入大肚瓶里。|  The winery stored the wine in carboys before bottling. 酒庄在装瓶前将葡萄酒储存在大肚瓶中。| synonyms: demijohn, flagon, jug | antonyms: vial, ampoule | noun

carboys:/ˈkɑːrˌbɔɪz/ | n. |大玻璃瓶（复数）| Plural of carboy. | The carboys were carefully packed for shipment. 这些大肚瓶都被仔细包装以便运输。| Several carboys of sulfuric acid were delivered to the lab.  几瓶硫酸被送到实验室。| synonyms: demijohns, flagons, jugs | antonyms: vials, ampoules | noun
