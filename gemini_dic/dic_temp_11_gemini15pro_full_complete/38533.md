concise:/kənˈsaɪs/| adj.|简明的，简洁的|Expressing much in few words; brief and to the point. |例句：Her essay was concise and well-written. 她的文章简明扼要，写得很好。

例句：He gave a concise summary of the meeting. 他简要地总结了会议内容。

近义词：brief, succinct, terse, compact|反义词:verbose, prolix, lengthy, rambling|词性:形容词

concite:/kənˈsaɪt/| v.|用简洁的语言表达；使简明|To express something concisely; to make concise. |例句：He concites his arguments effectively in the debate.  他在辩论中有效地精简了自己的论点。

例句：She managed to concite the lengthy report into a single page. 她设法将冗长的报告精简成一页。


近义词：abbreviate, condense, summarize|反义词:expand, elaborate, lengthen|词性:动词
