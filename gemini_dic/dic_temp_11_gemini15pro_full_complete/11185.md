archimedean:/ˌɑːrkɪˈmiːdiən/ | adj. | 阿基米德的；阿基米德式的 | Relating to Archimedes or his work, especially his principle of buoyancy. |  Archimedean screw is a device for raising water. 阿基米德螺旋是一种提水装置。 | synonymous: hydrostatic, buoyant | antonymous: sinking, non-buoyant | adjective


archimedean:/ˌɑːrkɪˈmiːdiən/ | adj. | 阿基米德的；阿基米德式的 | Characterized by the properties of Archimedes, such as the principle of leverage. | The Archimedean property of real numbers is a fundamental concept in analysis. 实数的阿基米德性质是分析学中的一个基本概念。 | synonymous: leveraging, balancing | antonymous: unstable, imbalanced | adjective
