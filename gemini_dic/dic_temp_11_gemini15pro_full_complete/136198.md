nonrelenting:/ˌnɒnrɪˈlɛntɪŋ/ | adj. | 不松懈的，不屈不挠的，持续的 | Not diminishing in intensity, strength, or effort; persistent.  | The nonrelenting pressure to succeed can be overwhelming.  持续的成功压力让人难以承受。|  The nonrelenting rain caused severe flooding. 不停的雨造成了严重的洪水。| 近义词: persistent, relentless, incessant, unremitting | 反义词: yielding, intermittent, lenient, merciful | 词性: 形容词
词源:  From non- + relenting (present participle of relent "to become less severe, soften in feeling"), from Old French ralentir "to slacken, slow down," from re- "back" + alentir "to make slow," from lent "slow."
