clannishly:/ˈklænɪʃli/| adv.|排外地，小集团地|In a way that shows a strong sense of loyalty and protectiveness towards one's own group, often excluding or distrusting outsiders.  Showing clannishness; like a clan.|The family, though friendly, was quite clannish and rarely socialized with people outside their tight-knit circle.  这个家庭虽然友好，但相当排外，很少与他们的小圈子以外的人交往。|The villagers behaved clannish towards the newcomers, making it difficult for them to integrate. 村民们对新来的人表现得很排外，使他们很难融入。|closely, exclusively, insularly|openly, inclusively|adverb
