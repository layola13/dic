embraces:/ɪmˈbreɪsɪz/
noun
拥抱；包含；欣然接受
The act of holding someone closely in one's arms, typically as a sign of affection, comfort, or love. It can also refer to the acceptance or adoption of something (an idea, a belief, a change).
She greeted her friend with a warm embrace. 她热情地拥抱了她的朋友。
The company embraces innovation. 这家公司欣然接受创新。
Synonyms: hugs, acceptance, adoption
Antonyms: rejection, refusal, spurning
n.

embracement:/ɪmˈbreɪsmənt/
noun
拥抱；信奉；包含
The act or state of embracing something or someone. It implies a close, often emotional connection or acceptance.
The embracement of new technologies has transformed the industry. 对新技术的信奉改变了这个行业。
Their embracement was long and heartfelt. 他们的拥抱漫长而 heartfelt。
Synonyms: acceptance, adoption, espousal, hug
Antonyms: rejection, refusal, spurning
n.
