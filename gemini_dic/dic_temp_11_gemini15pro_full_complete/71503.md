ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *flanc (Modern French flanc), from Frankish *hlank, from Proto-Germanic *hlankō (“flank, loin, hip”). Cognate with Old High German hlanca (“flank, loin”), Old English hlanca (“loin, haunch”).
