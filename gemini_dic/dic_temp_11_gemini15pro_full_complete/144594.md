outwearied:/ˌaʊtˈwɪərid/ | adj. | 筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的 | Extremely tired, exhausted.  |  The outwearied soldiers collapsed after the long march. 筋疲力尽的士兵们在长途行军后倒下了。 | Synonyms: exhausted, fatigued, weary, worn out | Antonyms: energetic, refreshed, invigorated | adjective

Etymology:  "out-" (completely) + "wearied" (past participle of "weary," meaning to tire).  From Old English "werig."
