nonmechanicalnesses:/ˌnɒnˌmɪˈkænɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
非机械性；非机械特性
The lack of mechanical properties or characteristics.  This refers to aspects that are not related to machinery or mechanical processes.
The artist emphasized the nonmechanicalnesses of his handcrafted sculptures.  这位艺术家强调了他手工雕塑的非机械性。
Near synonyms:  organicity, naturalness, fluidity
Opposite:  mechanicalness, artificiality, rigidity
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not; the opposite of
mechanical (adjective): relating to or operated by machinery
-ness (suffix): used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
