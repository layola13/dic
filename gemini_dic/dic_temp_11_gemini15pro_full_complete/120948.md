mischanced:/ˌmɪsˈtʃænst/
verb
发生意外，遭遇不幸
happened by mischance; suffered misfortune.
例句：The ship mischanced to strike a rock. 这艘船不幸撞上了岩石。
例句：He mischanced to lose his wallet on the way home. 他在回家的路上不小心丢了钱包。
近义词：unfortunate, unlucky, ill-fated, disastrous
反义词：fortunate, lucky, blessed
词性：verb

词源:
From mis- + chance.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating something bad or wrong.  "Chance" refers to luck or fortune.  Therefore, "mischanced" literally means having bad luck or fortune.
