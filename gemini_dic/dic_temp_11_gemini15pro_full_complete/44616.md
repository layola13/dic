crowds:/kraʊdz/| n.|人群，群众|A large number of people gathered together.|例句：The crowds cheered as the parade went by. 游行队伍经过时，人群欢呼起来。

例句：Crowds of tourists flocked to the beach. 成群的游客涌向海滩。|近义词：throngs, masses, hordes|反义词:individuals, few|名词

crowd:/kraʊd/| n. vi. vt.|人群，拥挤，挤满|A large number of people gathered together.  To gather together in large numbers. To fill or occupy to excess.|例句：A crowd gathered to watch the street performer. 一群人聚集在一起观看街头表演者。

例句：Don't crowd me! 别挤我！|近义词：throng, mass, horde|反义词:few, scatter|名词，动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *crūdan*, "to press, crowd," of uncertain origin. Related to Middle Dutch *cruderen*, "to push."
