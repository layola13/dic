bleed:/bliːd/
verb
流血
lose blood from the body as a result of injury or illness.
The cut on his knee started to bleed. 他的膝盖上的伤口开始流血了。
近义词:hemorrhage, gush
反义词:clot, coagulate
词性:v.

bleed:/bliːd/
verb
渗色；掉色
(of color or ink) spread beyond a desired area.
The red dye bled into the white fabric.  红色的染料渗到了白色的织物上。
近义词:run, blur
反义词:N/A
词性:v.

bleed:/bliːd/
verb
榨取；勒索
extort money from (someone), typically by intimidation or deception.
Loan sharks were bleeding him dry with exorbitant interest rates.  放高利贷的人用过高的利率榨干了他。
近义词:extort, exploit
反义词:donate, give
词性:v.

词源：Old English *blēdan (attested only in past participle blēden), of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bloeden and German bluten.
