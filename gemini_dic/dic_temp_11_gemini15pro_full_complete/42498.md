counterinflationary:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/ | adj. | 反通货膨胀的，抑制通货膨胀的 |  Designed to counteract inflation; tending to reduce inflation. | The government implemented counterinflationary measures to stabilize the economy. 政府实施了反通货膨胀措施以稳定经济。 | Counterinflationary policies are crucial during periods of rapid price increases.  在物价快速上涨时期，反通货膨胀政策至关重要。 | Synonyms: deflationary, anti-inflationary | Antonyms: inflationary | Adjective

Etymology->
From counter- + inflationary.  "Counter-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite," and "inflationary" relates to inflation, the increase in the general price level of goods and services in an economy over a period of time.
