occluder:/əˈkluːdər/ | n. | 闭塞器，遮挡物 | Something that occludes, or blocks; something that obstructs. | The dentist used an occluder to block off the patient's saliva flow. 牙医使用闭塞器来阻挡病人的唾液流动。|  The photographer used an occluder to block out some of the light. 摄影师用遮挡物挡住一些光线。 | synonym: blocker, obstructer, obturator | antonym: opener, unblocker | noun

occlude:/əˈkluːd/ | v. | 阻塞，遮挡 | To stop, close up, or obstruct (an opening, passage, or vessel). |  The thrombus occluded the artery. 血栓阻塞了动脉。|  The tall buildings occluded our view of the mountains. 高楼遮挡了我们观看山脉的视线。| synonym: block, obstruct, close | antonym: open, unblock, clear | verb
