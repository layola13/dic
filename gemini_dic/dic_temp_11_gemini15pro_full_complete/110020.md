librettists:/lɪˈbrɛtɪsts/
n.
歌剧脚本作者，剧本作家
A librettist is a person who writes the text of an opera or other long vocal work.
The librettist worked closely with the composer to create a seamless blend of music and drama.  歌剧脚本作者与作曲家密切合作，创作出音乐与戏剧的完美融合。

The librettists for the new musical were highly praised for their witty and insightful lyrics.  这部新音乐剧的脚本作者因其诙谐而富有洞察力的歌词而备受赞誉。

近义词: playwright, screenwriter
反义词: composer
词性: noun
