majestatal:/məˈdʒɛstətəl/| adj.|雄伟的，庄严的，威严的|Having or showing impressive beauty, stateliness, and grandeur.|例句：The majestic mountains loomed over the valley. 雄伟的山脉俯瞰着山谷。

例句：The king's majestic presence filled the room. 国王的威严充满了整个房间。|近义词：grand, magnificent, imposing, stately, august, noble, dignified|反义词：humble, insignificant, unimpressive, common, ordinary|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *maiestaticus,* from maiestas "greatness, dignity, majesty," from maior (comparative of magnus "great") "greater, larger, elder."
