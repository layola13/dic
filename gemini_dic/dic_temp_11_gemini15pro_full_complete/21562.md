bluer:/ˈbluːər/| adj.|更蓝的，更忧郁的|Comparative form of blue. More blue in color or more sad/depressed. |The sky is bluer today than yesterday. 今天的天空比昨天更蓝。|近义词：deeper blue, more azure, sadder, gloomier|反义词：paler, lighter, happier, brighter|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *blāw, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch blauw and German blau.

Bluer than a robin's egg = a very bright, vivid blue 比知更鸟蛋还蓝（形容非常鲜艳的蓝色）

注意事项:  "bluer" 很少用于描述情绪，更常用于描述颜色。  当用于描述情绪时，它通常出现在非正式场合。
