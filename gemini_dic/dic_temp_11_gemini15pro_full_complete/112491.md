louping:/ˈluːpɪŋ/| adj.|跳跃的，成环的|(of an illness) spreading quickly among a group of people.  Relating to or characteristic of leaping or bounding.  Forming a loop or loops.|例句：The louping ill virus is spread by ticks.  跳跃病病毒是由蜱传播的。

The hare made a louping run across the field. 野兔跳跃着跑过田野。|近义词：leaping, jumping, bounding, looping|反义词:still, stationary|词性:adjective

Etymology: From the Scots word "loup," meaning "leap" or "jump."  Ultimately from Old Norse "hlaupa."
