blister:/ˈblɪstər/| n. |水泡；气泡；(表面上的)突起；(漆面上的)泡|A small bubble on the skin filled with serum and caused by friction, burning, or other damage.  |Example: I got a blister on my heel from my new shoes. 我的新鞋子把我的脚后跟磨出了水泡。

Example:  The paint on the wall was bubbling and forming blisters. 墙上的油漆正在起泡，形成一个个突起。|Synonyms: bleb, vesicle, sore, bubble|Antonyms:  smoothness, flatness|noun


blister:/ˈblɪstər/| v. |(使)起泡；(使)起水泡；(使)出现突起；猛烈抨击；痛骂|To form blisters; to cause blisters to form.|Example:  The hot sun blistered the paintwork.  炎热的太阳使油漆起了泡。

Example: He blistered his hands while working in the garden. 他在花园里干活时把手磨出了水泡。|Synonyms:  scald, burn, excoriate, criticize harshly|Antonyms:  soothe, heal|verb
