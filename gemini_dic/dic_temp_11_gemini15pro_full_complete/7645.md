annulosa:/ˌænjuˈloʊsə/ | adj. |环节动物的；有环纹的|Having rings or segments; resembling an annulus.  Relating to or characteristic of the Annelida, a large phylum of segmented worms. |The earthworm is an annulosa creature. 蚯蚓是一种环节动物。| The annulosa larva has distinct segments. 这种环节动物的幼虫有明显的节段。|segmented, ringed, annular |smooth, unsegmented |adjective
