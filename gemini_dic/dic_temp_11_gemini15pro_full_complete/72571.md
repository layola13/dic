fluviometer:/ˌfluːviˈɒmɪtər/ | n. | 雨量计；水位计 | An instrument for measuring the height of water in a river or stream, or the amount of rainfall. | The fluviometer indicated a significant rise in the river level after the heavy rain. 雨量计显示暴雨过后河流水位显著上升。|  The researchers installed a fluviometer to monitor the stream's flow rate. 研究人员安装了一个水位计来监测溪流的流量。 | 近义词: rain gauge, pluviometer,  stream gauge | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

fluviograph:/ˌfluːviˈɒɡrɑːf/ | n. | 水位记录器 | A self-recording fluviometer that creates a continuous record of water level or rainfall over time. | The fluviograph provided valuable data on the river's fluctuating water levels throughout the year. 水位记录器提供了全年河流波动水位的宝贵数据。 | The data from the fluviograph helped the engineers predict potential flooding. 水位记录器的数据帮助工程师预测潜在的洪水。 | 近义词:  water level recorder,  automatic rain gauge | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
