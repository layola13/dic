bandwidths:/ˈbændwɪdθs/| n.|带宽（复数）|The range of frequencies within a given band, in particular that used for transmitting a signal.  The capacity of a communication channel to transmit data.|Our internet connection has limited bandwidths, so downloading large files takes a long time. 我们的互联网连接带宽有限，所以下载大文件需要很长时间。|近义词：capacity, throughput|反义词:limitation, restriction|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "band" + "width". "Band" in the context of electronics refers to a range of frequencies. "Width" refers to the extent of that range.
