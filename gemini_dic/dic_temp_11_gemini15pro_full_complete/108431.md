lawyer-like:/ˈlɔɪərˌlaɪk/
adj.
像律师的；精明而谨慎的；严谨的
Resembling or characteristic of a lawyer, especially in being precise, argumentative, or cautious.
He gave a lawyer-like explanation of the contract. 他对合同作出了像律师一样严谨的解释。

近义词:legalistic, precise, meticulous, cautious, argumentative
反义词:careless, imprecise, rash, inarticulate
