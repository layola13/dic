chorepiscopus: /ˌkɔːrɪˈpɪskəpəs/ | n. | 助理主教；乡村主教 | A chorepiscopus was a priest of a lower rank than a bishop, especially in the early church, who assisted the bishop in rural districts. |  The chorepiscopus oversaw the smaller churches in the countryside. 乡村主教负责管理乡间的较小教堂。 |  The chorepiscopus reported directly to the bishop. 助理主教直接向主教汇报。 | synonyms: suffragan bishop, rural dean | antonyms: archbishop, metropolitan | noun
