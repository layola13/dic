hepatorenal:/ˌhɛpətəʊˈriːnəl/ /ˌhɛpætəˈrinəl/| adj.|肝肾的；肝肾综合征的|Relating to both the liver and the kidneys, or a syndrome involving both.  It describes a condition where there is simultaneous impairment of liver and kidney function.|The patient presented with hepatorenal syndrome due to advanced cirrhosis.|Synonym: hepatic-renal|Antonym: N/A|adjective


hepatorenal syndrome:/ˌhɛpətəʊˈriːnəl ˈsɪndrəʊm/  /ˌhɛpætəˈrinəl ˈsɪndrəʊm/| n.|肝肾综合征|A serious complication of advanced chronic liver disease in which there is progressive kidney failure.|Hepatorenal syndrome is a life-threatening condition.|Synonym: HRS|Antonym: N/A|noun
