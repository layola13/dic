aloofness:/əˈluːfnəs/
n.|冷漠，疏远，超然|The state of being distant, reserved, and uninterested in other people.|He maintained an aloofness that made him seem unapproachable. 他保持着一种冷漠的态度，让人觉得难以接近。|Synonyms: remoteness, detachment, indifference, reserve|Antonyms: friendliness, warmth, sociability, involvement|noun

aloof:/əˈluːf/
adj.|冷漠的，疏远的，超然的|Not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant.|She remained aloof from the other students. 她对其他学生保持冷漠。|Synonyms: distant, remote, detached, standoffish|Antonyms: friendly, warm, sociable, involved|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From a (on) + loof (luff), originally a nautical term meaning "to keep the ship's head close to the wind," hence "at a distance."  The figurative sense of emotional distance developed later.
