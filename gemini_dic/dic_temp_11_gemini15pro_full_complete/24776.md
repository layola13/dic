broomstick: /ˈbruːmstɪk/ | n. |扫帚柄；飞行扫帚 |The handle of a broom, typically made of wood; A broom, especially as used for riding on by witches in legends and fairy tales. |She leaned on her broomstick and surveyed the garden. 她倚着扫帚柄，环顾花园。 | The witch flew away on her broomstick. 女巫骑着她的扫帚飞走了。 |synonyms: broom handle, besom (archaic) | antonyms: None | noun
