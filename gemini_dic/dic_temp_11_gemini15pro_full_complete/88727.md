hobnobber:/ˈhɑːbnɑːbər/ | n. | 交上流社会的人；与要人交往的人；攀附权贵者 | A person who spends time with important or famous people, especially in order to gain advantage. | He's a real hobnobber, always trying to get in with the rich and famous. 他真是个攀附权贵的人，总是试图与富人和名人交往。 |  The mayor was accused of being a hobnobber, more interested in socializing with celebrities than addressing the city's problems. 市长被指责是攀附权贵者，他对与名人交往比解决城市问题更感兴趣。 | synonyms: social climber, name-dropper | antonyms: recluse, loner | noun
