decentnesses:/ˈdiːsntnɪsɪz/
n.
端庄，正派，得体（复数）
The quality of being decent; propriety of conduct or speech.
Her inherent decentnesses prevented her from stooping to gossip. 她与生俱来的端庄使她不屑于说长道短。
Near Synonyms: decorum, propriety, respectability, seemliness
Antonyms: indecency, impropriety, indecorum
