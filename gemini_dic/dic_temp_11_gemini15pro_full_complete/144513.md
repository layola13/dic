outtravelling:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəlɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
胜过旅行；比…更会旅行
Surpassing someone in the extent or duration of travel.
He was outtravelling even the most seasoned explorers. 他甚至比最有经验的探险家旅行得更多。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: lagging, trailing

词性: verb (present participle)
