colonizability:/ˌkɒlənaɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可殖民性，可定殖性 | The ease with which a place can be colonized, referring to the susceptibility of an environment or population to colonization, often by another species or group. |  The colonizability of the island was high due to its abundant resources and lack of indigenous defenses.  这个岛屿的可殖民性很高，因为它资源丰富，缺乏土著防御。| colonizable nature, susceptibility to colonization | resistance to colonization | noun
