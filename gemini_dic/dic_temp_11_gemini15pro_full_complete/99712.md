interlots:/ˈɪntərˌlɒts/ | n. |空地，空隙; （尤指）两块地之间的空地 | A piece of land between lots, especially one that is undeveloped. | The developer planned to build a small park in the interlots between the houses. 开发商计划在房屋之间的空地上建一个小公园。 |  The city council decided to turn the interlots into community gardens. 市议会决定把空地变成社区花园。 | synonyms: interstitial space, gap, void | antonyms:  developed land, building, structure | noun

注意事项:
"Interlots" is not a very common word.  "Vacant lots," "empty lots," or simply "lots" are more frequently used.  "Interlots" specifically refers to the space *between* developed lots.
