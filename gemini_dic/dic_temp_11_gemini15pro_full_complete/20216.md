birdlet:/ˈbɜːrdlət/| n. |雏鸟；幼鸟|A young bird, especially one that has recently left the nest.|The birdlet hopped clumsily on the branch. 雏鸟笨拙地在树枝上跳来跳去。|例句：The birdlet, barely feathered, chirped loudly for its mother.  这只几乎没有羽毛的雏鸟大声地向它的母亲啁啾。|近义词：chick, fledgling, nestling|反义词: adult bird|词性:noun
