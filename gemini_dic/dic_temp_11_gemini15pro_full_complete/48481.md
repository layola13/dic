deep-six:/ˌdiːpˈsɪks/ | v. | 彻底丢弃；抛弃；销毁；隐瞒；扼杀 | To discard or dispose of something completely; to get rid of something permanently and thoroughly.  Often implies secrecy or suppression. | The company deep-sixed the project after it proved too costly.  公司在项目成本过高后将其彻底放弃了。

The CEO deep-sixed the report that highlighted his mismanagement. 首席执行官隐瞒了那份强调他管理不善的报告。 | 近义词: discard, ditch, scrap, kill, bury, suppress | 反义词:  revive, resurrect, restore, publicize | 词性: 动词
