outflash:/ˈaʊtflæʃ/
verb
突然闪耀，迸发
to flash or burst forth suddenly and brilliantly
The lightning outflashed, illuminating the dark sky. 闪电突然迸发，照亮了黑暗的天空。
The fireworks outflashed in a dazzling display of color. 烟花突然闪耀，绽放出炫目的色彩。
synonyms: burst forth, flash, flare
antonyms: fade, dim
词性: 动词
