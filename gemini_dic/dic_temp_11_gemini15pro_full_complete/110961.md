liquefier:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪər/| n. |液化器; 液化机|A device used to convert a gas or solid into a liquid. |The liquefier cooled the natural gas to a liquid state for transport.  液化器将天然气冷却成液态以便运输。|synonyms: condenser, liquefaction unit|antonyms: vaporizer, gasifier|noun

liquefy:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪ/| v. |液化; (使) liquefy |To convert a gas or solid into a liquid.|The process liquefies the gas by cooling it to a very low temperature.  这个过程通过将气体冷却到非常低的温度来使其液化。|synonyms: condense, melt, dissolve|antonyms: vaporize, solidify, freeze (in the context of changing from liquid to solid)|verb
