anti-russian:/ˌæntiˈrʌʃən/
adjective
反俄的; 反对俄罗斯的
Opposed to Russia, its people, or its policies.
The senator's anti-Russian stance caused controversy.  参议员的反俄立场引起了争议。
The article expressed anti-Russian sentiments. 这篇文章表达了反俄情绪。

近义词:Russophobic
反义词:pro-Russian
词性:adjective
