metagraphic:/ˌmɛtəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
元图的; 后图形的
Relating to or denoting a metagraph (a secondary image formed by a combination of parts of other images).  Metagraphics often involve manipulating existing images or symbols to create new meanings.
This artist's work is highly metagraphic, blending and distorting familiar icons to create something new. 这位艺术家的作品极具元图性，将熟悉的图标混合和扭曲以创造新的东西。

The metagraphic nature of the meme made it instantly recognizable and shareable.  这个表情包的元图性使其立即被识别和分享。

近义词:  composite, synthesized, blended
反义词:  original, unedited, simple
词性: adjective
