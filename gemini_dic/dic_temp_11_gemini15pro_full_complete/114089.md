magnificently:/mæɡˈnɪfɪs(ə)ntli/
adv.
壮丽地，宏伟地
in a magnificent manner; splendidly.
The palace was magnificently decorated for the royal wedding.  这座宫殿为了皇室婚礼而装饰得富丽堂皇。
The sunset painted the sky magnificently.  日落壮丽地描绘了天空。
近义词: splendidly, grandly, impressively, superbly
反义词: poorly, plainly, simply, inadequately
词性: adverb
词源: From Middle French *magnifiquement, from Latin magnificus (“magnificent”) +‎ -ment.
