hyperintelligently:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəntli/
adverb|超级智能地，极度聪明地|In a manner exhibiting extremely high intelligence, exceeding normal human capabilities.|The AI system hyperintelligently analyzed the complex data and predicted the market trends with remarkable accuracy. 人工智能系统超级智能地分析了复杂的数据，并以惊人的准确性预测了市场趋势。|近义词: exceptionally intelligently, brilliantly, ingeniously|反义词: stupidly, foolishly, unintelligently
词性: 副词

Etymology:
* hyper- (prefix): from Greek *hyper*, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
* intelligent: from Latin *intelligēns*, present participle of *intelligō* "to understand," from *inter-* "between" + *legō* "choose, gather, pick out, read"
* -ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
