countermure: /ˈkaʊntərmjʊr/ | n. |  外侧护墙，外壁，对墙 | An outer supporting wall; an outwork. A wall built in front of another wall, especially as a reinforcement. | The castle was defended by a strong countermure. 这座城堡由坚固的外侧护墙保护。|  Synonyms: bulwark, rampart, parapet | Antonyms: breach, gap | noun

countermure: /ˈkaʊntərmjʊr/ | v. |  筑外侧护墙 | To furnish with a countermure. To fortify with an outer wall. | They countermured the existing wall to strengthen the defenses. 他们在现有的墙上筑了外侧护墙以加强防御。| Synonyms: reinforce, fortify, buttress | Antonyms: weaken, undermine | verb
