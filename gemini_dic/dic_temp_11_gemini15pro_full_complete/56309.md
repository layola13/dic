doggy-paddling:/ˈdɒɡi ˌpædlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡi ˌpædlɪŋ/ (US)| n. |狗刨式游泳|A swimming stroke resembling the paddling of a dog, characterized by short, quick movements of the arms and legs.|The child was doggy-paddling in the shallow end of the pool. 孩子在泳池的浅水区狗刨。|近义词：dog-paddle (v.), paddle (v.)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider more advanced strokes like freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke, butterfly)|noun

dog-paddle:/ˈdɒɡ ˌpædl/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡ ˌpædl/ (US)| v. |狗刨|To swim using the doggy-paddle stroke.|He dog-paddled across the pool to reach his friend. 他狗刨着游过泳池去到朋友身边。|近义词：doggy-paddle (n.), paddle (v.)|反义词: (Same as doggy-paddling)|verb
