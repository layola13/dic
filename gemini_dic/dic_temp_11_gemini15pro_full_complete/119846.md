mildly:/ˈmaɪldli/| adv.|轻微地；温和地；适度地|In a mild manner; to a slight or moderate degree.  It describes something that is not extreme or intense.|The weather was mildly warm. 天气稍微有点暖和。|近义词：slightly, gently, moderately|反义词：intensely, severely, extremely|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *myldly, from mylde (“mild”) + -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mildiaz.
