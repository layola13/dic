demoralize: /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪz/ /dɪˈmɔːrələɪz/
|v.| 使士气低落，使沮丧 |To deprive (someone) of spirit, courage, or confidence; dishearten.| The team was completely demoralized by the unexpected loss.  这支队伍因意外失利而士气彻底低落。|Synonyms: dishearten, discourage, dispirit, deject, depress |Antonyms: encourage, hearten, inspire, uplift|verb
