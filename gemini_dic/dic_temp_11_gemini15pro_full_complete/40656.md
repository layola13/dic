conventual:/kənˈvɛnʃuəl/| adj.|修道院的；惯例的；传统的|Relating to a convent or monastery; conforming to accepted standards; traditional.|例句：The nuns lived a conventual life. 修女们过着修道院的生活。

例句：His views on marriage are rather conventual. 他对婚姻的看法相当传统。|近义词：monastic, traditional, customary, orthodox|反义词：unconventional, unorthodox, modern, secular|词性:形容词

conventual:/kənˈvɛnʃuəl/| n.|修道士，修女|A member of a convent, especially a monk or friar.|例句：The conventual greeted us at the door. 修道士在门口迎接我们。

例句：Several conventuals attended the service. 几位修道士参加了仪式。|近义词：monk, friar, nun|反义词:secular, layman|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conventualis*, from Latin conventus (“assembly, meeting, agreement”), past participle of convenire (“to come together, assemble”).
