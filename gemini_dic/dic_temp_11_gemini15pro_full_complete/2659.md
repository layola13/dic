adores:/əˈdɔːrz/| v. |崇拜；爱慕；非常喜欢|To love and respect someone deeply, or to like something very much.|She adores her grandchildren. 她非常疼爱她的孙子孙女们。|Synonyms: loves, cherishes, worships, idolizes|Antonyms: hates, despises, loathes|Verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *adouren*, from Old French *adorer*, from Latin *adorare* (“to worship, adore, pray to”), from *ad-* (“to”) + *orare* (“to pray, speak, plead”).
