darnings:/ˈdɑːrnɪŋz/
n.
修补；缝补处；织补
The act of mending holes in fabric by weaving thread across the gap.  Small repairs made by darning.
She spent the evening doing her darnings. 她晚上都在缝补衣物。
The darnings on his socks were almost invisible. 他袜子上的补丁几乎看不出来。
mendings, repairs, patches
tears, holes
noun
