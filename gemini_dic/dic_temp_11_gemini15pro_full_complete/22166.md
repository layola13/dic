bombasine:/ˌbɒmbəˈziːn/ /ˌbɑːmbəˈziːn/
bombasine: n. | bombazine, 斜纹哔叽| A twilled fabric, typically of mixed silk and worsted, or sometimes cotton and worsted, giving it a slightly shiny surface. |  She wore a dress of dark bombasine. 她穿着一件深色的斜纹哔叽连衣裙。| synonyms:twill, fabric | antonyms: None | noun


bombasine:/ˌbɒmbəˈziːn/ /ˌbɑːmbəˈziːn/
bombasine: adj. | made of bombasine | Made from the twilled fabric known as bombasine. | The bombasine curtains gave the room a somber feel. 斜纹哔叽窗帘使房间显得阴沉。| synonyms:twilled | antonyms: None | adjective
