denunciate:/dɪˈnʌnsiˌeɪt/ /dɪˈnaʊnsiˌeɪt/
verb
公开谴责，抨击；告发
To publicly declare something to be wrong or evil; to inform against.
The newspaper denounced the government's new policy.  这家报纸谴责了政府的新政策。
He was denounced as a traitor. 他被 denounced as a traitor. 他被谴责为叛徒。
近义词: condemn, criticize, censure, denigrate, accuse
反义词: praise, commend, laud, extol
词性: verb
