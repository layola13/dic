browny:/ˈbraʊni/| adj. n.| 褐色的，棕色皮肤的；巧克力果仁饼|A browny is a small, flat, baked square made from chocolate, butter, sugar, and often nuts.  A browny can also refer to someone with brown skin or hair.|例句：She baked a batch of delicious brownies. 她烤了一批美味的巧克力果仁饼。

例句：He is a browny with curly hair. 他是一个棕色皮肤卷发的人。|近义词：brown, brunette (for people), chocolate square|反义词:blond, fair (for people)|词性:adjective, noun
词源: From brown + -y.  The noun sense referring to the baked good originated in the US in the late 19th century.
