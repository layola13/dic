built-up:/ˌbɪlt ˈʌp/
adjective
建成的；发达的；密集的
covered with buildings; developed; having a lot of buildings close together
The city center is very built-up.  市中心建筑密集。
The area is heavily built-up.  这个地区建筑密集。
近义词: developed, urbanized, congested
反义词: rural, undeveloped, sparsely populated
词性: adjective

built-up:/ˌbɪlt ˈʌp/
noun
密集区；组合构件
An area with many buildings. A component made up of several parts.
The built-up prevented any view of the ocean.  密集的建筑遮挡了海景。
The bridge is a built-up structure of steel and concrete. 这座桥是由钢筋混凝土组合构件建成的。
近义词: development, complex, assembly
反义词: countryside, wilderness, component
词性: noun
