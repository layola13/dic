nonconservation:/ˌnɒnkɒnsəˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 非守恒，不守恒性 |  The nonconservation of a quantity means that its value can change over time or across different situations.  It's the opposite of conservation, where a quantity remains constant. |  The nonconservation of energy in certain systems is a topic of ongoing research. 在某些系统中，能量的不守恒性是一个持续研究的课题。 | non-persistence, variability | conservation, constancy | noun
