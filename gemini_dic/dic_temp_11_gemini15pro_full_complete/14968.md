baaing:/ˈbeɪɪŋ/ | n. | 羊叫声，咩咩叫 | The sound a sheep makes. |  The baaing of the sheep kept me awake all night. 羊的叫声让我整夜无法入睡。 | 近义词: bleating | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound) | 词性: noun

词源: From the verb "baa," imitative of the sound. 源自动词“baa”，模仿羊的叫声。
