night-schools:/ˈnaɪt skuːlz/
n.
夜校；夜间学校
Schools that offer classes in the evening, typically for adults who work during the day.
Many adults attend night-schools to improve their skills or gain new qualifications. 许多成年人上夜校来提高技能或获得新的资格证书。
He earned his degree by attending night-schools after work. 他下班后上夜校获得了学位。
