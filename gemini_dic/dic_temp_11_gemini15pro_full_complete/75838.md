fumitory:/ˈfjuːmɪtɔːri/ (US) /ˈfjuːmɪtri/ (UK)| n. |紫堇;延胡索|A delicate European plant (Fumaria officinalis) with greyish leaves and purplish flowers, formerly used in herbal medicine.
