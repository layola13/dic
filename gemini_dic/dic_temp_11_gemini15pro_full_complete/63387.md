entorganism:/ˈɛntɔːrɡənɪzəm/
n.
内共生体，体内共生生物
An organism that lives within the body or cells of another organism.
The entoorganism benefited from the stable environment within its host.  这个内共生体受益于其宿主体内稳定的环境。
Bacteria living within the gut can be considered entoorganisms.  生活在肠道内的细菌可以被认为是内共生体。
