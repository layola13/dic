lacrimoso:/ˌlækrɪˈmoʊsoʊ/| adj.| 泪流满面的；悲伤的；催人泪下的 |Tearful; mournful; expressing sorrow.  Relating to or causing tears. |The film's lacrimoso score enhanced the tragic love story.  这部电影催人泪下的配乐增强了悲剧爱情故事的效果。|Synonyms: tearful, weeping, sorrowful, doleful, plaintive| Antonyms: joyful, cheerful, happy, merry| adjective

Etymology: From Italian *lacrimoso*, from Latin *lacrimōsus* ("tearful, weeping"), from *lacrima* ("tear").
