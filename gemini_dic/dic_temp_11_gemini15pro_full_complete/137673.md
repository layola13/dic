nonuniteable:/ˌnɒnjuːˈnaɪtəbl/
adjective
不可结合的；不能愈合的
Not able to be united or joined together, especially of a broken bone.
The doctor determined that the fracture was nonuniteable and would require surgery. 医生断定骨折无法愈合，需要手术。
non-uniting, ununitable, unjoinable
unitable, joinable
词性: 形容词
