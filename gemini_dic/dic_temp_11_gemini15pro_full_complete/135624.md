nonpractical:/ˌnɑːnˈpræktɪkəl/ | adj. |不切实际的，不实用的| Not adapted for use or action; not sensible or realistic. |  His nonpractical suggestions were quickly dismissed.  他不切实际的建议很快就被否决了。| Example: Investing all your money in lottery tickets is a nonpractical plan. 把你所有的钱都投资在彩票上是一个不切实际的计划。| Synonyms: impractical, unrealistic, unworkable, infeasible | Antonyms: practical, realistic, workable, feasible | adjective
