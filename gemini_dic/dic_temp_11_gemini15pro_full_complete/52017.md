digraph:/ˈdaɪɡræf/| n.|双字母，二合字母|A combination of two letters representing one sound, such as "ph" in "phone" or "ea" in "head".|The digraph "th" represents two different sounds, as in "thin" and "this". 双字母组合“th”代表两种不同的发音，例如在“thin”和“this”中。|synonym: ligature|antonym: -|noun
