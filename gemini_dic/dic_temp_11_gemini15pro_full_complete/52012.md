diagrammatic:/ˌdaɪəɡrəˈmætɪk/
adjective
图解的；图表的；用图表示的
Of or relating to a diagram; using diagrams.
The book provides a diagrammatic representation of the human nervous system. 这本书提供了人类神经系统的图解表示。
The instructions were clear and diagrammatic, making it easy to assemble the furniture.  说明书清晰且图文并茂，使家具组装变得容易。
近义词:graphic, schematic, visual, pictorial
反义词:textual, descriptive, written
