directoire:/ˌdiˈrɛk.twɑːr/ /ˌdɪˈrektɔːr/
n.
督政府时期,督政府,督政府时期风格
A period of government in France from 1795 to 1799, between the Reign of Terror and the Consulate of Napoleon.  Also refers to the style of furniture and clothing popular during that time.
The Directoire period saw a reaction against the excesses of the Revolution.  督政府时期见证了对革命过度行为的反弹。
Her dress was clearly of the Directoire style. 她的裙子显然是督政府时期的风格。
近义词: Consulate (referring to the subsequent period), era, period
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun

Etymology:
From French *directoire*, from *directeur* ("director"), from Latin *dirigere* ("to direct").
