orthosilicic:/ˌɔːrθəʊsɪˈlɪsɪk/ |adj.|原硅酸的|Relating to or derived from orthosilicic acid (Si(OH)4), the most common form of silicic acid.|Orthosilicic acid is crucial for the growth of diatoms. 原硅酸对硅藻的生长至关重要。|近义词: silicic |反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From ortho- + silicic.  "Ortho-" is a prefix meaning "straight," "true," or "correct," often used in chemistry to denote the most hydrated form of an acid. "Silicic" refers to silicon-containing acids.
