overgrazes:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪzɪz/
|v.|过度放牧|To allow animals to graze (an area of land) so heavily that the vegetation is damaged and the ground becomes liable to erosion.|The farmer overgrazes his cattle on the small pasture.|近义词：overstock, exhaust|反义词：undergraze|verb (third-person singular simple present overgrazes, present participle overgrazing, simple past overgrazed, past participle overgrazed)
