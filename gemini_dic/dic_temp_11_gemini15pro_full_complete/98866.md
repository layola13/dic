intenable:/ɪnˈtenəbl/ /ɪnˈtenəbəl/| adj.|无法防守的，站不住脚的，难以维持的|Not able to be maintained or defended against attack or objection.|Her position had become untenable and she was forced to resign.|近义词：indefensible, unsustainable, unjustifiable|反义词：tenable, defensible, justifiable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin intenabilis, from in- ‘not’ + tenabilis ‘able to be held’.
