hillbilly:/ˈhɪlbɪli/ (US) /ˈhɪlbɪli/ (UK)| n.|乡巴佬，山里人|A derogatory term for someone from a rural, mountainous area, especially in the Appalachian Mountains of the US, often stereotyped as uneducated, unsophisticated, and poverty-stricken.|The movie portrayed the hillbilly as a lazy, ignorant person.|近义词：yokel, redneck, rustic, country bumpkin|反义词:sophisticate, cosmopolitan|noun
