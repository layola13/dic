ergat:/ˈɜːrɡæt/| n. |工蚁|A worker ant, especially one that is sterile.|The colony depended on the tireless work of the ergats.|近义词:worker ant|反义词:queen ant|词性:名词

Etymology: From Ancient Greek *έργάτης (ergátēs, “worker”), from *έργον (érgon, “work”).
