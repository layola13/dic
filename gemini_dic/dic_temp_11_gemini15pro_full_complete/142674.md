ostrogothic:/ˌɒstrəˈɡɒθɪk/ /ˌɑːstroʊˈɡɑːθɪk/
adjective
东哥特式的
Relating to the Ostrogoths, an East Germanic people who established a kingdom in Italy.
The Ostrogothic kingdom fell to the Byzantines in the 6th century.  东哥特王国在6世纪被拜占庭帝国征服。
The museum houses a collection of Ostrogothic artifacts.  博物馆收藏了一批东哥特文物。
近义词: East Germanic
反义词: Visigothic
词性: adjective
