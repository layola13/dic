mealy-mouthed:/ˈmiːliˌmaʊðd/ /ˈmiːliˌmaʊθt/
adjective
说话委婉的，拐弯抹角的，不敢直言的
Speaking indirectly and evasively, especially for fear of giving offense.
He gave a mealy-mouthed apology, never actually admitting he was wrong. 他做了一个拐弯抹角的道歉，实际上从未承认自己错了。
近义词:indirect, evasive, reticent, circumspect
反义词:direct, frank, candid, forthright
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From mealy (soft, crumbly) + mouthed.  The image is of someone whose mouth is too soft to speak plainly.
