what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么，|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词: which |反义词: (无直接反义词，可根据语境选择) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了些什么来帮忙？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 聚会上发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? 你离开前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long?  他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了什么十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would have happened if I hadn't been there? 如果我不在那里会发生什么？



被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？



疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？



否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I can say.  我什么也说不出来。


PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/你好吗？

So what? 那又怎样？
