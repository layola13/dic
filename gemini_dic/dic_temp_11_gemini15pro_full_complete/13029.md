astrakhans:/ˈæstrəkænz/
n.
阿斯特拉罕皮毛;用阿斯特拉罕皮毛制成的衣物
tightly curled fur from young lambs of the karakul breed; garments made of astrakhan.
My grandmother had a beautiful astrakhan coat. 我祖母有一件漂亮的阿斯特拉罕皮大衣。
Synonyms: karakul, persian lamb
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific type of fur)
noun
