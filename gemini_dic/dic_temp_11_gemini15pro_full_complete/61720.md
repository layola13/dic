emotionalize: /ɪˈmoʊʃənəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 使感情化，使情绪激动 | To imbue with emotion; to make emotional or sentimental. | The director emotionalized the scene with dramatic music. 导演用戏剧性的音乐使场景充满感情。|  The politician emotionalized the issue to gain support.  这位政治家为了获得支持而煽动民众情绪。 | 近义词: dramatize, sentimentalize, sensationalize | 反义词: rationalize, objectify | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From emotion + -ize.  "Emotion" comes from Middle French "esmovoir" meaning "to stir up, excite". "-ize" is a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become like" something.
