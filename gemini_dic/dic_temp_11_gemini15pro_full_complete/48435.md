deducting:/dɪˈdʌktɪŋ/
verb
扣除，减去
present participle of deduct, meaning "to subtract or take away (an amount or part) from a total"
After deducting taxes, my net income was much lower. 扣除税款后，我的净收入低了很多。
The company is deducting a percentage of my salary for health insurance. 公司从我的工资中扣除一部分用于健康保险。
synonyms: subtracting, removing, taking away, withholding
antonyms: adding, increasing, supplementing
