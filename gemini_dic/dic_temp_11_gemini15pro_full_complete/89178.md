homeostasis:/ˌhoʊmiːoʊˈsteɪsɪs/| n.|体内平衡，稳态|The process by which an organism maintains a stable internal environment, such as body temperature or blood sugar levels, despite changes in the external environment.|The human body maintains homeostasis through a complex system of feedback mechanisms.|synonyms: stability, equilibrium, balance|antonyms: imbalance, instability|noun
