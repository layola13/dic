cephalosome: /ˈsɛfələˌsoʊm/ | n. | 头胸部 | The fused head and thorax of some arthropods, such as crustaceans and arachnids. | The crab's cephalosome is covered by a hard carapace.  螃蟹的头胸部被坚硬的甲壳覆盖。 | Synonym: cephalothorax | Antonym: abdomen | noun

cephalosome: /ˈsɛfələˌsoʊm/ | n. | 头胸部 | The anterior part of the body in some invertebrates, comprising the head and thorax fused together. |  The spider's cephalosome bears the eyes, mouthparts, and legs. 蜘蛛的头胸部有眼睛、口器和腿。 | Synonym: cephalothorax | Antonym: abdomen | noun
