domineeringly:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋli/ /ˌdɑːmɪˈnɪrɪŋli/
adv.
专横地，跋扈地
in a domineering manner; overbearingly.  Showing a tendency to control others; bossy.
She domineeringly told everyone what to do. 她专横地告诉每个人该做什么。
He spoke domineeringly, silencing all opposition. 他专横地说着，压制了所有的反对意见。
近义词:bossily, imperiously, overbearingly, tyrannically, autocratically
反义词:submissively, meekly, humbly
词性:adverb
词源:From domineering + -ly.  Domineering comes from the verb 'domineer', from French *dominer*, from Latin dominari 'to rule, govern,' from dominus 'lord, master.'
