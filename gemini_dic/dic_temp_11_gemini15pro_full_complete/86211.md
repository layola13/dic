heathenizing:/ˈhiːðənaɪzɪŋ/
v.
使异教化；使不信教
The process of converting someone or something to heathenism or causing them to adopt heathen practices.
Missionaries feared the heathenizing influence of local customs on their converts.  传教士担心当地习俗对皈依者的异教化影响。
The conquerors attempted to heathenize the indigenous population by destroying their temples and sacred texts. 征服者试图通过摧毁土著居民的寺庙和圣典来使他们异教化。
近义词: paganizing, barbarizing
反义词: Christianizing, evangelizing
词性: 动词 (verb)
