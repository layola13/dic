asleep:/əˈsliːp/| adj.|睡着的，熟睡的|In a state of sleep; sleeping.|例句：The baby is asleep. 婴儿睡着了。|近义词：sleeping, slumbering, resting|反义词:awake, conscious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *on slæpe (literally "in sleep"), from on (preposition) + slæp (noun, "sleep"). The modern form arose through fusion of the two words.
