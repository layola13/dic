mortarless:/ˈmɔːrtərlɪs/
adj.
无砂浆的，不用砂浆的
Constructed without the use of mortar, a binding material used in masonry.
The Incas built magnificent mortarless stone walls. 印加人建造了宏伟的无砂浆石墙。
近义词:dry-stone, dry-laid
反义词:mortared
词性:形容词
