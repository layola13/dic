overassessing:/ˌoʊvərəˈsesɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过高评估；过分评估
Evaluating something too highly or giving it more importance than it deserves.
He's overassessing the risk of travelling by train. 他过高估计了乘火车旅行的风险。
overestimating, overvaluing
underassessing, undervaluing
v.
