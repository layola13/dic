mandalic:/mænˈdælɪk/
adjective
曼荼罗的;  mandala 形容词
Relating to or resembling a mandala.
例句：The artist created a complex and beautiful mandalic pattern. 艺术家创作了一个复杂而美丽的曼荼罗图案。

例句：The therapist used mandalic art therapy to help the patient explore their inner self.  治疗师使用曼荼罗艺术疗法来帮助病人探索他们的内心世界。

近义词：mandala-like, circular, geometric, symbolic
反义词:  (No clear antonym exists, potentially chaotic, disordered)
