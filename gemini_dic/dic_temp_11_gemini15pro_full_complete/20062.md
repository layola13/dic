biopoeic:/ˌbaɪoʊˈpɔɪɪk/
adjective
生物诗学的; 生命创造的;
Relating to biopoiesis, the process of living organisms producing other living organisms.  Pertaining to the creation of life, especially by other living organisms.
Biopoeic processes are fundamental to the continuation of life on Earth. 地球上的生命延续，生物诗学过程至关重要。
The scientist was fascinated by biopoeic mechanisms and sought to replicate them in the lab.  这位科学家对生物诗学机制很着迷，并试图在实验室中复制它们。
近义词:  life-creating,  biogenetic,  vital
反义词: abiogenic
词性: adjective
