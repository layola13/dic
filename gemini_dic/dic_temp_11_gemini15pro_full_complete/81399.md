grasses:/ˈɡræsɪz/ | n. |草；草地；牧草 |A common green plant with narrow leaves growing close together, especially covering the ground.  The plural form of "grass". |The horses grazed peacefully in the tall grasses. 马匹在高高的草丛中平静地吃草。| 近义词: herbage, vegetation, turf | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Old English "græs," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch "gras" and German "Gras."
