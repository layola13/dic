caique:/kaɪˈiːk/| n.|小艇，轻舟；(尤指)土耳其式小艇|A small boat, especially a Turkish one, propelled by oars or sails.|We took a caique to a secluded cove. 我们乘小艇去了一个僻静的小海湾。|近义词：skiff, dinghy, rowboat|反义词:ship, yacht|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *caïque*, from Turkish *kayık* (small boat), from Ottoman Turkish *قایق*‎ (*ḳayïḳ*), probably ultimately from Proto-Turkic * *käyük* (boat).
