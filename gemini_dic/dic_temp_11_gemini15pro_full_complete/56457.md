doloroso:/doʊləˈroʊsoʊ/ /dɒləˈrəʊsəʊ/ | adj. | 悲痛的，悲伤的，痛苦的 |  Causing or expressing sorrow or pain; mournful.  Derived from the Spanish word "doloroso," ultimately from the Latin "dolor" meaning "pain" or "grief." |  The violinist played a doloroso melody that brought tears to the audience's eyes. 小提琴手演奏了一曲悲伤的旋律，使观众热泪盈眶。

The funeral procession was a doloroso sight. 送葬队伍是一幅令人悲痛的景象。 | 近义词: sorrowful, mournful, plaintive, doleful, pathetic | 反义词: joyful, happy, cheerful | 词性: adjective
