outpursue:/ˌaʊtpərˈsuː/
verb
超越；胜过；比…更成功
to pursue something more effectively than someone else
例句：He outpursued his rivals and won the race. 他超越了对手，赢得了比赛。

例句：She outpursued all the other applicants for the job.  她在求职中胜过了所有其他申请人。

近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词：lag behind, fall behind

词性：v.
