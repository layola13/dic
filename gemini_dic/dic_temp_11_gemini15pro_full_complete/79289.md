glaum:/ɡlɔːm/ | n. | 欺骗；诡计；骗局 | An instance of deception; a trick; a swindle.  | He tried to sell me a broken car, but I saw through his glaum. 他想把一辆破车卖给我，但我识破了他的骗局。 | 近义词: trick, ruse, deception, hoax, fraud, swindle | 反义词: honesty, truthfulness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Scots <em>glaum</em>, <em>glam</em> ("grab, snatch"), of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse <em>gláma</em> ("to stare, gape").
