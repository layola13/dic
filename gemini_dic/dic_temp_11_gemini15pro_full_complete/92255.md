hypervariable:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛəriəbl/ | adj. | 高变的，高度可变的 |  Exhibiting a high degree of variability or mutation, especially relating to specific regions of DNA or protein sequences.  | The hypervariable regions of antibodies are responsible for their ability to bind to a wide range of antigens. 抗体的超变区负责它们与各种抗原结合的能力。 |  highly variable, mutable |  invariable, stable | adjective
