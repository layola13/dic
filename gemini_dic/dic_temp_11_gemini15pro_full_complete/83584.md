haematocryal:/ˌhiːmətəˈkraɪəl/| adj.|冷血的，变温的|Having cold blood; poikilothermic.  Relating to or characteristic of animals whose body temperature varies with the surrounding environment.|Certain haematocryal animals, like reptiles, bask in the sun to regulate their body temperature.|近义词：poikilothermic, cold-blooded|反义词：homeothermic, warm-blooded|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *haima* (blood) + *kryos* (cold).
