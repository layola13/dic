lardacein:/lɑːrˈdeɪsiɪn/
n.
淀粉样物质，蜡样蛋白
A waxy, translucent protein that is deposited in the tissues in certain diseases, especially amyloidosis.
Amyloidosis is characterized by the buildup of amyloid proteins, including lardacein, in organs and tissues.  淀粉样变性的特征是淀粉样蛋白（包括蜡样蛋白）在器官和组织中积聚。
Lardacein deposits can affect the function of various organs. 蜡样蛋白沉积会影响各种器官的功能。
近义词: amyloid

词性: noun
