humankind:/ˌhjuːmənˈkaɪnd/ | n. | 人类 | All of humanity; the human race. |例句：Humankind has made great strides in technology.  人类在科技方面取得了巨大进步。

例句：The future of humankind depends on our actions today. 人类的未来取决于我们今天的行动。| 近义词：humanity, mankind, man,  humankind | 反义词：None (No single antonym for the entire human race) | 词性：noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *humankynde*, equivalent to human + kind.
