aggressively:/əˈɡrɛsɪvli/
adverb
侵略地，攻击地，强势地
In a manner characterized by or tending toward aggression;  forcefully, offensively.  Showing a readiness to attack or confront.
The company aggressively pursued new markets.  该公司积极开拓新市场。
近义词:forcefully, assertively, militantly, proactively
反义词:passively, submissively, defensively, meekly
词性:adverb

aggressively:/əˈɡrɛsɪvli/
adverb
积极地，进取地
In a determined and energetic way, showing strong and confident action.
He aggressively sought promotion within the company. 他积极争取公司内的晋升。
近义词:actively, ambitiously, dynamically, energetically
反义词: passively, lazily, indifferently, apathetically
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From aggressive + -ly. Aggressive comes from Late Latin aggressīvus, from Latin aggress-, past participle stem of aggredi "to attack," from ad- "to" + gradi "to step, go."
