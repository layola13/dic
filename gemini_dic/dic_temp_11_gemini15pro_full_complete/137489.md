nontotalitarians:/ˌnɒntəʊˌtælɪˈtɛːrɪənz/ | n. |非极权主义者| People who do not adhere to or support a totalitarian system of government. |  The nontotalitarians advocated for democratic reforms. 非极权主义者倡导民主改革。| synonyms: democrats, liberals, libertarians | antonyms: totalitarians, authoritarians, dictators | noun

nontotalitarian:/ˌnɒntəʊˌtælɪˈtɛːrɪən/ | adj. |非极权主义的|  Not characterized by or supporting totalitarianism. | The country transitioned to a nontotalitarian regime.  这个国家过渡到一个非极权主义政权。| synonyms: democratic, liberal, free | antonyms: totalitarian, authoritarian, dictatorial | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + totalitarian.  "Totalitarian" comes from Italian *totalitario*, from *totale* (total) + *-ario* (forming adjectives). "Total" ultimately derives from Latin *totus* (whole).  "Non-" is a Latin prefix meaning "not."
