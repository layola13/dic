breaker:/ˈbreɪkər/| n.|断路器; 破坏者; 碎浪;|A device that interrupts the flow of electricity in a circuit when an overload occurs.  A person or thing that breaks something. A wave that breaks on the shore.|The circuit breaker tripped and cut off the power. 断路器跳闸，切断了电源。|
synonyms: interrupter, disconnector, switch, destroyer, wrecker|
antonyms: connector, builder, creator|noun


breaker:/ˈbreɪkər/| n.|打破者；打破常规者|Someone who breaks with established conventions or traditions.|He's a real breaker of conventions. 他真是一个打破常规的人。|
synonyms: iconoclast, maverick, revolutionary|
antonyms: conformist, traditionalist|noun
