caging:/ˈkeɪdʒɪŋ/
n.
关在笼子里；囚禁；装笼
the act of putting or keeping something or someone in a cage.
The caging of the tigers was controversial.  老虎被关进笼子这件事引起了争议。

近义词: confinement, imprisonment, enclosure
反义词: releasing, freeing, liberation
词性: 名词 (noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From cage + -ing.  Cage itself comes from Old French "cage," ultimately from Latin "cavea" meaning "cavity, cage."
