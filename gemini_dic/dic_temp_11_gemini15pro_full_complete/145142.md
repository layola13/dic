overasserting:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪŋ/
| adj. | 过分自信的；过分断言的 |  The act of asserting oneself or one's claims too forcefully or aggressively.  |  He was overasserting his authority, making everyone uncomfortable.  他过分强调自己的权威，让每个人都不舒服。 | synonyms:  overbearing, domineering, pushy, aggressive, assertive | antonyms:  submissive, meek, timid, unassertive, retiring | adjective

overassert:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrt/
| v. | 过分自信；过分断言 | To assert oneself or one's claims too forcefully or aggressively. |  She tended to overassert herself in meetings, interrupting others frequently.  她在会议上往往过于自信，经常打断别人。| synonyms:  overstate, exaggerate, overemphasize | antonyms:  understate, downplay, minimize | verb
