impedingly:/ɪmˈpiːdɪŋli/
adverb
阻碍地，妨碍地

The heavy traffic was moving impedingly slow.  交通拥堵，缓慢行驶。
Synonyms: hinderingly, obstructively, restrictively
Antonyms: helpfully, supportively, encouragingly
