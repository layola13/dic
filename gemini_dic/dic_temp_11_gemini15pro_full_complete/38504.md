conciliary: /kənˈsɪliəri/ | adj. | 和解的；安抚的；调解的；安抚性的；宗教会议的 | Relating to or intended to conciliate; relating to a council, especially a church council. |  The government adopted a conciliary approach to the striking workers. 政府对罢工工人采取了安抚的策略。| The conciliary decree was issued by the Pope.  教皇颁布了宗教会议的法令。 |  近义词: appeasing, pacifying, mediating, irenic | 反义词: antagonistic, hostile, aggressive | 词性: adjective

conciliary: /kənˈsɪliəri/ | n. |  宗教会议成员 | A member of a council, especially a church council. | The conciliaries gathered for the annual meeting. 宗教会议成员聚集在一起召开年度会议。 |  He was a respected conciliary known for his wisdom. 他是一位受人尊敬的宗教会议成员，以他的智慧而闻名。| 近义词: council member, delegate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *conciliarius, from Latin conciliare (see conciliate).
