naps:/næps/ | n. | 小睡，打盹 |A short period of sleep, typically during the day.|  He likes to take naps after lunch. 他喜欢午饭后小睡一会儿。

The cat was taking a nap in a sunbeam.  这只猫在阳光下打盹。

|近义词：dozes, siestas, rests|反义词：wakefulness, insomnia|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hnappian, frequentative of *hnappan “to doze, slumber,” of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch knappen “to snap, crack,” German knappen “to crunch.” The sense evolution seems to have been from “to make a snapping or clicking sound (while dozing)” to “to doze.”
