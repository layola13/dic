console:/ˈkɒnsəʊl/ (British), /kənˈsoʊl/ (American)| n. vt.|安慰，抚慰；控制台，操纵台|A console is a panel or unit that contains the controls for a machine, piece of electrical equipment, computer, etc.  As a verb, it means to comfort someone at a time of grief or disappointment.|The pilot adjusted the controls on the console.  飞行员调整了控制台上的控件。 She consoled him after his loss.  在他失去亲人后，她安慰了他。|近义词:comfort, solace, control panel, dashboard|反义词:distress, upset, aggravate|noun, verb
