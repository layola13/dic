half-fertileness:/ˌhæf fərˈtɪlnəs/
n.|半不育性|The condition where a hybrid offspring can only produce viable gametes with half the usual number of chromosomes, often leading to reduced fertility.|The hybrid plants exhibited half-fertileness, producing only a few viable seeds.|Near synonyms: semi-sterility, reduced fertility|Antonym: full fertility|noun

half-fertileness:/ˌhæf fərˈtɪlnəs/
n.|半育性|The condition where a hybrid offspring has reduced fertility due to problems with chromosome pairing during meiosis.|The mule's half-fertileness is a classic example of this phenomenon.|Near synonyms: semi-sterility, reduced fertility|Antonym: full fertility|noun
