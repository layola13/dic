cosy:/ˈkoʊzi/ (US) /ˈkəʊzi/ (UK)| adj.|舒适的，温暖的，惬意的|Giving a feeling of comfort, warmth, and relaxation.|例句：We spent a cosy evening in front of the fire. 我们在壁炉前度过了一个舒适的夜晚。

例句：The room was small and cosy. 房间小而舒适。

近义词：comfortable, snug, warm, homely|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, bleak|词性:adjective


cosy:/ˈkoʊzi/ (US) /ˈkəʊzi/ (UK)| n.|保暖罩，茶壶套|A padded covering for a teapot or other container to keep the contents warm.|例句：She put a cosy on the teapot. 她给茶壶套上一个保暖罩。

例句：The teapot cosy was knitted by her grandmother.  这个茶壶套是她祖母织的。

近义词：tea cosy, cover|反义词：-|词性:noun
