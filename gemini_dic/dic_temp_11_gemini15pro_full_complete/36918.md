colorman: /ˈkʌlərmən/ | n. |颜料商，染料商；油漆匠|A person who sells or manufactures colors, paints, or dyes; a painter and decorator.|例句：The colorman mixed a custom shade of blue for the artist.  颜料商为艺术家调制了一种定制的蓝色。

例句：The colorman carefully prepared the walls before applying the first coat of paint. 油漆匠在涂第一层油漆之前仔细地准备了墙壁。|近义词：painter, decorator, dye merchant, pigment seller |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "color" + "man."  "Color" comes from Old French "couleur," ultimately from Latin "color." "Man" comes from Old English "mann."
