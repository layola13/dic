consideration:/kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 考虑；体贴；报酬；尊敬 | Careful thought, typically over a period of time.  A factor taken into account in forming a judgment or decision.  A payment or reward.  Respect and politeness for the feelings of others. |例句：After much consideration, he decided to accept the offer. 经过深思熟虑后，他决定接受这个提议。

例句：Your application is under consideration. 你的申请正在考虑中。|近义词：thought, deliberation, reflection, contemplation, attention, regard, respect, courtesy |反义词:disregard, inattention, disrespect |词性:noun

consideration:/kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 考虑；体贴；报酬；尊敬 |  |例句：She showed great consideration for her elderly parents. 她对年迈的父母表现出极大的关怀。

例句：He received a large sum of money in consideration for his services. 他因服务而获得一大笔报酬。 |近义词：thoughtfulness, kindness, solicitude|反义词:rudeness, thoughtlessness, selfishness |词性:noun

词源: From Old French consideracion, from Latin consideratio, from considerare "to consider," from com- "together" + sidus "star" (literally "to observe the stars together").  The original sense was "observation of the stars," later broadened to "mental observation, reflection."
