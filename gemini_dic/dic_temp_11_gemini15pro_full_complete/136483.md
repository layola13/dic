nonrhymes:/ˈnɒnˌraɪmz/ | n. | 非韵字，不押韵的词 |Words or lines that do not rhyme with each other are called nonrhymes. 不押韵的词或行被称为非韵字。|  The poem is full of near rhymes and nonrhymes, creating a unique effect. 这首诗充满了近似韵和非韵，创造了一种独特的效果。|Near rhymes, half rhymes|Perfect rhymes, rhyming words| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + rhyme.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Rhyme" comes from Old French "rime," ultimately from Latin "rhythmus," meaning "rhythm."
