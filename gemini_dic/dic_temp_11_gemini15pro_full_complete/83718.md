hagseed:/ˈhæɡsiːd/| n.|魔女的种子；丑老太婆；讨厌的女人；(尤指)复仇女神；(莎士比亚戏剧《暴风雨》中)普洛斯彼罗的奴隶卡利班对他的称呼|A hagseed is a derogatory term for an old woman, especially one perceived as ugly or unpleasant.  It also refers to Caliban's name for Prospero in Shakespeare's The Tempest. |Felix was a hagseed, a man twisted by nature and nurture into an outcast.|近义词：witch, crone, harpy|反义词: beauty, charmer|noun
