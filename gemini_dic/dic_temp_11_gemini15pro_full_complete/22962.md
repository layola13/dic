bottoming:/ˈbɒtəmɪŋ/ /ˈbɑːtəmɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|触底，探底；达到最低点；底部；底部的|The economy is bottoming out after a long recession. 经济在长期衰退后正在触底反弹。|近义词：reaching the lowest point, hitting rock bottom, declining, slumping|反义词: peaking, rising, improving, recovering|词性:动词现在分词/名词/形容词

bottoming:/ˈbɒtəmɪŋ/ /ˈbɑːtəmɪŋ/| n.|触底，探底；底部|The bottoming of the stock market provided a buying opportunity. 股市触底提供了一个买入机会。|近义词：lowest point, nadir|反义词: peak, apex|词性:名词

bottoming:/ˈbɒtəmɪŋ/ /ˈbɑːtəmɪŋ/| adj.|底部的|The bottoming process can be painful but is often necessary for recovery.  触底过程可能是痛苦的，但通常是复苏的必要条件。|近义词：lowest, basal|反义词: top, highest|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "to bottom," which means "to reach the lowest point."  The verb "to bottom" comes from the noun "bottom," which originates from the Old English "botm."
