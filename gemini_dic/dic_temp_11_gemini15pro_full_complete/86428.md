heedy:/ˈhiːdi/| adj.|兴冲冲的，轻率的，头脑发热的|Characterized by heedless or rash enthusiasm; impetuous.|He made a heedy decision to invest all his savings in the volatile stock market. 他轻率地决定将所有积蓄投资于动荡的股票市场。|近义词：impetuous, rash, reckless, impulsive|反义词：cautious, prudent, careful|词性:形容词

词源：From heed + -y.  Heed itself comes from Old English *hēdan, from Proto-Germanic *hauzijan.
