lathe:/leɪð/| n. vt.|车床; 用车床加工|1. n. A machine for shaping wood, metal, or other material by means of a rotating drive that turns the piece being worked on against changeable cutting tools. |The carpenter used a lathe to shape the legs of the table. 木匠用车床来加工桌腿。|2. vt. To shape something on a lathe.|He lathed the wood into a beautiful vase. 他用车床把木头加工成一个漂亮的花瓶。|Synonyms: turning machine, machine tool (n.); turn, shape (vt.)|Antonyms: None|noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *læððe, from Proto-Germanic *laþþōn. Related to Old Norse laþ. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lādh-.
