abreast:/əˈbrɛst/| adj.|并肩的，并排的；了解的，跟上(形势)的|Side by side; up to date with the latest information or developments.|例句：The two runners were abreast as they crossed the finish line. 两名跑步者并肩冲过了终点线。|例句：It's important to stay abreast of current events. 跟上时事是很重要的。|近义词：alongside, level with, informed about, up to date with|反义词：behind, uninformed|词性:形容词

abreast:/əˈbrɛst/| adv.|并肩地，并排地|Side by side; alongside.|例句：They walked abreast along the beach. 他们沿着海滩并肩行走。|例句：The cars drove abreast down the highway. 汽车在高速公路上并排行驶。|近义词：alongside, side by side|反义词：behind|词性:副词
