affectability:/əˌfɛktəˈbɪləti/| n. |易受影响性；感情脆弱|The quality of being easily affected; susceptibility to emotional influence.|Her affectability made her prone to tears at even the slightest provocation. 她容易受影响的性格使她即使受到最轻微的刺激也会流泪。|近义词：sensitivity, impressionability, vulnerability|反义词：imperturbability, insensitivity, resilience|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *affectabilitas*, from Latin affectābilis (“liable to be affected”), from affectō (“affect”) + -bilis.
