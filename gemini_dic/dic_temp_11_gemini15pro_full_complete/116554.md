matiest:/ˈmeɪtiɪst/
adjective
最亲密的，最要好的
closest and dearest; most intimate.
例句：She's my matiest friend. 她是我最亲密的朋友。
例句：They were the matiest of couples. 他们是关系最亲密的一对。
近义词：closest, dearest, best, bosom, intimate
反义词：distant, estranged

ETYMOLOGY->
From mate + -iest (superlative suffix).  "Mate" comes from Middle Low German *māte, from Old Saxon *gimako "companion."
