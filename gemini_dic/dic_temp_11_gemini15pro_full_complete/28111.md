/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. int. adv.| 什么，多么 |Used to ask for information specifying something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|Which, how | N/A | pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb
What color is the sky? 天空是什么颜色的？


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you came here? 你来这里之前吃了什么？
What had she done before she left? 她走之前做了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she study in college? 她大学里要学什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference? 她将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the time you're 30? 到你30岁时你会取得什么成就？
What will she have written by the end of the year? 到年底她会写完什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she were here? 如果她在这里，她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
