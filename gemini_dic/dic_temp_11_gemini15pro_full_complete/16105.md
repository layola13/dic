bandy:/ˈbændi/| adj. v. |弯曲的，弓形的；(随便地)谈论，传播|Having a slight outward curve; to pass back and forth, especially carelessly or irresponsibly.|The hockey players bandied the puck across the ice. 曲棍球运动员在冰上你来我往地击球。|近义词：curved, bowed, exchange, toss, circulate |反义词:straight, withhold|adjective, verb
bandy:/ˈbændi/| v. |(随便地)谈论，传播|To discuss or mention repeatedly, often in a casual or indiscreet way.|His name was bandied about as a possible replacement for the coach. 他的名字被人们随便地提起，说他可能是教练的接替人选。|近义词：circulate, spread, toss around, discuss, mention|反义词:conceal, suppress|verb

Etymology:  From Old French *bander "to curve, bend," from Frankish *bandjan "to proclaim, summon," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bandijaną "to give a sign." The semantic shift from "proclaim" to "toss about" likely occurred through the image of a herald passing messages back and forth.
