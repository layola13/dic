factor:/ˈfæktər/ (US), /ˈfæktə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|因素，要素；因子；系数；代理人|Something that contributes to a result.|例句：Cost is an important factor to consider.  成本是一个需要考虑的重要因素。|例句：Her health was a factor in her decision to retire. 她的健康是她决定退休的一个因素。|近义词：element, component, ingredient, aspect, agent|反义词: N/A |noun
factor:/ˈfæktər/ (US), /ˈfæktə(r)/ (UK)| v.|把…分解成因子；将…作为因子考虑|To include something as a factor; to break down into factors|例句：They factored the cost of materials into the price. 他们把材料成本计入了价格。|例句：The equation can be factored. 这个方程式可以被分解成因子。|近义词：include, consider, analyze, break down|反义词: N/A |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *factowr*, from Anglo-Norman *factour, facteur*,  and Old French *facteur*, all from Latin *factor* (“doer, maker”), from *facere* (“to do, to make”).
