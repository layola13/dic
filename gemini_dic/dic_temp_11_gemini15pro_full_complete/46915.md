darknet:/ˈdɑːrknet/ | n. | 暗网 | An overlay network within the Internet that can only be accessed with specific software, configurations, or authorization, often used for anonymous communication and illegal activities. |  Law enforcement agencies are trying to crack down on illegal activities on the darknet. 执法机构正试图打击暗网上的非法活动。

The darknet is often associated with cybercrime and drug trafficking. 暗网通常与网络犯罪和毒品贩运有关。 | 近义词: dark web, deep web | 反义词: surface web, clearnet | 词性: 名词
