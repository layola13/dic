disavow:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊ/| vt.|否认，否定，拒绝承认|To deny responsibility for or connection with.|The government disavowed any knowledge of the secret talks. 政府否认对秘密会谈有任何了解。|近义词：deny, repudiate, renounce|反义词：acknowledge, admit, confess|词性:动词
词源:Late 16th century: from dis- + avow.
