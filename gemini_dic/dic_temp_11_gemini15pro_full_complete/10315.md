appealabilities:/əˌpiːləˈbɪlətiz/
n.
可上诉性；上诉权
The quality or state of being appealable.  The extent to which a judicial decision may be appealed.
The appealabilities of the lower court's decision are still being debated. 下级法院判决的可上诉性仍在辩论中。
近义词:appealability (singular form), right of appeal
反义词:finality, conclusiveness
词性:noun (plural)
