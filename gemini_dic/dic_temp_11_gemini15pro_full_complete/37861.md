compile:/kəmˈpaɪl/ (US), /kəmˈpaɪl/ (UK)| v. |编译，汇编，编辑|To gather together information or material from various sources, especially for a book, report, or list; to convert (a computer program) into a machine-code or lower-level form in which the program can be executed.|The researchers compiled data from hundreds of studies. 研究人员从数百项研究中汇编了数据。|synonyms: assemble, collect, gather, amass|antonyms: disperse, scatter|verb
