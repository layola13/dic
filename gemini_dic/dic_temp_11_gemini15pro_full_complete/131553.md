noncommodiously: /ˌnɒnkəˈməʊdiəsli/ | adv. | 不方便地，不舒适地 | In a way that is not spacious or convenient; uncomfortably. |  The furniture was arranged noncommodiously, making it difficult to move around. 家具摆放得很不方便，让人难以走动。| Synonyms: inconveniently, awkwardly, uncomfortably, crampedly | Antonyms: commodiously, comfortably, spaciously, conveniently | adverb

noncommodious: /ˌnɒnkəˈməʊdiəs/ | adj. | 不宽敞的，不舒适的 | Not spacious or convenient; uncomfortable. | The noncommodious apartment felt cramped with all our furniture. 这套不宽敞的公寓放满我们的家具后感觉很拥挤。 | Synonyms: cramped, inconvenient, uncomfortable, restricted | Antonyms: spacious, comfortable, convenient, roomy | adjective


Etymology: 
From Late Latin *noncommodiosus, from Latin non (“not”) + commodus (“convenient, suitable”).
