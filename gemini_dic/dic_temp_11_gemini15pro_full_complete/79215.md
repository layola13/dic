glandulation:/ˌɡlændʒʊˈleɪʃən/
n.
腺状隆起，腺状结构
The process of forming glands or becoming gland-like; a gland-like structure or formation.
The biologist noted the glandulation on the surface of the leaf. 生物学家注意到叶子表面的腺状隆起。
近义词：glandularization,  glandular structure
反义词： (No direct antonym, perhaps "smoothness" in a specific context)
