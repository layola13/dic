haloes:/ˈheɪloʊz/ (US) /ˈheɪləʊz/ (UK)| n.|光环，晕，神像之光圈|A circle of light appearing to surround the sun or moon, caused by refraction through ice crystals in the atmosphere.  Also, a symbolic representation of holiness or divinity, often depicted as a circle of light around the head of a saint or angel.|例句：The sun appeared with a bright halo around it. 太阳周围出现了一个明亮的光环。|例句：The artist painted a halo above the angel's head. 艺术家在天使的头上画了一个光环。|近义词：aura, nimbus, ring, corona|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "darkness" in a figurative sense) |词性:noun
