liaison:/liˈeɪzɒn(UK), liˈeɪzɑːn(US)| n.|联络，联系；联络人；私通|A close working relationship between people or organizations.|The police have appointed a liaison officer to work with the local community. 警察指派了一名联络官与当地社区合作。|synonyms: contact, connection, communication, intermediary, affair|antonyms: separation, disconnection, division|noun

liaison:/liˈeɪzɒn(UK), liˈeɪzɑːn(US)| n.|（语言学）连音|The pronunciation of an otherwise absent consonant sound at the end of the first word in front of a word beginning with a vowel.|The liaison between the words "an" and "apple" creates the sound "napple".  "an" 和 "apple" 两词之间的连音创造了 "napple" 的发音。|synonyms: linking, juncture|antonyms: elision|noun
