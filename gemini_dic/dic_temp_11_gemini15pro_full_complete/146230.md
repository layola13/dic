overexpose:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊz/ | v. | 过度曝光；过分暴露；使…过度曝光 | To expose to light, especially sunlight or other radiation, for too long or too intensely, resulting in damage or alteration.  | The photographer overexposed the film, resulting in a washed-out image. 摄影师过度曝光了胶片，导致图像褪色。

He overexposed his children to the media. 他让他的孩子们过度曝光于媒体。 | 近义词：bleach, solarize | 反义词：underexpose | 词性: 动词
