fluorite:/ˈflʊəˌraɪt/ | n. | 萤石 | A common mineral, calcium fluoride (CaF2), occurring in cubic crystals of various colors and used as a flux and in making glass and hydrofluoric acid. | Fluorite is often used in the manufacture of optical lenses.  萤石常用于制造光学透镜。| Synonyms: fluorspar | Antonyms: None | noun
