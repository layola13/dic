epimeral:/ˌɛpɪˈmɛrəl/ | adj. | 差向异构的 | Relating to epimers; differing only in the configuration around a single chiral center. |  Glucose and galactose are epimeral at C4. 葡萄糖和半乳糖在C4位是差向异构体。 | isomeric, diastereomeric | enantiomeric | adjective

epimer:/ˈɛpɪmər/ | n. | 差向异构体 | One of two stereoisomers that differ in configuration at any single asymmetric carbon atom (not an anomeric carbon). | Glucose and mannose are epimers. 葡萄糖和甘露糖是差向异构体。| isomer, diastereomer | enantiomer | noun
