bodied:/ˈbɒdid/ | adj. | 有…体格的；有…身形的；（酒）浓郁的 | Having a body of a specified type; (of a wine) having a full rich flavor. |例句：She was a full-bodied woman. 她是一位丰满的女人。

例句：This is a full-bodied red wine. 这是一款浓郁的红葡萄酒。 |近义词：substantial, full, fleshy (for wine), well-built, stout |反义词：thin, slight, light (for wine) | 词性:形容词

bodied:/ˈbɒdid/ | adj. |体现…的；表达…的 | Expressing or giving a physical form to something abstract. | 例句：His paintings are beautifully bodied representations of nature. 他的绘画优美地体现了自然。

例句：Her emotions were bodied in her dance. 她的舞蹈表达了她的情感。 | 近义词：embodied, expressed, manifested | 反义词：unexpressed, intangible | 词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bodigod, past participle of *bodigian "to provide with a body," from bodig "body."  The meaning shifted from "having a body provided" to "having a body of a certain type" over time.
