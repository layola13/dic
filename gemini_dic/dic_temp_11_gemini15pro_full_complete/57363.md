doxies:/ˈdɒksiz/| n.|腊肠犬（复数）,  告密者（复数）|Dachshunds, especially referring to them informally and often affectionately. Can also refer to informers or people who provide incriminating information, especially on a regular basis. |例句：The doxies were having a great time playing in the park. 腊肠犬们在公园里玩得很开心。

例句：The reporter relied on a network of doxies to uncover the scandal. 记者依靠一个告密者网络来揭露这起丑闻。|近义词：(for dachshunds) sausage dogs, wiener dogs; (for informers) informants, snitches|反义词: (no direct antonym)|名词
