fluorescein:/ˌflʊərəˈsiːɪn/ | n. | 荧光素 |A bright yellow, crystalline, water-soluble dye that becomes intensely fluorescent in alkaline solution. It is used in ophthalmology and as a tracer in various biological and chemical studies. | Doctors use fluorescein to detect corneal abrasions. 医生用荧光素来检测角膜擦伤。| The researchers injected fluorescein to track the flow of blood. 研究人员注射荧光素来追踪血液流动。| synonyms: dye, indicator | antonyms: N/A | noun
