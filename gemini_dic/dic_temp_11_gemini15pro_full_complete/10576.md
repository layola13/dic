apprehending:/ˌæprɪˈhɛndɪŋ/ | v. | 逮捕；理解；担心 |  The police are apprehending the suspect. 警察正在逮捕嫌疑犯。 | Example: The officers were apprehending the thief who stole the jewels.  警官们正在逮捕偷珠宝的小偷。 | Synonyms: arresting, capturing, seizing, grasping, understanding, fearing | Antonyms: releasing, freeing, misunderstanding, ignoring,  | verb

apprehending:/ˌæprɪˈhɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 理解的；担忧的 |  She had an apprehending look on her face. 她脸上带着担忧的表情。 | Example:  I felt apprehending about the upcoming exam. 我对即将到来的考试感到担忧。 | Synonyms: understanding, perceptive, worried, anxious | Antonyms: uncomprehending, oblivious, calm, relaxed | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *apprehendere, from Latin apprehendere  "to seize, grasp, lay hold of," from ad- "to" + prehendere "to grasp, seize."
