nonheuristic:/ˌnɒn.hjʊəˈrɪstɪk/ /ˌnɑːn.hʊrˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非启发式的 | Not using or involving heuristics; not based on trial and error or rules of thumb.  Relating to a problem-solving approach that does not rely on intuition or educated guesses but instead follows a defined procedure or algorithm. | This nonheuristic algorithm guarantees the optimal solution, even though it takes longer to compute.  这个非启发式算法保证了最优解，即使计算时间更长。

The company implemented a nonheuristic system for quality control to eliminate human error.  该公司实施了一个非启发式质量控制系统，以消除人为错误。 | Near Synonyms: algorithmic, systematic, procedural, methodical | Antonyms: heuristic, intuitive, trial-and-error | word type: adjective
