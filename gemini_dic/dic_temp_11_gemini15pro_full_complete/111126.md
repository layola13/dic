literately:/ˈlɪtərətli/| adv.|照字面地，逐字地，不夸张地|In a literal manner or sense; exactly.  She translated the passage literally. 她逐字逐句地翻译了这段文字。|literally, word for word|figuratively, metaphorically|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *litteraliter, from Latin litteralis (“of a letter”), from littera (“letter”).
