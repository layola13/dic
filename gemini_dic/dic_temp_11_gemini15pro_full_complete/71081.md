fiscally:/ˈfɪskəli/
adjective
财政上地
Relating to government revenue, especially taxes.
The government is fiscally responsible for funding education. 政府在财政上负责资助教育。
The new policy was fiscally sound. 新政策在财政上是合理的。
Synonyms: financially, economically
Antonyms: extravagantly, wastefully
词性: 形容词
