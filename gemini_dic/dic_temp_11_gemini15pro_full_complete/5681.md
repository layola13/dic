alude:/əˈlud/| v. |暗示，提及|To refer to something indirectly or casually; to hint at.|The speaker alluded to recent events without mentioning them specifically. 演讲者间接提到了最近的事件，但没有具体说明。|近义词：suggest, imply, hint, intimate, refer|反义词：state, declare, specify, explain|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alludere*, from *ad* ("to, toward") + *ludere* ("to play").  Originally meant "to play with," then "to mock" or "to jest," and finally settled on the meaning "to hint at."
