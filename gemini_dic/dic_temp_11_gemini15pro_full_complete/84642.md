hand-setting:/ˈhændˌsɛtɪŋ/
n.
手工排版；手工植入
The process of setting type by hand, placing individual characters into a composing stick.
The manual insertion of something, such as plants or gemstones.
The book was printed using traditional hand-setting techniques. 这本书是用传统的手工排版技术印刷的。
Diamonds are hand-set into the watch face. 钻石被手工镶嵌在表盘上。
typesetting, manual composition
automated typesetting, machine setting
noun
