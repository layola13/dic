dartingly:/ˈdɑːrtɪŋli/| adv. |迅捷地，飞快地|In a quick, sudden manner, like a dart.  Describes an action done quickly and suddenly, as if thrown like a dart.|She looked at him dartingly. 她飞快地看了他一眼。|The lizard's tongue flicked out dartingly to catch a fly. 蜥蜴的舌头飞快地弹出来抓住了苍蝇。|近义词：swiftly, rapidly, quickly, suddenly|反义词：slowly, gradually|词性:副词
