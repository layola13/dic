depurgatory:/ˈdɛpərɡətɔːri/ /ˈdɛpərɡətəri/
noun
炼狱；净化的地方
A place or state of purification or purgation, especially from sin; a place where souls after death are purged of their sins before entering heaven.
The novel depicts purgatory as a desolate wasteland. 这部小说将炼狱描绘成一片荒凉的荒地。
Synonyms: limbo, purification, cleansing
Antonyms: heaven, paradise
