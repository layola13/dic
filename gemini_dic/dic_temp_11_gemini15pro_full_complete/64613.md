eruptivities:/ɪˌrʌpˈtɪvɪtiz/ | n. |爆发性，喷发性|The eruptivities of different volcanoes vary greatly. 不同火山的爆发性差异很大。|例句：The study of eruptivities helps volcanologists predict future eruptions.  对火山爆发性的研究有助于火山学家预测未来的喷发。

例句：The eruptivities of the volcano have increased in recent years.  近年来，这座火山的爆发性有所增强。|近义词：explosiveness, volatility|反义词：dormancy, quiescence|词性: 名词

词源: From "eruptive" + "-ity" (suffix forming nouns of quality) + "-ies" (plural suffix). Ultimately from Latin "eruptio" (a breaking out).
