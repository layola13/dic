nook:/nʊk/| n. |角落，隐蔽处|A corner or recess, especially one offering seclusion or security. |例句：She found a quiet nook in the library to read. 她找到了图书馆一个安静的角落读书。

例句：The cat curled up in a sunny nook by the window.  猫蜷缩在窗边阳光照射的角落里。|近义词：corner, recess, cranny, alcove|反义词:exposure, openness|词性:noun
