liqueurs:/lɪˈkɜːrz/ (US), /lɪˈkjʊəz/ (UK)
n.
利口酒，甜酒
Sweet alcoholic drinks, often flavored with fruits, herbs, or spices.
例句：After dinner, they enjoyed a selection of fine liqueurs.  晚餐后，他们享用了一系列优质的利口酒。

例句：He offered her a glass of coffee liqueur. 他给她倒了一杯咖啡利口酒。

近义词：cordials, aperitifs, digestifs
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-alcoholic beverages)
词性: noun
