intermeningeal:/ˌɪntərˌmɪˈnɪndʒiəl/
adjective
脑膜间的；脑膜之间的
Located or occurring between the meninges (the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord).
An intermeningeal hemorrhage can be life-threatening. 脑膜间出血可能会危及生命。
