antispreader:/ˈæntiˌsprɛdər/ | n. | 防扩散装置；防扩散措施；防扩散者 |  A device or measure intended to prevent the spread of something, often referring to diseases or misinformation. |  The restaurant installed antispreaders on each table to mitigate the risk of COVID-19 transmission.  这家餐厅在每张桌子上都安装了防扩散装置，以降低新冠病毒传播的风险。 | synonyms: barrier, divider, preventative measure | antonyms: spreader, accelerator | noun


antispreader:/ˈæntiˌsprɛdər/ | n. | 散布虚假信息/病毒等的人  | A person who actively works against the spread of something, especially misinformation or harmful content.  | The antispreaders worked tirelessly to debunk the conspiracy theories circulating online. 这些反虚假信息传播者不知疲倦地揭穿网上流传的阴谋论。 | synonyms: debunker, fact-checker | antonyms:  propagandist, disinformation agent | noun

词源: "anti-" (against) + "spreader" (one who or that which spreads).  "Anti-" comes from Greek "anti" (against, opposite). "Spreader" comes from the verb "to spread," which has Germanic origins.
