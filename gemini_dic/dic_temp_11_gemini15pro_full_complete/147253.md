overmerit: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪt/
verb
过分奖励；奖励过高
To reward in excess of what is deserved.
He felt he had overmerited his employees for their minimal contributions. 他觉得他过分奖励了他的员工，因为他们的贡献微不足道。
The judge felt the criminal was overmerited by the light sentence. 法官认为对罪犯的轻判是一种过分的奖励。
近义词: overreward, overcompensate
反义词: underreward, undercompensate
词性: verb
