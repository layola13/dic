cogged:/kɑːɡd/ | adj. | 齿状的，有齿的 | Having cogs or teeth, like a gear.  Specifically, referring to a wheel with teeth, especially one designed to mesh with another cogged wheel. | The cogged wheels turned smoothly, transferring power from the engine. 齿轮平稳地转动，将动力从发动机传递出去。 | cog-wheeled, toothed, geared | smooth, toothless | adjective

cogged:/kɑːɡd/ | v. (past participle of cog) | （骰子）做了手脚的，灌铅的 |  (past participle of the verb "cog") To load dice or otherwise cheat at a game of chance.  Also, more generally, to manipulate or rig something unfairly. | The dice were cogged to ensure he won. 骰子被做了手脚以确保他赢。| loaded, rigged, fixed, manipulated | fair, honest | verb
