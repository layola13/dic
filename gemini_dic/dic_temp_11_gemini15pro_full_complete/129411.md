nominals:/ˈnɑːmɪnəlz/| n.|名词性词; 名词;|Words or phrases that function grammatically as nouns.  A nominal can be a single word (like "cat" or "happiness") or a group of words acting as a noun (like "the big red ball").|The nominals in the sentence "The fluffy cat sat on the soft mat" are "the fluffy cat" and "the soft mat." 这句话中的名词性词是“毛茸茸的猫”和“柔软的垫子”。|Synonyms: noun phrases, substantives|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a grammatical function)|n.
