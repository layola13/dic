bards:/bɑːrdz/| n.|吟游诗人|A poet, traditionally one reciting epics and associated with a particular oral tradition.|The bards of old sang tales of bravery and love.|近义词：poet, minstrel, singer, songwriter|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Irish *bardos, from Gaulish *bardos (“poet, singer”), from Proto-Celtic *bardos. Cognate with Welsh bard, Breton barz.
