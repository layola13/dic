crownet:/ˈkraʊnɪt/ | n. | 小冠冕，小花冠 |A small crown or garland worn on the head. | The princess wore a delicate crownet of flowers.  公主戴着精致的花冠。| synonyms: coronet, chaplet, wreath | antonyms: None. | Noun

crownet:/ˈkraʊnɪt/ | v. | 为…加冕，戴上小冠 | To crown or adorn with a small crown. |  They crownetted her the queen of the May festival. 他们为她加冕，让她成为五月节女王。 | synonyms: crown, adorn, wreathe | antonyms:  dethrone, uncrown | Verb
