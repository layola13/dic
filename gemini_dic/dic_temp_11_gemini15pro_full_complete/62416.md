endolymphangial:/ˌɛndoʊlɪmˈfændʒiəl/
adjective
内淋巴的
Relating to the endolymph and lymphatic vessels.  Pertaining to or located within both the endolymphatic system and lymphatic vessels.
Endolymphangial hydrops is a condition affecting the inner ear.  内淋巴积水是一种影响内耳的疾病。
The endolymphangial sac plays a crucial role in maintaining inner ear balance. 内淋巴囊在维持内耳平衡方面起着至关重要的作用。
近义词:  intra-labyrinthine (though not a perfect synonym, it relates to the inner ear where endolymph is found)
反义词:  N/A (There isn't a direct antonym for this anatomical term)
词性: adjective


词源:
endo- (Greek: within) + lymph (Latin: lympha, clear water) + -angial (relating to vessels)
