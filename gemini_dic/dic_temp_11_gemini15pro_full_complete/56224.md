doers:/ˈduːərz/| n.|实干家，行动者|People who take action rather than just talking about it.  那些付诸行动而不是只说不做的人。|例句：The doers in the company are the ones who get promoted. 公司里那些实干家是会得到晋升的。

例句：We need more doers and fewer talkers. 我们需要更多实干家，更少空谈家。|近义词：actors, performers, achievers, executors|反义词：talkers, procrastinators, idlers|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "do" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting agents). 源自动词 "do" + "-er" （构成表示行为者的名词的后缀）。
