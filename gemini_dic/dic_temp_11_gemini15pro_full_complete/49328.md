delilah:/dəˈlaɪlə/| n. |黛利拉|A treacherous woman who seduces and betrays her lover.  A woman who uses her charm to weaken a man's resolve. From the biblical figure who betrayed Samson.|例句：He called her his Delilah, suggesting she was trying to manipulate him. 他称她为他的黛利拉，暗示她试图操纵他。

例句：Like Delilah, she used her beauty to gain his trust, then betrayed him. 像黛利拉一样，她利用自己的美貌赢得了他的信任，然后背叛了他。|近义词：temptress, siren, seductress|反义词: loyalist, faithful |词性:noun
