ineffablenesses:/ɪnˈɛfəbəlˌnɛsɪz/
n.
不可名状；难以言喻
The quality of being too great, extreme, or sacred to be expressed or described in words.
The ineffablenesses of religious experience. 宗教体验的不可名状。
Synonyms: inexpressibility, indescribability, unutterability
Antonyms: expressibility, describability, utterability
noun
