adverts:/ˈædvɜːts/ /ˈædvɜːrts/| n.|广告|Short form of advertisements, meaning promotional messages designed to encourage people to buy products, services or ideas. |The company ran a series of adverts on television. 这家公司在电视上做了一系列广告。|Synonyms: commercials, promotions, publicity|Antonyms:  None (lack of advertising could be considered an antonym in a specific context)|Noun

adverts:/ədˈvɜːts/ /ədˈvɜːrz/| v.|做广告,宣传|(Third-person singular simple present indicative form of advert, meaning to promote something through advertising.)|He adverts his business on social media. 他在社交媒体上宣传他的生意。|Synonyms: promotes, publicizes, markets|Antonyms: conceals, hides, suppresses|Verb
