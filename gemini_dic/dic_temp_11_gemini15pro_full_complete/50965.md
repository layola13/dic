desilt:/diːˈsɪlt/
verb
去除淤泥;清除泥沙
To remove silt or mud from something, such as a river, harbor, or tank.
The harbor needs to be desilted regularly to maintain its depth.  港口需要定期清淤以保持其深度。
Dredging is a common method for desilting rivers. 疏浚是河流清淤的常用方法。
近义词: dredge, clear, clean
反义词: silt up, fill
词性: verb
