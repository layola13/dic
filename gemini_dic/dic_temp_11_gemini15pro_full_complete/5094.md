allayers:/əˈleɪərz/| n.|缓解者，减轻者，安慰者|Something that allays or relieves pain, fear, or worry.|The soft music was an allayer of their anxieties.  轻柔的音乐缓解了他们的焦虑。|近义词：soothers, relievers, mitigators, assuagers|反义词:aggravators, intensifiers|noun

allay:/əˈleɪ/| vt.|减轻，缓和，平息|To diminish or put at rest (fear, suspicion, or worry).|The government is trying to allay public fears about the spread of the virus. 政府正试图减轻公众对病毒传播的恐惧。|近义词：relieve, ease, soothe, mitigate, alleviate|反义词:aggravate, intensify, exacerbate|verb
