cumbersome:/ˈkʌmbərsəm/| adj.|笨重的，累赘的，难处理的|Large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; unwieldy.|The old furniture was cumbersome and difficult to move.  旧家具笨重，难以搬动。|Synonym: awkward, unwieldy, clumsy, bulky, burdensome|Antonym: convenient, manageable, easy, handy|adjective
