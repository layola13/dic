customariness:/ˈkʌstəmərɪnəs/
n.
习惯性；习俗性
The state of being customary or usual; prevalence.
The customariness of the morning greeting made it feel automatic.  早晨问候的习惯性让它感觉很自动化。
近义词：conventionality, normalcy, regularity, usualness
反义词：uncommonness, rarity, unusualness, novelty
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From customary + -ness.  Customary comes from Old French *custumier, from custume "custom," from Latin consuetudo "custom, habit, usage, intimacy, acquaintance," from consuescere "accustom," from con- "together" + suescere "become accustomed," related to suus "one's own."
