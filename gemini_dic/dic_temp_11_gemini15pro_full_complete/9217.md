antioxygenators:/ˌæntiˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtəz/ | n. |抗氧化剂|Substances that inhibit oxidation, often used to prevent the deterioration of materials or to slow down chemical reactions involving oxygen.  They are sometimes referred to as antioxidants, although this term is more commonly used in a biological context.| The food manufacturer added antioxygenators to preserve the freshness of the product. 食品制造商添加了抗氧化剂以保持产品的新鲜度。|Antioxidants, preservatives|Oxidants|noun
