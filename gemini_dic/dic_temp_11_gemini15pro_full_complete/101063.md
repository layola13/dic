inventibleness:/ɪnˈvɛntɪbəlnəs/
n.
inventiveness; ingenuity
The quality of being inventive or ingenious.
Her inventibleness allowed her to create a successful business from a simple idea.  她的创造力使她能够从一个简单的想法中创造出一个成功的企业。
inventiveness, ingenuity, creativity, resourcefulness, imagination
lack of imagination, dullness, unoriginality
