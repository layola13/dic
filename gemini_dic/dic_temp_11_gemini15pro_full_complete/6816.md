anarchize:/ˈænərˌkaɪz/ | v. | 使无政府状态化；煽动叛乱 | To cause anarchy; to overthrow established government. |  The rebels sought to anarchize the country and establish a new order. 叛乱分子试图使国家陷入无政府状态并建立新秩序。

The propaganda was designed to anarchize the population and incite rebellion.  这种宣传旨在使民众无政府状态化并煽动叛乱。 | 近义词: destabilize, overthrow, subvert, undermine | 反义词: stabilize, strengthen, support, uphold | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anarchiser*, from *anarchie* (anarchy) + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs from nouns). Ultimately from Greek *anarkhia* (ἀναρχία, "lack of a ruler," "anarchy"), from *an-* (ἀν-, "without," "not") + *arkhos* (ἄρχος, "ruler," "leader").
