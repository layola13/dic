foothalt:/ˈfʊthɔːlt/| n.|立足点；休息处；小驿站|A place where one can pause or rest, especially while walking or climbing.|We made a foothalt at a small village before continuing our journey. 我们在继续旅程之前在一个小村庄稍作停留。|Synonyms: rest stop, stopping place, way station|Antonyms: destination|noun


Etymology->
From "foot" (referring to walking) + "halt" (a stop or pause).
