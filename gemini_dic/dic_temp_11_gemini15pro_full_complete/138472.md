novella:/nəˈvɛlə/ /nɑːˈvɛlə/| n.|中篇小说；中篇故事|A short novel, typically between 17,500 and 40,000 words.|Example:  She wrote a novella about a young woman's journey of self-discovery. 她写了一部关于年轻女性自我发现旅程的中篇小说。

Example: His latest work is a novella set in a dystopian future. 他的最新作品是一部以反乌托邦未来为背景的中篇小说。


|近义词：short novel, novelette|反义词: novel|词性: noun
