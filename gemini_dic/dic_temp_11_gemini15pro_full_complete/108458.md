layaway: /ˈleɪəˌweɪ/ (US) /ˈleɪəweɪ/ (UK)| n.| 分期付款;预订商品服务|A system of paying for goods in installments, where the goods are kept by the store until the final payment is made. |I put the furniture on layaway and will pick it up when I've paid it off. 我把家具分期付款了，付清后再取。| I'm buying a new TV on layaway. 我正在分期付款买一台新电视。| 近义词: installment plan, payment plan| 反义词: outright purchase| 词性: 名词

layaway: /ˈleɪəˌweɪ/ (US) /ˈleɪəweɪ/ (UK)| vt.| 分期付款购买;预订|To purchase goods on a layaway plan. |She layawayed a new coat for the winter. 她分期付款买了一件新冬衣。|They layawayed a trip to Europe for next summer. 他们预订了明年夏天去欧洲的旅行。| 近义词: put on layaway, buy on installment| 反义词: pay in full| 词性: 动词
