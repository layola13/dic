leakage:/ˈliːkɪdʒ/| n.|泄漏，渗漏|The leakage of the confidential document caused a scandal. 机密文件的泄露引发了一场丑闻。|The pipe had a slow leakage that went unnoticed for weeks. 这根管道有缓慢的泄漏，几个星期都没被注意到。|近义词：escape, leak, spillage, seepage|反义词: containment|词性:名词

词源: From leak + -age.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lekaną (“to drip, leak”).
