deep-sixed:/ˌdiːpˈsɪkst/
verb
彻底放弃；丢弃；销毁；拒绝
to get rid of something completely; to dispose of or reject something.
The project was deep-sixed after the company lost funding.  这个项目在公司失去资金后被彻底放弃了。
He deep-sixed the idea of buying a new car. 他放弃了买新车的想法。
近义词:discard, abandon, reject, scrap, ditch, kill
反义词:approve, accept, embrace, implement
词性:verb
