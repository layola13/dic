charism: /ˈkærɪzəm/ /ˈkærɪsm/ | n. | 魅力，感召力，天赋 | A special power that some people have naturally that makes them able to influence other people and attract their attention and admiration. |  例句：The candidate's charisma helped him win the election. 候选人的魅力帮助他赢得了选举。

例句：She has a natural charisma that draws people to her. 她有一种天生的魅力，吸引着人们靠近她。

近义词：charm, allure, magnetism, appeal | 反义词：repulsion | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin charisma, from Greek kharisma "favor, divine gift," from kharizesthai "to show favor, grant a favor," from kharis "grace, favor, kindness," of unknown origin.
