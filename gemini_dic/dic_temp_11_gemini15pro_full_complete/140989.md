onement:/əˈtoʊnment/ /əˈtʌnmənt/| n. |赎罪，弥补，一致，和谐|An act of making amends or reparation for a wrong or injury.  The state of being at one with someone or something else, in harmony.|例句：He sought atonement for his sins. 他寻求罪孽的救赎。

例句：The onement between the two families brought peace to the community. 两个家族的和解给社区带来了和平。|近义词：reconciliation, reparation, expiation, redemption, harmony, unity|反义词：discord, disharmony, conflict|词性:名词

词源:  From Old English *ātonung, from atone + -ment.  Ultimately from at + one.
