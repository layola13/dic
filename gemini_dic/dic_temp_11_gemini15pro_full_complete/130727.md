nonalluvial:/ˌnɒnəˈluːviəl/
adjective
非冲积的
Not consisting of or formed from alluvium (sediment deposited by flowing water).
The nonalluvial soils of the region are less fertile.  该地区的非冲积土壤肥力较低。
