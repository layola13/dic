atonalist:/ˈeɪˈtəʊnəlɪst/ /əˈtoʊnəlɪst/
n.|无调性主义者；无调性音乐作曲家|A composer who writes atonal music, music that avoids traditional harmonies and key signatures.|Schoenberg was a leading atonalist of the 20th century.  勋伯格是20世纪主要的无调性音乐作曲家。|Synonyms: twelve-tone composer, serialist|Antonyms: tonal composer, traditionalist|Noun

atonal:/eɪˈtəʊnl/ /əˈtoʊnl/
adj.|无调性的|Lacking a tonal center or key; characterized by the avoidance of traditional harmony and key signatures.|The atonal music was challenging for some listeners.  无调性音乐对一些听众来说具有挑战性。|Synonyms: nontonal, twelve-tone, serial|Antonyms: tonal, harmonic|Adjective
