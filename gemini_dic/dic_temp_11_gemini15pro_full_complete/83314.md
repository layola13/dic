gynoecium:/ɡaɪˈniːsiəm/
n.
雌蕊群
The female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of one or more pistils.
The gynoecium of this flower has three carpels.  这朵花的雌蕊群有三个心皮。
pistil, carpel
stamen
