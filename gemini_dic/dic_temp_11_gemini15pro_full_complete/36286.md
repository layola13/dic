coheirs:/ˌkoʊˈɛrz/| n. |共同继承人|One of two or more persons who inherit the same property jointly or concurrently. |The two brothers were coheirs to their father's fortune.|Synonyms: joint heirs, fellow heirs|Antonyms: sole heir|Noun
