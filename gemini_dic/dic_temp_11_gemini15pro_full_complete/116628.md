matronymics:/ˌmætrəˈnɪmɪks/| n. |母姓制；取自母亲名字的姓氏|A system of naming in which an individual's surname is derived from the name of their mother, rather than their father.|Many cultures historically practiced matronymics, particularly in societies where women held significant power or lineage was traced through the maternal line.|近义词：metronymics|反义词: patronymics|词性:noun
