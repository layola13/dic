grayscale:/ˈɡreɪskeɪl/| n. adj.|灰度，灰阶|A range of gray shades from white to black, typically used in digital imaging.|The photo was converted to grayscale.|近义词：monochrome, black and white|反义词:color, full-color|词性:名词，形容词

Etymology:
"gray" + "scale". "Gray" comes from Old English "græg". "Scale" comes from Old French "escale" meaning "ladder rung, shell, husk."
