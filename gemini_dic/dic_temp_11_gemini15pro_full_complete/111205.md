lithontriptist:/ˈlɪθəntrɪptɪst/| n. | 碎石专家，碎石术医师 | A medical professional specializing in lithotripsy, a procedure for breaking up kidney stones or other calculi in the body. | The lithontriptist used shock waves to break up the kidney stones. 碎石专家用冲击波击碎了肾结石。| Near synonyms: urologist (though not all urologists perform lithotripsy) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists) | noun

lithotripsy:/ˈlɪθəˌtrɪpsi/| n. | 碎石术 | A medical procedure that uses shock waves or a laser to break up stones in the kidney, bladder, or ureter. | He underwent lithotripsy to remove his kidney stones. 他接受了碎石术来去除肾结石。| Near synonyms:  extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy (ESWL), laser lithotripsy | Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists) | noun
