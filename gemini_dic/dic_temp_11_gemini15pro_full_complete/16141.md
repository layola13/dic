baning: /ˈbeɪnɪŋ/
Verb
禁止；取缔
The act of prohibiting or forbidding something.
The government is baning smoking in public places. 政府正在禁止在公共场所吸烟。
The baning of certain pesticides has helped protect wildlife.  禁止某些杀虫剂的使用有助于保护野生动物。
Synonyms: prohibiting, forbidding, proscribing, barring, outlawing
Antonyms: permitting, allowing, authorizing
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "to ban," which comes from Old English "bannan" meaning "to summon, proclaim, command," later evolving to mean "to prohibit."
