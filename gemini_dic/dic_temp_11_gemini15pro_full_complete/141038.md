outlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/ | n. | 异常值，离群值 |  A data point that differs significantly from other observations.  A person or thing differing from all other members of a particular group or set. |  例句：This data point is an outlier and should be excluded from the analysis.  这个数据点是一个异常值，应该从分析中排除。

例句：She felt like an outlier in the group.  她觉得自己在这个群体中格格不入。| 近义词：anomaly, aberration, exception, rarity | 反义词：norm, standard, inlier| 词性: 名词

A person or thing situated inside or within a certain area or group.  | 例句：The algorithm identified all the onliers in the dataset.  该算法识别了数据集中的所有正常值。
