multiagency:/ˌmʌltiˈeɪdʒənsi/
adjective
多机构的
Involving or coordinated by several agencies.
The police launched a multiagency investigation into the organized crime ring. 警方对这个有组织犯罪团伙展开了多机构调查。
The multiagency task force was successful in apprehending the suspects.  多机构工作组成功地逮捕了嫌疑人。

近义词: interagency, collaborative, joint
反义词: solo, independent, single-agency
词性: adjective
