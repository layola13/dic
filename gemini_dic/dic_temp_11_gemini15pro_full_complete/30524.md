cephalaspis: /ˈsɛfələspɪs/| n. |头甲鱼|An extinct genus of jawless fish from the Silurian and Devonian periods, characterized by a bony head shield.|Example: Cephalaspis fossils are found in Europe and North America.  头甲鱼化石在欧洲和北美都有发现。|Example:  Scientists study Cephalaspis to understand early vertebrate evolution. 科学家研究头甲鱼以了解早期脊椎动物的进化。|近义词:osteostracan (广义上的骨甲鱼类)|反义词: (无，因为是已灭绝物种，没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek kephalē (“head”) + aspis (“shield”).
