bareheadedness:/ˈbeərˌhedɪdnəs/| n.|不戴帽子；光着头|The state of being bareheaded; having no hat or other covering on the head.  Being without a hat is often seen as a sign of disrespect in some cultures.|He braved the cold wind with a surprising bareheadedness. 他光着头顶着刺骨的寒风，真是令人惊讶。|Synonyms: uncovered head, hatlessness|Antonyms: covered head, hatted|noun
