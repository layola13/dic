oversilences:/ˈəʊvəˈsaɪlənsɪz/
|v.|使过度沉默；压制；用沉默掩盖|The government tried to oversilence the dissenting voices. 政府试图压制异议。|例句：His nervousness oversilenced him during the presentation.  他的紧张让他在演讲中过度沉默了。

例句：She oversilenced her true feelings to avoid conflict. 她为了避免冲突而掩盖了自己的真实感受。

|近义词：suppress, stifle, repress, mute, censor|反义词：express, voice, pronounce, articulate|词性：动词
