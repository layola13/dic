outground:/ˈaʊtɡraʊnd/
n.
外场; 边远地区; 偏远地区
An area of land away from the main buildings or activity of a place.
The school playing fields are on the outground. 学校的操场在外场。

近义词: outskirts, periphery, hinterland
反义词: center, core
