fsd:/ˌɛfɛsˈdiː/| abbreviation | 功能安全设计（Functional Safety Design） |  FSD is a systematic approach to designing systems to ensure they operate safely, even in the presence of faults. |  The FSD of the new car includes features like automatic emergency braking. 这款新车的功能安全设计包含自动紧急制动等功能。 |  近义词：safety engineering, safety system design | 反义词: N/A | noun
