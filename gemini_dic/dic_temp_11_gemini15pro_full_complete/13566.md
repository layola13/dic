attendants:/əˈtɛndənts/| n.|服务员，侍者，出席者，随从|People who are present at a meeting or event; people who assist or serve others.|例句：The attendants helped the guests find their seats. 服务员帮助客人们找到了座位。

例句：Many attendants at the conference were experts in their fields.  许多与会者都是各自领域的专家。|近义词：servers, helpers, assistants, participants, attendees|反义词：hosts, organizers, absentees|词性:Noun

attendant:/əˈtɛndənt/| adj.|伴随的，随之而来的|Accompanying or following as a consequence.|例句：There were several attendant problems with the new software. 新软件带来了一些随之而来的问题。

例句：The queen had her attendant ladies-in-waiting. 女王有她的侍女。|近义词：accompanying, related, associated, consequent|反义词：unrelated, separate, independent|词性:Adjective
