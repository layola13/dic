avolation:/ˌeɪvoʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|逃避；躲避；不愿飞翔|An unnatural reluctance to fly, especially in birds.|The young eagle seemed to suffer from avolation, refusing to leave the nest even when its siblings had all fledged.|近义词：avoidance, evasion, reluctance, disinclination|反义词:eagerness, willingness|noun
