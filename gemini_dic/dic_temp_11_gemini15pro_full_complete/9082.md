antimodernism:/ˌæntiˈmɒdərnɪzəm/ |/ˌæntiˈmɑːdərnɪzəm/| n. |反现代主义|Opposition to modernism, especially in the arts, architecture, literature, and culture. It often involves a preference for traditional forms and values. |The architect's designs were criticized for their blatant antimodernism, harking back to a bygone era.| 近义词: traditionalism, conservatism | 反义词: modernism, avant-gardism | noun
