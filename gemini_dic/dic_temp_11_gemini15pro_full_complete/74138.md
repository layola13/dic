foundationer:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənər/
n.
基金会工作人员；基金会成员；接受基金会资助的人
A person who works for or is supported by a foundation, especially a charitable one.
The foundationer reviewed grant applications from local non-profits.  这位基金会工作人员审查了当地非营利组织的拨款申请。
The young foundationer dedicated his career to promoting education.  这位年轻的基金会成员将他的职业生涯奉献于推动教育事业。
Synonyms: philanthropist, donor, contributor, benefactor, patron
Antonyms: recipient, beneficiary
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "foundation" + "-er" (suffix denoting a person associated with or performing an action related to the base word). "Foundation" itself comes from Old French "fondacion"  and ultimately from Latin "fundatio," from "fundare" (to found, establish).
