overmultitude:/ˌoʊvərˈmʌltɪtjuːd/
n.
过度众多; 人口过剩
An excessive multitude; a population beyond what is desirable or sustainable.
The overmultitude of rabbits in the area caused significant damage to crops.  该地区兔子数量过多，对农作物造成了重大损害。
The city suffered from an overmultitude of problems, including traffic congestion and pollution.  这座城市面临着诸多问题，包括交通拥堵和污染。
近义词: surfeit, plethora, excess, overabundance
反义词: scarcity, dearth, lack, paucity
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, beyond") + "multitude" (from Old French multitude, from Latin multitudo, from multus "much").
