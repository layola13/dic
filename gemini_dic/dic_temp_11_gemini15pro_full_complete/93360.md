illegiblenesses:/ɪˌlɛdʒɪˈbɪlɪtɪz/
n.
不可辨认；难以辨认
the state or quality of being illegible.
The illegiblenesses of the doctor's handwriting made it difficult to decipher the prescription. 医生的笔迹难以辨认，难以解读处方。
illegibility, unreadable, unclear
legibility, clarity, readability
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From illegible + -ness.  Illegible comes from  Late Middle English: from late Latin illegibilis, from in- ‘not’ + legibilis (from legere ‘read’).
