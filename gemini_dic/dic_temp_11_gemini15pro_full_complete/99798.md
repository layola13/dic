intermessages:/ˌɪntərˈmɛsɪdʒɪz/
n.
交互信息;穿插的信息
Messages exchanged between different parts of a system or between different systems.  These messages can be used for coordination, data exchange, or other communication purposes.
The system relies on accurate intermessages to function correctly. 系统依靠准确的交互信息才能正常运行。
The intermessages between the two computers became corrupted, leading to system failure. 两台计算机之间的交互信息损坏了，导致系统故障。
近义词: communications, interactions, exchanges
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) silence, isolation
词性: noun
