hitching:/ˈhɪtʃɪŋ/| n. v. | 搭便车，拴住，钩住 | The act of securing something with a hitch or knot. Also, the act of hitchhiking. |  He was hitching his horse to the wagon. 他正在把他的马套到马车上。| synonyms: fastening, securing, tying, attaching, hitchhiking | antonyms: unfastening, detaching, untying | noun, verb

hitching:/ˈhɪtʃɪŋ/| n. v. | 搭便车，拴住，钩住 | Traveling by soliciting free rides from passing vehicles. | She spent the summer hitching across the country. 她整个夏天都在搭便车环游全国。| synonyms: hitchhiking, thumbing a ride | antonyms: driving, flying, taking the bus | noun, verb
