assurances:/əˈʃʊrənsɪz/| n. |保证，担保，确信|A positive declaration intended to give confidence; a promise.| The government gave assurances that the economy would improve. 政府保证经济将会好转。|近义词：guarantees, promises, commitments, pledges|反义词：doubts, uncertainties| noun

Etymology:
From Old French *asseurance*  (Modern French *assurance*),  from *assurer* "to assure," from Late Latin *assecurare* "to make secure," from Latin *ad-* "to" + *securus*  "secure, safe."
