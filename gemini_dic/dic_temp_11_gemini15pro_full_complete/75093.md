frills:/frɪlz/| n.|装饰；褶边；不必要的额外事物|Something decorative added to clothing or other objects, or an unnecessary extra feature or embellishment.  It can also refer to unnecessary extras or luxuries.|The dress had lots of frills and lace.  这件连衣裙有很多褶边和蕾丝。|synonyms: ornamentation, decoration, embellishment, flourish, furbelow, fancy, extra|antonyms: basics, necessities, essentials|noun

frills:/frɪlz/| n.|（烹调用）动物内脏；家禽杂碎|The edible internal organs of an animal, especially a bird, used in cooking.|Chicken frills are a popular ingredient in some cuisines. 鸡杂在一些菜系中很受欢迎。|synonyms: offal, giblets|antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "prime cuts" or "choice meats")|noun
