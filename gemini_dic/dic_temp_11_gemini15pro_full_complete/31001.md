chalkiest:/ˈtʃɔːkiɪst/| adj.|最像粉笔的；最白垩的；最干燥的|Covered in the chalkiest dust imaginable.  被想象中最像粉笔的灰尘覆盖。|例句：The soil in that region is the chalkiest I've ever seen.  那个地区的土壤是我见过的最白垩的。

The bread was stale and chalkiest. 这面包不新鲜而且非常干。|近义词：chalky, dry, powdery|反义词：moist, damp, wet|词性:形容词
Etymology: From chalk + -y + -est.  Chalk ultimately comes from Old English *cealc, from Proto-Germanic *kalkiz.
