kalahari: /ˌkɑːləˈhɑːri/ /ˌkæləˈhɑːri/ | n. |卡拉哈里沙漠|A large semi-arid sandy savanna in Southern Africa extending 900,000 square kilometres (350,000 sq mi), covering much of Botswana, parts of Namibia and regions of South Africa.|The Kalahari Desert is known for its diverse wildlife. 卡拉哈里沙漠以其多样的野生动物而闻名。|Synonyms: desert, savanna|Antonyms: forest, jungle|Noun

kalahari: /ˌkɑːləˈhɑːri/ /ˌkæləˈhɑːri/ | adj. |卡拉哈里的|Relating to or characteristic of the Kalahari Desert.|The Kalahari landscape is strikingly beautiful. 卡拉哈里沙漠的景色非常美丽。|Synonyms: desert, arid|Antonyms: lush, fertile|Adjective
