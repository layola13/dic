noncondensation:/ˌnɒnkɒndenˈseɪʃən/ | n. |非凝结，不凝结|The process of a substance not changing from a gas to a liquid.|The noncondensation of the steam caused a build-up of pressure. 蒸汽的非凝结导致压力积聚。|Near synonyms: nonliquefaction, nonevaporation|Antonyms: condensation, liquefaction|noun
