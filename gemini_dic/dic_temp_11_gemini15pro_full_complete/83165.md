guttiferous:/ˌɡʌtˈɪfərəs/
adjective
产树脂的；树脂状的
Producing or containing gum or resin.
The guttiferous tree oozed a sticky sap.  这棵产树脂的树渗出粘稠的树液。
The fossilized insect was encased in guttiferous amber. 这只昆虫化石被包裹在树脂状的琥珀中。
resinous, gummy
watery, dry
