money-grubbing:/ˈmʌniˌɡrʌbɪŋ/
adjective
贪婪的；唯利是图的
Excessively interested in acquiring money.
He was a money-grubbing landlord who always raised the rent. 他是一个唯利是图的房东，总是提高租金。
The company is known for its money-grubbing practices. 这家公司以其贪婪的做法而闻名。
近义词: avaricious, greedy, grasping, mercenary
反义词: generous, altruistic, philanthropic
词性: adjective

词源:  "Money" + "grubbing" (from "grub," meaning to dig or search for something, especially food, in a persistent and undignified way).  The combination implies an insatiable desire for money, as if one were constantly digging for it.
