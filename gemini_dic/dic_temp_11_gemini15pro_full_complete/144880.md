over-restrictions:/ˌoʊvər-rɪˈstrɪkʃənz/
noun
过度限制，过分约束
Excessive limitations or constraints placed on something or someone.
The over-restrictions on free speech stifled creativity. 过度限制言论自由扼杀了创造力。
synonyms: limitations, constraints, regulations, curbs
antonyms: freedoms, liberties, deregulation
n.


over-restrict:/ˌoʊvər-rɪˈstrɪkt/
verb
过度限制，过分约束
To place excessive limitations or constraints on something or someone.
Parents who over-restrict their children's activities can hinder their development. 过度限制孩子活动的父母会阻碍他们的发展。
synonyms: limit, constrain, regulate, curb
antonyms: free, liberate, deregulate
v.
