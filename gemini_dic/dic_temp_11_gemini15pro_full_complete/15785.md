balik:/ˈbɑːlɪk/| n.|鱼; 回程; 返回; 回到(菲律宾); (尤指腌制后晒干的)咸鱼|a fish; a return journey; to return; (especially in the Philippines) salted and dried fish|例句：We caught a lot of balik on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼之旅中捕到了很多鱼。

例句：The balikbayan box is full of gifts for her family.  这个返乡盒子装满了送给家人的礼物。


近义词：fish, return, comeback|反义词:departure, going|词性:名词
