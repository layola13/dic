nominator:/ˈnɒmɪneɪtə(r)/ (US) /ˈnɑːməneɪtər/| n. |提名者；任命者|A person who nominates someone for a position, office, honor, etc. |例句：The nominator gave a glowing speech about the candidate.  提名者发表了关于候选人的热情洋溢的讲话。

例句：The award nominators carefully considered each applicant. 奖项提名者仔细考虑了每一位申请人。|近义词：proposer, suggester|反义词: nominee|词性: noun
