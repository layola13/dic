caricaturist:/ˌkærɪkəˈtʃʊərɪst/| n.|漫画家；讽刺画家|A person who creates caricatures, which are exaggerated portraits or drawings that often make fun of a person's features.|The caricaturist captured the politician's large nose and receding hairline perfectly.  这位漫画家完美地捕捉到了这位政客的大鼻子和后退的发际线。|Synonyms: cartoonist, lampoonist, satirist|Antonyms: portraitist, realist|noun
