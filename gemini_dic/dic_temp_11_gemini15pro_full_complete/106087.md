knickknackatory:/ˈnɪknækətɔːri/
noun
小摆设陈列室；小玩意儿收藏处
A place where knickknacks are displayed or stored.
Her living room felt like a knickknackatory, filled with small ornaments and trinkets. 她家的客厅就像个小摆设陈列室，摆满了各种小装饰品和小玩意儿。
synonyms: curio cabinet, display case, whatnot shelf
antonyms: minimalist space, empty room
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From knickknack + -atory (a suffix forming nouns meaning "a place for," as in observatory).
