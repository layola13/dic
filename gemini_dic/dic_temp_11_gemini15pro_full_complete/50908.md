deservedly: /dɪˈzɜːvɪdli/ | adv. | 应得地，理所当然地 | In a way that is deserved; justly. | He was deservedly praised for his bravery. 他因其勇敢而受到应得的赞扬。|  The team deservedly won the championship.  这支队伍理所当然地赢得了冠军。 | justly, rightfully, appropriately | undeservedly, unfairly | adverb

词源: From deserve + -ed + -ly.  "Deserve" comes from Old French "deservir," meaning "to deserve, merit," from Latin "deservire,"  meaning "to serve well, be worthy of."
