lumbriciform:/lʌmˈbrɪsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
蚯蚓状的；像蚯蚓的
Resembling an earthworm in shape; long, slender, and cylindrical.
The lumbriciform larva burrowed into the soil.  这种蚯蚓状的幼虫钻进了土壤。

近义词:wormlike, vermiform
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes like spherical, flat, etc.)
词性: adjective
