cutty:/ˈkʌti/| adj. n.|短的，矮胖的；短裙；短裤|A short, bobtailed, or docked thing, especially a short, plump woman or a short-tailed domestic animal; a short skirt or pair of shorts.|She wore a cutty little skirt. 她穿了一条短裙。|近义词：short, cropped, stubby, dumpy|反义词：long, tall, slender|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From Scots *cuttit (“cut short, docked, curtailed”), past participle of cut. Cognate with English cut.
