outcharm:/ˌaʊtˈtʃɑːrm/
verb
以魅力胜过
surpass in charm.
例句：She outcharmed all the other guests at the party. 她在派对上以魅力胜过所有其他客人。

例句：He managed to outcharm his rival and win the contract. 他设法以魅力胜过对手，赢得了合同。

近义词：outclass, surpass, eclipse
反义词：be outshone by, be overshadowed by
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + charm.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed." "Charm" comes from the Old French "charme" (magical incantation, song, charm), from the Latin "carmen" (song, verse, incantation).
