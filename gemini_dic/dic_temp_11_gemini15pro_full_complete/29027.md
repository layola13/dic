cartoon:/kɑːrˈtuːn/ /kɑːrˈtuːn/| n. |卡通片，漫画|A motion picture using animation techniques to photograph a sequence of drawings, rather than real people or objects.|例句：He watched cartoons on Saturday mornings. 他在星期六早上看动画片。|例句：The newspaper printed a political cartoon. 报纸刊登了一幅政治漫画。|近义词：animation, comic|反义词:reality, photograph|noun

cartoon:/kɑːrˈtuːn/ /kɑːrˈtuːn/| v. |为…画漫画|To make a cartoon of.|例句：The artist cartooned the mayor.  这位艺术家为市长画了漫画。|例句：He cartooned for the New Yorker. 他为《纽约客》杂志画漫画。|近义词：caricature, lampoon|反义词: - |verb
