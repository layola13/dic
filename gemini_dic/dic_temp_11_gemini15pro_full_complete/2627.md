n.|可采用性；可接受性|The quality of being adoptable; the capacity or suitability for being adopted.|This software has high adoptability across different platforms. 这款软件在不同平台上都具有很高的可采用性。|The adoptability of this new technology remains to be seen. 这项新技术的可采用性还有待观察。|近义词：suitability, acceptability, feasibility|反义词：unsuitability, unacceptability, infeasibility|词性:名词


词源: From the adjective "adoptable" + suffix "-ity" to form a noun of quality. "Adoptable" itself comes from the verb "adopt" + suffix "-able" denoting capability. The root "adopt" traces back to the Latin "adoptare,"  meaning "to choose for oneself."
