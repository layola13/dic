dumptrucks:/ˈdʌmptrʌks/
n.
翻斗卡车；自卸卡车
A large truck with a bed that can be tilted to dump out the load.
The dumptrucks were busy hauling gravel for the new road.  翻斗卡车正忙着为新路运送碎石。

The dumptruck backed up to the edge of the pit and released its load. 翻斗卡车倒到坑边，卸下了货物。

近义词:dumper, tipper, tipper truck
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'flatbed truck' as a contrasting type of truck)
词性:noun
