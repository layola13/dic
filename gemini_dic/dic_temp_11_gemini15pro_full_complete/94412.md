implicativeness:/ɪmˌplɪkətɪvnəs/
n.
含义;蕴涵性;暗示性
The quality of being suggestive or implying something.  This refers to the extent to which something conveys a meaning or implication beyond its literal expression.
Her silence had a certain implicativeness that made him uneasy. 她的沉默带有一种暗示性，让他感到不安。
implication, suggestiveness, connotation
explicitness, directness
noun
