de-integrate:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
verb
取消整合，分解，拆散
To reverse the process of integration; to separate or break apart something that was previously integrated.
The government decided to de-integrate the school system. 政府决定取消学校系统的整合。
The company is de-integrating its business units to increase efficiency.  公司正在拆分其业务部门以提高效率。
近义词:segregate, disintegrate, separate, dismantle
反义词:integrate, combine, unify
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
de- (prefix): meaning "reverse" or "remove," from Latin de-
integrate (verb): from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole," from integer "whole, untouched"
