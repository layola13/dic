LMIC:/ˈɛlɛmɪk/| adj.|低收入和中等收入国家|Low-and middle-income countries.  These are countries with a GNI per capita, calculated using the World Bank Atlas method, of $1,045 or less in 2022.|LMICs face unique challenges in accessing healthcare. 低收入和中等收入国家在获得医疗保健方面面临独特的挑战。|近义词:developing countries, less developed countries|反义词:high-income countries, developed countries|词性:adjective
