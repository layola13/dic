jahvistic:/dʒɑːˈvɪstɪk/
|adj.|雅威的；耶和华的；用雅威文献体写的|Relating to or characteristic of the J source, or Yahwist, one of the postulated authors of the Pentateuch, or first five books of the Bible.  Characterized by a vivid and anthropomorphic portrayal of God.|The jahvistic account of creation emphasizes God's direct involvement with the world.|近义词:Yahwistic|反义词:Elohistic
词性:形容词
