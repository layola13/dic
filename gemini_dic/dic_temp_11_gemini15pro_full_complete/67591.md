extraterritorially:/ˌekstrətɛrɪˈtɔːriəli/
adv.
域外地，治外法权地
Outside the territorial limits of a jurisdiction.
The embassy can act extraterritorially in some matters.  大使馆在某些事务上可以行使治外法权。
The diplomat claimed extraterritoriality and refused to be arrested by local police.  外交官声称享有治外法权，拒绝被当地警方逮捕。
近义词:abroad, overseas, internationally
反义词:domestically, locally
词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From extra- + territorial + -ly.  Extra- meaning "outside" and territorial relating to "territory."  The suffix -ly forms adverbs.
