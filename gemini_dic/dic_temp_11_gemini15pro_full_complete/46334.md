dachshund:/ˈdɑːksˌhʊnd/ (US), /ˈdæksˌhʊnd/ (UK)| n.|腊肠犬，达克斯猎犬|A breed of dog with a long body, short legs, and drooping ears, originally bred in Germany to hunt badgers.|例句：The dachshund squeezed under the fence. 腊肠犬从栅栏底下挤了过去。

例句：My dachshund loves to chase squirrels in the park. 我的腊肠犬喜欢在公园里追逐松鼠。

近义词：sausage dog, wiener dog|反义词:|词性:noun

Etymology->
From German _Dachshund_, literally "badger dog," from _Dachs_ "badger" + _Hund_ "dog."
