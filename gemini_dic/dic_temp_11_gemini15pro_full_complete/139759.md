octachord:/ˈɒktəkɔːd/| n.|八弦琴，八度音阶|A musical instrument with eight strings or a scale of eight notes.|The ancient Greek lyre was a type of octachord.  古希腊的里拉琴是一种八弦琴。|The composer experimented with octachords in his new piece.  这位作曲家在他的新作品中尝试了八度音阶。|Synonyms: eight-stringed instrument, lyre (in some contexts)|Antonyms: None|Noun
