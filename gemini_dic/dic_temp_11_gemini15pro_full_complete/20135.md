bipectinate:/baɪˈpektɪneɪt/
adjective
双栉状的，两侧都有梳齿状突起的
Having two rows of comb-like teeth or projections, one on each side of an axis.
The bipectinate antenna of the moth helps it detect pheromones.  这只蛾子的双栉状触角帮助它探测信息素。
bipinnate, double-combed
none
