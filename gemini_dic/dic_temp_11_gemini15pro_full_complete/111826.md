logger:/ˈlɒɡər/ (British), /ˈlɔːɡər/ (American)| n.|伐木工人；记录器|A person who cuts down trees for timber; a device or program that records events, processes, or data.|The logger felled the giant redwood. 伐木工人砍倒了巨大的红杉树。 The flight data recorder, also known as the black box, is a crucial logger for investigating aircraft accidents. 飞行数据记录器，也称为黑匣子，是调查飞机事故的关键记录器。|近义词:lumberjack (for person), recorder, monitor|反义词:(for person) conservationist, environmentalist|词性:名词
