commences:/kəˈmɛnsɪz/
verb
开始，着手
begin, start, initiate something formally or officially
The academic year commences in September.  学年从九月开始。
The trial commences tomorrow. 审判明天开始。
近义词:begin, start, initiate, inaugurate
反义词:end, finish, conclude, terminate
词性:v.
