dehydrator:/ˌdiːˈhaɪdreɪtər/ | n. | 脱水器，干燥器 | A device that removes water from something, typically food, to preserve it. |  I use a food dehydrator to make dried fruit snacks. 我用食物脱水器制作果干零食。

The dehydrator is essential for preserving herbs.  这个脱水器对于保存草药至关重要。 | synonyms: drier, desiccator | antonyms: humidifier, hydrator | noun
