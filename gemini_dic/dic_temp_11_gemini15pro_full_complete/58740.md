dung:/dʌŋ/| v.|施肥|He dunged the field with cow manure. 他用牛粪给田地施肥。|She dunged the garden regularly to ensure healthy growth.  她定期给花园施肥以确保植物健康生长。|近义词: fertilize, manure|反义词: (无直接反义词)|动词


词源:
From Old English *dung, from Proto-Germanic *dungaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰongʰ- (“to smear, spread”). Cognate with Old Norse dyngja (“dunghill”), Dutch dungen, German Dünger (“fertilizer”).  The verb is a back-formation from the noun.
