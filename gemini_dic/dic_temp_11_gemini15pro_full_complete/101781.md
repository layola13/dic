irreversibility:/ˌɪrɪˌvɜːrsəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可逆性，不可逆转性
The quality or state of being irreversible; inability to be reversed.
The irreversibility of the decision weighed heavily on her mind.  这个决定的不可逆转性让她心事重重。
The lecture emphasized the irreversibility of brain damage caused by stroke. 讲座强调了中风引起的脑损伤是不可逆转的。
permanence, finality, immutability
reversibility, changeability
noun
