obsidians:/əbˈsɪdiənz/
|n.|黑曜石|A hard, dark, glassy volcanic rock formed by the rapid solidification of lava without crystallization.|The ancient Aztecs used obsidians to make knives and tools. 古代阿兹特克人用黑曜石制作刀具和工具。|Synonyms: volcanic glass|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
