mateynesses:/ˈmeɪtɪnɛsɪz/
noun
僵局，逼和状态 (尤指国际象棋)
A situation in chess where the player whose turn it is to move is not in check but has no legal move.
Example: The game ended in mateynesses, with neither player able to achieve victory. 这局棋以僵局告终，双方都无法取得胜利。
Example:  He skillfully maneuvered his opponent into a position of mateynesses. 他巧妙地将对手逼入僵局。
Synonym: stalemate, deadlock, impasse
Antonym: checkmate, victory, win
