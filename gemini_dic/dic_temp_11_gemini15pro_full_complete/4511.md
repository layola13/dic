albumenize:/ælˈbjuːmənaɪz/
v.
使成蛋白状；使变成蛋白；涂以蛋白
To convert or coat with albumen.
Egg whites are used to albumenize photographic paper. 蛋清用来使相纸变成蛋白状。
近义词: coat, glaze
反义词: None
词性: Verb

albumenize:/ælˈbjuːmənaɪz/
n.
蛋白化
The process of converting or coating with albumen.
The albumenization of the print gave it a glossy finish.  照片的蛋白化处理使其具有光泽。
近义词: coating, glazing
反义词: None
词性: Noun
