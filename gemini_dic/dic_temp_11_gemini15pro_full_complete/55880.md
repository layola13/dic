divisionally:/dɪˈvɪʒənəli/
| adv.|按部门地，划分地|In a way that is related to divisions or departments within an organization. |The company is organized divisionally, with each division responsible for its own profits and losses.  这家公司是按部门组织的，每个部门负责自己的盈亏。|Synonyms: departmentally, sectionally|Antonyms: centrally, unifiedly
|词性: 副词
