overdecoratively:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkərətɪvli/
| adv. | 过度装饰地 | excessively or elaborately decorated; in a way that uses too much decoration | The room was overdecoratively furnished, with ornate carvings and gilded frames everywhere.  房间装饰得过于华丽，到处都是华丽的雕刻和镀金的框架。 | elaborately, excessively, ostentatiously | plainly, simply, minimally
|词性:副词
