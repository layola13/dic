crystalliform:/krɪˈstælɪfɔːrm/
adjective
晶状的，水晶形的
Resembling crystal in form. Having the external form of a crystal.
The cave walls were covered in crystalliform structures. 洞穴的墙壁上覆盖着晶状结构。
The mineral deposit exhibited a beautiful crystalliform pattern.  这种矿物沉积物呈现出美丽的晶体形状。
近义词:crystalline, crystal-like
反义词:amorphous
词性:adjective
