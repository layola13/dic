inferobranchian:/ˌɪnfəroʊˈbræŋkiən/
adjective
下鳃的
situated below the gills
Inferobranchian snails are found in deep sea hydrothermal vents. 下鳃蜗牛发现于深海热液喷口。
近义词:hypobranchial
反义词: (No common direct antonym; consider "epibranchial" for above the gills)
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *inferus* "low" + Greek *branchia* "gills"
