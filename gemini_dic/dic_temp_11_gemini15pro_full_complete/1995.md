actual:/ˈæktʃuəl/| adj.|真实的，实际的，事实上的|Existing in fact; real. |The actual cost was higher than we expected. 实际成本比我们预期的要高。|The actual time of arrival was delayed by an hour. 实际到达时间晚了一个小时。|近义词：real, genuine, true, factual, concrete|反义词：theoretical, hypothetical, supposed, potential, nominal|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *actualis*, from Latin actus (“act, action, performance”), past participle of agere (“to do, drive, act”).
