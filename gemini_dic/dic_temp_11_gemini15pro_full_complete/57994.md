drivenness:/ˈdrɪvənnəs/
noun
意志力; 驱动力; 内驱力
The quality of being driven; a strong inner motivation to achieve something.  It refers to a persistent, focused determination and ambition.
Her drivenness led her to start her own successful business. 她强烈的进取心促使她创办了自己的成功企业。
ambition, determination, drive, motivation, zeal
apathy, indifference, laziness, lethargy, passivity
