overhearers:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪrərz/| n. |偷听者；无意中听到的人|People who overhear a conversation unintentionally or secretly.  Those who happen to hear something said to someone else.| I didn't mean to eavesdrop, but I became one of the overhearers when they started arguing loudly in the cafe. 我本不想偷听，但当他们在咖啡馆大声争吵时，我成了其中一个无意听到的人。|Synonyms: eavesdroppers, listeners|Antonyms: speakers, talkers|noun


overhear:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪr/| v. |无意中听到；偷听到|To accidentally or unintentionally hear something said to someone else. To hear something without the speaker's knowledge or intention.| I overheard them talking about the surprise party. 我无意中听到他们在谈论惊喜派对。|Synonyms: eavesdrop on, listen in on|Antonyms: announce, broadcast|verb
