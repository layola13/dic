注意事项:

"Draftest" while grammatically correct, is not commonly used.  "Draft" itself implies an unfinished state, and using the superlative "-est" is often considered redundant.  Simpler alternatives like "most preliminary," "roughest," or "most unfinished" are generally preferred.
