ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mordre*, from Old French *mordre* (meaning "murder, killing, mortal sin"), from Frankish *murþr* or some other West Germanic source akin to Old English *morþor* (meaning "murder, crime, torment, sin"), from Proto-Germanic *murþrą* (meaning "murder, killing"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mer- (meaning "to die").
