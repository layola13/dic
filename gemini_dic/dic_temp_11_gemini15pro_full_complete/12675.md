assemblywomen: /əˈsɛmbliˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女议员（复数）
A group of women who are members of an assembly, especially a legislative body.
The assemblywomen voted unanimously on the new bill. 女议员们一致投票通过了这项新法案。

The assemblywomen debated the issue for hours. 女议员们就这个问题辩论了几个小时。


近义词: female legislators, female representatives
反义词: assemblymen
词性: noun
