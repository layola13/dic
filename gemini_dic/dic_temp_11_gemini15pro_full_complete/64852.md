活动, 娱乐, 消遣, 玩耍 (尤指户外活动)

The king enjoyed hunting and other rural esplees. 国王喜欢打猎和其他乡村娱乐活动。

近义词:activities, amusements, diversions, entertainments, pastimes, recreations
反义词:work, labor, toil

词源:
From Old French espleit, from Latin explicitus. Related to exploit. Originally meant 'profit, advantage'. The meaning shifted to 'amusement, diversion' in the 16th century.
