decreased:/dɪˈkriːst/
verb
减少，降低
made or become smaller or fewer in size, amount, intensity, or degree
The company's profits decreased by 10% last year.  该公司利润去年下降了10%。
近义词: reduced, lessened, diminished, lowered
反义词: increased, raised, enhanced, augmented
词性: 过去分词(past participle) -  decreased 也可以作为形容词使用，表示“减少的”。

decreased:/dɪˈkriːst/
adjective
减少的，降低的
having become smaller or fewer in size, amount, intensity, or degree.
There was a decreased demand for coal this year. 今年煤炭需求下降了。
近义词: reduced, lessened, diminished, lowered
反义词: increased, raised, enhanced, augmented
词性: 形容词(adjective)
