outstaggered:/ˌaʊtˈstæɡərd/| v. |超越，胜过，(尤指在耐力方面)超过|To surpass or exceed, especially in endurance.|The marathon runner outstaggered his opponents in the final mile. |马拉松运动员在最后一英里超过了他的对手。|近义词：outdo, surpass, outstrip, excel, transcend|反义词: lag behind, fall behind|词性:动词

词源:  "out-" (超越) + "stagger" (蹒跚，踉跄).  The prefix "out-" intensifies the meaning of "stagger," suggesting surpassing someone to the point where they are left staggering behind.
