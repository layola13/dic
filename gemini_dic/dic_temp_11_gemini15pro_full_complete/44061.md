crinigerous:/krɪˈnɪdʒərəs/
| adj. |
有毛发的，多毛的 |
Covered with hair or having a lot of hair. |
例句：The crinigerous caterpillar inched along the leaf.  多毛的毛毛虫沿着叶子慢慢爬行。

例句：Some species of bats are notably crinigerous.  某些种类的蝙蝠毛发特别多。
|
近义词：hairy, pilose, hirsute |
反义词：hairless, glabrous |
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *criniger* (hair-bearing), from *crinis* (hair) + *-ger* (carrying, bearing).
