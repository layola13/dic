honeysuckled:/ˈhʌniˌsʌkəld/| adj.| 忍冬花的；香甜的；芬芳的| Covered with or smelling of honeysuckle. | The honeysuckled cottage was charming.  忍冬花覆盖的小屋很迷人。| The air was honeysuckled and warm. 空气中弥漫着忍冬花的香气，温暖宜人。|近义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling, perfumed|反义词: odorless, unscented|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From honeysuckle + -ed.  Honeysuckle itself comes from the practice of sucking nectar from the flowers.
