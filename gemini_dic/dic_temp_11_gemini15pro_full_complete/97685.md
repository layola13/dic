inhumanities:/ˌɪnhjuːˈmænɪtiz/
n.
残酷行为，非人道行为
extreme cruelty or lack of humanity.
The inhumanities of war are appalling. 战争的残酷令人震惊。

近义词:atrocities, barbarities, cruelties
反义词:humanities, kindness, compassion
词性:名词

inhumanity:/ˌɪnhjuːˈmænəti/
n.
非人道，残忍
lack of humanity; cruelty.
The inhumanity of the prison system is a serious issue. 监狱系统的非人道性是一个严重的问题。

近义词:cruelty, barbarity, brutality
反义词:humanity, compassion, kindness
词性:名词
