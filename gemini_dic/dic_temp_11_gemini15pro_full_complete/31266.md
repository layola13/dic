chapatti: /tʃəˈpɑːti/ (UK), /tʃəˈpɑːti/ (US) | n. | 印度薄饼，恰巴提 | A thin, flat unleavened bread made from whole wheat flour, originating from the Indian subcontinent. |  We had chapatti and curry for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了恰巴提和咖喱。| Synonym: roti, flatbread | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps leavened bread like naan) | Noun
