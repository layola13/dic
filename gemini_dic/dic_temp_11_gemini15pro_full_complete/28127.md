capitations:/kæpɪˈteɪʃənz/
n.
人头费；按人头收费；按人头分配
a fixed amount of money paid to a doctor or hospital for each patient they treat
例句：
The doctor receives capitations for each patient registered at his practice.
这位医生按其诊所登记的每位病人收取人头费。

The hospital negotiated a capitation rate with the insurance company.
医院与保险公司就人头费率进行了协商。

近义词：per capita payment, headcount payment
反义词: fee-for-service
