joll:/dʒɒl/ | n. |  欢乐，庆祝，盛宴 | A festive gathering or celebration, often characterized by lively and boisterous activity. | They held a joll to celebrate the harvest. 他们举行盛宴庆祝丰收。 | 近义词：festivity, celebration, feast, revelry | 反义词：mourning, grief, sorrow | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *jollen, *jolien (“to make merry, rejoice, amuse oneself”), perhaps frequentative of *jollen, *jolien (“to jostle, shake”), or from Anglo-Norman jolif (“jovial, merry”). Ultimately from Old Norse jól (“Yule, Christmas”).
