mechanicalize:/məˈkænɪˌkaɪz/ /məˈkænɪkˌlaɪz/| v. |使机械化；用机械装置|To equip with machinery, especially in order to replace human or animal labor.|The factory was mechanized in the 1950s.  这家工厂在20世纪50年代实现了机械化。

The farm has been completely mechanized.  这个农场已经完全机械化了。


|近义词：automate, industrialize|反义词:  de-mechanize, manualize|词性:动词
