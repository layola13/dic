gambling's:/ˈɡæmblɪŋz/
noun
赌博（行为）的；与赌博有关的
Belonging to or associated with gambling.
The gambling's impact on the family was devastating. 赌博对这个家庭的影响是毁灭性的。
synonyms: betting's, wagering's
antonyms: saving's, investing's
POS: noun (possessive form)

Etymology: From the verb "gamble" + possessive suffix "-'s". "Gamble" originates from the Old English "gamenian" meaning "to play games," which in turn comes from the Proto-Germanic "*gamanan."
