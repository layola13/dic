for-fear-that:/fɔːr ˈfɪər ðæt/
conjunction
因为害怕；唯恐；以免
in case something unpleasant happens
He locked the door for fear that someone might break in. 他锁上了门，唯恐有人会破门而入。
Synonyms: lest, in case, so as not to
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a conjunction expressing fear of a consequence, but antonyms for "fear" itself could be: courage, bravery, boldness)
词性: 连词
