mannequin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ (US) /ˈmænɪkɪn/ (UK)| n.|人体模型；时装模特儿|A life-sized dummy used to display clothes in shop windows or used by artists, especially to draw or paint.|The mannequins in the window display were dressed in the latest fashions.  橱窗里的人体模型穿着最新款式的时装。|Synonyms: dummy, model, lay figure|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "person," "human," "live model")|Noun
