beseeming:/bɪˈsiːmɪŋ/
adjective
合适的，恰当的
suitable or appropriate; fitting
Her beseeming modesty impressed everyone. 她恰如其分的谦逊给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
近义词:appropriate, fitting, proper, suitable, apt
反义词:inappropriate, unfitting, improper, unsuitable, unbecoming
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *besemyng, *bisemyng, from Old English *besēmende (“becoming, fitting”), present participle of besēman (“to befit, suit”), from be- + sēman (“to beseem, befit”), from Proto-Germanic *sōmijaną (“to suit, fit”), from Proto-Indo-European *sem- (“one; same”). Cognate with Old Frisian bisemende (“fitting”), Dutch betamelijk (“proper, decent”), German bescheiden (“modest”). More at seem.
