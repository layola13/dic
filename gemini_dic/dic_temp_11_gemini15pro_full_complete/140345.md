ohmically:/oʊˈmɪkli/| adv.|欧姆地，电阻地|  Relating to or conforming to Ohm's law, which states that the current through a conductor between two points is directly proportional to the voltage across the two points.|The resistor behaves ohmically over a wide range of voltages. 电阻器在很宽的电压范围内表现出欧姆特性。|Synonym: resistively|Antonym: non-ohmically|Adverb
