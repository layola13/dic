beneficent:/bəˈnɛfɪsənt/| adj.|仁慈的；慈善的；有益的|Doing or producing good, especially performing acts of kindness or charity. Characterized by or suggestive of doing good.|The beneficent king provided food and shelter for the homeless. 仁慈的国王为无家可归的人提供食物和住所。|近义词：benevolent, charitable, kind, generous, philanthropic|反义词：maleficent, malevolent, harmful, detrimental|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *beneficens*, present participle of *benefacere* (“to do good”), from *bene* (“well”) + *facere* (“to do, make”).
