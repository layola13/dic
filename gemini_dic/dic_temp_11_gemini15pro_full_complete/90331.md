housing's:/ˈhaʊzɪŋz/
noun
住房（情况），房屋（供应）
Relating to houses or the provision of houses.
The city's housing's inadequate for the growing population. 这个城市的住房对于不断增长的人口来说是不够的。
Synonyms: accommodation, lodging, shelter
Antonyms: homelessness


ETYMOLOGY->
From "housing," which is the noun form of the verb "house," meaning "to provide shelter or lodging."  The "-'s" is a possessive marker, indicating ownership or belonging. Ultimately derived from the Old English "hūs."
