decarbonizers:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzərz/
|n.|脱碳者；减碳者；去碳剂|People or technologies that remove or reduce carbon dioxide emissions from the atmosphere or an industrial process.  旨在从大气或工业过程中去除或减少二氧化碳排放的人或技术。|The government is investing in new decarbonizers to help meet its climate targets. 政府正在投资新的脱碳技术以帮助实现其气候目标。|  The company is researching new decarbonizers for the cement industry.  这家公司正在研究水泥行业的新型脱碳剂。|synonyms: carbon reducers, emission reducers, climate solutions|antonyms: carbon emitters, polluters|noun
