absorbabilities:/əbˌzɔːrbəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n.|吸收率，吸收能力|The absorbabilities of different materials vary greatly. 不同材料的吸收率差异很大。|例句：The researchers compared the absorbabilities of various sponges. 研究人员比较了各种海绵的吸收能力。

例句：The absorbabilities of these fabrics are crucial for their intended use. 这些织物的吸收能力对其预期用途至关重要。


|近义词：absorption capacities, uptake rates|反义词: impermeabilities|词性:名词(plural)
