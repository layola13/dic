fountaining:/ˈfaʊntənɪŋ/ | n. | 喷泉状，喷射 | The action of liquid spurting or spraying like a fountain. | The fountaining of the geyser was spectacular.  间歇泉的喷涌非常壮观。| synonyms: spurting, spraying, jetting, gushing | antonyms: dripping, trickling | noun

fountaining:/ˈfaʊntənɪŋ/ | adj. | 喷泉似的，喷涌的 | Resembling a fountain in appearance or action. | The fountaining display of fireworks lit up the night sky. 喷泉般的烟火照亮了夜空。| synonyms: eruptive, spouting, cascading | antonyms: stagnant, still | adjective
