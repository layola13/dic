overchilling:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɪlɪŋ/
adjective
过度冷却
The process of cooling something to a temperature below the desired or optimal level.
The overchilling of the vaccine rendered it ineffective. 过度冷却使疫苗失效。
近义词: overcooling
反义词: overheating
词性: adjective


overchilling:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɪlɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度冷却(进行时)
The act of cooling something excessively.
They are overchilling the wine, it will be too cold to drink.  他们把酒冷却过度了，喝起来会太凉。
