earthwards:/ˈɜːθwədz/ | adv. | 向地球；朝向地面 | Towards the earth; in a downward direction. | The apple fell earthwards from the tree. 苹果从树上掉落到地面。|  The rocket plummeted earthwards after engine failure.  火箭引擎失效后朝地面坠落。 | 近义词: downwards, groundwards | 反义词: upwards, skywards | 词性: 副词

词源:  A compound of "earth" and the suffix "-wards," indicating direction.  "Earth" comes from Old English "eorþe," meaning "ground, soil, dry land," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*erþō."  "-wards" comes from Old English "-weardes," a directional suffix.
