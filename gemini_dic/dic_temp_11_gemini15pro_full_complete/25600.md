bumped: /bʌmpt/ (美式) /bʌmpt/ (英式)
| v. | 撞到，碰见，提高，取消预定 | To come into contact with something suddenly and forcefully, or to encounter someone unexpectedly. Also, to increase something or to remove someone from a reserved seat or booking. |  I bumped into an old friend at the grocery store. 我在杂货店撞见了一位老朋友。 | knock, hit, collide with, run into, encounter | avoid, miss, sidestep
| verb |

bumped: /bʌmpt/ (美式) /bʌmpt/ (英式)
| adj. | 凹凸不平的 | Having a surface that is not smooth or even. | The road was bumpy and difficult to drive on. 这条路坑坑洼洼的，很难开。 | uneven, rough, lumpy | smooth, flat, even
| adjective |
