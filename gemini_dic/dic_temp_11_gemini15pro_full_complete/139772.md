octal:/ˈɒktəl/ (UK) /ˈɑːktl/ (US)| adj. n.|八进制的，八进制数|Relating to or using a system of numerical notation that has 8 rather than 10 as a base.|The number 10 in octal is equal to the number 8 in decimal. 八进制的10等于十进制的8.| synonym: base-8| antonym: decimal, binary| adjective, noun
