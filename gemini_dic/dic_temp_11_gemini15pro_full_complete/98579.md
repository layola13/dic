institutionalizes: /ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使制度化；使机构化 | to establish something as a convention or norm in an organization or culture; to place someone in an institution such as a hospital or prison. |  The government institutionalized new regulations for environmental protection. 政府将新的环境保护法规制度化了。 |  The family decided to institutionalize their elderly mother as her dementia worsened.  由于母亲的痴呆症恶化，家人决定将她送进养老院。 | synonyms: establish, formalize, systematize, commit | antonyms: abolish, dissolve, dismantle | verb
