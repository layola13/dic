brachycephalous: /ˌbrækɪˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 短头型的，短颅的 | Having a short, broad head, relative to its length. This term is often used in anthropology and animal breeding. |  Example: Bulldogs are a brachycephalous breed. 斗牛犬是一个短头型的品种。

Example: The skull found at the archaeological site was determined to be brachycephalous. 在考古遗址发现的头骨被确定为短头型的。


近义词: short-headed, broad-headed | 反义词: dolichocephalous (长头型的) | 词性: adjective
