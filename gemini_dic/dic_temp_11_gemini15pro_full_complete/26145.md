busto:/ˈbʌstoʊ/ (US) /ˈbʌstəʊ/ (UK)| n.|半身像；胸像；失败；破产|A sculpture of a person's head and shoulders.  A complete failure; a fiasco.|例句：The museum displayed a busto of Julius Caesar. 博物馆展出了尤利乌斯·凯撒的半身像。

例句：The business venture turned into a complete busto. 这项商业冒险彻底失败了。|近义词：bust, sculpture, failure, fiasco|反义词：success, triumph|词性:noun
