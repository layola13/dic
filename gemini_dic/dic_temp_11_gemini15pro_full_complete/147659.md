overpopularly: /ˌōvərˈpɑːpjələrli/
adverb
过度流行地，过分受欢迎地
in a way that is too popular
The song was overpopularly played on the radio, to the point where people started to dislike it. 这首歌在电台上播放得过于频繁，以至于人们开始讨厌它。
Synonyms: excessively popularly, too popularly
Antonyms: unpopularly, rarely
词性: 副词
