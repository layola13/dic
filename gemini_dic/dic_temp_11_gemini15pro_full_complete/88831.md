hogh:/hog/ | n. |阉过的公猪; 肥猪; 自私贪婪的人|A castrated male pig, especially one raised and fattened for slaughter; a greedy or selfish person. |例句：The farmer raised hogs for market. 农民养猪是为了上市。|例句：He's such a hogh, always taking the biggest slice of cake. 他真是个贪婪的家伙，总是拿最大的一块蛋糕。| 近义词：pig, swine, glutton | 反义词: generous person| 词性:名词
