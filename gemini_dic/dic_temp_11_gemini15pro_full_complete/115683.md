margay:/ˈmɑːrɡeɪ/ /ˈmɑːrɡaɪ/ | n. | 豹猫 |A small, spotted wild cat native to Central and South America, known for its arboreal lifestyle and exceptional climbing abilities. | The margay is a skilled climber, able to rotate its ankles 180 degrees.  豹猫是一种攀爬高手，能够将脚踝旋转180度。

The margay's diet primarily consists of small mammals, birds, and reptiles. 豹猫主要以小型哺乳动物、鸟类和爬行动物为食。 | 近义词:ocelot, tiger cat, tree ocelot | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *margai, perhaps from Tupi *mbaraka'i "small wild cat".
