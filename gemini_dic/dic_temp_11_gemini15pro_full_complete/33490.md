chyometer: /kaɪˈɒmɪtər/ | n. |乳糜计，乳糜度计 | An instrument used to measure the chylosity (the milky appearance) of a fluid, particularly chyle.  It is used to quantify the concentration of chylomicrons in a fluid sample. | The chyometer revealed a significant increase in chylomicrons after the fatty meal. 乳糜计显示，进食油腻食物后，乳糜微粒显著增加。|  Synonym: Chyle meter | Antonym: N/A | noun
