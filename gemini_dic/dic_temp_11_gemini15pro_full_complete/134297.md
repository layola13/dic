noninjurious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʒʊəriəs/
adjective
无害的，不致伤的
Not harmful or injurious.
This cleaning solution is noninjurious to the environment.  这种清洁剂对环境无害。
The spider's bite, while painful, was ultimately noninjurious. 虽然蜘蛛咬人很疼，但最终没有造成伤害。
近义词:harmless, safe, innocuous
反义词:harmful, injurious, damaging
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not
injurious (adjective): causing injury
