/ˈlʌmbərd/ | v. | 笨拙地移动；缓慢吃力地移动 | moved in a slow, heavy, awkward way | The bear lumbered through the forest. 熊笨拙地在森林里走动。| The old truck lumbered down the road.  这辆旧卡车在路上缓慢吃力地行驶。|  近义词: trudge, plod, shuffle | 反义词: dart, sprint, glide | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *lomeren, of uncertain origin, possibly from or related to lome (“clay, loam”) and thus referring to moving heavily as if through mud.
