conveyancing:/kənˈveɪənsɪŋ/
n.|不动产转让，产权过户|The legal process of transferring ownership of property from one person to another.|The conveyancing process took longer than expected.  不动产转让过程比预期的要长。|近义词:property transfer, property transaction|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

conveyancing:/kənˈveɪənsɪŋ/
n.|运输;输送|The act of transporting or carrying something from one place to another. (Rare usage in this sense) |The conveyancing of goods by rail is efficient. 通过铁路运输货物很有效率。（罕用）|近义词:transport, transportation, carriage|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From convey + -ancing.  Convey comes from Old French conveier, from Latin *conviare "to accompany on the way".
