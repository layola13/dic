generalize:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |概括，归纳；推广；普及|To infer or form a conclusion/opinion from particular instances; to make a general statement or form a general principle after considering only a small number of facts; to make something more widespread or common. |Scientists are cautious about generalizing from their laboratory findings to real-world situations. 科学家们对从实验室的发现推广到现实世界的情况持谨慎态度。

Researchers generalized the findings to the wider population. 研究人员将研究结果推广到更广泛的人群。|Synonyms: summarize, abstract, extrapolate, universalize, popularize, broaden, expand |Antonyms: specialize, specify, restrict, limit, particularize |verb
