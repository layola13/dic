nonphonetical:/ˌnɒn.fəˈnet.ɪ.kəl/ | adj. | 非语音的，不符合语音规则的 | Not based on the sounds of speech; not representing pronunciation.  |  The spelling of "knight" is nonphonetical. “knight”的拼写不符合语音规则。 | synonyms: non-phonetic, unphonetic | antonyms: phonetic, pronounceable | adjective

Etymology:  From "non-" (not) + "phonetical" (relating to phonetics, the study of speech sounds). "Phonetical" ultimately derives from the Greek "phōnē" (sound, voice).
