hygienically:/ˌhaɪdʒiˈenɪkli/ | adv. | 卫生地；干净地 | In a way that maintains health and prevents disease, by being clean. | The food was prepared hygienically.  食物是卫生地准备的。 | Examples: The surgeon washed his hands hygienically before the operation.  外科医生在手术前卫生地洗了手。 The restaurant maintains a hygienically clean kitchen. 这家餐厅保持着卫生的厨房环境。 | Synonyms: cleanly, sanitarily | Antonyms: dirtily, unhygienically | Word Type: Adverb

hygiene:/ˈhaɪdʒiːn/ | n. | 卫生；保健 | Conditions or practices conducive to maintaining health and preventing disease, especially through cleanliness. |  Personal hygiene is important for good health. 个人卫生对健康很重要。 | Examples:  Dental hygiene is essential for healthy teeth and gums. 牙齿卫生对牙齿和牙龈的健康至关重要。  The hospital has strict hygiene protocols to prevent infections. 医院有严格的卫生规程以防止感染。| Synonyms: cleanliness, sanitation | Antonyms: filth, dirt | Word Type: Noun
