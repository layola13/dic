betrayal:/bɪˈtreɪəl/| n.|背叛，出卖，辜负|The betrayal of trust is a serious offense.  信任的背叛是严重的罪行。|例句：His betrayal of our secrets cost us dearly. 他的背叛使我们付出了沉重的代价。|近义词：treachery, perfidy, disloyalty, treason|反义词：loyalty, fidelity, faithfulness|词性:名词

betray:/bɪˈtreɪ/| vt.|背叛，出卖，辜负，显露，暴露|His trembling hands betrayed his nervousness. 他颤抖的双手暴露了他的紧张。|例句：She felt betrayed by her best friend. 她觉得被她最好的朋友背叛了。|近义词：deceive, mislead, double-cross|反义词：support, protect, defend|词性:动词
