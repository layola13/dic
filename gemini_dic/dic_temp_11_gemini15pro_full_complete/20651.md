blackrock: /ˈblækˌrɑːk/ (US) /ˈblækˌrɒk/ (UK)| n. adj.|黑岩，贝莱德集团|A large investment management corporation.  As an adjective, it can describe something related to or managed by BlackRock.|

例句：
BlackRock is the world's largest asset manager.
贝莱德是世界上最大的资产管理公司。

BlackRock manages trillions of dollars in assets.
贝莱德管理着数万亿美元的资产。
