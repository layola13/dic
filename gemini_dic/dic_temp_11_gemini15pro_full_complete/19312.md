bibelot:/ˈbɪbləʊ/ (US) /ˈbiːbləʊ/ (UK)| n. | 小摆设；小古董；珍奇的小玩意儿 |A small, decorative object; a trinket; a curio. | She collects bibelots from all over the world. 她从世界各地收集小摆设。

|The shelves were filled with bibelots and antique dolls.  架子上摆满了小摆设和古董娃娃。


|Synonyms: trinket, knick-knack, curio, bauble, ornament|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider) necessity, essential|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French bibelot, ultimately of unknown origin. Possibly related to the Old French word "beubelet," meaning "a small toy."
