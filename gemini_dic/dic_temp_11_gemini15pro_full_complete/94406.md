implication:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|含义；暗示；牵连；卷入|something that is suggested, or happens, indirectly|The implication was that I was lying. 这暗示着我在撒谎。|近义词：consequence, suggestion, inference|反义词：explicit statement|noun

implication:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|含义；暗示；牵连；卷入|The conclusion that can be drawn from something although it is not explicitly stated.|His implication in the crime was obvious. 他与这起犯罪的牵连显而易见。|近义词：involvement, entanglement|反义词：exoneration|noun
