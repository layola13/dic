brevirostral:/ˌbrevɪˈrɒstrəl/
adjective
短吻的，短喙的
Having a short snout or beak.
The pug is a brevirostral breed of dog. 巴哥犬是一种短吻的狗。
The brevirostral crocodile has a shorter snout than other species. 短吻鳄的鼻子比其他种类的鳄鱼短。
近义词:short-snouted, short-beaked
反义词:longirostral, long-snouted, long-beaked
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *brevis* (short) + *rostrum* (beak, snout).
