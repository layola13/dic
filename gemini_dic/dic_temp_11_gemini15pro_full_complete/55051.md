disquisition:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. |  专题论文；学术演讲；详尽的论述 | A formal discourse or treatise in which a subject is examined and discussed; a lengthy and detailed explanation or analysis of a particular subject. | He delivered a lengthy disquisition on the history of philosophy. 他发表了一篇关于哲学史的长篇专题论文。| The professor's disquisition on the nature of consciousness lasted for two hours. 教授关于意识本质的详尽论述持续了两个小时。| 近义词: treatise, discourse, dissertation, exposition, analysis | 反义词: summary, synopsis, outline | 词性: noun

disquisition:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. |专题论文；学术演讲；详尽的论述 | From Latin *disquisitio*, from *disquirere* "to examine, investigate," from *dis-* "apart" + *quaerere* "to seek." |  She wrote a disquisition on the use of metaphor in Shakespeare's plays. 她写了一篇关于莎士比亚戏剧中隐喻用法的专题论文。| His disquisition on the topic was both informative and tedious. 他关于这个话题的论述既翔实又冗长。| 近义词: treatise, discourse, dissertation, exposition, analysis | 反义词: summary, synopsis, outline | 词性: noun
