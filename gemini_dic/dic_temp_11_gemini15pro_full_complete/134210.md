nonindictments:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈdaɪtmənts/
n.
不起诉，不予起诉
The grand jury handed down nonindictments for all the officers involved.  大陪审团对所有涉案警官都不予起诉。
The nonindictments sparked protests across the city. 不起诉的决定引发了全城抗议。
Synonym: acquittals, dismissals
Antonym: indictments, charges
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + indictment.  "Indictment" comes from Old French "enditement,"  ultimately from Latin "indictare" meaning "to proclaim, accuse formally."
