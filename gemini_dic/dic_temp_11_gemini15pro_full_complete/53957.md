discreditabilities:/dɪsˌkrɛdɪtəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
不光彩的事情；丢脸的事情
Things that cause someone to lose respect or reputation; actions or qualities that bring shame or disgrace.
His past discreditabilities prevented him from getting the job.  他过去不光彩的事情使他没能得到这份工作。
Synonyms: disgraces, shames, dishonors, scandals
Antonyms: honors, achievements, merits
词性: 名词

discreditable:/dɪsˈkrɛdɪtəbl/
adjective
不光彩的；丢脸的
Bringing shame or disgrace; not respectable.
His discreditable behavior led to his dismissal. 他不光彩的行为导致他被解雇。
Synonyms: shameful, disgraceful, dishonorable, ignominious
Antonyms: honorable, respectable, creditable, admirable
词性: 形容词

discredit:/dɪsˈkrɛdɪt/
verb
使丧失信誉；使丢脸；使不值钱
To harm the reputation of; to cause to be doubted or disbelieved; to cause something to seem less valuable or important.
The scandal discredited the politician.  这桩丑闻使这位政治家名誉扫地。
Synonyms: disgrace, dishonor, shame, tarnish
Antonyms: honor, praise, extol, credit
词性: 动词
