candylike: /ˈkændiˌlaɪk/
adjective
像糖果的；甜美的
Resembling candy, especially in sweetness or appearance.
The frosting had a smooth, candylike texture.  糖霜有着光滑的、像糖果一样的质地。
Her perfume had a candylike sweetness. 她的香水有一种像糖果一样的甜味。
近义词: sugary, sweet, confectionery
反义词: bitter, sour, savory
词性: adjective
词源:  candy + like.  "Candy" comes from Arabic "qandi," meaning "candied," and "like" is a native English suffix meaning "resembling."
