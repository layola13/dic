enshrouded:/ɪnˈʃraʊdɪd/
adjective
被遮蔽的，被笼罩的
Covered or enveloped completely, as if by a shroud.
The mountain peak was enshrouded in mist. 山峰笼罩在薄雾之中。

enshrouded:/ɪnˈʃraʊdɪd/
verb (past participle)
遮蔽，笼罩
Covered or enveloped completely, as if by a shroud. This is the past participle form of the verb "enshroud".
The city was enshrouded in smog. 这座城市笼罩在烟雾之中。

enshrouded:/ɪnˈʃraʊdɪd/
adjective
隐藏的，神秘的
Hidden, concealed, or mysterious.  This meaning is derived from the idea of being covered and thus obscured.
The motive for the crime remains enshrouded in mystery. 犯罪动机仍然笼罩在神秘之中。


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "to cause to be in," "to put into," or "to put on") + shroud (meaning "a cloth or sheet in which a corpse is wrapped for burial"). The word "shroud" ultimately comes from the Old English scrūd, meaning "garment" or "clothing."


SYNONYMS->
veiled, covered, obscured, concealed, hidden, cloaked, masked, shrouded, enveloped


ANTONYMS->
revealed, exposed, uncovered, unveiled, open, clear, visible
