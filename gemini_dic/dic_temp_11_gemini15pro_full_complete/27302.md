calycifloral:/ˌkælɪsɪˈflɔːrəl/
adjective
萼筒花的；合萼花的
Having the petals and stamens attached to the calyx tube.  The calyx is the sepals of a flower, typically forming a whorl that encloses the petals and forms a protective layer around a flower in bud.
Roses and apples are examples of calycifloral plants. 玫瑰和苹果是萼筒花植物的例子。
近义词:gamospepalous, sympetalous
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps apetalous - lacking petals, as a contrasting floral structure)
词性:adjective
