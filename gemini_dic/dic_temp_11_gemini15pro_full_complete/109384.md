lenticel:/ˈlɛntɪsɛl/| n. |皮孔|A small raised pore in the stem of a woody plant that allows gases to exchange between the atmosphere and the internal tissues.|The lenticels on the cherry tree bark were clearly visible. 櫻桃樹樹皮上的皮孔清晰可見。|Synonym: pore, stoma|Antonym: N/A|noun
