convictive: /kənˈvɪktɪv/
adjective
令人信服的; 有说服力的
tending or serving to convict.  Producing conviction or belief.
例句：The prosecutor presented convictive evidence. 检察官提出了令人信服的证据。

例句：Her testimony was highly convictive. 她的证词非常有说服力。

近义词：persuasive, convincing, cogent
反义词：unconvincing, weak, dubious
词性: adjective
