auditual:/ɔːˈdɪtʃuəl/ | adj. | 听觉的 | Relating to the sense of hearing or perceived through the ear. |  The auditory nerve transmits signals from the inner ear to the brain.  听觉神经将信号从内耳传递到大脑。

| Example: Auditory processing disorder can make it difficult to understand speech. 听觉处理障碍会使理解言语变得困难。 | Synonym: aural, acoustic | Antonym: visual | Word type: adjective
