misrely: /ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪ/
verb
错误地依赖; 误信; 错信
To rely on someone or something mistakenly or to an excessive degree, especially when that reliance proves to be misplaced or leads to disappointment.
He misrelied on the information he received and made a costly mistake. 他错误地依赖了他收到的信息，犯了一个代价高昂的错误。
The company misrelied on outdated market research and launched a product that failed.  这家公司错误地依赖过时的市场研究，推出了一款失败的产品。
近义词: depend mistakenly, trust wrongly,  count on erroneously
反义词: trust wisely, rely correctly, depend appropriately
词性: verb (动词)

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "rely" (verb meaning "depend on with confidence")
