hypernormal:/ˌhaɪpərˈnɔːrməl/ | adj. | 超常的，极度正常的（看似正常，实则掩盖了潜在危机或异常） | Appearing normal or even exemplary, but masking a profound underlying abnormality or dysfunction.  |  例句：The calm before the storm felt hypernormal, like nothing was wrong, even though we knew the hurricane was coming. 暴风雨前的平静显得异常正常，好像一切都没问题，尽管我们知道飓风即将来临。

例句：Life in the totalitarian state was hypernormal; citizens went about their daily routines, pretending everything was fine, while living in constant fear. 极权主义国家的生活极度正常；公民们进行着他们的日常生活，假装一切都很好，却生活在持续的恐惧之中。 | 近义词：artificial, contrived, simulated, deceptive,  | 反义词：authentic, genuine, normal, natural | 词性: 形容词
