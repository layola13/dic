noncallable:/ˌnɒnˈkɔːləbl/
adjective
不可赎回的
Not able to be redeemed or paid off before maturity.
例句：Investors are attracted to noncallable bonds because they offer a guaranteed stream of income until maturity. 投资者被不可赎回债券所吸引，因为它们在到期前提供有保证的收入流。

例句：Noncallable preferred stock can be a stable investment but may offer limited upside potential.  不可赎回的优先股可以是一种稳定的投资，但可能提供有限的上涨潜力。

近义词：irredeemable, non-redeemable
反义词：callable, redeemable
词性：adjective
