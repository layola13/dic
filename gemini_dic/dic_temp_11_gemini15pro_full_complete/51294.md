detachers:/dɪˈtætʃərz/
n.|分离器，脱离器；拆卸器|People or things that detach something.  Those who disengage or separate something. |The detachers worked quickly to separate the rocket boosters.  分离器迅速地将火箭助推器分离。|separators, disconnectors|attachers, connectors|noun

detach:/dɪˈtætʃ/
v.|分离，拆卸；使摆脱|To separate (something) from something else. To become separated from something. To remove (someone) from a particular duty or place|He detached the trailer from the car. 他把拖车与汽车分离。|disconnect, disengage, separate|attach, connect, join|verb

detached:/dɪˈtætʃt/
adj.|分离的；超然的，不带感情的|Not emotionally involved; aloof. Separated.|She seemed detached and uninterested. 她似乎很冷漠，不感兴趣。|separate, aloof, indifferent|involved, attached, connected|adjective
