backpackers:/ˈbækˌpækərz/| n.|背包客|People who travel or hike carrying their belongings in a backpack.|例句：The hostel is popular with backpackers. 这家旅馆很受背包客欢迎。

例句：Many backpackers travel through Southeast Asia. 许多背包客在东南亚旅行。|近义词：hikers, travelers, globetrotters|反义词:residents, locals|词性:noun (plural)

词源: back + pack + -er + -s.  "Backpack" itself originates from the German "Rucksack".
