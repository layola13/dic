enable:/ɪˈneɪb(ə)l/ /ɛˈneɪb(ə)l/ | vt. | 使能够，使成为可能 | To make someone or something able to do something, or to make something possible. |例句：The software enables you to access the internet.  这个软件使你能够访问互联网。

例句：This new technology enables doctors to diagnose diseases more accurately. 这项新技术使医生能够更准确地诊断疾病。|近义词：allow, permit, facilitate, empower |反义词:disable, prevent, hinder |词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old French *enabler, from en- "in, on" + able "able, capable," from Latin habilis "handy, skillful," from habere "to have, to hold."
