/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.|

例句：
What is your name?
你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner?
你晚餐想吃什么？

近义词：which
反义词: (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word)
词性: pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb
词源: Old English *hwæt, neuter of hwā ‘who’
