monomial:/ˌmɑːnəˈnoʊmiəl/ | /ˌmɒnəˈnəʊmiəl/ | n. |单项式|A single term consisting of a product of numbers and variables with non-negative integer exponents.  An expression with only one term. | Example: 5x is a monomial. 5x是一个单项式。

Example:  7x²y³ is a monomial. 7x²y³是一个单项式。| 近义词: term | 反义词: polynomial | 词性: noun
