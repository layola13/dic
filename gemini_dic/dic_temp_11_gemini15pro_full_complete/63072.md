enroot:/ɪnˈruːt/ | v. | 使生根，使扎根；使牢固确立 | To establish deeply and firmly, as if by roots.  | The new CEO aimed to enroot the company's values in every employee. 新任首席执行官的目标是将公司的价值观根植于每位员工心中。 |  The organization worked to enroot democratic principles in the newly formed government.  该组织致力于在新成立的政府中确立民主原则。 | 近义词: establish, implant, fix, root | 反义词: uproot, dislodge | 词性: 动词

词源:
From en- + root.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in," "into," or "to put into."  "Root" refers to the part of a plant that attaches it to the ground. Therefore, "enroot" literally means "to put into roots."
