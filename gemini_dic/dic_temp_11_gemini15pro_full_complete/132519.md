nondeterministic:/ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非确定性的 | Not having a predictable outcome; random.  A system whose behavior is not fully predictable. |  The outcome of a nondeterministic algorithm depends on chance.  非确定性算法的结果取决于偶然性。

The weather is a nondeterministic system. 天气是一个非确定性系统。 | 近义词: stochastic, probabilistic, random | 反义词: deterministic, predictable | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "deterministic" (from "determine" + "-istic" (suffix forming adjectives)). "Determine" ultimately comes from Latin "determinare" (to set bounds to, limit).
