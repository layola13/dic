accommodators: /əˈkɒmədeɪtəz/ | n. |  调节者；适应者；提供住宿者 | People or organizations that provide lodging or adjust to the needs of others. | The hotel accommodators were very helpful during our stay.  这家酒店的接待人员在我们住宿期间非常乐于助人。 | synonyms: hosts, providers, facilitators | antonyms: hinderers, obstructors | noun

accommodators: /əˈkɒmədeɪtəz/ | n. |  适应者 | People who adapt or adjust to new situations or changes. |  The accommodators in the group quickly adjusted to the new schedule.  小组中的适应者很快适应了新的时间表。 | synonyms: adapters, adjusters | antonyms: nonconformists, resisters | noun
