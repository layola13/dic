blanch:/blɑːntʃ/ /blænʧ/| v. |漂白，使变白；使(脸色)变苍白；使(蔬菜)变脆；烫漂|To make white or pale by extracting color; to scald or parboil (vegetables) briefly.|She blanched the almonds before adding them to the cake. 她将杏仁漂白后再加入蛋糕中。

The frightening news caused her face to blanch.  这可怕的消息使她脸色苍白。|近义词：whiten, pale, bleach, scald, parboil|反义词: darken, color, flush|词性:动词

blanch:/blɑːntʃ/ /blænʧ/| n. |(冶金)退火，白化|The act or process of blanching.|The blanch of the metal removed impurities. 金属的退火去除了杂质。|近义词：whitening, paling|反义词: darkening|词性:名词
