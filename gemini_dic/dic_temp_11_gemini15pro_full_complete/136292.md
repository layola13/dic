nonrepresentatively:/nɒnˌreprɪzənˈteɪtɪvli/
adv. |非代表性地，不典型地| In a manner that does not accurately reflect or represent a larger group or whole. | The sample was chosen nonrepresentatively, leading to skewed results.  该样本选择不具有代表性，导致结果出现偏差。| synonym: atypically, uncharacteristically | antonym: representatively, typically
词性: 副词

nonrepresentative:/nɒnˌreprɪzənˈteɪtɪv/
adj. |非代表性的，不典型的| Not accurately reflecting or representing a larger group or whole. | The survey results were nonrepresentative of the population as a whole. 这项调查结果并不代表全体人口。| synonym: atypical, uncharacteristic | antonym: representative, typical
词性: 形容词

represent:/ˌreprɪˈzent/
v. |代表，象征| To stand for or symbolize something. | He was chosen to represent the company at the conference. 他被选中代表公司出席会议。| synonym: symbolize, embody | antonym: misrepresent, distort
词性: 动词

representation:/ˌreprɪzenˈteɪʃn/
n. |代表，象征，表现| The act of representing or the state of being represented. | The painting is a representation of a biblical scene. 这幅画描绘的是一个圣经场景。| synonym: portrayal, depiction | antonym: misrepresentation, distortion
词性: 名词

representative:/ˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/
adj. |代表性的，典型的| Serving as a typical example of something. | This painting is representative of his early work. 这幅画是他早期作品的代表。| synonym: typical, exemplary | antonym: atypical, uncharacteristic
n. |代表| A person chosen to speak or act on behalf of another person or group. |  She is a sales representative for a pharmaceutical company. 她是一家制药公司的销售代表。| synonym: agent, delegate | antonym: constituent
词性: 形容词，名词


词源: non- (not) + represent (to present again, stand for) + -ative (forming adjectives) + -ly (forming adverbs)
