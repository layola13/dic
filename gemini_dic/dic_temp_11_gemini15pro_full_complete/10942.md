preparatory:/prɪˈpærətɔːri/ /prɪˈpɛrətɔːri/
adjective
准备的，预备的
Relating to or denoting preparation or preparations.  Done or existing in preparation for something else.
例句：Preparatory work is needed before we start the project.  项目开始前需要做一些准备工作。

例句：He took a preparatory course before entering university.  他上大学前参加了一个预科课程。
近义词：preliminary, introductory, initial
反义词：concluding, final, subsequent
词性：adjective
