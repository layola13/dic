out-of-it:/ˌaʊt əv ˈɪt/ | adj. | 昏昏沉沉的；心不在焉的；不省人事的 | Not fully alert; dazed and confused.  Disconnected from reality. |  After the surgery, I felt completely out of it for a few hours. 手术后，我感觉昏昏沉沉了几个小时。

He seemed out of it during the meeting, like he hadn't slept in days.  他在会议期间似乎心不在焉，好像好几天没睡似的。 | 近义词: dazed, confused, disoriented, spaced-out, in a daze, foggy, groggy | 反义词: alert, focused, attentive, aware, present | 词性:形容词
