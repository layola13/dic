inspirationally:/ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənəli/
adverb
鼓舞人心地，启发地
In a way that inspires or motivates.
She spoke inspirationally about her journey. 她鼓舞人心地讲述了她的旅程。
The teacher presented the material inspirationally. 老师以启发性的方式讲解了教材。
近义词: motivatingly, encouragingly, upliftingly
反义词: discouragingly, depressingly


词性: adverb

词源:  From inspiration + -al + -ly.  "Inspiration" comes from Late Latin inspirationem (nominative inspiratio) "inbreathing, divine inspiration," noun of action from past-participle stem of inspirare "breathe or blow into," from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe."
