hyperconstitutional:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəl/
adj.
超越宪法的，极端维护宪法的
Excessively constitutional; upholding the constitution to an extreme degree.
The group's hyperconstitutional stance led them to oppose any changes to the original document.  该组织极端维护宪法的立场导致他们反对对原始文件的任何修改。
His hyperconstitutional interpretation of the law was met with skepticism. 他对法律的极端宪法解释遭到了质疑。
近义词:ultra-constitutional, strict constructionist
反义词: unconstitutional, anti-constitutional
词性:adjective

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, above, beyond  + "constitutional" (English): relating to a constitution
