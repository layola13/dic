fratted:/ˈfrætɪd/| adj.| 参加兄弟会的；像兄弟会成员的；兄弟会式的| Belonging to, resembling, or characteristic of a fraternity, especially in behavior or appearance. | He had that preppy, fratted look. 他看起来像个衣冠楚楚的兄弟会成员。| 近义词：fraternal | 反义词：independent, non-fraternal | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From fraternity + -ed.  The word "fraternity" comes from the Latin word "fraternitas," meaning "brotherhood."
