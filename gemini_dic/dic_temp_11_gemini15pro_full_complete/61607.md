emetine: /ˈemɪtiːn/ | n. | 吐根碱; 伊美汀 | An alkaloid extracted from ipecacuanha root, used as an emetic and expectorant, and in the treatment of amebiasis. | Emetine is a powerful drug and should only be used under medical supervision. 吐根碱是一种强效药物，只能在医生的监督下使用。| Emetine hydrochloride is sometimes prescribed for severe cases of amoebic dysentery. 盐酸依米丁有时用于治疗严重的阿米巴痢疾。 |Synonyms: ipecac, cephaeline | Antonyms: antiemetic | noun
