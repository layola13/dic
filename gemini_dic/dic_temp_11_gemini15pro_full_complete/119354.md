microclimate: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌklaɪmɪt/ | n. | 微气候；小气候 |  The climate of a small, specific place within a larger area, which differs from the climate of the surrounding area. |  The vineyard benefits from a unique microclimate that allows the grapes to ripen slowly.  这个葡萄园受益于独特的微气候，使葡萄缓慢成熟。 |  mesoclimate, local climate | macroclimate | noun
