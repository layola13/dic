laughable: /ˈlɑːfəbl/ (US) /ˈlɑːfəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可笑的，滑稽的|Something that is laughable is silly and causes laughter.  It implies that something is so ridiculous that it deserves to be mocked or ridiculed.|His attempt to fix the car was laughable. 他试图修理汽车的样子真是滑稽。|近义词：ridiculous, absurd, comical, amusing, hilarious|反义词：serious, solemn, grave|词性: adjective

词源:  From laugh + -able.  The suffix "-able" means "capable of being" or "worthy of being."  Therefore, laughable means "capable of being laughed at" or "worthy of laughter."
