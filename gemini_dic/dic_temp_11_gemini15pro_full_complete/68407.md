falses:/ˈfɔːlsɪz/ | n. | 错误，虚假，不正确 |  Plural form of false, referring to multiple instances of something that is not true or accurate. |  The report was full of falses and misrepresentations.  这份报告充满了错误和歪曲。 | synonyms: inaccuracies, untruths, lies | antonyms: truths, facts, realities | noun

false:/fɔːls/ | adj. | 错误的，虚假的，不真实的 | Not true or correct; artificial; deliberately misleading or deceptive. |  She gave false information to the police. 她向警方提供了虚假信息。 | synonyms: untrue, incorrect, erroneous, artificial, fake, counterfeit, spurious, deceptive, misleading, fallacious  | antonyms: true, correct, accurate, genuine, real, authentic | adjective
