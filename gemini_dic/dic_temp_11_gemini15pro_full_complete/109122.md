legibleness:/ˈledʒɪbəlnəs/
n.
清晰度，易读性
The legibleness of her handwriting is remarkable. 她的字迹非常清晰易读。
The legibleness of the document was crucial for the court case.  这份文件的清晰易读性对于法庭案件至关重要。
近义词:readability, clarity, clearness
反义词:illegibility, unclarity, obscurity
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *legiblenes, from Late Latin legibilis (“that can be read, legible”) + -ness.  Late Latin legibilis from Latin legō (“to read, gather, choose”) + -ibilis.
