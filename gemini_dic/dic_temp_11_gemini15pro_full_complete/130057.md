non-pluralistic:/ˌnɒnˈplʊərəˈlɪstɪk/ |adj.|非多元化的，非多元主义的|Not characterized by pluralism; lacking diversity of views, beliefs, or practices.  Rejecting the validity of views, beliefs, or practices other than one's own. |  The government's non-pluralistic approach to education stifled creativity and critical thinking. 政府在教育方面非多元化的做法扼杀了创造力和批判性思维。

The regime's non-pluralistic policies led to the suppression of dissenting voices. 该政权非多元化的政策导致了异议的压制。 | Near synonyms: monolithic, uniform, homogeneous, exclusive, intolerant, dogmatic| Antonyms: pluralistic, diverse, heterogeneous, inclusive, tolerant, open-minded |adjective
