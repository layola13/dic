licheniform:/laɪˈkɛnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
licheniform
Resembling a lichen in appearance.  Having a crust-like or branching growth similar to that of a lichen.
The licheniform growth on the rocks made them look ancient.  岩石上地衣状的生长物使它们看起来很古老。
crustose, foliose, leprose
smooth, glabrous
adjective
