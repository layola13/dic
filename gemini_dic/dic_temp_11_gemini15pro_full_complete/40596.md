convalescing: /ˌkɒnvəˈlesɪŋ/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesɪŋ/ (US)
verb
恢复健康；康复中
recovering one's health and strength over a period of time after an illness or operation.
She is convalescing at home after her operation. 她术后正在家康复。
He spent a month convalescing in the countryside. 他在乡下休养了一个月。
近义词: recuperating, recovering, healing
反义词: deteriorating, weakening, worsening
词性: verb (present participle)

convalescence: /ˌkɒnvəˈlesəns/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesəns/ (US)
noun
恢复期；康复期
the gradual recovery of health and strength after illness or injury.
She is now in convalescence after her surgery. 她现在正处于术后康复期。
He needed a period of convalescence after the accident. 事故发生后，他需要一段时间的康复。
近义词: recovery, recuperation, healing
反义词: relapse, deterioration
词性: noun

词源: From Latin convalescere, from con- 'together' + valescere 'grow strong'.
