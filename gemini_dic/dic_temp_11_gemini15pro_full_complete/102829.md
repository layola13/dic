jarringly:/ˈdʒɑːrɪŋli/ | adv. | 刺耳地，不和谐地 | In a harsh and unpleasant way, especially to the senses (particularly sound). | The music stopped jarringly, leaving an uncomfortable silence. 音乐戛然而止，留下令人不安的寂静。 | The bright pink walls clashed jarringly with the dark green carpet. 亮粉色的墙壁与深绿色的地毯产生了刺耳的冲突。 | Synonyms: discordantly, gratingly, harshly, stridently | Antonyms: harmoniously, melodically, smoothly, soothingly | 词性: 副词
