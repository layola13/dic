accident-proneness:/ˈæksɪdəntˌproʊn.nəs/| n.|事故倾向，易发生事故|A tendency to have accidents; the characteristic of having more accidents than is normal.|He was let go from his job due to his accident-proneness. 他因为容易发生事故而被解雇了。|近义词：accident susceptibility, proneness to accidents|反义词: carefulness, cautiousness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From accident + prone + -ness.  "Prone" comes from Latin "pronus" meaning "leaning forward, inclined."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
