axled:/ˈæksəld/
adjective
有轴的；装了轴的
Having an axle or axles.
The axled vehicle moved smoothly down the road.  这辆有轴的车辆在路上平稳地行驶。
axled cart  有轴的推车
近义词: wheeled
反义词: axleless (罕见)
词性: adjective

axled:/ˈæksəld/
verb (past participle)
给…装轴
Equipped with an axle.
The cart was axled and ready to go.  这辆推车装上了轴，准备出发。
The newly axled wagon was much easier to pull. 新装上轴的货车更容易拉了。
近义词: fitted with an axle
反义词: unaxled (罕见)
词性: verb (past participle)
