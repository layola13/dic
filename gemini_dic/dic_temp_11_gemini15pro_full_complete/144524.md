outvalued:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuːd/
verb
超越…的价值; 比…更有价值
surpass in value.
例句：The company's assets have outvalued its liabilities. 该公司的资产价值已超过其负债。

例句：His contributions to the team have greatly outvalued his initial salary expectations. 他对团队的贡献远远超过了他最初的薪资预期。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outshine, eclipse
反义词：undervalue, underestimate

词性:v.
