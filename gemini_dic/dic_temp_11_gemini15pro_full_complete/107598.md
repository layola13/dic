landslide:/ˈlændslaɪd/| n.|山体滑坡；压倒性胜利|A sudden and rapid movement of a large mass of earth and rocks down a slope; an overwhelming victory.|例句：The heavy rain caused a landslide. 大雨引发了山体滑坡。

landslide:/ˈlændslaɪd/| n.|压倒性胜利|An overwhelming victory, especially in an election.|例句：The election resulted in a landslide victory for the ruling party. 这次选举导致执政党取得压倒性胜利。

landslide:/ˈlændslaɪd/| n.|滑坡|The mass of earth and rocks that slides down a slope.|例句：The landslide buried the village. 滑坡掩埋了村庄。


近义词：avalanche, rockslide, mudslide, landslide victory|反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"close election" 表示选举结果接近)|词性:noun
