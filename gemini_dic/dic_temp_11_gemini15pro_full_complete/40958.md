cooperative:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪv/ /kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/| adj. n.|合作的，协作的；合作社|Involving mutual assistance in working toward a common goal.  A farm, business, or other organization that is owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits.|例句：The project was a cooperative effort between several organizations. 这个项目是几个组织合作的成果。|近义词：collaborative, joint, combined|反义词：uncooperative, individualistic, competitive|词性:形容词

cooperative:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪv/ /kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/| n.|合作社|A farm, business, or other organization that is owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits.|例句：She is a member of a housing cooperative. 她是住房合作社的成员。|近义词：collective, commune|反义词：corporation, conglomerate|词性:名词
