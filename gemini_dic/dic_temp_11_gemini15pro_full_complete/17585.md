beauticians:/bjuːˈtɪʃənz/| n. |美容师|A person, whose job is to give beauty treatments, such as doing people's hair, nails, and makeup.|例句：The beauticians at the spa were very skilled.  水疗中心的美容师技艺精湛。

例句：Many beauticians now offer eyelash extensions. 很多美容师现在提供睫毛嫁接服务。|近义词：cosmeticians, aestheticians|反义词: None|Noun
