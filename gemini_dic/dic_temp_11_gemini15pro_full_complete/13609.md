attestative:/əˈtɛstətɪv/
adjective
证明的，证实的
Serving to attest; affirmative; declaratory.
The lawyer presented attestative documents to prove his client's innocence. 律师出示了证明文件来证明他的客户是无辜的。
The witness provided attestative evidence in court.  证人在法庭上提供了证词。
synonyms: confirmatory, corroborative, validating, substantiating
antonyms:  disproving, refuting, contradicting, invalidating
词性: adjective
