calloused:/ˈkæləst/ /ˈkæləsɪd/| adj.|长满老茧的；冷淡无情的；麻木不仁的|Made hard or thick, especially from manual labor; emotionally hardened; insensitive.|His calloused hands showed years of hard work. 他的长满老茧的双手表明他多年辛苦劳作。|近义词：hardened, toughened, insensitive, unfeeling|反义词：soft, tender, sensitive, compassionate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From callus + -ed.  "Callus" comes from Latin callum "hard skin."
