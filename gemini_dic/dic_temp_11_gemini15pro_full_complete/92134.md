hypersecretions:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈkriːʃənz/
|n.|过度分泌；分泌过多|The excessive production and release of a bodily secretion.  This can refer to various substances like hormones, mucus, or gastric acid.|The patient's hypersecretions of mucus caused respiratory problems. 病人的粘液过度分泌导致了呼吸问题。|hyperproduction, oversecretion|hyposecretion|noun
