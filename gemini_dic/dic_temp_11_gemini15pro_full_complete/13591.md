attenuated:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪtɪd/ | adj. |减弱的，衰减的|Reduced in force, effect, or value; weakened.|The virus was attenuated in the laboratory. 病毒在实验室中被减弱了。|近义词：weakened, diminished, reduced, lessened, abated, subsided|反义词：strengthened, amplified, intensified, increased, boosted|词性：形容词
attenuate:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪt/ | v. |使减弱，使衰减|To reduce the force, effect, or value of; to weaken.|The drug attenuates the symptoms of the disease. 这种药物减轻了疾病的症状。|近义词：weaken, diminish, reduce, lessen, abate, subside|反义词：strengthen, amplify, intensify, increase, boost|词性：动词
attenuation:/əˌtɛnjuˈeɪʃən/ | n. |减弱，衰减|The reduction of the force, effect, or value of something.|The attenuation of the signal made it difficult to hear. 信号的衰减使得难以听到。|近义词：weakening, diminution, reduction, lessening, abatement, subsidence|反义词：strengthening, amplification, intensification, increase, boost|词性：名词

词源: From Latin attenuātus, past participle of attenuāre (“to make thin, weaken”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + tenuāre (“to make thin”), from tenuis (“thin”).
