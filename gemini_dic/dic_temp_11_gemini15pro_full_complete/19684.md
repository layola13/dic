bildstein:/ˈbɪldʃtaɪn/| n. |界石，里程碑，雕像|A sculpted stone, often used as a landmark or boundary marker. It can also refer to a statue.|The old bildstein marked the boundary between the two properties.  那块古老的界石标志着两处房产的边界。|Near the entrance to the village stood a bildstein of a local hero. 在村庄入口附近矗立着一座当地英雄的雕像。|Synonyms: landmark, boundary stone, milestone, statue, monument|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
