deliberators:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtərz/
n.
审议者；商讨者；评议员
People who engage in deliberation, carefully considering and discussing something before making a decision.
The deliberators spent hours weighing the evidence before reaching a verdict.  评议员们花了数小时权衡证据才做出裁决。
The deliberators argued passionately for their respective positions.  商讨者们热情地为各自的立场辩护。
Synonyms: discussants, panelists, jurors, judges, arbitrators
Antonyms:  impulsive people,  rash people
