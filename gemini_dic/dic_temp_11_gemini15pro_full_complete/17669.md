become:/bɪˈkʌm/ (UK) /bɪˈkʌm/ (US)| v.| 变得；成为 |To undergo a change or development; to grow to be. |She became a doctor after years of study. 她经过多年的学习成了一名医生。

He became very angry when he heard the news. 当他听到这个消息时，他变得非常生气。

近义词：get, turn, grow, develop
反义词: remain, stay
词性: 动词

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
The president is becoming increasingly unpopular. 总统越来越不受欢迎。（主动语态，become 一般不用于被动语态）

疑问句:
What will become of him? 他会怎么样？
How did she become so successful? 她是怎么变得如此成功的？

否定句:
He did not become a doctor, he became a lawyer. 他没有成为医生，他成了一名律师。
She will not become famous overnight. 她不会一夜成名。
