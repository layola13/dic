discontinuation's:/ˌdɪskənˌtɪnjuˈeɪʃənz/ | n. |中止，停止，中断|The discontinuation's impact on the project was significant.  这个项目的中止带来了重大的影响。| The discontinuation's of the drug caused withdrawal symptoms. 停止服用该药物引起了戒断症状。| cessation, termination, suspension | continuation, resumption | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English discontinuation, from Late Latin discontinuationem, accusative of discontinuatio (“interruption, break”), from discontinuatus, past participle of discontinuare (“to interrupt, to break off”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + continuāre (“to continue”).
