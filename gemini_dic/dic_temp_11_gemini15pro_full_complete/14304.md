automat:/ˈɔːtəmæt/ (US) /ˈɔːtəmæt/ (UK)| n. |自动售货机; 自动装置|A machine that automatically dispenses food, drinks, or other items when money is inserted.|例句：I bought a candy bar from the automat. 我从自动售货机买了一块糖果。|例句：The automat dispensed a cup of coffee after I inserted the coins. 我投币后，自动售货机出来一杯咖啡。|近义词：vending machine, dispenser|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly manual machine)|词性:noun
