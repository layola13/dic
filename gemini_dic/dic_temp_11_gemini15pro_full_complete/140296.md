ogams:/ˈoʊɡəmz/ | n. | 欧甘文 | An early form of writing used in Ireland, consisting of a series of notches cut into a rod or stone. | Example: The ogams on the standing stone were difficult to decipher. 这块立石上的欧甘文很难破译。 | Example:  Researchers are studying the ogams to understand more about early Irish culture. 研究人员正在研究欧甘文，以了解更多关于早期爱尔兰文化的信息。 | Synonym:  inscriptions, runes | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a writing system) | noun
