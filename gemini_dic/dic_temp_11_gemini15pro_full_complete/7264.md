anglophiles:/ˈæŋɡləfaɪlz/
n.
亲英派；喜爱英国文化者
A person who greatly admires or favors England and its people and customs.
Many anglophiles in the US try to emulate British accents.  美国许多亲英派试图模仿英国口音。

Many anglophiles enjoy drinking tea and watching period dramas. 许多喜爱英国文化的人都喜欢喝茶和看古装剧。
admirer of England, lover of British culture
Anglophobe
noun
