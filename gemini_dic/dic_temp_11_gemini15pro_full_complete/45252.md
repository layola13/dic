cultishness:/ˈkʌltɪʃnəs/
n.
狂热；邪教性质；膜拜
The quality of being cultish; excessive devotion to a person, group, or idea.
The band has a certain cultishness that draws in dedicated fans.  这个乐队有一种狂热的性质，吸引了大批忠实粉丝。
Her intense interest in the obscure author bordered on cultishness. 她对这位默默无闻的作家的强烈兴趣近乎狂热。
近义词: zealotry, fanaticism, obsession
反义词: apathy, indifference, moderation
词性: noun

Etymology:
From cultish + -ness.  Cultish is derived from cult + -ish.  Cult ultimately comes from Latin cultus "worship, cultivation,"  while -ish is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "having the character of."  -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
