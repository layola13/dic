mettles:/ˈmɛtlz/| n.|勇气，精神，气质|Mettle is spirit and resilience.|例句：The team showed their true mettle in the final minutes of the game.  这支队伍在比赛的最后几分钟展现了他们的真正实力。

例句：It takes mettle to stand up to a bully.  对抗恶霸需要勇气。|近义词：courage, spirit, fortitude, resolve, grit|反义词：cowardice, timidity, fearfulness|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *metel, from Old French metal, from Latin metallum (“metal, mine”).  The sense shifted from the hardness of metal to the "stuff" someone is made of. 最初来源于中古英语 *metel，来自古法语 metal，源自拉丁语 metallum（“金属，矿山”）。  含义从金属的硬度转变为某人是由什么“材料”构成的。
