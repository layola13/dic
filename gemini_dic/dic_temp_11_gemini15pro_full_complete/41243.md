coranto:/kəˈræntoʊ/ /kəˈrɑːntoʊ/
noun
库朗特舞曲；库朗特舞；（17世纪欧洲的）新闻期刊
A lively, fast dance and musical form of the Renaissance and Baroque periods, typically in triple time.  Also, a 17th-century European news periodical.
The musicians played a lively coranto.  音乐家们演奏了一首活泼的库朗特舞曲。
The coranto contained news from all over Europe.  这份新闻期刊包含了来自全欧洲的新闻。
近义词:courante, news sheet, gazette, periodical
反义词:(For the dance) slow dance, waltz  (For the news sheet) none
词性:n.
