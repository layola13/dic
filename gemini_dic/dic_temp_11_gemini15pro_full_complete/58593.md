dulia:/ˈduːliə/ | n. | 敬礼; 恭敬 | Veneration of a saint or other holy person.  It is a religious devotion directed to saints and angels, acknowledging their holiness and asking for their intercessions before God.  |  Catholics practice dulia to saints. 天主教徒对圣徒行敬礼。| 近义词: veneration, reverence, respect | 反义词: disrespect, contempt | 词性: noun

词源:
From Late Latin *dūlīa, from Ancient Greek δουλεία (douleía, “slavery, servitude, service”), from δοῦλος (doûlos, “slave”).
