non-referential:/ˌnɒnˌrɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/ /ˌnɑːnˌrɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 非指称的，无指对象的 | Not referring to a specific person, thing, or event in the real world; used to describe words or expressions that do not have a concrete referent. |  The word "it" in the sentence "It's raining" is non-referential.  这句话中的“it”是非指称的。 | Synonyms: dummy, expletive, pleonastic | Antonyms: referential, specific, concrete | word property
