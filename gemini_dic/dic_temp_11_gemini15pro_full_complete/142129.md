originalist:/əˈrɪdʒɪnəlɪst/
n.|原旨主义者；文本主义者|A person who interprets a text, especially the Constitution of the U.S., based on what they believe was its original meaning at the time it was written.|Example: The originalist argued that the Second Amendment protects an individual's right to own guns, regardless of militia service. 原旨主义者认为，第二修正案保护个人拥有枪支的权利，无论是否服兵役。

Example: Some originalists believe the Constitution's meaning is fixed and unchanging.  一些原旨主义者认为宪法的含义是固定不变的。
|近义词：textualist, strict constructionist|反义词:living constitutionalist|词性:名词
