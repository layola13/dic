exorbitantly:/ɪɡˈzɔːrbɪtəntli/
adverb
过高地，过分地
in a way that is much too expensive
例句：The rooms at the hotel were exorbitantly priced.  这家酒店的房间价格过高。

例句：They charge exorbitantly for even the simplest things. 即使是最简单的东西，他们也要价过高。

近义词：excessively, unreasonably, ridiculously
反义词：reasonably, moderately, affordably
词性：adverb
