geothermal:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈθɜːrməl/
adjective
地热的
Of, relating to, or produced by the internal heat of the earth.
例句：Geothermal energy is a sustainable resource. 地热能是一种可持续资源。

例句：Iceland uses geothermal power for heating and electricity. 冰岛利用地热能来供暖和发电。

近义词：volcanic, thermal
反义词：frigid

词性: adjective
