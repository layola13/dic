ethynes:/ˈɛθaɪnz/
n.
乙炔类
A class of unsaturated hydrocarbons characterized by the presence of a triple bond between two carbon atoms.
Acetylene (ethyne) is the simplest example of an ethyne.  乙炔（炔烃）是最简单的乙炔类化合物。
Ethyne is used in welding torches. 乙炔用于焊接炬。
近义词:alkynes
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps alkanes or alkenes as they represent different hydrocarbon saturation levels)
词性:noun
