blotted:/ˈblɒtɪd/ (UK) /ˈblɑːtɪd/ (US)| adj.|弄脏的；吸干墨水的；喝醉的|Covered with or stained by blots; having absorbed ink; drunk, intoxicated.|The desk was blotted with ink. 桌子上沾满了墨水。|Near synonyms: stained, smeared, spotted, inky, drunk, intoxicated|Near antonyms: clean, spotless, sober|adjective

blot:/blɒt/ (UK) /blɑːt/ (US)| n. vt.|污点，墨渍；吸墨纸；缺点；涂污，吸干|a spot or stain, especially of ink; a piece of blotting paper; a mark of disgrace or discredit; to dry with absorbent paper, to stain|He used a blotter to absorb the excess ink. 他用吸墨纸吸干多余的墨水。|Near synonyms: stain, spot, blemish, blotch, smear|Near antonyms: cleanliness, spotlessness|noun, verb
