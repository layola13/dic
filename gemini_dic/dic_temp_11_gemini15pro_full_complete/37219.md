comeuppance:/ˌkʌmˈʌpəns/ | n. |报应；应得的惩罚|A punishment or fate that someone deserves, especially for having done something bad or immoral. |  After years of bullying others, he finally got his comeuppance when he was expelled from school. 多年欺凌他人之后，他最终因被学校开除而得到了报应。

After cheating on the exam, she feared the comeuppance that awaited her. 考试作弊后，她害怕即将到来的惩罚。| 近义词: retribution, deserts, just deserts, punishment, payback, reckoning | 反义词: reward, benefit, advantage | 词性: 名词
