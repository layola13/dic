interspecific:/ˌɪntərspəˈsɪfɪk/ | adj. | 种间的，不同物种间的 | Existing or occurring between different species. | Interspecific competition for resources can shape community structure.  不同物种之间对资源的竞争可以塑造群落结构。| Interspecific hybridization can sometimes occur in nature.  不同物种间的杂交有时会在自然界发生。| synonyms: interspecies | antonyms: intraspecific | adjective

Etymology:
From inter- (between) + specific (species).  The prefix "inter-" is derived from Latin, meaning "between" or "among." "Specific" comes from Latin "species," meaning "kind" or "sort."
