dulcetnesses:/ˈdʌlsɪtnɪsɪz/
n.
甜美; 悦耳; 柔和
The quality of being dulcet; melodious sweetness.
The dulcetnesses of the birds' songs filled the air. 鸟儿甜美的歌声充满了空气。
近义词: sweetness, melodiousness, euphony, harmony
反义词: harshness, dissonance, cacophony
词性: 名词 (plural of dulcetness)

dulcet:/ˈdʌlsɪt/
adj.
甜美的; 悦耳的; 柔和的
Sweet and soothing (especially of sound).
The dulcet tones of the piano filled the room.  钢琴悦耳的声音充满了房间。
近义词: sweet, melodious, soothing, pleasant
反义词: harsh, grating, jarring, strident
词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *dulcitās, from dulcis (“sweet”).
