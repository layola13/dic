outsmart:/ˌaʊtˈsmɑːrt/
verb
智胜，比…更聪明
To defeat or get an advantage over someone by being more clever or cunning.
He outsmarted his opponents in the negotiation. 他在谈判中智胜了对手。
Synonyms: outwit, outfox, outmaneuver
Antonyms: be outsmarted, be fooled, be tricked
词性: 动词
