okapi:/oʊˈkɑːpi/ | n. | 霍加狓 |A central African mammal (Okapia johnstoni) related to and resembling the giraffe but smaller and with shorter legs, striped hindquarters, and a dark purplish-brown color.| Okapis are native to the dense rainforests of central Africa. 霍加狓原产于非洲中部茂密的雨林。|Synonyms:  forest giraffe, zebra giraffe | Antonyms: N/A | noun
