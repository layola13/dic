formations:/fɔːrˈmeɪʃənz/ /fɔːrˈmeɪʃnz/
noun
形成，构成；组成物；结构；队形
the action of forming or process of being formed.
Example: The rock formations in the park are stunning. 公园里的岩层构造令人叹为观止。

Example:  Cloud formations can tell us a lot about the weather. 云的形成可以告诉我们很多关于天气的信息。

Synonym: development, creation, establishment, structure, organization
Antonym: destruction, demolition, disintegration, disorganization
