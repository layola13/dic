The ice cream shop was full of lickers enjoying their cones.  冰淇淋店里挤满了正在享用蛋筒的顾客。 |近义词：flatterers, sycophants, yes-men (for the figurative meaning); enthusiasts, fans (for the meaning related to enjoyment)|反义词：critics, dissenters (for the figurative meaning)|词性:名词
