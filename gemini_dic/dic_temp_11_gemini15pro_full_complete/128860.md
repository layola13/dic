nihility:/naɪˈhɪləti/ /nɪˈhɪləti/ | n. |虚无；空虚；不存在的状态|The concept of nihility is difficult to grasp. 虚无的概念很难理解。|例句：

The philosopher contemplated the nihility of existence. 这位哲学家思考存在的虚无。

She felt a sense of nihility after her life's work was destroyed. 在她毕生的工作被毁之后，她感到一种虚无感。

|近义词：nothingness, void, emptiness, nullity|反义词:existence, being, reality|词性:名词
