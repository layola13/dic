barge:/bɑːrdʒ/ | n. |驳船；大型游艇|A flat-bottomed boat for carrying freight, typically on canals and rivers, or a large pleasure boat. |例句：The barge carried coal along the canal. 这艘驳船沿着运河运煤。


barge:/bɑːrdʒ/ | vi. | 笨拙地闯入；撞|To move forcefully or clumsily; to interrupt rudely or intrusively. |例句：He barged into the meeting without knocking. 他没敲门就闯进了会议室。


barges (plural of barge):/bɑːrdʒɪz/ | n. |驳船（复数）；大型游艇（复数）|Plural form of "barge". |例句：Several barges were tied up along the riverbank. 几艘驳船系在河岸边。



词源:  From Old French "barge," a type of boat, probably of Gaulish origin.

近义词：(n.) boat, vessel, ship, lighter (for freight barges); yacht, cruiser (for pleasure barges) (v.)  intrude, interrupt, burst, storm

反义词:  No direct antonym for the noun form.  For the verb form:  sneak, tiptoe, withdraw

词性:  Noun (n.), Verb (vi.)
