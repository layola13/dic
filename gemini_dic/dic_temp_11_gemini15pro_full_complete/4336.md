akees:/əˈkiːz/ | n. |西非荔枝果；阿开木果|The ackee fruit is the national fruit of Jamaica.  西非荔枝果是牙买加的国果。|例句：Ackee and saltfish is a popular Jamaican dish.  西非荔枝果和咸鱼是一道受欢迎的牙买加菜。|近义词： (As a fruit, no direct synonym, but could be grouped with tropical fruits like) lychees, rambutans|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology: From the Twi word *akyi*.  源自特威语单词 *akyi*。
