cicatricial:/sɪkəˈtrɪʃəl/| adj.|瘢痕性的|Relating to, or characterized by a scar or cicatrix.  Marked by or resembling scarring.|The cicatricial tissue was thick and inflexible. 瘢痕组织厚而僵硬。|Synonyms: scarred, scarry|Antonyms: unscarred, smooth|adjective

cicatrix:/sɪˈkeɪtrɪks/| n.|瘢痕|A scar of a healed wound.|The surgeon pointed out the cicatrix from his appendectomy.  外科医生指出了他阑尾切除术留下的疤痕。|Synonyms: scar, mark|Antonyms: unscarred skin|noun
