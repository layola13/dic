goodiness:/ˈɡʊdnɪs/
noun
善良；美德；精华；美味
The quality of being good, virtuous, or kind.  It can also refer to the essence or best part of something, or to something delicious.
She was known for her goodness and generosity. 她以善良和慷慨闻名。
The goodness of the soup was undeniable. 这汤的美味不容置疑。
Synonyms: virtue, kindness, benevolence, excellence, merit, deliciousness, flavor
Antonyms: badness, evil, wickedness, malevolence, awfulness
