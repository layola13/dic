desertedness:/dɪˈzɜːtɪdnəs/
n.
荒凉；空旷；被遗弃的状态
The state of being deserted or abandoned; emptiness.
The desertedness of the island was eerie.  这个岛的荒凉令人毛骨悚然。
solitude, emptiness, desolation, barrenness
population, liveliness, bustle
