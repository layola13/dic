becomingly:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋli/
adverb
适当地；得体地；相称地
in a way that suits the wearer or the occasion; attractively.
She was dressed becomingly in a simple black dress. 她穿着一件简单的黑色连衣裙，显得十分得体。
Synonyms: attractively, suitably, appropriately, gracefully, tastefully
Antonyms: inappropriately, unsuitably, awkwardly, gracelessly, tastefully
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From become + -ing + -ly.  "Become" traces back to Old English "becuman," meaning "to arrive, happen, befall." The suffix "-ing" forms a present participle, and "-ly" transforms the word into an adverb.
