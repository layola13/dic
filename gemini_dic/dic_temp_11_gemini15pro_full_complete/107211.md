词源:
From Old English *lecgan, from Proto-Germanic *lagjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *legh-  "to lay, lie, put."  Related to lie (verb).


近义词: place, put, set, deposit (for placing objects)
反义词: raise, lift (opposite of placing down)
词性: verb, adjective
