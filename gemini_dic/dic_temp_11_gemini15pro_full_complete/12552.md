aspirating:/ˈæspəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 吸气的；送气的；有抱负的 | Drawing breath in; characterized by aspiration of breath; having strong desires to achieve something. |例句：The aspirating sound of the 'h' in "house".  “house”中“h”的送气音。| 近义词: inhaling, ambitious, aspiring | 反义词: exhaling, unambitious | 词性: adjective
aspirating:/ˈæspəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |  吸入；抽出；送气 | To draw (fluid) by suction from a vessel or cavity; to pronounce with an exhalation of breath. | 例句：The doctor aspirated the fluid from the patient's lungs. 医生从病人的肺部抽出液体。 | 近义词: inhale, suck, withdraw, extract | 反义词: exhale, inject | 词性: verb (present participle)

词源: From Latin *aspirare, present active infinitive of aspiro (“I breathe upon, aspire to”).
