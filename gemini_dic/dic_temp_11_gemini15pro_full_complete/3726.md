aggrievednesses:/əˈɡriːvdnəsɪz/
n.
委屈；冤屈；不满
The state of being aggrieved; a feeling of having been wronged or unfairly treated.
He nursed his aggrievednesses for years.  他多年来一直怀恨在心。
近义词:grievances, resentments, complaints
反义词:contentment, satisfaction
