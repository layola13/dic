Etymology:

* non-: Prefix meaning "not." From Latin *non*.
* frugal: From Latin *frugalis* "economical, sparing, temperate," related to *frux* "fruit, profit, value."
* -ness: Suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality. From Old English *-nes*.
