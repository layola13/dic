outmanoeuvre:/ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/ /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/
verb
以策略取胜，智胜
to defeat someone by being more clever or skillful in your plans and actions
The company outmanoeuvred its rivals by launching a new product first.  这家公司通过率先推出新产品战胜了竞争对手。
