overhardened:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnd/
adjective
过度硬化的
Made too hard, especially by excessive tempering or heat treatment.
The overhardened steel became brittle and prone to cracking.  过度硬化的钢变得很脆，容易开裂。
The blacksmith overhardened the sword, making it too brittle for battle.  铁匠把剑过度硬化了，使它太脆，不适合战斗。
synonyms: brittle, excessively hardened
antonyms: soft, malleable
word type: adjective
