infested:/ɪnˈfɛstɪd/ | adj. | 遍布的；充斥的；受……侵扰的 | Overrun or swarming with pests or parasites.  |  The house was infested with rats. 这房子里到处都是老鼠。 |  The old mattress was infested with bed bugs.  旧床垫上爬满了臭虫。| 近义词: overrun, swarming, plagued, ridden | 反义词: clean, clear, uncontaminated | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *infestare, frequentative of infestus "hostile, unsafe," related to inferus "low" (see inferior).

PHRASE->
infested with (充满...): The ship was infested with rats.  这艘船上老鼠成灾。
heavily infested (严重滋生): The area was heavily infested with mosquitoes.  这个地区蚊子滋生严重。
become infested (变得滋生): The house became infested with termites.  这房子里滋生了白蚁。

rid of infestation (清除侵扰): They tried to rid the house of the rat infestation. 他们试图清除房子里的老鼠。
