deerlike:/ˈdɪrˌlaɪk/
adjective
像鹿的；似鹿的
Resembling a deer in appearance or behavior.
The small, deerlike creature darted across the road.  这小小的、像鹿一样的生物飞快地穿过马路。
The child had large, deerlike eyes.  这孩子有一双像鹿一样的大眼睛。
synonyms: graceful, gentle, timid, delicate, elegant
antonyms: clumsy, awkward, aggressive, bold
词性: adjective
