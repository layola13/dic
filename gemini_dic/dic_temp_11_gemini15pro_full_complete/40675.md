conversation:/ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃn/ | /ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 交谈，谈话 |  An informal talk involving two or more people, often on a particular topic. | We had a long conversation about our future plans. 我们就未来的计划进行了长时间的交谈。

conversation:/ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃn/ | /ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 交谈，谈话 | The act of talking together. |  I struck up a conversation with the woman sitting next to me on the plane. 我和坐在我旁边的女士攀谈起来。

近义词: talk, discussion, dialogue, chat
反义词: silence, quiet

词性: noun
