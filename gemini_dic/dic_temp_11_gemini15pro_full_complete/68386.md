fallow:/ˈfæloʊ/ (US), /ˈfæləʊ/ (UK)| adj. n. v. |休耕的；休闲的； fallow land 休耕地|left unplanted; plowed but not seeded; inactive; dormant; to plow land but not sow it for a season|例句：The farmer left the field fallow this year to improve the soil. 今年农民让这块地休耕以改良土壤。|近义词：uncultivated, idle, dormant, quiescent|反义词：cultivated, active, productive|词性:形容词

fallow:/ˈfæloʊ/ (US), /ˈfæləʊ/ (UK)| n. |休耕地；休闲期|land plowed and harrowed but left unsown for a period in order to restore its fertility as part of a crop rotation or to avoid surplus production.|例句：The fallow fields stretched as far as the eye could see.  休耕地一望无际。|近义词：idle land|反义词：cultivated land|词性:名词

fallow:/ˈfæloʊ/ (US), /ˈfæləʊ/ (UK)| v. |使休耕；使休闲|to plow land but not sow it for a season|例句：They decided to fallow the north field next year. 他们决定明年让北边的田地休耕。|近义词：rest, lie fallow|反义词：cultivate, plant|词性:动词
