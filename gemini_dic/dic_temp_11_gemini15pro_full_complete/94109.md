impeachments:/ɪmˈpiːtʃmənts/| n. |弹劾；控告；检举|The act of formally accusing a public official of misconduct.  The process of charging a public official with a crime or misdemeanor. |The president faced two impeachments during his time in office. 总统在任期间面临两次弹劾。|Synonyms: accusations, charges, indictments |Antonyms: acquittals, exonerations | Noun, plural
