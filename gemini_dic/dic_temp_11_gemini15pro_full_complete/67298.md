exspoliation: /ɛksˌpoʊliˈeɪʃən/
n.
掠夺，剥夺，搜刮
The act of seizing or depriving someone of property, possessions, or rights, typically by force or abuse of authority.
The exspoliation of the country's natural resources by foreign companies.  外国公司对该国自然资源的掠夺。
The exspoliation of cultural artifacts during wartime.  战时对文化文物的掠夺。
Synonym: plunder, pillage, despoilment, deprivation, confiscation
Antonym: restitution, restoration, compensation
noun
