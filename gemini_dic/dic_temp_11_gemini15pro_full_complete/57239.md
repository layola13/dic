downier:/ˈdaʊniər/| adj.|更柔软的，绒毛更多的|Covered in soft, fine down; more downy.  Like a softer, fluffier version of something already downy. |The baby bird had downier feathers than its siblings. 这只雏鸟的羽毛比它的兄弟姐妹更柔软。| The blanket felt downier after being washed.  这条毯子洗过之后感觉更柔软了。|近义词：softer, fluffier, plusher|反义词：coarser, rougher|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "down" (n.) referring to soft, fine feathers or hair, + "-y" (adj. suffix) forming "downy," + "-er" (comparative suffix) forming "downier."  Ultimately from Old Norse "dúnn."
