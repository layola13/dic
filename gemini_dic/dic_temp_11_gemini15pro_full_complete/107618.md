lanes:/leɪnz/
n.
车道，小巷，航线
narrow passageways between buildings or hedges.  A division of a road marked off with painted lines and intended to separate single lines of traffic. A route followed by ships or aircraft.
The car drifted out of its lane. 汽车偏离了车道。
The plane left the designated lane. 飞机离开了指定的航线。
We strolled down the quiet country lanes. 我们沿着安静的乡间小路漫步。
synonyms: alleys, paths, routes, roadways, courses
antonyms: fields, open areas, plains
