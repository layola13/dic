fahlband:/ˈfɑːlbænd/ | n. | 断层泥，断层带 | A zone of crushed and altered rock along a fault, often containing sulfide minerals. | The fahlband contained significant amounts of copper ore.  断层带含有大量的铜矿石。| Synonym: shear zone, fault zone | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "unaltered rock") | Noun

fahlband:/ˈfɑːlbænd/ | n. | 断层泥，断层带 | A zone of crushed rock within a larger geological formation, often mineralized and readily eroded, forming a linear depression. |  Erosion of the softer fahlband created a valley in the landscape.  较软的断层带的侵蚀在景观中形成了一个山谷。| Synonym: mineralized zone, crush zone | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "unmineralized rock") | Noun
