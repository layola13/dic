mudflaps: /ˈmʌdflæps/ (US), /ˈmʌdflæps/ (UK)| n. |挡泥板|A flap, usually made of rubber or plastic, behind a wheel of a vehicle to protect the vehicle and following vehicles from mud or other debris thrown up by the rotating tire.|The truck's mudflaps helped to keep the road behind it clear.|近义词：splash guards, fender skirts|反义词: N/A|名词
