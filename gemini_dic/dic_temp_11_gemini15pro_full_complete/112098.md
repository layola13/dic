longitudinals:/ˌlɒndʒɪˈtjuːdɪnəlz/ | n. |纵向构件，纵梁；纵向研究|  Structures or members that run lengthwise, or studies conducted over a long period of time. | The bridge's longitudinals provide essential support. 这座桥的纵梁提供了必要的支撑。|  The researchers conducted longitudinals on the effects of pollution.  研究人员对污染的影响进行了纵向研究。 | synonyms: lengthwise members, longitudinal studies | antonyms: transverse members, cross-sectional studies | noun

longitudinals:/ˌlɒndʒɪˈtjuːdɪnəlz/ | adj. |纵向的|  Relating to or extending along the length of something. | The ship's longitudinal axis ran from bow to stern. 船的纵轴从船头延伸到船尾。 | The study tracked the children's longitudinal development. 这项研究追踪了儿童的纵向发展。 | synonyms: lengthwise, running lengthwise | antonyms: transverse, crosswise | adjective
