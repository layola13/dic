believably:/bɪˈliːvəbli/| adv.|可信地，令人信服地|In a manner that is likely to be believed; plausibly.|She believably recounted her experience. 她令人信服地讲述了她的经历。|近义词：plausibly, credibly, convincingly|反义词：unbelievably, implausibly, doubtfully|词性:副词

词源: From "believable" + "-ly". "Believable" comes from "believe" (from Old English belīefan "to believe," from be- "by" + līefan "to allow, permit, believe") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
