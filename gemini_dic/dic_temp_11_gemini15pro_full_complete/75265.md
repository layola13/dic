frondiferous:/ˌfrɒnˈdɪfərəs/| adj.|多叶的；枝繁叶茂的|Bearing fronds or leaflike parts; leafy.|例句：The frondiferous ferns created a lush, green carpet on the forest floor.  枝繁叶茂的蕨类植物在森林地面上形成了 lush, green carpet。

例句：The frondiferous trees provided ample shade from the scorching sun. 枝繁叶茂的树木提供了充足的遮荫，以抵挡烈日。|近义词：leafy, lush, verdant|反义词：barren, leafless|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *frondifer*, from frōns (“leaf, foliage”) + -ifer (“-bearing”).
