accessional:/əkˈseʃənəl/| adj. n.|附属的，附加的；就任仪式用的（音乐）；就任仪式|Relating to an accession, especially to the accession of a monarch; a hymn or other music used at a ceremony of accession. |The museum acquired a number of accessional items, enriching its collection.  博物馆获得了一些附属物品，丰富了它的收藏。| The accessional hymn was played as the new king entered the cathedral. 当新国王进入大教堂时，演奏了就任仪式的圣歌。|Synonyms: supplementary, additional, accessory|Antonyms: essential, integral, primary|adjective, noun

accessional:/əkˈseʃənəl/| n.|附属物，附加物|Something added or acquired, a supplement. |The accessionals to the main collection were documented in a separate catalogue.  主要收藏的附属物被记录在一个单独的目录中。|  The library's accessionals include a collection of rare maps and manuscripts. 图书馆的附属物包括一系列珍稀地图和手稿。|Synonyms: supplement, addition, accessory|Antonyms: core, base, foundation|noun
