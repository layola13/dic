kevs:/kɛvz/| n.|千电子伏特 (kilo electron volts)|A kev (keV) is a unit of energy equal to one thousand electron volts. It is commonly used in nuclear physics and atomic physics to describe the energy of particles such as electrons, protons, and photons.|The X-ray machine operates at 100 keVs.|近义词:kilo electron volts|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
