bonne:/bɔn/ | adj. | good, kind, simple, naive | Good, pleasant, or agreeable. Often used in a condescending way to describe someone who is simple or naive.  Derived from the Latin word "bonus" meaning "good." |  例句：She's a bonne fille, but a bit naive.  她是个好姑娘，但有点天真。| 近义词：good, kind, gentle | 反义词：bad, wicked, cruel | 词性: adjective
