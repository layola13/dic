odontiasis:/ˌoʊdɒnˈtaɪəsɪs/
n.
出牙，长牙
The process of tooth development, especially the eruption of teeth through the gums.
The baby is experiencing the pain of odontiasis.  婴儿正在经历出牙的痛苦。
近义词:teething
反义词:None
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ὀδοντίασις (odontíasis), from ὀδοντιάω (odontiáō, “to teethe”), from ὀδούς (odoús, “tooth”).
