exossate:/ˌɛksˈɒseɪt/ /ˌɛkˈsɑːseɪt/
adj.
骨骼化的；骨骼的
Having the bones removed or separated from the flesh.  Reduced to a skeleton.
例句：The exossate remains of the dinosaur were carefully excavated.  恐龙的骨骼化遗骸被仔细地挖掘出来。

例句：The museum displayed an exossate hand of a primate.  博物馆展出了一只灵长类动物的骨骼化的手。
近义词：skeletal, bony
反义词：fleshy, plump
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exossatus*, past participle of *exossare* "to bone, remove the bones from," from *ex-* "out" + *ossa* "bones," plural of *os* "bone."
