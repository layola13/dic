goldberg: /ˈɡoʊldˌbɜːrɡ/ (US), /ˈɡəʊldˌbɜːɡ/ (UK)| n. |戈德堡，戈德堡装置，戈德堡变奏曲|A surname, also referring to a Goldberg machine (a deliberately over-engineered or overdone machine that performs a very simple task in a very complicated fashion) or Goldberg Variations (a musical work by Johann Sebastian Bach). |例句：He's a Goldberg. 他姓戈德堡。

|近义词：Rube Goldberg machine, contraption, invention|反义词: simple machine, efficient device|词性:noun

goldberg: /ˈɡoʊldˌbɜːrɡ/ (US), /ˈɡəʊldˌbɜːɡ/ (UK)| adj. |戈德堡式的，复杂的，迂回的|Resembling or characteristic of a Goldberg machine; overly complex or elaborate. |例句：That's a very Goldbergian solution to a simple problem.  那是一个解决简单问题的非常戈德堡式的方案。

|近义词：convoluted, complicated, elaborate, complex|反义词: simple, straightforward, direct|词性:adjective
