justificatory:/ˌdʒʌstɪfɪˈkeɪtəri/
adjective
辩解的，证明正当的
Serving to justify; vindicating.
例句：He offered a justificatory explanation for his actions. 他为自己的行为提供了一个辩解的解释。

例句：The report provided justificatory evidence for the decision. 这份报告为该决定提供了证明正当的证据。

近义词：exculpatory, vindicating, defensive, apologetic
反义词：incriminating, accusatory
词性：adjective
