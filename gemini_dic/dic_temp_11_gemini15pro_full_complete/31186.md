changeling:/ˈtʃeɪndʒlɪŋ/| n.| 偷换儿，被替换的孩子；（民间传说）妖精用以替换人类婴儿的丑陋或痴呆的孩子|A child believed to have been secretly substituted by fairies for the parents’ real child; a child who is different in some way, often because of illness or disability.|例句：In folklore, a changeling is a fairy child left in place of a human child. 在民间传说中，偷换儿是妖精留下来代替人类小孩的妖精小孩。

例句：He suspected the sickly child was a changeling. 他怀疑这个病恹恹的孩子是个被替换的孩子。|近义词：substitute, imposter, double|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "real child", "original child")|词性:noun
