hyperaggregates:/ˌhaɪpərˈæɡrɪɡəts/ | n. | 超聚集体，过度聚集体 |  Clumps of platelets, white blood cells, and red blood cells that form in the blood, particularly in small blood vessels, and can obstruct blood flow.  They are often associated with inflammatory conditions and can contribute to tissue damage. |  Hyperaggregates can form in the microvasculature during sepsis.  在败血症期间，微血管中会形成超聚集体。

Hyperaggregates were observed in the blood samples of patients with COVID-19. 在COVID-19患者的血液样本中观察到超聚集体。 | 近义词: aggregates, clumps | 反义词: dispersed cells | 词性: 名词
