avert:/əˈvɜːt/ | v. | 避免，防止，转移 | To prevent something bad or dangerous from happening. |  The company took steps to avert a financial crisis. 公司采取措施避免了一场金融危机。

A diplomatic solution averted war.  外交解决方案避免了战争。| synonyms: prevent, avoid, preclude, obviate, forestall | antonyms: cause, incite, provoke, precipitate | verb

Etymology: From Latin *avertere*, from *ab-* (away from) + *vertere* (to turn).
