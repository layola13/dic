hipparion:/hɪˈpɛəriən/ | n. |  三趾马 | An extinct three-toed horse of the Miocene and Pliocene epochs, widespread in the Northern Hemisphere. |  Fossils of Hipparion have been found in North America, Europe, and Asia.  三趾马的化石在北美、欧洲和亚洲都有发现。 |  synonyms:  Merychippus (a close relative), three-toed horse | antonyms:  modern horse (Equus), single-toed horse | noun
