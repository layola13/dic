nondamagingly:/nɒnˈdæmɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb
无损地，非破坏性地
in a way that does not cause damage
The technician carefully and nondamagingly removed the delicate component.  技术人员小心谨慎地无损地拆除了精密部件。
The software updates nondamagingly, preserving all your existing files. 该软件进行无损更新，保留您所有现有文件。
Synonyms: harmlessly, safely, innocuously
Antonyms: destructively, harmfully, damagingly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
damaging (adjective): from the verb "to damage," ultimately from Old French "damager"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
