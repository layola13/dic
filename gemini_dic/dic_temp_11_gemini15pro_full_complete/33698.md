-cion: /ʃən/ | n. | 行为; 状态; 结果 |  A suffix forming nouns of action, state, or result, often corresponding to verbs ending in -ce, -se, or -ct. | The coercion of the witness led to false testimony.  证人受到胁迫，导致作伪证。| 近义词: action, process, result | 反义词: inaction, stagnation | 词性: 后缀

词源:
From Old French -cion, -tion, from Latin -tiō, -siō, -ciō,  nominal suffix forming abstract nouns from verbs.
