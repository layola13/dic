organ-donor:/ˈɔːrɡən ˌdoʊnər/| n. |器官捐献者|A person who donates an organ (or organs) for transplantation.|例句：The organ donor saved three lives.  这位器官捐献者挽救了三个生命。

例句：My brother is a registered organ donor. 我的兄弟是一位注册的器官捐赠者。

近义词：donor, contributor|反义词:recipient|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
organ (n.): From Old French organe and Latin organum, meaning "instrument, tool," ultimately from Greek organon, meaning "instrument, tool, organ of the body."

donor (n.): From Latin donator, meaning "giver," from donare, meaning "to give, present, bestow."
