deciphered:/dɪˈsaɪfərd/ | v. | 解释；破译 |  succeeded in deciphering the coded message. 成功地破译了密码信息。 | Example: The archaeologist deciphered the ancient hieroglyphs. 考古学家破译了古代象形文字。

Example:  The spy deciphered the enemy's secret code.  间谍破译了敌人的密码。

近义词: decoded, interpreted, solved, cracked
反义词: encoded, encrypted, obscured

词性: verb (past participle of decipher)


词源: From Old French *deschifrer, from des- “dis-” + chifrer “to cipher, to calculate, to write in code.”
