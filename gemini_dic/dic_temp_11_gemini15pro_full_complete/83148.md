gutsy: /ˈɡʌtsi/ (US), /ˈɡʌt.si/ (UK)| adj.| 勇敢的，有胆量的，坚毅的| Showing courage, determination, and spirit.|  She made a gutsy decision to start her own business. 她做了一个勇敢的决定，创办了自己的公司。

She gave a gutsy performance in the final match. 她在决赛中表现得非常顽强。


近义词: courageous, brave, bold, spirited, determined, plucky|反义词: cowardly, timid, fearful|词性: adjective

词源: From "guts" (plural noun), meaning intestines or entrails.  Originally, having "guts" referred to having fortitude and courage, literally the strength of one's stomach.  This evolved into the adjective "gutsy."
