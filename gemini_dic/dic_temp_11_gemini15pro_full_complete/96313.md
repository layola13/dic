indigestibility:/ˌɪndɪˌdʒɛstɪˈbɪlɪti/ | n. | 消化不良，难消化 |The state or quality of being difficult or impossible to digest.  | This kind of mushroom has a reputation for indigestibility. 这种蘑菇以难消化而闻名。| The indigestibility of the legal jargon made it difficult for the average person to understand the contract.  法律术语的晦涩难懂使得普通人难以理解合同。| 近义词: indigestibleness, nondigestibility | 反义词: digestibility, digesibleness | 词性: noun
