larmier:/ˈlɑːrmiər/ (US), /ˈlɑːmɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|滴水石，滴水线，檐口滴水|A projecting moulding on the underside of a cornice, sill, or dripstone, designed to prevent rainwater from running down the wall.|The larmier on the window sill prevents water damage. 窗台上的滴水线防止了水渍的损害。|近义词：dripstone, drip mould, drip,  eavesdrop|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *larmier, from larme (“tear”), from Latin lacrima.
