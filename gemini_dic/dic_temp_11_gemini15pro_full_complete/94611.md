impregnatable:/ɪmˈprɛɡnətəbl/
adjective
可受孕的；易受影响的
Able to be impregnated; capable of being permeated or influenced by something.
The researchers studied the impregnatable nature of the material. 研究人员研究了这种材料的可浸透性。
The young minds were impregnatable with new ideas. 年轻人的思想很容易接受新思想。
synonyms: receptive, susceptible, permeable, penetrable
antonyms: impermeable, resistant, impervious
