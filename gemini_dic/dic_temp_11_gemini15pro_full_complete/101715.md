不可抑制性，压制不住
The quality or state of being irrepressible.  The fact of being impossible to repress or control.
Her irrepressibleness made her a natural leader. 她的热情奔放使她自然而然地成为了一位领导者。

词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From irrepressible + -ness.  Irrepressible is formed from in- (not) + re- (back) + press (to press down) + -ible (capable of being). Ultimately from Latin *in- + premere + -ibilis.
