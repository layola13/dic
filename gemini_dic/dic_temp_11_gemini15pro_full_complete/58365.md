dualest:/ˈdjuːəlɪst/ | adj. | 最对偶的；最二元的 |  Superlative form of "dual."  Describing something that exhibits the greatest degree of duality or involves the most contrasting elements.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered non-standard. |  While "best" or "most dual" are generally preferred, one might say in a highly specific context, "Of all the systems, this one is the *dualest* in its nature, incorporating both particle and wave properties." | 近义词: most dual, most contrasting, most dichotomous | 反义词: unified, homogenous, singular | 词性: 形容词
