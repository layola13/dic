outcursing:/ˌaʊtˈkɜːrsɪŋ/
|v.|胜过咒骂; 比...更会咒骂|To surpass or exceed in cursing; to curse more vehemently or skillfully than another.|He outcursed the sailor with a string of colorful epithets. 他用一连串生动的咒骂胜过了那个水手。|近义词: outswear,  curse more|反义词: bless, praise|词性: 动词
