disunite:/ˌdɪsjuˈnaɪt/
verb
分裂，使不团结
To cause to be no longer united; separate.
The Civil War disunited the country. 内战使国家分裂。
近义词: divide, separate, sever, disjoin, fragment
反义词: unite, join, connect, combine
词性:v.


disunite:/ˌdɪsjuˈnaɪt/
adjective
分裂的，不团结的
Not united; separated.
The disunited states were vulnerable to attack. 分裂的国家很容易受到攻击。
近义词: divided, separated, fragmented, disjointed
反义词: united, joined, connected, combined
词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English disunien, from Anglo-Norman desunir, from Old French desunir, from des- (prefix meaning ‘apart, away’) + unir (‘to unite’), ultimately from Latin ūnus (‘one’).
