disburthen:/dɪsˈbɜːrðən/
verb
卸下负担，解除…的负担
to relieve (someone or something) of a burden
He disburthened himself of his worries. 他倾诉了自己的烦恼。
近义词:unload, unburden, relieve
反义词:burden, load, encumber
词性:v.

disburthen:/dɪsˈbɜːrðən/
noun
卸货
the act of unloading
The disburthen of the cargo took several days.  卸货花了几天时间。
近义词:unloading, discharge
反义词:loading
词性:n.
