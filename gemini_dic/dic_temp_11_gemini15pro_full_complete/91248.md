hydroelectric:/ˌhaɪdroʊɪˈlɛktrɪk/ | adj. | 水力发电的 | Relating to the production of electricity by water power. |  The dam provides hydroelectric power to the entire region.  大坝为整个地区提供水力发电。| synonyms: water-powered, hydraulic | antonyms:  fossil-fuel-powered, nuclear-powered | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- + electric.  "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" (water), and "electric" ultimately derives from Greek "elektron" (amber), referencing the static electricity produced by rubbing amber.
