observation:/ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌɑːbzərˈveɪʃn/ (US)| n.|观察；观察结果；评论|The act of noticing or watching something carefully.  Also, a statement based on something one has seen, heard, or noticed.|例句：The scientist made a careful observation of the animal's behavior.  科学家仔细观察了动物的行为。|例句：Her observations on the current political climate were insightful. 她对当前政治气候的观察很有见地。|近义词：monitoring, surveillance, scrutiny, remark, comment|反义词:inattention, negligence|词性:noun
