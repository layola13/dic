mirror-like:/ˈmɪrərˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像镜子的;光滑的 | Resembling a mirror; smooth and highly reflective. | The lake was mirror-like, reflecting the surrounding mountains.  湖面像镜子一样，映照着周围的山峦。|  The car had a mirror-like finish. 这辆车有着像镜子一样的光洁度。 | 近义词: reflective, polished, glassy, smooth | 反义词: dull, rough, matte | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From "mirror" + "-like".  "Mirror" comes from Old French "mireor," ultimately from Latin "mirari" (to wonder at). "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
