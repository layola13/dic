megaphyllous:/ˌmɛɡəˈfɪləs/| adj.|巨叶的，大型叶的|Having large leaves with several or many veins, thought to have evolved from a branch system.  Characterized by large, often branched, vascular strands within the leaf.|Ferns are megaphyllous plants.  蕨类植物是巨叶植物。|Near Synonyms: macrophyllous|Antonyms: microphyllous|Adjective
