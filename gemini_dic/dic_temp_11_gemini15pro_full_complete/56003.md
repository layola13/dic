do-dos:/ˈduːduːz/ /ˈduːduːs/ | n. | 待办事项；必须做的事情 | A list of tasks that need to be completed. | I have so many do-dos today, I don't know where to start. 我今天有好多事情要做，不知道从哪里开始。| Example:  She added "grocery shopping" to her do-dos. 她把“买菜”加到她的待办事项清单里。| Synonyms: tasks, chores, errands, agenda | Antonyms:  completed tasks, finished jobs | Noun
