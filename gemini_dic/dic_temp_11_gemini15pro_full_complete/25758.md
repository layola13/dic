burbliest:/ˈbɜːrbliɪst/| adj.|最多泡的，最起泡的|Characterized by having the most bubbles or being the most bubbly.  It's the superlative form of "burbly".|The burbliest soda I've ever seen overflowed the glass.|近义词：foamiest, frothiest|反义词:flattest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "burbly" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Burbly" is likely a  19th-century coinage based on "burble" (to make a bubbling sound), which ultimately comes from imitative roots.
