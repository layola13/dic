inflections:/ɪnˈflɛkʃənz/ | n. | 变形，词形变化 | The process of changing the form of a word (typically the ending) to express a grammatical function or attribute, such as tense, mood, person, number, case, and gender. |  The inflection of the verb "to be" changes depending on the subject.  动词“to be”的变形取决于主语。

The plural inflection of "cat" is "cats."  “cat”的复数变形是“cats”。 | synonyms: conjugations, declensions, variations, modifications | antonyms: immutability, invariability | Noun
