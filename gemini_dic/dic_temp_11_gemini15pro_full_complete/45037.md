ctrl:/kənˈtrəʊl/ (British), /kənˈtroʊl/ (American)| n. vt.|控制；控制器；对照实验|A key on a computer keyboard used to perform a particular function, typically in combination with another key.  It stands for "control".|Press Ctrl+C to copy the text. 按Ctrl+C复制文本。|synonyms: command, regulate, manage|antonyms: release, lose control|noun, verb
