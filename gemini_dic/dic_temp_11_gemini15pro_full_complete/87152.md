herbist:/ˈɜːrbəlɪst/ (US), /ˈhɜːbəlɪst/ (UK)| n.| 药草师；草药医生|A person who is an expert in the use of herbs, especially medicinal herbs.|例句：The herbist recommended a tea made from chamomile and lavender for relaxation.  草药师推荐了一种用洋甘菊和薰衣草制成的茶来放松身心。

例句：She consulted an herbist for a natural remedy for her insomnia. 她咨询了一位草药师，寻求治疗失眠的自然疗法。

近义词：herbalist, botanist (in some contexts)|反义词: None|词性: Noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French herbier, from herbe ‘herb’.
