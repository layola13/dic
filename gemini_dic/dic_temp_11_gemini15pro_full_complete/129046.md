nitric:/ˈnaɪtrɪk/| adj.|硝酸的；含氮的|Relating to or containing nitrogen, especially in the pentavalent state.|Nitric acid is a highly corrosive mineral acid.|例句：Nitric oxide is a colorless gas. 一氧化氮是一种无色气体。

例句：Nitric acid is used in the production of fertilizers. 硝酸用于生产肥料。|近义词：nitrogenous, nitrate|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a descriptive term like "nitric")|词性: adjective
