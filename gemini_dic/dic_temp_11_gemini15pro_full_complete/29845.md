causation:/kɔːˈzeɪʃən/
n.
因果关系，原因
the act or process of causing something to happen or exist
例句：The lawyer argued there was no direct causation between the company's actions and the plaintiff's loss.  律师辩称该公司行为与原告损失之间没有直接的因果关系。

例句：Scientists are still researching the causation of the disease. 科学家们仍在研究这种疾病的病因。

近义词：causality, etiology, genesis
反义词:prevention,  inhibition
词性:noun
