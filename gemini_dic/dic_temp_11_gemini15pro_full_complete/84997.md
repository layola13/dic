hara-kiri:/ˌhɑːrəˈkɪri/ /ˌhærəˈkɪri/
noun
切腹自杀
ritual suicide by disembowelment with a sword, formerly practiced by samurai in Japan
例句：He committed hara-kiri rather than face dishonor.  他宁愿切腹自杀也不愿面对耻辱。

例句：Hara-kiri was a way for samurai to regain their honor.  切腹是武士重获荣誉的一种方式。

近义词：suicide, self-immolation
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "survival," "living")
词性: noun
