enlargeable:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒəbl/
adjective
可放大的；可扩大的
Something that is enlargeable can be made larger.
The image is enlargeable up to A3 size. 这张图片可以放大到A3尺寸。
This photograph is digitally enlargeable. 这张照片可以数码放大。
synonyms: expandable, magnifiable, scalable
antonyms: reducible, shrinkable
word origin: From enlarge + -able.
