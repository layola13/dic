Example:  Heterauxesis plays a crucial role in the development of many species, from insects to mammals. 异速生长在许多物种的发育中起着至关重要的作用，从昆虫到哺乳动物。 | 近义词: allometry, differential growth | 反义词: isometric growth |词性: noun
