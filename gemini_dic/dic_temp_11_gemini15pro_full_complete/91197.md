hydraulics: /haɪˈdrɔːlɪks/ | n. | 液压学，水力学 | The study of the mechanical properties of liquids, especially as used in engineering. |  Example 1:  The dam was designed using the principles of hydraulics.  大坝是根据水力学原理设计的。

Example 2:  He specialized in hydraulics and pneumatics. 他专攻液压和气动技术。 | Near Synonyms: fluid mechanics, fluid power | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


hydraulics: /haɪˈdrɔːlɪks/ | adj. | 液压的，水力的 | Operated by, moved by, or employing liquid (typically water or oil) under pressure. | Example 1: The hydraulic system in the car failed. 汽车的液压系统失灵了。

Example 2:  This machine uses a hydraulic press. 这台机器使用液压机。| Near Synonyms:  hydraulically operated, hydraulically powered | Antonyms: N/A | Adjective


Etymology:  From French *hydraulique*, from Latin *hydraulica* and Greek *ὑδραυλικός* (hydraulikos), meaning "water organ," from *ὕδωρ* (hydōr, "water") + *αὐλός* (aulos, "pipe"). The modern meaning, related to the conveyance of liquids through pipes, developed later.
