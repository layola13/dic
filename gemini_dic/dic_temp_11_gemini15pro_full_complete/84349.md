half-smiling:/ˌhɑːfˈsmaɪlɪŋ/
adjective
半笑着的，带着一丝微笑的
A facial expression showing a slight smile.
She gave him a half-smiling, half-worried look. 她给了他一个半笑半担忧的表情。
He offered a half-smiling apology. 他带着一丝微笑表示歉意。

近义词: faintly smiling, smirking
反义词: frowning, scowling
词性: adjective

词源:
"half-" + "smiling"
"half-"  Old English healf
"smiling"  Middle English smilen, from Old Norse smila
