gingerbreaded:/ˈdʒɪndʒəˌbredɪd/ | adj. | 姜饼色的，姜饼味的，(建筑)装饰华丽的 | Having the warm brown color of gingerbread; elaborately ornamented (especially of architecture). |  The house was gingerbreaded with intricate carvings. 这座房子用复杂的雕刻装饰得很华丽，像姜饼屋一样。 | The aroma of gingerbreaded cookies filled the air. 空气中弥漫着姜饼曲奇的香味。 | 近义词: ornate, embellished, decorated | 反义词: plain, simple | 词性: adjective

词源:  From gingerbread + -ed.  Gingerbread itself comes from Old French *gingembras (modern French gingembre), ultimately from Medieval Latin gingiber, from Greek zingiberi, from Prakrit singabera, perhaps from Sanskrit srngavera ("horn-body," referring to the shape of the rhizome).
