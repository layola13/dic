keelraked:/ˈkiːlˌreɪkt/
adjective
倾覆的，船底朝天的
having the keel raked or inclined; overturned; capsized.  Specifically (of a ship), having had its hull cleaned or repaired by careening.
The keelraked vessel lay helpless in the storm.  倾覆的船只在暴风雨中无助地躺着。
overturned, capsized, upturned
right-side up, upright
adjective
