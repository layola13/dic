imitability:/ˌɪmɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.|可模仿性|The quality of being imitable; capable of being imitated or copied.|The imitability of the product's design made it vulnerable to counterfeiting.  该产品设计的可模仿性使其容易被假冒。|imitableness,  reproducibility, copyability|inimitability, originality, uniqueness|noun
