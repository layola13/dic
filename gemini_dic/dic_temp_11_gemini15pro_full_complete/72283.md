flotilla:/fləˈtɪlə/ | n. | 小舰队，船队 |A small fleet of ships or boats, especially warships or small fishing vessels. |The fishing flotilla returned to harbor before the storm.  捕鱼船队在暴风雨来临前返回了港口。|Synonyms: fleet, squadron, armada |Antonyms: single ship, individual vessel | noun


flotilla:/fləˈtɪlə/ | n. | 一群（人或物）|A group of similar things or people, often moving together. |A flotilla of rubber ducks floated down the river. 一群橡皮鸭漂浮在河面上。|Synonyms: group, collection, cluster |Antonyms: individual, single, solitary | noun
