bluewing:/ˈbluːwɪŋ/| n.|蓝翅鸭，蓝翅水鸡|A type of bird known for its blue wings.  A North American dabbling duck with a bluish gray wing patch.|例句：The bluewing landed gracefully on the water. 蓝翅鸭优雅地落在水面上。

例句：We saw a flock of bluewings taking flight. 我们看到一群蓝翅鸭飞了起来。|近义词：teal, duck|反义词:|noun
