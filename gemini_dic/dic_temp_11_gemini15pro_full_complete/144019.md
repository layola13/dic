outreddening:/ˌaʊtˈrɛdnɪŋ/
verb
使...比...更红; 超过…的红;  变得比…更红
making or becoming redder than something else; surpassing in redness
The sunset was outreddening the nearby flowers. 日落比附近的花朵更红。
The leaves in autumn are outreddening the bricks of the house. 秋天的树叶比房子的砖块更红。
Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outdoing
Antonyms: paling, fading, bleaching
词性: 动词
