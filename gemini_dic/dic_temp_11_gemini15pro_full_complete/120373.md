minion:/ˈmɪnjən/| n.| 仆从，下属，爪牙|A minion is a follower or underling of a powerful person, especially a servile or unscrupulous one. | The dictator surrounded himself with minions who carried out his every whim. 独裁者身边都是唯命是从的爪牙。| synonyms: follower, underling, servant, lackey, henchman | antonyms: leader, boss, master | noun
