eaglewood:/ˈiːɡəlˌwʊd/| n. |沉香木，沉香|A fragrant resinous wood, used in incense and perfumes, from certain Asian trees.  It is formed in the heartwood when the tree is wounded, as by fungal infection.|The eaglewood beads gave off a delicate, sweet scent. 沉香木珠子散发出淡淡的甜香。|近义词：agarwood, aloeswood|反义词:|名词
