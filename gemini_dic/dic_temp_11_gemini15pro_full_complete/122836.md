molesters:/məˈlɛstərz/
n.
性骚扰者，猥亵儿童者
People who molest, typically sexually.
Several molesters were arrested near the school.  几个性骚扰者在学校附近被捕。
近义词：abusers, assailants, predators
反义词：protectors, guardians
词性：名词(复数)

molest:/məˈlɛst/
v.
骚扰，猥亵；干扰，妨碍
To annoy, disturb, or interfere with, especially by making sexual advances.
The man was arrested for molesting a child.  这名男子因猥亵儿童而被捕。
近义词：harass, abuse, assault
反义词：protect, defend
词性：动词

词源:
From Old French "molester" (to annoy, trouble), from Latin "molestare" (to trouble, disturb, annoy), frequentative of "molere" (to grind, trouble).  The sexual sense developed later.
