disclosure's:/dɪˈskloʊʒərz/
| n.|披露；揭露；公开；被揭露的事情|The act of making something known or the fact of being made known. |The company's disclosure of its financial problems caused a drop in its stock price. 该公司对其财务问题的披露导致其股价下跌。|synonyms: revelation, divulgence, unveiling, exposure |antonyms: concealment, secrecy, suppression|noun
