magicked:/ˈmædʒɪkt/ | v. | 用魔法变出；使魔法般地发生 | To have caused something to happen as if by magic. |  She magicked up a delicious meal seemingly out of nowhere. 她像变魔法一样，不知从哪里弄来了一顿美味佳肴。

| The fairy godmother magicked Cinderella a beautiful gown. 仙女教母用魔法变出了一件漂亮的礼服给灰姑娘。
| 近义词: conjured, materialized, produced | 反义词: None | 词性: 动词
