injurious:/ɪnˈdʒʊəriəs/| adj.|有害的，致伤的|causing or likely to cause harm or injury.|例句：Smoking is injurious to health. 吸烟有害健康。|例句：The accident was injurious to his health. 这场事故损害了他的健康。|近义词：harmful, damaging, detrimental, deleterious|反义词：beneficial, advantageous, helpful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *injuriosus "wrongful," from Latin injuria "injury, injustice," from in- "not, opposite of" (see in- (1)) + jus "right, law" (see just (adj.)).
