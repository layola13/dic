dodge:/dɑːdʒ/ /dɒdʒ/| v. n.|躲避，闪开；伎俩，托词|To avoid something, typically by a sudden quick movement; an act of evading something.|He dodged the blow. 他躲过了那一击。|近义词：evade, avoid, elude, duck, shirk|反义词: confront, face, meet|verb
dodge:/dɑːdʒ/ /dɒdʒ/| n.|躲避，闪避；伎俩，托词|An act of dodging; a clever or dishonest trick used to deceive someone.|That was a clever dodge to avoid paying taxes. 那是一个巧妙的伎俩来逃税。|近义词：ploy, ruse, trick, subterfuge|反义词: honesty, truthfulness|noun
