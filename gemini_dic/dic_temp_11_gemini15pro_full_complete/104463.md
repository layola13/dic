kaisar:/ˈkaɪzər/| n.|皇帝，凯撒|From German Kaiser, from Latin Caesar, originally a cognomen of Julius Caesar.  A title of emperors, especially of Germany and Austria.|例句：The Kaiser addressed his troops. 凯撒对他的军队讲话。

例句：Wilhelm II was the last German Kaiser. 威廉二世是最后一位德国皇帝。|近义词：emperor, monarch, sovereign|反义词：subject, citizen|Noun
