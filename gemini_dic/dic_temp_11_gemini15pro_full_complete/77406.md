gave:/ɡeɪv/ | v. |给，给予，赠送|Past tense of "give".  Indicates the action of transferring possession of something to someone else. |She gave me a book for my birthday. 她送给我一本书作为生日礼物。|He gave his time and energy to the project. 他把他的时间和精力都投入到这个项目中。|近义词：granted, bestowed, presented, offered|反义词:received, took, accepted|动词
