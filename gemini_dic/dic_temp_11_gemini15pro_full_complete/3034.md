aerobicized:/eɪˈroʊbɪsaɪzd/
verb
有氧化的；需氧的
Made or become aerobic; supplied with oxygen.
例句：The aerobicized compost is beneficial for plant growth.  有氧化的堆肥有利于植物生长。

例句：The bacteria were aerobicized in the lab.  这些细菌在实验室里被进行了需氧处理。
近义词：oxygenated, aerated
反义词：anaerobic
词性：v.
