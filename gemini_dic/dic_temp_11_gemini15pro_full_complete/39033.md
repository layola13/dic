conflicted:/kənˈflɪktɪd/ | adj. | 冲突的 | Involving conflict; characterized by discord or disagreement. |  The two countries have a long and conflicted history. 这两个国家有着长期而冲突的历史。| synonyms: antagonistic, hostile, at war, at odds,  | antonyms: harmonious, peaceful, agreeable | adjective
