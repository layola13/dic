overgrading:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdɪŋ/
verb
过度评分，过高评价
Giving a grade that is higher than deserved.
The teacher was accused of overgrading her students. 老师被指责给学生打分过高。
Overgrading can lead to inflated expectations. 过度评分会导致期望值过高。
近义词: inflate, overestimate, overvalue
反义词: undergrade, underestimate, undervalue
词性: verb

overgrading:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdɪŋ/
noun
过度评分，过高评价
The act of giving a grade that is higher than deserved.
There's been a lot of talk about overgrading in schools recently. 最近有很多关于学校评分过高的话题。
Overgrading can create problems later on. 过度评分会在以后造成问题。
近义词: inflation, overestimation, overvaluation
反义词: undergrading, underestimation, undervaluation
词性: noun
