genitives:/ˈdʒɛnɪtɪvz/| n.|所有格|A case of nouns and pronouns (and sometimes adjectives) indicating possession, origin, or association.  It corresponds roughly to the possessive case in English, marked by 's or of. |The king's crown is an example of a genitive. 国王的王冠是所有格的一个例子。|synonyms: possessive, |antonyms: N/A| noun


genitive:/ˈdʒɛnɪtɪv/| adj.|所有格的|Relating to or denoting the genitive case. |The genitive form of "dog" is "dog's".  “dog”的所有格形式是“dog's”。|synonyms: possessive, |antonyms: N/A| adjective
