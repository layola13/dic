bocca:/ˈbɔkːə/ (Italian)| n.|嘴，口|The Italian word for "mouth."  It refers to the opening in the lower part of the human face, surrounded by the lips, through which food is taken in and sounds are emitted.  Figuratively, it can also refer to an opening or entrance.|She tasted the wine in her bocca. 她用嘴品尝了葡萄酒。|Synonyms: mouth, opening (in a figurative sense)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
