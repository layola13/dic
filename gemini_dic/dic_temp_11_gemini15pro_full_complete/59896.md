eddo:/ˈɛdoʊ/ (US), /ˈɛdəʊ/ (UK)
n.
芋头；食用块茎
A tropical plant with a starchy, edible root.  Specifically, it refers to types of Colocasia, Xanthosoma and Alocasia.
Eddoes are a staple food in many tropical countries. 芋头是许多热带国家的主食。
