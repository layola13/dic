acrobatically:/ˌækrəˈbætɪkli/
adverb
杂技般地；特技般地
in the manner of an acrobat; with agility and skill.
The gymnast acrobatically flipped across the floor. 体操运动员像杂技演员一样灵巧地翻滚过地板。
Near synonyms: nimbly, agilely, deftly, skillfully
Opposite: clumsily, awkwardly
词性: 副词
