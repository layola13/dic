antiexpressive:/ˌæntiɪkˈsprɛsɪv/| adj.|反表现主义的，抑制情感表达的|Characterized by the suppression or restraint of emotional expression, often in art or literature.  It opposes the open display of feelings, favoring understatement or detachment.|The artist's antiexpressive style was a deliberate reaction against the emotional excesses of Romanticism.|近义词: restrained, reserved, unemotional, subdued, inhibited, impassive, stoic, deadpan|反义词: expressive, emotional, demonstrative, effusive, passionate|词性:形容词
