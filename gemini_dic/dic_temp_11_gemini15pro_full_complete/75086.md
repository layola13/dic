frillers:/ˈfrɪlərz/| n.|褶边，饰边，荷叶边 (通常复数形式)|A friller is a piece of gathered or pleated material used as a decoration or trimming on clothing or other items.  It usually refers to multiple frills. |The dress had delicate frillers around the neckline.|近义词：ruffles, flounces, furbelows|反义词:plainness, simplicity|词性:noun
