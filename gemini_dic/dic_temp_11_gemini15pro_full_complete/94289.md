impertinency:/ɪmˈpɜːrtɪnənsi/| n.|无礼，鲁莽，不恰当|Lack of respect; rudeness; an inappropriate action or remark.|例句：Her impertinency shocked the guests. 她的无礼震惊了客人们。

例句：He apologized for his impertinency. 他为自己的鲁莽道歉了。

近义词：rudeness, insolence, impudence, audacity|反义词: politeness, courtesy, respect|词性:名词

impertinent:/ɪmˈpɜːrtɪnənt/| adj.|无礼的，鲁莽的，不恰当的|Not showing proper respect; rude.|例句：She asked an impertinent question. 她问了一个不恰当的问题。

例句：He gave me an impertinent stare. 他傲慢地瞪着我。

近义词：rude, insolent, impudent, cheeky|反义词: polite, respectful, courteous|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impertinentia, from Latin impertinent- (“not pertinent”), from in- (“not”) + pertinent- (“pertaining to”), present participle of pertinēre (“to pertain”), from per- (“thoroughly”) + tenēre (“to hold”).
