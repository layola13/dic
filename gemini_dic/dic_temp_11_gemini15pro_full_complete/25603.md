bumpering:/ˈbʌmpərɪŋ/
adjective
异常大的;丰盛的;创纪录的
Exceptionally large or successful.
例句：
The company announced bumpering profits.
公司宣布了创纪录的利润。

例句：
A bumpering crop of tomatoes this year.
今年番茄获得了大丰收。

近义词：huge, enormous, massive, substantial, record-breaking, exceptional, extraordinary
反义词：small, meager, paltry, insignificant
词性: adjective
