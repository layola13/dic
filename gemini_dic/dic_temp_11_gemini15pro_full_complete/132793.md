nondynastic:/ˌnɒn daɪˈnæstɪk/
adjective
非王朝的；非世袭的
Not belonging to, related to, or characteristic of a dynasty or line of hereditary rulers.
The museum showcased nondynastic art from the period.  博物馆展示了那个时期的非王朝艺术。
The rise of nondynastic powers challenged the traditional order. 非王朝势力的崛起挑战了传统秩序。
近义词: non-hereditary, non-familial
反义词: dynastic, hereditary
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + dynastic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of," and "dynastic" refers to a line of hereditary rulers.
