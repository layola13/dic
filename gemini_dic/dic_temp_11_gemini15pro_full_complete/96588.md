inductees:/ɪnˌdʌkˈtiːz/
noun
入选者；被引荐者；新成员
People formally admitted to an organization or group, especially an honorary one.
The Hall of Fame inductees gave inspiring speeches.  名人堂的入选者们发表了鼓舞人心的演讲。
Synonyms: initiates, entrants, newcomers
Antonyms: veterans, seniors, old-timers
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
