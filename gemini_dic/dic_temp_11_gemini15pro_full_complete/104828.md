kedlock:/ˈkɛdlɒk/ (UK) /ˈkɛdlɑːk/ (US)| n.|野芥菜；野芥；锁; Charlock|A common yellow-flowered weed (Sinapis arvensis) of the mustard family, often infesting grain fields.  Also called charlock. Also, the seed of this plant. It was formerly used as a potherb and for its oil-rich seeds.|The farmer struggled to eradicate kedlock from his wheat field.|近义词：charlock, wild mustard|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps cultivated plant)|词性:noun
