infliction:/ɪnˈflɪkʃən/
n.|施加，施加痛苦，施加刑罚|The infliction of pain is considered cruel.  施加痛苦被认为是残酷的。|例句：The infliction of heavy casualties caused widespread grief. 施加大量伤亡造成了广泛的悲痛。

例句：The constant noise was an infliction on their peace and quiet.  持续不断的噪音对他们的安宁是一种折磨。

近义词：imposition, application, causing
反义词：relief, alleviation
词性：名词
