fink:/fɪŋk/ | n. | 告密者，叛徒；讨厌的人；工贼 | An informer; a despicable person; a strikebreaker. | He was labeled a fink for crossing the picket line. 他因为穿过纠察线而被贴上了工贼的标签。 | Synonyms: informer, rat, snitch, traitor, scab | Antonyms: loyalist, comrade, friend | noun
