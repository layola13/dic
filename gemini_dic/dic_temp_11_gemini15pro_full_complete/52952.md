ding-dong:/ˈdɪŋ dɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈdɪŋ dɑːŋ/ (US)| n., int., adv., adj. |叮咚声，门铃声|The sound of a doorbell or a chime.  It can also be used as an interjection to represent this sound, an adverb describing the manner of the sound, or an adjective describing something that produces this sound.|例句：The doorbell rang with a loud ding-dong. 门铃发出响亮的叮咚声。|近义词：chime, ring, jingle|反义词:silence, quiet|noun

ding-dong:/ˈdɪŋ dɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈdɪŋ dɑːŋ/ (US)| int. |叮咚（表示门铃声）|Used to represent the sound of a doorbell.|例句：Ding-dong!  I'll get it! 叮咚！我去开门！|近义词：ring, chime|反义词: (None for interjections)|interjection

ding-dong:/ˈdɪŋ dɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈdɪŋ dɑːŋ/ (US)| adv. |叮咚地|Describes the manner in which a bell or chime rings.|例句：The little bells jingled ding-dong merrily. 小铃铛叮咚作响，欢快无比。|近义词：janglingly, resonantly|反义词:silently, quietly|adverb
