dilettanteish:/ˌdɪləˈtɑːntɪʃ/| adj.|浅薄的，业余爱好的，半吊子的|Suggesting the superficial interest of a dilettante; amateurish.|He has a dilettanteish interest in art, never going beyond browsing in galleries.|近义词：amateurish, superficial, dabblers, desultory|反义词：professional, expert, skilled, proficient|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dilettante + -ish.  Dilettante comes from Italian dilettante, present participle of dilettare “to delight,” from Latin delectare.
