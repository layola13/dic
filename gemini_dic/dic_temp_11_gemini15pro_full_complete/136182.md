nonrejoinders:/ˌnɒnriˈdʒɔɪndərz/ | n. | 不答复，不反驳 | Absence of rejoinders; failure to reply or respond. |  The lawyer's nonrejoinders to the accusations seemed to confirm their validity. 律师对指控的不答复似乎证实了指控的有效性。 |  The witness's nonrejoinders to the pointed questions raised suspicions.  证人对尖锐问题的回避引起了怀疑。 | synonyms: silence, reticence, unresponsiveness | antonyms: replies, responses, answers | noun
