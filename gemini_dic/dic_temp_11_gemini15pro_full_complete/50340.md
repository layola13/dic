deployment:/dɪˈplɔɪmənt/| n.|部署，调动|the act of placing troops or equipment in position for military action; the positioning of equipment or personnel for a particular task or purpose|The deployment of troops to the border region increased tensions. 部署军队到边境地区加剧了紧张局势。|近义词：positioning, stationing, arrangement, allocation|反义词:withdrawal, retrieval|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French déployer, from Latin displicare ("to unfold, spread out").
