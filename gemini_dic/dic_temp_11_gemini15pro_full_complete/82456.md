grumous:/ˈɡruːməs/ | adj. | 凝块的；块状的 | Thick, clotted, or lumpy.  Referring to liquids or other substances that have formed into thick lumps or clots. | The doctor noted the grumous discharge from the wound.  医生注意到伤口流出凝块状的分泌物。|  The spoiled milk had a grumous texture. 变质的牛奶有块状的质地。 |  clotted, lumpy, thick, curdled |  smooth, thin, liquid | adjective
