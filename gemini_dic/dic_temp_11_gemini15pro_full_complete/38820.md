coneflower:/ˈkoʊnˌflaʊər/| n. |松果菊；紫锥花|A flowering plant of the daisy family, with purplish-pink petals and a prominent central cone. It is native to North America and is often cultivated in gardens.|例句：The coneflower attracted butterflies to the garden. 松果菊吸引了蝴蝶来到花园。

例句：She planted a variety of coneflowers in her flowerbed. 她在花坛里种了各种各样的松果菊。|近义词：Echinacea, rudbeckia|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cone" + "flower," referring to the cone-shaped central disk of the flower.
