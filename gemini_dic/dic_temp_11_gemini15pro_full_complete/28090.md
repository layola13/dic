capillose:/ˈkæpɪloʊs/| adj.|毛状的，毛细管的|Covered with fine hairs or filaments; resembling a capillary.  Having hairlike projections or structures.|例句：The capillose surface of the plant helped it retain moisture.  这种植物毛状的表面有助于保持水分。

例句：The fungus had a capillose structure, making it difficult to identify. 这种真菌具有毛状结构，使其难以识别。|近义词：hairy, filamentous, pilose, pubescent|反义词：glabrous, smooth|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin "capillus" (hair) + "-ose" (suffix indicating abundance or resemblance).
