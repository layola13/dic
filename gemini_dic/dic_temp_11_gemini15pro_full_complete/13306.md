athlete:/ˈæθliːt/ (US), /ˈæθliːt/ (UK)| n. |运动员；体育家；身强力壮的人|A person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise.|例句：The athlete trained hard for the Olympics.  这位运动员为奥运会刻苦训练。

|例句：She is a natural athlete. 她是一位天生的运动员。


|近义词：sportsman, sportswoman, competitor|反义词:couch potato, sluggard|词性:noun
