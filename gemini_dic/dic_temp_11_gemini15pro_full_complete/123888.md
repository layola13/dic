morbidity:/mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/ /mɔːrˈbɪdəti/
n.
发病率，病态，不健全
The state of being diseased or unhealthy; the incidence of disease in a population.
The morbidity rate for diabetes is increasing. 糖尿病的发病率正在上升。
近义词: illness, sickness, disease, unhealthiness
反义词: health, wellness
词性: 名词

morbidity:/mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/ /mɔːrˈbɪdəti/
n.
病态，阴沉
An unhealthy preoccupation with unpleasant subjects, especially death and disease.
His poetry is full of morbidity and despair. 他的诗歌充满了病态和绝望。
近义词: macabre, gruesome, morbidness
反义词: cheerfulness, optimism
词性: 名词
