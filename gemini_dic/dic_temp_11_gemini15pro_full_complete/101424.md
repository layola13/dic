iranianist: /ɪˌreɪniˈænɪst/ | n. | 伊朗学家；研究伊朗语言、文学、历史或文化的专家 | A scholar specializing in Iranian languages, literature, history, or culture. |  例句：The iranianist gave a lecture on Persian poetry.  这位伊朗学家做了一个关于波斯诗歌的讲座。| 例句：She's a renowned iranianist, specializing in ancient Persian art. 她是一位著名的伊朗学家，专攻古代波斯艺术。 | 近义词：Iran scholar, Persianist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Iranian + -ist.  "Iranian" refers to Iran, and "-ist" is a suffix denoting a person who studies or specializes in a particular subject.
