cheapo:/ˈtʃiːpoʊ/| n. |便宜货；廉价品|Something that is cheap and often of poor quality. |例句：I bought a cheapo watch online, and it broke within a week. 我在网上买了一块便宜手表，一周内就坏了。

例句：Don't buy those cheapos; they'll fall apart.  别买那些便宜货，它们会散架的。|近义词：bargain, steal, inexpensive item|反义词:luxury item, expensive item|词性:名词


cheapo:/ˈtʃiːpoʊ/| adj. |便宜的；廉价的|Cheap and often of poor quality. |例句：He always buys cheapo clothes. 他总是买廉价的衣服。

例句：It's a cheapo airline, so don't expect much. 这是家廉价航空公司，所以别期望太高。|近义词：cheap, inexpensive, budget|反义词:expensive, pricey, high-end|词性:形容词


词源:  "Cheapo" is a colloquial and informal term derived from "cheap" by adding the suffix "-o."  This suffix is often used to create informal nouns, sometimes with a slightly derogatory connotation.
