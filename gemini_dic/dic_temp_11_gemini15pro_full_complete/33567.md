cilantro:/səˈlæntroʊ/ (US), /sɪˈlæntroʊ/ (UK)| n.|香菜，芫荽|An aromatic herb, Coriandrum sativum, whose leaves are used as a flavoring, especially in Mexican and Asian cooking.  The leaves are called cilantro in American English and coriander leaves in British English.|I love the fresh taste of cilantro in salsa. 我喜欢莎莎酱里香菜的新鲜味道。|
cilantro:/səˈlæntroʊ/ (US), /sɪˈlæntroʊ/ (UK)| n.|香菜，芫荽|Cilantro is a key ingredient in many Southeast Asian dishes. 香菜是许多东南亚菜肴的关键成分。|
