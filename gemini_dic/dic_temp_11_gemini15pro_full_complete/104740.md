katydid: /ˈkeɪtiˌdɪd/ (US), /ˈkeɪtiˌdɪd/ (UK)| n. | 螽斯，纺织娘 | A large green insect of the grasshopper family, known for the chirping sounds made by the male, usually at night. |  例句：The katydids chirped loudly in the trees all night. 螽斯整夜都在树上大声鸣叫。

例句：We could hear the katydids singing in the meadow. 我们能听到草地上螽斯的鸣叫声。 | 近义词：bush cricket, long-horned grasshopper | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From earlier *katy-did-she-did, imitating the insect's sound.
