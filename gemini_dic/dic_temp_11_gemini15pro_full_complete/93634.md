imbosk:/ɪmˈbɒsk/ /ɪmˈbɑːsk/| v. |隐藏；隐蔽；使埋伏|To conceal or hide in a wooded area; to place in ambush.|He imbosked his troops in the forest, waiting for the enemy to approach. 他把部队埋伏在森林里，等待敌人靠近。| The deer imbosked itself in the thicket. 鹿把自己藏在灌木丛里。 |近义词：conceal, hide, shelter, secrete, ensconce|反义词：expose, reveal, uncover|词性：动词

Etymology:
From Old French *embosquer, from en- "in" + bosc "wood," from Late Latin boscus.
