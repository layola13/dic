buffet: /ˈbʌfeɪ/ (US), /ˈbʌfɪt/ (UK) | n. vt. | 自助餐，打击，连续猛击 | A meal where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes placed on a table or counter.  To strike repeatedly and violently. |  例句：The hotel offered a lavish breakfast buffet.  酒店提供丰盛的自助早餐。

例句：The strong winds buffeted the ship. 强风不断地击打着船。 | 近义词：(n.) smorgasbord, spread (vt.) batter, pound | 反义词: (n.) à la carte, set menu | 词性: noun, verb
