outquaffing: /ˌaʊtˈkwɑːfɪŋ/
verb
比…喝得更多
Drinking more than someone else.
He outquaffed his friends at the pub last night. 他昨晚在酒吧比他的朋友们喝得更多。
She boasted about outquaffing the entire rugby team. 她吹嘘自己比整个橄榄球队喝得都多。
Synonyms: outdrink, out-drink
Antonyms: be outdrunk, be out-drunk
词性: 动词
