coelectron:/kəʊɪˈlɛktrɒn/ | n. | 共价电子 | An electron in a covalent bond.  Specifically, one of two or more electrons that form a covalent bond and are shared between the atoms forming the bond. |  A single covalent bond is formed when two atoms share a pair of coelectrons.  两个原子共用一对共价电子时，就形成了一个单共价键。

Coelectrons play a crucial role in the stability of molecules. 共价电子对分子的稳定性起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: bonding electron, shared electron | 反义词: unpaired electron | 词性: noun
