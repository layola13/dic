denize:/dɪˈnaɪz/| vt.|使习惯于海上生活；使适应海洋环境；使能在海上航行；使通晓海事；(尤指)使(船)入级；把…编入海军名册|To accustom to the sea; to make familiar with seafaring; to make capable of navigating the sea; to make versed in nautical matters; (especially) to classify (a ship); to enroll in the navy.|The captain denized the new recruits by making them swab the deck every morning. 船长让新兵每天早上擦洗甲板，以此让他们适应海上生活。|近义词: acclimate, familiarize, habituate|反义词: unaccustomed|词性:动词
