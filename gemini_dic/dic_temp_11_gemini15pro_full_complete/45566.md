curlicued:/ˈkɜːrlɪˌkjuːd/| adj.|卷曲的，花体的|Having curls or flourishes; ornamented with curlicues.|Her handwriting was elegant and curlicued.|近义词：curly, scrolled, ornate, embellished|反义词:straight, plain|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From curl + -icue (a diminutive suffix) + -ed (forming an adjective).  "Curl" ultimately comes from the Old North French word "curler" meaning "to curl or bend."
