intercountry:/ˌɪntərˈkʌntri/ | adj. | 国家间的，国际间的 | Existing or occurring between two or more countries. |  Intercountry adoption is a complex process. 国际收养是一个复杂的过程。|

Intercountry trade has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，国家间贸易显著增加。|

Synonym: international, transnational | Antonym: domestic, intranational | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + country.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually," or "reciprocally." It comes from Latin.  "Country" comes from the Old French word "cuntrée," ultimately derived from the Latin "contra," meaning "against" or "opposite."  In this context, it referred to the land lying opposite or facing another.
