drifty:/ˈdrɪfti/| adj.|漂流的，飘忽不定的，漫无目的的|tending to drift; not guided or controlled; vague and dreamy|The drifty boat was carried out to sea. 那艘漂流的船被带到了海上。|The drifty clouds moved slowly across the sky.  飘忽不定的云朵慢慢地飘过天空。|近义词：aimless, drifting, afloat, vague, dreamy|反义词：focused, purposeful, directed|词性:形容词
词源: From drift + -y.
