innermost:/ˈɪnərˌmoʊst/| adj.|最里面的，最深处的，内心深处的|Being at the innermost part or center; farthest inward.  The innermost thoughts of a person are their most private and secret thoughts. |例句：She shared her innermost fears with her best friend. 她和她最好的朋友分享了她内心最深处的恐惧。

例句：The innermost circle of the maze was where the treasure lay. 迷宫最里面的圆圈就是宝藏所在的地方。|近义词：deepest, central, core, private, intimate|反义词：outermost, external, superficial|词性:形容词
