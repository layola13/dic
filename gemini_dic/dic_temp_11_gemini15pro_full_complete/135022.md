nonnervous:/nɒnˈnɜːrvəs/ | adj. | 非神经的；镇定的；不紧张的 | Not nervous; calm and composed.  Not relating to or involving nerves or the nervous system. |  The surgeon's nonnervous hands performed the delicate operation.  外科医生沉着冷静的双手完成了精细的手术。|  The growth was determined to be nonnervous tissue.  经确定，该增生是非神经组织。 | Synonyms: calm, composed, cool, collected, unflappable, serene, tranquil | Antonyms: nervous, anxious, agitated, jittery, apprehensive | adjective
