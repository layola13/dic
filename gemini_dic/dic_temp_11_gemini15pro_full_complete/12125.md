artsy-fartsy:/ˈɑːrtsi ˈfɑːrtsi/
adjective
附庸风雅的；装腔作势的
Pretentious or affectedly artistic.  Often used to describe something considered excessively or superficially artistic.
She dismissed his paintings as artsy-fartsy nonsense. 她认为他的画作是附庸风雅的胡言乱语。
artsy, pretentious, affected, highfalutin, ostentatious
down-to-earth, simple, unpretentious
adjective
