expulser:/ɪkˈspʌlsər/| n.|驱逐者，排出器|a person or thing that expels something.  Someone or something that forces something to leave.|The expulser removed the troublesome student from the classroom. 驱逐者将捣乱的学生从教室里赶了出去。|近义词：ejector, remover, evictor|反义词:admitter, acceptor|noun

expel:/ɪkˈspɛl/| vt.|驱逐，排出，开除|to force someone to leave a place or organization; to force something out.|The school expelled him for cheating. 学校因他作弊开除他了。|近义词：eject, oust, banish|反义词:admit, accept|verb
