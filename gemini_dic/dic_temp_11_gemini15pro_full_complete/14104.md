authentical:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkəl/ | adj. | 真实的，真正的 | Genuine; real; not false or copied. |  The document is authentical. 这份文件是真的。| 近义词: authentic, genuine, real, true | 反义词: fake, false, spurious, counterfeit | 词性: 形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French autentique, from late Latin authenticus, from Greek authentikos ‘having authority’, from authentēs ‘one who acts on his own authority’.
