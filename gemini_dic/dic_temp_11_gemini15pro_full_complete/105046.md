kerasine:/ˈkɛrəsiːn/ /ˈkɛrəsiːn/ | n. |煤油|A thin, flammable oil distilled from petroleum or shale oil, used as a fuel in jet engines and oil lamps.|The old lamp burned kerosene. 那盏旧灯烧煤油。|Synonyms: paraffin, paraffin oil, lamp oil|Antonyms: water, electricity|noun
