dispassioned:/dɪsˈpæʃənd/ | adj. | 不带感情的；冷静的；客观的 | Not influenced by strong emotion, and therefore able to think clearly and make good decisions. |  She gave a dispassioned account of the incident. 她对事件作了冷静的描述。

The judge listened to the arguments and delivered a dispassioned verdict. 法官听取了双方的辩论，作出了客观的裁决。

| 近义词: impartial, objective, unbiased, unemotional, calm, cool, detached | 反义词: passionate, emotional, biased, prejudiced | 词性: adjective


词源:  Late 16th century: from dis- (expressing reversal) + passionate.
