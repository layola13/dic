nondiphthongal:/ˌnɒndaɪfˈθɒŋɡəl/ | adj. | 非双元音的 |  Not containing or consisting of a diphthong (a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves toward another).  It refers to a single, pure vowel sound. | The vowel in "beet" is nondiphthongal, while the vowel in "bait" is a diphthong.  "beet"中的元音是非双元音的，而"bait"中的元音是双元音。 | monophthongal | diphthongal | adjective
