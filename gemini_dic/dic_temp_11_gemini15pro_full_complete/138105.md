norna:/ˈnɔːrnə/ | n. | 女巫，命运女神 | A female being in Norse mythology who has knowledge of fate and can influence destiny, often depicted as weaving tapestries that represent the course of people's lives.  |  The ancient norna spun the thread of the king's life. 古代的女巫纺出了国王的生命之线。 | synonyms: fate, destiny, seeress | antonyms:  N/A | noun

norna:/ˈnɔːrnə/ | n. | 命运 | Fate or destiny, often associated with a negative outcome. (This usage is less common than the "female being" meaning.) | The norna of the battle was defeat. 这场战斗的命运是失败。| synonyms: fate, destiny, doom | antonyms: fortune, luck | noun
