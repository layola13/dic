dortour: /dɔːrˈtʊər/ (This word is likely misspelled and not a standard English word.) | n. | (Likely intended as "detour") 迂回，绕行路线| A detour is a route that is different from the usual or planned route, often used to avoid a problem or blockage on the road. |  I had to take a detour because of the road closure. 因为道路封闭，我不得不绕道而行。 |  The accident forced us to take a long detour. 事故迫使我们绕了很长一段路。 | synonyms: bypass, roundabout, diversion | antonyms: direct route, straight path | noun
