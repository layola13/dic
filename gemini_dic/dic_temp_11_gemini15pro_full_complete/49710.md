democra:/dɪˈmɒkrə/| n. |民主;民主政体;民主国家|A system of government by the whole population or all the eligible members of a state, typically through elected representatives.  A state governed in such a way.  The common people, considered as the primary source of political power.|例句：The United States is a democra. 美国是一个民主国家。

例句：The democra is facing a challenging time. 这个民主国家正面临挑战。|近义词：republic, commonwealth|反义词：dictatorship, autocracy|词性:noun
Etymology:  From French *démocratie*, from Ancient Greek *dēmokratía* (“democracy, popular government, commonwealth”), from *dêmos* (“people”) + *krátos* (“strength, might, power”).
