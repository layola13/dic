kaftan:/ˈkæftæn/ (US), /ˈkæftɑːn/ (UK)| n.|长袍，卡夫坦|A long, loose robe with wide sleeves, worn chiefly in Eastern countries.|She wore a brightly colored kaftan to the beach party. 她穿着一件颜色鲜艳的卡夫坦去参加沙滩派对。|例句：The kaftan flowed gracefully as she walked.  她走动时，卡夫坦优雅地飘动。|近义词：robe, gown, tunic|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "fitted clothing")|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Turkish *kaftan*, from Persian *خفتان (xaftan)*. Ultimately of uncertain origin.
