encumberingly:/ɪnˈkʌmbərɪŋli/
adverb
沉重地；累赘地
in a way that makes something difficult to do
The rules encumberingly restrict development. 这些规则沉重地限制了发展。

近义词:cumbersomely, awkwardly, heavily, ponderously
反义词:easily, lightly, effortlessly
词性:adverb

词源:
From encumber + -ing + -ly.  Encumber comes from Old French encombrer, from en- 'in' + combre 'obstacle', ultimately from Latin cumulus 'heap'.
