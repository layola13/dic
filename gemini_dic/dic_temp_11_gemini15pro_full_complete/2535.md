admiralty:/ˈædmərəlti/| n.| 海军部；海军上将职位；海事法|The Admiralty was responsible for the navy. 海军部负责海军。|The case fell under the jurisdiction of the admiralty court.  这个案件属于海事法庭的管辖范围。|synonyms: navy, naval force, maritime law |antonyms: None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *admiralté* (Modern French *amirauté*), from Medieval Latin *admirālitās*, from *admirālis* (“admiral”). Ultimately from Arabic أمير البحر‎ (ʾamīr al-baḥr, “commander of the sea”).
