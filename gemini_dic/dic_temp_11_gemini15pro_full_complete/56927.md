doting:/ˈdoʊtɪŋ/| adj.|溺爱的，宠爱的|Showing excessive fondness or love.|She's a doting mother who constantly spoils her children. 她是一位溺爱孩子的母亲，总是宠坏他们。|例句：He's a doting grandfather who loves to spend time with his grandchildren. 他是一位慈爱的祖父，喜欢和孙子们在一起。

例句：My doting parents gave me everything I ever wanted. 我溺爱的父母给了我想要的一切。|近义词：adoring, affectionate, fond, loving|反义词：neglectful, indifferent, uncaring|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "dote," from Middle English doten, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old English *dyttan "to shut up, block."
