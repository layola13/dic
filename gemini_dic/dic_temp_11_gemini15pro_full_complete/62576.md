enervating:/ˈɛnərveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
使人衰弱的；令人丧失活力的
causing one to feel drained of energy or vitality.
The enervating humidity made it difficult to work.  潮湿闷热的天气使人难以工作。

近义词:weakening, debilitating, exhausting, draining
反义词:invigorating, energizing, stimulating
词性:adjective
