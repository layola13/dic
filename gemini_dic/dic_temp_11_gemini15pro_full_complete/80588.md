google:/ˈɡuːɡl/ (US) /ˈɡuːɡəl/ (UK)| n. v.|谷歌；搜索|A search engine and the company that owns it.  It also refers to the act of searching for information online using the Google search engine.|例句：I googled the restaurant to find its address. 我在谷歌上搜索了这家餐厅的地址。

google:/ˈɡuːɡl/ (US) /ˈɡuːɡəl/ (UK)| n. v.|谷歌；搜索|The act of searching for information on the internet using the Google search engine.|例句：She googled the symptoms and found some possible diagnoses.  她在谷歌上搜索了症状，找到了一些可能的诊断。

近义词：search, browse, explore|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for Google as a proper noun or the act of googling.  One could consider avoiding internet searches or using a different search engine as an opposite action.)|词性: noun, verb
