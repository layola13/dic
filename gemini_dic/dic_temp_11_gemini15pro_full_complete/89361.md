homunculus:/hɒˈmʌŋkjuːləs/ /hoʊˈmʌŋkjuːləs/ | n. |小人；侏儒；微型人；（中世纪炼金术士认为能创造的）人造人|A very small human or humanoid creature; a miniature representation of a person. |  In medieval times, alchemists believed they could create a homunculus, an artificial human being. 在中世纪，炼金术士认为他们可以创造一个侏儒，一个人造人。

The sensory homunculus is a distorted representation of the human body, based on the amount of sensory input received from different body parts.  感觉侏儒是一个扭曲的人体表现形式，基于从不同身体部位接收到的感觉输入量。 | 近义词: dwarf, midget, manikin | 反义词: giant | 词性: noun
