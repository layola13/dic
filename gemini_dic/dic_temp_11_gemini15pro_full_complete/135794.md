nonproletarian:/ˌnɒnˌprəʊlɪˈteəriən/ /ˌnɑːnˌproʊlɪˈteriən/
adjective, noun
非无产阶级的，非无产者
Not belonging to the proletariat; not working class; bourgeois.  A person who is not a member of the proletariat.
The nonproletarian classes included the landed aristocracy and the bourgeoisie.  非无产阶级包括地主贵族和资产阶级。
The shop owner, a staunch nonproletarian, opposed the worker's strike.  店主是一个坚定的非无产阶级分子，反对工人罢工。
Synonyms: bourgeois, middle-class, capitalist
Antonyms: proletarian, working-class
adjective
nonproletarian:/ˌnɒnˌprəʊlɪˈteəriən/ /ˌnɑːnˌproʊlɪˈteriən/
noun
非无产者
A person who is not a member of the proletariat.
The nonproletarians often held different political views than the workers. 非无产者通常持有与工人不同的政治观点。
Many of the nonproletarians in the town were small business owners. 镇上的许多非无产者都是小企业主。
Synonyms: bourgeois, capitalist
Antonyms: proletarian, worker
