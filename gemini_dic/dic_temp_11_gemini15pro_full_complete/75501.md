fry-ups:/ˈfraɪ ʌps/| n.|油煎食品；煎炸早餐；英式早餐|A meal of fried food, typically eaten for breakfast.  It usually consists of bacon, sausages, eggs, fried bread, tomatoes, mushrooms, black pudding, etc.|例句：We always have fry-ups on Sundays.  我们星期天总是吃油煎食物。

例句：She made a huge fry-up for breakfast. 她做了一大份油炸早餐。|近义词：cooked breakfast, full English (specifically in England)|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "light breakfast," "continental breakfast")|词性:noun
