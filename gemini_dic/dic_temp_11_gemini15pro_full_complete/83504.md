hackneyed:/ˈhæknid/ /ˈhæknɪd/| adj.|陈腐的，老套的|Made commonplace or trite; stale from overuse.  Lacking freshness or originality.|The plot of the movie was hackneyed and predictable.  这部电影的情节老套且可预测。|近义词：trite, clichéd, banal, stereotyped, overused, stale, worn-out|反义词：original, fresh, novel, innovative, creative|词性:形容词
