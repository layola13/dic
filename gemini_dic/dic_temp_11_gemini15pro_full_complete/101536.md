ironclads:/ˈaɪərnˌklædz/ | n. | 铁甲舰 | A warship of the mid-to-late 19th century, heavily armored with iron plates. | The battle between the Monitor and the Merrimack was a clash of ironclads.  两艘铁甲舰“莫尼特号”和“梅里马克号”之间发生了一场冲突。| Near synonyms:  warships, armored vessels | Antonym:  unarmored ship | Noun
