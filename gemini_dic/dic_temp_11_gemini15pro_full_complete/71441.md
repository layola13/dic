flambeed:/flɑːmˈbeɪd/
adjective
火焰烹调的； flambéed
Covered with liquor, such as brandy, and ignited briefly.
例句：The flambéed cherries jubilee was the perfect end to the meal. 火焰樱桃甜点为这顿饭画上了完美的句号。

例句：He ordered the flambéed steak, enjoying the theatrical display. 他点了火焰牛排，欣赏着这充满戏剧性的展示。

近义词：flaming, ignited
反义词：uncooked, raw
词性：adjective
