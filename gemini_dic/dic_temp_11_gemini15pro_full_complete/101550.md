ironish:/aɪˈrɑːnɪʃ/
adjective
讽刺的，略带讽刺意味的
Resembling or characteristic of irony; exhibiting irony.  Slightly or gently ironic.
He gave an ironish smile. 他露出了一个略带讽刺的微笑。
近义词: ironic, sarcastic, sardonic
反义词: sincere, earnest
词性: adjective

ironish:/aɪˈrɑːnɪʃ/
adjective
讽刺的，略带讽刺意味的
Having the quality of irony, in a less strong or direct way than “ironic”.
"That's ironish," she said, referring to the rain on their wedding day. “真讽刺，”她指的是他们婚礼当天下雨了。
近义词: ironic, wry
反义词: sincere, genuine
词性: adjective
