commiseratively:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪvli/
adverb
同情地，怜悯地
in a way that shows or expresses commiseration.
She patted his hand commiseratively. 她同情地拍了拍他的手。
近义词:sympathetically, compassionately, pityingly
反义词:unsympathetically, heartlessly, cruelly
词性:adverb

commiseration:/kəˌmɪzəˈreɪʃən/
noun
同情，怜悯
a feeling of sympathy and sorrow for the misfortunes of others; compassion.
She felt a deep commiseration for the victims of the earthquake. 她对地震灾民深感同情。
近义词:sympathy, compassion, pity
反义词:indifference, apathy, cruelty
词性:noun

commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/
verb
同情，怜悯
express or feel sympathy or pity; condole.
She commiserated with him over his loss. 她对他的损失表示同情。
近义词:sympathize, condole, empathize
反义词:ignore, disregard
词性:verb


词源:
From Late Latin *commiserativus, from Latin commiseror (“I pity”).
