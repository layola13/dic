exotom:/ˈɛksətɒm/ | n. |外切体|An exotom is a fragment of the ectoplasm, the outer layer of a cell's cytoplasm, that is shed or secreted by certain organisms, like some amoebas.  It can contain various substances like waste products or defensive compounds. |The amoeba released an exotom containing digestive enzymes.| 近义词: cytoplasmic fragment,  secreted vesicle | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
