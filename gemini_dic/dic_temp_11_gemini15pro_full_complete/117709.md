melancholian: /ˌmɛlənˈkɒliən/ | adj. | 忧郁的，悲伤的 | Affected with, characterized by, or showing melancholy; sad; depressed. |  She had a melancholian expression on her face. 她脸上带着忧郁的表情。|  He wrote a melancholian poem about lost love. 他写了一首关于失恋的忧郁诗。 | 近义词: sad, depressed, gloomy, mournful, wistful | 反义词: happy, cheerful, joyful, elated | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *melancholicus, from Greek melankholikós, from mélas (black) + kholé (bile).  The ancient Greeks believed that an excess of black bile caused melancholy.
