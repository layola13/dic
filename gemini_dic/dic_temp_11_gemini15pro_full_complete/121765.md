misquote: /ˌmɪsˈkwəʊt/ (British) /ˌmɪsˈkwoʊt/ (American)
Verb
错误地引用; 歪曲
repeat or copy out (a group of words from a text or speech), attributing them to a wrong author or context.
"Churchill is often misquoted"  “丘吉尔经常被错误引用”
Don't misquote me on this.  在这件事上不要歪曲我的意思。
misrepresent, distort, twist
quote accurately, represent faithfully
