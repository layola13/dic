kamptulicon:/kæmpˈtʌlɪkɒn/ | n. | 印度橡胶地毯；油毡席 | A floor covering made of rubber mixed with cork and canvas.  A durable, waterproof flooring material. |  The Victorian kitchen featured kamptulicon flooring. 维多利亚时代的厨房以油毡席地板为特色。

The kamptulicon was chosen for its durability and water resistance. 选择油毡席是因为它的耐用性和防水性。 | 近义词: linoleum, floorcloth | 反义词: carpet, rug | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κάμπτω (kámptō, “to bend”) + οὖλος (oûlos, “curly, woolly”) + -icon.  Coined in the mid-19th century.
