driftless:/ˈdrɪftləs/| adj.|无冰川的，不受冰川作用影响的|Free from glacial drift; not having been covered by glaciers during the last ice age.  A region exhibiting this characteristic is also called a driftless area.|The Driftless Area in the Midwest is known for its unique topography.  中西部无冰川区以其独特的地形而闻名。|The farm is located in the driftless region of Wisconsin.  这个农场位于威斯康星州的无冰川区。|unglaciated|glaciated|adjective
