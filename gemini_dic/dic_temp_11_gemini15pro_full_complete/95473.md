inciting:/ɪnˈsaɪtɪŋ/
adjective
煽动的，刺激的
encouraging or urging someone to do something violent, illegal, or unpleasant
The inciting incident in the novel is the murder of the protagonist's father.  这本小说中煽动性的事件是主人公父亲的谋杀。
The politician's inciting speech led to riots in the streets.  这位政客煽动性的演讲导致了街头骚乱。
近义词:inflammatory, provocative, agitating, stirring
反义词:calming, soothing, pacifying, appeasing
词性:adjective

inciting:/ɪnˈsaɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle of incite)
煽动，刺激
to encourage or stir up (violent or unlawful action)
His words were inciting the crowd to violence. 他的话煽动人群走向暴力。
The news report was inciting fear and panic among the public. 这则新闻报道在公众中煽动恐惧和恐慌。
近义词:provoking, instigating, fomenting
反义词:deterring, dissuading, discouraging
词性:verb (present participle)
