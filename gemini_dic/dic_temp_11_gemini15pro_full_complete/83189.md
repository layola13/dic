guvnor:/ˈɡʌv.nə(r)/ | n. |老板，上司，州长| A term of address for a man in a position of authority, often used humorously or affectionately.  Derived from "governor."| "Alright, guvnor, what can I do for you?" 好吧，老板，我能为你做什么？ | 近义词: boss, chief, governor, manager | 反义词: subordinate, employee | noun
