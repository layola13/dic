dungarees:/ˌdʌŋɡəˈriːz/
n.
工作裤；背带裤
Durable cotton trousers with a bib held up by straps over the shoulders, often worn as casual wear.
He wore a pair of faded blue dungarees. 他穿了一条褪色的蓝色背带裤。
近义词:overalls, bib overalls
反义词:(无明确反义词)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi डूंगरी (ḍūṅgrī), from डूंगर (ḍūṅgar, “hill, mountain”). Originally referring to a coarse calico cloth imported from India, later applied to garments made from this fabric.
