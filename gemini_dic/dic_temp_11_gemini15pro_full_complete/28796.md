carollers:/ˈkærəlɚz/ (US) /ˈkærələz/ (UK)| n.|颂歌者；圣诞颂歌演唱者|A group of people who sing carols, especially at Christmas.|例句：The carollers went from house to house, spreading Christmas cheer. 颂歌者挨家挨户地唱着歌，传播着圣诞节的欢乐。

例句：We listened to the carollers singing in the snow. 我们听着颂歌者在雪中唱歌。|近义词：singers, choristers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-singers")|词性:noun
