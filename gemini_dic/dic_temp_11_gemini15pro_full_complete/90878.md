hunkerousness: /ˈhʌŋkərəsnəs/
noun
蹲伏的状态；固执；顽固
A state of hunkering down; a stubborn or intractable attitude.
He showed his hunkerousness by refusing to leave his position. 他拒绝离开自己的位置，表现出了他的固执。
Near synonyms:  stubbornness, obstinacy, intransigence, tenacity
Near antonyms: compliance, submissiveness, acquiescence, flexibility
n.


ETYMOLOGY->

From "hunker" (to squat or crouch down) + "-ous" (forming adjectives) + "-ness" (forming nouns of quality or state).  "Hunker" likely comes from the Low German or Dutch "hunkeren," meaning "to sit on one's haunches."
