jurchens:/ˈdʒɜːrtʃənz/
noun
女真人（Jurchen people）
A Tungusic people who inhabited Manchuria and established the Jin dynasty (1115–1234) in northern China.
The Jurchens conquered the Khitan Liao dynasty. 女真人征服了契丹辽朝。
The Jurchens were skilled horsemen and archers. 女真人是熟练的骑手和弓箭手。
Synonyms: Jin people, Jurched
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Chinese *ʒi̯u˩˩-t͡ʃʰiɪn˩˩,  ultimately of unknown origin.  Related to Manchu  *jušen.
