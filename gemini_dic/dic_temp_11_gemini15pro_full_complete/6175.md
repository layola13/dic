amise: /əˈmiːz/  /əˈmaɪz/
n.|错误，疏忽，失误|An oversight or omission; a failure to do something.| He made an unfortunate amise in his calculations. 他在计算中犯了一个不幸的错误。|近义词：error, mistake, blunder, oversight, slip, lapse, omission|反义词：accuracy, correctness|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *amise, past participle of amesdre "to diminish, lose," from Vulgar Latin *adminuere, from Latin admonere "to warn." The current spelling is influenced by the French verb mis- "put, place." Originally meant "loss, damage, deprivation."
