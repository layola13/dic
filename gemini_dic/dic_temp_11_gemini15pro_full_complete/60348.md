egotist:/ˈiːɡətɪst/ | n. | 自我主义者；自负的人 | A person who is excessively conceited or self-absorbed; someone who talks primarily about themselves. |  He's such an egotist, he only ever talks about his own achievements. 他真是个自大狂，总是谈论自己的成就。

| Example 2:  She’s a bit of an egotist and finds it difficult to show empathy towards others. 她有点自负，很难对别人表示同情。 | Synonym: narcissist, egomaniac, braggart, blowhard | Antonym: altruist, humble person | noun
