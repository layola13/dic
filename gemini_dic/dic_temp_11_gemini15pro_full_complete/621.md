abranchiate:/əˈbreɪŋkieɪt/ | adj. |无鳃的，无鳃动物的 | Lacking gills; also, belonging to the Abranchiata, a group of amphibians that lack external gills. | The olm is an abranchiate amphibian that lives in caves.  洞螈是一种生活在洞穴里的无鳃两栖动物。|  gill-less | gilled | adjective

abranchiate:/əˈbreɪŋkieɪt/ | n. |无鳃动物 | An animal lacking gills, especially an amphibian. | Abranchiates like the olm rely on cutaneous respiration. 像洞螈这样的无鳃动物依靠皮肤呼吸。 | gill-less animal | gilled animal | noun
