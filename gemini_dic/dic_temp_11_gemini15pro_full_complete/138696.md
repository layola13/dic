null:/nʌl/| adj. n.|空的，零值的；空值|A null result indicates that the test found nothing. 空的结果表明测试什么也没发现。|例句：The pointer was assigned a null value. 指针被赋值为空值。

例句：The experiment yielded null results.  实验结果无效。

近义词：empty, void, zero, invalid|反义词:valid, non-zero, full|词性:形容词, 名词


词源: From Latin *nullus*, contraction of *ne ullus* "not any".
