jovialize:/ˈdʒoʊviəlaɪz/
verb
使愉快；使欢乐
to make jovial; to be jovial or merry
He jovialized the crowd with his jokes. 他用笑话让观众开心。
近义词: cheer, enliven, gladden, exhilarate
反义词: sadden, depress, deject
词性: verb

jovial:/ˈdʒoʊviəl/
adjective
愉快的；快活的
cheerful and friendly
He was in a jovial mood. 他心情愉快。
近义词: cheerful, merry, jolly, happy
反义词: gloomy, sad, morose
词性: adjective
