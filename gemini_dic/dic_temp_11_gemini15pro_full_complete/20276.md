birthweight:/ˈbɜːθˌweɪt/ (US) /ˈbɜːθweɪt/ (UK)| n.|出生体重|The weight of a baby at birth.|例句：The baby's birthweight was a healthy 7 pounds.  这个婴儿的出生体重是健康的7磅。


例句：Low birthweight can be a sign of premature birth.  低出生体重可能是早产的征兆。

近义词：neonatal weight, birth weight|反义词: None|词性:noun
