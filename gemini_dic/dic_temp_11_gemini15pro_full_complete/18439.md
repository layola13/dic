bema: /ˈbiːmə/ | n. | 讲台，高台，审判席 | A raised platform; in ancient Greece, a raised platform for judges or other officials, or for speakers; in early Christian churches, a platform in the sanctuary. | The preacher delivered his sermon from the bema. 传道者在讲台上发表了布道。| synonyms: platform, podium, dais, pulpit | antonyms: floor, ground | noun
