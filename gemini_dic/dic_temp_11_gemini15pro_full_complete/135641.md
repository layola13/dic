nonpreciousness:/nɒnˈprɛʃəsnəs/ | n. | 非贵重性；平凡；普通 | The nonpreciousness of the materials used makes the product affordable.  使用非贵重材料使得产品价格实惠。|  The artist emphasized the nonpreciousness of everyday objects in his work. 这位艺术家在他的作品中强调了日常物品的平凡性。 | Synonyms: commonness, ordinariness, humbleness | Antonyms: preciousness, rarity, value | noun

Etymology:  From non- (not) + precious + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  "Precious" comes from Old French "precios"  and ultimately from Latin "pretiosus" (valuable).
