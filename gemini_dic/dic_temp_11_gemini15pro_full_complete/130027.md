non-orthodox:/ˌnɒnˈɔːθədɒks/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈɔːrθədɑːks/| adj.|非正统的，非传统的|Not conforming to established doctrine, especially in religion; unconventional.|例句：His non-orthodox approach to teaching surprised many parents. 他的非传统教学方法让许多家长感到惊讶。|近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, heterodox, irregular|反义词：orthodox, conventional, traditional|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From non- + orthodox.  "Orthodox" comes from Late Latin orthodoxus, from Greek orthodoxos "having right opinion," from orthos "right, straight, true" + doxa "opinion, belief."
