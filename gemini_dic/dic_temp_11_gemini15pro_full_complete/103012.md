jihad:/dʒɪˈhɑːd/
noun
圣战，奋斗，努力
a struggle or fight against the enemies of Islam; (more generally) a vigorous campaign for any cause
例句：He called for a jihad against Western culture. 他号召对西方文化进行圣战。

例句：She saw her work as a jihad against poverty. 她将她的工作视为对抗贫困的奋斗。
synonym: crusade, holy war, struggle, fight
antonym: surrender, peace
词性: noun


jihads:/dʒɪˈhɑːdz/
noun
圣战（jihad的复数形式）
plural of jihad
例句：The history books described various jihads undertaken throughout the centuries. 历史书描述了几个世纪以来进行的各种圣战。

例句：Some misinterpreted the concept of jihads as calls for violence.  一些人误解了圣战的概念，将其理解为暴力号召。
synonym: crusades, holy wars, struggles, fights
antonym: surrenders, peaces
词性: noun
