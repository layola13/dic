muckrakers:/ˈmʌkreɪkərz/
noun
揭丑记者；专门揭发丑闻的作家
Journalists who expose corruption and wrongdoing in business, government, or other institutions.
Example: The muckrakers of the early 20th century played a key role in reforming the meatpacking industry.  20世纪初的揭丑记者在肉类加工业改革中发挥了关键作用。

Example:  Modern-day muckrakers continue to investigate and expose abuses of power. 当代的揭丑记者继续调查和揭露权力滥用。
synonym:  investigative journalists, exposers, whistleblowers
antonym:  propagandists, apologists
n.
