microaerophilic:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛrəˈfɪlɪk/ | adj. | 微需氧的 | Requiring oxygen for growth, but at a lower concentration than is present in the atmosphere.  | Campylobacter jejuni is a microaerophilic bacterium.  空肠弯曲杆菌是一种微需氧菌。 | microaerobic | aerobic | adjective
