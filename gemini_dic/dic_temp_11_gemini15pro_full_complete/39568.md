consciousness:/ˈkɒnʃəsnəs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/ (US)| n.|意识；知觉；觉悟|the state of being aware of and responsive to one's surroundings.|例句：He lost consciousness after the accident. 他在事故后失去了意识。|近义词：awareness, sentience, alertness|反义词：unconsciousness, insensibility|noun
