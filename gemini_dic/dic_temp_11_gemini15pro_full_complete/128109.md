nestler:/ˈnɛstlər/| n.|雏鸟，在巢中待哺的幼鸟; 窝中的人(尤指久卧病榻者); 隐居者|A young bird that is not yet ready to leave the nest; a person, especially one who is ill, who spends a lot of time in bed; a person who stays at home a lot. |The nestlers were chirping loudly for food. 雏鸟们大声啁啾着要吃的。|Synonyms: fledgling, chick, hatchling|Antonyms: adult bird|noun
