catadromous:/kəˈtædrəməs/
adjective
溯河产卵的；降河产卵的
Migrating down rivers to spawn in the sea.  Fish, such as eels, that live in fresh water and migrate to salt water to breed are called catadromous.
The European eel is a catadromous fish, spending its adult life in freshwater rivers but migrating to the Sargasso Sea to spawn. 欧洲鳗鱼是一种溯河产卵的鱼类，它的成年生活在淡水河流中，但会迁徙到马尾藻海产卵。

The American eel also exhibits a catadromous life cycle. 美洲鳗鱼也表现出溯河产卵的生命周期。

synonym:  potamodromous (partially), oceanodromous (opposite direction of migration)
antonym: anadromous
词性: adjective
