escheatage:/ɪˈʃiːtɪdʒ/
n.
无人继承的财产；归属权的转移
The reversion of property to the state, or (in feudal law) to a lord, on the death of an owner without legal heirs.
The state claimed the property by escheatage.  这个州根据无继承人财产收归国有法取得了这笔财产。
