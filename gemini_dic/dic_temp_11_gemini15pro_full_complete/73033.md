foothill:/ˈfʊthɪl/ | n. |山麓小丘; 丘陵地带 |A foothill is a low hill at the base of a mountain or mountain range. | We hiked through the foothills of the Himalayas. 我们在喜马拉雅山麓徒步旅行。|  The village nestled in the foothills. 村庄坐落在山麓丘陵地带。 | synonyms: lower slope, piedmont, spur | antonyms: summit, peak | noun
