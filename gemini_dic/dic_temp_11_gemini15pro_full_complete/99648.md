interlays:/ˌɪntərˈleɪz/ | v. | 交织，交错；插入；镶嵌 | To insert or weave between or among other things. |  The artist interlays gold leaf within the painting. 艺术家在画中镶嵌了金箔。 | Synonyms: intertwine, interweave, intermingle | Antonyms: separate, disentangle | Verb

interlay:/ˈɪntərleɪ/ | n. | 交织物；插入物；镶嵌物 | Something inserted or interwoven between other things. | A thin interlay of silk protected the delicate fabric. 一层薄薄的丝绸交织物保护着精致的布料。 | Synonyms: layer, insert, filling | Antonyms: exterior, surface | Noun

Etymology: From the prefix "inter-" (between) + "lay" (to place).
