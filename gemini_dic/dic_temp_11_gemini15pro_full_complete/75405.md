fructification:/ˌfruːktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
结果实;子实体;成就;成果
The process of bearing fruit; the state of having borne fruit; the product of such a process.  The realization of a plan or project.
例句：The fructification of his years of research was a groundbreaking new drug.  他多年研究的成果是一种突破性的新药。

例句：The apple tree's fructification was abundant this year. 今年苹果树结果实累累。


近义词：fruiting, ripening, development, fulfillment, realization, completion
反义词：failure, abortion, barrenness
词性：noun
