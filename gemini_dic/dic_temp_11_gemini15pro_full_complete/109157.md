legit:/ləˈdʒɪt/| adj.|合法的，正当的；真实的；很棒的|Something that is legit is considered authentic, genuine, or conforming to the law or rules.  It can also be used as slang to mean "cool" or "awesome".|That concert was legit! 那场音乐会太棒了！|synonyms: genuine, authentic, legal, valid, real, cool, awesome|antonyms: illegal, fake, counterfeit, bogus|adjective

legit:/ləˈdʒɪt/| n.|合法的东西；真实的东西|Something that is considered authentic or genuine.|Have you seen his new car? It's a legit. 你看到他的新车了吗？是辆真家伙。|synonyms: real thing, genuine article|antonyms: fake, counterfeit|noun
