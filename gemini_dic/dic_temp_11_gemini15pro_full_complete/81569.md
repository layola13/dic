graziers:/ˈɡreɪʒərz/
noun
牧场主，放牧者
People who raise grazing livestock, such as cattle or sheep.
The graziers moved their herds to higher ground for the summer. 牧场主们把他们的牛群移到更高的地上过夏天。
近义词: ranchers, herdsmen, stockmen, pastoralists
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

grazier:/ˈɡreɪʒər/
noun
牧场主，放牧者
A person who grazes livestock.
The grazier carefully monitored the condition of his pastures.  这位牧场主仔细地监测着牧场的状况。
近义词: rancher, herdsman, stockman, pastoralist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From graze (v.) + -er (suffix forming agent nouns).  Ultimately from Old English *græsian, of Germanic origin.
