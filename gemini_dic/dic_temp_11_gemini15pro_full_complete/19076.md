betrayal:/bɪˈtreɪəl/| n.|背叛，辜负，出卖|The betrayal of a friend is very painful.  背叛朋友是非常痛苦的。|例句：His betrayal of his team was unforgivable. 他对团队的背叛是不可原谅的。

|例句：She felt a deep sense of betrayal after her friend lied to her.  在她的朋友对她撒谎之后，她感到了一种深深的背叛感。|近义词：treachery, treason, perfidy, disloyalty|反义词：loyalty, fidelity, faithfulness|词性:noun

词源: From Old French *betraial*  "treachery," from *betrair* "to betray,"  ultimately from Latin *tradere* "to hand over, deliver."
