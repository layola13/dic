combbroach:/ˈkoʊmˌbroʊtʃ/| n. |梳蟑螂|A type of cockroach (Supella longipalpa) also known as the brown-banded cockroach, recognized by the two light-colored bands across its back.| I found a combbroach in the kitchen. 我在厨房里发现了一只梳蟑螂。|Synonyms: brown-banded cockroach, cockroach|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
Etymology: Comb + Roach.  "Comb" refers to the two light bands resembling the teeth of a comb. "Roach" is a shortened form of "cockroach."|
