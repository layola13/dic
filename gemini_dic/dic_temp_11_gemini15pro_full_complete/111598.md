lobster-trap:/ˈlɑːbstər træp/ | n. | 龙虾陷阱 | A trap designed to catch lobsters. | The lobsterman hauled up the lobster-trap, hoping for a good catch. 渔夫拉起龙虾陷阱，希望能有个好收成。 |  The lobster was caught in a lobster-trap. 这只龙虾被龙虾陷阱捉住了。 | synonyms: lobster pot, creel | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "lobster release" as a contrasting concept) | noun
