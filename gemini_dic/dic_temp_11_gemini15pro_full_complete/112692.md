lowlights:/ˈloʊlaɪts/| n.|低光部分；（尤指头发的）挑染的较深颜色部分；不精彩的部分；败笔|The lowlights in her hair really bring out the color of her eyes. 她头发上挑染的深色部分衬托出她眼睛的颜色。|例句：The photographer used lowlights to create a dramatic effect. 摄影师用低光营造出戏剧性的效果。

例句：One of the lowlights of the trip was the terrible hotel we stayed in.  这次旅行中最糟糕的部分之一就是我们住的那家可怕的酒店。|近义词：dark streaks, shadows, downsides, drawbacks|反义词:highlights, bright spots, advantages, merits|词性:noun
