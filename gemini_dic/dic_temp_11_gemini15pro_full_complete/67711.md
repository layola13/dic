exungulate:/ɛksˈʌŋɡjʊleɪt/
| adj. |无蹄的；失去蹄的|Having lost hoofs, or characterized by the absence of hoofs.  Relating to mammals that have evolved from hoofed ancestors but no longer possess hoofs.|The exungulate mammals like whales and dolphins evolved from hoofed ancestors.  鲸鱼和海豚等无蹄哺乳动物是从有蹄动物进化而来的。|近义词: hoofless|反义词: ungulate|词性: adjective
