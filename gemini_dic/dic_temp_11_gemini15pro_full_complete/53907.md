discounters:/ˈdɪskaʊntərz/
n.
折扣店；廉价商店
Retailers who sell goods at reduced prices.
Many discounters are opening new stores in the city. 许多折扣店正在城市里开新店。
Shoppers flocked to the discounters for bargains. 购物者涌向折扣店寻找便宜货。
synonyms: bargain stores, budget stores, cheap stores, off-price retailers
antonyms: luxury stores, high-end retailers, boutiques
noun
