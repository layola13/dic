larva:/ˈlɑːrvə/ (plural larvae /ˈlɑːrviː/)
|n.|幼虫；幼体|An immature form of an insect or other animal that undergoes metamorphosis, typically having a wormlike shape and lacking the adult's characteristic features.|The butterfly larva is a caterpillar.|近义词:grub, caterpillar, maggot|反义词:adult, imago|noun
