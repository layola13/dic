begone:/bɪˈɡɒn/ /bɪˈɡɔːn/
verb
离去；走开
Go away; depart at once.
Begone, foul beast! 离开，你这肮脏的野兽！
The days of imperial grandeur are begone. 帝国辉煌的日子已经一去不复返了。
Synonyms: depart, leave, go away, get out, vanish
Antonyms: arrive, come, stay, remain
词性:动词
