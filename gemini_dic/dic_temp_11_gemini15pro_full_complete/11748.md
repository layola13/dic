aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 芳香的；芬芳的；有香味的 | Having a pleasant and distinctive smell.  | The air was aromatic with the scent of pine needles. 空气中弥漫着松针的香味。 | fragrant, sweet-smelling, perfumed | odorless, fetid | adjective

aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/ | n. | 芳香植物；芳香物质 | A plant, substance, or chemical that has a strong, pleasant smell. | Many herbs and spices are aromatics. 许多草药和香料都是芳香植物。 | fragrance, perfume, spice | stench | noun


aromatous:/ˌærəˈmeɪtəs/ | adj. | 芳香的；芬芳的；有香味的  | Containing an aromatic compound, especially a benzene ring. (Primarily used in chemistry) |  Benzene is an example of an aromatous compound. 苯是一个芳香族化合物的例子。 | aromatic, fragrant |  non-aromatic, aliphatic | adjective
