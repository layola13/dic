co-inhere:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɪər/| v. |共同存在，相互依存|To exist together or be inherently connected.|例句：The different elements of the ecosystem co-inhere and depend on each other. 生态系统的不同元素相互依存，相互依赖。

例句：The concepts of space and time co-inhere in Einstein's theory of relativity.  在爱因斯坦的相对论中，空间和时间的概念是相互依存的。|近义词：coexist, interrelate, intertwine|反义词:separate, independent|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "co-" (together) + "inhaerere" (to stick in, to inhere).  "Inhaerere" is formed from "in-" (in) + "haerere" (to stick).
