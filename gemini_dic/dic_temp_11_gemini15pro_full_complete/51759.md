devotedness:/dɪˈvoʊtɪdnəs/| n.|挚爱，忠诚，虔诚|The dog showed unwavering devotedness to its owner. 这只狗对它的主人表现出 unwavering 的忠诚。|例句：Her devotedness to her children was admirable. 她对孩子的挚爱令人钦佩。|近义词：loyalty, dedication, commitment, piety|反义词：disloyalty, indifference, apathy|词性:名词
Etymology: From "devoted" + "-ness". "Devoted" comes from the Old French "devot" (past participle of "devoer" meaning "to vow, dedicate"), ultimately from the Latin "vovere" (to vow).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
