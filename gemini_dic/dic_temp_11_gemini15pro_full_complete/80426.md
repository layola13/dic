gonangium:/ɡoʊˈnændʒiəm/| n. |生殖鞘，芽鞘，胞囊|A gonangium is a protective capsule or covering, typically found in hydroids (a type of small aquatic animal related to jellyfish and corals), that encloses reproductive polyps.|The gonangium releases medusae, which are the free-swimming form of the hydroid.|近义词:capsule, covering|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "exposed polyp" in a descriptive sense)|noun
