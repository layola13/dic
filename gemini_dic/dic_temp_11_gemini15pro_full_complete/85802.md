hazarding:/ˈhæzərdɪŋ/| v. |冒险；使遭受危险|venturing something that could result in loss or danger.  |He was hazarding his reputation by getting involved in the shady deal. 他参与这笔可疑的交易是在拿自己的名誉冒险。|近义词：risking, venturing, gambling, endangering|反义词：protecting, safeguarding|词性: 动词 (Present Participle of hazard)

词源:
From Old French *hasard (Modern French hasard), from Arabic az-zahr (الزهر) "the die," from Persian zahr. Originally the name of a dice game.
