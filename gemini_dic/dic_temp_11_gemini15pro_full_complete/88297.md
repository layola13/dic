hipe:/haɪp/
n.|炒作；兴奋；兴奋剂|A state of excitement, often exaggerated or artificial, created around a person, product, or idea, typically for promotional purposes.|The marketing team generated a lot of hipe for the new phone.  市场团队为这款新手机制造了大量的宣传。|synonyms: hype, publicity, promotion|antonyms: understatement, downplaying|noun
