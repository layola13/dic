dreadingly:/ˈdrɛdɪŋli/
adverb
害怕地，恐惧地
in a way that shows fear and apprehension.
She dreadingly opened the letter. 她害怕地打开信。
He dreadingly awaited the results of the medical tests. 他恐惧地等待着体检结果。
近义词: fearfully, apprehensively, anxiously
反义词: eagerly, excitedly, confidently
词性: 副词

词源: From dread (verb) + -ing + -ly.  "Dread" comes from Old English *drǣdan, from Proto-Germanic *drēzaną (“to feel anxiety”).
