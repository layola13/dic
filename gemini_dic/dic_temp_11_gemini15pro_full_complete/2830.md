adventurer:/ədˈvɛntʃərər/ (US), /ədˈventʃərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|冒险家；探险者|A person who enjoys or seeks adventure, especially in exploring unknown lands or engaging in hazardous activities.|例句：He was a seasoned adventurer who had travelled the world. 他是一位经验丰富的冒险家，游历过世界各地。

例句：She dreamed of becoming an adventurer and exploring the Amazon rainforest. 她梦想成为一名冒险家，探索亚马逊雨林。|近义词：explorer, traveler, voyager, daredevil|反义词:homebody, stay-at-home|词性:noun
