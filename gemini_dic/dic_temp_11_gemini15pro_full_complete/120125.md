mimotannic: /ˌmɪmoʊˈtænɪk/
adjective
含羞草鞣酸的；含羞草单宁酸的
Relating to or containing mimotannic acid, a type of tannin found in some plants, particularly Mimosa species.  It is a specific type of tannin.
The bark of the mimosa tree yields a mimotannic acid used in tanning. 含羞草树的树皮会产生一种用于鞣革的含羞草鞣酸。
Mimotannic acid is extracted from the bark and used in tanning leather. 从树皮中提取的含羞草单宁酸，用于鞣革。
近义词: tannic
反义词: None
