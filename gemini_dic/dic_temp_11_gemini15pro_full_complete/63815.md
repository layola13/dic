epidermis:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪs/| n.|表皮，上皮|The epidermis is the outer layer of the skin. 表皮是皮肤的外层。|例句：The sunburned epidermis peeled off. 晒伤的表皮脱落了。

例句：Applying moisturizer helps to protect the epidermis from dryness. 涂抹保湿霜有助于保护表皮免受干燥。|近义词：skin, cuticle|反义词:dermis, hypodermis|词性:noun
