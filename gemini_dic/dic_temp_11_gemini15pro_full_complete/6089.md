americanism:/əˈmɛrɪkənɪzəm/ | n. | 美国主义；美国英语；美国特有的事物 | A word or phrase characteristic of American English; a custom or trait peculiar to the United States; devotion to or advocacy of the interests and culture of the U.S. | Example 1: Using "elevator" instead of "lift" is an Americanism.  用“elevator”而不是“lift”是美式英语。

Example 2: Baseball is considered a classic Americanism. 棒球被认为是典型的美国事物。 |Synonyms: American way, American characteristic, Yankeeism | Antonyms: Britishism, foreignism | noun
