hypinosis:/ˌhaɪpɪˈnoʊsɪs/ | n. |催眠不足;睡眠不足|A state of abnormally light or insufficient sleep; less than normal sleep. | He suffered from hypinosis and often felt tired during the day. 他患有睡眠不足，白天经常感到疲倦。| Example: The doctor diagnosed him with hypinosis after he complained of constant fatigue. 医生在他抱怨持续疲劳后，诊断他为睡眠不足。| Synonym: insomnia, sleep deprivation, hyposomnia | Antonym: hypersomnia | Noun
