drafters:/ˈdræftərz/ | n. |起草人，制图员|A person who draws plans or designs, especially technical drawings. |The drafters worked late into the night to finish the blueprints. 制图员们工作到深夜才完成蓝图。|近义词： designers, planners, architects (for building plans) |反义词: N/A| noun

drafters:/ˈdræftərz/ | n. |被征召入伍者|A person who is drafted into military service.  |Many young drafters were sent to the front lines. 许多年轻的被征召入伍者被派往了前线。|近义词：conscripts, recruits |反义词: volunteers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "draft,"  which comes from the Old French "draught" or "traict," meaning "a pulling, drawing, or sketch." This ultimately derives from the Latin "trahere," meaning "to draw or pull."
