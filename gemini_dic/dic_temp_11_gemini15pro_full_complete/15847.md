ballast: /ˈbæləst/ | n. | 压舱物；稳定物；镇流器 | Heavy material, such as gravel, sand, or iron, placed low in a vessel to improve its stability.  Something that gives stability, especially emotional stability. An electrical device that limits the current in a fluorescent lamp, etc. | The ship took on ballast before the voyage. 这艘船在航行前装载了压舱物。 | synonyms: counterweight, stabilizer, anchor | antonyms: instability, volatility | noun

ballasts: /ˈbæləsts/ | n. | 压舱物（复数）；稳定物（复数）；镇流器（复数）|  The plural form of "ballast." Refers to multiple instances of heavy materials used for stability, or multiple electrical ballasts. | The ship's ballasts were filled with seawater.  这艘船的压舱物里装满了海水。 | synonyms: counterweights, stabilizers, anchors | antonyms: instabilities, volatilities | noun
