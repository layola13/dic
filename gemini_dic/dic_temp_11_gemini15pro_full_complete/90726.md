humiliated:/hjuːˈmɪlieɪtɪd/| adj.|羞辱的，丢脸的|Made to feel ashamed or foolish.|例句：She felt humiliated when she tripped and fell in front of everyone. 她当着所有人的面绊倒了，感到很丢脸。

例句：He was humiliated by his boss's harsh criticism. 他被老板的严厉批评羞辱了。|近义词：ashamed, embarrassed, mortified|反义词：proud, honored, respected|词性:形容词

humiliate:/hjuːˈmɪlieɪt/| vt.|羞辱，使丢脸|To make someone feel ashamed or foolish.|例句：He tried to humiliate her in front of her friends. 他试图在她朋友面前羞辱她。

例句：The public apology humiliated him further. 公开道歉让他更加丢脸。|近义词：embarrass, mortify, shame|反义词：honor, respect, praise|词性:动词

humiliation:/hjuːˌmɪliˈeɪʃn/| n.|羞辱，丢脸|The feeling of being ashamed or foolish.|例句：The humiliation of defeat was hard to bear.  失败的耻辱难以忍受。
