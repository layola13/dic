creian:/ˈkreɪən/| v.|相信；信任；认为|To have confidence or faith in someone or something; to accept something as true.|例句：They creian that he was innocent. 他们相信他是无辜的。|近义词：believe, trust, deem, suppose, reckon|反义词：disbelieve, doubt, distrust|词性:动词

词源:
Middle English *creien, from Old English *crēogan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *kreujaną (“to believe”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱrew- (“to believe, trust”). Cognate with Old Frisian kreia, Old Saxon kriuian, Old High German kriuwan, Old Norse krygja, Gothic 𐌺𐍂𐌹𐌿𐌱𐌾𐌰𐌽 (kriubjan).  The modern form is influenced by Old Norse trúa.
