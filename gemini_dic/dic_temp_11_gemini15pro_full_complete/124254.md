词源:  From Middle English *mostik, a variant of *mest, superlative of *moche, from Old English *mǣst (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *maistaz, superlative of *mikilaz (“much, great, big”), from Proto-Indo-European *meǵh₂- (“great, big”). Cognate with West Frisian mast (“most”), Dutch meest (“most”), German meist (“most”), Old Norse mestr (“most”). Related to much, more.
