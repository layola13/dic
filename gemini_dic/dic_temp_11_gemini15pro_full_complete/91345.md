hydroplaning: /ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪnɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhaɪdrəʊˌpleɪnɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 水漂，水上滑行|The loss of control of a vehicle resulting from a layer of water between the tires and the road surface, preventing the tires from gripping the road.|例句：The car lost control due to hydroplaning on the wet road. 汽车因在湿滑路面上打滑而失控。

例句：Hydroplaning can be a serious hazard in rainy weather. 水漂现象在雨天可能是一个严重的危险。|近义词：aquaplaning|反义词: traction, grip|词性:noun
