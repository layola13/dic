modernism:/ˈmɒdənɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərˌnɪzəm/ (US)| n.|现代主义|A style or movement in the arts that aims to break with classical and traditional forms.  A movement embracing change and experimentation, especially in the arts, architecture and literature, during the late 19th and early 20th centuries.|Modernism in art rejected traditional styles and embraced new forms of expression.  艺术中的现代主义摒弃了传统风格，拥抱了新的表达形式。

The museum's collection includes many examples of early modernism.  博物馆的藏品包含许多早期现代主义的例子。|近义词：avant-garde, contemporary|反义词：traditionalism, classicism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From modern + -ism.  "Modern" comes from the Late Latin "modernus," meaning "of the present mode or fashion."
