maguari:/ˌmɑːɡwəˈriː/ | n. |鹊雁，黑顶鹊雁|A large South American bird (Anhima cornuta) related to the screamers, having a long, slender neck, a small head with a frontal horn, and a loud, resonant call.|例句：The maguari's call echoed across the wetlands. 鹊雁的叫声在湿地上回荡。

例句：We observed a maguari foraging in the shallows. 我们观察到一只鹊雁在浅滩觅食。|近义词：screamer, horned screamer |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
