indivisibility:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪzəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可分割性
The quality or state of being indivisible; incapable of being divided.
The indivisibility of the nation is a core principle.  国家的不可分割性是一个核心原则。
synonyms: unity, wholeness, totality, inseparability
antonyms: divisibility, separability
noun
