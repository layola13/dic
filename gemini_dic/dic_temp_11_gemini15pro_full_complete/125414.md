multiparty:/ˌmʌltiˈpɑːrti/ | adj. | 多方的，多党制的 | involving several or many parties |  The multiparty negotiations were complex and challenging. 多方谈判复杂而具有挑战性。| synonyms: multilateral, multinational | antonyms: bilateral, unilateral | adjective

multiparty:/ˌmʌltiˈpɑːrti/ | n. | 多方会谈，多党制 | a system in which multiple political parties have the capacity to gain control of government offices, separately or in coalition | The country transitioned to a multiparty system after the fall of the dictatorship.  这个国家在独裁统治垮台后过渡到多党制。| synonyms: multi-party system | antonyms: one-party system, single-party system | noun
