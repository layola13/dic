metromaniac: /ˌmɛtroʊˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 狂写诗癖者；写诗狂 | A person with an obsession for writing verse, especially bad verse. | He was a known metromaniac, filling notebooks with rambling, incoherent poems. 他是个有名的写诗狂，笔记本里写满了漫无边际、语无伦次的诗。| synonyms: versifier, rhymester, poetaster | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "prose writer" if contrasting styles) | noun
