devastates:/ˈdɛvəsteɪts/
verb
摧毁，毁灭
utterly destroys or ruins
The hurricane devastated the coastal town.  飓风摧毁了沿海城镇。

近义词: ruins, destroys, wrecks, ravages
反义词: builds, creates, restores, improves
词性: verb


devastating:/ˈdɛvəsteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
毁灭性的，极具破坏力的
causing widespread destruction or damage
The earthquake had a devastating impact on the region. 地震对该地区造成了毁灭性的影响。

近义词: destructive, catastrophic, ruinous, calamitous
反义词: beneficial, helpful, constructive, restorative
词性: adjective


devastation:/ˌdɛvəˈsteɪʃən/
noun
毁灭，破坏
widespread destruction or damage
The devastation caused by the war was immense. 战争造成的破坏是巨大的。

近义词: destruction, ruin, wreckage, havoc
反义词: construction, creation, restoration, improvement
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *devastare, from de- "down, completely" + vastare "to lay waste," from vastus "empty, desolate."
