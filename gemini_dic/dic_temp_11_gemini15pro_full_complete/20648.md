blackout:/ˈblækaʊt/ (US) /ˈblækaʊt/ (UK)| n.|停电；断电；灯火管制；消息封锁；昏厥；记忆丧失|A blackout is a period of darkness caused by a failure of the electricity supply, or a period of time when something is not available or is forbidden. It can also refer to a temporary loss of consciousness or memory.|例句：The entire city experienced a blackout during the storm.  整个城市在暴风雨中经历了停电。

例句：He suffered a blackout after the car accident. 他在车祸后昏厥了。|近义词：power outage, power failure, amnesia (for memory loss), suppression (for information blackout)|反义词：illumination, restoration (of power)|词性:名词
