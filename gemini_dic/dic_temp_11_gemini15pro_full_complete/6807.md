anarchic:/əˈnɑːkɪk/ | adj. |无政府的，混乱的|Lacking order or control; chaotic. Characterized by anarchy.|例句：The anarchic state of the country after the revolution was alarming. 革命后国家的无政府状态令人担忧。

例句：The classroom became anarchic when the teacher left the room. 老师离开教室后，教室里变得一片混乱。

|近义词：lawless, chaotic, disorderly, ungoverned, tumultuous|反义词：orderly, organized, controlled, lawful, regulated|词性:形容词
