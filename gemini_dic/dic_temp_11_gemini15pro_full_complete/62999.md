enormously:/ɪˈnɔːrməsli/| adv.|非常地，巨大地|very greatly; to a very great degree|The population has increased enormously in recent years. 近年来人口急剧增长。|近义词：hugely, immensely, vastly, tremendously|反义词：slightly, marginally, insignificantly|词性:副词
Etymology: From enormous + -ly.  Enormous comes from the Latin enormis "irregular, immense," from e- "out of" + norma "rule, pattern."
