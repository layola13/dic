electrification:/ɪˌlektrɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 电气化 | The process of converting something to use electrical power. |  The electrification of the railway line will make journeys faster and more efficient. 铁路电气化将使旅程更快、更高效。

The electrification of rural areas is a key step in improving living standards. 农村地区的电气化是提高生活水平的关键一步。 | 近义词: wiring, powering | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From electrify + -fication.  "Electrify" comes from "electric" + "-fy". "Electric" derives from the Late Latin "electricus", meaning "produced from amber by friction," ultimately from the Greek "ēlektron," meaning "amber." "-fy" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become."  "-fication" is a suffix forming nouns of action or process.
