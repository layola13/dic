mistutors:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːtərz/ | n. |  家教（复数）|  Plural form of "mistutor," meaning multiple people who provide inadequate or incorrect tutoring.  It combines the prefix "mis-" (meaning wrongly or badly) with "tutor." | The students complained about their mistutors, who frequently provided incorrect information.  这些学生抱怨他们的家教，他们经常提供错误的信息。|  bad tutors, incompetent tutors | good tutors, qualified tutors | noun
