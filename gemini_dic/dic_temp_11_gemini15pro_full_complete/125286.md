multiflorous:/ˌmʌltɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
多花的；多花序的
Having many flowers; bearing many flowers or blossoms.
The multiflorous rose bush filled the garden with fragrance.  这棵多花的玫瑰灌木让花园里充满了芬芳。
The multiflorous orchid was a prize-winning specimen.  这株多花的兰花是一个获奖的标本。
近义词: floriferous, multifloral, polyanthous
反义词: pauciflorous, oligoflorous
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *multiflōrus, from multi- (“many”) + flōs (“flower”).
