What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？


Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？


Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？


Simple Past:
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？


Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm yesterday? = 昨天晚上8点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？


Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? = 他们在看电影之前吃了什么？


Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？


Simple Future:
What will you do this weekend? = 你这个周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？


Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？


Future Perfect:
What will you have finished by Friday? = 你到星期五会完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们会取得什么成就？


Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做这件事五年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么专业多久了？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would say it. = 如果我知道该说什么，我就会说。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


Interrogative Sentences:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you planning to do tomorrow? = 你打算明天做什么？


Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 她不明白我在说什么。
