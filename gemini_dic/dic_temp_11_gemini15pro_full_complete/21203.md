blockheadedly:/ˌblɒkˈhɛdɪdli/ /ˌblɑːkˈhɛdɪdli/| adv.|愚蠢地，笨拙地|In a stupid or obstinate manner; like a blockhead.  Characterized by lack of intelligence or good judgment.|He blockheadedly ignored all the advice he was given and went ahead with his disastrous plan. 他愚蠢地无视所有给他的建议，继续执行他那灾难性的计划。|Synonyms: stupidly, foolishly, obtusely, densely, doltishly|Antonyms: intelligently, wisely, cleverly, astutely|Adverb
