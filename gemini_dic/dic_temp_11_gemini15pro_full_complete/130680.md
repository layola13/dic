nonaffectingly:/ˌnɒnəˈfɛktɪŋli/
adv.
不动人地；没有感情地；不矫揉造作地
in a way that does not show strong emotion or try to cause strong emotion in others.
She spoke nonaffectingly about the ordeal. 她不动声色地讲述了那段痛苦的经历。
Synonyms: unemotionally, impassively, dispassionately, indifferently
Antonyms: emotionally, affectingly, movingly
词性: 副词
