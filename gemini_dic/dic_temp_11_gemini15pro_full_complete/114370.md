majors:/ˈmeɪdʒərz/ | n. | 专业; 主修科目; | A student's major is the main subject that they study at college or university. |  She's majoring in history. 她主修历史。| synonyms:  specialization, concentration, field of study | antonyms: minor | noun

majors:/ˈmeɪdʒərz/ | v. | 主修; 专攻 | To major in a particular subject means to choose it as your main subject of study at college or university. | He majored in physics at Harvard. 他在哈佛大学主修物理。 | synonyms: specialize, concentrate | antonyms: minor | verb
