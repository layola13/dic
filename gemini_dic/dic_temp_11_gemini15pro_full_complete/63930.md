epipodial: /ˌɛpɪˈpoʊdiəl/
adjective |  腹足类的| Relating to the epipodium, a muscular ridge or fold on the foot of some gastropods. | The epipodial tentacles of the sea hare are sensitive to touch. 海兔的腹足触手对触碰很敏感。|近义词: parapodial | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could potentially use terms relating to other parts of the gastropod anatomy) |词性: adjective
