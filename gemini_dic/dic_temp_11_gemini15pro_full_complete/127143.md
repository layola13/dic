词源：
"Natterjack" is a combination of "natter" (meaning to chatter or gossip) and "jack," a common name added to various animals.  This likely refers to the toad's distinctive, loud croaking call.
"Natter" comes from the Middle Low German *knateren*, meaning "to gnash, crackle".
