monocarpous:/ˌmɒnəˈkɑːpəs/ | adj. | 单果的；一次结实的 | Bearing fruit only once and then dying. | Example: The agave plant is monocarpous, blooming spectacularly once before dying.  龙舌兰植物是单果的，在死前会壮丽地开花一次。

Example: Many bamboo species are monocarpous and can take decades to flower. 许多竹子种类都是单果的，可能需要几十年才会开花。 | Synonym: semelparous, hapaxanth | Antonym: polycarpous, iteroparous | adjective
