heptastich:/ˈheptəˌstɪk/
noun
七行诗
A stanza or poem of seven lines.
Edgar Allan Poe's "Annabel Lee" is a heptastich.  埃德加·爱伦·坡的《安娜贝尔·李》是一首七行诗。
synonym: septet
antonym: (No direct antonym, but could consider shorter stanzas like couplet or quatrain)
