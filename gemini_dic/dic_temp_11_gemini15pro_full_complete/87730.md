hexine:/ˈhɛksaɪn/| n. |己炔|An unsaturated hydrocarbon, specifically an alkyne, with six carbon atoms and a triple bond.  It is part of a larger group of chemicals called alkynes, which are characterized by a carbon-carbon triple bond.  Hexyne has several isomers depending on the location of the triple bond.|Hexyne is used in organic synthesis.|近义词:alkyne, hexyne isomer (e.g., 1-hexyne, 2-hexyne, 3-hexyne)|反义词:hexane (saturated hydrocarbon)|词性:noun
