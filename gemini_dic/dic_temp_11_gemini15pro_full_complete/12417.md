asiphonea:/ˌeɪsaɪˈfoʊniə/ | n. | 无管类; 无管亚纲 |A subclass of mollusks characterized by the absence of a siphuncle in the shell.  A group of extinct cephalopods that lacked a siphuncle. | This fossil belongs to the subclass Asiphonea.  这个化石属于无管亚纲。|  Asiphonea were common in the Paleozoic era. 无管类在古生代很常见。| synonyms:  Ellesmeroceratoidea (in some classifications) | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but can be contrasted with cephalopods *with* siphuncles, such as Nautiloidea and Ammonoidea) | noun
