incommensurate:/ˌɪnkəˈmɛnsərət/ |adj.|不相称的，不匹配的|Not proportionate; not of equal measure or extent; not adequate or sufficient.|Her experience is incommensurate with her age. 她的经验与她的年龄不相称。|The punishment was incommensurate with the crime.  惩罚与罪行不相称。|近义词:disproportionate, inadequate, insufficient, incompatible|反义词:commensurate, proportionate, adequate, sufficient|词性:形容词
词源:Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + commensurate.
