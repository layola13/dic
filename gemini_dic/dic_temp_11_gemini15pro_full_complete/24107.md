brew:/bruː/
n.| 酿造酒; 冲泡的饮料 |A mixture of hot water and ground coffee, tea, or other ingredients.|  I ordered a dark brew at the coffee shop. 我在咖啡店点了一杯黑咖啡。
v.| 酿造; 冲泡; 酝酿; 即将发生 | To make (beer) by soaking, boiling, and fermenting malt and hops.|  He brewed a pot of strong tea. 他泡了一壶浓茶. | synonyms: concoct, prepare,  steep, infuse | antonyms:  none | noun
