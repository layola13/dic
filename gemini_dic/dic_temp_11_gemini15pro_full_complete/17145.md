例句：His battle-scarred face showed the horrors he had witnessed. 他伤痕累累的脸庞展现了他所目睹的恐怖景象。

近义词：war-torn, veteran, hardened,  experienced | 反义词：pristine, untouched, unscathed | 词性: 形容词
