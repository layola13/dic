out-vote:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊt/
verb
以票数胜过；投票击败
to defeat in a vote
The proposal to build a new shopping mall was out-voted by the local residents.  当地居民投票否决了建造新购物中心的提案。
The chairman was out-voted by the board members. 董事长被董事会成员投票否决。
近义词:defeat, overrule
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性:v.
