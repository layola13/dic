disabuse:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːz/
verb
使醒悟，纠正…的错误
To free someone from a misconception or deception.
The doctor disabused her of the notion that her condition was incurable. 医生纠正了她认为自己的病情无法治愈的错误观念。

近义词: enlighten, undeceive, correct, set straight
反义词: deceive, mislead, delude
词性: verb (动词)
