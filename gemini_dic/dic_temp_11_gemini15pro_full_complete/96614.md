indulgence:/ɪnˈdʌldʒəns/ | n. | 放纵；沉溺；嗜好；宽容；恩惠 | The act of indulging; gratification of desire.  |  She allowed herself the indulgence of a long hot bath. 她让自己尽情享受了一次长时间的热水澡。 |Synonyms: gratification, pleasure, treat, luxury, pampering, excess, self-indulgence| Antonyms: abstinence, self-denial, austerity, moderation, restraint | Noun

indulge: /ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 放纵；沉溺；纵容；满足（愿望、爱好等） | To allow oneself or another person to have or enjoy something desired. | He indulged in a weekend of luxury. 他享受了一个奢华的周末。 | Synonyms:  gratify, satisfy, humor, spoil, pamper, coddle | Antonyms:  deny, deprive, restrict, restrain | Verb
