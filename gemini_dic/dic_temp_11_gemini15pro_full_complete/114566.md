malaxator:/ˈmæləkseɪtər/| n. |揉捏器，软化器|A device used for softening or kneading, especially in industrial processes like rubber or food production.  It mixes and works the material to achieve a desired consistency.|The malaxator thoroughly blended the rubber compound before vulcanization.  揉捏器在硫化前彻底混合了橡胶化合物。| The malaxator is essential for preparing the dough for certain types of bread. 揉捏器对于准备某些类型的面包面团至关重要。 |synonyms: mixer, kneader, blender, grinder|antonyms: separator, divider|noun
