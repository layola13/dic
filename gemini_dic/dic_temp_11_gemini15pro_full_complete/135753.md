nonprofanely:/ˌnɑːnprəˈfeɪnli/ /ˌnɒnprəˈfeɪnli/| adv.|非亵渎地，不使用粗俗语言地|In a way that does not use bad language, especially language that is offensive because it refers to sex or other private body functions. |She spoke nonprofanely, even when she was angry. 即使生气，她也彬彬有礼，不讲粗话。|近义词：respectfully, politely, decently|反义词：profanely, vulgarly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From non- + profane + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Profane" comes from the Latin "profanus," meaning "outside the temple," hence, "unholy" or "secular."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
