gunfire:/ˈɡʌnfaɪər/| n.|炮火，枪声|The sound of gunfire echoed through the streets. 炮火声在街道上回荡。|例句：The sudden gunfire startled everyone. 突如其来的枪声吓了所有人一跳。

例句：They were caught in the crossfire of heavy gunfire. 他们被困在了密集的交火中。

近义词：shooting, gunfire, volley, barrage|反义词:silence, peace|词性:名词

词源：From gun + fire.  "gun" (武器) + "fire" (开火).
