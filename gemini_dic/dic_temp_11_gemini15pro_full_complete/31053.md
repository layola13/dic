chameleon-like:/kəˈmiːliənˌlaɪk/| adj.|变色龙似的，善变的|Resembling a chameleon, especially in being able to adapt to different situations or change one's appearance or behavior easily.|例句：He has a chameleon-like ability to blend in with any crowd. 他有一种变色龙般的能力，能融入任何人群。|例句：Her chameleon-like personality makes it difficult to know who she really is. 她变色龙似的性格让人很难知道她到底是谁。|近义词：adaptable, versatile, changeable, fickle, mercurial|反义词：inflexible, rigid, unadaptable, constant, steadfast|词性:形容词
词源: From chameleon +‎ -like.
