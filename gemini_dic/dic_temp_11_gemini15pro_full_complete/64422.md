ergonomicses: /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪksɪz/ | n. | 人体工程学（复数，不常用） | The study of people's efficiency in their working environment.  This word is rarely used in the plural form. The singular form "ergonomics" is generally preferred. |  *Ergonomicses* is not typically used; stick with "ergonomics."  | 近义词: human factors, human engineering | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

etymology->
From Greek *ergon* (work) + *nomos* (law).
