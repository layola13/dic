appetite:/ˈæpɪtaɪt/| n.|食欲，胃口；欲望，渴望|A natural desire to satisfy a bodily need, especially for food.|She has a good appetite. 她胃口很好。|Synonyms: hunger, craving, desire, thirst|Antonyms: aversion, distaste, indifference|noun

appetite:/ˈæpɪtaɪt/| n.|食欲，胃口；欲望，渴望|A strong desire for something.|He has an insatiable appetite for knowledge. 他对知识有着永不满足的渴望。|Synonyms: yearning, longing, passion|Antonyms: apathy, indifference|noun
