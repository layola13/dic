insculption:/ɪnˈskʌlpʃən/
n.
雕刻；铭文；刻印
the act of carving words or designs on a surface, or something that is carved in this way
The inscription on the tombstone was barely legible. 墓碑上的铭文几乎难以辨认。
inscription:/ɪnˈskʌlpʃən/
近义词: carving, engraving, etching
反义词: erasure, obliteration
词性: noun
