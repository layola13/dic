baptismally:/bæpˈtɪzməli/
adv.
洗礼地，通过洗礼
Relating to or connected with baptism.
She was baptismally named Sarah. 她受洗时被命名为莎拉。
近义词:  through baptism
反义词: None
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "baptismal" + "-ly."  "Baptismal" comes from Late Latin "baptismālis," from "baptisma" (baptism), from Greek "báptisma," from "baptízein" (to baptize).
