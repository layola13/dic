The correct spelling is "country".  There is no word "countre" in English.

country: /ˈkʌntri/ (US) /ˈkʌntri/ (UK)| n.|国家，乡村，故乡|A nation with its own government, occupying a particular territory.|例句：China is my country. 中国是我的祖国。|例句：He lives in the country. 他住在乡下。|近义词：nation, land, state, rural area|反义词: city, urban area|词性: noun
