enopla:/ɪˈnoʊplə/
n.
刺胞动物门的一纲
A class within the phylum Cnidaria, characterized by the presence of nematocysts (stinging cells) in the epidermis and gastrodermis.
Enopla are a class of ribbon worms characterized by the presence of a proboscis armed with stylets. 刺胞动物门的一纲，其特征是在表皮和胃皮层中存在刺丝囊（刺细胞）。
The Enopla are a class of ribbon worms.  带吻虫纲是一个带状蠕虫纲。
近义词: Anopla (another class of ribbon worms)
反义词: None (as it's a taxonomic classification)
词性: Noun
