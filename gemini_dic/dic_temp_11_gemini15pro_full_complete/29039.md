cartoony: /kɑːrˈtuːni/ | adj. |卡通的，漫画般的|Resembling or suggestive of a cartoon, especially in being unrealistically simplified or exaggerated. | The characters in the game were very cartoony. 游戏中的角色非常卡通化。| The animation style was deliberately cartoony.  动画风格故意设计成卡通式的。 |近义词：cartoonish, animated, comic |反义词：realistic, lifelike |词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From cartoon + -y.  "Cartoon" originally referred to a preparatory drawing for a piece of art, and later came to mean a humorous or satirical drawing, often in a newspaper or magazine. The suffix "-y" is added to form adjectives, often indicating resemblance or possession of a quality.
