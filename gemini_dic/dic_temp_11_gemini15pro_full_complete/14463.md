availingly:/əˈveɪlɪŋli/ | adv. | 有效地，有益地 | In a way that is effective or helpful. | He tried availingly to persuade her. 他努力有效地说服她。| 近义词: effectively, usefully, beneficially | 反义词: ineffectively, uselessly, pointlessly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "avail" + suffix "-ingly".  "Avail" comes from Old French "availlir" meaning "to be of value or worth," ultimately from Latin "valere" meaning "to be strong, to be worth."
