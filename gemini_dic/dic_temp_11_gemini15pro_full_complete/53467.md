disarmed:/dɪsˈɑːrmd/
adjective
解除武装的；消除了敌意的；使人消气的
Deprived of weapons; No longer hostile or suspicious;  Charmed and made less hostile or suspicious.
The soldiers were disarmed and sent home. 士兵们被解除武装后送回家。
Her smile disarmed him completely. 她的笑容完全消除了他的敌意。
synonyms: defenseless, unarmed, vulnerable, pacified, appeased
antonyms: armed, hostile, aggressive
词性: 形容词
