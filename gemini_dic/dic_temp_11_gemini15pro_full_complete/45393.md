cupcake:/ˈkʌpˌkeɪk/ (US), /ˈkʌpkeɪk/ (UK)| n. |纸杯蛋糕|A small cake baked in a cup-shaped mold.|She brought a box of delicious cupcakes to the party. 她带了一盒美味的纸杯蛋糕来参加聚会。|Synonyms: fairy cake, patty cake|Antonyms: N/A|noun

cupcake:/ˈkʌpˌkeɪk/ (US), /ˈkʌpkeɪk/ (UK)| adj. (informal) |娇小可爱的，甜美的 (多用于形容女性)|Resembling or suggestive of a cupcake, especially in being small, sweet, and conventionally attractive.|She had a cupcake personality - sweet and bubbly. 她性格甜美活泼，像个小蛋糕一样。|Synonyms: sweet, charming, cute|Antonyms: harsh, unpleasant|adjective
