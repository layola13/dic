bullfighters:/ˈbʊlˌfaɪtərz/
n.
斗牛士（复数）
People who fight bulls, especially as a public spectacle.
The bullfighters entered the arena to a roar of applause. 斗牛士进入竞技场时，掌声雷动。

The bullfighters were skilled and brave. 这些斗牛士技艺高超，勇敢无畏。
