jobbers:/ˈdʒɒbəz/ (US) /ˈdʒɑːbərz/
n.
批发商，中间商
Wholesalers or middlemen who buy goods in bulk and sell them to retailers or other businesses.
Jobbers play an important role in the distribution chain. 批发商在分销链中扮演着重要的角色。
The jobbers were able to offer a competitive price.  这些批发商能够提供有竞争力的价格。

近义词:wholesalers, distributors, middlemen, traders
反义词:retailers, consumers
词性: 名词
