A widening or spreading outwards, like the flared end of a trumpet. |The astronomer observed an outflare from the distant star. 天文学家观察到来自遥远恒星的爆发。| The dress featured an outflare at the hem. 这件连衣裙的下摆呈喇叭状。 |近义词：burst, flare, eruption, blaze, flash|反义词：contract, diminish, dwindle|词性：动词, 名词
