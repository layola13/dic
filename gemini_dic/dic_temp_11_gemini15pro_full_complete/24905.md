bruiters:/ˈbruːtərz/
n.
散布谣言者；宣传者

The bruiters quickly spread the news of the royal engagement throughout the kingdom.  这些宣传者迅速将皇室订婚的消息传遍了整个王国。
The bruiters were responsible for the widespread panic. 这些散布谣言的人要对大范围的恐慌负责。
synonyms:  rumormongers, gossipmongers, publicists, promoters
antonyms:  concealers, suppressors
