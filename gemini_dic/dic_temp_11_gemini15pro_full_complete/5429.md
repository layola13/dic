almohads:/ˌælmoʊˈhɑːdz/
n.|阿尔摩哈德王朝|A Berber Muslim dynasty that ruled in North Africa and Spain from the 12th to the 13th centuries.|The Almohads unified much of North Africa and Spain under their rule. 阿尔摩哈德王朝统一了北非和西班牙的大部分地区。|The Almohads left a lasting legacy of art and architecture. 阿尔摩哈德王朝留下了持久的艺术和建筑遗产。|synonyms: dynasty, empire|antonyms: anarchy, disorder|noun
