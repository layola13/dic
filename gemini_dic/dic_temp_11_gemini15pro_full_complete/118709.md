metacrolein:/ˌmɛtəˈkroʊliɪn/ | n. |甲基丙烯醛|An unsaturated aldehyde, CH2=C(CH3)CHO, derived from acrolein by replacing a hydrogen atom with a methyl group. It is a highly reactive and flammable liquid with a pungent odor. |Metacrolein is an important intermediate in the production of methionine, an essential amino acid. 甲基丙烯醛是生产蛋氨酸（一种必需氨基酸）的重要中间体。| Metacrolein is a volatile organic compound that contributes to air pollution. 甲基丙烯醛是一种挥发性有机化合物，会导致空气污染。 |Synonyms: methacrolein| Antonyms: None| noun
