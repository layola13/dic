lavishly: /ˈlævɪʃli/ | adv. | 奢侈地，慷慨地 | in a rich or extravagant way; generously |  They entertained lavishly at Christmas. 他们在圣诞节招待客人非常盛情。 | 近义词: extravagantly, profusely, generously, abundantly | 反义词: sparingly, frugally, meagerly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "lavish," which comes from Middle English *lavas*, from Old French *lavasse* ("downpour, torrent"), from *laver* ("to wash"). The original sense was of something overflowing or abundant.
