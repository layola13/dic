aboundingly:/əˈbaʊndɪŋli/
adverb
极其丰富地，大量地
in a way that shows there is a very large quantity of something
Examples:
Fish aboundingly filled the river.
这条河里充满了大量的鱼。

The area is aboundingly rich in wildlife.
这个地区有着极其丰富的野生动物。

近义词: profusely, copiously, abundantly, plentifully
反义词: sparsely, scarcely, meagerly
词性: adverb

词源:
From abound + -ing + -ly.  Abound comes from the Old French abonder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from" (see ab-) + undare "to surge, overflow," related to unda "wave."
