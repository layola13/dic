flankerbacks:/ˈflæŋkərˌbæks/| n.|侧翼后卫（通常指橄榄球或足球）|Rugby players who play in the flanker back positions are responsible for both defense and attack.  橄榄球比赛中侧翼后卫球员负责防守和进攻。| The team's flankerbacks were instrumental in their victory.  该队的侧翼后卫对他们的胜利至关重要。|近义词：wingbacks, wingers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps centre-backs)|词性:noun
