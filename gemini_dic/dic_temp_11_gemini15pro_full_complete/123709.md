moonie:/ˈmuːni/| n.| 门徒，狂热追随者 (尤指统一教的)|A member of the Unification Church, founded by Sun Myung Moon, often characterized by intense devotion and obedience.|例句：The moonie handed out pamphlets on the street corner.  那个统一教教徒在街角分发小册子。|例句：He was known for being a dedicated moonie. 他以身为一个虔诚的统一教教徒而闻名。|近义词：follower, disciple, devotee|反义词:critic, opponent|词性:noun

词源:
Derived from the surname of Sun Myung Moon, founder of the Unification Church.  源自统一教创始人文鲜明的姓氏。
