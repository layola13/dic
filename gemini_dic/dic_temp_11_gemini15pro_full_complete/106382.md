kono:/ˈkoʊnoʊ/| pronoun|这个（日语）|This word is the Japanese word for "this". It's a demonstrative pronoun used to indicate something near the speaker. It's often romanized as "kono" but the pronunciation is closer to "koh-noh".  |例句： Kono hon wa watashi no desu.  这本书是我的。 This book is mine.|近义词：(In Japanese) kore (this - more general), kochira (this way/direction)|反义词:(In Japanese) sono (that - near listener), ano (that - far from both),  achira (that way/direction)|词性:pronoun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Kono pen wa akai desu. = 这支笔是红色的。
Kono hana wa kirei desu. = 这朵花很漂亮。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Watashi wa kono hon o yondeimasu. = 我正在读这本书。
Kare wa kono kuruma o unten shiteimasu. = 他正在开这辆车。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Watashi wa kono eiga o mimashita. = 我看过这部电影了。(Note: Japanese doesn't have a perfect tense exactly like English, this is the closest equivalent)
Kare wa kono shigoto o oemashita. = 他完成了这项工作。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
N/A (This tense doesn't have a direct equivalent in Japanese.)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Watashi wa kono resutoran de tabemashita. = 我在这家餐厅吃过饭。
Kare wa kono michi o arukimashita. = 他走过这条路。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Watashi wa kono hon o yondeimashita. = 我当时正在读这本书。
Kare wa kono uta o utatteimashita. = 他当时正在唱这首歌。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Watashi wa kono koto o shitteimashita. = 我已经知道这件事了。(Again, a close equivalent)
Kare wa kono basho ni kita koto ga arimashita. = 他来过这个地方。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
N/A (This tense doesn't have a direct equivalent in Japanese.)


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Watashi wa kono hon o yomimasu. = 我将要读这本书。 (Japanese often uses present tense for future meaning)
Kare wa kono shigoto o shimasu. = 他将要做这项工作。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Watashi wa kono jikan ni benkyou shiteiru deshou. = 我大概在这个时候会学习吧。(Expressing future continuous requires more complex phrasing)
Kare wa kono shigoto o shiteiru deshou. = 他大概会做这项工作吧。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Watashi wa kono hon o yomiowaru deshou. = 我大概会读完这本书吧。(Expressing future perfect requires more complex phrasing)
Kare wa kono shigoto o owaru deshou. = 他大概会完成这项工作吧。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
N/A (This tense doesn't have a direct equivalent in Japanese.)


虚拟语气:
Moshimo kono hon ga areba, yomimasu. = 如果有这本书的话，我会读。
Kono koto o shitteireba yokatta. = 要是知道这件事就好了。


被动语态:
Kono kuruma wa kare ni arawaremashita. = 这辆车被他洗了。
Kono uta wa minna ni utawaremasu. = 这首歌被大家唱。


疑问句:
Kore wa nan desu ka? = 这是什么？
Kono hon wa dare no desu ka? = 这本书是谁的？


否定句:
Kono pen wa watashi no dewa arimasen. = 这支笔不是我的。
Kono hana wa kirei dewa arimasen. = 这朵花不漂亮。


PHRASE->
(Phrases using "kono" are generally full sentences in Japanese, not set phrases like in English.)
Kono ato, nani o shimasu ka? = 这之后，你要做什么？
Kono hen ni, konbini wa arimasu ka? = 这附近，有便利店吗？
Kono mae, doko ni ikimashita ka? = 前阵子，你去了哪里？
Kono naka de, dare ga ichiban suki desu ka? = 这些人里面，你最喜欢谁？
Kono mama ja dame da. = 这样下去不行。
