matriculate: /məˈtrɪkjʊleɪt/ | v. | 入学，注册 | To enroll or be formally admitted into a college or university. |  She matriculated at Harvard University last fall. 她去年秋天在哈佛大学注册入学。| synonyms: enroll, register, admit | antonyms: withdraw, leave, drop out | verb

matriculate: /məˈtrɪkjʊleɪt/ | n. | 入学者 | A person who has been matriculated. | The matriculates were given a tour of the campus.  新生们被带着参观了校园。| synonyms: freshman, newcomer, enrollee | antonyms: graduate, alumnus/alumna | noun
