exactor:/ɪɡˈzæktər/| n.|勒索者，强求者，苛求者|A person who demands something, especially payment, typically one who does so using force or threats.|The exactor demanded payment immediately or threatened to take legal action.|近义词：extortionist, oppressor, tyrant|反义词: benefactor, philanthropist|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *exactor, from Latin exactus, past participle of exigere "to drive out, demand, enforce," from ex- "out" + agere "to drive, lead, do."
