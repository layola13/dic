bling:/blɪŋ/| n.| 闪光饰品；俗丽的珠宝首饰|Expensive, ostentatious jewelry and accessories. |She was covered in bling, from her diamond earrings to her gold watch. 她浑身上下都是闪光饰品，从钻石耳环到金表。|近义词：glitter, sparkle, flash|反义词:dullness, plainness|名词

bling:/blɪŋ/| v. | 闪耀，闪光|To shine or glitter, especially with ostentatious jewelry. |Her diamond necklace blinged under the spotlight. 她的钻石项链在聚光灯下闪闪发光。|近义词：glitter, sparkle, flash|反义词:fade, dim|动词
