antivenereal:/ˌæntivaˈnɪəriəl/
adjective
抗性病的，治疗性病的
Used to describe something that prevents or cures venereal diseases.
Antivenereal medications are essential for controlling the spread of STIs.  抗性病药物对于控制性传播感染的传播至关重要。
The clinic offered antivenereal treatment.  这家诊所提供性病治疗。
近义词: antisyphilitic, anti-STD
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "disease-causing" in a broad sense)
词性: adjective
