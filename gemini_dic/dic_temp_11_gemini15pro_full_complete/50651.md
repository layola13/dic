derivable:/dɪˈraɪvəbl/ | adj. | 可推导的，可引出的 | Able to be derived; deducible. | This theorem is derivable from the axioms. 这个定理可以从公理推导出来。| The conclusion is easily derivable from the premise. 这个结论很容易从前提推导出来。 |近义词：deducible, inferable, obtainable | 反义词：underivable | 词性:形容词

derive:/dɪˈraɪv/ | v. | 获得，取得；源于，来自 | To obtain something from a source or origin. | He derived great pleasure from reading. 他从阅读中获得了极大的乐趣。|  Many English words derive from Latin. 许多英语单词源于拉丁语。|近义词：obtain, gain, acquire, originate | 反义词：forfeit, lose | 词性:动词
