oke:/ˌoʊˈkeɪ/ | adj. | 好的，可以的 | An informal variant of "okay," expressing agreement, acceptance, or understanding.  It originated as a misspelling of "all correct" in the 19th century. | Oke, I'll do it. 好的，我会做的。|Synonyms: okay, alright, fine | Antonyms: bad, awful | adjective
