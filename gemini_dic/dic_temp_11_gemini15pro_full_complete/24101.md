brevipennate:/ˌbrevɪˈpɛneɪt/| adj.|短翅的；短翼的|Having short wings, or wings that are shorter than those of related species. This term is often used in ornithology to describe birds with reduced flight capabilities.|例句：The brevipennate kiwi is unable to fly. 短翅的几维鸟无法飞行。

例句：Brevipennate birds are often found on islands. 短翅鸟类经常在岛屿上被发现。|近义词：short-winged|反义词：long-winged|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Latin *brevis* ("short") + *penna* ("feather, wing").
