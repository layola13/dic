hasidism:/ˈhɑːsɪdɪzəm/ /ˈhæsɪdɪzəm/
n.
Hasidism; Hasidic Judaism
A Jewish religious movement characterized by its emphasis on mystical experience, joy, and the leadership of charismatic rabbis.
The Hasidism of Eastern Europe is known for its distinctive dress and customs.  东欧的哈西德派以其独特的服饰和习俗而闻名。

近义词: Hasidic Judaism,  Hasidic movement
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a more secular or less mystical form of Judaism could be considered a contrasting concept)
词性: noun


hasidisms:/ˌhɑːsɪˈdɪzəmz/ /ˌhæsɪˈdɪzəmz/
n.
Hasidisms
Plural form of Hasidism, referring to multiple forms or variations of the Hasidic movement.
The various hasidisms throughout the world share core beliefs but also have distinct traditions. 世界各地的各种哈西德派都有共同的核心信仰，但也各有不同的传统。

近义词: Hasidic movements, Hasidic sects (use with caution, as "sect" can have negative connotations)
反义词:  (Same as Hasidism - no direct antonym)
词性: noun


词源:  From Hebrew  ḥăsîd  meaning "pious one."  Ultimately from  ḥesed  meaning "loving-kindness" or "piety."
