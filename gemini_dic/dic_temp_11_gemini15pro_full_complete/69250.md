feastless:/ˈfiːstləs/| adj.|无宴会的；无节日的；缺乏欢乐的|Without a feast; lacking festivities or joy.  Derived from "feast" (meaning a large meal, especially a celebratory one) and the suffix "-less" (indicating absence or lack of).|例句：The refugees faced a feastless Christmas.  难民们面临着一个没有节庆的圣诞节。

例句：The feastless winter stretched before them.  漫长而无节日的冬天在他们面前展开。|近义词：bleak, dreary, somber, joyless, austere|反义词：festive, joyous, celebratory|词性:adjective
