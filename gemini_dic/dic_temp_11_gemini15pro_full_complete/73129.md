fora's:/ˈfɔːrəz/| n.|论坛; 公共场所; 法庭; 裁判庭|A forum or similar place of assembly or discussion.|The issue was debated in the fora of several countries.|近义词:forums, assemblies, meetings, conferences|反义词:None|Noun

Etymology:
From Latin *forum*, meaning "marketplace, public place". The word entered Middle English as *fore*, and the modern plural *fora* reflects the original Latin plural.  The apostrophe + s plural is generally considered nonstandard or archaic.  The standard plural in modern English is *forums*.
