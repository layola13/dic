discard:/dɪˈskɑːrd/ | v. n. | 丢弃，抛弃；被丢弃的东西 | to throw something away or get rid of it because you no longer want or need it |  He discarded his old clothes.  他丢弃了他的旧衣服。| synonyms: abandon, reject, dispose of | antonyms: keep, retain, preserve | verb

discard:/ˈdɪskɑːrd/ | n. | 丢弃，抛弃；被丢弃的东西 | the act of getting rid of something; something that has been thrown away |  The discard pile grew steadily.  废弃物堆稳步增长。| synonyms:  refuse, waste, trash | antonyms:  treasure, keepsake | noun
