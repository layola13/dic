nonjurant:/ˌnɒnˈdʒʊərənt/
n.|拒誓者；不宣誓者|A person who refuses to take an oath, especially the oath of allegiance or supremacy.|The nonjurors were deprived of their livings. 拒誓者被剥夺了生计。|近义词：recusant, dissenter|反义词：conformist|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *non jūrāns*, present participle of *non jūrāre* (to not swear), from Latin *nōn* (not) + *jūrāre* (to swear).
