bousy: /ˈbaʊzi/ | adj. | 喧闹的，吵闹的；爱喝酒的，好酒的 |  Given to or marked by boisterous conviviality and drinking.  A somewhat archaic or dialectal term. | The bousy crowd at the tavern sang loudly. 酒馆里喧闹的人群大声唱歌。| 近义词: boisterous, rowdy, convivial, drunken | 反义词: quiet, sober, calm | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely a variant or alteration of "boozy," which ultimately derives from "booze," meaning alcoholic drink.
