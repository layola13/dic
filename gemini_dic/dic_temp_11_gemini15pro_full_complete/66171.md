excessive:/ɪkˈsesɪv/| adj.|过多的，过分的，极度的|Going beyond the usual, necessary, or proper limit; immoderate.|The excessive use of force was condemned by human rights groups. 过度使用武力受到了人权组织的谴责。|近义词：immoderate, exorbitant, extreme, inordinate, undue|反义词：moderate, reasonable, temperate|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French excessif, -ive, from Latin excessivus, from excedere ‘go beyond’ (see exceed).
