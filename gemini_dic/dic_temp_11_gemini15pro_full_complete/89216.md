homeward:/ˈhoʊmwərd/ (US) /ˈhəʊmwəd/ (UK)| adj. adv.|回家地，向家地|Toward home. Indicates movement or direction towards one's home.|例句：She cast a homeward glance. 她向家里投去一瞥。|近义词：homebound|反义词: outward, away|词性:形容词/副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "hāmweard"  (homeward), composed of "hām" (home) and "weard" (toward).  The "-ward" suffix denotes direction.
