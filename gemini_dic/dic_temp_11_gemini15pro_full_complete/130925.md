nonascetical: /ˌnɒnəˈsɛtɪkəl/
adjective
非禁欲主义的; 世俗的
Not pertaining to or characteristic of asceticism; non-austere.  Not related to or characterized by abstinence from worldly pleasures, especially for religious reasons.
He lived a nonascetical life, enjoying good food and wine. 他过着非禁欲主义的生活，享受美食和美酒。
The nonascetical nature of the community was reflected in its vibrant social life.  这个社区的世俗性体现在其充满活力的社会生活中。

近义词: worldly, secular, hedonistic, indulgent
反义词: ascetic, austere, abstinent
词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + ascetical.  "Ascetical" comes from the Greek "askētikos," meaning "rigorously trained," relating to the practice of "askēsis," meaning "exercise" or "training," often in a spiritual or philosophical context.  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
