cloudlessly:/ˈklaʊdləsli/
adv.
无云地，晴朗地
Without clouds; in a clear sky.
The sun shone cloudlessly.  太阳晴朗地照耀着。
Near synonyms: brightly, clearly, sunnily
Antonyms: cloudily, overcast
词性: 副词
