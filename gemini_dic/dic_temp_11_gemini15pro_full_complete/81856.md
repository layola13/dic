gridelin:/ɡrɪdˈliːn/ | n. |格栅蛋白；格栅蛋白基因|A protein encoded by the GRID1 gene, involved in synaptic plasticity and receptor trafficking in the brain.  It plays a role in learning, memory, and motor control.  It's also implicated in some neurological disorders. | Mutations in the *gridelin* gene can cause spinocerebellar ataxia. *格栅蛋白基因的突变会导致脊髓小脑性共济失调。* The researchers studied the role of *gridelin* in neuronal development. *研究人员研究了格栅蛋白在神经元发育中的作用。* | synonyms:  GRID1 protein (more precise), glutamate receptor delta 1 subunit (more technical) | antonyms: N/A | noun
