hoarsen:/ˈhɔːrsən/ (US) /ˈhɔːsən/ (UK)| v. |（使）嘶哑，（使）粗糙|to make or become rough and harsh, especially in sound|例句：
The singer's voice hoarsened after the concert. 歌手的嗓音在演唱会后变嘶哑了。

Her voice was hoarsened by a cold. 她的声音因为感冒而嘶哑了。


|近义词：rasp, roughen, gravel,  |反义词: soften, smooth, clear |词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *horsen, from Old English *horsian, harsan (“to make hoarse”), from hors (“hoarse”). Cognate with Middle Dutch heersch (“hoarse”), German heiser (“hoarse”).
