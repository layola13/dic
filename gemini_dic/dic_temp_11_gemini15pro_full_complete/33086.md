chrissake:/fər ˈkraɪsts seɪk/ | interj. | 看在上帝的份上；哎呀；真是的 | An exclamation of annoyance, frustration, or exasperation.  Shortened form of "for Christ's sake". | "Oh, for chrissake, just leave me alone!" 哦，看在上帝的份上，让我一个人静一静！ | 近义词: for God's sake, for heaven's sake, dammit, darn it, heck | 反义词:  (No direct antonyms for interjections expressing frustration) | 词性: interjection
