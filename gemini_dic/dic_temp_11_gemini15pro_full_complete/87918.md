hierographical:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkəl/
adjective
象形文字的；与象形文字有关的
relating to hieroglyphics; expressed by hieroglyphics
The Rosetta Stone was crucial in deciphering hieroglyphical writing.  罗塞塔石碑对于破译象形文字至关重要。
Ancient Egyptian tombs are covered in hieroglyphical inscriptions. 古埃及陵墓上覆盖着象形文字铭文。
近义词: hieroglyphic
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "alphabetic" or "modern")
词性: adjective
