orthodox:/ˈɔːrθədɒks/ (UK), /ˈɔːrθədɑːks/ (US)| adj.|正统的；传统的；普遍接受的|Conforming to established doctrines, especially in religion; conventional.|例句：He is an orthodox Christian. 他是一位正统的基督徒。|例句：Her views on education are very orthodox. 她对教育的看法非常传统。|近义词：conventional, traditional, established, accepted, standard, customary, regular|反义词：unorthodox, unconventional, heterodox, heretical, radical, revolutionary|词性: 形容词
