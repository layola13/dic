hallux:/ˈhælʌks/| n. |拇趾，大脚趾|The hallux is the innermost digit of the foot.  拇趾是脚的最内侧的趾。|例句：The doctor examined his hallux carefully after he dropped a heavy object on it.  在他把一个重物掉在拇趾上后，医生仔细检查了他的拇趾。

|例句：Hallux valgus is a common foot problem.  拇趾外翻是一个常见的足部问题。|近义词：big toe, great toe|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *hallus*, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“hill, prominence”). Cognate with Old English *heall* (“heel”) and Greek *κόλλοψ* (*kóllops*, “knuckle”).  源自拉丁语 *hallus*，来自原始印欧语 *ḱel-（“小山，突起”）。同源词有古英语 *heall*（“脚后跟”）和希腊语 *κόλλοψ* (*kóllops*, “指关节”)。
