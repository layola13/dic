embraced: /ɪmˈbreɪst/ | v. | 拥抱，接受，包含 | To hold closely in one's arms, especially as a sign of affection; to welcome or accept something readily; to include or encompass something. |  She embraced her son warmly after he returned home. 她儿子回家后，她热情地拥抱了他。

The company embraced the new technology quickly.  这家公司很快就接受了这项新技术。 | 近义词: hug, accept, adopt, include | 反义词: reject, refuse, exclude | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *embracier, from en- "in" + brace "arms," from Latin bracchium "arm, forearm."
