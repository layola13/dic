dint:/dɪnt/
noun
力量; 影响力; 压力;
force; power; influence
He succeeded by dint of hard work. 他凭借努力工作取得了成功。
Synonyms: force, power, strength, effort
Antonyms: weakness, impotence

dint:/dɪnt/
noun
凹痕; 坑
dent; hollow
The hail left a dint in the car's hood. 冰雹在汽车引擎盖上留下了一个凹痕。
Synonyms: dent, depression, hollow, indentation
Antonyms: bulge, protrusion

dint:/dɪnt/
verb
使产生凹痕
to make a dent in
The hammer dinted the metal. 锤子把金属砸出了凹痕。
Synonyms: dent, gouge, nick
Antonyms: smooth, flatten
