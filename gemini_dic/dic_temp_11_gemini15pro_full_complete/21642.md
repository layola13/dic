blurt:/blɜːrt/| v. |脱口而出，突然说出|To say something suddenly and without thinking, usually because you are excited or nervous.|He blurted out the secret before he could stop himself. 他还没来得及停下来就把秘密脱口而出了。|She blurted out, "I'm pregnant!" 她脱口而出：“我怀孕了！”|近义词：exclaim, divulge, blab|反义词: conceal, suppress|词性:动词
词源:From Middle English *blurten, probably frequentative of *blert (“to bleat, low”) from Old English *blet (“a bleating”). Cognate with Low German blötern (“to blabber”), German blödern (“to bleat, blether”), Dutch blaten (“to bleat”).
