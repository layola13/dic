maddest:/ˈmædɪst/ | adj. | 最疯狂的，最生气的 |  Superlative form of "mad," meaning extremely insane, foolish, or angry.  It expresses the highest degree of madness. | He had the maddest idea of climbing Mount Everest in flip-flops. 他有一个最疯狂的想法，要穿着人字拖爬珠穆朗玛峰。| synonyms: craziest, insanest, furiousest | antonyms: sanest, calmest, most reasonable | adjective
