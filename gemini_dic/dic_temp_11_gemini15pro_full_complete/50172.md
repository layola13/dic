deodorize:/diːˈəʊdəˌraɪz/
verb
除臭，消除臭味
To eliminate or prevent the formation of unpleasant smells.
I use baking soda to deodorize the refrigerator. 我用小苏打除冰箱异味。
The spray deodorizes shoes and gym bags.  这种喷雾可以去除鞋子和运动包的异味。
synonyms: freshen, scent, perfume, air out
antonyms: stink, reek, pollute
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + odor + -ize.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse". "Odor" comes from Latin "odor" meaning "smell" or "scent". "-ize" is a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to cause to be" or "to become".
