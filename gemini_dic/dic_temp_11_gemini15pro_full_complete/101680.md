irreligiousness:/ˌɪrɪˈlɪdʒəsnəs/
n.
无宗教信仰；不信教；不虔诚
The state of being irreligious; lack of religious belief or practice.
His irreligiousness caused conflict with his devout family. 他的无宗教信仰导致了他与虔诚的家人之间的冲突。
synonyms: ungodliness, impiety, secularism, atheism, nonbelief
antonyms: religiousness, piety, devoutness, godliness
noun
