filiation:/ˌfɪliˈeɪʃn/
n.
父子关系；亲子关系；起源；从属关系
the fact of being the child of a particular parent or parents; the origin or derivation of something.
例句：The painting's filiation to the artist was confirmed by experts.  这幅画与这位艺术家的亲子关系得到了专家的证实。

例句：The historian traced the filiation of the political ideology back to ancient Greece.  历史学家将这种政治意识形态的起源追溯到古希腊。
近义词：descent, lineage, parentage, origin, derivation
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "adoption" in specific contexts)
词性: noun
