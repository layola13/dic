lagopous:/ˈlæɡəpəs/| n. |雷鸟, 雪鸡 |A bird of the grouse family, with feathered legs and feet, native to cold regions.  It typically changes color seasonally, becoming white in winter. |例句：The lagopous is well adapted to its harsh arctic environment. 雷鸟非常适应其严酷的北极环境。

例句：We saw a lagopous camouflaged against the snowy landscape. 我们看到一只雷鸟伪装在雪地里。|近义词：ptarmigan, grouse |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
