outweigh:/ˌaʊtˈweɪ/
verb
胜过，比...更重要
to be greater or more important than something else
The benefits of this treatment far outweigh any risks. 这种治疗的好处远远大于任何风险。
synonyms: override, prevail over, surpass, exceed
antonyms: be inferior to, be outweighed by
词性:动词

outweigh:/ˌaʊtˈweɪ/
verb
超过
to be heavier than something else
He outweighed his opponent by at least twenty pounds. 他的体重比对手至少重二十磅。（此义较少用）
synonyms: be heavier than
antonyms: be lighter than
词性:动词
