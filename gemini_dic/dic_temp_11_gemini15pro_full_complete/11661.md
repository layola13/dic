armistice: /ˈɑːrmɪstɪs/ | n. | 休战，停战 | An agreement made by opposing sides in a war to stop fighting for a certain time; a truce. |  The two countries signed an armistice after years of conflict.  这两个国家在多年的冲突后签署了停战协议。

| Example: The armistice brought a temporary peace to the war-torn region. 停战给饱受战争蹂躏的地区带来了暂时的和平。| Synonym: truce, ceasefire | Antonym: war, hostility | Noun
