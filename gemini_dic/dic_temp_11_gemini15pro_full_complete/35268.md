clusteringly:/ˈklʌstərɪŋli/
adv.
成群地，群集地
in a clustering manner; so as to form clusters.
The bees were clusteringly around the hive. 蜜蜂成群地围在蜂巢周围。
近义词：gregariously, in clusters, in groups
反义词：sparsely, individually, separately
词性: 副词

词源:  From cluster + -ing + -ly.  "Cluster" ultimately comes from Old English *clyster "clod, lump" (related to clot).
