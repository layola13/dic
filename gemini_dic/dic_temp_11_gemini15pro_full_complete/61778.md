empress:/ˈemprəs/| n. |女皇；皇后|A woman who rules an empire or is the wife or widow of an emperor. |例句：The empress ruled wisely and justly. 女皇统治英明而公正。

例句：Catherine the Great was a powerful empress of Russia.  凯瑟琳大帝是俄罗斯一位强大的女皇。|近义词：queen, monarch, sovereign|反义词: subject, commoner| noun
