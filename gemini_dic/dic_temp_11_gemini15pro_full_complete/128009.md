nephritis:/nɪˈfraɪtɪs/
n.
肾炎
Inflammation of the kidneys.
Example:  The doctor diagnosed him with nephritis after reviewing his blood work.  医生检查了他的血检结果后，诊断他患有肾炎。

Example:  Chronic nephritis can lead to kidney failure. 慢性肾炎会导致肾衰竭。

Synonym: kidney inflammation
Antonym: kidney health
词性: noun
