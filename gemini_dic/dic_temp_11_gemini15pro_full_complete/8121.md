anti-australian:/ˌænti-ɔːˈstreɪliən/
adj.
反澳大利亚的，反对澳大利亚的
Opposed to or hostile towards Australia, its people, or its policies.
He held strong anti-Australian sentiments due to past political disputes.  由于过去的政治纠纷，他持有强烈的反澳情绪。
The anti-Australian rhetoric in the article was inflammatory and divisive.  文章中反澳大利亚的言辞具有煽动性和分裂性。
近义词: antipodean (in older usage, sometimes derogatory)
反义词: pro-Australian, australophile
词性: adjective
