bladers:/ˈbleɪdərz/
n.
滑冰者; 轮滑者
People who use blades, especially for skating.
The bladers were showing off their skills in the park.  公园里的滑冰者正在炫耀他们的技巧。

近义词:skaters, rollerbladers
反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "non-skaters" in a specific context)
词性: noun
