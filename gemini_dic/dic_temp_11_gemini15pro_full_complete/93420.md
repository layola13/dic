illogicalnesses:/ɪˌlɒdʒɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
不合逻辑；无理性
The illogicalnesses of his argument were apparent to everyone. 他的论证不合逻辑，每个人都看得出来。
His statement was full of illogicalnesses. 他的陈述充满了不合逻辑之处。
Synonyms: irrationality, absurdity, unreasonableness, fallaciousness
Antonyms: logic, rationality, reasonableness, sense
词性: 名词
