disleaf:/ˌdɪsˈliːf/ | v. | 落叶，使落叶 | To cause the leaves of (a plant or tree) to fall off. | The harsh winter disleafed the trees earlier than usual. 严冬使树木比往常更早落叶。| The chemical defoliant disleafed the trees in the area. 化学落叶剂使该地区的树木落叶。 | 近义词: defoliate | 反义词: leaf | 词性: 动词

词源: dis- (prefix meaning "removal, separation") + leaf (n.)
