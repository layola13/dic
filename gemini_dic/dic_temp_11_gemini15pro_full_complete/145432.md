overcharities: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃærɪtiz/
noun
过度慈善; 过多的慈善行为
Excessive charitable giving or acts of charity.
The billionaire's overcharities, while well-intentioned, sometimes overshadowed the efforts of smaller, local organizations.  这个亿万富翁的过度慈善，虽然出于好意，但有时却掩盖了较小的地方组织的努力。
His overcharities led to financial instability for his own family. 他过度的慈善行为导致了他自己家庭的经济不稳定。
Near Synonyms: excessive philanthropy, overzealous giving
Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness
词性: 名词
