artiad:/ˈɑːrtiæd/| n.|偶蹄动物|An even-toed ungulate mammal.  Artiodactyls are hoofed animals with an even number of toes on each foot, typically two or four. Examples include pigs, hippos, camels, deer, giraffes, sheep, goats, and cattle.|Cows and sheep are both artiads.|近义词：even-toed ungulate|反义词:perissodactyl|词性:noun
