implode:/ɪmˈploʊd/ (US), /ɪmˈpləʊd/ (UK)| v. |内爆；崩溃，瓦解；剧减|To burst inward; to collapse violently inward.  To break down or fall apart suddenly and completely.  To decrease rapidly.|The submarine imploded under the immense pressure of the deep ocean. 潜水艇在深海巨大的压力下内爆了。|

The company's stock price imploded after the accounting scandal.  公司股票价格在会计丑闻后暴跌。|近义词：collapse, burst, crumble, disintegrate|反义词:explode, expand, grow|词性:动词
