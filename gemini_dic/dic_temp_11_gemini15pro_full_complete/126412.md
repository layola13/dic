mystagogue: /ˈmɪstəɡɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈmɪstəɡɑːɡ/ (US) | n. | 奥秘传授者；神秘宗教仪式主持人；故弄玄虚者 | A person who initiates others into mysteries or esoteric knowledge, especially religious ones; a teacher or interpreter of mysteries.  Often used with a negative connotation, implying someone who uses obscure language or rituals to impress or confuse. |  The self-proclaimed mystagogue claimed to have access to ancient secrets.  这个自称奥秘传授者的人声称可以接触到古代的秘密。

The mystagogue led the initiates through a series of elaborate rituals.  奥秘传授者带领新入教者进行了一系列复杂的仪式。 | 近义词: guru, initiate, shaman, oracle | 反义词: skeptic, doubter | 词性: noun
