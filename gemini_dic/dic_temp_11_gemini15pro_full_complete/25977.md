bursal:/ˈbɜːrsəl/
adjective
囊的；滑囊的
Relating to or resembling a bursa (a small fluid-filled sac that reduces friction between tissues in the body).
The bursal cavity is inflamed. 滑囊腔发炎了。
Synonyms: saclike, pouchlike
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
