arranger:/əˈreɪndʒər/ | n. | 安排者，筹划者 | A person who puts things in order, plans or makes preparations for something, especially musical scores. | The wedding arranger did a fantastic job. 婚礼策划人做得非常出色。|  The arranger adapted the symphony for a smaller orchestra.  这位编曲家将交响乐改编为适合小型管弦乐队演奏的版本。 | synonyms: organizer, planner, coordinator, composer (for music), adapter | antonyms: disorganizer | noun
