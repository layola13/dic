cloudxr:/klaʊd ɛks ɑːr/ | n. | 云XR | A proprietary NVIDIA technology that allows streaming of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) experiences over a network, typically using 5G or Wi-Fi. It enables high-quality XR content to be accessed on devices that wouldn't normally be powerful enough to run it locally. | Example: CloudXR makes it possible to experience high-fidelity VR on a lightweight headset.  云XR使得在轻量级头显上体验高保真VR成为可能。

Example: The company is using CloudXR to stream interactive training simulations to employees worldwide.  该公司正在使用CloudXR向全球员工传输交互式培训模拟。 | Synonym:  streamed XR, remote XR | Antonym: local XR | Noun
