beckoningly:/ˈbɛkənɪŋli/
adverb
诱人地；引诱地；召唤地
in a way that attracts or invites someone to do something, by gesture or words.
Her eyes beckoned him invitingly. 她的眼神诱人地召唤着他。
Near synonyms: invitingly, alluringly, enticingly, temptingly, seductively
Antonyms: repellingly, forbiddingly, off-puttingly, discouragingly
词性: adverb (副词)
