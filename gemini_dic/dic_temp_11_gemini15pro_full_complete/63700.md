ephemera's:/ɪˈfɛmərəz/
noun
蜉蝣的；短命事物的；纪念品的
belonging to or associated with ephemera (things that exist or are used or enjoyed for only a short time).
The ephemera's delicate wings caught the light. 这蜉蝣的纤细翅膀捕捉到了光线。
Synonyms: transient's, fleeting's, short-lived's
Antonyms: permanent's, lasting's, enduring's
