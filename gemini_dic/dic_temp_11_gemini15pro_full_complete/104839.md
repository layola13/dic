keeled:/kiːld/
adjective
有龙骨的；似龙骨的
having a keel or keels; shaped like a keel
The keeled sailboat sliced through the water. 这艘有龙骨的帆船划破水面。
近义词:carinate
反义词:keelless
词性:adjective

keeled:/kiːld/
verb
倾覆；翻船（尤指帆船）(keel的过去式和过去分词)
(of a boat, especially a sailing boat) to turn over on its side; capsize
The small boat keeled over in the strong wind. 小船在强风中倾覆了。
近义词:capsize, overturn
反义词:right
词性:verb
