nonthreateningly:/nɒnˈθrɛt(ə)nɪŋli/ /nɑːnˈθrɛtnɪŋli/
adverb
没有威胁地；不构成威胁地
in a way that does not cause fear or alarm.
She smiled nonthreateningly. 她没有威胁性地笑了笑。
Synonyms: harmlessly, safely, peacefully, innocuously
Antonyms: threateningly, menacingly, aggressively
词性: 副词
