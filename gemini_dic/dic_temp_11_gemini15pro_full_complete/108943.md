leen:/liːn/
adjective
瘦的，无脂肪的; 贫乏的，歉收的
thin and healthy; containing little or no fat; (of a period of time) difficult and unpleasant because people have little money
She was tall and lean. 她又高又瘦。
It was several lean years for the company. 对公司来说，这是几个歉收的年头。
近义词:thin, slender, slim, spare, gaunt, skinny, emaciated
反义词:fat, plump, obese, overweight
词性:adjective
