martyrdom:/ˈmɑːtərdəm/ | n. |殉道；殉教；牺牲；折磨；痛苦|The state of being a martyr, especially the suffering of death or persecution for one's beliefs, principles, or cause.  |  She endured years of pain and suffering, but considered it a form of martyrdom for her art. 她忍受了多年的痛苦和折磨，但她认为这是她为艺术献身的一种形式。 |synonyms: sacrifice, immolation, victimization| antonyms: survival, comfort, deliverance| noun

martyrship:/ˈmɑːtəʃɪp/ | n. |殉道；殉难；殉教|The condition or experience of being a martyr. |His martyrdom inspired others to fight for freedom. 他的殉难激励了其他人为自由而战。|synonyms: martyrdom, sacrifice, immolation| antonyms: survival, comfort, deliverance| noun
