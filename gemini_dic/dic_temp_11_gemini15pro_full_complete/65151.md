ethnarchy: /ˈeθnɑːrki/
n.
种族统治；民族统治
A form of government where power is held by a dominant ethnic group, often to the detriment of other ethnic groups within the same state.
The country's ethnarchy led to the marginalization of minority groups.  这个国家的种族统治导致了少数群体的边缘化。
The ethnarchy implemented policies that favored the dominant ethnic group.  种族统治政府实施了有利于统治民族的政策。
synonyms: ethnocracy, ethno-nationalism
antonyms: multiculturalism, pluralism
noun
