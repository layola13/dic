nonconserving:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɜːrvɪŋ/ | adj. | 非守恒的 | Not conserving; failing to conserve, especially a quantity such as energy or momentum that would remain constant in the absence of disturbing forces. |  The system is nonconserving due to energy loss through friction.  该系统由于摩擦造成的能量损失而非守恒的。| synonyms: dissipative, non-conservative | antonyms: conserving, conservative | adjective
