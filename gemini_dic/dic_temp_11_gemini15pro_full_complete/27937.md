cantine:/kænˈtiːn/ | n. | 食堂，小卖部 | A canteen is a restaurant, typically one in a factory, office building, school, or military base, where meals are served to a large number of people. | The factory canteen serves hot meals at lunchtime. 工厂食堂在午餐时间提供热食。|  The school canteen is always crowded during break time. 学校食堂在休息时间总是很拥挤。 | synonyms: cafeteria, mess hall, dining hall | antonyms: restaurant, cafe | noun
