amandine: /ˈæmənˌdiːn/ | adj. n. | 杏仁的；加杏仁的; 杏仁料理 |  Prepared or garnished with almonds, especially toasted and slivered.  A dish, typically fish, prepared in this way. | Example:  I ordered the trout amandine. 我点了杏仁鳟鱼。| Example: The amandine sauce was rich and nutty. 杏仁酱浓郁而带有坚果味。| 近义词: almond-flavored, almond-crusted | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain") | 词性: adjective, noun
