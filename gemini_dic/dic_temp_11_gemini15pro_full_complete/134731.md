nonmeat:/ˈnɑːnmiːt/
adjective
非肉类的；素食的
Made from or consisting of ingredients other than meat.  Not containing meat.
Example:  They offer a wide variety of nonmeat dishes. 他们提供各种各样的素食菜肴。
Example:  Nonmeat protein sources include beans and tofu. 非肉类蛋白质来源包括豆类和豆腐。
Synonym: vegetarian, meatless, plant-based
Antonym: meat, flesh
词性: 形容词
