inseminated:/ɪnˈsɛməˌneɪtɪd/
|v. |受精的；授精的|Introduced semen into (a female reproductive tract), especially by artificial means in order to cause pregnancy.|The cow was artificially inseminated.|近义词：impregnated, fertilized, sired|反义词：barren, infertile
