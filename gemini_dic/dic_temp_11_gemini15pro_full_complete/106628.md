kumiss:/kuːˈmɪs/| n. |马奶酒|A fermented mare's milk alcoholic beverage traditionally consumed in Central Asia.|Example 1:  He enjoyed a refreshing glass of kumiss after a long day's ride. 他骑马一整天后，享用了一杯清爽的马奶酒。

Example 2: Kumiss is an important part of the nomadic culture in Mongolia. 马奶酒是蒙古游牧文化的重要组成部分。|近义词：airag (another name for kumiss, particularly in Mongolia)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-alcoholic beverage")|词性:noun
