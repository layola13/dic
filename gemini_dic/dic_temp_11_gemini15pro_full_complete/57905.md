driftway:/ˈdrɪftweɪ/| n. |小路，羊肠小道|A narrow path or road, especially one made by the passage of animals or people.|The sheep followed the driftway across the moor. 羊群沿着这条小路穿过荒野。|近义词：path, track, trail, lane|反义词:highway, main road|词性:noun
Etymology: From 'drift' (meaning 'driven' or 'something piled up,' referring to snow or sand) + 'way.'  Originally referred to a road formed by drifting snow, later generalized to any narrow, informal path.
