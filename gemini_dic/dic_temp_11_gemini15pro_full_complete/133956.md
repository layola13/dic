nonheme:/ˌnɑːnˈhiːm/ |adj.|非血红素的|Not containing heme, the iron-containing component of hemoglobin.  Referring specifically to iron not found within hemoglobin. |Nonheme iron is found in plant-based foods. 非血红素铁存在于植物性食物中。|Nonheme iron is less readily absorbed than heme iron. 非血红素铁比血红素铁更难吸收。|近义词: non-heme (alternative spelling)|反义词: heme|词性: adjective
