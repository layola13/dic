outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/
verb
抢先报道；捷足先登；先下手为强
To publish a news story before (a competitor); to get ahead of (someone) by being the first to do or obtain something.
The rival newspaper outscooped them on the biggest story of the year.  这家竞争对手的报纸在年度最大新闻上抢先报道了他们。
The local paper was outscooped by a national daily. 地方报纸被一家全国性日报抢先报道了。
近义词: beat, forestall, preempt
反义词: be scooped
