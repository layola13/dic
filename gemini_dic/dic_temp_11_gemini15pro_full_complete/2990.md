aeolian:/iˈoʊliən/ | adj. |风成的；风蚀的|Relating to or arising from the action of the wind; wind-blown.  Of or relating to Aeolus, the Greek god of the winds.|The aeolian harp produces a haunting melody. 风弦琴奏出萦绕心头的旋律。|Synonyms: windswept, airy, breezy|Antonyms: sheltered, still, calm|adjective
