dysentery:/ˈdɪsənˌteri/| n.|痢疾|An infection of the intestines causing severe diarrhea with the presence of mucus and/or blood in the feces.|He contracted dysentery while traveling abroad. 他在国外旅行时感染了痢疾。|近义词：diarrhea, the flux, bloody flux|反义词: constipation |noun
