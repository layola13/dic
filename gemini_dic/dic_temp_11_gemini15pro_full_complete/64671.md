escalloped:/ɪˈskɑːləpt/ /ɪˈskæləpt/ | adj. | 扇贝形的，扇贝状的；用奶油烤的，用白汁烤的 |  baked in a creamy sauce, often with breadcrumbs; having a wavy edge like a scallop shell |  We had escalloped potatoes for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了奶油烤土豆。 | 近义词：scalloped, au gratin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain" or "unbaked") | 词性: 形容词

escallop:/ɪˈskɑːləp/ /ɪˈskæləp/ | n. | 扇贝 | a shellfish with a flat, rounded shell |  We ordered scallops as an appetizer.  我们点了扇贝作为开胃菜。 | 近义词：scallop shell | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *escalope, from Germanic *skalja (meaning "shell," "scale").
