externalize: /ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪz/ /ɛkˈstɝnəˌlaɪz/
verb
使外在化；使客观化；外包；将（感情）投射到别人身上
To express (a thought or feeling) outwardly. To perceive (something, such as an emotion) as existing outside oneself. To outsource or contract (a business process) to an external organization.
She externalized her anger by yelling at her friend. 她通过对朋友大喊大叫来发泄她的愤怒。

The company decided to externalize its customer service operations.  这家公司决定将其客户服务业务外包。

He tends to externalize his anxieties onto others. 他倾向于把自己的焦虑投射到别人身上。

近义词: express, vent, project, outsource, subcontract
反义词: internalize, repress
词性: verb
