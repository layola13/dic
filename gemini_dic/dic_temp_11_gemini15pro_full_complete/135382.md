nonperiodical:/ˌnɒnˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkəl/ | adj. | 非期刊的，不定期出版的 | Not published at regular intervals; occurring at irregular intervals. | This is a nonperiodical publication, released only when new research is available. 这是一份非期刊出版物，只有在有新研究成果时才发行。|  The company sends out nonperiodical newsletters to its clients.  这家公司不定期地向客户发送通讯。 |  non-serial, irregular, sporadic | periodical, regular | adjective
