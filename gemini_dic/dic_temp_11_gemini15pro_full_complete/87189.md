herdswoman:/ˈhɜːrdzwʊmən/ /ˈhɝːdzˌwʊmən/| n. |女牧人；牧羊女|A woman who herds and tends livestock, especially cattle or sheep.|The herdswoman led her flock to the green pasture. 女牧人把她的羊群带到绿色的牧场。|Synonyms: shepherdess, cowgirl (especially in American English), stockwoman|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider something like 'city dweller' for a contrasting lifestyle)|Noun
