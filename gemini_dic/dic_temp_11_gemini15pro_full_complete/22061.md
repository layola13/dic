boletuses:/ˌboʊləˈtaɪsiːz/ /ˌbɒləˈtaɪsiːz/
n.
牛肝菌, 复数形式
A boletus is a type of mushroom, often edible, with pores instead of gills on the underside of the cap.
Boletuses are prized by mushroom hunters for their flavor. 牛肝菌因其美味而受到蘑菇猎人的青睐。
The chef prepared a delicious dish of sauteed boletuses.  厨师准备了一道美味的炒牛肝菌。
synonyms: porcini, cep, king bolete
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a type of mushroom.  Could consider "toadstool" as a general antonym for edible mushroom, but not specific to boletus.)
