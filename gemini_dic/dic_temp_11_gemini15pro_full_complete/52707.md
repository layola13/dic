digressing:/daɪˈɡrɛsɪŋ/ /daɪˈɡresɪŋ/
|v.|
|离题; 跑题|
|Turning aside from the main subject of attention or course of argument; straying.|
|The speaker was digressing so much that the audience started to lose interest. 演讲者不断离题，听众开始失去兴趣。|

|He digressed from the main topic to discuss a related issue. 他偏离主题去讨论一个相关的问题。|


|近义词: deviating, straying, rambling, wandering|
|反义词: focusing, concentrating, staying on topic|
|词性: Verb (present participle)|
