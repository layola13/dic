fetterers:/ˈfetərərz/
n.
束缚者，压制者
Those who restrain or chain, either literally or figuratively.
The fetterers of free speech were condemned.  言论自由的压制者受到了谴责。

近义词:restrainers, suppressors, oppressors
反义词:liberators, emancipators
词性:名词(plural)


fetterer:/ˈfetərər/
n.
束缚者，压制者
Someone who restrains or restricts someone or something.
He saw himself as a fetterer of creativity in his corporate job. 他认为自己在公司的工作束缚了他的创造力。


近义词:restrainer, suppressor, oppressor
反义词:liberator, emancipator
词性:名词(singular)


词源: From the verb "fetter," which comes from the Old English "feter" related to "feet," originally referring to shackles for the feet.
