overread:/ˌoʊvərˈriːd/ | v. |  阅读过度；忽略；漏读 | To read over something quickly, often missing errors or important details; to fail to notice something when reading. | I overread the instructions and missed an important step. 我粗略地读了一遍说明书，漏掉了一个重要的步骤。

He overread the email and didn't realize the meeting had been cancelled. 他草草地读了邮件，没有意识到会议已经取消了。| 近义词：skim, scan, glance over, miss, overlook | 反义词：scrutinize, pore over, peruse |词性: 动词
