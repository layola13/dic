overshroud:/ˌoʊvərˈʃraʊd/
verb
覆盖，遮蔽
to cover or envelop something completely, especially to conceal it
The mountain peak was overshrouded in mist. 山峰被薄雾笼罩。
近义词: conceal, cover, envelop, obscure, veil
反义词: reveal, uncover, expose
词性: verb

overshroud:/ˌoʊvərˈʃraʊd/
noun
覆盖物，遮蔽物
something that covers or conceals
A dark overshroud of secrecy hung over the project.  一层神秘的黑暗笼罩着这个项目。
近义词: covering, veil, shroud
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "opening" or "exposure" depending on the context)
词性: noun


词源:
From over- + shroud.  "Over-" acts as a prefix intensifying the action of "shroud", meaning to cover completely. "Shroud" itself comes from Old English *scrūd, related to Old Norse skrūð "clothing, attire, shroud."
