marbury: /ˈmɑːrbəri/ (US), /ˈmɑːrbəri/ (UK)| n. |马伯里（姓氏）; (指)威廉·马伯里 (William Marbury),  是著名的“马伯里诉麦迪逊案”中的原告|  Marbury was appointed a justice of the peace, but his commission was not delivered. 马伯里被任命为治安法官，但他的委任状没有被送达。 |近义词: N/A (proper noun)|反义词: N/A (proper noun)|词性: noun

词源:  Likely a habitational name from a place called Marbury in Cheshire or another in Shropshire.  Both derive from Old English *mere burh "lake fortification".  (可能来源于柴郡或什罗普郡名为Marbury的地名，源于古英语 *mere burh，意为“湖泊堡垒”。)
