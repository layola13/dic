hyper-nutrition:/ˌhaɪpərnuˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |过度营养；营养过剩 |A state of over-nourishment or excessive intake of nutrients, which can have negative health consequences. | Example 1: Hyper-nutrition can lead to obesity and other health problems. 过度营养会导致肥胖和其他健康问题。

Example 2: The child suffered from hyper-nutrition due to excessive consumption of sugary drinks.  由于过度饮用含糖饮料，这个孩子营养过剩。 | Near Synonyms: overnutrition, excessive nutrition | Antonyms: malnutrition, undernutrition | noun
