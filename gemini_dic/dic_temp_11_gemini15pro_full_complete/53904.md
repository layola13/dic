discountenance:/dɪsˈkaʊntənəns/
verb
使气馁；使沮丧；反对；不支持
to make someone feel disconcerted or discouraged
例句：
She was discountenanced by the news of her friend's illness. 她被朋友生病的消息弄得沮丧。

The teacher discountenanced the student's disruptive behavior. 老师不赞成学生扰乱课堂秩序的行为。

近义词：discourage, dismay, disapprove, oppose
反义词：encourage, support, approve
词性：v.
