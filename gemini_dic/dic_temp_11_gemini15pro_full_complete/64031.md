epithelial:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl/ | adj.| 上皮的，上皮组织的 | Relating to or consisting of epithelium. |  Epithelial cells line the surfaces of organs and cavities in the body. 上皮细胞排列在身体器官和腔体的表面。|  Epithelial tissues play a crucial role in protection and secretion. 上皮组织在保护和分泌中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: epidermal, lining | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: adjective

词源: From modern Latin *epithelialis*, from Greek epi 'upon' + thēlē 'nipple'.  The original sense referred to the tissue covering the nipples, later extended to similar tissues.
