artifacting:/ˈɑːrtɪˌfæktɪŋ/
noun
伪影，人为现象
The process of producing unwanted alterations in data due to flaws in equipment or technique.
The MRI showed some artifacting around the metal implant.  核磁共振成像显示金属植入物周围有一些伪影。
synonyms: distortion, aberration, glitch, anomaly
antonyms: accuracy, precision
n.
