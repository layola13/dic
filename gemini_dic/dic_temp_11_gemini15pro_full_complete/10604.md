appraisal: /əˈpreɪzəl/ (US) /əˈpreɪzl/ (UK)| n.| 评估，估价，评价 |An act of assessing something or someone.  An expert appraisal of the painting. 对这幅画的专家鉴定。|例句：The appraisal of the house was higher than we expected. 房子的估价高于我们的预期。

例句：He gave a fair appraisal of the situation. 他对形势作出了公正的评价。

近义词：assessment, evaluation, valuation, judgment, estimate|反义词：criticism (sometimes), disregard|词性：noun
