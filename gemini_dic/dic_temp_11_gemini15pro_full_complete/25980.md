bursar:/ˈbɜːrsər/ | n. |财务主管；（大学的）奖学金管理员|A bursar is a person who manages the financial affairs of a college or university. |The bursar handles all student loan applications.  财务主管处理所有学生贷款申请。|Synonyms: treasurer, cashier|Antonyms: debtor, borrower|noun

bursars:/ˈbɜːrsərz/ | n. |财务主管们；（大学的）奖学金管理员们|Plural form of bursar, referring to multiple individuals who manage financial affairs, especially in educational institutions.|The bursars of several universities met to discuss funding strategies. 几所大学的财务主管开会讨论资助策略。|Synonyms: treasurers, cashiers|Antonyms: debtors, borrowers|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bursarier, from borse (purse), from Late Latin bursa (purse, bag), from Greek βύρσα (búrsa, "hide, skin of an animal").
