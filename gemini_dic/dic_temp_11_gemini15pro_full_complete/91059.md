hustler:/ˈhʌslər/| n.| 骗子；精力旺盛的人；妓女；拉客的人| someone who is aggressive and determined in trying to get what they want, especially money; a prostitute; someone who tries to persuade people to buy things or give them money in an aggressive or dishonest way | He was a hustler who made his living selling fake watches. 他是一个靠卖假表为生的骗子。| 近义词：go-getter, entrepreneur, striver, grafter (UK), hustler (informal), shark, operator, wheeler-dealer, scammer, con artist, fraudster (negative) | 反义词：idler, loafer, slacker| 词性: 名词

hustler:/ˈhʌslər/| vi.| 努力赚钱；卖淫；拉客| to work energetically and determinedly to achieve something, especially financial success; to work as a prostitute; to try to persuade people to buy things or give them money in an aggressive or dishonest way |  She hustled her way to the top of the company. 她努力工作，一路爬到了公司高层。| 近义词：strive, work hard, push, hustle (informal),  ply one's trade (for prostitutes), solicit, tout, peddle | 反义词：idle, laze, loaf| 词性: 动词
