antiexpressiveness:/ˌæntiɪkˈsprɛsɪvnəs/| n. | 反表达性，抑制表达，缺乏表达力 |The quality of discouraging or hindering expression, especially of emotions or individuality.  It refers to a system, language, or environment that actively inhibits or suppresses the ability to express oneself.  |  The strict uniform policy fostered a sense of antiexpressiveness among the students.  校服的严格规定助长了学生中的一种反表达性。 |  The corporate culture, with its emphasis on conformity, seemed to promote antiexpressiveness.  公司文化强调一致性，似乎在鼓励反表达性。 | synonyms: inexpressiveness, repression, suppression, inhibition | antonyms: expressiveness, articulateness, eloquence | noun
