dysfunctional:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəl/
adjective
机能失调的，功能障碍的
not operating normally or properly.
The dysfunctional family was constantly arguing.  这个机能失调的家庭总是争吵不断。
The printer is completely dysfunctional.  这台打印机完全坏了。
malfunctioning, impaired, broken
functional, working, healthy
adjective
