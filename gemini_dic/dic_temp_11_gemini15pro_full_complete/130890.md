nonarrogance:/ˌnɒnˈærəɡəns/
n.
谦逊，不傲慢
lack of arrogance; humility
Her nonarrogance and willingness to learn made her a popular colleague. 她谦逊好学的态度使她成为一位受欢迎的同事。
Synonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness
Antonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, pride
noun
