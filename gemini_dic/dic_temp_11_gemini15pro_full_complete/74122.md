fouled:/faʊld/| v. |犯规，弄脏，缠绕，阻塞|To commit a foul, especially in sports; to make something dirty or impure; to become entangled or clogged. |例句：He fouled the other player and gave away a penalty. 他对另一名球员犯规，被判罚点球。

|例句：The oil spill fouled the beaches. 漏油污染了海滩。|近义词：infringe, break, violate, dirty, pollute, contaminate, tangle, snag, jam, clog |反义词:clean, purify, untangle, clear|词性:动词

fouled:/faʊld/| adj. | 肮脏的，污秽的，堵塞的|Dirty, impure, or clogged.|例句：The air was fouled by smoke. 空气被烟雾污染了。

|例句：The ship's propeller was fouled by seaweed. 船的螺旋桨被海藻缠住了。|近义词：dirty, filthy, polluted, contaminated, clogged, blocked|反义词:clean, pure, clear|词性:形容词
