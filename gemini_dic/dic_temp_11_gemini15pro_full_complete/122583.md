modern-day:/ˈmɒdən deɪ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərn deɪ/ (US)| adj.|当代的，现代的|Relating to the present time; contemporary.|例句：Modern-day technology is constantly evolving. 当代科技不断发展。|例句：Many modern-day problems require innovative solutions. 许多现代问题需要创新的解决方案。|近义词：contemporary, present-day, current|反义词：ancient, old-fashioned|词性:形容词
词源:  A compound formed from "modern" (from Old French "moderne") and "day."
