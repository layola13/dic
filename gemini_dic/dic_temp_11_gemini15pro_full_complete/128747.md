niggardly:/ˈnɪɡərdli/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|Reluctant to give or spend; stingy; mean.|He was niggardly with praise, rarely offering a compliment.|近义词:stingy, miserly, parsimonious, frugal|反义词:generous, lavish, prodigal|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *nigardly*, from Old Norse *hnǫggr* (stingy) + Old English *-lic* (like).
