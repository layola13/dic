backhanders:/ˈbækˌhændəz/| n. |贿赂（尤指私下给的）; 回扣 |A sum of money or other material benefit offered or given, typically secretly, in return for a service or favour.| He was accused of taking backhanders from building contractors. 他被指控收受建筑承包商的贿赂。|Synonyms: bribe, kickback, payoff, sweetener, inducement |Antonyms: legitimate payment, donation| noun

backhander:/ˈbækˌhændə/| n. |反手击球 |A stroke played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke, as in tennis.|His backhander down the line was a thing of beauty. 他的反手击球沿线真是太漂亮了。|Synonyms: backhand stroke, backhand shot |Antonyms: forehand | noun
