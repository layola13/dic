impugnation:/ˌɪmpjʊɡˈneɪʃən/| n.|质疑，非难，攻击|The politician faced constant impugnation of his motives. 这位政治家不断面临对其动机的质疑。|例句：His impugnation of her character led to a lawsuit. 他对她的性格的攻击导致了一场官司。

例句：The defense attorney's strategy was to impugn the credibility of the prosecution's witnesses.  辩护律师的策略是质疑控方证人的可信度。

|近义词：challenge, question, dispute, attack, criticism|反义词:defense, support, praise, commendation|词性:名词
