liberty:/ˈlɪbərti/| n.|自由，自主，特权|The state of being free within society from oppressive restrictions imposed by authority on one's way of life, behavior, or political views.|Example: The statue is a symbol of liberty and freedom. 这座雕像是自由的象征。

Example:  They fought for liberty and justice. 他们为自由和正义而战。


|近义词：freedom, independence, autonomy|反义词：oppression, captivity, confinement|词性:noun
