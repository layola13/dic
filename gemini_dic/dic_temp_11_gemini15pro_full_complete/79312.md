gleamier:/ˈɡliːmiər/| adj.|更加闪亮的，更加光彩夺目的|More gleaming; shining more brightly.|The newly polished car looked gleamier than ever.  这辆刚抛光过的车看起来比以往任何时候都更加闪亮。|The moon was gleamier tonight than last night. 今晚的月亮比昨晚更明亮。|近义词：brighter, shinier, more radiant, more lustrous|反义词：duller, dimmer, less shiny|词性:形容词

词源: From gleam + -ier (comparative suffix).  Gleam itself comes from Old English *glǣm, of Germanic origin.
