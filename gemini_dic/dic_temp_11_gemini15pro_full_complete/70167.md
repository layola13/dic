fiberless:/ˈfaɪbərlɪs/
adjective
无纤维的；没有纤维的
Lacking fibers; having no fibers.
The fiberless meat was easy to chew.  这无纤维的肉很容易咀嚼。
The fruit has a smooth, fiberless texture.  这种水果质地光滑，无纤维。
Synonyms: smooth, fine-textured
Antonyms: fibrous, stringy
adjective
