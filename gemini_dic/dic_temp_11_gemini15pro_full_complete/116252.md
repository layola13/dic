mass-producible:/ˌmæs prəˈduːsəbl/ | adj. | 可大量生产的 | Able to be produced in large quantities, typically by machinery. |  Ford's assembly line made cars mass-producible. 福特的流水线使汽车能够大规模生产。|  The new technology made the product mass-producible and therefore more affordable. 新技术使该产品能够大规模生产，因此更便宜。 | mass-produced, manufacturable | unique, handcrafted | adjective
