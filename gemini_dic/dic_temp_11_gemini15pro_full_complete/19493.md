bidental:/baɪˈdɛntl/
adjective
双齿音的；用两颗牙齿发出的
Pronounced by placing the tip of the tongue between the upper and lower teeth.
She struggled to pronounce the bidental fricative. 她很难发出这个双齿摩擦音。

bidental:/baɪˈdɛntl/
noun
双齿音
A sound articulated with both upper and lower teeth.
The "th" in "thin" is a bidental. “thin”中的“th”是一个双齿音。


Etymology:  From Latin *bīdentālis (“having two teeth”), from bī- (“two”) + dentālis (“of teeth”), from dēns (“tooth”).

Synonyms:  interdental (more common for sounds)

Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider sounds produced with other parts of the mouth, like labial, velar, etc.)
