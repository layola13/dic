brahminist:/ˈbrɑːmɪnɪst/| n. adj.|婆罗门教徒，婆罗门教的|A follower of Brahmanism, or relating to Brahmanism.|例句：He was raised as a devout Brahminist. 他从小就被培养成为一名虔诚的婆罗门教徒。|例句：The Brahminist rituals are complex and fascinating. 婆罗门教的仪式复杂而引人入胜。|近义词：Brahman, Hindu (in a broad sense)|反义词:Buddhist, Jain|词性:名词, 形容词

词源: From Brahman + -ist.  Brahman comes from the Sanskrit word "bráhman," meaning "prayer, sacred knowledge."
