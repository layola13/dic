anamniotic:/ˌænæmniˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 无羊膜的 | Lacking an amnion, the innermost membrane that encloses the embryo of a mammal, bird, or reptile. |  Fish and amphibians are anamniotic vertebrates. 鱼类和两栖类是无羊膜脊椎动物。|  The anamniotic egg develops in water and lacks a shell. 无羊膜卵在水中发育，没有壳。 | synonyms:  anamniote | antonyms: amniotic | adjective
