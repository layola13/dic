harle:/hɑːrl/| n.|丑角，滑稽角色|A comic servant character, typically masked, dressed in multicolored, diamond-patterned costume, and carrying a wooden sword.|He played the harle in the commedia dell'arte troupe. 他在即兴喜剧剧团中扮演丑角。|Synonyms: clown, jester, buffoon|Antonyms: serious character|Noun
