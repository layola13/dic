idolizers:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzərz/| n. |崇拜者，偶像化者|People who idolize someone; ardent admirers.|例句：The pop star had many idolizers who would follow her everywhere.  这位流行歌星有很多崇拜者，他们会到处跟着她。

例句：His idolizers were devastated when news of his scandal broke. 当他的丑闻曝光时，他的崇拜者们都崩溃了。|近义词：fans, admirers, devotees, followers|反义词：critics, detractors|词性:noun (plural)
