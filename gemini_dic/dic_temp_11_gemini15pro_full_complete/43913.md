crevassing:/krəˈvæsɪŋ/
n.
裂缝形成，冰隙形成
The formation of crevasses, especially in glaciers.
Deep crevassing made the glacier impassable. 深深的裂缝使得冰川无法通行。
近义词: cracking, fracturing, fissuring
反义词: closing, sealing
词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "crevasse," which comes from French "crevasse," meaning "crevice" or "chasm." Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *crepacia, from Latin crepare "to crack, break, burst."
