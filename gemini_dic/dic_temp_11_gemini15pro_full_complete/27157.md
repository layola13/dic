call-sick:/ˌkɔːl ˈsɪk/| adj. |打电话请病假|To inform one's employer by telephone that one is unable to work due to illness.|例句：I had to call-sick this morning because I woke up with a terrible headache. 我今天早上不得不打电话请病假，因为我醒来时头痛得厉害。

例句：He called-sick three times last week. 他上周打了三次电话请病假。|近义词：report sick, phone in sick|反义词:come to work, present|词性：形容词
