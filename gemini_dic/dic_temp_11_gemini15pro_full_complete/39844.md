constablery: /ˈkɒnstəb(ə)lrɪ/ /ˈkɑːnstəb(ə)lɛri/ | n. |警察局，警员；治安机关| a police force or the buildings occupied by a police force. | The local constablery is investigating the crime. 当地警察局正在调查这起犯罪。| The constablery issued a warning about the rise in burglaries.  警察局发布了关于入室盗窃案增加的警告。| 近义词: police force, police station, police department | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *conestable*, from Old French conestable, from Late Latin comes stabuli (“count of the stable”), from Latin comes (“companion”) + stabuli (genitive of stabulum, “stable”). Originally referred to a high-ranking officer of the royal household. The sense shifted to a peace officer later.
