fretsaw:/ˈfrɛtsɔː/
n.
细锯，镂花锯
a fine-toothed saw used for cutting intricate curves
He used a fretsaw to create the delicate wooden ornament. 他用细锯制作了精美的木制装饰品。
近义词: scroll saw, coping saw, jeweler's saw
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a large, coarse saw like a chainsaw)
