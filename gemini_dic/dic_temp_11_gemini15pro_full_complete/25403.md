bulger:/ˈbʊlgər/ | n. |碎小麦，碾碎的干小麦|Wheat that has been parboiled, dried, and then coarsely ground.|Bulgur is a common ingredient in Middle Eastern cuisine. 碎小麦是中东菜肴的常见成分。|Example 2:  I made a salad with bulgur, tomatoes, and cucumbers. 我做了一个碎小麦、西红柿和黄瓜的沙拉。|Synonyms: cracked wheat, groats |Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could be contrasted with refined grains like white flour) |noun
