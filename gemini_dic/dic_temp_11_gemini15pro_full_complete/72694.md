focalization:/ˌfoʊkələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 焦点化;聚焦 | The act or process of focusing or concentrating. In narrative theory, it refers to the perspective through which a story is told.  | The focalization of the narrative shifted from the protagonist to a minor character.  叙事焦点从主人公转移到一个次要角色。 | synonyms: concentration, focus, centering | antonyms: diffusion, dispersal, spreading | noun

focalize:/ˈfoʊkəˌlaɪz/ | v. |  (使)聚焦; (使)集中 | To bring into focus; to concentrate. In narrative theory, it refers to the act of choosing a perspective from which to tell a story. | The author focalizes the story through the eyes of a child. 作者通过孩子的视角来聚焦故事。 | synonyms: focus, concentrate, center | antonyms: diffuse, disperse, spread | verb
