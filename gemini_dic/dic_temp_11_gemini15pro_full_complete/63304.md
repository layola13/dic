enthymeme:/ˈɛnθɪmiːm/| n.|省略三段论，省略式推理|An argument in which one premise is not explicitly stated.  An enthymeme relies on the audience's understanding or acceptance of an unstated premise.|Socrates is mortal because he's human. (The unstated premise is that all humans are mortal.) 苏格拉底终有一死，因为他也是人。（未说明的前提是人终有一死。）|

The advertisement relies on the enthymeme that younger skin is more beautiful. 这则广告依赖于年轻的皮肤更美丽的这种省略式推理。|synonym: argument, deduction, inference, reasoning|antonym: none|noun
