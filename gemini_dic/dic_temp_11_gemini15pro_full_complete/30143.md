ceint:/sɛ̃/ (French)| adj.| girdled, belted, surrounded |Encircled or surrounded, as if by a belt.  Specifically used in heraldry to describe a charge encircled by a band of a different tincture.  Less commonly used in general language.|The knight's shield was ceint with a band of gold. 骑士的盾牌上环绕着一条金带。|近义词：encircled, surrounded, belted, girdled|反义词: open, exposed|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Old French "ceint," past participle of "ceindre" (to gird, encircle), from Latin "cingere" (to gird).
