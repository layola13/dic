overshoe: /ˈoʊvərʃuː/ (US), /ˈəʊvəʃuː/ (UK)| n.|套鞋，鞋套|Made from various materials like rubber, plastic, or fabric, overshoes protect shoes from water, snow, mud, and other elements.
