hadiths:/ˈhædɪθs/ | n. | 圣训，圣训集 | A collection of traditions containing sayings of the prophet Muhammad that, with the Koran, constitute the major source of guidance for Muslims on all aspects of life. |  Many Muslims study the hadiths to understand the teachings of Islam. 许多穆斯林学习圣训以理解伊斯兰教的教义。 | synonyms: traditions, narrations | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps secular texts) | noun
