homilies:/ˈhɑːməliz/ /ˈhɒməliz/ | n. |布道，讲道，说教|A religious discourse that is intended primarily for spiritual edification rather than doctrinal instruction.|The priest gave a moving homily on the importance of forgiveness. 牧师就宽恕的重要性发表了一篇感人的布道。|近义词：sermon, lecture, discourse, preaching|反义词: condemnation, criticism | noun
