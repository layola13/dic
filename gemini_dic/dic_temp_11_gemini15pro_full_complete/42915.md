couturiers:/kuːˈtʊəriərz/
n.
时装设计师；女装设计师
Fashion designers; dressmakers, especially of high-fashion garments.
The couturiers of Paris are known for their exquisite designs.  巴黎的时装设计师以其精美的设计而闻名。
Synonyms: designers, dressmakers, stylists
Antonyms: customers, clients
noun
