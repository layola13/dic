brownnesses:/ˈbraʊnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
褐色，棕色; 深褐色;  (复数)
the quality or state of being brown.  The various shades or degrees of brown.
例句：The brownnesses of the autumn leaves were striking. 秋叶的各种褐色引人注目。

例句：He admired the different brownnesses of the coffee beans. 他欣赏咖啡豆的不同深浅的棕色。
synonym:brown shades, brown hues, brown tints
antonym:lightness, paleness, whiteness
n.
