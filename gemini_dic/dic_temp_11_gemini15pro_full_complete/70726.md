finchlike:/ˈfɪntʃlaɪk/| adj.|像雀的，雀类的|Resembling or characteristic of a finch, especially in small size, appearance, or behavior. |The bird had a finchlike beak, perfect for cracking seeds.  这只鸟有一个像雀一样的喙，非常适合嗑种子。| The small, finchlike bird flitted among the branches. 这只小小的、像雀一样的鸟在树枝间飞来飞去。|近义词：small, birdlike|反义词: large, unlike a finch|词性: adjective

词源:
From finch +‎ -like.
