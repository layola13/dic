encense:/ˈɪnsɛns/ | n. | 香, 香火 |A substance that produces a fragrant odor when burned, often used in religious ceremonies or for aromatherapy. | The temple was filled with the scent of incense. 寺庙里充满了香的味道。| synonyms: fragrance, perfume, aroma | antonyms: stench, odor, stink | noun

encense:/ɪnˈsɛns/ | vt. | 激怒, 使愤怒 | To make someone extremely angry. | His condescending remarks incensed her. 他居高临下的言论激怒了她。| synonyms: enrage, infuriate, anger, provoke | antonyms: appease, calm, soothe, placate | verb
