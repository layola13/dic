impaneling:/ɪmˈpænəlɪŋ/
n.
登记陪审员名单; 选定陪审团
The act of selecting and swearing in a jury.
The impaneling of the jury took several hours.  登记陪审员名单花了几个小时。
Near Synonyms:  jury selection, empaneling, empanelment
Antonyms: dismissal (of a jury)
noun

impanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/
vt.
登记陪审员名单; 选定陪审团
To enroll and swear in a jury.
The judge impaneled a jury of twelve people. 法官选定了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。
Near Synonyms:  empanel, swear in
Antonyms: dismiss
verb
