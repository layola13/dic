inferiors:/ɪnˈfɪəriərz/
n.
下属，下级；地位较低者；劣质的东西
people or things that are considered to be of lower quality, status, or ability than others.
The manager treated his inferiors with disdain.  经理轻蔑地对待他的下属。
His inferiors envied his quick promotion. 他的下属嫉妒他晋升迅速。
synonyms: subordinates, underlings, juniors
antonyms: superiors, betters
noun
