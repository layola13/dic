flicker:/ˈflɪkər/ (US), /ˈflɪkə(r)/ (UK)| vi. |闪烁，摇曳；一闪而过|To flicker means to burn or shine unsteadily, or to appear briefly and then disappear.  |The lights flickered and went out. 灯闪烁了一下就熄灭了。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flicorian, frequentative of *flican "to move quickly," of Germanic origin.
