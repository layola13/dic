agelessly:/ˈeɪdʒləsli/
adv.
永恒地，不老地
In a way that does not change or diminish with age.
She danced agelessly, her movements as fluid as ever. 她永恒地舞动着，她的动作一如既往地流畅。
Her beauty seems agelessly preserved. 她的美貌似乎永恒地保存了下来。
近义词: eternally, timelessly, forever
反义词: ephemerally, transiently, fleetingly
词性: 副词

ageless:/ˈeɪdʒləs/
adj.
不老的，永恒的
Not appearing to grow old or showing the effects of age.
The ageless beauty of the mountains captivated her. 群山永恒的美丽深深地吸引了她。
He has an ageless quality about him. 他身上有一种不老的特质。
近义词: timeless, enduring, everlasting
反义词: aging, old, temporary
词性: 形容词
