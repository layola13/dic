gasket:/ˈɡæskɪt/ | n. | 垫圈，密封垫 |A flat sheet of material (such as rubber, metal, or fiber) placed between two surfaces to make a tight seal and prevent leakage.|  The engine was leaking oil because the gasket was worn.  发动机漏油是因为垫圈磨损了。| synonyms: seal, washer, packing | antonyms: leak, opening | noun

gaskets:/ˈɡæskɪts/ | n. | 垫圈，密封垫（复数）| Multiple flat sheets of material used to create tight seals.|  He bought a set of new gaskets for the plumbing repairs. 他买了一套新的垫圈来修理管道。| synonyms: seals, washers, packings | antonyms: leaks, openings | noun
