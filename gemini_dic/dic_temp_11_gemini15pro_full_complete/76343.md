gad:/ɡæd/ | n. | 刺棒，尖头棒；刺激物 | A sharp-pointed stick; something that provokes or stimulates. |  He used a gad to prod the reluctant cattle. 他用刺棒戳不情愿的牛。| synonyms: goad, prod, spur | antonyms: deterrent, discouragement | noun

gad:/ɡæd/ | v. |  闲逛，游荡 | To roam about idly or restlessly; to wander. | She spent the afternoon gadding about town. 她一下午都在城里闲逛。 | synonyms: roam, wander, gallivant | antonyms: stay, remain | verb
