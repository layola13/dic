nonfissile:/ˌnɒnˈfɪsaɪl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪsəl/ | adj. | 非裂变的 | Not capable of undergoing fission (splitting into smaller nuclei) when bombarded with neutrons. |  Depleted uranium is nonfissile. 贫铀是非裂变的。 | synonyms: non-fissionable | antonyms: fissile | adjective

nonfissile:/ˌnɒnˈfɪsaɪl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪsəl/ | n. | 非裂变物质 | A substance that is not fissile. | This reactor uses a nonfissile material as a coolant.  这个反应堆使用非裂变材料作为冷却剂。| synonyms: non-fissionable material | antonyms: fissile material | noun
