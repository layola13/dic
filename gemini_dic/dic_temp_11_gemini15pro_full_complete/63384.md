entoplastron:/ˈɛntəˌplæstrən/
n.
腹甲前叶，前腹甲
A bony plate of the plastron of turtles and tortoises, located between the epiplastra anteriorly and the hyoplastra posteriorly.
The entoplastron of this ancient turtle fossil is remarkably well-preserved.  这只古代龟化石的腹甲前叶保存得非常完好。
近义词:interclavicle (in some contexts)
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical term)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐντός (entós, “within”) + πλάστρον (plástron, “breastplate”).
