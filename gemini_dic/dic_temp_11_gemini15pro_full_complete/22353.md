bonuses:/ˈboʊnəsɪz/ (US), /ˈbəʊnəsɪz/ (UK)
n.|奖金，红利，额外津贴|Something given or paid in addition to what is usual or expected.|The company offered bonuses to its employees for exceeding sales targets. 公司为超额完成销售目标的员工提供了奖金。|Synonyms:  rewards, perks, incentives, premiums|Antonyms: penalties, deductions, fines
