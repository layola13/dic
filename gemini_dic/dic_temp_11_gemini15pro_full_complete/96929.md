inexpugnable:/ˌɪnɪkˈspʌɡnəbl/
adjective
攻不可破的，无法征服的
Not able to be captured or defeated.  Impregnable.
例句：The castle proved inexpugnable, resisting every attack. 这座城堡被证明是攻不可破的，抵挡了每一次进攻。

例句：Their spirit remained inexpugnable despite the hardships they faced. 尽管面临重重困难，他们的精神依然坚不可摧。

近义词：invincible, unconquerable, impregnable, unassailable
反义词：vulnerable, weak, defenseless, conquerable
词性：adjective
