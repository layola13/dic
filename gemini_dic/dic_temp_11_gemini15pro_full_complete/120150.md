mincers:/ˈmɪnsəz/| n.|绞肉机；碎肉机；切碎机；<非正式>手|A mincer is a machine used to mince food, especially meat.  Mincers can also refer to hands, often in a humorous or informal context.|The butcher used a mincer to grind the beef. 肉贩用绞肉机绞碎牛肉.|近义词：grinder, chopper|反义词:|noun

mince:/mɪns/| n. v.|肉末；切碎；碎步走；装腔作势地说|As a noun, mince refers to finely chopped meat. As a verb, it means to chop food, especially meat, into very small pieces.  It can also describe a way of walking with short, quick steps, or speaking in an affected way, often implying insincerity.|She made a shepherd's pie with minced lamb. 她用羊肉末做了牧羊人派.
