overfoulness:/ˌoʊvərˈfaʊlnəs/| n. |过度污秽；极度肮脏|The overfoulness of the abandoned building was shocking. |例句：The overfoulness of the river was evident from the foul smell. 这条河的过度污秽从难闻的气味就能明显看出。

The overfoulness of the stable made it difficult to breathe.  马厩的极度肮脏使得呼吸困难。|近义词：filthiness, squalor, dirtiness, uncleanliness|反义词：cleanliness, purity, tidiness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "over" (OE ofer) + "foul" (OE fūl) + "-ness" (OE -nes). "Over" intensifies the meaning of "foul," which signifies something offensive to the senses, especially smell, and "-ness" creates the noun form denoting a state or quality.
