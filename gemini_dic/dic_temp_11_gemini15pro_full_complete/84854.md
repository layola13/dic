hangbird:/ˈhæŋbɜːrd/ | n. | 悬挂鸟，织布鸟 | A bird, especially of the family Ploeoceidae (weaver birds), that builds a hanging nest. | The hangbird constructs an elaborate nest from grasses and other plant fibers. 织布鸟用草和其他植物纤维建造了一个精致的巢。| The hangbird’s nest swayed gently in the breeze. 悬挂鸟的巢在微风中轻轻摇摆。 | synonyms: weaver bird, weaver finch | antonyms: N/A | noun
