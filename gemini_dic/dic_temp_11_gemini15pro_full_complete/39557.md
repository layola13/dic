conscientiously:/ˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəsli/| adv.|认真地，一丝不苟地，尽职尽责地|In a thorough and responsible way, guided by one's sense of duty and right.|She conscientiously completed all her homework assignments. 她认真地完成了所有的家庭作业。|He conscientiously checked every detail of the report. 他认真地检查了报告的每一个细节。|近义词：diligently, meticulously, carefully, thoroughly|反义词：carelessly, negligently, irresponsibly|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin conscientiōsus (“knowing with oneself”), from Latin consciēns (“conscious”).
