ethiop:/ˈiːθiɒp/ (UK), /ˈiːθiɑːp/ (US)| n. adj.|埃塞俄比亚人；埃塞俄比亚的|A person from Ethiopia, or relating to Ethiopia.|例句：He is an Ethiop. 他是一个埃塞俄比亚人。|例句：Ethiopian coffee is world-renowned. 埃塞俄比亚咖啡世界闻名。|近义词：Ethiopian|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *Æthiops, from Ancient Greek Αἰθίοψ (Aithíops, “Aithíop”, literally “burnt-face”), from αἴθω (aíthō, “I burn”) + ὤψ (ōps, “face”).  The term originally referred to various dark-skinned populations of Africa (and sometimes Asia).
