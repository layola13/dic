ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fēdan (attested only in past participle gefēded "fed"), from Proto-Germanic *fōdijaną (source also of Old Frisian fēda "to feed," Old Saxon fōdian, Old High German fuotan, German füttern "to feed," Old Norse fæða "to feed, nourish," Gothic 𐍆𐍉𐌳𐌾𐌰𐌽 (fōdjan)), from Proto-Indo-European *pā- "to protect, feed."
