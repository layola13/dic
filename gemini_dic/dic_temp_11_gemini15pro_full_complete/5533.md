alphabetizing: /ˈælfəbəˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | n. |按字母顺序排列|The act of arranging words or items in alphabetical order.|  She spent hours alphabetizing her CD collection. 她花了几个小时把她的CD收藏按字母顺序排列。| 近义词: ordering, sorting, arranging | 反义词: disordering, jumbling | 词性: Noun (Gerund)
Etymology: From alphabet + -ize + -ing.  Ultimately from Greek *álpha and *bêta, the first two letters of the Greek alphabet.
