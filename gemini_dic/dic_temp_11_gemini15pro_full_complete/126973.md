narrows:/ˈnæroʊz/ (US) /ˈnæroʊz/ (UK)| n. v.|变窄，缩小；狭窄的地方，海峡|The river narrows as it flows through the canyon. 这条河在流经峡谷时变窄了。|近义词：constricts, restricts, shrinks, straights|反义词：widens, broadens, expands|词性:名词,动词
Etymology: From Old English *nearwian, from nearu "narrow."
