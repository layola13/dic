fluttering:/ˈflʌtərɪŋ (US) / ˈflʌtərɪŋ (UK)| n.|飘动，振动，拍打|a quick, light movement|There was a fluttering of wings as the birds took flight. 鸟儿飞起时翅膀拍打着。|近义词：flapping, waving, quivering, trembling|反义词:stillness, motionlessness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flotteren, frequentative of *floteren, from Old Norse flotra (“to float”), from Proto-Germanic *flotōną (“to float”). Cognate with Icelandic flotra (“to flutter, hover”), Swedish fladdra (“to flutter”).
