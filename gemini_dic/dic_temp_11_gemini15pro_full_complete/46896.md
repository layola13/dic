darkeners:/ˈdɑːrkənərz/
n.|加深剂；变暗剂；遮光剂|Substances or things that make something darker. |The photographer used darkeners to enhance the contrast in the black and white prints. 摄影师使用加深剂来增强黑白照片的对比度。|synonyms: dimmers, obscurants|antonyms: brighteners, lighteners|noun
