dematerialized: /ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪzd/
adjective
非物质化的；去物质化的
Converted from physical to electronic form.  Existing in a non-physical or electronic form.
例句：Dematerialized shares are held electronically, eliminating the need for physical certificates. 非物质化股票以电子方式持有，无需纸质证书。

例句：The company is moving towards a dematerialized office, with most employees working remotely and documents stored in the cloud.  该公司正朝着非物质化办公的方向发展，大多数员工远程工作，文件存储在云端。

近义词：digital, electronic, intangible, virtual
反义词：physical, tangible, material
词性：adjective
