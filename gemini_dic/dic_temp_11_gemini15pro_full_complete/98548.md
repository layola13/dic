instilling:/ɪnˈstɪlɪŋ/
verb
灌输，逐渐灌输
gradually establish (an idea or attitude) in a person's mind.
例句：The coach is instilling a sense of discipline in his players. 教练正在向他的队员灌输纪律意识。

例句：Parents try to instill good values in their children. 父母努力给孩子灌输良好的价值观。

近义词：implanting, inculcating, infusing, imbuing
反义词：eradicating, removing, eliminating
词性：verb (present participle of instill)
