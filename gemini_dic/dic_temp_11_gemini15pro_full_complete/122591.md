modernises:/ˈmɒdənaɪzɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪzɪz/ (US)| v. | 使现代化 | To make something more modern; to update or improve something to suit contemporary needs and standards. |例句：The company modernises its equipment regularly to maintain a competitive edge.  该公司定期更新设备以保持竞争优势。

例句：The government is modernising the education system to prepare students for the 21st century. 政府正在使教育系统现代化，以使学生为21世纪做好准备。

近义词：updates, upgrades, innovates, improves, renovates | 反义词：outdates, archaises | 词性：动词
