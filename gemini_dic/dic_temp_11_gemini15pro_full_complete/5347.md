alludes:/əˈludz/| v. |暗示，间接提到|To suggest or call attention to indirectly; hint at.|The author alludes to classical mythology in his latest novel.  这位作者在他的最新小说中提到了古典神话。|
alludes:/əˈludz/| v. |暗示，间接提到|He alluded to problems with the new management. 他暗示了新管理层存在问题。|近义词：suggest, imply, hint, intimate, refer|反义词：state, declare, specify|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alludere*, from *ad-* "to" + *ludere* "to play".  Originally meaning "to play with," "to mock," the meaning shifted to "to hint at" in the 16th century.
