non-slip:/ˌnɒnˈslɪp/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈslɪp/ (US)| adj.|防滑的|Designed to prevent slipping.  Having a surface that prevents or reduces slipping.|例句：She bought a non-slip mat for the shower. 她买了一个淋浴用的防滑垫。|近义词：slip-resistant, anti-skid, grip|反义词:slippery, smooth|词性:adjective
Etymology: "non-" (not) + "slip" (to slide unintentionally)
