dlitt:/diːˈlɪt/ | n. | 文学博士学位 (Doctor of Letters) | Doctor of Letters, an honorary degree awarded by universities for outstanding literary achievement. It is considered a higher doctorate than a PhD. | She received a D.Litt. from Oxford University for her contributions to poetry. 她因对诗歌的贡献获得了牛津大学的文学博士学位。 | Example 2:  He was awarded a D.Litt. for his lifetime achievement in novel writing. 他因其在小说创作方面的终身成就而被授予文学博士学位。 | 近义词:Doctor of Letters, honorary doctorate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
