alonely:/əˈloʊnli/ | adj. | 孤独的，寂寞的 | Feeling or experiencing loneliness; sad because one is alone or isolated. |  例句：The elderly man felt alone after his wife passed away. 这位老先生在妻子去世后感到孤独。

例句：She spent a lonely evening at home. 她独自在家度过了一个寂寞的夜晚。

近义词：lonesome, solitary, isolated, secluded | 反义词：accompanied, sociable, gregarious | 词性：形容词
