molybdenite:/məˈlɪbdəˌnaɪt/| n. |辉钼矿|A soft black mineral, MoS₂, the chief ore of molybdenum. It resembles graphite and is used as a solid lubricant. |Molybdenite is an important source of molybdenum. 辉钼矿是钼的重要来源。| The geologist identified the sample as molybdenite. 地质学家鉴定该样品为辉钼矿。|Synonyms: molybdenum sulfide, MoS2|Antonyms: None|Noun
