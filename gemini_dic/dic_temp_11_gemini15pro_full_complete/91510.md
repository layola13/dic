hymne:/ˈhɪmn/ | n. |赞美诗，圣歌|A song of praise, especially one sung in a church or to a god. |例句：The congregation sang a beautiful hymn.  会众唱了一首优美的赞美诗。

例句：They closed the service with their favorite hymn.  他们以他们最喜欢的赞美诗结束了礼拜。

近义词：anthem, psalm, carol, chant |反义词:blasphemy, profanity |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ymne*, from Late Latin hymnus, from Ancient Greek ὕμνος (humnos, “song of praise”).
