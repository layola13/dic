bigwig:/ˈbɪɡwɪɡ/| n. |大人物，要人，显贵|A person of importance, especially in a particular sphere.|例句：The local bigwigs all attended the gala. 当地的名流都出席了晚会。|例句：He's a bigwig in the financial world. 他是金融界的大人物。|近义词：VIP, dignitary, notable, magnate|反义词：nobody, underling|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the practice of important judges and officials (especially in the 18th and 19th centuries) wearing large wigs.  Originally, the large wigs themselves were referred to as "bigwigs." The term gradually transferred to the individuals who wore them.
