assiento:/əˈsjɛntoʊ/
noun
协议，契约; (尤指昔日西班牙与其他国家签订的)黑奴贸易协定
A contract or agreement, especially one formerly between Spain and other countries relating to the slave trade.
The Asiento de Negros granted Britain the right to supply slaves to Spain's American colonies.  黑奴贸易协定赋予英国向西班牙美洲殖民地供应奴隶的权利。
The asiento system proved highly profitable for the British.  事实证明，协定体系对英国非常有利可图。
Synonyms:  contract, agreement, pact, treaty
Antonyms: disagreement, refusal, denial
