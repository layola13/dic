determinant:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/| n. |行列式；决定因素；决定物|A factor that decisively affects the nature or outcome of something.|The determinant of the matrix is zero.  该矩阵的行列式为零。|synonym: factor, element, cause, |antonym: inconsequential, insignificant|noun

determinant:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/| adj. |决定性的|Serving to determine or decide something.|Cost was a determinant factor in our decision.  成本是我们决策中的一个决定性因素。|synonym: decisive, crucial, pivotal, essential |antonym: insignificant, unimportant, minor|adjective
