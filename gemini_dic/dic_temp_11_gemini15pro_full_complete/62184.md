encomium:/ɛnˈkoʊmiəm/
n.
赞美; 颂词; 赞颂; 称赞
A formal expression of praise; a eulogy.
The critics' encomium solidified the movie's place in history as a classic.  影评人的高度赞扬巩固了这部电影作为经典的历史地位。

The professor delivered a moving encomium at his mentor's retirement party. 教授在导师的退休派对上发表了感人的颂词。

synonym:  praise, tribute, eulogy, panegyric, accolade
antonym: criticism, condemnation, censure, denunciation
noun
