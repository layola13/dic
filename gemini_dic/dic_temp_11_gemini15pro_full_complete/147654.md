overponderousness: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːndərəsnəs/
noun
过度沉重；过度冗长；过于严肃
The state of being excessively ponderous; heaviness, dullness, or excessive seriousness.
His overponderousness made the lecture incredibly tedious.  他的过度冗长使得讲座极其乏味。
近义词: heaviness, dullness, tediousness, pomposity
反义词: lightness, levity, brevity, conciseness
词性: noun

overponderous: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːndərəs/
adjective
过度沉重的；过度冗长的；过于严肃的
Excessively ponderous; heavy, dull, or excessively serious.
The overponderous language of the legal document made it difficult to understand. 这份法律文件冗长的语言使其难以理解。
近义词: heavy, dull, tedious, pompous
反义词: light, lively, brief, concise
词性: adjective

词源:  From over- + ponderous + -ness. Ponderous itself comes from Late Middle English: from Old French pondereux, from Latin ponderosus ‘weighty,’ from pondus ‘weight.’
