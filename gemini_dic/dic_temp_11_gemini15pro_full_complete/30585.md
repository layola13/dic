cercarian: /tʃɛrˈkɛəriən/ | adj. | 寻求的，探索的 |  Archaic form of "searching."  This word derives from Old French "cerchier" (to search) ultimately from Latin "circare" (to go around, traverse).  It is rarely used in modern English. |  The cercarian knight sought the Holy Grail. 寻求圣杯的骑士。 | 近义词: searching, seeking, exploring, questing | 反义词: indifferent, apathetic | 词性: adjective
