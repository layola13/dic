in-step: /ɪnˈstɛp/ | adj. | 步调一致的，同步的 |  Moving or acting in unison or agreement with others; harmonious. | The dancers moved in-step with the music. 舞蹈演员们随着音乐的节奏同步舞动。 | The government's policy is in-step with public opinion. 政府的政策与公众舆论一致。| 近义词: coordinated, synchronized, harmonious, unison | 反义词: out-of-step, discordant | 词性: 形容词

词源： "in" + "step"，"in" 表示“在…里面”，“step”表示“步伐”。
