hibernating:/ˈhaɪbərneɪtɪŋ/| adj. | 冬眠的，蛰伏的 | Spending the winter in a dormant state. | The bear is hibernating in its cave. 熊在它的洞穴里冬眠。|Synonyms: dormant, inactive, sleeping, torpid | Antonyms: active, awake, lively, waking | adjective

hibernating:/ˈhaɪbərneɪtɪŋ/| n. | 冬眠 | The state of being in hibernation. |  The bear's hibernating lasted for several months.  熊的冬眠持续了几个月。| Synonyms: dormancy, inactivity | Antonyms: activity, wakefulness | noun
