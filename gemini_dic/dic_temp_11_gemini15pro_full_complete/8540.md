anticeremonially:/ˌæntiˌserɪˈməʊniəli/
adverb
反常规地；不拘礼节地
in a way that avoids or ignores the usual ceremonies or formalities.
The couple anticeremonially eloped to Las Vegas. 这对夫妇不拘礼节地私奔到拉斯维加斯。
informally, casually, simply
ceremonially, formally, traditionally
词性: 副词
