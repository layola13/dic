aidless:/ˈeɪdlɪs/| adj.|无助的，得不到援助的|Without aid; helpless; deprived of help or assistance.|The aidless villagers were stranded after the flood. 洪水过后，无助的村民们被困住了。|The injured hiker was aidless in the wilderness. 受伤的徒步旅行者在荒野中孤立无援。|近义词：helpless, unsupported, unaided|反义词：aided, helped, supported|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From aid + -less.  "Aid" comes from Old French "aide" (help, assistance), which in turn comes from Latin "adjutum" (helped), the past participle of "adjuvare" (to help). The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
