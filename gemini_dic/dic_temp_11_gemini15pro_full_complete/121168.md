misdialing:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəlɪŋ/ /ˌmɪsˈdaɪlɪŋ/
n.
误拨
the act of dialing a wrong telephone number
I apologized for misdialing his number. 我为拨错他的号码而道歉。

misdial:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəl/ /ˌmɪsˈdaɪl/
v.
拨错电话号码
to dial a wrong telephone number
I misdialed and got a wrong number. 我拨错了号码，接通了错误的电话。

Etymology: mis- + dial
"mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly".
"dial" comes from the Old French word "dial" meaning "sundial".
