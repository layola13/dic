holophytic:/ˌhɒləˈfɪtɪk/ /ˌhoʊləˈfɪtɪk/
adjective
全植物性的；自养的
Obtaining nourishment as green plants do, by synthesizing complex organic compounds from inorganic raw materials, using energy from light.
例句：Green plants have a holophytic mode of nutrition. 绿色植物具有全植物性营养模式。
例句：Holophytic organisms, like plants, produce their own food.  像植物一样的自养生物自己生产食物。
近义词：autotrophic, photosynthetic
反义词：heterotrophic, saprophytic
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *holos* (whole, entire) + *-phytos* (planted, grown)
