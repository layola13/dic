four-strokes:/ˈfɔːr stroʊks/ | n. | 四冲程 |A type of internal combustion engine where the piston completes four separate strokes (intake, compression, power, and exhaust) during two revolutions of the crankshaft.  | This car has a four-stroke engine. 这辆车有四冲程发动机。| Near synonyms: four-cycle, Otto cycle | Antonym: two-stroke | noun


four-strokes:/ˈfɔːr stroʊks/ | adj. | 四冲程的 |Relating to or operating on the four-stroke cycle. |  This lawnmower has a four-stroke engine, so it's quieter than a two-stroke.  这台割草机是四冲程的，所以比两冲程的安静。| Near synonyms: four-cycle | Antonym: two-stroke | adjective
