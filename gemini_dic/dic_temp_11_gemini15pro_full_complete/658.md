abrogating:/ˈæbrəɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb
废除；取消
Putting an end to; formally abolishing.
The government is abrogating the treaty. 政府正在废除该条约。
annulling, revoking, repealing, rescinding
instituting, establishing, enacting

verb
废除的；取消的
Relating to the act of abolishing or ending something formally.
The abrogating clause in the contract rendered it void. 合同中的废除条款使其无效。
invalidating, nullifying
validating, establishing

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abrogare (to repeal), from ab- (away from) + rogare (to ask, propose a law).
