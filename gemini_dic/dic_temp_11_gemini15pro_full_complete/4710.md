aleuronic:/ˌæljuˈrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 糊粉的；糊粉层的 | Relating to aleurone, a protein found in the outer layer of the endosperm of grass seeds. | The aleuronic layer is rich in protein. 糊粉层富含蛋白质。| The aleuronic grains are important for germination. 糊粉粒对种子萌发很重要。 | synonymous: protein-rich, endospermic | antonymous:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-aleuronic) | adjective
