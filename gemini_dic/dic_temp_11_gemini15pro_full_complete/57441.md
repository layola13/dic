draconian:/drəˈkoʊniən/
adjective
严酷的，苛刻的
Excessively harsh and severe.
例句：The government imposed draconian laws to suppress dissent. 政府实施了严苛的法律来压制异议。

例句：The school had a draconian dress code. 学校有着极其严格的着装规定。

近义词：harsh, severe, cruel, oppressive, stringent, rigorous
反义词：lenient, merciful, moderate, mild
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Draco, an Athenian law-maker of the 7th century BC, whose code of laws prescribed death for almost every offense.
