locellate:/ˈlɒsəˌleɪt/ | adj. | 有小眼的；具單眼的 | Having small eyes or simple eyes (ocelli), especially as found in certain insects and other invertebrates. |  Many insects have locellate markings on their wings. 许多昆虫的翅膀上有小眼状的斑点。 |ocellated, eye-spotted| eyeless | adjective

locellus:/ləˈsɛləs/ | n. | 小眼;單眼 | A small eye or simple eye (ocellus), especially in insects and some other invertebrates. | The locellus of the insect helps it detect light changes. 昆虫的单眼帮助它探测光线变化。 | ocellus, simple eye | compound eye | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *locellus, diminutive of locus (“place”), because the ocelli appear as small spots or places.
