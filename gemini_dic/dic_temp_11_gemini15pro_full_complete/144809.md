over-hardens:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnz/
| v. |过度硬化；硬化过度|To make something excessively hard, beyond the desired or optimal level of hardness.|The blacksmith over-hardened the sword, making it brittle. 铁匠把剑过度硬化了，使它变得很脆。|over-stiffen, over-toughen|soften, weaken|verb


over-hardens:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnz/
| adj. |过度硬化的|Excessively hardened.|The over-hardened steel snapped easily. 过度硬化的钢很容易断裂。|over-stiffened, over-toughened|soft, weakened|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "harden" (from Old English heardian, from Proto-Germanic *hardjaną) meaning "to make hard".
