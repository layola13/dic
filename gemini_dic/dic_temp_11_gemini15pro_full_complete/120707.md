misaligned:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnd/| adj.|未对准的，不对齐的|Not properly aligned; out of alignment.  Positionally incorrect; not matching.  Differing in opinion or goals. |The wheels on my car are misaligned, causing it to pull to the right. 我的车轮没有对准，导致车子向右偏。

The text boxes were misaligned, making the document look unprofessional.  文本框没有对齐，使文档看起来不专业。

Our goals for the project were misaligned, leading to disagreements and delays. 我们对这个项目的目标不一致，导致了分歧和延误。|近义词：out of line, crooked, skewed, asynchronous,  discordant|反义词：aligned, straight, in line, synchronous,  harmonious|词性:形容词

misaligned:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnd/| v. |使未对准，使不对齐|(Usually used in the passive voice) To cause something to be out of alignment. |The equipment was misaligned during shipping. 设备在运输过程中位置错乱了。

The telescope was misaligned, making it impossible to get a clear image. 望远镜没有对准，无法获得清晰的图像。|近义词：dislocate, put out of line|反义词：align, straighten|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrong' or 'bad') + aligned (past participle of align, from Old French alignier 'arrange in a line', from a- 'to' + ligne 'line').
