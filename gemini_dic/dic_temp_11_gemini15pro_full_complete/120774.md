misappreciate:/ˌmɪsəˈpriːʃieɪt/
|v.|误解；低估；轻视；|To fail to appreciate rightly or adequately; to undervalue or underestimate.|She misappreciated his kindness, thinking he had ulterior motives. 她误解了他的好意，以为他别有用心。|Synonyms: underestimate, undervalue, underrate, misread, misconstrue|Antonyms: appreciate, value, overestimate, overrate|verb
