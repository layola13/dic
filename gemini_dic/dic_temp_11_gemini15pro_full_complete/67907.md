face-the-music:/feɪs ðə ˈmjuːzɪk/
|idiom|
|面对现实;承担后果;接受批评|
|To accept the unpleasant consequences of one's actions.|
|He knew he had to face the music sooner or later after failing the exam.|他明白考试不及格迟早要承担后果。|
|accept the consequences, take responsibility, pay the piper|
|avoid responsibility, evade consequences, shirk one's duty|
