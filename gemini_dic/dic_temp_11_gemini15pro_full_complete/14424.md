autumn: /ˈɔːtəm/ (British) /ˈɑːtəm/ (American) | n. | 秋天，秋季 | The season after summer and before winter, characterized by falling leaves and cooler temperatures. |  The leaves change color in autumn. 秋天的树叶会变色。

The crisp air of autumn is invigorating. 秋天清爽的空气令人精神焕发。


近义词: fall (American English) | 反义词: spring | 词性: noun
