dangerously:/ˈdeɪndʒərəsli/| adv. |危险地|In a way that is likely to cause harm or injury.|例句：He was driving dangerously. 他开车很危险。


例句：The building was dangerously close to collapsing. 这栋楼快要倒塌，十分危险。

近义词：hazardously, precariously, riskily, recklessly|反义词：safely, securely|词性：副词

词源: From Middle English *dangerosly, from Old French dangereusement, from dangereux + -ment. Ultimately from Latin dominarium (“ownership, property”).
