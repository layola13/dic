marinate:/ˈmærɪneɪt/ /ˈmærɪˌneɪt/| v. |腌制|To soak food in a marinade, typically before cooking, in order to flavor or tenderize it. |例句：I marinated the chicken in a mixture of herbs and spices. 我把鸡肉浸泡在香草和香料的混合物中腌制。

例句：The steak was marinated overnight for maximum flavor.  牛排腌制了一整夜，以获得最佳风味。|近义词：soak, pickle, steep|反义词: N/A|词性: verb


marinated:/ˈmærɪneɪtɪd/ /ˈmærɪˌneɪtɪd/| adj. |腌制的|Having been soaked in a marinade. |例句：We enjoyed the marinated tofu skewers. 我们很喜欢吃腌制的豆腐串。

例句：The marinated pork chops were grilled to perfection.  腌制好的猪排烤得恰到好处。|近义词：pickled, steeped|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mariner*, from Italian *marinare* "to pickle in brine," from *marina* "brine," from Latin *marinus* "of the sea," from *mare* "sea."  The original meaning referred to preserving food in seawater or brine.
