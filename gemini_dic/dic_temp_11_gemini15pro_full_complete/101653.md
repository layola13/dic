irregularist:/ɪˈrɛɡjʊlərɪst/
n.|不规则主义者；不合常规者|A person who advocates or practices irregularity, especially in art, literature, or behavior.|The artist, a self-proclaimed irregularist, refused to conform to traditional styles.|近义词：nonconformist, maverick, rebel, iconoclast|反义词：conformist, traditionalist|词性: 名词
Etymology: From irregular + -ist. "Irregular" comes from Late Latin "irregularis," from "in-" (not) + "regularis" (regular), ultimately from Latin "regula" (rule).
