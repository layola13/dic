fortifiable:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
可加强的，可筑防御工事的
Capable of being fortified or strengthened, especially against attack.
The city's location made it easily fortifiable.  这座城市的位置使其易于设防。
The general surveyed the fortifiable hilltop. 将军勘察了可以筑防御工事的山顶。
近义词:defensible, securable, protectable
反义词:vulnerable, indefensible, weak
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Middle English *fortifiable, from Old French fortifiable, from fortifier  "to fortify," from Vulgar Latin *fortificare, from Latin fortis  "strong" + facere  "to make."
