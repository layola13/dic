industrializes:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
工业化
To develop industries in (a country or region) on a wide scale.
例句：The government is trying to industrialize the rural areas. 政府正在努力将农村地区工业化。

例句：Many developing countries are rapidly industrializing. 许多发展中国家正在快速工业化。

近义词：develop, modernize, advance
反义词：ruralize, deindustrialize
词性：v.
