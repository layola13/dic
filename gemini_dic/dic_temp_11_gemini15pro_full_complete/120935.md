miscellanea:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪniə/| n.|杂项，杂集|A collection of various items or information, especially of a literary or artistic nature.|例句：The book is a miscellanea of essays and poems. 这本书是散文和诗歌的杂集。

例句：Her attic was filled with a miscellanea of old toys and forgotten treasures. 她的阁楼里堆满了各种各样的旧玩具和被遗忘的宝藏。


|近义词：assortment, collection, medley, variety|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) uniformity, homogeneity|词性:noun
