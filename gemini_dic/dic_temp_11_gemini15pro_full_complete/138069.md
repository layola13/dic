normalism:/ˈnɔːrməlɪzəm/ | n. |师范教育，师范主义| The study or practice of teaching, especially as conducted in normal schools or colleges. | The normalism movement of the 19th century aimed to professionalize teaching. 19世纪的师范教育运动旨在使教学专业化。|  The decline of normalism has been linked to the rise of university-based teacher training programs. 师范教育的衰落与大学师范培训项目的兴起有关。 | synonyms: teacher training, pedagogy, teacher education | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "amateur teaching" or "unstructured learning") | noun
