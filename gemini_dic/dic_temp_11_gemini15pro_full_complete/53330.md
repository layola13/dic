disaccustom:/ˌdɪsəˈkʌstəm/ | vt. | 使不习惯，戒除 | To cause someone to lose a habit or stop doing something regularly. | He is trying to disaccustom himself from smoking. 他正努力戒烟。

The soldiers were disaccustomed to civilian life after years of war.  多年战争后，士兵们不习惯平民生活。


近义词: wean, break, unlearn | 反义词: accustom, habituate | 词性: 动词
