melancholies:/ˈmɛləŋkɒliz/ (UK) /ˈmɛlənˌkɑːliz/ (US)|n.|忧郁，伤感，愁思|A feeling of pensive sadness, typically with no obvious cause.  A state of melancholy.|He suffered from deep melancholies after the death of his wife. 他妻子去世后，他陷入了深深的忧郁。|Synonyms: sadness, dejection, depression, gloom, sorrow|Antonyms: happiness, joy, elation, cheerfulness|noun
