bamboozlement:/bæmˈbuːzlmənt/
|n.|迷惑，欺骗，哄骗|The act of bamboozling or the state of being bamboozled; deception; trickery.|The magician's bamboozlement of the audience was truly impressive. 魔术师对观众的欺骗真是令人印象深刻。|Synonyms: deception, trickery, chicanery, hoodwinking, mystification|Antonyms: clarification, enlightenment, honesty, truth|noun
