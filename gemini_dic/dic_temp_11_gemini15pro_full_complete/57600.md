draperied:/ˈdreɪpərid/
adjective
垂着帷幔的，覆盖着布料的
covered with cloth hanging in loose folds
The windows were draperied in heavy velvet curtains. 窗户上挂着厚厚的丝绒窗帘。
近义词: draped, curtained
反义词: bare, uncovered
词性: adjective

词源: From drape + -ed.  "Drape" comes from the Old French word "draper" meaning "to make cloth."
