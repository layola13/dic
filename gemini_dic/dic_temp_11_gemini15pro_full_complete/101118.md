invertible:/ɪnˈvɜːrtəbl/ | adj. | 可逆的，可倒置的 | Able to be inverted or reversed.  A function is invertible if it has an inverse function. A matrix is invertible if its determinant is not zero. |  The process is invertible, so we can recover the original data.  这个过程是可逆的，所以我们可以恢复原始数据。 | reversible, inversible | non-invertible, irreversible | adjective

invertible:/ɪnˈvɜːrtəbl/ | n. | 可逆元素，可逆对象 |  An element or object that has an inverse. This term is commonly used in mathematics, particularly in the context of groups, rings, and matrices.  | The invertible elements of a group form a subgroup.  群的可逆元素构成一个子群。 | reversible element, inverse element | non-invertible element, singular element | noun
