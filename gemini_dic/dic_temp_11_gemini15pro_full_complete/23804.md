brazenfaced:/ˈbreɪzənˌfeɪst/ | adj. | 厚颜无耻的， brazen-faced (US) /ˈbreɪznˌfeɪst/|  Shameless and bold; impudent. | He told a brazenfaced lie, even though everyone knew the truth. | 他厚颜无耻地撒谎，即使每个人都知道真相。|  Synonyms: impudent, shameless, audacious, bold, insolent | Antonyms: bashful, shy, modest, humble | adjective

brazen-faced:/ˈbreɪznˌfeɪst/ (US) | adj. | 厚颜无耻的| Shameless and bold; impudent. | He had the brazen-faced audacity to ask for a raise after only a week on the job. | 他竟然厚颜无耻地要求加薪，才工作一个星期。| Synonyms: impudent, shameless, audacious, bold, insolent | Antonyms: bashful, shy, modest, humble | adjective

词源:
From "brazen" (made of brass) + "faced" (having a particular kind of face).  Implies a hardened, unembarrassed countenance like brass.
