overold:/ˌoʊvərˈoʊld/| adj.|太老的，过时的|Too old; past its prime.  This bread is overold and stale. 这面包太老了，不新鲜了。|近义词：ancient, antiquated, obsolete, outdated|反义词：new, young, fresh, modern|词性:形容词

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "over, above, beyond") + "old" (Old English ald, eald, from Proto-Germanic *aldaz, meaning "old, ancient")
