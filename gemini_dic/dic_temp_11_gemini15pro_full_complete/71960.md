flightinesses:/ˈflaɪtinɪsɪz/| n.|轻浮；反复无常；轻率|The state of being flighty; characterized by unpredictable changes in behavior, attitude, or mood.  A fickle or frivolous nature.|Her flightinesses made it difficult to rely on her.|Synonyms: capriciousness, fickleness, volatility, levity, frivolity|Antonyms: steadiness, constancy, stability, seriousness|noun
