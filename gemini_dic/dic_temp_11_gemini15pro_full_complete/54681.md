disorderedly:/dɪsˈɔːrdərli/
adverb
混乱地；无秩序地
in a disordered or messy way.
The books were piled disorderedly on the shelf. 书架上的书堆放得乱七八糟。
近义词:chaotically, messily, haphazardly
反义词:orderly, neatly, methodically
词性:副词
