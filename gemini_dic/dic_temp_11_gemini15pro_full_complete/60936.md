elevates:/ˈɛləˌveɪts/
verb
提升，提高，改进
raises something to a higher position or level, either literally or figuratively.
The platform elevates the actors above the audience.  这个平台将演员抬高到观众之上。
Reading good literature elevates the mind. 阅读优秀的文学作品能提升思想境界。
lift, raise, hoist, boost, upgrade
lower, depress, degrade
