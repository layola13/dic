bendier:/ˈbɛndiər/| adj.|更易弯曲的，更灵活的|More easily bent or flexed; more pliable.|The bendier wire was easier to shape into a complex design.  更易弯曲的金属丝更容易塑造成复杂的形状。|近义词：flexible, pliable, supple|反义词:rigid, stiff|词性:形容词

bend:/bɛnd/| v. n.|弯曲，屈服|To force something straight into a curved or angled shape; a curve or turn.|She bent the wire into a circle. 她把金属丝弯成一个圆圈。|近义词：curve, flex, bow|反义词:straighten, unbend|词性:动词/名词

词源: From Old English *bendan "to bend, curve, stretch, strain," from Proto-Germanic *bandjanan "to string (a bow)," related to *bindanan "to bind" (see bind (v.)). The original sense probably involved the tension of a bent bow.
