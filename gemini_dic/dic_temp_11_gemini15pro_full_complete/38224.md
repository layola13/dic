computationally:/ˌkɑːmpjuˈteɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 计算地，通过计算 | In terms of performance, it's computationally expensive. 就性能而言，它的计算成本很高。 | This problem is computationally complex. 这个问题在计算上很复杂。 | 近义词:algorithmically, digitally | 反义词:manually, analogously | 词性:副词

Etymology:
From computation + -al + -ly. Computation comes from Late Latin computationem, from computatus, past participle of computare "to count, sum up, reckon," from com- "together" + putare "to prune, cleanse, reckon."
