otolaryngology:/ˌoʊtoʊˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːlədʒi/ /ˌəʊtəʊˌlærɪŋˈɡɒlədʒi/
n.
耳鼻喉科
The branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the ear, nose, and throat.
Otolaryngology is a surgical subspecialty within medicine. 耳鼻喉科是医学中的一个外科分支。
The doctor specializes in otolaryngology. 这位医生专攻耳鼻喉科。
近义词:ENT (ear, nose, and throat)
反义词:None
词性:noun
