nonunderstood:/ˌnɒnʌndəˈstʊd/
adjective
未被理解的，不被了解的
Not understood; not grasped or comprehended.

例句：The instructions were poorly written and nonunderstood by most people.  这些指示写得很糟糕，大多数人都无法理解。

近义词：misunderstood, misinterpreted, uncomprehended, obscure, unclear
反义词：understood, comprehended, clear, lucid

词性:adjective
