disseminates:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪts/
|v.|传播，散布，宣传|To spread something, especially information, widely.|The organization disseminates information about the disease through pamphlets and public service announcements.  该组织通过小册子和公共服务公告宣传有关这种疾病的信息。|spreads, distributes, propagates, circulates, broadcasts|suppresses, conceals, withholds, restricts, limits|verb


disseminates:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪts/
|v.|散播（种子等）|To scatter or spread widely, like seeds.|The wind disseminates the dandelion seeds across the field. 风把蒲公英的种子吹散到田野里。|scatters, disperses, spreads|collects, gathers, concentrates|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dissēmināre*, from *dis-* (“apart, in every direction”) + *sēmen*, *sēminis* (“seed”).
