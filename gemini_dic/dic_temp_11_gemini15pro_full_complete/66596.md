exhumers:/ɪksˈhjuːmərz/
|n.|掘墓者；发掘者|People who dig up (exhume) something, usually bodies, from the ground.  This refers to the act of removing something buried, especially a corpse, from the earth.|The exhumers carefully removed the coffin from the ancient tomb.|近义词: excavators, disinterrers|反义词: buriers, interrers|noun
