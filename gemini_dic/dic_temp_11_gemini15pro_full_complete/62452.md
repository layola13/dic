endorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 赞同，支持；背书；代言 |  An act of giving one's public approval or support to someone or something.  A signature on the back of a check transferring ownership. An advertisement in which a famous person recommends a product. | The product has received endorsements from several celebrities. 该产品得到了几位名人的代言。| synonyms: approval, support, backing, sanction, advocacy, recommendation, testimonial | antonyms: disapproval, opposition, condemnation, rejection | noun

endorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 批注，签注 |  A note or signature written on a document. |  My driving license has several endorsements. 我的驾照上有好几条批注。 | synonyms: annotation, note, inscription, remark, comment | antonyms: deletion, erasure | noun
