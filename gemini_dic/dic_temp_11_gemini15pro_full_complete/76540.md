gallantly: /ˈɡæləntli/ | adv. | 勇敢地；豪迈地；殷勤地 | In a brave, spirited, or noble-minded manner; in a courteous and attentive way, especially toward women. | He gallantly offered his seat to the elderly lady. 他殷勤地把座位让给了那位老妇人。 | 近义词: bravely, courageously, chivalrously, courteously | 反义词: cowardly, timidly, rudely | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Old French "galant," meaning "courteous," "valiant," derived from "galer" meaning "to rejoice."
