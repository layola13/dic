abreast-with:/əˈbrɛst wɪð/| adj.|与...并驾齐驱，了解...的最新情况|informed about the latest developments or information on a particular subject.|例句：She makes a point of keeping abreast with the latest fashion trends. 她很注意跟上最新的时尚潮流。

例句：It is important for doctors to keep abreast with the latest medical research.  对于医生来说，跟上最新的医学研究进展非常重要。

|近义词：up-to-date with, informed about, familiar with, conversant with|反义词：ignorant of, uninformed about, behind the times|词性：形容词
