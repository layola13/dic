contortionistic:/kənˌtɔːrʃəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
柔术的；扭曲的
of or relating to contortion or contortionists; involving contortion
The contortionistic act was both amazing and disturbing.  这个柔术表演既令人惊叹，又令人不安。
She displayed contortionistic skills, bending her body into unbelievable shapes. 她展示了柔术技巧，将身体弯曲成令人难以置信的形状。
近义词:flexible, supple, acrobatic
反义词:stiff, rigid, inflexible
词性:adjective
词源:From contortion + -istic. Contortion from Late Latin contortio, from contort- , past participle stem of contorquēre to twist violently, from com- + torquēre to twist.
