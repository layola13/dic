deprived:/dɪˈpraɪvd/| adj.|被剥夺的，贫困的|Lacking the necessities of life, such as adequate food, shelter, or education; disadvantaged.|例句：The deprived children in the slum had little access to education.  贫民窟里贫困的孩子几乎没有受教育的机会。

例句：Many deprived communities lack access to clean water. 许多贫困社区缺乏清洁用水。|近义词：disadvantaged, impoverished, underprivileged, needy|反义词：privileged, affluent, wealthy|词性:形容词
词源:From the past participle of *deprive*, from Old French *depriver*, from Latin *dēprīvāre* "to bereave, rob, dispossess," from *dē-* "away, from" + *prīvāre* "to deprive," from *prīvus* "private, one's own, single."
