nominations:/ˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənz/
n.|提名；任命；推荐|The nominations for the Oscars were announced. 奥斯卡奖提名已经公布。|例句：The film received several nominations for awards.  这部电影获得了几个奖项的提名。|近义词：appointments, designations, recommendations|反义词：rejections, withdrawals|词性:名词


Etymology:
From Latin *nominātiōn- (stem of nominātiō), from nominātus, past participle of nomināre  "to name," from nōmen "name."
