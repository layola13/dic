freeze-framing:/ˈfriːz freɪmɪŋ/| n. |定格； freeze-framing (also freeze framing) |The technique of freeze-framing allows us to analyze a specific moment in a film.  定格技术使我们能够分析电影中的特定时刻。|例句：The director used freeze-framing to emphasize the actor's expression. 导演用定格来强调演员的表情。

freeze-framing:/ˈfriːz freɪmɪŋ/| v. |定格； freeze-frame (also freeze frame) |To pause a video or film on a particular frame.  将视频或电影暂停在特定帧上。|例句：He freeze-framed the video to see the suspect's face clearly. 他将视频定格以便清楚地看到嫌疑人的脸。|近义词：pausing, stopping|反义词:playing, continuing|词性:v.
