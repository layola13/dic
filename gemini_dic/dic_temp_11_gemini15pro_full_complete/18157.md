being-that:/ˈbiːɪŋ ðæt/ | conj. | 鉴于；因为；由于 |  Used to introduce a reason or explanation for something.  Similar to "since" or "given that". | Being that it was raining, we decided to stay inside. 因为下雨，我们决定待在室内。| 近义词: since, given that, because, as, considering that | 反义词: although, though, even though | 词性: conjunction

词源:  From the present participle of "be" + "that."  The construction developed from the use of "being" as a present participle introducing an absolute clause, followed by "that" introducing a dependent clause.
