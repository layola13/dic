half-timbered:/ˌhɑːfˈtɪmbərd/ |adj.|半木结构的|A building style where the wooden framework is exposed on the exterior walls, with the spaces between the timbers filled with plaster, brick, or other materials.|The town is famous for its charming half-timbered houses.|Synonyms: timber-framed, Tudor-style|Antonyms: modern, concrete|adjective
