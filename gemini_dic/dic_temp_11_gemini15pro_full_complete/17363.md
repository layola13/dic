beaders:/ˈbiːdərz/
n.
串珠者；饰珠者；使用珠子的人
People who make things with beads; those who use beads for decoration or crafting.
The beaders were selling their intricate jewelry at the craft fair.  串珠者在工艺品展览会上出售他们复杂的珠宝。
The beaders worked tirelessly, stringing tiny beads onto the delicate wire.  串珠者不知疲倦地工作，把小珠子串到细线上。
近义词: crafters, artisans, jewelry makers
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun (plural)


bead:/biːd/
n.
珠子；水珠
A small, usually round object with a hole through it, used for making jewelry or other decorative items; a drop of liquid.
She wore a necklace of brightly colored beads. 她戴着一条色彩鲜艳的珠子项链。
Beads of sweat trickled down his forehead. 汗珠从他的额头上滚落下来。
词源:  From Old English *bēad, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bidaną "to pray," because beads were originally used for counting prayers.
近义词: pearl, droplet, globule
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
