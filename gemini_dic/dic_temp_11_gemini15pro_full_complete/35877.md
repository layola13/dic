cockying:/ˈkɒkiɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkiɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|趾高气扬的，傲慢的|Acting in a conceited or arrogant manner, often with a swagger or show of superiority.  Derived from "cocky," which itself comes from "cock" (referring to a rooster, known for its strutting behaviour).|He walked into the room with a cockying swagger, clearly pleased with himself. 他趾高气扬地走进房间，显然对自己很满意。|She gave a cockying smile after winning the competition.  她赢得比赛后露出了傲慢的微笑。|近义词：arrogant, swaggering, conceited, haughty, supercilious|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性:形容词
