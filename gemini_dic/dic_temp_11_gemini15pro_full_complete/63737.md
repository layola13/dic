epic:/ˈɛpɪk/ | adj. n. |史诗的；壮丽的；英雄的；史诗；叙事诗 |A long poem, typically one derived from ancient oral tradition, narrating the deeds and adventures of heroic or legendary figures or the history of a nation.  A film, book, or other work portraying heroic deeds and adventures, usually on a grand scale. |  Example: The Odyssey is an epic poem. 《奥德赛》是一部史诗。

Example:  The battle was an epic struggle between good and evil. 这场战斗是善与恶之间的一场史诗般的斗争。| 近义词: heroic, grand, majestic, monumental | 反义词: mundane, ordinary, trivial | 词性: adjective, noun
