inherit:/ɪnˈhɛrɪt/ /ɪnˈherɪt/ | v. |继承，遗传|To receive (money, property, or a title) from someone when they die. To derive (a quality, characteristic, or predisposition) genetically from one's parents or ancestors.|She inherited a fortune from her grandfather. 她从她祖父那里继承了一大笔财产。

He inherited his father's love of music. 他遗传了他父亲对音乐的热爱。|近义词：receive, acquire, derive, obtain|反义词：forfeit, lose, relinquish|词性:动词
