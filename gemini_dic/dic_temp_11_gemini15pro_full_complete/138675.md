nugae:/ˈnjuːɡiː/ /ˈnuːɡeɪ/
noun
琐事；无价值的东西；无聊的事
Trifles; things of no importance or value; nonsense.
The book was filled with learned nugae, offering little of real substance. 这本书充斥着学究气的琐事，没有什么真正的实质内容。
Synonyms: trifles, trivialities, frivolities, bagatelles, trinkets
Antonyms: essentials, necessities, important matters
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nugae*, plural of *nuga* ("a trifle, jest, joke, witty saying").
