canebrake:/ˈkeɪnˌbreɪk/| n. |甘蔗林，芦苇荡|A thicket of canes, especially giant cane (Arundinaria gigantea).|例句：The pig disappeared into the dense canebrake.  猪消失在茂密的甘蔗林里。

例句：Canebrakes once covered vast areas of the southeastern United States.  甘蔗林曾经覆盖了美国东南部的大片地区。|近义词：thicket, brake, stand|反义词: clearing, open field|词性:noun
