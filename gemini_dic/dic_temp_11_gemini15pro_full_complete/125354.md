multilobe:/ˈmʌltiˌloʊb/ | adj. | 多裂的，多叶的 | Having multiple lobes or rounded divisions. |  The multilobe leaf of the oak tree is a distinctive feature. 橡树的多裂叶子是一个显著特征。| The doctor examined the patient's multilobe liver. 医生检查了病人多叶的肝脏。 | multilobed, multipartite | simple, entire | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "multi-" (meaning "many") + "lobe" (from Latin "lobus," meaning a rounded projection).
