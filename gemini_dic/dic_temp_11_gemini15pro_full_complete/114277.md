mainframes:/ˈmeɪnfreɪmz/| n.|大型机，主机|A large, high-speed computer, especially one supporting many users simultaneously.  It is typically used by large organizations for critical applications, bulk data processing such as census, industry and consumer statistics, enterprise resource planning, and financial transaction processing.|例句：The company relies on mainframes for its core business operations.  这家公司依靠大型机进行其核心业务运营。

例句：Mainframes are still used by many large banks and insurance companies. 许多大型银行和保险公司仍在使用大型机。|近义词：mainframe computer, big iron|反义词:microcomputer, personal computer|词性:noun
