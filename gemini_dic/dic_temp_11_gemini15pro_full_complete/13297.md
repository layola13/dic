atheromatous:/ˌæθərəˈmeɪtəs/| adj.|粥样的，动脉粥样硬化的|Relating to or affected by atheroma, a fatty deposit in the inner lining of an artery.|The patient's arteries were atheromatous, increasing his risk of a heart attack.  病人的动脉粥样硬化，增加了心脏病发作的风险。|Synonyms: atherosclerotic, sclerotic|Antonyms: healthy (referring to arteries), clear (referring to arteries)|adjective

Etymology->
From Greek *athēr* (gruel) + *-ōma* (tumor, swelling) + *-atos* (adjectival suffix)
