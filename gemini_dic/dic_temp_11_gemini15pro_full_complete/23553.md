brainsick: /ˈbreɪnsɪk/ | adj. | 疯狂的；精神错乱的；愚蠢的 | Affected with or exhibiting mental derangement; insane; mad.  | He had a brainsick idea to rob a bank with a water pistol. 他有个愚蠢的想法，要用一把水枪抢银行。 |  He made a brainsick decision to quit his job without another lined up. 他做了一个疯狂的决定，在没有找到下一份工作的情况下就辞掉了现在的工作。 | 近义词: crazy, insane, mad, demented, lunatic | 反义词: sane, rational, sensible | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From brain + sick.  "brain" refers to the organ of thought, and "sick" indicates a state of illness or dysfunction.  Therefore, "brainsick" literally means "sick in the brain."
