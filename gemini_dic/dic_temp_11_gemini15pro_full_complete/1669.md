acmic: /ˈækmɪk/ | adj. | 酸性的，酸味的 |  Relating to or containing acid; sour.  Derived from the Greek word "akmē" meaning "point, edge," which later evolved to mean "acid" due to the sharp, biting sensation of acidic substances. | The acmic taste of the lemon made her pucker. 柠檬的酸味让她皱起了脸。 | Synonyms: acidic, tart, sour | Antonyms: alkaline, basic, sweet | adjective
