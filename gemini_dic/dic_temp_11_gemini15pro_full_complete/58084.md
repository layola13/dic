drooping:/ˈdruːpɪŋ/| adj. | 下垂的，萎靡的 | hanging down; weak and tired | The flowers were drooping in the heat. 花朵在高温下都垂了下来。 | synonyms: sagging, wilting, declining | antonyms: upright, erect, perky | adjective

droop:/druːp/| v. | 下垂，萎靡 | to hang down; to become weak and tired |  Her shoulders drooped with weariness. 她疲惫地垂下肩膀。 | synonyms: sag, wilt, decline | antonyms: rise, perk up, revive | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *drupa "to droop," from Proto-Germanic *drupan, of uncertain origin.  Related to Old English dreopan "to drip, fall in drops," and possibly to Old English drūp "sad, dejected."
