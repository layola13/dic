diastoles:/daɪˈæstəliːz/ /daɪˈæstəliz/ | n. | 心舒期（复数） | The period of time when the heart refills with blood after contraction.  The plural form of diastole. |  The patient's diastoles were consistently low. 病人的舒张压一直很低。 | Near synonyms: relaxation phase, quiescent period | Antonyms: systoles | noun

diastole:/daɪˈæstəliː/ /daɪˈæstəli/ | n. | 心舒期 | The phase of the heartbeat when the heart muscle relaxes and allows the chambers to fill with blood. | The diastole is shorter than the systole. 心脏舒张期比收缩期短。| Near synonyms: relaxation phase, quiescent period | Antonyms: systole | noun
