amenage:/əˈmeɪndʒ/ /əˈmɛnɑʒ/ | v. | 布置，整理，装修 | To furnish or equip; to put in order; to arrange suitably. |  They amenaged the apartment with stylish furniture. 他们用时尚的家具布置了公寓。

The company amenaged the office to create a more collaborative workspace.  公司对办公室进行了装修，以打造一个更具协作性的工作空间。| 近义词: furnish, equip, outfit, decorate, arrange | 反义词: disarrange, clutter, mess up | 词性: verb

Etymology: From French *aménager*, from *à* (to) + *ménager* (to manage, to arrange), ultimately from Latin *mansionem* (dwelling).
