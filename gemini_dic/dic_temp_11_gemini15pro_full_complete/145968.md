overdiverseness:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsnəs/| n. |过度多样性|The state of being excessively diverse; having too much diversity.|The overdiverseness of the rainforest ecosystem makes it difficult to study individual species.  雨林生态系统的过度多样性使得研究单个物种变得困难。|Synonyms: excessive diversity, plethora of species, overabundance of variety |Antonyms: lack of diversity, homogeneity, uniformity|noun


overdiverseness:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsnəs/| n. |过度多样性|A situation where there is more diversity than is considered optimal or manageable.|The committee felt that the overdiverseness of opinions was hindering progress on the project. 委员会认为意见的过度多样性阻碍了项目的进展。|Synonyms:  superfluous diversity,  redundant variety|Antonyms:  simplicity,  lack of variation|noun
