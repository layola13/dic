Etymology:
From dowager + -ism.  Dowager comes from Old French douagiere, from douage, from douer "to endow," from Latin dotare "to endow."  The suffix -ism denotes a characteristic behavior or quality.  词源：来自 dowager + -ism。Dowager 来自古法语 douagiere，源自 douage，来自 douer “赋予”，来自拉丁语 dotare “赋予”。后缀 -ism 表示一种典型的行为或品质。
