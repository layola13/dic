nonrhythms:/nɒnˈrɪðəmz/ | n. | 非节奏，无节奏 | Absence of rhythm or regularity.  Irregular patterns or movements that lack a consistent beat or flow. | The dancer's nonrhythms were captivating, a mesmerizing display of unpredictable movement. 舞蹈演员的非节奏动作令人着迷，展现了不可预测的运动，让人目不转睛。

The nonrhythms of the city's traffic made it difficult to predict travel times. 城市交通的不规律性使得难以预测出行时间。 | Near synonyms: arrhythmia, irregularity, disorder, disharmony | Antonyms: rhythm, regularity, order, harmony | noun
