accessioned:/əkˈseʃənd/
verb
已登记入藏的，已加入的
formally recorded as belonging to an institution, especially a library or museum; to record something as belonging to a collection
The museum accessioned a new collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts.  博物馆将新一批古埃及文物登记入藏。
Synonyms: acquired, obtained, cataloged, registered
Antonyms: deaccessioned, removed, discarded
v.
