conformances:/kənˈfɔːrmənsɪz/
n.
符合，一致；遵从，遵守
acts of conforming to a rule or necessity
The company's actions are in conformance with industry standards. 该公司的行为符合行业标准。
conformity, compliance, obedience
nonconformity, noncompliance, disobedience
noun

conformance:/kənˈfɔːrməns/
n.
符合，一致；遵从，遵守
the act or state of conforming
The building was not in conformance with local fire codes. 这栋建筑不符合当地的消防规范。
conformity, compliance, obedience
nonconformity, noncompliance, disobedience
noun
