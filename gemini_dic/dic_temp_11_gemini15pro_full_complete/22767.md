borscht:/ˈbɔːrʃt/ (US), /ˈbɔːʃt/ (UK)| n.|罗宋汤|A beet soup of Ukrainian origin, usually served with sour cream.|例句：

My grandmother makes the best borscht.
我的祖母做的罗宋汤是最好的。

We had a hearty bowl of borscht for dinner.
我们晚饭喝了一大碗热腾腾的罗宋汤。|近义词：beet soup, beetroot soup |反义词: None (No direct antonym for a specific type of food)|noun
