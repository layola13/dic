overbeat:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːt/| v.|过度击打; 过度渲染; (心脏)过速跳动|To beat excessively, or to exaggerate or overemphasize something.|The drummer overbeat the cymbal, drowning out the other instruments. 鼓手过度敲击钹，盖过了其他乐器的声音。|Near synonyms: overemphasize, exaggerate, overdo, overplay|Near antonyms: understate, downplay|verb

overbeat:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːt/| n.| (音乐)强拍上的弱音节; 过强的拍子|(Music) An unaccented beat or note performed with undue emphasis. | The conductor criticized the orchestra's overbeat on the third measure. 指挥批评了乐队在第三小节的过强拍子。|Near synonyms: offbeat|Near antonyms: downbeat|noun
