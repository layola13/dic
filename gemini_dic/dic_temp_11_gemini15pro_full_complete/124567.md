mourn:/mɔːrn/| v. |哀悼，忧伤|To feel or express deep sorrow or regret for someone or something lost or dead.|She mourned the death of her father. 她哀悼她父亲的去世。|The nation mourned the loss of its leader.  全国哀悼失去他们的领袖。|近义词：grieve, lament, sorrow|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|词性：动词

词源: From Old English *murnan, from Proto-Germanic *murnan, from Proto-Indo-European *smer- (to remember).  Related to German murren (to grumble).  The original sense was "to remember, to care for," which developed into "to feel sorrow for."
