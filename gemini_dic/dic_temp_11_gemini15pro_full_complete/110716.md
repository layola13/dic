linemen:/ˈlaɪnmən/| n.|线路工人；（橄榄球）锋线球员|A lineman is a person who works on electric power lines or telephone lines.  A lineman in American football is a player who lines up on the line of scrimmage.|The linemen worked tirelessly to restore power after the storm.  暴风雨过后，线路工人不知疲倦地工作以恢复电力。

The team's linemen are known for their strength and agility.  这支球队的锋线球员以他们的力量和敏捷而闻名。|近义词：linesman, electrician (for power lines), lineman (football)|反义词: N/A|Noun
