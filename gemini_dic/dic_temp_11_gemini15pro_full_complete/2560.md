admit:/ədˈmɪt/ | v. |承认；准许进入；容纳|To confess to be true or to be the case. To allow to enter; to grant entrance to. To have room for; to be capable of containing.|He admitted his mistake. 他承认了自己的错误。

The museum admits visitors until 5 pm. 博物馆允许游客入内直到下午5点。

The theater admits 500 people.  这个剧院可容纳500人。|近义词：confess, acknowledge, concede, permit, allow|反义词：deny, refuse, reject|词性:动词

admit:/ədˈmɪt/ | n. |承认；入场许可|An act of admitting; permission to enter.|His admit was a relief to his family. 他的认罪让他的家人松了一口气。|近义词：confession, acknowledgement, entrance, admission|反义词：denial, refusal|词性:名词 (较少使用)
