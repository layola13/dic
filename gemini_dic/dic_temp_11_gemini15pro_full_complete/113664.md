machismos:/məˈtʃɪzmoʊz/
n.
大男子主义，男性至上主义
Exaggerated pride or self-importance in being a man, typically manifested in an excessive sense of masculinity, dominance over women, and aggressive or macho behavior.
His machismos prevented him from admitting he was wrong. 他的大男子主义让他无法承认自己错了。

近义词:masculinity, sexism, male chauvinism
反义词:feminism

词性:noun


machismo:/məˈtʃɪzmoʊ/
n.
大男子主义，男性至上主义
Strong or exaggerated sense of manliness; an assumptive attitude of male dominance.
He displayed a swaggering machismo. 他表现出一种招摇的大男子主义。

近义词:masculinity, sexism, male chauvinism
