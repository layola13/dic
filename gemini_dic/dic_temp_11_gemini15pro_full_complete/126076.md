mustily:/ˈmʌstɪli/ | adv. | 发霉地，霉臭地 | In a stale, moldy, or damp manner; smelling of damp and decay. | The old books smelled mustily, like a forgotten attic.  这些旧书散发着霉味，就像一个被遗忘的阁楼。 | The basement room smelled mustily of mildew.  地下室的房间散发着霉菌的霉味。| 近义词: moldily, damply, fustily | 反义词: freshly, airily, cleanly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From musty + -ly. Musty comes from Middle English *musti, from Old French moissi "moldy," past participle of moisir "to grow moldy," from Vulgar Latin *mucēre, from Latin mūcēre "be moldy, be musty."
