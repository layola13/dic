bast:/bæst/| n.| 内层树皮，韧皮纤维 |The bast of the tree was used to make rope. 这棵树的韧皮被用来制作绳子。|The fibrous material found beneath the bark of a tree, used for making rope, mats, etc. |The bast of the flax plant is used to make linen. 亚麻植物的韧皮纤维用来制作亚麻布。|近义词：phloem, fiber|反义词: xylem (木质部 - 功能上的反义词)|词性:noun
