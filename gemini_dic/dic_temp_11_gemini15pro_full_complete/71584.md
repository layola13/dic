flashmobbing: /ˈflæʃmɒbɪŋ/ | n. |快闪族活动；快闪行动| The act of participating in a flash mob.  A flash mob is a large public gathering at which people perform an unusual and seemingly random act and then disperse, often for the purposes of entertainment, satire, or artistic expression. |  Example: Flashmobbing has become a popular form of social expression. 快闪行动已经成为一种流行的社会表达方式。 | Example: The police were alerted to the possibility of flashmobbing in the city center.  警方接到了市中心可能发生快闪行动的警报。 | Synonym: flash mob, smart mob | Antonym: N/A | noun
