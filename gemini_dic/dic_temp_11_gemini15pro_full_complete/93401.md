illiteracy:/ɪˈlɪtərəsi/| n.|文盲，不识字|The country has a high rate of illiteracy.  这个国家的文盲率很高。| illiteracy is the inability to read or write. 文盲是指没有阅读或书写的能力。|例句：Illiteracy is a major problem in many developing countries. 文盲是许多发展中国家的主要问题。

|例句：The government is trying to reduce illiteracy by providing free education. 政府正在努力通过提供免费教育来降低文盲率。|近义词：analphabetism|反义词: literacy|词性:noun
