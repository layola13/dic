cram:/kræm/| v. n.| 塞满；填鸭式地学习；临时抱佛脚|To force, press, or squeeze something into a small space; to study intensively over a short period of time, especially for an exam.|I crammed all my clothes into the suitcase. 我把所有衣服都塞进了手提箱。|近义词：stuff, pack, jam, squeeze, revise (v.), swot (British English)|反义词: empty|词性:动词,名词

cram:/kræm/| n.| 挤满；拥挤；填鸭式学习|A crowded situation; intensive study over a short period.|The exam cram left her exhausted.  考试前的填鸭式学习让她筋疲力尽。|近义词：crush, crowd|反义词: emptiness|词性:名词
