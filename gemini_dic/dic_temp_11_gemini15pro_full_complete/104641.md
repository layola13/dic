karat:/ˈkærət/ (US), /ˈkærət/ (UK)| n. |克拉（黄金纯度的单位）|A unit of fineness for gold equal to 1/24 part of pure gold in an alloy.  For example, 24-karat gold is pure gold, while 18-karat gold is 18/24 or 75% gold.|This ring is made of 18 karat gold. 这枚戒指是由18K金制成的。

The necklace was advertised as 24 karat gold. 这条项链的广告宣传是24K金的。|近义词:carat|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
