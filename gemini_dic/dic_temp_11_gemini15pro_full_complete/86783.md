hem-in:/ˌhem ˈɪn/ | v. |包围；限制；阻碍|To surround and confine; to restrict or restrain.|The troops were hemmed in on all sides by the enemy. 部队被敌人四面包围了。|  The mountains hemmed the village in. 群山环绕着村庄。|近义词：confine, restrict, enclose, surround, trap|反义词：liberate, free, release|词性: 动词

hem-in:/ˌhem ˈɪn/ | n. |包围；限制；阻碍 (rarely used as a noun)|The act of hemming in; confinement.|The hem-in of the city by the floodwaters caused widespread panic.  洪水对城市的包围引起了广泛的恐慌。(较少用作名词)| 近义词：confinement, restriction, enclosure|反义词：liberation, freedom, release|词性: 名词

词源: From Old English *hemman "to confine, restrict," related to Old High German hemmu "hindrance," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hamjaną.
