nonadjudicated: /ˌnɒnəˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
未判决的；未裁定的
Not having been formally judged or decided upon, especially in a legal context.
The nonadjudicated cases piled up on the judge's desk.  未判决的案件堆积在法官的桌子上。
The status of the claim remained nonadjudicated for months. 这项索赔的状态几个月来一直未得到裁决。
近义词: unsettled, unresolved, pending, undetermined
反义词: adjudicated, decided, settled, resolved
词性: adjective
词源:  From "non-" (not) + "adjudicated" (past participle of "adjudicate"), which comes from Latin "adjudicare" (to award by judicial sentence).
