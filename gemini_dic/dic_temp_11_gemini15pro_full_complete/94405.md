implicating:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
牵连，暗示，表明（某人参与不良事件）
suggesting that someone is involved in something bad or criminal
The evidence implicates him in the robbery.  证据表明他参与了抢劫。
synonyms: incriminate, involve, connect, link
antonyms: exonerate, absolve, clear

implicating:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
牵连的，暗示的
involving or suggesting someone's involvement in something bad
The implicating evidence led to his arrest. 牵连的证据导致他被捕。
synonyms: incriminating, compromising
antonyms: exonerating, absolving


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin implicare ("to involve, entangle, enfold"), from in- ("in, on, upon") + plicare ("to fold").
