congregations: /ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ (UK) /ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ (US)
n.
集会，会众；（教堂的）教区居民
A group of people assembled for religious worship; a gathering.  The people who regularly attend a particular church.
The pastor addressed the congregation. 牧师向会众讲话。
The church has a large and diverse congregation.  这个教堂有一个庞大而多元化的教区居民。
近义词: assemblies, gatherings, audiences, crowds
反义词: dispersal, scattering
词性: noun
