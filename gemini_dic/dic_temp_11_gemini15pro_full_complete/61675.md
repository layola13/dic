emmetropia:/ˌɛmɪˈtroʊpiə/| n. |正视，屈光正常|The condition of the eye when parallel rays of light are focused exactly on the retina, thus producing a clear image.|She has the good fortune to have emmetropia and doesn't need glasses. 她很幸运拥有正视眼，不需要戴眼镜。|Example:  His emmetropia allowed him to see clearly without corrective lenses. 他的正视眼让他无需矫正镜片就能看得清楚。|Synonym: normal vision |Antonym: ametropia, myopia, hyperopia |noun
