conglutinate:/kənˈɡluːtɪneɪt/ | v. | 粘合，胶合 | to stick together; to unite by glue or other adhesive substance | The proteins conglutinate to form a solid mass. 蛋白质粘合形成固体块。| synonyms: adhere, bond, cement, cohere, fuse | antonyms: separate, detach, divide | verb

conglutination:/kənˌɡluːtɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 粘合，胶合 | the act or process of conglutinating; the state of being conglutinated | The conglutination of the blood cells formed a clot. 血细胞的粘合形成了血块。| synonyms: adhesion, bonding, cementation, cohesion, fusion | antonyms: separation, detachment, division | noun

conglutin:/kənˈɡluːtɪn/ | n. |  植物胶；麦谷蛋白 | A protein found in almonds, various seeds, and especially abundant in lupin seeds. |  Conglutin from lupin seeds is being researched for its potential health benefits.  羽扇豆种子中的麦谷蛋白因其潜在的健康益处正在被研究。| synonyms: lupin protein, seed protein | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *conglūtīnātus*, past participle of *conglūtīnāre* (“to glue together”), from *con-* (“together”) + *glūtināre* (“to glue”), from *gluten* (“glue”).
