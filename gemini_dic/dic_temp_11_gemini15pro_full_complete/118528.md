mesogaster:/ˈmɛsəˌɡæstər/ | n. | 中肠 |The middle part of the embryonic digestive tract, from which most of the small intestine and part of the large intestine develop. |The mesogaster gives rise to much of the digestive system. 中肠会发育成为大部分的消化系统。| The dorsal mesogaster suspends the stomach from the dorsal body wall. 背侧中肠将胃悬挂在背侧体壁上。|  midgut |foregut, hindgut| noun
