midgard:/ˈmɪdɡɑːrd/| n. |中庭，人间|In Norse mythology, Midgard is the realm of humans, located between Asgard (the realm of the gods) and Niflheim (the realm of the dead).  It is often depicted as a flat disc surrounded by a vast ocean. |The Vikings believed Midgard was connected to Asgard by the Bifrost bridge. 维京人相信中庭通过彩虹桥与阿斯加德相连。| Midgard is threatened by the serpent Jormungandr, who encircles the world.  尘世巨蟒耶梦加得环绕世界，威胁着中庭。| 近义词: Earth, world | 反义词: None (as a proper noun referring to a specific mythological realm)|词性: 名词
