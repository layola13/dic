intrusional: /ɪnˈtruːʒənəl/
adjective
侵入的，打扰的
Characterized by or relating to intrusion, especially unwelcome or disruptive intrusion.
The intrusional nature of the paparazzi made the celebrity uncomfortable.  狗仔队侵入性的行为让这位名人感到不舒服。
The constant notifications were intrusional and distracting. 不断的通知很烦人，让人分心。
Synonyms: invasive, intrusive, obtrusive, interfering, meddlesome
Antonyms: respectful, unobtrusive, discreet
词性: 形容词
