aesopic:/ˌiːsˈɒpɪk/ | adj. | 寓言的；说教的 |Of or relating to Aesop or his fables; conveying a moral or message through a story.  | Example 1: The aesopic nature of the story made it suitable for children.  这个故事的寓言性质使它适合儿童阅读。

Example 2:  His writing style was often described as aesopic, filled with hidden meanings.  他的写作风格常被描述为寓言式的，充满了隐含的意义。 | 近义词: allegorical, didactic, moralistic, fable-like | 反义词: literal, straightforward, unambiguous | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Aesop, the Greek fabulist, + -ic.  来源于希腊寓言作家伊索的名字，加上形容词后缀-ic。
