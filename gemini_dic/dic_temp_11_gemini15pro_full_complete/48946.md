defusing:/diːˈfjuːzɪŋ/
v.|拆除（炸弹等的）引信；缓和，平息|The expert carefully defused the bomb.  专家小心地拆除了炸弹的引信。|近义词：deactivating, neutralizing, mitigating, alleviating|反义词：activating, escalating, aggravating|词性: 动名词 (gerund) or present participle

defuse:/diːˈfjuːz/
v.|拆除（炸弹等的）引信；缓和，平息|The police negotiator successfully defused the hostage situation. 警方谈判专家成功地缓和了人质危机。|近义词：deactivate, neutralize, mitigate, alleviate|反义词：activate, escalate, aggravate|词性: 动词


Etymology: From de- + fuse (verb), from Old French fuser "to melt, liquefy," from Latin fundere "to pour, melt."  The original sense was to remove the fuse from an explosive device, thus preventing it from detonating. The figurative sense of "to reduce tension or danger" developed later.
