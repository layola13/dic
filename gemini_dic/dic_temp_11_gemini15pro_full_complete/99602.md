interjangle:/ˌɪntərˈdʒæŋɡl/ | v. |使纠缠，使混乱 | To mix up or entangle in a confused or complicated way. |  Example 1: The wires were so interjangled, I couldn't untangle them. 电线缠得太乱了，我解不开。

Example 2:  His thoughts were interjangled by the conflicting reports.  相互矛盾的报道使他的思绪混乱。 | 近义词: entangle, tangle, snarl, confuse, muddle | 反义词: untangle, disentangle, organize, order |词性:动词

interjangle:/ˌɪntərˈdʒæŋɡl/ | n. |混乱，纠缠 | A confused or tangled mass. | Example 1: The garden was an interjangle of weeds and overgrown bushes. 花园里杂草丛生，灌木丛生，一片混乱。

Example 2: The room was an interjangle of clothes and books. 房间里衣服和书乱七八糟。| 近义词: tangle, mess, jumble, confusion | 反义词: order, organization |词性:名词
