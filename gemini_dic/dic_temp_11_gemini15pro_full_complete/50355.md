depoliticized:/diːpəˈlɪtɪsaɪzd/
adjective
去政治化的，非政治化的
Removed from political influence or considerations.  The issue of healthcare should be depoliticized so that everyone can access necessary medical services.
The organization aimed to create a depoliticized space for dialogue.
近义词:neutral, non-political, apolitical
反义词:politicized
词性:adjective
