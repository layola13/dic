comes:/kʌmz/| v.|来，来到，出现，发生|Comes is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "come". It signifies movement towards the speaker or a specified location, or the arrival of a specific time or event. It can also indicate the origin or source of something, or the result of a process.|He comes to work early every day. 他每天上班都很早。|近义词：arrives, appears, originates|反义词:goes, departs, leaves|verb
