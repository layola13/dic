cyclotron:/ˈsaɪklətrɒn/ (US) /ˈsaɪklətrɒn/ (UK)| n.|回旋加速器|A type of particle accelerator in which charged particles spiral outwards from the centre as they are accelerated by a magnetic field and an alternating electric field.|The cyclotron is used to accelerate charged particles to high energies for research in nuclear physics.|Synonym: particle accelerator|Antonym: N/A|noun
