hexarch:/ˈhɛksɑːrk/| adj. n.|六首的，六执政官的|Having six chiefs or leaders; specifically (Botany) having six protoxylem strands in the central cylinder of a root.  A plant having such a structure.  Also, historically, one of six joint rulers.|The botanist identified the plant as hexarch due to its root structure.|近义词：heptarch (in a seven-part context)|反义词: monarch, diarch|adjective, noun
