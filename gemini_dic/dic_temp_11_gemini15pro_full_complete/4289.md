airworthiness:/ˈɛəwɜːrðɪnɪs/| n.|适航性；耐飞性|The state of an aircraft being fit and safe to fly.  An aircraft's airworthiness is determined by its adherence to strict safety standards and regulations.|The aircraft's airworthiness was confirmed before takeoff.  这架飞机的适航性在起飞前得到了确认。|Synonyms: fitness to fly, safety, suitability for flight|Antonyms: unsuitability for flight, unsafe condition|noun
