menial:/ˈmiːniəl/ | adj. n. | 卑微的，仆人的；佣人 | (of work) not requiring much skill and lacking prestige.  |  She found herself stuck in a menial job. 她发现自己被困在一个卑微的工作中。 |  Example: He wasn't afraid of menial work. 他不害怕干粗活。| Synonyms: unskilled, humble, low-status, subservient | Antonyms: skilled, prestigious, high-status | adjective

menial:/ˈmiːniəl/ | n. | 佣人，仆人 | A person who does menial work. |  She worked as a menial in the rich family's home. 她在富人家里做佣人。 | Example: The menials were expected to be unseen and unheard.  佣人们被期望是看不见也听不见的。 | Synonyms: servant, domestic, laborer | Antonyms: master, employer, boss | noun
