assailments:/əˈseɪlmənts/| n. |攻击，袭击，抨击|A series of violent attacks or criticisms.|The besieged city faced relentless assailments from the enemy.  被围困的城市面临着敌人无情的攻击。|The company has been subjected to repeated assailments in the press. 该公司在媒体上屡遭抨击。|近义词：attacks, onslaughts, criticisms, assaults|反义词：defenses, protections|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *asaillir "to leap upon, attack," from *as- "to, at" + *salire "to leap."
