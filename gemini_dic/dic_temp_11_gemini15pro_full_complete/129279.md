nodosity:/noʊˈdɑːsəti/ /nəʊˈdɒsəti/| n. |节，结节，瘤|A small rounded lump or swelling; a knot or node.  It can refer to a physical swelling or a figurative knot or complication in a situation. |The rope had several nodosities along its length.  绳子 sepanjangnya 有几个结节。

The nodosities in the plot made the story difficult to follow.  情节中的结节使故事难以理解。|synonyms: knot, node, lump, swelling, protuberance|antonyms: smoothness, evenness|noun
