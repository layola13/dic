beanlike:/ˈbiːnlaɪk/
adjective
像豆的；豆形的
Resembling a bean in shape or size.
The tiny, beanlike seeds are difficult to plant. 这些微小的、像豆子一样的种子很难种植。
The plant has small, beanlike pods. 这种植物有小的、豆状的荚。
synonyms: bean-shaped, fabiform, legume-like
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider using descriptions of dissimilar shapes, e.g., round, elongated, irregular)
词性: 形容词
