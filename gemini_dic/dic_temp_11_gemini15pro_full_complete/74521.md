fraternally:/frəˈtɜːrnəli/
adverb
兄弟般地；友爱地
in a brotherly manner; showing brotherly love
例句：They greeted each other fraternally. 他们兄弟般地互相问候。

例句：The letter ended fraternally, "Yours in brotherhood." 这封信以兄弟般的语气结尾，“你的兄弟”。

近义词：brotherly, amicably, warmly
反义词：unfriendly, coldly, hostilely
词性：adverb

fraternal:/frəˈtɜːrnl/
adjective
兄弟的；兄弟般的
of or like a brother or brothers
例句：They share a fraternal bond. 他们有着兄弟般的纽带。

例句：Fraternal organizations often engage in charitable work. 兄弟会组织经常从事慈善工作。

近义词：brotherly
反义词：unbrotherly
词性：adjective

词源：
From Late Latin *fraternalis, from Latin fraternus (“brotherly”), from frater (“brother”).
