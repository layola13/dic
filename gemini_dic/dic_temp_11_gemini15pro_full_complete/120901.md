miscalculators:/ˌmɪskælkjʊˈleɪtərz/ | n. | 错误计算者；算错的人 | People who miscalculate or make errors in calculations.  Those who make incorrect calculations. | The project's failure was partly due to the miscalculators who underestimated the costs.  这个项目的失败部分归咎于那些低估了成本的错误计算者。 |  The miscalculators caused a significant delay in the launch of the new product. 这些算错的人导致新产品发布的重大延误。 |近义词: blunderers, estimators (if their estimations are consistently wrong) |反义词: calculators, experts,  analysts | 词性: noun
