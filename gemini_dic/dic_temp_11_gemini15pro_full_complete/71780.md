flecked:/flekt/ | adj. | 斑点的，点缀的 | Marked with small spots or patches of color or light. | The horse had a flecked coat of brown and white.  这匹马的皮毛是棕色和白色的斑点。|  speckled, spotted, dotted, dappled | plain, solid, uniform | adjective


flecked:/flekt/ | v. |  点缀，使…有斑点 (This is less common than the adjective form) | To mark with small spots or patches of color or light. |  The sunlight flecked the forest floor. 阳光洒在森林的地面上，形成斑驳的光点。 | speckle, spot, dot, mottle | clear, cleanse | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flecken, frequentative of *flek, akin to Old Norse flekkr (“spot, stain”).
