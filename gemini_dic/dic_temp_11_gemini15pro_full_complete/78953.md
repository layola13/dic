girds:/ɡɜːrdz/
| v. |  束缚；准备；做准备；(用带子)捆扎 | prepares oneself for action | The knight girds himself for battle. 骑士准备战斗。 | prepare, brace, equip | relax, disrobe | verb

girds:/ɡɜːrdz/
| n. | 腰带；环绕物 | something that encircles or confines | The city walls were its girds against invaders. 城墙是它抵御入侵者的环绕物。 | belt, band, cincture | opening, gap | noun

Etymology:
From Old English *gyrdan, from Proto-Germanic *gurdjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gher-  "to grasp, enclose".
