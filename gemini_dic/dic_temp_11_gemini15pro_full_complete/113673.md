词源:  Mace (the weapon) comes from Old French *mace, from Late Latin *mattea, perhaps of Germanic origin. Macing (the act of using pepper spray) derives from the use of "Mace" as a brand name for a type of tear gas and pepper spray, which became a genericized trademark.
