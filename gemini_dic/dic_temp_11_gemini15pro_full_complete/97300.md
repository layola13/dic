infoliate: /ɪnˈfoʊliˌeɪt/
verb
使生叶；用叶子装饰；长出叶子
to cover with leaves; to decorate with leaves; to put forth leaves.
The trees began to infoliate in the spring. 春天，树木开始长出叶子。
The artist infoliated the sculpture with gold leaf. 艺术家用金箔装饰了雕塑。
The vines infoliated the trellis quickly. 藤蔓迅速地覆盖了格子架。
近义词: leaf, foliate, decorate
反义词: defoliate
词性: verb
