catgut:/ˈkætɡʌt/| n. |羊肠线|A strong cord made from the intestines of sheep or other animals, formerly used for surgical sutures and strings of musical instruments.|The surgeon used catgut to stitch the wound.  外科医生用羊肠线缝合伤口。| The violin strings were traditionally made of catgut.  小提琴弦传统上是用羊肠线制成的。| 近义词： suture, string, cord | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | 名词
