dissonance:/ˈdɪsənəns/ (US), /ˈdɪsənəns/ (UK)| n.|不和谐，不一致，冲突|A lack of harmony or agreement between things, especially opinions or beliefs.|The dissonance between their beliefs and their actions was striking. 他们的信仰和行动之间的不一致令人震惊。|近义词：discord, disagreement, conflict, clash, friction|反义词：harmony, agreement, concord, consonance|词性:名词
dissonant:/ˈdɪsənənt/ (US), /ˈdɪsənənt/ (UK)| adj.|不和谐的，不一致的|Lacking harmony or agreement.|The dissonant chords created a sense of unease. 不和谐的和弦造成了一种不安的感觉。|近义词：discordant, inharmonious, conflicting, clashing|反义词：harmonious, consonant, agreeable|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dissonantia, from Latin dissonans, present participle of dissonare "to sound harshly," from dis- "apart" + sonare "to sound."
