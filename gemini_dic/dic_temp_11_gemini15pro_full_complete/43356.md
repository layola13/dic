crane:/kreɪn/
n.|起重机，鹤|A tall metal structure with a long arm, used for lifting and moving heavy objects.  Also, a large, long-legged, long-necked bird.|例句：The crane lifted the heavy steel beams into place. 起重机将沉重的钢梁吊到位。

crane:/kreɪn/
n.|鹤|A large, long-legged, long-necked bird.|例句：We saw a flock of cranes flying overhead. 我们看到一群鹤从头顶飞过。


crane:/kreɪn/
v.|伸长脖子看|To stretch one's neck in order to see something.|例句：She craned her neck to see over the crowd. 她伸长脖子想看清人群那边的情况。

crane:/kreɪn/
v.|（用起重机）吊|To lift or move something with a crane.|例句：They craned the piano up to the second floor. 他们用起重机把钢琴吊到二楼。


近义词: (n.) hoist, derrick, (bird) heron, egret, (v.) stretch
反义词: (n.) none, (v.) duck, crouch
词性: noun, verb
词源: From Old English *cran, from Proto-Germanic *kranuh, of imitative origin.
