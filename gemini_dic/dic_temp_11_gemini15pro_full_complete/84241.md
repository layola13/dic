half-sighted:/ˌhɑːf ˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半盲的；目光短浅的 | Having poor eyesight; lacking foresight or intellectual insight. |  He's half-sighted and can't drive. 他半盲，不能开车。

She made a half-sighted attempt to clean the house. 她敷衍了事地打扫了一下房子。 | 近义词: short-sighted, nearsighted, myopic, visually impaired | 反义词: far-sighted, prescient, insightful | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "half" + "sighted". "Half" meaning partially and "sighted" referring to the ability to see.  The combination literally means having partial sight.
