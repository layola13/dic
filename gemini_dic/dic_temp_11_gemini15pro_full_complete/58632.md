dumbass:/ˈdʌmæs/ (US), /ˈdʌmɑːs/ (UK)| n.|蠢货，笨蛋|A stupid or foolish person.|例句：You dumbass, you locked your keys in the car again! 你这个蠢货，你又把钥匙锁车里了！

例句：Don't be such a dumbass; think before you act. 别这么蠢，行动之前先想想。|近义词：idiot, moron, imbecile, fool, dolt|反义词: genius, intellectual, smart person|词性: noun
