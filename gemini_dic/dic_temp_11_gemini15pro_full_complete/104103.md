jump-the-gun:/ˌdʒʌmp ðə ˈɡʌn/ | v. |抢跑；过早行动；操之过急|To do something before the proper time; to act prematurely.| The company jumped the gun by announcing the product before it was ready.  这家公司过早地宣布了产品，而产品还没有准备好。|Synonyms: act prematurely, rush into, jump the queue/line|Antonyms: wait, delay, procrastinate|verb

jump-the-gun:/ˌdʒʌmp ðə ˈɡʌn/ | idiom |抢跑；过早行动；操之过急|To do something before the proper time; to act prematurely.|  He jumped the gun and proposed before he really knew her. 他操之过急，在她还没真正了解她之前就求婚了。|Synonyms: act hastily, be precipitate, get ahead of oneself|Antonyms: wait and see, bide one's time, hold back|idiom
