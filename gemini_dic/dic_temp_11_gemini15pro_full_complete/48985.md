deghosting:/diːˈɡoʊstɪŋ/
n.|去鬼影，解除鬼影状态|The act of reappearing or re-engaging with someone after a period of ghosting (ignoring their attempts at communication).|例句：After months of silence, she tried deghosting him by sending a text.  几个月不沉默之后，她尝试给他发短信以解除鬼影状态。

例句：Deghosting is becoming more common as people realize the impact of ghosting on others. 随着人们意识到鬼影对其他人的影响，去鬼影变得越来越普遍。
|近义词：reconnecting, re-engaging|反义词：ghosting|词性：名词
