fiduciarily:/ˌfɪduːˈʃɪərəli/
adverb
受托地，信托地
In a fiduciary capacity; as a trustee.  Acting or done in good faith and in a manner that is expected of a person who has a legal relationship of trust with another.
The lawyer invested the money fiduciarily for his client.  这位律师以信托的方式为他的客户投资了这笔钱。
Synonyms: trustworthily, responsibly, ethically
Antonyms: dishonestly, irresponsibly, unethically
词性: 副词
