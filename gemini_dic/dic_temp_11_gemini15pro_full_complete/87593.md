heterostyled:/ˌhɛtərəˈstaɪld/ |adj.|异型花柱的；不同花柱型的|Having styles of different lengths in the flowers of different plants of the same species, leading to cross-pollination.|Primroses are heterostyled, with some plants having long styles and others having short styles.|近义词:dimorphic, trimorphic|反义词:homostyled|词性:形容词
