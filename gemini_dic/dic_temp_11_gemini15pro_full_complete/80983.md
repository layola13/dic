gradable:/ˈɡreɪdəbl/| adj.|可分级的，可评级的|Able to be graded or assessed on a scale.  It refers to adjectives that can have different degrees of intensity, often modified by words like "very," "quite," "rather," "extremely," etc.|The adjective "happy" is gradable.  "Happy"这个形容词是可分级的。|近义词：scalable, rankable|反义词：ungradable, absolute|词性:adjective
