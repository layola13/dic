endogeneity:/ˌɛndəʊdʒəˈnɪsɪti/ |n.| 内生性 |The quality or state of being endogenic; endogenousness.  A variable is said to be endogenic when it is correlated with the error term.|The researchers needed to account for the endogeneity of some variables in their model. 研究人员需要考虑模型中一些变量的内生性。| 近义词: endogenousness, internal causation | 反义词: exogeneity | 词性: noun
