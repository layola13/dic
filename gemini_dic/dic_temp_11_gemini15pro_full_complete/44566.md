crotonylene:/ˈkroʊtəˌniːliːn/| n. |巴豆二烯; 2-丁炔|A colorless gas, HC≡CCH=CH₂, used in organic synthesis. It is also known as 2-butyne.|Crotonylene is a highly reactive compound. 巴豆二烯是一种高活性化合物。|Crotonylene is used in the production of certain synthetic rubbers. 巴豆二烯用于生产某些合成橡胶。|Synonyms: 2-butyne, dimethylacetylene|Antonyms: None|Noun
