half-conservative:/ˌhæf kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/ | adj. | 半保留的 | Relating to or denoting replication of a nucleic acid in which one complete strand of each double helix is directly derived from the parent molecule. | DNA replication is a half-conservative process. DNA复制是一个半保留的过程。|  The half-conservative nature of DNA replication was confirmed by the Meselson-Stahl experiment.  DNA复制的半保留性质被Meselson-Stahl实验所证实。 |semi-conservative| non-conservative | adjective
