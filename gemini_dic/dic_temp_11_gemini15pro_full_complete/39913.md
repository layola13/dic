constrainable:/kənˈstreɪnəbl/| adj.|可约束的，可限制的|Able to be constrained or limited.  Something that can be controlled or restricted.|The budget is constrainable, meaning we can adjust it if necessary.  预算可调整，这意味着如果必要，我们可以调整它。|Synonyms: controllable, restrainable, limitable, restrictable|Antonyms: uncontrollable, unrestrainable, unlimited, unrestricted|adjective
