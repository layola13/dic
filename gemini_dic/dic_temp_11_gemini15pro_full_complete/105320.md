kibosh:/ˈkaɪbɒʃ/ (UK) /ˈkaɪbɑːʃ/ (US)| n. vt.|禁止，阻止；压制；终止|A decisive end to something; a stop put to something.|The principal put the kibosh on the students' plan to have a party during school hours. 校长阻止了学生们在上课时间举办派对的计划。|Synonyms: veto, stop, halt, block, nix, squelch, thwart|Antonyms: permit, allow, authorize, sanction, enable|noun, verb
