flammable:/ˈflæməbl/ | adj. | 易燃的；可燃的 | Easily set on fire; readily burning. | Gasoline is a flammable liquid. 汽油是易燃液体。

The curtains are made of flammable material. 窗帘是由易燃材料制成的。

近义词:inflammable, combustible | 反义词:nonflammable, fire-resistant | 词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *flammabilis, from Latin flamma (“flame”) + -bilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning “able to be,” “able to do,” or “worthy of being”).  Equivalent to flame +‎ -able.
