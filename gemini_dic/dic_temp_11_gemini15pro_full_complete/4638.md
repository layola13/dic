aldon:/ˈɔːldən/| n.|奥尔登（姓氏，地名）|A surname and a place name.  It is derived from the Old English personal name "Ealdwine," meaning "old friend."|Aldon is a small village in Essex, England. 奥尔登是英格兰埃塞克斯郡的一个小村庄。|Synonym: (for surname) family name, last name|Antonym: (None for a proper noun)|proper noun
