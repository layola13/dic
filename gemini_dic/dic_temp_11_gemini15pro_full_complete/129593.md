non-communicant: 未领圣餐者;不交流者;不合群的人
non-communicant:  A person who does not receive Holy Communion or who does not communicate with others.  Referring to someone who doesn't share information or participate in social interaction.
non-communicant:  The elderly woman became increasingly non-communicant after her husband's death.  这位老妇人在丈夫去世后变得越来越不合群。
non-communicant: He was a non-communicant member of the church, attending services but never taking communion. 他是不领圣餐的教会成员，参加礼拜但从不领圣餐。
non-communicant:  reclusive, withdrawn, uncommunicative, antisocial
non-communicant:  communicative, sociable, gregarious
non-communicant: noun, adjective
