examine:/ɪɡˈzæmɪn/ /ɪkˈzæmɪn/| vt.|检查，审查，仔细观察| To inspect or scrutinize carefully. |例句：The doctor examined the patient carefully. 医生仔细检查了病人。|近义词：inspect, scrutinize, investigate, analyze|反义词:ignore, overlook|verb
examine:/ɪɡˈzæmɪn/ /ɪkˈzæmɪn/| n.|检查，审查|An act of inspecting or scrutinizing. (This noun form is less common.)|例句：The evidence is under examine.  证据正在审查中。|近义词：inspection, scrutiny, investigation, analysis|反义词:neglect|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English examinen, from Old French examiner, from Latin examinare (“to weigh, test, examine”), from examen (“tongue of a balance, examination”), from exigere (“to weigh, drive out, ascertain, demand, complete”).
