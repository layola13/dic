nonmaterialistic:/ˌnɒnməˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非物质主义的；不重物质的 | Not focused on material possessions or wealth; valuing spiritual or intellectual pursuits over material gain. |  She lives a nonmaterialistic lifestyle, prioritizing experiences over possessions. 她过着非物质主义的生活方式，重视体验而非物质财富。

She's a nonmaterialistic person, more interested in making a difference than making money. 她是一个不物质的人，更感兴趣的是做出改变而不是赚钱。 | 近义词: spiritual, ascetic, simple, unworldly | 反义词: materialistic, consumerist, greedy | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + materialistic (adjective meaning "preoccupied with material possessions").  The root word "material" comes from the Latin "materia" meaning "matter" or "timber."
