overcultivation: /ˌoʊvərˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/
noun
过度耕作；过度种植
Excessive cultivation of land, leading to depletion of soil nutrients and reduced fertility.
The overcultivation of the land led to a decline in crop yields.  过度耕作导致农作物产量下降。
Overcultivation can cause soil erosion. 过度耕作会导致水土流失。
近义词: overfarming, overuse
反义词: undercultivation, fallow
