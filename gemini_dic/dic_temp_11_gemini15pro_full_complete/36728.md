collodionize:/kəˈloʊdiəˌnaɪz/
verb
火棉胶处理，用火棉胶涂抹
to treat or coat with collodion.
The photographer collodionized the glass plate before applying the light-sensitive emulsion. 摄影师在涂抹光敏乳剂之前先用火棉胶处理了玻璃板。
The wound was collodionized to protect it from infection.  伤口用火棉胶处理以防止感染。
近义词: coat, cover
反义词: uncover, expose
词性: verb

词源: From collodion + -ize.  Collodion itself comes from Greek kollōdēs, meaning "glue-like."
