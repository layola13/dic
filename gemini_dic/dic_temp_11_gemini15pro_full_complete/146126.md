overemphasized: /ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəˌsaɪzd/ | adj. | 过分强调的，过分着重的 | Having given something too much importance or emphasis. | The media overemphasized the importance of the election. 媒体过分强调了选举的重要性。|  The dangers of smoking have been overemphasized by some health officials. 一些卫生官员过分强调了吸烟的危害。|近义词: overstressed, exaggerated, overstated | 反义词: underemphasized, understated, downplayed | 词性: adjective

词源: over- (prefix meaning "too much") + emphasize (verb) + -ed (past participle suffix)
