airlike:/ˈɛərˌlaɪk/| adj.|像空气的;轻飘飘的;空灵的|Resembling air; ethereal; delicate and unsubstantial.|The music had an airlike quality, light and ethereal.|近义词：airy, ethereal, delicate, gossamer, insubstantial|反义词:heavy, dense, substantial|词性:形容词

Etymology: air + like.  "Air" comes from Old French "air" from Latin "aer" meaning air. "Like" comes from Old English "lic" meaning having the appearance or form of.
