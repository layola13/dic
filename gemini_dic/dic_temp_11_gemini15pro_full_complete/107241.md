lairiest:/ˈleəriɪst/ | adj. | 最像巢穴的；最隐蔽的 |  Having the nature of or resembling a lair; most secluded or hidden. | The lairiest part of the forest was deep within the tangled undergrowth, where even sunlight struggled to penetrate. 森林中最隐蔽的地方是纠结的灌木丛深处，即使是阳光也很难穿透。 | The fox sought out the lairiest spot in the rocks to give birth to her cubs. 狐狸在岩石中寻找最隐蔽的地方生下幼崽。 | 近义词：hidden, secluded, concealed, secret | 反义词：exposed, open, public | 词性: 形容词

词源: From lair + -iest (superlative suffix).  From Old English *lær,  meaning "a lying down place," related to lie.
