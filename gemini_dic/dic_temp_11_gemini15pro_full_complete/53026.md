diocesan:/daɪˈɒsɪzən/| adj. n.|主教管区的；主教管区居民|Relating to a diocese; a person who lives in or is a member of a diocese. |The diocesan synod meets annually.  主教管区会议每年召开一次。| The new diocesan was welcomed warmly. 新的主教管区居民受到了热烈的欢迎。 |近义词：episcopal, ecclesiastical|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective, noun


diocesener:/daɪˈɒsɪzənər/| n.|主教管区居民|A person who lives in or is a member of a diocese.  This term is less common than "diocesan" when referring to a person. |He is a diocesener of the Chicago diocese. 他是芝加哥教区的主教管区居民。|The dioceseners gathered for the annual festival. 主教管区居民聚集在一起参加年度节日。|近义词：diocesan, parishioner|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dioecesanus, from dioecesis (“diocese”), from Greek διοίκησις (dioíkēsis, “administration, province”), from διοικέω (dioikéō, “I administer”).
