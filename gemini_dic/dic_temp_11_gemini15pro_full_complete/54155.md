disentail:/ˌdɪsɪnˈteɪl/ | vt. | 使不必要，使不必然；使无效 | To deprive of an inheritance; to prevent (a legal right, especially to land) from passing to a particular heir. |  The new law disentailed the property, allowing it to be sold. 新法律使该财产不再受继承限制，允许其出售。

The judge's ruling disentailed the original agreement. 法官的裁决使原始协议失效。 | 近义词：invalidate, annul, nullify, void, cancel | 反义词： entail, necessitate, require | 词性：动词
