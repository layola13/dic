overnoble:/ˌoʊvərˈnoʊbəl/
adjective
过分高贵的，矫揉造作的
Making something seem too noble or refined, often to the point of being unnatural or affected.
He told an overnoble story of sacrifice and bravery, which sounded quite unbelievable. 他讲述了一个关于牺牲和勇敢的过分高贵的故事，听起来很不真实。
近义词：pretentious, affected, grandiose
反义词：humble, modest, simple
词性: adjective

overnoble:/ˌoʊvərˈnoʊbəl/
verb
过分抬高，过分美化
To elevate or idealize something excessively, often to an unrealistic degree.
The biographer overnobled his subject, glossing over his flaws and exaggerating his virtues.  传记作者过分美化了他的主人公，掩盖了他的缺点，夸大了他的优点。
近义词：exalt, glorify, idealize
反义词：debase, degrade, diminish
词性: verb
