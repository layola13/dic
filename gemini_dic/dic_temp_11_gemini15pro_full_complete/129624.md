non-consumption:/ˌnɒn kənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 不消费，非消费 |  The act of not consuming goods or services.  It can be a conscious choice, such as in minimalist lifestyles, or a result of circumstances like poverty or lack of access. |  The growing trend of non-consumption is a reaction against consumerism. 不消费的增长趋势是对消费主义的一种反抗。

Non-consumption of certain foods can be due to allergies.  某些食物的不消费可能是由于过敏。 | 近义词: abstention, avoidance, boycotting | 反义词: consumption, use, spending | 词性: noun
