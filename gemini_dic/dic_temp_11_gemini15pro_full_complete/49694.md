demo:/ˈdɛmoʊ/ (US) /ˈdɛməʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|演示，示范；样本唱片；示威游行|A demonstration or display, especially of a new product or technique. A sample recording intended to promote an artist or band.  A public meeting or march protesting against something or expressing views on a political issue.|
例句：The software company offered a free demo of their new product.  这家软件公司提供了他们新产品的免费演示。
例句：The band sent a demo to several record labels.  这支乐队向几家唱片公司寄送了样本唱片。
例句：There was a large demo against the proposed cuts in education funding. 有一场反对削减教育经费提案的大型示威游行。
近义词：demonstration, presentation, trial, sample, protest, march|反义词：concealment, suppression|词性:名词, 形容词
