decreet:/dɪˈkriːt/
n.
法令，判决
A formal order having the force of law.
The king issued a decreet banning the sale of alcohol. 国王颁布法令禁止销售酒精。
edict, order, decree, judgment, ruling
rescission, repeal, revocation
noun
