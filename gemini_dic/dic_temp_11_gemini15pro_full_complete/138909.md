nutraceutical: /ˌnuːtrəˈsuːtɪkəl/ | n. |营养保健品|A food or food product that supposedly provides health and medical benefits, especially the prevention and treatment of disease. | Example:  Many people take nutraceuticals like fish oil capsules hoping to improve their health. 很多人服用鱼油胶囊等营养保健品，希望改善健康状况。

Example: The company specializes in developing new nutraceuticals. 这家公司专门开发新的营养保健品。|Synonym:  functional food, dietary supplement, health supplement|Antonym: junk food, processed food|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"Nutraceutical" is a portmanteau of the words "nutrition" and "pharmaceutical". It was coined in 1989 by Stephen DeFelice, founder and chairman of the Foundation for Innovation in Medicine.
