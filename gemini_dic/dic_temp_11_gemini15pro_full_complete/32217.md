chicest:/ˈʃiːkɪst/
adjective
最时尚的，最优雅的
Most stylish and elegant.
She wore the chicest outfit at the party. 她在派对上穿了最时尚的服装。
近义词: most stylish, most fashionable, trendiest
反义词: dowdiest, frumpiest, least fashionable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French chic ("stylish") + -est (superlative suffix).  The word "chic" itself likely originated from the German word schick ("skill, ability, appropriate").
