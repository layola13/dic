administrate:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪt/
|v.|管理，治理|to manage or direct the affairs of a business, organization, or government|The government is responsible for administrating the country. 政府负责管理国家。|例句：The manager administrates a large team. 经理管理着一个大型团队。|近义词：manage, govern, direct, control|反义词：neglect, mismanage|verb

administrate:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪt/
|v.|执行，实施|to put (a law, plan, etc.) into effect|The new policy will be administrated next month. 新政策将于下个月实施。|例句：The nurse administrated the medication to the patient. 护士给病人服用了药物。|近义词：implement, execute, enforce, carry out|反义词：ignore, disregard|verb
