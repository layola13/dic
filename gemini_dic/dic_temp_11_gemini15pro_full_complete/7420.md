animism: /ˈænɪmɪzəm/ | n. |万物有灵论，泛灵论|The belief that natural objects, such as rivers and trees, and natural phenomena, like wind and thunder, have souls or spirits. |Many indigenous cultures practice a form of animism. 许多土著文化都实行某种形式的万物有灵论。|  The ancient Celts' beliefs were rooted in animism. 古代凯尔特人的信仰根植于万物有灵论。 | 近义词: pantheism, vitalism | 反义词: materialism, atheism | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *animismus*, from Latin anima (“breath, soul”).
