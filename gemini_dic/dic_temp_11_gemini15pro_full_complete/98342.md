insinuative:/ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 暗示的；含沙射影的；潜移默化的 | Suggesting or implying something bad or unpleasant in an indirect way. | Her insinuative remarks about his past made him uncomfortable. 她关于他过去的含沙射影的话让他很不舒服。 | He gave an insinuative smile. 他露出了一个意味深长的微笑。 | 近义词：suggestive, allusive, implying, hinting | 反义词：direct, explicit, forthright | 词性: adjective
