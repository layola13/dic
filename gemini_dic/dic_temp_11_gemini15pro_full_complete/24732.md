bronzite:/ˈbrɒnzaɪt/ | n. | 古銅輝石，紫蘇輝石 | A common ferromagnesian silicate mineral, (Mg,Fe)₂Si₂O₆, belonging to the pyroxene group and occurring in igneous and metamorphic rocks. It has a brown or greenish-brown color and a vitreous to pearly luster. | The geologist identified the rock as containing bronzite. 地质学家鉴定这块岩石含有古铜辉石。 |  Synonyms: enstatite, hypersthene | Antonyms: N/A | noun
