justed: /ˈdʒʌstɪd/ | adj. | 调整过的，校正过的 |  Past participle of "just," meaning to adjust or align something correctly. It implies that something has been altered to make it right, accurate, or suitable. | The carpenter justed the door so it wouldn't stick. 木匠调整了门，这样它就不会卡住了。| The telescope was justed for optimal viewing. 望远镜被调整到最佳观测状态。 | adjusted, aligned, corrected | misaligned, skewed | adjective
