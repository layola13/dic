bartizan: /ˈbɑːrtɪzən/ | n. | 城垛；悬楼；角楼 | A small overhanging turret projecting from a wall, usually at a corner, of a medieval building, used especially for defense. |  The castle's bartizan offered a commanding view of the surrounding countryside. 城堡的角楼可以俯瞰周围的乡村。

The bartizan was used by archers to defend the castle walls. 弓箭手利用角楼保卫城墙。 | 近义词: turret, battlement | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *bartizain, probably ultimately of Arabic origin.
