interelectronic:/ˌɪntərɪlɛkˈtrɒnɪk/ |adj.|电子间的|Relating to the interaction or force between electrons.|The interelectronic repulsion increases as the atoms get closer. 电子间的斥力随着原子彼此靠近而增加.|近义词: interatomic (though not a perfect synonym, it relates to interactions *between* atoms, which are influenced by interelectronic interactions).|反义词: None.|词性: adjective
