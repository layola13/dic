grandfatherly:/ˈɡrændˌfɑːðərli/| adj.|祖父般的；慈祥的|Kind and gentle, like a grandfather.  Relating to or characteristic of a grandfather.|例句：He gave me a grandfatherly smile. 他给了我一个慈祥的微笑。|例句：He has a grandfatherly manner. 他有一种祖父般的风度。|近义词：kind, gentle, paternal, avuncular, benevolent|反义词：harsh, severe, unkind|词性:形容词
词源: grand + father + -ly.  From "grandfather" + the adverbial suffix "-ly."
