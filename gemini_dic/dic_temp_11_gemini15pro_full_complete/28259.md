captioned:/ˈkæpʃənd/ | adj. | 配有字幕的；配有说明文字的 | Provided with a caption or captions; having a text describing or explaining the image or video. | The video was captioned for the hearing impaired.  这视频配有字幕，方便听障人士观看。|  subtitled, titled, described | uncaptioned | adjective

captioned:/ˈkæpʃənd/ | v. (past tense and past participle) | 为...加字幕；为...加说明文字 | To provide a caption or captions for; to add a descriptive or explanatory text to an image or video. |  She captioned the photo with a witty remark. 她给这张照片配了一条诙谐的文字说明。 | subtitle, title, describe |  -| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the noun "caption" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Caption" itself comes from the Latin "captio" (a taking, seizing), related to "capere" (to take, seize).  The meaning evolved from "heading" or "title" to include descriptive text accompanying an image.
