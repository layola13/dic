interclouding:/ˌɪntərˈklaʊdɪŋ/
n.
云间现象；部分蔽光
The occurrence of clouds at two or more levels.  Clouds at a higher level partially obscure clouds at a lower level.
The pilot noted some interclouding during the ascent. 飞行员在上升过程中注意到了一些云间现象。
The interclouding made it difficult to determine the altitude of the lower cloud layer. 云间现象使得难以确定较低云层的的高度。
near synonyms:  layered clouds, cloud layers, multi-level clouds
antonyms: clear sky, cloudless
noun
