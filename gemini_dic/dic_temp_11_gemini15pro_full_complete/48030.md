decistere:/ˈdɛsɪstiːr/| n. |十立方米|A unit of volume in the metric system equal to 10 cubic meters.  It is primarily used for measuring stacks of firewood.|The woodcutter stacked the logs into neat decistere piles.|近义词:stere (though a stere is one cubic meter)|反义词: N/A (Units of volume don't typically have antonyms)|noun
