bread-and-butter:/ˈbred ən ˈbʌtər/| adj. n.|必需的，基本的；谋生的；面包和黄油|Relating to basic needs or the means of making a living; also, literally, bread and butter.|例句：Teaching is my bread-and-butter job. 教书是我的谋生手段。
