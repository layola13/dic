considerablest:/kənˈsɪdərəblɪst/ | adj. | 相当大的，非常多的 |  Superlative form of considerable: most considerable.  It signifies a large amount, degree, or extent. While grammatically correct, "considerablest" is archaic and rarely used in modern English.  "Considerable" or "most considerable" are generally preferred. |  He inherited the considerablest fortune in the county. (Archaic) 他继承了该郡最大的财产。 |  substantial, significant, ample, large |  insignificant, small, meager, negligible | adjective
