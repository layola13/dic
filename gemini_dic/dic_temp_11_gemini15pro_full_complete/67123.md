exponentiation:/ˌɛkspoʊˌnɛnʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |求幂，乘方|The process of raising a number to a power. |Example: Exponentiation is a fundamental operation in mathematics. 求幂是数学中的基本运算。

Example:  The exponentiation of 2 to the power of 3 is 8.  2的3次方是8。|近义词: raising to a power, involution |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
