albescence:/ælˈbɛsəns/
noun
变白，发白
The process of becoming white or whitish.
The albescence of the wood made it look like driftwood.  木材的变白使它看起来像浮木。
近义词：whitening, bleaching
反义词：darkening, blackening
词性:n.

albescent:/ælˈbɛsənt/
adjective
变白的，发白的
Becoming white or whitish.
The albescent leaves of the plant indicated a lack of chlorophyll.  这种植物发白的叶子表明它缺乏叶绿素。
近义词：whitening, bleaching
反义词：darkening, blackening
词性:adj.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *albescere, from albus "white" + -escere (inceptive suffix).
