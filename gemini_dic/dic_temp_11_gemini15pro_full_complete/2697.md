adroitness:/əˈdrɔɪtnəs/
n.
熟练，灵巧
Skill and expertise in using the hands or body; cleverness.
Her adroitness with chopsticks impressed everyone at the table. 她熟练使用筷子的技巧给餐桌上每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
近义词: dexterity, skill, proficiency, expertise, deftness
反义词: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness
词性: 名词

adroit:/əˈdrɔɪt/
adj.
熟练的，灵巧的
Skillful and clever, especially in the use of hands or body.
He was an adroit negotiator, able to resolve complex issues with ease. 他是一位老练的谈判者，能够轻松解决复杂的问题。
近义词: skillful, dexterous, deft, adept, proficient
反义词: clumsy, awkward, inept
词性: 形容词
