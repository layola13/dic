batlet:/ˈbætlɪt/| n.|棒槌，洗衣板|A small bat for beating clothes while washing them, especially a paddle-shaped implement used to beat clothes against a flat stone by a stream or in a wash tub.|She used a batlet and a washing board to scrub the clothes clean. 她用棒槌和洗衣板把衣服洗干净了。|近义词：paddle, beater|反义词: None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *batellet, diminutive of batel "beater," from battre "to beat." Ultimately from Latin battuere, frequentative of batere.
