medians:/ˈmiːdiənz/
n.
中位数；中值；中线
The middle number in a sorted list of numbers.  If the list has an even number of members, the median is the average of the two middle numbers.
The median house price in the city is $500,000.  该市房屋价格中位数为50万美元。

The median income for families in this area is about $60,000.  该地区家庭收入中位数约为6万美元。


近义词:midpoint, center
反义词:extreme, edge
词性:noun
