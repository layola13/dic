company: /ˈkʌmpəni/ (US) /ˈkʌmpəni/ (UK)| n.|公司；陪伴；同伴；连队|A commercial business.|例句：He works for a software company. 他在一家软件公司工作。|例句：She started her own company five years ago. 她五年前创办了自己的公司。|近义词：corporation, firm, business, group, party|反义词:individual, solitude|词性:名词

company: /ˈkʌmpəni/ (US) /ˈkʌmpəni/ (UK)| n.|陪伴；同伴|The fact of being with a person or people, or the person or people you are with.|例句：I enjoy her company. 我喜欢和她在一起。|例句：I needed some company. 我需要有人陪伴。|近义词：companionship, fellowship, society|反义词:solitude, isolation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *compagnie* "group of soldiers, company, society," from Late Latin *compania* "a fellowship, society," from Latin *com* "with" + *panis* "bread".  Originally referring to a group of people who eat bread together.
