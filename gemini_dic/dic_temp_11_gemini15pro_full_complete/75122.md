frisk:/frɪsk/| v. n.|搜身，轻快地跳跃|To quickly and lightly pass your hands over someone's clothes to search for hidden weapons or other illegal things; to move about in a playful and energetic way|The police officer frisked the suspect. 警察搜查了嫌疑犯的身。|The lambs frisked in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：search, pat down, gambol, frolic|反义词:none|词性:动词，名词
词源:From Old French *frisquer "to dance, leap, gambol," of Germanic origin.
