hannibal:/ˈhænɪbəl/ | n. | 汉尼拔 | A Carthaginian general who crossed the Alps with elephants to attack Rome in the Second Punic War. | Hannibal is considered one of the greatest military commanders in history. 汉尼拔被认为是历史上最伟大的军事指挥官之一。| Hannibal led his army across the Alps, a feat of incredible logistical skill. 汉尼拔率领他的军队翻越阿尔卑斯山，这是一项令人难以置信的后勤技能壮举。|  Near synonyms: general, commander, strategist | Near antonyms: pacifist | noun
