nonresponse:/nɑːnˈrɪspɑːns/ /nɒnˈrɪspɒns/| n.|无回应，不答复|Lack of response or answer to a survey, questionnaire, or request.|The survey suffered from a high nonresponse rate, making the results unreliable. 这项调查的无回复率很高，导致结果不可靠。|Synonyms:  lack of response, silence, no reply|Antonyms: response, answer, reply|noun

nonresponse:/nɑːnˈrɪspɑːns/ /nɒnˈrɪspɒns/| n.|无反应，不作用|Lack of reaction or effect to a stimulus or treatment.|The patient showed nonresponse to the medication. 病人对药物没有反应。|Synonyms:  inertness, inactivity, unresponsiveness|Antonyms: response, reaction, responsiveness|noun
