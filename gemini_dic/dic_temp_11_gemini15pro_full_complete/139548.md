obtrusive: /əbˈtruːsɪv/ /əbˈtruːsɪv/ | adj. | 突兀的；唐突的；冒昧的 | Noticeable or prominent in an unwelcome or intrusive way. |  The security cameras were obvious but not obtrusive.  监控摄像头很显眼，但并不突兀。 | Synonyms: conspicuous, noticeable, prominent, intrusive, officious | Antonyms: inconspicuous, unobtrusive, subtle, discreet | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin obtrūsus, past participle of obtrūdere (“to thrust against, obtrude”), from ob- (“against, toward”) + trūdere (“to thrust, push”).
