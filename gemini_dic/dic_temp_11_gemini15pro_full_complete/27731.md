canier:/ˈkæniər/| adj.|更狡猾的，更精明的|more canny; more shrewd and astute.|He's become canier in his business dealings over the years. 这些年来，他在生意上变得更加精明了。|The canier fox outsmarted the hounds.  这只更狡猾的狐狸智胜了猎犬。|近义词：craftier, slyer, more cunning, more astute|反义词：naive, gullible, ingenuous|词性:形容词

词源:
From canny + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Middle English *cannie, perhaps from Scots *cannie "knowing, prudent," from can "know" (from Old English *cunnan) + -y (adjective suffix).
