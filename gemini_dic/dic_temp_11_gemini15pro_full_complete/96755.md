inenarrable:/ɪnˈɛnərəbl/
adjective
难以言表的；无法形容的
Too great, powerful, or beautiful to be described.
The sunset was an inenarrable spectacle of color and light. 日落是一场难以言表的色彩与光线的奇观。
Synonyms: indescribable, inexpressible, ineffable, unutterable
Antonyms: describable, expressible, utterable
词性: 形容词
