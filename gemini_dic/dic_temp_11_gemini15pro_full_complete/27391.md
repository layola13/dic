cameloid:/ˈkæməlɔɪd/| n. |骆驼科动物|A mammal of the Camelidae family, including camels, llamas, alpacas, vicuñas, and guanacos.  These animals are characterized by their long necks, humped backs (in some species), and padded feet.|Camels and llamas are both cameloids, adapted to harsh environments.  骆驼和美洲驼都是骆驼科动物，适应恶劣的环境。|The zoo has a diverse collection of cameloids.  动物园里收藏了各种各样的骆驼科动物。|Synonyms: camel family member|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)|noun
