overamplified: /ˌoʊvərˈæmplɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度放大的；过度增强的 |  Increased in amplitude or volume beyond a desirable or reasonable level.  This can apply to sound, but also metaphorically to other things like emotions or reactions. | The overamplified guitar solo made the song unbearable.  过度放大的吉他独奏使这首歌难以忍受。

The politician's overamplified rhetoric only served to polarize the debate.  这位政治家过度夸大的言辞只会加剧辩论的两极分化。 | 近义词: exaggerated, overblown, overstated | 反义词: understated, subdued, muffled | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "amplified" (past participle of "amplify," from Latin "amplificare," meaning "to enlarge, extend").
