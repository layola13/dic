arm-and-a-leg:/ˌɑːrm ənd ə ˈleɡ/
idiom
代价高昂；非常昂贵
Something that costs an arm and a leg is very expensive.
That car cost me an arm and a leg. 那辆车花了我一大笔钱。
近义词:expensive, costly, exorbitant, a fortune, a king's ransom
反义词:cheap, inexpensive, affordable
词性:idiom
