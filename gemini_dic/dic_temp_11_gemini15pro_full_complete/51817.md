dewdrops:/ˈdjuːdrɒps/ (US) /ˈdʒuːdrɒps/ (UK)| n.|露珠|Drops of dew formed on cool surfaces, especially plants, at night.|The dewdrops sparkled like diamonds in the morning sun. 早晨的阳光下，露珠像钻石般闪耀。|Synonyms: droplets, beads, pearls|Antonyms: dryness, aridity|Noun
