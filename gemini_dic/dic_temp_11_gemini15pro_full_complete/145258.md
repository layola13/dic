overboastfully: /ˌoʊvərˈboʊstfəli/
| adv. | 过分自夸地，夸大其词地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive boasting; arrogantly. | He overboastfully described his achievements, making everyone uncomfortable. 他夸大其词地描述了他的成就，让每个人都很不舒服。|  boastingly, vaingloriously, swaggeringly | modestly, humbly | adverb


Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "boast" (to brag) + "-fully" (adverbial suffix).  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "boast," and the suffix "-fully" transforms it into an adverb.
