godspeed:/ˈɡɒdspiːd/ /ˈɡɑːdspiːd/| n.|一路顺风，祝成功|An expression of good wishes to someone starting a journey or undertaking a venture.  It essentially means "May God speed you on your way."|Example 1:  He wished them godspeed as they embarked on their mission. 他在他们开始执行任务时祝他们一路顺风。

Example 2:  With a heartfelt godspeed, she sent her daughter off to college. 她衷心祝愿女儿上大学一路顺风。|近义词：good luck, best wishes, success, bon voyage|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
