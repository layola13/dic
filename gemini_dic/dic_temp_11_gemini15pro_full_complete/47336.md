de-shell:/diːˈʃɛl/
verb
去壳；剥壳
To remove the shell or outer covering from something, like a nut, seed, or crustacean.
The machine can de-shell peanuts efficiently.  这台机器可以高效地去壳花生。
Workers de-shelled the prawns before cooking them. 工人们在烹饪前将虾去壳。
近义词:shell, husk, peel, shuck
反义词:shell
词性:verb

de-shell:/diːˈʃɛl/
verb
(比喻) 摆脱束缚；走出…的阴影
To emerge from a restrictive environment or situation.
After years of living under his father's control, he finally de-shelled and started his own business. 在父亲的控制下生活多年后，他终于摆脱束缚，开始了自己的事业。
She de-shelled from her shyness and gave a confident presentation. 她克服了害羞，做了一个自信的演讲。
近义词:emerge, break free, escape
反义词:confine, restrict
词性:verb
