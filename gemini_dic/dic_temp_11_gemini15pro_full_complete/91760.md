hyperemotive:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊtɪv/ | adj. |过度情绪化的，感情用事的| Excessively emotional; readily agitated or moved.  Characterized by exaggerated emotional reactions. | She gave a hyperemotive speech about the plight of the refugees, moving many to tears. 她就难民的困境发表了一场过度情绪化的演讲，使许多人感动落泪。|  The child's hyperemotive response to the small cut suggested a deeper emotional issue.  孩子对小伤口的过度情绪化反应表明存在更深层次的情绪问题。| 近义词: oversensitive, emotional, excitable, dramatic | 反义词: calm, composed, impassive, stoic | 词性: adjective
