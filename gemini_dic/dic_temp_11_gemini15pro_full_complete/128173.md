networkers: /ˈnɛtwɜːrkərz/ (US) /ˈnɛtwɜːkəz/ (UK)
n.
建立人脉者，社交者
People who actively build and maintain a network of professional or social contacts.
She's one of the best networkers I know; she always seems to meet the right people. 她是我认识的最善于社交的人之一；她似乎总是能遇到合适的人。
He attended the conference primarily to connect with other networkers in his industry. 他参加会议主要是为了与他所在行业的其他人脉建立联系。
Synonyms: connectors, socializers, minglers
Antonyms: loners, recluses
Noun
