hi-res:/ˌhaɪ ˈrez/ | adj. | 高分辨率的 |  Short for "high resolution," referring to images, screens, or audio that have a high level of detail and clarity due to a large number of pixels or a high sampling rate. | This camera takes hi-res photos.  这台相机可以拍高分辨率的照片。 | synonyms: high-definition, high-quality | antonyms: low-res, low-resolution | adjective
hi-res:/ˌhaɪ ˈrez/ | n. | 高分辨率 |  Short for "high resolution," referring to the state or quality of having a high level of detail and clarity in images, screens, or audio. | The hi-res of the image allowed us to zoom in without losing detail.  图像的高分辨率让我们可以在不损失细节的情况下放大。| synonyms: high-definition | antonyms: low-res, low-resolution | noun
