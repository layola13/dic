headinesses:/ˈhɛdinɪsɪz/| n. |兴奋，晕眩，轻率|The state of being heady; intoxicating quality.  A feeling of dizziness or lightheadedness, often accompanied by euphoria. A tendency to act rashly or impulsively.|The headinesses of youth often lead to poor decisions. 年轻人的轻率常常导致糟糕的决定。|Synonyms: dizziness, lightheadedness, giddiness, recklessness, impulsiveness, rashness|Antonyms: levelheadedness, caution, prudence|noun
