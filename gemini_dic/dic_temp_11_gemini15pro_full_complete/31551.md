chartreuse: /ʃɑːrˈtruːz/ /ʃɑːrˈtuːs/ | n. adj. | 黄绿色;  查特酒 | A brilliant, yellowish-green color, similar to the color of the liqueur Chartreuse. Also, a strong, sweet, yellow or green liqueur made by Carthusian monks. |  The walls were painted a vibrant chartreuse. 墙壁被漆成了鲜艳的黄绿色。 She sipped a glass of Chartreuse after dinner. 晚饭后她啜饮了一杯查特酒。 | 近义词: lime green, acid green | 反义词: maroon, burgundy | 词性: 名词, 形容词

Etymology:
From French *chartreuse*, from the Grande Chartreuse, the head monastery of the Carthusian order near Grenoble, France, where the liqueur was first made.
