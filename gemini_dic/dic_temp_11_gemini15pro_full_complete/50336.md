deployability:/dɪˌplɔɪəˈbɪləti/| n.|可部署性，可配置性|The ease and speed with which something can be deployed, such as software or military troops.  指某事物（例如软件或军队）能够部署的容易程度和速度。|例句：The new software boasts improved deployability, allowing for faster implementation across the network.  这款新软件拥有改进的可部署性，允许在整个网络中更快地实施。

|例句：The deployability of the new system is a key factor in its success. 新系统的可部署性是其成功的关键因素。|近义词：implementability, usability, adaptability|反义词：inflexibility, rigidity|词性:noun
