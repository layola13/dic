falding: /ˈfɔːldɪŋ/ | n. | 折叠，对折 | The process of bending something, typically paper or cloth, so that one part of it lies on another. |  The precise folding of the protein is crucial to its function. 蛋白质的精确折叠对其功能至关重要。

Folding chairs are convenient for storage. 折叠椅便于存放。 | 近义词：bending, creasing, pleating | 反义词：unfolding, straightening | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old English *fealdan, from Proto-Germanic *falþaną, from Proto-Indo-European *pel- (to fold, bend).
