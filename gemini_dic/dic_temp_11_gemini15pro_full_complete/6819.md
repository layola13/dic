anarthropoda:/ˌænɑːrˈθrɒpədə/ | n. | 非节肢动物 | Animals that are not arthropods, lacking jointed appendages and an exoskeleton. |  Example: An earthworm is an anarthropoda. 蚯蚓是非节肢动物。 | Synonym: non-arthropod | Antonym: arthropod | noun
