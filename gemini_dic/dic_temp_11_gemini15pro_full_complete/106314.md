kobold:/ˈkəʊbɔːld/ (US), /ˈkɒblɒd/ (UK)| n.| 小妖精，妖怪，哥布林 |A mischievous, often malicious sprite in German folklore, resembling a small, ugly humanoid creature inhabiting mines or houses.  It is often depicted as causing trouble or doing menial work. |The miners whispered tales of kobolds haunting the deep tunnels. 矿工们低声说着关于小妖精在深邃的隧道里出没的故事。|Synonyms: goblin, imp, sprite, brownie, gnome|Antonyms: angel, fairy (sometimes)|noun
