banana:/bəˈnænə/ (US) /bəˈnɑːnə/ (UK)| n.|香蕉|A long curved fruit with a yellow skin that grows in bunches on a tropical plant.|例句：I eat a banana every morning for breakfast. 我每天早上早餐吃一根香蕉。

例句：The monkeys in the zoo love bananas. 动物园里的猴子喜欢香蕉。

近义词：plantain (cooking banana)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a fruit) |词性:noun


banana:/bəˈnænə/ (US) /bəˈnɑːnə/ (UK)| adj. (slang)|疯狂的，古怪的|Crazy, insane.|例句：He went completely banana when he found out he'd won the lottery.  他发现自己中了彩票后欣喜若狂。

例句：That new hairstyle is totally banana.  那个新发型真是太古怪了。


近义词：crazy, nuts, insane|反义词: sane, rational|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *banana*, from West African languages (probably Wolof).
