compulsions:/kəmˈpʌlʃənz/
noun
强制，强迫行为
An irresistible urge to behave in a certain way, especially against one's conscious wishes.
He felt compulsions to check the locks repeatedly. 他感到反复检查锁的冲动。
synonyms:obsessions, urges, drives, impulses, needs
antonyms:aversions, disinclinations
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin compulsio, from Latin compellere "to compel," from com- "together" + pellere "to drive."
