infrared:/ˌɪnfrəˈred/| adj. n.|红外线的；红外线|Electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths longer than visible light but shorter than radio waves.  It is experienced as heat.|例句：Infrared radiation is used in many electronic devices. 红外线辐射用于许多电子设备。|例句：The security cameras use infrared to see in the dark.  这些安全摄像头使用红外线在黑暗中观察。|近义词：thermal|反义词：ultraviolet|词性:形容词,名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From infra- (Latin, "below") + red. Coined in the 19th century.
