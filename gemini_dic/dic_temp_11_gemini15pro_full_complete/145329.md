overbrowse:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɑʊz/| v. |过度啃食，过度放牧|To graze (vegetation) so heavily that the plants are damaged or destroyed.|The deer have overbrowsed the shrubs in the park, leaving them stunted and bare. 鹿群过度啃食了公园里的灌木，使它们发育不良且光秃秃的。|近义词：overgraze|反义词: undergraze|词性:动词
