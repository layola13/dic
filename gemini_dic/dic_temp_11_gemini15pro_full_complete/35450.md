co-tenant:/ˈkoʊˌtɛnənt/| n.|共同租户，合租人|A co-tenant is a person who rents property jointly with another person or persons.|例句：My co-tenant and I share the rent and utilities. 我和我的合租人共同承担房租和水电费。

例句：The co-tenants had a disagreement about the use of the common areas. 合租人对公共区域的使用存在分歧。|近义词：joint tenant, roommate, housemate|反义词:landlord, lessor|词性:noun
