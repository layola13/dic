illustriously:/ɪˈlʌstriəsli/
adverb
辉煌地；杰出地；著名地
in a way that is very famous and admired and brings you respect
He served his country illustriously for over 30 years. 他辉煌地为国家服务了30多年。
Synonyms: famously, notably, prominently, remarkably, splendidly, brilliantly
Antonyms: obscurely, ingloriously, dishonorably
词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Latin illustris, from Latin illustrare "make bright, light up," figuratively "make clear, explain, distinguish, render famous," from in- "in" + lustrare "make bright," from lustrum "purification."  The meaning "make famous, renowned" is from c. 1600.
