desensitize:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/
|v.|使脱敏，使不再敏感|To make someone less sensitive to something.  To reduce or eliminate emotional responsiveness to something, especially something unpleasant or disturbing.|Exposure therapy can desensitize people to their phobias. 暴露疗法可以使人们对恐惧症脱敏。| accustom, habituate, inure|sensitize|verb
