leashes:/ˈliːʃɪz/
n.
皮带，束缚，控制
A strap or chain for restraining and guiding a dog or other animal.
The dogs strained at their leashes, eager to chase the squirrels. 狗拉着皮带，急于追逐松鼠。
leads, reins, harnesses
freedom, liberty
noun
