It can refer to the choice to remain unmarried, or the social and legal condition of not being married.|  Increasingly, young people are choosing nonmarriage. 越来越多的年轻人选择不结婚。|Synonyms: singlehood, celibacy (in some contexts), unmarried state |Antonyms: marriage, matrimony | Noun
