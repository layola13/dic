chbt: /ˈtʃætˌbɒt/ | n. | 聊天机器人 | A computer program designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the Internet. |  Example: I asked the chatbot for help with my order. 我向聊天机器人寻求订单帮助。

Example: The chatbot can answer frequently asked questions. 这个聊天机器人可以回答常见问题。

| Synonyms: bot, virtual assistant, conversational AI | Antonyms: human | noun
