opun:/ˈoʊpən/| adj.|开放的，营业的，公开的，坦率的，未决定的|Open refers to something not closed or shut; accessible, exposed, or unrestricted.  It can also describe something available for use or participation, or someone receptive to new ideas. |The shop is open until 9 pm.  这家商店营业到晚上9点。|Synonyms: accessible, available, public, uncovered, frank, receptive|Antonyms: closed, shut, private, concealed, secretive, resistant|adjective
