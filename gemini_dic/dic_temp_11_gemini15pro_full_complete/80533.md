goodish:/ˈɡʊdɪʃ/| adj.|相当好的，还不错的|Moderately good; fairly good.  It implies that something is good but not excellent.|例句：The food was goodish, but not great. 食物还不错，但不算太好。

例句：He's in goodish health for his age.  就他的年龄来说，他的健康状况相当不错。|近义词：decent, respectable, acceptable, fairly good, quite good|反义词：bad, poor, awful, terrible|词性:形容词

词源: good + -ish (suffix forming adjectives meaning "somewhat," "rather," "somewhat resembling")
