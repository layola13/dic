hydromica:/ˌhaɪdrəˈmaɪkə/ | n. | 水云母 |A group of clay minerals similar to illite, typically formed by the alteration of other micas.  They contain more water in their structure than true micas. |  The soil contained a high percentage of hydromica. 土壤中含有高比例的水云母。| Synonyms: illite, clay mineral | Antonyms: N/A | noun
