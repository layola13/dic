maqluba:/mɑːˈkluːbə/
n.|马库鲁巴（一种巴勒斯坦的菜肴）|Maqluba is a traditional Palestinian dish. 马库鲁巴是巴勒斯坦的传统菜肴。|Synonyms: upside-down dish, layered dish|Antonyms: N/A|noun
maqluba:/mɑːˈkluːbə/
n.|翻转的，颠倒的 (较为罕见，通常指菜肴)|The maqluba was perfectly cooked and flipped. 这道马库鲁巴煮得完美，翻转得也很好。|Synonyms: inverted, reversed|Antonyms: upright, right-side up|noun
