/hwɑːt/ | pron. | 什么 | Used to ask for information specifying something. | What are you doing? 你在做什么？ | Which, who |  | pronoun

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? 你最近学了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she wear to the party? 她会穿什么去参加聚会？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she finishes the course? 她完成课程时将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she gets her PhD? 到她获得博士学位时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
