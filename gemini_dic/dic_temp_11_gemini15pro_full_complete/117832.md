mellifluousness:/ˌmɛlɪˈfluːəsnəs/| n.|流畅甜美；悦耳|The mellifluousness of her voice captivated the audience. 她的声音甜美流畅，吸引了所有的观众。|例句：The mellifluousness of his prose is a pleasure to read. 他的散文流畅优美，读起来令人愉悦。

例句：The speaker's mellifluousness made the complex topic easier to understand. 演讲者流畅的表达使复杂的话题更容易理解。

|近义词：euphony, sweetness, smoothness, harmony|反义词:harshness, dissonance, cacophony|词性:名词
