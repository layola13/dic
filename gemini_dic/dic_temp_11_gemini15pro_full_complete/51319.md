detainingly:/dɪˈteɪnɪŋli/
adv.
拘留地，扣留地；延缓地
in a manner that detains or holds back.  Delaying or hindering someone's progress.
The officer looked at him detainingly.  警官拘留地看着他。
近义词：restrictively, hinderingly, obstructively
反义词：releasingly, freely, permissively
词性: 副词

词源:  From the verb "detain" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Detain" comes from Old French "detenir," from Latin "detinere," meaning "to hold back."
