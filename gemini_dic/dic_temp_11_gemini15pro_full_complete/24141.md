briary:/ˈbraɪəri/| adj.|荆棘丛生的，充满荆棘的|Overgrown with briars; thorny.  Covered in thorny bushes.|例句：The path was briary and difficult to traverse.  这条小路荆棘丛生，难以通行。

例句：The briary thicket provided shelter for small animals. 荆棘丛生的灌木丛为小动物提供了庇护所。|近义词：thorny, prickly, spiny|反义词:smooth, clear|词性:形容词
briary:/ˈbraɪəri/| n.|荆棘丛，灌木丛|A thicket of briars; a thorny patch.|例句：The children got lost in the briary. 孩子们在荆棘丛中迷路了。

例句：Birds nested deep within the briary. 鸟儿在荆棘丛深处筑巢。|近义词：thicket, brush, scrub|反义词:clearing, meadow|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English brere, from Old English brǣr (“briar”), from Proto-Germanic *brēzō (“thorn bush”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰars- (“thorn, bristle”). Cognate with Dutch braam (“bramble”), German Brombeere (“blackberry”).
