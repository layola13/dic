outloved: /ˌaʊtˈlʌvd/
verb
比...活得更长; 爱得更久
to live or love longer than someone or something else
例句：She outloved her husband by ten years. 她比她丈夫多活了十年。

例句：His love for his children outloved all his other passions. 他对孩子的爱超过了他所有其他的热情。
近义词：outlive, survive
反义词：predecease
词性：动词
