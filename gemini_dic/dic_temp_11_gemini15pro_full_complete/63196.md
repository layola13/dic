entasis:/ˈɛntəsɪs/| n.|柱身凸度，(柱身的)侧曲|A slight convex curve in the shaft of a column, introduced to correct the optical illusion of concavity that would otherwise occur.|The entasis of the columns gave the temple a sense of strength and elegance.  柱子的侧曲使神庙显得雄伟优雅。|Synonym: swelling, curve|Antonym: concavity, hollowness|noun
