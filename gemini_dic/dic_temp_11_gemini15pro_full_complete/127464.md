necessarian:/ˌnɛsəˈsɛəriən/
n.|必然论者；决定论者|A person who believes in necessitarianism.|He argued against the necessarian viewpoint, claiming free will exists. 他反对必然论的观点，声称自由意志是存在的。|近义词:determinist, fatalist|反义词:libertarian|词性:名词

Etymology: From "necessary" + "-arian" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who advocates or follows a particular doctrine or system).  "Necessary" comes from Old French "necessaire" and ultimately from Latin "necessarius" (essential, unavoidable), related to "necesse" (inevitable).
