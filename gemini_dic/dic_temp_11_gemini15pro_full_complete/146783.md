overidentify:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/| v. |过度认同|To identify excessively or inappropriately with someone or something, often to the point of losing objectivity.|He overidentified with the fictional character, blurring the lines between fantasy and reality. 他过度认同了那个虚构的角色，模糊了幻想与现实之间的界限。|近义词：empathize excessively, relate too strongly, project onto|反义词:detach, distance oneself|词性:动词

overidentification:/ˌoʊvər aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |过度认同|Excessive or inappropriate identification with someone or something.|Her overidentification with her patients led to burnout. 她对病人的过度认同导致了倦怠。|近义词：excessive empathy, over-relation|反义词:detachment, objectivity|词性:名词
