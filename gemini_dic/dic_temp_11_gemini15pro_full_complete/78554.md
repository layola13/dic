ghostwriter: /ˈɡoʊstˌraɪtər/ (US), /ˈɡəʊstˌraɪtər/ (UK)| n.|代笔作家，捉刀人|A person who writes for someone else, who then takes the credit for it. |例句：The celebrity's autobiography was actually written by a ghostwriter.  这位名人的自传实际上是由一位代笔作家写的。

例句：He hired a ghostwriter to help him finish his novel. 他雇了一名代笔作家来帮他完成小说。|近义词：co-writer, collaborator, amanuensis|反义词: author|词性: noun
