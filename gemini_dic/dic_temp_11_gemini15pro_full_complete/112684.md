lowland:/ˈloʊlənd/ (US), /ˈləʊlənd/ (UK)| n. adj.|低地；洼地|An area of land that is lower than the surrounding country.|例句：The lowlands are fertile and good for farming. 低地肥沃，适合耕种。

例句：They live in the lowlands of Scotland. 他们住在苏格兰低地。

近义词：valley, plain, flat, bottomland|反义词：highland, upland, mountain|词性：名词, 形容词
