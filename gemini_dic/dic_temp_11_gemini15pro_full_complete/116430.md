match-fixing: /ˈmætʃˌfɪksɪŋ/
noun
假球;操纵比赛结果;比赛造假
The dishonest arrangement of the result of a match before it is played.
Several players were arrested for match-fixing. 几名球员因操纵比赛结果被捕。
近义词: cheating, rigging, manipulation
反义词: fair play, sportsmanship
词性: noun


Etymology:
From "match" (a contest or game) + "fixing" (arranging something dishonestly or illegally).  "Fix" in this sense originates from the idea of "making something firm or definite," which evolved to mean "predetermining" and then "manipulating."
