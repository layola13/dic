interdetermine:/ˌɪntərdiˈtɜːrmɪn/ | v. | 相互决定，互相制约 | To determine or influence reciprocally; to affect or be affected by each other. | The economic and political systems interdetermine each other. 经济和政治制度相互决定。|  The development of language and culture interdetermine each other. 语言和文化的发展相互影响。 | 近义词: interact, influence, affect, shape | 反义词: isolate, separate | 词性: 动词

词源:  From inter- + determine.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "determine" means to fix or establish conclusively.
