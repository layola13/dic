overbrutalization:/ˌoʊvərˌbruːtələˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|过度残暴化；过度野蛮化|The overbrutalization of the penal system has led to increased violence within prisons. 监狱系统的过度残暴化导致监狱内暴力事件增多。|
|English Explanation: The act of making something excessively brutal or savage; the state of being excessively brutalized.|
|例句：The overbrutalization of war desensitizes soldiers to violence. 战争的过度残酷使士兵对暴力麻木。|
|例句：The film depicted the overbrutalization of indigenous populations. 这部电影描绘了土著居民被过度残暴化的景象。|
|近义词：excessive brutality, extreme savagery, hyper-violence|
|反义词：humanization, civilization, refinement|
|词性：noun|
