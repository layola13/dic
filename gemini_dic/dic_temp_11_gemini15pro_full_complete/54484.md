disinterment:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːrmənt/
n.
挖掘；发掘（尤指尸体）
The act of digging up something that has been buried, especially a corpse.
The disinterment of the body was ordered by the judge. 法官下令挖掘尸体。

近义词:exhumation, unearthing
反义词:burial, interment
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing reversal) + interment (burial).  Ultimately from Latin *in- (in, into) + terra (earth).
