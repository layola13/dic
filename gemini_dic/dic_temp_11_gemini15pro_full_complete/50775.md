descaler:/diːˈskeɪlər/| n.|除垢剂，除垢器|A substance or device used to remove scale (mineral deposits) from surfaces like kettles, pipes, and showerheads.|例句：Use a descaler to remove limescale buildup in your kettle. 用除垢剂去除水壶里的水垢。

例句：This descaler is specifically designed for coffee machines.  这款除垢剂是专门为咖啡机设计的。

近义词：cleaning agent, scale remover|反义词:|词性:noun

descaler:/diːˈskeɪlər/| v.|除垢，清除水垢|To remove scale from a surface.|例句：I need to descale my showerhead. 我需要清除淋浴喷头上的水垢。

例句：You should regularly descale your kettle to prolong its life. 你应该定期给水壶除垢以延长其使用寿命。


近义词：clean, remove scale|反义词:|词性:verb
