ariosophist:/ˌɛəriˈɒsəfɪst/
|n.|歌剧哲学家；歌剧理论家|A person who studies or writes about opera, often with a focus on its philosophical or theoretical aspects.  A scholar or theorist of opera.|The ariosophist published a lengthy treatise on the use of leitmotifs in Wagnerian opera.|近义词:opera scholar, musicologist, opera theorist|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Italian *arioso* (melodic, aria-like) + -sophist (from Greek *sophistēs*, meaning "wise man" or "expert").
