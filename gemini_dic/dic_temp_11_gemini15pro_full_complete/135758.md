nonprofessed:/ˌnɒnprəˈfɛst/
adjective
未公开表明的；未承认的；未宣誓的
Not openly declared or acknowledged; not professed.
He held nonprofessed sympathies for the rebel cause. 他对叛乱事业抱有不公开的同情。
近义词:undeclared, unavowed, tacit, implicit
反义词:professed, avowed, declared, explicit
词性:adjective

nonprofessed:/ˌnɒnprəˈfɛst/
adjective
未加入修道会的
Not having taken the vows of a religious order.
A nonprofessed member of the monastery.  一位未加入修道会的修道院成员。
近义词:lay, secular
反义词:professed, monastic
词性:adjective


词源:
From non- + professed, past participle of profess.  由non-（不，非）+ professed（profess的过去分词，意为公开宣称，承认）构成。
