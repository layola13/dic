almsgiving:/ˈɑːmzˌɡɪvɪŋ/
|n.|施舍，捐赠|The act of giving money or food to poor people.|例句：Almsgiving is a common practice in many religions.  在许多宗教中，施舍是一种常见的做法。

例句：Her almsgiving was generous and heartfelt. 她的施舍慷慨而真诚。
|近义词：charity, donation, philanthropy|反义词：stinginess, miserliness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old English ælmesgiefu, from ælmesse "alms" (from Late Latin eleemosyna, from Greek eleēmosynē "pity, alms") + giefu "gift".
