equatability:/ˌiːkwɒtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 平等性；可比性| The equatability of opportunities is a key element of a just society. 机会均等是公正社会的一个关键要素。| 例句：The equatability of access to healthcare is a major concern. 医疗保健机会的均等性是一个主要问题。 | 近义词：comparability, parity, equivalence | 反义词：disparity, inequality, difference | 词性: 名词


词源:  From "equatable" + "-ity".  "Equatable" comes from "equate" (from Latin "aequatus," past participle of "aequare" - to make equal) + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
