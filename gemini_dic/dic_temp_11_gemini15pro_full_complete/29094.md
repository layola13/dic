caryophyllin:/ˌkærioʊˈfɪlɪn/ | n. |石竹烯;丁香烯|A sesquiterpene lactone found in various plants, notably cloves and black pepper, with potential medicinal properties.|Black pepper contains significant amounts of caryophyllin.  黑胡椒含有大量的石竹烯。|Caryophyllin contributes to the aroma of cloves. 石竹烯构成了丁香的香味。|Synonyms: beta-caryophyllene (β-caryophyllene) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun
