limpidly:/ˈlɪmpɪdli/| adv.|清澈地，透明地|In a clear and transparent manner; easily understood.|The sunlight streamed limpidly through the crystal-clear water. 阳光清澈地流过水晶般清澈的水面。|Synonyms: clearly, transparently, lucidly, pellucidly|Antonyms: murkily, cloudily, obscurely, vaguely|词性:副词

Etymology:
From limpid + -ly.  Limpid itself comes from the Latin "limpidus," meaning "clear, liquid, transparent," related to "liquidus" (liquid).
