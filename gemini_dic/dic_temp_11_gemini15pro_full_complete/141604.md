opsiometer:/ˌɒpsiˈɒmɪtər/ | n. | 视力计，视距计 | An instrument for measuring the limits of distinct vision.  It is used to determine the farthest distance at which an object can be clearly seen. |  The optometrist used an opsiometer to measure the patient's visual range.| Synonyms: optometer | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
