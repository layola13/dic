interrelationship:/ˌɪntərɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp/ |n.|相互关系，相互联系|The interrelationship between the economy and the environment is complex. 经济与环境之间的相互关系是复杂的。|例句：The interrelationship between diet and health is well established. 饮食和健康之间的相互关系已得到充分证实。

例句：Understanding the interrelationship of all the system components is crucial. 理解所有系统组件之间的相互关系至关重要。


|近义词：interaction, interconnection, correlation, link, relationship|反义词：independence, isolation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + relationship.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Relationship" comes from the Old French "relacion"  meaning "connection." Ultimately derived from Latin "referre" meaning "to carry back."
