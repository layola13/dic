broadmouth:/ˈbrɔːdmaʊθ/ (US), /ˈbrɔːdmaʊθ/ (UK)| n. adj.|大嘴，阔嘴鱼；大嘴巴的人|A fish with a wide mouth, or a person who talks too much or indiscreetly.|The fisherman caught a broadmouth bass. 渔夫钓到了一条大嘴鲈鱼。|近义词：loudmouth, chatterbox, gossipmonger|反义词:reticent person, quiet person|词性:noun, adjective

broadmouth:/ˈbrɔːdmaʊθ/ (US), /ˈbrɔːdmaʊθ/ (UK)| adj.|大嘴巴的，口无遮拦的|Having a wide mouth, or being prone to talking too much or indiscreetly.|He's a broadmouth and can't keep a secret. 他是个大嘴巴，守不住秘密。|近义词：talkative, loquacious, garrulous|反义词:reserved, discreet|词性:adjective
