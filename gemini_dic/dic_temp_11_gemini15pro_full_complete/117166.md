meanwhile:/ˈmiːnwaɪl/| adv.|与此同时；在此期间|in the intervening period of time|The flight will take about three hours. Meanwhile, I'll read a book. 航班大约需要三个小时。与此同时，我要读一本书。|Synonyms: in the meantime, at the same time, concurrently|Antonyms: afterward, subsequently|adverb
