fulsomeness:/ˈfʊlsəmnəs/
noun
过分恭维；谄媚；油腻；令人作呕
excessive or insincere praise; the quality of being overly flattering or sickeningly sweet
His fulsomeness made her uncomfortable. 他的过分恭维让她感到不舒服。
近义词：effusiveness, adulation, flattery, unctuousness
反义词：sincerity, genuineness, reserve, restraint
词性:n.
