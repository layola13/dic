denouncing:/dɪˈnaʊnsɪŋ/
verb
公开谴责；痛斥
Publicly declare to be wrong or evil.
The government is denouncing the violence. 政府正在谴责暴力行为。
近义词: condemning, criticizing, censuring, lambasting
反义词: praising, commending, supporting, endorsing
词性: Verb (present participle)
