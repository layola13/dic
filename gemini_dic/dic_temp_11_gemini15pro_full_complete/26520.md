cabbalahs:/ˌkæbəˈlɑːz/
n.
卡巴拉，犹太神秘哲学，犹太神秘主义
esoteric Jewish mysticism; the system of Jewish theosophy and mysticism based on an interpretation of the Hebrew scriptures.
He was deeply interested in cabbalahs and other forms of mysticism. 他对卡巴拉和其他形式的神秘主义深感兴趣。
近义词: mysticism, esotericism, occultism
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑rationalism理性主义)
词性: 名词
