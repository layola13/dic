misorder:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrdər/ | vt. |使混乱，扰乱，使失调| to put into a state of confusion or lack of organization; to disrupt the normal or healthy functioning of| Stress can misorder your metabolism. 压力会扰乱你的新陈代谢。|Synonyms: disorganize, disrupt, disturb, unsettle | Antonyms: organize, arrange, order, regulate | verb
