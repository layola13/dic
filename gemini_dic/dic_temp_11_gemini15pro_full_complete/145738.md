overcriticizing: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度批评，吹毛求疵 | Criticizing someone or something too much or too harshly. | He's always overcriticizing my work, it's so demoralizing. 他总是过度批评我的工作，这太令人沮丧了。|  She's constantly overcriticizing her children's behavior. 她总是对她孩子的行为吹毛求疵。 | Synonyms: nitpicking, carping, fault-finding, caviling | Antonyms: praising, commending, lauding | Verb (present participle)
