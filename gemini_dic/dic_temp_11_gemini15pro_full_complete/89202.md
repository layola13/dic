homesicknesses:/ˈhoʊmsɪknɪsɪz/ /ˈhəʊmsɪknɪsɪz/
n.
思乡之情（复数）
A feeling of longing for one's home.
例句：He suffered from terrible homesicknesses during his first year at boarding school.  他在寄宿学校的第一年饱受思乡之苦。

例句：Her homesicknesses were so intense that she had to return home early. 她的思乡之情如此强烈，以至于不得不提前回家。

近义词：longing for home, nostalgia
反义词：(None -  a feeling doesn't have a true opposite, though one might consider contentment or comfort in a new place)
词性：noun
