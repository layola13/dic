europeanizations:/ˌjʊərəpiːənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|欧洲化（复数）|The process by which countries or regions become more integrated with Europe, adopting European norms, values, and practices.|例句：The europeanizations of Eastern European countries after the fall of the Soviet Union were rapid and transformative. 苏联解体后，东欧国家的欧洲化进程迅速且具有变革性。

例句：The study examined the different europeanizations across various sectors, including politics, economics, and culture. 这项研究考察了不同领域（包括政治、经济和文化）的欧洲化进程。|近义词：westernization, integration, globalization|反义词：isolation, localization|词性:noun
