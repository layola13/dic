disrobe:/dɪsˈroʊb/ | v. | 脱去衣服，使脱光衣服 | To remove one's clothes or the clothes of another person. | The king was disrobed before the ceremony. 国王在仪式前脱去了衣服。 | The nurse gently disrobed the patient. 护士轻轻地给病人脱了衣服。|近义词: undress, strip, unclothe |反义词: dress, clothe, robe |词性:动词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing reversal) + robe (garment).  Ultimately from Latin *disrobare, from dis- + roba "robe."
