defines:/dɪˈfaɪnz/ | v. | 定义，界定，规定 |  States or describes exactly the nature, scope, or meaning of something. |  The contract defines the terms of the agreement. 合同规定了协议的条款。 | synonyms: specifies, determines, outlines, describes | antonyms: confuses, obscures, misrepresents | verb

defines:/dɪˈfaɪnz/ | v. |  限定，明确 | Marks out the limits or boundaries of something. |  The fence defines the property line. 篱笆划定了房产线。 | synonyms: delimits, circumscribes, establishes | antonyms: expands, broadens, extends | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *definen*, from Old French *definir*, from Latin *dēfīnīre* (“to limit, bound, determine, explain”), from *dē* (“from, down from, away from”) + *fīnis* (“boundary, limit, end”).
