outbragging:/ˌaʊtˈbræɡɪŋ/
verb
互相吹嘘，比谁更爱吹牛
The act of boasting or bragging more excessively than another person.
They spent the whole evening outbragging each other about their accomplishments. 他们一晚上都在互相吹嘘自己的成就。
Synonyms: outboasting, vying, competing
Antonyms: modest, humble
词性: 动词 (gerund or present participle)
