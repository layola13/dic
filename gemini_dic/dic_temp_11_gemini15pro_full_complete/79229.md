glariness:/ˈɡlɛːrɪnɪs/
noun
刺眼; 显眼; 耀眼; 粗俗炫耀
The quality of being glaringly obvious or obtrusive.
The glariness of the stage lighting made it difficult to see the actors' faces. 舞台灯光的刺眼使演员的脸难以看清。
Synonyms: obviousness, conspicuousness, blatancy, prominence
Antonyms: subtlety, inconspicuousness, obscurity
