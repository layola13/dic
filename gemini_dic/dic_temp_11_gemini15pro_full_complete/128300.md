neuroses:/njʊˈroʊsiːz/ /njʊˈrəʊsiːz/
|n.|神经症，神经官能症|a relatively mild mental illness that is not caused by organic disease, involving symptoms of stress (depression, anxiety, obsessive behaviour, hypochondria) but not a radical loss of touch with reality.|She suffers from various neuroses. 她患有各种神经官能症。|近义词:mental disorder, emotional disorder, psychological disorder|反义词:mental health, emotional stability|noun
