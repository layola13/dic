diurna:/daɪˈɜːrnə/ /diˈɜːrnə/
adjective|日间的，白天的|Relating to or occurring in the daytime.  Active during the day.|Diurnal animals are active during the day. 白天活动的动物在白天很活跃。|Synonyms: daytime, daily|Antonyms: nocturnal, nightly|词性:形容词

diurna:/daɪˈɜːrnə/ /diˈɜːrnə/
noun|白天活动的动物|Animals that are active during the day.|Many desert animals are diurna to avoid the intense heat of the night. 很多沙漠动物是白天活动的，以避免夜间的酷热。|Synonyms:  daytime fauna|Antonyms: nocturnal animals|词性:名词
