overresponds:/ˌoʊvərɪˈspɑːndz/
verb
反应过度，过度回应
To react excessively or disproportionately to a stimulus or situation.
The financial markets often overrespond to news, both good and bad. 金融市场经常对消息反应过度，无论是好消息还是坏消息。
He tends to overrespond to criticism, taking it far too personally. 他倾向于对批评反应过度，认为这是针对个人的。
overreact, overdo
underreact, downplay
v.
