outthrobbing:/ˌaʊtˈθrɒbɪŋ/
verb
强烈跳动，剧烈搏动
pulsating or beating strongly, especially outwards
Her heart was outthrobbing with excitement as she opened the letter.  她打开信时，心脏因兴奋而剧烈跳动。
近义词: throbbing, pulsating, pounding
反义词: still, calm, quiet
词性: Verb (present participle)

outthrobbing:/ˌaʊtˈθrɒbɪŋ/
adjective
强烈跳动的，剧烈搏动的
Characterized by strong throbbing, pulsating, or beating, especially outwards.
The outthrobbing rhythm of the music filled the room. 强烈跳动的音乐节奏充满了整个房间。
近义词: throbbing, pulsating, pounding
反义词: still, calm, quiet
词性: Adjective (present participle being used as an adjective)


词源:  "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "throbbing" (present participle of "throb," from Old English "throbban," meaning to beat heavily or pulsate).
