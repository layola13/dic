nondramatic:/ˌnɒndrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非戏剧性的；平淡的；不夸张的 |  Lacking drama or emotional intensity; ordinary or uneventful. | The meeting was nondramatic, with everyone agreeing on the next steps. 会议平淡无奇，每个人都同意接下来的步骤。| The transfer of power was nondramatic. 权力交接平淡无奇。 | Synonyms: undramatic, low-key, uneventful, commonplace, ordinary | Antonyms: dramatic, exciting, sensational, theatrical | adjective
