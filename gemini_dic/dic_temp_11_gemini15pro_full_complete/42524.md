countermagnets:/ˈkaʊntərˌmæɡnɪts/
n.
反向磁体，抵消磁体
magnets with opposing polarities used to neutralize or reduce a magnetic field.
The device uses countermagnets to stabilize the levitation.  该设备使用反向磁体来稳定悬浮。
Countermagnets were installed to minimize interference from the Earth's magnetic field.  安装了反向磁体，以最大限度地减少地球磁场的干扰。
neutralizing magnets, opposing magnets
reinforcing magnets
noun
