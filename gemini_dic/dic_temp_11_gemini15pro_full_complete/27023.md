calderas:/kælˈdɛrəz/| n.|破火山口（复数）|A large, basin-shaped volcanic depression, more or less circular or cirquelike, the diameter of which is many times greater than that of the included vent(s).|例句：The Yellowstone caldera is a massive supervolcano. 黄石破火山口是一个巨大的超级火山。

|例句：Several dormant volcanoes have large calderas.  几个休眠火山拥有巨大的破火山口。|近义词：craters, volcanic depressions|反义词:|noun


caldera:/kælˈdɛrə/| n.|破火山口（单数）|A large, basin-shaped volcanic depression, more or less circular or cirquelike, the diameter of which is many times greater than that of the included vent(s).|例句：The caldera was formed by a massive eruption thousands of years ago.  这个破火山口是由几千年前的一次大规模喷发形成的。

|例句：The island has a caldera that is now filled with water. 这个岛有一个现在充满水的破火山口。|近义词：crater, volcanic depression|反义词:|noun
