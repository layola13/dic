electrotin:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈtɪn/ | n. | 电镀锡 | The process of coating a metal object with a thin layer of tin using electrolysis. | Example 1: The cans are made of steel with an electrotin coating. 这些罐子是用镀锡钢制成的。

Example 2: Electrotin is commonly used to protect steel from corrosion. 电镀锡通常用于保护钢材免受腐蚀。 | synonym: tin plating, tin coat | antonym: N/A | noun
