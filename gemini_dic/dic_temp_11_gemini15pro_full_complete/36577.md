collarless:/ˈkɒlərlɪs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlərlɪs/ (US)| adj.|无领的|Lacking a collar.  Used to describe clothing, particularly shirts or dresses.|例句：She wore a collarless shirt with a scarf. 她穿着一件无领衬衫搭配一条围巾。

例句：He prefers collarless jackets for a more casual look.  他更喜欢无领外套，看起来更休闲。

近义词：uncollared|反义词：collared|词性:形容词
