noun
单调；乏味；阴沉

冬季景色的单调让她渴望春天。
近义词:dullness, monotony, dreariness, bleakness
反义词:brightness, vibrancy, colorfulness
词性:n.

Etymology:
From drab + -ness.  Drab itself comes from the Old French *drape, meaning "cloth" or "clothing," particularly of a dull or brownish color.
