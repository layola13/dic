marshland:/ˈmɑːrʃlænd/ /ˈmɑːrʃlənd/| n.|沼泽地，湿地|An area of soft, wet land.  A tract of low wet land, often periodically inundated.|例句：The marshland is home to a variety of bird species. 这片沼泽地是各种鸟类的家园。

例句：The developers plan to drain the marshland and build houses. 开发商计划抽干沼泽地的水来建造房屋。|近义词：swamp, bog, fen, mire|反义词:dryland, upland|词性:noun
