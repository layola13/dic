chickweed:/ˈtʃɪkwiːd/| n. |繁缕；鸡草|A low-growing plant with small white flowers, often considered a weed, but also edible. It is a common food source for chickens and other birds, hence the name.|Chickweed is a common weed in my garden. 我的花园里长满了繁缕。|The chickens pecked at the chickweed. 小鸡啄食着繁缕。|近义词：Stellaria media (scientific name), weed, groundcover|反义词:|noun
