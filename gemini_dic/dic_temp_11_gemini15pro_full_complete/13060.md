astrography: /əˈstrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |  星位图学，天文制图学 | The study and practice of making maps or charts of the stars and other celestial bodies.  It involves plotting the positions and movements of celestial objects. | Astrography played a crucial role in early navigation. 星位图学在早期航海中发挥了至关重要的作用。

Astrography is essential for understanding the universe's structure. 星位图学对于理解宇宙的结构至关重要。 | 近义词: uranography, celestial cartography | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "astrology" as a contrasting field) | 词性: noun
