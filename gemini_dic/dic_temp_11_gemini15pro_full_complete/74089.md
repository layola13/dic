fosterable:/ˈfɒstərəbl/ (US) /ˈfɒstərəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可寄养的，可促进的|Able to be fostered or promoted.  Suitable for fostering (especially of a child).  Conducive to growth or development.|例句：The organization works to ensure that all children are placed in safe and fosterable homes.  该组织致力于确保所有儿童都被安置在安全和适合寄养的家庭中。

例句：The agency determined that the baby was fosterable and began searching for a suitable family.  该机构确定婴儿可以寄养，并开始寻找合适的家庭。|近义词：adoptable, suitable, appropriate|反义词：unsuitable, inappropriate|词性:形容词
