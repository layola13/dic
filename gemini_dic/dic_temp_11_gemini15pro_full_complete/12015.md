arthropodous:/ɑːrˈθrɒpədəs/| adj.|节肢动物的；有关节的|Relating to or characteristic of arthropods, invertebrate animals with jointed limbs and a segmented body, such as insects, spiders, and crustaceans.|例句：The arthropodous exoskeleton provides protection and support. 节肢动物的外骨骼提供保护和支撑。|例句：Scientists discovered a new arthropodous species in the rainforest. 科学家在雨林中发现了一种新的节肢动物。|近义词：invertebrate, jointed|反义词: vertebrate|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ἄρθρον (árthron, “joint”) + πούς (poús, “foot”) + -ous (adjective suffix).
