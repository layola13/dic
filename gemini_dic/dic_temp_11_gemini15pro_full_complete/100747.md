intransmissible:/ˌɪntrænsˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可传递的，不会传染的 | Not capable of being transmitted or passed on, especially of a disease. |  The doctor assured her that the illness was intransmissible. 医生向她保证这种疾病不会传染。|  The family heirloom's sentimental value is intransmissible.  这件传家宝的情感价值是无法传递的。 |  noncommunicable, noncontagious, untransferable | communicable, contagious, transferable | adjective
