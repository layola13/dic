microcalorimetry: /ˌmaɪkroʊˌkæləˈrɪmətri/ | n. | 微量热法 | The measurement of very small amounts of heat changes, especially in biological systems.  | Microcalorimetry is a powerful technique for studying the thermodynamics of biological processes. 微量热法是研究生物过程热力学的有力工具。 |  Microcalorimetry was used to determine the binding affinity of the drug to the protein.  微量热法被用来确定药物与蛋白质的结合亲和力。|  calorimetry, thermal analysis | N/A | noun
