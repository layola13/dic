nonelaborative:/ˌnɒnɪˈlæbərətɪv/
adjective
非精细的；不详尽的；简略的
Not detailed or thorough; superficial.
The student gave a nonelaborative answer to the complex question, leaving the teacher unsatisfied.  这个学生对复杂的问题给出了一个不详尽的答案，令老师不满意。
His explanation was nonelaborative, offering only a cursory overview of the topic. 他的解释很不详尽，只对主题进行了粗略的概述。
Synonyms:  superficial, cursory, perfunctory, simplistic, brief, concise
Antonyms: detailed, thorough, comprehensive, in-depth, elaborate, complex
词性: adjective
