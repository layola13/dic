barcon: /ˈbɑːrkən/ | n. |驳船;大型平底船|A flat-bottomed boat, typically with square ends, used for carrying cargo, especially on canals and rivers. |例句：The barcon was laden with coal. 这艘驳船装满了煤炭。|例句：The barcon slowly made its way down the river. 驳船缓缓地沿着河向下游驶去。|近义词：barge, lighter|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑ship, vessel - 这些词更通用，不一定是平底的)|词性: 名词

词源: From Middle French *barcon, from Old French *barc, from Proto-Germanic *bark- (“boat”). Cognate with Old English bærċ (“bark”).
