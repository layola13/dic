constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/ | n. adj. | 成分；选民；构成要素 | A constituent is a component part of something, or a voter who elects a representative. | The sugar is a key constituent of the cake. 糖是蛋糕的关键成分。 | component, element, ingredient | opposite, contrary | noun, adjective

constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/ | n. adj. | 选民 | A constituent is a person who lives in a particular area and is represented by an elected official. | The senator met with his constituents to discuss their concerns. 参议员与他的选民会面，讨论他们关心的问题。 | voter, elector | non-voter | noun, adjective
