nitrogelatin:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈdʒɛlətɪn/ | n. |硝化甘油炸胶, 爆胶|A high explosive made by dissolving nitrocellulose in nitroglycerin.|Nitrogelatin is a powerful explosive used in mining and demolition. 硝化甘油炸胶是一种用于采矿和爆破的强力炸药。|Synonyms: blasting gelatin, gelignite|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology: nitro- + gelatin.  "nitro-" comes from  Greek *nitron,  "soda, potash,"  + "-gelatin"  referring to the gelatinous nature of the substance.
