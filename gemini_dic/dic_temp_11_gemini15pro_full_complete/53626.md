discern:/dɪˈsɜːrn/ | vt. | 辨别，察觉 | To perceive or recognize something, especially something that is not obvious. | I could discern a faint light in the distance. 我能隐约看到远处微弱的灯光。| perceive, detect, distinguish | overlook, miss | verb
