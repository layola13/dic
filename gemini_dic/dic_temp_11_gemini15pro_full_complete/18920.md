bespoke:/bɪˈspoʊk/| adj.|定制的，定做的|Made to order; custom-made.  Specifically referring to clothing, but now extended to other products and services.|The businessman wore a bespoke suit.  这位商人穿着一套定制西装。|Synonyms: custom-made, tailor-made, made-to-order, handcrafted|Antonyms: ready-made, off-the-rack, mass-produced|Adjective
