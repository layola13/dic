demurer:/dɪˈmjʊərər/ | n. | demur; objection; protest |  A statement of objection or protest.  A pleading in a lawsuit that objects to an opponent's pleading, while admitting for argument's sake the facts alleged in that pleading. |  The lawyer filed a demurer to the plaintiff's complaint.  律师对原告的诉状提出了异议。 | synonyms: objection, protest, challenge, exception | antonyms: acceptance, agreement, approval, assent | noun

demure:/dɪˈmjʊər/ | adj. | reserved; modest; shy | Characterized by shyness and modesty; reserved.  Affectedly shy or modest. | She gave him a demure smile. 她羞涩地对他笑了笑。  He was attracted to her demure manner. 他被她娴静的举止所吸引。 | synonyms: modest, shy, reserved, bashful, coy, retiring | antonyms: bold, brazen, forward, immodest, outgoing | adjective
