barger: /ˈbɑːrdʒər/ | n. |驳船夫，驳船工人 | A person who operates or works on a barge. | The barger skillfully navigated the canal. 驳船夫熟练地在运河中航行。|  He loaded the cargo onto the barge with the help of another barger. 他在另一个驳船工人的帮助下将货物装上驳船。|  近义词: bargeman, boatman | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps landlubber informally) | 词性: noun

词源:  From *barge* + *-er* (agent suffix).  The word *barge* itself likely originates from the Old French *barge*, ultimately from the Late Latin *barca*.
