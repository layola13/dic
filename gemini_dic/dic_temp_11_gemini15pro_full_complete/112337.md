lorettine: /ˌlɔːrəˈtiːn/ | adj. | 洛雷托的；圣母之家的；圣母小教堂的 | Relating to Loreto, Italy, a town that is a major center of Catholic pilgrimage because of the Santa Casa, the Holy House, which is believed to be the house where the Virgin Mary lived. |  The Lorettine litany is a prayer to the Virgin Mary. 洛雷托祷文是对圣母玛利亚的祈祷。 | Near synonyms: Marian, Virginian | Antonym: None | adjective


lorettine: /ˌlɔːrəˈtiːn/ | n. | 洛雷托人；圣母之家成员 | A resident of Loreto, Italy or a member of a religious order associated with the Holy House of Loreto. | Many Lorettines make pilgrimages to the Santa Casa. 许多洛雷托人前往圣家朝圣。 | Near synonyms: pilgrim, devotee | Antonym: None | noun
