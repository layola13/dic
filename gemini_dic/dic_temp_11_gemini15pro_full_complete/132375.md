nondeliverances:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪvərənsɪz/ (US) /ˌnɑːndɪˈlɪvərənsɪz/
noun
未交付; 未履行; 未实现
The failure to deliver something as promised or expected; things that were not delivered.
The company apologized for the nondeliverances and offered refunds.  该公司为未交付的货物道歉并提供退款。
The report highlighted several nondeliverances in the project.  报告重点指出了项目中的几项未完成事项。
Near Synonyms: shortfalls, failures, omissions, defaults
Antonyms:  deliveries, accomplishments, fulfillments
词性: 名词

词源:
From non- + deliverance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "deliverance" comes from the Old French word "delivrance," meaning "the action of freeing or setting free; rescue, release."
