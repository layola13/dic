diathermometer:/ˌdaɪəθərˈmɒmɪtər/
|n.|
|透热计|
|An instrument for measuring the thermal conductivity of materials, especially the ability of a substance to transmit radiant heat.|
|The diathermometer was used to measure the heat conductivity of different types of insulation.|透热计被用来测量不同类型绝缘体的导热率。|
|thermal conductivity meter, heat conductivity meter|
|N/A|
|noun|


diathermancy:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːmənsi/
|n.|
|透热性|
|The property of transmitting radiant heat; transparency to heat rays.|
|The diathermancy of the glass allowed sunlight to warm the room.|玻璃的透热性使阳光温暖了房间。|
|thermal transmittance, heat permeability|
|athermancy|
|noun|

ETYMOLOGY->
From dia- (through) + thermo- (heat) + -meter (measuring instrument).  Ultimately from Greek dia (through) + therme (heat) + metron (measure).
