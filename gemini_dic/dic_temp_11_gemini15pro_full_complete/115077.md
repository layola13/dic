maneuverably:/məˈnuːvərəbli/
adverb
易操纵地，灵活地
Easily maneuvered; nimble.
The small car was maneuverably around the tight corners of the city streets. 这辆小汽车在城市街道狭窄的角落里灵活地行驶。
近义词: nimbly, deftly, adroitly, skillfully
反义词: clumsily, awkwardly, stiffly
词性: adverb

maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/
noun, verb
策略；调动；演习；用策略使…；调动；操纵
A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care. / To move skillfully or carefully.
The troops performed a series of complex maneuvers. 部队进行了一系列复杂的演习。
The captain maneuvered the ship through the narrow channel. 船长驾驶着船穿过狭窄的航道。
近义词: tactic, strategy, ploy, scheme / manipulate, guide, steer, navigate
反义词: inaction, stillness / neglect, abandon
词性: noun, verb

-able:/əbl/
suffix
能…的；可…的；适合…的
Capable of, suitable for, or worthy of (whatever is expressed by the stem).
manageable, readable, comfortable
近义词: -ible, -capable
反义词: -less
词性: suffix


Etymology:
From maneuver + -ably.  Maneuver itself comes from the French "manœuvrer," ultimately from Latin "manu operari," meaning "to work by hand."
