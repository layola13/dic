kraaling:/ˈkrɑːlɪŋ/| n.|围栏；牲畜栏；畜栏|An enclosure for livestock, especially cattle or sheep. |The farmer built a new kraaling for his sheep. 农民为他的羊建了一个新的畜栏。|近义词: enclosure, pen, corral|反义词: open field|词性:名词

词源: From Afrikaans *kraal*, ultimately from Portuguese *curral* "enclosure for livestock".
