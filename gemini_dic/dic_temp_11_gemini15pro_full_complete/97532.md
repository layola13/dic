ingraftment: /ɪnˈɡræftmənt/ /ɪnˈɡrɑːftmənt/
noun
移植；接枝；植入；(思想、习惯等的)灌输
the process of tissue or organs becoming established in a recipient after transplantation.  The successful establishment of transplanted tissue or organs in a recipient.
例句：The ingraftment of the new kidney was successful.  新肾的移植很成功。

例句：The doctor monitored the patient closely for signs of ingraftment rejection. 医生密切监测病人是否有移植排斥反应的迹象。

近义词：transplantation, implantation, grafting
反义词：rejection
词性：n.
