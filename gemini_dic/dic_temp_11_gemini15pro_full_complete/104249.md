jurors:/ˈdʒʊrərz/| n.|陪审员|A member of a jury.|The jurors deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict. 陪审员们商议了数小时才达成裁决。|近义词：juryman, jurywoman|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps defendant or accused)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *juroor, from jurer "to swear," from Latin jurare "to swear," from jus "law, right."
