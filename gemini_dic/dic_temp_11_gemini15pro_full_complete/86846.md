hemialbumin:/ˌhɛmiælˈbjuːmɪn/ | n. |半白蛋白|A protein resembling albumin, but of lower molecular weight, often produced by the breakdown of albumin.|Hemialbumin was detected in the patient's urine, suggesting kidney damage. 在病人的尿液中检测到半白蛋白，表明肾脏受损。|Near synonyms:  albumin fragment,  protein fragment|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) whole albumin, complete protein|noun
hemialbumin:/ˌhɛmiælˈbjuːmɪn/ | n. |半白蛋白|A protein fragment derived from albumin, typically smaller in size.|The presence of hemialbumin can be an indicator of certain medical conditions. 半白蛋白的存在可以指示某些医疗状况。|Near synonyms:  albumin derivative, polypeptide|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider perhaps) macromolecule|noun
