例句：The plant had lathy stems that reached towards the sunlight.  这种植物有细长的茎，向着阳光生长。|近义词：gangly, spindly, slender, thin, reedy, wiry|反义词：stocky, stout, thickset|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *lathi, from Old English *lætt (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *latjō, *latjǭ (“lath”). Cognate with West Frisian let, Dutch lat, German Latte.
