outblunder:/ˌaʊtˈblʌndər/
verb
犯更大的错误;在犯错方面胜过
To make more or bigger blunders than someone else.
He outblundered his opponent in the chess match, making three crucial mistakes that cost him the game. 他在棋局中比对手犯了更大的错误，三个关键的失误让他输掉了比赛。

outblunder (动词原形)
近义词: outdo in errors, surpass in mistakes, make bigger blunders than
反义词: outperform, excel, outsmart
