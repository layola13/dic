gumdrop:/ˈɡʌmdrɒp/ (UK) /ˈɡʌmdrɑːp/ (US)| n. |软糖，橡皮糖|A small, firm, brightly colored candy made of sweetened gelatin or gum arabic. |例句：My grandmother always kept a jar of gumdrops on her coffee table. 我祖母的咖啡桌上总是放着一罐橡皮糖。

例句：The children loved the assorted flavors of the gumdrops. 孩子们喜欢各种口味的橡皮糖。|近义词：jelly candy, gummy candy, jujube|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of candy) |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *gum* + *drop*, referring to the original shape of the candy as a small, dropped piece of gum arabic.
