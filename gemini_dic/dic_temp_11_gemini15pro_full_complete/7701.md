anomalistically: /ˌænəmɑːˈlɪstɪkli/ /ˌænəməˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. |反常地，异常地| In a way that deviates from the norm or what is expected; abnormally. | The comet behaved anomalistically, veering off its predicted course. 这颗彗星表现异常，偏离了预定的轨道。|  The stock market has been behaving anomalistically lately. 最近股市表现异常。 |Synonyms: abnormally, irregularly, atypically, unusually, exceptionally, peculiarly, oddly, strangely, bizarrely, aberrantly | Antonyms: normally, typically, regularly, usually, ordinarily |词性: 副词

Etymology:
From anomaly + -istic + -ally.  Anomaly comes from the Greek *anōmalos*, meaning "uneven," from *an-,* meaning "not," and *homalos*, meaning "even."
