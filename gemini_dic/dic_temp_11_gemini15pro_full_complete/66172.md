excessively:/ɪkˈsɛsɪvli/| adv.|过度地，过分地|Going beyond what is necessary or desirable; too much.  |She worried excessively about her exams. 她过分担心她的考试。|Synonyms: immoderately, unduly, inordinately, exorbitantly|Antonyms: moderately, reasonably, sparingly|Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *excessivus, from Latin excessus (“excess”), past participle of excedere (“to go beyond, exceed”).
