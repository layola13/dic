nomady:/ˈnoʊmædi/| n.|游牧;流浪;游牧生活|A way of life in which people do not live in one place but travel around, usually to find fresh pasture for their animals.  |Example 1:  The Tuareg people of the Sahara maintain a tradition of nomady, moving their herds across vast distances. 图阿雷格人在撒哈拉沙漠保持着游牧的传统，将他们的牧群迁移到很远的地方。

Example 2:  The documentary explored the challenges and rewards of a life of nomady in the Mongolian steppes. 这部纪录片探讨了在蒙古草原上游牧生活的挑战和回报。|近义词：nomadism, wandering, migration, roving|反义词:sedentary lifestyle, settled existence|词性:名词
