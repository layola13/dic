gymnasticses:/dʒɪmˈnæstɪksɪz/ | n. | 体操（运动）| Gymnastics is a sport involving physical exercises requiring balance, strength, flexibility, agility, coordination, and endurance. The movements involved in gymnastics contribute to the development of the arms, legs, shoulders, back, chest, and abdominal muscle groups.  It is typically used in the singular form.  The plural "gymnasticses" is grammatically correct but rarely used. | She excels at gymnastics. 她擅长体操。| Near Synonyms:  calisthenics, exercises | Antonym: inactivity | noun
