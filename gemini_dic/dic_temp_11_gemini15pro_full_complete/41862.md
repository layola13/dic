cosatu:/kɒˈsɑːtuː/
n.
南非工会大会（Congress of South African Trade Unions）
The Congress of South African Trade Unions, the largest trade union federation in South Africa.  It played a significant role in the struggle against apartheid.
COSATU has been a powerful voice for workers' rights in South Africa. 南非工会大会一直是南非工人权利的有力代言人。
The government held talks with COSATU about the proposed labor law changes. 政府就拟定的劳动法修改与南非工会大会举行了会谈。
近义词:  trade union federation, labor federation
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider employer organization or business association)
