betray:/bɪˈtreɪ/| vt.|背叛，出卖；暴露，显露|To be disloyal to someone or something, or to reveal something that should be kept secret.|He betrayed his country by selling secrets to the enemy. 他向敌人出卖机密，背叛了他的国家。

She betrayed her friend's trust. 她辜负了朋友的信任。

His trembling hands betrayed his nervousness. 他颤抖的双手暴露了他的紧张。|近义词：deceive, mislead, expose, reveal|反义词：support, protect, conceal|词性：动词
