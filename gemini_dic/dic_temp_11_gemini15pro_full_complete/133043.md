nonenvironmentally:/ˌnɒnɪnˌvaɪrənˈmentəli/ | adv. | 非环境地，不环保地 | Not in a way that relates to or considers the environment. |  The company has been criticized for acting nonenvironmentally by dumping waste into the river.  这家公司因向河里倾倒垃圾而受到批评，被指责其行为不环保。 |  例句：They disposed of the waste nonenvironmentally. 他们以不环保的方式处理了废物。 | 近义词：unsustainably, ecologically damaging | 反义词：environmentally, sustainably | 词性：副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
environment: from Old French environ "circuit, surroundings," from environner "to surround," from en- "in" + viron "circle," ultimately from Latin virere "to be green."
-al (suffix): forming adjectives meaning "relating to"
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
