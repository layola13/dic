implicit:/ɪmˈplɪsɪt/ | adj. | 含蓄的，不明确的；绝对的，无疑问的 | implied though not plainly expressed; unquestioning or unreserved; absolute |  She had implicit trust in her best friend. 她对她最好的朋友绝对信任。| synonyms: implied, tacit, unspoken, inherent, absolute | antonyms: explicit, express, specific, direct, stated, overt | adjective

implicit:/ɪmˈplɪsɪt/ | adj. | 内含的 | capable of being understood from something else though unexpressed |  His silence gave implicit consent. 他的沉默表示默认同意。 | synonyms: inherent, involved | antonyms: explicit | adjective
