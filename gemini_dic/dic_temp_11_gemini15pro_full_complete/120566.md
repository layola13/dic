mire:/maɪər/| n. |泥潭; 困境; 沼泽|A stretch of swampy or boggy ground. A situation or state of difficulty, distress, or embarrassment from which it is hard to extricate oneself.|The car got stuck in the mire. 汽车陷入了泥潭。|Synonyms: bog, marsh, swamp, quagmire, morass|Antonyms:  solid ground, terra firma|Noun


mire:/maɪər/| v. |使陷入泥潭; 陷入困境|To cause to become stuck in mire. To involve in a difficult situation.|The heavy rain mired the tractor in the field.  大雨使拖拉机陷在了田里。|Synonyms: entangle, embroil, bog down|Antonyms: extricate, free, liberate|Verb
