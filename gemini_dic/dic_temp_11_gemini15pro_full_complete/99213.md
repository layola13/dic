intercoccygeal: /ˌɪntərˌkɒksɪˈdʒiːəl/ /ˌɪntərˌkɑːksɪˈdʒiːəl/
adjective
尾骨间的；尾骨间的
Relating to the region between the coccyges (tailbone).
The intercoccygeal joint is slightly movable. 尾骨间关节略微可动。
近义词: coccygeal
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + coccygeal, from coccyx + -al.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Coccyx" comes from the Greek word "kokkyx," meaning "cuckoo," because the shape of the bone resembles a cuckoo's beak. "-al" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns.
