noncursive:/ˌnɒnˈkɜːsɪv/ | adj. | 非草体的，印刷体的 | Not cursive; printed.  Characterized by distinct, unconnected letters rather than flowing strokes joined together. | The teacher asked the students to write their names in noncursive letters. 老师要求学生用印刷体写他们的名字。| The document was printed in a clear, noncursive font. 这份文件是用清晰的印刷字体打印的。 | 近义词: print, block | 反义词: cursive, joined, running | 词性: 形容词

词源：non- (not) + cursive (flowing, running)  来自拉丁语 currere "to run".  non- 表示否定，cursive 表示草书，连笔书写。因此 noncursive 表示非草书的，印刷体的。
