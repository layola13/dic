draffy:/ˈdræfi/| adj.|邋遢的，不整洁的；草率的，粗糙的|Untidy, poorly dressed, or slovenly in appearance; carelessly or roughly done.|例句：He looked draffy in his wrinkled shirt. 他穿着皱巴巴的衬衫，看起来很邋遢。|例句：Her draffy handwriting was difficult to read. 她潦草的字迹很难辨认。|近义词：sloppy, untidy, messy, scruffy, careless|反义词：neat, tidy, smart, meticulous, careful|词性:形容词

词源:  Likely a variant of "drafty," referring to something exposed to drafts and thus likely to be unkempt or disordered.  The meaning then extended to general untidiness and carelessness.
