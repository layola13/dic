magnate:/ˈmæɡneɪt/| n. |大亨，巨头，权贵|A wealthy and influential person, especially in business or industry. |例句：The oil magnates controlled vast fortunes. 石油大亨们掌控着巨额财富。

例句：He became a shipping magnate through hard work and shrewd investments. 他凭借努力工作和精明的投资成为了航运巨头。|近义词：tycoon, mogul, industrialist, baron|反义词:pauper, commoner|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *magnas, magnatis*  ("great man, nobleman"), from Latin *magnus* ("great").
