add:/æd/ | v. | 加，增加，补充说 | to put something together with something else so as to increase the size, number, amount, etc. |  I added some sugar to my coffee. 我在咖啡里加了些糖。

|近义词：increase, augment, append, supplement |反义词: subtract, deduct, remove | verb

add:/æd/ | v. | 加起来，总计 | to combine numbers or amounts to get a total | The numbers add up to 100. 这些数字加起来是100。

|近义词：sum, total, tally |反义词: N/A | verb
