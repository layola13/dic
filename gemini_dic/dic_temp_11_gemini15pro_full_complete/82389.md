grubbily:/ˈɡrʌbɪli/
adv.|肮脏地，邋遢地|in a dirty or untidy way.  |He dressed grubbily in stained overalls. 他穿着沾满污渍的工作服，显得邋遢不堪。|近义词：dirtily, filthily, slovenly|反义词：cleanly, neatly|词性: 副词
词源: From grub + -ily.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grubją ("to dig").
