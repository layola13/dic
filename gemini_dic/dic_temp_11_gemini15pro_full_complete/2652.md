adorations:/ˌædɔːˈreɪʃənz/
n.
崇拜，爱慕；敬爱
Deep love and respect; worship.
Her adorations were directed toward the movie star. 她对这位电影明星充满了崇拜之情。
synonyms: worship, reverence, devotion, love, affection
antonyms: disdain, contempt, hatred, aversion
noun
