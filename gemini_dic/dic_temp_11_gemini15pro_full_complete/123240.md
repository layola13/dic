monocultural:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkʌltʃərəl/ (UK) /ˌmɑːnoʊˈkʌltʃərəl/ (US)| adj.|单一文化的|Characterized by a single culture, lacking cultural diversity.|The town was very monocultural, with little influence from outside its own traditions.  这个小镇文化单一，很少受到外部传统的影响。|例句：The monocultural nature of the community made it difficult for newcomers to integrate.  这个社区单一文化的性质使新来者难以融入。|近义词：homogeneous, uniform|反义词：multicultural, diverse|词性:形容词

monoculture:/ˈmɒnəʊˌkʌltʃə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnoʊˌkʌltʃər/ (US)| n.|单一栽培，单一文化|The cultivation of a single crop in a given area. Also refers to a society or system dominated by a single culture.|The reliance on monoculture farming can deplete the soil.  依赖单一种植农业会耗尽土壤肥力。|例句：The region's monoculture of sugarcane has made it vulnerable to disease.  该地区甘蔗的单一栽培使其容易受到疾病的侵害。|近义词：homogeneity|反义词：polyculture, diversity|词性:名词
