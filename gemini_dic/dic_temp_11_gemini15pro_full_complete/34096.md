civilizes:/ˈsɪvɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
使文明；教化
to bring out of a savage, barbarous, or uncivilized state; make civil
例句：Education civilizes people. 教育使人文明。
例句：The Romans civilized much of Europe. 罗马人教化了欧洲的大部分地区。
近义词：educate, refine, cultivate, enlighten
反义词：barbarize
词性：v.


civilizing:/ˈsɪvɪlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
教化的；文明的
making or tending to make civil; refining
例句：The civilizing influence of education is undeniable. 教育的教化作用是不可否认的。
例句：They believed in the civilizing mission of their culture. 他们相信自己文化的教化使命。
近义词：educative, refining, enlightening
反义词：barbarizing
词性：adj.
