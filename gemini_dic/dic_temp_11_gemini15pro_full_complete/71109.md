fisheries:/ˈfɪʃəriz/ | n. | 渔业；渔场；水产业 |  The business or practice of catching fish.  It can refer to the places where fish are caught, the industry as a whole, or a particular area of water designated for fishing. | The decline of coastal fisheries has had a devastating impact on local communities. 沿海渔业的衰退对当地社区造成了毁灭性的影响。|  fishing industry, aquaculture | hunting | noun
