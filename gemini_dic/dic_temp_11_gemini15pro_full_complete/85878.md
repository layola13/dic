headboards:/ˈhɛdbɔːrdz/
n.
床头板
a vertical board or panel forming the head of a bed.
The headboard was upholstered in a luxurious velvet. 床头板用奢华的天鹅绒装饰。
The children were jumping on the bed and broke the headboard. 孩子们在床上跳，把床头板弄坏了。
headboard, bedhead
footboard
noun
