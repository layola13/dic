hang-dog:/ˈhæŋdɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhæŋdɔːɡ/ (US)| adj.| 沮丧的，垂头丧气的，愧疚的 | Having a dejected or guilty appearance, like a dog with its tail between its legs. | He had a hang-dog expression on his face after being caught cheating.  他被抓到作弊后，脸上带着一副沮丧的表情。| Synonyms: ashamed, dejected, downcast, guilty, sheepish | Antonyms: cheerful, upbeat, confident, proud | adjective

hang-dog:/ˈhæŋdɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhæŋdɔːɡ/ (US)| n.| 沮丧的人，垂头丧气的人 | A person who looks dejected or guilty. |  He was a hang-dog after losing his job.  他丢了工作后变得垂头丧气。| Synonyms: sad sack, loser | Antonyms: winner, champion | noun
