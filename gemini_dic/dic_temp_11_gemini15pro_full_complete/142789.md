oulachan:/ˈuːləkən/ | n. |  烛鱼 | A small, oily fish (Thaleichthys pacificus) found in the Pacific Northwest, related to the smelt. They are an important food source for many animals and indigenous peoples. |  The oulachan run in huge numbers up coastal rivers. 烛鱼成群结队地溯游到沿海的河流。 |  近义词: smelt, eulachon | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) | 词性: noun


词源: From Chinook Jargon *úlakán, from a Lower Chinookan language (possibly Clackamas).
