bisque:/bɪsk/| n.|浓汤，尤其是用贝类、龙虾或蔬菜做成的浓汤|A rich, creamy soup, typically made with shellfish, especially lobster or crayfish, or vegetables.|例句：The lobster bisque was rich and flavorful. 龙虾浓汤味道浓郁，鲜美无比。

例句：She made a delicious tomato bisque for dinner. 她晚饭做了一道美味的番茄浓汤。|近义词：soup, chowder, potage|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of food)|词性:noun
