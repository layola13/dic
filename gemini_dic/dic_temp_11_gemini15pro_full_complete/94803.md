impulse:/ˈɪmpʌls/ (US), /ˈɪmpʌls/ (UK)| n.|冲动，脉冲，刺激|A sudden, strong and unreflective urge or desire to act.|He had a sudden impulse to buy a sports car. 他突然心血来潮想买一辆跑车。|synonyms: urge, desire, whim, instinct, compulsion|antonyms: hesitation, reluctance, inhibition|noun

impulse:/ˈɪmpʌls/ (US), /ˈɪmpʌls/ (UK)| n.|脉冲，冲击|A short, sharp surge of electrical or other energy.|The nerve impulses travel to the brain. 神经冲动传到大脑。|synonyms: pulse, surge, burst, jolt|antonyms: continuous flow, steady stream|noun
