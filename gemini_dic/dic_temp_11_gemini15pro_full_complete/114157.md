mahjong:/mɑːˈʒɒŋ/ /mɑːˈdʒɑːŋ/| n.|麻将|A Chinese game played by four people with small tiles resembling dominoes. It involves skill, strategy, and a degree of chance.|We played mahjong all afternoon. 我们打了一下午麻将。|Synonyms: mah-jongg, mahjongg|Antonyms: None|Noun

mahjong:/mɑːˈʒɒŋ/ /mɑːˈdʒɑːŋ/| adj.|(Informal) Relating to the game of mahjong.|He's got a mahjong set made of ivory. 他有一副象牙做的麻将。|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|Adjective
