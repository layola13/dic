explications:/ˌɛksplɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 解释，说明 | An explication is a detailed explanation or interpretation of a text, idea, or work of art. | The professor's explications of the poem helped the students understand its complex meaning. 教授对这首诗的解释帮助学生理解了其复杂的含义。 |  The artist provided explications for her unusual painting technique.  艺术家对她的特殊绘画技巧作了解释。 | synonyms: explanations, interpretations, elucidations, clarifications | antonyms: obfuscations, confusions, mystifications | noun
