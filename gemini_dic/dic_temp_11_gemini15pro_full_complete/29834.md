cauma:/ˈkaʊmə/| n. |烙印，烧灼的痕迹|A mark or scar produced by burning or branding, especially on the skin. |The cauma on the horse's flank identified it as belonging to the rancher. 马侧翼的烙印表明它属于那个牧场主。|近义词：brand, scar, mark|反义词: unmarked|词性:名词
