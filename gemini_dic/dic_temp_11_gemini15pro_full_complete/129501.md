non-artistic:/ˌnɒnɑːˈtɪstɪk/
adjective
非艺术的，缺乏艺术性的
Not artistic; lacking artistic qualities.
His non-artistic approach to design resulted in a rather plain website. 他非艺术的设计方法导致网站相当平淡。
Synonyms: unartistic, inartistic, uncreative
Antonyms: artistic, creative, aesthetic
词性: 形容词
