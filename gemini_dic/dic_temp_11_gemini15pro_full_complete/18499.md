bendiest:/ˈbɛndiɪst/| adj.|最弯曲的，最灵活的|Having the ability to bend easily or readily; most flexible.  It describes something that can bend to a greater degree than other comparable things.|例句：The bendiest tree branch swayed gently in the breeze.  最弯曲的树枝在微风中轻轻摇曳。

例句：He was known for having the bendiest fingers, which made him an excellent pianist. 他以拥有最灵活的手指而闻名，这使他成为了一名优秀的钢琴家。|近义词：most flexible, most supple, most pliable|反义词:stiffest, rigidest|词性:adjective
词源:From bend + -y + -est.  "Bend" comes from Old English *bendan, of Germanic origin. "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning “full of” or “characterized by.” "-est" is a superlative suffix.
