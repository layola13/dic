foresignify:/fɔːrˈsɪɡnɪfaɪ/
verb
预示，预兆
To signify or indicate beforehand; portend.
The dark clouds foresignified a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。
The economist's report foresignified a recession.  经济学家的报告预示着经济衰退。
Synonyms: presage, portend, foreshadow, augur, forebode
Antonyms: conceal, hide, belie
词性: 动词
