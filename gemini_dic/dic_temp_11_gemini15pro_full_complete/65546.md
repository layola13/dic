euthanise: /ˈjuːθənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈjuːθəˌnaɪz/ (US)| vt.|安乐死，使安乐死|To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness.|The vet euthanised the injured horse.  兽医对受伤的马实施了安乐死。

The family made the difficult decision to euthanise their beloved dog. 这家人艰难地决定对他们心爱的狗实施安乐死。 |近义词: put down, put to sleep|反义词: resuscitate, revive|词性: 动词


euthanasia: /ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/ (UK) /ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/ (US)| n.|安乐死|The painless killing of a patient suffering from an incurable and painful disease or in an irreversible coma.|Euthanasia is a complex and controversial issue. 安乐死是一个复杂且有争议的问题。

The debate about euthanasia continues to divide public opinion. 关于安乐死的争论继续分裂着公众舆论。|近义词: mercy killing|反义词: resuscitation|词性: 名词


词源：
From Greek, eu- (good) + thanatos (death).
