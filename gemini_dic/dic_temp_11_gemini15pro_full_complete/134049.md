nonideological:/ˌnɒnaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnaɪdiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非意识形态的
Not based on or relating to a particular ideology; not influenced by ideology.
例句：The organization claims to be nonideological and focuses solely on environmental issues.  这个组织声称是非意识形态的，只关注环境问题。

例句：He took a nonideological approach to the problem, focusing on practical solutions. 他采取了一种非意识形态的方法来解决这个问题，专注于实际的解决方案。

近义词：pragmatic, practical, neutral, objective
反义词：ideological, doctrinaire, partisan
