agv:/eɪdʒiːˈviː/
n.|自动导引车(Automated Guided Vehicle)|An automated guided vehicle (AGV) is a portable robot that follows markers or wires in the floor, or uses vision or lasers.  |The AGV transported materials across the factory floor.|Synonyms: automated guided cart, driverless vehicle, self-guided vehicle|Antonyms: manually operated vehicle|noun
