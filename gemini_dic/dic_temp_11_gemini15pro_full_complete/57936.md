drinkablenesses:/ˈdrɪŋkəbəlnəsɪz/
n.|适饮性，可饮用性|The quality or state of being drinkable.  The drinkablenesses of the various water sources were tested.| 例句：The drinkablenesses of the well water improved after the new filter was installed. 井水装了新的过滤器后，适饮性提高了。

例句：The report detailed the drinkablenesses of the river water at different points along its course.  这份报告详细描述了这条河在其不同河段的河水的适饮性。
|近义词：potability, drinkability|反义词：undrinkability, non-potability|词性: 名词 (plural noun)
