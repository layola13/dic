lengthinesses:/ˈlɛŋθinəsɪz/
n.
冗长；啰嗦
The lengthiness of the meeting bored everyone. 会议的冗长让每个人都感到无聊。
The lengthinesses of the legal documents were daunting.  这些法律文件的冗长令人畏惧。
近义词：prolixity, verbosity, wordiness
反义词：brevity, conciseness, succinctness
词性：名词 (plural noun)

Etymology:  From lengthy + -ness. Lengthy comes from length + -y. Length ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *langa-  ("long").
