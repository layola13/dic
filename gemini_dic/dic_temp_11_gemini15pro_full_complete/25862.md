burke:/bɜːrk/| v.|压制，扼杀，阻止，掩盖（尤指通过不正当手段）|To suppress or stifle something, especially by unfair means. |The government tried to burke the scandal. 政府试图掩盖这桩丑闻。|Synonyms: suppress, stifle, muzzle, hush up, smother, quash, repress |Antonyms: reveal, expose, publicize, uncover, divulge|verb
burke:/bɜːrk/| n.|（对议案的）阻挠|The act of burking, especially a parliamentary bill. |The burke of the bill angered many citizens.  对该法案的阻挠激怒了许多公民。|Synonyms: obstruction, suppression|Antonyms: promotion, advancement|noun
