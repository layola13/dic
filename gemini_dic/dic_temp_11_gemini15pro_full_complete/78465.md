gewgaws:/ˈɡuːɡɔːz/ /ˈɡuːɡɑːz/
noun
小玩意儿；廉价饰品
Showy things, especially those of little value.
She spent her allowance on cheap gewgaws. 她把零用钱都花在了廉价的小玩意儿上。
Synonyms: trinkets, baubles, knick-knacks, gimcracks
Antonyms: valuables, treasures
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, perhaps imitative. First recorded in the 16th century.
