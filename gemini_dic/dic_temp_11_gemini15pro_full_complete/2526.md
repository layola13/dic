admirable: /ˈædmərəbl/ (US) /ˈædmərəbl/ (UK)| adj. | 令人钦佩的；值得赞赏的 | Deserving respect and approval. |  She handled the crisis with admirable calm. 她以令人钦佩的冷静处理了危机。| synonyms: commendable, praiseworthy, laudable, estimable | antonyms: despicable, contemptible, deplorable, reprehensible | adjective

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *admirabilis, from Latin admirari ‘wonder at’.
