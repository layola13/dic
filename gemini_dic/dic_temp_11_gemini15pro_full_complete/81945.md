grimed:/ɡraɪmd/| adj.|肮脏的，污秽的|Covered with grime; dirty.|例句：The windows were grimed with dust and dirt. 窗户上满是灰尘和污垢。|例句：His face was grimed after working in the coal mine all day. 在煤矿工作了一整天后，他的脸上满是煤灰。|近义词：dirty, soiled, filthy, grubby|反义词：clean, spotless|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From grime + -ed. Grime itself comes from Middle English gryme, from Old Norse gríma ("mask, hood"). The sense development likely went from "soot from a fire" to "dirt that covers like a mask."
