bondability:/ˌbɑːndəˈbɪləti/
n.
可担保性，可保证性
The quality or state of being bondable; the capacity of a person or entity to be insured or guaranteed, especially in relation to financial responsibility or trustworthiness.
The contractor demonstrated bondability by providing proof of insurance and financial stability.  承包商通过提供保险证明和财务稳定性证明了其可担保性。
The bondability of the company is crucial for securing the loan.  该公司的可担保性对于获得贷款至关重要。
近义词：insurability, guarantee, security
反义词：unbondability, unreliability, riskiness
词性: 名词

词源:  From "bondable" + "-ity". "Bondable" comes from "bond" (verb), meaning "to provide surety for," + "-able" (suffix), meaning "capable of being."  Ultimately from Old English "bindan," meaning "to tie, bind."
