committable:/kəˈmɪtəbl/
adj.
可承诺的；可以承担义务的
able to be committed or pledged.
例句：
The project is committable, as long as we have the necessary resources.
这个项目是可以承诺的，只要我们有必要的资源。

The offer was not committable until all parties had signed the agreement.
在各方都签署协议之前，该提议是不可承诺的。

近义词：
binding, pledgeable, engageable
反义词：
non-committal, uncommittable, revocable
词性
adjective
