disperser:/dɪˈspɜːrsər/| n.|分散器，散布器，驱散器|A device or agent that disperses something.|The farmer used a seed disperser to plant his crops. 农民用播种机播种庄稼。|近义词：distributor, spreader, scatterer|反义词：concentrator, collector, gatherer|noun
disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/| v.|分散，散布，驱散|To distribute or spread over a wide area.|The police dispersed the crowd. 警察驱散了人群。|近义词：scatter, distribute, disseminate|反义词：gather, collect, assemble|verb
