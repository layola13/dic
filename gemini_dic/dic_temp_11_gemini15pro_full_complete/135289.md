nonpartner:/ˌnɒnˈpɑːtnə(r)/ | n. |非伴侣，非合伙人|A person who is not one's partner (in a romantic relationship, business, or other endeavor).|例句：The company offered discounts to employees and their nonpartners. 公司为员工及其非伴侣提供折扣。|例句：She attended the event with a nonpartner friend. 她和一个非伴侣朋友参加了活动。|近义词：non-member, outsider|反义词：partner, spouse, colleague|词性：名词
