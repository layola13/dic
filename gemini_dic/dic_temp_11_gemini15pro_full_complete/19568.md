bigamistically:/ˌbɪɡəˈmɪstɪkli/
adv.
重婚地
in a bigamistic manner; by bigamy.  以重婚的方式；通过重婚。
He married bigamistically while his first wife was still alive. 他在第一任妻子仍然在世的情况下重婚了。
Synonyms: illegally, unlawfully
Antonyms: legally, lawfully
词性: 副词
