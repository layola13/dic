hilarious:/hɪˈleəriəs/ /hɪˈlɛəriəs/| adj.|极其滑稽的，爆笑的|Extremely funny; causing loud amusement.|The comedian's jokes were hilarious.  喜剧演员的笑话非常滑稽。|近义词：funny, amusing, comical, side-splitting|反义词：serious, somber, dull|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *hilarius, from Latin hilaris (“cheerful, merry”), from Proto-Italic *hilaros, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰelh₁- (“to shine, gleam; green, yellow”). Cognate with Ancient Greek χλωρός (khlōrós, “greenish-yellow”).
