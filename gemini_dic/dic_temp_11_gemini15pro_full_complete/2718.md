adsorbing:/ədˈzɔːrbɪŋ/ /ədˈsɔːrbɪŋ/
adjective
吸附的；引人入胜的
taking up or attracting by chemical or molecular action; engaging or captivating
例句：The adsorbing properties of activated charcoal make it useful in water filters. 活性炭的吸附特性使其在滤水器中很有用。

例句：The professor gave an adsorbing lecture on the history of the Roman Empire.  教授做了一个关于罗马帝国历史的引人入胜的演讲。

近义词：absorbing, captivating, engaging, fascinating, interesting
反义词：repelling, uninteresting, boring, tedious
词性：adjective
