biennial: /baɪˈɛniəl/ | adj. | 两年一次的；持续两年的 | Lasting or occurring every two years. |  It's a biennial plant, meaning it lives for two years. 这是一种二年生植物，意味着它的寿命为两年。 | The biennial report summarizes the company's performance. 这份两年一度的报告总结了公司的业绩。 | 近义词: two-yearly | 反义词: annual | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *biennalis, from bi- "two" + annus "year".
