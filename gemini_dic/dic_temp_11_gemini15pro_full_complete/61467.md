embracery: /ɪmˈbreɪsəri/ /ɛmˈbreɪsəri/
n.
贿赂陪审团; 妨碍司法公正
The act of attempting to corrupt or influence a jury by promises, entreaties, money, or entertainment.
The lawyer was accused of embracery for attempting to bribe a juror.  这位律师因试图贿赂陪审员而被指控妨碍司法公正。
The judge condemned the blatant act of embracery that threatened to undermine the entire trial. 法官谴责了这一公然妨碍司法公正的行为，该行为有可能破坏整个审判。
近义词: jury tampering, bribery, corruption, undue influence
反义词: justice, fairness, impartiality
词性: noun

词源:
From Anglo-French *embracerie, from embracer "to embrace," figuratively "to take in one's arms, to win over."  源自盎格鲁法语 *embracerie，来自 embracer “拥抱”，比喻义为“搂在怀里，争取过来”。
