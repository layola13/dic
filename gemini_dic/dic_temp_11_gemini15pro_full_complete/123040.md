monembryony:/ˌmɒnəˈɛmbrɪəni/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɛmbrɪoʊni/ | n. | 单胚生殖，单性生殖 | Development of an embryo from a single cell, without fertilization.  The formation of only one embryo from a fertilized ovule.  |  Example: Apomixis is a form of monembryony where seeds are produced without fertilization. 无融合生殖是单胚生殖的一种形式，种子无需受精即可产生。

Example:  Citrus fruits often exhibit monembryony, which results in genetically identical offspring. 柑橘类水果通常表现出单胚生殖，从而产生基因相同的子代。| 近义词: apomixis, asexual reproduction | 反义词: polyembryony | 词性: noun
