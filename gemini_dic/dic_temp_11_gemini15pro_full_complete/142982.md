outbanters:/ˌaʊtˈbæntər/
|verb|
|胜过(某人)的玩笑；以玩笑胜过|
|To surpass someone in bantering; to outdo someone in witty or playful conversation.|
|He outbantered his opponent with a series of clever quips.|他以一系列机智的俏皮话胜过了对手。|
|She outbantered him with her quick wit and sharp humor.|她以敏捷的机智和犀利的幽默胜过他。|
|surpass in bantering, outdo in wit, outwit, defeat with humor|
|be outbantered|
