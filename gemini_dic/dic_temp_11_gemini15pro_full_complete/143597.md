outhustling: /ˌaʊtˈhʌslɪŋ/
|v. |胜过；比…更努力|Outworking someone, surpassing in effort and drive.  Putting in more effort and work than someone else, typically in a competitive situation.|She's outhustling her competitors and gaining market share. 她比竞争对手更努力，正在赢得市场份额。|Synonyms: outworking, outmaneuvering, outstripping|Antonyms: slacking, underperforming|Verb (present participle)
