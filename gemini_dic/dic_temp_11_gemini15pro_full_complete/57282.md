downsampling:/ˌdaʊnˈsæmplɪŋ/ | n. | 下采样，降采样 | The process of reducing the sampling rate of a signal.  It involves discarding some of the samples to create a smaller representation of the original signal. |  Downsampling the audio file reduced its size significantly.  降采样音频文件大大减小了其大小。 | decimation, subsampling | upsampling | noun

downsample:/ˌdaʊnˈsæmpl/ | v. | 对...进行下采样，降低...的采样率 | To reduce the sampling rate of a signal. |  We downsampled the image to make it load faster on the website. 我们对图像进行了下采样，以使其在网站上加载更快。 | decimate, subsample | upsample | verb
