annealing:/əˈniːlɪŋ/ /əˈniːlɪŋ/
n.|退火，韧化|The process of heating a material to a specific temperature and then cooling it slowly to remove internal stresses and improve its properties.  加热材料到特定温度然后缓慢冷却以消除内部应力并改善其性能的过程。|The annealing of the glass made it less brittle.  玻璃的退火使其不那么脆。|tempering, hardening|softening|noun
