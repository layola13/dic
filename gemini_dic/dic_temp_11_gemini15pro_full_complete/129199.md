noblemanly:/ˈnoʊblmənli/| adj.|贵族般的；高贵的；慷慨的|Having or showing qualities that are considered typical of a nobleman, such as dignity, generosity, and courage.|He behaved in a noblemanly fashion, offering his coat to the shivering woman.|近义词：aristocratic, dignified, generous, honorable, noble, magnanimous|反义词：base, ignoble, mean, petty, vulgar|词性:形容词
