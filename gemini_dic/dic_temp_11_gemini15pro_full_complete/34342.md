ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *claspen, *clapsen, from Old Norse *klaspa (“to clasp, grasp”), from Proto-Germanic *klampaz (“lump, clod”).  Cognate with Dutch klampen (“to clamp, fasten”), German klammern (“to cling, clamp”), Swedish klamra (“to clamp, staple”). Related to clump.
