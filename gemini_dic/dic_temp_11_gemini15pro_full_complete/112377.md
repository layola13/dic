losers:/ˈluːzərz/| n. |失败者，输家|A person or thing that loses or has lost something, especially a competition or game.  A person who is considered to be socially inept or unsuccessful. |The losers of the competition went home disappointed. 比赛的输家失望地回家了。|  He felt like a loser after failing the exam.  考试不及格后，他觉得自己像个失败者。|Synonyms: defeated, unsuccessful, also-ran, underdog (sometimes)|Antonyms: winners, victors, champions|Noun
