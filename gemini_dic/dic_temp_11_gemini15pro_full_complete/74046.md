fossa:/ˈfɒsə/ (UK) /ˈfɑːsə/ (US)| n.|窝，穴，凹陷；（尤指解剖学）窝，腔，小窝|A small cavity or depression, especially in a bone.  A ditch or trench. In zoology, a cat-like mammal native to Madagascar.|The surgeon pointed to the fossa on the skull.  外科医生指着颅骨上的窝。 The fossa contained the remains of an ancient civilization. 这个凹地里包含着古代文明的遗迹。  The fossa stalked its prey through the dense jungle.  窝鼬在茂密的丛林中跟踪它的猎物。|近义词：cavity, depression, trench, ditch, pit (general sense);  civet, mongoose (zoological sense)|反义词：ridge, protuberance (anatomical sense)|词性:noun
