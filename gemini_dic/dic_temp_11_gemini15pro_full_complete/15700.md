balaclava:/ˈbæləˌklɑːvə/ (US), /ˈbæləklɑːvə/ (UK)| n.|巴拉克拉法帽，面罩|A close-fitting knitted garment covering the head and neck, and sometimes also the face, typically worn in cold weather or for concealment.|The skier wore a balaclava to protect his face from the wind. 滑雪者戴着巴拉克拉法帽以保护他的脸免受风吹。|The burglar wore a balaclava to hide his identity.  窃贼戴着巴拉克拉法帽来隐藏身份。|Synonyms: ski mask, face mask|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps uncovered face)|Noun
