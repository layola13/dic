nonculpably:/nɒnˈkʌlpəbli/ | adv. | 无过错地，无罪地 | Not culpably; without blame or guilt.  |  He acted nonculpably in the situation, following all the correct procedures. 他在这种情况下行事无过错，遵循了所有正确的程序。 | He nonculpably repeated the information he had been given. 他毫无过错地重复了他被告知的信息。 | 近义词: blamelessly, innocently, irreproachably | 反义词: culpably, guiltily | 词性: 副词

词源:
From non- + culpably, from culpable + -ly. Culpable derives from Latin *culpābilis, from culpāre "to blame," from culpa "blame, fault."
