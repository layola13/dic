laboured:/ˈleɪbəd/ (UK) /ˈleɪbərd/ (US)| adj.|吃力的；费力的；不自然的；（文体）雕琢的|done with great effort and difficulty; strained or artificial|例句：His breathing was laboured and shallow. 他的呼吸很费力，很浅。|近义词：strained, difficult, arduous, forced, unnatural|反义词：easy, natural, effortless|词性：形容词
词源: From the verb "labour" + "-ed."  "Labour" comes from Old French "labourer," from Latin "laborare" meaning "to toil, work."
