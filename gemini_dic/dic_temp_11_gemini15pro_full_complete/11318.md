现在完成时 (Present Perfect):  'are' is not used in present perfect. It uses 'have' and 'has'.
They have been to Paris. = 他们去过巴黎。
We have finished our work. = 我们已经完成了工作。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous): 'are' is not used in present perfect continuous. It uses 'have been' and 'has been'.
