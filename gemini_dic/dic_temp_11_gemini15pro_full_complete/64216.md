equilibria:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriə/ /ˌɛkwɪˈlɪbriə/
n.
平衡；均衡
a state in which opposing forces or influences are balanced.
The government has to strike a delicate equilibrium between the interests of employers and employees. 政府必须在雇主和雇员的利益之间取得微妙的平衡。
