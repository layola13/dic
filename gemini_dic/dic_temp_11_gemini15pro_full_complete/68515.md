fanaticism:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəm/
n.
狂热; 盲信
Excessive enthusiasm or zeal; extreme devotion to a belief or cause.
His fanaticism for the cause led him to extreme measures.  他对这项事业的狂热使他采取了极端措施。

fanaticism:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəm/
n.
狂热，盲信
Excessive enthusiasm or zeal; extreme devotion to a belief or cause, often leading to intolerance of other views.
Religious fanaticism can be dangerous. 宗教狂热可能很危险。


近义词: extremism, zeal, bigotry, intolerance
反义词: moderation, tolerance, open-mindedness
词性: noun
