lectrice:/lɛkˈtris/| n. |女读者；女朗读者；（尤指大学的）女讲师|A woman who reads, especially one who reads aloud or gives lectures, particularly in a university setting.|例句：The lectrice captivated the audience with her expressive reading. 女朗读者用她富有表现力的朗读迷住了观众。

例句：The university appointed a new lectrice in French literature. 大学任命了一位新的法国文学女讲师。|近义词：reader, lecturer, instructor, speaker, presenter|反义词: listener, audience|noun
