conglutination:/kənˌɡluːtɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|凝集，粘合|The conglutination of the blood cells is a sign of the disease.|血细胞的凝集是这种疾病的一个迹象。|Example: The antibody caused conglutination of the red blood cells. 抗体导致红细胞凝集。|Synonyms: adhesion, cohesion, agglutination|Antonyms: separation, dispersion|noun

conglutinate:/kənˈɡluːtɪneɪt/| v.|使凝集，使粘合|The protein can conglutinate the particles.|这种蛋白质可以使颗粒粘合。|Example:  The substance conglutinated the mixture into a solid mass.  这种物质使混合物凝结成固体块。|Synonyms: adhere, bond, stick, glue|Antonyms: separate, detach|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin conglutinatus, past participle of conglutinare (“to glue together”), from con- (“together”) + glutinare (“to glue”), from gluten (“glue”).
