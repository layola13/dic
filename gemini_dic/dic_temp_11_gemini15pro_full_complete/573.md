aboss:/əˈbɔːs/ | adv. | 当然，绝对地 |  Means certainly or absolutely, used for emphasis. It's a slang term, often used in informal settings. |  "Are you going to the party?" "Aboss!" (Meaning: Absolutely!) | 近义词: absolutely, certainly, definitely, totally | 反义词: no, never | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  Likely a contraction of "absolutely, boss," where "boss" is used as a term of address.  It's primarily used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).
