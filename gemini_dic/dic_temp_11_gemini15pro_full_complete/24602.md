broider:/brɔɪdər/| v. |刺绣，绣花|To decorate cloth by sewing patterns on it with thread.|She broidered a beautiful design on the tablecloth. 她在一块桌布上绣了一个美丽的图案。|The dress was broidered with intricate floral patterns. 这件衣服绣有复杂的图案。|近义词：embroider, stitch, needlepoint|反义词: unravel|词性:动词
