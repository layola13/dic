globalism:/ˈɡloʊbəlɪzəm/ | n. |全球主义；全球化|The policy of treating the whole world as a single integrated unit, emphasizing the interdependence of nations.  It involves increased interconnectedness in trade, finance, culture, and communication.|例句：Globalization has led to increased trade between nations. 全球化导致国家间的贸易增加。

|例句：The impact of globalism is felt worldwide. 全球主义的影响在世界范围内都能感受到。|近义词：globalization, internationalism, universalism|反义词：nationalism, isolationism, localism|词性: noun
