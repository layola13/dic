baudrick:/ˈbɔːdrɪk/| n. | 腰带，绶带 | A baldric, also spelled baudrick, is a belt worn over one shoulder, which is typically used to carry a weapon (such as a sword) or other implement. | The knight wore a baudrick adorned with jewels. 骑士佩戴着一条饰有珠宝的绶带。 |  The king's baudrick was a symbol of his authority. 国王的绶带是他的权威象征。| Synonyms: baldric, sash, belt, girdle | Antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:
From Old French *baldrei, from a Germanic source, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *balþreiks.
