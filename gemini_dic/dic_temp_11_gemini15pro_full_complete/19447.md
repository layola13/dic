bicrenate:/baɪˈkriːneɪt/
adjective
双锯齿状的；重锯齿状的
Having a margin notched with concave curves, as the edge of a leaf.  A leaf margin with large rounded teeth, themselves with smaller teeth.
The bicrenate leaves of the elm tree are distinctive. 榆树的重锯齿状叶子很有特色。
The plant has bicrenate margins on its leaves.  这种植物的叶子上边缘呈重锯齿状。
近义词: doubly serrate, biserrate
反义词: entire, smooth
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *bi- (twice) + crenatus (notched, scalloped), past participle of crenare (to notch).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (to cut).
