alpha:/ˈælfə/| n. adj.|阿尔法, 开端, 首字母|The first letter of the Greek alphabet; used to represent the number one, or the first of a series.  Also used to describe the dominant individual in a group. |The alpha version of the software is still buggy.  这个软件的阿尔法版本仍然有很多bug。|近义词：first, beginning, leader, dominant|反义词:omega, last, subordinate|词性:名词, 形容词

/ˈæl.fə/ (美式发音)

词源: From the Greek letter "ἄλφα" (álpha), the first letter of the Greek alphabet. Ultimately from Phoenician "aleph", meaning "ox".
