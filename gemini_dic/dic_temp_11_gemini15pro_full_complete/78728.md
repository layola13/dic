gigglers:/ˈɡɪɡlɚz/| n. |咯咯笑的人；爱傻笑的人|People who giggle, especially habitually. |The room was filled with gigglers, enjoying the comedian's jokes. 房间里充满了咯咯笑的人，享受着喜剧演员的笑话。|  The gigglers in the back row were disrupting the class. 后排傻笑的人扰乱了课堂秩序。|近义词：laughers, chucklers|反义词: mourners, wailers|词性:noun (plural)
