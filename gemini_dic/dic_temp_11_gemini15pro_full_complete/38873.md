conferable:/kənˈfɜːrəbl/
adjective
可授予的；可赋予的
capable of being conferred or bestowed.
The honorary degree is conferable on anyone who has made a significant contribution to the field.  这个荣誉学位可以授予任何对该领域做出重大贡献的人。
This power is conferable by the committee. 这个权力是由委员会授予的.
Synonyms:  bestowable, grantable, awardable
Antonyms:  non-transferable, inalienable
词性: adjective
