bulrushy:/ˈbʊlˌrʌʃi/ | adj. |  芦苇丛生的，像芦苇的 | Resembling or full of bulrushes; growing among bulrushes. | The lake's edge was bulrushy and teeming with wildlife.  湖边芦苇丛生，野生动物众多。| The marshy area was thick and bulrushy. 这片沼泽地密密麻麻地长满了芦苇。 | synonyms: reedy, rushy, overgrown | antonyms: clear, open, bare | word type: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From bull + rush + -y.  "Bull" in this context likely refers to the large size or robust nature of the bulrush plant. "Rush" denotes a type of grass-like plant.  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
