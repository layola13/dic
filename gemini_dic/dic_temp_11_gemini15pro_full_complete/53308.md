disable:/dɪˈseɪbl/ (US), /dɪsˈeɪbl/ (UK)| vt. |使残废，使失去能力，使无效|To make someone unable to do something, or to make something stop working.|The accident disabled him from walking.  这起事故使他无法行走。| The virus disabled my computer. 病毒使我的电脑瘫痪了。 | synonyms: incapacitate, cripple, paralyze, deactivate | antonyms: enable, activate, empower | verb

disable:/dɪˈseɪbl/ (US), /dɪsˈeɪbl/ (UK)| adj. |残疾的，丧失能力的 |(of a person) having a physical or mental condition that limits their movements, senses, or activities. |The building has disabled access. 这栋楼有残疾人通道。| Many disabled people face discrimination in the workplace. 许多残疾人在工作场所面临歧视。 | synonyms: handicapped, impaired | antonyms: able-bodied, enabled | adjective
