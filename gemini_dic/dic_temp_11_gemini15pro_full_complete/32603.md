chiropractor: /ˈkaɪrəpræktər/ | n. | 脊椎按摩师 | A healthcare professional who specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of musculoskeletal disorders, particularly those of the spine, using manipulative therapy (adjustments). |  I visited a chiropractor for my back pain. 我去看脊椎按摩师治疗背痛。

My chiropractor recommended some exercises to strengthen my core muscles. 我的脊椎按摩师建议我做一些运动来加强核心肌肉。 | 近义词: osteopath, physical therapist | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a traditional physician who relies solely on medication) | 词性: noun
