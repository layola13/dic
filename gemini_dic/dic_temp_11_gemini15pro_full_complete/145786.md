overdebate:/ˌoʊvərdiˈbeɪt/ | v. | 过度辩论，过度讨论 | To debate or discuss something excessively, often to the point of exhaustion or futility. | The issue had been overdebated, and everyone was tired of talking about it.  这个问题已经被过度讨论了，每个人都厌倦了谈论它。| They overdebated the merits of the new policy, failing to reach a consensus. 他们过度讨论了新政策的优点，未能达成共识。 |  近义词：overdiscuss, belabor | 反义词: underdiscuss, skimp | 词性: 动词

overdebate:/ˌoʊvərdiˈbeɪt/ | n. | 过度辩论，过度讨论 | Excessive debate or discussion, often to the point of exhaustion or futility. |  The overdebate surrounding the topic led to no clear conclusion.  围绕这个话题的过度辩论并没有得出明确的结论。| There was so much overdebate that the original purpose of the meeting was lost.  有过多的辩论，以至于会议的最初目的都消失了。 | 近义词：overdiscussion | 反义词: underdiscussion | 词性: 名词
