cogue:/kəʊɡ/ | n. |小艇;一种小型渔船|A small fishing boat, typically one propelled by oars or sails.  It is a type of boat traditionally used in Scotland and Ireland.|The fishermen set out in their coques at dawn. 渔民们黎明时分乘着小艇出海了。|近义词：skiff, dinghy, rowboat|反义词:ship, yacht|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Scottish Gaelic and Irish *coga*, perhaps related to Middle Irish *cocán* "husk, shell, boat". Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *kūkos* "hook, bend".
