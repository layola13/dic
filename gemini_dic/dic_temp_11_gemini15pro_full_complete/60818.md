electroplater:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪtər/| n.|电镀工; 电镀器|A person or machine that electroplates objects.  A person or device that coats metal objects with a thin layer of another metal using electrolysis.|The electroplater carefully dipped the jewelry into the solution. 电镀工小心地将珠宝浸入溶液中。| The factory uses an automated electroplater. 这家工厂使用自动电镀机。|Synonyms: plater, galvanizer|Antonyms: N/A|noun

electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪt/| vt.  n.|电镀; 电镀品|To coat (a metal object) by electrolytic deposition with chromium, silver, gold, or another metal; a metal object plated in this way.|They electroplated the spoons with silver. 他们给勺子镀了银。| The antique teapot is an exquisite example of silver electroplate. 这把古董茶壶是一件精美的镀银制品。|Synonyms: plate, galvanize|Antonyms: strip, deplate|verb, noun
