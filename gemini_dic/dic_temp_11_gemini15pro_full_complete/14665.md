avowry:/əˈvaʊri/
n.|承认，公开声明；供认|An open declaration or acknowledgment; a confession.|He made an avowry of his guilt. 他承认了他的罪行。|Synonyms: acknowledgment, admission, confession, declaration, profession|Antonyms: denial, repudiation|noun

avowry:/əˈvaʊri/
n.|（法律）被告对原告指控的正式答辩| (Law) A formal answer by a defendant to a plaintiff's charges.|The defendant's avowry claimed ownership of the disputed property.  被告的答辩声称对争议财产的所有权。|Synonyms: plea, defense, answer|Antonyms: accusation, charge|noun
