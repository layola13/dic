mockney:/ˈmɒkni/ (UK) /ˈmɑːkni/ (US)| n. |模仿伦敦东区口音的人；伦敦东区口音|A mockney accent is an imitation of a working-class London accent, often adopted by people from other regions or social classes.|He adopted a mockney accent to try and fit in with the crowd. 他为了融入人群而模仿伦敦东区口音。|近义词: Estuary English (sometimes), pseudo-cockney|反义词: Received Pronunciation (RP)|词性:noun

mockney:/ˈmɒkni/ (UK) /ˈmɑːkni/ (US)| adj. |模仿伦敦东区口音的|Describing something or someone that imitates a working-class London accent.|Her mockney accent sounded very unconvincing. 她模仿的伦敦东区口音听起来很不自然。|近义词: Estuary (sometimes), pseudo-cockney|反义词: Received Pronunciation (RP), upper-class|词性:adjective
