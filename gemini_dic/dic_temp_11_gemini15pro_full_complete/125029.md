muji:/ˈmuːdʒiː/ (moo-jee)
n.
无印良品 (a Japanese retail company known for its minimalist aesthetic and no-brand goods)
A Japanese retail company known for its minimalist design and philosophy of "no brand" quality goods.
I bought a new notebook from Muji. 我从无印良品买了个新笔记本。
近义词:  minimalist brand, no-brand goods
反义词: branded goods
