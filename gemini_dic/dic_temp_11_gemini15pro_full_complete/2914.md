advisedness:/ədˈvaɪzɪdnəs/
n.
明智; 适当; 可取性
The quality of being advisable; wisdom or prudence.
The advisedness of this course of action is questionable.  这项行动的明智性值得怀疑。
近义词: prudence, wisdom, judiciousness, sagacity
反义词: foolishness, imprudence, recklessness, inadvisability
词性: 名词

词源:  From advise + -ed + -ness.  来自 advise (建议) + -ed (过去分词后缀，构成形容词) + -ness (名词后缀，表示状态或性质).
