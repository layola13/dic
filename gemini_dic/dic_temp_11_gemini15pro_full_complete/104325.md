jutlander:/ˈjʌtləndər/| n.|日德兰人|A native or inhabitant of Jutland, a peninsula in northern Europe comprising mainland Denmark and a small part of northern Germany.|Many Jutlanders are proud of their Viking heritage. 许多日德兰人以他们的维京人血统为荣。|The Jutlander spoke a dialect difficult for other Danes to understand.  这位日德兰人说一种其他丹麦人难以理解的方言。|近义词：Dane, Scandinavian|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider someone from a specific non-Jutland region, e.g.,  Zealander)|词性:noun
