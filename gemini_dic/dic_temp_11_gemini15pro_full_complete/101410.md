ipomoeic:/ˌɪpəˈmiːɪk/ | adj. | 旋花科的；与旋花科植物有关的 | Relating to or characteristic of the plant family Convolvulaceae (morning glories). | The botanist specialized in ipomoeic species.  这位植物学家专攻旋花科植物。|  The garden boasted a vibrant display of ipomoeic flowers.  花园里炫耀着充满活力的旋花科花卉。|  convolvulaceous | N/A | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Ipomoea, the name of a genus of plants in the Convolvulaceae family + -ic. *Ipomoea is derived from the Greek words ips (worm) and homoios (like), referring to the twining habit of some species.
