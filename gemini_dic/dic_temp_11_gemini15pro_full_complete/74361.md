frailest:/ˈfreɪlɪst/ | adj. | 最虚弱的，最脆弱的 |  Superlative form of "frail," meaning easily broken or damaged, physically weak, or delicate. |  The frailest of the kittens struggled to keep up with its siblings.  这只最虚弱的小猫努力跟上它的兄弟姐妹。| synonyms: weakest, feeblest, most delicate | antonyms: strongest, toughest, most robust | adjective

frail:/freɪl/ | adj. | 虚弱的，脆弱的 | Easily broken or damaged, physically weak, or delicate. | The vase was so frail that it broke when I bumped the table.  这花瓶太易碎了，我一碰到桌子它就破了。 | synonyms: weak, delicate, fragile | antonyms: strong, robust, sturdy | adjective
