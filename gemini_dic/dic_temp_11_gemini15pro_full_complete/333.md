abominable:/əˈbɒmɪnəbl/ (US) /əˈbɑːmɪnəbl/ (UK)| adj.|令人憎恶的；极坏的；糟糕的|Inspiring disgust and loathing; extremely unpleasant and causing disgust|The weather was abominable, with strong winds and heavy rain. 天气非常糟糕，狂风暴雨。|The abominable snowman is a legendary creature said to inhabit the Himalayas.  传说中的雪人是一种据说居住在喜马拉雅山的生物。|近义词：detestable, loathsome, abhorrent, dreadful, awful, terrible|反义词：pleasant, delightful, agreeable, wonderful, excellent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *abōminābilis, from Latin abōminor (“I deprecate as ominous, I shudder at, I detest”), from ab- (“from, away from”) + ōmen, ōminis (“omen, portent, foreboding”), from the Proto-Indo-European *h₃ṓsmen (“omen”).
