antipatriarchies:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriɑːrkiːz/
|n.|反父权制；反父权主义|Systems or movements that oppose or challenge patriarchy, a social system in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege and control of property.|Many feminist movements are built upon antipatriarchies, seeking to dismantle systems of male dominance.|近义词:feminism, gender equality, women's liberation|反义词:patriarchy, male dominance|词性:名词
