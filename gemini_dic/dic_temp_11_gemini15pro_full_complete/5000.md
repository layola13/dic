all-a-heap:/ˌɔːl ə ˈhiːp/ | adj. | 乱七八糟的，凌乱的 | in a state of disarray; messy and disorganized | The children left the toys all-a-heap in the living room. 孩子们把玩具乱七八糟地堆在客厅里。|  The room was all-a-heap after the party. 派对结束后，房间里乱七八糟的。| synonyms: messy, disordered, disheveled, chaotic, cluttered, jumbled | antonyms: tidy, neat, organized, orderly | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "all" + "a" + "heap," meaning entirely in a heap.  The "a" is an archaic preposition meaning "in" or "on."
