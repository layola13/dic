demi-devil:/ˈdɛmiˌdɛvəl/| n.|半恶魔，小恶魔|A being that is partly devilish; a minor devil.  A person who is mischievous but not truly evil. |He acted like a demi-devil, playing pranks but never causing real harm. 他表现得像个小恶魔，搞恶作剧，但从未造成真正的伤害。|Synonyms: imp, rascal, scamp|Antonyms: angel, saint|Noun
