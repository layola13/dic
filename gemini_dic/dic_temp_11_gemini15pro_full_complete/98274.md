insessor:/ɪnˈsɛsər/ | n. |栖木鸟，尤指雀形目鸟类|A bird, especially a passerine, that perches.|The robin is a common insessor in gardens. 知更鸟是花园里常见的栖木鸟。| 近义词: percher, passerine | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑非雀形目鸟类，例如 waterfowl 水鸟) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *insessor*, from *insidēre* "to sit upon," from *in* "on" + *sedēre* "to sit."
