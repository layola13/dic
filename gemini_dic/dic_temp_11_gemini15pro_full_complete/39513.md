conoidical:/kəˈnɔɪdɪk(ə)l/
adj.
圆锥形的；近似圆锥形的
Resembling a cone; nearly conical.
The shell has a conoidical shape.  这个贝壳呈圆锥形。
近义词: conical, cone-shaped
反义词: planar, flat
词性: adjective
