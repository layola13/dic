big:/bɪɡ/ | adj. |大的，重要的，重大的|Large in size, extent, or importance.|例句：He has a big house. 他有一所大房子。|例句：That was a big mistake. 那是一个很大的错误。|近义词：large, huge, enormous, great, significant|反义词：small, little, tiny, insignificant|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *bigge, *bigg, from Old Norse *biggr (“strong, weighty, important”), from Proto-Germanic *bikaz (“sharp, pointed, biting”). Cognate with Norwegian Bokmål bigg (“barley”), Nynorsk bygg (“barley”), Icelandic bygg (“barley”), Swedish bjugg (“emmer, two-grained wheat”), Middle Low German bigge (“barley”). More at bigg.
