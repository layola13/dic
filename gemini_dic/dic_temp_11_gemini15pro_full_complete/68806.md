fasciation:/ˌfæʃiˈeɪʃən/ |n.|簇生，带化|The abnormal flattening or coalescence of plant stems, often producing a crested or ribbon-like appearance.  植物茎的异常扁平化或合并，通常产生鸡冠状或带状外观。|The cactus displayed a striking fasciation, with its stem flattened into a fan shape.  这株仙人掌呈现出惊人的簇生现象，茎部扁平如扇形。|Synonyms: cresting, banding|Antonyms: normal growth|noun

fasciation:/ˌfæʃiˈeɪʃən/ |n.|迷恋，强烈兴趣|An intense interest or fascination with something. 对某事物的强烈兴趣或迷恋。|He had a fasciation with trains since childhood. 他从小就对火车着迷。|Synonyms: obsession, captivation, infatuation|Antonyms: apathy, indifference|noun
