iconoclastic: /ˌaɪkɑːnəˈklæstɪk/ | adj. |打破传统的，反传统的|Characterized by attack on cherished beliefs or institutions.|例句：His iconoclastic views on education have caused a great deal of controversy. 他对教育的打破常规的观点引起了很大的争议。

例句：The artist's iconoclastic sculptures challenged traditional notions of beauty.  这位艺术家的反传统的雕塑挑战了传统的审美观念。

近义词：rebellious, unorthodox, heretical, nonconformist, dissident |反义词:  conventional, orthodox, traditional |词性: adjective
