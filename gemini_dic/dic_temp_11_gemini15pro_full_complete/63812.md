epidermical:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪkəl/ | adj. |表皮的；肤浅的|Of or relating to the epidermis (the outer layer of skin). Also, superficial; not deep or thorough.|The burn was only epidermical, not affecting the deeper layers of skin.  烧伤只是表皮的，没有伤及皮肤深层。|例句：The cut was only epidermical and healed quickly.  这伤口只是表皮的，很快就痊愈了。|近义词：superficial, cutaneous|反义词：deep, profound|词性:形容词
epidermic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. |表皮的|Of or relating to the epidermis (the outer layer of skin).|The epidermic cells protect the body from external elements. 表皮细胞保护身体免受外部因素的侵害。|例句：Epidermic lotions can soothe dry skin. 表皮乳液可以舒缓干燥的皮肤。|近义词：cutaneous, superficial|反义词：deep, internal|词性:形容词
