fib:/fɪb/| n. vi.|谎言；无稽之谈|A lie, typically an unimportant one.|She told a little fib about her age.|近义词：lie, falsehood, untruth|反义词：truth|词性:名词,动词

fib:/fɪb/| vi.|撒小谎；瞎扯|Tell an unimportant lie.|He fibbed about his age to get into the club.|近义词：lie, prevaricate|反义词：tell the truth|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the early 19th century, of unknown origin. Possibly a shortened form of *fable*, or related to *fibble-fable* (nonsense).
