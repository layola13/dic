deciduous: /dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/
| adj. | 落叶的；脱落的；非永久性的 | Shedding foliage at the end of the growing season.  |  The oak is a deciduous tree. 橡树是一种落叶树。|  Falling off or shed seasonally or at a certain stage of development.  The deer's antlers are deciduous. 鹿角是会脱落的。 | synonyms:  temporary, ephemeral, transient | antonyms: evergreen, persistent, perennial | word type: adjective
