adamite:/ˈæd.əˌmaɪt/| n.|硅锌矿; 亚当派教徒|A mineral consisting of basic zinc arsenate, occurring naturally as orthorhombic crystals.|The geologists discovered a vein of adamite in the mine. 地质学家在矿井中发现了一条硅锌矿矿脉。|Synonyms: mineral, ore|Antonyms: N/A|noun
adamite:/ˈæd.əˌmaɪt/| n.|亚当派教徒 (指15世纪波西米亚的一个激进的宗教派别，主张恢复人类原初的纯真状态，有时会采取极端行为)|A member of a radical religious sect in 15th-century Bohemia that advocated a return to the state of primeval innocence, sometimes engaging in extreme practices.|Historical accounts suggest some adamites practiced nudism. 历史记载表明，一些亚当派教徒奉行裸体主义。|Synonyms: heretic, sectarian|Antonyms: conformist|noun
