coauthoring:/ˌkoʊˈɔːθərɪŋ/| n. |共同创作，合著|The act of writing something together with one or more other people. |例句：Coauthoring a book can be a rewarding experience.  合著一本书可以是一次有益的经历。

例句：They are coauthoring a research paper on climate change. 他们正在合作撰写一篇关于气候变化的研究论文。|近义词：collaborating, joint writing, co-writing|反义词:sole authorship|词性:noun (gerund)

coauthor:/ˌkoʊˈɔːθər/| n. |共同作者，合著者|A person who writes something with one or more other people. |例句：She was a coauthor of the study. 她是这项研究的合著者之一。

例句：He is looking for a coauthor for his next novel. 他正在为他的下一部小说寻找一位合著者。|近义词：collaborator, joint author, co-writer|反义词:sole author|词性:noun

co-author:/ˌkoʊˈɔːθər/| v. |共同创作，合著|To write something together with one or more other people. |例句：They co-authored a groundbreaking paper on artificial intelligence. 他们共同撰写了一篇关于人工智能的开创性论文。

例句：We are co-authoring a report on the current economic situation. 我们正在共同撰写一份关于当前经济形势的报告。|近义词：collaborate on, jointly write, co-write|反义词:write alone|词性:verb
