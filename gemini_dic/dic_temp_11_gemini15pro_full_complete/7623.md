annuitize:/əˈnuːɪtaɪz/ | v. |  使年金化，将（资本）转化为年金| To convert (capital) into a fixed income paid yearly for the rest of a person's life or other specified period. |  He decided to annuitize his lottery winnings.  他决定把他的彩票奖金变成年金。| synonyms: amortize | antonyms: capitalize | verb

annuitization:/əˌnuːɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 年金化 | The process of converting an asset or lump sum into a series of regular income payments. | The annuitization of his savings provided him with a steady income stream.  他的储蓄年金化为他提供了稳定的收入来源。| synonyms: amortization | antonyms: capitalization | noun
