out-fencing: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnsɪŋ/
|v.|胜过；以击剑胜过|To surpass or defeat someone in fencing, or metaphorically, in any kind of competition or debate, especially through skill or cleverness.|The young fencer out-fenced his opponent with a series of swift and precise moves.  年轻的击剑运动员以一系列快速而精准的动作战胜了对手。

| | |He out-fenced her in the negotiations, securing a better deal for his company. 他在谈判中技高一筹，为公司争取到了更有利的协议。|近义词：outmaneuver, outwit, surpass, defeat|反义词：lose to, be defeated by|词性：动词
