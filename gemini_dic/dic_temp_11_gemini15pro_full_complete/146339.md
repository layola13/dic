fervent: /ˈfɜːrvənt/ | adj. | 热情的，热烈的 | Having or displaying a passionate intensity. | She has a fervent belief in social justice. 她对社会正义有着热烈的信仰。 | He delivered a fervent speech about the importance of education. 他发表了关于教育重要性的热情洋溢的演讲。 | 近义词:  passionate, ardent, zealous | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, cold | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin fervent-, fervens, present participle of fervēre "to boil, glow," figuratively "to be excited, eager."  经由晚期拉丁语 fervent-, fervens，是 fervēre “沸腾，发光”，比喻义为“兴奋，渴望”的现在分词。
