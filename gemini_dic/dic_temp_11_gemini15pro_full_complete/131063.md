nonbarbarous:/ˌnɒnbɑːrˈbɛərəs/
adjective
非野蛮的；文明的
Not barbarous; civilized.
The nonbarbarous societies developed complex systems of governance.  非野蛮社会发展出了复杂的治理体系。
Ancient Greece, though a warrior society, fostered many nonbarbarous pursuits like philosophy and the arts. 古希腊虽然是一个尚武的社会，但也培养了许多非野蛮的追求，如哲学和艺术。
synonyms: civilized, cultured, refined, sophisticated
antonyms: barbarous, savage, uncivilized, barbaric
词性: 形容词
