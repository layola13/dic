gove: /ɡoʊv/ | n. | (dialectal, chiefly Scotland) A blacksmith or a forge. | A gove is a traditional blacksmith's workshop, especially in Scotland.  | The old gove stood at the edge of the village. 村里的老铁匠铺位于村庄的边缘。|  The blacksmith worked tirelessly in his gove. 铁匠在他的铁匠铺里不知疲倦地工作。| 近义词: smithy, forge | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Scots <i>goif</i>, <i>goiff</i>, <i>gove</i> (“a forge”), from Old Norse <i>gof</i>, <i>goǫf</i> (“a smithy”).  Related to Old English <i>ġeaf</i>, <i>ġiefu</i> (“gift, giving”).
