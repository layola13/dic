cocktail:/ˈkɒkteɪl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkˌteɪl/ (US)| n.|鸡尾酒；混合物；混合饮料|An alcoholic drink consisting of a spirit or spirits mixed with other ingredients, such as fruit juice, soda water, or cream.|例句：I ordered a cocktail at the bar. 我在酒吧点了一杯鸡尾酒。|例句：This situation is a cocktail of different problems.  这种情况是各种问题的混合体。|近义词：mixed drink, beverage|反义词:neat drink (纯饮)|词性:noun
