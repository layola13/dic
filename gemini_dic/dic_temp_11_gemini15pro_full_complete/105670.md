kingmaking:/ˈkɪŋˌmeɪkɪŋ/
noun
立王者，拥立君主；操纵政局
The act of influencing the succession to a throne or other powerful position.
The prime minister resigned, triggering a period of intense kingmaking within the ruling party.  首相辞职，引发了执政党内部激烈的权力斗争。
synonyms: power broking, political maneuvering, string-pulling, behind-the-scenes manipulation
antonyms:  powerlessness, impotence, submission, compliance
词性: 名词

kingmakings:/ˈkɪŋˌmeɪkɪŋz/
noun
立王者（复数）；操纵政局（复数）
Plural form of "kingmaking," referring to multiple instances of influencing successions or power dynamics.
The history of the kingdom is rife with stories of kingmakings and betrayals.  这个王国的历史充满了拥立君主和背叛的故事。
synonyms: power plays, political machinations
antonyms: democratic processes, fair elections
词性: 名词
