lose-it:/ˈluːz ɪt/| v.|失去控制；情绪失控；发脾气|To suddenly become unable to control your emotions and get angry, upset, or start to laugh.|例句：When she told me what he’d done, I just lost it. 当她告诉我他做了什么时，我一下子就崩溃了。|近义词：freak out, go crazy, lose control, snap|反义词：calm down, stay calm|词性：动词

lose-it:/ˈluːz ɪt/| n.|精神崩溃；情绪失控|An instance of losing control of one's emotions.|例句：He had a complete lose-it when he found out his car had been stolen. 当他发现他的车被偷了时，他彻底崩溃了。|近义词：meltdown, breakdown|反义词：composure, self-control|词性：名词
