outspending:/ˌaʊtˈspɛndɪŋ/
verb
超过…的支出, 花费超过
spending more than (someone or something else).
The Republican candidate outspent his Democratic rival by a significant margin. 共和党候选人的花费明显超过了他的民主党竞争对手。
近义词：outlay, surpass in spending
反义词：underspending
词性: v.


outspending:/ˌaʊtˈspɛndɪŋ/
present participle
超过…的支出, 花费超过
currently spending more than (someone or something else).
The company is outspending its competitors on research and development. 该公司在研发上的支出超过了竞争对手。
近义词：exceeding in expenditure, surpassing in spending
反义词：underspending
词性: v.
