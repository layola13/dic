detainees:/ˌdiːteɪˈniːz/
noun
被拘留者，扣押者
A person who is held in custody, especially by the police or military.
The detainees were held without charge for several weeks.  被拘留者们在没有受到指控的情况下被关押了几个星期。
The Red Cross visited the detainees to ensure their humane treatment. 红十字会探访了被拘留者，以确保他们受到人道待遇。
