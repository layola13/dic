bountyless:/ˈbaʊnti.ləs/
adjective
没有赏金的，没有报酬的，贫瘠的
Lacking bounty; without reward or payment; unproductive.
The bountyless desert offered little sustenance to the travelers.  这片贫瘠的沙漠几乎无法给旅行者提供食物。
The bountyless hunt left the trappers empty-handed and discouraged. 这次没有收获的狩猎让捕兽者空手而归，灰心丧气。
Synonyms: unrewarding, barren, unproductive, sterile, meager, scanty
Antonyms: bountiful, rewarding, productive, fertile, abundant
