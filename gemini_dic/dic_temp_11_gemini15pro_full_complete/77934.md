genocide:/ˈdʒɛnəˌsaɪd/ | n. |种族灭绝|The deliberate and systematic destruction of a racial, political, or cultural group.|The Rwandan genocide of 1994 was a horrific event. 1994年的卢旺达大屠杀是一起骇人听闻的事件。| 近义词:massacre, extermination, ethnic cleansing | 反义词:preservation, protection | 词性:名词

Etymology: From Greek *genos* (race, kind) + Latin *-cidium* (killing, cutting).
