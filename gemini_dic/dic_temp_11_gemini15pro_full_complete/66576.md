exhilaration:/ɪɡˌzɪləˈreɪʃn/| n. |兴奋， exhilaration;  欣喜;  欢欣;  高兴|A feeling of extreme happiness and excitement.|例句：She felt a sense of exhilaration as she reached the mountaintop. 她到达山顶时感到一阵兴奋。

例句：The exhilaration of victory filled the stadium. 胜利的喜悦充满了体育场。|近义词：elation, euphoria, joy, excitement, thrill|反义词：despair, depression, gloom, sadness, boredom|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exhilaratio, from exhilaratus, past participle of exhilarare (“to gladden, cheer”), from ex- (“out, from”) + hilarare (“to make cheerful”), from hilaris (“cheerful”).
