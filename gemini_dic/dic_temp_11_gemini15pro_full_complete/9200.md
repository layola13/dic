antioptimistically:/ˌæntiˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪkli/
| adv. |反乐观地，悲观地|In a manner lacking optimism; pessimistically.  Characterized by a lack of hope or expectation of a favorable outcome.|He antioptimistically predicted the company's downfall, despite the positive market trends. 他悲观地预测了公司的倒闭，尽管市场趋势积极。|Near Synonyms: pessimistically, gloomily, negatively, cynically|Near Antonyms: optimistically, hopefully, positively|adverb
