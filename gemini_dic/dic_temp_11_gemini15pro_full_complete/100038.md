interplacental:/ˌɪntərpləˈsɛntəl/
adjective
胎盘间的
Located or occurring between placentas, especially in cases of multiple pregnancies.
Example: Interplacental bridging vessels can occur in monochorionic twin pregnancies.  单绒毛膜双胞胎妊娠中可能出现胎盘间桥联血管。

Example: Interplacental transfer of nutrients can sometimes occur in multiple pregnancies.  在多胎妊娠中，有时会发生胎盘间的营养物质转移。
Synonym: interchorionic
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider "intra-placental" as an opposite concept relating to within a single placenta)
词性: adjective
