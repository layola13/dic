nonassonance:/nɒnˈæsənəns/
noun
不谐和，不协调
Lack of assonance; absence of vowel rhyme.  The intentional avoidance of similar vowel sounds within a passage of text, especially in poetry.
Example: The poet deliberately employed nonassonance to create a jarring effect.  诗人特意采用不谐和音来制造刺耳的效果。
Example: The nonassonance in the verse added a sense of unease.  诗句中的不谐和音增添了一种不安的感觉。
Near Synonyms: dissonance, discord, disharmony
Antonyms: assonance, consonance, harmony
