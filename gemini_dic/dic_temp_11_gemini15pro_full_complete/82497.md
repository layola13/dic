grutch:/ɡrʌtʃ/ | n. v. |怨恨，不满；抱怨，发牢骚 |A feeling of resentment or ill will. To complain or grumble.| He held a grutch against his brother for years.  他多年来一直对他的兄弟怀恨在心。 |  She grudged having to pay so much for the tickets. 她抱怨买票太贵了。 | synonyms: resentment, grievance, complaint, grumble | antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, acceptance | noun, verb
