evasiveness:/ɪˈveɪsɪvnəs/ |n.|逃避；推诿；含糊其辞|The evasiveness of his answers made her suspicious. 他的回答闪烁其词，让她起了疑心。|例句：His evasiveness only increased their suspicions. 他的逃避只会增加他们的怀疑。

例句：The politician's evasiveness on the issue frustrated the reporters.  这位政客在这个问题上的含糊其辞让记者们感到沮丧。

近义词：elusiveness, ambiguity, vagueness|反义词：directness, frankness, candor|词性:名词

词源: Late 18th century. From *evasive* + *-ness*.  *Evasive* comes from French *évasif*, from *évader* 'to evade'. Ultimately from Latin *evadere*, from *e-* (variant of *ex-*) 'out' + *vadere* 'to go'.
