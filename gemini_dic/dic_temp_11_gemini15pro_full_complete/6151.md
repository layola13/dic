amid:/əˈmɪd/| prep.|在…中间，在…之中，被…围绕|surrounded by; in the middle of.|例句：He stood amid the cheering crowd. 他站在欢呼的人群中。|例句：The hotel is situated amid beautiful scenery. 这家酒店坐落在美丽的风景之中。|近义词：among, amongst, in the middle of, surrounded by|反义词：outside, beyond|词性：介词

词源: From Middle English *amiddes, from Old English *on middan, from on (on) + middan (middle).
