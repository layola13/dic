cartel:/kɑːrˈtel/ /kɑːrˈtɛl/| n. |卡特尔，同业联盟，垄断联盟|An association of manufacturers or suppliers with the purpose of maintaining prices at a high level and restricting competition.|The drug cartel controlled the flow of cocaine into the country.  这个贩毒集团控制着可卡因流入该国。|Example: The OPEC cartel is a group of oil-producing nations.  欧佩克卡特尔是由石油生产国组成的集团。|Synonyms: syndicate, combine, coalition, alliance|Antonyms: competition, free market|noun
