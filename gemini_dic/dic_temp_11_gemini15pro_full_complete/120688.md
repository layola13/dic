misadventure:/ˌmɪsədˈvɛntʃər/ | n. |不幸的遭遇，灾难|An unfortunate or unlucky accident; a mishap. |例句：Their camping trip was filled with misadventure, from a flat tire to a torrential downpour. 他们的露营之旅充满了不幸，从爆胎到倾盆大雨。

例句：He recounted the misadventures of his youth with a wry smile. 他苦笑地讲述了他年轻时的不幸遭遇。|近义词：mishap, misfortune, accident, calamity|反义词：fortune, luck, success|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle French *mesaventure*, from mes- "badly, wrongly" (from Latin minus "less") + aventure "adventure".
