herpe:/ˈhɜːrpiː/| n.|疱疹|A viral infection causing small painful blisters and inflammation, most commonly at the junction of skin and mucous membrane in the mouth or nose or in the genitals.|She contracted genital herpes. 她感染了生殖器疱疹。|Synonyms: cold sore, fever blister|Antonyms: None|Noun
