hullabaloo:/ˈhʌləbəˌluː/| n. |喧闹，骚动，吵闹|a loud confused noise, especially one caused by a large mass of people. |例句：There was a tremendous hullabaloo outside. 外面一片喧嚣。

例句：The media created a hullabaloo about the incident. 媒体对这一事件大肆渲染。


|近义词：commotion, uproar, racket, din, clamor |反义词: quiet, silence, peace, calm |词性: noun
