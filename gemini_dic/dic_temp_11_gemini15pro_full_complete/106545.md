krokidolite:/kroʊˈkɪdəˌlaɪt/| n. |蓝石棉，青石棉|A fibrous form of riebeckite (a sodium-rich amphibole) that crystallizes in the monoclinic system. It is a type of asbestos.  It is also known as blue asbestos. |Example 1: Krokidolite is a hazardous material due to its fibrous nature.  蓝石棉由于其纤维状性质是一种危险材料。

Example 2:  Exposure to krokidolite can cause mesothelioma, a type of lung cancer. 接触蓝石棉会导致间皮瘤，一种肺癌。|近义词：blue asbestos, riebeckite asbestos|反义词: None (no direct antonym for a specific mineral)|词性:noun
