anticorporately:/ˌæntiˈkɔːrpərətli/
adverb
反公司地；反对公司化地
In a manner that opposes or resists the influence or power of corporations.  Acting in a way that challenges or undermines the interests or practices of large companies.
The activist group campaigned anticorporately, advocating for small businesses and local communities.  这个激进组织开展了反公司化运动，倡导支持小型企业和当地社区。
The documentary exposed the company's practices, arguing anticorporately that they were harming the environment. 这部纪录片揭露了该公司的做法，并以反公司化的立场辩称，这些做法正在损害环境。
近义词: anti-business, anti-establishment
反义词: pro-corporate, pro-business
词性: adverb
