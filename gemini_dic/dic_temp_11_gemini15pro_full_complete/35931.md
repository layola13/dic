cocurator:/koʊˈkjʊreɪtər/| n.|共同策展人|A person who shares the responsibility of curating an exhibition or collection with another or others.|例句：The cocurators worked together seamlessly on the new exhibition. 共同策展人在新展览上无缝合作。

例句：She and her cocurator brought a diverse range of perspectives to the project. 她和她的共同策展人为这个项目带来了各种各样的视角。|近义词：joint curator, co-organizer|反义词:|noun
