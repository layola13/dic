annicut:/ˈænɪkʌt/| n.|水坝，拦河坝，堰|A low dam built across a stream for irrigation purposes.|The farmers built an annicut to divert water to their fields. 农民们建造了一个拦河坝，将水引到他们的田地里。|近义词：dam, weir, barrage|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 breach (决口))|词性:noun
Etymology: From Tamil  *āṇai-kattu*  "elephant embankment."
