overinflation:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃən/ | n. | 过度膨胀；通货膨胀过度 |A situation where too much money is chasing too few goods, leading to a rapid increase in prices.|  The overinflation of the housing market led to a crash. 房地产市场的过度膨胀导致了崩盘。| Overinflation can erode the value of savings.  通货膨胀过度会削弱储蓄的价值。| synonyms: hyperinflation, excessive inflation | antonyms: deflation, disinflation | noun
