heebie-jeebies:/ˈhiːbiː ˈdʒiːbiːz/| n.|神经过敏；紧张不安；焦躁情绪|A feeling of nervousness, apprehension, or anxiety.|例句：Being alone in the dark gives me the heebie-jeebies. 独自一人在黑暗中让我感到紧张不安。

例句：I get the heebie-jeebies just thinking about public speaking.  一想到要公开演讲，我就感到紧张不安。|近义词：jitters, creeps, willies|反义词:calmness, composure|词性:名词

词源：The origin of "heebie-jeebies" is uncertain, but it's thought to have originated in American slang in the early 20th century.  Some speculate a connection to the Yiddish word "hibist," meaning "epileptic," but this is unconfirmed. The reduplicated form emphasizes the feeling of unease.
