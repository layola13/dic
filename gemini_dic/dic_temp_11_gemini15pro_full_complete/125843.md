murza:/ˈmʊərzə/| n.|穆尔扎，王子，贵族（特指鞑靼或其他突厥贵族）|A nobleman or prince, especially a Tatar or other Turkic noble.  Historically, a Russian noble title conferred on Tatar princes who entered the service of the tsars.|Ivan, a Murza, was known for his lavish parties. 伊凡，一个穆尔扎，以其奢华的派对而闻名。|Synonyms: prince, nobleman, lord, emir|Antonyms: commoner, peasant|noun
