grasping:/ˈɡræspɪŋ/ /ˈɡræːspɪŋ/| n.|抓取，理解|The act of seizing and holding firmly; comprehension.|例句：The grasping of the concept was difficult for the students.  学生们很难理解这个概念。|近义词：grip, understanding, comprehension|反义词：release, misunderstanding|noun
