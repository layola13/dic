antigalastic:/ˌæntiɡəˈlæstɪk/ | adj. | 防止笑的，止笑的 | Preventing or inhibiting laughter. |  The doctor prescribed an antigalastic medication to help control the patient's inappropriate laughter. 医生给病人开了止笑的药，以控制他不合时宜的笑。 |  
antigalastic:/ˌæntiɡəˈlæstɪk/ | n. | 止笑药，止笑剂 | A medicine or agent that prevents or inhibits laughter.  | The antigalastic proved effective in reducing the frequency of his laughing fits. 这种止笑剂有效地减少了他发笑的频率。 | synonyms: laughter-suppressing, anti-laughing | antonyms: laughter-inducing, hilarious | adjective/noun
