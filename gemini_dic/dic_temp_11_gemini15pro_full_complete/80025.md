go-to-bat-for:/ˌɡoʊ tə ˈbæt fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 替...辩护，支持，为...出力 | To support or defend someone, especially in a difficult situation. |  My boss really went to bat for me when the client complained. 我的老板在客户投诉时真的为我辩护。

| My lawyer went to bat for me and got the charges dropped. 我的律师为我辩护，撤销了指控。 | 近义词: support, defend, advocate for, stand up for | 反义词: oppose, undermine, betray | 词性: 动词短语
