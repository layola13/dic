nonparalyses:/ˌnɒnˌpəˈrælɪsiːz/
noun
非麻痹; 不瘫痪
The condition of not being paralyzed.  Absence of paralysis.
The doctor confirmed the patient's nonparalysis after the accident. 医生确认病人事故后没有瘫痪。
The new treatment aims for nonparalysis in stroke victims.  这种新疗法旨在使中风患者不瘫痪。
近义词:  mobility, function, movement
反义词: paralysis, palsy
词性: noun


nonparalytic:/ˌnɒnˌpærəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
非麻痹性的；不瘫痪的
Not causing or characterized by paralysis.
The injury was thankfully nonparalytic.  谢天谢地，这次受伤没有导致瘫痪。
They developed a nonparalytic strain of the virus. 他们研发出了一种不会导致瘫痪的病毒株。
近义词: mobile, functional
反义词: paralytic
词性: adjective

nonparalyzed:/ˌnɒnˈpærəlaɪzd/
adjective
未瘫痪的
Not paralyzed; able to move.
He was fortunate to emerge from the accident nonparalyzed. 他很幸运地从事故中幸存下来，没有瘫痪。
The patient's nonparalyzed limbs responded well to physiotherapy. 病人未瘫痪的肢体对物理治疗反应良好。
近义词:  mobile, functional, unimpaired
反义词: paralyzed
词性: adjective
