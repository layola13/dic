oppugnancy:/ˌɒpəɡˈnənsi/ /ˌɑːpəɡˈnənsi/| n. |反对，对抗，质疑|The state of being oppugnant; opposition; resistance.  The act of oppugning; gainsaying. |His oppugnancy to the new policy was clear from his outspoken criticism. 他对新政策的反对从他直言不讳的批评中可以清楚地看出。|Near synonyms: opposition, resistance, antagonism, hostility, defiance. |Near antonyms: agreement, acceptance, compliance, support, advocacy.|noun

oppugn:/əˈpjuːn/ /əˈpjuːn/| vt. |反对；质疑；攻击；抗辩|To call in question; to oppose with arguments; to resist; to attack as false or erroneous. |Several scientists have oppugned the theory. 几位科学家质疑了这一理论。 |Near synonyms: challenge, dispute, question, contradict, oppose. |Near antonyms: support, defend, uphold, endorse, advocate.|verb
