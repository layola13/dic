carload:/ˈkɑːrloʊd/ (US) /ˈkɑːləʊd/ (UK)| n. |一车（货物）; 满载一车之量|A quantity of something that fills a railroad freight car or a large truck.|The farmer brought a carload of watermelons to market.|shipment, truckload|少量|noun
Etymology->
From car + load.  "Car" in this context originally referred specifically to a railway car.
