authorism: /ˈɔːθəˌrɪzəm/
n. | 权威主义，专制主义 | The belief in or practice of government “from above,” in which authority is concentrated in a few rulers who demand unquestioning obedience from the masses.  |  The country suffered under years of authoritarianism. 这个国家遭受了多年的专制统治。| 近义词: absolutism, autocracy, despotism, dictatorship, totalitarianism | 反义词: democracy, liberalism, libertarianism | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French *autoritarisme*, from *autoritaire* "authoritarian," ultimately from Latin *auctoritas* "authority."
