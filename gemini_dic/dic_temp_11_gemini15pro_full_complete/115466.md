manufactural:/ˌmænjəˈfæktʃərəl/
adjective
制造的；工厂生产的
Relating to manufacturing or manufactured goods.
The report detailed the manufactural processes of the company.  这份报告详细介绍了该公司的制造工艺。
The city's economy is heavily reliant on its manufactural industries.  这座城市的经济严重依赖其制造业。
近义词: manufactured, industrial, factory-made
反义词: handmade, artisanal, homemade
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From manufacture + -al.  Manufacture itself comes from French *manufacturer*, from Latin *manu factura* "making by hand," from *manus* "hand" + *factura* "a making."
