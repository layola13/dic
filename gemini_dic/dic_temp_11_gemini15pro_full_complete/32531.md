chip:/tʃɪp/| n. v.|碎片，芯片，炸薯条，筹码|A small piece of something removed in the course of chopping, cutting, or breaking a hard material, such as wood or stone.  A thin slice of potato fried crisp and eaten as a snack. A small piece of semiconductor material used to make an integrated circuit.|He cut his hand on a chip of wood. 他被一块木屑割伤了手。|近义词：fragment, shard, sliver, microchip, counter, token|反义词: whole|noun
chip:/tʃɪp/| v.|削，凿，碎裂|To cut or break a small piece from a hard material. To damage something by breaking a small piece off it.|He chipped a piece of wood off the table. 他从桌子上削掉了一块木头。|近义词：break, crack, snip|反义词: repair|verb
