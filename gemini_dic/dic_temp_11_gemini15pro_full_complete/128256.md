neuromancer:/ˈnʊəroʊˌmænsər/ | n. | 神经漫游者；赛博空间牛仔 | A person who uses a direct neural interface to navigate and manipulate data in cyberspace.  A fictional archetype popularized by William Gibson's novel *Neuromancer*. |  He envisioned himself as a kind of digital neuromancer, jacking into the matrix of the city.  他把自己想象成某种数字神经漫游者，闯入城市的数据矩阵。 | Synonyms: hacker, cyberjock, console cowboy | Antonyms: None (this is a relatively recent and specific term) | Noun
