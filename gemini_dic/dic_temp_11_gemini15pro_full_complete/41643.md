correctives:/kəˈrɛktɪvz/ | n. | 纠正物；矫正剂；矫正措施 | Something that corrects or counteracts something undesirable. |  The teacher suggested some correctives for the student's poor writing skills. 老师针对学生糟糕的写作技巧提出了一些矫正方法。 | synonyms: remedies, solutions, adjustments, improvements | antonyms: aggravations, worsenings | noun

corrective:/kəˈrɛktɪv/ | adj. | 纠正的；矫正的；惩戒性的 | Intended to correct or counteract something undesirable. |  He was given corrective lenses to improve his vision. 他被配了矫正镜片来改善视力。 | synonyms: remedial, restorative, reformative, disciplinary | antonyms: damaging, worsening | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *correctivus, from Latin correct-, past participle stem of corrigere "to correct," from com- "together" (see com-) + regere "to lead straight, guide, rule" (see regal).
