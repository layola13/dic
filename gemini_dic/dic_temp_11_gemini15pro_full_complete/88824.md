hogger:/ˈhɑːɡər/ (US), /ˈhɒɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 垄断者，独占者；贪婪的人；大型车辆(尤指卡车)|A person who keeps or uses more than their fair share of something; a large vehicle, especially a truck.|He's a real hogger when it comes to the spotlight.  他真是个喜欢抢风头的人。|The hogger was driving way too fast for the icy conditions.  这辆大型卡车在冰雪路面上开得太快了。|synonyms: monopolizer, grabber, glutton, road hog |antonyms: sharer, giver|noun
