fleshing:/ˈflɛʃɪŋ/ /ˈflɛʃɪŋ/| n. |充实，具体化|The novel needed more fleshing out to make the characters believable. 这部小说需要更多细节来使人物更可信。|近义词：elaborating, developing, expanding, enriching|反义词：abridging, condensing, summarizing|noun

fleshing:/ˈflɛʃɪŋ/ /ˈflɛʃɪŋ/| adj. |肉色的，似肉的|The artist used a fleshing colored paint for the portrait.  艺术家用肉色的颜料画这幅肖像。|近义词：flesh-colored, pink, peach|反义词：pale, white|adjective

fleshing:/ˈflɛʃɪŋ/ /ˈflɛʃɪŋ/| v. |(flesh 的现在分词)  使…丰满；给…添枝加叶|He is fleshing out the story with more details. 他正在为故事添加更多细节使之更加丰满。|近义词：elaborating on, developing, expanding on|反义词：abridging, condensing|verb

词源: From Middle English *fleshynge, verbal noun from fleshen, from flesh +‎ -en.
