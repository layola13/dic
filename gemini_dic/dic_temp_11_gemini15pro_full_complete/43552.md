craze: /kreɪz/ | n. | 狂热，风靡一时的事物 | An enthusiasm for a particular activity or object which appears suddenly and achieves widespread popularity, especially one which is short-lived. |  The latest craze is for young people to dye their hair bright colours. 最新的潮流是年轻人把头发染成鲜艳的颜色。

| Example: There was a short-lived craze for wearing leg warmers in the 1980s.  在20世纪80年代，流行过一阵子穿腿套。| Synonym: fad, trend, fashion, rage | Antonym: apathy, indifference | noun
