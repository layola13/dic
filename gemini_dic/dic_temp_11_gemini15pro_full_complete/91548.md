hyper-excited:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtɪd/| adj.|极度兴奋的，过度兴奋的|Extremely excited; excessively enthusiastic.|She was hyper-excited about the upcoming concert. 她对即将到来的演唱会感到非常兴奋。|The children were hyper-excited on Christmas morning.  圣诞节的早晨，孩子们兴奋极了。|近义词：thrilled, exhilarated, ecstatic, overjoyed|反义词：calm, apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "hyper-" (prefix meaning "over, above, excessive") + "excited" (past participle of "excite," from Latin "excitare" meaning "to rouse, stir up").
