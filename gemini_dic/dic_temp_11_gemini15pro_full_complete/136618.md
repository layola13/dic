nonsatirizing:/nɒnˈsætəraɪzɪŋ/ /nɑːnˈsætəraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
非讽刺的，不讽刺的
Not using satire; not intended to be satirical.
His nonsatirizing approach to the issue was refreshing.  他以非讽刺的方式处理这个问题，令人耳目一新。
The report presented a nonsatirizing account of the events.  该报告以非讽刺性的方式叙述了事件。
near synonyms: serious, earnest, sincere, straightforward
antonyms: satirical, ironic, mocking, sarcastic
word origin:  A combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the present participle of the verb "satirize."  "Satirize" comes from the French "satiriser" and ultimately from the Latin "satira" meaning "satire."
