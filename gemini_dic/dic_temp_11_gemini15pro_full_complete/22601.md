bootstraps:/ˈbuːtˌstræps/| n.| 靴带; 自力更生; 凭借自身努力获得成功的方法|A set of loops or straps at the top of boots, used to help pull them on. Figuratively, it refers to achieving something without external help, relying solely on one's own resources.|He pulled himself up by his bootstraps and built a successful business. 他靠自己的努力建立了一个成功的企业。|近义词：self-reliance, initiative, resourcefulness|反义词: dependence, reliance|noun

bootstraps:/ˈbuːtˌstræps/| n.| 自力更生; 凭借自身努力获得成功的方法|The act of achieving something without external help, relying solely on one's own resources.|She started her business from bootstraps with just a small loan. 她只靠一小笔贷款就白手起家创办了自己的企业。|近义词：self-sufficiency, self-support|反义词: dependence, assistance|noun
