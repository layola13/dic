overfatten:/ˌoʊvərˈfætən/| v. |过度喂肥，使过胖|To make excessively fat; fatten beyond what is normal or desirable.|The farmer overfattened the geese for the holiday feast.  农民为了节日盛宴把鹅喂得太肥了。|
The prize-winning pig was deliberately overfattened.  这头获奖的猪是故意被喂肥的。|近义词：overfeed|反义词: underfeed|词性:动词

overfatten:/ˌoʊvərˈfætən/| adj. |过肥的|Excessively fat; fattened beyond what is normal or desirable. (This usage is less common than the verb form.)|While not technically overfatten, the dog was definitely overweight. 这只狗虽然严格来说不算过肥，但肯定超重了。|These overfatten chickens won't lay many eggs. 这些过肥的鸡不会下很多蛋。|近义词：overfed, obese|反义词: lean, thin|词性:形容词
