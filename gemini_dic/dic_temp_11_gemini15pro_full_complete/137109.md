nonsubmergence:/ˌnɒnsʌbˈmɜːrdʒəns/ |n.| 非浸没，不沉没 |The nonsubmergence of the buoy indicated a problem with its anchoring system.| 浮标没有沉没表明它的锚定系统有问题。|近义词: emergence, surfacing|反义词: submergence, immersion| 词性: 名词

词源: non- (not) + submergence (the act of submerging)  来自拉丁语 *submergere*,  sub- (under) + mergere (to plunge).
