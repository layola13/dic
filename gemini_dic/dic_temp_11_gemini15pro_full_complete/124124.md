mortgagee:/ˌmɔːrɡəˈdʒiː/
n.
抵押权人，贷款人
The mortgagee is the lender in a mortgage agreement.
抵押权人是抵押协议中的贷款人。
The bank is the mortgagee and I am the mortgagor.
银行是抵押权人，我是抵押人。
lender, creditor
mortgagor, debtor
noun
