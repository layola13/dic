knotty:/ˈnɑːti/ /ˈnɒti/| adj.|棘手的，多节的，难解决的|Full of knots; tangled; difficult to solve; complex.  It can refer to literal knots in wood or rope, or to difficult or complex problems.|The carpenter struggled with the knotty piece of wood. 木匠费力地处理那块多节的木头。

The lawyer untangled the knotty legal issues. 律师解开了棘手的法律问题。|近义词：complex, difficult, intricate, tangled, gnarled|反义词：simple, easy, straightforward, smooth|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *knotti, from Old English cnottig (“knotty”), equivalent to knot +‎ -y. Cognate with Scots knotit (“knotted”), West Frisian knotich (“knotty”), Dutch knoestig, knorrig (“gnarled, grumpy”), German knorrig (“gnarled, grumpy”).
