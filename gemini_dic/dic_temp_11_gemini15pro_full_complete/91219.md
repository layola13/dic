hydrobromate:/ˌhaɪdrəˈbroʊmeɪt/
n.
氢溴酸盐
a salt of hydrobromic acid
Potassium hydrobromate is used as a sedative. 氢溴酸钾用作镇静剂。
近义词:bromide (in some contexts)
反义词: N/A
词性:noun

Etymology:
From hydro- + bromate.  "Hydro-" denotes hydrogen or water, while "-bromate" indicates the anion of bromic acid (BrO3-), although in "hydrobromate" it refers to the anion of hydrobromic acid (Br-).
