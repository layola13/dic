conductance:/kənˈdʌktəns/ | n. | 电导，电导率 | The ability of a substance to conduct electricity, measured in siemens. | The conductance of the wire is high. 这根导线的电导率很高。 |  synonyms: conductivity, admissibility | antonyms: resistance, insulation | noun

conductance:/kənˈdʌktəns/ | n. |  传导率; 导水率 | The ability of a substance to allow the flow of something (like heat or water). | The conductance of the pipe affects water flow. 管道的导水率影响水流。 | synonyms: permeability, transmittance | antonyms: impermeability, resistance | noun
