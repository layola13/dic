loder:/ˈloʊdər/| n.|装载机; 装货机|A machine for loading materials, especially large quantities of earth, gravel, or similar materials, into a truck or other container.|The loder scooped up the gravel and deposited it in the truck.  装载机铲起砾石，并将其倒入卡车。|synonyms: loader, excavator, front loader, bucket loader, shovel, digger|antonyms: unloader|noun
