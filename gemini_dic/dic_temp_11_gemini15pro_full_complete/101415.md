iqaluit:/ɪˈkæluːɪt/| n.|伊卡卢伊特 (加拿大努纳武特地区的首府)|From Inuktitut *iqaluk* "fish" + *-it* "place of many."  A place where there are many fish. It refers to the abundance of Arctic char in the nearby waters.| Iqaluit is the capital of Nunavut. 伊卡卢伊特是努纳武特的首府。|Synonym: capital city|Antonym: N/A|noun
