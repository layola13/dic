highjack:/ˈhaɪdʒæk/| v. n.|劫持，强占|To seize or commandeer (a vehicle, aircraft, or ship) in transit by force or threat of force; to commandeer something for one's own purposes.|The terrorists highjacked the plane. 恐怖分子劫持了飞机。|近义词：seize, commandeer, hijack|反义词:release, surrender|词性:动词/名词

highjacks (plural form): /ˈhaɪdʒæks/| v. n.|劫持，强占 (复数形式)|The plural form of "highjack," referring to multiple instances of seizing or commandeering.|Several ships were highjacked by pirates. 几艘船被海盗劫持了。|近义词：seizes, commandeers, hijacks|反义词:releases, surrenders|词性:动词/名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From "hijack," a blend of "high" and "jack," possibly influenced by highway robbery.
