nonintuitiveness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtjuːɪtɪvnəs/| n.|非直观性，不直觉性|The nonintuitiveness of the software interface made it difficult for users to navigate.  该软件界面的非直观性使用户难以操作。|例句：The nonintuitiveness of quantum mechanics often baffles students. 量子力学的非直观性常常使学生感到困惑。

The nonintuitiveness of the control panel led to frequent user errors.  控制面板的非直观性导致用户频繁出错。|近义词：counterintuitiveness, opacity, complexity|反义词：intuitiveness, clarity, simplicity|词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + intuitive + -ness.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," intuitive comes from Latin *intuire*, "to look at, contemplate," and -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
