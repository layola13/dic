mechanical:/məˈkænɪk(ə)l/
adjective
机械的；力学的；呆板的；无意识的
relating to machines and how they work; operated by or involving a machine or mechanism; done without thought or feeling, automatic
例句：The car had a mechanical problem. 汽车出现了机械故障。
例句：He gave a mechanical response. 他机械地回答。
近义词：automatic, robotic, motorized
反义词：manual, natural, spontaneous
词性: adjective


mechanical:/məˈkænɪk(ə)l/
adjective
手工的；技巧的
done by hand; relating to skills with the hands
例句：Mechanical skills are important for many jobs. 手工技能对许多工作很重要。
例句：He has great mechanical aptitude. 他有很强的机械能力。
近义词：manual, technical, skilled
反义词：automatic, unskilled, clumsy
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *mechanicus, from Greek mēkhanikos (“of or belonging to machines, inventive, mechanical”), from mēkhanē (“machine, contrivance, device, engine”) + -ikos (suffix forming adjectives).
