earthling:/ˈɜːrθlɪŋ/ | n. | 地球人；凡人 | An inhabitant of the Earth.  A human being, especially when considered in relation to extraterrestrial beings. |  例句：The earthlings were amazed by the sight of the spaceship. 地球人对宇宙飞船的景象感到惊奇。

例句：The aliens considered the earthlings to be primitive. 外星人认为地球人是原始的。


近义词：human, person, individual, mortal | 反义词：alien, extraterrestrial | 词性：noun
