no-ball:/ˈnəʊ bɔːl/ (UK), /ˈnoʊ bɔːl/ (US)| n.|无球，坏球|A delivery in cricket that is not a legal ball according to the rules, usually because the bowler's foot was not behind the popping crease when the ball was delivered.  It results in an extra run being awarded to the batting side.|The bowler overstepped, so the umpire called a no-ball. 投球手越线了，所以裁判判罚无球。|Synonyms: illegal delivery, foul ball|Antonyms: legal delivery, fair ball|noun

no-ball:/ˈnəʊ bɔːl/ (UK), /ˈnoʊ bɔːl/ (US)| adj.|无效的，坏球的|Relating to or being a no-ball.|The no-ball delivery gave the batsmen an extra run. 无效的投球给了击球手一分。|Synonyms: illegal, invalid|Antonyms: legal, valid|adjective
