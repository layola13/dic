inglobate:/ɪnˈɡloʊbeɪt/ | vt. | 包含，合并，吸收 | To incorporate or absorb into a larger mass; to engulf. | The artist inglobated various found objects into her sculpture. 艺术家将各种各样的现成物品融入到她的雕塑中。|  The company inglobated several smaller firms into its operations. 这家公司将几家小公司并入了其业务中。 | 近义词：incorporate, absorb, include, encompass, integrate | 反义词：exclude, separate, remove | 词性：动词

inglobate:/ɪnˈɡloʊbeɪt/ | adj. | 包含的，合并的 | Incorporated or absorbed into a larger mass. | An inglobate mass of various materials. 由各种材料组成的混合体。| The report presented an inglobate view of the company's performance. 该报告对公司的业绩进行了全面的概述。 | 近义词：incorporated, integrated, combined | 反义词：separate, discrete | 词性：形容词


词源：From Latin inglobāre, from in- (“in, into”) + globāre (“to make round, form into a ball”), from globus (“globe, sphere”).
