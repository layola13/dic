antiemetics:/ˌæntiɪˈmɛtɪks/ | n. |止吐药| Medications used to prevent or treat nausea and vomiting. | The doctor prescribed antiemetics to help with the patient's post-operative nausea. 医生给病人开了止吐药来缓解术后恶心。|  Synonyms: antinauseants | Antonyms: emetics | noun

antiemetic:/ˌæntiɪˈmɛtɪk/ | adj. |止吐的| Preventing or relieving nausea and vomiting. | The antiemetic properties of ginger are well known.  生姜的止吐特性是众所周知的。| Synonyms: antinauseant | Antonyms: emetic | adjective
