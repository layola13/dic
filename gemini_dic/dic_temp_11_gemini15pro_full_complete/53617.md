discarnate:/dɪsˈkɑːrneɪt/
v.|使脱离肉体|To separate from the body. |The ritual was meant to discarnate the soul.|近义词：disembody|反义词：incarnate, embody
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin discarnātus, past participle of discarnāre (“to make fleshless”), from dis- (“apart from, away from”) + carnāre (“to make flesh”), from carō, carn- (“flesh”).
