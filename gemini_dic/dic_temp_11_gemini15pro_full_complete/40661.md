convergences:/kənˈvɜːrdʒənsɪz/
n.
趋同，汇聚，融合
The convergence of several factors led to the economic crisis.  多种因素的汇聚导致了经济危机。
The convergence of the two rivers creates a fertile delta. 两条河流的交汇处形成了肥沃的三角洲。
Synonyms: confluence, meeting, junction, union, intersection
Antonyms: divergence, separation, branching

Etymology: From Late Latin convergentia, from convergere "to incline together," from com- "together" + vergere "to bend, turn, incline."
