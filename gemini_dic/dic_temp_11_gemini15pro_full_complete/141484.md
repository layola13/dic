opinionless:/əˈpɪnjənlɪs/
adjective
无意见的；没有主见的；漠不关心的
Lacking or expressing no personal opinions.
He remained opinionless on the matter, preferring to listen to others' views. 他对这件事保持沉默，宁愿听取别人的意见。
Synonyms: neutral, indifferent, impassive, unopinionated, noncommittal
Antonyms: opinionated, assertive, dogmatic, prejudiced
词性: 形容词
