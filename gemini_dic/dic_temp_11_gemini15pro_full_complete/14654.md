avowance:/əˈvaʊəns/| n. |承认；公开宣称；坦白|An open declaration or acknowledgment; a confession.|He made an avowance of his guilt. 他坦白了自己的罪行。|近义词：admission, confession, acknowledgment, declaration, profession|反义词：denial, repudiation|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *avowaunce*, from Anglo-Norman *avouer* (to avow). Ultimately from Latin *advocare* (to call to witness).
