hypothesizing:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 假设，假定 |  The act of forming a hypothesis; offering a possible explanation or theory. | Scientists are hypothesizing that the virus originated in bats. 科学家们假设这种病毒起源于蝙蝠。| Synonyms: theorizing, speculating, conjecturing, supposing | Antonyms: proving, demonstrating, confirming | verb (present participle)

hypothesize:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪz/ | v. | 假设，假定 | To put something forward as a hypothesis. |  Researchers hypothesize that the drug may be effective in treating Alzheimer's disease. 研究人员假设这种药物可能对治疗老年痴呆症有效。 | Synonyms: theorize, speculate, conjecture, suppose | Antonyms: prove, demonstrate, confirm | verb

hypothesis:/haɪˈpɒθəsɪs/ | n. | 假设，假说 | A supposition or proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation. |  Several hypotheses have been put forward to explain the extinction of the dinosaurs.  已经提出了几种假说来解释恐龙的灭绝。 | Synonyms: theory, supposition, conjecture, speculation | Antonyms: proof, fact, reality | noun
