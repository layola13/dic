nonedified:/ˌnɒnˈɛdɪfaɪd/
adjective
未受启发的，未被教化的
Not spiritually or intellectually improved; unedified.
After the tedious lecture, the audience felt nonedified.  在听了乏味的讲座后，听众感觉没有受到启发。
The sermon left me feeling completely nonedified. 这场布道让我完全没有受到启迪。
Synonyms: unenlightened, uninstructed, unimproved
Antonyms: edified, enlightened, inspired
词性: adjective
