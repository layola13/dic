antiphonary:/ænˈtɪfəˌnɛri/ | n. |对歌集，轮唱圣歌集|A book containing antiphons, verses, or responses sung or chanted during divine service, especially in the Roman Catholic Church.  A book of antiphonal chants. | The choir sang from the ancient antiphonary.  唱诗班用古老的对歌集唱歌。|  The monks chanted the antiphons from the illuminated antiphonary.  僧侣们吟唱着来自装饰华丽的对歌集里的对歌。|Synonyms: antiphonal, gradual | Antonyms: None | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antiphonarium*, from Latin antiphona (“antiphon”) (from Ancient Greek ἀντίφωνον (antíphōnon, “sounding in response”)) + -arium.
