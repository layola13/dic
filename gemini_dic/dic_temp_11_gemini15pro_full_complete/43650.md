creatress:/kriˈeɪtrɪs/
noun
女创造者，女性创始人
A female who creates or brings something into existence.
例句：The creatress of this magnificent sculpture was a young artist from Italy.  这位宏伟雕塑的创造者是一位来自意大利的年轻艺术家。

例句：She is recognized as the creatress of a new genre in literature. 她被认为是文学新体裁的创始人。

近义词：creator, founder, originator, maker, author
反义词：destroyer, demolisher
