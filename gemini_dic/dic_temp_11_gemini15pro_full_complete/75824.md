fumigates:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪts/| v. |熏蒸，烟熏消毒|To expose to smoke or fumes, especially for disinfection or to destroy pests.|例句：The warehouse fumigates the stored grain regularly to prevent insect infestation. 仓库定期熏蒸储存的谷物以防止虫害。|例句：They fumigated the house to get rid of the termites. 他们熏蒸房子以清除白蚁。|近义词：disinfect, sterilize, exterminate|反义词:infect, contaminate|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin fumigatus, past participle of fumigāre "to fumigate," from Latin fumus "smoke."
