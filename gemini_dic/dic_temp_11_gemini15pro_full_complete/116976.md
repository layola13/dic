如果你是想了解疑问词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. | 什么 | used to ask for information about something | Example: What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| Synonyms: which, whatever | Antonyms: N/A (疑问词通常没有反义词) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb

What do you think? 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for lunch? 她午饭想吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching? 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? 他在那里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用那笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
