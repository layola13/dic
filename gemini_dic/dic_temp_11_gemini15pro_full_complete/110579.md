limited-access-highways:/ˈlɪmɪtɪd ˈækses ˈhaɪweɪz/| n.|有限制出入的高速公路|Highways designed for high-speed vehicular traffic, with limited or no access from adjacent properties and controlled entrances and exits.  These roads often feature multiple lanes, medians, and restrictions on slower-moving vehicles.|Example: Limited-access highways are crucial for efficient long-distance travel. 有限制出入的高速公路对于高效的长途旅行至关重要。

Example:  Many limited-access highways have tolls. 许多限制出入的高速公路收费。|近义词：freeways, expressways, motorways|反义词：surface streets, local roads|词性:noun
