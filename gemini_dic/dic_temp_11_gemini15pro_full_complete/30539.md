cephalon:/ˈsɛfəlɒn/ (US) /ˈkɛfəlɒn/ (UK)| n. |头 |The anterior part of the body in many arthropods, comprising the fused head and thorax.  It's the head section of an arthropod, like an insect or crustacean, that often includes the eyes, antennae, and mouthparts.|The trilobite's cephalon was well-preserved in the fossil.  三叶虫的头部在化石中保存完好。|Near synonyms: head, cranial region|Antonyms: abdomen, pygidium (in trilobites)|noun
