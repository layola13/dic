outwatch:/ˈaʊtwɒtʃ/ (US) /aʊtˈwɒtʃ/ (UK)| v.|监视；密切注视；比……活得长；熬过；|To watch longer or more intently than someone or something else.  To survive or outlive someone or something.|He outwatched his rivals and eventually won the contract. 他比他的竞争对手坚持更久，最终赢得了合同。|近义词：observe, monitor, surveil, outlive, survive|反义词: overlook, miss, succumb|词性:动词
