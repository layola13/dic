disappointingly:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人失望地 | In a manner that causes disappointment; in a way that fails to meet expectations. | The team played disappointingly and lost the game. 球队表现令人失望，输掉了比赛。| The movie ended disappointingly. 这部电影的结局令人失望。 | 近义词: sadly, regrettably, unfortunately | 反义词: encouragingly, pleasingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From disappoint + -ing + -ly.  "Disappoint" comes from the Old French "desapointer," meaning "to undo an appointment."
