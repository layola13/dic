mindedness:/ˈmaɪndɪdnəs/| n.| 心态; 思想倾向 |A state or quality of being inclined to think in a particular way. |例句：Her open-mindedness allowed her to consider new perspectives. 她的开放心态使她能够考虑新的观点。

例句：He showed a growth mindset throughout the challenging project. 在整个充满挑战的项目中，他展现了成长的心态。|近义词：mentality, attitude, disposition, outlook|反义词: close-mindedness, bias, prejudice|词性: noun
