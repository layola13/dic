intercommunicable:/ˌɪntər.kəˈmjuː.nɪ.kə.bəl/
adjective
可互相交流的；可互相沟通的；(疾病)可互相传染的
Capable of being communicated or exchanged between two or more people or groups; (of a disease) capable of being transmitted from one person to another.
The two networks are intercommunicable, allowing data to be shared easily. 这两个网络可以互通，可以轻松共享数据。
Measles is a highly intercommunicable disease. 麻疹是一种高度传染性疾病。
近义词:communicable, contagious, transmittable
反义词:incommunicable, non-contagious
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + communicable.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "communicable" comes from the Late Latin communicabilis, meaning "that can be shared."
