discriminator:/dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtər/| n.|鉴别器，判别器，区分者|A person or thing that discriminates, especially a device used to detect or distinguish between different signals, substances, etc.  A discriminator helps to differentiate or separate.|The discriminator in the circuit helps to separate the audio signal from the background noise.|近义词：differentiator, distinguisher, separator|反义词:integrator, combiner|noun
