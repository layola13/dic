misspending:/mɪsˈspɛndɪŋ/
noun
乱花钱；浪费
The act of spending money unwisely or wastefully.
He regretted his misspending of his youth. 他后悔自己虚度了青春。
近义词: squandering, wasting, dissipation
反义词: saving, investing
词性: noun

misspend:/ˌmɪsˈspɛnd/
verb
浪费；乱花(金钱或时间)
To spend (money or time) unwisely or wastefully.
He misspent his inheritance on gambling. 他把遗产都浪费在了赌博上。
近义词: squander, waste, fritter away
反义词: save, invest, utilize
词性: verb
