crystallometry:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɒmɪtri/
n.
晶体测量学
The science of measuring the angles between the faces of crystals.
Crystallometry plays a crucial role in identifying and classifying minerals. 晶体测量学在鉴定和分类矿物中起着至关重要的作用。
The geologist used crystallometry to determine the mineral composition of the rock. 地质学家使用晶体测量学来确定岩石的矿物成分。
近义词:crystallography
反义词:None
词性:noun

Etymology:
From crystal + -metry (suffix meaning "measurement of").  "Crystal" comes from the Greek "krystallos," meaning "ice" or "rock crystal." "-metry" comes from the Greek "metria," meaning "measuring."
