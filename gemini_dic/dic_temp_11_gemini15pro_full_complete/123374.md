monomeniscous:/ˌmɒnəˈmɛnɪskəs/ | adj. | 单层膜的；单子囊的 | Having a single envelope or membrane, particularly referring to pollen grains with a single wall layer.  | Example:  The botanist identified the pollen as monomeniscous due to its single wall structure.  植物学家根据其单壁结构将花粉鉴定为单层膜的。

Example: Monomeniscous pollen is characteristic of certain plant families. 单层膜花粉是某些植物科的特征。| 近义词: unilocular (referring to anthers) | 反义词: polymeniscous (rarely used, refers to having multiple membranes) | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *mono-* (single) + *mēninx* (membrane) + *-ous* (adjective suffix).
