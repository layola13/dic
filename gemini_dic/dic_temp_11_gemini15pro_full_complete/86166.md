heartsickness:/ˈhɑːrtsɪknɪs/ | n. | 心痛，伤感 | Deep sadness or emotional distress, often associated with love or loss. |  She felt a deep heartsickness when she heard the news of his death. 她听到他去世的消息后感到深深的悲痛。

|  His heartsickness was evident in his downcast eyes.  他的悲伤从他低垂的眼睛里显露无疑。 | 近义词：grief, sorrow, anguish, heartache | 反义词：joy, happiness | 词性: noun

词源: From heart + sickness, literally "sickness of the heart."
