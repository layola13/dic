irreproachable:/ˌɪrɪˈproʊtʃəbl/
|adj.|无可指责的，无可非议的|Beyond criticism; faultless.|Her conduct throughout the ordeal was irreproachable.  她在整个考验中的行为无可指责。|近义词：blameless, impeccable, faultless, unblemished|反义词：blameworthy, culpable, faulty, flawed|词性: 形容词


词源:

From Late Middle English *irreprocheable*, from Old French irreprochable, from Medieval Latin irreprochabilis, from Latin in- + reprochabilis (“reproachable”), from reprochare (“to reproach”).  Essentially meaning "not able to be reproached".
