martingale:/ˈmɑːrtɪŋɡeɪl/| n.|鞅，马丁格尔，(防止马仰头的)嚼环|A system of betting in which one doubles the stakes after each loss, hoping to recoup all losses when one eventually wins.  A strap fastened to a horse's girth, passing between its front legs and ending in two rings to which the reins are attached, used to prevent the horse from throwing back its head.|He tried the martingale system at the casino, but quickly lost all his money. 他在赌场尝试了鞅系统，但很快就输光了所有钱。

The martingale prevented the horse from rearing up.  嚼环防止了马仰头。|近义词：system, strategy(for the betting system), harness, bridle(for the horse equipment) |反义词: None|noun
