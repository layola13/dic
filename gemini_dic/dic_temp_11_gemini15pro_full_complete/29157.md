caseworker:/ˈkeɪswɜːrkər/ | n. |个案工作者，社会工作者|A person employed by a social welfare agency or other organization to provide social services to individuals, families, or groups, especially those in need or at risk.|The caseworker helped the family find suitable housing.  这个个案工作者帮助这个家庭找到了合适的住所。|Synonyms: social worker, counselor, therapist |Antonyms: None|noun
