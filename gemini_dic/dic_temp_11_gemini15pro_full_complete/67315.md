ecstasy: /ˈɛkstəsi/ (US) /ˈɛkstəsi/ (UK)| n.|狂喜，欣喜若狂；入迷，忘我|A state of overwhelming emotion, especially joy; an emotional or religious frenzy or trance-like state.|She was in an ecstasy of delight when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息欣喜若狂。|Near synonyms: rapture, bliss, euphoria|Near antonyms: despair, misery, agony|noun

ecstasy: /ˈɛkstəsi/ (US) /ˈɛkstəsi/ (UK)| n.|（常用复数形式ecstasies）忘形，狂喜|Fits of ecstasy.|He went into ecstasies over her singing. 他对她的歌声着了迷。|Near synonyms: raptures, transports|Near antonyms: depressions, sorrows|noun
