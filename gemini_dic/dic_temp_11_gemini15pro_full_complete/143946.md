outquaffed: /ˌaʊtˈkwæft/
verb
胜过(某人)的酒量; 比(某人)喝得更多
to drink more than someone else
He outquaffed all his friends at the party. 他在聚会上比所有朋友都喝得多。
Synonyms: outdrink, out-drink
Antonyms: be outdrunk, be out-drunk
词性: 动词
