ignominiousness:/ˌɪɡnəˈmɪniəsnəs/| n.|耻辱，不名誉，丢脸|The state of being ignominious; deep disgrace or dishonor.|His ignominiousness after the scandal was unbearable.  丑闻曝光后，他的耻辱令人无法忍受。|Synonyms: shame, disgrace, humiliation, dishonor, degradation|Antonyms: honor, glory, dignity, respect|noun

ignominious:/ˌɪɡnəˈmɪniəs/| adj.|耻辱的，不名誉的，丢脸的|Deserving or causing public disgrace or shame.|The team suffered an ignominious defeat. 该队遭遇了耻辱的失败。|Synonyms: shameful, disgraceful, humiliating, dishonorable, degrading|Antonyms: honorable, glorious, dignified, respectable|adjective
