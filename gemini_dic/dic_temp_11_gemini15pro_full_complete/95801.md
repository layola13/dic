inconvertibility:/ˌɪnkənˌvɜːrtɪˈbɪləti/
n.|不可兑换性，不可变换性|The state or quality of not being convertible, especially of currency or assets, into another form, such as another currency, gold, or goods.|The inconvertibility of the currency made international trade difficult.  该货币的不可兑换性使国际贸易变得困难。|non-convertibility, non-exchangeability|convertibility, exchangeability|noun
