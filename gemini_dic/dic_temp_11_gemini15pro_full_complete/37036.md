comanages:/ˌkəʊˈmænɪdʒɪz/
|v.|共同管理，共同经营|To manage something jointly with another person or group.|The two companies comanage the project.  两家公司共同管理这个项目。|
She comanages the family business with her brother. 她和她哥哥共同管理家族企业。|近义词：jointly manage, co-administer, share management of|反义词：solely manage, manage alone|词性:动词
