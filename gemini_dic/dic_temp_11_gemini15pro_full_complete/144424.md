outsulked:/ˌaʊtˈsʌlkt/
verb
比...更闷闷不乐; 生闷气胜过
outdo someone in sulking; be more successful than another at sulking.
She outsulked her brother, refusing to speak for days. 她比她哥哥更闷闷不乐，好几天都不说话。
近义词：outlast, surpass, exceed (in sulking)
反义词：cheer up, brighten, be happy

词性: verb (动词)

词源:  "Out-" (prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed") + "sulked" (past tense of "sulk," meaning "to be silent, morose, and bad-tempered out of annoyance or disappointment").  The word combines the idea of exceeding someone in the act of sulking.
