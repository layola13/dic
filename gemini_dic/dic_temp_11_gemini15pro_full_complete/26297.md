butyric:/bjuːˈtɪrɪk/ | adj. | 丁酸的；酪酸的 | Relating to or derived from butyric acid, a fatty acid with a strong unpleasant smell. |  Butyric acid is responsible for the rancid smell of butter. 丁酸是造成黄油腐臭味的原因。|  The butyric fermentation process produces a characteristic odor. 丁酸发酵过程会产生一种特殊的气味。 | 近义词: rancid, fetid | 反义词: fragrant, aromatic | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *butyrum "butter" (from Greek boutyron), + -ic.
