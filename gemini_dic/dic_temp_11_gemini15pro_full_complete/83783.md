hairier:/ˈhɛəriər (US), ˈheəriər (UK)| adj.|毛发更多的，更毛茸茸的|Covered with more hair; more hairy than something/someone else.  This refers to a greater quantity of hair.|例句：My dog is hairier in the winter. 我的狗冬天毛更多。

例句：He's hairier than his brother. 他比他兄弟毛发更多。|近义词：shaggier, furrier, fuzzier|反义词：smoother, less hairy, balder|词性:形容词
