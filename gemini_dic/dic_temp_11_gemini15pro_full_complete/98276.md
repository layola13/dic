insessorial:/ˌɪnsəˈsɔːriəl/ | adj. |  适于栖息的，适于抓握的 | Adapted for perching or grasping, particularly referring to the feet of birds. | Example:  The robin has insessorial feet, allowing it to perch comfortably on branches. 知更鸟有适于栖息的脚，可以让它舒服地栖息在树枝上。

Example:  Owls, with their strong, insessorial talons, are efficient hunters of small rodents. 猫头鹰凭借其强壮、适于抓握的爪子，是捕猎小型啮齿动物的高效猎手。| 近义词: prehensile, grasping | 反义词:  cursorial | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *insessus*, past participle of *insidere*  "to sit upon," from *in-* "on" + *sedere* "to sit."
