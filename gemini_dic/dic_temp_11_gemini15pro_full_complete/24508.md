broad-minded:/ˌbrɔːdˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|心胸宽广的，开明的|Willing to consider and tolerate different ideas and opinions.|She's very broad-minded about different religions. 她对不同的宗教信仰非常宽容。|Example: My parents are quite broad-minded and let me make my own decisions. 我的父母思想很开明，让我自己做决定。|Synonyms: tolerant, open-minded, liberal, accepting, unbiased|Antonyms: narrow-minded, intolerant, prejudiced, bigoted, dogmatic|Adjective
