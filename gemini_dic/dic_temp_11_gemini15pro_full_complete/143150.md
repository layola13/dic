outcharging:/ˌaʊtˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
verb
过度收费，收费过高
The act of charging a price higher than what is considered fair or reasonable.
The restaurant was accused of outcharging tourists. 这家餐馆被指控对游客过度收费。
overcharge, price gouging
undercharge
v.
