networking:/ˈnetwɜːrkɪŋ/| n.|建立人脉，社交，网络连接|The process of interacting with other people to exchange information and develop professional or social contacts.|例句：She's very good at networking and has made some useful contacts. 她非常擅长社交，并建立了一些有用的人脉。

例句：The conference provided excellent opportunities for networking.  这次会议提供了极佳的社交机会。|近义词：socializing, connecting, interacting|反义词:isolating|noun
