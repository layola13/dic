hypnic:/ˈhɪpnɪk/| adj.|催眠的，入睡的|Relating to the state of being between wakefulness and sleep, often accompanied by hallucinations or unusual sensations.|例句：Hypnic jerks are sudden, involuntary muscle spasms that occur as a person is falling asleep. 入睡抽搐是指一个人在入睡时发生的突然的、不自主的肌肉痉挛。

例句：She experienced vivid hypnic hallucinations just before drifting off. 她刚要睡着就经历了生动的入睡期幻觉。|近义词：hypnagogic, presleep|反义词：wakeful|词性:形容词
