nonbindingness:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪndɪŋnɪs/ | n. | 无约束力；非强制性 | The state of not being legally or morally obligatory. |  The nonbindingness of the agreement allowed both parties to withdraw without penalty.  该协议的非约束力允许双方在没有处罚的情况下退出。| synonyms: optionality, voluntariness | antonyms: obligation, compulsion | noun


nonbinding:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪndɪŋ/ | adj. | 无约束力的；非强制性的 | Not legally or morally obligatory. | The resolution is nonbinding and carries no legal weight.  该决议不具有约束力，没有法律效力。  | synonyms: advisory, unenforceable | antonyms: binding, obligatory | adjective
