desecrating:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtɪŋ/
v.
亵渎；玷污
The act of violating or treating something sacred with disrespect.
They were accused of desecrating graves. 他们被指控亵渎坟墓。
近义词:profaning, violating, defiling
反义词:consecrating, sanctifying, venerating
词性:动词(present participle)

desecrate:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪt/
v.
亵渎；玷污
To violate or treat something sacred with disrespect.
The vandals desecrated the church by spraying graffiti on the walls. 破坏者在墙上喷涂涂鸦，亵渎了教堂。
近义词:profane, violate, defile
反义词:consecrate, sanctify, venerate
词性:动词

词源: From Latin *desecrātus*, past participle of *desecrāre* (“to uncover, unveil; to profane”), from *de-* (“un-”) + *sacrāre* (“to consecrate, dedicate”), from *sacer* (“sacred”).
