beat-hollow:/biːt ˈhɒləʊ/ (UK) /biːt ˈhɑːloʊ/ (US)| adj.|击败; 彻底打败; 使…筋疲力尽|Completely defeated; exhausted and demoralized.  Derived from "beat" meaning to defeat and "hollow" implying emptiness or lack of substance.|The team was beat-hollow after their crushing defeat.|Synonyms: routed, vanquished, thrashed, exhausted, drained|Antonyms: victorious, triumphant|adjective
