literal:/ˈlɪtərəl/ | adj. | 字面的；原义的；逐字的；缺乏想象力的 | Taking something in its literal sense; exactly as stated; word for word; lacking imagination. |  The literal meaning of "cat" is a small domesticated feline. “cat”的字面意思是小型家养猫科动物。

| synonyms: exact, precise, verbatim, unembellished | antonyms: figurative, metaphorical, symbolic, imaginative | adjective
