acres:/ˈeɪkərz/| n.|英亩，土地，耕地|A unit of land area equal to 4,840 square yards (0.4047 hectare).|例句：The farm covers hundreds of acres.  这个农场占地数百英亩。

例句：They own several acres of woodland. 他们拥有几英亩的林地。|近义词：land, property, estate, territory|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English æcer "field," from Proto-Germanic *akraz (source also of Old Norse akr, Old Frisian eker, Old Saxon akkar, Dutch akker, Old High German ackar, German Acker "field, acre"), from Proto-Indo-European root *agros "field."
