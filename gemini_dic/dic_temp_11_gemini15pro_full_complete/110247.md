lives:/laɪvz/ | n. | 生命 | The plural form of "life." Refers to the existence of living beings, especially humans, and the experiences and events that happen during their time alive. | They lived full and happy lives.  他们过着充实而幸福的生活。


My two cats have nine lives. 我的两只猫有九条命。(比喻顽强生命力)

近义词: existences, lifetimes | 反义词: deaths | 词性: 名词 (Noun)
