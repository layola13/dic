intersprinkles:/ˌɪntərˈsprɪŋkəlz/
verb
散布；点缀；穿插
scatters or places something at intervals among other things.
She intersprinkled the flowers with tiny lights. 她把小花上点缀着小灯。
intersperse, scatter, distribute, dot, pepper
collect, gather, amass
v.
