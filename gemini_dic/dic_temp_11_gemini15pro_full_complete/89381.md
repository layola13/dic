homophonic:/ˌhɒməˈfɒnɪk/ (UK), /ˌhoʊməˈfɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.|同音的|Relating to or being homophones; sounding alike but having different meanings.|The words "to," "too," and "two" are homophonic.|近义词：homonymous, like-sounding|反义词：heterophonic|词性:形容词
