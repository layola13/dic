bournous:/ˈbʊərnʊs/ /bərˈnuːs/| n. |伯努斯，一种带帽斗篷|A long, hooded cloak worn especially by Arabs and Berbers. | He wore a heavy bournous against the desert chill. 他穿着一件厚厚的伯努斯斗篷以抵御沙漠的寒冷。|近义词：cloak, cape, mantle|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "light clothing")|词性:noun
