othman:/ˈɒθmən/ | n. |奥斯曼|A male given name, of Arabic origin, meaning "young bustard" (a type of bird). It is also a common surname and often associated with the Ottoman Empire, founded by Osman I.|Osman is a popular name in Turkey. 奥斯曼在土耳其是一个很受欢迎的名字。| He's a descendant of Othman I. 他是奥斯曼一世的後裔。 |Synonyms: Ottoman (as adjective or relating to the empire), Osman|Antonyms: N/A|noun
