bellhopped:/ˈbelˌhɒpt/ (US) /ˈbelˌhɔpt/ (UK)| adj.|搬运行李的；当侍者的；像侍者的|Working as a bellhop, typically in a smart uniform, carrying luggage and performing other services for guests in a hotel.  Characterized by the activities or appearance of a bellhop, often implying a youthful, energetic, and sometimes subservient demeanor.|He looked very bellhopped in his uniform. 他穿着制服，看起来很像个侍者。|Near Synonyms:  bellboy, pageboy, porter|Antonyms: manager, guest|adjective
