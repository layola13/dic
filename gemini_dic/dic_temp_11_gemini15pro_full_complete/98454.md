inspirator: /ˈɪnspəreɪtər/ | n. | 激励者，鼓舞者 |  A person or thing that inspires.  Someone who gives inspiration or encourages others. | The coach was an inspirator to the team, motivating them to victory.  教练是球队的激励者，激励他们走向胜利。 | Synonyms: motivator, encourager, influencer | Antonyms: discourager, deterrent | noun
