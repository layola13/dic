beach-head:/ˈbiːtʃˌhed/| n. |滩头阵地；据点；立足点| A position gained by an army that has landed on an enemy shore, which it defends and uses to advance from.  A secure initial position that can be used as a base for further advancement. | The troops established a beach-head after a fierce battle. 部队经过激战，建立了滩头阵地。| The company sees this new product as a beach-head in the European market. 该公司将这款新产品视为进军欧洲市场的立足点。| foothold, bridgehead, base, starting point |  hinterland | noun
