cryogen:/ˈkraɪədʒən/ | n. | 冷冻剂；低温学 | A substance used to produce very low temperatures. |  Liquid nitrogen is a common cryogen used in scientific research. 液氮是科研中常用的冷冻剂。

Cryogens are essential for preserving biological samples. 冷冻剂对于保存生物样本至关重要。 | 近义词: refrigerant | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun


cryogenic:/ˌkraɪəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 低温的；低温学的 | Relating to or involving very low temperatures. | Cryogenic freezing is used to preserve food. 冷冻冷藏用于保存食物。

The cryogenic engine powered the rocket into space. 低温发动机为火箭进入太空提供动力。 | 近义词: low-temperature | 反义词: high-temperature | 词性: adjective
