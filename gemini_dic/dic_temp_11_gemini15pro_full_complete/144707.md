ovariole:/oʊˈværioʊl/| n. |卵巢管|A tube-like structure in the insect ovary where egg cells develop.|The entomologist studied the ovarioles of the fruit fly to understand its reproductive cycle. 昆虫学家研究了果蝇的卵巢管，以了解其繁殖周期。|Synonyms: egg tube (less technical)|Antonyms: None|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ovarium* (ovary) + *-ole* (diminutive suffix)
