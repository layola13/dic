abbot:/ˈæbət/ | n. | 男修道院院长；方丈 | The head of a monastery for men. | The abbot welcomed the visitors to the monastery.  方丈欢迎来访者到寺院。| synonyms: superior, prior, head | antonyms: monk, novice | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *abbad, from Late Latin abbas, from Greek abbas, from Aramaic abbā, meaning "father".
