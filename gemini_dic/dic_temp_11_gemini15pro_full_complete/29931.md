cavally:/ˈkævəli/ /kəˈvæli/| n.|鲯鳅(鱼的一种),|A large marine fish (Caranx hippos) of the jack family, found in warm Atlantic waters and valued as a food and game fish.  It is also known as the crevalle jack.|We caught a huge cavally while deep-sea fishing. 我们在深海捕鱼时钓到了一条巨大的鲯鳅。|近义词：jack, crevalle jack|反义词:|noun
