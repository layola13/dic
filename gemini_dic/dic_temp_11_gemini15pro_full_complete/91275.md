hydrogenous:/haɪˈdrɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
含氢的; 由氢产生的
Consisting of or containing hydrogen; produced by hydrogen.
Hydrogenous deposits are formed by precipitation from seawater. 含氢沉积物是由海水沉淀形成的。
Synonyms: hydrogen-containing, hydrogen-rich
Antonyms: anhydrous
词性: 形容词
