mislodge:/mɪsˈlɒdʒ/ /mɪsˈlɑːdʒ/
verb
移开；取出；把…逐出
To remove something from a place where it is fixed or wedged.
The explosion mislodged several stones from the wall. 爆炸把墙上的几块石头震了下来。
The impact mislodged the driver from his seat. 撞击把司机从座位上震了下来。
dislodge, remove, displace
fix, secure, lodge
v.
