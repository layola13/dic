double-park:/ˈdʌbəl pɑːrk/ | v. | 并排停车 | To park a vehicle alongside another vehicle that is already parked at the curb, thus occupying the space of two vehicles. |  I had to double-park for a minute to run into the store. 我不得不并排停放一会儿，跑到商店里去。

| Example:  Delivery trucks often double-park in busy city streets.  送货卡车经常在繁忙的城市街道上并排停放。 | Synonym:  parallel park (partially) | Antonym:  park legally/properly | Verb
