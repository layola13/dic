feuds:/fjuːdz/
n.|世仇；长期不和；争执|A prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute, especially between two families or groups.|The two families have been locked in a bitter feud for generations. 这两个家族几代人以来一直陷于激烈的世仇中。|
近义词:quarrels, disputes, conflicts, rivalries
反义词:reconciliations, agreements, harmonies
词性:名词(复数)

feud:/fjuːd/
n.|世仇；长期不和；争执|A prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute, especially between two families or groups.|The Hatfields and McCoys engaged in a long and bloody feud. 哈特菲尔德家族和麦考伊家族进行了漫长而血腥的世仇。|
近义词:quarrel, dispute, conflict, rivalry
反义词:reconciliation, agreement, harmony
词性:名词(单数)

feud:/fjuːd/
v.|长期争斗；结仇|To engage in a prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute.|The two families have been feuding for years. 这两个家族多年来一直在争斗。|
近义词:quarrel, dispute, fight, clash
反义词:reconcile, compromise, cooperate
词性:动词
