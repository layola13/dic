kneelingly:/ˈniːlɪŋli/| adv.|跪着地，虔诚地|In a kneeling position; with reverence or submission.  Derived from "kneel" + "-ing" + "-ly".  The suffix "-ly" transforms the present participle "kneeling" into an adverb.|She knelt kneelingly before the altar. 她虔诚地在祭坛前跪下。|Near synonyms: reverently, humbly, submissively|Near antonyms: disrespectfully, arrogantly, defiantly|词性:副词
