culturally:/ˈkʌltʃərəli/
adv.
文化地，从文化角度
In a way that relates to the ideas, customs, and social behaviour of a society.
The program was culturally enriching for the students.  这个项目对学生来说在文化方面获益良多。
culturally diverse  文化多元的
The city is becoming increasingly culturally diverse. 这座城市正变得越来越多元化。

Synonyms: socially, customarily, traditionally
Antonyms: naturally, inherently


Etymology:
From culture + -al + -ly.  Culture comes from Latin cultura "cultivation, agriculture," figuratively "care, culture, training,"  from past participle stem of colere "to till."
