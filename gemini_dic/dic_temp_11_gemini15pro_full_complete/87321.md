herniation:/ˌhɜːrniˈeɪʃən/ | n. |疝；突出|The abnormal protrusion of an organ or other body structure through a defect or natural opening in a covering membrane, muscle, or bone.| The doctor diagnosed a herniation of the spinal disc. 医生诊断出椎间盘突出。|Near synonyms: protrusion, prolapse, extrusion| Antonyms: reduction, repositioning| noun

herniate:/ˈhɜːrnieɪt/ | v. |形成疝；突出|To protrude through an abnormal opening.|  The intervertebral disc herniated, causing severe back pain. 椎间盘突出，引起严重的背痛。|Near synonyms: protrude, bulge, rupture| Antonyms: reduce, retract| verb
