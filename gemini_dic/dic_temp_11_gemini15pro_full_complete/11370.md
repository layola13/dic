areopagist:/ˌæriˈɒpədʒɪst/
n.
雅典最高法院法官；法官；严肃的批评家
A member of the Areopagus, the ancient Athenian council or court, or a person similar to a member of that council, especially a severe critic.
例句：The areopagist listened intently to the arguments presented before the court.  这位雅典最高法院法官专心地听着法庭上提出的论点。

例句：He acted as an areopagist, offering his stern judgment on the matter. 他扮演了一个法官的角色，对这件事提出了严厉的批评。
近义词：judge, critic, arbiter
反义词:defendant, accused


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Areopagīta, from Ancient Greek Ἀρεοπαγίτης (Areopagítēs, “member of the Areopagus”), from Ἄρειος πάγος (Áreios págos, “Areopagus”).
