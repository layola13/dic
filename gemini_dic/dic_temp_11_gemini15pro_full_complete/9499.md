antisacerdotal:/ˌæntiˌsæsərˈdoʊtəl/
adjective
反僧侣的，反对僧侣权力的
opposed to the power or influence of priests or the priesthood.
The writer's antisacerdotal views were evident in his satirical portrayal of the clergy.  这位作家的反僧侣观点在他对神职人员的讽刺描绘中显而易见。
The king's antisacerdotal policies led to the confiscation of church lands. 国王的反僧侣政策导致教会土地被没收。
近义词: anticlerical
反义词: pro-clerical, sacerdotal
词性: adjective
