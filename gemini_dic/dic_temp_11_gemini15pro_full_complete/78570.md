/wɒt/ |pron.|什么|Used to ask for information about something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|which, who| |pronoun|
What is her phone number? 她电话号码是多少？

/wɒt/ |det.|什么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|What color is your car? 你的车是什么颜色的？|which, whatever| |determiner|
What book are you reading? 你在读什么书？

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for lunch yesterday? 你昨天午餐吃了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she told him before he left? 他走之前她告诉他什么了？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about before the police arrived? 警察到达之前他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon? 到明天下午他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
