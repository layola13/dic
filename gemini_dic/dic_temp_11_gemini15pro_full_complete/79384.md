glide:/ɡlaɪd/| v. n.|滑行，滑动；(鸟或飞机)滑翔；悄悄地走；流畅地进行|To move smoothly and effortlessly, as if without friction, often at a steady pace.  Also, a smooth, effortless movement.|The eagle glided effortlessly through the air.  (老鹰毫不费力地在空中滑翔。)  The skater glided across the ice. (滑冰者在冰上滑行。)|近义词：slide, drift, flow, soar|反义词：stumble, jolt, jar|词性:动词, 名词
