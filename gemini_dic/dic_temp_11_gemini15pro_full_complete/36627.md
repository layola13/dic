collectivising: /kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪŋ/
verb
集体化
The act of organizing something, such as agriculture or industry, on the basis of collective ownership or control.
The government was collectivising farms in the region. 政府正在该地区将农场集体化。
collectivization, nationalization
privatization, individualization
