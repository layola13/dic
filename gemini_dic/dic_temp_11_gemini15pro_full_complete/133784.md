nongenerative:/ˌnɒnˈdʒenərətɪv/
adjective
非生成的，非衍生的
Not generative; not capable of producing or creating something new.
This theory relies on a nongenerative model of language acquisition.  这个理论依赖于一种非生成的语言习得模型。
The artist's recent work has been criticized for being nongenerative and derivative.  这位艺术家最近的作品因缺乏创造性和衍生性而受到批评。
near synonyms: uncreative, unproductive, sterile, barren, derivative
antonyms: generative, creative, productive, fertile
词性: adjective
