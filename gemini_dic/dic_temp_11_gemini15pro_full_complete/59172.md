dysfunctionality:/dɪsˌfʌŋkʃəˈnæləti/
n.|功能失调；机能障碍|The state of being dysfunctional; impaired or abnormal functioning.  A system or process that is not working properly or effectively.|The family’s dysfunctionality stemmed from years of unresolved conflict. 这个家庭的功能失调源于多年未解决的冲突。|malfunction, impairment, abnormality|functionality, normality
dysfunctional:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəl/
adj.|功能失调的；机能障碍的|Not operating normally or properly. Characterized by impaired or abnormal functioning.|The dysfunctional family struggled to communicate effectively.  这个功能失调的家庭难以进行有效的沟通。|malfunctioning, impaired, abnormal|functional, normal
