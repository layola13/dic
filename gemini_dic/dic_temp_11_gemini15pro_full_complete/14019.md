aurora:/əˈrɔːrə/ | n. | 极光; 曙光; | A natural electrical phenomenon characterized by the appearance of streamers of reddish or greenish light in the sky, especially near the northern or southern magnetic pole. |  The auroras were breathtakingly beautiful.  极光美得令人窒息。 | synonyms: northern lights, southern lights, polar lights | antonyms: N/A | noun

aurorae:/əˈrɔːriː/ | n. | 极光（aurora的复数形式）| The plural form of aurora, referring to multiple instances of the natural light display. | We witnessed spectacular aurorae during our trip to Iceland. 我们在冰岛之旅中目睹了壮观的极光。| synonyms: northern lights, southern lights, polar lights | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Aurora, the Roman goddess of dawn.
