incorrigibly:/ˌɪnˈkɔːrɪdʒəbli/
adjective
无可救药地， incorrigibly
Not able to be corrected or reformed.
He is incorrigibly lazy. 他懒得无可救药。
Synonyms: irredeemably, hopelessly, inveterately
Antonyms: reformably, corrigibly
词性: adjective


incorrigibly:/ˌɪnˈkɔːrɪdʒəbli/
Etymology: From Late Latin incorrigibilis, from in- + corrigibilis (“corrigible”).
用法说明: Used to describe someone or something that is bad in a way that cannot be changed or improved.
