nonpotable:/ˌnɒnˈpəʊtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈpoʊtəbl/| adj.|非饮用的，不可饮用的|Not safe or suitable for drinking.|The water in the stream is nonpotable and should not be consumed.  溪里的水不能饮用，不应该饮用。|The factory discharged nonpotable water into the river. 这家工厂向河里排放了非饮用的水。| 近义词：undrinkable, contaminated, polluted|反义词:potable, drinkable|词性:形容词

non-:  prefix meaning "not"  非，不
potable:  from Latin *potabilis*, from potare "to drink"  可饮用的
