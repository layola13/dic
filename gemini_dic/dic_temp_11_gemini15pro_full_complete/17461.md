bearberry:/ˈberˌberi/ /ˈbɛrˌbɛri/ | n. |熊果，熊果植物|A low-growing evergreen shrub (Arctostaphylos uva-ursi) with leathery leaves, small pink or white flowers, and red berries, native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.  It is used in herbal medicine as a urinary antiseptic.|The bearberry grows well in sandy soil. 熊果在沙质土壤中生长良好。|Synonyms: kinnikinnick, uva-ursi|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
