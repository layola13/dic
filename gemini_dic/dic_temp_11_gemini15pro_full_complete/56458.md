dolorous:/ˈdoʊləərəs/, /ˈdɒlərəs/ | adj. | 悲痛的，忧伤的 | Feeling or expressing great sorrow or distress. |  The dolorous news of her death cast a shadow over the entire community. 她去世的悲痛消息给整个社区蒙上了一层阴影。

The funeral procession was a dolorous sight. 送葬队伍是一幅悲哀的景象。

近义词: sorrowful, mournful, grievous, doleful, lugubrious | 反义词: joyful, happy, cheerful | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *dolorous, borrowed from Old French dolereus, dolorous (“painful, sorrowful”), from Latin dolōrōsus (“full of pain, causing pain”), from dolor (“pain, grief”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘full of, prone to, characterized by’).
