auspices:/ˈɔːspɪsɪz/
n.
保护,支持;赞助,主办
protection or support; patronage; sponsorship
The event was held under the auspices of the local council.  这次活动是在地方议会的赞助下举行的。
patronage, support, backing
opposition, hindrance
noun

词源：From Latin *auspicia, plural of *auspicium (“divination by observing birds”), from avis (“bird”) + specio (“I look”). Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European *h₂ówis (“bird”) and *speḱ- (“to see, observe”).
