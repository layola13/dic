lout:/laʊt/| n. | 粗鲁的人；笨拙的人；流氓 | A rude, aggressive, and noisy person, especially a young man. |  He behaved like a complete lout, shouting and swearing at everyone. 他表现得像个十足的流氓，对每个人大喊大叫，满嘴脏话。| Synonyms: hooligan, thug, ruffian, vandal, boor | Antonyms: gentleman, lady, refined person | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *louten, from Old Norse *lútr (“bent, stooping”), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hleuzaną (“to bend down, stoop”)
