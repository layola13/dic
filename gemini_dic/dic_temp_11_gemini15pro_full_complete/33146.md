chromascope: /ˈkrəʊməskoʊp/ (UK) /ˈkroʊməskoʊp/ (US) | n. | 色度镜，色度计 | An instrument for exhibiting the optical effects of color. It typically uses prisms or diffraction gratings to separate light into its component colors. |  The chromascope projected a rainbow of colors onto the wall. 色度镜将彩虹般的颜色投射到墙上。 |  The artist used a chromascope to study the interplay of light and color.  这位艺术家使用色度镜来研究光与色的相互作用。| synonyms: spectroscope, colorimeter | antonyms: N/A | noun
