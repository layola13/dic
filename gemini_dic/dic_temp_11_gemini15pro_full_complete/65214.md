etiolated:/ˈiːtiəˌleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 苍白的；黄化的；柔弱的 |  (of a plant) pale and drawn out due to a lack of light; deprived of natural vigor.  | The etiolated seedlings were pale and spindly.  这些黄化的幼苗苍白而细长。 | synonyms: bleached, blanched, pale, wan, weakened, feeble | antonyms: robust, healthy, strong, vigorous | adjective

etiolate:/ˈiːtiəˌleɪt/ | v. | (使)变白；(使)黄化；(使)衰弱 | To cause (a plant) to become pale and drawn out due to a lack of light; to deprive of natural vigor. |  Lack of sunlight etiolated the plants. 缺乏阳光使植物变得黄化。| synonyms: blanch, bleach, weaken, enfeeble | antonyms: strengthen, invigorate | verb
