adapting:/əˈdæptɪŋ/
v.|适应; 改编|The process of changing to suit different conditions or a new purpose.|例句：They are adapting to the new environment. 他们正在适应新环境。|近义词：adjusting, modifying, accommodating|反义词:resisting, opposing|词性:动词现在分词
adapting:/əˈdæptɪŋ/
adj.|适应的|Suitable for a new use or purpose; modified.|例句：An adapting species is more likely to survive.  一个适应性强的物种更有可能生存。|近义词：flexible, adjustable|反义词:inflexible, rigid|词性:形容词
