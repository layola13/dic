moodinesses:/ˈmuːdinəsɪz/
n.
喜怒无常，情绪反复变化
The state of being moody, characterized by frequent changes in mood.
Her moodinesses made it difficult to predict her reactions. 她的喜怒无常让人难以预测她的反应。
近义词：capriciousness, temperamentalness, variability, volatility
反义词：stability, even-temperedness, calmness
词性：名词 (plural)

词源: From moody + -ness + -es (plural suffix)
"moody" comes from Middle English *modi, from Old English *mōdig "proud, arrogant," from *mōd "mind, mood, courage."
