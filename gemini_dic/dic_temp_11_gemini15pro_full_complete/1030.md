accelerated:/əkˈseləreɪtɪd/ /əkˈsɛləˌreɪtɪd/
adjective
加速的，加快了的
Increased in speed or rate.
例句：The accelerated pace of life in the city can be overwhelming. 城市生活节奏的加快可能让人不堪重负。

例句：The project's timeline was accelerated due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，该项目的进度加快了。

近义词：faster, quicker, hastened, expedited
反义词：decelerated, slowed, reduced

词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "accelerate," which comes from the Latin word "accelerare," meaning "to hasten."  "Accelerare" is formed from "ad-" (to, towards) + "celer" (swift).
