compare:/kəmˈpɛr/ (US) /kəmˈpeə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|比较，对照，比喻|To examine (two or more objects, ideas, people, etc.) in order to discover similarities and differences.|She compared the two products carefully before making a decision. 她仔细比较了两种产品之后才做决定。|近义词：contrast, juxtapose, liken, equate|反义词：differ, distinguish|verb

compare:/kəmˈpɛr/ (US) /kəmˈpeə(r)/ (UK)| n.|比较，对照，比喻|The act of comparing two or more things.|There's no comparison between the two restaurants - this one is much better.  这两家餐馆没有可比性——这家好多了。|近义词：comparison, analogy, similitude|反义词：difference, distinction|noun
