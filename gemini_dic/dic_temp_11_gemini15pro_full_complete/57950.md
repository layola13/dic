drip-fed: /ˈdrɪpˌfɛd/ | adj. | 慢慢地提供的，一点一点地给予的 |  Released or supplied in small amounts over a period of time.  | The information was drip-fed to the media over several weeks.  这些信息在几周内被一点一点地透露给媒体。 |  The company drip-fed funds into the project. 公司逐渐地将资金投入到项目中。| gradual, piecemeal, bit-by-bit | sudden, all at once | adjective

drip-fed: /ˈdrɪpˌfɛd/ | v. |  慢慢地提供，一点一点地给予 | To release or supply something in small amounts over a period of time.  | They drip-fed the information to the public. 他们一点一点地把信息透露给公众。 | The charity was drip-fed donations throughout the year.  这家慈善机构全年都在接受零星的捐款。| administer gradually, release slowly, trickle | deluge, flood | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "drip" (to fall in small drops) + "fed" (past participle of "feed," to supply).  The image is of something being supplied like liquid dripping slowly.
