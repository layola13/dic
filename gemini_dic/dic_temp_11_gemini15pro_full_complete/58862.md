durable:/ˈdjʊərəbl/ /ˈdʊrəbl/| adj.|持久的，耐用的|Able to last a long time without becoming damaged.|This sofa is made of durable fabric. 这款沙发是用耐用面料制成的.|近义词：long-lasting, sturdy, robust, tough|反义词:fragile, flimsy, weak|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *durable*, from Old French *durable*, from Latin *dūrābilis* (“lasting, enduring”), from *dūrō* (“to harden, to last, to endure”) + *-bilis* (suffix forming adjectives meaning “able to be”, “able to”, “able to be done”, or “tending to”).
