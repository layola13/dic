adhesion:/ədˈhiːʒən/ /ædˈhiːʒən/
n.
粘附；粘着；粘连
The adhesion of the glue is very strong.  胶水的粘性很强。
The surgeon separated the tissues with minimal adhesion formation.  外科医生将组织分开，尽量减少粘连的形成。
近义词：sticking, bonding, adherence
反义词：separation, detachment
词性：名词

Etymology:  From Latin adhaesiō (“a sticking to”), from adhaerēre (“to stick to”), from ad- (“to”) + haerēre (“to stick”).
