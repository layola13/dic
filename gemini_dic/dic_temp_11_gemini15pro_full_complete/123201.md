monochromes: /ˈmɒnəkrəʊmz/ /ˈmɑːnəkroʊmz/ | n. | 单色画, 单色照片; 单色印刷品 | A photograph or picture developed or executed in black and white or in varying tones of only one color. | The gallery featured an exhibition of monochromes, stark images that captured the essence of the desert landscape.  画廊举办了一个单色画展览，这些鲜明的图像捕捉到了沙漠景观的精髓。| synonyms: monochrome pictures, black and white photographs, grayscale images | antonyms: polychromes, color photographs | noun

monochrome: /ˈmɒnəkrəʊm/ /ˈmɑːnəkroʊmz/ | adj. | 单色的 | Having or consisting of one color or shades of one color. | The artist preferred to work in monochrome, finding beauty in the subtle gradations of gray.  这位艺术家喜欢用单色创作，在灰色的微妙渐变中发现美。| synonyms: one-color, single-colored, monotone | antonyms: polychrome, multicolored | adjective

monochrome: /ˈmɒnəkrəʊm/ /ˈmɑːnəkroʊmz/ | n. | 单色 | A painting, design, photograph, or other visual representation executed primarily in shades of a single color. | The room was decorated in a striking monochrome of deep blue. 房间以深蓝色的醒目单色装饰。 | synonyms: one-color scheme, single-color palette | antonyms: polychrome, multicolored scheme | noun
