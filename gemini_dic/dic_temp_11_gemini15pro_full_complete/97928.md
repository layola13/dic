innocuousness:/ɪˈnɑːkjuəsnəs/
n.
无害；无毒
the state or quality of being innocuous
The innocuousness of the comment surprised everyone.  这条评论的无害让每个人都感到惊讶。

近义词:harmlessness, safety, inoffensiveness
反义词:harmfulness, dangerousness, toxicity
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From innocuous + -ness.  Innocuous comes from Late Latin innocuus  "harmless," from in- "not" + nocuus "harmful," from nocere "to harm."
