immobilized: /ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/ (US) /ɪˈməʊbəlaɪzd/ (UK) | adj. |  固定的，无法移动的 | Made unable to move or function. |  The broken leg was immobilized in a cast.  腿断了，用石膏固定住了。 |  fixed, stationary, stuck | mobile, movable | adjective

immobilize: /ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ (US) /ɪˈməʊbəlaɪz/ (UK) | v. |  使固定，使无法移动 | To prevent something or someone from moving or operating as normal. | The police immobilized the vehicle with a tire clamp. 警察用轮胎夹锁住了车辆。| fix, restrict, disable | mobilize, activate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From im- (not) + mobile (movable) + -ize (to make). Ultimately from Latin mobilis "movable," from movere "to move."
