duty: /ˈdjuːti/ (US) /ˈdʒuːti/ (UK) | adj. (less common) | 值班的，上班的 | Relating to or required by duty; working at a particular time. |  The duty officer answered the phone. 值班官员接了电话。 | on-duty, working | off-duty | adjective
