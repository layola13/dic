judaistic:/ˌdʒuːdeɪˈɪstɪk/| adj.|犹太主义的；犹太教的|Relating to Judaism, its beliefs, practices, or culture, often with a connotation of strict adherence to traditional customs and laws, sometimes perceived as excessively so.|Her lifestyle was very judaistic, adhering strictly to kosher dietary laws. 她的生活方式非常犹太化，严格遵守犹太教的饮食戒律。|Synonyms: Jewish, Hebraic|Antonyms: secular, non-religious|adjective
