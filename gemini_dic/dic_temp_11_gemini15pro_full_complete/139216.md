objectors:/əbˈdʒɛktərz/
n.
反对者；抗议者；异议者
A person who expresses or feels disapproval or opposition to something.
例句：The objectors to the new highway plan voiced their concerns at the public meeting.  新的公路计划的反对者在公众会议上表达了他们的担忧。

例句：Several objectors raised concerns about the environmental impact of the project.  一些反对者对该项目的环境影响提出了担忧。

近义词：protestors, dissenters, opponents
反义词：supporters, proponents, advocates
词性: noun
