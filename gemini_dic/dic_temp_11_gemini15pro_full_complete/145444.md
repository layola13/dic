overchildishly: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃaɪldɪʃli/
adverb
过于幼稚地，过分孩子气地
in a way that is too childish
He giggled overchildishly at the silly joke. 他对这个愚蠢的笑话孩子气地咯咯笑着。
She reacted overchildishly to the criticism. 她对批评的反应过于幼稚。
近义词: immaturely, naively, foolishly
反义词: maturely, responsibly, wisely
词性: adverb

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "childish" (adjective meaning "of or like a child") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
