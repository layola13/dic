deconglomerated: /ˌdiːkənˈɡlɒməreɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˌdiːkənˈɡlɑːməreɪtɪd/ (US)
adjective
去集团化的；分解的
Broken down into smaller, independent units, especially of a large business organization.
The company deconglomerated its holdings and sold off several subsidiaries.  这家公司将其持有的资产去集团化，并出售了几个子公司。
deconglomerated its assets and focused on core businesses.  这家公司将其资产去集团化，并专注于核心业务。
Near Synonyms:  decentralized, dispersed, fragmented, separated, diversified
Antonyms:  consolidated, centralized, merged, integrated
词性: adjective
