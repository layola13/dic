overimposed:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊzd/| adj.|重叠的，叠加的，过分强加的|placed or laid over something else, often unnecessarily or excessively;  forced upon someone.|The overimposed images created a confusing blur. 重叠的图像造成了一片模糊不清的景象。|Synonyms: superimposed, overlaid, added, inflicted|Antonyms: removed, separate, underlying|adjective

overimpose:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊz/| v.|重叠，叠加，过分强加|to place or lay something over something else, often unnecessarily or excessively; to force something upon someone.|The artist overimposed several layers of paint to achieve the desired effect.  艺术家重叠了几层颜料以达到预期的效果。 He overimposed his will on his children. 他把自己的意愿强加给孩子们。|Synonyms: superimpose, overlay, add, inflict|Antonyms: remove, separate|verb
