appassionata:/əˌpæʃəˈnɑːtə/ | adj. n.| 热情的；充满激情的；《热情奏鸣曲》（特指贝多芬的钢琴奏鸣曲作品57号）| Characterized by or expressing intense emotion; Beethoven's Piano Sonata No. 23 in F minor, Op. 57, popularly known as the Appassionata. | The pianist played the Appassionata with great passion.  钢琴家充满激情地演奏了《热情奏鸣曲》。|She delivered an appassionata speech about the importance of education. 她发表了一场关于教育重要性的充满激情的演讲。| 近义词: passionate, fervent, ardent, fiery | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, cold | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: Italian, from appassionato, past participle of appassionare 'to impassion,' from ad- 'to' + passione 'passion.'
