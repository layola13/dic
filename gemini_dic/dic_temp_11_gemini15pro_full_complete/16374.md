barbershop: /ˈbɑːrbərʃɑːp/ (US) /ˈbɑːbəʃɒp/ (UK)| n.| 理发店；理发铺|A place where men get their hair cut.| I went to the barbershop to get a haircut. 我去理发店理发了。|近义词：barber's, hair salon (for both men and women)|反义词: None|词性:noun


barbershops: /ˈbɑːrbərʃɑːps/ (US) /ˈbɑːbəʃɒps/ (UK)| n.| 理发店（复数）|Plural form of barbershop, referring to more than one barbershop.|There are three barbershops on this street. 这条街上有三家理发店。|近义词：barber's (plural), hair salons|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From barber + shop. "Barber" comes from Old French "barbier," meaning "barber," ultimately from Latin "barba," meaning "beard." "Shop" comes from Old English "scoppa," meaning "shed, booth."
