lip-readers:/ˈlɪp ˌriːdərz/ | n. |读唇者|People who understand speech by watching the movements of a speaker's lips. |The deaf students were all excellent lip-readers. 这些聋哑学生都是优秀的读唇者。|Lip-readers can often understand conversations even in noisy environments.  即使在嘈杂的环境中，读唇者也常常能理解对话。|近义词:speechreaders|反义词:None|Noun
