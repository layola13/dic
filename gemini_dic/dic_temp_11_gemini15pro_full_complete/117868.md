melodiograph:/məˈləʊdiəɡrɑːf/
noun
旋律记录器，旋律图谱
A device or system for recording or representing melodies.
The melodiograph transcribed the folk tune directly onto paper. 旋律记录器直接将民歌曲调转录到纸上。
synonyms: melody writer, music notation software, music transcriber
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
