leaning-on:/ˈliːnɪŋ ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 依靠，依赖 |  To depend on someone or something for support, either physically or emotionally.  "Leaning on" literally describes physical support, but it's often used figuratively to describe emotional or psychological dependence. |  He was leaning on a cane for support. 他拄着拐杖支撑着自己。


She's been leaning on her friends for emotional support since her husband died. 自从丈夫去世后，她一直依靠朋友们的情感支持。 |  depending on, relying on, counting on | independent, self-reliant | verb phrase
