overdemanded:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈmændɪd/| adj.|需求过高的，供不应求的|Excessive demand for something; more demand than can be met.|例句：Skilled workers in the tech industry are currently overdemanded.  科技行业的技术工人目前供不应求。

例句：Overdemanded products often lead to price increases.  供不应求的产品往往会导致价格上涨。|近义词：sought-after, in-demand, popular|反义词：oversupplied, abundant, plentiful|词性:形容词
