guardhouses:/ˈɡɑːrdˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
警卫室；岗亭
A small building for a guard or guards, typically located at the entrance to a military base, prison, or other secure area.
The soldier stood guard in the guardhouse.  士兵在警卫室站岗。
The guardhouses were positioned at each corner of the prison walls.  警卫室位于监狱围墙的每个角落。
synonyms: guardroom, gatehouse, sentry box
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider concepts like "open space," "public area")
