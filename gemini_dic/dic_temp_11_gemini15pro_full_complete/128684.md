nictitation: /ˌnɪktɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 眨眼；瞬膜的眨动 | the act of blinking or winking, especially with the nictitating membrane | The nictitation of the owl was almost imperceptible. 猫头鹰的眨眼几乎难以察觉。 | Synonyms: blink, wink, flutter | Antonyms: stare, gaze | noun
