delayable:/dɪˈleɪəbl/
adj.
可延期的，可推迟的
able to be delayed or postponed.
The meeting is delayable if everyone agrees.  会议如果每个人都同意是可以推迟的。
The launch of the new product is delayable until next month. 新产品的发布可以推迟到下个月。
近义词:postponable, deferrable
反义词:urgent, immediate
词性:adjective
