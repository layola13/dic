interlobar:/ˌɪntərˈloʊbɑːr/
adjective
叶间的；小叶间的
Situated between lobes, particularly referring to anatomical structures.
The interlobar arteries supply blood to the kidneys.  叶间动脉向肾脏供血。
The interlobar fissures separate the lobes of the lungs. 叶间裂将肺的叶分开。
近义词:interlobular
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "intralobar" - within a lobe)
词性:adjective
