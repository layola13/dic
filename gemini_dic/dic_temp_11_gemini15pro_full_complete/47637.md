debing:/ˈdiːbɪŋ/| n.| 去毛刺; 去飞边; |The process of removing burrs or sharp edges from a manufactured part.  去除制造零件上的毛刺或锋利边缘的过程。|Debinging is an important step in the manufacturing process. 去毛刺是制造过程中重要的一步。| The factory uses a specialized machine for debinging the plastic parts.  这家工厂使用专门的机器去除塑料零件的毛刺。 |synonyms: trimming, deflashing, smoothing | antonyms: roughening, sharpening |noun
