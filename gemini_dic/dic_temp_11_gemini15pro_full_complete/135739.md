nonproblems:/ˈnɒnˌprɒbləmz/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌprɑːbləmz/| n. |小问题，非问题，不构成问题的事情|A minor issue or something that is not a significant concern.  It often suggests the speaker believes the issue is being overblown or doesn't warrant the attention it's receiving.|The staff shortages were dismissed as nonproblems by management.  管理层认为员工短缺并非问题。|Near synonyms: minor issues, trifles, insignificant matters|Antonyms: serious problems, major issues, crises|Noun
