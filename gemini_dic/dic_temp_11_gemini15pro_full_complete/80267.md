goggly:/ˈɡɒɡli/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːɡli/ (US)| adj.|瞪大眼睛的，眼球突出的|Having large, staring, and protuberant eyes, often as a result of surprise, fear, or excitement.  The eyes themselves can be described as goggly.|She gave me a goggly look when I told her the news. 她听到这个消息后瞪大了眼睛看着我。|Synonyms: wide-eyed, pop-eyed, bug-eyed, goggle-eyed|Antonyms: narrow-eyed, squinty-eyed|Adjective
