mullock:/ˈmʌlək/ | n. | 碎石，废石，无用的岩石 | Waste rock from a mine, or other worthless rock or earth. |  The miners had to remove tons of mullock before they reached the coal seam. 矿工们必须清除大量的废石才能到达煤层。

mullock:/ˈmʌlək/ | n. |  (澳大利亚俚语) 垃圾，废物，无用之物 | (Australian slang) Rubbish, trash, or anything worthless. | Don't fill the shed with all that mullock!  别把棚子里堆满那些垃圾！


mullock:/ˈmʌlək/ | n. |  (澳大利亚俚语) 废话，胡说 | (Australian slang) Nonsense, rubbish talk. | He was talking a load of mullock. 他在胡说八道。


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly from Cornish *mollek 'rotten' or from Middle Dutch *mollick 'soft earth'.

SYNONYMS->
debris, rubble, waste rock, tailings (mining), rubbish, trash, junk, garbage (informal), nonsense (informal), drivel (informal)

ANTONYMS->
treasure, valuable, ore (mining)
