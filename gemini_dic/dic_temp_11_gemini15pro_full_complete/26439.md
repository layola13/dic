byproduct:/ˈbaɪprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品；副作用 | something produced in addition to the main product |  The company uses the waste from its manufacturing process as a byproduct to create fertilizer.  这家公司将其生产过程中的废料用作副产品来生产肥料。 | synonyms: side effect, spin-off, consequence, result | antonyms: main product, primary product | noun

byproduct:/ˈbaɪprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 附带结果；意外后果 | an incidental or unintended result | The increase in crime was an unfortunate byproduct of the economic downturn.  犯罪率的上升是经济衰退带来的一个不幸的附带结果。 | synonyms: side effect, spin-off, consequence, result | antonyms: intended consequence, planned result | noun
