martyrology:/ˌmɑːrtəˈrɒlədʒi/ /ˌmɑːrtəˈrɑːlədʒi/
n.
殉道者名录；殉道史；殉道者传
A catalog or list of martyrs.  A history of martyrs, especially of those who have died for their religious beliefs.  An account of the sufferings of martyrs.
The martyrology of the early church is filled with inspiring stories of courage.  早期教会的殉道史充满了鼓舞人心的勇气故事。
synonyms:  history of martyrs, record of martyrs, chronicle of martyrs, register of martyrs, calendar of saints
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider perhaps "hagiography" which focuses on saints generally not just martyrs)
noun
