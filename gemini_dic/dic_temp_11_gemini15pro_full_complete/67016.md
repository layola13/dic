expirer:/ɪkˈspaɪərər/| n.| 到期者；期满者；失效者|Someone or something that expires.  A thing, such as a license or subscription, that is no longer valid.|My driver's license is an expirer next month. 我的驾照下个月就过期了.|Near synonyms: non-renewer, lapse|Antonym: renewer|noun

expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/| v.| 到期；期满；失效；终止；死亡|To come to an end; to cease to be valid. To die.|My passport expires next year. 我的护照明年到期。|Near synonyms: terminate, cease, lapse, run out, die|Antonyms: renew, extend, begin, start|verb
