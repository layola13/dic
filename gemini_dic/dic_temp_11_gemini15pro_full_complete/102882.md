javascripts:/ˈdʒɑːvəˌskrɪpts/| n. |JavaScript（通常写成JS）|JavaScript, often abbreviated JS, is a programming language that is one of the core technologies of the World Wide Web, alongside HTML and CSS. Over 98% of websites use JavaScript on the client side for webpage behavior, often incorporating third-party libraries. |Example:  The website uses javascripts to create interactive elements.  该网站使用JavaScript来创建交互元素。|近义词: ECMAScript (a standardized version of JavaScript), JScript (Microsoft's dialect of JavaScript)|反义词:  N/A (JavaScript doesn't have a direct opposite as a programming language).|词性: noun
