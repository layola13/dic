bonsai:/ˈbɒnsaɪ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːnsaɪ/ (US)| n. |盆景|A miniature tree or shrub grown in a shallow pot, trained to mimic the shape and scale of a full-size tree.|She has a beautiful collection of bonsai trees in her apartment. 她公寓里有一系列美丽的盆景。|The bonsai master carefully pruned the tiny branches. 盆景大师小心翼翼地修剪着细小的树枝。|近义词:miniature tree, dwarf tree|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) full-size tree|Noun
