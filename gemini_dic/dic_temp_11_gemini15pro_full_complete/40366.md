contractual:/kənˈtræktʃuəl/
adjective
契约的；合同的
Relating to or part of a formal agreement or contract.
例句：We have a contractual obligation to finish the project by the end of the year. 我们有合同义务在年底前完成这个项目。

例句：The company is bound by contractual agreements with its suppliers.  该公司受其与供应商签订的合同协议的约束。

近义词：agreed, stipulated, binding, covenant
反义词：non-contractual, informal

词性
adjective
