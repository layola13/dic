aspersion:/əˈspɜːrʒən/
|n.|诽谤，中伤|an attack on the reputation or integrity of someone or something|The politician's reputation was damaged by the aspersions cast by his opponent. 这位政治家的名誉受到了对手的诽谤。|
|近义词：slur, defamation, calumny, vilification|
|反义词：praise, commendation, acclaim|
|词性：名词|

aspersion:/əˈspɜːrʒən/
|n.|洒水，喷洒(尤指宗教仪式)|the act of sprinkling, especially in a religious ceremony|The priest made the aspersion with holy water.  牧师用圣水洒水。|
|近义词：sprinkling|
|反义词：-|
|词性：名词|

PHRASE->
cast aspersions on = 诽谤，中伤
to cast aspersions on someone's character = 诽谤某人的品格
to cast aspersions on someone's integrity = 诽谤某人的诚信
to cast aspersions on someone's reputation = 诽谤某人的声誉
to cast unwarranted aspersions = 进行无根据的诽谤


词源：源自拉丁语aspersio，意为“洒水，喷洒”。最初是指宗教仪式上的洒水，后来引申为“诽谤，中伤”，因为诽谤就像泼脏水一样玷污名誉。
