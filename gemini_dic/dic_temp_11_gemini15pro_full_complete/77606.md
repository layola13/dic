geer:/ɡɪər/ | n. | 齿轮; 传动装置; 排挡; (尤指汽车的)档位; 衣服; 装备; |A toothed wheel that works with others to alter the relation between the speed of a driving mechanism (such as an engine or motor) and the speed of the driven parts (such as the wheels of a vehicle).| The car's gears were grinding. 这辆车的齿轮发出嘎吱嘎吱的响声。| synonyms: cog, gearwheel, equipment, apparatus, paraphernalia, outfit, tackle, kit | antonyms:  (For equipment) lack, deficiency | noun
