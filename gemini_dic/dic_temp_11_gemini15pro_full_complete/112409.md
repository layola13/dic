lothario:/loʊˈθɛərioʊ/
noun
花花公子，浪荡子
A man who habitually seduces and deceives women.
例句：He was known as a lothario, breaking hearts wherever he went. 他被称为花花公子，走到哪里都会伤透人心。

例句：The play featured a charming but unscrupulous lothario.  这出戏以一个迷人但不择手段的花花公子为主角。


近义词：womanizer, playboy, philanderer, seducer, Don Juan, Casanova
反义词：faithful husband, loyal partner
词性：n.
