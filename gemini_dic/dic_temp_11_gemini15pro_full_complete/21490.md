blubberlike:/ˈblʌbərlaɪk/
adjective
像鲸脂的；肥胖的
Resembling blubber; fat and flabby.
The walrus had a thick, blubberlike layer of fat under its skin. 海象皮下有一层厚厚的、像鲸脂一样的脂肪。
The man's blubberlike arms jiggled as he walked.  那个男人肥胖的胳膊在他走路时晃来晃去。
近义词:fatty, flabby, obese, plump
反义词:thin, lean, slender
词性:adjective

词源:
From "blubber" + "-like".  "Blubber" comes from Middle Dutch "bluber" (bubble, blister), referring to the way whale blubber looks.  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
