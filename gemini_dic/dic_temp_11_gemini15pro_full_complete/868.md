abstrusely:/əbˈstruːsli/ | adv. | 深奥地；难解地 | In a manner that is difficult to understand; obscurely. | He explained the complex theory abstrusely, leaving the students confused. 他深奥地解释了这个复杂的理论，让学生们感到困惑。| synonym: obscurely, esoterically, cryptically, enigmatically | antonym: clearly, simply, plainly, understandably | adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *abstrusus, past participle of abstrudere “to thrust away, hide,” from ab- “away from” + trudere “to thrust, push.”  The adverbial form is derived by adding "-ly" to the adjective "abstruse."
