coalescent:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛsənt/| adj. n.|合并的，聚结的；聚结体|Growing together; uniting into a whole.  Referring to the process by which, looking back through time, the genealogy of a sample of individuals from a population merges to a common ancestor.|The coalescent model is used in genetics.  聚结模型用于遗传学。|近义词：merging, uniting, combining, fusing, joining|反义词：diverging, separating, splitting|adjective, noun

coalesce:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛs/| v.|合并，聚结|To come or grow together to form one thing or system.|The puddles coalesced into a small pond. 水坑合并成一个小池塘。|近义词：merge, unite, combine, fuse, join|反义词：diverge, separate, split|verb
