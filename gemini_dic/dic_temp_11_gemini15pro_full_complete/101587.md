irradiate:/ɪˈreɪdieɪt/ (US), /ɪˈreɪdɪeɪt/ (UK)| v. |照射，辐射；照亮，照耀；传播，散布|To expose to radiation, especially X-rays or other ionizing radiation. To illuminate brightly. To spread or diffuse something, like knowledge or information. |The sun irradiated the Earth. 太阳照射着地球。|The doctor irradiated the tumor. 医生用放射线照射肿瘤。|近义词：illuminate, shine, radiate, beam, emanate|反义词：darken, obscure, conceal|词性:动词

词源: From Latin irradiatus, past participle of irradiare ("to shine upon"), from in- ("in, on, upon") + radiare ("to shine, radiate"), from radius ("ray, spoke").
