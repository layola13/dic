dyke:/daɪk/| n.|堤坝，沟渠；（俚语，冒犯性）女同性恋|A long wall or embankment built to prevent flooding from the sea or a river. (Also a derogatory slang term for a lesbian.)|例句：The dyke protects the farmland from flooding. 这堤坝保护农田免受洪水侵袭。

例句：She was called a dyke because of her short hair and masculine clothes.  因为她的短发和男性化的衣服，她被称为女同性恋。(冒犯性用法，请谨慎使用)|近义词：levee, embankment (for the non-slang meaning), lesbian (for the slang meaning, though less offensive)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the literal meaning.  No antonym for the slang meaning should be used as it would likely be offensive.)|词性:noun


dyke:/daɪk/| v.|筑堤，防洪|To build a dyke or embankment.|例句：They dyked the river to prevent flooding. 他们在河边筑堤以防洪水。

例句：The land was dyked to create new farmland.  这块土地被筑堤造田。|近义词：embank, levee|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:verb
