deters:/dɪˈtɜːrz/
verb
阻止，威慑，使不敢
discourages someone from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.
The heavy fines deter people from speeding.  巨额罚款阻止了人们超速行驶。
The security cameras are meant to deter shoplifters.  这些监控摄像头旨在震慑商店扒手。
synonyms: discourages, prevents, hinders, obstructs
antonyms: encourages, promotes, incites, instigates
词性: 动词 (verb)
