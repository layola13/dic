mountaineered:/ˌmaʊntɪˈnɪərd/ | v. | 登山; 攀登; 克服(困难) |  engaged in mountain climbing; overcame (a difficulty) |  They mountaineered in the Himalayas. 他们在喜马拉雅山脉登山。| 近义词：climbed, ascended, scaled, conquered | 反义词：descended | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From mountain + -eer (agent suffix) + -ed (past tense suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *montanea (mountainous region), from mons (mountain).
