bat-around:/ˌbæt əˈraʊnd/ | n. |  非正式讨论；试探性讨论 | An informal discussion of an idea or plan, typically involving a number of people, in order to test reactions or gain feedback. | We had a quick bat-around to see what everyone thought of the proposal. 我们简单地讨论了一下，看看大家对这个提议有什么看法。| synonyms: discussion, brainstorming, debate, exchange of views | antonyms: lecture, monologue, decree | noun


bat-around:/ˌbæt əˈraʊnd/ | v. |  非正式地讨论；试探性地讨论 | To discuss an idea or plan informally with a number of people, in order to test reactions or gain feedback. |  They batted the idea around for a while before making a decision.  他们在做决定之前对这个想法讨论了一段时间。 | synonyms: discuss, brainstorm, debate, kick around | antonyms: dictate, impose, decree | verb
