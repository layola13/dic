nonsubstitutions:/ˌnɒnsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃənz/ | n. | 非替代品；非替换物 |  The policy of nonsubstitutions ensures the integrity of the original ingredients.  非替代品的政策保证了原始成分的完整性。 | The contract stipulated nonsubstitutions of materials. 合同规定了材料的非替代性。| synonyms: replacements, alternatives | antonyms: equivalents, substitutes | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "substitution" (from Latin "substituere" - to put in place of).
