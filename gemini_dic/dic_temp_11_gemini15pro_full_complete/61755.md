empathetic:/ˌempəˈθetɪk/ (US), /ˌem.pəˈθet.ɪk/ (UK)| adj.|有同理心的，感同身受的|Showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another.|例句：She was very empathetic when I told her about my problems. 她在我向她讲述我的问题时非常有同理心。|近义词：compassionate, understanding, sensitive|反义词：apathetic, uncaring, indifferent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek "empatheia" (feeling into), from "en-" (in) + "pathos" (feeling).
