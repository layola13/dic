imprisons:/ɪmˈprɪzənz/
verb
监禁，关押
to put someone in prison
例句：The government imprisons people who break the law. 政府将违法的人关进监狱。

例句：He was imprisoned for ten years for his crimes. 他因犯罪被监禁了十年。

近义词：confine, incarcerate, jail, detain
反义词：release, liberate, free
词性：v.
