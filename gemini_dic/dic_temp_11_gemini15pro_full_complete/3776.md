agitprop:/ˈædʒɪtprɒp/ (US) /ˈædʒɪtprɒp/ (UK)| n.|政治宣传|Agitprop is political propaganda, especially the kind that is used to promote the ideas of a particular group or party.|The government used agitprop to stir up support for the war.|Synonyms: propaganda, indoctrination, brainwashing|Antonyms: education, enlightenment, truth|Noun

Etymology:  From Russian агитпроп (agitprop), a portmanteau of  агитация (agitacija, “agitation”) and пропаганда (propaganda, “propaganda”).
