bissextile:/bɪˈsɛkstɪl/ /baɪˈsɛkstɪl/
adjective
闰年的；闰日的
Relating to a leap year.  A leap year itself.
The bissextile year occurs every four years. 闰年每四年出现一次。
The Gregorian calendar provides for a bissextile day every four years, with the exception of century years not divisible by 400. 格里高利历规定每四年有一个闰日，除了不能被400整除的世纪年。
near synonym: leap
antonym: common
word type: adjective
