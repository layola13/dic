mythologist:/mɪˈθɒlədʒɪst/| n.|神话学家；神话研究者|A mythologist is a person who studies myths, legends, and folklore. 神话学家是研究神话、传说和民间故事的人。|例句：The mythologist spent years researching ancient Greek myths.  这位神话学家花了数年时间研究古希腊神话。

|例句：She is a renowned mythologist specializing in Norse mythology. 她是一位著名的北欧神话专家。|近义词：folklorist, student of mythology|反义词:|noun
