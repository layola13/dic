engulfment:/ɪnˈɡʌlfmənt/| n.|吞没， engulf 的名词形式|The engulfment of the town by floodwaters was a tragedy.  小镇被洪水吞没是一场悲剧。|The flames quickly spread, leading to the complete engulfment of the building. 火势迅速蔓延，导致整栋建筑被吞没。|近义词：submergence, immersion, inundation, overwhelming|反义词:emergence, uncovering|词性:noun

engulf:/ɪnˈɡʌlf/| vt.|吞没，淹没|The rising tide engulfed the beach.  上涨的潮水吞没了海滩。|His problems engulfed him, leaving him feeling hopeless. 他的问题把他淹没了，让他感到绝望。|近义词：submerge, immerse, inundate, overwhelm|反义词:expose, reveal|词性:verb
