non-crop:/ˌnɒnˈkrɒp/ | adj. | 非耕作的；非作物的 |  Relating to land or plants that are not cultivated for food or other agricultural products.  It often refers to areas of natural vegetation or land used for purposes other than farming. |  Example 1: The non-crop area provided a habitat for various wildlife.  非耕作区为各种野生动物提供了栖息地。

Example 2:  The study focused on the biodiversity of non-crop vegetation. 这项研究的重点是非耕作植被的生物多样性。 | Near synonyms: uncultivated, fallow, wild | Antonyms: cultivated, cropped, agricultural | adjective
