lasciviously: /ləˈsɪviəsli/ | adv. | 淫荡地，好色地 | In a manner expressing or suggesting sexual desire, often in a playful or exaggerated way.  |  He grinned lasciviously at her. 他淫笑着看着她。|  Synonyms: lustfully, lewdly, wantonly, salaciously | Antonyms: chastely, purely, modestly | adverb

Etymology->

From Late Latin *lasciviousus + -ly. From Latin lascivus (“wanton, playful, sportive, frolicsome”) (ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lēg- (“to be slack, weak; to lie”)) + -osus.
