corbel:/ˈkɔːrbəl/ (US), /ˈkɔːbəl/ (UK)| n.| 檐托；托座；（岩石的）突出部分|A corbel is a structural piece of stone, wood, or brick jutting from a wall to carry a weight, a type of bracket.  It can also refer to a similar projection of rock.|The corbels supporting the balcony were intricately carved. 支撑阳台的托座雕刻精美。|Synonyms: bracket, support, console, cantilever|Antonyms: base, foundation|noun

corbel:/ˈkɔːrbəl/ (US), /ˈkɔːbəl/ (UK)| v.| 用托座支撑；使成托座状|To support or furnish with corbels.|They corbeled the roof to extend it outwards. 他们用托座支撑屋顶以向外延伸。|Synonyms: brace, support, buttress|Antonyms: undermine, weaken|verb
