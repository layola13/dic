nonerratic:/ˌnɒnɪˈrætɪk/
adjective
非不稳定的；稳定的；规律的
Not erratic; regular or consistent.
The nonerratic heartbeat was a good sign.  稳定的心跳是个好兆头。
The clock ticked with nonerratic precision. 时钟以稳定的精度滴答作响。
近义词:stable, regular, consistent, steady, predictable
反义词:erratic, unstable, irregular, inconsistent, unpredictable
词性:adjective
词源: From non- + erratic (from Late Latin erraticus, from Latin errare 'to wander').  "non-" 表示否定，"erratic" 源于拉丁语 "errare"，意为“徘徊，游荡”。
