digitalised: /ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 数字化的 | Converted from analog to digital form; made digital. | The library has digitalised all its old manuscripts.  图书馆已将其所有旧手稿数字化。 |  The company has digitalised its entire archive. 公司已将其整个档案数字化。| 近义词: digitized, electronic, computerized | 反义词: analog, non-digital | 词性: 形容词

词源: From digital + -ise/-ize.  The suffix "-ise" or "-ize" is used to form verbs meaning "to make or become like" or "to cause to be."  In this case, "digitalise" means "to make digital."
