marcato:/mɑːrˈkɑːtoʊ/ (US), /mɑːˈkɑːtəʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv.| 强调的，突出的；|Marked; stressed; with emphasis.  Indicates a note or chord played in a distinct, forceful manner.|The composer indicated a marcato passage in the score. 作曲家在乐谱中标示了一段强音的乐段。|近义词：stressed, accented, emphasized|反义词：legato, smooth|词性:形容词/副词
词源: Italian, past participle of marcare ‘to mark’.
