antimystic:/ˌæntiˈmɪstɪk/
adjective
反对神秘主义的; 反对 mysticism 的
One who opposes mysticism or mystics. Characterized by opposition to mysticism or mystics.
He held an antimystic view of the world, preferring scientific explanations to spiritual ones. 他对世界持有反对神秘主义的观点，更喜欢科学的解释而不是精神的解释。
Near Synonyms: rational, empirical, skeptical, pragmatic
Antonyms: mystical, spiritual, esoteric, occult
word property: adjective
