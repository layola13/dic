minke:/ˈmɪŋki/ | n. |小须鲸|A relatively small baleen whale (Balaenoptera acutorostrata) with a sharply pointed snout, found in all oceans. |例句：The minke whale is the smallest of the rorquals. 小须鲸是须鲸中最小的。

例句：Minke whales are often seen close to shore. 小须鲸经常在近岸被看到。|近义词：rorqual (some species) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "minke" is uncertain.  One theory suggests it's named after a Norwegian whaler called Meincke, who supposedly mistook them for blue whales.  Another proposes a connection to the Norwegian word "mink," meaning small.
