hydroponics:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪks/ /ˌhaɪdroʊˈpɑːnɪks/| n. |水培，水耕法|The process of growing plants in sand, gravel, or liquid, with added nutrients but without soil.|例句：Hydroponics allows you to grow crops in areas with poor soil. 水培法可以让你在土壤贫瘠的地区种植作物。

例句：The tomatoes were grown using hydroponics. 这些西红柿是用水培法种植的。|近义词：soilless culture, aquaculture (in some contexts)|反义词: geoponics, soil-based agriculture |词性:noun
