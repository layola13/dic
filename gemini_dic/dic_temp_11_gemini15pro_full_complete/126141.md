mutineers:/ˌmjuːtɪˈnɪərz/ | n. |叛乱者，哗变者|A person who openly resists or refuses to obey the orders of a person in authority, especially sailors or soldiers against their officers.|The mutineers took control of the ship. 哗变者控制了这艘船。|Synonyms: rebels, insurgents, rioters |Antonyms: loyalists, conformists | noun
