cancellers:/ˈkænsələrz/| n.|取消者，抵制者|People who cancel or withdraw support from a person, organization, or product, often due to perceived offensive behavior or opinions.|Many companies fear cancellers and try to avoid controversy. 许多公司害怕抵制者并试图避免争议。|The cancellers were vocal on social media. 抵制者在社交媒体上表达了强烈的不满。|synonyms: boycotters, critics, detractors|antonyms: supporters, advocates|noun
