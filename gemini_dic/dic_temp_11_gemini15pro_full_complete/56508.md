domesticators:/dəˈmestɪˌkeɪtərz/
n.
驯化者，培育者
People who tame animals or cultivate plants for human use.
The early domesticators of wheat transformed agriculture. 早期的麦子驯化者改变了农业。
cultivators, breeders, tamers
wilders, hunters
noun
