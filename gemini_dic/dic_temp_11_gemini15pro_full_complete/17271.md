bayou:/ˈbaɪuː/ /ˈbaɪ.juː/| n.| 河汊，支流，（尤指美国南部的）滞流河道 |A bayou is a slow-moving stream or a marshy lake or wetland, typically found in the southeastern United States.|  We canoed down the bayou. 我们乘独木舟顺流而下。

|The house was situated on a picturesque bayou. 这房子坐落在风景如画的河汊上。|近义词：creek, inlet, backwater|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Louisiana French *bayou*, from Choctaw *bayok*.
