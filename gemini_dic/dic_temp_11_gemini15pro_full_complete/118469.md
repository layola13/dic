meshes:/ˈmɛʃɪz/ /ˈmeʃɪz/| v.|啮合，相配，协调|To fit together or integrate smoothly.|The two systems meshed perfectly. 这两个系统完美地啮合在一起。|近义词：interlock, engage, coordinate|反义词:clash, disconnect|verb
