deobstruent:/diːɒbˈstruːənt/ | adj. n. | 去除阻塞的；去阻滞剂 | Removing obstructions; a medicine or substance that removes obstructions. |

例句：
The doctor prescribed a deobstruent to clear her airways.
医生给她开了去阻滞剂来清理呼吸道。

This herbal tea acts as a gentle deobstruent.
这种凉茶是一种温和的去阻滞剂。


近义词：aperient, laxative, cathartic, purgative |
反义词：obstructive, blocking |
词性：adjective, noun |


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *deobstruēns, present participle of deobstruere (“to unblock, clear”), from de- (“from, away from”) + obstruere (“to build in front of, block, obstruct”). |
