fronton:/ˈfrʌntɒn/ (UK), /ˈfrʌntɑːn/ (US)| n. |山形墙，（尤指巴斯克地区的）回力球场|A triangular or rounded gable terminating the roof of a building, especially a classical temple.  In the Basque region, it also refers to a walled court for playing pelota (a Basque ball game).|The pediment of the temple was decorated with elaborate carvings.  寺庙的山形墙装饰着精美的雕刻。

The town's main attraction is its historic fronton, where pelota matches are held every weekend.  这个镇的主要景点是它的历史悠久的回力球场，每个周末都会在那里举行回力球比赛。|近义词：gable, pediment (for the architectural meaning)|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fronton*, diminutive of *front* ("forehead, front"), from Latin *frōns* ("forehead, front").  The architectural sense relates to the fronton resembling a forehead.  The sporting sense comes from the front wall of the court.
