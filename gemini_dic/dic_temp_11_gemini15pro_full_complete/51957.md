diachronicnesses:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɑːnɪknɪsɪz/
|n.|历时性|The diachronicnesses of language change are fascinating to study.  语言变化的历时性引人入胜。|Diachronic linguistics examines language development through time. 历时语言学研究语言随时间的发展。|近义词：diachrony, historical development|反义词：synchrony|词性:名词

词源:
From diachronic + -ness.  Diachronic comes from Greek dia- "through" + chronos "time".
