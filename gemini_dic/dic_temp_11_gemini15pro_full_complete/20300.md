bisection:/baɪˈsekʃən/| n.|二等分，平分，对分|The act of dividing something into two equal parts.|The bisection of the angle resulted in two 45-degree angles.  这个角的二等分形成了两个45度的角。|synonyms: division, partition, halving|antonyms: join, combination, union|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bisectionem*, accusative of *bisectio* ("a cutting in two"), from *bisectus*, past participle of *bisecare* ("to cut in two"), from *bi-* ("two") + *secare* ("to cut").
