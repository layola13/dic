downscales:/ˈdaʊnˌskeɪlz/| v. |缩小规模，降低规格|Reduces the size or scale of something, often referring to businesses, operations, or images.|The company downscaled its operations after the economic downturn. 经济衰退后，公司缩减了业务规模。|The image was downscaled to fit the website.  图片被缩小以适应网站。|近义词：reduce, shrink, downsize|反义词: upscale, expand, enlarge|词性:动词

downscales:/ˈdaʊnˌskeɪlz/| n. |缩小规模，降低规格（名词化）|The act of reducing the size or scale of something. |The downscale of the project was necessary due to budget cuts. 由于预算削减，该项目的缩减是必要的。|There has been a noticeable downscale in the company's activities this year. 今年公司活动明显减少。|近义词：reduction, shrinking, downsizing|反义词: upscale, expansion, enlargement|词性:名词
