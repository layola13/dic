euro's:/ˈjʊəroʊz/ | n. | 欧元（复数，非正式） | Plural form of "euro," the single currency used by many countries in the European Union.  While grammatically incorrect, it's sometimes used informally to refer to multiple euro coins or amounts. The correct plural is "euros." |  He had a few euro's in his pocket. 他口袋里有一些欧元（硬币）。| Near Synonyms: euros (correct plural),  | Antonyms: (None - currency doesn't have direct opposites) | noun
