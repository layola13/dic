elasmobranch:/ɪˌlæzməˈbræŋk/ | n. adj. |  板鳃亚纲动物；板鳃类的 |  A cartilaginous fish of a group that comprises the sharks, rays, and skates.  Characterized by having gills attached to slits rather than covered by an operculum.| The great white shark is an elasmobranch. 大白鲨是板鳃亚纲动物。 | 近义词: cartilaginous fish, chondrichthyan | 反义词: teleost (硬骨鱼) | 词性: noun, adjective


elasmobranch:/ɪˌlæzməˈbræŋkiət/ | adj. |  板鳃类的 | Belonging or pertaining to the Elasmobranchii, a subclass of cartilaginous fishes including sharks, rays, and skates. | Elasmobranch fishes have cartilaginous skeletons. 板鳃类鱼有软骨骨骼。| 近义词: chondrichthian, cartilaginous | 反义词: teleostean | 词性: adjective


词源:
From Greek *elasmos* "thin plate, metal plate" + *branchia* "gill".
