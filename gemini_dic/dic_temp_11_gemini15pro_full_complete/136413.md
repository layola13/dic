nonretentiveness:/ˌnɒn rɪˈtɛntɪvnəs/ |/ˌnɑːn rɪˈtɛntɪvnəs/| n. |非保留性，记性不好|The nonretentiveness of his memory was a constant source of frustration. 他的记忆力差一直是他沮丧的根源。|The nonretentiveness of the soil caused the water to drain away quickly. 土壤的非保水性导致水分迅速流失。|近义词：forgetfulness, amnesia|反义词：retentiveness, memory|词性: noun

Etymology:  From non- + retentiveness.  Retentiveness from retain + -ness. Retain from Old French retenir, from Latin retinēre, from re- + tenēre "to hold".
