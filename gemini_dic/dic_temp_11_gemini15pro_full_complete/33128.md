christmases:/ˈkrɪsməsəz/ | n. | 圣诞节们 |  The plural form of "Christmas," referring to multiple Christmas holidays.  | We've spent many happy Christmases together. 我们一起度过了许多快乐的圣诞节。 | Near synonyms: holiday seasons, Yuletides | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific holiday) | noun

Etymology: From Old English *Cristes mæsse, meaning "Christ's mass."  The plural form is created by adding "-es" to the singular "Christmas."
