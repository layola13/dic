curtain:/ˈkɜːrtən/ (US), /ˈkɜːtn/ (UK)| n. v. |窗帘，幕布；掩蔽物|A piece of cloth intended to block light, conceal something, or serve as a decorative screen.  It can also be used figuratively to refer to the end of something. |例句：The curtains were drawn to keep out the sun. 窗帘被拉上以遮挡阳光。|近义词：drape, blind, screen|反义词: unveiling, exposure|词性:名词

curtain:/ˈkɜːrtən/ (US), /ˈkɜːtn/ (UK)| v. |用窗帘遮蔽；掩蔽|To cover or conceal with a curtain.|例句：She curtained the windows for privacy. 她用窗帘遮住了窗户以保护隐私。|近义词：screen, cover, conceal|反义词: uncover, expose|词性:动词
