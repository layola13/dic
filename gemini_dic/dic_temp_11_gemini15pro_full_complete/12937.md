astatize: /eɪˈstætɪzaɪz/ /əˈsteɪtɪzaɪz/ | v. | 消磁，使无磁性 | To deprive of magnetic properties; to render non-magnetic. |  The scientist astatized the needle of the compass to prevent interference. 科学家将指南针的指针消磁以防止干扰。

The device was astatized to ensure accurate readings.  该装置被消磁以确保读数准确。 | 近义词: demagnetize | 反义词: magnetize | 词性: 动词 | 词源:  a- (not) + Greek *stāsis 'standing'  (from histanai 'to cause to stand'), referring to the tendency of a magnetized needle to maintain a fixed direction.
