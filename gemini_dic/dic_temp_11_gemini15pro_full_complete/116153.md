masqueraded:/ˌmæskəˈreɪdɪd/
|v.|伪装的，乔装的|Having disguised oneself, typically as someone or something else, often for amusement or deception.|She attended the ball, masqueraded as a princess.|近义词: disguised, camouflaged, masked|反义词: unmasked, revealed|词性:Verb (past tense of masquerade)


Etymology: From the French "mascarade," meaning "masquerade ball" or "masked party." Ultimately derived from the Italian "maschera," meaning "mask."
