antireducing:/ˌæntiɹɪˈdusɪŋ/ | adj. | 抗还原的，防止还原的 |  Preventing or inhibiting reduction (a chemical reaction that involves the gain of electrons).  This term describes a substance or agent that opposes or slows down reduction processes. | The antireducing agent protected the sensitive compound from degradation.  这种抗还原剂保护了敏感化合物免于降解。

The solution contains an antireducing agent to maintain the stability of the metal ions.  该溶液含有抗还原剂以保持金属离子的稳定性。 | Near synonyms: antioxidant, reducing inhibitor | Antonyms: reducing, reductant | adjective
