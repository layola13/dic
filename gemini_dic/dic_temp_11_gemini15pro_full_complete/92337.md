hypochondria: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriə/ (British) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndriə/ (American)| n. |疑病症，臆想病|A psychological disorder characterized by excessive worry about having a serious illness, despite medical reassurance to the contrary.|She suffers from hypochondria and is always convinced she has a fatal disease.|近义词:  health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder|反义词:  robust health, well-being|词性: noun

hypochondriac: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriæk/ (British) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndriæk/ (American)| n. adj. |疑病症患者，疑病的|A person who suffers from hypochondria or who is excessively worried about their health.|He's a terrible hypochondriac and rushes to the doctor for every little ache and pain.|近义词: valetudinarian |反义词: stoic|词性: noun, adjective
