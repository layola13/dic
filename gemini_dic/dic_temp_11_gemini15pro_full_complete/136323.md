nonrequisition:/ˌnɒnˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 非申请，未经申请 | An item or action taken without a formal request.  This typically refers to supplies or services provided without being specifically asked for. | The delivery of extra office supplies was a nonrequisition.  办公室额外供应品的交付是未经申请的。 | The hospital received a nonrequisition shipment of medical supplies.  医院收到了一批未经申请的医疗用品。 | 近义词: unsolicited, voluntary | 反义词: requisitioned, requested | 词性: noun
Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + requisition (noun meaning "a formal request for something")
