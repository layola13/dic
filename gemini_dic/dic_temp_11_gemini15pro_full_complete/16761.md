baryphony:/bæˈrɪfəni/
n.
沉重的声音，粗重的声音；不和谐的声音
A harsh or dissonant sound; the effect of such sounds.
The baryphony of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地的嘈杂声让人难以集中注意力。
The baryphony of the orchestra tuning up was surprisingly pleasant.  管弦乐队调音时的不和谐声音出奇地悦耳。
近义词:cacophony, dissonance, discord
反义词:euphony, harmony, consonance
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek *barýs* (heavy) + *-phonía* (sound, voice).
