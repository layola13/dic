gastroraphy: /ˌɡæstrəˈræfi/
noun
胃缝合术
The surgical suturing of a wound or tear in the stomach.
The patient underwent gastroraphy after suffering a perforated ulcer.  病人患穿孔性溃疡后接受了胃缝合术。
近义词: stomach repair, stomach suture
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially gastrectomy - surgical removal of the stomach)
词性: n.
