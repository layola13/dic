alebench: /ˈeɪlˌbɛntʃ/
noun
啤酒长凳; 酒馆里的长凳
A long bench or table in a pub or alehouse where people sit to drink.
例句：The old men sat on the alebench, nursing their pints. 老人们坐在啤酒长凳上，慢慢地喝着他们的啤酒。

例句：The alebench was crowded with patrons enjoying the lively atmosphere.  啤酒长凳上挤满了享受热闹气氛的顾客。


近义词：bench, table, seat
反义词: None

词性: n.
