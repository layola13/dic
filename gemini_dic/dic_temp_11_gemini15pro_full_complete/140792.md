on-a-cloud:/ɒn ə klaʊd/ | adj. | 欣喜若狂的，非常高兴的 | Extremely happy; feeling wonderful. |  例句：She was on a cloud after winning the lottery. 她中了彩票后欣喜若狂。

例句：He's been on a cloud ever since he got engaged. 他自从订婚以来一直非常高兴。

近义词：elated, overjoyed, blissful, ecstatic | 反义词：depressed, miserable, downcast | 词性：形容词
