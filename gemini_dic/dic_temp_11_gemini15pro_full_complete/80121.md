goblin:/ˈɡɒblɪn/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːblɪn/ (US)|n.|小妖精，哥布林|A mischievous and often malicious creature from folklore, typically depicted as small and grotesque.|The children were scared of the goblins in the story. 孩子们被故事里的小妖精吓坏了。|The goblin hid under the bridge, waiting to trick travelers. 小妖精藏在桥下，等着捉弄过路人。|Synonyms: imp, sprite, gremlin|Antonyms: angel, fairy (often, though not always)|noun
