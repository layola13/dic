chlorocruorin:/ˌklɔːrəˈkruːərɪn/
n.
叶绿素血红蛋白
A green respiratory pigment found in the blood of certain marine annelid worms.
The marine worm uses chlorocruorin to transport oxygen in its blood.  这种海洋蠕虫利用叶绿素血红蛋白在其血液中运输氧气。
Chlorocruorin is similar in function to hemoglobin but contains iron in a different oxidation state. 叶绿素血红蛋白在功能上与血红蛋白相似，但铁的氧化态不同。
近义词: erythrocruorin, hemoglobin
反义词: None
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From chloro- (green) + -cruorin (a substance in the blood), from Latin cruor (blood).
