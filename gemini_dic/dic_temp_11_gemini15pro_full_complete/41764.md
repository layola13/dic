corruptibility:/kəˌrʌptɪˈbɪləti/
n.
可腐败性；易腐败性；腐败倾向
The susceptibility to corruption; the capacity to be corrupted.
The corruptibility of the officials led to the downfall of the government. 官员们的腐败导致了政府的垮台。

近义词:  venality, dishonesty, susceptibility to bribery, graft
反义词:  incorruptibility, integrity, probity, honesty
