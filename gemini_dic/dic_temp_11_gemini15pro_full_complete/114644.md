malfeasance:/ˌmælˈfiːzəns/
n.
渎职；不法行为；不正当行为
wrongdoing, especially by a public official
The investigation uncovered widespread malfeasance in the city government. 调查揭露了市政府普遍存在的渎职行为。
近义词:misconduct, wrongdoing, impropriety, corruption
反义词:good conduct, propriety
