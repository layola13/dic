nonoffensiveness:/ˌnɒnəˈfɛnsɪvnəs/ /ˌnɑːnəˈfɛnsɪvnəs/
n.
无冒犯；不侵犯；不得罪人
The state or quality of being not offensive; inoffensiveness.
Her remarks were notable for their nonoffensiveness. 她的言辞以不具冒犯性而著称。
Synonyms: inoffensiveness, harmlessness, innocuousness
Antonyms: offensiveness, aggressiveness, hostility
