fortune:/ˈfɔːrtʃuːn/ (US) /ˈfɔːtʃən/ (UK)| n.|运气，命运；财富，财产|Chance or luck, especially in the way it affects people's lives; a large amount of money or property|She had the good fortune to be born into a wealthy family. 她很幸运地出生在一个富裕的家庭。|synonyms: luck, chance, fate, destiny, prosperity, wealth, riches|antonyms: misfortune, adversity, poverty, destitution|noun

fortune:/ˈfɔːrtʃuːn/ (US) /ˈfɔːtʃən/ (UK)| v.|〈古〉偶然发生，碰巧|happen to (archaic) |He fortuned upon a rare antique. 他偶然发现了一件稀有的古董。(古用法，现在很少使用)|synonyms: chance, happen|antonyms: N/A|verb (archaic)
