miasma:/maɪˈæzmə/ /miˈæzmə/
|n.|瘴气，臭气，不良气氛|A highly unpleasant or unhealthy smell or vapor. Also, a pervasive atmosphere or influence that is unpleasant, harmful, or depressing.|The miasma of cigarette smoke filled the bar. 酒吧里弥漫着令人作呕的香烟味。|Synonyms: stench, stink, reek, fetor, odor, effluvium, aroma (negative connotation), aura (negative connotation)|Antonyms: fragrance, perfume, scent (positive connotation), freshness|noun
