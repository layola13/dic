converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ /kənˈvɝːdʒ/
verb
汇聚，集中；趋同
to come from different directions and meet at the same point
例句：The two rivers converge at this point. 这两条河流在此处汇合。

例句：The paths converged at the top of the hill.  这些小路在山顶汇合。

近义词：meet, join, intersect, merge, unite, coalesce
反义词：diverge, separate
词性：v.
