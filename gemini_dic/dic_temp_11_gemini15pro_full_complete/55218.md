dissever:/dɪˈsɛvər/
verb
切断，割断，分离
To separate or divide, especially by cutting or breaking.
例句：The king dissevered the heads of his enemies. 国王砍下了敌人的头颅。

例句：The rope was old and easily dissevered.  绳子很旧，很容易断开。

近义词：sever, separate, divide, detach, disconnect, rupture
反义词：unite, join, connect, combine
词性：v.


dissever:/dɪˈsɛvər/
verb
断绝（关系）
To break off (a relationship); discontinue.
例句：The two countries dissevered diplomatic relations.  这两个国家断绝了外交关系。

例句：She dissevered all ties with her family. 她断绝了与家人的所有联系。

近义词：break off, terminate, end, dissolve
反义词：establish, build, strengthen
词性：v.
