demirelief:/ˌdɛmi rɪˈliːf/
|n.|半浮雕|Sculpture that projects slightly from the background surface.  It is less prominent than high relief (alto-relievo) but more prominent than bas-relief (low relief).|The artist specialized in demirelief, creating subtle yet captivating textures on the bronze doors.|近义词:mezzo-relievo, medium relief|反义词:alto-relievo (high relief), bas-relief (low relief)|词性:noun

demirelief:|/ˌdɛmi rɪˈliːf/|
|n.|半浮雕|A form of sculptural relief in which the figures project about half their natural circumference from the background. It is intermediate between high relief and bas-relief.|The friezes on the temple were carved in demirelief, depicting scenes from mythology.|近义词:mezzo-relievo|反义词:alto-relievo, bas-relief|词性:noun
