depolarization:/diːˌpəʊləraɪˈzeɪʃn/ /diːˌpoʊləriˈzeɪʃn/
noun
去极化
The process of reversing the charge across a cell membrane (such as a nerve cell), typically from negative to positive, resulting in the transmission of a nerve impulse.
The depolarization of the neuron's membrane triggered an action potential. 神经元细胞膜的去极化触发了动作电位。
