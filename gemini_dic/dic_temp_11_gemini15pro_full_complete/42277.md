counter-earth:/ˈkaʊntərɜːrθ/| n. | 对地星，对地行星 | A hypothetical planet on the opposite side of the Sun from Earth, always hidden from us.  A planet sharing Earth's orbit but always on the opposite side of the Sun. |  The science fiction story postulated a counter-earth populated by advanced beings. 这个科幻故事假设了一个由高级生物居住的对地行星。 | synonym: antichthon | antonym: Earth | noun


counter-earth:/ˈkaʊntərɜːrθ/| n. | 对地，相对地球的事物 | Something that is diametrically opposed to or the antithesis of Earth or its qualities. | The dystopian novel portrayed a counter-earth ravaged by pollution. 这部反乌托邦小说描绘了一个被污染蹂躏的对地。| synonym: opposite | antonym: Earth | noun
