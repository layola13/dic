mesopodial:/ˌmɛsəˈpoʊdiəl/ | adj. | 中足的，位于中足的 | Relating to the middle portion of the foot (podium) in mollusks, especially gastropods.  It refers to the middle part of a snail or slug's foot, often used in a scientific context to describe locomotion or anatomical features. | The snail's mesodial gland secretes mucus to aid in locomotion.  蜗牛的中足腺分泌粘液以帮助运动。| 近义词: mid-podium (less common) | 反义词: anterior podium (front part of the foot), posterior podium (rear part of the foot) | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *mesos* "middle" + *pous, podos* "foot".
