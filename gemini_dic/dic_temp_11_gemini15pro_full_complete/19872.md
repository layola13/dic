binging:/ˈbɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 狂欢的，放纵的 | Characterized by excessive or compulsive engagement in an activity. |  His binging behavior is worrying his family. 他放纵的行为让他的家人很担心。 | synonyms: excessive, compulsive, uncontrolled | antonyms: moderate, controlled, restrained | adjective

PHRASE->

binge-watch:  狂看；一口气看完 (e.g., I binge-watched the entire season last night. 我昨晚一口气看完了整季。)
binge-eat:  狂吃 (e.g., He tends to binge-eat when he's stressed. 他在压力大的时候往往会狂吃。)
go on a binge:  开始狂欢；放纵 (e.g., She went on a shopping binge. 她疯狂购物。)
binge-drinking:  狂饮 (e.g., Binge-drinking can be very dangerous. 狂饮非常危险。)
binge session:  狂欢时段 (e.g., We had a movie binge session last weekend. 我们上周末疯狂看电影。)


词源：Binging comes from the verb "binge," which originated in the early 20th century.  Its exact etymology is uncertain, but it may be related to British dialect words meaning "to soak" or "to steep."  The current sense of excessive consumption developed later.
