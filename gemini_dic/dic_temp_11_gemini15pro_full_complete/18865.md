beseen:/biː siːn/
verb phrase
被看见，被注意到
to be noticed or visible
例句：She wanted to be seen at the best parties. 她想去最好的派对上露面。

例句：He tried to be seen with important people. 他试图与重要人物在一起，以获得关注。

近义词：noticed, observed, viewed, perceived
反义词：unseen, unnoticed, ignored, overlooked
