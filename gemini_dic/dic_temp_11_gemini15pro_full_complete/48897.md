defraud:/dɪˈfrɔːd/
verb
欺骗，诈骗
to illegally obtain money from someone by deception
He defrauded the company of millions of dollars. 他骗取了公司数百万美元。
近义词：cheat, swindle, deceive
反义词：compensate, reimburse
词性:v.

defrauds:/dɪˈfrɔːdz/
verb
欺骗，诈骗（第三人称单数）
to illegally obtain money from someone by deception
He defrauds investors with promises of high returns. 他以高回报的承诺欺骗投资者。

反义词：compensates, reimburses
词性:v.
