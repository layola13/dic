loamy:/ˈloʊmi/| adj.|肥沃的，壤土的|Consisting of or resembling loam.|The loamy soil was ideal for growing vegetables. 这种肥沃的土壤非常适合种植蔬菜。|近义词：fertile, rich|反义词:barren, infertile|adjective
