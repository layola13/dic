mesencephalic:/ˌmezɛnˈsɛfəlɪk, ˌmɪdbrən/ | adj. | 中脑的；与中脑有关的 | Relating to the midbrain, the middle part of the brain stem. | The mesencephalic locomotor region plays a key role in initiating movement.  中脑运动区在启动运动中起关键作用。 | The patient exhibited mesencephalic symptoms, suggesting damage to that area of the brain.  该患者表现出中脑症状，表明大脑该区域受损。 | 近义词: midbrain | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek μέσος (mésos, “middle”) +  ἐγκέφαλος (enképhalos, “brain”).
