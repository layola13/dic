footplates:/ˈfʊtplɪːts/| n.|踏板，脚踏板|A flat plate or bar on which the foot can be placed for support or to operate a mechanism, such as a pedal on a machine or a lever. |The engineer pressed down on the footplates to increase the speed of the train.  工程师按下踏板以提高火车的速度。|Synonyms: pedals, treads, footrests|Antonyms: N/A|noun

footplates:/ˈfʊtplɪːts/| n.|（尤指火车头）司机室|The compartment in a locomotive where the driver and fireman stand. |He spent his working life in the footplates of steam engines. 他在蒸汽机车的驾驶室里度过了他的职业生涯。|Synonyms: cab, driver's compartment|Antonyms: N/A|noun
