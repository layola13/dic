bogan:/ˈboʊɡən/ (AU), /ˈbɒɡən/ (UK)| n. |粗俗的人，没教养的人；庸俗的人|A bogan is a person who is considered to be uncultured or unsophisticated, often associated with a particular style of clothing, music, and behaviour.  The term is predominantly used in Australia and New Zealand. |He was dressed like a typical bogan in a flannelette shirt and ripped jeans. 他穿着一件法兰绒衬衫和破洞牛仔裤，看起来就像个典型的粗人。|近义词：redneck, chav, yob |反义词:sophisticate, cultured person|noun

bogan:/ˈboʊɡən/ (AU), /ˈbɒɡən/ (UK)| n. |粗鄙的人，缺乏品味的人|A bogan is someone considered to be lacking in taste or refinement.  While primarily Australian and New Zealand slang, its usage has spread elsewhere. |She wouldn't be caught dead listening to that bogan music. 她死也不会听那种俗气的音乐。|近义词：pleb, yahoo |反义词:aesthete, connoisseur|noun
