observes:/əbˈzɜːrvz/
verb
观察；遵守；评论；庆祝
He observes the behavior of birds. 他观察鸟类的行为。
The scientist observes the stars through a telescope. 科学家通过望远镜观察星星。
observes:/əbˈzɜːrvz/
verb
遵守（法律、习俗等）
They observe the Sabbath. 他们遵守安息日。
Everyone should observe traffic laws. 每个人都应该遵守交通规则。
observes:/əbˈzɜːrvz/
verb
评论；谈论
He observed that the weather was unusually cold. 他评论说天气异常寒冷。
She observed that the painting was a masterpiece. 她评论说这幅画是一件杰作。
observes:/əbˈzɜːrvz/
verb
庆祝（节日、生日等）
They observe Christmas every year. 他们每年都庆祝圣诞节。
We observe his birthday with a party. 我们举办派对庆祝他的生日。

近义词:watches, follows, complies with, celebrates, comments, notes
反义词:ignores, disregards, violates, neglects


词源: From Old French *observer, from Latin observāre (“to watch, observe, comply with”), from ob- (“toward”) + servāre (“to keep, watch”).
