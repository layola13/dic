词源: From Middle English *clenen, from Old English clǣne (“clean, pure, innocent, clear; elegant, distinct”), from Proto-Germanic *klainą (“clean, small, thin”). Cognate with West Frisian klean (“clean”), Dutch klein (“small”), German klein (“small”). Related to Old English clǣnnes (“cleanness, purity”).  The verb is derived from the adjective.
