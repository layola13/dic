alarms:/əˈlɑːrmz/| n.|警报，闹钟|a sudden fear or feeling of danger which makes you want to escape or attack; a warning of danger|The fire alarm went off. 火警响了。|synonyms: warning, alert, signal|antonyms: reassurance, calm|noun

alarms:/əˈlɑːrmz/| v.|使惊恐，使担心|to make someone suddenly feel afraid or worried|The news of the attack alarmed the public. 袭击的消息使公众感到恐慌。|synonyms: frighten, scare, worry|antonyms: soothe, calm, reassure|verb
