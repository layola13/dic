demography:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfi/ (US) /dɪˈmɑːɡrəfi/ (UK)| n.|人口统计学|The study of statistics such as births, deaths, income, or the incidence of disease, which illustrate the changing structure of human populations.|例句：The demography of the country has changed dramatically in the last decade.  这个国家的人口结构在过去十年发生了巨大的变化。

例句：Understanding the demography of a region is crucial for effective urban planning. 了解一个地区的人口统计对于有效的城市规划至关重要。


近义词：population studies, vital statistics|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
