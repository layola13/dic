astylar:/əˈstaɪlə(r)/
adjective
无柱式的；无柱的
Without columns or pilasters.
The temple was astylar, relying on massive walls for support. 这座寺庙是无柱式的，依靠厚重的墙壁支撑。
The architect chose an astylar design for the modern art museum.  这位建筑师为现代艺术博物馆选择了无柱式设计。
近义词: columnless, pillarless
反义词: columned, pillared
词性: adjective

词源:
From a- (without) + stylar (relating to a column or style). Ultimately from Greek στῦλος (stylos, “pillar, column”).
