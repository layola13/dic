diprotodon:/daɪˈprəʊtədɒn/ (UK), /daɪˈproʊtədɑːn/ (US)| n. |双门齿兽|An extinct giant marsupial that resembled a wombat and was the largest known marsupial to have ever existed. It lived in Australia during the Pleistocene epoch.|例句：The diprotodon was a massive herbivore that roamed ancient Australia. 双门齿兽是一种巨大的食草动物，曾在古代澳大利亚漫游。

例句：Fossil evidence shows that the diprotodon became extinct around 46,000 years ago. 化石证据表明，双门齿兽大约在46000年前灭绝。|近义词：giant wombat, Pleistocene megafauna|反义词: N/A (extinct animal, no direct opposite)|词性: noun
