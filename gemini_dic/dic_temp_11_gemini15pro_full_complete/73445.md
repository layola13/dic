foremother:/ˈfɔːrmʌðər/| n. |女祖先，女性先驱|A woman from whom a person is descended, especially a woman who lived a long time ago; a female ancestor; a female predecessor. |例句：Susan B. Anthony is considered a foremother of the women's suffrage movement. 苏珊·B·安东尼被认为是妇女选举权运动的先驱。

例句：Our foremothers fought for the rights we enjoy today. 我们的女祖先为我们今天享有的权利而奋斗。|近义词：ancestor, predecessor, progenitor|反义词:descendant|词性:noun
