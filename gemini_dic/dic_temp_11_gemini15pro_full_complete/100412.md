intertextualities:/ˌɪntərˌtɛkstʃuˈælɪtiz/ | n. |互文性（复数）| The complex interrelationships between a text and other texts taken as basic to the creation or interpretation of the text.|  The novel's intertextualities with classic mythology enrich its meaning. 这部小说与古典神话的互文性丰富了它的意义。|近义词：connections, relationships, references|反义词: N/A | Noun

intertextuality:/ˌɪntərˌtɛkstʃuˈælɪti/ | n. |互文性（单数）| The relationship between texts, especially literary ones.| The intertextuality between the two poems is evident in their shared imagery.  这两首诗的互文性体现在它们共同的意象上。|近义词：connection, relationship, reference|反义词: N/A | Noun
