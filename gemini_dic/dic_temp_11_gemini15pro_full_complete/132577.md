nondieting:/ˌnɒnˈdaɪətɪŋ/
n.
非节食，不控制饮食
The practice of rejecting diets and focusing on intuitive eating and body acceptance.  非节食是指拒绝节食，专注于直觉饮食和身体接纳的做法。
She's adopted a nondieting approach to eating. 她采取了非节食的饮食方式。
近义词:intuitive eating, mindful eating
反义词:dieting
词性:名词
