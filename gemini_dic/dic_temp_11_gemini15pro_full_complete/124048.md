morphinated: /ˈmɔːrfɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 吗啡化的；注射了吗啡的 | Affected by morphine; under the influence of morphine. |  He appeared morphinated and drowsy. 他看起来像是注射了吗啡，昏昏欲睡。| The patient was clearly morphinated, his speech slurred and his movements slow. 病人很明显是注射了吗啡了，说话含糊不清，行动迟缓。 | 近义词: drugged, narcotized | 反义词: sober, clear-headed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Derived from "morphine" + "-ated".  "Morphine" comes from Morpheus, the Greek god of dreams, due to the drug's sleep-inducing properties. "-ated" is a suffix meaning "treated with" or "acted upon by".
