lionesses:/ˈlaɪəˌnɛsɪz/ | n. |母狮，雌狮 |A female lion.  |例句：The lionesses were hunting zebras. 母狮们正在猎捕斑马。

|例句：A pride of lionesses rested in the shade. 一群母狮在阴凉处休息。


|近义词：female lion |反义词：lion (雄狮) |词性：名词

词源:
From Middle English *lionnesse, *lionesse, from Anglo-Norman lionesse, Old French lionnesse, feminine of lion, from Latin leō (“lion”), ultimately from Ancient Greek λέων (léōn).
