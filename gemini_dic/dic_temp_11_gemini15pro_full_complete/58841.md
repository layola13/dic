例句：Her duplicity made it hard to trust her. 她的两面性让人很难信任她。

近义词：deceit, hypocrisy, two-facedness, double-dealing, dishonesty, falseness, mendacity, perfidy|反义词: honesty, sincerity, straightforwardness|词性:noun
