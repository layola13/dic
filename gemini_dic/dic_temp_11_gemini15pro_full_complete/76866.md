ganglioform: /ˈɡæŋɡliəfɔːrm/ | adj. | 神经节状的，似神经节的 | Resembling or having the form of a ganglion. | The ganglioform swelling suggested a nerve issue.  这个神经节状的肿胀提示了神经问题。| Similar words: gangliar, ganglionated, nodular |  Opposite words:  smooth, non-nodular | adjective


ganglioform: /ˈɡæŋɡliəfɔːrm/ | n. | 神经节状体 | A structure resembling a ganglion. |  The biopsy revealed a ganglioform mass. 活检显示一个神经节状肿块。 | Similar words: ganglion, node | Opposite words: N/A | noun



Etymology:  From "ganglio-" (combining form meaning "ganglion") + "-form" (suffix meaning "having the form of").  "Ganglion" ultimately derives from Greek "γάγγλιον" (ganglion), meaning "tumor" or "knot."
