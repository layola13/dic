incompressible:/ˌɪnkəmˈprɛsəb(ə)l/
adjective
不可压缩的
Not able to be compressed.  Unable to be reduced in volume or size by pressure.
Air is compressible, but water is virtually incompressible.  空气是可压缩的，但水几乎不可压缩。
Liquids are generally considered incompressible. 液体通常被认为是不可压缩的。
近义词:noncompressible, uncompressible, inelastic
反义词:compressible, elastic
词性:adjective

incompressibility:/ˌɪnkəmˌprɛsəˈbɪləti/
noun
不可压缩性
The quality or state of being incompressible. The inability to be compressed.
The incompressibility of water makes it useful in hydraulic systems.  水的不可压缩性使其在液压系统中非常有用。
The experiment demonstrated the incompressibility of the liquid.  实验演示了液体的不可压缩性。
近义词:noncompressibility
反义词:compressibility
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + compressible, from Late Latin compress-, past participle stem of comprimere (to compress), from com- (together) + premere (to press).
