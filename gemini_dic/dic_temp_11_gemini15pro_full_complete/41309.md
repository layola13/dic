cordwainer:/ˈkɔːrdˌweɪnər/| n.|鞋匠|A shoemaker, especially one who makes high-quality shoes.|例句：The cordwainer carefully stitched the leather uppers to the soles. 鞋匠仔细地把皮革鞋面缝到鞋底上。

例句：The cordwainer crafted bespoke shoes for the wealthy clientele.  鞋匠为富有的客户制作定制鞋。|近义词：shoemaker, cobbler|反义词:|名词
