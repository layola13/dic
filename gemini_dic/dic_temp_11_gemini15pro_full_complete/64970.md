estimate: /ˈɛstɪmət/ (US) /ˈɛstɪmeɪt/ (UK)| vt.| 估计；估价；评估| To form an approximate idea of the value, number, quantity, or extent of something. |  They estimated the cost of the project at $5 million. 他们估计该项目的成本为 500 万美元。| synonyms: appraise, assess, calculate, evaluate, gauge | antonyms: measure,  calculate precisely | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French estimer, from Latin aestimare ‘to value, assess’, from aes ‘bronze, money’.
