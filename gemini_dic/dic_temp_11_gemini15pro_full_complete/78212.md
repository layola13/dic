geral:/ʒɛˈɾal/ /dʒɛˈrɑːl/ | adj.| 一般的，普通的，总的，全体的；总体的，全面的 | General means common, usual, or widespread. It can also refer to something affecting or concerning all or most people, places, or things; overall. | The general opinion is that the movie is excellent. 大家的普遍看法是这部电影很出色。 | synonyms: common, usual, widespread, overall, generic, universal | antonyms: specific, particular, individual, exceptional | adjective
