drizzle-puss:/ˈdrɪzəlˌpʊs/| n.|爱抱怨的人，愁眉苦脸的人|A person who is habitually gloomy, complaining, or pessimistic.  Someone who frequently expresses dissatisfaction or misery, often about trivial matters.  Evokes the image of a cat whose face looks perpetually wet and unhappy, like it's been drizzling on it.|例句：He's such a drizzle-puss, always complaining about the weather. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是抱怨天气。

例句：Don't be such a drizzle-puss! It's not that bad. 别这么愁眉苦脸的！没那么糟糕。|近义词：grump, complainer, whiner, pessimist|反义词:optimist, cheerful person|词性:noun
