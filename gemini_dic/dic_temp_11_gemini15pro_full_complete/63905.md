epiphanize:/ɪˈpɪfənaɪz/ | v. | 使顿悟，使突然明白 | To cause (someone) to have an epiphany; to reveal something in a sudden, intuitive way. |  The therapist helped her patient epiphanize the root of her anxiety.  治疗师帮助她的病人顿悟了焦虑的根源。

The near-death experience epiphanized him to the preciousness of life. 濒死体验让他顿悟了生命的宝贵。 | 近义词: illuminate, enlighten, reveal, clarify | 反义词: confuse, obscure, bewilder | 词性: 动词
