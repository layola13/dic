dermohaemal:/ˌdɜːrmoʊˈhiːməl/| adj.|皮肤血液的；真皮血管的|Relating to or designating a circulatory system in which the blood or haemolymph is in direct contact with the tissues, circulating in sinuses or spaces within the body wall.  The system is characteristic of many invertebrates.|The dermohaemal sinus system is typical of echinoderms.|Synonym: cutaneous hemal|Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider "closed circulatory system")|adjective

dermohaemal:/ˌdɜːrmoʊˈhiːməl/| n.|真皮血管系统|The system of sinuses and lacunae in echinoderms in which coelomic fluid circulates.|Echinoderms possess a unique dermohaemal system.|Synonym: hemal system (in echinoderms)|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun
