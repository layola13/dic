breviary: /ˈbriːviəri/ (US) /ˈbriːvɪəri/ (UK)| n. |祈祷书；摘要；简略 |A book containing the daily prayers, hymns, and readings prescribed for use in the Divine Office, especially by Roman Catholic priests.  A shortened or condensed version of a longer work. | The priest carried his breviary with him. 神父随身带着他的祈祷书。|  The article is a breviary of the original report. 这篇文章是原始报告的摘要。| synonyms: prayer book, missal, digest, compendium, abstract, epitome | antonyms: expansion, elaboration | noun
