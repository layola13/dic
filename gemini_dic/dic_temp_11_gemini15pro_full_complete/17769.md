bedkey: /ˈbedkiː/
noun
床钥匙
a key to a bed, typically in a dormitory or hostel.  This could also refer to a key that unlocks a special feature of a bed, like an adjustable base.
She lost her bedkey and couldn't get into her room. 她弄丢了床钥匙，进不了房间了。
synonyms: room key, dorm key
antonyms: N/A
