fever:/ˈfiːvər/ (US) /ˈfiːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|发烧，发热；狂热，热情|A medical condition in which the body temperature is higher than normal.|She has a high fever and needs to see a doctor. 她发高烧，需要去看医生。|Synonyms: temperature, pyrexia|Antonyms: normal temperature, coolness|Noun


fever:/ˈfiːvər/ (US) /ˈfiːvə(r)/ (UK)| v.|使发烧；使狂热|To cause a fever or a state of excitement.|The news of the election fevered the nation.  选举的消息让全国都为之兴奋。|Synonyms: inflame, excite|Antonyms: calm, soothe|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fevre* (Modern French *fièvre*), from Latin *febris* ("fever").
