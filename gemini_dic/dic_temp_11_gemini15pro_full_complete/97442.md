infuscation:/ɪnˌfʌsˈkeɪʃən/| n. |昏暗；模糊；迷惘|The act of making something dark, unclear, or obscure.  The state of being darkened, unclear, or obscure.|The infuscation of the legal jargon made it difficult for the average person to understand.|近义词：obscuration, obfuscation, darkening, confusion, bewilderment|反义词:clarification, elucidation, enlightenment|noun
