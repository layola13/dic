hijera:/iˈxera/ | n. | 变性人，阉人，阴阳人 | A person assigned male at birth who adopts a feminine gender identity, often including dressing in traditionally feminine clothing and taking on feminine gender roles.  In some cultures, hijras may undergo ritual castration.  |  Example: The hijra community in India has a long and complex history.  印度的变性人社群有着悠久而复杂的历史。| Synonym: transgender person, trans woman (in some contexts), eunuch (historically, though not always accurate) | Antonym:  cisgender | noun
