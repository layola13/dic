differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | adj. n.| 差别的，微分的；差速器；微分|A differential is a small change or distinction between similar things.  As a noun, it can also refer to a gear mechanism that allows wheels to rotate at different speeds, especially in a motor vehicle. |The differential in price between the two cars was significant. 两辆车的价格差异很大。|Synonyms: distinct, dissimilar, varied, contrastive, divergent, disparate|Antonyms: similar, identical, uniform, alike|adjective

differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | n.| 差额，差别；微分|The differential between the two salaries is quite substantial. 两个工资之间的差额相当大。|Synonyms: difference, distinction, disparity, gap, margin|Antonyms: similarity, likeness, resemblance|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *differentiālis, from Latin differentia (“difference”), from differō (“to differ, to set apart”), from dis- (“apart”) + ferō (“to bear, carry”).
