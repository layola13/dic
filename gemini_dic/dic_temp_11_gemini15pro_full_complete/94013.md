impalpably:/ɪmˈpælpəbli/
adverb
难以察觉地；无形地
in a way that cannot be felt physically
The atmosphere in the room had changed impalpably. 房间里的气氛发生了难以察觉的变化。

近义词: imperceptibly, intangibly, subtly
反义词: palpably, tangibly, noticeably
词性: adverb

词源:
From Late Latin impalpabilis, from in- "not" + palpabilis "tangible," from palpare "to touch."
