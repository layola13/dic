manchuko:/mænˈtʃuːkoʊ/
noun
满洲国 (伪满洲国)
A puppet state established in Manchuria by the Empire of Japan in 1932, officially known as the State of Manchuria (until 1934) and later as the Empire of Manchukuo (until 1945). It was not recognized by most of the international community.
The Japanese invasion led to the creation of Manchukuo. 日本的侵略导致了伪满洲国的建立。
Synonyms:  Manchukuo, puppet state
Antonyms:  independent state, sovereign nation
