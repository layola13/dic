consonanted:/ˈkɑːnsənəntɪd/
adjective
辅音的；充满辅音的
Containing many consonants.
The word "strengths" is heavily consonanted.  这个单词 "strengths" 辅音很多。
Consonanted syllables are harder to pronounce. 充满辅音的音节更难发音。
Synonym: consonant-rich
Antonym: vowel-rich
词性: 形容词
