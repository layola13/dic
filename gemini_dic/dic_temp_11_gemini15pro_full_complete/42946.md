cover-letter: /ˈkʌvər lɛtər/
noun
求职信
A letter sent with a resume or curriculum vitae explaining the applicant's credentials and interest in the open position.
I wrote a compelling cover letter to accompany my resume. 我写了一封引人注目的求职信来配合我的简历。
synonyms: covering letter, application letter
antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a cover letter)
