luted:/ˈluːtɪd/ /ˈljuːtɪd/| v. |密封的，粘合的|Sealed or closed with lute (a type of clay or cement).  The chemist luted the flask to prevent leaks. 化学家用粘土密封了烧瓶以防止泄漏。|例句：The window was luted with putty. 窗户是用油灰密封的。|例句：They luted the cracks in the wall with clay.  他们用粘土封住了墙上的裂缝。|近义词：sealed, cemented, closed, fastened|反义词:open, unsealed|词性：动词 (past participle)

词源: From Latin "lutum" (mud, clay).
