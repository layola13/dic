impromptu:/ɪmˈprɒmptjuː/ /ɪmˈprɑːmptuː/| adj. adv. n.|即兴的，即席的；即兴表演|Done without being planned, organized, or rehearsed.|She gave an impromptu performance at the party. 她在聚会上进行了即兴表演。|近义词：offhand, extemporaneous, unrehearsed, improvised|反义词：planned, prepared, rehearsed|词性: adjective, adverb, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French impromptu, from Latin in promptu "in readiness," from in- "in" + promptus "ready."
