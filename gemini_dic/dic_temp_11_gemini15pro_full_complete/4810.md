algorithmically: /ˌælɡəˈrɪðmɪkli/ | adv. | 算法上地，通过算法 | In computer science, problems are often solved algorithmically. 在计算机科学中，问题通常是通过算法解决的。| The data was processed algorithmically to identify patterns.  数据通过算法处理以识别模式。 | 近义词: computationally, programmatically | 反义词: heuristically, manually | 词性: 副词 | 词源: From "algorithm" + "-ically". "Algorithm" comes from the Latinized name of  Persian mathematician Muḥammad ibn Mūsā al-Khwārizmī.
