nonprejudicially:/ˌnɒnprɛdʒʊˈdɪʃəli/
adv.
无偏见地，不损害地
without causing harm or disadvantage to any particular person or group; without affecting someone’s rights
The matter was resolved nonprejudicially to either party. 这件事得到了解决，对任何一方都没有造成损害。

近义词: impartially, fairly, neutrally, objectively
反义词: prejudicially, biasedly, unfairly


词性: adverb
词源:  From "non-" (not) + "prejudicial" (harmful or detrimental to someone's rights or interests).  "Prejudicial" comes from the Latin "praejudicium" (prejudgment).
