microservices: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɜːrvɪsɪz/ | n. | 微服务 | An architectural style that structures an application as a collection of loosely coupled services, which implement business capabilities.  Each service runs its own process and communicates with lightweight mechanisms, often an HTTP resource API. |  Netflix uses microservices to power its streaming service.  Netflix使用微服务为其流媒体服务提供支持。

|  Many companies are adopting microservices architecture for its flexibility and scalability. 许多公司正在采用微服务架构，因为它具有灵活性和可扩展性。 | synonyms: distributed systems, SOA (service-oriented architecture) | antonyms: monolithic architecture | noun
