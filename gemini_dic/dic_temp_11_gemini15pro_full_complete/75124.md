frisked:/frɪskt/| v. |搜身，轻快地跳跃|To pass the hands quickly over (someone) in a search for hidden weapons, drugs, or other items.  Also, to move about playfully and energetically.|The police officer frisked the suspect. 警察搜查了嫌疑犯的身。 The lambs frisked in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：search, pat down, frolic, gambol|反义词:trudge, plod|词性:动词
词源:From Middle English *frisken, frequentative of frisken (“to leap, dance”), from Old Norse *fryska (“to jump, frisk”).  Related to Old English fersc (“fresh”).
