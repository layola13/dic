angiopathy:/ˌændʒiˈɒpəθi/
n.
血管病变，血管病
Any disease of the blood vessels.
例句：Diabetic angiopathy is a common complication of diabetes. 糖尿病性血管病变是糖尿病的常见并发症。

例句：The doctor diagnosed him with peripheral angiopathy. 医生诊断他患有周围血管病变。

近义词：vasculopathy, vascular disease
反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease state)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  angio- (vessel) + -pathy (disease), from Greek angeion (vessel) + pathos (suffering).
