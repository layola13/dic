leger:/ˈledʒər/| n. |分类账，总账|A book or other collection of financial accounts of a particular type. |例句：The company's leger showed a significant loss this quarter. 这家公司的总账显示本季度亏损严重。

例句：He meticulously recorded every transaction in the leger. 他一丝不苟地在总账上记录每一笔交易。|近义词：account book, ledger, register |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

词源:
From Old French *legier "ledger," from Middle French papier legier "ledger paper," literally "light paper," from legier "light" (see legerdemain). So called because made of high quality, thin paper.  来源于古法语 *legier “分类账”，来自中古法语 papier legier “分类账纸”，字面意思是“轻纸”，来自 legier “轻”（参见 legerdemain）。之所以这样称呼，是因为它是由高质量的薄纸制成的。
