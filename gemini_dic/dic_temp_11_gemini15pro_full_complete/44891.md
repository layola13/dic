cryogenics:/ˌkraɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪks/ | n. |低温学|The branch of physics that deals with the production and effects of very low temperatures.| Cryogenics plays a vital role in preserving biological samples. 低温学在保存生物样本中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: low-temperature physics | 反义词: high-temperature physics | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From cryo- (Greek "icy cold, frost") + -gen (Greek, "producing") + -ics (suffix forming names of sciences).
