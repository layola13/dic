outstudied:/ˌaʊtˈstʌdid/
verb
超越，在学习上胜过
surpassed someone in academic achievement through more diligent study.
He outstudied his classmates and got the highest grade in the class. 他比班上其他同学都用功，得到了最高分。
近义词: surpassed, outperformed, excelled
反义词: lagged behind, underperformed

词性
v.
