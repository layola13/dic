balsamiferous:/bɔːlˌsæmˈɪfərəs/
adjective
产香脂的；有香脂香味的
Producing or yielding balsam; having the fragrance of balsam.
The balsamiferous trees filled the air with a sweet, resinous scent.  这些产香脂的树木使空气中充满了甜美的树脂香味。
The ancient Egyptians used balsamiferous oils in their embalming practices. 古埃及人在他们的防腐实践中使用香脂油。
近义词: fragrant, aromatic, sweet-smelling, resinous
反义词: odorless, scentless
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *balsamifer, from balsamum "balsam" + -fer "bearing."
