close-fitting:/ˌkloʊs ˈfɪtɪŋ/| adj.|紧身的，贴身的|Fitting very closely or tightly to the body.|She wore a close-fitting dress to the party. 她穿了一件紧身连衣裙去参加派对。|近义词：tight, snug, fitted, form-fitting|反义词:loose, baggy, loose-fitting|词性:形容词

词源:
From "close" (meaning near or tight) + "fitting" (present participle of "fit," meaning to be the right shape and size).
