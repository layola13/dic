intraformational:/ˌɪntrəfɔːrˈmeɪʃənəl/
adjective
地层内部的；地层内的
Occurring within a single geologic formation.  Relating to processes or relationships within a single geologic formation.
The intraformational conglomerate was formed by erosion and redeposition within the same formation.  这个地层内部砾岩是由同一地层内的侵蚀和再沉积作用形成的。
Intraformational folding is common in this area.  该地区常见地层内褶皱。
Synonyms: within a formation, internal to a formation
Antonyms: interformational (between formations)
词性: adjective
