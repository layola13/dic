deadlocked:/ˈdɛdlɒkt/ (British), /ˈdɛdlɑːkt/ (American)| adj.|僵持的，僵局的|A situation in which no progress can be made because of disagreement; at an impasse.|The negotiations have become deadlocked, with neither side willing to compromise. 谈判陷入僵局，双方都不愿妥协。|The jury was deadlocked, unable to reach a verdict. 陪审团陷入僵局，无法达成裁决。|stalemated, gridlocked|flexible, progressing|adjective
