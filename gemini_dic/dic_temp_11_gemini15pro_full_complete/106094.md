knife-like:/ˈnaɪfˌlaɪk/
adjective
像刀一样的; 刀锋般的
Resembling a knife, especially in sharpness or shape.
The surgeon made a knife-like incision.  外科医生做了一个像刀锋一样的切口。
He felt a knife-like pain in his chest. 他感到胸口一阵刀割般的疼痛。
sharp, keen, piercing, cutting
blunt, dull
