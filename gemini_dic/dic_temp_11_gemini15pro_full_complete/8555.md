antichthon:/ænˈtɪkθən/ | n. | 对地星；相对世界 | A hypothetical planet on the opposite side of the sun from the Earth, always invisible because it is perpetually concealed by the sun. |  The ancient Greeks speculated about the existence of an antichthon. 古希腊人推测存在一个对地星。 | synonyms: counter-earth | antonyms: Earth | noun

antichthon:/ænˈtɪkθən/ | n. |  相对物；对映体 | Something that is diametrically opposite or contrasted to something else.  | The concept of good and evil can be seen as antichthons. 善与恶的概念可以被视为对映体。 | synonyms: opposite, antithesis, counterpart | antonyms:  similar, equivalent | noun
