cocoons:/kəˈkuːnz/
n.|茧; 舒适安全的地方|A silky case spun by the larvae of many insects for protection during the pupal stage.  Also used metaphorically to describe a safe, comfortable, and protected environment.|The caterpillars spun cocoons and waited to transform into butterflies. 毛毛虫结茧，等待化蝶。|近义词：shells, chrysalises|反义词: exposure|词性: 名词 (plural)


cocoon:/kəˈkuːn/
vt.|把…包在茧里; 保护;|To wrap or enclose something or someone in a protective or comforting way.|She cocooned herself in blankets on the cold night. 在寒冷的夜晚，她用毯子把自己裹得严严实实的。|近义词：protect, shelter, insulate|反义词: expose|词性: 动词
