electronegativity:/ɪˌlektrəʊˌneɡəˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |电负性|A measure of the tendency of an atom to attract a bonding pair of electrons.|Fluorine has the highest electronegativity of all the elements. 氟是所有元素中电负性最高的。|近义词：electron affinity |反义词: electropositivity |词性: noun

Etymology: electro- (electric) + negative + -ity (noun suffix)  词源：electro-（电的）+ negative（负的）+ -ity（名词后缀）
