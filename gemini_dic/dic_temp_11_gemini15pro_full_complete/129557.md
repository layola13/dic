non-captive:/nɒnˈkæptɪv/ | adj. | 非圈养的，野生的；自由的，不受控制的 | Not captive; wild; free and not controlled. | Example:  Non-captive pandas are rare. 野生大熊猫很稀有。

Example: The study focused on the behavior of non-captive dolphins.  这项研究的重点是非圈养海豚的行为。

| 近义词: wild, free-ranging, feral | 反义词: captive, confined, domesticated | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "captive" (from Latin "captivus," meaning "taken prisoner," from "capere," meaning "to take").
