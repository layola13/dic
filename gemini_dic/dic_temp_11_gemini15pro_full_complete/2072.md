acyclic:/əˈsaɪklɪk/| adj.|非循环的，无环的|Not cyclic; having no cycles or loops.|The graph is acyclic, meaning it contains no cycles.|Synonyms: noncyclic, noncircular|Antonyms: cyclic, circular|adjective

acyclic:/əˈsaɪklɪk/| n.|无环化合物|An organic compound without rings in its structure.|Acyclic hydrocarbons are also known as aliphatic hydrocarbons.|Synonyms: noncyclic compound, open-chain compound|Antonyms: cyclic compound, ring compound|noun
