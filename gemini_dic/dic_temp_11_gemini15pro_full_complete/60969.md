elfs:/ɛlfs/ | n. | 小精灵，妖精 | A supernatural creature of folklore and fantasy, typically having magical powers, pointed ears, and a mischievous nature.  |  The elfs danced in the moonlight.  小精灵在月光下跳舞。 | Synonyms: sprite, pixie, fairy | Antonyms: ogre, giant | noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *elf, from Old English ælf (elf, fairy, goblin, incubus), from Proto-Germanic *albaz (elf), of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse álfr (elf), and ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *albh- (white).
