nondispensational:/ˌnɒndɪspɛnˈseɪʃənəl/
adjective
非时代论的
Not relating to or adhering to dispensationalism (a theological system that divides history into periods marked by distinct divine covenants).
The pastor's nondispensational approach to biblical interpretation emphasized the continuity of God's plan throughout history.  牧师对圣经解释的非时代论方法强调了上帝计划在整个历史中的连续性。
He wrote a nondispensational commentary on the book of Revelation. 他写了一篇关于启示录的非时代论注释。
近义词: covenant theology, non-dispensational
反义词: dispensational, premillennial (often associated with dispensationalism)
词性: adjective


词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + dispensational (from dispensation, referring to a system of order, administration, or management)
