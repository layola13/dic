nuance:/ˈnjuːɑːns/ (US), /ˈnjuːɒns/ (UK)| n.|细微差别；微妙之处|a subtle difference in or shade of meaning, expression, or sound.|The nuances of her voice conveyed her true feelings. 她声音的细微变化传达了她真实的感受。|synonyms: subtlety, shade, distinction, nicety, refinement|antonyms: obviousness, clarity, bluntness|noun


nuances:/ˈnjuːɑːnsiz/ (US), /ˈnjuːɒnsiz/ (UK)| n.|细微差别；微妙之处（复数）|subtle differences in or shades of meaning, expression, or sound.|He was aware of the cultural nuances of the language. 他意识到这门语言的文化细微之处。|synonyms: subtleties, shades, distinctions, niceties, refinements|antonyms: obviousnesses, clarities, bluntnesses|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nuance*, "shade, slight difference," from *nuer*, "to shade," derived from *nue*, "cloud."
