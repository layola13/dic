bolt's:/boʊlts/| n.|螺栓，门闩，闪电，弩箭(bolt的名词所有格)|A metal pin or rod, usually with a head at one end and a screw thread at the other, used for fastening things together. Also can refer to a flash of lightning or a crossbow arrow.  It's the possessive form of "bolt".|The bolt's threads were stripped.  螺栓的螺纹滑丝了。|近义词：fastener, latch, thunderbolt, arrow|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the meaning of bolt being used)|noun
