board-up:/ˈbɔːrd ʌp/| v. n.|用木板封住，封窗，木板封盖|To cover windows or doors of a building with boards, typically as protection against damage or intrusion.|例句：They boarded up the windows before the hurricane. 他们在飓风来临前用木板封住了窗户。|近义词：cover, seal, secure|反义词: uncover, open|词性:动词

board-up:/ˈbɔːrd ʌp/| n.|用木板封住的状态，封窗，木板封盖|The act of boarding up something or the boards used to do this.|例句：The board-up protected the shop from looters. 木板封盖保护了商店免受抢劫者的侵害。|近义词：covering, barrier|反义词: opening|词性:名词

词源: "board" (木板) + "up" (向上，完全地).  "Up" here intensifies the action of "board," implying a complete covering.
