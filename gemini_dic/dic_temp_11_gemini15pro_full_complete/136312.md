nonrepudiative:/ˌnɒnriˌpjuːdiˈeɪtɪv/
adjective
不可否认的;  无法抵赖的
Unable to deny having performed a particular action, especially by means of a digital signature.  This ensures that someone cannot deny that they sent a message, or signed a document electronically.
例句：Nonrepudiative services are crucial for online transactions.  不可否认的服务对于在线交易至关重要。

例句：A digital signature provides nonrepudiation, meaning the sender cannot deny sending the message. 数字签名提供了不可否认性，这意味着发送者不能否认发送了消息。

近义词：undeniable, irrefutable, incontestable
反义词：repudiable, deniable, contestable
词性:adjective


词源:
From non- + repudiate (from Latin repudiare 'to reject, divorce') + -ive.  Essentially meaning "not able to be rejected or denied."
