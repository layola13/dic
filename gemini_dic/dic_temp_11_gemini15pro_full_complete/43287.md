craftspersons:/ˈkræftsˌpɜːsnz/ /ˈkræftsˌpɜːrsənz/ |n.|工匠；手艺人|A skilled worker who makes things by hand.|例句：The craftspersons of the village are renowned for their intricate wood carvings. 这个村庄的工匠以其复杂的木雕而闻名。

例句：Many craftspersons sell their wares at the local market. 许多工匠在当地市场出售他们的商品。

近义词：artisan, craftsperson, craftsman, craftswoman, handcraftsman, handicrafter, artificer|反义词: amateur, unskilled worker|词性: 名词
