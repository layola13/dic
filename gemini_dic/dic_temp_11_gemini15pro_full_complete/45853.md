cut both ways:/kʌt boʊθ weɪz/
idiom
有利有弊；双刃剑
have both advantages and disadvantages; have a double effect
The decision to raise taxes will cut both ways - it will bring in more revenue but could also discourage investment.  加税的决定有利有弊——它会带来更多收入，但也可能抑制投资。
This new law cuts both ways. It will protect some, but harm others. 这项新法律有利有弊。它会保护一些人，但也会伤害另一些人。
synonyms: be a double-edged sword, have two sides, have pros and cons
antonyms: be unequivocally beneficial/detrimental, be entirely positive/negative
