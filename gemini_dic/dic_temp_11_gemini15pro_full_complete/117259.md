mechanicals: /məˈkænɪkəlz/
noun
机械部件; 机械装置; (尤指车辆的)机械部分; 机械学
the working parts of a machine, especially of a vehicle.
例句：The car's mechanicals were in good condition. 这辆车的机械部件状况良好。

例句：He's good at fixing mechanicals. 他擅长修理机械装置。

近义词：machinery, mechanism, apparatus, equipment, components
反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym for a physical object)
词性: noun (plural)


mechanicals: /məˈkænɪkəlz/
noun
(the mechanicals) (戏剧)舞台机械师; 布景员; 道具员
(theater) the stage machinery or the people who operate it.
例句：The mechanicals set up the scenery quickly and efficiently. 舞台机械师快速高效地搭好了布景。

例句：The director praised the mechanicals for their smooth handling of the scene changes. 导演称赞布景员顺利地完成了场景切换。

近义词：stagehands, crew, technicians
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mechanical" + "-s" (plural suffix).  "Mechanical" comes from Late Latin *mechanicus, from Greek mēkhanikos "of or pertaining to machines," from mēkhanē "machine, contrivance, device."
