abolishes:/əˈbɒlɪʃɪz/
verb
废除，取消
formally puts an end to (a system, practice, or institution).
The government abolishes the death penalty. 政府废除了死刑。
synonyms: eliminates, ends, terminates, eradicates, extinguishes, dismantles, revokes, repeals, annuls, nullifies, invalidates, cancels
antonyms: establishes, institutes, introduces, creates, implements, enacts, restores, reinstates
