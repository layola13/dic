nonstories:/ˈnɒnˌstɔːriz/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌstɔːriːz/ (UK)
n.
虚构的故事，不真实的故事；琐碎的事情
Fictional or trivial accounts; things that are not worth reporting or considering as significant stories.
The tabloid was filled with nonstories about celebrities. 这家小报上充斥着关于名人的虚构故事。
Newspapers sometimes publish nonstories to fill space. 报纸有时会刊登一些琐碎的事情来填补版面。
synonyms: fabrications, trivialities, insignificant matters
antonyms: true stories, factual accounts, important news
noun
