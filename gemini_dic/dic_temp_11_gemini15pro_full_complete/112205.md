looper:/ˈluːpər/| n.|循环器，环状物，重复播放的设备|A device that continuously plays a recorded loop of audio or video.  This allows musicians to layer sounds or create rhythmic patterns. |The guitarist used a looper to create a complex backing track. 吉他手用循环器创作了一段复杂的背景音轨。| synonyms: loop pedal, loop station| antonyms: N/A| noun
