outlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/
n.
异常值，离群值
a data point that differs significantly from other observations
The outlier in the data set skewed the results.  这个数据集中的异常值使结果出现了偏差。

outlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/
n.
局外人，例外
a person or thing situated away or detached from the main body or system
He was considered an outlier in the academic community. 他被认为是学术界的局外人。


Etymology->
From out +‎ -lier, from lie (“to recline”)  来自out +‎ -lier，源于lie（“躺卧”）


Synonyms->
anomaly, exception, aberration, rarity, irregularity
Antonyms->
norm, standard, average, typical
