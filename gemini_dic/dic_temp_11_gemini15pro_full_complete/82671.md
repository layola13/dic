guesstimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmət/| n. v.| 估计，估算 |An estimate based on a combination of guesswork and calculation. |  I guesstimate that the project will take three months to complete. 我估计这个项目需要三个月才能完成。 | Example:  The CEO guesstimated the company's profits for the next quarter. 首席执行官估算了公司下一季度的利润。 | 近义词: estimate, approximation, conjecture, surmise | 反义词: calculation, measurement | 词性: noun, verb

Etymology:  A blend of "guess" and "estimate," first recorded in the mid-20th century. 由“guess”（猜测）和“estimate”（估计）混合而成，最早记录于20世纪中期。
