cripes:/kraɪps/| int.|哎呀；我的天哪；(表示惊讶、恼怒、失望等)|An exclamation of surprise, annoyance, or disappointment.  It's a mild oath.|Cripes, I forgot my keys! 哎呀，我忘了带钥匙了！|近义词：gosh, gee, darn, heck|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection)|interjection
词源:  Probably a euphemism for "Christ".
