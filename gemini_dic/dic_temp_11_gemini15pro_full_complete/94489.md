important:/ɪmˈpɔːrtnt/ (UK), /ɪmˈpɔːrtnt/ (US)| adj.|重要的，重大的|Of great significance or value; likely to have a profound effect on success, survival, or happiness.|例句：Education is important for everyone.  教育对每个人都很重要。|近义词：significant, crucial, essential, vital|反义词：unimportant, insignificant, trivial|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *important* (modern French *important*), present participle of *importer* "to signify, mean, matter, be of consequence," from Latin *importare* "to bring in, introduce, convey, imply, signify," from *in-* "into" + *portare* "to carry."
