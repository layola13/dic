graves: /ɡreɪvz/
noun
坟墓; 墓穴; 埋葬…的地方
A place of burial for a dead body, typically a hole dug in the ground and marked by a stone or mound.
The family visited their loved one's graves on the anniversary of their death.  家人在他们亲人去世的周年纪念日去扫墓。
Tombs, sepulchers, burial grounds
Crypts, mausoleums

graves: /ɡreɪvz/
noun
死亡; 毁灭; 结束
A person's death. (Figurative)
His reckless driving sent him to an early grave. 他鲁莽的驾驶让他早早送了命。
Death, demise, end
Life, birth
