in-a-hole:/ɪn ə hoʊl/| adj.|处于困境中，陷入麻烦，负债|Being in a difficult situation, often financially.| Example: He's in a hole after losing his job and racking up debt. 他失业后负债累累，陷入了困境。

Example:  The company is in a hole and needs a major investment to survive.  这家公司陷入了困境，需要一大笔投资才能生存下去。|近义词：in trouble, in debt, struggling, behind, underwater|反义词:solvent, prosperous, affluent|词性:形容词
