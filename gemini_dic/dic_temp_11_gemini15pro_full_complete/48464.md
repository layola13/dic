deemphasizes:/ˌdiːˈɛmfəsaɪzɪz/
verb
不再强调，减弱…的强调
to reduce the importance or prominence given to something
The new CEO deemphasizes the importance of marketing and focuses on product development. 新任首席执行官不再强调市场营销的重要性，而专注于产品开发。
The report deemphasizes the negative aspects of the project.  报告淡化了该项目的负面影响。
downplay, minimize, understate
emphasize, highlight, stress
verb
