eyeball:/ˈaɪbɔːl/ | n. vt. |眼球；注视，盯着看 |The transparent front part of the eye. To look at someone or something intently. |He got poked in the eyeball. 他被戳到眼球了。

She eyeballed the stranger suspiciously. 她怀疑地盯着那个陌生人。|近义词：stare, gaze, glare, watch, observe, scrutinize, inspect |反义词: glance, glimpse |n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From eye + ball.  The word "eye" comes from Old English ēage, and "ball" comes from Old Norse bollr.
