nonadjudicatively:/ˌnɒnəˈdʒuːdɪkətɪvli/
| adv. |非裁决地，非判决地|Without reaching or involving a formal judgment or legal decision.  It describes actions or processes that avoid making a binding determination of right or wrong, guilt or innocence, or liability.|The committee reviewed the case nonadjudicatively to provide recommendations for improvement. 委员会对案件进行了非裁决性审查，以提供改进建议。

The issue was resolved nonadjudicatively through mediation.  这个问题通过调解以非裁决的方式解决了。|近义词:informally, extrajudicially, unofficially |反义词:judicially, formally, officially |词性:副词

词源:
non- (prefix): meaning "not"
adjudicate (verb): from Latin *adjudicare* "to award by judgment," from *ad-* "to" + *judicare* "to judge"
-ive (suffix): forming adjectives
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs
