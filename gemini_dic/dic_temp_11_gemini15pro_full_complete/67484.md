extra-essential:/ˌekstrə ɪˈsenʃəl/
adjective
极其重要的，必不可少的
more essential than essential; absolutely necessary
例句：These extra-essential vitamins are crucial for maintaining optimal health. 这些极其重要的维生素对于维持最佳健康至关重要。

例句：Access to clean water is extra-essential during a natural disaster. 在自然灾害期间，获得清洁用水是极其重要的。

近义词：crucial, vital, indispensable, paramount
反义词：unessential, unimportant, trivial
词性：adjective
词源：extra- (prefix meaning "beyond, outside of") + essential (adjective meaning "absolutely necessary")
