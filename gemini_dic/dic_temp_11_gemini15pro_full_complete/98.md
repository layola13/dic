abaca:/əˈbɑːkə/
n.
马尼拉麻, 麻蕉
A strong fiber obtained from the leaves of a Philippine plant, used in making rope and various fabrics.
Manila hemp is a strong fiber obtained from the leaves of the abaca plant. 马尼拉麻是从麻蕉植物的叶子中提取的强韧纤维。
The rope was made of high-quality abaca. 这根绳子是用优质的马尼拉麻制成的。
近义词：Manila hemp, Musa textilis
反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant fiber)
词性: noun
