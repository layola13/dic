hansard:/ˈhænzərd/| n.|议会记录，(尤指)英国议会辩论的正式记录|The Hansard is a verbatim record of parliamentary debates.  议会记录是议会辩论的逐字记录。|例句：Researchers often consult Hansard to understand the historical context of legislation. 研究人员经常查阅议会记录以了解立法的历史背景。

例句：The minister's comments were recorded in Hansard. 部长的评论被记录在议会记录中。|近义词：record, transcript, minutes|反义词:None|词性:noun
