beetler:/ˈbiːtlər/| n.|甲虫; 敲打者; 锤打者|A person or thing that beats, especially a mallet used for beating clothes, carpets, etc. or a person who uses such a mallet. Also, a beetle (insect).|The beetler hammered the carpet to remove the dust. 敲打者用锤子敲打地毯以去除灰尘。|Synonyms: hammerer, mallet-wielder, beetle (insect)|Antonyms: None|noun

beetler:/ˈbiːtlər/| n.|甲虫|Any of numerous insects of the order Coleoptera, having biting mouthparts and hard, shell-like front wings.|The beetler scurried across the forest floor. 甲虫快速地穿过森林地面。|Synonyms: beetle, insect|Antonyms: None|noun
