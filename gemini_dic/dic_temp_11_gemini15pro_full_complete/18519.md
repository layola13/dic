benedictory:/ˌbenɪˈdɪktəri/
adjective
祝福的，表示好意的
expressing or conveying a blessing
The priest offered a benedictory prayer.  神父做了祝福祈祷。

近义词:blessing, consecratory, dedicatory
反义词:maledictory, cursing

词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin benedictorius, from benedicere ‘bless’ (see benediction).
