biocellate: /ˌbaɪoʊˈsɛleɪt/ | adj. | 双细胞的，有两个细胞的 | Composed of two cells. Having two cells.  | Example: The biocellate organism was observed under a microscope.  这个双细胞生物在显微镜下被观察到。 | Example: Biocellate structures are fundamental to the development of certain species. 双细胞结构对于某些物种的发育至关重要。 | 近义词: bicellular | 反义词: unicellular | 词性: adjective

词源: bio- (life) + -cell (cell) + -ate (suffix forming adjectives)
