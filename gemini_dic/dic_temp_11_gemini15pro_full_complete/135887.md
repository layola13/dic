nonprovocation:/ˌnɒnˌprɒvəˈkeɪʃn/
|n.|无缘无故；不挑衅；非挑衅|Absence of provocation; the state of not provoking or giving cause for anger, hostility, or resentment.|The attack was an act of pure nonprovocation. 这次袭击完全是无缘无故的。|Synonyms: peacefulness, pacifism, nonaggression|Antonyms: provocation, aggression, incitement|noun
