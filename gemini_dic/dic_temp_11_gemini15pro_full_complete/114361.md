Majorcan: /məˈdʒɔːrkən/ | adj. n. | 马略卡岛的；马略卡岛人 | Relating to the Spanish island of Majorca, or its people. |  She bought a beautiful Majorcan pottery vase. 她买了一个漂亮的马略卡陶艺花瓶。 |  He is a Majorcan by birth. 他是土生土长的马略卡人。 | 近义词: Mallorcan | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Majorca (Spanish Mallorca), from Latin Maiorica "larger island," feminine of Maioricus, from maior "larger" (see major (adj.)) + -icus.
