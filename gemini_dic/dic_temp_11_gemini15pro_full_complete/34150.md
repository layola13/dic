clairvoyant:/ˌklɛərˈvɔɪənt/| adj. n.|透视的，有洞察力的；千里眼的人|A person who claims to have a supernatural ability to perceive events in the future or beyond normal sensory contact.  Able to see beyond the range of ordinary perception.|She went to see a clairvoyant who told her she would meet a tall, dark stranger. 她去找了一个千里眼，千里眼告诉她会遇到一个高个子、黑头发的陌生人。|近义词：psychic, telepathic, prescient|反义词：unperceptive, oblivious|词性:adjective, noun

clairvoyant:/ˌklɛərˈvɔɪənt/| n.|千里眼的人|A person who claims to have a supernatural ability to perceive events in the future or beyond normal sensory contact.|The clairvoyant predicted a great change in her life.  这个千里眼预言她的人生将发生巨大的变化。|近义词：psychic, medium, seer|反义词：none|词性:noun
