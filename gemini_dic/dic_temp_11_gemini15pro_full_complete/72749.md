fogeyism:/ˈfoʊɡiɪzəm/ | n. | 墨守成规；守旧；老古董思想 | The tendency to be resistant to change and new ideas, often associated with older people.  |  His fogeyism prevented him from appreciating the new music. 他的墨守成规使他无法欣赏新的音乐。 | Example:  He dismissed the new technology as mere novelty, a clear sign of his fogeyism.  他认为新技术只是新奇玩意儿，这清楚地表明了他的守旧思想。 | Synonyms: conservatism, old-fashionedness, traditionalism | Antonyms:  modernity, progressiveness, open-mindedness | noun
