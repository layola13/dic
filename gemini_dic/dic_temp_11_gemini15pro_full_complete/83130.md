gut-wrenchingly:/ˈɡʌtˌrɛntʃɪŋli/
adverb
令人心碎地，极其痛苦地
in a way that causes great emotional pain and distress
The film portrays gut-wrenchingly the suffering of the refugees. 这部电影令人心碎地描绘了难民的苦难。
Synonyms: heart-rending, agonizing, harrowing
Antonyms: comforting, soothing


ETYMOLOGY->
From gut (n.) + wrench (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Gut" in this context refers to the stomach or abdomen, and "wrench" means to twist or pull violently.  The word literally suggests a feeling of being physically twisted in the stomach due to emotional pain.
