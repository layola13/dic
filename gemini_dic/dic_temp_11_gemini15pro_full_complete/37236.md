comforted:/ˈkʌmfərtɪd/
v.|安慰的，得到安慰的|Past participle of "comfort."  Meaning soothed, consoled, or relieved from distress.|She felt comforted by his words. 她被他的话安慰了。|近义词：solaced, relieved, consoled|反义词：distressed, upset, troubled
词性: 动词(过去分词)

comfort:/ˈkʌmfərt/
n.|安慰，舒适|A state of physical ease and freedom from pain or constraint.|The soft blanket provided her with comfort.  柔软的毯子给了她舒适感。|近义词：solace, relief, ease|反义词：discomfort, distress, pain
词性: 名词

comfort:/ˈkʌmfərt/
v.|安慰，抚慰|To soothe, console, or relieve from distress.|He comforted her after her loss. 他在她失去亲人后安慰她。|近义词：solace, relieve, console|反义词：distress, upset, trouble
词性: 动词
