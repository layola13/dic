nebulizer:/ˈnɛbjʊˌlaɪzər/ | n. |喷雾器，雾化器 |A device that turns liquid medicine into a fine mist that can be inhaled. |例句：The doctor prescribed a nebulizer to help with her breathing. 医生开了个喷雾器来帮助她呼吸。

例句：He uses a nebulizer to administer his asthma medication. 他使用喷雾器来吸入哮喘药物。|近义词：atomizer, vaporizer |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
