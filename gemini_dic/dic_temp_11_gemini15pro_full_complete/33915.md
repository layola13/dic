circumvolution:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvəˈluːʃən/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈvəˈluːʃən/
|n.|迂回；盘旋；卷绕；（尤指）冗长复杂的描述|A long and complicated process or sequence of events, especially one full of twists and turns.  A turning or winding around.  One complete turn; a revolution.|The circumvolutions of the plot were difficult to follow.  情节的迂回曲折难以理解。|近义词：convolution, twist, turn, winding, meander, complexity, intricacy|反义词：straightforwardness, simplicity, directness|词性：名词

circumvolution:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvəˈluːʃən/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈvəˈluːʃən/
|n.|（尤指脑回的）脑回|A gyrus, especially of the cerebrum.|The circumvolutions of the brain are responsible for higher-level thinking. 大脑的脑回负责高级思维。|近义词：gyrus, fold|反义词：sulcus|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin circumvolutionem, accusative singular of circumvolutio (“a rolling around”), from circumvolvere (“to roll around”), from circum (“around”) + volvere (“to roll”).
