murderously:/ˈmɜːrdərəsli/| adv.|凶狠地，杀气腾腾地|In a manner suggesting murder; with murderous intent.  Characterized by or exhibiting extreme hostility or destructiveness.|He glared murderously at his rival. 他凶狠地瞪着他的对手。|近义词：viciously, savagely, brutally, fiercely|反义词：gently, kindly, peacefully|词性:副词
词源: From murder + -ous + -ly. Ultimately from the Latin *mort- (“death”).
