disinterestedly:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪdli/
| adv. |无私地，公正地|In a disinterested way, without bias or personal stake.|The judge disinterestedly reviewed the evidence.  法官公正地审查了证据。|近义词：impartially, objectively, neutrally|反义词：selfishly, biasedly, partially
|词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "disinterested" + "-ly".  "Disinterested" comes from "dis-" (not) + "interested" (having a personal stake or bias).  The root "interest" ultimately comes from the Latin "interesse" (to be of importance).
