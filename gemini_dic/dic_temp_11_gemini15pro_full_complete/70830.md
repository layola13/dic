finiking:/ˈfaɪnɪkɪŋ/
adjective
挑剔的，吹毛求疵的
Too particular or fastidious; fussy.
She's always finiking about the house, nothing is ever clean enough. 她总是对房子吹毛求疵，没有什么是足够干净的。
近义词：fussy, picky, fastidious, choosy, particular
反义词：easygoing, undemanding, tolerant

词性: adjective
