operations:/ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 操作，运营，行动 | The company's operations are spread across several countries. 该公司的业务遍布多个国家。|例句：Military operations were launched at dawn. 军事行动在黎明时分展开。| 近义词：activities, actions, proceedings | 反义词：inaction, cessation | noun

operations:/ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 手术 | He underwent a series of operations on his knee. 他接受了一系列膝盖手术。|例句：The operation was a success. 手术很成功。| 近义词：surgery, procedure | 反义词： | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *operacioun, from Old French operacion, operation, from Latin operātiō (“a working, operation, labor”), from operor (“I work”).
