madrasa:/məˈdræsə/ /məˈdrɑːsə/ | n. |伊斯兰学校，经学院|A Muslim school, especially one attached to a mosque.|例句：He studied at a madrasa in his youth. 他年轻时在一所经学院学习。

例句：The madrasa provided religious and secular education. 这所经学院提供宗教和世俗教育。

近义词：Islamic school, religious school|反义词:secular school|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic مدرسة (madrasa), meaning "school," from the root درس (dars) "to study," ultimately from Aramaic.
