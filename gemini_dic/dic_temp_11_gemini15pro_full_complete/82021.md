gripping:/ˈɡrɪpɪŋ/| adj.|扣人心弦的，引人入胜的|firmly holding the attention or interest; exciting.|The gripping novel kept me reading until dawn. 这本扣人心弦的小说让我读到天亮。|Synonyms: fascinating, captivating, enthralling, compelling|Antonyms: boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting|adjective

gripping:/ˈɡrɪpɪŋ/| n.|紧握，抓牢 (较少用作名词)|The act of gripping something firmly.|His gripping of the steering wheel was white-knuckled. 他紧紧地抓住方向盘，指节都发白了。(较少用)|Synonyms: grasp, clutch, hold|Antonyms: release, loosen|noun
