hooklet:/ˈhʊklɪt/| n.|小钩，钩状物，倒钩|A small hook or hook-like part, especially one used for fastening or catching something. |The fish swallowed the hooklet and couldn't escape. 鱼吞下了倒钩，无法逃脱。|近义词：barb, hook, clasp|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From hook + -let, diminutive suffix.  Essentially a "small hook".
