gern:/ɡɛrn/| adv.|乐意地，愿意地| gladly, willingly; used with verbs like mögen (to like) to express that one enjoys doing something.| Ich mag gern Schokolade essen. (I like to eat chocolate / I enjoy eating chocolate.)|近义词：bereitwillig, freudig|反义词:ungern, widerwillig|词性:副词

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Ich lerne gern Deutsch. = I enjoy learning German.
Er liest gern Bücher. = He enjoys reading books.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Ich spiele gern gerade Klavier. = I'm enjoying playing the piano right now.  (Note: While grammatically correct, this is less common than using the simple present to express enjoyment.)
Sie singt gern gerade. = She’s enjoying singing right now.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Ich habe gern mit ihm gearbeitet. = I enjoyed working with him.
Wir haben gern Zeit miteinander verbracht. = We enjoyed spending time together.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "gern" as it emphasizes the duration of the action, not the enjoyment.)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Ich lernte gern Deutsch. = I enjoyed learning German.
Er las gern Bücher. = He enjoyed reading books.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Ich spielte gern gerade Klavier, als du angerufen hast. = I was enjoying playing the piano when you called. (Less common, similar to present continuous)
Sie sang gern gerade, als der Strom ausfiel. = She was enjoying singing when the power went out.

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Ich hatte gern mit ihm gearbeitet, bevor er die Firma verließ. = I had enjoyed working with him before he left the company.
Wir hatten gern Zeit miteinander verbracht, bevor sie wegzog. = We had enjoyed spending time together before she moved away.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "gern".)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Ich werde gern Deutsch lernen. = I will enjoy learning German.
Er wird gern Bücher lesen. = He will enjoy reading books.

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Ich werde gern Klavier spielen, wenn du kommst. = I will be enjoying playing the piano when you come. (Less common)
Sie wird gern singen, wenn wir ankommen. = She will be enjoying singing when we arrive.

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Bis dahin werde ich gern viel Deutsch gelernt haben. = By then I will have enjoyed learning a lot of German. (Awkward, less common)
Bis dahin wird er gern viele Bücher gelesen haben. = By then he will have enjoyed reading many books.

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "gern".)

虚拟语气:
Wenn ich mehr Zeit hätte, würde ich gern öfter ins Kino gehen. = If I had more time, I would enjoy going to the cinema more often.
Ich wünschte, ich könnte gern besser singen. = I wish I enjoyed singing better.  (This phrasing is a bit unusual. It's more natural to say "I wish I were a better singer.")

被动语态:
(Gern is not used in the passive voice.)

疑问句:
Liest du gern Bücher? = Do you enjoy reading books?
Spielst du gern Klavier? = Do you enjoy playing the piano?

否定句:
Ich lerne nicht gern Mathe. = I don't enjoy learning math.
Er liest nicht gern Zeitungen. = He doesn't enjoy reading newspapers.
