counterfires:/ˈkaʊntərˌfaɪərz/ | n. |反击火力，还击，反炮火|  The artillery launched counterfires against the enemy positions. 炮兵向敌方阵地发射了反击火力。 | English Explanation:  Fire returned to suppress or destroy enemy fire.  It can refer to any kind of retaliatory fire, but is often used in a military context. | Example: The counterfires were swift and accurate. 反击火力迅速而准确。| Synonym:  return fire, retaliatory fire | Antonym:  initial attack, first strike | noun


counterfire:/ˈkaʊntərˌfaɪər/ | v. |还击，反击| The troops counterfired against the sniper. 部队向狙击手进行了反击。| English Explanation: To return fire; to shoot back.  Primarily used in a military context. | Example:  They counterfired immediately after the first volley.  他们在第一轮齐射后立即反击。| Synonym:  return fire, shoot back | Antonym:  hold fire, cease fire | verb
