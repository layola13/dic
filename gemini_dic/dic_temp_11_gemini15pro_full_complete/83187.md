guv:/ɡʌv/| n.|老板; 政府; 爸爸; (Guv)勋爵|A colloquial term of address for a man, especially one in a position of authority, often used humorously or ironically.  It can also be a shortened form of "governor."|Example 1: "Alright, guv, what can I do for you?" 好的，老板，我能为您做些什么？

Example 2: "Ask the guv, he'll know."  问问老板，他会知道的。|近义词：boss, governor, dad, father|反义词:employee, subordinate|noun
