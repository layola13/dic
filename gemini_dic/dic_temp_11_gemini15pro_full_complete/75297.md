frontierlike:/ˈfrʌnˌtɪrˌlaɪk/
adjective
类似边境的，边疆似的
Resembling or characteristic of a frontier; having the qualities of a frontier (wild, undeveloped, uncharted, etc.)
The town had a frontierlike atmosphere, with saloons and dusty streets.  这个小镇有一种类似边境的气氛，有酒吧和尘土飞扬的街道。
The explorer ventured into the frontierlike wilderness. 探险家冒险进入类似边境的荒野。
Synonyms:  pioneering, untamed, wild, undeveloped, uncharted
Antonyms:  developed, civilized, urban, tame, explored
词性: 形容词
