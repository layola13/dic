gobbles:/ˈɡɒblz/ (British) /ˈɡɑːblz/ (American)| v.|狼吞虎咽；贪婪地吃；咯咯叫|This word is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "gobble." It means to eat something quickly and noisily, or to make a sound like a turkey.|He gobbles his food down in minutes. 他几分钟就把食物狼吞虎咽地吃完了。| The turkey gobbles loudly. 火鸡咯咯地叫着。|synonyms: devour, gulp, guzzle, bolt, wolf (down)|antonyms: nibble, savor|verb
