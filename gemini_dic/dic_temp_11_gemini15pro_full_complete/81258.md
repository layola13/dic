granitelike: /ˈɡrænɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像花岗岩的；坚硬的
Resembling granite in hardness, texture, or appearance.
The cliff face was granitelike in its solidity.  悬崖表面坚硬得像花岗岩。
The statue had a granitelike firmness.  这座雕像有着花岗岩般的坚固。
近义词: rocklike, stony, hard, inflexible
反义词: soft, pliable, yielding
词性: adjective

词源:
From granite + -like.  由granite（花岗岩）和-like（像…的）组成。
