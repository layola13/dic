horus:/ˈhɔːrəs/| n.|荷鲁斯|Horus is one of the most significant ancient Egyptian deities. He was often depicted as a falcon or a falcon-headed man.  He is the son of Osiris and Isis and is considered a god of the sky, war, and protection.| Horus was worshipped throughout Egypt. 荷鲁斯在整个埃及都受到崇拜。| The Eye of Horus is a powerful symbol of protection. 荷鲁斯之眼是一个强大的保护象征。| synonyms: god, deity| antonyms: mortal, human| noun
