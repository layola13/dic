necrosis:/nɛˈkroʊsɪs/| n. |坏死，组织坏死|The localized death of living cells or tissues in the body, resulting from injury or disease.| The doctor diagnosed the tissue as necrosis. 医生诊断该组织坏死。| 近义词：gangrene, mortification| 反义词: growth, regeneration| 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek νέκρωσις (nékrōsis, “a killing, making dead”), from νεκρός (nekrós, “dead body, corpse”).
