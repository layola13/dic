derk:/dɜːrk/
noun
a stupid or foolish person
a dull, unintelligent person.
He's such a derk, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，他又把钥匙锁在车里了。
Synonyms: idiot, fool, dolt, dunce, numbskull
Antonyms: genius, intellect, scholar
词性: 名词

derk:/dɜːrk/
verb
to act stupidly or foolishly.
to behave in a clumsy or foolish way.
Stop derking around and help me with this! 别傻乎乎的了，帮我一下这个！
Synonyms: goof off, mess around, clown around
Antonyms: focus, concentrate, apply oneself
词性: 动词
