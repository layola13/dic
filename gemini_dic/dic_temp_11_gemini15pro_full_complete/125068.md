mulishness:/ˈmjuːlɪʃnɪs/ | n. | 顽固，固执 | The mulishness of his character made it impossible to reason with him. |  His mulishness prevented any compromise. 他很固执，以致于无法妥协。| stubbornness, obstinacy, pigheadedness | flexibility, compliance | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mule + -ishness.  "Mule" itself comes from Latin "mulus."  The suffix "-ishness" denotes a state or quality of being like the root word, in this case, like a mule, known for its stubbornness.
