beheaders:/biˈhɛdərz/| n.|斩首者，刽子手|People who behead others; executioners. |The beheaders were feared throughout the land. 刽子手在全国各地都令人恐惧。| The king employed beheaders to deal with traitors. 国王雇用刽子手来处置叛徒。 |近义词：executioner, headsman|反义词: savior, protector|词性:名词

词源:  "beheader" comes from the verb "behead," which in turn is formed from the prefix "be-" (meaning "to make" or "to cause to be") and the noun "head." The "-er" suffix denotes the person performing the action.
