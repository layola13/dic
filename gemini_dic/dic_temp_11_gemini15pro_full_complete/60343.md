egophonic:/ˌiːɡəˈfɒnɪk/ |adj.|羊鸣样的，支气管羊鸣音的|Relating to or characterized by egophony, an increased resonance of voice sounds heard when auscultating the lungs, often indicating consolidation of lung tissue, as in pneumonia.  It sounds like the bleating of a goat.|The doctor detected an egophonic sound in the patient's right lung, suggesting pneumonia.|近义词：bronchophonic|反义词： (No direct antonym, but absence of egophony suggests healthy lung tissue)|词性: adjective
