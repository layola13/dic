inched:/ɪntʃt/| v.|缓慢移动，一点一点地移动|to move very slowly and carefully in a particular direction|The snail inched along the leaf.  蜗牛沿着叶子缓慢爬行。|例句：The traffic inched forward.  车辆缓慢地向前移动。|近义词：crept, crawled, edged|反义词: sped, raced|词性:动词
