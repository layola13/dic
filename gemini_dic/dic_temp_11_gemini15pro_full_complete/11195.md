architected:/ˈɑːrkɪˌtɛktɪd/
Verb|设计；架构；策划|To design and build something, especially a computer system or a piece of software.|The system was architected to be highly scalable.|近义词：designed, engineered, constructed, planned, devised|反义词: dismantled, destroyed
词性: 动词
