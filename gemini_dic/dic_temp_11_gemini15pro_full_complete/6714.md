analogism:/əˈnælədʒɪzəm/ | n. |类比；类推；比拟|A comparison between two things, typically for the purpose of explanation or clarification.  A similarity or comparison between two different things or the relationship between them. |He drew an analogism between the human heart and a pump. 他将人的心脏比作泵。|Synonyms: analogy, comparison, simile, metaphor |Antonyms: difference, dissimilarity |noun
