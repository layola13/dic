outrightest:/ˌaʊtˈraɪtɪst/ | adj. | 最彻底的，最完全的 |  Superlative form of outright; most complete, most absolute.  This word is not commonly used and may sound awkward.  "Outright" is generally preferred. | He made the outrightest denial I've ever heard. 他做出了我听过的最彻底的否认。 |  Example: She won the election by the outrightest margin in history. 她以历史上最彻底的优势赢得了选举。 | Near Synonyms:  complete, total, absolute, utter, thoroughgoing | Antonyms: partial, incomplete, qualified | adjective
