inadequate: /ɪnˈædɪkwət/ | adj. | 不充分的，不适当的 | Not enough; not good enough for a particular purpose. |  The school’s resources are inadequate for the number of students. 学校的资源不足以满足学生的数量。

The heating system is inadequate for such a large house.  供暖系统不足以应付这么大的房子。

| 近义词: insufficient, deficient, lacking, unsatisfactory | 反义词: adequate, sufficient, enough, competent | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inadequatus, from in- ‘not’ + adequatus (see adequate).
