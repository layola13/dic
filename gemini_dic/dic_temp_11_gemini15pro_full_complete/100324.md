intersperse:/ˌɪntərˈspɜːrs/ (US), /ˌɪntəˈspɜːs/ (UK)| vt.|散布，点缀，穿插|To distribute something among other things at intervals.|He interspersed his speech with jokes. 他在演讲中穿插了笑话.|近义词：scatter, sprinkle, distribute, mix, insert|反义词：concentrate, gather, collect|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin interspersus, past participle of interspargere (“to scatter among, sprinkle”), from inter (“between, among”) + spargere (“to scatter, strew”).
