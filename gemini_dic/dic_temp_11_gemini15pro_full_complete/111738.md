locus:/ˈloʊkəs/ (US), /ˈləʊkəs/ (UK)| n. |地点；场所；轨迹|A particular position, point, or place.  A central point of activity, attention, or concentration. The position of a gene or mutation on a chromosome.|The earthquake's locus was under the ocean. (地震的中心在海底。)|synonyms: location, position, site, spot, place, point, center, hub, focus|antonyms: periphery, edge, boundary|noun


locus:/ˈloʊkəs/ (US), /ˈləʊkəs/ (UK)| n. |（几何）轨迹|A curve or other figure formed by all the points satisfying a particular equation of the relation between coordinates, or by a point, line, or surface moving according to mathematically defined conditions.|The locus of all points equidistant from a given point is a circle. (所有到一个给定点等距的点的轨迹是一个圆。)|synonyms: path, trajectory, orbit|antonyms: N/A|noun
