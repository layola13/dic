irrecordable:/ˌɪrɪˈkɔːrdəbl/
adjective
不可记录的，无法记录的
Not able to be recorded, either because of technical limitations or because it is too fleeting or intangible.
The meeting was so chaotic, the minutes were practically irrecordable.  会议太混乱了，会议记录实际上无法记录。
Her fleeting expression of joy was irrecordable. 她转瞬即逝的喜悦表情无法记录下来。
Synonyms: unrecordable, undocumented, intangible
Antonyms: recordable, documented, tangible
词性: 形容词
