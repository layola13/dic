coalfields:/ˈkoʊlˌfiːldz/
noun
煤田
Areas where coal is found and mined.
The decline of the coalfields has had a devastating impact on many communities. 煤田的衰落对许多社区造成了毁灭性的影响。
The government is investing in renewable energy sources to transition away from reliance on coalfields. 政府正在投资可再生能源，以摆脱对煤田的依赖。
synonyms: coal mines, coal basins
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: renewable energy sources, oil fields, gas fields)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "coal" + "field". "Coal" comes from Old English "col", meaning "burning piece, ember, charcoal," from Proto-Germanic "*kula-" (source also of Old Frisian "kole," Middle Dutch "cole," Dutch "kool," Old High German "chol," German "Kohle"). "Field" comes from Old English "feld" meaning "open country, open land," from Proto-Germanic "*felthaz" (source also of Old Saxon "feld," Old Frisian "feld," Dutch "veld," Old High German "feld," German "Feld," Old Norse "vollr," Gothic "filþa").
