ichthyophagist:/ɪkθiˈɒfədʒɪst/
n.
食鱼者，鱼食者
A person or animal that eats primarily fish.
例句：The traditional diet of the islanders made them ichthyophagists. 岛民的传统饮食使他们成为食鱼者。

例句：The otter is a skilled ichthyophagist, catching fish with ease. 水獭是熟练的食鱼者，能轻松地捕捉鱼。
近义词：piscivore
反义词: herbivore, carnivore (broader sense)
词性: noun
