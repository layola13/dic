disavowing:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊɪŋ/
|v.|否认，拒绝承认， repudiate|The government disavowed any knowledge of the secret talks. 政府否认对秘密会谈有任何了解。|
|v.|与...断绝关系|She disavowed her former friends after she became famous. 她成名后与以前的朋友断绝了关系。|
|Synonym:|deny, repudiate, renounce, reject|
|Antonym:|acknowledge, admit, accept, claim|
|verb|
