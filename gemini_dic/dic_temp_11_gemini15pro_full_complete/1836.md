acrodont:/ˈækrəˌdɑːnt/
adjective
端生齿的
(of a tooth) attached to the summit of the alveolar ridge without sockets.
Acrodont teeth are characteristic of most lizards.  端生齿是大多数蜥蜴的特征。

acrodont:/ˈækrəˌdɑːnt/
noun
端生齿动物
An animal with acrodont teeth.
Many lizards are acrodonts. 很多蜥蜴是端生齿动物。
