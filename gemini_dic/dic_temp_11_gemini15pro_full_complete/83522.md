haddocks:/ˈhædəks/ | n. | 黑线鳕鱼 | A food fish (Melanogrammus aeglefinus) related to and resembling the cod, but smaller and having a black lateral line. | We had haddocks for dinner last night. 我们昨晚晚餐吃了黑线鳕鱼。| 近义词: cod, whiting | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他种类的鱼) | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Middle English *haddok, from Old Norse *haddakr (compare Icelandic haddockr, Faroese hýsa), of uncertain origin.
