ameliorative:/əˈmiːliəˌreɪtɪv/
adjective
改善的，改良的
Having the effect of making something better; improving.
The government is taking ameliorative measures to improve the economy. 政府正在采取改善措施来改善经济。
The new law had an ameliorative effect on working conditions. 新法律对工作条件起到了改善作用。

近义词:improving, enhancing, beneficial, remedial, corrective
反义词:worsening, deteriorating, harmful, detrimental

词性:adjective

词源:Late 18th century: from French *amélioratif, -ive, from améliorer ‘to improve’, from meilleur ‘better’ (from Latin melior).
