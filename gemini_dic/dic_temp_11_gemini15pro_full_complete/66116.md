excellentest:/ˈɛksələntɪst/
adjective
最优秀的，极好的 (nonstandard, humorous)
A humorous and nonstandard superlative form of "excellent," used for emphasis and often perceived as incorrect.  It implies an extreme degree of excellence, surpassing even "most excellent."
例句：That's the excellentest cake I've ever tasted! 这是我吃过最最美味的蛋糕了！

例句：He's the excellentest footballer in the whole school. 他是全校最棒的足球运动员。
近义词：best, greatest, superb, outstanding, magnificent
反义词：worst, terrible, awful, dreadful, appalling
词性: 形容词


词源:
From excellent + -est (superlative suffix). While "-est" is a standard superlative suffix, adding it to "excellent" is considered non-standard and humorous due to "excellent" already having an implied superlative meaning.
