equisonance:/ˈɛkwɪsənəns/ /ˈiːkwɪsənəns/| n. |均音，谐音，同音|A similarity of sounds, especially vowels; the repetition of vowel sounds, especially in accented syllables, as in a line of verse.|The poet used equisonance to create a sense of flowing rhythm. 诗人用谐音来营造一种流畅的节奏感。|Near synonyms: assonance, consonance, resonance|Near antonyms: dissonance, cacophony|noun
