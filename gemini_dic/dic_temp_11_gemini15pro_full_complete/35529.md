coagulation:/koʊˌæɡjʊˈleɪʃən/ /kəʊˌæɡjʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|凝固，凝结|The coagulation of blood is essential to stop bleeding. 血液的凝结对于止血至关重要。|The doctor prescribed medication to prevent blood coagulation. 医生开了药来防止血液凝固。|近义词：clotting, thickening|反义词:thinning, liquefaction|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *coagulātiō*, from Latin coagulātus, past participle of coagulāre (“to curdle, congeal”), from co- (“together”) + agere (“to drive, do”).
