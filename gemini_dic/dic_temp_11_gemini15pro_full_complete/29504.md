catarrhine:/ˈkætəˌraɪn/| adj. n.|狭鼻类的，狭鼻类动物|Of or relating to primates of a group that comprises the Old World monkeys, apes, and humans.  Characterized by nostrils that are close together and directed downwards.|Baboons are catarrhine primates. 狒狒是狭鼻类灵长类动物。|The catarrhine nose is a distinguishing feature. 狭鼻是区别性特征。|近义词：Old World monkey, ape|反义词：platyrrhine|词性: adjective, noun
