demosthenes:/dɪˈmɒsθəniːz/
n.
德摩斯梯尼
Ancient Greek statesman and orator.
Demosthenes is considered one of the greatest orators of ancient Greece. 德摩斯梯尼被认为是古希腊最伟大的演说家之一。
