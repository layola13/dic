amateurism:/ˈæmətərɪzəm/ /ˈæmətʃərɪzəm/| n.|业余主义，业余爱好|The practice of engaging in an activity, especially a sport, on an unpaid basis, rather than as a professional.  The state or quality of being an amateur.|He took up painting as an amateurism. 他把绘画作为一种业余爱好。|近义词：dilettantism, nonprofessionalism|反义词：professionalism|词性:noun
