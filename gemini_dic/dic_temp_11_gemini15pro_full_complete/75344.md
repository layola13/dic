frostlike:/ˈfrɒstlaɪk/ (US) /ˈfrɔːstlaɪk/ (UK)| adj. |像霜的；冰霜似的|Resembling frost in appearance or characteristics; frosty.  Covered with or as if with frost.|The windows were covered in a frostlike pattern. 窗户上覆盖着一层像霜一样的图案。|The car's windshield had a frostlike film on it. 汽车的挡风玻璃上有一层像霜一样的薄膜。|近义词：frosty, icy, glacial|反义词:thawed, warm, heated|词性:形容词
词源: From frost + -like.  "frost" comes from Old English "forst" meaning "frost, freezing cold". "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling, similar to".
