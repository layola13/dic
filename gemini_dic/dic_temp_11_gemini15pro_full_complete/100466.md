interurban:/ˌɪntərˈɜːrbən/| adj. n.|城际的；城际交通工具；|Existing or traveling between cities or towns, especially by electric railway.|The interurban railway connected the two neighboring cities.|近义词：intercity, between cities|反义词:intraurban, within a city|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology: From inter- (between) + urban (city).
