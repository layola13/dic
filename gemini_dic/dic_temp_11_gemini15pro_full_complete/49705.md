demobilize:/diːˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ /diːˈməʊbəlaɪz/
verb
遣散；使复员；使退伍
To release (military personnel) from active duty.  To disband (troops, an army, etc.).  To change from a state of war or mobilization to a peacetime footing.
The government demobilized the troops after the war.  战争结束后，政府遣散了军队。

The army was demobilized after the armistice was signed.  停战协议签署后，军队复员了。

The factory was demobilized and converted to peacetime production.  这家工厂停止了军事生产，转为和平时期的生产。

近义词: discharge, dismiss, muster out, release
反义词: mobilize, enlist, recruit, call up
词性: verb
