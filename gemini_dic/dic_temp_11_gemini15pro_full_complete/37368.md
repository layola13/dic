commensurate:/kəˈmɛnʃərɪt/ /kəˈmɛnʃərət/
adjective
相称的，相当的
Corresponding in size or degree; in proportion.
Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。
The punishment should be commensurate with the crime.  惩罚应该与罪行相称。
近义词: proportionate, equivalent, comparable, corresponding, appropriate
反义词: disproportionate, inadequate, insufficient
词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin commensuratus ‘measured together’, from com- ‘together’ + mensuratus (past participle of mensurare ‘to measure’).
