carbonator:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪtər/ | n. | 碳酸化器，汽水机 | A device that adds carbon dioxide to beverages to make them fizzy. |  例句：He bought a soda carbonator to make sparkling water at home.  他买了一台苏打碳酸化器，在家自制苏打水。

例句：The carbonator wasn't working properly, so the soda was flat.  碳酸化器坏了，所以苏打水没气。

近义词：soda maker, seltzer maker | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "decarbonator" though not commonly used) | 词性: noun
