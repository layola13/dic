grewsome:/ˈɡruːsəm/ (US), /ˈɡruːsəm/ (UK)| adj.|可怕的，令人毛骨悚然的|Causing repulsion or horror; grisly.|例句：The scene of the accident was gruesome. 事故现场惨不忍睹。

例句：She told us a gruesome tale of murder and mayhem. 她给我们讲了一个关于谋杀和混乱的可怕故事。|近义词：horrifying, grisly, macabre, ghastly, appalling|反义词：pleasant, delightful, agreeable|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grewsom*, from Old English *grēwsam* (“horrible, terrible, cruel, fierce”), equivalent to *grēow* + *-sam*. Cognate with Old Frisian *griwsam* (“horrible”), Dutch *gruwzaam* (“horrible, terrible, atrocious, cruel”), German *grausam* (“cruel, terrible”). More at *grue*.
