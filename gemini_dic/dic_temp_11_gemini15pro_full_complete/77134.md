gasconade:/ˌɡæs.kəˈneɪd/ | vi.|夸口；吹嘘；|To boast or brag.|He gasconaded about his wealth and connections. 他吹嘘自己的财富和人脉。|Synonyms: boast, brag, bluster, swagger |Antonyms: be modest, downplay|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gasconnade*, from *Gascon*, inhabitant of Gascony, a region in southwestern France known for its boastful inhabitants.
