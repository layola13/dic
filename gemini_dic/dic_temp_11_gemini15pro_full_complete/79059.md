givers:/ˈɡɪvərz/| n.|给予者；赠送者|People who give gifts or contribute to others are called givers.  给予礼物或为他人做出贡献的人被称为给予者。|例句：The givers were thanked for their generosity. 赠送者因其慷慨而受到感谢。

|近义词：donors, contributors, benefactors|反义词：takers, recipients|词性:noun
