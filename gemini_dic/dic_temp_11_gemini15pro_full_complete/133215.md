nonexcepted:/ˌnɑːnɪkˈsɛptɪd/
adjective
未豁免的，非例外的
Not excepted; not excluded from a general rule or liability.
Employees in nonexcepted positions are covered by the Fair Labor Standards Act.  未豁免职位的员工受《公平劳动标准法》的保护。
The nonexcepted employees are entitled to overtime pay.  非豁免员工有权获得加班费。
近义词: non-exempt, included
反义词: excepted, exempt
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non.
excepted (past participle of except): meaning "excluded"  来自拉丁语 excipere,  ex- "out" + capere "to take."
