heartstrings:/ˈhɑːrtstrɪŋz/
noun
心弦
The deepest emotions or feelings of compassion.  Figuratively, the strings of a heart, as if the heart were a musical instrument whose sound represented the emotions.
The movie really tugged at my heartstrings. 这部电影真的触动了我的心弦。
近义词:emotions, feelings
反义词:apathy, indifference
词性:n.
