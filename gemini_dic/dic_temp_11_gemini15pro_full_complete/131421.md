nonclimactic:/ˌnɒnklaɪˈmæktɪk/
adjective
非高潮的，平淡的
lacking a climax; disappointing or anticlimactic.
The ending of the movie was surprisingly nonclimactic. 这部电影的结局出人意料地平淡。
Synonyms: anticlimactic, disappointing, flat, uneventful, underwhelming
Antonyms: climactic, exciting, thrilling, dramatic, intense
词性: adjective
