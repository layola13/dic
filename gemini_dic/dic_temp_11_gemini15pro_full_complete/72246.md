florification:/ˌflɔːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 开花；繁荣；兴盛 | The process of flowering or blossoming; a period of flourishing or thriving.  The florification of the arts occurred during the Renaissance.  文艺复兴时期艺术蓬勃发展。| 例句：The florification of the cherry blossoms in spring is a breathtaking sight. 春天樱花盛开是一幅令人叹为观止的景象。

例句：The city experienced a florification of trade in the 18th century.  这座城市在18世纪经历了贸易的繁荣。| 近义词：blooming, blossoming, flourishing, thriving | 反义词：decline, decay, withering | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *florificatio, from Latin florificare "to make flowers," from flor- "flower" + -ificare "-ify."
