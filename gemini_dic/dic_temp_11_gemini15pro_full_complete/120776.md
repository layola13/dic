misappreciation:/ˌmɪsəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/
n.
误解，低估，错误评价
An act of misappreciating something or someone; a failure to value or understand something or someone correctly.
Her misappreciation of his artistic talent made him leave the company. 她对他艺术才能的低估使他离开了公司。

His misappreciation of the situation led to further problems. 他对形势的误判导致了进一步的问题。

近义词: misunderstanding, undervaluation, misinterpretation, misjudgment
反义词: appreciation, understanding, recognition, valuation
词性: 名词
