gentries:/ˈdʒɛntriz/
n.
乡绅；贵族阶级
The landed gentry held considerable power in the 18th century.
18世纪，拥有土地的乡绅阶级掌握着相当大的权力。
people of high social standing or gentle birth, especially in the past
The decline of the landed gentries.
没落的地主阶级。
例句：The local gentry held a grand ball at the manor house. 当地的绅士们在庄园里举行了一场盛大的舞会。

近义词：aristocracy, nobility, elite
反义词：commoners, peasantry

词性: noun (plural)

词源:  From Old French *gentrie (modern French gentry "noble birth, nobility"), from gentil "noble, of gentle birth" (see gentle).
