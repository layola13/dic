dissenterism: /dɪˈsɛntərɪzəm/
n.
异议，持不同意见，不墨守成规
The act of dissenting; disagreement with the prevailing views or beliefs, especially in religious matters.
Example 1:  Dissenterism was common in England during the 17th century, leading to the formation of various nonconformist groups.  17世纪的英格兰，不墨守成规的现象很普遍，导致了各种不墨守成规的群体的形成。

Example 2:  The government cracked down on any form of political dissenterism. 政府镇压任何形式的政治异议。
近义词: nonconformity, heresy, heterodoxy, disagreement, dissent
反义词: conformity, orthodoxy, agreement, assent

ETYMOLOGY->
From dissent + -er + -ism.  Dissent ultimately comes from the Latin dissentire, meaning "to disagree."
