lobsterish:/ˈlɒbstərɪʃ/ (US) /ˈlɒbstərɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|像龙虾的；龙虾似的|Resembling or characteristic of a lobster, especially in color, form, or movement.|例句：The child's sunburn made his skin look lobsterish.  孩子的晒伤使他的皮肤看起来像龙虾一样红。
