anarch:/ˈænɑːrk/ | n. | 无政府主义者 | A person who believes in or tries to bring about anarchy. |  He was a known anarch, advocating for the abolition of all forms of government.  他是一个著名的无政府主义者，主张废除一切形式的政府。 | Synonyms: rebel, insurgent, revolutionary | Antonyms: conformist, traditionalist | Noun


anarchy:/ˈænərki/ | n. | 无政府状态; 混乱; 无秩序 | A state of disorder due to absence or nonrecognition of authority. | The country descended into anarchy after the coup. 政变后，这个国家陷入了无政府状态。| Synonyms: chaos, disorder, lawlessness | Antonyms: order, control, government | Noun
