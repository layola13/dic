contextualizes:/kənˈtekstʃʊəˌlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 置于语境中， contextualize的第三人称单数形式 | To place (something, such as a word or activity) in a context. | The author contextualizes the events of the novel by describing the political climate of the time. 作者通过描述当时的政治气候，将小说的事件置于特定的背景之下。 | synonyms: place in context, situate, frame, explicate, elucidate | antonyms: decontextualize, abstract, isolate | verb
