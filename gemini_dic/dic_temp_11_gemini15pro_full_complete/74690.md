freeform:/ˈfriːfɔːrm/| adj. n.|自由形式的，自由格式；自由形式，自由格式|Not having or following a regular or standard form, structure, or style. |The dancers enjoyed the freeform nature of the improvisation class. 舞者们享受着即兴表演课的自由形式。|近义词：unstructured, informal, flexible, open-ended|反义词：structured, formal, rigid, fixed|词性:形容词, 名词
freeform:/ˈfriːfɔːrm/| n.|自由形式，自由格式|A style or format that is not restricted by traditional rules or conventions.|The artist preferred working in freeform, letting his creativity guide him.  这位艺术家更喜欢以自由形式创作，让他的创造力引导他。|近义词：improvisation, ad-lib, extemporization|反义词：structure, framework, formula|词性:名词

Etymology->
From free + form.  "Free" meaning unrestricted and "form" referring to shape or structure.  The combined meaning reflects something without a predetermined or fixed structure.
