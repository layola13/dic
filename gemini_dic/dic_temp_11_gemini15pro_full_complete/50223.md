depectible:/dɪˈpektɪbəl/
adj.
可描绘的; 可描述的;
Capable of being depicted or described.
The scenery was so beautiful, it was barely depictible in words.  这景色太美了，几乎无法用言语来形容。
depictable
paintable, portrayable, describable, representable
undepictable, indescribable
adjective
