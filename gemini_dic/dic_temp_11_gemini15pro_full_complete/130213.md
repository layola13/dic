non-semitic:/nɒn-səˈmɪtɪk/
adjective
非閃族的
Not belonging to the group of Semitic languages (such as Arabic, Hebrew, and Aramaic) or peoples.
The Sumerian language is non-Semitic.  苏美尔语是非闪族语系的。
The ancient Egyptians spoke a non-Semitic language. 古埃及人说一种非闪族语系的语言。
近义词: non-Afro-Asiatic
反义词: Semitic
