boatsman:/ˈboʊtsmən/| n.|船夫；水手|A person who operates or manages a boat, especially a small one. |例句：The boatsman ferried us across the river. 船夫把我们渡过了河。

例句：The experienced boatsman navigated the treacherous waters with ease.  经验丰富的船夫轻松地驾驭着危险的水域。|近义词：boatman, ferryman, oarsman, sailor |反义词: passenger|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bātsmann, from bāt (“boat”) + mann (“man”). Cognate with Dutch bootsman, German Bootsmann, Danish bådsmand.
