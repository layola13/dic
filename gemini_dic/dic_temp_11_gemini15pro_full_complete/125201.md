multicipital:/ˌmʌltɪˈsɪpɪtəl/
adjective
多头的，多首的
Having many heads or appearing to have many heads.
The multicipital hydra in Greek mythology was a fearsome beast.  希腊神话中的多头九头蛇是一个可怕的野兽。
The artist's multicipital sculpture was both fascinating and unsettling.  这位艺术家的多头雕塑既迷人又令人不安。
近义词: many-headed, polycephalous
反义词: single-headed, monocephalous
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *multiceps, from multi- "many" + caput "head".
