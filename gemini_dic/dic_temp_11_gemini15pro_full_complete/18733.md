bergmeal:/ˈbɜːrɡmiːl/ | n. | 岩粉，硅藻土 | A white or cream-colored powder consisting chiefly of the fossilized remains of diatoms, used especially as an absorbent, abrasive, and filtering medium. |  Diatomaceous earth, also known as bergmeal, is a versatile substance with various applications.  硅藻土，也被称为岩粉，是一种用途广泛的物质。

Bergmeal is often used in gardening to control pests. 岩粉常用于园艺中以控制害虫。 | 近义词：diatomaceous earth, kieselguhr | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
