epipteric:/ˌepɪˈtɛrɪk/| adj.|翼膜的，翼状的|Relating to or denoting a small cartilaginous or bony structure, typically one of a pair, overlying the pterotic bone in the auditory region of the skull of many bony fishes.|The epipteric bone is a key feature in fish taxonomy.  翼蝶骨是鱼类分类学中的一个关键特征。|Synonym: pteroic|Antonym: None|adjective

epipteric:/ˌepɪˈtɛrɪk/| n.|翼状骨|A small cartilaginous or bony structure, typically one of a pair, overlying the pterotic bone in the auditory region of the skull of many bony fishes.|The researchers examined the epipteric in several fish specimens. 研究人员检查了几个鱼类标本的翼状骨。|Synonym: None|Antonym: None|noun
