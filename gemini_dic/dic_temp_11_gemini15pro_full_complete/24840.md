brownest:/ˈbraʊnɪst/| adj.|最棕色的|The superlative form of brown, meaning having the deepest or most intense brown color.|例句：The brownies she baked were the brownest I'd ever seen. 她烤的布朗尼是我见过的颜色最深的。

例句：Of all the bears, the grizzly had the brownest fur. 在所有的熊中，灰熊的皮毛颜色最深。|近义词：darkest, deepest (in color)|反义词：lightest, palest|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *brūn, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bruin and German braun. The -est suffix is used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
