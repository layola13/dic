bedtick:/ˈbedtɪk/| n.|床虱，臭虫|A small, wingless, bloodsucking insect that infests beds and feeds on human blood. | I woke up covered in itchy bedtick bites. 我醒来时身上满是发痒的臭虫叮咬。|Synonyms: bedbug, chinch|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


Etymology:
From "bed" + "tick,"  where "tick" refers to a parasitic arachnid.  The word "tick" in this context likely arose from its resemblance to the parasitic ticks that infest animals.
