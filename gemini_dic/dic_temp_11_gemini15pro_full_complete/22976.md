botuliform:/ˈbɒtʃʊlɪfɔːm/ /ˈbɑːtʃʊlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 肉毒杆菌状的；腊肠状的 | Resembling a sausage; alluding to the appearance of botulism poisoning which can cause sausage-like swelling. | The doctor noted the botuliform swelling on the patient's arm. 医生注意到病人手臂上腊肠状的肿胀。 |  The infected area had a botuliform appearance. 感染区域呈现出腊肠状的外观。 | synonymous: sausage-shaped, tubular | antonymous: flat, smooth | adjective
