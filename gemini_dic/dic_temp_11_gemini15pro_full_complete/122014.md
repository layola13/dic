misstated:/ˌmɪsˈsteɪtɪd/
adjective
错误陈述的，不准确的
incorrectly stated; wrongly described
The financial report misstated the company's profits.  财务报告错误地陈述了公司的利润。
The witness misstated the facts of the case.  证人错误陈述了案件的事实。
近义词: misrepresented, distorted, falsified
反义词: accurately stated, correctly represented

词性: adjective

词源:  From mis- + stated, past participle of state.  "Mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly."
