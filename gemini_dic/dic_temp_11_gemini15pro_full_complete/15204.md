backcountry:/ˈbækˌkʌntri/ | n. | 荒野；偏远地区 |  A rural or mountainous region far from cities or roads. |  We went hiking in the backcountry of Montana. 我们去蒙大拿州的荒野远足。 | wilderness, outback, hinterland | urban, city | noun

backcountry:/ˈbækˌkʌntri/ | adj. | 偏远的；荒野的 |  Located in or relating to a remote, undeveloped area. |  They live a backcountry lifestyle. 他们过着偏远地区的生活方式。 | remote, rural, isolated | urban, metropolitan | adjective
