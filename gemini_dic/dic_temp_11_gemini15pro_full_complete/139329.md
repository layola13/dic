obolo:/ˈɒbələʊ/ (UK), /ˈoʊbəloʊ/ (US)| n. | 小额钱币，微薄的捐款 | A small coin or sum of money, especially one given as an offering or contribution. |  He offered an obolo to the beggar. 他给了乞丐一小笔钱。| synonyms: pittance, mite, trifle, modicum | antonyms: fortune, wealth | noun
