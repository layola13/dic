enthrone:/ɪnˈθroʊn/ | vt. | 使登基；加冕；推崇；尊崇 | To place (someone, especially a monarch) on a throne, or in a position of authority or dignity.  | The new king was enthroned in a lavish ceremony. 新国王在一个盛大的仪式上登基。 | The team enthroned their new captain after their victory.  球队在获胜后拥立了他们的新队长。 |  近义词: crown, invest, install | 反义词: dethrone, depose | 词性: 动词
