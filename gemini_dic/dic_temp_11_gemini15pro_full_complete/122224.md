miswires:/ˌmɪsˈwaɪərz/
verb
错误连接，接错线
To connect wires incorrectly, leading to malfunction.
The electrician miswired the circuit, causing a short circuit. 电工把电路接错了，导致短路。
The control panel was miswired, so the machine wouldn't start. 控制面板接线错误，所以机器无法启动。
misconnect, misarrange
connect correctly, wire correctly
v.
