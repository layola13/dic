outdebating:/ˌaʊtdɪˈbeɪtɪŋ/
verb
胜过辩论; 在辩论中击败
defeating someone in a debate
He outdebated his opponent with his well-reasoned arguments. 他以充分的论据在辩论中击败了对手。
Synonyms: outarguing, besting, defeating
Antonyms: losing to, being defeated by
v.
