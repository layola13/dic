mismetre:/mɪsˈmiːtər/ | n. | 格律错误；韵律错误；诗律错误 | A line of verse having a faulty or irregular metre. |  The poem was marred by a glaring mismetre in the third stanza. 这首诗的第三节中有一个明显的格律错误。| Synonyms:  metrical fault, rhythmic flaw, prosodic error | Antonyms:  perfect metre, correct rhythm | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + metre.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly." "Metre" comes from the Greek "metron," meaning "measure."
