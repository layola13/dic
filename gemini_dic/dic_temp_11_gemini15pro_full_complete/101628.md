irredeemable:/ˌɪrɪˈdiːməbl/ | adj. | 不可救药的，无法赎回的，不能弥补的 | Not able to be saved, improved, or corrected |  The damage to their relationship was irredeemable. 他们的关系受到了无法弥补的损害。 |  His reputation was irredeemable after the scandal.  丑闻之后，他的名声无法挽回。 | Synonyms: hopeless, incurable, irrecoverable, irreparable | Antonyms: redeemable, retrievable, salvageable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + redeem (to buy back, recover) + -able (capable of being)
