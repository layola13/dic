bewilder:/bɪˈwɪldər/| vt.|使迷惑，使不知所措|To confuse or puzzle someone deeply; to cause to lose one's bearings. |The complex instructions completely bewildered her. 复杂的指令让她完全不知所措。|Synonyms: confuse, puzzle, perplex, disorient, confound|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, orient|Verb
