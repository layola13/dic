nonsentient:/nɒnˈsɛntɪənt/ /nɑːnˈsɛntiənt/| adj.|无知觉的，无感觉的|Not having the power of sensation or feeling; inanimate.|例句：Rocks and minerals are nonsentient objects. 岩石和矿物质是无知觉的物体。|近义词：inanimate, insensate, unconscious|反义词：sentient, conscious, aware|词性:形容词

nonsentient:|/nɒnˈsɛntɪənt/ /nɑːnˈsɛntiənt/| n.|无知觉的事物|Something that is not sentient; an inanimate object.|例句：The robot, though complex, was still a nonsentient being.  尽管很复杂，这个机器人仍然是一个无知觉的存在。|近义词：inanimate object|反义词：sentient being|词性:名词
