expressionistic:/ɪkˌsprɛʃəˈnɪstɪk/
| adj. |表现主义的|Relating to or characteristic of expressionism, a style of art, literature, or music that seeks to express the artist's inner feelings or ideas rather than accurately depict objective reality.|The painting was done in an expressionistic style, using bold colors and distorted figures to convey the artist's anguish.|近义词：intense, passionate, dramatic, evocative|反义词：realistic, objective, naturalistic|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Expressionismus*, derived from *Expression* (expression) + *-ismus* (a suffix forming nouns denoting systems, doctrines, or practices). Ultimately from Latin *expressio* (expression).
