binominous:/baɪˈnɒmɪnəs/ | adj. | 双名的，二名的 | Having two names, especially a scientific name with two parts (genus and species). |  A rose is a binominous flower, designated as *Rosa* followed by the species. 玫瑰是一种双名花卉，命名为*Rosa*，后接物种名称。

| Near synonyms: binomial, two-named | Antonyms: polynomial, multinomial | adjective
