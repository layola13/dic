hived:/haɪvd/
verb|使...入蜂房，储藏|To have placed or stored in a hive, or a place resembling a hive; to reside collectively, like bees.|The beekeeper hived the swarm. 养蜂人把蜂群放入了蜂箱。|Near synonyms: housed, stored, sheltered|Near antonyms: dispersed, scattered
adjective|蜂巢状的，群居的|Resembling a beehive; living collectively like bees.|They lived in a hived community. 他们生活在一个蜂巢状的社区里。|Near synonyms: clustered, grouped, concentrated|Near antonyms: dispersed, scattered
noun|蜂房,蜂群|A place where bees live, a beehive; a densely populated place.|The hived buzzed with activity. 蜂房里嗡嗡作响，一片繁忙。|Near synonyms: beehive, apiary, colony|Near antonyms: emptiness, void

词性:
verb, adjective, noun

词源:
From hive + -ed.  Ultimately from Old English *hīfe.
