nonluminescent:/ˌnɒnlʊːmɪˈnɛsənt/
adjective
不发光的
Not emitting light, especially not emitting light by processes such as phosphorescence or fluorescence.
The coating is nonluminescent and thus does not interfere with night vision.  这种涂层不发光，因此不会干扰夜视。
The bacteria were genetically modified to be nonluminescent.  这些细菌经过基因改造后不发光。
non-radiative, non-illuminating, dark, dull, matte
luminescent, bright, shining, glowing
adjective
