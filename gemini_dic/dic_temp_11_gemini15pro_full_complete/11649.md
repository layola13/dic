armiger:/ˈɑːrmɪdʒər/| n.|盾徽拥有者；纹章持有者；有资格佩戴盾形纹章的人|A person entitled to bear heraldic arms. |例句：He was recognized as an armiger by the College of Arms. 他被纹章院承认为盾徽拥有者。

例句：The armiger displayed his coat of arms proudly on his shield.  这位盾徽拥有者自豪地在他的盾牌上展示他的盾形纹章。|近义词：esquire, nobleman|反义词: commoner|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *armiger, from Old French armiger, from Latin armiger "armor-bearer," from arma "arms" + -ger "bearing."
