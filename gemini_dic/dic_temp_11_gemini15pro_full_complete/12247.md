ascendants:/əˈsɛndənts/| n.|祖先，祖宗；上升星座|People trace their ascendants back hundreds of years. 人们追溯他们的祖先到几百年前。|An ascendant is a person from whom you are descended. 祖先是指你传承下来的一个人。|例句：He could trace his ascendants back to the 16th century. 他可以追溯他的祖先到16世纪。

例句：Her ascendant in astrology is in Gemini. 她的上升星座是双子座。|近义词：ancestors, forebears, progenitors|反义词:descendants|词性:名词
