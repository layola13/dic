interpolation:/ɪntɜːpəˈleɪʃən/ /ɪnˌtɝːpəˈleɪʃən/| n.|插入；插值；内插法|The act of inserting something of a different nature into something else, especially the insertion of extra material into a text or film.  Also, a mathematical method of constructing new data points within the range of a discrete set of known data points.| The author's interpolations confused the meaning of the original text. 作者插入的内容混淆了原文的意思。|近义词：insertion, addition, interpretation|反义词:deletion, removal|词性:noun
