bellhop: /ˈbelˌhɑːp/ (US) /ˈbelhɒp/ (UK)| n. |行李员；门童|A hotel employee who carries luggage and performs other services for guests.|The bellhop carried our bags to our room. 行李员把我们的包搬到了房间。|Example:  The bellhop helped us with our luggage.  行李员帮我们搬运行李。

Example:  We tipped the bellhop generously. 我们给了门童丰厚的小费。|Synonyms: porter, page, attendant|Antonyms: guest, customer|Noun
