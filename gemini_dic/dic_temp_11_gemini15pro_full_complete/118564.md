mesopterygium:/ˌmɛzoʊˈtɛriʤiəm/ | n. | 中翼; 中鳍骨 | The basal cartilage or bone of the central axis of a paired fin.  It's the skeletal element that supports the middle portion of a fin, particularly in fish. | The mesopterygium of the shark's pectoral fin is well-developed.  鲨鱼胸鳍的中鳍骨非常发达。| The mesopterygium provides structural support for the fin. 中鳍骨为鱼鳍提供结构支撑。 | synonyms:  basal pterygium, radial pterygium (depending on context) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym.  Perhaps "distal pterygium" as it refers to the opposite end of the fin structure.) | noun
