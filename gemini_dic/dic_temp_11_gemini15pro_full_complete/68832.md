fascisms:/ˈfæʃɪzəmz/
|n.|法西斯主义|A political philosophy, movement, or regime (such as that of the Fascisti) that exalts nation and often race above the individual and that stands for a centralized autocratic government headed by a dictatorial leader, severe economic and social regimentation, and forcible suppression of opposition.|The rise of fascisms in Europe during the 20th century was a dark period in history.|近义词:authoritarianism, totalitarianism, despotism|反义词:democracy, liberalism, socialism|词性:名词
