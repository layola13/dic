kulon:/ˈkuːlɒn/ | n. | 库仑 |A unit of electric charge equal to the quantity of electricity conveyed in one second by a current of one ampere.  It is the standard unit of charge in the International System of Units (SI).| The charge on a single electron is approximately 1.602 x 10^-19 coulombs. 一个电子的电荷大约是1.602 x 10^-19 库仑。|  The capacitor stores a charge of 10 coulombs. 电容器储存了10库仑的电荷。| Synonyms: unit of charge | Antonyms: N/A | noun
