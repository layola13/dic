inebriated:/ɪˈniːbriːeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 醉酒的 |(of a person) drunk; intoxicated.  |例句：He was so inebriated that he could barely stand. 他醉得几乎站不住了。|近义词：drunk, intoxicated, tipsy |反义词:sober |词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *inebriatus, past participle of inebriare (“to intoxicate”) < in- + ebrius (“drunk”).
