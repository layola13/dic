imminentnesses:/ˈɪmɪnəntnəsɪz/
n.
迫在眉睫；即将发生的事情（复数）
The state of being imminent; the quality of being about to occur.  The imminentnesses of several deadlines caused stress among the team.
Several imminentnesses hung over them, including the threat of eviction and the looming deadline.
近义词：approaching, nearing, impending, looming
反义词：distant, remote, far-off
词性：名词


Etymology:
From imminent + -ness.  Imminent from Latin imminēns, imminent- present participle of imminēre to overhang, from in- + minēre to project.
