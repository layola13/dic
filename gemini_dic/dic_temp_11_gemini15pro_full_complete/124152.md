mortisers:/ˈmɔːrtaɪzərz/
noun
榫眼机；打眼机
A machine tool used to cut mortises (rectangular holes) in wood, typically for joining pieces together with tenons.
The carpenter used a mortising machine to create precise mortises for the chair legs.  木匠用榫眼机为椅腿制作精确的榫眼。
The mortisers in the workshop were constantly in use.  车间里的榫眼机一直在使用中。
synonyms: mortising machine, hollow chisel mortiser, chain mortiser, benchtop mortiser
antonyms:  N/A (Tools don't typically have direct antonyms)
