lasting:/ˈlɑːstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈlɑːstɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|持久的，持续的|Continuing or remaining for a long time.|The medicine provides lasting relief from pain. 这种药能持久缓解疼痛。|近义词：enduring, permanent, durable, long-lasting|反义词：temporary, transient, fleeting, short-lived|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *lastyng*, present participle of *lasten* (“to last”), from Old English *læstan* (“to continue, perform, carry out, last, extend, follow, observe”), from Proto-Germanic *lastaną* (“to follow, continue”), from Proto-Indo-European *lendʰ- (“to go”).  Related to German *leisten* (“to achieve, perform”).
