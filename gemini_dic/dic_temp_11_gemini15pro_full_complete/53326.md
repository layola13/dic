disaccommodate:/ˌdɪsəˈkɑːmədeɪt/
verb
使不便; 拒绝提供住宿
To cause inconvenience or discomfort to; to refuse or be unable to provide lodging for.
The hotel was overbooked and had to disaccommodate several guests.  这家酒店预订过多，不得不拒绝为几位客人提供住宿。
The sudden change of plans disaccommodated everyone involved. 突然的计划改变给所有相关人员带来了不便。
近义词: inconvenience, disturb, upset, bother
反义词: accommodate, assist, help
词性: v.

Etymology:
From dis- (not, opposite of) + accommodate (to provide lodging for, to adapt).
