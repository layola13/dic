geostrategic:/ˌdʒiːoʊstrəˈtiːdʒɪk/
adjective
地缘战略的
Relating to the importance of a geographical area or location in terms of political or military strategy.
例句：The country's geostrategic location made it a key player in international relations.  这个国家的地缘战略位置使其成为国际关系中的关键角色。

例句：The construction of the new port is a geostrategic move to enhance the nation's naval power. 新港口的建设是加强国家海军力量的地缘战略举措。

近义词：geographical, geopolitical
反义词:  N/A
词性: adjective
