nonnitrogenous:/ˌnɒn naɪˈtrɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
非氮的；不含氮的
Not containing nitrogen.  Specifically, referring to organic compounds that do not contain nitrogen atoms.
Plants need both nitrogenous and nonnitrogenous nutrients to grow.  植物需要氮和非氮营养才能生长。
Synonyms: nitrogen-free
Antonyms: nitrogenous
词性: 形容词
