bulging:/ˈbʌldʒɪŋ/| adj.|膨胀的，凸出的|Swelling outwards; protruding.|例句：His pockets were bulging with candy. 他的口袋里鼓鼓囊囊地装着糖果。|例句：The bulging muscles in his arms showed his strength. 他胳膊上鼓起的肌肉显示出他的力量。|近义词：protruding, swelling, distended, prominent|反义词：concave, hollow, flat|词性：形容词
bulge:/bʌldʒ/| n. vi. vt.|凸起，膨胀；使膨胀|A rounded swelling or protuberance that distorts a flat or straight surface.|例句：There was a noticeable bulge in his suitcase. 他的手提箱里有一个明显的凸起。|例句：The sails bulged in the wind. 帆在风中鼓了起来。|近义词：protrusion, swelling, bump, lump|反义词：indentation, hollow, depression|词性：名词，动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bulgen, *bolgen, from Old Norse *bulgja (“to swell”), from Proto-Germanic *bulgijaną (“to swell”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰelǵʰ- (“to swell”). Cognate with Old English belgan (“to be angry, swell”).
