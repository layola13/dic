nondefiances:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪənsɪz/
n.
不服从(的行动);不反抗(的行为)
Lack of defiance; obedience or submission.
The nondefiance of the citizens surprised the dictator. 公民的顺从让独裁者感到惊讶。
近义词: obedience, submission, compliance
反义词: defiance, resistance, rebellion
词性: 名词 (plural)

nondefiance:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪəns/
n.
不服从;不反抗
Lack of defiance; obedience or submission.
His nondefiance was interpreted as weakness. 他的不反抗被解读为软弱。
近义词: obedience, submission, compliance
反义词: defiance, resistance, rebellion
词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + defiance (from Old French *defiance, from defier "to challenge, defy," from Latin diffidare "to distrust, despair of," from dis- "apart, away" + fidare "to trust," from fides "faith")
