octroi:/ˈɒktrwɑː/| n.|地方税; 入市税; 通行税|A tax or duty levied by a local government on goods brought into a town or city for sale.|The octroi on imported goods increased the cost of living in the city. 城市对进口商品征收的入市税增加了城市的生活成本。|例句：
The octroi levied on goods entering the city was substantial.  对进入该市的货物征收的入市税相当可观。

The merchants protested against the high octroi charges. 商人们抗议高额的入市税。

|近义词：duty, tariff, levy, tax|反义词:subsidy|词性:noun
