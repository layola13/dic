bioenergetics:/ˌbaɪoʊˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪks/
noun
生物能学
The study of energy transformations in living organisms.
Bioenergetics explains how organisms obtain, store, and use energy. 生物能学解释了生物体如何获取、储存和利用能量。
Research in bioenergetics is crucial for understanding cellular processes. 生物能学研究对于理解细胞过程至关重要。
近义词: bioenergetics (singular usually not used)
反义词: None
词性: noun
