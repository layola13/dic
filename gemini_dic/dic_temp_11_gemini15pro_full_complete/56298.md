doggoner:/ˈdɒɡə(r)nər/ (US), /ˈdɒɡənə(r)/ (UK)| interj. n.|该死的，讨厌的（感叹词）;  家伙，东西（名词）|A mild oath or exclamation expressing surprise, frustration, or emphasis.  Can also be used as a noun meaning "person" or "thing" in a general or slightly derogatory sense.|

该死的！我错过了公共汽车。
