mooring:/ˈmʊərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɔːrɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |系泊;  停泊处;  (常作 moorings) 系泊设备|A place where a boat or ship is moored.  The ropes, chains, anchors, etc., by which a boat or ship is moored. |The yacht was secured to its mooring.  游艇被固定在它的系泊处。|Synonyms: anchor, berth, dock, pier|Antonyms: unmoor, cast off|noun

mooring:/ˈmʊərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɔːrɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |(尤指船) 停泊，系泊|(especially of a boat or ship) to be moored or to moor something. |We moored the boat to the dock. 我们把船停泊在码头上。|Synonyms: anchor, berth, dock, tie up|Antonyms: unmoor, cast off|verb
