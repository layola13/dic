ductility:/dʌkˈtɪləti/
n.|延展性，韧性|The ductility of the metal allows it to be drawn into wires.  这种金属的延展性使它可以被拉成金属丝。|例句：The ductility of copper makes it ideal for wiring. 铜的延展性使它成为理想的电线材料。|近义词：malleability, flexibility, plasticity|反义词：brittleness, rigidity|词性：名词

ductile:/ˈdʌktaɪl/
adj.|可延展的，易延展的，韧性的|Copper is a ductile metal. 铜是一种延展性很好的金属。|例句：Gold is a highly ductile metal, which is why it can be made into very thin leaf. 金是一种延展性很高的金属，这就是为什么它可以被制成非常薄的金箔。|近义词：malleable, flexible, pliable|反义词：brittle, rigid, inflexible|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ductilis*, from *ductus* ("leading, drawing out"), past participle of *ducere* ("to lead").  源自拉丁语 *ductilis*，来自 *ductus*（“引导，拉出”），*ducere*（“引导”）的过去分词。
