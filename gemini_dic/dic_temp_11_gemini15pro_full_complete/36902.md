colorism: /ˈkʌlərɪzəm/ | n. | 肤色歧视；种族主义 | Prejudice or discrimination against individuals with a dark skin tone, typically among people of the same ethnic or racial group. |  Example: Colorism affects many communities of color. 肤色歧视影响着许多有色人种社区。

Example:  She faced colorism within her own family.  她在自己的家庭中面临肤色歧视。| 近义词: discrimination, prejudice, racism, bigotry | 反义词: equality, acceptance, tolerance | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From color + -ism.  First recorded in the late 1970s. Coined by Alice Walker.
