discernibly:/dɪˈsɜːrnəbli/
adverb
可辨别地，明显地
in a way that can be noticed or understood
The weather has discernibly improved. 天气明显好转了。
Synonyms: noticeably, perceptibly, clearly, distinctly, obviously
Antonyms: imperceptibly, subtly, faintly

ETYMOLOGY->
From discernible + -ly.  Discernible comes from the late Middle English verb "discernen," from Old French "discerner," and ultimately from Latin "discernere" (to separate, distinguish).  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "discernible" into an adverb.
