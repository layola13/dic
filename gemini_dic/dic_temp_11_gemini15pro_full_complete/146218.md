overexplain:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspleɪn/| v. |过度解释；解释得过于详细|To explain something in excessive detail, often to the point of confusion or boredom. |例句：I tried to explain the situation, but I think I overexplained it and just made things worse. 我试图解释情况，但我想我解释得太多了，反而把事情弄糟了。

例句：He tends to overexplain things, which can be a bit tedious. 他倾向于过度解释事情，这可能有点乏味。|近义词：belabor, elaborate excessively|反义词: understate, summarize|词性:动词
