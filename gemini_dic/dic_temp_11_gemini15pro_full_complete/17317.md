bdellovibrio:/dɛloʊˈvɪbrioʊ/
noun
蛭弧菌
A genus of small, highly motile, Gram-negative bacteria of the delta/epsilon subdivision of Proteobacteria that prey on other Gram-negative bacteria.
Bdellovibrio bacteriovorus is a predatory bacterium that attacks and enters the periplasmic space of other Gram-negative bacteria.
蛭弧菌是一种捕食性细菌，它攻击并进入其他革兰氏阴性菌的周质空间。
Synonyms:  predatory bacteria,  periplasmic predator
Antonyms: prey bacteria
