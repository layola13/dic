cultism:/ˈkʌltɪzəm/| n.|异教；狂热崇拜；膜拜|Excessive devotion to a person, idea, or thing, resembling the veneration given to a religious figure.  It often involves a charismatic leader and a system of beliefs or practices that are considered unorthodox or extreme.|The cultism surrounding the new pop star was astonishing.  这个新晋流行歌星受到的狂热崇拜令人震惊。|近义词：fanaticism, zealotry, idolatry|反义词:  disinterest, apathy|noun
