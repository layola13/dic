Etymology:
From "overreserved" + "-ness". "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Reserved" comes from the Old French "reserver"  meaning "to keep back, retain".  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
