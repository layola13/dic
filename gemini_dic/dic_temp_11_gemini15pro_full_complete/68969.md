fathomer:/ˈfæðəmə(r)/ | n. | 探测者，测量者；理解者 | Someone who fathoms (measures the depth of something, or understands something deeply). | The oceanographer was a skilled fathomer of the deep sea.  这位海洋学家是一位熟练的深海探测者。|近义词: surveyor, sounder, interpreter, analyst |反义词:  N/A | Noun

fathom:/ˈfæðəm/ | v. | 测量(深度)；理解，弄清 | To measure the depth of (water); to understand (something) fully. |  She couldn't fathom his motives. 她无法理解他的动机。|近义词: understand, comprehend, grasp, gauge |反义词: misunderstand | Verb
