elementarity:/ˌɛləmɛnˈtærɪti/| n.|基本性，初级性，简单性|The elementarity of the concept makes it easy to understand.  这个概念的简单性使它易于理解。|例句：The teacher stressed the elementarity of the principles. 老师强调了这些原理的基本性。

例句：Because of the elementarity of the task, it was assigned to the new intern. 因为这项任务很简单，所以分配给了新来的实习生。|近义词：simplicity, basicness, fundamentality|反义词：complexity, sophistication, advancedness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin elementarius (belonging to the elements or rudiments), from Latin elementum (element).  The "-ity" suffix transforms the adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
