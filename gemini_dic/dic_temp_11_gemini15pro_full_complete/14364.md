autoplate:/ˈɔːtəʊpleɪt/| n.|自动印版机，自动镀板机|A machine that automatically produces or coats printing plates.|The newspaper uses a new autoplate to make the printing process more efficient. 这家报纸使用一台新的自动印版机，使印刷过程更高效。|近义词:platemaking machine, automatic plater|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "manual plate maker")|noun
