erasure:/ɪˈreɪʒər/ (US), /ɪˈreɪʒə(r)/ (UK)| n.|消除，抹杀（指销毁证据、信息或历史）|The systematic removal or suppression of something, often related to history, culture, or identity.|The erasure of women's contributions to science is a serious issue.  女性对科学的贡献被抹杀是一个严重的问题。|synonyms: suppression, obliteration, expungement|antonyms: commemoration, remembrance, preservation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *erasura, from Latin erasus, past participle of eradere "to scrape out," from ex- "out" + radere "to scrape."
