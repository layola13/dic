flexibles:/ˈflɛksɪbl̩z/
| adj. |灵活的，柔韧的，可变的|Able to bend easily without breaking; adaptable or variable.|These flexible working hours are popular with parents.|近义词: adaptable, pliable, supple, elastic, adjustable, versatile |反义词: inflexible, rigid, stiff, unyielding|词性:形容词 (通常用作复数形式，指灵活的工作时间或安排)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *flexibilis, from Latin flex-, stem of flectere "to bend" + -ibilis -ible.
