menhaden:/ˈmenˌheɪdn/ | n. | 鲱鱼,油鲱 | A small oily fish (Brevoortia tyrannus) of the western Atlantic, abundant and commercially important as a source of fish meal and oil. | Menhaden are a crucial part of the marine ecosystem. 鲱鱼是海洋生态系统的重要组成部分。| Menhaden are often ground into fishmeal. 鲱鱼常被磨成鱼粉。 | synonyms: bunker, pogy | antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a specific fish species) | noun
