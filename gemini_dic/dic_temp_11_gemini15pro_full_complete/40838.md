convulsive:/kənˈvʌlsɪv/ | adj. | 抽搐的，痉挛的 | Characterized by or producing convulsions; spasmodic. |  He suffered convulsive fits after the accident. 他在事故后出现了抽搐。| synonyms: spasmodic, jerky, fitful, twitching, uncontrolled | antonyms: steady, controlled, calm, smooth | adjective

convulse:/kənˈvʌls/ | vt. | 使抽搐，使震动，使剧烈震荡 |  To shake violently; agitate. | The earthquake convulsed the city. 地震使城市剧烈震动。 | synonyms: shake, agitate, rock, jolt, shudder | antonyms: calm, soothe, stabilize, settle | verb
