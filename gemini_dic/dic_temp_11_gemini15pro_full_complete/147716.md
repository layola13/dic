overpresumption:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmpʃən/
|n.|过分自信；僭越；放肆|An overestimation of oneself or one's abilities; excessive presumption.  The act of presuming too much; taking liberties. |His overpresumption led him to believe he could win the race without training. 他过分的自信让他以为不用训练就能赢得比赛。|Synonyms: arrogance, audacity, conceit, presumption, temerity |Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence|noun
