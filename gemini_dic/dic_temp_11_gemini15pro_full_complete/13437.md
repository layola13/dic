atop:/əˈtɒp/ | prep. adv.| 在…顶上；在…上面 |On top of; on the surface of. | The flag flew atop the building. 旗帜飘扬在楼顶上。 |  The cat sat atop the bookshelf. 猫坐在书架顶上。 | 近义词：on, upon, above | 反义词: beneath, below, underneath | 词性: 介词, 副词

词源: From a- + top.  "A-" in this context is a reduced form of "on."
