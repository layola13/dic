/ˈkɒntekst/ /ˈkɑːntekst/ | n. | 上下文；语境；背景 |The meaning of a word can change depending on its context.  一个词的意思会根据它的上下文而改变。| Example: You have misinterpreted my words; they were said in a humorous context. 你误解了我的话；我说这些话的时候是在开玩笑的语境下。|Synonyms: background, circumstances, setting, situation, environment| Antonyms:  isolation, detachment| noun


/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪz/ /kənˈtekstʃuəˌlaɪz/ | vt. | 置于语境中；把…放在特定的背景下考虑 | To fully understand the event, we need to contextualize it within the broader historical narrative. 为了充分理解这一事件，我们需要将其置于更广泛的历史叙述背景下进行考量。| Example: The professor contextualized the poem by discussing the poet's life and times. 教授通过讨论诗人的生平和时代背景来解释这首诗。| Synonyms: place, situate, locate, position | Antonyms:  decontextualize, abstract | verb
