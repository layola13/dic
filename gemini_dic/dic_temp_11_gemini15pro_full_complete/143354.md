outfabling:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪbəlɪŋ/
verb
胜过；编造更精彩的故事
surpassing in the telling of fables or stories.  Creating a more elaborate or fantastical story than another.
The bard was outfabling his rivals with tales of dragons and magic.  吟游诗人用龙和魔法的故事胜过他的对手们。
近义词: outdoing, excelling, surpassing, outstripping
反义词: failing, losing, falling short
