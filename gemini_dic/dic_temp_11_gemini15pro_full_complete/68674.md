farce:/fɑːrs/| n.|滑稽剧，闹剧，胡闹，荒唐的事情|A humorous play or film based on improbable situations, often with slapstick elements.  A ludicrous or empty show; mockery.|The trial was a complete farce. 这场审判完全是一场闹剧。|近义词：mockery, travesty, parody, sham, burlesque|反义词：tragedy, drama, seriousness|名词
