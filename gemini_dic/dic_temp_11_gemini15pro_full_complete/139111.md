obdurateness:/ˌɒbdjʊrətnəs/| n.|顽固，执拗，冷酷无情|The obdurateness of his refusal surprised everyone.  他的断然拒绝让所有人都感到惊讶。|例句：His obdurateness in the face of pleas for mercy was shocking. 他面对求饶的冷酷无情令人震惊。

例句：The judge was angered by the defendant's obdurateness.  被告的顽固态度激怒了法官。


近义词：stubbornness, intransigence, inflexibility, hardness, impenitence|反义词:yielding, compliance, remorse, penitence|词性:名词

词源：From Late Latin *obdūratus (past participle of obdūrāre  "to harden"), from Latin ob- "against" + dūrāre "to harden," from dūrus "hard."
