blackguardly:/ˈblæɡɑːrdli/
adjective
卑鄙的；无赖的；恶棍似的
Characteristic of a blackguard; villainous; scurrilous.
His blackguardly behavior shocked everyone at the party.
他卑鄙的行为让派对上的每个人都感到震惊。

近义词: villainous, scoundrelly, dastardly, despicable, contemptible
反义词: honorable, noble, respectable, virtuous

词性: adjective

词源:  From blackguard + -ly.  "Blackguard" originally referred to the lowest menials in a large household, often responsible for dirty work.  Over time, the term evolved to denote a person of bad character.
