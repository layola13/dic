neap:/niːp/| adj. n.|小潮的，小潮；|Relating to or denoting tides midway between spring tides that attain the least height.|The neap tides occur twice a month.|近义词:low, minimal|反义词:spring, high|词性:形容词，名词
Etymology: From Old English *nēp-flōd "neap tide," from Proto-Germanic *neptiz, from PIE root *nebh- "cloud, mist." The original sense was likely related to the obscured or less pronounced nature of neap tides.
