However, it is also known to be carcinogenic.|Naphthylamine was once used extensively in the dye industry, but its use has been significantly reduced due to its carcinogenic properties. 萘胺曾经广泛用于染料工业，但由于其致癌性，其使用已大大减少。

Naphthylamine can cause bladder cancer. 萘胺会导致膀胱癌。|近义词：aromatic amine|反义词: None|词性:noun
