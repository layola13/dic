altarage:/ˈɔːltərɪdʒ/
n.
祭坛 emoluments; 祭坛收入; 供养圣职人员的收入;
Emoluments or offerings due to a priest for services at the altar.
The altarage from the village church was barely enough to support the priest. 村里教堂的祭坛收入几乎不足以供养牧师。
近义词:offering, oblation, tithe
反义词:desecration, sacrilege
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *alterage, from Old French *alterage, from Medieval Latin *altaragium, from Latin altare (“altar”) + -agium.
