lead-free:/ˌledˈfriː/
adjective
无铅的
Containing no lead.
This paint is lead-free and safe for children.  这种油漆是无铅的，对儿童安全。
Lead-free gasoline is now required by law.  法律现在要求使用无铅汽油。
leadless
leaded
adjective

Etymology->
From lead +‎ -free.
