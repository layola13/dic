gastritic:/ɡæsˈtrɪtɪk/| adj.|胃炎的，患胃炎的|Relating to or affected with gastritis (inflammation of the lining of the stomach).|例句：He experienced gastritic pain after eating spicy food. 他吃完辛辣食物后感到胃痛。|例句：Gastritic symptoms can include nausea and vomiting. 胃炎症状可能包括恶心和呕吐。|近义词：inflamed (inflamed stomach lining), dyspeptic|反义词: healthy (stomach)
词性：形容词

词源：From Modern Latin gastrītis + -ic, from Ancient Greek gaster (stomach).
