cozily: /ˈkoʊzɪli/ (US) /ˈkəʊzɪli/ (UK)| adv.|舒适地，惬意地|In a cozy manner; snugly and comfortably.|例句：They sat cozily by the fire. 他们舒适地坐在火炉旁。|例句：The cat curled up cozily on the rug. 猫舒服地蜷缩在地毯上。|近义词：snugly, comfortably, warmly|反义词：uncomfortably, coldly, exposedly|词性:副词
词源：From cozy + -ly. Cozy from Middle High German kūs(e), 'choice'
