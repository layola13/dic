alicart:/ˈælɪkɑːrt/ | n. |购物车| A virtual shopping cart used on e-commerce websites, especially associated with Alibaba's platforms. alicart:/ˈælɪkɑːrt/ | n. | 阿里购物车 |  Specifically refers to the shopping cart feature on Alibaba's e-commerce platforms like AliExpress, Tmall, and Taobao.
