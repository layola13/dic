deigning:/deɪnɪŋ/
| v. |屈尊，俯就| condescending to do something that one considers to be beneath one's dignity. |The king deigned to speak to the peasant. 国王屈尊与农夫说话。|近义词：condescend, stoop, vouchsafe|反义词：refuse, decline|verb

deigning:/deɪnɪŋ/
| adj. |屈尊的，俯就的|showing an attitude of superiority. |She gave him a deigning look. 她给了他一个居高临下的眼神。|近义词：condescending, patronizing, supercilious|反义词：humble, respectful|adjective
