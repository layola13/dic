explicators:/ˈɛksplɪkeɪtərz/
n.
解释者，说明者
Those who explain or clarify something; commentators.
The explicators offered several interpretations of the poem.  这些解释者对这首诗提供了多种解读。
synonyms: interpreters, elucidators, commentators, analysts, exegetes
antonyms: obfuscators, confounders
noun
