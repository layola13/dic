glossy:/ˈɡlɒsi/ (UK), /ˈɡlɔːsi/ (US)| adj.|光滑的，有光泽的；虚假的，华而不实的|smooth and shiny; superficially attractive; deceptively good-looking|The magazine is printed on glossy paper. 这本杂志印在光滑的纸上。|近义词：shiny, polished, sleek, glazed|反义词：dull, matte, rough|adjective
