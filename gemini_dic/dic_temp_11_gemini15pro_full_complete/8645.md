anticonformities:/ˌæntiˈkənˈfɔːrmɪtiz/
|n.|不整合,反整合|A surface of unconformity in which stratified sedimentary rocks overlie an erosion surface cut into igneous or metamorphic rocks.|The geological survey revealed numerous anticonformities in the region. 地质调查显示该地区存在大量不整合面。|The anticonformity indicates a significant gap in the geological record.  这个不整合面表明地质记录中存在一个明显的缺口。|近义词:unconformity|反义词:conformity|Noun
