doorstepped:/ˈdɔːrstɛpt/| v. |上门采访；突然造访|To approach someone at their doorstep, often unexpectedly, typically for an interview or to confront them about something.|Reporters doorstepped the politician as he left his home. 记者在他离开家时在他门口采访了他。|The charity worker doorstepped me while I was eating dinner.  慈善工作者在我吃晚饭时突然造访。|近义词：ambush, accost, confront|反义词: avoid, evade|词性:动词

词源:
"door" + "step"  字面意思是“门口的台阶”，引申为在门口拦截或采访。
