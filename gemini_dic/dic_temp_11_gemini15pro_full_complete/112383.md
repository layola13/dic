loss's:/lɒsɪz/ | n. | 损失，亏损 |  The possessive form of "loss," indicating that something belongs to or is associated with a loss.  It signifies the loss's impact, consequences, or attributes. | The loss's impact on the community was devastating.  这个损失对社区的影响是毁灭性的。| synonyms: detriment's, deprivation's | antonyms: gain's, profit's |  noun (possessive form)


Etymology:
From Middle English *los, from Old English los (“loss, damage, destruction, perdition”), from Proto-Germanic *lausą (“loss”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)leus- (“to loosen, detach”).
