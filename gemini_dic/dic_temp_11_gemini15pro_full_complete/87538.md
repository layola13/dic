heterogeny:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒɛni/ | n. |异质性，异源性|The state or quality of being heterogeneous; dissimilarity or variety of elements or parts.|例句：The heterogeny of the population is reflected in the wide variety of languages spoken. 人口构成的异质性体现在其使用的语言种类繁多。

例句：The heterogeny of the rock formations made it difficult to predict where oil might be found. 岩层结构的异质性使得预测石油的所在地变得困难。|近义词：diversity, variety, heterogeneity, difference, dissimilarity|反义词：homogeneity, uniformity, similarity, sameness|词性:noun

词源: From Ancient Greek  ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) + -geny.
