housewifish:/ˈhaʊswɪfaɪʃ/| adj.| 家庭主妇气的，有家庭主妇特点的；过于节俭的|Having the qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with a housewife; excessively frugal or concerned with domestic matters.|She has a rather housewifish attitude towards spending money. 她花钱的态度非常像家庭主妇，很节俭。|近义词：frumpy, dowdy, matronly|反义词：stylish, fashionable, chic|词性:形容词

词源:  "housewife" + "-ish" (suffix indicating resembling or characteristic of).  The word "housewife" itself comes from Old English "hūs" (house) + "wīf" (woman).
