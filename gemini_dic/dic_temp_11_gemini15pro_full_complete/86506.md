heiresses:/ˈɛə.rɪs.ɪz/
noun
女继承人(复数)
A female person who inherits or is entitled to inherit property, rank, title, or office, especially as an heir of someone who has died.
The heiresses to the vast fortune lived in opulent mansions. 这些巨额财产的女继承人住在豪华的宅邸里。
synonyms: inheritrix, heritrix
antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
