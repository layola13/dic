leiotrichous:/ˌlaɪoʊˈtrɪkəs/
adjective
直发的；毛发柔软的
Having straight and smooth hair.
例句：The leiotrichous texture of her hair made it easy to style. 她直顺的头发很容易做造型。

例句：Leiotrichous breeds of dogs often require less grooming. 直毛品种的狗通常需要较少的梳理。
近义词：straight-haired, smooth-haired
反义词：ulotrichous, woolly-haired
词性：adjective
