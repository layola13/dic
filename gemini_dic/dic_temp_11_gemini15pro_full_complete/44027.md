ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *krimpen, frequentative of *krimmen (“to shrink, wrinkle”), from Proto-Germanic *krimjaną (“to shrink, wrinkle”). Related to German krimmen (“to shrink, wrinkle”), Danish krympe (“to shrink”), Swedish krympa (“to shrink”). The sense of "hindering or obstructing" is a figurative extension of the original meaning.
