aplanogamete:/əˈplænəɡæmiːt/
noun
不动配子，非游动配子
A non-motile gamete, as distinguished from a motile gamete, such as a zoospore.
Aplanogametes are formed in some green and brown algae. | 不动配子形成于一些绿藻和褐藻中。
The aplanogamete fused with another from a different thallus. | 这个不动配子与来自另一个叶状体的另一个不动配子融合。
近义词: isogamete (when morphologically similar), gamete
反义词: zoospore, planogamete
词性: n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + plano- (wandering, motile) + gamete (reproductive cell).  Plano- comes from the Greek "planos" (wandering).  Gamete comes from the Greek "gametes" (spouse).
