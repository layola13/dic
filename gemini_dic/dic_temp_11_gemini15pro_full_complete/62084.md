encephaloid:/ɛnˈsɛfələɪd/| adj. n.| 脑样的，似脑的；髓状癌|Resembling brain substance in appearance; soft and whitish.  Also, a historical term for a type of malignant tumor, typically a soft carcinoma.|The biopsy revealed an encephaloid tumor.  活检显示一个髓样瘤。|近义词：cerebriform, medullary|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From en- + cephalo- + -oid. En- means "in, into". Cephalo- refers to the head or brain, from Greek kephalē. -Oid means "resembling," from Greek -oeidēs.
