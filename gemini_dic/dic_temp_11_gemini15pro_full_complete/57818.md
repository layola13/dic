drenchers:/ˈdren.tʃərz/
noun
淋透者；倾盆大雨；浸湿器
Something that drenches, especially heavy rain.
The drenchers soaked us to the skin.  倾盆大雨把我们淋透了。
synonyms: downpours, cloudbursts, deluges
antonyms: drizzles, sprinkles
