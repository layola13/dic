overassuredness:/ˌoʊvərəˈʃʊrdnəs/ | n. | 过于自信；过分自信 | The state of being excessively confident or self-assured, often to the point of arrogance or unrealistic expectations. | His overassuredness led him to underestimate the competition. 他的过分自信导致他低估了竞争对手。|  近义词: overconfidence, arrogance, presumption, cockiness | 反义词: humility, modesty, diffidence, insecurity | noun

overassured:/ˌoʊvərəˈʃʊrd/ | adj. | 过于自信的；过分自信的 | Excessively confident or self-assured, often to the point of arrogance or unrealistic expectations. | He was overassured about his chances of winning. 他对自己的获胜机会过于自信。| 近义词: overconfident, arrogant, presumptuous, cocky | 反义词: humble, modest, diffident, insecure | adjective
