extracorporeal:/ˌɛkstrəkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/
adjective
体外的；离体的
Existing or occurring outside the body.
例句：Extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) is a life-support technique used when the lungs or heart are unable to function adequately. 体外膜肺氧合 (ECMO) 是一种在肺或心脏无法充分发挥功能时使用的生命支持技术。

例句：Extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy (ESWL) is a non-invasive treatment for kidney stones. 体外冲击波碎石术 (ESWL) 是一种无创的肾结石治疗方法。

近义词：ex vivo, in vitro
反义词：in vivo, intracorporeal
词性：adjective
