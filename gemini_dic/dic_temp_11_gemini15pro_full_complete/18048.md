begrimed:/bɪˈɡraɪmd/
adjective
污秽的，沾满污垢的
covered with grime; very dirty
The mechanic's hands were begrimed after working on the engine.  技工修完发动机后，双手沾满了污垢。
The begrimed walls of the alley gave it a sinister feel.  小巷里沾满污垢的墙壁给人一种不祥的感觉。
近义词:dirty, soiled, filthy, grimy
反义词:clean, spotless, pristine
词性:adjective
