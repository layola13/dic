fribbler:/ˈfrɪblər/| n. | 轻浮的人，玩弄感情的人；浪费时间的人 |Someone who engages in frivolous activities; a trifler; someone who wastes time on trivial matters. | He's such a fribbler, always flitting from one hobby to the next. 他真是个轻浮的人，总是从一个爱好跳到另一个爱好。|Synonyms: trifler, dabbler, idler, dallier | Antonyms:  hard worker, diligent person | noun

fribble:/ˈfrɪbəl/| v. |  玩弄；浪费时间；轻率地对待 | To act or behave frivolously; to waste time on trivial matters; to treat something lightly or without seriousness. | Stop fribbling and get to work! 别再浪费时间了，快去工作！|Synonyms: trifle, dally, dawdle | Antonyms:  focus, concentrate | verb

fribbling:/ˈfrɪblɪŋ/| adj. | 轻浮的；不重要的；琐碎的 | Characterized by frivolity; unimportant; trivial. |  She dismissed his concerns with a fribbling wave of her hand. 她轻浮地挥了挥手，对他的担忧不屑一顾。|Synonyms: frivolous, trifling, insignificant, trivial | Antonyms: serious, important, significant | adjective
