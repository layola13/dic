nondeterminists:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnɪsts/
|n.|非确定性者；非决定论者|People who believe in or study nondeterministic systems or philosophies.  Those who subscribe to nondeterminism, a philosophical position that rejects the notion that all events are predetermined.|Nondeterminists often argue that free will is possible. 非决定论者经常争论自由意志是可能的。|Synonyms: indeterminists|Antonyms: determinists|Noun

nondeterministic:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk/
|adj.|非确定性的；非决定论的|Not determined in advance; random or unpredictable, especially in computer science, referring to algorithms or systems that can produce different outcomes for the same input.|Nondeterministic algorithms are often used in artificial intelligence. 非确定性算法常用于人工智能。|Synonyms:  indeterministic, probabilistic, stochastic|Antonyms: deterministic, predictable|Adjective
