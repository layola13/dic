extraction:/ɪkˈstrækʃən/ | n. |提取；取出；抽出；拔出；摘录；选段|The process of removing something, usually with effort or force.  The extraction of minerals from the earth. |例句：The dentist gave me a local anesthetic before the extraction of my wisdom tooth.  牙医在我拔智齿之前给我打了局部麻醉。|例句：The extraction of oil from shale rock is a complex process. 从页岩中提取石油是一个复杂的过程。|近义词：removal, withdrawal, derivation |反义词: insertion, implantation |词性: noun
