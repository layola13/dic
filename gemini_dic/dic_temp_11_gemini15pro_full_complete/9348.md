antiproductivity:/ˌæntiˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 反生产力，反效率 |The state of being unproductive or hindering productivity.  It refers to activities or habits that seem enjoyable or relaxing in the short term but ultimately detract from achieving goals or completing tasks. |Excessive meetings can lead to antiproductivity in the workplace. 过多的会议会导致工作场所的反生产力。


Antiproductivity can stem from procrastination or a lack of focus. 反生产力可能源于拖延或缺乏专注。| 近义词：inefficiency, unproductiveness, counterproductivity | 反义词：productivity, efficiency, effectiveness | 词性: 名词
