decimetric:/ˌdɛsɪˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|分米的，十厘米的|Relating to or having a wavelength of between 10 and 100 centimeters, which corresponds to frequencies between 300 MHz and 3 GHz. This range is commonly used in UHF television, microwave ovens, and some radar systems.|Decimetric radar waves are used in some weather forecasting systems. 分米雷达波用于一些天气预报系统。|Synonyms: centimeter-wave, UHF|Antonyms: kilometer-wave, myriametric|adjective

decimeter:/ˈdɛsɪˌmitər/| n.|分米|A unit of length in the metric system, equal to one tenth of a meter (10 centimeters).|The book is approximately 2 decimeters wide. 这本书大约宽2分米。|Synonyms: 10 centimeters|Antonyms: dekameter|noun
