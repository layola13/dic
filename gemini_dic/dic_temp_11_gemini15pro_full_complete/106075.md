knew:/njuː/ /nuː/
verb
知道，了解，认识
past tense of "know" -  indicating past familiarity or understanding of something or someone.
I knew him when we were children.  我小时候认识他。
She knew the answer to the question. 她知道问题的答案。

近义词:understood, recognized, realized
反义词:ignored, was unaware of
词性:verb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I know the answer. 我知道答案。
He knows how to drive. 他知道怎么开车。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am getting to know her better. 我正在更加了解她。 (know is not typically used in the continuous tense, but "get to know" is an exception)
He is finding out the truth. 他正在发现真相。（Similar to above,  "find out" is used as a progressive alternative to "know")


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have known him for years. 我认识他很多年了。
She has known the truth all along. 她一直都知道真相。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been getting to know my new neighbors. 我一直在了解我的新邻居。（Again, using a phrase with a similar meaning that can be used in the continuous tense）
He has been learning about the history of the war. 他一直在学习战争史。（Focusing on the process of gaining knowledge, not the state of knowing itself）


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I knew him when we were in school. 我们上学时我认识他。
She knew the answer immediately. 她立刻就知道答案了。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was getting to know him when he moved away. 他搬走的时候我正在了解他。
She was learning about the planets in science class. 她在科学课上学习关于行星的知识。（Similar rationale as present continuous, using a related phrase that works in the continuous tense）


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had known him before he became famous. 他成名前我就认识他了。
She had known the truth before anyone else. 她比任何人都先知道真相。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been getting to know him for a few months before he moved. 他搬走前我已经认识他几个月了。（"Getting to know" is used here for the continuous aspect）
They had been studying the issue for years before they found a solution.  他们在找到解决方案之前已经研究这个问题很多年了。（Focusing on the process of gaining knowledge leading up to a point in the past）


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will know the answer tomorrow. 我明天就会知道答案了。
He will know what to do. 他会知道该怎么做。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be getting to know my colleagues better over the next few weeks. 在接下来的几周里，我将更好地了解我的同事。
He will be learning about computer programming next semester. 他下学期将学习计算机编程。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have known him for ten years. 到明年，我就认识他十年了。
By the time the project is finished, she will have known everything about it. 到项目完成时，她将对它了如指掌。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been getting to know my new team for three months. 到下个月，我将已经认识我的新团队三个月了。
By the time he graduates, he will have been studying English for six years. 到他毕业时，他将已经学习英语六年了。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would tell you. 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew how to fix this. 我希望我知道如何解决这个问题。


被动语态 (Passive Voice):
The truth was known by everyone. 真相大家都知道。（Passive voice is not commonly used with "know")
It is known that he is a good person. 大家都知道他是个好人。


疑问句:
Did you know him? 你认识他吗？
How did she know the answer? 她怎么知道答案的？


否定句:
I didn't know him. 我不认识他。
She doesn't know the answer. 她不知道答案。


PHRASE->
You know what? 你知道吗？（用于引出一个想法或评论）
I don't know about that. 我对此不太确定。
I know, right? 我知道，对吧？（表示同意）
Who knows? 谁知道呢？
As far as I know... 据我所知...
