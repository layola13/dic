hyperform:/ˈhaɪpərfɔːrm/ | n. | 超形态，超形 |A form that goes beyond the usual or typical form, often exceeding limits or expectations. A concept often used in art, architecture, and design to describe exaggerated or amplified forms.|The architect's design for the new museum is a hyperform, pushing the boundaries of traditional building shapes.  这个新博物馆的建筑师设计是一个超形态，突破了传统建筑形状的界限。| 近义词: exaggerated form, amplified form, extreme form | 反义词: standard form, typical form, conventional form | 词性: noun

hyperform:/ˈhaɪpərfɔːrm/ | adj. | 超形态的 |Having the qualities of a hyperform; exceeding the usual or typical form. |The hyperform sculpture dominated the plaza, its swirling lines attracting attention from all directions. 超形态的雕塑占据了广场，其旋转的线条吸引了来自四面八方的目光。| 近义词: exaggerated, amplified, extreme | 反义词: standard, typical, conventional | 词性: adjective
