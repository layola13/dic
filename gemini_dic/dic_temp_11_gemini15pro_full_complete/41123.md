coprophagan:/ˌkɒprəˈfeɪɡən/ | n. | 食粪者 | An organism that consumes feces. | Rabbits are coprophagans, reingesting some of their droppings to extract additional nutrients. 兔子是食粪动物，会重新摄入一些粪便以提取额外的营养。| 近义词:dung beetle, scatophage | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

coprophagy:/ˌkɒprəˈfædʒi/ | n. | 食粪行为 | The act of consuming feces. | Coprophagy is observed in some animal species as a means of obtaining essential nutrients. 在某些动物物种中观察到食粪行为，以此作为获取必需营养素的一种方式。| 近义词:scatophagy | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源：
From Greek *κόπρος (kópros, “dung”) + *-φαγος (-phagos, “-eater”), from φαγεῖν (phageîn, “to eat”).
