earshot:/ˈɪrʃɑːt/ | n. |听力所及的范围；耳朵听得见的地方 |The conversation was held within earshot of the children.  孩子们能听到这次谈话。|  The speaker's voice was loud enough to carry to everyone within earshot. 演讲者的声音足够大，能让所有听得见的人都听到。| Synonyms: hearing, range of hearing, sound, reach, proximity | Antonyms: out of hearing, beyond reach,  distant | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ereshotte, from Old English ēarshēot (“earshot”), equivalent to ear + shot. Cognate with Scots earshot (“earshot”). More at ear, shot.
