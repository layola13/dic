deputize:/ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪz/| v. |代理；委任…为副职；授权|To appoint someone to act as a substitute or deputy.|The sheriff deputized several citizens to help search for the missing child.  警长授权几名市民协助寻找失踪儿童。|Synonyms: appoint, delegate, commission, authorize, empower|Antonyms: dismiss, discharge, remove, revoke|verb

deputize:/ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪz/| n. |代理；委任…为副职；授权|(This form is less common) The act of deputizing. |His deputize of the young officer surprised everyone.  他委任这位年轻军官代理职务让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: appointment, delegation, commission|Antonyms: dismissal, discharge, removal|noun
