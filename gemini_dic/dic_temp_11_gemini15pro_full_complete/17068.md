bathymetry:/bəˈθɪmətri/
n.|水深测量；海底地形|The study of underwater depth of lake or ocean floors.  It is the underwater equivalent of topography.|例句：The bathymetry of the lake was charted using sonar.  用声纳绘制了湖泊的水深图。|例句：Detailed bathymetry is essential for safe navigation. 详细的水深测量对于安全航行至关重要。|近义词：hydrography, sounding|反义词:topography|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βαθύς (bathús, “deep”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
