ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *Cornyshe, *Cornysch, from Old English *Cornwilisċ, *Cornwælisċ (“Cornish, of Cornwall”), equivalent to Cornwall +‎ -ish. Cognate with West Frisian Kornysk (“Cornish”), Dutch Kornisch (“Cornish”).  More at Cornwall.
