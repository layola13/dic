gauntly:/ˈɡɔːntli/ | adv. | 憔悴地，消瘦地 | In a thin and bony way, often due to suffering, hunger, or age.  |  He stared gauntly at the horizon. 他憔悴地凝视着地平线。 |  thinly, haggardly, leanly, emaciatedly |  robustly, healthily | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From gaunt + -ly.  Gaunt itself comes from Old North French *gant "lean, thin," probably of Germanic origin.
