dictature:/dɪkˈteɪtʃər/ | n. |独裁统治，专政|A form of government in which one person or a small group possesses absolute power without effective constitutional limitations.|例句：The country suffered under a brutal dictatorship.  这个国家遭受残酷的独裁统治。

|例句：His dictatorship lasted for over twenty years. 他的独裁统治持续了二十多年。

|近义词：tyranny, despotism, autocracy|反义词：democracy, republic|词性: noun
