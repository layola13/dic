approximant:/əˈprɒksɪmənt/ (UK), /əˈprɑːksɪmənt/ (US)| n. adj.|近似值，近似音|A sound that is produced by bringing one articulator close to another, but without them touching.  As an adjective, it describes a sound with this quality.|The /j/ sound in "yes" is an approximant.  "yes"中的/j/音是一个近似音。|近义词: glide, liquid|反义词: obstruent|词性: noun, adjective

approximant:/əˈprɒksɪmənt/ (UK), /əˈprɑːksɪmənt/ (US)| adj.|近似的|Almost correct or accurate but not completely so.|The approximant cost of the project is $1 million.  该项目的近似成本为100万美元。|近义词: approximate, near, close|反义词: exact, precise|词性: adjective
