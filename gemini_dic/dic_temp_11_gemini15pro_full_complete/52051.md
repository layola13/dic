diallers:/ˈdaɪələrz/| n.|拨号器；拨号程序；自动拨号软件|A dialler is a piece of software that automatically dials a series of telephone numbers. 拨号器是一种自动拨打一系列电话号码的软件。|Many companies use predictive diallers to connect with potential customers. 许多公司使用预测拨号器与潜在客户联系。|Telemarketers often utilize diallers to increase their outreach. 电话销售员经常使用拨号器来扩大他们的外联。|synonyms: autodialer, automatic dialing system|antonyms: manual dialing|noun
