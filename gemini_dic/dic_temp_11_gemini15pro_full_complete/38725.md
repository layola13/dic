conditionalities:/ˌkɒndɪʃəˈnælɪtiz/ | n. | 条件; 附加条件;限制条款 | Conditions, requirements, or stipulations attached to something, often aid, loan, or agreement. | The loan was granted with several conditionalities attached. 这笔贷款附带了几个条件。 | The IMF imposed strict conditionalities on the bailout package. 国际货币基金组织对救助方案施加了严格的条件。 |  synonyms: stipulations, provisions, requirements, prerequisites, strings | antonyms: unconditional, unrestricted | noun
