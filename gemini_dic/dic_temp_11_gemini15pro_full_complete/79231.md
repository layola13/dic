glaringly:/ˈɡlɛərɪŋli/
adverb
明显地；炫目地；耀眼地
in a way that is very obvious and easily seen
The report highlighted glaringly obvious errors in the company's accounts. 这份报告突出了公司账目中显而易见的错误。
The sun was glaringly bright.  太阳耀眼地明亮。
Synonyms: obviously, conspicuously, strikingly, blatantly, patently
Antonyms: subtly, inconspicuously, discreetly
词性: 副词
