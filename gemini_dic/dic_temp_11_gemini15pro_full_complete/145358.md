overburdensome:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdənsəm/| adj.|繁重的，负担过重的|Too much; more than one can bear.  Causing excessive hardship, worry, or grief.|例句：The overburdensome regulations stifled small businesses.  这些繁重的规章制度扼杀了小型企业。|例句：He found the overburdensome workload unbearable and eventually quit his job. 他觉得繁重的工作难以忍受，最终辞掉了工作。|近义词：onerous, excessive, weighty, oppressive|反义词：light, manageable, easy|词性:形容词

词源:
From over- + burdensome.  "Burdensome" itself comes from "burden" (from Old French "burden," meaning "load, pack") + "-some" (a suffix meaning "characterized by").  Therefore, "overburdensome" literally means "characterized by an excessive burden."
