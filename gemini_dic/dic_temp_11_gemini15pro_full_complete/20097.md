biospheres:/ˈbaɪoʊsfɪrz/
n.
生物圈
The regions of the surface and atmosphere of the Earth or another planet occupied by living organisms.
The Earth's biosphere includes all living things and the environments they live in.  地球的生物圈包括所有的生物以及它们生活的环境。
The impact of pollution on the biosphere is devastating. 污染对生物圈的影响是毁灭性的。
