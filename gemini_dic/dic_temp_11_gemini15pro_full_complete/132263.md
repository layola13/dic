Etymology:
* non- : Prefix meaning "not," from Latin *non*.
* dedication : From Late Latin *dedicatio*, from Latin *dedicare* "to dedicate," from *de-* "down, completely" + *-dicare* "proclaim, dedicate," related to *dicere* "to say."
