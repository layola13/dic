outsummed:/ˌaʊtˈsʌmd/ | v. | 超过总和，累计超过 | To exceed in total amount; to surpass in accumulated value. |  The donations outsummed our wildest expectations. 捐款总额超过了我们最乐观的预期。| The new recruit's sales figures quickly outsummed those of the veteran staff. 新员工的销售额很快就超过了资深员工。| 近义词: exceeded, surpassed, outstripped | 反义词: lagged behind, fell short of | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "summed" (past participle of "sum," meaning "to add up").  The word is formed by combining the prefix "out-" with the past participle of the verb "sum," indicating surpassing in the act of summing or totaling.
