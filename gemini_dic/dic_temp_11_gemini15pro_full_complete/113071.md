lumpy:/ˈlʌmpi/| adj.|块状的；疙疙瘩瘩的；凹凸不平的|Covered with or containing lumps; uneven in texture.  Characterized by lumps or bumps.|The porridge was lumpy and unappetizing. 粥块状的，一点也不诱人。|近义词： bumpy, knobbly, uneven, irregular|反义词：smooth, even, flat|词性:形容词
Etymology: From lump + -y.  "lump" comes from Middle English lumpe, of uncertain origin.
