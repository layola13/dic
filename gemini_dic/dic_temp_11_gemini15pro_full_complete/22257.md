bone-headed:/ˈboʊnˌhɛdɪd/
adjective
愚蠢的，笨头笨脑的
stupid; foolish; lacking intelligence or good judgment.
That was a bone-headed move, forgetting your passport! 那是愚蠢的举动，竟然忘记了你的护照！
boneheaded decision 愚蠢的决定

synonyms: stupid, foolish, idiotic, silly, unwise, unintelligent, dense, dumb, doltish, brainless, mindless, witless, vacuous, inane, asinine, obtuse, dim-witted, half-witted, slow-witted, simpleminded, empty-headed, birdbrained
antonyms: intelligent, smart, clever, bright, wise, sensible, judicious, prudent

词性: 形容词
