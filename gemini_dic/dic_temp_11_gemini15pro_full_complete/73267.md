foreallege:/ˌfɔːrəˈlɛdʒ/ | v. |预先声明；预先指控|To allege or assert something beforehand; to state or cite something in advance as a plea or excuse.|The defendant forealleged his innocence in the face of overwhelming evidence.  被告在压倒性的证据面前预先声称自己是无辜的。| The lawyer forealleged the defendant's mental instability as a mitigating factor. 律师预先提出被告的精神不稳定作为减轻罪责的因素。|  近义词：pre-allege, allege beforehand| 反义词：deny, refute |词性:动词
