euphemistically:/ˌjuːfəˈmɪstɪkli/| adv.|委婉地|In a way that avoids saying something unpleasant or offensive directly.  It uses mild or indirect words or expressions to replace harsh or blunt ones.|He euphemistically referred to his resignation as "pursuing other opportunities." 他委婉地将他的辞职称为“追求其他机会”。|Synonym:indirectly, delicately, subtly|Antonym:directly, bluntly, frankly|adverb
