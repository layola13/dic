back-slashes:/ˈbækˌslæʃɪz/| n.|反斜杠（复数）|Multiple back-slashes. |Example: Too many back-slashes can make your code difficult to read.  太多的反斜杠会使你的代码难以阅读。|Synonym: reverse slashes|Antonym: forward slashes ( / )|Noun
