cabernets:/ˌkæbərˈneɪs/| n. |赤霞珠葡萄酒（通常用复数形式）|A full-bodied, dry red wine made from Cabernet Sauvignon grapes, often blended with other varieties.|例句：We offer a wide selection of cabernets from Napa Valley. 我们提供来自纳帕谷的各种赤霞珠葡萄酒。

例句：These cabernets are known for their rich tannins and complex aromas. 这些赤霞珠葡萄酒以其丰富的单宁和复杂的香气而闻名。|近义词：red wines, Bordeaux (sometimes), |反义词: (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with white wines or light-bodied reds) |词性:noun
