halfstocking:/ˈhæfˌstɑːkɪŋ/ /ˈhɑːfˌstɒkɪŋ/| n. |半截袜，短袜|A stocking that reaches only to the calf or just below the knee.  A sock that does not cover the entire leg. |She wore halfstockings and a pair of Mary Janes. 她穿着短袜和一双玛丽珍鞋。|Synonyms: ankle sock, sock, bobby sock|Antonyms: stocking, thigh-highs|Noun
