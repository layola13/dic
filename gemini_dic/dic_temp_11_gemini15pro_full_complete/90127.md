hot-wire:/ˌhɑːtˈwaɪər/ /ˌhɒtˈwaɪə(r)/ | v. |启动(汽车)，热启动，用导线使…短路 | To start (a car) by connecting the ignition wires directly, bypassing the ignition switch.  | He hot-wired the stolen car and sped off. 他启动了偷来的车，飞驰而去。| The thieves hot-wired the car to get it started. 小偷用导线短路启动了汽车。 |近义词：bypass, short-circuit |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用正常的启动方式表达) start normally |词性: 动词

hot-wired:/ˌhɑːtˈwaɪərd/ /ˌhɒtˈwaɪəd/ | adj. |兴奋的，紧张的 | Excited, stimulated, or nervous. | I felt hot-wired after the thrilling chase.  惊险的追逐之后，我感到很兴奋。| She was hot-wired with anticipation for the concert. 她对音乐会充满了期待，激动不已。|近义词：excited, thrilled, stimulated |反义词: calm, relaxed |词性: 形容词
