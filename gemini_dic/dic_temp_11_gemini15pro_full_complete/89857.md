hornwort:/ˈhɔːrnwɜːrt/| n. |金鱼藻；角苔|A submerged aquatic plant with narrow, forked leaves, often used in aquariums.  A type of non-vascular plant (bryophyte) that grows in damp environments. |例句：The hornwort provided good cover for the baby fish in the tank.  鱼缸里的金鱼藻为小鱼提供了很好的掩护。

Hornworts are commonly found in damp soil. 角苔常见于潮湿的土壤中。|近义词：coontail (for aquatic hornwort), bryophyte (for non-vascular hornwort)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology: From "horn" + "wort" (Old English wyrt, meaning "plant," "herb," or "root"). The name refers to the elongated, horn-like sporophytes that emerge from the gametophyte.
