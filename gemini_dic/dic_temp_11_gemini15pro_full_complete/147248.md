overmelodiousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˌmɛloʊdiəsnəsɪz/
|n.| 过分悦耳; 过分旋律化 | Excessive melodiousness; an overabundance of melody. | The overmelodiousnesses of the music became cloying after a while.  这音乐过分的悦耳，过了一会儿就变得腻人了。|近义词: sweetness, euphony |反义词: dissonance, cacophony |noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over, beyond") + "melodious" (from Late Latin melodiosus, from Greek melōidos "melodic," from melos "song, melody" + -ous) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), forming nouns of state or quality).
