forswat:/fɔːrˈswɒt/ |v. (past tense forswate; past participle forsworn or forswat)| 放弃，抛弃；发誓戒除 | To renounce or give up something, especially by an oath; to abjure.| He forswat his former life of crime and dedicated himself to helping others. 他发誓放弃以前犯罪的生活，并致力于帮助他人。|近义词： renounce, abjure, forsake, relinquish |反义词： adopt, embrace, maintain |词性：动词

Etymology:
From Old English *forswerian "to swear falsely, perjure oneself," later "to renounce upon oath," from for- "against, away" + swerian "to swear."  The past tense form "forswat" is archaic and rarely used in modern English.
