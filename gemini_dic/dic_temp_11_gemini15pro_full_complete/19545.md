biformity:/baɪˈfɔːrmɪti/ | n. |双形性，二形性|The existence of two distinct forms or types within a species or group. |This plant exhibits biformity, with both male and female flowers on the same individual.  这种植物表现出双形性，在同一个体上同时具有雄花和雌花。| dimorphism, duality | uniformity, similarity | noun
