condurango:/kɒnˈdjʊəræŋɡoʊ/ | n. | 康杜兰哥树皮；康杜兰哥树；康杜兰哥提取物 | The bark of the South American vine *Marsdenia condurango*, used as a tonic and stomachic.  It is also sometimes used in alternative medicine as a treatment for cancer, although there is no scientific evidence to support this use. |  Condurango bark is traditionally used to stimulate appetite. 康杜兰哥树皮传统上用于刺激食欲。 |  Condurango has been studied for its potential anti-cancer properties. 康杜兰哥已被研究其潜在的抗癌特性。 | 近义词: (as a bitter tonic) gentian, quassia | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
