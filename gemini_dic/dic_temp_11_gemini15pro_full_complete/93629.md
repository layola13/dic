imbody:/ɪmˈbɒdi/ (UK), /ɪmˈbɑːdi/ (US)| v. | 体现；使具体化；包括；包含 | To express or represent (something) in a physical form.  To incorporate as a part of something. |  This painting embodies the spirit of the Renaissance. 这幅画体现了文艺复兴的精神。

|The new car embodies the latest technology. 这款新车融合了最新的科技。 |  synonyms:  represent, personify, epitomize, exemplify, incorporate, contain, include | antonyms: exclude, omit | verb
