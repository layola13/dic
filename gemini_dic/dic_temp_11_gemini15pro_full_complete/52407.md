diddums:/ˈdɪdəmz/| n.| 小宝贝；小可怜儿；(表示轻蔑)娇气的人|A child's word for a hurt, pain, or something lost or damaged. Also used condescendingly for someone who complains about trivial matters.| "Aw, diddums, did you fall down?"  “噢，小可怜儿，你摔倒了吗？” |Near Synonyms: poor thing, sweetie, darling (when used condescendingly: crybaby, whiner)|Antonyms: tough guy, stoic|noun
diddums:/ˈdɪdəmz/| int.| (表示同情或轻蔑)哎呀；可怜的|An exclamation expressing sympathy or mock sympathy.| "Diddums! Does your tummy hurt?" “哎呀！你的肚子疼吗？”|Near Synonyms:  aw, oh dear (condescendingly:  boo-hoo) |Antonyms:  Good! Serves you right!|interjection
