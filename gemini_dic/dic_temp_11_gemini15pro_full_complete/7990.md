anthologized: /ænˈθɑːlədʒaɪzd/ | v. |  选入文集；被收入选集 | included in an anthology |  Her short story was anthologized in "The Best American Short Stories". 她的短篇小说被选入了《美国最佳短篇小说集》。 | 近义词: included, collected, compiled | 反义词: excluded, omitted | 词性: 动词

词源: From anthology + -ize.  Anthology comes from the Greek "anthologia" meaning "collection of flowers" (hence, a collection of literary pieces).
