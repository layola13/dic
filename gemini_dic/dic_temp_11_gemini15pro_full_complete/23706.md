brasserie:/bræsəri/ | n. |啤酒屋；小餐馆|A restaurant serving beer, wine, and simple food.|例句：We had dinner at a charming brasserie in Paris. 我们在巴黎一家迷人的啤酒屋吃了晚餐。|例句：The brasserie offers a wide selection of Belgian beers. 这家啤酒屋提供各种各样的比利时啤酒。|近义词：restaurant, cafe, bistro, pub|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *brasserie*, from *brasser* "to brew," from *bras* "malt," ultimately from Gaulish *brace* "barley".
