mit:/mɪt/ | n. | 手套 (尤指分指手套) | A covering for the hand having a separate sheath for each finger and the thumb. | He lost one of his mits. 他丢了一只手套。| 近义词: glove | 反义词: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mitte*, from Old English *mitte* (“mitten”), from Proto-Germanic *mitōn* (“mitten”). Cognate with Dutch *mitten* (“mitten”), German *Mitten* (“mitten”), Swedish *vante* (“mitten, glove”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meyth₂-* (“to exchange”).
