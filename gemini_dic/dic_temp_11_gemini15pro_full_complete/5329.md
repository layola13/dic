alloxanate: /ˌælɒkˈsænɪt/ /ˌælɑːkˈsænɪt/
noun
阿脲酸盐
a salt or ester of alloxanic acid. Alloxanic acid is formed by the oxidation of uric acid.
The alloxanate was used in the experiment.  阿脲酸盐被用于实验中。
Near synonyms:  urate, purinate (related compounds)
No direct antonyms for a specific chemical salt.
