concretized:/ˈkɒŋkriːtaɪzd/ /ˈkɑːŋkriːtaɪzd/
verb
具体化，使具体化
to make something concrete or definite; to give form to an idea or plan.
The architect concretized his vision by creating a detailed model.  建筑师通过创建一个详细的模型来具体化他的设想。
His vague ideas finally concretized into a solid proposal. 他模糊的想法最终具体化成一个具体的提案。
Synonyms: solidify, materialize, realize, actualize
Antonyms: abstract, generalize, conceptualize
词性: 动词
