equalling:/ˈiːkwəlɪŋ/ | v. | 等于，相当于 | Being equal to something; amounting to the same value as something else. |  The cost of the repairs equalled the value of the car. 修理费用等于汽车的价值。| Synonyms: matching, corresponding, being equivalent to | Antonyms: differing from, exceeding, falling short of | verb

equalling:/ˈiːkwəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 相等的，等同的 (less common usage) | Being the same in quantity, size, degree, or value. |  His share of the profits was equalling to mine.  他的利润份额与我的相等。 | Synonyms: equal, equivalent, identical | Antonyms: unequal, different, disparate | adjective
