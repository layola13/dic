nonabstemiously: /ˌnɒnæbˈstiːmiəsli/ | adv. | 不节制地，放纵地 |  In a manner that is not characterized by abstinence or moderation, especially in eating or drinking.  It signifies indulgence or excess. | He ate nonabstemiously at the buffet, sampling everything. 他在自助餐上大吃特吃，每样都尝了一遍。|  The king lived nonabstemiously, indulging in feasts and revelry. 国王生活放纵，沉迷于盛宴和狂欢。 | 近义词: immoderately, excessively, intemperately | 反义词: abstemiously, temperately, moderately | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (not) + "abstemious" (moderate, sparing in eating and drinking) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Abstemious" comes from Latin "abstemius," from "ab-" (from, away from) + "temetum" (strong drink).
