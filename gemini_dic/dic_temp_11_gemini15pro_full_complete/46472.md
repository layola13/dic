ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *daili, *dayli, from Old English dæġlīċe (“daily”), equivalent to day +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots dailie (“daily”), West Frisian deilich (“daily”), Dutch dagelijks (“daily”), German täglich (“daily”).
