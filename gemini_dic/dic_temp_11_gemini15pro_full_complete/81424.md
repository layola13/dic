grate:/ɡreɪt/| v. n.|磨碎，擦碎；使人烦躁| grate cheese 磨碎奶酪 | The noise grated on my nerves. 噪音刺激我的神经。|近义词：shred, grind, rasp|反义词: soothe, please|词性:动词

grated:/ˈɡreɪtɪd/| adj.|磨碎的，擦碎的| grated cheese 磨碎的奶酪 | I prefer grated Parmesan on my pasta. 我喜欢在我的意大利面上撒磨碎的帕玛森奶酪。|近义词：shredded, ground|反义词: whole, ungrated|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *graten, from Old North French grater, gratter (“to scrape, scratch”),  from Frankish *kratton, from Proto-Germanic *krattaną (“to scratch, scrape”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“to cut”). Cognate with Old High German krazōn (“to scratch”), Icelandic krassa (“to scratch”).
