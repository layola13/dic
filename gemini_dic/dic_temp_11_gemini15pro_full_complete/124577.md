mourning:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɔːnɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.| 悲痛，哀悼；服丧|The expression of sorrow for someone’s death.|例句：The whole nation was in mourning after the president's death.  举国上下都在为总统的逝世而哀悼。

例句：She wore black clothing as a sign of mourning. 她穿着黑色的衣服以示哀悼。|近义词：grief, sorrow, bereavement|反义词：celebration, rejoicing|词性: 名词, 形容词

mourn:/mɔːrn/ (US) /mɔːn/ (UK)| v.| 哀悼，悲痛|To feel or express sorrow for the death or loss of someone or something.|例句：They mourned the loss of their beloved pet. 他们为失去心爱的宠物而悲伤。

例句：He mourned his friend's passing. 他哀悼朋友的去世。|近义词：grieve, lament, sorrow|反义词：celebrate, rejoice|词性: 动词
