coronagraph:/ˈkɔːrənəɡræf, ˈkɒrənəɡrɑːf/ | n. |日冕仪|An instrument that blocks light from the disk of the sun, permitting observation of the faint solar corona. | Astronomers use a coronagraph to study the sun's corona. 天文学家用日冕仪来研究太阳的日冕。

The coronagraph allowed scientists to observe the solar flares. 日冕仪使科学家能够观察太阳耀斑。

|近义词:  heliograph (less common and refers specifically to a device for signaling using sunlight)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
