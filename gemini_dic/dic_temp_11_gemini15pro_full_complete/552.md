abort's:/əˈbɔːrts/ | n. | 流产；失败；夭折 |  A pregnancy that ends before the fetus can survive outside the womb, either naturally or induced. Also, the premature termination of a process or project. | The doctor confirmed the abort's cause was a chromosomal abnormality. 医生确认了流产的原因是染色体异常。| synonyms: miscarriage, termination, failure | antonyms: birth, success, completion | noun

abort:/əˈbɔːrt/ | v. |  中止；流产；夭折 | To end a pregnancy prematurely. Also, to stop a process or project before it is completed. |  She chose to abort the pregnancy. 她选择终止妊娠。 | synonyms: terminate, cancel, halt | antonyms: continue, proceed, complete | verb
