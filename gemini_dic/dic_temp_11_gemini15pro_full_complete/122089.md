mistiest:/ˈmɪstiɪst/| adj.|最雾蒙蒙的|Covered with or obscured by mist, especially to a greater degree than something else.|例句：It was the mistiest morning I'd ever seen. 这是我见过的雾气最浓的早晨。|近义词：foggiest, haziest|反义词：clearest, brightest|词性:形容词
词源：From mist + -y + -est.  Ultimately from Old English *mist * "dark, obscure."
