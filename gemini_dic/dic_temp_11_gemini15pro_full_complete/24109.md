brew-ups:/ˈbruː ʌps/ (UK) /ˈbruː ʌps/ (US)| n.| 泡的茶，煮的咖啡 (尤指英式英语)|A brew-up is a hot drink, typically tea, made in a simple way, often in large quantities. It's particularly common in British English.|例句：We stopped for a quick brew-up before continuing our hike. 我们在继续徒步旅行前停下来快速地泡了杯茶。

例句：He made a brew-up for everyone on the building site. 他为建筑工地上所有的人泡了茶。

近义词：cuppa (UK, informal), tea, coffee|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps cold drink)|词性:noun
