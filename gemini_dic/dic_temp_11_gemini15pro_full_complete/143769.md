outmeasured:/ˌaʊtˈmeʒərd/
verb
超过，胜过
surpassed in measurement, especially height; exceeded
例句：The new skyscraper outmeasured all the other buildings in the city.  这栋新的摩天大楼比城市里所有其他建筑物都要高。

例句：Her performance outmeasured everyone's expectations. 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。

近义词：surpassed, exceeded, outstripped, outdone
反义词： dwarfed, overshadowed

词性: v.
