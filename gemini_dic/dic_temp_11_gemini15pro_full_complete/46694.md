damson:/ˈdæmzən/ | n. | 西洋李子，丹森李子 | A small, dark purple plum, often used for making preserves. |  She made a delicious damson jam. 她做了一罐美味的西洋李子酱。

The damsons are ripe for picking this year. 今年的西洋李子成熟可以采摘了。 | 近义词: plum, prune | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fruit) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *damascene (recorded as *damescen, damassin), from Late Latin damascenum, accusative of damascenus "of Damascus," from Damascus, the capital of Syria.
