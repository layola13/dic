antistimulants:/ˌæntaɪˈstɪmjələnts/ | n. | 抗兴奋剂，抑制剂 |  Substances that reduce stimulation of the central nervous system. These drugs are often used to treat conditions like ADHD and sometimes narcolepsy. |  The doctor prescribed antistimulants to help him sleep. 医生给他开了抗兴奋剂来帮助他睡眠。 | Synonyms: depressants, sedatives | Antonyms: stimulants,兴奋剂 | noun

antistimulant:/ˌæntaɪˈstɪmjələnt/ | adj. | 抗兴奋的，抑制性的 | Having the property of reducing stimulation of the central nervous system. | Antistimulant medication can be helpful for managing hyperactivity. 抗兴奋药物有助于控制多动症。 | Synonyms: calming, soothing, tranquilizing | Antonyms: stimulating, exciting  | adjective
