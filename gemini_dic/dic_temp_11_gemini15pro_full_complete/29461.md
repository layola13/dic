catalyze:/ˈkætəlaɪz/| v. |催化，促进|To cause or accelerate a process or reaction, often without itself being consumed or changed.|The enzyme catalyzes the breakdown of starch into sugar. 这种酶能催化淀粉分解成糖。|synonyms: accelerate, expedite, facilitate, promote, stimulate|antonyms: hinder, inhibit, obstruct, impede|verb

catalyze:/ˈkætəlaɪz/| v. |催化，促进|To bring about or cause to happen, especially something significant.|His presence catalyzed a change in the group's dynamics. 他的出现促成了小组动态的变化。|synonyms: precipitate, trigger, spark, initiate|antonyms: prevent, suppress, stifle|verb
