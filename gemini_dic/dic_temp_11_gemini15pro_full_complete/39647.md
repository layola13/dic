conservation:/ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 保护，保存；守恒 |The conservation of wildlife is crucial for maintaining biodiversity.  野生动物的保护对于维持生物多样性至关重要。|例句：The museum is dedicated to the conservation of ancient artifacts.  这家博物馆致力于古代文物的保护。|近义词：preservation, protection, maintenance|反义词：destruction, waste, depletion|词性:名词

conservation:/ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃən/ | n. | (对自然资源的)保护，(对能源的)节约 |Energy conservation is important for protecting the environment.  节约能源对于保护环境很重要。|例句：Water conservation is essential in dry regions.  在干旱地区，节水至关重要。|近义词：saving, husbandry|反义词：waste, consumption|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin conservatio(n-), from conservare ‘preserve’.
