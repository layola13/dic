detractor's:/dɪˈtræktərz/| n.|批评者；贬低者；诽谤者|Belonging to or associated with a detractor; a person who disparages someone or something.  It signifies possession.  The detractor's comments were unfounded.|The detractor's harsh words damaged the artist's reputation. 诽谤者尖刻的言辞损害了艺术家的声誉。|Synonyms: critic's, attacker's, slanderer's|Antonyms: supporter's, admirer's, advocate's|noun (possessive form)
