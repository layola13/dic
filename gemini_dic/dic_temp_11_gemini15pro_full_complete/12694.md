assert:/əˈsɜːrt/| v.|断言；维护；坚持|To state or declare positively and often forcefully or aggressively.  To maintain or defend (claims, rights, etc.).|He asserted his innocence. 他断言自己是无辜的。|Synonyms: declare, affirm, maintain, avow|Antonyms: deny, contradict, refute|verb

assert:/əˈsɜːrt/| n.|断言；主张|A positive statement or declaration, often without support or proof.|His assertion of innocence was not believed. 他声称自己无辜，但没有人相信。|Synonyms: declaration, affirmation, statement, claim|Antonyms: denial, refutation, contradiction|noun
