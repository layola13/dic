chark:/tʃɑːrk/| v. n.| 烧焦，炭化；炭，焦炭| To burn (something, especially wood) so that it becomes black and brittle.  The charred remains of the house. |例句：The wood charked in the intense heat.  木头在高温下烧焦了。

|例句：The steak was charked on the outside but still rare inside. 牛排外面烧焦了，但里面还是生的。|近义词：char, scorch, sear|反义词: extinguish, douse |词性:动词，名词

chark:/tʃɑːrk/| n.| 炭，焦炭|The black, brittle material that remains after wood or other organic matter has been burned. |例句：The fire reduced the building to chark. 火灾把建筑物烧成了焦炭。

|例句：He used chark to draw a picture. 他用木炭画了一幅画。|近义词：charcoal, cinder, ember|反义词: fuel, wood |词性:名词
