o's:/oʊz/ | n. | 某物/某人所属的东西（复数）| Plural possessive form, short for of + noun.  Used to indicate possession or belonging to something or someone. | The cat licked its paws. (its = belonging to it)  猫舔了它的爪子。

The dog wagged its tail. (its = belonging to it) 狗摇着它的尾巴。 | 近义词: belonging to, possessed by | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun (possessive pronoun)
