itchier:/ˈɪtʃiər/ (US), /ˈɪtʃɪər/ (UK)| adj.|更痒的|Comparative form of "itchy," meaning more itchy.|My mosquito bite is itchier today than it was yesterday. 我的蚊子包今天比昨天更痒了。|The wool sweater made his skin itchier. 羊毛衫让他的皮肤更痒了。|近义词：more itchy|反义词：less itchy|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *gyccean "to itch," of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old Norse gikka "to twitch, totter." The comparative form "-ier" comes from French "-ier."
