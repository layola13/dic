disbelieved:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvd/
verb
不信，怀疑
refused to accept as true or real.
I disbelieved his story about winning the lottery. 我不相信他中了彩票的故事。
synonyms: doubted, questioned, mistrusted
antonyms: believed, trusted, accepted
VERB

disbelieve:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːv/
verb
不信，怀疑
refuse to accept as true or real.

antonyms: believe, trust, accept
VERB
