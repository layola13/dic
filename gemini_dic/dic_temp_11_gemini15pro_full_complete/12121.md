artsman:/ˈɑːrtsmən/| n.|工匠，技工|A skilled worker who practices a craft.|例句：The artsman crafted a beautiful wooden chair.  这位工匠制作了一把漂亮的木椅。|例句：The artsman's skill was evident in the intricate details of his work.  工匠的技艺体现在他作品的复杂细节中。|近义词：artisan, craftsman, artificer|反义词:amateur, novice|词性:noun

词源:
From Middle English *artesman, equivalent to art +‎ -s +‎ man.  Compare Dutch aartsman, German Artsmann, Danish artsmand.
