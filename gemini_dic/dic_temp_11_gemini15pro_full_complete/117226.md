meatloaf:/ˈmiːtloʊf/| n.|肉糕，肉饼|A dish consisting of ground meat, breadcrumbs, and seasonings, shaped into a loaf and baked or fried.|My mom makes a delicious meatloaf every Sunday. 我妈妈每个星期天都做美味的肉饼。|近义词：meatball, ground meat dish|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *metelof*, from *mete* (meat) + *lof* (loaf).  First recorded use circa 1450.
