defroster:/diːˈfrɒstər/ (US) /diːˈfrɒstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|除霜器，除霜装置|A device that removes frost or ice, typically from a car windshield or a refrigerator.|例句：I turned on the defroster to clear the ice from the windshield. 我打开了除霜器来清除挡风玻璃上的冰。|近义词：heater, warmer|反义词：freezer|词性：名词

defrosters:/diːˈfrɒstərz/ (US) /diːˈfrɒstəz/ (UK)| n.|除霜器，除霜装置（复数）|Plural form of "defroster," referring to multiple devices that remove frost or ice.|例句：The car's defrosters were working overtime in the freezing weather. 在寒冷的天气里，汽车的除霜器一直在超负荷工作。|近义词：heaters, warmers|反义词：freezers|词性：名词
