disslander:/dɪˈslændər/
noun
诽谤者；造谣者
A person who disseminates slander; a slanderer.
He was a known disslander, spreading rumors about everyone in town. 他是镇上有名的造谣者，到处散布关于每个人的谣言。
近义词:slanderer, defamer, libeler, calumniator
反义词:advocate, defender
