nonilluminations:/ˌnɒnɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃənz/
noun
非启迪；未被启迪之物；未被阐明之物
Lack of enlightenment or understanding; things that are not illuminated or clarified.
The artist's work explored the nonilluminations of the human psyche.  这位艺术家的作品探索了人类心灵中未被启迪的部分。
His writings were full of obscure references and nonilluminations. 他的作品充满了晦涩的指涉和未被阐明的内容。
Near Antonyms: illuminations, insights, revelations
Antonyms: enlightenments, clarifications
