fertilise: /ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈfɜːrtəlaɪz/ (US)| vt.|使肥沃；施肥；使受精|To make (land) produce more by adding chemicals or organic substances; to cause an egg or seed to start developing into a new young animal or plant by joining it with a male cell. |例句：Farmers fertilise their crops to increase yield. 农民给农作物施肥以提高产量。

The flowers are fertilised by bees. 花朵由蜜蜂授粉。|近义词：enrich, nourish, inseminate|反义词：sterilize|词性：动词
