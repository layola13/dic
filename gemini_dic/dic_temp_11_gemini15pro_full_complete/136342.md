nonresilience:/ˌnɒnrɪˈzɪliəns/
n.|非弹性，非韧性，无恢复力|The nonresilience of the material makes it unsuitable for this application. 这种材料缺乏弹性，使其不适合用于此应用。|例句：The city's nonresilience in the face of the earthquake was shocking.  这座城市在地震面前缺乏恢复力，令人震惊。

|例句：The nonresilience of the ecosystem led to its collapse.  生态系统的脆弱导致了它的崩溃。
|近义词：fragility, vulnerability, weakness|反义词: resilience, elasticity, toughness|词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
resilience (noun): From Latin resiliens, present participle of resilire "to spring back, rebound," from re- "back" + salire "to leap."
