nonmelodiously: /ˌnɒn məˈləʊdiəsli/ (British) /ˌnɑːn məˈloʊdiəsli/ (American) | adv. | 不悦耳地，不和谐地 |  In a way that is not melodious; lacking melody or musical quality.  It describes something that is not pleasant to listen to because it lacks a tuneful or harmonious quality. | The choir sang nonmelodiously, making the audience cringe.  唱诗班唱得很难听，让观众感到畏缩。| The rusty gate creaked nonmelodiously as it opened. 生锈的大门打开时发出刺耳的声音。 | 近义词: discordantly, inharmoniously, unmusically | 反义词: melodiously, harmoniously, musically | 词性: 副词
