contralto:/kənˈtræltoʊ/ (US) /kənˈtrɑːltəʊ/ (UK)| n.|女低音；女低音歌手|A contralto is the lowest female singing voice, or a singer with this type of voice.|The contralto's rich voice filled the hall.|近义词:alto, low voice|反义词:soprano, high voice|词性:noun

contraltos:/kənˈtræltoʊz/ (US) /kənˈtrɑːltəʊz/ (UK)| n.|女低音们；女低音歌手们|Plural form of contralto, referring to multiple singers with the lowest female singing voice.|The contraltos blended their voices beautifully in the chorus.|近义词:altos, low voices|反义词:sopranos, high voices|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *contralto*, from *contra* (against, opposite) + *alto* (high).  Originally referring to the voice part opposite or below the *alto* (which originally referred to the highest voice).
