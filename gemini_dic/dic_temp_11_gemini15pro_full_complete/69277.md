feathers:/ˈfeðərz/ (US), /ˈfeðəz/ (UK)| n. |羽毛|The soft, light covering of a bird's body, consisting of numerous small, interlocking parts.|例句：The bird preened its feathers. 鸟儿梳理它的羽毛。

例句：The pillow is filled with goose feathers. 枕头里填充的是鹅毛。|近义词：plumage, down|反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 scales 鳞片, fur 毛皮 等根据语境选择)|词性:名词
