earl:/ɜːl/ (British), /ɝːl/ (American)| n.|伯爵|A British nobleman ranking above a viscount and below a marquess.|例句：The Earl of Sandwich is said to have invented the sandwich.  据说三明治伯爵发明了三明治。

例句：He was given the title of Earl of Essex. 他被授予埃塞克斯伯爵的称号。|近义词：nobleman, lord, peer|反义词：commoner|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *eorl, from Old Norse jarl "chieftain, warrior," of uncertain ultimate origin. Cognate with Danish and Swedish jarl.  Replaced native Old English ealdorman.
