foreloader:/ˈfɔːrˌloʊdər/| n.|前置装载机|A machine, typically a tractor or other heavy vehicle, equipped with a bucket or other attachment at the front for loading materials such as earth, gravel, or snow.|The foreloader scooped up the pile of gravel and deposited it in the truck. 前置装载机铲起一堆碎石，然后将其倒入卡车中。|
The construction crew used a foreloader to clear the debris. 施工人员使用前置装载机清理废墟。|Synonyms: front-end loader, loader, bucket loader|Antonyms: N/A|noun
