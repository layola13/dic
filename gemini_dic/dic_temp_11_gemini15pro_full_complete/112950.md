lugubriously:/luːˈɡuːbriəsli/
|adv.|
|悲哀地，忧郁地|
|In a mournful or gloomy manner; sadly.|
|He shook his head lugubriously. 他悲伤地摇了摇头。|
|The dog howled lugubriously at the moon. 那只狗对着月亮悲鸣。|
|mournfully, sadly, gloomily, dolefully|
|cheerfully, happily, joyfully, brightly|
|副词|

Etymology:
From Late Latin *lūgubriōsus, from Latin lūgubris (“mournful, doleful”), from lūgeō (“to mourn, lament”).
