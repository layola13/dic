officiation: /əˌfɪʃiˈeɪʃən/
noun
主持，执行仪式；司仪工作
the act of officiating at a religious service or other ceremony.
The officiation at the wedding was conducted by the groom's uncle.  婚礼由新郎的叔叔主持。
officiating, administration, supervision, conducting
negligence, inaction
