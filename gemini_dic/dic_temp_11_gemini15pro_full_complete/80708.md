gorillas:/ɡəˈrɪləz/ | n. |大猩猩|A large ape, the largest living primate, found in the forests of central Africa. |  Example 1: The gorillas in the zoo were fascinating to watch.  动物园里的大猩猩很吸引人观看。

Example 2:  Researchers are studying the social behavior of gorillas in the wild.  研究人员正在研究野生大猩猩的社会行为。 | 近义词:apes | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider smaller primates like monkeys) | 词性:noun


Etymology: From the Greek word *Gorillai*, meaning "hairy women," reported by Hanno the Navigator, a Carthaginian explorer of the 5th century BC.
