bounce-around:/ˈbaʊns əˈraʊnd/ | phrasal verb | 到处走动；反复讨论；征求意见 | To travel from place to place; to discuss an idea with several people to get their opinions. |  We bounced around Europe for a month. 我们在欧洲各地游玩了一个月。

| 近义词: travel around, knock about, discuss, canvass | 反义词: stay put, settle down | 词性: 动词短语

bounce-around:/ˈbaʊns əˈraʊnd/ | n. |  (非正式) 讨论；四处走动 | An informal discussion or the act of traveling from place to place. | The team had a bounce-around about the new project. 团队就新项目进行了一番讨论。

| 近义词: discussion, travels, wanderings | 反义词: stillness, inactivity | 词性: 名词 (较少使用)
