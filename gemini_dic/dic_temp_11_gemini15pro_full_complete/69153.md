favus:/ˈfeɪvəs/| n. |蜂窝疮，黄癣|A contagious skin disease, especially of the scalp, caused by a fungus and characterized by dry, sulfur-yellow, cup-shaped crusts.|Favus is a highly contagious fungal infection. 黄癣是一种高度传染性的真菌感染。|The child was diagnosed with favus. 这个孩子被诊断出患有黄癣。|近义词：ringworm, tinea|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑healthy scalp 健康的头皮)|词性:noun

词源: From Latin *favus* ("honeycomb"), from its appearance.
