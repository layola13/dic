incubate:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪt/ (US) /ˈɪnkjʊbeɪt/ (UK)| v. n.|孵化，培养，潜伏|to keep something warm so that it can develop, or to develop slowly|The eggs are incubated by the female bird. 母鸟孵蛋。|近义词：foster, develop, nurture, hatch|反义词: neglect, stifle|verb

incubate:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪt/ (US) /ˈɪnkjʊbeɪt/ (UK)| n.|潜伏期，孵化期|the period of time during which something is incubated|The disease has a long incubation period. 这种疾病有很长的潜伏期。|近义词：gestation, latency|反义词: - |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incubatus*, past participle of incubare (“to lie on, brood, hatch”), from in- (“on”) + cubare (“to lie down”).
