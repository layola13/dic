floccillation:/ˌflɒkɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. |絮凝；结絮；小毛丛|The process of forming small clumps or flakes, especially in a liquid. This can be seen in the formation of clouds or the curdling of milk.| The floccillation of the protein in the milk was caused by the addition of acid. 牛奶中蛋白质的絮凝是由添加酸引起的。 |  aggregation, clumping, coagulation | dispersion, separation | noun
