decahedron:/ˌdɛkəˈhiːdrən/ | n. |十面体|A solid figure with ten plane faces.|A decahedron is a three-dimensional shape with ten flat surfaces. 十面体是一个具有十个平面的三维形状。|The dice used in some role-playing games are decahedrons. 一些角色扮演游戏中使用的骰子是十面体。|polyhedron, ten-sided figure| |noun
decahedron:/ˌdɛkəˈhiːdrən/ | n. |十面体|A solid figure with ten plane faces.|A regular decahedron has congruent equilateral pentagons as faces. 正十面体具有全等的等边五边形作为面。|He was fascinated by the complex geometry of the decahedron. 他被十面体的复杂几何形状所吸引。|polyhedron, ten-sided figure| |noun
