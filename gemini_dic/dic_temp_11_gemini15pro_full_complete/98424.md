inspectability:/ɪnˌspɛktəˈbɪləti/
n.|可检查性|The quality of being easily inspected or examined.  The ease with which something can be thoroughly checked or assessed.|The bridge’s inspectability was compromised by the rust and peeling paint. 这座桥由于生锈和油漆剥落而难以检查。|Synonyms: examinability, reviewability, auditability|Antonyms: obscurity, inaccessibility|noun
