out-roguing:/ˌaʊtˈroʊɡɪŋ/
verb
胜过，比...更无赖
Surpassing someone in roguish or unscrupulous behavior.
He out-rogued the other con men by selling them fake maps to buried treasure. 他通过向其他骗子出售埋藏宝藏的假地图，在无赖行径上胜过了他们。
Near Synonyms: outsmarting, outwitting, outmaneuvering
Antonyms: being outsmarted, being outwitted
词性:动词

out-roguing:/ˌaʊtˈroʊɡɪŋ/
noun
胜过，比...更无赖的行为
The act of surpassing someone in roguish or unscrupulous behavior.
Her out-roguing of her rivals ensured her victory in the election. 她胜过对手的无赖行为确保了她在选举中的胜利。
Near Synonyms: trickery, deception, chicanery
Antonyms: honesty, integrity
词性:名词
