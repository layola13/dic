fingerboards:/ˈfɪŋɡəbɔːrdz/| n. |指板|The part of a stringed instrument, such as a guitar, violin, or cello, against which the strings are pressed to produce different notes.  A fingerboard is usually a thin, flat piece of wood.|He polished the fingerboard of his guitar. 他擦拭了他吉他上的指板。|Synonyms: fretboard (primarily for guitars and similar instruments)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

fingerboard:/ˈfɪŋɡəbɔːrd/| n. |(尤指滑板运动用的)指板|A miniature skateboard ridden with the fingers.|He landed a difficult trick on his fingerboard. 他用指板完成了一个高难度动作。|Synonyms: mini skateboard|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
