finder:/ˈfaɪndər/| n.|发现者; 查找器; 寻物器; 取景器|A person or thing that finds something.  Someone who discovers something. A device in a camera that shows the photographer the area that will be included in the picture.|例句：He was the finder of the lost treasure. 他是失落宝藏的发现者。

例句：The viewfinder on my camera is broken. 我相机上的取景器坏了。|近义词：discoverer, locator, seeker|反义词：loser|词性：名词
