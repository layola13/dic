hebdomadal:/hɛbˈdɒmədəl/ /hɛbˈdɑːmədəl/| adj.|每周的；每七天一次的|Occurring or done every seven days; weekly. |例句：The hebdomadal meeting was held every Monday. 每周例会都在星期一举行。

例句：The hebdomadal newsletter kept everyone informed about company news.  每周的通讯让每个人都了解公司新闻。|近义词：weekly, septenary|反义词:daily, monthly|词性:形容词

hebdomadal:/hɛbˈdɒmədəl/ /hɛbˈdɑːmədəl/| n.|周刊；周报|A weekly publication, especially a periodical.|例句：He subscribed to several hebdomadals. 他订阅了几份周刊。

例句：The hebdomadal provided insightful commentary on current events. 这份周刊对时事提供了有见地的评论。|近义词：weekly, periodical|反义词:daily, monthly|词性:名词
