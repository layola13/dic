invades:/ɪnˈveɪdz/| v. |入侵，侵略，侵犯|enters a place in a forceful or unwanted way, often in large numbers|例句：The army invaded the neighboring country. 军队入侵了邻国。

例句：The cancer cells invaded the surrounding tissues. 癌细胞侵入了周围的组织。

近义词：attacks, conquers, encroaches, occupies, overruns|反义词：retreats, withdraws, defends, protects|词性：动词
