nonracially:/ˌnɒnˈreɪʃəli/ /ˌnɑːnˈreɪʃəli/
adjective
非种族地；不分种族地
Without regard to race; in a way that does not discriminate based on race.
例句：The scholarships are awarded nonracially. 奖学金的颁发不分种族。

例句：The organization strives to promote nonracially biased hiring practices. 该组织致力于推广不带种族偏见的招聘实践。

近义词：inclusively, equitably, fairly
反义词：racially, discriminatorily

词性
adjective
