近义词：end, stopping, termination, discontinuation |反义词：commencement, beginning, start, continuation | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cessatio, from Latin cessare "to cease," frequentative of cedere "to yield, go, withdraw."
