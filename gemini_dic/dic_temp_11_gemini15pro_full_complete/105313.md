kibitz:/ˈkɪbɪts/| v.| kibitzed (past tense), kibitzing (present participle), kibitzes (third person singular)|插嘴，瞎指挥，(尤指纸牌游戏中)旁观支招|To offer unsolicited advice or criticism, especially in a card game. |He kibitzed throughout the poker game, annoying the other players. 他在整个扑克游戏中瞎指挥，惹恼了其他玩家。|Synonyms: meddle, interfere, butt in, offer unsolicited advice |Antonyms: mind one's own business, stay out of it|verb

kibitzer:/ˈkɪbɪtsər/| n.|kibitzers (plural)|爱管闲事的人，旁观支招者|A person who kibitzes. |The kibitzers offered unwanted advice to the chess players.  这些爱管闲事的人向棋手们提供了不必要的建议。|Synonyms: meddler, interferer, busybody |Antonyms:  -|noun
