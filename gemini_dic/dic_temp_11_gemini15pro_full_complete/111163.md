lithobilic:/ˌlɪθəˈbaɪlɪk/ | adj. |  喜石生的，石生的 | Growing or living on or in rocks.  | Example: Certain lichens are lithobilic, thriving on the surfaces of exposed rocks. 某些地衣是石生的，在裸露的岩石表面茁壮成长。
Example:  Lithobilic organisms have unique adaptations to survive in this harsh environment. 石生生物具有独特的适应能力，可以在这种恶劣的环境中生存。 | 近义词: saxicolous, rupestral, rock-dwelling | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like terrestrial, aquatic, arboreal for organisms living in other environments) | 词性: adjective
