immortalize:/ɪˈmɔːrtəˌlaɪz/
verb
使不朽，使永垂不朽
The artist immortalized her in a painting.  这位艺术家在一幅画中使她永垂不朽。
例句：Shakespeare's works have immortalized him. 莎士比亚的作品使他名垂千古。

例句：This battle was immortalized in a famous poem. 这场战役在一首著名的诗中被永久传颂。

近义词：memorialize, commemorate, perpetuate
反义词：forget, ignore
词性：v.

词源：From Late Latin *immortalizare, from Latin immortalis (“immortal”) + -izare.  来自晚期拉丁语 *immortalizare，源自拉丁语 immortalis（“不朽的”）+ -izare。
