mesmerical:/mɛzˈmɛrɪkəl/ | adj. | 催眠的，迷人的，令人着迷的 |  Producing or tending to produce a hypnotic effect; fascinating; captivating.  Derived from the name of Franz Mesmer, an Austrian physician who popularized hypnotism. |  The mesmerical performance left the audience spellbound.  这场催眠般的表演让观众着迷。 | synonyms: hypnotic, captivating, enchanting, fascinating, spellbinding | antonyms:  repulsive, boring, mundane, tedious, uninteresting | adjective
