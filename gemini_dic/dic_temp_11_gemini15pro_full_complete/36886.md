colorers:/ˈkʌlərərz/
noun
着色者; 涂色者
People who add color to something, typically using crayons, paints, or other coloring tools.
The colorers were busy decorating the classroom for the party. 着色者们正忙着为派对装饰教室。
colorists, painters, artists
non-colorers
