aventurine:/əˈvɛntʃərɪn/ | n. | 砂金石，东陵石 |A translucent quartz spangled with bits of mica, hematite, or other mineral.  A variety of quartz containing small inclusions, especially of mica, that cause a glistening effect. |例句：She wore a necklace made of aventurine. 她戴了一条砂金石项链。

例句：The aventurine sparkled in the sunlight.  阳光下，砂金石闪闪发光。|近义词：sunstone, goldstone |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
