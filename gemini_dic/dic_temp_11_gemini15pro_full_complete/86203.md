heathenish:/ˈhiːðənɪʃ/| adj.|异教的；野蛮的；不文明的|Characteristic of heathens; pagan; irreligious; uncivilized; barbarous.|Example 1:  His heathenish behavior shocked the church community. 他的野蛮行为震惊了教会社区。

Example 2: They considered her taste in music heathenish. 他们认为她对音乐的品味很不文明。|近义词：pagan, barbaric, uncivilized, savage, barbarous|反义词：civilized, cultured, refined, Christian|词性:形容词
