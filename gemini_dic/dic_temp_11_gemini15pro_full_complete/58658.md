dumdum:/ˈdʌmdʌm/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜；达姆弹（一种扩张型子弹）|A stupid or foolish person; an expanding bullet. |例句：He acted like such a dumdum when he locked his keys in the car. 他把钥匙锁在车里时，表现得像个傻瓜。

例句：The use of dumdum bullets is prohibited by international law. 国际法禁止使用达姆弹。|近义词：fool, idiot, moron, imbecile, dolt, simpleton, nincompoop; (for bullet) expanding bullet, hollow-point bullet |反义词：genius, intellectual; (for bullet) full metal jacket bullet |词性：名词
