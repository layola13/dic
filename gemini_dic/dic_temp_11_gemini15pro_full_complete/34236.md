clannishness:/ˈklænɪʃnəs/| n.|排外性，抱团主义，小集团意识|The tendency to be close-minded and exclusive, favoring one's own group and distrusting outsiders.  A state of being clannish.|The clannishness of the villagers made it difficult for newcomers to integrate. 村民的排外性使新来的人很难融入。|近义词：exclusiveness, insularity, cliquishness|反义词：inclusiveness, openness, broad-mindedness|词性:noun

Etymology: From clannish + -ness.  Clannish comes from clan (meaning a group of families claiming a common ancestor) + -ish (forming adjectives). Ultimately from Irish clann "family, offspring, descendants."
