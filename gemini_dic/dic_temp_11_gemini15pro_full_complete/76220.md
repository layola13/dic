futility:/fjuːˈtɪlɪti/ /fjuːˈtɪləti/| n. |无用，徒劳，无益|The futility of their efforts became apparent. 他们的努力徒劳无功，这一点变得显而易见。|例句：He realized the futility of arguing with her. 他意识到和她争论是徒劳的。|近义词：uselessness, pointlessness, vainness, fruitlessness|反义词：usefulness, effectiveness, productivity|词性:名词

futile:/ˈfjuːtaɪl/ /ˈfjuːt(ə)l/| adj. |无用的，徒劳的，无效的|It's futile to argue with him. 和他争论是徒劳的。|例句：All our attempts to rescue the dog were futile. 我们所有试图营救那只狗的努力都是徒劳的。|近义词：useless, pointless, vain, fruitless|反义词：useful, effective, productive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French futilité, or from late Latin futilitas, from Latin futilis ‘leaky, futile’, from fundere ‘to pour’.
