constraints:/kənˈstreɪnts/ /kənˈstreɪnts/| n. |限制；约束；强制|A limitation or restriction.|Budget constraints forced them to cut staff. 预算限制迫使他们裁员。|limitations, restrictions, restraints|freedom, liberty|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *constrinctus, past participle of constringere ("to bind together, constrain"), from com- ("together") + stringere ("to draw tight").
