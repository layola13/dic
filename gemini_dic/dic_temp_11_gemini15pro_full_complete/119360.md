micrococcus:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈkɒkəs/| n. |微球菌|A genus of spherical bacteria in the family Micrococcaceae, typically occurring in irregular clusters. These bacteria are generally Gram-positive and aerobic. They are widespread in the environment and are commonly found on human skin.| Micrococci are commonly found on the skin. 微球菌通常存在于皮肤上。|Micrococcus luteus is a common example of this genus. 微球菌属的一个常见例子是藤黄微球菌。 | synonym:  coccus,  spherobacterium| antonym: bacillus (rod-shaped bacterium)| noun
