nondivisional:/ˌnɒndɪˈvɪʒənəl/
adjective
非部门的；非事业部的
Not divided into separate divisions or departments; operating as a single integrated unit.
The company adopted a nondivisional organizational structure to foster collaboration.  这家公司采用了非部门化的组织结构以促进合作。
The nondivisional approach allowed for quicker decision-making. 非部门化的方法可以更快地做出决策。

近义词:unified, integrated, centralized, consolidated
反义词:divisional, departmentalized, decentralized

词性:adjective
