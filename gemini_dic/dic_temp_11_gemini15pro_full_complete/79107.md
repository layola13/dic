gladdener:/ˈɡlædnər/| n. |使人高兴的人或事物|Something or someone that brings joy or happiness.|Her smile was a gladdener to everyone in the room. 她的笑容使房间里的每个人都感到高兴。|The flowers were a welcome gladdener after the long winter.  这些花在漫长的冬天过后令人欣喜。|近义词：delight, joy, comfort, solace |反义词:saddener, depressant|noun
