invade:/ɪnˈveɪd/| v.|入侵，侵略|To enter and occupy a country or area by force, or to attack and spread through it in large numbers.|例句：Germany invaded Poland in 1939.  德国在1939年入侵了波兰。|例句：The weeds quickly invaded the flowerbeds. 杂草迅速侵占了花坛。|近义词：attack, conquer, occupy, overrun|反义词：retreat, withdraw, defend|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *invadere*, from *in-* "in, into" + *vadere* "to go, walk, step".
