hemorrhoid:/ˈheməˌrɔɪd/| n.|痔疮|A swollen vein or group of veins in the anus and rectum.|He suffers from painful hemorrhoids. 他患有疼痛的痔疮。|近义词:piles|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:名词


词源: From Late Latin *haemorrhoidalis, from Greek haimorrhoides (plural), from haima "blood" + rhoos "flowing."
