clubes:/ˈkluːbeɪs/ (Brazilian Portuguese), /ˈklʊbeɪs/ (anglicized)| n. |俱乐部（复数）|The plural form of "clube" (club) in Portuguese, used mainly in Brazilian Portuguese.  It refers to multiple clubs or societies. |Os clubes de futebol são muito populares no Brasil. = Football clubs are very popular in Brazil.|近义词：associations, societies, organizations|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "individuals" if referring to the collective nature of clubs)|词性:Noun
