dreggy:/ˈdrɛɡi/| adj.|渣滓的；沉淀的；污浊的|Containing dregs; muddy; foul.  Relating to or resembling dregs.  Figuratively, the worst or least desirable elements of something.|The dreggy bottom of the harbor was filled with pollution.|近义词：sedimentary, turbid, murky, feculent, impure, corrupt|反义词：clear, pure, pristine|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "dregs" + "-y."  "Dregs" comes from the Old Norse "dreggjar," plural of "dregg," meaning "sediment," related to the Old English "dragan" (to draw, drag).
