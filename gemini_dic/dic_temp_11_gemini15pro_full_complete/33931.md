cirrhous:/ˈsɪrəs/| adj.|卷云状的，触须状的，有卷须的|Characterized by thin, wispy filaments or curls, resembling a cirrus cloud or a tendril.  Relating to or resembling a cirrus cloud; having a delicate, wispy appearance.|The cirrhous clouds drifted across the sky. 卷云飘过天空。|The plant had cirrhous leaves. 这种植物的叶子呈卷须状。|synonyms: tendrilled, filamentous, wispy, feathery|antonyms: thick, dense, solid|adjective
