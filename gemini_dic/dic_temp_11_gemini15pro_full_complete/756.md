absolution:/ˌæbsəˈluːʃən/ | n. |赦免，免罪|the act of forgiving someone for their sins or crimes|The priest gave him absolution. 神父赦免了他的罪。|synonyms: forgiveness, pardon, remission, exoneration|antonyms: condemnation, conviction, incrimination|noun

absolution:/ˌæbsəˈluːʃən/ | n. |解脱，释放|release from a duty, obligation, or responsibility|He felt a sense of absolution after confessing his mistake. 承认错误后，他感到如释重负。|synonyms: liberation, release, freedom, deliverance|antonyms:  burden, responsibility, obligation |noun
