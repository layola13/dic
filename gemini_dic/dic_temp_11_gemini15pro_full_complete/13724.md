atypicality:/ˌeɪtɪpɪˈkælɪti/| n.|非典型性，异常性|The atypicality of the symptoms made diagnosis difficult.  这些症状的非典型性使诊断变得困难。|例句：The atypicality of the weather this summer has been remarkable.  今年夏天反常的天气引人注目。|近义词：abnormality, irregularity, anomaly, eccentricity|反义词：typicality, normality, regularity|词性:名词
Etymology:  From a- (not) + typical + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Greek "tupos" (type).
