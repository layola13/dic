diisatogen:/daɪˌæsəˈtoʊdʒən/ | n. |营养滋补品；双碘甘油磷酸钙|A tonic formerly marketed as a nutrient and restorative. It contained glycerophosphates, casein, sodium, and a small amount of iodine.| He swore by diisatogen, claiming it cured his fatigue. 他坚信双碘甘油磷酸钙能治好他的疲劳。|Synonyms: tonic, restorative, supplement|Antonyms: poison, toxin|noun
