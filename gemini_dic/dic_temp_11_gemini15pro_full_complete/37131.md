come-forward:/kʌm ˈfɔːrwərd/| phrasal verb |站出来；主动提供信息；自告奋勇|To present oneself or make something known, especially by volunteering information or offering help.|Several witnesses have come forward with information about the robbery.  几个目击者主动提供了关于抢劫案的信息。

The company is hoping that more investors will come forward. 公司希望更多投资者能站出来。|近义词：volunteer, step forward, speak up|反义词：hold back, stay silent|词性:动词短语

Etymology:
From "come" (Old English *cuman, Proto-Germanic *kweman) + "forward" (Old English forweard)
