cabinets:/ˈkæbɪnɪts/| n.|橱柜，内阁|A cabinet is a piece of furniture with shelves, drawers, etc., used for storing things, often found in kitchens or bathrooms.  A cabinet can also refer to a group of advisors to a head of state. |例句：The kitchen cabinets are made of solid oak. 厨房橱柜是用实橡木做的。

例句：The Prime Minister and her cabinet discussed the new policy. 首相和她的内阁讨论了新政策。|近义词：cupboard, closet, locker, (for government) ministry|反义词: (no direct opposite for the furniture meaning)|词性:noun
