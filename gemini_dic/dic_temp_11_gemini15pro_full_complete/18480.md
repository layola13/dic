benches:/ˈbɛntʃɪz/
n.
长凳，工作台
A long seat for several people, typically made of wood or stone.  A long table for working at, especially in a laboratory or workshop.
The park has many benches for people to rest on. 公园里有很多长凳供人们休息。
The scientist conducted experiments at his laboratory bench. 科学家在他的实验室工作台上进行实验。
