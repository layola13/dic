clocklike: /ˈklɒklaɪk/ (UK) /ˈklɑːklaɪk/ (US)
adjective
像时钟一样的；规律的；精确的
Resembling the operation of a clock; regular, precise.
The machine made a clocklike ticking sound.  这台机器发出像时钟一样的滴答声。
Her breathing was slow and clocklike. 她的呼吸缓慢而规律。
近义词: regular, precise, rhythmic, steady, uniform
反义词: irregular, erratic, unsteady, unpredictable
词性: adjective
