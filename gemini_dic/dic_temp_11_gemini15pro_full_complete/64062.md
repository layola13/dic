epitomize: /ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪz/ | v. | 体现；是…的典型；概括 | To be a perfect example of something; to embody the essential characteristics of something. |  Her career epitomizes the success that can be achieved through hard work. 她的职业生涯是通过努力工作取得成功的典型。

The new building epitomizes modern architecture. 这座新建筑是现代建筑的典范。| 近义词: embody, exemplify, represent, typify, symbolize | 反义词: contradict, belie |词性: 动词
