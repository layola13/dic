manchukuo:/mænˈtʃuːkwɔː/| n. |伪满洲国|A puppet state established by Japan in Manchuria in 1932, formally known as the "Empire of Manchukuo." It existed until the end of World War II in 1945.|Japan invaded Manchuria in 1931 and subsequently established Manchukuo.  日本于1931年入侵满洲，随后建立了伪满洲国。

The international community largely refused to recognize Manchukuo's legitimacy. 国际社会普遍拒绝承认伪满洲国的合法性。

|近义词:puppet state (傀儡国家)|反义词:independent state (独立国家)|词性:noun
