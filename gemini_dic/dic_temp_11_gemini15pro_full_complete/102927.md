jaywalk:/ˈdʒeɪwɔːk/ | vi. | 乱穿马路 | To cross a street carelessly or illegally, especially by not using a crosswalk. | Example:  He was fined for jaywalking. 他因乱穿马路被罚款。

Example:  Jaywalking is dangerous and illegal. 乱穿马路既危险又违法。 | 近义词: cross against the light, cross illegally | 反义词: cross at the crosswalk, cross legally | 词性: verb
