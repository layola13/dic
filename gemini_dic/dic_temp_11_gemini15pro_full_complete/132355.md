nondeleteriously:/ˌnɒndɛlɪˈtɪəriəsli/
adverb|无害地，无损地|in a way that does not harm or damage something.|The surgery was performed nondeleteriously, and the patient recovered quickly.  手术进行得无损健康，病人恢复得很快。|Synonym: harmlessly, innocuously, safely|Antonym: deleteriously, harmfully, injuriously
词性: 副词


Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "deleterious" (adjective meaning "harmful") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
