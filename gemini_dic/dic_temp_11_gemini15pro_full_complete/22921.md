botryoid:/ˈbɒtrɪɔɪd/ /ˈbɑːtrɪɔɪd/| adj.|葡萄状的，似葡萄状的|Resembling a bunch of grapes.  Having a shape like a cluster of grapes.|The tumor had a botryoid appearance.  肿瘤呈葡萄状。|Synonyms: grapelike, clustered, racemose|Antonyms: smooth, flat|adjective

botryoid:/ˈbɒtrɪɔɪd/ /ˈbɑːtrɪɔɪd/| n.|葡萄状体|A mineral formation resembling a bunch of grapes.|Botryoids of chalcedony were found in the cave. 在山洞里发现了葡萄状的玉髓。|Synonyms: cluster, bunch|Antonyms: N/A|noun
