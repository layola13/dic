inure:/ɪˈnjʊər/ /ɪˈnʊr/ | vt. | 使习惯于；使适应 |  to accustom someone to something unpleasant, especially by repeated exposure.  |  The soldiers were inured to the hardships of war.  士兵们已经习惯了战争的艰苦。| 近义词:  accustom, harden, toughen | 反义词: sensitize, soften | verb

inure:/ɪˈnjʊər/ /ɪˈnʊr/ | vi. |  生效；起作用（常与to连用）| to come into operation; take effect. | The benefits of the new policy will inure to the advantage of the poor. 新政策的益处将使穷人受益。| 近义词: benefit, serve, accrue | 反义词: harm, hinder | verb
