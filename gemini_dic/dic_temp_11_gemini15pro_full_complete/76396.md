gaffing:/ˈɡæfɪŋ/| n. | 用鱼叉捕鱼; 用鱼钩钓鱼 | The act of catching fish using a gaff (a large hook on a pole). |  He landed the giant tuna after a long struggle, finally gaffing it and hauling it aboard. 他经过长时间的搏斗，终于用鱼叉叉住了巨大的金枪鱼，并把它拖上了船。| 近义词: hooking, spearing | 反义词: releasing | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From gaff (n.) + -ing (suffix denoting an action or process).  "Gaff" itself is likely from Middle Dutch *gaffel or Old Norse gafl, meaning "fork."
