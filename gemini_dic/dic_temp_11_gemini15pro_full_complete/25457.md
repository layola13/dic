bulldozed:/ˈbʊldoʊzd/| v. |推平；摧毁；强制通过；|To clear (ground) with a bulldozer. To demolish or destroy with or as if with a bulldozer. To force through or overcome opposition to.| The construction crew bulldozed the old building to make way for the new shopping mall.  建筑工人用推土机推倒了旧楼，以便为新的购物中心腾出地方。|Synonyms:demolish, destroy, flatten, level, raze |Antonyms:build, construct, erect|verb

bulldozing:/ˈbʊldoʊzɪŋ/| n. |推平；摧毁；|The act of clearing ground or demolishing something with a bulldozer.| The bulldozing of the forest caused significant environmental damage.  推平森林造成了严重的環境破壞。|Synonyms:demolition, destruction, flattening |Antonyms:construction, building|noun
