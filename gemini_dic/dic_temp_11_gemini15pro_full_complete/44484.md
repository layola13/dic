crossbreeding:/ˈkrɒsˌbriːdɪŋ/ /ˈkrɔːsˌbriːdɪŋ/| n.|杂交育种|The act of breeding two different varieties or species of plants or animals.|例句：Crossbreeding can result in new varieties with desirable traits. 杂交育种可以培育出具有优良性状的新品种。

crossbreeding:/ˈkrɒsˌbriːdɪŋ/ /ˈkrɔːsˌbriːdɪŋ/| n.|杂交繁殖|The process of producing offspring from parents of different breeds or species.|例句：Crossbreeding of dogs has led to many different breeds. 狗的杂交繁殖导致了许多不同品种的狗。

crossbreeding:/ˈkrɒsˌbriːdɪŋ/ /ˈkrɔːsˌbriːdɪŋ/| n.|交叉繁殖|The production of hybrid offspring.|例句：The farmer experimented with crossbreeding different types of corn.  这位农民尝试杂交不同类型的玉米。

crossbreeding:/ˈkrɒsˌbriːdɪŋ/ /ˈkrɔːsˌbriːdɪŋ/| n.|异种交配|Breeding between different species or varieties.|例句：Crossbreeding can sometimes lead to sterile offspring. 异种交配有时会导致不育后代。


近义词：hybridization, interbreeding, cross-fertilization|反义词: inbreeding, purebreeding|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross- + breeding.  "Cross" in this context refers to mixing or combining different things, and "breeding" refers to the process of producing offspring.
