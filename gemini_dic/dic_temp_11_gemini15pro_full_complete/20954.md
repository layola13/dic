bleeper:/ˈbliːpər/| n.|寻呼机，哔哔声，电子设备|A small electronic device that beeps to alert the user to a message or call.  A short, high-pitched electronic sound.|例句：My bleeper went off during the meeting.  我的寻呼机在会议期间响了。

The bleeper on the microwave signaled that the food was ready. 微波炉上的哔哔声提示食物已经准备好了。|近义词：pager, beeper, alarm, signal|反义词:silence|noun
