cryptogam:/ˈkrɪptəɡæm/ | n. | 隐花植物；隐孢子植物 | A plant that reproduces by spores, without flowers or seeds.  |  Example: Mosses and ferns are examples of cryptogams. 苔藓和蕨类植物是隐花植物的例子。 | Example:  The botanist specialized in the study of cryptogams. 这位植物学家专门研究隐花植物。 | Synonym:  spore plant, non-flowering plant | Antonym:  phanerogam, flowering plant | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kryptos* (hidden) + *gamos* (marriage/union), referring to the hidden reproductive structures.  源自希腊语 *kryptos*（隐藏的）+ *gamos*（婚姻/结合），指的是隐藏的繁殖结构。
