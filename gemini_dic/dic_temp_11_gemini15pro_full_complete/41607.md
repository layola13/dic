corpuscle:/ˈkɔːrpəsəl/ (US) /ˈkɔːpʌsəl/ (UK)| n. |微粒；小体；血球|A minute particle of matter.  A cell, especially a red or white blood cell, in the body.| Red blood corpuscles carry oxygen throughout the body. 红血球将氧气输送到全身。|Synonyms: particle, speck, granule, molecule, atom|Antonyms: aggregate, mass|noun
