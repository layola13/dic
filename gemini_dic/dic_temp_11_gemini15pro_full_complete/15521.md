baft:/bæft/
n.
粗棉布，廉价的棉织物
A coarse cotton fabric, a cheap cotton cloth.
The curtains were made of a simple baft. 窗帘是用一种简单的粗棉布做的。
The merchant sold baft by the yard. 商人按码出售粗棉布。
近义词:calico, muslin, cheesecloth
反义词:silk, satin, velvet
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Persian پُود (pūd, “cotton cloth, woof”).
