babbage:/ˈbæbɪdʒ/| n.|巴贝奇；查尔斯·巴贝奇 (特指英国数学家和发明家)|Charles Babbage (1791-1871), an English mathematician and inventor, is considered a "father of the computer" for his conceptual design of the first mechanical computer, the Analytical Engine.  He also designed the Difference Engine, a mechanical calculator.|例句：Charles Babbage is considered the father of computing. 查尔斯·巴贝奇被认为是计算机之父。

例句：The Babbage Engine was a groundbreaking invention. 巴贝奇引擎是一项开创性的发明。|近义词：inventor, pioneer, innovator|反义词:follower|词性:名词
