nonpresumptively:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈzʌmptɪvli/
adv.
非推定地，不冒昧地
without presumption; in a way that does not assume or take for granted.
The committee nonpresumptively considered all applications, carefully reviewing each candidate's qualifications. 委员会不带任何预设地考虑了所有申请，仔细审查了每位候选人的资格。
The doctor nonpresumptively suggested further tests, wanting to gather more data before making a diagnosis. 医生并不武断地建议进一步检查，想在做出诊断之前收集更多数据。
近义词: cautiously, tentatively, reservedly
反义词: presumptuously, boldly, assertively
词性: adverb

词源:
From "non-" (not) + "presumptive" (based on probability or assumption) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Presumptive" comes from the Latin "praesumptus," past participle of "praesumere" (to anticipate, take for granted).
