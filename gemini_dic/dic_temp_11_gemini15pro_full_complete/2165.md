addendum:/əˈdɛndəm/ /æˈdɛndəm/
n.
附录，补充，补遗
Something that is added, especially a supplement to a book or document.
The addendum to the contract included a clause about liability.  合同的附录包含一条关于责任的条款。
synonyms:  supplement, appendix, addition, attachment, annex
antonyms:  base, original, main body
noun

addendum:/əˈdɛndəm/ /æˈdɛndəm/
n.
附录，补充，补遗
Something that has been added as an afterthought or to fill a deficiency.
She made an addendum to her will leaving her collection of porcelain dolls to her niece. 她对遗嘱做了补充，把她的瓷娃娃收藏留给了她的侄女。
synonyms: afterthought, postscript, coda
antonyms:  prelude, preface, introduction
noun
