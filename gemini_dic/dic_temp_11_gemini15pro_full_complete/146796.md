overillustratively:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlʌstrətɪvli/
adv.
过分说明地，过于图解地
explained or illustrated in excessive detail
The point was already clear, but the speaker overillustratively provided numerous examples.  这个观点已经很清楚了，但演讲者仍然提供了过多的例子。
She overillustratively described every detail of her trip, making the story unnecessarily long. 她过分详细地描述了旅行的每一个细节，使故事变得不必要的冗长。
近义词: excessively, redundantly, verbosely
反义词: concisely, succinctly, briefly
词性: 副词
