ancipitous:/ænˈsɪpɪtəs/
adjective
双刃的；两面的；危险的；不确定的
Having two edges or sides; uncertain or dangerous because of presenting opposing possibilities of advantage and disadvantage.
The ancipitous nature of the drug meant it could either cure the disease or worsen it. 这种药物的两面性意味着它既可以治愈疾病，也可能使病情恶化。

His ancipitous position in the company made him vulnerable to both praise and criticism. 他在公司里不确定的地位使他既容易受到赞扬，也容易受到批评。
