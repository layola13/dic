disciplining:/ˈdɪsəplɪnɪŋ/
|v.|训练；惩罚；处罚|The act of training someone to obey rules or a code of behavior, using punishment to correct disobedience.|例句：The parents were disciplining their child for misbehaving. 父母正在因为孩子行为不端而惩罚他。|近义词：training, punishing, correcting, chastising, penalizing|反义词：praising, rewarding, encouraging|词性:动词(gerund or present participle)

disciplining:/ˈdɪsəplɪnɪŋ/
|adj.|惩戒性的|Relating to or intended for discipline.|例句：Disciplining measures were taken to improve student behavior.  采取了惩戒措施来改善学生行为。|近义词：corrective, punitive|反义词：lenient, permissive|词性:形容词


词源:
From the Latin word "disciplina" meaning "instruction, knowledge," derived from "discipulus" meaning "pupil, student."
