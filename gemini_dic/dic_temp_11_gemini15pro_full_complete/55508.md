distraughtly:/dɪˈstrɔːtlɪ/
adverb
心烦意乱地，发狂地
In a manner showing extreme emotional disturbance; frantically.
She wept distraughtly after hearing the news. 她听到这个消息后心烦意乱地哭了起来。
distraughtly:/dɪˈstrɔːtlɪ/
近义词:frantically, hysterically, wildly
反义词:calmly, peacefully, serenely
词性:adverb
