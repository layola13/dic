lightfast:/ˈlaɪtˌfæst/| adj.|耐光的，不褪色的|Resistant to fading or discoloration caused by exposure to light.  The term describes materials, especially pigments or dyes, that retain their color even after prolonged exposure to sunlight or other sources of light.|This artist uses lightfast paints so her work won't fade.  这位艺术家使用耐光的颜料，所以她的作品不会褪色。|The museum carefully controls the lighting to protect the lightfastness of the exhibits. 博物馆仔细控制照明，以保护展品的耐光性。|近义词：colorfast, non-fading|反义词：fugitive (color), non-lightfast|词性:形容词
