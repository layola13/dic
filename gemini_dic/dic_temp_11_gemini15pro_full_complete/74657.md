free-versifier:/friːˈvɜːsɪfaɪər/ | n. | 自由诗诗人 | A person who writes free verse, poetry that does not rhyme or have a regular rhythm. |  Walt Whitman was a famous free-versifier. 沃尔特·惠特曼是一位著名的自由诗诗人。 | Synonym: free verse poet | Antonym:  sonneteer, formalist poet | noun
