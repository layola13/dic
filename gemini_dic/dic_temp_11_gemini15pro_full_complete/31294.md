chapkan:/ˈtʃæpkən/| n. |袷袢，长袍|A long coat worn especially in India and Central Asia, often buttoning down the front.|He wore a richly embroidered chapkan for the ceremony. 他在典礼上穿了一件绣得很华丽的袷袢。|近义词：coat, robe, gown|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "shorts" as a contrasting garment)|noun
