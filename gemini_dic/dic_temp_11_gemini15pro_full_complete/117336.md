medevacing:/ˈmɛdɪvækɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
医疗后送
The act of evacuating someone who needs medical care.
Example: They were medevacing the injured climbers from the mountain.  他们正在把受伤的登山者从山上医疗后送下来。

Example:  The soldier was medevaced after stepping on a landmine.  这名士兵在踩到地雷后被医疗后送。
近义词: evacuating (for medical reasons), airlifting (often)
反义词: None (specific to medical evacuation)
词性: Verb (present participle)


medevac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/
noun
医疗后送
The act or process of evacuating someone who needs medical care; also, a vehicle used for this purpose.
Example: The medevac arrived quickly to transport the injured skier.  医疗后送车辆迅速抵达，运送受伤的滑雪者。

Example: The hospital requested a medevac for the critically ill patient. 医院请求对危重病人进行医疗后送。
近义词: medical evacuation, air ambulance
反义词: None (specific to medical evacuation)
词性: Noun


medevac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/
verb
医疗后送
To evacuate someone who needs medical care.
Example:  They medevaced the wounded soldier by helicopter. 他们用直升机医疗后送了受伤的士兵。

Example:  The Coast Guard medevaced the fisherman suffering from a heart attack. 海岸警卫队医疗后送了心脏病发的渔民。
近义词: evacuate (for medical reasons), airlift (often)
反义词: None (specific to medical evacuation)
词性: Verb

词源:  A shortening and blending of "medical evacuation."
