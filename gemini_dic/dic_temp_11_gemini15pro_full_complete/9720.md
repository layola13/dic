antiunemployment:/ˌæntiʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt/
adjective
反失业的；防止失业的
Relating to or designed to prevent or reduce unemployment.
The government introduced new antiunemployment measures. 政府推出了新的反失业措施。
Synonyms:  counter-unemployment,  job-creation, employment-generating
Antonyms: unemployment-inducing,  job-destroying
词性: adjective
