bruter:/ˈbruːtər/| n.|野蛮人；残暴的人|A person who is cruel and violent. |例句：The bruter attacked the innocent villagers.  那个野蛮人袭击了无辜的村民。

例句：He acted like a bruter, showing no mercy. 他表现得像个野蛮人，毫不留情。|近义词：savage, barbarian, beast, brute|反义词：civilized person, gentleman|词性:名词

词源: From Middle French *brutier, from brute (“brute”), from Late Latin brutus (“dull, irrational, insensitive”), probably of Celtic origin, compare Old Irish brū (“belly, womb”).
