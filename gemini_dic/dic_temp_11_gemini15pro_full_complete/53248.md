directress:/daɪˈrɛktrɪs/ /dɪˈrɛktrəs/| n. |女指导; 女主管; 女校长|A woman who directs, manages, or controls something, especially a school, company, or organization.  Now largely replaced by the gender-neutral term "director". |例句：The directress of the orphanage was a kind and compassionate woman. 孤儿院的女院长是一位善良而富有同情心的女人。

|例句：The directress of the ballet company was renowned for her strict discipline.  芭蕾舞团的女团长以其严格的纪律而闻名。|近义词：director, manager, administrator, superintendent, principal, head|反义词: subordinate, employee, follower|词性:noun
