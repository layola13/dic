overinfluential:/ˌoʊvərˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl/
adjective
过度有影响力的；影响力过大的
Having excessive influence; too influential.
The overinfluential lobbyists managed to sway the vote in their favor. 那些影响力过大的说客成功地左右了投票结果。
The media can be overinfluential in shaping public opinion. 媒体在塑造公众舆论方面可能过度有影响力。
近义词: dominant, powerful, controlling
反义词: insignificant, powerless, weak
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "influential" (from "influence" derived from Latin "influentia" meaning "inflowing," from "in-" (in) + "fluere" (to flow)).
