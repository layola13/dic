autumnally: /ɔːˈtʌmnəli/ | adv. | 秋天地；秋天般地 | In a manner characteristic of autumn. | The leaves fell autumnally. 叶子像秋天一样飘落。| The wind blew autumnally through the trees. 秋风瑟瑟地吹过树林。| 近义词: fall-like, autumn-like | 反义词: spring-like, summer-like | 词性: 副词

词源: From autumn + -al + -ly.  "Autumn" comes from Old French "automne," ultimately from Latin "autumnus."
