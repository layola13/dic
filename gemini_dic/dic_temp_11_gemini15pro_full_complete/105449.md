kilby:/ˈkɪlbi/| n. | 基尔比（姓氏）；千字节（非正式）|  A unit of computer memory or data storage capacity equal to 1024 bytes (actually 2^10 bytes).  Kilby is also a surname, most famously associated with Jack Kilby, the co-inventor of the integrated circuit. | Jack Kilby won the Nobel Prize in Physics. 杰克·基尔比获得了诺贝尔物理学奖。 | My computer has 16 kilobytes of RAM. 我的电脑有16千字节的内存。 | 近义词:  KB (abbreviation), | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) | noun
