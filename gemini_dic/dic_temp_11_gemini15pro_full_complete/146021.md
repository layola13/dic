overdraining:/ˌəʊvəˈdreɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度排水，排水过度的 | The act of draining excessively, removing too much water or moisture from something, typically soil.  This can lead to problems for plants and the environment. | The overdraining of the peat bogs has led to a loss of biodiversity. 过度排水的泥炭沼泽导致了生物多样性的丧失。 | The overdraining of the farmland resulted in poor crop yields.  农田的过度排水导致作物产量低下。 | 近义词: excessive drainage, over-drying | 反义词: waterlogging, underdraining | 词性:形容词

overdrain:/ˌəʊvəˈdreɪn/ | v. | 过度排水 | To drain excessively, removing too much water or moisture. | Be careful not to overdrain the pasta, or it will be sticky. 注意不要把意大利面沥干过度，否则会很粘。 |  Don't overdrain the swimming pool, or you'll damage the pump. 不要过度排空游泳池，否则会损坏水泵。 | 近义词: overdry | 反义词: underdrain | 词性:动词
