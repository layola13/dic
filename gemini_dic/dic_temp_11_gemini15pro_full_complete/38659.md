concussed:/kənˈkʌst/| adj.|脑震荡的；受震荡的|Affected by concussion; stunned.|He was concussed after the car accident. 他在车祸后脑震荡了。|近义词：dazed, stunned, bewildered, disoriented|反义词:lucid, alert, clear-headed|词性:形容词
concuss:/kənˈkʌs/| vt.|使脑震荡|To injure the brain by a violent blow, causing temporary loss of consciousness, etc.|The fall concussed him badly. 摔倒使他脑震荡很严重。|近义词：stun, daze|反义词:heal, cure|词性:动词
