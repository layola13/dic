diachylum:/dɪˈækɪlʌm/ | n. |橡膏；膏药；硬膏|An adhesive plaster spread upon cloth, muslin, silk, or other material, and used as a dressing for wounds, ulcers, etc.|The doctor applied a diachylum to the wound. 医生在伤口上贴了一块膏药。|近义词: plaster, patch, bandage |反义词: None (No direct opposite for a medical dressing) |词性: Noun

词源: From Late Latin *diachylon, from Greek diakhylon, from dia- "through" + chylos "juice". Originally referring to a medicinal preparation made from juices.
