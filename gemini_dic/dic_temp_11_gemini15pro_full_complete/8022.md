anthracometer:/ˌænθrəˈkɒmɪtər/ | n. |煤尘测量器，测黑度计| An instrument for measuring the darkness or blackness, especially of smoke or the atmosphere, typically as an indication of pollution levels.  It's used to assess the concentration of coal dust in the air. |  The inspector used an anthracometer to measure the pollution from the factory's chimney.  检查员用煤尘测量器测量工厂烟囱的污染程度。| Synonym:  blackness meter, opacity meter | Antonym: N/A | noun
