fitched:/fɪtʃt/ | adj. |  尖的，有尖端的 | Having a pointed end or peak.  Specifically used in heraldry to describe a cross with the lower arm pointed. | The shield displayed a fitched cross. 盾牌上展示了一个尖底十字。 | Near Synonyms: pointed, peaked, sharpened | Antonyms: blunt, rounded | Adjective

fitched:/fɪtʃt/ | v. (past participle of fitch) |  固定，安置 (古语, 现已少用) | Fixed or placed (archaic and now rarely used). Originally meaning "to fix or set firmly," often in relation to a tent peg or stake.  This usage is now largely obsolete. | The tent was firmly fitched to the ground.  帐篷被牢牢地固定在地上。(古语用法) | Near Synonyms: fixed, secured, fastened | Antonyms: loosened, unfastened | Verb
