bicker: /ˈbɪkər/ | vi. n.| 争吵，口角 | To argue about petty and trivial matters. |  The children were bickering over who got the last cookie. 孩子们在为谁吃到最后一块饼干而争吵。

They bickered constantly about money. 他们经常为了钱争吵。| 近义词: squabble, argue, quarrel, wrangle | 反义词: agree, concur, harmonize | 词性: 动词(vi.), 名词(n.)

词源:  From Middle English *bikeren, frequentative of *biken (“to peck, stab”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly from a Scandinavian source (compare Norwegian bikkje (“female dog”), Swedish bikka (“female dog”)).
