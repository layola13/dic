levitical: /ləˈvɪtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
利未的；利未族的；利未记的
Relating to the Levites, the priestly tribe of Israel, or to the Book of Leviticus in the Old Testament.
The levitical priesthood had significant power in ancient Israel.  利未祭司在古代以色列拥有重要的权力。
The book contains detailed instructions on levitical rituals. 这本书包含了利未仪式的详细说明。
近义词: priestly, clerical
反义词: secular, lay
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Late Latin *leviticus, from Greek Λευιτικός (Leuitkos), from Hebrew לֵוִי (Levi).
