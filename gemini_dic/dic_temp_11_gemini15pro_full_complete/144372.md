outstinking:/ˌaʊtˈstɪŋkɪŋ/
|adj.|比...更臭，臭过...| Surpassing something else in its unpleasant smell.  It implies a strong and offensive odor. | The outstinking garbage made me gag.  这臭气熏天的垃圾让我作呕。|近义词：out-smelling, overpowering, foul, rank, fetid|反义词: fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic|词性:形容词

词源:  "Out-" (prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding) + "stinking" (present participle of "stink," meaning to emit a strong offensive smell).  The word is formed by combining a common prefix with the present participle of a common verb to create a comparative adjective.
