over-garrisoned:/ˈoʊvərˈɡærɪsənd/
adjective
驻军过多的，兵力过剩的
Having more troops stationed in a place than is necessary or practical.
The island was over-garrisoned, with twice the necessary number of soldiers.  这个岛驻军过多，士兵人数是所需的两倍。
The small fort was over-garrisoned and supplies quickly ran low.  这个小堡垒驻军过多，补给很快就用完了。
近义词:overmanned, overstaffed
反义词:undergarrisoned, undermanned

词性:adjective
