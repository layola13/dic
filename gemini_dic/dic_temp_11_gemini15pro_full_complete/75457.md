fruity:/ˈfruːti/| adj.|水果味的；（气味或味道）芳香的；（尤指声音）圆润的，浑厚的；略带酒味的；古怪的，疯疯癫癫的|Resembling, suggesting, or containing fruit; (of a smell or taste) pleasantly sweet and fresh; (especially of a voice) mellow and rich; slightly alcoholic; crazy or eccentric.|例句：The wine has a fruity aroma.  这酒有水果的香味。

例句：He has a wonderfully fruity voice. 他有着浑厚圆润的好嗓音。

近义词：sweet, fragrant, mellow, rich, luscious, juicy, ripe, aromatic, flavorful, tasty, eccentric, crazy, wacky, bizarre, nutty|反义词:bland, dry, dull, tasteless, normal, sane, conventional|词性:adjective
