mordanting:/ˈmɔːrdəntɪŋ/| n. |媒染；媒染法|The process of treating a fabric with a mordant to fix dyes.|The artisan used mordanting to ensure the vibrant colors of the silk scarf would not fade. 工匠使用媒染法来确保丝巾鲜艳的颜色不会褪色。|近义词：dyeing, fixing|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'bleaching' in some contexts)|noun
