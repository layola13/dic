inescapable:/ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbl/
adjective
不可避免的；无法逃脱的
Not able to be avoided or escaped.
例句：The inescapable conclusion is that we need to change our strategy.  不可避免的结论是我们需要改变策略。

例句：The feeling of guilt was inescapable.  内疚感挥之不去。

近义词：unavoidable, inevitable, certain, ineluctable
反义词：avoidable, escapable, preventable
词性: adjective

词源:  From in- "not" + escapable (from escape, from Old French escaper "to escape," from Late Latin excappare "to escape from a cape or cloak," hence "to slip away," from ex- "out of" + Late Latin cappa "cape").
