gemless:/ˈdʒɛmləs/
adjective
无宝石的；无装饰的
lacking gems; unadorned.
The gemless ring was simple but elegant. 这枚没有宝石的戒指朴素而优雅。
近义词:unadorned, plain, simple, unembellished
反义词: jeweled, ornamented, decorated, bejeweled
词性:adjective
词源: gem (宝石) + -less (无，没有)
