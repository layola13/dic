echos:/ˈɛkoʊz/ (US), /ˈekəʊz/ (UK)| n.|回声；共鸣；附和|A repetition of a sound produced by the reflection of sound waves from a surface.|The canyon walls echoed with the sound of their laughter. 峡谷的墙壁回荡着他们的笑声。|Synonyms: reverberations, repetitions, reflections|Antonyms: silence, quiet|Noun
