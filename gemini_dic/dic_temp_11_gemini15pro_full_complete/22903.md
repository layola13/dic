botheration:/ˌbɒðəˈreɪʃn/| n.|烦恼，麻烦，讨厌的事|Something that causes slight annoyance or trouble.|例句：Oh, botheration! I've forgotten my keys. 哦，真烦！我忘了带钥匙了。|例句：It's such a botheration having to fill out all these forms.  填所有这些表格真是太麻烦了。|近义词：nuisance, annoyance, trouble, hassle, inconvenience|反义词：pleasure, delight, convenience|词性:名词

词源: From bother + -ation.  From Middle English bodren, of uncertain origin.
