bobbinet:/ˈbɒbɪnɛt/| n.|薄纱，六角网眼纱|A delicate, machine-made net fabric, hexagonal in structure, typically used for veils, dresses, and curtains.|The bride wore a veil of exquisite bobbinet.|lace, tulle, netting|---|noun
Etymology: From French *bobine* (spool, bobbin) + *-et* (diminutive suffix), referring to the spools used in the machine that makes the net.
