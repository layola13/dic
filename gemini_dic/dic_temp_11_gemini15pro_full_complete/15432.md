bacteroidal:/bækˈtɪrɔɪdəl/
adjective
类菌体的；拟细菌的
Resembling a bacterium, especially in shape.  Having the characteristics of, or relating to, bacteroids.
Nitrogen-fixing bacteria in the root nodules of legumes assume a bacteroidal form.  豆科植物根瘤中的固氮细菌呈现类菌体形态。
近义词: bacterial, bacterioid
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly non-bacteroidal)
词性: adjective
