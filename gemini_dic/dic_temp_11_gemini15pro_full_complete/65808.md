evidentiary:/ˌevɪˈdɛnʃəri/ | adj. | 证据的，关于证据的 | Relating to or based on evidence. | The evidentiary hearing will determine the admissibility of the evidence.  证据听证会将决定证据的可采性。| The lawyer presented evidentiary documents to support his client's case. 律师出示了证据文件来支持其当事人的案件。 |  近义词: probative, evidential, demonstrative | 反义词: inconsequential, irrelevant | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ēvidentiārius, from Latin ēvidēns (“evident”).
