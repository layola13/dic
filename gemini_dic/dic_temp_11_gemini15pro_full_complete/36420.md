cokiest:/ˈkoʊkiɪst/| adj.|最爱说俏皮话的，最爱开玩笑的|This form is the superlative of "coky," meaning excessively self-assured or conceited. "Cokiest" describes someone who displays the highest degree of this playful arrogance.  It's often used in a lighthearted or ironic way.|He was always the cokiest guy in the room, cracking jokes and making witty remarks. 他总是房间里最爱说俏皮话的人，讲笑话，说俏皮话。|近义词：cheeky, sassy, impertinent, bold, audacious|反义词：humble, modest, timid, reserved|词性:形容词

词源:
From "cocky" + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Cocky" likely originates from "cock" (of a bird), referring to a rooster's strutting and self-assured demeanor.
