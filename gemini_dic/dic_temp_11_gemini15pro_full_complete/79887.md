gnathonical:/næθˈɒnɪkəl/
adjective
爱批评的，好争辩的，强词夺理的
Given to fault-finding, contentious, argumentative.
He launched into a gnathonical diatribe about the state of modern music. 他对现代音乐的状况进行了一番吹毛求疵的长篇大论。
近义词：carping, critical, captious, censorious, fault-finding, quibbling
反义词：approving, complimentary, laudatory
词性：adjective
