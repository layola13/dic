confidante:/ˌkɒnfɪˈdænt/ /ˌkɑːnfɪˈdænt/| n.| 知己，闺蜜（女性）; 心腹朋友（女性）|  A female friend to whom one confides secrets or private matters. | She told her confidante everything about her relationship problems. 她把感情上的所有问题都告诉了她的知己。| synonyms: intimate, close friend, bosom friend | antonyms: enemy, stranger | noun

confidant:/ˈkɒnfɪdænt/ /ˈkɑːnfɪdɑːnt/| n.| 知己，心腹朋友（男性或女性）|  A person with whom one shares a secret or private matter, trusting them not to repeat it to others. | He had been her confidant for many years. 他多年来一直是她的心腹朋友。| synonyms: intimate, close friend, confidante (female) | antonyms: enemy, stranger | noun
