nematode:/ˈnɛməˌtoʊd/ (US), /ˈnɛmətəʊd/ (UK)| n. |线虫；蛔虫|A worm of the large phylum Nematoda, such as a roundworm or threadworm.|例句：Plant parasitic nematodes can cause significant crop losses. 植物寄生线虫会导致严重的作物损失。

例句：The soil sample contained several different species of nematodes. 土壤样本中包含几种不同种类的线虫。|近义词：roundworm, threadworm|反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific type of organism) |词性: noun
