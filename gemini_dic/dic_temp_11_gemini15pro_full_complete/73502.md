foreseeably:/fɔːrˈsiːəbli/| adv.|可预见地，预料之中地|in a way that can be foreseen or predicted|例句：Foreseeably, the heavy rain caused flooding.  可预见的是，这场大雨造成了洪水。|近义词：predictably, expectedly, likely|反义词:unexpectedly, surprisingly|词性:副词
词源: From foresee + -able + -ly.  Foresee comes from Old French *foreseer, from for- + veer,  meaning "to see".
