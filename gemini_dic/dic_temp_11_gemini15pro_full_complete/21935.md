bogey:/ˈboʊɡi/ (US), /ˈbəʊɡi/ (UK)| n. | 柏忌；妖怪；鬼怪；鼻屎|A score of one stroke over par on a hole in golf. Also, an evil or mischievous spirit; a goblin or hobgoblin, sometimes used to frighten children.  In informal usage can also refer to dried nasal mucus. |He got a bogey on the seventh hole. 他在第七洞打出了柏忌。|近义词：goblin, hobgoblin, phantom, apparition (for spirit meaning), double bogey (in golf) |反义词:birdie (in golf)|词性:noun
