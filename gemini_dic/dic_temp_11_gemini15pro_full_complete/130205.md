non-segmentation:/ˌnɒnˌsɛɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 非分割; 非分段 |  The absence of division or partitioning into separate parts or segments.  This refers to the lack of breaks or boundaries within something, whether it's a physical object, a process, or a piece of data. | The company adopted a non-segmentation marketing strategy, targeting all demographics with the same message.  该公司采用非细分市场营销策略，以相同的信息针对所有受众群体。|  integration, unification, wholeness | segmentation, division, partitioning | noun

non-segmented:/ˌnɒnˈsɛɡmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 非分段的; 非分割的 | Not divided into segments. |  They used a non-segmented approach to data analysis, treating the entire dataset as a single unit. 他们使用非分段方法进行数据分析，将整个数据集视为一个单元。 | integrated, unified, whole | segmented, divided, partitioned | adjective
