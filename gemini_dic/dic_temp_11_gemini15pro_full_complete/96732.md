inelastic:/ˌɪnɪˈlæstɪk/| adj.|无弹性的，缺乏弹性的|Not elastic; lacking flexibility; unyielding.|例句：The demand for gasoline is relatively inelastic. 汽油的需求相对缺乏弹性。|例句：An inelastic collision is one in which kinetic energy is not conserved. 非弹性碰撞是指动能不守恒的碰撞。|近义词：rigid, inflexible, unyielding|反义词：elastic, flexible, resilient|词性:形容词
Etymology:in- (not) + elastic (from Late Latin elasticus, from Greek elastikos, from elaunein "to drive, beat out")
