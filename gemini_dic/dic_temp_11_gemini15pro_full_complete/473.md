abnegate:/ˈæbnɪɡeɪt/| v. |放弃，舍弃，克制|To deny oneself (something); renounce or reject (something desired or valuable).|She abnegated all claims to the family fortune. 她放弃了所有对家族财产的要求。

He abnegated his personal desires for the good of the team.  他为了团队的利益而放弃了个人愿望。

|近义词：renounce, relinquish, forgo, forsake, sacrifice|反义词：indulge, embrace, accept, claim|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abnegatus*, past participle of *abnegare* (“to deny, refuse”), from *ab* (“from, away from”) + *negare* (“to deny”).
