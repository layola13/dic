exclusive:/ɪkˈskluːsɪv/ /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/| adj. n.|独有的，排外的；独家新闻|Not divided or shared with others; limited to a particular group or area.|This room is for the exclusive use of guests. 这个房间是专供客人使用的。|近义词：sole, private, restricted, limited|反义词: inclusive, shared, general, public|词性:形容词

exclusive:/ɪkˈskluːsɪv/ /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/| n.|独家新闻；专有权|A news story that is reported by only one news organization.|The newspaper ran an exclusive on the mayor's resignation. 这家报纸独家报道了市长辞职的消息。|近义词：scoop|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
