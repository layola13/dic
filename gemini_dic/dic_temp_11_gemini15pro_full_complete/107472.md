lanarkite:/ˈlænərkʌɪt/ | n. | 块铅矿 | A basic lead sulfate mineral, Pb2SO5.  It is named after Lanark, Scotland, where it was first discovered. | Lanarkite is a rare mineral found in oxidized lead deposits. 块铅矿是一种罕见的矿物，发现于氧化的铅矿床中。|  Lanarkite can be identified by its distinct crystal structure. 块铅矿可以通过其独特的晶体结构来识别。 | 近义词: leadhillite, susannite (mineralogically related) | 反义词:  None (specific mineral, no direct opposite) | 词性: noun
