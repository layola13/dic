moos:/muːz/| v. (第三人称单数)|哞哞叫|The cow moos loudly. 牛大声地哞哞叫。|The lonely cow moos for its calf.  孤独的母牛为了它的小牛哞哞叫。|近义词：low, bellow|反义词:whimper, mew|词性:动词

Etymology->
From Middle English *mowen, *muwen, from Old English *mūgian (“to low”), from Proto-Germanic *mūgōną (“to low”), from Proto-Indo-European *meuh₁- (“to low, bellow”). Cognate with West Frisian mûgje (“to low”), Dutch moeën (“to low”), German muhen (“to low”), Danish muhe (“to low”), Swedish mua (“to low”).
