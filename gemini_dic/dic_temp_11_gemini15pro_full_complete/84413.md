hallowedly:/ˈhæləʊɪdli/ (US) /ˈhæləʊɪdli/ (UK)| adv.|神圣地，虔诚地|In a hallowed or holy manner; with reverence.  It describes an action performed with deep respect and reverence, as if in a sacred space or context.|She hallowedly placed the flowers on the altar.|近义词：reverently, piously, devoutly|反义词：irreverently, profanely|词性:副词

词源: From "hallowed" + "-ly."  "Hallowed" comes from Old English "hālgod," past participle of "hālgan" (to hallow, consecrate), related to "hālig" (holy).
