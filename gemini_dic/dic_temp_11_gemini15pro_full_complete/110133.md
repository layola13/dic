lidos:/ˈliːdoʊz/ | n. | 露天游泳池；（尤指海滨的）露天游泳场 | Open-air swimming pools, especially those at the seaside. | We spent the afternoon at the lidos. 我们下午在露天游泳池度过。| The lidos were crowded with tourists. 露天游泳池里挤满了游客。| 近义词: swimming pools, outdoor pools | 反义词: indoor pools | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Italian *lido* "shore, bank," from Latin *litus* "shore."  Originally referred to the shore of Venice, particularly the Lido island, famous for its beaches and bathing establishments.
