eleemosynarily:/ˌɛliːˌmɒsɪˈnɛərɪli/ /ˌɛliːˌmɑːsɪˈnɛrəli/| adv. |以施舍的方式；出于慈善；像乞丐一样|In a manner resembling or characteristic of an almsgiver or beggar; charitably. |He gave eleemosynarily to the homeless man. 他以施舍的方式给了那个无家可归的人一些钱。| 近义词：charitably, philanthropically | 反义词：stingily, meanly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *eleemosynarius, from Latin eleemosyna (“alms”), from Ancient Greek ἐλεημοσύνη (eleēmosúnē, “pity, alms”).
