literatus: /ˌlɪtəˈreɪtəs/ /ˌlɪtəˈrɑːtəs/ | n. | 学者，文人，文学家 | A learned person, especially one who is well-versed in literature. |  He was considered a literatus, respected for his knowledge of classic texts. 他被认为是一位学者，因其对经典著作的了解而受到尊重。

| Example:  She was a true literatus, with a vast library and a love for all things literary. 她是一位真正的文学家，拥有一个巨大的图书馆，热爱所有文学的东西。

| Synonym: scholar, intellectual, learned person, man of letters, savant | Antonym: ignoramus, illiterate, philistine | Noun
