outpeopling:/ˌaʊtˈpiːplɪŋ/
n.
人口超过；人口外迁；移民人数超过本地居民
The act of exceeding another group in population, often through migration or higher birth rates.
European colonization resulted in the outpeopling of indigenous populations in many parts of the world. 欧洲殖民导致世界许多地区的土著居民人口被超过。
synonyms: colonization, displacement, migration
antonyms: depopulation, decline
