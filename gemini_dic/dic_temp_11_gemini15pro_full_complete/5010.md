all-best:/ɔːl-best/ | adj. | 最好的，最优的 |  Of the highest quality; the best possible.  It's composed of "all" meaning everything or the whole of something, and "best" meaning of the highest quality.  Implies a selection or curation of the best from a larger set. |  They sent their all-best representatives to the conference. 他们派出了最好的代表参加会议。 | synonyms: top-notch, first-rate, premium, elite | antonyms: worst, poorest, inferior | adjective
