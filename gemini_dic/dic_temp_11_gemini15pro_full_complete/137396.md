nontensilities:/ˌnɒntɛnˈsɪlɪtiz/| n. |非张力性，非紧张性|The absence of tension or tensility; the state of not being tensile.  This word is often used in the context of materials science or biomechanics to describe materials or structures that do not exhibit tension or the ability to withstand pulling forces.|The nontensilities of the tissue made it unsuitable for suturing.  这种组织的非张力性使其不适合缝合。|The bridge's nontensilities were a concern for the engineers.  这座桥的非张力性是工程师们担心的问题。|近义词:laxity, looseness, flexibility|反义词:tensility, tension, tightness|词性:noun
