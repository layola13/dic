cruth:/krʊθ/| n. | 克鲁斯琴;一种古老的威尔士弦乐器|A cruth is an ancient Welsh stringed musical instrument, similar to a lyre or fiddle. |例句：The musician played a haunting melody on the cruth.  音乐家在克鲁斯琴上演奏了一曲萦绕心头的旋律。

例句：The cruth is a traditional instrument in Welsh folk music. 克鲁斯琴是威尔士民间音乐的传统乐器。|近义词：lyre, fiddle|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑modern instruments 现代乐器)|词性:noun
