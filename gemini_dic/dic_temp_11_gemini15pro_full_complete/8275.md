anti-jesuit: /ˌæntiˈdʒɛzjuːɪt/ | adj. n. | 反耶稣会的；反耶稣会派的人 | Opposed to the Jesuits, a Roman Catholic religious order.  |  He held strong anti-jesuit sentiments. 他持有强烈的反耶稣会情绪. | The writer's anti-jesuit stance was evident in his latest book.  这位作家的反耶稣会立场在他的最新著作中显而易见。 | 近义词: anti-Catholic,  | 反义词: pro-Jesuit, Jesuit | adjective, noun


anti-jesuit: /ˌæntiˈdʒɛzjuːɪt/ | n. | 反耶稣会派的人 | A person opposed to the Jesuits. | He was known as a fervent anti-jesuit.  他被称为狂热的反耶稣会人士. |  Many anti-jesuits criticized the order's influence. 许多反耶稣会人士批评该教团的影响力。 | 近义词: opponent of the Jesuits | 反义词: Jesuit, supporter of the Jesuits | noun


词源:

"anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "Jesuit" (from Societas Jesu, the Latin name for the Society of Jesus).
