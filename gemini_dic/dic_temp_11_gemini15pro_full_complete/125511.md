multisection:/ˈmʌltiˌsɛkʃən/ | n. |多截面，多段，多分段|The process of dividing something into multiple sections.  The act or an instance of dividing something into many sections. |The multisection of the novel made it easier to read. 这部小说的多章节划分使它更容易阅读。|近义词：division, segmentation, partitioning|反义词: unification, integration|noun
