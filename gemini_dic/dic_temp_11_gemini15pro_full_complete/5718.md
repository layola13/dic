alvar:/ˈælˌvɑːr/| n.| 石灰岩荒地；白地|A limestone plain with thin or no soil and sparse vegetation.|The alvar supported a unique ecosystem of rare plants.|近义词：barren, wasteland|反义词:forest, woodland|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Swedish *alvar* (dialectal, now only in place names), from Old Norse *alfr*, elf.  The term originally referred to areas believed to be inhabited by elves, likely due to their otherworldly appearance.
