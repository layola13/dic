decoupage:/ˌdeɪkuˈpɑːʒ/
n.|剪纸装饰；拼贴画；|The art of decorating surfaces by pasting cutouts of paper or other materials onto them, then coating them with varnish or lacquer.|She learned the art of decoupage from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里学会了剪纸装饰的艺术。|近义词:collage, montage|反义词:None|词性:noun

decoupage:/ˌdeɪkuˈpɑːʒ/
v.|用剪纸装饰；拼贴；|To decorate something using decoupage.|She decoupaged the old table with colorful tissue paper. 她用五颜六色的薄纸装饰了旧桌子。|近义词:decorate, adorn|反义词:strip, bare|词性:verb
