creviced:/ˈkrɛvɪst/| adj.|有裂缝的，多裂缝的|Having crevices; full of crevices. |The old wall was creviced and crumbling.  古老的墙壁布满了裂缝，正在崩塌。|The climber jammed his hand into a crevice in the rock face. 攀岩者把手塞进岩壁的裂缝里。|近义词：cracked, fissured, fractured|反义词:smooth, seamless|形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From crevice + -ed.  Crevice comes from Old French crevace, from crever "to break, burst," from Latin crepare "to crack, rattle, break."
