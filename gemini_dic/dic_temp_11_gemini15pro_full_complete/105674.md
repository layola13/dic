kingpin:/ˈkɪŋpɪn/| n.|主要人物，关键人物，大头目|A person who is essential to the success of an organization or operation.  Someone who is in a leading or influential position. |例句：He was the kingpin of the entire drug operation. 他是整个贩毒集团的幕后主使。

例句：She's the kingpin in the local art scene. 她是当地艺术界的核心人物。

近义词：leader, boss, chief, mastermind, linchpin|反义词:underling, subordinate|词性:名词

kingpins:/ˈkɪŋpɪnz/| n.|主要人物（复数），关键人物（复数），大头目（复数）|Plural form of kingpin.  Refers to multiple individuals who are essential to the success of an organization or operation. |例句：The police arrested the kingpins of several organized crime families. 警方逮捕了几个有组织犯罪家族的头目。

例句：The kingpins of the tech industry gathered for an annual conference. 科技行业的巨头们聚集在一起参加年度会议。

近义词：leaders, bosses, chiefs, masterminds|反义词:underlings, subordinates|词性:名词
