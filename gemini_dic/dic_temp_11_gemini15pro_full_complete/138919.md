nutrition:/njuˈtrɪʃn/| n.|营养，营养学|The process by which living organisms obtain and utilize the nutrients required for growth, maintenance, and repair of tissues.|Proper nutrition is essential for good health. 适当的营养对健康至关重要。|synonyms: nourishment, sustenance|antonyms: malnutrition, starvation|noun
