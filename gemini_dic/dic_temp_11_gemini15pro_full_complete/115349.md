manometer:/məˈnɒmɪtər/ (UK) /məˈnɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n. |压力计；测压计|An instrument for measuring the pressure of gases or liquids.|例句：The engineer used a manometer to measure the pressure in the pipeline. 工程师用压力计测量管道中的压力。

例句：A manometer indicated a drop in pressure. 压力计显示压力下降。|近义词：pressure gauge, pressure meter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *manomètre*, from Greek *manos* (rare, thin) + *metron* (measure). Originally referred to instruments that measured the rarity or thinness of air.
