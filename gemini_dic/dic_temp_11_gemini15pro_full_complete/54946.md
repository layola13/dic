PHRASE->

(The word "dispost" doesn't have common phrasal verb usage in modern English.  The following are phrases using "dispose," a related but distinct word.)

dispose of = 处理掉
at one's disposal = 由某人支配
garbage disposal = 垃圾处理器
waste disposal = 废物处理
well-disposed = 好意的


注意事项:

The primary meaning of "dispost" in modern English is obsolete and rarely used.  It's much more common to see the related word "dispose" used with "of" as a phrasal verb, meaning "to get rid of." The examples and phrases reflect this current usage.
