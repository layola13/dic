indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ (US), /ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ (UK)| v. |放纵；沉溺；满足；纵容| to allow oneself or another person to have something enjoyable, especially more than is good for you; to satisfy a particular desire, interest, etc.|例句：I indulged myself with a long hot bath. 我泡了个长时间的热水澡，让自己享受一番。|近义词：pamper, spoil, gratify, humor, cater to|反义词：deny, deprive, restrict|词性：动词
indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ (US), /ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ (UK)| n. |放纵；沉溺| An act of indulging; something indulged in.|例句：Chocolate is my only indulgence. 巧克力是我唯一的放纵。|近义词：treat, luxury, extravagance|反义词：abstinence, self-denial|词性：名词
