declivous:/dɪˈklaɪvəs/
adjective
倾斜的；向下倾斜的
sloping downwards; declining
The declivous terrain made the hike challenging.  向下倾斜的地形使徒步旅行变得具有挑战性。
The road was declivous, making it difficult for the car to ascend. 这条路是下坡的，汽车很难爬上去。
近义词:sloping, descending, downhill
反义词:acclivous, ascending, uphill
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin dēclīvis (“sloping downwards”), from dē (“down”) + clīvus (“slope”).
