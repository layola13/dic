hamperedness:/ˈhæmpərdnɪs/
noun
阻碍，妨碍，受限制的状态
The state of being hampered; the condition of being held back or restricted.
Her hamperedness by the injury prevented her from competing. 她因伤受阻，无法参赛。
hamper, hindrance, constraint, restriction
freedom, liberty, ease, facility
