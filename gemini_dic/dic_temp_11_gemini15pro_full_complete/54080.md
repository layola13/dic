disembark:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrk/ /ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrk/ |v. |下船，下飞机，下车|To leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.|The passengers began to disembark from the plane. 乘客开始下飞机。|近义词：debark, get off, leave|反义词:embark, board|词性:动词

disembark:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrk/ /ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrk/ |n. |下船，下飞机，下车|The act of leaving a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.|The disembarkation of the passengers was delayed. 乘客下船/飞机/车被延误了。|近义词：debarkation, egress|反义词:embarkation, boarding|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desembarquer, from des- (prefix meaning "off, from") + embarquer ("to embark"), from en- ("in, on") + barque ("barge").
