nolleity:/nɒˈliːɪti/ | n. |无效，不存在，无价值|The contract was declared a nolleity due to a lack of consideration.  该合同由于缺乏对价而被宣布无效。| The judge ruled the agreement a nolleity. 法官裁定该协议无效。|Near synonyms: nullity, invalidity, nothingness|Antonyms: validity, existence, effectiveness| noun
Etymology:  From Late Latin *nolleitas, from Latin nolle (“to be unwilling”) + -ity. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *nel- (“not, no”).
