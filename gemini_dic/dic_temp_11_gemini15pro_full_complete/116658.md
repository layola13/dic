mattest:/ˈmætɪst/| adj.|最无光泽的，最暗淡的|Having the least gloss or shine; most dull.|The mattest paint finish is best for hiding imperfections.|近义词：dullest, flattest|反义词：glossiest, shiniest|词性:形容词
词源:  'Matte' comes from the Old French word 'mat,' meaning dull or unpolished. 'Mattest' is the superlative form of 'matte.'
