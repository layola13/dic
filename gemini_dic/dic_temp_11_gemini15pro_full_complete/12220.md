Asperger's:/ˈæspɜːrɡərz/ (US) /ˈæspɜːɡəz/ (UK)| n.|阿斯伯格综合症|A developmental disorder affecting communication and social interaction, characterized by difficulties with social interaction, repetitive behaviors, and intense interests in specific subjects. Although previously considered a separate diagnosis, it is now classified within the autism spectrum disorder (ASD).|例句：He was diagnosed with Asperger's syndrome at a young age. 他在很小的时候就被诊断出患有阿斯伯格综合症。

例句：Many people with Asperger's have successful careers. 许多阿斯伯格综合症患者拥有成功的职业生涯。|近义词：Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)|反义词: Neurotypical|词性: noun
