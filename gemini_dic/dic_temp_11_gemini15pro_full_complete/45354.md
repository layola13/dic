cunctation:/kʌŋkˈteɪʃən/| n.| 迟疑，拖延|A deliberate delay; procrastination. |例句：His cunctation cost them valuable time. 他的迟疑使他们损失了宝贵的时间。

例句：The general's cunctation allowed the enemy to regroup. 将军的拖延让敌人得以重新集结。

近义词：hesitation, procrastination, delay, dilatoriness|反义词: haste, alacrity, swiftness|词性:noun

词源: From Latin *cunctātiō*, from *cunctārī* (“to delay, linger”), from *cunctus* ("all, whole").  The sense evolution is perhaps from taking one's time to consider all aspects of a situation before acting.
