over-filters:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪltərz/ | v. | 过度过滤 | Applies filters excessively, often to photos or videos, resulting in an artificial or unnatural look.  | She over-filters her selfies so much that they don't even look like her anymore. 她过度美化她的自拍照，以至于它们看起来都不像她了。| over-edits, over-processes | under-filters | verb

over-filtered:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪltərd/ | adj. | 过度过滤的 |  Having been excessively filtered, typically referring to photos or videos that appear unnatural or artificial due to excessive editing. | The photo was so over-filtered that it looked plastic. 这张照片被过度美颜，看起来像塑料一样。| over-edited, over-processed | natural, unedited | adjective
