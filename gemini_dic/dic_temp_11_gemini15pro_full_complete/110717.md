linen:/ˈlɪnɪn/| n. adj.|亚麻布；亚麻制品；亚麻色|A fabric woven from flax.  Also, items made from this fabric (e.g., sheets, tablecloths).  As an adjective, it describes the pale grayish-yellowish-white color of undyed flax.|She bought new linen sheets for the guest room. 她为客房买了新的亚麻床单。|近义词：flax, cloth, fabric|反义词:synthetic, nylon|词性:noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *līnen, from Proto-Germanic *līninam, from Proto-Indo-European *līnom (“flax”). Cognate with Old High German līnan (“linen”), Latin līnum (“flax”), Greek λίνον (línon, “flax, linen”), Russian лён (ljon, “flax”).
