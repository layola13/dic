matterless:/ˈmætərlɪs/
adj.
无关紧要的，不重要的
Having no importance; insignificant.
His opinion is matterless in this situation. 在这种情况下，他的意见无关紧要。
近义词：insignificant, unimportant, trivial, inconsequential
反义词：important, significant, crucial, essential
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From matter + -less.  "Matter" comes from Old French "matere" and ultimately from Latin "materia" meaning "material, substance, timber." The suffix "-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without."
