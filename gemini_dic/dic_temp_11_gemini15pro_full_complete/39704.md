consignatary:/kənˌsaɪnəˈteəri/
n.
收货人；受托人
A person or party to whom goods are consigned or entrusted.
The consignatary is responsible for receiving and distributing the shipment. 收货人负责接收和分发货物。
The consignatary will be notified upon arrival of the goods. 货物到达后会通知收货人。
Synonym: consignee, recipient
Antonym: consignor, shipper
noun
