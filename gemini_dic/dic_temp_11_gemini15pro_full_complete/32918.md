choosers:/ˈtʃuːzərz/
noun
选择者；挑选者
People who make choices.
例句：The choosers selected the best candidate for the job. 选择者挑选了最适合这份工作的候选人。

例句：Smart choosers compare prices before buying. 精明的选择者会在购买前比较价格。

近义词：selectors, pickers, deciders
反义词：N/A
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "choose" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Choose" comes from Middle English *chosen, from Old English *cēosan, from Proto-Germanic *keusaną, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵews-  "to taste, choose".
