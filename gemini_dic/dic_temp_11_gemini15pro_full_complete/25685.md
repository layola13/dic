bungs:/bʌŋz/
noun
塞子；瓶塞；（桶的）底部；屁股
Corks or stoppers, especially for barrels or casks; the bottom of a cask or barrel; buttocks.
The bungs were tightly fitted to prevent leakage. 塞子塞得很紧以防漏水。
