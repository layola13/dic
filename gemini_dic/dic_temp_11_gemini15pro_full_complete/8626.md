anticommercialists:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪsts/
| n. |反商业主义者；反商业化者|People who oppose commercialism or the dominance of commercial interests, especially in cultural or artistic matters.  Those who believe that commercial values negatively impact society, prioritizing profit over other considerations.|The anticommercialists protested the new shopping mall, arguing it would destroy the character of the town. 反商业主义者抗议新的购物中心，认为它会破坏城镇的特色。| critics, dissenters, opponents| proponents, supporters, advocates| noun
