excommunicating:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
逐出教会
officially exclude (someone) from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
The priest excommunicated him for his heretical beliefs.  神父因他的异端信仰而将他逐出教会。
synonyms:  expelling, ousting, banishing, excluding, barring, debarring, ostracizing, shunning, blackballing, casting out, kicking out
antonyms:  admitting, including, welcoming
