entrepreneuring:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːrɪŋ/
n.|创业，企业家精神|The act of starting and running a business, or the skills and qualities needed to do this.  The spirit of innovation, risk-taking, and initiative associated with starting and operating a business.|She has a natural talent for entrepreneuring. 她有创业的天赋。|synonyms: enterprise, business development, innovation|antonyms:  risk aversion, stagnation, complacency|noun
