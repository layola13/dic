equanimity:/ˌiːkwəˈnɪməti/ | n. | 平静；镇定；沉着 | Mental calmness, composure, and evenness of temper, especially in a difficult situation. |例句：She accepted both the good and the bad with equanimity. 她平静地接受了好与坏。

例句：He maintained his equanimity despite the chaos around him. 尽管周围一片混乱，他仍然保持镇定。|近义词：composure, calmness, tranquility, serenity |反义词：anxiety, agitation, distress, panic |词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin aequanimitas, from aequanimus (“even-minded, calm”), from aequus (“even, level, equal”) + animus (“mind, spirit”).
