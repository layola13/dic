launching-pad: /ˈlɔːntʃɪŋ pæd/ (US) /ˈlɔːntʃɪŋ pæd/ (UK)| n. |发射台，起点|A platform or structure from which rockets, missiles, or spacecraft are launched.  Also used metaphorically for a starting point for a career or project.|The Kennedy Space Center is a famous launching-pad for space exploration.  肯尼迪航天中心是著名的太空探索发射台。|Synonyms: launchpad, launching platform, springboard, starting point|Antonyms: destination, endpoint|noun
