deviationists:/ˌdiːviˈeɪʃənɪsts/
n.
离经叛道者，背离者，偏差者
People who depart from an accepted norm, especially in social or political beliefs.
The party expelled the deviationists who challenged the leader's authority. 该党开除了那些挑战领导人权威的离经叛道者。
近义词: dissidents, heretics, nonconformists, rebels
反义词: conformists, traditionalists
词性: 名词

deviationist:/ˌdiːviˈeɪʃənɪst/
adj.
离经叛道的，背离的
Departing from an accepted norm, especially in social or political beliefs.
The government cracked down on deviationist tendencies within the party. 政府打击了党内的离经叛道的倾向。
近义词: dissident, heretical, nonconformist, rebellious
反义词: conformist, traditional
词性: 形容词


词源:  From "deviation" + "-ist."  "Deviation" comes from Late Latin "deviatio," from "deviare" meaning "to turn aside," from "de-" (from, away) + "via" (way, road).
