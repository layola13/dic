mahal:/məˈhɑːl/| n.|宫殿，大厦；(对心爱的人的称呼)宝贝，亲爱的|A large and impressive building, especially a palace; a term of endearment.|例句：The Taj Mahal is a beautiful mahal.  泰姬陵是一座美丽的宫殿。|近义词：palace, mansion, darling, sweetheart, beloved|反义词:hut, shack|noun


mahal:/mɑːˈhɑːl/| adj.|昂贵的，奢华的|(especially of food or accommodation) expensive or luxurious.|例句：We stayed in a mahal hotel. 我们住在一个豪华的酒店。|近义词：expensive, luxurious, costly, pricey|反义词:cheap, inexpensive, economical|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi महल (mahal), from Arabic محل‎ (maḥall, “place, location”), from وضع‎ (waḍaʿa, “to put, place”). Ultimately from Proto-Semitic *maḍraħ- (“place where something is put, location”).
