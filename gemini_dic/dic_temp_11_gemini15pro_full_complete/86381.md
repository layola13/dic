hedge:/hɛdʒ/| n. vt. vi.|树篱，防止损失的手段，避免作出正面答复|A row of bushes or small trees planted close together, especially along a border or to form a fence.  A way of protecting oneself against financial loss or other risks.|The garden is surrounded by a tall hedge. 花园被高高的树篱包围着。|近义词：fence, barrier, safeguard, protect|反义词: expose, risk|noun

hedge:/hɛdʒ/| vt. vi.|用树篱围住，限制，避免作出正面答复|To enclose or bound with a hedge. To limit or qualify (something) so as to avoid commitment or take less risk.|He hedged his bets by applying to several colleges. 他为了保险起见，申请了好几所大学。|近义词：enclose, surround, limit, restrict|反义词: open, free|verb
