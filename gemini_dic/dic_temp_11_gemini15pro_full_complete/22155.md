bombarder:/bɒmˈbɑːdə(r)/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdər/ | n. |轰炸机；投弹手；炮击手|A military aircraft designed to drop bombs. A person who operates a bomber or artillery.|例句：The bomber flew over enemy territory. 轰炸机飞越敌方领土。|近义词：attacker, assailant|反义词:defender, protector|noun
bombarder:/bɒmˈbɑːdə(r)/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdər/ | v. |轰炸；炮击；大量提问|To attack with bombs or artillery. To assail with many questions.|例句：The city was bombarded day and night.  这座城市日夜遭到轰炸。|近义词：bomb, shell, pepper, barrage|反义词:defend, protect|verb
