nonreceptively:/ˌnɒnrɪˈsɛptɪvli/
adverb
不接受地，无接受能力地
in a way that does not involve or show an ability or willingness to receive something.
The child stared nonreceptively at the teacher.  这孩子茫然地盯着老师。
He listened nonreceptively to the lecture, his mind wandering elsewhere. 他心不在焉地听着讲座，思绪飘到了别处。
近义词: unreceptively, passively, indifferently
反义词: receptively, openly, willingly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
receptive (adjective): from Latin *receptīvus*, from recipere "to receive, take back, recover, admit, accept,"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
