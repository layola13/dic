garbless:/ˈɡɑːrbləs/
adjective
无垃圾的；整洁的
free of garbage; neat and clean
The park was spotless and garbless after the cleanup. 公园清理后一尘不染，没有垃圾。
近义词:clean, tidy, spotless, immaculate
反义词:dirty, messy, littered, filthy
词性:adjective

词源:
"garbless" is a combination of "garbage" (meaning waste material) and the suffix "-less" (meaning without).  Therefore, it literally means "without garbage".
