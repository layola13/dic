insomnia: /ɪnˈsɑːmniə/ (US) /ɪnˈsɒmniə/ (UK)| n. |失眠|Habitual sleeplessness; inability to sleep.|She has been suffering from insomnia for months. 她已经失眠好几个月了。|He couldn't concentrate at work because of his insomnia. 由于失眠，他无法集中精力工作。|近义词：sleeplessness, wakefulness|反义词：somnolence, hypersomnia|词性: noun

insomniac: /ɪnˈsɑːmniæk/ (US) /ɪnˈsɒmniæk/ (UK)| n. adj. |失眠症患者，失眠的|A person who suffers from insomnia.|He's a chronic insomniac and rarely gets a full night's sleep. 他是一个慢性失眠症患者，很少睡整夜觉。|The insomniac nights were taking a toll on her health.  失眠的夜晚正在损害她的健康。|近义词：sleepless person|反义词：sleeper|词性: noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insomnis* (sleepless), from *in-* (not) + *somnus* (sleep).
