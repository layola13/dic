muscularity:/ˌmʌskjʊˈlærəti/
n.
肌肉发达；强壮
the state of having well-developed muscles.
He admired the muscularity of the weightlifters.  他羡慕举重运动员的肌肉发达。
近义词：muscledness, brawn, strength
反义词：weakness, frailty, thinness
词性: 名词

muscularity:/ˌmʌskjʊˈlærəti/
n.
肌肉发达
the state of having well-developed muscles.
The bodybuilder displayed impressive muscularity. 健美运动员展示了令人印象深刻的肌肉发达程度。
近义词：bulk, power, physique
反义词：thinness, slenderness, weakness
词性: 名词

词源:  Late 16th century: from late Latin muscularis + -ity.
