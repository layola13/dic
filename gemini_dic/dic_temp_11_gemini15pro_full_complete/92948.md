ideograph:/ˈaɪdiəɡræf/ /ˈɪdiəɡræf/| n. |表意文字，表意符号|A written symbol that represents an idea or object directly rather than a particular word or speech sound, as in Chinese characters and some Egyptian hieroglyphs.|Chinese characters are ideographs, representing whole words or concepts.|近义词：logogram, symbol, character|反义词:|noun
