dystrophias:/dɪˈstrɒfiəz/
|n.|肌营养不良症；营养障碍|A group of progressive muscle disorders characterized by the weakening and wasting of the muscles.|Muscular dystrophy is a group of dystrophias that cause progressive weakness and loss of muscle mass. 肌营养不良症是一组导致进行性无力和肌肉质量丧失的肌营养不良症。|synonyms: disorders, diseases, ailments|antonyms: health, wellness|noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek *dys-* (bad, ill) +  *-trophē* (nourishment).
