impound:/ɪmˈpaʊnd/| v. n.|扣押，没收；拘留；把…关在圈栏里|to seize and hold in the custody of the law; to confine within an enclosure|The police impounded his car after the accident. 警方在事故后扣押了他的车。|近义词：confiscate, seize, impound, detain|反义词:release, free|verb

impound:/ɪmˈpaʊnd/| n.|扣押，没收；拘留；圈栏|the act of impounding; a place where stray animals are kept|The car was taken to the impound lot. 车被带到了扣押场。|近义词：confiscation, seizure, detention, enclosure|反义词:release, freedom|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *empunder, from en- + pound (n.2) "enclosure."
