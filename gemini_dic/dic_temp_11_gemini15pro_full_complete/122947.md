momentums:/məˈmɛntəmz/| n.|动力; 势头|The plural form of "momentum".  It refers to the force or speed with which something moves.|The team has lost its momentum and needs to regain it.  团队失去了动力，需要重新振作起来。|Near synonyms:  forces,  impetus,  inertia|Near antonyms:  inertia (when referring to resistance to change), stagnation|noun

注意事项:

*"momentums" while grammatically correct as a plural form, is rarely used.  "Momentum" is typically treated as an uncountable noun.  It's more common to say "a lot of momentum" or "more momentum" than "momentums."
