nondiaphanousnesses:/ˌnɒndaɪəˈfænəsnəsɪz/
n.|不透明性；不透光性|The quality or state of being nondiaphanous; opacity. |The nondiaphanousnesses of the curtains made the room dark. 窗帘的不透光性使房间很暗。|近义词:opaqueness, opacity|反义词:transparency, translucence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + diaphanous + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Diaphanous" comes from the Greek "diaphanēs" meaning "transparent, translucent," from "dia-" (through) + "phainein" (to show).
