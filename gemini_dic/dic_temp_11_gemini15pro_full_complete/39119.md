confucianists: /kənˈfjuːʃənɪsts/
n.
儒家学者；儒士；孔门弟子
Followers or advocates of Confucianism, a system of philosophical and ethical teachings founded by Confucius and developed by Mencius.

Confucianists believe in the importance of education and moral principles.
儒家学者相信教育和道德原则的重要性。

Many Confucianists hold positions of influence in the government.
许多儒家学者在政府中担任要职。


近义词: followers of Confucius, disciples of Confucius
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider opponents of Confucianism depending on context)
词性: noun
