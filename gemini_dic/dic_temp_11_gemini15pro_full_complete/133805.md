nongerundial:/nɒnˌdʒɪˈrʌndiəl/ | adj. | 非动名词的 | Not pertaining to or resembling a gerund; not used as a gerund. |  This word is nongerundial and cannot be used as a noun. 这个词是非动名词的，不能用作名词。|  non-gerundival | gerundial | adjective

Etymology:
* non-: Prefix meaning "not." From Latin *nōn* "not."
* gerundial:  From Late Latin *gerundivus*,  related to Latin *gerundum* "gerund." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵer- "to carry, bear."
