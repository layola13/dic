infuriation:/ɪnˌfjʊəriˈeɪʃən/ | n. |愤怒，狂怒 |The state of being infuriated; extreme anger. |His constant lateness was a source of infuriation to his colleagues. 他总是迟到，这让他同事们非常愤怒。|  近义词: rage, fury, wrath, anger, exasperation | 反义词: calmness, peacefulness, tranquility, serenity | 词性: 名词

infuriate:/ɪnˈfjʊərieɪt/ | v. |激怒，使大怒 |To make someone extremely angry. |Her condescending tone infuriated him. 她那居高临下的语气把他激怒了。| 近义词: enrage, anger, incense, provoke, madden | 反义词: calm, soothe, placate, appease | 词性: 动词
