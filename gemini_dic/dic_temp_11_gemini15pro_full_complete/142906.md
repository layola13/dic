out-quarter:/ˌaʊtˈkwɔːrtər/
adjective
远离中心城区; 偏远的
Located away from the main or central part of a town or city; peripheral.
The out-quarter suburbs have seen a rise in property values.  偏远的郊区房价上涨。
synonym: outlying, peripheral, remote, distant
antonym: central, inner-city, downtown
词性: 形容词


out-quarter:/ˌaʊtˈkwɔːrtər/
verb
把…安排在远离中心城区的住所;把…安排在城外住宿
To accommodate (someone) in lodgings away from the main or central part of a town or city.
The company out-quartered its employees in nearby towns due to a lack of housing in the city center. 由于市中心缺乏住房，公司将员工安排在附近的城镇住宿。
synonym: accommodate, house, lodge, billet
antonym: relocate centrally
词性: 动词


词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning outside, beyond) + "quarter" (originally meaning a lodging or dwelling place).  “out-”（表示外部、超越的前缀）+ “quarter”（最初表示住所或居住地）。
