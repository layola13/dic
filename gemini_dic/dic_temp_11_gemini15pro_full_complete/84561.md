hammeringly:/ˈhæmərɪŋli/
adv.
锤击般地，极其，非常
With overwhelming force; decisively.  Used to emphasize the intensity or effectiveness of something.
The evidence against him was hammeringly clear.  证据对他来说非常清楚。
Synonym: overwhelmingly, decisively, conclusively, emphatically
Antonym: subtly, gently, weakly
词性: 副词
