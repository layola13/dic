nonsecessional:/ˌnɒn səˈseʃənəl/
adjective
非会议的；不分会的
Not relating to or divided into sessions.
The nonsecessional nature of the program allowed students to enroll at any time.  这个非会议性质的课程允许学生随时注册。
The organization held nonsecessional workshops throughout the year.  该组织全年举办非会议性质的研讨会。
近义词: continuous, ongoing
反义词: sessional, periodic
词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sessional" (adjective meaning "relating to or divided into sessions").  "Session" ultimately derives from the Latin "sessio" meaning "a sitting."
