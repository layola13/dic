outspreads:/ˌaʊtˈsprɛdz/ | v. (outspreads, outspreading, outspread) | 展开，铺开 |  To spread out or extend to a wider area or surface. | The eagle outspreads its wings and soars.  老鹰展开翅膀翱翔。| synonyms: extend, unfurl, expand, stretch | antonyms: contract, shrink, fold | verb

outspread:/ˈaʊtˌsprɛd/ | adj. | 展开的，铺开的 | Spread out or extended to a wider area or surface. |  The bird had outspread wings. 鸟儿张开翅膀。 | synonyms: extended, expanded, unfurled, stretched | antonyms: contracted, shrunk, folded | adjective

outspread:/ˈaʊtˌsprɛd/ | n. | 展开，延伸 | The act or state of being spread out. | The outspread of the virus was alarming. 病毒的蔓延令人担忧。 | synonyms: expansion, extension, stretch | antonyms: contraction, shrinkage | noun

Etymology: From out- + spread. The word "spread" comes from Old English *sprǣdan, from Proto-Germanic *spraidijan.
