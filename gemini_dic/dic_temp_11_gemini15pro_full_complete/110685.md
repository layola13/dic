linea:/ˈlɪniə/| n.|线条，谱系，血统|A long, thin mark, especially one made with a pen or brush.  A series of ancestors or descendants of a particular person or animal.|The artist drew a fine linea with a charcoal pencil. 艺术家用炭笔画了一条细线。|Synonyms: line, streak, stripe|Antonyms: blur, smudge|Noun
