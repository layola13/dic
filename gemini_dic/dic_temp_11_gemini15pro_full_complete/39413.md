connascence:/kəˈnæsəns/ |n.| 共生，内聚性 | Connascence is a measure of the degree of coupling between two software modules. It describes the kinds of connections between them and how changes in one module might affect the other. |  High connascence between modules can make a software system difficult to maintain and evolve.  The higher the connascence, the more difficult it is to change one module without impacting others. | synonym: coupling, interdependence | antonym: decoupling, independence | noun
