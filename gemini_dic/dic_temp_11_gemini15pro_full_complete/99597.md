interiors:/ɪnˈtɪəriərz/
n.
内部；室内装潢；内景
the inside part of something, especially a building; the decoration or furnishings of a room or building
例句：The interiors of the houses were beautifully decorated. 这些房子的内部装饰精美。
例句：She specializes in designing stylish interiors for modern homes. 她专门为现代住宅设计时尚的室内装饰。
近义词：inside, inner parts, furnishings, decor
反义词：exterior, outside
词性：名词

interior:/ɪnˈtɪəriər/
n.
内部；内地；内政
the inside part of something; the inland part of a country; the domestic affairs of a country
例句：The interior of the church was dark and quiet. 教堂的内部昏暗而安静。
例句：They explored the vast interior of the Australian continent. 他们探索了澳大利亚大陆广阔的内陆地区。
近义词：inside, inner, inland
反义词：exterior, outside, coastal
词性：名词

interior:/ɪnˈtɪəriər/
adj.
内部的；内地的；国内的
situated on the inside; relating to the inland part of a country; relating to the domestic affairs of a country
例句：The interior walls were painted a soft beige. 内墙被漆成了柔和的米黄色。
例句：The interior ministry is responsible for national security. 内政部负责国家安全。
近义词：inner, inside, inland, domestic
反义词：exterior, outer, outside, foreign
词性：名词/形容词
