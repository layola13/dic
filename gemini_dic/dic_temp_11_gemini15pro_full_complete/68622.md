fantasying:/ˈfæntəsaɪzɪŋ/
v.
幻想；想像
The act of indulging in fantasies; imagining unreal or improbable things.
She was fantasying about winning the lottery. 她幻想着中了彩票。
近义词:imagining, daydreaming, dreaming
反义词:facing reality
词性:动词(present participle)
