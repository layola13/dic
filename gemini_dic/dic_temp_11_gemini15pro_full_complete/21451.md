blowout:/ˈbloʊaʊt/ (US) /ˈbləʊaʊt/ (UK)| n.| 爆裂，井喷；(非正式)轻松的胜利；(非正式)盛宴，狂欢；(美式)轮胎爆胎|A blowout is a sudden rupture or malfunction, often involving pressure. blowout:/ˈbloʊaʊt/ (US) /ˈbləʊaʊt/ (UK)| n.| (美式,俚语)头发吹干造型|A blowout is a hair styling technique using a hairdryer and often a round brush to create volume and smoothness.| I got a blowout at the salon for the wedding. 我去沙龙做了婚礼的发型。|近义词:hair styling, coif|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
