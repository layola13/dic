consorters:/kənˈsɔːrtərz/
noun
合作者，同伙，共谋者
people who associate with each other; companions or associates, especially in a questionable activity.
例句：The police arrested the thief and his consorters. 警察逮捕了小偷和他的同伙。

例句：The king's consorters were all nobles and knights. 国王的同伴都是贵族和骑士。

近义词：accomplices, associates, collaborators, partners, confederates
反义词：enemies, opponents, adversaries
词性:n.
