mythically:/ˈmɪθɪk(ə)li/
adv.
神话般地；虚构地；传奇般地
In a manner relating to, resembling, or characteristic of myths or mythmaking.
The hero was mythically described as having incredible strength.  这位英雄被神话般地描述为拥有不可思议的力量。

反义词:realistically, factually, historically
词性:副词
