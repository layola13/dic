enforcement:/ɪnˈfɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 强制，强迫 | the act of enforcing something. | The enforcement of the new regulations caused a lot of confusion. 新规的实施造成了很多混乱。| synonyms: compulsion, coercion, constraint | antonyms: freedom, liberty, persuasion | noun
