aggravating:/ˈæɡrəveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人恼火的，加重的 | Making a problem or situation worse or more serious; annoying. |  The traffic jam was aggravating, making me late for my appointment. 交通堵塞真让人恼火，害我约会迟到了。


The constant noise was aggravating his headache.  持续不断的噪音加重了他的头痛。 | 近义词：irritating, annoying, exasperating, worsening | 反义词：soothing, calming, alleviating, improving | 词性：形容词

aggravate:/ˈæɡrəveɪt/ | vt. | 使恶化，使加重，激怒 | To make a problem, injury, or offense worse or more serious; to annoy or exasperate someone. |  The humid weather aggravated his eczema. 潮湿的天气加重了他的湿疹。


His constant complaining aggravated his colleagues. 他不停的抱怨激怒了他的同事。 | 近义词：worsen, exacerbate, irritate, provoke | 反义词：improve, alleviate, soothe, calm | 词性：动词
