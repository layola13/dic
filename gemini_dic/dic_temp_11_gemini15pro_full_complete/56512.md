domesticize:/dəˈmɛstɪˌsaɪz/ /dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪt/ | vt.|驯化，教化|To adapt (an animal or plant) to life in intimate association with and to the advantage of humans.|例句：Humans domesticated the dog thousands of years ago.  人类在几千年前驯化了狗。

例句：They are trying to domesticate a wild species of cat. 他们正试图驯化一种野猫。


近义词：tame, train, cultivate|反义词:wild, untamed|词性:verb(动词)
