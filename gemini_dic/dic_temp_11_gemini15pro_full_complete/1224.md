accompanier:/əˈkʌmpəniər/ | n. | 伴奏者；陪同者 |Someone who accompanies another person, especially on a musical instrument or on a journey.  | The singer's accompanier played the piano beautifully. 歌手的伴奏者优美地演奏了钢琴。 |  The Queen’s accompanier on the trip was her private secretary. 女王此行的陪同人员是她的私人秘书。 | synonyms: companion, escort, attendant, partner | antonyms: loner, solo | noun

accompanist:/əˈkʌmpənɪst/ | n. | 伴奏者 | A person who plays a musical instrument as an accompaniment to another instrument, voice, or group. | The accompanist played a sensitive introduction to the song. 伴奏者为这首歌演奏了一段细腻的引子。 | The young violinist's accompanist was a seasoned pianist.  年轻小提琴手的伴奏是一位经验丰富的钢琴家。| synonyms: accompanier | antonyms: soloist | noun


词源:

From Late Middle English acompaniour, from Old French acompaniour, from acompaniier (see accompany).
