overclear:/ˌoʊvərˈklɪr/| adj.|过分清晰的，过度明确的，过于透明的|Excessively clear; more clear than necessary or desirable.  It implies a level of clarity that is unnatural or artificial, often to the point of being suspicious or revealing too much.|例句：The explanation was overclear, making me suspect they were hiding something. 解释过于清晰，让我怀疑他们在隐瞒什么。

例句：The overclear water of the swimming pool looked almost sterile. 游泳池里的水过于清澈，看起来几乎是无菌的。|近义词：transparent, lucid, explicit, overly clear|反义词：opaque, obscure, ambiguous, vague|词性:adjective
