adjuvant:/ˈædʒʊvənt/| n. adj.|佐剂；辅助的|A substance that enhances the body's immune response to an antigen.|The vaccine was given with an adjuvant to boost its effectiveness. 疫苗与佐剂一起给药以增强其有效性。|Synonyms: booster, enhancer, catalyst|Antonyms: inhibitor, suppressant|noun

adjuvant:/ˈædʒʊvənt/| adj.|辅助的|Serving to aid or contribute|Adjuvant therapy was used to treat the cancer. 辅助疗法被用来治疗癌症。|Synonyms: supplementary, ancillary, auxiliary|Antonyms: primary, main, principal|adjective
