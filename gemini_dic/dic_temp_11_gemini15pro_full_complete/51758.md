devotedly:/dɪˈvoʊtɪdli/| adv.|虔诚地，忠实地，专心地|In a devoted manner; with dedication and loyalty.  Showing strong love and affection.|She devotedly cared for her sick mother. 她虔诚地照顾她生病的母亲。|近义词：faithfully, loyally, diligently, earnestly|反义词：apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "devoted," which comes from the past participle of the verb "devote." Ultimately from Latin "dēvōtus," past participle of "dēvovēre" (to vow, dedicate).
