ergodic:/ɜːrˈɡɒdɪk/ | adj. | 各态历经的；遍历性的 | Relating to or denoting a process in which every sequence or sizable sample is representative of the whole. |  If the system is ergodic, then the time average along a single trajectory will be equal to the ensemble average. 如果系统是各态历经的，那么沿着单一轨迹的时间平均值将等于系综平均值。|  Ergodic processes are important in statistical mechanics. 各态历经过程在统计力学中很重要。 |  stochastic, random | deterministic, predictable | adjective
