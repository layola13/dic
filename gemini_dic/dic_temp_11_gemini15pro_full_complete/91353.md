hydropsy:/ˈhaɪdrɒpsi/ (US) /ˈhaɪdrəpsi/ (UK)| n. |水肿，积水|An abnormal accumulation of serous fluid in the tissues or in a body cavity.|The doctor diagnosed the patient with hydropsy due to kidney failure. 医生诊断该病人因肾衰竭而患有水肿。|Synonyms: edema, dropsy, swelling|Antonyms: dehydration|noun
