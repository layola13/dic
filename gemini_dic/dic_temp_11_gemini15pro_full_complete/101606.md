irreclaimable:/ˌɪrɪˈkleɪməbl/
adjective
不可挽回的，不能纠正的
Not able to be reclaimed or reformed; incapable of being recovered, restored, or improved.
The damage to the ecosystem was irreclaimable.  这个生态系统的破坏是不可挽回的。
The company lost an irreclaimable amount of money.  该公司损失了一笔无法挽回的资金。
近义词:irretrievable, irreparable, unrecoverable, lost
反义词:reclaimable, recoverable, retrievable, restorable
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + reclaimable (from reclaim, from re- (back, again) + claim (to demand, assert a right to)). Ultimately from Latin reclamare (to cry out against, oppose).
