consonants:/ˈkɑːnsənənts/ | n. |辅音|A basic speech sound in which the breath is at least partly obstructed and which can be combined with a vowel to form a syllable. |  Many words begin with consonants. 许多单词以辅音开头。| synonyms: non-vowels | antonyms: vowels | noun
