amputation:/ˌæmpjuˈteɪʃn/| n. |截肢，切断|The amputation of his leg was necessary to prevent the spread of infection. 他的腿必须截肢以防止感染扩散。|例句：The surgeon performed the amputation with precision.  外科医生精确地进行了截肢手术。

The amputation of the limb saved the patient's life. 截肢保住了病人的性命。

|近义词：excision, removal, ablation|反义词:reattachment, implantation|词性:noun
