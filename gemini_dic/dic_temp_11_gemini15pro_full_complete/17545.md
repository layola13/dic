beatifications:/biːˌætɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
宣福礼，荣列圣品
the action of declaring (someone) to beatified.
The beatifications of the two nuns drew thousands of pilgrims to the Vatican.  两位修女的宣福礼吸引了成千上万的朝圣者前往梵蒂冈。
近义词:canonizations, exaltations
反义词:condemnations, denunciations
