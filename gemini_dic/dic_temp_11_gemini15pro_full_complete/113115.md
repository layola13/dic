lung-busting:/ˈlʌŋˌbʌstɪŋ/| adj.|令人喘不过气来的，费力的|Extremely strenuous; requiring great effort and causing breathlessness.  It describes an activity that is so physically demanding that it makes you feel like your lungs are about to burst.|例句：Running a marathon is a lung-busting experience.  跑马拉松是一项令人喘不过气来的经历。

例句：The hike up the mountain was a lung-busting climb.  爬上这座山是一次累人的攀登。|近义词：exhausting, strenuous, grueling, arduous|反义词：easy, effortless, simple|词性:形容词
