outflashes:/ˌaʊtˈflæʃɪz/
verb
闪耀胜过，发出更强的闪光
shines more brightly than something else.
The lightning outflashed the city lights. 闪电比城市灯光更耀眼。
outdazzles, outshines
-
verb
(third-person singular simple present outflashes, present participle outflashing, simple past and past participle outflashed)
