mucusin:/ˈmjuːkəsɪn/
n.
粘蛋白
A glycoprotein constituent of mucus.
Mucusin is a major component of mucus, helping to trap bacteria and other foreign particles. 粘蛋白是粘液的主要成分，有助于捕获细菌和其他异物。
Mucusin plays a crucial role in protecting the lining of the airways. 粘蛋白在保护呼吸道内壁方面起着至关重要的作用。
