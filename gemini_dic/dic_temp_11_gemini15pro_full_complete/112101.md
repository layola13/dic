longlivedness:/ˌlɔːŋˈlɪvdnɪs/| n.|长寿，寿命长|The tortoise is known for its longlivedness. 乌龟以长寿而闻名。|例句：The secret to his family's longlivedness remains a mystery. 他家族长寿的秘诀仍然是个谜。

例句：Scientists are studying the longlivedness of certain species of trees. 科学家们正在研究某些树种的长寿。|近义词：longevity, durability|反义词：short life, brevity|词性:名词

词源:  From long-lived + -ness.  "long-lived" dates back to the late 14th century, from "long" + "lived" (past participle of "live").  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns meaning "state or quality of being," from Old English "-nes," "-nis."
