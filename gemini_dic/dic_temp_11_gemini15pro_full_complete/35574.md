coalpit:/ˈkəʊlpɪt/ (UK) /ˈkoʊlpɪt/ (US)| n. |煤矿，煤坑|A deep hole or shaft from which coal is mined. |The miners spent their days toiling in the dangerous coalpit. 矿工们每天都在危险的煤矿里辛苦劳作。

The canary in the coalpit warned of the impending danger.  煤矿里的金丝雀预示着即将到来的危险。


|近义词：mine, colliery|反义词: N/A |noun|

coalpit:/ˈkəʊlpɪt/ (UK) /ˈkoʊlpɪt/ (US)| n. |困境，危险的处境 (比喻义)|A difficult or dangerous situation. (Figurative) |The economy is in a coalpit right now.  目前的经济状况处于困境。

Their relationship had become a coalpit of resentment and anger. 他们的关系已经变成了怨恨和愤怒的深渊。


|近义词：predicament, quagmire|反义词: haven, sanctuary |noun|
