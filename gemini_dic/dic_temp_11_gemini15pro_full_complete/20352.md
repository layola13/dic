bismuthine:/ˈbɪzməθiːn/
n.
铋化氢; 氢化铋
A colorless, unstable, poisonous gas (BiH3) analogous to ammonia and arsine.
Bismuthine is a highly unstable gas. 铋化氢是一种极不稳定的气体。
Bismuthine decomposes rapidly at room temperature. 铋化氢在室温下迅速分解。
synonym: bismuth hydride, hydrogen bismuthide
antonym: N/A
noun
