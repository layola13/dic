counterarguing: /ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuɪŋ/ | v. | 反驳，提出相反的论点 | Presenting reasons or evidence in opposition to an argument. |  He was skilled at counterarguing his opponent's points. 他善于反驳对手的观点。

|  She spent the entire debate counterarguing the other team's proposals.  她在整个辩论过程中都在反驳对方团队的提议。

| 近义词: rebutting, refuting, disputing, challenging | 反义词: agreeing, conceding, accepting | 词性: 动词 (Verb)
