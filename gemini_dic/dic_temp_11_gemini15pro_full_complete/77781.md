generalises:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzɪz/
verb
概括，归纳，推广
To infer or form a conclusion/opinion from a set of facts or examples; to make a general statement about something based on limited evidence.
This study generalises the findings from a small sample to the wider population. 这项研究将从小样本中获得的发现推广到更广泛的人群。
The theory generalises to a wide range of phenomena.  该理论可以推广到各种现象。
synonym: summarize, conclude, infer, deduce, extrapolate
antonym: specialize, particularize, specify
词性: 动词(verb)
