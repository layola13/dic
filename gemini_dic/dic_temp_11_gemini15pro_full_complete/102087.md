isoprene: /ˈaɪsəˌpriːn/ /ˈɪsəˌpriːn/ | n. | 异戊二烯 |  A branched-chain unsaturated hydrocarbon, 2-methyl-1,3-butadiene, used in the manufacture of synthetic rubber. It is a volatile liquid with a characteristic odor and is also a natural product, being a component of turpentine and a precursor of many natural substances. |  Isoprene units are linked together to form long chains in natural rubber. 异戊二烯单元连接在一起，在天然橡胶中形成长链。| 近义词: 2-methylbuta-1,3-diene | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
