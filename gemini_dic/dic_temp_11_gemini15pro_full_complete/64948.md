esterification:/ɪˌstɜːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|酯化（作用）|The process of forming an ester by reacting an alcohol with a carboxylic acid.  This process usually involves an acid catalyst and heat. |The esterification of acetic acid and ethanol produces ethyl acetate. 醋酸和乙醇的酯化反应生成乙酸乙酯。|近义词: ester formation|反义词: hydrolysis|词性: noun
