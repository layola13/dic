alcheringa:/ˌæltʃəˈrɪŋə/ | n. | 梦幻时代;  创世时期;  (澳大利亚土著传说中的)梦幻时代 |A mythical time of creation in Australian Aboriginal culture.  |The alcheringa is a central concept in many Aboriginal Australian belief systems.  梦幻时代是许多澳大利亚原住民信仰体系的核心概念。 |  The paintings depict stories from the alcheringa. 这些画描绘了梦幻时代的故事。 | synonyms: dreamtime, creation period | antonyms: present, reality | noun

alcheringa:/ˌæltʃəˈrɪŋə/ | n. |  (尤指澳大利亚的)第一次开花;  (尤指澳大利亚的)第一次鸣叫 | The first flowering or the first song of birds, especially in Australia. | The alcheringa of the wattle trees marks the start of spring. 金合欢树的第一次开花标志着春天的开始。| The kookaburra's alcheringa echoed through the valley.  笑翠鸟的第一次鸣叫在山谷中回荡。| synonyms: first bloom, first song | antonyms: last bloom, last song | noun
