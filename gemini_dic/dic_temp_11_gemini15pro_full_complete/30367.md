centerboards: /ˈsɛntərˌbɔrdz/
n.
中心板；活动龙骨
A retractable keel or fin on a sailboat, used to reduce leeway and improve windward performance.
The centerboards were lowered as the boat sailed closer to the wind.  船只迎风航行时，中心板被降下。

rudder
