appointees:/əˌpɔɪnˈtiːz/
n.
被任命者；指定人
people who have been appointed to a particular job or position
The new appointees will begin work next week. 新任命的官员将于下周开始工作。
synonyms: nominees, selectees, designates
antonyms: electors, nominators
