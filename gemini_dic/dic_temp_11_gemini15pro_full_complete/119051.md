metif: /meɪˈtiːf/ (also métis) | n. | 混血儿（尤指欧洲人和美洲原住民的混血后代）; | A person of mixed Indigenous and European, usually French, ancestry. |  Louis Riel was a prominent Métis leader. 路易·里尔是一位著名的梅蒂斯人领袖。

The Métis people have a rich cultural heritage. 梅蒂斯人拥有丰富的文化遗产。


近义词: mixed-race, hybrid | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


词源: From Canadian French métis, from French métis, from Latin mixticius ‘mixed’, from mixtus, past participle of miscere ‘to mix’.
