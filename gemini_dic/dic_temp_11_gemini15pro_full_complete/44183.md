crl:/ˈsiːɑːrˈɛl/ |abbr.| Carriage Return Linefeed (回车换行)|A telecommunications acronym used to denote a carriage return and line feed. It signifies the end of a line of text.|Example: The old printers used CRL to move to the next line. 旧式打印机使用 CRL 移至下一行。|Synonym: CR/LF, newline|Antonym: N/A|abbreviation
