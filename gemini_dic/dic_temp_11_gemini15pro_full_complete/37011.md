columella:/ˌkɒljʊˈmɛlə/ (US) /ˌkɑːljəˈmɛlə/| n. |鼻小柱|The fleshy termination of the nasal septum.|The surgeon carefully reconstructed the columella after the injury.|Synonyms: nasal septum termination|Antonyms: N/A|noun
