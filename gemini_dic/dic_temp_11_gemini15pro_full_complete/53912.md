discouraged:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒd/
adjective
沮丧的，灰心的
Feeling or showing a loss of confidence or enthusiasm.
He felt discouraged after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感到很沮丧。
近义词: disheartened, demoralized, dejected
反义词: encouraged, hopeful, optimistic
词性: adjective

discourage:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/
verb
使气馁，使沮丧
To cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm.
The coach's harsh words discouraged the team. 教练严厉的话使队员们灰心丧气。
近义词: deter, dissuade
反义词: encourage, inspire
词性: verb
