oscilloscopic: /ˌɒsɪləˈskɒpɪk/ (US) /ˌɒsɪləˈskɒpɪk/ (UK)| adj.|示波器的；示波法测定的|Relating to or displayed by an oscilloscope; measured by an oscilloscope.|The oscilloscopic trace showed a regular sine wave. 示波器轨迹显示出一个规则的正弦波。|Synonyms: cathode-ray, electronic|Antonyms: None|adjective

oscilloscope: /əˈsɪləskəʊp/ (US) /əˈsɪləskəʊp/ (UK)| n.|示波器|An electronic instrument used for displaying and measuring electrical signals, typically as a two-dimensional graph.|The engineer used an oscilloscope to analyze the circuit's output. 工程师用示波器分析电路的输出。|Synonyms: cathode-ray oscilloscope, CRO|Antonyms: None|noun
