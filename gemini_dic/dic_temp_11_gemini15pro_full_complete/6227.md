amnestied:/ˈæmnɪstiːd/ | v. |被赦免的|Released from punishment or legal consequences, typically for political offenses.  Past participle of "amnesty". | The political prisoners were amnestied after the new government came to power.  那些政治犯在新政府上台后被赦免了。|Synonyms: pardoned, reprieved, exempted, absolved | Antonyms: convicted, sentenced, penalized | verb
