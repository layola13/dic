oratorically:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːrɪkli/
adverb
修辞地；雄辩地
In a manner relating to the art of public speaking; eloquently.
He spoke oratorically, captivating the audience with his powerful voice. 他演讲时很有感染力，用他洪亮的声音吸引了观众。
She delivered the eulogy oratorically, moving many to tears.  她充满感情地致悼词，使许多人感动得流下了眼泪。
近义词: eloquently, rhetorically, expressively
反义词: plainly, simply, inarticulately
词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *oratoricalis, from Latin orator + -icalis.  Ultimately from Latin orare "to speak, pray."
