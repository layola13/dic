heaver:/ˈhiːvər/| n.|举重运动员；呼吸困难的人；剧烈喘息的人|A person who lifts heavy objects, especially as a job or sport; or a person who breathes with difficulty or gasps.|例句：The heaver strained to lift the enormous weight.  举重运动员费力地举起巨大的重量。

例句：After running the marathon, he became a heaver, struggling to catch his breath. 跑完马拉松后，他呼吸困难，努力喘气。|近义词：weightlifter, lifter, strongman|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly weakling)|词性:noun
