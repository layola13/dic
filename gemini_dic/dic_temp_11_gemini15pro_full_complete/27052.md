calendar: /ˈkælɪndər/ (US) /ˈkælɪndə(r)/ (UK)| v. |安排；预定|To enter an appointment or event in a calendar.|  The meeting is calendared for next Tuesday. 会议安排在下周二。| synonyms: schedule, book, arrange | antonyms: cancel, postpone | verb
