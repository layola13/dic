leeboard:/ˈliːbɔːrd/
noun
活动式船舷板， leeboard
A flat board attached to the side of a sailboat to prevent it from drifting leeward.
The leeboard was lowered into the water to provide lateral resistance. 活动式船舷板被放下水中以提供横向阻力。
centerboard, daggerboard
