hunadi: /huːˈnɑːdi/ | n. | 胡纳迪 |  A Romanian surname, derived from the historical region of Hunedoara in Transylvania. It can also refer to the ruling family of that region, notably John Hunyadi, a leading military and political figure in 15th-century Hungary. | Example: John Hunadi was a renowned warrior.  约翰·胡纳迪是一位著名的战士。| Synonym: (As a surname, no direct synonym.
