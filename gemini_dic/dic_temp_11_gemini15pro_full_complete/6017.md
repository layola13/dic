ameliorable:/əˈmiːliərəbl/
adjective
可改善的，可改进的
capable of being made better or improved.
The patient's condition is ameliorable with proper treatment.  病人的病情通过适当的治疗可以得到改善。
Her bad habits are ameliorable through therapy. 她的坏习惯可以通过治疗得到改善。
近义词: improvable, amendable, reformable,  remediable
反义词: incurable, irremediable, unimprovable
词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French ameillorer, based on Latin meliorare ‘make better’, from melior.
