meanies:/ˈmiːniz/| n. |坏蛋，刻薄鬼| A person who is unkind, cruel, or deliberately hurtful. |Those meanies took my ball!  那些坏蛋抢走了我的球！| The meanies wouldn't let me play with them. 那些刻薄鬼不让我跟他们一起玩。|Synonyms: bullies, brats, villains, jerks|Antonyms: kind people, sweethearts, angels|Noun
