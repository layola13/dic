dogmatician:/dɒɡˈmætɪʃən/ | n. | 教条主义者；独断家 | A person who asserts opinions as though they were unquestionable truths. | He was a strict dogmatician, unwilling to consider any views other than his own. 他是一个严格的教条主义者，不愿考虑除了他自己以外的任何观点。| Synonyms: doctrinaire, authoritarian, rigid | Antonyms: freethinker, skeptic, open-minded | noun

dogmatic:/dɒɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 教条的；武断的 | Inclined to lay down principles as incontrovertibly true. |  She delivered her pronouncements in a dogmatic tone. 她以教条的语气发表了她的声明。 | Synonyms: assertive, opinionated, peremptory | Antonyms: flexible, open-minded, receptive | adjective
