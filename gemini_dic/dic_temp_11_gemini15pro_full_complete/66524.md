exhauster:/ɪɡˈzɔːstər/ /ɪɡˈzɔːstər/ | n. | 排气装置，抽风机 | A device that removes air, gases, or fumes, typically by suction. |  The exhauster in the kitchen removes cooking fumes. 厨房里的排气装置可以去除烹饪油烟。

The mine uses powerful exhausters to remove stale air. 矿井使用强大的抽风机排出污浊空气。


| synonym: extractor, fan, ventilator | antonym:  intake, blower | noun
