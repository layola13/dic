ossifier: /ˈɒsɪfaɪər/ (US) /ˈɒsɪfaɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|骨化器；成骨细胞|A cell that is responsible for the formation of bone.|Osteoblasts are the main ossifiers in the body. 成骨细胞是体内主要的骨化器。|Synonyms: osteoblast, bone-forming cell|Antonyms: osteoclast|Noun


ossify: /ˈɒsɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈɒsɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v. |骨化；硬化；僵化|1. Turn into bone; become bony.  2. Become rigid or inflexible in habits, opinions, etc.|1. The cartilage will eventually ossify into bone. 软骨最终会骨化成骨。 2. His political views had ossified over the years. 他的政治观点多年来已经僵化。|Synonyms: harden, solidify, rigidify, fossilize|Antonyms: soften, liquefy|Verb
