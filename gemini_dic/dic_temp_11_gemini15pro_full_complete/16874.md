basilical:/bəˈsɪlɪkəl/
adj.|长方形的，教堂式的|Having the form of a basilica; oblong, with a nave, aisles, and apse.|The church has a basilical plan.|近义词:churchlike, cathedral-like|反义词:|adjective

basilical:/bəˈsɪlɪkəl/
n.|长方形教堂|A large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse, used in ancient Rome as a court of law or for public assemblies. Also, an early Christian or medieval church built on this plan.|St. Peter's Basilica in Rome is a renowned example of basilical architecture.|近义词:cathedral, church|反义词:|noun
