nibbler:/ˈnɪblər/| n.|啃咬者；少量进食者；|A nibbler is someone or something that nibbles, often referring to a person who eats small amounts of food throughout the day rather than having large meals, or an animal that gnaws on things.|The rabbit is a nibbler, constantly chewing on grass and twigs.|近义词：grazer, browser, picker|反义词: gourmand, glutton|noun
