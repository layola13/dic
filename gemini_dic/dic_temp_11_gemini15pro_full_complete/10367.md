appen:/ˈæpən/
词性: suffix | 后缀
中文翻译: 附加, 添加
英文解析: A suffix that forms verbs meaning "to add or attach (something) to something else." It can also form nouns derived from such verbs.  It comes from Latin *appōnere, from ad- "to" + pōnere "to put, place".
例句:
The suffix "-en" is often used to form verbs, as in "strengthen" or "lengthen".
后缀“-en”常用于构成动词，如“strengthen”或“lengthen”。

The word "happen" is not directly related to the suffix "-appen".
“happen”一词与后缀“-appen”没有直接关系。
