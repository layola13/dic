amped:/æmpt/ | adj. |兴奋的，激动的，充满活力的| Filled with enthusiasm or excitement; energized.| I'm so amped for the concert tonight! 我今晚去看演唱会，太兴奋了！| 近义词: excited, enthusiastic, energized, pumped, psyched | 反义词: bored, unenthusiastic, apathetic, tired, lethargic | 词性:形容词

amped:/æmpt/ | v. |（使）兴奋，（使）激动 (past tense and past participle of amp) | To make someone or something more exciting or energetic. | The coach amped up the team before the big game. 教练在大赛前鼓舞了球队。| 近义词: energize, excite, stimulate, invigorate | 反义词: calm, soothe, tranquilize, bore| 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From amp (v.), short for amplify.  Ultimately from Latin *amplifico, from amplus (“large”) + -fico (making).
