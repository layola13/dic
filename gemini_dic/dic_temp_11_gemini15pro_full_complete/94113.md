impeccable:/ɪmˈpekəbl/| adj.|无瑕疵的，完美的|In a state of perfection; faultless.|Her impeccable manners impressed everyone at the dinner party. 她的无可挑剔的举止给晚宴上的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|例句：His English pronunciation is impeccable. 他的英语发音完美无瑕。|近义词：flawless, perfect, immaculate, unblemished|反义词：faulty, flawed, imperfect, blemished|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin impeccabilis, from in- ‘not’ + peccare ‘to sin’.
