fleeciest:/ˈfliːsiɪst/| adj.|最羊毛似的，最柔软的|Superlative form of fleecy: most fleecy.  Covered with or resembling fleece; woolly and soft.|The fleeciest blanket I've ever felt kept me warm all night. 我摸过的最柔软的毯子让我整晚都很暖和。|The newborn lamb had the fleeciest wool. 新生羔羊的羊毛最柔软。|近义词：softest, woolliest, fluffiest|反义词：coarsest, roughest|词性:形容词

词源: From fleece + -y + -est.  "Fleece" comes from Old English *flys, from Proto-Germanic *fleusaną.
