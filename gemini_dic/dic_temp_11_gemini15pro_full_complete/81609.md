greatly:/ˈɡreɪtli/| adv.|很大程度上，非常|To a great extent; much.  It enhances the meaning of the word it modifies.|例句：The new policy greatly improved working conditions. 新政策大大改善了工作条件。|近义词：considerably, significantly, substantially, vastly|反义词: slightly, little, insignificantly|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *gretly*, from Old English *grēatlice* (“greatly, nobly, splendidly”), equivalent to *great* + *-ly*. Cognate with Scots *greatlie* (“greatly”), West Frisian *greatlik* (“greatly”), Dutch *grootlijks* (“largely, extensively”), German *großlich* (“largely, extensively”).
