antiprogressive:/ˌæntiprəˈɡrɛsɪv/| adj.|反进步的，反对进步的|Opposing or hindering progress or reform; resistant to new ideas or methods.|The antiprogressive policies hindered the development of the nation. 反动政策阻碍了国家的发展.|
例句：
His antiprogressive views made him unpopular with the younger generation.
他的反进步观点让他在年轻一代中不受欢迎。

The antiprogressive faction within the party blocked any attempts at reform.
党内的反进步派阻止了任何改革的尝试。

|近义词：reactionary, conservative, regressive|反义词：progressive, liberal, reformist|词性：形容词
