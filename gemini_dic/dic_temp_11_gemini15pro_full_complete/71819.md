fleer:/flɪr/| v. |嘲笑，讥讽|To laugh or grimace in a way that shows scorn or contempt.|He fleered at their attempts to climb the mountain. 他嘲笑他们想要爬山的尝试。|The bully fleered at the smaller children. 欺负弱小者对较小的孩子们冷笑。|近义词：sneer, mock, scoff, jeer|反义词：compliment, praise|词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *flerien, from Old Norse *flīra (“to grin, make grimaces”).
