crabbled:/ˈkræbəld/ | v. | 捕捞螃蟹; 挑剔; 抱怨; |  To fish for crabs. Also, to find fault with; criticize. |  He crabbled for hours but only caught a few small crabs. 他捞了几个小时的螃蟹，但只抓到几只小螃蟹。 |  The editor crabbled at every sentence in my article. 编辑对我文章中的每个句子都吹毛求疵。 | 近义词: fished (for crabs), criticized, complained | 反义词: praised, complimented | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the verb "crab" (to fish for crabs), which in turn comes from the Old English "crabba." The extended meaning of "to find fault" arose later, perhaps in analogy to a crab's pinching claws.
