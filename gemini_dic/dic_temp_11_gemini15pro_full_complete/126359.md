myriapoda:/ˌmɪriˈæpədə/ | n. | 多足纲动物 | A class of arthropods with many legs, including centipedes and millipedes. | Myriapoda are found in a variety of habitats. 多足纲动物存在于各种各样的栖息地。|  The millipede is a myriapod.  千足虫是一种多足纲动物。| synonyms: Myriopoda | antonyms: None | noun

myriapod:/ˌmɪriˈæpɒd/ | n. | 多足类动物 | Any of various arthropods of the class Myriapoda, characterized by numerous body segments, each bearing one or two pairs of legs. | Centipedes and millipedes are myriapods. 蜈蚣和千足虫是多足类动物。 |  I found a myriapod crawling in my garden. 我在我的花园里发现了一只多足类动物在爬行。 | synonyms:  centipede, millipede | antonyms: None | noun
