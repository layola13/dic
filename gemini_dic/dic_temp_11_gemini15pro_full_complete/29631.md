cater:/ˈkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| v.|提供餐饮服务，迎合|to provide food and drinks for a social event or to supply what is needed or desired|例句：The company caters for weddings and corporate events. 这家公司承办婚礼和企业活动。|近义词：provide, supply, serve|反义词:|verb

cater to:/ˈkeɪtər tuː/ (US), /ˈkeɪtə(r) tuː/ (UK)| v.|迎合，满足|to try to satisfy a particular need or demand, sometimes in a way that does not show high standards|例句：The magazine caters to the interests of businesswomen. 这本杂志迎合了商业女性的兴趣。|近义词：indulge, pander to|反义词:|verb
