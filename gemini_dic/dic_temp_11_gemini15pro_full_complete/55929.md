dixielands: /ˈdɪksiˌlændz/ | n. | 南方乐曲；南方地区（尤指美国南部）|  A style of jazz music, originating in New Orleans in the early 20th century, characterized by a strong rhythm and improvisation. Often refers to the Southern states of the USA, especially those associated with this music. |  I love listening to dixielands music, it's so lively. 我喜欢听南方爵士乐，它非常活泼。|Synonyms: South, Confederate States| Antonyms: North, Union States| noun

dixieland: /ˈdɪksiˌlænd/ | adj. | 南方的；美国南部的 | Relating to or characteristic of the southern states of the U.S., especially with regard to their traditional culture and music. |  That restaurant plays dixieland jazz. 那家餐厅演奏南方爵士乐。 | Synonyms: Southern, Confederate | Antonyms: Northern, Union | adjective
