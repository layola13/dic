deluding:/dɪˈluːdɪŋ/
adjective
欺骗的，迷惑的
making someone believe something that is not true
例句：He was deluding himself into thinking he could win. 他欺骗自己，以为自己能赢。
例句：The advertisement was deluding, promising results that were impossible. 这则广告具有欺骗性，承诺了一些不可能实现的结果。

近义词：deceptive, misleading, tricking, beguiling
反义词：honest, truthful, enlightening, revealing
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēlūdō*, present active participle of *dēlūdere*  "to mock, deceive, play false," from *de-* "down, away" + *lūdere* "to play."  The sense evolution is from "play away from" to "mock" and then "deceive."
