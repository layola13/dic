druidism:/ˈdruːɪdɪzəm/ |n.|德鲁伊教；德鲁伊文化|A religion practiced by the ancient Celts of Gaul, Britain, and Ireland, characterized by belief in a pantheon of gods and goddesses, reverence for nature, and the practice of rituals and sacrifices.|例句：Modern Druidism often incorporates elements of paganism and nature worship. 现代德鲁伊教通常融合了异教和自然崇拜的元素。

例句：The study of Druidism reveals a complex system of beliefs and practices. 对德鲁伊教的研究揭示了一个复杂的信仰和实践体系。|近义词：paganism, Celtic religion|反义词: Christianity, Islam|词性: noun
