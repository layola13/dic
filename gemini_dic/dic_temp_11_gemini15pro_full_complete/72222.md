florets:/ˈflɔːrɪts/ | n. |小花; 小花序|A small flower, especially one of the small flowers making up a composite flower head, such as a daisy or broccoli.|The cauliflower was made up of tiny florets.|Synonyms: floweret, blossom, bloom|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a small flower part.  Perhaps "whole head" in the context of a composite flower) | Noun

floret:/ˈflɔːrɪt/ | n. |小花; 小花序|One of the small flowers that make up a larger flower head, like in broccoli or cauliflower.  Can also refer to a small flower in general.|She carefully separated the florets of the broccoli before steaming them. |Synonyms: floweret, blossom, bloom |Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a small flower part) | Noun
