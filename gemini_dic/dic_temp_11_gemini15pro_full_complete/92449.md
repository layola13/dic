hypophysis:/haɪˈpɒfɪsɪs/ /haɪˈpɑːfɪsɪs/
n.
垂体
The master gland of the endocrine system, located at the base of the brain.  It secretes hormones that control growth, metabolism, and reproduction.
The hypophysis plays a crucial role in regulating the body's functions.  垂体在调节身体机能方面起着至关重要的作用。
