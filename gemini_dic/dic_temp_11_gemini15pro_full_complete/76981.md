gardai:/ˈɡɑːrdiː/| n.|警察（爱尔兰共和国警察部队）|The Gardaí are investigating the incident. 爱尔兰警察正在调查这起事件。|例句：The gardaí were called to the scene of the accident.  警察被叫到事故现场。

例句：Several gardaí were injured in the riot.  几名警察在骚乱中受伤。

近义词：police, police officers|反义词:criminals|词性:noun
