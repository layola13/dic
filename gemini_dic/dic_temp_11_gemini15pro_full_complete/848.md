abstraction's:/æbˈstrækʃənz/
noun
抽象的概念; 抽象化; 抽象作品; 心不在焉;
The possessive form of "abstraction," referring to something that belongs to or is associated with the concept of abstraction.  It signifies the quality of dealing with ideas rather than events.
The artist's work explored the abstraction's power to evoke emotion.  这位艺术家的作品探索了抽象引发情感的力量。
synonyms: concept's, idea's, notion's
antonyms: concrete's, reality's, specific's
