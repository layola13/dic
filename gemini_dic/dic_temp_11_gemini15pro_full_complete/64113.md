eprint:/ˈiːprɪnt/ | n. | 电子预印本，网络预印本 | An electronic version of a research paper that is made available online before it is formally published in a journal. |  Example:  The researcher posted the eprint on arXiv before submitting it to a journal. 研究人员在将论文提交给期刊之前，将电子预印本发布在arXiv上。

Example:  Many physicists share their findings through eprints before peer review. 许多物理学家在同行评审之前通过电子预印本分享他们的发现。 | 近义词: preprint, online preprint | 反义词: postprint | 词性: noun
