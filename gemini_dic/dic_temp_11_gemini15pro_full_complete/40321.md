contouring:/ˈkɒntʊərɪŋ (UK), kənˈtʊrɪŋ (US)/| n.| 修容，轮廓塑造 | The act of using makeup to create shadows and highlights that enhance the facial structure. | She used contouring to make her cheekbones look more defined. 她用修容使她的颧骨看起来更立体。| synonyms: sculpting, shaping | antonyms: highlighting, flattening | noun

contour:/ˈkɒntʊər (UK), ˈkɑːntʊr (US)/| n. | 轮廓，等高线 | The outline or shape of something, especially a curved shape. | The artist drew the contours of the model's face.  这位艺术家画出了模特脸部的轮廓。| synonyms: outline, shape, profile | antonyms:  interior, center | noun
