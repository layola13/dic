chlorosis:/kləˈrəʊsɪs/ /klɔːˈroʊsɪs/| n.|萎黄病，变色病|An abnormal condition in plants characterized by yellowing of the leaves, due to a lack of chlorophyll.  This can be caused by nutrient deficiencies, disease, or environmental factors.|The farmer noticed chlorosis in his crops and suspected a magnesium deficiency.|近义词:yellowing, etiolation|反义词: (None direct antonyms, could consider "healthy foliage," "vigorous growth")|noun
