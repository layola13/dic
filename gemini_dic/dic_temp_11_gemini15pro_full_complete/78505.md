ghazel:/ˈɡɑːzəl/ | n. | 抒情诗; غزل | A lyric poem with a fixed number of verses and a repeated rhyme, often on the theme of love, and normally set to music. |  She wrote a beautiful ghazel about the joys of spring. 她写了一首关于春天喜悦的优美的抒情诗。

The ghazel is a popular form of poetry in Persian and Urdu literature.  抒情诗是波斯语和乌尔都语文学中一种流行的诗歌形式。| 近义词: lyric, poem, ode | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps prose) | 词性: noun
