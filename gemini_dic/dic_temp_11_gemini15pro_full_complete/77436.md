gawker:/ˈɡɔːkər/| n. | 围观者；(尤指)爱看热闹(或耸人听闻事物)的人 | someone who stares rudely or stupidly, especially at something unpleasant or shocking |  The street was crowded with gawkers after the accident. 事故发生后，街上挤满了围观者。| synonyms: onlooker, spectator, observer, bystander, rubbernecker | antonyms: participant | noun
