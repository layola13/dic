disputably:/dɪˈspjuːtəbli/ | adv. | 有争议地；可争辩地 | Open to argument or debate; not definitely or certainly. |  It is disputably the best movie of the year.  它可以说是年度最佳影片。 | Synonyms: arguably, debatably, possibly, conceivably | Antonyms: indisputably, undoubtedly, certainly, definitely | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From dispute (v.) + -able + -ly.  Dispute comes from Old French "disputer" and ultimately from Latin "disputare" meaning "to discuss, debate, argue."
