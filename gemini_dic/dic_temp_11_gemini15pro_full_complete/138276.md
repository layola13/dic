notarial:/nəʊˈtɛəriəl/| adj.|公证的；公证人的|Relating to or done by a notary public.  Pertaining to the office or duties of a notary.|例句：The document requires a notarial seal. 这份文件需要公证印章。|例句：He obtained notarial certification for the contract. 他获得了合同的公证认证。|近义词：certified, attested, authenticated|反义词：uncertified, unattested, unauthenticated|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *notariālis, from Latin notārius (“shorthand writer, secretary”), from nota (“mark, shorthand character”).
