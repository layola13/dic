anti-darwinists:/ˈæntiˈdɑːrwɪnɪsts/
n.
反达尔文主义者
Those who oppose or reject Darwin's theory of evolution by natural selection.
The conference attracted a number of anti-darwinists who presented alternative theories.  这会议吸引了一些反达尔文主义者，他们提出了替代理论。
Anti-darwinists often cite gaps in the fossil record as evidence against evolution. 反达尔文主义者经常引用化石记录中的空白作为反对进化论的证据。
近义词:creationists, opponents of evolution
反义词:darwinists, evolutionists
