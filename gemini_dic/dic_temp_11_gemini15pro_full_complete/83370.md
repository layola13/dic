gyronny:/ˈdʒaɪrɒni/ | adj. | 瓜分状的，螺旋状的 | Divided into six or eight triangular sections of alternating tinctures, radiating from the center of a shield. | The gyronny flag was striking against the blue sky. 瓜分状的旗帜在蓝天下显得格外醒目。| 近义词: spiral, whirling | 反义词: straight, linear | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Old French *gironné, from giron (“gore, triangular piece”), from Frankish *giron, from Proto-Germanic *wīrą (“wire, metal thread”).
