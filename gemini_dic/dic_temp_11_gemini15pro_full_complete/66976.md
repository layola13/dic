experimentist:/ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntɪst/
n.
实验者；实验主义者
A person who performs experiments, especially in science.  One who favors experimentation or emphasizes experiment.
The experimentist carefully recorded the results of each trial. 这位实验者仔细记录了每次试验的结果。
synonyms: experimenter, researcher, scientist, investigator
antonyms: theorist, speculator
noun

experiment:/ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/
n.
实验；试验
A scientific procedure undertaken to make a discovery, test a hypothesis, or demonstrate a known fact.
The experiment proved the hypothesis.  这个实验证明了假设。
synonyms: test, trial, investigation
antonyms: observation, theory
noun
