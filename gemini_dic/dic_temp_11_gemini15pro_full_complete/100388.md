intersubsistence:/ˌɪntər səbˈsɪstəns/ | n. |相互依存，互相依赖 |The intersubsistence of the two communities was essential for their survival.  两个社群的相互依存对他们的生存至关重要。|例句：The symbiotic relationship highlights the intersubsistence of the two species.  这种共生关系突出了两个物种的相互依存。

例句：The complex intersubsistence of the global economy makes individual nations vulnerable to distant crises. 复杂的全球经济相互依存关系使得个别国家容易受到远方危机的影响。

|近义词：interdependence, mutual dependence, codependence |反义词: independence, autonomy |词性:名词

词源:  "inter-" (between, among) + "subsistence" (the state of remaining in being or existence).  "Inter-" 来自拉丁语，"subsistence" 来自拉丁语 "subsistentia,"  意为 “存在，生存”。
