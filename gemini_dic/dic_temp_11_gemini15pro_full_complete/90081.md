hostesses:/ˈhoʊstɪsɪz/ | n. |女主人；女主持人；女服务员|A woman who receives or entertains guests in a social or official capacity, or a woman who attends to patrons in a restaurant. |The hostesses greeted the guests warmly at the door. 女主人在门口热情地迎接客人。

The airline hostesses demonstrated the safety procedures.  航空公司的空姐演示了安全程序。| 近义词:host, server, waitress | 反义词: guest, visitor | Noun
