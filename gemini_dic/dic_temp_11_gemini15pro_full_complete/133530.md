nonfiduciaries:/ˌnɑːn.fɪˈduːʃiˌɛriz/ | n. | 非信托人；非受托人 |  Persons or entities that do not have a legal or ethical duty to act in the best interests of another party.  They are not bound by fiduciary duties such as loyalty, prudence, and good faith. |  The financial advisors in this case were acting as nonfiduciaries, so they were not legally obligated to prioritize their clients' best interests.  非信托人；非受托人 | 近义词: nontrustees | 反义词: fiduciaries | 词性: noun (plural)

nonfiduciary:/ˌnɑːn.fɪˈduːʃiˌɛri/ | adj. | 非信托的；非受托的 | Describing a relationship where one party does not have a legal or ethical duty to act in the best interests of another. |  He offered nonfiduciary investment advice. 他提供了非信托投资建议。 | 近义词: nontrust | 反义词: fiduciary | 词性: adjective
