credences:/ˈkriːdənsɪz/| n. |信用；凭证；国书|Mental acceptance of something as true or real; belief.  A credential, especially a letter of introduction or recommendation.|The ambassador presented her credences to the king. 大使向国王递交了国书。|Synonyms: beliefs, convictions, trust, faith|Antonyms: disbelief, doubt, skepticism|Noun
