overirrigating: /ˌōvərˈɪrɪˌɡeɪtɪŋ/
Verb, present participle
过度灌溉
The act of watering plants or crops excessively, to the point of causing harm.
The farmer was overirrigating his fields, which led to root rot.  农民过度灌溉了他的田地，导致根部腐烂。
Overwatering the lawn can damage the grass. 过度浇灌草坪会损害草。
近义词: overwatering
反义词: underwatering
词性: Verb (present participle)

Etymology:
From over- + irrigating, the present participle of irrigate.
"Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "excessive, too much."
"Irrigate" comes from the Latin "irrigare," meaning "to water, to moisten," from "in-" (in, into) + "rigare" (to water, to wet).
