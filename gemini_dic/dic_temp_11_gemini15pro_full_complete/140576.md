oligochete:/ˈɑːlɪɡoʊˌkiːt/ | n. | 寡毛类动物 |A subclass of annelid worms characterized by having few bristles (chaetae) on their bodies, typically including earthworms. | Earthworms are a common type of oligochete. 蚯蚓是一种常见的寡毛类动物。| Oligochaetes play a vital role in soil health. 寡毛类动物对土壤健康起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: earthworm (specifically) | 反义词: polychaete | 词性: noun
