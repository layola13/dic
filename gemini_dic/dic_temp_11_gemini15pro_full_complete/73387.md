forehear:/ˈfɔːrˌhɪər/| n.|前炉，熔窑的前部|The forehearth is used to condition the molten glass before it enters the forming machine. 前炉用于在熔融玻璃进入成型机之前对其进行调节。|The temperature of the forehearth is carefully controlled. 前炉的温度受到精确控制。|近义词：working end, front end|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑furnace back end)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'fore-' (meaning 'front' or 'before') + 'hearth' (originally meaning a fireplace or furnace).  'Forehearth' thus refers to the front part of a furnace.
