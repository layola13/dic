heuristic's:/hjʊˈrɪstɪks/ | n. | 启发式方法，经验法则 |  The possessive form of "heuristic," referring to something belonging to or associated with a heuristic.  A heuristic is a practical method for problem-solving or self-discovery that isn't guaranteed to be optimal or perfect, but sufficient for the immediate goals.
