biomap:/ˈbaɪoʊˌmæp/
noun
生物地图
A visual representation of the distribution of living organisms, especially across different geographic areas.
Researchers created a biomap of the Amazon rainforest to track biodiversity. 研究人员创建了一张亚马逊雨林的生物地图来追踪生物多样性。
近义词:biogeographical map, ecological map
反义词: N/A
词性:n.


biomap:/ˈbaɪoʊˌmæp/
verb
绘制生物地图
To create a biomap of a specific area or organism.
Scientists are working to biomap the ocean floor. 科学家们正在努力绘制海底的生物地图。
近义词:map, chart, plot
反义词: N/A
词性:v.


Etymology:  "bio-" (Greek bios "life") + "map" (from Latin mappa "napkin, cloth; public notice, list").
