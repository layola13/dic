nontobacco:/ˌnɒn təˈbækəʊ/ (UK) /ˌnɑːn təˈbækoʊ/ (US)| adj.|非烟草的|Relating to or denoting products that do not contain tobacco.  Formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the word "tobacco".|例句：The store sells a variety of nontobacco nicotine products.  这家商店出售各种非烟草尼古丁产品。

|例句：Nontobacco chewing gum can help people quit smoking. 非烟草口香糖可以帮助人们戒烟。|近义词：tobacco-free|反义词：tobacco, nicotine-containing|词性：adjective
