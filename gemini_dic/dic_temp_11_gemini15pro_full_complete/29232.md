cassandras:/kəˈsændrəz/
|n.|卡桑德拉们|Plural form of Cassandra, referring to multiple people who make predictions of doom or disaster, especially those who are disbelieved.|The cassandras of the financial world predicted the market crash, but no one listened.|近义词：doomsayers, prophets of doom|反义词:optimists|noun
