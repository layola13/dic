macrobiotics:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɒtɪks/
noun
长寿饮食法; 宏观营养学
A way of eating based on the principles of balancing yin and yang, thought to promote health and longevity. It emphasizes whole grains, vegetables, and locally grown foods, while avoiding processed foods, sugar, and most animal products.
She adopted a macrobiotic diet in hopes of improving her health. 她为了改善健康采用了长寿饮食法。
Synonyms: whole foods diet, plant-based diet, healthy diet
Antonyms: junk food diet, processed food diet
n.

macrobiotic:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɒtɪk/
adjective
长寿饮食法的; 宏观营养学的
Relating to macrobiotics or its principles.
He follows a macrobiotic lifestyle. 他遵循长寿饮食法的生活方式。
Synonyms: holistic, natural, healthful
Antonyms: unhealthy, processed, artificial
adj.
