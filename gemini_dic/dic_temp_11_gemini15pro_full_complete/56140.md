documentarily:/ˌdɑːkjəˈmentərəli/ | adv. | 以纪录片的方式；有文件证明地 | in a way that relates to, or is presented in, a documentary | The event was documentarily recorded for posterity.  这个事件被记录下来以供后代参考。 |  The history of the town was documentarily preserved through photographs and letters.  该镇的历史通过照片和信件以纪录片的形式保存了下来。 | 近义词: factually, officially | 反义词: fictionally, anecdotally | 词性: 副词

词源: From "documentary" + "-ly". "Documentary" comes from the Late Latin "documentarius," meaning "pertaining to instruction or proof,"  from "documentum"  meaning "lesson, proof, example, warning."
