hyperopia:/ˌhaɪpərˈoʊpiə/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈrəʊpiə/ (UK)| n. |远视|A vision condition in which distant objects are seen clearly but close ones do not come into proper focus.|She was diagnosed with hyperopia and prescribed glasses.|近义词:farsightedness, longsightedness|反义词:myopia, nearsightedness|noun
