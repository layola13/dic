botanically:/bəˈtænɪkli/| adv.|植物学地，从植物学的角度|Relating to the study of plants; from a botanical perspective. |例句：The rose is botanically classified as a shrub.  玫瑰在植物学上被归类为灌木。|例句：Botanically speaking, a tomato is a fruit, not a vegetable. 从植物学角度来说，西红柿是一种水果，而不是蔬菜。|近义词：plant-based, phytologically|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "non-botanically" informally)|词性:副词
