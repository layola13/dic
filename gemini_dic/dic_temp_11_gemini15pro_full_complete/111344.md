liveliness:/ˈlaɪvlɪnɪs/| n.| 活泼，生动，活泼性|The liveliness of the music got everyone dancing.  这音乐的活泼性让每个人都跳起舞来。|English Explanation: The quality of being lively; animation; briskness.|例句：The performance lacked liveliness. 表演缺乏活力。|例句：The city is known for its liveliness and energy. 这座城市以其活力和能量而闻名。|近义词：animation, vibrancy, energy, spirit, vigor|反义词：dullness, apathy, lifelessness|词性:noun

词源: From lively + -ness.  Lively comes from Old English *līflic* "living, vital," from *līf* "life."
