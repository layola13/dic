overimitated:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪtɪd/
|adj.|过度模仿的|Copied or mimicked to an excessive degree, often including unnecessary or irrelevant actions.|The child overimitated the adult, copying even the meaningless gestures.|近义词：mimicked, aped, copied excessively|反义词：independent, original|词性:形容词


overimitate:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪt/
|v.|过度模仿|To copy or mimic someone's actions to an excessive degree, often including unnecessary or irrelevant actions.|Children often overimitate adults, even when the actions don't make sense.|近义词：mimic, ape, copy excessively|反义词：innovate, create|词性:动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "imitate" (from Latin imitatus, past participle of imitari "to copy, portray, imitate," from image "likeness").
