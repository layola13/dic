boast-of:/boʊst ɒv/| phrasal verb |吹嘘，夸耀|To speak with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or qualities.|He boasted of his golfing skills. 他吹嘘他的高尔夫球技。|Synonyms: brag about, crow about, vaunt|Antonyms: be modest about, downplay, understate|phrasal verb
boast-of:/boʊst ɒv/| phrasal verb |拥有，以...自豪|To possess something that is a source of pride.|The town boasts of a beautiful beach.  这个小镇拥有一片美丽的沙滩。|Synonyms: pride oneself on, possess, have|Antonyms: lack, be without|phrasal verb
