loathing:/ˈloʊðɪŋ/| n. |厌恶，憎恨|A feeling of intense dislike or disgust; extreme aversion.|She felt a deep loathing for her abusive husband. 她对她虐待她的丈夫深恶痛绝。|Synonyms: abhorrence, detestation, hatred, aversion, disgust|Antonyms: love, adoration, liking, fondness|noun

loathe:/loʊð/| vt. |厌恶，憎恨|Feel intense dislike or disgust for.|I loathe spiders. 我讨厌蜘蛛。|Synonyms: detest, abhor, despise, hate|Antonyms: love, adore, like|verb
