monastical: /məˈnæstɪkəl/ | adj. | 修道院的；僧侣的；禁欲的 | Of or relating to monasteries or monks; characteristic of the life of a monk, especially in its austerity and seclusion. |  He led a quiet, almost monastical life dedicated to his studies. 他过着安静的、几乎像僧侣一样的生活，专注于他的学业。

The monastical atmosphere of the library encouraged quiet contemplation. 图书馆里修道院般的气氛鼓励人们安静地思考。| 近义词: monastic, ascetic, cloistered, contemplative | 反义词: worldly, secular, hedonistic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *monasticalis, from monasticus (see monastic).
