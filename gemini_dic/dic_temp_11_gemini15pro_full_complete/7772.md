anseres:/ˈænsəriːz/
noun
雁形目；雁形目鸟类
The Anseres are an order of birds that includes ducks, geese, and swans.
例句：The Anseres are known for their webbed feet. 雁形目鸟类以其蹼足而闻名。

例句：Many species of Anseres are migratory. 许多雁形目鸟类是迁徙的。
近义词: waterfowl, Anatidae
反义词: None (Anseres is a taxonomic classification, not a descriptive adjective with a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
