interlaid:/ˌɪntərˈleɪd/
verb (past tense and past participle of interlay)
中文翻译：交错放置；镶嵌；插入
英文解析：placed between or among other things.  Specifically, it refers to the act of inserting something between layers of something else.
例句：Sheets of parchment were interlaid between the pages of the book to protect the illuminations. 书页之间夹有羊皮纸以保护彩绘。

例句：Thin layers of gold were interlaid with the wood to create a decorative effect.  金箔薄片镶嵌在木头中，营造出装饰效果。

近义词：interspersed, inserted, layered, sandwiched
反义词：removed, extracted
词性：v.
