amethodist: /əˈmɛθədɪst/ | n. adj. |卫理公会教徒，卫理公会的| A member of the Methodist Church, a Protestant denomination known for its emphasis on social justice and personal holiness. |  例句：My grandmother was a devout Methodist. 我的祖母是一位虔诚的卫理公会教徒。

例句：The Methodist church down the street holds services every Sunday. 街尾的卫理公会教堂每周日都举行礼拜。| 近义词：Protestant, Christian | 反义词: None (It's a specific denomination, not a concept with a direct opposite.) | 词性: noun, adjective


词源: From method + -ist. Originally a derogatory term applied to a group of 18th-century Oxford students, including John and Charles Wesley, because of their methodical approach to religious study and practice.
