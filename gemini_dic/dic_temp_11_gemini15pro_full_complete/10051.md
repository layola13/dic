aplysia:/əˈplɪziə/ /əˈplɪʒə/| n. |海兔|A genus of large marine gastropods belonging to the order Anaspidea, known for their simple nervous systems and use in neuroscience research.  They are also called sea hares.|例句：Aplysia californica is a species of sea hare commonly used in neurological studies.  加州海兔是一种常用于神经学研究的海兔品种。

例句：The aplysia's gill-withdrawal reflex is a key subject of study in learning and memory. 海兔的缩鳃反射是学习和记忆研究中的一个关键课题。|近义词：sea hare|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
