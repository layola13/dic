magne:/ˈmæɡni/| n. |磁石，磁铁|A piece of iron or other material that has its component atoms so ordered that the material exhibits properties of magnetism, such as attracting other iron-containing objects or aligning itself in an external magnetic field.|The magne attracted the iron filings. 磁铁吸引了铁屑。|Synonyms: lodestone, magnet|Antonyms: non-magnet|noun
