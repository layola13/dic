nollie:/ˈnɒli/ (UK) /ˈnɑːli/ (US)| n.|诺利，豚跳 (滑板技巧)|A skateboarding trick where the rider uses their front foot to lift the nose of the board off the ground and then uses their back foot to push the board forward.|He landed a clean nollie down the five stairs. 他干净利落地用诺利滑下了五级台阶.|近义词: (No direct synonym, but related tricks include noseflip, heelflip)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
