bluejay:/ˈbluːdʒeɪ/| n.|蓝松鸦|A common North American bird with bright blue plumage, known for its noisy calls.|例句：A bluejay perched on the branch, squawking loudly. 一只蓝松鸦栖息在树枝上，大声鸣叫。

例句：The bluejay flitted from tree to tree, searching for acorns. 蓝松鸦在树林间飞来飞去，寻找橡子。|近义词：jaybird|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他鸟类，例如 sparrow 麻雀，但意义不大)|词性:noun
