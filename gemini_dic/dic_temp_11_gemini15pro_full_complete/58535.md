duffle:/ˈdʌf(ə)l/| n.|行李袋；粗呢大衣|A large cylindrical bag made of canvas or other strong fabric, typically closed by a drawstring and carried over the shoulder.|He packed his clothes in a duffle bag for the weekend trip. 他把周末旅行的衣服装在一个行李袋里。|近义词：bag, holdall, kitbag|反义词:|noun

duffel:/ˈdʌf(ə)l/| n.|粗呢；粗绒布|A coarse woolen cloth with a thick nap.|The coat was made of heavy duffel. 这件外套是用厚粗呢做的。|近义词：cloth, fabric, material|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Duffel, a town in Belgium where the thick cloth was originally made.
