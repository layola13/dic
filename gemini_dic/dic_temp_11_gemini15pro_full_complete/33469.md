chylaqueous:/kaɪlˈeɪkwiəs/
adjective
乳糜状的；乳糜液的
Relating to or resembling chyle, a milky fluid containing emulsified fats and other products of digestion, formed in the small intestine during digestion and conveyed by the lacteals to the lymphatic circulation; mixed with watery fluid.  A mixture of chyle and another watery fluid.
The chylaceous fluid leaked from the lymphatic vessels. 乳糜状的液体从淋巴管中渗漏出来。
Near synonyms: chylous, milky, lacteal
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider using terms like "clear," "serous," "non-milky" depending on the context)
word type: adjective
