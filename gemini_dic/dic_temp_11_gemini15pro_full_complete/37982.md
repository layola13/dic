complicatedness:/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪdnəs/ /ˈkɑːmplɪkeɪtɪdnəs/| n. |复杂性，错综复杂|The complicatedness of the situation made it difficult to find a solution.  这种情况的复杂性使得很难找到解决方案。|近义词：complexity, intricacy, elaborateness|反义词：simplicity, easiness|词性:名词

Etymology: From complicate + -ed + -ness.  "Complicate" comes from the Latin "complicare," meaning "to fold together."  "-ed" and "-ness" are English suffixes indicating past participle and the state or quality of being, respectively.
