coexecutor:/koʊɪɡˈzɛkjətər/| n. |共同执行人，共同遗嘱执行人|A person appointed jointly with another or others to carry out the terms of a will.|John and Mary were named coexecutors of their father's will.  约翰和玛丽被任命为他们父亲遗嘱的共同执行人。|Synonym: joint executor|Antonym: N/A|noun
