considerable:/kənˈsɪdərəbl/ /kənˈsɪdrəbəl/| adj.|相当大的；重要的；值得考虑的|Notable or large in size, amount, or extent; worthy of attention or respect.|例句：She inherited a considerable fortune. 她继承了一大笔财产。|近义词：significant, substantial, large, ample, noteworthy|反义词：insignificant, negligible, small, minor|词性:形容词
Etymology: From late Middle English: considerabel, from Old French: considerable, from Late Latin: considerabilis, from Latin: considerare (to consider).
