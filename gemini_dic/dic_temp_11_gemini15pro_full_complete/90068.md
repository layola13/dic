hostal:/ˈhɒstəl/ (British), /ˈhɑːstəl/ (American)| n.|旅馆，招待所 (尤指西班牙语国家廉价的旅馆)|A hostal is a type of inexpensive lodging, especially one found in Spanish-speaking countries.  It's similar to a hostel or a small hotel.|We stayed in a charming hostal in Barcelona. 我们在巴塞罗那一个迷人的旅馆住下了。|Synonym: hostel, inn, lodging, guesthouse|Antonym: hotel (often implies greater luxury)|noun
