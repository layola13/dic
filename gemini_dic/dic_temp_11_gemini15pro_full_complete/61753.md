empassionate:/ɪmˈpæʃənət/| adj.|充满激情的，热烈的|To fill with passion; to inspire with intense feeling or strong emotion.|Her impassioned plea for justice moved the jury. 她充满激情的正义诉求打动了陪审团。|近义词：passionate, ardent, fervent, zealous|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, impassive|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from en- + passion + -ate; related to passion
