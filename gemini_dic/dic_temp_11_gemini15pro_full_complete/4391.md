alamin:/ˈæləmɪn/ | n. | 世界；宇宙；一切 |  A poetic or archaic term for "the world" or "the universe," often implying everything that exists.  Derived from Arabic العالمين (al-ʿālamīn), the dual form of عالم (ʿālam), meaning "world."
