dander:/ˈdændər/| n. |皮屑；怒火；兴奋 |Small scales from animal skin or hair, or feathers. Also means anger or temper. |例句：The cat's dander caused her allergies to flare up. 猫的皮屑使她的过敏症发作。

例句：He got his dander up when they insulted his family. 当他们侮辱他的家人时，他勃然大怒。|近义词：anger, ire, temper, fur, scales |反义词:calm, peacefulness |词性:noun

dander:/ˈdændər/| v. |闲逛，漫步 |To saunter; stroll.  |例句：He dandered down the street, enjoying the sunshine. 他在街上闲逛，享受着阳光。

例句：She dandered through the park, lost in thought.  她在公园里漫步，陷入了沉思。 |近义词：stroll, saunter, wander, amble |反义词:rush, hurry |词性:verb
