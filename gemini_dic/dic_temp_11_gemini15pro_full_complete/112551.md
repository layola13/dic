loveless:/ˈlʌvlɪs/| adj.|无爱的，缺乏爱情的，冷漠的|Lacking love or affection; cold and unfeeling.|She grew up in a loveless home. 她在一个缺乏爱的家庭中长大。|The loveless marriage finally ended in divorce. 这段没有爱情的婚姻最终以离婚告终。|近义词：unloving, cold, unaffectionate, heartless, uncaring|反义词：loving, affectionate, caring, warmhearted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From love + -less (suffix meaning "without").
