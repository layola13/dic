microtrabecular:/ˌmaɪkroʊtræˈbɛkjələr/| adj.|微梁的|Relating to or consisting of microtrabeculae, which are fine, interconnected strands within the cytoplasm of a cell.  These strands form a complex network that helps maintain the cell's shape and internal organization.|The microtrabecular structure of the cell plays a vital role in intracellular transport. 细胞的微梁结构在细胞内运输中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms:  intracellular, cytoplasmic |Antonyms: extracellular|adjective
