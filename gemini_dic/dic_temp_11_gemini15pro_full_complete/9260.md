antipatriotic:/ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪk/ /ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
不爱国的；反爱国的
Opposed to one's country; unpatriotic.  Showing a lack of love or loyalty to one's country.

His antipatriotic speeches led to his arrest. 他的反爱国言论导致他被捕。

Burning the national flag is considered an antipatriotic act.  焚烧国旗被认为是不爱国的行为。

近义词: unpatriotic, treasonous, seditious, disloyal
反义词: patriotic, loyal, nationalistic

词性: adjective
