felling:/ˈfelɪŋ/| n.|砍伐；砍倒；砍伐量；（尤指被砍倒的）树木；感觉，直觉|The act of cutting down a tree; an intuitive feeling.|例句：The felling of the old oak tree saddened the community.  老橡树被砍倒使社区居民感到难过。

例句：He had a felling that something bad was about to happen. 他有一种不祥的预感。|近义词：logging, cutting, intuition, hunch|反义词:planting, growth|词性:名词

felling:/ˈfelɪŋ/| adj.|压倒性的；强烈的|Overpowering; intense.|例句：A felling sense of dread came over him.  一种强烈的恐惧感笼罩着他。

例句：The heat was felling.  酷热难耐。|近义词：overwhelming, intense, powerful|反义词:weak, mild|词性:形容词
