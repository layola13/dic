interchain:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪn/| adj.|链间的，跨链的|Occurring or existing between chains, especially between blockchains.|Example: Interchain communication protocols enable different blockchains to interact and exchange value. 跨链通信协议使不同的区块链能够交互和交换价值。

Example:  Interchain transactions are becoming increasingly common in the decentralized finance space. 跨链交易在去中心化金融领域变得越来越普遍。|近义词：cross-chain|反义词：intrachain|词性: 形容词
