apportionate: /əˈpɔːrʃənət/ | adj.  v. | 分配的，按比例分配的；分摊 |  Divided or distributed proportionally; to divide and assign according to a plan or principle. | The funds were apportioned among the various departments.  这些资金被分配给了各个部门。|  The judge apportioned blame to both drivers involved in the accident. 法官将事故责任分摊给两名涉事司机。 | 近义词: allocate, distribute, allot, assign | 反义词: withhold, retain | 词性: adjective, verb | 词源: Late Middle English: from Old French *apportioner, from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + portion ‘share’.
