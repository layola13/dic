experimentalize:/ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəlaɪz/ | v. | 实验; 进行实验 | To conduct experiments; to try out new methods or ideas. |  Scientists are experimentalizing with new drugs to treat the disease. 科学家们正在试验新的药物来治疗这种疾病。

The company is experimentalizing with different marketing strategies. 这家公司正在尝试不同的营销策略。 | 近义词: experiment, test, trial | 反义词: theorize |词性: 动词
