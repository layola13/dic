conventionalizing:/kənˈvɛnʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使常规化；使习俗化；使惯例化
The process of making something conform to convention or established practice.
The artist was accused of conventionalizing his style to appeal to a wider audience.  艺术家被指责为了迎合更广泛的观众而使他的风格变得常规化。
They are conventionalizing a radical new approach to education. 他们正在将一种激进的新的教育方法常规化。
conventionalize,  standardize, formalize
individualize, personalize, revolutionize
v.
