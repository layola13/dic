bouser:/ˈbaʊzər/| n.|酒鬼，酗酒者|A person who drinks a lot of alcohol, especially habitually.|He was a known bouser and often seen staggering home from the pub.|近义词：drunkard, alcoholic, sot, tippler|反义词:teetotaler|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier bouze, booze + -er.  Ultimately from Middle Dutch būsen (“to drink heavily”).
