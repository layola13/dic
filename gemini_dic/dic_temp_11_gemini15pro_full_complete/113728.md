macrodactylous: /ˌmækroʊˈdæktɪləs/ | adj. | 巨趾的，指（趾）异常大的 | Having abnormally large fingers or toes. |  Example: The macrodactylous child needed special shoes to accommodate his oversized feet.  巨趾儿童需要特殊的鞋子来适应他超大的脚。

Example: Macrodactylous hands can make everyday tasks difficult. 巨趾的手会使日常工作变得困难。


|近义词: mega(lo)dactylous |反义词: brachydactylous (短指趾的) |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From macro- (large) + -dactylous (fingered or toed), from Greek *makros* (large, long) + *daktylos* (finger, toe).
