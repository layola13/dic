hausa: /ˈhaʊsə/ | n. adj.| 豪萨语; 豪萨人; 豪萨族的 |A Chadic language spoken mainly in northern Nigeria and adjacent regions. Also refers to the people or culture of this region. |  Example: She is fluent in Hausa. 她精通豪萨语。

Example:  The Hausa people are known for their rich cultural heritage. 豪萨人以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。| 近义词: Hausa language| 反义词: (无明确反义词) | 词性: 名词, 形容词
