business-to-consumer: /ˈbɪznəs tə kənˈsuːmər/ | adj. n. | 企业对消费者；B2C |  Relating to or involving the selling of goods or services directly to consumers.  It describes a commercial transaction or business model where a company sells products or services directly to individual customers, who are the end-users of those products or services. | Amazon is a prime example of a successful business-to-consumer company.  亚马逊是一家成功的企业对消费者公司的典型例子。

Business-to-consumer marketing often focuses on emotional appeals. 企业对消费者营销通常侧重于情感诉求。| 近义词: direct-to-consumer, B2C | 反义词: business-to-business (B2B) | 词性: adjective, noun
