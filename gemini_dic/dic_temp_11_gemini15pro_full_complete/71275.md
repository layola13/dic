fixatif:/ˈfɪksətɪf/| n.|固定剂，定影剂|A substance used to fix something, especially a color or smell, and prevent it from changing or fading. |She sprayed the drawing with fixatif to prevent smudging. 她给画喷了定影剂以防弄脏。|Synonyms: fixative, preservative, sealant|Antonyms: solvent, thinner|Noun

fixatif:/ˈfɪksətɪf/| n.|固定液|A liquid used to preserve specimens for microscopic examination. |The biologist used a fixatif to prepare the tissue sample. 生物学家用固定液制备组织样本。|Synonyms: preservative, stabilizing agent|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
