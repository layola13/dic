awriest: /ˈɔːriɪst/ | adj. | 最歪斜的，最扭曲的 | Most awry; most askew; most crooked. |  The painting hung awriest on the wall, making it look deliberately misplaced.  这幅画歪歪扭扭地挂在墙上，看起来像是故意放错地方的。

The awriest smile I'd ever seen spread across his face. 我见过的最扭曲的笑容在他脸上蔓延开来。 | 近义词: crooked, askew, wry, distorted | 反义词: straight, aligned, symmetrical | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From *awry* (adjective and adverb) + *-est* (superlative suffix).  *Awry* comes from the Old Norse *á* (on) + *vreiðr* (twisted, astray).
