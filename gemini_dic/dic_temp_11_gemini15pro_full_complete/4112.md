ain't /eɪnt/ | contraction | am not, is not, are not, has not, have not | 不是，没有 | Ain't that the truth! 那不就是事实吗！ | isn't, aren't, hasn't, haven't | is, are, has, have | contraction
词性:缩略词

词源: ain't 是 am not, is not, are not, has not 和 have not 的缩略形式。它起源于18世纪的英语，最初是 amn't 的一种非正式说法，后来逐渐演变成 ain't。

用法说明: ain't 在正式场合被认为是不标准的英语，但在非正式的口语和一些方言中仍然很常见。


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):

I ain't going to the party. = 我不去参加派对了。
He ain't happy about the situation. = 他对这种情况不高兴。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):

I ain't working today. = 我今天不工作。
They ain't playing games right now. = 他们现在没有玩游戏。



现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

I ain't seen him in years. = 我好几年没见过他了。
She ain't finished her homework yet. = 她还没完成作业。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

I ain't been feeling well lately. = 我最近感觉不太好。
They ain't been studying very hard. = 他们最近学习不是很努力。



一般过去时 (Simple Past):

I ain't go to school yesterday. = 我昨天没去上学。(非标准用法，应为 I didn't go to school yesterday.)
He ain't see the movie. = 他没看那部电影。(非标准用法，应为 He didn't see the movie.)


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

I ain't watching TV when you called. = 你打电话的时候我没有在看电视。(非标准用法，应为 I wasn't watching TV when you called.)
They ain't playing outside at that time. = 他们当时没有在外面玩。(非标准用法，应为 They weren't playing outside at that time.)



过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

I ain't seen her before the party. = 在派对之前我没见过她。(非标准用法，应为 I hadn't seen her before the party.)
He ain't finished his work before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前没有完成工作。(非标准用法，应为 He hadn't finished his work before the deadline.)



过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

I ain't been working there for very long. = 我在那里工作时间不长。(非标准用法，应为 I hadn't been working there for very long.)
They ain't been studying for the exam very hard. = 他们没有很努力地准备考试。(非标准用法，应为 They hadn't been studying for the exam very hard.)



一般将来时 (Simple Future):
(ain't 通常不用于将来时, 以下例句仅为展示如果用ain't，语法上会如何)
I ain't gonna go to school tomorrow. = 我明天不会去上学。(非常不正式)
He ain't gonna see her next week. = 他下周不会见她。(非常不正式)


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
(ain't 通常不用于将来时)
I ain't gonna be working tomorrow.  = 我明天不会工作。（非常不正式）
They ain't gonna be playing tomorrow. = 他们明天不会玩。（非常不正式）

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
(ain't 通常不用于将来时)
I ain't gonna have finished by tomorrow. = 我明天之前不会完成。（非常不正式）
He ain't gonna have seen her by then. = 到那时他不会见到她。（非常不正式）

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(ain't 通常不用于将来时)
I ain't gonna have been working for long. = 我不会工作太久。（非常不正式）
They ain't gonna have been studying very hard. = 他们不会一直努力学习。（非常不正式）

PHRASE->

Ain't that the truth? = 那不就是事实吗？
Ain't I right? = 我说的对吗？
It ain't over till it's over. =  事情没结束之前不算结束.
You ain't seen nothing yet. = 你还没见识到真正的厉害。
If it ain't broke, don't fix it. = 没坏就别修。


注意事项:  避免在正式场合使用 ain't。
