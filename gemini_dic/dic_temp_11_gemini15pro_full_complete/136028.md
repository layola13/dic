nonrealizations:/ˌnɒnˌriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 未实现，未实现的事物 | The nonrealizations of his youthful dreams haunted him.  他年轻时的梦想未能实现，这一直困扰着他。|  The project's nonrealizations were due to lack of funding.  该项目未能实现是由于缺乏资金。 | 近义词: failures, lack of achievements | 反义词: achievements, successes | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, from ne (“not”) + oinom (“one”).  Meaning "not."
realization (noun): from realize (verb) + -ation (suffix). Realize comes from Late Latin realis, from Latin res (“thing”). Meaning "the act of making something real or actual."
