bibulous:/ˈbɪbjələs/
adj.
嗜酒的;吸水的;
Given to or marked by the consumption of alcoholic beverages; absorbent.
He led a bibulous life, frequenting bars and pubs. 他过着嗜酒的生活，经常出入酒吧。
The bibulous paper towels quickly soaked up the spilled water. 吸水的纸巾很快吸干了洒出来的水。
近义词: alcoholic, absorbent, porous, spongy
反义词: abstemious, sober, teetotal, impermeable
词性: 形容词


词源:
From Latin bibulus ("drinking, absorbent"), from bibō ("I drink").
