circumvection:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvekʃən/ | n. |环流，循环|The process of heat transfer through the movement of fluids, where hotter, less dense material rises and cooler, denser material sinks, resulting in a circular motion. |Ocean currents are an example of circumvection. 洋流是环流的一个例子。|Synonyms: circulation, convection, gyration |Antonyms: stagnation, stillness |noun

circumvection:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvekʃən/ | n. |对流|The transfer of heat by the circulation or movement of the heated parts of a liquid or gas. |Circumvection plays a crucial role in the Earth's climate. 对流在地球气候中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: convection, circulation |Antonyms: conduction, radiation |noun
