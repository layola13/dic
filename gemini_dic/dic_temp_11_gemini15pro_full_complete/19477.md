biddably:/ˈbɪdəbli/| adv.|顺从地，温顺地|In a manner that is obedient or compliant; readily submitting to the will of others.|The child biddably followed his mother's instructions.  孩子顺从地听从了母亲的指示。|Synonyms: obediently, compliantly, submissively, meekly, docilely|Antonyms: rebelliously, defiantly, recalcitrantly|Word Type: Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "biddable" + "-ly."  "Biddable" comes from the verb "bid" (meaning to command or request) + "-able" (suffix meaning capable of being).  "Bid" traces back to Old English "bēodan."
