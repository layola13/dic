ennoblers:/ɪˈnoʊblərz/
n.|提升者；使高贵的人或物|Something that ennobles elevates, dignifies, or gives greater status or worth.  It can be a quality, an experience, or an achievement. |Volunteering at the homeless shelter was an ennobling experience.|Synonyms: dignifiers, elevators, exalter|Antonyms: degraders, debasers|noun
