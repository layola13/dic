infantilism:/ˈɪnfəntɪlɪzəm/| n. |幼稚化，幼稚症|A psychological condition in which an adult exhibits behavior characteristic of a much younger person, particularly a child.  It can manifest in emotional immaturity, dependence, and a preference for childish activities.|His constant whining and temper tantrums were clear signs of infantilism.|近义词：immaturity, childishness, puerility|反义词:maturity, adulthood|noun
