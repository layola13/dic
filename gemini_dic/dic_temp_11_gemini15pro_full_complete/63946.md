episcopalianism:/ɪˌpɪskəˈpeɪliənɪzəm/
n.
主教制;圣公会教义
The system of church government by bishops, or adherence to this system.
例句：Episcopalianism is the dominant religion in England.  圣公会是英格兰的主要宗教。

例句：She converted to Episcopalianism after years of being a Methodist.  在做了多年的卫理公会教徒之后，她皈依了圣公会。

近义词：Anglicanism
反义词：(No direct opposite, but could consider Presbyterianism, Catholicism, etc. depending on the context)
词性: noun
