nonluminousness:/ˌnɒnluːmɪˈnɒsnəs/
n.
无光泽;不发光
lack of luminosity; the inability to emit light.
The nonluminousness of the moon makes it dependent on the sun for visibility. 月球本身不发光，所以它需要依靠太阳才能被看到。
The thick fog increased the nonluminousness of the night. 浓雾加剧了夜晚的黑暗。
近义词:dullness, opacity
反义词:luminosity, brightness
词性:名词
