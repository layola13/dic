bowden:/ˈboʊdən/| n.|鲍登线，鲍登缆线|A type of flexible cable consisting of an inner wire surrounded by a coiled outer sheath, used for transmitting motion, especially in control systems.|The Bowden cable connects the gear lever to the transmission. 这条鲍登线连接着变速杆和变速器。|近义词：cable, wire, line|反义词:|n.
