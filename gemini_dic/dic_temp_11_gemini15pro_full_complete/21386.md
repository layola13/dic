blotter:/ˈblɒtər/ (UK) /ˈblɑːtər/ (US)| n.|吸墨纸，吸墨器；记事簿，流水账|A piece of thick blotting paper used to absorb excess ink. Also, a book or pad for brief, informal notes or records. |She used a blotter to dry the ink on the letter. 她用吸墨纸吸干信上的墨水。|The police officer recorded the incident in his blotter. 警察把这件事记录在他的记事簿上。|synonyms: absorbent paper, blotting pad, notepad, journal|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something that *repels* liquid like a "waterproof sheet")|noun

blotters:/ˈblɒtərz/ (UK) /ˈblɑːtərz/ (US)| n.|吸墨纸（复数），吸墨器（复数）；记事簿（复数），流水账（复数）|Plural form of "blotter."
