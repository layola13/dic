howzit:/haʊzɪt/| interj.|你好；怎么样|A casual greeting, similar to "how's it going?" or "how are you?". It's commonly used in South Africa, particularly among Afrikaans speakers.|Howzit, my china?  你好吗，我的朋友？|近义词：Hi, hello, how's it going, what's up|反义词: (No direct antonym for a greeting)|interjection
