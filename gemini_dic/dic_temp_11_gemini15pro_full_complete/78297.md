germinators:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtərz/
n.
发芽器；催芽器；使发芽的东西
A device or substance that promotes germination.  A germinator is a controlled environment used to sprout seeds for growing plants.
The farmer used a special germinator to sprout the seeds before planting.  农民在播种前使用特殊的催芽器来催芽种子。

The warm, moist environment of the germinator encouraged the seeds to sprout.  催芽器温暖潮湿的环境促使种子发芽。
Synonym:  incubator, propagator
Antonym:  desiccant, inhibitor
词性: 名词
