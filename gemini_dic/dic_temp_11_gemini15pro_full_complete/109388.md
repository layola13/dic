lenticular:/lɛnˈtɪkjʊlər/ |adj.|透镜状的；双凸透镜状的；豆状的|Resembling a lens in shape; biconvex; lens-shaped.  Relating to or shaped like a lens.  Also, relating to a type of image that appears to be three-dimensional when viewed from a particular angle.|The lenticular clouds hovered over the mountains.  这些透镜状的云朵盘旋在山脉上空。|The lenticular screen on my phone gives the images a 3D effect. 我手机上的透镜状屏幕使图像具有3D效果。|lens-shaped, biconvex, lentiform| |adjective
