fusion:/ˈfjuːʒən/| n.|融合，熔化，聚变|The process or result of joining two or more things together to form a single entity.|The fusion of different musical styles created a unique sound. 不同音乐风格的融合创造了一种独特的声音。|近义词：amalgamation, blend, merger, synthesis, union|反义词：separation, division, fission|noun

fusion:/ˈfjuːʒən/| n.|熔化，熔合|The process of becoming liquid by heat.|The fusion of the metal created a bright orange glow.  金属的熔化产生了一种明亮的橙色光芒。|近义词：melting, liquefaction|反义词：solidification, freezing|noun
