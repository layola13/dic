consume:/kənˈsuːm/ (US), /kənˈsjuːm/ (UK)| v. |消耗，消费，吃，喝，毁灭|To use up, eat, or destroy something completely.|The fire consumed the entire building. (大火吞噬了整座建筑物).|synonyms: devour, expend, use up, absorb, ingest |antonyms: produce, create, save, conserve |verb


consume:/kənˈsuːm/ (US), /kənˈsjuːm/ (UK)| adj. |被（感情）吞噬的，充满...的 (rare, literary)|Overwhelmed or engrossed by a powerful feeling or emotion|He was consumed by guilt. (他被内疚吞噬了).|synonyms: obsessed, engrossed, preoccupied |antonyms: indifferent, apathetic |adjective
