misogynistic: /mɪˌsɒdʒɪˈnɪstɪk/ (British) /mɪˌsɑːdʒɪˈnɪstɪk/ (American)| adj. |厌女的；仇视女性的|Relating to or characteristic of a misogynist.|The lyrics of the song were criticized for their misogynistic undertones.|近义词：sexist, chauvinist|反义词: feminist, philogynist|词性: adjective
