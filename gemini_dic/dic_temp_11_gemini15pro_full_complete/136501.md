nonrotating:/ˌnɒnˈrəʊteɪtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈroʊteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非旋转的，不旋转的
Not rotating; not turning on or around an axis.
例句：
A nonrotating space station would not experience artificial gravity.
一个不旋转的空间站将不会体验到人造重力。

The nonrotating shaft provided stability to the structure.
不旋转的轴为结构提供了稳定性。

近义词：stationary, fixed, immobile
反义词：rotating, revolving, spinning
词性：adjective
