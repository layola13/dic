emendicate:/ˈɛmɛndɪkeɪt/| v. |乞讨；行乞|To beg for alms; to obtain by begging. |He was forced to emendicate on the streets to survive. 他被迫在街上乞讨才能生存。|She had to emendicate for food after losing her job. 她失业后不得不乞讨食物。|近义词：beg, panhandle, solicit, implore|反义词：give, donate, provide|词性：动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēmendicātus, past participle of ēmendīcāre (“to beg, get by begging”), from Latin mendīcus (“beggar”).
