nonenvious:/ˌnɒnˈɛnviəs/| adj.|没有嫉妒心的，不羡慕的|Not feeling or showing envy; free from envy. |She felt a nonenvious admiration for her friend's success. 她对朋友的成功感到毫无嫉妒的钦佩。

She was genuinely nonenvious of her sister's new car. 她真心不嫉妒姐姐的新车。

|近义词：unenvious, free from envy|反义词:envious, jealous, covetous|词性:形容词
