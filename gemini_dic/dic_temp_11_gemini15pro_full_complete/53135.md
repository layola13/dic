noun
外交; 外交手腕; 交际手段; 策略
the art and practice of conducting negotiations between nations
例句：International diplomacies helped to avert the war. 国际外交帮助避免了战争。

例句：He used all his diplomacies to persuade them to agree. 他用尽一切外交手段说服他们同意。

近义词：negotiations, tact, skill, statesmanship
反义词：aggression, hostility, conflict, antagonism
词性:n.


diplomacy: /dɪˈploʊməsi/
noun
外交; 外交手腕; 交际手段; 策略
the art and practice of conducting negotiations between nations
例句：Diplomacy is often the best way to resolve international disputes.  外交通常是解决国际争端的最佳途径。

例句：She showed great diplomacy in handling the sensitive situation. 她处理敏感局势时表现出了高超的外交手腕。


近义词：tact, finesse, negotiation, statesmanship
反义词：aggression, hostility, conflict, antagonism
词性:n.

词源: From Old French *diplomatie, from Medieval Latin diploma "official document," originally "folded paper," from Greek diploma "folded letter, state paper," from diploun "to fold double."
