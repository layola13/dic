insurrectionaries:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃəˌnɛriz/
n.
叛乱者，起义者
People who take part in an insurrection; rebels.
The insurrectionaries stormed the government building. 叛乱分子袭击了政府大楼。
The insurrectionaries were quickly captured. 叛乱分子很快就被抓获了。
synonyms: rebels, revolutionaries, mutineers, insurgents
antonyms: loyalists, conformists
noun
