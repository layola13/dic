deport:/dɪˈpɔːrt/ /diːˈpɔːrt/
|vt. |驱逐出境；放逐；举止；持身|to force (a person who is not a citizen) to leave a country; to behave in a particular way|The government deported the illegal immigrants. 政府驱逐了非法移民。|Synonyms: expel, banish, exile|Antonyms: admit, receive|verb
deporter:/dɪˈpɔːrtər/ /diːˈpɔːrtər/
|n. |驱逐者，放逐者|a person who deports someone|The government officials acted as deporters. 政府官员充当了驱逐者的角色。|Synonyms: expeller, banisher|Antonyms: welcomer, receiver|noun
