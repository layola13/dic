blather:/ˈblæðər/ (US), /ˈblæðə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|喋喋不休，胡说八道|To talk foolishly and at length; to utter nonsense.  As a noun, it refers to foolish, voluble talk.|He blathered on for hours about nothing important. 他连续几个小时胡说八道些无关紧要的事情。|近义词：babble, chatter, prate, ramble|反义词: be quiet, be silent|词性: verb, noun
Etymology:  From Old Norse *blaðra "to talk nonsense," probably of imitative origin.
