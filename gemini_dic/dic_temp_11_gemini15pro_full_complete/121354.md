misgrade:/ˌmɪsˈɡreɪd/
verb
错误地评分;评错等级
to give a student the wrong grade on a piece of work
The teacher misgraded my exam by ten points. 老师把我的考试成绩少给了十分。
近义词:undergrade, wrongly grade
反义词:grade correctly
词性:v.

misgrade:/ˌmɪsˈɡreɪd/
noun
错误的评分
a grade that is wrongly given
There was a misgrade on my report card. 我的成绩单上有一个错误的分数。
近义词:grading error, incorrect grade
反义词:correct grade
词性:n.
