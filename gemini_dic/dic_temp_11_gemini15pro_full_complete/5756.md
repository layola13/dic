amadou:/ˈæməduː/
n.
火绒，引火物
A spongy, flammable substance prepared from certain fungi, used as tinder.
He used amadou to start the campfire. 他用火绒生起了篝火。
近义词:tinder, kindling
反义词:extinguisher
