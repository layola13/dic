metoposcopist:/ˌmɛtəˈpɒskəpɪst/ | n. | 面相学家，面相术士 | A person who practices metoposcopy (the study of facial features, especially the lines on the forehead, as indicators of character or destiny).  |  The metoposcopist claimed to be able to read her future from the lines on her forehead.  面相学家声称可以从她额头上的纹路看出她的未来。 | 近义词: physiognomist, face reader | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun

词源:
From Greek *metopon* (forehead) + *-skopia* (observation).
