hospitably:/ˈhɑːspɪtəbli/ | adv. | 好客地，殷勤地 | In a welcoming and friendly manner, showing generosity and kindness to guests. |  They received us hospitably, offering food and drinks upon our arrival. 他们热情地接待了我们，我们一到就给我们提供了食物和饮料。|  kindly, warmly, graciously, generously | inhospitably, coldly, unwelcomingly | adverb

hospitable:/ˈhɑːspɪtəbl/ | adj. | 好客的，殷勤的 | Friendly and welcoming to guests or strangers. |  They were very hospitable and made us feel at home. 他们非常热情好客，让我们感觉像在家一样。| welcoming, friendly, generous, kind | inhospitable, unfriendly, unwelcoming | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hospitably, from Old French *hospitablement, from Latin hospitābilis (“hospitable”) + -ment (“-ly”).  The Latin word is derived from hospes (“guest, host”).
