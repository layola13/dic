oil:/ɔɪl/ | n. |油,石油,油画颜料|A viscous liquid derived from petroleum, especially for use as a fuel or lubricant. Also refers to any similar viscous liquid.  It can also refer to a painting made using oil paints.|The car needs an oil change. 这辆车需要换油。|  The artist used oil paints to create the masterpiece.  这位艺术家用油画颜料创作了这幅杰作。| synonyms: petroleum, lubricant, fuel, grease | antonyms: water, solid | noun
