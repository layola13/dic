nondeductive:/ˌnɑːndɪˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
非演绎的
Not based on deduction; not proceeding from the general to the specific.  Reasoning that constructs or evaluates general propositions that are derived from specific examples.  It is often contrasted with deductive reasoning.
Inductive reasoning is a nondeductive form of logic. 归纳推理是一种非演绎逻辑形式。
Nondeductive arguments do not offer conclusive proof. 非演绎论证不能提供确凿的证据。
Synonyms: inductive, empirical, experimental
Antonyms: deductive, a priori, theoretical
词性: 形容词
