misallot:/ˌmɪsəˈlɒt/ /ˌmɪsəˈlɑːt/
verb
错误分配，分配不当
to allot wrongly or improperly; to make a wrong or improper allotment of.
The funds were misallotted due to a clerical error. 由于文书错误，资金分配不当。
The company misallotted its resources and ended up with a shortage of funds.  这家公司错误分配了资源，最终导致资金短缺。
misallocate, misappropriate
allocate correctly, distribute properly
词性: 动词
