gazumper:/ɡəˈzʌmpə(r)/
|n.|抬价抢购者；(尤指)截胡者；加价争购者|Someone who offers a higher price for something (especially a house) than someone else who has already agreed to buy it.|The gazumper offered £5,000 more than the asking price.  这个抬价抢购者比要价多出了5000英镑。|Synonyms: outbidder, snipper|Antonyms: underbidder|Noun

gazump:/ɡəˈzʌmp/
|vt.|抬价抢购；截胡；加价争购|To offer a higher price for something (especially a house) than someone else who has already agreed to buy it.|They were gazumped at the last minute by a cash buyer.  他们在最后一刻被一个现金买家截胡了。|Synonyms: outbid, snip|Antonyms: underbid|Verb
