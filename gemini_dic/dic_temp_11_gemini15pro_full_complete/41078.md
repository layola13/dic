copperplate:/ˈkɒpəpleɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpərpleɪt/ (US)| n. adj.|铜版；铜版印刷；铜版字体|A polished steel plate on which designs are engraved for printing; also, a print made from such a plate.  Relating to or characteristic of copperplate engraving or printing, especially in being precise and elegant.|The invitation was printed in elegant copperplate.  邀请函是用精美的铜版印刷的。|Synonyms: engraving, etching|Antonyms: typescript, digital print|noun
copperplate:/ˈkɒpəpleɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpərpleɪt/ (US)| adj.|（书法）清晰优美的；（尤指）铜版体|Having a smooth, flowing, and elegant style, resembling the writing or printing produced by copperplate engraving.|Her copperplate handwriting was much admired. 她的优美书法备受赞赏。|Synonyms: elegant, flowing, calligraphic|Antonyms: messy, illegible, scrawled|adjective
