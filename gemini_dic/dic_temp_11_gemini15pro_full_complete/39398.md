conjuress:/ˈkʌndʒərɪs/ | n. | 女魔术师；女巫 | A woman who practices magic; a sorceress. |  She was accused of being a conjuress and using dark magic. 她被指控为女巫，使用黑魔法。 | The conjuress brewed a potion said to grant eternal youth.  据说这位女巫调制了一种能让人长生不老的药水。 | 近义词: sorceress, witch, enchantress | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源:
From late Middle English *conjuresse*, from Anglo-Norman *conjuresse*, feminine of *conjureur* (“conjurer”), equivalent to *conjure* +‎ *-ess*. Cognate with French *conjureuse*.
