fuellers:/ˈfjuːələrz/ | n. |加油员；燃料添加者|People who add fuel to something, especially vehicles or aircraft.  Those responsible for supplying fuel. |The fuellers quickly refilled the plane's tanks. 加油员迅速加满了飞机的油箱。| The fuellers worked tirelessly through the night to ensure the trucks were ready for the morning deliveries.  加油员们整夜不知疲倦地工作，以确保卡车准备好早上的送货。|Synonyms:  refuelers, gas attendants, fuel handlers|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
