jacksaws:/ˈdʒækˌsɔːz/| n.| 手摇锯，弓锯| A saw with a narrow blade stretched in a frame, used for cutting metal or curves in wood.| He used a jacksaw to cut the metal pipe. 他用弓锯切割金属管。|例句：The carpenter used a jacksaw to cut intricate curves in the wood. 木匠用弓锯在木头上切割复杂的曲线。

例句：I needed a jacksaw to cut through the thick metal bars. 我需要一把弓锯来锯断粗金属棒。


近义词：coping saw, fretsaw, keyhole saw|反义词: (No direct antonym for a tool. Could consider power saw as a contrasting tool.) |词性:noun
