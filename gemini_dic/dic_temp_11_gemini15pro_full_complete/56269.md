dogfighters:/ˈdɒɡˌfaɪtəz/ (US) /ˈdɒɡˌfaɪtəz/ (UK)| n. |斗狗者；驾驶战斗机进行空战的飞行员|People who engage in dogfighting (making dogs fight each other for sport) or pilots who fly fighter aircraft in aerial combat.|例句：The police are cracking down on dogfighters in the area. 警方正在该地区打击斗狗者。

例句：The dogfighters were arrested and their dogs were confiscated. 斗狗者被逮捕，他们的狗被没收了。

|近义词：pilot (for aerial combat),  animal abuser (for dogfighting)|反义词: animal rescuer, pacifist|词性:noun
