microfossil:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌfɑːsəl/| n. |微体化石|A fossil that can only be studied using a microscope, typically smaller than one millimeter.|Microfossils, such as foraminifera, can reveal information about past climates.|Synonyms: nannofossil|Antonyms: macrofossil|noun
