discretely:/dɪˈskriːtli/ | adv. | 谨慎地；小心地；不引人注目地 | In a separate and distinct manner; individually, not as a group.  Also, in a way that avoids attracting attention. | She discretely slipped him a note. 她悄悄地塞给他一张纸条。 | synonym: subtly, privately, secretly, quietly | antonym: openly, publicly, blatantly, obviously | adverb

discretely:/dɪˈskriːtli/ | adv. | 谨慎地；小心地；不引人注目地 | Done in a way that is not obvious or attracting attention. | He coughed discretely to get her attention. 他谨慎地咳嗽了一声以引起她的注意。| synonym: cautiously, tactfully, unobtrusively | antonym: conspicuously, ostentatiously | adverb

词源:
From Latin discretus, past participle of discernere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + cernere "to sift, separate, distinguish."  The adverb form, discretely, follows this etymology, implying an action performed in a separated, distinct, and thus often unnoticed manner.
