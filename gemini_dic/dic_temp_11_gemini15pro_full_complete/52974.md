dinichthys:/daɪˈnɪkθɪs/
n.
恐鱼属（一种已灭绝的巨型鱼类）
A genus of extinct gigantic arthrodire placoderm fish that lived during the Late Devonian period.
Dinichthys was a fearsome predator of the Devonian seas. 恐鱼是泥盆纪海洋中可怕的掠食者。
The Dinichthys terrelli could grow up to 10 meters long.  邓氏恐鱼可以长到10米长。
近义词:Dunkleosteus (closest relative), Titanichthys (another large placoderm)
