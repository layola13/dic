outstanding:/ˌaʊtˈstændɪŋ/| adj.|杰出的，优秀的；显著的；未完成的，未解决的|Exceptionally good;  prominent;  remaining to be paid, done, or dealt with|Her performance was outstanding. 她的表演非常出色。|The outstanding balance on your account is $100.  你的账户未付余额是100美元。|近义词：excellent, remarkable, exceptional, prominent, unpaid, unresolved|反义词：mediocre, ordinary, insignificant, paid, resolved|词性:形容词

词源:
From out + stand + -ing.  Originally literally "standing out (from the crowd)," hence "prominent, conspicuous."  The financial sense "unpaid" arose from the image of a debt "standing out" as something still to be dealt with.
