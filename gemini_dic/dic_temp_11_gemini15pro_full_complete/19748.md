billeted:/ˈbɪlɪtɪd/
verb|安置；提供住宿；把…分配到宿舍|The soldiers were billeted in local homes. 士兵们被安置在当地居民家中。|
past participle|已安置的；已分配宿舍的|The billeted troops quickly made themselves at home. 被分配宿舍的部队很快就安顿下来了。|
adjective|被分配宿舍的|The billeted officers enjoyed their comfortable quarters. 被分配宿舍的军官们享受着舒适的住处。|

Etymology: From the French "billet," meaning "a note, ticket, or small piece of paper," later evolving to mean a lodging assignment order.  Ultimately from Latin "bulla," meaning "a bubble, boss, or stud," later used for a seal affixed to a document.
