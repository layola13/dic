apartments:/əˈpɑːrtmənts/
n.
公寓，住宅
a set of rooms for living in, especially on one floor of a building
例句：
The apartments overlook the park. 这些公寓俯瞰公园。
She lives in a luxury apartments in the city center. 她住在市中心的一套豪华公寓里。

近义词：flats, units, residences, lodgings
反义词：house (独立式住宅)
词性：名词(plural)
