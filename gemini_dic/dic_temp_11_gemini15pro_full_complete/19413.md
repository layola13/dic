bichloride:/baɪˈklɔːraɪd/ | n. | 二氯化物；升汞（氯化汞） | A compound containing two atoms of chlorine combined with another element or radical.  Specifically, mercuric chloride (HgCl₂), a highly poisonous crystalline compound. |  Bichloride of mercury is a potent antiseptic.  升汞是一种强效的防腐剂。| Synonyms: mercuric chloride, corrosive sublimate | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound. Could consider "antidote" in a medical context, but not a true antonym.) | noun
