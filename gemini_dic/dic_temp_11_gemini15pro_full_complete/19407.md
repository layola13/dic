biceps: /ˈbaɪsɛps/ | n. | 二头肌 |  The large muscle at the front of the upper arm.  It is technically called the biceps brachii.  The plural form "biceps" is more commonly used than the singular "bicep". | She injured her biceps lifting weights.  她举重时伤了二头肌。| synonyms: muscle, flexor | antonyms: tricep | noun
