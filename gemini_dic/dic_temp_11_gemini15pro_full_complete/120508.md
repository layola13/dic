minuted:/ˈmɪnɪtɪd/
|v.|记录在案的；把…写进会议记录|
|Past participle of minute (verb).  Meaning: recorded in the minutes of a meeting.|
|The agreement was duly minuted.  该协议已正式记录在案。|
|recorded, documented, logged, noted|
|omitted, ignored|
|verb|


minuted:/ˈmɪnɪtɪd/
|adj.|详细的；仔细的|
|Characterized by close attention to detail.|
|He gave a minuted account of the events. 他对事件进行了详细的描述。|
|detailed, meticulous, thorough, precise|
|cursory, superficial, brief|
|adjective|
