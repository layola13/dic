carboniferous:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnɪfərəs/ | adj. n. |  石炭纪的；含煤的；石炭纪 | Of, relating to, or denoting the fifth period of the Paleozoic era, between the Devonian and Permian periods, about 359 to 299 million years ago.  Also refers to the system of rocks formed during this period, rich in coal deposits. | The carboniferous period is known for its vast coal swamps. 石炭纪以其广阔的煤炭沼泽而闻名。 | The area has carboniferous limestone deposits. 这个地区有石炭纪的石灰石矿床。 | synonyms: coal-bearing, Paleozoic | antonyms:  modern, recent | adjective, noun
