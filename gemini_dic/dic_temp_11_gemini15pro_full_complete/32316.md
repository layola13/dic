chihuahua: /tʃɪˈwɑːwɑː/ (US), /tʃɪˈwɑːwə/ (UK)| n. |吉娃娃|A very small breed of dog with large, erect ears and a short coat, originating in Mexico.  It is named after the Mexican state of Chihuahua.|例句：My chihuahua, Pepe, barks at everything. 我的吉娃娃佩佩见到什么都叫。

例句：Chihuahuas are known for their big personalities despite their small size. 吉娃娃以其小巧的体型和强大的气场而闻名。|近义词：dog, canine, pup, toy dog|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific breed)|词性:noun
