improvvisatore:/ɪmprɒvɪzətɔːreɪ/ /ɪmˈprɑːvɪzətɔːreɪ/
n.
即兴表演者，即兴创作的艺术家
A person who improvises, especially a musical performer or a poet.
The improvvisatore captivated the audience with his spontaneous verses.  这位即兴诗人用他自发的诗句吸引了观众。
The young improvvisatore skillfully wove a melody on the piano.  这位年轻的即兴演奏家熟练地在钢琴上编织了一段旋律。
近义词：improviser, extemporizer
反义词: planner
