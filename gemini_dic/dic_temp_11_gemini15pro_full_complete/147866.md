overrationalizes:/ˌoʊvərˈræʃənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度理性化；过分合理化
To ascribe excessively rational motives to one's own or another's actions, even when such motives seem unlikely or are contradicted by evidence.  The person is usually trying to conceal or justify their true motivations, often subconsciously.
He overrationalizes his failures, blaming everything but his own lack of effort. 他过度合理化自己的失败，把一切都归咎于除了他自己缺乏努力之外的原因。
Synonyms: justify, excuse, rationalize, explain away
Antonyms:  accept, admit, acknowledge, confess
词性: 动词
