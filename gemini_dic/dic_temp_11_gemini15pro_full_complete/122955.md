momot:/ˈmoʊmɒt/| n. |桃子，桃儿；|A momot is a type of bird found in Central and South America, known for its brightly colored plumage and long tail feathers.  Momot can also occasionally be used as an archaic or dialectal word for "peach".|The resplendent quetzal and the momot are both stunning examples of tropical birdlife.  (凤尾绿咬鹃和桃子鸟都是热带鸟类的绝佳例子。)  / The old farmer still uses the word "momot" when referring to peaches. (老农夫在提到桃子时仍然用“momot”这个词。)|近义词：(for the bird) motmot, (for the archaic fruit meaning) peach|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
