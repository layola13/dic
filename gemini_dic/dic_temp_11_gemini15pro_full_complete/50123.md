dentitions:/dɛnˈtɪʃənz/
n.
牙列；齿系
The arrangement or condition of the teeth in a particular species or individual.
Humans have two dentitions – deciduous (baby) teeth and permanent teeth.  人类有两副牙列——乳牙和恒牙。
teeth, set of teeth
toothlessness
noun
