overrunningly:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌnɪŋli/
adv.
泛滥地，过多地，蔓延地
Excessively; in an overflowing manner; to an overwhelming degree.
The weeds were overrunningly present in the garden, choking the flowers.  杂草在花园里过度蔓延，扼杀了花朵。
His speech was overrunningly long and tedious, losing the audience's attention. 他的演讲冗长乏味，失去了听众的注意力。
近义词: excessively, overwhelmingly, abundantly, profusely
反义词: sparsely, moderately, scarcely, lightly
词性: 副词


词源:  From "overrun" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Overrun" comes from "over-" (meaning "above, beyond") + "run" (meaning "to move swiftly").  The "-ing" suffix creates a present participle, and the "-ly" suffix transforms it into an adverb.
