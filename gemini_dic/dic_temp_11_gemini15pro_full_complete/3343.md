affray:/əˈfreɪ/
noun
公开斗殴，骚乱
a noisy, public fight or disturbance
Two men were arrested after an affray outside a bar.  两名男子在酒吧外斗殴后被捕。

近义词: brawl, fight, fray, melee, rumpus, disturbance
反义词: peace, calm, tranquility
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esfreer "to frighten," from Vulgar Latin *exfridare, from Latin frigidus "cold," figuratively "to make someone's blood run cold (with fear)." The sense shifted from "frighten" to "fight, brawl" in Anglo-French.
