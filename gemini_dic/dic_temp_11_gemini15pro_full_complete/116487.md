materializer:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzər/| n. | 实现者；具体化者；实体化者 |Someone or something that materializes something, that is, causes something to appear in physical form or to become actual fact. | The inventor claimed to be a materializer of dreams, turning abstract ideas into real-world inventions.  这位发明家声称自己是梦想的实现者，将抽象的想法转化为现实世界的发明。|Synonyms:  realizer, actualizer, concretizer | Antonyms: destroyer, dissolver, abolisher | noun

materializer:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzər/| n. | 物化器 |A device (often fictional) that can cause objects to appear seemingly out of nowhere. | In the science fiction movie, the alien used a materializer to create weapons. 在这部科幻电影中，外星人使用物化器来制造武器。 | Synonyms:  fabricator, replicator, generator | Antonyms: dematerializer, disintegrator | noun
