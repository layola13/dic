nonserials:/nɒnˈsɪəriəlz/
noun
非连续出版物; 非期刊出版物
Publications that are not issued in a regular series, such as monographs, reports, and proceedings.
The library has a vast collection of nonserials, including government documents and rare books.  图书馆收藏了大量的非连续出版物，包括政府文件和珍本。
The cataloging of nonserials is different from that of periodicals. 非连续出版物的编目与期刊不同。
synonyms:monographs, reports, proceedings, pamphlets, books
antonyms:serials, periodicals, journals, magazines
n.
