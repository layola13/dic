overconfidently: /ˌoʊvərˈkɒnfɪdəntli/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈkɒnfɪdəntli/ (UK) | adv. | 过于自信地；过分自信地 | In a manner that shows excessive confidence in oneself or one's abilities. | He overconfidently predicted his victory in the election. 他过于自信地预测自己会在选举中获胜。 | Example: She overconfidently strode onto the stage, only to forget her lines. 她过于自信地走上舞台，结果却忘了台词。| Synonyms: arrogantly, presumptuously, cockily | Antonyms: humbly, modestly, diffidently | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + confident + -ly.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much". "Confident" comes from the Latin "confidere" meaning "to trust entirely".  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
