homeless:/ˈhoʊmlɪs/| adj.|无家可归的，无住所的|Without a home; lacking permanent housing.|Many homeless people sleep on the streets. 许多无家可归的人露宿街头。|近义词：destitute, roofless|反义词：housed, sheltered|词性:形容词
homeless:/ˈhəʊmlɪs/| n.|无家可归的人|People who are homeless.|The homeless often face discrimination. 无家可归的人经常面临歧视。|近义词：vagrant, derelict|反义词：resident, householder|词性:名词

词源: Old English *hāmleas, from hām "home" + -leas "without."
