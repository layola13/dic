impanels:/ɪmˈpænəlz/ | v. |组成陪审团；选定（陪审团）| to enroll a list of jurors; to select or enlist (a jury). | The judge impaneled a jury of twelve citizens. 法官组成一个由十二名公民组成的陪审团。|  The court clerk impaneled the jury for the murder trial.  法院书记员为谋杀案审判选定了陪审团。| synonyms: enroll, enlist, swear in, constitute, select, choose | antonyms: discharge, dismiss | verb
