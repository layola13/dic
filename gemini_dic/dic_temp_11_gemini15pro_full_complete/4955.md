alizari:/əˈlɪzərɪn/ /əˈlɪzərɪn/| n. |茜素; 茜红|A red crystalline compound, C14H8O4, formerly obtained from the root of the madder plant and now produced synthetically. It is used as a dye, especially for dyeing wool and cotton.|例句：Alizarin is a popular dye for producing rich red hues. 茜素是一种流行的染料，用于生产丰富的红色调。

例句：The artist used alizarin crimson to paint the vibrant sunset.  这位艺术家用茜素深红来描绘充满活力的日落。|近义词：madder, rose madder|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific color) |词性:noun
