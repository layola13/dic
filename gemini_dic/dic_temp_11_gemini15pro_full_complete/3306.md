afflictions:/əˈflɪkʃənz/
|n.|痛苦，折磨，苦难|Something that causes pain or suffering.|The country has been beset by natural afflictions such as floods and earthquakes. 这个国家一直受到洪水和地震等自然灾害的困扰。|
afflictions:/əˈflɪkʃənz/
|n.|灾难，不幸|A state of pain, distress, or grief.|He bore his afflictions with patience and fortitude. 他以耐心和毅力忍受着他的痛苦。|
afflictions:/əˈflɪkʃənz/
|n.|（疾病造成的）痛苦|Pain or suffering caused by illness.|Her afflictions included arthritis and migraines. 她的病痛包括关节炎和偏头痛。|
近义词: troubles, hardships, miseries, woes, distresses, trials, tribulations, burdens, problems, difficulties
反义词: comforts, blessings, joys, pleasures, advantages, benefits
词性: 名词 (plural)


词源:
From Middle English *affliccioun, from Old French affliction, from Latin afflictiō (“a striking down, weakening, damaging”), from afflīgō (“strike down, damage”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + flīgō (“strike, dash”).
