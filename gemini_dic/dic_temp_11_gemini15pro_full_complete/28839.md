carpellary:/kɑːrˈpɛləri/
adj.
心皮的; 雌蕊群的
Relating to, or consisting of, carpels.  A carpel is the female reproductive organ of a flower, typically consisting of a stigma, style, and ovary.
The flower has five carpellary segments.  这朵花有五个心皮部分。
carpellary leaves form the pistil. 心皮叶构成雌蕊。
近义词: pistillate, gynoecial
反义词: staminate
词性: adjective
