doc:/dɒk/ (UK) /dɑːk/ (US)| n.|文件，文档；医生（非正式）|A doc is a document, especially one stored on a computer.  Informally, doc can also refer to a doctor.|例句：Please send me the doc by email.  请通过电子邮件将文档发送给我。

例句：I need to see a doc about this cough. 我需要去看医生看看这个咳嗽。|近义词：document, file, physician (formal)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


doc:/dɑːk/ (US)| v.|修改，篡改（非正式）|To doc something means to alter or tamper with it, often in a way that is dishonest.|例句：He docced the photo to make himself look thinner. 他修改了照片，让自己看起来更瘦。

例句：Someone docced the figures in the report. 有人篡改了报告中的数据。|近义词：alter, tamper with, falsify|反义词: N/A|词性:verb


词源:
Abbreviation of *document* and *doctor*.  The use for "doctor" dates back to the late 19th century. The use for "document" is more recent, gaining popularity with the rise of computers and electronic files.
