octosyllabic: /ˌɒktəʊsɪˈlæbɪk/ /ˌɑːktəsɪˈlæbɪk/
adjective
八音节的
Having or consisting of eight syllables.
The poet employed an octosyllabic meter in his latest work.  这位诗人在他的最新作品中使用了八音节的韵律。
octosyllabic verse is a common meter in English poetry. 八音节诗是英语诗歌中常见的韵律。
Near Synonyms: eight-syllable
Antonyms: monosyllabic, disyllabic
