fillet:/ˈfɪlɪt/ (US), /ˈfɪleɪ/ (UK)| n. v. |鱼片，肉片；（去除骨头的）里脊肉；条状物 |A boneless piece of meat or fish, especially the side of a fish or a strip of lean meat cut from the loin or breast of an animal.
