argument: /ˈɑːrɡjumənt/ (US) /ˈɑːɡjʊmənt/ (UK)| n.| 争论，辩论；论据，论点|A reason or set of reasons given with the aim of persuading others that an action or idea is right or wrong. |例句：They had a heated argument about politics. 他们就政治问题进行了激烈的争论。

例句：She presented a compelling argument for her proposal. 她为她的提议提出了令人信服的论据。

近义词：dispute, debate, contention, quarrel, disagreement|反义词: agreement, harmony, concord|词性:noun
