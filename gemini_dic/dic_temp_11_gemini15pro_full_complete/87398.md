herring-like:/ˈherɪŋˌlaɪk/
adjective
像鲱鱼的；鲱鱼似的
Resembling a herring in appearance or characteristics.
The small, silvery fish were herring-like in shape. 这些小银鱼的形状像鲱鱼。
The fish had a herring-like smell. 这条鱼有一股鲱鱼的味道。
similar, resembling, akin
dissimilar, unlike
adjective
