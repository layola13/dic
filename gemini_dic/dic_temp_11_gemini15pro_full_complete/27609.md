cancellations:/ˌkænsəˈleɪʃənz/
n.
取消，撤销
The airline announced numerous cancellations due to the storm.  航空公司因暴风雨宣布取消多架航班。
The cancellations caused a lot of inconvenience for travelers. 这些取消给旅客带来了很多不便。
Synonyms: abolishments, revocations, withdrawals
Antonyms: confirmations, approvals, bookings
noun

cancel:/ˈkænsəl/
v.
取消，撤销
I had to cancel my trip because of the pandemic. 因为疫情，我不得不取消我的旅行。
The event was cancelled due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，活动被取消了。
Synonyms: abort, call off, scrap
Antonyms: confirm, approve, proceed
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *cancellen*, from Middle French canceller, from Medieval Latin cancellare "to draw lines across a writing," from Latin cancelli "lattice," from cancer "crab, lattice." The original meaning referred to crossing out writing with intersecting lines like a lattice.
