intercessory:/ˌɪntərˈsesəri/| adj.|代祷的，说情的，调解的|Of or relating to intercession; characterized by or given to interceding.  Acting as an intermediary between two parties, typically to resolve a dispute or offer a prayer on behalf of another. |例句：Her intercessory prayers brought comfort to the family. 她的代祷为这个家庭带来了安慰。

例句：He played an intercessory role in the negotiations. 他在谈判中扮演了调解人的角色。|近义词：mediating, intervening, pleading, praying|反义词: accusing, condemning|词性: adjective
