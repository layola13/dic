concessional:/kənˈseʃənəl/| adj.|优惠的，让步的|Granted or offered as a concession; involving or given at a reduced rate or price.  Relating to or involving a concession. |The government is offering concessional loans to small businesses. 政府正在向小型企业提供优惠贷款。|Synonyms: preferential, reduced, discounted, subsidized, favorable|Antonyms: standard, regular, full-price|Adjective

concessional:/kənˈseʃənəl/| n.|优惠，让步（罕用作名词）|A thing that is granted as a right or privilege. | The concessionals offered by the company were very attractive. 公司提供的优惠条件非常吸引人。|Synonyms: allowance, privilege, grant|Antonyms: restriction, prohibition|Noun (rare usage)
