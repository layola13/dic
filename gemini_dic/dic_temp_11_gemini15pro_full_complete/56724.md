doorcheek:/ˈdɔːrtʃiːk/
n.
门框侧柱，门垛
The vertical part of a doorframe.
He leaned against the doorcheek, watching the street. 他靠在门框上，看着街道。
近义词：doorjamb, doorpost
反义词: (无明显的反义词)
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From "door" + "cheek,"  referring to the side pieces of the doorframe as being like cheeks flanking the opening.
