ground-rents:/ˈɡraʊnd rɛnts/
n.
地租
Regular payments made by a leaseholder to a freeholder for the right to occupy a piece of land.
The ground-rents on the properties in this area are quite high.  这个地区的地租相当高。
synonyms: ground rent, land rent
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider concepts like "property tax exemption" or "ownership")
