mithrim:/ˈmɪθrɪm/| n. |迷斯林山脉（Mithrim Mountains）|  A fictional mountain range in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium, located in the north of Beleriand in the First Age. They are described as having deep, snow-clad gorges and being the source of several rivers.| The Elves of Gondolin lived hidden in the Echoriath, a spur of the Mithrim.  冈多林的精灵藏身于迷斯林山脉的支脉埃霍里亚斯。|Synonyms: mountains, range, peaks|Antonyms: valleys, plains|noun
