friability:/fraɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. |易碎性；脆弱性 |The quality of being easily crumbled or pulverized.  | The friability of the biscuits made them difficult to transport.  饼干的易碎性使得它们难以运输。|Synonyms: fragility, brittleness, crumbliness |Antonyms: strength, toughness, durability | noun

friable:/ˈfraɪəbl/ | adj. |易碎的；脆弱的 | Easily crumbled or pulverized. | The dry, friable soil was easy to till.  干燥易碎的土壤很容易耕作。|Synonyms: brittle, crumbly, fragile |Antonyms: strong, tough, durable | adjective
