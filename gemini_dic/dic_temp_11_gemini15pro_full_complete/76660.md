galvanical:/ɡælˈvænɪkəl/ | adj. | 電流的；電擊的；驚人的 | Of or relating to galvanism; produced by galvanism. galvanical:/ɡælˈvænɪkəl/ | adj. |  驚人的；令人震驚的 |  Figuratively, producing a sudden and dramatic effect.  | The news of his sudden death had a galvanical effect on the community. 他突然去世的消息对社区产生了惊人的影响。| synonyms: shocking, startling, electrifying | antonyms: calming, soothing | adjective
