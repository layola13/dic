humbuggable:/hʌmˈbʌɡəbl/
adjective
可被欺骗的, 易受骗的
Susceptible to being humbugged; easily deceived or tricked.
That gullible tourist was completely humbuggable and fell for every scam.  那个容易上当的游客完全可以被欺骗，并且落入了每一个骗局。
The humbuggable investor lost his life savings in a Ponzi scheme.  容易受骗的投资者在庞氏骗局中损失了毕生积蓄。
近义词:gullible, naive, credulous
反义词:discerning, skeptical, astute
词性:adjective

词源:  "Humbug" dates back to the 18th century, of uncertain origin, possibly student slang.  "Humbuggable" is a later formation, adding the suffix "-able" to create an adjective meaning "capable of being humbugged."  “Humbug”可以追溯到18世纪，起源不确定，可能是学生俚语。“Humbuggable”是后来的构成，添加后缀“-able”来创建一个形容词，意思是“能够被欺骗”。
