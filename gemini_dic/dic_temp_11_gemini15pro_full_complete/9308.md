antipleasures:/ˈæntiˌpleʒərz/
noun
不快乐，不愉快的事物
Things that do not give pleasure; displeasures.
He seemed to take antipleasure in our success. 他似乎对我们的成功感到不快。
近义词：displeasures, discomforts, annoyances
反义词：pleasures, joys, delights
词性：n.

Etymology:
From anti- + pleasure.  "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against," "opposite of," or "in opposition to."  "Pleasure" comes from Old French "plaisir," ultimately from Latin "placere" meaning "to please."
