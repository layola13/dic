nucleated:/ˈnjuːkliːeɪtɪd/
adjective
有核的；成核的
Having a nucleus or nuclei; formed into a nucleus.
Red blood cells are not nucleated. 红细胞没有细胞核。
nucleated settlement  集镇
The crystals began to nucleate. 晶体开始成核。
synonyms: centered, nuclear, centralized
antonyms: anucleate, non-nucleated
adjective
