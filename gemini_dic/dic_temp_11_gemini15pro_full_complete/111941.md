lollingly:/ˈlɒlɪŋli/ | adv. | 懒洋洋地，悠闲地 | In a relaxed, lazy manner, typically with the tongue hanging out. | The dog lay lollingly in the sun.  狗懒洋洋地躺在阳光下。|  lazily, idly, indolently | energetically, busily | adverb

Etymology:
From "loll" (meaning to hang loosely or droop) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Loll" likely originates from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Old Norse "lolla" (to dangle).
