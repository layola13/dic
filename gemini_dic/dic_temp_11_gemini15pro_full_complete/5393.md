almadia:/ælˈmeɪdiə/ /ælˈmɑːdiə/
n.
a canoe-like boat
A small raft or canoe, typically made of logs or reeds, used for transportation on rivers or lakes, especially in South America and the Middle East.
例句：The almadia glided silently down the river.  阿尔马迪亚小船静静地顺流而下。

例句：Fishermen used almadias to navigate the shallow waters. 渔民们使用阿尔马迪亚小船在浅水中航行。
近义词：canoe, raft, skiff
