antibiotics:/ˌæntibaɪˈɒtɪks/ /ˌæntibaɪˈɑːtɪks/| n.|抗生素|A medicine (such as penicillin or its derivatives) that inhibits the growth of or destroys microorganisms.|The doctor prescribed antibiotics for her infection. 医生给她开了抗生素治疗感染。|Synonyms: antimicrobials, antibacterial|Antonyms: None|Noun

antibiotic:/ˌæntibaɪˈɒtɪk/ /ˌæntibaɪˈɑːtɪk/| adj.|抗生的；抗菌的|Relating to or produced by antibiotics; tending to inhibit the growth of or destroy microorganisms.|Antibiotic resistance is a growing problem. 抗生素耐药性是一个日益严重的问题。|Synonyms: antibacterial, antimicrobial|Antonyms: None|Adjective
