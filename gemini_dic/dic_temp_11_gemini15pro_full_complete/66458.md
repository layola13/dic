exemptions:/ɪɡˈzempʃənz/| n.|豁免，免除|The law provides tax exemptions for charitable donations.  法律规定对慈善捐款免税。|例句：Low-income families are granted exemptions from certain taxes. 低收入家庭可免除某些税收。

例句：He received an exemption from military service due to a medical condition. 由于健康原因，他获得了免服兵役的豁免。|近义词：immunity, exception, release, exclusion|反义词：obligation, responsibility, inclusion|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *exempcion, from Latin exemptiō (“a taking out, freeing from, exemption”), from exemptus (past participle of eximere, “to take out, remove; deliver, free”), from ex (“out of, from”) + emere (“to take, buy”).
