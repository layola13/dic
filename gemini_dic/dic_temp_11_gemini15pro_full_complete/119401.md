microfarad:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌfæræd/ | n. | 微法拉 | A unit of capacitance equal to one millionth of a farad.  It's a measure of a capacitor's ability to store electrical charge. | This capacitor has a capacitance of 10 microfarads.  这个电容器的电容为10微法拉。 |  Synonyms: μF (symbol), one millionth of a farad | Antonyms: N/A | noun
