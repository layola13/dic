What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚晚餐做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
