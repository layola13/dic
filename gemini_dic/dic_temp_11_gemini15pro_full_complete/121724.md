misprofess:/ˌmɪsprəˈfɛs/
verb
假装精通；虚假地声称；谎称
falsely claim skill in or knowledge of
He misprofessed an ability to speak French. 他谎称自己会说法语。
近义词: feign, pretend, sham
反义词: admit, confess, acknowledge
词性: verb

Etymology:
From mis- + profess, from Old French professer, from Latin profiteri "to declare publicly, profess," from pro- "forth" + fateri "confess, acknowledge."
