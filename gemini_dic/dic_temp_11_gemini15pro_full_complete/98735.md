insurmountably:/ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbli/
adv.
不可克服地，无法逾越地
in a way that cannot be dealt with successfully
The task seemed insurmountably difficult. 这项任务看起来难于登天。
近义词: insuperably, impassably, unassailably
反义词: surmountably, easily, readily
词性: 副词 (Adverb)
词源: From "insurmountable" + "-ly". "Insurmountable" comes from "in-" (not) + "surmountable" (able to be overcome). "Surmountable" comes from the French "surmonter" (to overcome), ultimately from "sur-" (over) + "monter" (to mount, climb).
