noncontributiveness:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjətɪvnəs/ | n. | 无贡献；不贡献；无益 |  The state or quality of not contributing, being unhelpful, or not adding value. | His noncontributiveness to the team project resulted in a lower grade. 他对团队项目的无贡献导致成绩较低。|  The noncontributiveness of the meeting frustrated everyone.  会议的无益性让每个人都很沮丧。| 近义词: unhelpfulness, uselessness, ineffectiveness | 反义词: helpfulness, usefulness, effectiveness | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (not) + contributive (contributing) + -ness (state of being)
