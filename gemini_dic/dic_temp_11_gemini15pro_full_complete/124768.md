msat:/ˈɛmˌsæt/ | n. |毫卫星|millisecond arcsecond, a unit of angular measurement equal to one thousandth of a milliarcsecond, used in very-long-baseline interferometry (VLBI).  It is a very small unit of angle, used to measure extremely precise positions of celestial objects.|The position of the quasar was determined to within a few msat. 类星体的位置被确定在几个毫卫星之内。|Synonym: None (highly specific unit)|Antonym: None (unit of measurement)|noun
