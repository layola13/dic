freakishly:/ˈfriːkɪʃli/| adv.|怪异地；反常地；令人毛骨悚然地|In a strange or unusual way that is unpleasant or frightening.|The weather has been freakishly warm this winter. 今年冬天的天气异常暖和。|近义词：bizarrely, strangely, oddly, unusually, abnormally|反义词：normally, typically, usually|词性:副词
词源: From freak (n.) + -ish (adj. suffix) + -ly (adv. suffix).  "Freak" originally referred to a sudden capricious change of mind or whim, later evolving to mean a person or thing that is markedly unusual or abnormal.
