bombastry:/ˈbɒmbæstri/ (US) /ˈbɒmbæstri/ (UK)| n.|夸夸其谈；浮夸的言辞|High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people.|His speech was full of bombastry, but lacked substance. 他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，但缺乏实质内容。|例句：The politician's bombastry failed to impress the voters.  这位政客的夸夸其谈未能给选民留下深刻印象。

例句：The CEO's bombastry about company profits hid the fact that they were losing money. 首席执行官对公司利润的夸大掩盖了他们正在亏损的事实。|近义词：pomposity, grandiloquence, rhetoric|反义词：simplicity, modesty, understatement|词性:noun
