jaggedy:/ˈdʒæɡɪdi/ /ˈdʒæɡədi/| adj.|参差不齐的，锯齿状的，不规则的|Having a rough or irregular edge; unevenly notched or toothed.|The coastline was jaggedy and dramatic.  海岸线崎岖不平，引人注目。|The old knife had a jaggedy blade.  这把旧刀的刀刃参差不齐。|近义词：uneven, ragged, serrated, notched|反义词：smooth, even, straight|词性:形容词

词源:
From *jagged* + *-y* (suffix forming adjectives).  *Jagged* itself comes from *jag*, of uncertain origin.
