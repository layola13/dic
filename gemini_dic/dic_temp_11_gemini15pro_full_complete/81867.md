grief-stricken:/ˈɡriːfˌstrɪkən/| adj.|悲痛欲绝的，极度悲伤的|Overwhelmed by or suffering from great sorrow, especially due to a loss.|She was grief-stricken after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她悲痛欲绝。|例句：The grief-stricken family gathered at the funeral.  悲痛欲绝的家人们聚集在葬礼上。

例句：He was grief-stricken by the news of his friend's sudden death.  听到朋友突然去世的消息，他悲痛欲绝。

|近义词：bereaved, heartbroken, devastated, inconsolable|反义词：joyful, happy, elated|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From grief + stricken (past participle of strike).  "Stricken" in this context means deeply affected or afflicted.
