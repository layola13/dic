ergotized:/ˈɜːrɡəˌtaɪzd/ | adj. |麦角中毒的，感染麦角菌的|Affected by ergot fungus.  This term describes a condition in plants, especially rye, and in humans or animals who consume ergot-contaminated grains.|The ergotized rye was dangerous to consume. 这些感染了麦角菌的黑麦食用起来很危险。|Synonyms: poisoned, infected, contaminated|Antonyms: healthy, uncontaminated|Adjective


ergotized:/ˈɜːrɡəˌtaɪzd/ | v. |使…感染麦角菌（过去分词）|Past participle of "ergotize," meaning to affect with ergot.  It indicates a state of being poisoned or infected by the ergot fungus.|The grain had been ergotized by the wet weather. 粮食因潮湿的天气而感染了麦角菌。|Synonyms: poisoned, infected, contaminated|Antonyms: cured, cleansed|Verb (past participle)
