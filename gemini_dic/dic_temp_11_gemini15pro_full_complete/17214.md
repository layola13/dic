bawbee:/bɔːˈbiː/
n.
半便士，少量钱
A Scottish coin of low value, equal to half an English penny.
He hasn't a bawbee to his name. 他身无分文。
近义词:penny, farthing, mite
反义词:fortune
词性:名词
