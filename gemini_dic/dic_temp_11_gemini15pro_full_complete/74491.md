frankness:/ˈfræŋknəs/| n. |坦率，直率，真诚|The quality of being open, honest, and direct in speech or writing.  It implies a lack of hesitation or reserve in expressing one's true thoughts and feelings.|She spoke with surprising frankness about her personal problems. 她以惊人的坦率谈论了她的个人问题。|
|近义词：candor, honesty, openness, sincerity, forthrightness|反义词：deception, dishonesty, evasiveness, indirectness|词性: 名词
