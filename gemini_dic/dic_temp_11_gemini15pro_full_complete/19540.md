biforate:/ˈbaɪfəreɪt/| adj.| 两叉的，有兩個分叉的 | Having two forks or branches; forked. |  The biforate leaf of the plant was its most distinctive feature.  这种植物的两叉叶子是它最显著的特征。 |  forked, branched, bifurcate |  simple, unified | adjective


Etymology->
From Latin *bifuratus*, past participle of *bifurare* (“to divide into two forks”), from *bi-* (“two”) + *furatus*, past participle of *furare* (“to fork”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰer-* (“to curve, bend”).
