incumbent:/ɪnˈkʌmbənt/| n. |现任者；在职者|A person currently holding a particular office or position. |The incumbent president faces a tough challenge in the upcoming election. 现任总统在即将到来的选举中面临严峻挑战。|Synonyms: holder, occupant, officeholder |Antonyms: challenger, candidate, applicant |noun

incumbent:/ɪnˈkʌmbənt/| adj. |义不容辞的；必须履行的|Necessary for (someone) as a duty or responsibility. |It is incumbent upon all of us to protect the environment. 保护环境是我们所有人的责任。|Synonyms: obligatory, mandatory, required |Antonyms: optional, voluntary, discretionary |adjective
