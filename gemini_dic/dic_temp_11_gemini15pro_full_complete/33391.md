chuprassie: /tʃʌpˈræsi/ | n. | 侍从，门房，办公室勤杂人员 | An office attendant or messenger, especially in India.  Derived from Hindi and Urdu. Historically, a chuprassie performed a variety of tasks such as delivering messages, serving tea, and running errands. |  The chuprassie brought in the tea.  侍从把茶端了进来。

| The chuprassie opened the door for the visitors.  门房为来访者开了门。 | Synonyms: peon, orderly, attendant | Antonyms: executive, manager | Noun
