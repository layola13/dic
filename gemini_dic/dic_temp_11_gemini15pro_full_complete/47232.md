dazzlement:/ˈdæzlmənt/| n. |眩目，眼花缭乱|The sudden dazzlement of the headlights made him lose control of the car.  突然的头灯眩目使他失去了对汽车的控制。|近义词：blindness, glare, radiance|反义词: clarity, dimness|noun


dazzle:/ˈdæzl/| v. |使眩目，使眼花缭乱|The sunlight dazzled me as I stepped outside. 当我走到外面时，阳光使我眼花缭乱。|近义词：blind, amaze, impress|反义词: bore, dull|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dæzelan, frequentative of *dæzan "to stupefy," related to Old Norse dasa "to become exhausted."
