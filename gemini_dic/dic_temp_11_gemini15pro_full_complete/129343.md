noisome:/ˈnɔɪsəm/ | adj. | 恶臭的; 令人作呕的; 讨厌的; 有害的 | Having an offensive smell; disgusting; very unpleasant; harmful. | The noisome stench of the rotting garbage filled the alley. 腐烂垃圾的恶臭弥漫着小巷。| The prison was a dark and noisome place.  监狱是一个黑暗而令人作呕的地方。| synonyms: foul, fetid, noxious, offensive, disgusting, repugnant, repulsive | antonyms: fragrant, pleasant, agreeable, sweet | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *noisum, noysum*, from Anglo-Norman *nuisum, noisum* and Old French *noisant*, present participle of *nuire* (“to harm, hurt, injure”), from Latin *nocēre* (“to harm, hurt, injure”).
