knock-down-drag-out:/ˌnɒkˌdaʊnˈdræɡˌaʊt/| adj.|激烈的，殊死的，彻底的|A knock-down-drag-out fight is one that is very violent and involves a lot of physical contact, often continuing until one of the participants is completely exhausted.  It signifies a fight that is unrestrained and brutal, extending until one side is utterly defeated.|They had a real knock-down-drag-out fight over the inheritance. 他们为了遗产大打出手。|Synonyms:  violent, fierce, brutal, all-out, no-holds-barred|Antonyms:  peaceful, calm, restrained, civilized|adjective

knock-down-drag-out:/ˌnɒkˌdaʊnˈdræɡˌaʊt/| n.|激烈的争吵或打斗|A knock-down-drag-out argument or fight.|It was a real knock-down-drag-out at the board meeting. 董事会上吵得不可开交。|Synonyms: brawl, free-for-all, donnybrook|Antonyms: agreement, truce, peace|noun
