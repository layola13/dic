enjail:/ɪnˈdʒeɪl/ | vt. |监禁，关押| To put someone in jail; to imprison. | The king enjailed his enemies. 国王监禁了他的敌人。

The journalist was enjailed for publishing classified information.  这名记者因发布机密信息而入狱。 | 近义词：imprison, incarcerate, confine, lock up | 反义词：release, liberate, free |词性:动词
