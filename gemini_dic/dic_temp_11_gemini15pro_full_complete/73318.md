forecourt:/ˈfɔːrkɔːrt/ |/ˈfɔːrkɔrt/|
n.|前院，广场；加油站前的空地|An open area in front of a large building or petrol/gas station.|The hotel has a large forecourt where guests can park their cars.  酒店有一个很大的前院，客人可以把车停在那里。|
synonyms: courtyard, plaza, apron, foreyard|
antonyms: backyard, back lot|

forecourt:/ˈfɔːrkɔːrt/ |/ˈfɔːrkɔrt/|
