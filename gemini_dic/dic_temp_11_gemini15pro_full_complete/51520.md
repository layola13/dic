detritic:/dɪˈtrɪtɪk/| adj.|碎屑的，由碎屑组成的|Formed from or consisting of detritus; detrital.  |例句：The detritic limestone is composed of shell fragments. 这种碎屑灰岩由贝壳碎片组成。

例句：Detritic zircon grains can be used to date geological formations. 碎屑锆石颗粒可以用来确定地质构造的年代。|近义词：detrital, clastic|反义词：crystalline, non-clastic|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *dētrītus, from Latin dēterere “to rub or wear away.”
