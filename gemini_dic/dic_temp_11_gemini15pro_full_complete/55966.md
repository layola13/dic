dlc:/ˌdiː ɛl ˈsiː/ | n. | 可下载内容（Downloadable Content） | Additional content that can be downloaded for a video game, often including new levels, characters, or items. |  Example: The new DLC for the game adds a whole new world to explore.  这个游戏的新DLC增加了一个全新的世界可以探索。

Example:  I'm excited to download the latest DLC for my favorite racing game. 我很兴奋能下载我最喜欢的赛车游戏的最新DLC。 | 近义词: expansion pack, add-on | 反义词: base game | 词性: 名词
