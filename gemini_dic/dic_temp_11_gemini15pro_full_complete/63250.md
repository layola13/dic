enterprisingly:/ˌɛntərˈpraɪzɪŋli/
adverb
有进取心地，积极地
In an enterprising way; with initiative and resourcefulness.
She enterprisingly started her own business at the age of 20. 她20岁就积极地创办了自己的企业。
He enterprisingly sought out new clients for his struggling business.  他积极地为其苦苦挣扎的企业寻找新客户。

近义词:resourcefully, energetically, ambitiously, proactively, dynamically
反义词:lazily, passively, apathetically, indifferently, lethargically
词性:adverb

词源: From enterprise + -ing + -ly.  "Enterprise" comes from Old French "entreprise," meaning "undertaking."
