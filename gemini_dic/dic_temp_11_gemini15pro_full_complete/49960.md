denial:/dɪˈnaɪəl/ | n. | 否认，拒绝，回绝 | The act of saying that something is not true. | She issued a denial of the accusations. 她发表声明否认了这些指控。|  He was in denial about his addiction. 他拒绝承认自己上瘾。 | synonyms: rejection, refutation, contradiction | antonyms: acceptance, admission, acknowledgment | noun

denial:/dɪˈnaɪəl/ | n. | 拒绝给予，拒绝承认 | The act of refusing to give or grant something. | Their denial of his request was unexpected.  他们拒绝了他的请求，这出乎意料。| The refugees were faced with denial of their basic human rights. 难民的基本人权被剥夺了。| synonyms: refusal, withholding | antonyms: granting, provision, acceptance | noun
