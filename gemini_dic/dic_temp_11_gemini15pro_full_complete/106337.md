kohl:/koʊl/ (US), /kəʊl/ (UK)| n.|眼影粉，描眼膏|A dark powder used as eye makeup, especially in the Middle East and South Asia.|She applied kohl to her eyes to make them look bigger. 她涂了眼影粉让眼睛看起来更大.|近义词:eye makeup, eyeliner|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology: From the Arabic كُحْل (kuḥl), meaning "antimony," a metallic element used in cosmetics.
