cherry-picks:/ˈtʃɛri pɪks/ | v. | 挑选；精选 | Selectively chooses the most desirable or favorable items, often ignoring the less desirable ones. | He cherry-picked the best strawberries from the basket. 他从篮子里挑了最好的草莓。|  He cherry-picked data to support his argument, ignoring evidence to the contrary. 他精心挑选数据来支持自己的论点，而忽略了相反的证据。 | select, handpick, cull | reject, disregard | verb

cherry-picks:/ˈtʃɛri pɪks/ | n. | 精选之物 | The items that have been selectively chosen as the best or most favorable. | These examples are cherry-picks and don't represent the whole picture. 这些例子是精心挑选的，并不代表全部情况。| The museum's collection of cherry-picks from local artists is impressive.  博物馆收藏的当地艺术家的精选作品令人印象深刻。 | selections, best picks, cream of the crop | leftovers, rejects | noun
