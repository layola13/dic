hysterophyte:/ˌhɪstərəˈfaɪt/
n.
滞后植物，演替后期植物
A plant that appears in the later stages of ecological succession.
Hysterophytes are typically shade-tolerant and slow-growing, dominating mature ecosystems. 滞后植物通常耐阴且生长缓慢，在成熟的生态系统中占主导地位。
Oak trees are a common example of a hysterophyte in deciduous forests. 橡树是落叶林中滞后植物的常见例子。
近义词:late-successional species, climax species
反义词:pioneer species
词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *hysteros* (ὕστερος) "later" + *-phyte* (-φυτο) "plant".
