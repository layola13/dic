inflater:/ɪnˈfleɪtər/ | n. | 膨胀器；充气机；通货膨胀者 | A device or substance that inflates something.  Something that causes inflation. |  Example: Use the inflater to pump up the air mattress. 用充气机给气垫床充气。

Example:  The government's spending policies were seen as an inflater of the economy. 政府的支出政策被视为经济的通货膨胀因素。 | 近义词: pump, blower, inflator pump | 反义词: deflator | 词性: 名词
