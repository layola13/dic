illusion:/ɪˈluːʒn/ (US), /ɪˈljuːʒn/ (UK)| n.|幻觉，错觉|Something that deceives by producing a false or misleading impression of reality.|The magician created the illusion of a disappearing rabbit. 魔术师制造了兔子消失的幻觉。|
近义词：delusion, mirage, fantasy|
反义词：reality, truth, actuality|
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: via Old French from Latin *illusio(n-), from illudere ‘to mock’, from in- ‘against, towards’ + ludere ‘to play’.
