attractivity:/əˈtræktɪvɪti/
n.|吸引力，魅力|The attractivity of the city for tourists is undeniable. 这座城市对游客的吸引力是不可否认的。|The region's attractivity stems from its natural beauty and cultural heritage. 该地区的吸引力源于其自然风光和文化遗产。|近义词：appeal, allure, charm, magnetism|反义词：unattractiveness, repulsion|词性:名词

Etymology:
From attract + -ivity.  Attract comes from Late Latin *attractare, frequentative of Latin attrahere "to draw to oneself," from ad- "to" + trahere "to draw."
