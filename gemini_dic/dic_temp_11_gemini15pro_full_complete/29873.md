causeways:/ˈkɔːzweɪz/ (US), /ˈkɔːzweɪz/ (UK)| n. |堤道，碶道|A raised road or path, typically built across water or marshland.|We drove along the causeway to reach the island. 我们沿着堤道到达了岛屿。|The ancient causeway connected the two settlements.  这条古老的堤道连接着两个定居点。|近义词：road, path, highway, embankment, levee|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 waterway 水路)|词性:名词
