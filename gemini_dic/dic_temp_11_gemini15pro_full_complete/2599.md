/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US)| pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection|什么，多么|used to ask for information about something; used to express surprise or annoyance|

例句：
What is your name?
你叫什么名字？

What did you say?
你说了什么？

近义词：which, how|反义词: N/A|pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
