jingly:/ˈdʒɪŋɡli/| adj.|叮当响的，悦耳的|Making a light ringing sound, like small bells.|The sleigh bells made a jingly sound as the horses trotted through the snow. 马拉着雪橇穿过雪地时，雪橇上的铃铛发出叮叮当当的声音。|近义词：tinkling, chiming, ringing|反义词:silent, quiet|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *jingling,* present participle of *jingle,* from Middle English *ginglen,* of imitative origin.
