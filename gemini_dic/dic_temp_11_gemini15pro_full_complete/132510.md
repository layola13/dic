/ˌnɒndɪˈteɪld/ | adj. | 非详细的，未详细说明的 | Not detailed; lacking in detail. | The report was nondetailed and provided little useful information. 报告不够详细，几乎没有提供什么有用的信息。| The instructions were very nondetailed, leaving me confused.  说明非常不详细，让我很困惑。 | 近义词: undetailed, general, brief, sketchy, cursory | 反义词: detailed, comprehensive, thorough, specific, in-depth | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + detailed (adjective from the verb "detail," meaning "to provide details," from Old French "detaillier," from "detail," meaning "small part," from "de-" (down) + "tailler" (to cut)).
