minesweeping:/ˈmaɪnˌswiːpɪŋ/
n.
扫雷，清除地雷
The process of clearing an area of landmines.
例句：Minesweeping operations are still ongoing in the region.  该地区的扫雷行动仍在进行中。

例句：The team carried out painstaking minesweeping before the refugees could return. 在难民返回之前，该小组进行了艰苦的扫雷工作。

近义词：mine clearance, demining
反义词:mine laying

词性:名词
