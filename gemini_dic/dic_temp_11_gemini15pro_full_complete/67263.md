expurgators:/ˈekspərɡeɪtərz/
noun
净化者；删节者
A person who expurgates something, especially a book or other written material.
The expurgators removed all offensive language from the novel. 净化者从小说中删除了所有冒犯性的语言。
Synonyms: censor, editor, reviser, bowdlerizer
Antonyms:  uncensored, unedited

expurgate:/ˈekspərɡeɪt/
verb
净化；删节
To remove matter considered objectionable or unsuitable from (a book or account).
The editors expurgated the novel before its release. 编辑在小说发行前将其净化。
Synonyms: censor, bowdlerize, redact, sanitize
Antonyms: include, insert, permit
