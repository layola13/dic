ametabolism:/əˌmɛˈtæbəlɪzəm/| n.|无变态|Absence of metamorphosis, especially the direct development of an insect from egg to adult without intervening larval and pupal stages.|Silverfish exhibit ametabolism.  衣鱼表现出无变态。|The collembolan demonstrates ametabolism in its life cycle.  跳虫在其生命周期中表现出无变态。|近义词:direct development|反义词:metamorphosis|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ἀ- (a-) meaning "without" + *μεταβολή (metabolḗ)  meaning "change"  + *-ισμός (-ismós) forming abstract nouns.
