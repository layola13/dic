no-hoper:/ˌnəʊˈhəʊpə(r)/| n.|无望之人；没有希望成功的人|A person who is unlikely to succeed or improve. |He was labelled a no-hoper at school. 他在学校被贴上了无望之人的标签。|Synonym: lost cause, failure, hopeless case|Antonym: achiever, high-flyer, prospect|Noun
