animality:/ˌænɪˈmælɪti/
noun
动物性，兽性
The state of being an animal; animal nature or character.
He gave in to his primal animality. 他屈服于他原始的兽性。

His animality showed in his crude behavior. 他的粗鲁行为显露出了他的兽性。

近义词: bestiality, savagery, brutality
反义词: humanity, civility, rationality
词性: noun
