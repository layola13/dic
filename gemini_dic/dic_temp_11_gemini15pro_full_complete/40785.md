convivialities:/kənˌvɪviˈælɪtiz/
n.
欢乐；欢宴；热闹的聚会
The state or quality of being convivial; festive activity.  A convivial gathering or atmosphere.
The wedding reception was filled with laughter and convivialities.  婚礼招待会上充满了欢声笑语和热闹的气氛。
近义词: festivities, merrymaking, revelries, celebrations
反义词: solemnity, gloom, seriousness
词性: noun (plural)

conviviality:/kənˌvɪviˈælɪti/
n.
欢乐；欢宴；热闹的聚会
The quality of being friendly and lively; fondness for company; sociability.
The conviviality of the pub made it a popular gathering place. 这家酒吧的热闹气氛使它成为一个受欢迎的聚会场所。
近义词: sociability, friendliness, joviality, cheerfulness
反义词: unsociability, aloofness, coldness
词性: noun

词源:
From Late Latin *convivialis, from convivium "a living together, a feast," from com- "together" (see com-) + vivere "to live" (see quick).
