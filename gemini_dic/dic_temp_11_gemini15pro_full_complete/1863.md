acropetal: /ækˈrɒpɪtəl/ | adj. | 向顶的，向基部渐狭的 | Developing or maturing from the base toward the apex (as a flower cluster) or from the base upward (as a plant part). |  The plant exhibited acropetal flowering, with the lower blossoms opening first.  这种植物呈现向顶开花的特征，下面的花先开放。

The leaves of the fern unfurled in an acropetal manner. 这种蕨类的叶子以向顶的方式展开。 | 近义词: basifugal | 反义词: basipetal | 词性: adjective
