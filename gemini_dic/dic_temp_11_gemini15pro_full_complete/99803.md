intermezzo:/ˌɪntərˈmetsoʊ/ (US), /ˌɪntəˈmetsəʊ/ (UK)| n.|间奏曲，插曲；幕间剧|A short piece of music played between the acts of a play or opera, or a short period of time between two longer events.|The orchestra played a lively intermezzo between the first and second acts.  管弦乐队在第一幕和第二幕之间演奏了一段轻快的间奏曲。

The two weeks I spent in Italy were a delightful intermezzo between jobs. 我在意大利度过的两周是在两份工作之间一段愉快的插曲。|近义词：interlude, interval, break|反义词：main act, centerpiece|词性：名词
