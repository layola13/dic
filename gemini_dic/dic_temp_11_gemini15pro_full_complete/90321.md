housewifey:/ˈhaʊswɪfaɪ/ | adj. | 家庭主妇般的；有家庭主妇特质的 |  Resembling or characteristic of a housewife, especially in a stereotypical way, often implying a focus on domestic duties, traditional values, and a slightly dated or frumpy appearance. | She had a very housewifey demeanor, always offering tea and biscuits. 她举止非常像家庭主妇，总是端茶送饼干。| 近义词：domestic, homey, traditional | 反义词：modern, career-oriented, unconventional | 词性：形容词

housewifey:/ˈhaʊswɪfaɪ/ | n. | 家庭主妇（非正式，有时略带贬义）| A housewife (informal and sometimes slightly derogatory). |  She's a real housewifey, always baking and cleaning. 她真是个家庭主妇，总是烘烤和打扫。| 近义词：housewife, homemaker | 反义词：career woman, working woman | 词性：名词
