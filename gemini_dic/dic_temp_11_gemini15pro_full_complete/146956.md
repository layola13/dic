overinvolved: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɒlvd/ (US), /ˌəʊvərɪnˈvɒlvd/ (UK) | adj. | 过分投入的，过分参与的 | Excessively involved in something, especially in the affairs of others, to the point of interfering or being overly concerned.  Characterized by excessive emotional entanglement or preoccupation. | She was overinvolved in her son's life, constantly checking his homework and calling his teachers. 她过分介入儿子的生活，不断地检查他的作业，并打电话给他的老师。

He is overinvolved in his work, neglecting his family and his own well-being.  他过分投入工作，忽略了家人和他自己的健康。| 近义词: interfering, meddlesome, intrusive, obsessed | 反义词: detached, uninvolved, apathetic | 词性: adjective
