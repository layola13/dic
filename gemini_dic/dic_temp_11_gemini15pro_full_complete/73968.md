fortifyingly:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋli/
adverb
令人鼓舞地，增强体力地
in a way that makes you feel stronger and more confident
The crisp mountain air acted fortifyingly on her spirits. 清新的山间空气让她精神振奋。
Synonyms: encouragingly, invigoratingly, strengtheningly
Antonyms: weakeningly, dishearteningly, debilitatingly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fortify + -ing + -ly.  "Fortify" comes from Old French "fortifier,"  from Late Latin "fortificare," from Latin "fortis" (strong) + "-ficare" (to make).
