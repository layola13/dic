exotoms: /ˈɛksətɒmz/ | n. | 外显子组 |  The entire set of exons in an organism's genome.  Exons are the coding regions of genes that are transcribed into messenger RNA and translated into proteins. | The study of the human exotom revealed much about the functional parts of the genome. 人类外显子组的研究揭示了许多关于基因组功能部分的信息。| Example 2:  Researchers are comparing the exotoms of different species to understand evolutionary relationships. 研究人员正在比较不同物种的外显子组以了解进化关系。| 近义词: exome | 反义词: intron/introns | 词性: noun
