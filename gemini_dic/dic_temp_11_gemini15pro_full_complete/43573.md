creaked:/kriːkt/| v. |发出吱吱嘎嘎的声音；嘎吱作响|to make a harsh, grating sound, typically as a result of friction or strain. |The old door creaked open. 老旧的门嘎吱一声开了。|The stairs creaked under his weight. 楼梯在他的重压下吱吱作响。|近义词：groaned, squeaked, grated|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用silenced, muffled)|词性: 动词
