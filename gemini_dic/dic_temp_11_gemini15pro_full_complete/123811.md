moppet:/ˈmɒpɪt/ (UK) /ˈmɑːpɪt/ (US)|n.|小孩; 小家伙 (尤指可爱或顽皮的)|A child, especially a small, endearing or mischievous one.|The little moppet was covered in mud from playing in the garden.  那个小家伙在花园里玩耍，弄得一身泥。|Synonyms: child, kid, toddler, youngster, urchin|Antonyms: adult, grown-up|noun

moppet:/ˈmɒpɪt/ (UK) /ˈmɑːpɪt/ (US)|n.|拖把; 墩布 (旧用法)|A mop or rag for cleaning (archaic).|She used a moppet to clean the spilled milk. 她用拖把擦洒了的牛奶。|Synonyms: mop, rag, cloth|Antonyms: N/A|noun
