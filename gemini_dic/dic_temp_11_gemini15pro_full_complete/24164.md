brickenstein:/ˈbrɪkənˌstaɪn/
n.
砖块斯坦/砖块怪人
A fictional character or creation made of bricks, often referencing Frankenstein's monster.  A playful name for a large or impressive Lego creation, or something built meticulously from bricks.
My son built a huge robot out of Legos and named him Brickenstein.  我儿子用乐高积木搭建了一个巨大的机器人，并给他取名为“砖块斯坦”。
近义词:Lego creation, brick monster, brick figure
反义词:None
