maltreaters:/ˌmælˈtriːtərz/ | n. |虐待者，施虐者|People who treat others cruelly or violently. |The maltreaters were brought to justice. 施虐者被绳之以法。|  The authorities are working to identify the child's maltreaters. 当局正在努力确认虐待儿童的人。| 近义词：abusers, tormentors, oppressors | 反义词：protectors, caregivers | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From maltreat + -er.  "Maltreat" comes from the French "maltraiter," from "mal" (badly) + "traiter" (to treat).  Ultimately from Latin "male" (badly) and "tractare" (to handle, manage, treat).
