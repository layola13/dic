ospreys:/ˈɒspreɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːspreɪz/ (US)| n.|鱼鹰（复数）|A large fish-eating bird of prey with long narrow wings and a white underside, found throughout the world except in Antarctica.|We saw a pair of ospreys nesting near the lake. 我们看到一对鱼鹰在湖边筑巢。|
