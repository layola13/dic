hypertrophic:/haɪpərˈtrɒfɪk/ (US) /haɪpəˈtrɒfɪk/ (UK)| adj.|肥大的，过度生长的|Enlarged or overgrown, especially as a result of increased size of individual cells rather than an increase in the number of cells. |Example: The bodybuilder's hypertrophic muscles were the result of years of intense training. 健美运动员肥大的肌肉是多年高强度训练的结果。

Example:  Doctors discovered a hypertrophic growth on the patient's heart. 医生在病人的心脏上发现了一个肥大的增生物。|近义词：enlarged, overgrown, expanded|反义词:atrophied, shrunken, diminished|词性:形容词
