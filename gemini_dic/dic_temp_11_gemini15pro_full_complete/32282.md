chiefest:/ˈtʃiːfɪst/
adjective
最重要的；主要的
Most important or influential.
例句：He was her chiefest confidant. 他是她最主要的知己。

例句：The chiefest of these reasons is lack of funding.  这些原因中最主要的是缺乏资金。

近义词：principal, main, leading, foremost, paramount, primary, supreme
反义词：least important, minor, secondary
词性：adjective

词源：From Middle English *chiefest, a superlative of chief, from Old French chief, from Latin caput (“head”).
