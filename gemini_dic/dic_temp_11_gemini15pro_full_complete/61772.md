empathy:/ˈɛmpəθi/| n.|同情，同感，共鸣|The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.|She felt deep empathy for the victims of the earthquake. 她对地震灾民深感同情。|Synonyms: compassion, understanding, sympathy|Antonyms: apathy, indifference|noun
