decoys:/ˈdiːkɔɪz/ (US), /ˈdiːkɔɪz/ (UK)| v. |诱骗；引诱|To decoy is to lure or entice into a trap or dangerous situation. |The police decoyed the criminal into a sting operation. 警察将罪犯诱入了一次突击行动。|

decoy:/ˈdiːkɔɪ/ (US), /ˈdiːkɔɪ/ (UK)| v. |诱骗；引诱|To decoy is to lure or entice into a trap or dangerous situation. |They decoyed him away from his friends. 他们把他从他的朋友身边引开。|


Etymology: From Middle Dutch *dēcoy, from de- "down" + coy "cage". Originally referred to a cage used to trap ducks.


近义词: lure, bait, trap, snare, enticement
反义词: warning, deterrent, repellent
词性: noun (plural decoys), verb (decoys, decoying, decoyed)
