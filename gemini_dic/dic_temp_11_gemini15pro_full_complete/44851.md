crustiness:/ˈkrʌstinəs/| n.|硬皮, 粗糙, 暴躁|The crustiness of the bread made it difficult to eat. 面包的硬皮使它难以食用。|例句：The old man's crustiness hid a kind heart.  这位老人的暴躁掩盖了一颗善良的心。

例句：The crustiness of the soil made it hard to plant anything. 土壤的硬化使种植任何东西都很困难。|近义词：hardness, crispness, gruffness, surliness|反义词:softness, tenderness, gentleness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From crusty + -ness.  Crusty itself comes from crust + -y, with crust ultimately deriving from Latin crusta "rind, shell, bark."
