nonphonetic:/ˌnɒn.fəˈnet.ɪk/ | adj. | 非语音的，不表音的 | Not representing speech sounds in a direct or systematic way, especially not corresponding to pronunciation. |  This spelling system is nonphonetic, making it difficult for learners.  这个拼写系统不表音，让学习者感到困难。 | Example: The English language is notoriously nonphonetic. 英语是出了名的不表音。 | Synonyms: non-phonetic, unphonetic, non-phonemic, unphonemic | Antonyms: phonetic, phonemic | adjective
