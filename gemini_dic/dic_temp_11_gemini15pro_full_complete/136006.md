nonrationalists:/ˌnɒnˈræʃənəlɪsts/ | n. | 非理性主义者 | People who do not base their beliefs or actions on reason and logic.  Those who prioritize emotions, intuition, or other non-rational factors in their decision-making processes. |  The nonrationalists in the group were more likely to be swayed by emotional appeals.  小组中的非理性主义者更容易被情感诉求所左右。| synonyms: mystics, romantics, intuitionists | antonyms: rationalists, logicians, empiricists | noun
nonrationalist:/ˌnɒnˈræʃənəlɪst/ | adj. | 非理性主义的 | Not based on or guided by reason and logic. Characterized by a reliance on emotions, intuition, or other non-rational factors. |  His nonrationalist approach to problem-solving often led to unexpected outcomes. 他非理性主义的解决问题的方法常常导致意想不到的结果。 | synonyms: irrational, emotional, intuitive | antonyms: rational, logical, empirical | adjective
