cystolith: /ˈsɪstəlɪθ/ | n. | 胞石 | A small, hard, usually calcareous body found in the epidermal cells of certain plants, especially in the leaves of some members of the families Moraceae (e.g., fig) and Urticaceae (e.g., nettle). | Cystoliths are often visible as small bumps on the surface of the leaf. 胞石通常在叶子的表面可见，呈小突起状。 |  Synonyms: concretion | Antonyms: N/A | noun

cystolith: /ˈsɪstəlɪθ/ | n. | 膀胱结石 | A stone in the urinary bladder. | Cystoliths can cause pain and difficulty urinating. 膀胱结石会导致疼痛和排尿困难。 | Synonyms: bladder stone, vesical calculus | Antonyms: N/A | noun
