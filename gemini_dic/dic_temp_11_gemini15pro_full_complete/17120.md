battening:/ˈbætənɪŋ/ | n. |板条，装板条；养肥，增肥 |The act of fastening or covering with battens; the material used for this. Also, the act of thriving or fattening. |The battening on the roof needs to be replaced. 屋顶上的板条需要更换。| 近义词：boarding, planking, cladding | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑thinning 衰减 if referring to fattening) | noun

batten:/ˈbætən/ | v. |用板条固定；养肥，增肥；准备，做好准备（尤指做坏事）| To fasten or cover with battens; to thrive or fatten; to prepare oneself, especially for something bad. |They battened down the hatches before the storm. 他们在暴风雨来临前用板条固定了舱口。| 近义词：secure, reinforce, fatten | 反义词: weaken, starve (depending on the meaning) | verb
