ecotype:/ˈiːkəʊtaɪp/ | n. |生态型|A genetically distinct population within a species that is adapted to specific environmental conditions.  A subgroup of a species that has adapted to local environmental conditions.|The ecotype found in the mountains is more resistant to cold temperatures.  在山区发现的生态型更耐寒。|Synonym: subspecies, race, variety|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "species" as a broader term)|noun
