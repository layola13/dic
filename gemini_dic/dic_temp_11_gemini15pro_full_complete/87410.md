herse:/hɜːrs/ | n. | 棺材架;灵车 |A framework, usually with wheels, for conveying a coffin to the grave; a hearse. | The herse carried the coffin slowly to the graveyard. 灵车缓缓地将棺材运送到墓地。 | Example: The mourners followed the herse to the cemetery. 哀悼者们跟随灵车前往墓地。| Synonym: bier, catafalque | Antonym: N/A | noun

herse: /hɜːrs/ | v. | (用灵车)运送(死者);埋葬 | To convey (a dead body) in a hearse; to bury. (Archaic) | They hersed the king with great ceremony. 他们以隆重的仪式埋葬了国王。 | Example: The fallen soldier was hersed with full military honors.  这位阵亡的士兵以全面的军礼安葬。 | Synonym: inter, bury | Antonym: exhume, disinter | verb

词源：From Old French herce "harrow," also "frame for candles or tapers set on a coffin," from Latin hirpex "harrow," later associated with hercia "framework for a coffin." The sense shifted from the framework for candles to the vehicle carrying the coffin.
