antiexpressionism:/ˌæntiɪkˈspreʃənɪzəm/
n.
反表现主义
A movement in art, especially painting, that reacted against Abstract Expressionism by rejecting emotionalism and emphasizing cool detachment and objective representation.
The artist's work was a clear example of antiexpressionism, favoring geometric shapes over gestural brushstrokes.  这位艺术家的作品是反表现主义的明显例子，它偏爱几何形状而不是姿态性的笔触。
近义词:Formalism, Minimalism
反义词:Expressionism, Abstract Expressionism
词性:noun
