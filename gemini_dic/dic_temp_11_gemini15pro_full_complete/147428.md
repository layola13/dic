overnursing:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrsɪŋ/ | n. |过度护理，过分照顾|The act of providing too much care or medical attention, often to the detriment of the patient's independence and recovery.  It can lead to learned helplessness and delayed development.| The child's overnursing by his parents hindered his ability to develop self-reliance.  这个孩子被父母过度照顾，阻碍了他自立能力的发展。 |Overcare, overprotection, excessive care|Undercaring, neglect|Noun
