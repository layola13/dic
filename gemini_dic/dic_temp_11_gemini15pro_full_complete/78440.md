get-wise:/ɡet waɪz/ | adj. | 变聪明，学乖，醒悟 | To become aware of or informed about something, often something that was previously hidden or unknown.  It implies gaining knowledge or understanding, often through experience or by being informed by someone else. |  He finally got wise to their tricks. 他终于识破了他们的伎俩。 | 近义词:  catch on, wise up, become aware | 反义词:  be naive, be gullible | 词性: 形容词
