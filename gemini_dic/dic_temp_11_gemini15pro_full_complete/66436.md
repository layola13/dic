exemplariness:/ɪɡˈzɛmplərɪnɪs/
n.
模范性；典范性；堪称楷模
The quality of being exemplary; worthiness of imitation.
Her exemplariness in fulfilling her duties was praised by her superiors. 她尽职尽责的模范性受到了上司的赞扬。
Synonyms: perfection, ideality, flawlessness
Antonyms: imperfection, inadequacy
noun
