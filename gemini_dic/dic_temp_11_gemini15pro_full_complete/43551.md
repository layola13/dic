crayons:/ˈkreɪɑːnz/ (US), /ˈkreɪənz/ (UK)| n. |蜡笔；彩色蜡笔|Small sticks of colored wax, used for drawing or coloring.|例句：The children were drawing pictures with crayons. 孩子们正在用蜡笔画画。

例句：She bought a new box of crayons for her niece. 她给侄女买了一盒新的蜡笔。|近义词：colored pencils, wax crayons|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "paints," "markers")|词性: Noun
