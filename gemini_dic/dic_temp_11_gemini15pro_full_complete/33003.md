chorisis:/kɔːˈraɪsɪs/ | n. |分离，分体 |The separation of one part of a body from another, especially the separation of parts normally joined, as in a congenital fissure.  A congenital cleft or fissure; a division of an organ or part into two by a cleft or fissure.|  Example:  Chorisis of the eye can lead to vision problems.  眼睛的分体可能导致视力问题。 | Example: The doctor diagnosed chorisis of the spine. 医生诊断出脊柱分离。 | Synonym:  fissure, cleft, division, separation | Antonym:  fusion, union, joining | noun
