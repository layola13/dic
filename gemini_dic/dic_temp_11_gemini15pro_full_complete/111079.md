listicles:/ˈlɪstɪkəlz/| n. |列表文章；清单体文章|An online article presented in the form of a numbered or bulleted list.  A piece of online writing that uses a list as its basic structure.|Example: BuzzFeed is known for its listicles. BuzzFeed以其列表文章而闻名。|Example:  Listicles are a popular format for online content. 列表文章是一种流行的在线内容形式。|近义词：list, article, post|反义词: essay, treatise|noun
