attorn:/əˈtɜːrn/ | v. | 转让；承认新业主；（尤指租户）同意将租金或服务转给新业主 | To transfer one's tenancy or allegiance to another.  To agree to recognize a new owner of property or a new landlord. | The tenant attorned to the new landlord.  租户承认了新房东。| Synonyms: transfer, acknowledge, recognize | Antonyms: disavow, reject | verb

attorn:/əˈtɜːrn/ | n. |  承认新业主；转让 | The act of attorning. | The attornment of the tenant to the new landlord was legally documented.  租户对新房东的承认已合法记录在案。| Synonyms: transfer, acknowledgment | Antonyms: rejection, disavowal | noun
