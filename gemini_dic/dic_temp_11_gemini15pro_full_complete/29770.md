catties:/ˈkætiz/| n.|斤（中国旧制重量单位）|A catty is a traditional Chinese unit of weight, equal to approximately 1.3 pounds or 600 grams.|He bought two catties of pork. 他买了二斤猪肉。|Synonyms: jin, kati|Antonyms: N/A|noun
