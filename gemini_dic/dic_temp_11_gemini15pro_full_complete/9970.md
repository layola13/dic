aphids:/ˈeɪfɪdz/ (US), /ˈeɪfɪdz/ (UK)| n. |蚜虫|Small, soft-bodied insects that feed by sucking sap from plants.|例句：The aphids are destroying my roses. 蚜虫正在摧毁我的玫瑰花。|例句：Aphids can spread plant viruses. 蚜虫会传播植物病毒。|近义词：greenfly, blackfly, plant lice|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 aphid predator 蚜虫捕食者)|词性:noun
