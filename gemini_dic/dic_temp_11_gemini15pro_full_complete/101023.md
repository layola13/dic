invariantly:/ɪnˈveəriəntli/| adv.|总是；不变地；一定地|Always; without variation; without exception.  It describes something that happens consistently and predictably.|The sun invariably rises in the east. 太阳总是从东方升起。|The results of the experiment were invariably positive. 实验结果总是积极的。|Synonyms: always, constantly, consistently, perpetually, uniformly, inevitably, necessarily|Antonyms: sometimes, occasionally, rarely, infrequently, variably|adverb
