gastronomist:/ɡæˈstrɒnəmɪst/ | n. | 美食家；烹调专家 | A person who is knowledgeable about good food and drink, often a connoisseur or professional critic. |  She's a well-known gastronomist and often writes restaurant reviews for the local paper. 她是一位著名的美食家，经常为当地报纸撰写餐厅评论。 | synonym:gourmet, epicure, foodie | antonym:glutton (贬义 - 贪吃的人) | noun

gastronomist:/ɡæˈstrɒnəmɪst/ | n. | 美食家；烹调专家 | A person who is an expert in gastronomy. |  The renowned gastronomist gave a lecture on molecular gastronomy.  这位著名的美食家做了一场关于分子料理的讲座。 | synonym:culinary expert, food connoisseur | antonym:philistine (对美食艺术不感兴趣的人) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French gastronomiste, from Greek gastēr (γαστήρ) "belly, stomach" + nomos (νόμος) "law, custom".  So literally, "laws of the stomach."
