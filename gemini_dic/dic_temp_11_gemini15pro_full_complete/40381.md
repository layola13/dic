contradictor:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktə(r)/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktər/ | n. |反驳者，矛盾者|A person who contradicts someone or something.| He is a known contradictor of the government's policies. 他是有名的政府政策反对者。| Synonyms: opponent, adversary, antagonist, gainsayer | Antonyms: supporter, advocate, proponent | noun
contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktɔːri/ | adj. |矛盾的，对立的，不一致的|Containing or characterized by contradiction.| The witness gave contradictory statements to the police. 目击者向警方提供了相互矛盾的陈述。| Synonyms: conflicting, opposing, inconsistent, incompatible | Antonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious | adjective
