abrenunciation:/əˌbrɛnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/
n.
放弃，脱离关系，断绝关系
The act of renouncing or rejecting something, typically a right, claim, or belief.
His abrenunciation of his former life was complete. 他彻底放弃了他以前的生活。
近义词：renunciation, rejection, abandonment
反义词：acceptance, embrace, affirmation
词性：名词

词源：From ab- + renunciation.  "Ab-" is a Latin prefix meaning "away from" or "off,"  and "renunciation" comes from the Latin word "renuntiare," meaning "to disown, renounce, or reject."
