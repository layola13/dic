anxietude:/æŋˈzaɪəˌtjuːd/ | n. | 焦虑，不安 | A state of anxiousness or uneasiness.  A feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease, typically about an imminent event or something with an uncertain outcome. | She suffered from constant anxietude about her health. 她一直担心自己的健康。|  近义词: anxiety, worry, apprehension, solicitude, disquiet | 反义词: calmness, tranquility, serenity, composure | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *anxietudo*, from Latin anxius (“anxious”) + -tudo (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).
