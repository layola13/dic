fortissimo:/fɔːrˈtɪsɪmoʊ/ (US), /fɔːˈtɪsɪməʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.| 极强地，很响亮地；极强音，最强音 |  An Italian word used in music to indicate that a passage should be played very loudly.  It is the superlative of *forte*. | The orchestra played the final chords *fortissimo*.  管弦乐队以最强音演奏了最后的和弦。 | 近义词: very loud, extremely loud, loudest | 反义词: pianissimo, softly | 词性: adjective, adverb, noun
Etymology: From Italian *fortissimo*, superlative of *forte* ("loud"), ultimately from Latin *fortis* ("strong").
