comprehensive:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪv/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhensɪv/| adj.|全面的，综合的，广泛的，理解的|Covering or including everything or almost everything; complete.|例句：We offer a comprehensive range of services. 我们提供全方位的服务。|近义词：complete, thorough, extensive, full, inclusive|反义词：limited, restricted, narrow, incomplete, partial|词性:形容词

comprehensive:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪv/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhensɪv/| n.|综合学校|A secondary school that accepts pupils of all abilities.|例句：My child attends a local comprehensive. 我的孩子在当地一所综合学校上学。|近义词：secondary school, high school|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑私立学校 private school, 或专科学校 specialized school)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin comprehensivus, from comprehendere "to grasp, seize," from com- "together" + prehendere "to grasp."
