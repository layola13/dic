downlike:/ˈdaʊnlaɪk/| adj.|像绒毛的，柔软的|Resembling down; soft and fluffy.|The blanket had a downlike texture. 这条毯子有着像绒毛一样的质感。|The chick's feathers were downlike and soft. 小鸡的羽毛像绒毛一样柔软。|近义词：downy, fluffy, soft, feathery|反义词：coarse, rough, prickly|词性:形容词

词源:
From "down" (referring to the soft underfeathers of birds) + "-like" (suffix meaning resembling or similar to).
