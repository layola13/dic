jubilations:/ˌdʒuːbɪˈleɪʃənz/| n. |欢腾，庆祝活动|A feeling of great happiness and triumph, especially due to success.|The team's victory sparked wild jubilations among their fans. 球队的胜利引发了球迷们的狂欢。|
近义词：celebrations, rejoicing, festivities, revelry|
反义词：mourning, grief, sorrow|
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源:
From Late Latin jubilationem, accusative of jubilatio (“a shouting for joy”), from jubilare (“to shout for joy”), from jubilum (“a shout of joy”), of imitative origin.
