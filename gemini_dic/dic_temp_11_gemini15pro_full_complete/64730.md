eschewers:/ɪˈʃuːərz/
|n.|回避者；躲避者|People who deliberately avoid or abstain from something.  Those who shun or stay away from a particular activity, belief, or substance.|例句：He is among the eschewers of social media. 他是社交媒体的回避者之一。

例句：The restaurant is popular with eschewers of processed food. 这家餐厅很受避免加工食品的人的欢迎。|近义词：avoiders, abstainers, shunners, rejecters|反义词：embracers, seekers, enthusiasts, partakers|词性: noun (plural)

eschewer:/ɪˈʃuːər/
|n.|回避者；躲避者|A person who deliberately avoids or abstains from something. Someone who shuns or stays away from a particular activity, belief, or substance.|例句：She is an eschewer of violence in all its forms.  她是各种形式暴力的回避者。

例句：As an eschewer of fast fashion, he prefers to buy high-quality, durable clothing. 作为一名快时尚的回避者，他更喜欢购买高质量、耐用的服装。|近义词：avoider, abstainer, shunner, rejecter|反义词：embracer, seeker, enthusiast, partaker|词性: noun


eschew:/ɪsˈtʃuː/
|vt.|回避；避开；放弃|To deliberately avoid or abstain from something. To shun or stay away from a particular activity, belief, or substance.|例句：He eschews all forms of gambling. 他回避所有形式的赌博。

例句：Many athletes eschew unhealthy foods. 许多运动员避免不健康的食品。|近义词：avoid, abstain from, shun, forgo, renounce|反义词：embrace, seek, indulge in, partake in|词性: verb

词源: From Old French *eschiver "to shun, avoid, eschew," probably of Frankish origin, related to Old High German sciuhen "to frighten off, avoid,"  German scheuen "to shy away from."
