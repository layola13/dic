facade:/fəˈsɑːd/| n.|虚假的外表；假象|a deceptive outward appearance; a misrepresentation|He maintained a facade of calm, but inside he was panicking. 他表面上保持镇静，但内心却惊慌失措。|synonyms: pretense, mask, disguise, show|antonyms: genuineness, authenticity, reality|noun
