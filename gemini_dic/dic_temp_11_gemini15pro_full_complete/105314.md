kibitzer:/ˈkɪbɪtsər/| n. | 爱指手画脚的人，旁观者 | A person who kibitzes; someone who offers unwanted advice or commentary. | The kibitzers were annoying the players with their constant chatter.  那些爱指手画脚的人不断地闲聊，让玩家很烦。| Synonyms: busybody, meddler, interferer | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "observer" in a neutral sense) | noun
