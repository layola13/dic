necklaces:/ˈnɛklɪsɪz/
n.
项链（复数）
An ornament worn around the neck, typically consisting of a string of beads, jewels, or links of metal.
She wore a beautiful string of pearls as a necklace. 她戴着一串漂亮的珍珠项链。
