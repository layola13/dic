gangrene:/ˈɡæŋɡriːn/| n.|坏疽；腐败；道德败坏|localized death and decomposition of body tissue, resulting from obstructed circulation or bacterial infection.|The doctor diagnosed gangrene in his toe. 医生诊断他的脚趾患有坏疽。|近义词：necrosis, decay, rot|反义词:health, growth|noun


gangrenes:/ˈɡæŋɡriːnz/| v. (third-person singular simple present)|使坏死；腐败|(of flesh) Be affected with gangrene.|His leg gangrenes due to the severe infection. 他的腿由于严重感染而坏疽。|近义词：decays, rots, festers|反义词:heals, recovers|verb
