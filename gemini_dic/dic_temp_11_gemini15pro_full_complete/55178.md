dissensions:/dɪˈsɛnʃənz/| n. |分歧，不和，争吵|Disagreement, sharp difference of opinion.|例句：The dissensions within the party threatened to tear it apart.  党内的分歧有可能使其分裂。

例句：The committee was plagued by dissensions about the best course of action. 委员会一直被关于最佳行动方案的争论所困扰。|近义词：disagreements, disputes, discord, strife, conflict|反义词：agreement, harmony, concord, unity|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French dissension, from Latin dissensio(n-), from dissentire ‘differ in opinion’, from dis- ‘apart’ + sentire ‘feel’.
