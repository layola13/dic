disauthorize:/dɪsˈɔːθəraɪz/ | v. | 撤销授权，不准，拒绝认可 | To withdraw or cancel the authority of; to deprive of credit or authority. |  The CEO disauthorized the manager's purchase order. 首席执行官撤销了经理的采购订单。

The board disauthorized further negotiations. 董事会不再授权进一步谈判。


Synonyms: revoke, rescind, cancel, countermand, repudiate, retract,  invalidate
Antonyms: authorize, empower, commission, enable, sanction, approve, endorse
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + authorize.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," "away," expressing negation, reversal, or deprivation. "Authorize" comes from the Old French "autoriser" and ultimately from the Latin "auctorizare," meaning "to give authority to."
