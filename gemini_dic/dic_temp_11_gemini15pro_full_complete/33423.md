churchwardenly:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdənli/
adjective
像教堂管理员的；古板的；专横的
Resembling or characteristic of a churchwarden, especially in being officious or self-important.  Often implies a fussy, conservative, and somewhat authoritarian manner.
He puffed out his chest and delivered a churchwardenly reprimand about the state of the flowerbeds. 他挺起胸膛，像教堂管理员一样对花坛的状况进行了斥责。
Synonyms: officious, pompous, self-important, authoritarian, overbearing, fussy, interfering, meddlesome
Antonyms: humble, unassuming, modest, easygoing, laid-back
词性: 形容词
