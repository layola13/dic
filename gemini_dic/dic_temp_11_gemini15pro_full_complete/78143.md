geophagist:/ˌdʒiːˈɒfədʒɪst/
n.
食土癖者，食土者
A person who eats earth, especially clay or chalk.
She learned that some pregnant women in Africa are geophagists, consuming clay to supplement their diets. 她了解到非洲一些孕妇有食土癖，会吃粘土来补充营养。

A geophagist may crave the taste of dirt or believe it has medicinal properties. 食土癖者可能渴望泥土的味道，或者相信它有药用价值。


近义词:
clay-eater, dirt-eater
反义词:
(No direct antonym exists, but one could consider someone with a normal diet.)
