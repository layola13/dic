anti-creative:/ˌænti-kriˈeɪtɪv/| adj.|反创造性的，扼杀创造力的|Hindering or discouraging creativity; opposed to original thought or artistic expression.  It describes something that actively works against the creative process.|The company's rigid rules and regulations created an anti-creative environment.  这家公司的僵化规章制度造成了一个扼杀创造力的环境。

The teacher's harsh criticism had an anti-creative effect on the students. 老师的严厉批评对学生产生了反创造性的影响。|近义词：unimaginative, uninspired, restrictive, stifling|反义词：creative, imaginative, inspiring, innovative|词性:形容词
