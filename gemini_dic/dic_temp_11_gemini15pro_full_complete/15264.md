backhouse:/ˈbækˌhaʊs/ (US), /ˈbækˌhaʊs/ (UK)| n. | 后院；厕所；附属建筑 |A building behind the main house, often used as a toilet or for storage. | The old backhouse was converted into a workshop.  旧的后院被改造成一个工作室。| synonyms: outhouse, privy, lavatory, restroom, toilet, latrine, water closet (WC) | antonyms: main house, front house | noun


backhouse:/ˈbækˌhaʊs/ (US), /ˈbækˌhaʊs/ (UK)| n. | 私下交易场所；非法经营场所 (informal) |A place where secret or illegal activities take place. |  Police raided the backhouse where counterfeit goods were being produced. 警察突袭了生产假货的秘密场所。 | synonyms: den, hideout, dive | antonyms: legitimate business | noun
