extensions:/ɪkˈstɛnʃənz/ | n. | 扩展，延伸，延期，分机 | An addition to something that makes it longer or larger in space or time; a part that is added to something to enlarge or prolong it. | The house has recently had an extension built on. 这房子最近建了一个扩建部分。 | synonyms: addition, supplement, enlargement, expansion, annex, appendage | antonyms: reduction, shortening, curtailment | noun

extensions:/ɪkˈstɛnʃənz/ | n. | 延长，延期 | An act of making something longer or larger; an act of postponing something. | He applied for an extension of his visa. 他申请延长签证。 | synonyms: prolongation, lengthening, continuation, postponement, deferment | antonyms: shortening, curtailment, advancement | noun

extensions:/ɪkˈstɛnʃənz/ | n. | 分机 | A telephone that is connected to the main telephone line in a building or organization. | Please dial the extension number for the sales department. 请拨销售部的分机号码。 | synonyms: branch line, subsidiary line | antonyms: main line | noun

extensions:/ɪkˈstɛnʃənz/ | n. | 扩展名（计算机文件） | A group of letters that are added to the end of a computer file name to show the type of file. | The file has a .pdf extension. 该文件扩展名是 .pdf。 | synonyms: file type, suffix | antonyms: prefix | noun
