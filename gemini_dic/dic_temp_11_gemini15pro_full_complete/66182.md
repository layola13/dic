exchangers:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒərz/| n.|交换者，兑换者|People who exchange something, such as currency, goods, or information.|The currency exchangers at the airport offered competitive rates. 机场货币兑换处提供具有竞争力的汇率。|Synonyms: traders, dealers, brokers|Antonyms: hoarders, keepers|noun


exchangers:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒərz/| n.|交换器|A device or system that facilitates exchange, such as a heat exchanger.|The heat exchangers in the power plant are crucial for its operation.  发电厂的热交换器对其运行至关重要。|Synonyms: converters, transformers|Antonyms: N/A|noun
