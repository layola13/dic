disruptive:/dɪsˈrʌptɪv/
adjective
破坏性的；扰乱性的；引起混乱的
Causing or tending to cause disruption.
The disruptive behavior of the student in class caused the teacher to stop the lesson. 这个学生在课堂上的破坏性行为导致老师停止了课程。
The pandemic had a disruptive effect on global trade. 这场疫情对全球贸易造成了破坏性的影响。
innovative, groundbreaking, revolutionary, troublesome, unruly
constructive, helpful, beneficial
adjective
