overjudiciousness:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒuːˈdɪʃəsnəs/
|n.|过度判断；过分审慎|The state of being excessively judicious, often to the point of being overly critical or cautious.  Characterized by an excessive or undue reliance on judgment or discretion, often resulting in unnecessary caution or hesitation.|His overjudiciousness prevented him from taking necessary risks in business. 他过分的谨慎让他在生意上错失了必要的冒险。|近义词: overcautiousness, overscrupulousness, hypercriticism|反义词: recklessness, imprudence, impulsiveness|词性:noun

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "judicious" (from Latin "iudicium" meaning "judgment") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
