exsiccative:/ɪkˈsɪkətɪv/ | adj. n. |干燥的; 干燥剂 |Having the power to dry; tending to dry; drying; -- applied especially to substances which absorb moisture from the air or other bodies.|Silica gel is a common exsiccative. |硅胶是一种常见的干燥剂。|Synonyms: desiccant, drying, dehydrating |Antonyms: humectant, moisturizing |adjective, noun


exsiccate:/ˈɛksɪkeɪt/ | v. |使干燥，弄干 |To dry up; to exhaust of moisture. |The botanist exsiccated the plant specimens for preservation. |植物学家将植物标本干燥以保存。|Synonyms: desiccate, dehydrate, dry |Antonyms: moisten, hydrate |verb
