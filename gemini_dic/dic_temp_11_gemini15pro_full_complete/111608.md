lobworm:/ˈlɒbwɜːm/ (UK) /ˈlɑːbwɜːrm/ (US)| n.|蚯蚓, 钓饵|A large earthworm, especially one used for fishing bait. |例句：The fisherman dug up a lobworm for bait. 渔夫挖出一条蚯蚓作诱饵。

例句：Lobworms are a favorite food of many birds. 蚯蚓是许多鸟类最喜欢的食物。

近义词：earthworm, nightcrawler, dew worm|反义词:|词性：名词
