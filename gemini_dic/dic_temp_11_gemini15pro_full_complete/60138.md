effects:/ɪˈfɛkts/ | n. | 效果，影响，结果 | The effects of the medicine were immediate. 这种药的效果立竿见影。 | results, consequences, outcomes | causes, origins | noun

Etymology: From Old French *effete, past participle of effectuer ("to carry out, perform"), from Latin effectus ("a making out, accomplishing, performance"), past participle of efficere ("to work out, accomplish"), from ex- ("out") + facere ("to make, do").
