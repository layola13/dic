mistrusted:/mɪsˈtrʌstɪd/
Verb|不信任，怀疑|Regarded with suspicion or lack of confidence.|He mistrusted his own judgment after the accident. 事故之后，他不再相信自己的判断力。|近义词：doubted, suspected, questioned|反义词：trusted, believed, confided in
