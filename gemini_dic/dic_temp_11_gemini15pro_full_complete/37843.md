competing:/kəmˈpiːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 竞争的，相互矛盾的 | Engaging in a contest or striving for the same goal as another. |  Several companies are competing for the contract.  几家公司正在竞争这份合同。|Synonyms: vying, contending, rivaling | Antonyms: cooperating, collaborating | adjective

competing:/kəmˈpiːtɪŋ/ | n. | 竞争，比赛 | The act of striving against another for the same goal. | The competing demands of work and family life can be stressful. 工作和家庭生活相互矛盾的需求可能令人压力很大。| Synonyms: rivalry, contention, contest | Antonyms: cooperation, collaboration | noun
