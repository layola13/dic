hunkerousness:/ˈhʌŋkərəsnəs/
n.|蹲伏状态；执拗；顽固|A state of hunkering down; a stubborn or resolute attitude.  The quality or state of being hunkerous.|He showed his hunkerousness by refusing to leave his position. 他拒绝离开自己的位置，表现出了他的顽固。|近义词：stubbornness, obstinacy, tenacity, resolve|反义词：acquiescence, compliance, flexibility|词性:名词

词源: From "hunker" (to squat or crouch) + "-ous" (forming adjectives) + "-ness" (forming nouns).  "Hunker" is likely of Low German origin.
