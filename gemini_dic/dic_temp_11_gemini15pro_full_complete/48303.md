decrement:/ˈdɛkrɪmənt/ (US), /ˈdɛkrɪm(ə)nt/ (UK)| n. v.|减少，减量; 递减|A decrement is a reduction or decrease in a quantity or value. As a verb, it means to reduce or decrease something by a fixed amount.|

例句：
The decrement in sales was a cause for concern.
销售额的下降令人担忧。

The program decrements the counter by one each time.
该程序每次将计数器递减一。


近义词：decrease, reduction, decline, lessening|
反义词: increment, increase, rise, augmentation|
词性: noun, verb
