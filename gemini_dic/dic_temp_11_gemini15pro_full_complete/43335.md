crampest:/ˈkræmpɪst/| adj.|最狭窄的，最局促的，最受限制的|Superlative form of "cramped": most cramped.  Meaning extremely confined, restricted, or lacking in space.|The crampest room I've ever stayed in was barely big enough for a bed. 我住过的最狭窄的房间几乎只够放一张床。|The attic room was crampest, with barely enough space to stand. 阁楼房间是最狭窄的，几乎没有足够的空间站立。|近义词:tightest, most confined, smallest|反义词:roomiest, most spacious, largest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "cramp" + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Cramp" originates from Old English "cramp" or Proto-Germanic "krampiz", relating to something bent or crooked.
