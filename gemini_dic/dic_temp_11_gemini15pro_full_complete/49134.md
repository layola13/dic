deist:/ˈdiːɪst/
n.

A person who believes in deism.

近义词：freethinker, rationalist
反义词：theist
词性：noun

deism:/ˈdiːɪzəm/
n.
自然神论
Belief in the existence of a supreme being, specifically a creator who does not intervene in the universe.
Deism became popular during the Enlightenment. 自然神论在启蒙运动时期变得流行。
近义词：natural religion
反义词：theism
词性：noun


词源:
From French déiste (17th century), from Latin deus (“god”).
