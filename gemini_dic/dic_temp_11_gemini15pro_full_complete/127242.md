nautch: /nɔːtʃ/ | n. | 印度传统舞蹈，通常指带有性暗示的舞蹈 | A traditional Indian dance performance, often with sexual overtones. |  The British colonialists often misinterpreted and sexualized the nautch. 英国殖民者经常误解并将nautch舞蹈性化。

The nautch was a popular form of entertainment in Mughal courts.  在莫卧儿王朝的宫廷里，nautch是一种流行的娱乐形式。 | 近义词: dance, performance | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "sermon" or "lecture" in a contrasting context) | 词性: noun

词源: From Hindi nāc (dance), from Sanskrit nṛtya.
