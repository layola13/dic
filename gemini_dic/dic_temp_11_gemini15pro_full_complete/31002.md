chalkiness:/ˈtʃɔːkɪnɪs/
n.
垩白色; 白垩质;  粉状
The state or quality of being chalky; resembling chalk in appearance, texture, or consistency.
The apples had a chalkiness that made them unpleasant to eat. 这些苹果有白垩般的质感，吃起来很不舒服。
近义词:friability, crumbliness
反义词:smoothness, firmness
词性:名词
