beelzebub:/biːˈɛlzɪbʌb/| n. |别西卜，魔王|Beelzebub is a name derived from a Philistine god, often used as another name for the Devil or a high-ranking demon.  It originates from the Hebrew  Ba'al Zəbûb, meaning "Lord of the Flies".|He invoked Beelzebub in his dark rituals. 他在黑暗仪式中召唤了别西卜。|Near synonyms: Satan, Lucifer, Devil|Antonym: God
词性: 名词

beelzebubs:/biːˈɛlzɪbʌbz/| n. |别西卜们，魔王们 (Beelzebub的复数形式)|The plural form of Beelzebub, referring to multiple entities associated with the Devil or high-ranking demons.|The preacher warned of the influence of beelzebubs in the world.  传教士警告世人要小心魔王们的影响。|Near synonyms: devils, demons, satans|Antonym: angels, gods
