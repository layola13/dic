moblogged:/moʊˈblɑːɡd/ /ˈməʊblɒɡd/| v. | 用手机写博客；移动博客 | to post to a blog using a mobile device, typically a cell phone. | I moblogged the whole concert using my phone. 我用手机把整场音乐会都记录在博客上了。 |  I moblogged photos of the sunset directly from the beach. 我直接从海滩上用手机把日落的照片上传到博客。 | 近义词: blogged, posted, updated | 反义词: None | 词性: 动词

词源:  A blend of "mobile" and "blogged," the past participle of "blog."  It arose in the early 2000s with the advent of camera phones and mobile internet access.
