disputacity: /ˌdɪspjʊˈtæsɪti/
noun
争论性，争议性
The quality or state of being disputable or controversial.  It refers to the likelihood of something being argued or debated.  It's not a common word in standard English.
The disputacity of the election results led to widespread protests. 选举结果的争议性导致了广泛的抗议。
contestedness, contentiousness, debatability
indisputability, certainty, clarity
