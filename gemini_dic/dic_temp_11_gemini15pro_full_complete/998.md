acarpellous:/eɪkɑːrˈpɛləs/
adj.
无心皮的;没有心皮的
Lacking carpels; having no carpels.  A carpel is the female reproductive organ of a flower, so an acarpellous flower is one that lacks this structure.
The mutant strain produced acarpellous flowers, rendering it sterile.  这种突变株开出无心皮的花，使其不育。
The acarpellous flowers were unable to produce seeds. 这些无心皮的花朵无法产生种子。
近义词:carpel-less
反义词:carpellate
词性:adjective
