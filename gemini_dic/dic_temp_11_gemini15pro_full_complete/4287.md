airworthier:/ˈɛərwɜːrðiər/ |adj.|更适航的|More suitable for air travel; being in a better condition for safe flight.|The newly renovated plane is airworthier than before. 这架新装修的飞机比以前更适航了。|Example: After the repairs, the aircraft was deemed considerably airworthier.  修理之后，这架飞机被认为适航性大大提高了。|Synonyms: safer, sounder, more reliable|Antonyms: less airworthy, unsafe, unreliable|adjective
