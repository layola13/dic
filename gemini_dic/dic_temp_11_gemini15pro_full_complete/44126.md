crispy:/ˈkrɪspi/| adj.|脆的，酥脆的|Pleasantly firm and dry; easily broken or crumbled.|The fries were perfectly crispy.|近义词： crunchy, brittle, flaky|反义词: soggy, soft|词性:形容词
词源: From the Middle English *crisp*, from Old English *crisp* "curly," related to *crīps* "crisp, crinkled." Ultimately of imitative origin.
