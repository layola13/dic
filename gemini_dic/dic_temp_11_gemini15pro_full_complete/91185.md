hydrates:/ˈhaɪdreɪts/ | n. | 水合物 | A compound, typically a crystalline one, in which water molecules are chemically bound to another compound or an element. | Copper sulfate pentahydrate is a common example of a hydrate. 五水硫酸铜是水合物的常见例子。 | synonyms: compound, complex | antonyms: anhydrous | noun

hydrates:/ˈhaɪdreɪts/ | v. | 使水合，水化 | To cause to combine with water. |  The cement hydrates and hardens. 水泥水化变硬。 | synonyms: moisturize, hydrate | antonyms: dehydrate | verb
