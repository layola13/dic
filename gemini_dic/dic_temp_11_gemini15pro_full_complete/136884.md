nonsobriety:/nɒn.səˈbraɪ.ə.ti/
n.|非清醒，不节制|The state of not being sober; lack of sobriety.  This can refer to being under the influence of alcohol or other drugs, or more broadly to a lack of restraint or moderation.|His nonsobriety led to poor decision-making. 他的不节制导致了糟糕的决策。| Near synonyms: intoxication, inebriation, drunkenness (when referring to alcohol)| Antonyms: sobriety, temperance, abstinence|Noun
