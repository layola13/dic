manneristically:/ˌmænəˈrɪstɪkli/
adverb
以特定方式地，矫揉造作地
in a manner that is characteristic of a particular person or artist; in a stylized manner.
He coughed manneristically. 他装腔作势地咳嗽。
近义词:affectedly, artificially, stylistically
反义词:naturally, genuinely, unpretentiously
词性:副词

词源:
From manner + -istic + -al + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *manuarius (“relating to the hand”).
