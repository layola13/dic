gymnasium: /dʒɪmˈneɪziəm/ (US) /dʒɪmˈneɪziəm/ (UK)| n. |体育馆，健身房|A large room with equipment for exercising and playing sports. gym: /dʒɪm/ (US) /dʒɪm/ (UK)| n. |健身房，体育馆|(Short for gymnasium) A place for physical exercise.| I go to the gym three times a week. 我每周去健身房三次。| synonyms: gymnasium, fitness center, health club| antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps couch, sofa) | noun


Etymology:  Both "gymnasium" and "gym" come from the Greek word "gymnasion,"  meaning "a place for naked exercise," ultimately derived from "gymnos" meaning "naked." In ancient Greece, athletes exercised naked.
