outhammers:/ˌaʊtˈhæmər/
verb
胜过，以更大的力度敲打
to hit or strike something with greater force than someone or something else.
例句：
He outhammered his opponent in the final round.
他在最后一轮比赛中以更大的力度击败了对手。

例句：
The blacksmith outhammered the other competitors in the contest.
这位铁匠在比赛中比其他参赛者敲打得更有力。

近义词：outdo, surpass, outperform, exceed
反义词：be outdone by, be surpassed by, be outperformed by
词性：verb
