nonpragmatic:/ˌnɒnpræɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非实用主义的，不务实的 | Not practical or realistic; concerned with theory rather than practical results. |  His nonpragmatic approach to the problem led to a lot of wasted time and resources.  他处理这个问题的方法不切实际，导致大量时间和资源的浪费。 | Example: He had a nonpragmatic approach to business, focusing on ideals rather than profits. 他不务实地经商，注重理想而不是利润。 | Synonyms: impractical, unrealistic, idealistic, theoretical, ivory-tower | Antonyms: pragmatic, practical, realistic, down-to-earth | Word type: Adjective
