eliminators:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪtərz/
n.|消除器；淘汰者；清除剂|People or things that eliminate something or someone.  Those who remove or get rid of something unwanted or undesirable.|The eliminators in the tournament were ruthless. 淘汰赛中的选手们毫不留情。|Synonyms: removers, exterminators, eradicators|Antonyms: creators, builders, preservers
