infashionable:/ɪnˈfæʃ(ə)nəbl/
adjective
不时髦的，不流行的
Not fashionable; out of style.
Her infashionable clothes made her stand out in the crowd. 她那过时的衣服使她在人群中显得格格不入。
近义词:outmoded, outdated, unfashionable, out of style
反义词:fashionable, stylish, trendy, chic
词性:adjective

infashionable:/ɪnˈfæʃ(ə)nəbl/
adjective
不合时宜的
Not appropriate or suitable for the current time or situation.
His infashionable ideas were quickly dismissed. 他不合时宜的想法很快就被否决了。
近义词:inappropriate, unsuitable, outdated
反义词:appropriate, suitable, timely
