high-days:/ˈhaɪˌdeɪz/ |n.| 节日，喜庆日，盛典 |Special festive days or holidays; times of celebration. | We feasted and made merry during the high-days. 我们在节日期间尽情欢乐和庆祝。 | 近义词: holidays, festivals, celebrations | 反义词: ordinary days, weekdays | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *highe daies*, from Old English *hēahdæȝ*, from *hēah* (“high”) + *dæȝ* (“day”).  Essentially, "high days" referred to important holy days in the church calendar.
