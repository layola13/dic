improbablenesses:/ɪmˌprɒbəˈbɪlɪtɪz/ |n.| 不可能性；难以置信的事情 |The improbablenesses of the situation made it hard to believe.  这种情况的难以置信程度让人难以相信。|例句：The sheer improbablenesses of his story made it difficult to take seriously. 他的故事的纯粹不可能性使得很难认真对待。

The improbablenesses of winning the lottery are enormous.  赢得彩票的可能性微乎其微。|近义词：impossibilities, unlikelihoods|反义词：probabilities, likelihoods|词性:名词

Etymology: From improbable + -ness.  Improbable from im- (not) + probable (likely).  Probable from Latin probabilis, from probare (to prove, test).
