deaths:/deθs/
noun
死亡人数，死亡事件
the number of people who have died, or an instance of dying
The hospital reported five deaths overnight.  医院报告夜间有五人死亡。
近义词:fatalities, mortalities
反义词:births, lives
词性:n.

Etymology:
From Middle English *dethes, plural of deth, from Old English dēaþ (“death”), from Proto-Germanic *dauþuz (“death”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰówtis (“throat, dissolution”). Cognate with West Frisian dead (“death”), Dutch dood (“death”), German Tod (“death”), Danish død (“death”), Swedish död (“death”), Icelandic dauði (“death”).
