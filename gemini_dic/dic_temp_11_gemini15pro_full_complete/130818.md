nonapparitional:/ˌnɒnˌæpəˈrɪʃənəl/
adjective
非幽灵的；非幻影的；非鬼魂的
Not of or pertaining to an apparition; not appearing as a ghost or phantom.
The event was described as a nonapparitional experience, more like a strong feeling of presence.  这个事件被描述为一种非幽灵的体验，更像是一种强烈的存在感。
The scientist sought a nonapparitional explanation for the strange phenomena.  科学家试图为这种奇怪的现象寻找一种非超自然的解释。
近义词:tangible, real, concrete, material
反义词:apparitional, ghostly, spectral, phantom
词性:adjective

词源：
From non- + apparitional, from apparition, from Late Latin apparitionem (nominative apparitio) "an appearance," noun of action from past-participle stem of apparere "to appear," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + parere "to come forth, be visible," related to parere "to bring forth, give birth to" (see parent (n.)).  来自non- + apparitional，来自apparition，来自晚期拉丁语apparitionem（主格apparitio）“出现”，来自apparere“出现”的过去分词词干的动作名词，来自ad-“to”（见ad-）+ parere“出现，可见”，与parere“生产，生育”有关（见parent（n.））。
