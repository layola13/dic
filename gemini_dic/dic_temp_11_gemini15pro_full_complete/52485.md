dieticians:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃənz/
n.
营养师，饮食学家
Experts in food and nutrition who advise people on healthy eating habits.
例句：The dieticians recommend eating a balanced diet. 营养师建议均衡饮食。

例句：Many hospitals employ dieticians to help patients with special dietary needs. 很多医院雇佣营养师来帮助有特殊饮食需求的病人。

近义词：nutritionists
反义词: 无
