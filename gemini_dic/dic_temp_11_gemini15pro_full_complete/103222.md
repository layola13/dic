jettee: /ˈdʒɛti/ (US), /ˈdʒɛtiː/ (UK)| n. |防波堤，突堤，码头|A structure that projects into a body of water, typically used as a pier, breakwater, or walkway.|例句：We walked along the jettee, enjoying the sea breeze. 我们沿着突堤散步，享受着海风。

例句：The ferry docked at the end of the jettee. 渡轮停靠在突堤的尽头。


近义词：pier, wharf, dock, breakwater|反义词:|词性：noun
