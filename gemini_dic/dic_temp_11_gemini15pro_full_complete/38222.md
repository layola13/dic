computational:/ˌkɑːmpjuˈteɪʃənl/ | adj. | 计算的，与计算有关的 | Relating to or involving the use of computers, especially in scientific calculations. |

例句：Computational linguistics is a rapidly growing field. 计算语言学是一个快速发展的领域。

例句：The project required a significant amount of computational power.  这个项目需要大量的计算能力。


近义词：calculated, computing, algorithmic | 反义词:  non-computational, analog | 词性: adjective


compute:/kəmˈpjuːt/ | v. | 计算，估算 | Calculate or reckon (a figure or amount). | 词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French computer, from Latin computare ‘to count, sum up,’ from com- ‘together’ + putare ‘to think.’ |

例句：The program can compute the value of pi to a million decimal places.  这个程序可以计算π的值到小数点后一百万位。

例句：We need to compute the total cost of the project. 我们需要计算项目的总成本。


近义词：calculate, reckon, estimate | 反义词: guess, estimate (when used informally) | 词性: verb
