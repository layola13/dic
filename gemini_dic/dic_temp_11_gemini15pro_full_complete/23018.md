boulangism:/buːˈlɑːnʒɪzəm/
n.
布朗热主义
A late 19th-century French political movement characterized by authoritarian, populist, nationalist, and anti-Semitic ideals, led by General Georges Boulanger.
General Boulanger's boulangism threatened the stability of the Third Republic. 布朗热将军的布朗热主义威胁到第三共和国的稳定。

近义词:populism, nationalism
反义词:internationalism, liberalism
词性:noun
