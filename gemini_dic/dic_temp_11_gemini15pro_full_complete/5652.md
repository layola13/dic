altitudinarian:/ˌæltɪtjuːdɪˈnɛəriən/
noun
高地居民；居住在高海拔地区的人；崇尚高海拔的人
A person who lives at a high altitude, or one who is fond of high altitudes.
Many altitudinarians find the thin air invigorating. 许多高地居民觉得稀薄的空气令人精神焕发。
synonyms: mountaineer, highlander
antonyms: lowlander
