neutered:/ˈnjuːtərd/| adj.|绝育的，阉割的|Deprived of the ability to reproduce by surgical removal of reproductive organs.  This term is typically used for animals, but can sometimes be used euphemistically for humans.|例句：The vet neutered our cat last week. 上周兽医给我们的猫做了绝育手术。|近义词：spayed (for females), castrated, desexed|反义词：intact, fertile|词性:形容词
neuter:/ˈnjuːtər/| n. adj. v.|中性的，中性词，使中性化|1.(n.) A word that refers to something that is neither masculine nor feminine. 2.(adj.) Neither masculine nor feminine in gender.  3.(v.)  To make something neutral or ineffective; to castrate or spay.|例句：In English, "it" is a neuter pronoun. 在英语中，“it”是一个中性代词。|近义词：neutral, asexual|反义词：masculine, feminine|词性:名词，形容词，动词
