omnibusses:/ˈɑːmnɪbʌsɪz/ | n. |公共汽车（尤指大型的）|A large motor vehicle carrying passengers by road, typically one serving a particular route.|The omnibusses were crowded during rush hour. 公共汽车在高峰时间很拥挤。|Synonym: buses, coaches|Antonym: car (in the context of public transport)|noun

omnibus:/ˈɑːmnɪbʌs/ | adj. |总括的，综合的；多项式的|Containing or including many items.|The omnibus bill covered several different topics.  这项综合法案涵盖了几个不同的主题。|Synonym: comprehensive, inclusive|Antonym: limited, specific|adjective
