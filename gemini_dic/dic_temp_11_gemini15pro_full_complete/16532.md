barking:/ˈbɑːrkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|吠叫的，咆哮的；吠叫，咆哮声|The continuous, loud barking of the dog kept me awake all night. 狗持续的大声吠叫让我整夜无法入睡.  (adj.) / The barking echoed through the forest. 吠叫声在森林中回荡。(n.) |近义词：yelping, baying (v.), yelp, bay, howl (n.) |反义词: whimpering, whining (v.), whimper, whine (n.)|
词性: adjective, noun

barking:/ˈbɑːrkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|吠叫，咆哮|The dog is barking at the mailman.  狗在对着邮递员吠叫。|近义词：yelping, baying |反义词: whimpering, whining|
词性: verb (present participle)

词源:  From Middle English *barkinge, verbal noun from barken (“to bark”).
