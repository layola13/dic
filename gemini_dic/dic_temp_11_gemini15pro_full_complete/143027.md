outbled:/ˌaʊtˈblɛd/

流血过多; 失血过多
To lose a large quantity of blood; to bleed profusely.
He outbled and died within minutes of the accident. 他在事故发生几分钟后就因失血过多而死亡。
Synonyms: hemorrhage, bleed profusely
Antonyms: clot, coagulate
