glaucomatous:/ˌɡlɔːˈkoʊmətəs/ /ˌɡlɔːˈkɒmətəs/
adjective
青光眼的，患青光眼的
Relating to or affected by glaucoma, a disease of the eye.
His father suffered from glaucomatous damage to his optic nerve. 他父亲的视神经遭受了青光眼损害。
近义词: None (Specific to glaucoma)
反义词: healthy, normal (in relation to eye health)
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *glaucomatosus, from Greek glaukōma 'cataract, glaucoma', from glaukos 'gleaming, bluish-grey', from the same Indo-European base as Latin glaucus 'bluish-green or grey' and English gleam. + -ous.
