lay-up:/ˈleɪ ʌp/ (US), /ˈleɪ ʌp/ (UK)| n.|上篮，停工期，临时解雇，(篮球)擦板球|An easy shot made from very close to the basket, typically after dribbling to the basket.|He made a simple lay-up for two points. 他上篮得了两分。|Near synonyms: lay in, layup |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "jump shot" or "three-pointer" as contrasting basketball shots)|noun
lay-up:/ˈleɪ ʌp/ (US), /ˈleɪ ʌp/ (UK)| n.|停工期，临时解雇|(especially in industrial contexts) a period when equipment or a business is not operating or producing goods.|The factory had a two-week lay-up for repairs. 工厂停工两周进行维修。|Near synonyms: shutdown, closure|Antonyms: operation, production|noun
