goitrogenous:/ˌɡɔɪtrəˈdʒɛnəs/ | adj. | 致甲状腺肿的；产生甲状腺肿的 | Containing or producing goitrogens, substances that interfere with the normal function of the thyroid gland. |  Foods like broccoli and cabbage are considered goitrogenous, especially when eaten raw.  西兰花和卷心菜等食物被认为是致甲状腺肿的，尤其是在生吃时。 | Synonyms: goitrogenic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "thyroid-supporting" or "iodine-rich" for contrasting effects) | adjective
