overimaginativeness:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmædʒɪnətɪvnəs/
n.
过度想象力; 过分异想天开
The state of having an overly active or vivid imagination, often leading to unrealistic or fanciful thoughts.
His overimaginativeness often led him to believe in conspiracies. 他过度的想象力经常让他相信阴谋论。
近义词：fantasy, fancifulness, creativity, inventiveness
反义词：realism, practicality, down-to-earthness
词性: noun

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + imaginative (adjective meaning "having or showing imagination") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
