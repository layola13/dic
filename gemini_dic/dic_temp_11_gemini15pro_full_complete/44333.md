croquet:/kroʊˈkeɪ/ /ˈkroʊkeɪ/| n. |槌球；槌球运动|A lawn game played on a court, typically by two or four players, with mallets, hoops, and balls. The object is to hit the balls through the hoops in a set order.|例句：They spent a sunny afternoon playing croquet on the lawn. 他们在阳光明媚的下午在草坪上玩槌球。

例句：The croquet set was a gift from her grandparents. 这套槌球是她祖父母送的礼物。|近义词：ground billiards|反义词:|词性:noun

croquets:/kroʊˈkeɪz/ /ˈkroʊkeɪz/| v. (third-person singular simple present) |打槌球|Plays croquet.|例句：He croquets with skill and precision. 他打槌球技术娴熟，精准到位。
