adamant:/ˈædəmənt/ | adj. n. | 坚定的，坚硬无比的；金刚石 |  Unshakable in determination; resolutely firm; also, an extremely hard substance, originally diamond. | He was adamant that he had not seen the woman before. 他坚决声称以前从未见过那个女人。 | 近义词: resolute, firm, unyielding, inflexible, obdurate | 反义词: yielding, flexible, compliant | 词性: adjective, noun

adamants:/ˈædəmənts/ | n. | 坚硬无比的物质（金刚石的复数） |  Plural of adamant, referring to extremely hard substances, originally diamonds. | The adamants glittered under the bright lights. 这些金刚石在明亮的灯光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词: diamonds | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源:
From Old French *adamant, from Latin adamantem, accusative of adamas "hardest iron, diamond, magnet," from Greek adamas, adamant-, literally "unconquerable, invincible," from a- "not" (see a- (3)) + daman "to tame, subdue" (see tame (v.)).
