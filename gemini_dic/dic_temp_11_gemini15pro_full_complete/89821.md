hornednesses:/ˈhɔːrnɪdnəsɪz/| n. |有角的状态；角的數量|The state or quality of having horns; the number or arrangement of horns. | The hornednesses of different antelope species vary greatly. 不同種類羚羊的角的狀態差異很大。|近义词:cornucopia (abundance, but not directly related to horns)|反义词: hornlessness|词性:noun
