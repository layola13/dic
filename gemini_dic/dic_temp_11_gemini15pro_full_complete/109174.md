legitimises:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
使…合法化；使…正统化
to make something legal or acceptable
The government has legitimised the use of cannabis for medicinal purposes. 政府已经将药用大麻的使用合法化。
synonyms: validate, justify, authorize, sanction
antonyms: outlaw, prohibit, ban, criminalize
词性: 动词 (verb)

legitimise (美式拼写 legitimize):/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/
