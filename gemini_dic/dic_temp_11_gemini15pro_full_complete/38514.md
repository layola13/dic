conciliatorily:/kənˈsɪliətɔːrɪli/
adverb
和解地；调停地；抚慰地
in a way that is intended to placate or pacify.
He spoke conciliatorily, hoping to avoid further argument. 他说话的语气很和 conciliatory，希望避免进一步的争论。
近义词:peacefully, appeasingly, placatingly, soothingly
反义词:aggressively, antagonistically, hostilely
词性:adverb

词源:
From conciliate + -ory + -ly.  Conciliate comes from Latin conciliare "to bring together, unite in feelings, make friendly," from concilium "council, meeting," from com- "together" (see com-) + calare "to call, summon" (see calendar).
