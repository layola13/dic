barreling:/ˈbærəlɪŋ/

barreling| n. |快速移动，飞驰 |The act of moving at high speed. |The barreling of the truck down the hill was terrifying. 卡车飞驰下山的情景十分可怕。|近义词：rush, dash, sprint |反义词:creep, crawl

PHRASE->
barrel along:  快速前进  The ship barreled along through the rough seas. 船在波涛汹涌的大海中快速前进。
barrel into: 撞上  The car barreled into a tree. 汽车撞到了一棵树上。
barrel through:  飞快地穿过  He barreled through the crowd. 他飞快地穿过人群。
barrel out of:  飞快地冲出  She barreled out of the room. 她飞快地冲出了房间。
barrel down:  飞驰而下 The truck barreled down the hill. 卡车飞驰下山。


词源： From barrel (n.), referring to the rolling motion of a barrel.  来自名词 barrel（桶），指的是桶的滚动运动。
