observance:/əbˈzɜːrvəns/| n.|遵守，奉行；庆祝；仪式|The observance of the law is important. 遵守法律很重要。|例句：Religious observances are an important part of their culture. 宗教仪式是他们文化的重要组成部分。

例句：The family gathers for the observance of Thanksgiving.  这家人聚在一起庆祝感恩节。|近义词：compliance, adherence, celebration, ceremony|反义词：violation, breach, neglect|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French observance, from observer ‘observe’.
