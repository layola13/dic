govern:/ˈɡʌvərn/ (US) /ˈɡʌvən/ (UK)| v. |统治，管理，支配，控制|to officially control and direct the public business of a country, city, group of people, etc.|The country is governed by a coalition government. 这个国家由一个联合政府统治。|The new regulations govern the sale of firearms. 新规章管控枪支销售。|近义词：rule, administer, manage, control, direct|反义词：follow, obey|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *governer*, from Latin *gubernare* "to steer, guide, direct, govern," from Greek *kybernan* "to steer, guide."
