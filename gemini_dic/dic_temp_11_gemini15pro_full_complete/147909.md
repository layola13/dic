overreflectiveness:/ˌoʊvərˌriːflɛkˈtɪvnəs/ | n. | 过度反思，过度反省 |  The tendency to think too much about oneself and one's actions, often leading to anxiety and indecision.  It involves excessive introspection and rumination, focusing on past mistakes or future uncertainties. |  Her overreflectiveness often paralyzed her decision-making process. 她过度反思常常导致她无法做出决定。 |  Overthinking, rumination, introspection, self-analysis | decisiveness, action-oriented | noun
