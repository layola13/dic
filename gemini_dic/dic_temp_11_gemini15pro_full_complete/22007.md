boilersuit: /ˈbɔɪləˌsuːt/ (UK) /ˈbɔɪlərˌsuːt/ (US)| n.|连体工作服，工装裤|A one-piece garment worn as protective clothing, typically by manual workers.|例句：He wore a blue boilersuit while working on the car. 他在修车时穿着一件蓝色的连体工作服。

例句：The mechanic's boilersuit was covered in grease. 机械师的连体工作服上沾满了油污。|近义词：coveralls, overalls, jumpsuit|反义词:suit, dress|词性:noun
