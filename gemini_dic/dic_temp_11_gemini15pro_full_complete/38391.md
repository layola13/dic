conceptualistic: /kənˌsɛptʃuəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
概念主义的; 概念化的
Relating to or characteristic of conceptualism, a philosophical theory that emphasizes the importance of abstract concepts or general ideas.  Characterized by or based on concepts or mental abstractions.
His art is highly conceptualistic, focusing on ideas rather than representation. 他的艺术高度概念化，注重理念而非具象表现。

Conceptualistic art often challenges traditional notions of aesthetics.  概念主义艺术经常挑战传统的审美观念。

Synonyms: abstract, conceptual, ideational, intellectual, theoretical
Antonyms: concrete, material, physical, practical, realistic
词性: adjective
