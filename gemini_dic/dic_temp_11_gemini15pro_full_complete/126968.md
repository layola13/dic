narrowest:/ˈnæroʊɪst/| adj.|最狭窄的|Superlative form of narrow; having the least width.|The narrowest part of the road is just ahead.  道路最窄的部分就在前面。|近义词：slimmest, thinnest, tightest|反义词:widest, broadest|词性:adjective
词源:From Old English nearu "near, close, confined, small, compressed," from Proto-Germanic *narwaz "narrow, confined," from PIE root *ner- (1) "on the left hand; below, under." The superlative ending "-est" is also of Germanic origin.
