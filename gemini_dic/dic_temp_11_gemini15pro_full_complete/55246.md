dissimilitude's:/ˌdɪsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːdz/ | n. |  不同, 差异, 相异 |  The state or quality of being dissimilar; unlikeness; lack of resemblance.  It refers to the differences or points of unlikeness between things.  It is the noun form of "dissimilar," formed by adding the suffix "-itude" (indicating a state or quality) to "dissimilar," and then the possessive apostrophe and 's'.| The dissimilitude's between the twins were striking, despite their identical genes. 这对双胞胎之间的差异令人吃惊，尽管他们的基因相同。| The dissimilitude's between the two paintings, supposedly by the same artist, raised questions of authenticity.  两幅据说是同一艺术家创作的画作之间的差异引发了人们对真伪的质疑。| 近义词: difference, disparity, dissimilarity, unlikeness, divergence, variation | 反义词: similarity, likeness, resemblance, similitude, conformity | 词性: 名词
