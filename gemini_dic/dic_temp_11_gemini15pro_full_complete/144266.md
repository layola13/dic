outsonneting:/ˌaʊtˈsɑːnɪtɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的十四行诗; 在十四行诗写作方面胜过
surpassing someone in the writing of sonnets.
Shakespeare is said to have outsonneted all his contemporaries. 据说莎士比亚的十四行诗超过了他所有的同时代人。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词: be inferior to, be worse than
词性: verb
