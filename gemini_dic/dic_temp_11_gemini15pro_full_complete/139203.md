objectivation:/əbˌdʒɛktɪvɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 客体化；物化 | The act of treating a person as an object, especially sexually.  The degradation of someone to the status of a mere object. |  Her objectivation in the media made her feel dehumanized.  媒体对她的物化让她感到被剥夺了人性。 | Synonyms: dehumanization, depersonalization, reification | Antonyms: humanization, personalization, subjectification | noun
