interreceives:/ˌɪntərɪˈsiːvz/ | v. |互相接收，相互收发 | To send and receive messages or data between two or more parties. | The computers interreceive data constantly to maintain synchronization.  这些计算机不断地相互接收数据以保持同步。| Example 2: The two radio stations interreceive signals to ensure clear communication.  这两个无线电台相互接收信号以确保清晰的通信。 | synonyms: exchange, communicate, interact | antonyms: isolate, disconnect | verb
