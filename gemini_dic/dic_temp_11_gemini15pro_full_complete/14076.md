australopith:/ɔˌstræləˈpɪθ/ | n. |南方古猿|An extinct hominid of the genus Australopithecus, closely related to and thought to be an ancestor of modern humans.  They lived in Africa between four and two million years ago.|例句：The discovery of "Lucy," an Australopithecus afarensis skeleton, was a major breakthrough in understanding human evolution.  发现“露西”（一个南方古猿阿法种的骨架）是理解人类进化的一项重大突破。

例句：Australopiths are known for their bipedalism, walking upright on two legs. 南方古猿以其双足行走，用两条腿直立行走而闻名。

近义词：hominid, hominin | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider modern human as a contrasting descendant) | 词性: noun
