occultation:/ˌɒkəlˈteɪʃən/ /ˌɑːkəlˈteɪʃən/| n.|掩星；掩蔽；遮蔽|The occultation of the star by the moon provided valuable data. 月亮遮蔽了这颗恒星，提供了宝贵的数据。|例句：The occultation of Jupiter by the moon was a spectacular sight. 木星被月球遮蔽是一个壮观的景象。

例句：The occultation of the sun by the moon is called a solar eclipse. 太阳被月亮遮蔽被称为日食。


近义词：eclipse, obscuration, covering|反义词: revelation, exposure|词性: noun
