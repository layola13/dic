nitrates:/ˈnaɪtreɪts/ | n. |硝酸盐|Salts or esters of nitric acid, containing the anion NO3−.  They are commonly used in fertilizers and explosives. |High levels of nitrates in drinking water can be harmful.  饮用水中高浓度的硝酸盐可能是有害的。| nitrates are essential for plant growth. 硝酸盐对植物生长至关重要。 | 近义词: salt (in some contexts) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider substances that counteract nitrate effects in specific contexts.) | noun

nitrate:/ˈnaɪtreɪt/ | v. |硝化，用硝酸盐处理|To treat or combine with nitric acid or a nitrate.|Farmers nitrate the soil to improve crop yields. 农民用硝酸盐处理土壤以提高作物产量。|The process nitrated the cellulose to form nitrocellulose.  这个过程硝化纤维素形成硝化纤维素。| 近义词: fertilize (in some contexts) | 反义词: denitrify| verb
