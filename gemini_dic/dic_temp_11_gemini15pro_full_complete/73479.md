forerank:/ˈfɔːˌræŋk/| n.|前列，最前列，领先地位|A position of leadership or superiority.  It signifies being at the forefront or in a prominent position. |She quickly rose to the forerank of her profession. 她很快升至行业的领先地位。

The company is in the forerank of technological innovation.  这家公司处于技术创新的最前列。|近义词：vanguard, forefront, lead, prominence|反义词:rear, back, obscurity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'fore-' (meaning 'before' or 'in front') + 'rank' (meaning a row or line, or a position in a hierarchy).  Ultimately from Old English 'fore' and 'ranc'.
