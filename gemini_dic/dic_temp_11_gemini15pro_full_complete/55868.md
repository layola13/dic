divinize:/dɪˈvaɪnaɪz/| vt.|神化，使神圣|To make divine; to treat or regard as divine.  To invest with a sacred character or attribute. |They divinized their ancestors. 他们神化了他们的祖先。|The Romans divinized their emperors. 罗马人神化了他们的皇帝。|近义词：deify, consecrate, exalt, glorify|反义词：desecrate, profane|词性:动词

词源:
From Late Latin *divinizare, from Latin divinus (“divine”), from divus (“god”).
