linguistics:/lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/ | n. | 语言学 | The scientific study of language and its structure, including the study of morphology, syntax, phonetics, and semantics. | Linguistics is a fascinating field of study. 语言学是一个引人入胜的研究领域。|  She studied linguistics in college. 她在大学学习语言学。 | 近义词: philology | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *linguistique*, from Latin *lingua* (tongue, language) + *-isticus* (suffix forming adjectives).
