maturity:/məˈtʃʊrəti/ /məˈtʊrəti/| n. |成熟，成熟期，到期|The state of being fully developed or grown.  The date on which a financial obligation becomes due.|例句：The wine has reached full maturity.  这葡萄酒已经完全成熟了。

例句：The bond reaches maturity next year.  这债券明年到期。|近义词：ripeness, adulthood, completion, due date|反义词:immaturity, youth, incompleteness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *maturité*, from Old French *maturéte*, and directly from Latin *mātūritās* (“ripeness, maturity”), from *mātūrus* (“ripe, mature”).
