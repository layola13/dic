melancholics:/ˌmɛlənˈkɑːlɪks/ | n. | 忧郁的人；患忧郁症的人 | People who suffer from or are prone to melancholy; sad, pensive people. |  Many artists and poets were melancholics, finding inspiration in their sadness. 许多艺术家和诗人都是忧郁的人，在悲伤中寻找灵感。|  The melancholics sat staring out the window, lost in their thoughts.  忧郁的人们坐在窗边凝视着窗外，沉浸在自己的思绪中。 | synonyms: pessimists, depressives | antonyms: optimists, cheerful people | noun
