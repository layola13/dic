non-renewable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈnjuːəbl/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈnuːəbl/ | adj. | 不可再生的 | Not able to be renewed or replaced; (of a natural resource) existing in finite quantity and not capable of being replenished. | Fossil fuels are non-renewable resources. 化石燃料是不可再生资源。 |  The world is increasingly reliant on non-renewable energy sources. 世界越来越依赖不可再生能源。 | 近义词: finite, exhaustible, depletable | 反义词: renewable, sustainable | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
renewable: from re- "again" + new + -able "capable of being"
