museumish: /mjuːˈziːəmɪʃ/
adj.
像博物馆的;博物馆似的;古色古香的;过时的
Resembling or characteristic of a museum, often in a way that suggests being old-fashioned, stuffy, or out of touch with contemporary trends.
The house felt a bit museumish, with its dusty antique furniture.  这房子感觉有点像博物馆，里面摆满了积满灰尘的古董家具。
Her clothes were museumish, like something out of a Victorian novel. 她的衣服古色古香，就像维多利亚时代小说里的东西。
近义词: antiquated, dated, old-fashioned, fusty, stuffy
反义词: modern, contemporary, trendy, fashionable
词性: 形容词

词源: "museum" + "-ish" (suffix indicating resemblance or characteristic of).  由"museum"（博物馆）加上后缀"-ish"（表示类似或具有...的特征）构成。
