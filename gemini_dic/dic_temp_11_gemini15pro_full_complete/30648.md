ceriph:/ˈserɪf/| n.|衬线体,衬线字体|A small stroke or flourish at the ends of the main strokes of a printed or written letter.|The font Times New Roman uses serifs.  泰晤士新罗马字体使用衬线。|近义词:cross-stroke, terminal, flourish|反义词:sans serif|noun
