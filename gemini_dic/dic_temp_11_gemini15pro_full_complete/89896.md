horrific:/həˈrɪfɪk/| adj.|可怕的，令人恐惧的|Causing horror; extremely shocking and disturbing.|The scene of the accident was horrific. 事故现场惨不忍睹。|近义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, frightful, terrifying|反义词：pleasant, delightful, wonderful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *horrificus*, from horrificare "to make bristle with fear," from horrere "to bristle, shudder" + -ficus "-making," related to facere "to make."
