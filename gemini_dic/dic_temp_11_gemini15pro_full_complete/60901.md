elenchize:/ɪˈlɛŋkaɪz/ | v. | 驳斥；反驳；通过问答来检验（某人）| To examine critically; to cross-examine in order to test the soundness of an argument. | Socrates elenchized his opponents to expose their faulty reasoning. 苏格拉底驳斥他的对手以揭露他们错误的推理。

|  Socrates was known for his ability to elenchize even the most sophisticated thinkers. 苏格拉底以其驳斥即使是最老练的思想家的能力而闻名。 | 近义词：refute, rebut, disprove, confute | 反义词：confirm, corroborate, substantiate, prove | 词性：动词

From Ancient Greek ἐλέγχειν (elénkhein, “to examine, refute, convince”).
