Juilliard: /ˈdʒuːliɑːrd/ | n. |茱莉亚音乐学院|A prestigious performing arts conservatory located in New York City.  It offers training in dance, drama, and music.|He studied piano at Juilliard. 他在茱莉亚音乐学院学习钢琴。|Synonym:  conservatory, academy | Antonym: N/A | Noun

Etymology: Named after Augustus D. Juilliard, a wealthy textile merchant who bequeathed a large portion of his estate to establish the school.
