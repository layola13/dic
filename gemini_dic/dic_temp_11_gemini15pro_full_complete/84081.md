half-indignant:/ˌhɑːf ɪnˈdɪɡnənt/
adjective
略带愤怒的；半恼怒的
Feeling or showing mild indignation; slightly offended or annoyed.
She gave him a half-indignant, half-amused look. 她给了他一个半恼怒、半好笑的眼神。
Near synonyms: slightly indignant, mildly annoyed, a little offended, somewhat resentful
Antonyms: pleased, delighted, happy, content
词性: 形容词
