aspy:/ˈæspi/ | n. | 间谍；毒蛇 (尤指欧洲蝰蛇) | A spy; a venomous snake, especially the European viper. | He worked as an aspy for the government. 他为政府做间谍。/  The aspy is a venomous snake found in Europe. 欧洲蝰蛇是一种在欧洲发现的毒蛇。| 近义词: spy, secret agent, viper, adder | 反义词: (For spy) loyalist, patriot (For viper) harmless snake | 词性: noun


aspy: /əˈspaɪ/ | adj. | 像间谍的；警觉的；敏锐的 | Like a spy; watchful; keen. | She had an aspy look in her eyes. 她的眼神很像间谍。/ The cat had an aspy way of watching the birds. 猫以一种警觉的方式观察着鸟儿。| 近义词: watchful, vigilant, observant, keen, sharp | 反义词: oblivious, inattentive, unobservant | 词性: adjective


词源:

From Old French *aspe (modern French aspe), from Latin aspidem, accusative of aspis, from Greek aspis. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₁óphis (“snake”).
