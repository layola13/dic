overprints:/ˈoʊvərˌprɪnts/ /ˈəʊvəˌprɪnts/
n.|叠印；加印；套印|The process of printing one design or color over another, often to correct an error or add information.|The stamp had several overprints to mark its new value. 这枚邮票加盖了几次以标记其新面值。|surcharges, additional printings|underprints|noun
overprints:/ˈoʊvərˌprɪnts/ /ˈəʊvəˌprɪnts/
v.|叠印；加印；套印|To print one design or color over another, often to correct an error or add information.|They overprinted the old stamps with the new country's name. 他们在旧邮票上加印了新国家的名称。|superimpose, print over|erase, remove|verb
