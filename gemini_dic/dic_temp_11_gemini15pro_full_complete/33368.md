chundering:/ˈtʃʌndərɪŋ/
verb
呕吐,强烈呕吐
vomiting heavily and noisily.
例句：He spent the night chundering after eating dodgy street food. 他吃了不干净的街边小吃后，整晚都在呕吐。

例句：I was chundering for hours after that dodgy kebab.  吃完那个可疑的烤肉串后，我吐了好几个小时。

近义词：vomiting, puking, throwing up
反义词：(None - the opposite of vomiting isn't a single word, but a state of not being sick)
词性:v.
