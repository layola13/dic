mingiest:/ˈmiːndʒɪɪst/| adj.|最吝啬的，最小气的|Unwilling to give or spend; mean.|He is the mingiest person I know; he never buys anyone a drink. 他是我认识的最吝啬的人，他从不请任何人喝酒。|Synonyms: stingy, miserly, parsimonious, tightfisted|Antonyms: generous, lavish, extravagant|adjective

Etymology:  From "mingy" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Mingy" is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "mingle" in the obsolete sense of "to be stingy," or from a dialectal word meaning "to stint" or "to save."
