capacitor:/kəˈpæsɪtər/ (US), /kəˈpæsɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |电容器；电容|A device used to store an electric charge, consisting of one or more pairs of conductors separated by an insulator.|This circuit uses a capacitor to smooth out the voltage. 这个电路使用一个电容器来稳定电压。|Synonyms: condenser|Antonyms: N/A|noun

capacitance:/kəˈpæsɪtəns/ (US), /kəˈpæsɪtəns/ (UK)| n. |电容；电容量|The ability of a system to store an electric charge.|The capacitance of this capacitor is 10 microfarads.  这个电容器的电容是10微法。|Synonyms: capacity|Antonyms: N/A|noun
