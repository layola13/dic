egged:/ɛɡd/
|v.|怂恿，煽动，激励；向…投掷鸡蛋|urged or encouraged someone to do something, often something foolish or risky; pelted with eggs|The crowd egged the fighter on.  人群怂恿斗士继续战斗。|近义词：encouraged, incited, goaded, prompted, spurred, provoked|反义词:discouraged, dissuaded|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old Norse *eggja "to incite, urge," related to Old English edge "to sharpen."  The sense of "to pelt with eggs" is a later development.
