over-exaggeration: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 过分夸张；夸大其词 | An act of exaggerating something to an excessive degree.  It involves representing something as larger, better, or worse than it really is, often for dramatic effect. |  His story about catching a twenty-pound fish was a complete over-exaggeration. 他说他钓到一条二十磅重的鱼，纯属夸大其词。| synonyms: hyperbole, exaggeration, embellishment, amplification, overstatement | antonyms: understatement, minimization, downplaying | noun

over-exaggerate: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 过分夸大；夸张 | To exaggerate something to an excessive degree. | Don't over-exaggerate your accomplishments on your resume. 不要在简历上过分夸大你的成就。| synonyms: exaggerate, overstate, embellish, amplify, overemphasize | antonyms: understate, minimize, downplay | verb
