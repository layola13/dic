navew:/ˈneɪvɪuː/ /ˈnævɪuː/
n.
芜菁甘蓝；芜菁
A biennial plant with a large, white root, cultivated in Europe as a vegetable.
The navew has a slightly bitter taste, similar to a turnip. 芜菁甘蓝的味道略苦，类似于萝卜。
Navews were a common food source in medieval Europe.  芜菁甘蓝是中世纪欧洲常见的食物来源。
近义词：turnip, rutabaga
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old French *navet (Modern French navet), diminutive of Latin nāpus (“turnip”).
