brierier:/ˈbraɪəriər/ | adj. | 更加荆棘丛生的，更棘手的 | More thorny, more prickly, more difficult or complicated. | The path became brierier the further we went into the woods.  我们越往森林深处走，小路就越荆棘丛生。|  The political situation is becoming brierier. 政治局势变得更加棘手。| 近义词：thornier, pricklier, more difficult, more complicated | 反义词：smoother, easier, simpler | 词性：形容词

词源:
From Middle English *brerier, comparative of brere (“brier”), from Old English brǣr, from Proto-Germanic *brēzō (“thorn bush”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰars- (“point, bristle”).  The -ier suffix is a comparative adjective ending.
