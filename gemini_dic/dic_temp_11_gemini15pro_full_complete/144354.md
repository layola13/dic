outstay:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪ/
verb
超过(…的)期限；逗留过久
to stay longer than the permitted or desired time
例句：He outstayed his welcome. 他待得太久了，不受欢迎了。

例句：She had outstayed her visa by several months. 她的签证已经过期好几个月了。

近义词：overstay
反义词：leave

词性: v.
