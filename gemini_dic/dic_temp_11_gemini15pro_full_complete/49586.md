demerit:/diːˈmɛrɪt/
n.|缺点，过失，处分|A mark given against someone for misconduct. |He received a demerit for being late. 他因为迟到而受到处分。|fault, flaw, shortcoming|merit, advantage|noun


demerit:/dɪˈmɛrɪt/
v.|记过，处分|To give a demerit to someone. |The teacher demerited him for talking in class. 老师因为他上课讲话而处分他。|penalize, punish, reprimand|reward, praise|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desmerite (modern French démérite), from des- "away from" + merite "merit."
