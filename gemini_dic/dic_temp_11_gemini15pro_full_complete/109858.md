词源:  From Old English *lēah, from Proto-Germanic *lauhaz, meaning "woodland, clearing, meadow."  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *lewh- meaning "to cut, break off." This suggests the original meaning referred to a cleared area within a wood.
