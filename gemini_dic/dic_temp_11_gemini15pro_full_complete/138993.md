nymphical:/nɪmˈfɪkəl/
adjective
仙女的；若虫的
Relating to or characteristic of a nymph, especially in appearance or behavior.  A nymph is a mythological spirit of nature imagined as a beautiful maiden inhabiting rivers, woods, or other locations.  It can also refer to the immature form of some insects.
The painting depicted a nymphical creature with flowing hair and gossamer wings. 这幅画描绘了一个长着飘逸的头发和薄纱翅膀的仙女般的生物。
The dragonfly, in its nymphical stage, lives underwater. 蜻蜓在若虫阶段生活在水下。
Synonyms:nymph-like, fairy-like, ethereal, delicate
Antonyms:coarse, rough, mature, adult
