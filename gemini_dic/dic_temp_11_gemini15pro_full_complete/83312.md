gynocracy: /ˌɡaɪˈnɒkrəsi/ /ˌdʒɪˈnɑːkrəsi/
n.
妇女统治; 女权主义
Government by women; a society ruled by women.
The Amazonian tribe was a gynocracy, with all leadership positions held by women.  亚马逊部落是一个妇女统治的社会，所有领导职位都由妇女担任。
The author explored the concept of gynocracy in her latest novel.  这位作者在她最新的小说中探讨了妇女统治的概念。
Synonyms: matriarchy, female rule
Antonyms: patriarchy, male rule
noun
