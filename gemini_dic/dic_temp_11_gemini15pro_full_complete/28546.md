cardiography:/ˌkɑːrdiˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌkɑːrdiˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |心电描记法；心动描记术|The process of recording the electrical activity of the heart.|Electrocardiography (ECG) is a common type of cardiography. 心电图 (ECG) 是一种常见的心电描记法。|Synonyms: electrocardiography, ECG, EKG|Antonyms: None|Noun

cardiographic:/ˌkɑːrdiəˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌkɑːrdiəˈɡrɑːfɪk/| adj. |心电图的；心脏图解的|Relating to cardiography.|The cardiographic readings showed an irregular heartbeat. 心电图读数显示心跳不规律。|Synonyms: electrocardiographic|Antonyms: None|Adjective

cardiograph:/ˈkɑːrdiəɡrɑːf/ /ˈkɑːrdiəɡræf/| n. |心电图仪；心动描记器|A device used to record the electrical activity of the heart.|The cardiograph recorded the patient's heart rate. 心电图仪记录了病人的心率。|Synonyms: electrocardiograph|Antonyms: None|Noun
