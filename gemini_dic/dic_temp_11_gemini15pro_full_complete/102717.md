jangleress:/ˈdʒæŋɡlə.rɛs/
noun
饶舌妇, 喋喋不休的女人
A woman who talks excessively and often foolishly or discordantly.
She was known as a jangleress in the village, always gossiping about her neighbors.  她在这个村子里被称为饶舌妇，总是说邻居的闲话。
近义词: chatterbox, gossip, blabbermouth
反义词: reticent woman, quiet woman
词性: noun
