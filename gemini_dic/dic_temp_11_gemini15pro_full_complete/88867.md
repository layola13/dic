hoistaway:/ˈhɔɪst əˈweɪ/| phrasal verb | 吊走，升起并移走 | To lift and move something away using a hoist or similar lifting device. |  The crane hoisted the container away from the ship.  起重机将集装箱从船上吊走。

The stage crew hoisted away the backdrop after the performance.  演出结束后，舞台工作人员将背景幕布吊走。| 近义词: lift away, raise away, remove by hoisting | 反义词: lower, drop | 词性: 短语动词
