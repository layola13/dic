blurbing:/ˈblɜːrbɪŋ/
noun
推荐；宣传；吹捧
The act of writing or providing blurbs, short promotional descriptions of a book, film, etc.
He spent the afternoon blurbing his new novel on social media. 他一下午都在社交媒体上宣传他的新小说。
synonyms: promoting, publicizing, hyping
antonyms: criticizing, panning, denouncing
词性: 名词

blurb:/blɜːrb/
noun
简介；推荐语；广告词
A short promotional description of a book, film, etc., written for promotional purposes.
The blurb on the back of the book made it sound very exciting. 书背面的简介使这本书听起来非常激动人心。
synonyms: puff, plug, notice
antonyms: critique, review
词性: 名词
