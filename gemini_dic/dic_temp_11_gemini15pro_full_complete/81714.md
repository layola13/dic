greenkeepers:/ˈɡriːnˌkiːpərz/
n.
场地管理员；草坪管理员
A person who is employed to maintain the grounds of a golf course, bowling green, or sports field.
The greenkeepers worked tirelessly to prepare the course for the championship.  场地管理员们不知疲倦地为锦标赛准备场地。

The greenkeepers were busy mowing the lawns.  草坪管理员们正忙着修剪草坪。

近义词：groundskeeper, groundsman
反义词：（无直接反义词）
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "green" (referring to the grassy areas) + "keeper" (one who maintains or cares for something).
