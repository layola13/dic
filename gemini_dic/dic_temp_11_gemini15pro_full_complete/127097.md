nationalism:/ˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |民族主义；国家主义|A political ideology that involves a strong identification of a group of individuals with a nation and emphasizes the promotion of that nation's interests above all others.  It often includes a sense of national consciousness exalting one nation above all others and placing primary emphasis on promotion of its culture and interests as opposed to those of other nations or supranational groups.|例句：Nationalism can be a unifying force, but it can also lead to conflict. 民族主义可以是一种团结的力量，但也可能导致冲突。

例句：The rise of nationalism in the 20th century led to both world wars. 20世纪民族主义的兴起导致了两次世界大战。|近义词：patriotism, jingoism|反义词:internationalism, globalism|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nationalisme*, from *national* (national) + *-isme* (-ism). Ultimately from Latin *natio* (nation).
