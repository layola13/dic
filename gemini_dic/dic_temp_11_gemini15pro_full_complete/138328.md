词源:
From Middle English *not, from Old French note (“mark, sign, note; musical note; letter, written message”), from Latin nota (“mark, sign, character, letter; brand; stamp”), from notō (“mark, note, observe”), related to nōscō (“learn to know, get knowledge of, become acquainted with”).
