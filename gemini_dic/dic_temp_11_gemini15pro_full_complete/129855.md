non-incidental:/ˌnɒn ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəl/ | adj. | 非偶然的，非附带的，主要的 | Not occurring by chance; essential; inherent. | The report highlighted the non-incidental role of social media in spreading misinformation.  这份报告强调了社交媒体在传播错误信息方面的主要作用。 |  The damage to the car was non-incidental; it appeared to be deliberate. 车辆的损坏不是偶然的；这似乎是故意的。 | 近义词: essential, crucial, key, important, deliberate, intentional | 反义词: incidental, accidental, unimportant, minor, trivial | 词性: adjective

词源:
non-:  prefix meaning "not"
incidental: from Latin *incidentalis, from incidens "falling upon, happening," present participle of incidere "to fall into, fall upon," from in- "into, upon" + cadere "to fall."
