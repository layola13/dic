gelatin:/ˈdʒɛlətɪn/| n.|明胶，凝胶|A translucent, colorless, brittle (when dry) protein obtained by boiling skin, tendons, ligaments, and/or bones with water. It is used as a gelling agent in food, pharmaceuticals, photography, and other industries.|例句：The dessert was made with gelatin.  这道甜点是用明胶做的。

例句：Gelatin is used in many food products. 明胶用于许多食品中。|近义词：jelly, agar, collagen|反义词: None|Noun
