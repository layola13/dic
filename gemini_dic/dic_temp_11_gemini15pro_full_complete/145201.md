overbalance:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləns/| v. (past tense: overbalanced, past participle: overbalanced, present participle: overbalancing, third-person singular: overbalances)|失去平衡；过重；加重…一边；使…过重|To cause to lose balance and fall; to be too heavy on one side.|His desire for power overbalanced his judgment. 他对权力的渴望使他失去了判断力。|近义词： outweigh, preponderate,  overweigh,  prevail over|反义词: balance, stabilize, steady|词性:动词

Etymology: "over-" (Old English ofer) + "balance" (Old French balance, from Late Latin bilanx "having two scales")
