non-spartans:/nɒnˈspɑːtənz/ | n. | 非斯巴达人 |  People who are not from Sparta, an ancient Greek city-state. Refers to those who did not adhere to the Spartan lifestyle, known for its military discipline and austerity. | The non-spartans often viewed the Spartans with a mixture of awe and fear. 非斯巴达人经常对斯巴达人既敬畏又恐惧。|  Non-Spartan traders brought goods from all over Greece to the Spartan markets.  非斯巴达商人把来自希腊各地的货物带到斯巴达市场。 | synonyms: non-Lacedaemonians, outsiders | antonyms: Spartans, Lacedaemonians | noun
