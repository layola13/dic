hoedown:/ˈhoʊˌdaʊn/| n.|乡村舞会，欢快的聚会|A social gathering featuring lively, often traditional music and dancing, typically associated with rural areas.|We went to a hoedown last weekend and had a blast. 我们上周末去了一个乡村舞会，玩得很开心。|The barn was decorated for the annual hoedown. 谷仓为一年一度的乡村舞会装饰一新。|近义词：party, celebration, shindig, jamboree|反义词:funeral, wake|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hoe + down. Originally referred to lively dances performed by slaves during their limited free time, often using hoes as makeshift instruments.  The "down" element likely refers to the forceful downward movements in the dance.
