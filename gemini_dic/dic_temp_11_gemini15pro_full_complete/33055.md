chow-down:/ˈtʃaʊ daʊn/
词性|n.|
中文翻译|大吃一顿；狼吞虎咽|
英文解析|An informal term for a large meal, often eaten quickly and enthusiastically.|
例句|We had a real chow-down at the barbecue yesterday. 昨天我们在烧烤会上大吃了一顿。|

例句|After the game, the team had a massive chow-down at a local restaurant.  比赛结束后，球队在当地一家餐馆大吃了一顿。|

近义词|feast, gorge, pig out|
反义词|(No direct antonym, but consider: snack, nibble)|
