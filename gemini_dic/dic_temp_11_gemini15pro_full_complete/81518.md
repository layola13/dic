gravigrade:/ˈɡrævɪɡreɪd/| adj. n.| 行走地面的，跖行的；跖行动物| An animal that walks on the soles of its feet, like a bear or a human.  A descriptive term for animals that walk with the entire sole of the foot on the ground. |例句：Bears and humans are examples of gravigrade animals. 熊和人类是跖行动物的例子。

例句：The gravigrade posture of the gorilla allows it to carry heavy objects. 大猩猩的跖行姿势使其能够搬运重物。|近义词：plantigrade|反义词: digitigrade, unguligrade|词性: adjective, noun
