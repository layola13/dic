engineerin:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 工程；工程学 | The application of scientific and mathematical principles to practical ends such as the design, manufacture, and operation of efficient and economical structures, machines, processes, and systems. |  He studied engineering at university. 他在大学学习工程学。| synonyms: construction, technology, building | antonyms: destruction, demolition | noun
