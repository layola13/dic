frappe:/fræp/ (US), /frap/ (US informal), /fræpeɪ/ (UK) | n. |冰沙，奶昔；(欧洲)加冰块的咖啡|A blended iced drink, typically made of coffee, milk, and sugar. In Europe, it often refers to iced coffee.| I ordered a caramel frappe. 我点了一杯焦糖冰沙。|  She prefers a chocolate frappe on hot days.  在炎热的日子里，她更喜欢巧克力冰沙。| synonyms: milkshake, smoothie, iced coffee (Europe) | antonyms: hot coffee, hot chocolate | noun

frappe:/fræp/ (US), /frap/ (US informal), /fræpeɪ/ (UK) | v. |(将…)冷冻，冰镇|To chill (a drink) or freeze (food).| The recipe said to frappe the dessert before serving.  食谱上说要在上甜点之前把它冷冻一下。|  They frapped the fruit juice for a refreshing summer treat.  他们把果汁冰镇了一下，做成清爽的夏日饮品。 | synonyms: chill, freeze, ice | antonyms: heat, warm | verb
