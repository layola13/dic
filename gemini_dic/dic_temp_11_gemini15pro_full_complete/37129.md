come-clean:/kʌm ˈkliːn/
| phrasal verb |坦白; 全盘招供;说出真相 |To confess or reveal everything, especially something that has been kept secret. |He finally came clean and admitted to stealing the money. 他最终坦白，承认偷了钱。|
|
|例句：She decided to come clean about her past. 她决定坦白她的过去。|
|
|例句：The politician was pressured to come clean about his campaign finances.  这位政客被迫公开他的竞选资金情况。|
|
|近义词：confess, admit, reveal, disclose |
|
|反义词：conceal, hide, cover up |
|
|词性：动词短语|
