larkishness:/ˈlɑːrkɪʃnɪs/
n.
嬉闹；爱开玩笑；玩皮
The quality of being playful and mischievous.
Her larkishness was infectious, and soon everyone was joining in the fun. 她的嬉闹很有感染力，很快大家都加入了玩乐的行列。
playfulness, mischievousness, lightheartedness, frivolity, jollity
seriousness, solemnity, gravity, sobriety

ETYMOLOGY->
From lark (n.) + -ishness.  "Lark" in the sense of "frolic, spree" originates from the Old Norse word "leikr" meaning "play, game, sport".  The suffix "-ishness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
