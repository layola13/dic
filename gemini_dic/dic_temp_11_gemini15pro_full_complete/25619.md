bumps:/bʌmps/| v.| 碰撞，颠簸行进|To collide with something, to travel in a jerky manner.|The truck bumped against the wall. 卡车撞到了墙上。|Near synonyms: knocks, collides, jolts, bounces|Near antonyms: glides, flows|verb
