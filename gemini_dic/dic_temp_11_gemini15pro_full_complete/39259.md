congregative: /ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡətɪv/ (US) /ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪv/ (UK) | adj. | 集体的，群居的 | Tending to congregate; bringing or coming together into a group or crowd. |例句：Congregative nesting is common among seabirds. 海鸟通常群居筑巢。

例句：The congregative behavior of the bees around the hive was fascinating. 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围的聚集行为令人着迷。


近义词：gregarious, social, collective | 反义词：solitary, individualistic | 词性：形容词

congregate: /ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (US) /ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (UK) | v. | 聚集，集合 | To come together into a crowd or mass; assemble. |例句：People congregated in the square to watch the fireworks. 人们聚集在广场上观看烟花。

例句：Students congregated outside the classroom before the exam. 学生们在考试前聚集在教室外面。


近义词：gather, assemble, amass | 反义词：disperse, scatter | 词性：动词
