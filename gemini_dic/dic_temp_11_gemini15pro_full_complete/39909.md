constitutor:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtə(r)/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtər/| n. | 设立者，组成者，制定者 |  A person who establishes, constitutes, or appoints something, especially a trust or other legal entity.  | The constitutor of the trust ensured that the funds would be used for charitable purposes.  信托的设立者确保资金将用于慈善目的。 |  founder, establisher, creator, author | antonym:  dissolver, abolisher | noun

constitute:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːt/| vt. | 构成，组成，设立，任命 | To make up; be the parts of; to establish or create |  Twelve months constitute a year. 十二个月构成一年。| form, compose, comprise, establish | antonym: dissolve, abolish | verb
