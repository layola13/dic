demesmerize:/diːˈmezməraɪz/
verb
解除催眠；使清醒
To free from a hypnotic spell; to break an enchantment.
The magician demesmerized the audience after his spectacular performance. 魔术师在精彩的表演后解除了观众的催眠。
The therapist worked to demesmerize her patient who was convinced he was a king.  治疗师努力使他的病人清醒过来，这个病人坚信自己是个国王。
Synonyms: disenchant, break the spell, awaken, rouse
Antonyms: mesmerize, hypnotize, captivate, enthrall
word type:verb
