nonstimulations:/ˌnɒnˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 无刺激，缺乏刺激 | Absence of stimuli or a lack of stimulation. | The prolonged nonstimulations of the brain can lead to cognitive decline. 长期缺乏对大脑的刺激会导致认知能力下降。|  The artist suffered from a period of nonstimulations, finding no inspiration for new work. 这位艺术家经历了一段缺乏灵感的时期，找不到创作新作品的灵感。 | 近义词: inactivity, stagnation, dullness | 反义词: stimulation, excitement, activity | 词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not," from Old Latin noenum (ne-oinum) "not one," from ne "not" + oinos "one."
stimulation (noun): from Late Latin stimulationem (nominative stimulatio) "an instigation, urging on," noun of action from past-participle stem of stimulare "to incite, urge on," from stimulus "goad, prick, sting" (see stimulus).
