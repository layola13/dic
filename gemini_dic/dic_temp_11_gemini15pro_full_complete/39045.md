confluxibility:/ˌkɒnflʊksɪˈbɪləti/ /ˌkɑːnflʊksɪˈbɪləti/| n. |汇流性，融合性|The property of being capable of flowing together or merging.|The confluxibility of the two rivers created a fertile delta. 这两条河流的汇流性创造了一个肥沃的三角洲。|近义词：mergeability, combinability, convergence|反义词：divergence, separation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin "confluxus" (flowing together) + "-ibility" (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  词源：源自拉丁语 "confluxus"（流在一起）+ "-ibility"（构成性质抽象名词的后缀）。
