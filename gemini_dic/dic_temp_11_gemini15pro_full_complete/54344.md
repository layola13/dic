dishevelled:/dɪˈʃevəld/ | adj. |  凌乱的，蓬乱的 | Being in a state of disarray; untidy; disordered.  Characterized by disorder or disarrangement, especially of clothing or hair. | Her hair was dishevelled after the windy walk. 她被风吹得头发凌乱。| synonyms: untidy, messy, rumpled, tousled, unkempt, disarranged | antonyms: tidy, neat, orderly, groomed, kempt | word type: adjective

dishevel:/dɪˈʃevəl/ | v. |  使凌乱，使蓬乱 | To cause to become disarranged and untidy. | The strong wind dishevelled her hair.  强风吹乱了她的头发。| synonyms: disarrange, disorder, ruffle, muss | antonyms: arrange, order, tidy, groom | word type: verb
