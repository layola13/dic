brainwash: /ˈbreɪnwɒʃ/ (British) /ˈbreɪnwɑːʃ/ (American)
| n. vt. | 洗脑 | To force someone to accept your ideas or beliefs, usually by repeating them often and not allowing any other ideas or beliefs to be expressed. | The government tried to brainwash the people into believing their propaganda. 政府试图给人民洗脑，让他们相信他们的宣传。|
|  |  |  |  He was brainwashed into joining the cult. 他被洗脑加入了邪教。 | synonyms: indoctrinate, propagandize, condition, program | antonyms: deprogram, enlighten, educate | noun, verb
