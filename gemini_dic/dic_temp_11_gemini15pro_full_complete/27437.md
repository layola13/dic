cammer:/ˈkæmər/| n. |摄像师，尤指使用网络摄像头的人|A person who films or broadcasts using a webcam, especially one who streams live video to the internet. |例句：The cammer captured the entire incident on their live stream.  这位摄像师在他们的直播中捕捉到了整个事件。

例句：Many cammers use platforms like Twitch to broadcast their content. 许多摄像师使用像Twitch这样的平台来播放他们的内容。|近义词：streamer, broadcaster, vlogger|反义词: viewer, audience |Noun
