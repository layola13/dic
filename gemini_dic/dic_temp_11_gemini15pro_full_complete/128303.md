neuroskeleton: /ˈnjʊərəʊˈskɛlɪtən/ | n. | 神经骨架 | A network of microtubules, neurofilaments, and microfilaments that provide structural support to neurons and help transport materials within the cell. |  The neuroskeleton plays a crucial role in maintaining the shape and function of nerve cells. 神经骨架在维持神经细胞的形状和功能方面起着至关重要的作用。|  The integrity of the neuroskeleton is essential for axonal transport.  神经骨架的完整性对于轴突运输至关重要。 | cytoskeleton, neuronal scaffolding |  N/A | noun
