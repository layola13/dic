blastocoele:/ˈblæstəˌsiːl/| n.|囊胚腔|The fluid-filled cavity within the blastula.  囊胚中的充满液体的腔。|The blastocoele provides space for cell migration during gastrulation. 囊胚腔为原肠胚形成期间的细胞迁移提供空间。|The blastocoele allows the blastula to increase in size without increasing the amount of cytoplasm.  囊胚腔允许囊胚在不增加细胞质量的情况下增大尺寸。|cavity, blastocyst cavity| |noun
