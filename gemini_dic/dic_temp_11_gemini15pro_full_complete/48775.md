deflating:/dɪˈfleɪtɪŋ/| adj.|放气的；泄气的；使人泄气的|reducing the amount of air or gas in something; making someone feel less confident or important|The deflating news of the job losses cast a shadow over the town.  失业的消息令人沮丧，给整个城镇蒙上了一层阴影。|Synonyms: discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting|Antonyms: encouraging, heartening, uplifting|Adjective

deflate:/dɪˈfleɪt/| v.|放气；泄气；使人泄气|to let air or gas out of something; to make someone feel less confident or important|He slowly deflated the balloon. 他慢慢地把气球放了气。  Her harsh criticism deflated him. 她严厉的批评使他泄气。|Synonyms: collapse, flatten, shrink; discourage, dishearten|Antonyms: inflate, expand; encourage, hearten|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deflāre, from de- (“down, away”) + flāre (“to blow”).
