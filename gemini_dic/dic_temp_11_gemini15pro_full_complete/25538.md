bullterrier:/ˈbʊlˌtɛriər/| n.|斗牛犬|A breed of dog, originally developed in England in the 19th century, known for its muscular build, short coat, and egg-shaped head.|例句：The bullterrier is a powerful and loyal dog. 斗牛犬是一种强壮且忠诚的狗。

例句：My neighbor has a friendly bullterrier. 我的邻居有一只友好的斗牛犬。|近义词：pit bull (informal), Staffordshire bull terrier|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bull + terrier.  "Bull" refers to the breed's use in bull-baiting (a now-illegal blood sport), and "terrier" refers to its terrier ancestry.
