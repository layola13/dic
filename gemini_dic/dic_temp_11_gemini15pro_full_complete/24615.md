broke:/broʊk/| adjective|破产的，没钱的；坏掉的|Having no money; without financial resources. Also, damaged or in a non-working condition.| I'm broke until payday. 我直到发薪日都没钱了。|The washing machine is broke. 洗衣机坏了。| synonyms: penniless, bankrupt, insolvent, broken, damaged | antonyms: wealthy, rich, affluent, functioning, working | adjective

broke:/broʊk/| verb|打破；违反；终止；公布；驯服；破产|Past tense of "break."  Can refer to physically breaking something, violating a rule, ending a relationship, revealing information, taming an animal, or becoming bankrupt. |He broke his leg skiing. 他滑雪摔断了腿。|She broke the law. 她违反了法律。| synonyms: shattered, fractured, violated, breached, ended, revealed, tamed, bankrupt | antonyms: repaired, mended, obeyed, followed, began, concealed, wild, wealthy | verb
