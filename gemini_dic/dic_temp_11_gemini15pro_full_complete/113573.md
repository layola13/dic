macaws:/məˈkɔːz/ | n. | 金刚鹦鹉 | Large, long-tailed parrots with brightly colored plumage, native to tropical America. | The macaws flew through the rainforest, their vibrant feathers a flash of color against the green. 金刚鹦鹉飞过雨林，它们鲜艳的羽毛在绿色背景下像一道闪光。 |  The macaws squawked loudly, demanding attention. 金刚鹦鹉大声鸣叫，要求关注。 | synonyms: parrots, parakeets | antonyms: None | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *macau*, from Tupi *macavu*.
