fly-bys:/ˈflaɪ baɪz/ (US), /ˈflaɪ baɪz/ (UK)| n.|飞越，掠过|A flight past a point, especially close to a planet or moon to record scientific data.  A brief visit.|例句：The spacecraft made a fly-by of Jupiter.  这艘宇宙飞船飞越了木星。

例句：The senator made a quick fly-by of his home state. 参议员短暂地访问了他的家乡州。|近义词：flypast, flyover|反义词: landing, touchdown|词性:noun
