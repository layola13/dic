immigrant:/ˈɪmɪɡrənt/| n. adj.|移民；移民的|A person who comes to live permanently in a foreign country.  Relating to or characteristic of immigrants.|例句：The new immigrants quickly adapted to the local customs.  这些新移民很快适应了当地的风俗习惯。|例句：The immigrant population has contributed significantly to the country's economy. 移民人口对该国的经济做出了重大贡献。|近义词：newcomer, settler, migrant|反义词：emigrant, native|词性:noun, adjective|词源:Late Middle English: from Latin immigrant- ‘immigrated’, from the verb immigrare, from in- ‘into’ + migrare ‘migrate’.
