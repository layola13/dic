haruspication:/ˌhærəspɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 占卜; 预测; (尤指)观察动物内脏以预卜吉凶 | The practice of examining the entrails of sacrificed animals, especially the liver, in order to divine the future. |  The Romans relied on haruspication to guide their decisions in times of war. 罗马人在战争时期依靠占卜来指导他们的决策。

The haruspication revealed a favorable outcome for the upcoming battle. 占卜显示即将到来的战斗结果有利。 | 近义词：divination, augury, prophecy | 反义词:  None (lack of a direct antonym for this specialized term) | 词性: Noun
