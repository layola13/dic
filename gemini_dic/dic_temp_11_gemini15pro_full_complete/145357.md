overburden:/ˌəʊvərˈbɜːdən/| v. n.|使负担过重，过重的负担|To overload with burdens or responsibilities; an excessive load or burden.|The company is overburdened with debt. 这家公司负债累累。|Synonyms: overload, overtax, overwhelm|Antonyms: underwhelm, lighten, relieve|verb, noun


overburdens:/ˌəʊvərˈbɜːdənz/| v.|使负担过重|Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "overburden."  It indicates that something overloads or puts an excessive burden on someone or something.|He overburdens himself with work. 他工作负担过重。|Synonyms: overloads, overtaxes, overwhelms|Antonyms: lightens, relieves|verb
