kingstone:/ˈkɪŋstən/ | n. | 金士顿; Kingston（牙买加首都）; 킹스턴 (한국어) |Kingston is the capital and largest city of Jamaica. 金士顿是牙买加的首都和最大城市。| Kingston upon Thames is a town in southwest London.  泰晤士河畔金斯顿是伦敦西南部的一个城镇。 | synonyms: capital (when referring to Kingston, Jamaica) | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:  "king's town".  From Old English *cyninges + tūn.  The name is common in England, and Kingston, Jamaica, was named after Kingston upon Thames in England.
