bewitching:/bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋ/
adjective
迷人的，令人着迷的
charming, enchanting; captivating someone by magic
Her bewitching smile made everyone fall in love with her. 她迷人的微笑让每个人都爱上了她。
The bewitching beauty of the landscape captivated us. 風景令人著迷的美麗深深地吸引了我們。
synonyms: captivating, enchanting, alluring, charming, fascinating, mesmerizing
antonyms: repulsive, repellent, unappealing, unpleasant, unattractive
词性: 形容词
