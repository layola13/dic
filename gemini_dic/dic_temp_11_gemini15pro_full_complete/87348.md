heroically:/hɪˈroʊɪkli/ /hɪˈrəʊɪkli/| adv.|英勇地，英雄般地|In a manner resembling a hero; bravely, courageously.  Showing great courage or determination in the face of danger or difficulty.|He heroically rescued the child from the burning building. 他英勇地从燃烧的建筑物中救出了孩子。|Near Synonyms: bravely, courageously, valiantly, gallantly, daringly|Near Antonyms: cowardly, timidly, fearfully|Adverb
