buccinal:/bʌˈsɪnəl/
adjective
喇叭的；似喇叭的；号角的
Relating to or resembling a trumpet or horn.
The buccinal notes of the foghorn echoed across the harbor.  喇叭状的雾角声在港口回荡。
synonyms:trumpet-like, horn-like, clarion
antonyms:soft, muted
词性:adjective

buccinal:/bʌˈsɪnəl/
noun
喇叭；号角
A trumpet or horn.
The ancient buccinal was made of ivory. 古老的喇叭是用象牙做的。
synonyms:trumpet, horn, bugle
antonyms:N/A
词性:noun
