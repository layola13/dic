misbinding:/ˌmɪsˈbaɪndɪŋ/
n.
错误装订，装订错误
an act or instance of binding something incorrectly, or the state of being bound incorrectly.
The misbinding of the book resulted in several pages being out of order. 这本书装订错误，导致几页乱了顺序。

近义词: misprint, error, mistake, fault
反义词: correct binding
