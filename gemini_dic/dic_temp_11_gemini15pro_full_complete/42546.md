countermoved:/ˌkaʊntərˈmuːvd/ | v. | 反制，应对 | To make a move in opposition to another move. |  The company countermoved by launching their own competing product.  这家公司通过推出自己的竞争产品来应对。 | Synonyms:  riposted, retaliated, responded | Antonyms:  acquiesced, yielded, surrendered | verb

countermove: /ˈkaʊntərˌmuːv/ | n. | 反制行动，应对措施 | An action taken in opposition to another action. | His countermove was unexpected and effective. 他的反制行动出人意料且有效。 | Synonyms:  retaliation, response, riposte | Antonyms:  submission, surrender, concession | noun
