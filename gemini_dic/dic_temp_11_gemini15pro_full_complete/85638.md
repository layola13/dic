haunt:/hɔːnt/ (British), /hɑːnt/ (American)| v. n.|萦绕；困扰；常去；鬼魂出没；(鬼魂)常出没于|To regularly visit a place or person, especially as a ghost or spirit; to be persistently and disturbingly present in one's mind.|The ghost of his father haunted him. 他父亲的鬼魂萦绕着他。|近义词：plague, trouble, frequent, visit, inhabit|反义词：avoid, desert|词性:动词,名词

haunt:/hɔːnt/ (British), /hɑːnt/ (American)| n.|常去的地方；鬼魂出没的地方|A place frequently visited.|The pub was a favourite haunt of artists and writers. 这家酒吧是艺术家和作家们常去的地方。|近义词：resort, hangout, abode|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
