interpersonality:/ˌɪntərˌpɜːrsəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 人际关系，人际交往 |  The complex interplay of factors that influence interactions between people. It encompasses communication styles, personality traits, and social skills. |  Her strong interpersonality allowed her to easily navigate social situations. 她很强的人际交往能力使她能够轻松应对各种社交场合。 | synonyms: interpersonal skills, social skills, social intelligence, interpersonal relations | antonyms: social awkwardness, social ineptitude | noun
