disclaimer:/dɪˈskleɪmər/ | n. | 免责声明；放弃权利声明 | A statement that denies something, especially responsibility. | The website included a disclaimer about the accuracy of the information provided.  该网站包含一个关于所提供信息准确性的免责声明。 | synonyms: denial, repudiation, rejection | antonyms:  acceptance, admission, affirmation | noun

disclaimer:/dɪˈskleɪmər/ | v. | 放弃权利；拒绝承认 | To deny any responsibility or knowledge of something.  | The company disclaimed any responsibility for the faulty product. 公司拒绝承认对有缺陷的产品负有任何责任。 | synonyms: renounce, reject, disavow | antonyms: acknowledge, admit, accept | verb
