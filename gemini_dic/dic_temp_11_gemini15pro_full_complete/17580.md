beauteous:/ˈbjuːtiəs/| adj.|美丽的，秀丽的|Very beautiful; pleasing to the eye.  Derived from "beauty" with the suffix "-ous" indicating "full of" or "possessing the qualities of". From Middle English *beautevous*, from Old French *beauteus*.|例句：The beauteous sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues. 秀丽的日落将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。

例句：She possessed a beauteous smile that could light up a room. 她拥有一个可以照亮整个房间的美丽笑容。

|近义词：beautiful, gorgeous, lovely, pretty, attractive, stunning|反义词：ugly, hideous, unattractive, plain|词性:adjective
