barrooms: /ˈbɑːruːmz/
n.
酒吧间；酒馆
Saloons or bars; places where alcoholic beverages are served.
The old cowboy stumbled out of one of the many barrooms in town.  那个老牛仔从镇上众多酒吧间中的一个踉跄而出。

The barrooms were filled with laughter and the clinking of glasses. 酒吧间里充满了笑声和酒杯碰撞的声音。

近义词: saloons, pubs, taverns, bars
反义词: churches, schools, libraries

词性: noun (plural)

词源:  From "bar" (referring to the counter where drinks are served) + "room".  "Bar" ultimately comes from the Old French "barre" meaning "bar, barrier".
