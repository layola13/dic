commender:/kəˈmɛndər/| n.|推荐人；指挥官；司令官|A person who recommends something or someone, or a person in a position of authority, especially in the military.|例句：The commander ordered his troops to advance. 指挥官命令部队前进。

例句：My professor was my recommender for the scholarship. 我的教授是我的奖学金推荐人。|近义词：recommender, advocate, supporter, leader, chief, officer|反义词：opponent, enemy, detractor|词性:noun
