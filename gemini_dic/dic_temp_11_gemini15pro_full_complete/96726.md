inefficiency:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. |效率低下，低效，无效率 |The state of not achieving maximum productivity; wasting resources such as time, energy, or money. |The inefficiency of the old system was costing the company a fortune. 旧系统的低效使公司损失惨重。|Synonyms: incompetence, ineffectiveness, wastefulness, unproductiveness |Antonyms: efficiency, effectiveness, productivity|noun


inefficient:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃənt/ | adj. |效率低的，低效的，无效率的 |Not achieving maximum productivity; wasting or failing to make the best use of time or resources. |The inefficient use of energy is a major environmental concern.  能源的低效使用是一个主要的环境问题。|Synonyms: incompetent, ineffective, wasteful, unproductive |Antonyms: efficient, effective, productive|adjective
