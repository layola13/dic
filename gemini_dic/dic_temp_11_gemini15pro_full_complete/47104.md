daughterless:/ˈdɔːtərləs/
adjective
无女儿的；(果蝇)无雌性后代的
Lacking daughters; (of Drosophila) producing no female offspring.
The daughterless gene in Drosophila is crucial for female development. 果蝇中的无女儿基因对雌性发育至关重要。
The queen was daughterless and therefore had no heir to the throne. 女王没有女儿，因此没有王位继承人。
近义词:childless, sonless (in the specific context of lacking female children)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) having daughters,  maternal
词性: adjective
