arschin:/ˈɑːrʃɪn/| n.|阿尔申（俄国长度单位，等于0.71米）|A Russian unit of linear measure equal to 28 inches (0.71 meter).|The fabric was sold by the arschin.  这种布是按阿尔申出售的。|The carpet measured three arschins in length. 这块地毯有三阿尔申长。|近义词：ell, cubit|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian аршин (aršin), from Turkic *aršın, ultimately from Persian ارش (arš, “cubit”).
