over-tinted:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪntɪd/
adjective
过度着色的
having too much tint; excessively tinted.  The color is too dark or intense.
The photograph was over-tinted, making everyone look like they had a sunburn.  这张照片过度着色，让每个人看起来都像晒伤了一样。
The windows were over-tinted, making it difficult to see out.  窗户颜色太深，很难看清外面。
overcolored, excessively tinted
under-tinted, lightly tinted
adjective
