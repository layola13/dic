engirdle:/ɪnˈɡɜːrdl/
verb
环绕，包围
to surround; to encircle
例句：Mountains engirdle the valley. 群山环绕着山谷。

例句：The fog engirdled the city.  大雾笼罩着城市。

近义词：encircle, surround, encompass
反义词：expose, uncover
词性：verb
