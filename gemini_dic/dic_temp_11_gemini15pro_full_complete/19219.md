bezel:/ˈbɛzl/| n.|嵌边，边框，斜面|A bezel is a sloping edge on a cutting tool or other object.|The watch has a diamond bezel. 这块手表有钻石边框。|synonyms: rim, frame, edge, border|antonyms: center, interior|noun


bezel:/ˈbɛzl/| v.|给…镶边|To bezel something means to fit it with a bezel.|He bezeled the mirror with silver. 他给镜子镶了银边。|synonyms: frame, enclose, surround|antonyms: uncover, expose|verb
