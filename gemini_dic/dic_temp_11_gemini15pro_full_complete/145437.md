It implies a price that is lower than what is considered fair or reasonable, often suggesting poor quality or an unsustainable market situation.| Example: The overcheapness of the clothes made me question their quality. 便宜的衣服让我怀疑它们的质量。|Synonyms: cheapness, inexpensiveness, affordability |Antonyms: expensiveness, dearness, costliness| Noun
