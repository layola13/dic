hornier:/ˈhɔːrniər/ (US), /ˈhɔːnɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更角质的，更饥渴的，更性兴奋的|More horny; exhibiting a greater degree of sexual excitement or desire.|例句：He felt hornier after watching that movie. 看完那部电影后，他感到更加性兴奋了。

例句：She's been acting hornier lately. 她最近表现得更加饥渴了。|近义词：randier, lustful, lecherous, aroused|反义词：frigid, uninterested, indifferent|词性:形容词

词源: From "horny" + "-er" (comparative suffix).  "Horny" itself derives from "horn," referring to the hardened, horn-like state of the penis when erect.  Over time, the meaning broadened to encompass general sexual arousal.
