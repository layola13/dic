overdiversely:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsli/ | adv. | 过度多样化地 | In a way that exhibits excessive diversity; too diversely. | The ecosystem was overdiversely populated, making it unstable.  这个生态系统的物种过度多样化，使其变得不稳定。 | Example 2: The museum's collection was criticized for being overdiversely arranged, making it difficult to follow a coherent narrative.  这家博物馆的藏品因排列过于多样化而受到批评，难以遵循连贯的叙事。 | 近义词: excessively diversely | 反义词: homogeneously, uniformly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "diverse" (adjective meaning "showing a great deal of variety") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
