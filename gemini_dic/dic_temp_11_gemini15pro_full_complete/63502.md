enucleation:/ɪˌnjuːkliˈeɪʃən/ | n. |去核;摘除| The surgical removal of something, especially a tumor or the eyeball, from its socket or surrounding tissues. |The enucleation of the tumor was successful. 肿瘤的摘除手术很成功。|  The vet performed an enucleation of the cat's eye. 兽医摘除了猫的眼球。|  excision, removal, extraction | implantation, insertion | noun
