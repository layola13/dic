jiving:/ˈdʒaɪvɪŋ/
verb|摇摆舞，欺骗，开玩笑| Lively, energetic dancing or behaving deceptively or teasingly. |例句：They were jiving to the music all night. 他们整晚都在随着音乐摇摆。|近义词：dancing, swinging, deceiving, teasing|反义词：being truthful, being serious
verb|(使)充满活力，(使)活跃|To be full of life and energy; to make something lively and energetic.|例句：The party was really jiving after the band started playing. 乐队开始演奏后，派对的气氛真的活跃起来了。|近义词：energizing, enlivening|反义词：dulling, deadening
adjective|充满活力的，活跃的|Lively, energetic, exciting.|例句：The music was jiving and everyone was having a good time. 音乐充满活力，每个人都玩得很开心。|近义词：vibrant, dynamic, exciting|反义词：dull, boring, lifeless
