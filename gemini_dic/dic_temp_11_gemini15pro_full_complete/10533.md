appositions:/ˌæpəˈzɪʃənz/ | n. | 同位语 | A grammatical construction in which two noun phrases are placed side by side, with one identifying or explaining the other. | My best friend, Sarah, is a doctor.  我的最好的朋友，莎拉，是个医生。 | synonyms: appositive,  equivalent | antonyms: N/A | noun


apposition:/ˌæpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 同位 | The act of placing two elements side by side, especially for comparison or contrast. | The apposition of the two images revealed their subtle differences.  这两幅图像的并置揭示了它们微妙的差异。 | synonyms: juxtaposition, collocation | antonyms: separation, division | noun
