citronellas:/ˌsɪtrəˈnɛləz/| n.|香茅油，香茅精油|An essential oil obtained from citronella grass, used as an insect repellent.|例句：The citronella candles kept the mosquitoes away. 香茅蜡烛驱赶了蚊子。

例句：She used citronella oil on her skin to prevent mosquito bites.  她在皮肤上涂抹香茅油以防止蚊虫叮咬。


近义词：lemongrass oil, insect repellent|反义词:attractant|词性:noun
