cellaress:/ˈselərəs/| n.| 女侍酒师;酒窖女主人|A woman in charge of a cellar, especially a wine cellar. |She hired a trained cellaress to manage her extensive wine collection. 她聘请了一位训练有素的女侍酒师来管理她大量的葡萄酒收藏。|近义词：sommelier (通常指专业的侍酒师，不限性别) wine steward|反义词: N/A| noun

cellaress:/ˈselərəs/| n.| 酒窖女主人; (旧时) 女管家|A woman in charge of a household's provisions, stored in the cellar. (archaic usage)|In the old manor, the cellaress held the keys to the pantry and the stores. 在古老的庄园里，女管家掌管着食品储藏室和仓库的钥匙。|近义词：housekeeper, steward|反义词: N/A| noun
