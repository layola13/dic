inappetence:/ˌɪnˈæpɪtəns/ | n. |食欲不振；厌食；缺乏欲望|Lack of appetite or desire.|例句：The patient displayed inappetence following the surgery. 病人在手术后表现出食欲不振。

例句：His inappetence for exercise worried his doctor. 他对运动缺乏兴趣，这让他的医生很担心。|近义词：anorexia, loss of appetite, aversion to food |反义词: appetite, hunger, desire |词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *inapetentia, from in- "not" + Latin appetentia "desire, appetite," from appetere "to strive after."
