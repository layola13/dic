evening: /ˈiːvnɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈiːvnɪŋ/ (US)| n. |晚上，傍晚|the period of time at the end of the day, usually from about 6 p.m. to bedtime|  We went for a walk in the evening. 我们晚上去散步了。|synonyms:  night, nightfall, dusk, twilight| antonyms: morning, dawn, daybreak| noun

evening: /ˈiːvnɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈiːvnɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |晚上的，傍晚的|of or occurring in the evening| We attended an evening performance. 我们参加了一场晚上的演出。|synonyms:  nightly, nocturnal| antonyms: morning, daytime| adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æfnung, from æfen ("evening") + -ung. Cognate with German Abend.
