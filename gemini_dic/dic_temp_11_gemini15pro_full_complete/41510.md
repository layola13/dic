corollas:/kəˈrɒləz/ | n. |花冠|The petals of a flower, forming a whorl within the calyx and enclosing the reproductive organs. | The bee buzzed around the vibrant corollas, collecting pollen. 蜜蜂在鲜艳的花冠周围嗡嗡作响，收集花粉。| synonyms: petals, perianth | antonyms: calyx, sepals | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *corolla, diminutive of corona "crown, garland".
