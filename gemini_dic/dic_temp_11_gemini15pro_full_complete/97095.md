infestations:/ˌɪnfeˈsteɪʃənz/| n. |侵扰，感染，蔓延|The presence of large numbers of pests or parasites in a place or on a plant or animal.| 例句：The house was condemned because of rat infestations. 这房子因为鼠患被宣布不适宜居住。

例句：The crop was destroyed by infestations of insects.  庄稼被虫害毁掉了。


近义词：plagues, swarms, outbreaks|反义词: absences, eradication|词性: noun
