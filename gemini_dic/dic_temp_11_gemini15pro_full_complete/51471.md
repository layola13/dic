detoured:/diːˈtʊərd/| adj. |绕道的，迂回的|Forced to take a different route than usual.| Due to the accident, we were detoured through back roads. 由于事故，我们被迫绕道而行，走了些偏僻小路。|Synonyms: rerouted, diverted|Antonyms: direct, straight|Adjective

detour:/ˈdiːtʊər/| n. |绕道，迂回路线|A longer route that is used because the usual route is closed or blocked.|The detour added an extra hour to our journey. 绕道让我们多花了一个小时。|Synonyms: bypass, roundabout|Antonyms: direct route, straight path|Noun

detour:/diːˈtʊər/| v. |绕道，使绕道而行|To take a longer route than usual because the usual route is closed or blocked.|The police detoured traffic around the accident scene. 警察让车辆绕过事故现场。|Synonyms: reroute, divert|Antonyms: direct, guide|Verb
