foresighted:/ˈfɔːrˌsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有远见的；深谋远虑的 | Having or showing foresight; able to anticipate future needs or events. |  She made a foresighted decision to invest in renewable energy. 她做了一个有远见的决定，投资可再生能源。

| Example: His foresighted planning saved the company from bankruptcy. 他深谋远虑的计划使公司免于破产。 | Synonym:  prudent, wise, judicious, prescient | Antonym: shortsighted, myopic, improvident | word type: adjective
