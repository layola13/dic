corrigibleness:/kəˌrɪdʒəˈbɪlnəs/
n.
可改正性；可纠正性
The quality of being corrigible; capable of being corrected, amended, or reformed.
Her corrigibleness made her a quick learner. 她的可改正性使她学得很快。
Synonyms: amendability, reformability, rectifiability
Antonyms: incorrigibility, intractability, obstinacy
noun
