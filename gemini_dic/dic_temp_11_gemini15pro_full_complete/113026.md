lumiere:/luːˈmjɛər/ | n. | 光；光明；启发；（尤指电影的）灯光 | Light, enlightenment, especially the lighting used in films. | The lumiere of the stage made the actors look ethereal. 舞台的光芒使演员们看起来飘渺若仙。 | 近义词: light, illumination, brilliance, radiance | 反义词: darkness, obscurity | 词性: 名词


词源:  French, from Old French, from Latin *luminaria, from luminare to illuminate, from lumen light.
