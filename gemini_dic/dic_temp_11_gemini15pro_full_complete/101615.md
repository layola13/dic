irreconcilable: /ɪˌrɛkənˈsaɪləbl/ /ɪˌrɛkənˈsaɪləbəl/ | adj. | 不可调和的，无法和解的 | Not able to be brought into agreement or harmony; incompatible. | The two sides held irreconcilable differences on the issue of land ownership.  双方在土地所有权问题上存在不可调和的分歧。|

例句：
Their differences were irreconcilable, leading to the end of their partnership. 他们的分歧无法调和，导致了他们合作关系的结束。

Her political views are irreconcilable with his. 她的政治观点与他的无法相容。

近义词：incompatible, conflicting, antagonistic, uncompromisable

反义词：compatible, reconcilable, harmonious, agreeable

词性：adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French irreconciliable, from in- ‘not’ + a derivative of reconciler (see reconcile).
