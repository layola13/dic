fictionalities: /ˌfɪkʃəˈnælɪtiz/
n.
虚构性；虚构的事物；虚构的成分
The quality or state of being fictional; things that are fictional or invented elements of a story.
The author blurred the lines between fact and fictionalities in his memoir.  作者在他的回忆录中模糊了事实和虚构的界限。
His work explores the fictionalities of memory and identity. 他的作品探讨了记忆和身份的虚构性。
近义词:fabrications, inventions, fictions
反义词:realities, truths, facts
词性:名词


词源:
From "fictionality" + "-ies" (plural suffix).  "Fictionality" comes from "fictional" + "-ity." "Fictional" derives from the Latin "fictio," meaning "a feigning, fashioning, fiction," from "fingere," meaning "to shape, form, invent."
