countermandable:/ˌkaʊntərˈmɑːndəbl/ | adj. | 可撤销的，可取消的 |  Able to be countermanded; capable of being revoked or reversed. | The general's orders were countermandable by higher authority. 将军的命令可以被更高层的权力撤销。| synonyms: revocable, reversible, rescindable, cancellable | antonyms: irreversible, irrevocable, uncancellable | word type: adjective

countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmɑːnd/ | v. n. | 撤销，取消；相反的命令 | To revoke or cancel (a previous command); an order that revokes a previous one. |  Headquarters countermanded the attack. 总部取消了进攻。| synonyms: revoke, rescind, cancel, reverse, annul | antonyms: order, command, authorize, approve | word type: verb, noun
