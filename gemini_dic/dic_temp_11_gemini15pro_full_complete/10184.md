adjective
疑惑的；怀疑的；不确定的；悖论的
expressing or of the nature of doubt or uncertainty, especially as regards philosophical or religious matters.
例句：The aporetical nature of the philosopher's questions left the students pondering their own existence. 哲学家提出的疑惑性质的问题让学生们思考起了自己的存在。

近义词：doubtful, skeptical, uncertain, questionable, ambiguous

词性: adjective

词源: From Ancient Greek ἀπορητικός (aporētikós, “causing difficulty, perplexing”), from ἀπορέω (aporéō, “to be at a loss”).
