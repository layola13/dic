overengineering:/ˌōvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/ | n. | 过度工程，过度设计 | The act of designing and building something to be more complex or elaborate than necessary.  It often involves adding features or functionalities that are not required by the user or client, resulting in increased cost, complexity, and development time. | The software was so overengineered that it was slow and buggy.  这个软件过度设计了，导致它运行缓慢且漏洞百出。 | overdesign, overbuild, gold-plating | underengineering, simplification | noun
