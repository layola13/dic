groundly:/ˈɡraʊndɪdli/ | adv. | 坚实地，有根据地，彻底地 |  in a well-founded or thorough manner; with firm or secure basis | The theory is not groundedly established.  这个理论没有坚实的基础。| 近义词: thoroughly, solidly, firmly, substantially, completely | 反义词: superficially, shallowly, insecurely, precariously | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "ground" (meaning foundation or basis) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Ground" comes from Old English "grund," which is related to other Germanic words meaning "bottom" or "foundation." The suffix "-ly" is used to form adverbs from adjectives.
