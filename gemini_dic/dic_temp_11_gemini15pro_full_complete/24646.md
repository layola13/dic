brokest:/ˈbroʊkɪst/| adj.|破产的，身无分文的|Superlative form of "broke," meaning having absolutely no money.  It emphasizes a state of extreme poverty or lack of funds. |例句：He was the brokest man in town. 他是镇上最穷的人。

例句：After gambling all night, she was completely brokest.  赌了一整夜后，她彻底破产了。|近义词：penniless, impoverished, destitute|反义词:rich, wealthy, affluent|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "break," originally meaning "to fracture." The financial meaning developed from the idea of being "broken" financially.
