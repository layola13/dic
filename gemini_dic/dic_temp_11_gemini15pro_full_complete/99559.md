interhybridize:/ˌɪntərˈhaɪbrɪdaɪz/
verb
杂交; 跨种杂交
to hybridize between species or varieties
Scientists have successfully interhybridized different species of orchids.  科学家们成功地将不同种类的兰花进行了杂交。
The roses were interhybridized to create a new variety.  这些玫瑰被杂交以创造一个新品种。
