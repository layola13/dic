muffin-tops:/ˈmʌfɪn tɒps/ (US) /ˈmʌfɪn tɒps/ (UK)| n. | 腰间赘肉，肚腩肉|The roll of fat that spills over the top of tight-fitting low-rise jeans or pants, resembling the top of a muffin.  It refers to the flesh that bulges out over the waistband of trousers, especially low-slung ones.|例句：She tried to hide her muffin-top by wearing a longer shirt. 她试图穿一件更长的衬衫来遮住她的腰间赘肉。

例句：Low-rise jeans can sometimes create an unflattering muffin-top. 低腰牛仔裤有时会产生难看的肚腩肉。|近义词：spare tire, love handles, belly fat|反义词:flat stomach, toned abs|词性:noun
