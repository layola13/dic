cnidaria:/naɪˈdɛəriə/ | n. | 刺胞动物门 | A phylum of aquatic invertebrate animals that includes jellyfish, corals, and sea anemones, characterized by stinging cells called cnidocytes. | Jellyfish and corals are members of the phylum Cnidaria. 水母和珊瑚是刺胞动物门的成员。| Synonyms: Coelenterata (older term) | Antonyms: None | Noun
