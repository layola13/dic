mamba:/ˈmɑːmbə/ (US), /ˈmæmbə/ (UK)| n.|曼巴蛇|Any of several highly venomous African elapid snakes of the genus *Dendroaspis*, typically slender-bodied and arboreal in habit.|The black mamba is one of the most dangerous snakes in the world. 黑曼巴蛇是世界上最危险的蛇之一。|Synonyms:  venomous snake, serpent|Antonyms: harmless snake|noun

mamba:/ˈmɑːmbə/ (US), /ˈmæmbə/ (UK)| n.|曼巴舞步|A fast-paced, energetic dance move popularized by Kobe Bryant.|He celebrated his victory with his signature mamba dance move. 他以标志性的曼巴舞步庆祝胜利。|Synonyms: dance move, celebration|Antonyms: N/A|noun
