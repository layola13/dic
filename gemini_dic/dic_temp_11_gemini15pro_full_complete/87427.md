herzegovinian: /ˌhɜːrtsəɡəˈvɪniən/ /ˌhɜːrzəɡoʊˈvɪniən/ | adj. n.|黑塞哥维那的；黑塞哥维那人|Relating to or originating from Herzegovina, a region in Bosnia and Herzegovina.  A native or inhabitant of Herzegovina.|例句：She has a Herzegovinian grandmother. 她有一个黑塞哥维那的祖母。|例句：The Herzegovinian landscape is stunning. 黑塞哥维那的风景令人叹为观止。|近义词：Bosnian and Herzegovinian|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology:
From Herzegovina + -ian.  Herzegovina itself comes from *Herceg-ov(ina), meaning "duke's (land)," from herceg "duke" (a borrowing from German Herzog) + the Slavic possessive suffix -ov- + the country suffix -ina.
