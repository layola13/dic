biliously:/ˈbɪliəsli/| adj.|脾气坏的，易怒的；恶心想吐的|Affected by or associated with nausea or vomiting; bad-tempered; irritable.  Derived from "bile," a fluid secreted by the liver, once believed to cause irritability and anger.|He spoke biliously about the government's new policy. 他恶狠狠地批评了政府的新政策。|Synonyms: nauseous, queasy, irritable, irascible, choleric|Antonyms: good-natured, pleasant, amiable|adjective

biliously:/ˈbɪliəsli/| adv.|恶心想吐地；脾气坏地|In a bilious manner; with nausea or irritability.|She glared at him biliously. 她厌恶地瞪着他。|Synonyms: nauseously, peevishly, irritably|Antonyms: pleasantly, amiably|adverb
