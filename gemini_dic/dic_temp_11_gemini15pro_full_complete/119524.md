microsc:/ˈmaɪkrəskɒp/| adj.|微小的，显微的|Relating to or involving a microscope; conducted with the aid of a microscope; extremely small; minute.|例句：Microscopic examination revealed the presence of bacteria. 显微镜检查显示存在细菌。|例句：The dust particles were microscopic. 灰尘颗粒是微小的。|近义词：tiny, minute, minuscule|反义词:macroscopic, large, huge|词性:形容词

词源: From Greek *mikros* (small) + *-skopion* (instrument for viewing)
