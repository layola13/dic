interfibrous:/ˌɪntərˈfaɪbrəs/
adjective
纤维间的；纤维状组织内的
Located or occurring between fibers.
例句：Interfibrous cement binds the individual fibers together.  纤维间的粘合物将单根纤维粘合在一起。

例句：The interfibrous substance plays a crucial role in tissue strength. 纤维状组织内的物质对组织强度起着至关重要的作用。
近义词：interfilamentous, intrafibrillar
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective
