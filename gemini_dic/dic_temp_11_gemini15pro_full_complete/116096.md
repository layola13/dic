martyrizing:/ˈmɑːrtəˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |折磨；使痛苦；使殉难|  The constant criticism was martyrizing him.  不断的批评使他痛苦不堪。|English Explanation: To cause great suffering to; to torment or torture, either physically or mentally, often for a belief or cause.  Can also mean to make someone appear a martyr, often falsely. | Example 1: He felt martyrized by his boss's constant demands. 他觉得被老板不断的需求折磨得痛苦不堪。

Example 2: She martyrized herself by taking on all the extra work. 她承担了所有额外的工作，让自己受苦。| 近义词: tormenting, torturing, agonizing, persecuting | 反义词: comforting, soothing, pleasing | 词性: 动词
