omnivore:/ˈɑːmnɪvɔːr/ (US) /ˈɒmnɪvɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|杂食动物|An animal that eats both plants and animals.|Bears are omnivores, consuming berries, fish, and insects. 熊是杂食动物，吃浆果、鱼和昆虫。|Synonyms: generalist feeder|Antonyms: herbivore, carnivore|noun
omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ (UK) /ɑːmˈnɪvərəs/ (US)| adj.|杂食的|Eating both plants and animals; indiscriminately taking in or using whatever is available.|Pigs have omnivorous diets and will eat almost anything. 猪是杂食性的，几乎什么都吃。|Synonyms: all-devouring, polyphagous|Antonyms: herbivorous, carnivorous|adjective
