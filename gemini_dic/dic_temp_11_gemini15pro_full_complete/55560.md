distrusting:/dɪsˈtrʌstɪŋ/
adjective
不信任的，怀疑的
inclined to distrust; suspicious
例句：He cast a distrusting glance at the stranger. 他不信任地瞥了陌生人一眼。

例句：She has a distrusting nature, always questioning everyone's motives. 她生性多疑，总是质疑每个人的动机。

近义词：suspicious, wary, skeptical, cynical
反义词：trusting, credulous, naive
词性: adjective
