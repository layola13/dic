misassigning:/ˌmɪsəˈsaɪnɪŋ/
verb
错误分配；分配错误
The act of assigning something incorrectly or to the wrong person or place.
The manager was reprimanded for misassigning the project to an inexperienced team.  经理因将项目错误地分配给一个缺乏经验的团队而受到谴责。
The algorithm is misassigning data points to the wrong clusters.  该算法将数据点错误地分配给了错误的集群。
misallocating, misplacing, misdirecting
correctly assigning, properly allocating
verb
