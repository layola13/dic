bittern:/ˈbɪtərn/ | n. | 麻鳽;卤水 |A type of wading bird with mottled brown plumage, found in marshy areas. A bitter liquid remaining after salt has been extracted from seawater. |例句：The bittern stood motionless in the reeds, camouflaged against the background. 麻鳽一动不动地站在芦苇丛中，与背景融为一体。

例句：Magnesium salts are extracted from the bittern that remains after common salt has been crystallized out of seawater. 镁盐是从海水结晶出普通盐后剩下的卤水中提取出来的。|近义词：heron, egret (for the bird); brine (for the liquid) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
