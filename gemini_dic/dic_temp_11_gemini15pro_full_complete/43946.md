cribber:/ˈkrɪbər/ (US), /ˈkrɪbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|抄袭者，婴儿床，饲料槽，马槽|A person who copies from others' work, especially in an examination; a small bed with high sides for a baby; a manger or trough for animal feed. |The teacher caught the cribber using notes during the test. 老师抓到那个抄袭者在考试中使用笔记。|Synonyms: cheater, copier, plagiarist, cot, cradle, manger, trough|Antonyms: originator, innovator|noun
