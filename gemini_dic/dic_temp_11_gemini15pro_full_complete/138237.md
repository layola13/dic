nostalgic: /nəˈstældʒɪk/ | adj. | 怀旧的，念旧的 | Characterized by or exhibiting feelings of nostalgia. |  I felt nostalgic looking at old photos from my childhood. 我看着童年时的老照片，感到很怀旧。

The old house evoked nostalgic memories of my grandparents. 这栋老房子唤起了我对祖父母的怀旧记忆。


近义词: wistful, sentimental, reminiscent | 反义词: unsentimental, forward-looking | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From French *nostalgique*, from Greek *νόστος (nóstos)* "return home" + *ἄλγος (álgos)* "pain, grief".  Originally referred to homesickness.
