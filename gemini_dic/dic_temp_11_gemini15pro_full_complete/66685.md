exogenetic:/ˌeksoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
外生的，外成的
Relating to, or produced by external factors or an external agent.  Formed or occurring on the surface of the earth.
Exogenetic processes are those that take place on or near the Earth's surface.
外生作用是指发生在地球表面或接近地球表面的作用。

Erosion is an exogenetic process.
侵蚀是一种外生作用。

Near-surface volcanism can be considered an exogenetic process.
近地表火山活动可以被认为是一种外生作用。

synonym: external, exterior, outer, superficial
antonym: endogenetic, internal, interior, inner
