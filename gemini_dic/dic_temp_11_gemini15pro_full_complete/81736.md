greensward:/ˈɡriːnzˌwɔːrd/ /ˈɡriːnˌswɔːrd/
n.
草地；绿茵
A stretch of green, grassy land.
例句：
The children frolicked on the greensward. 孩子们在草地上嬉戏。

The picnickers spread their blanket on the greensward. 野餐的人在草地上铺开了毯子。


近义词：lawn, turf, grass, meadow
反义词：barren land, wasteland
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grenswward, from Old English grēneswearde (“green turf”), from grēne (“green”) + swearde (“turf, skin, rind”). Cognate with Scots greensward (“greensward”).  Equivalent to green + sward.
