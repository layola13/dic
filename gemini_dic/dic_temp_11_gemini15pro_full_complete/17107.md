battailant: /bætəˈjɑːnt/ | adj. |  战斗的，好战的 |  Fighting; disposed to fight.  Derived from Old French "bataillant," present participle of "bataillier," meaning "to fight," from "bataille," meaning "battle." Ultimately from Late Latin "battualia," meaning "military exercise." |  He had a battailant spirit, always ready for a challenge.  他有一种好战的精神，时刻准备迎接挑战。 |  bellicose, pugnacious, combative | peaceful, pacifistic | adjective
