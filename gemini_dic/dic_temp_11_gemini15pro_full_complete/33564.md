ciggy:/ˈsɪɡi/| n.|香烟，烟卷|A colloquial and informal term for a cigarette.  It's primarily used in British English.|例句：Fancy a ciggy?  想抽根烟吗？|近义词：cigarette, fag (British slang), butt|反义词:None (The opposite would be something like "nicotine patch" or "vaping device" if referring to alternatives.)|词性:noun
Etymology: Shortened form of "cigarette," influenced by similar diminutives ending in "-y."
