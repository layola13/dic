croupous: /ˈkruːpəs/ | adj. |  伪膜性的；格鲁布性肺炎的 | Relating to or affected with croup, especially characterized by a membrane-like exudate. |  The doctor diagnosed a croupous pneumonia. 医生诊断为格鲁布性肺炎。|  membranous, pseudomembranous | - | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *croupeux, from croupe (“rump, croup”), from Old Norse kroppr (“lump, hunch”).
