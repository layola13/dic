overselling:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛlɪŋ/ | n. | 过度销售；夸大其词 | The act of selling more of something than can be delivered or more than is needed.  It can also refer to exaggerating the benefits or value of something. | The company was accused of overselling its products, promising features that didn't exist.  该公司被指控夸大其产品，承诺了不存在的功能。|  overpromotion, exaggeration | underselling, downplaying | noun

oversell:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛl/ | vt. | 过度销售；夸大 | To sell more of something than can be delivered or more than is needed. To exaggerate the benefits or value of something. |  Don't oversell the product; just be honest about its capabilities. 不要夸大产品；只要如实说明它的功能即可。  | overstate, exaggerate, hype | understate, downplay | verb
