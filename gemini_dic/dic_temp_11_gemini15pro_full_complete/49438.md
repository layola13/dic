delphically:/dɛlˈfɪk(ə)li/
adjective
神谕般地；晦涩难懂地；深奥地
in a way that is difficult to interpret; ambiguously; oracularly
The spokesperson answered the question delphically, leaving everyone more confused than before.  发言人含糊其辞地回答了这个问题，让每个人都比以前更困惑了。

The prophecy was delivered delphically, open to multiple interpretations.  预言是用神谕般的语言传达的，可以有多种解释。

synonym: enigmatically, obscurely, cryptically, ambiguously, oracularly
antonym: clearly, plainly, explicitly, directly
