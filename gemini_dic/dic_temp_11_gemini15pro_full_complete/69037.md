fatuousness:/fəˈtʃuːəsnəs/
|n.|愚蠢；愚昧；空洞
|The quality of being silly and pointless.|
|His speech was filled with fatuousness and lacked any substance. 他的演讲充满了愚蠢和空洞，没有任何实质内容。|
|stupidity, foolishness, silliness, inanity, vacuity|
|intelligence, wisdom, sense, sagacity|
|noun|


fatuous:/ˈfætʃuəs/
|adj.|愚蠢的；昏庸的；无聊的
|Silly and pointless.|
|She made a fatuous remark about the weather. 她对天气做出了愚蠢的评论。|
|silly, foolish, stupid, inane, vacuous|
|intelligent, wise, sensible, sagacious|
|adjective|
