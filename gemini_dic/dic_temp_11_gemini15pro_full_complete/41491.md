cornu:/ˈkɔːrnjuː/
n.
horn; horn-shaped projection
A horn or horn-shaped projection.  The anatomical term refers to a horn-shaped part of an organ or body structure.
The cornu of the uterus is where the fallopian tube meets the uterine body. 子宫角是输卵管与子宫体相交的地方。
The greater cornu of the hyoid bone provides attachment for muscles involved in swallowing.  舌骨大角为参与吞咽的肌肉提供附着点。
synonyms: horn, projection, protuberance, appendage
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it describes a shape)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cornū*, meaning "horn".
