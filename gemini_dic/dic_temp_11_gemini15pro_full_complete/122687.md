moelline:/ˈmoʊlɪn/| n.|莫林, 一种从非洲植物Knoxia moelline提取的红色染料|A red dye extracted from the African plant Knoxia moelline, used traditionally for coloring fabrics and other materials.|The intricate patterns on the cloth were achieved using moelline. 这块布上复杂的图案是用莫林染成的。|近义词:dye, pigment, colorant|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From the scientific name of the plant *Knoxia moelline*, from which the dye is derived.
