feldspathic:/fɛldˈspæθɪk/
adjective
长石质的，含长石的
Containing or resembling feldspar.
The rock is feldspathic and granular. 这块岩石是长石质的，呈颗粒状。
The soil is highly feldspathic.  这种土壤富含长石。
Synonyms: feldspar-rich, feldspar-bearing
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could use terms describing rocks lacking feldspar, like quartz-rich)
詞性: adjective
