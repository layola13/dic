nonauthenticities:/ˌnɒnɔːθɛnˈtɪsɪtiz/
n.
非真实性；伪造；虚假
The lack of original documents raised concerns about the nonauthenticities of the artifacts.  这些文物的原始文件缺失，引发了人们对其真伪的担忧。
The museum is investigating the nonauthenticities of several recently acquired paintings.  博物馆正在调查几幅最近收购的画作的真伪。
近义词:fakes, forgeries, falsifications
反义词:authenticities, genuineness, validity
词性:名词

词源:non- (not) + authenticity + -ities (plural suffix)  来源于authenticity（真实性），加上否定前缀non- 和复数后缀-ities构成。
