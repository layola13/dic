blightingly:/ˈblaɪtɪŋli/| adv.|毁灭性地，有害地|In a way that causes blight; destructively.  It refers to something having a severely detrimental effect, like a disease or a harmful influence.|The recession blightingly affected the small businesses in the town. 经济衰退对镇上的小企业造成了毁灭性的影响。|Synonym: destructively, ruinously, devastatingly|Antonym: beneficially, helpfully|Adverb
