bludgeon: /ˈblʌdʒən/ | n. vt. | 棍棒；用棍棒打 | A short, heavy club. To hit someone repeatedly with a heavy object. | He was bludgeoned to death with a baseball bat. 他被人用棒球棒打死了。| 近义词：club, cudgel, beat, batter | 反义词: caress, soothe | noun, verb

bludgeon: /ˈblʌdʒən/ | vt. | （用权力、论据等）压制；威逼 | To force or coerce someone into doing something. | The government tried to bludgeon the opposition into submission. 政府试图压制反对派使其屈服。 | 近义词：coerce, bully, intimidate | 反义词: persuade, reason | verb
