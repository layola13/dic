legalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 平等主义的；平等主义者 |  Believing in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. |  The party's platform reflects its egalitarian principles.  该党的纲领反映了其平等主义原则。 | Synonyms: equitable, fair, just, impartial | Antonyms: elitist, hierarchical, discriminatory | adjective
