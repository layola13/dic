extrorsal:/ɛkˈstrɔːrsəl/| adj.|外转的，向外翻的|Turned or directed outwards, especially away from the axis of the body.|The extrorsal antennae of the insect helped it navigate.  这种昆虫外转的触角帮助它导航。|近义词：outward, external|反义词：introrsal, inward|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *ex* (out) + *torsus* (twisted, turned), past participle of *torquēre*.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *twerk-*, "to twist."
