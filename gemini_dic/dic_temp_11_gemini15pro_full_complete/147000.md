overlabored:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪbərd/ | adj. | 过于费力的；过分雕琢的； labored |  Characterized by excessive labor or effort; showing signs of tedious, excessive work, especially in writing or artistic expression.  | The prose was dense and overlabored, making it difficult to read. 这篇文章文字冗长且过于费力，难以阅读。 |  The comedian's overlabored jokes failed to land with the audience.  这位喜剧演员过于费力的笑话没能逗乐观众。 | 近义词: labored, strained, overwrought, contrived, artificial | 反义词: natural, effortless, simple, concise | 词性: adjective

overlabor:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪbər/ | vt. |  过度劳累；过分修饰；| To work or exert oneself excessively; to elaborate or refine to an excessive degree. |  Don't overlabor the point; it's already clear.  不要过度解释这一点；它已经很清楚了。 | She overlabored the details, making the story unnecessarily long. 她过分修饰细节，使得故事不必要地冗长。 | 近义词: overwork, belabor | 反义词: simplify, streamline | 词性: verb
