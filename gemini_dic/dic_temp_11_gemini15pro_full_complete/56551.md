domineering:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ /ˌdɑːmɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
adjective
专横的，跋扈的
Trying to control other people without considering their feelings or opinions.
She has a very domineering personality. 她性格很专横。

近义词:bossy, tyrannical, overbearing, authoritarian, dictatorial, imperious
反义词:submissive, meek, compliant, docile
词性:adjective
