mobilise:/ˈməʊbəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ (US)| v.|动员，调动；使行动起来|To organize and prepare something for action, especially for war or other emergencies; to make something able to be moved or used easily. |例句：The government mobilized the army for war. 政府动员军队准备战争。

例句：They mobilized resources to help the flood victims. 他们调动资源来帮助洪水灾民。|近义词：assemble, rally, deploy, activate|反义词：demobilize, disband|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mobiliser*, from mobile (“movable”) + -iser.  Ultimately from Latin mobilis (“movable, flexible, fickle, changeable”), from movēre (“to move”).
