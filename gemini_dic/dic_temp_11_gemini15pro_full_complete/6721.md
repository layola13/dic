analogousnesses:/əˈnæləɡəsnəsɪz/
n.
相似性，类似性
The state or quality of being analogous; resemblance or comparability.
The analogousnesses between the two systems are striking. 这两个系统之间的相似之处令人吃惊。
analogies, similarities, resemblances, parallels
differences, dissimilarities, contrasts
