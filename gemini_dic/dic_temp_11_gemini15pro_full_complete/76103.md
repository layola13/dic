further:/ˈfɜːrðər/ (US) /ˈfɜːðə(r)/ (UK)| adj. adv.| 更远的，更进一步的；此外，而且 |  (as an adjective) more distant or advanced in space or time; (as an adverb) at or to a greater distance or degree, or additionally.| We need to discuss this matter further. 我们需要进一步讨论这件事。|Synonyms: additional, supplementary, more, extra| Antonyms: less, nearer, closer|词性: 形容词，副词

further:/ˈfɜːrðər/ (US) /ˈfɜːðə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 促进，推进 | to help the progress or development of something|  He took a second job to further his career. 他从事第二份工作以促进他的事业发展。|Synonyms: advance, promote, forward, progress| Antonyms: hinder, impede, obstruct|词性: 动词
