nonrhyming:/ˌnɒnˈraɪmɪŋ/ | adj. | 非押韵的，不押韵的 | Not rhyming; having no rhymes. | Example: The poem was deliberately nonrhyming to create a sense of dissonance. 这首诗故意不押韵，以营造一种不和谐感。

Example:  Nonrhyming poetry is often used in free verse.  自由诗经常使用不押韵的诗歌。 | Synonyms: unrhymed, blank | Antonyms: rhyming, rhymed | Word type: Adjective

Etymology: non- + rhyming.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "rhyming" refers to words or lines of verse that have corresponding terminal sounds.
