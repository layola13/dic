Mayfair: /ˈmeɪfeə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmeɪfer/ (US) | n. | 梅费尔 | A fashionable and wealthy district of London, England, known for its elegant shops, restaurants, and hotels. |  She lives in a luxurious apartment in Mayfair. 她住在梅费尔区的一间豪华公寓里。 |  The Mayfair hotel offers impeccable service. 梅费尔酒店提供无可挑剔的服务。| 近义词: affluent area, upscale neighborhood | 反义词: slum, ghetto | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the May Fair, a market held annually in the area from 1686 to 1764.
