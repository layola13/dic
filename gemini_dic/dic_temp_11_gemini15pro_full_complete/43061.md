cowl:/kaʊl/| n.|头巾，兜帽，通风帽，整流罩|A large, loose hood, especially one forming part of a monk's habit or worn by a person for warmth; a covering for a chimney, engine, etc.|The monk wore a cowl. 这位僧侣戴着头巾。|The chimney cowl prevents downdrafts. 烟囱的通风帽可以防止下沉气流。|synonyms: hood, cape, cloak, bonnet|antonyms: (For clothing) none; (For chimney) opening|noun

cowl:/kaʊl/| v.|用头巾盖住，遮盖|To cover or furnish with a cowl.|She cowled her head with a scarf. 她用围巾盖住了头。|The engine was cowled for protection.  发动机被罩上以保护它。|synonyms: cover, conceal, hide|antonyms: uncover, expose, reveal|verb
