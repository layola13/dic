efficient:/ɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|高效的，有效的|Performing or functioning in the best possible manner with the least waste of time and effort.|The new machine is very efficient and saves us a lot of time. 这台新机器非常高效，节省了我们很多时间。|近义词：effective, productive, competent|反义词:inefficient, ineffective|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from Latin efficient- ‘accomplishing’, from the verb efficere, from ex- ‘out, thoroughly’ + facere ‘do, make’.
