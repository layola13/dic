nabbed:/næbd/
verb
抓住，逮捕
to catch or arrest someone suddenly
The police nabbed the thief as he was trying to escape. 警察在小偷试图逃跑时将其抓获。
Synonyms: caught, arrested, apprehended, seized, captured
Antonyms: released, freed, liberated
词性: 动词

nabbed:/næbd/
verb
攫取，抢夺
to quickly take something
He nabbed the last piece of cake before anyone else could get it. 他在其他人拿到之前抢走了最后一块蛋糕。
Synonyms: grabbed, snatched, seized, took
Antonyms: gave, offered, surrendered
词性: 动词
