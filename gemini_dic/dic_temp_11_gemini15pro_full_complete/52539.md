diffidently:/ˈdɪfɪdəntlɪ/ | adv. | 害羞地，胆怯地 | In a manner lacking self-confidence; timidly. | She diffidently approached the stage, unsure of her performance. 她胆怯地走上舞台，对自己的表演没有信心。|  He diffidently suggested an alternative solution. 他怯生生地提出了一个替代方案。| 近义词: timidly, shyly, hesitantly, reservedly | 反义词: confidently, boldly, assertively | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Latin *diffīdēns, diffīdent- (present participle of diffīdere “to distrust, lack confidence”), from Latin dis- “apart, away” + fīdere “to trust.”
