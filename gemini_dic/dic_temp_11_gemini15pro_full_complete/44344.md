|例句：Cross-a-boundary collaborations between scientists and artists can lead to innovative discoveries.  科学家和艺术家之间的跨界合作可以带来创新性的发现。|近义词：interdisciplinary, transdisciplinary, multidisciplinary|反义词：specialized, focused, traditional|词性:形容词
