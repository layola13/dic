goldtit:/ˈɡoʊldˌtɪt/ (US), /ˈɡəʊldtɪt/ (UK)| n. |金翅雀|A small, brightly coloured European finch (bird) with a yellow stripe on its wing.|I saw a goldtit flitting about in the garden. 我看到一只金翅雀在花园里飞来飞去。|近义词：bird, finch|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From 'gold' + 'tit' (referring to a small bird), likely in reference to its bright yellow wing markings.
