AlexNet: /ˌælɛksˈnɛt/ | n. | AlexNet | A convolutional neural network (CNN) architecture named after Alex Krizhevsky, who was the lead author of the paper that described it. It won the 2012 ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge (ILSVRC) with a significant margin, marking a turning point in the field of computer vision and deep learning. | Example: AlexNet revolutionized image classification tasks. AlexNet彻底改变了图像分类任务。 | Synonym: CNN, deep learning model | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "traditional computer vision algorithms") | Noun
