duebill: /ˈdjuːbɪl/ | n. | 欠条，借据 | A short-term, informal note acknowledging a debt and promising payment. |  I gave him a duebill for the $50 I lent him. 我给他写了一张50美元的借据。| Synonym: IOU, promissory note | Antonym: receipt | noun

duebill: /ˈdjuːbɪl/ | n. | 应付凭单，提货单 | A certificate issued by a company acknowledging its obligation to deliver goods or services. | The company issued duebills for the delayed shipments. 公司为延迟发货开具了应付凭单。| Synonym: voucher, chit | Antonym: N/A | noun
