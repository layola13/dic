arase:/əˈreɪz/ | v. |擦除，抹去| To remove or erase completely; to obliterate. |  She tried to erase the painful memory from her mind. 她试图从脑海中抹去痛苦的记忆。

The wind erased the footprints in the sand. 风抹去了沙滩上的脚印。


近义词:delete, remove, expunge, obliterate | 反义词:write, record, inscribe | 词性:verb
