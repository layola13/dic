lithesome: /ˈlaɪðsəm/ | adj. | 轻盈的，柔软的，敏捷的 |  Characterized by easy flexibility and grace; supple; agile.  | The dancer's lithesome movements captivated the audience. 舞蹈家轻盈的动作吸引了观众。 | The gymnast's lithesome body allowed her to perform difficult maneuvers with ease. 体操运动员柔软的身体使她能够轻松地完成高难度动作。 | 近义词: supple, agile, graceful, nimble | 反义词: stiff, rigid, clumsy, awkward | 词性: adjective

词源: From lithe + -some. Lithe comes from Old English *liðe, from Proto-Germanic *linþjaz (“soft, mild”).
