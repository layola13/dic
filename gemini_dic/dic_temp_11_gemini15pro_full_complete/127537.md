necrophagan:/ˈnɛkrəˌfeɪɡən/ | n. | 食尸鬼；恋尸癖者；亵渎死者的人 |  A person who defiles or violates corpses, often for sexual gratification; also refers to someone with an obsessive fascination with death and corpses.  From Greek *nekros* "corpse" + *phagein* "to eat". |  The police arrested the necrophagan who had been desecrating graves in the local cemetery. 警方逮捕了那个一直在当地墓地亵渎坟墓的恋尸癖者。 | synonyms: ghoul, body snatcher, grave robber | antonyms:  None (The concept lacks a direct opposite.) | noun

necrophagous: /nɪˈkrɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 食尸的；以尸体为食的 | Feeding on corpses. | Vultures are necrophagous birds. 秃鹫是食腐鸟类。 | synonyms: carrion-eating, corpse-eating | antonyms: herbivorous, carnivorous (though not perfect opposites, these describe contrasting diets) | adjective
