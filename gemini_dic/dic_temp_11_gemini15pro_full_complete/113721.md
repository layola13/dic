macrocosmic: /ˌmækrəˈkɒzmɪk/ /ˌmækroʊˈkɑːzmɪk/ | adj. |宏观的，宇宙的|Relating to the universe or cosmos, especially as a whole; the opposite of microcosmic.|例句：His paintings attempt a macrocosmic view of history. 他的绘画试图展现一种宏观的歷史视角。|例句：The novel presents a macrocosmic picture of society during wartime. 这部小说展现了战时社会的宏观图景。|近义词：universal, cosmic, global|反义词：microcosmic, miniature|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *macrocosmus, from Ancient Greek μακρόκοσμος (makrókosmos, “great world, universe”), from μακρός (makrós, “long, large”) + κόσμος (kósmos, “order, ornament, world”).
