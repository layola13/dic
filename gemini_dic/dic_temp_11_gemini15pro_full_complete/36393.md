coinvents:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛnts/
verb
共同发明
To invent something jointly with another person or group.
Marie and Pierre Curie coinvented the theory of radioactivity. 玛丽和皮埃尔·居里共同发明了放射性理论。
Thomas Edison is often incorrectly credited with coinventing the light bulb.  托马斯·爱迪生经常被错误地认为是共同发明了灯泡。
synonyms: collaborate on an invention, jointly invent, invent together
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) independently invent, solely invent
词性:v.
