glutted:/ˈɡlʌtɪd/
adjective
供应过剩的，饱和的
overfilled or saturated
The market is glutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。
近义词:oversupplied, saturated, flooded
反义词:deficient, lacking, scarce
词性:adjective

glutted:/ˈɡlʌtɪd/
verb
(使)充满；(使)过饱；(使)充斥
fill to excess; oversupply
The oil refineries were glutted with crude oil.  炼油厂原油供应过剩。
近义词:flood, overload, saturate
反义词:deplete, drain, empty
词性:verb (past participle)

词源:
From the verb "glut," from Middle French "glouter" (to swallow, gulp down), from Latin "glutire" (to swallow).
