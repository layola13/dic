Carpathians: /kɑːrˈpeɪθiənz/ | n. |  喀尔巴阡山脉 | A mountain range in central Europe. |  The Carpathians stretch across several countries, including Romania and Ukraine. 喀尔巴阡山脉横跨几个国家，包括罗马尼亚和乌克兰。| synonyms: Carpathian Mountains | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology: From German *Karpathen*, from Latin *Carpates*, from a substrate language, probably Dacian.  The name may be related to an Albanian word meaning "rock."
