mellifluousness:/ˌmɛlɪˈfluːəsnəs/| n. |流畅,甜美,悦耳|The quality of being smooth and sweet, typically used to describe sounds or voices.|Her voice has a mellifluousness that calms the savage beast. 她的声音有一种能驯服野兽的甜美。|近义词：euphony, sweetness, smoothness, harmony|反义词：cacophony, harshness, dissonance|noun
