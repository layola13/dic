cop-out:/ˈkɑːpˌaʊt/ | n. | 逃避；推卸；回避 | An act of avoiding or backing out of something, especially a commitment or responsibility. | He promised to help but then copped out at the last minute. 他答应帮忙，却在最后一刻退缩了。|  She made a lot of promises during the campaign, but it seems like they were all just cop-outs.  她在竞选期间许下了很多诺言，但似乎都只是逃避责任的说辞。 | synonyms: evasion, excuse, pretext, dodge, escape, loophole | antonyms: commitment, confrontation, facing (the issue), engagement | noun
