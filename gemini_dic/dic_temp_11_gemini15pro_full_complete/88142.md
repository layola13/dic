hilarity:/hɪˈlærəti/ /hɪˈlerədi/| n.|欢闹，狂欢，高兴|Great hilarity ensued. 接著是一阵欢闹。|近义词：mirth, merriment, gaiety, amusement, laughter|反义词: gloom, sadness, seriousness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hilaritas, from Latin hilaris ("cheerful"), from Proto-Italic *hilāris, probably from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- (“to shine, gleam; yellow, green”).
