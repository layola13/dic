births:/bɜːrθs/ /bɝːθs/
n.
出生，诞生
the act or process of being born
例句：The hospital recorded 100 births last month.  上个月这家医院记录了100例新生儿出生。

例句：Multiple births are becoming more common. 多胞胎出生正变得越来越普遍。

近义词：deliveries, nativities
反义词：deaths
词性：名词
