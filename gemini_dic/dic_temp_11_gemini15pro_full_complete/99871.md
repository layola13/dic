intermuscularity:/ˌɪntərˈmʌskjʊˈlærɪti/
n.
肌肉间性；肌肉间的状态或性质
The state or quality of being intermuscular; the arrangement or condition of the muscles between or among themselves.
Intramuscular fat, also known as marbling, contributes to the tenderness and flavor of meat.  The degree of intermuscularity can affect the overall eating experience. 肌肉内的脂肪，也称为大理石纹，有助于肉的嫩度和风味。肌肉间的程度会影响整体的饮食体验。
近义词: musculature, intramuscular structure
反义词: None (This is a specific anatomical term, lacking a direct antonym.)
词性: noun

intermuscular:/ˌɪntərˈmʌskjʊlər/
adj.
肌肉间的；位于肌肉之间的
Situated between muscles.
Intermuscular injections are often preferred over intravenous injections for certain medications. 肌肉注射通常比某些药物的静脉注射更受欢迎。
The intermuscular septum separates adjacent muscles. 肌间隔将相邻的肌肉分开。
近义词: intramuscular
反义词: None (This is a specific anatomical term, lacking a direct antonym.)
词性: adjective
