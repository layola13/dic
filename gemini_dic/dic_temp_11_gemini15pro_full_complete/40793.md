convocational:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃənəl/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈseɪʃənəl/| adj.|谈话的，会话的，召集的|Relating to or resembling conversation; involving or using conversation.  Also, pertaining to a convocation or assembly.|The atmosphere at the party was convivial and convocational.  聚会上的气氛欢快，大家都在交谈。|近义词：conversational, dialogic, communicative|反义词: taciturn, silent|词性:adjective
convocation:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈseɪʃən/| n.|召集，集会，会议|The act or process of convoking or calling together, especially for an assembly; an assembly of persons convoked.|The university president addressed the convocation of graduating students. 校长在毕业典礼上致辞。|近义词：assembly, gathering, meeting|反义词: dispersal, scattering|词性:noun
