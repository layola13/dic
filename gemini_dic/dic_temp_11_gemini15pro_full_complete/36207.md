cogitabund:/ˈkɒdʒɪtəbʌnd/
adjective
沉思的；深思熟虑的
Given to deep thought; thoughtful.
He sat in a cogitabund pose, stroking his chin. 他沉思着坐着，抚摸着下巴。
近义词: pensive, thoughtful, meditative, contemplative, reflective
反义词: unthinking, frivolous, careless, inattentive
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin cogitabundus, present participle of cogitare (“to think”), frequentative of cogere (“to compel, collect, bring together”).  Literally, "compelling together" (thoughts).
