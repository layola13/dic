impair:/ɪmˈpeər/| vt.|损害，削弱|To weaken or damage something, especially a faculty or function.|Alcohol impairs your judgment. 酒精会损害你的判断力。|The accident impaired his vision. 事故损害了他的视力。|synonyms: damage, harm, weaken, diminish, undermine|antonyms: improve, enhance, strengthen, boost|verb

impairment:/ɪmˈpeər.mənt/| n.|损伤，损害|The state of being weakened or damaged.|He suffers from visual impairment. 他患有视力障碍。|Cognitive impairment can be caused by various factors. 认知障碍可能由多种因素引起。|synonyms: damage, harm, weakness, deficiency|antonyms: improvement, enhancement, strength|noun
