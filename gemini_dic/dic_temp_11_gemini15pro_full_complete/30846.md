chaconne:/ʃəˈkɒn/ /tʃəˈkoʊn/| n.|恰空舞曲; 恰空舞; |A chaconne is a type of musical composition, typically in slow triple time, built on a repeating bass line.  恰空是一种音乐作品，通常是慢速的三拍子，建立在重复的低音线上。|Bach's Chaconne for solo violin is a masterpiece. 巴赫的独奏小提琴恰空舞曲是一部杰作.|Synonyms: passacaglia, ground bass|Antonyms: None|Noun

chaconne:/ʃəˈkɒn/ /tʃəˈkoʊn/| n.|恰空舞; |A stately dance in triple time. 一种庄严的三拍子舞蹈。|The chaconne was a popular dance in the Baroque era. 恰空舞是巴洛克时期的一种流行舞蹈。|Synonyms: passacaglia|Antonyms: None|Noun
