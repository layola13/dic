dissolute:/ˈdɪsəluːt/ | adj. | 放荡的；放纵的；肆意的 |  Lacking moral restraint; indulging in excessive pleasures or vices. |  He led a dissolute life, filled with drinking, gambling, and other excesses. 他过着放荡的生活，酗酒、赌博和其他过度的行为充斥其中。| 近义词: debauched, depraved, dissipated, immoral | 反义词: moral, virtuous, upright, chaste | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *dissolutus*, past participle of *dissolvere* "to loosen, dissolve," from *dis-* "apart" + *solvere* "to loosen."
