commutation:/ˌkɒmjuˈteɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːmjuˈteɪʃn/| n. | 交换，减刑，通勤，折算 |The act of exchanging one thing for another; the substitution of a lesser penalty for a greater one; the process of traveling regularly between one's home and place of work.| He received a commutation of his death sentence to life imprisonment. 他被减刑，由死刑改为无期徒刑。| synonyms: exchange, substitution, trade, barter, swap,  reduction, mitigation, remission, pardon, amnesty | antonyms:  increase, aggravation, extension | noun

commutation:/ˌkɒmjuˈteɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːmjuˈteɪʃn/| n. | 折算，变换 |The conversion of something, such as a payment or benefit, into another form.|  The commutation of pension rights into a lump sum. 将养老金权利折算成一次性支付。| synonyms: conversion, transformation, transmutation | antonyms:  preservation, retention | noun
