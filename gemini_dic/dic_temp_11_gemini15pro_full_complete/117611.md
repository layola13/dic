megaphyll:/ˈmɛɡəfɪl/| n. | 巨叶，大型叶 |A leaf with several or many large veins branching apart or running parallel and connected by a network of smaller veins, characteristic of ferns and seed plants.| Ferns and seed plants have megaphylls. | 近义词: macrophyll | 反义词: microphyll | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mega* "large" + *phyllon* "leaf"
