incorporative:/ɪnˈkɔːrpərətɪv/
adjective
结合的，合并的
tending to incorporate or combine things, especially different nationalities or ethnic groups into a whole.
The United States has a history of being an incorporative society, welcoming immigrants from many different backgrounds. 美国一直以来都是一个包容性社会，欢迎来自不同背景的移民。
The new company structure is incorporative, merging several smaller departments into larger, more efficient units. 新的公司结构是合并式的，将几个较小的部门合并成更大、更高效的单位。
近义词: integrative, combining, unifying, consolidating
反义词: divisive, separating, segregating
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin incorporativus, from incorporare (to incorporate), from in- (in, into) + corporare (to form into a body), from corpus (body).
