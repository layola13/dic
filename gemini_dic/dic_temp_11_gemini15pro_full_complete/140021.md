oeillade:/ˌœɪˈjɑːd/ | n. | 眉目传情；送秋波；意味深长的眼色 | An amorous glance; a significant glance. | She gave him a quick oeillade across the crowded room. 她隔着拥挤的房间迅速地向他抛了个媚眼。|  He caught her oeillade and knew she was interested. 他捕捉到了她的眼神，知道她对他有意思。 | 近义词: glance, wink, look | 反义词: glare, scowl | 词性: noun

词源: From French *œillade*, from *œil* (“eye”) (from Latin *oculus*) + *-ade* (suffix forming nouns of action).
