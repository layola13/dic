loblolly:/ˈlɑːblɑːli/ /ˈlɒblɒli/| n.|火炬松，美国南方松，糊涂虫，懒汉|A fast-growing pine of the southeastern US, having long needles in clusters of three and a reddish-brown bark. Also, a thick gruel, or a mudhole; a mire.|例句：The loblolly pines grew tall and straight. 火炬松长得又高又直。

例句：He got his truck stuck in the loblolly. 他的卡车陷进了泥潭里。|近义词：pine, mud, mire|反义词: (For pine) hardwood, (for mud/mire) solid ground|词性: noun
