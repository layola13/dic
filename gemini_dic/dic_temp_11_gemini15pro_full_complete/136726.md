nonsensical:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪkəl/ /nɑːnˈsɛnsɪkəl/
adjective
荒谬的，无意义的
illogical, absurd, or meaningless.
His argument was completely nonsensical. 他的论点完全是胡说八道。
近义词：absurd, illogical, foolish, silly, preposterous, meaningless
反义词：sensible, logical, rational, meaningful
词性：adjective
