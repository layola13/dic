musted:/ˈmʌstɪd/ | v. (archaic or dialectal) | 必须，不得不 (过去式用法，现已过时或仅用于方言) | A form of "must" used as a simple past tense, though now archaic or dialectal.  It implies obligation or necessity in the past. | He musted go to work even though he was sick.  他即使生病了也必须去上班。|  They musted leave their home due to the flood. 由于洪水，他们不得不离开家园。| 近义词: had to, was obliged to | 反义词: didn't have to, wasn't obliged to | 词性: 动词

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
This usage is archaic and generally incorrect in modern English.  "Must" doesn't have a present tense conjugation for expressing past obligation.
Incorrect: He musts go to work every day.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
This usage is archaic and generally incorrect in modern English.
Incorrect: He is musting to go to work.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
This tense wouldn't use "musted."  Use "have had to" instead.
I have had to work late every day this week. 我这周每天都不得不工作到很晚。
They have had to postpone their trip due to the storm. 由于暴风雨，他们不得不推迟旅行。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This tense wouldn't use "musted." Use "have been having to" instead, though it can be awkward.
I have been having to wake up early all month. 我整个月都不得不早起。
She has been having to deal with difficult clients. 她一直不得不应付难缠的客户。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He had to go to work yesterday. 他昨天不得不去上班。
She had to finish the report by Friday. 她必须在星期五之前完成报告。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was having to work late last night. 昨晚他不得不工作到很晚。
She was having to deal with a difficult customer when I called. 我打电话时，她正在应付一个难缠的客户。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had had to leave before the meeting started. 他在会议开始前不得不离开。
They had had to sell their car to pay the bills. 他们不得不卖掉他们的车来支付账单。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been having to work overtime for weeks before he finally quit.  在他最终辞职之前，他已经连续几周不得不加班工作。
She had been having to care for her sick mother for months. 她已经照顾她生病的母亲好几个月了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will have to work tomorrow. 他明天将不得不工作。
She will have to finish the project by next week. 她必须在下周之前完成这个项目。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

By the time she graduates, she will have had to complete several internships. 到她毕业的时候，她将不得不完成几次实习。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

By the time she finishes her degree, she will have been having to study long hours for years.  到她完成学位的时候，她将不得不长时间学习很多年。

虚拟语气:
If he musted go, he would go. (Archaic) 如果他非得去，他会去的。
If she musted work, she would work. (Archaic) 如果她非得工作，她会工作的。

被动语态:
This form is very rare and generally incorrect.  Passive voice is not typically used with "must."
Incorrect: Work musted be done.

疑问句:
Musted he go? (Archaic) 他必须去吗？
Musted she leave? (Archaic) 她必须离开吗？

否定句:
He musted not go.  (Archaic) 他不能去。
She musted not leave. (Archaic) 她不能离开。
