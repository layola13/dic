covey:/ˈkʌvi/| n. |一群（尤指鸟类，尤指鹌鹑或鹧鸪）；一小群人|A small flock of birds, especially quail or partridge; a small group of people.|The covey of quail burst from the undergrowth.  一群鹌鹑从灌木丛中冲了出来。|Synonyms: flock, group, company, band, bevy|Antonyms: individual, single|noun

covey:/ˈkʌvi/| v. |孵化；窝藏；庇护|To sit on (eggs); to shelter; to harbor.|The hen coveys her chicks under her wings. 母鸡把小鸡藏在翅膀下。|Synonyms: brood, incubate, shelter, harbor, protect|Antonyms: expose, abandon|verb
