camerate:/ˈkæməreɪt/
adjective

Arched, vaulted, or like a honeycomb.
The beetle's camerate thorax was a distinguishing feature.  这种甲虫拱形的胸部是其一个显著特征。
The ancient temple had a camerate ceiling. 这座古老的寺庙有一个穹顶状的天花板。
synonym: vaulted, arched, domed, honeycombed
antonym: flat, planar
词性: adjective


camerate:/ˈkæməreɪt/
noun
(复数 camerates)
蜂巢状结构
A structure resembling a honeycomb.
The fossil revealed intricate camerates, suggesting it was part of a larger organism. 这块化石显示出复杂的蜂巢状结构，表明它是某个更大生物体的一部分。
The inside of the bone had numerous camerates. 骨头的内部有很多蜂巢状结构。
synonym: honeycomb, alveolus (单数), alveoli (复数)
antonym: solid, compact
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *cameratus*, past participle of *camerare* "to vault, arch," from *camera* "vault, arched roof."
