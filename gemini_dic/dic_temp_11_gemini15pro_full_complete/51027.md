deslummed:/dɪˈzlʌmd/
adjective
目眩的，眼花缭乱的
greatly impressed, amazed, or overwhelmed by something dazzling or brilliant
The tourists were deslummed by the magnificent palace. 游客们被壮丽的宫殿所震撼。
synonyms: dazzled, amazed, awestruck, stunned, bewildered, fascinated
antonyms: unimpressed, indifferent, bored, apathetic
词性: adjective
