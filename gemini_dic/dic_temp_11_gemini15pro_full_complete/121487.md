mislaying:/mɪsˈleɪɪŋ/
verb
放错地方；乱放
unintentionally putting something where it cannot readily be found
I seem to have mislaid my keys. 我好像把钥匙放错了地方。
近义词: losing, misplacing
反义词: finding, locating
词性: verb (present participle of mislay)

Etymology:
From mis- + lay (v.).  The prefix "mis-" means "wrongly" or "badly."  "Lay" in this context means "to put or place something down." Thus, "mislay" literally means "to put something in the wrong place."
