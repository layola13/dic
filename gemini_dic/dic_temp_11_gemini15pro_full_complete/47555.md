deauthorized:/diːˈɔːθəraɪzd/
verb
撤销授权，取消授权
To withdraw or cancel the authorization or permission granted to someone or something.
The administrator deauthorized the user's access to the confidential files.  管理员撤销了用户访问机密文件的权限。

The company deauthorized the reseller to sell its products. 该公司取消了经销商销售其产品的授权。

近义词:revoke, rescind, cancel, withdraw
反义词:authorize, empower, commission
词性:verb
