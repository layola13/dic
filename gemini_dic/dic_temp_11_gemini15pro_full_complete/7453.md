anisomerous:/ˌænɪˈsɒmərəs/ | adj. | 不等数的，各部分数量不相等的 | Having unequal numbers of parts in the whorls of a flower; exhibiting anisomery. | Example 1: The anisomerous flower had five petals, but only three sepals.  这朵不等数的花有五个花瓣，但只有三个萼片。

Example 2:  Anisomerous arrangements of floral parts are common in certain plant families.  花部的不等数排列在某些植物科中很常见。 | Synonym: asymmetrical, unequal | Antonym: isomerous, symmetrical | word type: adjective
