overbattle:/ˈoʊvərˌbætəl/| v. n.|过度争斗，过分交战|To fight or argue excessively or unnecessarily; an instance of excessive fighting or arguing.|The two teams overbattled for possession of the ball, resulting in several penalties.  两支队伍为了争夺球权而过度争斗，导致了好几次罚球。

The political candidates overbattled on trivial issues, alienating many voters.  这些政治候选人在一些琐碎的问题上过度争斗，疏远了许多选民。|近义词：overfight, contend excessively|反义词:compromise, concede|词性:动词,名词
