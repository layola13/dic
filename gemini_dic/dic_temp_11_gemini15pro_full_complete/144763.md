over-designing:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/
|n.|过度设计|The act of designing something to a degree that is excessive or unnecessary, often resulting in increased complexity, cost, or difficulty of use.|Over-designing the product led to unnecessary complications and delays.|over-engineering, overcomplicating|simplifying, streamlining|noun


over-design:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪn/
|v.|过度设计|To design something to a degree that is excessive or unnecessary.|They over-designed the bridge, making it far more expensive than it needed to be.|over-engineer, overcomplicate|simplify, streamline|verb
