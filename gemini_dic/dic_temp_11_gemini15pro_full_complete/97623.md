inhaul:/ˈɪnhɔːl/ | n. | 拉入索，回收索 | A rope used to haul in a sail or part of a sail, especially the jib or spinnaker. | The sailor tightened the inhaul to flatten the jib.  水手拉紧回收索以展平三角帆。| synonyms: downhaul, sheet | antonyms: outhaul | noun
