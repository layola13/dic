chevage:/ˈtʃɛvɪdʒ/
n.
人头税；臣属对领主的缴纳的贡金
A poll tax or payment made by a villein or other unfree tenant to his lord as an acknowledgment of his servile status or to secure exemption from certain onerous duties.
In medieval times, serfs often paid chevage to their lords. 在中世纪，农奴经常向他们的领主缴纳人头税。
