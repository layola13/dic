overresisting:/ˌoʊvərɪˈzɪstɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度抵抗的 | Offering excessive resistance; resisting too much. |  The lawyer argued that the defendant was overresisting arrest, leading to the escalation of force. 律师辩称被告过度抵抗逮捕，导致了武力升级。

The child, scared of the doctor, was overresisting the simple examination.  孩子害怕医生，对简单的检查过度抵抗。| 近义词: overreacting, overcompensating | 反义词: complying, submitting | 词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "resist" (verb meaning "to withstand the action or effect of") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
