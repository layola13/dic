inured:/ɪˈnjʊərd/ | adj. | 习惯于（通常指不好的事物）；对…无动于衷 | Made tough by habitual exposure; accustomed to something unpleasant or difficult.  |  The soldiers were inured to the hardships of war.  这些士兵已经习惯了战争的艰苦。 |  accustomed, hardened, habituated, desensitized | sensitive, unaccustomed, vulnerable | adjective

inure:/ɪˈnjʊər/ | v. | 使习惯于；使适应 | To accustom to hardship, difficulty, pain, etc.; toughen |  Years of living in poverty had inured her to hardship.  多年的贫困生活使她对艰苦习以为常。 | accustom, harden, habituate | soften, weaken | verb
