connects:/kəˈnekts/ | v. | 连接，联系 | joins or links together | The bridge connects the two islands. 这座桥连接着两个岛屿. |  link, join, unite | separate, disconnect | verb

connects:/kəˈnekts/ | n. |  联系，连接（通常用复数形式）| things that join or link people or places | He has good connects in the music industry. 他在音乐界人脉很好. | links, ties, relationships | separations, divisions | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English connecten, from Latin connectere (“to tie or fasten together”), from com- (“together”) + nectere (“to bind”).
