nonsoporifics:/ˌnɒnsɒpəˈrɪfɪks/
noun
非安眠药; 不催眠的物质
Substances that do not induce sleep.
Doctors prescribed nonsoporifics to help him stay awake during the day. 医生给他开了不催眠的药物，以帮助他在白天保持清醒。
Near Synonyms: stimulants, invigorants
Antonyms: soporifics, hypnotics, sedatives
n.
