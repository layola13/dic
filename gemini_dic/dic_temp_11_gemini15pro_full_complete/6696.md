analgesia:/ˌænəlˈdʒiːziə/ /ˌænəlˈdʒiːʒə/
noun
无痛法；镇痛；痛觉缺失
Absence of sensibility to pain, especially the relief of pain without loss of consciousness; absence of pain.
The doctor administered analgesia before the surgery. 医生在手术前进行了镇痛。
Synonyms: pain relief, anesthesia, numbness
Antonyms: pain, hyperalgesia
