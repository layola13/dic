kilts:/kɪlts/
n.
苏格兰短裙
A pleated skirt, usually of tartan, traditionally worn by men and boys in the Scottish Highlands.
The Scottish dancers all wore kilts.  苏格兰舞者都穿着短裙。
近义词:skirt
反义词:trousers, pants
词性:noun
