hew-out:/hjuːˈaʊt/
Verb
凿出；开凿出；辛苦地创造出
to shape something, especially a large piece of stone or wood, by cutting it
例句：
The statue was hewn out of a single block of marble.
这尊雕像是用一整块大理石凿成的。

例句：
They hewn out a new life for themselves in the wilderness.
他们在荒野中为自己开辟了新的生活。

近义词：carve, sculpt, fashion, shape, create
反义词：construct, build (These are not perfect antonyms, but represent opposite processes in some contexts.)
词性：Verb

hew-out:/hjuːˈaʊt/
Adjective
(only before noun)  凿成的；雕刻成的
made or shaped by cutting or carving from a large piece of stone or wood
例句：
A hewn-out tomb/cave
一个凿成的墓穴/洞穴

例句：
a hewn-out canoe
一个凿出的独木舟

近义词：carved, sculpted
反义词：constructed, built (Again, not perfect antonyms but represent opposite approaches.)
词性：Adjective
