adjective
可计算的，可表示的，可以想象的
capable of being figured; calculable; imaginable.
The cost of the project is figurable, but it will be expensive.  这个项目的成本是可以计算的，但会很昂贵。

近义词: calculable, computable, imaginable, conceivable
反义词: incalculable, incomputable, unimaginable, inconceivable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *figurabilis, from Latin figura (“shape, form, figure”) + -bilis (“-able”).
