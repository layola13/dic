anorthic:/ˌænɔːrˈθɪk/ | adj. |三斜的，非直角的 | Relating to or denoting a crystal system that has three unequal axes intersecting at oblique angles.  | Anorthic crystals are characterized by having no axes of symmetry. 三斜晶体的特征是没有对称轴。| Anorthite is a common anorthic feldspar mineral.  钙长石是一种常见的三斜长石矿物。|Synonyms: triclinic | Antonyms: isometric, orthorhombic | Adjective
