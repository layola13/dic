loped:/loʊpt/ |v. |轻快地大步跑; 慢跑|  To run or move with long, easy strides. |The horse loped across the field. 马儿轻快地跑过田野。|  The dog loped along beside its owner. 狗在主人旁边慢跑。|  近义词：canter, trot, jog| 反义词：sprint, gallop|词性:动词
