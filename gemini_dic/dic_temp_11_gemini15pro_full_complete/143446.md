outfoot:/ˌaʊtˈfʊt/
v. |超过，胜过，跑得比…快|To surpass or outrun in speed, especially in a race. |He outfooted the other runners and won the race. 他跑得比其他选手快，赢得了比赛。| The hare outfooted the tortoise at the beginning of the race. 兔子在比赛开始时跑得比乌龟快。 | outrun, surpass, outstrip | lag behind, fall behind | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning "beyond" or "exceeding") + "foot" (referring to running or speed).
