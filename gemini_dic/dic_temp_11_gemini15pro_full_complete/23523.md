brain-washed:/ˈbreɪnˌwɒʃt/ (US) /ˈbreɪnˌwɒʃt/ (UK)| adj.|洗脑的，被灌输的|Having had your beliefs and attitudes changed by means of intense indoctrination.|例句：The cult leader had completely brainwashed his followers.  邪教头目彻底给他的追随者洗脑了。|近义词：indoctrinated, programmed, conditioned|反义词：independent, freethinking|词性：形容词

brainwashing:/ˈbreɪnˌwɒʃɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbreɪnˌwɒʃɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|洗脑|The process of systematically and forcibly changing a person's beliefs and attitudes.|例句：He claimed he had been subjected to brainwashing while in captivity. 他声称在被囚禁期间遭受了洗脑。|近义词：indoctrination, propaganda, manipulation|反义词：education, enlightenment|词性：名词
