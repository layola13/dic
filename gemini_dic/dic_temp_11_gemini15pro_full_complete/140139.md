off-whites:/ˌɒfˈwaɪts/ /ˌɔːfˈwaɪts/| n. adj.|灰白色，米白色|A color that is a very pale tint of white, often slightly tinged with yellow or grey.  It refers to colors that are not pure white, but close to it.|She wore an off-white dress to the wedding. 她穿了一件米白色的连衣裙参加婚礼。|近义词：cream, ivory, ecru|反义词：black, pure white|词性:名词, 形容词
