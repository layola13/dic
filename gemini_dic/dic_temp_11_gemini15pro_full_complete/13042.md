astringency:/əˈstrɪndʒənsi/| n. |涩味，收敛性|The astringency of the wine puckered my mouth. |例句：The unripe persimmon had a noticeable astringency.  未成熟的柿子有明显的涩味。

例句：The astringency of the tea cleansed my palate.  这茶的涩味清洁了我的味觉。|近义词：tartness, dryness, sharpness|反义词:sweetness, mildness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *astringentia, from Latin astringent- (stem of astringēns), present participle of astringere ‘to bind fast’, from ad- ‘to’ + stringere ‘to draw tight’.
