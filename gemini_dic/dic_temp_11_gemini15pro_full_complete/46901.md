darkful:/ˈdɑːrkfʊl/
adjective
黑暗的，阴沉的
Characterized by or full of darkness; gloomy.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.
The darkful forest was filled with strange noises.  黑暗的森林里充满了奇怪的声音。
Near synonyms: dark, gloomy, murky, dim
Opposite: bright, light, sunny
