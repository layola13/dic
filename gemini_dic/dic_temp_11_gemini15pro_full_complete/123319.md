monographer:/məˈnɑːɡrəfər/
n.
专著作家，专题论文作者
A person who writes a monograph (a detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it).
Alan Macfarlane, a renowned monographer of the English family, has produced numerous works on this topic. 艾伦·麦克法兰是一位著名的英国家庭专题作家，就此主题撰写了大量著作。
