emanatory:/ˈemənətɔːri/ |adj.| 发出的，流出的；散发性的|Something emanatory issues or originates from a source. |A faint, emanatory glow surrounded the crystal. 水晶周围环绕着微弱的、散发出来的光芒。|近义词：issuing, emanating, radiating, diffusing|反义词：absorbing, attracting, collecting|词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *emanatorius, from emanare (“to flow out”), from ex- (“out of”) + manare (“to flow”).
