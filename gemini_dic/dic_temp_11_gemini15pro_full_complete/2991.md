aeolian's:/iˈoʊliən/ | adj.|风成的，风蚀的| Relating to or arising from the action of the wind; wind-blown.  Aeolian processes are responsible for shaping many desert landscapes.|The aeolian harp produced an ethereal sound as the wind blew through it. 风吹过风神琴，发出空灵的声音。|Synonyms: wind-blown, wind-formed|Antonyms: water-formed, glacial|adjective

aeolian:/iˈoʊliən/ | n.|风成物|A deposit of windblown sediment, such as loess. Also, a wind instrument, such as the aeolian harp.|The dunes were a classic example of an aeolian formation. 这些沙丘是风成地貌的典型例子。|Synonyms: wind deposit|Antonyms: water deposit|noun
