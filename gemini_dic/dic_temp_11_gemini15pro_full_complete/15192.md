backcast:/ˈbækˌkæst/ (US), /ˈbækˌkɑːst/ (UK)| n. v.| 回溯推算，追溯预测；追忆往事|  An estimate of a past value of a variable, made from its present value.|The researchers backcast the population figures to estimate the size of the city a century ago. 研究人员回溯推算了人口数据，以估算一个世纪前这座城市的规模。|近义词：retrodict, hindcast|反义词：forecast, predict|词性:名词/动词
