词源:  From in- + consequential + -ness.  "Consequential" comes from Late Latin consequentia "a following, consequence," from Latin consequentem (nominative consequens), present participle of consequi "to follow after," from com- "with, together" (see com-) + sequi "to follow" (see sequel).
