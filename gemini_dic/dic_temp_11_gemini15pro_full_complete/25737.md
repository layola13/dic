buoyant:/ˈbɔɪənt/ | adj. | 轻快的；有浮力的；上涨的 | Able to float or rise to the top of a liquid; cheerful and optimistic. |  The boat remained buoyant even with the extra weight. 这艘船即使增加了重量也能保持漂浮。| synonyms: cheerful, optimistic, jaunty, lighthearted, airy, bouncy, lively, animated, sprightly, perky, upbeat, effervescent, sparkling, bright, sunny, positive, confident, self-assured | antonyms: gloomy, pessimistic, depressed, downcast, sinking | etymology: From Old French *boiant "floating," present participle of boier "to float," from Gallo-Romance *boiare, of uncertain origin, perhaps Celtic or Germanic. |
