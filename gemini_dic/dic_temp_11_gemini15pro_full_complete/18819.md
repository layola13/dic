berth:/bɜːrθ/
n.|卧铺；停泊处；职位；(议会中的)席位|A sleeping place on a train or ship; a place where a ship is tied up; a job or position, especially one that is difficult to get; a place for a member of Parliament|The train offers comfortable berths for overnight journeys. 火车提供舒适的卧铺，供旅客夜间旅行。|Synonyms: bunk, cabin, dock, mooring, position, slot|Antonyms: None|Noun

berth:/bɜːrθ/
vt.|使停泊；为…提供卧铺|To moor a ship; to provide a berth for someone|The captain berthed the ship in the harbor. 船长将船停泊在港口。|Synonyms: dock, moor, accommodate|Antonyms: launch, disembark|Verb
