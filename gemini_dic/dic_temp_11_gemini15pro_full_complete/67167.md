expostulate:/ɪkˈspɒstʃʊleɪt/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəleɪt/| v. | 抗议；劝诫；告诫；责备 | To reason earnestly with someone against something that person intends to do or has done; remonstrate. |  She expostulated with him about the dangers of driving too fast. 她劝诫他开车太快的危险。 | synonyms: remonstrate, protest, object, argue, reason, plead, complain | antonyms: agree, consent, approve, commend | verb

expostulation:/ɪkˌspɒstʃʊˈleɪʃən/ /ɛkˌspɑːstʃəˈleɪʃən/| n. | 抗议；劝诫；告诫；责备 | The act of expressing strong disapproval or disagreement. | His expostulations were ignored. 他的抗议被忽视了。 | synonyms: remonstrance, protest, objection, argument | antonyms: agreement, consent, approval | noun
