mammalians:/ˈmæməlz/ | n. | 哺乳动物 | Animals belonging to the class Mammalia, characterized by mammary glands (which in females produce milk for their young), a neocortex, three middle ear bones, and hair or fur. |例句： Whales are marine mammals.  鲸鱼是海洋哺乳动物。

例句： Humans are also mammals. 人类也是哺乳动物。 |近义词：mammal |反义词: (None -  There isn't a direct opposite of a biological class.) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Mammalia, from Latin mamma ("breast, udder") + -alia, neuter plural of -alis,  referring to the characteristic mammary glands.
