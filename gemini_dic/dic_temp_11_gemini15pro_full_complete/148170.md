overservile:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvaɪl/
|adj.|
|过分恭顺的；奴颜婢膝的|
|Excessively eager to serve or please; fawning.|
|The overservile waiter hovered around the table, constantly refilling our water glasses.  那个过分殷勤的服务员在桌子旁转来转去，不断地给我们续水。|

|He bowed low in an overservile manner. 他奴颜婢膝地深深鞠了一躬。|
|近义词：fawning, obsequious, servile, subservient|
|反义词：assertive, independent, haughty|
|词性: adjective|

词源:
|From over- + servile, from Latin servilis "of a slave," from servus "slave."|
