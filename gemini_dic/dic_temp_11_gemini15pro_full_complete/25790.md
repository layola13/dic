bureaus:/ˈbjʊəroʊz/ (US), /ˈbjʊərəʊz/ (UK)
n.
 bureaus (plural of bureau)
办公室；办事处；局；（美国政府部门的）处，司，署
A bureau is an office or department for transacting particular business.
The Federal Bureau of Investigation is responsible for investigating federal crimes in the US.  美国联邦调查局负责调查美国的联邦犯罪。
The tourism bureau provides information about local attractions. 旅游局提供当地景点的信息。
