naturalised:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 入籍的，归化的；驯化的 | Having acquired the rights and privileges of a citizen of a country to which one was not a native. |  例句：He became a naturalised British citizen after living in the UK for five years. 他在英国居住五年后成为了英国公民。


例句：The naturalised plants thrived in the local climate.  这些驯化的植物在当地气候下茁壮成长。


近义词：citizen, adopted, acclimatized | 反义词：alien, foreign, non-native | 词性：形容词
