bandsawing:/ˈbændˌsɔːɪŋ/
noun
带锯切割，带锯加工
The act or process of cutting material using a bandsaw.
The carpenter used bandsawing to create intricate curves in the wood.  木匠用带锯在木头上切割出复杂的曲线。
sawing, cutting
milling, grinding
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From band saw + -ing. Band saw from band (n.) + saw (n.).
