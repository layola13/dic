gutlessly:/ˈɡʌtlɪsli/| adv.|怯懦地，胆小地|Without courage or determination; in a cowardly manner.  It describes actions done in a way that lacks bravery.|He gutlessly abandoned his friends in the face of danger. 他在危险面前胆怯地抛弃了他的朋友们。|近义词：cowardly, cravenly, timidly|反义词：bravely, courageously, valiantly|词性:副词

词源:  gutless (adj.) "lacking courage," 1882, from gut (n.1) + -less.  Gut in this sense refers to the bowels, which were traditionally seen as the seat of courage.  The adverbial form, gutlessly, is formed by adding the suffix -ly.
