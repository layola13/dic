nonrestricted:/ˌnɒnrɪˈstrɪktɪd/ | /ˌnɑːnrɪˈstrɪktɪd/ | adj. | 无限制的，不受约束的 | Not limited or restricted in any way. |  Employees enjoy nonrestricted access to the company gym. 员工可以不受限制地使用公司健身房。| The information is nonrestricted and available to the public.  该信息不受限制，公众可以获取。 | 近义词: unlimited, unrestricted, open, free, accessible | 反义词: restricted, limited, confined, controlled | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + restricted (past participle of the verb "restrict," from Latin "restrictus," meaning "drawn back, bound").
