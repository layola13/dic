custodian:/kʌˈstoʊdiən/ | n. | 保管人，监护人，管理员 | A person who has responsibility for or looks after something. | The custodian of the museum is responsible for its security. 博物馆的管理员负责其安全。|  synonyms: guardian, keeper, caretaker, protector, superintendent | antonyms: destroyer, vandal | noun

custodian:/kʌˈstoʊdiən/ | n. | 托管人 | A person or institution that holds assets on behalf of another. | The bank acts as custodian for the client's securities.  这家银行充当客户证券的托管人。 | synonyms: trustee, guardian, keeper | antonyms: owner, possessor | noun
