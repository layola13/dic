dharmic:/ˈdɑːrmɪk/ | adj. | 佛法的，与佛法相关的，符合佛法的 | Relating to dharma, especially in Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism.  It describes actions, thoughts, and beliefs that are considered righteous or in accordance with cosmic order. |  Example:  She follows a dharmic path. 她遵循佛法之路。

Example:  Dharmic principles guide his life. 佛法原则指导他的生活。

| 近义词: righteous, virtuous, ethical | 反义词: adharmic, unrighteous, unethical | 词性: adjective
