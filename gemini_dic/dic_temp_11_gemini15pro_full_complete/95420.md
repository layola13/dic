incidentals:/ˌɪnsɪˈdentlz/ | n. |杂费，附带费用|  Minor expenses that are not part of the main cost of something. | Example: Remember to budget for incidentals like snacks and drinks.  记住要把零食和饮料等杂费列入预算。| Synonym: miscellaneous expenses, sundries, incident expenses | Antonym: main expenses, primary costs | noun

incidental:/ˌɪnsɪˈdentl/ | adj. |附带的，偶然发生的| Happening as a minor accompaniment to something else. | Example: The discovery of the new species was incidental to their main research.  发现新物种是他们主要研究的附带收获。| Synonym: minor, secondary, accompanying, ancillary, nonessential, subordinate | Antonym: essential, primary, main, principal | adjective
