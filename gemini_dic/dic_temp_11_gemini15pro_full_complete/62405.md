endogenesis:/ˌɛndoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
内生，内源性
The process of formation or growth from within; internal production or origin.
例句：Endogenesis is a key concept in understanding geological formations. 内生是理解地质构造的关键概念。

例句：The endogenesis of new blood cells is crucial for maintaining health. 新血细胞的内生对于维持健康至关重要。
近义词：internal development, internal growth, autogenesis
反义词：exogenesis
词性：名词
