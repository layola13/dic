constitutional:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənl/ (UK), /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənl/ (US)| adj.|宪法的；章程的；体质的；本质的|Relating to a constitution, which is a body of fundamental principles or established precedents according to which a state or other organization is acknowledged to be governed.|The constitutional right to free speech is highly valued. 言论自由的宪法权利非常重要.|Synonyms: inherent, essential, fundamental, intrinsic, innate|Antonyms: unconstitutional, illegal, unlawful|adjective

constitutional:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənl/ (UK), /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənl/ (US)| n.|保健散步|A walk taken for one's health.|She takes a constitutional every morning in the park. 她每天早上都在公园里散步以保持健康.|Synonyms: stroll, walk, promenade|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the noun form)|noun
