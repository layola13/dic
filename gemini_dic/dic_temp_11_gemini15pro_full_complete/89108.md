homalographic:/ˌhɒməlɒˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌhoʊməlɒˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |同形异义的|Having the same spelling but different meanings and origins.|The words "bass" (a type of fish) and "bass" (a low voice) are homalographic.|近义词:homonymous, homographic|反义词:heterographic|词性:adjective
