hubris: /ˈhjuːbrɪs/ (US) /ˈhjuːbrɪs/ (UK)| n. |傲慢；狂妄自大；目中无人| Excessive pride or self-confidence, often leading to downfall.|  His hubris led him to believe he was untouchable. 他的傲慢使他相信自己是不可触碰的。|Example: The leader's hubris ultimately led to his downfall.  这位领导人的傲慢最终导致了他的垮台。|Synonyms: arrogance, vanity, conceit, pride, haughtiness|Antonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὕβρις (húbris), denoting excessive pride toward the gods.
