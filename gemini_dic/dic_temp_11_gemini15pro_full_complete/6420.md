amphitrocha:/ˈæmfɪˌtroʊkə/| n. |双毛轮幼虫|A free-swimming larval stage of certain mollusks and annelids, characterized by two bands of cilia, one preoral and one postoral.|The amphitrocha larva of the polychaete worm swims freely in the plankton.  多毛纲蠕虫的双毛轮幼虫在浮游生物中自由游动。|Larva, trochophore| - |Noun
Etymology: From Greek *ἀμφί (amphí, “around, on both sides”) + *τρόχος (tróchos, “wheel, hoop”).
