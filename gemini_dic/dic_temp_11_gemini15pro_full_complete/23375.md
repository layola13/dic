brachioganoid:/ˌbrækiˈoʊɡənɔɪd/
adjective
腕鳍鱼纲的
Relating to or denoting Brachioganoidei, an extinct subclass of ray-finned fishes from the Paleozoic and Mesozoic eras, characterized by thick, bony scales and a heterocercal tail.
Brachioganoids were a successful group of fishes that lived in both freshwater and marine environments. 腕鳍鱼纲是一群成功的鱼类，生活在淡水和海洋环境中。
The museum displayed a fossil of a brachioganoid fish. 博物馆展出了一块腕鳍鱼纲鱼类的化石。
近义词:ganoid (广义上的硬鳞鱼)
