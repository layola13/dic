denationalization:/ˌdiːˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 国有化取消; 私有化 |The process of transferring an industry or assets from public to private ownership.  The government is pursuing a policy of denationalization. 政府正在推行私有化政策。|synonyms: privatization, divestiture|antonyms: nationalization, state ownership|noun
