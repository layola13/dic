cornucopia:/ˌkɔːrnjəˈkoʊpiə/ (US) /ˌkɔːnjuˈkəʊpiə/ (UK)| n. |丰饶之角；大量；丰富|A symbol of abundance and nourishment, often depicted as a goat's horn overflowing with fruits, flowers, and grains.  Figuratively, it represents a plentiful supply of something.|The Thanksgiving table was a cornucopia of delicious food. 感恩节的餐桌上摆满了美味佳肴。|Synonyms: abundance, profusion, plethora, bounty|Antonyms: scarcity, dearth, lack, paucity|noun
