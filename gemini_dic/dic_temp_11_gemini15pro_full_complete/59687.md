echolocation:/ˌekəʊləʊˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |回声定位，回声探测|The bat uses echolocation to navigate in the dark.  蝙蝠利用回声定位在黑暗中导航。|例句：Bats use echolocation to find their prey. 蝙蝠利用回声定位来寻找猎物。

例句：Dolphins also use echolocation to navigate and hunt. 海豚也使用回声定位来导航和捕猎。|近义词：sonar, biosonar |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From echo + location. Echo from Latin ēchō "echo," from Greek  ēkhō "sound, noise; echo." Location from Old French locacion and directly from Latin locātiō, from locāre "to place," from locus "a place."
