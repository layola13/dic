hydropical:/haɪˈdrɒpɪkəl/ (US) /haɪˈdrɑːpɪkl/| adj. |水肿的，积水的|Suffering from, or characteristic of, dropsy; resembling dropsy; dropsical.  Relating to or affected with edema.|The patient presented with hydropical limbs, a clear sign of advanced heart failure.| 近义词: edematous, dropsical| 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "healthy," "normal," or terms describing the absence of swelling) |词性:形容词
