fellowshiped:/ˈfɛləʊʃɪpt/ | v. |结交, 赋予奖学金| To have granted a fellowship to someone. |例句：The university fellowshiped several promising young scientists. 这所大学为几位有前途的年轻科学家提供了奖学金。

例句：He was fellowshiped by the Royal Society. 他被皇家学会授予了会员资格。|近义词：granted, awarded, endowed |反义词: revoked, withdrawn |词性: 动词

词源: From "fellowship" + "-ed".  "Fellowship" comes from Old English "fēolagscipe", meaning "companionship, partnership".
