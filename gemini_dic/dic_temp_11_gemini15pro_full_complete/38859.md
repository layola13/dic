confederated:/kənˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪd/| adj.|联合的，同盟的|Joined together by an agreement or treaty, especially of states or nations.|The confederated tribes held a council to discuss trade agreements. 联合部落召开会议讨论贸易协定。|近义词：allied, federated, united|反义词:independent, separate|adjective

confederate:/kənˈfɛdərɪt/ |n.| 同盟者，同伙|A person, group, or nation that joins with another for a common purpose; an ally.|He was a confederate in the robbery. 他是抢劫案的同伙。|近义词：accomplice, ally, partner|反义词:enemy, opponent|noun

confederate:/kənˈfɛdəˌreɪt/ |v.| 结盟，联合|To form an alliance or confederation.|The smaller states confederated to form a stronger political unit. 较小的州联合起来组成了一个更强大的政治实体。|近义词：ally, unite, join forces|反义词:separate, divide|verb
