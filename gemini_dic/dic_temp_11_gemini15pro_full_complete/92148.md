hypersensually:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnʃuəli/
adverb
过度感性地，极度感性地
in a hypersensual manner; excessively or acutely sensitive, especially in regards to physical sensations.
She described the scene hypersensually, focusing on the textures and scents. 她极度感性地描述了那个场景，着重描写了质感和气味。
近义词: excessively sensually, acutely sensually
反义词: insensitively, apathetically
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + sensually (from sensual + -ly).  The root word "sensual" comes from Late Latin "sensualis," meaning "of the senses," derived from Latin "sensus," meaning "sense, feeling, perception."
