nonsubstituted:/ˌnɒnˈsʌbstɪtʃːʊːtɪd/| adj.|未取代的，未被取代的|Not substituted; having no substitutions made.  Specifically referring to a chemical compound where none of the hydrogen atoms or other functional groups have been replaced by another atom or group.|The reaction yielded a nonsubstituted benzene ring. 该反应产生了一个未取代的苯环。|unsubstituted, original, pure|substituted, replaced|adjective
