nonsubmissible:/ˌnɒnsʌbˈmɪsəbl/
adjective
不可提交的，不能提交的
Not capable of being submitted.  This refers to something that, due to its nature or rules and regulations, cannot be presented for consideration, approval, or judgment.
The incomplete application was deemed nonsubmissible.  该不完整的申请被认为不可提交。
Due to its classified nature, the document is nonsubmissible to the public. 由于其机密性质，该文件不可向公众提交。
近义词:unsubmittable, ineligible
反义词:submittable, eligible
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  非，相反的
submit (verb): to present something for consideration, approval, or judgment  提交
-ible (suffix): capable of being  可以...的
