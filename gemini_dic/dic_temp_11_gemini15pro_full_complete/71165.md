fissiparous:/fɪˈsɪpərəs/| adj.|分裂生殖的；易分裂的|Reproducing by fission; inclined to cause or undergo division into separate parts or groups.|例句：Certain bacteria are fissiparous, multiplying by splitting into two. 某些细菌是分裂生殖的，通过分裂成两个来繁殖。

例句：The fissiparous nature of the political party led to its eventual downfall.  这个政党的易分裂性质导致了它最终的垮台。|近义词：divisible, separable, schismatic|反义词：unitary, cohesive, indivisible|词性:形容词

fissiparity:/ˌfɪsɪˈpærɪti/| n.|分裂生殖|Reproduction by fission, the division of a cell or body into two or more parts.|例句：Fissiparity is a common method of asexual reproduction in single-celled organisms. 分裂生殖是单细胞生物中无性繁殖的常见方法。

例句：The biologist studied fissiparity in amoebas.  生物学家研究了变形虫的分裂生殖。|近义词：fission, schizogony|反义词：sexual reproduction|词性:名词

fissipara:/fɪˈsɪpərə/| n.|分裂生殖的生物|An organism that reproduces by fission.|例句：Amoebas are fissipara, reproducing by dividing into two. 变形虫是分裂生殖的生物，通过分裂成两个来繁殖。


例句：The study focused on the life cycle of the fissipara. 这项研究的重点是分裂生殖生物的生命周期。|近义词：fissiparous organism|反义词：sexually reproducing organism|词性:名词
