outbaked:/ˌaʊtˈbeɪkt/
verb
烘烤得比…更熟；在烘烤方面胜过
To bake longer or more thoroughly than.
She outbaked her competitor in the pie contest. 她在馅饼比赛中烘烤得比她的对手更熟练。
outdo, surpass
be outbaked by
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + baked, past participle of bake.
