drawer:/ˈdrɔːr (UK), ˈdrɔːər (US)| n. |抽屉；开票人，出票人|A box-like container, without a top, that slides in and out of a piece of furniture, such as a desk, chest, or bureau, and is used for storing items.  A person who draws or issues a bill of exchange or check. |例句：I keep my socks in the top drawer. 我把袜子放在最上面的抽屉里。

例句：The drawer of the check was a local businessman.  这张支票的出票人是当地的一位商人。

|近义词：compartment, tray (for the container sense); issuer (for the person sense)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
