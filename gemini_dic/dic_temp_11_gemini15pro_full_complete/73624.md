foreword:/ˈfɔːrwɜːrd/| n.|前言，序言|An introductory note or preface, especially in a book, typically written by someone other than the author.|The foreword of the book was written by a renowned scholar. 这本书的前言是由一位著名学者撰写的。|近义词：preface, introduction, prologue|反义词：afterword, epilogue|noun
