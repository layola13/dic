anthropidae:/ˌænθrəˈpaɪdiː/
n.
人科动物
The family of primates including humans and great apes.
Humans, gorillas, chimpanzees, and orangutans are all members of the Anthropidae family.  人类、大猩猩、黑猩猩和猩猩都是人科动物。
近义词:hominids
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider other animal families like Felidae (cats))
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + -idae (family suffix).
