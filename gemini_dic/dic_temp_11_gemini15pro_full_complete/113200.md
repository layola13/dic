lurker:/ˈlɜːrkər/ (US), /ˈlɜːkə/ (UK)| n. | 潜伏者，窥探者 | Someone who reads discussions in an online forum or social media group without participating.  A person who waits hidden in order to attack or do something harmful. |  He was a forum lurker for months before finally creating an account. 他在论坛潜水了几个月，最终才创建了一个帐户。

The police arrested a lurker who had been watching children in the park. 警察逮捕了一个在公园里偷窥小孩的潜伏者。 | 近义词：observer, onlooker, creeper, stalker (more negative) | 反义词：participant, contributor | 词性：名词
