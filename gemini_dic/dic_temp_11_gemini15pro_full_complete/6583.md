anacamptics:/ˌænəˈkæmptɪks/ | n. |回声学；共鸣学|The study of the reflection of sound waves, especially in enclosed spaces.  The science that deals with the reflection of sound and the production of echoes.| The architect had to consider the anacamptics of the concert hall to ensure good acoustics.  这位建筑师必须考虑音乐厅的回声效果以确保良好的音响效果。|Synonyms:  acoustics, echolocation | Antonyms: sound absorption| noun
