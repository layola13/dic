evacuatory:/ɪˈvækjʊətɔːri/
adjective
排泄的; 疏散的
Relating to or used for evacuation, especially of waste matter from the body or of people from a dangerous place.
The evacuatory system of the building was efficient.  这栋建筑的疏散系统很有效率。
The nurse administered an evacuatory enema.  护士进行了排泄灌肠。
近义词: purgative, cleansing, excretory
反义词: retentive
词性: adjective
