hurly-burly:/ˈhɜːrli ˈbɜːrli/ /ˈhɝːli ˈbɝːli/
noun
喧闹；骚动；混乱
A state of commotion and confusion; a noisy and disorderly situation.
The marketplace was a hurly-burly of sights and sounds.  市场里一片喧闹嘈杂。
Synonyms: commotion, tumult, uproar, chaos, bedlam
Antonyms: peace, quiet, tranquility, order, calm
词性: 名词
