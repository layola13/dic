cumulatist:/ˈkjuːmjʊlətɪst/ | n. |积雨云学家，堆积论者|A meteorologist specializing in the study of cumulus clouds, or one who believes in the theory of cumulation. |例句：The cumulatist predicted heavy rainfall based on the formation of towering cumulonimbus clouds. 积雨云学家根据高耸的积雨云的形成预测了强降雨。

例句：The cumulatist argued that small changes over time can lead to significant transformations. 堆积论者认为，随着时间的推移，小的变化会导致重大的转变。|近义词：meteorologist,  cloud physicist|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially someone who denies gradual change) |词性: noun
