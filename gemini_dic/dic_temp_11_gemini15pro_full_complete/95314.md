incautiously:/ɪnˈkɔːʃəsli/ | adv. | 不谨慎地，轻率地 |  Not cautious; without due care or attention. | He incautiously stepped onto the thin ice. 他不谨慎地踩上了薄冰。 | She incautiously revealed her secret. 她不谨慎地透露了她的秘密。 | 近义词: carelessly, recklessly, heedlessly, rashly | 反义词: cautiously, carefully, prudently | 词性: 副词
