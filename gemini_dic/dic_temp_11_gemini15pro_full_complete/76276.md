fyllot:/ˈfɪlət/ | n. | 醉汉; 酒鬼 | A habitual drunkard. | He staggered around like a fyllot. 他像个醉汉一样踉踉跄跄地走来走去。| 近义词: drunkard, alcoholic, inebriate | 反义词: teetotaler | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *filleth, *fyllithe (“drunkard”), equivalent to fill +‎ -eth, -ith (agent suffix). Cognate with Scots fylleth, fyllit (“drunkard”), Icelandic fyllir (“drunkard”).  More at fill, -eth.
