indenting:/ɪnˈdɛntɪŋ/ | n. | 缩进；缩排；锯齿状 | The process of creating indents in a text or other material.  Creating a space at the beginning of a line of text. |  The indenting of the first line of each paragraph makes the text easier to read.  每段第一行的缩进使文本更易于阅读。 | indentation, formatting | protrusion, extension | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "indent," which comes from the Old French "endent," meaning "tooth," referring to the tooth-like shape created by indenting.  Ultimately from Latin "dens," meaning "tooth."
