chakda:/ˈtʃɑːkdɑː/ | n. |投球手；(板球运动中)旋转球|A spin bowler in cricket, especially one who delivers the ball with a sharp spin. |Example 1: The chakda deceived the batsman with his clever spin.  这个投球手用他巧妙的旋转球骗过了击球手。

Example 2:  He's a skilled chakda, known for his tricky deliveries. 他是一位技术娴熟的旋转球投手，以其刁钻的投球而闻名。|Synonym: spin bowler, tweaker |Antonym: fast bowler |Part of Speech: noun

Etymology: From Hindi/Urdu चकड़ा/چکڑا (cakra/cakṛā), meaning "wheel" or "disk," referring to the spinning motion of the ball.
