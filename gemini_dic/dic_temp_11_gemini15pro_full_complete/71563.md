flashed:/flæʃt/ | v. | 闪光，闪现，掠过，（使）突然显露 | A light flashed in the distance. 远处闪过一道光。| gleam, glitter, flicker | fade, dim | verb
flashed:/flæʃt/ | adj. | 闪光的，快速的 |  a flashed smile  一闪而过的微笑 | gleaming, glittering | dull, matte | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flashen, from Old Norse *flasa (“to blaze, flash”), from Proto-Germanic *flaisōną (“to shine, blaze”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlei- (“to shine”). Cognate with Icelandic flasa (“to flicker, flare”), Norwegian flasa (“to blaze”), Swedish flasa (“to peel, scale”).
