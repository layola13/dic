fructify:/ˈfrʌktɪfaɪ/
verb
使结果实；使成功；使富有成效
to make fruitful or productive; to bear fruit; to come to fruition
例句：
The investment we made five years ago is finally beginning to fructify.
我们五年前做的投资终于开始有成果了。

His hard work and dedication finally fructified in a major scientific discovery.
他的辛勤工作和奉献最终促成了一项重大的科学发现。

近义词:  bear fruit, produce, yield, flourish, thrive, prosper
反义词:  fail, wither, stagnate
词性: verb
