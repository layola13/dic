omnidirectionally:/ˌɒmniˈdɪˈrekʃənəli/ | adv. | 全方位地；各方向地 |  In a manner that encompasses all directions; radiating outwards in every direction. | The security camera rotated omnidirectionally, capturing a 360-degree view of the area. 安全摄像头全方位旋转，捕捉了该区域的 360 度视图。 |  The antenna broadcasts omnidirectionally. 天线全方位广播。| Synonyms: spherically, radially, universally | Antonyms: unidirectionally, linearly | adverb

omni-directional:/ˌɒmniˈdɪˈrekʃənəl/ | adj. | 全向的；全方位的 | Capable of operating or receiving signals in all directions. |  An omnidirectional microphone picks up sound from all around. 全向麦克风可以接收来自周围的声音。 | The car's omnidirectional antenna ensured consistent radio reception. 汽车的全向天线确保了稳定的无线电接收。 | Synonyms: multidirectional, all-directional | Antonyms: directional, unidirectional | adjective
