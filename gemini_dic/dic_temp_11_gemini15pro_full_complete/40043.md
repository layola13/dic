consummating:/ˈkɑːnsəmˌeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɒnsəmˌeɪtɪŋ/
verb
完成；使完美；圆房
making (something) complete or perfect, especially by having sexual intercourse.
例句：They consummated their marriage on their honeymoon. 他们在蜜月旅行中圆了房。

例句：The deal was consummated with a handshake.  这笔交易以握手告终。
近义词：complete, finalize, conclude, fulfill, seal, finish
反义词：begin, start, initiate, abandon
词性：v.
