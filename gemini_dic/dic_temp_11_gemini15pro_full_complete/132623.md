nondisarmament:/ˌnɒndɪsˈɑːməmənt/
n.
不裁军，不解除武装
The state of not disarming; a refusal or failure to disarm.
例句：The nondisarmament of certain groups poses a threat to regional stability.某些集团不解除武装对区域稳定构成威胁。

例句：Their continued nondisarmament led to increased tensions.他们持续的不裁军导致紧张局势加剧。
近义词：non-disarmament, armament, militarization
反义词：disarmament, demilitarization
词性：名词
