juniorship:/ˈdʒuːnjərʃɪp/| n.| 初级职位；资历浅的职位；低年级学生身份|The juniorship offers a great opportunity for career development. 这个初级职位为职业发展提供了绝佳的机会。|He accepted a juniorship at the law firm. 他接受了这家律师事务所的初级职位。|近义词：junior status, junior position, apprenticeship, traineeship|反义词：seniorship, senior status|词性: 名词

词源:  From "junior" + "-ship" (a suffix forming nouns of state or condition). "Junior" comes from the Latin "iunior" (younger).
