overcensoriousness:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɔːriəsnəs/| n. |过度审查，过度批评|The state of being excessively critical or censorious.  It implies a tendency to find fault and to judge harshly, often to an unreasonable degree.|His overcensoriousness stifled creativity in the team. 他的过度批评扼杀了团队的创造力。|近义词：hypercriticism, faultfinding, captiousness|反义词:tolerance, leniency, permissiveness|词性:noun

overcensorious:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɔːriəs/| adj. |过度审查的，过度批评的|Inclined to criticize or censor excessively; too critical.|Her overcensorious attitude made it difficult to have an open discussion. 她过度批评的态度使得难以进行公开讨论。|近义词：hypercritical, faultfinding, captious|反义词:tolerant, lenient, permissive|词性:adjective
