antiepilepsy:/ˌæntiˈɛpɪlɛpsi/| adj.|抗癫痫的|Used to describe drugs or treatments that prevent or reduce the frequency or severity of epileptic seizures.  It relates to preventing or controlling epilepsy.|例句：She takes antiepilepsy medication daily. 她每天服用抗癫痫药物。|近义词：anticonvulsant|反义词:proconvulsant|词性:形容词
antiepilepsy:/ˌæntiˈɛpɪlɛpsi/| n.|抗癫痫药|A medication or treatment used to control epilepsy.|例句：The doctor prescribed a new antiepilepsy for her. 医生给她开了一种新的抗癫痫药。|近义词：anticonvulsant|反义词:proconvulsant|词性:名词
