anointing:/əˈnɔɪntɪŋ/
n.
涂油，膏抹；恩膏；任命
The ceremonial act of anointing someone with oil, often as part of a religious ritual or ceremony. It can also refer to the appointment or designation of someone to a special position or role.
The king's anointing was a sacred ceremony. 国王的涂油礼是一个神圣的仪式。
The anointing of the new CEO took place last week. 新任首席执行官的任命仪式上周举行。
近义词: consecration, ordination, inauguration, appointment
反义词: dismissal, removal

anoint:/əˈnɔɪnt/
vt.
涂油于；（尤指宗教仪式上）抹油使神圣；任命，指定
To apply oil to someone, especially as part of a religious ceremony.  It can also mean to designate or choose someone for a special position or role.
The priest anointed the baby's forehead. 牧师在婴儿的额头上涂油。
He was anointed as the team's captain. 他被任命为队长。
近义词: consecrate, ordain, inaugurate, appoint
反义词: dismiss, remove
