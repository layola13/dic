inerudite:/ˌɪnɪˈruːdaɪt/ | adj. | 无学问的，不博学的 | Lacking knowledge or erudition; unlearned. |  He made some inerudite remarks about the complexities of quantum physics. 他对量子物理的复杂性发表了一些无知的言论。 | He gave an inerudite speech, full of basic errors. 他发表了一篇毫无学识的演讲，充满了基本的错误。 | 近义词: ignorant, uneducated, unlearned, illiterate, uninformed | 反义词: erudite, learned, scholarly, knowledgeable, educated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *ineruditus*, from *in-* (not) + *eruditus* (learned, educated).  *Eruditus* is the past participle of *erudire* (to educate, train), from *e-* (out) + *rudis* (rough, untrained).
