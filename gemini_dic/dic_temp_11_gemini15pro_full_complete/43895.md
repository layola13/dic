cretic:/ˈkriːtɪk/ | n. adj.| 长短短格；扬抑抑格的 | A metrical foot of three syllables, a long syllable followed by two short syllables (or stressed followed by two unstressed).  In classical quantitative meter, a long followed by two shorts; in accentual-syllabic verse, a stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables. |  The poem is written in cretic meter. 这首诗是用长短短格写的。 | Synonym: amphimacer (less common) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a metrical foot, perhaps trochee or iamb depending on context) | noun, adjective
