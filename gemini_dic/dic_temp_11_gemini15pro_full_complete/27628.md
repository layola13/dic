cancriform:/ˈkæŋkrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
蟹形的；像蟹的
Resembling a crab; having the form of a crab.
The cancriform nebula, though faint, is remarkably detailed through a telescope.  通过望远镜观察，蟹状星云虽然微弱，但却异常清晰。
The artist sculpted a cancriform creature emerging from the sea. 艺术家雕刻了一个从海里冒出来的蟹形生物。
近义词:crablike, crab-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing a contrasting shape, e.g., circular, linear)
词性: adjective
