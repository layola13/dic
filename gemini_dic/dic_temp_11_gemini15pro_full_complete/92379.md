hypocrateriform:/ˌhaɪpəˈkreɪtərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
漏斗状的，碟状的
Shaped like a shallow funnel; salverform.
The petals of the flower were hypocrateriform, flaring out from a narrow tube. 这朵花的花瓣呈漏斗状，从一根细管向外张开。
Near Synonyms: salverform, funnel-shaped, saucer-shaped
Antonyms: tubular, campanulate
词性: adjective
