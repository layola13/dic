disabuse:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːz/
verb
使醒悟，纠正，消除…的错误想法
to free someone from a mistaken belief
例句：He quickly disabused me of the notion that I could do the work easily. 他很快打消了我认为这项工作很容易做的念头。

例句：She was disabused of her romantic illusions. 她不再抱有浪漫的幻想。

近义词：undeceive, enlighten, correct, set straight
反义词：deceive, mislead, delude
词性：v.
