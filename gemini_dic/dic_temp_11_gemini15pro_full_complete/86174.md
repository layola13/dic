heartswelling:/ˈhɑːrtswɛlɪŋ/| adj.|令人心潮澎湃的，感人至深的|Causing a feeling of swelling emotion in one's chest, typically joy or pride.|Seeing my daughter graduate was a heartswelling moment. 看著女儿毕业是一个令人心潮澎湃的时刻。|近义词：moving, touching, heartwarming, uplifting|反义词：heartbreaking, depressing, saddening|词性:形容词
Etymology: heart + swelling.  "Swelling" in this context refers to an expansion of feeling, not a literal physical swelling.  The word evokes the sensation of the chest expanding with emotion.
