audible:/ˈɔːdəbl/ | adj. | 可听的 | Able to be heard. | Her whisper was barely audible. 她的低语几乎听不见。| 近义词：hearable, perceptible, distinct | 反义词：inaudible, faint, indistinct | 词性: adjective
