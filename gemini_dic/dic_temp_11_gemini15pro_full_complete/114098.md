magnify:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪ/| v. |放大，扩大，夸大|To make something appear larger than it is, especially with a lens or microscope; to increase the importance or effect of something.|例句：The lens magnified the image ten times. 镜头将图像放大了十倍。|近义词：enlarge, amplify, exaggerate, boost, intensify|反义词:diminish, reduce, minimize, decrease|词性:动词
magnify:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪ/| v. |放大，扩大，夸大|To increase the importance or effect of something.|例句：The media magnified the significance of the event. 媒体夸大了该事件的重要性。|近义词：enlarge, amplify, exaggerate, boost, intensify|反义词:diminish, reduce, minimize, decrease|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English magnifien, from Old French magnifier, from Latin magnificāre (“to extol, praise, make large”), from magnus (“great, large”) + -ficāre, combining form of facere (“to make, do”).
