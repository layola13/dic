corkscrewy: /ˈkɔːrkskruːi/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrkskruːwi/ (US)| adj.|螺旋形的，扭曲的|Shaped like a corkscrew; spiraling or twisting.|The old tree had a corkscrewy branch that reached out over the path. 这棵老树有一个螺旋状的树枝伸到小路上方。|The path up the mountain was corkscrewy and steep. 上山的路是螺旋形的，而且很陡峭。|近义词：spiral, twisted, winding, helical|反义词:straight, uncurled|词性:形容词
词源: From "corkscrew" (a tool for removing corks from bottles) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from "cork" + "screw".
