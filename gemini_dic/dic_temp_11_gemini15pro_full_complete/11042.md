arcarum:/ɑːrˈkɛərəm/ | n. | 秘事，秘密，奥秘 |  A Latin word meaning "of secrets" or "of mysteries." Often used in the plural, arcana, to refer to specialized or esoteric knowledge. | The arcana of ancient alchemy are still fascinating to many. 古代炼金术的奥秘至今仍令许多人着迷。 | synonyms: mysteries, secrets, esoteric knowledge | antonyms: common knowledge, open secrets | noun
