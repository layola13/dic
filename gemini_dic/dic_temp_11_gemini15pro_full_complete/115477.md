manumise:/ˈmænjʊmaɪz/ /ˈmænjuːmaɪz/ | vt. | 解放；释放（奴隶）| To release from slavery or servitude; to emancipate. |  The plantation owner finally manumised all his slaves.  种植园主最终释放了他所有的奴隶。

The government passed a law to manumise all indentured servants. 政府通过了一项法律，释放所有契约仆人。 | 近义词：emancipate, liberate, free, release | 反义词：enslave, imprison, confine | 词性：动词
