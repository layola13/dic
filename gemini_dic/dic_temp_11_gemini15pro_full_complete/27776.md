cannibalization:/ˌkænɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 食人，同类相食；(产品或服务的)自相残杀 | The act of eating the flesh of another individual of the same species. In business, it refers to a reduction in sales volume, sales revenue, or market share of one product as a result of the introduction of a new product by the same producer. | The introduction of the new model led to the cannibalization of sales of the older one.  推出新款导致老款的销量下降。 | Synonyms:  erosion, undermining, encroachment | Antonyms:  synergy, cooperation | noun


cannibalize:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/ | v. | 食人肉；拆用…的零件；损害…的利益 | To take parts from (something, such as a machine) in order to repair or build something else. In business, it refers to the negative impact of a company's new product on the sales performance of its existing and related products. |  They had to cannibalize parts from another plane to repair the damaged one.  他们不得不从另一架飞机上拆下零件来修理损坏的飞机。  | Synonyms:  strip, dismantle, salvage, usurp | Antonyms:  assemble, construct, build | verb
