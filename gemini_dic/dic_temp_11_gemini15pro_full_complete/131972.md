nonconversancies:/ˌnɒnkɒnvəˈsənsiz/ |n.| 非谈话，非交谈; 不善交际 |Lack of conversational skills or situations where conversation is limited or absent.  These nonconversancies can lead to feelings of isolation.|  The enforced nonconversancies of the monastery led to a sense of peace for some, but loneliness for others. | 近义词:  uncommunicativeness, silence, reticence | 反义词: conversations, communication, sociability | 词性: noun

Etymology:  Derived from "non-" (not) + "conversancy" (familiarity with something, especially through experience). "Conversancy"  comes from the late Middle English "conversaunt," meaning "familiar with," ultimately from the Latin "conversari" (to associate with, live with).
