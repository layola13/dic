decontaminate:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪt/
|v.|净化；清除污染；消毒|To remove or neutralize contaminants, making something safe to handle or use.|The workers decontaminated the area after the chemical spill. 工人们在化学品泄漏后对该区域进行了净化。|The hospital staff decontaminated the operating room. 医院工作人员对手术室进行了消毒。|sterilize, sanitize, purify, cleanse|contaminate, pollute|verb


decontaminated:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd/
|adj.|去污的；净化的；消毒的|Free from contamination; made safe by removing or neutralizing contaminants.|The decontaminated equipment was then ready for use. 去污后的设备就可以使用了。|The decontaminated water is safe to drink. 净化后的水可以安全饮用。|sterilized, sanitized, purified, cleansed|contaminated, polluted|adjective
