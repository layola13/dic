nonallied:/ˌnɒnəˈlaɪd/
adjective
非盟国的；不结盟的
Not allied; of or relating to nations that do not belong to a military alliance.
Many nonallied nations attended the conference on world peace. 许多不结盟国家参加了世界和平会议。
The nonallied movement played a significant role during the Cold War. 不结盟运动在冷战时期发挥了重要作用。
