inure:/ɪˈnjʊər/| v. |使习惯，使适应，（尤指）使习惯于（不愉快或困难的事）| to accustom someone to something, especially something unpleasant or difficult|例句：The hardships of army life inured the soldiers to discomfort. 军队生活的艰苦使士兵们习惯了不适。|例句：These children have been inured to violence. 这些孩子已经对暴力麻木了。|近义词：accustom, habituate, harden, toughen|反义词:sensitize, soften|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *enurer, inurer (“to accustom, use, exercise”), from en- (“in”) + user (“to use”), from Latin ūtor (“I use”).
