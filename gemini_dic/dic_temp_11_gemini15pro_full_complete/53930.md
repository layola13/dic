discourteous:/dɪsˈkɜːrtiəs/ | adj. | 不礼貌的，失礼的 | Showing a lack of courtesy; rude. |  His discourteous behavior offended many guests. 他的无礼行为冒犯了许多客人。 | Example:  It was discourteous of him to interrupt her while she was speaking.  他打断她说话很不礼貌。| Synonyms: rude, impolite, disrespectful, inconsiderate, uncivil | Antonyms: polite, courteous, respectful, considerate, civil | word property: adjective
