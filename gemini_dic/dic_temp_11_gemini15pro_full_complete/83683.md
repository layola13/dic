haggard: /ˈhæɡərd/ (US) /ˈhæɡəd/ (UK)| adj. |憔悴的，形容枯槁的|Looking exhausted and unwell, especially from lack of sleep or worry.|She looked haggard after her long journey. 她长途旅行后看起来很憔悴。|近义词：drawn, gaunt, exhausted, weary|反义词:healthy, vibrant, refreshed|词性:形容词
haggard: /ˈhæɡərd/ (US) /ˈhæɡəd/ (UK) |n.|野鹰|A wild hawk, especially a female peregrine falcon|The falconer trained the haggard to hunt.  这位猎鹰人训练这只野鹰去捕猎。|近义词:hawk, falcon|反义词:N/A (动物一般没有反义词)|词性:名词
