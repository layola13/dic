empires:/ˈempaɪərz/| n. |帝国；大企业|A major political unit having a territory of great extent or a number of territories or peoples under a single sovereign authority; especially : one having an emperor as chief of state.  A large and powerful business organization.|The Roman Empire was vast and powerful. 罗马帝国幅员辽阔，实力强大。|Synonyms: kingdom, realm, domain, dynasty|Antonyms: colony, dependency|noun
