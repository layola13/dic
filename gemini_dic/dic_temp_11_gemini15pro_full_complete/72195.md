floppy's:/ˈflɒpiz/| n.|软盘（的）|A floppy disk, or floppy, is a type of disk storage composed of a disk of thin and flexible magnetic storage medium, sealed in a rectangular plastic enclosure lined with fabric that removes dust particles.  软盘，是一种由薄而柔软的磁性存储介质组成的磁盘存储器，密封在一个衬有织物的矩形塑料外壳中，可以去除灰尘颗粒。|This game requires a floppy drive. 这个游戏需要一个软驱。|近义词：diskette|反义词:hard drive|词性:名词

floppy:/ˈflɒpi/| adj.|松软的，下垂的|hanging loosely;  limp. 松散地悬挂；柔软的。|The dog has floppy ears. 这只狗的耳朵耷拉着。|近义词：limp, droopy|反义词:stiff, firm|词性:形容词
