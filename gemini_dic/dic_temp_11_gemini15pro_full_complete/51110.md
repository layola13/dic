despicably:/dɪˈspɪkəbli/| adv.|卑鄙地，可鄙地|in a despicable manner; vilely.  Characterized by meanness or vileness.|He acted despicably towards his former friends. 他对以前的朋友的行为卑鄙龌龊。|近义词：contemptibly, shamefully, basely, vilely|反义词：honorably, nobly, admirably|词性: adverb
词源: From despicable + -ly.  Despicable comes from Late Latin *despicabilis, from Latin despicari "to look down on," from de- "down" + spicari "to look at."
