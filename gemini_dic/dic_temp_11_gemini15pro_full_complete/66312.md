excrescence:/ɪkˈskrɛsns/ /ɛkˈskrɛsns/| n. |赘生物，瘤，难看的东西|something that grows out of something else and is not wanted or necessary, or is considered ugly; an abnormal outgrowth|The statues were covered in unsightly excrescences of lichen. 雕像上覆盖着难看的青苔赘生物。|近义词：growth, protuberance, lump, outgrowth, wart|反义词: indentation, concavity|词性: noun

excrescence:/ɪkˈskrɛsns/ /ɛkˈskrɛsns/| n. |（不好的）突出物，多余物|something that develops on or comes from something else and is considered to be undesirable and unnecessary|Bureaucracy is seen by many as an excrescence on modern society. 许多人认为官僚主义是现代社会的多余物。|近义词：addition, appendage, superfluity|反义词: essential, necessity |词性: noun
