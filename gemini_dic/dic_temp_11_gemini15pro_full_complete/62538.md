enduro:/ɛnˈdjʊəroʊ/ /ɛnˈdjʊəroʊ/ | n. | 耐力赛，耐力摩托车比赛 | A long-distance race, especially for motorcycles or mountain bikes, typically over rough terrain. |  He competed in the Erzberg Rodeo enduro. 他参加了埃尔茨伯格耐力赛。| synonyms: endurance race, cross-country race | antonyms: sprint, short race | noun

enduro:/ɛnˈdjʊəroʊ/ /ɛnˈdjʊəroʊ/ | adj. | 耐力的，用于耐力赛的 | Designed or suitable for endurance racing. |  He rides an enduro motorcycle. 他骑一辆耐力摩托车。 | synonyms: endurance, long-distance | antonyms: sprint, short-distance | adjective
