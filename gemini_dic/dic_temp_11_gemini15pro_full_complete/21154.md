blizzard: /ˈblɪzərd/ | n. |暴风雪；大批；大量涌现|A severe snowstorm with high winds and low visibility. |  The blizzard made travel impossible. 暴风雪使得出行变得不可能。|  The city was hit by a blizzard last night. 昨晚这座城市遭受了暴风雪的袭击。| 近义词: snowstorm, whiteout | 反义词: sunshine, calm | 词性: 名词

blizzard: /ˈblɪzərd/ | v. |用暴风雪袭击；使眼花缭乱；大量发送 | To overwhelm with a large quantity of something. |  They were blizzarded with emails after the announcement.  公告发布后，他们被大量的电子邮件淹没了。 | The company blizzarded the market with new products.  这家公司向市场大量投放新产品。| 近义词: bombard, inundate, overwhelm | 反义词: trickle, dribble | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the American English word "blizzard," originally meaning a violent blow or a volley of gunfire, its meaning shifted to a snowstorm in the mid-19th century.  Possibly related to the German word "blitz," meaning lightning.
