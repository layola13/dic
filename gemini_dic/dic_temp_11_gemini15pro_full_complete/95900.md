incredulous:/ɪnˈkrɛdʒʊləs/| adj.|怀疑的，不相信的|Unwilling or unable to believe something.|例句：He looked at her with an incredulous expression. 他带着怀疑的表情看着她。|例句：She was incredulous that he could have done such a thing. 她不相信他会做出这种事。|近义词：disbelieving, skeptical, doubtful|反义词：credulous, believing, trusting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *incredulus*, from *in-* (not) + *credulus* (believing, trusting), from *credere* (to believe). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\ḱred-dʰeh₁-* (to put one's heart, to trust).
