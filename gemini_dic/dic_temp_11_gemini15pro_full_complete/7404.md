animatics:/ˌænɪˈmætɪks/ | n. | 动画样片，动态故事板 | A preliminary version of a film, produced by shooting successive sections of a storyboard and adding a soundtrack.  It's used to visualize the film and check the pacing and timing before full animation begins. | Example: The director reviewed the animatics to ensure the action sequences flowed smoothly. 导演审查了动画样片，以确保动作场面流畅。

Example:  Creating animatics helped the team identify potential problems with the story early on. 创建动画样片帮助团队及早发现故事中潜在的问题。 | Near synonyms: story reel, previsualization, Leica reel | Antonym: final animation | noun
