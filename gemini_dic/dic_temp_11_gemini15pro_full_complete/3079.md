aerometry:/ˌɛəˈrɒmɪtri/ | n. | 空气测量学；气体测量学 | The science of measuring the weight or density of air and other gases.  It involves the use of instruments like aerometers to determine properties like pressure, temperature, and humidity, which are essential in fields like meteorology and aviation. | Aerometry plays a vital role in understanding weather patterns. 空气测量学在理解天气模式方面起着至关重要的作用。

Aerometry is crucial for calculating the lift of aircraft.  空气测量学对于计算飞机的升力至关重要。 | 近义词: pneumatics, gasometry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek "ἀήρ" (aēr, "air") + "-metry" (meaning "measurement").
