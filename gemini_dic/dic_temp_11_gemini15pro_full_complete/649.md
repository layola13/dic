abridging:/əˈbrɪdʒɪŋ/
|v.| 缩短，删节|Reducing the length of a written work while preserving its basic content. |The editor is abridging the novel for younger readers. 编辑正在为年轻读者删节这本小说。|Shortening, condensing, cutting|Expanding, lengthening|verb

abridge:/əˈbrɪdʒ/
|v.| 缩短，删节|To shorten or condense (a written work) without losing the sense.|They abridge the original version of the play. 他们删节了该剧的原始版本。|Shorten, condense, curtail|Expand, lengthen|verb

abridged:/əˈbrɪdʒd/
|adj.|删节的，缩短的|Shortened in length, especially of a written work.|He read an abridged version of "War and Peace." 他读了《战争与和平》的删节版。|Shortened, condensed, abbreviated|Unabridged, complete, full|adjective
