damasking:/ˈdæməskɪŋ/ | n. |大马士革织物; 织物上的大马士革花纹;  (金属表面)镶嵌装饰|The process of producing a damask weave or pattern, or the pattern itself. It involves weaving a reversible figured fabric with a pattern visible because of the way light reflects from its surface. It can also refer to a method of decorating metal by inlaying different metals or etching a pattern. | The tablecloth had intricate damasking.  桌布上有复杂的大马士革花纹。| 近义词: brocade, tapestry | 反义词: plain weave | 词性: noun

damask:/ˈdæməsk/ | n. adj. |大马士革织物;  大马士革钢;  玫瑰色; |A rich, figured fabric of silk, linen, wool, cotton, or synthetic fibers, typically woven with a pattern visible because of the way light reflects from its surface. As an adjective, it can describe something made of this fabric or having a similar pattern or appearance.  It can also refer to a type of steel originally made in Damascus, known for its strength and patterns, or a deep pink color. |She wore a damask gown to the ball. 她穿着一件大马士革长袍去参加舞会。  | 近义词: brocade, tapestry | 反义词: plain weave | 词性: noun, adjective
