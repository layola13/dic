evaporate:/ɪˈvæpəreɪt/ (US), /ɪˈvæpəreɪt/ (UK)| v. |蒸发，挥发；消失，消散|To change from a liquid or solid state into vapor; to disappear; to vanish.|The water in the puddle evaporated in the sun. 水坑里的水在阳光下蒸发了。|近义词：vaporize, disappear, vanish, dissipate|反义词: condense, materialize, appear|词性:动词

evaporation:/ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃn/ (US), /ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |蒸发，挥发；消失，消散|The process of changing from a liquid or solid state into vapor.|Evaporation is an important part of the water cycle. 蒸发是水循环的重要组成部分。|近义词：vaporization, disappearance|反义词: condensation|词性:名词
