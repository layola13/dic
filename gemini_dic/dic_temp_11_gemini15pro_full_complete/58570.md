dulcet:/ˈdʌlsɪt/| adj.|悦耳的，甜美的|Pleasing to the ear; melodious; sweet and soothing.|Her dulcet tones calmed the crying baby. 她甜美的声音使哭闹的婴儿平静下来。|The dulcet sounds of the harp filled the room. 竖琴悦耳的声音充满了整个房间。|近义词：melodious, harmonious, sweet, soothing, euphonious|反义词：harsh, discordant, grating, strident|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *doucet*, from Old French *doucet*, diminutive of *dous* (“sweet”), from Latin *dulcis* (“sweet”).
