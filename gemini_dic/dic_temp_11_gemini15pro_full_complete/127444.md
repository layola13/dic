nebula:/ˈnebjələ/ (US), /ˈnɛbjʊlə/ (UK)| n.|星云|A cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter.|The Orion Nebula is a popular target for amateur astronomers.|Synonyms: cloud, haze, mist|Antonyms: clarity, clearness|noun

nebulae:/ˈnebjʊliː/ (plural)|n.|星云（复数）|Plural form of nebula.|Many nebulae can be seen through powerful telescopes.|Synonyms: clouds, hazes, mists|Antonyms: clarities, clearnesses|noun
