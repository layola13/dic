expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/ | v. | 解释；阐述；详细说明 | To explain or discuss something in detail. | The professor expounded on the theory of relativity. 教授详细阐述了相对论。|  He expounded his views on the subject at great length. 他详细阐述了他对这个问题的看法。| 近义词：explain, elaborate, clarify, interpret | 反义词：summarize, condense | 词性: 动词
