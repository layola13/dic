non-deduction:/ˌnɒn dɪˈdʌkʃən/ |n.| 非演绎，非推论 |The principle of non-deduction emphasizes empirical observation over logical inference. 非演绎原则强调经验观察重于逻辑推理。|  Non-deduction plays a crucial role in scientific discovery, where hypotheses are tested through experiments. 非演绎在科学发现中起着至关重要的作用，其中假设是通过实验来检验的。 | induction, empiricism | deduction, rationalism | noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
deduction (noun): from Latin "deductio" meaning "a leading down, inference"
