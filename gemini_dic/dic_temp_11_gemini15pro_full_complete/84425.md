hallucinate:/həˈluːsɪneɪt/ /həˈlusɪˌneɪt/| v. | 产生幻觉；胡思乱想 | To experience hallucinations, seeing, hearing, or feeling things that are not really there. |  He began to hallucinate after being lost in the desert for days. 他在沙漠里迷路多日后开始出现幻觉。

He was hallucinating from the high fever. 他因高烧而产生幻觉。

近义词：fantasize, imagine, envision, delude, trip
反义词：perceive, discern
词性：动词
