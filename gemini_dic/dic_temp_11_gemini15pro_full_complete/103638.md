jokenite:/ˈdʒoʊkəˌnaɪt/| n. |开顽笑的人；爱开玩笑的人|A jokenite is someone who enjoys telling jokes and making people laugh. They often have a playful and lighthearted personality.  | He's such a jokenite, always cracking jokes and making everyone smile. 他真是个爱开玩笑的人，总是讲笑话，让每个人都笑。

|例句：The jokenite kept the whole table entertained with his witty stories.  这个爱开玩笑的人用他机智的故事让整桌人都很开心。

|例句：She's a real jokenite, always trying to lighten the mood with a funny comment. 她真是个爱开玩笑的人，总是试图用有趣的评论来缓和气氛。
