outblundered:/ˌaʊtˈblʌndərd/
verb
犯了比...更大的错误，在失误方面胜过
Made bigger or more mistakes than someone else.
He outblundered his opponent in the chess match, making three crucial errors that cost him the game. 他在国际象棋比赛中犯的错误比对手更多，三个关键失误让他输掉了比赛。
Synonyms: outplayed (in a negative sense), surpassed in errors,  made more mistakes than
Antonyms: outmaneuvered, outsmarted, played better than
词性: 动词
