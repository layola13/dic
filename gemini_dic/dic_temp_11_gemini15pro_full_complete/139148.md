obeying:/əˈbeɪɪŋ/
v.
服从，遵守
Present participle of obey. The act of complying with or fulfilling the commands, requests, or rules of someone or something.
The children are obeying their teacher. 孩子们正在服从他们的老师。
Soldiers are expected to obey orders without question. 士兵应该无条件服从命令。

近义词: complying, adhering, following, observing, heeding
反义词: disobeying, defying, resisting, ignoring
词性: verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English obeien, from Old French obeir, from Latin oboedīre (“to obey, be obedient to, give ear to, listen to”), from ob- (“towards”) + audire (“to hear”).
