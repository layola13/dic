driveabilities:/ˌdraɪvəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可驾驶性，操控性能 | The plural form of driveability, referring to the qualities of a vehicle that make it easy and pleasant to drive.  This encompasses factors like acceleration, braking, steering, and overall handling. | This car's driveabilities are excellent, making it a joy to drive on winding roads. 这辆车的操控性能非常好，在蜿蜒的道路上驾驶是一种乐趣。|  The different models offer varying driveabilities to suit different driving styles. 不同的型号提供不同的驾驶性能，以适应不同的驾驶风格。 | 近义词：handling, performance, maneuverability | 反义词：undriveability (less common) | 词性: 名词
