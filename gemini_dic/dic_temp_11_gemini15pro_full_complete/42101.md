cotrustees:/ˌkoʊtrʌˈstiːz/
n.
共同受托人
Two or more people who are jointly responsible for managing property or assets on behalf of a beneficiary.
My sister and I are cotrustees of our father's estate. 我和我姐姐是我父亲遗产的共同受托人。
The cotrustees have a fiduciary duty to the beneficiaries. 共同受托人对受益人负有信托义务。
近义词: joint trustees
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) sole trustee
词性: noun
