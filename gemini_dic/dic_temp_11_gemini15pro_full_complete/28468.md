carburizes:/ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
渗碳，使增碳
To introduce carbon into (iron or steel) in order to harden it.
The blacksmith carburizes the steel to make it stronger. 铁匠对钢进行渗碳处理以增强其强度。
The process carburizes the surface of the metal.  这个过程使金属表面渗碳。
synonyms:carbonize, harden
antonyms:soften, weaken
词性:v.
