PHRASE->
The practice of declawing cats is controversial. 给猫去爪的做法备受争议。
Declawing a cat involves removing the claws and the last bone of each toe.  给猫去爪涉及移除爪子和每个脚趾的最后一节骨头。
Some countries have banned declawing cats.  一些国家已经禁止给猫去爪。
Many vets now advise against declawing cats.  现在许多兽医都建议不要给猫去爪。
There are alternatives to declawing, such as regular nail trimming and providing scratching posts. 去爪有一些替代方案，例如定期修剪指甲和提供猫抓板。
