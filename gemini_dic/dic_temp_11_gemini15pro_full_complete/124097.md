morselization:/ˌmɔːrsəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|摩尔斯电码化，将信息转换为摩尔斯电码|The morselization of the message allowed it to be transmitted over long distances.  将信息摩尔斯电码化使得它可以进行长距离传输。|Example: The secure communication system relied on the morselization of sensitive data.  这个安全通信系统依赖于对敏感数据的摩尔斯电码化。|Synonyms: encoding, ciphering|Antonyms: decoding, deciphering|Noun

morselize:/ˈmɔːrsəlaɪz/ |v.|摩尔斯电码化，将…转换成摩尔斯电码|They morselized the message before sending it. 他们在发送之前将信息转换成摩尔斯电码。|Example:  He quickly morselized the coordinates of their location. 他迅速将他们所在位置的坐标转换成摩尔斯电码。|Synonyms: encode, cipher|Antonyms: decode, decipher|Verb
