castigate:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪt/
verb
严厉批评；谴责
to criticize someone or something severely
The teacher castigated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师严厉批评了考试作弊的学生。
rebuke, reprimand, censure
praise, commend
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin castigatus, past participle of castigare (“to correct, chastise”), from castus (“pure”) + agere (“to drive, lead, do”).
