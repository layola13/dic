construer:/kənˈstruːər/| v.|解释，理解，分析|To interpret or understand the meaning of something, especially a piece of writing or an action.|例句：He construed her silence as a sign of agreement. 他把她的沉默理解为同意。

例句：The lawyer construed the contract in favor of his client. 律师对合同作出了有利于其客户的解释。

|近义词：interpret, understand, analyze, decipher, explain|反义词：misinterpret, misunderstand|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old French construere, from Latin construere  "to heap up, build, construct, arrange, compile," from com- "together" (see com-) + struere "to pile, build."  The meaning shifted from literal building to building an interpretation.
