airheads:/ˈɛrˌhɛdz/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋；缺心眼的人|A silly or stupid person; someone who is not thinking clearly or intelligently.|例句：Those airheads forgot to bring the map. 那些傻瓜忘了带地图了。

例句：Don't be such an airhead! 别这么傻！|近义词：ditz, scatterbrain, space cadet, bimbo (often offensive), idiot, fool|反义词：genius, intellectual, brainiac|词性:noun
