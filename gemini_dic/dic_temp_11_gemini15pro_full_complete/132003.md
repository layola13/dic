noncorporeal:/ˌnɒnkɔːˈpɔːriəl/
adjective
非物质的；无形的；精神上的
Not having a physical body or form; intangible or spiritual.
例句：Ghosts are often described as noncorporeal beings.  鬼魂常被描述为非物质的存在。

例句：The noncorporeal nature of thoughts makes them difficult to study scientifically.  思想的非物质性使其难以用科学方法研究。

近义词：immaterial, intangible, spiritual, disembodied, ethereal
反义词：corporeal, physical, tangible, material
词性：adjective
