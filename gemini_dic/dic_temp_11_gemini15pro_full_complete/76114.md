furthermore:/ˈfɜːrðərˌmɔːr/| adv.|此外，而且|In addition to what has already been said; moreover.  It introduces a new point that strengthens the argument or adds further information. |She's an excellent athlete; furthermore, she's a brilliant student. 她是一位优秀的运动员；此外，她还是一位才华横溢的学生。|Furthermore, the evidence clearly points to his guilt. 而且，证据清楚地表明他有罪。|近义词：moreover, in addition, besides, also, what's more|反义词:however, nevertheless, nonetheless|词性:副词
