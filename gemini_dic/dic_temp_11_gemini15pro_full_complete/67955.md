facilely:/ˈfæsɪlli/ /fəˈsɪlli/| adv.|容易地；流畅地；轻车熟路地|Easily; readily; fluently.  It describes something done with ease and smoothness, often implying superficiality or lack of depth.|He facilely dismissed their concerns, not truly addressing the issue. 他轻易地驳回了他们的担忧，并没有真正解决问题。|近义词：easily, readily, smoothly, effortlessly|反义词：laboriously, arduously, painstakingly|词性:副词
Etymology:  From Latin facilis "easy," from facere "to do, make."
