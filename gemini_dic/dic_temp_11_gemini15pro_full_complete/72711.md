focusers:/ˈfoʊkəsərz/| n.|聚焦者，专注者|People or things that help to concentrate attention or effort on a particular issue or task.  Those who can maintain concentration and avoid distractions.|The focusers in the group kept the discussion on track.  小组中的专注者让讨论保持在正轨上。|近义词：concentrators, specialists, experts|反义词:distracted people, procrastinators|noun
