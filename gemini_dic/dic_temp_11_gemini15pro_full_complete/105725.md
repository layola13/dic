kinswomen:/ˈkɪnzˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女性亲属；女眷
Female relatives; women related by blood or marriage.
She invited all her kinswomen to the wedding. 她邀请了所有的女性亲属参加婚礼。
My grandmother and her kinswomen used to gather every Sunday for tea. 我的祖母和她的女眷们过去每个星期天都会聚在一起喝茶。
近义词:relatives, family members
反义词:strangers
词性:名词
