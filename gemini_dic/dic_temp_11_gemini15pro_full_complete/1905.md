actually:/ˈæktʃuəli/ /ˈæktʃəli/
adv.
事实上，实际上
in fact or really
例句：He said he was busy, but actually he wasn't. 他说他很忙，但实际上他并不忙。

例句：I didn't actually see him, I just heard his voice. 我实际上并没有看到他，只是听到了他的声音。

近义词：really, in fact, truly, indeed
反义词：ideally, theoretically, supposedly
词性：副词
