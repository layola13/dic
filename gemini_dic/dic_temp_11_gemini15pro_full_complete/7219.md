anglicanize:/ˈæŋɡlɪkənaɪz/
verb
使英国化; 使英国国教化
To make or become Anglican in character or form; to conform to Anglican usages.
The missionaries attempted to anglicanize the native population.  传教士试图使当地居民英国国教化。
The church service was anglicanized to appeal to a broader audience. 教堂礼拜仪式被英国国教化，以吸引更广泛的信众。
近义词: anglicize
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: verb
