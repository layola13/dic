adamantine:/ˌæd.əˈmæn.taɪn/ | adj. | 坚硬无比的；固执的；金刚石制的 | Extremely hard; unyielding; made of or resembling adamant (a mythical, unbreakable substance). |  The warrior's adamantine shield deflected every blow.  战士坚不可摧的盾牌挡住了每一次打击。

The CEO's adamantine refusal to negotiate put the company at risk.  首席执行官坚决拒绝谈判，使公司面临风险。| 近义词：unbreakable, inflexible, rigid, impenetrable, resolute, firm | 反义词：breakable, fragile, flexible, yielding, soft | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adamantinus, from Latin adamas, adamantis (“adamant, hardest iron, diamond”), from Ancient Greek ἀδάμας (adámas, “untamed, invincible, diamond”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + δαμάζω (damázō, “I tame, subdue”).
