fishbowls:/ˈfɪʃboʊlz/
n.
鱼缸（复数）
A transparent container, typically made of glass or plastic, filled with water and used to keep fish and other aquatic animals.
The fishbowls in the restaurant were filled with colorful fish.  餐厅里的鱼缸里满是色彩斑斓的鱼。

fishbowls:/ˈfɪʃboʊlz/
n.
(比喻)受到密切关注的环境
A situation characterized by constant scrutiny and lack of privacy.
Working in a small town can feel like living in a fishbowl. 在一个小镇工作感觉就像生活在一个鱼缸里，时刻受到关注。


fishbowls:/ˈfɪʃboʊlz/
近义词: aquariums, tanks
反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑open water, ocean 开阔水域，海洋 来表示相对的概念)
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

fishbowls:/ˈfɪʃboʊlz/
词源: fish + bowl
"fish" 来自古英语 "fisc," 指的是鱼。
"bowl" 来自古英语 "bolla," 指的是碗。
两个词组合在一起，表示盛放鱼的碗状容器。
