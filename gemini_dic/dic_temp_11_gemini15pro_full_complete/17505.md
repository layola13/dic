beastification: /ˌbiːstɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
兽化；使成兽形
The process of making something or someone beast-like, either literally or figuratively.
The novel explores the beastification of the protagonist as he descends into madness. 这部小说探讨了主人公在陷入疯狂时逐渐兽化的过程。

The beastification of the once noble knight was a tragic sight. 曾经高贵的骑士的兽化是一个悲惨的景象。

近义词: brutalization, dehumanization
反义词: humanization, civilization
词性: noun
