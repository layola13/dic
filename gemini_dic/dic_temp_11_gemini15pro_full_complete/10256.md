apotheosizes:/əˈpɒθiːəˌsaɪzɪz/
|v.|神化，颂扬，理想化|To elevate to the rank of a god; to glorify or idealize.|The movie apotheosizes the struggles of the working class. 这部电影颂扬了工人阶级的奋斗。|synonyms: exalts, glorifies, deifies, idolizes|antonyms: denigrates, vilifies, disparages|verb

apotheosis:/əˌpɒθiːˈəʊsɪs/
|n.|神化，典范，巅峰|The highest point in the development of something; culmination or climax.|The Olympic Games represent the apotheosis of athletic achievement. 奥运会代表了体育成就的巅峰。|synonyms: culmination, climax, peak, pinnacle, zenith|antonyms: nadir, bottom, base|noun
