invalid: /ɪnˈvælɪd/ | adj. n. | 无效的；病弱的；残疾人 | not valid; having no force or effect; (of a person) chronically ill or disabled. |  The ticket is invalid after its expiration date.  这张票过了有效期就失效了。| 近义词：null, void, flawed, incorrect, unsound,  false, illegitimate,  disabled,  frail, sickly | 反义词：valid,  legitimate, sound,  healthy, robust | 词性: 形容词, 名词

invalid: /ˈɪnvəlɪd/ | vt. | 使伤残；使退役 | To remove from active service because of sickness or injury. | He was invalided out of the army after being injured in combat. 他在战斗中受伤后从军队退役。 |近义词：disable, cripple | 反义词：cure, heal | 词性: 动词


词源:
From Late Middle English invalide, from Old French invalide, from Latin invalidus (“weak, infirm”), from in- (“not”) + validus (“strong”).
