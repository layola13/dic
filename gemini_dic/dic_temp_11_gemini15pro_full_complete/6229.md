amnesty: /ˈæmnɪsti/ | n. | 大赦，特赦 | An official pardon for people who have been convicted of political offenses. |  The government granted amnesty to all political prisoners. 政府对所有政治犯给予了特赦。

The king declared an amnesty for all rebels. 国王宣布对所有反叛者进行特赦。 | 近义词: pardon, reprieve, forgiveness | 反义词: prosecution, punishment | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *amnestie*, from Latin *amnestia*, from Greek *amnēstía* "forgetfulness, oblivion," from *amnēstos* "forgotten, unmentioned," from *a-* "not" + *mnestis* "remembrance, memory."
