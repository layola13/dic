adytum: /ˈædɪtəm/ (US), /ˈædɪtəm/ (UK)| n. | 圣所，内殿 |The innermost part of a temple or sanctuary, especially the most sacred part, accessible only to priests or initiates. |  The high priest entered the adytum to consult the oracle. 大祭司进入内殿咨询神谕。

The adytum held the statue of the deity. 内殿里供奉着神像。|  sanctuary, inner sanctum, holy of holies |  vestibule, entrance | noun
