antigambling: /ˌæntiˈgæmblɪŋ/
adjective
反对赌博的；禁止赌博的
Opposed to gambling; designed to discourage or prevent gambling.
The government introduced new antigambling legislation. 政府出台了新的反赌博立法。
近义词: against gambling
反义词: pro-gambling
词性: adjective

antigambling: /ˌæntiˈgæmblɪŋ/
noun
反赌博
Opposition to gambling.
Antigambling sentiment is growing in the community.  社区的反赌博情绪日益高涨。
近义词: opposition to gambling
反义词: support for gambling
词性: noun


词源:  "anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "gambling" (from the verb "gamble")
