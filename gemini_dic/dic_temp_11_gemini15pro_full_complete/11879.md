arrogances: /ˈærəɡənsɪz/
| n. |傲慢；自大|The quality of being arrogant; an instance of this.|His arrogance alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢疏远了他的同事。|近义词: haughtiness, pride, conceit, superciliousness, pomposity|反义词: humility, modesty, humbleness|词性: 名词 (plural)
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French arrogance, from Latin arrogantia, from arrogant- ‘claiming for oneself’, from the verb arrogare.
