demographically:/ˌdeməˈɡræfɪkli/
|adv.|
|人口统计学地；就人口统计而言|
|Relating to the structure of populations.|
|The neighborhood is demographically diverse, with people from all walks of life. 这个社区在人口结构方面很多元化，来自各行各业的人都有。|

|The city is demographically similar to many other midwestern cities. 这座城市在人口统计方面与许多其他中西部城市相似。|

|Synonym: statistically, population-wise|
|Antonym: N/A|
|词性: adverb (副词)|


ETYMOLOGY->
From demography + -ically.  Demography comes from French *démographie*, from Ancient Greek *dēmos*  (people) +  *-graphia* (writing, description).
