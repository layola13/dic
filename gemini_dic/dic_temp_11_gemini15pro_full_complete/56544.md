dominator:/ˈdɒmɪˌneɪtə(r)/ /ˈdɑːmɪˌneɪtər/ | n. |支配者，统治者，控制者|A person or group that dominates or controls another.|The Roman Empire was a major dominator of the ancient world. 罗马帝国是古代世界的主要统治者。|Synonyms: ruler, controller, master, lord, tyrant|Antonyms: subject, subordinate, follower|noun

dominator:/ˈdɒmɪˌneɪtə(r)/ /ˈdɑːmɪˌneɪtər/| n. |优势者，领先者|A person, team, or country that is clearly superior to others in a competition or field.|The company is a dominator in the software market. 这家公司是软件市场的领先者。|Synonyms: leader, champion, victor, winner|Antonyms: loser, underdog|noun
