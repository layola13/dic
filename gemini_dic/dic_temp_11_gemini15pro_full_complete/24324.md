brilliantly:/ˈbrɪljəntli/| adv.|灿烂地，辉煌地，出色地|In a brilliant manner; with great skill or intelligence.|She played the piano brilliantly. 她钢琴弹得非常出色。|近义词：splendidly, superbly, magnificently, excellently|反义词：badly, poorly, terribly, awfully|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle French *brilliant*, from Italian brillante, present participle of brillare "to shine," from Vulgar Latin *berillare, alteration of Latin beryllus "beryl," from Greek βήρυλλος (beryllos).  The adverbial suffix -ly comes from Old English -līce.
