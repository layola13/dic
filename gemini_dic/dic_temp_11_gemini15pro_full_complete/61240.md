emanate:/ˈeməneɪt/| v. |发出，散发，起源于|To flow out from a source; to originate.|A sweet smell emanated from the bakery.  从面包店里飘出一股香味。|近义词：emit, issue, radiate, stem, spring|反义词:absorb, withdraw|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Latin emanare "to flow out," from ex- "out" + manare "to flow."
