The nonvitalness of the appendix is a well-known fact.  阑尾的不重要性是一个众所周知的事实。

词性: 名词

词源: non- + vital + -ness
non-:  prefix meaning "not"
vital:  adjective meaning "absolutely necessary or important; essential."
-ness: suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
