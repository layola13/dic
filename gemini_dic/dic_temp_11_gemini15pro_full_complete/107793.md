lappish:/ˈlæpɪʃ/| adj.|粗心大意的；笨拙的；愚蠢的|Lacking care or attention; clumsy or foolish.|He made a lappish mistake and forgot his passport. 他犯了一个粗心大意的错误，忘记了护照。|例句：Her lappish handling of the situation caused further problems. 她对局势的笨拙处理导致了更多问题。|近义词：careless, clumsy, awkward, foolish, negligent|反义词：careful, meticulous, attentive, skillful, intelligent|词性:形容词

词源: From Lapland + -ish.  Originally referred to the Sami people of Lapland, often stereotyped as simple or uncivilized. The meaning shifted to describe clumsy or foolish behavior.
