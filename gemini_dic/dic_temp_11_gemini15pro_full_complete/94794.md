impugning:/ɪmˈpjuːnɪŋ/
| v. | 质疑；抨击；非难 | Challenging the honesty or integrity of something or someone. | The lawyer is impugning the witness's testimony. 律师正在质疑证人的证词。|  近义词: challenging, questioning, disputing, attacking, contradicting | 反义词: supporting, upholding, defending, endorsing, confirming | 词性: 动词 (present participle of impugn)

impugn:/ɪmˈpjuːn/
| v. | 质疑；抨击；非难 |  To dispute the truth, validity, or honesty of (a statement or motive); call into question. |  She impugned his motives. 她质疑他的动机。| 近义词: challenge, question, dispute, attack, contradict | 反义词: support, uphold, defend, endorse, confirm | 词性: 动词

词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French empugner, from Latin impugnare ‘fight against’, from in- ‘against’ + pugnare ‘fight’.
