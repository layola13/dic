centrefold: /ˈsɛntəfəʊld/ (British) /ˈsɛntərfoʊld/ (American)
centrefold: n.|插页, 中央图片, 折页|A large photograph of a usually scantily clad person printed across two facing pages in the center of a magazine, especially one such photograph in a magazine for men.|The magazine's centrefold this month is a famous actress.|Synonym: playmate, pin-up|Antonym: None (lack of a direct antonym; could consider "cover model" as a contrasting term, depending on context)|Noun

centrefold: /ˈsɛntəfəʊld/ (British) /ˈsɛntərfoʊld/ (American)
centrefold: adj.|插页的，中央图片的|Relating to or featured as a centrefold.|He became famous as a centrefold model.|Synonym: None|Antonym: None|Adjective
