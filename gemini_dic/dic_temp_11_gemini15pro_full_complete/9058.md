antimerging:/ˌæntiˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 反合并的，防止合并的 | Preventing or opposing a merger.  This describes something that actively works against the combining of two or more entities, often companies. | The company adopted an antimerging strategy to maintain its independence.  该公司采取了反合并策略以保持其独立性。| The antimerging clause in the contract prevented the two companies from joining forces. 合同中的反合并条款阻止了两家公司合并。 | 近义词: anti-amalgamation, anti-consolidation | 反义词: merging, consolidating | 词性: adjective
