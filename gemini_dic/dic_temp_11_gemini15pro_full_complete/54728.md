disparageable:/dɪˈspærɪdʒəbəl/
adjective
可贬低的，可轻视的
Capable of being disparaged; belittled or spoken of negatively.
Her disparageable behavior led to her dismissal. 她的可贬低的行为导致她被解雇。
The politician made disparageable remarks about his opponent.  这位政客对他对手发表了贬低的言论。

synonyms: criticizable, denigrable, depreciable
antonyms: laudable, praiseworthy, commendable
word type: adjective
