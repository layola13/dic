anaesthetists: /əˈniːsθətɪsts/ (UK) /ænəsˈθiːtɪsts/ (US)| n.|麻醉师|A doctor who specializes in administering anesthetics (drugs that cause loss of sensation) before, during, and after surgery.|例句：The anaesthetists carefully monitored the patient's vital signs during the operation. 麻醉师在手术期间仔细监测病人的生命体征。

例句：The anaesthetists explained the risks of the procedure to the patient. 麻醉师向病人解释了手术的风险。

近义词：anesthesiologists|反义词: None|词性:Noun
