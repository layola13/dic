combusted:/kəmˈbʌstɪd/

|adj.|已燃烧的|burned. Describes something that has been burned.|The combusted remains of the building were still smoldering.  这栋建筑物烧毁后的残骸仍在冒烟。|Synonyms: burned, charred, incinerated|Antonyms: unburned, intact|Adjective
