handhold:/ˈhændhəʊld/ (UK) /ˈhændhoʊld/ (US)| n.|支撑物；帮助；搀扶|Something that provides support or assistance, especially when climbing or walking on uneven ground.  A means of support or encouragement in a difficult situation.|例句：The climber used the handholds in the rock face to pull himself up. 攀岩者利用岩壁上的支撑点把自己拉上去。

例句：She offered him a handhold as they crossed the stream.  他们过河时，她扶了他一把。|近义词：support, assistance, aid, help, grip|反义词:hindrance, obstacle|词性:noun


handhold:/ˈhændhəʊld/ (UK) /ˈhændhoʊld/ (US)| adj.|手持的；便携式的|Designed to be held in the hand; portable.|例句：A handhold GPS device. 一个手持GPS设备。

例句：Handhold vacuum cleaner. 手持式吸尘器。|近义词：portable, handheld|反义词:stationary, fixed|词性:adjective
