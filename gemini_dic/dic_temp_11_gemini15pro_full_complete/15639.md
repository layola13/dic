baited:/ˈbeɪtɪd/| adj.|用饵引诱的；激怒的，受刺激的|furnished with bait; deliberately annoyed or taunted|The fisherman cast his baited line into the water. 渔夫把挂着诱饵的鱼线抛入水中。|The baited bull charged at the matador.  被激怒的公牛向斗牛士冲锋。|近义词:trapped, lured, provoked, goaded|反义词:unbaited, calm, soothed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bait (n.) + -ed.  The word "bait" itself comes from Old Norse "beita" meaning "to cause to bite," related to "bíta" meaning "to bite."
