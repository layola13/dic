nonabstractness:/ˌnɒnæbˈstræktnɪs/
n.|非抽象性；具体性|The quality of being nonabstract; concreteness.  The state of being specific and tangible, rather than theoretical or conceptual.|The nonabstractness of the evidence made the case easy to solve. 证据的具体性使得案件很容易解决。|concreteness, specificity, tangibility|abstractness, generality, vagueness|noun
