monk:/mʌŋk/| n. |僧侣，修道士|A member of a religious community of men typically living under vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience.|The monk spent his days in prayer and contemplation.  这位僧侣终日祈祷和冥想。|近义词：friar, brother|反义词: layman|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *munuc, from Proto-Germanic *munikaz, from Late Latin monachus, from Greek monakhos "single, solitary, living alone,"  from monos "alone."  Originally referring to hermits, later extended to members of communal monastic orders.
