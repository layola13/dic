what: /wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 |

英文解析:  "What" is a versatile word used as a pronoun, determiner, and adverb. As a pronoun, it asks for information about something. As a determiner, it modifies a noun to ask which thing or things are meant. As an adverb, it can emphasize degree or quantity.

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚饭想吃什么？

What a beautiful day! = 多么美好的一天！
What interesting books you have! = 你拥有多么有趣的书籍！
