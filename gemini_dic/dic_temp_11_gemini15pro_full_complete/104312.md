justle:/ˈdʒʌsəl/| v. n.|推挤，挤开；竞争；撞|To push or shove against someone or something, often in a crowd; to compete or struggle with others for something.|例句：
The passengers jostled each other to get off the train.
乘客们互相推挤着下火车。

He jostled his way through the crowd.
他挤过人群。

Commuters jostled for seats on the bus.
通勤者在公交车上争抢座位。

The company is jostling for position in the competitive market.
这家公司正在竞争激烈的市场中争夺地位。|近义词：shove, push, elbow, bump, contend, compete|反义词：yield, give way|词性:动词, 名词
