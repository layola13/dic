importunable:/ɪmˈpɔːrtənəbəl/ | adj. | 不可输入的，无法进口的 | Not able to be imported; not allowed to be imported. |  The importunable goods were confiscated by customs.  这些无法进口的货物被海关没收了。

The country's strict regulations made certain products importunable.  该国严格的法规使得某些产品无法进口。 | 近义词: non-importable, inadmissible | 反义词: importable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "importunable" is derived from "import" + "-able" + "un-".  "Import" comes from the Latin "importare," meaning "to bring in."  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being." "Un-" is a prefix meaning "not." Therefore, "importunable" literally means "not capable of being imported."
