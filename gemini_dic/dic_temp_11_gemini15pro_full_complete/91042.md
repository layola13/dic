husky:/ˈhʌski/ (US), /ˈhʌski/ (UK)| adj. n.|沙哑的，强壮的；哈士奇犬|A husky voice is deep and rough, often because of a sore throat.  A husky person is big and strong.  A husky is a type of dog.|例句：She spoke in a husky whisper. 她用沙哑的低语说话。|近义词：hoarse, deep, strong, robust, burly|反义词:clear, weak, frail|词性:形容词

husky:/ˈhʌski/ (US), /ˈhʌski/ (UK)| n.|哈士奇犬|A husky is a breed of dog known for its thick fur and ability to pull sleds.|例句：My husky loves to play in the snow. 我的哈士奇喜欢在雪地里玩耍。|近义词：sled dog|反义词: (No direct antonym for a breed of dog)|词性:名词

词源: From the obsolete husk (meaning "Inuit") + -y. Ultimately from Algonquian.
