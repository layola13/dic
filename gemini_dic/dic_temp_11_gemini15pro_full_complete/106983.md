laconian: /ləˈkɒnɪk/ (UK) /ləˈkɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.| 简洁的；简短的；言简意赅的|  Of or relating to Laconia (ancient Sparta) or its inhabitants, especially in their reputed brevity of speech.  Characterized by the use of few words; terse; concise.| The CEO's laconian response to the journalist's question spoke volumes. 首席执行官对记者问题的简洁回答意味深长。|Synonyms: terse, concise, brief, succinct, pithy|Antonyms: verbose, prolix, wordy, loquacious|adjective

laconic: /ləˈkɒnɪk/ (UK) /ləˈkɑːnɪk/ (US)| n.|  A person of few words; a laconic speaker.| He was a laconic, even taciturn man. 他是个沉默寡言，甚至可以说是 taciturn 的人。|Synonyms:  taciturn person, reticent person|Antonyms:  loquacious person, garrulous person|noun
