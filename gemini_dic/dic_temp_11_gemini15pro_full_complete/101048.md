inveiglements:/ɪnˈveɪɡlmənts/| n. |诱惑；诱骗；引诱|The act of enticing someone to do something, typically by deception or flattery.  The state of being enticed in this way.|He was led astray by the inveiglements of the city.|Synonyms: allurements, enticements, temptations, seductions|Antonyms: dissuasions, deterrents|noun
