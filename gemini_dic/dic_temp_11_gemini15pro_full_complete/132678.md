nondispersals:/ˌnɒndɪˈspɜːsəlz/
noun
不扩散，不分散
The nondispersal of seeds can lead to overcrowding. 种子不分散会导致过度拥挤。
nondispersal is the lack of dispersal, or the failure of organisms or propagules to spread or distribute from one place to another.
The nondispersal of the refugees led to a concentrated crisis in the border town.  难民的不分散导致边境城镇出现集中危机。
Near synonyms: localization, concentration, containment
Antonyms: dispersal, scattering, dissemination
词性: 名词
