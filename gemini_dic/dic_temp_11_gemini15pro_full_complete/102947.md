jazziness:/ˈdʒæzɪnɪs/
noun
活力; 爵士乐的风格;
The quality of being jazzy; a lively, energetic, or exciting quality, often associated with jazz music.
Her performance was full of jazziness and pizzazz. 她的表演充满了活力和魅力。
The jazziness of the music made everyone want to dance.  音乐的活力让每个人都想跳舞。
liveliness, vibrancy, energy, spirit, pep
dullness, lifelessness, apathy
