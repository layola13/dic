overmilk:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlk/| v.|过度挤奶；产奶过多|To extract too much milk from (a cow, goat, etc.).  The farmer overmilked his cows, which led to health problems for the animals.|The farmer overmilked his prize cow. = 那位农民过度挤奶了他那头获奖的奶牛。|近义词: overproduce (milk)|反义词: undermilk|词性:动词

overmilk:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlk/| n.|过量的牛奶|An excessive amount of milk. There was so much overmilk that they had to throw some away. | There was so much overmilk from the dairy farm. = 奶牛场有多余的牛奶。|近义词: excess milk, surplus milk|反义词: milk shortage|词性:名词
