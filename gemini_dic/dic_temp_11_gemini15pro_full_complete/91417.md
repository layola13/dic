hyenaism:/ˌhaɪˈiːnɪɪzəm/
n.
鬣狗主义；卑鄙的行为；损人利己的行为
The behavior or practice of preying on the weak or unfortunate, like a hyena; rapaciousness, greed, or cruelty.
The dictator's regime was marked by hyenaism, with officials enriching themselves at the expense of the starving population.  独裁者的政权以鬣狗主义为标志，官员们以饥饿的民众为代价来充实自己。
His business practices bordered on hyenaism, exploiting loopholes and taking advantage of unsuspecting clients. 他的商业行为近乎鬣狗主义，利用漏洞，欺骗毫无戒心的客户。
近义词:rapacity, predation, exploitation, ruthlessness, cruelty
反义词:altruism, compassion, generosity, kindness
