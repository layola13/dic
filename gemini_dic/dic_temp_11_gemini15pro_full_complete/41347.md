corinthian: /kəˈrɪnθiən/ | adj. n. | 科林斯的；科林斯式的；放荡的人 | Of or relating to the ancient Greek city of Corinth, its people, or their style of architecture, especially its ornate order of columns.  A wealthy, luxury-loving person. |  例句：The Corinthian columns of the temple were magnificent.  寺庙的科林斯式圆柱雄伟壮观。| 近义词：ornate, elaborate, luxurious | 反义词：simple, plain, austere | 词性: 形容词, 名词

corinthian: /kəˈrɪnθiən/ | n. | 放荡的人 | A wealthy, luxury-loving, and often immoral person, especially a man. | 例句：He lived like a Corinthian, indulging in every pleasure. 他像个科林斯人一样生活，沉溺于各种享乐。 | 近义词：hedonist, libertine, profligate | 反义词：ascetic, puritan | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *Corinthius*, from Greek *Kórinthos* (Κόρινθος), the name of the city.
