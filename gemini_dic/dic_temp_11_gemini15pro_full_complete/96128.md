indemnifier:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪər/| n. |赔偿者，保障者|A person or entity who agrees to protect another from loss or damage.  A person or organization that promises to pay for any damage or loss caused by someone else.| The insurance company acted as the indemnifier in the case of the car accident. 保险公司在车祸案件中充当了赔偿者。|Synonyms: guarantor, surety, underwriter|Antonyms: indemnitee, claimant|noun

indemnify:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪ/| vt. |赔偿，保障|To compensate for loss or damage; to secure against future loss or damage.|The contract indemnifies the company against lawsuits.  合同保障公司免受诉讼。|Synonyms: compensate, reimburse, protect, secure|Antonyms: penalize, charge, fine|verb
