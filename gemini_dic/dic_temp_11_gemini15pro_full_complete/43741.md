creel:/kriːl/
| n.|鱼篓，钓鱼篓；（纺织）纱架|A wicker basket for carrying fish. A frame for holding bobbins or spools of yarn.|The fisherman carried a creel full of trout. 渔夫提着一个装满鳟鱼的鱼篓。|Synonyms: basket, hamper, container|Antonyms: None|Noun

creels:/kriːlz/
| n.|鱼篓（复数），钓鱼篓（复数）；（纺织）纱架（复数）|Plural form of "creel," referring to multiple wicker baskets for carrying fish or frames for holding yarn.|The fishermen brought their creels back to the shore. 渔夫们把他们的鱼篓带回了岸边.|Synonyms: baskets, hampers, containers|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English crele, from Old French creil, of unknown origin. Possibly related to Middle Dutch kriezel.
