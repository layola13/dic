imping:/ɪmˈpɪŋ/
verb
碰撞，冲击；侵犯；强加于
to strike or dash against; to encroach; to impinge on someone's rights
The hail impinged on the roof. 冰雹砸在屋顶上。
His personal problems started to impinge on his work. 他的个人问题开始影响他的工作。
近义词:infringe, encroach, impact, collide
反义词:avoid, eschew
