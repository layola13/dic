deducts:/dɪˈdʌkts/| v. |扣除，减去|To subtract or take away (an amount or part) from a total.|The company deducts taxes from your paycheck. 公司从你的工资中扣除税款。|例句：
He deducts $20 from his salary each week for savings. 他每周从工资中扣除20美元用于储蓄。

They deducted the cost of the repairs from the rent. 他们从租金中扣除了维修费用。

|近义词：subtract, withdraw, discount|反义词:add, increase|词性:动词
