comatose:/ˈkəʊmətəʊs/ (UK), /ˈkoʊmətʊs/ (US)| adj.|昏迷的；不省人事的|In a state of deep unconsciousness for a prolonged or indefinite period, resembling a coma.|The patient remained comatose for several days after the accident.  事故发生后，病人昏迷了好几天。|Near synonyms: unconscious, insensible, inert, stuporous|Near antonyms: conscious, awake, alert|adjective

comatose:/ˈkəʊmətəʊs/ (UK), /ˈkoʊmətʊs/ (US)| n.|昏迷的人|A person who is in a coma.|Doctors worked tirelessly to save the comatose man. 医生们不知疲倦地工作以拯救这名昏迷的男子。|Near synonyms: - |Near antonyms: - |noun (rare)
