nonactors:/ˌnɒnˈæktərz/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈæktəz/ (UK)| n.|非演员；非表演者|People who are not actors or performers.  It refers to individuals who are not involved in the acting profession. |Many of the extras in the film were nonactors.  这部电影里的很多临时演员都不是专业演员。|Near synonyms: civilians, laypeople, amateurs (in the context of acting)|Antonyms: actors, performers|Noun
