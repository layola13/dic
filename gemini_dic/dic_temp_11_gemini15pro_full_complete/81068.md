grainier:/ˈɡreɪniər/ | adj. | 粒状更粗的；颗粒感更强的；（照片）更粗糙的 | Having a coarser or more granular texture; (of a photograph) appearing grainy or less clear. | The photo was grainier than I expected. 这张照片比我预期的要粗糙。 | 近义词: coarser, rougher, granular | 反义词: smoother, finer, less grainy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From grain + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin granum "grain".
