catabasion:/kætəˈbeɪʒən/
noun
下降；下场；（尤指古希腊戏剧中）演员退场；（古希腊剧场）通向舞台的地下通道
The descent of actors from the stage in ancient Greek theatre, especially by means of a crane or platform.  Also, a staircase or passage leading down from the stage.
The play ended with a spectacular catabasion, with gods descending from the heavens.  这出戏以壮观的演员退场结束，众神从天而降。
descent, exit, departure
ascent, entrance, arrival
n.
