exoskeletal:/ˌeksəˈskɛlɪtl/| adj.|外骨骼的；体外骨骼的|Relating to or resembling an exoskeleton; having a hard protective outer covering.|The crab's exoskeletal shell protects it from predators.|近义词：external, outer|反义词:endoskeletal, internal|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From exo- (outside, external) + skeletal (relating to the skeleton).  "Exo-" comes from Greek "exo" (outside). "Skeletal" comes from Greek "skeletos" (dried body, mummy) ultimately from "skellein" (to dry up, parch).
