disconcerts:/ˌdɪskənˈsɜːrts/ /ˌdɪskənˈsɝːts/
verb
使不安；使惊慌；使困惑
To disturb the composure of; unsettle:  to cause to feel embarrassment, uneasiness, or bewilderment.
The sudden question disconcerted her. 突如其来的问题让她感到不安。
近义词: unsettle, ruffle, disturb, discompose, perturb, unnerve, agitate, fluster, confuse, bewilder
反义词: calm, soothe, reassure, comfort, encourage
词性:v.
