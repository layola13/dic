half-enforced:/ˌhæf ɪnˈfɔːrst/
adjective
半强制的，部分实施的
Combining "half" (meaning partially) and "enforced" (meaning compelled by authority), it describes something that is only partially or incompletely put into effect or implemented due to lack of resources, will, or other factors.
The half-enforced regulation led to confusion and inconsistent application. 半强制执行的法规导致了混乱和不一致的应用。
The ceasefire agreement was half-enforced, with sporadic outbreaks of violence. 停火协议执行不力，零星爆发暴力事件。
partially enforced, weakly implemented
fully enforced, strictly implemented
