knotlike:/ˈnɒtlaɪk/ | adj. | 像结的，结状的 | Resembling a knot; knotted. | The old man's hands were knotlike and gnarled. 老人的双手像结一样粗糙而扭曲。|  The tree roots formed a knotlike mass underground.  树根在地下形成了一个像结一样的团块。 | 近义词: knotted, gnarled, twisted | 反义词: smooth, straight | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From knot + -like.  "Knot" comes from Old English "cnotta." "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
