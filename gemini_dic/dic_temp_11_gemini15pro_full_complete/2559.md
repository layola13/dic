advisory: /ədˈvaɪzəri/ (US), /ədˈvaɪzəri/ (UK)| adj. n.| 咨询的，顾问的；公告，报告 | Giving or containing advice; a report that gives information or a warning.|  The government issued a travel advisory about the region. 政府发布了关于该地区的旅行公告。|Synonyms: consultative, cautionary, warning, notice, bulletin | Antonyms: compulsory, mandatory |词性: 形容词, 名词

advisory: /ədˈvaɪzəri/ (US), /ədˈvaɪzəri/ (UK)| n.|  公告，报告; 咨询机构 | A report that gives information or a warning; a group that gives advice. | The weather advisory warned of heavy snow. 天气公告警告会有大雪。| Synonyms: warning, notice, bulletin, council, committee | Antonyms: command, order |词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *advisori, from Old French advisorie, from aviser (“to advise”), from Vulgar Latin *advidere (“to see, look at, consider”), from Latin ad- (“to, towards”) + vidēre (“to see”).
