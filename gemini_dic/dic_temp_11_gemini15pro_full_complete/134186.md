noninclusion:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkluːʒən/ | n. | 未包含，不包括 | The noninclusion of certain groups in the study skewed the results.  这项研究未包含某些群体，导致结果出现偏差。 |  The noninclusion of her name on the guest list was a deliberate snub.  嘉宾名单上没有她的名字是故意冷落她。 | 近义词：exclusion, omission | 反义词：inclusion, incorporation | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + inclusion (from Latin includere "to shut in, enclose")
