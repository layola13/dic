mizzle:/ˈmɪzəl/ | v. n. | 毛毛雨，下毛毛雨 | To rain lightly; a light rain. | It began to mizzle as we started our hike.  我们开始徒步旅行时，开始下起了毛毛雨。| 近义词: drizzle, sprinkle | 反义词: downpour, torrent | 词性: 动词, 名词
词源: From Middle English *miselen, frequentative of Old English mistian (“to be misty”), from mist (“mist”).  源自中古英语 *miselen，古英语 mistian（“有雾的”）的重复形式，来自 mist（“雾”）。
