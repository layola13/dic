nonmobilities:/ˌnɒnmoʊˈbɪlətiz/
n.|不流动性；不动性|The lack of ability to move or be moved; the state of being immobile or fixed in place.  This can refer to people, objects, or even abstract concepts like capital or social status.|The increasing nonmobilities of older populations pose significant challenges for healthcare systems. 老年人口日益缺乏流动性给医疗保健系统带来了重大挑战。

The nonmobilities of investment capital during the financial crisis exacerbated the economic downturn.金融危机期间投资资本的不流动性加剧了经济衰退。|immobility, inflexibility, fixedness, stagnation|mobility, fluidity, flexibility, dynamism|noun
