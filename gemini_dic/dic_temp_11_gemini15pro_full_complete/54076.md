disedify: /ˌdɪsˈɛdɪfaɪ/
verb
使不再有教益;使不再启迪
to deprive of edifying qualities; to cause to be no longer instructive or morally uplifting
The explicit lyrics of the song disedified many listeners. 这首歌露骨的歌词让许多听众觉得不再有教益。
Synonyms: corrupt, demoralize, pervert
Antonyms: edify, uplift, enlighten, inspire
词性: 动词
