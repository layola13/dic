dormitory: /ˈdɔːrmɪtɔːri/ (US) /ˈdɔːmɪtri/ (UK)| n.|宿舍，寝室|A large room containing several beds, especially in a boarding school or university, or a building containing such rooms.|Example 1:  The dormitory is quite noisy at night.  宿舍晚上很吵。

Example 2: He lived in a dormitory during his first year of college. 他大学一年级时住在宿舍里。|Synonyms: dorm, hostel, residence hall|Antonyms: apartment, house, private room|noun
