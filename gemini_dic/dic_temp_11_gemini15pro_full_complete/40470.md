contributed:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪd/ | v. | 贡献，捐献，促成，投稿 |  Past participle of contribute.  Meaning "gave something (time, money, effort, etc.) to a common fund or cause" or "played a part in causing something". | He contributed $100 to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐赠了100美元。| Synonyms: donated, offered, gave, furnished, supplied | Antonyms: withheld, withdrew, took, received | verb
