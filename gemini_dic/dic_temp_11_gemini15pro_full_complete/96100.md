indefinable:/ˌɪndɪˈfaɪnəbl/
n.|不可名状之物；难以定义的事物|Something that is difficult or impossible to define or explain.|The indefinable quality of her charm made her irresistible. 她难以言喻的魅力使她无法抗拒。|近义词:indescribable, inexpressible, vague, elusive|反义词:definable, describable, concrete, specific|词性:形容词
indefinables:/ˌɪndɪˈfaɪnəblz/
n.|不可名状之物（复数）|Plural form of "indefinable," referring to multiple things that are difficult or impossible to define or explain.|The painting captured the indefinables of the natural world. 这幅画捕捉到了自然界难以名状的特质。|近义词:indescribables, inexpressibles|反义词:definables, describables|词性:名词

词源:
From in- (not) + definable.  "Definable" comes from the Latin "definire" (to limit, define), from "de" (down, completely) + "finis" (end, limit).
