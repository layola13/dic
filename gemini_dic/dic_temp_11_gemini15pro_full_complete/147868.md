overrationally: /ˌoʊvərˈræʃənəli/
| adv. | 过于理性地，过度理性地 |  In an excessively rational manner; based on logic or reason to an extreme degree. | He analyzed the situation overrationally, ignoring his gut feelings.  他过度理性地分析了情况，忽略了自己的直觉。| Example: She approached the problem overrationally, neglecting the emotional aspects. 她过于理性地处理这个问题，忽略了情感方面。 | Synonyms: excessively rationally, too logically, hyper-rationally | Antonyms: irrationally, emotionally, instinctively | 词性: 副词
