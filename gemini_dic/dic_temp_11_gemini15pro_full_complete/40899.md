cool-down:/ˈkuːl daʊn/| n.| 冷却；缓和；放松运动|A period of less strenuous activity following exercise, designed to return the body to its normal resting state.|After a strenuous workout, it's important to have a cool-down period.|近义词：relaxation, recovery|反义词:warm-up|名词
cool-down:/ˈkuːl daʊn/| v.| 冷却；缓和；平静下来|To become or cause something to become less hot; to become or make someone calmer and less excited.|Allow the engine to cool down before checking the oil. 让发动机冷却下来后再检查机油。|近义词：calm, relax|反义词:heat up|动词
