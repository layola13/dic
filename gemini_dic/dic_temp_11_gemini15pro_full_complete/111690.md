lockful:/ˈlɒkfʊl/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkfʊl/ (US)| n. |一锁之量；满锁；一把锁能锁住的量|The amount that fills a lock, especially in canals.  A lockful of water is needed to raise the boat. |例句：A lockful of water poured over the dam.  大量的水倾泻过大坝。

例句：It took several lockfuls of water to fill the canal.  灌满运河需要好几锁的水。|近义词：lock,  |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "trickle" in context of water flow) |词性:noun
