half-fascinating:/ˌhæfˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋ/
adj.
一半令人着迷的，略感有趣的
Partially fascinating; intriguing to a limited extent.  It suggests something holds some interest but not enough to fully captivate.
The documentary was half-fascinating, half-boring.  这部纪录片一半有趣，一半无聊。
The story he told was half-fascinating, but I couldn't quite grasp the ending. 他讲的故事有点意思，但我没能完全理解结尾。
near synonyms: mildly interesting, somewhat intriguing, partly engaging
antonyms:  dull, tedious, boring, uninteresting, repulsive
adjective
