outwritten:/ˌaʊtˈrɪtn/
verb
胜过(写作上)；在写作上超过
surpassed in writing; defeated in writing competition
Shakespeare outwrote all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的写作超过了他所有的同时代人。

近义词:surpass, outdo, excel
反义词: be inferior to, be surpassed by
词性:verb
