dynamical:/daɪˈnæmɪkl/| adj.|动态的，有活力的，动力的| characterized by continuous change, activity, or progress |The dynamical system is complex and difficult to predict. 这个动力系统很复杂，难以预测。|近义词：active, energetic, vigorous, forceful, potent|反义词：static, inert, passive, inactive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin dynamicus, from Greek dynamikos “powerful,” from dynamis “power.”
