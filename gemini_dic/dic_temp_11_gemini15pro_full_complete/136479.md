nonrhetorical:/ˌnɒn rɪˈtɒrɪkəl/
adjective
非修辞的；非反问的；无需回答的
Not rhetorical; not asked for effect or to make a statement rather than to elicit information.  Used to describe a question genuinely seeking an answer.
Example: He asked a nonrhetorical question, genuinely wanting to know the answer. 他问了一个并非反问的问题，是真的想知道答案。

Example: Her nonrhetorical plea for help was met with silence. 她真诚的求助却无人回应。


synonym:genuine, sincere, earnest
antonym:rhetorical, insincere


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + rhetorical.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "rhetorical" comes from the Greek "rhētorikē" (art of oratory).
