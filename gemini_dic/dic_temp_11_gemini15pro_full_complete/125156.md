multiatom:/ˌmʌltiˈætəm/ |adj.|多原子的| Composed of more than one atom.  Consisting of many atoms.|例句：Multiatom molecules exhibit more complex vibrational modes than diatomic molecules. 多原子分子比双原子分子表现出更复杂的振动模式。

例句：Scientists are studying the behavior of multiatom ions in various chemical reactions. 科学家正在研究多原子离子在各种化学反应中的行为。|近义词：polyatomic|反义词：monoatomic, diatomic|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "multi-" (many, multiple) + "atom" (the smallest unit of an element).  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" (much, many). "Atom" comes from Greek "atomos" (indivisible).
