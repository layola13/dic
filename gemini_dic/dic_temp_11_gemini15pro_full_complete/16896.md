basipodite:/ˈbeɪsɪpəˌdaɪt/| n. |基节，底节|The basipodite is the segment of an arthropod limb that is attached to the body.|The crab's claw is attached to the basipodite.  螃蟹的爪子连接在基节上。|近义词: coxopodite (sometimes considered synonymous)|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially dactylopodite - referring to the terminal segment)|词性:noun

basipodite:/ˈbeɪsɪpəˌdaɪt/| n. |底节|The basipodite forms the base of the appendage in crustaceans.|The muscles controlling the movement of the limb are attached to the basipodite. 控制肢体运动的肌肉连接到底节。|近义词: coxa (in insects)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
