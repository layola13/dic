cropper:/ˈkrɒpər/ (US) /ˈkrɑːpər/ (UK)| n.| 庄稼；农作物；种植者；收割者；剪短头发的人；(尤指鸟的)嗉囊；大丰收|A person or thing that crops something, especially a bird's crop, a plant that crops well, or a person who cuts hair very short.|The farmer is a successful cropper of wheat. 这位农民是一位成功的小麦种植者。|Synonyms: cultivator, grower, farmer, harvester, shearer |Antonyms: consumer, destroyer|noun

cropper:/ˈkrɒpər/ (US) /ˈkrɑːpər/ (UK)| n.|惨败；失败|A heavy fall or crash.|He came a cropper on the ski slopes. 他在滑雪坡上摔得很惨。|Synonyms: fall, crash, failure, tumble |Antonyms: success, triumph|noun
