leverage: /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. vt.|杠杆作用；影响力；杠杆比率；(用杠杆)撬动|The company has a high level of leverage. 这家公司有很高的杠杆率。| synonyms: advantage, influence, power, weight | antonyms: disadvantage, weakness, impotence | noun
leverage: /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/ (UK)| vt.|利用(资源、观点等)；(用杠杆)撬动|They leveraged their knowledge to gain a competitive edge. 他们利用他们的知识获得了竞争优势。| synonyms: utilize, exploit, capitalize on | antonyms: neglect, ignore, waste | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *leviere "lever", from lever "to raise", from Latin levare "to lift, lighten, raise," frequentative of levis "light."
