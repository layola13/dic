detractress:/dɪˈtræktərəs/
noun
诽谤者；贬低者（尤指女性）
A woman who detracts from the merit or reputation of someone or something.
Her political rivals spread rumors, employing every possible detractor to undermine her campaign. 她的政治对手散布谣言，雇佣所有可能的诽谤者来破坏她的竞选活动。
Synonyms: critic, slanderer, backbiter, defamer
Antonyms: supporter, admirer, advocate
