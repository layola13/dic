coif:/kwɑːf/ (US), /kɔɪf/ (UK)|n. vt.|发型，头饰；梳理(头发)|A woman's hairstyle, often elaborate and formal; to style or arrange (someone's hair), especially in an elaborate way|She wore an elaborate coif for the wedding. 她为婚礼梳了个精致的发型。|The stylist coifed her hair into an elegant updo.  造型师把她的头发盘成一个优雅的发髻。|synonyms: hairstyle, hairdo, coiffure|antonyms: unkempt hair, messy hair|noun, verb
