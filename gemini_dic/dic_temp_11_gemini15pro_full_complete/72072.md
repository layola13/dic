flittingly:/ˈflɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 轻快地；一闪而过地；短暂地 | In a manner that is quick and light; briefly and not lasting | The butterfly flittingly landed on the flower. 蝴蝶轻快地落在了花上。| synonyms: briefly, fleetingly, momentarily, quickly, lightly | antonyms: slowly, heavily, enduringly | adverb
flitting:/ˈflɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 飞逝的；一闪而过的；短暂的 |  Passing quickly; lasting only a short time |  A flitting thought crossed her mind.  一个一闪而过的念头掠过她的脑海。| synonyms: brief, fleeting, ephemeral, transient | antonyms: lasting, enduring, permanent | adjective
