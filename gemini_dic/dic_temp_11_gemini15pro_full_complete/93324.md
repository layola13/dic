ill-tempered:/ˌɪlˈtempərd/
adj.
坏脾气的
Having a bad temper; easily angered.
He's an ill-tempered old man. 他是一个脾气暴躁的老人。

The ill-tempered customer yelled at the cashier.  那个脾气不好的顾客对收银员大喊大叫。

近义词:bad-tempered, grumpy, irritable, cranky, surly
反义词:good-tempered, amiable, pleasant, affable, cheerful
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From ill (bad, harmful) + tempered (having a particular temperament).  The word "temper" originally referred to the mixture of elements in metal, and later came to mean a person's state of mind or disposition.
