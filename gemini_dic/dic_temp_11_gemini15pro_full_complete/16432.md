bare-out:/ˈbɛər aʊt/| phrasal verb | 证实，支持 | to confirm or support the truth or validity of something | The evidence bares out his claim.  证据证实了他的说法。|近义词：confirm, corroborate, substantiate, verify |反义词：contradict, refute, disprove | verb phrase
bare-out:/ˈbɛər aʊt/| phrasal verb | 暴露，显露 | to reveal or expose something | The investigation bared out the corruption within the company. 调查揭露了公司内部的腐败。|近义词：reveal, expose, uncover |反义词：conceal, hide | verb phrase
