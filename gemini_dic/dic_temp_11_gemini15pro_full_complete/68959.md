fatherliness:/ˈfɑːðərlɪnɪs/
n.
慈父般的;父亲的仁慈;父亲风范
The quality of being kind and gentle like a good father.
He radiated fatherliness and warmth. 他散发着慈父般的关爱和温暖。
近义词：paternalism, fatherhood
反义词：unfatherliness, coldness, harshness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From father + -liness.  "-liness" is a suffix used to form nouns meaning "the quality of being (adjective)," similar to "-ness".  "Father" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *fader, from Proto-Indo-European *ph₂tḗr.
