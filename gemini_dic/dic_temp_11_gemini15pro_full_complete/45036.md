ctr:/ˈsiː tiː ɑːr/ (US), /ˈsiː tiː ɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.| 点击率 (Click-Through Rate)|Click-through rate is a metric that measures the percentage of people who click on a link or advertisement after seeing it. |The website's ctr increased significantly after the new marketing campaign. 该网站的新营销活动后点击率显著提高。|Synonyms: click rate, hit rate|Antonyms: bounce rate|noun
