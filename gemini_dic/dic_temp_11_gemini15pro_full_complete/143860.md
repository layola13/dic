outplodded:/ˌaʊtˈplɒdɪd/
verb
艰难地跋涉，吃力地走完
walked or moved laboriously or with difficulty
The weary hikers outplodded the final mile to the summit.  疲惫的徒步旅行者艰难地走完了最后一英里到达山顶。
近义词: trudged, slogged, plodded
反义词: sped, raced, dashed
词性: verb 过去式和过去分词


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + plod.  Out- here intensifies the meaning of "plod." Plod comes from  late 16th century: of unknown origin.
