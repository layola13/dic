noumenal:/nuːˈmɛnəl/| adj.| 本体论的；理智的；可理解的| Relating to a thing as it is in itself, as distinct from a thing as it is knowable by the senses; intelligible; pertaining to the understanding.|  The philosopher explored the noumenal world, a realm beyond our sensory experience. 这位哲学家探索了本体论的世界，一个超越我们感官经验的领域。

The concept of a noumenal self is central to Kant's philosophy. 本体论的自我概念是康德哲学的核心。|近义词：transcendental, metaphysical, intelligible|反义词：phenomenal, empirical, sensible|词性:adjective
