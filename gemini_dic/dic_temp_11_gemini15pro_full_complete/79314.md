gleaming:/ˈɡliːmɪŋ/| adj.|闪闪发光的，闪亮的| shining brightly; reflecting light|The gleaming sword caught the light. 闪亮的剑反射着光。|近义词：shining, sparkling, glittering, radiant, luminous|反义词：dull, dim, matte|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English gleminge, present participle of glemen (“to gleam”), from Old English glǣm (“gleam”).
