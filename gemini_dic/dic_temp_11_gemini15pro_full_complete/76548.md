gallature:/ɡæləˈtʃʊr/ |n.|（织物上的）凸起，起泡|A raised or blistered area on a fabric.|The velvet had a gallature where it had been pressed.|Synonym: blister, swelling|Antonym: smoothness, flatness|noun
