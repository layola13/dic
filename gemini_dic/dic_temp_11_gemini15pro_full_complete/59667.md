echinodermatous:/ɪˌkaɪnəˈdɜːrmətəs/
adjective
棘皮动物的
Relating to or belonging to the phylum Echinodermata, which includes starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers.  Characterized by radial symmetry, typically a five-point radial symmetry, and a water vascular system.
The starfish is an echinodermatous creature. 海星是一种棘皮动物。
The brittle star, with its delicate arms, is also echinodermatous.  腕足纤细的蛇尾也是棘皮动物。
Near Synonyms:  echinoid, asteroid, holothurian
Antonyms:  vertebrate, chordate
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *echinos* (hedgehog, sea urchin) + *derma* (skin)
