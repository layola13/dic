guilt:/ɡɪlt/| n.|内疚，罪行，犯罪|A feeling of having done wrong or failed in an obligation.|She felt a pang of guilt for not visiting her grandmother. 她因为没去看望祖母而感到一阵内疚。|Synonyms: remorse, shame, regret|Antonyms: innocence, blamelessness|noun

guilt:/ɡɪlt/| vt.|使内疚，定...的罪|To cause (someone) to feel guilty; to find (someone) guilty of a crime.|He guilted her into going to the party. 他让她感到内疚，使她去了聚会。|Synonyms: blame, condemn|Antonyms: exonerate, absolve|verb
