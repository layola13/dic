hornblower:/ˈhɔːrnbloʊər/| n.|吹号手；号兵；(尤指)海军信号员；《霍恩布洛尔》(英国 C.S. Forester 所著系列小说及其主人公)|A person who blows a horn, especially a signal horn in the navy; also, the protagonist of a series of novels by C.S. Forester.|Horatio Hornblower is a fictional character. 霍雷肖·霍恩布洛尔是一个虚构人物。|The ship's hornblower signaled the approach of land. 船上的号手吹响了号角，示意陆地临近。|synonyms: trumpeter, bugler|antonyms: listener, audience|noun
