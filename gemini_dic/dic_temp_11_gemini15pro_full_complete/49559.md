demean:/dɪˈmiːn/| vt.|贬低，降低身份|To cause a lowering of status, dignity, or self-respect.|His actions demeaned the entire profession. 他的行为贬低了整个行业。|Synonyms: degrade, humiliate, belittle|Antonyms: elevate, ennoble, honor|verb

demean:/dɪˈmiːn/| vt.|举止，表现 (较少用)|To behave or conduct oneself (usually in a specified way).|He demeaned himself with dignity throughout the trial. 在整个审判过程中，他都表现得很有尊严。|Synonyms: behave, conduct, comport|Antonyms: misbehave, misconduct|verb
