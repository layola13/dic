inclusivenesses:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
包容性（复数）
The quality or state of including or covering all the services, facilities, groups, etc., that are needed or wanted.  The plural form is less common but can be used to refer to multiple instances or types of inclusiveness.
The company is known for its inclusivenesses in hiring practices and employee benefits. 这家公司以其招聘实践和员工福利方面的包容性而闻名。
Many universities are striving for greater inclusivenesses in their student bodies. 许多大学都在努力使其学生群体更加具有包容性。
近义词: inclusivity, integration, diversity, acceptance
反义词: exclusiveness, discrimination, segregation
词性: 名词
