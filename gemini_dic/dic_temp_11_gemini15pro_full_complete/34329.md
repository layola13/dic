clashed:/klæʃt/| n.|冲突，碰撞声|A loud, harsh, metallic sound.|The clash of cymbals marked the end of the performance. 铙钹的撞击声标志着表演的结束。|synonyms: clang, bang, crash|antonyms: silence, quiet|noun
