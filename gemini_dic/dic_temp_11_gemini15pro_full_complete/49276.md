deliberation:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/| n.|深思熟虑，审议，考虑|Careful consideration or discussion of a subject.|The jury reached its verdict after hours of deliberation. 陪审团经过数小时的审议后作出了裁决。|近义词：consideration, contemplation, reflection|反义词：impulse, recklessness|noun

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/| adj.|故意的，蓄意的；慎重的，从容的|Done consciously and intentionally; slow and careful.|The attack was a deliberate attempt to destabilize the government. 这次袭击是蓄意破坏政府稳定的企图。|近义词：intentional, purposeful, careful|反义词：accidental, unintentional, hasty|adjective

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/| v.|仔细考虑，商议|Engage in long and careful consideration.|The committee deliberated for several hours before making a decision. 委员会商议了几个小时才做出决定。|近义词：consider, ponder, discuss|反义词：rush, hasten|verb


词源：
From Latin *deliberatus*, past participle of *deliberare* "to weigh in mind, consider," from *de-* "thoroughly" + *librare* "to weigh," related to *libra* "balance, scales."
