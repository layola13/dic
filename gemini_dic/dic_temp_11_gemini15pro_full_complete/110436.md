likely:/ˈlaɪkli/| adj. adv.|可能的，很可能的；或许，大概|Something that is likely will probably happen or is expected. |It's likely to rain later. 后来很可能会下雨。|近义词：probable, possible, expected|反义词：unlikely, improbable|词性:形容词/副词
词源:From Middle English *likli, from Old Norse líkligr (“likely, probable”), from líkr (“like, resembling”) + -ligr (an adjective suffix).
