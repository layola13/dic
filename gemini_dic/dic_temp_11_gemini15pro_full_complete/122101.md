mistinesses:/ˈmɪstɪnɪsɪz/
noun
朦胧；模糊不清；迷茫
The state of being misty; a hazy or indistinct quality.
The mistinesses of the early morning gave the landscape a dreamlike quality. 清晨的薄雾使景色如梦似幻。
Near synonyms: haziness, fogginess, murkiness
Antonyms: clarity, clearness, sharpness
词性: 名词
