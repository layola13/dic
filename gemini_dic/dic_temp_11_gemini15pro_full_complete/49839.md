demurrage:/dɪˈmɜːrɪdʒ/ | n. | 滞期费，延滞费 |  A charge payable to the owner of a chartered ship on failure to load or discharge the ship within the time agreed. | The charterer had to pay a substantial demurrage charge for the delay.  承租人不得不为延误支付巨额滞期费。|Synonyms: detention charge, waiting charge | Antonyms: dispatch money, premium| noun


demurrage:/dɪˈmɜːrɪdʒ/ | n. |  (铁路货车)滞留费 | A charge payable for detaining a railroad car or other freight conveyance beyond the time allowed for loading or unloading. |  The railroad company charged demurrage for the extra days the car sat on the siding.  铁路公司对货车在侧线停放的额外天数收取滞留费。| Synonyms: detention charge, storage charge | Antonyms: dispatch money, credit | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *demorage, from demorer "to linger, delay," from Latin demorari "to delay," from de- "down" + morari "to delay."
