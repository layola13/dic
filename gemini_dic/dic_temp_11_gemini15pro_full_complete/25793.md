buret:/bjʊˈrɛt/ /bʊˈret/| n.|滴定管|A graduated glass tube with a tap at one end, for delivering known volumes of a liquid, especially in titrations.|The chemist carefully filled the buret with the solution. 化学家小心地将溶液注入滴定管。|Synonyms: burette|Antonyms: None|noun

burettes:/bjʊˈrɛts/ /bʊˈrets/| n.|滴定管（复数）|Plural form of "buret," referring to multiple graduated glass tubes used for delivering known volumes of liquid.|The lab was equipped with several burettes for the titration experiment. 实验室配备了数个滴定管用于滴定实验。|Synonyms: burettes|Antonyms: None|noun
