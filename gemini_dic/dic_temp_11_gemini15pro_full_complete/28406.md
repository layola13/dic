carbonating:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪtɪŋ/
verb
碳酸化（使…加入二氧化碳）
The process of adding carbon dioxide to a liquid, typically water, to create carbonation.
They are carbonating the water to make sparkling water. 他们正在将水碳酸化以制作苏打水。
The machine is carbonating the beverage.  这台机器正在给饮料充气。
近义词:aerating, fizzing
反义词:decarbonating
词性:v.
