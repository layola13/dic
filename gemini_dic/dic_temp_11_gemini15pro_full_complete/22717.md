boride:/ˈbɔːraɪd/| n. |硼化物|A binary compound of boron with a more electropositive element.|Titanium boride is exceptionally hard. 钛硼化物异常坚硬。|近义词: boron compound|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From bor(on) + -ide, a suffix used in forming the names of binary compounds.
