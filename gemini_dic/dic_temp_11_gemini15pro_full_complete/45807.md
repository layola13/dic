custodies: /ˈkʌstədiz/ | n. | 监护权，保管，拘留 | The legal right and responsibility to care for someone, especially a child, or something.  Also refers to imprisonment or confinement. | The court awarded the mother sole custody of the children. 法院判决孩子的唯一监护权归母亲所有。

The suspect is currently in police custody. 嫌疑人目前被警方拘留。 | 近义词：care, guardianship, safekeeping, detention, imprisonment | 反义词：freedom, release | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin *custodia* "a guarding, keeping, custody, prison," from *custos* "guard, keeper."
