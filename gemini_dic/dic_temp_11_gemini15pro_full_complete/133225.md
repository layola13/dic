nonexcitable:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsaɪtəbl/| adj.|不易兴奋的，非兴奋性的|Not easily excited; not capable of being stimulated to action.  Relating to tissues that do not respond to nervous stimulation by generating an impulse.|Cardiac muscle is excitable, but bone is nonexcitable.|近义词：insensitive, unreactive, impassive|反义词:excitable, responsive, sensitive|词性:adjective
词源:non- (prefix meaning "not") + excitable (from excite + -able)
