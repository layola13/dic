coheirship:/koʊˈɛrʃɪp/
n.
共同继承权，共同继承人的身份
The state or right of inheriting along with another or others; joint heirship.
The two sisters have coheirship of their father's estate.  这两个姐妹共同继承了她们父亲的遗产。
joint inheritance, joint tenancy
sole inheritance
noun
