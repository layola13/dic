hatchettite:/ˈhætʃɪtaɪt/
n.
斧石
A monoclinic-prismatic mineral containing arsenate and manganese.  A hydrated arsenate of manganese and magnesium.
Hatchettite is a relatively rare mineral found in various locations worldwide. 斧石是一种相对稀有的矿物，在世界各地都有发现。
Small hatchettite crystals were observed within the vugs. 在晶洞内观察到小的斧石晶体。
Synonyms: None widely used.
Antonyms: N/A
