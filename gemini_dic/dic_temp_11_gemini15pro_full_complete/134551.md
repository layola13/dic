nonliteralness:/nɒnˈlɪtərəlnəs/ |n.| 非字面性，引申义 |The nonliteralness of the poem makes it difficult to interpret. 这首诗的非字面性使它难以解释。|例句：The nonliteralness of the metaphor adds depth to the poem.  这个比喻的非字面性增加了诗的深度。

例句：Her statement was marked by its nonliteralness, leaving us to decipher her true meaning. 她的陈述以其非字面性为特征，让我们去解读她真正的意思。|近义词：figurativeness, symbolism, metaphor, allegory|反义词：literalness, directness|词性: 名词

词源：non- (not) + literal + -ness (state of being)  来自 "nonliteral" 加上名词后缀 "-ness"，表示“非字面”的状态。 "literal" 源自拉丁语 "litteralis"，意思是“与字母有关的”。
