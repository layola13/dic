kabaddi:/kəˈbʌdi/| n.|卡巴迪|A contact team sport, originating in ancient India, played between two teams of seven players. The objective is for a single player on offence, called a "raider", to run into the opposing team's half of the court, tag out as many of their defenders as possible, and return to their own half of the court, all without inhaling.  |Example 1: Kabaddi is a very popular sport in India. 卡巴迪在印度是一项非常受欢迎的运动。

Example 2:  The kabaddi match was intense and exciting. 这场卡巴迪比赛紧张又刺激。|近义词:tag, wrestling (loosely)|反义词:None|词性:noun
