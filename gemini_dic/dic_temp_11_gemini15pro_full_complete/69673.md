fender-benders:/ˈfɛndər bɛndərz/
noun
小型交通事故；轻微碰撞
A minor traffic accident, typically involving slight damage to the vehicles.
My dad had a fender-bender on the way to work this morning, but thankfully no one was hurt. 我爸爸今天早上上班路上发生了一起小车祸，幸好没有人受伤。

fender-benders:/ˈfɛndər bɛndərz/
noun
碰擦事故
A collision between vehicles, usually resulting in minor damage.
There were several fender-benders due to the icy roads. 由于路面结冰，发生了多起碰擦事故。

fender-benders:/ˈfɛndər bɛndərz/
plural noun
轻微的交通事故（复数）
Minor car accidents, often involving only slight damage.
Fender-benders are common during rush hour traffic. 在交通高峰期，轻微的交通事故很常见。

fender-benders:/ˈfɛndər bɛndərz/
plural noun
小碰撞（复数）
Small collisions or crashes, especially between vehicles.
The insurance company handled a lot of fender-benders last month. 上个月保险公司处理了很多小碰撞事故。


近义词:minor accidents, small collisions, bumps, scrapes
反义词:major accidents, serious collisions, crashes, wrecks
词性:noun (plural)
