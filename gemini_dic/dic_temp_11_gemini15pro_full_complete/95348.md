incentivize: /ɪnˈsɛntɪvaɪz/ | v. | 激励；刺激；鼓励 | To motivate or encourage someone to do something, typically by providing a reward or avoiding a penalty. |  The government incentivized the purchase of electric cars by offering tax breaks. 政府通过提供税收优惠来鼓励购买电动汽车。

The company incentivizes its employees to work harder by offering bonuses. 公司通过提供奖金来激励员工更加努力地工作。 | 近义词: motivate, encourage, stimulate, prompt | 反义词: discourage, deter, hinder | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *incentive* + *-ize*.  *Incentive* comes from Late Latin *incentivus* "inciting, encouraging," from past participle stem of *incentivare* "to set the tune," from *in- "into" + *cantare* "to sing."
