openmindedness:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdnəs/| n.| 开放性，开明；虚心|The state of being receptive to new ideas or experiences; willingness to consider different perspectives.|She approached the problem with openmindedness and a willingness to learn.|近义词：receptiveness, tolerance, impartiality|反义词：close-mindedness, intolerance, prejudice|词性:名词

open-minded:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.| 虚心的，开明的；思想开放的|Willing to consider new ideas; unprejudiced.|He was an open-minded man, always willing to listen to different viewpoints.|近义词：receptive, tolerant, unbiased|反义词：close-minded, intolerant, prejudiced|词性:形容词
