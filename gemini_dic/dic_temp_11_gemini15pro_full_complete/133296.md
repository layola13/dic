nonexperientially:/ˌnɒnɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəli/ /ˌnɑːnɪkˌspɪriˈɛnʃəli/
adverb
非经验地；非通过经验地
Not based on or acquired through experience.
His arguments were presented nonexperientially, relying solely on theoretical frameworks. 他提出的论点是非经验性的，仅仅依赖于理论框架。
The concept was understood nonexperientially, derived from abstract reasoning rather than practical observation. 这个概念是非经验性地理解的，源于抽象推理而非实际观察。
近义词:theoretically, abstractly, conceptually
反义词:empirically, practically, experientially
词性:adverb
