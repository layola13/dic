dyspeptical:/dɪsˈpɛptɪk(ə)l/
adjective
消化不良的；脾气坏的
Suffering from or pertaining to dyspepsia; having indigestion. Also, figuratively, irritable; bad-tempered.
He felt quite dyspeptical after eating too much rich food. 他吃了太多油腻的食物后感到消化不良。
The dyspeptical old man yelled at the children playing in his yard.  那个脾气暴躁的老人对着在他院子里玩耍的孩子们大吼大叫。
Synonyms:indigestive, crabby, irritable, grumpy, choleric
Antonyms:good-natured, amiable, pleasant, agreeable, eupeptic
词性:形容词

dyspepsia:/dɪsˈpɛpsɪə/
noun
消化不良
Indigestion.
He suffered from dyspepsia after eating spicy food. 他吃了辛辣食物后消化不良。
Synonyms:indigestion, upset stomach
Antonyms:good digestion
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dyspepsia, from Ancient Greek δυσπεψία (duspepsía, “indigestion”), from δυσ- (dus-, “bad, difficult”) + πέψις (pépsis, “digestion”).
