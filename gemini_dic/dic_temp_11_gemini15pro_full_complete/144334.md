outstaggering: /ˌaʊtˈstæɡərɪŋ/
adjective
令人惊愕的，令人震惊的
so surprisingly impressive as to stun or overwhelm
The amount of money spent on the project was outstaggering.  这个项目的花费令人咋舌。
The beauty of the sunset was outstaggering. 日落的景色美得令人窒息。
synonyms: astounding, astonishing, staggering, overwhelming, breathtaking
antonyms: underwhelming, unimpressive, insignificant, negligible
adjective
