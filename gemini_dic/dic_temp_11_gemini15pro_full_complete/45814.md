customable:/ˈkʌstəməbl/ | adj. | 可定制的，可个性化的 | Able to be customized or altered to suit individual needs or preferences. |  This software is highly customable, allowing users to personalize their experience.  这款软件高度可定制，允许用户个性化他们的体验。 | Example: The car's interior is customable, with a range of colors and materials to choose from. 汽车内饰可定制，有多种颜色和材料可供选择。 | Synonyms: customizable, personalized, adaptable, modifiable, tailor-made | Antonyms: fixed, standard, unchangeable | Word type: Adjective
