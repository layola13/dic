depulse:/dɪˈpʌls/
v.
驱逐，击退，逐出
to drive out or away; repel
The enemy forces were depulsed after a fierce battle. 敌军在激战后被击退。
The defenders depulsed the attackers. 防守者击退了进攻者。
近义词: repel, drive back, repulse, rout
反义词: attract, welcome, invite
词性: 动词
