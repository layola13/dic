hypotheticals:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkəlz/
n.
假设，假想情况
situations or scenarios that are imagined or suggested but not necessarily real or true.
Many philosophers like to debate hypotheticals. 许多哲学家喜欢辩论假设的情况。
synonyms: possibilities, suppositions, assumptions, conjectures
antonyms: realities, facts, certainties
noun

hypothetical:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkəl/
adj.
假设的，假定的
based on or involving a hypothesis; supposed.
Let's consider a hypothetical situation.  让我们考虑一个假设的情况。
synonyms: theoretical, assumed, supposed, conjectural
antonyms: real, actual, factual, proven
adjective
