methylamine:/ˈmɛθəlˌæmiːn/ /ˈmiːθəlˌæmiːn/| n. |甲胺|A colorless, flammable, alkaline gas, CH3NH2, with a strong ammoniacal odor, used in organic synthesis. It is the simplest primary amine.|Methylamine is used in the production of certain pesticides. 甲胺用于生产某些杀虫剂。|
Methylamine is a precursor to methamphetamine. 甲胺是甲基苯丙胺的前体。|近义词:aminomethane|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
