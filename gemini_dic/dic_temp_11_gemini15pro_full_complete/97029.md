infectiously:/ɪnˈfɛkʃəsli/| adv.|传染性地；有感染力地|In a way that tends to spread to or influence others. |Her laughter was infectious, and soon everyone was joining in. 她的笑声很有感染力，很快大家都加入了进来。|Synonyms: contagiously, catching, spreading|Antonyms: uninspiring, dull|词性: 副词

infectiously:/ɪnˈfɛkʃəsli/| adv.|有感染力地|In a way that inspires enthusiasm or emotion in others.|He spoke with infectious enthusiasm about his new project. 他谈论他的新项目时充满了感染力。|Synonyms: inspiringly, enthusiastically, passionately|Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently|词性: 副词
