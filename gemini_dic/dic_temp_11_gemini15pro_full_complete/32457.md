chimpanzee:/ˌtʃɪmpænˈziː/ /ˌtʃɪmpənˈziː/| n.|黑猩猩|A small, very intelligent African ape with black or brown hair.|例句：The chimpanzee used a stick to get the insects out of the hole.  黑猩猩用棍子把昆虫从洞里弄出来。

例句：Chimpanzees are known for their intelligence and social behavior. 黑猩猩以它们的智力和社会行为而闻名。|近义词：ape, primate|反义词: None (No direct opposite for an animal species)|词性:noun
