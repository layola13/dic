-fied:/faɪd/ | suffix | -化的，-的 |  Transforms verbs or nouns into adjectives, indicating a state or quality resulting from the action of the verb or the association with the noun. |  A terrified look swept across her face. 她脸上掠过一丝惊恐的表情。 | 近义词: None (as a suffix) | 反义词: None (as a suffix) | suffix

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English -fied, -fed, from Old French -fié, from Latin -ficātus, past participle of -ficāre (to make, do, perform), from -ficus (making, doing) + -āre (infinitive suffix).  The "-ficus" element is related to the Latin verb facere (to make, do).
