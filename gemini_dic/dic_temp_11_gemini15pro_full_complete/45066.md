cubically:/ˈkjuːbɪkli/| adv.|立方地，三次方地|In mathematics, "cubically" refers to something related to the third power or a cube. It describes how something increases or decreases in volume or capacity proportionally to the cube of a linear dimension.|The volume of the container increases cubically with its length.  容器的体积随着其长度的立方而增加。|The error grows cubically with time.  误差随时间的立方而增长。|Near Synonyms: exponentially, rapidly|Near Antonyms: linearly, slowly|词性:副词
