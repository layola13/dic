morningstar:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ stɑːr/ (US) /ˈmɔːnɪŋ stɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.| 晨星；金星；启明星|A bright planet (usually Venus) seen in the eastern sky before sunrise.|例句：The morningstar shone brightly in the pre-dawn sky. 启明星在黎明前的天空中闪耀。|近义词：Venus, Daystar, Lucifer|反义词: evening star|noun

morningstar:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ stɑːr/ (US) /ˈmɔːnɪŋ stɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.| (公司或投资产品名称) 晨星|The name of a company known for investment research and management.|例句：Morningstar provides independent investment research. 晨星提供独立的投资研究。|近义词： (In some contexts related to finance)  investment research firm, financial data provider|反义词: N/A|noun
