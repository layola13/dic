bryologist:/braɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ /braɪˈɑːlədʒɪst/
noun
苔藓学家
A scientist who studies bryophytes (mosses, liverworts, and hornworts).
Example: The bryologist spent years cataloging the diverse moss species of the cloud forest.  苔藓学家花了数年时间对云雾森林中各种各样的苔藓物种进行分类。

Example:  A renowned bryologist discovered a new species of liverwort in the remote jungle. 一位著名的苔藓学家在偏远的丛林中发现了一种新的地钱。
synonym:  moss expert, bryophyte specialist
antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)
