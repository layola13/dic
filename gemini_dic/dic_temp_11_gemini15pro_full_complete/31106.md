champlevé:/ˌʃɑːmpləˈveɪ/ /ˌʃɑːmpləˈveɪ/
n.|嵌丝珐琅|A decorative enamelwork technique where hollows are carved into a metal surface and filled with enamel.
The artist created a beautiful champlevé brooch with intricate designs. 艺术家创作了一枚带有复杂设计的美丽的嵌丝珐琅胸针。
The museum's collection includes several exquisite examples of medieval champlevé.  博物馆的藏品包括几个精美的中世纪嵌丝珐琅的例子。
近义词:enamelwork, cloisonné|反义词:|名词
