disbursal:/dɪsˈbɜːrsəl/
n.|支付，支出，拨款|The disbursal of funds for the project was delayed.  该项目的资金支付被推迟了。|
例句：The disbursal of the loan was completed last week.  贷款的支付上周完成了。
例句：The company is responsible for the disbursal of pension payments. 公司负责支付养老金。
近义词：payment, distribution, disbursement
反义词：receipt, collection
词性：名词

disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/
vt.|支付，支出，拨款|The government disbursed millions of dollars in aid. 政府支付了数百万美元的援助。|
例句：The charity disbursed funds to the victims of the earthquake.  慈善机构向地震灾民支付了资金。
例句：The finance department disbursed the salaries on Friday.  财务部门在星期五支付了工资。
近义词：pay, distribute, expend
反义词：collect, receive
词性：动词

词源:
From Old French *desborser, from des- "out, away" + borse "purse".  来自古法语 *desborser，由 des- “出，离开” + borse “钱包” 组成。
