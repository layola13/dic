/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) |pron. det. int. adv.| 什么 |Used to ask for information specifying something.|
What is your name?|你的名字是什么？

What do you want?|你想要什么？

近义词: which |反义词: N/A | pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
