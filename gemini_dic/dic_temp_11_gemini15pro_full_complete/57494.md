dragee:/drɑːˈʒeɪ/
noun
糖衣药丸；糖衣杏仁；糖果
A small rounded sweet consisting of a sugar-coated almond or other nut. Also, a small pill coated with sugar.
The wedding cake was decorated with silver dragees. 婚礼蛋糕上装饰着银色的糖衣杏仁。
The doctor prescribed a dragee to help with the patient's cough. 医生开了一种糖衣药丸来帮助病人止咳。
