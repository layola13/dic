lathlike:/ˈlæθlaɪk/| adj.|像板条的；细长的；瘦而硬的|Resembling a lath; thin, long, and rigid.|The model had a lathlike figure.  这位模特身材瘦长。|The fence was made of lathlike strips of wood.  这个栅栏是用细长的木条做的。|近义词：thin, slender, skinny, spindly, reedy|反义词：thick, stout, robust|词性:形容词
词源：From lath + -like.  Lath comes from Old English *lætt.
