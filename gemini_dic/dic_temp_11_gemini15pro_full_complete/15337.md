backspins:/ˈbækˌspɪnz/| n. |后旋，回旋|A backward rotation or spin, often used in the context of ball sports like basketball, table tennis, or billiards.  It's the act of imparting reverse spin to a ball. |He put a wicked backspin on the ball, causing it to stop dead on the green. 他给球加了一个强烈的回旋，使它在果岭上停了下来。|近义词：reverse spin, underspin|反义词: topspin |noun
