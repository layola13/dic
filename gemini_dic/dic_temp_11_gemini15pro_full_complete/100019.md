interpermeated:/ˌɪntərˈpɜːrmieɪtɪd/
verb
互相渗透，弥漫
spread or diffuse through something, especially a liquid, gas, or odor.
The smell of coffee interpermeated the air. 咖啡的气味弥漫在空气中。
近义词:pervade, saturate, infuse, permeate
反义词:separate, divide
词性:verb

Etymology:
From Latin inter (between, among) + permeare (to pass through).
