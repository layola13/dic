bisecting:/baɪˈsektɪŋ/ | adj. | 二等分的，平分的 | Dividing into two equal parts. |  The line bisecting the angle creates two congruent angles. 这条线将角平分， creating two congruent angles. | 近义词：halving, dividing | 反义词: joining, uniting | 词性: 形容词

bisect:/baɪˈsekt/ | vt. | 二等分，平分 | To divide into two equal parts. | The road bisects the town. 这条路把小镇一分为二。| 近义词：halve, divide | 反义词: join, unite | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *bisectus, past participle of bisecare "to cut in two," from bi- "two" + secare "to cut."
