darers:/ˈdɛrərz/
n.
敢于冒险的人，大胆者
People who dare to take risks.
例句：The darers climbed the mountain despite the warnings.  那些胆大的人不顾警告爬上了山。

例句：Only the darers will succeed in this challenging market. 只有敢于冒险的人才能在这个充满挑战的市场中取得成功。

近义词：risk-takers, adventurers, daredevils
反义词：cowards, timid people


词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  From the verb "dare" (Old English *durran, from Proto-Germanic *durzaną), meaning "to have the courage to do something." The "-er" suffix denotes a person who performs the action of the verb.
