auxanometer: /ɔːɡˈzænəˌmiːtər/ | n. | 植物生长测量器 | An instrument for measuring the rate of growth in plants. | The botanist used an auxanometer to track the growth of the seedlings. 植物学家使用生长测量器来跟踪幼苗的生长。|  The researchers used an auxanometer to measure the effect of different light wavelengths on plant growth. 研究人员使用生长测量器来测量不同光波长对植物生长的影响。 | 近义词:  growth meter | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *auxein* (to grow) + *-ano-* (upward) + *-meter* (measuring instrument).
