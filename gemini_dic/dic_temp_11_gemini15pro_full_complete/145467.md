overclassification:/ˌoʊvərˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度分类，过分分类 | The act of classifying something too narrowly or with too much detail, resulting in too many categories or an overly complex system. | Example 1: Overclassification of data can make analysis more difficult and less meaningful.  数据过度分类会使分析更加困难，意义也更小。

Example 2: The overclassification of mental disorders can lead to unnecessary stigmatization. 精神疾病的过度分类会导致不必要的污名化。 | 近义词: overcategorization | 反义词: underclassification | 词性: noun
