looniest:/ˈluːnɪɪst/| adj.|最疯狂的，最愚蠢的|Most foolish, eccentric, or crazy.|例句：That's the looniest idea I've ever heard! 那是我听过的最疯狂的想法！

例句：He had the looniest collection of hats. 他有一堆最古怪的帽子。

近义词：craziest, maddest, wildest, silliest|反义词：sanest, most sensible|词性:形容词
