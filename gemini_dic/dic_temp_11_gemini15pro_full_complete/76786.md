gamifies:/ˈɡeɪmɪfaɪz/
verb
使游戏化；游戏化
Applies game mechanics and design techniques to non-game contexts to engage users and motivate participation.
The company gamifies its employee training program to make it more engaging.  公司将员工培训项目游戏化，以提高参与度。
Duolingo gamifies language learning by awarding points and virtual currency.  Duolingo 通过奖励积分和虚拟货币来使语言学习游戏化。
近义词: makes a game of, ludifies
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly) makes serious, formalizes
词性: verb
