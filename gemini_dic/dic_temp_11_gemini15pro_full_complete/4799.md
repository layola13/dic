algologist:/ælˈɡɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |藻类学家|A scientist who studies algae (phycology).|例句：The algologist collected samples from the lake to study the algal blooms. 这位藻类学家从湖中采集样本以研究藻华。

例句：The algologist specialized in the study of diatoms. 这位藻类学家专门研究硅藻。|近义词：phycologist|反义词:|词性:noun

词源:  From  *algology* + *-ist*.  *Algology* from  *algo-*  ("alga") + *-logy* ("study of"). Ultimately from Greek  *álogos*  ("seaweed").
