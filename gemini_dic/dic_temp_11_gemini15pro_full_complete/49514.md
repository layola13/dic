demand:/dɪˈmænd/ |/dɪˈmænd/| n. vt.| 要求，需求；需要|A strong request; the state of being wanted or sought after.| The demand for housing is high in this city.  这个城市住房需求很高。|近义词：request, require, claim, need|反义词:supply, offer|noun, verb


demand:/dɪˈmænd/ |/dɪˈmænd/| v.| 要求，需要|To ask for something forcefully; to require.| I demand an explanation! 我要求一个解释！ |近义词：request, require, insist|反义词:offer, provide|verb
