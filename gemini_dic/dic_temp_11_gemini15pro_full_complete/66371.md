excusingly:/ɪkˈskjuːzɪŋli/
adverb|辩解地；抱歉地；请求原谅地|In a way that offers an excuse or apology. |"He smiled excusingly, as if to say he couldn't help it."  他带着歉意笑了笑，好像在说他情不自禁。|"She coughed excusingly and left the room." 她抱歉地咳了一声，离开了房间。|近义词: apologetically, regretfully|反义词: unapologetically, defiantly|词性: adverb

Etymology:
From excuse + -ing + -ly.  "Excuse" comes from the Old French "excuser," from Latin "excusare"  meaning "to free from blame."
