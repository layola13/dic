entoderm:/ˈɛntəˌdɜːrm/ (US), /ˈɛndəˌdɜːm/ (UK)| n. |内胚层|The innermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, from which the lining of the digestive tract and associated structures develops.|The endoderm gives rise to the lining of the stomach and intestines. 内胚层形成胃和肠的内壁。|hypoblast|ectoderm, mesoderm|noun
