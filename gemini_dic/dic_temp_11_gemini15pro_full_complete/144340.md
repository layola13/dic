Etymology:
From outstanding + -ness + -'s (possessive).  "Outstanding" originates from the verb "to stand out," meaning to be prominent or noticeable.  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.  The final "-'s" indicates possession, belonging to the outstandingness.
