brakeless:/ˈbreɪkləs/| adj.|无闸的，没有刹车的|Without brakes; lacking a braking mechanism.  This refers to a vehicle, typically a bicycle, that does not have brakes.|He rode a brakeless bike down the hill, which was incredibly dangerous.|近义词：unbraked|反义词: braked|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "brake" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence). "Brake" comes from Middle Dutch *breken "to break," related to Old English brecan.
