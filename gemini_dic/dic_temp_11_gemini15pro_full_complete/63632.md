eolipile: /ˈiːəˌpaɪl/ | n. |汽转球，蒸汽机，伊奥利皮勒 | A hollow metal sphere filled with water and heated from below, causing the water to boil and steam to escape through nozzles, thereby rotating the sphere.  It's an early example of a steam engine, often attributed to Hero of Alexandria. | The museum displayed a working replica of an eolipile.  博物馆展示了一个可以运作的汽转球复制品。 | The eolipile demonstrated the power of steam.  汽转球展示了蒸汽的力量。 | 近义词: steam engine, aeolipile | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly manual tool) | 词性: noun
