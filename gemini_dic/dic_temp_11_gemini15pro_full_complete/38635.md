concupiscence:/kənˈkjuːpɪsəns/| n. |贪欲，色欲|An ardent, usually sensual, longing or desire.|例句：Concupiscence is often considered a root of sin in many religions.  在许多宗教中，贪欲常被认为是罪恶的根源。

例句：He struggled with concupiscence throughout his life. 他一生都在与色欲作斗争。|近义词：lust, desire, craving, passion|反义词：abstinence, chastity, moderation|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concupiscentia*, from Latin concupiscere "to long for, covet," from con- "together" + cupiscere "to desire, wish," related to cupidus "eager, passionate."
