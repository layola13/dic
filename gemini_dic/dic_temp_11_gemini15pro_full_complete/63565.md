envigor:/ɪnˈvɪɡər/ /ɛnˈvɪɡɚ/ | v. | 使充满活力；使精力充沛；激励 | To give strength and energy to. |  The cool air invigorated us after the long, hot drive.  凉爽的空气让我们在漫长而炎热的驾驶后恢复了活力。

The new CEO’s speech invigorated the employees. 新任首席执行官的讲话激励了员工。 | 近义词: energize, revitalize, stimulate, strengthen | 反义词: weaken, exhaust, enervate, debilitate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *invigorare, from in- + vigor "strength, vigor."
