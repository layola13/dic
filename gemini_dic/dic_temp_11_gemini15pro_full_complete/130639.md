nonadmissions:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪʃənz/ /ˌnɑːnədˈmɪʃənz/
noun
不录取，不接纳
The act of not admitting someone or something; refusal of entry or acceptance.
The university sent out nonadmissions letters to thousands of applicants.  大学向数千名申请者发出了不录取通知书。
The nonadmissions policy of the club was strict.  俱乐部的非会员不得入内政策非常严格。
synonyms: rejection, refusal, denial, exclusion
antonyms: admission, acceptance, inclusion
n.
