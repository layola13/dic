capped:/kæpt/| adj.|戴帽子的；有限的；受限制的|Having a cap or cover; limited or restricted.|The capped interest rate protected borrowers from extreme fluctuations.  利率上限保护借款人免受极端波动。|近义词：limited, restricted, covered|反义词:unlimited, unrestricted, open|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the verb "cap" (meaning "to provide with a cap") + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  The verb "cap" ultimately derives from the Latin "caput" meaning "head".
