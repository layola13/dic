clamlike:/ˈklæmlaɪk/| adj.|像蛤蜊的；沉默寡言的|Resembling a clam, especially in being closed or silent. |The child sat clamlike, refusing to answer any questions.  孩子像蛤蜊一样坐着，拒绝回答任何问题。|近义词：tight-lipped, reticent, uncommunicative|反义词：talkative, loquacious, communicative|词性:形容词
clamlike:|词源: clam + like. From clam (n.) + -like (suffix).|词源解析: 由名词clam（蛤蜊）和后缀-like（像...的）构成。|词性:形容词
