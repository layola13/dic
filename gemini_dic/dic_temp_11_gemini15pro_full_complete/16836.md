bashful:/ˈbæʃfəl/ (US), /ˈbæʃfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|害羞的，腼腆的|Reluctant to draw attention to oneself; shy.  Characterized by shyness and modesty.|例句：The bashful child hid behind her mother.  害羞的孩子躲在她妈妈身后。|近义词：shy, timid, coy, reserved, diffident|反义词：bold, confident, outgoing, extroverted|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *baschfull, from bash + -ful.  Bash is likely of Scandinavian origin, related to Swedish baska (“to flog, beat”). The original sense was probably “easily abashed,” which evolved to simply “shy.”
