counterstep:/ˈkaʊntərˌstep/| n.|反击，应对措施|An action taken to oppose or counteract another.|The government announced a series of countersteps to address the economic crisis. 政府宣布了一系列应对经济危机的措施。|例句：The company took countersteps to prevent a hostile takeover.  该公司采取了应对措施以防止恶意收购。

例句：Her counterstep to his accusation was a lawsuit. 她对他的指控的反击是提起诉讼。|近义词：countermeasure, response, reaction|反义词：inaction, passivity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + step.  "Counter-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite". "Step" refers to an action or measure taken.
