momentarily:/ˌmoʊmənˈterəli/ /ˌməʊmənˈteərɪli/| adv.|片刻地，暂时地；马上，很快|For a moment; briefly.  In American English, it can also mean "very soon". This second meaning is often criticized by prescriptivists.|The pain stopped momentarily. 疼痛暂时停止了。|The elevator will be with you momentarily. (American English) 电梯马上就到。|近义词：briefly, shortly, soon (in American English)|反义词：permanently, indefinitely|词性:副词
