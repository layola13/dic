auctioneer: /ˌɔːkʃəˈnɪər/ | n. | 拍卖师；拍卖人 | A person who conducts auctions by accepting bids and declaring goods sold. | The auctioneer banged the gavel and declared the painting sold. 拍卖师敲响木槌，宣布这幅画成交。| The auctioneer called for bids on the antique vase.  拍卖师开始对古董花瓶叫价。 |  Synonyms: seller, broker, agent | Antonyms: buyer, bidder | noun
