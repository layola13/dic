dyscrasite:/ˈdɪskɹəˌsaɪt/| n. |辉银矿|A silver antimonide mineral, Ag3Sb, occurring in silver ores. It is a source of silver.|Dyscrasite is a valuable ore mineral found in association with other silver minerals. 辉银矿是一种有价值的矿石矿物，与其他银矿物伴生。|Dyscrasite is often found in hydrothermal veins. 辉银矿常发现于热液矿脉中。|Synonyms: antimonial silver|Antonyms: N/A|noun
