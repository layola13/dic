jobster:/ˈdʒɒbstər/ (US) /ˈdʒɒbstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|求职者；找工作的人|A person who is looking for a job or who is employed in a particular job.  A term popularized by the now-defunct website Jobster.com|例句：Many jobsters are using online platforms to search for new opportunities. 许多求职者正在使用在线平台寻找新的机会。

例句：The jobster finally landed a position after months of searching.  这个求职者在数月的寻找后终于找到了一份工作。|近义词：job seeker, applicant, candidate|反义词:employer, recruiter|词性:noun
