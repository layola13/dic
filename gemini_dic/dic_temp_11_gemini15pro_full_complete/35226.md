clues:/kluːz/ | n. | 线索，提示 |  Pieces of information or evidence that help to solve a mystery or problem. | The detective followed the clues to find the thief. 侦探顺着线索找到了小偷。 | synonyms: hints, tips, leads, evidence, indications | antonyms: answers, solutions | noun

clues:/kluːz/ | v. | 提示，暗示 | To give someone a hint or piece of information indirectly. |  His tone of voice clued me in to his true feelings. 他的语气暗示了我他的真实感受。 | synonyms: hint, suggest, intimate, tip off | antonyms: conceal, hide | verb
