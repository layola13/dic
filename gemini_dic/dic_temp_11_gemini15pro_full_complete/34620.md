clef:/klɛf/
noun
谱号
A symbol placed at the beginning of a staff to indicate the pitch of the notes written on it.
The clef at the beginning of the staff indicates that the notes are in the treble range. 谱号位于五线谱的开头，表示音符处于高音区。

The composer used an unusual clef for the cello part. 作曲家在大提琴部分使用了不寻常的谱号。

近义词: key signature
反义词: None
词性: noun
