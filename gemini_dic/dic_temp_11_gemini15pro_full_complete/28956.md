carry-out:/ˈkæriˌaʊt/ (US), /ˈkæriˌaʊt/ (UK)| n. adj.|外卖，外带; 外卖的，外带的|Food that is prepared in a restaurant and taken elsewhere to be eaten.  Also used as an adjective to describe this type of food.|例句：We ordered Chinese carry-out for dinner. 我们叫了中式外卖当晚餐。

例句：I'd like a carry-out order of Kung Pao Chicken, please. 我想要一份宫保鸡丁外卖。|近义词：take-out, takeaway, to go|反义词：dine-in, eat-in|词性：名词, 形容词
