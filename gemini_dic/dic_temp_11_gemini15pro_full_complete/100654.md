intonationally:/ˌɪntəˈneɪʃənəli/
adverb
语调地，音调上地
By raising his voice intonationally at the end of the sentence, he implied a question.  他通过在句末提高语调暗示这是一个问句。
He spoke intonationally flat, showing little emotion. 他说话语调平淡，几乎没有表现出什么情绪。
Synonym: melodically, tonally
Antonym: monotonously
词性: 副词

Etymology: From "intonation" + "-ally". "Intonation" comes from Late Latin "intonatio," from "intonare" "to thunder, intone," from "in-" + "tonare" "to thunder."
