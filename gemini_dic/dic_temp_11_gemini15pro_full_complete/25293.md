bugbane:/ˈbʌɡbeɪn/
noun
北美黑升麻；臭虫草
A tall North American plant of the buttercup family, with feathery white flowers.  It is also known as black cohosh.  The roots and rhizomes are used medicinally.
The bugbane grew tall in the shady part of the garden.  花园阴凉处长着高高的北美黑升麻。
synonyms: black cohosh, cimicifuga racemosa
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a plant name, could consider  "cultivated plant" if implying it's a wild plant)
