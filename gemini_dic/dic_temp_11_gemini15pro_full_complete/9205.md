antioriental:/ˌæntiɔːˈriːəntl/
adj.|反东方的；仇视东方的|Opposed to or prejudiced against people or cultures of East Asia.|The politician's antioriental rhetoric fueled racial tensions.  这位政客的反东方言论加剧了种族紧张局势。|例句：His antioriental sentiments were evident in his writings. 他的反东方情绪在他的作品中显而易见。|近义词：xenophobic, racist, bigoted|反义词：pro-Asian, tolerant, accepting|词性:形容词

词源:
"anti-" (prefix) + "oriental"
"anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of."
"oriental" comes from Latin "orientalis" meaning "eastern," ultimately from "oriens" meaning "east," related to "orior" meaning "to rise" (as the sun rises in the east).
