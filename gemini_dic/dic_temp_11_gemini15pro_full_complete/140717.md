omnify:/ˈɑːmnɪfaɪ/| v. |使无所不包；使普遍化；赋予一切|To make universal or all-encompassing; to endow with everything.|The company's goal is to omnify its services, making them accessible to everyone, everywhere. 这家公司的目标是使其服务无所不包，让每个人在任何地方都能使用。

The platform aims to omnify the customer experience, providing a seamless journey across all touchpoints. 该平台旨在使客户体验无所不包，在所有接触点提供无缝的旅程。|近义词:universalize, globalize, generalize|反义词:restrict, limit, confine|词性:动词
词源: omni- (all) + -fy (to make) 来自拉丁语 omnis (all).
