dataframe:/ˈdeɪtə freɪm/ | n. |数据框，数据帧| A two-dimensional labeled data structure with columns of potentially different types.  It is generally the most commonly used pandas object. |  The dataframe contains information on sales by region.  这个数据框包含了按地区划分的销售信息。 |  The pandas library in Python allows you to create and manipulate dataframes.  Python中的pandas库允许您创建和操作数据帧。 | synonym: table, grid | antonym: N/A | noun
