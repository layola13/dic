convenery: /ˈkɒnvənəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnvənɛri/ (US)| n. |修道院；修道团体；女子修道院|A building or buildings occupied by a community of monks or nuns living under religious vows, or the community itself, especially one of nuns.|例句：The young woman decided to dedicate her life to God and joined the convenery.  这位年轻女子决定献身于上帝，加入了修道院。


例句：The convenery gardens were a place of peace and tranquility. 修道院的花园是一个宁静祥和的地方。


近义词：nunnery, monastery, abbey, cloister|反义词: - |词性: 名词


词源：From Anglo-French *covenerie, from Old French covent + -erie.  Ultimately from Latin conventus "meeting, assembly".
