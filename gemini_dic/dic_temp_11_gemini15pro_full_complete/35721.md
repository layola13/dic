coccinella:/ˌkɒksɪˈnelə/ (UK) /ˌkɑːksɪˈnelə/ (US)| n. |瓢虫；七星瓢虫|A small beetle with a rounded, usually brightly coloured, spotted shell.|例句：The coccinella landed on the flower. 瓢虫落在花上了。

例句：Coccinella are considered beneficial insects because they eat aphids. 瓢虫被认为是有益的昆虫，因为它们吃蚜虫。

近义词：ladybug, ladybird|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
