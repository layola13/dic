longways:/ˈlɒŋweɪz/ (UK), /ˈlɔːŋweɪz/ (US)| adv.|纵向地， lengthwise|Meaning "in the direction of the length," used especially of the position or direction of something long and narrow.  It can be interchangeable with 'lengthwise'.|They arranged the tables longways in the room. 他们把桌子在房间里纵向排列。| The boat was moored longways to the pier. 这艘船纵向停泊在码头边。|近义词: lengthwise|反义词: crosswise, sideways|词性: 副词

longways:/ˈlɒŋweɪz/ (UK), /ˈlɔːŋweɪz/ (US)| n.|长边，长度|Meaning "in the direction of the length," referring to the longest dimension of something.  Less common than the adverbial use.|The longways of the rug was parallel to the fireplace.  地毯的长边与壁炉平行。|We measured the longways of the field. 我们测量了田地的长度。|近义词: length|反义词: width, breadth|词性: 名词
