backsight:/ˈbækˌsaɪt/| n.|后视，后视读数|A reading taken on a known point to determine the instrument height or reduce observations to a common datum.|The surveyor took a backsight to the benchmark. 测量员对基准点进行了后视。|近义词：plus sight|反义词: foresight|noun

backsight:/ˈbækˌsaɪt/| n.|回顾，事后聪明|Understanding of a situation or event only after it has happened or developed.|With backsight, I realize I should have taken that job offer. 事后看来，我意识到我应该接受那份工作。|近义词：hindsight, retrospect|反义词: foresight|noun
