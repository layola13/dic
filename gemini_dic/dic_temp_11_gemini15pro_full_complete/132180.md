nondamageable:/ˌnɒnˈdæmɪdʒəbl/ | adj. | 不可损坏的，无损的 | Not capable of being damaged; impervious to harm. | This case is made of nondamageable material.  这个盒子是用不可损坏的材料制成的。| The phone screen is coated with a nondamageable film. 手机屏幕上涂了一层不可损坏的膜。 | 近义词: indestructible, impervious, resistant | 反义词: damageable, vulnerable, fragile | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non < PIE *ne "not".  Indicates negation.
damageable (adjective): From damage (noun) + -able (suffix).  Damage comes from Old French damage, ultimately from Latin damnum "loss, hurt".  -able forms adjectives meaning "capable of being".
