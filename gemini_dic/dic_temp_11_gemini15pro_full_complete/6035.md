amenableness:/əˈmiːnəb(ə)lnəs/
n.|顺从；服从；通情达理|The amenableness of the employees to the new regulations made the transition smooth.  员工们对新规章的顺从使得过渡很顺利。|The government praised the amenableness of the rebels to negotiation. 政府赞扬了叛军愿意谈判的态度。|近义词：compliance, acquiescence, tractability, responsiveness|反义词：resistance, defiance, intractability, obstinacy|词性:名词
amenable:/əˈmiːnəb(ə)l/
adj.|易控制的；顺从的；通情达理的|He was amenable to suggestions and willing to compromise. 他乐于接受建议，并愿意妥协。|The problem is amenable to a simple solution. 这个问题可以用一个简单的办法解决。|近义词：receptive, agreeable, compliant, susceptible|反义词：unwilling, resistant, uncooperative, intractable|词性:形容词

词源：From French *amènement* "bringing, leading," from amener "to bring, lead," from Latin *adducere*, from ad- "to" + ducere "to lead."  The sense evolution went from "liable to be brought before a court" to "accountable, responsible" to "responsive, willing to yield."
