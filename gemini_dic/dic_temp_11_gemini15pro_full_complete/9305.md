antiplastic:/ˌæntiˈplæstɪk/
adjective
抗塑性的，抑制生长的
Preventing or inhibiting growth or formation, especially of scar tissue.
This cream contains antiplastic agents that help minimize scarring after surgery.  这种药膏含有抗塑性成分，有助于减少手术后的疤痕。
antiproliferative,  antigrowth
pro-growth, growth-promoting
