ETYMOLOGY->
From dirigible + -ity.  Dirigible comes from French dirigeable,  from diriger "to direct," from Latin dirigere "to direct, guide," from dis- "apart" + regere "to guide, rule."  The suffix -ity denotes a state or quality.
