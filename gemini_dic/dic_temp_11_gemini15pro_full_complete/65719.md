eventognathi: /ɪˌvɛntoʊˈnæθaɪ/ /ɪˌvɛntoʊˈnæθi/
| n. |真骨鱼下纲|A large subclass of ray-finned fishes characterized by a unique jaw structure that allows for protrusion of the premaxilla, facilitating suction feeding.|Eventognathi include fish like carp, minnows, and suckers.|
|近义词: Ostariophysi (partially overlaps, but Ostariophysi is a superorder and includes more groups)|反义词: None (taxonomic term, doesn't have a direct opposite)|词性: Noun
