irrefragable:/ɪˈrefrəɡəbəl/
adjective
无可反驳的，不容置疑的
Indisputable; unbreakable; not to be refuted or overthrown.
Her logic was so irrefragable that no one could argue with her. 她的逻辑如此无可辩驳，以至于没有人能和她争论。
近义词:incontestable, undeniable, irrefutable
反义词:refutable, disputable, questionable
词性:adjective
