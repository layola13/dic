organisational: /ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənl/ (UK) /ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənl/ (US)| adj.|组织的，机构的|Relating to the way in which an organization is structured, arranged, or managed.|例句：The company underwent a major organisational restructuring. 公司进行了重大的组织结构调整。|例句：Organisational skills are essential for project managers. 组织能力对项目经理至关重要。|近义词：structural, administrative, managerial|反义词: disorganized, chaotic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From organize + -ation + -al.  "Organize" ultimately comes from the Greek word "organon" meaning "tool" or "instrument".
