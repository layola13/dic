nonactionable:/ˌnɒnˈækʃənəbl/
adjective
不可控告的，无法起诉的
Not giving sufficient reason to take legal action.
The lawyer determined that the complaint was nonactionable.  律师认定该投诉不可控告。
Their insults were annoying but nonactionable. 他们的侮辱令人恼火，但无法起诉。
近义词:unactionable, non-suable
反义词:actionable, suable
词性:adjective

nonactionable:/ˌnɑːnˈækʃənəbl/
adjective
不可控告的，无法起诉的
Not providing grounds for a lawsuit.
The comment, while offensive, was deemed nonactionable.  虽然这条评论令人反感，但被认为不可控告。
He was advised that the breach of contract was nonactionable.  他被告知违反合同的行为不可起诉。
近义词:unactionable, non-suable
反义词:actionable, suable
词性:adjective


词源:
non- (prefix) + actionable

non-:  from Latin *non "not"
actionable: from action + -able. Action from Old French *acion, from Latin actionem meaning "a doing, a process".  -able from Old French -able and Latin -abilis, meaning "capable of being".
