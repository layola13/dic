outdrinks:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪŋks/
verb
胜过…的酒量; 比…喝得多
consumes more alcoholic beverages than someone else.
He outdrinks everyone at the party. 他在派对上比任何人都喝得多。
近义词: out-chugs, out-imbibes
反义词: is outdrunk by
词性: verb
