iciness:/ˈaɪsinəs/| n.|冰冷，冷淡，冷漠|The iciness of her stare made him shiver. 她的眼神冰冷得让他打了个寒颤。|例句：The iciness of the wind chilled me to the bone. 刺骨的寒风让我冷到了骨子里。

例句：Her iciness towards him was obvious to everyone. 她对他的冷漠显而易见。|近义词：coldness, frigidity, chilliness|反义词:warmth, cordiality|词性:名词

词源: From icy + -ness.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *īsaz.
