overopinionated: /ˌoʊvərəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪd/
| adj. | 过于自以为是的，固执己见的 | Having or expressing too many opinions, especially in a way that is annoying or unwelcome.  | He's so overopinionated that no one wants to have a discussion with him. 他太固执己见了，以至于没有人想和他讨论。 | Example: She's known for being overopinionated and often dominates conversations. 她以固执己见而闻名，经常主导谈话。 | Synonyms: dogmatic, opinionated, assertive, insistent, inflexible | Antonyms: open-minded, receptive, flexible, agreeable, amenable | adjective
