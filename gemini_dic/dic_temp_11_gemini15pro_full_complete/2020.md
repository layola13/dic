actuated: /ˈæktʃueɪtɪd/
adjective
启动的，驱动的，激发的
Driven or impelled to action; started or set in motion.
The pump is actuated by a small electric motor.  这个泵是由一台小型电动机驱动的。
The security alarm was actuated by a motion sensor.  安全警报是由运动传感器触发的。
Synonym: activated, triggered, motivated, prompted
Antonym: deactivated, stalled, inhibited
词性: adjective
