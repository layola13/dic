altazimuth: /ˌæltˈæzɪməθ/ | n. | 地平坐标，地平经纬仪 | An altazimuth is a mounting for a telescope or other instrument that allows it to be rotated both vertically (in altitude) and horizontally (in azimuth). | The observatory's main telescope is mounted on an altazimuth.  天文台的主望远镜安装在一个地平装置上。|  The altazimuth mounting allowed for precise tracking of the satellite. 地平式装置允许精确跟踪卫星。 | synonym: altazimuth mounting | antonym: equatorial mount | noun

altazimuth: /ˌæltˈæzɪməθ/ | adj. | 地平的 | Relating to or measured by an altazimuth. | The altazimuth coordinates of the star were recorded. 记录了这颗星的地平坐标。| The telescope's altazimuth design made it easy to use.  这架望远镜的地平式设计使它易于使用。| synonym: horizontal | antonym: equatorial | adjective
