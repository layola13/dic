dentirostral:/ˌdɛntɪˈrɒstrəl/
adjective
齿喙的，指向喙的
Relating to the beak or bill, specifically to the tooth-like projections on the beak of some birds.
Some species of birds have dentirostral beaks that help them grip slippery fish.  某些鸟类有齿喙，可以帮助它们抓住滑溜的鱼。
The puffin's dentirostral beak is perfectly adapted for catching small fish. 海鹦的齿喙非常适合捕捉小鱼。
serrated, toothed
smooth
