matildes:/mɑːˈtildɛz/| n.| 波浪号；颚化符|A diacritical mark (~) placed over a letter, especially in Spanish, to indicate a palatal nasal sound, as in cañon.|The word "cañon" uses a tilde over the 'n'.|近义词:diacritic, accent mark|反义词: (No direct antonym for a punctuation mark)|noun
