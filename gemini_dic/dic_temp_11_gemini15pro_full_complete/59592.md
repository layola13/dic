ebullient:/ɪˈbʌl.i.ənt/ /ɪˈbuː.li.ənt/
adjective
热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的；沸腾的
overflowing with enthusiasm and excitement; boiling.
She was in an ebullient mood after receiving the good news. 她收到好消息后心情非常激动。
The ebullient crowd cheered for their team. 热情洋溢的观众为他们的球队欢呼。
synonyms: enthusiastic, exuberant, lively, cheerful, buoyant, effervescent, high-spirited
antonyms: depressed, gloomy, downcast, dejected, subdued, apathetic, listless
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ēbulliēns, present participle of ēbullire (“to boil over”), from ex- (“out”) + bullire (“to boil, bubble”), from bulla (“bubble”).
