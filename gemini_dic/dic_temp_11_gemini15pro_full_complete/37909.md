complementary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/ | adj. | 补充的，互补的 | Enhancing or emphasizing the qualities of something else, especially when contrasting. |  The two colors are complementary. 这两种颜色互补。| synonyms: corresponding, matching, balancing, reciprocal | antonyms: conflicting, clashing, contradictory | adjective

complementary:/ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentɛri/ | adj. | 补充的，互补的 | Enhancing or emphasizing the qualities of something else, especially when contrasting. | Complementary colors create a visually appealing contrast. 互补色创造了视觉上吸引人的对比。 | synonyms: corresponding, matching, balancing, reciprocal | antonyms: conflicting, clashing, contradictory | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin complementarius, from complementum "that which fills up or completes," from complere "to fill up, complete," from com- "together" + plere "to fill."
