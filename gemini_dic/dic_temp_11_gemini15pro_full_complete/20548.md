blab:/blæb/| v. n.|泄露秘密，闲聊；饶舌；泄露的秘密|To reveal secrets, typically indiscreetly; to chatter.|例句：He blabbed everything to the police. 他把一切都告诉了警察。|近义词：tattle, gossip, divulge, reveal|反义词: conceal, hide|词性:动词,名词


blabs:/blæbz/| v. (blab的第三人称单数形式)|泄露秘密，闲聊；饶舌|Third person singular present tense form of "blab".|例句：She blabs all her secrets to her diary. 她把所有的秘密都写在日记里。|近义词：tattles, gossips, divulges, reveals|反义词: conceals, hides|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch  *blabberen, frequentative of  blappen  "to blabber," probably of imitative origin.
