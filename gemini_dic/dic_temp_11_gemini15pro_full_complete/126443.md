mystificator:/ˈmɪstɪfɪkeɪtər/ | n. |神秘化者；故弄玄虚的人|Someone who mystifies; a person who practices mystification.  A person who deliberately makes something appear mysterious or obscure, especially for their own advantage. | He was a master mystificator, shrouding his true intentions in a veil of secrecy. 他是一个故弄玄虚的大师，用一层神秘的面纱掩盖了他的真实意图。|Synonyms: charlatan, deceiver, trickster, illusionist, fraud, impostor | Antonyms: explainer, clarifier, demystifier | noun
