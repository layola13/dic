clogged:/ˈklɒɡd/ (UK) /ˈklɑːɡd/ (US)| adj.|堵塞的，阻塞的|Blocked or filled with something that prevents movement or flow.|The drain is clogged with hair.  排水管被头发堵塞了。|近义词：blocked, obstructed, jammed|反义词:clear, open, unobstructed|adjective


clog:/ˈklɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈklɑːɡ/ (US)| v. n.|（使）阻塞，障碍物|To block or become blocked.|The sink clogged and the water overflowed. 水槽堵塞了，水溢出来了。|近义词：block, obstruct, jam|反义词:clear, open, unclog|verb, noun


词源:  From Middle English *cloggen, from Old Norse *klokka (“block, log”), from Proto-Germanic *klukkaną (“lump, block”).  Cognate with Dutch klog (“clog, wooden shoe”), German Klotz (“block, lump”).
