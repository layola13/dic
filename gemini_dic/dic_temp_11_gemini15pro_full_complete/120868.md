misbestowed:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊd/
adjective
错误授予的，给予不当的
placed or given wrongly or inappropriately.
His kindness was misbestowed on the ungrateful wretch. 他对那个忘恩负义的家伙的仁慈被错误地赋予了。
misapplied, misused, misplaced, ill-bestowed
appropriately bestowed, well-placed
