baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /ˈbɑːkʃiːʃ/
n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in some Asian and Middle Eastern countries.  Also, a bribe.|
He gave the porter a small baksheesh.|他给了搬运工一小笔小费。
She offered a baksheesh to the official to expedite the process.|她向官员行贿以加快办理进程。
近义词:tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:noun

baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /ˈbɑːkʃiːʃ/
vt.|给…小费，贿赂…|To give a tip or bribe to someone.|
He baksheeshed the waiter for better service.|他给了服务员小费以获得更好的服务。
The company was accused of baksheeshing government officials.|该公司被指控贿赂政府官员。
近义词:tip, bribe
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:verb
