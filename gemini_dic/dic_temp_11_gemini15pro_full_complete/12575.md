aspish:/ˈæspɪʃ/
adjective
像蛇一样的; 阴险的; 恶毒的
Resembling or suggesting a viper, especially in being venomous or malignant.
She gave him an aspish look. 她恶狠狠地瞪了他一眼。
近义词: viperine, venomous, malignant, malevolent, spiteful, malicious, cruel, nasty
反义词: kind, benevolent, benign
词性: adjective


aspish:/ˈæspɪʃ/
adjective
渴望的，贪婪的; 充满野心的
Marked by eager desire and ambition : covetous
He had an aspish desire for power. 他对权力有着贪婪的渴望。
近义词: covetous, greedy, avaricious, ambitious, grasping
反义词: generous, selfless
词性: adjective


词源:
From asp + -ish.  Asp refers to a small venomous snake of Africa and Eurasia.  The suffix -ish forms adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the qualities of."
