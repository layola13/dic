kamikoto:/ˌkɑːmiˈkoʊtoʊ/
|n.|神言；日语中的“神言”（かみごと）|A Japanese word meaning "the word of God" or "divine utterance."  It often refers to words or pronouncements considered authoritative or absolute.|The emperor's decree was seen as kamikoto and obeyed without question.  天皇的法令被视为神言，毫无疑问地被服从了。|近义词:edict, decree, pronouncement, oracle|反义词:blasphemy, heresy|noun
