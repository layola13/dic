motivations:/ˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃənz/ | n. | 动机，动力 | Something that causes a person to act in a particular way; the reason or reasons for acting or behaving in a particular way. |  Her motivations for leaving her job were never clear. 她离开工作的原因一直不清楚。

The company's main motivation is profit. 该公司的主要动机是利润。| synonyms: incentives, drives, reasons, impulses | antonyms: deterrents, disincentives | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *motivacioun*, from Late Latin *mōtivātiō*, from Latin *mōtus* (“motion”), past participle of *moveō* (“I move”).
