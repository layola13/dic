nonreceivables:/ˌnɑːnriˈsiːvəblz/
n.
非应收款项
Amounts due that are not considered receivables, such as deferred revenue or prepayments.
The company's nonreceivables increased significantly this quarter due to a large influx of customer prepayments for services to be rendered next year.  该公司本季度的非应收款项大幅增加，原因是客户为明年将要提供的服务预付了大量款项。
Near synonyms: non-trade receivables, deferred revenue, prepayments
Antonyms: receivables, trade receivables
noun
