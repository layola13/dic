colonist:/ˈkɒlənɪst/ (US: /ˈkɑːlənɪst/)| n. |殖民者；殖民地居民|A person who lives in a colony, especially one sent there by a colonizing power.|例句：The early colonists faced many hardships. 早期的殖民者面临许多困难。

例句：The colonists established a new settlement. 殖民者建立了一个新的定居点。

近义词：settler, pioneer, immigrant|反义词：native, indigenous|词性：名词
