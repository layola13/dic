crocky:/ˈkrɒki/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːki/ (US)| adj.|破旧的，摇摇欲坠的；虚弱的；有病的；令人讨厌的，让人不舒服的|Something that is crocky is old, dilapidated, or in poor condition; it can also mean unwell, ill, or generally unpleasant or undesirable.|例句：The old house was a bit crocky and needed a lot of repairs. 那老房子有点破旧，需要大量修缮。

例句：I felt a bit crocky after eating that dodgy kebab.  吃了那个可疑的烤肉串后，我觉得有点不舒服。|近义词：rickety, ramshackle, dilapidated, unwell, ill, dodgy, unpleasant|反义词：sturdy, robust, healthy, pleasant, agreeable|词性:形容词
