nonexertion:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɜːʃən/ | n. | 不用力; 不费力 | Absence of exertion; the state of not exerting oneself physically or mentally. |  He valued the nonexertion of a quiet Sunday afternoon. 他很珍惜安静的周日下午的悠闲时光。|  The painting seemed to be a study in nonexertion, capturing a moment of peaceful stillness. 这幅画似乎是对不费力的研究，捕捉到了宁静祥和的瞬间。 | 近义词: relaxation, rest, inactivity, ease | 反义词: exertion, effort, activity, labor | 词性: 名词


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "exertion" (from Latin "exertus," past participle of "exserere" meaning "to put forth").
