immoderateness:/ɪˌmɒdərətnəs/| n. |无节制，过度，不温和|The state or quality of being immoderate; excess; lack of restraint.|His immoderateness in eating led to health problems. 他饮食无节制导致了健康问题。|Synonyms: excessiveness, extravagance, intemperance, unrestraint|Antonyms: moderation, temperance, restraint|noun


immoderate:/ɪˈmɒdərət/| adj. |无节制的，过度的，不温和的|Not moderate; exceeding just or reasonable limits; excessive.|Immoderate drinking can damage your liver. 过度饮酒会损害你的肝脏。|Synonyms: excessive, extravagant, intemperance, unrestraint|Antonyms: moderate, temperate, restrained|adjective
