hyperoxygenate:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/ | v. |过度充氧；使氧合过多|To increase the oxygen concentration in (a tissue, organ, or the body as a whole) above normal levels.|The patient was hyperoxygenated before the procedure.  在手术前，病人被过度充氧。| The diver was hyperoxygenated before the deep dive. 潜水员在深潜之前进行了过度充氧。|近义词:oxygenate, superoxygenate|反义词:deoxygenate|词性:动词

词源: hyper- (过度) + oxygen (氧) + -ate (动词后缀)
