forcipated:/ˈfɔːrsɪpeɪtɪd/| adj.|钳形的，镊子状的，分叉的|Resembling forceps; forcipate.  Having the shape or form of forceps; forked.|The beetle has forcipated mandibles.  这只甲虫有钳形的下颚。|近义词：forked, pincer-like, chelate|反义词:unbranched, undivided|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *forcipatus*, past participle of *forcipare* "to seize with forceps", from *forceps* "forceps, tongs".
