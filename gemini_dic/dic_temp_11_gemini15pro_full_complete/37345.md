commendam:/kəˈmɛndəm/ | n. | 托管，委托，兼管 | A benefice given in commendam is entrusted to the care of a clergyman, other than the incumbent, until a proper pastor is provided. | The bishop held the living in commendam. 主教兼管着这个教区。 | 近义词: trust, care, custody | 反义词: neglect | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *commenda, from Latin commendare "to entrust," from com- "together" + mandare "to entrust."
