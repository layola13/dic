brasilin:/brəˈzɪlɪn/
noun
巴西木素;苏木精
A reddish crystalline compound, C16H14O5, extracted from Brazilwood and used as a dye and in microscopy.
Brazilin is a natural dye extracted from brazilwood. 巴西木素是一种从巴西木中提取的天然染料。
Microscopists use brasilin to stain cell nuclei. 显微镜学家使用巴西木素来染色细胞核。
Synonyms: brazilwood extract, Natural Red 24
Antonyms: None (as it's a specific chemical compound)
