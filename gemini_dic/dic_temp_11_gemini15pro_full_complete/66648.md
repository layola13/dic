exists:/ɪɡˈzɪsts/ | v. |存在，生存|Being or occurring in fact or actuality; having reality. |Dinosaurs no longer exist. 恐龙已经不复存在了。| The problem still exists. 这个问题仍然存在。|近义词：live, survive, occur, be present|反义词:perish, vanish, disappear|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin existere "stand forth, appear, emerge; arise, come into being, begin to be; be," from ex "out" (see ex-) + sistere "cause to stand," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm."

USAGE->

He exists on very little money. 他靠很少的钱生存。
Water exists in three forms: solid, liquid, and gas. 水以三种形式存在：固态、液态和气态。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The problem is still existing. 这个问题仍然存在。 (Note: "existing" is less common than "exists" in present continuous)
