obliger:/əˈblaɪdʒər/| n.|施恩者，帮手|A person who obliges or does a favor.  The museum is grateful to its many obligers. 博物馆非常感谢它的众多捐助者。|近义词：benefactor, patron, supporter|反义词：ingrate, freeloader|noun

oblige:/əˈblaɪdʒ/| vt.|迫使，责成；施恩惠于，帮…的忙|1. To compel or require someone to do something by legal, moral, or social means. The law obliges parents to send their children to school. 法律规定父母必须送孩子上学。 2. To do a favor or service for someone.  Could you oblige me by opening the window? 你能帮我开一下窗户吗？|近义词：compel, force, require, favor, accommodate|反义词：exempt, excuse, refuse|verb
