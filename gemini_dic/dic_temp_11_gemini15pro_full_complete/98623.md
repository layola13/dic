instrumentality:/ˌɪnstrəmɛnˈtælɪti/ | n. |手段，工具，媒介; 代理; 中介|A means by which something is done or achieved; agency; intermediary. |The internet has become a vital instrumentality for communication. 互联网已成为重要的通信手段。|Synonyms: means, agency, medium, vehicle, channel, tool, apparatus, implement, device|Antonyms: obstruction, hindrance, impediment|noun

instrumentality:/ˌɪnstrəmɛnˈtælɪti/ | n. |作用，影响力|The power or quality of being instrumental in producing a particular effect. |The instrumentality of education in shaping a person's future cannot be overstated.  教育在塑造一个人未来方面的影响力怎么强调都不为过。|Synonyms: influence, impact, effect, power, force, leverage|Antonyms: impotence, weakness, ineffectiveness|noun
