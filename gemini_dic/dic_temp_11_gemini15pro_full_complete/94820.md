impurities:/ɪmˈpjʊrɪtiz/| n. |杂质，不纯物|the presence of a substance that is harmful or lowers the quality of something| The water contained impurities that made it unsafe to drink.  水里含有杂质，不能安全饮用。| synonyms: contaminants, adulterants, pollutants | antonyms: purities, cleanliness | noun

impurity:/ɪmˈpjʊrɪti/| n. |杂质，不纯|the state of being impure; the quality or condition of containing a contaminant |The impurity of the water made it unsuitable for drinking. 水的杂质使其不适合饮用。| synonyms: contaminant, adulterant, pollutant | antonyms: purity, cleanliness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *impurite*, from Old French *impureté*, from Late Latin *impūritās*, from Latin *impūrus* (“impure”), from *in-* (“not”) + *pūrus* (“pure”).
