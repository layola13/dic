multarticulate:/ˌmʌltɪɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/
| adj. |多关节的；多音节的；善于表达的|Having many joints or articulations; having many syllables; capable of expressing oneself clearly and fluently.|The multarticulate creature slithered through the undergrowth.  这多关节的生物在灌木丛中滑行。|近义词：articulate, eloquent, fluent, loquacious|反义词：inarticulate, tongue-tied|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *multus* (many) + *articulatus* (jointed, articulate).
