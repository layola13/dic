janitrix:/ˈdʒænɪtrɪks/
noun
女清洁工, 女门房
A female janitor.  This term is considered by some to be outdated and potentially sexist, as it specifically marks the gender of the role.  Modern usage often prefers the gender-neutral term "janitor" or "custodian."
The janitrix cleaned the floors every evening. 女清洁工每天晚上都打扫地板。
Synonyms: cleaner, custodian, caretaker, housekeeper
Antonyms: N/A (The opposite wouldn't be a profession but a state of uncleanliness or lack of caretaking.)
