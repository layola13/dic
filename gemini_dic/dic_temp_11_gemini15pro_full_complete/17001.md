basylous:/ˈbæsɪləs/| adj.|基本的，基础的，基部的|Relating to, situated at, or forming the base.  Pertaining to or arising from a base. Located at or near the base of a plant or animal organ.|The basylous leaves are arranged spirally around the stem. 基部的叶子螺旋状地排列在茎的周围。|Near-basal, basal|Apical, distal|adjective
Etymology->From Ancient Greek βάσις (básis, “base”) + -ous.
