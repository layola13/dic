mischarge:/ˌmɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒ/
n.|误收费，多收的费用|An incorrect charge, especially one that is too high.|The customer noticed a mischarge on his bill and contacted the company. 顾客注意到账单上有笔误收费，于是联系了公司。|overcharge, overbilling|undercharge|noun
mischarge:/ˌmɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒ/
vt.|错误地收费，多收（费用）|To charge incorrectly, especially to charge too much.|The cashier mischarged me for the groceries. 收银员多收了我一些杂货的钱。|overcharge, overbill|undercharge|verb
