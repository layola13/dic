campaign:/kæmˈpeɪn/| n. vi.|运动，战役|A series of planned activities that are intended to achieve a particular result.|The presidential campaign was long and arduous. 总统竞选漫长而艰巨。|近义词：drive, movement, operation|反义词：inaction, passivity|noun

campaign:/kæmˈpeɪn/| vi.|参加运动，作战|To take part in or lead a campaign.|She campaigned tirelessly for women's rights. 她不知疲倦地为妇女权利奔走。|近义词：advocate, fight, strive|反义词：yield, surrender|verb
