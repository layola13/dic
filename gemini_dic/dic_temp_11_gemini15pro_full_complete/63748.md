epicele:/ˈɛpɪˌsiːl/| n. |腹股沟疝|A hernia protruding into the upper part of the scrotum.  A type of inguinal hernia in which bowel protrudes into the scrotum.|The patient presented with an epicele that required surgical repair. 病人出现了一个需要手术修复的腹股沟疝。|Synonym: inguinal hernia, scrotal hernia|Antonym: N/A (Hernias don't have a direct opposite.)|Noun
