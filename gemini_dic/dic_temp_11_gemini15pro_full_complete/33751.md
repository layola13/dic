circular:/ˈsɜːrkjələr/ | adj. |圆形的，环形的；循环的；迂回的|Having the shape of a circle; round. Moving in a circle or circuit; recurring or revolving. Indirect or roundabout.|The archaeologist discovered a circular stone altar. 考古学家发现了一个圆形石坛。|近义词：round, cyclic, rotary|反义词：straight, linear|词性: adjective

circular:/ˈsɜːrkjələr/ | n. |通告，通知|A letter or notice that is sent to a large number of people.|The school sent out a circular about the upcoming sports day. 学校发了一份关于即将到来的运动会的通知。|近义词：notice, bulletin, leaflet|反义词：-|词性: noun
