cowhiding:/ˈkaʊˌhaɪdɪŋ/
n.
鞭打；以牛皮鞭笞
The act of whipping someone with a cowhide.
He received a cowhiding for stealing the apples. 他因为偷苹果而被鞭打。
flogging, whipping, lashing
praising, commending
noun
