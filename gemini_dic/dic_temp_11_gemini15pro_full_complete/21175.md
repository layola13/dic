blobbing:/ˈblɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈblɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| n. |涂抹; 扩展;  |The act of spreading or expanding like a blob, often in a messy or uncontrolled way.  It can also refer to working with or manipulating blobs of something, particularly in computing contexts (e.g., blob storage).|The paint was blobbing on the canvas.|近义词：spreading, expanding, smearing, daubing|反义词: contracting, shrinking|词性:Noun (Gerund/Present Participle)

blobbing:/ˈblɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈblɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| v. |涂抹; 扩展;  |Present participle of "to blob," meaning to spread or expand like a blob.
