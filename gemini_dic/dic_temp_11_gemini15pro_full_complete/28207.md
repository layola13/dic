capriform:/ˈkæprɪfɔːrm/
adjective
羊形的；山羊状的
Resembling or shaped like a goat, especially having a goat's horns.
The capriform statue in the garden was quite striking. 花园里山羊形状的雕塑引人注目。
Near synonyms: goat-like, hircine
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider using descriptions like "unlike a goat," "not goat-shaped")
词性: adjective
