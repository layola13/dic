demilitarization:/diːˌmɪlɪtərɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|去军事化，非军事化|The demilitarization of the area was a key part of the peace treaty.  该地区的非军事化是和平条约的关键部分。|
Example: The demilitarization of the Rhineland after World War I was intended to prevent future conflict.  第一次世界大战后莱茵兰的非军事化旨在防止未来的冲突。
Example:  The agreement called for the complete demilitarization of the border zone. 该协议要求边境地区完全非军事化。
Synonym: disarmament, neutralization
Antonym: militarization, armament
