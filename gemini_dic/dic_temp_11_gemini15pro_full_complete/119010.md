methoding:/ˈmɛθədɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
制定方法，系统化
Systematically developing and applying methods to a task or process.  This word is not standard English and is rarely used.
There's no good example sentence because "methoding" is not standard English.  A more natural way to express this would be "developing a method" or "systematizing."
近义词: systematizing, organizing, structuring, planning
反义词: improvising, randomizing
词性: verb (present participle)
