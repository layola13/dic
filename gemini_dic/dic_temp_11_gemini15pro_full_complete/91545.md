hyper-conservative:/ˌhaɪpər kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/ | adj. |极端保守的，极端守旧的 | Extremely conservative; holding rigidly to traditional values and resistant to change. |  His hyper-conservative views on social issues alienated many voters. 他在社会问题上极端保守的观点疏远了许多选民。

| Example: The hyper-conservative faction within the party blocked any attempts at reform.  党内极端保守派阻挠了任何改革的尝试。| Synonym: ultraconservative, reactionary, die-hard | Antonym: progressive, liberal, radical | Adjective
