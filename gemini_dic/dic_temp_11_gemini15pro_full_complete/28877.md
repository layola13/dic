carpological: /ˌkɑːrpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
果实学的；果实形态学的
Relating to the study of fruits and seeds, especially their structure and classification.
Carpological studies can help us understand the evolutionary relationships between different plant species. 果实学研究可以帮助我们理解不同植物物种之间的进化关系。
The botanist's carpological research focused on the dispersal mechanisms of various seeds.  这位植物学家的果实学研究集中在各种种子的传播机制上。
近义词: fructuous
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms related to other plant parts like "foliage" or "floral")
词性: adjective
