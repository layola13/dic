kahau:/kəˈhaʊ/ | n. |长鼻猴，雄性长鼻猴|A large monkey (Nasalis larvatus) of Borneo, the male of which has a very large, pendulous nose.|The kahau is known for its distinctive proboscis.  长鼻猴以其独特的长鼻而闻名。|The kahau swung effortlessly through the trees. 长鼻猴轻松地在树间荡来荡去。|Synonym: proboscis monkey, long-nosed monkey |Antonym: N/A | Noun
