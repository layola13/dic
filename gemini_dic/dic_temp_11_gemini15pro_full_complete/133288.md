nonexpediently:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspiːdɪəntli/
adverb
不适宜地，不利地
Not practical or advisable; not suitable for achieving a particular purpose.
The funds were nonexpediently allocated, resulting in a budget shortfall. 资金分配不当，导致预算短缺。
He acted nonexpediently by ignoring the advice of his lawyer. 他无视律师的建议，行事不明智。
近义词:inadvisably, unwisely, imprudently, inappropriately
反义词: wisely, prudently, advisably, appropriately
词性:adverb

词源:
From non- + expedient + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "expedient" comes from the Latin "expediens" (present participle of "expedire" meaning "to extricate, disengage, set free"), and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
