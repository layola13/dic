kids:/kɪdz/ | n. | 孩子们 | Children (plural).  It refers to more than one child. |  The kids are playing in the park. 孩子们正在公园里玩耍。

The kids are excited about the trip. 孩子们对这次旅行感到兴奋。 | 近义词: children, youngsters, offspring | 反义词: adults, grown-ups | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *kides*, plural of *kid*, a young goat, from Old Norse *kið* (young goat). The meaning shifted to refer to human children, probably influenced by the playful nature of young goats.
