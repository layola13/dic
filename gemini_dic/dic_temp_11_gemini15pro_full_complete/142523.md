ossiculum:/ɒˈsɪkjʊləm/ | n. |小骨 |A small bone, especially one of the three bones in the middle ear (malleus, incus, and stapes).| The ossicles of the middle ear transmit vibrations from the eardrum to the inner ear. 中耳的小骨将振动从鼓膜传递到内耳。| synonyms: bonelet, ossicle | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a small bone) | noun
