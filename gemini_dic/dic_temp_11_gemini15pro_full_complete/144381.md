outstream:/ˈaʊtˌstriːm/| n.|外部流媒体;站外视频广告|A type of video advertising that appears outside of the traditional online video platforms, such as YouTube or Vimeo. It refers to video ads served on websites and apps other than video-sharing platforms. |Example: Outstream video ads can appear within articles, social media feeds, or mobile apps.  站外视频广告可以出现在文章、社交媒体Feed或移动应用中。

Example: The company decided to invest more in outstream video advertising to reach a wider audience. 该公司决定在站外视频广告上投入更多资金，以覆盖更广泛的受众。|近义词:native video advertising|反义词:instream video advertising|词性:noun
