ETYMOLOGY->
From Medieval Latin *alcoholicus, from Arabic al-kuḥl "kohl," referring to a fine powder used as eye makeup.  The sense transferred from "finely divided substance" to "pure spirit of wine," then to "liquor," and finally to addiction to it.
