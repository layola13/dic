bal:/bɔːl/ | n. | 舞会，正式舞会 | A large formal gathering for dancing. |  She wore a beautiful gown to the bal. 她穿了一件漂亮的礼服去参加舞会。| synonym: ball, formal dance | antonym: casual gathering | noun

bal:/bæl/ | n. | (法语)面具舞会，化妆舞会 | A masked ball or fancy dress ball. | The bal masqué was a grand affair. 化妆舞会是一场盛大的活动。| synonym: masked ball, masquerade | antonym:  informal party | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bal*, from Old French *bal*, from Late Latin *ballum*, "to dance," of Germanic origin.
