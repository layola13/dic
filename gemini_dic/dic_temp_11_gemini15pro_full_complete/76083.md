furriers:/ˈfɜːriərz/
n.
皮货商；毛皮商
A person who makes, repairs, or sells fur garments.
The furriers are preparing for the winter season. 皮货商们正在为冬季做准备。
近义词：fur trader, peltmonger
反义词： 无
词性：名词
