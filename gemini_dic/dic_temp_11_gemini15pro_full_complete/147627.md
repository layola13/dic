overplotting:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːtɪŋ/
n.
过度绘制，重叠绘制
The act of plotting too many data points in a single visualization, obscuring patterns and making it difficult to interpret the data.
The scatter plot suffered from overplotting, making it hard to see the true distribution of the data.  散点图存在过度绘制的问题，难以看出数据的真实分布。
Overplotting made the graph unreadable. 过度绘制使图表难以阅读。
overlap, occlusion
clarity, separation
noun
