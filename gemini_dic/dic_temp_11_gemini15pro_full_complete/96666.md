industries:/ˈɪndəstriːz/
noun
工业，产业
the aggregate of manufacturing or technically productive enterprises in a particular field, often named after its principal product
The automotive industry is facing a major shift towards electric vehicles. 汽车行业正面临着向电动汽车的重大转变。
synonyms: businesses, trades, manufactures, commerce
antonyms: unemployment, idleness
词性: 名词
