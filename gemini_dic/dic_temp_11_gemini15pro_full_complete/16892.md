basinlike:/ˈbeɪsɪnlaɪk/
adjective
像盆的；盆状的
Resembling a basin in shape; shaped like a basin.
The valley was basinlike, surrounded by high mountains.  山谷像盆地一样，四周环绕着高山。
The crater had a basinlike formation.  火山口形成了盆地状。
bowl-shaped, concave, depressed
convex, protruding
