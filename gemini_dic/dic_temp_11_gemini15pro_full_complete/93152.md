ignifluous:/ɪɡˈnɪfluːəs/
adjective
喷火的；如火的
Emitting or exuding flames; fiery.
The ignifluous dragon scorched the earth with its breath.  喷火的巨龙用它的呼吸烧焦了大地。
The volcano's ignifluous eruption lit up the night sky. 火山喷发的火焰照亮了夜空。
近义词: fiery, flaming, incandescent, burning
反义词: extinguished, cold, icy


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ignifluus, from ignis (“fire”) + fluere (“to flow”).
