noninterchangeability:/ˌnɒnɪntətʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnɪntərˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可互换性，不可交换性|The noninterchangeability of these parts makes repairs difficult.  这些零件的不可互换性使得维修很困难。|例句：The noninterchangeability of the two systems caused significant compatibility issues. 这两个系统不可互换导致了严重的兼容性问题。

例句：The noninterchangeability of specialized medical equipment necessitates careful inventory management.  专用医疗设备的不可互换性使得必须谨慎管理库存。|近义词：incompatibility, uniqueness, singularity|反义词：interchangeability, compatibility, exchangeability|词性:名词

noninterchangeable:/ˌnɒnɪntətʃeɪndʒəbl/ /ˌnɑːnɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/| adj. |不可互换的，不可交换的|These parts are noninterchangeable, so be careful not to lose any. 这些零件不可互换，所以小心不要丢失任何一个。|例句：The parts for this engine are noninterchangeable with other models.  这个引擎的零件与其他型号不可互换。

例句：The company uses noninterchangeable components to protect its intellectual property.  该公司使用不可互换的组件来保护其知识产权。|近义词：incompatible, unique, specific|反义词：interchangeable, compatible, generic|词性:形容词
