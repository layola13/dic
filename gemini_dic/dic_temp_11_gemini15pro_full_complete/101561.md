ironsides:/ˈaɪərnˌsaɪdz/| n.|铁甲; 坚强的人; 克伦威尔; |A person of inflexible resolve, especially in politics.  Originally a nickname of Oliver Cromwell. |Cromwell's Ironsides were feared throughout England. 克伦威尔的铁骑兵让整个英格兰闻风丧胆。|近义词：stalwart, resolute, steadfast|反义词:weak, irresolute, wavering|词性:名词

ironsides:/ˈaɪərnˌsaɪdz/| n.|铁甲舰|A warship clad in iron armor plating, especially one of the earliest such ships.|The ironside proved effective against traditional wooden warships. 铁甲舰被证明对传统的木制战舰很有效。|近义词：ironclad|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "wooden ship")|词性:名词
