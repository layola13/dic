besiegement: /bɪˈsiːdʒmənt/
n.
包围，围困
The state of being besieged; the act of besieging.
The city was under besiegement for months.  这座城市被围困了数月。
The castle withstood a long besiegement.  这座城堡经受住了长时间的围困。
近义词: siege, blockade, encirclement
反义词: liberation, relief
