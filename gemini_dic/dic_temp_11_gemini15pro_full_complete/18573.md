benevolent:/bəˈnevələnt/| adj.|仁慈的；慈善的；乐善好施的|Well meaning and kindly. Showing kindness or goodwill.|例句：He was a benevolent ruler, loved by all his people. 他是一位仁慈的统治者，深受子民爱戴。

例句：Her benevolent smile warmed the hearts of everyone present. 她慈祥的微笑温暖了在场所有人的心。|近义词：kind, compassionate, charitable, humane, philanthropic|反义词：malevolent, cruel, unkind, heartless|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *benevolens*, present participle of *benevolēre* "to wish well," from *bene* "well" + *volēre* "to wish".
