backwoodsiest:/ˌbækˈwʊdziɪst/
adjective
最偏僻的，最落后的
Most remote and undeveloped; characteristic of remote, sparsely populated areas.
The backwoodsiest parts of the Appalachian Mountains are home to unique communities. 阿巴拉契亚山脉最偏僻的地区是一些独特社区的家园。
Synonyms:  remote, isolated, rural, rustic, undeveloped, uncivilized
Antonyms: urban, metropolitan, developed, sophisticated, civilized
词性: adjective
