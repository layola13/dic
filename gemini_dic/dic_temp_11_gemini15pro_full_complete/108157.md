latitudes:/ˈlætitjuːdz/| n.|纬度，自由，自由范围|The geographical latitudes determine the climate.  地理纬度决定气候。|例句：

At these latitudes, the sun does not rise high in the sky.
在这些纬度上，太阳不会升得很高。

The company gives its employees considerable latitude in choosing their work schedules.
公司给予员工相当大的自由度来选择他们的工作时间表。|近义词：freedom, scope, range, leeway|反义词:restriction, constraint|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *latitude*, from Old French *latitude*, from Latin *lātitūdō* (“breadth, width, extent, size, compass, range”), from *lātus* (“broad, wide”).
