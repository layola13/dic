misemphasized:/ˌmɪsˈɛmfəsaɪzd/ | adj. |  错误强调的；未充分强调的 |  Incorrectly or insufficiently emphasized.  Having the wrong emphasis placed upon. | The report misemphasized the role of technology in the company's success, focusing instead on marketing. 这份报告错误地强调了技术在公司成功中的作用，反而侧重于营销。

The artist felt his work was misemphasized in the exhibition, overshadowed by more flamboyant pieces.  这位艺术家觉得他的作品在展览中没有得到充分的重视，被更华丽的作品掩盖了。| 近义词：underemphasized, downplayed, de-emphasized | 反义词：overemphasized, exaggerated, highlighted | 词性: adjective

词源:
mis- (prefix):  Meaning "wrong," "bad," or "lack of." From Old English *mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly," ultimately from PIE *mei- "to change, go, move."
emphasize (verb): From Late Latin emphasizare, from Greek emphasizen "to mark with emphasis," from emphasis "significance," from emphainein "to exhibit, display," from en "in" + phainein "to show."
