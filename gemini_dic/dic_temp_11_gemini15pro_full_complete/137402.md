nontenure:/ˌnɒnˈtenjər/ | adj. | 非终身制的 | Not having tenure; not permanent, especially referring to a job or position. |  Example: He accepted a nontenure track position at the university. 他在大学接受了一个非终身教职的职位。

Example: Nontenure faculty often have to worry about their contracts being renewed. 非终身教职的教师常常需要担心他们的合同是否续签。| 近义词: non-permanent, temporary, contract | 反义词: tenured, permanent | 词性: adjective
