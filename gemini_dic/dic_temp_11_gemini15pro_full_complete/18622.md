benthos:/ˈbenθɒs/ (US) /ˈbenθɒs/ (UK)| n. |底栖生物，水底生物群|The organisms that live on or in the bottom of a body of water. |The study focused on the impact of pollution on the benthos of the river. 这项研究的重点是污染对河流底栖生物的影响。|Synonyms: bottom fauna, bottom dwellers|Antonyms: plankton, nekton|noun


benthic:/ˈbenθɪk/ (US) /ˈbenθɪk/ (UK)| adj. |底栖的，水底的|Relating to, or occurring at the bottom of a body of water. |Benthic organisms play a vital role in the aquatic ecosystem. 底栖生物在水生生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: bottom-dwelling|Antonyms: pelagic|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βένθος (benthos, “depth of the sea”).
