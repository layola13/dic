cutwater:/ˈkʌtwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈkʌtwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|破浪体，分水尖，船首|The forward edge of a ship's stem that cuts through the water.  It's designed to reduce resistance and improve the vessel's performance.|The ship's cutwater sliced through the waves.  这艘船的破浪体划破了波浪。|Synonyms: forefoot, stem, prow|Antonyms: stern|noun
