osteopathically:/ˌɒstiəˈpæθɪkli/
adverb
用整骨疗法地
in a way that relates to osteopathy or is done by an osteopath.
She was treated osteopathically for her back pain. 她接受了整骨疗法治疗背痛。
Synonym: osteopathically
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps conventionally/medically)
词性: adverb
