afflictive:/əˈflɪktɪv/| adj.|痛苦的，折磨人的|Causing pain or suffering; distressing.|The afflictive memories of the war haunted him. 战争痛苦的记忆萦绕着他。|近义词:painful, distressing, agonizing, tormenting|反义词:soothing, comforting, pleasant|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *afflictivus, from Latin afflictus (past participle of affligere “to strike down, damage”), from ad- “to” + fligere “to strike”.
