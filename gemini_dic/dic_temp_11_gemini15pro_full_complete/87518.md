heterodoxness:/ˌhɛtərəˈdɒksnəs/ | n. | 异端性；非正统性 | The state or quality of being heterodox; unorthodoxy.  | His heterodoxness in religious matters led to his excommunication.  他的宗教异端导致他被逐出教会。 | Synonyms: unorthodoxy, dissidence, heresy, nonconformity | Antonyms: orthodoxy, conformity, conventionality | noun


heterodox:/ˈhɛtərəˌdɒks/ | adj. | 异端的；非正统的 | Not conforming with accepted or orthodox standards or beliefs. |  His heterodox views on economics were not well received by the establishment. 他非正统的经济观点并未受到当权派的欢迎。 | Synonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, dissenting, heretical | Antonyms: orthodox, conventional, conformist | adjective
