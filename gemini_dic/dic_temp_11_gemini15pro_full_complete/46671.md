dampener:/ˈdæmpənər (US) /ˈdæmpənə(r) (UK)| n.|抑制因素；扫兴的人或事；减震器|something that makes feelings of excitement or enjoyment less strong|The rain put a bit of a dampener on the picnic.  这场雨给野餐增添了一丝扫兴。|synonyms: deterrent, discouragement, damper, killjoy, spoiler, curb, check, restraint|antonyms: encouragement, stimulant, boost|noun

dampener:/ˈdæmpənər (US) /ˈdæmpənə(r) (UK)| n.|减震器；缓冲器|(chiefly technical) a device that reduces shock or vibration|The car's dampeners need to be replaced. 这辆车的减震器需要更换。|synonyms: shock absorber, buffer, cushion|antonyms: amplifier|noun
