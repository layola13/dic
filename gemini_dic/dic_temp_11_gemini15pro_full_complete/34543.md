clear:/klɪr/| adj. v. adv.|清晰的，清楚的；清除；清晰地，清楚地|Free from cloud, mist, or anything that dims, obscures, or hinders vision; easily seen or understood; free from obstruction.|The sky was clear and blue. 天空晴朗而湛蓝。|近义词：transparent, obvious, evident, distinct, lucid, bright, unclouded|反义词：opaque, cloudy, obscure, vague, muddled, dim, unclear|词性:形容词

clear:/klɪr/| v. |清除；变晴|To remove an obstruction or unwanted item from; to make or become free from cloud, mist, etc.|Please clear the table after dinner. 晚饭后请清理桌子。|近义词：remove, eliminate, clean, tidy, empty, vacate|反义词：fill, clutter, obstruct, block|词性:动词

clear:/klɪr/| adv.|清晰地，清楚地|In a clear and distinct manner.|She spoke clearly and concisely. 她讲话清晰而简练。|近义词：distinctly, plainly, obviously, evidently, lucidly|反义词：indistinctly, vaguely, obscurely|词性:副词

词源:  From Middle English *clere, from Old French cler (Modern French clair), from Latin clārus (“clear, bright, loud”), from Proto-Italic *klāros, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱl̥h₂rós (“bright, loud”).  Related to clamor.
