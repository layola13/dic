individualising:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
个性化；个别化
The process of making something suitable for individual needs or characteristics.
例句：The teacher is individualising the learning program for each student.  老师正在为每个学生制定个性化的学习计划。

例句：They are individualising instruction to meet the needs of diverse learners. 他们正在个性化教学以满足不同学习者的需求。

近义词：personalizing, customizing, tailoring
反义词：standardizing, generalizing
词性：verb (动词)


individualised:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzd/
adjective
个性化的；个别化的
Made or adapted to suit individual needs or characteristics.
例句：Each student receives individualised attention. 每个学生都得到个性化的关注。

例句：The company offers individualised training programs. 这家公司提供个性化的培训计划。

近义词：personalized, customized, tailored
反义词：standardized, generalized
词性：adjective (形容词)


individualisation:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
个性化；个别化
The action or process of individualising something or someone.
例句：Individualisation of treatment is crucial for effective healthcare.  个性化治疗对于有效的医疗保健至关重要。

例句：The school emphasizes the individualisation of learning. 学校强调学习的个性化。

近义词：personalization, customization
反义词：standardization, generalization
词性：noun (名词)


词源：From individual + -ize + -ation/-ing.  来自 individual（个体） + -ize（使…化） + -ation/-ing（名词/动名词后缀）
