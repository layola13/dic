overattachment:/ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃmənt/| n. |过度依恋|Excessive emotional dependence on someone or something.|She displayed an overattachment to her childhood teddy bear. 她对她童年时的泰迪熊表现出过度依恋。|例句：His overattachment to his mother is hindering his personal growth. 他对母亲的过度依恋阻碍了他的个人成长。|近义词：dependence, clinginess|反义词:independence, detachment|词性:noun


overattach:/ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃ/| v. |过度依恋|To become excessively emotionally dependent on someone or something.|The child overattached to the blanket. 这个孩子过度依恋那条毯子。|例句：He tends to overattach to romantic partners. 他倾向于过度依恋他的恋爱对象。|近义词：cling, depend excessively|反义词:detach, become independent|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + attachment.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Attachment" comes from the Old French "attachement" meaning "a fastening, tie."
