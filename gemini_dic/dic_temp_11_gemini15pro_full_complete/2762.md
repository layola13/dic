adultly:/əˈdʌltli/
adv.
成年地；成熟地；像成年人一样地
In a manner characteristic of a responsible adult.
She handled the situation very adultly. 她非常成熟地处理了这种情况。
近义词: maturely, responsibly, soberly
反义词: childishly, immaturely
词性:副词

Etymology: From adult + -ly.  "Adult" comes from the Latin word "adultus," the past participle of "adolescere," meaning "to grow up."  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial suffix in English, derived from Old English "-lice."
