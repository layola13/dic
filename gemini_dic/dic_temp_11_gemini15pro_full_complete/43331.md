cramped:/kræmpt/| adj.| 狭窄的，拥挤的；痉挛的；(字迹)难看的|Confined or severely limited in space; uncomfortably small.|The room was cramped and stuffy. 房间狭小而闷热.|近义词：confined, restricted, tight, crowded, packed, jammed, constricted|反义词:spacious, roomy, comfortable, expansive|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crampen*, from Old English *cramp*, from Proto-Germanic *krampuz* (“crooked, bent”), related to *krimpaz* (“to press together, pinch”). Cognate with Dutch *kramp* (“cramp, spasm”), German *Krampf* (“cramp, spasm, convulsion”), Swedish *kramp* (“cramp, spasm”).  The sense of "confining, restricting" developed later.
