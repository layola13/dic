caloricity:/ˌkæləˈrɪsɪti/ | n. | 热值，发热量，产热量 | The caloricity of a fuel is the amount of heat it produces when burned. | The caloricity of coal is higher than that of wood. 煤的热值比木材高。| synonyms: heating value, caloric value, thermal value | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but one might consider concepts like "cooling capacity" or "heat absorption") | noun
