overreflection:/ˌoʊvərɪˈflɛkʃən/| n. |过度反思|Excessive reflection or contemplation, often leading to indecision or anxiety.  The act of thinking too much about oneself, one's actions, and their possible consequences.|He was paralyzed by overreflection and couldn't make a simple decision. 他因过度反思而瘫痪，无法做出简单的决定。| Overreflection prevented her from taking any risks. 过度反思让她不敢冒险.|近义词：rumination, brooding, introspection|反义词：impulsiveness, spontaneity|noun
