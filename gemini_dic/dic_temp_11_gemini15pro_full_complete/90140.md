hotchpotch:/ˈhɒtʃpɒtʃ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtʃpɑːtʃ/ (US)| n. |大杂烩；混合物|A confused mixture of different things.|The play was a hotchpotch of styles and periods.|近义词:medley, jumble, mixture, mishmash, blend,  |反义词: uniformity, consistency|noun


hotchpotches:/ˈhɒtʃpɒtʃɪz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtʃpɑːtʃɪz/ (US)| n. |大杂烩；混合物（复数）|Confused mixtures of different things.|Her room was a hotchpotches of clothes, books, and art supplies.|近义词:medleys, jumbles, mixtures, mishmashes, blends |反义词: uniformities, consistencies|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hochepot*, from Old French *hochepot* (modern French *hochepot*), literally "shake-pot," from *hocher* "to shake" + *pot*.  The term originally referred to a stew of various meats and vegetables.
