overlooker:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊkər/| n.|监督者；俯瞰者；瞭望者|A person who oversees or supervises others or something; a person who has a view from above.|例句：The overlooker ensured that the construction project stayed on schedule. 监督员确保了建设项目按计划进行。

例句：From the mountaintop, the overlooker had a breathtaking view of the valley. 从山顶上，瞭望者看到了山谷的壮丽景色。|近义词：supervisor, superintendent, observer, viewer|反义词:worker, subordinate|词性:noun
