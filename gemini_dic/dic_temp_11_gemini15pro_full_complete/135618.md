nonpracticabilities:/ˌnɑnpræktɪkəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
不可行性；不切实际之处
The nonpracticabilities of the proposed plan made it impossible to implement.  这个提议的计划的不可行性使得它无法实施。
The report highlighted the nonpracticabilities of building a dam in that location.  报告强调了在那个位置建造大坝的不切实际之处。
impracticalities, infeasibilities
feasibilities, possibilities
