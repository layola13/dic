newly:/ˈnjuːli/| adv.|新近地，最近地|Recently, lately; just now or recently. |The newly elected president gave her inaugural address. 新当选的总统发表了就职演说。|The museum has newly acquired a collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆最近获得了一批古代文物。|近义词：recently, lately, freshly, just|反义词：formerly, previously, anciently|词性:副词

词源:
From Middle English *newely, *neuly, from Old English *nīwlice (“newly”), equivalent to new +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots newlie (“newly”), West Frisian nijlik (“newly”), Dutch nieuwlijks (“newly”), German neulich (“recently”).
