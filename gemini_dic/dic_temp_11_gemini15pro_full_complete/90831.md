hunching:/ˈhʌntʃɪŋ/ /ˈhʌntʃɪŋ/| v.| 弯腰驼背，耸肩|The act of bending one's back and shoulders forward.|He was sitting hunched over his computer. 他正弯腰驼背地坐在电脑前。|近义词：slouching, stooping, crouching|反义词: straightening, standing erect|动词

hunching:/ˈhʌntʃɪŋ/ /ˈhʌntʃɪŋ/| n.| 弯腰驼背，耸肩|The posture or act of hunching.|His hunching made him look shorter than he was. 他的驼背让他看起来比实际矮。|近义词：slouch, stoop|反义词: straight posture|名词


词源: From hunch (v.) + -ing.  The verb "hunch" likely originated from a Scandinavian source, related to Norwegian "hnukka" (to bend down).
