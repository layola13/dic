armorer:/ˈɑːrmərər/ (US) /ˈɑːmərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|盔甲师，军械师|A person who makes and repairs armor.|The king's armorer crafted a magnificent suit of steel. 国王的盔甲师制作了一套华丽的钢制盔甲。|例句：The armorer carefully polished the breastplate. 盔甲师仔细地擦亮了胸甲。

例句：The armorer repaired the dented helmet. 盔甲师修理了凹陷的头盔。

|近义词：armourer (UK spelling), weaponsmith, blacksmith|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
