conservancies:/kənˈsɜːrvənsiz/
n.
自然保护区；管理委员会
areas of land where animals and plants are protected; organizations that protect natural areas
The conservancies in Kenya are working hard to protect endangered species. 肯尼亚的自然保护区正在努力保护濒危物种。
Several conservancies manage the protected forests in this region.  几个管理委员会管理着这个地区的受保护森林。
近义词:reserves, sanctuaries, protection agencies
反义词:exploiters, destroyers
