appreciativenesses:/əˌpriːʃiəˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
感激, 感谢之情
The state of being appreciative; the quality of being thankful or showing gratitude.

Her appreciativenesses for the gift were evident in her heartfelt thank you note.
她对礼物的感激之情在她 heartfelt 的感谢信中显而易见。

The family expressed their deep appreciativenesses for the community's support during their difficult time.
这家人对社区在他们困难时期提供的支持表示深深的感谢。

近义词:gratitude, thankfulness, appreciation
反义词:ingratitude, ungratefulness, thanklessness
词性:名词(plural)


词源:  From appreciate + -ive + -ness + -es (plural). Ultimately from Latin *appretiatus, past participle of appretiare "to set a price to," from ad- "to" + pretium "price."
