overdrown:/ˌoʊvərˈdraʊn/| v.|淹死，溺死|To drown (someone or something) completely; to submerge fatally in water.|The kitten accidentally overdrowned in the bathtub. 小猫不小心在浴缸里淹死了。|近义词：drown, submerge|反义词: rescue, save|词性:动词
词源：From Old English *oferdrencan, from ofor- "over" + drencan "to drench, submerge".
