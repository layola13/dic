drumster:/ˈdrʌmstər/| n.|鼓手；敲鼓者|A person who plays the drums. |例句：The drumster kept a steady beat throughout the song.  鼓手在整首歌中保持着稳定的节奏。

例句：The band's drumster is known for his incredible solos.  乐队的鼓手以其令人难以置信的独奏而闻名。|近义词：drummer, percussionist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-musician")|Noun
