gnomical: /ˈnoʊmɪkəl/ | adj. |格言式的，警句的；精辟的；神秘的，晦涩的 | Relating to or resembling a gnome, especially in being wise or witty; expressed in or of the nature of short, pithy maxims or aphorisms; enigmatic or obscure. |  His gnomical utterances were difficult to interpret. 他那些格言式的话语很难理解。| synonyms: aphoristic, proverbial, epigrammatic, pithy, sententious, wise, sage, cryptic, enigmatic, obscure | antonyms: prosaic, mundane, verbose, rambling | adjective
