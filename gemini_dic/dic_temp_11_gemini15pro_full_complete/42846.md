court-martialed: /ˌkɔːrt ˈmɑːrʃəld/ /ˌkɔːrt ˈmɑːrʃəld/ | v. | 受军事法庭审判；被军事法庭判罪 | to try (a member of the armed forces) by court-martial. | The soldier was court-martialed for desertion.  这名士兵因擅离职守而受军事法庭审判。| Synonyms: try, prosecute | Antonyms: acquit, exonerate | Verb (past participle)

court-martial: /ˈkɔːrt ˈmɑːrʃəl/ /ˈkɔːrt ˈmɑːrʃəl/ | n. | 军事法庭 | a judicial court for trying members of the armed forces accused of offenses against military law. | The court-martial found him guilty. 军事法庭判他有罪。| Synonyms: military tribunal, military court | Antonyms: civilian court | Noun
