Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to help? = 她做了些什么来帮忙？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading? = 他一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when you saw her? = 你看到她的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she bring to the party? = 她会带什么来参加派对？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this weekend? = 这周末你会做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? = 下学期她会学什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the time you're 30? = 到你30岁的时候，你会取得什么成就？
What will she have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时，她会完成什么？

What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？
