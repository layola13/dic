misadvising:/ˌmɪsədˈvaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|错误地建议；提供不良的建议|The lawyer was sued for misadvising his client.|该律师因向客户提供错误建议而被起诉。|
Synonyms: misguiding, misleading, misdirecting
|Antonyms: advising correctly, guiding well|verb|
