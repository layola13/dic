enfeeblement:/ɪnˈfiːblmənt/ | n. | 衰弱，虚弱 |The state of being weakened or debilitated, either physically or mentally. |  His long illness resulted in a gradual enfeeblement of his body.  他长期患病导致身体逐渐衰弱。 | 近义词：weakening, debilitating, debilitation, impairment | 反义词：strengthening, invigoration | 词性: noun

enfeeble:/ɪnˈfiːbl/ | vt. | 使衰弱，使无力 |To make weak or feeble. | The disease enfeebles the immune system. 这种疾病会削弱免疫系统。| 近义词：weaken, debilitate, undermine, impair | 反义词：strengthen, invigorate, fortify | 词性: verb
