compliable:/kəmˈplaɪəbl/ | adj. | 顺从的，依从的，服从的 | Willing to agree with other people or to obey rules. |  She is a very compliant child and always does as she is told. 她是一个非常听话的孩子，总是按照吩咐去做。

The company expects its employees to be compliant with the rules. 公司希望其员工遵守规则。


近义词: obedient, submissive, amenable, docile, tractable | 反义词: uncooperative, resistant, defiant, obstinate, recalcitrant | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English (in the sense ‘bending easily’): from Old French pliable, from plier ‘to bend’, from Latin plicare ‘to fold’. The current sense dates from the mid 19th century.
