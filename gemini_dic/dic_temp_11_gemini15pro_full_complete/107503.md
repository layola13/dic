lancinate:/ˈlænsɪneɪt/| v. adj.|刺穿；撕裂；（疼痛）剧烈，尖锐|To pierce or tear; to cause a sharp, sudden pain.|The lancinating pain in his chest made him cry out. 他胸口的剧痛让他叫了出来。|近义词：pierce, stab, tear, rend, agonizing, excruciating|反义词: soothe, dull, numb|词性:动词, 形容词
词源: From Latin *lancināre, present active infinitive of lancinō (“I tear, mangle, lacerate”), from lancea (“lance”).
