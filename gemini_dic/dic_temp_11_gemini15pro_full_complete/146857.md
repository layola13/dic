overindulges:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒɪz/
|v.|放纵；过度沉溺|To gratify desires or appetites beyond moderation; to allow oneself or another person to have or do too much of something enjoyable, especially food or drink.|He overindulges in sweets and now has a toothache. 他吃太多甜食，现在牙疼了。|近义词：pamper, spoil, coddle|反义词：abstain, moderate, restrict|Verb (Third person singular present)
