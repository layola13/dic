overcrowdedness's:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪdnəsɪz/ | n. | 过度拥挤的状态 | The state of being overcrowded; excessive crowding.  | The overcrowdedness's of the train made it difficult to breathe. 火车里过于拥挤的状态让人难以呼吸。|  The overcrowdedness's of the refugee camp led to outbreaks of disease. 难民营过度拥挤导致疾病爆发。 | 近义词: congestion, overcrowding | 反义词: spaciousness, roominess | 词性: 名词

词源: overcrowdedness + 's (possessive)

overcrowdedness 来自于 overcrowded + -ness.
overcrowded 来自于 over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + crowded (adjective meaning "filled with too many people or things").
crowded 来自于 crowd (noun meaning "a large number of people gathered closely together") + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).
's 表示所有格。
