antilittering:/ˌæntiˈlɪtərɪŋ/
adjective
反乱扔垃圾的; 防止乱扔垃圾的
Preventing or discouraging littering.
The city council implemented an antilittering campaign. 市议会开展了一场反乱扔垃圾的运动。
The park has antilittering signs everywhere. 公园里到处都有禁止乱扔垃圾的标志。
近义词:anti-litter,  cleanliness-promoting
反义词:littering, polluting
词性:adjective

anti:/ˈænti/
prefix
反对，抵抗，防止
Against; opposed to; preventing or inhibiting.
Antibiotic drugs are used to fight bacterial infections. 抗生素药物用于对抗细菌感染。
近义词:against, opposed to
反义词:pro, for
词性:prefix

littering:/ˈlɪtərɪŋ/
noun,  verb(gerund)
乱扔垃圾
The act of throwing trash or garbage on the ground in a public place.
Littering is a serious environmental problem. 乱扔垃圾是一个严重的环境问题.
The council introduced fines for littering. 议会对乱扔垃圾处以罚款。
近义词:trashing, dumping
反义词:cleaning, tidying
词性:noun, verb(gerund)
