apodeme:/ˈæpəˌdiːm/| n.|内骨骼，内突，肌腱附着点|An inward projection of the exoskeleton of an arthropod, serving as an attachment point for muscles.|The flight muscles of insects are attached to apodemes. 昆虫的飞行肌附着在内骨骼上。|Synonyms:  endoskeleton, endosternite|Antonyms: exoskeleton|noun


apodeme:/ˈæpəˌdiːm/| n.|内骨骼，内突，肌腱附着点|An inward growth of the exoskeleton of an arthropod that provides an attachment point for muscles.|The apodemes in a crab's claw are strong and allow for powerful gripping. 蟹爪中的内突强壮有力，可以强力抓取。|Synonyms:  internal skeleton, internal support|Antonyms: external skeleton|noun
