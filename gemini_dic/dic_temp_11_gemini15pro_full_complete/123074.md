moneyest: /ˈmʌniɪst/ |  adj. (non-standard) | 最有钱的 | A non-standard superlative form of "moneyed," meaning having a lot of money.  It's grammatically incorrect and not commonly used. | Example: He's the moneyest guy I know. (non-standard) 他是我认识的最有钱的人。(非标准用法) | Near Synonyms: wealthiest, richest | Antonyms: poorest, most impoverished | part of speech: adjective
