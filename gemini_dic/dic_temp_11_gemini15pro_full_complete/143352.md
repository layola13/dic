outfabled:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪbəld/
|adj.|胜过传说中的，比传说更精彩的|Surpassing what is fabled; more remarkable than what is told in fables.|Her beauty outfabled even the most enchanting nymphs of Greek mythology. 她的美貌甚至胜过希腊神话中最迷人的仙女。|例句：His bravery outfabled the heroes of old. 他的勇敢胜过古代的英雄们。|近义词：legendary, mythical, fabled, storied|反义词：ordinary, commonplace, mundane|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "fabled" (told in fables or legends).  The prefix "out-" intensifies the meaning of "fabled."
