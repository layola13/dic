compositing:/ˈkɑːmpəzɪtɪŋ/ | n. |合成，影像合成|The process of combining visual elements from separate sources into single images, often used in film, television, and graphic design. | The compositing of the various elements in the scene created a seamless and realistic effect.  场景中各种元素的合成创造了无缝且逼真的效果。 | synonyms: blending, merging, combining | antonyms: separating, dividing | noun
