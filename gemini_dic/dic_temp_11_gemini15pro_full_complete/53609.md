discardable:/dɪˈskɑːrdəbl/
adjective
可丢弃的，可抛弃的
Something that is discardable is designed to be thrown away after use.
Disposable nappies are convenient, but not very environmentally friendly.  一次性尿布很方便，但不太环保。
Old newspapers are discardable.  旧报纸是可以丢弃的。
近义词: disposable, expendable, throwaway
反义词: reusable, permanent, durable
词性: adjective

discard:/dɪˈskɑːrd/
verb
丢弃，抛弃
To discard something is to throw it away or get rid of it because you no longer want or need it.
He discarded his old clothes. 他丢弃了他的旧衣服。
She discarded the idea of going to Paris. 她放弃了去巴黎的想法。
近义词: dispose of, jettison, dump, scrap
反义词: keep, retain, save
词性: verb

noun
丢弃，抛弃
The act of discarding something.
The discard pile in card games.  纸牌游戏中丢弃的牌堆。
The discards from the factory were polluting the river.  工厂的废弃物污染了河流。
近义词: disposal, rejection
反义词: retention, keeping
词性: noun
