gogglier:/ˈɡɒɡliər/ | adj. |  戴着护目镜的；瞪大眼睛的；更加突出的 |  More goggly; having or showing large, staring, or prominent eyes, often enhanced by wearing goggles. |  His eyes got even gogglier when he saw the roller coaster.  他看到过山车时，眼睛瞪得更大了。| 近义词：bug-eyed, pop-eyed, wide-eyed | 反义词：narrow-eyed, squinty | 词性：形容词

goggles:/ˈɡɒɡlz/ | n. | 护目镜；风镜；游泳镜 | Protective eyewear, typically with a flexible frame and a single lens or two lenses joined together, worn to shield the eyes from water, dust, wind, etc. |  She always wears goggles when she swims. 她游泳时总是戴着护目镜。| 近义词：eyewear, glasses, spectacles | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词

词源: From *goggle* (v.) + *-ier*. *Goggle* likely imitates the sound of bulging eyes.
