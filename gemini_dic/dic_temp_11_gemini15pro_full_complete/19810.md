bimarginate:/baɪˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪt/
adjective
双边距的，有两条边界的
Having two margins.  Specifically referring to a leaf or other structure in botany that has two distinct margins.
The bimarginate leaf had a distinctive appearance. 这片双边距的叶子有着独特的外观。
The botanist identified the plant by its bimarginate leaves.  植物学家通过其双边距的叶子识别了这种植物。
近义词: double-margined
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly) unimarginate (if such a term existed)
词性: adjective
