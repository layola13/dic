misunderstandingly:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændɪŋli/
adv.
误解地，由于误解
Incorrectly because of a failure to understand something properly.
She misunderstandingly took his silence as disapproval. 她误解了他的沉默，以为他不赞成。
近义词: erroneously, mistakenly
反义词: correctly, accurately
词性: 副词


词源:
From misunderstanding + -ly.  "Misunderstanding" comes from the prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "understanding" (from "understand," which originates from Old English "understandan," meaning "to comprehend").  The suffix "-ly" turns the noun "misunderstanding" into an adverb.
