family-physician: /ˈfæmɪli fɪˈzɪʃən/ (US) /ˈfæmɪli fɪˈzɪʃən/ (UK)| n. | 家庭医生 |A doctor who provides comprehensive medical care to individuals and families, focusing on general practice and preventative care. | Example: I've been seeing the same family physician for over ten years.  我看了同一个家庭医生十多年了。 | Example:  Our family physician recommended getting flu shots this year. 我们的家庭医生建议今年接种流感疫苗。|Synonyms: general practitioner, GP, primary care physician | Antonyms: specialist | noun
