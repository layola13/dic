misrehearse: /ˌmɪsrɪˈhɜːrs/ | v. | 排练得不好，排练不充分 | To rehearse inadequately or incorrectly. |  The actors misrehearsed the crucial scene, leading to a disastrous opening night. 演员们关键场景排练得不好，导致首演之夜惨不忍睹。| The band misrehearsed the new song and it sounded terrible at the concert. 乐队新歌排练得不好，在音乐会上听起来很糟糕。| 近义词: flub, bungle, botch | 反义词: perfect, master | 词性: 动词
