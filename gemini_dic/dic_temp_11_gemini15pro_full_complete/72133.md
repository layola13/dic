floodgates:/ˈflʌdɡeɪts/| n.|防洪闸门; 闸门 (比喻) 限制;约束|A barrier used to control the flow of water, especially one in a canal or river; (figuratively) something that holds back a powerful force or feeling.|The dam burst, opening the floodgates and unleashing a torrent of water.  大坝决堤，打开了闸门，释放出洪流。|Near synonyms: barriers, gates, controls|Opposite: opening, unleashing|Noun

floodgates:/ˈflʌdɡeɪts/| n.|闸门; (比喻) 限制;约束|Something that, if opened, allows a large quantity of something to be released.|Opening the floodgates to cheap imports could ruin domestic industries. 开放廉价进口品的闸门可能会摧毁国内产业。|Near synonyms: barriers, restrictions, restraints|Opposite: outlet, channel|Noun
