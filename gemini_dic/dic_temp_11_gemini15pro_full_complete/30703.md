certifiers:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪərz/
n.
认证者，核证者
People or organizations that officially confirm the truth or validity of something.
The certifiers ensured the product met industry standards. 认证机构确保产品符合行业标准。

certifiers:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪərz/
n.
证明者，颁发证书者
People or organizations that issue certificates.
The certifiers issued a certificate of authenticity. 证明者颁发了真品证书。


近义词: validators, authenticators, verifiers
反义词:  N/A (没有明确的反义词)
词性: noun

词源：certify (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents)

certify: from Late Latin *certificare "to make certain," from Latin certus "settled, fixed, sure" (see certain) + -ficare, combining form of facere "to make, do" (from PIE root *dhe- "to set, put").
