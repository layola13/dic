glutting: /ˈɡlʌtɪŋ/
verb
使…充满；使…过剩；狼吞虎咽
overfilling or supplying to excess.

He was glutting himself on chocolate.
他正在狂吃巧克力。

The market is glutted with cheap imports.
市场上充斥着廉价的进口商品。


近义词: gorging, stuffing, cramming, satiating, oversupplying
反义词: starving, depriving, lacking
词性: v. (present participle of glut)


词源:
From Middle English *glouten, from Old French glotir (“to swallow, devour”), of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old French glotte (“throat, gullet”). Cognate with Italian glottire (“to gulp down”). More at glut.
