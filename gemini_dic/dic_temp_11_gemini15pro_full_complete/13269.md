athanor:/ˈæθənɔːr/| n.|炼金炉，消化炉|A furnace used by alchemists to maintain a uniform and constant heat for long periods of time, typically for alchemical digestion.|The alchemist tended his athanor day and night, hoping to transmute lead into gold. 炼金术士日夜照料他的炼金炉，希望能将铅变成金子。|Synonyms: furnace, kiln, oven|Antonyms: freezer, refrigerator|noun
