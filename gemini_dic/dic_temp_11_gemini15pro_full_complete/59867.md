ecuadorians:/ˌɛkwəˈdɔːriənz/
n.
厄瓜多尔人
People from Ecuador, a country in South America.
Many Ecuadorians emigrated to Spain during the economic crisis. 许多厄瓜多尔人在经济危机期间移民到西班牙。
Ecuadorians are known for their vibrant culture and delicious cuisine. 厄瓜多尔人以其充满活力的文化和美味佳肴而闻名。
synonyms: Ecuadorian people, citizens of Ecuador
antonyms: non-Ecuadorians, foreigners (in the context of Ecuador)
