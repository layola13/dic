catchingness:/ˈkætʃɪŋnɪs/ | n. | 流行性；感染性；朗朗上口 |The quality of being easily remembered or imitated, often referring to a tune, slogan, or idea.| The song's catchingness made it an instant hit.  这首歌朗朗上口，很快就流行起来。| synonyms: memorability, infectiousness, appeal | antonyms: forgettability, obscurity | noun

Etymology: From "catching" (adjective) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  "Catching" comes from the verb "catch," with origins in Old North French "cachier."
