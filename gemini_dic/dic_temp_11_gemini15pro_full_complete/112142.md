loofah:/ˈluːfə/ (US), /ˈluːfɑː/ (UK)| n.| 丝瓜，丝瓜络，丝瓜筋| A tropical climbing plant bearing a large, long fruit with a fibrous interior. Also refers to the dried fibrous interior of the fruit, used as a sponge or bath scrubber.| I use a loofah in the shower every day. 我每天洗澡都用丝瓜络。|近义词：sponge, scrubber, washcloth|反义词: None|Noun


loofahs:/ˈluːfəz/ (US), /ˈluːfɑːz/ (UK)| n.| 丝瓜，丝瓜络，丝瓜筋（复数）| Plural form of loofah, referring to multiple loofahs.| I bought two new loofahs at the market. 我在市场买了两个新丝瓜络。|近义词：sponges, scrubbers, washcloths|反义词: None|Noun
