countereducational:/ˌkaʊntərˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənəl/
adj.
反教育的，不利于教育的
Hinders or opposes education; not conducive to learning.
The government's countereducational policies have led to a decline in literacy rates. 政府的反教育政策导致了识字率的下降。
The constant distractions from social media can be countereducational for students. 社交媒体持续不断的干扰可能对学生不利于学习。
近义词:anti-educational, detrimental to education
反义词:educational, pro-educational
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
counter- (prefix): against, opposite  (e.g., counterclockwise, counterproductive)
educational (adjective): relating to education
