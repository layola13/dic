knock-on-wood:/ˌnɒk ɒn ˈwʊd/ (British) /ˌnɑːk ɑːn ˈwʊd/ (American)| idiom | 敲木头（避凶，祈求好运） | An act of rapping one's knuckles on a piece of wood to avoid bad luck, especially after speaking of good fortune.  It's a superstitious practice believed to prevent bad luck from occurring or to ensure that good luck continues. |  I've been healthy all year, knock on wood. 我一年都很健康，敲木头。

I just got a new job, knock on wood! 我刚找到一份新工作，敲木头！| 近义词: touch wood | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a superstitious action) | 词性: idiom
