botero: /boʊˈtɛroʊ/ | n. | 博特罗（指Fernando Botero，哥伦比亚画家和雕塑家）; 博特罗风格（指丰满夸张的艺术风格）|  Refers to Fernando Botero, a Colombian figurative artist known for his voluminous figures, or the style of art characterized by such figures. |  The museum has a whole wing dedicated to Botero.  博物馆有一个完整的侧厅专门展出博特罗的作品。|  The painting's exaggerated proportions are typical of Botero.  这幅画夸张的比例是典型的博特罗风格。| synonyms:  Boteroesque, voluminous, plump, rounded | antonyms: slender, thin, emaciated | noun
