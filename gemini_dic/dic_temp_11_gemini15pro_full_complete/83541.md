hadron:/ˈhædrɒn/ (UK) /ˈhædrɑːn/ (US)| n.|强子|A subatomic particle, such as a proton or neutron, that is made up of quarks and is subject to the strong force.|Protons and neutrons are both hadrons. 质子和中子都是强子。|The Large Hadron Collider is used to study these particles. 大型强子对撞机用于研究这些粒子。|Synonym: composite particle|Antonym: lepton|Noun
