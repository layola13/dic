open-airish:/ˌoʊpənˈɛərɪʃ/
adjective
露天式的，户外式的
Resembling or suggestive of the open air; somewhat open-air.  Having a quality or feeling of being outdoors, even if not strictly so.
The restaurant had an open-airish feel with its large windows and breezy atmosphere.  这家餐厅有大窗户和通风的环境，给人一种露天式的感觉。
The stage had an open-airish design, although it was technically indoors.  舞台设计具有露天风格，虽然严格来说是在室内。
近义词: airy, breezy, outdoorsy, al fresco
反义词: stuffy, enclosed, indoors, closed-in
词性: adjective
