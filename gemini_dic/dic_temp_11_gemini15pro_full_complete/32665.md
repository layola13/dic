chittra:/ˈtʃɪtrə/ | n. | 画像，图画 | A picture, painting, or drawing, especially a religious image.  This word is primarily used in South Asian contexts, particularly related to Hindu and Buddhist art. |  Example: The temple walls were adorned with vibrant chittras of deities. 寺庙的墙壁上装饰着鲜艳的神像图画。

Example:  Devotees offered prayers before the intricately painted chittra.  信徒们在精心绘制的画像前祈祷。| 近义词:picture, image, painting, portrait, icon | 反义词: N/A (lack of a direct opposite for a visual representation) | 词性:noun
