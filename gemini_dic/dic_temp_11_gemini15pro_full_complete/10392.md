appendicularia:/ˌæpɛnˌdɪkjuˈlɛəriə/ | n. | 尾海鞘 |A small, free-swimming, pelagic tunicate (sea squirt) of the class Appendicularia, characterized by a permanent tail and a gelatinous "house" that it secretes and lives within. | The appendicularia filters plankton through its intricate house. 尾海鞘通过其复杂的“房子”过滤浮游生物。 | synonym: larvacean | antonym: N/A | noun


appendicularian:/əˌpɛndɪˈkjʊləriən/ | adj. | 尾海鞘的;与尾海鞘有关的 |Relating to or characteristic of appendicularians. |  Appendicularian houses are remarkably complex structures. 尾海鞘的房子是结构非常复杂的。| synonym: larvacean | antonym: N/A | adjective
