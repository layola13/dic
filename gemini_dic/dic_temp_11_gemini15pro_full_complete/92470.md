hypostyle:/ˈhaɪpəˌstaɪl/| adj. n.|多柱式的，多柱厅|A hall with a roof supported by many columns.|例句：The hypostyle hall of the temple was awe-inspiring.  寺庙的多柱式大厅令人敬畏。

例句：The ancient Egyptian temple featured a grand hypostyle hall. 这座古埃及寺庙的特点是一个宏伟的多柱厅。|近义词：columned, pillared|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps open-plan)|词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ὑπόστυλος* (hypóstylos, “supported by pillars”), from *ὑπό* (hupó, “under”) + *στῦλος* (stûlos, “pillar, column”).
