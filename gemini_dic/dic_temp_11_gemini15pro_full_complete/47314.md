de-litter:/diːˈlɪtər/
verb
清除垃圾；清理废弃物
To remove litter from (a place).
The volunteers de-littered the park after the concert.  志愿者们在音乐会后清理了公园的垃圾。
The city council launched a campaign to de-litter the streets. 市议会发起了一项清理街道垃圾的运动。
clean, tidy up, clear
litter, pollute
