imagineer: /ɪˌmædʒɪˈnɪr/
n.
梦想家；幻想工程师；创意工程师
A person who is creative and imaginative, especially in the design and development of new products, ideas, or experiences.  A portmanteau of "imagine" and "engineer."
Walt Disney called the engineers and designers who worked in his theme parks "Imagineers."  华特·迪士尼把他主题公园里的工程师和设计师称为“幻想工程师”。
近义词: innovator, creator, designer, visionary, inventor
反义词: pragmatist, realist
词性: noun

imagineer: /ɪˌmædʒɪˈnɪr/
v.
构思；想象设计；创造
To conceive of and develop something new and innovative, especially in the fields of entertainment, technology, or design.
The team of imagineers spent months imagineering the new attraction.  这个幻想工程师团队花了几个月的时间构思新的景点。
近义词:  devise, conceive, invent, create, design
反义词: copy, imitate
词性: verb

Etymology:  A portmanteau of "imagine" and "engineer," coined by Walt Disney in the 1950s.  由“imagine”和“engineer”两个词组合而成，由华特·迪士尼于20世纪50年代创造。
