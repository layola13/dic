mass-producer:/ˈmæs prəˈduːsər/ | n. | 大量生产者；批量生产商 |  A person or company that produces goods in large quantities, often using machinery and standardized processes. | Henry Ford was a pioneer mass-producer of automobiles.  亨利·福特是汽车批量生产的先驱。

Toyota is one of the world's largest mass-producers of cars.  丰田是世界上最大的汽车批量生产商之一。 | manufacturer, maker | artisan, craftsman | noun
