fodder:/ˈfɒdə(r)/ (UK) /ˈfɑːdər/ (US)| n.|饲料，秣料；素材，原料|food, especially dried hay or feed, for cattle and other livestock; something that is used as a source for a specific purpose|例句：The cows were fed fodder. 奶牛被喂了饲料。
