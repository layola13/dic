drunkenly:/ˈdrʌŋkənli/| adv.|醉醺醺地，酒醉地|In a manner of someone who is drunk.  Describes actions performed while intoxicated.|He stumbled drunkenly down the street. 他醉醺醺地走在街上。|近义词：intoxicatedly, tipsily|反义词: soberly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "drunken" + "-ly". "Drunken" comes from Old English "druncen", past participle of "drincan" (to drink).  The "-ly" suffix forms adverbs.
