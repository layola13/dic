margining:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnɪŋ/
n.
追加保证金；保证金要求
The process of depositing additional funds into a margin account to cover potential losses.
例句：Margining calls increased as the market became more volatile.  市场波动加剧，追加保证金的要求也增加了。

例句：The broker explained the margining requirements for the new account.  经纪人解释了新账户的保证金要求。

近义词：collateralizing, securing
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词 (derived from the verb "margin")
