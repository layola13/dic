detraining:/diːˈtreɪnɪŋ/
noun
停止训练；去训练；训练中止
The physiological process of losing the benefits gained from exercise due to a period of inactivity.
After just two weeks of detraining, he noticed a decrease in his endurance.  两周不停止训练后，他注意到自己的耐力下降了。
synonyms: inactivity, cessation of training, rest, layoff
antonyms: training, exercising, working out
n.

detraining:/diːˈtreɪnɪŋ/
verb
停止训练；退出训练
To cease or reduce training, resulting in a loss of fitness.
She decided to detrain after the marathon due to an injury.  由于受伤，她决定在马拉松比赛后停止训练。
synonyms: cease training, stop exercising, take a break from training
antonyms: train, exercise, work out
v.
