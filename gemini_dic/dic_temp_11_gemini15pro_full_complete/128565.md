nibblers:/ˈnɪblərz/
|n.|零食爱好者；慢慢啃食者；少量进食者|People or animals that nibble; those who eat small amounts of food slowly or pick at their food.|The nibblers at the party were enjoying the appetizers.  聚会上那些少量进食的人正享受着开胃菜。|近义词：grazers, pickers, snackers|反义词：gorger, glutton|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "nibble" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Nibble" comes from the Middle English "nyblen," frequentative of "nippen," meaning "to bite gently."  Ultimately of Germanic origin.
