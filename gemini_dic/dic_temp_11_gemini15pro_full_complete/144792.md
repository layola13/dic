over-flooding:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdɪŋ/
|n.|过度泛滥，过度充斥|The over-flooding of the river caused significant damage to the surrounding areas. 河流的过度泛滥对周边地区造成了重大破坏。|The market is experiencing an over-flooding of cheap imported goods. 市场正经历廉价进口商品的过度充斥。| deluge, inundation, overflow| drought, scarcity|noun


Etymology->
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "flooding" (present participle of "flood," from Old English flōd)
