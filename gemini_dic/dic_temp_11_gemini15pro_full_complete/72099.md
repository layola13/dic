flocculation:/ˌflɒkjʊˈleɪʃən/ (US) /ˌflɑːkjʊˈleɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|絮凝，絮凝作用|The process by which small particles clump together to form larger masses, typically aided by a chemical agent called a flocculant.|Example: Flocculation is used in water treatment to remove impurities.  絮凝用于水处理以去除杂质。

Example: The flocculation of the sediment made the water clearer.  沉积物的絮凝使水更清澈了。|近义词：coagulation, aggregation, agglomeration|反义词: dispersion, peptization|noun
