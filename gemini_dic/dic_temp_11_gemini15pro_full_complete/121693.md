mispracticed: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪst/
verb
(mal)practice
中文翻译：失职；滥用职权；医疗事故；误诊；不正当行为
英文解析：To engage in malpractice; to perform (one's profession, especially medicine or law) wrongly or improperly.
例句：The doctor was sued for mispracticing medicine after a patient died due to negligence.  这个医生在一名病人因疏忽而死亡后被起诉医疗事故。

例句：The lawyer was accused of mispracticing law by withholding crucial evidence.  这名律师被指控隐瞒关键证据，滥用律师职权。

近义词：malpractice, misconduct, wrongdoing, abuse
反义词：proper practice, ethical conduct
词性：verb (past tense)


mispracticed: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪst/
adjective
中文翻译：失职的；滥用职权的；医疗事故的
英文解析：Characterized by malpractice or improper professional conduct.
例句：The mispracticed surgeon lost his license.  这个失职的外科医生被吊销了执照。

例句：The case involved mispracticed legal procedures that jeopardized the client's rights.  该案件涉及不当的法律程序，损害了客户的权利。

近义词：malpracticing, unethical, improper
反义词：ethical, proper
词性：adjective (past participle used as adjective)
