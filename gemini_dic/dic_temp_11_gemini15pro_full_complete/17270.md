bayonetting:/ˌbeɪəˈnɛtɪŋ/
n.
刺刀突刺；用刺刀拼杀
The act of attacking someone with a bayonet.
The soldiers resorted to bayonetting when they ran out of ammunition. 士兵们在弹药用尽时 resorted to bayonetting when they ran out of ammunition.
近义词：stabbing, spearing
反义词：None (The opposite would be the act of *not* stabbing or attacking with a bayonet, which lacks a concise single-word equivalent)
词性：noun (gerund or verbal noun)


bayonet:/ˈbeɪənɪt/
n. 
刺刀
A knife that can be attached to the end of a rifle and used as a weapon.
He fixed his bayonet to his rifle and charged. 他把刺刀装在步枪上，发起了冲锋。
近义词：dagger, knife
反义词：None (A bayonet is a specific type of knife; its opposite would be a concept like "peace" or a tool with the opposite function which doesn't exist)
词性：noun
