certainties:/ˈsɜːrtntiz/
n.|确定性，必然性|Something that is certain; a fact.|The only certainty in life is death.  人生中唯一确定的事情就是死亡。|assurances, convictions|doubts, uncertainties|noun

certainty:/ˈsɜːrtnti/
n.|确定，必然|The state of being certain; something that is certain.|I can't say with absolute certainty that he'll be there. 我不能绝对肯定地说他会到那里。|assurance, conviction|doubt, uncertainty|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *certeinte, borrowed from Old French certaineté, from certain (“certain”) + -eté (“-ity”), from Latin certus (“settled, fixed, sure”) + -tās (“-ty”).
