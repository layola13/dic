architrave: /ˈɑːkɪtreɪv/ | n. |  框缘，楣梁 | The molded frame around a door or window, especially the lower beam resting on the columns. | The architrave of the doorway was intricately carved.  门口的框缘雕刻精美。 | synonyms: frame, molding, trim | antonyms: none | noun

architrave: /ˈɑːkɪtreɪv/ | n. |  (比喻)主要框架，基础 | The basic framework or essential part of something. |  His philosophical arguments formed the architrave of his political beliefs. 他的哲学论证构成了他政治信仰的主要框架。 | synonyms: foundation, core, basis | antonyms: none | noun
