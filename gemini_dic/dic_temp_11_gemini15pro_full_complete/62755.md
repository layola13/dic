engorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒ/
verb
使充血；使膨胀；狼吞虎咽
to cause (a body part) to become swollen with blood, fluid, etc.; to eat greedily
The mosquito engorged itself with blood.  蚊子吸饱了血。
近义词：swell, bloat, distend, gorge, stuff
反义词：shrink, contract, deflate
词性：v.


engorges:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒɪz/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒɪz/
verb
(使)充血；(使)膨胀；狼吞虎咽（engorge的第三人称单数形式）
third-person singular simple present indicative form of engorge
The tick engorges itself on the dog's blood. 蜱虫吸食狗的血而膨胀。
近义词：swells, bloats, distends, gorges, stuffs
反义词：shrinks, contracts, deflates
词性：v.

词源：
From Old French engorger "to devour greedily, swallow up," from en- "in" + gorge "throat."
