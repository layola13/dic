immortalises:/ɪˈmɔːtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使不朽，使永垂不朽
To confer enduring fame upon; to preserve from oblivion.  To make immortal.
His bravery was immortalized in song. 他的英勇事迹被歌颂而永垂不朽。
immortalize, commemorate, eternalize
forget, disregard
词性:动词


immortalizes:/ɪˈmɔːrtəlaɪzɪz/

使不朽，使永垂不朽
