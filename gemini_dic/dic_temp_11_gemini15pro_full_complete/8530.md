anticensorship:/ˌæntiˈsɛnsərʃɪp/
adjective
反审查的
Opposing censorship; advocating for freedom of expression.
The anticensorship group protested the government's new media regulations. 反审查组织抗议政府新的媒体法规。
The journalist's anticensorship stance made him a target. 这名记者的反审查立场使他成为目标。
近义词:pro-free speech, anti-censorship
反义词:pro-censorship, censorious
词性:adjective
