half:/hæf/ | adj. n. adv.|一半的，半个；一半，半个|One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole.|例句：I'll be there in half an hour. 我半小时后到。|近义词：partially, partly|反义词：whole, double|词性：形容词

half:/hæf/ | n.|一半，半个|One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole.|例句：He ate half of the pizza. 他吃了一半的披萨。|近义词：moiety, portion|反义词：whole, double|词性：名词

half:/hæf/ | adv.|一半地，部分地|To the extent of half or approximately half.|例句：The glass is half full. 杯子半满。|近义词：partially, partly|反义词：wholly, fully|词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *healf, from Proto-Germanic *halbaz, from Proto-Indo-European *pol- "half". Cognates include Dutch half, German halb, Gothic halbs.
