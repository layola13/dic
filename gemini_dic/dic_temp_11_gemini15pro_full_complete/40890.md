cookouts:/ˈkʊkˌaʊts/
n.
户外野餐，烧烤聚会
An informal meal cooked and eaten outdoors.
We had a cookout in the park last weekend.  我们上周末在公园里举行了一次烧烤野餐。
barbecue, picnic, grill-out
indoor party
