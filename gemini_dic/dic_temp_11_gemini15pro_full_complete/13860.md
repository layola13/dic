augmentation:/ɔːɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. |增加, 增大, 扩张|The augmentation of the team with new players significantly improved their performance.  用新球员扩大队伍显著提高了他们的表现。|例句：The augmentation of the troops was necessary for the upcoming battle.  为了即将到来的战斗，增加军队是必要的。

|例句：The recent augmentation in online sales has boosted the company's profits. 最近网上销售额的增长提高了公司的利润。|近义词：increase, expansion, enlargement, enhancement|反义词：reduction, decrease, diminution|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *augmentatioun, from Middle French augmentation, from Late Latin augmentātiō (“an increasing, enlarging”), from Latin augmentātus, past participle of augmentō (“I increase, enlarge”), from augmentum (“an increase”), from augēre (“to increase”).
