dicoccous:/daɪˈkɒkəs/| adj.|双球菌的，二球菌的|Having two cocci (spherical bacteria) joined together.|The lab identified a dicoccous bacteria as the source of the infection. 实验室鉴定出一种双球菌是感染源。|近义词：diplococcal|反义词：(No direct antonym, could use "single-celled," "monococci" in specific contexts, but these are not true opposites.)|词性: 形容词
Etymology: From di- (two) + coccus (spherical bacterium).
