fissiparity:/fɪsɪˈpærɪti/ | n. |裂殖生殖；分裂生殖| Reproduction by spontaneous division of the body into two or more parts, each of which grows into a complete organism. | Amoeba reproduce by fissiparity. 变形虫通过裂殖生殖进行繁殖。 | synonyms: binary fission, schizogenesis | antonyms: sexual reproduction | noun
