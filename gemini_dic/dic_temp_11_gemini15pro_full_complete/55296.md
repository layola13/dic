dissolute:/ˈdɪsəluːt/| adj.|放荡的，放纵的，道德败坏的|Lacking moral restraint; indulging in sensual pleasures or vices. |He led a dissolute life, indulging in every excess. 他过着放荡的生活，沉溺于各种各样的过度享乐。|Synonyms: debauched, dissipated, profligate, immoral, corrupt|Antonyms: moral, virtuous, upright, chaste, temperate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *dissolutus*, past participle of *dissolvere* "to loosen, dissolve," from *dis-* "apart, asunder" + *solvere* "to loosen."
