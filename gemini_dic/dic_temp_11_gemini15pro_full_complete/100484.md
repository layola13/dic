intervascular:/ˌɪntərˈvæskjələr/
adj.
血管内的,  сосудистый
Located within a blood vessel or blood vessels.
The intervascular fluid exchange is crucial for maintaining homeostasis. 血管内液体的交换对维持体内平衡至关重要。
The doctor administered the medication intervascularly. 医生通过血管内注射给药。
近义词: intravascular
反义词: extravascular
词性: 形容词
