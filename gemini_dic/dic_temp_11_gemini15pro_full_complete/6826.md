anaseismic:/ˌænəˈsaɪzmɪk/ | adj. |抗震的，耐震的，防震的 |Relating to or denoting construction designed to withstand earthquakes.  Built to be resistant to the effects of earthquakes. |例句：The new building was constructed using an aseismic design. 这座新建筑采用抗震设计建造。

例句：Anaseismic structures are crucial in earthquake-prone areas. 抗震结构在地震多发地区至关重要。|近义词：earthquake-resistant, earthquake-proof, seismic-resistant |反义词: non-aseismic, vulnerable to earthquakes | 词性: adjective
