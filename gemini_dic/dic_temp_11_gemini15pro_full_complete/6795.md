anaplastic:/ˌænəˈplæstɪk/| adj.|退行发育的，未分化的，间变的|Characterized by a loss of differentiation and reversion to a more primitive form, typically in the context of tumor cells.  Relating to or characterized by anaplasia.|The biopsy revealed anaplastic carcinoma.  活检显示间变性癌。|Synonyms: undifferentiated, primitive, dedifferentiated, atypical|Antonyms: differentiated, mature, typical|Adjective

anaplasia:/ˌænəˈpleɪʒə/| n.|退行发育，间变|A reversion of cells to a more primitive or undifferentiated form, characteristic of malignant tumors.|Anaplasia is a key indicator of aggressive cancer. 间变是侵袭性癌症的一个关键指标。|Synonyms: dedifferentiation, reversion|Antonyms: differentiation, maturation|Noun
