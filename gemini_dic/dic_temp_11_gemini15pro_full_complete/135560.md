nonpolemics:/ˌnɒnpəˈlɛmɪks/
n.
非争论性言论；非辩论性言论
the avoidance of polemics, especially in religious discussion.  The book is a work of nonpolemics, seeking common ground rather than highlighting differences.
This book is a refreshing example of nonpolemics in a highly contentious field. 这本书在一个充满争议的领域中，提供了一个令人耳目一新的非辩论性视角。
He adopted a tone of nonpolemics, hoping to foster understanding. 他采取了非争论性的语气，希望促进理解。
近义词:conciliation, compromise, diplomacy, peacemaking
反义词:polemics, argument, disputation, controversy
词性:noun
