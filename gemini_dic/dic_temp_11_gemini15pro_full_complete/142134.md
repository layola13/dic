originals:/əˈrɪdʒənəlz/| n. | 原件；原创作品；原版；最初的；（尤指艺术作品的）真迹；原著；（乐队的）原创歌曲 |  Something that is original has not been copied from something else, and is therefore considered the source.  | The museum has several originals by Picasso.  这家博物馆收藏了几幅毕加索的真迹。| synonyms: authentic, genuine, first, initial | antonyms: copy, imitation, fake, reproduction | noun

/əˈrɪdʒənəl/| adj. | 原来的；起初的；最早的；独创的；新颖的；创新的；原作的；真迹的 | Existing, present, or occurring from the beginning; first or earliest. |  This is the original manuscript of the novel. 这是小说的原稿。| synonyms: initial, primary, innovative, creative | antonyms: later, subsequent, derived, unoriginal | adjective
