nonrationalist:/nɒnˈræʃənəlɪst/ | adj. n.|非理性主义的；非理性主义者|Not based on or guided by reason; appealing to emotions or beliefs rather than logic.  A person who rejects or criticizes rationalism.|例句：His nonrationalist approach to decision-making often led to unpredictable outcomes. 他非理性主义的决策方式经常导致不可预测的结果。|例句：The artist was a self-proclaimed nonrationalist, finding inspiration in dreams and intuition. 这位艺术家自称是非理性主义者，从梦境和直觉中寻找灵感。|近义词：irrational, emotional, intuitive, mystical|反义词：rational, logical, reasoned, empirical|词性:形容词，名词
