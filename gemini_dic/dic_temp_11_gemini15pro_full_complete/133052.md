nonepicureans:/ˌnɒnˌɛpɪkjʊˈriːənz/
| n. |非享乐主义者|People who do not subscribe to the philosophy of Epicureanism, which emphasizes pleasure and the absence of pain as the highest good.|The nonepicureans at the feast focused more on the conversation than the lavish spread.|Near Antonyms: epicureans, hedonists|Far Antonyms: ascetics|noun
