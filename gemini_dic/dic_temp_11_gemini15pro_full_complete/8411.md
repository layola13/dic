anti-swedish:/ˌæntiˈswiːdɪʃ/
adjective
反瑞典的；敌视瑞典的
Opposed to, hostile toward, or prejudiced against Sweden, its people, or its culture.
He expressed some anti-Swedish sentiments during the hockey game.  他在曲棍球比赛期间表达了一些反瑞典的情绪。

The article was seen as having an anti-Swedish bias. 这篇文章被认为带有反瑞典的偏见。

synonym
hostile, prejudiced, antagonistic, opposed
antonym
pro-Swedish, supportive, friendly

词性
adjective
