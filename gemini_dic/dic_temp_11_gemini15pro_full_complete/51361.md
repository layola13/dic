detergent:/dɪˈtɜːrʒənt/ /dɪˈtɝːdʒənt/
n.| 洗涤剂；清洁剂 |A substance used for cleaning, typically a synthetic liquid or powder that dissolves in water.|  I use a biological detergent for my laundry. 我用生物洗涤剂洗衣服。| synonyms: cleanser, soap, washing powder| antonyms: dirt, grime| noun

detergent:/dɪˈtɜːrʒənt/ /dɪˈtɝːdʒənt/
adj.| 去污的，清洁的 |Having cleaning power.|  The detergent action of the soap removed the stain. 肥皂的去污作用去除了污渍。| synonyms: cleansing, purifying| antonyms: soiling, dirtying| adjective
