outmaking:/ˌaʊtˈmeɪkɪŋ/
verb
胜过; 超越
the act of surpassing or exceeding something or someone.
He focused on outmaking his competitors in sales. 他专注于在销售方面超越他的竞争对手。
synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, outstripping, bettering, topping
antonyms: failing, losing, succumbing

Etymology:  "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "making" (from "make," meaning to create or produce).  The sense evolved from the idea of producing something better or greater than another.
