ointments:/ˈɔɪntmənts/
noun
药膏；软膏
a smooth oily substance that is put on the skin to heal it or to make it softer
The doctor prescribed some ointments for her rash. 医生给她开了些药膏来治疗皮疹。
ointment (singular)
