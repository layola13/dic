abolition:/ˌæbəˈlɪʃən/| n.|废除，废止|The abolition of slavery marked a turning point in history. 奴隶制的废除标志着历史的转折点。|synonyms: abolishment, eradication, extermination|antonyms: establishment, institution, enactment|noun

abolition:/ˌæbəˈlɪʃən/| n.|取消，撤销|The abolition of the death penalty is a controversial issue.  死刑的废除是一个有争议的问题。|synonyms: cancellation, revocation, repeal|antonyms:  implementation, enactment, establishment|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abolitio, from Latin abolere "to destroy, abolish," from ab- "away, from" + olere "to grow" (related to alere "to nourish").  The sense evolution is from "to cause to decay" to "destroy utterly" to "annul, abolish."
