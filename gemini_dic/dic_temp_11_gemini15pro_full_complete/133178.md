nonevangelically:/ˌnɒnɪˌvænˈdʒɛlɪk(ə)li/ | adv. | 非福音式地，非传福音式地 | In a manner that does not resemble or promote the spread of the Christian gospel. |  The author presented his arguments nonevangelically, focusing on logic and reason rather than emotional appeals. 作者以非福音的方式提出了他的论点，侧重于逻辑和理性，而不是情感诉求。|  non-proselytizingly, secularly | evangelically, zealously | adverb

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "evangelical" (from Greek "euangelion" meaning "good news") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
