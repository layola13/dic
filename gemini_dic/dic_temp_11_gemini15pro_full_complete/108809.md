leathernecks: /ˈlɛðərˌnɛks/| n.|皮革脖子；美国海军陆战队士兵|A term for a member of the United States Marine Corps, referring to the high leather collars that were once part of the Marine uniform.  These collars were intended to protect the Marines' necks from sword cuts.|The leathernecks stormed the beach. 海军陆战队士兵们冲上了海滩。|Synonyms: Marines, Devil Dogs|Antonyms: None|Noun
