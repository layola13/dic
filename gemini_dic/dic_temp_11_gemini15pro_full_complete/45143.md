cuddliness:/ˈkʌdəlnəs/| n.|可爱；想拥抱的程度；令人想搂抱的特性|The quality of being cuddly; inviting to cuddle.|The kitten's cuddliness made it irresistible.|Synonyms:  lovable, huggable, adorable, endearing|Antonyms: coldness, aloofness, unapproachability|noun

cuddle:/ˈkʌdəl/| v. n.|拥抱；搂抱；依偎；蜷缩|To hold close in one's arms, especially to show affection.|She cuddled the baby close.|Synonyms:  hug, embrace, snuggle, nestle|Antonyms:  reject, push away, shun|verb, noun
