interpeals:/ˌɪntərˈpiːlz/ | n. | 呼吁，恳求，申诉 | an earnest request or appeal | The refugees made interpeals for help and asylum.  难民们呼吁帮助和庇护。 | 近义词：appeals, pleas, requests, supplications | 反义词: demands, commands | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *interpellare, from Latin inter- 'between' + pellere 'to drive, push'. Originally meant to interrupt or disturb.
