assumptions:/əˈsʌmpʃənz/| n.|假设，设想|Something that is accepted as true or as certain to happen, without proof.|The project was based on the assumption that the economy would continue to grow.|近义词：presumption, supposition, hypothesis|反义词：fact, reality, certainty|noun
