credit-worthy:/ˈkrɛdɪtˌwɜːrði/
adjective
值得信贷的；信誉良好的
Deserving of credit or a loan; financially sound.
例句：The bank considers him a credit-worthy customer.  银行认为他是一位值得信贷的客户。

例句：Only credit-worthy applicants will be considered for the loan. 只有信誉良好的申请人才有资格获得贷款。

近义词：reliable, dependable, trustworthy, solvent
反义词：insolvent, unreliable, untrustworthy, risky
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
credit (from Latin creditum "something entrusted to another, a loan," past participle of credere "to believe, trust") + -worthy (from Old English -weorðe "worthy of," related to weorð "worth").  The term literally means "worthy of being entrusted with credit."
