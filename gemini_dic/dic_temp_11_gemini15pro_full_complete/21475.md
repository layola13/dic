blowziness:/ˈblaʊzinəs/| n.|邋遢，不整洁，蓬乱|The blowziness of the room was unsettling.  房间的邋遢令人不安。|例句：The blowziness of her hair suggested she had just woken up. 她蓬乱的头发表明她刚起床。|近义词：untidiness, messiness, disorder, dishevelment, sloppiness|反义词：tidiness, neatness, orderliness|词性:名词

Etymology: From blowzy + -ness.  Blowzy itself comes from Middle English *blowsen, "to blossom," suggesting a full, disordered appearance like an overblown flower.
