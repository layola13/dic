hominini:/ˈhɒmɪˌnaɪniː/ /ˈhɑːmɪˌnaɪniː/| n.|人族；人亚族|A taxonomic tribe of the subfamily Homininae that comprises the extant genera *Homo* (humans) and *Pan* (chimpanzees and bonobos), along with extinct ancestral genera.|The study of hominini evolution helps us understand our own origins.  人族进化研究有助于我们了解自身的起源。|Near synonyms: hominins, hominids|Antonym: N/A (Taxonomic groups don't typically have direct antonyms)|noun
