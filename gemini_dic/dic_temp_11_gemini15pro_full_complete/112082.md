longingnesses:/ˈlɒŋɪŋnɪsɪz/ | n. |渴望， yearning |A state of intense yearning or desire for something or someone absent or lost. | She felt a deep longingness for her homeland. 她深深地怀念着她的家乡。|  The longingnesses of exile are difficult to bear. 流亡的渴望难以忍受。 |Synonyms: yearnings, hankerings, cravings, desires | Antonyms: apathy, indifference, contentment | noun
