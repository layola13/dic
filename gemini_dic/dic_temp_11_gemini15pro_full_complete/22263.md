bonefish:/ˈboʊnˌfɪʃ/| n.|骨鱼;北梭鱼|A marine fish, Albula vulpes, of warm seas, popular as a game fish because of its speed and fighting ability.|The bonefish is a prized game fish known for its powerful runs.|Synonyms:  albula, ladyfish (loosely)|Antonyms: None|Noun

bonefish:/ˈboʊnˌfɪʃ/| adj. (Informal, less common)|与骨鱼有关的；像骨鱼一样的|Used to describe something related to or resembling a bonefish. |The bonefish flats are shallow and sandy.|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|Adjective
