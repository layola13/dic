afflicters:/əˈflɪktərz/
noun
折磨者，使痛苦的人
Those who cause pain or suffering to others.
The afflicters were punished for their cruelty.  折磨者因其残忍而受到惩罚。
synonyms: tormentors, oppressors, persecutors
antonyms: comforters, healers, helpers
