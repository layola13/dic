embattle:/ɪmˈbætəl/ | vt. | 战斗；使准备战斗；| To prepare for battle; to array for battle. | The king embattled his troops along the border. 国王沿着边界部署了他的军队。 |  The knight embattled himself for the coming duel. 骑士为即将到来的决斗做好了准备。| 近义词：prepare, arm, fortify | 反义词: disarm, weaken | 动词

词源：
From en- + battle, from Old French *embatre, from en- + batre "to beat".
