clavicorn: /kləˈvɪkɔːrn/
adjective
棒角的，棍棒状触角的
Having clubbed antennae.  Relating to or denoting beetles of the suborder Clavicornia (now usually classified within Polyphaga), which have club-shaped antennae.
Clavicorn beetles are a diverse group. 棒角甲虫是一个种类繁多的群体。
The ladybug is a clavicorn beetle. 瓢虫是一种棒角甲虫。
近义词: club-horned
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider antennae with different shapes, e.g., filiform, serrate, pectinate)
词性: adjective
