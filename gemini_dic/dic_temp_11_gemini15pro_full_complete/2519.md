administratorships:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtərʃɪps/
|n.|管理职位；遗产管理权|The position and duties of an administrator, especially of an estate.  The state or fact of being an administrator. | He held several administratorships during his career.  他职业生涯中担任过多个管理职位。|Synonyms: administration, management, directorship, governorship, superintendency|Antonyms: subordination, dependence|noun

administratorships:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtərʃɪps/
|n.|（复数）遗产管理权（通常指多个遗产）|The responsibilities and legal rights associated with managing the estate of a deceased person, often referring to multiple estates. |She was burdened with three administratorships after her relatives passed away. 她亲戚去世后，她背负着三个遗产管理权。|Synonyms:  executorships, trusteeships|Antonyms:  beneficiaries, heirs|noun
