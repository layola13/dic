diastema:/daɪˈæstɪmə/ /daɪˈæstimə/| n. |齿隙，牙缝|A space or gap between two teeth.|She has a diastema between her two front teeth. 她两颗门牙之间有个牙缝。|Synonyms: gap, space|Antonyms: closure|noun


diastema:/daɪˈæstɪmə/ /daɪˈæstimə/| n. |(生物)分隔, 隔离|A natural separation or break between two parts of an organism.|The river formed a diastema, isolating the two populations of frogs. 这条河形成了一个分隔，隔离了两个青蛙种群。|Synonyms: separation, division, gap|Antonyms: connection, union|noun
