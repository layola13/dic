noncontentious:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛnʃəs/ | adj. | 无争议的；不引起争论的 | Not likely to cause an argument or disagreement. | The meeting was noncontentious, and everyone agreed on the next steps. 会议没有争议，每个人都同意接下来的步骤。| The bill passed with noncontentious support from both parties. 这项法案在两党毫无争议的支持下获得通过。 | 近义词: uncontroversial, undisputed, amicable, agreed upon | 反义词: contentious, controversial, disputed, debatable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "contentious" (likely to cause argument)  "contentious" comes from the Latin "content-, contendere" meaning "to strive, struggle."
