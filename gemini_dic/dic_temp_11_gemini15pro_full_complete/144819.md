over-immunized: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmjʊnaɪzd/
adjective
过度免疫的
Having received more immunizations than are necessary or recommended.
The child was over-immunized, receiving multiple doses of the same vaccine within a short period.  这个孩子过度免疫了，在短时间内接种了多剂相同的疫苗。
The veterinarian warned against over-immunizing pets, as it can lead to adverse reactions. 兽医警告不要过度给宠物免疫，因为这会导致不良反应。
synonyms: excessively immunized
antonyms: under-immunized, unvaccinated
词性: adjective
