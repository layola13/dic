microphthalmia:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈfθælmiə/| n.|小眼球症|A congenital defect in which one or both eyes are abnormally small.|The child was diagnosed with microphthalmia at birth.  这个孩子出生时被诊断出患有小眼球症。|Synonyms: microphthalmos|Antonyms: macrophthalmia|noun
