ambivalence:/æmˈbɪvələns/| n.|矛盾心理， ambivalence |The film explores the theme of ambivalence towards technological progress. 这部电影探讨了对技术进步的矛盾心理。|近义词：uncertainty, indecision, equivocation|反义词：certainty, decisiveness, conviction|noun

Etymology->
From Latin ambi- (“both”) + valentia (“strength”).  Thus, having "both strengths," referring to the simultaneous presence of opposing feelings.
