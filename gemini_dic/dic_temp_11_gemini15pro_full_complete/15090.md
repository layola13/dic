baccate:/ˈbækət/
adjective
浆果状的；有肉质果实的
Having a pulpy texture like a berry.
The baccate fruits of the plant are attractive to birds.  这种植物的浆果状果实对鸟类很有吸引力。
近义词: fleshy, pulpy, berry-like
反义词: dry, hard
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *baccātus (“berry-like”), from bacca (“berry”).
