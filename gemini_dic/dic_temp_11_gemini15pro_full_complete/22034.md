bokeh:/ˈboʊkeɪ/ (US), /ˈbɒkeɪ/ (UK)| n. |散景|The aesthetic quality of the blur produced in the out-of-focus parts of an image produced by a lens.  It is often described in qualitative terms, such as smooth, pleasing, harsh, etc.|The photographer skillfully used bokeh to create a dreamy atmosphere in the portrait.|近义词:blur, out-of-focus|反义词:sharpness, focus|noun
