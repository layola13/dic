attenuator:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪtər/| n.|衰减器，减弱器|A device that reduces the strength of a signal, power, or current.|The electrician installed an attenuator to reduce the signal strength to the antenna.|Synonyms: damper, reducer, suppressor|Antonyms: amplifier, booster|noun
