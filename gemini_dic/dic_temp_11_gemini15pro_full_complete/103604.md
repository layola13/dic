joint-venturer:/ˌdʒɔɪnt ˈvɛntʃərər/| n. |合资企业家，合营者|A person or company that participates in a joint venture.|  Example:  The two joint venturers agreed to share the profits equally. 两家合资企业同意平分利润。

Example:  The Chinese joint venturer provided the manufacturing expertise. 中方合资企业提供了制造专业技术。|近义词：partner, collaborator, associate|反义词:competitor, rival|词性:noun
