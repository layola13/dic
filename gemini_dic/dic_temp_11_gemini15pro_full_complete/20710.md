blags:/blæɡz/ | n. pl. | 吹牛；谎言；骗局 | Persuasive talk, typically designed to deceive or impress someone. | He told a pack of blags to get the job.  他为了得到这份工作，编造了一堆谎言。 |  synonyms: lies, fibs, deceit, fabrication, storytelling, yarns, whoppers | antonyms: truths, facts, honesty, sincerity | noun

blags:/blæɡz/ | v. (blags, blagging, blagged) | 骗取；蒙混 | To obtain something by using persuasion or deception. | He blagged his way into the party. 他骗过了保安，混进了派对。| synonyms: wheedle, coax, charm, swindle, con, hustle | antonyms: earn, deserve, be honest | verb
