belamour:/ˈbɛləmʊr/| n.|爱人，情人；甜蜜的爱情；调情|A beloved person; a sweetheart; amorous dalliance. |She called him her belamour, her one true love.|近义词：sweetheart, lover, darling, beloved|反义词：enemy, foe|词性:noun

词源: From Middle French *bel amour* ("beautiful love"), from *bel* ("beautiful") + *amour* ("love").
