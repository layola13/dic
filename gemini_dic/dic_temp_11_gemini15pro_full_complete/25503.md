bullied:/ˈbʊlid/ | v. | 欺负；胁迫 | to treat someone cruelly and unfairly, especially on a regular basis.  | He was bullied at school for being overweight. 他因为肥胖在学校被欺负。| Synonyms: harassed, intimidated, persecuted, tormented | Antonyms: protected, defended, supported | verb

bullied:/ˈbʊlid/ | adj. | 被欺负的 | subjected to bullying. | The bullied child was too afraid to speak up.  被欺负的孩子太害怕了不敢说话。| Synonyms: victimized, harassed, persecuted | Antonyms: protected, defended, supported | adjective
