ombudsmanship:/ˈɒmbʊdzmənʃɪp (UK), ˈɑːmbʊdzmənʃɪp (US)/
n.
申诉专员制度；申诉专员的职务或职权
The system or role of an ombudsman.  An ombudsman investigates complaints and tries to resolve disputes, especially between individuals and organizations.
The university has a strong ombudsmanship program to handle student grievances.  这所大学有一个强有力的申诉专员制度来处理学生的抱怨。
The effectiveness of the ombudsmanship depends on its independence and impartiality. 申诉专员制度的有效性取决于其独立性和公正性。
近义词: advocacy, mediation, arbitration
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) suppression, disregard, indifference
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Swedish *ombudsman* (agent or representative), from *ombud* (commission, agency) + *man* (man).
