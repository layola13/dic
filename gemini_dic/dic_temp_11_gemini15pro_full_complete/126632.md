naim:/neɪm/ | n. | 名字，名称;  名誉, 名声 |A word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known or referred to; fame or reputation. |例句：What is your naim? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：name, appellation, title, designation |反义词: anonymity, obscurity | noun

naim:/neɪm/ | v. | 命名，提名;  指定, 任命 |To give a name to; to mention or identify by name; to nominate or appoint. |例句：They naimed their son after his grandfather. 他们以祖父的名字为儿子命名。|近义词：name, call, christen, dub, entitle, nominate, appoint, designate |反义词:  | verb
