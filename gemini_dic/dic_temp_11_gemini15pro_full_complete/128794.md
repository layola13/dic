nighted:/ˈnaɪtɪd/
adjective
夜间的；被夜幕笼罩的；黑暗的
Overtaken by night; dark.  Affected or covered by darkness.
例句：The nighted sky was filled with stars. 夜晚的天空布满了星星。

例句：The travelers found themselves in a nighted forest. 旅行者发现自己身处黑暗的森林中。

近义词：dark, darkened, nocturnal
反义词：daylit, sunlit
词性: adjective
