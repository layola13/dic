housemaids:/ˈhaʊsmeɪdz/| n. |女佣，家佣|A female servant employed to do housework.|The housemaids cleaned the entire mansion every day. 女佣们每天都打扫整座豪宅。|近义词：maids, servants, domestics|反义词:employer, master|noun

词源:  From Middle English *housemayde*, from Old English *hūsmaġd* (“house-maid”), equivalent to *house* +‎ *maid*.
