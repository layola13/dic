如果你是想了解疑问词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供如下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something | Example: What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | Synonym: which (in some cases) | Antonym: (no direct antonym) | pronoun, determiner, adverb
