maharajas:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːdʒəz/ | n. | 大君，土邦主，(尤指印度的)王公 | A Hindu prince, especially one of high rank, often ruling a state in India. |  The maharajas lived in opulent palaces.  大君们住在富丽堂皇的宫殿里。

The maharajas of India lost much of their power under British rule.  在英国统治下，印度的大君们失去了大部分权力。| synonyms: prince, ruler, sovereign, potentate | antonyms: subject, commoner | noun
