cricothyroid:/ˌkraɪkoʊˈθaɪrɔɪd/| adj. n.|环甲状的，环甲肌；环甲关节|Relating to the cricoid and thyroid cartilages.  A muscle that tenses the vocal cords. The joint between the cricoid and thyroid cartilages.|The cricothyroid muscle is essential for phonation. 环甲肌对发声至关重要。

The cricothyroid joint allows for changes in pitch. 环甲关节可以改变音调。|近义词:laryngeal(形容词用法时)|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From crico- + thyroid.  "Crico-" comes from the Greek "krikos," meaning "ring" (referring to the cricoid cartilage).  "Thyroid" comes from the Greek "thyreos," meaning "shield" (referring to the thyroid cartilage).
