damagingly:/ˈdæmɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb|有害地，破坏性地|In a way that causes damage or harm.|例句：The scandal reflected damagingly on the company.  这起丑闻对公司造成了有害的影响。|近义词：harmfully, detrimentally, destructively, injuriously|反义词：beneficially, helpfully, positively|词性: 副词
词源: From the adjective "damaging" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Damaging" comes from the verb "damage," which ultimately derives from the Old French word "damage," meaning "loss, harm."
