metagnathism:/mɛˈtæɡnəθɪzəm/
|n.|后颚型，后颚性|A condition in arthropods where the mouthparts are located posteriorly, behind the other head appendages.|The metagnathism of the spider allows it to inject venom into its prey. 蜘蛛的后颚性使其能够将毒液注入猎物体内。|近义词: opisthognathism|反义词: prognathism|词性: noun


Etymology:
From meta- (Greek: "after, behind") + gnath- (Greek: "jaw") + -ism (suffix forming nouns of condition or state).
