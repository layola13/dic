animating:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
赋予活力的，使有生气的，动画的
making something more lively or interesting; giving life or movement to something
Her smile was animating. 她的笑容充满了活力。
The music was animating the dancers. 音乐使舞者们充满活力。
synonyms: enlivening, invigorating, vitalizing, stimulating, inspiring
antonyms: deadening, dulling, depressing, subduing, disheartening
词性:形容词


animating:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪŋ/
verb
使有生气;使活泼;激励;推动;制作动画
to make someone seem more lively or full of energy
His presence animated the whole party. 他的出现使整个聚会气氛活跃起来。
To animate a puppet show.  表演木偶戏。
synonyms:  enliven, invigorate, stimulate, inspire, motivate
antonyms: deaden, depress, subdue, discourage, dishearten
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin animatus, past participle of animare "give breath to, make alive," from Latin anima "breath, soul."
