acceptable:/əkˈseptəbl/| adj.|可接受的，合意的|Something that is acceptable is satisfactory and meets the required standards or is considered suitable.|The quality of the work was acceptable. 这项工作的质量是可以接受的。|近义词：adequate, satisfactory, tolerable, decent|反义词：unacceptable, inadequate, unsatisfactory, intolerable|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin acceptabilis, from acceptare ‘accept’.
