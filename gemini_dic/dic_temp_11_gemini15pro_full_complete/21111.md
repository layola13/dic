blissed:/blɪst/| adj.|极乐的，狂喜的|Extremely happy; in a state of perfect happiness.|She looked utterly blissed after the birth of her child. 她生完孩子后看起来无比幸福。|近义词：ecstatic, joyful, elated, rapturous|反义词：miserable, wretched, unhappy|词性:形容词
词源: From bliss + -ed.  Bliss comes from Old English *blīþs, from blīþe "happy, kind, friendly, gracious, merciful, gentle, pleasant."
