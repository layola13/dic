historicism:/hɪˈstɔːrɪˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 历史主义 | The theory that social and cultural phenomena are determined by historical context.  It often involves the belief that ideas and values are specific to their time and cannot be understood outside of it, or applied universally.  It can also refer to the strong consciousness of and preoccupation with the historical background of events or artistic works. |  Historians debated the merits of historicism. 历史学家们争论历史主义的价值。

Her analysis revealed a strong element of historicism in his writings. 她的分析揭示了他的著作中强烈的历史主义元素。

| 近义词: contextualism, relativism | 反义词: universalism, absolutism | 词性: noun
