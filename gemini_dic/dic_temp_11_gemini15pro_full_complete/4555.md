alchemist:/ˈælkɪmɪst/| n. |炼金术士；点金术士|A person who practices alchemy, a medieval chemical science and speculative philosophy aiming to achieve the transmutation of base metals into gold, the discovery of a universal cure for disease, and the prolongation of life.|例句：The alchemist spent years searching for the philosopher's stone. 这位炼金术士花了数年时间寻找点金石。

例句：In the novel, the alchemist guides the young shepherd on his journey. 在小说中，炼金术士引导年轻的牧羊人踏上旅程。

近义词：magician, sorcerer, wizard|反义词:|词性:noun
