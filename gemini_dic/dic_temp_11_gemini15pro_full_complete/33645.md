cinematic:/ˌsɪnəˈmætɪk/| adj.|电影的，电影艺术的，像电影的|Relating to the cinema or techniques used in making films; having qualities that are suggestive of motion pictures.|The movie had a very cinematic feel, with sweeping shots of the landscape. 这部电影有一种非常电影化的感觉，有着横扫整个景色的镜头。|近义词：filmic, theatrical, dramatic|反义词：realistic, mundane|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cinématique*, from Greek *kínēma* "movement," from *kineîn* "to move."  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *key- "to set in motion, to stir."
