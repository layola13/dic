codriver:/ˈkoʊˌdraɪvər/| n.|共同驾驶者，副驾驶|The person who shares the driving responsibilities with the main driver.  Can also refer to a person who actively participates in and promotes a project or initiative alongside another. |Example: My wife acted as the codriver on our road trip, navigating and keeping me awake. 我的妻子在我们的公路旅行中担任副驾驶，负责导航和让我保持清醒。|Example: She was an invaluable codriver in the project, offering crucial insights and support.  她在项目中是一位 invaluable 的共同推进者，提供了关键的见解和支持。|近义词：co-pilot, navigator, partner, collaborator|反义词:driver, solo driver|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"co-" is a prefix meaning "together" or "jointly."  It comes from the Latin "com-" (meaning "with," "together").
"drive" comes from the Old English "drīfan," meaning "to push," "to compel," "to drive."
