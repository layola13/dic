cymograph:/ˈsaɪməɡræf/| n. |记波器，描记器|An instrument for recording variations in pressure, especially blood pressure, or other physiological phenomena, in the form of a curve on a revolving drum.|The cymograph recorded the subtle changes in the patient's heart rate. 这台记波器记录了病人细微的心率变化。|Synonyms: recorder, kymograph|Antonyms: None|noun
