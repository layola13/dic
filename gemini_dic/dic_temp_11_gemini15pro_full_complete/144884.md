over-ripeness:/ˈəʊvərˈraɪpnəs/ /ˈōvərˈrīpnəs/ | n. | 过熟，过度成熟 | The over-ripeness of the fruit made it mushy. | The bananas had reached a state of over-ripeness and were starting to brown. | overmaturity, ripeness | unripeness, immaturity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + ripeness.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above," "across," "excessive," etc. "Ripeness" comes from the Old English "rīpe," meaning "ripe."
