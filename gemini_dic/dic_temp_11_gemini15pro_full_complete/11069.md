archaeologist: /ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /ˌɑːrkiˈɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)| n.|考古学家|A person who studies human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|The archaeologist carefully brushed the dirt from the ancient pottery. 考古学家小心翼翼地把泥土从古代陶器上刷掉。|近义词: excavator, historian, anthropologist|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps vandal or looter in some contexts)|Noun
