exponentiation:/ˌɛkspoʊˌnɛnʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 指数运算，乘方 | the mathematical operation of raising a quantity to a power | The exponentiation of 2 to the third power is 8 (2^3 = 8).  2的三次方是8。 | synonyms: raising to a power, involution | antonyms: root extraction, logarithm | noun

exponentiations:/ˌɛkspoʊˌnɛnʃiˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 指数运算（复数）| multiple instances of the mathematical operation of raising a quantity to a power | The calculations involved several exponentiations.  这些计算涉及多次指数运算。| synonyms: raising to powers, involutions | antonyms: root extractions, logarithms | noun
