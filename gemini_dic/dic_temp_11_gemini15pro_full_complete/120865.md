misbeseem:/ˌmɪsbɪˈsiːm/
verb
不适合，不相称
to be unsuitable for or unbecoming to; to not befit
Her gaudy dress misbeseems the solemnity of the occasion. 她那件俗丽的衣服与这个严肃的场合不相称。
近义词:misbecome, ill-suit
反义词:suit, befit, become
词性:verb
