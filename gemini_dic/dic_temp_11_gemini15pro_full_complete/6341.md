amperian:/æmˈpɪəriən/ | adj.| 安培的，与电流有关的；安培环路的 |Relating to an electric current or its magnetic field, especially to Ampère’s law.  Specifically relating to an imaginary closed curve (Amperian loop) used in the calculation of a magnetic field produced by an electric current.| An Amperian loop is useful for calculating the magnetic field of a solenoid. 安培环路对于计算螺线管的磁场很有用。|  electromagnetic, current-related | non-amperian | adjective
Etymology-> From André-Marie Ampère (1775–1836), French physicist + -ian.
