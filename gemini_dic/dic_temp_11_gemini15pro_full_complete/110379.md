lignite:/ˈlɪɡnaɪt/ | n. |褐煤|A soft brownish-black coal, intermediate between peat and bituminous coal, containing a high percentage of volatile matter and moisture.|Lignite is used as a fuel for power plants.|Near synonyms: brown coal|Antonym: anthracite|noun

lignites:/ˈlɪɡnaɪts/ | n. |褐煤（复数）|The plural form of lignite, referring to multiple instances of this type of coal.|Various lignites are mined in different regions.|Near synonyms: brown coals|Antonym: anthracites|noun
