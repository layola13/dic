nonemployment:/ˌnɒnɪmˈplɔɪmənt/ | n. |失业，非就业|The state of not being employed; lack of employment.  The nonemployment rate includes people who are not actively looking for work. |The region suffers from high nonemployment.  该地区失业率很高。|Synonyms: unemployment, joblessness|Antonyms: employment, occupation|noun
