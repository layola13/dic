on-the-make: /ˌɒn ðə ˈmeɪk/ (British), /ˌɑːn ðə ˈmeɪk/ (American)
| adj. | 追求私利的；一心想往上爬的；急于赚钱或获得性关系的 | Eager to gain personal advantage, typically wealth or sexual relationships. | He was a ruthless businessman, always on the make. 他是个冷酷无情的商人，总想着捞好处。 |  近义词: ambitious, opportunistic, grasping, predatory | 反义词: altruistic, selfless | 词性: 形容词

on-the-make: /ˌɒn ðə ˈmeɪk/ (British), /ˌɑːn ðə ˈmeɪk/ (American)
| n. | 追求私利的人 | A person who is eager to gain personal advantage. | He's a real on-the-make, always looking for angles. 他真是个一心想往上爬的人，总是在寻找机会。 | 近义词: opportunist, social climber, hustler | 反义词: philanthropist | 词性: 名词


词源:  "Make" in this context originally meant "to achieve or gain something."  The phrase "on the make" emerged in the late 19th century, initially referring to actively pursuing wealth or advancement.  Later, it acquired the connotation of pursuing sexual opportunities.
