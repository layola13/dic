ishi:/ˈiːʃiː/ | n. |石頭; 石块 (Inuktitut) |A stone or rock.  This word comes from Inuktitut, an Inuit language spoken in Canada. |例句：The inukshuk was built of many ishi.  因纽特石堆是用许多石头建造的。

例句：Elders told stories of how the ishi were placed.  长者讲述了这些石头是如何被放置的故事。|近义词：stone, rock |反义词: None (It's a concrete noun) |词性:noun
