bobcat:/ˈbɑːbˌkæt/ /ˈbɒbˌkæt/| n.|山猫，红猫|A North American wild cat (Lynx rufus) with a short tail, typically reddish brown with dark spots and streaks.|例句：We saw a bobcat while hiking in the mountains. 我们在山上徒步旅行时看到了一只山猫。|近义词：wildcat, lynx|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑domestic cat家猫)|noun
