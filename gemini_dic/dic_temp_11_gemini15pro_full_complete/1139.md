accidental:/ˌæksɪˈdentl/ | adj. | 意外的；偶然的；附带的 | Happening by chance, not planned or intended. |  The discovery of penicillin was accidental. 青霉素的发现是偶然的。

The damage was purely accidental. 损害纯属意外。 | synonyms: unintentional, unintended, chance, unforeseen, unexpected, unlooked-for | antonyms: intentional, deliberate, planned, premeditated | adjective


accidental:/ˌæksɪˈdentl/ | n. |  偶然事件；意外事故（通常指轻微的） | Something that happens by chance, especially something unfortunate or damaging that is not serious. |  He had a few accidentals during the concert.  他在音乐会上出现了一些失误。

There were a few accidentals in the manuscript. 手稿中有一些偶然的错误。 | synonyms: mishap, mischance, misfortune | antonyms: intention, plan | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accidentalis, from Latin accidens (“happening, chance, accident”), present participle of accido (“happen to, befall”).
