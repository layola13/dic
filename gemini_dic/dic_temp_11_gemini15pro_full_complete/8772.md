antidynastical: /ˌænti daɪˈnæstɪkəl/ /ˌænti daɪˈnæstɪkl/
| adj. | 反王朝的，反对王朝统治的 | Opposed to dynasties or dynastic rule; against the rule of a particular family or group. | The antidynastical sentiments were growing among the populace.  民众的反王朝情绪日益高涨。| synonyms: anti-monarchical, revolutionary, rebellious | antonyms: pro-dynastic, monarchist, royalist | adjective
