affluently: /ˈæfluəntli/ | adv. | 富裕地，丰富地 | In a manner characterized by wealth and abundance; richly. | They lived affluently, with multiple homes and luxury cars.  他们生活富裕，拥有多处房产和豪车。 |  例句：She dressed affluently in designer clothes. 她穿着昂贵的品牌服装，显得十分富裕。| 近义词：wealthily, richly, opulently | 反义词：poorly, meagerly | 词性: 副词

affluent: /ˈæfluənt/ | adj. | 富裕的 | Having a great deal of money; wealthy. |  They live in an affluent neighborhood. 他们住在富人区。 | 例句：The affluent family enjoyed lavish vacations.  这个富裕的家庭享受着奢华的假期。| 近义词：wealthy, rich, prosperous | 反义词：poor, impoverished, destitute | 词性: 形容词

词源：From Latin *affluentem (nominative affluens) "abundant, copious, flowing to," present participle of affluere "to flow to," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + fluere "to flow" (see fluent).  Meaning "wealthy, copious" first recorded in English 1590s.
