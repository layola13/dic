moonsail:/ˈmuːnseɪl/| n.|月帆，太阳帆|A large, reflective sail used to propel a spacecraft by means of the pressure of sunlight or, theoretically, moonlight.|例句：The moonsail, unfurling in the vacuum of space, caught the faint light of the distant sun. 月帆在真空的太空中展开，捕捉到远处微弱的阳光。

例句：Scientists are exploring the feasibility of a moonsail propelled by lasers. 科学家们正在探索用激光推进月帆的可行性。|近义词：solar sail, lightsail|反义词:None|词性:noun
