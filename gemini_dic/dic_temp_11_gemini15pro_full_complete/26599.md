cachalot:/ˈkæʃəlɒt/ (US) /ˈkæʃəlɒt/ (UK)| n. |抹香鲸|A large toothed whale (Physeter macrocephalus), the largest of the toothed whales, found in all oceans. It is distinguished by its very large, square head and narrow lower jaw.  It is hunted for its oil (spermaceti) and ambergris.|例句：The cachalot is known for its deep dives. 抹香鲸以其深潜而闻名。

例句：The whalers hunted the cachalot for its valuable spermaceti. 捕鲸人捕猎抹香鲸是为了获取珍贵的鲸脑油。|近义词：sperm whale|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
