cashier:/kæˈʃɪr/ (US), /kæˈʃɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.  |收银员|A person who handles payments and gives change in a shop, bank, etc.|The cashier scanned my groceries. 收银员扫描了我的杂货。|Synonyms: teller, clerk, checkout operator|Antonyms: customer, shopper|noun

cashier:/kæˈʃɪr/ (US), /kæˈʃɪə(r)/ (UK)| vt. |解雇，开除（尤指军官）|To dismiss from a position of trust or responsibility, especially from the armed forces.|He was cashiered from the army for disobeying orders. 他因不服从命令而被军队开除。|Synonyms: dismiss, discharge, sack|Antonyms: employ, hire, promote|verb
