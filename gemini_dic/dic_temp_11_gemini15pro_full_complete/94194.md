imperceptibility:/ˌɪmpərˌsɛptɪˈbɪlɪti/| n. |不可察觉性；难以察觉|The imperceptibility of the change made it difficult to detect.  这种变化难以察觉，很难被发现。|例句：The imperceptibility of the gradual change in temperature was remarkable.  温度逐渐变化的难以察觉令人注目。

The drug's effects were marked by their imperceptibility. 这种药物的作用特点是难以察觉。|近义词：invisibility, undetectability, impalpability|反义词：perceptibility, visibility, noticeability|词性:noun


imperceptible:/ˌɪmpərˈsɛptɪbl/| adj. |不可察觉的；难以察觉的|The change was so imperceptible that nobody noticed it. 变化如此细微，以至于没有人注意到。|例句：The difference between the two shades of blue was almost imperceptible.  这两种蓝色之间的区别几乎难以察觉。

Her heartbeat was so faint as to be almost imperceptible.  她的心跳非常微弱，几乎难以察觉。|近义词：unnoticeable, invisible, undetectable|反义词：perceptible, noticeable, visible|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *imperceptibilis, from Latin in- (“not”) + perceptibilis (“perceptible”), from percipere (“to perceive”).
