fissured:/ˈfɪʃərd/| adj.|裂缝的，有裂缝的|Having long narrow cracks or openings.|The drought left the earth dry and fissured. 干旱使土地变得干燥和龟裂。|近义词：cracked, split, fractured, clefted|反义词：smooth, whole, intact|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *fissura* ("cleaving, cleft, fissure"), from *findere* ("to split, cleave").
