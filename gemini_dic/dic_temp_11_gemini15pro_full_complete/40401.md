contraindicate:/ˌkɑːntrəˈɪndɪkeɪt/ | v. |禁忌；阻止；显示...不适合|To suggest or indicate that (a particular treatment or procedure) should not be used. | The patient's high blood pressure contraindicates this medicine.  这位病人的高血压使得不宜使用这种药物。 | Synonyms: proscribe, forbid, preclude | Antonyms: indicate, suggest, advise | verb

contraindication:/ˌkɑːntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |禁忌症候|A factor that makes a particular treatment or procedure inadvisable. |  A history of blood clots is a contraindication for this type of surgery. 有血栓病史是这类手术的禁忌症。| Synonyms:  objection, deterrent | Antonyms: indication, recommendation | noun
