elmina:/ɛlˈmiːnə/ | n. |埃尔米纳|A town and former slave trading post in Ghana, West Africa.  It is home to two castles, Elmina Castle and Cape Coast Castle, which played significant roles in the transatlantic slave trade.| Elmina Castle is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 埃尔米纳城堡是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。|Near synonyms: castle, fort, trading post | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
