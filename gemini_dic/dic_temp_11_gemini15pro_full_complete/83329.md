gypsum:/ˈdʒɪpsəm/ | n. | 石膏 | A soft white or gray mineral consisting of hydrated calcium sulfate. It is used to make plaster of Paris and fertilizers, and occurs in various forms, including alabaster and selenite. | Gypsum is a common mineral found in sedimentary rocks. 石膏是一种常见的沉积岩矿物。| synonyms: plaster, plaster stone, sulfate of lime | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a mineral) | noun

gypsine:/ˈdʒɪpsaɪn/ | adj. | 石膏的；含石膏的 | Relating to or containing gypsum. | The walls were made of gypsine plaster. 墙壁是用石膏灰泥做的。| synonyms: gypseous | antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a descriptive adjective) | adjective
