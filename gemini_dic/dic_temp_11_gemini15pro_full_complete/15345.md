backstay: /ˈbæk.steɪ/ (US) /ˈbæk.steɪ/ (UK)
n.
后支索，后拉索
A strong rope or wire running from the top of a mast to the stern of a ship, used to support the mast.
The backstay snapped during the storm.  暴风雨中后支索断了。

近义词: stay, support, brace
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "loosening" in a functional sense)
词性: noun
