arrowwood:/ˈæroʊˌwʊd/
n.
箭木
any of several North American shrubs of the genus *Viburnum*, especially *V. dentatum*, having straight stems formerly used for arrow shafts.
Several species of arrowwood are native to this region.  这个地区生长着好几种箭木。

近义词:Viburnum (荚蒾属植物,更广义)
反义词: (无明显的反义词，可根据语境选择例如：softwood - 软木)
词性: noun
