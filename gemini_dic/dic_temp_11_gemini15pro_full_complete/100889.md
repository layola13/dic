intuit:/ɪnˈtjuːɪt/ | v. | 理解；凭直觉感知；由直觉推断 | To understand or know something by intuition. | She intuited that something was wrong. 她凭直觉感到出了问题。 |  intuit that he was unhappy. 直觉到他不开心 | synonyms: perceive, sense, divine, discern | antonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret | verb

intuition:/ˌɪn.tʃuːˈɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 直觉 | The ability to understand something immediately, without the need for conscious reasoning. |  She had an intuition that he was lying. 她直觉到他在撒谎。 | He acted on intuition. 他凭直觉行事。 | synonyms: hunch, instinct, feeling, premonition | antonyms: reasoning, logic | noun
