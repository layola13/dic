aslake:/əˈsleɪk/
verb
平息，减轻，缓和
to make something less strong, or to satisfy a feeling
The cool water aslaked his thirst.  这凉水缓解了他的口渴。
近义词:quench, satisfy, relieve, soothe
反义词:intensify, aggravate, increase
词性:verb

词源: From Old English *aslacian, from a- (prefix indicating "in, on, at, to") + slæc "slack".  Related to slack.
