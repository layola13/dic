innateness:/ɪˈneɪtnəs/ |n.| 天生；固有；先天性 |The innateness of language is a complex and controversial topic. 语言的先天性是一个复杂且有争议的话题。|  例句：The innateness of musical talent is still debated.  音乐天赋的先天性仍然存在争议。| 近义词：inherence, naturalness, congenitality | 反义词：acquiredness, learnedness | 词性：名词

innate:/ɪˈneɪt/ |adj.| 天生的；固有的；先天的 |Humans have an innate capacity for language. 人类具有天生的语言能力。| 例句：He has an innate sense of rhythm. 他有一种与生俱来的节奏感。 | 近义词：inborn, inherent, natural | 反义词：acquired, learned | 词性：形容词

词源: From Late Latin *innatus, from Latin in- “in” + natus “born,” past participle of nasci “to be born.”
