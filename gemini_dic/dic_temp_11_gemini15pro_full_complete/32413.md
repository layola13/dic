chillsome:/ˈtʃɪlsəm/| adj.|令人寒心的，阴森的|Causing a feeling of fear and coldness; eerie.  Evoking a sense of chill, often due to a spooky or sinister atmosphere.|The chillsome silence of the abandoned house made me uneasy.  那栋废弃房屋令人毛骨悚然的寂静让我感到不安。|近义词：chilling, eerie, spooky, creepy, sinister|反义词：cozy, warm, comforting|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "chill" + "-some" (a suffix meaning "causing" or "characterized by").  The word "chill" ultimately derives from Old English "ciele."
