oligarchic:/ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪk/ /ˌoʊlɪˈɡɑːrkɪk/ | adj. |寡头政治的，由少数人统治的|Relating to or characterized by government by a small group of people, especially for corrupt and selfish purposes. |The country's oligarchic system concentrates power in the hands of a few wealthy families. 这个国家的寡头政治体制将权力集中在少数富裕家庭手中。

The oligarchic regime suppressed dissent with an iron fist.  这个寡头政权用铁腕压制异议。|近义词：autocratic, plutocratic, authoritarian |反义词：democratic, egalitarian |词性: adjective

oligarchy:/ˈɒlɪɡɑːki/ /ˈoʊlɪɡɑːrki/ | n. |寡头政治，寡头统治集团|A small group of people having control of a country, organization, or institution. |The country was ruled by a powerful oligarchy. 这个国家由一个强大的寡头统治集团统治。

The oligarchy controlled vast resources and industries.  这个寡头统治集团控制着大量的资源和产业。|近义词：plutocracy, aristocracy |反义词：democracy |词性: noun
