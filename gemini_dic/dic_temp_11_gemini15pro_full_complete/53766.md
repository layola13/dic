discomforter:/dɪsˈkʌmfərtər/
n.
令人不快的人或事物; 使人不舒服的东西;  不舒适的寝具(尤指羽绒被、鸭绒被)
Something that causes discomfort; a person or thing that makes one feel uneasy or distressed. In some regions, it refers to a thick, padded bed covering, similar to a duvet or comforter.
The howling wind was a real discomforter on the camping trip.  呼啸的风真是露营旅行中令人不快的因素。
My old mattress is a real discomforter. 我的旧床垫真不舒服。
The news of the layoffs was a discomforter to everyone in the office. 裁员的消息让办公室里的每个人都感到不安。
近义词: nuisance, bother, annoyance, inconvenience,irritant, comforter (in the context of a bed covering)
反义词: comforter (in the sense of something that brings comfort), solace, relief

discomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/
n.
不适，不舒服
A feeling of slight pain or physical uneasiness.
The tight shoes caused her considerable discomfort.  紧的鞋子使她感到很不舒服。
He felt a growing discomfort about the situation. 他对这种情况感到越来越不安。
近义词: uneasiness, pain, distress, soreness
反义词: comfort, ease, relief

discomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/
vt.
使不适，使不安
To make someone feel uneasy or cause them physical discomfort.
The bright lights discomfited her.  明亮的灯光让她感到不舒服。
The politician's evasive answers discomfited the audience. 政治家闪烁其词的回答让听众感到不安。
近义词: disturb, trouble, upset, bother
反义词: comfort, soothe, reassure
