impressionally:/ɪmˈprɛʃənəli/
adverb
令人印象深刻地
in a way that makes a strong impression.
She spoke impressionally about her travels. 她讲述她的旅行经历，令人印象深刻。
近义词: strikingly, memorably, remarkably
反义词: unimpressively, blandly, ordinarily
词性: 副词


词源:  From "impressional" + "-ly."  "Impressional" comes from "impression" (from Latin "impressio,"  meaning a pressing into or upon) + "-al."  The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs.
