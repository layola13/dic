outdrives:/ˈaʊtˌdraɪvz/
noun
外部驱动器;舷外驱动装置;超越，胜过
A unit comprising an engine, propeller shaft, and propeller mounted outside the hull of a boat. / To surpass someone in driving.
The boat's outdrives were powerful enough to handle the rough seas. 这艘船的舷外驱动装置动力强劲，足以应对波涛汹涌的大海。
He easily outdrives his competitors in every race. 他在每场比赛中都能轻松超越他的对手。
近义词: outboard motors, surpasses, exceeds
反义词: lags behind, falls short
词性:n. v.


outdrive:/ˈaʊtˌdraɪv/
verb
超越，胜过(在驾驶方面)
To drive faster or better than.
He outdrove everyone in the race and won easily. 他在比赛中超越了所有人，轻松获胜。
She consistently outdrives her rivals on the track. 她在赛道上总是超越她的对手。
近义词: outstrip, surpass, excel
反义词: trail, lag behind
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
"outdrive" comes from the combination of "out-" (meaning outside, beyond) and "drive" (meaning to propel, to operate a vehicle).  The noun form specifically refers to a propulsion system mounted outside a boat's hull, while the verb form refers to surpassing someone in driving skill or speed.
