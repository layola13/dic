furacious:/fʊˈreɪʃəs/
adj.
偷东西的；有偷窃癖的；
Given to stealing; thievish.
The furacious raccoon snuck into the campsite and stole a bag of marshmallows.  这只偷东西的浣熊溜进营地，偷了一袋棉花糖。
近义词:thieving, light-fingered, pilfering
反义词:honest, law-abiding


词性:adjective
