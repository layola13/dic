kneeling:/ˈniːlɪŋ/ /ˈniːlɪŋ/| n. adj.|跪着，跪下的|The act of being on one's knees.  A position in which one is on one's knees.|He said a prayer while kneeling. 他跪着祈祷。|近义词：genuflecting, crouching|反义词:standing|词性:noun, adjective

kneel:/niːl/ /niːl/| v.|跪下，跪|To go down on one's knees.|She knelt down to pray. 她跪下来祈祷。|近义词：genuflect, crouch|反义词:stand|词性:verb
