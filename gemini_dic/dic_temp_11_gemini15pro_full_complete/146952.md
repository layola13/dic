overinvestment:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstmənt/ | n. | 过度投资 | The act of investing too much capital in a particular asset, sector, or market, leading to an imbalance and potential negative consequences. | The dot-com bubble was a classic example of overinvestment in technology stocks.  互联网泡沫是科技股过度投资的典型例子。| overcapitalization, excess investment | underinvestment | noun
