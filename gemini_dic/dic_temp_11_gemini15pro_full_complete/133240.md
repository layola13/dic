英文解析: The state or quality of being nonexcusable; inability to be excused or justified.  It refers to actions or behaviors so bad that they cannot be forgiven or overlooked.
例句：The nonexcusablenesses of his actions led to his dismissal. 他的行为不可饶恕，导致他被解雇。

例句：The sheer nonexcusablenesses of the crime shocked the nation.  这起罪行的不可饶恕性震惊了全国。

近义词: unforgivability, inexcusability, unjustifiability
反义词: excusability, forgivability, justifiability
