overskeptically:/ˌəʊvəˈskeptɪkli/ | adv. | 过于怀疑地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive skepticism or doubt. | He examined the evidence overskeptically, refusing to believe anything without absolute proof. 他过于怀疑地审查了证据，拒绝相信任何没有绝对证据的事情。|  She listened overskeptically to his promises, doubting his sincerity. 她过于怀疑地听着他的承诺，怀疑他的真诚。| 近义词: excessively skeptically, too skeptically, hypercritically | 反义词: trustingly, credulously, naively | 词性: 副词
