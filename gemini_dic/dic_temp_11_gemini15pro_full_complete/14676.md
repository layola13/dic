aw:/ɔː/ |interjection| 啊;噢 |An exclamation expressing sudden pain, fear, or surprise.| Example: "Aw, I stubbed my toe!" 啊，我的脚趾撞到了！| Synonym: ouch, oh | Antonym: (No direct antonym for an interjection) | Interjection


aw:/ɔː/ |adjective| (informal)令人怜爱的;可爱的 |Inspiring affection or sympathy, especially in a slightly condescending way.| Example: "Aw, look at that cute puppy!" 啊，看看那只可爱的小狗！ | Synonym: cute, adorable, sweet | Antonym: ugly, unpleasant | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aw, probably of imitative origin.  Related to Old Norse *á, and probably influenced by Old French *a.
