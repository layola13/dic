nonsubmersible:/ˌnɒnsʌbˈmɜːsəbl/ /ˌnɑːnsʌbˈmɜːrsəbl/| adj.| 非潜水式的，不能沉入水中的 |Not designed or able to be submerged in water. |This watch is nonsubmersible, so don't wear it while swimming. 这款手表是非潜水式的，所以游泳时不要佩戴。|  The electrical components are nonsubmersible and must be protected from water damage. 电气元件不能浸水，必须防止水损。 | 近义词:non-waterproof, buoyant | 反义词: submersible, waterproof | 词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
submersible (adjective/noun): From submerge + -ible, meaning "capable of being submerged."  Submerge ultimately derives from Latin submergere, from sub- "under" + mergere "to dip, plunge."
