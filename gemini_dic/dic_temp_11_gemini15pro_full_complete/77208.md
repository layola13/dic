gasteropoda:/ˌɡæstəˈrɒpədə/ | n. | 腹足纲 |A large taxonomic class of mollusks, including snails and slugs, characterized by a single shell (often coiled), a broad foot used for locomotion, and a head with tentacles.| Snails, belonging to the Gastropoda class, are common in gardens.  花园里常见的蜗牛属于腹足纲。 | synonyms: univalves, gastropods | antonyms: None | noun
