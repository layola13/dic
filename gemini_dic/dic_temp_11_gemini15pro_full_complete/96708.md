ineffectiveness:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪvnəs/| n. |无效；无用；无能|lack of the ability to produce the desired effect|例句：The ineffectiveness of the policy was evident in the rising unemployment rate. 这项政策的无效性在不断上升的失业率中显而易见。

例句：She was frustrated by the ineffectiveness of the medication.  她对药物的无效感到沮丧。


近义词：futility, uselessness, impotence, inefficacy|反义词: effectiveness, efficacy, potency|词性:noun
