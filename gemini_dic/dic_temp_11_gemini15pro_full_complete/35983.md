coders:/ˈkoʊdərz/ (US), /ˈkəʊdəz/ (UK)| n. |程序员，编码员|A person who writes code for computer programs.  A coder translates instructions into a language a computer can understand.|The team of coders worked tirelessly to finish the project.  这个程序员团队不知疲倦地工作以完成项目。|programmers, developers, software engineers|non-coders|noun
