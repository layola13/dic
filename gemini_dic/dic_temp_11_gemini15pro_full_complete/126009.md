musolini:/ˌmuːsəˈliːni/ | n. | 墨索里尼 | Benito Mussolini (1883-1945), Italian dictator and founder of Fascism. |  Mussolini led Italy into World War II as an ally of Nazi Germany. | 近义词: dictator, fascist | 反义词: democrat | 词性: 名词

词源:  Derived from the Italian surname Mussolini, likely of occupational origin, possibly referring to a maker or seller of muslin cloth.
