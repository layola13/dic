extolling:/ɪkˈstəʊlɪŋ/
verb
赞美；称颂；颂扬
praising enthusiastically.
The critics are extolling the virtues of her new novel. 评论家们正在称赞她新小说的优点。
synonyms: praising, lauding, eulogizing, commending, applauding, acclaiming
antonyms: criticizing, condemning, denouncing
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin extollere "to raise, lift up," from ex- "out, up" + tollere "to lift, raise."
