otter:/ˈɒtə(r) (UK), ˈɑːtər (US)/| n.|水獭|A semiaquatic fish-eating mammal of the weasel family, with a long body, short legs, webbed feet, and a thick, dark brown fur.|We saw a family of otters playing in the river. 我们看到一家水獭在河里嬉戏。|Near synonyms:  weasel, mink, beaver|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal, could consider land animals in general)|noun
