abdicant:/ˈæbdɪkənt/
n.|退位者，放弃者|Someone who abdicates a position of power or responsibility. |The abdicant king lived a quiet life in exile. 退位的国王在流亡中过着平静的生活。|近义词：renouncer, resigner|反义词：successor, inheritor
abdicant:/ˈæbdɪkənt/
adj.|退位的，放弃的|Relating to abdication, the act of giving up a position of power or responsibility. |The abdicant emperor's decision surprised the nation.  退位皇帝的决定震惊了全国。|近义词：renouncing, resigning|反义词：succeeding, inheriting


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abdicant-, present participle of abdicare (“to renounce, disown, reject”), from ab- (“away from”) + dicare (“to proclaim, dedicate, make a vow”)
