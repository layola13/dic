crow:/kroʊ/ | n. v.| 乌鸦；啼叫|A large, black bird with a harsh cry.  The rooster crows at dawn.|例句：A crow perched on the fence post. 一只乌鸦栖息在篱笆柱上。|近义词：raven, rook|反义词:(无明确的反义词)|词性:名词,动词

crow:/kroʊ/ | v.| 啼叫；自鸣得意|To make the loud, harsh cry of a rooster. He crowed about his victory.|例句：The rooster crowed loudly at sunrise. 公鸡在日出时大声啼叫。|近义词：boast, brag|反义词:be modest, downplay|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *crāwe*, from Proto-Germanic *krāwō*, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱrā́w- (“to crow, croak”).
