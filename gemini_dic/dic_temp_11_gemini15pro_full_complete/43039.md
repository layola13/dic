cowhearted:/ˈkaʊˌhɑːrtɪd/
adj.
胆小的，懦弱的
Timid or cowardly.
He was too cowhearted to stand up to the bully. 他太胆小了，不敢反抗那个恶霸。
近义词:timid, cowardly, fearful, pusillanimous
反义词:brave, courageous, bold, valiant
词性:形容词

词源:
From cow + hearted.  "Cow" in this context refers to the perceived timidity of cows.  "Hearted" signifies having the heart or disposition of.  Thus, "cowhearted" means having the heart of a cow, hence timid.
