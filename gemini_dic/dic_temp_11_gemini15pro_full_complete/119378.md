microcrith: /ˈmaɪkroʊkrɪθ/ | n. |微克立夫，百万分之一克立夫| A unit of mass equal to one millionth of a crith.  It is used to express the mass of gas molecules. | The microcrith is a very small unit of mass. 微克立夫是一个非常小的质量单位。| The molecular weight of hydrogen is approximately 2 microcriths. 氢的分子量大约是2微克立夫。 |近义词: atomic mass unit (amu), dalton |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

词源:
From micro- + crith, from Greek *krithe, "barleycorn," originally a unit of mass based on the weight of a barley grain.
