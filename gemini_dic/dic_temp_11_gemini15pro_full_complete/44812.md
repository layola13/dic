crusading:/kruːˈseɪdɪŋ/
adjective
参与运动的；进行改革的；积极从事某项活动的
engaged in a crusade; campaigning vigorously for a cause
例句：
She is a crusading journalist, always fighting for the truth. 她是一位积极从事运动的记者，总是为真相而战。

He's on a crusading mission to improve the local schools. 他正积极从事改善当地学校的运动。

近义词：
campaigning, fighting, striving, advocating, championing
反义词：
apathetic, indifferent, passive

词性:
adjective
