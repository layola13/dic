cradlelike:/ˈkreɪdlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像摇篮一样的，摇篮似的|Resembling a cradle, especially in being protective, comforting, or gently swaying.|The valley nestled between the hills, cradlelike and serene.  山谷依偎在群山之间，像摇篮一样宁静安详。|The gentle rocking of the boat had a cradlelike effect.  小船轻轻摇晃，像摇篮一样。|近义词：protective, comforting, sheltering|反义词:exposed, harsh, unforgiving|词性:adjective
词源: From "cradle" (Old English cradol) + "-like" (Old English -līc).
