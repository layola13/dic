assientist:/əˈsɪstɪst/
adjective
援助主义的; 援助者的
Relating to or characteristic of an assientist; a person who supports or promotes a system of financial assistance or subsidies.
The British government's assientist policies in the 18th century aimed to stimulate colonial trade. 18世纪英国政府的援助主义政策旨在刺激殖民贸易。
The assientist company provided loans and subsidies to colonial planters. 这家援助公司向殖民种植园主提供贷款和补贴。
近义词:  paternalistic, interventionist
反义词:  laissez-faire, non-interventionist
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From assist + -ist.  Ultimately from Latin assistere "to stand by, support."
