biofeedback:/ˈbaɪoʊˌfiːdbæk/| n. |生物反馈|A system for electronically recording, amplifying, and feeding back information regarding a subtle physiological state, such as blood pressure or muscle tension. This allows the individual to gain some voluntary control over these functions. |Example 1: The therapist used biofeedback to help the patient control his migraines. 治疗师使用生物反馈帮助病人控制偏头痛。

Example 2:  Biofeedback techniques can be used to treat anxiety and stress. 生物反馈技术可以用来治疗焦虑和压力。|近义词：neurofeedback|反义词: None|词性:noun


biofeedback:/ˌbaɪoʊˈfiːdbæk/| n. |生物反馈|The process of learning to control bodily functions by monitoring signals from the body.|Example 1: She learned to manage her stress through biofeedback. 她学会了通过生物反馈来控制压力。

Example 2: Biofeedback training can help people lower their blood pressure. 生物反馈训练可以帮助人们降低血压。|近义词：neurofeedback|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
bio-: from Greek "bios" meaning "life".
feedback:  from "feed" + "back". "Feed" meaning to supply and "back" referring to a return.
