nonsugars:/nɒnˈʃʊɡəz/ | n. | 非糖类物质 | Substances other than sugars, typically referring to components of food like fiber, minerals, and vitamins.  |  Example:  The nonsugars in fruits and vegetables contribute to their health benefits. 水果和蔬菜中的非糖类物质有助于它们的健康益处。  | Synonym: non-saccharides | Antonym: sugars | Noun
