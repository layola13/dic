lactiferous:/lækˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
乳汁的；产生乳汁的；输送乳汁的
Producing or conveying milk; milky.
例句：The lactiferous ducts carry milk from the mammary glands to the nipple. 乳腺管将乳汁从乳腺输送到乳头。

例句：The lactiferous glands in mammals are responsible for milk production. 哺乳动物的乳腺负责产奶。

近义词：milk-producing, milk-bearing, mammary
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
