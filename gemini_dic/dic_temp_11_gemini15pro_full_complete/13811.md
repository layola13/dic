auditable:/ˈɔːdɪtəbl/| adj.|可审计的|Able to be audited; suitable for auditing.  This means that something can be checked or examined officially to see if it is correct and legal.|The company's financial records are auditable.  这家公司的财务记录是可审计的。|The system is designed to be auditable and transparent. 该系统的设计目标是可审计和透明的。|近义词：verifiable, inspectable, accountable|反义词：unauditable, opaque|词性:形容词
词源: From "audit" + "-able". "Audit" comes from Latin "auditus," the past participle of "audire" meaning "to hear." Originally, audits were conducted orally.
