boundlessness:/ˈbaʊndləsnəs/| n. |无限；无边无际|The boundlessness of the universe is awe-inspiring. 宇宙的浩瀚令人敬畏。|近义词：immensity, vastness, infinity|反义词：limitation, finiteness|词性:名词

词源:  From boundless + -ness.  Boundless itself comes from bound (meaning "limit") + -less (meaning "without").  Ultimately from Old French *bodne, from a Germanic source.
