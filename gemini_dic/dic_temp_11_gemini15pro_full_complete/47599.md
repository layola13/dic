debating:/dɪˈbeɪtɪŋ/
verb, gerund or present participle|辩论，讨论|The act of engaging in a formal discussion or argument on a particular topic.|They were debating the merits of the new proposal. 他们正在讨论新提案的优点。|discussing, arguing, disputing|agreeing, concurring|verb, noun
debate:/dɪˈbeɪt/
noun, verb|辩论，讨论；争论，考虑|A formal discussion on a particular topic in a public meeting or legislative assembly, in which opposing arguments are put forward.|The debate on climate change continues. 关于气候变化的辩论仍在继续。|discussion, argument, dispute|agreement, concord|noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *debatre "to fight, contend, argue," from de- "down" + batre "to beat."
