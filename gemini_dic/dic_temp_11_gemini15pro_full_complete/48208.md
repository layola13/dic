deconglomerate:/ˌdiːkənˈɡlɒmərət/ | v.  n. | 分解，拆分（企业集团）；企业集团分解；| To separate a large corporation into smaller, independent companies.  To break up a conglomerate. | The company decided to deconglomerate and focus on its core business.  这家公司决定拆分并专注于其核心业务。| The deconglomeration of the company resulted in several smaller, more agile businesses.  公司的分解形成了几个更小、更灵活的企业。 | synonyms: break up, divest, dismantle, separate, unbundle | antonyms: merge, consolidate, combine, amalgamate | verb
deconglomerate:/ˌdiːkənˈɡlɒmərət/ | n. |  分解后的公司；| A company that has been separated from a larger conglomerate. | Each deconglomerate focused on a specific market segment. 每个分解后的公司都专注于特定的细分市场。 | synonyms: spin-off, subsidiary, division | antonyms: conglomerate, parent company | noun
