consumer-friendliness:/kənˈsuːmər ˈfrɛndlɪnɪs/ | n. | 用户友好性；消费者友好度 | The degree to which a product, service, or system is easy to use and understand by consumers.  | This new software boasts improved consumer-friendliness, making it accessible to a wider audience.  这款新软件拥有改进的用户友好性，使其能够被更广泛的受众使用。 | synonyms: user-friendliness, usability, accessibility, convenience | antonyms: user-unfriendliness, inaccessibility, complexity | noun

consumer-friendly:/kənˈsuːmər ˈfrɛndlɪ/ | adj. | 用户友好的；方便用户的；面向消费者的 | Designed to be easy to use and understand by consumers. |  The website is very consumer-friendly, with clear navigation and helpful FAQs.  这个网站非常用户友好，导航清晰，常见问题解答也很有帮助。 | synonyms: user-friendly, accessible, usable, convenient, easy-to-use | antonyms: user-unfriendly, inaccessible, complex, difficult-to-use | adjective


Etymology:

consumer (from Old French *consumir, from Latin consumere "to consume") + friendly (from Old English frēondlīc) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).
