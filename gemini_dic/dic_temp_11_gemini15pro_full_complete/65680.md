evener:/ˈiːvənər/ | n. | 平衡器，均勻器，使平坦的工具 | A device or tool used to make something level or even. |  The farmer used an evener to distribute the weight of the load evenly across the horse's back.  农民用平衡器使马背上的负载重量均匀分布。| synonyms: leveler, grader | antonyms: roughener, unevener | noun

evener:/ˈiːvənər/ | adj. | 更平坦的，更均匀的 (comparative form of "even") | More level or uniform than something else.  | This road is evener than the one we took yesterday. 这条路比我们昨天走的那条更平坦。| synonyms: smoother, flatter | antonyms: rougher, bumpier | adjective
