demolish:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃ/ (US) /dɪˈmɑːlɪʃ/ (UK)| vt.|拆除，摧毁，驳倒|To completely destroy a building, structure, or idea.|例句：The old factory was demolished to make way for a new shopping mall.  旧工厂被拆除，以便为新的购物中心腾出空间。

例句：The lawyer demolished the witness's testimony.  律师驳倒了证人的证词。|近义词：destroy, raze, wreck, dismantle|反义词：build, construct, erect|词性:动词

demolished:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃt/ (US) /dɪˈmɑːlɪʃt/ (UK)| adj.|被拆除的，被摧毁的|Reduced to ruins; destroyed.|例句：The demolished building was once a grand hotel. 这栋被拆毁的建筑曾经是一家豪华酒店。

例句：His reputation was demolished by the scandal. 他的名誉被丑闻摧毁了。|近义词：destroyed, ruined, wrecked|反义词：intact, whole|词性:形容词
