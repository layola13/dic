disimplicate:/dɪsˈɪmplɪkeɪt/
verb
使免受牵连；澄清；证明…的清白
To free from implication or entanglement; to absolve from guilt or responsibility.
The lawyer worked hard to disimplicate his client from the robbery charges. 律师努力为他的客户洗脱抢劫罪名。

近义词:exonerate, absolve, acquit, vindicate, clear
反义词:implicate, incriminate, accuse
