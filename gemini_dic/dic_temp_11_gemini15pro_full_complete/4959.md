alkahest:/ˈælkəhɛst/| n.|万能溶剂;  炼金药|A hypothetical universal solvent, sought by alchemists.  A substance believed to be capable of dissolving any other substance, especially one that alchemists formerly believed could dissolve gold into a potable elixir.|The alchemist dedicated his life to finding the alkahest.  这位炼金术士毕生致力于寻找万能溶剂。|Near synonyms: universal solvent, menstruum|Antonyms: None (as it's a hypothetical substance)|noun
