fandango:/ˌfænˈdæŋɡoʊ/ (US), /fænˈdæŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 活泼的西班牙舞；喧闹；纷扰|A lively, spirited Spanish dance, typically with castanets; a commotion or fuss.|例句：They danced a passionate fandango. 他们跳了一支热情奔放的凡丹戈舞。

例句：There was a real fandango when the news broke. 消息传出后，一片哗然。

|近义词：dance, revelry, commotion, flutter|反义词:calm, quiet|词性:noun
