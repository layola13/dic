library: /ˈlaɪbrəri/ (US), /ˈlaɪbri/ (UK) | n. |图书馆；藏书；书房；文库| A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to. | I often go to the library to study. 我经常去图书馆学习。|  She has a large library in her home. 她家里有一个很大的书房。 | synonyms: archive, atheneum, book repository | antonyms:  bookstore (a place to *buy* books), empty room | noun
