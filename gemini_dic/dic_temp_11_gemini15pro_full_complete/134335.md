noninteger:/ˌnɒnˈɪntɪdʒər/ | n. | 非整数 | A number that is not an integer (a whole number).  It can be a fraction, a decimal, or an irrational number. | Example: Pi (π) is a noninteger.  π是一个非整数。

Example: 1.5 is a noninteger. 1.5是一个非整数。 | 近义词: non-whole number, fraction, decimal | 反义词: integer, whole number | 词性: noun
