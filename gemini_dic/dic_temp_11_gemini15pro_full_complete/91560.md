hyper-personal:/ˌhaɪpərˈpɜːrsənəl/ | adj. | 超个性化的 | Marked by an unusual degree of personalization, often using data and technology to tailor experiences or communications to individual needs and preferences. |  Example: The company uses AI to create hyper-personal marketing campaigns. 这家公司使用人工智能来创建超个性化的营销活动。 | Example:  The app provides a hyper-personal news feed based on your reading history.  这个应用程序根据你的阅读历史提供超个性化的新闻推送。 | 近义词: personalized, customized, tailored, bespoke | 反义词: generic, standardized, mass-produced | 词性: adjective

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, above, beyond
"personal" (Latin): relating to a particular person
