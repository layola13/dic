chatoyment:/ʃæˈtɔɪmənt/| n. |微光; 光彩; 变彩; 变色效应|A changeable luster or color play observed in some gemstones, like tiger's eye, caused by light reflecting from parallel fibrous inclusions or cavities within the stone.|The cat's eye gemstone displayed a mesmerizing chatoyancy. 这颗猫眼宝石展现出迷人的光彩。|近义词：luster, sheen, shimmer, iridescence|反义词：dullness, flatness|词性:noun

词源: From French *chatoyer* "to shine like a cat's eyes," from *chat* "cat" + *oeil* "eye."
