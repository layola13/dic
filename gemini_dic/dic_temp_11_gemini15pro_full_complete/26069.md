bushmen's:/ˈbʊʃmənz/
n.
布须曼人的（东西）
belonging to or associated with the Bushmen (San people) of southern Africa.
The bushmen's paintings depicted animals and hunting scenes. 布须曼人的绘画描绘了动物和狩猎场景。
Bushmen's knowledge of the desert is extensive. 布须曼人对沙漠的了解非常广泛。
Synonym: San people's
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "settlers'" as a contrasting group)
noun (possessive)
