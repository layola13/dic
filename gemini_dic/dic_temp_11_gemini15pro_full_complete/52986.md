dinkiest:/ˈdɪŋkiɪst/
adjective
最小的；最矮小的
smallest, most diminutive
例句：She drives the dinkiest car I've ever seen. 她开着我见过的最小的车。

例句：They lived in the dinkiest little apartment. 他们住在最小的公寓里。

近义词：smallest, tiniest, miniature, minute
反义词：largest, biggest, huge, enormous
词性：adjective

dinky:/ˈdɪŋki/
adjective
小的；矮小的；别致的
small, tiny, and often fashionable or stylish
例句：She owns a dinky little sports car. 她拥有一辆别致的小跑车。

例句：It was a dinky little cafe with only a few tables. 那是一家很小的咖啡馆，只有几张桌子。

近义词：small, tiny, petite, compact, miniature
反义词：large, big, huge, enormous
词性：adjective
