impresses:/ɪmˈprɛsɪz/
|v.|使印象深刻；使铭记|Makes an impression on someone; affects deeply or strongly; causes admiration or respect.
|Her knowledge of the subject impressed me. 她对这个主题的了解给我留下了深刻的印象。
|The teacher impressed the importance of hard work on her students. 老师让学生们铭记努力工作的重要性。
|Synonyms: affects, influences, moves, sways, touches
|Antonyms: disappoints, bores, discourages
|Verb (Third-person singular simple present)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French empresser, from Latin imprimere (to press into, imprint), from in- (in, into, on, upon, against) + premere (to press).
