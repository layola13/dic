oversay:/ˌəʊvərˈseɪ/ /ˌoʊvərˈseɪ/ | v. | 过分强调；夸大其词；言过其实 | To exaggerate or overstate something. | He tends to oversay his accomplishments in interviews.  他在面试中往往夸大其词。

He oversaid the dangers of the trip to dissuade her from going. 他夸大了旅行的危险，劝她不要去。 | 近义词：exaggerate, overstate, embellish | 反义词：understate, downplay | 词性: 动词
