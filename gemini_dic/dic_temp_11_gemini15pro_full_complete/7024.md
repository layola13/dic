androtomous:/ænˈdrɒtəmʌs/
adjective
雄性分裂的，雄性先熟的
(of certain hermaphroditic animals) Having the male reproductive organs mature before the female.
例句：Androtomous species often exhibit sequential hermaphroditism. 雄性先熟的物种通常表现出顺序性雌雄同体。

例句：The androtomous nature of the species allows for increased reproductive success in certain environments.  该物种的雄性先熟特性使其在某些环境中能够提高繁殖成功率。

近义词：protandrous
反义词：protogynous
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀνδρότομος (andrótomos), from ἀνήρ (anḗr, “man”) + τέμνω (témnō, “to cut”).
