clap:/klæp/| n. v.|鼓掌，拍手，啪的一声|A sudden loud noise, as of thunder.  Also, to strike the hands together repeatedly, typically in applause. |The audience clapped enthusiastically after the performance.  演出结束后，观众热情地鼓掌。|Synonyms: applaud, slap, strike |Antonyms: silence, stillness|noun, verb

clap:/klæp/| n. v.|轻拍，拍打|To hit someone lightly, especially in a friendly way. |He clapped me on the back and congratulated me. 他拍拍我的背祝贺我。|Synonyms: pat, tap |Antonyms: punch, hit|noun, verb
