ill-judged:/ˌɪlˈdʒʌdʒd/
adjective
判断错误的；不明智的
Showing or resulting from poor judgment; unwise.
Her ill-judged remarks caused a lot of offense. 她不明智的言论引起了很多人的反感。
Synonyms: unwise, foolish, imprudent, misguided, injudicious, inappropriate, ill-advised, ill-considered
Antonyms: wise, sensible, prudent, judicious, well-advised, well-considered
词性: 形容词
