nonrealistic:/ˌnɒnriːəˈlɪstɪk/ | /ˌnɑːnriːəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|不现实的，不切实际的|Not realistic; not representing things as they are in real life.  Not based on or concerned with reality.|His plans for early retirement were completely nonrealistic given his current savings.|Synonyms: impractical, unrealistic, fanciful, utopian, imaginary, quixotic, far-fetched|Antonyms: realistic, practical, pragmatic, down-to-earth|adjective
