book-in:/bʊk ˈɪn/
verb
登记；预订
The process of registering or reserving a place, typically in a hotel or other accommodation.
We booked in at the hotel and went to our room. 我们在酒店登记入住，然后去了我们的房间。
The receptionist booked us in for a double room. 接待员为我们预订了一间双人房。
check in, register
check out
