in regard to:/ɪn rɪˈɡɑːrd tuː/ /ɪn rɪˈɡɑrd tə/ | prep. |关于；至于|Concerning; with respect to; relating to.  It introduces a topic or subject. |例句：I am writing in regard to your recent application. 我写信是关于您最近的申请。|例句：In regard to your question, the answer is yes. 关于你的问题，答案是肯定的。 |近义词：concerning, regarding, respecting, about, with respect to, relating to|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a preposition, but one might consider "irrespective of" or "disregarding" as expressing an opposite sentiment.)|词性:介词

PHRASE->
No commonly used phrases with "in regard to" exist in the same way that verb phrases do.  It stands alone as a prepositional phrase. However, it's often followed by specific nouns: "in regard to your request," "in regard to the matter at hand," etc.
