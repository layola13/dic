acupressure: /ˈækjʊˌprɛʃər/ | n. | 指压，穴位按摩 | A massage technique that applies pressure to specific points on the body, believed to stimulate the flow of energy and promote healing. |  Example 1: Acupressure can be used to relieve headaches. 指压可以用来缓解头痛。

Example 2:  She learned acupressure to help with her back pain. 她学习指压来帮助缓解背痛。 | 近义词: acupuncture, massage therapy, shiatsu | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps conventional medicine) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From acu- (needle, sharp) + pressure.  Ultimately from Latin *acus (needle) and premere (to press).
