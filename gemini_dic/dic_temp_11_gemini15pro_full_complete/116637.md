matte:/mæt/ | adj. n. | 无光泽的，不光亮的；亚光漆；无光表面 |  (of a surface or colour) dull and flat, without a shine. | The car has a matte black finish. 这辆车有哑光黑色漆面。 | synonyms: dull, flat, non-glossy, non-reflective | antonyms: glossy, shiny, polished | adjective

matte:/mæt/ | n. |  无光表面；亚光照片；无光效果 | A matte surface or finish, especially one produced artificially on a photograph or other picture.  |  The photographer used a matte to soften the image. 摄影师用了一个遮光罩来柔化图像。 | synonyms: dull finish, flat finish, non-glossy finish | antonyms: gloss, sheen, polish | noun
