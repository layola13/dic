exponential:/ˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəl/ | adj. n. | 指数的，幂的；指数，幂 |A mathematical term related to a constant raised to a power.| The growth of the bacteria was exponential.  细菌的生长呈指数级。 |近义词:rapid, accelerating, escalating |反义词:linear, slow, gradual |词性:形容词, 名词

exponentials:/ˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəlz/ | n. | 指数函数，指数 | The plural form of exponential, referring to more than one exponential function or value. |  He was studying the graphs of various exponentials. 他正在研究各种指数函数的图像。 |近义词:powers, exponents|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly logarithms, considering inverse relationship) | 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exponentialis*, from exponent- (stem of exponens), present participle of exponere "to set forth, explain."
