bozzoli: /bɒtˈsɔːli/ | n. | cocoons; silkworm cocoons |  The plural form of the Italian word "bozzolo," meaning "cocoon." Refers to the silken cases spun by silkworms to protect themselves during pupation. | The silk farmer carefully collected the bozzoli. 养蚕人小心翼翼地收集蚕茧。 |  cocoons, chrysalides |  None | noun
