jaunt:/dʒɔːnt/ | vi. |  进行短途旅行，远足 | To make a short pleasure trip. | They decided to jaunt over to the neighboring town for lunch. 他们决定去邻镇吃午饭。 | 近义词: travel, journey, tour | 反义词: stay, remain | 词性: 动词

词源:  From obsolete French *jancer "to play tricks, deceive," of uncertain origin.
