conceptualizable:/kənˌsɛptʃuəˈlaɪzəbl/
adj.
可以概念化的，可以形成概念的
able to be formed or expressed as a concept; capable of being visualized or imagined.
例句：Is happiness truly conceptualizable?  幸福真的可以被概念化吗？

例句：The abstract idea is difficult to conceptualizable for the average person.  这个抽象的概念对普通人来说很难理解。

近义词：conceivable, imaginable, thinkable
反义词：inconceivable, unimaginable, unthinkable
词性: adjective
