flavin:/ˈfleɪvɪn/| n.|黄素，核黄素|A yellowish pigment found in many enzymes, especially important in oxidation reactions in cells.  It acts as a coenzyme for various redox reactions.|Riboflavin, also known as vitamin B2, is a flavin. 核黄素，也称为维生素B2，是一种黄素。|Flavin nucleotides play a crucial role in metabolism. 黄素核苷酸在新陈代谢中起着至关重要的作用。|synonyms: riboflavin, isoalloxazine|antonyms: None|noun
