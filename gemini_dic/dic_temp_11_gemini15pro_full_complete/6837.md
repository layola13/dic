anathema: /əˈnæθəmə/ | n. | 诅咒，令人厌恶的事物 | Something that is intensely disliked or loathed; a formal curse or condemnation issued by a religious authority. |  His political views were anathema to me. 他的政治观点让我深恶痛绝。 | The Pope pronounced anathema against the heretics. 教皇宣布对异教徒施以诅咒。 | synonyms: abomination, curse, execration, bane | antonyms: blessing, benediction | noun
