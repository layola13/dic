historicize:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsaɪz/
verb
赋予历史意义；将…置于历史背景下；从历史角度分析
To place (something) in a historical context, especially by explaining the historical circumstances that led to its creation or existence, or to interpret something with reference to its historical context.
The author historicizes the events of the novel by describing the social and political conditions of the time.  作者通过描述当时的社会和政治状况，赋予了小说中的事件以历史意义。

Historians often historicize documents to understand their significance. 历史学家经常将文献置于历史背景下进行分析，以理解其重要性。

近义词: contextualize, interpret, analyze
反义词: decontextualize
