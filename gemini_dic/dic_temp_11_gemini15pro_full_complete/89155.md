homelier:/ˈhoʊmliər/ (US) /ˈhəʊmliər/ (UK)| adj.|更朴素的，更家常的，更不好看的|Plain or unattractive in appearance; simple and unpretentious.|例句：Her clothes were homelier than usual today. 她今天的衣服比平常更朴素。|近义词：plain, simple, unattractive, unpretentious|反义词：beautiful, attractive, elegant, sophisticated|词性:形容词

homelier:/ˈhoʊmliər/ (US) /ˈhəʊmliər/ (UK)| adj.|更像家的，更舒适的|Suggesting home life; cozy and comfortable.|例句：The atmosphere of the small inn was homelier than that of the grand hotel.  这家小旅馆的氛围比那家大酒店更温馨。|近义词：cozy, comfortable, homey, familiar|反义词：uncomfortable, unfamiliar, foreign|词性:形容词
