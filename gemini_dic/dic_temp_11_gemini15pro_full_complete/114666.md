malicho:/məˈlitʃoʊ/ /məˈliːtʃəʊ/
noun
mischief, trouble, wrongdoing
A Spanish word meaning "mischief," "trouble," or "wrongdoing." It often implies a playful or mischievous kind of trouble, rather than something serious or malicious.
例句：There's some malicho afoot.  有些恶作剧正在酝酿。

例句：The children were full of malicho that day. 那天孩子们充满了调皮捣蛋。
近义词：mischief, prank, trouble, wrongdoing, deviltry, shenanigans
反义词：goodness, virtue, obedience
词性:n.
