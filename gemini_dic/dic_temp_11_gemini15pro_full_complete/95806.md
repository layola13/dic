inconvincibly:/ˌɪnkənˈvɪnsɪbli/
adjective
不可战胜地；无可辩驳地
in a way that cannot be convincingly disputed or overcome.
His arguments were presented so inconvincibly that no one could disagree. 他的论点提得如此无可辩驳，以至于没有人不同意。
近义词:irrefutably, undeniably, indisputably
反义词: disputably, questionably
词性:adjective
