needleworks:/ˈniːdlˌwɜːrks/
noun
针线活，刺绣品
The art or practice of sewing, knitting, crocheting, or other such crafts, especially for decorative purposes. Also refers to the finished products of such crafts.
Her grandmother's needleworks adorned the walls of the living room. 她祖母的针线活装饰着客厅的墙壁。
embroidery, sewing, knitting, crocheting
