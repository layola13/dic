fountful: /ˈfaʊntfʊl/
adjective
充满泉水的；丰富的；源源不断的
full of fountains; abundant; copious
The fountful garden was a haven of peace and tranquility.  这个充满泉水的花园是和平与宁静的避风港。
The speaker was fountful of ideas. 演讲者充满了想法。
近义词: abundant, plentiful, copious, profuse
反义词: scarce, meager, deficient, barren
词性: adjective

词源:  "Fountful" combines "fount" (a source or spring, from the Old French "font" and ultimately the Latin "fons") with the suffix "-ful" (meaning "full of" or "characterized by").
