grouch:/ɡraʊtʃ/| n. vi. |抱怨，牢骚；脾气坏的人|A person who often complains or is bad-tempered.  A feeling of discontent or ill-humor.|例句：He's always been a grouch. 他一直都是个爱抱怨的人。

He woke up in a grouch. 他醒来时心情不好。|近义词：grumbler, complainer, curmudgeon|反义词：optimist, cheerful person|n.

grouch:/ɡraʊtʃ/| vi. |抱怨，发牢骚|To complain or grumble.|例句：He's always grouching about something. 他总是抱怨一些事情。

Stop grouching and get on with the job. 别抱怨了，继续工作吧。|近义词：grumble, complain, moan|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|v.
