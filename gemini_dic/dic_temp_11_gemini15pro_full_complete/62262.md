encyclical:/ɪnˈsɪklɪk(ə)l/ |adj. n.|通谕的，教皇通谕；|A papal letter sent to all bishops of the Roman Catholic Church. |The Pope issued an encyclical on the environment. 教皇发布了一份关于环境的通谕。|近义词：bull, decree, edict, letter, missive|反义词: None|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *encyclicus, from Ancient Greek ἐγκύκλιος (enkýklios, “circular, general”), from ἐν (en, “in, on”) + κύκλος (kýklos, “circle”).
