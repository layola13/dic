intrigue:/ɪnˈtriːɡ/ | n. | 阴谋，诡计；密谋；(常作复数) 政治阴谋 | a secret plan to do something, especially something that will harm another person |  The book is full of intrigue and betrayal. 这本书充满了阴谋和背叛。 | synonyms: conspiracy, plot, scheme, machination | antonyms: honesty, openness | noun

intrigue:/ɪnˈtriːɡ/ | vt. | 激起…的兴趣；引发…的好奇心 | to interest someone a lot, especially by being strange, unusual, or mysterious | The mystery surrounding her disappearance intrigued the public. 她失踪的谜团引起了公众的兴趣。 | synonyms: fascinate, captivate, interest, attract | antonyms: bore, repel | verb
