ligament:/ˈlɪɡəmənt/| n. |韧带|A short band of tough, flexible, fibrous connective tissue that connects two bones or cartilages or holds together a joint.|The doctor examined the ligament damage in his knee. 医生检查了他膝盖的韧带损伤。|Synonym:sinew, tendon|Antonym:bone|noun

ligament:/ˈlɪɡəmənt/| n. |纽带，联系|Something that unites or connects people or things.|Trade is an important ligament between the two countries. 贸易是两国之间重要的纽带。|Synonym:bond, tie, connection|Antonym:division, separation|noun
