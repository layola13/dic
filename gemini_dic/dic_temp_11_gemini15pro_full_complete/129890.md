non-intuitive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtʃuːɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/| adj.|不直观的，反直觉的|Not easily understood or operated without explicit instruction or study; not obvious.  Something that doesn't follow from what you would expect based on your existing knowledge or experience.|The user interface is non-intuitive and difficult to navigate. 用户界面不直观，难以操作。|Synonyms: counterintuitive, unintuitive, illogical, unreasonable, abstruse|Antonyms: intuitive, obvious, clear, straightforward|adjective
