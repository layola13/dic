murmurers:/ˈmɜːrmərərz/ (US), /ˈmɜːmərəz/ (UK)| n. |低语者，咕哝者|People who murmur; those who complain in a low, indistinct voice.|The murmurers in the crowd grew louder as the speaker continued.  人群中的低语者随着演讲者的继续而声音越来越大。|Example: The murmurers in the back of the room disrupted the lecture.  教室后面的低语者扰乱了课堂秩序。|Synonyms: whisperers, grumblers, complainers|Antonyms: shouters, proclaimers|Noun
