anguine:/ˈsæŋɡwɪn/| adj.|乐观的，充满希望的|Optimistic or hopeful, especially in a difficult situation.|例句：He remained sanguine about the prospects for peace. 他对和平的前景保持乐观。|例句：Despite the challenges, she had an sanguine attitude. 尽管面临挑战，她仍然保持乐观的态度。|近义词：optimistic, cheerful, hopeful, positive|反义词：pessimistic, gloomy, despondent, despairing|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *sanguineus “of blood, blood-red,” from Latin sanguis “blood,” of uncertain origin.  The original meaning referred to someone having a ruddy complexion, believed to indicate a healthy, cheerful temperament. This sense gradually evolved into the modern meaning of "optimistic."
