overharassing: /ˌoʊvərˈhærəsɪŋ/ /ˌəʊvəˈhærəsɪŋ/
verb
过度骚扰
Subjecting someone to excessive or persistent harassment.
The company was accused of overharassing its employees with constant emails and phone calls. 这家公司被指控通过不断的电子邮件和电话过度骚扰其员工。
He felt he was being overharassed by the police, who stopped him several times a week. 他觉得他被警察过度骚扰了，警察每周都会拦住他好几次。
近义词: persecuting, hounding, badgering
反义词: supporting, encouraging
词性: verb (动词)
