fabulists:/ˈfæbjəlɪsts/
noun
寓言家；说谎者
A person who writes or tells fables; a liar, especially a habitual liar.
The old sailor was known among his crew as a great fabulist, spinning yarns of mythical creatures and daring adventures. 老水手在他的船员中被称为伟大的寓言家，他讲述着神话生物和大胆冒险的故事。

Aesop is probably the most famous fabulist of all time. 伊索可能是史上最著名的寓言家。

Synonyms: liar, storyteller, prevaricator, fibber, inventor
Antonyms: truth-teller, honest person
