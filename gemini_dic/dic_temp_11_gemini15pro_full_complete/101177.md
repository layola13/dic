invigorates:/ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪts/| v. |使精力充沛，使活跃，使充满活力|To give strength and energy to.|The cold shower invigorates me in the morning. 早晨的冷水澡让我精神焕发。|近义词：energizes, stimulates, refreshes, revitalizes|反义词: weakens, exhausts, drains, enervates|词性:动词
词源: From Late Latin *invigoratus, past participle of invigorare "to give vigor to," from in- "in" + vigor "vigor."
