holistic:/həˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|整体的，全面的|Characterized by the belief that the parts of something are intimately interconnected and explicable only by reference to the whole.|例句：Holistic medicine attempts to treat both the mind and the body.  整体医学试图治疗身心两方面。

例句：We need a more holistic approach to education. 我们需要一种更全面的教育方法。|近义词：comprehensive, integrated, whole|反义词：partial, fragmented, segmented|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *holos* "whole"  +  "-istic" (suffix forming adjectives).
