ligula:/ˈlɪɡjʊlə/| n.|舌状花, 小舌|A strap-shaped structure, particularly a small outgrowth at the junction of the leaf sheath and blade in grasses, or a similar structure in other plants; also, a strap-shaped extension of the corolla in certain flowers.|The ligula of the ryegrass was clearly visible.|Synonyms: ray floret, strap, tongue|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly structures like petals or sepals depending on context)|noun
