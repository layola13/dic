nonamortizable:/ˌnɒnˌæmərˈtaɪzəbl/
adjective
不可摊销的
Not able to be amortized; not subject to amortization.  This refers to assets that are not depreciated or amortized over time, such as land.
The company's land holdings are considered nonamortizable assets.  该公司持有的土地被认为是不可摊销的资产。
Government grants are often treated as nonamortizable. 政府拨款通常被视为不可摊销的。
Synonym: nondepreciable
Antonym: amortizable, depreciable
