hithermost:/ˈhɪðəməʊst/ (UK) /ˈhɪðərˌmoʊst/ (US)| adj.|最近的；最靠近这边的| Situated nearest to this place; closest.|例句：The hithermost point of the island was marked by a lighthouse.  岛上最近的一点有一座灯塔。

例句：The travelers rested at the hithermost edge of the forest.  旅行者们在森林最近的边缘休息。|近义词：nearest, closest, adjacent|反义词：farthest, furthest, remotest|词性：形容词

词源:
From Old English *hidermost, a superlative formed from hider "hither."  来自古英语 *hidermost，由 hider “到这边” 构成最高级。
