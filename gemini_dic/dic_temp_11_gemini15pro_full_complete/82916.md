gummers:/ˈɡʌmərz/
n.
牙医用磨牙器; 磨牙的人或物; (尤指)磨牙的工人; 修整工具; 
Tools or people that sharpen or smooth, especially tools used by dentists to sharpen dental instruments or workers who sharpen tools.
The dentist asked his assistant to pass him the gummers. 牙医让他的助手把磨牙器递给他。
The old gummers carefully sharpened the blades of the scythe. 老磨刀匠小心翼翼地磨着镰刀的刀片。
synonyms: sharpeners, grinders, honing tools
antonyms: dullers
noun
