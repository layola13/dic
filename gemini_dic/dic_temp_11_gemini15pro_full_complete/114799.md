malvaceous:/ˌmælˈveɪʃəs/
adjective
锦葵科的; 属于锦葵科的
Relating to or belonging to the mallow family (Malvaceae), characterized by flowers with five petals and numerous stamens fused into a column.
The okra plant is malvaceous. 秋葵是锦葵科植物。
Synonyms: mallow-like
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "non-malvaceous" or names of plants from other families)
词性: 形容词
