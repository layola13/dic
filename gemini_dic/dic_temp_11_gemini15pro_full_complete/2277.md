adenographic: /ˌædɪnəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |腺体描记的| Relating to or involving adenography (the radiographic visualization of glands, especially lymph glands). |  Adenographic images helped the doctors pinpoint the location of the affected lymph nodes. 腺体描记图像帮助医生精确定位了受影响的淋巴结的位置。 |  近义词:  lymphangiographic (specifically for lymphatic glands) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "adeno-" (from Greek *adḗn* meaning "gland") + "-graphic" (from Greek *graphikos* meaning "of or for writing or drawing").
