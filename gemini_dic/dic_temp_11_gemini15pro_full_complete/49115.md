deinternationalize:/diːɪntɜːrnæʃənəlaɪz/
verb
使非国际化; 去除国际化
To reduce the international character or involvement of something; to make something less international.
The new government is trying to deinternationalize the economy. 新政府正试图减少经济的国际化程度。
The company deinternationalized its operations after the financial crisis. 金融危机后，该公司减少了其业务的国际化程度。
Near Synonyms: localize, nationalize, domesticate
Near Antonyms: internationalize, globalize
词性: 动词
