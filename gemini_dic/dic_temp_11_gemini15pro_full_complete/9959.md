aphesis:/ˈæfɪsɪs/| n.|省略，词首母音省略|The word "lone" is an aphesis of "alone."  单词 "lone" 是 "alone" 的省略形式。| An example of aphesis is 'scuse me for excuse me.  一个省略的例子是 'scuse me 代替 excuse me。| 近义词: elision, abbreviation, contraction | 反义词: prosthesis, addition | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ἀφαίρεσις (aphaíresis, “a taking away”), from ἀφαιρέω (aphairéō, “I take away”), from ἀπό (apó, “from, away from”) + αἱρέω (hairéō, “I take”).
