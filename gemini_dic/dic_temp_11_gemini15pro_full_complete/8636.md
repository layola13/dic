anticompetitively:/ˌæntikəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli/
| adv. |反竞争地，非竞争性地|In a manner that reduces or eliminates competition, often unfairly.|The company acted anticompetitively by buying out all of its rivals.  该公司通过收购所有竞争对手来进行反竞争行为。|Example:  They priced their products anticompetitively to drive smaller businesses out of the market.  他们以反竞争的价格出售产品，以将小型企业赶出市场。|Synonyms: monopolistically, restrictively, unfairly|Antonyms: competitively, fairly|Word Type: Adverb

anticompetitive:/ˌæntikəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/
| adj. |反竞争的，非竞争性的|Tending to reduce or eliminate competition, often unfairly.|Anticompetitive practices are harmful to consumers. 反竞争行为对消费者有害。|Example: The merger was blocked due to anticompetitive concerns. 由于反竞争的担忧，合并被阻止了。|Synonyms: monopolistic, restrictive|Antonyms: competitive, pro-competitive|Word Type: Adjective


Etymology:
From anti- (against) + competitive (relating to competition) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "compete" comes from the Latin competere, meaning "to strive together."
