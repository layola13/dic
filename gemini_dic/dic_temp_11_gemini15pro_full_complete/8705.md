anticritical:/ˌæntiˈkrɪtɪkəl/
adjective
反批评的；反对批评的
opposed to or hostile towards criticism; disliking or avoiding criticism.
His anticritical stance made it difficult to have an open discussion about the project's flaws.  他反对批评的立场使得公开讨论该项目的缺陷变得困难。
The artist's anticritical response to the reviews showed a lack of professionalism.  这位艺术家对评论的反批评回应表明他缺乏专业精神。
近义词: defensive, uncritical, averse to criticism
反义词: critical, receptive to criticism
词性: adjective
