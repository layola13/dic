entrants:/ˈɛntrənts/| n.|参赛者，新成员|People or groups entering a competition or organization are called entrants.  The entrants for the marathon were lined up at the starting gate.|例句：Many entrants in the competition were first-time novelists. 许多参赛者都是第一次写小说的作家。

例句：New entrants to the workforce often lack experience.  新进入劳动力市场的人通常缺乏经验。|近义词：participants, competitors, candidates, newcomers, initiates|反义词:incumbents, veterans|Noun
