frilly:/ˈfrɪli/| adj.|多褶边的；装饰华丽的；繁琐的|Characterized by many frills or ruffles; elaborately ornamented; fussy.|The little girl wore a frilly pink dress. 小女孩穿着一件粉红色的褶边连衣裙。|近义词：lacy, ruffled, ornate, fussy|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:形容词
frill:/frɪl/| n.|褶边；装饰；不必要的添加物|A strip of gathered or pleated material used as a decoration or trimming; an unnecessary extra feature or embellishment.|The curtains had a delicate lace frill. 窗帘上有一圈精致的蕾丝褶边。|近义词：ruffle, flounce, furbelow, ornament|反义词：plainness, simplicity|词性:名词

词源: From *frillen*, frequentative of Middle English *frillen* "to wrinkle, gather into folds," probably of imitative origin.
