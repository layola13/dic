indoctrinate: /ɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪt/ /ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪt/
verb
教导，灌输
to teach someone to fully accept the ideas, opinions, and beliefs of a particular group and to not consider other ideas, opinions, and beliefs
例句：
The army indoctrinates its soldiers to obey orders without question.
军队向士兵灌输绝对服从命令的思想。

Children were indoctrinated from an early age with the ideals of communism.
孩子们从小就被灌输共产主义理想。

近义词: brainwash, propagandize, instill, inculcate
反义词: enlighten, educate (in a broader sense), freethink
词性: verb
