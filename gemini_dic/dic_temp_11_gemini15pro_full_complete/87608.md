heterozygote: /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡoʊt/ (US) /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡɒt/ (UK)| n.|杂合子|An organism that has two different alleles at a particular gene locus on homologous chromosomes.|The heterozygote for eye color might have one allele for brown eyes and one for blue eyes.|近义词: hybrid|反义词: homozygote|词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *héteros* "other" + *zygōtós* "yoked."
