isodynamous:/ˌaɪsəʊdaɪˈnæməs/
adjective
等力的，等磁力的
Having equal force or intensity, especially of magnetism.
The two magnets are isodynamous, exerting the same magnetic force. 这两块磁铁等力，施加相同的磁力。
Isodynamous lines on a map connect points of equal magnetic intensity. 地图上的等磁力线连接磁场强度相等的点。
近义词: equipotent, equivalent
反义词: anisotropic (各向异性的)
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *isos* "equal" + *dynamis* "power, force".
