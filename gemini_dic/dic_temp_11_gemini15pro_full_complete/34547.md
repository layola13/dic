clear-cutnesses:/ˌklɪərˈkʌtnɪsɪz/
|n.|清晰度，明确性|The state or quality of being clear-cut; definiteness; precision.|The clear-cutnesses of the boundaries between the properties prevented any disputes.  这些房产之间界限的清晰度防止了任何争议。|Clarity, definiteness, precision, distinctness, sharpness|Ambiguity, vagueness, obscurity, indistinctness, fuzziness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From clear-cut + -ness.  Clear-cut itself comes from clear (free from obscurity) + cut (sharply defined). The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
