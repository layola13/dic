词源：
"Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"Destructive" comes from the Latin "destructus," the past participle of "destruere," which means "to pull down," "demolish," "destroy."
"-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
