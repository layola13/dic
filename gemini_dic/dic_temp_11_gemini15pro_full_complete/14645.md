avoucher:/əˈvaʊtʃər/ | n. | 代金券；凭证；收据 | A document that can be exchanged for goods or services. |  I have a gift avoucher for a free meal at that restaurant. 我在那家餐厅有一张免费餐券。

| Example: The travel agent gave me an avoucher for my hotel room. 旅行社给了我一张酒店房间的凭证。 | Synonym: coupon, certificate, ticket, warrant | Antonym: bill, invoice | noun
