cowbells:/ˈkaʊˌbɛlz/ | n. |牛铃；牛颈铃|A bell worn around the neck of a cow, typically producing a distinctive clanging sound.  It can also refer to a similar-sounding percussion instrument. |The cows wore cowbells so the farmer could locate them. 农场主给奶牛戴上牛铃以便找到它们。| The band used cowbells in their song to create a unique sound. 乐队在他们的歌曲中使用牛铃来创造一种独特的声音。 | synonyms:  bells, chimes | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun
