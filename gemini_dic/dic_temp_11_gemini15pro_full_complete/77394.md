gaussian:/ˈɡaʊsiən/ | adj. n.|高斯的，高斯函数|Relating to or involving the German mathematician Carl Friedrich Gauss or his work, especially in statistics (relating to the normal distribution).  A Gaussian function or a normal distribution.|例句：The gaussian distribution is commonly used in statistics. 高斯分布在统计学中很常用。

例句：Gaussian blur is a common image processing technique. 高斯模糊是一种常见的图像处理技术。|近义词：normal (distribution), bell-shaped (curve)|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:
Named after Carl Friedrich Gauss (1777-1855), a German mathematician.
