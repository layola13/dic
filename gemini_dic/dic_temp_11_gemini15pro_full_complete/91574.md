hyperacoustics:/ˌhaɪpərəˈkuːstɪks/ | n. | 超声学; 超音速空气动力学 |The study of sounds at very high frequencies, typically above the audible range, and the associated aerodynamic phenomena at supersonic speeds.  It often involves the interaction of sound waves with high-speed airflow. |  Hypersonic aircraft development requires a deep understanding of hyperacoustics. 超音速飞机的研制需要对超声学有深入的了解。

The conference explored recent advancements in hyperacoustics and their applications in aerospace engineering.  这次会议探讨了超声学及其在航空航天工程中的应用的最新进展。 | 近义词: ultrasonics, supersonics | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "subsonics" for speeds below the speed of sound) | 词性: noun
