antitropal:/ænˈtɪtrəpəl/
adjective
反向的，倒生的 (指胚珠)
(of an ovule) inverted; turned back 180 degrees on its stalk.
The antitropal ovule is common in flowering plants.  反向胚珠在开花植物中很常见。
The lily has an antitropal ovule arrangement. 百合的胚珠排列是反向的。
近义词: inverted, resupinate
反义词: orthotropous, atropous
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against, opposite) + tropal (turning, relating to a turning), from Greek tropos "a turn".
