lamington:/ˈlæmɪŋtən/| n. |拉明顿蛋糕|A small square of sponge cake coated in chocolate icing and desiccated coconut.|例句：I had a delicious lamington with my afternoon tea. 我下午茶吃了一块美味的拉明顿蛋糕。

例句：Lamingtons are a popular treat in Australia and New Zealand. 拉明顿蛋糕在澳大利亚和新西兰很受欢迎。|近义词：sponge cake, dessert, treat|反义词: None|词性:noun
