dulcoration:/ˌdʌlkəˈreɪʃən/ |n.|甜化，使变甜;  缓和; 减轻|The dulcoration of the medicine made it more palatable for children.  这种药的甜化使它更容易让孩子们接受。|English Explanation: The act of sweetening or making something less harsh or severe.|例句：The baker used honey for the dulcoration of the cake batter.  面包师用蜂蜜来使蛋糕糊变甜。

例句：The diplomat's dulcoration of the harsh terms of the treaty helped to avoid war.  外交官对条约中苛刻条款的缓和帮助避免了战争。

|近义词：sweetening, mitigation, alleviation|反义词：exacerbation, aggravation|词性: noun
