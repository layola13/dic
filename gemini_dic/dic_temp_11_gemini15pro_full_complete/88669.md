hoarsest:/ˈhɔːrsɪst/ (US), /ˈhɔːsɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最嘶哑的|Having the roughest, most grating voice; most hoarse.|例句：His voice was the hoarsest I'd ever heard. 他的声音是我听过的最沙哑的。

例句：After yelling at the game, her voice was hoarsest the following day. 在比赛中大喊大叫后，她的声音在第二天变得最沙哑。|近义词：raspy, gravelly, husky, croaky|反义词：clear, smooth, melodious|词性:形容词
