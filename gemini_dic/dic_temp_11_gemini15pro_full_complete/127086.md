nathula:/ˈnɑːθuːlə/
proper noun|乃堆拉山口|A mountain pass in the Himalayas in East Sikkim district. It connects the Indian state of Sikkim with the Tibet Autonomous Region of China.|The Nathula Pass is one of the highest motorable roads in the world. 乃堆拉山口是世界上最高的机动车道之一。|Nathula Pass is a strategically important location. 乃堆拉山口是一个战略要地。|Synonyms: Nathu La, Nathu-La|Antonyms: N/A|noun
