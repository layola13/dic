markov:/ˈmɑːrkɔːf/ (US), /ˈmɑːkɒf/ (UK)| adj. n.| 马尔可夫 |Relating to or based on a Markov process, a stochastic process in which the future state of the system depends only on the present state and not on the sequence of events that preceded it.  As a noun, it refers to Andrey Markov (1856-1922), a Russian mathematician who developed the concept of Markov processes.|例句：The weather forecast uses a Markov model to predict future conditions. 天气预报使用马尔可夫模型来预测未来的天气状况。

Markov chains are used in various fields like computer science and physics. 马尔可夫链被用于计算机科学和物理学等各个领域。|近义词：stochastic, probabilistic|反义词: deterministic|词性: adjective, noun
