PHRASE->
bone up on:  认真学习，专心钻研  He needs to bone up on his history before the exam. 他需要在考试前认真学习历史。
have a bone to pick with someone:  对某人有意见  I have a bone to pick with you about the way you treated me. 我对你对待我的方式有意见。
make no bones about:  直言不讳，毫不掩饰  She made no bones about her dislike of the new policy. 她毫不掩饰自己对新政策的不满。
close to the bone:  接近事实，触及痛处  His remarks about her weight were a little too close to the bone. 他关于她体重的评论有点太触及痛处了。
work one's fingers to the bone:  拼命工作  She worked her fingers to the bone to provide for her family. 她拼命工作来养家糊口。
