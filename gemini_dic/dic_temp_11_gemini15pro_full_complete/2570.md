admitters:/ədˈmɪtərz/
n.|承认者；准许进入者|People who admit someone or something, especially into a place, group, or institution.|The admitters of the club are very selective.  俱乐部的准许进入者非常挑剔。|synonyms: acceptors, allowers|antonyms: rejectors, deniers|noun
