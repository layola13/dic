Etymology:
From Middle English *gilti, from Old English *gyltig, from Proto-Germanic *kultigaz (“guilty, indebted”), equivalent to guilt +‎ -y. Cognate with West Frisian skuldich (“guilty”), Dutch schuldig (“guilty, indebted”), German schuldig (“guilty, indebted, owing”).
