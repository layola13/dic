outgoingest:/ˌaʊtˈɡoʊɪŋɪst/
|adj.|最外向的|Superlative form of outgoing; most sociable and friendly.|Example: She's the outgoingest person I've ever met. 她是我见过的最外向的人。|Example: He was voted the outgoingest kid in the class. 他被选为班上最外向的孩子。|Synonyms: most gregarious, most sociable, most extroverted|Antonyms: most introverted, most reserved, most shy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From outgoing + -est (superlative suffix).  "Outgoing" itself comes from the verb "go out" with the "-ing" suffix indicating a present participle used adjectivally.
