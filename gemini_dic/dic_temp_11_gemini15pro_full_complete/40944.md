coop:/kuːp/
n.|鸡笼，小屋，牢房|A small cage or enclosure, especially for poultry.|The chickens are kept in a coop. 鸡被关在鸡笼里。
coop:/kuːp/
v.|把…关进笼子，限制|To confine in a small space.|The farmer cooped the chickens up for the night.  农民把鸡关进鸡笼过夜。
coop:/kuːp/
n.|合作社，合作组织|A cooperative business or enterprise.|They buy their groceries from a food coop.  他们从食品合作社购买食品杂货。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *coupe*, *cope*, from Old English *cype* (“basket, cage, coop”), from Proto-Germanic *kupiz* (“curved container”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)keub- (“to bend, curve”). Cognate with Dutch *kuip* (“tub, vat”), German *Kübel* (“tub, bucket”).


SYNONYMS->
cage, pen, enclosure, hutch (for n. related to animals)
jail, prison, cell (for n. meaning "牢房")
cooperative (for n. meaning "合作社")
confine, imprison, restrict (for v.)


ANTONYMS->
free, release, liberate (for v.)
