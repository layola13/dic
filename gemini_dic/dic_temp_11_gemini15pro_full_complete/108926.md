leechery:/ˈliːtʃəri/| n. |好色，荒淫，纵欲|Excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustfulness.|His reputation for lechery made many people uncomfortable. 他的好色名声让很多人感到不舒服。| The king's lechery was legendary. 国王的荒淫是传奇性的。|近义词：lust, debauchery, lasciviousness|反义词：chastity, abstinence, purity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lecherie*, from Old French *lecherie*, from *lechier* ("to lick"), from Frankish *lekkon* ("to lick"), from Proto-Germanic *likōną* ("to lick").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *leigh-* ("to lick").  Related to English *lick*. The original sense was "gluttony", but the meaning shifted to "sexual indulgence" in the Middle Ages.
