monotheistically:/ˌmɒnəʊθiːˈɪstɪkli/ /ˌmɑːnəθiːˈɪstɪkli/| adv.|一神论地，一神教地|In a manner characterized by belief in a single god.|  They worship monotheistically, acknowledging only one supreme deity. 他们一神论地进行崇拜，只承认一个至高神。|  She was raised monotheistically and still holds those beliefs today.  她从小接受一神教教育，至今仍持有这些信仰。|近义词: religiously, piously|反义词: polytheistically, paganly|词性:副词

词源:
From monotheism + -istically. Monotheism is from Greek monos "single, alone" + theos "god".
