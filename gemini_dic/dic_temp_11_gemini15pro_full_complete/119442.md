microlinguistic:/ˌmaɪkroʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/| adj.|微观语言学的|Relating to the study of language as a system of small units, such as sounds, words, and grammar, rather than as a tool for communication or social interaction.|Microlinguistic analysis focuses on the internal structure of language. 微观语言学分析侧重于语言的内部结构。|近义词：grammatical, phonological, lexical|反义词：macrolinguistic|词性:形容词
