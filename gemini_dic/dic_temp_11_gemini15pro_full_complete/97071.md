infernalize:/ɪnˈfɜːrnəlaɪz/ | v. |使...地狱化；使...极度痛苦；内化（负面情绪）| To make something infernal; to subject to hellish or tormenting conditions; to internalize (negative emotions). |  He seemed to infernalize his suffering, turning it inward until it consumed him. 他似乎把自己的痛苦地狱化了，将其转向内心，直到它吞噬了他。| Example: The constant criticism infernalized her self-doubt.  不断的批评使她的自我怀疑加剧。| Synonyms: demonize, torment, internalize | Antonyms: soothe, comfort, externalize | verb
