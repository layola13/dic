epigrammatically:/ˌepɪɡrəˈmætɪkli/
adv.
警句式地，简洁地
In a concise, witty, and pointed manner, often resembling an epigram.
He summed up the situation epigrammatically: "Brevity is the soul of wit."  他用警句概括了情况：“简洁是智慧的灵魂。”
She epigrammatically described the politician as "a man of few words and fewer actions." 她用简洁的语言描述这位政治家：“寡言少行的人。”
近义词: concisely, wittily, pithily, succinctly
反义词: verbosely, tediously, lengthily
词性: 副词
