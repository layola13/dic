goldilocks-planet:/ˈɡoʊldilɑːks ˈplænɪt/| n. |金凤花行星|A planet located within a star's habitable zone, where the temperature is just right - not too hot and not too cold - for liquid water to exist on the surface, thus potentially supporting life.|Scientists are searching for goldilocks planets that could harbor life. 科学家们正在寻找可能孕育生命的金凤花行星。|Near synonyms: habitable planet, Earth-like planet|Near antonyms: uninhabitable planet|noun
