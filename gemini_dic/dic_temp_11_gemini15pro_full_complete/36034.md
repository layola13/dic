codiscoverer:/ˌkoʊdɪˈskʌvərər/| n. |共同发现者|A person who discovers something with another person or group.| Marie Curie and her husband, Pierre, were codiscoverers of radium.  玛丽·居里和她丈夫皮埃尔是镭的共同发现者。|  Rosalind Franklin was a key codiscoverer of the structure of DNA. 罗莎琳德·富兰克林是DNA结构的关键共同发现者。 |collaborator, joint discoverer|  opponent|noun
