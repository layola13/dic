contaminate:/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/
verb
污染，玷污，弄脏
To make something impure or unsuitable by contact with something unclean, bad, etc.
The water was contaminated with chemicals. 水被化学物质污染了。
pollute, taint, infect
purify, cleanse
