as-a-matter-of-fact:/æz ə ˈmætər əv fækt/
adverb|事实上，其实|In reality; actually.  It introduces a statement that contradicts a previous statement, adds confirming information, or emphasizes a point.|I thought he was joking, but as a matter of fact, he was serious.|In fact, actually, indeed|In theory, hypothetically|Adverb
