laudatorily:/ˈlɔːdəˌtɔːrɪli/
adverb
值得称赞地，赞赏地
in a manner deserving praise; commendably
Her performance was laudatorily reviewed by the critics. 她的表演受到了评论家们的高度赞扬。
The project was completed laudatorily, ahead of schedule and under budget.  这个项目提前完成并且低于预算，值得称赞。
近义词: commendably, praiseworthily, admirably
反义词: discreditably, shamefully, ignominiously
词性: adverb

词源:
From Latin *laudātōrius* (“praiseworthy”) + *-ly* (adverbial suffix). *Laudātōrius* is derived from the verb *laudō* (“I praise”).
