highness:/ˈhaɪnɪs/| n.|殿下；高度；高地|A title of respect used when addressing or referring to royalty or other high-ranking people.  It also refers to a state of being high or elevated, either physically or in status.|例句：Your Highness, may I have a word? 殿下，我可以说句话吗？

例句：The highness of the mountain made it difficult to climb. 这座山的高度使得攀登变得困难。|近义词：majesty, excellency, altitude, elevation|反义词：lowness|词性:名词
