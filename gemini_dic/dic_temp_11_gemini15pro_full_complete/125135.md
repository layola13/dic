multi-partyism: /ˈmʌltiˈpɑːrtiɪzəm/ | n. | 多党制 | A system in which multiple political parties have the capacity to gain control of government offices, separately or in coalition. |  India is a classic example of multi-partyism. 印度是多党制的典型例子。| synonyms: multiparty system, pluralistic system | antonyms: one-party system, single-party rule | noun
