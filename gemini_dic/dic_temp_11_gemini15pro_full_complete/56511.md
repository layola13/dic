domesticity:/ˌdoʊmɛˈstɪsɪti/ /ˌdɒmɛˈstɪsɪti/| n. | 家庭生活；家务；家居 | The state or quality of being domestic; life at home; homemaking. |  She found great joy in domesticity, preferring a quiet evening at home to a night out. 她觉得家庭生活乐趣无穷，宁愿在家安静地度过一个晚上，也不愿出去玩。| 近义词：home life, family life, housekeeping | 反义词: worldliness, restlessness | 词性: noun

domesticity:/ˌdoʊmɛˈstɪsɪti/ /ˌdɒmɛˈstɪsɪti/| n. |  驯养（状态）| The state of being tame or domesticated. | The domesticity of the dog makes it a suitable pet. 狗的驯化使它成为合适的宠物。 | 近义词: tameness, domestication | 反义词: wildness, ferality | 词性: noun
