liquidising:/ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪŋ/
v.
液化；将…制成液体
The process involves liquidising the fruit.  这个过程包括将水果液化。
She liquidised the strawberries to make a smoothie. 她把草莓打成糊状做冰沙。

solidify, thicken
