doubtful:/ˈdaʊtfəl/ (US), /ˈdaʊtfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|可疑的，怀疑的，不确定的|Feeling uncertain or unsure about something; not convinced; questionable.  Stemming from the late Middle English *doutful*, from *dout* (doubt) + *-ful*.  Ultimately from Latin *dubitare* "to hesitate, waver."|It is doubtful whether he will come. 他是否会来令人怀疑。|近义词：uncertain, unsure, dubious, questionable|反义词：certain, sure, confident, definite|词性:形容词
