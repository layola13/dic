conceivable:/kənˈsiːvəbl/ (US) /kənˈsiːvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可以想象的，可能的|Capable of being imagined or grasped mentally; possible.|It's conceivable that she might be right.  她可能是对的，这是可以想象的。

It's hardly conceivable that they would do such a thing.  他们会做这种事几乎是不可想象的。

近义词: imaginable, possible, plausible, thinkable|反义词: inconceivable, unimaginable, impossible|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin concevibilis, from concipere ‘conceive’.
