analogousness:/əˈnæləɡəsnəs/| n.|相似性，类比性|The state or quality of being analogous; similarity or comparability.|The analogy between the human heart and a pump is helpful, but the analogousness shouldn't be taken too literally.  人心和泵之间的类比是有帮助的，但这种相似性不应过于字面化。|synonyms: similarity, resemblance, likeness, parallel, comparability|antonyms: difference, dissimilarity, unlikeness, contrast, disparity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From analogous + -ness.  Analogous comes from the Late Latin analogus, meaning "proportional," from the Greek analogos, meaning "according to a ratio."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
