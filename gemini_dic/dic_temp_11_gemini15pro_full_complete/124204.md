moshe:/ˈmoʊʃeɪ/ /ˈmɔːʃeɪ/| n. |摩西|A Hebrew name, meaning "drawn out (of the water)," it is traditionally associated with the biblical figure Moses, who led the Israelites out of slavery in Egypt.  It is a common given name for Jewish males.|My son's name is Moshe. 我的儿子叫摩西。|Synonyms: Moses (English equivalent), Moishe (Yiddish variant)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
