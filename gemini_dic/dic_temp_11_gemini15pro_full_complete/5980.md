ambulatory:/ˈæmbjʊlətɔːri/ (US), /ˈæmbjʊlətri/ (UK)| adj. n.|能步行的，流动的；门诊病人；流动诊所|able to walk; relating to or adapted for walking;  (of medical treatment) not requiring a stay in a hospital.|The patient is now ambulatory and can return home.  这个病人现在可以走动了，可以回家了。|近义词：mobile, walking, peripatetic|反义词：bedridden, immobile, sedentary|词性: adjective, noun

ambulatory:/ˈæmbjʊlətɔːri/ (US), /ˈæmbjʊlətri/ (UK)| n.|门诊病人；流动诊所|a person who is able to walk; an outpatient clinic|The hospital has a new ambulatory for treating minor injuries. 这家医院有一个新的门诊部，用于治疗轻伤。|近义词：outpatient, walker|反义词：inpatient|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ambulatorius, from Latin ambulo (“I walk”) + -torius (forming adjectives).
