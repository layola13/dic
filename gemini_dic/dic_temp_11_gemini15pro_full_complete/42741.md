contour:/ˈkɒntʊə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntʊr/ (US)| n. vt.|轮廓，等高线；勾画轮廓|A line that represents the shape of something, especially one showing the difference in height of land|The artist skillfully drew the contours of the model's face. 艺术家熟练地勾勒出模特脸部的轮廓。|synonyms: outline, profile, shape|antonyms: shapelessness, formlessness|noun, verb

contour:/ˈkɒntʊə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntʊr/ (US)| v. |勾画轮廓|To draw the outline of something|The mountains were contoured against the evening sky.  群山在夜空中勾勒出轮廓。|synonyms: outline, define, delineate|antonyms: blur, obscure|verb
