gingerliness:/ˈdʒɪndʒərlɪnɪs/
n.
谨慎,小心,轻手轻脚
The quality or state of being gingerly; cautiousness; care; delicacy.
She approached the injured bird with gingerliness, afraid to frighten it. 她小心翼翼地靠近那只受伤的小鸟，生怕吓到它。
近义词：carefulness, caution, delicacy, wariness
反义词：recklessness, carelessness, boldness
词性: 名词


gingerly:/ˈdʒɪndʒərlɪ/
adv.
谨慎地；小心地；轻手轻脚地
In a careful or cautious manner.
He gingerly stepped over the broken glass. 他小心翼翼地跨过碎玻璃。
近义词：carefully, cautiously, delicately
反义词：recklessly, carelessly, boldly
词性: 副词


gingerly:/ˈdʒɪndʒərlɪ/
adj.
谨慎的；小心的；轻手轻脚的
Cautious; careful.
He took a gingerly step onto the ice. 他小心翼翼地踏上冰面。
近义词：careful, cautious, delicate
反义词：reckless, careless, bold
词性: 形容词


词源:
From ginger (adj.) + -ly (adv. suffix).  The adjective "ginger" originally meant "made of ginger," then came to mean "sprightly, lively" (perhaps due to the stimulating properties of ginger), and later evolved to mean "cautious, wary" (perhaps because a lively horse needs careful handling).
