lamentableness:/ˌlæmənˈteɪbəlnəs/
n.
悲哀；可悲；令人惋惜
The state or quality of being lamentable; mournfulness.
The lamentableness of the situation was apparent to everyone.  这种情况的悲惨对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
近义词: sadness, sorrow, grief, deplorableness, pitifulness
反义词: joy, happiness, cheerfulness
词性: 名词

词源: From lamentable + -ness.  来自lamentable（令人惋惜的）+ -ness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。  Lamentable itself comes from Old French lamentable, from Latin lamentābilis, from lamentārī “to lament”.
