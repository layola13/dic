dampest:/ˈdæmpɪst/
adjective
最潮湿的
superlative adjective of damp: most damp
The dampest corner of the basement was covered in mildew.  地下室最潮湿的角落覆盖着霉菌。
近义词:wettest, soggiest, muggiest
反义词:driest, aridest
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *damp, from Old English *damp, from Proto-Germanic *dampaz. Cognate with West Frisian damp (“vapor, steam”), Dutch damp (“vapor, steam”), German Dampf (“steam, vapor”), Danish damp (“vapor, steam”). Related to damp.
