formative:/ˈfɔːrmətɪv/ (US) /ˈfɔːmətɪv/ (UK)| adj. n.|形成的，构成要素|Relating to formation or development; a formative element or stage. |The formative years of a child are crucial for their development.  孩子的形成时期对其发展至关重要。|Synonyms: developmental, shaping, influential |Antonyms: destructive, hindering| adjective

formative:/ˈfɔːrmətɪv/ (US) /ˈfɔːmətɪv/ (UK)| n.|构成要素|Something that forms or shapes something else; a formative element. |Experiences during adolescence can be powerful formatives of adult personality.  青春期的经历可以是成年人个性的强大构成要素。|Synonyms: element, component, factor |Antonyms:  product, result| noun
