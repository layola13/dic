chowter:/ˈtʃaʊtər/| v. n.|大声喧哗，喋喋不休；喧闹声，喋喋不休|To talk loudly and rapidly, often in a complaining or nagging way; a loud, rapid, and often complaining or nagging talk.|例句：He chowtered on about the unfairness of it all. 他不停地大声抱怨这一切多么不公平。

例句：The constant chowter from the next room was distracting. 隔壁房间不断传来的喧闹声让人分心。|近义词：chatter, jabber, natter, prate|反义词：whisper, murmur|词性：动词，名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Likely of imitative origin, related to words like "chatter" and "clatter," suggesting a noisy and repetitive sound.
