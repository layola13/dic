metabiological:/ˌmɛtəˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
超生物学的; 后生物的
Going beyond the scope of biology; pertaining to phenomena not explained by current biological principles.  Relating to or concerned with factors outside the traditional boundaries of biology, such as artificial life, synthetic biology, or the search for life beyond Earth.
Metabiological research often explores the potential for life forms based on alternative biochemistries. 超生物学研究经常探索基于替代生物化学的生命形式的潜力。
Scientists are beginning to consider metabiological questions about the nature of life itself. 科学家们开始考虑关于生命本质的超生物学问题。
近义词: postbiological,  transbiological
反义词: biological
词性: adjective
