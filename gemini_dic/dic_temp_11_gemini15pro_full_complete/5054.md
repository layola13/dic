all-powerful:/ɔːlˈpaʊərfəl/
adjective
全能的；万能的；无所不能的
Having unlimited power; able to do anything.
The all-powerful king ruled the land.  全能的国王统治着这片土地。
God is often described as all-powerful. 上帝常被描述为无所不能的。
Synonyms: omnipotent, almighty, supreme
Antonyms: powerless, weak, impotent
词性: 形容词
