overdevoted: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈvoʊtɪd/
adjective
过度投入的，过度 dévoué 的
Showing excessive devotion or dedication, often to the detriment of oneself or others.
He was so overdevoted to his work that he neglected his family. 他过度投入于工作，以至于忽略了他的家人。
She is overdevoted to her cats, spending all her money and time on them. 她对她的猫过度投入，把所有的时间和金钱都花在了它们身上。
近义词:obsessed, fanatical, zealous
反义词:indifferent, apathetic, detached
词性:adjective
