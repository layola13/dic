overpublicizes: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌblɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
过度宣传；过分张扬
To publicize something excessively.  Giving something too much publicity, often to the point of making it seem less important or interesting.
The media overpublicizes the lives of celebrities. 媒体过度宣传名人的生活。
The company overpublicized the new product, and consumers quickly lost interest.  这家公司过度宣传了新产品，消费者很快就失去了兴趣。
synonyms: overhype, overpromote, oversaturate
antonyms: underpublicize, downplay, conceal
词性: 动词
