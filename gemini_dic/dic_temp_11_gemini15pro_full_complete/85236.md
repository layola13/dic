harlotize: /ˈhɑːrlətaɪz/
verb
使...像妓女；使...卖淫; 使...堕落
to make someone behave like a harlot; to corrupt or debase someone morally.
She accused the media of harlotizing young girls by portraying them as sex objects. 她指责媒体将年轻女孩描绘成性对象，使她们变得像妓女。
近义词:  debauch, corrupt, demoralize, pervert
反义词: uplift, elevate, purify, ennoble
词性: verb
