construes:/kənˈstruːz/
verb
解释，理解，分析
interprets (something) in a particular way
The lawyer construed the contract in favor of his client. 律师对合同作出了有利于其客户的解释。
interpret, understand, explain, define, take
misinterpret, misunderstand, misconstrues
v.
