burin:/ˈbjʊərɪn/ | n. | 雕刻刀，凿子 | A steel tool with a sharp, beveled point, used for engraving metal, wood, or stone. |  The engraver used a fine burin to create intricate details on the silver plate. 雕刻家用一把精细的雕刻刀在银盘上刻画出复杂的细节。

| The burin slipped, marring the surface of the woodblock.  雕刻刀滑了一下，损坏了木板的表面。 | 近义词: graver, chisel | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

From Old French *burin*, from *burer* "to bore, pierce," probably of Germanic origin.
