cephalopod:/ˈsɛfələpɒd/ (US) /ˈkɛfələpɒd/ (UK)| n. |头足纲动物|A marine mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, such as an octopus or squid, having tentacles around the head.|例句：Octopuses and squids are cephalopods. 章鱼和鱿鱼是头足类动物。|例句：The giant squid is one of the largest cephalopods.  大王乌贼是最大的头足类动物之一。|近义词：mollusk, invertebrate|反义词: vertebrate |词性: noun
