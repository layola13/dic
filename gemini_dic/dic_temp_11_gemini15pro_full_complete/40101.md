contaminators:/kənˈtæmɪneɪtərz/
|n.|污染者；污染物|People who or things that contaminate something.  Those responsible for introducing harmful substances into an environment or product.|The contaminators were fined heavily for polluting the river. 污染河流的人被处以巨额罚款。|synonyms: polluters, pollutants, defilers|antonyms: purifiers, cleaners|noun


contaminate:/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/
|v.|污染，弄脏|To make something impure or unsuitable by contact with something unclean, bad, etc.|The water supply was contaminated with bacteria.  供水被细菌污染了。|synonyms: pollute, taint, defile|antonyms: purify, cleanse|verb
