dragbar:/ˈdræɡbɑːr/ (US), /ˈdræɡbɑː/ (UK)| n. |拖动条，拖动杆|A visual control element that allows a user to adjust a value or range by dragging it with a mouse or finger.|She adjusted the volume using the dragbar at the bottom of the screen. 她用屏幕底部的拖动条调节音量。|The dragbar on the map allows you to pan around. 地图上的拖动杆可以让你四处平移。|Synonym: slider, handle, control|Antonym: N/A|Noun
