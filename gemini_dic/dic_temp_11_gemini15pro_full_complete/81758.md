greeve:/ɡriːv/| v. |使悲伤，使苦恼|To cause grief or sorrow to; distress.|例句：The loss of his dog deeply greeved him. 他失去爱犬使他悲痛欲绝。|例句：She was greeved by the news of her friend's illness.  她朋友生病的消息使她很伤心。|近义词：sadden, grieve, upset, distress|反义词：comfort, console, gladden|词性：动词

词源: From Middle English *greven, from Old French grever, from Late Latin *grāvō, grāvāre, present active infinitive of grāvis (“heavy”).  Doublet of aggravate.
