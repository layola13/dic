icj:/ˈaɪ siː ʒeɪ/ |abbr.|国际法院 (International Court of Justice)|The ICJ is the principal judicial organ of the United Nations. 国际法院是联合国的主要司法机关。|例句：The ICJ settled the border dispute between the two countries. 国际法院解决了这两个国家之间的边界争端。

例句：The case was referred to the ICJ for arbitration.  该案件被提交国际法院仲裁。

近义词：World Court, international tribunal|反义词: None|词性:abbr. (abbreviation)
