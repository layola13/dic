orchidologist:/ˌɔːrkɪˈdɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |兰花学家|A person who studies orchids.  A specialist in the branch of botany concerned with orchids.|例句：The orchidologist carefully examined the rare orchid specimen. 兰花学家仔细检查了珍稀的兰花标本。

例句：She consulted an orchidologist about the best way to care for her orchids. 她咨询了一位兰花学家关于照顾兰花的最佳方法。|近义词：orchid specialist, botanist (more general)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *orchid* + *-ologist*.  *Orchid* comes from the Greek *orkhis*, meaning "testicle," because of the shape of the root tubers of some orchids. *-ologist* comes from the Greek *-logos*, meaning "study of."
