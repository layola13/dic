detail's: /ˈdiːteɪlz/ | n. | 细节，详情 |  Particulars or facts of something, often not previously known or not yet revealed. |  Could you please provide more detail's about the incident? 你能提供更多关于这起事件的细节吗？| synonyms: specifics, particulars, facts, information | antonyms: generalities, vagueness, summary | noun


Etymology: From Old French *detaillier* "to cut into pieces, divide," from *de-* "apart, down" + *tailler* "to cut." The original sense was "a small part or item," which later evolved to mean "a minute particular or circumstance."
