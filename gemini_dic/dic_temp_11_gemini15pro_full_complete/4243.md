airol:/ˈɛəˌrɒl/ | n. |爱罗尔（一种药物，铋的氧化物和次没食子酸盐的混合物，用于治疗皮肤病）| A medicinal preparation consisting of a mixture of bismuth oxide and subgallate, used in treating skin affections.|  The doctor prescribed airol for her eczema. 医生给她开了爱罗尔治疗湿疹。 | Synonyms: bismuth subgallate, dermatol | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

Etymology->
From French *airol*, a trade name. Ultimately from Latin *aes* (“bronze, copper”), due to bismuth's metallic properties and resemblance to other metals.
