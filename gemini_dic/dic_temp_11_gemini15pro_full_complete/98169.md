inscribers:/ɪnˈskraɪbərz/
noun
题写者；铭刻者；登记者；雕刻者
A person or thing that inscribes something.
The inscribers of the monument carefully chiseled each letter.  纪念碑的题写者仔细地凿刻每一个字母。
inscriber, engraver, carver, writer
eraser

词性: noun
