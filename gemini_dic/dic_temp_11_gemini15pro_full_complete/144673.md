outwrought:/ˌaʊtˈrɔːt/
adjective
过度劳累的；过度紧张的；精疲力竭的
Exhausted and agitated, typically from overwork or mental distress.
She was outwrought after the long and difficult journey. 她经过漫长而艰难的旅程后疲惫不堪。
The constant stress of the job left him feeling outwrought. 工作的持续压力让他感到精疲力竭。
近义词:overwrought, exhausted, stressed, strained, agitated, frantic
反义词:calm, relaxed, composed, serene
词性:adjective

词源:
From Old English *ūt(a)wroht, equivalent to out- + wrought, past participle of work.  Originally meant "elaborately worked, ornamented," later evolving to mean "overworked" and then "overwrought" in the emotional sense.
