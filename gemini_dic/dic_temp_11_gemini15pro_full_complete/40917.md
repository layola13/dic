coolest:/ˈkuːlɪst/ | adj. |最酷的，最冷静的，最棒的 | Superlative form of cool.  Meaning most fashionable, stylish, impressive, excellent, or calm and composed. | That's the coolest car I've ever seen! 那是我见过最酷的车！|  The spy remained coolest under pressure.  这个间谍在压力下保持了极度的冷静。 |Synonyms: best, greatest, most excellent, trendiest, chicest, most stylish, calmest | Antonyms: worst, lamest, uncoolest, most boring, most agitated | adjective

cool:/kuːl/ | adj. n. v. |凉爽的，酷的，冷静的，冷漠的 | As an adjective: moderately cold; fashionable and impressive; calm and composed. As a noun: a moderately low temperature. As a verb: to become or cause to become less hot. |例句：The evening air was cool. 晚上的空气很凉爽。|近义词：(adj.) chilly, stylish, trendy, calm; (v.) chill, refrigerate |反义词：(adj.) warm, unfashionable, agitated; (v.) heat, warm | adjective/noun/verb
