outtravelled:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəld/
verb
比...旅行更多，旅行经历比...丰富
having travelled more extensively than
例句：He has outtravelled most of his friends. 他比他的大多数朋友旅行的都多。

例句：She’s outtravelled everyone in her family. 她的旅行经历比她家里的任何人都丰富。

近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed
反义词：lag behind

词性: verb (past participle)

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "surpassing, exceeding") + "travelled" (past participle of "travel")
