margravate:/ˈmɑːrɡreɪveɪt/| n. |边疆伯爵领地；侯爵领地|The territory ruled by a margrave or margravine.  A historical title and territory, similar to a march or county, typically situated on a border and granted special administrative powers for defense.|The margravate of Brandenburg played a crucial role in German history.|近义词：march, county, principality|反义词: None|词性:noun
