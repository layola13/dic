clerestory:/ˈklɪərstɔːri/ (US), /ˈklɪəstəri/ (UK)| n.|天窗，高侧窗|A high section of wall that contains windows above eye level, admitting light to the central part of a building.|The clerestory windows flooded the nave with light. 天窗的窗户让教堂的中殿充满了光线。|Synonyms: high window, skylight, transom|Antonyms: basement window|noun
