amusingly:/əˈmjuːzɪŋli/| adv.|有趣地，好笑地|In a way that causes laughter or provides entertainment.  Derived from "amuse" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old French "amuser" (to entertain, divert).|She amusingly recounted the story of her disastrous first date. 她有趣地讲述了她灾难性的第一次约会的故事。|He amusingly imitated the teacher's mannerisms. 他滑稽地模仿了老师的举止。|Synonyms: humorously, entertainingly, comically, wittily|Antonyms: seriously, solemnly, gravely, dully|word property:adverb
