orris:/ˈɔːrɪs/ /ˈɑːrɪs/| n.|鸢尾根，鸢尾花的根茎，香根鸢尾|The scent of orris is often used in perfumes. 鸢尾根的香味常用于香水。|The orris root is prized for its fragrance. 鸢尾根因其香味而备受珍视。|近义词:iris root|反义词: (无明确反义词)|词性:noun

词源:
From Middle French *oris, from Old French oriz, from Medieval Latin ireos, from Latin iris, from Ancient Greek ἶρις (îris, “rainbow, iris flower”).
