montgolfier:/mɒnˈɡɒlfiə(r)/ /ˌmɑːntˈɡoʊlfiər/
n.
热气球；蒙哥尔菲尔热气球
A hot-air balloon of the type invented by the Montgolfier brothers.
The Montgolfier brothers were pioneers in hot air ballooning.  蒙哥尔菲尔兄弟是热气球的先驱。
近义词：hot-air balloon, balloon

词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the surname of the Montgolfier brothers, Joseph-Michel and Jacques-Étienne, who invented the hot-air balloon in 1783. The word entered English shortly after their invention.
