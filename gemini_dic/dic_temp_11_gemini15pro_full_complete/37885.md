complainer:/kəmˈpleɪnər/ | n. | 抱怨者，爱抱怨的人 | A person who complains frequently or habitually. |  例句：He's a constant complainer, always finding something to gripe about. 他是一个不停的抱怨者，总是能找到一些事情来抱怨。

例句：The manager was tired of dealing with complainers all day. 经理厌倦了整天与抱怨者打交道。 | 近义词：whiner, griper, grouch | 反义词：praiser, optimist | 词性：名词
