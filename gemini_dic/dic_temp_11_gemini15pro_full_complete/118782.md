注意事项:
Organometallic is often used synonymously with metallorganic, although some sources distinguish between the two based on the nature of the metal-carbon bond.

Some texts use "metalorganic" specifically for compounds where the metal is bonded to an organic ligand through a heteroatom (like oxygen or nitrogen), while "organometallic" is reserved for compounds with a direct metal-carbon bond. However, this distinction is not universally applied.
