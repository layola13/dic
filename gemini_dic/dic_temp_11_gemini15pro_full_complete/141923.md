ordinarier:/ˌɔːrdɪˈnɛəriər/ | adj. | 更普通的，更平常的 (comparative form of ordinary) | More ordinary or usual; of lower rank. |  Example 1:  His performance was ordinarier than usual. 他的表现比平常更普通。

Example 2: The ordinarier soldiers were given the menial tasks.  更普通的士兵被分配去做卑微的任务。 | 近义词: more common, more usual, less remarkable, inferior | 反义词: more unusual, more extraordinary, superior | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *ordinarie*, from Old French *ordinarie*, from Late Latin *ordinarius*, from Latin *ordo* ("order, rank"). The comparative form "-er" is a standard English suffix.
