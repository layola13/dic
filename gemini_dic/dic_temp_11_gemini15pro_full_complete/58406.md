dubitate:/ˈduːbɪteɪt/ /ˈdjuːbɪteɪt/| v. |犹豫，怀疑，迟疑|To be uncertain or hesitant; to waver in opinion or belief. |例句：He dubitates before making important decisions.  他做重要决定前总是犹豫不决。

例句：She dubitated whether to accept the job offer. 她犹豫是否接受这份工作。|近义词：hesitate, waver, vacillate, question, doubt|反义词：believe, trust, accept, decide, be certain|词性：动词
