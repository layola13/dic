lycopodiaceous:/ˌlaɪkəˌpoʊdiˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
石松类的；属于石松科的
of or relating to the Lycopodiaceae, a family of vascular plants, including the club mosses.
例句：The lycopodiaceous plants are ancient and have a unique reproductive cycle. 石松类植物很古老，并且有独特的繁殖周期。

例句：Lycopodiaceous spores were once used in flash photography. 石松孢子曾经用于闪光摄影。

近义词：club-moss
反义词: (No specific antonym exists, as it is a classification)
词性: adjective
