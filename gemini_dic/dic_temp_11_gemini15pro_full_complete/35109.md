cloture:/ˈkloʊtʃər/ (US), /ˈkləʊʒə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 终止辩论；停止讨论（尤指通过投票）|The act or process of ending debate on a proposal and taking a vote.  It is often used to prevent a filibuster.|The Senate invoked cloture to end the debate. 参议院启动了终止辩论程序以结束辩论。|近义词：closure, termination, ending|反义词: continuation, prolongation|词性:名词

cloture:/ˈkloʊtʃər/ (US), /ˈkləʊʒə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 终止辩论；停止讨论（尤指通过投票）|To bring to a close by cloture, especially in a legislative assembly. |The senator moved to cloture the debate.  这位参议员提议终止辩论。|近义词：conclude, terminate, end|反义词: continue, prolong|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *clôture*, from Old French, from clore "to close," from Latin claudere "to shut, close."
