overcarking:/ˌəʊvəˈkɑːkɪŋ/
adjective
过度操劳的，负担过重的
overburdening with work or care; loading with excessive burdens
He collapsed from overcarking. 他因过度操劳而累垮了。
Overcarking himself with work, he neglected his health. 他因工作过度劳累而忽略了健康。
近义词: overworked, overburdened, stressed
反义词: relaxed, rested
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *overcarken, from over- + carken (“to load, burden”), from Old Norse *karkr (“burden, load”).  The verb form "to overcark" is now obsolete.
