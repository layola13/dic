incertain:/ɪnˈsɜːrtn/ /ɪnˈsɜːrtn/| adj.|不确定的，不肯定的，无把握的|Not certain; not definitely known or decided.|例句：The future remains uncertain. 未来仍然不确定。|例句：He felt uncertain about his decision. 他对自己的决定感到不确定。|近义词：unsure, doubtful, dubious, questionable, ambiguous, indeterminate|反义词：certain, sure, definite, confident|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French incertain, from in- ‘not’ + certain.
