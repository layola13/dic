interfoliate:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊliˌeɪt/ | v. |插入；交错；夹入|To insert between leaves or folds.|She interfoliated the tissue paper between the pages of the book to protect them. 她把薄纸夹在书页之间以保护它们。|近义词: interleave, insert, intercalate |反义词: remove, extract |词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *interfoliātus*, past participle of *interfoliāre* ("to put leaves between"), from *inter-* ("between") + *folium* ("leaf").
