ace-out:/eɪs ˈaʊt/ | v. | 淘汰，险胜 | To defeat someone narrowly, often in a competition involving skill or strategy.  It can also mean to perform exceptionally well, effectively eliminating the competition. | He aced out his rival in the final round of the tournament. 他在锦标赛的最后一轮险胜了他的对手。 | Synonyms: edge out, narrowly defeat, outperform, triumph, win | Antonyms: lose, be defeated, underperform | verb

ace-out:/eɪs ˈaʊt/ | n. | 险胜 | A narrow victory. | His ace-out in the final seconds secured the championship for the team.  他在最后几秒的险胜为团队赢得了冠军。 | Synonyms: narrow victory, close win, photo finish | Antonyms: defeat, loss, blowout | noun
