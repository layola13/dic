couscous:/ˈkuːskuːs/
n.
库斯库斯（一种北非食物）
A North African dish of semolina steamed over a stew of meat and vegetables.
Couscous is a staple food throughout the North African cuisines of Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Libya and Mauritania. 库斯库斯是摩洛哥、阿尔及利亚、突尼斯、利比亚和毛里塔尼亚等北非菜系的主食。
My favorite dish is lamb couscous. 我最喜欢的菜是羊肉库斯库斯。
近义词: semolina (粗粒小麦粉)
