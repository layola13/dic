altissimo:/ælˈtɪsɪmoʊ/ | adj. n. | 最高音的；最高音 |  The highest notes of an instrument or voice, especially those above the normal range.  It can also refer to the register itself. Derived from Italian, meaning "very high." | The soprano hit an altissimo C, astonishing the audience. 女高音唱到了最高的C音，震惊了全场。 | synonyms: highest, topmost | antonyms: lowest, deepest | word type: adjective, noun

altissimo:/ælˈtɪsɪmoʊ/ | n. | 最高音 | The highest register of a voice or instrument; a very high note. |  He played a soaring melody in the altissimo register. 他在最高音区演奏了一段高亢的旋律。 | synonyms: high register, upper register | antonyms: low register, lower register | word type: noun
