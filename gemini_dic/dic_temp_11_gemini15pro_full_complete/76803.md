gammoned:/ˈɡæmənd/| adj.|被彻底击败的，输得精光的|Defeated utterly, especially in backgammon (but now used more generally); also, hoaxed or deceived.|例句：He was completely gammoned in the last game of backgammon.  他在最后一场西洋双陆棋比赛中输得精光。

例句：I was gammoned by his sob story and lent him the money. 我被他编造的悲惨故事骗了，把钱借给了他。|近义词：defeated, thrashed, routed, tricked, deceived|反义词：victorious, winning|词性:形容词
词源:From the game of backgammon, where a gammon is a double loss.  源自西洋双陆棋游戏，其中gammon指的是双倍输。
