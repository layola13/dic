autocratrix:/ɔːˈtɒkrətrɪks/
n.
a female autocrat; a woman who rules with absolute power
A woman who governs with absolute authority.
The autocratrix issued a decree banning all public gatherings.  女独裁者发布了禁止所有公共集会的法令。
The iron-fisted autocratrix brooked no dissent.  这位铁腕女独裁者不容忍任何异议。
Synonyms: despot, tyrant, dictator
Antonyms: democrat
