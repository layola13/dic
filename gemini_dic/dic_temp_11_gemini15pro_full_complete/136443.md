nonreverent:/nɒnˈrɛv(ə)r(ə)nt/| adj.|不敬的，不虔诚的|Showing a lack of respect for things that are generally taken seriously.|He made a nonreverent remark about the Pope. 他对教皇说了一句不敬的话。|近义词：irreverent, disrespectful, impious, profane|反义词：reverent, respectful, pious|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + reverent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "reverent" comes from the Latin "revereri" meaning "to stand in awe of, to respect deeply."
