co-determination:/ˌkoʊdɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 共同决策; 劳资共同决定; 劳资共决 | A system of joint workplace decision-making by employees and management.  | Example: Co-determination plays a vital role in German industrial relations.  共同决策在德国的劳资关系中起着至关重要的作用。

Example: The company embraced co-determination to foster a more collaborative environment.  公司采用共同决策来营造更具协作性的环境。 | Synonym: joint decision-making, employee participation, industrial democracy | Antonym: autocracy, management control | noun
