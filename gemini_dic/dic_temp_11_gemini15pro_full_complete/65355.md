eulogium:/juːˈləʊdʒiəm/
n.
赞颂；颂词；悼词
a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, especially a formal eulogy
He delivered a moving eulogium at his father's funeral. 他在父亲的葬礼上致了动人的悼词。
Synonyms: tribute, praise, accolade, panegyric, encomium
Antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure
noun
