lacteally:/lækˈtiːəli/
adverb| 乳汁地；像乳汁地；通过乳糜管| relating to or resembling milk; via the lacteals (lymph vessels that absorb fats from the small intestine). |  Fats are absorbed lacteally into the lymphatic system. | 近义词:  chyliferously | 反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: adverb

lacteal:/ˈlæktiəl/
adjective| 乳汁的；乳白色的；输送乳糜的| relating to or resembling milk; conveying chyle. | The lacteal fluid is absorbed into the lymphatic system. | 近义词: milky, chylous | 反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective

lacteal:/ˈlæktiəl/
noun| 乳糜管| a lymph vessel that conveys chyle. | The lacteals absorb fats from the small intestine. | 近义词: chyle vessel | 反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *lactealis*, from lac ("milk").
