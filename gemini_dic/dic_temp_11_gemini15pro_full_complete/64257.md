equipollently:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒləntli/ | adv. | 等效地，同等地 |  In a way that is equipollent; with equal power or force; equivalently. | The two statements are equipollently true. 这两个陈述等效为真。 | Equivalently, similarly | Differently, unequally | adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *aequipollenter, from Latin aequipollens ("equivalent in power, force, or significance") + -ly.  Aequipollens is formed from aequi- ("equal") + pollens ("powerful").
