conventionary:/kənˈvɛnʃənəri/ /kənˈvɛnʃəneri/
adjective
传统的；惯例的；因循守旧的
Based on or in accordance with convention.  Following traditional or commonly accepted beliefs or practices.
例句：His views on art are rather conventionary.  他对艺术的看法相当传统。

例句：She gave a conventionary speech, lacking any originality. 她发表了一篇毫无新意的、传统的演讲。

近义词：traditional, customary, orthodox, conventional
反义词：unconventional, unorthodox, radical, innovative
词性: adjective
