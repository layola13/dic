electricity:/ɪˌlekˈtrɪsəti/ | n. | 电；电流；电学 | A form of energy resulting from the existence of charged particles (such as electrons or protons), either statically as an accumulation of charge or dynamically as a current. |  Electric cars are becoming increasingly popular. 电动汽车正变得越来越受欢迎。

The electricity went out during the storm.  暴风雨中停电了。 | 近义词: power, current, energy | 反义词: None (Electricity doesn't have a direct opposite.) | 词性: noun
