non-norses:/nɒnˈnɔːrsɪz/ | n. |非北欧人|People who are not of Norse descent.  Specifically, it refers to individuals not originating from Scandinavia, including Denmark, Norway, and Sweden, or related areas like Iceland, the Faroe Islands, and Greenland.  Historically, the term can also refer to people who were not Vikings. |The sagas often describe clashes between the Norse and non-norses.  这些传奇故事经常描述北欧人和非北欧人之间的冲突。|Synonyms: non-Scandinavians, outsiders (in a Norse context)|Antonyms: Norse, Scandinavians|Noun
