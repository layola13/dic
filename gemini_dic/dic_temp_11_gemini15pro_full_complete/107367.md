lamellose:/ˈlæməˌloʊs/| adj.|薄片状的，层状的| Composed of or arranged in thin plates or layers like the gills of a mushroom.|例句：The mushroom had a lamellose cap. 这个蘑菇有一个薄片状的菌盖。|例句：Lamellose structures are common in certain types of fungi. 薄片状结构在某些类型的真菌中很常见。|近义词：laminar, layered, foliated, scaly|反义词:smooth, solid|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *lamellōsus*, from lamella (“thin plate”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
