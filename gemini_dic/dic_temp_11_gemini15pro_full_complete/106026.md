kneader:/ˈniːdər/
n.|揉捏机；揉面机；揉捏者|A machine or person that kneads.
|My grandmother was a skilled kneader of dough.|我的祖母是一位揉面高手。
|近义词：mixer, blender|反义词:|词性:n.

knead:/niːd/
v.|揉捏；按摩|To work dough or clay into a uniform mixture by pressing, folding, and stretching.  Also, to massage.
|She kneaded the dough for ten minutes.|她揉了十分钟的面团。
|He gently kneaded her shoulders.|他轻轻地按摩她的肩膀。
|近义词：massage, work, manipulate|反义词:|词性:v.
