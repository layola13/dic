allusory:/əˈluːsəri/
adjective
虚幻的; 错觉的
creating or tending to create an illusion; deceptive
The allusory image of the oasis shimmered in the desert heat.  海市蜃楼般的绿洲影像在沙漠的酷热中闪烁。
His promises proved to be allusory. 他的承诺被证明是虚幻的。
近义词:illusory, deceptive, misleading, fallacious
反义词:real, genuine, factual, true
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *allūsōrius, from Latin allūsiō (“a playing with, a mocking”), from allūdō (“I play with, mock, allude to”). Equivalent to allude +‎ -sory.  来自晚期拉丁语 *allūsōrius，源自拉丁语 allūsiō（“玩弄，嘲弄”），来自 allūdō（“我玩弄，嘲弄，暗示”）。相当于 allude +‎ -sory。
