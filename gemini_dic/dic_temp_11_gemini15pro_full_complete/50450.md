depreciation:/dɪˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃn/ /dɪˌpriːsiˈeɪʃn/| n.|贬值，折旧|a reduction in the value of an asset over time, due in particular to wear and tear.|The depreciation of the car was calculated at 20% per year. 这辆车的折旧率被计算为每年20%。|synonyms: devaluation, decline, reduction|antonyms: appreciation, increase, rise|noun

depreciation:/dɪˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃn/ /dɪˌpriːsiˈeɪʃn/| n.|贬低，轻视|the act of belittling or disparaging someone or something.|He resented the depreciation of his work by the critics. 他对评论家贬低他的作品感到愤慨。|synonyms: belittlement, disparagement, denigration|antonyms: praise, commendation, appreciation|noun
