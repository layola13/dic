middleware:/ˈmɪdlˌwɛr/| n.|中间件|Software that acts as a bridge between an operating system or database and applications, especially on a network.|Example 1: The middleware allows different software systems to communicate with each other.  这个中间件允许不同的软件系统相互通信。

Example 2:  We use middleware to connect our web server to the database. 我们使用中间件将我们的网络服务器连接到数据库。|近义词：interface, bridge, connector|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
