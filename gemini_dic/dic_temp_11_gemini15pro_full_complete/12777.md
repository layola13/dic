assimilability:/əˌsɪmələˈbɪləti/ | n. |同化性; 可同化性|The ability to be assimilated; the quality of being easy to assimilate.  This can refer to the absorption of nutrients, the integration of immigrants into a society, or the incorporation of new ideas or information.|  The assimilability of immigrants into a new culture depends on various factors. 移民融入新文化的同化性取决于多种因素。| Synonyms: digestibility, integrability, adaptability | Antonyms: indigestibility, incompatibility, resistance | noun

assimilable:/əˈsɪmələbl/ | adj. |可同化的; 可吸收的|Capable of being assimilated; easily absorbed or integrated.|  The food was easily assimilable and provided the necessary nutrients.  这种食物很容易吸收，并提供了必要的营养。| Synonyms: digestible, integrable, adaptable | Antonyms: indigestible, incompatible, resistant | adjective
