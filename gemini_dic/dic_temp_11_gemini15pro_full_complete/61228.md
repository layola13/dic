emaciation:/ɪˌmeɪʃiˈeɪʃn/ /ɪˌmeɪsiˈeɪʃn/| n. |消瘦；憔悴；衰弱|The state of being abnormally thin or weak.|The emaciation of the prisoners was shocking. 囚犯们的消瘦令人震惊。|Synonyms: thinness, leanness, gauntness, boniness,  starvation, atrophy,  wasting,  frailty|Antonyms: obesity, plumpness, stoutness,  robustness, health, vigor|noun

emaciated:/ɪˈmeɪʃiˌeɪtɪd/ /ɪˈmeɪsiˌeɪtɪd/| adj. |消瘦的；憔悴的；衰弱的|Abnormally thin or weak, especially because of illness or a lack of food.|The refugees arrived at the camp looking emaciated and exhausted.  难民们到达营地时，看起来消瘦不堪，精疲力竭。|Synonyms: thin, lean, gaunt, bony,  starved, atrophied,  wasted,  frail|Antonyms: obese, plump, stout,  robust, healthy, vigorous|adjective
