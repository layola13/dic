antedate:/ˌæntiˈdeɪt/ | v.  n. |先于，早于;  提前日期; 先行 |To exist or happen before something else; to give something an earlier date than its actual one. | The church antedates the town by several centuries. 这座教堂比这座城镇早几个世纪。|Synonyms: precede, predate, pre-exist |Antonyms: postdate, follow, succeed |verb

antedate:/ˈæntiˌdeɪt/ | n. |比…早发生的事件; 先例 |Something that existed or happened before something else. |The discovery of this manuscript is an antedate to all previously known works.  这个手稿的发现比所有先前已知的作品都要早。 |Synonyms:  predecessor, forerunner, precursor |Antonyms:  successor, descendant |noun
