gangplank:/ˈɡæŋˌplæŋk/ | n. | 跳板，舷梯 | A movable bridge used for boarding or leaving a ship. |  The pirates ran across the gangplank and onto the ship. 海盗们跑过跳板，上了船。

The passengers disembarked via the gangplank.  乘客们通过跳板下船。 | 近义词: ramp, walkway, bridge | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From *gang-* (Old Norse *gangr* "a going, way, course") + *plank* (Old English *planca* "plank, board").  Essentially, a "walking board" or "way board."
