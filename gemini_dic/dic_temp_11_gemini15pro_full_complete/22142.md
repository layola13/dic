boluses:/ˈboʊləsəz/| n. |丸剂，药丸，大丸药；（供动物吞咽的）团状食物|A bolus is a small, rounded mass of a substance, especially of chewed food at the moment of swallowing.  A bolus can also refer to a single dose of a drug or other substance given all at once.|The veterinarian administered the boluses to the cow.  兽医给牛喂了药丸。
