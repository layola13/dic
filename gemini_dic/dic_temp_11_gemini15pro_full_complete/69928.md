fervidly:/ˈfɜːrvɪdli/ | adv. | 热烈地，热情地 |  In a fervent or passionate manner; with great intensity of feeling. | He fervidly defended his beliefs. 他热烈地捍卫自己的信仰。 | Example: She fervidly argued for the importance of education. 她热烈地论证了教育的重要性。 | Synonyms: passionately, intensely, ardently, zealously | Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently, coldly | Word Type: Adverb

fervid:/ˈfɜːrvɪd/ | adj. | 热情的，热烈的 |  Characterized by intense emotion; passionate. |  He delivered a fervid speech about the need for change. 他发表了一篇充满激情的演讲，讲述了变革的必要性。 | Example: The fans gave a fervid welcome to their team. 球迷们热情地欢迎他们的球队。 | Synonyms: passionate, ardent, zealous, enthusiastic | Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, cold | Word Type: Adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *fervidus*, from fervere "to boil, glow."
