furnacelike:/ˈfɜːrnɪslaɪk/
adjective
像火炉的；极热的
Resembling a furnace, especially in heat.
The desert air was furnacelike, baking the sand to a scorching temperature. 沙漠里的空气像火炉一样，把沙子烤得滚烫。
The small room became furnacelike with all the bodies crowded inside.  这个小房间挤满了人，变得像火炉一样热。
synonyms:fiery, scorching, blazing, burning,infernal
antonyms:freezing, frigid, icy, arctic, glacial
词性:形容词
