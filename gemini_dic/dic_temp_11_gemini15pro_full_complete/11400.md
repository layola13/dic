argentate:/ˈɑːrdʒənˌteɪt/
adjective
镀银的; 银色的
coated with silver; silvery.
The argentate surface of the mirror reflected the candlelight. 镜子镀银的表面反射着烛光。
近义词:silver, silvery, silvered
反义词:golden, gold

词性:adjective
词源:From Latin argentātus, past participle of argentāre (“to silver, plate with silver”), from argentum (“silver”).
