frightful:/ˈfraɪtfəl/| adj.|可怕的，令人恐惧的|Causing fright; terrifying.  Inspiring fear or horror; extremely unpleasant.|The storm was a frightful experience.  这场暴风雨是一次可怕的经历。|Synonyms: terrifying, dreadful, horrible, awful, appalling, ghastly, grim, dire, horrendous|Antonyms: pleasant, delightful, lovely, enjoyable|adjective

frightful:/ˈfraɪtfəl/| adj.|非常的，极度的 (informal)|Extremely large, bad, etc.; used to emphasize something negative. (informal)|It was a frightful mess in the kitchen. 厨房里乱得一塌糊涂。|Synonyms: terrible, awful, dreadful|Antonyms: wonderful, excellent, marvelous|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frightful*, from fright + -ful.  Fright itself comes from Old English *fyrhtu* (fear, terror).
