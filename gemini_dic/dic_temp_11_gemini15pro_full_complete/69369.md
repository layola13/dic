fedity: /fɪˈdɪti/ | n. | 腐臭；污秽；不洁 | The state of being fetid; a fetid quality or condition.  | The fedity of the stagnant water was overwhelming.  停滞不前的水的腐臭味势不可挡。 | 近义词: stench, stink, reek, foulness | 反义词: fragrance, sweetness, freshness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *fēditātem, from Latin fētidus (“stinking”), from *fēteō (“stink”), perhaps ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂- (“to fart”).
