interthreaded:/ˌɪntərˈθrɛdɪd/ | adj. |交织的，穿插的，相互联系的 | woven or threaded together; intricately connected |  The novel's plot is interthreaded with multiple storylines. 这部小说的情节交织着多条故事线。

The lives of the two families were closely interthreaded. 这两个家庭的生活紧密地交织在一起。| 近义词: intertwined, interwoven, interlaced | 反义词: separate, disconnected | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From inter- + threaded, past participle of thread.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "thread" refers to a thin strand of something, like string or yarn.  Therefore, "interthreaded" literally means "threaded between" or "threaded among."
