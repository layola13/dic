exaggerations:/ɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.|夸张；夸大之词；言过其实|statements that represent something as better or worse than it really is.|He told us some incredible exaggerations about his past. 他给我们讲述了一些关于他过去的令人难以置信的夸张故事。|近义词:overstatements, embellishments, hyperboles|反义词:understatements, minimizations|词性:名词

exaggeration:/ɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃən/
|n.|夸张；夸大之词；言过其实|the act of making something seem larger, more important, better, or worse than it really is.|It's an exaggeration to say I'm a great singer. 说我是个很棒的歌手是夸大其词了。|近义词:overstatement, embellishment, hyperbole|反义词:understatement, minimization|词性:名词
