outshop:/ˈaʊtʃɒp/ (UK) /ˈaʊtʃɑːp/ (US)| n.|外店；外包商店；外购|A shop or store not located within the main shopping area of a town or city, or a shop belonging to a factory and selling its products directly to the public. |We bought some discounted clothes at the factory's outshop. 我们在工厂的外购商店买了一些打折的衣服。|Synonym: outlet store, factory shop|Antonym:  main store, flagship store|noun


outshop:/ˈaʊtʃɒp/ (UK) /ˈaʊtʃɑːp/ (US)| v. |外购；从外部供应商处购买|To purchase goods or services from an external supplier rather than producing them internally. |The company decided to outshop its IT support.  这家公司决定外购其IT支持。|Synonym: outsource, contract out|Antonym: insource, produce internally|verb
