immigration: /ˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. |移民，移居入境|The act of coming to live permanently in a foreign country. |  The country saw a wave of immigration after the war. 战后，这个国家经历了一波移民潮。| migration, influx | emigration, departure | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immigrātō, from Latin immigrāre (“to go into, remove to, migrate to”), from in- (“into”) + migrāre (“to migrate”).
