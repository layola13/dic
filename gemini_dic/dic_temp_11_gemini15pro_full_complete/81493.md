graven:/ˈɡreɪvən/| adj.|雕刻的；铭刻的；严肃的|Carved or engraved; (of an expression) serious and solemn. |例句：The inscription was graven in the stone.  铭文被刻在石头上。|例句：His face was graven with lines of worry. 他的脸上刻满了忧虑的皱纹。|近义词：carved, engraved, etched, inscribed, solemn, serious|反义词：smooth, unblemished, cheerful, lighthearted|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *graven, from Old English grafen, past participle of grafan (“to dig, carve, engrave”), from Proto-Germanic *grabaną (“to dig, carve”). Cognate with Dutch graven (“to dig, engrave”), German graben (“to dig”), Swedish grava (“to dig, engrave”).
