bugaboo:/ˈbʌɡəˌbuː/ (US) /ˈbʌɡəbuː/ (UK)| n.|妖怪；吓人的东西；令人烦恼的事物|Something that causes worry or fear; a persistent problem or source of annoyance.|例句：
Finding a parking space in the city is a real bugaboo. 在城市里找停车位真是件麻烦事。

Social anxiety is a major bugaboo for her. 社交焦虑是她的一大困扰。


近义词：problem, worry, difficulty, hassle, nuisance, annoyance|反义词:solution, advantage, benefit|词性:noun
