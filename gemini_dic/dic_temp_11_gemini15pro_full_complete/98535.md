instigation:/ˌɪnstɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n.|煽动，怂恿，教唆|the act of urging someone to do something, especially something bad|例句：The riot was started at the instigation of a small group of agitators.  这场骚乱是在一小撮煽动者的怂恿下开始的。

例句：At his wife's instigation, he finally agreed to see a doctor. 在他妻子的敦促下，他最终同意去看医生。|近义词：incitement, encouragement, goading, prodding|反义词:dissuasion, deterrence, prevention|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin instigātiōn- (stem of instigātiō ), from Latin instigāre ‘incite, urge on,’ from in- ‘in, on’ + stigāre ‘goad.’
