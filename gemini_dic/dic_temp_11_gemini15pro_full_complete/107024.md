lactescence:/lækˈtɛsəns/
n.
乳汁状, 乳汁分泌, 乳白色
The condition of containing or resembling milk; milky appearance.
The milky fluid exuded by some plants, such as the milkweed, when injured.
例句：The lactescence of the sap was a key indicator of the plant species.  这种树液的乳白色是该植物物种的关键指标。

例句：The mushroom's lactescence was a sign of its toxicity.  这种蘑菇的乳汁分泌是其毒性的标志。
近义词：milkiness, lactification
反义词：(None -  lack of lactescence could be described but no single antonym)
词性: noun


lactescent:/lækˈtɛsənt/
adj.
产乳的，乳汁状的，乳白色的
Producing milk or a milky substance; resembling milk.
例句：The lactescent sap of the fig tree can be irritating to the skin. 无花果树的乳白色汁液会刺激皮肤。

例句：The lactescent fluid oozed from the cut stem. 乳白色的液体从切开的茎中渗出。
近义词：milky, lacteal
反义词：(None - absence of the quality could be described but no single antonym)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *lactescent- (stem of lactescens), present participle of lactescere "to turn to milk, begin to give milk," from lac (genitive lactis) "milk" + -escere (inceptive suffix).
