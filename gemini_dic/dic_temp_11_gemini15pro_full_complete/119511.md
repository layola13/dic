microphyte: /ˈmaɪkrəˌfaɪt/| n. |微型植物；微植物|A microscopic plant, especially a microalga or bacterium. |Example 1: Microphytes form the base of the food chain in many aquatic ecosystems. 微型植物在许多水生生态系统中构成食物链的基础。

Example 2:  The researchers studied the various microphytes present in the sample of pond water. 研究人员研究了池塘水样本中存在的各种微型植物。|Synonym: microflora, microorganism |Antonym: macrophyte|noun
