hypertrophying:/haɪˈpɜːrtrəfiɪŋ/
verb|肥大，过度生长|The process of increasing in size, especially of a muscle or organ, due to an increase in the size of its cells.|The athlete's intense training regime was hypertrophying his leg muscles.|近义词:enlarging, expanding, augmenting|反义词:atrophying, shrinking, diminishing|v.
