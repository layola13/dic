fustiest:/ˈfʌstiɪst/| adj.|最闷的；最腐朽的；最过时的|Smelling stale, damp, and stuffy, as a closed-in room might.  Having a stale, old-fashioned, or stuffy quality.  Typically used to describe things, ideas, or attitudes that are outdated and out of touch with modern times.|The library was filled with the fustiest of tomes, their pages brittle with age.  这个图书馆里满是陈腐的巨著，书页都因年代久远而脆了。

The professor's fustiest ideas about economics were challenged by his students.  教授关于经济学最陈腐的观点受到了学生们的挑战。|近义词：stale, musty, stuffy, old-fashioned, outdated, antiquated|反义词：fresh, modern, contemporary, new, innovative|词性:adjective
