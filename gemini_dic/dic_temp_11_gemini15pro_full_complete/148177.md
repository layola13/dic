overset:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛt/| v. | 覆盖；倾覆；推翻；压倒 | to cover completely; to capsize or overturn; to overthrow; to overwhelm | The boat overset in the storm.  船在暴风雨中翻了。| Synonyms: overturn, capsize, overthrow, subvert | Antonyms: right, stabilize | verb

Etymology: From Old English *ofersettan, from ofer (over) + settan (to set).
