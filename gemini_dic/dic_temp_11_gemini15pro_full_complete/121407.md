misimprove:/ˌmɪsɪmˈpruːv/
verb
弄巧成拙；越改越糟；把…改坏了
to make something worse when you try to improve it
He tried to fix the leak but only misimproved the situation. 他试图修理漏水的地方，结果却弄巧成拙，情况更糟了。
近义词: worsen, exacerbate, aggravate
反义词: improve, enhance, ameliorate
词性:v.

词源:
From mis- + improve.  "Mis-" is a prefix of negative or pejorative force, meaning "bad, badly, wrongly," from Old French mes- (Modern French més-), from Latin minus "less." "Improve" comes from the late Middle English: from Anglo-Norman French emprower, based on Latin prodesse ‘be of use’.
