caregiver:/ˈkerɡɪvər/| n. |照顾者，护理员|A person who provides care for a child, an elderly person, or someone who is sick or disabled.|My grandmother's caregiver is very attentive and kind. 我的祖母的护理员非常细心和善良。|The caregiver assists with bathing and dressing.  护理员协助洗澡和穿衣。|近义词：attendant, guardian, nurse|反义词: ward, dependent|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From care + giver.  "Giver" comes from the Old English word "giefan" meaning "to give."
