monotonously:/məˈnɒtənəsli/ /məˈnɑːtənəsli/| adv.|单调地，乏味地|in a monotonous manner; in a way that lacks variation and is boring or tedious.|She spoke monotonously, and I almost fell asleep. 她说话单调乏味，我差点睡着了。|The rain droned monotonously against the windowpane. 雨单调地敲打着窗玻璃。|近义词：tediously, dully, boringly|反义词：interestingly, excitingly, variedly|词性:副词
monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ /ˈmɑːnətoʊn/| n.|单调的声音，单调的讲话|a sound or utterance that does not vary in pitch or tone.|The lecturer's monotone made it difficult to stay awake. 演讲者的单调声音让人很难保持清醒。|The constant monotone of the engine lulled me to sleep.  发动机持续不断的单调声音让我昏昏欲睡。|近义词：drone, chant|反义词：melody, harmony|词性:名词
monotonic:/məˈnɒtənɪk/ /məˈnɑːtənɪk/| adj.|单调的，单一的|varying in one direction only; either continuously increasing or continuously decreasing.|The function is monotonic increasing.  这个函数是单调递增的。|The graph showed a monotonic decline in sales. 图表显示销售额单调下降。|近义词：unchanging, steady, consistent|反义词：fluctuating, varying, oscillating|词性:形容词
