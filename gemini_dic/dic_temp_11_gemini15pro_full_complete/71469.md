flames:/fleɪmz/| n.|火焰，烈火；热情，激情|Hot bright burning gas produced by something on fire. / A strong feeling of love or passion.|The flames danced in the fireplace. 火焰在壁炉里跳动着。|近义词：fire, blaze, inferno, passion, ardor|反义词:calmness, apathy|noun

flames:/fleɪmz/| v. (third-person singular simple present flames, present participle flaming, simple past and past participle flamed)|燃烧；爆发；闪耀|Burn brightly and strongly; suddenly become very angry or excited; send an angry or insulting electronic message to (someone).|The building was engulfed in flames. 这座建筑被火焰吞没了。|近义词：blaze, burn, ignite, flare|反义词:extinguish, quench|verb
