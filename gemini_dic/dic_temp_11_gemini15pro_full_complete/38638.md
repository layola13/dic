concupiscential:/kənˌkjuːpɪˈsɛnʃəl/
adjective
贪欲的；肉欲的；色欲的
Driven by strong sexual desire; lustful. Relating to or characterized by concupiscence.
He gave in to his concupiscential desires. 他屈服于自己的肉欲。
Near Synonyms: lustful, lecherous, carnal, lascivious, sensual, desirous
Antonyms: chaste, pure, virtuous, abstinent
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin concupiscentialis, from concupiscentia (see concupiscence).
