memoirists:/ˈmɛmwɑːrɪsts/
noun
回忆录作者，回忆录作家
A person who writes memoirs; a writer of memoirs.
Many memoirists choose to focus on a specific period of their lives. 许多回忆录作者选择专注于他们生命中的特定时期。
Contemporary memoirists often explore themes of identity and belonging. 当代回忆录作家经常探索身份认同和归属感的主题。
synonyms: autobiographers, chroniclers, writers, authors
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: fiction writers, novelists)
