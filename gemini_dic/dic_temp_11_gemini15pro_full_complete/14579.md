avicula:/əˈvɪkjʊlə/ | n. | 小鸟，小翼 |A small bird; a small wing.  Specifically, a small anatomical process shaped like a small wing. | The avicula of the ear is a small, wing-like projection.  耳朵的小翼是一个小的、翅膀状的突起。| Synonym:  winglet,  earlet (for ear avicula)| Antonym: N/A (anatomical term, no direct opposite) | noun
