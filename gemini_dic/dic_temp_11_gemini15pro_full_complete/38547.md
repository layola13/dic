concluding:/kənˈkluːdɪŋ/
adjective
结束的，结论性的
serving to end something; final
The concluding chapter of the book summarized the main points.  这本书的最后一章总结了要点。

concluding:/kənˈkluːdɪŋ/
verb
结束，推断出
arrive at a judgment or opinion by reasoning; infer
Concluding that she was not interested, he left her alone.  断定她不感兴趣后，他就离开了她。


Etymology:
From Late Latin *concludentem, present participle of concludere "to conclude," from com- "together" + claudere "to close."


Synonyms:
final, last, closing, terminal, ultimate, decisive, definitive
ending, finishing, terminating

Antonyms:
beginning, opening, starting, initial, introductory, preliminary
