gregariousness:/ˌɡrɛˈɡɛəriəsnəs/| n.|合群；群居；社交性|The state or quality of being gregarious; fondness for the company of others.|Her gregariousness made her the life of the party. 她的合群使她成为聚会的焦点。|近义词：sociability, conviviality, affability|反义词：unsociability, aloofness, introversion|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gregarius* "belonging to a flock or herd," from *grex* (genitive *gregis*) "flock, herd."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
