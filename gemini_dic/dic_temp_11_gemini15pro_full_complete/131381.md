noncircumspectly:/ˌnɒnsɜːrkəmˈspɛktli/
| adv. |不谨慎地，轻率地|Without caution or careful consideration; rashly or imprudently. |He acted noncircumspectly and regretted his hasty decision. 他行事不谨慎，后悔自己草率的决定。|She noncircumspectly shared private information on social media. 她轻率地在社交媒体上分享了私人信息。|近义词:recklessly, carelessly, rashly, imprudently, indiscreetly|反义词:carefully, cautiously, prudently, thoughtfully, discreetly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + circumspect (careful, cautious) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Circumspect comes from Latin circumspectus, past participle of circumspicere "to look around," from circum- "around" + specere "to look."
