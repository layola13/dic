dilates:/daɪˈleɪts/ /ˈdaɪˌleɪts/
|v.|膨胀，扩大| (something) becomes wider or larger. |The pupils of the cat's eyes dilated in the darkness. 猫的瞳孔在黑暗中扩大了。|近义词：expand, widen, enlarge, stretch|反义词：contract, constrict, shrink, narrow|词性: 动词
Etymology: From Latin *dīlātāre, frequentative of *dīlātare, from dīlātus, past participle of differre (“to carry apart, scatter, spread, extend”).
