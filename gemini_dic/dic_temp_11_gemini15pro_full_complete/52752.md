dilatabilities:/dɪˌleɪtəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |扩张性，膨胀性|The dilatabilities of different materials vary greatly. 不同材料的膨胀性差异很大。|例句：The dilatabilities of gases are much greater than those of solids. 气体的膨胀性比固体的膨胀性大得多。

例句：The experiment measured the dilatabilities of several types of rubber.  这个实验测量了几种橡胶的膨胀性。

近义词：expandabilities, extensibilities|反义词:incompressibilities, rigidities|词性:noun (plural)

词源: From Latin "dilatare" (to spread out) + "-able" (capable of being) + "-ity" (state or quality).
