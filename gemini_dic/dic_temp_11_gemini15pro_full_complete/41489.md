cornstarch:/ˈkɔːrnstɑːrtʃ/ (US) /ˈkɔːnstɑːtʃ/ (UK)| n. |玉米淀粉|A fine, white, powdery starch made from corn kernels, used for thickening sauces, gravies, puddings, etc.|I used cornstarch to thicken the gravy.|近义词：cornflour (UK), arrowroot, tapioca starch|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Perhaps "wheat flour" as a contrasting thickening agent)|noun
