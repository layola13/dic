co-ordinating:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
协调的，同等的
bringing different elements into a relationship that ensures effective combined action.
例句：She's responsible for co-ordinating the work of all the departments. 她负责协调所有部门的工作。

例句：The co-ordinating committee will meet next week. 协调委员会将于下周召开会议。

近义词：harmonizing, synchronizing, integrating
反义词：disorganizing, conflicting
词性: adjective


co-ordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/
verb
协调，配合
to organize, harmonize, or synchronize effectively.
例句：We need to co-ordinate our efforts to achieve our goals. 我们需要协调我们的努力来实现我们的目标。

例句：The various agencies involved need to co-ordinate their activities.  参与的各个机构需要协调他们的活动。

近义词：harmonize, synchronize, integrate
反义词：disorganize, conflict
词性: verb


co-ordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnət/
noun
坐标
each of a group of numbers used to indicate the position of a point, line, or plane.
例句：The co-ordinates of the point are (3,4).  这个点的坐标是(3,4)。

例句：Please provide the co-ordinates of your current location. 请提供你当前位置的坐标。

近义词：position, location
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


词源: From the Latin word "coordinatus," past participle of "coordinare," meaning "to arrange in order," from "co-" (together) + "ordinare" (to arrange).
