appraisingly: /əˈpreɪzɪŋli/ | adv. | 评估地；估价地；鉴赏地 |  In a manner that assesses the value or quality of something.  It suggests careful and thoughtful consideration. | He looked at the painting appraisingly, considering its composition and brushstrokes. 他鉴赏地看了看这幅画，思考着它的构图和笔触。| 近义词: assessingly, evaluatively, critically, judiciously | 反义词: carelessly, thoughtlessly, indifferently | 词性: 副词

词源: From appraise + -ing + -ly.  Appraise comes from Old French "apreisier"  meaning "to set a price," ultimately from Latin "appretiare" "to value."
