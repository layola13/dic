clown: /klaʊn/ (US), /klaʊn/ (UK)| n. vi.| 小丑；粗鲁笨拙的人|A person who amuses others by ridiculous behavior or appearance, typically wearing comical clothes and makeup and performing exaggerated actions.| The circus clown juggled five balls at once.  马戏团小丑一次同时抛接五个球。|  He acted like a clown throughout the meeting.  他在整个会议期间都像个小丑一样。| synonyms: jester, buffoon, comedian, fool | antonyms: serious person, professional | noun

clown: /klaʊn/ (US), /klaʊn/ (UK)| vi.| 扮小丑；装傻|To behave in a silly or foolish way.| Stop clowning around and get to work!  别再装傻了，快去工作！| He was clowning around with his friends.  他和他的朋友们在一起嬉闹。| synonyms: fool around, play the fool, act silly | antonyms: behave oneself, be serious | verb
