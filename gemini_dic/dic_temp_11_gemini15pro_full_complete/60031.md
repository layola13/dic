educability:/ˌɛdʒʊkəˈbɪlɪti/
n.
可教育性；可教性
The capacity or potential of a person or group to be educated.
Her educability was evident from a young age. 她很小的时候就显现出她的可教育性。
The program aims to improve the educability of disadvantaged children.  这个项目旨在提高弱势儿童的可教育性。
近义词: teachability, trainability, aptitude
反义词: ineducability, unteachability
