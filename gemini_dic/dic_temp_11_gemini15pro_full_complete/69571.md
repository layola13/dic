felonious:/fəˈloʊniəs/
adjective
重罪的；极恶的
Relating to or involved in a felony.  Characterized by or showing criminal intent.
The felonious act of stealing a car.  偷车的重罪行为。
He gave her a felonious look. 他恶狠狠地瞪了她一眼。
近义词:criminal, illegal, unlawful, villainous
反义词:lawful, legal, innocent
词性:adjective
