elix:/ˈɛlɪks/| n.| 灵丹妙药，长生不老药 |A magical or medicinal potion, often sought after for its supposed ability to prolong life or grant immortality.|  The alchemist claimed to have discovered the elix of life.  炼金术士声称发现了长生不老药。 | 近义词: potion, elixir, cure-all | 反义词: poison, toxin | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *elixer*, from Medieval Latin *elixir*, from Arabic *al-ʾiksīr* (الإكسير), from Late Greek *ξερίακόν* (*xēriakón*), "powder for drying wounds," from Greek *ξηρός* (*xērós*), "dry".
