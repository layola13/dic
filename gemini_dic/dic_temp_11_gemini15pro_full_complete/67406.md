externalities:/ˌɛks.tɜːˈnæl.ɪ.tiz/ | n. | 外部性，外部效应 |  A side effect or consequence of an industrial or commercial activity that affects other parties without this being reflected in the cost of the goods or services involved. |  Pollution is a negative externality of many industrial processes. 污染是许多工业生产过程的负外部性。| synonyms: spillover effects, side effects, repercussions, consequences | antonyms: internalities | noun
