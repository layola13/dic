nilly-willy:/ˈnɪli ˈwɪli/ | adv. | 不由自主地；不管愿意不愿意地 | In a way that shows you have no control over your actions and cannot prevent something from happening.  |  She was swept nilly-willy into a new romance. 她不由自主地陷入了一段新的恋情。 |  Synonyms: willy-nilly, perforce, inevitably, of necessity | Antonyms: willingly, voluntarily, intentionally, deliberately | adverb
