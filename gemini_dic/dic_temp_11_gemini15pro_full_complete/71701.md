flavor:/ˈfleɪvər/ (US) /ˈfleɪvə/ (UK)| n. vt.|味道，风味；特色，特点；香料|a particular quality of taste or smell perceived in food or other substances; a distinctive atmosphere or quality|The ice cream comes in a variety of flavors.  这种冰淇淋有多种口味。|She added some vanilla flavor to the cake batter. 她往蛋糕糊里加了些香草香精。|近义词: taste, aroma, savor, spice, character|反义词: blandness, insipidness|词性:noun, verb
