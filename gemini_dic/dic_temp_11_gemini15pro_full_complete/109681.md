leucoryx:/ˌluːkɔːˈraɪks/ | n. | 白羚羊 | A large, heavily built antelope with long, straight horns, native to the Arabian Peninsula and some parts of Africa. |  The leucoryx is an endangered species.  白羚羊是一种濒危物种。

The leucoryx has been reintroduced to several areas where it was previously extinct. 白羚羊已被重新引入到它以前灭绝的几个地区。 | 近义词: Arabian oryx, white oryx | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek λευκόoryξ (leukóryx, “white gazelle or antelope”), from λευκός (leukós, “white”) + ὄρυξ (óryx, “gazelle, antelope”).
