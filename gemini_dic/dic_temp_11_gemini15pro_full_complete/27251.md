calorifically:/ˌkæləˈrɪfɪkli/
adverb
热量地；关于热量的
in a way that relates to the amount of heat something produces.
Brown rice is calorifically dense. 糙米热量密度高。
The food was calorifically inadequate for a growing child.  这种食物的热量对于一个正在成长的孩子来说是不够的。
Near synonyms: thermally,  energetically
Antonyms:  poorly, inadequately
词性: adverb
