outstepping:/ˌaʊtˈstɛpɪŋ/| v. |超越；超出…的限度|Going beyond the limits of what is considered acceptable or appropriate. |  He was reprimanded for outstepping his authority. 他因越权而受到谴责。

The company was accused of outstepping its legal boundaries. 这家公司被指控超越了其法律界限。|近义词：exceeding, overstepping, transgressing|反义词: observing, respecting, complying|词性: 动词 (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + stepping, the present participle of step (v.).  "Out-"  is a prefix indicating exceeding or going beyond. "Step" originally meant to move by lifting and setting down one's foot.  Therefore, "outstepping" literally means stepping outside of or beyond a boundary.
