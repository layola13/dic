nonexcessiveness:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsɛsɪvnəs/
noun
非过度性；适度
The state or quality of not being excessive; moderation.
His nonexcessiveness in eating and drinking contributed to his good health.  他饮食的适度有助于他的健康。
近义词: moderation, temperance, restraint
反义词: excessiveness, immoderation, extravagance
词性: noun

Etymology:  From non- + excessiveness.  "Excessive" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin excessivus, from excedere ‘go beyond’ (see exceed).
