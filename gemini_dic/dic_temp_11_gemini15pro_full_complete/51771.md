devotionalism:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
虔诚；宗教信仰；献身；挚爱
religious fervor; piety; dedication
She approached her studies with a quiet devotionalism. 她以虔诚的态度对待她的学业。

近义词: piety, devoutness, religiousness, spirituality
反义词: irreverence, impiety, apathy, indifference
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From devotional + -ism.  Devotional from devote + -ional. Devote from Latin dēvōtus, past participle of dēvovēre (“to vow, dedicate, consecrate”), from dē- (“down, away”) + vovēre (“to vow”).
