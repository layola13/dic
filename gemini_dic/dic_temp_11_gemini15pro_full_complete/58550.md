duh:/dʌ/
interjection
呃，啊哈，表示显而易见
Expressing that something is obvious or self-evident.
Duh, everyone knows that! 呃，每个人都知道！
Duh, I told you that already. 啊哈，我早就告诉你了。
近义词:obviously, well, no kidding
反义词:no, really?
