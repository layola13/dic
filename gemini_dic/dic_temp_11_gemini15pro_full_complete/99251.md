intercompared:/ˌɪntər.kəmˈpeərd/ | v. |相互比较，互相比较 |To compare (things) with one another. |
The results of the two studies were intercompared. 这两项研究的结果进行了相互比较。|
The performance of different algorithms was intercompared using a benchmark dataset.  不同算法的性能使用基准数据集进行了相互比较。|
近义词: compared, contrasted, juxtaposed |
