clownishness:/ˈklaʊnɪʃnəs/
noun
滑稽；小丑气；弄臣作风
The quality of resembling or behaving like a clown, especially in being foolish or playful.
His clownishness amused the children. 他的滑稽逗乐了孩子们。
Synonyms: buffoonery, silliness, foolery, jestering
Antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gravity, dignity
词性: 名词
