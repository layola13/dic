fall-back-on:/ˌfɔːl ˈbæk ɑːn/| phrasal verb | 依靠，求助于，退而求其次 | to use something, especially a form of money, when other things have failed |  If the business fails, she has her teaching qualifications to fall back on. 如果生意失败了，她还有教师资格可以依靠。 | resort to, rely on, depend on |  None | verb phrase

fall-back-on:/ˌfɔːl ˈbæk ɑːn/| phrasal verb | 应急的，备用的 | used to describe something that can be used if a main plan or system fails | This computer is my fall-back-on if my laptop breaks. 如果我的笔记本电脑坏了，这台电脑就是我的备用电脑。 |  alternative, backup, reserve | None | adjective phrase (attributive)
