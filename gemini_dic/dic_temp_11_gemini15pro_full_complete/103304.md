jiff:/dʒɪf/| n.| 瞬间，片刻，一刹那 |A very short time; a moment. |I'll be back in a jiff. 我马上回来。|近义词：moment, instant, second, trice|反义词:eternity, age|noun


USAGE->

A jiff seems like an eternity when you're waiting for important news.  当你等待重要消息时，片刻就像永恒。
It only takes a jiff to send a text message.  发送短信只需片刻。
