homomorphous:/ˌhɒməˈmɔːfəs/ /ˌhoʊməˈmɔːrfəs/| adj.|同形的，同晶形的|Having the same form or shape; exhibiting homomorphism.  Specifically in crystallography, having the same crystalline form but not necessarily the same chemical composition.|The two minerals are homomorphous, making them difficult to distinguish visually.|近义词：isomorphic, analogous, similar|反义词:heteromorphous, dissimilar|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek "homo-" (same) + "morphē" (form) + "-ous" (adjective suffix).
