breakable:/ˈbreɪkəbl/| adj.|易碎的，脆弱的|Something that is breakable is easy to break. |The plates are breakable, so please handle them with care.  盘子易碎，请小心轻放。|fragile, delicate, brittle|robust, sturdy, durable|adjective
词源: From break (verb) + -able (suffix).  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being". Break comes from Old English *brecan.
