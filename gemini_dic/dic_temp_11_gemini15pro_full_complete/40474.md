contributing:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪŋ/
adjective
贡献的，起作用的
playing a part in bringing something about.
例句：He made a contributing factor to the team's success. 他对团队的成功做出了贡献。

例句：Stress is a contributing factor to many illnesses. 压力是许多疾病的诱因。

近义词：helpful, instrumental, conducive
反义词：detrimental, hindering, obstructive
词性：adjective

contribute:/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/
verb
贡献，捐献；投稿
give (something, especially money) in order to help achieve or provide something.
例句：He contributed a large sum of money to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。

例句：She contributes regularly to the magazine. 她定期为该杂志投稿。

近义词：donate, give, supply
反义词：withdraw, withhold, detract
词性：verb

contribution:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃn/
noun
贡献，捐款；稿件
a gift or payment to a common fund or collection.
例句：His contribution to the project was invaluable. 他对这个项目的贡献是无价的。

例句：Thank you for your generous contribution to the fund. 感谢您对基金的慷慨捐助。

近义词：donation, gift, offering
反义词：deduction, subtraction, withdrawal
词性：noun

词源: From Latin *contribuere, from con- "together" + tribuere "to grant, bestow."
