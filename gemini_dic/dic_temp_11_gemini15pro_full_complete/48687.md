The correct spelling is **defer**.  "Deffer" is not a standard English word.

defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)| v.|推迟，延期，顺从|To postpone something to a later time; to yield to someone's wishes or judgment.|例句：The decision has been deferred until next week.  这个决定被推迟到下周。|近义词：postpone, delay, adjourn|反义词:expedite, hasten|verb

defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)| n.|推迟，延期|The act of postponing or delaying something.|例句：A deferment of payment was granted. 缴费延期被批准了。|近义词：postponement, delay|反义词:advancement|noun (rare usage)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deferrer*, from Latin *dīfferre* ("to carry different ways, separate, scatter; put off, delay"), from *dis-* ("apart") + *ferre* ("to carry").
