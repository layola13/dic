discretionary:/dɪˈskreʃəneri/| adj.|自由决定的；酌情处理的；可自行选择的|Available at the discretion of the manager. 由经理酌情处理。|例句：Managers have discretionary powers to deal with individual cases.  经理们有权酌情处理个别案件。

例句：Judges have wide discretionary powers when it comes to sentencing.  法官在量刑方面有很大的自由裁量权。|近义词：optional, elective, non-obligatory, voluntary|反义词：obligatory, mandatory, compulsory|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *discretiōnārius, from Latin discretiō (“separation, distinction, discernment”), from discernere (“to separate, distinguish”).
