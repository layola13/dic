barrenly:/ˈbærənli/ | adv. | 贫瘠地，荒芜地 | In a barren or unproductive way; bleakly and lifelessly. | The fields stretched barrenly towards the horizon. 田野荒凉地伸向地平线。| synonym: bleakly, desolately, sterilely | antonym: fertilely, luxuriantly, productively | 词性: 副词

barrenly:/ˈbærənli/ | adv. | 不结果实地，不生育地 |  Figuratively, lacking in something desirable or productive. |  She stared barrenly at the blank page, unable to write. 她茫然地盯着空白页，写不出任何东西。| synonym: unproductively, fruitlessly | antonym: fruitfully, productively | 词性: 副词
