hermeneutics:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪks/ | n. | 诠释学，解释学 | The branch of knowledge that deals with interpretation, especially of the Bible or literary texts.  It involves the study of the principles and methods of interpretation. |  Example:  Hermeneutics plays a vital role in understanding ancient texts.  诠释学在理解古代文本中起着至关重要的作用。

Example: Biblical hermeneutics is a complex field of study. 圣经诠释学是一个复杂的研究领域。 | 近义词：interpretation, exegesis, explanation | 反义词: misinterpretation, distortion | 词性: noun

hermeneutical:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪkəl/ | adj. | 诠释的，解释的 | Relating to the principles and methods of interpretation. | Example: A hermeneutical approach is necessary to understand the author's intent.  要理解作者的意图，需要采用诠释学的方法。

Example: He offered a hermeneutical analysis of the poem. 他对这首诗进行了诠释学分析。 | 近义词：interpretive, exegetical, explanatory | 反义词: uninterpretive | 词性: adjective
