ETYMOLOGY->
From individual + -ize. "Individual" comes from Late Latin *individuālis*, from Latin individuus "indivisible, individual," from in- "not" + dividuus "divisible," from dividere "to divide."  The suffix "-ize" comes from Greek -ízein, used to form verbs.
