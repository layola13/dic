habitably:/ˈhæbɪtəbli/
adjective
可居住地，适宜居住地
Suitable for habitation; offering the conditions necessary for people to live.
例句：The planet is not currently habitably but may be in the future.  这个星球目前不适宜居住，但将来可能会适宜。

例句：Scientists are searching for habitably planets in other solar systems.  科学家们正在其他太阳系中寻找适宜居住的行星。
近义词：livable, inhabitable
反义词：uninhabitable, unlivable
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *habitabilis, from Latin habitāre (“to inhabit, to dwell”), from habitus, perfect passive participle of habēre (“to have, hold”).  Surface analysis is habit- + -able.
