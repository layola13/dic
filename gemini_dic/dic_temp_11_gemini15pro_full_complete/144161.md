outshouldered:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldərd/
verb
超过，胜过
surpassed, especially in competition or status, often by having broader shoulders or greater strength.  To push or force someone or something out of a place or position, especially by using one's shoulders.
The new supermarket outshouldered the small local shops and they eventually closed.  这家新的超市胜过了当地的小商店，最终它们都关门了。
He outshouldered his rival in the final stretch of the race. 他在比赛的最后阶段超过了他的对手。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, surpass, outstrip, eclipse, outdo, outperform
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性:动词
