intransigent:/ɪnˈtrænsɪdʒənt/ | adj. n. | 不妥协的，不让步的；不妥协的人 | Unwilling or refusing to change one's views or to agree about something. |  The intransigent parties refused to compromise.  双方毫不妥协，拒绝让步。 | Synonyms: uncompromising, inflexible, unyielding, obstinate | Antonyms: flexible, accommodating, yielding, compliant | adjective

intransigent:/ɪnˈtrænsɪdʒənt/ | n. | 不妥协的人 | A person who is unwilling or refusing to change their views or to agree about something. | The intransigents in the group prevented a consensus.  小组中不妥协的人阻碍了共识的达成。| Synonyms: diehard, hardliner, stick-in-the-mud | Antonyms: moderate, compromiser | noun
