evincement:/ɪˈvɪnsmənt/
n.|表明; 显示; 证明|The act of evincing or making evident; the state of being evinced.|His evincement of sadness was clear to everyone. 他悲伤的表现对每个人都很明显。|近义词: manifestation, demonstration, display, exhibition|反义词: concealment, suppression|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *evincere "to overcome, conquer," from ex- "out" + vincere "to conquer."  The noun form "evincement" arises from the verb "evince," meaning to reveal the presence of (a quality or feeling) clearly.
