carpet-bomb:/ˈkɑːrpɪt bɒm/ (US) /ˈkɑːpɪt bɒm/ (UK)| v. n.|地毯式轰炸|To bomb (an area) heavily and indiscriminately.|The city was carpet-bombed during the war.  战争期间，这座城市遭到了地毯式轰炸。|The air force carried out a series of carpet-bombing raids. 空军进行了一系列地毯式轰炸袭击。|近义词：bombard, blitz, blanket-bomb|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"precision bombing" 精确轰炸)|词性:动词, 名词
