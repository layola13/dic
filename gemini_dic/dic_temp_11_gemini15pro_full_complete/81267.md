granivorous:/ɡrəˈnɪvərəs/
adjective
食谷的；以谷物为食的
Feeding on grain or seeds.
Granivorous birds play a vital role in seed dispersal. 食谷鸟类在种子传播中起着至关重要的作用。
Sparrows are granivorous birds commonly found in urban areas. 麻雀是在城市地区常见的食谷鸟类。
近义词: seed-eating, grain-feeding
反义词: carnivorous, herbivorous
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin granum "grain" + -vorus "-eating," from vorare "to devour."
