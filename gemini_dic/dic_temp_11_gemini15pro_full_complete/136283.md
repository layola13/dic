nonreprehensibility:/nɒnˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
无可非议, 无可指责
The state or quality of being not reprehensible; blamelessness.
His nonreprehensibility in the matter was clear to everyone.  他在这件事上无可非议，每个人都清楚。
Synonyms: blamelessness, irreproachability, unimpeachability, innocence
Antonyms: culpability, guilt, blameworthiness
noun


Etymology->
From non- + reprehensible + -ity.  Reprehensible comes from Late Latin reprehensībilis, from reprehendere "to blame, censure," from re- "back" + prehendere "to seize, grasp."
