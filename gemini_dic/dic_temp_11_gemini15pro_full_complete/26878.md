cakelet:/ˈkeɪklɪt/ | n. |小蛋糕，迷你蛋糕 |A small cake, often individually sized. |例句：The bakery offered a variety of cakelets, including chocolate and vanilla. 这家面包店提供各种小蛋糕，包括巧克力和香草口味的。

例句：She brought a box of colorful cakelets to the party. 她带了一盒五颜六色的小蛋糕来参加聚会。

近义词：cupcake, mini cake, petit four |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps large cake) |词性:noun
