mewled:/mjuːld/ | v. |  轻轻地哭泣，发出微弱的哭声 | to cry weakly, typically like a baby or kitten | The newborn baby mewled softly in its mother's arms.  新生儿在母亲的怀里轻轻地哭泣。 | whimpered, cried, sobbed | shouted, yelled | verb

mewled:/mjuːld/ | adj. (rare, poetic) |  （罕见的，诗歌中的）发出微弱哭声的 | characterized by a weak crying sound | The mewled sounds of the kittens filled the barn. 小猫微弱的哭声充满了谷仓。| whimpering, crying | loud, boisterous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *meulen, from Old English *mēawlian, frequentative of mēawan (“to mew”). Cognate with Dutch miauwen (“to mew”).
