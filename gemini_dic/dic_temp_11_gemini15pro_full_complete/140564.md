oligarchical:/ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪkəl/ | adj. |寡头政治的；由少数人统治的 |Relating to or characteristic of an oligarchy; governed by a small group of people. |The country's oligarchical system concentrated power in the hands of a few wealthy families.  这个国家的寡头政治体系将权力集中在少数几个富裕家庭手中。|  近义词: autocratic, dictatorial, totalitarian, despotic | 反义词: democratic, egalitarian | 词性: 形容词

oligarchy: /ˈɒlɪɡɑːki/ | n. | 寡头政治；寡头统治集团 | A small group of people having control of a country, organization, or institution. | The oligarchy controlled the country's resources and suppressed dissent.  寡头统治集团控制着国家的资源并压制异议。| 近义词: autocracy, dictatorship | 反义词: democracy | 词性: 名词
