glaciers:/ˈɡleɪʃərz/ (US), /ˈɡlæsiəz/ (UK)| n. |冰川|Large masses of ice that form on land and move slowly down slopes or spread outwards.  They are formed from snow that compacts and recrystallizes over time.|例句：The glaciers are melting at an alarming rate. 冰川正在以惊人的速度融化。

|例句：Glaciers carve out valleys and shape the landscape. 冰川雕刻出山谷并塑造了地貌。|近义词：ice fields, ice caps|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "desert" in a contrasting landscape context) |词性: Noun
