night-and-day:/ˌnaɪt ən ˈdeɪ/| adv. adj.|夜以继日地，持续不断地|Continuously, without interruption, for a long period.|例句：He worked night and day to finish the project. 他夜以继日地工作以完成这个项目。|近义词：constantly, incessantly, continuously|反义词:intermittently, occasionally|词性:副词, 形容词
night-and-day:/ˌnaɪt ən ˈdeɪ/| adj.|夜以继日的|Relating to something that happens or continues without interruption.|例句：It was a night-and-day effort to get the house ready for the party.  为了让房子准备好开派对，我们夜以继日地努力。|近义词：continuous, constant, nonstop|反义词:intermittent, occasional|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "night and day," literally referring to the continuous cycle of day and night, hence the meaning of continuous or unceasing activity.  The phrase itself is of Germanic origin, with cognates in other Germanic languages.
