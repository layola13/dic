nonunitarian:/ˌnɒnjuːnɪˈtɛəriən/
adjective|非一元论的，非上帝一位论的|Not pertaining to or characterized by Unitarianism; rejecting the doctrine of the Trinity.|The nonunitarian beliefs of early Christians are a complex and debated topic. 早期基督徒的非一元论信仰是一个复杂且备受争议的话题。|nontrinitarian|Unitarian, Trinitarian
noun|非一元论者，非上帝一位论者|A person who is not a Unitarian; one who rejects the doctrine of the Trinity.|He identified as a nonunitarian, preferring a more nuanced understanding of the divine. 他自认为是非一元论者，更倾向于对神性有更细致的理解。|nontrinitarian|Unitarian, Trinitarian
