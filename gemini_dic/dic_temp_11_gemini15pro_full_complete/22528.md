boondoggle:/ˈbuːndɒɡəl/ /ˈbuːndɑːɡəl/ | n. vi. | 浪费时间或金钱的琐碎事物；闲混，浪费时间 | A wasteful or unnecessary project or activity, often involving public funds.  To engage in trivial or useless activity. | The new airport terminal turned out to be a massive boondoggle, costing millions but barely used.  新机场航站楼结果是一个巨大的浪费，耗资数百万美元，却几乎没有使用。| Synonyms: waste, trifle, folly, extravagance, white elephant | Antonyms: necessity, essential, asset, investment | Noun, Verb
