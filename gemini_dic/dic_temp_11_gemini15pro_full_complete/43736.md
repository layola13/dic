creel:/kriːl/
n.
鱼篓，鱼笼
A wicker basket used for carrying fish.
The fisherman pulled a full creel from the river.  渔夫从河里拉起一个装满鱼的鱼篓。

Etymology:  From Middle English crele, from Old French creil, of uncertain origin.

Synonyms:  basket, wicker basket, fish basket
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "aquarium" in a metaphorical sense if considering where fish are kept)
