considering:/kənˈsɪdərɪŋ/ | prep. conj.|考虑到，鉴于；就...而论| Taking everything into account; in view of.| Considering the circumstances, she did a good job. 考虑到情况，她做得很好。|近义词：given, in view of, taking into account|反义词:disregarding, ignoring|词性:介词, 连词

Etymology:
From Middle English consideringe, present participle of consideren, from Old French considerer, from Latin considerare, from com- "together" + sidus "star, constellation". Originally, "to contemplate the stars," later generalized to "observe attentively, examine, ponder."
