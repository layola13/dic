feculency:/ˈfɛkjʊlənsi/
n.
渣滓；沉淀物；污物；混浊
the quality or state of being feculent; dregs; sediment.
The feculency in the bottom of the wine bottle indicated its age.  酒瓶底部的沉淀物表明了它的年份。
近义词：sediment, dregs, lees, grounds
反义词：clarity, purity, clearness
词性：名词

feculent:/ˈfɛkjʊlənt/
adj.
混浊的；污秽的；含渣滓的
containing or consisting of dregs; foul with impurities; turbid.
The river, swollen with rain, was brown and feculent. 被雨水涨满的河水呈棕色，浑浊不堪。
近义词：turbid, muddy, cloudy, impure
反义词：clear, pure, clean
词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *faeculentus*, from faex (“dregs, sediment”).
