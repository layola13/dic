cowgirl: /ˈkaʊɡɜːrl/ (US), /ˈkaʊɡɜːl/ (UK)| n. |女牛仔；牧牛女|A woman who works on a ranch or participates in rodeo events, especially one who herds and tends cattle.|例句：The cowgirl expertly lassoed the calf. 女牛仔熟练地用套索套住了小牛。

例句：She dreamed of being a cowgirl and riding horses across the plains. 她梦想成为一名女牛仔，骑着马驰骋在平原上。|近义词：ranch hand, horsewoman|反义词: cowboy (in terms of gender)|词性: noun
