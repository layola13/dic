headlong:/ˈhedlɔːŋ/ (US), /ˈhedlɒŋ/ (UK)| adv. adj.|轻率地，鲁莽地；头朝前的|With reckless haste; headfirst.  Also used as an adjective to describe something done in this manner.|

例句：
He ran headlong into the wall.
他一头撞到了墙上。

She made a headlong rush for the door.
她一头冲向门口。


近义词：recklessly, hastily, rashly, precipitately, headfirst|
反义词：cautiously, deliberately, carefully|
词性：adverb, adjective|

headlong:/ˈhedlɔːŋ/ (US), /ˈhedlɒŋ/ (UK)| adj.|轻率的，鲁莽的|Done with reckless haste.|

例句：
It was a headlong decision that he later regretted.
这是一个他后来后悔的轻率决定。

The country's headlong plunge into war.
这个国家一头栽进了战争。

近义词：reckless, hasty, rash, impetuous, precipitate|
反义词：cautious, deliberate, careful|
词性：adjective|


词源：From Middle English *hedlong, equivalent to head +‎ long.  Cognate with Dutch *heedlang, German *heidlang (“headlong”).|
