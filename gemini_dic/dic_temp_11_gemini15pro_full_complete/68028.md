factoring-in:/ˈfæktərɪŋ ɪn/| phrasal verb | 考虑到，把…因素包括进去 | To include or consider something when making a decision or judgment. |  The company is factoring in the rising cost of raw materials when setting prices.  公司在定价时考虑了原材料成本上涨的因素。

The new budget factors in increased spending on education. 新预算考虑了增加教育支出。 | synonyms: consider, account for, include, take into account | antonyms: disregard, ignore, exclude, omit | verb phrase
