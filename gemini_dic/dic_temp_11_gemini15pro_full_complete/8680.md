anticonvulsants:/ˌæntiːkənˈvʌlsənts/| n.|抗惊厥药|Medications used to prevent or reduce the severity of seizures (convulsions).  These drugs work by altering the electrical activity in the brain that causes seizures.|The doctor prescribed anticonvulsants to control her epilepsy. 医生给她开了抗惊厥药来控制她的癫痫。|Synonyms: antiepileptics, antiseizure drugs|Antonyms: convulsants, proconvulsants|noun
