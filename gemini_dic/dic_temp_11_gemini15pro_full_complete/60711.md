electro-hydraulically:/ɪˌlektroʊ haɪˈdrɔːlɪkli/
adjective
电动液压地
Operated or controlled by a combination of electric and hydraulic power.
The landing gear was electro-hydraulically retracted.  起落架是用电动液压方式收回的。
The electro-hydraulically controlled system provides precise movement. 电动液压控制系统提供精确的运动。
Near Synonyms: electro-hydraulic
Antonyms: manually, mechanically
词性: adjective
