hamperedness's:/ˈhæmpərdnɪsɪz/ |n.|受阻碍的状态|The state of being hampered; the condition of being hindered or restricted.| The hamperedness's of the project due to lack of funding was evident. 由于缺乏资金，该项目的受阻状态是显而易见的。|近义词: hindrance, obstruction, impediment, restriction |反义词: freedom, ease, facility |词性:名词

词源:  From hampered + -ness + -'s (possessive).  Hampered itself comes from the verb hamper, which has uncertain origins, possibly related to a Middle Dutch word meaning "to entangle in a net."
