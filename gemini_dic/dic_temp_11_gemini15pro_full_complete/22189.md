bombora:/bɒmˈbɔːrə/ /bɑːmˈbɔːrə/
noun
暗礁浪，近岸巨浪；
A large wave breaking on a submerged reef or sandbank, especially one that is a popular surfing location.
例句：The surfers paddled out to catch the bombora.  冲浪者划出去捕捉暗礁浪。

例句：The bombora was breaking consistently, providing perfect waves. 暗礁浪持续不断地拍打着，提供了完美的浪。

近义词：wave, breaker, swell
反义词： (No direct antonym, but possibly) calm, still water
词性：noun
