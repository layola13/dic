heediness:/ˈhiːdinəs/
noun
谨慎,小心,注意
The quality or state of being heedful; attentiveness.
He showed heediness in his approach to the dangerous situation. 他在处理危险局势时表现出谨慎。
近义词: carefulness, cautiousness, prudence, vigilance
反义词: carelessness, recklessness, negligence


ETYMOLOGY->
From heed + -ness.  Heed comes from Old English *hēdan, from Proto-Germanic *hauzijan.
