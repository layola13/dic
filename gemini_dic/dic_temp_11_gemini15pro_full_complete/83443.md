habitational:/ˌhæbɪˈteɪʃənl/
adjective
居住的; 住宅的
Suitable for or used as a dwelling or residence.
The building was deemed habitational and fit for families. 这栋建筑被认为是适合居住的，适合家庭居住。
The city council approved the plan for a new habitational development. 市议会批准了新的住宅开发计划。

residential, dwelling, inhabitable, occupied
non-habitational, uninhabitable
