anti-oxygenation:/ˌæntiˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ |n.| 抗氧化，抗氧化作用 |The process of preventing or slowing down oxidation.  This involves inhibiting the reaction of a substance with oxygen.| Anti-oxygenation is crucial in food preservation to prevent spoilage.  抗氧化在食品保存中至关重要，可以防止腐败变质。|Synonyms: antioxidation, oxidation inhibition|Antonyms: oxidation, oxygenation|noun
