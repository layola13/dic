hausas:/ˈhaʊsəz/| n.|豪萨人|People who belong to the Hausa ethnic group, predominantly found in northern Nigeria and other parts of West Africa.  They are known for their rich culture, including a distinct language (also called Hausa), vibrant textiles, and a long history of trade and scholarship.|例句：The Hausas are a large and influential ethnic group in West Africa. 豪萨人是西非一个庞大而有影响力的民族群体。

例句：Many Hausas are Muslim and their culture reflects Islamic influences. 许多豪萨人是穆斯林，他们的文化反映了伊斯兰教的影响。|近义词：Hausa people|反义词: (Not applicable for ethnic groups) |词性:noun
