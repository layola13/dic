overlaxly:/ˌoʊvərˈlæksli/
adverb| 过于松懈地，过度放松地 | in a way that is too relaxed and does not provide enough control or care | He was overlaxly dressed for the occasion. 他穿得过于随便，不适合这个场合。 |  例句：The rules were overlaxly enforced. 规则执行得过于松懈。

例句：The security at the airport was overlaxly managed, leading to concerns about safety.  机场的安保管理过于松懈，引发了对安全的担忧。


近义词：negligently, carelessly, loosely |
反义词：strictly, rigorously, carefully |
词性：adverb


词源： "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "lax" (adjective meaning "not sufficiently strict, severe, or careful") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
