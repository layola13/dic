bugout:/ˈbʌɡaʊt/| n.|紧急撤退，匆忙逃离|A sudden, hasty retreat or flight, especially from danger or an impending disaster.|例句：They made a bugout when the wildfire approached their homes. 当野火逼近他们的家园时，他们匆忙逃离了。


例句：The family had a bugout plan in case of a hurricane.  这个家庭制定了一个飓风来临时的紧急撤离计划。


近义词：escape, flight, evacuation|反义词: arrival, advance, stay|词性:名词
