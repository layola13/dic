honky-tonk: /ˈhɒŋki tɒŋk/ (British) /ˈhɑːŋki tɑːŋk/ (American) | n. | 低级酒吧；廉价酒馆；喧闹的场所 | A cheap, noisy bar or nightclub, typically one where country music is played. |  We spent the evening at a honky-tonk, listening to live music and dancing. 我们在一家低级酒吧度过了一个晚上，听着现场音乐跳舞。

He played piano in various honky-tonks across the state. 他在全州各地的低级酒吧里弹钢琴。


近义词: dive bar, roadhouse, juke joint
反义词: upscale bar, sophisticated club
词性: noun
