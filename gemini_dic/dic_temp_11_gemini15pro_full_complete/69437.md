fees:/fiːz/
n.
费用，杂费
A sum of money charged for professional services or a privilege.
The university fees are quite high this year. 今年的大学学费相当高。

近义词:charges, costs, dues, payments, rates
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 income, earnings, revenue 等表示收入的词)
词性:名词
