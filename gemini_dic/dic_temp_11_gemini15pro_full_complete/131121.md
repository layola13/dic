nonbitterer:/nɒnˈbɪtərər/ | n. | 非苦味者，不怨恨的人 |  A person who does not feel or express bitterness or resentment.  | Example: He was a nonbitterer, always looking for the positive in every situation. 他是一个不怨恨的人，总是能在任何情况下看到积极的一面。| Example: She emerged from the ordeal a nonbitterer, ready to move forward. 她从苦难中走出来，没有怨恨，准备继续前进。 | Synonym: optimist, forgiver | Antonym: bitter person, complainer | noun
