likes:/laɪks/ | v. | 喜欢；喜爱 | third-person singular present tense form of like | She likes to read books in her spare time. 她喜欢在空闲时间读书。|  He likes playing basketball. 他喜欢打篮球。 | 近义词: enjoys, prefers, favors | 反义词: dislikes, hates | 词性: 动词


Etymology:
From Middle English *liken, *likien, from Old English līcian (“to please, be pleasing; seem suitable, appear fitting; be agreeable”), from Proto-Germanic *līkjōną (“to be suitable, fitting”), from Proto-Indo-European *leǵ- (“to collect, gather”). Cognate with Old Frisian līkja (“to please”), Dutch lijken (“to seem, appear”), German gleichen (“to resemble, be like”), Old Norse líka (“to please, like”), Gothic (leikan, “to please”).
