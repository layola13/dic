jerkin:/ˈdʒɜːrkɪn/| n. |无袖短上衣，紧身短上衣|A short, close-fitting, sleeveless jacket, often made of leather, worn over other clothing, especially in the 16th and 17th centuries.|He wore a leather jerkin over his tunic. 他在束腰外衣外面穿了一件皮革无袖短上衣。|Synonyms: vest, doublet|Antonyms: coat, jacket|noun
