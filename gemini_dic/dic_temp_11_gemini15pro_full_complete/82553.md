guano:/ˈɡwɑːnoʊ/| n. |鸟粪，海鸟粪，蝙蝠粪|A natural fertilizer made from the droppings of seabirds or bats, rich in nitrogen, phosphate, and potassium. |The farmers used guano to fertilize their crops. 农民们用鸟粪给庄稼施肥。|近义词:fertilizer, manure|反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑soil depletion 土壤贫瘠)|词性:noun
