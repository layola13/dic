everyones:/ˈevriwʌnz/ |pron. | 每个人；所有人 |  A nonstandard form of everyone, used to emphasize inclusion.  This form is generally considered incorrect in formal writing. | We want everyones opinion on this matter. 我们想要每个人对此事的意见。| 近义词：everybody, everyone | 反义词：nobody, no one | 词性: 代词

词源:  A blend of *every* and *one* with an added *-s* for plural emphasis, though grammatically incorrect. *Every* comes from Old English *æfre ælc* meaning "ever each." *One* comes from Old English *ān*.

例句：

Everyone is welcome to join the party.
每个人都欢迎参加派对。

Everyone has their own strengths and weaknesses.
每个人都有自己的优点和缺点。
