idolatrously:/aɪˈdɑːlətrəsli/
adverb
崇拜地，偶像崇拜地
In a manner that involves excessive admiration or devotion to someone or something, resembling the worship of an idol.
He gazed idolatrously at the pop star.  他崇拜地凝视着那个流行歌星。
She idolatrously followed every trend set by the fashion icon. 她盲目追随那位时尚偶像引领的每一个潮流。
近义词: reverently, adoringly, worshipfully
反义词: irreverently, disrespectfully
词性: adverb

词源:
From idol + -atrous + -ly.  "Idol" comes from the Old French "idole," ultimately from the Greek "eidōlon" meaning "image" or "phantom."  "-atrous" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "given to," from the Greek "-atros."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
