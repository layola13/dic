intercombination:/ˌɪntərˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |相互结合，（量子力学）禁戒跃迁|A transition between two electronic states differing in spin multiplicity.|The phosphorescence of the molecule is due to intercombination.  该分子的磷光是由禁戒跃迁引起的。|Synonyms: transition, change|Antonyms: stability, constancy|noun


intercombination:/ˌɪntərˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |相互结合，（量子力学）禁戒跃迁|A radiative transition between two electronic states having different multiplicities.|Intercombination transitions are usually less probable than transitions between states of the same multiplicity. 禁戒跃迁通常比相同多重态之间的跃迁概率更低。|Synonyms: conversion, transformation|Antonyms:  preservation, maintenance|noun
