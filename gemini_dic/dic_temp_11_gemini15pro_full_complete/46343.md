dacron:/ˈdeɪkrɒn/| n.|涤纶, 达克纶|A synthetic polyester fiber used in clothing, sails, and other products. It is known for its strength, wrinkle resistance, and quick-drying properties.|例句：My new jacket is made of Dacron. 我的新夹克是涤纶做的。

例句：Dacron sails are popular among sailors. 达克纶帆很受帆船爱好者的欢迎。|近义词：polyester, Terylene|反义词:cotton, wool|词性:noun

词源:Dacron is a trademarked name coined by DuPont for its polyester fiber. It's derived from the chemical name polyethylene terephthalate (PET).
