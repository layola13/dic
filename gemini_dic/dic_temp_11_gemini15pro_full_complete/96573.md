indubitably:/ˌɪndɪˈbjuːtəbli/| adv.|毫无疑问地，确实地|Without any doubt; unquestionably.|Her talent is indubitably remarkable. 她的才华确实非凡。|近义词：certainly, definitely, undoubtedly, unquestionably|反义词：doubtfully, questionably|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Middle English: from Old French indubitable, from Latin indubitabilis, from in- ‘not’ + dubitabilis (from dubitare ‘to doubt’).
