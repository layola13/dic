brittle:/ˈbrɪtl/| adj.|易碎的，脆弱的|Easily broken or cracked.|The old vase was so brittle that it crumbled when I touched it.  这旧花瓶太脆了，我一碰就碎了。|The dry leaves are brittle. 干枯的叶子很脆。|近义词：fragile, frail, delicate|反义词：resilient, tough, flexible|词性:形容词
brittles:/ˈbrɪtlz/| n.|脆糖，易碎的东西|Hard, brittle candies, typically containing nuts.|I love peanut brittles. 我喜欢花生脆糖。|The explosion shattered the glass into a million brittles. 爆炸把玻璃震碎成无数的碎片。|近义词：shards, fragments (when referring to broken pieces)|反义词：N/A (as a noun, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|词性:名词
