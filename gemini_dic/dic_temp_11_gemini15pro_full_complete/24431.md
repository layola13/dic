bristlelike:/ˈbrɪsəlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像鬃毛一样的；毛发状的
Resembling a bristle; stiff and short.
The cactus had bristlelike spines.  仙人掌有像鬃毛一样的刺。
The insect's legs were covered in bristlelike hairs. 这昆虫的腿上覆盖着像鬃毛一样的毛。
synonyms: bristly, setaceous, hispid
antonyms: smooth, soft, downy
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bristle + -like.  Bristle comes from Old English *byrst, meaning "bristle," related to German Borste.  -like is a common suffix forming adjectives indicating resemblance.
