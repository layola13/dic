lovelily:/ˈlʌvɪli/| adv.|可爱地；美好地|In a lovely manner; beautifully.|She sang lovelily. 她唱得优美动听。|近义词：beautifully, charmingly, delightfully, exquisitely, prettily|反义词：unattractively, uglily|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From lovely + -ly.  "Lovely" comes from Middle English *loveli, from Old English *luflic, meaning "worthy of love," from lufu "love" + -lic "-ly."
