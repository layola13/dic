nonconsent:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɛnt/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsɛnt/ | n. | 未经同意，不同意 | Absence of consent; lack of permission or agreement. |  The surgery was performed without her nonconsent.  手术未经她同意就进行了。| Near synonyms:  dissent, refusal, objection | Antonyms: consent, agreement, permission | noun

nonconsensual:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɛnʃuəl/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsɛnʃuəl/ | adj. | 未经同意的，非自愿的 | Not agreed to or permitted by the person involved. | The act was deemed nonconsensual and therefore illegal.  该行为被认为是未经同意的，因此是非法的。| Near synonyms: forced, involuntary, coerced | Antonyms: consensual, voluntary, agreed upon | adjective
