itineracy:/aɪˈtɪnərəsi/ | n. | 旅行；旅程；巡回；流动性 | the state of traveling from place to place |  Her itineracy meant she rarely stayed in one place for long. 她的四处奔波意味着她很少在一个地方待很长时间。|  nomadism, wandering, roaming, traveling, peregrination | settlement, stability, permanence | noun

itinerant:/aɪˈtɪnərənt/ | adj. |  巡回的；流动的； itinerant preacher | traveling from place to place, especially to work | An itinerant worker travels from farm to farm picking fruit.  一位流动工人辗转于各个农场采摘水果。 | traveling, peripatetic, nomadic, wandering, roaming | settled, resident, stationary | adjective
