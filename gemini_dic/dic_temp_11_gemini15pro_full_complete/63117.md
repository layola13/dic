ensile:/ɪnˈsaɪl/ /ɛnˈsaɪl/| n. v.|青贮饲料，青贮|To preserve (green fodder) in a silo or airtight container, typically by partial fermentation.|例句：The farmer ensiled the corn for winter feed. 农民将玉米青贮以备冬季喂养。|近义词：forage, silage, fodder|反义词:|n. v.


ensile:/ɪnˈsaɪl/ /ɛnˈsaɪl/| v.|把…做成青贮饲料；将…贮藏在筒仓内|To store in a silo.|例句：They ensile the grass in the summer. 他们在夏天把草贮藏在筒仓里。|近义词：store, preserve|反义词:|v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (put in) + silo. Ultimately from Spanish silo.
