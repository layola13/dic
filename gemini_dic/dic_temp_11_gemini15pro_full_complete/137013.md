nonstabilities:/ˌnɒnstəˈbɪlətiz/ /ˌnɑːnstəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不稳定性；不稳定状态 | The plural form of nonstability, referring to multiple instances or types of instability.  It describes the lack of stability or the tendency to change or fluctuate. | The nonstabilities of the stock market make it a risky investment. 股票市场的不稳定性使其成为一项风险投资。|  The political nonstabilities in the region are a cause for concern. 该地区政治上的不稳定令人担忧。 | synonyms: instabilities, fluctuations, volatilities | antonyms: stabilities, steadiness, constancy | noun
