outtwine:/ˌaʊtˈtwaɪn/
verb
缠绕；交织
to twist or twine around something, especially so as to enclose or entangle it.
The ivy outtwined the oak tree.  常春藤缠绕着橡树。
intertwine, entwine, interweave
untwine, disentangle
v.
