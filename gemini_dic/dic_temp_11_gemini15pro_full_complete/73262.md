fore-spurrer:/fɔːrˈspɜːrər/
n.|先驱；激励者|Someone who incites or encourages an action or undertaking.|The king's fore-spurrer rallied the troops before the battle. 国王の先驱在战斗前激励了军队。|Synonyms: instigator, initiator, motivator, prompter|Antonyms: deterrent, hinderer, obstructer|noun

fore-spurrer:/fɔːrˈspɜːrər/
n.|先行者；前驱者|Someone who goes before, leading the way.|The explorers acted as fore-spurrers, mapping the unknown territory. 探险家充当先行者，绘制了未知的领域。|Synonyms: pioneer, forerunner, precursor, vanguard|Antonyms: follower, laggard|noun
