klutz:/klʌts/| n.|笨手笨脚的人，冒失鬼|A clumsy, awkward person.|例句：He's such a klutz; he always spills his drinks. 他真是个笨手笨脚的人，总是把饮料洒出来。

例句：She dropped her phone again; she's a real klutz. 她又把手机摔了，真是个冒失鬼。|近义词：bungler, butterfingers, oaf|反义词：graceful person, adept|词性:名词

klutzes:/ˈklʌtsɪz/| n.|笨手笨脚的人们，冒失鬼们|Plural form of "klutz," referring to multiple clumsy or awkward people.|例句：Those klutzes tripped over the rug and spilled the punch.  那些笨手笨脚的家伙被地毯绊倒，把潘趣酒洒了。
