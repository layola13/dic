actuarial:/ˌæktʃuˈɛəriəl/ | adj. | 精算的；保险精算师的 | Relating to the calculation of risks and premiums by actuaries. |  Actuaries use actuarial tables to calculate risk. 精算师使用精算表来计算风险。| 近义词: statistical, insurance-related | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

actuary:/ˈæktʃʊəri/ | n. | 精算师 | A person who compiles and analyzes statistics and uses them to calculate insurance risks and premiums. |  He works as an actuary for a large insurance company. 他在一家大型保险公司担任精算师。| 近义词: statistician, risk analyst | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *actuarius (“recorder, clerk”), from Latin actus (“act, deed, legal document”), past participle of agere (“to do, drive, act”).  The original meaning referred to a clerk who recorded official acts.  The modern sense developed from the role of calculating life expectancy and related statistics for insurance purposes.
