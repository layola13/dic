frithy:/ˈfrɪθi/| adj.|起泡沫的，多泡的；（古语）多林木的|Covered with or consisting of froth; foamy. (Archaic) Densely wooded.|The beer was frithy and inviting.  啤酒泡沫丰富，十分诱人。|Synonyms: foamy, frothy, spumy|Antonyms: flat, still|adjective
