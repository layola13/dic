disposer:/dɪˈspəʊzər (UK), dɪˈspoʊzər (US)| n.|处理者，处置者，支配者；[尤指]垃圾处理器|A person or thing that disposes of something, especially a garbage disposal unit. |She put the food scraps into the garbage disposer. 她把食物残渣放进了垃圾处理器。|近义词：manager, administrator, controller|反义词:keeper, hoarder|noun

disposal:/dɪˈspəʊzl (UK), dɪˈspoʊzl (US)| n.|处置，处理；支配权|The act or means of getting rid of something.  The power to use something or someone.|The disposal of hazardous waste is a serious issue.  危险废物的处理是一个严重的问题。|近义词：removal, clearance, riddance|反义词:acquisition, retention|noun

dispose:/dɪˈspəʊz (UK), dɪˈspoʊz (US)| v.|处置，处理；布置，安排|Get rid of by throwing away or giving or selling to someone else.  Arrange in a particular position.|He disposed of the evidence. 他处理掉了证据。|近义词：discard, jettison, arrange|反义词:keep, retain|verb
