asunder:/əˈsʌndər/
adverb, adjective
分开地，成碎片地
into separate pieces; apart.
例句：The ship was torn asunder by the storm.  这艘船被暴风雨撕成了碎片。

例句：Their lives were torn asunder by the war. 他们的生活被战争撕裂了。

近义词：apart, separated, divided, broken, fragmented
反义词：together, united, joined, combined
词性：adverb, adjective

词源：Old English on sundran "into pieces," from sundor "apart" (see sundry) + an "on."  来自古英语 on sundran “成碎片”，由 sundor “分开”（见 sundry） + an “在…上” 组成。
