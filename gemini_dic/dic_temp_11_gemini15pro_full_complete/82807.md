guise:/ɡaɪz/| n.|伪装，幌子|an external form, appearance, or manner of presentation, typically concealing the true nature of something.|He visited in the guise of a doctor. 他装扮成医生的样子来访。|synonyms: appearance, semblance, pretense, disguise, mask, facade, cloak, veil|antonyms: reality, truth|noun
