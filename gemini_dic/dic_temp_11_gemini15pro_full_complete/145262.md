overboiled:/ˌoʊvərˈbɔɪld/| adj.|煮过头的，煮得太久的|Cooked for too long, resulting in a loss of texture and flavor.|The vegetables were overboiled and mushy. 蔬菜煮过火了，都糊了。|The pasta was sadly overboiled.  这意大利面很遗憾煮过头了。|近义词：overcooked|反义词:undercooked|词性:形容词
词源: over- + boiled (past participle of boil) "over-" 表示 "过度"，"boiled" 表示 "煮熟的"，组合起来表示 "煮过头的"。
