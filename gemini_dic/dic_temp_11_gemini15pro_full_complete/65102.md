etherealized:/ɪˈθɪəriəlaɪzd/
verb
使空灵化，使飘渺化
made ethereal or spiritual; highly refined; delicate
Her etherealized voice floated through the hall. 她空灵的声音飘荡在大厅里。
近义词: spiritualized, refined, sublimated
反义词: materialized, coarsened
词性: verb
