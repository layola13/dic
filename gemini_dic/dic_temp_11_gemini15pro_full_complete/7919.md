antependium:/ˌæntiˈpɛndiəm/
n.
帷幔，祭坛正面悬挂的装饰布
A decorative hanging for the front of an altar.
The antependium was richly embroidered with gold thread.  祭坛帷幔用金线绣得很精美。

近义词: altar frontal, altar cloth, pall
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific decorative item.)
词性: noun
