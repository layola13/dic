espaliering:/ɪˈspælɪərɪŋ/
n.
支撑树木使其沿墙或框架生长；整枝
The act of training a tree or shrub to grow flat against a wall or trellis, typically by pruning and tying branches.
The espaliering of fruit trees is a common practice in small gardens.  支撑果树沿墙生长是小花园里的常见做法。
training, pruning, shaping
 uncontrolled growth

espalier:/ɪˈspælɪər/
n.
棚架；支架
A trellis or framework on which fruit trees or shrubs are trained to grow flat.
The espalier was covered in fragrant jasmine.  棚架上爬满了芬芳的茉莉花。
trellis, framework, lattice
freestanding

espalier:/ɪˈspælɪər/
vt.
使(树木)沿墙或框架生长；整枝
To train (a tree or shrub) to grow flat against a wall or trellis.
He espaliered the pear tree against the south-facing wall.  他把梨树沿南墙整枝。
train, prune, shape
neglect
