marmoraceous:/ˌmɑːrməˈreɪʃəs/
| adj. |大理石似的，大理石状的|Resembling marble in appearance or texture; like marble. |The countertop had a marmoraceous finish, giving the kitchen a luxurious feel.  厨房的台面有大理石般的光洁度，给厨房增添了奢华感。 |例句：The polished floor had a marmoraceous sheen. 磨光的地板有大理石般的光泽。|近义词：marble-like, marbly|反义词:  n/a|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin "marmor" (marble) + "-aceous" (suffix indicating resemblance).
