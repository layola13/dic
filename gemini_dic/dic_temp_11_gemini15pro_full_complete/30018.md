cayenne:/keɪˈen/ /kaɪˈen/| n. |辣椒, 卡宴辣椒, 辣椒粉|A hot pepper originally from French Guiana, widely cultivated in tropical countries.  It is used to make cayenne pepper (a powdered spice).|I added a pinch of cayenne to the chili for extra heat. 我在辣椒里加了一撮辣椒粉以增加辣味。

|近义词：chili pepper, red pepper|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 mild pepper 温和的辣椒)|noun


cayenne pepper:/keɪˈen ˈpepər/ /kaɪˈen ˈpepər/| n. |辣椒粉|A powdered spice made from dried cayenne peppers.|Cayenne pepper is often used in Cajun and Creole cooking. 辣椒粉常用于卡津菜和克里奥尔菜肴中。

|近义词：chili powder, red pepper flakes|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 paprika 甜椒粉)|noun
