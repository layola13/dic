chestnuts:/ˈtʃɛsnʌts/ (US) /ˈtʃɛsnʌts/ (UK)| n.|栗子; 栗树; 栗色; (非正式)老掉牙的笑话或故事|A type of edible nut, the seed of a chestnut tree. Also refers to the tree itself, the color of the nut, or a stale joke or story.|We roasted chestnuts over the open fire. 我们在篝火上烤栗子。|Synonyms: nuts, (for the color) brown, maroon, auburn, (for the stale joke) cliche, platitude|Antonyms: (for the color) bright, vibrant|noun
