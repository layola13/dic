overnegligence:/ˌoʊvərˈnɛɡlɪdʒəns/| n. |过度疏忽，极度疏忽|Excessive negligence; failure to exercise even a slight degree of care.|His overnegligence led to the disastrous fire. 他的极度疏忽导致了那场灾难性的大火。| The overnegligence of the landlord resulted in numerous safety violations. 房东的过度疏忽导致了许多安全违规。|近义词：gross negligence, recklessness, carelessness|反义词：diligence, care, attention|词性:名词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "negligence" (from Latin negligentia, from negligens "neglectful")
