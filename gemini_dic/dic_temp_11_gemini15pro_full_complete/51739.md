devolution:/ˌdiːvəˈluːʃən/ /ˌdevəˈluːʃən/
n.
权力下放，移交；退化，衰退
the transfer or delegation of power to a lower level, especially by central government to local or regional administration.  A decline to a lower or worse state.
The devolution of power from the central government to the regions. 中央政府向地方政府下放权力。
The devolution of the human race into barbarism. 人类退化到野蛮状态。
decentralization, delegation, transfer
centralization, retention
noun
