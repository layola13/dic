ferretto:/fəˈrɛtoʊ/
noun
small iron tool
A small iron tool, specifically a burnisher used by bookbinders for polishing the edges of books.
Bookbinders use a ferretto to achieve a smooth, polished finish on the edges of a book.
synonyms: burnisher, polishing tool
antonyms: roughening tool
n.
