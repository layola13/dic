leptocardian: /ˌlɛptəˈkɑːrdiən/ | adj. n. |  瘦心纲的；瘦心纲动物 | Belonging to or relating to the Leptocardii, a subphylum of primitive chordates; a member of the Leptocardii. |  Leptocardians are marine animals. 瘦心纲动物是海洋动物。 The leptocardian larva is similar to the adult. 瘦心纲动物的幼虫与成虫相似。 |  synonym: lancelet, amphioxus | antonym: vertebrate | adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Greek *leptós* (thin, fine) + *kardía* (heart).
