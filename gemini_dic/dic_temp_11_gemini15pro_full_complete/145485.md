overcleverly:/ˌoʊvərˈklɛvərli/ | adv. | 过于聪明地，自作聪明地 |  Formed by combining "over" (meaning excessively) and "cleverly" (meaning in a clever or intelligent way). It implies an excessive or ostentatious display of cleverness, often to the point of being annoying or counterproductive. | He overcleverly tried to outsmart the police, but ended up getting caught. 他自作聪明地想智胜警察，结果反而被抓住了。| Synonyms: excessively cleverly, too cleverly, pretentiously cleverly | Antonyms: simply, plainly, artlessly | Word Type: Adverb
