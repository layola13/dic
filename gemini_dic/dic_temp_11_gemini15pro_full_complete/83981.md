half-criticalnesses:/ˌhæf-ˈkrɪtɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 半批评; 不完全的批评;  |A state of being only partially or mildly critical.  This word is not commonly used and might sound awkward. It's better to use phrases like "mild criticism," "partial criticism," or "hesitant criticism." | He expressed his half-criticalnesses of the project, praising its innovation while pointing out minor flaws. 他表达了对该项目的不完全的批评，赞扬了它的创新，同时指出了细微的缺陷。 | Near synonyms:  reservations, slight objections, qualified approval | Near antonyms:  outright condemnation, harsh criticism | noun
