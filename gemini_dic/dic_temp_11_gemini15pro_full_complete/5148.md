alleluias: /ˌælɪˈluːjəz/ | n. |哈利路亚，赞美上帝的欢呼|A joyful shout of praise to God, often used in religious ceremonies and songs.  It is the plural form of "alleluia".|The choir sang powerful alleluias during the Easter service.  唱诗班在复活节仪式上高唱哈利路亚。|The congregation responded with loud alleluias.  会众以响亮的哈利路亚回应。|Synonyms: praises, hallelujahs |Antonyms: blasphemies, curses | Noun
