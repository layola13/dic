localize:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪz/ (US), /ˈləʊkəlaɪz/ (UK)| v.|本地化；局部化；确定…的位置|To adapt something to a local environment or market, or to confine something to a specific area.|The company decided to localize its products to better suit the Asian market.  这家公司决定将其产品本地化，以更好地适应亚洲市场。|synonyms: adapt, adjust, confine, restrict|antonyms: globalize, internationalize|verb

localize:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪz/ (US), /ˈləʊkəlaɪz/ (UK)| v.|定位；找出…的部位|To determine the location of something.|Doctors were able to localize the tumor using a CT scan. 医生能够使用CT扫描来定位肿瘤。|synonyms: pinpoint, locate, find|antonyms: lose, misplace|verb
