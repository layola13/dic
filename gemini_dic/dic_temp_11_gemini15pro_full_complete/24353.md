brindles: /ˈbrɪndəlz/ | n. |虎斑纹，斑纹; |Darker streaks or spots on a gray or tawny ground, such as the markings of a tiger or a brindled dog. | The boxer's coat was a beautiful mixture of brindles. 这只拳师犬的皮毛是美丽的虎斑纹混合.| 近义词: stripes, streaks, markings | 反义词: solid color | 词性: 名词

brindled: /ˈbrɪndəld/ | adj. | 有虎斑纹的，有斑纹的 | Having dark streaks or spots on a gray or tawny ground. | We adopted a brindled greyhound. 我们收养了一只带有斑纹的灰狗. | 近义词: striped, streaked, mottled | 反义词: solid, plain | 词性: 形词

词源: From Middle English *brindeled, from brindel (“striped material”), probably of Scandinavian origin.
