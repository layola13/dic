induced:/ɪnˈdjuːst/ /ɪnˈduːst/| adj.|诱导的，感应的，引起的|Caused or brought about by something else.|例句：The drug induced a state of euphoria.  这种药物引起了一种欣快感。|近义词：caused, stimulated, provoked|反义词：natural, spontaneous|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *inducere*, to lead in, bring in.  From *in-* (in) + *ducere* (to lead).
