conspire:/kənˈspaɪər/ | v. | 密谋，共谋，协力 | to secretly plan with someone else to do something bad, illegal, or against someone else |  The rebels conspired to overthrow the government. 叛乱分子密谋推翻政府。

They conspired against him to prevent him from getting the job. 他们合谋反对他，阻止他得到那份工作。

| 近义词：plot, scheme, collude | 反义词： cooperate, assist | verb
