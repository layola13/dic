delegating: /ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 授权的；委托的 |  Describing the act of assigning responsibility or authority. |  He has a delegating leadership style. 他有一种授权式的领导风格。 | Synonyms: authorizing, empowering | Antonyms: controlling, restrictive | adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *delegatus, past participle of delegare "to send as deputy," from de- "down, away" + legare "to send with a commission, depute, bequeath," from lex (genitive legis) "law."  The original sense was "to send as a representative," later "to commit, entrust."
