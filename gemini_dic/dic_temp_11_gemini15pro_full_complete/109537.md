lesseeships:/ˌlesiːʃɪps/
n.
租赁权；承租人的身份地位
The state or fact of being a lessee; a leasehold.
The lesseeships of the apartments were transferred to a new management company. 这些公寓的租赁权转让给了一家新的管理公司。
synonyms: leaseholds, tenancies
antonyms: ownerships, landlordships
noun
