gazump:/ɡəˈzʌmp/
gazump: v. | 哄抬价格；抢先高价收购| To outbid someone (usually on a house purchase) at the last minute. |  The seller gazumped us by accepting a higher offer from someone else. 卖方接受了别人的更高报价，把我们给哄抬出局了。| 近义词: outbid, overbid | 反义词: underbid |词性: 动词
