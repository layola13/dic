introspectional:/ˌɪntrəˈspɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 内省的，反省的 | Given to or characterized by introspection; examining one's own thoughts and feelings.  |  She is an introspectional person, always analyzing her own motivations. 她是一个内省的人，总是分析自己的动机。|  He spent an introspectional weekend reviewing his life choices. 他度过了一个反省的周末，回顾了他的人生选择。|  reflective, meditative, contemplative, pensive | extroverted, outgoing | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From introspect + -ional.  Introspect comes from Latin introspicere "to look into," from intro- "inward" + specere "to look."
