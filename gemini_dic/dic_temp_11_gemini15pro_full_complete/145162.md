overassuming:/ˌoʊvərəˈsuːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 自以为是的，傲慢的 | Having or showing too much confidence and a tendency to believe that one is more important or capable than one actually is. |  He was an overassuming young man who thought he knew everything. 他是一个自以为是的年轻人，认为自己什么都知道。 | Example: She made an overassuming remark about her role in the project. 她对自己在项目中的作用发表了自以为是的评论。|Synonyms: arrogant, presumptuous, conceited, pompous | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | Word type: Adjective
