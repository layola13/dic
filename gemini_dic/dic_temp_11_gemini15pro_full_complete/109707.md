leveche:/ləˈveɪtʃ/ /ləˈvetʃ/| n.|热风，黎凡特风|A hot, dry, dust-laden wind blowing from the Sahara across North Africa and out into the Mediterranean Sea, often causing hazy conditions.|The leveche blew all day, coating everything in a fine layer of red dust.|近义词：sirocco, khamsin, harmattan|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表示寒冷潮湿风的词语，例如：mistral)|词性: noun
