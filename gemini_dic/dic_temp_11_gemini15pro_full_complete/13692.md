attributes:/ˈætrɪˌbjuːts/ | n. | 属性，特征，特质 | qualities or features that belong to a person or thing |  The car has many desirable attributes, including great fuel economy. 这辆车有很多令人满意的特性，包括非常省油。 | synonyms: characteristics, traits, qualities, properties | antonyms: defects, flaws | noun


/əˈtrɪbjuːt/ | v. | 归因于，归属于 | regard something as being caused by someone or something | He attributed his success to hard work. 他将自己的成功归因于努力工作。 | synonyms: ascribe, assign, credit, impute | antonyms: disavow, deny | verb
