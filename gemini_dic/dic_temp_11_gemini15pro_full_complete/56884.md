dosing:/ˈdoʊsɪŋ/| n.|给药，剂量|The dosing of this medication is crucial for its effectiveness.  这种药物的剂量对其有效性至关重要。|Examples: 1. The doctor carefully calculated the dosing for the patient. 医生仔细计算了病人的剂量。
