antisupernatural:/ˌæntiˌsüpərˈnætʃrəl/ | adj. | 反超自然的，反对超自然现象的 | Opposed to or denying the existence of supernatural powers or phenomena. |  He had a very antisupernatural attitude, dismissing any talk of ghosts or spirits.  他抱有非常反超自然的态度，对任何关于鬼魂或神灵的谈论都不屑一顾。

| Example: She expressed antisupernatural views, arguing that science could explain everything. 她表达了反超自然的观点，认为科学可以解释一切。 |  Synonyms: skeptical, rational, scientific, empirical  | Antonyms: supernatural, mystical, occult, paranormal | adjective
