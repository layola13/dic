miniature:/ˈmɪnɪtʃər/ (US), /ˈmɪnɪtʃə/ (UK)| adj. n.|微型的，缩影；微型复制品|A miniature version of something is much smaller than the usual size.  A miniature is a very small painting, usually of a person.|She bought a miniature of the Eiffel Tower as a souvenir. 她买了一个埃菲尔铁塔的微缩模型作为纪念品。|synonyms: small, tiny, minute, diminutive|antonyms: large, huge, gigantic|adjective, noun
