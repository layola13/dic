chuffily:/ˈtʃʌfɪli/| adv.|不高兴地，恼怒地|In a bad-tempered or annoyed way, often because one's dignity has been offended.|"He chuffily refused the offer, feeling insulted by the low price." 他恼怒地拒绝了提议，觉得低价是对他的侮辱。|近义词：grumpily, sullenly, huffily|反义词：happily, cheerfully|词性:副词
词源：From the adjective "chuffy" + "-ly". "Chuffy" originated in the late 19th century and is likely related to "chuff," an earlier word meaning "puffed up," perhaps imitating the sound of heavy breathing associated with annoyance.
