multicorporation: /ˌmʌltiˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 多公司; 多法人集团 | A large organization composed of multiple incorporated entities, often operating in diverse industries or geographical locations.  |  Example: The multicorporation expanded its reach by acquiring smaller companies in emerging markets.  这家多公司集团通过收购新兴市场上的小公司扩大了业务范围。

Example:  Managing a multicorporation presents unique challenges in terms of coordination and communication.  管理一个多公司集团在协调和沟通方面提出了独特的挑战。

| 近义词: conglomerate, multinational corporation, corporate group | 反义词: small business, single entity company | 词性: noun
