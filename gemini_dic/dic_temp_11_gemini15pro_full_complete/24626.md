brokenly:/ˈbroʊkənli/| adv.| 断断续续地，支离破碎地| in a broken or fragmented manner; with interruptions or pauses; falteringly. | She spoke brokenly, her voice choked with tears. 她断断续续地说着，声音哽咽着泪水。|例句：He sobbed brokenly. 他断断续续地抽泣着。|近义词：haltingly, falteringly, intermittently, spasmodically|反义词：fluently, continuously, smoothly|词性:副词
