hanukkah:/ˈhɑːnəkə/ (US), /ˈhænəkə/ (UK)| n. |光明节|A Jewish festival lasting eight days and commemorating the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem.  It is celebrated by lighting candles on a special menorah.|We celebrate Hanukkah by lighting candles each night. 我们每晚点燃蜡烛庆祝光明节。|
Hanukkah is a time for families to gather and celebrate. 光明节是家人团聚庆祝的时刻。|
Synonyms: Festival of Lights, Feast of Dedication|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
