insectivorous:/ɪnˌsɛkˈtɪvərəs/
adjective
食虫的
feeding on insects; (of plants) trapping and digesting insects.
例句：Bats are insectivorous mammals.  蝙蝠是食虫哺乳动物。

例句：The Venus flytrap is an insectivorous plant.  捕蝇草是一种食虫植物。
近义词：carnivorous, insect-eating
反义词：herbivorous
词性：adjective
