n.|激励者，刺激物|Someone or something that excites.|The band was a real excitator for the crowd.  这支乐队真是让观众兴奋。|stimulant, impetus,  motivator|depressant, inhibitor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *excitator, from Latin excitat-, past participle stem of excitare "to rouse, excite," from ex- "out" + citare "to put in motion, summon, rouse."
