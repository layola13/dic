microfiche: /ˈmaɪkroʊfiːʃ/ (US) /ˈmaɪkrəʊfiːʃ/ (UK)| n.|缩微胶卷；微缩胶片|A flat sheet of film containing microphotographs of the pages of a newspaper, catalogue, or other document.|The library has a large collection of newspapers on microfiche.|Synonyms: microfilm (when referring to the film itself), microform (a broader term), fiche (informal)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "hard copy," "original document")|noun
