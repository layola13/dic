lorded:/ˈlɔːrdɪd/| v.|作威作福，盛气凌人|To act in a superior and domineering manner, often by flaunting power or authority.|He lorded his victory over his defeated opponent. 他对战败的对手耀武扬威。|Synonyms: domineer, swagger, boast, brag|Antonyms: submit, yield, obey|verb

lorded:/ˈlɔːrdɪd/| v.|以…为主，主要由…构成 (主要用于被动语态，be lorded (over/by))|To be dominated or characterized by something, often used in passive voice.|The landscape is lorded over by towering mountains.  这片景观以巍峨的高山为主。|Synonyms: dominated, characterized, governed|Antonyms: insignificant, minor, secondary|verb
