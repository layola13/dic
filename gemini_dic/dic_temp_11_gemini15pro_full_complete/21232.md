blogosphere:/ˈblɒɡəsfɪə(r)/ /ˈblɑːɡəsfɪr/
n.
博客圈；博客世界
The collective community of all blogs and bloggers.  The term is a portmanteau of the words blog and sphere.
The blogosphere has been buzzing with news of the celebrity wedding.  博客圈一直在热议这位名人的婚礼。
近义词:blogging community, online community
反义词:None
词性:Noun
