concessionnaire:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛər/| n. |特许经营商，经销商|A person or company that has been granted a concession, especially the right to sell a particular product or service in a specific area.|The car concessionnaire offers a wide range of models. 这家汽车经销商提供各种型号的汽车。|Synonyms: dealer, distributor, retailer, agent|Antonyms: producer, manufacturer|Noun

concessionnaire:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛər/| n. | concession holder, franchisee|A person or company to whom a concession has been granted.|The concessionnaire for the national park operates the gift shops and restaurants. 国家公园的特许经营商经营礼品店和餐馆。|Synonyms: licensee, operator|Antonyms: licensor, grantor|Noun
