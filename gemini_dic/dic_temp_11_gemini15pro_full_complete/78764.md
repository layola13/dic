gilding:/ˈɡɪldɪŋ/ | n. | 镀金；金箔；虚饰 | The act of applying gold leaf or gold paint to a surface; something that is gilded; a superficial or deceptive display of beauty or value. |  The gilding on the frame had begun to flake off.  画框上的镀金开始剥落了。| synonyms: gold plating, gilt, ornamentation, embellishment | antonyms: stripping, bareness, simplicity | noun

gild:/ɡɪld/ | vt. | 镀金；虚饰；使…光彩夺目 | To cover with a thin layer of gold; to give a deceptively attractive or valuable appearance to. |  The setting sun gilded the clouds. 落日给云彩镀上了一层金黄色。 | synonyms: adorn, embellish, enhance, glorify | antonyms: tarnish, dull, spoil, mar | verb
