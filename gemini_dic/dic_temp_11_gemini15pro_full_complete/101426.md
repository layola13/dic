iranic:/aɪˈrænɪk/ | adj.| 伊朗的；伊朗语的；印欧语系伊朗语族的 |Of or relating to Iran, its people, or their language.  Relating to any of the branch of the Indo-European language family that includes Persian, Pashto, Kurdish, and Ossetic. |例句：The museum has a fascinating collection of Iranic art.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的伊朗艺术品。

例句：Kurdish is an Iranic language. 库尔德语是一种伊朗语族的语言。|近义词：Persian, Iranian |反义词: (No direct antonym, could be non-Iranian/non-Persian depending on context) |词性: adjective
