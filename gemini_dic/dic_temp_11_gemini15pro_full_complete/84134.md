half-misunderstood:/ˌhæfˌmɪsʌndərˈstʊd/| adj.|半被误解的，不完全被理解的|Partially or incompletely understood; understood to some extent but not fully.  The meaning implies that while some aspects of something are grasped correctly, other parts remain unclear or misinterpreted.|Her intentions were half-misunderstood, leading to unnecessary conflict.|Near synonyms: partially understood, incompletely understood, partly grasped, misinterpreted in part |Antonyms: fully understood, completely grasped, perfectly clear, unambiguous|adjective
