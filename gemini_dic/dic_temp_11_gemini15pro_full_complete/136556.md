nonsacrifice:/ˌnɒnˈsækrɪfaɪs/
noun
非牺牲，不牺牲
The state of not involving or requiring sacrifice.
Maintaining a healthy work-life balance is a nonsacrifice for long-term productivity. 保持健康的工作与生活平衡对于长期生产力来说并非牺牲。
Negotiating a win-win solution was a nonsacrifice for both parties.  协商一个双赢的解决方案对双方来说都不是牺牲。
近义词:compromise, concession, accommodation
反义词:sacrifice, offering, immolation
词性:n.
