evaporating: /ɪˈvæpəreɪtɪŋ/
|v. |蒸发; 消失; 逐渐消散|The water is evaporating quickly in the sun. 水在阳光下迅速蒸发。|Present participle of evaporate|vanishing, disappearing, dissipating|forming, condensing
词性: 动词 (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin evaporatus, past participle of evaporare ("to disperse in vapor"), from e- ("out") + vaporare ("to emit vapor"), from vapor ("vapor").
