co-opted:/koʊˈɑːptɪd/
verb|吸收；拉拢；指派|To add (someone) to a group, committee, etc., by the votes of the existing members; to appropriate something for one's own use, especially without the owner's permission.|The rebel group co-opted several villagers into their ranks.|近义词:appoint, elect, select, incorporate, appropriate|反义词:exclude, reject, dismiss|v.

co-opted:/koʊˈɑːptɪd/
adjective|被吸收的；被拉拢的；被指派的|Having been added to a group, committee, etc., by the votes of the existing members.|The co-opted members of the committee brought a fresh perspective.|近义词:appointed, elected, selected, incorporated|反义词:excluded, rejected, dismissed|adj.
