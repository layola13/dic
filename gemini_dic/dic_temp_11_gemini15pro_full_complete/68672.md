farben:/ˈfɑːrbən/ (German pronunciation)| n. (plural)|颜色，色彩|The German word for "colors" or "paints."  It is the plural form of "Farbe."|Die Farben des Regenbogens sind wunderschön. The colors of the rainbow are beautiful.|近义词：(English) colors, hues, tints, shades (German) Farbtöne|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps consider "colorlessness")|词性:Noun (plural)

词源:
From Old High German *farawa, from Proto-Germanic *farwō. Cognate with Old English fealu (“pale, yellow, fallow”).  Related to Old English fearh (“pig”) (from the color of its bristles).


注意事项:
"Farben" is always plural in German.  The singular is "Farbe."
