broadener:/ˈbrɔːdənər/| n.|扩展者；拓宽者；增广者|Something that broadens something else, particularly something that broadens one's horizons or experiences.|Travel is a great broadener of the mind. 旅行能开阔眼界。|近义词：expander, widener, enhancer|反义词：restricter, limiter, narrower|词性:noun

Etymology:  From the verb "broaden," which comes from "broad" + "-en." "Broad" traces back to Old English "brād," meaning "wide" or "extensive."  The suffix "-en" forms verbs meaning "to make or become." The suffix "-er" denotes an agent or instrument that performs the action of the verb.
