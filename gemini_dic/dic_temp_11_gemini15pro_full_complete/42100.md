cotrustee:/koʊˈtrʌsˈtiː/| n.|共同受托人|A person who shares the responsibility of managing a trust with one or more other people.|My sister and I are cotrustees of our father's estate. 我和我的妹妹是我父亲遗产的共同受托人。|Synonyms: joint trustee, co-administrator|Antonyms: beneficiary|noun
