expedite:/ˈekspədaɪt/| v. | 加快；促进；迅速完成 |To make a process happen more quickly.|例句：We need to expedite the delivery of these goods. 我们需要加快这些货物的交付。|例句：The company took steps to expedite the production process. 公司采取措施加快生产过程。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, quicken, speed up|反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct|词性:动词

expedition:/ˌekspəˈdɪʃn/| n. | 远征；探险；考察；迅速 |A journey organized for a particular purpose.|例句：The expedition to the North Pole was a dangerous undertaking.  北极探险是一项危险的事业。|例句：He led an expedition into the Amazon rainforest. 他带领一支探险队进入亚马逊雨林。|近义词：journey, voyage, trek|反义词：delay, stay|词性:名词

expeditious:/ˌekspəˈdɪʃəs/| adj. |迅速的；敏捷的 |Done with speed and efficiency.|例句：The company provided expeditious service. 公司提供了快捷的服务。|例句：We need an expeditious solution to this problem. 我们需要一个快速的解决方案来解决这个问题。|近义词：quick, swift, prompt, efficient|反义词：slow, inefficient|词性:形容词
