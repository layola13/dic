aguish:/ˈeɪɡwɪʃ/| adj.|忽冷忽热的，疟疾似的，发冷的|Feeling or looking unwell, as if one has a fever; shivering.|例句：He felt aguish and weak after being caught in the rain. 他淋雨后感到忽冷忽热，浑身无力。

例句：She had an aguish look about her, suggesting she was coming down with something. 她看起来病恹恹的，像是要生病了。|近义词：feverish, shivery, chilly, unwell|反义词：healthy, well|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *aguese, *aguish, from ague +‎ -ish.  Ague itself comes from Old French ague, from feminine of agu (sharp, acute), from Latin acūtus (sharp).
