homothermous:/ˌhəʊməˈθɜːməs/ /ˌhoʊməˈθɜːrməs/| adj.|恒温的，温血的|Maintaining a relatively constant body temperature despite changes in the surrounding temperature.|例句：Humans and other mammals are homothermous animals. 人类和其他哺乳动物是恒温动物。

例句：Homothermous animals can regulate their internal body temperature. 恒温动物可以调节它们的内部体温。|近义词：endothermic, warm-blooded|反义词：poikilothermic, cold-blooded|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek *homoios* (ὁμοῖος, “similar”) + *thermē* (θέρμη, “heat”).
