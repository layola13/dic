gawp:/ɡɔːp/ | v. | 凝视，呆呆地看 | to stare openly and stupidly |  He just stood there gawping at the accident. 他只是站在那里呆呆地看着事故。| stare, gaze | glance, glimpse | verb

gawps:/ɡɔːps/ | v. (第三人称单数) | 凝视，呆呆地看 | Third person singular present tense form of "gawp" | She gawps at the television screen for hours. 她盯着电视屏幕看了好几个小时。| stares, gazes | glances, glimpses | verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *galpen, *gaupen, of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Old Norse gapa (“to gape”).
