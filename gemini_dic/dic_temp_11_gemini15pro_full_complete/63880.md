epilogize:/ˈɛpɪlɒdʒaɪz/ /ˈɛpɪˌloʊdʒaɪz/| v. |作结语；写结语；以结语结束|to add or write an epilogue to something; to conclude with an epilogue|He epilogized his speech with a call to action.  他以行动号召结束了他的演讲。

The author epilogized the novel with a brief chapter summarizing the characters' later lives. 作者用简短的一章总结了人物后来的生活，以此作为小说的结语。|近义词：conclude, summarize, recap|反义词: preface, introduce, begin |词性:动词
