knawel:/ˈnɔːl/ | n. | 绳子草，纳韦尔| A low-growing Eurasian plant (Scleranthus annuus) with small greenish flowers, often a troublesome weed in grain fields.  It is a member of the pink family (Caryophyllaceae). |  The knawel was spreading rapidly through the farmer's field. 绳子草在农夫的田地里迅速蔓延。 | Synonyms: German knotgrass, annual knawel | Antonyms: N/A | noun
