deambulation:/diːæmbjuˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 步行，走动 | The act of walking or moving about. |  The patient's deambulation was slow and unsteady after the surgery. 手术后，病人的步履缓慢且不稳。

After a week in bed, he was encouraged to resume deambulation.  卧床一周后，他被鼓励重新开始行走。


近义词: walking, ambulation, perambulation | 反义词: immobility, confinement, stillness | 词性: noun
