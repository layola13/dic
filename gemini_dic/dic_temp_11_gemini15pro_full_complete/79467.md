gloat:/ɡloʊt/| vi.| 幸灾乐祸，沾沾自喜 | to feel or express great pleasure or satisfaction because of your own success or good fortune, especially when someone else has been less successful or fortunate |  He gloated over his rival's failure. 他对竞争对手的失败幸灾乐祸。| synonyms: relish, revel, crow | antonyms: commiserate, sympathize | verb

gloat:/ɡloʊt/| n.| 幸灾乐祸，沾沾自喜 | a feeling of great pleasure or satisfaction because of your own success or good fortune, especially when someone else has been less successful or fortunate | He gave a gloat of satisfaction. 他露出了满足的得意神情。| synonyms: triumph, exultation | antonyms: commiseration, sympathy | noun
