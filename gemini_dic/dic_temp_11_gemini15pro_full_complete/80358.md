goldsmith:/ˈɡəʊldsmɪθ/ (US: /ˈɡoʊldˌsmɪθ/) | n. |金匠；金饰工匠| A person who makes or sells articles of gold. |例句：The goldsmith crafted a beautiful necklace. 金匠制作了一条美丽的项链。

例句：My grandfather was a skilled goldsmith. 我的祖父是一位技艺精湛的金匠。|近义词：jeweler, silversmith|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *goldsmiþ, from gold "gold" + smiþ "smith".  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gulþą (“gold”) and *smiþaz (“smith”).
