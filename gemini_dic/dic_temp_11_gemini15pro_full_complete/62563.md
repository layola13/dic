energising:/ˈenədʒaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈenərˌdʒaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|使人精力充沛的，充满活力的|Giving energy; invigorating.  Making someone feel more energetic.|例句：A brisk walk in the morning can be very energising. 早上轻快的散步会让人精神焕发。

例句：The music was energising and uplifting.  这音乐振奋人心，令人鼓舞。

近义词：invigorating, stimulating, refreshing, revitalising, uplifting|反义词：tiring, exhausting, draining, enervating|词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "energise" + "-ing" suffix.  "Energise" comes from "energy" + "-ise." "Energy" ultimately derives from the Greek "energeia," meaning "activity, operation."
