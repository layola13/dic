charterhouse:/ˈtʃɑːrtərhaʊs/| n. |修道院，特许学校|A Carthusian monastery, or a school founded by a charter, especially Charterhouse School in Godalming, Surrey, England.|He attended Charterhouse school. 他就读于查特豪斯公学。|Synonyms: monastery, school, public school|Antonyms: None|Noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *charterhous*, from Anglo-Norman *chartrehouse*, from Old French *chartreuse*, feminine of *chartreux* (“Carthusian”), from *Chartreuse*, the name of the original Carthusian monastery.
