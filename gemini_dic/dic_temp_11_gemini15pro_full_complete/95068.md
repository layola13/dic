inadvertence:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtəns/| n.|粗心大意，疏忽|Lack of attention; an unintentional oversight or mistake.|例句：Her inadvertence led to a serious error in the report. 她的疏忽导致报告中出现了一个严重的错误。

例句：The mistake was caused by pure inadvertence. 这个错误完全是由于粗心造成的。

|近义词：carelessness, negligence, oversight, inattention|反义词：carefulness, attention, diligence|词性：名词


inadvertent:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtənt/| adj.|非故意的，无意的|Not resulting from or achieved through deliberate planning.|例句：An inadvertent remark caused offense. 一句无心的话引起了冒犯。

例句：The damage was inadvertent, but still costly. 损害是无意的，但仍然代价高昂。


|近义词：unintentional, accidental, unplanned|反义词：intentional, deliberate, planned|词性：形容词
