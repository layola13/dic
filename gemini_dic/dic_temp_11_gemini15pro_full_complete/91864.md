hyperinflation:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪʃn/ | n. |恶性通货膨胀|A period of rapid and excessive price increases in an economy, typically at a rate exceeding 50% per month.|例句：The country's economy was crippled by hyperinflation.  这个国家的经济因恶性通货膨胀而瘫痪。

例句：Hyperinflation made it difficult for people to afford basic necessities. 恶性通货膨胀使人们难以负担基本生活必需品。|近义词：runaway inflation, galloping inflation|反义词:deflation, disinflation|词性:名词
