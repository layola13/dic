overrestricted:/ˌōvərriˈstrɪktɪd/ | adj. | 过度限制的，限制过严的 |  Subject to too many or excessively limiting rules or regulations. | The overrestricted diet made the child irritable and malnourished.  这个限制过严的饮食使得孩子烦躁且营养不良。|  The company's overrestricted internet policy frustrated employees. 公司限制过严的互联网政策让员工感到沮丧。 | synonyms: excessively restricted, overly regulated, severely limited,  too confined | antonyms: unrestricted, unregulated, free, liberal, permissive | adjective
