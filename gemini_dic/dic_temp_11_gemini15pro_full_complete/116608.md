matrimonies:/ˈmætrɪmoʊniz/
noun
婚姻（复数）
The state of being married; marriage.  Usually used in a formal or legal context, often implying multiple marriages.
例句：The king's numerous matrimonies caused political instability. 国王的多次婚姻造成了政治动荡。

例句：The records documented the various matrimonies and divorces of the town's residents. 记录记载了该镇居民的各种婚姻和离婚。

近义词：marriages, wedlocks
反义词：divorces, separations
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *matrimonie*, from Old French *matrimoine*, from Latin *matrimonium* (“marriage, wedlock”), from *māter* (“mother”).
