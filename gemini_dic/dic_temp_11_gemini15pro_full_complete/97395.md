infrastructure:/ˈɪnfrəˌstrʌktʃər/| n. |基础设施，基础建设|The basic physical and organizational structures and facilities (e.g. buildings, roads, power supplies) needed for the operation of a society or enterprise.|The country needs to invest heavily in its infrastructure to support economic growth. 这个国家需要大力投资基础设施建设以支持经济增长。|近义词：foundation, base, groundwork|反义词: superstructure|词性: 名词
