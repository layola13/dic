dahabeah:/ˌdɑːhəˈbiːə/ | n. | Dahabeya (一种尼罗河上的帆船) | A lateen-rigged sailing vessel used on the Nile River in Egypt, traditionally used for leisurely trips.  It is a smaller and more luxurious version of a felucca. |  We sailed down the Nile on a beautiful dahabiya, enjoying the stunning scenery. 我们乘坐一艘漂亮的dahabeya沿着尼罗河航行，欣赏着迷人的景色。 | Example 2: The dahabiya offered comfortable cabins and delicious meals, making our journey truly unforgettable.  这艘dahabeya提供了舒适的船舱和美味的餐点，使我们的旅程真正难忘。| 近义词: felucca, houseboat | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑speedboat快艇，暗示速度和现代性) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Arabic دَهَبِيَّة‎ (dahbīyah), from دَهَب (dahab, “gold”).  Originally referred to gilded pleasure boats. 源于阿拉伯语 دَهَبِيَّة‎ (dahbīyah)，来自 دَهَب (dahab, “黄金”)。最初指的是镀金的游船。
