diseasement:/dɪˈziːzmənt/ | n. |疾病;患病(状态);不安;不适|The state of being diseased or afflicted with a disease; uneasiness, discomfort.  This word is archaic and rarely used. | He suffered from a general diseasement of the body. 他遭受着全身性的疾病。| 近义词: illness, sickness, malady, ailment | 反义词: health, wellness | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From Middle English *diseysement, from Old French desaisement, from des- (dis-) + aise (“ease, comfort”).
