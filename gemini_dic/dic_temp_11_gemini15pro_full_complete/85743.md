hawkish:/ˈhɔːkɪʃ/ | adj. |鹰派的，强硬的|Favoring aggressive policies, especially in foreign affairs.  Like a hawk; predatory. |例句：The senator's hawkish stance on defense spending concerned many voters.  参议员在国防开支上的强硬立场让许多选民感到担忧。

例句：His hawkish views on foreign policy made him unpopular with some. 他在外交政策上的鹰派观点让他不受一些人的欢迎。|近义词：aggressive, belligerent, hard-line |反义词：dovish, peaceful, conciliatory |词性:adjective
词源->
From hawk + -ish.  Relating to the characteristics of a hawk, known for its predatory nature.
