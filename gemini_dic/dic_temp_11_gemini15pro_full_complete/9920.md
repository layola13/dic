aperiodic:/ˌeɪpɪəriˈɒdɪk/ /ˌeɪpɪriˈɑːdɪk/| adj.|非周期的，无周期的|Occurring at irregular intervals; not periodic.  Not characterized by predictable cycles or regular intervals.|The aperiodic nature of the earthquakes makes them difficult to predict.|Synonyms: irregular, nonperiodic, nonsystematic, erratic, sporadic, intermittent|Antonyms: periodic, regular, cyclic, systematic, rhythmic|adjective
