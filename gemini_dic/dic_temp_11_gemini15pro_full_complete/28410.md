carbonators:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪtərz/
noun
碳酸化器
A device that adds carbon dioxide to beverages.
The bartender used a carbonator to make sparkling water.  调酒师用碳酸化器制作苏打水。
synonyms: carbonation system, soda maker
antonyms: decarbonator
