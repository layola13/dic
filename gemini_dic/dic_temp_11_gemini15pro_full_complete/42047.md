cosupreme:/kəʊˈsuːpriːm/ | adj. | 至高无上的，共同至上的 |  A blend of "co-" (meaning together, joint) and "supreme" (meaning highest in rank or authority). cosupreme:/kəʊˈsuːpriːm/ | n. | 共同拥有最高权威的人或实体 | A person or entity sharing supreme power or authority. | The two cosupremes ruled the land together. 两位共同拥有最高权威的人一起统治这片土地。 | joint ruler, co-regent | subordinate | noun
