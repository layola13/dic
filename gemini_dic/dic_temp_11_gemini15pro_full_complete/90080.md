hostess:/ˈhəʊstɪs (UK) /ˈhəʊstəs (US)/ | n. |女主人，女主持人，女服务员|A woman who receives or entertains guests in her own home or elsewhere.  A woman employed in a restaurant, club, etc. to welcome and seat patrons and to assist them with other services. |例句：The hostess greeted us warmly at the door. 女主人在门口热情地迎接我们。

The hostess showed us to our table. 女服务员把我们带到我们的桌子旁。|近义词：host, server, waitress |反义词:guest |词性:noun
