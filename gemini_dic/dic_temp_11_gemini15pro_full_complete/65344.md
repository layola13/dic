eulogic: /juːˈlɒdʒɪk/ (US) /juːˈlɒdʒɪk/ (UK)
adjective
赞美的，颂扬的
Praising someone or something highly, especially in a formal speech.
The eulogic speech moved many in the audience to tears.  这位颂扬性的演讲使许多观众感动得流下了眼泪。
The professor delivered a eulogic oration at the retirement ceremony. 教授在退休仪式上发表了颂扬性的演讲。
Synonyms: laudatory, commendatory, complimentary, praising
Antonyms: critical, derogatory, defamatory
词性: adjective
