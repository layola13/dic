noncreditableness:/ˌnɒnˈkrɛdɪtəblnəs/
n.
不可信，不足信
lack of credibility; the quality of being unbelievable or untrustworthy.
His noncreditableness made it difficult for anyone to believe his story. 他的不可信使得很难有人相信他的故事。
近义词:unbelievability, implausibility, incredibility
反义词:credibility, believability, plausibility
词性:名词
