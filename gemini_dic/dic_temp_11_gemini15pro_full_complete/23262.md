bowstringing:/ˈboʊˌstrɪŋɪŋ/
n.
弓弦的制作或安装; 用弓弦勒死;
The act of stringing a bow; strangulation with a bowstring.
The bowstringing was expertly done, ensuring the bow was ready for use. 弓弦的安装做得非常专业，确保弓箭可以使用。
The museum displayed artifacts related to ancient methods of bowstringing.  博物馆展出了与古代弓弦制作方法相关的文物。
近义词: stringing
反义词: unstringing
词性: 名词

bowstringing:/ˈboʊˌstrɪŋɪŋ/
v.
用弓弦勒死;
To strangle with a bowstring.
The captive was bowstringed at dawn.  俘虏在黎明时被弓弦勒死了。
Historical accounts detail numerous instances of execution by bowstringing. 历史记载详细描述了大量用弓弦处决的例子。
近义词: strangling, garroting
反义词: releasing
词性: 动词
