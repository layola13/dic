amusements:/əˈmjuːzmənts/
n.
娱乐活动，消遣，娱乐设施
activities that are intended to entertain and please people; a way of enjoying yourself when you are not working
The park offers a variety of amusements for children and adults.  这个公园为儿童和成人提供各种娱乐活动。
The hotel boasts a range of amusements, including a swimming pool and a games room. 这家酒店拥有一系列娱乐设施，包括游泳池和游戏室。
synonyms: entertainments, diversions, recreations, pastimes
antonyms: work, labor, toil
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
