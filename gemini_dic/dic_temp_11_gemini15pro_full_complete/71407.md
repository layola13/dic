flagstoned:/ˈflæɡˌstəʊnd/ (UK) /ˈflæɡˌstoʊnd/ (US)| adj.|铺着石板的|Covered or paved with flagstones (flat, rectangular stones).|The courtyard was flagstoned, cool and inviting. 院子铺着石板，凉爽宜人。|The flagstoned patio was perfect for summer evenings. 石板露台是夏日夜晚的完美场所。|近义词： paved, cobbled, stoned|反义词: unpaved, dirt|词性: adjective
