disseminated:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪtɪd/
|adj.|传播的，散布的，弥漫的|Spread widely, distributed over a large area. |The disease is disseminated through contaminated water. 这种疾病通过受污染的水传播。|Synonyms: spread, distributed, dispersed, scattered, diffused|Antonyms: concentrated, localized, confined|adjective

disseminate:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt/
|v.|传播，散布，散播|To spread or distribute something, especially information, widely.|The organization disseminates information about environmental issues. 该组织传播有关环境问题的信息。|Synonyms: spread, distribute, circulate, propagate, broadcast|Antonyms: suppress, withhold, conceal|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin disseminatus, past participle of disseminare "to sow or scatter seed," from dis- "widely" + seminare "to sow," from semen "seed."
