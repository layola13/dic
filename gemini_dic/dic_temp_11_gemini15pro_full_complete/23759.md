bravi:/ˈbrɑːviː/ |interj.|好！妙！|Used to express approval, especially at a performance.  It is the plural of "bravo".|The audience shouted "Bravi!" at the end of the opera.|近义词: excellent, well done, encore|反义词: boo, hiss|词性: interjection
Etymology: Italian, plural of *bravo*, from Latin *bravus* "wild, savage" (source of French *brave*)
