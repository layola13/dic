jumpiest:/ˈdʒʌmpiɪst/| adj.|最神经质的，最容易激动的|Superlative form of jumpy: most jumpy.  Indicates the highest degree of nervousness or excitability.|That chihuahua is the jumpiest dog I've ever met. 那只吉娃娃是我见过的最神经质的狗。|The jumpiest kid in the class always startled at loud noises. 班上最容易激动的孩子总是被大声的噪音吓到。|近义词：nerviest, edgiest, twitchiest|反义词：calmest, coolest, most relaxed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From jumpy + -est.  "Jumpy" originates from "jump," which dates back to the 14th century and is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.  The "-est" suffix is a superlative ending.
