extrude:/ɪkˈstruːd/ /ɪkˈstruːd/ |v.|挤出，压出，突出，逐出|To push or force something soft or semi-liquid out of something else.|The machine extrudes plastic into various shapes. 这台机器将塑料挤压成各种形状。|近义词：force out, squeeze out, press out|反义词: intrude, insert|词性: 动词

extruded:/ɪkˈstruːdɪd/ /ɪkˈstruːdɪd/ |adj.|挤出的，压出的，突出的|Having been pushed or forced out of something.|The extruded plastic is then cooled and cut. 挤压出的塑料然后被冷却和切割。|近义词：pressed, squeezed, forced out|反义词: inserted, intruded|词性: 形容词
