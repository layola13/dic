anticreationists:/ˌæntiˌkriːˈeɪʃənɪsts/
n.
反创造论者
People who oppose or reject the belief in creationism, especially the belief that the universe and life on Earth were created by a supernatural being or god as described in religious texts.
Many scientists and educators are anticreationists and advocate for the teaching of evolution in schools.
opponents of creationism, evolutionists
