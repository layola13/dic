ETYMOLOGY->
From Hungarian *hunkó ‘crude, clumsy fellow,’ from hun ‘Hun.’  Originally referred to a Hungarian laborer, then generalized to any strong, muscular man. The meaning shifted to 'attractive' in the mid-20th century.
