funereally:/fjuːˈnɪəriəli/
adverb
丧葬地，凄凉地
In a manner suggestive of a funeral; gloomily or mournfully.
The procession moved funereally through the streets.  队伍沉丧地穿过街道。
The rain fell funereally all day.  雨凄凉地下了一整天。
Synonyms:  somberly, mournfully, gloomily, dolefully, sadly
Antonyms:  joyfully, cheerfully, happily
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *funerealis, from Latin funus (“funeral”).
