multigraph:/ˈmʌltɪɡræf/| n. |多重图|A graph in which two vertices may be connected by more than one edge.  A multigraph can contain loops. |The network diagram was represented as a multigraph, showing multiple connections between servers.|近义词：pseudograph|反义词: simple graph|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- + graph.  "Multi-" comes from Latin multus "much, many". "Graph" comes from Greek gráphō "to write, scratch, draw."
