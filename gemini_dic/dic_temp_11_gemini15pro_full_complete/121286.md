misfeign:/ˌmɪsˈfeɪn/
verb
假装；伪装；装作
To feign something incorrectly or inappropriately.  To pretend to feel a particular emotion insincerely.
He misfeigned indifference, but his trembling hands betrayed his anxiety. 他假装不在乎，但他颤抖的双手暴露了他的焦虑。
近义词:pretend, simulate, feign, affect
反义词:be genuine, be sincere
词性:v.
