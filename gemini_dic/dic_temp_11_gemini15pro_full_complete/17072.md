batiked:/bæˈtiːkt/
adjective
蜡染的；用蜡染法印染的
Decorated with a design made by waxing parts of a fabric before dyeing it, so that the waxed parts resist the dye.
The artist displayed her batiked scarves and wall hangings. 艺术家展示了她的蜡染围巾和壁挂。
synonyms: dyed, printed, patterned, decorated
antonyms: plain, undyed, unpatterned
词性: adjective
