depreciating:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
贬值的；跌价的
decreasing in value over a period of time.
例句：The value of the car is depreciating rapidly.  这辆车的价值正在迅速贬值。

例句：Depreciating assets can cause financial difficulties. 贬值的资产会导致财务困难。

近义词：declining, reducing, lessening, diminishing, falling, sinking, lowering, weakening, cheapening
反义词：appreciating, increasing, rising
词性：adjective
