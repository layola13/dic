axially:/ˈækʃiəli/| adv.|轴地；沿轴线地|Along or around an axis; in an axial direction. |The force is applied axially. 力是沿轴线方向施加的。|The turbine rotates axially. 涡轮机沿轴旋转。|近义词：longitudinally, lengthwise|反义词：radially, transversely|词性:副词

axially:/ˈækʃiəli/| adj.|轴向的|Relating to or situated around an axis. |An axial skeleton includes the skull, vertebral column, and rib cage.  中轴骨骼包括头骨、脊柱和肋骨。|The axial symmetry of the flower is striking.  这朵花的轴对称引人注目。|近义词：longitudinal, lengthwise|反义词：radial, transversely|词性:形容词
