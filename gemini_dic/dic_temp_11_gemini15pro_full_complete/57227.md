downgrader:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪdər/| n.|降级者，降级程序，降级软件|A person or thing that downgrades something, especially software.|The software downgrader allows you to revert to an older version of the operating system. 这个软件降级程序允许你恢复到旧版本的操作系统。|synonyms: reducer, diminisher, detractor |antonyms: upgrader, enhancer, improver|noun
downgrade:/ˌdaʊnˈɡreɪd/| v.  n.|降级，降低，贬低|To reduce to a lower grade, rank, or level of importance.|The airline downgraded me from business class to economy. 航空公司把我从商务舱降级到经济舱.  |synonyms: reduce, lower, demote |antonyms: upgrade, promote, elevate|verb, noun
