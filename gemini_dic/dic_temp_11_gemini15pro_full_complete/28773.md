carnous:/ˈkɑːrnəs/| adj.|肉的；似肉的；肉色的|Resembling flesh in appearance, texture, or color.  Having a fleshy consistency.|The fungus had a carnous texture, similar to a mushroom. 这种真菌的质地很像肉，类似于蘑菇。|Synonyms: fleshy, meaty, pulpy|Antonyms: lean, thin, bony|Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *carnosus*, from *caro* ("flesh")
