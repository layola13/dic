overclassified: /ˌoʊvərˈklæsɪfaɪd/
adjective|过度分类的|Classified at too high a level of security or sensitivity.|The documents were overclassified, making it difficult for researchers to access them. 这些文件被过度分类，使研究人员难以获取。|例句：The report was overclassified, hindering public access to vital information.  这份报告被过度分类，阻碍了公众获取重要信息。

例句：Much of the information was needlessly overclassified, burying important data under layers of bureaucracy. 很多信息被不必要地过度分类，将重要数据掩埋在官僚主义的层层之下。|近义词：overprotected, restricted, overly sensitive|反义词：underclassified, declassified, public|词性:adjective
