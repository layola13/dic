boustrophedon:/ˌbuːstrəˈfiːdən/ | adj. n. |  迴旋式，往复式 |  Written or printed in alternating lines from right to left and from left to right, as in ancient Greek inscriptions.  A pattern resembling the movement of an ox plowing a field. | The ancient inscription was written in boustrophedon. 这段古代铭文是以迴旋式书写的。 | synonyms:  back-and-forth, alternating, serpentine | antonyms: unidirectional, linear | adjective, noun

boustrophedonic:/ˌbuːstrəˈfiːdɒnɪk/ | adj. | 迴旋式的，往复式的 | Relating to or written in boustrophedon. |  The boustrophedonic script was difficult to decipher.  这种迴旋式文字难以解读。 | synonyms:  back-and-forth, alternating, serpentine | antonyms: unidirectional, linear | adjective
