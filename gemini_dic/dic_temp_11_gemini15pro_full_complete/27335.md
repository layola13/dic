camarilla:/ˌkæməˈrɪlə/ | n. | 小集团，派系，私党 | A group of unofficial advisors, often with a negative connotation, who influence a powerful person, such as a ruler or politician. | The president’s camarilla had a reputation for corruption and backroom deals. 总统的小集团以腐败和幕后交易而闻名。|  The king’s camarilla was known for its extravagance and influence over policy. 国王的私党以其奢侈和对政策的影响而闻名。 |  clique, cabal, faction |  opposition, opponents | noun
