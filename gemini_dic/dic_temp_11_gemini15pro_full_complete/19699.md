biliferous:/ˈbɪlɪfərəs/
adjective
产胆汁的，分泌胆汁的
Producing or secreting bile.
Bile is a fluid that is made and released by the liver and stored in the gallbladder.  Biliferous ducts carry bile.
The liver is a biliferous organ.  肝脏是一个产胆汁的器官。
Damage to the biliferous system can cause serious health problems. 胆汁分泌系统的损害会导致严重的健康问题。
Synonyms: bile-producing, bile-secreting, choleretic
Antonyms:  None.
词性: adjective
