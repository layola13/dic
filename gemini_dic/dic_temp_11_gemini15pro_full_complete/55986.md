dynamic:/daɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. n. |动态的，有活力的；动力学；动态 | characterized by constant change, activity, or progress |  例句：The stock market is a dynamic environment. 股票市场是一个动态的环境。| 近义词：active, energetic, forceful, vigorous | 反义词：static, inert, passive | 词性: 形容词

dynamic:/daɪˈnæmɪk/ | n. | 动态，动力 |  a force that stimulates change or progress within a system or process | 例句：The dynamics of the team were affected by the new manager. 新经理影响了团队的活力。| 近义词：force, power, energy | 反义词：stasis, inertia | 词性: 名词
