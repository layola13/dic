diskless:/ˈdɪskləs/| adj.|无盘的|Lacking a disk drive; referring to a computer or other device that relies on a network connection to access storage. |例句：A diskless workstation boots from a network server. 一个无盘工作站从网络服务器启动。

例句：Diskless network booting can simplify system administration. 无盘网络启动可以简化系统管理。|近义词：network-booted, thin client|反义词:disk-based|词性:形容词
