greeting:/ˈɡriːtɪŋ/| n.|问候，致敬，祝贺|An act of communication in which one person acknowledges the presence of another. |Example: He offered a friendly greeting. 他友好的打了个招呼。|Synonym: salutation, welcome, hello|Antonym: farewell, goodbye|noun

greeting:/ˈɡriːtɪŋ/| n.|（复数）greetings|祝词，贺词|A message of good wishes. |Example: Season's greetings! 节日快乐！|Synonym: regards, compliments, best wishes|Antonym: curse, malediction|noun
