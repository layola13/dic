concessionaire:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛər/ /kənˌseʃəˈner/| n.|特许经营者， concession holder|A person or company to whom a concession has been granted, especially to operate a business in a particular place.|The concessionaire for the national park's restaurants has changed this year. 今年国家公园餐厅的特许经营者换了。|synonyms: franchisee, licensee, operator|antonyms: grantor, licensor|noun

concessionaire:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛər/ /kənˌseʃəˈner/| n.|让步者，妥协者|Someone who makes a concession.|The concessionaire finally agreed to lower the price. 让步者最终同意降低价格。|synonyms: compromiser, negotiator|antonyms: hardliner|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *concessionnaire*, from concession + -aire. Ultimately from Latin concedere (to yield, concede).
