extended:/ɪkˈstendɪd/ /ɛkˈstɛndɪd/| adj.|延长的，扩展的，伸展的|prolonged, increased in length, scope, or duration.  Derived from the verb "extend," meaning to stretch out or make longer.|The hotel offered an extended stay discount.  这家酒店提供延住折扣。|近义词：lengthy, prolonged, expanded, comprehensive|反义词：short, brief, limited, contracted|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin "extendere,"  "ex-" (out) + "tendere" (to stretch).
