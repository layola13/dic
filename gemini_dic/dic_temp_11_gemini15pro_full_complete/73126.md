for-the-most-part:/fɔːr ðə moʊst pɑːrt/| adv.|大体上，通常，多半|Mostly; in general; usually.  It indicates that something is true in general, but there may be exceptions.|The food at the restaurant was, for the most part, quite good, though the dessert was disappointing. 这家餐馆的食物总体来说还不错，虽然甜点令人失望。|mostly, generally, largely, mainly, chiefly|rarely, seldom|adverb
