de-stratified:/diːˈstrætɪfaɪd/
adjective
去分层的，消除等级的
Removed the stratification from; made less stratified.
The researchers de-stratified the sample by age and gender to ensure a representative analysis. 研究人员按年龄和性别对样本进行去分层，以确保分析具有代表性。
The government implemented policies to de-stratify access to education. 政府实施了消除教育机会分层的政策。
近义词:homogenized, integrated, mixed
反义词:stratified, layered, hierarchical
词性:adjective

词源:
"de-" (prefix): meaning "remove", "reverse", "reduce", or "away from". From Latin.
"stratified" (verb): from "stratum" (Latin) meaning "layer" + "-fied" (suffix) meaning "to make or cause to be."
