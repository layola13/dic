nonprocreation:/ˌnɒnproʊkriˈeɪʃən/
|n.| 不生育，不生殖 |The ethical considerations of nonprocreation are becoming increasingly complex. 不生育的伦理考量正变得日益复杂。| Nonprocreation can be a conscious choice for various reasons.  不生育可以是出于各种原因的有意识的选择。| 近义词：childlessness, non-reproduction | 反义词：procreation, reproduction | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  not; opposite of. From Latin *non.
procreation (noun): the act of producing offspring. From Late Middle English: from late Latin procreatio(n-), from procreare ‘beget’, from pro- ‘forth’ + creare ‘create’.
