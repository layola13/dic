girlishness:/ˈɡɜːrlɪʃnəs/ /ˈɡɝːlɪʃnəs/| n. |少女气质；女孩子气；幼稚|The quality of being girlish;  childlike or immature behavior, especially in a woman.|She retained a certain girlishness even in her forties. 她即使到了四十多岁，仍然保留着某种少女气质。|近义词：femininity, immaturity, childishness|反义词：maturity, sophistication|词性:名词

Etymology: From girl + -ish + -ness. "girl" comes from Middle English *girle*, of uncertain origin. "-ish" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "of the nature of, resembling," from Old English -isc. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being," from Old English -nes.
