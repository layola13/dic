entertained:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnd/ | v. | 娱乐；招待；怀有；考虑 |  To provide amusement or enjoyment for someone.  |  例句：
The comedian entertained the audience with his jokes. = 那位喜剧演员用他的笑话娱乐了观众。

We entertained our guests with a barbecue in the garden. = 我们在花园里用烧烤招待了客人。 | 近义词：amused, diverted, hosted, considered | 反义词：bored, wearied, ignored, dismissed | 词性: 动词
