didascalic:/ˌdɪdəˈskælɪk/
adjective
教诲的，说教的
Intended to teach; having the tone or character of a teacher.
The novel's didactic tone alienated some readers who felt they were being preached to. 这部小说的教诲语气疏远了一些读者，他们觉得是在被说教。
didactic
instructive, educational, moralizing, preachy
entertaining, engaging


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek didaktikos "apt at teaching," from didaskein "to teach."
