clip-clop:/klɪpˈklɒp/ (UK), /klɪpˈklɑːp/ (US)| n. & vi.|（马蹄）得得声，（发出）得得声|The sound of a horse's hooves striking a hard surface.|The horse clip-clopped down the lane. 马沿着小路发出得得的马蹄声。|近义词：clatter, trot, stamp|反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound.  Consider "silence" if contrasting the presence of the sound with its absence.)|noun, verb
