invigilancy:/ɪnˈvɪdʒɪlənsi/| n.|监考，监督，警戒|The state of being invigilant; watchfulness, close observation.|The invigilancy of the proctors ensured no cheating occurred during the exam.  监考人员的监督确保了考试期间没有作弊行为发生。|近义词：supervision, surveillance, oversight|反义词:negligence, carelessness|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin invigilantia, from invigilans (present participle of invigilare ‘watch over’), from in- ‘upon, towards’ + vigilare ‘keep watch’.
