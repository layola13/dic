localness:/ˈloʊkəlnəs/
n.
地方性；局部性；本地化程度
The quality or state of being local; attachment or restriction to a particular place.
The localness of the produce is a selling point.  这种产品的本地性是一个卖点。
locality, regionality
globalness, universality
noun
