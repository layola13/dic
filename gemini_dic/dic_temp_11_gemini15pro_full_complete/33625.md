cindered:/ˈsɪndərd/| adj.|被煤渣覆盖的，烧成灰烬的|Covered with cinders or ashes; reduced to ashes.|The cindered remains of the building stood as a stark reminder of the fire.|近义词：ashed, burnt, charred|反义词：unburnt, intact|词性:形容词
词源: From "cinder" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Cinder" comes from Old English "sinder," from Proto-Germanic "*sindron," related to Old High German "sintar."
