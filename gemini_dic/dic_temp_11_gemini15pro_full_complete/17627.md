bebopper:/ˈbiːbɒpə(r)/ (US) /ˈbiːbɒpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |比波普乐迷，比波普演奏者|A devotee or performer of bebop, a style of jazz characterized by complex harmonies, fast tempos, and virtuosic improvisation.|Charlie Parker was a legendary bebopper. 查理·帕克是一位传奇的比波普乐手。|Synonym: jazz musician, bebop artist|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional jazz musician")|Noun
