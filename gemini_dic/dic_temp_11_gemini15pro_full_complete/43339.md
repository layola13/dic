crampiest:/ˈkræmpiɪst/| adj.|最拥挤的，最狭窄的，最局促的|Superlative form of "crampy," meaning extremely confined and uncomfortable, causing cramps.|The attic room was the crampiest I'd ever seen.  阁楼房间是我见过的最狭窄的。|The crampiest airplane seats make long flights unbearable. 最拥挤的飞机座位让长途飞行难以忍受。|近义词：tightest, smallest, most confined, most uncomfortable|反义词：roomiest, largest, most spacious, most comfortable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From "cramp" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Cramp" ultimately comes from the Proto-Germanic *kramp- (meaning "crooked, bent").
