nonethnological: /ˌnɒnˌɛθnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非民族学的
Not pertaining to ethnology; not related to the study of the characteristics of different peoples and the relationships between them.
The museum's new exhibit featured nonethnological artifacts, focusing instead on geological formations.  博物馆的新展览以非民族学文物为特色，重点是地质构造。
His research interests are primarily nonethnological, concentrating on social psychology. 他的研究兴趣主要是非民族学的，专注于社会心理学。
Near synonyms: non-anthropological, non-cultural
Antonyms: ethnological, anthropological, cultural
word type: adjective
