hemingway:/ˈhemɪŋweɪ/| n.|海明威（美国作家）|Ernest Miller Hemingway (1899-1961), an American novelist, short story writer, and journalist, known for his concise and evocative prose style and his adventurous lifestyle. He won the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1954. |Example: Hemingway's "The Old Man and the Sea" is a classic of American literature.  海明威的《老人与海》是美国文学的经典之作。|Synonym:  novelist, writer, author|Antonym: N/A|Noun
