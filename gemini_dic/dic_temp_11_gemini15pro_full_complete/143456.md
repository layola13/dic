outfrothed:/ˌaʊtˈfrɒθd/ /ˌaʊtˈfrɑːθd/
verb
吐出泡沫，胜过(在起泡沫方面)
outdo in frothing; surpass in the production of froth.  The beer outfrothed from the tap. 啤酒从龙头里涌出泡沫。
The cappuccino machine outfrothed all the others at the coffee expo.  这台卡布奇诺机在咖啡博览会上比所有其他机器产生的泡沫都多。
近义词:foam, bubble, effervesce
反义词:subside, flatten
