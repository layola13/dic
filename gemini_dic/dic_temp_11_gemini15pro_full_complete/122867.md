mollifier:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪə(r)/ (US) /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪər/| n.|缓和剂；调解人；润肤剂|something that softens or soothes; a person who appeases or conciliates; an emollient|例句：The negotiator acted as a mollifier in the heated dispute.  谈判代表在激烈的争端中充当了调解人。

例句：The cream acts as a mollifier for dry skin.  这种乳霜可以滋润干燥的皮肤。|近义词：mediator, conciliator, softener, emollient, soother|反义词：irritant, agitator, exacerbator|词性:noun

mollifiers:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪəz/ (US) /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪərz/| n. (plural)|缓和剂；调解人；润肤剂（复数）|plural form of "mollifier"|例句：Several mollifiers were used in the experiment to reduce friction.  实验中使用了多种润滑剂来减少摩擦。

例句：The diplomats acted as mollifiers between the warring nations.  外交官们在交战国之间充当了调解人。|近义词：mediators, conciliators, softeners, emollients, soothers|反义词：irritants, agitators, exacerbators|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mollificator, from Latin mollificare "to soften," from mollis "soft" + -ficare  (from facere "to make").
