analogically:/ˌænəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 类推地，比拟地 |  In a way that involves using an analogy; by way of analogy.  It means reasoning or explaining something by comparing it to something else that is similar. |  We can understand the structure of an atom analogically by comparing it to the solar system.  我们可以通过将原子的结构比作太阳系来类推理解它。|  similarly, comparatively, correspondingly | literally, directly | adverb
