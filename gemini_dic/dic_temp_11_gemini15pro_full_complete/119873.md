miliolite:/ˈmɪlioʊˌlaɪt/| n.|鲕粒岩；鲕状岩|A sedimentary rock consisting of small spherical grains of calcium carbonate coated with concentric layers, formed by the action of waves or currents.|The cliffs are composed primarily of miliolite, a rock formed from the shells of tiny marine organisms.|近义词:oolite, roe stone|反义词:|noun
