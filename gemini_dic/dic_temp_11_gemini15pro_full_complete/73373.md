foregrounded:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊndɪd/
adj.
突出的，被强调的
Brought to the forefront; made prominent or emphasized.
The artist foregrounded the woman's face in the portrait.  艺术家在肖像画中突出了女人的脸。
foregrounded:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊndɪd/
verb (past participle)
突出，强调
To bring something to the forefront; to make something prominent or emphasized.
The issues of poverty and inequality were foregrounded in the documentary.  这部纪录片突出了贫困和不平等的问题。

近义词:emphasized, highlighted, prominent, salient
反义词:backgrounded, obscured, minimized

词性:adjective, past participle of verb "foreground"


词源: From the noun "foreground" + "-ed." "Foreground" itself comes from "fore-" (meaning "front, before") + "ground" (meaning "surface, area").
