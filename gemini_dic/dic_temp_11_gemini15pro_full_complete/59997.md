editorialising:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzɪŋ/
n.|社论化，发表评论|The act of expressing opinions rather than just reporting facts, often in a way that tries to influence the reader or viewer.|His reporting was marred by excessive editorialising.|Synonyms: commenting, opining, pontificating|Antonyms: reporting, stating facts|noun

editorialise:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlaɪz/
v.|发表社论，发表评论|To express opinions rather than just report facts, often in a way that tries to influence the reader or viewer.|The news anchor was criticized for editorialising during the broadcast.|Synonyms: comment, opine, pontificate|Antonyms: report, state facts|verb
