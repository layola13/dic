gargoyled:/ˈɡɑːrɡɔɪld/ |adj.|  怪兽状的，滴水嘴兽状的 | Resembling or ornamented with gargoyles.  The building was gargoyled with grotesque figures. 这栋建筑装饰着怪诞的滴水嘴兽。| 近义词: grotesque, monstrous | 反义词: plain, simple | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "gargoyle" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Gargoyle" comes from Old French "gargouille" meaning "throat," related to the verb "gargouiller" meaning "to gurgle," ultimately from Latin "gurgulio" meaning "gullet, windpipe."
