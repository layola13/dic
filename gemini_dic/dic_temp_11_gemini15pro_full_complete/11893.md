arrogator: /ˈærəɡeɪtər/ | n. | 擅自占有者，僭越者 |  Someone who appropriates something to themselves without justification, especially power, authority, or property. | He acted as an arrogator, seizing control of the company despite having no legal right. 他像个僭越者，尽管没有合法权利，却夺取了公司的控制权。 |  usurper, appropriator |  renouncer, abdicator | noun

arrogate: /ˈærəɡeɪt/ | v. | 擅自占有，僭越 | To claim or seize without justification; to take or claim something without having the right to do so. |  She arrogated the leadership role, even though it had been assigned to someone else.  她擅自担任了领导角色，即使这个角色已经分配给别人了。 The king arrogated the right to dissolve parliament. 国王僭越了解散议会的权力。 |  usurp, appropriate, seize, assume | renounce, relinquish, abdicate | verb
