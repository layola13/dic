noncommodiousness:/ˌnɒnkəˈməʊdiəsnəs/
| n. |不宽敞，不方便|Lack of commodiousness; the state of being inconvenient or not spacious.|The noncommodiousness of the apartment made it difficult to live comfortably.  这套公寓的不宽敞使得舒适地生活变得困难。|Example: The noncommodiousness of the small car made the long journey uncomfortable. 这辆小汽车的狭小使得长途旅行很不舒服。|Synonyms:  inconvenience, crampedness, lack of space|Antonyms: spaciousness, commodiousness, roominess|noun
