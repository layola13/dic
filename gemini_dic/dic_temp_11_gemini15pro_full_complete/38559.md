concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (US)| v. |调制；捏造；编造|To create or devise something, especially in a skillful or ingenious way, often by combining various elements or ingredients; to invent a story or plan, especially in order to deceive someone.|She concocted a delicious soup from leftover vegetables. 她用剩下的蔬菜调制出一碗美味的汤。|synonyms: devise, invent, formulate, fabricate, create, prepare, mix, blend|antonyms: discover, find, destroy, dismantle|verb


concoction:/kənˈkɒkʃn/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkʃn/ (US)| n. |调制品；混合物；捏造的故事|Something concocted, especially a mixture of various ingredients; a story or plan that has been invented, especially in order to deceive someone.|The drink was a strange concoction of fruit juices and spices. 这种饮料是用果汁和香料调制而成的奇怪混合物。|synonyms: mixture, blend, combination, brew, invention, fabrication|antonyms: component, element, ingredient, analysis, separation|noun
