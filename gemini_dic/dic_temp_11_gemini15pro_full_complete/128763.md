nigglingly:/ˈnɪɡlɪŋli/
adverb|琐碎地；烦人地；吹毛求疵地|in a niggling or petty way; annoyingly; fussily|The problem nigglingly persisted despite my efforts to fix it. 这个问题尽管我努力修复，但仍然烦人地持续存在。|例句：He was nigglingly critical of her work. 他对她的工作吹毛求疵。|近义词：pettily, fussily, annoyingly, irritatingly|反义词：generously, magnanimously, considerably, significantly
