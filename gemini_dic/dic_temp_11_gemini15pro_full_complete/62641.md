enfranchised: /ɪnˈfræntʃaɪzd/ /ɛnˈfræntʃaɪzd/
| adj. |被赋予公民权的；有选举权的；获得特许经营权的|Having the legal right to vote; given a right or privilege. |例句：1.  Women were enfranchised in 1920 in the United States.  美国妇女在1920年获得了选举权。

2.  The company was enfranchised to operate a ferry service.  该公司获得了经营渡轮服务的特许权。|近义词：empowered, authorized, privileged|反义词:disenfranchised, excluded|词性:形容词
