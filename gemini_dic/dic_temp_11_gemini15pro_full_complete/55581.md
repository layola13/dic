distyle:/ˈdaɪstaɪl/| adj.|双柱式的；有两根柱子的|Having two columns, especially in an ancient Greek or Roman temple porch or portico.|The temple was distyle in antis, meaning it had two columns between the projecting side walls of the porch.|近义词:two-columned|反义词:tetrastyle (four-columned), hexastyle (six-columned)|adjective
