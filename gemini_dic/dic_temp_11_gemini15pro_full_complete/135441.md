nonperspective:/ˌnɒnpərˈspektɪv/
adjective
非透视的，无透视的
Lacking or not based on the principles of perspective; not having or exhibiting perspective.
The child's drawing was nonperspective, with all the figures the same size regardless of distance.  这孩子的画是非透视的，所有的人物大小都一样，与距离无关。
The ancient Egyptian art often features nonperspective representations of people and objects. 古埃及艺术通常以非透视的方式来表现人物和物体。

synonyms: non-perspectival, flat, two-dimensional
antonyms: perspectival, three-dimensional
