outthreaten:/ˌaʊtˈθrɛtn/
verb
比…威胁更大；在威胁方面胜过
to threaten more effectively or to a greater degree than someone or something else
The new virus outthreatened the previous strain with its higher infection rate. 这种新病毒的感染率更高，比之前的毒株威胁更大。
outvie, surpass, exceed
-
