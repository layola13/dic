nonconflictive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈflɪktɪv/
adjective
非冲突性的；不引起冲突的
Not tending to cause conflict; peaceful or harmonious.
The meeting was surprisingly nonconflictive, with everyone agreeing on the next steps. 这次会议出奇地没有冲突，每个人都同意接下来的步骤。
The organization promotes a nonconflictive approach to dispute resolution.  该组织提倡用非冲突的方式解决争端。

近义词:peaceful, harmonious, amicable, cooperative
反义词:conflictive, antagonistic, hostile, contentious
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non
conflict (noun/verb): from Latin conflictus, past participle of confligere "to strike together," from com- "together" + fligere "to strike"
-ive (suffix):  forms adjectives meaning "tending to" or "having the nature of"
