idiosyncrasy: /ˌɪdiəˈsɪŋkrəsi/ (US), /ˌɪdɪəˈsɪŋkrəsi/ (UK)| n.| 特质，癖好，特性|A mode of behavior or way of thought peculiar to an individual.  A distinctive or peculiar feature or characteristic of a place or thing.| She had a few harmless idiosyncrasies, like always wearing mismatched socks. 她有一些无伤大雅的怪癖，比如总是穿不配对的袜子。|Synonyms: peculiarity, quirk, eccentricity, mannerism|Antonyms: normality, conformity|Noun
