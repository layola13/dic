eunuch:/ˈjuːnək/ | n. |太监；阉人|A man who has been castrated, especially one employed in a position of authority in a royal court or harem. | The emperor surrounded himself with eunuchs. 皇帝身边都是太监。| Synonyms: castrato, gelding | Antonyms: man | noun

eunuch:/ˈjuːnək/ | n. | 無能的人；缺乏魄力的人 (比喻义) | A person who is weak or lacking in power or virility. |He was a political eunuch, unable to make any significant decisions. 他是个政治上的无能之辈，做不了任何重要的决定。| Synonyms: weakling, coward | Antonyms: leader, strongman | noun
