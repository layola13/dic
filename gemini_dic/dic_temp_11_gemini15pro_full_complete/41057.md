copiousnesses:/ˈkoʊpiəsnɪsɪz/
n.
丰富,大量, 充裕
The state or quality of being copious; abundance.
The copiousnesses of the harvest ensured no one went hungry.  丰收的充裕保证了没有人挨饿。
Synonyms: abundance, profusion, plentifulness
Antonyms: scarcity, dearth, paucity
noun


copious:/ˈkoʊpiəs/
adj.
丰富的，大量的，充裕的
Abundant in supply or quantity.
He took copious notes during the lecture. 他在讲座期间做了大量的笔记。
Synonyms: abundant, plentiful, ample
Antonyms: scarce, meager, scanty
adjective
