crackpotism: /ˈkrækˌpɒtɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈkrækˌpɑːtɪzəm/ (US)
|n.|古怪行为；怪诞言论；荒谬的主张|The belief that someone is insane, especially based on their unconventional or eccentric behavior or opinions. Holding unconventional or eccentric beliefs or exhibiting unconventional or eccentric behavior.|His crackpotism was evident in his insistence that the earth was flat.|近义词：eccentricity, craziness, madness, lunacy, folly|反义词：sanity, rationality, normalcy|词性:名词
