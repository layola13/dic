brought:/brɔːt/
verb
带来，拿来，取来
Past tense of bring.  Carried or conveyed something to a place or person.
She brought a cake to the party. 她带了个蛋糕来参加聚会。
He brought his friend along. 他带了他的朋友一起来。
近义词: carried, fetched, delivered
反义词: took, removed
