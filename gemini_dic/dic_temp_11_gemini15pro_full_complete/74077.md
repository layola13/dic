fossilized:/ˈfɒsəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈfɑːsəlaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|僵化的；成为化石的；老化的|Transformed into a fossil; preserved in fossil form; outdated or rigid and unchanging.|例句：The fossilized remains of a dinosaur were discovered.  发现了一具恐龙化石。|近义词：petrified, ossified, antiquated|反义词：modern, contemporary, current|词性:形容词

fossilize:/ˈfɒsəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈfɑːsəlaɪzd/ (US)| v.|使成化石；使僵化；使陈腐|To convert into a fossil; to become rigid and unchanging.|例句：The remains of the plant fossilized over millions of years.  这株植物的遗骸经过数百万年变成了化石。|近义词：petrify, ossify|反义词：modernize, revitalize|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fossiliser*, from fossile (“fossil”), from Late Latin fossilis (“dug up”), from Latin fossus (“ditch, trench, grave”), from fodere (“to dig”).
