argumentative:/ˌɑːrɡjəˈmentətɪv/ | adj. | 好辩的，爱争论的 | Given to expressing divergent or opposite views. |例句：He's so argumentative, it's impossible to have a peaceful conversation with him. 他太好辩了，根本无法和他进行平静的对话。

例句：She has a very argumentative personality, always ready for a debate. 她性格好辩，随时准备辩论。|近义词：contentious, disputatious, quarrelsome |反义词：agreeable, amenable, compliant |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *argumentātīvus, from Latin argumentātus (past participle of argumentārī ‘to argue, prove’), from argumentum ‘proof, argument’.
