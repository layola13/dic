nemalite:/ˈnɛməˌlaɪt/| n. |纤滑石，纤维状的水镁石|A fibrous variety of brucite, Mg(OH)₂ , usually found in aggregates of slender, flexible fibers.|Nemalite is often found associated with serpentine deposits. 纤滑石常与蛇纹石矿床共生。|Synonyms: brucite fiber, chrysotile, asbestos|Antonyms: None|noun
