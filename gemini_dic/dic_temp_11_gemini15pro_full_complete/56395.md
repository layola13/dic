dolefully:/ˈdoʊlfəli/| adv.|悲哀地，忧郁地|In a way that expresses sadness or sorrow.|She sighed dolefully and walked away. 她悲伤地叹了口气，走开了。|例句：He shook his head dolefully. 他悲伤地摇了摇头。|例句：The dog howled dolefully at the moon. 狗对着月亮悲哀地嚎叫。|近义词：sadly, mournfully, sorrowfully|反义词：happily, cheerfully|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *dolefulli, equivalent to doleful +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Old French dol "grief, sorrow" (from Latin dolor) + -ful.
