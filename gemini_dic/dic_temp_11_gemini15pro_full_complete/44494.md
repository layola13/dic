词源:  "Crossfire" originates from the literal meaning of lines of gunfire crossing each other.  This military term was then extended metaphorically to describe situations of verbal attack or conflict from multiple sides. “Crossfire”源于字面意义上的几条交火的枪线。这个军事术语后来被隐喻地扩展到描述来自多方面的口头攻击或冲突的情况。
