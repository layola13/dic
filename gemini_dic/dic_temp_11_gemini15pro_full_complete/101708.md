irreplaceably:/ˌɪrɪˈpleɪsəbli/| adv.|不可替代地|In a way that cannot be replaced.  It describes something so unique or valuable that it cannot be substituted.|例句：She will be irreplaceably missed by her family. 她会被她的家人深深地怀念，无人可以取代。

例句：The antique vase was irreplaceably damaged in the fire.  古董花瓶在大火中被损坏，无法修复。|近义词：invaluably, uniquely, essentially|反义词:replaceably, dispensably|词性:副词

词源:
ir- (prefix): not, opposite of (Latin origin)
replaceable (adjective): able to be replaced
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
