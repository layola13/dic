amalgamation:/əˌmælɡəˈmeɪʃn/| n.|合并，混合，融合|The amalgamation of the two companies created a larger, more powerful entity.  两家公司的合并创造了一个更大、更有实力的实体。|例句：The amalgamation of different musical styles created a unique sound. 不同音乐风格的融合创造了一种独特的声音。

例句：The recipe is an amalgamation of several different cuisines.  这个食谱融合了几种不同的菜系。|近义词：merger, union, blend, combination, fusion|反义词：separation, division, disunion|词性:noun

amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəmeɪt/| v.|合并，混合，融合|The two schools will amalgamate next year.  这两所学校明年将合并。|例句：Several small political parties amalgamated to form a single larger party.  几个小政党合并成一个更大的政党。

例句：The artist amalgamated various techniques in his paintings. 这位艺术家在他的绘画中融合了各种技巧。|近义词：merge, combine, unite, blend, fuse|反义词：separate, divide, disunite|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *amalgamare, from Latin amalgama (“amalgam”) + -are (verb-forming suffix).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek μάλαγμα (málagma, “soft mass, emollient”).
