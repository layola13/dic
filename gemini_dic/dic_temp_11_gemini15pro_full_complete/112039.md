long-windednesses:/ˌlɔːŋˈwɪndɪdnɪsɪz/ |n.|冗长；啰嗦|The long-windedness of his speech bored the audience.  他的演讲冗长乏味，使听众感到厌烦。| English Explanation: The state or quality of being long-winded; tedious length or wordiness in speech or writing.  |Example: The long-windednesses of the legal documents made them difficult to understand.  法律文件的冗长使得它们难以理解。|Synonyms: verbosity, prolixity, tediousness |Antonyms: conciseness, brevity| Noun
