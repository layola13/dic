monitrix: /ˈmɒnɪtrɪks/ (British), /ˈmɑːnɪtrɪks/ (American)
|n.|女监督员，女监察员|A female monitor; a woman who admonishes or warns.|The monitrix kept a watchful eye on the students during the exam. 女监督员在考试期间密切关注着学生们。|近义词: supervisor, overseer, inspector, invigilator|反义词: subordinate, dependent|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "monitrix," feminine form of "monitor," meaning "one who warns."  Ultimately from "monēre" (to warn, advise, remind).
