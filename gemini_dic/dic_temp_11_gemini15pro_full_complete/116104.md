martyr:/ˈmɑːrtər/ (US) /ˈmɑːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|烈士，殉道者|A person who is killed because of their religious or other beliefs.|Joan of Arc is a famous martyr.  圣女贞德是一位著名的烈士。|synonyms: victim, sufferer, hero|antonyms: coward, traitor|noun

martyr:/ˈmɑːrtər/ (US) /ˈmɑːtə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|折磨，使殉难|To kill someone because of their religious or other beliefs.|Early Christians were martyred for their faith. 早期基督徒因其信仰而殉难。|synonyms: persecute, torture, kill|antonyms: protect, save|verb
