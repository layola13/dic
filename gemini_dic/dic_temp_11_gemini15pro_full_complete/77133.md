gasconade:/ˌɡæs.kəˈneɪd/ | n. vi.| 自夸；吹牛；夸口 |  Boastful or extravagant talk.  A vaunting and pompous manner of speaking, especially about oneself. | He was known for his gasconade, often exaggerating his accomplishments. 他以吹牛而闻名，经常夸大自己的成就。 |  The soldier's gasconade about his bravery in battle was met with skepticism.  这个士兵吹嘘自己在战斗中的英勇行为，却遭到了怀疑。 | 近义词: boasting, bragging, bluster, swagger, vainglory | 反义词: modesty, humility | 词性: noun, verb


gasconade:/ˌɡæs.kəˈneɪd/ | vi.| 自夸；吹牛；夸口 | To boast; to brag. | He gasconaded about his wealth and connections. 他吹嘘自己的财富和人脉。 | She gasconaded endlessly about her trip to Europe. 她不停地吹嘘她的欧洲之旅。 | 近义词: boast, brag, bluster, swagger | 反义词: be modest, downplay | 词性: verb

词源: From Gascon, a region in southwestern France, whose inhabitants were stereotyped as boastful. 源自法国西南部的加斯科涅地区，那里居民的刻板印象是爱吹牛。
