gaussage: /ˈɡaʊsɪdʒ/ | n. | 高斯測量; 高斯測量法;  (高斯) 磁场强度测量 | The act or process of measuring magnetic field strength, especially using a gaussmeter.  It often refers to measurements related to degaussing (the process of reducing or eliminating unwanted magnetic fields). |  The technician performed a gaussage of the equipment after demagnetization. 技术人员在消磁后对设备进行了高斯测量。|  Magnetic field measurement, magnetometry | N/A | noun
