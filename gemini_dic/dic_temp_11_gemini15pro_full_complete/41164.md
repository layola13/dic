copyable:/ˈkɒpiəb(ə)l/| adj.|可复制的|able to be copied.|例句：The document is copyable.  这份文件是可复制的。|例句：The image is easily copyable. 这张图片很容易复制。|近义词：reproducible, duplicable|反义词：uncopyable, unique|词性:形容词

词源: From "copy" (v.) + "-able" (suffix).  "Copy" comes from Old French "copie," ultimately from Latin "copia" (abundance, plenty).  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of being."
