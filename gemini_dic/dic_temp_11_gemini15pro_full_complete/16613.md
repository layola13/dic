baronage:/ˈbærənɪdʒ/
n.
男爵爵位；全体男爵
The collective body of barons.  The dignity or rank of a baron.
The baronage of England played a key role in the Magna Carta. 英格兰的男爵在《大宪章》中扮演了关键角色。
synonyms: peerage, nobility, aristocracy
antonyms: commoners, peasantry
noun

Etymology->
From Old French *baronage*, from *baron* + *-age*.
