colorises:/ˈkʌl.ə.raɪ.zɪz/
v.
给...着色，给...染色
adds color to something, especially a black and white film or photograph
The software colorises old black and white photos.  这款软件可以给黑白老照片上色。
近义词:colors, tints, dyes
反义词:bleaches, desaturates
词性:动词
