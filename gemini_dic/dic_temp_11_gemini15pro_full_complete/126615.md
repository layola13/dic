naïve:/nɑːˈiːv/ /naɪˈiːv/| adj.|天真的，幼稚的，轻信的|Showing a lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment; innocent and unsuspecting.|She was a naive young woman who believed everything she was told. 她是一个天真的年轻女子，别人说什么她都信。|Synonyms: innocent, unsophisticated, gullible, artless, ingenuous|Antonyms: sophisticated, worldly, cynical, shrewd, astute|adjective

naif:/nɑːˈiːf/ /naɪˈiːf/| n.|天真的人，幼稚的人|A naive person.|He's such a naif, he believes everything he reads on the internet. 他真是个天真的人，他在网上看到什么都信。|Synonyms: innocent, simpleton|Antonyms: sophisticate, cynic|noun
