king-hit:/ˈkɪŋ hɪt/| n. vt.| 恶意猛击，偷袭| A sudden and unexpected blow, often delivered to the head, typically when the victim is unaware or unprepared.  A cowardly attack.| He was king-hit from behind and knocked unconscious. 他被人从背后偷袭，打得不省人事。|近义词： sucker punch, cheap shot, blindside|反义词: fair fight|词性: 名词，动词

词源：Australian slang, from king + hit, perhaps implying a powerful blow fit for a king. 最初是澳大利亚俚语，由 king + hit 组成，可能意味着适合国王的强力一击。
