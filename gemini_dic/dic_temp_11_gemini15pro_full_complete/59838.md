ectomorphy:/ˈɛktəˌmɔːrfi/
n.
外胚层体型；瘦长体型
Relating to or characterized by the predominance of ectomorphic characteristics.  A body type characterized by a slender build, long limbs, and a relatively low percentage of body fat.
He had a classic ectomorphic build, tall and thin. 他有着典型的外胚层体型，又高又瘦。
Ectomorphy is often associated with a fast metabolism. 外胚层体型通常与新陈代谢快有关。
近义词:asthenic, leptosomic
反义词:endomorphy, mesomorphy
