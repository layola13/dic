mild:/maɪld/| adj.|温和的；轻微的；淡味的；文雅的|gentle or moderate in type or degree or effect; not sharp, spicy, or strong in flavour; (of weather) moderately warm, especially less cold than expected.|The symptoms were mild. 症状轻微。|近义词:gentle, moderate, lenient, clement, temperate, soft, light, slight|反义词:harsh, severe, strong, acute, extreme|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *milde (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mildijaz, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)meyl- (“soft, mild”). Cognate with Old Saxon mildi, Old Frisian milde, Old Norse mildr, Dutch mild, German mild, Gothic 𐌼𐌹𐌻𐌳𐍃 (milds).
