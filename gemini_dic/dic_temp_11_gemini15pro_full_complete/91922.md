hypermasculine:/ˌhaɪpərˈmæskjʊlɪn/
adjective
极端男性化的；过分阳刚的
Exaggeratedly masculine; exhibiting exaggerated masculine traits.

例句：
He had a hypermasculine attitude that made some people uncomfortable.
他极端男性化的态度让一些人感到不舒服。

The movie portrayed a hypermasculine hero who solved all his problems with violence.
这部电影刻画了一个极端男性化的英雄，他用暴力解决所有问题。

近义词：
machismo, overly masculine

反义词：
effeminate, feminine


词性:
adjective


词源:
hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + masculine (adjective)
"hyper-" (前缀，意为“过度，过分”) + masculine（形容词）
