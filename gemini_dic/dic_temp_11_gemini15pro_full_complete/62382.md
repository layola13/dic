endocardial:/ˌɛndəˈkɑːrdiəl/| adj.|心内膜的|Relating to the endocardium, the thin, smooth membrane which lines the inside of the chambers of the heart and forms the surface of the valves.|Endocardial biopsies are sometimes necessary to diagnose certain heart conditions. 心内膜活检有时是诊断某些心脏疾病所必需的。|Near synonyms: intracardiac|Antonyms: epicardial|adjective
