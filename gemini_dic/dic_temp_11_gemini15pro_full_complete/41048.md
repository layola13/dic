copies:/ˈkɒpiz/ (British) /ˈkɑːpiz/ (American)| n.|副本，复印件，拷贝|Multiple copies of the document were distributed. 该文件的多个副本已分发。|例句：I made three copies of the report. 我复制了三份报告。|例句：She kept the original and gave me two copies. 她保留了原件，给了我两份复印件。|近义词：duplicates, reproductions, replicas|反义词：original|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *copies*, from Old French *copie*, from Latin *copia* (“abundance, plenty”), related to *co-ops*, from *ops* (“wealth, resources, power, plenty”).  The sense shifted from "abundant supply" to "transcript, reproduction."
