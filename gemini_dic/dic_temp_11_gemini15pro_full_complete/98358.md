insistent:/ɪnˈsɪstənt/| adj.|坚持的，坚决的，持续的|Demanding something repeatedly and refusing to give up.  Characterized by urgency or persistence.|She was insistent that she had seen him leave the house. 她坚持说她看到他离开了房子。|Synonyms: persistent, tenacious, persevering, firm, resolute|Antonyms: hesitant, wavering, irresolute, yielding|adjective


insistent:/ɪnˈsɪstənt/| adj.|紧急的，急迫的|(of something needing attention) urgent or pressing|The insistent ringing of the telephone woke her up.  电话铃声的持续响起把她吵醒了。|Synonyms: urgent, pressing, compelling, demanding|Antonyms: unimportant, trivial, minor|adjective
