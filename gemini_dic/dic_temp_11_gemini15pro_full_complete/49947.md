dendrolite:/ˈdɛndrəˌlaɪt/| n. |树枝石，树枝状矿物|A mineral resembling a plant or tree, often branching in form. It is typically formed by the deposition of minerals in cracks or fissures in rock, creating intricate patterns.|The geologist found a beautiful dendrolite in the cave.  地质学家在山洞里发现了一块美丽的树枝石。|Synonyms: arborescent mineral, branching mineral|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
