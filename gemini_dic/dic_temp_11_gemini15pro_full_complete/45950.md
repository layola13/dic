cvp:/ˌsiːviːˈpiː/ |n.|成本-数量-利润; 成本-数量-利润分析|Cost-Volume-Profit; Cost-Volume-Profit analysis.  CVP analysis is a tool used in management accounting to understand the relationship between sales volume, costs, and profit. |  The company conducted a CVP analysis to determine the break-even point. 公司进行了成本-数量-利润分析以确定盈亏平衡点。| synonyms: cost-volume-profit relationship, break-even analysis | antonyms: N/A | noun

cvp:/ˌsiːviːˈpiː/ |n.|中央静脉压|Central Venous Pressure. CVP is the blood pressure in the venae cavae, near the right atrium of the heart. | The doctor monitored the patient's CVP to assess their fluid status. 医生监测病人的中央静脉压以评估他们的体液状况。| synonyms: central venous pressure reading | antonyms: N/A | noun
