knights:/naɪts/| n.|骑士，武士；爵士|A man awarded a non-hereditary title by a monarch in recognition of merit, especially for service to the country, and entitled to use Sir before his name.|The knights of the Round Table are legendary figures. 圆桌骑士是传说中的人物。|近义词：champion, warrior, cavalier|反义词:villain, knave|noun
