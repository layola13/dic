dysenterical:/ˌdɪsənˈterɪk(ə)l/
adjective

He suffered from dysenterical symptoms, including severe diarrhea and abdominal pain. 他患有痢疾症状，包括严重的腹泻和腹痛。
The dysenterical outbreak spread quickly through the refugee camp. 痢疾疫情在难民营迅速蔓延。
synonyms: dysenteric
antonyms: healthy
