farmhouse:/ˈfɑːrmhaʊs/ (US) /ˈfɑːmhaʊs/ (UK)| n. |农舍；农场住宅|A house attached to or located on a farm.  A dwelling on a farm, typically the main residence of the farm family or owner.|We spent a relaxing weekend at a charming farmhouse in the countryside. 我们在一个迷人的乡村农舍度过了一个轻松的周末。|The old farmhouse had been in the family for generations. 这座古老的农舍世代相传。|近义词：homestead, grange|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) city apartment, urban dwelling|词性: noun
