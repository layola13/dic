mosaicking:/moʊˈzeɪɪkɪŋ/
n.|镶嵌；镶嵌艺术；镶嵌工艺|The process of creating a mosaic, a picture or pattern produced by arranging together small colored pieces of hard material, such as stone, tile, or glass.|She learned the art of mosaicking from her grandmother.|近义词: tessellation, inlay, |反义词:|词性:名词

mosaicking:/moʊˈzeɪɪkɪŋ/
v.|镶嵌；用马赛克装饰|To decorate with a mosaic.|They are mosaicking the bathroom floor.|近义词: tile, inlay, tessellate|反义词:|词性:动词
