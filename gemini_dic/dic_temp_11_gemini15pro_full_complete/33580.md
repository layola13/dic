ciliform:/ˈsɪliəfɔːrm/| adj.|纤毛状的，毛状的|Resembling cilia; having the form or appearance of cilia.|The paramecium is a ciliform protozoan. 草履虫是一种纤毛状原生动物。|The bacterium has ciliform appendages.  这种细菌有纤毛状附属物。|近义词：ciliate, hairy, pilose|反义词:smooth, glabrous|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ciliformis, from cilium (“eyelash, eyelid”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
