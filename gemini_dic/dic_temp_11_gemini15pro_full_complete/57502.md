draging:/ˈdræɡɪŋ/|v.|拖曳，拖拉; 缓慢而费力地移动;  (时间)过得缓慢而令人厌倦|The dog was draging its injured leg. 狗拖着受伤的腿。|近义词：hauling, pulling, towing, trailing|反义词：carrying, lifting|词性：动词(Present Participle)
Etymology: From Middle English *draggen*, from Old Norse *draga* (“to draw, pull, drag”).
