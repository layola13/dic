accentuate:/əkˈsɛntʃueɪt/ /ækˈsɛntʃuˌeɪt/
verb
强调，突出
to emphasize a particular feature of something or to make something more noticeable
Her dress accentuated her tiny waist. 她的连衣裙突出了她纤细的腰部。

The scarf accentuates the color of her eyes.  这条围巾突出了她眼睛的颜色。


emphasize, highlight, stress, underscore
downplay, de-emphasize, minimize
词性:动词
