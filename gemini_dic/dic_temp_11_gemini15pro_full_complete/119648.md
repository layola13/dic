middlebrowisms:/ˈmɪdəlˌbraʊɪzəmz/
noun
中庸趣味；附庸风雅；庸俗的文化品味
Relatively conventional, unchallenging, and somewhat pretentious literary or cultural interests or tastes, associated with the educated middle class.  Appealing to or typical of a person who is considered to have conventional, unchallenging tastes in literature, the arts, etc.
He dismissed the book as full of middlebrowisms, preferring more intellectual fare. 他认为这本书充满了庸俗趣味，更喜欢更有思想深度的作品。
synonyms:  banality, platitude, cliché, commonplace, conventionality
antonyms:  originality, unconventionality, innovation, nonconformity, avant-garde
