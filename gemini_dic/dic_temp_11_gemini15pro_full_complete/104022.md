juju:/ˈdʒuːdʒuː/ | n. | 护身符；魔力；咒语 | An object believed to have magical powers; a charm or amulet. Also, a magical spell or incantation. | She wore a juju around her neck for protection. 她脖子上戴着一个护身符以求保护。

The witch doctor claimed his juju could cure any illness.  巫医声称他的咒语可以治愈任何疾病。 | 近义词: charm, amulet, talisman, fetish, spell, incantation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "scientific medicine" in a conceptual sense) | 词性: 名词
