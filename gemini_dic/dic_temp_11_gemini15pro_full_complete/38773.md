conducibility:/kənˌd(j)uːsɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
传导性，传导率; 有利性
The quality or state of being conducive.  The conducibility of copper makes it an excellent material for electrical wiring.
Copper's high conducibility makes it ideal for wiring. 铜的高传导性使其成为理想的布线材料。
Near Synonyms:  conductivity,  facilitation,  suitability,  advantageousness
Antonyms: resistance, hindrance, unsuitability
词性: noun
