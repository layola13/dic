burlesqued:/bərˈlɛskt/
verb
滑稽地模仿，夸张地模仿
to imitate or parody something in a humorous or exaggerated way.
Her performance burlesqued the pretensions of the fashion industry. 她的表演滑稽地模仿了时尚界的自命不凡。
The comedian burlesqued the president's mannerisms.  这位喜剧演员滑稽地模仿了总统的举止。
synonyms: parodied, mocked, satirized, lampooned
antonyms: imitated, copied, emulated
v.
