anthocyanin:/ˌænθəˈsaɪənɪn/| n.|花青素|A water-soluble vacuolar pigment that may appear red, purple, or blue depending on the pH.|The vibrant colors of blueberries are due to anthocyanins.|Synonyms: flavonoid (more general), pigment|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps chlorophyll as a contrasting pigment)|noun
