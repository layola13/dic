misunderstood:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstʊd/| adj.|被误解的，被错误理解的|Incorrectly interpreted or understood.  Not understood in the correct or intended way.|She felt misunderstood by her family. 她觉得家人不理解她。|He gave a misunderstood instruction which led to chaos. 他给了一个被误解的指示，导致了混乱。|misinterpreted, misconstrued|understood, comprehended|adjective

misunderstand:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd/| v.|误解，误会|To interpret (something) incorrectly. To fail to understand (someone or something) correctly.|I think you misunderstand my intentions. 我想你误解了我的意图。|She completely misunderstood the instructions and did it wrong. 她完全误解了指示，做错了。|misinterpret, misconstrue|understand, comprehend|verb
