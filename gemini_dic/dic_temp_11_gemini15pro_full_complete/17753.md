bedewy:/bɪˈdjuːi/
adjective
沾满露水的；带露水的
Covered or moistened with dew.
The bedewy grass sparkled in the morning sun.  沾满露水的草地在晨曦中闪闪发光。
The spider web was bedewy and glistening.  蜘蛛网沾满了露水，闪闪发光。
近义词:dewy, moist, damp
反义词:dry, arid
词性:adjective
