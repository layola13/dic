maule:/ˈmɔːl/ | n. | 紫色;紫红色; | A dark purplish-red color. | The curtains were a deep maule. 窗帘是深紫红色的。 | 近义词:purple, violet, crimson | 反义词: white, yellow | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *maule, from Latin *mavula, diminutive of malva "mallow." Related to mauve.
