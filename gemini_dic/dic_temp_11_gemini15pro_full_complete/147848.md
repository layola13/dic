overquantity:/ˈoʊvərˈkwɑːntɪti/
n.|过量，过多|An amount of something that is more than necessary, desirable, or permissible.|The warehouse was filled to the brim, a clear sign of overquantity.|Synonym: surplus, excess, glut|Antonym: shortage, deficit, scarcity|Noun

overquantity:/ˈoʊvərˈkwɑːntɪti/
v. |过量供应，过多提供|To supply or provide more of something than is necessary, desirable, or permissible.|The farmer overquantitied the market with his produce, leading to a drop in prices.|Synonym: oversupply, glut, saturate|Antonym: undersupply, restrict, limit|Verb
