consolidate:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪt/| vt. |巩固，加强，合并|to make something stronger or more solid, or to combine several things into one.|The company consolidated its position in the market by buying out its main competitor.  该公司通过收购其主要竞争对手巩固了其市场地位。|The two banks consolidated to form a larger, more powerful institution.  这两家银行合并成一个更大、更有实力的机构。|synonyms: strengthen, reinforce, merge, combine, unite|antonyms: weaken, divide, separate|verb
consolidate:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪt/| n. |巩固，合并|the act of consolidating something, or the state of being consolidated.|The consolidation of the two companies took several months.  两家公司的合并花了几个月的时间。|The government is working on the consolidation of power. 政府正在致力于巩固权力。|synonyms: strengthening, reinforcement, merger, combination, union|antonyms: weakening, division, separation|noun
