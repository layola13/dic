gamer:/ˈɡeɪmər/| n.|游戏玩家；游戏迷|A person who plays video games or participates in role-playing games, especially one who does so enthusiastically and regularly.|He's a serious gamer, spending hours each day playing online. 他是一个认真的游戏玩家，每天花几个小时玩在线游戏。|近义词：player, enthusiast|反义词:non-gamer|Noun
