mccabe:/məˈkeɪb/| n.|麦凯布; 麦克卡比（姓氏）|A surname.  It is also used in "McCabe cyclomatic complexity," a software metric used to indicate the complexity of a program.|例句：Thomas McCabe developed the cyclomatic complexity metric. 托马斯·麦凯布开发了圈复杂度指标。

例句：There's a Mr. McCabe here to see you.  这里有一位麦凯布先生要见你。|近义词: (For the surname - none applicable.  For the metric - perhaps Halstead complexity)|反义词:(None applicable)|词性:noun
