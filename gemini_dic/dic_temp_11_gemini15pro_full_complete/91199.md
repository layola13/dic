hydrencephalocele:/ˌhaɪdrənˈsɛfələˌsil/
n.
脑积水膨出, 积水性脑膨出
A congenital neural tube defect characterized by protrusion of the brain and its meninges through a defect in the skull, associated with accumulation of cerebrospinal fluid within the sac.  This is a severe form of neural tube defect.
