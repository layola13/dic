nonsentiently:/nɒnˈsɛntiəntli/ | adv. | 无感觉地，无知觉地 | Without feeling or consciousness; in a nonsentient manner. | The robot moved nonsentiently across the room.  机器人无知觉地穿过房间。|  The computer nonsentiently processed the data. 计算机无感觉地处理数据。 | 近义词: insensibly, unfeelingly | 反义词: consciously, feelingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "nonsentient" + "-ly". "Nonsentient" is formed from the prefix "non-" (not) + "sentient" (able to perceive or feel things). "Sentient" ultimately comes from the Latin "sentientem" (feeling).
