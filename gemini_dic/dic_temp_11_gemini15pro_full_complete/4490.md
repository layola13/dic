albinism:/ˈælbɪˌnɪzəm/ | n. |白化病|A group of inherited disorders characterized by a partial or total lack of melanin pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.|  People with albinism are often sensitive to sunlight. 白化病患者通常对阳光敏感。|  Albino, achromia, hypopigmentation| hyperpigmentation, melanism| noun
