alleviating:/əˈliːvieɪtɪŋ/
Verb (present participle)
减轻，缓解
Making something less severe or intense.
Taking painkillers can be effective in alleviating a headache.
服用止痛药可以有效缓解头痛。
近义词:reducing, easing, mitigating, lessening, assuaging
反义词:aggravating, exacerbating, worsening, intensifying
词性:v.

Etymology:
From Late Latin *alleviatus*, past participle of *alleviare*, from Latin *ad* (“to”) + *levis* (“light”).
