observation-cars:/ˌɑːbzərˈveɪʃən kɑːrz/ |n.|观光车厢；瞭望车厢|A railroad car with large windows, often at the rear of a train, designed for viewing the scenery.|We rode in the observation-car to get the best views of the mountains. 我们乘坐观光车厢，以便更好地欣赏山景。|The observation car offered panoramic views of the passing countryside. 观光车厢提供了途经乡村的全景。|Synonyms: viewing car, lounge car|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps) regular coach, enclosed car|Noun
