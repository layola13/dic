boobish:/ˈbuːbɪʃ/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻乎乎的|Lacking intelligence or good judgment; silly or foolish.  Derived from "boob," meaning a foolish person.  Often used to describe someone who is naive or easily tricked.|She made a rather boobish mistake on the exam.|Synonyms: foolish, silly, stupid, naive, inane, asinine|Antonyms: intelligent, smart, wise, astute, clever|adjective
