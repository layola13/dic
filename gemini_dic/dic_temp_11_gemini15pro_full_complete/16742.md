barterers:/ˈbɑːrtərərz/
noun
以物易物的人; 交换者
People who exchange goods or services without using money.
The barterers met at the market square to trade their wares.  以物易物的人聚集在市场广场交换他们的货物。
The two barterers agreed to exchange a goat for a bushel of wheat.  两个以物易物者同意用一只山羊换一蒲式耳小麦。
traders, exchangers, swappers
buyers, sellers, vendors
n.
