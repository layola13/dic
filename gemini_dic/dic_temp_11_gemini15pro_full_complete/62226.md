encrisped:/ɪnˈkrɪspt/ | adj. | 卷曲的，波浪形的；（诗歌用语）使卷曲，使波浪起伏 | Having a wavy or curled appearance; (poetic) to make wavy or curled | The encripsled waves crashed against the shore. 波浪卷曲着拍打着海岸。 | 近义词: curled, wavy, frizzled, crimped | 反义词: straight, smooth | 词性: 形容词
词源: From en- + crisp + -ed. "En-" acts as an intensifier, and "crisp" refers to something being tightly curled or wavy.  "-ed" forms the past participle, often used adjectivally.
