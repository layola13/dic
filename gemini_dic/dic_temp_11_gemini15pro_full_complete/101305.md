inwall:/ˈɪnwɔːl/ | adj. n.|内置的，墙内的；内墙 | Built into a wall; a wall that is built within another wall. |
The inwall speakers provided excellent surround sound. 内置扬声器提供了出色的环绕声。|
They decided to build an inwall safe. 他们决定建造一个墙内保险箱。|
近义词：built-in, recessed |反义词: external, freestanding |词性: adjective, noun

inwall:/ˈɪnwɔːl/ | n.|内墙 | An inner wall; a partition wall. |
The inwall separated the two rooms. 内墙隔开了两个房间。|
The plumbers had to access the pipes through the inwall. 水管工不得不穿过内墙才能接触到管道。|
近义词：partition, inner wall |反义词: outer wall, exterior wall |词性: noun
