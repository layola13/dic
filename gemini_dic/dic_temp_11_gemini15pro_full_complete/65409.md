euphonism:/ˈjuːfəmɪzəm/|n.|委婉语；委婉说法|A mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered to be too harsh or blunt when referring to something unpleasant or embarrassing.|Passing away is a euphemism for dying.  去世是死亡的委婉说法。|The company made some staff adjustments—a euphemism for redundancies. 公司对员工做了一些调整——这是裁员的委婉说法。|Synonyms: circumlocution, understatement, polite term|Antonyms: dysphemism, bluntness, directness|noun
