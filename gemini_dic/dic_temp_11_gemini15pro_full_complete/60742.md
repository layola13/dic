electrocutions: /ɪˌlɛktroʊˈkjuːʃənz/
n.
触电死亡（的事件）; 电刑处死（的事件）
The act of killing someone by electric shock, or instances of being killed in this way.
Several electrocutions were reported after the hurricane brought down power lines.  飓风过后，电线倒塌，据报道发生了数起触电死亡事件。
The prison warden oversaw several electrocutions during his career.  这位监狱长在他的职业生涯中监督执行了几次电刑。
近义词: electric shocks,  electrocution deaths
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "survival from electric shock")
词性: Noun (plural)
