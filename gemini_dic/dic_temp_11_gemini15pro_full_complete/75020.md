friendship:/ˈfrɛndʃɪp/ | n. |友谊，友情|The state of being friends; a relationship between friends.|  She values her friendships above all else. 她最珍视的是友谊。

|近义词：amity, companionship, fellowship|反义词：enmity, hostility|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *frēondscipe (attested only as frēondscype), from frēond (“friend”) +‎ -scipe (“-ship”). Cognate with Old Frisian frīondscip (“friendship”), Old Saxon friundskap (“friendship”), Old High German frīuntschaft (“friendship”), Old Norse vinátta (“friendship”).
