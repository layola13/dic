overclaimed:/ˌoʊvərˈkleɪmd/
verb
过度索赔；过分要求
To have claimed or requested more than is due or warranted.
The contractor overclaimed expenses for the construction project.  承包商对建筑项目的费用索赔过高。
The insurance company suspected the claimant had overclaimed for the damages. 保险公司怀疑索赔人对损失的索赔过高。
Synonyms: exaggerated, inflated, overstated
Antonyms: underclaimed, understated
