barrelled:/ˈbærəld/ | v. |  装桶; (使)高速移动 | To put into a barrel; to move at high speed. |  They barrelled the apples for cider.  他们把苹果装桶做苹果酒。| The truck barrelled through the intersection. 卡车飞驰穿过十字路口。| Synonyms: barreled, cask, keg, speed, rush | Antonyms: crawl | word type: verb
