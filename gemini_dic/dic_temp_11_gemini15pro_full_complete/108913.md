leds:/lɛdz/ | n. | 发光二极管 (Light Emitting Diodes) |  A semiconductor device that emits light when an electric current passes through it. | The Christmas lights were made of LEDs.  圣诞灯是用LED做的。| synonyms:  diode lamps, solid-state lamps | antonyms: incandescent bulbs, fluorescent lamps | noun

leds:/lɛdz/ | v. |  (lead 的第三人称单数形式) 引导，带领| To guide or direct someone or something. | He leds the team to victory. 他带领团队取得了胜利。| synonyms: guide, direct, escort, conduct, usher, pilot, steer, head, command, govern, rule | antonyms: follow, trail, obey | verb
