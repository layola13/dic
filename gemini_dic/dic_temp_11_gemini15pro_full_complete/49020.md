degras:/ˈdeɪɡrɑːs/
n.
去毛剂；脱脂剂
A degreasing agent, especially one used in preparing hides for tanning.
Leatherworkers use degras to soften leather. 皮革工人使用去毛剂来软化皮革。
Synonyms: degreaser,  detergent
Antonyms:  --
