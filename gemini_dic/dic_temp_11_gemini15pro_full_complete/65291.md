eucopolysaccharides:/ˌjuːkoʊˌpɒliˈsækəraɪdz/ | n. |优多糖|A group of polysaccharides characterized by their high water solubility and often containing uronic acids, along with various monosaccharides. They are commonly found in plant cell walls and have diverse biological functions.|例句：Eucopolysaccharides are important components of the plant cell wall matrix. 优多糖是植物细胞壁基质的重要组成部分。

例句：The eucopolysaccharides in aloe vera contribute to its healing properties.  芦荟中的优多糖有助于其愈合特性。|近义词：glycans, polysaccharides|反义词: None (no direct antonym exists)|词性:noun

Etymology:  "eu-" (Greek, meaning "well" or "true") + "co" (Latin, meaning "together") + "poly" (Greek, meaning "many") + "saccharide" (from Greek "sakkharon," meaning "sugar")
