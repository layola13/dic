outgleaming:/ˌaʊtˈɡliːmɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的光辉; 比…更闪耀
surpassing in brightness or splendor.
The rising sun outgleamed the city lights.  冉冉升起的太阳比城市灯光更加耀眼。
近义词: outshining, eclipsing, surpassing
反义词: dimming, obscuring, paling
词性: Verb
