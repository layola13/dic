antinausea:/ˌæntiˈnɔːziə/ /ˌæntiˈnɔːʒə/| adj.|止吐的，抗恶心的|Preventing or relieving nausea.|The doctor prescribed antinausea medication. 医生开了止吐药。|近义词：antiemetic|反义词:nausea-inducing|adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposing  (from Greek *anti)
nausea (noun): a feeling of sickness with an inclination to vomit (from Latin nausea, from Greek nausia, seasickness)
