accusing:/əˈkjuːzɪŋ/
adjective
指责的，控告的
indicating or suggesting that one believes someone has done something wrong.
She gave him an accusing look. 她用 accusing 的眼神看着他。（中文翻译：她用责备的眼神看着他。）
His accusing tone made her feel guilty. 他指责的语气让她感到内疚。（中文翻译：他责备的语气让她感到内疚。）
synonyms: blaming, incriminating, critical, reproaching
antonyms: forgiving, understanding, excusing
词性: 形容词
