overcredited:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪtɪd/
adjective
给予过多信用的；被过誉的
Given too much credit or recognition.
The actor was overcredited for the film's success; the director's vision was the real driving force.  这位演员在电影的成功中被过誉了；导演的远见才是真正的推动力。
The young intern was overcredited for the project's completion, when in reality the team had carried most of the weight.  这位年轻的实习生在项目完成方面被过誉了，实际上团队承担了大部分的工作。
Near Synonyms: overpraised, overrated, overvalued
Antonyms: undercredited, undervalued, underrated
Word Origin: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "credited" (past participle of "credit," from Latin "creditum," meaning "loan, belief, trust")
