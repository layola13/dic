came-about:/keɪm əˈbaʊt/| v.|发生，产生，出现|This idiom describes how something happened or originated.  It is often used to explain the origins of a situation or event. | It came about that they met at a conference.  他们是在一次会议上认识的。| 近义词：occurred, happened, transpired, arose, developed| 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could use "was prevented")|词性: 动词短语
