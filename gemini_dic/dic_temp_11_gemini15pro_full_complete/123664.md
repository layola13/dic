moochers:/ˈmuːtʃərz/
n.
乞丐，揩油的人，寄生虫
People who habitually take advantage of the generosity of others without giving anything in return.
Those moochers never pay for their own drinks. 那些揩油的人从不自己付钱买饮料。
scroungers, freeloaders, parasites
givers, contributors
noun
