lends:/lɛndz/
verb
借出，贷给
grants the temporary use of something on the condition that it or its equivalent will be returned
He lends his car to his brother every weekend. 他每个周末都把车借给他弟弟。
synonyms: loans, advances, provides, supplies
antonyms: borrows, takes, receives
词性: 动词
