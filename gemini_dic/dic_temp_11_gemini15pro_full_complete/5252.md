allodial:/əˈloʊdiəl/
adjective
自由保有土地的；完全保有土地的
Of, relating to, or denoting land owned absolutely and not subject to any feudal service or duty.
例句：He owned the land allodially. 他完全拥有这块土地。

例句：Allodial title is the absolute ownership of real property.  自由保有土地所有权是指对不动产的绝对所有权。

近义词：freehold, absolute
反义词：feudal, leasehold
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Medieval Latin *allodialis, from Old High German allōd, meaning "full property," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to all "all" + ōd "property."
