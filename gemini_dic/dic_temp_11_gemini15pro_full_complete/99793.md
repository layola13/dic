intermeshed:/ˌɪntərˈmeʃt/
adjective
相互缠绕的，紧密交织的
So closely connected that they are difficult to separate
The company's operations are intermeshed with the local community. 该公司的运营与当地社区紧密相连。
Their lives had become so intermeshed that they couldn't imagine separating.  他们的生活已经如此紧密地交织在一起，以至于他们无法想象分开。
近义词:intertwined, interwoven, entangled
反义词:separate, detached, disentangled
词性:adjective
词源:From inter- + mesh (to engage or fit together)
