depardieux:/ˌdɛpɑːrˈdjøː/ /ˌdɛpɑːrˈdɜː/
interj.
我的天哪；哎呀
An exclamation of surprise or emphasis, archaic.
例句：Depardieux! I had no idea you were here. 我的天哪！我不知道你在这里。

例句：Depardieux, what a magnificent sight! 哎呀，多么壮观的景象！

近义词：good heavens, my goodness, by Jove, zounds, egad
反义词:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation of surprise)
词性: interjection
