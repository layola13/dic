anises: /ˈænɪsɪz/ | n. |茴香（复数）|The plural form of anise, referring to the seeds of the Pimpinella anisum plant, used as a spice and in medicine. |Aniseeds are often used in baking. 茴香籽通常用于烘焙。|Synonym: aniseed |Antonym: N/A |noun

anises: /ˈænɪsɪz/ | n. |八角茴香（复数）|The plural form of anise, sometimes referring to the star anise spice, Illicium verum, although technically a different plant. |These anises have a stronger, licorice-like flavor.  这些八角茴香的味道更浓，像甘草。|Synonym: star anise (sometimes) |Antonym: N/A |noun
