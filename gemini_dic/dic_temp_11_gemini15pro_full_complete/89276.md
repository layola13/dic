homochromous:/ˌhəʊməˈkrəʊməs/ | adj. | 同色的，颜色相同的 | Of the same color throughout; uniformly colored. | Example 1: The homochromous plumage of the bird made it difficult to spot against the similarly colored foliage.  这只鸟颜色一致的羽毛使它很难在颜色相似的树叶中被发现。

Example 2:  The artist used a homochromous palette of blues to create a calming effect. 艺术家使用了一个同色系的蓝色调色板来营造一种平静的效果。 | 近义词: monochromatic, unicolor, concolorous | 反义词:  varicolored, multicolored, polychromatic | 词性: adjective

词源:
homo- (Greek: same) + chroma (Greek: color)
