brokerly:/ˈbroʊkərli/| adj.|经纪人似的，像经纪人的；有经纪人风度的|Resembling or characteristic of a broker; having the manner or style of a broker.  Often implies a focus on deal-making, negotiation, or sometimes a slightly aggressive or pushy salesmanship.|He had a brokerly manner, always looking for the next deal. 他有一种经纪人似的作风，总是在寻找下一笔交易。|Synonyms: entrepreneurial, businesslike, commercial, mercenary|Antonyms: altruistic, charitable, philanthropic|adjective
