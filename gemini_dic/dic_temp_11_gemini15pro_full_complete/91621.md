hyperbarbarisms:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːrbərɪzəmz/
|n.|过度野蛮行为；极端野蛮行径|An act of extreme barbarity; excessive cruelty or savagery.  It refers to acts that are considered beyond the pale of even typical barbarism.|The dictator's regime was marked by numerous hyperbarbarisms, including public executions and torture.|近义词：atrocities, savagery, brutality|反义词：civilization, humanity, kindness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From hyper- + barbarism.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, above, beyond." "Barbarism" comes from the Greek "barbarismos," referring to the speech or customs of foreigners, perceived as uncivilized.
