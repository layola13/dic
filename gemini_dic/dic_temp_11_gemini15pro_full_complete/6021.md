ameliorate:/əˈmiːliəreɪt/
verb
改善，改进
to make something bad or unpleasant better
The new law is intended to ameliorate the situation of the poorest families. 新法律旨在改善最贫困家庭的处境。

近义词:improve, enhance, better, upgrade
反义词:worsen, aggravate, deteriorate
词性:v.


Etymology:
From Late Latin *amelioratus, past participle of ameliorare "to make better," from Latin ad- "to" + melior "better."
