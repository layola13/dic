kludge:/klʌdʒ/
|n. vt.|权宜之计；凑合的方案；粗制滥造的系统|A clumsy or inelegant, yet effective, solution to a problem.|The software is a kludge, but it works. 这个软件很笨拙，但是能用。|近义词：workaround, makeshift, hack|反义词：elegant solution, streamlined system|词性: 名词, 动词

kludged:/klʌdʒd/
|v.|（用权宜之计）修补；拼凑|Past tense and past participle of "kludge," meaning to patch together or fix something in a clumsy or inelegant way.|The system was kludged together from spare parts.  这个系统是用备件拼凑起来的。|近义词：patched up, cobbled together|反义词：properly repaired, expertly crafted|词性: 动词

词源：
The origin of "kludge" is uncertain, but it likely emerged in the mid-20th century, possibly from the German word "klotz," meaning "block" or "lump."  Another possible origin is the Scottish word "cludgie," a slang term for a toilet, implying something messy or awkward.
