ensue:/ɪnˈsuː/
verb
接着发生; 继而发生;
to happen after something else, especially as a result of it
例句：The police officer said that problems would ensue if the fans were not allowed to leave the stadium in an orderly fashion.  警察说如果不让球迷们有秩序地离开体育场，就会出现问题。

例句：After the heavy rains, floods ensued.  暴雨过后，发生了洪水。

近义词：follow, result, succeed, stem, arise, derive
反义词：precede, cause
词性：verb
