nasturtium:/nəˈstɜːʃəm/ /næˈstɜːrʃəm/| n.|旱金莲；金莲花|A flowering plant with brightly coloured, usually orange or yellow, funnel-shaped flowers and rounded leaves. Its leaves and flowers are edible.|The nasturtiums in her garden were a vibrant splash of colour.|近义词：Indian cress|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *nasturcium*, alteration of *nasitortium*, literally "nose-twister," from *nasus* "nose" + *torquēre* "to twist," referring to the pungent smell.
