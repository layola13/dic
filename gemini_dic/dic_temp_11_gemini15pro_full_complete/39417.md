connation:/ˌkɑːnəˈteɪʃn/| n.|含义，言外之意|An idea or feeling that a word invokes in addition to its literal or primary meaning.|The word "home" has connotations of warmth and family. “家”这个词含有温暖和家庭的含义。|近义词：implication, overtone, nuance|反义词：denotation|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *connōtātiōn- (stem of connōtātiō ), from connōtāre ‘signify in addition’, from con- ‘together with’ + nōtāre ‘to mark’.
