fensi: /ˈfɛnsi/ (US), /ˈfænsi/ (UK) | n. |粉丝; 狂热爱好者; 迷| A fan of someone or something, especially a devoted enthusiast.| Example: She's a big fensi of that K-pop band. 她非常喜欢那个韩国流行乐队。| Example: He's a real fensi of vintage cars. 他是个不折不扣的老爷车迷。| 近义词: fan, admirer, devotee, enthusiast | 反义词: hater, critic | 词性: noun

词源: "fensi" is a transliteration of the English word "fans," plural of "fan."  It's borrowed directly and used in Chinese slang and informal contexts.
