obligor:/ˈɑːblɪɡɔːr/ (US), /əˈblɪɡɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|债务人，义务人|A person or entity that is contractually or legally obligated to provide something, typically payment or performance of a specific duty, to another party (the obligee).|The obligor is required to make monthly payments to the obligee.|近义词：debtor, payer|反义词：obligee, creditor|noun
