avowal:/əˈvaʊəl/
n.
公开声明；承认；坦白
An open statement of something; an acknowledgment; a frank declaration or admission.
She made an avowal of her love for him. 她坦白了她对他的爱。
近义词：declaration, admission, confession, affirmation, acknowledgment
反义词：denial, repudiation, disclaimer
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *avowal*, from Anglo-Norman *avouer* (to avow), from Latin *advocare* (to call to, summon, claim).
