babiest:/ˈbeɪbiɪst/| adj. |最婴儿化的，最幼稚的|  Most like a baby; most infantile.  Characterized by childish or immature behavior. |例句：He has the babiest face I've ever seen. 他有着我见过最婴儿化的脸庞。

例句：She acted so babiest when she didn't get her way. 当她没有达到目的时，她表现得非常幼稚。|近义词：childish, infantile, immature, juvenile |反义词:mature, adult, grown-up |词性:形容词
词源:  "babiest" is the superlative form of the adjective "babyish." "Baby" itself comes from Middle English *babi*, likely imitative of baby sounds. The "-ish" suffix forms adjectives meaning "like" or "having the qualities of." The "-est" suffix creates the superlative form.
