biretta:/bɪˈrɛtə/ | n. | 方帽；主教冠；学士帽 | A square cap with three or four ridges on top, worn by Roman Catholic clergy, especially during religious ceremonies. | The bishop donned his biretta before the service. 主教在仪式开始前戴上了方帽。 |  The cardinal's biretta was scarlet.  红衣主教的方帽是猩红色的。 | synonyms: cap, headwear | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bareheadedness") | noun
