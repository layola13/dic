factorize:/ˈfæk.tə.raɪz/ /ˈfæk.tər.aɪz/ | v. | 因式分解，分解…的因子 | To express (a number, polynomial, matrix, etc.) as a product of factors. |  We can factorize the expression x² - 4 as (x-2)(x+2).  我们将表达式 x² - 4 因式分解为 (x-2)(x+2)。 | synonyms: resolve, decompose, analyze | antonyms: expand, multiply | verb

factorize:/ˈfæk.tə.raɪz/ /ˈfæk.tər.aɪz/ | n. | 因式分解 | The process of expressing (a number, polynomial, matrix, etc.) as a product of factors. | The factorization of the number 12 is 2 x 2 x 3.  数字 12 的因式分解是 2 x 2 x 3。 | synonyms: decomposition, resolution, analysis | antonyms: expansion, multiplication | noun
