imaginers:/ɪˈmædʒɪnərz/
noun
想象者，幻想家
A person who imagines or conceives of new ideas, especially in a creative or inventive way.
Walt Disney Imagineers create magical experiences for people around the world.  华特迪士尼幻想工程师为世界各地的人们创造神奇的体验。

The imaginers behind the new video game have created a stunning virtual world.  这款新电子游戏背后的想象者们创造了一个令人惊叹的虚拟世界。

近义词:creators, innovators, visionaries, dreamers
反义词:realists, pragmatists
词性:n
