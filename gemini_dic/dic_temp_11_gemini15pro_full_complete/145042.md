overaggravating: /ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
过分加剧的；过分恶化的
Excessively aggravating; making a situation or problem worse than it already is.
That overaggravating noise is giving me a headache.  那过分恼人的噪音让我头疼。
His overaggravating comments only fueled the argument. 他过分激烈的评论只会加剧争论。
Synonyms:  exasperating, infuriating, irritating, annoying
Antonyms:  soothing, calming, alleviating, mitigating
词性: 形容词
