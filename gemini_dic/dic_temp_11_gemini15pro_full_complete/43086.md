cowritten:/ˌkoʊˈrɪtn/
adjective
共同撰写的
written by two or more people
The song was cowritten by John Lennon and Paul McCartney. 这首歌是由约翰·列侬和保罗·麦卡特尼共同创作的。
co-authored, jointly written
solely written
adjective
