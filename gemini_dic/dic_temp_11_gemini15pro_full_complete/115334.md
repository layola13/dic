mannitol:/ˈmænɪˌtɒl/ (US) /ˈmænɪˌtɔːl/ (UK)| n.|甘露醇|A white crystalline alcohol found in many plants and used as a diuretic (increases urination) and a sweetening agent.|Mannitol is administered intravenously to reduce pressure inside the skull.|Synonyms: osmotic diuretic, hexitol|Antonyms: antidiuretic|noun
