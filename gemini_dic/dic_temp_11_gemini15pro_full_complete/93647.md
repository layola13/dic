imbreed:/ɪnˈbriːd/ | v. |近亲繁殖，同系交配 | To breed from closely related animals or people, especially over many generations. |
例句：The negative effects of inbreeding are well documented. 近亲繁殖的负面影响已有充分的文献记载。

例句：The royal family had a history of inbreeding.  这个皇室家族有近亲结婚的历史。

近义词：interbreed, line breed | 反义词：outbreed, crossbreed | 词性：动词
