beaverish:/ˈbiːvərɪʃ/| adj.|忙碌的，勤奋的，像海狸一样忙碌的|Resembling or characteristic of a beaver, especially in being industrious or constantly busy.|He's been beaverish lately, working late every night to finish the project. 他最近一直很忙，每天晚上都工作到很晚以完成这个项目。|例句：She's been beaverish all week, preparing for the conference. 她整个星期都忙于准备会议。

例句：He had a beaverish attitude towards his work, always eager to take on more. 他对工作有一种勤奋的态度，总是渴望承担更多。


|近义词：industrious, diligent, assiduous, hardworking|反义词：lazy, indolent, idle|词性:形容词
