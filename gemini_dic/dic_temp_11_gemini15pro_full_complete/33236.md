chronography: /krəˈnɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 年代学；编年史；计时法 | The study of time, especially its accurate measurement and methods of recording it. It can also refer to the descriptive recording of historical events in chronological order. |  His book is a detailed chronology of the war. 他的书是对这场战争的详细编年史。| synonyms: annals, chronicle, history, record | antonyms:  disorder, chaos | noun

chronography: /krəˈnɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 年代学；编年史；计时法 | The science of measuring time accurately.
