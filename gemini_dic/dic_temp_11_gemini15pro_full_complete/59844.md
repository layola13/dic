ectoplasmic:/ˌɛktəˈplæzmɪk/| adj.| 外质的；灵魂的；幽灵似的|Relating to ectoplasm, a viscous substance supposedly emanating from a medium during a trance.|A faint, ectoplasmic glow seemed to emanate from the darkened room.  房间里似乎散发着微弱的、灵魂般的光芒。|Synonyms: spectral, ghostly, phantom|Antonyms: material, corporeal|adjective


ectoplasm:/ˈɛktəʊˌplæzəm/| n.| 外质；灵魂物质|A viscous substance supposedly exuded by a medium during a trance.|The medium claimed to be able to materialize ectoplasm.  灵媒声称能够使灵魂物质实体化。|Synonyms: spiritual matter, psychic emanation|Antonyms: physical matter|noun
