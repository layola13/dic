geniculation:/dʒəˌnɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/
n.
弯曲，屈膝，(尤指)膝关节
The bending of a joint, especially the knee.
The geniculation of the nerve occurs at the level of the superior colliculus.  该神经在顶盖水平弯曲。
flexion, bending
straightening, extension
noun

geniculate:/ˈdʒɛnɪkjʊlət/
adj.
膝状的，有节的，弯曲的
Bent like a knee; having joints or nodes.
The geniculate ganglion is a collection of sensory neurons located in the facial canal. 膝状神经节是位于面神经管内的一组感觉神经元。
jointed, knotted, bent
straight, unbent
adjective
