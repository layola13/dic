aleck:/ˈælɪk/| n.|自作聪明的人，爱抬杠的人|A person, typically a man, who is regarded as annoyingly clever and self-assured; a smart aleck.|He's such an aleck, always trying to show off how much he knows. 他真是个自作聪明的人，总是试图炫耀他知道多少。|近义词：smart aleck, wise guy, know-it-all|反义词: humble person, modest person|词性:noun

alecks:/ˈælɪks/| n.|自作聪明的人，爱抬杠的人（复数）|Plural form of "aleck," referring to multiple people who are annoyingly clever and self-assured.|Those alecks think they know everything. 那些自作聪明的人以为他们什么都知道。|近义词：smart alecks, wise guys, know-it-alls|反义词: humble people, modest people|词性:noun
