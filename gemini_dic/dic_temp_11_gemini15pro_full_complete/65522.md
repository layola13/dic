eurypterus:/jʊˈrɪptərəs/
n.
广翅鲎（板足鲎的一种）
A large extinct arthropod of the Paleozoic Era, related to horseshoe crabs and scorpions.  It is a type of sea scorpion.
The Eurypterus remipes is one of the most well-known species. 广翅鲎雷米普斯是最著名的物种之一。
The museum displayed a fossil of a Eurypterus. 博物馆展出了广翅鲎的化石。
Synonyms: sea scorpion
