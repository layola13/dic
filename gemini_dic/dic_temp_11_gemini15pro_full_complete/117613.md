megaphyton:/ˈmɛɡəˌfaɪtɒn/ /ˈmɛɡəˌfɪˌtɑn/| n. |巨型叶植物；大型叶|A plant with large, well-developed leaves, typically associated with ferns and seed plants.|Example: Ferns are considered megaphytons due to their large fronds.  蕨类植物因其巨大的叶子而被认为是巨型叶植物。

Example:  The evolution of megaphytons was a significant step in plant development. 巨型叶植物的进化是植物发展的重要一步。|近义词:macrophyll|反义词:microphyll|词性:noun
