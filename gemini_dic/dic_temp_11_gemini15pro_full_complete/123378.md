monometallist:/ˌmɒnəʊˈmɛtəlɪst/ | n. |单本位制论者|A person who advocates monometallism (a monetary system in which the standard economic unit of account is based on a single metal, traditionally gold or silver).|The monometallists argued vehemently against the introduction of bimetallism. 单本位制论者强烈反对实行双本位制。|Synonym: gold standard advocate, bullionist|Antonym: bimetallist|noun
