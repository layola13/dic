emulsifier:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪər/| n.|乳化剂|A substance that stabilizes an emulsion, preventing liquids that usually do not mix from separating.|Egg yolk is a natural emulsifier used in mayonnaise.|Synonyms: stabilizer, binding agent|Antonyms: demulsifier|noun
