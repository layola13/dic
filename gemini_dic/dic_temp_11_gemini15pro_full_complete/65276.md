euchlorine:/jʊˈklɔːriːn/
noun
氯化物
A mixture of chlorine dioxide and chlorine.  A greenish-yellow gas with an irritating odor.
Euchlorine was used as a disinfectant in the 19th century.  氯化物在19世纪被用作消毒剂。
Synonyms: chlorine dioxide, chlorine peroxide
