gamebred:/ˈɡeɪmbred/| adj.| 斗性十足的；为比赛而生的；有战斗力的| Bred or trained for hunting or fighting; having a spirited, competitive nature. | He's a gamebred fighter, always ready for a challenge. 他是一位斗性十足的战士，随时准备迎接挑战。| The gamebred stallion was eager to race. 这匹为比赛而生的种马渴望比赛。 |  近义词：spirited, plucky, courageous, gritty, tenacious | 反义词：timid, cowardly, fearful, apprehensive | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "game" (in the sense of spirited or plucky) + "bred" (past participle of "breed," meaning to raise or train). The word emphasizes the inherent qualities instilled through breeding or training.
