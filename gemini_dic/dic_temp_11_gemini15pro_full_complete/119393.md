microelectronic:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪlɛkˈtrɒnɪk/ | adj. |微电子的|Relating to or involving microelectronics; based on or using microelectronic technology.  Pertaining to the design and fabrication of integrated circuits and other small electronic components.| Microelectronic circuits are used in a wide range of devices. 微电子电路应用于各种设备。| synonyms: integrated, miniaturized | antonyms: macroelectronic, large-scale | adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
micro- (small) + electronic (relating to electronics) from Greek "mikros" (small) + "elektron" (amber, referring to static electricity).
