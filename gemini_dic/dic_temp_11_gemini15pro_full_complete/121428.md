misinforming: /ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ | v. | 误导，提供错误信息 | Giving someone wrong or inaccurate information, often intentionally. |  They were accused of misinforming the public about the dangers of the product. 他们被指控向公众提供了有关该产品危险性的错误信息。

The advertisement was misinforming consumers about the product's actual capabilities.  这则广告误导了消费者对该产品实际功能的认知。

| 近义词: misleading, deceiving, lying | 反义词: informing, enlightening, educating | 词性: 动词
