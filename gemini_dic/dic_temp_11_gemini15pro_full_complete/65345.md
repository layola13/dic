eulogical: /juːˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (UK) /juːˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ (US)| adj.|颂扬的，赞美的|Expressing or containing eulogy; praising highly.|例句：He delivered a eulogical speech at the funeral. 他在葬礼上致了悼词。|例句：The obituary was eulogical in its description of her life and achievements.  讣告高度赞扬了她的一生和成就。|近义词：laudatory, complimentary, praising, commendatory|反义词：critical, derogatory, defamatory|词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *eulogia* "praise," from *eu* "well" + *logos* "word, speech."
