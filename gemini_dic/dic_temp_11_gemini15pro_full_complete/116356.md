mastic:/ˈmæstɪk/ (US), /ˈmɑːstɪk/ (UK)| n. adj.|乳香，胶泥，填缝料|A resin obtained from the mastic tree, used in varnishes, adhesives, and chewing gum.  Also, a putty-like material used for sealing cracks or gaps.|The artist used mastic varnish to protect the painting.  艺术家用乳香清漆保护这幅画。|近义词:resin, gum, sealant, putty|反义词:(n.) solvent  (adj.) porous|n. adj.
