fotive:/ˈvəʊtɪv/| adj.|奉献的，还愿的|Offered or performed in fulfillment of a vow or in gratitude or devotion.|例句：She lit a votive candle in the church. 她在教堂里点燃了一支还愿蜡烛。|近义词：dedicatory, consecrated, offertory|反义词: None|词性: Adjective
fotive:/ˈvəʊtɪv/| n.|还愿物|An object offered or dedicated in fulfillment of a vow or in gratitude or devotion.|例句：The museum displays a collection of votive offerings. 博物馆展出了一批还愿物。|近义词：offering, dedication, oblation|反义词: None|词性: Noun
