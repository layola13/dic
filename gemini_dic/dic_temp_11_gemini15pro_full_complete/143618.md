outjesting:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɛstɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的玩笑; 以玩笑胜过
Surpassing in jesting; exceeding in wit or humor.
He outjested the comedian with a witty retort. 他以机智的反驳胜过了喜剧演员的玩笑。
Synonyms: outwitting, surpassing, exceeding
Antonyms: losing to, being outdone by
词性: 动词
