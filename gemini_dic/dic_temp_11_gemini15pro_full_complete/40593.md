convalescent:/ˌkɒnvəˈlɛsənt/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛsənt/| adj. n. |恢复期的，康复中的；恢复期病人 |Recovering from an illness or medical treatment. | The convalescent patient was finally able to walk.  这位康复中的病人终于能够行走了。 | 近义词: recovering, recuperating | 反义词: sick, ill | 词性: 形容词，名词

convalesce:/ˌkɒnvəˈlɛs/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛs/| vi. |(从疾病或受伤中)恢复，康复 |Recover one's health and strength over a period of time after an illness or operation. |She spent weeks convalescing at home after the accident. 事故发生后，她在家里疗养了几个星期。 | 近义词: recuperate, recover | 反义词: deteriorate, worsen | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *convalescere, from Latin con- ‘together’ + valescere ‘grow strong’.
