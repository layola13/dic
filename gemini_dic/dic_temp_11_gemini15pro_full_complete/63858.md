epigram:/ˈepɪɡræm/ | n. | 警句，隽语 | A short, witty saying, often with a satirical or paradoxical twist. |  Example 1: Oscar Wilde was famous for his epigrams. 奥斯卡·王尔德以他的警句而闻名。

Example 2:  "I can resist everything except temptation" is one of Oscar Wilde's most famous epigrams. “我能抵抗一切，除了诱惑”是奥斯卡·王尔德最著名的警句之一。 | synonym: quip, witticism, aphorism, maxim, adage | antonym: platitude, cliché, banality | noun
