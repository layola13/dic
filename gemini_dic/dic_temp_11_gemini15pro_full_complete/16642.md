barouchet:/bɑːruːˈʃeɪ/ | n. |  马车; 四轮马车 | A four-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, especially one with a folding hood. |  We took a barouchet through the park. 我们乘四轮马车穿过公园。 | Example: The barouchet clip-clopped down the cobblestone street. 四轮马车沿着鹅卵石街道发出得得的响声。| Synonym: carriage, coach | Antonym: N/A | noun

Etymology: From French *barouchet*, diminutive of *barouche*.
