boast: /boʊst/ | v. n. | 吹嘘，自夸；拥有（值得自豪的东西）|  To speak with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities. Can also refer to possessing something to be proud of. |  He boasted about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。| synonyms: brag, crow, vaunt | antonyms: downplay, minimize, deprecate | verb

boast: /boʊst/ | n. | 吹嘘，自夸；值得骄傲的事物 |  An act of boasting; a proud statement; something that is boasted about. | The town boasts a beautiful beach.  这个小镇拥有一片美丽的沙滩。| synonyms: bragging, vaunt, puffery | antonyms: modesty, humility | noun
