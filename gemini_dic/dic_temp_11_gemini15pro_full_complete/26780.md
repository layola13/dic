caesurae:/siˈzjʊəriː/ /sɪˈzʊriː/
noun
停顿；休止；诗行中的停顿
A pause or break in a line of verse, dictated by the natural rhythm of the language or enforced by the metrical pattern.
The poem uses caesurae to create a sense of drama. 这首诗运用停顿来营造一种戏剧性的感觉。
