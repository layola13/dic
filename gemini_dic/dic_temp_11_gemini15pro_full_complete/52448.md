died-off:/daɪd ˈɒf/ | phrasal verb | 灭绝，逐渐消失 |  To gradually decrease in number until none are left.  It describes a population or a group of things ceasing to exist over a period of time. |  The dinosaurs died off millions of years ago.  恐龙在数百万年前灭绝了。

The local population of wild rabbits has died off due to disease.  由于疾病，当地野生兔的数量逐渐消失了。| 近义词: become extinct, die out, perish, disappear | 反义词: flourish, thrive, proliferate | 词性: 短语动词


Etymology:  'die' (Old English *dēgan, from Proto-Germanic *dagijaną) + 'off' (Old English 'of', meaning 'away from, from, off').  The combination "die off" creates the sense of gradually ceasing to exist.
