horse-like:/ˈhɔːrslaɪk/
adjective
像马的；马似的
Resembling a horse in appearance, qualities, or behavior.
The creature had a horse-like head and a lion's body.  这个生物有着像马一样的头和狮子的身体。
The athlete displayed horse-like strength and endurance.  这位运动员展现出像马一样的力量和耐力。
近义词:equine, horsely
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective
词源:horse + like.  'Horse' comes from Old English *hors, from Proto-Germanic *hursa-, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱurso-. 'Like' comes from Old English gelīc, of Germanic origin.
