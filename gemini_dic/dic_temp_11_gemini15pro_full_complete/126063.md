muster:/ˈmʌstər/ (US), /ˈmʌstə/ (UK)| n. v.|集合，召集，聚集； muster up 鼓起(勇气等)|

**Noun (名词):**
* The troops passed in muster before the general. 部队在将军面前接受检阅。
* They couldn't muster enough votes to win the election. 他们无法召集到足够的选票来赢得选举。

**Verb (动词):**
* The general mustered his troops. 将军召集了他的部队。
* She mustered all her courage to face the challenge. 她鼓起所有的勇气来面对挑战。

|近义词：assemble, gather, summon, collect, rally|
|反义词: disperse, scatter, dismiss|
|词性: noun, verb|
