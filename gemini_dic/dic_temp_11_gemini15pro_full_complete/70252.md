fictionalizes:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使小说化；虚构化
to make into fiction; to treat as if fictional
The movie fictionalizes the life of a famous scientist.  这部电影将一位著名科学家的生平小说化了。
The author fictionalized the events of his childhood. 作者将他童年的经历虚构化了。
Synonyms: novelize, dramatize, romanticize, invent, fabricate
Antonyms: document, report, factualize
词性: 动词
