generation's:/ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.|一代人的；同辈人的|belonging to or associated with a generation.  The possessive form of "generation."| This is a problem for our generation's children. 这是我们这一代孩子的问题。|近义词: contemporaries', cohort's |反义词: ancestors', predecessors'|Possessive noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *generacioun*, from Old French *generacion*, from Latin *generātiō* ("a begetting, birth, descent, race, kind, family, production"), from *generātus*, perfect passive participle of *generō* ("I beget, bring forth, create"), from Proto-Italic *ǵenh₁erā-*, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵenh₁- (“to give birth, beget”).
