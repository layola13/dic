cut a deal:/kʌt ə diːl/
verb phrase
达成交易；做成买卖；达成协议
To reach an agreement or compromise, often in business or politics.
The two companies cut a deal to share the profits.  两家公司达成协议分享利润。
The government cut a deal with the rebels to end the war. 政府与叛军达成协议结束战争。
近义词: make a deal, strike a bargain, reach an agreement, come to terms
反义词: break off negotiations, call off a deal
