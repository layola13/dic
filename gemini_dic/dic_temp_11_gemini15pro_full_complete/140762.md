omnivorism:/ˈɒmnɪvɔːrɪzəm/ (US) /ˈɑːmnɪvɔːrɪzəm/| n. |杂食性|The practice of consuming both animal and plant matter as food.|Humans are a classic example of omnivorism.|近义词：omnivory|反义词: herbivorism, carnivorism|名词

omnivore:/ˈɒmnɪvɔː(r)/ (US) /ˈɑːmnɪvɔːr/| n. |杂食动物|An animal that eats both plants and animals.|Bears are omnivores; they eat berries and fish.|近义词：omnivorous animal|反义词: herbivore, carnivore|名词

omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ (US) /ɑːmˈnɪvərəs/| adj. |杂食的|Eating both plants and animals.|Pigs are omnivorous creatures, consuming a wide variety of foods.|近义词：all-devouring|反义词: herbivorous, carnivorous|形容词
