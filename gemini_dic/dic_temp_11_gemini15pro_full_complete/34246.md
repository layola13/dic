clap-stick's:/ˈklæpˌstɪks/ | n. |击掌棒;响板 |A pair of sticks held one in each hand and clapped together rhythmically, typically used as a percussion instrument. |例句：The dancers clicked their clap-sticks together in a lively rhythm. 舞蹈者们用响板打出活泼的节奏。

例句：He made his own clap-sticks from pieces of hardwood. 他用硬木块制作了自己的响板。|近义词：claves, rhythm sticks |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
