co-operative:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/
n.|合作社；合作商店|An organization or business owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits.|Example:  She's a member of a housing co-operative. 她是一个住房合作社的成员。|Synonym: collective, communal|Antonym: private, individual|Noun
co-operative:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/
adj.|合作的；协作的|Involving mutual assistance in working toward a common goal.|Example: The project requires co-operative effort from all team members. 这个项目需要所有团队成员的共同努力。|Synonym: collaborative, joint|Antonym: uncooperative, individual|Adjective


Etymology:
From the late 18th century, from co-operate + -ive. Co-operate comes from Latin co- "together" + operari "to work".
