epipedometry:/ˌɛpɪˌpɪdəˈmɛtri/
n.
表面测量学; 表面测绘
The measurement of surface area, especially of the human body.
Epipedometry is used in burn units to accurately assess the extent of skin damage. 表面测绘用于烧伤病房，以准确评估皮肤损伤的程度。
Epipedometry plays a crucial role in determining the appropriate treatment for burn victims.  表面测绘在确定烧伤患者的适当治疗方案中起着至关重要的作用。
synonyms: surface measurement, planimetry
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but one could consider volumetric measurement as a contrasting concept)
noun
