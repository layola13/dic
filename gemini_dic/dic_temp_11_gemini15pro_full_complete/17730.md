bedecked:/bɪˈdekt/
adjective
装饰的，装点的
adorned or decorated elaborately.
The Christmas tree was bedecked with colorful ornaments. 圣诞树上装饰着五彩缤纷的饰品。
近义词:adorned, decorated, ornamented, embellished
反义词:bare, plain, unadorned, undecorated
词性:adjective

bedecked:/bɪˈdekt/
verb (past participle)
装饰，装点
To decorate elaborately; to adorn.  It's the past participle of the verb "bedeck."
The queen's gown was bedecked with jewels. 女王的礼服上装饰着珠宝。
近义词:adorned, decorated, ornamented, embellished
反义词:stripped, bare
词性:verb (past participle)
