lovably:/ˈlʌvəbli/| adj.|可爱地，讨人喜爱地|In a way that inspires love or affection.  It describes something or someone that is easy to love.|She has a lovably quirky personality. 她有着讨人喜爱的古怪个性。|近义词：adorably, charmingly, sweetly|反义词：detestably, hatefully|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "love" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability or worthiness). "Love" comes from Old English "lufu,"  from Proto-Germanic "*lubō,"  of uncertain origin.
