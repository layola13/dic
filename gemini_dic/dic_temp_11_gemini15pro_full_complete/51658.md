deviatory:/ˌdēviˈātɔːri/ /ˌdiːviˈeɪtəri/
adjective
偏离的，离轨的
Straying from a norm or standard; differing from an expected course or procedure.
例句：The spacecraft's deviatory trajectory caused concern among mission control.  航天器的偏离轨道引起了任务控制中心的担忧。

例句：His deviatory behavior led to his dismissal from the company.  他偏离常规的行为导致他被公司解雇。

近义词：aberrant, divergent, anomalous, irregular, atypical
反义词：normal, standard, typical, regular, usual
词性：adjective
