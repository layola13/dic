hyperimmunizing:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪmjʊnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
超免疫
The process of inducing a heightened immune response, typically by repeated administrations of an antigen.
The researchers are hyperimmunizing horses to produce antivenom. 研究人员正在对马进行超免疫以生产抗蛇毒血清。
synonyms:  immunizing intensely, boosting immunity, enhancing immune response
antonyms: immunosuppressing, weakening immunity
词性: 动词
