anecdotally:/ˌænɪkˈdəʊtəli/ | adv. | 传闻地，轶事地 | based on personal accounts rather than facts or research |  Many people have anecdotally reported feeling better after taking this supplement, but there's no scientific evidence to support this.  很多人传闻服用这种补充剂后感觉好多了，但没有科学证据支持这一点。 | Example 2: Anecdotally, the new traffic laws seem to be reducing accidents, though official statistics aren't available yet.  据说，新的交通法规似乎正在减少事故，尽管官方统计数据尚未公布。| Synonyms: informally, unofficially, reportedly | Antonyms: scientifically, empirically, officially | part of speech: adverb
