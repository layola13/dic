bewet:/bɪˈwɛt/ | v. |弄湿，浸湿 | To make something wet; to soak or drench. | The sudden rain bewet his clothes. 突然的雨淋湿了他的衣服。| synonyms: soak, drench, saturate | antonyms: dry, desiccate | verb

Etymology: From Middle English *biweten*, from Old English *bewǣtan*, from *be-* (prefix meaning "thoroughly") + *wǣtan* (to wet).
