agleam:/əˈɡliːm/| adj.|闪闪发光的，闪亮的|Shining brightly; gleaming.|Her eyes were agleam with excitement. 她的眼睛因兴奋而闪闪发光。|The polished silver was agleam in the candlelight. 擦亮的银器在烛光下闪闪发光。|近义词：shining, gleaming, glistening, sparkling, radiant|反义词：dull, dim, lackluster|词性:形容词
Etymology: From a- + gleam.  "A-" is a prefix meaning "on" or "in a particular state or condition."  "Gleam" comes from the Old English "glæm," meaning "brightness," related to the Old Norse "gleima" and the German "gleißen."
