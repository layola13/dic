imido:/ˈɪmɪdoʊ/
imido|adj.|亚氨基的|Relating to or containing the =NH group, derived from ammonia by replacement of two hydrogen atoms.|The imido group is a key component in many organic compounds. 亚氨基是许多有机化合物的关键组成部分。|近义词:imino|反义词:N/A|词性:形容词

imido:/ˈɪmɪdoʊ/
imido|n.|亚氨基|The =NH group, derived from ammonia by replacement of two hydrogen atoms.|The imido ligand can form strong bonds with metal ions. 亚氨基配体可以与金属离子形成强键。|近义词:imino|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *imidus, from im- (variant of in- "in, into, on, upon, against") + -idus (adjectival suffix).  Related to amine.
