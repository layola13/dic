demi-tint:/ˈdɛmiˌtɪnt/| n.|半色调|A method of reproducing a continuous tone image, such as a photograph, by using dots of varying sizes or spacing to simulate different tones.|The artist used demi-tint to create a subtle effect in his drawing.|近义词:halftone|反义词:full tone|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French demi-teinte, from demi- "half" + teinte "tint". Ultimately from Latin dimidius "half" and tingere "to dye".
