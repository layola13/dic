melanins:/ˈmɛlənɪnz/ | n. |黑色素|A dark brown to black pigment occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals. It is responsible for tanning of skin exposed to sunlight.|例句：Exposure to sunlight increases melanin production. 暴露在阳光下会增加黑色素的产生。

例句：People with darker skin have more melanin. 皮肤较黑的人拥有更多黑色素。|近义词：pigment |反义词: None |词性:noun
