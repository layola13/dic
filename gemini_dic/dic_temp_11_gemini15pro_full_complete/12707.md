assertor:/əˈsɜːrtər/ | n. |断言者，主张者|Someone who asserts something; a declarer. |例句：He was a strong assertor of his rights. 他坚定地维护自己的权利。

例句：The assertor of this theory has yet to provide concrete evidence.  这个理论的倡导者还没有提供具体的证据。


近义词：proponent, advocate, champion, defender, upholder |反义词:opponent, denier, challenger|词性:noun
