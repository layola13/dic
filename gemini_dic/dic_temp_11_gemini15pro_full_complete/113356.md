PHRASE->
(While "ly" itself doesn't form phrases, it modifies words to create adverbial phrases.  Here are some examples of commonly used adverbs ending in "ly":)

relatively slowly 相对缓慢地
surprisingly quickly 惊人地快
incredibly softly 难以置信地轻柔
exceptionally beautifully 格外美丽地
particularly clearly 特别清楚地
