flecks:/fleks/
noun
小斑点，斑纹，少量
small marks, spots, or specks of a different color or material, often found on a surface; a small amount of something.
There were flecks of paint on the floor. 地板上有一些油漆斑点。
The recipe calls for a few flecks of chili. 食谱要求放一些辣椒碎。
Synonyms: specks, spots, dots, particles, bits
Antonyms: swathes, expanse
词性: 名词


flecks:/fleks/
verb
(使)出现斑点，(使)有斑纹
to mark with flecks; to become covered with flecks.
The rust flecked the paintwork. 铁锈使油漆表面出现斑点。
His face flecked with tears. 他脸上沾满了泪水。
Synonyms: spot, speckle, dot, stipple
Antonyms: clear, clean
词性: 动词
