overcensorious:/ˌōvərˌsensˈɔːriəs/
adjective
过度审查的，吹毛求疵的
Inclined to criticize or find fault too readily; excessively critical.
She is overcensorious of her children's behavior, constantly nitpicking at every little thing they do. 她对孩子的行为过于苛刻，总是对他们做的每一件小事都吹毛求疵。
The overcensorious editor made so many changes to the article that it was almost unrecognizable. 吹毛求疵的编辑对文章做了如此多的修改，以至于几乎面目全非。
Synonyms: hypercritical, fault-finding, captious, censorious
Antonyms: tolerant, lenient, forgiving, indulgent
word type: adjective
