noctambulation: /nɒkˌtæmbjʊˈleɪʃn/ /nɑːkˌtæmbjəˈleɪʃən/
n.
梦游，夜游
The act of walking or performing other actions while asleep.
Sleepwalking, a sleep disorder characterized by walking or other activity while seemingly asleep.
Example: The child's noctambulation worried his parents.  这孩子的梦游症让他的父母很担心。

Example:  Episodes of noctambulation are common in children. 梦游症在儿童中很常见。

近义词: sleepwalking, somnambulism
反义词: wakefulness
词性: noun
