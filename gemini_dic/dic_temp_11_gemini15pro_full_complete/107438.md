lampist:/ˈlæmpɪst/| n.|点灯人，灯光师|A person who lights lamps, especially street lamps or theater lights.  Originally, someone who tended oil lamps.  Now more commonly associated with theater lighting.|The lampist adjusted the spotlight to highlight the lead actress.|Synonym: lighting technician, stagehand (in a theater context)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "stagehand" if considering other theater roles which aren't lighting related)|noun
