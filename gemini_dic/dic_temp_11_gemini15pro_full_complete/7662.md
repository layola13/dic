anodally:/ænˈɒdəli/ | adv. | 阳极地，向阳极地 |  Relating to or occurring at the anode, the positive electrode in an electrolytic cell. | The metal is anodally oxidized.  这种金属被阳极氧化。 | Synonym: positively | Antonym: cathodically | Word type: adverb

anodic:/ˈænɒdɪk/ | adj. | 阳极的 | Relating to or occurring at the anode. | The anodic reaction produces oxygen. 阳极反应产生氧气。| Synonym: positive | Antonym: cathodic | Word type: adjective
