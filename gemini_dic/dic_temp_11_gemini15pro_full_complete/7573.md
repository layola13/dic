annotation:/ˌænəˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 注解，注释，批注 | A note added to a text or diagram, giving explanation or comment. |  This edition of the book includes helpful annotations.  这版书包含有用的注释。

The annotation explains the historical context of the poem. 这段注释解释了这首诗的历史背景。


近义词：note, comment, remark, explanation, gloss
反义词： (No direct antonym, perhaps "omission")
词性：noun
