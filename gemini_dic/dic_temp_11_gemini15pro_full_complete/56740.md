doornail:/ˈdɔːrneɪl/| n.|门钉|A large-headed nail used to strengthen doors, especially old wooden ones.  Now primarily used in the idiom "dead as a doornail".|The old wooden door was studded with heavy doornails.|Near synonyms: nail, stud, rivet|Opposite: (No direct opposite, as it's a specific type of nail)|Noun
