ambitious:/æmˈbɪʃəs/| adj.|有雄心的，野心勃勃的|Having or showing a strong desire and determination to succeed.|He's an ambitious young lawyer. 他是一位雄心勃勃的年轻律师。|近义词:aspiring, determined, driven|反义词:unambitious, lazy, apathetic|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *ambitiōsus*, from *ambitiō* (“a going around, a canvassing for office, ambition”)
