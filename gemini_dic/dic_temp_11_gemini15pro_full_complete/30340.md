cent:/sɛnt/ | n. |分，一分钱|A unit of money equal to one hundredth of a dollar or other decimal currency unit. |Example: The gum costs ninety-nine cents.  这口香糖要99美分。|Synonyms: penny, red cent |Antonyms: dollar, euro |noun

cent:/sɛnt/ | n. |百分之一|One part in a hundred. |Example:  Fifty per cent of the students passed the test. 50%的学生通过了考试。|Synonyms: percentage, hundredth |Antonyms: whole, total |noun
