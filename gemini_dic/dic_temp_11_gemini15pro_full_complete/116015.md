marshlike:/ˈmɑːrʃlaɪk/| adj.|沼泽般的，像沼泽的|Resembling a marsh; swampy.|The ground was soft and marshlike after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，地面变得柔软，像沼泽一样。|The path led through marshlike terrain. 这条小路穿过一片沼泽般的地形。|近义词：swampy, boggy, miry|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词

词源:  From marsh +‎ -like.  "Marsh" comes from Old English *mersc (akin to Old Frisian mersk, Middle Dutch maersc, mersc, Dutch marsch, German Marsch), from West Germanic *marisk-, from Proto-Germanic *mariskaz, from Proto-Indo-European *mori- (“sea, lake, marsh”). "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
