mariculturist:/ˌmærɪˈkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. | 海水养殖者；海产养殖家 | A person who cultivates marine life, such as fish or shellfish, for food or other products. |例句：The mariculturist carefully monitored the water quality in the fish farm.  海水养殖者仔细监测养鱼场的水质。

例句：Experienced mariculturists understand the delicate balance of the marine ecosystem.  经验丰富的海水养殖者了解海洋生态系统的微妙平衡。| 近义词：aquaculturist, fish farmer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps poacher if considering illegal fishing impacting mariculture) | 词性：noun
