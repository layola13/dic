communisms: /ˈkɒmjʊnɪzəmz/
|n.|共产主义（复数）|A theory or system of social organization in which all property is owned by the community and each person contributes and receives according to their ability and needs.|例句：Communisms, in their various forms, share a core set of beliefs.  各种形式的共产主义都有一些共同的核心信念。

例句：Many countries experimented with different forms of communisms in the 20th century.  许多国家在20世纪尝试了不同形式的共产主义。|近义词：Marxism, socialism, collectivism|反义词：capitalism, individualism|词性:名词
