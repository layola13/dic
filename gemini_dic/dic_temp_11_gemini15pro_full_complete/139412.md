observerships:/əbˈzɜːrvərʃɪps/
n.
见习;实习;观察员职位
A period of time spent observing a professional at work in order to gain practical experience.
She completed a two-week observership in the cardiology department.
她在心脏病科完成了为期两周的见习。
internship, residency, apprenticeship, clerkship
