misrecite: /ˌmɪsrɪˈsaɪt/
verb
错误地背诵，背错
to recite something incorrectly
He misrecited several lines of the poem. 他把诗的几行背错了。
近义词: misquote, misspeak, fumble
反义词: recite correctly
词性: verb
