hummingbirds:/ˈhʌmɪŋbɜːrdz/| n. |蜂鸟|A small, brightly colored bird with a long, thin beak and the ability to hover in the air by rapidly beating its wings.|Example: Hummingbirds are known for their ability to fly backward. 蜂鸟以其向后飞行的能力而闻名。|Example:  We saw several hummingbirds flitting around the flowers. 我们看到几只蜂鸟在花丛中飞来飞去。|近义词:hummer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From *humming* (describing the sound made by their rapidly beating wings) + *bird*.
