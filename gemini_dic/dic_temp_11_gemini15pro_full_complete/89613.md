hoodwink:/hʊdˈwɪŋk/ | vt. | 欺骗，蒙蔽 | To deceive or trick someone. |  He hoodwinked investors into believing his scheme would make them millions. 他欺骗投资者相信他的计划会让他们赚到数百万美元。

He hoodwinked the guards and escaped from prison. 他蒙骗了守卫，逃出了监狱。

近义词: deceive, trick, dupe, swindle, cheat, defraud | 反义词: enlighten, inform, be honest | 词性: 动词
