baloga:/bəˈloʊɡə/| n.|巴洛加舞（一种菲律宾传统舞蹈）|A traditional Filipino dance that involves bamboo poles being clapped together rhythmically while dancers step in and out of the poles.|例句：The baloga dancers moved gracefully between the clicking bamboo poles. 巴洛加舞者在咔哒作响的竹竿间优雅地移动。

例句：We watched a mesmerizing baloga performance at the cultural festival. 我们在文化节上观看了一场迷人的巴洛加舞表演。|近义词：tinikling (another Filipino bamboo dance)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
