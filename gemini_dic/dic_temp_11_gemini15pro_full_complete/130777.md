nonane:/ˈnoʊneɪn/ | n. | 壬烷 |A straight-chain alkane with nine carbon atoms. It is a component of kerosene and diesel fuel. | Nonane is a colorless liquid at room temperature. 壬烷在室温下是无色液体。| Nonane is used as a solvent and in organic synthesis. 壬烷用作溶剂和有机合成。 | 近义词: alkane (general term) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | 词性: noun
