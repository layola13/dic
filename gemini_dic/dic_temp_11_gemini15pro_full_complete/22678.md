borderlands:/ˈbɔːrdərlændz/ | n. | 边疆；边界地带；交界地带 | A region on the edge of a country or territory; an area where different cultures or ideas meet and mix. | The novel is set in the borderlands between Scotland and England. 这部小说以苏格兰和英格兰的边界地带为背景。| synonyms: frontier, border, marches, fringe | antonyms: interior, heartland | noun
