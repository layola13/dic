bulimus:/ˈbjuːlɪməs/| n. |锥实螺|A genus of tropical air-breathing land snails, often large and brightly colored.  (热带地区一种会呼吸空气的陆生蜗牛，通常体型较大，颜色鲜艳。)|Bulimus snails are found in South America. 锥实螺发现于南美洲。|Synonym: land snail,  |Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps aquatic snail) |noun
