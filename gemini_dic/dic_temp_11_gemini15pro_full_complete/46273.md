czarinian:/zɑːˈrɪniən/
czarinian| adj. n.|沙皇的；沙皇支持者的；沙皇统治的；沙皇的支持者|Relating to a czar or a czar's rule; a supporter of the czar.|The czarinian era was marked by opulent displays of wealth and power. 沙皇时代以财富和权力的炫耀为标志。|The czarinian sympathizers were exiled after the revolution.  沙皇的支持者在革命后被流放。|近义词：imperial, tsarist|反义词：anti-tsarist, revolutionary|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Czar (from Caesar) + -inian (suffix forming adjectives and nouns).  最终源自拉丁语 Caesar（凯撒）。
