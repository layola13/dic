nonexactingly:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzæktɪŋli/
adverb
不严格地，不精确地
not demanding precision or accuracy; in a way that is not strict or rigorous.
He measured the ingredients nonexactingly, resulting in a cake that was too sweet. 他不精确地测量了配料，导致蛋糕太甜了。
近义词: loosely, imprecisely, casually, leniently
反义词: exactly, precisely, rigorously, strictly
词性: adverb
