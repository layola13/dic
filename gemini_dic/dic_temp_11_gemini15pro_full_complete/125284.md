multiflagellate:/ˌmʌltiˈflædʒɪlət/ | adj. n. | 多鞭毛的；多鞭毛生物 | Having or being characterized by multiple flagella (whip-like appendages used for locomotion). | Example: *Multiflagellate* bacteria are common in aquatic environments.  多鞭毛细菌在水生环境中很常见。| Example: The *multiflagellate* organism moved swiftly through the water.  这种多鞭毛生物在水中快速移动。| 近义词: polyflagellate | 反义词: uniflagellate, biflagellate | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin *multus* meaning "much, many"
flagellate (from Latin *flagellum* "whip"): referring to the whip-like appendages called flagella.
