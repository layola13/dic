futurology:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɒlədʒi/ /ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɑːlədʒi/
n.
未来学
The study of future possibilities based on current trends.
Futurology suggests that virtual reality will become increasingly integrated into our daily lives.  未来学表明，虚拟现实将越来越融入我们的日常生活。
Futurology often explores potential advancements in technology and their societal impact. 未来学经常探索技术的潜在进步及其对社会的影响。

近义词:future studies, forecasting, prognosis
反义词:past, history
词性:noun
