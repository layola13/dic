bespeaking:/bɪˈspiːkɪŋ/
v. |预示，表明；显示；表示；预订，订购|
bespeaking suggests, indicates, or shows something. It can also mean ordering or reserving something in advance.
Her pale face bespoke her illness. 她苍白的脸色表明她病了。

He bespoke a new suit for the wedding. 他为婚礼订做了一套新西装。
近义词: indicating, suggesting, signifying, ordering, reserving
反义词: concealing, hiding
词性: 动词 (verb)

PHRASE->
bespeaking of (说到…)  Bespeaking of travel, have you planned your vacation yet?  说到旅行，你计划好假期了吗？
bespeaking a certain… (表现出某种…)  His attire bespoke a certain elegance. 他的服装表现出某种优雅。
nothing bespeaking… (没有任何…的迹象)  There was nothing bespeaking any danger.  没有任何危险的迹象。
all bespeaking… (都表明…)  The evidence all bespoke his innocence.  所有的证据都表明他是无辜的。
clearly bespeaking… (清楚地表明…)  His actions clearly bespoke his intentions. 他的行为清楚地表明了他的意图。
