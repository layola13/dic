overadvertising:/ˌoʊvərˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋ/
n.|过度广告；广告过度|Excessive advertising; advertising too much.
The overadvertising of children's toys has become a serious issue. 儿童玩具的过度广告已经成为一个严重问题。
Christmas is a time when we see a lot of overadvertising. 圣诞节是我们看到大量过度广告的时候。
近义词: excessive advertising, overpromotion
反义词: underadvertising, underpromotion
词性: noun


词源:
over- (prefix) + advertising (noun)

"over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across." It indicates an excess or too much of something.
"advertising" comes from the verb "advertise," which originated in the late 16th century from the French "advertir," meaning "to warn, inform."  It evolved to its current meaning of promoting goods or services.
