outwith:/ˌaʊtˈwɪθ/
preposition
在…之外；不包括在…之内
outside the scope or area of something.
The matter is outwith my control. 这件事不在我的控制范围内。
近义词:outside, beyond, excluding
反义词:within, inside, including
词性:介词

词源:
Scottish, from out + with.  Essentially meaning "outside of" or "without".
