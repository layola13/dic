obnubilate:/ˌɒbnjuːbɪleɪt/ /ˌɑːbnjuːbəleɪt/
verb
使模糊；使昏暗；使困惑
to darken or obscure; to confuse or bewilder
The fog obnubilated the coastline. 浓雾使海岸线变得模糊不清。
The dense smoke obnubilated the view. 浓烟遮蔽了视线。
近义词:obscure, becloud, obfuscate, confuse, bewilder
反义词:clarify, elucidate, illuminate
词性:verb
