hiddenly:/ˈhɪdnli/| adv.|隐藏地，秘密地|in a hidden or secret manner; secretly.  It describes the manner in which something is done, emphasizing concealment and secrecy.|The documents were hiddenly stored in a secret compartment. 这些文件被秘密地藏在一个秘密隔间里。|She smiled hiddenly, knowing something they didn't. 她偷偷地笑了，知道一些他们不知道的事情。|近义词：secretly, covertly, clandestinely, stealthily|反义词：openly, publicly, visibly|词性: 副词

From Middle English *hidinliche, equivalent to hidden +‎ -ly.
