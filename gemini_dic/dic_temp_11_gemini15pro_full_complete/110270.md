lifters:/ˈlɪftərz/| n.|举重运动员；起重工具；电梯；升降机；兴奋剂|People who lift, especially heavy objects, or things that lift.  A lifter can be a person engaged in weightlifting, a mechanical device used for lifting, or something that elevates mood or spirits.|The weightlifters competed for the gold medal. 举重运动员们为金牌而战。

The forklift is a useful lifter in the warehouse. 叉车在仓库里是一个很有用的起重工具。|近义词：hoists, cranes, elevators, weightlifters, stimulants|反义词:depressants|词性:noun
