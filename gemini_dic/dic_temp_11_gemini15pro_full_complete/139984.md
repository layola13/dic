odorousness:/ˈoʊdərəsnəs/
n.
气味；发出气味的状态
The state or quality of being odorous; having a strong smell, especially an unpleasant one.
The odorousness of the garbage made me nauseous. 垃圾的气味让我恶心。
近义词:smell, aroma, fragrance, scent, stink, reek
反义词:odorlessness, inodorousness
词性:名词

odorousness:/ˈoʊdərəsnəs/
词源:Late Middle English: from Old French *odoureus (from Latin odor ‘smell’) + -ous + -ness.
词性:名词
