larghetto:/lɑːrˈɡɛtoʊ/ | adv. adj. n.| 稍慢地，广板，广板乐章 |A musical tempo slightly slower than andante but faster than largo.  A piece of music in this tempo. |The second movement of the concerto is a beautiful larghetto. 这首协奏曲的第二乐章是一段优美的广板。|近义词：andante, lento |反义词: allegro, presto |词性: adverb, adjective, noun
Etymology: Italian, diminutive of largo 'broad', from Latin largus.
