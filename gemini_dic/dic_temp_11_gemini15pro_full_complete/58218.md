druggie:/ˈdrʌɡi/ | n. |瘾君子，吸毒者|A person who regularly uses illegal drugs. |例句：He was a druggie who lived on the streets. 他是一个流落街头的瘾君子。

例句：The druggie was arrested for possession of heroin.  这名吸毒者因持有海洛因而被捕。|近义词：addict, junkie, user |反义词: abstainer, non-user| noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From drug + -ie, a diminutive suffix.  First recorded in the 1960s.
