direptitiously:/ˌdaɪrəpˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
偷偷地；暗中地
in a way that is intended to avoid being noticed; secretly.
She direptitiously slipped the note into his hand. 她偷偷地把纸条塞到他手里。

direptitiously:/ˌdaɪrəpˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
秘密地；悄悄地
secretly; stealthily; surreptitiously
He direptitiously filmed the meeting. 他偷偷地拍下了会议过程。

direptitiously:/ˌdaɪrəpˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
鬼鬼祟祟地
furtively; clandestinely
The spy direptitiously entered the building.  间谍偷偷溜进了大楼。



近义词:secretly, stealthily, surreptitiously, furtively, clandestinely
反义词:openly, publicly, obviously
词性:adverb

词源: From Latin *direptitus, past participle of *diripere "to snatch away, plunder," from dis- "apart" + rapere "to seize."
