doubloons:/dʌˈbluːnz/
noun
双倍金币（旧时西班牙金币）; 双金元
A gold coin of Spain and Spanish America, equal to two escudos or about sixteen silver dollars.
The pirates buried their chest of doubloons on the deserted island. 海盗们把装满双倍金币的箱子埋在了荒岛上。

synonyms: gold coins, treasure, riches
antonyms: debts, poverty
