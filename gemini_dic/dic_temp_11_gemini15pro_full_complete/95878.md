increasingly:/ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli/
| adv. |越来越多地，日益|More and more; to an increasing extent.|例句：The city is becoming increasingly crowded.  这座城市越来越拥挤。|近义词： progressively, gradually, more and more |反义词:decreasingly, less and less|词性:副词
Etymology: From increase + -ing + -ly.  "Increase" comes from Old French "encreiss-" (infinitive "encreistre"), from Latin "increscere" "to grow," from "in-" "into" + "crescere" "to grow."
