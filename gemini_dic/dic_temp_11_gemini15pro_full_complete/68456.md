familiarises:/fəˈmɪliəraɪzɪz/
verb
使熟悉；使了解
to make someone familiar with or knowledgeable about something
The training course familiarises new employees with the company's procedures.  培训课程让新员工熟悉公司的程序。
The museum's interactive exhibits familiarise children with science.  博物馆的互动展览让孩子们了解科学。
synonyms: acquaint, accustom, introduce
antonyms: estrange, alienate
词性: 动词(verb)
