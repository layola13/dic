elocutionary:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃəneri/ | adj. |雄辩的，口才好的，表达清晰的 | Relating to the way in which something is expressed; characterized by fluent and effective speech. |  Her elocutionary skills impressed everyone in the room. 她的演讲技巧给房间里的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。

|  The professor's lectures were known for their elocutionary style.  这位教授的讲座以其雄辩的风格而闻名。 | 近义词: articulate, eloquent, fluent, expressive | 反义词: inarticulate, mumbled, unclear, hesitant | 词性: adjective

elocution:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 演说术，演讲风格，口才 | The art of clear and effective public speaking; a style of speaking or reading aloud in public. | He took elocution lessons to improve his public speaking. 他上了演讲课来提高他的公开演讲能力。

|  Her elocution was impeccable, every word delivered with precision. 她的演讲无可挑剔，每个字都表达得非常精准。 | 近义词: oratory, speaking, delivery, articulation | 反义词: silence, muteness | 词性: noun
