circumscript:/ˈsɜːrkəmskrɪpt/ /ˈsɝːkəmskrɪpt/| adj. v.|受限制的，被约束的；限制，约束|Limited or restricted in scope or action; to draw a line around; to encircle.|例句：His authority was carefully circumscribed. 他的权力被谨慎地限制了。|近义词：restricted, limited, confined, bound, constrained|反义词：unlimited, unrestricted, unbound, expansive|词性:adjective, verb
circumscript:/ˈsɜːrkəmskrɪpt/ /ˈsɝːkəmskrɪpt/| v.|限制，约束|To draw a line around; to encircle; to limit or confine.|例句：The king's power was circumscribed by the constitution. 国王的权力受到宪法的限制。|近义词：restrict, limit, confine, bound, constrain|反义词：expand, enlarge, broaden|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *circumscriptus*, past participle of *circumscribere* ("to draw a line around, encircle, enclose, limit, confine, restrict"), from *circum* ("around") + *scribere* ("to write, draw").
