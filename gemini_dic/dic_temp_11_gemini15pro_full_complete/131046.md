nonavoidance:/ˌnɒnəˈvɔɪdəns/| n.|不回避，不逃避|The policy of nonavoidance promotes facing challenges head-on.  不回避的政策鼓励直面挑战。|Example:  Her nonavoidance of the difficult conversation was admirable. 她不回避艰难的对话令人钦佩。|Synonym: confrontation, acceptance|Antonym: avoidance, evasion|noun
