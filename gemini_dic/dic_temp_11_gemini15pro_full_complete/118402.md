merou:/ˈmɛruː/| n.|石斑鱼|A type of grouper fish found in warm seas.  It is a popular food fish.|例句：The merou was the biggest fish I caught that day. 那天我钓到的最大的鱼是石斑鱼。

例句：The restaurant served grilled merou with lemon sauce.  这家餐厅供应柠檬酱烤石斑鱼。|近义词：grouper, rockcod|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mérou*, from Occitan *meròu*, from Latin *mullus* (red mullet). The term originally referred to a type of mullet, but later shifted to denote groupers.
