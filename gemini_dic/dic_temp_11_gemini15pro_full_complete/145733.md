overcriticism:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪˌsɪzəm/
n.|过度批评，过分批评|The constant overcriticism made the child lose confidence.  不断的过度批评使孩子失去了信心。|Excessive criticism can be detrimental. 过度的批评可能是有害的。|近义词：hypercriticism, excessive criticism, faultfinding, nitpicking|反义词: praise, encouragement, approval|词性:noun
Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "criticism" (from "criticize," from French "critiquer," ultimately from Greek "kritikos" meaning "able to judge")
