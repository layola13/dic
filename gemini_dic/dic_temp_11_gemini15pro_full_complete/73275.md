forebear:/ˈfɔːrˌber/ (US) /ˈfɔːˌbeər/ (UK)| n. |祖先，祖辈|An ancestor, especially one more remote than a grandparent; a forefather or foremother.|My forebears came to America from Ireland in the 19th century. 我的祖先在19世纪从爱尔兰来到美国。|近义词：ancestor, progenitor, forefather, antecedent|反义词:descendant, offspring|词性:名词

forebear:/ˈfɔːrˌber/ (US) /ˈfɔːˌbeər/ (UK)| n. |祖先，祖辈|Something that existed or occurred in the past and from which something else has developed.|This clock is the forebear of all modern timepieces. 这个钟是所有现代计时器的鼻祖。|近义词：predecessor, precursor|反义词:successor|词性:名词
