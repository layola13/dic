heritages:/ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒɪz/
n.
遗产，传统
Valuable things such as historic buildings, works of art, and traditions that have been passed down from previous generations.
The city has a rich cultural heritage.  这座城市拥有丰富的文化遗产。
legacy, inheritance, tradition, patrimony
disregard, neglect
noun
