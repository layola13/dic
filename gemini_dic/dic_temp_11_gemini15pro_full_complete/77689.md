gemelliparous:/ˌdʒɛmɪˈlɪpərəs/
adjective
双胞胎的；生双胞胎的
Giving birth to twins.  Relating to the birth of twins.
例句：The gemelliparous ewe gave birth to two healthy lambs. 这只产双胞胎的母羊生了两只健康的羊羔。

例句：Gemelliparous pregnancies carry higher risks than singleton pregnancies. 双胞胎妊娠比单胎妊娠风险更高。

近义词：twin-bearing
反义词：nulliparous (从未生育过), primiparous (初产的)
词性：adjective
