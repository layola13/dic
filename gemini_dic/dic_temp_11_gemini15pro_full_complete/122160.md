mistrustfulness:/ˌmɪsˈtrʌstfʊlnəs/| n.|不信任，猜疑，怀疑|The state or quality of being mistrustful; a tendency to distrust others. |Her mistrustfulness of strangers made it difficult for her to make friends. 她对陌生人的不信任使得她很难交朋友。|近义词：suspicion, distrust, doubt, skepticism|反义词：trust, confidence, faith|词性:名词

Etymology:
From mistrust + -ful + -ness. Mistrust from mis- (wrongly) + trust (belief in reliability).
