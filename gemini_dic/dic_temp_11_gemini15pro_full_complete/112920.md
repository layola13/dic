luffa:/ˈlʌfə/ (US) /ˈluːfə/ (UK)| n.|丝瓜，丝瓜络|A tropical climbing plant bearing long, cylindrical gourds with a fibrous interior. The dried fibrous interior of the gourd is used as a sponge.|例句：She used a luffa to exfoliate her skin. 她用丝瓜络去角质。
