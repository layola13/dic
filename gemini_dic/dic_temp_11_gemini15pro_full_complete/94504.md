importunately:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənətli/ | adv. | 持续地；再三地；恳切地 | In a persistent, pressing, or urgent manner.  |  He importunately begged for another chance. 他再三恳求再给他一次机会。 | persistently, insistently, pressingly | casually, indifferently | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *importunatus, past participle of importunari ("to importune"), from Latin importunus ("unfit, inconvenient, troublesome").  The adverbial form "-ly" was added later in English.
