mundivagant:/ˌmʌndiˈveɪɡənt/| adj.|周游世界的；漫游世界的|Wandering or traveling throughout the world.|例句：He led a mundivagant life, exploring remote corners of the globe. 他过着周游世界的生活，探索地球上偏远的角落。|例句：Her mundivagant spirit led her to a career in international journalism. 她漫游世界的精神引导她从事国际新闻事业。|近义词：peripatetic, nomadic, itinerant, wandering|反义词：settled, sedentary, static|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *mundivagus*, from mundus ("world") + vagus ("wandering").
