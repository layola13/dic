bassist:/ˈbeɪsɪst/| n.|贝斯手；低音歌手|A musician who plays the bass guitar or double bass.  A person who sings bass, the lowest vocal range.|例句：The bassist laid down a funky groove. 贝斯手演奏了一段时髦的律动。

例句：The band's bassist is also a talented songwriter. 乐队的贝斯手也是一位才华横溢的歌曲作者。|近义词：bass player|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
