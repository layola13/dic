intercolonial:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈloʊniəl/| adj.|殖民地之间的；跨殖民地的|Existing or occurring between colonies.  Relating to the relations between colonies.|例句：Intercolonial trade boosted the economies of all involved. 殖民地之间的贸易促进了所有参与方的经济。

例句：The intercolonial railway connected the eastern provinces of Canada.  殖民地间的铁路连接了加拿大东部的省份。|近义词：interterritorial, interstate|反义词:intracolonial|词性:adjective
