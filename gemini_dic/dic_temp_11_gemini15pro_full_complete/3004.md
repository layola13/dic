aepyornis:/ˌiːpɪˈɔːrnɪs/ | n. | 象鸟 | An extinct flightless bird native to Madagascar, also known as the elephant bird.  It was the largest bird that ever lived. | The aepyornis laid the largest eggs of any known bird. 象鸟产下了所有已知鸟类中最大的蛋。|  The aepyornis, unfortunately, is extinct due to human hunting. 不幸的是，象鸟由于人类的猎杀而灭绝了。| synonym: elephant bird | antonym: N/A | noun
