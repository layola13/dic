cross-leggedness:/ˌkrɒsˈlɛɡɪdnəs/ /ˌkrɔːsˈlɛɡɪdnəs/| n.|盘腿，交叉腿的姿势|The state or condition of having one's legs crossed, typically at the knees.|He sat in cross-leggedness on the floor. 他盘腿坐在地板上。|Synonyms: cross-legged position,  sitting cross-legged|Antonyms: standing,  legs uncrossed|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross-legged + -ness.  Cross-legged from cross (adjective meaning "intersecting") + legged (having legs). -ness is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
