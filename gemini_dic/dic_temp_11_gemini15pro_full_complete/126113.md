mute:/mjuːt/ (adj.) /mjuːt/ (v.)| adj.  沉默的；无声的；v.  使…静音；减弱…的声音|
(adj.) Unable to speak, silent; (v.) To silence or reduce the sound of something.|
(adj.) The child remained mute throughout the interrogation.  这孩子在整个审讯过程中都保持沉默。
(v.)  Please mute your microphone during the presentation.  请在演示过程中将您的麦克风静音。|
(adj.) silent, speechless, dumb, voiceless |(adj.) loud, noisy, talkative |(v.) silence, soften, muffle |(v.) amplify, increase, unmute| adjective, verb


mute:/mjuːt/ (n.)| n.  弱音器；哑巴|A device used to soften the sound of a musical instrument; A person who is unable to speak.|
The trumpet player used a mute to create a softer sound.  小号手使用弱音器来创造更柔和的声音。
In the old movie, the mute communicated through sign language.  在那部老电影里，哑巴通过手语交流。|
(n.) silencer, damper |(n.) amplifier | noun

From Old French *mut (Modern French muet), from Latin mutus "dumb, silent, speechless."
