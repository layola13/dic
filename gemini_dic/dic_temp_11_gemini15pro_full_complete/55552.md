districts:/ˈdɪstrɪkts/ | n. |区域，地区，行政区|A district is a particular area of a town, city, country, or other area, especially one that has an official boundary. |The city is divided into several districts. 这个城市被分成几个区。| The school serves two districts. 这所学校服务于两个地区。 | 近义词: areas, regions, zones, sectors, territories | 反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用 whole, entirety 表示整体) | noun
