lactim: /ˈlæktɪm/ | n. | 内酰胺 | A cyclic amide formed by the removal of a molecule of water from the hydroxyl group of an alpha-hydroxy acid and its carboxyl group.  It is a tautomeric form of a lactam. | The lactim form is less stable than the lactam form in most cases. 内酰胺形式在大多数情况下不如内酰胺形式稳定。|  The equilibrium between lactam and lactim forms is influenced by the solvent. 内酰胺和内酰胺形式之间的平衡受溶剂的影响。 |  tautomer |  - | noun
