cablegram:/ˈkeɪbəlɡræm/ | n. | 电报 | A message sent by cable. |  Example 1:  He received a cablegram informing him of his father's illness. 他收到一封电报，通知他父亲生病了。

Example 2:  In the early 20th century, cablegrams were the fastest way to communicate across long distances. 在20世纪初，电报是远距离通讯最快的方式。 | 近义词：telegram, wire, message | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider: letter, phone call - slower forms of communication) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From cable + -gram.  "Cable" refers to the submarine telegraph cable used to transmit messages, and "-gram" comes from Greek grámma, meaning "something written."
