noninsects:/ˌnɒnˈɪnsekt/ | n. |非昆虫|Animals that are not insects, such as spiders, mites, centipedes, and millipedes.|例句：Spiders are noninsects, despite having eight legs. 蜘蛛虽然有八条腿，但它们不是昆虫。|例句：Many people mistakenly classify noninsects like spiders and scorpions as insects. 许多人错误地将蜘蛛和蝎子之类的非昆虫归类为昆虫。|近义词：invertebrate (a broader term) |反义词：insect|词性:名词
