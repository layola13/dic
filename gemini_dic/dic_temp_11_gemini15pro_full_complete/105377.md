kickup:/ˈkɪkʌp/| n.| 喧闹，骚动，麻烦；(马等的)后踢|A kickup is a disturbance, commotion, or fuss. kick up:/kɪk ˈʌp/| v.| 引起，惹起(麻烦等)；(马等)抬起后腿踢；扬起(灰尘等)| To kick up means to cause or create (trouble, a fuss, etc.).
