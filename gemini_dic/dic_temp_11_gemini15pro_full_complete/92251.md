hyperurban:/ˌhaɪpərˈɜːrbən/
adjective
超级都市化的
Characterized by excessive urban development, typically involving high population density, extensive infrastructure, and a concentration of economic and social activity.
The hyperurban landscape of Tokyo is a testament to human ingenuity and also a source of environmental challenges. 东京超级都市化的景观证明了人类的创造力，也是环境挑战的根源。

The study focused on the hyperurban environments of megacities in Asia. 这项研究的重点是亚洲特大城市的超级都市化环境。

Near synonyms:  ultra-urban, mega-urban, densely populated, heavily urbanized
Antonyms: rural, suburban, exurban, sparsely populated
word class: adjective
