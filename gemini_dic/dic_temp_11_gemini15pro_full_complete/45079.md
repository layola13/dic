cubis:/ˈkjuːbɪs/ | n. | 立方体 (数学中的三次幂) |  The term "cubis" refers to a cube or the result of something being cubed (raised to the third power) in mathematics. It's derived from the Latin word "cubus" meaning "cube." While not a common standalone word in everyday English, it's used in mathematical contexts. |  The volume of a cube is calculated by cubing the length of one side.  一个立方体的体积是通过将其一边的长度进行立方计算得出的。| 近义词: cube, third power | 反义词: (No direct opposite) | 词性: noun
