anticulture:/ˌæntiˈkʌltʃər/
n.|反文化|Opposition to or rejection of the prevailing social norms and values, especially in the arts and intellectual life.|The punk rock movement was a powerful expression of anticulture in the 1970s.  朋克摇滚运动是20世纪70年代反文化的有力表达。|
|The rise of social media has given a voice to various forms of anticulture. 社交媒体的兴起为各种形式的反文化提供了声音。|
近义词:counterculture, iconoclasm|反义词:culture, mainstream|词性:名词
