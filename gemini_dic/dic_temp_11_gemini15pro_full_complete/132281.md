nondefendant:/ˌnɑːndɪˈfɛndənt/
n.
非被告人，未被起诉的人
A person or entity that is not named as a defendant in a lawsuit.
The nondefendant witnessed the entire incident.  非被告人目睹了整个事件。
The judge dismissed the case against the nondefendant. 法官驳回了对非被告人的诉讼。
Synonyms: non-party, not accused
Antonyms: defendant, accused
noun
