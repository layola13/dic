collarette:/ˌkɒləˈret/ | n. | 小颈圈，环状领饰 | A small collar, ruff, or similar trimming worn around the neck. |  The dancer wore a shimmering collarette. 舞蹈演员戴着一个闪闪发光的小颈圈。 | synonyms: ruff, collar, neckband, choker | antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider absence of adornment) | noun

collarette:/ˌkɒləˈret/ | n. | (鸟类、昆虫)颈环，翎环 | A ring of different colored feathers or hairs around the neck of a bird or insect. | The pigeon had a distinctive white collarette. 这只鸽子有一个独特的白色颈环。 | synonyms: ring, band, circle | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun

collarette:/ˌkɒləˈret/ | n. | (真菌)菌环 | A ring of tissue around the stem of a mushroom. | The mushroom's collarette was a delicate, frilly structure. 蘑菇的菌环是一个精致的、褶皱状的结构。| synonyms: annulus, ring | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun

Etymology: From French "collerette," diminutive of "collet," meaning "small collar," ultimately from Latin "collum," meaning "neck."
