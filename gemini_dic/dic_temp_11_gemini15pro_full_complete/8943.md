antihistorically:/ˌænti(h)ɪˈstɔːrɪkli/
| adv. | 反历史地，脱离历史背景地 | In an antihistorical manner; without regard to or in contradiction of history. |  The author antihistorically portrays the events, ignoring the social context of the time.  作者反历史地描绘了这些事件，忽略了当时的社会背景。| 近义词: ahistorically | 反义词: historically | 词性: 副词
