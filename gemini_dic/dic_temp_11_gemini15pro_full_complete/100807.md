intriguing: /ɪnˈtriːɡɪŋ/ | adj. | 迷人的，引人入胜的，激起好奇心的 | arousing one's curiosity or interest; fascinating | The plot of the movie was intriguing.  这部电影的情节引人入胜。| synonyms: fascinating, captivating, compelling, absorbing, engaging | antonyms: boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intrigare, from Latin intricare (“to entangle”), from in- + tricae (“perplexities, hindrances, toys, tricks”).
