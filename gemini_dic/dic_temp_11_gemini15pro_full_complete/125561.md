multitaskers:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːskərz/ | n. | 多任务处理者 | People who can perform multiple tasks simultaneously. |  She's one of the best multitaskers I know, juggling work, family, and hobbies. 她是我认识的最擅长同时处理多项任务的人之一，能兼顾工作、家庭和爱好。| Synonyms: polymaths, multi-processors | Antonyms: single-taskers, specialists | Noun
