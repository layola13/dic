bacciform:/ˈbækɪfɔːrm/
adjective
浆果状的
Having the form of a berry; berry-shaped.
The plant has small, bacciform fruits. 这种植物有小的浆果状果实。
近义词: berry-like, berry-shaped
反义词: (No specific antonym, consider antonyms of shape like shapeless, amorphous, etc. depending on context.)
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *bacca* (berry) + *-formis* (having the form of)
