iciest:/ˈaɪsiɪst/| adj.|最冰冷的|Superlative form of icy: most icy. Characterized by the presence of ice; extremely cold.|The iciest winds blew across the frozen plains. 最冰冷的风吹过冰冻的平原。|The lake was iciest in the early morning. 清晨的时候湖水最冰冷。|近义词：coldest, frostiest, chilliest, glacial|反义词：warmest, hottest|词性:形容词
