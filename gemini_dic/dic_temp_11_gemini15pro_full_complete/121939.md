missificate:/ˌmɪsɪfɪˈkeɪt/
verb
使…神秘化；使…含糊其辞；歪曲；曲解
To make mysterious or obscure; to mystify.  To distort or misrepresent.
He missificated the facts to confuse the jury. 他歪曲事实以迷惑陪审团。
近义词: obfuscate, mystify, distort, misrepresent
反义词: clarify, elucidate, explain
词性: verb
