bevelings:/ˈbɛvəlɪŋz/
n.
斜角，斜面，倒角
The bevelings on the mirror added a touch of elegance.  镜子上的斜边增添了一丝优雅。
The bevelings on the edges of the table prevent splintering. 桌子边缘的倒角防止了裂片。
n.
削角，倒棱，斜切
The bevelings on the wood were precise and clean. 木材上的斜切精确而干净。
The process of bevelings requires specialized tools. 斜切过程需要专门的工具。

Etymology: From the verb "bevel" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-s" (plural). "Bevel" comes from the Old French "bivel," meaning "oblique."

Synonyms: chamfers, angles, slopes, bevels
Antonyms:  squares, right angles
