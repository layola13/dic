globated:/ˈɡloʊbeɪtɪd/| adj.|球形的，球状的|Shaped like a globe; spherical.|The artist created a globated sculpture resembling the Earth.  艺术家创作了一个类似地球的球形雕塑。|近义词：spherical, round, globular|反义词:flat, planar|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *globatus, past participle of *globare “to make round,” from globus “globe.”
