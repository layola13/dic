neocons:/ˈniːoʊkɑːnz/
n.
新保守主义者
A person advocating neoconservatism.  A neoconservative is an advocate of neoconservatism, a political ideology characterized by an emphasis on free-market capitalism, interventionist foreign policy, and a strong national defense.
The neocons pushed for the invasion of Iraq. 新保守主义者推动了入侵伊拉克的行动。
Synonyms: neoconservative
Antonyms:  liberal, dove
