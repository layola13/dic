mater:/ˈmeɪtər/| n.|母体，子宫；母校；（英国大学的）女舍监|From Latin māter "mother."  Refers to the biological mother or something that acts as a source or origin.  In British English, it can also refer to a housemother at a university.|She returned to her alma mater for the reunion. 她回到了母校参加聚会。|Synonyms: mother, origin, source|Antonyms: offspring, child|noun
