aphrasia:/əˈfreɪʒə/ /əˈfreɪʒɪə/
n.
失语症,表达不能
Loss of the ability to express oneself by speech, writing, or signs, due to an injury or disease of the brain centers controlling language.
After the stroke, he suffered from aphrasia and could no longer speak.  中风后，他患上了失语症，再也无法说话了。
近义词:dysphasia, loss of speech
反义词:fluency
词性:noun
