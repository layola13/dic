guiltily:/ˈɡɪltɪli/| adv.|内疚地，愧疚地|In a manner expressing or suggesting guilt.  Feeling or showing remorse for having done wrong.|She looked at him guiltily. 她内疚地看着他。|Synonyms: remorsefully, shamefacedly, apologetically|Antonyms: innocently, unabashedly|Adverb
