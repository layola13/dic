groutiness:/ˈɡraʊtinəs/ | n. | 浆糊状；水泥浆状；粗糙 | The groutiness of the mixture made it difficult to spread.  这种混合物像水泥浆一样，很难涂抹。 | The groutiness of the paint made it unsuitable for fine detail work.  这种油漆粗糙，不适合精细的细节工作。 |  near synonyms: thickness, lumpiness, coarseness | near antonyms: smoothness, fineness, fluidity | noun

Etymology->
From grout + -iness.  "Grout" comes from Old English "grūt" meaning "coarse meal, grits."  The "-iness" suffix is used to form nouns indicating a state or quality.
