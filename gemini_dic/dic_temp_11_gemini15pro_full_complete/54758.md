dispatched:/dɪˈspætʃt/ | v. |派遣，发送，迅速处理，杀死|to send someone or something somewhere for a particular purpose|The troops were dispatched to the border. 部队被派往边境。|近义词：send, deliver, kill, execute|反义词: receive, hold, retain|词性:动词
dispatch:/ˈdɪspætʃ/ | n. |派遣，发送，急件，新闻报道|the act of sending someone or something somewhere|The dispatch of the troops was delayed by bad weather. 部队的派遣因恶劣天气而延误。|近义词：sending, delivery, message, report|反义词: receipt, holding|词性:名词
