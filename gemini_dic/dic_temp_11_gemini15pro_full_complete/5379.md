alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 冲积的；由冲积物形成的 | Relating to or deposited by a stream or running water. |  The alluvial soil of the Nile delta is very fertile. 尼罗河三角洲的冲积土非常肥沃。

alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. |  | The farmers planted their crops in the alluvial plain. 农民们在冲积平原上种植庄稼。 | 近义词：fluvial, sedimentary | 反义词:  | 词性:形容词

alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 | A deposit of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil. | The alluvium deposited by the river created a rich farmland. 河流沉积的冲积层形成了肥沃的农田。

alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. |  |  The archaeologist found ancient artifacts buried in the alluvium. 考古学家在冲积层中发现了埋藏的古代文物。 | 近义词：deposit, sediment | 反义词: | 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alluvius*, from alluere "to wash against," from ad- "to" + -luere, related to lavare "to wash."
