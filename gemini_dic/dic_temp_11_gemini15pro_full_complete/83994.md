half-defiantly:/ˌhæf dɪˈfaɪəntli/ | adv. | 半挑衅地，略带反抗地 |  In a manner that shows some resistance or disobedience, but not completely. It suggests a subtle or partial act of defiance.  |  He half-defiantly crossed his arms, a small gesture of rebellion. 他半挑衅地交叉双臂，这是一个小小的反抗姿态。


She half-defiantly muttered under her breath, disagreeing with the decision but not wanting to cause a scene. 她低声咕哝着，半挑衅地表示不同意这个决定，但又不想引起骚动。 | 近义词: somewhat defiantly, slightly defiantly, partly defiantly | 反义词: submissively, obediently, respectfully | 词性: 副词


词源:  "half-" (Old English healf) + "defiantly" (from "defy" - Old French defier, from Latin dēfidāre, from dē- + fidāre "to trust").
