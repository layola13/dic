ecumenical: /ˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪkəl/ /ˌiːkjuːˈmɛnɪkəl/
adjective
普遍的，广泛的；基督教各教派的
Promoting or relating to unity among the world's Christian churches.  Representing a number of different Christian churches.
例句：The Pope called for greater ecumenical cooperation. 教皇呼吁加强基督教各教派的合作。

例句：The conference was attended by representatives of various ecumenical organizations.  会议由来自不同基督教组织的代表参加。

近义词：universal, general, worldwide, global, interdenominational
反义词：sectarian, parochial, denominational
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin oecumenicus, from Ancient Greek οἰκουμενικός (oikoumenikós, “of or belonging to the inhabited world; general, universal”), from ἡ οἰκουμένη (hē oikoumenē, “the inhabited world”), feminine present participle of οἰκέω (oikéō, “I inhabit”).
