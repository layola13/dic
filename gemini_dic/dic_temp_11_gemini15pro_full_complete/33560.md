cigars:/sɪˈɡɑːrz/
n.
雪茄
A cylindrical roll of cured tobacco leaves for smoking.
He lit a cigar after dinner.  他晚饭后点燃了一支雪茄。

近义词: stogies, cheroots
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: cigarettes, tobacco-free alternatives)
词性: Noun
