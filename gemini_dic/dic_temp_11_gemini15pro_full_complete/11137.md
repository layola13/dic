archegony:/ɑːrˈkɛɡəni/
n.
无性生殖，孤雌生殖
The development of an egg without fertilization, as in parthenogenesis.
Example 1: Archegony is a form of asexual reproduction.  孤雌生殖是一种无性生殖的形式。
Example 2:  The study of archegony is crucial to understanding certain insect populations. 研究孤雌生殖对于理解某些昆虫种群至关重要。
Synonym: parthenogenesis, asexual reproduction
Antonym: sexual reproduction
noun
