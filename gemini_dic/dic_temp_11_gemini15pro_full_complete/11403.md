argentic:/ɑːrˈdʒɛntɪk/
adjective
含银的;银的
Containing silver; relating to or containing silver.
Argentic photography uses silver halide crystals to capture images. 含银摄影使用卤化银晶体来捕捉图像。
synonyms: silver, silvery
antonyms: non-argentic


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *argentique*, from Latin *argentum* (silver).
