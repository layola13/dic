clerkships: /ˈklɜːrkʃɪps/ | n. | 实习期; 办事员职位; 书记员职位 | A period of time spent as a clerk, especially one spent training for a particular profession, such as law or medicine.  A position or job as a clerk. |  She completed her clerkships at a prestigious law firm. 她在一家著名的律师事务所完成了实习期。 He held several clerkships before becoming a judge. 在成为法官之前，他担任过几个书记员的职位。 | synonyms: internship, apprenticeship, traineeship, practicum | antonyms:  employment,  permanent position | noun
