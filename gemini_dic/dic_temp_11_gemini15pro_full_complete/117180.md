measliest:/ˈmiːzliɪst/| adj.|最微不足道的，最少的，最差的|Superlative form of measly: most measly.  It signifies the smallest or most insignificant amount or degree of something, often used to express dissatisfaction or contempt.|He gave me the measliest portion of the cake. 他给了我最少的一块蛋糕。|Synonyms:  paltry, meager, scanty, pitiful, insignificant|Antonyms:  generous, abundant, ample, substantial|adjective
