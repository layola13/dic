dbms:/ˈdiːbiːɛmˈɛs/| n.|数据库管理系统(Database Management System)|A software system used to define, create, manipulate, maintain, and control access to a database.|例句：The DBMS allows users to access and modify data stored in the database. 数据库管理系统允许用户访问和修改存储在数据库中的数据。

例句：We are using a relational DBMS for our project.  我们正在为我们的项目使用关系型数据库管理系统。|近义词：database system, database software|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Abbreviation of "Database Management System".  "Database" originates from "data" and "base".  "Management" comes from Old French "manegement" meaning "the act of managing". "System" comes from Late Latin "systema" from Greek "sustēma"  meaning "organized whole".
