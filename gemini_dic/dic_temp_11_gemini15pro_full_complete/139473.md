obstinate:/ˈɒbstɪnət/ (UK) /ˈɑːbstɪnət/ (US)| adj.|固执的，顽固的|Unreasonably determined to have one's own way, not yielding to reason or persuasion.|He was obstinate and refused to change his mind. 他很固执，拒绝改变主意。|The obstinate child refused to eat his vegetables.  这个固执的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。|近义词：stubborn, headstrong, unyielding, inflexible, intractable|反义词：compliant, amenable, docile, yielding, flexible
词性: 形容词
