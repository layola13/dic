decree: /dɪˈkriː/
n. | 法令，政令；判决 | An official order issued by a legal authority. | The king issued a decree banning all public gatherings. 国王颁布法令禁止所有公众集会。| edict, order, proclamation | repeal, countermand | noun
decree: /dɪˈkriː/
vt. |  颁布法令；裁定；注定 | To issue a decree; to officially decide or order something. | The court decreed that the company must pay compensation. 法院裁定该公司必须支付赔偿金。| order, command, dictate | forbid, prohibit | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *decreer*, from Latin *decernere* "to decide, determine," from *de-* "down, completely" + *cernere* "to distinguish, separate, sift, decide."
