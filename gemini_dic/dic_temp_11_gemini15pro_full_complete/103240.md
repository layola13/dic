jettying:/ˈdʒɛtiɪŋ/
n.|突堤效应，射流效应|The projection of a structure, such as a pier or wharf, into a body of water, causing a disruption in the natural flow and potentially leading to erosion or deposition.|The jettying of the new marina caused significant changes to the coastline. 新码头的突堤效应导致海岸线发生了重大变化。|projection, extension|recession, retreat|noun
