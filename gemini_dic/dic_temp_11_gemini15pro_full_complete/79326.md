glean:/ɡliːn/ | v. |收集, 拾穗; 费力地收集(信息) | To gather (grain or other produce) after a harvest. To collect (information) bit by bit. | The researchers gleaned valuable information from old newspapers. 研究人员从旧报纸中收集到宝贵的信息。|  She gleaned some useful ideas from the conference. 她从会议中收集了一些有用的想法。 | synonyms: gather, collect, harvest, reap, cull | antonyms: scatter, disperse, sow | verb

gleanings:/ˈɡliːnɪŋz/ | n. |拾穗所得之物; 收集到的信息 | Things that are gleaned; information collected from various sources, often with difficulty. | The book is a collection of gleanings from his travels. 这本书是他旅行见闻的汇编。 | Her gleanings from the internet research proved invaluable. 她从互联网研究中收集到的信息非常宝贵。 | synonyms: findings, collection, compilation, harvest | antonyms: N/A | noun
