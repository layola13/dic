conventioneer:/kənˌvɛnʃəˈnɪər/
n.
与会者，参加会议的人
A person who attends a convention.
The hotel was filled with conventioneers attending the annual technology conference.  这家酒店挤满了参加年度科技大会的与会者。
Attendees, participants, delegates
Absentee
