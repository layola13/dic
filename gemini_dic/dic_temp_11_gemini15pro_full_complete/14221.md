autoclastic:/ˌɔːtəˈklæstɪk/
adjective
自碎屑的；由自身碎片组成的
Formed from fragments of the same rock, especially by volcanic action or tectonic processes.
The autoclastic breccia was formed by the shattering of the lava flow as it cooled. 这块自碎角砾岩是由熔岩流冷却时破碎形成的。

The geologist identified the rock formation as autoclastic. 地质学家将该岩层鉴定为自碎屑岩。
近义词:self-fragmental
反义词:allochthonous

ETYMOLOGY->
From auto- (self) + -clastic (broken, fragmental), from Greek klastos "broken."
