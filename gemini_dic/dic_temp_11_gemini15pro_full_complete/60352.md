egotize: /ˈiːɡətaɪz/ | v. | 以自我为中心，自私自利 | To be excessively self-centered and concerned with one's own needs and desires, often at the expense of others. |  He tends to egotize and make everything about himself. 他总是以自我为中心，让一切都围着他转。|  She egotized throughout the entire meeting, interrupting others and boasting about her accomplishments.  她在整个会议中都非常自私，打断别人并吹嘘自己的成就。| 近义词: boast, brag, show off | 反义词: humble, selfless | 词性: 动词
