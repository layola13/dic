bleeding:/ˈbliːdɪŋ/| n. adj.|流血，出血；（非正式）勒索；（非正式）放血；（比喻）痛苦，折磨|The act of losing blood. Can also be used informally to mean extortion or draining of resources. Figuratively, it can refer to emotional pain or suffering.|
The soldier was bleeding profusely from his wound.  这个士兵的伤口大量出血。

The company is bleeding money.  这家公司在不断亏损。

My heart is bleeding for him. 我为他感到非常难过。

|近义词：haemorrhage, blood loss|反义词: clotting, healing|词性: noun, adjective


bleeding:/ˈbliːdɪŋ/| adj.|（非正式）讨厌的，该死的|Used informally as an intensifier to express annoyance or anger.|
That bleeding idiot locked the keys in the car!  那个该死的白痴把钥匙锁在车里了！

This bleeding computer is so slow! 这该死的电脑太慢了！


|近义词：damn, bloody|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: adjective
