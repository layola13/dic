multi-chromes:/ˈmʌltiˌkroʊmz/ | n. | 多色镀膜，多色偏光，变色龙颜料 | Pigments that exhibit multiple colors depending on the angle of view and lighting conditions. | The makeup artist used multi-chromes to create a dazzling, iridescent eye look. 化妆师使用了多色镀膜来打造炫目、虹彩般的眼妆。|Synonyms: iridescent pigments, color-shifting pigments, chameleon pigments | Antonyms: monochromatic pigments, single-color pigments | noun

multi-chrome:/ˈmʌltiˌkroʊm/ | adj. | 多色的，变色的 | Exhibiting multiple colors depending on the angle of view and lighting conditions. |  The multi-chrome car paint shimmered in the sunlight.  多色车漆在阳光下闪闪发光。| Synonyms: iridescent, color-shifting, chameleon | Antonyms: monochromatic, single-color | adjective
