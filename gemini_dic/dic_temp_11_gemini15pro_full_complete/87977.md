high-hatters:/ˈhaɪˌhætərz/| n.|自命不凡的人；摆架子的人|People who act superior and condescending to others.  Those who display an air of arrogance and disdain.| He surrounded himself with high-hatters, looking down on anyone who wasn't as wealthy. 他周围都是些自命不凡的人，瞧不起任何不如他富有的人。

The band's new drummer is amazing on the high-hats.  这个乐队的鼓手打踩镲的技术惊人。 (This refers to the musical instrument, not the people).|近义词：snobs, elitists, snooty people|反义词：humble people, down-to-earth people|词性: noun
