impalas:/ɪmˈpɑːləz/ | n. |黑斑羚，高角羚| A graceful African antelope known for its leaping ability.  A type of antelope found in eastern and southern Africa. |  We saw a herd of impalas grazing on the savanna. 我们看到一群黑斑羚在热带草原上吃草。|  The impalas leaped effortlessly over the bushes. 黑斑羚毫不费力地跳过了灌木丛。| 近义词：antelope, gazelle | 反义词: predator | 词性: 名词
