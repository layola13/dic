dermestes:/dərˈmɛstiːz/
noun
皮蠹；皮蠹甲蟲
A genus of beetles in the family Dermestidae, commonly known as skin beetles.  These beetles are known for their ability to digest keratin, the protein found in hair, skin, and feathers.
The museum curator was concerned about a dermestes infestation in the taxidermy collection.  博物馆馆长担心标本收藏中皮蠹虫害的蔓延。
synonyms: skin beetle, hide beetle, larder beetle (some species)
antonyms: N/A
