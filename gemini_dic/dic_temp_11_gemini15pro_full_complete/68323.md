fall-back:/ˈfɔːlbæk/| n. adj.|后备，备用；退路|A fall-back is something that can be used if the usual thing is not available or does not work.  It can also describe something used as a last resort.| We have a fall-back position if the negotiations fail. 如果谈判失败，我们有一个备用方案。|synonyms: backup, reserve, alternative, substitute|antonyms: primary, main, principal|noun
fall-back:/ˈfɔːlbæk/| adj.|后备的，备用的|Used to describe something kept in reserve.| This is our fall-back plan. 这是我们的备用计划。|synonyms: backup, reserve, alternative, substitute|antonyms: primary, main, principal|adjective
