/ˌnɒnləʊˈkeɪʃən/ |/ˌnɑːnləʊˈkeɪʃən/| n. |非位置; 非场所; 非地点|A state or condition of not being in a specific location or place; the absence of a fixed or definite location. | The increasing trend of remote work emphasizes nonlocation as a key factor in modern employment. 越来越多的远程工作趋势强调了非位置是现代就业的一个关键因素。|  The digital nomad lifestyle embraces nonlocation, allowing individuals to work from anywhere in the world. 数字游民的生活方式拥抱非位置，允许个人在世界任何地方工作。 | 近义词: absence of location, placelessness, ubiquity (in some contexts) | 反义词: location, place, site | noun
