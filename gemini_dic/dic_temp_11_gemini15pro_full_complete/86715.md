helming:/ˈhelmɪŋ/| v. |掌舵；指挥；领导|Being in charge or control of something, like steering a ship or leading a project.|She is helming the new marketing campaign. 她正在领导新的营销活动。|The captain was helming the ship through the storm. 船长掌舵着船渡过了风暴。|近义词：leading, directing, managing, controlling, steering, commanding|反义词：following, obeying|词性：动词
