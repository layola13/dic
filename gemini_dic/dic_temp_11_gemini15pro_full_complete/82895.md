guilty: /ˈɡɪlti/ | adj. | 有罪的；内疚的 | Feeling or showing remorse for having committed a wrong or failed in an obligation. |  She felt guilty about lying to her mother. 她对向母亲撒谎感到内疚。| synonyms: ashamed, remorseful, culpable, responsible | antonyms: innocent, blameless | adjective

guilty: /ˈɡɪlti/ | adj. | 犯…罪的；有…罪责的 | Responsible for a specified wrongdoing. | He was found guilty of murder. 他被判犯有谋杀罪。| synonyms: culpable, responsible, liable | antonyms: innocent, blameless | adjective
