millstone:/ˈmɪlˌstoʊn/| n. |磨石；重负|A large, heavy circular stone used for grinding grain.  Something that is a burden or obstacle.|例句：The debt was a millstone around his neck.  这债务成了他的沉重负担。

例句：The old traditions were a millstone hindering progress.  这些旧传统成了阻碍进步的绊脚石。|近义词：burden, encumbrance, obstacle, impediment|反义词:advantage, asset, benefit|词性:名词
