outcaviled: /ˌaʊtkəˈvɪld/
verb
胜过诡辩; 巧辩胜过
surpassed in caviling; outdone in petty or frivolous objection.
He outcaviled his opponent in the debate, picking apart every flaw in their argument. 他在辩论中以诡辩胜过了对手，指出了他们论点中的每一个缺陷。
近义词: outargued, outwitted, outsmarted
反义词: defeated, bested
