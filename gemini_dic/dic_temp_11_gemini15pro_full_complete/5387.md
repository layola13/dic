allying:/əˈlaɪɪŋ/
v.|结盟，联合|The two countries are allying against their common enemy. 这两个国家正在联合起来对抗共同的敌人。|
例句：Several smaller parties allied with the communists.  几个小党派与共产党人结盟了。

例句：He allied himself with the reformers. 他与改革派结盟。
近义词：uniting, joining, combining, cooperating, collaborating
反义词：separating, dividing, opposing
词性: 动词(present participle of ally)


ally:/əˈlaɪ/
n.|盟友，同盟国|France is a close ally of the United States. 法国是美国的亲密盟友。|
例句：He's a close ally of the prime minister. 他是首相的亲密盟友。

例句：During World War II, Britain, France, and the US were allies. 在第二次世界大战期间，英国、法国和美国是盟国。
近义词：friend, partner, supporter, confederate
反义词：enemy, opponent, adversary, foe
词性: 名词


ally:/əˈlaɪ/
v.|结盟，与...联盟|The two groups allied to fight for better working conditions. 这两个团体联合起来争取更好的工作条件。|
例句：They allied themselves with other groups. 他们与其他团体结盟。

例句：He allied with the rebels. 他与叛军联盟。
近义词：unite, join forces, combine, cooperate, collaborate
反义词：separate, divide, oppose
词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *alien, from Old French alier, from Late Latin *alliare, present active infinitive of *alliō (“I bind to, attach, unite”), from Latin ad (“to, toward”) + ligō (“I bind”).
