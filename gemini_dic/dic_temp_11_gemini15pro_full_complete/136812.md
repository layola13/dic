nonshopper:/ˈnɑːnˌʃɑːpər/ /ˈnɒnˌʃɒpə(r)/ | n. |非购物者，不购物的人|A person who does not enjoy or frequently engage in shopping.| Example: He's a nonshopper; he hates going to the mall.  他是一个不购物的人；他讨厌去商场。| Example:  She's a dedicated nonshopper, preferring to spend her time on other activities. 她是一个坚定的不购物者，更喜欢把时间花在其他活动上。| 近义词: non-consumer,  | 反义词: shopper, consumer | noun
