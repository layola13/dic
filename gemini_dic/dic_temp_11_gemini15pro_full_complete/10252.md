apotheoses: /əˈpɒθiˌəʊsiːz/ /əˈpɑːθiˌoʊsiːz/
|n.|神化，典范，完美典型|The elevation of someone or something to divine status; the perfect example of something.|His election to the presidency was the apotheosis of his career. 他当选总统是他职业生涯的巅峰。|近义词：culmination, climax, peak, pinnacle, zenith|反义词：nadir, bottom, low point|词性：名词

apotheosis: /əˈpɒθiˌəʊsɪs/ /əˈpɑːθiˌoʊsɪs/
|n.|神化，典范，完美典型|The elevation of someone or something to divine status; the perfect example of something.|The apotheosis of Roman emperors was a common practice.  罗马皇帝的神化是一个普遍的做法。|近义词：culmination, climax, peak, pinnacle, zenith|反义词：nadir, bottom, low point|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin apotheosis, from Greek apotheōsis, from apotheoun "deify," from apo- "from" + theos "god."
