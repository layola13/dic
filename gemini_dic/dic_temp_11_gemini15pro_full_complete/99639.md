interlanguage:/ˌɪntərˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ/ | n. | 中介语；过渡语 |  A language or linguistic system used by a second-language learner who is in the process of learning a target language. It is characterized by features of both the learner's native language and the target language, as well as features unique to the interlanguage itself.  |  Her interlanguage was characterized by grammatical errors typical of Spanish speakers learning English. 她的中介语带有西班牙语使用者学习英语时常犯的语法错误。

|  The researcher analyzed the students' interlanguage to understand their learning process. 研究人员分析了学生的中介语以了解他们的学习过程。 | 近义词: learner language | 反义词: native language, target language | 词性: noun
