masks:/mɑːsks/ /mæsk/| n. |面具，口罩，掩饰|A covering for all or part of the face, worn for protection, disguise, performance, or other purposes. |例句：The surgeon wore a mask during the operation.  外科医生在手术期间戴着口罩。

例句：They wore masks to conceal their identities.  他们戴着面具来隐藏身份。|近义词：disguises, coverings, veils, respirators (for breathing)|反义词:faces|词性:noun


masks:/mɑːsks/ /mæsk/| v. |掩盖，遮蔽，伪装|To conceal something from view or knowledge. |例句：She masked her disappointment with a smile. 她用微笑掩饰了她的失望。


例句：Makeup can mask imperfections.  化妆可以掩盖瑕疵。|近义词：conceal, hide, disguise, cover|反义词:reveal, expose|词性:verb
