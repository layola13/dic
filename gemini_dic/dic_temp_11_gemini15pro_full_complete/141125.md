onu:/ˈoʊ ɛn juː/ /ˈɒn juː/ | n. | 光网络单元 (Optical Network Unit) | A device located at the subscriber's premises, connecting their equipment to the optical fiber network.  It converts optical signals from the fiber into electrical signals for devices like telephones, computers, and televisions, and vice versa. | The technician installed a new ONU at my house for faster internet.  技术员在我家安装了一个新的光网络单元，以提高网速。

The ONU is essential for accessing fiber optic internet services. 光网络单元对于访问光纤互联网服务至关重要。


近义词：ONT (Optical Network Terminal) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
