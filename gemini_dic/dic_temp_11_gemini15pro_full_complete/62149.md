enclosable:/ɪnˈkloʊzəbl/
adjective
可封闭的；可围住的
That can be enclosed or included within something else.
The garden is enclosable with a fence.  这个花园可以用栅栏围起来。
The documents are enclosable in this envelope.  这些文件可以装在这个信封里。
Synonyms: containable, confinable
Antonyms: open, unenclosed
词性: 形容词
