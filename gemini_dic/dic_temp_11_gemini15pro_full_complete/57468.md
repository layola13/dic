draftily:/ˈdrɑːftɪli/
adverb
有穿堂风地；冷飕飕地
in a drafty manner; so as to be exposed to drafts of air.
The old house was draftily built and always cold in winter. 这座老房子建得有穿堂风，冬天总是很冷。
近义词: airily, breezily, coldly
反义词: warmly, snugly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From draft + -ily.  "Draft" comes from the Old Norse "draught," meaning "a drawing, pulling, or act of drawing."  The suffix "-ily" forms adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a ... manner."
