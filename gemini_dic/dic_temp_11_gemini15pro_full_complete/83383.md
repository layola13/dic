gyte:/ɡaɪt/ | n. | 产卵，鱼类的产卵场 |The salmon return to their native gyte to spawn. 鲑鱼回到它们的原生产卵场产卵。|  例句：The gyte is an important habitat for many fish species.  产卵场是许多鱼类的重要栖息地。

例句：Salmon struggle upstream to reach their gyte. 鲑鱼逆流而上到达它们的产卵地。 | 近义词：spawning ground, breeding ground | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Old Norse *gyta (“to pour”), from Proto-Germanic *geutaną (“to pour”). Cognate with Old English ġēotan (“to pour”).
