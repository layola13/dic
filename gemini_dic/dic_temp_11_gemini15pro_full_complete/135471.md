nonpestilential:/ˌnɒnpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəl/
adjective
非瘟疫性的；不传播瘟疫的
Not pestilential; not tending to produce pestilence or plague.
The air in the mountains was cool and nonpestilential.  山里的空气凉爽，没有瘟疫。
The report confirmed the disease was nonpestilential. 报告证实这种疾病是非瘟疫性的。
近义词:  healthy, salubrious, wholesome
反义词:  pestilential, contagious, infectious
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From non- + pestilential.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "pestilential" comes from the Latin word "pestilentia," meaning "plague" or "pestilence."
