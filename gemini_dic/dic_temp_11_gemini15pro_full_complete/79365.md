glens:/ɡlɛnz/| n. |峡谷（glen的复数）|Narrow valleys, typically with a stream flowing through them.|The glens of Scotland are renowned for their beauty. 苏格兰的峡谷以其美丽而闻名。|The hikers explored the many glens in the area.  徒步旅行者探索了该地区的许多峡谷。|近义词：valleys, ravines, gorges|反义词:mountains, peaks|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scottish Gaelic *gleann*, from Old Irish *glenn*, meaning "valley".  Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *glennā*.
