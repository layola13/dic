machinabilities:/məˌʃiːnəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可加工性(复数)|The ease with which a material can be machined. This encompasses factors like how easily it can be cut, drilled, or shaped without excessive tool wear or damage to the workpiece.|The machinabilities of different alloys vary considerably. 不同合金的可加工性差异很大。|近义词:workability,  malleability|反义词: intractability, unworkability|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From machinable + -ity + -es (plural suffix).  "Machinable" comes from "machine" (from Old French "machine," ultimately from Latin "machina") + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).
