biographers:/baɪˈɑːɡrəfərz/
n.
传记作家
Writers of biographies; people who write accounts of other people's lives.
Many biographers have written about the life of Abraham Lincoln. 许多传记作家都写过亚伯拉罕·林肯的生平。
近义词:writers, historians
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps subjects or figures)
词性:Noun
