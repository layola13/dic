low-brow:/ˈloʊbraʊ/| adj.|低俗的，庸俗的|Not intellectual or cultured; appealing to the masses.|例句：I find reality TV shows to be incredibly low-brow. 我觉得真人秀节目非常低俗。

例句：That's low-brow humor; not everyone appreciates it. 这是低俗的幽默；不是每个人都欣赏。

近义词：uncultured, unrefined, vulgar, tasteless, plebeian, philistine|反义词:highbrow, sophisticated, refined, cultured|词性:形容词
