insensitivity:/ɪnˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/| n. |麻木不仁；漠不关心；感觉迟钝|Lack of sensitivity or awareness; the inability to feel or perceive something, either physically or emotionally.|He showed a surprising insensitivity to her feelings. 他对她的感受表现出惊人的麻木不仁。|近义词：indifference, apathy, callousness, unresponsiveness|反义词：sensitivity, empathy, compassion, responsiveness|词性:名词

insensitivity:/ɪnˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/| n. |不敏感；反应迟钝|Lack of response to a stimulus.|The insensitivity of the nerve endings can be a symptom of certain medical conditions. 神经末梢的不敏感可能是某些疾病的症状。|近义词：unresponsiveness, numbness|反义词：sensitivity, responsiveness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + sensitive + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  The word "sensitive" ultimately comes from Latin "sentire" (to feel).
