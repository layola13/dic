jamadar:/ˈdʒæmədɑːr/| n.|军士；(旧时印度和巴基斯坦的)低级警官；仆役的头目|A jamadar is a rank in the armies of India, Pakistan, Nepal and Bangladesh, typically equating to sergeant.  It has also been used as a title for a head servant. |The jamadar ordered his men to advance. 军士命令他的士兵前进。|Synonyms: sergeant, headman, foreman|Antonyms: subordinate, follower|noun
