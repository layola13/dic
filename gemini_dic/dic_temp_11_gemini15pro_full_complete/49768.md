demonetization:/diːˌmʌnɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|
|废除货币; 废止货币流通|
|The act of stripping a currency unit of its status as legal tender.|
|The demonetization of 500 and 1000 rupee notes in India caused widespread disruption.  印度500和1000卢比纸币的废除造成了广泛的混乱。|

|The government's sudden demonetization policy led to long queues outside banks. 政府突然的废钞政策导致银行外排起了长队。|

|近义词: withdrawal, abolition|
|反义词: monetization|
|词性: noun|


demonetize:/diːˈmʌnɪtaɪz/
|vt.|
|使货币退出流通; 废除货币|
|To strip a currency unit of its status as legal tender.|
|The government demonetized the old currency notes. 政府废除了旧钞。|

|Many countries have demonetized gold coins. 许多国家已经停止了金币的流通。|

|近义词: withdraw, abolish|
|反义词: monetize|
|词性: verb|
