intervisiting:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪtɪŋ/
adjective
互相访问的；互访的
Engaging in mutual visits; visiting each other.
The intervisiting program between the two schools fostered a strong sense of community.  两个学校之间的互访项目促进了强烈的社区意识。
The intervisiting families enjoyed sharing their cultures and traditions.  互访的家庭喜欢分享彼此的文化和传统。
近义词: reciprocal visiting, exchanging visits
反义词: non-interacting, isolated
词性: adjective
