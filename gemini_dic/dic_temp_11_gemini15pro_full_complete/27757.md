cannabis:/ˈkænəbɪs/| n. |大麻，大麻属植物|A tall plant with strong-smelling leaves that is used to produce hemp fibre and drugs such as marijuana and hashish.|例句：Cannabis is a controlled substance in many countries.  大麻在许多国家是受管控的物质。

例句：The cultivation of cannabis is illegal.  种植大麻是非法的。|近义词：marijuana, pot, weed, ganja|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a plant)|词性:noun
