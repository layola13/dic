association:/əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/ |əˌsəʊʃiˈeɪʃn/| n. |协会；联盟；社团；联系；联想|A group of people organized for a joint purpose.  The connection of ideas or things in the mind.|例句：He joined the National Rifle Association. 他加入了国家步枪协会。

例句：The drug has been used in association with other treatments. 该药一直与其他疗法联合使用。|近义词：organization, union, society, connection, link|反义词:dissociation, separation|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *associationem (nominative associatio), from Latin associare ("to join to, unite, attach, connect").
