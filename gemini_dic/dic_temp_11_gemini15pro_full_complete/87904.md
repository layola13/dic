hierocracy:/haɪˈɪərəkrəsi/ | n. | 神权政治；僧侣统治 | A system of government ruled by priests or religious leaders. |  The ancient Egyptian civilization was a hierocracy, with the pharaoh considered a divine ruler.  古埃及文明是一个神权政治，法老被认为是神圣的统治者。

The hierocracy controlled every aspect of life in the city.  神权统治控制着城市生活的方方面面。 | synonyms: priestcraft, theocracy | antonyms: secularism, democracy | noun
