nonconstituent:/nɒnkənˈstɪtʃuənt/| adj. n.|非成分的，非选民的|A linguistic unit that is not a constituent of a larger unit.  A person who is not a constituent of a particular elected official.|The phrase "the red" in "the red house" is a constituent, but "red house" in "the red house paint" is a nonconstituent.|近义词:nonmember, outsider|反义词:constituent, member|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology:
From non- + constituent.  Constituent ultimately derives from Latin *constituēns*, present participle of *constituere* "to set up, establish".
