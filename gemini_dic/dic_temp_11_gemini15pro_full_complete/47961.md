deciduata: /dɪˈsɪdʒuətə/ | n. |蜕膜; 落叶层;  |The maternal part of the placenta formed from the endometrium during pregnancy.  The layer of leaves and other organic matter that accumulates on the forest floor. | The doctor examined the decidua after the birth. 医生在分娩后检查了蜕膜。 The thick decidua provided a rich habitat for invertebrates. 厚厚的落叶层为无脊椎动物提供了丰富的栖息地。 | 近义词: endometrium (specifically for the uterine lining),  leaf litter | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


deciduous: /dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/ | adj. |落叶的; 蜕膜的; |Shedding leaves annually. Relating to the decidua. |Deciduous trees lose their leaves in the fall. 落叶树木在秋天落叶。  The deciduous teeth are replaced by permanent teeth. 乳牙会被恒牙取代。| 近义词:  temporary, ephemeral (for shedding), caducous | 反义词: evergreen, persistent | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deciduus, from decidere "to fall off," from de- "down, off" + cadere "to fall."
