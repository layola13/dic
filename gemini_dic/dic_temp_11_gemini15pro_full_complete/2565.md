admittances:/ədˈmɪtənsɪz/
n.
允许进入; 承认; 进入权; 入场费
The process of being admitted; allowance of entry. The right or fact of being admitted.  The number of people admitted to a hospital, clinic, etc.
The museum grants free admittance to children under 12.  该博物馆允许12岁以下儿童免费入场。
New admittances to the hospital are restricted due to the pandemic. 由于疫情，医院限制了新病人入院。
Synonyms: entry, access, admission, acceptance
Antonyms: refusal, rejection, denial, exclusion
