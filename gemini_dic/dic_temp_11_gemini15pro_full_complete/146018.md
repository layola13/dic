overdrainage:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnɪdʒ/
noun
过度排水，排水过度
Excessive removal of water from an area, typically agricultural land or wetlands.
Overdrainage can lead to soil compaction and reduced crop yields.  过度排水会导致土壤板结和作物减产。
The overdrainage of the marsh destroyed the habitat of many species.  沼泽的过度排水破坏了许多物种的栖息地。
over-irrigation, waterlogging
underdrainage, water retention
n.
