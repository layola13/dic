bonnet:/ˈbɒnɪt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːnɪt/ (US)| n.|帽子；引擎盖；女帽|A kind of hat, typically tied under the chin and worn by women or children, or the hinged metal covering over the engine of a motor vehicle.|例句：The little girl wore a pretty pink bonnet. 小女孩戴着一顶漂亮的粉红色帽子。

例句：He opened the bonnet to check the oil level. 他打开引擎盖检查机油液位。|近义词：hat, cap (for headwear), hood (for car engine cover)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
