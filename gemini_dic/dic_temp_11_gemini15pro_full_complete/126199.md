mvp:/ˌɛm viː ˈpiː/ (US), /ˌɛm viː ˈpiː/ (UK)| n.|最有价值球员 (Most Valuable Player)|A player who is considered most valuable to their team.  The MVP award is often given at the end of a season or tournament.| LeBron James was named the MVP of the finals. 勒布朗·詹姆斯被评为总决赛最有价值球员。|synonyms: star player, key player, all-star|antonyms: benchwarmer, reserve player|noun

mvp:/ˌɛm viː ˈpiː/ (US), /ˌɛm viː ˈpiː/ (UK)| n.|最小可行产品 (Minimum Viable Product)|A product with just enough features to attract early-adopter customers and validate a product idea early.|The startup released an MVP to gather user feedback. 这家初创公司发布了一个最小可行产品来收集用户反馈。|synonyms: prototype, test version, beta version|antonyms: finished product, final version|noun
