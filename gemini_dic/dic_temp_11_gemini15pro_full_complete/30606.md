cerebration:/ˌsɛrɪˈbreɪʃən/ | n. |思考，思维活动|The process of thinking; mental activity.  Thought, especially of a serious or concentrated kind.|例句：After much cerebration, he finally found a solution to the problem. 经过一番深思熟虑后，他终于找到了问题的解决方案。|例句：Deep cerebration is required to solve this complex puzzle.  解决这个复杂的谜题需要深入的思考。|近义词：thinking, contemplation, reflection, deliberation, cogitation |反义词：mindlessness, thoughtlessness|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin cerebratio, from cerebrare "to use the brain," from cerebrum "brain."
