bendlet:/ˈbendlət/| n.|斜纹小带，细长条|A small or diminutive bend, especially one of six or more appearing on a shield in heraldry.  A narrow band or stripe, especially one that is slanted or diagonal.|The shield features three golden bendlets on a blue field.|近义词：stripe, band, bar|反义词: N/A|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bendel, diminutive of bende ("band, stripe"), from Frankish *band ("sign, banner"), from Proto-Germanic *bandą ("bond, something that binds").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhendh- ("to bind").
