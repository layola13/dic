grahamite:/ˈɡreɪəˌmaɪt/ | n. | 格雷厄姆沥青，一种天然沥青 | A natural asphalt occurring in veins, named after J. Lorimer Graham.  It is black, brittle, and infusible. |  Grahamite was mined in Oklahoma and used for paving.  格雷厄姆沥青在俄克拉荷马州被开采并用于铺路。 | 近义词: asphalt, bitumen | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: Named after J. Lorimer Graham (1836-1891), a chemist and asphalt expert.  源于化学家和沥青专家J. Lorimer Graham的名字。
