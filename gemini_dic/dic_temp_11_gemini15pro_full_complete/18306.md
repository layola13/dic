bellboy: /ˈbelbɔɪ/ (US) /ˈbelbɔɪ/ (UK)| n.|行李员，侍者|A hotel employee who carries luggage and performs other services for guests.|例句：The bellboy took our suitcases to our room. 行李员把我们的行李箱搬到了我们的房间。

例句：We tipped the bellboy generously for his helpfulness. 我们因为行李员的热心服务而给了他丰厚的小费。|近义词：porter, page|反义词: None|词性:noun
