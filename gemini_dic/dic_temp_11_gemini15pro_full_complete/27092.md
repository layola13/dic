calico:/ˈkælɪkoʊ/ /ˈkælɪkəʊ/
noun
印花棉布；印花棉布制品; 三色猫
Plain or printed cotton cloth, typically having a brightly colored floral pattern. A cat with a coat of three colours, typically black, white, and ginger.
She chose a bright calico for the curtains. 她选了一块鲜艳的印花棉布做窗帘。

My cat is a calico. 我的猫是三色猫。

Synonyms: chintz, gingham, muslin, percale (for fabric); tortoiseshell (for cat, but not exact)
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific type of fabric or cat coloration)
词性: noun
