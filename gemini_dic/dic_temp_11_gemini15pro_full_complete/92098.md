hyperresponsively:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈspɑːnsɪvli/
adverb|过度反应地，反应过强地|Responding excessively or to a greater degree than is expected or considered normal.|The child reacted hyperresponsively to the loud noise, bursting into tears.|近义词:overly sensitively, excessively reactively|反义词:unresponsively, apathetically|词性:副词
Etymology: From "hyper-" (over, excessive) + "responsive" (quick to react) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
