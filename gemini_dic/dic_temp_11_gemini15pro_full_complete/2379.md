adipoceration:/ˌædɪpəʊsəˈreɪʃən/| n.|尸蜡形成，脂肪蜡化|The process of transformation of dead animal tissues into a waxy substance called adipocere.|The archaeologist discovered a body undergoing adipoceration in the peat bog. 考古学家在泥炭沼泽中发现了一具正在进行脂肪蜡化的尸体。|Synonyms: saponification|Antonyms: decomposition, putrefaction|Noun


adipose:/ˈædɪˌpoʊs/| adj.|脂肪的，肥胖的|Containing or relating to fat.|Adipose tissue is the body's main way of storing energy. 脂肪组织是人体储存能量的主要方式。|Synonyms: fatty, obese, corpulent|Antonyms: lean, thin, slender|Adjective
