dower:/ˈdaʊər (US), ˈdaʊə (UK)| n. vt.|嫁妆，天赋；给予嫁妆，赋予|A dower is the property or money brought by a bride to her husband on their marriage.  It can also refer to a natural gift or talent.|例句：She brought a substantial dower to the marriage. 她为婚姻带来了丰厚的嫁妆。

例句：She was dowered with both beauty and intelligence. 她天生丽质，聪慧过人。|近义词：dowry, endowment, gift|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps debt?) |词性:noun, verb
