cheeseboards:/ˈtʃiːzbɔːrdz/| n. |奶酪板；奶酪拼盘|A board on which cheese is served or displayed, often with accompanying items like crackers, fruit, and nuts.|We arranged a beautiful cheeseboard for our guests. 我们为客人准备了一个漂亮的奶酪拼盘。|Synonyms: cheese platter, serving board|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "empty plate")|Noun
