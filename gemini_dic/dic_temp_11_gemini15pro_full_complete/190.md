abbas:/ˈæbəs/ /ˈæbæs/ | n. | 阿巴斯（阿拉伯男名） | An Arabic male given name, meaning "lion" or "stern".  Derived from the Arabic word "abbas" (عباس), meaning "frowning" or "stern-faced". It later gained the connotation of "lion" due to its association with courage and strength. | Abbas Kiarostami was an acclaimed Iranian filmmaker. 阿巴斯·基亚罗斯塔米是一位著名的伊朗电影制作人。

Abbas ibn Abd al-Muttalib was the uncle of the Islamic prophet Muhammad. 阿巴斯·伊本·阿卜杜勒·穆塔利布是伊斯兰教先知穆罕默德的叔叔。| 近义词: (No direct synonyms for a name, but similar sounding names might be considered:  Abas, Abis) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
