mossie:/ˈmɒzi/ (UK), /ˈmɑːzi/ (US)| n. |蚊子|Informal word for mosquito.  It's primarily used in Australia, New Zealand, and parts of the UK.  Derived by clipping and adding the diminutive suffix -ie to "mosquito".|例句：The mossies were terrible last night. 昨晚蚊子太多了。

例句：She swatted a mossie that was buzzing around her head. 她拍死了一只在她头上嗡嗡叫的蚊子。


|近义词：mosquito, gnat|反义词: (No direct antonym for a single insect)|词性:noun
