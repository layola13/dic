mimetene:/ˈmɪmɪˌtiːn/ | n. | 铅硒酸矿 | A mineral consisting of lead selenate, PbSeO4.  It is usually found as yellow to brown crystals and is a secondary mineral formed in the oxidized zone of lead deposits containing selenium. | Mimetene is often found alongside other secondary lead minerals.  铅硒酸矿常与其他次生铅矿物一起发现。| Mimetite, anglesite, crocoite | - | Noun
