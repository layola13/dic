gossipingly: /ˈɡɒsɪpɪŋli/ | adv. | 流言蜚语地，爱说闲话地 | in a gossiping manner; by gossiping |  She spoke gossipingly about her neighbors. 她饶舌地谈论她的邻居们。|  She gossipingly told me about the office romance. 她碎嘴地告诉我办公室恋情。 | 近义词：chattily, talkatively | 反义词：discreetly, reservedly | 词性: 副词

词源: From gossip + -ing + -ly.  "Gossip" comes from Old English *godsibb, meaning "sponsor at baptism," later coming to mean a close friend or acquaintance, and eventually evolving to mean idle talk or rumor.
