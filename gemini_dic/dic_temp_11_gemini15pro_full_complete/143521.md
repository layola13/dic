outgreens:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnz/
verb
胜过(在绿化方面)；在环保方面超过
surpasses in terms of environmental friendliness or green initiatives
The new eco-friendly housing development outgreens all the others in the city with its solar panels and rainwater harvesting system. 这个新的环保住宅开发项目凭借其太阳能电池板和雨水收集系统，在环保方面胜过该市所有其他项目。
The company outgreens its competitors by using only recycled materials.  这家公司只使用回收材料，在环保方面超过了竞争对手。
surpasses, outdoes, exceeds, bests
lags behind, falls short of
v.
