overmeddle:/ˌōvərˈmedl/ | v. | 过度干预，多管闲事 | To interfere in something that is not one's concern; to meddle excessively. |  He tends to overmeddle in his children's lives. 他倾向于过度干预孩子的生活。|  She overmeddled in the dispute and made things worse.  她在争执中过多干预，使事情变得更糟。 | 近义词: interfere, intrude, meddle | 反义词: ignore, neglect | 词性: 动词

overmeddling:/ˌōvərˈmedlɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度干预的，多管闲事的 | Given to meddling excessively. | His overmeddling mother-in-law was a constant source of frustration. 他多管闲事的岳母一直是他沮丧的来源。 | The overmeddling neighbor caused tension on the street.  爱管闲事的邻居造成了街上的紧张气氛。 | 近义词: interfering, intrusive, meddlesome | 反义词:  unobtrusive, respectful | 词性: 形容词
