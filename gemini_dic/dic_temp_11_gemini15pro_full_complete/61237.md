emailing:/ˈiːmeɪlɪŋ/
verb
发送电子邮件；用电子邮件发送
the activity of sending messages using email
例句：I spent the morning emailing clients. 我花了一上午时间给客户发邮件。
例句：Emailing is a quick and efficient way to communicate.  电子邮件是一种快速有效的沟通方式。
近义词：messaging, corresponding
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "mailing" if referring to physical mail)
词性：动名词 (gerund) or present participle
