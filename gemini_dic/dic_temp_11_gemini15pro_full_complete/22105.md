bolshevik: /ˈbɒlʃɪvɪk/ (UK) /ˈboʊlʃəvɪk/ (US)| n. | 布尔什维克 | A member of the majority faction of the Russian Social Democratic Party, which seized power in the October Revolution of 1917.  They later became the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. | The Bolsheviks, led by Lenin, overthrew the provisional government. 布尔什维克在列宁的领导下推翻了临时政府。| synonym: communist, revolutionary | antonym: Menshevik, Tsarist | noun

bolshevik: /ˈbɒlʃɪvɪk/ (UK) /ˈboʊlʃəvɪk/ (US)| adj. | 布尔什维克的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Bolsheviks. |  The Bolshevik revolution changed the course of Russian history. 布尔什维克革命改变了俄国历史的进程。 | synonym: communist, revolutionary | antonym: Menshevik, Tsarist | adjective
