oreodont:/ˈɔːriəˌdɒnt/ | n. | 偶蹄类动物(已灭绝) | An extinct group of artiodactyl mammals, also known as oreodonts, that flourished in North America during the Eocene and Oligocene epochs.  They were herbivorous and somewhat pig-like in appearance. |  Example: The oreodont fossils were found in the Badlands.  这些偶蹄类动物化石是在荒地发现的。

Example:  Oreodonts were a common sight in prehistoric North America.  偶蹄类动物在史前北美很常见。 | 近义词:  tylopod (broad category, not a true synonym) | 反义词: N/A (extinct group, no direct opposite) | 词性: noun
