conveniently:/kənˈviːniəntli/ | adv. | 方便地，便利地 | In a convenient manner; easily and without difficulty. | The supermarket is conveniently located near my house. 超市很方便，就在我家附近。 |  The library is conveniently situated next to the school. 图书馆位置便利，就在学校旁边。 | 近义词: easily, handily, readily | 反义词: inconveniently, awkwardly, difficultly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French *convenient, from Latin conveniēns, -ent- ‘coming together, fitting, agreeable’, present participle of convenīre (see convene).
