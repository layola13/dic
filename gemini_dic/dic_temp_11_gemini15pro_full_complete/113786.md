macrotrichium:/ˌmækroʊˈtrɪkiəm/ | n. | 巨毛藻属；巨毛藻 | A genus of filamentous green algae in the family Cladophoraceae, characterized by long, unbranched filaments and often forming dense mats in freshwater habitats. |  Example: Macrotrichium is a common algae found in rivers. 巨毛藻是河流中常见的藻类。

Example: The macrotrichium was blocking the water flow. 巨毛藻阻塞了水流。 | 近义词: Cladophora (a related genus), filamentous algae | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *makros* "long" + *thrix* "hair"
