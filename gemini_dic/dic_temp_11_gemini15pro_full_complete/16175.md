banknote:/ˈbæŋknəʊt/ (US: /ˈbæŋknoʊt/) | n. | 钞票，纸币 | A piece of paper money issued by a bank, promising to pay the bearer a specified sum. |  Example 1:  I paid for the groceries with a twenty-dollar banknote. 我用一张二十美元的钞票支付了杂货费用。

Example 2: Counterfeit banknotes are a serious problem for businesses.  假钞对企业来说是一个严重的问题。 | Synonym: bill, note, currency | Antonym: coin | noun
