myriad:/ˈmɪriəd/ | adj. n. |无数的，大量的；无数，大量|A countless or extremely great number.  Derived from the Greek "myrioi" meaning "ten thousand". Originally referred to a specific number (10,000), but evolved to mean a vast, indefinite number.|例句：There are myriad opportunities for young people today.  如今年轻人有很多机会。|近义词：innumerable, countless, numerous, multitude|反义词：few, scarce|词性:形容词/名词
