interfrontal:/ˌɪntərˈfrʌntəl/
adjective
额间骨间的
Situated between the frontal bones.
The interfrontal suture is a fibrous joint that fuses during development. 额间缝是一种在发育过程中融合的纤维连接。
The interfrontal region is located between the eyebrows. 额间区域位于眉毛之间。
Synonym: metopic
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider terms referring to other skull regions, like occipital)
词性: adjective
