nonsalubrious:/ˌnɒnsəˈluːbriəs/ | /ˌnɑːnsəˈluːbriəs/ | adj. | 不健康的，有害的 | Not conducive to good health; unhealthy. |  The nonsalubrious air quality in the city caused many respiratory problems. 城市里不健康的空气质量导致了许多呼吸系统疾病。|  Living in such a nonsalubrious environment is detrimental to one's health.  生活在如此不健康的环境中对一个人的健康有害。| 近义词: unhealthy, insalubrious, noxious, detrimental | 反义词: healthy, salubrious, wholesome | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *nonsalūbris*, from non- "not" + salūbris "healthful".
