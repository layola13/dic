knicks:/nɪks/| n. |纽约尼克斯队；(非正式)内裤；小玩意儿；小伤口；轻微的疼痛|A professional basketball team based in New York City. Also informally refers to underwear, small items, minor cuts, or slight pains.|例句：The Knicks played well last night. 纽约尼克斯队昨晚打得很好。

例句：He bought a pair of new knicks. 他买了一条新内裤。

近义词：underwear (for the informal meaning), trinkets (for small items), cuts (for minor wounds), pains (for slight aches)|反义词:(No direct antonyms for all meanings. Could be Lakers or Celtics as rival teams)|词性:noun


knick:/nɪk/| n. vt.|小刻痕；小伤口；轻微的疼痛；使有缺口|A small cut, notch, or chip. Also, a slight pain.  As a verb, it means to make a small cut or notch.|例句：He got a knick on his finger while chopping vegetables. 他切菜时手指上划了个小口子。

例句：She knicked the paintwork while moving the furniture. 她搬家具时刮坏了油漆。

近义词：cut, scratch, chip, notch, pain, ache|反义词:repair, heal|词性:noun, verb
