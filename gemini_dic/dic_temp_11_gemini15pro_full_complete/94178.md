imperatival:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪvəl/
| adj.|命令式的，祈使的|Relating to or expressing a command; having the form of a command.|Speak louder! is an imperatival sentence.  大声点说话！这是一个祈使句。|commanding, authoritative|suggestive, advisory|adjective


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Imperatival sentences are often used in the simple present tense.
祈使句通常用一般现在时。

Close the door.
关门。

Be quiet!
安静！


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The present continuous is rarely used in imperatival sentences.
现在进行时很少用于祈使句。

*Be closing the door. (Not usually used)
*(不常用)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The present perfect is not used in imperatival sentences.
现在完成时不用于祈使句。

*Have closed the door. (Incorrect)
*（不正确）


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The present perfect continuous is not used in imperatival sentences.
现在完成进行时不用于祈使句。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The past tense is not used in imperatival sentences.
过去时不用于祈使句。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The past continuous is not used in imperatival sentences.
过去进行时不用于祈使句。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The past perfect is not used in imperatival sentences.
过去完成时不用于祈使句。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The past perfect continuous is not used in imperatival sentences.
过去完成进行时不用于祈使句。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The future tense can sometimes be implied in imperatival sentences.
将来时有时可以隐含在祈使句中。

You will do as I say! (Although imperatival, this uses future tense.)
你得听我的！（虽然是祈使句，但这使用的是将来时。）

Do it tomorrow. (Implies future action)
明天做。（暗示未来的行动）


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

Be working on this tomorrow. (Uncommon usage, but possible in specific contexts)
明天开始做这个。(不常用，但在特定语境下可能使用)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
The future perfect is not used in imperatival sentences.
将来完成时不用于祈使句。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
The future perfect continuous is not used in imperatival sentences.
将来完成进行时不用于祈使句。



虚拟语气:
Imperatival mood itself is a kind of subjunctive.
祈使语气本身就是一种虚拟语气。

Let it be.
顺其自然吧。


被动语态:
The passive voice is not commonly used in imperatival sentences.
被动语态通常不用于祈使句。

Let it be done. (This uses a passive construction with an imperatival "let")
让它完成。（这是用祈使句“let”构成被动语态）


疑问句:
Imperatival sentences can be formulated as questions.
祈使句可以表述为疑问句。

Could you please close the door?
你能关上门吗？

Will you be quiet?
你能安静点吗？


否定句:
Don't open the door.
不要开门。

Do not make a sound.
不要出声。



PHRASE->
Use imperatival voice.
使用祈使语气。

Give me that book.
把那本书给我。

Don't be silly.
别傻了。

Come here.
过来。

Tell me the truth.
告诉我真相。
