breathingly:/ˈbriːðɪŋli/| adv.|屏息地，气喘吁吁地|In a manner characterized by noticeable breathing, often suggesting excitement, exertion, or anticipation.|She listened breathingly to the story, captivated by every word. 她屏息凝神地听着故事，被每个字都迷住了。|例句：He ran breathingly to the finish line. 他气喘吁吁地跑到终点线。|近义词：gaspingly, breathlessly|反义词: calmly, quietly|词性:副词

词源: From "breathing" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "brēthan" (to breathe).
