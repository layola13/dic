mulatto:/məˈlɑːtoʊ/ /mʊˈlætəʊ/| n.|混血儿（尤指白人和黑人的混血后代）|A person of mixed white and Black ancestry, especially a person with one white and one Black parent. This term is now considered offensive due to its origins in slavery and racial classification.|例句：In the 19th century, the term "mulatto" was commonly used in legal documents to classify people of mixed race. 19世纪，“mulatto”一词常用于法律文件中，对混血人种进行分类。

例句：The use of "mulatto" is now widely discouraged because of its historical association with slavery and racial discrimination.  现在普遍不鼓励使用“mulatto”，因为它与奴隶制和种族歧视的历史有关。|近义词：mixed-race, biracial, multiracial|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
