investure:/ɪnˈvɛstʃər/ | n. | 授职仪式; 授权;  (权力、财产等的)正式授予 |A ceremony in which someone formally receives an official position, title, or the right to something. | The king's investure was a grand affair. 国王的授职仪式是一件盛大的事情。|  The investure of power to the new president was delayed due to political unrest. 由于政治动荡，新总统的授权被推迟了。 | inauguration, installation, investiture ceremony, enthronement | divestiture | noun
