barterable: /ˈbɑːrtərəbl/ (US) /ˈbɑːtərəbl/ (UK)
adjective
可交换的，可易货的
Able to be exchanged for goods or services without using money.
Examples:
Are your skills barterable in this economy?  你的技能在这种经济环境下可以交换吗？

These handmade crafts are barterable for food at the local market. 这些手工制品可以在当地市场换取食物。

Synonyms:
exchangeable, tradable, swappable

Antonyms:
non-exchangeable, non-tradable
