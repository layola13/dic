imperative:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/| adj. n.|必要的，紧急的；命令，祈使语气|Of vital importance; crucial.  A command or order.|例句：It is imperative that we act now. 我们现在必须采取行动。|近义词：essential, crucial, vital, urgent, command, order|反义词:optional, unnecessary, elective|词性:adjective

imperative:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/| n.|命令，祈使语气|A command or order.  The grammatical mood that expresses a command or request.|例句："Sit down!" is an imperative. “坐下！”是一个祈使句。|近义词：command, order, directive|反义词:request, suggestion|词性:noun
