naturistically:/ˌnætʃərˈɪstɪkli/
adv.
自然主义地
in a way that is based on the belief that only natural laws and forces operate in the world
例句：She lives naturistically, growing her own food and using natural remedies. 她过着自然主义的生活，自己种植食物，并使用自然疗法。

例句：The artist painted the scene naturistically, capturing the light and shadow perfectly.  这位艺术家自然地描绘了这个场景，完美地捕捉了光影。
近义词：naturally, simply
反义词：artificially, synthetically
词性: 副词
