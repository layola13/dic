materialize:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪz/ /məˈtɪriəlaɪz/| v. |实现；出现；突然显现|To become real or actual; to appear suddenly or unexpectedly.|例句：The promised funding failed to materialize.  承诺的资金未能兑现。

例句：A figure materialized out of the fog.  一个人影从雾中出现。

近义词：appear, emerge, manifest, take shape, come into being|反义词:disappear, vanish, dematerialize|词性:动词
