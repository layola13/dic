consuetude:/ˈkɒnswiːtjuːd/ /ˈkɑːnswiːtuːd/
n.
习惯，惯例，风俗
A custom or way of behaving that has existed for a long time.
The old consuetude of shaking hands has become less common due to the pandemic. 由于疫情，握手的老习惯变得不那么普遍了。
Custom, practice, tradition, habit, usage, convention.
Innovation, change, novelty.
noun
