conjugial: /kənˈdʒuːdʒiəl/ | adj. | 婚姻的；夫妻的；配偶的 | Of or relating to marriage or the relationship between husband and wife.  |  例句：They lived in conjugial bliss. 他们过着幸福的婚姻生活。 | 例句：Conjugial love is a cornerstone of a strong family.  夫妻之爱是牢固家庭的基石。 | 近义词：marital, matrimonial, spousal | 反义词：unmarried, single, divorced | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *conjugialis, from Latin conjugium "marriage," from conjugere "to yoke together," from com- "together" + jugum "yoke."
