ostentatious:/ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 炫耀的，卖弄的 | Characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice. |  He drives an ostentatious sports car. 他开着一辆炫耀的跑车。| synonyms: showy, pretentious, flamboyant, flashy | antonyms: modest, humble, understated, unpretentious | adjective

ostentation:/ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 炫耀，卖弄 |  Pretentious and vulgar display, especially of wealth and luxury, intended to impress or attract notice. | The ostentation of the wedding was breathtaking. 婚礼的铺张令人叹为观止。| synonyms: showiness, pretentiousness, flamboyance, flashiness | antonyms: modesty, humility, understatement | noun

ostentator:/ˈɑːstənˌteɪtər/ | n. | 炫耀者，卖弄者 | A person who displays ostentation. |  He was an ostentator, always showing off his expensive clothes. 他是个炫耀者，总是炫耀他昂贵的衣服。| synonyms: show-off, braggart, exhibitionist | antonyms: modest person | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ostentator, agent noun from ostentare "to display, show," frequentative of ostendere "to exhibit, show," from ob "in front of, towards" + tendere "to stretch."
