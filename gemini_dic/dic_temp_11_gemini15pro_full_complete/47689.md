debulliate:/diːˈbʌlieɪt/
verb
消除…的沸腾；使平静
To remove bubbles from; to boil down.  Also, figuratively, to calm, soothe, or pacify.
The chef carefully debulliated the sauce.  厨师小心翼翼地消除了酱汁中的气泡。
Her calming words debulliated his anger. 她平静的话语平息了他的愤怒。
近义词:calm, soothe, pacify, quiet, allay
反义词:agitate, excite, provoke, inflame
词性:v.

Etymology:
From Latin *debullire, from de- (“down from, away”) + bullire (“to boil”).
