indulgence:/ɪnˈdʌldʒəns/ | n. | 放纵；沉溺；嗜好；宽容；特许；免除 | the act of allowing yourself or another person to have something enjoyable, especially more than is good for you  |  She allowed herself the indulgence of a long hot bath. 她让自己尽情享受了一次长时间的热水浴。 | synonyms: gratification, pleasure, luxury, treat, pampering | antonyms: abstinence, self-denial | noun

indulgence:/ɪnˈdʌldʒəns/ | n. | （尤指宗教的）特赦；赦罪 | (especially in the Roman Catholic Church) a declaration by the Church that a person’s sins are forgiven, or a way of showing this |  In the Middle Ages people could buy indulgences from the Church. 在中世纪，人们可以从教会购买赎罪券。 | synonyms: pardon, remission, forgiveness, absolution | antonyms: condemnation, punishment | noun
