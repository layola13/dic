comprising:/kəmˈpraɪzɪŋ/
verb
包括，包含，由…组成
consisting of; made up of
The committee comprises six members. 委员会由六名成员组成。
The house comprises two bedrooms, a living room, and a kitchen. 这栋房子包括两间卧室，一间客厅和一间厨房。
Synonyms:  consisting of, composed of, including, containing
Antonyms: excluding, omitting
词性: 动词
