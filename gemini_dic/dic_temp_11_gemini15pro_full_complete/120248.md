mingos:/ˈmɪŋɡoʊz/ | n. | 芒果 (复数) | Plural form of "mango".  Refers to more than one mango fruit. |  I bought five mingos at the market. 我在市场上买了五个芒果。|  | mangoes (singular: mango)| | Noun

Etymology: From Portuguese *manga*, from Malayalam *māṅṅa*, ultimately from Tamil *māṅkay* (fruit of the mango tree).
