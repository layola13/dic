changeable:/ˈtʃeɪndʒəbəl/| adj.|可变的，易变的|Subject to change or variation; likely to vary.|The weather here is very changeable.  这里的天气变化无常。|Synonyms: variable, mutable, fickle, unstable, erratic, fluctuating, inconstant|Antonyms: constant, stable, unchanging, fixed, permanent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From change + -able.  "Change" ultimately comes from the Latin "cambiare" meaning "to barter or exchange."
