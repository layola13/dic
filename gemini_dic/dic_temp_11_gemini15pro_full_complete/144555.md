outwaits:/ˌaʊtˈweɪts/
verb
比...等得更久; 比...更有耐心
waits longer than; surpasses in patience
He outwaited his rivals and eventually won the contract. 他比他的竞争对手等得更久，最终赢得了合同。
The hunter outwaited the deer. 猎人比鹿更有耐心，最终等到了它。
outlast, outstay
