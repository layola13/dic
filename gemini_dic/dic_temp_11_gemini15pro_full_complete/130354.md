non-Turks:/nɒnˈtɜːks/ | n. |非突厥人|People who are not of Turkic origin or descent.  This refers to individuals who do not identify as ethnically Turkic or belong to Turkic-speaking groups. |Many non-Turks live in Turkey, including Kurds, Armenians, and Greeks.  土耳其居住着许多非突厥人，包括库尔德人、亚美尼亚人和希腊人。

The Ottoman Empire was a diverse place, encompassing Turks and non-Turks alike. 奥斯曼帝国是一个多元化的地方，包括突厥人和非突厥人。|近义词:foreigners, outsiders (in a Turkish context)|反义词:Turks|词性:名词
