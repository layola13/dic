communities:/kəˈmjuːnɪtiz/ |n.| 社区，群体 |A group of people living in the same place or having a particular characteristic in common.|例句：Many online communities have formed around shared interests. 许多在线社区围绕共同的兴趣形成。|近义词：groups, societies, neighborhoods|反义词:individual, isolation|Noun

communities:/kəˈmjuːnɪtiz/ |n.| 界 |A body of people or things viewed collectively.|例句：The scientific community is divided on this issue. 科学界在这个问题上存在分歧。|近义词：circle, sphere, world|反义词: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *communite*, from Old French *comunité*, from Latin *communitās* (“community, fellowship, shared property; affability, courtesy, kindness”), from *communis* (“common”).
