economises:/iˈkɒnəmaɪzɪz/
|v.|节省，节约，有效利用|To spend less money than usual; to reduce one's expenses.  |She economises by buying groceries in bulk. 她通过批量购买食品杂货来节省开支。|
|v.|俭省，精打细算|To use something sparingly or efficiently. |The company economised on fuel by using more efficient vehicles.  公司通过使用更高效的车辆来节省燃料。|
Synonyms: saves, conserves, cuts back, reduces, scrimps, thrifties
Antonyms: squanders, wastes, splurges
verb

Etymology:
From French économiser, from économie (“economy”), from Ancient Greek οἰκονομία (oikonomía, “household management”), from οἶκος (oîkos, “house”) + νέμω (némō, “I manage, distribute”).
