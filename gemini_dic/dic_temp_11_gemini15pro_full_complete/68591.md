fantail:/ˈfænˌteɪl/ /ˈfænˌteɪl/| n. |扇尾；扇尾鸽；扇尾鱼|A fantail is something that has a tail shaped like a fan.  It can refer to a bird, a fish, or even a type of pigeon with a broad, spread-out tail.|The fantail pigeon strutted around, showing off its impressive plumage.  那只扇尾鸽昂首阔步地走来走去，炫耀着它令人印象深刻的羽毛。|Synonyms: fan-shaped tail, spread tail|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps a pointed tail)|noun

fantail:/ˈfænˌteɪl/ /ˈfænˌteɪl/| adj. |扇尾的|Having a tail shaped like a fan.|The fantail goldfish is a popular aquarium pet.  扇尾金鱼是一种受欢迎的观赏鱼。|Synonyms: fan-tailed|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
