initiatory:/ɪˈnɪʃiətɔːri/ /ɪˈnɪʃəˌtɔri/| adj. n.| 入门的，初步的；创始的；入会仪式| serving to initiate; introductory; pertaining to initiation; connected with initiation |The initiatory rites of the ancient mystery cults were shrouded in secrecy. 古代神秘教派的入会仪式笼罩在神秘之中。|近义词：introductory, preliminary, initial, beginning, inaugural|反义词：concluding, final, ending|词性:adjective, noun

initiatory:/ɪˈnɪʃiətɔːri/ /ɪˈnɪʃəˌtɔri/| n.| 入会仪式，启蒙仪式| a ceremony or rite of initiation |  The initiatory for new members included a symbolic washing of the feet.  新成员的入会仪式包括象征性的洗脚。 |近义词：rite, ceremony, ritual|反义词：None|词性:noun

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *initiatorius, from Latin initiat- ‘initiated’, from the verb initiare (see initiate).
