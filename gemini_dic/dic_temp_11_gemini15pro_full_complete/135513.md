nonplastics:/ˌnɑːnˈplæstɪks/
n.
非塑料制品
materials that are not plastic
Consumers are increasingly choosing nonplastics like glass and metal. 消费者越来越多地选择玻璃和金属等非塑料制品。
近义词: alternatives to plastics, non-plastic materials
反义词: plastics
词性: 名词
