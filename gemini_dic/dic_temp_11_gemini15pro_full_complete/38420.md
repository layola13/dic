concertation: /ˌkɒnsəˈteɪʃən/ (US) /ˌkɑːnsərˈteɪʃn/| n. | 协商，磋商，协调 |The act of working together or consulting with others to make a joint decision or plan.  |例句：Concertation between government and industry is essential for economic growth. 政府和产业间的协商对于经济增长至关重要。

例句：The successful project was a result of concertation among various departments.  这个成功的项目是各部门协商的结果。

近义词：collaboration, cooperation, consultation, negotiation | 反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict |词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "concertation," from "concerter" (to arrange, agree upon), from Italian "concertare," ultimately from Latin "concertare" (to contend, dispute, concert), from "com-" (together) + "certare" (to contend, strive). The meaning shifted from "contending" to "agreeing" over time.
