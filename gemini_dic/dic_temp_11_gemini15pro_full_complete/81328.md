graphemically:/ɡræˈfɪmɪkli/ | adv. | 拼写地，以拼写的方式 |  Relating to or expressed in graphemes, the smallest units in a writing system. |  The word "cat" is graphemically represented by three letters.  这个单词“cat”由三个字母拼写表示。 | synonym: orthographically | antonym: phonetically | 词性: 副词
