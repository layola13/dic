dirigent:/ˈdɪrɪdʒənt/ /ˈdɪrɪʒənt/
n.|指挥；乐队指挥|A person who directs the performance of an orchestra or choir.|The dirigent led the orchestra with passion and precision. 指挥家充满激情和精准地指挥着乐队。|Synonyms: conductor, maestro, director|Antonyms: follower, musician (in a general sense)|noun

dirigent:/ˈdɪrɪdʒənt/ /ˈdɪrɪdʒənt/
adj.|指挥的；指导的|Serving to direct or guide.|He has a dirigent mind, always planning and organizing. 他有着指挥家的头脑，总是计划和组织。|Synonyms: directing, guiding, leading|Antonyms: following, subordinate|adjective
