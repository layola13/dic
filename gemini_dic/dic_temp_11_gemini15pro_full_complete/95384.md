incharitable:/ɪnˈtʃærɪtəbl/
adjective
无情的，刻薄的，不善良的
Lacking or showing a lack of charity; unkind; unsympathetic.
It was an incharitable remark about her appearance.  她对外貌的评论很不友善。
He made some incharitable comments about his colleagues. 他对同事们说了一些刻薄的话。
近义词:unkind, unsympathetic, harsh, cruel, mean
反义词:charitable, kind, compassionate, benevolent, generous
词性:adjective
