demister:/diːˈmɪstər/ | n. | 除雾器；去雾器 | A device used to remove droplets of liquid from a stream of gas. |  The demister in the bathroom mirror prevents it from fogging up. 浴室镜子里的除雾器防止它起雾。| synonyms: defogger, dehumidifier | antonyms: humidifier, fogger | noun

demist:/diːˈmɪst/ | v. | 除雾；去除雾气 | To remove mist or condensation from something. | I had to demist the car windows before I could drive. 我开车前必须先清除车窗上的雾气。| synonyms: defog, clear | antonyms: fog, mist | verb
