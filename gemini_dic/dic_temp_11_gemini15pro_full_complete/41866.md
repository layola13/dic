coscinomancy:/ˈkɒsɪnəʊmænsi/ /ˈkɑːsɪnəʊmænsi/ | n. | 筛占法 | Divination by observing the movements or positions of a sieve suspended or thrown.  It involves interpreting the way a sieve moves or falls to predict the future or answer questions. |  Coscinomancy was a popular form of divination in ancient Greece. 筛占法在古希腊是一种很流行的占卜形式。

Coscinomancy is considered a superstitious practice. 筛占法被认为是一种迷信的做法。 | 近义词：divination, fortune-telling | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可用skepticism表示怀疑态度) | 词性: noun
