What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on? = 你一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if I press this button? = 如果我按下这个按钮会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？

Future Perfect:
