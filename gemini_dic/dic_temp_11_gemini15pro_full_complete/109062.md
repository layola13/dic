legalize: /ˈliːɡəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使合法化，合法化 | To make something legal; to give legal status to something that was previously illegal. |  The government legalized marijuana for recreational use. 政府将娱乐用大麻合法化。

The state legalized gambling in 1995.  该州于1995年将赌博合法化。| synonyms: legitimize, authorize, decriminalize | antonyms: prohibit, ban, outlaw | verb
