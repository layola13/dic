fishgig:/ˈfɪʃɡɪɡ/| n.|鱼叉|A barbed spear or pronged instrument used for spearing fish.|He caught a large salmon with his fishgig.|近义词：spear, harpoon|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fish + gig (a pronged spear).  The "gig" part likely comes from Old Norse *geiga "to shake, sway."
