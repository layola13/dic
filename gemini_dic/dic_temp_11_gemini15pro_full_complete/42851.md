courtbred:/ˈkɔːrtbrɛd/| adj.|受过良好教养的，有贵族气质的；温文尔雅的|Having refined manners or upbringing, characteristic of aristocratic society.|She has a courtbred elegance about her. 她身上有一种贵族般的优雅气质。|Synonyms: refined, polished, cultivated, aristocratic, genteel, well-bred|Antonyms: uncouth, vulgar, coarse, unrefined, ill-mannered|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From court (royal court) + bred (past participle of breed, meaning "raised").  The word suggests being raised in a courtly environment, thus acquiring refined manners and behavior.
