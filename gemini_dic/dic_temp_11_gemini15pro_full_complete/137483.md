nontoned:/nɒnˈtəʊnd/ | adj. | 非声调的，无调的 |  Lacking tones, especially in speech or music; uninflected.  This describes something that doesn't vary in pitch or tone. It's most often used in linguistics to describe languages or dialects that don't use tone to distinguish meaning. |  Many European languages are nontoned, unlike Mandarin Chinese. 许多欧洲语言是非声调语言，不像普通话。 | The robot's speech was nontoned and monotonous.  机器人的声音没有音调，单调乏味。 | synonyms: uninflected, monotonic, monotone | antonyms: toned, inflected | adjective
