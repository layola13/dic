mortling:/ˈmɔːrtlɪŋ/| n.| 垂死挣扎; 濒死状态;  (尤指动物)临死前的挣扎 |A mortling is the final struggle of a dying person or animal.|The deer gave a few weak mortlings before expiring.| 近义词: death throes, agony, last gasp | 反义词: birth, life | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "mortal" (subject to death) + "-ing" (present participle ending), suggesting the ongoing process of dying.
