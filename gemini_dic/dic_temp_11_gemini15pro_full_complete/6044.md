amended:/əˈmendɪd/| adj.|修改的，修正的|Changed or corrected; improved.|例句：The amended contract includes a new clause about liability. 修改后的合同包含一条关于责任的新条款。|例句：They submitted an amended version of the proposal. 他们提交了修改后的提案版本。|近义词：revised, corrected, altered, modified|反义词：original, unchanged, uncorrected|词性:形容词

词源: From the Latin "emendare," meaning "to remove faults, correct."  The prefix "e-" means "out" or "away," and "menda" means "fault" or "blemish."
