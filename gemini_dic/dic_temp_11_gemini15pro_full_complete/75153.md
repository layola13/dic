fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/| n. v. |油炸馅饼； fritter away 浪费，挥霍|A small quantity of batter, containing fruit, vegetables, or meat, that is fried and eaten.|She made apple fritters for dessert. 她做了苹果油炸饼当甜点。|近义词:donut, beignet, pancake|反义词: N/A|词性:名词、动词
fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/| v. |浪费，挥霍|To waste time, money, or energy on trifling matters.|He frittered away his inheritance on gambling. 他把遗产都浪费在了赌博上。|近义词:squander, waste, dissipate|反义词:save, conserve, hoard|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fritre (“fry”) (compare friture), from Latin frictūra (“a frying”), from frictus, past participle of frigō (“fry, roast”).
