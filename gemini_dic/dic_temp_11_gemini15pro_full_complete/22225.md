bonchretien:/ˌbɒnˌkrɛtˈjɛn/ | n. | 好基督梨 | A variety of pear, also known as the Williams pear, known for its sweet flavor and juicy texture. It is named after Peter Quetin, a gardener of Louis XIV of France. |  This bonchretien pear is perfectly ripe. 这个好基督梨熟透了。|  The bonchretien is my favorite type of pear. 好基督梨是我最喜欢的梨品种。|  Synonyms: Williams pear, Bartlett pear (in the US) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
