alveolarity:/ˌælviˈəlærɪti/ | n. | 齿槽音性；肺泡音 | The quality of a speech sound being articulated with the tip of the tongue against the alveolar ridge (the gum ridge behind the upper teeth). |  The alveolarity of the /t/ sound is very clear in her pronunciation. 她的发音中 /t/ 音的齿槽音性很明显。|  alveolar articulation, alveolar pronunciation | N/A | noun
