fulmine:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪ/ (US), /ˈfʊlmiːn/ (UK)| n.|雷电; 霹雳; 谴责; 猛烈抨击|A sudden, violent outburst; a denunciation or condemnation.  It refers to both a literal thunderbolt and a figurative outburst of anger or criticism.|The politician launched a fulmine against his opponent's policies.  这位政治家猛烈抨击了对手的政策。|Synonyms: thunderbolt, blast, denunciation, diatribe|Antonyms: praise, commendation|noun

fulminate:/ˈfʌlmɪneɪt/ (US), /ˈfʌlmɪneɪt/ (UK)| v.|大声斥责; 猛烈抨击; 爆炸; detonate|To explode suddenly and violently; to issue a denunciation or condemnation.|He fulminated against the injustice of the system. 他猛烈抨击该系统的不公正。|Synonyms: denounce, condemn, criticize, explode, detonate|Antonyms: praise, commend, support|verb
