artboard:/ˈɑːrtbɔːrd/
n.
画板，绘图板
A surface, often part of a design software program, used for creating and arranging visual elements.
The designer arranged all the images on the artboard before exporting the final design.  设计师在导出最终设计之前将所有图像排列在画板上。
