caress: /kəˈrɛs/ /kəˈres/ | n. vt. |爱抚，抚摸；拥抱；珍爱；|An act of touching or holding someone gently and affectionately. |She caressed his cheek tenderly. 她温柔地抚摸着他的脸颊。|He caressed the smooth surface of the wood. 他抚摸着光滑的木头表面。|近义词：stroke, touch, fondle, cuddle, embrace|反义词：hit, strike, abuse|词性:名词,动词
词源: From Old French *caresser*, from Late Latin *caritiare* "to make dear, caress," from *carus* "dear."
