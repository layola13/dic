misween:/mɪsˈwiːn/
verb
感到不适；行为不端
to behave badly, especially as a child; to be unwell
He was always misweening as a child, throwing tantrums and refusing to listen. 他小时候总是行为不端，乱发脾气，不肯听话。
She felt a bit misweened after eating too much candy. 她吃了太多糖果后感觉有点不舒服。
近义词: misbehave, ail
反义词: behave, thrive
词性: verb
