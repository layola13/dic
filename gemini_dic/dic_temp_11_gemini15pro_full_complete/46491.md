dainty:/ˈdeɪnti/ /ˈdeɪnti/| adj. n.|精致的；美味的；挑剔的；娇美的；美食|Something dainty is small, delicate, and pretty.  A dainty person is refined and delicate in appearance, manner, or tastes.  Dainty can also refer to a delicacy, a choice morsel of food.|She wore a dainty silver necklace. 她戴着一条精致的银项链。|近义词：delicate, exquisite, elegant, refined, fastidious, choosy, particular|反义词：coarse, crude, clumsy, inelegant, unrefined, undemanding|词性: adjective, noun
dainty:/ˈdeɪnti/ /ˈdeɪnti/| n.|美食；美味佳肴|A dainty is a delicacy, a choice morsel of food.|The cafe served dainty sandwiches and cakes. 这家咖啡馆供应精致的三明治和蛋糕。|近义词：delicacy, treat, morsel|反义词：None (as a noun)|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *daintie* "delightful, pleasant, agreeable," from *dainté,* past participle of *deindre,* *daindre* "to consider worthy, deign," ultimately from Latin *dignari* "to deem worthy," from *dignus* "worthy."
