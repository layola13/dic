bentwoods:/ˈbɛntˌwʊdz/
noun
曲木家具
Furniture (especially chairs) made by steaming and bending wood.
The cafe was furnished with simple bentwoods and marble-topped tables.  这家咖啡馆配备了简单的曲木家具和大理石面的桌子。
