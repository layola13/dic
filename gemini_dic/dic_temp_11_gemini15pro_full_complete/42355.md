counterargue:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuː/ | v. | 反驳，提出反对意见 | To argue against something that someone else has said. | He counterargued that the evidence was flawed. 他反驳说证据有缺陷。|  She counterargued his proposal with a detailed presentation of her own. 她用自己详细的陈述来反驳他的提议。 | 近义词: rebut, refute, dispute, oppose | 反义词: agree, concede, support | 词性: 动词
