clayey:/ˈkleɪi/ | adj. | 粘土的；含粘土的 | Consisting of or containing clay; resembling clay. | The soil in my garden is quite clayey.  我的花园里的土壤非常粘。| near synonyms: argillaceous, argilliferous, clayish | near antonyms: sandy, gravelly | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From clay + -y (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'characterized by' or 'full of').  Clay itself comes from Old English clæg.
