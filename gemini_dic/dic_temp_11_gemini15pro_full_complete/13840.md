audity:/ˈɔːdɪti/ | n. | 审计；查账；财务审查 | An official inspection of an organization's or individual's accounts, typically by an independent body. |  Example 1: The company undergoes a yearly audity. 这家公司每年都会进行一次审计。

Example 2:  The audity revealed some irregularities in the financial records. 审计揭露了财务记录中的一些违规行为。 | Synonyms: audit, examination, review, inspection | Antonyms:  negligence, disregard | Noun
