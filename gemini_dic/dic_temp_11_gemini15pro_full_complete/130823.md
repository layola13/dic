nonappealingly:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːlɪŋli/
adverb
没有吸引力地；不引人注目地
in a way that is not attractive or interesting
He dressed nonappealingly in a stained old sweatshirt. 他穿着一件沾满污渍的旧运动衫，看起来毫无吸引力。
The food was presented nonappealingly. 食物摆放得毫无吸引力。
Synonyms: unattractively, displeasingly, repellently
Antonyms: attractively, appealingly, enticingly
词性: 副词
