gaily:/ˈɡeɪli/| adv.|快乐地，高兴地|In a cheerful or lighthearted manner; merrily.|She danced gaily around the room. 她高兴地在房间里跳舞。|The birds sang gaily in the trees. 鸟儿在树上欢快地歌唱。|近义词：cheerfully, merrily, joyfully, happily|反义词：sadly, gloomily, morosely|词性:副词
gaily:|词源：From Middle English *gayli, *gayliche, from Old French gaiement, from gai (“gay, joyful”) + -ment (“-ly”).| | | | |词性:副词
