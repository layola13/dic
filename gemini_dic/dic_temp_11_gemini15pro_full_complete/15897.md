balloter:/ˈbælətər/| n.|投票者；（尤指）不记名投票者|A person who votes, especially in a secret ballot.|The balloters lined up to cast their votes. 投票者排队投票。|Synonyms: voter, elector|Antonyms: non-voter, abstainer|noun

balloter:/ˈbælətər/| v.|投票；抽签决定|To vote, especially by secret ballot; to decide something by drawing lots.|They balloted for a new chairman. 他们投票选举新主席。|Synonyms: vote, elect, poll|Antonyms: abstain|verb
