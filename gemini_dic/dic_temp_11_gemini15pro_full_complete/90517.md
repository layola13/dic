hudsonian:/hʌdˈsoʊniən/ | adj. | 哈德逊湾的；哈德逊河的；哈德逊学派的 | Of or relating to the Hudson River, Hudson Bay, or the Hudson River School of painting. |  The museum has a large collection of Hudsonian landscapes.  博物馆收藏了大量的哈德逊河画派风景画。 | Nearctic, Canadian | N/A | adjective

Etymology ->
From Hudson + -ian.  Ultimately from the personal name *Hudde*.
