crowdy:/ˈkraʊdi/ | adj. | 拥挤的 | Full of people, packed.  Resembling or characteristic of a crowd.  This word is considered informal or dialectal and "crowded" is generally preferred. | The market was crowdy and noisy. 市场很拥挤，很吵闹。 | The beach was crowdy with tourists. 海滩上挤满了游客。 |  近义词: crowded, packed, jammed, thronged | 反义词: empty, deserted, sparse | 词性: 形容词
词源: From crowd + -y.
