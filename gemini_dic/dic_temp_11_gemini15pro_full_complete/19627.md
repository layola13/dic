bijou:/ˈbiːʒuː/ (US), /ˈbiːʒuː/ (UK)| n.|小巧精致的饰品；珍品；精品|A small, delicate, and exquisitely wrought trinket, often jeweled.  A small, elegant, and often expensive item.|She wore a beautiful bijou brooch on her lapel. 她胸前戴着一枚漂亮的小巧精致的胸针。

The restaurant was a bijou, hidden away in a quiet alley. 这家餐厅是一家隐藏在安静小巷里的精品餐厅。|近义词：trinket, bauble, gem, jewel, treasure|反义词: monstrosity, eyesore|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French bijou ("jewel, trinket"), from Breton bizou ("ring"), of uncertain origin.
