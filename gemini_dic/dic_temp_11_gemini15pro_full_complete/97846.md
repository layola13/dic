inlapidation:/ɪnˌlæpɪˈdeɪʃən/| n.|荒废，破败；用石头砸死的行为|The state of being ruined or decayed; the act of stoning someone to death.|The inlapidation of the ancient city was evident. 这座古城的破败显而易见。|The inlapidation was a common punishment in ancient times.  用石头砸死是古代一种常见的惩罚。|近义词:ruin, dilapidation, decay, deterioration|反义词:restoration, renovation, repair|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inlapidāre, from in- + lapis (“stone”).  Meaning "to stone to death" is older; meaning "to bring to ruin" arose from the image of a building ruined by stones thrown at it.
