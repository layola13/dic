mayer:/ˈmeɪər/ | n. | 市长 | A mayor is the elected head of a city or town. |  例句：The mayor announced a new city initiative. 市长宣布了一项新的城市计划。

例句：The mayor is responsible for the city's budget. 市长负责城市的预算。| 近义词：burgomaster, city manager | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *maire, *mayre, from Anglo-Norman maire, Old French maire (“mayor, magistrate, elder”), from Latin maior (“greater, elder, superior”), comparative of magnus (“great, large, big, important”). Doublet of major.
