liquidity:/lɪˈkwɪdɪti/| n.|流动性；资产变现能力|The company's liquidity allows it to invest in new projects.  这家公司的流动性使其能够投资新项目。|Synonyms: fluidity, convertibility|Antonyms: illiquidity, solidity|noun

Etymology->
From Latin liquidus (“liquid, flowing, clear”), from liquēre (“to be fluid, liquid, or clear”).
