nightman:/ˈnaɪtmæn/| n. |夜行者；夜间工作的人；（尤指）罪犯|A person who works at night or roams around at night, often with criminal intent.|例句：The nightman emptied the bins. 清洁工清空了垃圾桶。

例句：The police were on the lookout for the nightman who had been terrorizing the neighborhood. 警方正在搜捕那个恐吓邻居的夜行者。|近义词：prowler, night owl, night worker|反义词:day worker|词性:noun
