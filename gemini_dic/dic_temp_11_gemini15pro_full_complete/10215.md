apostoless: /əˈpɒslɪs/ | n. | 女使徒，女性传道者 | A female apostle; a woman who is sent on a mission to spread a particular belief or doctrine. |  Mary Magdalene is often considered an apostoless.  玛丽·抹大拉常被认为是一位女使徒。|  She traveled as an apostoless, spreading the new faith. 她作为一名女使徒旅行，传播新的信仰。 | synonyms: female apostle, preacher, missionary, evangelist | antonyms: unbeliever, heretic | noun
