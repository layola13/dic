detonations:/ˌdetəˈneɪʃənz/
n.
爆炸，引爆，爆轰
the act of exploding something, or the fact of something exploding
例句：The detonations shook the entire building. 爆炸震动了整栋楼。

The detonations were heard miles away. 爆炸声几英里外都能听到。

近义词：explosions, blasts
反义词：implosions
词性：名词(plural)

词源: From Latin detonare "to thunder down," from de- "down" + tonare "to thunder."
