berating:/bɪˈreɪtɪŋ/
berate:/bɪˈreɪt/
verb
严厉斥责，痛骂
To criticize or scold someone angrily for something they have done.
The coach berated the players for their poor performance. 教练因球员表现不佳而严厉斥责他们。
He berated her for being late. 他因她迟到而严厉斥责她。
近义词: scold, rebuke, reprimand, chastise, upbraid
反义词: praise, commend, compliment
词性: verb
