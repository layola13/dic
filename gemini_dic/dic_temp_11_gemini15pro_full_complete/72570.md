fluviograph:/ˈfluːviəɡrɑːf/
n.
河流水位记录仪，水位计
An instrument that automatically records the variations in the height of the water level in a river or stream.
The fluviograph showed a significant rise in the river level after the heavy rain.  河流水位记录仪显示暴雨后河流水位显著上升。
The data from the fluviograph is used to predict potential floods.  来自水位计的数据被用于预测潜在的洪水。
synonyms:  river gauge, water level recorder, limnigraph
antonyms:  None
noun
