metaplastic:/ˌmɛtəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
化生的，转化生的
Relating to or characterized by metaplasia, the change of cells to a form that is not normal for that tissue.
Metaplastic changes in the epithelium can be a precursor to cancer. 上皮的化生性改变可能是癌症的前兆。
synonyms: transformed, converted, altered
antonyms: unchanged, normal, typical
