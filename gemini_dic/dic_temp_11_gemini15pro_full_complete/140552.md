oliban:/ˈoʊlɪbæn/| n. |乳香，熏香|A gum resin obtained from certain trees, burned as incense.|The air was thick with the scent of oliban. 空气中弥漫着浓浓的乳香气味。|近义词：frankincense, incense|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *oliban, from Medieval Latin olibanum, from Arabic al-lubān (“the frankincense”), from lubān (“frankincense”). Ultimately from Akkadian *lubānu.
