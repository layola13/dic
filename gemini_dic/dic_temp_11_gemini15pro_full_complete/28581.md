careerist:/kəˈrɪərɪst/
n.|野心家；一心追求升迁的人|A person whose primary goal is to advance their career, often at the expense of others or ethical considerations. | He's such a careerist; he'd do anything to get ahead. 他真是个野心家，为了升迁什么事都做得出来。|synonyms: climber, opportunist, go-getter|antonyms:  altruist, humanitarian|noun

careerist:/kəˈrɪərɪst/
adj.|追求升迁的；野心勃勃的| Primarily concerned with advancing one's career, often at the expense of others or ethical considerations. |She has a careerist attitude, always focusing on the next promotion. 她一心想升职，态度非常功利。|synonyms: ambitious, driven, pushy|antonyms: selfless, unambitious|adjective
