ordinally:/ˈɔːrdɪnəli/ | adv. | 通常地，按顺序地 | In a usual, regular, or ordered way, following a particular sequence. | The books are arranged ordinally by author. 这些书按作者的顺序排列。|  The contestants were ordinally ranked based on their scores. 参赛者根据他们的分数依次排名。 | 近义词: sequentially, numerically, consecutively | 反义词: randomly, haphazardly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Middle English ordinal, from Medieval Latin ōrdinālis, from Latin ōrdinālis (“of or belonging to order”), from ōrdō (“row, rank, order”).
