morelle:/mɔːˈrɛl/ | n. | 欧洲黑茄，颠茄，黑莓 |A poisonous plant (Atropa belladonna) with dark purple flowers and shiny black berries. Also sometimes used to refer to the berries of other plants, particularly the black nightshade (Solanum nigrum).|  She was poisoned by eating morelles she found in the forest. 她吃了在森林里找到的颠茄而中毒了。| synonym: belladonna, nightshade | antonym: (No direct antonym for a plant, could use "edible plant" as a contrasting concept)| noun
