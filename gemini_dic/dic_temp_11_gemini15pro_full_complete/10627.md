approbator:/ˈæprəˌbeɪtər/ | n. | 批准者，认可者 | Someone who approves or sanctions something. | The approbator of the project gave the final go-ahead.  项目批准者最终同意了。 | 近义词: ratifier, endorser, supporter, sanctioner | 反义词: critic, objector, opponent | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *approbator, from Latin approbare "to approve," from ad- "to" + probare "to test, prove good," from probus "good, honest."
