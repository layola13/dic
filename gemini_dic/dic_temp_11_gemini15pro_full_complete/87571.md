heterophonic:/ˌhɛtərəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈfɑːnɪk/ | adj. |异音的|Characterized by heterophony.|The heterophonic texture of the piece created a rich and layered sound.|近义词:polyphonic, multiphonic|反义词:homophonic, monophonic|词性:形容词
