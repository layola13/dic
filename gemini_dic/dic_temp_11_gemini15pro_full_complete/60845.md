electrotonize:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈtəʊnaɪz/| v. |电紧张|To alter the electrical potential of a nerve or muscle by passing a current through it.|Electrotonus can be used to stimulate or inhibit nerve activity. 电紧张可以用来刺激或抑制神经活动。|Near synonyms: stimulate, activate, polarize|Near antonyms: inhibit, deactivate, depolarize|verb

electrotonus:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈtəʊnəs/| n. |电紧张|The altered electrical potential of a nerve or muscle caused by the passage of an electric current.|The electrotonus caused by the stimulation was measurable. 由刺激引起的电紧张是可以测量的。|Near synonyms: polarization, potential difference|Near antonyms: depolarization|noun
