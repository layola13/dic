avenging:/əˈvɛndʒɪŋ/| adj.|复仇的；报复的|Taking vengeance for a wrong or injury; inflicting punishment in return for an offense.|例句：He set out on an avenging mission. 他开始了一项复仇任务。

例句：The avenging angel descended from the heavens. 复仇的天使从天而降。


近义词：retributive, vindictive, retaliatory|反义词:forgiving, merciful|词性:形容词
