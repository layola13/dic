battalion:/bəˈtæljən/ | n. | 营，大队 | A large body of troops ready for battle, typically consisting of three to five companies and numbering from 300 to 1,000 soldiers. | The battalion was deployed to the front lines. 这个营被部署到了前线。 | synonyms: regiment, unit, force, contingent | antonyms: individual, platoon (smaller unit) | noun


battalion:/bəˈtæljən/ | n. | (喻)大批，大群 | A large group of people or things. | A battalion of lawyers worked on the case.  一大群律师处理这个案件。| synonyms: horde, throng, multitude, mass | antonyms: handful, few | noun
