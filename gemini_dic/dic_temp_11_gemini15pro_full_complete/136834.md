nonsilicate:/nɒnˈsɪlɪkeɪt/ /nɑːnˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
adjective|非硅酸盐的|Not composed of or containing silicate.|Nonsilicate minerals make up a small percentage of the Earth's crust.|近义词: non-siliceous|反义词: silicate
noun|非硅酸盐|A mineral that does not contain silicon and oxygen as the principal components of its structure.|Olivine is a common silicate mineral, while pyrite is a nonsilicate.|近义词: non-siliceous mineral|反义词: silicate mineral
词性: adjective, noun
