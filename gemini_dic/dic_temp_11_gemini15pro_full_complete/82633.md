gudgeon:/ˈɡʌdʒən/| n.|鲦鱼；易受骗的人；枢轴；插销|A small freshwater fish of the carp family, often used as bait.  A person who is easily tricked or cheated. A pivot or journal. A type of metal pin or bolt used as a fastening.|例句：The fisherman used a gudgeon as bait. 渔夫用鲦鱼作诱饵。

He was a gudgeon, easily taken in by her lies. 他很容易上当受骗，被她的谎言所蒙蔽。|近义词：dupe, sucker, fool, pivot, pin|反义词:skeptic|词性:noun


gudgeon:/ˈɡʌdʒən/| vt.|用插销固定；欺骗|To fix or fasten with a gudgeon. To cheat or swindle.|例句：They gudgeoned the gate to the post. 他们用插销把大门固定在柱子上。

He gudgeoned the old lady out of her savings. 他骗走了老太太的积蓄。|近义词：swindle, cheat, defraud, deceive|反义词: protect, support |词性:verb
