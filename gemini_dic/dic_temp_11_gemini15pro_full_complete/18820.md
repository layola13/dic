bertillon:/bərˈtiljən/
n.
Bertillon system, Bertillonage
伯蒂永测身法（一种早期的人体测量识别系统）
A system of criminal identification based on anthropometric measurements.  It was developed by Alphonse Bertillon, a French police officer.
The police used Bertillon measurements to identify the suspect. 警方使用伯蒂永测身法来识别嫌疑人。
近义词:anthropometry, biometrics
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


bertillon:/bərˈtiljən/
adj.
Bertillon
伯蒂永的，与伯蒂永测身法相关的
Relating to or based on the Bertillon system of identification.
The Bertillon method was eventually replaced by fingerprinting. 伯蒂永方法最终被指纹识别取代。
近义词: anthropometric
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
