issues:/ˈɪʃuːz/ (US), /ˈɪʃuːz/ (UK)| n.|问题，议题，期刊，发行，子女|A matter that is in dispute between two or more parties.|The committee is considering the issue of school funding. 委员会正在审议学校经费问题。|synonyms: problems, concerns, topics, matters|antonyms: solutions, resolutions|noun

issues:/ˈɪʃuːz/ (US), /ˈɪʃuːz/ (UK)| v.|发行，发布，出版，流出|To formally send out or make available.|The government issued a statement about the incident. 政府就此事发表了声明。|synonyms: release, distribute, publish|antonyms: withhold, suppress|verb
