metol:/ˈmɛtɒl/ (US) /ˈmiːtɒl/ (UK)| n.|米吐尔，对甲氨基苯酚硫酸盐|A photographic developing agent, chemically p-methylaminophenol sulfate, usually used in combination with other developing agents.|Metol is a common ingredient in black and white photographic developers. 米吐尔是黑白照片显影剂中的常见成分。|The photographer mixed metol and hydroquinone to create a fine-grain developer. 摄影师将米吐尔和对苯二酚混合，以制成细颗粒显影剂。|Synonyms: Elon, Rhodol|Antonyms: N/A|noun
