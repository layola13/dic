cleanability:/ˌkliːnəˈbɪləti/
n.
可清洁性，清洁的容易程度
The ease with which a material or surface can be cleaned.
This new countertop material boasts excellent cleanability.  这种新的台面材料拥有极佳的清洁性能。
The cleanability of the bathroom tiles is a major selling point. 浴室瓷砖的易清洁性是一个主要的卖点。
cleanableness, washability
uncleanability, difficulty of cleaning
noun
