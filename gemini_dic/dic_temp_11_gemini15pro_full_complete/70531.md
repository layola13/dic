filibuster:/ˈfɪlɪbʌstər/| n. v.| 阻挠议事，长篇演说|A prolonged speech or series of speeches made to delay action on a bill or other measure in a legislative assembly.| The senator used a filibuster to prevent a vote on the bill.  参议员用冗长演说来阻止对该法案的投票。|Synonyms: stonewall, obstruct, delay|Antonyms: expedite, facilitate|noun, verb

filibuster:/ˈfɪlɪbʌstər/| v.| 阻挠议事|To obstruct or delay legislative action by making long speeches.| The opposition party filibustered the bill for weeks. 反对党用冗长演说阻挠了该法案数周。|Synonyms: stonewall, obstruct, delay|Antonyms: expedite, facilitate|verb
