outcatching: /ˌaʊtˈkætʃɪŋ/
verb (present participle of outcatch)
胜过捕捞；比…捕捞得更多
The act of catching more than someone or something else.
The experienced fisherman was outcatching his younger apprentice.  经验丰富的渔民比他年轻的学徒捕的鱼多。
The new fishing net was outcatching the old one by a significant margin.  新的渔网比旧渔网捕捞得多得多。
近义词: outfishing, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: being outfished, falling behind
词性: Verb (present participle)
