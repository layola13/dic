misemployed:/ˌmɪsɪmˈplɔɪd/
adjective
中文翻译：未充分就业的；大材小用的；用非所长的
英文解析：Employed in a job that does not make full use of one's skills and abilities.
例句：Many graduates are misemployed in low-skilled jobs. 许多毕业生都大材小用，从事着低技能的工作。

例句：He felt misemployed in his current role, yearning for something more challenging.  他觉得自己目前的工作大材小用，渴望更有挑战性的工作。

近义词：underemployed, underutilized
反义词：well-suited, appropriately employed
词性：adjective
