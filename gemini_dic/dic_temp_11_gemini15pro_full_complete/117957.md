He's constantly memeing on social media. 他 постоянно создаёт мемы в социальных сетях. | 近义词：sharing memes, creating memes, spreading memes | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) avoiding social media, abstaining from online culture | 词性: noun (gerund)
