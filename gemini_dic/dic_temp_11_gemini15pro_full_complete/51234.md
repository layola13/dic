destroys:/dɪˈstrɔɪz/
verb
摧毁，毁灭
puts an end to the existence of something by damaging or attacking it
The fire destroyed the building. 大火摧毁了这栋建筑。
annihilates, ruins, demolishes
creates, builds, preserves
