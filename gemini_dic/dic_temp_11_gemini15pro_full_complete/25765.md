burden:/ˈbɜːrdn/ (US) /ˈbɜːdən/ (UK)| n. vt.|负担，重担；负荷；责任；|Something that is carried; a load.  A duty, responsibility, or something that causes worry or suffering.|The donkey struggled under its heavy burden. 驴子在重负下挣扎。|近义词：load, weight, responsibility, obligation|反义词:relief, advantage|noun
burden:/ˈbɜːrdn/ (US) /ˈbɜːdən/ (UK)| vt.|使负重担；加重压于；烦扰；|To load heavily; weigh down. To trouble or annoy.|He was burdened with heavy debts. 他背负着沉重的债务。|近义词：load, encumber, oppress, tax|反义词:unburden, relieve|verb
