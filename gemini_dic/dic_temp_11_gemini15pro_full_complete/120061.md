millstreams:/ˈmɪlˌstriːmz/
n.
磨坊溪流；小河
Small streams, often ones that power a mill.
The gentle murmur of the millstreams lulled her to sleep.  轻轻的磨坊溪流声使她昏昏欲睡。
The millstreams flowed through the valley. 磨坊溪流流经山谷。
近义词:brooks, creeks, streams
反义词:rivers (although not a perfect opposite, it represents a larger body of water)
词性:noun

millstream: /ˈmɪlˌstriːm/
n.
磨坊水流
A stream of water that drives a mill wheel.
The millstream provided power for the old grist mill. 这条磨坊水流为古老的磨粉机提供动力。
The millstream was diverted to irrigate the fields. 磨坊水流被改道用于灌溉田地。
近义词:raceway (specifically for mills)
反义词: (no direct opposite)
词性:noun


词源:
From "mill" (Old English *mylen < Latin molina "mill") + "stream" (Old English stream).
