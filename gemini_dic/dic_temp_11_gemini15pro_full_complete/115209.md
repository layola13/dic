manifester:/ˈmænɪˌfɛstər/| n.| 表现者，显化者，表明者 |Someone or something that manifests something.  A person who practices the law of attraction and believes in manifesting their desires into reality.| She considers herself a manifester, believing she can attract anything she desires. 她认为自己是一个显化者，相信自己可以吸引任何她想要的东西。| synonyms: exhibitor, displayer, revealer, demonstrator | antonyms: concealer, hider, suppressor | noun
