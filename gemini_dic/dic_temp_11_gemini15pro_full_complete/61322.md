ambassador: /æmˈbæsədər/ | n. | 大使；代表；使节 | A diplomat of the highest rank, sent by one country as its long-term representative to another.  A person who acts as a representative or promoter of a specified activity. | The US ambassador to the UN gave a speech. 美国驻联合国大使发表了讲话。 |  The band chose him as their ambassador to promote their new album.  乐队选择他作为他们的代表来宣传他们的新专辑。| synonyms: envoy, diplomat, emissary, legate, delegate, representative, agent | antonyms:  None (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite.) | noun
