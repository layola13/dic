buoyancy:/ˈbɔɪənsi/ | n. | 浮力，轻快，乐观 | The ability to float in water or other fluid; cheerful optimism; the upward force that keeps things afloat.  | The life jacket provided the necessary buoyancy to keep him afloat. 救生衣提供了必要的浮力，让他能浮在水面上。 | Synonyms: lightness, floatability, cheerfulness, optimism | Antonyms: heaviness, negativity, pessimism | noun

buoyant:/ˈbɔɪənt/ | adj. | 有浮力的，轻快的，乐观的 | Able to float easily; cheerful and optimistic. |  He felt buoyant and hopeful about the future. 他对未来感到乐观和充满希望。 | Synonyms:  light, floating, cheerful, optimistic | Antonyms: heavy, sinking, gloomy, pessimistic | adjective
