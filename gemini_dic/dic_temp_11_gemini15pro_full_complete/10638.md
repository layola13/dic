appropriament: /əˈproʊpriəm(ə)nt/ | n. |拨款；专用；侵占|The appropriament of funds for the new project was approved.  这个新项目的资金拨款已获批准。| An appropriament of land for a new park.  将一块土地划拨用作新公园。|  例句：The government approved the appropriament of funds for disaster relief. 政府批准了用于救灾的资金拨款。  The artist's appropriament of another's work sparked controversy.  这位艺术家挪用他人作品的行为引发了争议。 | 近义词：allocation, appropriation, seizure | 反义词: restitution, return | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Middle English *appropriement, from Anglo-Norman appropriement, from approprier "to appropriate," ultimately from Latin appropriare "to make one's own," from ad- "to" + proprius "one's own."
