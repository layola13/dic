overequipped:/ˌoʊvərɪˈkwɪpt/
adjective
装备过度的，配备过剩的
Having more equipment than is necessary or appropriate.
The expedition was overequipped, carrying far too much gear for the short trek. 探险队装备过度，为这次短途跋涉携带了太多的装备。
The modern kitchen is often overequipped with gadgets that are rarely used. 现代厨房通常配备过多的很少使用的器具。
近义词: oversupplied, overloaded
反义词: underequipped, ill-equipped
词性: adjective

词源:
over- (prefix) + equipped (past participle of equip)
"over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across."
"equip" comes from Old French "equiper," meaning "to fit out a ship," from "esquip," meaning "ship."
