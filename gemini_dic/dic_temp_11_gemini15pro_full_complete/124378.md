motivelessnesses:/ˈmoʊtɪvləsnəsɪz/| n. |无动机；缺乏动力|The motivelessnesses of the crime baffled the investigators.  这种罪行的无动机性使调查人员感到困惑。|例句：The apparent motivelessnesses of the attack shocked the community.  这次袭击明显的无动机性震惊了整个社区。

The sheer motivelessnesses of the vandalism left residents feeling uneasy.  纯粹的无动机破坏行为让居民感到不安。|近义词：lack of motivation, apathy, listlessness|反义词：motivation, drive, ambition|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From motiveless + -ness.  Motiveless is from motive + -less.  Motive comes from Old French motif, ultimately from Latin motus "motion."
