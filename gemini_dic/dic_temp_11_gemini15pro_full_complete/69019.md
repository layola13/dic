fattier:/ˈfætiər/| adj.|更肥的，脂肪更多的|Comparative form of "fatty." Containing a higher proportion of fat.|The steak was much fattier than I expected. 这块牛排比我预期的要肥得多。|近义词：richer, oilier, greasier|反义词:leaner, skinnier|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *fætt (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *faitaz, from Proto-Indo-European *peyH-. Cognate with Old Frisian fett, Old Saxon fat, Old High German feizit, Old Norse feitr, Gothic 𐍆𐌰𐌹𐍄𐍃 (faits).
