apoplectical:/ˌæpəˈplɛktɪkəl/| adj.|中风的；极度愤怒的；狂怒的|Overcome with or exhibiting apoplexy; extremely angry; furious.|例句：He was apoplectical with rage when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后勃然大怒。

例句：Her father was apoplectical when she announced she was eloping. 当她宣布要私奔时，她的父亲气得暴跳如雷。|近义词：furious, enraged, livid, incensed|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *apoplecticus, from Greek apoplēktikos, from apoplēssein “disable by a stroke."  The original meaning referred to a medical condition, a stroke.  The figurative meaning of "extremely angry" developed later, based on the symptoms of apoplexy, such as loss of speech and uncontrollable movements.
