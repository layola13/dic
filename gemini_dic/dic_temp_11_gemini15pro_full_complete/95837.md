incorpse: /ɪnˈkɔːrps/ (UK) /ɪnˈkɔːrp/ (US) | vt. | 包含，纳入，合并 | To include or integrate something into something else, especially as a constituent part. |  The new car design incorporates several innovative safety features. 这款新车的设计包含了几项创新的安全特性。

The committee will incorporate your suggestions into the final report. 委员会会将你的建议纳入最终报告。 | 近义词: integrate, include, embody, assimilate, combine | 反义词: exclude, separate, remove | 词性: 动词 (verb)

词源: From Late Latin incorporare "to embody," literally "to form into a body," from in- "into, in" + corporare "to form into a body," from corpus "body."
