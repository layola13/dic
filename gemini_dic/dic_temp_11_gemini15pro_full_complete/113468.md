lyraid:/ˈlaɪrɪd/ /ˈlɪərɪd/| n.|天琴座流星雨；天琴座流星|A meteor shower that occurs annually in late April.  一年一度发生在四月下旬的流星雨。|The Lyrids are usually a modest shower. 天琴座流星雨通常规模不大。|Synonyms: meteor shower, shooting star|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
