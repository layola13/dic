fluorescent:/flʊəˈrɛsənt/ /flɔːˈresnt/
adjective
荧光的；发荧光的
Emitting light during exposure to radiation from an external source, such as ultraviolet light.
The fluorescent lights buzzed overhead. 荧光灯在头顶嗡嗡作响。

fluorescent:/flʊəˈrɛsənt/ /flɔːˈresnt/
adjective
（颜色）鲜艳的，耀眼的
Vivid or glowing.
She wore a fluorescent pink top. 她穿着一件鲜艳的粉红色上衣。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *florescent- (stem of *florescere "begin to blossom, bloom," from florere "to bloom, blossom, flourish," from flos "flower") + -ent.

SYNONYMS->
bright, vivid, glowing, luminous, radiant, brilliant

ANTONYMS->
dull, dark, dim
