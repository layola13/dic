marshland:/ˈmɑːrʃlænd/ /ˈmɑːrʃˌlænd/| n.|沼泽地；湿地|An area of soft, wet land.  A tract of low wetland, periodically flooded.|例句：The marshland is home to a variety of birds. 这片沼泽地是各种鸟类的家园。

例句：The developers plan to drain the marshland and build houses. 开发商计划抽干沼泽地并建造房屋。|近义词：swamp, bog, fen, wetland|反义词:dryland|词性:noun
