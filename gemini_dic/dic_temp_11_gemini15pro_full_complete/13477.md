atrophy:/ˈætrəfi/ (US), /ˈætrəˌfiː/ (UK)| n. v.| 萎缩，衰退|A gradual decline in effectiveness or vigor due to disuse or neglect.  The process of atrophying.|

例句：
The muscles in his leg atrophied after he was in a cast for several months.
他腿上的肌肉在他打了好几个月的石膏后萎缩了。

Her artistic skills atrophied from lack of practice.
由于缺乏练习，她的艺术技能退化了。


近义词：degeneration, deterioration, decline, wasting, withering|
反义词：growth, development, strengthening|
词性: noun, verb


atrophy:/ˈætrəfi/ (US), /ˈætrəˌfiː/ (UK)| v.| 萎缩，衰退|To gradually decline in effectiveness or vigor due to disuse or neglect. To undergo the process of atrophying.|

例句：
Without regular exercise, your muscles will atrophy.
如果不定期锻炼，你的肌肉会萎缩。

His social skills have atrophied since he started working from home.
自从他开始在家工作以来，他的社交技能已经退化了。


近义词：degenerate, deteriorate, decline, waste away, wither|
反义词：grow, develop, strengthen|
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *atrophie*, from Late Latin *atrophia*, from Greek *atrophia* "a wasting away," from *atrophos* "without nourishment," from *a-* "without" + *trophe* "nourishment."
