drowsed: /draʊzd/ | adj. |  昏昏欲睡的；困倦的 | Feeling sleepy and lethargic. |  I felt drowsed after taking the medication.  服药后我感到昏昏欲睡。 | sleepy, lethargic, drowsy | alert, awake, energetic | adjective

drowse: /draʊz/ | v. | 打瞌睡；昏昏欲睡 | To sleep lightly or be in a state between sleeping and waking. | The warm sun drowsed me into a peaceful sleep. 温暖的阳光让我昏昏欲睡，进入了一个平静的睡眠。 | doze, nap, slumber | awaken, energize | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drowsen, from Old English *drūsian, of uncertain origin.  Related to Old Norse drȳsja ("to sink, droop").
