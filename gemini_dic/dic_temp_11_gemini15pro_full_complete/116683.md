maturational:/ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃənəl/
adjective
成熟的，发育的
Relating to the process of becoming mature; developmental.  Pertaining to maturation.
The child's maturational development is progressing as expected.  这孩子的成熟发育正在按预期进行。
Her maturational stage allows her to understand complex concepts. 她的成熟阶段使她能够理解复杂的概念。
developmental,  evolutionary,  growth-related
regressive, undeveloped
