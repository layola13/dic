nonowner:/ˌnɒnˈəʊnə(r)/ | n. |非业主，非物主|A person who does not own something, especially a car or property.|例句：The insurance policy covers nonowners of the vehicle as well.  该保险单也涵盖车辆的非车主。

例句：Many apartment buildings have parking spaces designated for nonowners. 许多公寓楼都有指定给非业主的停车位。|近义词：renter, tenant, lessee|反义词：owner, proprietor|词性: 名词
