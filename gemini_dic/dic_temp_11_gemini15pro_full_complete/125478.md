multipotent:/ˌmʌltiˈpoʊtnt/
adjective
多能的
Having the capacity to develop into more than one cell type, but more limited than pluripotent.
Bone marrow contains multipotent stem cells that can differentiate into various blood cells.  骨髓包含可以分化成各种血细胞的多能干细胞。
Multipotent stem cells offer promising therapeutic potential for various diseases. 多能干细胞为各种疾病提供了有希望的治疗潜力。
近义词: pluripotent (more general), totipotent (most general)
反义词: unipotent
词性: adjective
