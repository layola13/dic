meds:/mɛdz/
n.
药物；药；药品 (Informal abbreviation of medications)
She's on a lot of meds for her condition. 她因为病情服用很多药物。
近义词: medicine, medication, drugs, pharmaceuticals
反义词: (No direct antonym for medications, but one could consider "remedies" as a somewhat contrasting term, as they often refer to natural treatments instead of pharmaceuticals.)
词性: noun (plural)
