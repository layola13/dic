bonhomie:/ˌbɒnəˈmiː/ (US) /ˌbɒnəˈmiː/ (UK)| n.|友好，和睦，亲切|A feeling of friendliness and good cheer; geniality.  A pleasant and affable disposition.|例句：The holiday season was filled with bonhomie.  节日充满了和睦友好的气氛。|例句：His bonhomie made him a popular host. 他的热情好客使他成为受欢迎的主人。|近义词：geniality, affability, cordiality, amiability|反义词：hostility, animosity, coldness|词性:名词

词源: French *bonhomie*, from *bonhomme* "good fellow," from *bon* "good" + *homme* "man."
