hyperdivision:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɪvɪʒn/ | n. | 超分割 | The act of dividing something into numerous parts, often excessively or beyond what is necessary.  It often implies a complex or overly intricate division.  | The hyperdivision of the company into multiple small subsidiaries made management incredibly difficult. 公司被超分割成许多小型的子公司，使得管理变得异常困难。

The hyperdivision of responsibilities created confusion and inefficiency. 责任的过度划分造成了混乱和低效。 | 近义词：subdivision, fragmentation, partitioning | 反义词：consolidation, unification | 词性: 名词

词源: "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, excessive") + "division" (Latin, "divisio," from "dividere," meaning "to divide").
