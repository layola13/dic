duddery:/ˈdʌdəri/
noun
破烂衣服；破旧物品；无价值的东西
rags; shabby belongings; worthless stuff
The old man's attic was filled with a duddery of forgotten items. 那个老人的阁楼里堆满了被遗忘的破烂物品。
synonyms: junk, rubbish, clutter, debris
antonyms: valuables, treasures
n.

duddery:/ˈdʌdəri/
adjective
破旧的；无价值的
shabby; worthless
He wore duddery clothes. 他穿着破旧的衣服。
synonyms: shabby, ragged, tattered, worn-out
antonyms: pristine, new, valuable
