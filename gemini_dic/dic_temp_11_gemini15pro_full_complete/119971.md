millepede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ (US), /ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ (UK)| n. |千足虫，马陆|A long, thin creature with many legs, typically having two pairs of legs on each body segment.|例句：I saw a millepede crawling across the garden path. 我看到一只千足虫爬过花园小路。|例句：The millepede curled up into a tight spiral when I touched it. 当我碰到它时，千足虫蜷缩成一个紧密的螺旋形。|近义词：millipede (variant spelling), polypod|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *millepeda, from Latin mille "thousand" + pes "foot."
