noncontention:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛnʃən/ | n. |无争议；不争论；放弃竞争|Absence of contention or dispute; agreement.|The election was decided by noncontention, as the other candidate withdrew.|Synonyms: agreement, accord, harmony, unanimity|Antonyms: contention, dispute, disagreement, conflict|noun

noncontentious:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛnʃəs/ | adj. |无争议的；不争论的；和平的|Not contentious; not involving or likely to cause disagreement or argument.|The meeting was surprisingly noncontentious, with everyone agreeing on the main points.|Synonyms: amicable, peaceful, harmonious, agreeable|Antonyms: contentious, controversial, disputed, argumentative|adjective
