neckplate:/ˈnɛkpleɪt/| n.|琴颈板，护板|A plate of metal or other material attached to the neck of a stringed instrument, especially a guitar or banjo, often for decorative or protective purposes. |The neckplate on his vintage guitar was beautifully engraved. 他的老式吉他的琴颈板雕刻精美。|The guitar's neckplate was loose and needed tightening. 吉他的琴颈板松了，需要拧紧。|近义词:fingerboard overlay, scratchplate (when referring to the protective function)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
