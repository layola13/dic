iatrogenic:/ˌaɪətroʊˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |医源性的；医疗引起的| Relating to illness caused by medical examination or treatment. | The patient experienced iatrogenic complications due to the medication.  病人由于药物治疗而经历了医源性并发症。| Synonym: doctor-induced, medically induced | Antonym: natural, spontaneous | adjective

iatrogenesis:/ˌaɪətroʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. |医源病；医源性并发症| The production of a disease or disorder by medical treatment. | The study investigated the prevalence of iatrogenesis in hospitalized patients. 这项研究调查了住院病人医源病的发生率。| Synonym: medical complication, adverse effect | Antonym: natural healing, spontaneous recovery | noun
