ostracizable:/ˈɒstrəsaɪzəbl/ /ˈɑːstrəsaɪzəbl/
adjective
可放逐的，可排斥的
Capable of being ostracized; subject to ostracism.  Someone or something ostracizable is likely to be excluded or banished from a group or society.
His controversial views made him ostracizable in the community.  他备受争议的观点使他在社区中被排斥。
The unpopular politician was ostracizable due to his corrupt practices.  这位不受欢迎的政客由于腐败行为而被排斥。
近义词: excludable, banishable, shutable out
反义词: includable, acceptable, welcome
词性: adjective
词源: From ostracize + -able.  Ostracize comes from Greek ostrakizein, from ostrakon 'potsherd, tile' (on which votes were written in ancient Athens).
