epaulement:/eɪˈpɔːlmɑːn/ | n. | 肩垫，肩部支撑; (芭蕾舞) 肩膀的姿势 | A position in ballet in which the dancer stands at an oblique angle to the audience, head turned towards one shoulder. The epaulement creates a graceful, elegant line and adds dimension to the dancer's posture.  | The dancer's graceful epaulement enhanced the beauty of the arabesque.  这位舞者的优雅肩姿增强了阿拉贝斯克舞姿的美感。 | Synonyms: posture, pose, bearing | Antonyms: slouch | noun
