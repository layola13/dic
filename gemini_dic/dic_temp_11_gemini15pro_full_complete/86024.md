healful:/ˈhiːlfʊl/| adj.|有益健康的，促进健康的|Conducive to good health; wholesome.|例句：A healthful diet is important for maintaining good health.  有益健康的饮食对保持健康很重要。

例句：The doctor recommended a healthful lifestyle. 医生建议健康的生活方式。|近义词：healthy, wholesome, salutary, beneficial|反义词：unhealthy, harmful, detrimental|词性:adjective
词源:From Middle English *helful, from Old English hǣlful (“healthy, sound, whole”), equivalent to heal +‎ -ful. Cognate with Dutch heilzaam (“wholesome”), German heilvoll (“wholesome”).
