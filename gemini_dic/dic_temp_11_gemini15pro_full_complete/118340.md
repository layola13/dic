meridian: /məˈrɪdiən/ | n. adj.| 子午线，顶点，经线；最高点 | A circle of constant longitude passing through a given place on the earth's surface and the terrestrial poles. / The highest point or culmination of something. |  The Greenwich meridian is zero degrees longitude. 格林尼治子午线是零度经线。/  He was at the meridian of his power. 他正处于权力的顶峰。 | synonyms: culmination, peak, zenith, apex, acme | antonyms: nadir, bottom, low point | noun

meridian: /məˈrɪdiən/ | adj.| 最高点的，顶峰的，子午线的 | Of or relating to a meridian or the highest point. | The meridian sun was scorching hot.  正午的太阳 scorching hot. | synonyms: culminating, peak, zenithal | antonyms: nadir, bottom, low | adjective
