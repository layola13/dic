fagged:/fæɡd/
adjective
筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的
Exhausted, very tired.
例句：After a long day of hiking, I felt completely fagged.  在徒步旅行了一整天后，我感到筋疲力尽。
例句：The children were fagged out after their day at the amusement park. 孩子们在游乐园玩了一天后累坏了。
近义词：exhausted, tired, weary, worn out
反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性:adjective

词源:
From fag (v.) + -ed.  The verb "fag" originally meant "to work hard" or "to toil," and later developed the meaning of "to tire out."
