cookee:/ˈkʊki/| n.|新手，菜鸟；容易受骗的人|A cookee is an inexperienced person, especially one who is easily deceived or taken advantage of.  It can also refer to a helper, especially in a kitchen or on a ranch.|He's a cookee in the stock market, so he lost a lot of money. 他是股市新手，所以亏了很多钱。|近义词：newbie, beginner, novice, greenhorn, rookie, pushover, dupe|反义词：expert, veteran, professional|Noun
