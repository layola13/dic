glorifier:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪər/ | n. | 颂扬者，赞美者 | Someone or something that glorifies something or someone.  |  The poet was a glorifier of nature's beauty. 这位诗人是歌颂大自然之美的诗人。 |  Near synonyms: extoller, praiser, lauder, celebrant | Near antonyms: critic, detractor, denigrator | noun

glorify:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ/ | v. | 赞美，颂扬;美化，吹捧 | 1. To describe or represent something as admirable, especially excessively or undeservedly. 2. To give glory to; to honor or praise. |  1. The movie glorifies violence. 这部电影美化暴力。 2. They glorified their leader. 他们颂扬他们的领袖。 |  Near synonyms: extol, praise, laud, celebrate, exalt | Near antonyms: criticize, denigrate, belittle | verb
