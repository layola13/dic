allotropism:/əˈlɒtrəpɪzəm/| n. |同素异形体|The existence of two or more different physical forms of an element in the same physical state. | Diamond and graphite are allotropes of carbon. 钻石和石墨是碳的同素异形体。|Synonym: allotropy|Antonym: N/A|noun


allotropy:/əˈlɒtrəpi/| n. |同素异形|The existence of two or more different physical forms of an element in the same physical state. |The allotropy of carbon allows it to exist as both diamond and graphite. 碳的同素异形使其可以以金刚石和石墨的形式存在。|Synonym: allotropism|Antonym: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *allotropie*, from Ancient Greek ἄλλος (állos, “other”) + τρόπος (trópos, “manner, form”).
