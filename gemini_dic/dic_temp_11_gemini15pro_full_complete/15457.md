badaud:/bɑːˈdoʊ/| n.|闲逛者，游手好闲的人|A person who strolls about idly; a loiterer; an idler. |例句：The badauds gathered to watch the street performer. 闲逛者聚集起来观看街头艺人的表演。

例句：He spent his days as a badaud, simply wandering the streets of Paris. 他像个游手好闲的人一样，整天在巴黎街头闲逛。|近义词：loafer, idler, stroller, gazer, onlooker|反义词：worker, busybody|词性：名词
