daphne:/ˈdæfni/| n. |达芙妮，瑞香|A flowering shrub, of a genus that includes the spurge laurel.  A female given name, from Greek mythology.|例句：The daphne in the garden is in full bloom. 花园里的瑞香盛开了。

例句：Daphne is a beautiful name. 达芙妮是个好听的名字。|近义词：laurel, shrub (for the plant),  Chloe,  Corinne (for the name)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *daphnē*, from Ancient Greek δάφνη (*dáphnē*) meaning "laurel", "bay tree". In Greek mythology, Daphne was a nymph who was transformed into a laurel tree to escape Apollo's advances.
