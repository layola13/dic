formativenesses:/ˌfɔːrmətɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|形成性，塑造性|The quality or state of being formative; the degree to which something shapes or influences something else.|The formativenesses of early childhood experiences are crucial for personality development. 早期童年经历的塑造性对于人格发展至关重要。|近义词：influence, impact, shaping, molding|反义词：inertness, stagnation|词性: noun


Etymology:
From formative + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Formative" comes from the Late Latin *formatīvus, from Latin formatus (past participle of formāre, "to form"). "-ness" is a native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality. "-es" is a plural suffix.
