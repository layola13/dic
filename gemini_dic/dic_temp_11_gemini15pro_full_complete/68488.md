famines:/ˈfæmɪnz/
n.
饥荒（复数）
extreme scarcity of food
The region has suffered several famines in the last century.  该地区在上个世纪遭受了几次饥荒。

近义词:starvation, hunger, scarcity
反义词:abundance, plenty
词性:名词


词源: From Old French famine ("hunger, starvation"), from Latin famēs ("hunger"), related to famulus ("servant, slave")
