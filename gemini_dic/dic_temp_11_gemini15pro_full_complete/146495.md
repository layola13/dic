overgarnishes:/ˌoʊvərˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪz/
| n. | 过度装饰；过多的装饰物 | Excessive garnishes on a dish, often to the point of detracting from the food itself. | The overgarnishes made the plate look cluttered and hid the beautiful presentation of the main course. 这过多的装饰使盘子看起来很杂乱，遮盖了主菜的精美外观。 |近义词：excessive decoration, too many garnishes |反义词:minimal decoration, simple garnish |词性:名词


Etymology:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "garnish" (Old French garnir, from Frankish *warnjan "to provide, furnish, defend")
