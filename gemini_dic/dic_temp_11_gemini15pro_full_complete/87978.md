high-hatting:/ˌhaɪˈhætɪŋ/ | n. | 傲慢对待；摆架子；轻蔑 | Treating someone with disdain or condescension; acting superior. |  He was high-hatting his old friends after he became famous. 他成名后对老朋友摆架子。| synonyms: condescending, patronizing, snubbing, snobbish | antonyms:  humble, respectful, approachable | noun

high-hat:/ˈhaɪˌhæt/ | v. | 傲慢对待；摆架子；轻蔑 | To treat someone with disdain or condescension; to act superior. | She high-hatted the waiter because he brought the wrong order.  因为服务员上错了菜，她对他摆架子。| synonyms: condescend, patronize, snub | antonyms: respect, admire | verb
