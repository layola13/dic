disappointed:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪd/ | adj. | 失望的，沮丧的 | Feeling sad or displeased because someone or something has failed to fulfill one's hopes or expectations. | I was disappointed that I didn't get the job. 我很失望没有得到那份工作。|  近义词: disheartened, discouraged, let down | 反义词: pleased, happy, satisfied | 词性:形容词

disappointed:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪd/ | v. | 使失望 |Past participle of disappoint. | She was disappointed by the movie's ending. 她对电影的结局感到失望。|  近义词: saddened, disheartened | 反义词: pleased, delighted | 词性:动词

词源: From the Old French *desapointer, from des- "reversal of action" + apointer "to appoint, agree upon," ultimately from Latin appunctare "to add a point to."  The original meaning was "to undo an appointment."
