irrespirable:/ˌɪrɪˈspaɪrəbl/
adjective
不可呼吸的，窒息的
Not breathable; unfit for breathing.
The air in the mine shaft was irrespirable after the explosion.  矿井爆炸后，井下的空气无法呼吸。
The fumes from the burning plastic were thick and irrespirable. 燃烧的塑料产生的浓烟又浓又令人窒息。
近义词:suffocating, unbreathable, stifling
反义词:breathable, respirable
词性:adjective
