catheter:/ˈkæθɪtər/ (US), /ˈkæθɪtə/ (UK)| n. |导管；插管|A thin tube inserted into a body cavity, duct, or vessel to permit injection or withdrawal of fluids or to keep a passage open.|The nurse inserted a catheter into the patient's bladder. 护士将导管插入病人的膀胱。|Synonyms: tube, cannula|Antonyms: N/A|noun


catheters:/ˈkæθɪtərz/ (US), /ˈkæθɪtəz/ (UK)| n. |导管（复数）|Plural form of catheter, referring to multiple thin tubes inserted into body cavities, ducts, or vessels.|The hospital ordered a new supply of catheters. 医院订购了一批新的导管。|Synonyms: tubes, cannulas|Antonyms: N/A|noun
