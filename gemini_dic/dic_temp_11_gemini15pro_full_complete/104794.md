kazus: /ˈkɑːzʊs/ | n. | 案例，讼案，(尤指)诉讼原因 |A case, especially one that is being investigated or is the subject of litigation. |The kazus of the lawsuit was a breach of contract.  该诉讼的案由是违反合同。| synonym: case, lawsuit, suit, action | antonym: acquittal, dismissal | noun

kazus: /ˈkɑːzʊs/ | n. | (引起战争或争端的)导火索，借口，起因 |  An event or situation which is used to justify a war or other action, especially one that is seen as not being a genuine cause.  |The assassination of the Archduke was the kazus belli for World War I.  刺杀大公是第一次世界大战的导火线。 | synonym: pretext, excuse, justification, rationale | antonym: reason, cause, truth | noun
