clubhouse:/ˈklʌbˌhaʊs/ (US), /ˈklʌbˌhaʊs/ (UK)| n.|会所；俱乐部；棚屋；（高尔夫球场的）俱乐部会所|A building occupied by a club or used for recreational activities, especially one associated with a sports club. It can also refer to a small simple building.|例句：The golf clubhouse offers stunning views of the course. 高尔夫俱乐部会所提供了球场的壮丽景色。

例句：The yacht club clubhouse was destroyed by the hurricane. 游艇俱乐部会所被飓风摧毁了。|近义词：club, building, facility, hall|反义词: None|词性: noun
