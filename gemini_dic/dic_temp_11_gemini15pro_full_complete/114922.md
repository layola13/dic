managee:/ˌmænəˈdʒiː/
noun
被管理者；受管理者
A person who is managed or supervised by someone else.
The managee was unhappy with the new company policy.  被管理者对公司的新政策不满意。
The manager held a meeting with all his managees. 经理和他所有的被管理者开了一个会。
synonyms: subordinate, employee, staff member
antonyms: manager, supervisor, boss
