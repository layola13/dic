ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *braveli*, from Old French *brave*, meaning "wild, courageous," of Germanic origin.  Related to Old High German *brāo*, meaning "proud, magnificent."  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "brave" into an adverb.
