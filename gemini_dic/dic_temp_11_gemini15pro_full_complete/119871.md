milieux:/miːˈljɜː(z)/ /miːˈljɜː/
n.
环境;圈子;社会背景
A person's social environment.  A milieu is a setting or surrounding, a person's social environment.
He grew up in a military milieu. 他生长在军事环境中。

近义词:environment, setting, surroundings, atmosphere, context, background, sphere, circle, world
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以根据具体语境选择) isolation, solitude

词性:noun (plural milieux or milieus)
词源:French, from Latin medius 'middle'.
