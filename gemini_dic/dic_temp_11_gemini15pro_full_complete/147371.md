overneglectfully: /ˌoʊvər nɪˈɡlɛktfəli/
adverb
过度疏忽地，极其忽略地
Excessively neglectfully; to a very great extent marked by neglect.
He overneglectfully left the children alone in the house. 他极其疏忽地把孩子们单独留在了家里。
The garden was overneglectfully maintained, with weeds growing everywhere. 花园疏于管理，杂草丛生。
Synonym: grossly neglectfully, extremely carelessly
Antonym: attentively, meticulously
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "neglect" (from Latin *neglectus*, past participle of *neglegere* "disregard, neglect") + "-ful" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs)
