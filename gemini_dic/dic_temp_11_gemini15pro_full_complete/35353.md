co-foundress:/ˌkoʊˈfaʊndrɪs/
n.
女性共同创始人
A woman who establishes something jointly with another or others, especially a business.
例句：The co-foundress of the company stepped down after ten years.  这家公司的女共同创始人十年后辞职了。

例句：She and her sister were co-foundresses of a successful charity. 她和她姐姐是一个成功慈善机构的共同创始人。

近义词：co-founder, co-creator
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：Noun
