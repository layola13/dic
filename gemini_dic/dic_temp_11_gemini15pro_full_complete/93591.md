imband:/ɪmˈbænd/ | v. | 编成带，组成乐队，联合 | To form into a band; to unite in a company. |  The musicians imbanded together to form a new orchestra.  音乐家们组成乐队，组建了一个新的管弦乐队。

The rebels imbanded together to overthrow the government. 叛军联合起来推翻政府。 | 近义词：unite, ally, join, league | 反义词：separate, divide, disband | 词性：动词

词源：From im- (in-) + band (a group).  "im-" 表示 "in" 或 "into"，"band" 指的是 "一群" 或 "带"。
