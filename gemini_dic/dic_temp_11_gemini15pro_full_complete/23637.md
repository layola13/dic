branchlike:/ˈbræntʃlaɪk/| adj.|树枝状的，像树枝的|Resembling a branch in form or arrangement. |The fungus had a branchlike structure. 这种真菌具有树枝状结构。|The lightning bolt split into branchlike patterns. 闪电分叉成树枝状。|近义词：branched, dendritic, ramified|反义词: unbranched, straight|词性: adjective

词源: branch (n.) + -like (suffix).  "Branch" comes from Old French "branche," ultimately from Vulgar Latin *branca. "-like" is a native English suffix meaning "resembling."
