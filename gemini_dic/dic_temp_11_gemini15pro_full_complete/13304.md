athirst:/əˈθɜːrst/| adj.|渴望的，口渴的|Feeling a strong desire or craving for something, especially for something non-physical, like knowledge or experience; also, literally thirsty.|例句：He was athirst for knowledge. 他渴望知识。

例句：The travelers were athirst in the desert. 旅行者在沙漠中非常口渴。|近义词：eager, keen, thirsty, craving, longing|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, satisfied, quenched|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *ofþyrst, from of- (intensive prefix) + þyrst (“thirst”). Cognate with Dutch ofdorst (“athirst”).
