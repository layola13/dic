hypnosis:/hɪpˈnoʊsɪs/ /hɪpˈnəʊsɪs/
noun
催眠；催眠状态
a state of human consciousness involving focused attention and reduced peripheral awareness characterized by an enhanced capacity for response to suggestion.
The therapist used hypnosis to help the patient quit smoking.  治疗师用催眠帮助病人戒烟。

hypnosis:/hɪpˈnoʊsɪs/ /hɪpˈnəʊsɪs/
noun
催眠术
the induction of a state of hypnosis.
He learned self-hypnosis to manage his stress. 他学习了自我催眠来控制压力。

近义词:mesmerism, trance
反义词:wakefulness, alertness
词性:noun
