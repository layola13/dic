articles:/ˈɑːrtɪkəlz/ | n. | 冠词；文章；物品；条款 | Articles are words that define a noun as specific or nonspecific. Articles can also refer to written compositions published in print or online, or to physical objects. Finally, articles can refer to distinct clauses in a contract or treaty. |  例句：The articles in this magazine are very interesting. 这本杂志上的文章很有趣。

例句：Please read the articles of the agreement carefully. 请仔细阅读协议的条款。 | 近义词：items, goods, pieces, clauses, stories, reports | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
