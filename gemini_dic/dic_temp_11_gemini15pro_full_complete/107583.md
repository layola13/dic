landownings:/ˈlændˌoʊnɪŋz/
n.
土地所有权，地产
A person's landownings refer to all the land that they own.
The family's vast landownings stretched for miles.  这个家族广阔的土地绵延数英里。
近义词: estates, properties, holdings
反义词: None (lack of landownership is not a single word)
词性: noun (plural)


landowning:/ˈlændˌoʊnɪŋ/
adj.
拥有土地的，地主的
Describes someone who owns land, often a significant amount.
The landowning class held considerable power. 拥有土地的阶级掌握着相当大的权力。
近义词: landed
反义词: landless
词性: adjective


词源:  "land" + "owning" (present participle of "own"). "Land" comes from Old English "land," and "own" comes from Old English "āgan."
