assiduously:/əˈsɪdʒuəsli/| adv.|勤奋地，刻苦地|Showing great care, attention, and effort.|She worked assiduously on her project, determined to finish it before the deadline. 她勤奋地做着她的项目，决心在截止日期前完成。|近义词：diligently, industriously, painstakingly|反义词：lazily, carelessly, negligently|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Latin *assiduus* ("sitting near, attending upon, constant, incessant"), from *ad-* ("to, at") + *sidere* ("to sit").  The English word "assiduous" (adjective form) appeared in the mid-16th century. The adverbial form "assiduously" followed shortly after.
