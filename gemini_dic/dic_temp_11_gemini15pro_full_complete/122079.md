misthrew:/ˌmɪsˈθruː/
verb
投掷失误，扔偏，误掷
threw something inaccurately or without the intended result.
He misthrew the ball and it went out of bounds. 他投球失误，球出界了。
近义词: mishandled, bungled, flubbed
反义词: threw accurately, succeeded
词性: verb
