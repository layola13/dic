materiated:/məˈtɪəriˌeɪtɪd/
verb
实现化; 实体化
having become materialized; having assumed material form
例句：The ghost materialized before our eyes.  鬼魂出现在我们眼前。

例句：His hopes materialized when he won the lottery.  他中彩票后，他的希望实现了。

近义词：appear, emerge, manifest, solidify
反义词：dematerialize, disappear, vanish
词性:v.
