nonranging:/ˈnɒnˈreɪnʤɪŋ/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˈreɪndʒɪŋ/
| adj. |非漫游的，不四处觅食的|Not ranging or roaming; confined to a particular area or habitat.  Not seeking food over a wide area.|The nonranging animals were easier to observe. 这些不四处觅食的动物更容易被观察。|近义词：sedentary, restricted, localized|反义词：ranging, roaming, migratory|词性:形容词


nonranging:/ˈnɒnˈreɪnʤɪŋ/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˈreɪndʒɪŋ/
| n. |(罕用) 非漫游的动物|An animal that doesn't range or roam.|The study focused on the behavior of nonranging herbivores.  这项研究的重点是非漫游食草动物的行为。|近义词：sedentary animal|反义词：ranging animal, roaming animal|词性:名词
