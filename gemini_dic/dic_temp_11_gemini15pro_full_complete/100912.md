intumulated: /ɪnˈtjuːmjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. |  肿胀的，膨胀的 |  Swollen, enlarged, distended, especially as a result of an accumulation of fluid.  Derived from Latin "intumescere" (to swell up).  | The patient's intumulated ankle indicated a serious sprain. 病人的脚踝肿胀，表明扭伤很严重。 | The intumulated lymph nodes were a sign of infection. 肿胀的淋巴结是感染的迹象。 | Synonyms: swollen, distended, bloated, puffed up | Antonyms: deflated, shrunken, contracted | Word Type: adjective
