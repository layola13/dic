auriferous:/ɔːˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
含金的；产金的
Containing gold; yielding gold.
The auriferous quartz veins were a source of great wealth for the miners. 这些含金的石英脉是矿工们巨大财富的来源。
auriferous rocks are found in this region. 在这个地区发现了含金的岩石。
Synonyms: gold-bearing, gold-producing, gold-rich
Antonyms: barren, infertile, unproductive
Etymology: From Latin *aurifer* (gold-bearing), from *aurum* (gold) + *-fer* (bearing).
词性: adjective
