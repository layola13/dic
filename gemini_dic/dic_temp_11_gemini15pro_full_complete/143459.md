outfrown:/ˌaʊtˈfraʊn/
verb
战胜；皱眉比过
to frown longer or more severely than
He outfrowned his opponent, refusing to be intimidated. 他比对手皱眉更久，拒绝被吓倒。
She outfrowned the storm clouds, determined to enjoy her day. 她比乌云皱眉更厉害，决心享受她的一天。
近义词: outstare, outface
反义词: submit, yield
词性: verb
