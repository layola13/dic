festivous: /ˈfɛstɪvəs/ | adj. | 欢宴的；节日的；欢乐的 | Relating to or suggestive of a festival, especially Christmas.  Characterized by joyful celebration; merry. |  例句：The hall was decorated in a festive manner for the Christmas party.  大厅为了圣诞派对被装饰得很有节日气氛。

例句：Everyone was in a festive mood, singing carols and exchanging gifts.  每个人都沉浸在节日的气氛中，唱着颂歌，交换着礼物。


| 近义词：jolly, merry, cheerful, celebratory, convivial | 反义词：somber, gloomy, depressing, mournful | 词性：adjective
