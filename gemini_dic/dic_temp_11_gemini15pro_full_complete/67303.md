exsuccous:/ɛkˈsʌkəs/
adjective
无汁的; 干燥的
Lacking sap or juice; dry.
The exsuccous leaves crumbled easily in my hand.  这些干枯的叶子在我手里很容易就碎了。
近义词: dry, desiccated, arid
反义词: succulent, juicy, moist
词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *exsuccus*, from ex- 'out of' + succus 'juice'.
