anteversion:/ˌæntiˈvɜːrʒən/
noun
前倾，前移
The abnormal forward tilting or displacement of an organ, especially of the uterus.
例句：Uterine anteversion is a normal variation in the position of the uterus. 子宫前倾是一种子宫位置的正常变异。

例句：Anteversion of the uterus can sometimes cause discomfort during pregnancy. 子宫前倾有时会在怀孕期间引起不适。
近义词：tilting, displacement
反义词：retroversion
词性:n.
