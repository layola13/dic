amphigenesis:/ˌæmfɪˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
双亲遗传，两性生殖
The process of reproduction involving the fusion of male and female gametes.
Amphigenesis is fundamental to the diversity of life on Earth.  两性生殖是地球生命多样性的基础。
Sexual reproduction, as opposed to asexual reproduction, involves amphigenesis.  有性生殖，与无性生殖相反，包含了两性生殖。
近义词：sexual reproduction, syngamy
反义词：asexual reproduction, agamogenesis
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek amphi- (both, on both sides) + genesis (origin, creation).
