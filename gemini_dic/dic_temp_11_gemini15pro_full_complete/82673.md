guesstimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmət/| n. | 估计；推测 | An estimate based on guesswork or incomplete information.  A rough calculation based on little or no data. |  We'll have to make a guesstimate of how many people will attend the event. 我们必须估计一下有多少人会参加这个活动。|  estimate, approximation, conjecture, supposition | precise calculation, accurate measurement | noun
guesstimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmət/| v. | 估计；推测 | To form an estimate based on guesswork or incomplete information. To make a rough calculation based on little or no data. |  I would guesstimate that the project will take about three months to complete. 我估计这个项目大约需要三个月才能完成。 | estimate, approximate, conjecture, surmise | calculate, measure | verb
