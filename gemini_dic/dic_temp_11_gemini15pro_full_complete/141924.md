ordinaries:/ˈɔːrdɪnɛriz/
n.|普通事物；常例；平常饭菜；普通股|Things that are common, usual, or not special.  In finance, it refers to ordinary shares.|The restaurant serves good, wholesome ordinaries.|近义词:regulars, commonalities, commonplace, standard fare|反义词:rarities, exceptions, specialties|词性:名词


ordinaries:/ˈɔːrdɪnɛriz/
n.|普通股|Ordinary shares of a company.|He invested heavily in ordinaries.|近义词:common stock, equities|反义词:preferred stock, bonds|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ordinarie*, from Old French *ordinarie*, from Latin *ordinarius* (“arranged in order, regular, ordinary”), from *ordo* (“order, rank”).
