chiming-in:/ˈtʃaɪmɪŋ ɪn/| v.|插嘴，附和|The act of interjecting a remark or opinion into a conversation, often briefly and in agreement with what is being said.  Literally, like a chime ringing in time with other bells.|例句：He kept chiming in with his own anecdotes. 他不断插嘴讲他自己的轶事。

例句：She chimed in with a word of encouragement. 她插话说了一句鼓励的话。|近义词：interjecting, interrupting, butting in, adding, contributing|反义词:remaining silent, withholding comment|词性:动词(不及物动词)
