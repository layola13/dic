currently: /ˈkɜːrəntli/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrəntli/ (US) | adv. | 当前，目前 | at the present time; now |  例句：She is currently working on a new project. 她目前正在进行一个新项目。| 近义词：now, presently, at present, currently | 反义词：formerly, previously | 词性：副词

Etymology: From the late Middle English *current*, meaning "running," from Old French *curant*, present participle of *curre*, "to run," from Latin *currere*, "to run."  The adverbial suffix *-ly* was added later.

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I currently live in London. 我现在住在伦敦。
He currently works as a teacher. 他目前担任教师。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am currently reading a fascinating book. 我目前正在读一本引人入胜的书。
They are currently traveling through Europe. 他们目前正在环游欧洲。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have currently finished writing my thesis. 我目前已经完成了论文写作。
She has currently achieved all her goals for this year. 她目前已经实现了今年的所有目标。(This usage is slightly less common; "so far" or "already" would be more typical).

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have currently been working on this project for three months.  我目前已经在这个项目上工作了三个月。
He has currently been learning Spanish for a year. 他目前已经学习西班牙语一年了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He was currently living in Paris at the time.  他当时住在巴黎。 (Note: "at the time" is often used with "currently" in the past tense).
She was currently unemployed when she met him. 她遇到他时目前正处于失业状态。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was currently working on a report when the power went out. 我正在写报告时停电了。
They were currently discussing the issue when I entered the room. 我进房间时他们正在讨论这个问题。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had currently completed his studies when the offer came. 当机会来临时，他已经完成了学业。 (Again, "currently" feels slightly awkward here;  "already" or "by then" would be more natural)
She had currently reached the summit of her career. 她当时已经达到了事业的巅峰。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had currently been living in New York for five years when I decided to move. 我在纽约住了五年，然后决定搬家。
They had currently been negotiating the deal for several weeks. 他们已经就这笔交易谈判了几个星期。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will currently be living in Berlin next year.  明年我将住在柏林。(This usage is less common; simply "I will be living in Berlin next year" is preferred).
He will currently be working as a consultant. 他届时将担任顾问。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will currently be traveling through Asia this time next year. 明年这个时候我将正在亚洲旅行。(Again, using "currently" here is redundant. "This time next year I will be traveling through Asia" is more natural.)
They will currently be attending a conference in London. 他们届时将参加在伦敦举行的会议。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will currently have finished my degree. 到明年，我将已经完成了学位。 (Again, "currently" is redundant here. "By next year, I will have finished my degree" is preferred).
She will currently have lived in five different countries by the time she's 30. 到她30岁时，她将已经住在五个不同的国家。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will currently have been working here for two years. 到下个月，我将已经在这里工作两年了。(Avoid using "currently" here; it's redundant.)
He will currently have been studying French for five years by the time he graduates. 到他毕业时，他将已经学习法语五年了。

PHRASE->
(Currently does not form strong idiomatic phrases.  It is generally used on its own.)
