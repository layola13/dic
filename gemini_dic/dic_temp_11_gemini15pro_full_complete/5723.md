alveated:/ˈælviˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
蜂窝状的，多孔的
having small cavities or pits; resembling a honeycomb.
The fossilized coral presented an alveated surface. 这块珊瑚化石呈现出蜂窝状的表面。

近义词:honeycombed, pitted, porous, cellular
反义词:smooth, solid, compact
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *alveatus, from alveus (“cavity, hollow”).  Related to alveolus.
