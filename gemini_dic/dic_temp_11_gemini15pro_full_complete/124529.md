mountainless:/ˈmaʊntɪnləs/
adjective
无山的，没有山的
lacking mountains; having no mountains
The vast, mountainless plain stretched as far as the eye could see.  广阔无垠的平原一直延伸到视野的尽头。
The island nation is remarkably mountainless. 这个岛国非常平坦，没有山脉。
Synonyms: flat, level, plain, featureless
Antonyms: mountainous, hilly, rugged
adjective
