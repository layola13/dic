emblem:/ˈembləm/ (US), /ˈembləm/ (UK)| n.|象征; 徽章; 符号|A thing that represents or stands for something else, especially a material object representing something abstract.|The eagle is a national emblem of the United States.  鹰是美国的国家象征。

The dove is an emblem of peace. 鸽子是和平的象征。|symbol, badge, logo, insignia, crest|antonym (no single perfect antonym, depends on context: counter-symbol, opposing symbol)|noun
