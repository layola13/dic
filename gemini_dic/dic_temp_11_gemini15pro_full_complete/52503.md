differences:/ˈdɪfrənsɪz/| n. |差异，不同|The differences between the two cultures are significant. 这两种文化之间的差异很大。|例句：There are many differences between British and American English. 英式英语和美式英语之间有很多不同之处。|近义词：disparities, variations, distinctions|反义词：similarities, likenesses|noun


differences:/ˈdɪfrənsɪz/| n. |差别，差距|The difference in price is negligible. 价格上的差别可以忽略不计。|例句：The difference in their ages is only two years. 他们之间的年龄差距只有两岁。|近义词：gaps, discrepancies|反义词：equalities, similarities|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *difference*, *differens*, from Anglo-Norman *difference*, *diferense*, and Old French *difference*, from Latin *differentia* (“a difference, disagreement”), from *differēns* (“differing”), present participle of *differre* (“to differ, disagree”), from *dis-* (“apart, away”) + *ferre* (“to carry, bear”).
