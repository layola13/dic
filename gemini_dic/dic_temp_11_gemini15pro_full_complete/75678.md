fulfills:/fʊlˈfɪlz/
verb
实现，履行，完成
carries out a task or responsibility; satisfies a condition or requirement
The company fulfills its obligations to its employees.  这家公司履行了对员工的义务。
She fulfilled her dream of becoming a doctor. 她实现了成为一名医生的梦想。
satisfy, meet, achieve, accomplish
fail, neglect, default
