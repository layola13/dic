flotsam:/ˈflɒtsəm/ (UK), /ˈflɑːtsəm/ (US)| n. |漂浮物，废料；（尤指）海上漂流的货物残骸| wreckage of a ship or its cargo found floating on or washed up by the sea.  Anything floating that has been discarded or lost, often as a result of a shipwreck.| The beach was littered with flotsam and jetsam after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上到处都是漂浮物和垃圾。| 近义词：debris, wreckage, jetsam | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

flotson:/ˈflɒtsən/ (UK), /ˈflɑːtsən/ (US)| n. |（尤指）海上漂流的货物| goods from a shipwreck found floating on the sea. More generally, anything discarded and afloat.|  After the hurricane, flotson from the wrecked ships was scattered across the bay.  飓风过后，失事船只的漂浮货物散落在海湾各处。| 近义词：driftwood, wreckage, jetsam | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Anglo-Norman *floteson, from Old French flotaison "a floating," from floter "to float," from Latin fluctuare, frequentative of fluere "to flow."
