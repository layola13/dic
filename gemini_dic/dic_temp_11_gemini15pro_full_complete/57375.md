doyens:/ˈdɔɪənz/ | n. | 元老，院长，老前辈，权威 | A doyen is a senior member of a group, especially one who is highly respected. | The doyen of the diplomatic corps offered advice to the new ambassador.  外交使团的元老向新任大使提供了建议。| synonyms: veteran, elder, leader, authority | antonyms: novice, beginner, newcomer | noun

doyen:/ˈdɔɪən/ | n. | 元老，院长，老前辈，权威 |  A doyen is the most respected or prominent person in a particular field. |  She is considered a doyen of the fashion industry. 她被认为是时尚界的元老。| synonyms: expert, master, guru, luminary | antonyms: apprentice, student, beginner | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *doyen*, from Old French *deien*, from Late Latin *decanus*, meaning "leader of ten," ultimately from Latin *decem*, "ten." Originally referred to the head of a group of ten monks or soldiers.
