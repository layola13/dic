jat:/dʒæt/ | n. | jat人，jat族| A member of a traditionally agricultural caste in northern India and Pakistan. |  The jat farmers are known for their resilience. jat族的农民以其韧性而闻名。| synonyms: peasant, farmer | antonyms: urbanite, city-dweller | noun


Etymology: From Hindi जाट (jāṭ).  Ultimately from Sanskrit अर्जुन (arjuna), meaning "white," "clear," or "silver,"  possibly referring to their complexion or the color of the river near their original homeland.
