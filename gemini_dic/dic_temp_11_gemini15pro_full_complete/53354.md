| Example: She was a disadventurous eater, sticking to plain pasta and chicken. 她饮食上不爱冒险，只吃普通的意大利面和鸡肉。 | Synonym: unadventurous, cautious, timid, unenterprising | Antonym: adventurous, daring, bold, enterprising | word property: adjective
