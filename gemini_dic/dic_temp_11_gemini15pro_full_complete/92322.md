hypnotist:/ˈhɪpnətɪst/| n. |催眠师，催眠术士|A person who practices hypnotism.  A person who can induce hypnosis in others.|例句：The hypnotist put the subject into a deep trance. 催眠师使受试者进入深度恍惚状态。

例句：The hypnotist claimed he could cure her phobia. 催眠师声称他能治愈她的恐惧症。|近义词：mesmerist, magnetizer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

hypnotize:/ˈhɪpnətaɪz/| vt. |催眠，使着迷|To induce a state of hypnosis in (someone). To fascinate or mesmerize.|例句：The magician hypnotized his audience with his incredible tricks. 魔术师用他不可思议的戏法迷住了观众。

例句：She was hypnotized by the flickering flames of the fire. 她被闪烁的火焰迷住了。|近义词：mesmerize, entrance, captivate|反义词: N/A|词性: verb
