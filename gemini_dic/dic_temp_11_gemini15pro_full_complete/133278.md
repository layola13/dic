nonexpanding:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspændɪŋ/ | adj. | 不膨胀的，不扩张的 | Not expanding; not becoming larger or wider. | The nonexpanding universe theory was eventually superseded. 不膨胀宇宙理论最终被取代了。| The container is nonexpanding, so the contents remain compact. 这个容器是不膨胀的，所以里面的东西保持紧凑。 | 近义词: fixed, stable, inflexible, unyielding | 反义词: expanding, growing, increasing, dilating | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + expanding.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "expanding" comes from the Latin "expandere," meaning "to spread out."
