defocalization:/diːfoʊkələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |散焦，去焦，失焦|The act or process of causing something to lose focus or clarity.|The artist used defocalization to create a dreamlike effect in the photograph. 艺术家利用散焦在照片中营造出一种梦幻般的效果。|blurring, softening|focusing, sharpening|noun

defocalizations:/diːfoʊkələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |散焦，去焦，失焦（复数）|Plural form of defocalization, referring to multiple instances of losing focus or clarity.|The multiple defocalizations in the film created a sense of disorientation.  影片中多次出现的散焦营造了一种迷失方向的感觉。|blurrings, softenings|focusings, sharpenings|noun
