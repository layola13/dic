outpopulated:/ˌaʊtˈpɑːpjəleɪtɪd/
Verb
人口超过
having a larger population than another area or group
例句：The city was quickly outpopulated by its suburbs. 这个城市的人口很快就被郊区超过了。

例句：The rabbits outpopulated the foxes on the island.  岛上兔子的数量超过了狐狸。
近义词：exceed in population, surpass in number
反义词：depopulated
词性：v.
