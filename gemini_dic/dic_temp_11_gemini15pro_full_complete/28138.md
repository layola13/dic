capitulants:/kəˈpɪtʃʊlənts/
n.
参加会议者；分会会长
Members of a chapter, especially of a religious order, who meet in chapter.
The capitulants gathered for their annual meeting. 分会会长们聚集在一起参加年会。
members, delegates, representatives
non-participants, absentees
