antinaturalistic:/ˌæntɪˌnætʃrəˈlɪstɪk/
adj.
反自然主义的
Opposed to or rejecting naturalism, especially in art or literature.
His antinaturalistic paintings emphasized abstract forms and vibrant colors. 他反自然主义的绘画强调抽象的形式和鲜艳的色彩。
近义词:artificial, contrived, nonrealistic
反义词:naturalistic, realistic
词性:形容词
