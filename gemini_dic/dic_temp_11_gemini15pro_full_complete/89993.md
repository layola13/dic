horsy:/ˈhɔːrsi/ (US), /ˈhɔːsi/ (UK)| adj.|爱马的；像马的；马术的；粗俗的|Of or relating to horses; resembling a horse; devoted to or interested in horses or horseback riding; (informal, often disapproving) large and strong in a way that is not graceful.|例句：She has always been a horsy girl. 她一直是个热爱马的女孩。

例句：He had a rather horsy face. 他的脸长得有点像马。

近义词：equestrian, equine|反义词: delicate, refined|词性: adjective
