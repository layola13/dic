netty:/ˈnɛti/ | n. | 网络框架 | A non-blocking I/O client-server framework for the development of Java network applications. It simplifies network programming like TCP and UDP socket server. | Netty is commonly used in high-performance applications. Netty常用于高性能应用程序。| 近义词:  Mina, Grizzly | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a framework) | 词性: noun
