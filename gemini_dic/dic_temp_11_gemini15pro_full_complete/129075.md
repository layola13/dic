nitrol:/ˈnaɪtrɒl/ (US), /ˈnaɪtrɒl/ (UK)| n.|硝化甘油, 硝酸酯类药物|A heavy yellow oily explosive liquid used in dynamite and also as a vasodilator in the treatment of angina pectoris. It is a nitric ester of glycerol.|例句：The nitrol helped relieve his chest pain. 硝化甘油帮助缓解了他的胸痛。

例句：He kept nitrol tablets in his pocket in case of an angina attack. 他口袋里放着硝酸甘油片，以防心绞痛发作。|近义词：glyceryl trinitrate, nitroglycerin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
