longipennate:/ˌlɒndʒɪˈpɛneɪt/ /ˌlɔːndʒɪˈpɛneɪt/
adjective
长翼的，长羽毛的
Having long wings or long feathers, especially long flight feathers.
例句：The longipennate wings of the albatross allow it to soar for hours. 信天翁的长翼使它能够翱翔数小时。

例句：The falcon's longipennate feathers contribute to its speed and agility.  猎鹰的长羽毛有助于它的速度和敏捷性。

近义词：long-winged
反义词：brachypterous (短翅的)
词性: adjective
