crediting:/ˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/
v.
记入；归功于；相信
Attributing something to someone or something; believing something to be true.
The company is crediting the increase in sales to their new marketing campaign.  该公司将销售额的增长归功于他们新的营销活动。
He is credited with discovering the planet. 他被认为是发现了这颗行星。
Synonyms: attributing, ascribing, assigning, imputing, recognizing
Antonyms: debiting, discounting, disbelieving
