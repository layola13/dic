certification:/ˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 证明；认证；证书 |  The official act of certifying something.  The document that officially certifies something. |  He received certification as a pilot. 他获得了飞行员资格认证。  She presented her teaching certification to the school board. 她向学校董事会出示了她的教师资格证书。 | accreditation, license, diploma | denial, revocation | noun

etymology->
From Late Middle English: certificacioun, from Old French certification, from Late Latin certificatio(n-), from certificare ‘certify’.
