exequatur:/ˌeksɪˈkweɪtər/
n.
执行令；许可执行令
An official order by a government allowing a legal decision made in another country to be put into effect.
The foreign judgment was granted an exequatur by the domestic court. 国内法院对该外国判决给予了执行令。
The court issued an exequatur to enforce the foreign arbitral award. 法院签发了执行令，以执行外国仲裁裁决。
Synonyms: authorization, warrant, permission, sanction
Antonyms: prohibition, veto, denial, refusal
noun
