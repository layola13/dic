nondepressingly:/ˌnɒndɪˈprɛsɪŋli/ | adv. | 不令人沮丧地 | In a way that does not cause or characterized by depression. | The sun shone nondepressingly, lifting everyone's spirits.  阳光明媚，令人心情愉悦。| Synonyms: cheerfully, brightly, optimistically | Antonyms: depressingly, gloomily, sadly | Adverb
