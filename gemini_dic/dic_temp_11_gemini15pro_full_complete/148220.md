oversight:/ˈoʊvərˌsaɪt/| n.|疏忽，失察；监督，监管|An unintentional failure to notice or do something. / The action of overseeing something.|例句：The error was caused by an oversight in the checking process. 这个错误是由于检查过程中的疏忽造成的。

例句：The committee has oversight of the government's spending. 该委员会负责监督政府的支出。|近义词：lapse, omission, supervision, surveillance|反义词：attention, care, scrutiny|词性: noun
