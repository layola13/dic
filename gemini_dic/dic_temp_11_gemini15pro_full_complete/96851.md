inexactnesses:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzæktnəsɪz/
n.
不精确性；误差；错误
Lack of exactness or precision.
The inexactnesses in the measurements led to flawed results. 测量中的不精确性导致结果有缺陷。
Synonyms: inaccuracies, errors, imprecisions, deviations, discrepancies
Antonyms:  accuracies, precision, exactness, correctness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:
From in- (not) + exactness.  Exactness comes from the Latin "exactus," past participle of "exigere" (to demand, drive out, weigh, measure).
