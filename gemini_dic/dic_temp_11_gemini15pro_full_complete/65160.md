ethnicity:/ɛθˈnɪsɪti/ | n. | 种族，民族 |  A social group that shares a common and distinctive culture, religion, language, or the like. |  Example 1:  Her ethnicity is Korean.  她的种族是韩国人。

Example 2: The census collects information on ethnicity and race. 人口普查收集有关种族和民族的信息。 | Synonym: race, origin, ancestry, heritage | Antonym: N/A | noun
