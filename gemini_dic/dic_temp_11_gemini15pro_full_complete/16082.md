bandolier:/ˌbændəˈlɪr/ | n. | 子弹带，弹药带 | A belt worn over the shoulder and across the chest, typically used to carry ammunition. | The soldier adjusted his bandolier before heading into battle. 士兵在进入战斗前调整了他的子弹带。 | Example: The cowboy wore a bandolier filled with cartridges across his chest. 牛仔胸前挎着一条装满子弹的子弹带。 | Synonyms: cartridge belt, ammunition belt | Antonyms: N/A | noun

bandolier:/ˌbændəˈlɪr/ | n. | (比喻) (肩上斜挎的) 一串，一系列 | A collection of similar items, often worn or carried in a way that resembles a bandolier. | The chef wore a bandolier of colorful spices. 厨师戴着一串色彩缤纷的香料。 | Example:  She wore a bandolier of cameras around her neck. 她脖子上挂着一串相机。 | Synonyms: string, array, series | Antonyms: N/A | noun
