overdry: /ˌoʊvərˈdraɪ/ | adj. | 过于干燥的，过度干燥的 | Excessively dry; dried too much.  | The overdry cake crumbled when I tried to slice it. 蛋糕太干了，我切的时候都碎了。 | The desert air left my skin feeling overdry. 沙漠的空气让我的皮肤感觉非常干燥。| 近义词: parched, desiccated | 反义词: moist, damp | 词性:形容词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "dry" (adjective).  "Over-" 来自古英语 "ofer,"  "dry" 来自古英语 "dryge."
