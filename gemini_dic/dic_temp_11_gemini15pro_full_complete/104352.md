juxtaposition:/ˌdʒʌkstəpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 并置，并列 | the fact of two things being seen or placed close together with contrasting effect | The juxtaposition of these two images highlights the contrast between wealth and poverty. 这两幅图像的并置突出了贫富之间的对比。|  近义词：contrast, comparison, proximity | 反义词: separation, distance | noun
