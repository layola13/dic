adjective

skilled in oratory; eloquent
例句：He was an oratorious young man, captivating his audience with every word. 他是一位口才流利的年轻人，他的每一句话都吸引着他的听众。

近义词：eloquent, articulate, fluent, silver-tongued

词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *ōrātōrius*, from ōrātor (“speaker, orator”) + -ius.
