annat:/ˈænət/ | n. |年金；年金受益人；年金领取者| An annual payment, often for life, made to someone as retirement income or as compensation for injury. |  My grandmother receives an annat from her late husband's pension. 我的祖母从她已故丈夫的养老金中领取年金。

| Example: The annat provides a steady income stream for retirees. 这笔年金为退休人员提供了稳定的收入来源。| Synonyms: annuity, pension, allowance | Antonyms: lump sum, one-time payment | noun


Etymology: From Old French *annate, from Medieval Latin annata (“annual payment”), from Latin annus (“year”).
