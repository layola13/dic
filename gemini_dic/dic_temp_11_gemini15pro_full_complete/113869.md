madisterium: /ˌmædɪˈstɪəriəm/
noun
教学场所; 学校; 学塾
A place of instruction; a school.  This word is rarely used in modern English.
The madisterium was a small building where the village children learned to read and write.  这个学塾是一栋小楼，村里的孩子们在那里学习读书写字。
Synonyms: school, academy, institute, seminary
Antonyms:  None (a direct antonym is difficult to find as the word itself is archaic and refers to a specific type of school)
词性: 名词
