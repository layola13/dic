baiting:/ˈbeɪtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbeɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 引诱；激怒；折磨 | the act of deliberately annoying or taunting someone, often online, to provoke an angry or emotional response. | He was accused of online baiting after posting inflammatory comments on the forum. 他在论坛上发布煽动性评论后被指控网络引诱。|  近义词：provoking, taunting, harassing, goading | 反义词: placating, soothing | 词性: noun

baiting:/ˈbeɪtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbeɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |  下诱饵；折磨 | present participle of *bait* meaning to deliberately annoy or taunt. |  They were baiting the dog by waving a steak in front of it.  他们用一块牛排在狗面前晃来晃去，折磨它。| 近义词：tormenting, teasing, harassing, provoking | 反义词: comforting, appeasing | 词性: verb (present participle)
