hypercautiously:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɔːʃəsli/| adv.|非常谨慎地，过度小心地|In a way that is excessively cautious.  Showing extreme or excessive caution.|He hypercautiously approached the stray dog, offering it a piece of bread. 他非常谨慎地靠近那只流浪狗，递给它一块面包。|Example:  She hypercautiously navigated the icy sidewalk. 她小心翼翼地在结冰的人行道上前行。|Synonyms: overcautiously, excessively cautiously, extremely carefully, unduly cautiously|Antonyms: recklessly, carelessly, rashly|词性: 副词

hypercautious:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɔːʃəs/| adj.|非常谨慎的，过度小心的|Excessively cautious; extremely careful.|She's hypercautious about making any financial decisions. 她对任何财务决定都非常谨慎。|Example: The hypercautious driver always drove below the speed limit.  这位过度谨慎的司机总是低于限速行驶。|Synonyms: overcautious, excessively cautious, extremely careful, unduly cautious|Antonyms: reckless, careless, rashly|词性: 形容词

词源：
"Hyper-" (过度, 超过) + "cautious" (谨慎的)  = hypercautious (过度谨慎的)

"Hyper-" comes from Greek "hyper" (over, above).
"Cautious" comes from Latin "cautus" (wary, careful), past participle of "caveo" (I beware, take heed).
