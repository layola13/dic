alethiology:/ˌælɪθiˈɒlədʒi/
n.
真理学；真理的研究
The study of truth; the branch of philosophy that deals with the nature of truth.
Alethiology explores the different theories of truth, such as the correspondence theory and the coherence theory.  真理学探索不同的真理理论，例如对应理论和一致性理论。

Alethiology is a complex and often debated topic in philosophy. 真理学是哲学中一个复杂且经常被争论的话题。

近义词:epistemology, philosophy of truth

词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *ἀλήθεια* (alétheia, “truth”) +‎ *-λογία* (-logía, “study of”).
