overdraw:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɔː/ /ˌəʊvəˈdrɔː/ | v. |透支；夸大|To draw money from a bank account in excess of the balance; to exaggerate.| I overdrew my account by $50. 我透支了我的账户50美元。

He tends to overdraw the difficulties of the situation. 他往往夸大情况的困难。|近义词：exaggerate, overstate, embellish|反义词: understate, downplay|词性:动词

overdraw:/ˈoʊvərdrɔː/ /ˈəʊvədrɔː/ | n. |透支|The act of overdrawing a bank account.| The bank charged me a fee for the overdraw. 银行因透支向我收取了费用。|近义词：overdraft|反义词: deposit|词性:名词
