electricians:/ɪˌlekˈtrɪʃənz/
n.|电工|A person who installs and maintains electrical systems.|The electricians are rewiring the house. 电工们正在给房子重新布线。|近义词:wireman, electrical worker|反义词:None|词性:Noun (plural)
Etymology: From electrician + -s (plural suffix). Electrician comes from electric + -ian (suffix forming nouns denoting persons engaged in or connected with something). Electric ultimately derives from the Greek word "elektron" meaning "amber," due to the static electricity produced by rubbing amber.
