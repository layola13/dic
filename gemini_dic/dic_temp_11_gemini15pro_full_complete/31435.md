charismatics: /ˌkærɪzˈmætɪks/
| n. | 魅力超凡的人；有超凡魅力的人（通常指宗教领袖） | People with charisma, especially religious leaders. | The charismatics drew large crowds with their passionate sermons.  这些魅力超凡的人用他们充满激情的布道吸引了大批群众。 | 近义词：leaders, influencers, visionaries | 反义词：followers,  unremarkable people| noun


charismatics: /ˌkærɪzˈmætɪks/
| adj. | 超凡魅力的；神授能力的 | Relating to or characterized by charisma, especially in religious contexts. |  The charismatic movement emphasizes spiritual gifts such as healing and prophecy.  魅力运动强调精神天赋，如治愈和预言。 | 近义词：magnetic, captivating, inspiring | 反义词：uninspiring, dull, boring | adjective


Etymology:
From Greek "khárisma" meaning "gift of grace" or "favor."
