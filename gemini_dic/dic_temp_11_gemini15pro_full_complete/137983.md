nonzonally:/ˌnɒnˈzəʊnəli/
adverb
非纬向地，非地带性地
Not in zones or belts; varying or distributed without reference to latitude.
Ocean currents can transport heat nonzonally, influencing regional climates. 洋流可以非纬向地输送热量，从而影响区域气候。
The distribution of some species is nonzonal, reflecting specific habitat requirements. 一些物种的分布是非地带性的，反映了特定的栖息地要求。
Synonyms: irregularly, unevenly, patchily
Antonyms: zonally, latitudinally
word type: adverb
