metallograph:/məˈtæləɡræf/ | n. | 金相显微镜；金相图谱 | An instrument for examining the structure of metals by magnifying the image of a prepared surface specimen.  It can also refer to a photographic representation of this structure. |  The metallograph revealed the presence of micro-cracks in the steel sample. 金相显微镜显示钢样品中存在微裂纹。

| 近义词: metallographic microscope | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider macroscopic observation as a contrasting concept) | 词性: noun


metallography:/məˈtælɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 金相学 | The study of the structure of metals and alloys, typically using microscopic and other techniques. | The course covered the basics of metallography and materials testing. 这门课程涵盖了金相学和材料测试的基础知识。

| 近义词: materialography | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
