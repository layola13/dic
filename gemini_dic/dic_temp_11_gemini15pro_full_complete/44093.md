crippling:/ˈkrɪplɪŋ/| adj.|严重的，造成严重后果的；令人难以承受的|Causing severe and disabling damage, making something very difficult to deal with.|The country's economy suffered a crippling recession. 这个国家的经济遭受了严重的衰退。

The crippling effects of the disease left him unable to walk. 这种疾病造成的严重后果使他无法行走。


近义词:devastating, debilitating, paralyzing|反义词:beneficial, helpful|词性:形容词
