hornbook:/ˈhɔːrnˌbʊk/ | n. | 角页书，识字课本 | A thin board with a handle, covered with a sheet of paper usually protected by a transparent sheet of horn, and containing the alphabet, numerals, etc., formerly used in teaching children to read. |  Children in the 17th century learned their ABCs from a hornbook. 17世纪的孩子们用角页书学习字母表。 | synonyms: primer, abecedarian | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "advanced textbook") | noun

hornbook:/ˈhɔːrnˌbʊk/ | n. |入门书，基础知识 | A basic or introductory guide to a subject. | This book serves as a hornbook on contract law. 这本书是合同法的入门书。 | synonyms: primer, introduction, guide | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "advanced treatise") | noun
