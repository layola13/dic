diandria:/daɪˈændriə/| n.|二雄蕊植物；二雄蕊纲|A plant having two stamens in each flower.  A class of plants having two stamens.|例句：Diandira is a class in the Linnaean system. 二雄蕊纲是林奈分类系统中的一个纲。

例句：The flower exhibits diandria, with only two stamens present.  这朵花展现了二雄蕊的特征，只有两个雄蕊。|近义词：stamens (雄蕊), androecium (雄蕊群) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
