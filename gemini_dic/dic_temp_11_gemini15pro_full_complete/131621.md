noncompositeness:/ˌnɒnkɒmpɒzɪtnəs/ |n.| 非合成性，非复合性 |The noncompositeness of prime numbers is a fundamental concept in number theory. 素数的非合成性是数论中的一个基本概念。| non-compositeness is the state of being not composed of other elements or factors.  Noncompositeness is the opposite of compositeness, which describes numbers that can be factored into smaller whole numbers.  It primarily applies to prime numbers. | Example 1: The noncompositeness of 7 makes it a prime number.  7的非合成性使其成为质数。

Example 2:  Understanding noncompositeness is crucial for cryptography. 理解非合成性对于密码学至关重要。 | synonym: simplicity, indivisibility | antonym: compositeness, divisibility | noun
