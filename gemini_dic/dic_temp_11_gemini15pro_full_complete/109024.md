lefty: /ˈlɛfti/ | n. adj. | 左撇子；左派 | A person who uses their left hand more skillfully than their right, especially for writing; (informal) a person with left-wing political views. |  He's a lefty, so he sits on the left side of the table when he plays tennis. 他是左撇子，所以打网球时坐在桌子的左侧。|  The party attracts both liberals and lefties. 该党吸引了自由主义者和左派人士。 | synonyms: left-hander, southpaw (n.); left-wing, radical (adj.) | antonyms: righty, right-hander (n.); right-wing, conservative (adj.) | noun, adjective
