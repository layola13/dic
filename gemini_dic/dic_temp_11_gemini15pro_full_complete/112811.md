lucernarian:/ˌluːsərˈnɛəriən/
noun
灯笼水母；钵水母纲动物
An invertebrate animal belonging to the class Scyphozoa, typically having a bell-shaped body and tentacles.
Lucernarians are fascinating creatures that live in the deep ocean. 灯笼水母是生活在深海的迷人生物。
Lucernarians attach themselves to rocks or seaweed. 灯笼水母附着在岩石或海藻上。

antonyms:None
