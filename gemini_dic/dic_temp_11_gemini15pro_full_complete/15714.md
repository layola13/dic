balancement:/ˈbælənsmənt/| n.|平衡，均衡，对称|The balancement of power between the two countries is delicate. 两国之间的权力平衡非常微妙。|例句：The architect paid close attention to the balancement of the building's design. 建筑师密切关注建筑设计的平衡。

The careful balancement of flavors in the dish made it a culinary masterpiece.  这道菜中味道的精心平衡使它成为烹饪杰作。|近义词：equilibrium, equipoise, symmetry|反义词:imbalance, asymmetry|词性:名词

Etymology: From French balancement, from balancer (to balance), from Old French balance (balance, pair of scales). Ultimately from Late Latin bilanx (having two scales), from Latin bis (twice) + lanx (plate, dish, scale of a balance).
