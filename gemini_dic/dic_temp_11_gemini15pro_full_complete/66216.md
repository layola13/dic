excited: /ɪkˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 兴奋的，激动的 | Feeling or showing strong emotion and enthusiasm. | I'm so excited about the upcoming trip! 我对即将到来的旅行感到非常兴奋！|  She was visibly excited to receive the news. 她收到这个消息时明显很兴奋。 | 近义词: thrilled, enthusiastic, exhilarated | 反义词: bored, apathetic, calm | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin excitare "to rouse, call out, summon forth," from ex- "out" + citare "to put in motion, rouse, excite."
