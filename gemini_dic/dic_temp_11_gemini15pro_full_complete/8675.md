anticonventional:/ˌæntiːkənˈvenʃənəl/| adj.|反传统的，非传统的|Opposing or rejecting established customs, beliefs, or practices. |Her anticonventional lifestyle surprised her family. 她的反传统生活方式让她的家人感到惊讶。|Example: He has an anticonventional approach to art, preferring abstract expressionism over realism. 他对艺术采取了反传统的方法，喜欢抽象表现主义而不是现实主义。|Synonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, nonconformist, iconoclastic, heterodox|Antonyms: conventional, traditional, orthodox, conformist|Word type: adjective
