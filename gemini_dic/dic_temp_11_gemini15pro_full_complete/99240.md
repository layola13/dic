intercommissions:/ˌɪntərkəˈmɪʃənz/
n.
相互委托；共同委托；互相委任
The act of two or more parties commissioning each other, or jointly commissioning a third party.
The two organizations formed intercommissions to investigate the shared concerns. 这两个组织成立了共同委员会来调查共同关心的问题。
joint commissions, mutual commissions
sole commissions
noun
