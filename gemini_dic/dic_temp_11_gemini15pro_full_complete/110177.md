lientery:/ˈlaɪəntri/| n.|痢疾，腹泻|A disease characterized by frequent evacuations of undigested food.  A condition in which food passes through the digestive system with little or no digestion.|He suffered from lientery after eating contaminated food. 他吃了被污染的食物后患上了痢疾。|Synonyms: diarrhea, dysentery|Antonyms: constipation|noun
