overforce: /ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrs/ | v. |过度用力；强迫；夸张|To exert excessive force; to strain; to exaggerate.|He overforced his voice and ended up losing it. 他过度用力使用嗓子，结果失声了。| The comedian overforced the joke and it fell flat.  这位喜剧演员把笑话讲得太夸张了，结果很无聊。|近义词：exaggerate, strain, overexert|反义词: understate, relax|词性:动词

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "force" (from Old French "force," ultimately from Latin "fortis" meaning "strong")
