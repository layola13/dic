overexpansion:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænʃən/ | n. |过度扩张，过度膨胀|An expansion that goes beyond reasonable or sustainable limits. Excessive growth, often leading to instability or collapse.| The overexpansion of the company led to its eventual bankruptcy. 公司的过度扩张最终导致其破产。| overextension, inflation, surplus, excess | contraction, deflation, reduction, shortage | noun
