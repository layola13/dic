fanny's:/ˈfæniz/| n.|范妮的（女性名字Fanny的所有格形式）|Fanny's refers to something belonging to or associated with a person named Fanny.  It indicates possession.|This is Fanny's bag. 这是范妮的包。|Fanny's house is beautiful. 范妮的房子很漂亮。|近义词: (If referring to possession) belonging to Fanny, owned by Fanny |反义词:  N/A|词性:Possessive Noun


Etymology->
From the given name Fanny, a diminutive of Frances, ultimately from the Germanic name Franc, meaning "Frenchman" or "free". The possessive form is created by adding 's.
