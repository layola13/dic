lambed:/læmd/ | v. | 产羔羊；生小羊 | To give birth to a lamb (of a ewe). | The ewe lambed triplets this spring.  这只母羊今年春天产下了三胞胎小羊。|  The farmer was delighted when his prize ewe lambed twins.  当这位农民的获奖母羊产下双胞胎时，他欣喜若狂。 | 近义词: delivered, birthed (more general) | 反义词: None (This is a specific biological process without a direct opposite.) | 词性: verb
词源: From Old English *lambian, from lamb (noun).
