moustached:/ˈmʌstæʃt/ /ˈmʌstɑːʃt/| adj. |有胡子的，留胡子的|Having a moustache (a strip of hair grown above the upper lip).|例句：The moustached man looked distinguished. 这位留着胡子的男士看起来很尊贵。|例句：She was intimidated by the moustached bouncer at the club entrance.  她被俱乐部入口处那个留着胡子的保镖吓到了。|近义词：bearded, whiskered|反义词: clean-shaven|词性:形容词

词源: From moustache + -ed.  Moustache comes from French moustache, from Italian mostaccio, from Medieval Greek μουστάκιον (moustakion), diminutive of μύσταξ (mustax, “upper lip, moustache”), of uncertain origin.
