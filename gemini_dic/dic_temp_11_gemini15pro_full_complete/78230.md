gerbil: /ˈdʒɜːrbɪl/ (British) /ˈdʒɜːrbəl/ (American)| n.| 沙鼠；跳鼠 |A small rodent with long hind legs, native to arid regions of Asia and Africa, often kept as a pet.| I bought a gerbil for my daughter's birthday. 我为女儿的生日买了一只沙鼠。|  My gerbil loves to run on its wheel. 我的沙鼠喜欢在它的轮子上跑。|  Synonyms: rodent, mouse, hamster | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider predator like snake, owl) | noun
