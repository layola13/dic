agreeingly:/əˈɡriːɪŋli/
adverb
同意地，一致地
in a way that shows agreement
例句：She nodded agreeably. 她点头表示同意。

例句：He smiled agreeably at her suggestion. 他对她提出的建议报以赞同的微笑。

近义词：concordantly, harmoniously, approvingly
反义词：disagreeably, discordantly, objectingly
词性：副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From agree + -ing + -ly.  "Agree" comes from Old French agreer, from à (to) + gre (pleasure, satisfaction), from Latin gratus (pleasing, thankful).
