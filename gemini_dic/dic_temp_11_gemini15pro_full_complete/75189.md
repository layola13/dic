frizzled:/ˈfrɪzəld/| adj.|卷曲的，卷毛的；油炸的|Having a mass of small tight curls; (of hair) crisply curled.|Her hair was frizzled by the humidity. 她的头发被湿气弄卷了。|近义词：curly, kinky, crispy|反义词:straight|词性:形容词
frizzle:/ˈfrɪzəl/| v.|（使）卷曲；（使）发出咝咝声|To form small crisp curls; to fry until crisp and curled.|The bacon frizzled in the pan. 培根在锅里发出咝咝声。|近义词：curl, sizzle, fry|反义词:straighten|词性:动词
