concavely:/kɒnˈkeɪvli/ | adv. |凹地，凹陷地|In a concave manner; curving inwards.|The lens was concavely shaped to disperse the light.  这个透镜呈凹形以分散光线。|Example: The mirror reflected concavely, distorting the image. 镜子凹陷地反射，使图像变形。|Synonyms: inwards, hollowly|Antonyms: convexly, outwardly|Part of Speech: Adverb

concave:/ˈkɒnkeɪv/ | adj. |凹的|Curving inwards.|A concave lens is used to correct nearsightedness. 凹透镜用于矫正近视。|Example: The bowl had a concave bottom. 碗的底部是凹的。|Synonyms: hollow, incurved|Antonyms: convex, bulging|Part of Speech: Adjective
