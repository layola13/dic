nonbiologist:/ˌnɒn baɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌnɑːn baɪˈɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. |非生物学家|A person who is not a biologist; someone who does not study biology.|例句：The nonbiologist struggled to understand the complex scientific terms.  这位非生物学家很难理解复杂的科学术语。

例句：The explanation was geared towards nonbiologists, making it easy to grasp.  这个解释是面向非生物学家，使它易于理解。|近义词：layperson, nonscientist|反义词：biologist|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
non- + biologist

"non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of."  It comes from Latin.
"biologist" comes from the Greek "bios" (life) + "-logos" (study).  It refers to someone who studies living organisms.
