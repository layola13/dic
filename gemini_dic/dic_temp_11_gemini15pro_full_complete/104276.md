justicehood: /ˈdʒʌstɪshʊd/
n.
正义状态; 正义的象征;  (超级英雄的) 正义事业
The state or quality of being just or righteous, often associated with superheroes and their pursuit of justice.
Batman dedicated his life to justicehood, protecting Gotham from criminals.  蝙蝠侠毕生致力于正义事业，保护哥谭市免受罪犯侵害。
Near synonyms: righteousness, uprightness, morality, virtue
Antonyms: wickedness, injustice, iniquity
