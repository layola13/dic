awol:/ˈeɪwɒl/ (US), /ˈeɪwɔːl/ (UK)| adj. n.| 缺勤的；擅离职守者；逃兵 | Absent Without Official Leave (or Leave), meaning away from one's military post without permission. | Example: The soldier went AWOL after learning about his mother's illness.  这名士兵得知母亲生病后擅离职守。

Example:  He was arrested as an AWOL from the army. 他作为逃兵被逮捕了。 | 近义词: absent, deserted, truant | 反义词: present, accounted for | 词性: adjective, noun
