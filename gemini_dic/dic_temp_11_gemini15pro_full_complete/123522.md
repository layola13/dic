monotonist:/məˈnɒtənɪst/ | n. | 单调乏味的人；单音调者 | A person who speaks in a monotone or leads a monotonous life.  | He's such a monotonist; his conversations are always so dull. 他真是个乏味的人，他的谈话总是那么无聊。 |  The lecturer was a bit of a monotonist, and many students struggled to stay awake. 演讲者有点单调，许多学生都在努力保持清醒。 | Synonyms: bore, dullard | Antonyms: enthusiast, lively person | noun
