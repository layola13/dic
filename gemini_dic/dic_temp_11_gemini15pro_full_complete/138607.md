nuciferous:/njuːˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
含核的；产坚果的
bearing nuts or a nut.
例句：The nuciferous tree provided a bountiful harvest of pecans. 这棵产坚果的树收获了大量的山核桃。

例句：Squirrels are particularly fond of nuciferous trees. 松鼠特别喜欢结坚果的树。

近义词：nut-bearing, nut-producing
反义词： (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "fruit-bearing" if contrasting with other fruit types)
词性: adjective
