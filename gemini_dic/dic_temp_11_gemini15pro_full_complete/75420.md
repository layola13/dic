frugiferous:/fruːˈdʒɪfərəs/
adjective
结果实的;多产的
Bearing or producing fruit in abundance.
例句：The frugiferous tree was laden with ripe apples.  这棵多产的树上挂满了成熟的苹果。

例句：The orchard was especially frugiferous this year, yielding a bumper crop.  今年果园的产量特别高，获得了大丰收。

近义词：fruitful, productive, prolific
反义词：barren, sterile, unproductive
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *fructifer* (fruit-bearing), from *fructus* (fruit) + *-fer* (carrying, bearing).  源自拉丁语 *fructifer*（结果实的），由 *fructus*（果实）+ *-fer*（携带，负荷）组成。
