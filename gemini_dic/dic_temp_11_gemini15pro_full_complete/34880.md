clip-on:/ˈklɪp ɒn/ (US), /ˈklɪp ɒn/ (UK)| adj. n.|夹式的，夹式配件|A clip-on device is one that is attached by a clip.|例句：She wore clip-on earrings because her ears weren't pierced. 她戴着夹式耳环，因为她的耳朵没有穿孔。

例句：He bought a clip-on tie for his son's school uniform. 他给儿子的校服买了一条夹式领带。|近义词：attachable, removable|反义词:permanent, fixed|词性:adjective, noun


clip-on:/ˈklɪp ɒn/ (US), /ˈklɪp ɒn/ (UK)| n.|夹式配件|A clip-on item is something that can be attached with a clip.|例句：He lost the clip-on for his sunglasses. 他弄丢了太阳镜的夹片。
