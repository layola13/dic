jinx:/dʒɪŋks/| n. vt.|厄运；霉运；招来厄运的人（或事物）；使倒霉|A jinx is something that is thought to bring bad luck.|例句：There's a jinx on this car - it's always breaking down. 这辆车真倒霉，总是出故障。|近义词：curse, hex, spell|反义词:blessing, charm|noun

jinx:/dʒɪŋks/| vt.|使倒霉；给…带来厄运|To jinx someone or something is to cause them to have bad luck.|例句：I think I've jinxed the game by saying we were going to win. 我觉得我说我们会赢，结果给比赛带来了厄运。|近义词：curse, hex|反义词:bless|verb
