monotherapy:/ˌmɒnəˈθɛrəpi/ | n. |单一疗法|Treatment of a disease with a single drug or therapeutic method.  The use of a single drug to treat a condition rather than a combination of drugs.| The doctor decided on monotherapy with a new antibiotic.|Synonyms: single-drug therapy, single-agent therapy |Antonyms: combination therapy, polytherapy| noun
