adjective

Existing or occurring between royal courts.

The interaulic rivalries between the European powers played a significant role in the outbreak of the First World War.

近义词: courtly, royal, regal
反义词: common, plebeian
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *interaulicus*, from inter- ‘between’ + aula ‘court, hall’.
