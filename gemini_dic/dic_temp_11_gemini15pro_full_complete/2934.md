advocateship:/ˈædvəkətˌʃɪp/
n.
拥护；辩护；倡导
The act of pleading for, supporting, or recommending; active espousal.
She took up the advocateship of the disabled. 她承担了残疾人的辩护工作。

近义词: advocacy, support, championship, promotion
反义词: opposition, resistance, antagonism

词性: 名词

词源: From advocate + -ship.  "Advocate" comes from the Latin "advocatus," past participle of "advocare"  meaning "to summon" or "to call to one's aid."  The suffix "-ship" denotes state, condition, or quality.
