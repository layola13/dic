inexpiate:/ɪnˈɛkspiɪt/ | adj.| 不可赎的，不能补偿的 | Unable to be atoned for; unpardonable.  | His crime was so heinous, it was deemed inexpiable. 他的罪行如此滔天，被认为是不可饶恕的。 |  The inexpiable betrayal shattered their family.  这不可饶恕的背叛摧毁了他们的家庭。|  近义词：unpardonable, unforgivable, irredeemable | 反义词: forgivable, pardonable, excusable | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *inexpiabilis, from Latin in- "not" + expiare "to atone for."
