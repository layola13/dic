die-down:/daɪ daʊn/
verb
逐渐平息，逐渐减弱
To gradually decrease in intensity or volume; to become less strong or noticeable.
The excitement from winning the lottery eventually died down.  赢得彩票的兴奋最终逐渐平息了。
The storm died down after a few hours.  几个小时后，暴风雨逐渐减弱了。
近义词: subside, abate, dwindle, lessen, fade
反义词: intensify, increase, flare up
词性: verb (phrasal verb)
