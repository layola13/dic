largesse:/lɑːrˈʒɛs/
n.
慷慨，大方；赠品，赏赐
Generosity in giving; lavish or bountiful contributions.
The king distributed largesse to the people. 国王向人民分发赏赐。

近义词: generosity, bounty, liberality, munificence
反义词: stinginess, meanness, parsimony, miserliness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old French largesse, from large (wide, ample), from Latin largus (abundant, copious).
