champ:/tʃæmp/ | n. | 冠军，优胜者；斗士；|A person who has defeated all others in a competition or series of competitions, especially in sport.|  The champ will defend his title next month.  这位冠军下个月将卫冕他的头衔。 | 近义词: winner, victor, champion | 反义词: loser, runner-up | 词性: 名词
champ:/tʃæmp/ | v. | 大声咀嚼; 焦躁地咬|To chew or bite upon something noisily or impatiently.| The horse champed at the bit. 那匹马焦躁地咬着嚼子。| 近义词: munch, gnaw, chomp | 反义词: nibble | 词性: 动词

词源:
Shortening of champion.
