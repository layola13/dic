huds:/hʌdz/| n.|抬头显示器（Head-Up Displays的缩写）|A transparent display that presents data without requiring users to look away from their usual viewpoints.|The pilot checked the huds for altitude and airspeed. 飞行员查看了抬头显示器上的高度和空速。|Synonyms: HUD, head-up display|Antonyms: N/A|noun
