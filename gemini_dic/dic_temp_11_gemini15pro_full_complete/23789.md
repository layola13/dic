brawninesses:/ˈbrɔːninəsɪz/
n.
强壮；肌肉发达
The state or quality of being brawny; muscular strength.
His brawninesses were evident in his rippling biceps. 他的强壮表现在他结实的二头肌上。
He was known for his brawninesses and athleticism.  他以强壮的体格和运动能力而闻名。
synonyms: muscularity, strength, power, might
antonyms: weakness, frailty, feebleness
noun


Etymology:
From brawny + -ness (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  Brawny itself comes from Old English *brāwn, of uncertain origin.
