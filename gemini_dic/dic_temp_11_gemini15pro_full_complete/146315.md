overfeel: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːl/
verb
过度感受；过分情绪化
To experience or express emotions too intensely or excessively.
She tends to overfeel everything, which can be exhausting. 她总是过度情绪化，这让人很疲惫。
近义词: overreact, exaggerate, be overly sensitive
反义词: underreact, downplay
词性: verb

overfeel: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːl/
adjective (rarely used)
过度感性的；过分情绪化的
Characterized by excessive emotionality.
His overfeel response to the criticism was surprising.  他对批评的过度感性反应令人惊讶。
近义词: oversensitive, emotional, dramatic
反义词: unemotional, stoic
词性: adjective


词源： "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "feel" (verb)
