acidifier:/əˈsɪdɪfaɪər/| n.|酸化剂|A substance added to food or other products to increase acidity or lower pH.|Citric acid is a common acidifier used in food processing.  柠檬酸是食品加工中常用的酸化剂。|synonyms: acidulant, acid, souring agent|antonyms: alkalizer, neutralizer, base|noun
