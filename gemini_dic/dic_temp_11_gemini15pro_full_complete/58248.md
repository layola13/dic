drumes: /druːmz/ | n. |鼓，通常指架子鼓| A set of drums; usually refers to a drum kit. |He plays the drumes in a rock band. 他在一个摇滚乐队里打鼓。|Synonyms: drums, drum kit, percussion |Antonyms: None |Noun


Etymology:
Likely a misspelling of "drums," which comes from Middle English *drumme*, from Middle Dutch *tromme*, ultimately of imitative origin.
