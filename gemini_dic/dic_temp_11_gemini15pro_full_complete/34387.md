classify: /ˈklæsɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈklæsɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v. | 分类，归类 | To arrange or organize things into groups according to shared qualities or characteristics. |  We classify animals into different groups based on their characteristics.  我们根据动物的特征将它们分成不同的类别。

Scientists classify plants by their structure and reproductive methods. 科学家根据植物的结构和繁殖方法对它们进行分类。| synonyms: categorize, sort, group, arrange, order | antonyms:  mix, combine, jumble, disorganize | verb
