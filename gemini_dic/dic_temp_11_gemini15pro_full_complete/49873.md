demotivator:/diːˈmoʊtɪveɪtər/| n. |  desmotivador (Spanish), démotivateur (French)| something that makes someone less enthusiastic, less willing to work hard, or less likely to be successful |  The constant criticism from his boss was a demotivator.| synonyms: disincentive, deterrent, dampener | antonyms: motivator, incentive, encouragement | noun

demotivate:/diːˈmoʊtɪveɪt/| vt. | 使失去动力，使灰心 | To make someone less enthusiastic about something. | The team's recent losses have demotivated the players. 球队最近的失利让球员们失去了动力。 | synonyms: discourage, dishearten, dispirit | antonyms: motivate, encourage, inspire | verb
