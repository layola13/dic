breaststroked: /ˈbrɛststroʊkt/ | v. | 蛙泳 | Swam using the breaststroke; propelled oneself through the water using the breaststroke. |  He breaststroked across the pool. 他蛙泳游过了泳池。| 近义词: swam, propelled | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially floated/drifted) | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  "breaststroke" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Breaststroke" comes from the arm movements resembling a hug or embrace, historically referred to as "breast" stroke, distinguishing it from overhand strokes.
