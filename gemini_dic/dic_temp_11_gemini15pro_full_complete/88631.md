hmi:/ˌeɪtʃ emˈaɪ/ | n. | 人机界面 | Human-Machine Interface,  a user interface that connects a person to a machine, system, or device. | The car's HMI is very intuitive and easy to use. 这辆车的人机界面非常直观，易于使用。| The pilot used the aircraft's HMI to adjust the flight path. 飞行员使用飞机的人机界面调整飞行路径。 | synonyms: user interface, control panel, dashboard | antonyms: N/A | noun
