fetus:/ˈfiːtəs/ (US) /ˈfiːtəs/ (UK)| n. |胎儿; 胚胎|An unborn offspring of a mammal, in particular an unborn human more than eight weeks after conception.|例句：The fetus is developing normally. 胎儿发育正常。

例句：Doctors monitored the fetus's heartbeat. 医生监测胎儿的胎心。


近义词：embryo, unborn child|反义词:newborn, infant|词性:noun
