cunner:/ˈkʌnər/ | n. |康纳鱼，小隆头鱼|A small, brownish fish (Tautogolabrus adspersus) of the wrasse family, found in shallow waters of the western Atlantic. It is often used as bait.|He caught a cunner while fishing off the pier. 他在码头钓鱼时钓到了一条康纳鱼。|近义词：wrasse, bergall|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

cunner:/ˈkʌnər/ | v. |闲聊，聊天|To chat or talk idly.|The old men were cunnering on the porch. 老人们在门廊上闲聊。|近义词：chat, gossip, gab|反义词: N/A|词性:verb
