lacking-in:/ˈlækɪŋ ɪn/| adj.|缺乏的，不足的|deficient in some quality or element; wanting.  This phrase describes a deficiency in a particular area or aspect.|He is lacking in confidence. 他缺乏自信。|Synonyms: deficient in, wanting, short of, bereft of, devoid of,  |Antonyms:  rich in, full of, replete with, abundant in|adjective
