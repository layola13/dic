kufo:/ˈkuːfəʊ/| n.|空腹，空肚子|An empty stomach; a state of hunger.  This word is likely of Bantu origin and primarily used in East Africa.|Example:  I woke up with a serious kufo this morning.  今天早上我醒来时肚子很饿。|Example:  After a long hike, we all had a major kufo.  长途跋涉之后，我们都饿坏了。|Synonym: hunger, empty stomach|Antonym: satiety, fullness|noun
