alnager:/ˈɔːlnədʒər/| n.| 检验员；检查员（尤指毛织品）；（旧时）度量衡官员|A person, especially in the past, who was an official responsible for inspecting the quality and measuring the length of manufactured woolen cloth, ensuring it met the required standards.|The alnager ensured the cloth was of the proper width.|近义词：inspector, examiner, official|反义词:|noun
