decongregating:/ˌdiːkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb
分散，疏散
The act of dispersing a group of people who have congregated.
The police are decongregating the crowd after the protest. 警察正在抗议结束后疏散人群。
The students were decongregating after the school bell rang.  下课铃响后，学生们开始散开。
dispersing, scattering, breaking up
gathering, assembling, congregating
