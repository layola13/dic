autoplastic:/ˌɔːtəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
自体的；自我调整的
Relating to adaptation by altering one's own bodily processes or behavior, rather than the external environment.  A process of adaptation whereby an organism changes itself to cope with environmental demands.
例句：Autoplastic adaptation involves modifying internal processes to cope with stress.  自体适应涉及修改内部过程以应对压力。

例句：Unlike alloplastic adaptation, which involves changing the environment, autoplastic adaptation involves changing oneself. 与涉及改变环境的异塑性适应不同，自体适应涉及改变自身。
近义词：self-adjusting, self-modifying
反义词：alloplastic
词性：adjective
