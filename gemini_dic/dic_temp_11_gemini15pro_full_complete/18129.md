beholding:/bɪˈhoʊldɪŋ/ | adj. | 受恩惠的，感激的 | Obligated; indebted; owing thanks.  Derived from the now archaic verb "behold" meaning "to see, observe," it implies being in a position of gratitude for something seen or received. |  I am beholding to you for your kindness. 我非常感激你的好意。|  He felt beholding to his mentor for his success.  他觉得他的成功要归功于他的导师。|  grateful, indebted, obliged | ungrateful, unappreciative | adjective
