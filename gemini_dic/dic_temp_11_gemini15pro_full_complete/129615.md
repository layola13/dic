non-conformity: /ˌnɒn kənˈfɔːmɪti/ (British) /ˌnɑːn kənˈfɔːrməti/ (American) | n. | 不一致；不顺从；不墨守成规 |  Failure or refusal to conform to a prevailing rule or practice. |  Her non-conformity to traditional teaching methods made her a popular teacher. 她不墨守成规的教学方法使她成为受欢迎的老师。 | synonyms:  dissidence,  rebellion,  individuality,  unorthodoxy,  noncompliance | antonyms: conformity, obedience, compliance | noun
