equipaged:/ɪˈkwɪpɪdʒd/
adjective
配备的，装备的
furnished or equipped; especially : outfitted for a particular purpose
The ship was well equipaged for its voyage. 这艘船为航行做好了充分的装备。
近义词:equipped, outfitted, furnished
反义词:unequipped, bare

词性:adjective
词源:From French équipager, from équipage 'equipment', from équiper 'to equip'.
