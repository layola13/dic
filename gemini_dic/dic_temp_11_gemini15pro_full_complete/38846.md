confectioner:/kənˈfɛkʃənər/| n.|甜点师；糖果商；糖果店|A person who makes or sells sweets or candies.  A shop that sells sweets or candies.|例句：The confectioner created a magnificent wedding cake.  这位甜点师制作了一个华丽的结婚蛋糕。

例句：We bought some delicious fudge from the local confectioner. 我们从当地的糖果店买了一些美味的软糖。|近义词：candy maker, pastry chef, baker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


confectionery:/kənˈfɛkʃənəri/| n.|糖果；甜点|Sweets or candies collectively.|例句：The shop sold a wide variety of confectionery. 这家商店出售各种各样的糖果。

例句：Children love to eat confectionery. 孩子们喜欢吃糖果。|近义词：sweets, candies, treats|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *confeccioner*, from Old French *confectionnaire*, from Late Latin *confectionarius*, from Latin *confectio* (“preparation, making”), from *conficere* (“to make, prepare”), from *com-* (“together”) + *facere* (“to do, make”).
