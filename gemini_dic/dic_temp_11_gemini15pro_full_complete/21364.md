blossomless:/ˈblɑːsəmlɪs/ | adj. | 无花的，不开花的 | Lacking blossoms; without flowers. | The blossomless tree stood stark against the winter sky.  这棵无花的树在冬日的天空下显得格外荒凉。|  The old apple tree was blossomless this year due to the late frost. 由于晚霜，这棵老苹果树今年没有开花。 | barren, sterile, flowerless | blooming, flowering | adjective
