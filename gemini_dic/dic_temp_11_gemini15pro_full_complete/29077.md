carvers:/ˈkɑːrvərz/| n.|雕刻家，雕工|A person who carves wood, stone, or other materials into artistic or decorative objects.|The carvers spent months creating intricate designs on the temple walls.  这些雕刻家花了几个月的时间在庙墙上雕刻复杂的图案。|Synonyms: sculptor, engraver, artist, craftsman|Antonyms: destroyer, demolisher|Noun
carves:/kɑːrvz/| v.|雕刻，切割|To cut (a hard material) in order to produce an object, design, or inscription.|He carves beautiful figures out of wood. 他用木头雕刻出美丽的形象。|Synonyms: sculpt, engrave, chisel, whittle|Antonyms: build, construct|Verb
