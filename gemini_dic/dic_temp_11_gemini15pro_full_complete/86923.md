hemoptysis:/hiːˈmɒptɪsɪs/ (US), /hɪˈmɒptɪsɪs/ (UK)| n.|咯血；咳血|The coughing up of blood or blood-stained mucus from the bronchi, larynx, trachea, or lungs.|He was diagnosed with tuberculosis after experiencing hemoptysis.|近义词：blood-spitting, coughing up blood|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From the Greek words *haima* (αἷμα) meaning "blood" and *ptysis* (πτύσις)  meaning "spitting".
