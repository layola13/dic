motherings:/ˈmʌðərɪŋz/
n.
母爱，慈母般的照顾; (尤指动物的)母性行为; 庆祝母亲节;  (尤指猫)踩踏(主人等)，(猫)用爪子揉捏
Acts of maternal care or affection, especially among animals; the celebration of Mother's Day; the act of a cat kneading its paws on a person or object.
The cat showed its motherings by purring and kneading on my lap.  这只猫在我的腿上发出呼噜声并揉捏着，展现了它的母爱。
Her motherings extended to all the children in the neighborhood.  她的母爱延伸到邻里的所有孩子。
近义词: nurturing, cherishing, maternal instincts
反义词: neglect, indifference
词性:名词(noun)
