gasohols: /ˈɡæsəhɔːlz/ | n. |汽油醇；含酒精汽油|A mixture of gasoline and ethyl alcohol used as fuel in internal combustion engines. | Brazil is a major producer and consumer of gasohols. 巴西是汽油醇的主要生产国和消费国。| Gasohols can help reduce reliance on fossil fuels. 汽油醇有助于减少对化石燃料的依赖。|Synonyms: ethanol blends, fuel blends |Antonyms: pure gasoline, fossil fuels |noun
