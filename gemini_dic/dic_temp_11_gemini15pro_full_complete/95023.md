inaccessibility:/ɪnəkˌsesəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可接近性，难以获得|The inaccessibility of the remote mountain village made it difficult to provide aid.  这个偏远山村难以到达，使得救援工作难以进行。|例句：The information's inaccessibility frustrated researchers.  信息的难以获取让研究人员感到沮丧。

The inaccessibility of affordable healthcare is a major problem.  负担得起的医疗保健的不可及性是一个主要问题。|近义词：unavailability, impenetrability, remoteness|反义词：accessibility, availability, nearness|词性:名词

词源:  Late 16th century. From in- ‘not’ + accessible + -ity.
