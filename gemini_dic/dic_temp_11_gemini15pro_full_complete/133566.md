nonflagellate:/ˌnɒnˈflædʒɪleɪt/ | adj. | 无鞭毛的 | Lacking flagella, whip-like appendages used for locomotion in some cells and microorganisms. |  Many bacteria are nonflagellate and rely on other mechanisms for movement. 许多细菌没有鞭毛，依靠其他机制移动。

Nonflagellate algae are less mobile than their flagellated counterparts. 无鞭毛藻类比有鞭毛藻类移动性差。 | 近义词: aflagellate | 反义词: flagellate | 词性: 形容词


词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not" or "without."  From Latin *non*.
flagellate (adjective/noun): From Latin *flagellum* "whip, scourge."  Referring to the whip-like structure of flagella.
