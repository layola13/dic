epibolic:/ˌɛpɪˈbɒlɪk/ | adj. | 上包的，外包的 | Relating to or characterized by epiboly, a process in embryonic development where one layer of cells spreads over and encloses another layer. |  The epibolic movement of the ectoderm is crucial for gastrulation. 外胚层的包覆运动对原肠胚形成至关重要。

The researchers studied the epibolic closure of the neural tube. 研究人员研究了神经管的包覆闭合。 | 近义词：overgrowing, enveloping | 反义词：None | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
epi- (Greek): upon, over + -bole (Greek): a throwing, cast
