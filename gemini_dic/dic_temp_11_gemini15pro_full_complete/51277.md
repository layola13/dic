desynchronizes:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzɪz/| v. |使失去同步，不同步化|To cause to occur at different times; to cause to lose synchronization.|The sudden power outage desynchronized the server clocks. 突然断电导致服务器时钟不同步了。|近义词：disrupt, uncouple, disconnect|反义词: synchronize, coordinate|词性:动词
词源: de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + synchronize (from Greek syn- "together" + chronos "time")
