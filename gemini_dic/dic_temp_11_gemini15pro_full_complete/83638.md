haemogram:/ˈhiːməɡræm/ (US) /ˈhiːməɡræm/ (UK)| n.|血象; 血图; 血液化验单|A haemogram is a blood test that measures the levels of different types of blood cells and other components in the blood. It is used to diagnose and monitor various medical conditions.|The doctor ordered a haemogram to check her blood count. 医生要求验血以检查她的血细胞计数。|Synonyms: blood test, complete blood count (CBC), blood panel|Antonyms: N/A|noun
