desker:/ˈdɛskər/| n.|办公桌工作者；长期伏案工作的人|Someone who works at a desk, especially for long periods. |Example:  Modern offices are full of deskers who spend hours in front of computers. 现代办公室里到处都是长时间坐在电脑前的伏案工作者。

Example: Being a desker can lead to health problems if you don't take regular breaks.  如果不定时休息，长期伏案工作会导致健康问题。|近义词：office worker, white-collar worker, sedentary worker|反义词:manual laborer, blue-collar worker|词性:noun
