overbook: /ˌoʊvərˈbʊk/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈbʊk/ (UK)
verb
超额预订
to make too many reservations for (a flight, hotel, restaurant, etc.), so that more space or accommodations have been sold than are available.
The airline had overbooked the flight, and several passengers were left stranded. 这家航空公司超额预订了航班，导致几名乘客滞留。
The restaurant was overbooked, so we had to wait for a table. 餐厅超额预订了，所以我们不得不等位子。
近义词: oversell
反义词: underbook
词性: verb
