clientelage:/ˌkliːənˈtelɑːʒ/
noun
客户群，顾客，常客
The body of clients of a professional person or business.
The restaurant has a loyal clientelage. 这家餐厅拥有一批忠实的顾客。
Synonyms: clientele, customers, patrons, following
Antonyms: competitors, rivals
