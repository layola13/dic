back-track:/ˈbæktræk/ (US), /ˈbæktræk/ (UK)| v. n.|倒退，折返；取消，撤回|To return along the same path or route; to withdraw or reverse a previous statement or action.|例句：
The hikers had to backtrack when they realized they were lost.  
登山者意识到他们迷路了，不得不原路返回。

We had to backtrack to find the dropped wallet.
我们不得不折返回去找掉落的钱包。

|近义词：retreat, reverse, withdraw, u-turn|反义词：advance, proceed, continue|词性：动词，名词
