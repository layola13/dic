fjordic:/fjɔːrˈdɪk/
adjective
峡湾的；峡湾特有的
Relating to or characteristic of a fjord.
The rugged, fjordic coastline of Norway is breathtaking. 挪威崎岖的峡湾海岸线令人叹为观止。
Near synonyms: fjord-like, inlet-like
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider using descriptions of contrasting landscapes like "flat," "plain," "open," "coastal, but not fjordic")
词性: adjective
