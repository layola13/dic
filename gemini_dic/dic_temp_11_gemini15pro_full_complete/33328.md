chuffing:/ˈtʃʌfɪŋ/ | adj. | 非常高兴的，非常满意的 | very pleased or satisfied |  例句：I was chuffed to bits with my new car. 我对我的新车非常满意。| 近义词：pleased, delighted, happy, thrilled | 反义词：displeased, unhappy, dissatisfied | 词性：形容词

chuffing:/ˈtʃʌfɪŋ/ | interj. | （表示恼怒）见鬼，该死 | (Expressing annoyance) damn, dash it | 例句：Chuffing hell, I've lost my keys again! 该死的，我又把钥匙弄丢了！| 近义词：damn, blast, bother | 反义词： (No direct antonym for this usage) | 词性：感叹词


词源:  Originates from the sound of a steam engine "chuffing" which implied a sense of purposeful movement and thus, by extension, satisfaction or accomplishment.  Initially used as a dialect term, primarily in Northern England.
