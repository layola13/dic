laer: /ˈlɛər/ (This spelling is likely a misspelling.  Assuming it's meant to be "lair") | n. | 巢穴，窝点；藏身处 |A lair is a place where a wild animal, especially a dangerous one, lives or hides. | The lion retreated to its lair. 狮子退回到它的巢穴。|  The police raided the criminal's lair. 警察突袭了罪犯的窝点。 | synonyms: den, hideout, burrow | antonyms: exposure, open area | noun
