immaterialist:/ɪməˈtɪəriəlɪst/| n.|唯心主义者；精神主义者|A person who believes that material things have no independent existence and that only mind or spirit is real.|He's an immaterialist who believes the physical world is an illusion. 他是一个唯心主义者，认为物质世界是一种幻觉。|Synonyms: idealist, spiritualist|Antonyms: materialist, physicalist|noun

immaterial:/ˌɪməˈtɪəriəl/| adj.|不重要的；无关紧要的；非物质的；精神上的|Not important or relevant; not consisting of matter; spiritual.|The exact date is immaterial. 确切日期无关紧要。|Synonyms: irrelevant, unimportant, insignificant, inconsequential, nonmaterial, spiritual|Antonyms: material, relevant, important, significant, consequential, physical|adjective
