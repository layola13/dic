bibliopolist:/ˌbɪbliˈɒpəlɪst/
n.
a seller of rare or used books; a book dealer
A person who sells books, especially rare or used books.
The bibliopolist carefully examined the first edition Shakespeare.  这位书商仔细地检查了这本莎士比亚的第一版书籍。
The dusty shelves of the bibliopolist's shop held treasures waiting to be discovered.  书商满是灰尘的书架上摆放着等待被发现的珍宝。
