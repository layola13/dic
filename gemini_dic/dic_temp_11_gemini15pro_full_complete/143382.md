outfielded:/ˌaʊtˈfiːldɪd/
verb
在比赛中胜过，在球场上胜过
surpassed in fielding; performed better in the outfield (in baseball)
The young shortstop completely outfielded the veteran player.  年轻的游击手在防守上完全胜过了老将。
outplayed, surpassed, bested
underperformed
