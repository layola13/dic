echinus:/ɪˈkaɪnəs/ /ɛˈkaɪnəs/| n.|海胆；刺猬；棘皮动物|A sea urchin; a hedgehog; any small spiny mammal or any of numerous invertebrate animals, especially a sea urchin.|例句：The echinus is covered in spines. 海胆全身覆盖着刺。

例句：The ancient Greeks used the shell of the echinus as a drinking cup. 古希腊人用海胆壳做酒杯。


近义词：sea urchin, hedgehog, burr|反义词:|词性:noun

echinus:/ɪˈkaɪnəs/ /ɛˈkaɪnəs/| adj.|多刺的|Prickly; spiny.|例句：The echinus fruit is echinus to the touch. 海胆果摸起来很扎手。

例句：The cactus has an echinus surface. 仙人掌表面多刺。

近义词：spiny, prickly, thorny|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:adjective
