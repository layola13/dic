counterdemonstrating: /ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 反示威，抗议 | Engaging in a demonstration against another demonstration. |  Example: They were counterdemonstrating against the proposed cuts to education funding. 他们在反示威，抗议提议削减教育经费。| Synonym: protesting, opposing, resisting | Antonym: supporting, endorsing | verb

counterdemonstrate: /ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪt/ | v. | 反示威，抗议 | To organize or participate in a demonstration against another demonstration. | Example:  The group decided to counterdemonstrate against the hate rally.  该团体决定举行反示威来对抗仇恨集会。 | Synonym: protest against, oppose, resist | Antonym: support, endorse | verb

Etymology:
"counter-" (prefix): from Old French "contre-" and Latin "contra" meaning "against"
"demonstrate": from Latin "demonstrare" meaning "to point out, show clearly"
