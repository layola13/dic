overnormally:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməli/
adv.
超过正常地；过度正常地
Exceeding what is normal; excessively normal.
He was overnormally cautious after the accident. 事故发生后，他变得格外谨慎。
The area experienced overnormally high rainfall this year.  今年该地区的降雨量异常高。
近义词: excessively, abnormally, unusually
反义词: normally, typically, usually
词性: 副词
