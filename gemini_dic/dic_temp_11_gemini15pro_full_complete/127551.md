necropolises: /nəˈkrɒpəlɪsiːz/
n.
墓地，坟场
A large cemetery, especially one of ancient times.  A city of the dead.
The Valley of the Kings is one of the most famous necropolises in the world.  帝王谷是世界上最著名的墓地之一。
Ancient Egyptian necropolises were often elaborate and filled with treasures. 古埃及的墓地通常很精致，里面充满了宝藏。
synonyms: cemeteries, graveyards, burial grounds
antonyms: cities, towns, villages (places of the living)
noun
