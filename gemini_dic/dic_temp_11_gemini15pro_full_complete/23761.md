bravingly:/ˈbreɪvɪŋli/| adv.|勇敢地，无畏地|In a brave manner; courageously.  It describes an action performed with bravery. |She bravingly faced the raging storm. 她勇敢地面对着肆虐的风暴。|近义词：courageously, valiantly, fearlessly, boldly, daringly|反义词：cowardly, timidly, fearfully|词性:副词
Etymology: From "brave" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Brave" comes from the Old French "brave," ultimately from the Italian "bravo," meaning "wild, savage, courageous."
