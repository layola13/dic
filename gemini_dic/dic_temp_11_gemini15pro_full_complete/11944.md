arsis:/ˈɑːrsɪs/
noun
扬音，重读
The upward stroke or lift of the hand or foot in conducting music or dancing.  Also, the accented part of a musical measure or rhythmic pattern.
The conductor emphasized the arsis with a sharp upward motion of his baton.  指挥用指挥棒的急剧向上运动强调了扬音。
The arsis falls on the first beat of the measure in this piece. 这首曲子的扬音落在小节的第一拍。
