joneses:/ˈdʒoʊnziːz/
n.
与…攀比的人；(尤指) 比阔气的人
the Joneses (plural) : the people who live next door or in your neighborhood, used in the phrase "keep up with the Joneses," which means to try to have all the possessions and do all the activities that your neighbors or social equals have.
They're always trying to keep up with the Joneses. 他们总是试图跟邻居攀比。
neighbors, peers, rivals
(This word doesn't typically have an antonym)
noun
