isle:/aɪl/
n.
岛，小岛
A piece of land surrounded by water.
例句：The isle was a haven for exotic birds.  这个小岛是异国鸟类的避风港。

例句：They sailed to a deserted isle. 他们航行到一个荒岛。

近义词：island, islet
反义词:continent, mainland
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *isle (Modern French île), from Latin insula (“island”).  Doublet of island.
