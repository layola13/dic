non-geometric:/ˌnɒnˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
非几何的
Not related to or based on the principles of geometry; not having a regular or symmetrical shape.
例句：The artist preferred non-geometric shapes in her abstract paintings.  这位艺术家在她的抽象画中更喜欢非几何形状。

例句：The growth of the bacteria followed a non-geometric pattern.  细菌的生长遵循非几何模式。

近义词：irregular, asymmetrical, amorphous, shapeless
反义词：geometric, regular, symmetrical


词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "geometric" (from Greek "geometria," meaning "measurement of earth").  “non-”（前缀，意思是“不”）+ “geometric”（来自希腊语“geometria”，意思是“土地测量”）。
