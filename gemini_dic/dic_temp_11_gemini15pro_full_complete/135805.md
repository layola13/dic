nonprolixity:/ˌnɒn.prəˈlɪk.sə.ti/
n.
简洁，简明
The quality of being concise and not wordy.
His nonprolixity was a welcome change from the usual long-winded speeches.  他简洁明了的讲话受到了欢迎，与通常冗长的演讲相比，这是一个令人欣喜的变化。
conciseness, brevity, succinctness
prolixity, verbosity, wordiness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + prolixity.  Prolixity comes from Late Latin *prolixitas, from Latin prolixus "extended, long," from pro- "forth" + liqui "to flow."
