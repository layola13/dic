overdiscounted:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪsˌkaʊntɪd/
| adj. |过度贴现的|Discounted too heavily; having a price reduction that is excessive.  This often refers to financial instruments or assets.|The overdiscounted stock attracted bargain hunters.|近义词：undervalued, cheap, bargain|反义词:overvalued, expensive, premium|词性:形容词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + discount (verb meaning "reduce the price of") + -ed (suffix forming the past participle)
