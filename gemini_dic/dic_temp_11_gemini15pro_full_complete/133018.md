nonentitious:/ˌnɒnɛnˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
虚构的，不存在的；无实体的
Having no actual or real existence; not existing.
His nonentitious claims were quickly dismissed by the court. 他虚构的主张很快被法庭驳回了。
近义词: nonexistent, unreal, imaginary, fictitious, fabricated
反义词: real, existent, actual, genuine, substantial
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *non entia*, from non (“not”) + entia, neuter plural of ens (“being”).  Effectively means "not being [things]".
