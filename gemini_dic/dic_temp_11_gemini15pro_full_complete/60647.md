eclecticism: /ɪˈklɛktɪˌsɪzəm/
n.
折衷主义；博采众长
the practice of choosing what seems best from various styles, doctrines, or systems
Her house is a fascinating mix of styles, a true example of eclecticism. 她的房子是各种风格的迷人组合，是折衷主义的真实例证。
synonym:  selectivity, catholicity, all-inclusiveness
antonym:  narrowness, exclusiveness, dogmatism
词性: 名词
