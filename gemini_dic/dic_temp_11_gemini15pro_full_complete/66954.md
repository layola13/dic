experientialist:/ɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəlɪst/
n.
经验主义者; 重视经验的人
Someone who emphasizes experience.
例句：The experientialist teacher encouraged hands-on learning.  这位经验主义的老师鼓励动手学习。

例句：As an experientialist, she believed that knowledge comes from direct observation. 作为一名经验主义者，她相信知识来自直接观察。
近义词：empiricist, pragmatist
反义词：theorist, rationalist
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From experiential + -ist.  Experiential from experience + -al. Experience from Latin experientia, from experiri "to try, test."
