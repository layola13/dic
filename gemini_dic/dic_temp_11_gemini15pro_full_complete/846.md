abstracting:/æbˈstræktɪŋ/
n.|抽象化；提取；摘要|The process of considering something theoretically or separately from something else.  从理论上或与其他事物分开考虑某事的过程。|They were abstracting data from the reports. 他们正在从报告中提取数据。|近义词: summarizing, extracting, generalizing|反义词: concretizing, specifying, detailing|词性: Noun (Gerund or present participle of 'abstract')

abstracting:/æbˈstræktɪŋ/
v.|抽象；提取；概括|To consider something theoretically or separately from something else. 从理论上或与其他事物分开考虑某事。|He's abstracting the key principles from the research. 他正在从研究中提取关键原则。|近义词: extracting, summarizing, removing|反义词: adding, including, inserting|词性: Verb (Present participle of 'abstract')
