mechanize:/ˈmɛkəˌnaɪz/| v. |使机械化，用机械装置|to equip with machinery, especially in order to replace human or animal labor. |例句：The factory was mechanized to increase production. 工厂机械化以提高产量。

例句：Farming in the region has been increasingly mechanized. 该地区的农业越来越机械化了。

近义词：automate, motorize|反义词:manualize|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mécaniser*, from *mécanique* "mechanical" (see mechanic) + *-iser* (see -ize).
