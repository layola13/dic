humidly:/ˈhjuːmɪdli/ | adv. |潮湿地，湿润地 | In a humid manner; with humidity. | The air hung humidly around them. 空气潮湿地笼罩着他们。| The bathroom mirror steamed up humidly after the shower.  淋浴后浴室的镜子蒙上了一层潮气。|近义词：damply, moistly |反义词：dryly, aridly |词性: 副词

词源:
From humid + -ly.  "Humid" comes from the Latin "humidus," meaning "moist" or "wet."
