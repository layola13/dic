expressive:/ɪkˈsprɛsɪv/| adj.|富有表现力的；意味深长的|effectively conveying thought or feeling.|Her expressive eyes conveyed a deep sadness. 她富有表现力的眼睛传达出深深的悲伤。|近义词：articulate, communicative, eloquent|反义词：unexpressive, reserved, inhibited|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin expressivus, from Latin expressus (past participle of exprimere "to press out, portray"), from ex- "out" + premere "to press".
