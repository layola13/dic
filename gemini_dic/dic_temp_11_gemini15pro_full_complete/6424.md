amphopeptone:/ˌæmfoʊˈpɛptəʊn/
n.
氨基蛋白胨
A mixture of peptones derived from both animal and plant proteins, providing a broad range of nutrients for microbial growth.
Amphopeptone is a common ingredient in microbiological culture media. 氨基蛋白胨是微生物培养基中的常见成分。
Amphopeptone provides a rich source of nitrogen for bacterial growth. 氨基蛋白胨为细菌生长提供了丰富的氮源。
Synonym: tryptone, peptone
Antonym: None (lacks a direct opposite)
