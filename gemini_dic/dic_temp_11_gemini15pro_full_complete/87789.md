hibernator:/ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtər/| n. |冬眠动物|An animal that hibernates, spending the winter in a dormant state.|The hibernator awoke in the spring, thin and hungry. 冬眠的动物在春天醒来，又瘦又饿。|Near synonyms: dormant animal, winter sleeper|Antonym: non-hibernator|noun

hibernator:/ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtər/| n. |冬眠者|A person who spends a lot of time inactive, especially during the winter.|He became a hibernator during the cold months, rarely leaving his house.  在寒冷的月份里，他变成了一个冬眠者，很少离开家。|Near synonyms: homebody, recluse|Antonym: active person|noun
