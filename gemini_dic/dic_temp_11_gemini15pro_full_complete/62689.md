engenders:/ɪnˈdʒɛndərz/
verb
产生，引起
to cause or give rise to (a feeling, situation, or condition).
The issue engendered controversy.  这个问题引起了争议。
The president's speech engendered a sense of hope. 总统的演讲 engender 了希望。
Synonyms:  cause, create, produce, generate, bring about, give rise to, provoke, incite, inspire, precipitate, breed, spawn
Antonyms: prevent, stop, suppress, quell, extinguish, stifle
词性:v.
