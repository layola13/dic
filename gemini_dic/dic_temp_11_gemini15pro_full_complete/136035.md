nonreasonable:/nɒnˈriːzənəbl/ | adj. | 不合理的，不讲道理的 | Not guided by or based on good sense; unreasonable. |  His demands were completely nonreasonable. 他的要求完全不合理。| 近义词: unreasonable, illogical, irrational | 反义词: reasonable, logical, rational | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + reasonable (from Old French raisonable, from raison "reason")
