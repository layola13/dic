librarian: /laɪˈbreəriən/ | n. | 图书管理员 | A person who works in a library, organizing and managing books and other resources, and assisting library users. | The librarian helped me find a book on ancient history. 图书管理员帮我找到了一本关于古代历史的书。 |  The librarian recommended some new novels for me to read. 图书管理员推荐了一些新小说给我读。 | synonyms: archivist, curator, information specialist | antonyms: N/A | noun
