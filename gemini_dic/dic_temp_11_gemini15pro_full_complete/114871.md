mammonist: /ˈmæmənɪst/ | n. |拜金主义者；贪财者 | A person who is obsessed with wealth and material possessions; a person who values money above all else. | He was a complete mammonist, caring only for the size of his bank account. 他是一个十足的拜金主义者，只关心自己银行账户里的钱有多少。|  She became a mammonist, driven by her desire for luxury. 她变成了一个拜金主义者，被她对奢侈品的渴望所驱使。 | 近义词: materialist, mercenary, avaricious person | 反义词: philanthropist, altruist | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Mammon, the Aramaic word for wealth, personified in the New Testament as a false god, representing riches and greed.  Ultimately from Late Latin *mammōna, from Aramaic māmōnā (“riches, money”).
