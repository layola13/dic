divan's:/daɪˈvænz/
n.
长沙发，长榻
A long, low sofa, usually without arms or a back, often found in Middle Eastern countries.
The sultan reclined on his divan, surrounded by cushions.  苏丹斜倚在他的长榻上，周围都是垫子。

近义词:sofa, couch, settee
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "chair" as a contrasting piece of furniture)
词性:noun (名词)
