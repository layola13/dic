agitator:/ˈædʒɪteɪtər/| n.|搅拌器，搅拌装置|a mechanical device used for stirring or mixing liquids.|The agitator in the washing machine helps to clean the clothes thoroughly. 洗衣机里的搅拌器有助于彻底清洁衣物。|Synonyms: stirrer, mixer, whisk|Antonyms: settler, separator|noun
