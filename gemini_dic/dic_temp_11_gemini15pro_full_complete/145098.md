overappraises:/ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪzɪz/| v. |高估，过高评价|To estimate the value of something too highly.|He overappraised the value of his old car. 他高估了他的旧车的价值。|The auction house overappraised the painting, and it didn't sell. 拍卖行对这幅画的估价过高，导致它没能售出。|近义词：overestimate, overvalue|反义词: undervalue, underestimate|词性:动词
