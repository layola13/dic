mother-to-be:/ˈmʌðər tə biː/
n.
孕妇；准妈妈

例句：She's a radiant mother-to-be, expecting her first child in the spring. 她是一位容光焕发的准妈妈，预计春天迎来她的第一个孩子。

例句：The store specializes in clothing for mothers-to-be. 这家商店专门为孕妇提供服装。

近义词：expectant mother, pregnant woman
反义词: (None -  This describes a temporary state, not an inherent characteristic.)
