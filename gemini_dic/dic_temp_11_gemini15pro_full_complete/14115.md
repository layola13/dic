authenticator:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtər/ | n. | 身份验证器，认证器 | A device or process used to verify the identity of a user, device, or other entity.  It confirms that something is genuine. |  The user logged in using a two-factor authenticator. 用户使用双因素身份验证器登录。| synonym: verifier, validator | antonym:  imposter,  forger | noun

authenticator:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtər/ | n. | 认证者，鉴定者| A person who certifies the authenticity of something. | The museum curator is the authenticator of the ancient artifacts.  博物馆馆长是古代文物的鉴定者。| synonym:  expert,  specialist | antonym:  fraud,  charlatan | noun
