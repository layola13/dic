crozier: /ˈkroʊʒər/ (US), /ˈkrəʊʒə(r)/ (UK)| n.|权杖，牧杖;蕨类植物的幼芽|A ceremonial staff carried by a bishop, shaped like a shepherd's crook; a young, curled fern frond. |The bishop raised his crozier in blessing. 主教举起权杖祝福。 The emerging croziers of the ferns unfurled in the spring sunshine. 春天的阳光下，蕨类植物的新芽舒展开来。|近义词：staff, crook, (for ferns) frond|反义词: None|词性: noun

croziers: /ˈkroʊʒərz/ (US), /ˈkrəʊʒəz/ (UK)| n.|权杖，牧杖（复数）;蕨类植物的幼芽（复数）|Plural of crozier.  |The bishops' croziers were adorned with precious jewels. 主教们的权杖上装饰着珍贵的珠宝。  The forest floor was covered with the delicate croziers of ferns. 森林的地面上覆盖着蕨类植物的嫩芽。|近义词：staves, crooks, (for ferns) fronds|反义词: None|词性: noun
