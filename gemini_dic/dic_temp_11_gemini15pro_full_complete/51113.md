despisableness:/dɪˈspaɪzəblənəs/
n.
卑鄙；可鄙；可憎
The state or quality of being despicable; contemptibleness.
His despisableness was evident in his cruel treatment of animals. 他的卑鄙在他虐待动物的行为中显而易见。
contemptibleness, vileness, baseness
worth, goodness, virtue
