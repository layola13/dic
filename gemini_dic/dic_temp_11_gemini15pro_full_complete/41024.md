cope:/koʊp/ (US) /kəʊp/ (UK)| v. | 处理，应付 | to deal successfully with a difficult situation |  He was having trouble coping with the demands of his new job. 他难以应付新工作的要求。

She coped admirably with the stress of her exams. 她出色地应付了考试的压力。

近义词: manage, handle, deal with, contend with | 反义词: succumb, fail, surrender | 词性: 动词
