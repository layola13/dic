music: /ˈmjuːzɪk/ (US) /ˈmjuːzɪk/ (UK)| n. |音乐|Vocal or instrumental sounds (or both) combined in such a way as to produce beauty of form, harmony, and expression of emotion.| I love listening to music. 我喜欢听音乐。|Synonyms: melody, tune, harmony, composition |Antonyms: noise, dissonance, cacophony |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *musik, musike, from Old French musique, from Latin mūsica, from Ancient Greek μουσική (mousikḗ, “art of the Muses”), from Μοῦσα (Moûsa, “Muse”).  The Muses were Greek goddesses who presided over the arts and sciences.
