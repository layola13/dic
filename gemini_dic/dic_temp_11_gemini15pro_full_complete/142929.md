out-toil:/ˌaʊtˈtɔɪl/
verb
超越；通过更辛苦工作胜过
to work harder than someone else
He out-toiled his competitors and won the contract. 他比竞争对手更努力，赢得了合同。
近义词: outwork, surpass, beat
反义词: be outdone, lose to

词性: verb
