oersted:/ˈɜːrstɛd/ | n. | 奥斯特 | A unit of magnetic field strength in the centimeter-gram-second system of units.  One oersted is equal to 1000/4π ampere per meter. | The magnetic field strength inside the solenoid was measured in oersteds. 螺线管内部的磁场强度是用奥斯特来测量的。 | Example: The Earth's magnetic field strength is approximately 0.5 oersted.  地球的磁场强度大约为0.5奥斯特。| synonym: gauss (approximately) | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) | noun
