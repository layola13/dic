barbarian: /bɑːrˈbeəriən/ (US) /bɑːˈbeəriən/ (UK)| n. adj.|野蛮人，未开化的人；野蛮的，粗鲁的|A person considered to be uncivilized or primitive; savagely cruel; uncultured.|例句：The barbarians sacked the city. 野蛮人洗劫了这座城市。|例句：His table manners were truly barbarian. 他的餐桌礼仪真是粗鲁不堪。|近义词：savage, uncivilized, brute, vandal|反义词：civilized, cultured, refined|词性: 名词, 形容词
词源: From Latin *barbarus*, from Greek *bárbaros* "foreign, strange, ignorant," originally "babbler," referring to those who did not speak Greek (imitating their sounds).
