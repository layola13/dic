biosecurity:/ˌbaɪoʊsɪˈkjʊrəti/
n.|生物安全|Protection against harmful biological agents, including pathogens, invasive species, and toxins, that can affect human, animal, or plant health and the environment.|例句：Biosecurity measures are essential to protect our agricultural industry. 生物安全措施对于保护我们的农业至关重要。

例句：The farm has strict biosecurity protocols to prevent the spread of disease.  这个农场有严格的生物安全协议以防止疾病传播。

近义词：biosafety, bioprotection
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps vulnerability)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
bio- (life) + security
