generosity:/ˌdʒenəˈrɒsəti/ /ˌdʒenəˈrɑːsəti/| n.|慷慨，大方|The quality of being kind and generous.|Her generosity knows no bounds. 她的慷慨无边无际。|近义词：liberality, munificence, bounty|反义词：stinginess, meanness, miserliness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle French *generosite*, from Late Latin generōsitās (“nobility of birth; magnanimity”), from Latin generōsus (“of noble birth; magnanimous”), from genus (“birth, descent, race, stock, kind, sort”).
