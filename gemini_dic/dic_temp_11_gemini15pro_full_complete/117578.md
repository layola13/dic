megalocephalia:/ˌmeɡəloʊsəˈfeɪliə/| n. |巨头畸形|Abnormal enlargement of the head; a condition in which the head is abnormally large.  This is a medical term referring to a physical condition.|Example: The doctor diagnosed the child with megalocephalia after observing the excessive growth of his head. 医生在观察到孩子头部过度生长后，诊断他患有巨头畸形。

Example: Megalocephalia can be caused by a variety of underlying conditions. 巨头畸形可能由多种潜在疾病引起。|近义词:macrocephaly|反义词:microcephaly|词性:noun
