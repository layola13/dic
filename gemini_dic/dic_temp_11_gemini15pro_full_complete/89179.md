homeostat:/ˈhoʊmioʊˌstæt/ | n. |体内平衡调节器; 自我平衡系统 |A device or system that automatically regulates or stabilizes a particular condition, such as temperature or blood pressure, within a narrow range. | The human body is a complex homeostat, constantly adjusting its internal environment. 人体是一个复杂的体内平衡调节器，不断地调整其内部环境。

The thermostat acted as a homeostat, maintaining a comfortable temperature in the room.  恒温器充当体内平衡调节器，保持房间温度舒适。| synonyms: regulator, stabilizer, controller | antonyms: destabilizer | noun
