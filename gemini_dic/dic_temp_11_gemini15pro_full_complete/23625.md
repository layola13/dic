branching-out:/ˈbræntʃɪŋ aʊt/| n./ v. (phrasal verb)|拓展，扩大范围，发展新业务|The act of expanding into new areas, often in business or personal development. It implies exploring new possibilities and diversifying one's activities.|Example: The company is branching out into new markets overseas.  这家公司正在拓展海外新市场。

Example:  After working as a teacher for ten years, she decided to branch out and start her own business.  做了十年老师后，她决定拓展事业，自己创业。|近义词：expanding, diversifying, exploring, developing, extending|反义词: contracting, limiting, narrowing, specializing|词性: noun/phrasal verb (intransitive)
