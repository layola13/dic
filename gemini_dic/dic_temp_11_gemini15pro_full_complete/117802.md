melinite:/ˈmɛlɪˌnaɪt/| n. |苦味酸，黄色炸药|A high explosive consisting chiefly of picric acid.  It is a powerful explosive, used in shells and bombs.|The French used melinite extensively in World War I. 法国在第一次世界大战中广泛使用了苦味酸。|近义词：picric acid, lyddite, explosive|反义词: (No direct antonym for an explosive, perhaps consider "inert material")|词性:noun
