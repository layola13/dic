lumpiness:/ˈlʌmpɪnɪs/
n.
块状；结块；粗糙
The state or quality of being lumpy; unevenness.
The batter had a slight lumpiness to it, so I whisked it more thoroughly. 面糊有点结块，所以我更彻底地搅拌了一下。
lump, unevenness, roughness, nodularity
smoothness, evenness, uniformity
noun
