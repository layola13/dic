hipping:/ˈhɪpɪŋ/
verb
装运，运输(尤指货物)
the act of transporting goods, especially by sea or air
例句：The hipping of the goods was delayed due to bad weather. 货物的运输由于恶劣天气而延误。

例句：The company specializes in the hipping of perishable goods. 这家公司专门从事易腐烂货物的运输。
近义词：shipping, transport, freight
反义词：receiving
词性：v.
