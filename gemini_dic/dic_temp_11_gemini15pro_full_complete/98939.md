inter-applications:/ˌɪntərˌæplɪˈkeɪʃənz/| adj.| 应用程序间的，跨应用的|Describing communication or interaction between different applications or software programs.|Example: Inter-application communication is crucial for a seamless user experience. 跨应用程序通信对于无缝的用户体验至关重要。|Example: The system supports inter-application data exchange. 该系统支持应用程序间的数据交换。|近义词：cross-application, between applications|反义词: intra-application, within-application|词性:形容词
