non-unionize: /ˌnɒnˈjuːnjənaɪz/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈjuːniənaɪz/ (US)
verb
不加入工会，使不加入工会
To refrain from joining or organizing a labor union, or to prevent workers from doing so.
The company actively discouraged efforts to non-unionize its workforce.  这家公司积极阻止其员工加入工会。
Management threatened to close the factory if the workers tried to non-unionize.  如果工人试图加入工会，管理层威胁要关闭工厂。
近义词: deunionize, resist unionization
反义词: unionize, organize
词性: verb
