ergot:/ˈɜːrɡət/ | n. |麦角; 麦角菌|A fungus that grows on rye and other grains, producing alkaloids that can cause ergotism (a disease with symptoms such as convulsions and gangrene).|The ergot contaminated the rye crop, leading to a widespread outbreak of ergotism.|Synonyms: ergot fungus, spurred rye |Antonyms: N/A |Noun

ergot:/ˈɜːrɡət/ | n. |麦角碱|Any of a group of alkaloids derived from ergot, used medicinally to induce uterine contractions and to treat migraine.|The doctor prescribed ergot alkaloids to control postpartum bleeding.|Synonyms: ergotamine, ergonovine |Antonyms: N/A |Noun
