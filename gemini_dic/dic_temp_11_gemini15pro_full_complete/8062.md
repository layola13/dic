anthropometric:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|人体测量的|Relating to the measurement of the human body and its parts.|例句：Anthropometric data is used in designing ergonomic furniture. 人体测量数据用于设计符合人体工程学的家具。

例句：The study collected anthropometric measurements such as height, weight, and limb length. 这项研究收集了身高、体重和肢体长度等人体测量数据。|近义词：biometric,  morphometric|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

anthropometry:/ˌænθrəˈpɒmɪtri/| n.|人体测量学|The scientific study of the measurements and proportions of the human body.|例句：Anthropometry plays a crucial role in understanding human variation and evolution. 人体测量学在理解人类变异和进化中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Forensic anthropometry is used to identify human remains. 法医人体测量学用于识别人类遗骸。|近义词：biometrics,  morphometry|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
