inquisition:/ˌɪŋkwɪˈzɪʃən/| n.|调查, 审讯, 宗教裁判所|A period of prolonged and intensive questioning or investigation.  A former tribunal of the Roman Catholic Church (1232–1859) created to discover and suppress heresy.|The police launched an inquisition into the crime. 警方对这起犯罪展开了调查。|synonyms: investigation, inquiry, probe, examination|antonyms: disregard, neglect|noun
