holists:/ˈhoʊlɪsts/| n.|整体论者|People who believe in holism, a philosophy that emphasizes the importance of the whole and the interdependence of its parts.  A holist may apply this philosophy to medicine, ecology, education, or other fields.|Example 1: The holists believe that the human body should be treated as a whole, not just as a collection of individual parts. 整体论者认为，人体应该被视为一个整体，而不仅仅是各个部分的集合。

Example 2:  Many holists argue for sustainable practices that consider the entire ecosystem. 许多整体论者主张考虑整个生态系统的可持续实践。|近义词：generalists, universalists|反义词：reductionists, specialists|词性: noun
