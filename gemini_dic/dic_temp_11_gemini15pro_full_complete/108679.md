leaguite: /ˈliːɡaɪt/
noun
同盟者；盟友；联赛成员
A member of a league.
例句：He's a proud leaguite of the National Basketball Association. 他是美国国家篮球协会的自豪成员。

例句：The leaguites voted to accept the new rules.  联盟成员投票接受了新规则。

近义词：member, ally, associate, teammate
反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy
词性: n.


Etymology:
From league + -ite, ultimately from Latin *liga "a tying or binding," related to ligare "to bind."
