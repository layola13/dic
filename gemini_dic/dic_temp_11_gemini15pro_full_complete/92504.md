hypothesis:/haɪˈpɒθəsɪs/ (US) /haɪˈpɒθɪsɪs/ (UK)| n. |假设，假说|A supposition or proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation.|Scientists are testing the hypothesis that the virus originated in bats. 科学家们正在检验病毒起源于蝙蝠的假说。|Synonyms: theory, supposition, assumption, conjecture, premise|Antonyms: fact, proof, reality|noun
