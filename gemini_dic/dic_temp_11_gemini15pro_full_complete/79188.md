glamourless:/ˈɡlæmər.ləs/ /ˈɡlæmər.lɪs/| adj.|平凡的，乏味的，毫无魅力的|Lacking glamour; not exciting or fashionable.|例句：She led a glamourless life as a factory worker. 她过着工厂工人平凡乏味的生活。

例句：The reality of the job was far more glamourless than I had imagined.  这份工作的现实远比我想象的要乏味得多。

近义词：unglamorous, dull, drab, mundane, ordinary, humdrum|反义词: glamorous, exciting, fashionable|词性: adjective

glamour:/ˈɡlæm.ər/ /ˈɡlæm.ɚ/ |n.|魅力，吸引力，诱惑力|An attractive or exciting quality that makes certain people or things seem appealing.|例句：The city has lost its glamour. 这座城市已经失去了它的魅力。

例句：She was captivated by the glamour of Hollywood. 她被好莱坞的魅力所吸引。

近义词: allure, charm, magic, attraction|反义词: dullness, drabness|词性: noun
