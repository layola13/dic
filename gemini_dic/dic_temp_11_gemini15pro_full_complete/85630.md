haunt:/hɔːnt/ |v.  n.|萦绕，常去；鬼魂出没；(记忆等)挥之不去|To visit frequently; to appear in the form of a ghost; (of a memory, feeling, etc.) to stay in someone's mind and make them unhappy.|The old house is said to be haunted by the ghost of its former owner.  这栋老房子据说闹鬼，是以前主人的鬼魂。|Synonyms: frequent, visit, plague, torment|Antonyms: avoid, shun, desert|verb

haunt:/hɔːnt/ |n.|常去的地方；消磨时光的地方|A place that a person visits frequently or where they spend a lot of time.|The cafe is a favorite haunt of artists and writers.  这家咖啡馆是艺术家和作家们最喜欢去的地方。|Synonyms: hangout, resort, retreat|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider words like "unfamiliar place," "rarely visited location")|noun
