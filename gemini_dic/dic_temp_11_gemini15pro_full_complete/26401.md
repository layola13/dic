bycatch:/ˈbaɪkætʃ/| n.| 兼捕，误捕|The accidental capture of non-target species, such as dolphins, sea turtles, and seabirds, during fishing for a different species.  指在捕捞目标鱼种时意外捕获的非目标物种，例如海豚、海龟和海鸟。|例句：The fishermen tried to minimize bycatch by using specialized nets. 渔民们尝试使用特制渔网来减少兼捕。|近义词：incidental catch, unintended catch|反义词: target catch|词性:名词
