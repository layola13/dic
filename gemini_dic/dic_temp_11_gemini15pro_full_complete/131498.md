noncollectivistic: /ˌnɒnkəˈlɛktɪvɪstɪk/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnkəˈlɛktɪvɪstɪk/ (US)
adjective
非集体主义的
Not characterized by collectivism; emphasizing individual rights, needs, and independence over the collective good.
Individualistic societies are often described as noncollectivistic, prioritizing personal achievement.  非集体主义社会通常被描述为优先考虑个人成就。
The company's noncollectivistic culture encouraged competition among employees.  公司非集体主义的文化鼓励员工之间的竞争。

近义词: individualistic, independent
反义词: collectivist, communal
词性: adjective
