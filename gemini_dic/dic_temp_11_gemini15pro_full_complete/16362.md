barbell: /ˈbɑːrˌbel/ (US) /ˈbɑːbel/ (UK)| n.|杠铃|A long metal bar with weights attached to each end, used for weightlifting.|He lifted the barbell with ease. 他轻松地举起了杠铃。| The weightlifter added more plates to the barbell.  举重运动员在杠铃上增加了更多杠铃片。|近义词：weight, dumbbell|反义词: None|词性: noun

词源: From the early 18th century.  From bar + bell.  Originally referring to a bar with a knob or ball at each end.
