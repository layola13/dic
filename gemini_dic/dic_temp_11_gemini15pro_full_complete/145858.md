overdepressivenesses:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
过度抑郁，极度沮丧
The state of being excessively or extremely depressed.
His overdepressivenesses worried his family and friends. 他的极度沮丧令家人和朋友担忧。
近义词:extreme depression, severe melancholia
反义词:elation, euphoria
词性:noun


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "depressive" (adjective meaning "sad, gloomy") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives) + "-es" (plural suffix)
