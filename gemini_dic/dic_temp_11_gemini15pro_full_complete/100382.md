interstriven:/ˌɪntərˈstrɪvən/
adjective
互相竞争的，互相冲突的
Having striven with one another; mutually striving or contending.
例句：Their interstriven ambitions led to a bitter rivalry. 他们互相竞争的野心导致了激烈的竞争。

例句：The two companies were locked in an interstriven battle for market share.  这两家公司陷入了争夺市场份额的激烈竞争中。

近义词：competitive, conflicting, rivalrous, antagonistic
反义词：cooperative, harmonious, collaborative
词性：adjective

词源:
From inter- + striven, past participle of strive.  "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among," and "striven" means "struggled or contended." Thus, "interstriven" signifies a struggle or contention between or among entities.
