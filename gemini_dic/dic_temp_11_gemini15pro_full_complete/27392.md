camelopard:/kəˈmɛləpɑːrd/| n. |长颈鹿|An archaic word for giraffe, derived from the belief that it was a hybrid of a camel and a leopard.|例句：The camelopard gracefully stretched its long neck to reach the leaves. 长颈鹿优雅地伸长脖子去够树叶。

例句：Ancient writers described the camelopard as a curious beast with a camel's body and a leopard's spots.  古代作家将长颈鹿描述为一种奇怪的野兽，有着骆驼的身体和豹子的斑点。|近义词：giraffe|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
