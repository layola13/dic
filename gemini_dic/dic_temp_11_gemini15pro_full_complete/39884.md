constituents:/kənˈstɪtʃuənts/| n.|成分; 选民; 要素;|A constituent is a component part of something.  A constituent can also be a voter who lives in a particular area and is represented by an elected official. |The constituents of the mixture are sugar, flour, and eggs. 混合物的成分是糖、面粉和鸡蛋。|synonyms: components, elements, ingredients, voters, electors|antonyms: whole, entirety|noun
