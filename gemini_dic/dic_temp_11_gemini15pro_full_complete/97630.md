inherent:/ɪnˈhɪərənt/ /ɪnˈhɛrənt/| adj.|固有的，内在的，与生俱来的|Existing as a natural or basic part of something.|Curiosity is inherent in human nature. 好奇心是人类固有的天性.|近义词：intrinsic, innate, built-in, essential, fundamental|反义词：extrinsic, acquired, external|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin *inhaerent- ‘sticking in’, from the verb inhaerere, from in- ‘in’ + haerere ‘to stick’.
