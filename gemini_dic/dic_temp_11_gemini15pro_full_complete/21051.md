blindage:/ˈblaɪndɪdʒ/ /ˈblaɪn.dɪdʒ/ | n. |掩体，装甲，护板|A structure, especially one made of wood, earth, or sandbags, built to protect soldiers or equipment from enemy fire or attack.  A protective layer or shield, especially of metal armor, on a vehicle or structure.| The soldiers sought shelter in the blindage during the bombardment. 士兵们在炮击期间躲进了掩体。 The tank's blindage protected it from enemy fire.  坦克的装甲保护它免受敌人的炮火。|Synonyms:  protection, shield, cover, armor, fortification|Antonyms: vulnerability, exposure|noun
