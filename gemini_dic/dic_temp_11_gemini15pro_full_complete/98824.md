intellectuals: /ˌɪntɪˈlɛktʃuəlz/ | n. |知识分子，脑力劳动者| People who engage in serious study and thought, especially of a philosophical or scientific nature. |  例句：The intellectuals debated the merits of the new theory. 知识分子们就新理论的价值进行了辩论。

例句：Many intellectuals opposed the war. 许多知识分子反对这场战争。


近义词：scholars, thinkers, academics|反义词:ignoramuses, philistines|词性:noun
