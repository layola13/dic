dialoguing:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdaɪəlɔːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n. / adj. (less common)|对话，交流 / 对话式的|The process of having a conversation or discussion, especially one aimed at reaching an agreement or understanding.  Sometimes used as an adjective to describe something related to dialogue. |例句：They are dialoguing with community leaders to address the issue. 他们正在与社区领导人对话以解决这个问题。|近义词：conversing, discussing, negotiating, communicating|反义词：arguing, disputing, fighting, silencing|词性: Noun/Adjective

词源:  From the verb "dialogue" + "-ing" suffix, ultimately from the Greek "dialogos" (conversation).
