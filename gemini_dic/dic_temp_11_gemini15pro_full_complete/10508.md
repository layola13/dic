appointments:/əˈpɔɪntmənts/| n.|任命，职位|The act of appointing or the position to which one is appointed.| His appointment to the board of directors was announced yesterday. 他被任命为董事会成员的消息昨天宣布了。|synonyms: designations, nominations, selections|antonyms: dismissals, removals|noun
