autoclave: /ˈɔːtəˌkleɪv/ | n. | 高压灭菌器，高压釜 | A strong, heated container used for chemical reactions and other processes using high pressures and temperatures, especially for sterilizing medical equipment. |  例句：The surgical instruments were sterilized in the autoclave.  手术器械在高压灭菌器中消毒。| 近义词：sterilizer | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑contaminator 污染器) | 词性: 名词


autoclaves: /ˈɔːtəˌkleɪvz/ | n. | 高压灭菌器，高压釜（复数） | Plural form of autoclave. | 例句：The lab has two new autoclaves.  实验室有两个新的高压灭菌器。| 近义词：sterilizers | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 名词


词源:  From French auto- (self) + clave (key, from Latin clavis).  Originally referred to a self-locking device.
