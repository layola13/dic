bales:/beɪlz/| n.|大包，捆；（尤指）大捆的商品（如棉花、干草、稻草、羊毛、纸或废料）|Large bundles of goods such as cotton, hay, straw, wool, paper, or rags, tightly bound with cords or hoops.|The farmer stacked the bales of hay in the barn.  农民把一捆捆的干草堆放在谷仓里。

The ship carried bales of cotton. 这艘船运载着大包的棉花。|近义词：bundles, packs, stacks|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

bale:/beɪl/| n.|灾祸，不幸；痛苦|Something evil, calamitous, or painful.|His heart was filled with bale. 他的心里充满了痛苦。

The news brought a bale of woe to the family. 这个消息给这个家庭带来了不幸。|近义词：misfortune, woe, calamity|反义词: fortune, happiness|词性:名词

bale:/beɪl/| v.|打包，捆扎|To make up into a bale or bales; bind.|They baled the hay for easy storage. 他们把干草打包以便储存。

The workers baled the cotton into large bundles. 工人们把棉花捆扎成大包。|近义词：bundle, pack, bind|反义词: untie, unpack|词性:动词

词源：From Old French *bale, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch baal and German Ballen.
