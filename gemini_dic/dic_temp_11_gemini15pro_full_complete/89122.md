home-grown:/ˈhoʊmˌɡroʊn/ (American) /ˈhəʊmˌɡrəʊn/ (British)| adj.| 自家种植的；本土的；国产的| Grown at home; originating in one's own country; not foreign. | We serve only home-grown vegetables in our restaurant. 我们餐厅只供应自家种植的蔬菜。|The company is proud of its home-grown talent.  这家公司以其本土人才而自豪。| 近义词: local, domestic, indigenous | 反义词: foreign, imported | 词性: adjective

home-grown:/ˈhoʊmˌɡroʊn/ (American) /ˈhəʊmˌɡrəʊn/ (British)| n.| 自产之物；土生土长的人或物|Something grown at home; a person or thing originating in one's own country. | The market featured home-grown produce and crafts.  市场上有自产的农产品和手工艺品。| He is a home-grown talent who rose through the ranks. 他是一位土生土长的人才，一步步晋升。| 近义词: local product, native | 反义词: import, foreigner | 词性: noun
