grow-up:/ˈɡroʊ ʌp/ (US) /ˈɡrəʊ ʌp/ (UK)| n. | 成年人；成熟的人 |A person who has reached adulthood and is considered mature. |例句：He's a real grow-up now, taking responsibility for his actions. 他现在是一个真正的成年人了，对自己的行为负责。

例句：She acts like a grow-up, even though she's only ten years old. 即使她只有十岁，她也表现得像个大人。|近义词：adult, mature person|反义词：child, adolescent|词性: 名词

grow up:/ɡroʊ ˈʌp/ (US) /ɡrəʊ ˈʌp/ (UK)| vi. | 长大；成熟 |To develop from childhood to adulthood; to become mature. |例句：I grew up in a small town. 我在一个小镇长大。

例句：He needs to grow up and stop acting so childishly. 他需要长大，别再表现得这么幼稚了。|近义词：mature, develop|反义词：regress, remain childish|词性: 动词
