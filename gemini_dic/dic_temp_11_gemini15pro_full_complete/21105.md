blips:/blɪps/
n.
短暂的闪光；信号；暂时性问题；小光点
a brief, temporary signal or interruption; a small flashing light or dot on a radar screen
The radar screen showed a series of blips. 雷达屏幕上显示出一系列光点。
flash, flicker, spike, glitch
continuation, stability
noun
