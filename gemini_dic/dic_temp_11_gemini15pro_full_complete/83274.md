gymnospermous:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈspɜːrməs/
adjective
裸子植物的
Relating to or characteristic of a gymnosperm, a plant whose seeds are not enclosed within an ovary.
Gymnospermous plants, such as conifers, bear their seeds directly on the scales of cones.  裸子植物，例如针叶树，直接在球果的鳞片上结籽。
Conifers are gymnospermous plants. 针叶树是裸子植物。
近义词:  naked-seeded
反义词: angiospermous
词性: adjective
