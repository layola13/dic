apolar:/eɪˈpoʊlər/
adjective
非极性的
lacking polarity; not having poles.  Specifically, in chemistry, referring to a molecule or a part of a molecule that does not have a dipole moment because of the symmetrical arrangement of its polar bonds.
Apolar solvents like hexane dissolve apolar substances like fats and oils. 非极性溶剂（如己烷）溶解非极性物质（如脂肪和油）。
Apolar molecules do not dissolve easily in water. 非极性分子不容易溶于水。
nonpolar, non-polar
