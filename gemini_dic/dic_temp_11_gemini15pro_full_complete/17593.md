beautifiers:/ˈbjuːtɪfaɪərz/
n.
美容师；美化物；美化者
Something that beautifies or someone who applies beauty treatments.  Those products are marketed as skin beautifiers. 这些产品作为皮肤美化物销售。
She uses several different beautifiers in her daily routine. 她每天使用几种不同的美化产品。
synonyms:  cosmetics, embellishments, adornments, improvers, enhancers
antonyms:  uglifiers, disfigurements, blemishes, deformities
