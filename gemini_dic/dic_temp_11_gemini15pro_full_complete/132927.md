nonelucidative:/ˌnɒnɪˈlʊːsɪˌdeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非启发性的，不清晰的 | Not clear or helpful in explaining something; failing to elucidate. | The professor's nonelucidative explanation left the students even more confused. 教授不清晰的解释让学生们更加困惑了。|  The speaker's nonelucidative remarks only deepened the mystery surrounding the event. 演讲者不清晰的言论只加深了围绕该事件的谜团。 | 近义词: unclear, obscure, confusing, opaque, unhelpful, unilluminating | 反义词: clear, lucid, illuminating, enlightening, helpful, explanatory | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "elucidative" (from Latin "elucidare," meaning "to make clear," from "lux" meaning "light").
