heteracanth: /ˌhetərəˈkænθ/ | n. |异棘鱼; 异棘目鱼类|A fish belonging to the order Heteracanthiformes, characterized by modified spines in the dorsal fin. |Example 1: The heteracanth is a fascinating creature of the deep sea.  异棘鱼是一种迷人的深海生物。

Example 2:  Researchers discovered a new species of heteracanth. 研究人员发现了一种新的异棘鱼。|近义词：acanthodian (更广义的“棘鱼”), gnathostome (有颌脊椎动物) |反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑与软骨鱼类 cartilaginous fish 作比较) |词性: noun
