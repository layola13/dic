overdub:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌb/ | v. n. | 叠录，配音 | To record additional sound (such as music or dialogue) on an existing recording | The actor's lines were overdubbed in post-production. 演员的台词在后期制作中被叠录了。 |  The singer overdubbed the vocal track to improve the harmony. 歌手叠录了人声轨道以改善和声。 | synonyms: dub, re-record, mix | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "original recording") | verb, noun
