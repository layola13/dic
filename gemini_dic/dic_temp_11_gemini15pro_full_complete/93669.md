reimbursement:/ˌriːɪmˈbɜːrsmənt/ | n. | 报销，偿还 |The act of paying back money to someone for expenses they have incurred. |  I submitted my travel expenses for reimbursement. 我提交了我的旅行费用报销单。

| Example:  The company's reimbursement policy is very clear. 公司的报销政策非常明确。 | Synonym: compensation, repayment, refund | Antonym: charge, expense, payment | noun
