in-action:/ɪn ˈækʃən/ | adj. | 在行动中，在运行中，在起作用 | actively engaged in doing something; operating or functioning | The new software is now in-action.  新软件现在正在运行中。|  The rescue team was in-action within minutes of the call.  救援队在接到电话后几分钟内就投入了行动。 | 近义词: active, operating, working, functioning | 反义词: inactive, idle, dormant | 词性:形容词

词源:  From "in" + "action". "In" indicating a state or condition, and "action" referring to the process of doing something.
