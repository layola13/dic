misadjustment:/ˌmɪsəˈdʒʌstmənt/
n.|失调；不适应；错误调整|The misadjustment of the equipment resulted in a loss of signal. 设备的失调导致信号丢失。|
例句：
The therapist helped him overcome his emotional misadjustment.  治疗师帮助他克服了情绪失调。

His social misadjustment made it difficult for him to make friends. 他的社会适应不良使他难以交朋友。

近义词：maladjustment, imbalance, disharmony, disorder
反义词：adjustment, adaptation, harmony, balance
词性: 名词

词源：mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + adjustment (noun meaning "the act of adjusting or the state of being adjusted")
