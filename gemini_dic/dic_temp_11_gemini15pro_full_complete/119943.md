milkweed:/ˈmɪlkwiːd/| n. |乳草|A herbaceous plant that produces a milky sap and is typically toxic to mammals but serves as the primary food source for monarch butterfly caterpillars.  The sap often contains cardiac glycosides. |Monarch butterflies lay their eggs on milkweed plants.  帝王蝶把卵产在乳草植物上。| Milkweed grows along roadsides and in fields. 乳草生长在路边和田野里。|近义词：silkweed (sometimes used for specific species)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From milk + weed.  Refers to the milky sap produced by the plant.
