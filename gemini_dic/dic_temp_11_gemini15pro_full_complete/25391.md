bulchin:/ˈbʊltʃɪn/
noun
雄性小牛; (尤指一岁以下的) 小公牛
a young male bovine animal, especially one less than a year old.
The farmer separated the bulchins from their mothers. 农民把小公牛和它们的母亲分开。
Synonyms: bull calf, steer, young bull
Antonyms: cow, heifer
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bulchyn, *bolchyn, from Old English *bulċin, a diminutive of bula (“bull”). Cognate with Scots bulchin (“male calf”).
