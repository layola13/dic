amianthus:/æmiˈænθəs/
noun
石棉
a fibrous mineral, formerly used for making fireproof materials but now known to cause cancer when inhaled.
Asbestos, a fibrous silicate mineral, was widely used in construction materials for its fire-resistant properties. 石棉是一种纤维状硅酸盐矿物，因其耐火性能而被广泛用于建筑材料中。
Exposure to asbestos fibers can lead to serious health problems, including lung cancer. 暴露于石棉纤维会导致严重的健康问题，包括肺癌。
synonyms: asbestos
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a material, but perhaps consider "safe insulation" in context)
