colocynth:/ˈkɒləsɪnθ/ /ˈkɑːləsɪnθ/| n.|药葫芦；药葫芦的果实；药葫芦的提取物|A Mediterranean and Asian trailing plant (Citrullus colocynthis) of the gourd family, having hairy stems, large-lobed leaves, yellow flowers, and a fruit the size of an orange with a hard rind and a bitter pulp formerly used medicinally as a purgative.|Colocynth is a powerful purgative and should be used with caution. 药葫芦是一种强泻药，应谨慎使用。|The colocynth plant bears a fruit resembling a small orange. 药葫芦植物结出类似小橙子的果实。|Synonyms: bitter apple, bitter cucumber, coloquintida|Antonyms: None|Noun
