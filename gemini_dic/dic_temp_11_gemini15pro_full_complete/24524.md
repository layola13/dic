broadcloth:/ˈbrɔːdklɔːθ/ /ˈbrɑːdklɔːθ/
noun
细棉布，宽幅棉布
A finely woven cotton or linen fabric, typically used for shirts or dresses.
She wore a dress made of crisp white broadcloth. 她穿着一件挺括的白细棉布连衣裙。
synonyms: poplin, linen, cotton, cambric
antonyms: burlap, canvas, twill
n.

broadcloth:/ˈbrɔːdklɔːθ/ /ˈbrɑːdklɔːθ/
adjective
宽幅的
(of fabric) woven in a wide width.
Broadcloth is often used for making bed sheets. 宽幅棉布常用来制作床单。
synonyms: wide, broad
antonyms: narrow, thin
adj.
