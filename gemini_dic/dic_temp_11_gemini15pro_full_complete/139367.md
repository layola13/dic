obscuring:/əbˈskjʊərɪŋ/
|v. |使模糊，遮掩|Making something difficult to see or understand clearly. |The fog was obscuring the view of the mountains. 浓雾遮蔽了山景。|近义词：hiding, concealing, covering, veiling, shrouding|反义词: revealing, clarifying, exposing, illuminating|Verb

obscuring:/əbˈskjʊərɪŋ/
|adj. |模糊的，遮蔽的|Acting to obscure something. |The obscuring mist made driving dangerous.  遮蔽的薄雾使驾驶危险。|近义词：hiding, concealing, covering, veiling, shrouding|反义词: revealing, clarifying, exposing, illuminating|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin obscurans, present participle of obscurare "to darken," from Latin obscurus "dark, dim, indistinct."
