glumness:/ˈɡlʌmnəs/| n. |忧郁，闷闷不乐，沮丧|A state of being sad and quiet; a feeling of depression. |例句：A heavy glumness settled over the room after the bad news. 坏消息传来后，房间里弥漫着沉重的忧郁气氛。

例句：Her glumness was evident in her downcast eyes and quiet demeanor.  她的忧郁在她低垂的眼睛和安静的举止中显而易见。|近义词：dejection, gloom, melancholy, sadness, despondency|反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, joy, lightheartedness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From glum + -ness.  "Glum" originates from the Middle Low German "glum," meaning "gloomy" or "sullen."
