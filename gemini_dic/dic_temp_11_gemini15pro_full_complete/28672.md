cariole:/ˈkæriˌoʊl/ (US), /ˈkæriɒl/ (UK)| n.|轻便马车；单马双轮车|A small, light, open carriage, usually two-wheeled and drawn by one horse.|例句：The young couple rode through the park in a cariole. 这对年轻夫妇乘坐一辆轻便马车穿过公园。

例句：He hired a cariole to take him to the neighboring village. 他租了一辆轻便马车去邻村。


近义词：carriage, buggy, gig|反义词:|n.
