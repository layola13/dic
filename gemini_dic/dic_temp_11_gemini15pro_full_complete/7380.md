animalish:/ˈænɪməlɪʃ/
adjective
动物似的；野兽般的；兽性的
Resembling or characteristic of an animal, typically in a negative or derogatory way; beastly.
He had an animalish appetite, devouring everything in sight. 他有着野兽般的食欲，吞噬了眼前的一切。
She displayed an animalish rage when provoked.  被激怒时，她表现出兽性的愤怒。
近义词:beastly, bestial, brutish, feral, savage
反义词:human, humane, civilized, refined, cultured
词性:adjective
