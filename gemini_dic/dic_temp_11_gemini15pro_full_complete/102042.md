isolations:/ˌaɪsəˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 隔离；孤立；分离 |The act of isolating something or someone; the state of being isolated. | The government imposed strict isolations during the pandemic. 政府在大流行期间实施了严格的隔离。|  The patient was kept in isolation to prevent the spread of infection.  病人被隔离以防止感染传播。 | 近义词：separation, segregation, quarantine | 反义词：integration, connection, inclusion | 词性：名词

词源: From Late Latin *isolationem,  from isolatus, past participle of isolare "to isolate,"  from insula "island."
