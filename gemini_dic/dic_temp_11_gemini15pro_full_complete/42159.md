couchette:/kuːˈʃet/ | n. |卧铺（尤指火车上的）|A narrow bed or bunk on a train or ship, especially one in a passenger compartment. |例句：I booked a couchette on the overnight train to Paris. 我在开往巴黎的夜班火车上订了一个卧铺。

例句：The couchette was surprisingly comfortable.  这卧铺出奇地舒适。|近义词：berth, bunk |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cabin" or "compartment" for a more private sleeping arrangement) |词性: noun
