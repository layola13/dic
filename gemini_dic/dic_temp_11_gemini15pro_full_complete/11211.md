ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *architecture*, from Latin *architectura*, from Ancient Greek *ἀρχιτεκτονική (arkhitektonikḗ)*, from *ἀρχιτέκτων (arkhitéktōn)* meaning "master builder, director of works," from *ἀρχι- (arkhi-)* meaning "chief, master" + *τέκτων (téktōn)* meaning "builder, carpenter."
