butted:/ˈbʌtɪd/
verb
顶撞，用头撞
To strike or push with the head or horns.
The goat butted the fence. 山羊用头撞击篱笆。
近义词：rammed, bumped, hit
反义词：pulled, withdrew
词性：v.


butted:/ˈbʌtɪd/
adjective
有粗端的，装有把手的
Having a thicker end or a handle fitted.
a butted axe.  一把装有把手的斧头。
近义词：handled, gripped
反义词：pointed, sharpened
词性：adj.
