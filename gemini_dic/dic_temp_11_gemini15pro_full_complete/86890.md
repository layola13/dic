hemisphered:/ˈhɛmɪsfɪəd/
adjective
半球形的；分为两半球的
Shaped like a hemisphere or divided into hemispheres.
The hemisphered brain controls different functions.  大脑的两个半球控制着不同的功能。
The artist created a hemisphered sculpture. 艺术家创作了一个半球形的雕塑。
Near Synonyms:  semicircular, dome-shaped
Near Antonyms:  spherical, globular
Etymology: From hemisphere + -ed
