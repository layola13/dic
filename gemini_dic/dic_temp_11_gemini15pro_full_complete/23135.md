bournonite:/ˈbʊərnəˌnaɪt/| n. |车轮矿|A steel-gray, metallic sulfide mineral, PbCuSbS₃, crystallizing in the orthorhombic system.|例句：Bournonite is a complex sulfide mineral. 车轮矿是一种复杂的硫化物矿物。

例句：The miners discovered a vein of bournonite. 矿工们发现了一条车轮矿矿脉。


|近义词：chalcostibite, tetrahedrite (similar composition)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
