hyperhilarity:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈlærəti/| n. | 极度兴奋，狂喜 |  A state of extreme hilarity or excessive mirth.  |  The comedian's jokes induced hyperhilarity in the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话引起了观众的狂喜。 | synonyms: exuberance, elation, euphoria | antonyms: gloom, depression, melancholy | noun

Etymology:  From hyper- "excessive" + hilarity.  "Hilarity" comes from Latin "hilaritas" meaning "cheerfulness," from "hilaris" meaning "cheerful."
