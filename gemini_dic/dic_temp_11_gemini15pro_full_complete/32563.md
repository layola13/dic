chippy's:/ˈtʃɪpiz/| n.|炸鱼薯条店；木匠的工棚|A chippy's is a shop that sells fish and chips. 炸鱼薯条店是一家出售炸鱼薯条的商店。|例句：Let's go to the chippy's for dinner. 让我们去炸鱼薯条店吃晚饭。|近义词：fish and chip shop, chipper (chiefly British)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:名词

chippy's:/ˈtʃɪpiz/| n.|木匠的工棚|A chippy's can also refer to a carpenter's workshop.  chippy's也可以指木匠的工棚。|例句：He's working in the chippy's today. 他今天在木匠工棚里工作。|近义词：workshop, shed|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:名词
