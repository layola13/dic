v.|概念化，将…概念化|The process of forming a concept or idea.  This involves abstracting and generalizing from specific instances to create a mental representation.|She is conceptualizing a new marketing strategy. 她正在构思一个新的营销策略。|conceptualise, envision, imagine| disregard, ignore | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conceptuālis*, from Latin conceptus (“a conceiving, a thought, purpose”), past participle of concipiō (“conceive”). Equivalent to concept +‎ -ual. First attested in the mid-17th century.
