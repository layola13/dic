ophiuchus:/ˌɒfiˈjuːkəs/| n. |蛇夫座|A constellation straddling the celestial equator, representing a man grasping a serpent. It is sometimes considered the 13th sign of the zodiac.|例句：Ophiuchus is a large constellation located near Sagittarius and Scorpius. 蛇夫座是一个位于人马座和天蝎座附近的大星座。

例句：Some astrologers believe Ophiuchus should be included in the zodiac. 一些占星家认为蛇夫座应该被纳入黄道十二宫。|近义词：Serpent-bearer (字面翻译)|反义词:  None|词性:noun
