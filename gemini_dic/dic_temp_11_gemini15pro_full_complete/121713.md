misprinting:/ˌmɪsˈprɪntɪŋ/
noun
印刷错误，印错
an error introduced into text during printing.
The book contained several misprintings that made it difficult to understand. 这本书里有好几个印刷错误，使它难以理解。
misprint, typographical error, erratum
correct printing
n.
