abidingnesses:/əˈbaɪdɪŋnəsɪz/| n. |持久性，持续性|The state or quality of being abiding; lastingness.  It refers to the enduring nature of something, like a feeling or a belief.|His abidingnesses to his principles impressed everyone. 他对原则的坚持给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：permanence, durability, steadfastness|反义词：transience, ephemerality, instability|词性:名词
Etymology: From abiding + -ness.  Abiding comes from the Middle English abiden, from Old English ābīdan, meaning "to remain, continue, await."
