fernticle: /ˈfɜːrn.tɪ.kəl/ | n. |蕨类植物的小叶; 羽片 |A small leaflet or division of a fern frond. |The delicate fernticles unfurled in the spring sunshine. 春天的阳光下，精巧的蕨类植物小叶舒展开来。|Synonyms: pinnule, leaflet, segment|Antonyms: frond (arguably, as the whole vs. the part)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *fern* + *-icle* (diminutive suffix).  *Fern* comes from Old English *fearn*.  *-icle* comes from Latin *-iculus*, a diminutive suffix.
