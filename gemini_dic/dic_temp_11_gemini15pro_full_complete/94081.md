impasti:/ɪmˈpɑːsti/ /ɪmˈpæsti/| n.| (尤指绘画的)厚涂颜料，肌理; (食物的) 混合物，面团|A thick application of paint, as in impasto painting, or the texture produced by this technique. Also, a mixture, especially of flour and water, used in making pasta or other food. | The artist used heavy impasto to create a textured surface.  这位艺术家用厚涂颜料来创作有纹理的表面。

The chef carefully kneaded the impasto for the pizza dough. 厨师仔细地揉披萨面团。|近义词：texture, consistency, mixture, blend, compound (for food)|反义词: thinness (for paint application)|词性: noun
