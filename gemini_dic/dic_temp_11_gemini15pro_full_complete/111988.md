lone:/loʊn/| adj.|孤独的，单独的，独自的|Being the only one; solitary.  Existing or acting in isolation from others.|He was a lone wolf, preferring to work by himself. 他是一匹独狼，喜欢独自工作。|Example: The lone tree stood on the hill. 孤独的树立在山上。|近义词：solitary, single, alone, isolated|反义词：accompanied, together, numerous|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *lone, *allone (“alone”), from Old English ānlīne (“only, sole, single”), alteration of ānlic (“only, sole, singular”), from ān (“one”) + -līc (“-ly”). Cognate with Scots alane (“alone”), Saterland Frisian alleen (“alone”), West Frisian allinne (“alone”), Dutch alleen (“alone”), Low German alleen (“alone”), German allein (“alone”). More at one, -ly.
