certitudes:/ˈsɜːrtɪtjuːdz/
n.
确定性；必然性；确信的事情
Something that someone believes completely; the state of mind of being completely sure
例句：She spoke with absolute certitude about the future of the company. 她对公司的未来充满信心。

例句：He had no certitude about what he wanted to do in life. 他对自己的人生目标没有确切的想法。


近义词：certainties, convictions, assurances
反义词：doubts, uncertainties, ambiguities
词性：名词 (Noun)
