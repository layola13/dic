exorcisory:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzəri/ /ˈɛksɔːrsɪzəri/
| adj. |驱魔的，驱邪的 |Relating to or used in exorcism; having the power to expel evil spirits. |The priest performed an exorcisory ritual.  神父举行了驱魔仪式。|Synonyms: cleansing, purifying, expelling |Antonyms:  blessing, consecrating

exorcism:/ˈɛksɔːrsɪzəm/ /ˈɛksɔːrsɪzəm/
| n. |驱魔，驱邪 |The act of expelling evil spirits from a person or place. |The family called a priest to perform an exorcism. 这家人请了一位神父来驱魔。|Synonyms: purification, cleansing, expulsion |Antonyms: blessing, consecration
