lithoclast: /ˈlɪθəˌklæst/ | n. | 岩屑；碎石；石屑 | A fragment of rock formed by the mechanical breaking of a larger rock mass. |  The geologist examined the lithoclasts in the sedimentary rock to determine its source.  地质学家检查了沉积岩中的岩屑，以确定其来源。| Synonyms: rock fragment, clast, detritus | Antonyms: bedrock, solid rock | noun
