abolitionist:/ˌæbəˈlɪʃənɪst/
n.
废奴主义者
A person who favors the abolition of a practice or institution, especially capital punishment or (formerly) slavery.
Abraham Lincoln was not an abolitionist, but he opposed the expansion of slavery. 亚伯拉罕·林肯不是废奴主义者，但他反对奴隶制的扩张。
synonyms: reformer, crusader, activist, advocate
antonyms:proslavery, slaveholder
noun
