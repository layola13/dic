disserved:/ˌdɪsˈsɜːvd/
verb
中文翻译：损害，伤害，帮倒忙
英文解析：to do something that harms or hinders someone or something
例句：Her attempts to help him only disserved him. 她试图帮助他，却适得其反。

例句：The new policy disserved the interests of small businesses. 新政策损害了小企业的利益。

近义词：harmed, hindered, damaged, impaired, undermined
反义词：helped, aided, assisted, benefited, supported
词性：v.
