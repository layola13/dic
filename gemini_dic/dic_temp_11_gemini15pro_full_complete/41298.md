cordoned:/ˈkɔːrdənd/ (US) /ˈkɔːdɒnd/ (UK)| adj.|被封锁的，被隔离的|Enclosed or marked off with a cordon, typically to prevent access or to enclose an area considered dangerous or contaminated.|例句：The crime scene was cordoned off by police.  犯罪现场被警察封锁了。

The entire block was cordoned off after the gas leak.  煤气泄漏后，整个街区都被封锁了。|近义词：blocked, sealed, closed, restricted, barricaded|反义词：open, accessible, unrestricted|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "cordon" + "-ed". "Cordon" comes from the French word "cordon," meaning "string" or "cord," ultimately from the Late Latin "chorda."
