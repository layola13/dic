knolly:/ˈnɒli/| n.|小丘，圆丘|A small rounded hill or hillock.|We climbed up the knolly to get a better view of the valley. 我们爬上小山丘，以便更好地欣赏山谷的景色。|Example: The sheep grazed peacefully on the knolly. 羊群在小山上安静地吃草。|Synonym: hillock, mound, knoll|Antonym: valley, hollow|Noun
