escapeless:/ɪˈskeɪplɪs/
adjective
不可避免的，无法逃脱的
Unable to be avoided or escaped from.
The prisoners found themselves in an escapeless situation. 囚犯们发现自己处于无法逃脱的境地。

Her feeling of guilt was deep and escapeless. 她深深地感到内疚，无法摆脱。
Synonym: unavoidable, inescapable, inevitable
Antonym: avoidable, escapable
词性: 形容词
