disrespect:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspekt/| n. vt.|不尊重，轻视|lack of respect; to treat without respect.|例句：He showed disrespect to his elders. 他对长辈表示不尊重。

例句：They disrespected the rules and traditions of the community. 他们不尊重社区的规则和传统。|近义词：contempt, disdain, scorn|反义词:respect, reverence, honor|词性:名词，动词


词源:dis- (prefix expressing negation) + respect (from Latin respectus, "regard, consideration")
