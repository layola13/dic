ministration:/ˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
n.
管理；执行；服务；政府
the process of providing care, treatment, or service to someone or something; the process of applying or giving something to someone.
The ministration of the new president brought about significant changes. 新总统的执政带来了重大变化。

The nurse's gentle ministration eased the patient's pain.  护士的细心照料减轻了病人的痛苦。
近义词：administration, management, governance, service, care
反义词：neglect, inaction
