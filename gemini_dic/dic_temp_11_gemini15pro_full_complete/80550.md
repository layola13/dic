goodo:/ˈɡʊdoʊ/
interj.
好极了;太好了;可以;行
An exclamation of satisfaction or agreement.
例句：Right, goodo, I'll see you there then. 好，行，那我到时候见。

例句：Goodo, that's settled then. 好，那就这么说定了。

近义词：okay, alright, fine, excellent, splendid
反义词：bad, terrible, awful
词性：感叹词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "good" + "-o" (a suffix used to form colloquial words).  First recorded in the late 19th century, primarily in British and Australian English.
