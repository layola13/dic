arrected: /əˈrɛstɪd/
Verb (past participle)
被逮捕的；被拘留的
Stopped by the police for a crime.
The suspect was arrected at the scene of the crime. 嫌疑犯在犯罪现场被捕。
arrested: The escaped prisoner was arrested after a week on the run. 逃犯潜逃一周后被捕。

近义词: apprehended, captured, detained, seized
反义词: released, freed
