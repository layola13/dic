antievolutionally:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənəli/
adverb|反进化地|In a way that opposes or resists the theory of evolution.|His views were interpreted antievolutionally by some religious groups. 他的一些宗教团体将他的观点解释为反进化的。|
近义词: anti-Darwinian, creationist|
反义词: evolutionarily, pro-evolution|
词性: adverb

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek)
evolution (noun): the process by which different kinds of living organisms are thought to have developed and diversified from earlier forms during the history of the earth. (from Latin evolutio, meaning "unrolling")
-ary (suffix): relating to or connected with
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
