begem:/bɪˈdʒɛm/
verb
镶嵌宝石；点缀
adorn or encrust (something) with or as with gems.
The crown was begemmed with diamonds and rubies.  王冠上镶嵌着钻石和红宝石。
近义词:adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, stud
反义词:strip, denude
词性:v.
