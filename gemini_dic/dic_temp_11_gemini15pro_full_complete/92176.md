hyperspeculatively:/ˌhaɪpərˈspɛkjʊlətɪvli/
adverb
过度推测地；极度投机地
in a way that involves a lot of speculation, especially of an extreme kind.
The project was funded hyperspeculatively, with no guarantee of return. 这个项目是过度投机性地资助的，没有任何回报保证。
Hyperspeculatively investing in volatile stocks can be risky. 对波动性股票进行过度投机性投资可能会有风险。
Synonyms: excessively speculatively, wildly speculatively, extremely conjecturally
Antonyms: cautiously, prudently, conservatively
词性: 副词
