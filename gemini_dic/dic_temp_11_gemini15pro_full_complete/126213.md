my-bad:/maɪ bæd/ | interjection | 我的错；不好意思 | An informal way to admit a mistake or apologize, similar to "oops" or "sorry". | Example 1: "My bad, I didn't see you there." 我的错，我没看到你在那里。

Example 2: "Oh, my bad, I spilled your drink." 哦，我的错，我洒了你的饮料。| 近义词: sorry, oops, my fault | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an apology) | 词性: interjection
