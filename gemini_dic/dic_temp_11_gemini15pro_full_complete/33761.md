circulate: /ˈsɜːrkjəleɪt/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjʊleɪt/ (UK)
verb
循环，流通，传播
to move around or through something, or to make something do this.  To spread or be spread around.
Blood circulates through the body. 血液在体内循环。
The rumor circulated quickly through the office. 谣言在办公室迅速传播。
近义词: spread, distribute, disseminate, flow, travel
反义词: stagnate, restrict, confine
词性: verb

circulate: /ˈsɜːrkjəleɪt/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjʊleɪt/ (UK)
noun
流通，传播
the act of circulating something.
The library has a good circulation of books. 图书馆的书籍流通情况很好。
近义词: distribution, dissemination, flow
反义词: stagnation, restriction
词性: noun
