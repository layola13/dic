invitingness:/ɪnˈvaɪtɪŋnɪs/
n.
魅力；吸引力；诱惑力
The quality of being inviting; attractiveness or allure.
Her invitingness made it easy to make friends. 她的魅力让她很容易交到朋友。
近义词：attractiveness, appeal, allure, charm, magnetism
反义词：unattractiveness, repulsiveness, coldness, hostility
词性: 名词

invite:/ɪnˈvaɪt/
v.
邀请
To request the presence or participation of.
I invited him to dinner. 我邀请他共进晚餐。
近义词：request, ask, summon, bid
反义词：exclude, reject, turn away
词性: 动词

inviting:/ɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ/
adj.
吸引人的；诱人的
Attractive or tempting; enticing.
The smell of freshly baked bread was inviting.  刚烤好的面包的香味很诱人。
近义词：appealing, attractive, tempting, alluring, enticing
反义词：unappealing, unattractive, repellent, off-putting
词性: 形容词
