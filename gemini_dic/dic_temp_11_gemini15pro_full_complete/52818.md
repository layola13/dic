diluted:/daɪˈluːtɪd/ | adj. | 稀释的，冲淡的 | weakened in force, intensity, or value by admixture of something less essential | The company's earnings were diluted by the recent acquisition. 该公司的收益因最近的收购而被稀释了。| synonyms: weakened, thinned, attenuated | antonyms: concentrated, strengthened, intensified | adjective

dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ | v. | 稀释，冲淡 | make (a liquid) thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent to it. |  Remember to dilute the juice with water before drinking it. 记得喝果汁前用水稀释一下。| synonyms: weaken, thin, attenuate | antonyms: concentrate, strengthen, intensify | verb

Etymology:
From Latin *diluere, from di- "apart, thoroughly" + luere "to wash."
