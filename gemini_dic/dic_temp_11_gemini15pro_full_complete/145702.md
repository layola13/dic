overcounting:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊntɪŋ/ | n. | 过度计数，重复计数 | The act of counting something more than once, leading to an inaccurate total. |  Example: Overcounting can occur when multiple people count the same items without coordination.  重复计数会在多人没有协调的情况下计算相同的项目时发生。


Example:  We avoided overcounting by assigning each team member a specific section to count. 我们通过给每个团队成员分配一个特定的区域来计数，从而避免了重复计数。 | Near Synonyms: double counting, multiple counting | Antonyms: undercounting | Noun
