healthward:/ˈhɛlθwərd/| adv. adj.|向着健康的方向，促进健康的|Moving or tending toward better health.  Toward a state of health or recovery.|例句：He was healthward bound after a long illness.  他久病之后，正朝着康复的方向发展。|近义词：improving, recovering, healing|反义词: declining, worsening|词性:副词，形容词

healthward:/ˈhɛlθwərd/| adj.|促进健康的|Contributing to or promoting health.|例句：The healthward effects of exercise are well known. 运动对健康的好处是众所周知的。|近义词：healthful, salutary, beneficial|反义词: unhealthy, detrimental|词性:形容词
