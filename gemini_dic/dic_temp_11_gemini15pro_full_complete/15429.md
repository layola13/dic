bacterioscopy:/bækˌtɪəriˈɒskəpi/| n. |细菌镜检; 细菌镜检术|The examination of bacteria by means of a microscope.| Doctors used bacterioscopy to identify the cause of the infection. 医生们用细菌镜检来确定感染的原因。|microscopic examination of bacteria, bacterial examination| N/A |noun
Etymology:  From bacterio- (bacteria) + -scopy (viewing, examination). Ultimately from Greek *baktērion* (small staff, rod) + *-skopein* (to look at).
