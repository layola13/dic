mutilate:/ˈmjuːtɪleɪt/| v. |残害，毁坏|To inflict serious damage on something, especially by cutting or tearing.|The documents had been deliberately mutilated. 这些文件被故意损毁了。|Synonyms: damage, injure, disfigure|Antonyms: repair, restore, heal|Verb
