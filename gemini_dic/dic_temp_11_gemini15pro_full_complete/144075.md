outrun:/ˌaʊtˈrʌn/ | v. | 超越，超过；跑得比…快；逃脱；超过…的限度 | From out- + run.  Originally meaning to run faster than. The sense of exceeding a limit is from the late 19th century. | The marathon runner outran his competitors in the last mile. 马拉松选手在最后一英里超过了他的竞争对手。 | 近义词: surpass, exceed, outstrip, overtake | 反义词: lag behind, trail | 词性: 动词

outrun:/ˌaʊtˈrʌn/ | n. | 超越的距离；领先的程度 (较少用作名词) |  The noun form is less common but refers to the extent to which something outruns another. | The outrun of the winner was considerable. 获胜者的领先程度相当大.  (This usage is less common.) | 近义词: lead, advantage | 反义词: deficit, disadvantage | 词性: 名词
