overcharity:/ˌōvərˈtʃærɪti/
n.
过度慈善；过分的仁慈
Excessive charity or generosity, often to the point of being detrimental.
He showed overcharity to his employees, often overlooking their mistakes, which led to a decline in productivity. 他对员工过度仁慈，经常忽视他们的错误，导致生产力下降。
近义词：excessive generosity, overindulgence
反义词：stinginess, miserliness
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + charity.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Charity" comes from Old French "charité" and ultimately from Latin "caritas," meaning "love, dearness."
