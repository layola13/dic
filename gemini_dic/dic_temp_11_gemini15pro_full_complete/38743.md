condolement:/kənˈdoʊləmənt/
n.
哀悼，慰问
an expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a death
We offer our sincere condolences to his family. 我们向他的家人表示诚挚的慰问。
sympathy, commiseration, solace
celebration, felicitation
noun
