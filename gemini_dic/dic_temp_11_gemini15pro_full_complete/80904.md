governs:/ˈɡʌvərnz/
verb
统治，管理，支配
controls and directs the public business of a country, city, group of people, etc.
The prime minister governs the country. 首相治理国家。
synonyms: rules, controls, manages, administers, directs, leads, commands, presides over, reigns over
antonyms: obeys, follows, submits
