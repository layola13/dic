hypovitaminosis:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌvaɪtəmɪˈnoʊsɪs/
n.
维生素缺乏症
A condition in which the body lacks one or more vitamins.  This can lead to a variety of health problems depending on the specific vitamin deficiency.
The doctor diagnosed her with hypovitaminosis D. 医生诊断她患有维生素D缺乏症。
近义词:vitamin deficiency, avitaminosis (in severe cases)
反义词:hypervitaminosis
词性:noun
