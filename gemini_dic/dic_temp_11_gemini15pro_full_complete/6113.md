ametabola:/əˌmɛtəˈboʊlə/
| n. |无变态类，无变态昆虫| Insects that undergo little or no metamorphosis, such as silverfish and springtails.  They hatch from the egg resembling miniature adults and molt repeatedly until they reach adult size.| Silverfish are ametabola and don't change much in form as they grow. 衣鱼是无变态昆虫，在生长过程中形态变化不大。| 近义词: apterygota | 反义词: metabola | 词性: 名词
