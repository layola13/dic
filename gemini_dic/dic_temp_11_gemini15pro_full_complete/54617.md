dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/ |v. |肢解，分割，分裂|To cut or tear (a body) apart limb from limb; to divide into pieces. |The serial killer dismembered his victims and buried the parts in different locations. 连环杀手肢解了他的受害者，并将尸块埋在不同的地方。|例句：The empire was dismembered after the war. 战后帝国被分裂了。| 近义词: mutilate, dissect, sever | 反义词: assemble, unite| verb

dismembered:/dɪsˈmɛmbərd/ |adj. |被肢解的，被分割的|Having been cut or torn apart limb from limb; divided into pieces. |The dismembered body was found scattered across the field.  被肢解的尸体散落在田野里。 |例句：The dismembered company was sold off in parts.  被分割的公司被分批出售。|近义词: mutilated, severed | 反义词: whole, intact | adjective
