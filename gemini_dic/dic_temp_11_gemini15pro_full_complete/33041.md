chotis:/ˈtʃoʊtiːs/| n. |乔蒂斯舞曲，一种西班牙双人舞|A lively Spanish dance for two, similar to the seguidilla.|They danced a chotis at the fiesta. 他们在节日上跳了乔蒂斯舞曲。|近义词：seguidilla, jota|反义词: None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Spanish *chotis*, possibly from German *Schottisch* (“Scottish”), referring to a type of dance.
