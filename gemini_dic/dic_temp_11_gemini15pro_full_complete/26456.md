bystander:/ˈbaɪˌstændər/| n.|旁观者，局外人|A person who is present at an event or incident but does not take part.|例句：Many bystanders witnessed the accident. 许多旁观者目睹了这起事故。|例句：He remained a bystander, watching the argument unfold. 他仍然是一个旁观者，看着争论展开。|近义词：onlooker, observer, witness|反义词：participant, intervener|词性:noun
