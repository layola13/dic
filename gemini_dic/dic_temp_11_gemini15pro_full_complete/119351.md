microcinematography:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌsɪnɪməˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |显微电影术，显微电影摄影|The process of making films through a microscope, recording the activities of microscopic organisms or processes. | Microcinematography allows us to observe cellular processes in real time. 显微电影摄影使我们能够实时观察细胞过程。|Synonyms: time-lapse microscopy, microphotography | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps macroscopy) | noun


microcinematography:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌsɪnɪməˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |显微电影摄影|The technique of making motion pictures of microscopic objects or phenomena. | The researchers used microcinematography to study the movement of bacteria. 研究人员使用显微电影摄影来研究细菌的运动。|Synonyms: cinephotomicrography | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun
