impostor:/ɪmˈpɑːstər/ (US), /ɪmˈpɒstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |冒名顶替者，骗子|A person who pretends to be someone else in order to deceive others.|He was exposed as an impostor, claiming to be a doctor when he had no medical training. 他被揭露是一个冒名顶替者，声称自己是医生，而实际上他没有接受过任何医学训练。|The impostor tricked the elderly woman out of her life savings.  这个骗子骗走了老妇人的毕生积蓄。|近义词：fraud, charlatan, fake, pretender|反义词:genuine person, honest person|词性:noun
