deictic:/ˈdaɪktɪk/ | adj. | 指示的，指示性的 | Relating to or denoting a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used (such as 'here,' 'you,' or 'tomorrow'). | Example: "The word 'here' is deictic because its meaning depends on the speaker's location." “here”这个词是指示性的，因为它的含义取决于说话者的位置。

Example:  Deictic expressions like "this" and "that" require context for understanding.  像“这个”和“那个”这样的指示性表达需要上下文才能理解。 | Near synonyms: indexical, demonstrative | Antonyms:  non-deictic | word property: adjective

deictic:/ˈdaɪktɪk/ | n. | 指示词 | A word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used (such as 'here,' 'you,' or 'tomorrow'). | Example: "Pronouns are often deictics." 代词通常是指示词。

Example: The deictic "there" can refer to many different places depending on the context. 指示词“那里”可以指代许多不同的地方，具体取决于上下文。| Near synonyms: indexical, demonstrative | Antonyms:  non-deictic | word property: noun
