brochures:/ˈbroʊʃʊrz/ (US), /ˈbrəʊʃəz/ (UK)| n. |小册子，宣传册|A small booklet or pamphlet, often containing advertising material or information about a product, service, or place.|The travel agent gave me some brochures about holidays in Greece. 旅行社给了我一些关于希腊度假的宣传册。|Example:  The company printed colorful brochures to advertise their new product.  公司印制了彩色的小册子来宣传他们的新产品。|Synonyms: pamphlets, leaflets, booklets|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "tome" for a very large book)|Noun
