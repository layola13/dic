monkishnesses:/ˈmʌŋkɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
僧侣气质；修道士作风；禁欲主义
The quality or state of being monkish; excessive devotion to monasticism or its practices.  A collection of characteristics or behaviors associated with monks, such as austerity, piety, or seclusion.
His monkishnesses included fasting and long hours of prayer. 他的僧侣行为包括禁食和长时间祈祷。
Near Synonyms: asceticism, austerity, piety, devotion, religiousness
Antonyms: worldliness, hedonism, materialism
