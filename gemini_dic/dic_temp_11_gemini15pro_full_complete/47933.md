decertation: /ˌdiːsərˈteɪʃən/
n.
dissertation, thesis
A long formal piece of writing on a particular subject, especially for a university degree.
She's writing her decertation on the history of feminist literature. 她正在写关于女性主义文学史的学位论文。
He dedicated two years to researching and writing his decertation. 他花了2年时间研究和撰写论文。
synonyms: thesis, dissertation, treatise, essay, paper, study
antonyms: summary, abstract
noun
