foveate:/ˈfoʊvieɪt/| v. |注视；集中注意力于…|To look at something with the fovea, the central part of the retina responsible for sharpest vision.  To focus attention on something.|The hawk foveated the mouse scurrying through the grass.  老鹰注视着在草丛中乱窜的老鼠。|近义词：focus, concentrate, fixate|反义词: ignore, neglect|词性:动词
foveated:/ˈfoʊvieɪtɪd/| adj. |具中央凹的；集中注意力的|Having a fovea or foveae; adapted for foveation. Focused or concentrated.|The foveated vision of eagles allows them to spot prey from great distances.  鹰的中央凹视觉使它们能够从很远的地方发现猎物。|近义词：focused, concentrated|反义词: unfocused, distracted|词性:形容词
