eyrie/ˈɛəri/ /ˈɪəri/
noun
高处的巢；鹰巢
A large nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle, typically built high in a tree or on a cliff.
The eagle soared above its eyrie, perched high on the mountain crag.  这只鹰在它位于山崖上的巢穴上方翱翔。
