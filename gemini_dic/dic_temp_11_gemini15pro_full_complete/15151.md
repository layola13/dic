back-gammon: /ˈbækˌɡæmən/ | n. |西洋双陆棋 |A board game for two players, played on a board with points on which pieces are moved according to the throw of dice. The object is to be the first to bear off all one's pieces. | He enjoys a game of backgammon after dinner. 他喜欢晚饭后玩一盘西洋双陆棋。| 近义词: tables (古称) | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 名词


词源:

From Middle English *backgammon*, possibly from back (adv.) + gamen (“game”), or from Welsh *bachgammon* (“little battle”).
