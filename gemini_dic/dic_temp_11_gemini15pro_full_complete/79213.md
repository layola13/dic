glands:/ɡlændz/ | n. |腺体| An organ in the body that synthesizes and releases substances such as hormones or enzymes. | The endocrine glands secrete hormones directly into the bloodstream. 内分泌腺将激素直接分泌到血液中。

The salivary glands produce saliva, which helps with digestion. 唾液腺产生唾液，有助于消化。 | synonyms: organs, secretory organs | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical organ) | noun
