USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he eaten for breakfast? = 他早餐吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has she been working on all week? = 她整个星期一直在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我希望我知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 他不明白我在说什么。


PHRASE->

what if = 如果...会怎么样

注意事项:
ib. 的音标取决于它是作为缩写词还是完整单词发音。作为缩写词，它通常发音为/ɪb/。作为完整单词 (ibidem)， 它发音为 /aɪˈbiː/。此条目已包含两种情况。
