arthrology: /ˌɑːrˈθrɒlədʒi/ /ˌɑːrˈθrɑːlədʒi/
noun
关节学
The study of the structure and function of joints.
Arthrology is a complex subject, crucial for understanding human movement.  关节学是一门复杂的学科，对于理解人体运动至关重要。
The veterinarian's knowledge of arthrology was key to diagnosing the dog's leg injury.  兽医的关节学知识是诊断狗腿部受伤的关键。
近义词: syndesmology
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider areas *not* related to joints, such as myology (study of muscles))
词性: noun
