chilopoda:/kɪˈlɒpədə/ | n. | 唇足纲; 蜈蚣类 | Chilopoda is a class of arthropods that includes centipedes. 唇足纲是一种节肢动物门，包括蜈蚣。| Centipedes, members of the class Chilopoda, are predatory arthropods. 蜈蚣是唇足纲的成员，是掠食性节肢动物。 | 近义词: centipedes | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

chilopod:/ˈkaɪləˌpɑːd/ | adj. | 唇足纲的; 蜈蚣类的 | Chilopod features include elongated bodies and numerous legs. 唇足纲动物的特征包括细长的身体和众多的腿。 | The chilopod venom can be painful to humans. 蜈蚣的毒液对人类来说很疼。 | 近义词: centipede | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
