kill the fatted calf:/kɪl ðə ˈfædɪd kæf/
|idiom|
|宰杀肥牛犊;热情款待，铺张庆祝|
|To celebrate someone's return or something special by having a large, lavish meal, often involving the slaughter of a prized animal.  It alludes to the biblical parable of the Prodigal Son, where the father kills a fattened calf to celebrate his son's return.|
|They killed the fatted calf for their daughter's wedding. 他们为女儿的婚礼大摆筵席。|
|celebrate lavishly, roll out the red carpet, pull out all the stops, go all out, make a big fuss over|
|be frugal, skimp, hold back|
