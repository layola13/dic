lose-out: /luːz ˈaʊt/ | phrasal verb | 输掉，错过，吃亏 | to not get something good, or to get less of something good than someone else |  He lost out on the promotion because of his lack of experience.  他因为缺乏经验而错失了升职机会。| 近义词: miss out, lose, forfeit | 反义词: win, gain, succeed | 词性: 动词短语

lose-out: /luːz ˈaʊt/ | n. | 损失，失败 | the act of losing or failing to gain something |  His lose-out in the election was a big disappointment. 他在选举中的失利令人非常失望。| 近义词: loss, failure, defeat | 反义词: victory, win, success | 词性: 名词 (较少使用)

词源： “lose out” 是一个短语动词，由动词 “lose” 和副词 “out” 组成。 “lose” 源自古英语 “losian”，意为“毁灭，丧失”。 “out” 则加强了“lose” 的含义，表示完全失去或错失。
