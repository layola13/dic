heyday:/ˈheɪdeɪ/| n. |全盛时期，鼎盛时期|The period of greatest prosperity or power.|例句：In its heyday, the company employed over 5,000 people. 在其全盛时期，这家公司雇佣了超过5000名员工。

例句：The 1920s were the heyday of jazz.  20世纪20年代是爵士乐的黄金时代。

近义词：prime, peak, bloom, blossom|反义词:decline, downfall|词性:名词
