carbonizable:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnaɪzəbl/ |adj.|可碳化的，可炭化的|Capable of being carbonized (converted into carbon by heating).| Wood is a carbonizable material. 木材是一种可碳化的材料.|The plastic, though synthetic, was surprisingly carbonizable. 尽管是合成的，这种塑料却惊人的容易碳化。|近义词:combustible, flammable|反义词:non-combustible, fireproof|词性:adjective
carbonize:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/ |v.|使碳化，炭化|To convert or be converted into carbon by heating.| High heat can carbonize organic matter. 高温可以使有机物碳化。|The intense fire carbonized the wood, leaving only charcoal.  猛烈的火将木头碳化，只留下木炭。|近义词:char, burn|反义词:N/A|词性:verb
