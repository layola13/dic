contributories:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɔːriz/
n.| 贡献者，捐助者，促成因素|People who contribute something, especially money, or factors that contribute to a result. |The contributories to the fund were mainly large corporations.  该基金的捐助者主要是大型企业。|contributors, donors, funders| detractors, opponents
n.| (破产公司) 股东；(尤指无限责任公司) 负连带责任的股东|Shareholders in a bankrupt company, especially one with unlimited liability, who are liable to contribute to its debts.|The contributories of the failed bank were forced to pay large sums of money. 这家倒闭银行的股东被迫支付巨额资金。|shareholders, members| creditors
