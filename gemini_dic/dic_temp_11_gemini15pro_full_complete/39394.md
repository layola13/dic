conjurement:/ˈkɒndʒəmənt/ (US) /ˈkɒndʒərəmənt/ (UK)| n.| 符咒，咒语，魔法|The act of conjuring; the calling up of spirits or devils by invocation or incantation; enchantment.  A spell; an enchantment.|He attempted a conjurement to bring back his lost love.|Synonyms: incantation, spell, charm, invocation, sorcery, witchcraft|Antonyms: benediction, blessing|noun
