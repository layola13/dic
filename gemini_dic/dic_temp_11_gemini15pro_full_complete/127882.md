neocortical:/ˌniːoʊˈkɔːrtɪkəl/| adj.|新皮层的|Relating to the neocortex, the most recently evolved part of the cerebral cortex.|The neocortical regions of the brain are responsible for higher-level cognitive functions. 大脑的新皮层区域负责高级认知功能。|近义词:cerebral, cortical|反义词:None|词性:形容词

Etymology:
neo- (Greek): new, recent
cortical (Latin): relating to the cortex
