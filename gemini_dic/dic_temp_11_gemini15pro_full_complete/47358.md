deaconess:/ˈdiːkənɪs/ | n. |女执事，女助手|A woman who is a deacon, a member of the clergy ranking next below a priest, especially in certain Protestant churches.  A woman who performs charitable or religious work.|例句：The deaconess visited the sick members of the congregation. 女执事探望了会众中生病的成员。

例句：She trained to be a deaconess in the Episcopal Church. 她在圣公会接受培训成为一名女执事。|近义词：churchwoman, laywoman, assistant, helper |反义词: layman (This is a nuanced antonym, referring to a non-ordained member of a church, not necessarily the opposite gender.) |词性: noun
