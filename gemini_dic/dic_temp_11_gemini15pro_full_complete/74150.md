foundry: /ˈfaʊndri/ (US) /ˈfaʊndri/ (UK)| n.|铸造厂，铸造车间|A workshop or factory for casting metal.|The foundry workers poured molten iron into the mold. 铸造厂的工人们将熔化的铁水倒入模具中。|Synonyms: casting plant, metalworks|Antonyms: None|noun

Etymology: From Old French *fondrie, from fondre "to melt," from Latin fundere "to pour, melt."
