fireproofing:/ˈfaɪərˌpruːfɪŋ/| n.|防火；防火处理；耐火性|The act or process of making something fireproof, or the materials used to do this. |Example 1: The building's fireproofing prevented the spread of the flames. 这栋建筑的防火措施阻止了火焰蔓延。

Example 2:  They invested heavily in fireproofing the new auditorium. 他们在新礼堂的防火措施上投入了巨资。|Synonyms: fire resistance, flame retardance|Antonyms: flammability, combustibility|noun
