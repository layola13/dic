commonwealth: /ˈkɒmənˌwelθ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənˌwelθ/ (US)| n.|共和国，联邦，联合体|A nation, state, or other political entity founded on law and united by compact or tacit agreement of the people for the common good.  A group of sovereign states and their dependencies associated by historical ties, as with the British Commonwealth.|例句：The Commonwealth of Nations includes many former British colonies. 英联邦包括许多前英国殖民地。

例句：Australia is a member of the Commonwealth. 澳大利亚是英联邦成员国。

|近义词：federation, republic, alliance|反义词：dictatorship, monarchy|词性: noun
