overdeepened: /ˌoʊvərˈdiːpənd/ | adj. | 过度加深的，过度挖掘的 |  Made excessively deep, often referring to glacial valleys or lake basins. This occurs when glacial erosion is particularly intense, resulting in a depression deeper than would be expected based on the surrounding topography. | The fjord is an overdeepened glacial valley. 这条峡湾是一个过度加深的冰川山谷。| The lake, nestled in the mountains, was overdeepened by glacial action during the last ice age.  坐落在山脉中的湖泊在上一个冰河时代被冰川作用过度加深了。 | 近义词: excessively deepened | 反义词: shallow | 词性: 形容词
