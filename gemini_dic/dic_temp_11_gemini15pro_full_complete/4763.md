alfred:/ˈælfrid/| n. |阿尔弗雷德（男子名）|A masculine given name of Old English origin, meaning "elf counsel" or "wise elf". It is composed of the elements ælf, meaning "elf", and ræd, meaning "counsel".  Alfred the Great was a 9th-century king of Wessex who fought against the Vikings.|例句：Alfred is a classic and timeless name. 阿尔弗雷德是一个经典而永恒的名字。

例句：My grandfather's name was Alfred. 我的祖父名叫阿尔弗雷德。

近义词：Al, Fred (as nicknames) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
