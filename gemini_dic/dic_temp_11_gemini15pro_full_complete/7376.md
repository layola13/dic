animalculist:/ˌænɪˈmælkjəlɪst/
n.
微生物学家；精子学家
One who believes in or studies animalcules, especially spermatozoa.
Historically, a person who believed that a miniature preformed animal existed in the sperm and developed into a fetus inside the female.
例句：
The animalculist meticulously observed the sample through his microscope.
这位微生物学家通过显微镜仔细地观察了样本。

例句：
In the 17th century, animalculists debated the role of sperm in reproduction.
在17世纪，精子学家争论精子在生殖中的作用。


近义词：microscopist, microbiologist
反义词: (No direct antonym exists)
