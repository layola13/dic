fountainheads:/ˈfaʊntɪnˌhɛdz/ | n. | 源泉，根源，起源 |  A source or origin, especially of a river or stream; a primary source of something. |  The fountainheads of his inspiration were the ancient myths and legends.  他的灵感的源泉是古代神话和传说。 | synonyms: origins, sources, roots, beginnings, wellsprings | antonyms: ends, conclusions, results, outcomes | noun

fountainheads:/ˈfaʊntɪnˌhɛdz/ | n. | 源头，发源地 |  The place where a river or stream begins. | The explorers searched for the fountainheads of the Amazon. 探险家们寻找亚马逊河的源头。 | synonyms: headwaters, source, origin, wellspring | antonyms: mouth, delta, estuary | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "fountainhead," a compound of "fountain" and "head."  "Fountain" comes from Old French "fontaine," ultimately from Latin "fontana," meaning "spring, source." "Head" comes from Old English "hēafod," meaning "head, top, beginning."
