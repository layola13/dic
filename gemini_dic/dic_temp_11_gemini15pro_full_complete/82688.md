guestrooms: /ˈɡɛstˌruːmz/ /ˈɡɛstˌrʊmz/
n.
客房，宾客房
Rooms in a hotel or other lodging establishment that are available for rent by guests.
The hotel has 200 comfortable guestrooms. 这家酒店有200间舒适的客房。
The guestrooms are equipped with modern amenities. 这些客房配备了现代化的设施。
synonyms: accommodations, rooms, lodgings
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "private rooms," "owner's suite," etc. depending on context)
noun
