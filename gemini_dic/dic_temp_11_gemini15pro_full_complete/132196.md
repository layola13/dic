nondatival:/ˌnɒndeɪˈteɪvəl/ | adj. | 非与格的 | Not involving or relating to the dative case (a grammatical case used to mark the indirect object of a verb).  A nondatival verb does not take a dative complement.  | This verb takes a direct object, making it nondatival.  这个动词接受直接宾语，使其成为非与格的。 |  Nondatival verbs often take accusative objects.  非与格动词通常接受宾格宾语。 | non-dative | dative | adjective
