gainsayer:/ɡeɪnˈseɪər/| n. |反驳者，否认者|A person who denies, contradicts, or opposes something.|例句：He was a gainsayer of the new policy, arguing it would be ineffective. 他是这项新政策的反对者，认为它不会有效。

例句：There are always gainsayers who will question the validity of scientific findings. 总会有一些人质疑科学发现的有效性。|近义词：opponent, objector, dissenter, critic, antagonist|反义词：proponent, supporter, advocate|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gainsayen*, from Old Norse *gegnsegia* (to speak against), from *gegn* (against) + *segja* (to say).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gaggan* (against) and *sagjaną* (to say).
