coquina:/koʊˈkiːnə/| n.|贝壳砂岩，介壳灰岩|A soft porous limestone composed mainly of shell fragments.|The ancient city walls were built of coquina. 这座古城的城墙是用贝壳砂岩建造的。|Synonyms: shell limestone, bioclastic limestone|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider hard rock, granite)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *coquina* "cockle, shellfish," diminutive of *concha* "shell," from Latin *concha* "shellfish, mussel," from Greek *konkhē* "mussel, cockle."
