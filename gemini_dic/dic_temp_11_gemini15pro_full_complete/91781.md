hyperethical:/ˌhaɪpərˈeθɪkl/
adj.
极度合乎道德的，过分拘泥道德的
Excessively ethical; overly concerned with morality.
She was so hyperethical that she refused to even tell a white lie. 她非常拘泥道德，甚至连善意的谎言都不肯说。

He has a hyperethical approach to business, which sometimes puts him at a disadvantage. 他对商业采取了一种过分拘泥道德的做法，这有时使他处于劣势。
近义词: scrupulous, overscrupulous, righteous
反义词: unethical, immoral, unscrupulous
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- "excessive" + ethical.  The prefix "hyper-" comes from the Greek  "ὑπέρ" (hyper) meaning "over, above, beyond." "Ethical" comes from the Late Latin "ethicus," from Greek "ēthikós"  "of or for morals," from "ēthos" "moral character."
