nonchalantly:/ˌnɒnˈʃæləntli/| adv.|漠不关心地，若无其事地|in a casually relaxed and unconcerned manner.  Showing a lack of interest or concern.|He nonchalantly tossed the ball into the air. 他漫不经心地把球抛向空中。|Synonyms: casually, coolly, indifferently, calmly|Antonyms: anxiously, nervously, excitedly|词性: 副词

nonchalant:/ˌnɒnˈʃælənt/| adj.|漠不关心的，若无其事的|feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety, interest, or enthusiasm.|She had a nonchalant attitude about her exams. 她对考试的态度漠不关心。|Synonyms: casual, cool, indifferent, calm|Antonyms: anxious, nervous, excited|词性: 形容词
