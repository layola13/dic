nonenviousness:/nɒnˈɛnviəsnəs/
n.
无嫉妒; 不羡慕; 不妒忌
Lack of envy; the state of not being envious.
She showed a remarkable nonenviousness towards her more successful colleague. 她对更成功的同事表现出非凡的不妒忌。
近义词: lack of envy, generosity of spirit, magnanimity
反义词: envy, jealousy, covetousness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- + envious + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "envious" comes from the Old French "envieus," ultimately from the Latin "invidia" (envy), and "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
