freehand:/ˌfriːˈhænd/| adj. adv.|徒手的，不用工具的；随意地|Done by hand without the aid of instruments or guides. |She drew a freehand circle on the paper. 她在纸上徒手画了一个圆。|近义词：unaided, unassisted, impromptu|反义词:mechanical, guided|adjective/adverb

freehand:/ˌfriːˈhænd/| n.|徒手画|A drawing or sketch done by hand without the aid of instruments or guides. |The architect's freehand sketches were impressive. 建筑师的徒手草图令人印象深刻。|近义词：sketch, drawing|反义词:blueprint, measured drawing|noun
