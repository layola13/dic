nonvacuum:/ˌnɒnˈvækjuːm/
noun
非真空
A state or environment that is not a vacuum; containing matter, typically air or other gases.
The experiment was conducted in a nonvacuum environment.  这个实验是在非真空环境下进行的。
The sound waves travelled through the nonvacuum. 声波在非真空中传播。
synonyms: atmosphere, medium, plena
antonyms: vacuum
