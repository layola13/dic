olivine: /ˈɒlɪˌviːn/ (UK) /ˈɑːləˌviːn/ (US) | n. |橄榄石|A magnesium iron silicate mineral ((Mg, Fe)₂SiO₄) that is a common constituent of igneous rocks, typically olive-green in color. |Olivine is a common mineral in Earth's mantle. 橄榄石是地幔中常见的矿物。

Olivine is the primary component of peridotite, a rock that makes up much of the upper mantle. 橄榄石是橄榄岩的主要成分，而橄榄岩构成了上地幔的大部分。
|近义词: chrysolite (宝石级橄榄石)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
