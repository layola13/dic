lovey-dovey: /ˈlʌvi ˈdʌvi/ | adj. | 卿卿我我的；情意绵绵的 | Expressing or showing a lot of love and affection, often excessively. |  They were so lovey-dovey on their honeymoon. 他们蜜月旅行时卿卿我我的。| synonyms: affectionate, amorous, cuddly, doting | antonyms: cold, distant, unaffectionate | adjective

lovey-dovey: /ˈlʌvi ˈdʌvi/ | n. |  卿卿我我的情侣；情意绵绵的举动 |  A person who is excessively affectionate or an act of excessive affection. | Stop being such a lovey-dovey! 别这么卿卿我我的！ | synonyms: lovebirds, sweethearts | antonyms: enemies, rivals | noun
