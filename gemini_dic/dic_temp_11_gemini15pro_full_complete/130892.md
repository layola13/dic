nonarrogancies:/ˌnɒnˈærəɡənsiz/
noun
不自负；谦逊
lack of arrogance; the state of not being arrogant
His nonarrogance and willingness to learn made him a beloved figure. 他不自负而且好学的品质使他广受爱戴。
Near Antonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, pride, conceit, egotism
Antonyms: arrogance

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + arrogance (from Latin arrogantia, meaning "presumption, claim to undue importance")
