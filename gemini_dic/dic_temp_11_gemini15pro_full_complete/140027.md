oenanthol:/ˈiːnənˌθɒl/ /ˈiːnənˌθɑːl/| n. |庚醛; 正庚醛|An aldehyde, C₇H₁₄O, having a strong disagreeable odor; n-heptaldehyde.  It is used in organic synthesis and as a flavoring agent.|Oenanthol is used in the production of certain fragrances. 庚醛用于生产某些香料。

Oenanthol has a characteristic fatty odor. 庚醛具有一种特有的脂肪气味。|近义词：heptaldehyde, n-heptaldehyde|反义词: None (庚醛没有直接的反义词，因为它是特指一种化合物).|词性:noun
