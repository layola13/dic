hyperesthete:/ˌhaɪpərˈesθiːt/ | n. |感觉过敏者，感觉过强的人|A person who is abnormally sensitive to sensory stimuli, experiencing heightened or excessive sensations.| The hyperesthete found the city's constant noise overwhelming.  这个感觉过敏者觉得城市的持续噪音让人难以忍受。| 近义词: sensitive person, sensualist | 反义词: anesthetic, insensitive person | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *hyper* (over, excessive) + *aisthetes* (one who perceives), from *aisthanesthai* (to perceive).
