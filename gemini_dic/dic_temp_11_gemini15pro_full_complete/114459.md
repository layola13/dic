maki:/ˈmɑːki/ | n. | 寿司卷，卷寿司 | A Japanese dish consisting of sushi rice and other ingredients, such as fish or vegetables, rolled in seaweed. |  Example: I ordered a spicy tuna maki roll for lunch.  我午饭点了一份辣金枪鱼寿司卷。| Example:  The maki was fresh and delicious.  这寿司卷很新鲜美味。 | Synonym: sushi roll | Antonym: N/A | noun
