hamperedly:/ˈhæmpərdli/
adverb
受阻地，困难地
in a hampered manner; with difficulty or obstruction.
His progress was hamperedly slow due to the heavy rain. 由于大雨，他的进展缓慢而艰难。
The team worked hamperedly, struggling with outdated equipment.  团队工作困难重重，与过时的设备作斗争。
近义词: awkwardly, clumsily, laboriously, cumbersomely
反义词: easily, smoothly, effortlessly, readily
词性: adverb

词源:  From the verb "hamper" + "-ed" (past participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Hamper" comes from the Old North French "hanapier" meaning "to fasten with a hook or clasp," later evolving to mean "to impede or hinder."
