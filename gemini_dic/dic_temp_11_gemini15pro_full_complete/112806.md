lucency:/ˈluːsənsi/| n. |透明度；透光性；清晰度|The lucency of the X-ray image allowed the doctor to see the fracture clearly.  X光图像的透明度使医生能够清楚地看到骨折。|例句：The lucency of the diamond was breathtaking. 这颗钻石的透明度令人叹为观止。|近义词：transparency, clarity, translucence|反义词: opacity, density|noun

词源:  From Latin *lucentia, from lucēns (present participle of lucēre, "to shine").
