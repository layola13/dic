fleen:/fliːn/
verb
跳蚤; 逃走;
To rid of fleas; to flee.  This word is archaic and rarely used.
He fleened his dog. 他为他家的狗捉跳蚤。
The thief fleened the scene. 小偷逃离了现场。
近义词:escape, flee, abscond
反义词:stay, remain
词性:verb
