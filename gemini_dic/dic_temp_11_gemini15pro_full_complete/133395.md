nonfactitiously:/ˌnɒnfækˈtɪʃəsli/
adv.
非人为地；真实地；自然地
In a genuine or natural manner; not artificially or contrived.
She laughed nonfactitiously, a sound of pure joy. 她自然地笑了，那是一种纯粹的快乐的声音。
Near Synonyms: genuinely, naturally, sincerely, authentically, unpretentiously, artlessly
Antonyms: artificially, factitiously, falsely, contrivedly, pretendedly
词性: 副词
