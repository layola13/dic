am:/æm/, /əm/ | v. | 是 |  The first person singular present tense form of *be*. Used to indicate existence, identity, or state of being. | I am a student. 我是一名学生。|  is, are | wasn't, weren't | verb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I am happy. 我很高兴。
I am a doctor. 我是一名医生。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am watching TV. 我正在看电视。
I am eating dinner. 我正在吃晚饭。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have been busy all day.  我一整天都很忙。（注意：am不直接用于现在完成时，而是用have been）
I have been living here for two years. 我已经在这里住了两年。（注意：am不直接用于现在完成时，而是用have been）


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been working on this project for a week. 我已经在这个项目上工作了一个星期。（注意：am不直接用于现在完成进行时，而是用have been）
I have been studying English since I was a child. 我从小就一直在学习英语。（注意：am不直接用于现在完成进行时，而是用have been）

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I was tired yesterday. 我昨天很累。
I was at home last night. 我昨晚在家。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was walking home when it started to rain. 我正走回家的时候开始下雨了。
I was reading a book while she was cooking. 她做饭的时候，我在看书。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had been to London before. 我以前去过伦敦。（注意：am不直接用于过去完成时，而是用had been）
I had been working there for five years before I quit.  我辞职前在那里工作了五年。（注意：am不直接用于过去完成时，而是用had been）

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been waiting for an hour before he arrived.  在他到达之前，我已经等了一个小时。（注意：am不直接用于过去完成进行时，而是用had been）
I had been studying all night for the exam. 我整晚都在为考试学习。（注意：am不直接用于过去完成进行时，而是用had been）


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be there tomorrow. 我明天会在那里。
I will be happy to help. 我很乐意帮忙。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be flying to Paris next week. 我下周将飞往巴黎。
I will be attending a conference tomorrow afternoon. 我明天下午将参加一个会议。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished the project by Friday. 我将在星期五之前完成这个项目。（注意：am不直接用于将来完成时，而是用will have been）
I will have been living here for ten years next month. 下个月我将在这里住了十年。（注意：am不直接用于将来完成时，而是用will have been）


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been working on this project for six months by then. 到那时，我将在这个项目上工作了六个月。（注意：am不直接用于将来完成进行时，而是用will have been）
I will have been studying Spanish for two years by next summer. 到明年夏天，我将已经学习西班牙语两年了。（注意：am不直接用于将来完成进行时，而是用will have been）



PHRASE->
I am sure. 我确定。
I am not sure. 我不确定。
I am happy to help. 我很乐意帮忙。
I am going to the store. 我要去商店。
I am tired. 我累了。

词源: From Old English *eom*, from Proto-Germanic *immi*, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ésmi*.
