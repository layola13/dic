hillbillies:/ˈhɪlˌbɪliz/ | n. | 山里人，乡巴佬 |  A term, often derogatory, for people who live in rural, mountainous areas, especially in the Appalachian Mountains of the eastern United States.  It suggests a lack of sophistication, education, and contact with modern society. |  The movie portrayed the hillbillies as ignorant and superstitious. 这部电影把山里人描绘成无知和迷信的人。| Synonyms:  country folk, mountain people, rustics | Antonyms: city dwellers, cosmopolitans | Noun
