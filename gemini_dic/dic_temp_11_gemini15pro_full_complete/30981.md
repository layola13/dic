chaldaism: /ˈkældɪɪzəm/ | n. | 迦勒底主义; 占星术; 巴比伦文化 |  A system of magic or divination practiced by the Chaldeans of ancient Babylonia, often associated with astrology and other occult practices.  It can also refer more broadly to the culture and traditions of ancient Babylonia. |  The popularity of chaldaism waned with the rise of Christianity. 迦勒底主义的流行随着基督教的兴起而衰落。|  He studied ancient texts on chaldaism and other forms of divination. 他研究了关于迦勒底主义和其他占卜形式的古代文献。 | Synonyms: astrology, divination, occultism, Babylonian culture | Antonyms: science, rationalism | noun
