devotement:/dɪˈvəʊtmənt/ | n. | 献身，奉献；热爱 | an act of devoting something or someone to a particular purpose or person; strong love or loyalty |  She showed complete devotement to her children. 她对她的孩子们表现出完全的奉献。| 近义词: dedication, commitment, devotion | 反义词: neglect, indifference | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French devotement, from devoter ‘devote’.
