legerdemainists:/ˌlɛʒər dəˈmeɪnɪsts/
|n.|魔术师；手法敏捷的人；变戏法的人|A person skilled in legerdemain; a sleight-of-hand performer.  Someone who is adept at tricks and illusions, especially involving dexterity of the hands.|The audience was captivated by the legerdemainists' incredible illusions.  观众被魔术师不可思议的幻象所吸引。|Synonyms: magician, conjurer, illusionist, prestidigitator|Antonyms:  clumsy person, unskilled person|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *leger de main* "light of hand," from *leger* "light" (from Latin *levis*) + *de* "of" + *main* "hand" (from Latin *manus*).
