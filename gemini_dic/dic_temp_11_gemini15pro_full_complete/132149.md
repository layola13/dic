noncurative:/ˌnɒnˈkjʊərətɪv/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈkjʊərətɪv/ (UK)
adjective
不可治愈的；非治疗性的
not able to cure a disease or medical condition
The doctor informed him that his condition was noncurative. 医生告知他，他的病情是无法治愈的。
His illness was diagnosed as noncurative, but manageable with medication. 他的疾病被诊断为无法治愈，但可以通过药物控制。
近义词: incurable, irremediable, terminal
反义词: curable, treatable, remedial
词性: adjective

noncurative:/ˌnɒnˈkjʊərətɪv/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈkjʊərətɪv/ (UK)
noun
非治疗性药物或疗法
a treatment or medication that does not cure a disease, but may relieve symptoms or improve quality of life
Palliative care focuses on noncuratives to ease suffering.  姑息治疗侧重于非治疗性措施来减轻痛苦。
The doctor prescribed noncuratives to manage his pain. 医生开了非治疗性药物来控制他的疼痛。
近义词: palliative
反义词: curative
词性: noun
