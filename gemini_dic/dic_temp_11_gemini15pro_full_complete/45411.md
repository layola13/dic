cuppa:/ˈkʌpə/ (UK) | n. | 一杯茶 | A cup of tea. Primarily used in British English. | Fancy a cuppa? 想喝杯茶吗？|  I need a cuppa after that long walk.  走了那么久之后，我需要一杯茶。 | synonyms:  cup of tea, brew (informal), char (chiefly Northern England) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider beverages other than tea as contrasting, e.g., coffee, juice) | noun
