musafar:/mʊˈsɑːfər/| n.|旅行者，旅客|A traveler, especially one who undertakes a long journey.|例句：The musafar embarked on a pilgrimage to Mecca.  这位旅行者踏上了前往麦加的朝圣之旅。

例句：The weary musafar sought shelter for the night.  疲惫的旅行者寻找过夜的住所。|近义词：traveler, voyager, pilgrim, journeyer|反义词:resident, local, settler|词性:noun
词源: From Urdu مسافر (musāfir), from Arabic مسافر (musāfir), active participle of سافر (sāfara, “to travel”). Ultimately from the Proto-Semitic *sfr (“to travel, to walk, to roam”).
