crenate:/ˈkriːneɪt/ | adj. | 圆齿状的，钝锯齿状的 | Having a scalloped or rounded toothed edge. | The leaf margins were crenate, giving them a delicate appearance. 叶子边缘呈圆齿状，使它们看起来很精致。| The bacteria formed crenate colonies on the agar plate. 细菌在琼脂平板上形成了圆齿状的菌落。 | 近义词: scalloped, notched, serrated | 反义词: smooth, entire | 词性: adjective

crenation:/kriːˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 圆齿状，钝锯齿状 | The formation of scalloped or rounded notches on an edge, especially of a leaf or shell; the shrinking of cells, especially red blood cells, in a hypertonic solution. | The crenation of the red blood cells was visible under the microscope. 红细胞的圆齿状在显微镜下可见。 | The crenation of the leaf margin is a key identifying feature. 叶缘的圆齿状是一个关键的识别特征。| 近义词: scalloping, notching, serration | 反义词: smoothing | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "crenatus," past participle of "crenare" (to notch, indent), from "crena" (notch, indentation).
