assuagers:/əˈsweɪdʒərz/
noun
慰藉者；缓和者；减轻者
People or things that assuage or lessen pain, grief, or anger.
The therapists acted as assuagers of the patients' anxieties.  治疗师充当了病人焦虑的缓和者。
synonyms: soothers, relievers, mitigators, alleviators, comforters
antonyms: aggravators, intensifiers, exacerbators
n.
