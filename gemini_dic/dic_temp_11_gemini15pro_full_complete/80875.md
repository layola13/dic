governabilities:/ˌɡʌvərnəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可治理性，治理能力|The ease with which a unit can be governed, taking into account factors like political stability, administrative capacity, and social cohesion.|例句：The country's governabilities were weakened by the ongoing civil war.  这个国家的治理能力因持续的内战而被削弱。

例句：Improving governabilities in fragile states is a key challenge for international development.  提高脆弱国家的治理能力是国际发展面临的关键挑战。|近义词：manageability, controllability, tractability|反义词：ungovernability, intractability|词性:Noun (plural)
