loan-translations:/ˈloʊn trænsˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 翻译借词; 语义转借 | A word or phrase adopted from another language by literally translating its parts. | Example: The English term "skyscraper" is a loan-translation of the German word "Wolkenkratzer".  英文单词“skyscraper”（摩天大楼）是从德语单词“Wolkenkratzer”翻译借用而来。

Example: The term "Superman" is a loan-translation from the German "Übermensch".  英文单词“Superman”（超人）是从德语单词“Übermensch”翻译借用而来。| synonym: calque, loan translation | antonym: N/A | noun
