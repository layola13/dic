empresses: /ˈemprɪsɪz/ | n. | 女皇；皇后（empress 的复数） | The empresses of ancient Egypt wielded considerable power. 古埃及的女皇拥有相当大的权力。 |  Empresses ruled vast empires and commanded armies. 女皇统治着庞大的帝国，指挥着军队。 | 近义词：queens, monarchs | 反义词: commoners, subjects | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Old French *emperesse, from Latin imperatricem (nominative imperatrix), feminine of imperator ("emperor"). Ultimately from in- + parare ("to prepare, command").
