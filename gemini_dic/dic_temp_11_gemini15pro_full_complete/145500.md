n.

A state of excessive emotional or physical intimacy.  The plural form of "overcloseness."
Their overclosenesses made others uncomfortable. 他们的过度亲密让其他人感到不舒服。
Synonyms: intimacy, closeness, familiarity
Antonyms: distance, detachment, aloofness
