disbud:/dɪsˈbʌd/
verb
去除花蕾
To remove buds, especially from a plant, to prevent or control growth.
Gardeners often disbud roses to encourage larger blooms.  园丁经常去除玫瑰的花蕾以促进开出更大的花朵。
The grower disbudded the chrysanthemums to produce exhibition-quality flowers. 种植者摘除了菊花的花蕾以培育出展览级的花朵。
近义词: prune, pinch, nip
反义词:  graft (嫁接), cultivate (培育)
词性: verb (动词)

disbud:/dɪsˈbʌd/
noun
去除花蕾的行为
The act of removing buds.
Early disbudding is key to shaping young fruit trees. 早期去除花蕾是塑造幼果树的关键。
The disbudding of the lateral shoots promotes apical dominance. 去除侧芽可以促进顶端优势。
近义词: pruning, pinching, nipping
反义词: grafting (嫁接), cultivation (培育)
词性: noun (名词)
