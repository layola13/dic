illiterati:/ɪˌlɪtəˈrɑːti/
n.
文盲（总称），没有文化的人
people who are illiterate or who lack cultural awareness.
The politician's speech was clearly not aimed at the illiterati, as it was filled with complex jargon. 这位政治家的演讲显然不是针对没文化的人的，因为它充满了复杂的术语。
The illiterati are often excluded from participating fully in society.  没有文化的人常常被排除在完全参与社会之外。
近义词：uneducated, ignorant, uncultured
反义词：literate, educated, cultured
词性：名词
