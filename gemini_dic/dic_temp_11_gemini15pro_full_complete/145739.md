overcross:/ˈoʊvərkrɔːs/ /ˈəʊvəkrɒs/
n.|天桥；交叉；跨越|An overpass, especially for pedestrians. A crossing over something else.  The act of crossing over something.|The train rumbled over the overcross. 火车隆隆地驶过天桥。|近义词：overpass, bridge, crossing|反义词：underpass|词性:名词
overcross:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɔːs/ /ˌəʊvəˈkrɒs/
v.|越过；交叉；跨越|To cross over something. To intersect or overlap.|The climbers overcrossed the chasm on a rope bridge. 登山者通过索桥越过峡谷。|近义词：traverse, cross, bridge, span|反义词：undercross|词性:动词
