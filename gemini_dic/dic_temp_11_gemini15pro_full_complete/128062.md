nerve-rackingly:/ˈnɜːrvˌrækɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人紧张地，令人焦虑地 |  In a way that causes a lot of worry or anxiety.  Derived from "nerve-racking" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Nerve-racking" itself comes from "nerve" (referring to one's state of mind and resilience) + "racking" (from "rack," meaning to torture or cause great pain). |  The exam was nerve-rackingly difficult. 考试紧张得让人难以承受。 |  She waited nerve-rackingly for the results. 她焦急地等待着结果。 | Synonyms: anxiously, stressfully, agonizingly | Antonyms: calmly, peacefully, soothingly | 词性:副词
