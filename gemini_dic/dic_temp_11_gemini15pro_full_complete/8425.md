anti-zionists: /ˌæntiˈzaɪənɪsts/ | n. | 反犹太复国主义者 | People who oppose Zionism, the movement for the establishment and development of a Jewish nation in what is now Israel. |  The conference attracted a number of anti-zionists who voiced their concerns.  这次会议吸引了一些反犹太复国主义者，他们表达了他们的担忧。

Anti-zionists protested the Israeli government's policies. 反犹太复国主义者抗议以色列政府的政策。

| 近义词：anti-Israel activists | 反义词：Zionists | 词性：名词

---
Etymology:

* **anti-**:  a prefix meaning "against," "opposite of," "opposed to," from Greek *anti*.
* **Zionist**:  relating to Zionism, from Zion, a hill in Jerusalem, often used symbolically to refer to the Land of Israel.
