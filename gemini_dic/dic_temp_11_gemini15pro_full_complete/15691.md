bakshish:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/
noun
小费，赏钱，贿赂
A tip or gratuity, especially in the Middle East and South Asia; also, a bribe.
例句：He offered the porter a small bakshish to carry his bags.  他给了搬运工一点小费让他搬运行李。

例句：The official hinted that a little bakshish would expedite the process.  这位官员暗示，一点贿赂可以加快这个过程。
synonym:tip, gratuity, bribe, reward, recompense
antonym:penalty, fine, forfeiture
词性:n.
