extravaganza:/ɪkˌstrævəˈɡænzə/
|n.|盛大表演；铺张的演出；盛宴|a large, elaborate, and spectacular entertainment or production|The coronation was a dazzling extravaganza of pageantry and music. 加冕典礼是一场令人眼花缭乱的盛会，充满了壮观的仪式和音乐。|Synonyms: spectacle, pageant, celebration, fete, festival|Antonyms: simplicity, modesty, restraint|noun

extravaganza:/ɪkˌstrævəˈɡænzə/
|n.|奢侈品；过分的东西|something extravagant or excessive|The wedding was an extravaganza that cost a fortune. 婚礼是一场耗资巨大的盛宴。|Synonyms: excess, luxury, lavishness, opulence|Antonyms: frugality, economy, moderation|noun
