brown-out:/ˈbraʊn.aʊt/| n.|电压骤降; 灯光转暗; 部分灯火管制|A reduction in voltage in an electrical power supply system.  It can cause dimming of lights and malfunction of electronic equipment.|The city experienced a brown-out during the heat wave. 城市在热浪期间经历了电压骤降。|The brown-out caused the computer to crash.  电压骤降导致计算机崩溃。|近义词:voltage drop, power dip, power sag|反义词:power surge, blackout|名词

词源:  The term "brown-out" originated from the dimming effect on incandescent lights when the voltage drops.  Incandescent bulbs glow less brightly with reduced voltage, shifting the light towards a brownish hue.
