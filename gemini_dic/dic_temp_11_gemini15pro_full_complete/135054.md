nonnormally:/nɑːnˈnɔːrməli/ | adv. | 非正态地，不正常地 |  In a way that deviates from a normal distribution. It describes situations or data that do not follow the typical bell-shaped curve of a normal distribution.  | The data was distributed nonnormally, skewing heavily to the left.  数据非正态分布，严重左偏。| Synonym: atypically, abnormally | Antonym: normally, typically | 词性: 副词
