frumpishly:/ˈfrʌmpɪʃli/
adverb
邋遢地，不修边幅地
in a frumpy manner; dowdily.  Characterized by being old-fashioned, drab, and unattractive.
She dressed frumpishly in an old, shapeless dress. 她穿着一件旧的、不成形的连衣裙，显得邋遢。
He sat frumpishly on the sofa, his clothes rumpled. 他邋遢地坐在沙发上，衣服皱巴巴的。
近义词: dowdily, shabbily, sloppily, untidily
反义词: smartly, stylishly, elegantly, fashionably
词性: adverb

词源:
From frump + -ish + -ly.  Frump itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to dialects words meaning "ill-tempered" or "sullen."  The "-ish" suffix forms adjectives meaning "somewhat like," and "-ly" forms adverbs.
