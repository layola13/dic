nonactivity:/ˌnɒnækˈtɪvɪti/
noun
不活动；闲置；停滞
Lack of activity; inactivity.
例句：Periods of nonactivity can lead to muscle atrophy.  不活动期会导致肌肉萎缩。

例句：The prolonged nonactivity of the factory resulted in job losses.  工厂长时间的停工导致了失业。

近义词：inactivity, idleness, quiescence
反义词：activity, action, movement
词性：noun
