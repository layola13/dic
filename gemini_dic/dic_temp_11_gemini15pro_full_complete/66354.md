excursus:/ɪkˈskɜːrsəs/
|n.|
|离题讨论；附录；附注；额外说明|
|A detailed discussion of a particular point in a book, etc., typically one that is a digression from the main subject.|
|The author added a lengthy excursus on the history of the region.|作者加了一篇关于该地区历史的长篇附注。|
|digression, aside, detour, tangent, deviation|
|main topic, central theme, core subject|
|noun|
