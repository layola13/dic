archaeopteryxes:/ˌɑːrkiːˈɒptərɪksiːz/ | n. |始祖鸟（复数）|Archaeopteryx is a genus of bird-like dinosaurs that is transitional between non-avian feathered dinosaurs and modern birds.  Archaeopteryx is often considered the earliest known bird.|The discovery of archaeopteryxes revolutionized our understanding of avian evolution. 始祖鸟的发现彻底改变了我们对鸟类进化的理解。

The museum displays a fossil of one of the archaeopteryxes found in Germany.  这家博物馆展出了一块在德国发现的始祖鸟化石。|近义词:urvogel (German)|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider modern birds as a contrasting concept.)|词性:noun
