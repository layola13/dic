amma:/ˈæmə/ | n. | 妈妈；母亲 (South Indian, chiefly Tamil & Malayalam) | A term of endearment for mother, primarily used in South Indian languages like Tamil and Malayalam. | Example: Amma, I'm hungry. 妈妈，我饿了。| Example:  My amma makes the best biryani. 我妈妈做的印度香饭最好吃。|Synonym: mother, mom, mum | Antonym: (Generally no direct antonym for a familial term like "mother") | noun

amma:/ˈæmə/ | int. | 哦; 啊; 表示惊讶或痛苦 (informal) | An informal interjection expressing surprise or pain.  While less common than the "mother" meaning, this usage exists in some dialects. | Example: Amma! That hurt! 啊！好痛！ | Example: Amma! I didn't expect that! 哦！我没想到！ | Synonym: ouch, oh | Antonym: (No direct antonym for an interjection) | interjection
