acrotic: /əˈkrɒstɪk/ | adj. n. | 藏頭詩的，藏頭詩 | A poem or other composition in which the first letter of each line spells out a word, name, or phrase. |  The poet wrote an acrostic using his lover's name.  诗人用他爱人的名字写了一首藏头诗。 |  The children enjoyed creating acrostics using their own names. 孩子们喜欢用自己的名字创作藏头诗。 | synonyms: abecedarian, alliterative | antonyms: N/A | adjective, noun
