amentiferous: /əˈmɛntɪfərəs/
adjective
结果实的; 穗状花序的
bearing catkins; producing aments
The amentiferous trees shed their pollen in the spring.  结果实的树木在春天散播花粉。
The willow is an amentiferous tree.  柳树是一种穗状花序的树。
近义词: catkin-bearing
反义词: acarpous (不结果实的)
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *amentum (“catkin”) + -ferous (“bearing”). *amentum from āmēns ("strap, thong"), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eh₁mō (“rawhide, strap, thong”). -ferous from Latin -fer (“bearing”), from ferō (“I bear, carry”).
