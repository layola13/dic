clone:/kloʊn/ (US) /kləʊn/ (UK)| n. v.|克隆，复制；无性繁殖|A cell, group of cells, or organism that is produced asexually from and is genetically identical to a single ancestor.  An exact duplicate or copy of something.|
The scientist cloned the sheep. 科学家克隆了这只羊。
Dolly the sheep was a clone. 多利羊是一只克隆羊。
|近义词：duplicate, copy, replica|反义词:original|词性:名词,动词


clone:/kloʊn/ (US) /kləʊn/ (UK)| v.|克隆，复制；无性繁殖|To make an identical copy of (a cell, DNA molecule, or organism); to reproduce or propagate asexually.|
They are experimenting with cloning animals. 他们正在进行克隆动物的实验。
He cloned the hard drive. 他克隆了硬盘。
