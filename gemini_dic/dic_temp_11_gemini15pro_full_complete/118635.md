注意事项:
The pronunciation can vary slightly depending on the speaker's origin. The first pronunciation provided is closer to standard French, while the second is a more anglicized pronunciation.  It is more commonly used in formal settings.  While it can be translated as "gentlemen" or "sirs," it carries a slightly more formal and perhaps even old-fashioned connotation.
