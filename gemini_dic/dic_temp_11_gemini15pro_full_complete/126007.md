musmon:/ˈmʌsmən/ | n. | 欧洲盘羊 | A wild sheep (Ovis musimon) native to Corsica and Sardinia, introduced elsewhere. It is thought to be an ancestor of domestic sheep. |  The musmon grazed peacefully on the hillside.  欧洲盘羊在山坡上平静地吃草。 | synonyms: mouflon, wild sheep | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an animal species) | noun
