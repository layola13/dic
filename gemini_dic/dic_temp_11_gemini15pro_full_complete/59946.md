edi:/ˈiːdiː/ | n. | Electronic Data Interchange (电子数据交换)| The exchange of business documents between computers using a standard format. | Example:  EDI has revolutionized the way companies exchange information. 电子数据交换彻底改变了公司交换信息的方式。| Example:  We use EDI to process orders automatically. 我们使用电子数据交换自动处理订单。 | Synonym: electronic commerce, e-commerce | Antonym: paper-based exchange | noun
