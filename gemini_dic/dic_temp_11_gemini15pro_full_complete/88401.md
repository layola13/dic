hiroshima:/ˌhɪrəˈʃiːmə/ | n. |广岛|A city in southwestern Japan, on Honshu island.  It was largely destroyed by the first atomic bomb used in warfare (6 August 1945).|The bombing of Hiroshima was a tragic event. 广岛轰炸是一个悲剧事件。|Hiroshima is a symbol of the devastation of nuclear war. 广岛是核战争破坏的象征。|Nagasaki|None|noun
