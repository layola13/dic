altarpieces:/ˈɔːltərˌpiːsɪz/
noun
祭坛画；祭坛装饰
A painted or carved screen placed above and behind an altar.
The altarpiece depicts the Crucifixion. 这幅祭坛画描绘了耶稣受难。
synonyms: reredos, retable
antonyms: None.
n.
