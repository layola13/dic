arrogant: /ˈærəɡənt/ (US), /ˈærəɡənt/ (UK) | adj. | 傲慢的，自大的 |  Having or revealing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities.  | He was so arrogant, he thought he was better than everyone else. 他太傲慢了，觉得自己比任何人都优秀。 |  She spoke with an arrogant tone that made everyone dislike her. 她说话的语气很傲慢，让每个人都不喜欢她。 |Synonyms: haughty, conceited, pompous, supercilious, presumptuous | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | adjective
