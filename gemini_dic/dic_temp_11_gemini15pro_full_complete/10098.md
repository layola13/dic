apodictical:/ˌæpəˈdɪktɪkəl/| adj.|不容置疑的，必然的|Clearly expressed; incontestable.  Derived from Greek *apodeiktikos*, "demonstrative," from *apodeiknynai*, "to demonstrate."  Relating to or of the nature of apodictic reasoning; necessarily true or certain.|His apodictical pronouncements on the subject left no room for debate. 他对这个话题不容置疑的声明使之没有讨论的余地。|近义词：incontestable, undeniable, irrefutable, unquestionable|反义词：dubious, questionable, debatable, disputable|词性:形容词
