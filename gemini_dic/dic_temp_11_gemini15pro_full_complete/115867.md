marle:/mɑːrl/ | n. |泥灰岩；泥灰土；用泥灰改良 | A calcareous clay, or mixture of clay and calcium carbonate, used as a fertilizer. | The farmer spread marle on his fields to improve the soil.  农民在田里撒了泥灰来改良土壤。 | Synonyms:  clay, loam, topsoil | Antonyms:  sand, gravel | noun

marle:/mɑːrl/ | v. |用泥灰改良（土地） | To fertilize or dress (land) with marle. | He marled the fields every few years. 他每隔几年就用泥灰改良田地。| Synonyms:  fertilize, enrich, amend | Antonyms:  deplete, impoverish | verb
