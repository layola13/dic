frillies:/ˈfrɪliz/| n. |女式内衣，褶边，装饰品|Ornamental trimming, especially lace or ruffles, typically on women's underwear or clothing.  It can also refer to the underwear itself in a playful or euphemistic way.|She bought some new frillies for her honeymoon. 她为蜜月买了一些新的内衣。|Synonyms: lingerie, underwear, undergarments, lace, ruffles, finery|Antonyms: plain clothes, basics|Noun


frilly:/ˈfrɪli/| adj. |多褶边的，装饰华丽的|Characterized by frills or ruffles; overly ornate or elaborate.|The little girl wore a frilly pink dress. 小女孩穿了一件粉红色的褶边连衣裙。|Synonyms: ruffled, lacy, ornate, fussy, elaborate|Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned|Adjective
