noun
品红，紫红色（magenta的复数）
Plural form of magenta, referring to multiple instances or shades of the vibrant purplish-red color.
The artist used various magentas in her painting.  这位艺术家在她的画作中使用了各种各样的紫红色。
Magentas and deep purples dominated the fashion show.  紫红色和深紫色在时装秀中占据主导地位。
近义词:purples, fuchsias, reddish-purples
反义词:greens, yellows, blues
词性:n.


magenta:/məˈdʒɛntə/
noun, adjective
品红，紫红色
A vibrant purplish-red color.  Can be used as a noun referring to the color itself, or as an adjective describing something of that color.
The dress was a striking magenta.  这件连衣裙是醒目的紫红色。
She printed the poster on magenta paper.  她用紫红色的纸打印了海报。
近义词:fuchsia, purple, reddish-purple
反义词:green, yellow, blue
词性:n., adj.

词源:
From the Italian town of Magenta, Lombardy, where a battle was fought in 1859, shortly after which a new aniline dye of this color was discovered and named.
