auspiciousnesses:/ɔːˈspɪʃəsnɪsɪz/
noun
吉祥；吉兆
The state of being auspicious; an instance of something being auspicious.  A plural form of auspiciousness.
The auspiciousnesses surrounding the birth of the prince were celebrated throughout the kingdom.  王子出生时的种种吉兆在全国各地都得到了庆祝。
Near Synonyms:  propitiousness, favorableness, fortunateness
Antonyms:  inauspiciousness, unfavorableness, misfortune
