gneissosity:/naɪˈsɒsɪti/ | n. | 片麻岩化作用；片麻构造 | The degree to which a rock exhibits gneissic texture, characterized by alternating bands of light and dark minerals.  | The gneissosity of the rock sample suggests it underwent high-grade metamorphism. 这块岩石样本的片麻构造表明它经历了高级变质作用。 | synonyms: foliation, banding | antonyms: homogeneity, isotropy | noun
