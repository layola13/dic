floreal:/ˈflɔːriəl/ | adj. n.| 花的，花季的；花神节的 | Relating to flowers; pertaining to the Floreal, a month in the French Republican Calendar. | The floreal decorations were stunning. 花的装饰令人惊艳。| The Floreal festival celebrates the beauty of spring. 花神节庆祝春天的美丽。 | 近义词: floral, blossoming, flowery | 反义词:  barren, lifeless | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From French *floréal*, from Latin *flōrālis* (“relating to flowers”), from *flōs* (“flower”). The noun refers to a month in the French Republican Calendar (April 20 - May 19), named after the Latin word for flower.
