decarbonization:/diːˌkɑːrbənɪˈzeɪʃn/ /diːˌkɑːrbənəˈzeɪʃn/| n. |脱碳；去碳；碳减排|The process of reducing or removing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, especially as a way to mitigate climate change. |The decarbonization of the economy is a key challenge for the 21st century.  经济脱碳是21世纪的关键挑战。|Synonyms: decarbonisation, carbon reduction, emissions reduction|Antonyms: carbonization, carbon increase|noun
