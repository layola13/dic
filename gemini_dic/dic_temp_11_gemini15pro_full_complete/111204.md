lithontriptic:/ˌlɪθənˈtrɪptɪk/
adjective
碎石的，溶石的
Capable of dissolving or breaking down stones, especially calculi in the kidneys or gallbladder.
The doctor prescribed a lithontriptic medication to help break down the kidney stones. 医生开了溶石的药物来帮助分解肾结石。
Synonyms:antilithic
Antonyms:N/A
词性:adjective


lithontriptic:/ˌlɪθənˈtrɪptɪk/

A medicine or agent that dissolves or breaks down stones, especially calculi in the kidneys or gallbladder.
