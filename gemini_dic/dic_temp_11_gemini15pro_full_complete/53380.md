disagreeableness:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəblnəs/
n.
不愉快,不友好,令人讨厌
the quality of being unpleasant or offensive
Her disagreeableness made it difficult for her to make friends. 她的不友好让她很难交到朋友。
Synonyms: unpleasantness, hostility, animosity, rudeness, unkindness
Antonyms: pleasantness, agreeableness, affability, kindness, cordiality
noun

disagreeableness:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəblnəs/
n.
难相处,乖戾
bad-tempered and unfriendly; difficult to deal with
The child's disagreeableness wore on his parents' nerves.  这孩子的乖戾让他的父母很伤脑筋。
Synonyms: cantankerousness, irascibility, grumpiness, sullenness, churlishness
Antonyms: good-naturedness, amiability, cheerfulness, pleasantness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From disagree + -able + -ness.  Disagree comes from the Old French desagreer,  from des- (expressing negation) + agreer (to agree).
