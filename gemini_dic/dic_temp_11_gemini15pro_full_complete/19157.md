bewailingly:/bɪˈweɪlɪŋli/
adverb
悲叹地，哀号地
in a manner expressing grief or complaint by wailing.
She sat by the coffin bewailingly, tears streaming down her face. 她悲痛欲绝地坐在棺材旁，泪流满面。
The dog howled bewailingly after its owner left. 主人离开后，狗悲鸣地嚎叫。
近义词: plaintively, mournfully, sorrowfully
反义词: joyfully, cheerfully
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From bewail + -ing + -ly.  Bewail comes from Middle English biwailen, from be- + wailen "to wail."  Wail comes from Old Norse væla "to lament."
