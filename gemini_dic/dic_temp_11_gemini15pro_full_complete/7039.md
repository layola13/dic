anecdotal: /ˌænɪkˈdəʊtl/ (US) /ˌænɪkˈdɒtl/ (UK) | adj. |轶事的；趣闻的；传闻的|Based on personal accounts rather than facts or research.  Relating to or consisting of anecdotes.|例句：Anecdotal evidence suggests that the medicine is effective.  传闻证据表明这种药是有效的。

例句：The book is full of amusing anecdotes about his life. 这本书里充满了关于他生活的有趣的轶事。|近义词：informal, unscientific, hearsay |反义词：empirical, scientific, statistical |词性: adjective
