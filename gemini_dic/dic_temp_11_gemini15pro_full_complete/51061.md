desolder:/ˌdiːˈsɑːdər/ /ˌdiːˈsɒldə(r)/| vt.|  拆焊，去焊|To remove solder from (a soldered joint), typically in order to disconnect components.| I had to desolder the connection to replace the faulty capacitor. 我不得不拆焊连接才能更换故障的电容器。| The technician desoldered the chip from the motherboard. 技术员从主板上拆焊了芯片。| 近义词: unsolder| 反义词: solder| 词性: 动词
