It's formed by adding an unnecessary "-es" plural suffix to "nowadays," which is already treated as a plural or singular adverb.  Avoid using this form in formal or standard English. |  Incorrect: Nowadayses, people rely heavily on technology. | Nowadays | Archaic: In olden days | adverb
