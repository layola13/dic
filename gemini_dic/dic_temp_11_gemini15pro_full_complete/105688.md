kinic:/ˈkɪnɪk/ | adj. |奎宁酸的；金鸡纳酸的 | Relating to or derived from quinic acid, a crystalline acid found in cinchona bark and coffee beans. | Quinic acid is a cyclic polyhydroxy acid. 奎宁酸是一种环状多元羟基酸。| The kinic acid pathway is important in plant metabolism. 奎宁酸途径在植物代谢中很重要。 | 近义词:  (No direct synonyms, but related terms include quinate,  referring to salts or esters of quinic acid) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
