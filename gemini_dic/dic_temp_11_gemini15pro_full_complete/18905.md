besmoke:/bɪˈsmoʊk/
v.
熏制，用烟熏，使充满烟
to fill or cover with smoke
The chimney besmoked the entire house. 烟囱把整个房子都熏黑了。

近义词:smoke, fumigate
反义词:clear, ventilate
词性:动词
