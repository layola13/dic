dollman:/ˈdɑːlmən/ | n. |玩偶人；玩偶制造者；玩偶收藏家|A person who makes, collects, or plays with dolls. | He was a skilled dollman, crafting exquisite porcelain figures. 他是一位技艺精湛的玩偶制造者，制作精美的瓷器玩偶。| 近义词: dollmaker, doll collector | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "iconoclast" if the dolls have cultural significance) | noun
