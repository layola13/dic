galvanised:/ˈɡælvənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzd/ (US)| adj.| 镀锌的；刺激；激励 | Coated with zinc to protect from rust; shocked or excited into taking action. |  The roof was made of galvanised steel. 屋顶是用镀锌钢做的。| synonyms: stimulated, energized, roused, spurred, incited, provoked,  | antonyms: demotivated, discouraged, depressed, apathetic | adjective

galvanise:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪz/ (US)| v.|  镀锌；刺激；激励| Coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc. Shock or excite (someone), typically into taking action.|  The news galvanised them into action. 这消息激励他们行动起来。 | synonyms: stimulate, energize, rouse, spur, incite, provoke | antonyms: demotivate, discourage, depress | verb


Etymology:
From French *galvaniser*, from galvanisme ‘galvanism’, named after Luigi Galvani (1737–98), Italian physiologist.
