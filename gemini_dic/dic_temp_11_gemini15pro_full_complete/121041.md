misconduct:/mɪsˈkɑːndʌkt/ | v. |  处理不当，行为不端 | to manage badly or improperly; to behave unprofessionally. | The CEO was accused of misconducting company funds. 首席执行官被指控对公司资金管理不善。 | synonyms: mismanage, mishandle | antonyms: manage well, conduct properly | verb
