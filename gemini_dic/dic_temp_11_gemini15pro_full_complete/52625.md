digged-in:/dɪɡdˈɪn/
adjective
顽固的，坚决的； entrenched, embedded
The enemy troops were dug in and prepared for a long siege.  敌军已挖好战壕，准备进行长期围攻。
近义词: entrenched, embedded, fixed, rooted, immovable, adamant
反义词: flexible, yielding, adaptable

digged-in:/dɪɡdˈɪn/
adjective
(非正式)准备长期抵抗的；settled in, established
They were digged-in in their opposition to the new policy. 他们坚决反对这项新政策。
近义词: resistant, opposed, defiant, unyielding
反义词: compliant, agreeable, amenable
