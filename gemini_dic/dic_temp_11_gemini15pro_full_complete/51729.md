devoiced:/diːˈvɔɪst/| adj.|清音的，非浊音的|Made voiceless; uttered without vibration of the vocal cords.|The final /d/ in "walked" is often devoiced in casual speech. 在日常口语中，“walked”词尾的 /d/ 经常被清音化。|近义词：unvoiced, voiceless|反义词：voiced|词性:形容词

词源:  From de- + voiced, past participle of voice.  Essentially meaning "removed of voice (vocal cord vibration)".
