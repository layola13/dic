geriatrician:/ˌdʒɛrɪəˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |老年病学家，老年医学专家 | A medical doctor who specializes in the care of older adults. |例句：My grandmother sees a geriatrician for her health concerns. 我的祖母去看老年病专家解决她的健康问题。

例句：The geriatrician recommended a new medication for my grandfather's arthritis. 老年病专家建议给我祖父的关节炎使用一种新药。

近义词：gerontologist | 反义词:pediatrician | 词性:noun
