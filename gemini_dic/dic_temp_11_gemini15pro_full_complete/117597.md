-megaly:/ˈmɛɡəli/| n.|肿大，肥大|enlargement, typically of an organ or part|Hepatosplenomegaly is a common finding in various diseases. 肝脾肿大是多种疾病的常见发现。|近义词:enlargement, swelling|反义词:atrophy, shrinking|词性:名词后缀

词源:
From Greek -megalia, from megas (large).  源自希腊语 -megalia，来自 megas（大的）。
