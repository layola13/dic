confederator:/kənˈfɛdəˌreɪtər/ | n. |同盟者，联邦的支持者| A person who joins with others in a confederacy or league; a supporter of a confederation.  | Example:  The confederators signed the treaty to formalize their alliance. 同盟者签署了条约以正式结盟。

| Example:  He was a staunch confederator, believing in the strength of a united front. 他是一位坚定的联邦支持者，相信统一战线的力量。 | Near Synonyms: ally, associate, partner, collaborator | Antonyms: opponent, adversary, enemy | Noun
