enfolding:/ɛnˈfoʊldɪŋ/ /ɪnˈfəʊldɪŋ/
verb|包围，包住，拥抱|The fog was thick, enfolding the city in a damp embrace. 浓雾很厚，将城市笼罩在潮湿的怀抱中。|例句：The darkness enfolds the earth. 黑暗笼罩着大地。|近义词：enveloping, embracing, surrounding, encompassing|反义词：unfolding, revealing, exposing|v.
enfolding:/ɛnˈfoʊldɪŋ/ /ɪnˈfəʊldɪŋ/
adjective|包围的，包住的|The enfolding darkness made it hard to see.  包围的黑暗使人难以看清。|例句：The child felt safe in her mother's enfolding arms. 孩子在母亲环抱的双臂中感到安全。|近义词：enveloping, embracing, surrounding|反义词：unfolding, revealing, exposing|adj.
