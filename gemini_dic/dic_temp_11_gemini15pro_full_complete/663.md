abrogators:/ˈæbrəɡeɪtərz/
n.
废除者，取消者
Those who abrogate something, such as a law, treaty, or custom.
The abrogators of the treaty faced widespread criticism.  这些废除条约的人面临广泛的批评。
synonyms: abolisher, repealer, nullifier, invalidator
antonyms: ratifier, upholder, supporter, maintainer
noun

abrogate:/ˈæbrəɡeɪt/
vt.
废除，取消
To officially end a law, an agreement, or a custom.
The government abrogated the outdated law. 政府废除了过时的法律。
synonyms: abolish, repeal, annul, revoke, rescind
antonyms: enact, establish, institute, uphold, ratify
verb
