cardioinhibitory:/ˌkɑːrdioʊɪnˈhɪbɪtɔːri/ /ˌkɑːdiəʊɪnˈhɪbɪtəri/
adjective
心脏抑制的
Relating to or causing the slowing or stopping of the heartbeat.  Reducing the heart rate.
Example: The vagus nerve has a cardioinhibitory effect on the heart.  迷走神经对心脏有抑制作用。
Example: Certain drugs have cardioinhibitory properties and must be used with caution.  某些药物具有心脏抑制作用，必须谨慎使用。
Synonym: cardiodepressant, negative chronotropic
Antonym: cardiostimulatory, positive chronotropic
词性: adjective
