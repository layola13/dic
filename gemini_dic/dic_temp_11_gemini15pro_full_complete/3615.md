ageings:/ˈeɪdʒɪŋz/
noun
老化，陈化，成熟
the process of growing old or maturing, or the changes that happen to something as it gets older
The ageings of the cheese are carefully controlled.  奶酪的成熟过程受到严格控制。
aging, maturation, ripening
rejuvenation
