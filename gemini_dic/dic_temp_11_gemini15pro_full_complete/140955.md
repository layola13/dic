one-size-fits-all:/ˌwʌn saɪz ˈfɪts ɔːl/
adjective
一体适用的；通用的
Designed to be suitable for a wide range of situations or needs, regardless of individual differences.
This one-size-fits-all approach to education isn't working.  这种一体适用的教育方法行不通。
近义词:universal, generic, standard
反义词:customized, tailored, bespoke
词性:adjective


one-size-fits-all:/ˌwʌn saɪz ˈfɪts ɔːl/
noun
一体适用的东西
Something designed to be suitable for everyone or every purpose.
The government's one-size-fits-all policy on healthcare has been criticized. 政府一体适用的医疗保健政策受到了批评。
近义词:panacea, cure-all
反义词:specialized item, tailored solution
词性:noun
