bribing:/ˈbraɪbɪŋ/
noun, gerund
贿赂; 行贿
The act of giving or offering a bribe.
He was accused of bribing a government official. 他被指控贿赂政府官员。
Synonyms:  suborning, corrupting, greasing someone's palm
Antonyms: honesty, integrity
