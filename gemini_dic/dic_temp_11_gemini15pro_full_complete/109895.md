lianam: /liˈɑːnəm/ | n. | 藤本植物; 藤蔓 | A woody climbing plant that hangs from trees, especially in tropical rainforests. |  The monkeys swung from lianam to lianam through the jungle canopy. 猴子在丛林的树冠层里从一根藤蔓荡到另一根藤蔓。| 近义词: vine, creeper | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑tree 树，bush 灌木等非攀爬植物) | 词性: 名词


词源:  Lianam is borrowed from French *liane*, itself from a Carib word.  Ultimately likely from a word meaning "to bind" or "to tie".
