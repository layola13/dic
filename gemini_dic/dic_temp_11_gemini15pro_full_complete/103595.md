joined-up:/ˌdʒɔɪnd ˈʌp/ | adj. | 连笔的，连接的，联合的 |  Characterized by handwriting in which letters within words are connected by strokes. Also means coordinated or integrated, especially of organizations or plans. |  She has beautiful joined-up handwriting. 她有一手漂亮的连笔字。| synonyms: connected, cursive, integrated, coordinated | antonyms: disconnected, separate, disjointed | adjective

joined-up:/ˌdʒɔɪnd ˈʌp/ | adj. | 联合的，协调的 |  Working together in a coordinated and effective way. | The government departments need a more joined-up approach to tackling crime. 政府部门需要一个更加联合的方式来处理犯罪问题。| synonyms: collaborative, unified, combined | antonyms: fragmented, divided, separate | adjective
