menostasis: /ˌmɛnəˈsteɪsɪs/
n.
月经停止，停经
Cessation of menstruation.
Menostasis is a natural part of aging for women. 停经是女性衰老的自然过程。
The doctor determined that the patient's menostasis was premature. 医生确定该患者的停经过早。
近义词：menopause, amenorrhea
反义词：menarche
词性：名词
