amygdalaceous: /əˌmɪɡdəˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
杏仁状的；似杏仁的；与杏仁核有关的
Relating to, resembling, or shaped like an almond;  specifically, pertaining to the amygdala, a part of the brain involved in processing emotions.
The amygdalaceous nuclei are essential for emotional processing. 杏仁核对于情绪处理至关重要。
The neurologist pointed to the amygdalaceous region of the brain scan. 神经学家指着脑部扫描图上的杏仁状区域。
近义词: almond-shaped, amygdaloid
