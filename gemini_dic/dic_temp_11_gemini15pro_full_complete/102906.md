jawbreakingly:/ˈdʒɔːˌbreɪkɪŋli/| adv.|令人费解地；难 pronunciate 地；拗口地|Extremely difficult to pronounce; so long or complicated as to be difficult to say; astonishingly. |The actor delivered a jawbreakingly long monologue. 演员一口气说了一段长到令人难以置信的独白。|Synonyms:  tongue-twisting, convoluted,  unpronounceable,  complex,  astonishing, staggering|Antonyms: simple, easy, pronounceable, short|词性:副词

jawbreakingly:/ˈdʒɔːˌbreɪkɪŋli/| adv.|令人惊愕地；惊人地|Astonishingly; amazingly. |The view from the mountaintop was jawbreakingly beautiful. 从山顶望下去的景色美得惊人。|Synonyms: stunning, breathtaking, spectacular, awesome, amazing|Antonyms: unimpressive, ordinary, dull|词性:副词
