fly-blind:/flaɪ blaɪnd/
|adv.| 盲目飞行，无资料地进行；冒险行事|To proceed without sufficient information or preparation; to take a risk without knowing the potential consequences.|例句：The company decided to fly blind and launch the product without market research.  公司决定冒险行事，在没有市场调研的情况下推出产品。

例句：With no map and a broken compass, they were forced to fly blind through the storm. 没有地图，指南针也坏了，他们被迫在暴风雨中盲目飞行。|近义词：wing it, improvise, take a chance, take a gamble, take a leap of faith |反义词:prepare, plan, be cautious, be prudent|词性:副词
