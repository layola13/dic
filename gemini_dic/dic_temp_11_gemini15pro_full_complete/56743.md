PHRASE->
close/shut the door on something:  结束，拒绝考虑 | He closed the door on any further negotiations. 他拒绝了进一步的谈判。
open doors: 创造机会 |  Studying abroad opened doors for her career.  出国留学为她的职业生涯创造了机会。
behind closed doors:  秘密地，私下地 | The meeting was held behind closed doors. 会议是秘密举行的。
show someone the door:  把某人赶出去 | He was rude to the customers, so the manager showed him the door. 他对顾客很粗鲁，所以经理把他赶了出去。
at death's door:  濒临死亡 |  He was at death's door after the accident. 事故发生后，他奄奄一息。
