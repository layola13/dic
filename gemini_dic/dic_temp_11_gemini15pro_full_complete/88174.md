hilltopping:/ˈhɪlˌtɑːpɪŋ/
n.
山顶聚集，山頂交配
The aggregation of butterflies and other insects on hilltops, typically for mating.  Insects, especially butterflies, congregate at prominent points in the landscape, such as hilltops, to find mates.
Butterflies were observed engaging in hilltopping behavior. 观察到蝴蝶正在进行山顶聚集行为。

The researchers studied hilltopping behavior in several butterfly species. 研究人员研究了几个蝴蝶物种的山顶聚集行为。

Synonyms:  summiting, peak aggregation, mating aggregation
Antonyms:  valley congregation (not a standard term, illustrative opposite)
noun

Etymology:  A compound word formed from "hill" + "top" + "-ing" (present participle suffix, indicating an ongoing activity).
