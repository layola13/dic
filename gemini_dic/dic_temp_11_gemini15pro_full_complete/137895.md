nonvitalnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈvaɪtəlnəsɪz/
noun
非必要性；不重要性
The state or quality of being nonvital; lack of vital importance.
Due to budget cuts, the company had to eliminate all nonvitalnesses. 由于预算削减，公司不得不取消所有非必要开支。
The report highlighted the nonvitalnesses of certain departments.  这份报告强调了某些部门的不重要性。
Synonyms: unimportance, insignificance, triviality
Antonyms: importance, significance, essentiality
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
vital (adjective): from Latin *vītālis "of or belonging to life", from *vīta "life"
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
