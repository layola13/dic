benefic:/bɪˈnɛfɪk/ | adj. | 有益的，有利的 | Bringing about good; beneficial.  | This herb has benefic properties. 这种草药具有有益的特性。 |  The new policy has had a benefic effect on the economy. 这项新政策对经济产生了有利的影响。 | 近义词: beneficial, advantageous, salutary, wholesome | 反义词: harmful, detrimental, deleterious | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *beneficus, from Latin bene "well" + -ficus "-making," from facere "to do, make."  Related to beneficial.
