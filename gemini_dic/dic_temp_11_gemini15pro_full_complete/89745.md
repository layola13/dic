hoppestere:/ˈhɒpɪstər/ (rare) | n. | 舞者，跳跃者 |  A person who hops or dances, especially one who performs lively, jumping dances.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.  It likely derives from "hop" (to jump) + "-ster" (a suffix indicating a person who performs an action). |  He was a nimble hoppestere, entertaining the crowd with his energetic leaps. 他是一个灵活的舞者，用充满活力的跳跃来娱乐人群。 | 近义词：dancer, jumper, leaper | 反义词: sitter, stander | 词性: noun
