nonconfident:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnfɪdənt/ | adj. |不自信的，缺乏自信的 | Lacking confidence; not self-assured. | She gave a nonconfident presentation, her voice trembling slightly. 她做了一个不自信的演讲，声音微微颤抖。|  He felt nonconfident about his ability to pass the exam.  他对通过考试的能力感到不自信。 | 近义词: insecure, diffident, hesitant, timid, unsure | 反义词: confident, self-assured, assertive, bold | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "confident" (from Latin "confidentem," present participle of "confidere" meaning "to trust").
