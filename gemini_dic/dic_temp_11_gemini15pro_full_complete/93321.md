ill-omened:/ˌɪlˈoʊmənd/ | adj. | 不祥的，凶兆的 |  Characterized by bad omens; portending evil or harm; unlucky.  | The project began on an ill-omened Friday the 13th.  这个项目在一个不祥的星期五13号开始。| Synonyms: sinister, foreboding, portentous | Antonyms: propitious, favorable, auspicious | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From ill + omened (past participle of omen).  "Ill" meaning bad or unfavorable, and "omen" meaning a sign or portent of things to come.
