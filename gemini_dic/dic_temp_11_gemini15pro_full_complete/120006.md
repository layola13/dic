millimetre:/ˈmɪlɪmiːtər/ (US) /ˈmɪlɪmiːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|毫米|A unit of length equal to one thousandth of a metre.|The screw was only a few millimetres long.  螺丝只有几毫米长。|Synonyms: mm, millimeter|Antonyms: kilometre, kilometer|noun

millimeter:/ˈmɪlɪmiːtər/ (US)  /ˈmɪlɪmiːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|毫米|American spelling of millimetre.|The thickness of the sheet is one millimeter. 这张纸的厚度是一毫米。|Synonyms: mm, millimetre|Antonyms: kilometre, kilometer|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *millimètre*, from *milli-* (thousandth) + *mètre* (meter). Ultimately from Greek *μέτρον* (metron, “measure”).
