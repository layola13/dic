overpurchase: /ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrtʃəs/ /ˌəʊvəˈpɜːtʃəs/
verb
过度购买；买得过多
To buy more of something than is needed or can be used.
She overpurchased groceries and had to throw some away. 她购买的食品杂货太多了，不得不扔掉一些。
overbuy, overspend
underspend, underbuy
v.


overpurchase: /ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrtʃəs/ /ˌəʊvəˈpɜːtʃəs/
noun
过度购买；过量采购
The act of buying more of something than is needed or can be used.
The overpurchase of supplies led to storage problems.  物资的过度采购导致了存储问题。
overbuying, excess purchase
underbuying, shortage
n.
