avows:/əˈvaʊz/
|v.|公开承认，公开声明|To declare or state something firmly and publicly.|He avowed his love for her. 他公开承认爱她。|近义词：admits, confesses, declares, proclaims, asserts|反义词：denies, refutes, retracts|verb

avowed:/əˈvaʊd/
|adj.|公开承认的，公开声明的|Openly acknowledged or declared.|He is an avowed supporter of the president. 他是总统的公开支持者。|近义词：admitted, confessed, declared, proclaimed, asserted|反义词：undeclared, unacknowledged, denied|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avouer (Modern French avouer), from Latin advocare "to summon, call to (oneself), claim," from ad- "to" + vocare "to call."
