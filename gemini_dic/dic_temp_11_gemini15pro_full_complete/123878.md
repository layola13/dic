moray:/ˈmɔːreɪ/ /məˈreɪ/| n.|海鳝|A large voracious eel-like fish of warm seas.  It lives in holes and crevices in rocks and coral reefs and has powerful jaws and sharp teeth.|The moray eel lurked in the coral, waiting for unsuspecting prey. 海鳝潜伏在珊瑚中，等待毫无戒心的猎物。

The diver spotted a green moray hiding amongst the rocks. 潜水员发现了一条绿海鳝藏在岩石中。|近义词：eel|反义词:|n. (noun)
