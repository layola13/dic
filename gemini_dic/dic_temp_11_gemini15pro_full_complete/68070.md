faddily:/ˈfædɪli/ | adv. | 浮躁地；流行一时地；时尚地 | In a faddish manner; frivolously and superficially.  Characterized by a fleeting, superficial enthusiasm. |  She dressed faddily, always chasing the latest trends. 她穿着很时髦，总是追逐最新的潮流。| Synonym: trendily, fashionably, stylishly, fleetingly | Antonym: timelessly, classically, enduringly | 词性: 副词

faddish:/ˈfædɪʃ/ | adj. | 浮躁的；流行一时的；时尚的 |  Intensely fashionable for a short time. | The faddish diet promised rapid weight loss but was ultimately unhealthy.  这种流行一时的饮食承诺快速减肥，但最终对健康有害。| Synonym: trendy, fashionable, stylish, fleeting | Antonym: timeless, classic, enduring | 词性: 形容词

fad:/fæd/ | n. | 一时的流行；风尚 | An intense and widely shared enthusiasm for something, especially one that is short-lived and without basis in the object's qualities; a craze. |  The latest fad among teenagers is collecting vintage sneakers.  青少年中最近的流行趋势是收集复古运动鞋。 | Synonym: craze, trend, fashion, vogue | Antonym: tradition, standard, classic | 词性: 名词
