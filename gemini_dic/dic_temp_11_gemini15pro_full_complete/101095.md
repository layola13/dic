Inverness-shire: /ˌɪnvərˈnɛsʃər/ /ˌɪnvərˈnɛʃɪr/ | n. |因弗内斯郡| A former county of the Scottish Highlands, now part of the Highland council area.  It is a historic county, a registration county and a lieutenancy area. |例句：Inverness-shire is known for its stunning scenery. 因弗内斯郡以其迷人的景色而闻名。 |例句：He spent his childhood in Inverness-shire. 他在因弗内斯郡度过了他的童年。 | 近义词: Highland (as a general region),  | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
Inverness (from Scottish Gaelic Inbhir Nis "Mouth of the Ness") + -shire (from Old English scīr "district, care, official charge").  The term "shire" denotes a county, especially in Great Britain.
