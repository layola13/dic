hyperexaltations:/ˌhaɪpərˌɛɡzɔːlˈteɪʃənz/
|n.|过度兴奋，极度亢奋|States of extreme exaltation; excessive enthusiasm or excitement.|His hyperexaltations bordered on mania, making him difficult to be around. 他的过度兴奋近乎狂躁，让他很难相处。|The hyperexaltations of the crowd after the victory were infectious. 胜利后人群的极度兴奋很有感染力。|Near Synonyms:  euphoria, elation, rapture, frenzy|Near Antonyms:  depression, apathy, indifference|noun
