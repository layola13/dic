extract:/ɪkˈstrækt/ /ˈekstrækt/ | v. n. |提取，摘录；榨出，提炼；选段，摘录| To remove or take out something, often with effort or by using a special tool or method. As a noun, it refers to a passage taken from a book, film, piece of music, etc. |

例句：
The dentist extracted my wisdom tooth.
牙医拔掉了我的智齿。

They extracted oil from shale.
他们从页岩中提取石油。

近义词：remove, obtain, excerpt, passage
反义词: insert, inject
词性：verb, noun


extract:/ˈekstrækt/ | n. | 提取物，精华 | A substance that has been extracted from something else. |

例句：
Vanilla extract is used in baking.
香草精用于烘焙。

The extract of the report was published in the newspaper.
报告的摘录发表在报纸上。

近义词：essence, concentrate
反义词: whole, complete
词性：noun
