camelcase:/ˈkæməlˌkeɪs/| n.|驼峰式大小写|A typographical convention in which multiple words are joined together as a single word with the first letter of each of the internal words capitalized, with no spaces or hyphens between them.|Example:  ThisVariableIsWrittenInCamelCase.  这个变量是用驼峰式大小写写的。|近义词:  PascalCase, UpperCamelCase|反义词: snake_case, kebab-case|noun
