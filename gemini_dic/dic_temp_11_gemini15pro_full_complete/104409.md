kadiaster:/kəˈdæstər/| n.|地籍；地籍册；土地登记簿|A comprehensive register of the real estate or real property of a country or region, recording the quantity, value, and ownership of land parcels.|The government is updating the kadiaster to reflect recent property transactions. 政府正在更新地籍以反映最近的房地产交易。|synonyms: cadastre, land register, property register|antonyms: None|noun
