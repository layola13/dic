falling-out:/ˈfɔːlɪŋ aʊt/
n.
争吵，不和
a quarrel or disagreement
例句：They had a falling-out over money. 他们因为钱的事闹翻了。

例句：The two brothers had a bitter falling-out and haven't spoken in years.  两兄弟大吵一架，多年来一直没有说话。

近义词：disagreement, quarrel, argument, dispute, rift
反义词：agreement, reconciliation, accord
词性：名词
