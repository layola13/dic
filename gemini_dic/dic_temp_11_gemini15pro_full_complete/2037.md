aculeate: /əˈkjuːliət/ | n. |  带刺的动物 | An animal with a sting, such as a bee or wasp.  |  Bees and wasps are examples of aculeates. 蜜蜂和黄蜂是带刺动物的例子。 | stinging insect, hymenopteran |  non-stinging insect | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aculeatus*, past participle of *aculeare* "to furnish with prickles," from *aculeus* "prickle, sting, thorn," diminutive of *acus* "needle."
