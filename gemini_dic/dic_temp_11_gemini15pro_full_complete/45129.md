cud:/kʌd/
noun
反刍的食物
partly digested food returned from the first stomach of ruminants to the mouth for further chewing.
The cow chewed its cud contentedly.  奶牛心满意足地嚼着反刍的食物。
Synonyms:bolus, chew
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
