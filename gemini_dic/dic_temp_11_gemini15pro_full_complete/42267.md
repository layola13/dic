counter-book:/ˈkaʊntərˌbʊk/ | n. |对照帐簿; 副本 |A book used to check or verify entries made in another book, especially in accounting. | The accountant used the counter-book to verify the transactions recorded in the main ledger. 会计师使用对照帐簿来核实总分类帐中记录的交易。| The bank teller consulted the counter-book to confirm the customer's deposit.  银行出纳员查阅了副本以确认客户的存款。 | synonym: duplicate, copy, control book | antonym: original | noun
