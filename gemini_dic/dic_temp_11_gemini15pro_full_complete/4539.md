alcaic:/ælˈkeɪɪk/
adjective
阿尔凯克体的，阿尔凯克诗体的
Relating to or written in the style of the Greek lyric poet Alcaeus.
The poem was written in alcaic stanzas. 这首诗是用阿尔凯克诗节写的。
Synonyms: Aeolic, lyric
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider "modern" in a poetic context)
词性: adjective
