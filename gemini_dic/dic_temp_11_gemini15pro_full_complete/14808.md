axel:/ˈæksəl/| n.|阿克塞尔跳，轴，车轴|A figure-skating jump in which the takeoff is from the forward outside edge of one skate, followed by 1½ rotations in the air. Also, a shaft on which a wheel rotates.|例句：He landed the triple axel perfectly. 他完美地完成了三周半跳。|近义词：spin, rotation|反义词:|noun
