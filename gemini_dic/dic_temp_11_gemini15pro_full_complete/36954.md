colouristic:/ˌkʌləˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 色彩的，着色的 | Relating to or characterized by the use of colour, especially in a striking or unusual way. |  Her paintings are highly colouristic, emphasizing bold hues and contrasts. 她的绘画色彩丰富，强调大胆的色调和对比。

The film's colouristic style is reminiscent of early Technicolor movies.  这部电影的色彩风格让人想起早期的彩色电影。 | 近义词: chromatic, colorful, vivid | 反义词:  monochromatic, drab, dull | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From French *coloristique*, from *couleur* (color) + *-istique* (suffix forming adjectives relating to a particular art or science). Ultimately from Latin *color*.
