cloistered:/ˈklɔɪstərd/| adj.|隐居的，与世隔绝的|Shut away from the world, as if in a cloister (a monastery or convent).| The writer led a cloistered life, rarely leaving his home.  这位作家过着隐居的生活，很少离开家。|  The monks lived a cloistered existence, dedicated to prayer and study. 僧侣们过着与世隔绝的生活，致力于祈祷和学习。| 近义词：secluded, sheltered, isolated, withdrawn| 反义词: worldly, public, open, exposed |词性:形容词

cloister:/ˈklɔɪstər/| n. vt.|修道院，回廊；禁锢，使隔离|A covered walkway, especially in a religious building, or a place of religious seclusion.|The monks walked in the cloister. 僧侣们在回廊里散步。|  She cloistered herself in her study to finish the book. 她把自己关在书房里完成这本书。| 近义词：monastery, convent, abbey, sanctuary| 反义词: world, outside, public|词性:名词,动词
