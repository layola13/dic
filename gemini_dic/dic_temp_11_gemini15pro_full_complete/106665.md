kuskus:/ˈkʊskʊs/| n. |库斯库斯（一种北非食物）|A North African dish of semolina steamed over a stew of meat and vegetables.|We had couscous for dinner last night. 我们昨晚晚餐吃了库斯库斯。|近义词:semolina (粗粒小麦粉)|反义词: None (没有确切的反义词)|词性:名词

Etymology: From Berber *kǝsksu, meaning "well-rolled, well-formed, rounded".
