millennium:/mɪˈlɛniəm/ /məˈlɛniəm/ | n. | 千年，千禧年 |A period of one thousand years, especially as calculated from the traditional date of the birth of Christ. | The third millennium began on January 1, 2001.  第三个千年始于2001年1月1日。

The Roman Empire spanned over a millennium. 罗马帝国跨越了一千多年。


近义词： chiliad | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially a very short period) | 词性: noun
