antimonopolistic:/ˌæntiˌmənɒpəˈlɪstɪk/ /ˌæntiˌmɑːnəpəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反垄断的
Relating to or opposing monopolies or monopolistic practices.  Designed to prevent or control monopolies or trusts.
The government implemented antimonopolistic measures to protect consumers from unfair pricing. 政府实施了反垄断措施以保护消费者免受不公平定价。
Congress passed antimonopolistic legislation to promote competition in the marketplace. 国会通过了反垄断立法以促进市场竞争。
近义词:antitrust, trust-busting
反义词:monopolistic, pro-monopoly
词性:adjective
