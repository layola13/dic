gramashes: /ˈɡræməʃɪz/ | n. |  绑腿；护胫 | Leggings or gaiters, usually made of canvas or leather, worn to protect the legs below the knee. | He wore gramashes to protect his legs from thorns while hiking. 他在徒步旅行时戴着绑腿，以保护他的腿免受荆棘的伤害。 | 近义词: gaiters, leggings | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

词源: From French *gamaches*, from Spanish *polainas* or Catalan *polaines*, ultimately of unknown origin.
