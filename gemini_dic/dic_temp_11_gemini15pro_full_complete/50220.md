贫瘠的；耗尽的；缺乏的
Lacking in variety or numbers of species. Made poor or barren.
例句：The depauperate soil yielded a meager harvest.  贫瘠的土壤只产出了微薄的收成。

例句：The island's flora was depauperate due to its isolation.  由于与世隔绝，岛上的植物种类匮乏。

近义词：impoverished, depleted, meager, barren, scant
反义词：rich, abundant, fertile, plentiful, diverse
词性: adjective
