liturgiologist:/ˌlɪtərˈdʒiɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 礼仪学家 | A scholar of liturgy, the customary public worship performed by a religious group. | The liturgiologist explained the historical significance of the different vestments worn by the priest.  礼仪学家解释了神父所穿不同法衣的历史意义。| Synonym: ritualist, ceremonialist | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider "iconoclast" in a broader sense) | noun

liturgiologist:/ˌlɪtərˈdʒiɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 礼拜仪式学家 |  A specialist in the study of liturgies. | The liturgiologist gave a fascinating lecture on the evolution of the Eucharist. 礼拜仪式学家就圣餐礼的发展做了一个精彩的演讲。| Synonym:  expert in liturgy | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
