apportioner:/əˈpɔːrʃənər/
n.
分配者，分摊者
A person or thing that apportions something.
The apportioner divided the funds equally among the departments.  分配者将资金平均分配给各个部门。
Synonyms: distributor, allocator, assigner
Antonyms: collector, gatherer
noun
