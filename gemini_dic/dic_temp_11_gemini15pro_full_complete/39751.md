consolatoriness:/kənˌsɒləˈtɔːrɪnɪs/ | n. | 安慰，慰藉; 抚慰; 令人欣慰 | The quality of being consoling; the ability to give comfort or solace. |  The consolatoriness of her words brought him some peace. 她的安慰话语给他带来了一些平静。 | Synonyms: comfort, solace, consolation | Antonyms: distress, grief, sorrow | noun

consolatory:/kənˈsɒlət(ə)ri/ | adj. | 安慰的; 慰藉的; 令人欣慰的 | Offering or providing comfort or solace. |  She gave him a consolatory pat on the back. 她安慰地拍了拍他的背。 | Synonyms: comforting, soothing, reassuring | Antonyms: distressing, upsetting, worrying | adjective
