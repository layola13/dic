inferences:/ˈɪnfərənsɪz/
n.|推论，推断，结论|The detective made several inferences based on the evidence.  侦探根据证据做出了几个推断。|
inferences:|n.|推论，推断，结论|From his silence, I drew the inference that he was angry.  从他的沉默中，我推断他生气了。|
inferences:|n.|推论，推断，结论|Scientists are drawing inferences about the planet's atmosphere. 科学家正在对这颗行星的大气层做出推断。|近义词：conclusions, deductions, interpretations|反义词：facts, evidence, premises|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *inference*, from Late Latin *inferentia*, from Latin *īnferō* (“bring in, bring upon, conclude”) (from *in- + ferō* (“bear, carry”)).
