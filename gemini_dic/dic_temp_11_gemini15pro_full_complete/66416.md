executorial:/ˌɛksɪˈkjuːtɔːriəl/
adjective
执行的；遗嘱执行的
Relating to the execution of a will or other legal document, or to the duties of an executor.
The executorial process can be complex and time-consuming. 遗嘱执行程序可能复杂且耗时。
The lawyer provided executorial services to the family.  律师向该家庭提供了遗嘱执行服务。
近义词: administrative, executive
反义词:  N/A
词性: adjective

executorial:/ˌɛksɪˈkjuːtɔːriəl/
noun (rare)
执行权;执行令
The right or authority to execute a will or other legal document.  
The executorial was granted to the deceased's eldest son. 执行权被授予了死者的长子。(This usage is less common)
近义词: mandate, authority
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *executorialis, from Latin executor (“executor”). See execute.
