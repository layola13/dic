outwing:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋ/
verb
超越，胜过，在侧翼飞行并超越
To surpass or excel someone, especially in flight or maneuverability, by gaining a superior position on the wing or flank.
The fighter pilot outwinged his opponent and gained a tactical advantage.  这名战斗机飞行员胜过了他的对手，获得了战术优势。
The experienced sailor outwinged the novice during the regatta.  这位经验丰富的帆船运动员在帆船赛中胜过了新手。
synonym: outmaneuver, surpass, outflank, excel
antonym: lag behind, trail, fall behind
