chivalries: /ˈʃɪvəlriz/ | n. | 骑士精神，骑士制度；侠义行为 |  The acts and spirit characteristic of the ideal knight; especially, courtesy, generosity, valor, and dexterity in arms. |  He displayed great chivalries in defending the weak. 他在保护弱者时展现了伟大的骑士精神。|  The knights of old were known for their chivalries. 古代骑士以其骑士精神而闻名。|近义词：courtesies, gallantry, heroism, nobility, valor | 反义词：cowardice, rudeness, cruelty, meanness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *chevalerie, from chevaler "knight," from Late Latin caballarius "horseman," from caballus "horse."
