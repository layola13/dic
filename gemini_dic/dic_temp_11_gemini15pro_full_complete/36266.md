cohabit:/kəʊˈhæbɪt/ /koʊˈhæbɪt/| v. |同居，姘居|To live together in the same dwelling as a couple, typically without being married.|They decided to cohabit before getting married. 他们决定在结婚前同居。|近义词：live together, shack up|反义词: live apart, separate|动词

cohabitation:/kəʊˌhæbɪˈteɪʃn/ /koʊˌhæbɪˈteɪʃn/| n. |同居|The state of living together as a couple, especially without being married.|Cohabitation is becoming increasingly common in modern society. 在现代社会，同居变得越来越普遍。|近义词：living together|反义词:marriage, separation|名词
