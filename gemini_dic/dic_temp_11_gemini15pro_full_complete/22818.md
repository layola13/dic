bosphorus:/ˈbɒsfərəs/ /ˈbɑːspərəs/| n.|博斯普鲁斯海峡; 伊斯坦布尔海峡|A strait connecting the Black Sea and the Sea of Marmara.|例句：Istanbul is located on the Bosphorus. 伊斯坦布尔位于博斯普鲁斯海峡。|例句：Ships from all over the world pass through the Bosphorus. 来自世界各地的船只都经过博斯普鲁斯海峡。|近义词：strait, channel|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek Βόσπορος (Bósporos), meaning "ox-ford" or "cow-ford."  It likely refers to the myth of Io, who was transformed into a cow and swam across the strait.
