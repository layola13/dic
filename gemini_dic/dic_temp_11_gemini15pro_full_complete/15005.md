babelish:/ˈbeɪbəlɪʃ/| adj.|含糊不清的，言语混乱的|Resembling the confusion of the Tower of Babel; incomprehensible; gibberish.  Characterized by a mixture of languages or dialects; confused, unintelligible speech or writing.|例句：His babelish explanation of the complex theory left everyone confused. 他对这个复杂理论含糊不清的解释让每个人都感到困惑。

例句：The tourists struggled to understand the babelish chatter of the street vendors. 游客们很难听懂街头小贩们含糊不清的叫卖声。|近义词：gibberish, jargon, unintelligible, confused, incomprehensible|反义词：clear, understandable, coherent, articulate, lucid|词性:形容词
