lumpectomies: /ˌləmˈpɛktəmiz/ | n. | 乳房肿块切除术 | Surgical removal of a lump or tumor from the breast, typically for biopsy or treatment of breast cancer. |  She had two lumpectomies to remove benign tumors. 她做了两次乳房肿块切除术以切除良性肿瘤。|  The lumpectomy was successful in removing the cancerous tissue. 乳房肿块切除术成功地切除了癌变组织。| 近义词: partial mastectomy, breast-conserving surgery | 反义词: mastectomy | 词性: noun
