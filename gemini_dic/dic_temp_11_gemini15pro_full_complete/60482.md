ejector:/ɪˈdʒɛktər/| n.|喷射器; 弹射器; 排出器;|A device that ejects something. The pilot activated the ejector seat and safely escaped the crashing plane.|例句：The ejector seat saved the pilot's life. 弹射座椅救了飞行员的命。

例句：The ink cartridge ejector wasn't working properly. 墨盒弹出器无法正常工作。|近义词：propeller, ejecting device, launcher|反义词:injector|noun
