doilies:/ˈdɔɪliz/ (US) /ˈdɔɪliːz/ (UK)| n. |垫子，小饰巾|Small ornamental mats, often crocheted or lace, typically placed under plates, vases, or other objects to protect furniture surfaces.|She placed the cake on a delicate doily. 她把蛋糕放在一个精致的垫子上。|The antique dresser was covered with doilies. 古董梳妆台上铺满了小饰巾。|Synonyms: placemat, coaster, mat|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps tablecloth as a larger covering)|Noun
