descended:/dɪˈsɛndɪd/| v. adj.|下降，遗传，突袭|To move from a higher to a lower place; to be a relative of a past person or group; to attack suddenly.|例句：The hikers descended the mountain.  徒步旅行者下山了。|近义词：go down, decline, derive, originate|反义词:ascend, climb, rise|词性:动词
descended:/dɪˈsɛndɪd/| adj.|下降的，遗传的|Coming from an ancestor or source.|例句：She is descended from royalty. 她出身皇室。|近义词：derived, inherited, ancestral|反义词: ascended|词性:形容词
