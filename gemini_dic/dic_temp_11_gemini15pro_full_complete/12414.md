asininity:/ˌæsɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. | 愚蠢，蠢事 | Extreme stupidity or foolishness. |  His asininity was evident in his decision to invest all his savings in a pyramid scheme. 他的愚蠢体现在他把所有积蓄都投资于传销的决定上。

| Example: The sheer asininity of the plan was breathtaking.  这个计划的愚蠢程度令人叹为观止。 | Synonym: stupidity, foolishness, idiocy, absurdity | Antonym: wisdom, intelligence, brilliance, sagacity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *asininitas*, from asininus (“asinine, stupid”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  Ultimately from asinus (“donkey, ass”).
