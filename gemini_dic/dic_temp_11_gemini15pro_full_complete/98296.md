insiders:/ɪnˈsaɪdərz/ | n. |内部人士；知情人；圈内人|A person who has special knowledge or access to confidential information within a group or organization. |例句：The insiders were able to profit from the company's confidential information. 知情人士能够利用公司的机密信息获利。

例句：Company insiders are not allowed to trade on non-public information. 公司内部人士不得利用非公开信息进行交易。


近义词：informant, confidant, expert|反义词:outsider|词性:名词
