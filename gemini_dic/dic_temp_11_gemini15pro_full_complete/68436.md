falterers:/ˈfɔːltərərz/
noun
踌躇者；蹒跚者；支吾者
Those who falter or stumble, either literally or figuratively.  Those who hesitate or waver in their purpose, action, or speech.
The falterers in the race soon fell behind.  比赛中犹豫不决的人很快就落后了。
synonyms: hesitators, waverers, doubters, stammerers
antonyms:  determined, resolute, confident, fluent
词性: 名词

falter:/ˈfɔːltər/
verb
支吾；蹒跚；踉跄；动摇
To speak hesitantly or brokenly. To lose strength or momentum and begin to fail. To move unsteadily or hesitantly.
His voice faltered as he spoke about the accident. 他谈到事故时，声音颤抖了。
synonyms: hesitate, waver, stumble, stutter
antonyms:  continue, persevere, proceed, flow
词性: 动词
