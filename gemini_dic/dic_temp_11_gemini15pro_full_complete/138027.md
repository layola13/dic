noosers:/ˈnuːzərz/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A derogatory term for someone considered foolish or unintelligent.  It's primarily Australian slang.|例句：Those noosers lost all their money on a bad investment. 那些傻瓜在一笔糟糕的投资中损失了所有的钱。|例句：Don't be such a nooser!  别这么傻！|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, dimwit, dope|反义词:genius, smart person|词性:noun
