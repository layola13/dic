houndish:/ˈhaʊndɪʃ/
adjective
像猎犬的；卑鄙的；追逐的
Resembling or characteristic of a hound; relating to or engaged in the relentless pursuit of something.
He had a houndish look about him, sniffing out every opportunity. 他看起来像猎犬一样，嗅探着每一个机会。
The paparazzi were houndish in their pursuit of the celebrity. 狗仔队对名人的追逐不依不饶。
synonyms:dogged, relentless, persistent, tenacious, unrelenting
antonyms:apathetic, indifferent, passive
词性:形容词
