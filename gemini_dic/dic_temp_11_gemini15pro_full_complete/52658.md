digitigrade:/ˈdɪdʒɪtɪˌɡreɪd/| adj. n.|趾行的；趾行動物|Walking on the toes, or relating to animals that walk on their toes.  An animal that walks on its toes.|例句：Cats and dogs are digitigrade animals. 猫和狗是趾行動物。

例句：The digitigrade stance of the cheetah allows it to run at incredible speeds. 猎豹的趾行姿态使其能够以惊人的速度奔跑。|近义词：unguligrade (specifically hoofed animals)|反义词：plantigrade|词性: adjective, noun


digitigrade:/ˈdɪdʒɪtɪˌɡreɪd/| n.|趾行動物|An animal that walks on its toes.|例句：The cheetah, a digitigrade, can achieve remarkable speeds. 猎豹，一种趾行动物，可以达到非凡的速度。

例句：Many predators are digitigrades, enabling them to move swiftly and silently. 许多掠食者是趾行动物，这使它们能够快速而安静地移动。|近义词：ungulate (specifically hoofed digitigrades)|反义词：plantigrade|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *digitus* (finger, toe) + *-gradus* (walking), from *gradi* (to walk).
