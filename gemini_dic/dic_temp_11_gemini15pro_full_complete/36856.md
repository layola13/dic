colophon: /ˈkɒləfən/ (UK), /ˈkɑːləfɑːn/ (US) | n. | 出版商标记；版权页；结束语 |  A publisher's emblem or imprint, especially one printed on the title page or spine of a book.  It can also refer to a brief statement at the end of a book giving details of its publication. |  The colophon featured a woodcut of a printing press.  版权页上有一个木刻印刷机的图案。 | synonyms: emblem, imprint, device, logo, trademark, insignia | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a specific term. Perhaps "anonymity" in the sense of hiding the publisher's identity could be considered a loose antonym) | noun
