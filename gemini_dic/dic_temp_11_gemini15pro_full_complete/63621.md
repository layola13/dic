enzootic:/ɛnzoʊˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 地方性的（疾病）；（疾病）限于某一地区的 | Regularly affecting animals in a particular district or at a particular season. | Example 1: The enzootic disease was confined to a small area.  这种地方性疾病仅限于一个小地区。

Example 2:  Researchers are studying the enzootic bovine leukosis in the region. 研究人员正在研究该地区的地方性牛白血病。 | Near synonyms: endemic, regional, localized | Antonyms: epidemic, pandemic, widespread | Adjective
