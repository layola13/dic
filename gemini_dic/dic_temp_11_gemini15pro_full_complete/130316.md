non-technically:/ˌnɒnˈtɛknɪkli/ | adv. | 非技术性地，通俗地 |  Explained in a way that avoids technical terms and jargon, making it easy for anyone to understand. |  He explained the complex process non-technically so that everyone could grasp it. 他用通俗易懂的方式解释了这个复杂的过程，以便每个人都能理解。| Synonyms: simply, plainly, easily, clearly | Antonyms: technically, complexly, intricately | adverb
