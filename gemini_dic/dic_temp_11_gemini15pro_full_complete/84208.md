half-ripe:/ˌhɑːf ˈraɪp/ /ˌhæf ˈraɪp/| adj.|半熟的，未完全成熟的|Partially ripened; not fully mature.  Relating to fruit or vegetables that are not yet fully ripe, but are beginning to develop their mature color and flavor.|The half-ripe mangoes were still a bit tart. 这些半熟的芒果还有点酸。|The tomatoes were half-ripe, showing a blend of green and red. 这些西红柿半熟，呈现出绿色和红色的混合。|Near-ripe, partially ripe|Ripe, overripe|adjective
