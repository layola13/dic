greenfield:/ˈɡriːnfiːld/ | adj. n. |  未开发的，全新的；绿地； |  Relating to or denoting a site that has not been built on before, or an area of undeveloped land.  A piece of undeveloped land. |  The company decided to build their new factory on a greenfield site.  该公司决定在一片未开发的土地上建造他们的新工厂。 The project is a greenfield development. 这个项目是一个全新的开发项目。 | 近义词：undeveloped, virgin, pristine | 反义词：developed, urban, brownfield | 词性：形容词，名词

Etymology:
From 'green' (referring to vegetation or a lack of development) + 'field' (a piece of open land).
