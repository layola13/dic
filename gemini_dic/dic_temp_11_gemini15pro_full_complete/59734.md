eco-friendly:/ˈiːkoʊˈfrɛndli/
adjective
环保的；对环境无害的
Not harmful to the environment.
例句：
We try to use eco-friendly cleaning products.
我们尽量使用环保的清洁产品。

This company is committed to developing eco-friendly packaging.
这家公司致力于开发环保包装。

近义词：environmentally friendly, green, sustainable
反义词：eco-destructive, environmentally harmful
词性：adjective
