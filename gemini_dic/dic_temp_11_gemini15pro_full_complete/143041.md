outblown:/ˌaʊtˈbloʊn/
adjective
吹散的，盛开的
blown out; extinguished, as a flame or light. Expanded to full bloom or size, especially excessively so.
例句：The outblown rose petals littered the ground.  吹落了的玫瑰花瓣散落在地上。

例句：The outblown sails of the ship billowed in the wind. 船鼓胀的风帆在风中飘扬。
近义词：expanded, full-blown, open, unfolded
反义词：unblown, closed, furled, budding
词性: adjective
