nonballoting:/ˌnɒnˈbælətɪŋ/
adjective
非投票的;没有投票权的
Not entitled to vote; not having voted.
Nonballoting members of the committee were allowed to attend the meeting. 没有投票权的委员会成员被允许参加会议。
The nonballoting stockholders could not influence the outcome of the election.  没有投票权的股东无法影响选举结果。
synonym: voteless, disenfranchised
antonym: voting, enfranchised
词性:adjective
