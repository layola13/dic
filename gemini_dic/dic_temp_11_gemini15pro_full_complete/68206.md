fair-trading:/ˌfer ˈtreɪdɪŋ/ | n. |公平交易；公平贸易；公平贸易|Trade in which fair prices are paid to producers in developing countries.|例句：Fair-trading practices ensure that workers receive a living wage. 公平交易行为确保工人获得生活工资。

例句：The company is committed to fair-trading principles. 这家公司致力于公平贸易原则。|近义词：ethical trade, responsible trade|反义词：unfair trade, exploitative trade|词性:名词

fair-trade:/ˌfer ˈtreɪd/ | adj. |公平交易的|Relating to or supporting fair trade.|例句：She always buys fair-trade coffee. 她总是买公平交易的咖啡。

例句：The fair-trade movement has grown significantly in recent years. 公平贸易运动近年来发展迅速。|近义词：ethical, responsible|反义词：unethical, irresponsible|词性:形容词
