casuistic:/ˌkæʒuˈɪstɪk/ /ˌkæʃuˈɪstɪk/
adjective
诡辩的，决疑法的
Relating to casuistry; involving or employing subtle and often specious reasoning, especially about moral issues.
例句：His argument seemed more casuistic than truly moral. 他的论点看起来更像是诡辩而不是真正的道德。

例句：The lawyer's casuistic defense of the defendant was met with skepticism. 律师对被告的诡辩式辩护遭到了质疑。

近义词：sophistic, quibbling, specious
反义词：principled, straightforward, honest
词性：adjective
