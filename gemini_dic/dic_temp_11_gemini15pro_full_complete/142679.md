ot:/ɒt/ | n. |  one-time password (OTP) | A one-time password is a automatically generated string of characters that authenticates a user for a single transaction or login session. | Example:  I need the OTP to access my bank account. 我需要一次性密码才能访问我的银行账户。| Synonym: one-time pin, dynamic password | Antonym: static password | noun
