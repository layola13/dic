harpsichord: /ˈhɑːrpsɪkɔːrd/ (US) /ˈhɑːpsɪkɔːd/ (UK)| n. |大键琴|A keyboard instrument resembling a grand piano but having strings that are plucked by leather or quill plectra. |例句：The delicate sound of the harpsichord filled the concert hall.  大键琴的优美声音充满了音乐厅。

例句：Bach wrote many pieces for the harpsichord. 巴赫为大键琴创作了许多作品。|近义词：clavier, virginal|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps piano as a contrasting instrument)|词性:noun
