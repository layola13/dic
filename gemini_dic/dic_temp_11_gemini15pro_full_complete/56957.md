douanier:/duːɑːnˈjeɪ/ /duːɑːnjeɪ/
n.
海关官员；关税员
A customs officer.
The douanier checked our passports and luggage. 海关官员检查了我们的护照和行李。
近义词:customs officer, customs agent
反义词:smuggler
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *douanier*, from *douane* ("customs"), from Italian *dogana*, from Arabic *دِيوَان* (*dīwān*, "council, custom house"). Ultimately from Persian *دیوان* (*dīvān*).
