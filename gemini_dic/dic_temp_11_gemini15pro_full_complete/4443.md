alb's:/ælbz/ | n. | 白袍 |  A plural possessive form of "alb," referring to the white linen vestment worn by clergy. | The priest carefully folded the albs after the service.  神父在仪式结束后仔细地叠好了白袍。 | 近义词: vestments, robes | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "street clothes") | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *albe*, from Old French *albe*, from Latin *alba* ("white").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *albʰós* ("white").
