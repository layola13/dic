nonmutably:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtəbli/ | adv. | 不可变地；永久地 | In a nonmutable manner; permanently. | The data is stored nonmutably, ensuring its integrity. 数据以不可变的方式存储，确保其完整性。 | The contract is nonmutably bound to this address.  合同不可变更地绑定到此地址。 | 近义词: immutably, permanently, unchangeably | 反义词: mutably, changeably | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix) + mutable + -ly
* non-:  a prefix meaning "not," freely used as an English formative, especially of adjectives and their derivatives (nonabrasive; nonacceptance; nonacid; nonactive).
* mutable:  from Late Latin mutabilis "changeable," from Latin mutare "to change" (from PIE root *mei- (1) "to change, go, move").
* -ly: adverbial suffix
