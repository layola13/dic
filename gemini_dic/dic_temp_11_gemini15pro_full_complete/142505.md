oso:/ˈosoʊ/ (US), /ˈɒsəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.| daring, bold; bear (Spanish)|A Spanish word meaning "daring" or "bold" as an adjective, or "bear" as a noun.  It's often used in English when referring to the bear, especially in scientific or zoological contexts.|例句：The oso pardo is a magnificent animal.  棕熊是一种壮丽的动物。|近义词：(daring) audacious, brave, intrepid; (bear) Ursus (scientific name)|反义词:(daring) timid, cowardly|词性: adjective, noun

oso:/ˈosoʊ/ (US), /ˈɒsəʊ/ (UK)| n.| bear (Spanish)|"Oso" in Spanish means "bear". It is often used in compound words related to bears, like "oso hormiguero" (anteater).|例句：Vi un oso en el zoológico. I saw a bear at the zoo.|近义词：bear, Ursus|反义词：N/A (Animals typically don't have direct antonyms)|词性: noun
