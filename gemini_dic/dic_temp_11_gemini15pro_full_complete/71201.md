fistulate:/ˈfɪstʃəˌleɪt/ /ˈfɪstjʊˌleɪt/| v. |形成瘘管；使成瘘管|To make or become fistulous; to develop a fistula.|The infection caused the wound to fistulate.|近义词：ulcerate, suppurate|反义词: heal|词性:动词

fistulous:/ˈfɪstʃələs/ /ˈfɪstjʊləs/| adj. |瘘管的；有瘘管的|Characterized by or containing a fistula.|The patient presented with a fistulous tract.|近义词：ulcerated, suppurating|反义词: healthy|词性:形容词

fistula:/ˈfɪstʃələ/ /ˈfɪstjʊlə/| n. |瘘管|An abnormal passage or tube between two internal organs or leading from an internal organ to the surface of the body.|The surgeon repaired the fistula in his bowel.|近义词：sinus, tract|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
