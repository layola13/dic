noninherent:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɪərənt/ | adj. | 非固有的，非内在的 | Not inherent; not forming an essential or permanent part of something. | The problems are noninherent, caused by external factors. 这些问题并非固有的，是由外部因素造成的。| The fragility of the vase is noninherent, a result of poor craftsmanship.  这个花瓶的易碎性并非本身固有，而是糟糕工艺造成的。 | 近义词: extrinsic, accidental, incidental, adventitious | 反义词: inherent, intrinsic, essential, innate | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "inherent" (from Latin "inhaerēns," present participle of "inhaerēre" "to stick in, cling to, adhere to").
