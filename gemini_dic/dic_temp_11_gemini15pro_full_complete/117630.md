megastardoms: /ˈmɛɡəˌstɑːrdəmz/ | n. | 巨星地位；超级明星身份 |  The state of being a megastar, encompassing fame, influence, and often wealth.  It refers to the collective status of multiple individuals who have achieved this level of celebrity. | Many young actors dream of achieving megastardoms like those of Hollywood legends. 许多年轻演员都梦想着能达到像好莱坞传奇人物那样的巨星地位。|  Hollywood megastardoms can be fleeting. 好莱坞的巨星地位可能转瞬即逝。 | Synonym: superstardom, celebrity | Antonym: obscurity, anonymity | noun
