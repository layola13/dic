beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/ | v. | 欺骗；哄骗；使着迷 | To charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way. |  She beguiled them with her beauty and wit. 她用她的美貌和智慧迷惑了他们。

The salesman beguiled the old lady into buying a worthless necklace.  推销员哄骗老太太买了一条毫无价值的项链。

| 近义词: charm, enchant, captivate, allure, seduce, deceive, trick | 反义词: repel, disgust, disenchant |词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bigilen*, from Old French *guiler* (“to deceive”), of Germanic origin.
