hunted:/ˈhʌntɪd/| v. adj.| 猎取，追捕；被追捕的，感到焦虑的 |  past tense and past participle of hunt.  Means to chase and kill (wild animals) for sport or food, or to search persistently for someone or something.| The poachers hunted elephants for their ivory. 偷猎者捕猎大象以获取象牙。| synonyms: chased, pursued, tracked, sought, searched for | antonyms: protected, preserved| verb
