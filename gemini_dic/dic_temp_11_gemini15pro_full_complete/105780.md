kirschwasser:/ˈkɪəʃˌvɑːsər/ /ˈkɪrʃˌwɑːsər/| n. |樱桃白兰地；樱桃酒|A clear, colorless brandy distilled from fermented cherries, especially the morello cherry.  It is typically unsweetened. |例句：He added a splash of kirschwasser to his dessert. 他在甜点里加了一点樱桃白兰地。

例句：Kirschwasser is a traditional ingredient in Black Forest gateau. 樱桃酒是黑森林蛋糕的传统配料。|近义词：cherry brandy, kirsch|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German Kirschwasser, literally "cherry water," from Kirsch "cherry" (from Old High German kirisča, from Vulgar Latin *ceresia, from Latin cerasus "cherry tree," from Greek kerasos) + Wasser "water" (from Old High German wazzar).
