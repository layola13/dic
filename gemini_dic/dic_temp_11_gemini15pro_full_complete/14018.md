auroral:/əˈrɔːrəl/
adjective
极光的；曙光的；玫瑰色的
Relating to or resembling the aurora borealis or aurora australis; characteristic of the dawn.
The auroral display was breathtaking.  极光景色令人叹为观止。
近义词:polar, northern, dawn
反义词:crepuscular, nocturnal

词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "Aurora" (goddess of the dawn) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives).
