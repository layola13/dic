medievally:/ˌmediˈiːvəli/ /ˌmiːdiˈiːvəli/ | adv. | 中世纪地，中古时代地 | In a manner characteristic of the Middle Ages. | The castle was medievally decorated with tapestries and suits of armor.  这座城堡以中世纪风格装饰，挂着挂毯和盔甲。|  The manuscript was medievally illuminated with intricate gold leaf designs.  这份手稿以中世纪风格用复杂的金色叶子图案进行装饰。 | 近义词: archaically, anciently | 反义词: modernly, contemporarily | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Medieval + -ly.  Medieval comes from  Latin *medius* (middle) + *aevum* (age).
