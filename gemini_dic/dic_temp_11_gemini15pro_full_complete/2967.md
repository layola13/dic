adzuki:/ædˈzuːkiː/
n.
红豆，赤豆
A small, round, dark red bean grown throughout East Asia and used in many Asian dishes.
Adzuki beans are often used in Japanese sweets. 红豆经常用于制作日式甜点。
The adzuki bean paste is a popular filling for mochi. 红豆沙是麻糬的常见馅料。

近义词:red bean
反义词:(无)
词性:noun
