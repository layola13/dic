isthmoid:/ˈɪsθmɔɪd/| adj. n.|峡部的，峡部|Relating to or resembling an isthmus; narrow or constricted.  A bone that forms part of the cranial floor and separates the nasal cavity from the brain. |The isthmoid bone is a complex structure. 筛骨是一个复杂的结构。| The isthmoid sinus is located deep within the skull. 筛窦位于颅骨深处。|近义词:isthmian, narrow, constricted|反义词:wide, broad|adjective, noun
