contaminous:/kənˈtæmɪnəs/| adj.|接触传染的；污染的|Spreading by contact, especially through the air; tending to contaminate.  Capable of transmitting a disease, such as by direct contact.|The patient was placed in isolation because the disease was highly contaminous.|近义词:contagious, infectious, communicable|反义词:noncontagious, sterile|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin contaminōsus, from Latin contaminātus (past participle of contamināre ‘to contaminate’), from contamen ‘pollution’.
