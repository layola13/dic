boanerges:/ˌboʊəˈnɜːrdʒiːz/| n. | 雷子 | A name given by Jesus to James and John, the sons of Zebedee, probably referring to their fiery or enthusiastic nature.  It translates to "sons of thunder." |  The preacher was a real boanerges, his voice booming through the sanctuary.  这位传教士真是个雷子，他的声音在圣殿中回荡。 | synonyms: firebrand, zealot | antonyms: pacifist, moderate | noun
