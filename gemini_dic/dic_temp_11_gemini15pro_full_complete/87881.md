hidrotic:/haɪˈdrɒtɪk/ /haɪˈdrɑːtɪk/
adjective
多汗的; 发汗的;  与出汗有关的
Characterized by profuse sweating; diaphoretic.
Example: The patient presented with hidrotic skin and rapid pulse. 病人皮肤出汗，脉搏加快。
Example:  Hidrotic eczema can be uncomfortable and irritating. 多汗性湿疹会让人感到不舒服和 раздражающий.
Synonym: diaphoretic, perspiring
Antonym: anhidrotic
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hidrōtikos*, from *hidrōs* "sweat".  Related to *hydr-* "water".
