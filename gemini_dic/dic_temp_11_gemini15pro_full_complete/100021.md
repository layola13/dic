interpermeating:/ˌɪntərˈpɜːrmieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
相互渗透的，互相穿透的
mutually penetrating or pervading each other; spreading or flowing throughout.
The artist's work is characterized by interpermeating layers of color and texture.  这位艺术家的作品特点是色彩和纹理相互渗透。
The two cultures have become so interpermeating that it's hard to distinguish between them.  两种文化已经变得如此相互渗透，以至于很难区分它们。
近义词:interpenetrating, intermingling, pervading, fusing
反义词:separate, distinct, discrete
词性:adjective

词源:
From inter- + permeate, from Latin permeare "to pass through," from per- "through" + meare "to go, pass."
