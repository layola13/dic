overfastidiously: /ˌoʊvərˌfæˈstɪdiəsli/
adverb
过于挑剔地，过分讲究地
in an excessively fastidious manner; too concerned with accuracy or detail.
He polished his shoes overfastidiously, ensuring there wasn't a single speck of dust. 他过于挑剔地擦拭着他的鞋子，确保没有一丝灰尘。
She arranged the flowers overfastidiously, moving each stem a fraction of an inch until it was perfect in her eyes. 她过分讲究地摆放着花朵，把每一根茎都移动一点点，直到在她眼里完美为止。
近义词: meticulously, scrupulously, excessively
反义词: carelessly, negligently, sloppily
词性: adverb

词源:
From over- + fastidiously.  Fastidious from Latin *fastidiosus, from fastidium (“loathing, disgust”), from fastus (“contempt, arrogance”) + taedium (“weariness, disgust”).
