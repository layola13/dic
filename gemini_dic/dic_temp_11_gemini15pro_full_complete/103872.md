jubilancies: /ˌdʒuːbɪˈleɪnsiz/
noun
欢欣，喜悦
A feeling of great happiness and triumph, especially due to a success.
The jubilancies of the crowd filled the stadium after their team won the championship.  人群的欢呼声在他们队赢得冠军后充满了体育场。
jubilation, rejoicing, exultation
gloom, despair
