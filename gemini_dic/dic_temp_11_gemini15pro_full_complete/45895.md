cuties:/ˈkjuːtiz/| n.|美人儿，可爱的人或物|A term of endearment, often used for children or animals, but can also be used playfully for adults.  It emphasizes the attractiveness and charm of the subject.|例句：Look at those cuties playing in the park! 看看那些在公园里玩耍的小可爱们！

例句：She called her kittens her little cuties. 她称她的小猫为她的小可爱。|近义词：sweethearts, darlings, charmers|反义词:uglies, monsters|Noun
