balsa:/ˈbɔːlsə/ | n. | 轻木，巴尔沙木 | A very lightweight wood used for making models and rafts. |  The model airplane was made of balsa.  这架模型飞机是用轻木做的。| synonyms: corkwood | antonyms: hardwood | noun


balsa:/ˈbɔːlsə/ | adj. | 轻木制的 | Made of balsa wood. | He bought a balsa wood glider. 他买了一个轻木滑翔机。 | synonyms: lightweight | antonyms: heavy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *balsa* "raft," because the wood was often used for making rafts, from Old Provençal *balssa*, ultimately of pre-Roman origin.
