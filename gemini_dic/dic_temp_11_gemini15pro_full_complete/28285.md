capture:/ˈkæptʃər/ (US) /ˈkæptʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |捕获，俘虏；夺取，攻占;吸引（注意力、兴趣等）|The capture of the city marked the end of the war.  这座城市的攻陷标志着战争的结束。|近义词：seize, catch, arrest, grab, secure, win, gain, attract|反义词：release, free, lose, surrender|词性: 名词, 动词

capture:/ˈkæptʃər/ (US) /ˈkæptʃə(r)/ (UK)| v. |录制;拍摄，摄影；捕捉，抓住|The photographer captured the beautiful sunset. 摄影师捕捉到了美丽的日落。|近义词：record, film, photograph, seize, catch|反义词：miss, lose|词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *captura, from Latin captus (past participle of capere “to take”), related to captāre “to strive to seize.”
