grazers:/ˈɡreɪzərz/| n.|食草动物；放牧者|Animals that graze; people who graze livestock. |The grazers moved across the plains, eating the grass. 食草动物在平原上迁徙，吃着草。|  Cows and sheep are common grazers. 牛和羊是常见的食草动物。|近义词：herbivores, ruminants|反义词：predators, carnivores|词性:noun
