hernani:/ɛrˈnɑːni/| n.|埃尔纳尼; 维克多·雨果的戏剧《埃尔纳尼》的主人公; 《埃尔纳尼》|A romantic drama by Victor Hugo, first performed in 1830. The play is set in 1519 Spain and tells the story of the bandit Hernani and his love for Doña Sol, who is also desired by the King of Spain and her elderly uncle. |Example:  I saw a production of Hernani last year.  去年我看了一场《埃尔纳尼》的演出。

Example: Hernani is a complex and passionate character. 埃尔纳尼是一个复杂而充满激情的人物。|近义词: protagonist, hero, outlaw |反义词: villain, antagonist |词性: noun
