malapropism: /ˌmæləˈprɒpɪzəm/ | n. |  用词错误；误用词语 | the unintentional misuse of a word by confusion with one of similar sound, especially one with a ludicrously inappropriate meaning.  |  Example: "He's the very pineapple of politeness!" (instead of pinnacle) 他真是彬彬有礼的典范！（应该用pinnacle，而不是pineapple）

Example:  She said "dance the flamingo" instead of "dance the flamenco". 她把弗拉门戈舞(flamenco)说成了火烈鸟舞(flamingo)。 | 近义词: solecism, catachresis, misnomer | 反义词:  correct usage | 词性: noun
