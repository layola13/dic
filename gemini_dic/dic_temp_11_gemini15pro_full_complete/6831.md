anastigmatic:/ˌænəstɪɡˈmætɪk/
adjective
消像差的；无像散的
Corrected for astigmatism; free of astigmatism. An anastigmatic lens system is one that is designed to minimize or eliminate astigmatism and other optical aberrations, producing a sharper and more accurate image.
This camera features an anastigmatic lens for superior image quality. 这款相机配备了消像差镜头，可提供卓越的图像质量。
The anastigmatic telescope provided clear and distortion-free views of the night sky. 这台消像差望远镜提供了清晰无失真的夜空景象。

synonym:  aplanatic, corrected
antonym: astigmatic, uncorrected
词性: adjective
