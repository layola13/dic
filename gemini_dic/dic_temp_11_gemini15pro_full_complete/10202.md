apostemate: /əˈpɒstɪmət/ | n. | 脓肿 | An abscess; a collection of pus. | The doctor lanced the apostemate to drain the infection. 医生切开脓肿以排出感染。| 近义词: abscess, boil, ulcer | 反义词: healthy tissue | 词性: 名词


词源:
From Late Latin *apostema, from Ancient Greek ἀπόστημα (apóstēma, “abscess”), from ἀφίστημι (aphístēmi, “I put away, separate”), from ἀπό (apó, “away from”) + ἵστημι (hístēmi, “I stand, set”).
