godward:/ˈɡɒdwəd/ (British) /ˈɡɑːdwərd/ (American)| adv. adj.|向上帝，朝向上帝|Toward God; in the direction of God.  It can be used both as an adverb and, less commonly, as an adjective.|He knelt and prayed godward. 他跪下向上帝祈祷。|Synonyms: heavenward, skyward|Antonyms: earthward, downward|Adverb

godward:/ˈɡɒdwəd/ (British) /ˈɡɑːdwərd/ (American)| adj.|向上帝的，朝向上帝的|Directed towards or facing God.|His godward gaze showed his devotion. 他向上帝凝视的目光表明了他的虔诚。|Synonyms: heavenward, skyward|Antonyms: earthward, downward|Adjective
