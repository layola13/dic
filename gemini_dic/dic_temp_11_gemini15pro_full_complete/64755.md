escroll:/ɪˈskroʊl/ (US), /ɛˈskroʊl/ (UK)| n. |电子卷轴; 滚动条 |A continuous, virtual document displayed on a computer screen, typically longer than the screen itself, which can be scrolled through using a mouse, trackpad, or other input device. Also, the bar at the side or bottom of a computer screen that is used to move the text or image up or down.|I used the escroll to read the long article online.|近义词: scroll, scrollbar |反义词: N/A |词性:名词


词源:  A blend of "electronic" and "scroll."  It emerged with the advent of graphical user interfaces and the need to navigate long documents on screen.
