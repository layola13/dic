dubiousness:/ˈduːbiəsnəs/| n.|可疑性；怀疑；不确定性|The dubiousness of his claims made it hard to trust him.  他的说法可疑，让人难以相信他。|例句：The dubiousness of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据的可疑性导致案件被驳回。

例句：There's a certain dubiousness about his motives. 他的动机有些可疑。|近义词：uncertainty, doubtfulness, skepticism, suspicion|反义词：certainty, sureness, conviction|词性:名词

dubious:/ˈduːbiəs/| adj.|可疑的；怀疑的；不确定的|I'm dubious about his chances of winning. 我怀疑他能赢。|例句：She was dubious about the whole idea. 她对整个想法持怀疑态度。

例句：He had a dubious reputation. 他名声不太好。|近义词：uncertain, doubtful, questionable, suspect|反义词：certain, sure, definite|词性:形容词
