inductive:/ɪnˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
归纳的；诱导的；感应的
Characterized by or using induction, a process of reasoning from particular facts or instances to a general conclusion.  Based on observed instances rather than on established principles or laws.  Also relating to electromagnetic or electrostatic induction.
This research uses an inductive approach, starting with observations and building towards a theory. 这项研究采用归纳法，从观察入手，逐步建立理论。
The scientist made an inductive leap, formulating a general principle based on limited data.  这位科学家做了一个归纳性的飞跃，根据有限的数据制定了一个普遍的原则。
synonyms: inferred, derived, reasoned, generalizing
antonyms: deductive, a priori
词性: 形容词
