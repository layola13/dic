gaster:/ˈɡæstər/| n.|胃，腹，腹部|The gaster is the bulbous posterior portion of the metasoma found in hymenopteran insects like ants, bees, and wasps.  它是膜翅目昆虫（如蚂蚁、蜜蜂和黄蜂）后体节的球状后部。|The worker ant carried food back to the nest in its gaster. 工蚁把食物带回巢穴的腹部。|Synonyms: abdomen, belly, stomach|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a body part.
