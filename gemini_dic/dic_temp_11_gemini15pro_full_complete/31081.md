champagne:/ʃæmˈpeɪn/ | n. |香槟酒；香槟色|A sparkling wine associated with the Champagne region of France.  It is often served on celebratory occasions.|例句：They celebrated their anniversary with a bottle of champagne. 他们用一瓶香槟庆祝他们的周年纪念日。

|例句：She wore a champagne-colored dress to the wedding. 她穿了一件香槟色的连衣裙去参加婚礼。|近义词：sparkling wine, bubbly|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of wine or color)|词性:noun


champagne:/ʃæmˈpeɪn/ | adj. |香槟色的|Having the pale yellowish-orange color of champagne.|例句：The walls were painted a delicate champagne color. 墙壁被漆成了淡雅的香槟色。

|例句：Her champagne-colored shoes matched her dress perfectly. 她的香槟色鞋子和她的裙子很搭。|近义词：pale yellowish-orange, beige, buff|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a color)|词性:adjective
