aaa:/ˈeɪ ˈeɪ ˈeɪ/| n.|三重A|Highest possible rating assigned to bonds and other investments by credit rating agencies, indicating a very low risk of default.|Example: The company's bonds have an AAA rating. 该公司的债券评级为AAA。|Synonym: prime, top-notch|Antonym: junk, speculative|Noun

/ˈeɪ ˈeɪ ˈeɪ/|abbr.|美国汽车协会 (American Automobile Association)|A federation of motor clubs throughout North America that provides services to its members, including roadside assistance, travel planning, and insurance.|Example:  I called AAA when my car broke down. 我的车抛锚时，我打电话给美国汽车协会。|Synonym: (no direct synonym for the organization itself)|Antonym: (no direct antonym)|Abbreviation
