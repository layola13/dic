iteming:/ˈaɪtəmaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 逐条列出，详细列举 | The act of listing individual items or details. |  She spent an hour itemizing her expenses for the trip. 她花了一个小时逐条列出旅行的费用。

| 近义词: listing, detailing, enumerating | 反义词: summarizing, generalizing | 词性: noun, derived from the verb "itemize"


itemize:/ˈaɪtəmaɪz/ | vt. | 逐条列出，详细列举 | To list individual items or details. | Please itemize your deductions on your tax return. 请在你的纳税申报单上逐条列出你的扣除额。

| 近义词: list, detail, enumerate | 反义词: summarize, generalize | 词性: verb
