enforceability:/ɪnˌfɔːrsəˈbɪləti/| n.|可执行性，可强制性|The quality of being enforceable, meaning that a contract, law, or regulation can be legally enforced.  This often depends on clarity, legality, and the availability of mechanisms for enforcement.|The enforceability of the contract is in question due to a vague clause.  由于一个模糊的条款，合同的可执行性受到质疑。|Near Synonyms: validity, legality, binding power|Antonyms: invalidity, unenforceability|noun
