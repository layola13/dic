nonmovabilities:/ˌnɒnˌmuːvəˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|不可移动性；不动产|The nonmovabilities of the property made it difficult to sell.  该房产的不可移动性使其难以出售。| This refers to things that cannot be moved, typically land or fixtures attached to it.  这是指无法移动的东西，通常是土地或附着在其上的固定装置。 | The contract detailed the nonmovabilities included in the sale. 合同详细列出了出售中包含的不动产。|近义词：immovables, fixtures|反义词:movables, chattels|noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
movable (adjective): from Old French movable, from Late Latin movabilis "movable," from Latin movere "to move"
-ity (suffix): from Latin -itas, used to form nouns of quality or condition
-ies (suffix): plural ending
