incurable:/ɪnˈkjʊrəbl/ | adj. | 不可治愈的，无法医治的 | Not able to be cured or healed. |  His cancer is incurable. 他的癌症是晚期，无法治愈。 | incurable disease 不治之症 | Synonyms: irremediable, terminal, hopeless, irrecoverable | Antonyms: curable, treatable, remediable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin *incurabilis, from in- ‘not’ + curabilis (from curare ‘to cure’).
