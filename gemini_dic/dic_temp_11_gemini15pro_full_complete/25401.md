bulge:/bʌldʒ/| n. vi. | 膨胀，肿胀；凸起；激增 | A rounded swelling or protuberance that distorts a flat surface.  A sudden, marked increase. To swell outwards.|  The suitcase was bulging with clothes.  这个手提箱因装满衣服而鼓起来。|近义词：swell, protrude, expand, balloon, bulge|反义词：contract, shrink, deflate, recede, flatten|noun, verb
