disquietingly:/dɪsˈkwaɪətɪŋli/
adverb
令人不安地；令人担忧地
in a manner that causes anxiety or uneasiness
The silence was disquietingly long.  这沉默长得令人不安。
近义词: disturbingly, unsettlingly, alarmingly, worryingly
反义词: calmingly, soothingly, reassuringly
词性: adverb

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/
noun
不安；焦虑；担忧
a feeling of anxiety or worry
The news caused him considerable disquiet. 这消息使他非常不安。
近义词: unease, anxiety, worry, concern
反义词: calm, peace, tranquility
词性: noun

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/
verb
使不安；使担忧
to make someone feel anxious or worried
The rumors disquieted her. 这些谣言让她感到不安。
近义词: disturb, unsettle, alarm, worry
反义词: calm, soothe, reassure
词性: verb

Etymology:
From late Middle English *disquiete*, from Old French *desquiete*, based on Latin *disquietus*, past participle of *disquietare* (“to disturb, disquiet”), from *dis-* (“not, un-”) + *quietus* (“quiet, at rest”).
