etherealize:/ɪˈθɪəriəlaɪz/| v. |使空灵；使飘渺；使精神化；使超脱尘世|To make something ethereal; to give something a delicate, airy, or unearthly quality.|She closed her eyes and tried to etherealize her mind, focusing on the gentle sounds of the wind chimes.|近义词：spiritualize, refine, sublimate|反义词：materialize, solidify|词性：动词

Etymology:
From ethereal + -ize.  Ethereal comes from Late Latin aethereus, from Greek aithérios, from aithēr "upper air".
