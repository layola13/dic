littler:/ˈlɪtlər/| adj.|更小的，较小的|Comparative form of "little," meaning smaller in size, amount, or degree.  Used less frequently than "smaller." |The littler kitten hid under the chair.  更小的小猫藏在椅子下面。|近义词：smaller, tinier, minute|反义词:bigger, larger, greater|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *littelre, a comparative of *litel (little), from Old English *lytel.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lītilaz.
