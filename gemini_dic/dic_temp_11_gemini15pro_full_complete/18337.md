belligerent:/bəˈlɪdʒərənt/| adj. n.|好战的；交战的；交战国|Inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness, hostility, or combativeness.  (adj.) / A nation or person engaged in war or conflict. (n.)|例句：His belligerent attitude made it difficult to work with him. 他好斗的态度使人很难与他共事。

例句：The two belligerent countries finally agreed to a ceasefire.  这两个交战国最终同意停火。|近义词：aggressive, hostile, warlike, combative, pugnacious|反义词：peaceful, conciliatory, amicable, cooperative|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Latin *belligerantem (nominative belligerans), present participle of belligerare "to wage war," from bellum "war" + gerere "to wage, to bear, to carry on."
