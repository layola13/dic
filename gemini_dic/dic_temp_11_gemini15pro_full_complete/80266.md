goggling:/ˈɡɒɡlɪŋ/ /ˈɡɑːɡlɪŋ/| v. | 瞪大眼睛看；眼珠转动 | staring with wide-open eyes; rolling one's eyes |  He was goggling at her in amazement.  他惊奇地瞪大眼睛看着她。| synonyms: gawking, staring, gaping | antonyms: squinting, glancing | verb

goggling:/ˈɡɒɡlɪŋ/ /ˈɡɑːɡlɪŋ/| n. | 瞪眼；眼珠转动 | the act of staring with wide-open eyes; rolling one's eyes |  His goggling was quite comical. 他的瞪眼样子很滑稽。 | synonyms: gawking, staring, gaping | antonyms: squinting, glancing | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From the verb "goggle," which likely originated in the 18th century as a frequentative form of "gog" (meaning "to stare"). "Gog" itself might be related to Middle English "gogen," of uncertain origin.
