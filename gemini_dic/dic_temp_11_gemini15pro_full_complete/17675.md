becomingness:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋnɪs/| n.|合适；得体；雅致|The quality of being attractive, suitable, or appropriate, especially in appearance or behavior.  It refers to that which enhances or sets off one's appearance or character to advantage.|Her dress had a simple becomingness that made her look elegant. 她的裙子有一种简洁的雅致，让她看起来很优雅。|近义词：suitability, appropriateness, grace, elegance, decorum|反义词：unsuitability, inappropriateness, awkwardness, ungainliness|noun
