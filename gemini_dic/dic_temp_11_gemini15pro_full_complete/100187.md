interrelations:/ˌɪntərɪˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |相互关系，相互联系|The complex interrelations between the economy and the environment are difficult to fully understand. 经济与环境之间复杂的相互关系很难完全理解。|例句：The interrelations between different departments are crucial for the smooth running of the company.  不同部门之间的相互联系对于公司的顺利运营至关重要。

例句：The book explores the interrelations of language, culture, and identity. 这本书探讨了语言、文化和身份之间的相互关系。|近义词：connections, relationships, interactions, links|反义词：independence, isolation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally") + relation (from Old French relacion or Latin relatio, meaning "a bringing back, restoring; report, narrative," from referre "to carry back") + -s (plural suffix).
