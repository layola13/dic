outhastened:/ˌaʊtˈheɪsənd/
verb
(迅速)超过，赶上
To hasten out or away; to go out hastily.  This word emphasizes speed and a quick departure.
He outhastened his pursuers and escaped into the woods. 他超过了追赶者，逃进了树林。
The frightened deer outhastened the hunter. 受惊的鹿跑得比猎人快。
近义词: outpaced, outstripped, outran
反义词: lagged behind, trailed

词性: verb
