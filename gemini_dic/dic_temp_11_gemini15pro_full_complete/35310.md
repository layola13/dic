what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. |
例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What time is it? 现在几点了？

近义词：which, how | 反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | pronoun
what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) | determiner | 什么，哪一个，哪一些 |  Used to ask about the identity or nature of a person or thing. |
例句：What book are you reading? 你在读什么书？

例句：What color is your car? 你的车是什么颜色？

近义词：which | 反义词: (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | determiner
what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) | adverb | 多么，何等 | Used to emphasize the degree or extent of something. |
例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！

例句：What a surprise! 多么惊喜！
