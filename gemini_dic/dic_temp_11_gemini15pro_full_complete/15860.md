balletically:/bæˈlɛtɪkli/
adverb|芭蕾舞般地，优雅地|In a manner characteristic of ballet; gracefully and fluidly.|She moved balletically across the stage. 她像芭蕾舞演员一样优雅地穿过舞台。|例句：He bowed balletically. 他优雅地鞠了一躬。|近义词：gracefully, elegantly, fluidly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly|词性:副词
词源: From ballet + -ic + -ally.  Ultimately from Italian *balletto* (little dance).
