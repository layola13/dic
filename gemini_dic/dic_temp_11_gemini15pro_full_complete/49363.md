deliquesce:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛs/| vi.|潮解，溶解|To absorb moisture from the air and dissolve.|The salt deliquesced in the humid air.  盐在潮湿的空气中潮解了。|Synonyms: dissolve, melt, liquefy|Antonyms: solidify, harden, dry|Verb

deliquescence:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛsəns/| n.|潮解，溶解|The process of absorbing moisture from the air and dissolving.|The deliquescence of the salt caused a mess. 盐的潮解造成了一团糟。|Synonyms: dissolving, melting, liquefaction|Antonyms: solidification, hardening, drying|Noun
