livening:/ˈlaɪvənɪŋ/
adjective
使活跃；使生动；使有生气
making something more interesting or exciting
The band played a lively tune, livening up the party.  乐队演奏了一首欢快的曲子，使派对气氛活跃起来。

近义词:animating, invigorating, exhilarating, stimulating
反义词:dulling, boring, depressing, subduing
词性:adjective


livening:/ˈlaɪvənɪŋ/
noun
活跃；生动
the act of making something more lively or interesting
The livening of the party atmosphere was due to the band's music. 派对气氛的活跃归功于乐队的音乐。

近义词:animation, invigoration, stimulation
反义词:dullness, boredom, depression
词性:noun


词源: From the verb "liven," which is derived from "life" + "-en" (to make).  来自动词 "liven"，它源于 "life" + "-en"（使...）。
