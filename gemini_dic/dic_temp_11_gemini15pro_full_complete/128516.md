newton:/ˈnjuːtn/ | n. |牛顿（力的单位；姓氏）|The SI unit of force. One newton is equal to the force that would give a mass of one kilogram an acceleration of one metre per second per second.  It is also a surname.|例句：The force of gravity on a 1 kg object is about 9.8 newtons.  一个1公斤的物体受到的重力约为9.8牛顿。|例句: Isaac Newton was a brilliant physicist.  艾萨克·牛顿是一位杰出的物理学家。|近义词：(For the unit)  N (abbreviation), unit of force|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of force)|词性:noun
