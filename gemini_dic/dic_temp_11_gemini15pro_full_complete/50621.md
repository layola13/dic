dereliction:/ˌdɛrəˈlɪkʃən/
n.
玩忽职守，怠慢职责；遗弃，抛弃
The dereliction of duty by the night watchman allowed the thieves to enter.  夜间值班员玩忽职守，让小偷进来了。
The building stood in a state of dereliction. 这座建筑处于荒废状态。
Synonyms: negligence, neglect, abandonment, delinquency
Antonyms: diligence, care, attention, responsibility
词性: 名词

derelictions:/ˌdɛrəˈlɪkʃənz/

Synonyms: negligences, neglects, abandonments, delinquencies
Antonyms: diligences, cares, attentions, responsibilities
词性: 名词
