microtomy: /maɪˈkrɒtəmi/ (UK) /maɪˈkrɑːtəmi/ (US)| n.|显微切片术，显微切片法|The process or technique of cutting extremely thin sections of material for examination under a microscope.|例句：Microtomy is an essential technique in histology. 显微切片术是组织学中必不可少的技术。

例句：The lab assistant was skilled in microtomy and prepared excellent slides.  实验室助理精通显微切片术，并制作了出色的切片。|近义词：sectioning|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
