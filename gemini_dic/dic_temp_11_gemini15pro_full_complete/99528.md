intergenerationally:/ˌɪntərˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəli/
adv.
代际地，跨代地
Happening or existing between different generations.
例句：Intergenerationally transmitted trauma can have lasting effects.  代际传递的创伤会产生持久的影响。

例句：The family heirloom was passed down intergenerationally.  这件传家宝是代代相传的。
近义词：across generations, between generations
反义词：intragenerationally (within a generation)
词性: 副词
