inchangeability:/ɪnˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/| n.|不变性，不可改变性|The inchangeability of the laws of physics is a fundamental principle. 物理定律的不变性是一个基本原则。|The document emphasized the inchangeability of the contract terms. 该文件强调了合同条款的不可更改性。|近义词：immutability, permanence, constancy|反义词：mutability, variability, changeability|词性:noun

Etymology:
From in- (not) + changeable + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Late Latin *cambiabilis (exchangeable), from cambiare (to exchange).
