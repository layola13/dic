ambulation: /ˌæmbjəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 步行，行走；移动 | The act of walking or moving about. |  After surgery, the patient's ambulation was slow and painful. 手术后，病人的行走缓慢而痛苦。|  The physical therapist helped the patient with ambulation exercises. 物理治疗师帮助病人进行行走练习。|  近义词：walking, locomotion, mobility | 反义词：immobility, paralysis | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *ambulationem, accusative of *ambulatio (“walking”), from Latin ambulō (“I walk”).
