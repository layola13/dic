occultly:/əˈkʌltli/ | adv. | 神秘地；秘密地；玄奥地 | In a manner that is hidden, secret, or mysterious; involving or relating to the occult. |  He smiled occultly, as if sharing a secret with himself. 他神秘地笑了笑，好像在和自己分享一个秘密。 | secretly, covertly, mysteriously, esoterically | openly, publicly, plainly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *occultē, from Latin occultus (“hidden, secret”), past participle of occulere (“to cover, conceal”), from ob (“over, against, before”) + calere (“to hide”).
