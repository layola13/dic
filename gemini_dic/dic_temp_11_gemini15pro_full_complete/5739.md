always:/ˈɔːlweɪz/ (US), /ˈɔːlweɪz/ (UK)| adv.|总是，一直，永远|Always refers to something that happens all the time or every time. It indicates a continuous or repeated action or state.|She always arrives early for work. 她总是上班早到。|Synonyms: constantly, invariably, perpetually|Antonyms: never, rarely, seldom|adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I always eat breakfast. 我总是吃早餐。
He always walks to school. 他总是步行上学。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is always complaining. 她总是抱怨。
He is always helping others. 他总是帮助别人。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have always wanted to travel the world. 我一直想环游世界。
She has always been kind to me. 她一直对我很好。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He has been always asking questions. 他一直都在问问题。(不太自然，通常用现在进行时)
They have been always working hard. 他们一直都在努力工作。(不太自然，通常用现在进行时)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He always walked to school when he was a child. 他小时候总是步行上学。
She always arrived early for meetings. 她总是早到开会。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was always getting into trouble. 他总是惹麻烦。
She was always helping her mother. 她总是帮助她的母亲。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had always wanted to be a doctor. 他一直想成为一名医生。
She had always been a good student. 她一直是个好学生。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been always dreaming of becoming a pilot.  他一直梦想着成为一名飞行员。(不太自然，通常用过去完成时)
She had been always working towards her goal. 她一直朝着她的目标努力。(不太自然，通常用过去完成时)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will always remember this day. 我将永远记住这一天。
He will always be my best friend. 他将永远是我最好的朋友。

注意事项:
'always' 在否定句中位置通常在助动词和主要动词之间。例如：He doesn't always go to the gym. 他并非总是去健身房。

'always' 用于进行时态可以表示说话者对某人反复做某事的厌烦或不满。 例如：She's always complaining! 她总是抱怨！
