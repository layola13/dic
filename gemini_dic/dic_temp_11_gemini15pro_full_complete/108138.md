latinization:/ˌlætɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|拉丁化，拉丁字母化|The process of converting a word or text from another writing system into the Latin alphabet.  The latinization of Chinese names can sometimes lead to confusion.|例句：The latinization of place names often obscures their original meaning.  地名拉丁化常常掩盖其原始含义。

例句：The latinization of Japanese uses the Hepburn system.  日语的拉丁化使用赫本系统。|近义词：romanization, transliteration|反义词:|noun
