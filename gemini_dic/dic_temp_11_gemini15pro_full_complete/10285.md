apparition:/ˌæpəˈrɪʃn/ /ˌæpəˈrɪʃən/| n. | 出现，显现；幽灵，幻影 |The sudden apparition of my old friend startled me. 我的老朋友突然出现吓了我一跳。|Example:  A ghostly apparition floated through the halls of the old castle.  一个幽灵般的幻影飘过古老城堡的大厅。

Example: The apparition of the ship on the horizon signaled the end of our long wait.  船只出现在地平线上，标志着我们漫长等待的结束。|Synonyms: ghost, phantom, specter, vision, manifestation|Antonyms: disappearance, vanishing, departure|noun
