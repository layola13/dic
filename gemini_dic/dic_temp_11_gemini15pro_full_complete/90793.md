humorous:/ˈhjuːmərəs/ (US) /ˈhjuːmərəs/ (UK)| adj.|幽默的，滑稽的|Causing amusement; funny.|例句：He told a humorous story. 他讲了一个幽默的故事。|近义词：funny, comical, witty, amusing|反义词：serious, somber, grave|词性:形容词
humourous: /ˈhjuːmərəs/|adjective|幽默的，滑稽的|Funny and entertaining; causing laughter or amusement.|例句：She has a humorous way of looking at things. 她看待事物的方式很幽默。|近义词：amusing, comical, droll, witty|反义词：serious, grave, solemn, humorless|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *humoreux*, from Old French *umoros* "moist, damp," from Late Latin *umorosus*, from Latin *umor* "liquid, moisture" (see humor). The meaning shifted from "moist" to "capricious" (like the weather) to "whimsical, jocular" in the 16th century.
