chief:/tʃiːf/ | adj. n.|主要的，最重要的；首领，领袖|A person or thing that is most important or in control.|The chief executive officer resigned. 首席执行官辞职了.|近义词：principal, main, leader, head|反义词：minor, subordinate, follower|词性:形容词，名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *chef, borrowed from Old French *chief* (“head, leader, end, extremity”), from Latin *caput* (“head”).
