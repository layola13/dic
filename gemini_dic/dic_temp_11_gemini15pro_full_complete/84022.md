half-dying:/ˌhæf ˈdaɪɪŋ/
adjective
奄奄一息的；快要死的
Being in a very weakened or sickly state, close to death.
The half-dying plant was revived with some water and sunlight.  这株奄奄一息的植物在浇水和阳光的照射下复活了。
The hiker, lost and half-dying of thirst, was finally rescued.  这位迷路的徒步旅行者快要渴死了，最终获救了。
Near death; expiring; moribund.
