activate:/ˈæktɪveɪt/ | v. | 激活，启动，使活动 | To make something active or operative; to start or trigger a process or function. | The software is activated by entering a product key.  该软件通过输入产品密钥激活。

The alarm system is activated by any movement.  任何移动都会触发警报系统。 | synonyms: trigger, start, initiate, enable, switch on, turn on | antonyms: deactivate, disable, shut down | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *activatus*, past participle of *activare*, from Latin *activus* ("active").
