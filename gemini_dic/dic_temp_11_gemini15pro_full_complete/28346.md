carapaced:/ˈkærəpeɪst/| adj.|有壳的，甲壳状的|Having a carapace; covered with a carapace.  A carapace is a hard, protective outer shell.|例句：The carapaced turtle retreated into its shell. 有壳的海龟退回到它的壳里。|例句：The carapaced beetle scuttled across the forest floor.  甲壳状的甲虫匆匆穿过森林地面。|近义词：shelled, armored, protected|反义词:soft, unprotected|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *carapace*, from Spanish *carapacho* "shell, armor," probably ultimately from Arabic *qirāfa*, plural of *qirf*, meaning "bark, rind, shell, husk."
