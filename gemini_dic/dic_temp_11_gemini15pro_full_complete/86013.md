heady:/ˈhedi/| adj.|令人兴奋的；醉人的；轻率的；任性的|Potent; intoxicating; exhilarating; impetuous.  Characterized by or causing a feeling of dizziness or excitement.|The heady scent of jasmine filled the air. 茉莉花的醉人香味弥漫在空气中。|Synonyms: intoxicating, exhilarating, exciting, thrilling, stimulating, potent, strong, powerful |Antonyms: calming, soothing, relaxing, mild, gentle|adjective

heady:/ˈhedi/| adj.|鲁莽的；轻率的|Rash; impetuous.|He made a heady decision to quit his job without another lined up. 他轻率地决定在没有找到另一份工作的情况下辞职。|Synonyms: reckless, rash, impulsive, impetuous, hasty, imprudent |Antonyms: cautious, careful, prudent, considered, deliberate|adjective
