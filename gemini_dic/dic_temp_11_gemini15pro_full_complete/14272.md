autogenous:/ɔːˈtɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
自生的，自发的
(of a disease) originating within the body; (of a material) produced from within, especially (of a graft) derived from the patient's own body.
例句：The doctor explained that the infection was autogenous, originating within the patient's own system. 医生解释说，感染是自生的，起源于病人自身的系统。

例句：Autogenous bone grafts are often preferred in reconstructive surgery.  自体骨移植在重建外科中通常是首选。
近义词：self-generated, self-produced, endogenous, innate
反义词：exogenous, acquired
词性：adjective
