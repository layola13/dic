opted:/ˈɒptɪd/ (UK), /ˈɑːptɪd/ (US)
verb
选择，挑选
make a choice from a range of possibilities.
She opted for a career in medicine. 她选择了医学事业。
He opted to stay home instead of going to the party. 他选择待在家里而不是去参加派对。
choose, select, pick, decide on
decline, reject, refuse
