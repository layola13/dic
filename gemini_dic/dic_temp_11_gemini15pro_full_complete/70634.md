filterer:/ˈfɪltərər/| n.|过滤者，筛选者|A person or thing that filters something.|The filterer removed all the impurities from the water. 过滤者将水中的所有杂质都去除了。|近义词：sifter, strainer, purifier|反义词:contaminator, polluter|词性:noun

Etymology:  From the verb "filter" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Filter" comes from the Old French "filtre" from Medieval Latin "filtrum," likely from Germanic origin.
