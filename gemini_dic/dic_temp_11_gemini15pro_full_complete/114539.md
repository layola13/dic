malapropism: /ˌmæləˈprɒpɪzəm/ | n. |  用词错误；误用词语 | the unintentional misuse of a word by confusion with one that sounds similar |  "He's the very pineapple of politeness!" is a malapropism, as the speaker intended to say "pinnacle." 他说“他是礼貌的菠萝！”是一种用词错误，因为他想说的是“顶峰（pinnacle）”。 |  misusage, solecism, catachresis |  correct usage | noun
