funebrious:/fjuːˈnɪbriəs/ | adj. | 悲伤的；忧郁的；丧葬的 |  Gloomy, mournful, or suggestive of funerals.  Characteristic of or suitable for a funeral.  It comes from the Latin "funebris," meaning "of or belonging to a funeral." |  The old mansion had a funebrious atmosphere. 这座老宅有一种悲伤的氛围。 |  Her funebrious expression made it clear she had received bad news. 她悲伤的表情表明她收到了坏消息。 | Synonyms: mournful, somber, doleful, gloomy, melancholy | Antonyms: cheerful, joyful, happy, lighthearted | adjective
