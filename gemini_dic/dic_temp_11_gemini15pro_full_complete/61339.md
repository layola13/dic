embeddable:/ɪmˈbɛdəb(ə)l/| adj.|可嵌入的，可植入的|Something that is embeddable can be inserted or integrated into another entity, such as a piece of software, a webpage, or a physical object.|The video player is embeddable on any website.  这个视频播放器可以嵌入到任何网站。|Synonyms: insertable, integrable, incorporable|Antonyms: unembeddable, non-integrable|Adjective

embeddable:/ɪmˈbɛdəb(ə)l/| n.|可嵌入的东西|Something that can be embedded.|This new embeddable allows users to interact directly with the content. 这个新的可嵌入对象允许用户直接与内容交互。|Synonyms: plugin, widget, component|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
