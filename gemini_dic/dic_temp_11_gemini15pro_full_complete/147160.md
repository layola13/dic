overloved: /ˌoʊvərˈlʌvd/
adjective
被过度喜爱的；被溺爱的
Loved to excess; given too much love or attention.
The overloved child became spoiled and demanding.  这个被过度宠爱的孩子变得娇惯和苛求。
My overloved dog refuses to eat anything but steak. 我那被过度宠爱的狗除了牛排什么都不吃。
synonyms: spoiled, pampered, coddled, overindulged
antonyms: neglected, ignored, overlooked
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "loved" (past participle of "love").  由表示“过度”的前缀"over-"和动词"love"的过去分词"loved"构成。
