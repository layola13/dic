nondilatabilities:/ˌnɒndaɪˌleɪtəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n.|不可扩张性；不可膨胀性|The nondilatabilities of certain materials make them unsuitable for specific applications.  某些材料的不可扩张性使得它们不适用于特定用途。| The artery showed marked nondilatability. 这条动脉显示出明显的不可扩张性。|近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stiffness|反义词：dilatability, expansibility, flexibility|noun
