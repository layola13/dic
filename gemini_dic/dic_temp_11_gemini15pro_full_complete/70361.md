fiercer:/ˈfɪərsər/ (US), /ˈfɪəsə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更凶猛的，更激烈的，更强烈的|Comparative form of fierce: more fierce.  Used to describe something that is more aggressive, intense, or powerful than something else.|The competition is fiercer this year. 今年的竞争更加激烈。|The lioness gave a fiercer roar. 母狮发出更凶猛的咆哮。|近义词：more aggressive, more intense, stronger, more violent|反义词：gentler, milder, calmer, weaker|词性:形容词
