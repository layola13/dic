cognoscente:/ˌkɒnjəˈʃɛnteɪ/ /ˌkɑːnjəˈʃɛnteɪ/ | n. |鉴赏家；行家| A person who has expert knowledge in a subject, especially in the fine arts or in a matter of taste.| He is a cognoscente of fine wines. 他是一位葡萄酒鉴赏家。 | 近义词: connoisseur, expert, specialist | 反义词: ignoramus, novice | 词性: noun

cognoscenti:/ˌkɒnjəˈʃɛntiː/ /ˌkɑːnjəˈʃɛntiː/ | n. |鉴赏家；行家（复数）| Plural form of cognoscente, referring to a group of people who have expert knowledge in a particular subject.| The cognoscenti gathered at the gallery opening.  鉴赏家们聚集在画廊开幕式上。 | 近义词: connoisseurs, experts, specialists | 反义词: ignoramuses, novices | 词性: noun
