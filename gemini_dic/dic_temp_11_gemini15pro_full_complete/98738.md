insurrectionally:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənəli/
adverb
暴动地；叛乱地
in a way that relates to or involves insurrection.
例句：The group planned to act insurrectionally and overthrow the government.  这群人计划发动叛乱推翻政府。

例句：They behaved insurrectionally, inciting others to riot. 他们煽动他人暴乱，表现得像要造反一样。

近义词：rebelliously, seditiously, mutinously
反义词：obediently, submissively, loyally
词性：adverb

词源：From insurrection + -ally.  Insurrection comes from Late Latin insurrectio, from insurgere "to rise up."
