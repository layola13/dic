administer:/ədˈmɪnɪstər/ | v. |管理，执行，给予|To manage, control, or execute; to give or apply (especially medicine).|The nurse administered the medication to the patient.  护士给病人服用了药物。|Synonyms: manage, direct, govern, execute, dispense|Antonyms: neglect, ignore|verb

administer:/ədˈmɪnɪstər/ | v. |施行，实施|To put a law, system, or plan into action.|The government will administer new regulations next year. 政府明年将实施新的法规。|Synonyms: implement, enforce, apply, execute|Antonyms: disregard, disobey|verb
