encysted:/ɪnˈsɪstɪd/ | adj. | 包囊的，被膜包围的 | Enclosed in a cyst or capsule; surrounded by a protective wall. | The doctor discovered an encysted tumor in the patient's liver. 医生在病人的肝脏里发现了一个包囊性肿瘤。 | Example 2: The encysted parasite was difficult to remove.  包囊的寄生虫很难清除。 | 近义词: encapsulated, enclosed, embedded | 反义词: exposed, free | 词性: adjective

encysted:/ɛnˈsɪstɪd/ | v. |  (使)形成包囊, (使)被膜包围 | To enclose in a cyst or capsule.  | The infection had encysted in his lungs. 感染在他的肺部形成了包囊。 | Example 2:  The process encysted the foreign body, preventing it from spreading.  这个过程使异物形成了包囊，防止其扩散。 | 近义词: encapsulate, enclose, embed | 反义词: expose, free | 词性: verb
