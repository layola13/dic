jackdaw:/ˈdʒækˌdɔː/ (US), /ˈdʒækˌdɔː/ (UK)| n.|寒鸦|A crow-like bird with dark plumage and a distinctive grey nape.  Known for its intelligence and sometimes mischievous behavior.|例句：I saw a jackdaw perched on the chimney. 我看到一只寒鸦栖息在烟囱上。

例句：The jackdaw mimicked the sound of a car alarm. 寒鸦模仿了汽车警报的声音。|近义词：crow, raven, rook|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
