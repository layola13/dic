julaceous:/ˌdʒuːˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
千足虫似的；多足的；圆柱形的
Resembling a millipede or woodlouse; having many legs; cylindrical or wormlike.
The julaceous creature wriggled through the damp soil. 这只千足虫似的生物在潮湿的土壤中蠕动。
The caterpillar had a smooth, julaceous body. 这只毛毛虫有着光滑的、多足动物似的身体。
synonyms: millipede-like, myriapodous, wormlike, cylindrical
antonyms: flat, two-legged, four-legged
词性: adjective
