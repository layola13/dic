headcheese:/ˈhedˌtʃiːz/| n.|猪头冻，肉冻|A cold cut made from the flesh of a pig's head, often set in aspic.|例句：My grandmother used to make delicious headcheese. 我祖母过去常常做美味的猪头冻。

例句：Headcheese is a traditional dish in many European countries. 猪头冻是许多欧洲国家的传统菜肴。|近义词：brawn, souse|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh meat" as a contrasting concept)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "head" and "cheese," though it contains no cheese. The "cheese" part likely refers to the shape and texture after setting.  The term dates back to the Middle Ages.
