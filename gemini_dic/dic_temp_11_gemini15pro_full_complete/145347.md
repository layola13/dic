overbulky: /ˌoʊvərˈbʌlki/
adjective
过于笨重的；体积过大的
Excessively large and awkward to handle or store.
The package was overbulky and difficult to carry.  这个包裹体积过大，难以搬运。
This furniture is overbulky for our small apartment.  这家具对我们的小公寓来说太笨重了。
近义词: cumbersome, unwieldy, bulky, massive
反义词: compact, small, manageable
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "bulky" (adjective meaning "large and awkward").  "Bulky" comes from "bulk," which ultimately derives from Old Norse "bulki," meaning "cargo, heap."
