coiled:/kɔɪld/| adj.|卷曲的，盘绕的|Wound or arranged in a series of circles or rings.|The snake lay coiled in the grass. 蛇盘绕在草丛中。|近义词：curled, twisted, spiralled, wound|反义词:straight, uncoiled|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb 'coil', which comes from Old French 'coillir' (to gather, collect), ultimately from Latin 'colligere' (to gather together).
