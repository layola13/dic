outtease: /ˌaʊtˈtiːz/
verb
超越挑逗；比…更会挑逗
to surpass someone in teasing or flirtatious behavior; to tease or flirt more skillfully than someone else.
She managed to outtease him with her witty remarks and playful glances. 她用机智的言辞和俏皮的眼神成功地超越了他对她的挑逗。
He tried to outtease her, but she was too quick for him. 他试图比她更会挑逗，但她反应太快了。
近义词: outwit, outmaneuver, surpass, excel
反义词: be outdone, lose to

词性: v.

词源:  A combination of "out-" (meaning to surpass or exceed) and "tease" (meaning to playfully provoke or annoy).  The "out-" prefix indicates exceeding or surpassing someone in the action of the verb that follows.
