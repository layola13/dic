antispeculatively:/ˌæntiˈspɛkjʊlətɪvli/
|adv.| 反投机地，非投机性地 |  In a way that discourages or avoids speculation.  | The government acted antispeculatively to curb inflation. 政府采取反投机措施来抑制通货膨胀。|Synonym: cautiously, prudently | Antonym: speculatively, recklessly | adverb

Etymology->
From "anti-" (against) + "speculative" (based on speculation) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Speculative" comes from Latin "speculatus," past participle of "speculari" (to look at, observe, contemplate), from "specula" (watchtower).
