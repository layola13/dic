decorator:/ˈdekəreɪtər/| n.|装饰者，装潢师，室内设计师|A person who decorates or adorns something, especially interiors.|例句：The decorator chose a lovely shade of blue for the walls.  室内设计师为墙壁选择了一种可爱的蓝色。|近义词：designer, adornist, embellisher|反义词:None|Noun

decorator:/ˈdekəreɪtər/| n.| (computing)装饰器|A design pattern that allows behavior to be added to an individual object, dynamically, without affecting the behavior of other objects from the same class. It is a special kind of design pattern.|例句：Python uses decorators extensively for adding functionality to functions. Python 广泛使用装饰器为函数添加功能。|近义词：wrapper, modifier|反义词:None|Noun
