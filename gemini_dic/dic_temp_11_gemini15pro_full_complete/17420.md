beams:/biːmz/| n. |光束，横梁|A beam of sunlight streamed through the window. 一束阳光透过窗户照射进来。|近义词：rays, shafts, girders|反义词:darkness, shadow|noun
beams:/biːmz/| v. |照射，播送，眉开眼笑|Her face beamed with happiness. 她的脸上洋溢着幸福的笑容。|近义词：shine, radiate, grin|反义词:frown, scowl|verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *beem, from Old English *bēam (“tree, beam, post, ray of light”), from Proto-Germanic *bauman (“tree, beam”), from Proto-Indo-European *bhaumon (“wood, tree, beam”). Cognate with West Frisian beam (“tree”), Dutch boom (“tree”), German Baum (“tree”), Danish bom (“boom, bar, tree”), Swedish bom (“barrier, bar”), Icelandic baumur (“beam, pole”). The verb sense "to smile radiantly" is from the late 19th century.
