bornite:/ˈbɔːrnaɪt/| n. |斑铜矿|A sulfide mineral of copper and iron, Cu₅FeS₄, that is an important ore of copper. It is characterized by a purplish-blue tarnish when freshly fractured, hence the common name "peacock ore."|The geologist identified the sample as bornite due to its iridescent tarnish.  地质学家根据样品彩虹色的锖色鉴定出它是斑铜矿。

Bornite is a valuable ore of copper. 斑铜矿是一种有价值的铜矿石。|近义词:peacock ore, erubescite|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) |词性:noun
