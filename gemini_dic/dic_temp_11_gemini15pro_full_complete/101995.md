isochronous:/ˌaɪsəˈkrɒnəs/ /ˌaɪsəˈkrɑːnəs/| adj.|等时的，同步的|Occurring at the same time or intervals.  Having equal duration.  Characterized by or pertaining to isochronism.|例句：The isochronous beating of the heart. 心脏等时的跳动。

例句：The dancers' movements were isochronous with the music. 舞蹈演员的动作与音乐同步。|近义词：synchronous, simultaneous, concurrent, coincident|反义词：asynchronous, non-synchronous|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἰσόχρονος (isókhronos, “of equal time, equal in duration”), from ἴσος (ísos, “equal”) + χρόνος (khrónos, “time”).
