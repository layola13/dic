mortgagor:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɔːr/ (US), /ˈmɔːɡɪdʒɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |抵押人，按揭人|A person who mortgages property.  The mortgagor borrows money and gives the mortgage to the lender (mortgagee) as security.|The mortgagor defaulted on their loan payments. 抵押人拖欠了贷款还款。|Synonym: borrower, debtor|Antonym: mortgagee, lender|noun
