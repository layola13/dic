non-presbyterian:/nɒnˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriən/
adjective
非长老会的
Not belonging to or associated with the Presbyterian Church.
Many non-Presbyterian Christians also celebrate Christmas. 许多非长老会基督徒也庆祝圣诞节。
Synonyms: non-conformist, dissenter
Antonyms: Presbyterian
