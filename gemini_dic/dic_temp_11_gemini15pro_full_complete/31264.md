chaparral: /ˌʃæpəˈræl/ (US), /ˌtʃæpəˈræl/ (UK)| n. |丛林, 灌木丛|A dense growth of shrubs and thorny bushes, especially in southern California and other areas with a Mediterranean climate.| The chaparral is home to many unique species of plants and animals.  丛林是许多独特动植物的家园。|The fire swept quickly through the dry chaparral.  大火迅速席卷了干燥的丛林。|Synonym: thicket, scrubland, brush|Antonym: forest, grassland|Noun
