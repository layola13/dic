labipalp:/ˈleɪbiˌpælp/| n. | 唇须，下唇须 |A small sensory appendage near the mouth of an arthropod, often used for tasting or manipulating food.  It's part of the mouthparts, specifically belonging to the labium (lower lip).| The butterfly used its labipalps to taste the nectar of the flower. 蝴蝶用它的唇须品尝花蜜。| synonym:palp | antonym: N/A |noun
