bronzed:/brɑːnzd/ | adj. | 古铜色的；晒黑的 | Having a tan; being the color of bronze. |  例句：Her skin was bronzed from a week at the beach. 她的皮肤在海滩晒了一周后变成了古铜色。

例句：The bronzed statue gleamed in the sunlight.  古铜色的雕像在阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词：tanned, sun-kissed, copper-colored | 反义词：pale, fair | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From bronze + -ed, meaning "having the appearance of bronze."  Ultimately from Persian *birinj/*biranj "brass, bronze."
