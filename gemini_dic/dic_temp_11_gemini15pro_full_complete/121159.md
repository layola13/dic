misdevoted:/ˌmɪsdɪˈvoʊtɪd/
adjective
误用的；滥用的；奉献给了错误的对象或目标的
wrongly or unwisely devoted or dedicated.
He misdevoted his talents to trivial pursuits. 他把自己的才能误用在琐碎的事情上。
Synonyms: misused, misapplied, misspent, squandered
Antonyms: well-spent, wisely used, properly applied
词性: 形容词
