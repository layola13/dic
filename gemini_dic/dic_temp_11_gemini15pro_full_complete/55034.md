disquietednesses:/dɪsˈkwaɪɪtɪdnəsɪz/
noun
不安，焦虑，担忧
The state of being disquieted; uneasiness or anxiety.
His disquietednesses about the future were evident in his nervous pacing. 他对未来的焦虑在他紧张的踱步中显而易见。
Near Synonyms: anxieties, worries, concerns, unease, apprehensions
Antonyms: calmness, tranquility, serenity, peace, composure
词性: 名词
