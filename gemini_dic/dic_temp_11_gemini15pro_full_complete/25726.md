n.|触球|A lightly hit baseball, often made strategically to advance a base runner. |The batter surprised everyone with a bunt down the third base line.|近义词:sacrifice|反义词:home run
v.|触击|To make a bunt. |He bunted the ball to move the runner to second base.|近义词:sacrifice hit|反义词:slug, blast
词性:名词，动词

bunt:/bʌnt/
n.|船头|The curved, nearly vertical part of a ship's bow. |The waves crashed against the bunt of the ship.|近义词:bow, prow|反义词:stern
