keynoting:/ˈkiːˌnoʊtɪŋ/| n. |主旨演讲；基调发言；|The act of giving a keynote speech or address, setting the central theme or tone of a conference, meeting, or event.|She's keynoting at a major tech conference next month. 她下个月将在一个重要的科技会议上做主题演讲。|近义词：presenting, addressing, speaking|反义词: listening, attending|noun

keynoting:/ˈkiːˌnoʊtɪŋ/| v. |作主旨发言；定基调|Acting as a keynote speaker; setting the central theme or tone.|He keynoted the conference with a speech on innovation. 他以一篇关于创新的演讲为会议定下了基调。|近义词：leading, headlining, featuring|反义词: following, observing|verb
