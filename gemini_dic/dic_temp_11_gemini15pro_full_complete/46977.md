darwin:/ˈdɑːrwɪn/ | n. | 达尔文 |  Charles Robert Darwin (1809-1882), English naturalist who formulated the theory of evolution by natural selection. Also used to refer to his theories. | Charles Darwin is the author of On the Origin of Species. 查尔斯·达尔文是《物种起源》的作者。 |  近义词: evolutionist, biologist | 反义词: creationist | Noun

darwin:/ˈdɑːrwɪn/ | adj. | 达尔文的；达尔文主义的 | Relating to Charles Darwin or his theories of evolution. | Darwinian evolution is a cornerstone of modern biology. 达尔文进化论是现代生物学的基石。 | 近义词: evolutionary | 反义词: creationist | Adjective
