outsprinted:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪntɪd/
verb
中文翻译：以更快速度冲刺超越；在冲刺中胜过
英文解析：past participle of outsprint, meaning to run faster than someone in a sprint.
例句：He was outsprinted in the final meters of the race. 他在比赛的最后几米被超越了。

例句：The young athlete outsprinted the veteran champion.  年轻的运动员在冲刺中胜过了老将冠军。


近义词：outrun, outpace, beat
反义词：lose to, be overtaken by
词性：v.
