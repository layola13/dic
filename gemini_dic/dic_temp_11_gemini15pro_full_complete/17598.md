beautifully:/ˈbjuːtɪfli/ | adv. | 美麗地，漂亮地 | In a beautiful manner; aesthetically pleasing. | She sings beautifully. 她唱歌唱得优美。| The sunset painted the sky beautifully. 夕阳把天空染得格外美丽。 | 近义词：attractively, gracefully, elegantly, exquisitely | 反义词：uglily, horribly, awfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *beutevousli, from Old French beauteusement, from beau (“beautiful”) + -ment (“-ly”).
