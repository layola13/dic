ETYMOLOGY->
non- + discretionary

"non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".

"discretionary" comes from the Late Latin word "discretīōnārius," meaning "distinguishing, discerning," ultimately from "discernere," meaning "to separate, distinguish."
