gerant:/ʒeɪˈrɒ̃/ /ʒeɪˈrɑːnt/
n.
manager, director
A person who manages or directs a business or organization.
The gérant of the hotel was very helpful.  酒店经理非常乐于助人。
synonyms: manager, director, administrator, superintendent, executive, boss
antonyms: employee, subordinate, worker
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French gérant, present participle of gérer (“to manage”), from Latin gerō (“I bear, carry on, manage”).
