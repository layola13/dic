indigenously:/ɪnˈdɪdʒɪnəsli/ | adv. | 本土地；土著地；内在地 | In a way that is native or original to a particular place or region; naturally. |  The plant grows indigenously in the Amazon rainforest.  这种植物原产于亚马逊雨林。 | Synonyms: natively, originally, naturally, domestically | Antonyms: foreignly, exotically | adverb

indigenous:/ɪnˈdɪdʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 本土的；土著的；起源于…的；固有的 | Originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native. |  The kangaroo is indigenous to Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚土生土长的动物。 | Synonyms: native, aboriginal, endemic, original | Antonyms: foreign, alien, exotic, introduced | adjective
