beingness:/ˈbiːɪŋnɪs/
n.|存在; 存在性; 实体|The state or quality of being; existence.  The fact of existing; conscious existence.|She explored the beingness of the universe. 她探索了宇宙的存在。|近义词：existence, presence, reality|反义词：nonexistence, nothingness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From being + -ness.  "Being" comes from the Old English "beon," meaning "to be" or "to exist." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns expressing a state or quality.
