feedstocks:/ˈfiːdstɑːks/ | n. |原料; 给料; 饲料|Raw materials used in an industrial process.|例句：Crude oil is a major feedstock for the petrochemical industry. 原油是石化工业的主要原料。

例句：The plant uses corn as a feedstock for producing ethanol. 该工厂使用玉米作为生产乙醇的原料。|近义词：raw materials, inputs, supplies |反义词: outputs, products |词性:noun


Etymology: "feed" + "stock". "Feed" comes from Old English *fēdan "to feed, nourish," from Proto-Germanic *fodjan. "Stock" comes from Old English stocc "trunk, block, log," also "place, post, position," from Proto-Germanic *stukkaz "tree stump, post." The combined term refers to the stock of materials used for feeding a process.
