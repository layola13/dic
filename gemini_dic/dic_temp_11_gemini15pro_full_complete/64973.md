estimating: /ˈestɪmeɪtɪŋ/ | n./v. | 估计；估价；评估 | the act of judging the size, amount, cost, etc. of something |  They are estimating the cost of the project. 他们正在估算项目的成本。| calculating, assessing, evaluating, gauging | underestimating, overestimating | verb/noun

estimating: /ˈestɪmeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 估计的 |  used to describe something that is based on an estimate |  The estimating cost is $10,000.  估计成本是一万美元。 | approximate, estimated | exact, precise | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *estimaten, from Old French estimer, from Latin aestimāre (“to value, appraise, estimate”), from aes (“bronze, money”) + tīmāre (“to assess, value”).
