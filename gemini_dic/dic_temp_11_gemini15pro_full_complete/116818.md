maxes-out:/ˈmæksɪzˈaʊt/| phrasal verb |达到极限，达到最大值|reaches the maximum limit or capacity; to use something completely|He maxed out his credit card buying Christmas presents. 他为了买圣诞礼物刷爆了信用卡。|近义词:reaches the limit, peaks, tops out|反义词: minimizes, reduces|词性:动词短语

maxes-out:/ˈmæksɪzˈaʊt/| phrasal verb |发挥最大能力，竭尽全力|performs at the highest possible level; exerts maximum effort|The athlete maxed out during the final race.  这位运动员在决赛中发挥出了最佳水平。|近义词:goes all out, gives it all, pushes to the limit|反义词:holds back, conserves energy|词性:动词短语

词源: "max" 源自 "maximum" 的缩写，"out" 表示达到极限或完全。
