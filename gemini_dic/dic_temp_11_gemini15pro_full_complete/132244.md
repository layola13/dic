The nondecisive leader lost the confidence of his team.  这位优柔寡断的领导失去了团队的信任。

近义词: indecisive, irresolute, hesitant, wavering | 反义词: decisive, resolute, determined | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "decisive" (from Latin "decidere" meaning "to cut off, decide")
