denudation: /ˌdiːnjuːˈdeɪʃn/ /ˌdɪnjuːˈdeɪʃn/
noun
剥蚀；剥夺；脱去
the process of stripping something of its covering, possessions, or assets; laying something bare.
The denudation of the forest by excessive logging caused severe soil erosion. 过度砍伐导致森林剥蚀，造成严重的水土流失。
erosion, stripping, deforestation, uncovering
covering, afforestation, concealment
n.
