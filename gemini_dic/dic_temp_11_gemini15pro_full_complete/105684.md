kingtruss:/ˈkɪŋtrʌs/ | n. |  屋架；大王桁架 | A king post truss is a structural component typically used in roof construction.  It consists of two angled rafters meeting at an apex, with a vertical "king post" connecting the apex to the horizontal tie beam below. | The barn's roof was supported by a series of sturdy kingtruss. 谷仓的屋顶由一系列坚固的大王桁架支撑。 |  The old house featured intricate kingtruss construction. 这座老房子以复杂的大王桁架结构为特色。 | synonyms: truss, roof frame | antonyms: N/A | noun
