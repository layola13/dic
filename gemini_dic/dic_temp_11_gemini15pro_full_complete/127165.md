naturalises:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使归化；使入籍；移植；引入
to grant full citizenship to (a foreigner).  To introduce and establish (a plant or animal) in a new region or environment.
The government naturalises immigrants who meet certain requirements. 政府将符合特定要求的移民归化。
Many plants from other countries have been naturalised in this area. 许多来自其他国家的植物已被引进到这一地区。
synonyms:acclimatize, accommodate, accustom, adapt, adjust, assimilate, conform, fit, habituate, inure, season
antonyms:exclude, deport
词性:动词
