encores:/ˈɑːŋkɔːrz/ (US), /ˈɒŋkɔːz/ (UK)| n.|安可，加演，重演；（安可时表演的）加演节目|An encore is an extra performance given at the end of a concert or other performance, often in response to prolonged applause from the audience.  It can also refer to the piece performed during the encore.|The band played two encores. 乐队加演了两首曲子。|Synonyms: curtain call, encore performance, repeat performance|Antonyms: N/A|noun

encores:/ˈɑːŋkɔːrz/ (US), /ˈɒŋkɔːz/ (UK)| vt.|要求（演员）加演，再演|To encore means to request an additional performance, typically by applauding enthusiastically.|The audience encored the singer several times. 观众多次要求歌手加演。|Synonyms: call back, demand a repeat|Antonyms: N/A|verb
