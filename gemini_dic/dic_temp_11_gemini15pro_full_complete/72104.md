floccus:/ˈflɒkəs/ (US) /ˈflɑːkəs/ (UK)| n. |絮状物；绒毛；一小片|A small fluffy mass, especially a tuft of wool or cotton.|The dandelion seeds floated away on delicate flocculi. 蒲公英的种子飘散在纤细的绒毛上。|Synonyms: tuft, wisp, fluff, flake|Antonyms: clump, mass|noun


floccus:/ˈflɒkəs/ (US) /ˈflɑːkəs/ (UK)| n. |锁状云|A type of cloud formation resembling tufts of wool.|The floccus clouds hinted at the coming storm. 锁状云预示着即将到来的暴风雨。|Synonyms: cirrus floccus|Antonyms: N/A|noun
