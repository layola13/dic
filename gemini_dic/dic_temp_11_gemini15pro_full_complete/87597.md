/ˌhɛtərˈɒktəkəs/ | /ˌhɛt̬əˈrɒktəkəs/ | adj. | 异向排列的，互生的 | Arranged alternately on opposite sides of a stem, as leaves or flowers.  | Example: The heterotactic arrangement of the leaves allowed for maximum sunlight exposure. 异向排列的叶子有利于最大限度地吸收阳光。 | Example:  Heterotactous phyllotaxy is common in many plant families.  异向排列的叶序在许多植物科中很常见。 |  Near Synonyms: alternate, distichous | Antonyms: isotactic, spiral | word property: adjective

/ˌhɛtərˈɒktəkəs/ | /ˌhɛt̬əˈrɒktəkəs/ | n. | 异向排列 | The state or condition of being heterotactic. | Example: The botanist noted the heterotaxis in the plant's leaf arrangement. 植物学家注意到该植物叶片排列的异向性。 | Example: Heterotaxis can be an important characteristic for plant identification. 异向性可以作为植物鉴定的一个重要特征。 | Near Synonyms: alternation | Antonyms: isotaxy | word property: noun
