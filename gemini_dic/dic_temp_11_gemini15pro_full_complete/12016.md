arthropods:/ˈɑːrθrəpɒdz/ (UK) /ˈɑːrθrəpɑːdz/ (US)| n. |节肢动物|Invertebrate animals having an exoskeleton, a segmented body, and paired jointed appendages.  This includes insects, arachnids, myriapods, and crustaceans.|Examples: Insects, spiders, crabs, and millipedes are all arthropods.  昆虫、蜘蛛、螃蟹和千足虫都是节肢动物。|近义词:invertebrates (some)|反义词:vertebrates|词性:noun
