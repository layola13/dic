defueling:/diːˈfjuːəlɪŋ/
noun
卸油；撤除燃料
The process of removing fuel from a vehicle, aircraft, or other system.
The defueling of the aircraft took several hours.  飞机的卸油工作花了几个小时。
近义词:fuel removal, draining
反义词:fueling, refueling
n.

defueling:/diːˈfjuːəlɪŋ/
verb
卸油；撤除燃料
To remove fuel from a vehicle, aircraft, or other system.
They are defueling the rocket for safety reasons. 他们出于安全原因正在给火箭卸油。
近义词:remove fuel, drain fuel
反义词:fuel, refuel
v.
