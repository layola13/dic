act upon:/ˈækt əˈpɒn/ (US) /ˈækt əˈpɒn/ (UK)
verb phrase
对…起作用；按照…行事；奉行；
influence or affect something; take action in accordance with something
例句：The committee acted upon the recommendations of the report. 委员会根据报告的建议采取了行动。
例句：He acted upon his lawyer's advice. 他按照律师的建议行事。
近义词：affect, influence, implement, follow, execute
反义词：disregard, ignore, neglect
词性：动词短语


act:/ækt/ (US) /ækt/ (UK)
verb, noun
行动；扮演；假装；法令；一幕；
perform an action; play a role; pretend; a law made by a government; a main division of a play
例句：He acted quickly to put out the fire. 他迅速采取行动灭火。
例句：She acted the part of Juliet in the school play. 她在学校的戏剧中扮演朱丽叶。
近义词：perform, behave, portray, law, statute
反义词：inaction, passivity
词性：动词, 名词


upon:/əˈpɒn/ (US) /əˈpɒn/ (UK)
preposition
在…之上；根据；紧接着；
on; based on; immediately after
例句：The book is upon the table. 书在桌子上。
例句：Upon hearing the news, she burst into tears. 一听到这个消息，她就放声大哭。
近义词：on, onto, after, following
反义词：off, below, beneath
词性：介词
