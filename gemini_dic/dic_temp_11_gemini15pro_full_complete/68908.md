fasts:/fæsts/ | n. | 禁食，斋戒 | Periods of time when people abstain from eating or drinking, often for religious or health reasons. |  She observed several fasts during the religious holiday.  她在这个宗教节日里进行了多次禁食。| synonyms: abstinence, abstentions | antonyms: feasts, binges | noun


fasts:/fæsts/ | v. | 禁食，斋戒(fast的第三人称单数形式) | To abstain from eating or drinking, often for religious or health reasons. | He fasts every Friday for religious reasons. 他每个星期五都因为宗教原因禁食。| synonyms: abstains, forgoes | antonyms: feasts, indulges | verb


Etymology: From Old English *fæstan "to fast," from Proto-Germanic *fastan, from Proto-Indo-European *pasto-. Related to Latin pastor "shepherd" (originally "one who feeds").
