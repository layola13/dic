declassifying:/diːˈklæsɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
解密; 取消…的机密等级
Removing the security classification from (information or documents).
The government is declassifying some of its Cold War files. 政府正在解密一些冷战时期的文件。
synonyms: disclosing, revealing, unveiling, making public
antonyms: classifying, concealing, hiding
v.
