calking:/ˈkɔːkɪŋ/| n.|堵缝，填隙，填塞料|The process of sealing joints or seams to prevent leaks or drafts.|例句：The calking around the windows needs to be replaced. 窗户周围的填缝需要更换。

例句：Apply calking to the gaps between the tiles. 在瓷砖之间的缝隙中涂抹填缝料。


近义词：sealing, filling, caulking|反义词:|词性:noun

calk:/kɔːk/| v.|堵缝，填隙|To seal joints or seams to prevent leaks or drafts.|例句：He calked the cracks in the boat. 他堵住了船上的裂缝。

例句：You should calk the windows before winter. 你应该在冬天来临之前堵住窗户的缝隙。

近义词：seal, fill, caulk|反义词:|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old North French *cauquer, from Latin calcare "to tread, press," from calc- "heel."  Originally referred to pressing oakum (loose hemp fiber) into seams of ships to make them watertight.
