far-rows: /ˈfɑːr roʊz/ | n. |  远排; 远处的行; (尤指田地里的)远处的垄沟 | Rows that are situated a considerable distance away.  Refers especially to rows of crops or plants. |  The farmer pointed to the far-rows and said that's where the best potatoes grew.  农民指着远处的垄沟说，那里生长着最好的土豆。| synonyms: distant rows, remote rows | antonyms: near rows, close rows | noun

far-rows: /ˈfɑːr roʊz/ | adj. | 远排的; 远处的 |  Describing something related to or located in far rows.  | The far-rows field yielded a surprisingly large harvest. 远处的田地获得了惊人的大丰收。 | synonyms: distant, remote | antonyms: near, close | adjective
