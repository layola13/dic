disfeature:/dɪsˈfiːtʃər/
n.
缺点，缺陷，不足之处
An undesirable or unattractive feature or characteristic.
This phone's small screen is a disfeature for me.  这款手机的小屏幕对我来说是一个缺点。
Synonyms: drawback, flaw, defect, shortcoming, disadvantage
Antonyms: advantage, feature, benefit, merit
noun
