mesometrium:/ˌmɛzoʊˈmiːtriəm/| n. |子宫阔韧带|The mesometrium is the largest part of the broad ligament of the uterus, excluding the mesosalpinx (which supports the fallopian tubes) and mesovarium (which supports the ovaries). It acts as a mesentery to the uterus.  It is composed of two layers of peritoneum that separate to enclose the parametrium.|The surgeon carefully identified the mesometrium during the hysterectomy.  外科医生在子宫切除术中仔细地识别了子宫阔韧带。|Synonym:  broad ligament (in part)|Antonym:  None|noun
