acolyte:/ˈækəlaɪt/| n.|助手，侍从，追随者|A person who assists in a religious service or ceremony; a follower or assistant.|The priest was assisted by his acolytes during the mass. 神父在弥撒期间由他的助手协助。|近义词：assistant, follower, supporter, disciple|反义词：leader, opponent, antagonist|noun

acolyte:/ˈækəlaɪt/| n.|（尤指狂热的）追随者|A devoted follower or admirer.|He is an acolyte of the famous philosopher. 他是那位著名哲学家的狂热追随者。|近义词：follower, admirer, devotee, fan|反义词：critic, detractor|noun
