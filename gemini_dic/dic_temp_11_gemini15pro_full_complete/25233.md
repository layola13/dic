budlike: /ˈbʌdlaɪk/ | adj. | 花蕾状的，像芽的 | Resembling a bud in form. | The plant produced small, budlike flowers.  这种植物开出小小的、像花蕾一样的花。| Similar: nascent, burgeoning, incipient, developing | Opposite: mature, developed, full-grown | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'bud' + '-like'. 'Bud' comes from Middle English 'budde', of uncertain origin, possibly related to Dutch 'bot' (bud, sprout). '-like' is a common suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of".
