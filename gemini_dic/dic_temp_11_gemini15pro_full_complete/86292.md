heavyish:/ˈhɛviɪʃ/| adj.|有点重的，稍重的|Somewhat heavy; rather heavy.  It describes something that is heavier than might be expected, but not excessively so.|例句：The box was heavyish, but I could still lift it.  箱子有点重，但我还能搬动它。

例句：The backpack felt heavyish after I added the water bottles.  加了水瓶后，背包感觉有点重了。|近义词：fairly heavy, rather heavy, a bit heavy|反义词:light, lightweight|词性:形容词
