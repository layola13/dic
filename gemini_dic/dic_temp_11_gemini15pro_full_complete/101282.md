involve:/ɪnˈvɒlv/ (British), /ɪnˈvɑːlv/ (American)| v. |包含，牵涉，使参与，潜心于|To include something as a necessary part or result; to cause somebody to take part in something.|例句：The job involves a lot of traveling.  这份工作需要经常出差。

例句：He was involved in a car accident. 他卷入了一场车祸。|近义词：include, entail, engage, immerse|反义词:exclude, detach|词性:动词
