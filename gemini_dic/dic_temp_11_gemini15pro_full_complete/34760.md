clifflike:/ˈklɪflaɪk/| adj.|像悬崖的，峭壁似的|Resembling a cliff; extremely steep.|The coastline was clifflike, dropping sharply into the sea. 海岸线像悬崖一样，陡峭地落入海中。|The mountain had a clifflike face. 这座山有一个像悬崖一样的面。|近义词：precipitous, sheer, abrupt|反义词:sloping, gradual|词性:adjective

词源: cliff (n.) + -like (suffix)

cliff: From Old English *cliff, from Old Norse klif "cliff, crag."
-like:  A suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," "like," from Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-likan.
