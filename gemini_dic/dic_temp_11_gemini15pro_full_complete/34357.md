classicalism:/ˈklæsɪsɪzəm/| n.|古典主义|A style in art, architecture, literature, and music that is based on the styles of ancient Greece and Rome.|The museum's collection showcases the beauty of European classicalism.  这家博物馆的藏品展示了欧洲古典主义之美。|
The architecture of the building reflects a strong sense of classicalism. 这座建筑的结构体现了强烈的古典主义风格。|近义词：classicism, neoclassicism|反义词：romanticism, modernism|词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *classicisme*, from *classique* (“classic”) + *-isme* (“-ism”).  Ultimately from Latin *classicus* (“belonging to the first class of citizens”), from *classis* (“class”).
