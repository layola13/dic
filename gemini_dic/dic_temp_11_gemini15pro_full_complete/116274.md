masse:/mæs/ | n. |群众，民众；大量，众多| A large number of people or things; a body of people considered as a whole. | The politician addressed the assembled masse. 这位政治家向聚集的群众发表了讲话。|  The masse of data was overwhelming. 大量的数据让人不知所措。| synonyms: crowd, multitude, throng, host, majority | antonyms: minority, few, individual | noun

masse:/mɑs/ (This pronunciation is less common, usually used in the context of "en masse") | adv. |全体地，一起地| All together; in a body. | The students left the classroom en masse. 学生们一起离开了教室。| The workers resigned en masse. 工人们集体辞职。| synonyms: together, collectively, jointly, simultaneously | antonyms: individually, separately, singly | adverb
