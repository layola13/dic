deputes:/ˈdɛpjʊteɪz/ | n. | deputies |  A person appointed to act for or represent another; a substitute.  It is the plural form of "deputy". | The president sent his deputes to the conference. 总统派了他的代表们去参加会议。| The deputes cast their votes. 代表们投了票。 | synonyms: representatives, delegates, agents, envoys, commissioners | antonyms: leaders, principals, chiefs | noun
