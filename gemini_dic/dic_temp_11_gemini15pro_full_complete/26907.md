calamander:/ˌkæləˈmændər/ | n. | 乌木；黑檀；印度乌木 |A hard, dark-colored wood from a Sri Lankan ebony tree, prized for its beautiful markings. |  The antique desk was made of lustrous calamander. 这张古董桌子是用光泽的乌木制成的。

The calamander wood, now rare, was highly sought after for furniture making. 现在稀有的乌木曾经是制作家具的热门材料。 | 近义词：ebony, rosewood | 反义词: softwood | 词性: 名词
