outdistricts:/ˈaʊtˌdɪstrɪkts/
noun
郊区，偏远地区
Areas on the edge of a city or town, or in the countryside away from main centers of population.
People living in the outdistricts often have to commute long distances to work.  住在郊区的人们经常要长途通勤上班。
The outdistricts are less developed than the city center. 郊区不如市中心发达。
synonyms: outskirts, suburbs, hinterland, periphery
antonyms: city center, downtown, urban area
n.
