flex one's muscles: 展示实力  He flexed his muscles by buying the biggest company in town. 他通过收购城里最大的公司来展示实力。
flex one's brain:  动脑筋  She needed to flex her brain to solve the complex puzzle. 她需要动脑筋来解决这个复杂的谜题。
flex something (like a rule):  变通 (规则)  The boss flexed the rules to accommodate the employee's special circumstances. 老板为了适应员工的特殊情况而变通了规则。
flex time:  弹性工作时间  She appreciates the flex time offered by her company. 她很感激公司提供的弹性工作时间。
no flex zone:  不允许炫耀或展示实力的地方  The library is a no flex zone, so please be quiet. 图书馆是不允许炫耀或展示实力的地方，所以请保持安静。
