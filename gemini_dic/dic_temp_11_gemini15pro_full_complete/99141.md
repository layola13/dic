intercession:/ˌɪntərˈseʃən/ | n. | 祈求；代祷；调解；斡旋 | The act of intervening on behalf of another, especially by prayer or petition.  |  Her intercession saved him from punishment. 她的求情使他免于受罚。 | synonyms: mediation, intervention, arbitration, plea, appeal | antonyms: indifference, neglect, inaction | noun

intercession:/ˌɪntərˈseʃən/ | n. | 祈求；代祷；调解；斡旋 | The act of pleading on behalf of another. |  Through the intercession of friends, the dispute was settled.  通过朋友们的调解，争端得到了解决。| synonyms:  supplication, petition | antonyms: condemnation, accusation | noun
