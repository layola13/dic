nonpotential:/ˌnɒnpəˈtɛnʃəl/
adjective
非势的，非位的
Not derivable from a potential; specifically (Mech.) not having or involving potential energy.  Not relating to or involving potential difference.
The nonpotential forces in a system can cause energy dissipation. 系统中的非势能力量会导致能量耗散。
The magnetic field caused by a steady current is a nonpotential field. 稳恒电流产生的磁场是非势场。
nonconservative, dissipative
conservative, potential
