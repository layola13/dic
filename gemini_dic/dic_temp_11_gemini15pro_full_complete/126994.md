nasalities:/neɪˈzælɪtiz/
n.
鼻音；鼻音化
sounds produced with the velum lowered, allowing air to escape through the nose
The singer's nasalities added a unique quality to her voice. 歌手的鼻音为她的声音增添了独特的韵味。

近义词: nasal sounds, nasalization
反义词: orality (口音)
词性: noun
