casteisms: /ˈkɑːstɪzəmz/ | n. | 种姓主义；种姓歧视 | Prejudice or discrimination based on caste, especially in India.  The practice of treating people differently based on their perceived social status, often inherited. |  India has struggled with casteisms for centuries. 印度几个世纪以来一直在与种姓主义作斗争。| Casteism is a deeply rooted problem in some societies. 种姓主义在一些社会中是一个根深蒂固的问题。 | synonyms: discrimination, prejudice, bigotry, bias, intolerance | antonyms: equality, tolerance, acceptance, impartiality, fairness | noun
