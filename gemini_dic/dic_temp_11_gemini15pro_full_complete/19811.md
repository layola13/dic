bimastism:/baɪˈmæstɪzəm/
n.
双乳房；双乳畸形
The condition of having two breasts, or two mammary glands.  This is the normal condition in female mammals.
She was born with bimastism, a common trait among mammals. 她生来就有双乳房，这是哺乳动物的共同特征。
Bimastism is a defining characteristic of female mammals. 双乳房是雌性哺乳动物的一个显著特征。

近义词:  mammality (哺乳动物的特性)  (This refers to the broader characteristic of having mammary glands, not specifically two.)
反义词: amastia (无乳症)
词性: noun
