fairly:/ˈferli/| adv.|相当地，公平地，还算|moderately; to a certain extent; justly; impartially|The test was fairly difficult. 这项测试相当难。|近义词:quite, rather, reasonably, pretty|反义词:unfairly, very, extremely|词性:副词
词源:From Middle English *fairely, derived from Old English fæġerlice (“fairly, beautifully, suitably”), equivalent to fair +‎ -ly.
