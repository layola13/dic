nonignitibility:/ˌnɒnɪɡˌnaɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可燃性，不燃性
The nonignitibility of the material makes it suitable for use in fire-resistant clothing.
这种材料的不可燃性使其适用于制造防火服。
non-ignitibility, incombustibility
ignitibility, flammability
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + ignitability, from ignite (v.) + -ibility.  Ignite comes from the Latin *ignitus, past participle of *ignire "to set on fire," from ignis "fire."
