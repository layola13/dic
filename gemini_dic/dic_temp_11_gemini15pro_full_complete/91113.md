hyaloid:/ˈhaɪəlɔɪd/| adj. n.|透明的，玻璃状的；玻璃体|Relating to or resembling glass; transparent; vitreous.  Specifically, pertaining to the vitreous humor of the eye.|The doctor examined the patient's hyaloid artery. 医生检查了病人的玻璃体动脉。|Near synonyms: vitreous, glassy, transparent|Antonyms: opaque, cloudy|adjective, noun

hyaloid:/ˈhaɪəlɔɪd/| n.|玻璃体|The transparent gelatinous substance that fills the eyeball behind the lens.|Damage to the hyaloid can cause vision problems.  玻璃体损伤会导致视力问题。|Near synonyms: vitreous humor, vitreous body|Antonyms: N/A (Anatomical term, no direct opposite)|noun
