falcation:/fɔːlˈkeɪʃən/
n.
弯曲,镰刀形,用镰刀割
the act of curving or bending, especially into a sickle shape; the act of cutting with a sickle
The falcation of the moon was clearly visible in the night sky.  月亮的弯曲在夜空中清晰可见。

近义词:curvature, bend, crescent, reaping
反义词:straightening
