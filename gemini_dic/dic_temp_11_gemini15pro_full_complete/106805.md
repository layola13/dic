labiodentals:/ˌleɪbiəˈdɛntlz/
| adj. n.| 唇齿音；唇齿形的|Sounds produced by the lower lip touching the upper teeth, such as /f/ and /v/.  Also refers to the consonants that are produced this way. |The sounds /f/ and /v/ are labiodental consonants. /f/和/v/是唇齿辅音。|近义词:None (for the articulation manner).  Could list specific labiodental consonants like /f/ and /v/ if referring to the sounds themselves.|反义词:None.  Different manners of articulation could be considered opposites, but not a single antonym.|adjective, noun
