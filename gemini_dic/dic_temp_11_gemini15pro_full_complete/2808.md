advantageous:/ˌædvənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|有利的，有益的|Giving someone an advantage; beneficial. Providing a favorable situation or outcome.|Choosing this location for our business is advantageous because of the low tax rates. 选择这个地点作为我们的企业所在地是有利的，因为这里的税率很低。|例句：An advantageous position 有利位置

例句：Learning a new language can be advantageous to your career. 学习一门新的语言对你的职业生涯是有利的。|近义词：beneficial, favorable, profitable, helpful, useful|反义词：disadvantageous, unfavorable, detrimental, harmful|词性:adjective
