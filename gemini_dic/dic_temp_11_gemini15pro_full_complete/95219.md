inbreak: /ˈɪnbreɪk/
n.
入侵，侵入；突然爆发
A sudden invasion or outbreak, especially of something undesirable.
The inbreak of floodwaters caused widespread damage. 洪水的入侵造成了广泛的破坏。
近义词: irruption, incursion, invasion, outbreak
反义词: retreat, withdrawal
