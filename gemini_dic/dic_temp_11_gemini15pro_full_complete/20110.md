biotechnologists:/ˌbaɪoʊˌtɛkˈnɑːlədʒɪsts/
n.
生物技术专家，生物科技人员
Scientists and biotechnologists are working on new ways to combat diseases. 科学家和生物技术专家正在研究对抗疾病的新方法。
Biotechnologists play a crucial role in developing new medicines and therapies. 生物技术专家在新药物和疗法的开发中起着至关重要的作用。
近义词: genetic engineers, life scientists, bioscientists
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun (Plural)


Etymology:
bio- + technology + -ist + -s
bio-: from Greek bios "life"
technology: from Greek tekhnologia "systematic treatment of an art, craft, or technique," from tekhne "art, skill, craft"
-ist: a suffix forming agent nouns, denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something, or holds certain principles.
-s: plural marker
