circumpolar:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈpoʊlər/ | adj. |环极的，绕极的| Situated around or near a pole, especially the North or South Pole.  Relating to the regions around the North or South Pole.|  例句：Circumpolar stars are visible all night long. 环极星整夜可见。

例句：The circumpolar current flows around Antarctica. 环极洋流环绕南极洲流动。|近义词：polar, arctic, antarctic |反义词:equatorial, tropical|词性:形容词
