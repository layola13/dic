ashok: /əˈʃoʊk/ | n. | 阿育王，阿输迦，无忧树 | A male given name, derived from Sanskrit, meaning "without sorrow" or "painless". It is most commonly associated with Ashoka the Great, an Indian emperor of the Maurya Dynasty. It can also refer to the Ashoka tree (Saraca asoca), a sacred tree in Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism. | Ashoka was a great emperor. 阿育王是一位伟大的皇帝。 |  Ashoka unified most of the Indian subcontinent. 阿育王统一了印度次大陆的大部分地区。| synonyms: none (for the name itself, though synonyms can be found for "emperor" or "tree" depending on the context) | antonyms: none | noun
