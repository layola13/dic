coarsest:/ˈkɔːrsɪst/ /ˈkoʊrsɪst/| adj.|最粗糙的，最粗俗的|Superlative form of coarse: most coarse.  It refers to something having a rough texture, being of low quality, or lacking refinement in behavior or language.|The coarsest sandpaper is used for removing the most stubborn paint. 最粗糙的砂纸用来去除最顽固的油漆。|Synonyms: roughest, harshest, crudest, vulgarist|Antonyms: finest, smoothest, softest, gentlest|adjective
