fabulist:/ˈfæbjəlɪst/| n.|寓言家；说谎者|A person who writes or tells fables; a liar, especially a habitual liar. |Aesop was a renowned fabulist of ancient Greece.  伊索是古希腊著名的寓言家。

He was known as a fabulist, embellishing the truth with fantastical tales. 他被称为说谎者，用奇幻的故事来修饰真相。|近义词：liar, storyteller, fibber, prevaricator|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|词性: noun
