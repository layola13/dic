foresail:/ˈfɔːrseɪl/ /ˈfɔːrsl/| n.|前帆|The lowest sail set on the foremast of a square-rigged vessel; a jib. 在横帆式帆船的前桅上设置的最低的帆；艏三角帆。|The foresail ripped in the strong wind. 強風中前帆撕裂了。|近义词：jib, headsail|反义词:mainsail|词性:noun
