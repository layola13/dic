greengage:/ˈɡriːnɡeɪdʒ/| n.|青梅，绿梅|A small, round, greenish-yellow plum with a smooth skin.  A sweet and slightly tart fruit.|例句：I made a delicious greengage jam this summer. 我今年夏天做了一罐美味的青梅果酱。

例句：The greengages are ripening on the tree. 树上的青梅正在成熟。|近义词：plum, gage|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *grenegage*, from Old North French *grein gage*, literally, "green gage". The "gage" refers to a variety of plum cultivated by Sir William Gage in the 18th century.
