dependently:/dɪˈpɛndəntli/
adv.
依赖地，依靠地
in a dependent or subordinate manner; relying on someone or something else for support.
He dependently clung to his mother's arm. 他依赖地抓着他母亲的胳膊。
近义词: subserviently, passively
反义词: independently, autonomously
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From dependent + -ly. Dependent comes from the Old French *dependant*, present participle of dependre "to hang down, depend," from Latin dependere "to hang down, hang from, be dependent on," from de- "down" + pendere "to hang, weigh."
