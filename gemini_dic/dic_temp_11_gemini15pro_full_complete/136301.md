nonreprisal:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpraɪ.zəl/ | n. | 不报复，不采取报复行动 |  The agreement included a clause of nonreprisal, ensuring neither side would retaliate against the other. |  Example:  The treaty guaranteed nonreprisal for prisoners of war.  战俘条约保证不报复战俘。

Example:  The government declared a policy of nonreprisal against the protestors. 政府宣布对示威者采取不报复政策。 | Synonym:  forbearance, leniency, clemency, amnesty | Antonym:  retaliation, revenge, reprisal | noun
