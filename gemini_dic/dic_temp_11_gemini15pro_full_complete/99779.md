intermezzo:/ˌɪntərˈmetsoʊ/ | n. | 间奏曲，插曲；幕间节目 | A short piece of entertainment performed between the acts of a play or opera, or between the movements of a longer musical work.  | The clown provided a brief intermezzo between the two acts of the play.  小丑在两幕戏之间表演了一段简短的幕间节目。 |  The orchestra played a lively intermezzo.  管弦乐队演奏了一段轻快的间奏曲。| synonym: interlude, interval, break | antonym: main act, principal performance | noun
