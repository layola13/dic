flimsiness:/ˈflɪmzɪnəs/
n.
薄弱, 脆弱, 易损坏
the quality of being flimsy; easily damaged or destroyed
The flimsiness of the material made it unsuitable for a tent.  这种材料的脆弱性使它不适合做帐篷。
fragility, weakness, frailty
strength, sturdiness, robustness
noun
