eburnine:/ˈɛbɜːrnaɪn/
adjective
象牙质的; 像象牙的
Resembling ivory; ivory-white.
Her eburnine complexion made her look like a porcelain doll. 她象牙色的肤色使她看起来像个瓷娃娃。
近义词:ivory, alabaster, white
反义词:dark, black
词性:adjective
