geospatially:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈspeɪʃəli/
adjective
地理空间地
Relating to or involving the geographic location of something.  Characterized by location in or relation to space on the earth's surface.
Geospatial data is used in many fields, including urban planning and environmental monitoring. 地理空间数据被用于许多领域，包括城市规划和环境监测。
The geospatial analysis revealed patterns of deforestation. 地理空间分析揭示了森林砍伐的模式。
近义词: geographically
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider conceptually: non-spatial, aspatial)
词性: adjective
