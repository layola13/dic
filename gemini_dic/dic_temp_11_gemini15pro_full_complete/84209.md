half-ripened:/ˌhɑːfˈraɪpənd/ | adj. | 半熟的，未完全成熟的 | Not fully ripened; partially ripe. | The half-ripened berries were tart but edible. 半熟的浆果很酸，但可以食用。|The half-ripened fruit was still too hard to eat. 半熟的水果仍然太硬，不能吃。| 近义词: underripe, immature | 反义词: ripe, mature | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "half" + "ripened," the past participle of the verb "ripen."  "Half" comes from Old English "healf," and "ripen" comes from Old English "rīpian."
