formalize:/ˈfɔːməlaɪz/ (US) /ˈfɔːməlaɪz/ (UK)
verb
使正式化；使规范化；正式提出；用公式表示
To make something official or definite by expressing it in a clear and definite way, often in writing; to give something a definite structure or shape.
The agreement was formalized in a signed contract.  该协议以签署合同的方式正式化。
They formalized their partnership by creating a limited liability company. 他们通过成立一家有限责任公司使他们的合作关系正式化。
近义词: codify, systematize, regularize, standardize, institutionalize
反义词: informalize (less common), loosen, relax
词性: verb
