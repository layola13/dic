neuropathy:/nʊˈrɒpəθi/ /nʊˈrɑːpəθi/
n.
神经病，神经病变
A disease or dysfunction of one or more peripheral nerves, typically causing numbness or weakness.
Example: Diabetic neuropathy can cause pain and numbness in the feet and legs. 糖尿病神经病变会导致脚和腿疼痛和麻木。

Example: He suffers from peripheral neuropathy due to chemotherapy. 他因化疗而患有周围神经病变。
neuralgia, nerve damage
health, normal nerve function
noun
