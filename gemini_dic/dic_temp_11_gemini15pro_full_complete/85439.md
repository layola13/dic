hash-brown:/ˈhæʃ braʊn/ (US), /ˈhæʃ braʊn/ (UK)| n. |土豆煎饼，薯饼|A dish of cooked potatoes, especially one in which the potatoes are chopped into small pieces and fried until brown. |例句：I ordered hash browns with my breakfast. 我早餐点了薯饼。
