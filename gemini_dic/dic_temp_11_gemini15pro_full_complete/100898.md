intuitionist:/ɪnˌtuːˈɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 直觉主义者；直觉论者 | A person who believes in intuitionism, a philosophy that emphasizes the role of intuition in acquiring knowledge. |  Many mathematicians rejected Brouwer's intuitionist ideas. 许多数学家反对布劳威尔的直觉主义思想。| synonym:  instinctivist | antonym: rationalist | noun

intuitionistic:/ɪnˌtuːɪˈʃənˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 直觉主义的；直觉论的 | Relating to or characteristic of intuitionism. |  He adopted an intuitionistic approach to the problem. 他采用了一种直觉主义的方法来解决这个问题。 | synonym: instinctive, visceral | antonym: rational, logical | adjective
