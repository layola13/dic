nondivulging:/ˌnɒndaɪˈvʌldʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 不泄露的，保密的 | Not revealing private or secret information. |  The company has a strict policy of nondivulging client information.  这家公司有严格的保密客户信息的政策。| Synonym: confidential, secret, undisclosed | Antonym: revealing, disclosing, public | word property: adjective

nondivulging:/ˌnɒndaɪˈvʌldʒɪŋ/ | n. | 不泄露，保密 | The act of not revealing private or secret information. |  The nondivulging of this information is crucial to national security.  不泄露这些信息对国家安全至关重要。 | Synonym: confidentiality, secrecy, discretion | Antonym: disclosure, revelation, publicity | word property: noun


Etymology:
From non- (not) + divulging (present participle of divulge), from Latin divulgare "to make public," from di- "widely" + vulgare "to publish," from vulgus "the common people."
