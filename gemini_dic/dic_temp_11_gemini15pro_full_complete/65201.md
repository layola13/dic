ethylic: /ɛˈθɪlɪk/ /iːˈθɪlɪk/
adjective
乙醇的，含乙基的
Of or relating to ethyl alcohol; containing ethyl.
例句：Ethylic fermentation is crucial in the production of alcoholic beverages. 乙醇发酵在酒精饮料的生产中至关重要。

例句：The chemist analyzed the compound and found it to have ethylic properties.  化学家分析了这种化合物，发现它具有乙醇的特性。

近义词：alcoholic
反义词: non-alcoholic
词性: adjective
