N/A.  "fer" is a suffix and does not form stand-alone phrases.  It combines with other words to create new words. For example:

transfer of power = 权力转移
transfer student = 转校生
transfer of ownership = 所有权转移
transfer of data = 数据传输
transfer of funds = 资金转移
