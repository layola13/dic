exteriority:/ˌɛkstɪəriˈɔːrɪti/
n.
外在性，外部性
The state or quality of being exterior; outwardness.
The artist explored themes of interiority and exteriority in her work.  这位艺术家在她的作品中探索了内在性和外在性的主题。
exteriorization, externality, outside, surface
interiority, internality, inside
noun
