furphies:/ˈfɜːrfiz/ /ˈfɝfiz/
|n.|荒诞的故事；无稽之谈；谣言|False or absurd stories; nonsense; rumours.  Australian slang.|He's always telling furphies about his adventures. 他总是编造关于他冒险经历的荒诞故事。|Synonyms: falsehoods, fabrications, lies, canards|Antonyms: truths, facts|noun

furphy:/ˈfɜːrfi/ /ˈfɝfi/
|n.|荒诞的故事；无稽之谈；谣言|A false or absurd story; nonsense; rumour. Australian slang.  Also refers to a type of water cart used in WWI, from which rumours and gossip were often spread.|Did you hear the latest furphy going around the office? 你听说了办公室里流传的最新谣言吗？|Synonyms: falsehood, fabrication, lie, canard|Antonyms: truth, fact|noun
