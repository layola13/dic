experimentarian:/ɪkˌspɛrɪmɛnˈtɛəriən/
n.
实验者; 乐于尝试新事物的人
A person who likes to try new things, especially new types of food or experiences.
She's a real experimentarian when it comes to cooking, always trying unusual ingredients.  她在烹饪方面是个真正的实验者，总是尝试不寻常的食材。
近义词: innovator, pioneer, adventurer, explorer
反义词: traditionalist, conservative
词性: 名词
