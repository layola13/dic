noneruptive:/ˌnɑːnɪˈrʌptɪv/
adjective
非喷发的；未爆发的
Not eruptive; not bursting forth.
The volcano is currently in a noneruptive state. 这座火山目前处于非喷发状态。
The doctor diagnosed a noneruptive skin condition. 医生诊断出一种非喷发性皮肤病。
Synonyms: dormant, quiescent, inactive
Antonyms: eruptive, active, explosive
词性: adjective
