outsail:/ˌaʊtˈseɪl/ | v. | 超过…的航行速度；在航海比赛中胜过 | To sail faster than another vessel. To surpass in sailing skill or speed. |  The smaller yacht easily outsailed the larger one in the strong winds.  这艘较小的游艇在大风中轻易地超过了那艘较大的游艇。| Synonyms: outstrip, overtake, surpass | Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind | verb

outsail:/ˌaʊtˈseɪl/ | v. | 比…更成功，技高一筹 (比喻义) | To be more successful than; outmaneuver. |  She outsailed her competitors in the business world.  她在商界胜过她的竞争对手。 | Synonyms: outmaneuver, surpass, outperform | Antonyms: be outdone, lose to | verb
