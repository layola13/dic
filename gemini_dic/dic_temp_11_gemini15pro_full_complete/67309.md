exsuscitate: /ˌɛksˈsʌsɪteɪt/
verb
使苏醒；使复活；唤起
To rouse from inactivity or apparent death; revive.  To stir up; excite.
The doctor exsuscitated the patient who had overdosed. 医生使服药过量的病人苏醒过来。
The politician's speech exsuscitated the crowd's patriotism.  政治家的演讲激起了民众的爱国热情。

近义词: revive, resuscitate, awaken, arouse, stimulate, invigorate
反义词: suppress, subdue, deaden, stifle, quell
