outmerchant:/ˌaʊtˈmɜːrtʃənt/
verb
胜过…的商业头脑;在商业上超过
to surpass in business acumen or trading; to outdo in business dealings.
The new company outmerchanted its competitors with innovative marketing strategies. 这家新公司以创新的营销策略在商业上胜过了竞争对手。
The online retailer quickly outmerchanted traditional brick-and-mortar stores.  这家在线零售商迅速在商业上超过了传统的实体店。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, outperform
Antonyms: underperform, be outdone by
词性:动词
