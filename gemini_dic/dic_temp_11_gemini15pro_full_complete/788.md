absorbency:/əbˈzɔːrbənsi/ /əbˈsɔːrbənsi/
noun
吸收性，吸水性
The ability of a material to absorb liquids or gases.
This towel has high absorbency.  这条毛巾吸水性很好。
The diaper's absorbency is key to keeping the baby dry.  尿布的吸收性是保持婴儿干燥的关键。
近义词：absorption, porosity
反义词：impermeability, impenetrability
词性: noun

Etymology:
From absorb + -ency.  "Absorb" comes from the Latin "absorbere,"  meaning "to suck up," from "ab-" (from, away) + "sorbere" (to suck in). "-ency" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state from adjectives ending in -ent.
