dismissive:/dɪsˈmɪsɪv/| adj.|轻蔑的，不屑一顾的|Showing a lack of interest or respect; treating something as unimportant.|She gave a dismissive wave of her hand. 她轻蔑地挥了挥手。|Synonyms: contemptuous, scornful, disdainful|Antonyms: respectful, attentive, interested|adjective

dismissive:/dɪsˈmɪsɪv/| adj.|解雇的，免职的|(less common) Relating to dismissal from a job.|The dismissive letter from the company was cold and impersonal. 公司的解雇信冷冰冰的，没有人情味。|Synonyms:  expelling, discharging|Antonyms: hiring, employing|adjective
