nonabdicative: /ˌnɒnˌæbdɪˈkeɪtɪv/
adjective
不放弃的；不退位的
Not abdicating; refusing to relinquish power or responsibility.

The king's nonabdicative stance surprised many, as he was facing immense pressure to step down.  国王不退位的立场让许多人感到惊讶，因为他面临着巨大的下台压力。

She took a nonabdicative approach to her role as CEO, refusing to delegate even the smallest tasks. 她以不放弃的态度对待自己的首席执行官角色，即使是最小的任务也不愿委派。

近义词:  unyielding, resolute, steadfast
反义词: abdicative, yielding, resigning
词性: adjective
