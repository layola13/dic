hedge-about:/ˈhɛdʒ əˈbaʊt/
| adj.|被树篱包围的；受限制的；躲躲闪闪的|Surrounded by hedges; restricted; evasive.  Relating to or characterized by evasion or ambiguity.|The politician gave a hedge-about answer to the journalist's question, avoiding a direct commitment.|近义词：evasive, ambiguous, guarded, noncommittal, circuitous|反义词:direct, forthright, candid, open|词性:adjective
词源: From "hedge" (noun) + "about" (adverb/preposition), literally meaning "surrounded by a hedge." Figuratively, it refers to being restricted or evasive in speech or action, as if hiding behind a hedge.
