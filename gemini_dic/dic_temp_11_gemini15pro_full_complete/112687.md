lowlands:/ˈləʊləndz/ | n. | 低地；平原 | An area of land that is lower than the surrounding country. | The fertile lowlands are ideal for farming.  肥沃的低地非常适合耕种。 | plains, valleys | highlands, uplands | noun

lowland:/ˈləʊlənd/ | adj. | 低地的 | Relating to or characteristic of lowlands. | The lowland areas are prone to flooding. 低洼地区容易发生洪水。| low-lying | highland, upland | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lowelondes*, from Old English *lāglond*, from *lāg* (“low”) + *land* (“land”).  Cognate with Dutch *laagland* (“lowland”), German *Land* (“land”).
