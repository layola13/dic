folio: /ˈfoʊlioʊ/ (US) /ˈfəʊliəʊ/ (UK)| n. |对折纸张；对开纸；页码；文件夹；（律师等的）委托案件|A large sheet of paper folded once to make two leaves, or four pages, of a book or manuscript. Also, a book or manuscript made up of such sheets, or a page number. It can also refer to a collection of documents about a particular person, subject, etc., or the business of a lawyer or law firm.|

例句：
The artist carefully sketched his design on a large folio.  艺术家在一张大对开纸上仔细地画了他的设计。

The lawyer reviewed the folio of his client's case.  律师审查了客户案件的卷宗。


近义词：sheet, leaf, page, folder, document, case
反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning)
词性: noun
