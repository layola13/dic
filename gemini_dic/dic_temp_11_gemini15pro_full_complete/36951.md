colourings:/ˈkʌlərɪŋz/
n.
着色，染色；颜色；染料；色素
the act of adding colour to something, or the colours that are added
例句：The colourings in this painting are vibrant and exciting. 这幅画中的色彩鲜艳而激动人心。

例句：Food colourings are often added to enhance the appearance of processed foods.  食用色素经常被添加到加工食品中以增强其外观。

近义词：colorations, hues, tints, shades, pigments, dyes
反义词: bleachings (漂白)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "colour" (color in American English) + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs, often indicating the result or product of an action). "Colour" ultimately comes from the Old French "color" (later "couleur"), derived from the Latin "color".
