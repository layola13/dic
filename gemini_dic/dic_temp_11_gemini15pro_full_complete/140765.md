omnivorousness:/ˌɒmˈnɪvərəsnəs/ | n. | 杂食性；什么都吃；无所不知 |The state or quality of being omnivorous; eating any kind of food.  It can also refer to a wide range of interests or tastes. |Her omnivorousness in reading allowed her to discuss a wide variety of topics. 她广泛的阅读使她能够讨论各种各样的话题。| 近义词：versatility, all-inclusiveness, eclecticism | 反义词：pickiness, selectivity, fastidiousness | 词性: noun

omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. | 杂食的；什么都吃的；无所不知的 | Eating both animal and plant foods; having a wide variety of tastes or interests. |Pigs are omnivorous animals. 猪是杂食动物。| 近义词：versatile, all-embracing, eclectic | 反义词：herbivorous, carnivorous, specialist | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *omnivorus* (all-devouring), from *omnis* (all) + *vorare* (to devour).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
