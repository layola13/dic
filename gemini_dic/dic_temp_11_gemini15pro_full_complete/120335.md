minimalism:/ˈmɪnɪməlɪzəm/
n.
极简主义
A style or technique (as in music, literature or design) that is characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity.
Minimalism is becoming increasingly popular as people seek to declutter their lives.  极简主义越来越受欢迎，因为人们寻求简化他们的生活。
simplicity, reductionism
maximalism, extravagance
noun
