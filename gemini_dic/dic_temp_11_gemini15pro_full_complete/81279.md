grant-maintained: /ˈɡrɑːnt meɪnˈteɪnd/ | adj. | 拨款维持的；政府资助的 |  Funded directly by the government, rather than by a local authority.  Relating to a school in the UK that received its funding directly from central government, rather than through the local education authority. This system existed from 1988 to 1998. |  The grant-maintained school had more freedom in managing its budget. 这所拨款维持的学校在管理预算方面有更大的自由。 | Synonym: government-funded, state-funded | Antonym: privately funded | Adjective

grant-maintained: /ˈɡrɑːnt meɪnˈteɪnd/ | n. | 拨款维持的学校 | A school that is grant-maintained. | Several grant-maintained schools achieved high academic standards.  几所拨款维持的学校达到了很高的学术水平。 | Synonym: government-funded school, state-funded school | Antonym: private school | Noun
