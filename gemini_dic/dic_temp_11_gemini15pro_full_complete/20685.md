bladderless:/ˈblædələs/| adj.|无膀胱的，没有膀胱的|Lacking a bladder, the organ that stores urine.|The bladderless fish excretes waste directly into the water.  这种无膀胱的鱼直接将废物排到水中。|近义词: anuric (although this refers to the lack of urine production, not necessarily the lack of a bladder)|反义词: N/A (Having a bladder is the normal state, no single word directly opposes "bladderless")|词性: adjective
