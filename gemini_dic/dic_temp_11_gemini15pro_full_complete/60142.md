effectually:/ɪˈfɛktʃuəli/
adverb
有效地；彻底地
in a way that produces a desired effect; completely
The problem was effectually dealt with.  这个问题得到了有效的处理。
The medicine effectually cured his illness.  这药有效地治好了他的病。
synonyms: effectively, efficiently, successfully, completely, thoroughly
antonyms: ineffectually, inefficiently, unsuccessfully, partially
词性: 副词
