isodynamic:/ˌaɪsəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/
adjective
等力的；等磁力的
Having equal force or intensity. Specifically relating to muscles or magnetic fields that exert equal force.
例句：The isodynamic lines on the map showed areas of equal magnetic intensity. 地图上的等磁力线显示了磁场强度相等的区域。

例句：Isodynamic exercises are designed to strengthen muscles against a constant resistance. 等动练习旨在增强肌肉对抗恒定阻力的能力。
近义词：isometric, isotonic
反义词：anisodynamic (rarely used)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From iso- (Greek: "equal") + dynamic (Greek: "power, force").
