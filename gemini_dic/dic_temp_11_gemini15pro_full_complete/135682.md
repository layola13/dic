nonprescriber:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈskraɪbər/ | n. | 非处方药师；非处方者 |  A healthcare professional who is not authorized to prescribe medication. |  Patients can often consult a nonprescriber for advice on lifestyle changes. 病人通常可以咨询非处方者关于生活方式改变的建议。|  The nonprescriber recommended some over-the-counter pain relief. 非处方者推荐了一些非处方止痛药。 | Synonyms: non-physician,  healthcare advisor | Antonyms: prescriber, physician | noun
