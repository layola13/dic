chenille:/ʃəˈniːl/| n. |雪尼尔纱, 雪尼尔线|A soft, fuzzy yarn or fabric with a pile resembling a caterpillar.|例句：She bought a chenille throw for the sofa. 她买了一条雪尼尔毛毯盖沙发。

例句：The chenille robe felt luxurious against her skin.  这件雪尼尔长袍贴着她皮肤感觉很奢华。|近义词：yarn, thread, fabric|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chenille*, literally "caterpillar," from Latin *canicula*, diminutive of *canis* "dog." The fabric is so named because of its resemblance to a hairy caterpillar.
