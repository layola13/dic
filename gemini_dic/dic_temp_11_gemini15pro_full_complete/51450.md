dethrone:/diˈθroʊn/| vt.| 废黜；罢免；取代|To remove a king or queen from power; to remove someone from a position of authority or dominance.|The rebels dethroned the king and established a republic. 叛军废黜了国王并建立了共和国。

The new smartphone has dethroned the previous market leader.  这款新的智能手机取代了之前的市场领导者。|近义词：oust, depose, unseat, displace, supersede|反义词：crown, enthrone, install|词性: 动词
