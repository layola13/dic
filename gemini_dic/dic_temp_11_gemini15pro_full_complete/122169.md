mistune: /ˌmɪsˈtjuːn/ /ˌmɪsˈtuːn/ | n. | 失调；走调；不和谐 | A state of being out of tune, inharmonious, or discordant. | The piano was slightly mistuned, giving the piece a peculiar sound. 钢琴稍微有点走调，使得这首曲子听起来有些怪异。 |  The band's performance was marred by a mistune in the violins. 乐队的演奏因为小提琴的走调而大打折扣。|近义词：discord, dissonance, cacophony | 反义词：harmony, tune, concord | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From mis- (meaning "badly, wrongly") + tune.  The prefix "mis-" is of Old English origin, while "tune" comes from Old French "tun," ultimately from Latin "tonus."
