koalas:/kəʊˈɑːləz/ | n. |考拉，树袋熊 |A furry, arboreal Australian marsupial (Phascolarctos cinereus) that has thick grey fur and feeds on eucalyptus leaves. | Koalas are native to Australia. 考拉原产于澳大利亚。|  Koalas sleep up to 20 hours a day. 考拉每天最多睡20个小时。| synonyms: koala bears | antonyms: N/A | noun
