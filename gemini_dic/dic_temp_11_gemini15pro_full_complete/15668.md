bakeware:/ˈbeɪkˌweər/ | n. |烘焙用具；烤盘烤模|Dishes and pans used for baking. |She has a whole cupboard full of bakeware. 她有一整个橱柜的烘焙用具。| I need to buy some new bakeware for the holiday baking. 我需要为节日烘焙买一些新的烘焙用具。|近义词：baking dishes, baking pans, ovenware |反义词: None |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bake + ware.  "Bake" comes from Old English "bacan" meaning "to dry by heat." "Ware" comes from Old English "waru" meaning "articles of merchandise, goods."  Thus, bakeware literally means goods for baking.
