create:/kriˈeɪt/ /kriˈeɪt/| vt.|创造，创作；引起，造成|To bring into existence; to produce through imaginative skill.|例句：The artist created a beautiful sculpture. 艺术家创作了一座美丽的雕塑。

例句：The new law created a lot of confusion. 新法律造成了很多混乱。

近义词：make, produce, generate, originate, invent, design, form, compose, establish, build, construct, fabricate, fashion

反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin, eliminate, abolish

词性：动词
