ettos:/ˈɛtoʊz/
noun
指小东西的复数形式，通常用于意大利语
plural of -etto, a diminutive suffix in Italian, meaning "small"
These tiny, colorful houses are known as "casette," or little houses, in Italian, but we might playfully call them "ettos" in English. 这些五颜六色的小房子在意大利语中被称为“casette”，意思是小房子，但在英语中我们可能会开玩笑地称它们为“ettos”。
近义词： miniatures, tiny versions
反义词: large versions, magnifications
词性: noun
