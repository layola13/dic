antioxygenation:/ˌæntiˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 抗氧化，抗氧化作用 | The process of preventing or slowing down oxidation, especially in the body or in food. |  Antioxygenation is important in preserving food quality. 抗氧化对于保持食物品质很重要。

Antioxygenation processes are crucial for preventing cell damage. 抗氧化过程对于防止细胞损伤至关重要。 | 近义词: antioxidation | 反义词: oxidation | 词性: noun
