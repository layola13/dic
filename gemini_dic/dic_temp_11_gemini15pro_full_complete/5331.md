alloxantin:/ˌælɒˈzænθɪn/ | n. | 别嘌呤醇 | A purine derivative, C8H6N4O8, related to uric acid, formed by the oxidation of uric acid or hypoxanthine.  It is used in biological research and as a reagent. | Alloxantin can inhibit xanthine oxidase, the enzyme responsible for producing uric acid. 别嘌呤醇可以抑制黄嘌呤氧化酶，这种酶负责产生尿酸。| Alloxantin plays a role in certain biological pathways. 别嘌呤醇在某些生物途径中起作用。 | Synonyms: none (highly specialized term) | Antonyms: none (highly specialized term) | noun
