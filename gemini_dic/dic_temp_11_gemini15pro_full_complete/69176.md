fayalite:/ˈfeɪəˌlaɪt/| n. |铁橄榄石|An olive-green to brownish-black mineral of the olivine group, consisting of iron silicate (Fe2SiO4). It is found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.|Fayalite is a common mineral in some basalts. 铁橄榄石在某些玄武岩中是一种常见的矿物。|Fayalite alterations can provide valuable information about geological processes. 铁橄榄石蚀变可以提供有关地质过程的宝贵信息。|近义词：iron silicate|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Fayal Island (Faial Island) in the Azores, where it was first discovered, + -ite.
