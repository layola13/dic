laurinitis:/ˌlɔːrɪˈnaɪtɪs/
n.
月桂酸炎，蹄叶炎
Inflammation of the laminae in the hoof of a horse or other ungulate.
The vet diagnosed the horse with laurinitis.  兽医诊断这匹马患有蹄叶炎。
Laminitis can be a serious and debilitating condition for horses. 蹄叶炎对马来说是一种严重且使人衰弱的疾病。
近义词:founder (especially in severe cases)
反义词:None
词性:noun
