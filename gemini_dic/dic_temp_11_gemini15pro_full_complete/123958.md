morinda:/məˈrɪndə/| n.|海巴戟天属; 巴戟天|A genus of flowering plants in the coffee family, Rubiaceae, native to tropical regions of the world.  Many species, such as *Morinda citrifolia* (noni), are used in traditional medicine.|The morinda plant is known for its medicinal properties. 海巴戟天植物以其药用价值而闻名。|Synonym: Indian mulberry (for *Morinda citrifolia*)|Antonym: N/A|noun

morinda:/məˈrɪndə/| n.|诺丽果; 巴戟天果实|The fruit of the morinda plant, especially *Morinda citrifolia*.|Noni juice is made from the morinda fruit. 诺丽果汁是用海巴戟天果实制成的。|Synonym: noni, cheese fruit (for *Morinda citrifolia*)|Antonym: N/A|noun
