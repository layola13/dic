autoclaving:/ɔːtəˈkleɪvɪŋ/
n.|高压灭菌，蒸汽灭菌|The process of sterilizing equipment and supplies by subjecting them to high pressure saturated steam at 121 °C (249 °F) for around 15–20 minutes depending on the size and contents of the load being sterilized.|The lab technician was autoclaving the surgical instruments. 实验室技术员正在对外科器械进行高压灭菌。|sterilization, disinfection|contamination|noun
