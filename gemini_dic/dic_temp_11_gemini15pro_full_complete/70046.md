fetter:/ˈfɛtər/ (US), /ˈfɛtə/ (UK)| n. v.|束缚，脚镣；限制|A chain or shackle placed on the feet of a prisoner or animal to prevent escape; something that confines or restrains.|The prisoner was in fetters. 囚犯戴着脚镣。|Synonyms: shackle, chain, restrain, confine, restrict|Antonyms: liberate, free, release|Noun
