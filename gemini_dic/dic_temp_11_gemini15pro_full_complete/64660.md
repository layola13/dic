escalate:/ˈɛskəleɪt/ (US) /ˈɛskəleɪt/ (UK)| v. | 使逐步升级; 扩大; 逐步增高; 加剧 | To increase or intensify something, especially gradually or step by step.  To become or cause to become more intense or serious. | The conflict escalated into a full-blown war. 冲突逐步升级为全面战争。


The cost of housing continues to escalate. 住房成本持续上涨。


|近义词：intensify, increase, heighten, aggravate, amplify, worsen, boost, raise, step up, mount, accelerate|反义词：de-escalate, decrease, diminish, reduce, lessen, mitigate, alleviate|词性:动词
