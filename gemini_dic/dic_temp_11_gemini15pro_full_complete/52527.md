difficile:/dɪˈfɪsɪl, -ˈfiːsɪl/| adj.|困难的，艰难的；(指人)难相处的，不易取悦的|Difficult means needing much effort or skill to accomplish, deal with, or understand.  It can also describe someone who is not easily pleased or satisfied.|例句：The hike was difficult due to the steep terrain. 这次徒步旅行由于地形陡峭而很困难。|例句：He's a difficult person to work with, always complaining. 他是一个很难共事的人，总是抱怨。|近义词：hard, arduous, challenging, demanding, awkward, troublesome|反义词：easy, simple, straightforward, agreeable, pleasant|词性:形容词

词源:
From French difficile, from Latin difficilis, from dis- "not" + facilis "easy."
