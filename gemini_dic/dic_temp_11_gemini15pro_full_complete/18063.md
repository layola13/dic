beguilements:/bɪˈɡaɪlmənts/| n. |诱惑；魅力；哄骗|The act of beguiling; charm or attraction that misleads or deludes. |Her beguilements were legendary, captivating men with a single glance. 她有着传奇般的魅力，只需一眼就能迷住男人。|  The salesman's beguilements led us to buy a car we couldn't afford.  推销员的花言巧语让我们买了一辆我们负担不起的车。 |近义词：allure, charms, enchantments, enticements, temptations|反义词: repulsion, aversion|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *begilement, from begilen (“to beguile”), equivalent to beguile +‎ -ment.  Beguile itself comes from the Old French beguiler, from the prefix be- + guiler (“to deceive”). Ultimately from Frankish *wigilan, from Proto-Germanic *wīgalōną (“to trick, conjure”).
