fiat:/ˈfiːæt/ (US) /ˈfaɪæt/ (UK)| n.|命令，法令，谕令；(尤指货币的)法定地位|An authoritative decree, sanction, or order. A legally fixed value, especially of a currency.|The CEO issued a fiat that all employees must attend the meeting. 首席执行官发布命令，所有员工都必须参加会议。|The currency derives its value from government fiat. 这种货币的价值来源于政府法令。|synonym: decree, edict, order, command|antonym: request, plea, suggestion|noun
