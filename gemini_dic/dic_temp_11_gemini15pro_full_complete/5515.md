alphabet:/ˈælfəbɛt/ | n. |字母表|A set of letters or symbols in a fixed order, used to represent the basic sounds of a language.|She's learning the alphabet. 她正在学习字母表。| synonyms: ABCs, letters | antonyms: None | noun

alphabet:/ˈælfəˌbetɪk/ | adj. |按字母顺序的|Arranged in the order of the letters of the alphabet.|The files are stored in alphabetical order. 文件按字母顺序存储。| synonyms: ordered, sequential | antonyms: disordered, random | adjective
