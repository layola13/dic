assentiveness:/əˌsɛntɪvnəs/
n.
同意，赞同; 默许
The quality or state of being assentive; readiness to assent or agree.
His assentiveness to the plan surprised everyone. 他对该计划的赞同令所有人感到惊讶。
Synonyms: agreement, acceptance, concurrence, acquiescence
Antonyms: disagreement, dissent, opposition, refusal
