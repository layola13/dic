mortising:/ˈmɔːrtɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 榫眼加工；开榫 | The process of creating a mortise (a hole or recess) in a piece of wood or other material, usually to receive a tenon (a projecting member) as part of a joint. |  He spent hours mortising the wood for the chair legs. 他花了几个小时为椅腿开榫眼。|  Near synonyms:  grooving, slotting, recessing | Antonyms: joining, assembling | noun


mortise: /ˈmɔːrtɪs/ | v. | 开榫；凿榫眼 | To cut a mortise (a hole or recess) in a piece of wood or other material, usually to receive a tenon. | He carefully mortised the frame to create a strong joint. 他仔细地给框架开榫，以创造一个牢固的连接。 | Near synonyms: groove, slot, recess | Antonyms: join, assemble | verb
