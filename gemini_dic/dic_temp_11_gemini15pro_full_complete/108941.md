Since {leeme} appears to be a playful respelling of "me", and sometimes of "readme", the following examples focus on the standard usages of "me":

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):

He gives me the book. = 他把书给我。
She tells me the truth. = 她告诉我真相。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):

He is giving me the book right now. = 他现在正在把书给我。
She is telling me a story at the moment. = 她此刻正在给我讲故事。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

They have given me a lot of support. = 他们给了我很多支持。
She has told me everything. = 她已经把一切都告诉我了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

He has been helping me all day. = 他一整天都在帮我。
They have been teaching me English for months. = 他们几个月来一直在教我英语。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):

He gave me the book yesterday. = 他昨天把书给了我。
She told me a secret last week. = 她上周告诉我一个秘密。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

He was helping me when you called. = 你打电话时他正在帮我。
She was telling me about her trip when we were interrupted. = 我们被打断时，她正在给我讲她的旅行。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

He had given me the book before I left. = 我离开之前他把书给了我。
She had told me the news before the announcement. =  公告发布前她已经把这个消息告诉了我。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

He had been helping me for hours before I finally finished. = 在我最终完成之前，他已经帮了我好几个小时了。
She had been telling me stories all evening. = 她整个晚上都在给我讲故事。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):

He will give me the book tomorrow. = 他明天会把书给我。
She will tell me the answer next week. = 她下周会告诉我答案。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

He will be helping me this time tomorrow. = 明天这个时候他会在帮我。
She will be telling me the details at the meeting. = 会议上她会告诉我细节。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

He will have given me the book by next week. = 到下周他就会把书给我了。
She will have told me everything by then. = 到那时她会把一切都告诉我的。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

By next year, he will have been helping me for two years. = 到明年，他将已经帮助我两年了。
By the time she graduates, she will have been studying English for six years. = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习英语六年了。



疑问句:

Can you give me that book? = 你能把那本书给我吗？
Did she tell you about me? = 她跟你谈起我了吗？


否定句:

He didn't give me the book. = 他没有把书给我。
She hasn't told me anything. = 她什么都没告诉我。



PHRASE->

Give me a break! = 饶了我吧！/ 让我休息一下！
Tell me about it! = 我完全同意！/可不是嘛！
Wait for me! = 等等我！
Listen to me! = 听我说！
Excuse me. = 对不起。/打扰一下。
