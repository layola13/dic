declensional:/dɪˈklɛnʃənəl/ | adj. | 变格的，屈折的 | Relating to or marked by declension, the variation of the form of a noun, pronoun, or adjective, by which its grammatical case, number, and gender are identified. |  The declensional system in Latin is more complex than in English. 拉丁语中的变格系统比英语中的复杂。 |  inflectional | non-declensional | adjective
