degeneratively:/dɪˈdʒɛnərətɪvli/
adverb
退化地，变性地
in a way that relates to or involves degeneration.
His condition degeneratively worsened over time.  他的病情随着时间推移逐渐恶化。
The disease degeneratively affects the nervous system. 这种疾病会使神经系统退化。
Synonym: progressively, increasingly
Antonym: regeneratively, restoratively
词性: 副词
