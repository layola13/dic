asthenic: /æsˈθɛnɪk/ /æsˈθiːnɪk/
adjective
虚弱的；无力气的；体质虚弱的
Characterized by asthenia; weak; lacking energy and vitality.
The asthenic patient was too weak to sit up in bed.  这位虚弱的病人连坐起来都无力。
Synonyms: weak, feeble, debilitated, frail, infirm
Antonyms: strong, robust, vigorous, healthy
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *asthenēs* (weak), from *a-* (without) + *sthenos* (strength).
