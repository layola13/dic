objectionist:/əbˈdʒɛkʃənɪst/
n.
反对者，异议者
A person who habitually or persistently raises objections.
例句：The objectionist held up the proceedings for hours.  反对者将会议进程拖延了几个小时。

例句：He's known as an objectionist, always finding fault with everything. 他以反对者著称，总是对所有事情吹毛求疵。
synonym:dissenter, opponent, protestor, critic
antonym:supporter, proponent, advocate
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From objection + -ist.  Objection from Latin objectus, past participle of obicere “to throw before, oppose.”
