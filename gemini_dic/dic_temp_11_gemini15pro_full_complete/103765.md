journalish:/ˈdʒɜːrnəlɪʃ/ | adj. | 像日记的；流水账似的；碎碎念的 | Resembling or characteristic of a journal, especially in being informal or personal; often used to describe writing that is overly detailed,琐碎 or self-absorbed. | Her blog posts are quite journalish, full of daily minutiae. 她的博客文章很像日记，充满了日常琐事。| Near Synonyms:  diary-like, diaristic, personal, informal, chatty, rambling | Antonyms: formal, concise, impersonal, objective | Word type: Adjective

Etymology:  Derived from "journal" (from Old French "journal" meaning "daily") + "-ish" (a suffix indicating resemblance or characteristic of).
