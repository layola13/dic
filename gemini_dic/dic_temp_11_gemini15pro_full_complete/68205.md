fair-trades:/ˈfeər treɪdz/| n.|公平交易|Trade in which fair prices are paid to producers in developing countries.|例句：She buys fair-trade coffee. 她购买公平贸易咖啡。|例句：The fair-trade movement has grown in recent years. 公平贸易运动近年来有所发展。|近义词：ethical trade, responsible trade|反义词：free trade, exploitative trade|词性:名词
