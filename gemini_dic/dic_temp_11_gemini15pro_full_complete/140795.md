on all hands:/ɒn ɔːl hændz/
adverb
在各方面；到处；一致地；全体一致地
agreed or admitted by everyone
例句：It is on all hands agreed that this must stop. 大家都同意必须停止这种行为。

例句：The decision was welcomed on all hands.  这个决定受到了各方的欢迎。

近义词：everywhere, universally, unanimously
反义词：nowhere, partially, disputably
词性：副词
