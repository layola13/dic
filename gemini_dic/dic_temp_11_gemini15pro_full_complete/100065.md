interpolated:/ɪnˈtɜːr.pə.leɪt/ | v. |  插入，内插 | to insert (something of a different nature) into something else; to introduce (words) into a text or conversation | He interpolated some remarks while I was speaking. 我讲话时他插了几句。 | synonyms: insert, interject, inject, introduce | antonyms: delete, remove, extract | verb
