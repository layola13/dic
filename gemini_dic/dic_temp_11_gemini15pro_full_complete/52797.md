diligently:/ˈdɪlɪdʒəntli/| adv.|勤奋地，勤勉地|In a way that shows care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties.|She worked diligently on her project, ensuring every detail was perfect. 她勤奋地做她的项目，确保每个细节都完美无缺。|近义词：assiduously, industriously, carefully, earnestly|反义词：lazily, carelessly, negligently|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *diligentli, from Old French diligemment, from Latin dīligenter, from dīligēns (“diligent”).
