drub:/drʌb/| v. | 痛打；彻底击败 | to beat severely; to defeat decisively |  He drubbed his opponent in the chess match. 他在国际象棋比赛中彻底击败了对手。

The team was drubbed 5-0.  该队以0比5惨败。 | 近义词：beat, thrash, trounce, defeat | 反义词: lose, surrender | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *drubben, probably of Scandinavian origin; compare Swedish drubba (“to cudgel, to bang, to drudge”), Norwegian drubba (“to beat, to thump”), Icelandic drybba (“to trouble, to disturb”). More at drab.
