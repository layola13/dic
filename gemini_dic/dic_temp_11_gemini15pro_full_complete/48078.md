declarement: /dɪˈklɛərmənt/
n.
声明;宣布;公告
A formal or explicit statement or announcement.
The government issued a declarement of war. 政府发布了战争声明。
The company made a declarement about its new product. 公司发布了关于其新产品的声明。
synonym: announcement, proclamation, declaration, statement
antonym: denial, retraction, concealment, suppression
