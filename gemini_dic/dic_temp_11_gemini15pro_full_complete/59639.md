ecderon: /ˈɛkdərɒn/ | n. | 外胚层;上皮 | The outermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, from which the epidermis, nervous system, and sensory organs develop. | The ecderon gives rise to the skin and nervous system. 外胚层形成皮肤和神经系统。|  The neural crest cells originate from the ecderon. 神经嵴细胞起源于外胚层。 | synonyms: ectoderm, ectoblast | antonyms: endoderm, mesoderm | noun
