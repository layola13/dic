chelidon:/kɪˈliːdɒn/ (US) /ˈkelɪdɒn/ (UK)| n. |燕子|A swallow (bird).  Specifically, a member of the genus *Chelidon*, known as house martins.|The chelidon returned to its nest under the eaves each spring. 燕子每年春天都会回到屋檐下的巢里。|近义词：swallow, martin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *chelidōn*, from Greek *χελιδών (khelidṓn)*, meaning "swallow."
