atomism:/ˈætəmɪzəm/ | n. |原子论；原子主义|The belief that all matter is composed of tiny indivisible particles called atoms, or, in social or political contexts, the belief that society is made up of individuals pursuing their own self-interest. | Democritus and Leucippus were among the first to develop the theory of atomism. 德谟克利特和留基波是最初发展原子论的学者之一。

|The political philosophy of individualism is a form of atomism. 个人主义的政治哲学是原子论的一种形式。|近义词：reductionism, individualism, particularism|反义词：holism, collectivism|词性：noun
