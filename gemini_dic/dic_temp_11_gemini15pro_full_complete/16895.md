basion:/ˈbæːsiən/ /ˈbeɪsiən/ | n. | 基点 | The midpoint of the anterior margin of the foramen magnum in the base of the skull.  The craniometric point at the middle of the anterior margin of the foramen magnum.| The basion is an important landmark in cephalometry. 基点是头颅测量学中的一个重要标志。|  landmark, reference point |  | noun
Etymology: From New Latin *basiōn-, from Ancient Greek βάσις (basis, base).
