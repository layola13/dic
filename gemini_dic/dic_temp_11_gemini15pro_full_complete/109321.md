lengthener:/ˈleŋθənər/ | n. |延长器；加长物|Something that increases length. |She used a hair lengthener to add extensions to her hair. 她用接发器加长了头发。|Synonyms: extender, elongator |Antonyms: shortener, reducer |noun

lengthen:/ˈleŋθən/ | v. |加长，延长；变长|To make or become longer. |The days lengthen in the summer. 夏天白天变长了。|Synonyms: extend, elongate, protract |Antonyms: shorten, reduce, curtail |verb
