inwith: /ɪnˈwɪð/ | prep. |  参与；加入；赞同；流行；时髦 | Being involved or participating in something; fashionable or popular. |  He's in with the wrong crowd. 他和一群不好的人混在一起。 | 近义词: involved in, participating in, fashionable, trendy | 反义词: out of, excluded from | 词性: 介词


词源: "In" comes from Old English "in." "With" comes from Old English "wið," meaning "against, opposite, toward, near."  The combined meaning evolved over time to express involvement or inclusion.
