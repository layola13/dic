dude:/duːd/| n.|伙计，哥们，老兄|A man; a guy.  Originally, a dandy or fop, especially one from the East Coast or city who vacations on a ranch in the West. Now, a general term of address for a man.|例句：Hey, dude, what's up? 嘿，伙计，怎么了？|近义词：guy, fellow, chap, bloke (British)|反义词: (No direct antonym; perhaps "lady" or "ma'am" in a specific context, but generally not applicable)|noun

注意事项:
词源: From German *Dudenkopp "nitwit, blockhead" (literally "Duden head"), a proper name used as a generic term for a foolish person. Later, it became slang for "a fastidiously dressed Easterner," and eventually evolved to its current general meaning.
