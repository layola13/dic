heralded:/ˈhɛrəldɪd/ | v. | 预示；预告；宣布（...的到来）；（尤指公开地）称赞 |  Meaning: to be a sign that something important, and usually good, is starting to happen, or to make something publicly known, especially by celebrating or praising it | The invention of the internet was heralded as a new era of communication.  互联网的发明被誉为通讯新时代的开始。|Synonyms:  announce, proclaim, publicize, trumpet,  | Antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress | verb

heralding:/ˈhɛrəldɪŋ/ | n. | 预示；预告 |  Meaning: the act of heralding something. | The heralding of a new era in space exploration.  太空探索新时代的预示。|Synonyms:  announcement, proclamation | Antonyms: concealment, suppression | noun
