cranny:/ˈkræni/| n.|裂缝，缝隙|A small, narrow opening or crevice, especially in a rock or wall.|例句：The mouse disappeared into a cranny in the wall.  老鼠消失在墙上的一个裂缝里。

例句：She tucked the letter into a cranny in the rock. 她把信塞进岩石的缝隙里。

近义词：crevice, fissure, crack, chink, gap|反义词:closure, seal|词性:noun

词源：From Middle English *cranye, *crani, from Old North French *cran, *crain (“notch, indentation”), from a Vulgar Latin *crena (“notch”), from Latin crena (“notch, indentation”).  Related to crenellation.
