north-eastwards:/ˌnɔːrθˈiːstwədz/| adv.|向东北方向|Moving or directed towards the north-east.|The ship sailed north-eastwards. 船向东北方向航行。|近义词：north-easterly, northeastward|反义词:south-westwards|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From north-east + -wards.  "North-east" itself comes from Old English "northēast," combining "north" and "east." The suffix "-wards" indicates direction.
