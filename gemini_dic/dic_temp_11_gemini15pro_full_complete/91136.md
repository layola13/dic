hybridizable:/ˌhaɪbrɪˈdaɪzəbl/
adjective
可杂交的
Capable of being hybridized; able to form a hybrid.
The two species are hybridizable, producing offspring that share characteristics of both parents. 这两个物种可以杂交，产生的后代具有双亲的特征。

Plants from different but closely related species are sometimes hybridizable. 来自不同但密切相关的物种的植物有时可以杂交。


近义词:crossable, interbreedable
反义词:non-hybridizable


词性:adjective
词源:From hybridize + -able. Hybridize comes from hybrid + -ize. Hybrid ultimately derives from the Latin hibrida, meaning "offspring of a tame sow and a wild boar."
