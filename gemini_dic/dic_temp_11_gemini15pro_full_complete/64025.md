epitasis:/ɪˈpɪtəsɪs/| n. |加剧，恶化，（戏剧、小说等的）高潮|The intensification of a disease or other abnormal condition. In literature, particularly in drama, the epitasis refers to the part of the play where the action develops and the plot thickens, leading towards the climax. |The doctor was concerned about the epitasis of his patient's symptoms.  医生很担心病人症状的加剧。

The epitasis of the play was filled with suspense. 这部戏的高潮部分充满了悬念。|近义词：aggravation, intensification, climax, culmination|反义词:amelioration, improvement, remission|词性:noun
