abp:/eɪ biː piː/ | n. | arterial blood pressure; Android Binding Project | 动脉血压；安卓绑定项目 |  Arterial blood pressure (ABP) is the pressure exerted by circulating blood upon the walls of arteries.  The Android Binding Project (ABP) is a support library that allows you to bind UI components in your layouts to data sources in your app using a declarative format rather than programmatically. | The doctor monitored the patient's ABP during surgery. 医生在手术期间监测病人的动脉血压。 | The ABP simplifies data binding in Android development.  安卓绑定项目简化了安卓开发中的数据绑定。 | synonyms: (for arterial blood pressure) blood pressure, BP; (for Android Binding Project) data binding library | antonyms: (for arterial blood pressure) N/A; (for Android Binding Project) N/A | noun
