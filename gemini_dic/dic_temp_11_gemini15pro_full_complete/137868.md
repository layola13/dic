nonvirtuously: /ˌnɒnˈvɜːtʃuəsli/
| adv. | 不道德地，邪恶地 | In a way that lacks virtue; wickedly or sinfully. | He acted nonvirtuously, betraying his friends for personal gain. 他行为不端，为了个人利益背叛了朋友。 |  He behaved nonvirtuously, choosing to cheat rather than study. 他表现得不道德，选择作弊而不是学习。 | 近义词: immorally, wickedly, sinfully, dishonorably | 反义词: virtuously, morally, righteously | 词性: 副词

词源:
From non- (not) + virtuously (in a virtuous manner).  "Virtuous" comes from Late Middle English: from Old French vertuous, from Latin virtuosus ‘full of virtue’, from virtus ‘manliness, worth’.
