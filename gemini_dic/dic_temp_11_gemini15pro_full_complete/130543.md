nonacidity:/ˌnɒnəˈsɪdəti/
noun
无酸性；缺乏酸性
The nonacidity of the soil is beneficial for certain plants.
土壤的无酸性对某些植物有益。
nonacidity: The absence or lack of acidity.
This water's nonacidity makes it safe for aquatic life.  这种水的无酸性使其对水生生物安全。
Near synonyms: alkalinity, basicity
Antonym: acidity
