ineluctably:/ˌɪnɪˈlʌktəbli/
adverb
不可避免地
in a way that is impossible to avoid
例句：The events of that day led ineluctably to war. 那一天的事件不可避免地导致了战争。

例句：He felt himself drawn ineluctably to her. 他感到自己不可避免地被她吸引。

近义词：inevitably, unavoidably, inescapably
反义词：avoidably, preventably
词性：副词

词源：From Late Latin ineluctabilis, from in- ‘not’ + eluctari ‘struggle out of’.
