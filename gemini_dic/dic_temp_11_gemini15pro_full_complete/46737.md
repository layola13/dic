dandle:/ˈdændl/| v.| 轻轻地摇晃，逗弄 (尤指小孩) | to move a baby or young child up and down gently in your arms or on your knee |  She dandled the baby on her knee. 她把婴儿放在膝上轻轻地摇晃。| synonyms: bounce, rock, sway | antonyms: drop, ignore | verb

dandle:/ˈdændl/| v.| 奉承，迎合 | to treat someone in a way that is designed to make them feel important and happy |  He dandled his investors with promises of quick profits.  他以快速获利的承诺来奉承他的投资者。| synonyms: flatter, cajole, humor | antonyms: criticize, rebuke | verb
