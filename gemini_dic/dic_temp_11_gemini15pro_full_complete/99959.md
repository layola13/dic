interoperably:/ˌɪntərˈɒpərəbli/ | adv. | 可互操作地 | In a way that allows different systems or components to work together. |  The systems were designed to communicate interoperably.  这些系统被设计成可互操作地进行通信。 |  compatibly, interchangeably | non-interoperably | adverb

interoperable:/ˌɪntərˈɒpərəbəl/ | adj. | 可互操作的 |  Able to exchange information and operate with other systems or equipment without special effort.  |  The software is interoperable with other platforms.  该软件可与其他平台互操作。 | compatible, interchangeable | incompatible, proprietary | adjective
