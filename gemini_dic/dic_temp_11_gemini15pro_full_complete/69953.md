fester:/ˈfestər/| v. n.| 溃烂，恶化； festering adj. 溃烂的，恶化的|To become worse or more infected, especially as a result of suppressed anger or resentment.  A sore that festers.|The wound festered for days.  伤口溃烂了好几天。|近义词：rankle, suppurate, worsen, decay|反义词:heal, improve, mend|词性:动词, 名词

fester:/ˈfestər/| n.|溃疡，脓疮|A festering sore or wound.|The doctor lanced the fester.|近义词：sore, ulcer, abscess|反义词:healthy tissue|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *festerer "to fester," from Vulgar Latin *festare "to stink, decay," from Latin festuca "stalk, straw."  The original sense was probably "to rot like straw."
