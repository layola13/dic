glee:/ɡliː/
n.
高兴，欢欣，喜悦
A feeling of great happiness, especially from satisfaction or triumph.
She couldn't hide her glee at the news. 她无法掩饰听到这个消息的喜悦。
joy, delight, elation
sorrow, sadness
noun
