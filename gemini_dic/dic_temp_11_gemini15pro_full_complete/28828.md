carp-entered:/ˈkɑːrpˌɛntərd/
adjective
木工制作的，木结构的
constructed by a carpenter; made of wood; having a wooden framework.
The carp-entered cabin was sturdy and rustic. 小木屋坚固而质朴。
Near synonyms: wooden, timber-framed, timber-built
Antonyms: concrete, steel, brick
词性: 形容词
