interdeceived:/ˌɪntərdiˈsiːvd/
verb
互相欺骗
To deceive each other; to be mutually deceived.
The two spies interdeceived each other, each believing the other to be a double agent. 两个间谍互相欺骗，每个人都认为对方是双重间谍。
The warring factions were interdeceived about the strength of each other's forces. 交战各派互相欺骗了对方的实力。
Synonyms: mislead each other, dupe each other, deceive one another
Antonyms: be honest with each other, be truthful with one another
词性: verb
