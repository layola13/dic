caulescent:/kɔːˈlɛsənt/
adjective
有茎的；茎状的
Having a well-developed stem above ground; possessing a stem or stems.
The sunflower is a caulescent plant, unlike mosses which are acaulescent.  向日葵是一种有茎植物，不像苔藓是无茎的。
The botanist identified the new species as caulescent due to its distinct stem structure.  植物学家根据其独特的茎结构将新物种鉴定为有茎植物。
近义词: stemmed, stalked
反义词: acaulescent, stemless
词性: adjective
