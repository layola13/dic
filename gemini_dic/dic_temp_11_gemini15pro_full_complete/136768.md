nonsequential:/ˌnɒnˌsɪˈkwɛnʃəl/| adj.|非顺序的，不连续的|Not following in a regular numerical or logical order; random.|例句：The chapters of the book are nonsequential and can be read in any order. 这本书的章节是非顺序的，可以按任何顺序阅读。

例句：The data received was nonsequential, making analysis difficult.  接收到的数据是非顺序的，使得分析变得困难。|近义词：random, disordered, unsystematic, illogical, chaotic|反义词：sequential, ordered, systematic, logical, consecutive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + sequential.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "sequential" comes from the Latin word "sequi," meaning "to follow."
