decrepitness:/dɪˈkrɛpɪtnəs/| n. |衰老；破旧；老朽|The state of being decrepit; dilapidated condition.  The decrepitness of the old house was evident in its crumbling walls and sagging roof.|The decrepitness of the building made it unsafe.|近义词：feebleness, frailty, infirmity, debility, decay, dilapidation|反义词：strength, robustness, soundness, vigor, sturdiness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *decrepitus, past participle of decrepitare (“to grow weak”).
