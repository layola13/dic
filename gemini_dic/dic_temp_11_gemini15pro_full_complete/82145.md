groomed:/ɡruːmd/ /ɡrʊmd/| adj.|修饰整齐的，打扮得体的；受过训练的，准备好的|Having one's hair or appearance neat and carefully arranged; trained or prepared for a particular purpose or activity.|例句：The hotel caters to a well-groomed clientele.  这家酒店面向衣着光鲜的顾客。

例句：He seemed perfectly groomed for a career in politics. 他似乎为从政做好了完美的准备。

近义词：tidy, neat, well-kept, polished, primed, prepared|反义词: unkempt, messy, disheveled, untidy|词性:形容词

词源：From Old English *grūma "man," related to Old Frisian *grōma "boy, male child," from Proto-Germanic *grōmaz. Original sense was "boy, servant;" later "servant who tends horses," then generalized to "one who tends to another's appearance."
