lambastes:/læmˈbeɪsts/
verb
严厉批评；痛斥
criticize (someone or something) harshly.
The coach lambasted the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。
The critic lambasted the new play as uninspired and derivative.  评论家严厉批评这出新戏缺乏灵感，而且是抄袭之作。
castigate, censure, excoriate
praise, commend
v.
