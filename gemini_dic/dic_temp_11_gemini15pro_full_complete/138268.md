notable:/ˈnəʊtəbl/ (US) /ˈnoʊtəbl/| adj. n.|显著的，值得注意的；名人，显要人物|Deserving of attention or distinction; a famous or important person.|例句：There has been a notable improvement in his work. 他的工作有了显著的改进。|近义词：important, significant, distinguished, prominent, remarkable, outstanding|反义词：insignificant, unimportant, unremarkable, ordinary|词性:形容词

notable:/ˈnəʊtəbl/ (US) /ˈnoʊtəbl/| n.|名人，显要人物|A famous or important person.|例句：Many notables attended the gala. 许多名人都出席了这场盛会。|近义词：celebrity, dignitary, luminary|反义词：nobody, unknown|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French notable, from Latin notabilis ‘noteworthy’, from notare ‘to note’.
