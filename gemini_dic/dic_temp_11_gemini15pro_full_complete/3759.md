agistor:/ˈædʒɪstər/ | n. | 代牧人；饲养家畜者（尤指为他人代养）| A person who takes in and pastures cattle or other livestock for a fee. | The farmer acted as an agistor for his neighbor's sheep during the summer months. 这位农民在夏季几个月里充当邻居羊群的代牧人。|  Example: The agistor charged a reasonable fee for his services.  代牧人对其服务收取合理的费用。 |Synonyms: grazier, herdsman, pastor, stockman | Antonyms: owner | noun
