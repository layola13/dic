cycloid:/ˈsaɪklɔɪd/| n. |摆线，旋轮线|A curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle as it rolls along a straight line.|The path of the valve tip in a rotary engine is a cycloid.|近义词: roulette, trochoid|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *cyclois, from Ancient Greek κυκλοειδής (kukloeidḗs, “circle-shaped”), from κύκλος (kúklos, “circle, wheel”) + -ειδής (-eidḗs, “-shaped”).
