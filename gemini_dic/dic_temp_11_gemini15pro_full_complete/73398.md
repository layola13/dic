foreigner:/ˈfɒrənə(r) (UK), ˈfɔːrənər (US)| n.|外国人，外地人|A person born in or coming from a country other than one's own.|She felt like a foreigner in her own town after being away for so long.  在她离家这么久之后，她感觉在自己家乡像个外国人。|Synonyms: alien, outsider, non-native, immigrant|Antonyms: native, local, citizen|Noun
