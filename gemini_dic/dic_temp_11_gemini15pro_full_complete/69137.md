favosite:/ˈfeɪvəsaɪt/| n. |蜂巢珊瑚；蜂窝珊瑚石|A massive, colonial, stony coral having polygonal corallites closely packed together in a honeycomb pattern.  A fossil of such a coral.|The museum displayed a large favosite fossil.  博物馆展出了一块大型蜂巢珊瑚化石。|近义词:coral, honeycomb coral|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fāvosus* ("honeycombed"), from *fāvus* ("honeycomb").
