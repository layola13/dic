cinctured:/ˈsɪŋktʃərd/
adjective
束带的，环绕的
Encircled or girdled with a belt or band.
The knight was cinctured with a sword belt. 骑士的腰间束着剑带。
The mountain was cinctured with clouds. 山腰云雾缭绕。
Near synonyms: belted, girdled, bound, encompassed
Near antonyms: unbound, loose, free
word type: adjective
