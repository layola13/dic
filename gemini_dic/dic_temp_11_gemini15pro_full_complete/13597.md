attenuator:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪtər/| n. |衰减器，减弱器|A device that reduces the strength of a signal, power, or current.|The engineer used an attenuator to reduce the signal strength before it reached the receiver. 工程师在信号到达接收器之前使用衰减器来降低信号强度。|近义词：reducer, damper, suppressor|反义词: amplifier, booster|noun


attenuate:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪt/| v. |衰减，减弱，降低|To reduce the force, effect, or value of something.|The thick walls attenuated the sound of the traffic. 厚厚的墙壁减弱了交通噪音。|近义词：reduce, diminish, lessen, weaken|反义词: amplify, boost, strengthen, intensify|verb
