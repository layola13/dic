antihistamine:/ˌæntiˈhɪstəmiːn/ /ˌæntiˈhɪstəmɪn/ | n. |抗组胺药|A drug or other compound that inhibits the physiological effects of histamine, used especially in the treatment of allergies.|She took an antihistamine for her hay fever. 她服用了抗组胺药来治疗花粉热。|Synonyms: allergy medication, anti-allergic|Antonyms: histamine agonist|noun


antihistamine:/ˌæntiˈhɪstəmiːn/ /ˌæntiˈhɪstəmɪn/ | adj. |抗组胺的|Having the effect of inhibiting the physiological effects of histamine.| Antihistamine eye drops can help relieve itchy eyes. 抗组胺眼药水可以帮助缓解眼睛发痒。|Synonyms: anti-allergic|Antonyms: histamine promoting|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + histamine.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to". Histamine is a nitrogenous compound involved in local immune responses, as well as regulating physiological function in the gut and acting as a neurotransmitter for the brain, spinal cord, and uterus.
