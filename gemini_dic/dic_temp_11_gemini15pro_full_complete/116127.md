marxisms:/ˈmɑːrksɪzəmz/| n. |马克思主义（不同流派）|Various interpretations and applications of Marx's theories, often differing significantly from each other.|例句：The course explored different marxisms and their impact on 20th-century history. 这门课程探讨了不同的马克思主义及其对20世纪历史的影响。

例句：Academic debates about the various marxisms can be quite complex.  关于各种马克思主义的学术辩论可能相当复杂。|近义词：Marxist theories, communist ideologies|反义词:capitalism, liberalism|词性:noun

Etymology: From Karl Marx (1818-1883), German philosopher, economist, and sociologist, + -ism (suffix forming nouns denoting doctrines, beliefs, or practices).
