bricklike:/ˈbrɪklaɪk/| adj.|像砖一样的，坚硬的|Resembling a brick in shape, hardness, or color.  |The building had a bricklike facade. 这栋建筑有一个像砖一样的正面。|近义词：brick-shaped, hard, solid, firm|反义词:soft, yielding, flexible|词性:adjective
Etymology:  "brick" + "-like"  "brick" comes from Middle Dutch "bricke" and ultimately from Frankish "*brika". "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
