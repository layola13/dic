diaheliotropism:/ˌdaɪəˌhiːliˈɒtrəpɪzəm/| n. |向日性|The directional growth of a plant, especially its leaves, in which the plant follows the sun across the sky.|The sunflowers exhibited strong diaheliotropism, their heads tracking the sun's movement throughout the day. 向日葵表现出强烈的向日性，它们的花盘整天都随着太阳的移动而转动。|近义词:heliotropism|反义词:apheliotropism|词性:noun

Etymology:
From dia- (through, across) + helio- (sun) + -tropism (turning, movement).
