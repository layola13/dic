emissaryship:/ˈɛmɪsɛriʃɪp/
n.
特使的职位或身份; 使命
The position or role of an emissary; a mission.
He was given an emissaryship to negotiate a peace treaty. 他被任命为特使去谈判和平条约。
近义词:mission, delegation, assignment
反义词:None.
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From emissary + -ship.  "Emissary" comes from the Late Latin *emissarius, from Latin emissus, past participle of emittere "to send forth."  The suffix "-ship" forms abstract nouns denoting state, condition, or quality.
