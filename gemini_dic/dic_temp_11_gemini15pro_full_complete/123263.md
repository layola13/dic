monodimensional:/ˌmɒnəʊdaɪˈmɛnʃənəl/ /ˌmɑnədaɪˈmɛnʃənəl/| adj.|一维的，单向度的|Having only one dimension or aspect; relating to a single dimension.|The artist's work was criticized for being too monodimensional, lacking depth and complexity. 艺术家的作品因过于单一、缺乏深度和复杂性而受到批评。|Example: The problem is not monodimensional; it has social, economic, and political aspects.  这个问题不是单方面的，它有社会、经济和政治方面。|Synonyms: unidimensional, one-dimensional, linear, simple|Antonyms: multidimensional, complex, multifaceted|Adjective
