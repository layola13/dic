jank:/dʒæŋk/| n. adj.|破烂的，劣质的；垃圾软件；故障；|Something that is of extremely poor or unreliable quality, especially in the context of software or technology. jank:/dʒæŋk/| v.|使出现故障，使运行缓慢|To cause something to malfunction or perform poorly, especially in the context of software or technology.|The new update completely janked my computer.  新的更新完全搞坏了我的电脑。|近义词：break, glitch, malfunction|反义词:fix, repair, improve|词性:动词
