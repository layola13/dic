lauds:/lɔːdz/ | n. | 赞美; 赞歌; 称赞 |  An expression of praise or approval.  It often refers to hymns or songs of praise, especially in a religious context. | The congregation sang lauds to the glory of God.  会众歌颂上帝的荣耀。 | synonyms: praise, acclaim, commendation, extolment, tribute | antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure | noun
