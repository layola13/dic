charterless: /ˈtʃɑːrtərlɪs/
adjective
无执照的；无特许状的；未经批准的
lacking a charter or license; unauthorized.
The charterless airline was shut down by authorities.  这家无执照的航空公司被当局关闭了。
The group operated a charterless radio station. 这个团体运营着一个未经批准的广播电台。
Synonyms: unlicensed, unauthorized, illegal, illegitimate
Antonyms: licensed, authorized, legal, legitimate
词性: 形容词
