nonchurches:/ˈnɒnˌtʃɜːtʃɪz/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌtʃɜːtʃɪz/ (UK)
n.|非教会;非传统教会场所|Places or organizations that offer spiritual or community experiences outside of traditional church settings.  These can include house churches, online communities, or other gatherings focused on spirituality or shared values.|Many young people are finding community in nonchurches. 许多年轻人正在非教会中找到归属感。|Synonyms: alternative spiritual communities, faith-based communities outside of traditional churches|Antonyms: churches, traditional religious institutions
