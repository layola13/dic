ETYMOLOGY->
"mini-" + "calculator".  "Mini-" is a prefix meaning "small," derived from Latin "minus" (less). "Calculator" comes from the Latin "calculāre" (to calculate, reckon), from "calculus" (pebble, small stone used in reckoning).  Originally, calculations were performed using pebbles or other small objects.
