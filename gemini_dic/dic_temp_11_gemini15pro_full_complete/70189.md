fibrinogen:/faɪˈbrɪnədʒən/ | n. | 纤维蛋白原 | A soluble plasma glycoprotein that is converted by thrombin into fibrin during blood coagulation.  It is essential for normal blood clotting. |  High levels of fibrinogen are a risk factor for heart disease. 纤维蛋白原水平高是心脏病的一个危险因素。|  Fibrinogen is synthesized in the liver. 纤维蛋白原在肝脏中合成。 | synonyms: clotting factor I | antonyms: None | noun
