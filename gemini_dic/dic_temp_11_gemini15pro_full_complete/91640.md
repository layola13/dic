hyperbolicity:/ˌhaɪpərboˈlɪsəti/
n.|夸张法，夸张；双曲性|The quality or state of being hyperbolic; exaggerated representation; the condition of having hyperbolic geometry.|His writing is full of hyperbolicity. 他的写作充满了夸张。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement, amplification|反义词：understatement, minimization|词性:名词
Etymology: Late 16th century: from French hyperbolicité or late Latin hyperbolicitas, from Greek huperbolikos ‘exaggerated’ + -ity.
