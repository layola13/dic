muzzy:/ˈmʌzi/ | adj. | 模糊的，昏沉的 |  (of the mind or senses) confused, unclear, or vague. | The old photograph was muzzy and out of focus.  这张老照片模糊不清。 | 近义词: blurry, fuzzy, hazy, indistinct, vague | 反义词: clear, sharp, distinct | 词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From dialectal *muz(z)y, perhaps ultimately related to Old English *mus "moss" (compare Dutch mos "moss"). The notion could be of something being covered in moss and therefore obscured.
