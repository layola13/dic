chug:/tʃʌɡ/| v. n.|发出嘎嚓声；（使）发出噗噗声；一口气喝干；（车辆）缓慢行驶|To make a short, low, dull, explosive sound, as of an engine exhausting; to move or cause to move slowly and with much effort, especially while making a chugging sound.  To drink something in large gulps without pausing.|The old train chugged along the tracks.  老火车沿着铁轨缓慢行驶。 He chugged his beer and ordered another. 他一口气喝完了啤酒，又点了一杯。|近义词：gulp, guzzle, chug-a-lug, puff, rumble|反义词：sip|词性：动词，名词
