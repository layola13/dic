coats:/kəʊts/ (British) /koʊts/ (American)| n. |外套; (油漆、清漆等的)层; 动物皮毛|An outer garment worn outdoors, typically having sleeves and fastening down the front. Also, a layer of a substance, such as paint, covering a surface, or the fur, hair, or wool of an animal.|例句：She wore several coats to keep warm in the cold weather. 她穿了几件外套在天冷的时候保暖。|近义词：jackets, overcoats, wraps|反义词: linings, undergarments|词性:名词

coats:/kəʊts/ (British) /koʊts/ (American)| v. |涂上，覆盖|To cover something with a layer of a substance.|例句：He coated the cake with icing. 他在蛋糕上涂了一层糖霜。|近义词：cover, layer, spread|反义词: expose, strip, uncover |词性:动词
