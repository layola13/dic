giveaways:/ˈɡɪvəˌweɪz/
n.
赠品；泄露；免费赠送的物品；（比赛的）致胜因素
Something that is given away free, especially for promotional purposes; a thing that betrays or reveals something.
The radio station was doing a giveaways.  这家广播电台正在进行赠品派送。
The biggest giveaway was his nervousness. 他最大的破绽是他的紧张。
synonyms: freebies, premiums, gifts, prizes, promotions, reveals, clues, indicators, tells
antonyms: purchases, secrets, concealments
noun
