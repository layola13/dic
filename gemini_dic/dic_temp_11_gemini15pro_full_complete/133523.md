nonfiction:/ˌnɑːnˈfɪkʃən/ | n. | 非虚构作品；纪实文学 | Prose writing that is based on facts, real events, and real people, such as biography or history. |  I prefer reading nonfiction to fiction. 我更喜欢读非虚构作品而不是小说。 |  She's working on a new piece of nonfiction about the Civil War. 她正在写一部关于内战的新非虚构作品。 | synonyms: factual prose, documentary,  | antonyms: fiction, fantasy, fabrication | noun
