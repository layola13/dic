kinology:/kɪˈnɑːlədʒi/ /kaɪˈnɒlədʒi/
noun
运动学;人体运动学
The study of the principles of mechanics and anatomy in relation to human movement.
Kinesiology is the study of human movement. 人体运动学是对人体运动的研究。
Kinesiology tape is designed to support muscles and joints. 肌内效贴布旨在支撑肌肉和关节。
近义词: biomechanics, kinesiatrics
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: n.


Etymology:
From Greek kīnēsis "movement" + -logy.
