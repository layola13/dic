nonplacental:/ˌnɒnpləˈsɛntəl/| adj.|非胎盘的|Not having or developing a placenta, the vascular organ that unites the fetus to the uterus in most mammals.|例句：The echidna is a nonplacental mammal.  针鼹是一种非胎盘哺乳动物。

例句：Nonplacental animals lay eggs. 非胎盘动物产卵。|近义词：aplacental|反义词: placental|词性:形容词
