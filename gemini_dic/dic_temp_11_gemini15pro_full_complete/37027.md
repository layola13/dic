.com:/ˌdɒtˈkɒm/ (UK) /ˌdɑːtˈkɑːm/ (US)| n. | 网络域名，商业网站 |  Abbreviation of "commercial," used as a top-level domain name for websites related to commercial organizations or activities.  It's now used for a wide variety of websites, not just commercial ones. | Example: Amazon.com is a popular e-commerce website. 亚马逊网站是一个受欢迎的电子商务网站。 | Synonym: website, web address | Antonym: N/A | noun
