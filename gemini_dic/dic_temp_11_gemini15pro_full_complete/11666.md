armlike:/ˈɑːrmlaɪk/
adjective
像手臂的；臂状的
Resembling an arm in shape or function.
The robot had long, armlike appendages that it used to manipulate objects.  这个机器人有长长的、像手臂一样的附属物，用来操纵物体。
The branches of the old oak tree were gnarled and armlike, reaching towards the sky.  这棵老橡树的树枝扭曲如臂，伸向天空。
synonyms: arm-shaped, brachial, appendicular
antonyms: leglike

ETYMOLOGY->
From arm +‎ -like.
