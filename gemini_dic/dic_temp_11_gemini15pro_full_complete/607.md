abraded:/əˈbreɪdɪd/
adjective
磨损的，擦伤的
worn away by friction; scraped or rubbed off
The abraded surface of the rock showed signs of glacial activity.  岩石被磨损的表面显示出冰川活动的迹象。
近义词:scraped, scratched, worn, eroded
反义词:smooth, polished, pristine, intact
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin abradere, from ab- + radere "to scrape."
