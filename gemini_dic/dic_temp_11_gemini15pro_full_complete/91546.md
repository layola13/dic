hyper-elegantly:/ˈhaɪpərˈɛlɪɡəntli/
|adv.|极其优雅地；非常精致地|Combining "hyper-" (meaning excessively) and "elegantly" (in a refined and graceful manner).  It describes something done with an extreme level of elegance and sophistication.|She dressed hyper-elegantly for the gala, shimmering in a designer gown. 她为了晚会盛装打扮，身穿一件闪耀夺目的设计师礼服，显得格外优雅。|The ballroom was decorated hyper-elegantly with crystal chandeliers and fresh flowers.  舞厅用水晶吊灯和鲜花装饰得非常精致。| 近义词：exquisitely, supremely elegantly, very elegantly|反义词:sloppily, clumsily, inelegantly|词性:副词
