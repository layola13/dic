europeanizes:/jʊərəˈpiːənaɪzɪz/
verb
使欧洲化
To cause to adopt or be influenced by European customs, institutions, or ways of life.
The government's policies are europeanizing the country's legal system. 政府的政策正在使国家的法律体系欧洲化。
European contact has europeanized many aspects of indigenous cultures. 与欧洲的接触使土著文化的许多方面欧洲化了。
