effluvium:/ɪˈfluːviəm/ | n. |  散发物，臭气，（尤指疾病后的）脱发 | An unpleasant or harmful substance, especially a gas or smell, that is released into the atmosphere. Also, the shedding of hair, especially in large quantities, as a result of illness or stress. |  The effluvium from the chemical plant made the air thick and acrid. 化工厂排放的散发物使空气变得浓稠而刺鼻。 | synonym: emanation, exhalation, odor, aroma (depending on context), shedding, hair loss | antonym:  (For smell) fragrance, perfume; (For hair loss) hair growth | noun
