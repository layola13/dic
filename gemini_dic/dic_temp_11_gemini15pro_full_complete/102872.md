jauntiness:/ˈdʒɔːntinəs/ |n.|轻松愉快；活泼；潇洒|The jauntiness in her step revealed her good mood. 她轻快的步伐显示出她的好心情。|例句：She walked with a certain jauntiness. 她走起路来带着某种轻松愉快的神情。

Her jauntiness was infectious. 她的活泼劲儿很有感染力。


|近义词：liveliness, sprightliness, cheerfulness, buoyancy|反义词：dullness, listlessness, gloominess|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From jaunty + -ness. Jaunty from French gentil, meaning "noble, elegant."  The meaning shifted over time to "easy and sprightly in manner or bearing."
