multistability:/ˌmʌltiːstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 多稳态 | The ability of a system to exist in multiple stable states. | The multistability of the circuit allows it to store multiple bits of information. 电路的多稳态性使其能够存储多位信息。| synonyms: multimodality, polymorphism | antonyms: monostability, unimodality | noun


multistability:/ˌmʌltiːstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 多稳定性 | The property of a system to have more than one stable equilibrium. |  The researchers are exploring the multistability of the ecosystem. 研究人员正在探索生态系统的多稳定性。| synonyms: multimodality, polymorphism | antonyms: monostability, unimodality | noun
