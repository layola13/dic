leverlike: /ˈliːvərlaɪk/
adjective
杠杆式的；像杠杆的
Resembling a lever; acting like a lever.  Functioning or shaped in a way that is similar to a lever.
The crane's arm moved in a leverlike motion.  起重机的臂以杠杆式运动移动。
The child used a stick as a leverlike tool to pry open the box. 孩子用一根棍子作为杠杆式工具撬开盒子。
近义词: levered,  lever-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite quality, e.g., "unyielding," "inflexible")
词性: adjective
