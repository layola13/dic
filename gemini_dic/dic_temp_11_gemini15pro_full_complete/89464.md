honey-tongued:/ˈhʌniˌtʌŋd/| adj.|甜言蜜语的；花言巧语的|Speaking or writing in an excessively sweet or flattering manner, often insincerely.|He's a honey-tongued salesman who can convince anyone to buy anything. 他是一个巧舌如簧的推销员，能说服任何人买任何东西。|
例句：
She was wary of the honey-tongued stranger. 她对这个花言巧语的陌生人很警惕。
中文：她对这个花言巧语的陌生人很警惕。


He used his honey-tongued charm to get what he wanted. 他用他甜言蜜语的魅力来得到他想要的东西。
中文：他用他甜言蜜语的魅力来得到他想要的东西。


|近义词：smooth-talking, silver-tongued, flattering, glib|反义词：blunt, frank, honest, straightforward|词性：形容词|词源：From honey + tongued, referring to speech as sweet as honey.|
