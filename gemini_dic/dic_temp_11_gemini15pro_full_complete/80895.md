governmentally: /ˌɡʌvərnˈmentəli/
adjective
政府地；以政府方式地
In a governmental manner; by or through a government.
The issue was handled governmentally, with all the proper procedures followed.  这个问题是由政府处理的，所有适当的程序都得到了遵守。
The funds were distributed governmentally to various agencies. 这些资金由政府分配给各个机构。
Synonym: officially, publicly
Antonym: privately, unofficially
词性: adjective
