defect:/ˈdiːfekt/ /dɪˈfekt/| n. |缺点，缺陷，瑕疵|A shortcoming, imperfection, or lack in something.|The product was recalled due to a manufacturing defect.  该产品因制造缺陷被召回。|Synonyms: flaw, imperfection, blemish, fault, bug|Antonyms: perfection, strength, merit|noun

defect:/dɪˈfekt/| vi. |叛逃，投敌|To abandon one's country or cause and join the opposing side.|Several soldiers defected to the enemy.  几名士兵叛逃到敌方。|Synonyms: desert, abandon, forsake, renounce|Antonyms: remain loyal, support, defend|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *defectus*, past participle of *deficere* "to fail, desert, abandon," from *de-* "down, away" + *facere* "to do, make."
