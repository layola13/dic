overcompliances:/ˌoʊvərəmpˈlaɪənsɪz/
n.
过度遵从，过度合规
The act of complying with rules, regulations, or requirements to an excessive or unnecessary degree.
The company's overcompliance with environmental regulations resulted in increased costs.  这家公司对环境法规的过度遵从导致成本增加。
Overcompliances with safety protocols can sometimes hinder efficiency. 对安全协议的过度遵守有时会阻碍效率。
近义词：overregulation, over-regulation, excessive compliance
反义词：noncompliance, undercompliance
词性：名词
