nonvisionary:/ˌnɒnˈvɪʒənəri/ | /ˌnɑːnˈvɪʒəneri/ | adj. | 非有远见的，缺乏远见的 | Lacking foresight or imagination; not visionary. |  The company's nonvisionary leadership led to its eventual downfall.  这家公司缺乏远见的领导导致了它最终的衰败。

The nonvisionary approach to urban planning resulted in traffic congestion and pollution.  缺乏远见的城市规划方法导致了交通拥堵和污染。 | Near Synonyms: unimaginative, uninspired, shortsighted, myopic, unoriginal, conventional, pragmatic | Antonyms: visionary, imaginative, inspired, farsighted, prescient, innovative, original | adjective
