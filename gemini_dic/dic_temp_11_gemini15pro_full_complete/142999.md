outbeams:/ˌaʊtˈbiːmz/
verb
发出更强的光
To surpass in radiance or brilliance; to shine more brightly than.
The new lighthouse outbeamed the old one. 新灯塔的光比旧灯塔更亮。
Synonym: outshine, eclipse, overshadow
Antonym: dim, darken
词性: 动词
