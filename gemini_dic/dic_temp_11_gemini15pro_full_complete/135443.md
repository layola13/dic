nonpersuadable:/ˌnɒnpərˈsweɪdəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnpərˈsweɪdəbl/ | adj. | 不可说服的；固执的 | Not able to be persuaded; stubborn. |  The board members were nonpersuadable and refused to change their minds. 董事会成员固执己见，拒绝改变主意。|  He held a nonpersuadable belief that he was destined for greatness. 他坚信自己注定要成就一番伟业，谁也劝不动他。 | 近义词:intransigent, obstinate, unyielding | 反义词:persuadable, amenable, receptive | 词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non < Indo-European base *ne "not"
persuadable (adjective): from Latin persuadere "to persuade," from per- "thoroughly" + suadere "to advise"
