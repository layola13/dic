licensing:/ˈlaɪs(ə)nsɪŋ/
n.

The act of granting a license.
Software licensing can be complicated.  软件授权可能很复杂。
authorization, permitting
prohibition, forbiddance
noun

license:/ˈlaɪsns/
n.
许可证，执照
A permit from an authority to own or use something, do a particular thing, or carry on a trade.
Do you have a driver's license? 你有驾驶执照吗？
permit, authorization
prohibition, ban
noun

license:/ˈlaɪsns/
vt.

forbid, prohibit
