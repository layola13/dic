mycothrix:/ˌmaɪkəˈθrɪks/
noun
霉菌丝；毛霉菌丝
A genus of fungi belonging to the family Onygenaceae, characterized by its fine, hair-like mycelium.
Mycothrix infections are rare but can occur in immunocompromised individuals.  霉菌丝感染罕见，但可能发生在免疫功能低下的人身上。
The researchers isolated Mycothrix from a soil sample. 研究人员从土壤样本中分离出霉菌丝。
synonyms:  hypha, mycelium (in a broader sense)
antonyms:  None (lacks a direct opposite)
