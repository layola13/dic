bromatologist:/ˌbroʊməˈtɑːlədʒɪst/ /ˌbrɒməˈtɒlədʒɪst/
n.
食品学家；食品化学家
A scientist who studies the chemistry and composition of food.
The bromatologist analyzed the nutritional content of the new cereal.  食品学家分析了新谷物的营养成分。

The bromatologist is researching the effects of food additives. 食品学家正在研究食品添加剂的影响。

近义词:food scientist, nutritionist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *brōmat-, *brōma ("food") + -ology ("study of") + -ist ("one who studies").
