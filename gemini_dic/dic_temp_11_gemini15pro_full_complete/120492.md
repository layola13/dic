minua:/ˈminuə/ |pron.|我，我（宾格）|(Finnish) The accusative and partitive singular forms of the pronoun 'minä' (I, me).  It functions as the direct object of a verb or indicates a partial object.|He saw minua. (He saw me.)|Near synonyms: me (English), mich (German), mig (Danish) |Antonyms:  N/A (Pronouns don't typically have antonyms)|Pronoun
