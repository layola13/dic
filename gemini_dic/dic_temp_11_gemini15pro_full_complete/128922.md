nincompoopish:/ˌnɪŋkəmˈpuːpɪʃ/
|adj.|
|愚蠢的；傻的|
|Resembling or characteristic of a nincompoop; foolish or stupid.|
|His nincompoopish behavior embarrassed everyone at the party.| 他愚蠢的行为让派对上的每个人都感到尴尬。
|Her nincompoopish idea of hiding the evidence in plain sight was quickly discovered.| 她把证据藏在显眼的地方，这个愚蠢的想法很快就被发现了。
|
|近义词:silly, foolish, stupid, idiotic, inane, absurd|
|反义词:intelligent, clever, wise, sensible, astute|
|词性:adjective|
