methadone:/ˈmɛθəˌdoʊn/ (US), /ˈmɛθədən/ (UK)| n. |美沙酮|A synthetic opioid drug used as a pain reliever and as a treatment for heroin or other opioid dependence.|The doctor prescribed methadone to help him overcome his heroin addiction. 医生给他开了美沙酮来帮助他克服海洛因成瘾。|Synonyms: dolophine, amidone|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "naloxone" as an opioid antagonist used to reverse opioid overdose)|noun
