extra-illustrator:/ˌekstrəˈɪləstreɪtər/
n.
插图补充者，图集扩展者
A person who adds extra illustrations, often prints or engravings, to a book, thereby expanding and enriching its visual content.
He was a passionate extra-illustrator, adding hundreds of prints to his copy of Dickens's "Bleak House." 他是一位充满激情的插图补充者，在他的《荒凉山庄》副本中添加了数百幅版画。
collector, enthusiast
detractor, minimalist
noun
