dancette:/dænˈsɛt/ | n. | 小舞蹈，舞步 | A short or simple dance. | She performed a graceful dancette. 她表演了一段优雅的小舞蹈。 |  The children learned a simple dancette for the school play. 孩子们为学校的戏剧学习了一段简单的舞步。 | 近义词: dance, jig, step | 反义词: stillness, rest | 词性: 名词

dancette: /dænˈsɛt/ | 词源:  From Old French "dancette," diminutive of "danse" (dance), from Late Latin "dansa." Ultimately from Frankish or another West Germanic source. |  用法:  Primarily used to describe a short, simple, or lighthearted dance. Can also refer to a single dance step or a brief dancing movement. Less common in modern usage than "dance." | 词性: 名词
