companionway:/kəmˈpænjənweɪ/ /kəmˈpæn.jən.weɪ/
n.
通道，(尤指船上)升降口，扶梯
A stair or ladder leading from one deck to another on a ship or boat.
The crew scrambled up the companionway to the deck.  船员们爬上扶梯到达甲板。
stairway, staircase, ladder, hatchway
gangway (舷梯，跳板), corridor (走廊)
noun
