immateriality:/ɪˌmətɪəriˈæləti/
|n.|非物质性；无关紧要；无形性|The state of being immaterial; not consisting of matter; not relevant.|His past mistakes are immaterial to his present success. 他过去的错误与他现在的成功无关。|
|近义词：insignificance, irrelevance, unimportance|
|反义词：materiality, relevance, importance|
|词性：noun|

immaterial:/ˌɪməˈtɪəriəl/
|adj.|无关紧要的；非物质的；无形的|Not important or relevant; not consisting of matter; spiritual.|The exact date is immaterial; just tell me the month. 确切的日期无关紧要；告诉我月份就行了。|
|近义词：irrelevant, unimportant, insignificant, inconsequential|
|反义词：material, relevant, important, significant, consequential|
|词性：adjective|
