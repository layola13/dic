ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + wrap.  "Be-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "all around," "over," or "thoroughly." "Wrap" comes from the Old English word "wræppan," meaning "to turn, twist, or roll."  The combined meaning suggests a complete or thorough wrapping.
