mutchkin:/ˈmʌtʃkɪn/| n.|小酒杯，液量单位（苏格兰）|A mutchkin is a Scottish unit of liquid measure, equal to 0.42 liters (approximately 14.2 fluid ounces).|He bought a mutchkin of whisky. 他买了一小杯威士忌。|近义词：nip, dram, tot|反义词:gallon|词性:noun

mutchkin:/ˈmʌtʃkɪn/| n.|矮小的人；小孩|A small person, especially a child (informal, often affectionate).|She's a wee mutchkin, isn't she? 她真是个小不点儿，不是吗？|近义词：child, kid, tot, little one|反义词:giant|词性:noun
