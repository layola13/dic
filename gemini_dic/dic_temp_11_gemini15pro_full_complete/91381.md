hydrotelluric:/ˌhaɪdrəʊtɪˈljʊərɪk/
adjective
氢碲的；由氢和碲构成的
of, relating to, or derived from hydrogen telluride.
Hydrogen telluride is a hydrotelluric acid. 碲化氢是一种氢碲酸。
Near synonyms: telluride, telluric
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider compounds not involving tellurium or hydrogen, such as sulfuric, hydrochloric, etc. depending on context.)
词性: adjective
