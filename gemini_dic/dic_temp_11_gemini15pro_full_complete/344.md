abhorrers:/əˈbɔːrərz/
n.
憎恶者；厌恶者
People who abhor something; those who feel intense dislike or disgust.
The abhorrers of violence protested the war. 那些憎恶暴力的人抗议战争。
近义词:haters, despisers, loathers
反义词:lovers, admirers, supporters
词性:名词

abhorrers:/əˈbɔːrərz/
词源:From the verb "abhor" + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-s" (plural suffix).  "Abhor" comes from the Latin "abhorrēre," meaning "to shrink back from, shudder at," from "ab-" (away from) + "horrēre" (to bristle, shudder).
