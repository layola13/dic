decemviral:/ˌdɛsɪmˈvaɪrəl/
adjective
十人委员会的，由十人组成的
Relating to a decemvirate (a body of ten men holding power, especially the *decemviri legibus scribundis* of ancient Rome who codified the laws of the Twelve Tables).
The decemviral laws were harsh and unpopular.  十人委员会的法律苛刻且不受欢迎。
The decemviral period in Roman history was marked by tyranny. 罗马历史上的十人执政时期以暴政为标志。
Synonym:  decemvirate (as a noun referring to the body itself)
Antonym: (No direct antonym, one could consider words like "democratic," "representative," or antonyms for "tyrannical" depending on the context.)
词性: adjective
