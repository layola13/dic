hardcover:/ˈhɑːrdkʌvər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdkʌvə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|精装书；精装的|A book bound with stiff covers.| I prefer hardcovers because they last longer. 我更喜欢精装书，因为它们更耐用。|  The hardcover edition is more expensive. 精装版更贵。|近义词：hardback, case-bound|反义词：paperback, softcover|词性: 名词, 形容词

Etymology:
From "hard" + "cover". "Hard" meaning firm and unyielding, and "cover" referring to the outer protective layer of a book.
