ionicism:/ˌaɪˈɑːnɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 离子性，离子状态 |The state or process of being or becoming ionized; the degree to which something is ionized.  | The ionicism of the solution was high due to the presence of salts. 溶液由于盐的存在而具有高离子性。 |  near synonyms: ionization, electrolytic dissociation | antonyms: non-ionization, molecularity | noun

ionicism:/ˌaɪˈɑːnɪsɪzəm/ | n. | （修辞）夸张的词语，夸张的风格 | Exaggerated or inflated language or style. |  His speeches were full of ionicism and grand pronouncements. 他的演讲充满了夸张的词语和宏大的宣言。 | near synonyms: hyperbole, exaggeration, bombast | antonyms: understatement, simplicity | noun
