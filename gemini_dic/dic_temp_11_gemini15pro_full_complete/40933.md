cooly:/ˈkuːlli/| adv.| 冷静地；沉着地；冷淡地|In a cool manner; calmly, unemotionally, indifferently.|He cooly assessed the situation before acting. 他冷静地评估了情况才采取行动。|She cooly dismissed his advances. 她冷淡地拒绝了他的追求。|近义词: calmly, composedly, nonchalantly|反义词: excitedly, nervously, warmly|词性: 副词
cooly:/ˈkuːli/| n.| 苦力；搬运工 (主要用于印度及其他亚洲国家)|An unskilled laborer or porter, especially in India and other Asian countries.  (Often considered offensive.)|The coolies carried heavy loads on their backs. 苦力们背着沉重的货物。|This term is now largely considered offensive and should be avoided.  这个词现在很大程度上被认为是冒犯性的，应该避免使用。|近义词: laborer, porter (though these don't carry the same historical baggage)|反义词:  N/A (due to the specific and potentially offensive nature of the word)|词性: 名词
