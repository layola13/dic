keennesses:/ˈkiːnəsnəsɪz/
noun
敏锐, 热切, 渴望
The quality of being keen; sharpness or intensity of perception, feeling, or desire.
His keenness for the project was evident in his enthusiasm.  他对这个项目的热切之情体现在他的热情之中。
The keenness of his eyesight was remarkable. 他视力的敏锐令人惊叹。
Synonyms: eagerness, enthusiasm, zeal, sharpness, acuteness
Antonyms: apathy, indifference, dullness
词性: 名词
