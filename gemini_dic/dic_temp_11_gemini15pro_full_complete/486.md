abnormalize:/ˌæbnɔːrməlaɪz/ | vt. | 使不正常，使反常 | To cause to deviate from a normal or typical condition or behavior. |  The stress of the situation seemed to abnormalize his behavior. 压力似乎使他的行为变得反常。|  The extreme heat abnormalized the growth of the plants.  极端高温使得植物的生长异常。 | 近义词: derange, disorganize, unsettle, disturb | 反义词: normalize, stabilize, regulate | 词性: 动词

词源:  From ab- (away from) + normal + -ize (to make).  源自 ab-（远离）+ normal（正常）+ -ize（使...）。
