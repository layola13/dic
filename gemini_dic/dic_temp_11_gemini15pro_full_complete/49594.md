demersed:/dɪˈmɜːrst/
adjective
浸没的，在水下的
growing or living under water; submerged.
例句：The demersed plants thrived in the clear pond.  这些沉水植物在清澈的池塘里茁壮成长。

例句：The archaeologist discovered a demersed city off the coast. 考古学家在海岸附近发现了一座水下城市。

近义词：submerged, immersed, underwater
反义词：emersed, above water
词性：adjective

词源: From Latin *demergere, from de- + mergere “to plunge, immerse.”
