electromagnetism:/ɪˌlektroʊˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/| n.|电磁学；电磁力|The study of the physical interaction between electrically charged particles and magnetic fields.  It encompasses the study of both electric and magnetic phenomena and how they are interconnected.| Electromagnetism is a fundamental force of nature. 电磁学是大自然中的一种基本力。

The generator works on the principles of electromagnetism.  发电机是根据电磁学原理工作的。|近义词:electrodynamic force|反义词:None|noun
