macronutrients:/ˌmækroʊˈnuːtriənts/ | n. |宏量营养素|Nutrients required in large amounts, providing energy and supporting growth and development. These typically include carbohydrates, proteins, and fats.|  A balanced diet should include sufficient macronutrients. 均衡的饮食应包含足够的宏量营养素。|  Athletes often pay close attention to their macronutrient intake. 运动员通常密切关注他们的宏量营养素摄入量。 | synonyms:  macronutrient, macronutrients | antonyms: micronutrients | noun
