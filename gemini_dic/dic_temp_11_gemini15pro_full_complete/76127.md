furze:/fɜːz/
n.
荆豆，金雀花
A spiny, evergreen shrub with yellow flowers, common on heaths and wastelands.
The common sight on the moors was furze, also known as gorse.  荒野上常见的景象是荆豆，也被称为金雀花。
The bees buzzed lazily around the fragrant furze blossoms. 蜜蜂懒洋洋地在芬芳的荆豆花周围嗡嗡叫。
近义词：gorse, whin
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其生长环境相反的植物，例如water lily 水百合)
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fyrs, from Proto-Germanic *furz- (compare West Frisian fûrs “furze,” Dutch vurs “prickly shrub”).
