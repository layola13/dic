mooch:/muːtʃ/
verb
蹭，揩油，闲逛
to get something from someone without paying for it; to wander, loiter
He mooched a cigarette off me. 他向我蹭了一根烟。
He was mooching around the streets. 他在街上闲逛。
Synonyms: sponge, cadge, bum, scrounge, loiter, wander
Antonyms: provide, give, earn, work


mooch:/muːtʃ/
noun
蹭吃蹭喝的人，闲逛
a person who gets things from others without paying; an act of wandering or loitering
He's such a mooch, always borrowing money and never paying it back. 他真是个蹭吃蹭喝的人，总是借钱不还。
I went for a mooch around the shops. 我去商店闲逛了一圈。
Synonyms: freeloader, sponger, loafer
Antonyms: provider, giver, worker
