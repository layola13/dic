dewiness:/ˈduːɪnɪs/ /ˈdjuːɪnɪs/
n.
露水般的清新; 清新湿润
The state or quality of being dewy; freshness and moistness.
The dewiness of the morning air was refreshing.  早晨空气中露水般的清新令人神清气爽。
近义词：freshness, moisture, humidity
反义词：dryness, aridity
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dewy + -ness.  Dewy comes from Old English *deawig, from deaw (dew).
