loge:/loʊʒ/ | n. | 包厢；演员休息室; 小屋；眺望台 |A small compartment, often elevated, providing a private viewing area, as in a theater or stadium; a small house or cottage, often in a secluded or scenic location. |  She watched the opera from her private loge. 她从她的私人包厢观看了歌剧。|  The hunting lodge served as a loge with a stunning view of the valley.  狩猎小屋是一个可以欣赏山谷美景的眺望台。 | synonyms: box, booth, cabin, cottage | antonyms:  auditorium, hall | noun
