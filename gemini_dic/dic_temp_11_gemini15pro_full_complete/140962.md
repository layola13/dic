one-upmanship: /ˌwʌnˈʌpmənʃɪp/
noun
耍花招胜过他人；高人一筹
The practice of gaining a feeling of superiority over another person, especially by succeeding where they have failed.
He couldn't resist a little one-upmanship, pointing out that his bonus was bigger than hers. 他忍不住要炫耀一番，指出他的奖金比她多。
Synonyms: competitiveness, rivalry, vying, one-upping
Antonyms: cooperation, collaboration, modesty, humility
