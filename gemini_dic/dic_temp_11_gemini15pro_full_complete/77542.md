what: /wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) |pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

英文解析:  "What" is a versatile word used as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection. As a pronoun, it asks for information specifying something. As a determiner, it modifies a noun and asks which thing or things are meant. As an adverb, it modifies an adjective or adverb and means "to what extent?" As an interjection, it expresses surprise or disbelief.

例句：

What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want? = 你想要什么？

What a beautiful day! = 多么美好的一天！

What did you say? = 你说了什么?

What book are you reading? = 你在读什么书？


近义词：which, how

反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym.)

词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
