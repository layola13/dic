aurelius:/ɔːˈriːliəs/ | adj. n. |奥勒留的，奥勒留（人名）| Relating to or characteristic of Marcus Aurelius Antoninus, Roman emperor and Stoic philosopher.  Also used as a given name. |  Example: His stoic demeanor was quite Aurelian. 他的坚忍风范颇有奥勒留的风范。| Example:  Aurelius was a wise and just ruler. 奥勒留是一位英明公正的统治者。| 近义词: Stoic (as an adjective), philosophical | 反义词:  hedonistic, unphilosophical | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
Derived from the Roman family name Aurelius, ultimately of uncertain origin.
