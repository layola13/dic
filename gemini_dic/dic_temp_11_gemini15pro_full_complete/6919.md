ancientry:/ˈeɪnʃəntri/ | n. | 古代; 古老的事物; 古代的人（统称）| The state of being ancient; ancient times; the people of ancient times. |  The museum houses artifacts of great ancientry.  博物馆收藏了古老的文物。| 近义词: antiquity, ancientness | 反义词: modernity, modern times | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *ancienrie, from Old French ancienneté (“oldness, seniority”), from ancien (“old, ancient”) + -eté (suffix forming abstract nouns).  The English word was influenced by the Latin word  antiquitas.
