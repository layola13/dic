curvilinear:/ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪniər/| adj.|曲线形的，弯曲的|Consisting of or bounded by curved lines; represented by a curved line.|The artist specializes in curvilinear designs, incorporating flowing lines and organic shapes. 这位艺术家擅长曲线设计，作品融合了流畅的线条和有机形状。|The roller coaster's curvilinear track provided a thrilling ride. 过山车的曲线轨道提供了惊险刺激的旅程。|近义词：curved, bending, sinuous, meandering|反义词：straight, linear, rectilinear|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *curvilineus*, from *curvus* ("curved") + *linea* ("line").
