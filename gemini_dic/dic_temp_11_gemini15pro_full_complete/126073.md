mustier:/ˈmʌstiər/ (US), /ˈmʌstɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更霉的；更陈腐的；更过时的|Smelling or tasting stale, damp, and moldy; also, outdated or antiquated.|The attic was mustier than I remembered, filled with the scent of old books and dust. 阁楼比我记忆中的更霉，充满了旧书和灰尘的气味.|Synonyms: moldy, stale, fusty, musty, damp, antiquated, outdated|Antonyms: fresh, airy, modern, new|adjective

mustiest:/ˈmʌstiɪst/ (US), /ˈmʌstɪɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最霉的；最陈腐的；最过时的|The superlative form of "musty," indicating the highest degree of staleness, dampness, and moldiness, or the most outdated or antiquated.|Of all the rooms in the abandoned house, the basement was the mustiest. 在这栋废弃的房子里，地下室是最霉的。|Synonyms: moldiest, stalest, fustiest, mustiest, dampest, most antiquated, most outdated|Antonyms: freshest, airiest, most modern, newest|adjective
