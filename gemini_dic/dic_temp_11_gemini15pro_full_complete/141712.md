oracular:/əˈrækjʊlər/ |adj.|神谕的；预言的；玄妙的；晦涩难懂的| Relating to an oracle; prophetic; resembling an oracle in obscurity of expression. | The old woman spoke in an oracular tone, making vague predictions about the future.  老妇人以神谕般的语气说话，对未来做出模糊的预测。|例句：Her oracular pronouncements were difficult to interpret. 她那些像神谕一样的声明很难理解。| 近义词：prophetic, predictive, sibylline, cryptic, enigmatic | 反义词：clear, straightforward, explicit | 词性: adjective

oracular:/əˈrækjʊlər/ |n.|神谕；预言| A divine communication or revelation. |  The king sought an oracular from the temple priestess. 国王向神庙女祭司寻求神谕。|例句：The villagers treated her pronouncements as oraculars. 村民们把她的话当作神谕。|近义词：prophecy, prediction, divination |反义词:  None| 词性: noun (rare usage)
