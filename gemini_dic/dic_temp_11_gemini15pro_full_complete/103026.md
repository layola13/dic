jejunities:/dʒɪˈdʒuːnɪtiz/
n.
禁食，斋戒（通常指宗教原因）
periods of fasting, especially for religious reasons
During Ramadan, Muslims observe jejunities from dawn till dusk. 在斋月期间，穆斯林从黎明到黄昏都要禁食。
The monk observed long jejunities as part of his spiritual practice.  这位僧侣长时间禁食，作为他修行的一部分。
近义词：fasts, abstinences
反义词：feasts, indulgences
词性：名词
