nonspottable:/ˌnɒnˈspɒtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈspɑːtəbl/
adjective
无污点的；不易弄脏的
Not easily spotted or stained; immaculate.
Her white dress remained nonspottable even after the picnic.  即使野餐后，她的白裙子仍然一尘不染。
The new tablecloth is nonspottable and easy to clean. 新桌布不易弄脏，容易清洗。
近义词:stain-resistant, spotless, immaculate
反义词:stainable, spottable
