god-man:/ˈɡɒdˌmæn/ /ˈɡɑːdˌmæn/ | n. | 神人;神化的人;耶稣基督 | A being believed to be both divine and human, especially Christ. |  Jesus Christ is often referred to as the God-man. 耶稣基督通常被称为神人。| The ancient Egyptians viewed their pharaohs as god-men. 古埃及人把他们的法老视为神人。|近义词:incarnation, divine human, deity |反义词:mortal, human |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *godmann, from god (god) + mann (man).  The term gained prominence in Christian theology to describe the dual nature of Jesus Christ.
