narwhal:/ˈnɑːrwɔːl/ (US), /ˈnɑːwəl/ (UK)| n. |独角鲸，一角鲸|A medium-sized toothed whale (Monodon monoceros) that possesses a large "tusk" from a protruding canine tooth, found in the Arctic waters.|例句：The narwhal is a unique creature of the Arctic. 独角鲸是北极地区一种独特的生物。

例句：Researchers are studying the narwhal's tusk to understand its function. 研究人员正在研究独角鲸的长牙以了解其功能。|近义词：unicorn whale (非正式), toothed whale|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

narwhals:/ˈnɑːrwɔːlz/ (US), /ˈnɑːwəlz/ (UK)| n. |独角鲸（复数）|Plural form of narwhal.|例句：We saw a pod of narwhals swimming near the ice floe. 我们看到一群独角鲸在浮冰附近游动。

例句：Narwhals are hunted by Inuit people for their meat and skin. 因纽特人猎杀独角鲸以获取它们的肉和皮。|近义词：unicorn whales (非正式), toothed whales|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
