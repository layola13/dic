initialed:/ɪˈnɪʃəld/ | adj. | 首字母缩写的，签名的 | Marked with initials.  | Example: The document was initialed by all parties involved. 所有相关方都在文件上签了首字母。 | Example: She initialed the contract before sending it back.  她在寄回合同之前在上面签了首字母。 | Synonyms: signed, monogrammed | Antonyms: unsigned, blank | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the past participle of *initial* (v.), from French *initier*, from Late Latin *initiare* "to begin, initiate," from Latin *initium* "beginning."
