nonappreciations:/ˌnɒnəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃənz/
|n.|缺乏欣赏，不领情|Absence or lack of appreciation; instances of failing to appreciate something or someone.|His constant nonappreciations of her efforts made her feel demoralized. 他对她努力的不断不领情让她感到 demoralized.|近义词: ingratitude, disregard, indifference|反义词: appreciation, gratitude, recognition|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, absence of (来自拉丁语 non)
appreciation (noun): recognition and enjoyment of the good qualities of someone or something (来自拉丁语 appretiat- ‘valued’, from the verb appretiare, from ad- ‘to’ + pretium ‘price’)
-s (suffix): plural marker
