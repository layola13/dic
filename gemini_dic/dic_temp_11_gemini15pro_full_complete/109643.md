lettuces: /ˈlɛtɪsɪz/
n.
莴苣（复数）
A leafy green vegetable used in salads and other dishes.  Various types of lettuces exist, such as romaine, iceberg, and butter lettuce.
We grow several different lettuces in our garden. 我们在花园里种植几种不同的莴苣。

近义词: greens, salad greens
词性: noun
