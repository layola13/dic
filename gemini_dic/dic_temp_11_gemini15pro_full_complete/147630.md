overplows:/ˌōvərˈplaʊz/ /ˌoʊvərˈplaʊz/| vt. |过度耕作，过分犁地|To plow too much or too deeply, often leading to soil erosion or degradation. |The farmer overplowed the field, leading to significant soil erosion. 农民过度耕作了田地，导致严重的土壤侵蚀。| The farmer accidentally overplowed the neighbor's property line. 农民不小心耕过了邻居的土地界线。|overcultivate, overfarm|underplow|verb
