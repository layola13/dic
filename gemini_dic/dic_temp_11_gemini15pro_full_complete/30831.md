cha-ching:/tʃəˈtʃɪŋ/ | interj. | 赚钱的声音；收银机的声音 |  Imitates the sound of a cash register, implying that money has been made or received. |  Cha-ching! I just sold another painting!  赚钱啦！我又卖出一幅画了！| 近义词: ker-ching | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: interjection

cha-ching:/tʃəˈtʃɪŋ/ | n. |  赚钱的声音；收银机的声音 | The sound of a cash register, often used figuratively to represent making money. | The comedian joked, "Cha-ching! Another gig booked!"  喜剧演员开玩笑说：“钱来了！又预定了一场演出！”| 近义词: ker-ching | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
