barbed:/bɑːrbd/| adj.|带刺的；尖刻的；讽刺的|Having sharp points or hooks.|The barbed wire fence kept the cattle in. 带刺的铁丝网把牛围了起来。|Synonyms: pointed, sharp, thorny, prickly, spiky, jagged, hooked|Antonyms: blunt, smooth, kind, gentle|adjective

barbed:/bɑːrbd/| adj.|讽刺的，挖苦的|(of a remark)  sarcastic and unkind, and intended to hurt someone's feelings|He made a barbed comment about her weight. 他对她的体重做出了尖刻的评论。|Synonyms: sarcastic, cutting, biting, acrimonious, caustic, mordant, trenchant|Antonyms: kind, gentle, complimentary|adjective
