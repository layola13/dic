They are known for consuming large quantities of food rapidly.|He's a real hogringer, he ate a whole pizza by himself! 他真是个贪吃鬼，他一个人吃了一整个披萨！|近义词：glutton, gourmand, gormandizer|反义词: ascetic, moderate eater|noun
