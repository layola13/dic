navigation:/ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n.| 导航，航行，航海|the process or activity of accurately ascertaining one's position and planning and following a route.|例句：The ship's navigation system failed.  这艘船的导航系统失灵了。|例句：Navigation through the dense forest was difficult.  在茂密的森林中航行很困难。|近义词：guidance, direction, piloting|反义词:drifting, wandering|noun
