cantankerous:/kænˈtæŋkərəs/
|adj.|
|脾气坏的，爱争吵的|
|Bad-tempered, argumentative, and uncooperative.|
|The cantankerous old man yelled at the children for walking on his lawn.|那个脾气暴躁的老人对着在草坪上走的孩子们大喊大叫。|
|He's become increasingly cantankerous in his old age.|他年纪越大脾气越坏。|
|bad-tempered, grumpy, irritable, quarrelsome, curmudgeonly|
|good-natured, agreeable, amiable, pleasant|
|adjective|

ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier *contakeryous*, possibly influenced by *ranker*,  ultimately of uncertain origin.
