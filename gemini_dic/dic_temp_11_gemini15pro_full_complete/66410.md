executively:/ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪvli/| adv.| 执行地，管理地，行政地 |  In a manner befitting a high-ranking official or executive. | The project was executively overseen by the CEO. 該項目由CEO執行監督。| 近义词: managerially, administratively | 反义词: non-executively, operationally | 词性:副词


词源: From executive + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin exsequi "to follow out, enforce".
