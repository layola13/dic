asic:/ˈeɪsɪk/ /ˈæsɪk/ | n. |专用集成电路；应用型专用集成电路|An integrated circuit customized for a particular use, rather than intended for general-purpose use.|Example: This new graphics card uses a powerful ASIC for cryptocurrency mining. 这款新的显卡使用强大的专用集成电路进行加密货币挖矿.|Example:  ASICs are designed for specific tasks, making them more efficient.  专用集成电路是为特定任务设计的，这使得它们更高效。|近义词：custom chip, integrated circuit, IC|反义词: general-purpose processor, CPU|词性:noun
