carpooler: /ˈkɑːrˌpuːlər/ (US) /ˈkɑːˌpuːlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|拼车者，合伙用车的人|A person who travels to and from work or school regularly with other people in a car, usually taking turns to drive.|例句：She's a regular carpooler to reduce her carbon footprint. 她是一位经常拼车的环保人士。

例句：Finding reliable carpoolers can save you a lot of money on gas. 找到可靠的拼车伙伴可以为你节省很多油钱。

近义词：ridesharer, commuter|反义词: solo driver|词性: noun
