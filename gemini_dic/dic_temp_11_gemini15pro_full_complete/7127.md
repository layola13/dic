angel-cake:/ˈeɪndʒəl keɪk/ | n. | 天使蛋糕 | A light, spongy cake made with egg whites, sugar, and flour, often flavored with vanilla or almond extract. |  My grandmother always baked a delicious angel-cake for my birthday. 我祖母总是为我的生日烤一个美味的天使蛋糕。| synonyms: sponge cake, chiffon cake | antonyms:  pound cake, fruitcake | noun

angel-cake:/ˈeɪndʒəl keɪk/ | adj. (informal, less common) |  轻而易举的，非常容易的 |  The exam was angel-cake. 考试非常容易。| synonyms: easy, effortless | antonyms: difficult, challenging | adjective
