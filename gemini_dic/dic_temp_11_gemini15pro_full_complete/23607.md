bran:/bræn/| n.|麸皮；谷糠|The bran is removed from the grain during milling.  在碾磨过程中，麸皮会从谷物中分离出来。|例句：I add bran to my breakfast cereal for extra fiber. 我在早餐麦片里加麸皮以增加纤维。|例句：The bran flakes were a bit dry.  麸皮麦片有点干。|近义词：chaff, husk|反义词:flour, endosperm|词性:名词
