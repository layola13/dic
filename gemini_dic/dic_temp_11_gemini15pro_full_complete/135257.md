nonparallelisms:/ˌnɒnˌpærəˈlɛlɪzəmz/ | n. | 非平行结构，不平行 | Lack of parallelism in grammatical structure. This can involve mismatched tenses, different sentence structures within a list, or inconsistent use of parts of speech.  | The essay suffered from several nonparallelisms, making it difficult to read. 这篇文章有很多不平行的结构，读起来很费劲。 |  近义词: inconsistencies, dissymmetry | 反义词: parallelism, symmetry | noun
