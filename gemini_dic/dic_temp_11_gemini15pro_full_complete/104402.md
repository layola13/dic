kaco:/ˈkɑːtsoʊ/ | n. | 粪便；排泄物 |  A vulgar slang term for excrement.  Derived from Czech "kace" meaning "shit" or "crap". | Example: The dog left a pile of kaco on the lawn. 狗在草坪上留下了一堆粪便。 | Example:  Don't step in that kaco!  别踩到那坨屎！ | Synonym: shit, crap, feces, dung, poo | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for excrement) | noun
