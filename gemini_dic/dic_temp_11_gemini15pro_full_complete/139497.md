obstructionism:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪzəm/
n.
阻挠主义；故意阻挠
The deliberate prevention of progress or change, especially in politics.
The senator was accused of obstructionism for refusing to vote on the bill.  这位参议员因拒绝就该法案投票而被指责为阻挠主义。
His obstructionism tactics delayed the project for months.  他的阻挠策略使项目延误了数月。
Synonyms:impediment, hindrance, resistance, opposition
Antonyms:cooperation, assistance, support, facilitation
noun
