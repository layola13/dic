hypothetically:/ˌhaɪpəˈθetɪkli/| adv.|假设地，假想地|In a way that is imagined or suggested but not necessarily real or true.  It describes something that is based on a supposed or possible situation, rather than on actual facts.|Hypothetically, if you won the lottery, what would you do?  假设你中了彩票，你会做什么？|Hypothetically speaking, if we could travel at the speed of light, we could reach other galaxies. 假设我们能以光速旅行，我们就能到达其他星系。|presumably, theoretically|actually, really|adverb
