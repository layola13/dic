counterprojects:/ˈkaʊntərˌprɑːdʒekts/ | n. | 对抗项目，反制项目 | Projects undertaken in opposition to another project, often with the aim of neutralizing or negating its effects. | The government launched a series of counterprojects to undermine the opposition's infrastructure plans. 政府启动了一系列对抗项目来破坏反对派的基础设施计划。|  Countermeasures were put in place to address the potential fallout from the failed initiative. 实施了应对措施以解决失败的举措可能造成的负面影响。 | synonyms: countermeasures, counter-initiatives | antonyms: supporting projects, collaborative projects | noun
