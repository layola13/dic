asphyxiators:/əsˈfɪksiˌeɪtərz/| n. |窒息物；使窒息的人或物|Something that causes asphyxia (a condition arising when the body is deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death); a person who asphyxiates someone.|The faulty heater emitted carbon monoxide, acting as an asphyxiator and endangering the entire family.|近义词：choking agent, suffocant, strangler|反义词:resuscitator, ventilator|词性:noun

asphyxiator:/əsˈfɪksiˌeɪtər/| n. |窒息物；使窒息的人或物|A substance or object that can cause asphyxiation, depriving the body of oxygen and leading to unconsciousness or death.  Can also refer to a person who deliberately causes asphyxiation.|Police suspected the pillow was used as an asphyxiator in the murder.|近义词：choking agent, suffocant|反义词:oxygen mask, ventilator|词性:noun
