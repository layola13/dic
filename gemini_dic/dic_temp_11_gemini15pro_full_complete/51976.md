diadem: /ˈdaɪədɛm/ (US), /ˈdaɪədɛm/ (UK)| n.| 王冠，冕，头饰| A jeweled crown or headband worn as a symbol of royalty or high honor.| The queen wore a sparkling diadem. 女王戴着闪耀的王冠。|Synonyms: crown, tiara, coronet|Antonyms: none|noun

diadem: /ˈdaɪədɛm/ (US), /ˈdaɪədɛm/ (UK)| v. |加冕，戴上王冠 (This usage is much less common than the noun form) |To adorn with or as if with a diadem.|  He diademed her with flowers. 他用鲜花给她戴上花冠。|Synonyms: crown, adorn|Antonyms:  none|verb
