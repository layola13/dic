abstracter:/əbˈstræktər/ | n. |  摘要员，提取员 | A person who makes abstracts, summaries, or extracts from documents, books, etc.  |  The abstracter summarized the key findings of the research paper. 摘要员总结了研究论文的主要发现。 | Synonyms: summarizer, epitomizer | Antonyms: expander, elaborator | noun


abstracter:/əbˈstræktər/ | n. | 产权调查员 | A person who searches for and examines public records, especially records of titles to real property, and prepares and certifies the history of ownership. | The abstracter verified the chain of title for the property. 产权调查员核实了该房产的所有权历史。| Synonyms: title examiner, title searcher | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From abstract + -er.  "abstract" comes from Late Latin abstractus, past participle of abstrahere "to draw away," from Latin abs- "away" + trahere "to draw."
