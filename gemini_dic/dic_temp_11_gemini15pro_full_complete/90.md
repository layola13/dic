aas:/eɪ eɪ ɛs/ | n. | aminoacyl-tRNA synthetases |氨酰-tRNA合成酶 | Aminoacyl-tRNA synthetases (aaRSs) are enzymes that attach the appropriate amino acid onto its corresponding tRNA. This process is crucial for protein synthesis. |  AARSs play a vital role in ensuring the fidelity of protein translation. 氨酰-tRNA合成酶在确保蛋白质翻译的保真度方面起着至关重要的作用。

The specificity of aaRSs is essential for accurate protein synthesis. 氨酰-tRNA合成酶的特异性对于精确的蛋白质合成至关重要。 | 近义词: tRNA ligase | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: noun
