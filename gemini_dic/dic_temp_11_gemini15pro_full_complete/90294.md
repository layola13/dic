housemasterships:/ˈhaʊsˌmɑːstərʃɪps/
n.
舍监职位；舍监职务
The position or positions of a housemaster, especially in a British boarding school.  This refers to the role and responsibilities associated with being a housemaster.
The school had several housemasterships available. 这所学校有几个舍监职位空缺。
Synonyms: housemaster positions, housemaster roles
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: student roles, teaching positions)
noun


housemasterships:/ˈhaʊsˌmɑːstərʃɪps/
n.
舍监的职责或权限
The duties and authority associated with being a housemaster.
His housemasterships included maintaining discipline and overseeing the welfare of the students in his house. 他的舍监职责包括维持纪律和监督他所负责宿舍里学生的福利。
Synonyms: housemaster duties, housemaster responsibilities
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: student freedoms, student rights)
