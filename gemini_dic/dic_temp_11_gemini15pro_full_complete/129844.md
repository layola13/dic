non-hyperbolic:/ˌnɒn haɪpəˈbɒlɪk/ | adj. | 非双曲线的；非夸张的 | Not hyperbolic; not exaggerated or extravagant.  |  The witness gave a non-hyperbolic account of the events. 目击者对事件进行了非夸张的描述。

The scientist preferred non-hyperbolic language in his research papers.  这位科学家在他的研究论文中更喜欢使用非夸张的语言。 |  Synonyms: realistic, moderate, understated, temperate | Antonyms: hyperbolic, exaggerated, overstated, extravagant | adjective
