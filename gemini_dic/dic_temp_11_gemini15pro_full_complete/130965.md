nonassonants:/nɒnˈæsənənts/ | adj. |非谐音的；非类音的| Not assonant; not containing assonance. Assonance is the resemblance of sound between syllables of nearby words, arising particularly from the rhyming of two or more stressed vowels, but with different consonants following.  Nonassonants, therefore, lack this vowel rhyme. | The poet deliberately used nonassonants to create a jarring effect. 这位诗人故意使用非谐音词来制造刺耳的效果。 |  The lyrics were striking for their use of harsh, nonassonant sounds.  这首歌词因其使用刺耳的非谐音而引人注目。 | Near Antonyms: assonant, rhyming, harmonious  | Antonyms: dissonant, clashing | adjective
