PHRASE->

be well advised to  最好…  You would be well advised to listen to your doctor. 你最好听从医生的建议。

as advised  按照建议  I took the medication as advised. 我按照建议服用了药物。

ill-advised  不明智的；欠考虑的  It was an ill-advised decision. 这是一个不明智的决定。

poorly advised  不明智的；建议不当的  He was poorly advised by his lawyer. 他的律师给了他不明智的建议。

advisedly  谨慎地；故意地  I use the word "advisedly". 我是特意用这个词的。
