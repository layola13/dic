antiscoletic:/ˌæntiˌskɒˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 抗脊柱侧凸的，矫正脊柱侧凸的 | Preventing or correcting scoliosis (lateral curvature of the spine). |  An antiscoletic brace can help correct spinal curvature.  抗脊柱侧凸的支架可以帮助矫正脊柱弯曲。| synonyms: corrective, orthopedical | antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym) | adjective

antiscoletic:/ˌæntiˌskɒˈlɪtɪk/ | n. | 抗脊柱侧凸的药物或装置 | A medicine or device used to prevent or correct scoliosis. | The doctor prescribed an antiscoletic for the patient's scoliosis. 医生为病人的脊柱侧凸开了抗脊柱侧凸的药物。 | synonyms: brace, orthosis | antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + scoliotic (relating to scoliosis).  Scoliosis comes from Greek skoliōsis, from skolios (curved).
