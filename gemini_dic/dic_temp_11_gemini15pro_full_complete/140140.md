off-year:/ˈɔːfˌjɪr/ /ˈɑːfˌjɪr/| n. adj.|非大选年，淡季|A year in which major elections, such as presidential or midterm elections, are not held.  As an adjective, it describes something related to such a year, often implying reduced activity or importance.|
例句：
Turnout is typically lower in off-years.
非大选年的投票率通常较低。

This business usually experiences a slump in off-years.
这家公司通常在非大选年业绩下滑。


近义词：non-election year|
反义词：election year|
词性: noun, adjective
