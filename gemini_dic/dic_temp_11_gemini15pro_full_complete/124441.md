motorized:/ˈmoʊtəraɪzd/ | adj. | 机动的，装有发动机的 | equipped with a motor or motors |  The farm uses motorized equipment for harvesting.  农场使用机动设备进行收割。 | motorized vehicles have become essential for modern transportation. 机动车辆对于现代交通来说至关重要。 | 近义词: powered, automated, mechanized | 反义词: manual, unpowered | 词性: adjective

词源:  From motor + -ize + -ed.  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or cause to be," and "-ed" creates the past participle form, which here functions as an adjective.
