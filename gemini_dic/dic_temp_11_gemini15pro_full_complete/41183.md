copyists:/ˈkɑːpiɪsts/ | n. | 抄写员；誊写员；模仿者 | A person who copies documents, manuscripts, or other written material, or who imitates the work of another. | The medieval copyists preserved many ancient texts. 中世纪的抄写员保存了许多古代文献。 |  The copyists worked tirelessly to reproduce the illuminated manuscripts. 抄写员不知疲倦地复制着这些泥金装饰手抄本。 | synonyms: scribe, transcriber, duplicator, imitator | antonyms: originator, creator, innovator | noun
