bodhisattva:/ˌboʊdiːˈsʌtvə/ | n. |菩萨|A person who is able to reach nirvana but delays doing so out of compassion in order to save suffering beings.|The Dalai Lama is considered a bodhisattva. 达赖喇嘛被认为是一位菩萨。|Synonyms: enlightened being, compassionate one |Antonyms: Arhat (in some interpretations)|noun

bodhisattvas:/ˌboʊdiːˈsʌtvəz/ | n. |菩萨们|Plural form of bodhisattva, referring to multiple individuals who are on the path to enlightenment and dedicate themselves to helping others.|Many bodhisattvas are depicted in Buddhist art. 许多菩萨在佛教艺术中被描绘。|Synonyms: enlightened beings, compassionate ones |Antonyms: Arhats (in some interpretations)|noun
