antiegoist:/ˌæntiˈiːɡəʊɪst/ |n.| 反利己主义者;  |A person who opposes egoism. Someone who believes in and practices altruism. | Example:  The antiegoist devoted her life to helping others.  反利己主义者将自己的一生都奉献给了帮助他人。

Example: He was known as an antiegoist for his selfless acts. 他因无私的行为而被称为反利己主义者。 | 近义词: altruist, philanthropist | 反义词: egoist, narcissist | 词性: 名词
