adventitious: /ˌædˌvɛnˈtɪʃəs/ | adj. |  偶然的，外来的 | Coming from outside; not inherent or innate; accidental.  | The book's adventitious popularity stemmed from a celebrity mentioning it on social media. 这本书意外的流行源于一位名人在社交媒体上提到了它。 | Synonyms: extrinsic, accidental, incidental, extraneous | Antonyms: inherent, intrinsic, innate, essential | 词性: 形容词
