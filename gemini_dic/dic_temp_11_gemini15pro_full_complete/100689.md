intracardially:/ˌɪntrəkɑːrdiəli/
adverb
心臟內地；心內地
Within the heart; into the heart.
The medication was administered intracardially.  这种药物是通过心内注射给药的。
Epinephrine was injected intracardially.  肾上腺素被注射入心脏。
近义词:intracardiac
反义词: (no common direct antonym; consider extracardially - outside the heart)
词性:adverb
