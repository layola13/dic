longbeard:/ˈlɒŋbiəd (British), ˈlɔːŋbɪrd (American)| n.|长须者，长胡子的人；某些留着长须的动物（如山羊）|A person, especially a man, with a long beard. Also refers to certain animals with long beards, such as goats.|例句：The old longbeard told tales of his youth.  这位留着长胡子的老人讲述了他年轻时的故事。

例句：The mountain goat, a magnificent longbeard, stood proudly on the cliff. 那只雄伟的山羊，留着长长的胡须，骄傲地站在悬崖上。|近义词：gaffer (old man), patriarch|反义词:youth, clean-shaven man|词性:noun
