freshly:/ˈfreʃli/ | adv. | 新鲜地，刚，最近 | Recently; newly; in a fresh manner. |例句：The freshly baked bread smelled wonderful.  刚烤好的面包闻起来很棒。|例句：She was freshly dressed in a new outfit. 她穿着一套新衣服，显得格外清新。| 近义词：recently, newly, lately | 反义词：stale, old, long ago | 词性: 副词

词源:
From fresh + -ly.  来自形容词fresh（新鲜的）加上副词后缀-ly。
