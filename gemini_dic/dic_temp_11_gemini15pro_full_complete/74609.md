freckled:/ˈfrɛkld/| adj.|雀斑的，有雀斑的|Covered with freckles; spotted.|例句：She has a freckled nose. 她有一个雀斑的鼻子。|例句：The freckled boy smiled shyly.  这个有雀斑的男孩害羞地笑了。|近义词：spotted, speckled|反义词:clear, unblemished|词性:形容词

词源:
From *freckele*, diminutive of *freken*, from Old Norse *frekn* "freckle".
