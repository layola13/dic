ersatzes:/ˈɜːrzɑːtsəz/ /ˈɛərzɑːtsəz/ | n. | 替代品；代用品 |  Something that is used as a substitute for something else, especially something inferior.  It often implies that the substitute is not as good as the original. |  During the war, they had to use ersatz coffee made from roasted grains.  战争期间，他们不得不使用由烘焙谷物制成的代用咖啡。

| 近义词：substitute, replacement, imitation, alternative, proxy | 反义词：original, genuine, authentic | 词性：名词

ersatz:/ˈɜːrzɑːts/ /ˈɛərzɑːts/ | adj. | 代用的；仿制的 |  Made or used as a substitute, typically an inferior one, for something else. |  The ersatz leather began to peel after only a few months.  这种人造皮革才用了几个月就开始剥落了。

| 近义词：artificial, synthetic, imitation, fake, spurious | 反义词：genuine, real, authentic, natural | 词性：形容词

词源:  From German Ersatz, from ersetzen 'to replace', from er- (intensive prefix) + setzen 'to set'.
