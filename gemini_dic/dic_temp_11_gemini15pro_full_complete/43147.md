cozies:/ˈkoʊziz/| n.|舒适温暖的东西（尤指毛毯、保暖衣物等）;  舒适的氛围；温馨的时光|Small, comfortable things that make a place feel warm and inviting, especially blankets or warm clothing. Also can refer to a feeling of warmth and comfort.|She snuggled up on the sofa with a cup of tea and her favorite cozies. 她依偎在沙发上，喝着茶，盖着她最喜欢的毛毯。|synonyms: comforts, snuggles, blankets, warm clothes|antonyms: discomforts, chills|noun

cozies:/ˈkoʊziz/| v. (cozies, cozied, cozying)|使舒适；使亲密；依偎；奉承；讨好|To make oneself or someone else feel comfortable and warm, often by snuggling or cuddling.  Can also mean to act in a friendly way towards someone, often with the hope of gaining something.|She cozied up to him by the fire. 她依偎着他在火炉旁。|synonyms: snuggle, cuddle, nestle, ingratiate|antonyms: distance, alienate|verb
