deny-oneself:/dɪˈnaɪ wʌnˈsɛlf/
|v.|克制自己，舍弃；戒绝|To abstain from something desired or pleasurable; to forgo something for oneself.|She denied herself all luxuries to save money for her child's education. 她为了孩子的教育，克制自己，放弃了一切奢侈品。|
|v.|拒绝给予自己|To refuse to grant oneself something.|He denied himself the pleasure of a second helping of dessert. 他没有让自己享受再吃一份甜点的乐趣。|
近义词:abstain, forgo, sacrifice, deprive
反义词:indulge, pamper, gratify
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *denyen oneself*, equivalent to deny + oneself.  Deny comes from Old French *denier*, from Latin *dēnegāre* "to deny, refuse," from *dē-* "away, from" + *negāre* "to say no," related to *ne* "not." Oneself is a reflexive pronoun formed from one + self.
