normalizes:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪzɪz/
verb
使正常化；使标准化
to make something standard or normal.
The new software normalizes all the data.  这个新软件将所有数据标准化。
The peace treaty normalized relations between the two countries.  和平条约使两国关系正常化。
standardizes, regularizes
deviates, distorts
词性: 动词

normalizes:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪzɪz/
noun
(normalization的复数形式) 正常化；标准化
the process of making something standard or normal.
The normalizes of trade relations are essential for global economic growth. 贸易关系的正常化对全球经济增长至关重要。  (This example is a bit awkward and would likely be phrased differently in natural speech. A more common usage would be "The normalization of...")
These normalizes helped stabilize the market. 这些标准化措施帮助稳定了市场。(Again, this is awkward.  A more natural phrase would be "These normalizations" or even better, rephrased completely, e.g., "These standardization measures...")
standardizations, regularizations
deviations, distortions
词性: 名词 (注意: normalizes 作为名词比较少见，通常使用 normalization)
