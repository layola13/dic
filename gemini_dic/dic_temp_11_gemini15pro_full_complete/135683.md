nonprescribers:/nɒnprɪˈskrɑɪbəz/ | n. | 非处方者 |  People who are not authorized or qualified to prescribe medication.  This typically refers to individuals other than licensed medical professionals like doctors, physician assistants, or nurse practitioners. |  Nonprescribers should not offer medical advice regarding medication. 非处方者不应提供有关药物的医疗建议。|  The study included both prescribers and nonprescribers in the survey.  这项研究在调查中包括了处方者和非处方者。 | Synonyms: non-physicians, laymen | Antonyms: prescribers, physicians | Noun
