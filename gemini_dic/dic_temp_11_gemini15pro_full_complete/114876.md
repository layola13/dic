mammonize:/ˈmæmənaɪz/
v.
崇拜财富；使唯利是图
to devote oneself to the pursuit of wealth; to make greedy for riches.
He was accused of mammonizing the church. 他被指控使教会变得唯利是图。
近义词: materialize, secularize, commercialize
反义词: spiritualize
词性: 动词
