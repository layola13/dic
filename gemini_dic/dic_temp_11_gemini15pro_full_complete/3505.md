aftershaft:/ˈæftərʃæft/ | n. | 副羽，后羽 |A small, supplementary feather or plume that grows from the underside of the main shaft of a feather, typically found in birds. |The downy aftershaft provides additional insulation for the bird.  这绒毛状的副羽为鸟类提供了额外的保暖层。| synonym:  hypopenna, pluma  | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps main feather/primary feather) | noun
