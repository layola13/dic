dry-cleaned:/ˌdraɪˈkliːnd/ | adj. | 干洗的 | Cleaned with chemical solvents rather than with water. | Example: My suit is dry-cleaned. 我的西装是干洗的。 | Example: This dress must be dry-cleaned. 这件连衣裙必须干洗。 | 近义词: chemically cleaned | 反义词: washed, laundered | 词性: adjective

dry-clean:/ˌdraɪˈkliːn/ | v. | 干洗 | To clean (clothing) with chemical solvents rather than with water. | Example: I need to dry-clean this coat. 我需要干洗这件外套。 | Example:  They dry-clean all their delicate clothes. 他们把所有精致的衣服都干洗了。 | 近义词: chemically clean | 反义词: wash, launder | 词性: verb

词源:  "dry-" + "cleaned"， "dry" (meaning without water) and the past participle of the verb "clean".  The term emerged in the mid-19th century with the development of non-water-based cleaning solvents.
