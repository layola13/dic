bed's:/ˈbedz/
noun
床的；床铺的
Possessive form of bed, indicating ownership or association with a bed.
This is my bed's headboard. 这是我的床头板。
The cat is sleeping on the bed's edge. 猫正睡在床边。
Synonyms: belonging to the bed, of the bed
Antonyms: N/A
词性:  名词所有格
