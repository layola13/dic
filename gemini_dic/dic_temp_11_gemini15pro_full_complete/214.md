abbreviation:/əˌbriːviˈeɪʃn/ |n.| 缩写，缩写词 |An abbreviation is a shortened form of a word or phrase. |例句：The abbreviation for "mister" is "Mr."  先生的缩写是“Mr.”


例句：NASA is an abbreviation for National Aeronautics and Space Administration. NASA是美国国家航空航天局的缩写。

近义词:shortening, contraction, acronym |反义词:expansion, elaboration |词性:noun
