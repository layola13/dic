antireactionaries:/ˌæntiˈriːˈækʃəˌnɛriz/
n.|反动分子，反对反动势力的人|People who oppose reactionaries or reactionary policies.  Those who fight against those who oppose progress or reform.|The antireactionaries protested the government's new policies. 反动分子抗议政府的新政策。|
antireactionary:/ˌæntiˈriːˈækʃəˌnɛri/
adj.|反动的，反对反动的|Opposing or resisting reaction or reactionaries.  Characterized by opposition to political or social reaction.|The antireactionary movement gained momentum after the war.  战后反动运动势头强劲。|
antireactionary:/ˌæntiˈriːˈækʃəˌnɛri/
n.|反动行为，反动言论|An action or statement that opposes reaction or reactionaries.|His speech was filled with antireactionary sentiment.  他的演讲充满了反动情绪。|
近义词:progressive, liberal, reformist, radical
反义词:reactionary, conservative, right-wing
