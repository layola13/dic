clarify:/ˈklærəfaɪ/ (US) /ˈklærɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v. | 澄清；阐明；使清晰 | to make something clearer or easier to understand by giving more details or a simpler explanation | Could you clarify your last remark? 你能解释一下你最后的那句话吗？

|近义词：explain, elucidate, expound, illuminate|反义词：obscure, confuse, complicate|词性:动词


clarification:/ˌklærəfɪˈkeɪʃn/ (US) /ˌklærɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ (UK)| n. | 澄清；阐明；净化 | the action of making a statement or situation less confused and more comprehensible |  I requested clarification on several points. 我要求澄清几点。 |近义词：explanation, elucidation |反义词：obfuscation, confusion |词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *clarificare, from Latin clarus (“clear”) + -ficare (from facere (“to make”)).
