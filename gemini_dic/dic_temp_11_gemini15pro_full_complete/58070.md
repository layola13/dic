drongo:/ˈdrɒŋɡəʊ/ (UK) /ˈdrɔːŋɡoʊ/ (US)| n. |伯劳，蠢货，笨蛋|A bird of the family Dicruridae, found in Africa, Asia, and Australia, typically with glossy black plumage, some having long forked tails. Also informally used to refer to a stupid or foolish person. |例句：The drongo swooped down and caught the insect in mid-air.  伯劳俯冲下来，在半空中抓住了昆虫。

例句：He called me a drongo for forgetting my keys. 他因为我忘了钥匙而叫我笨蛋。|近义词：fool, idiot, nincompoop (for a person)|反义词: genius, clever person (for a person)|词性:noun
