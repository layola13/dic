hypersaintly:/ˌhaɪpərˈseɪntli/
adjective
极度神圣的；过分虔诚的
Excessively or affectedly saintly; overly pious.
She adopted a hypersaintly attitude after the scandal, hoping to regain public favor.  丑闻之后，她摆出一副过分虔诚的态度，希望能重新赢得公众的青睐。

He spoke in a hypersaintly tone that made everyone uncomfortable. 他用一种极其圣洁的语气说话，让每个人都感到不舒服。

近义词: sanctimonious, pietistic, holier-than-thou
反义词: impious, irreverent, sinful
词性: adjective
词源:  "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, beyond") + "saintly" (from "saint")
