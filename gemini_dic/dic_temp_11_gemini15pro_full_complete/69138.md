favosites:/ˈfeɪvəˌsaɪts/| n.|蜂巢珊瑚；蜂窝珊瑚属|A genus of extinct coral of the Paleozoic era, characterized by polygonal closely packed corallites resembling a honeycomb.  These fossils are often found in Silurian and Devonian rocks.|例句：The geologist discovered a large favosites fossil in the quarry.  地质学家在采石场发现了一个大型蜂巢珊瑚化石。

例句：Favosites are an important index fossil for the Devonian period.  蜂巢珊瑚是泥盆纪的重要标准化石。|近义词：coral, honeycomb coral|反义词: None (as it's a specific genus)|词性: Noun
