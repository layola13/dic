coronule: /ˈkɒrənjuːl/ (US) /ˈkɒrənjuːl/ (UK)| n. |小冠，花冠，冠状物|A small crown or crown-like structure, especially a circle of small petals or appendages at the base of the corolla in some flowers.|The coronule of the daffodil is a distinctive feature. 水仙花的小冠是其显著特征。|Synonyms: corona, coronet|Antonyms: (No direct antonym.  Consider "base" or "bottom" in specific contexts.)|Noun
