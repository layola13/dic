mangel:/ˈmæŋɡəl/ | n. | 严重缺乏; 损坏; 严重受损 | a serious lack or shortage of something; a serious flaw or damage |  The country suffered from a severe mangel of skilled workers.  这个国家严重缺乏熟练工人。| synonyms: lack, shortage, deficiency, dearth, scarcity, paucity | antonyms: abundance, surplus, excess | noun


mangel:/ˈmæŋɡəl/ | v. | 严重损坏; 弄糟; 毁坏 (主要用于英国英语) | to damage something badly; to spoil or ruin something (chiefly British English) | The frost mangled the crops. 霜冻毁坏了庄稼。 | synonyms: damage, ruin, spoil, mar, impair, mutilate | antonyms: repair, fix, improve, enhance | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Mangel*, from Middle High German *mangel*, meaning "lack, defect." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mangalaz* "deficient."
