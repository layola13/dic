busky:/ˈbʌski/| adj.|健壮的，茂盛的| Of strong or sturdy build; bushy.  A busky lad. 一个健壮的小伙子。|例句：The busky plants thrived in the fertile soil. 这些茂盛的植物在肥沃的土壤中茁壮成长。

例句：The farmer had a busky frame, evidence of years of hard work.  这位农民有着健壮的体格，这是多年辛勤劳动的证明。|近义词：sturdy, robust, thickset, bushy|反义词：frail, weak, slender, sparse|词性:形容词|词源: Likely a variant of "bushy," from Middle English "busch" and Old English "busc," meaning a shrub or thicket.  The sense of "busky" meaning strong or well-built might have developed metaphorically, comparing a person's physique to the dense growth of a bush.|
