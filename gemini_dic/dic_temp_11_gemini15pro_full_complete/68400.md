falsehood:/ˈfɔːlshʊd/| n.|谎言；谬误；虚假|A statement or belief that is untrue. |例句：The politician was accused of spreading falsehoods about his opponent.  这位政客被指控散布关于他对手的谎言。

例句：They exposed the falsehood of his claims. 他们揭露了他的主张的虚假性。

近义词：lie, untruth, fib, fabrication, deception, fallacy|反义词:truth, veracity, reality|词性:noun
