aeromechanical:/ˌɛroʊmɪˈkænɪkəl/
adjective

The aeromechanical properties of the wing are crucial for stable flight.  机翼的空气动力学特性对于稳定飞行至关重要。

The helicopter's aeromechanical systems were meticulously inspected.  直升机的空气机械系统经过了仔细的检查。
