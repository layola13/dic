convertibleness:/kənˌvɜːrtɪblənəs/| n. |可兑换性，可变换性|The quality or state of being convertible; convertibility.  The ease with which something can be changed into another form, especially into money.|The convertibleness of currency is crucial for international trade. 货币的可兑换性对国际贸易至关重要。|Synonyms: convertibility, exchangeability, interchangeability|Antonyms: inconvertibility, non-exchangeability|noun
