ingrafter:/ɪnˈɡræftər/ | n. | 接枝者；嫁接者 |  A person who grafts plants, especially fruit trees.  | The skilled ingrafter carefully joined the scion to the rootstock.  熟练的嫁接者小心地将接穗与砧木连接起来。| synonyms: grafter, implanter | antonyms: N/A | noun

ingraft:/ɪnˈɡræft/ | vt. | 嫁接；移植；灌输 | To insert (a shoot or bud) as a graft; to implant or introduce (something) into something else; to instill (an idea or habit). |  He ingrafted a new variety of apple onto the old tree. 他在老树上嫁接了一个新品种的苹果。 They tried to ingraft new ideas into the minds of the students. 他们试图将新的思想灌输给学生。 | synonyms: graft, implant, instill, inculcate, introduce | antonyms: uproot, remove, excise | verb
