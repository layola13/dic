odorless:/ˈoʊdərləs/| adj.|无味的，没有气味的|Having no odor; without a smell. |例句：The gas is odorless and therefore dangerous. 这种气体无味，因此很危险。|例句：The flowers are beautiful but odorless. 这些花很漂亮，但是没有气味。|近义词：scentless, unscented, inodorous|反义词:fragrant, aromatic, scented|词性:形容词
词源: From 'odor' (smell) + '-less' (without).  Ultimately from Latin 'odor' (smell, fragrance).
