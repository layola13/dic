词源: "Overheartiness" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" with the adjective "hearty" and the suffix "-ness."  "Hearty" comes from Middle English *herty*, from Old English *heorte* (heart) + *-ig* (suffix forming adjectives). The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
