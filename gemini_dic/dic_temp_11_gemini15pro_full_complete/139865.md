oculary:/ˈɒkjʊlə(r)i/ /ˈɑːkjʊleri/ | n. |目镜；目镜的|A piece of lens or combination of lenses nearest the eye in a telescope, microscope, or other optical instrument.|She peered through the ocular of the microscope. 她透过显微镜的目镜观察。|Synonyms: eyepiece, lens|Antonyms: objective (lens) | noun

oculary:/ˈɒkjʊlə(r)i/ /ˈɑːkjʊleri/ | adj. |眼睛的；视觉的|Of or relating to the eye or vision.|Ocular problems can be corrected with glasses. 眼部问题可以用眼镜矫正。|Synonyms: visual, ophthalmic|Antonyms: auditory| adjective
