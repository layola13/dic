nominalised:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 名词化的 |  Converted into a noun or noun phrase.  Transformed from a verb or adjective into a noun. | Example: "Running is good exercise" uses a nominalised form of the verb "run".  跑步是一项很好的运动。 | Example: The nominalisation of "difficult" is "difficulty".  “困难”的名词化是“困难”。 | Synonyms: nouned, substantivised | Antonyms: verbalised | word type: adjective
