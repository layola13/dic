enchasten:/ɪnˈtʃeɪsən/ | v. | 惩戒；抑制；约束；磨炼 | To correct by punishment or reproof; to restrain; to subdue; to chasten. |  He needed to enchasten his impulsive behavior. 他需要抑制自己冲动的行为。

The hardships of life enchastened her. 生活的艰辛磨练了她。 | 近义词: chasten, discipline, subdue, restrain, curb | 反义词: indulge, encourage, liberate | 词性: 动词
