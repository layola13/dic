ectodermal:/ˌɛktəˈdɜːrməl/
adjective
外胚层的
Relating to or derived from the ectoderm, the outermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo.
The ectodermal layer gives rise to the skin and nervous system. 外胚层会发育形成皮肤和神经系统。
The scientist studied the ectodermal development of the frog embryo. 科学家研究了蛙胚胎的外胚层发育。
Synonyms:  epiblastic
Antonyms: endodermal, mesodermal
词性: adjective
