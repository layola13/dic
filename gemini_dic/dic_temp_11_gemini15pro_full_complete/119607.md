midanik: /ˈmɪdənɪk/ | adj. | 醉酒的，酗酒的 |  A Yiddish word meaning "drunkard" or "alcoholic". It describes someone who habitually drinks excessively. | He was a known midanik, often seen stumbling through the streets. 他是一个众所周知的酒鬼，经常被人看到在街上踉踉跄跄。 | 近义词: drunkard, alcoholic, sot, inebriate | 反义词: teetotaler, sober | 词性: 形容词
