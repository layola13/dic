implausibility:/ɪmˌplɔːzəˈbɪləti/
n.|难以置信；不像真的|The implausibility of his story made it difficult to believe.  他的故事令人难以置信，很难让人相信。|
例句：The implausibility of his excuse was obvious to everyone. 他的借口的不可信度对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
例句：The sheer implausibility of the plot made the movie ridiculous.  这情节的纯粹荒谬性使这部电影变得滑稽可笑。
近义词：unlikeliness, incredibility, improbability|
反义词：plausibility, likelihood, credibility|
词性: noun


Etymology:
From im- (not) + plausible (believable) + -ity (forming nouns of quality or state).  Plausible comes from Late Latin plausibilis, from Latin plaudere (to applaud).
