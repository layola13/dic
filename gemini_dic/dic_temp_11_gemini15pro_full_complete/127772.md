nehiloth:/nɛhɪloʊθ/ /nəˈhiːlɒθ/ | n. | 内希罗特（诗篇标题用语，意义不确定）| A term found in the headings of Psalms 5, 53, 88, and 85. Its meaning is uncertain, but it is thought to be a musical term, possibly indicating a wind instrument or a tune. | Example: Psalm 5 is inscribed "To the chief Musician upon Nehiloth, A Psalm of David."  诗篇5题为“交给诗班长，用内希罗特调，大卫的诗”。| 近义词: (无明确近义词，因其含义不确定) | 反义词: (无明确反义词，因其含义不确定) | 词性: 名词
