nonadopters:/ˌnɒnəˈdɒptəz/ | n. | 非采用者，未采用者 | People who choose not to use a particular product, service, or innovation.  | Example: The marketing team needs to understand why some consumers remain nonadopters of our new technology. 市场团队需要了解为什么一些消费者仍然没有采用我们的新技术。| Example: The nonadopters cited cost as the primary reason for not switching to the new system. 未采用者将成本列为不切换到新系统的主要原因。 | Synonyms: laggards, resisters | Antonyms: adopters, users | Noun
