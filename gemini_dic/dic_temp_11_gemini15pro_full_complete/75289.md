fronted:/ˈfrʌntɪd/ | adj. | 前置的，前端的 | placed or moved to the front; having a front or facade of a specified kind. |  The company has a fronted building with large windows.  这家公司的建筑正面有大窗户。| synonyms:  foremost, leading, anterior, forward | antonyms: rear, back, hind, posterior | adjective

fronted:/ˈfrʌntɪd/ | v. |  担任...的负责人，领导，提供资金 | to act as the front for; to provide financial backing for. | A well-known actor fronted the advertising campaign. 一位著名演员领衔了这场广告宣传活动。| synonyms: lead, head, spearhead, finance, fund | antonyms: follow, support, trail | verb

Etymology: From the verb "front" + "-ed" suffix. The verb "front" originates from the Old French "front" meaning "forehead, front," which in turn comes from the Latin "frons" meaning "forehead, brow, front."
