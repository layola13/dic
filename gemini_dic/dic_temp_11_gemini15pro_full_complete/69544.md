fellow:/ˈfeləʊ/ (US) /ˈfeləʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|同伴，同事，家伙，会员； 同伴的，同事的|A person who is in the same position, involved in the same activity, or otherwise associated with another.|He's a good fellow. 他是个好人。|Synonyms: companion, associate, peer, colleague, comrade|Antonyms: enemy, opponent, stranger, foe|Noun, Adjective
