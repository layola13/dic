hypertriglyceridaemic: /ˌhaɪpərˌtraɪɡlɪsəˈraɪdiːmɪk/ | adj. | 高甘油三酯血症的 |  Characterized by elevated levels of triglycerides in the blood.  This condition is often associated with other metabolic disorders and can increase the risk of cardiovascular disease. |  The patient was diagnosed as hypertriglyceridaemic after a routine blood test.  该患者在一次常规血液检查后被诊断为高甘油三酯血症。

The doctor prescribed medication to help manage the patient's hypertriglyceridaemic condition. 医生开了药来帮助控制病人的高甘油三酯血症。 | 近义词: hyperlipidemic | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective

词源:  hyper- (Greek: over, excessive) + tri- (Greek: three) + glyceride (a type of fat) + -emic (suffix indicating a blood condition)
