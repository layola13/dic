aberrantly: /æˈbɛrəntli/
adverb
异常地，反常地
In a way that is not usual or expected; deviating from the normal or correct way.
The cells were dividing aberrantly. 细胞异常分裂。
Synonyms: abnormally, anomalously, atypically, irregularly, unusually, oddly, strangely, peculiarly, exceptionally, freakishly, bizarrely
Antonyms: normally, typically, usually, regularly
词性: 副词
