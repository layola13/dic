overbid:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪd/ /ˌəʊvəˈbɪd/| v.  n.|出价过高，过高出价|To bid more than (something is worth or more than one can afford).|例句：I overbid on the house and now I'm struggling to make the mortgage payments. 我出价过高买了这栋房子，现在难以支付抵押贷款。

例句：She knew she had overbid, but she really wanted the antique vase. 她知道自己出价过高了，但她真的很想要那个古董花瓶。|近义词：outbid, bid too high|反义词:underbid|词性:动词, 名词

overbid:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪd/ /ˌəʊvəˈbɪd/| n. |过高的出价|A bid that is too high.|例句：The winning overbid was far more than the item's actual value.  中标的过高出价远远超过了该物品的实际价值。

例句：His overbid cost him the auction. 他的过高出价让他输掉了拍卖。|近义词：excessive bid|反义词:low bid|词性:名词
