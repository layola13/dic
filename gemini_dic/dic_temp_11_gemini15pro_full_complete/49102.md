deign:/deɪn/
verb
屈尊，俯就
to do something that one considers to be beneath one's dignity
The CEO rarely deigned to speak to ordinary employees.
这位首席执行官很少屈尊与普通员工说话。

近义词:condescend, stoop, vouchsafe
反义词:refuse, decline, snub
词性:v.
