aphelion:/əˈfiːliən/ /æˈfiːliən/
n.
远日点
The point in the orbit of a planet, asteroid, or comet at which it is furthest from the sun.
The comet reached its aphelion and began its long journey back towards the sun.  彗星到达了它的远日点，开始它返回太阳的漫长旅程。
