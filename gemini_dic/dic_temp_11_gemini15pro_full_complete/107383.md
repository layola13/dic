lamenting:/ləˈmentɪŋ/ | adj. | 悲叹的，哀悼的 | Expressing grief or sorrow, usually in a demonstrative way. |  She sat alone, lamenting the loss of her beloved pet. 她独自坐着，哀悼她心爱宠物的离去。|  The lamenting cries of the mourners filled the air.  送葬者的哀号声响彻天空。 |  mourning, grieving, sorrowful, bewailing | rejoicing, celebrating | adjective

lamenting:/ləˈmentɪŋ/ | v. | 悲叹，哀悼 |  Expressing grief or sorrow, usually in a demonstrative way.
