histological:/ˌhɪstəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 组织学的 | Relating to the study of the microscopic structure of tissues. | The histological examination revealed cancerous cells. 组织学检查 revealed cancerous cells. | 近义词: microscopic, tissue-related | 反义词: macroscopic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From "histology" + "-ical."  "Histology" comes from Greek "histos" (tissue) + "logos" (study).
