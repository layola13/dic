Etymology: non- + symbolical + -ness + -es
"Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."
"Symbolical" comes from the Late Latin "symbolicus," meaning "emblematic, representative," from the Greek "symbolicos."
"-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
"-es" is a plural suffix.
