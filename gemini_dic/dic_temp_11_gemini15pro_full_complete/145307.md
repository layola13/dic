overbriefing:/ˌəʊvərˈbriːfɪŋ/
n.
过度指示；过分详细的指示
The act of giving someone too much information or instruction, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency.
The overbriefing before the mission led to confusion among the team.  任务前的过度指示导致了团队的混乱。
Synonyms: overinstructing, overexplaining, information overload
Antonyms: underbriefing, lack of information
noun
