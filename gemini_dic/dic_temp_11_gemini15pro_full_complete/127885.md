neocracy:/niˈɒkrəsi/ | n. | 新政体；新贵统治 | Government by new or inexperienced rulers. A system of government where power is held by those who are new to it, often implying a lack of experience or skill. | The neocracy, inexperienced and idealistic, quickly found themselves overwhelmed by the complexities of governance. 新政体，缺乏经验且理想主义，很快发现自己被治理的复杂性所淹没。| Synonyms: kakistocracy, oligarchy | Antonyms: aristocracy, meritocracy | noun
