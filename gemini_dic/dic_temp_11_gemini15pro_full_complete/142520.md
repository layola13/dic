ossianic:/ˌɒsiˈænɪk/
adjective
奥西恩式的；像奥西恩诗歌的；伤感地怀旧的
Characteristic of or resembling the poems of Ossian, esp. in being melancholy or nostalgic.
The music had an ossianic quality, full of yearning and lament for a lost past.  这音乐有一种奥西恩式的特质，充满了对逝去时光的渴望和哀叹。
Near Synonyms:  romantic, sentimental, nostalgic, melancholic, elegiac
Antonyms:  cheerful, upbeat, optimistic, joyous, realistic
词性: adjective
