lawfulness:/ˈlɔːfəlnəs/ |n.|合法性；守法；依法|The lawfulness of his actions was questioned. 他的行为的合法性受到了质疑。| legality, legitimacy | illegality, unlawfulness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lawefulnes*, equivalent to *laweful* + *-nes*.  *Laweful* from Old English *laguful* (“lawful”), equivalent to *lagu* (“law”) + *-ful*.  *-nes* from Old English *-nes*, *-ness*, from Proto-Germanic *-nassus*, *-nissi* (compare German *-nis*, Dutch *-nis*). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *-(e)nos*.
