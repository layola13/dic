dextrorotary: /ˌdɛkstroʊˈroʊtəri/ /ˌdɛkstrəˈrəʊtəri/ | adj. | 右旋的 | Rotating the plane of polarized light to the right, clockwise. | The solution was dextrorotary, indicating the presence of a specific sugar.  该溶液是右旋的，表明存在一种特定的糖。

The dextrorotary form of glucose is more common in nature.  葡萄糖的右旋形式在自然界中更为常见。 | Near Antonyms: levorotary | Antonyms: laevorotatory | adjective
