accessing:/ˈæksesɪŋ/
verb
访问，获取
The process of retrieving data or information from a computer system or other source.
He is accessing the company's database. 他正在访问公司的数据库。
She is accessing her email account. 她正在访问她的电子邮件账户。
近义词: retrieving, obtaining, using
反义词: blocking, restricting
词性: verb (动词现在分词)

access:/ˈækses/
noun
访问权限，入口
The right or opportunity to use or enter a place, or the way in which you can do this.
Students have access to the library. 学生可以进入图书馆。
The only access to the island is by boat. 到达该岛的唯一途径是乘船。
近义词: entry, admittance, approach
反义词: denial, restriction
词性: noun (名词)

access:/ˈækses/
verb
访问，使用
To obtain or retrieve data or information from a computer system or other source.
I need to access the files on the server. 我需要访问服务器上的文件。
Can you access the internet from your phone? 你可以用手机上网吗？
近义词: obtain, retrieve, use
反义词: block, restrict
词性: verb (动词)
