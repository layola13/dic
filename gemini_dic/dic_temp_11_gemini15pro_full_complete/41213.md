coracle: /ˈkɒrək(ə)l/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrək(ə)l/ (US) | n. | 小圆舟，轻便小船 | A small, round boat made of wickerwork covered with hides or oilcloth, propelled with a paddle. | Example 1: The fisherman used a coracle to navigate the shallow river. 渔夫用小圆舟在浅河中航行。

Example 2: Coracles are still used in some parts of the world for fishing and transportation. 在世界某些地方，小圆舟仍然用于捕鱼和交通。 | Near synonyms:  currach,  small boat,  dinghy | Antonym: ship | Noun
