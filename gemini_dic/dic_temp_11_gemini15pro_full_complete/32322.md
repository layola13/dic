chikungunya:/ˌtʃɪkənˈɡʊnjə/ /ˌtʃɪkənˈɡʌnjə/| n. |基孔肯雅热|A viral disease transmitted by mosquitoes, characterized by fever and severe joint pain.|例句：Chikungunya is spreading rapidly in the Caribbean. 基孔肯雅热正在加勒比地区迅速蔓延。

例句：She was diagnosed with chikungunya after returning from her trip. 她旅行回来后被诊断出患有基孔肯雅热。|近义词：breakbone fever (colloquial), dengue (similar symptoms)|反义词: (None - diseases don't typically have antonyms) |词性:noun


Etymology:
From the Makonde language of Tanzania and Mozambique, meaning "that which bends up" or "to become contorted," referring to the stooped posture of sufferers due to the severe joint pain.
