gantry: /ˈɡæntri/ (US), /ˈɡɑːntri/ (UK)| n.|龙门架；高架起重机；（火箭发射的）发射塔架|A gantry is a metal framework that supports a crane, railway signals, or other equipment.| The rocket was positioned on the gantry ready for launch. 火箭被安置在发射塔架上，准备发射。|Synonyms: framework, scaffold, crane, hoist|Antonyms: N/A|noun

gantry: /ˈɡæntri/ (US), /ˈɡɑːntri/ (UK)| adj.|龙门式的；高架的|A gantry crane is a crane built atop a gantry, which is a structure used to span an area.| The gantry crane lifted the heavy container off the ship. 龙门起重机将沉重的集装箱从船上吊起。|Synonyms: overhead, elevated|Antonyms: ground-level, low|adjective
