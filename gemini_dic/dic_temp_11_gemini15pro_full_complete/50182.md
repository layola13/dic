deoppilative:/diːˈɒpɪlətɪv/
adjective
通气的；开窍的；疏通的
Removing obstructions; opening the pores; relieving constipation.
This herbal tea is known for its deoppilative properties, helping to clear congestion. 这种草药茶以其通气的特性而闻名，有助于清除充血。
The medication acts as a deoppilative, clearing blocked airways. 这种药物起到通气的作用，清除阻塞的气道。
synonyms:aperient, laxative, cathartic, purgative
antonyms:obstructive, constipating
word type:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēoppilātīvus, from dēoppilāre (“to deoppilate”), from dē- (“de-”) + oppilāre (“to stop up, obstruct”), from ob- (“ob-”) + *pillāre, possibly related to pīla (“pillar, pier”).
