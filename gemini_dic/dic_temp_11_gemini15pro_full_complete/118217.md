meracious:/məˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
部分的；不完全的；有缺陷的
Having merits and faults; imperfect.
例句：His meracious performance was marred by several errors. 他不完美的演奏被几个错误破坏了。

例句：The meracious plan had some good points but also significant flaws.  这个有缺陷的计划有一些优点，但也存在重大缺陷。

近义词：imperfect, flawed, deficient, incomplete
反义词：perfect, flawless, complete, whole
词性：adjective
