nutlet:/ˈnʌtlɪt/ | n. | 小坚果; 小核果 | A small nut, especially one of the several parts into which certain fruits divide.  A dry, indehiscent, one-seeded fruit. | The mint family is characterized by its aromatic properties and its fruit, which typically breaks into four nutlets upon drying.  薄荷科的特征是其芳香特性及其果实，果实通常在干燥时会分裂成四个小坚果。 | The nutlets of the plant are dispersed by wind.  这种植物的小坚果靠风传播。 | synonyms: nut, achene, seed | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a botanical term like nutlet) | noun
