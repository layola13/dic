huddlingly:/ˈhʌdlɪŋli/
adv.
拥挤地，挤作一团地
in a manner characterized by huddling; close together, as if for warmth or protection.
The children huddled together huddlingly in the cold. 孩子们在寒冷中挤作一团。
近义词:closely, snugly, tightly
反义词:loosely, separately, spread out
词性:副词

词源: From huddle + -ing + -ly. Huddle comes from the Low German "huddeln" meaning "to conceal or cover up."
