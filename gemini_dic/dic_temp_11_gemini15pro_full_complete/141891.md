ordains:/ɔːrˈdeɪnz/| vt.|规定；命令；任命（神职人员）|to establish or decree by law, edict, or command; to invest with ministerial or priestly functions|The church ordains women as priests. 教会任命妇女为牧师。|近义词：decree, command, appoint, consecrate|反义词：forbid, prohibit|词性：动词
Etymology: From Old French *ordener*, from Latin *ōrdināre* "to set in order, arrange, regulate," from *ōrdō* "row, rank, order."
