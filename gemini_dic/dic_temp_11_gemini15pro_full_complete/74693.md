freeganism:/ˈfriːɡənɪzəm/
n.
拾荒主义；免费素食主义
Freeganism is the practice of reclaiming and eating food that has been discarded.
Freeganism is a philosophy of limited participation in the conventional economy and minimal consumption of resources, particularly through recovering wasted goods like food.  Freegans often dumpster dive for food.

例句：
Freeganism is a growing movement in some urban areas.
在一些城市地区，拾荒主义正在兴起。

Freeganism is one way to reduce food waste.
拾荒主义是减少食物浪费的一种方式。


近义词：anti-consumerism, dumpster diving
反义词:consumerism
词性:noun
