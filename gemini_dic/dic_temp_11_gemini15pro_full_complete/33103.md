christi:/ˈkrɪsti/| n.|基督徒|A follower of Christianity.|例句：He is a devout Christi. 他是一位虔诚的基督徒。

例句：Early Christis faced persecution. 早期的基督徒面临迫害。|近义词：Christian|反义词:non-Christian, pagan|词性:名词

词源: From Latin Christus, from Ancient Greek Χριστός (Khristós), meaning "anointed one," a translation of the Hebrew מָשִׁיחַ (māšîaḥ, “messiah”).
