keyboarding:/ˈkiːbɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. |键盘输入, 键盘操作 |The act of typing or entering data using a keyboard. |Example: Keyboarding skills are essential in today's digital world.  键盘输入技能在当今的数字世界中至关重要。|Example:  She improved her keyboarding speed through practice. 她通过练习提高了键盘输入速度。|Synonym: typing, data entry |Antonym: handwriting, dictation |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From keyboard + -ing.  Keyboard itself comes from key (a device for operating a lock or other mechanism) + board (a flat piece of wood or other rigid material).
