overcommends:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmendz/
verb
过度推荐；过分赞扬
to praise someone or something too much
The manager overcommends his employees, making it difficult to distinguish truly exceptional performance.  经理过度赞扬他的员工，使得很难区分真正出色的表现。
He overcommends the restaurant, setting unrealistic expectations. 他过分赞扬那家餐馆，设定了不切实际的期望。
近义词: overpraise, exaggerate
反义词: understate, downplay
词性:v.
