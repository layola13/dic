orthopercussion:/ˌɔːrθoʊpərˈkʌʃən/
noun
叩诊法
A method of physical examination involving percussion directly on the body surface, typically using a finger as a plexor.
The doctor used orthopercussion to assess the patient's lung condition. 医生使用叩诊法评估病人的肺部状况。
近义词: percussion
反义词: (No direct antonym for a diagnostic method)
