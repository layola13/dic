anaesthetically:/ˌænɪsˈθetɪkli/ | adv. |麻醉地；失去知觉地|In a way that produces or relates to anaesthesia; numbly. |The patient was anaesthetically prepared for surgery. 病人被麻醉准备手术。| 近义词: numbly, insensibly | 反义词: sensitively, consciously | 词性: 副词

anaesthetically:/ˌænɪsˈθetɪkli/ | adv. |麻醉地；失去知觉地|Relating to or resembling the effects of an anesthetic. |He spoke anaesthetically, his voice flat and emotionless. 他说话的声音麻木而毫无感情，就像打了麻药一样。| 近义词: dully, lifelessly | 反义词: alertly, responsively | 词性: 副词
