high-technologies:/ˌhaɪ tekˈnɒlədʒiz/ |n.| 高科技；尖端技术 |High-technologies are transforming the way we live and work. 高科技正在改变我们的生活和工作方式。|  The company invests heavily in high-technologies. 这家公司在高科技方面投入巨资。| 近义词: advanced technologies, cutting-edge technologies | 反义词: low-technologies, outdated technologies | 词性: 名词

high-technology:/ˌhaɪ tekˈnɒlədʒi/ |adj.| 高科技的；尖端技术的 | The high-technology industry is booming. 高科技产业正在蓬勃发展。| This is a high-technology product with many advanced features. 这是一款具有许多先进功能的高科技产品。 | 近义词: advanced, cutting-edge, sophisticated | 反义词: low-tech, outdated, primitive | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "high" + "technology". "High" here means advanced or superior.  "Technology" comes from the Greek words "tekhnē" (art, skill, craft) and "logia" (study of).
