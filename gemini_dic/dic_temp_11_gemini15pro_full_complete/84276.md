half-tearfulnesses /ˌhæfˈtɪərfʊlnəsɪz/
|n.|
|半哭半笑的状态; 欲哭无泪的状态|
|A state of being partially tearful, often suppressed or restrained; a feeling of being on the verge of tears, but not fully expressing them.  It suggests a mixture of sadness and other emotions, potentially including happiness or relief.|
|She exhibited a strange half-tearfulness throughout the ceremony, a blend of joy and sorrow.|她整个仪式都表现出一种奇怪的半哭半笑的状态，一种喜悦和悲伤的交织。

|He swallowed, a sense of half-tearfulness rising in his chest as he accepted the award.|他咽了咽口水，一种欲哭无泪的感觉涌上心头，他接受了这个奖项。
|near-tears, weepiness, wistfulness|
|unfeelingness, coldness|
|noun|
