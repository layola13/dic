nonlinearity:/ˌnɒnˌlɪnɪˈærɪti/ | n. | 非线性 | The quality or state of being nonlinear.  A system or function that does not have a linear relationship between input and output. |  The nonlinearity of the system makes it difficult to predict its behavior.  这个系统的非线性使得很难预测它的行为。 | nonlinear behavior, deviation from linearity | linearity | noun
