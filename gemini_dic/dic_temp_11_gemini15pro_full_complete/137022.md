nonstandardized:/ˌnɒnˈstændədaɪzd/ /ˌnɑːnˈstændərdaɪzd/| adj.|非标准化的，未标准化的|Not conforming to a standard; not standardized.  Something that is nonstandardized hasn't been made to comply with a particular standard or norm.|The nonstandardized tests made it difficult to compare student performance across schools.  这些非标准化的测试使得比较不同学校学生的表现变得困难。|non-standard, unstandardized|standard, standardized|adjective
