monochromatic:/ˌmɒnəkrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 单色的，单频的 | Containing or using only one color or wavelength of light. | The artist created a monochromatic painting using only shades of blue. 艺术家只用蓝色调创作了一幅单色画。 |  The room was decorated in a monochromatic scheme of grey. 房间以灰色的单色方案装饰。 | 近义词: monotone, single-colored | 反义词: polychromatic, multicolored | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek *μόνος (mónos, “alone, single”) + χρῶμα (khrôma, “color”).
