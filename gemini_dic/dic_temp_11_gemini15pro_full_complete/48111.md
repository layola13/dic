declinometer:/ˌdɛklɪˈnɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌdeɪklɪˈnɒmɪtər/ (UK)| n. |测斜仪，磁偏角仪|An instrument used for measuring the angle of a slope, dip, or magnetic declination.|Geologists used a declinometer to measure the dip of the rock strata. 地质学家使用测斜仪测量岩层的倾角。|
The surveyor used a declinometer to determine the slope of the land. 测量员用测斜仪来确定土地的坡度。|
Synonyms: inclinometer, tiltmeter, dip circle|
Antonyms: N/A|
