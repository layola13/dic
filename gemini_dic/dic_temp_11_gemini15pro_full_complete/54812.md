dispersal:/dɪˈspɜːrsl/
n.
散布，分散，传播
the action or process of distributing things or people over a wide area
例句：The dispersal of seeds is crucial for plant reproduction.  种子的散布对于植物繁殖至关重要。

例句：The police ordered the dispersal of the crowd. 警察命令人群 dispersal。

近义词：distribution, scattering, dissemination
反义词：concentration, gathering, accumulation
词性：名词


disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/
v.
分散，散开，消散
distribute or spread over a wide area
例句：The crowd dispersed quickly after the concert.  演唱会结束后，人群迅速散去。

例句：The wind dispersed the smoke. 风吹散了烟雾。

近义词：scatter, disseminate, distribute
反义词：gather, collect, congregate
词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere "to scatter," from dis- "apart" + spargere "to strew, scatter".
