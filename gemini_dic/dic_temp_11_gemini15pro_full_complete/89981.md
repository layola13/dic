horsewomen:/ˈhɔːrsˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女骑手；骑马的女人
A woman who rides horses, especially as a profession or sport.
The horsewomen gathered at the stables before the competition. 女骑手们在比赛前聚集在马厩里。
The horsewomen were skilled in dressage. 这些女骑手精通盛装舞步。
近义词：female riders, equestriennes
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly horsemen)
词性：noun
