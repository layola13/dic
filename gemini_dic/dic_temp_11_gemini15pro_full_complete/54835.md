dispiriting:/dɪˈspɪrɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人沮丧的，使人泄气的
Causing someone to lose enthusiasm and hope; disheartening.
The news of the team's loss was dispiriting.  球队输球的消息令人沮丧。
近义词: disheartening, discouraging, demoralizing, depressing
反义词: encouraging, heartening, inspiring, uplifting
词性: adjective

dispirit:/dɪˈspɪrɪt/
verb
使沮丧，使泄气
To make someone lose enthusiasm and hope.
The constant rain dispirited the campers.  连绵不断的雨使露营者感到沮丧。
近义词: discourage, demoralize, depress
反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire
词性: verb
