fandango:/ˌfænˈdæŋɡoʊ/
n.| 活泼的西班牙舞；喧闹的活动|A lively, spirited Spanish dance, typically performed by a man and a woman, characterized by castanets, hand clapping, and intricate footwork. Also, can refer to any lively or exciting activity.|They danced a passionate fandango.|近义词：flamenco, bolero, dance, celebration|反义词:boredom, stillness|词性:noun (plural)
