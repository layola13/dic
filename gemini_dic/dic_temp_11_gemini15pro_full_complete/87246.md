hereto:/ˌhɪəˈtuː/ | adv. | 至此，在此 |  To this place, document, or point in time.  | Example: The parties hereto agree to the following terms.  双方在此同意以下条款。 | Example: Attached hereto is a copy of the contract.  合同副本附在此处。 | Synonym: herein, herewith | Antonym: therefrom, thence | word class: Adverb
