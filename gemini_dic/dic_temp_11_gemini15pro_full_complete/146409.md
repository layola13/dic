overfocussed:/ˌoʊvərˈfəʊkəst/ | adj. | 过于专注的，过度聚焦的 | Concentrating too intensely on one thing, often to the detriment of other things. | Example: He was so overfocussed on the details that he missed the bigger picture. 他过于专注于细节，反而忽略了全局。

Example:  She became overfocussed on her work and neglected her family. 她过度专注于工作，忽略了她的家人。 | 近义词: obsessed, fixated, preoccupied | 反义词: distracted, inattentive | 词性: 形容词
