gearings:/ˈɡɪərɪŋz/
n.
传动装置，齿轮装置，(尤指公司的)资本 gearing ratio
The system of gears in a machine or vehicle, especially in a car. Also, the ratio of a company's debt to its equity.
The car's gearings were damaged in the accident. 这辆车的传动装置在事故中损坏了。
High gearings can increase a company's risk. 高的资本负债率会增加公司的风险。
synonyms: gears, transmission, leverage
antonyms: N/A
noun
