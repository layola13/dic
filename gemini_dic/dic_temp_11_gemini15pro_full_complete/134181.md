nonincitement:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsaɪtmənt/
noun
非煽动;不煽动
The absence of incitement; the act of not inciting violence, hatred, or illegal activity.
The prosecutor stressed the defendant's nonincitement to violence during the protest.  检察官强调被告在抗议期间没有煽动暴力。
His speech focused on peaceful solutions and nonincitement. 他的演讲集中在和平解决问题和不煽动。
近义词:pacification, appeasement, conciliation, mediation
反义词:incitement, provocation, agitation
