forspent:/fɔːrˈspɛnt/
adjective
筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的
Utterly exhausted; worn out.
The forspent marathon runners collapsed at the finish line.  筋疲力尽的马拉松运动员在终点线倒下了。
The forspent travelers finally reached their destination.  疲惫不堪的旅行者终于到达了目的地。
Near synonyms: exhausted, worn out, spent, drained, fatigued
Near antonyms: energetic, invigorated, refreshed
word class: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forspenten, from Old English forspendan (“to spend, use up, consume, exhaust”), equivalent to for- +‎ spend. Cognate with Dutch verspenden (“to squander, waste”), German verspenden (“to donate, give away”), Swedish förspenda (“to squander”).
