accountants:/əˈkaʊntənts/
n.
会计师；会计人员
A person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts.
The accountants prepared the company's annual report.  会计师们准备了公司的年度报告。

The firm hired a team of accountants to audit their finances.  这家公司聘请了一组会计师来审计他们的财务状况。

近义词:bookkeepers, auditors
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与accountants职责相反的职业，例如：salespeople)
词性:noun (plural)

词源: From Old French *accontant, present participle of aconter (“to count, render account”), from a (“to”) + conter (“to count”).
