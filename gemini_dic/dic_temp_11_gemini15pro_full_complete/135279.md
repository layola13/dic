nonparochial:/ˌnɒnpəˈrəʊkiəl/ | adj. | 非教区的；不狭隘的；开放的 | Not limited to a narrow or local perspective; broad-minded; inclusive. |  Example: The school adopted a nonparochial approach to education, welcoming students from all backgrounds. 这所学校采取了非教区式的教育方法，欢迎来自不同背景的学生。

Example:  Her nonparochial views on social issues made her a valuable member of the committee. 她对社会问题的开放观点使她成为委员会的宝贵成员。 | 近义词: broad-minded, liberal, tolerant, open-minded, ecumenical, cosmopolitan | 反义词: parochial, narrow-minded, insular, provincial | 词性: adjective
