disburse: /dɪsˈbɜːrs/ /dɪsˈbɜːrs/ | v. |支付，支出，拨款 | To pay out (money) from a fund. | The government disbursed millions of dollars in aid. 政府支付了数百万美元的援助款。| Synonyms: expend, pay out, distribute, spend | Antonyms: receive, collect, deposit | verb

disbursement: /dɪsˈbɜːrsmənt/ /dɪsˈbɜːrsmənt/ | n. |支付，支出，拨款 | The act of paying out money, especially from a public or other fund. | The company recorded a large disbursement for research and development. 公司记录了一大笔研发支出。| Synonyms: expenditure, payment, outlay | Antonyms: receipt, income, collection | noun
