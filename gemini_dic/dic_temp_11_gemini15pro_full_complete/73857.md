formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪt/ (US) /ˈfɔːmjʊleɪt/ (UK)| v. |制定；规划；构想；确切地表达|to create or devise methodically (a strategy or a proposal). To express (an idea or plan) in a clear or definite form.|The government is formulating new economic policies. 政府正在制定新的经济政策。|synonyms: devise, conceive, frame, articulate, express|antonyms:  improvise, neglect, disregard|verb
