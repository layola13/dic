besetter:/bɪˈsɛtər/
n.|困扰者，折磨者|Someone or something that besets or troubles someone or something.|The company was beset by financial difficulties, and its besetters were numerous.|近义词:afflicter, tormentor, harasser, annoyer, attacker, assailant|反义词:helper, supporter, comforter, rescuer|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "beset" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Beset" comes from the Old English "besettan," meaning "to surround," from "be-" (around) + "settan" (to set).
