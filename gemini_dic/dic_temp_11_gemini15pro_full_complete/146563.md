overgrade:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪd/| vt.|过高评价，高估；给予…过高的等级|To assign a higher grade or value to something than it deserves. |The teacher accidentally overgraded the student's essay. 老师不小心给学生的作文打了过高的分数。

The wine was overgraded and sold for more than it was worth.  这种葡萄酒被高估了，售价超过了它的实际价值。|近义词：overestimate, overvalue, exaggerate|反义词: underestimate, undervalue, downgrade|词性:动词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "grade" (verb meaning "to assign a grade or value to something")
