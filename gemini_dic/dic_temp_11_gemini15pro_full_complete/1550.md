achiever:/əˈtʃiːvər/| n. |成就者，完成者|A person who achieves something notable or substantial, especially success.|例句：He is a high achiever in his field. 他是该领域的佼佼者。|例句：She is a natural achiever and excels at everything she does. 她是一个天生的成功者，擅长她做的每一件事。|近义词：winner, successor, high-flyer|反义词:failure, loser|词性:名词

**词源:**

From the verb "achieve," which comes from Old French "achever" (to finish, complete, achieve), from "a-" (to) + "chef" (head, end). Ultimately from Latin "ad caput" (to the head, end).
