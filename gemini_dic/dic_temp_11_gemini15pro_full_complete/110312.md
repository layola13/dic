light-weight:/ˈlaɪtˌweɪt/| adj. n.|轻量级的，轻量级选手|Of low weight; not heavy.  Also, a person of less than average weight, especially a boxer, wrestler, etc.|This is a light-weight suitcase, perfect for traveling. 这是一个轻便的行李箱，非常适合旅行。|Synonyms: portable, featherweight, flimsy|Antonyms: heavy, heavyweight, bulky|adjective

light-weight:/ˈlaɪtˌweɪt/| n.|轻量级选手|A person of less than average weight, especially a boxer, wrestler, etc.|He's a light-weight in the boxing world. 他是拳击界的轻量级选手。|Synonyms: bantamweight, featherweight|Antonyms: heavyweight|noun
