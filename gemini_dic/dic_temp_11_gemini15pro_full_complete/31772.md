checkage:/ˈtʃɛkɪdʒ/
noun
检查; 查验; 核对; 检验费
The process of checking or verifying something.  The fee charged for this process.
The checkage of the luggage took a long time. 行李的检查花了很长时间。
There is a checkage fee for this service. 这项服务需要支付检验费。
Synonyms: inspection, verification, examination, audit
Antonyms: neglect, disregard
