clay-like:/ˈkleɪˌlaɪk/
adjective
像黏土的；似黏土的
Resembling clay in texture, consistency, or appearance.
The soil was clay-like and difficult to dig. 土壤像黏土一样，很难挖。
The sculptor molded the clay-like substance into a beautiful form.  雕塑家将这种类似黏土的物质塑造成美丽的形状。
近义词: clayey, argillaceous
反义词: sandy, gravelly
词性: adjective
