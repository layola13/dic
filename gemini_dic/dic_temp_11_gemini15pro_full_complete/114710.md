malleal:/ˈmæliəl/| adj.|锤骨的，有关锤骨的|Relating to the malleus, the outermost of the three small bones in the middle ear.|The malleal ligament connects the malleus to the tympanic membrane.  锤骨韧带将锤骨连接到鼓膜。|Near synonyms: tympanic, auditory|Antonyms: None|Adjective
