overarousal: /ˌoʊvərəˈraʊzəl/ | n. | 过度兴奋，过度刺激 | A state of heightened physiological and emotional activity, often experienced as anxiety, agitation, or irritability.  It occurs when the nervous system is overly stimulated and can lead to difficulty focusing, sleeping, and managing emotions. |  The child's overarousal manifested as hyperactivity and difficulty concentrating.  这个孩子的过度兴奋表现为多动和注意力不集中。

The loud noises and bright lights caused overarousal in the sensitive child.  喧闹的声音和明亮的灯光导致这个敏感的孩子过度兴奋。 | 近义词: hyperstimulation, overstimulation | 反义词: calmness, tranquility | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "arousal" (from "arouse," meaning to awaken or excite)
