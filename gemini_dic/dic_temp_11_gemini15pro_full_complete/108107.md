laths:/læθs/ /læðz/| n.|板条，狭板，屋顶板条|Thin strips of wood, especially used as a base for plaster or tiles.|The laths were nailed to the rafters before the tiles were put on. 屋顶板条在瓦片铺设之前被钉在椽子上。|The plaster was applied over laths of wood.  灰泥被涂抹在木板条上。|synonyms: slats, strips, battens|antonyms: beam, plank|noun
