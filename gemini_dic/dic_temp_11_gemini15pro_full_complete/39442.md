connection:/kəˈnɛkʃən/ | n. | 连接，联系，关系，连接点 | a relationship in which a person, thing, or idea is linked or associated with something else. |  I lost my internet connection. 我失去了网络连接。

There is a strong connection between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间有很强的联系。 | synonyms: link, association, relationship, bond, tie, affiliation | antonyms: disconnection, separation, division | noun


connection:/kəˈnɛkt/ | v. | 连接，联系 | to join or fasten something to something else | Please connect the printer to the computer. 请将打印机连接到电脑。

The police connected him with the robbery. 警方把他和抢劫案联系起来。 | synonyms: join, link, attach, unite, combine | antonyms: disconnect, separate, detach | verb
