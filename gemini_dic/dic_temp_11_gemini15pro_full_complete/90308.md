house:/haʊs/| n. vt.|房子，住宅；容纳；为…提供住处|A building for people to live in.  |例句：My house is near the park. 我的房子在公园附近。

|近义词：residence, dwelling, home, abode|反义词:homelessness|词性:名词,动词

词源：Old English hūs "dwelling, shelter, house, home," from Proto-Germanic *husan (source also of Old Saxon, Old Frisian hus, Old Norse hūs, Dutch huis, German Haus), from PIE *ku- "to swell, arch, vault" (source also of Sanskrit kosthas "storehouse, receptacle," Latin custos "guardian"), in reference to a vaulted or arched structure. The verb sense is from late 14c.
