oddness:/ˈɒdnəs/ (US) /ˈɑːdnəs/| n. |古怪；奇异；奇特|The quality of being strange or unusual.|There was a certain oddness about him that she couldn't put her finger on. 她觉得他有点古怪，但又说不上来具体是什么。|Synonyms: strangeness, peculiarity, eccentricity, quirkiness, bizarreness|Antonyms: normality, usualness, ordinariness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From odd + -ness.  "Odd" comes from Old Norse "oddi" meaning "third point," referring to the third point of a triangle, hence something sticking out or unusual.
