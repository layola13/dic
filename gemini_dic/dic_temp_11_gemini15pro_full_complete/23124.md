bourgeoisifies:/ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfaɪz/
verb
使资产阶级化；使中产阶级化
To cause to become bourgeois; to give a middle-class character to.
The new shopping mall bourgeoisified the formerly working-class neighborhood.  这个新的购物中心使以前工人阶级的社区中产阶级化了。
The advertising campaign bourgeoisified the image of the rugged outdoorsman.  这个广告活动使粗犷的户外运动者的形象变得中产阶级化了。
Synonyms: gentrify, upgrade, refine
Antonyms: proletarianize, radicalize
词性: 动词
