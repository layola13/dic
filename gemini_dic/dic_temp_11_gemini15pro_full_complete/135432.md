nonpersistency:/ˌnɑːnpərˈsɪstənsi/ |n.| 非持久性，不持续性 |Lack of persistence; failure to continue or endure.  It refers to the cessation of a behavior or treatment, especially in a medical context. |The nonpersistency of the patient with her medication led to a relapse. 病人没有坚持服药导致病情复发。|Synonyms:  discontinuation, lapse, relapse, abandonment |Antonyms: persistence, continuance, perseverance| noun

nonpersistent:/ˌnɑːnpərˈsɪstənt/ |adj.| 非持久的，不持续的|Not persistent; not continuing or enduring.  It describes something that doesn't last or continue, often used in a medical or scientific context. |The nonpersistent nature of the symptoms made diagnosis difficult.  症状的不持续性使诊断变得困难。|Synonyms:  intermittent, temporary, transient, ephemeral |Antonyms: persistent, continuous, enduring, permanent| adjective
