nuclein:/ˈnjuːkliɪn/ | n. | 核素，核蛋白 | A complex organic substance, present in the nuclei of cells, consisting of a protein and a nucleic acid (DNA or RNA).  It was discovered by Friedrich Miescher in 1869. |  Miescher's discovery of nuclein was a landmark in the understanding of heredity.  米歇尔对核素的发现是遗传学理解上的一个里程碑。 | synonyms:  chromatin, nuclear protein | antonyms: cytoplasm | noun
