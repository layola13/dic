indefectibility:/ˌɪndɪˌfɛktɪˈbɪlɪti/
noun
无错误；无缺点；完美
The quality of being free from defect or failing.
The Pope’s pronouncements on matters of faith are believed by Catholics to have the quality of indefectibility.  教皇就信仰问题发表的声明被天主教徒认为具有无谬误性。
The lawyer argued for the indefectibility of his client's alibi.  律师辩称其当事人的不在场证明是无懈可击的。
infallibility, impeccability, faultlessness
fallibility, defectiveness
