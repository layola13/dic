cryogenically:/ˌkraɪəˈdʒɛnɪkli/ | adv. | 低温地；冷冻地 | In a way that involves very low temperatures. | The samples were cryogenically frozen for preservation.  这些样本被低温冷冻保存。|  The food was cryogenically stored to maintain its freshness.  食物被低温储存以保持新鲜。 | 近义词: at low temperatures, under cryogenic conditions | 反义词: at high temperatures, at room temperature | 词性: 副词

cryogen:/ˈkraɪədʒən/ | n. | 冷冻剂；制冷剂 | A substance used to produce very low temperatures. | Liquid nitrogen is a common cryogen used in scientific research. 液氮是科学研究中常用的冷冻剂。 |  They used a powerful cryogen to freeze the biological samples. 他们使用了一种强力冷冻剂来冷冻生物样本。 | 近义词: refrigerant, coolant | 反义词: heating agent | 词性: 名词

cryogenics:/ˌkraɪəˈdʒɛnɪks/ | n. | 低温学 | The branch of physics that deals with the production and effects of very low temperatures. |  Cryogenics plays a vital role in preserving biological materials. 低温学在保存生物材料方面起着至关重要的作用。 | He specialized in cryogenics and its applications in medicine. 他专攻低温学及其在医学中的应用。 | 近义词: low-temperature physics | 反义词: high-temperature physics | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Greek *kryos* (frost, ice) + *-genic* (producing).
