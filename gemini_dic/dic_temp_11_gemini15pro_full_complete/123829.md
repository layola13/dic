morainic:/məˈreɪnɪk/
adj.
冰碛的；由冰碛形成的
Relating to, consisting of, or resembling a moraine.
The morainic hills blocked the valley. 冰碛丘陵挡住了山谷。
近义词:glacial, icy
反义词:non-glacial
