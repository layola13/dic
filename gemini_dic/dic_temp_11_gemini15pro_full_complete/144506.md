outtraded:/ˌaʊtˈtreɪdɪd/
verb
胜过交易; 在交易方面超过
surpassed in trading; performed better in trading than someone else
例句：The small company outtraded its larger competitors. 这家小公司在交易方面胜过了规模更大的竞争对手。

例句：Despite their size, they were outtraded by a newer, more agile firm. 尽管他们的规模很大，但他们在交易方面却被一家更新、更灵活的公司超越了。

近义词：outperformed, bested, surpassed
反义词：underperformed

词性:v.

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "to surpass" or "exceed") + "trade" (verb meaning "to buy and sell goods or services").  The "-ed" ending signifies the past tense or past participle form of the verb.
