fissipedal:/fɪˈsɪpɪdəl/
adjective
裂足的；趾裂的
Having separate toes or digits, especially relating to animals.  Characterized by having toes or digits that are separated or cleft.
The fissipedal feet of the tree frog allow it to grip branches easily.  树蛙的裂足使其能够轻松抓住树枝。

近义词: toed, digitate
反义词: webbed

词性: adjective
