nonreciprocities:/ˌnɒnˌresɪˈprɒsɪtiz/
|n.|非互惠性，非对等性
|The lack of reciprocity or the state of being nonreciprocal; instances where reciprocity does not exist or is not practiced.|
|The trade nonreciprocities between the two countries have led to escalating tensions.|两国之间的贸易不对等导致紧张局势升级。
|近义词:asymmetry, imbalance, inequality, disproportion|
|反义词:reciprocity, mutuality, symmetry, balance|
|词性:noun|
