direct:/dəˈrɛkt/ /daɪˈrɛkt/| adj.  v.  adv.|直接的，直率的；指挥，指导；指示方向|The flight is direct to New York. 这趟航班直飞纽约。|近义词：straight, straightforward, guide, instruct, manage, control|反义词：indirect, circuitous, roundabout|词性:形容词

direct:/dəˈrɛkt/ /daɪˈrɛkt/| v.|指挥，指导；指示方向|He directed the building of the new bridge. 他指挥建造了这座新桥。|近义词：guide, instruct, manage, control|反义词：misdirect, mislead|词性:动词

direct:/dəˈrɛkt/ /daɪˈrɛkt/| adv.|直接地，径直地|I went direct to the manager. 我径直去找了经理。|近义词：straight, immediately|反义词：indirectly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *directus*, past participle of *dirigere* "to set straight, guide," from *dis-* "apart" + *regere* "to guide, rule."
