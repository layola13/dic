hector:/ˈhɛktər/ | v. | 霸凌，威吓，欺侮 | to bully or intimidate someone; to harass or badger persistently | The older boys hectored the younger ones into giving them their lunch money.  那些大男孩威吓小男孩，让他们交出午餐钱。 | bully, intimidate, harass, badger, pester | encourage, support | verb

hector:/ˈhɛktər/ | n. | 恃强凌弱者，威吓者 | a bully; someone who intimidates or harasses others | He was a real hector, always picking on the smaller kids. 他真是个恃强凌弱者，总是欺负小孩子。 | bully, intimidator | supporter, encourager | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hector, the Trojan hero in Homer's Iliad, known for his courage and strength, but whose name became associated with bullying behavior.
