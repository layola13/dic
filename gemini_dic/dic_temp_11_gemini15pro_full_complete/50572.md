deracinated:/dɪˈræsɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
中文翻译：连根拔起的，背井离乡的；无根的
英文解析：Uprooted from one's native land or environment; alienated or displaced culturally.  It implies a loss of connection to one's roots, traditions, and sense of belonging.
例句：The deracinated refugees struggled to adapt to their new surroundings.  背井离乡的难民努力适应新的环境。

例句：The novel explores the psychological effects of being deracinated from one's homeland. 这部小说探讨了背井离乡对心理的影响。

近义词：uprooted, displaced, exiled, alienated, estranged
反义词：rooted, settled, established, indigenous, native
词性：adjective
