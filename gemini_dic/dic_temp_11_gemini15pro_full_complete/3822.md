agone:/əˈɡoʊn/| adv.| 以前，以往|Ago; past. Used chiefly in poetry or elevated prose.  It emphasizes the passage of a significant amount of time.|例句：It happened many years agone. 许多年前发生的事。|近义词：ago, past, since, previously|反义词:hence, later|词性:副词

Etymology: From Middle English *agon, from Old English *āgān (attested only in ongon "gone by, past"). Equivalent to a- +‎ gone.
