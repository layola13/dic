mirrors:/ˈmɪrərz/ (US) /ˈmɪrəz/ (UK)| n. |镜子;反映;反射|A surface, typically of glass coated with a metal amalgam, that reflects images.|The dancer practiced in front of the mirrors. 舞蹈演员在镜子前练习。|synonyms: reflectors, looking glasses|antonyms: absorbers|noun

mirrors:/ˈmɪrərz/ (US) /ˈmɪrəz/ (UK)| v. |反映;反射;模仿|To show an image of; to reflect, as in a mirror; to imitate or copy.|Her behavior mirrors her mother's. 她的行为反映了她母亲的行为。|synonyms: reflects, imitates, echoes|antonyms: distorts, misrepresents|verb
