nonprobable:/ˌnɒnˈprɒbəbl/ | adj. | 不可能的，未必发生的 | Not likely to happen or be true; improbable. | The success of the project seemed nonprobable given the lack of funding.  鉴于缺乏资金，该项目的成功似乎不太可能。|  It's nonprobable that he'll finish on time. 他不太可能按时完成。 | 近义词: improbable, unlikely, doubtful | 反义词: probable, likely, possible | 词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + probable, from Latin probabilis "provable, credible," from probare "to prove, test."
