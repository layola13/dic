enorthotrope:/ɛnˈɔːrθəˌtroʊp/| n. |正交各向异性材料|A material exhibiting enorthotropy, meaning it has different mechanical properties in three mutually perpendicular directions. It's a specific type of orthotropy where one of the principal directions is aligned with the normal to a plane of material symmetry.|The composite material used in the aircraft wing is an enorthotrope, designed to withstand different stresses in flight. 这架飞机机翼使用的复合材料是正交各向异性材料，旨在承受飞行中的不同应力。|近义词：orthotropic material|反义词: isotropic material|词性:noun
