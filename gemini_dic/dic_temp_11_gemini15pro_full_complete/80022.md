PHRASE->
go through with something:  完成某事
例句:  Despite her fears, she decided to go through with the operation. 尽管害怕，她还是决定做手术。

go through something with a fine-tooth comb: 仔细检查某事
例句: The police went through the suspect's apartment with a fine-tooth comb. 警察仔细搜查了嫌疑人的公寓。

go through the motions:  敷衍了事
例句: He wasn't really interested in the project, so he just went through the motions.  他对这个项目并不真正感兴趣，所以只是敷衍了事。

go through the roof:  暴涨，激增
