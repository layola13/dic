nonfermenting:/ˌnɑːnfərˈmɛntɪŋ/ | adj. | 非发酵的 | Not undergoing or capable of undergoing fermentation. |  Example 1: Nonfermenting bacteria are often found in soil and water. 非发酵细菌常在土壤和水中发现。

Example 2: The lab identified the bacteria as a nonfermenting gram-negative rod. 实验室将该细菌鉴定为非发酵革兰氏阴性杆菌。| Near synonyms: nonfermentative, non-saccharolytic | Antonyms: fermenting, fermentative | adjective
