censorial:/senˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
审查的；检查的
Relating to a censor or censorship; tending to censor or criticize harshly.
The government's censorial policies restricted freedom of speech. 政府的审查政策限制了言论自由。
Synonyms: restrictive, suppressive, controlling, prohibitive
Antonyms: permissive, lenient, tolerant
词性: 形容词

censorial:/senˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
审查员的；检查员的
Of or belonging to a censor.
He adopted a censorial tone when discussing the film. 他在讨论这部电影时采取了审查的语气。
Synonyms: supervisory, overseeing, regulatory
Antonyms: unregulated, uncontrolled
词性: 形容词
