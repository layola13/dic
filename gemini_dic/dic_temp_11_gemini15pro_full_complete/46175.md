cyperaceous:/ˌsaɪpəˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
莎草科的；似莎草的
Relating to or resembling plants of the sedge family (Cyperaceae), typically having grasslike leaves and inconspicuous flowers.
Sedges and rushes are cyperaceous plants. 莎草和灯芯草都是莎草科植物。
Near synonyms: grasslike, sedgelike
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider words like "showy" if referring to flowers)
Etymology: From Modern Latin Cyperaceus, from Cyperus (the genus of sedges) + -aceous (suffix meaning "resembling").
Word type: Adjective
