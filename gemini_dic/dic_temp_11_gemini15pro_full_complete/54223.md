disforest:/diːˈfɔːrɪst/ /diːˈfɑːrɪst/
verb
砍伐森林，清除树林
The land was disforested to make way for cattle ranching.  这片土地上的树木被砍伐，用来放牧牛群。
Disforestation is a major environmental problem.  森林砍伐是一个主要的环境问题。
近义词: deforest, clear-cut
反义词: reforest, afforest
词性: verb

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing reversal or absence) + forest.  来自dis-（表示反转或缺失）+ forest（森林）。
