acquaintanceships: /əˈkweɪntənʃɪps/
n.
相识；熟人关系
The state of being acquainted; the fact of knowing someone.  A number of such relationships.
She had a wide circle of acquaintanceships through her work. 通过工作，她结识了很多人。
acquaintance, familiarity, friendship, companionship, association
estrangement, unfamiliarity, isolation
