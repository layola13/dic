ghetto: /ˈɡɛtoʊ/ (US) /ˈɡɛtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |贫民区；少数民族聚居区；被隔离区|A part of a city, especially a slum area, occupied by a minority group or groups. |  Many Jewish people were forced to live in ghettos during World War II.  二战期间，许多犹太人被迫住在贫民窟里。

|例句：The Warsaw Ghetto was the largest of all the Jewish ghettos in Nazi-occupied Europe during World War II. 华沙犹太人区是二战期间纳粹占领的欧洲所有犹太人区中最大的一个。

|例句：Growing up in the ghetto wasn't easy. 在贫民区长大不容易。

|近义词：slum, barrio, enclave|反义词：affluent area, upscale neighborhood|词性: noun
