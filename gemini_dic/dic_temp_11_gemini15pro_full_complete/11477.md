argy-bargying:/ˈɑːɡi ˈbɑːɡiɪŋ/
noun
争吵，争论
a noisy quarrel about something unimportant
例句：The children were argy-bargying over who got the last cookie. 孩子们正在为谁拿到最后一块饼干而争吵。

例句：They were having a bit of an argy-bargy about the bill. 他们就账单问题发生了一些争执。
近义词：quarreling, bickering, squabbling
反义词：agreeing, concurring
词性:n.
