life-force:/ˈlaɪf fɔːrs/
n.
生命力，活力
The vital energy or animating principle held to be inherent in all living things.
He seemed to be filled with a renewed life-force after his vacation.  他度假回来后似乎充满了新的活力。
The ancient tree radiated a powerful life-force. 这棵古树散发着强大的生命力。

近义词:vitality, energy, spirit, vigor, élan vital
反义词:lethargy, inertia, lifelessness
词性:noun
