caperingly:/ˈkeɪpərɪŋli/
adv.
雀跃地，欢快地
in a playful and lively way, skipping or leaping about
She danced caperingly across the meadow. 她雀跃地穿过草地。
近义词: playfully, merrily, joyfully, exuberantly
反义词: gloomily, sadly, dejectedly
词性: 副词

词源:  From the verb "caper" (to leap or skip playfully), which comes from the Middle French "capre" and ultimately from the Latin "caper" (goat). The "-ingly" suffix transforms the verb into an adverb.
