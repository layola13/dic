invincibles:/ɪnˈvɪnsəblz/| n.|不可战胜的人（或物）；无敌者；常胜队|A team or group of people who seem to be impossible to defeat.  It is often used in a sporting context.|例句：The Invincibles were the nickname given to the Arsenal team that went undefeated throughout the 2003–04 Premier League season.  这支常胜队是赋予在2003-04赛季英超联赛中保持不败的阿森纳队的绰号。

例句：They considered themselves invincibles, until they met their match.  他们自认为是不可战胜的，直到遇到了他们的对手。|近义词：unbeatables, conquerors, champions|反义词:losers, defeated|词性:noun (plural)
