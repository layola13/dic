arrack:/ˈærək/ | n. |烧酒，亚拉克酒|An alcoholic liquor typically distilled from the sap of the coconut palm or from rice. |例句：He ordered a glass of arrack. 他点了一杯亚力酒。

例句：Arrack is a popular drink in some parts of Asia.  亚力酒在亚洲某些地区是一种很受欢迎的饮料。|近义词：liquor, spirits, alcohol|反义词:None|词性:noun

词源:From Sinhala අරක්කු (arakku), from Arabic عَرَق‎ (ʕaraq, “sweat, distillation”). Ultimately from Aramaic.
