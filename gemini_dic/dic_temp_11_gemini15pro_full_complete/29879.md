caustic-soda:/ˈkɔːstɪk ˈsoʊdə/| n.|苛性钠，烧碱|A highly alkaline substance used in manufacturing soap and many other products. It is chemically sodium hydroxide (NaOH).|Caustic soda is used in the production of paper. 苛性钠用于造纸。|Synonyms: sodium hydroxide, lye, alkali|Antonyms: acid|noun
