canter's:/ˈkæntərz/| n.|马术慢跑者的（所有格）; 慢跑者的马厩（或马场）|Canter's is a famous deli in Los Angeles.  坎特餐厅是洛杉矶一家著名的熟食店。|Canter's Deli has been a Los Angeles institution for decades.  坎特熟食店几十年来一直是洛杉矶的一个标志性场所。|近义词：rider's, equestrian's|反义词:|名词

canter's:/ˈkæntərz/| n.|慢跑（ canter的名词所有格）|This is a comfortable canter's pace. 这是一种舒适的慢跑速度。|The horse settled into a smooth canter's rhythm. 这匹马稳定地进入了慢跑的节奏。|近义词: rider's canter|反义词: gallop's |名词


词源:  From "canter" + possessive suffix "-'s". "Canter" itself likely originates from the Canterbury gallop, a relatively gentle pace used by pilgrims traveling to Canterbury Cathedral.
