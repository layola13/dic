dialog:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/ (UK), /ˈdaɪəlɔːɡ/ (US)| n.|对话，交谈|A conversation between two or more people, especially one featuring characters in a novel, play, or film.|例句：The dialog in the movie was witty and engaging. 电影中的对话诙谐有趣，引人入胜。|近义词：conversation, discussion, exchange, talk|反义词：monologue, soliloquy|noun


dialog:/ˈdaɪəlɔːɡ/ (US)| n.|对白|The lines spoken by characters in a play, film, or novel.|例句：The actors delivered their dialog with great emotion.  演员们充满感情地念着对白。|近义词：lines, script, speech|反义词：stage direction|noun
