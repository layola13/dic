garbo:/ˈɡɑːrboʊ/| n.|垃圾; 废物; 劣质东西;  (尤指)演艺界名流;  格丽泰·嘉宝(Greta Garbo, 1905-1990, 瑞典出生的美国电影演员);|Greta Garbo was a famous actress known for her mystique and talent. 格丽泰·嘉宝是一位以其神秘感和才华而闻名的女演员。|The old building was full of garbo. 这栋老房子里堆满了垃圾。|近义词：trash, rubbish, refuse, waste, celebrity, star|反义词:treasure, valuable|词性:noun

Etymology:  Shortened from garbage, which ultimately comes from Anglo-French *garber, meaning "to equip with." The association with waste likely developed through a metaphorical extension of the sense of "things with which a place is equipped" to "things discarded."
