infortuned:/ɪnˈfɔːrtʃʊnd/ | adj. | 不幸的，运气不好的 |  Unlucky; unfortunate.  Derived from "in-" (not) + "fortuned" (having good fortune). While "fortuned" is archaic now, the negative form "infortuned" persists, though also considered archaic or literary. |  The infortuned travelers were caught in a blizzard.  不幸的旅行者被困在了暴风雪中。 | Synonyms: unfortunate, unlucky, hapless, ill-fated, ill-starred | Antonyms: fortunate, lucky, blessed, charmed | Adjective
