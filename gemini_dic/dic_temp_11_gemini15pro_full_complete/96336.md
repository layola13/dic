indigo:/ˈɪndɪɡoʊ/ | n. adj.|靛蓝，靛蓝色|A dark blue dye originally extracted from the indigo plant. Also, the deep violet-blue color of this dye.|例句：The artist used indigo to create a night sky. 艺术家用靛蓝色来创作夜空。|例句：She wore an indigo dress to the party. 她穿着一件靛蓝色的连衣裙去参加聚会。|近义词：blue, violet, sapphire|反义词：yellow, orange|词性:名词，形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English ynde, inde, from Old French inde, from Medieval Latin indicum, from Latin Indicum (sc. pigmentum) ‘Indian (dye)’, from Greek indikon (pharmakon) ‘Indian (drug)’, from India.
