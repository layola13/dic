braille:/breɪl/| n.|盲文|A system of writing and printing for blind or visually impaired people, in which raised dots represent letters and numbers.|She learned to read braille when she was five. 她五岁时学会了读盲文。|近义词:blind writing|反义词:print|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Louis Braille (1809-1852), French inventor of the system.
