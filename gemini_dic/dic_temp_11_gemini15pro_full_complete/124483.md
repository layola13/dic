mouldered:/ˈmoʊldərd/ | v. | 腐烂，朽坏 | decayed or disintegrated, especially because of dampness or neglect; to slowly decay or waste away | The old books in the attic had mouldered over the years.  阁楼里的旧书多年来已经腐烂了。|  The abandoned house slowly mouldered away. 这栋废弃的房子慢慢地腐朽了。 | synonyms: decayed, rotted, decomposed, crumbled | antonyms: flourished, thrived, blossomed | verb
