disgruntle: /dɪsˈɡrʌnt(ə)l/
verb
使不高兴，使不满
to make someone slightly angry or dissatisfied
例句：The employees were disgruntled by the company's new policy. 员工们对公司的新政策感到不满。

例句：He was disgruntled at being left out of the team. 他因被排除在团队之外而感到不高兴。

近义词：displease, annoy, irritate, vex
反义词：please, satisfy, content
词性: verb
