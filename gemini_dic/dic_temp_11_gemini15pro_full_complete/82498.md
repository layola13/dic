gruy:/ɡriː/ | n. | 粗麦粉，格鲁伊耶尔干酪 | A type of coarse-ground hominy, or a type of Gruyère cheese. | Example 1: The gruy was used to make a hearty porridge.  粗麦粉被用来做了一碗丰盛的粥。

Example 2: The chef grated some gruy over the potatoes.  厨师在土豆上磨碎了一些格鲁伊耶尔干酪。 | Synonym: (for hominy) grits, polenta; (for cheese) Swiss cheese | Antonym: None | Noun
