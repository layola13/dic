estrus:/ˈɛstrəs/ (US) /ˈiːstrəs/ (UK)| n.|发情期，动情期|A recurring period of sexual receptivity and fertility in many female mammals; heat.|The lioness was in estrus and attracting the attention of all the males. 母狮处于发情期，吸引了所有雄狮的注意。|近义词：heat, rut|反义词:anestrus|词性:名词
