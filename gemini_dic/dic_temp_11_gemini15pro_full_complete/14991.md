babblery:/ˈbæb(ə)lri/ | n. | 喋喋不休，空谈，废话 |  A long-winded and often meaningless or confusing speech or piece of writing.  Meaningless chatter; prattle. | The politician's speech was filled with babblery and lacked substance. 这位政客的演讲充满了空谈，缺乏实质内容。 | synonyms: chatter, prattle, gibberish, nonsense, drivel | antonyms:  conciseness, brevity, succinctness | noun

babblery:/ˈbæb(ə)lri/ | n. | 饶舌的人们; 爱饶舌的场所 | A group of people who talk incessantly or a place where such talk occurs. | The cafe was a babblery of students discussing their exams. 这家咖啡馆里满是讨论考试的学生，非常吵闹。 | synonyms:  clamor, hubbub, din | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun
