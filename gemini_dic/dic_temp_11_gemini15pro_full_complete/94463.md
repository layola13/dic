impoliticly:/ɪmˈpɒlɪtɪkli/
| adv. |不明智地，失策地|Not wisely or expediently; in a way that is not politic.|例句：He impoliticly criticized his boss in front of the entire team. 他不明智地在整个团队面前批评了他的老板。|例句：She spoke impoliticly about the sensitive issue, causing a great deal of offense. 她不明智地谈论了这个敏感问题，引起了很大的冒犯。|近义词：injudiciously, unwisely, foolishly, imprudently|反义词: wisely, judiciously, prudently, discreetly|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From im- (not) + politic (wise, prudent) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *politikos* (of citizens, civic, political), from *politēs* (citizen), from *polis* (city).
