decretorily:/ˌdɛkrɪˈtɔːrɪli/
| adv. |断然地，专横地|In a manner that expresses or implies a final decision or authoritative command; peremptorily.|He decretorily refused to consider the proposal. 他断然拒绝考虑这项提议。|近义词：peremptorily, authoritatively, decisively, dogmatically|反义词：hesitantly, tentatively, uncertainly|词性:副词

decretory:/dɪˈkriːtəri/
| adj. | 法令的，决定性的|Relating to or having the force of a decree; definitive.|The judge's decision was decretory. 法官的决定是最终的。|近义词：definitive, conclusive, final|反义词：provisional, tentative|词性:形容词
