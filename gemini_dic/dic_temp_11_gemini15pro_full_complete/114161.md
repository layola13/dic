mahoe: /məˈhoʊiː/ (US), /məˈhəʊiː/ (UK)| n.|  木槿;  黄槿;  白椴树|A type of tree or shrub found in tropical regions, particularly in the Caribbean and Pacific, known for its strong, flexible wood and fibrous bark.  Sometimes refers specifically to *Hibiscus tiliaceus* (sea hibiscus) or *Talipariti elatum* (blue mahoe).| The mahoe tree is a common sight along the coast. 黄槿树是海岸线上常见的景象。

The mahoe wood was used for carving traditional tools. 黄槿木被用来雕刻传统工具。|近义词：hibiscus, linden (depending on the specific species)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
