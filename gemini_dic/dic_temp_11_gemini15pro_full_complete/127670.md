nefarious:/nɪˈfɛəriəs/| adj.|邪恶的；恶毒的；极坏的|Wicked, villainous, or criminal.|The nefarious plot to overthrow the government was foiled by the police. 警方挫败了推翻政府的邪恶阴谋。|近义词：wicked, evil, villainous, heinous, atrocious|反义词：righteous, virtuous, benevolent, good|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nefārius* (“wicked, impious”), from *nefās* (“wrong, sin”), from *ne-* (“not”) + *fās* (“divine law, right”).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *dʰeh₁-* (“to set, put”).
