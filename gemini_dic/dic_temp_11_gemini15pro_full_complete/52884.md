diminutions:/ˌdɪmɪˈnjuːʃənz/ | n. |减少，缩小，降低|  A decrease in size, extent, or importance.  The word often implies a gradual or progressive lessening. | The diminutions in funding have severely impacted the program.  资金的减少严重影响了这个项目。| decrease, reduction, decline | increase, growth, expansion | noun

diminutions:/ˌdɪmɪˈnjuːʃənz/ | n. |(音乐)减弱音| In music, a passage played with gradually decreasing volume. | The diminutions at the end of the piece were beautifully executed.  这首曲子结尾的减弱音演奏得非常优美。| decrescendo | crescendo | noun
