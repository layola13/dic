knebelite:/ˈnɛbəˌlaɪt/| n.|铁橄榄石|A mineral, manganese iron silicate, Mn2SiO4,  belonging to the olivine group. It is typically brownish-red in color.|Knebelite is a common mineral in manganese-rich metamorphic rocks.|近义词:fayalite, tephroite|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of  Major P. Knebel, a Russian official in charge of mines in the Urals, where the mineral was first discovered.
