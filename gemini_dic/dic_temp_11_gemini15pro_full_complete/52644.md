digitalises:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
数字化；将…数字化
converts (information or images) to digital form.
The company digitalises all incoming mail.  这家公司将所有收到的邮件都数字化了。
Many companies are now digitalising their archives. 许多公司现在正在将其档案数字化。
Synonyms: digitize, computerize, automate
Antonyms: analogize
词性: 动词
