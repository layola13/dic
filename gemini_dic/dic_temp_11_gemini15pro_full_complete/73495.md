foresaw:/fɔːrˈsɔː/
verb
预见，预知
knew or saw beforehand; predicted.
She foresaw the dangers of the journey. 她预见了旅途的危险。

The ancient prophets foresaw the coming of the Messiah. 古代的先知预见了弥赛亚的到来。


synonym: anticipated, predicted, foreseen, expected
antonym:  unforeseen, unexpected

词性: 动词
