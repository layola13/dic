exporter:/ɪkˈspɔːrtər/ /ˈɛkspɔːrtər/| n.|出口商；出口国|A person, company, or country that sells goods to another country.|China is a major exporter of electronics. 中国是主要的电子产品出口国。|The company is a leading exporter of coffee beans. 这家公司是咖啡豆的主要出口商。|近义词:seller, vendor, shipper, trader|反义词:importer|词性:noun
