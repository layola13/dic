edictally:/iˈdɪktəli/ | adv. | 以命令的方式；强制地；专横地 | By decree; authoritatively. Done or delivered in the form of an edict. | The king edictally declared a new national holiday. 国王以命令的方式宣布了一个新的全国假日。 |  | Example: The new policy was edictally imposed on the employees, leaving no room for discussion. 新政策以强制方式实施于员工，没有留下任何讨论的余地。 | synonym: peremptorily, imperiously, authoritatively, dogmatically | antonym: democratically, persuasively, collaboratively |词性: 副词
