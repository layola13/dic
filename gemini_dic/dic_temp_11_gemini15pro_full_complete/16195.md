banland: /ˈbænˌlænd/
noun
荒地，禁地
Land that is barren or uncultivated, especially that which is prohibited for cultivation or settlement.
The explorers found themselves in a vast banland, unable to grow crops or find suitable shelter. 探险家们发现自己身处一片广袤的荒地，无法种植作物或找到合适的住所。
synonyms: wasteland, barren land, desert, wilderness
antonyms: fertile land, cultivated land, arable land
