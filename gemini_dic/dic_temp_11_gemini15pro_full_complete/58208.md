drudgingly:/ˈdrʌdʒɪŋli/ | adv. | 辛苦地，劳碌地 | In a way that involves hard, boring work. | He drudgingly completed the tedious task. 他辛苦地完成了这项乏味的任务。 | She drudgingly cleaned the entire house. 她辛辛苦苦地打扫了整个房子。 | 近义词: laboriously, toilsomely, tediously | 反义词: effortlessly, easily | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "drudge" + "-ly". "Drudge" comes from Middle English "druggen", of uncertain origin.
