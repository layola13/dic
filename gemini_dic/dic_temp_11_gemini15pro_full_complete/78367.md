gesticulate:/dʒɛsˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
做手势；用手势表达
To make gestures, especially dramatic ones, while speaking or to express something.
The speaker gesticulated wildly, emphasizing his points with sweeping hand movements. 演讲者疯狂地做着手势，用大幅度的挥手动作强调他的观点。
He gesticulated to the waiter to bring the bill. 他做手势示意服务员拿账单过来。
synonyms: gesture, motion, signal, wave, brandish
antonyms: be still, stay put
词性: 动词
