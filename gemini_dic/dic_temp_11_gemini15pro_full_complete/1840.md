acrolectal:/ˌækrəˈlɛktəl/
adjective
高级方言的，标准方言的
Relating to or denoting the most prestigious variety of a language, especially one regarded as the standard form.
Her acrolectal pronunciation marked her as someone from a wealthy background. 她标准的发音表明她出身富裕。
Synonyms: standard, prestigious, formal
Antonyms: basilectal, vernacular, informal
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From acro- (Greek 'highest, topmost') + -lect (from Greek 'speech, dialect') + -al (adjective suffix).
