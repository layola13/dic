kirchnerism: /ˈkɪʁʃnɛˌʁɪzəm/ /ˈkɜːrtʃnərɪzəm/
noun
基什内尔主义
The political ideology and policies associated with Néstor Kirchner and Cristina Fernández de Kirchner, former presidents of Argentina.  It is characterized by left-wing populism, social welfare programs, and interventionist economic policies.
Argentina experienced significant economic growth during the Kirchnerism years.  阿根廷在基什内尔主义时期经历了显著的经济增长。

Kirchnerism's focus on social programs helped reduce poverty. 基什内尔主义对社会项目的关注帮助减少了贫困。

近义词:  Peronism, left-wing populism,  左翼民粹主义
反义词:  Neoliberalism,  新自由主义
词性: n.
