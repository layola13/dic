immunizes:/ˈɪmjʊˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
使免疫；使免除
Makes someone immune to a disease, usually by vaccination.
The doctor immunizes children against measles. 医生为孩子们接种麻疹疫苗。
vaccinates, protects
infects, exposes
