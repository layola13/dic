hauntings:/ˈhɔːntɪŋz/ |n.|萦绕；困扰；常去的地方|The hauntings of her past continued to plague her. 她过去的萦绕一直困扰着她。|例句：The hauntings in the old house frightened the children.  老房子里的鬼魂吓坏了孩子们。

例句：Her hauntings of the museum finally paid off when she found a rare artifact. 她经常去博物馆，最终找到了一个稀有的文物。|近义词：apparitions, ghosts, specters, visions, memories, frequentations|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑peace, tranquility)|词性: 名词 (Noun)


haunting:/ˈhɔːntɪŋ/ |adj.|萦绕心头的；令人难忘的；使人困扰的|The haunting melody stayed with me for days.  那萦绕心头的旋律在我脑海里停留了好几天。|例句：The photograph evoked haunting memories of his childhood. 这张照片唤起了他童年时期令人难忘的记忆。

例句：She had a haunting feeling that something bad was going to happen. 她有一种挥之不去的预感，觉得会有不好的事情发生。|近义词：unforgettable, memorable, disturbing, troubling, eerie, poignant|反义词:forgettable, comforting|词性: 形容词 (Adjective)


词源: From the verb "haunt," which comes from the Old French "hanter" meaning "to frequent, to haunt," ultimately from Frankish "haim" meaning "home."
