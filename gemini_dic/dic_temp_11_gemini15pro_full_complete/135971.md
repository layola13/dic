nonracialism:/nɒnˈreɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.
非种族主义
The principle or policy of opposing racism and promoting racial equality.
South Africa embraced nonracialism after the end of apartheid. 南非在种族隔离结束后接受了非种族主义。
Nelson Mandela was a staunch advocate of nonracialism. 纳尔逊·曼德拉是非种族主义的坚定拥护者。
Synonyms: equality, integration, multiracialism
Antonyms: racism, segregation, apartheid, discrimination
