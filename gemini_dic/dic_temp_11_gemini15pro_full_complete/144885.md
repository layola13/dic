over-ripenesses: /ˈoʊvərˈraɪpənɪsɪz/ | n. | 过熟，过度成熟 | The state of being excessively ripe, often to the point of spoilage. | The over-ripenesses of the bananas made them mushy and unappetizing. 香蕉过熟，变得糊状，不好吃了。| Synonyms: overmaturity, rottenness | Antonyms: unripeness, immaturity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessively) + "ripeness" (the state of being ripe). "Ripeness" comes from the Old English "ripe" (ready for harvest).
