mosh:/mɒʃ/ (UK) /mɑːʃ/ (US)| v. n.| 猛烈撞击，激烈地跳舞，mosh pit（舞池）|To dance energetically in a crowd, often involving bumping into other people. Also, the area where this dancing takes place.  Derived from forming a "mosh" of people.|例句：The band started playing and the crowd started to mosh. 乐队开始演奏，人群开始猛烈撞击跳舞。

|例句：He got caught in the mosh pit and lost his shoe. 他被困在舞池里，丢了一只鞋。|近义词：slam, jump, thrash|反义词:waltz, slow dance|词性:verb, noun
