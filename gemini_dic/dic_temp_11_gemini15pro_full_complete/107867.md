larger:/ˈlɑːrdʒər/ (US), /ˈlɑːdʒə/ (UK)| adj.|更大的，较大的|  From Middle English *larger, comparative of *large, from Old French large, from Latin largus (“abundant, copious, large, extensive”), of uncertain origin. |He ordered a larger portion of fries. 他点了一份更大的薯条。|近义词：bigger, greater, more extensive|反义词:smaller, lesser, tinier|词性:adjective
