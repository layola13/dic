box's:/ˈbɑːksɪz/| n.|盒子的；箱子的|A possessive form of "box," indicating ownership or belonging.  It refers to something that belongs to or is associated with a box. |This is my box's lid. 这是我的盒子的盖子。|The box's contents were a mystery. 盒子里装的东西是个谜。|近义词:container's, crate's|反义词:None (Possessive forms don't typically have antonyms)|词性:Possessive noun
