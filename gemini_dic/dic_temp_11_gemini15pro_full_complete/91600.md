hyperaggressiveness:/ˌhaɪpərəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs/| n. |过度侵略性，极度攻击性|The state of being excessively aggressive, characterized by frequent or extreme displays of anger, hostility, or violence.|His hyperaggressiveness led to several fights at school. 他的过度侵略性导致在学校打了好几架。| 近义词: belligerence, pugnacity, aggressiveness | 反义词: passiveness, docility, meekness | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (prefix meaning "excessive, above") + aggressiveness (noun form of aggressive).  The root word "aggressive" comes from the Latin "aggressus," past participle of "aggredi," meaning "to go toward," from "ad-" (to) + "gradi" (to step).
