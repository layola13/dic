humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n. interj.|欺骗；骗局；谎话；无稽之谈；胡说；废话|something that is intended to deceive; nonsense|例句：The report is a complete humbug.  这份报告纯属胡说八道。

例句：He dismissed the claims as sheer humbug. 他把这些说法斥为纯粹的废话。|近义词：nonsense, fraud, deception, sham, hoax|反义词：truth, honesty, reality|词性:名词,感叹词

词源:
The origin of "humbug" is uncertain. It may be related to "hum" (make a murmuring sound) and "bug" (bother, annoy), suggesting deceptive or misleading talk. Another theory connects it to "Hamburg," referring to counterfeit coins supposedly made there.
