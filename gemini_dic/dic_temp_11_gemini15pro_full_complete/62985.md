enology:/iːˈnɑːlədʒi/ /iːˈnɒlədʒi/
n.
葡萄酒酿造学; 葡萄酒学
The science and study of all aspects of wine and winemaking, excluding viticulture (the growing of the grapevine).
He studied enology in France.  他在法国学习葡萄酒酿造学。

近义词:viniculture, viticulture
反义词: N/A
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *œnologie*, from Ancient Greek *οἶνος* (*oînos*, “wine”) + *-λογία* (*-logía*, “-logy”).
