fantasizes:/ˈfæntəˌsaɪzɪz/
verb
幻想，想象，做白日梦
indulges in fantasies; creates imaginary scenarios in one's mind
He often fantasizes about winning the lottery. 他经常幻想自己中了彩票。
She fantasizes about living in a big house in the countryside. 她幻想着住在乡下的一栋大房子里。
Synonyms: daydreams, imagines, muses, dreams, visualizes
Antonyms:  faces reality, reasons,  thinks logically
词性: 动词(verb)
