autosave:/ˈɔːtəʊseɪv/| n. & v. |自动保存|A function in software that automatically saves data at regular intervals, preventing loss of work in case of a system crash or power outage.|The autosave feature saved my work when the computer crashed. |电脑崩溃时，自动保存功能保存了我的工作。|synonyms: automatic save, auto-save|antonyms: manual save|noun, verb
