jamb:/dʒæm/| n.|门框，侧柱，堵塞物|A jamb is a vertical side piece or post of a doorway, window, or fireplace.|The door jamb was damaged in the storm. 门框在暴风雨中受损了。|The heavy box jammed the doorway.  沉重的箱子堵住了门口。|Synonyms: frame, post, support, sidepiece, blockage|Antonyms: opening, gap|noun

jamb:/dʒæm/| vt.|塞紧，卡住|To jamb something means to wedge or jam it tightly so that it cannot move.|He jambed the paper into the shredder. 他把纸塞进碎纸机。|The gears jammed, and the machine stopped working. 齿轮卡住了，机器停止了工作。|Synonyms: wedge, cram, stuff, stick, block|Antonyms: release, free, loosen|verb
