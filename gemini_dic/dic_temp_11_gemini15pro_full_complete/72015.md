flinty:/ˈflɪnti/| adj.|坚硬的；冷酷的；含燧石的|Resembling flint, especially in hardness, sharpness, or coldness of manner.|His flinty gaze met mine and I knew he wouldn't budge.  他那冷酷的目光与我相遇，我知道他不会让步。|近义词：hard, unyielding, impassive, stern|反义词：soft, yielding, compassionate, warm|词性:形容词

flinty:/ˈflɪnti/| n.|燧石|A very hard gray rock consisting of nearly pure silica (SiO2).  一种非常坚硬的灰色岩石，几乎由纯二氧化硅 (SiO2) 组成。|He struck the flinty rock to make a fire. 他敲击燧石生火。|近义词：chert, flintstone|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑柔软的岩石如chalk)|词性:名词
