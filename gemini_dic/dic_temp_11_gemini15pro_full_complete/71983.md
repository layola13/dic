flinch:/flɪntʃ/| vi.|畏缩，退缩， flinch (from something) |  To make a sudden small movement because you are afraid or in pain.  | He didn't even flinch when the nurse cleaned the wound.  护士清洗伤口时，他甚至连动都没动。 | synonyms: wince, shrink, recoil | antonyms:  brave, confront, endure | verb
