outrivaling:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəlɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过
surpassing or exceeding in rivalry; outdoing
Her new restaurant quickly outrivaled all the others in the area. 她的新餐厅很快就超过了该地区所有其他餐厅。
synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, eclipsing, overshadowing
antonyms: losing to, being surpassed by, falling behind
