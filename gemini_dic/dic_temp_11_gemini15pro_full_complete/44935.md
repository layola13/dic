cryptographer:/krɪpˈtɒɡrəfər/ /krɪpˈtɑːɡrəfər/| n. |密码学家；密码破译者|A person who studies or practices cryptography, the art of writing or solving codes.|例句：The cryptographer worked tirelessly to decipher the enemy's message.  密码学家不知疲倦地工作以破译敌人的信息。

例句：She's a renowned cryptographer who specializes in ancient codes. 她是一位著名的密码学家，专攻古代密码。|近义词：codebreaker, cryptanalyst, decoder|反义词: None|词性: Noun
