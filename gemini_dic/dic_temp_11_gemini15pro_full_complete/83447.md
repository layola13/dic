habitats:/ˈhæbɪtæts/
n.
栖息地，生境
The natural environment in which an organism lives.
Polar bears are losing their habitats due to climate change.  北极熊正因气候变化而失去它们的栖息地。

Rainforests are habitats for a wide variety of plant and animal species.  热带雨林是各种动植物物种的栖息地。

近义词:  environments, homes, territories
反义词:  none (no single antonym perfectly captures the opposite of habitat)
词性: noun
