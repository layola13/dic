alexa:/əˈlɛksə/| n.| 阿莱克萨 (亚马逊的虚拟助手)|A virtual assistant developed by Amazon, capable of voice interaction, music playback, making to-do lists, setting alarms, streaming podcasts, playing audiobooks, and providing weather, traffic, sports, and other real-time information.|例句：Ask Alexa to play some music.  让Alexa播放一些音乐。|近义词：Siri, Google Assistant, Cortana|反义词: None|noun

alexa:/əˈlɛksə/| n.| (女名) 阿莱克萨|A female given name, a short form of Alexandra.|例句：Alexa is my best friend.  阿莱克萨是我最好的朋友。|近义词：Alexandra, Alex|反义词: None|noun
