dimnesses:/ˈdɪmnɪsɪz/
noun
昏暗；朦胧；模糊不清
The state of being dim or unclear.
The dimnesses of the old photographs made it hard to identify the people in them. 这些老照片的模糊不清使得很难辨认出照片中的人。
Near Synonyms: darkness, obscurity, faintness, dullness
Antonyms: brightness, clarity, vividness
词性: 名词

noun
昏暗；朦胧；模糊不清

Near Synonyms: darkness, obscurity, faintness, dullness

词性: 名词


dim:/dɪm/
adjective
昏暗的；朦胧的；暗淡的
Not bright or clear.
The dim light made it hard to see. 昏暗的光线使人难以看清。
Near Synonyms: dark, faint, obscure, dull
Antonyms: bright, clear, vivid, brilliant
词性: 形容词

dim:/dɪm/
verb
使变暗；使变模糊
To make or become less bright or clear.
The fog dimmed the headlights of the car. 雾使汽车的前灯变暗了。
Near Synonyms: darken, obscure, cloud, dull
Antonyms: brighten, illuminate, clarify
词性: 动词
