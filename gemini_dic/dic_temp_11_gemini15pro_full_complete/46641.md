damnedest:/ˈdæm.dɪst/ | adj. | 最怪异的；最令人惊讶的；最难理解的 |  An intensified form of "damned," used as an adjective to emphasize something remarkable, surprising, or perplexing. It often expresses a sense of bewilderment or amusement. |  He did the damnedest thing – he painted his car bright pink!  他做了最怪异的事——他把他的车漆成了亮粉色！| Synonyms: strangest, oddest, most peculiar, most surprising | Antonyms: normal, ordinary, usual | Adjective
