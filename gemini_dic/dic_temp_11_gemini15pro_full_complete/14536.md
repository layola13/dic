averred:/əˈvɜːrd/
verb
断言；郑重声明
state or assert to be the case
The witness averred that he had seen the defendant at the scene of the crime.  证人断言他曾在犯罪现场看到被告。
近义词: asserted, declared, affirmed, stated, maintained
反义词: denied, refuted, contradicted
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *averren*, from Old French *averer* (to verify, confirm), from Late Latin *adverare* (to make true, prove), from Latin *ad-* (to) + *verus* (true).
