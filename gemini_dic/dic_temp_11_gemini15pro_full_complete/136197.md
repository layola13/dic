nonreleases:/ˌnɒnriˈliːsɪz/
n.
未发布的内容；未公开的信息
things that are not released or made public
The company keeps its nonreleases confidential.  这家公司对其未发布的内容保密。
近义词：unpublished material, withheld information
反义词：releases, publications
词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "releases" (plural of "release," from Old French "relaisser," meaning "to let go")
