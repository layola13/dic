betrothed:/bɪˈtrəʊðd/ /bɪˈtroʊθt/| adj. n.|已订婚的，订婚者|Engaged to be married; a person to whom one is engaged.|例句：They were betrothed when they were very young. 他们很年轻的时候就订婚了。|近义词：engaged, promised, affianced|反义词:single, unattached|词性:形容词

betrothed:/bɪˈtrəʊðd/ /bɪˈtroʊθt/| n.|订婚者|A person to whom one is engaged.|例句：He sent a gift to his betrothed. 他送了一份礼物给他的未婚妻。|近义词：fiancé, fiancée, spouse-to-be|反义词:ex, stranger|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *betrouthed*, past participle of *betrouthen* (“to pledge one's faith, vow”), from Old English *betrēowþian* (“to pledge, engage”), equivalent to *be-* + *trēowþ* (“truth, faith, fidelity”). Cognate with Dutch *betrouwen* (“to entrust, confide”), German *betrauen* (“to entrust”).  The noun use is a shortening of betrothed person.
