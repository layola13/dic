metathetic:/ˌmɛtəˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
换位的，语音转化的
Relating to or involving metathesis, a sound change involving the transposition of sounds within a word.
Metathesis is a common linguistic phenomenon that affects the order of sounds in words.
近义词: transposed, inverted
反义词: unchanged, static
词性: adjective
