indefatigableness:/ˌɪndɪˈfætiɡəblənəs/| n.|不屈不挠，不知疲倦|The indefatigableness of the rescue workers was truly inspiring.  救援人员的不屈不挠的精神 truly inspiring.|近义词：tirelessness, persistence, perseverance|反义词：fatigue, weariness, lethargy|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + defatigable (tireless) + -ness (noun suffix).  Defatigable comes from French  "défatigable," from Late Latin "defatigabilis," ultimately from Latin "fatigare" (to weary).
