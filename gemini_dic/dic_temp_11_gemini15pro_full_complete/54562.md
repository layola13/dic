dislodgeable:/dɪsˈlɒdʒəbl/
adjective
可移动的，可驱逐的
able to be removed from a fixed position.
The plaque buildup was dislodgeable with a toothbrush.  用牙刷可以清除牙菌斑。
The rock was precariously balanced and looked easily dislodgeable.  这块岩石摇摇欲坠，看起来很容易移动。
synonyms: removable, detachable, displaceable
antonyms: fixed, immovable, secure
词性: 形容词
