gilbert:/ˈɡɪlbərt/| n.|吉尔伯特 (人名；磁通量的单位)|A unit of magnetomotive force, equal to 0.7958 ampere-turns.  Also a male given name.|例句：His name is Gilbert. 他的名字是吉尔伯特。

例句：The magnetomotive force was measured in gilberts. 磁动势是用吉尔伯特测量的。|近义词：(For unit) Ampere-turn|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


gilbert:/ˈɡɪlbərt/| adj.|吉尔伯特的|Of or relating to the Gilbert Islands or their people.|例句：Gilbert islanders are known for their seafaring skills. 吉尔伯特岛民以其航海技能而闻名。
