isoallelic:/ˌaɪsoʊəˈliːlɪk/
adjective
同等位基因的
Having to do with or being isoalleles; alleles that are functionally equivalent but have originated through mutation from the same ancestral gene.
Isoallelic variants can often be distinguished only by DNA sequencing. 同等位基因变异通常只能通过DNA测序来区分。
Isoallelic mutations can have subtle effects on protein function. 同等位基因突变可能对蛋白质功能产生微妙的影响。
Near synonyms: allelic, equivalent
Antonyms: non-allelic, different
