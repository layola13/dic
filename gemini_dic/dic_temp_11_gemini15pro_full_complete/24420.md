brisking:/ˈbrɪskɪŋ/| v. |使活跃，使 brisk(轻快)，使兴旺|The cold air brisked up the walkers. 冷空气使步行者们精神抖擞。|近义词：enlivening, invigorating, stimulating|反义词：sluggish, slowing|词性:动词
词源: From brisk + -ing.  Ultimately from the French word *brusque* meaning lively or quick.
