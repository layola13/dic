coquilla:/koʊˈkiːjə/ | n. | 贝壳，海贝；贝壳状物 | A small, often brightly colored shell, especially one used for decoration or in making jewelry. |  She collected coquillas on the beach.  她在海滩上收集贝壳。 | seashell, conch, shell |  - | noun
Etymology: Spanish *coquilla*, diminutive of *concha* "shell," from Latin *concha* "shellfish, mussel, shell".
