decrier's:/dɪˈkraɪərz/
n.
批评者; 贬低者; 诋毁者
The decrier's words were harsh and unfounded.
批评者的话语尖刻且毫无根据。
Etymology: From the verb "decry," from Old French "descrier" (to proclaim, announce, cry down), from "de-" (down) + "crier" (to cry).
Synonyms: critic, attacker, denouncer, slanderer
Antonyms: supporter, advocate, defender, praiser
词性: noun (名词)
