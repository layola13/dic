nonliable:/ˌnɑːnˈlaɪəbl/
adjective
无责任的；无需承担责任的
Not legally responsible for something.
The court ruled that the company was nonliable for the damages. 法院裁定该公司无需对损失承担责任。
The homeowner is nonliable for injuries sustained by trespassers. 房主对入侵者遭受的伤害不承担责任。
近义词:exempt, immune, not responsible
反义词:liable, responsible
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + liable, from Anglo-French liable, from Old French lier “to bind,” from Latin ligare.  The prefix "non-" means "not." So, "nonliable" literally means "not bound" or "not obligated."
