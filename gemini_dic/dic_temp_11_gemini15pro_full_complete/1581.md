achromatism:/əˈkroʊmətɪzəm/ | n. |消色差，无色性|The absence of color or chromatic aberration; the inability to perceive colors.|The telescope lens was designed for achromatism, producing clearer images. 这架望远镜的镜头设计用于消色差，从而产生更清晰的图像。|近义词:colorlessness, monochromatism|反义词:chromatism, polychromatism|名词

achromatic:/ˌeɪkroʊˈmætɪk/ | adj. |消色的，无色的|1. Relating to achromatism; free from color. 2. Colorless; without color.|The achromatic lens corrected the color distortion. 消色差透镜校正了颜色失真。  The artist preferred an achromatic palette for his sketches. 这位艺术家喜欢用无色的调色板来画素描。|近义词:colorless, neutral|反义词:chromatic, colorful|形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, “not, without”) + χρῶμα (khrôma, “color”) + -ism (suffix forming nouns of state or condition).
