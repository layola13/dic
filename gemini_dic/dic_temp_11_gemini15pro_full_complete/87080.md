heptateuch:/ˈhɛptətjuːk/
n.
七经
The first seven books of the Old Testament (Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, Deuteronomy, Joshua, and Judges).
The scholar specialized in the Heptateuch and could quote passages from memory.  这位学者专门研究七经，可以背诵其中的段落。
近义词:Pentateuch (五经, 指的是七经的前五卷)
反义词:None

词性:noun
