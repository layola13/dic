dunnage:/ˈdʌnɪdʒ/| n. |垫舱物，填充物，行李|Loose materials used to support and protect cargo in a ship's hold or other container, or padding used to protect furniture during shipping. It can also refer to personal belongings or baggage.|The ship was loaded with dunnage to prevent the cargo from shifting. 这艘船装载了垫舱物，以防止货物移动。

The movers used dunnage to protect the furniture during transport. 搬运工使用填充物来保护家具在运输过程中的安全。|近义词：packing, padding, stuffing, filling, ballast|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


dunnage:/ˈdʌnɪdʒ/| n. |（尤指船员的）个人物品，行李|Personal belongings or baggage, especially of sailors.|The sailors stowed their dunnage in their bunks. 水手们把他们的行李放在铺位里。

He rummaged through his dunnage looking for his passport. 他翻遍了他的行李，寻找他的护照。|近义词：luggage, baggage, belongings, effects|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dunnage, perhaps from Anglo-Norman *dunage, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French donjon ("keep, tower"), from which dungeon derives, originally referring to mats or padding used in a dungeon.
