elocutionize: /ɪˌlɒkjuˈʃənaɪz/ /ɪˌloʊkjuˈʃənaɪz/
verb
雄辩地演说，朗诵；训练演说
to speak or read aloud in a formal and dramatic way; to teach elocution
例句：He elocutionized the poem with great passion. 他充满激情地朗诵了这首诗。

例句：She took lessons to learn how to elocutionize effectively. 她上课学习如何有效地进行演说。

近义词：declaim, orate, pronounce, articulate
反义词：mumble, stammer
词性：v.
