congratulation: /kənˌɡrætʃuˈleɪʃn/ (US), /kənˌɡrætʃʊˈleɪʃn/ (UK)| n.| 祝贺，恭贺|an expression of pleasure at someone's success or good fortune. |例句：Congratulations on your graduation! 恭喜你毕业！

例句：Please accept my congratulations on your promotion. 请接受我对您升职的祝贺。

近义词：felicitation, commendation, praise|反义词: condolence|词性:noun

congratulation: /kənˌɡrætʃuˈleɪʃn/ (US), /kənˌɡrætʃʊˈleɪʃn/ (UK)| n.| 祝贺（常用复数形式）|an expression of pleasure at someone's success or good fortune. |例句：They offered their congratulations on her victory.  他们对她取得的胜利表示祝贺。

例句：He received many congratulations on his achievement.  他因成就而收到了许多祝贺。

近义词：greetings, best wishes|反义词: commiserations|词性:noun
