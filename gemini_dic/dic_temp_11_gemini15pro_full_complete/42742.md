contour:/ˈkɒntʊə(r) (UK), /ˈkɑːntʊr (US)| n. |轮廓，等高线|an outline representing or bounding the shape or form of something|例句：The contour of the hills against the sky was breathtaking.  群山映衬着天空的轮廓令人叹为观止。|近义词：outline, shape, form, profile|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "shapelessness")|词性:noun
contour:/kənˈtʊə(r) (UK), /kənˈtʊr (US)| vt. |画轮廓，描绘|to mold or form something along particular lines|例句：The artist skillfully contoured the face in the portrait.  这位艺术家巧妙地在肖像画中描绘了脸部轮廓。|近义词：shape, mold, form|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:verb
