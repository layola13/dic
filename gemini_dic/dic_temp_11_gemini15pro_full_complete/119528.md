microscopes:/ˈmaɪkrəˌskoʊps/| n. |显微镜|An optical instrument used for viewing very small objects, typically magnified several hundred times.|Scientists use microscopes to study cells. 科学家使用显微镜研究细胞。|The bacteria were visible only under a powerful microscope. 这种细菌只有在高倍显微镜下才能看到。|近义词:magnifying glass (放大镜, only for lower magnification)|反义词:telescope (望远镜)|词性:noun
