disposables:/dɪˈspəʊzəblz/
n.|一次性用品|Items designed to be used once and then discarded.|Many restaurants now use disposable cutlery and plates. 许多餐馆现在使用一次性餐具和盘子。|近义词:throwaways, expendables|反义词:reusables, durables|词性:名词

Etymology: From dispose (v.) + -able (suffix). Dispose comes from Old French dis-, meaning "apart," and poser, meaning "to place."
