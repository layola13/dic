de-anglicizes:/diːˈæŋɡləˌsaɪzɪz/
verb
去除英语化；使不那么英国化
To remove English influences or characteristics from something, typically language, culture, or institutions.
The government is trying to de-anglicize place names in the country. 政府正试图去除该国地名中的英语化痕迹。
de-anglicize the spelling of a word  将一个单词的拼写去英语化
synonyms: localize, vernacularize, indigenize
antonyms: anglicize, westernize
词性: 动词
