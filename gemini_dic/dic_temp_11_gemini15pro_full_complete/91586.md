hyperacuteness:/ˌhaɪpərəˈkjutnəs/| n. |极度敏锐，感觉过敏|  Extreme acuteness or sensitivity, especially of the senses.  It refers to a heightened or exaggerated sensitivity to stimuli. | The dog's hyperacuteness of smell allowed it to track the lost child.  这只狗极其敏锐的嗅觉使它能够追踪到失踪的孩子。 | hypersensitivity, oversensitivity | dullness, insensitivity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (Greek: 'over, beyond') + acuteness (from Latin 'acus' meaning 'needle, sharp').
