catalogue:/ˈkætəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈkætəlɔːɡ/ (US)| n. v.|目录；产品样本|A list of items, typically one in alphabetical or other systematic order, especially of items for sale or available information.|例句：The library has a catalogue of all its books. 图书馆有所有书籍的目录。|近义词：list, directory, index, register, inventory|反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly disorder, chaos if referring to the organized nature of a catalogue)|noun
catalogue:/ˈkætəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈkætəlɔːɡ/ (US)| v.|为…编目录；登记|To make a complete list of something.|例句：The museum is cataloguing its collection of paintings. 博物馆正在为它的绘画藏品编目。|近义词：list, index, register, record|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly remove, delete if referring to the action of adding to a catalogue)|verb
