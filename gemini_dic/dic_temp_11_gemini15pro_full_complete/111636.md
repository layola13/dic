localizes:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
本地化；局限；定位
To confine or restrict to a particular place or area; to adapt to local conditions.
The infection localized in his leg. 感染局限在他的腿部。
The company localizes its products for different markets.  这家公司针对不同的市场对其产品进行本地化。
synonyms: pinpoint, situate, adapt, confine, restrict
antonyms: globalize, spread, disperse, disseminate
