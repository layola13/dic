coelacanthous:/ˌsiːləˈkænθəs/ | adj. |腔棘鱼的，属于腔棘鱼类的|Relating to or resembling a coelacanth, a type of ancient fish. |例句：The coelacanthous fish is a living fossil. 腔棘鱼是一种活化石。

例句：Scientists were amazed to discover a coelacanthous fish still living in the deep ocean. 科学家们惊讶地发现腔棘鱼仍然生活在深海中。

近义词：crossopterygian (although this is a broader term) | 反义词: None (there's no direct opposite for a taxonomic adjective) | 词性: Adjective
