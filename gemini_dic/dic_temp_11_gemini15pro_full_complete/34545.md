clear-cut:/ˌklɪrˈkʌt/| adj.|清晰的，明确的，明显的|Sharply defined; unambiguous.  Free from obscurity or ambiguity; easily understood; definite.|The lawyer presented a clear-cut case to the jury. 律师向陪审团提出了一个清晰的案例。|Synonyms: definite, precise, unambiguous, evident, obvious|Antonyms: vague, ambiguous, unclear, obscure|adjective


clear-cut:/ˌklɪrˈkʌt/| n.|皆伐|The process of clearing an area of forest by cutting down all the trees.|The clear-cut left the hillside bare and vulnerable to erosion. 皆伐使得山坡光秃秃的，容易受到侵蚀。|Synonyms: deforestation, logging, clearfelling|Antonyms: reforestation, afforestation|noun
