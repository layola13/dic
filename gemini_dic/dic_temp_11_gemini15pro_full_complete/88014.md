high-teas:/ˌhaɪˈtiz/| n.|下午茶（指丰盛的下午茶，通常包含热菜）|A high tea is a substantial afternoon meal, typically including cooked dishes, as opposed to a lighter afternoon tea.|We enjoyed a delicious high-tea at the hotel. 我们在酒店享用了美味的下午茶。|近义词：afternoon tea, tea party|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑 breakfast, lunch, dinner)|名词
