daubingly:/ˈdɔːbɪŋli/ | adv. | 涂抹地，粗略地 | In a manner of daubing; smearingly or clumsily.  Applying something thickly and carelessly. | He daubingly applied the paint to the canvas, creating a messy, abstract piece. 他粗略地把颜料涂在画布上，创作出一幅杂乱的抽象画。 | 近义词: smearingly, clumsily, messily, carelessly | 反义词: meticulously, carefully, precisely | 词性: 副词

daub:/dɔːb/ | v. n. | 涂抹，涂污；涂料 |  To smear (as with paint); to cover or coat carelessly or clumsily.  A smear or blob of something sticky or messy. |  She daubed some sunscreen on her nose.  她在鼻子上涂了些防晒霜。 | 近义词: smear, smudge, plaster, coat | 反义词: clean, wipe, erase | 词性: 动词/名词

词源:  From Middle English *dauben, from Old French dauber "to plaster, whitewash," probably of Germanic origin.
