nonfugitiveness:/ˌnɑːnfjuːdʒɪˈtɪvnəs/
n.
非逃亡性；不逃亡的状态
The nonfugitiveness of the defendant allowed the trial to proceed as scheduled.  被告的非逃亡状态使得审判能够按计划进行。
The judge considered the defendant's nonfugitiveness when setting bail. 法官在设定保释金时考虑了被告的非逃亡状态。
Near Antonyms: fugitiveness, flightiness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + fugitiveness.  Fugitiveness comes from Late Latin fugitivus (“fleeting”), from Latin fugit (“flees”), from fugere (“to flee”).
