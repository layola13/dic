aircrew: /ˈɛərkruː/
noun
机组人员，机组
The aircrew prepared the plane for takeoff.  机组人员准备飞机起飞。
The entire aircrew was highly trained. 整个机组人员都受过严格训练。
The aircrew consists of pilots, flight attendants, and engineers. 机组人员包括飞行员、空乘人员和工程师。
Synonyms: flight crew, cockpit crew, cabin crew
Antonyms: passengers
