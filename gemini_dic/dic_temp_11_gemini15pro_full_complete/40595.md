convalesces:/ˌkɒnvəˈlɛsɪz/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛsɪz/| v. |恢复健康，康复|To recover health and strength gradually after sickness or weakness. |例句：She is convalescing at home after her operation. 她手术后正在家康复。

例句：He spent a month convalescing in the countryside. 他在乡下疗养了一个月。

近义词：recover, recuperate, improve, heal|反义词: deteriorate, weaken, decline |词性:动词
