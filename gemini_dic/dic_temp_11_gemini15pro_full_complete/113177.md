lurdan:/ˈlɜːrdən/| n.|懒汉；笨蛋；懦夫|A lazy, sluggish person; a coward.|例句：He was such a lurdan, always sleeping in and avoiding work. 他真是个懒汉，总是睡懒觉，逃避工作。

例句：Don't be such a lurdan! Get up and help me with this. 别这么懒了！起来帮我做这个。|近义词：sloth, idler, sluggard, laggard, drone, dawdler|反义词：go-getter, hard worker, dynamo|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lorden, *lurden, from Old French *lourdin, from lourde (“heavy”).  Ultimately from Frankish *lurd (“heavy, inert, clumsy”).
