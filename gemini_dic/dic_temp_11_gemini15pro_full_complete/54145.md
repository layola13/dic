disengagements:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/
n.
脱离，解脱，撤离
The act of disengaging; the state of being disengaged.  A release from an engagement or obligation.  A withdrawal of military forces from an area.

The disengagements between the two armies reduced the tension along the border.  两军之间的脱离接触减少了边境的紧张局势。

withdrawal, detachment, separation, extrication
engagement, involvement, commitment, connection
noun
