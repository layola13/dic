deathlessly:/ˈdeθləsli/| adv.|不朽地，永恒地|In a manner that suggests immortality; eternally.|His name will live deathlessly in the annals of history. 他的名字将永远铭刻在历史的史册上。|She sang deathlessly, her voice echoing through the ages. 她永恒地歌唱，她的声音回荡在各个时代。|近义词：eternally, immortally, forever, perpetually|反义词：mortally, transiently, ephemerally|词性:副词
Etymology:  From "deathless" + "-ly."  "Deathless" comes from "death" (Old English "dēaþ") + "-less" (Old English "-lēas," meaning "without").
