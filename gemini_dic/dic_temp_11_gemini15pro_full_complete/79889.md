gnathopodite:/ˈnæθəpəˌdaɪt/| n. |颚足|A jointed appendage of the head of an arthropod, often modified for feeding.|The crayfish uses its gnathopodites to grasp food. 小龙虾用它的颚足抓住食物。|Synonym: mouthpart|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps body segment)|noun
