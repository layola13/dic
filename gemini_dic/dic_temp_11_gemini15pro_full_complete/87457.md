hesperidin:/ˌhɛspəˈrɪdɪn/ | n. | 橙皮苷，维生素P | A flavanone glycoside found in citrus fruits, especially oranges, and used as a dietary supplement. It is believed to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. |  Hesperidin is found in high concentrations in oranges. 橙皮苷在橙子中的含量很高。|  Hesperidin supplements are often taken to improve blood circulation. 橙皮苷补充剂通常用于改善血液循环。 | synonyms: vitamin P, bioflavonoid | antonyms: N/A | noun
