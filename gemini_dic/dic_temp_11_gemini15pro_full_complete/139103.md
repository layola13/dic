obdurate:/ˈɒbdjʊrət/ (US) /ˈɑːbdərət/ (UK)| adj.|顽固的，执拗的，冷酷无情的|Hardened in feelings; stubbornly persistent in wrongdoing.  Unrepentant; resistant to persuasion or softening influences.|The obdurate child refused to eat his vegetables.  这个固执的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。|synonyms: stubborn, obstinate, inflexible, unyielding, adamant, intractable, impenitent, hardened, callous|antonyms: compliant, yielding, amenable, flexible, remorseful, compassionate|adjective
