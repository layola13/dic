covariant:/koʊˈværiənt/| adj.|协变的，共变的|Varying in the same manner or direction; changing or behaving in a similar way.  Specifically used in mathematics and computer science to describe how types or objects transform in relation to each other.|The covariant nature of the vectors ensured their compatibility in the calculations.|近义词：correlated, corresponding, related, concurrent|反义词：contravariant, invariant|adjective

covariant:/koʊˈværiənt/| n.|协变量，共变量|In statistics, a variable that is related to or influences another variable.|Researchers identified several key covariates that affected the study's outcome.|近义词：explanatory variable, predictor variable, independent variable|反义词：N/A|noun
