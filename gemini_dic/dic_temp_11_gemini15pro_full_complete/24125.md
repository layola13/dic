brey:/breɪ/
n.|糠，粗磨谷物|The brey was fed to the chickens. 糠被用来喂鸡。|The horse enjoys a mix of oats and brey. 这匹马喜欢吃燕麦和粗磨谷物的混合物。|chaff, bran|husk, grain|noun

词源:  From Middle English *brei, *bray, from Old French brei (“bran, crushed oats”), from Frankish *breis, from Proto-Germanic *breizam (“fragment”). Cognate with Old High German breisa (“crushed grain, bran”). More at braise.
