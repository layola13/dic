damagers:/ˈdæmədʒərz/
n.|损害者，造成损害的人或物|People who cause damage are called damagers. 造成损害的人被称为损害者。|The hailstorm was one of the biggest damagers of crops this year.  今年的冰雹是农作物最大的损害者之一。| destroyers, saboteurs |repairers, builders
词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  From the verb "damage" (from Old French "damager," meaning "to inflict loss or injury upon") + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments).
