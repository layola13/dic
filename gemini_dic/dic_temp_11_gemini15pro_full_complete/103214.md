jetsam: /ˈdʒɛtsəm/ | n. |  废弃物，(尤指)船只失事后漂流的货物 | Goods thrown overboard from a ship in distress, or goods washed ashore after such an event. |  The storm left a trail of jetsam along the beach.  暴风雨过后，海滩上留下一堆废弃物。 |Synonyms: wreckage, debris, flotsam| Antonyms: treasure, valuables | noun

jetsam: /ˈdʒɛtsəm/ | n. |  (比喻)被社会抛弃的人或物 | (Figurative) People or things that have been discarded or rejected by society. | The city's streets are filled with the jetsam of society.  城市的街道上充满了被社会抛弃的人。| Synonyms: outcasts, refuse | Antonyms: elite, privileged | noun
