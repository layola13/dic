noncollectively:/ˌnɑːnkəˈlɛktɪvli/
adv.
非集体地；个别地
Not collectively; individually or separately.
Each artist is judged noncollectively, based on their individual merit.  每位艺术家都是单独评判的，基于他们个人的优点。
The items were sold noncollectively, with each piece priced individually.  这些物品是非集体出售的，每件都有单独的定价。
近义词:individually, separately, singly
反义词:collectively, jointly, together
词性:副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not (来自拉丁语 non)
collective (adjective):  formed by a collection of persons or things (来自拉丁语 collectivus)
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs from adjectives
