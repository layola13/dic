fiorite:/ˌfiːəˈriːt/ | adj. | 花的，花状的；繁茂的 | Resembling or decorated with a flower or flowers; flourishing. | The wallpaper had a fiorite pattern.  壁纸上有花卉图案。|  The meadows were fiorite with wildflowers. 草地上野花盛开。 | 近义词: floral, flowery, blooming, blossoming | 反义词: barren, sterile | 词性: adjective
词源: From Italian *fiorito*, past participle of *fiorire* "to bloom, to flower," from *fiore* "flower," from Latin *flōs* (genitive *flōris*) "flower."
