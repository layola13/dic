antirational:/ˌæntiˈræʃənəl/
adjective
反理性的；非理性的
Opposed to or rejecting the use of reason.
His antirational beliefs led him to reject scientific evidence. 他反理性的信念使他拒绝接受科学证据。

antirational:/ˌæntiˈræʃənəl/
adjective
反理性的；非理性的
Characterized by a distrust of or opposition to reason.
The antirational movement gained traction during a time of social upheaval. 在社会动荡时期，反理性运动获得了动力。


Synonym:
irrational, illogical, unreasonable, absurd

Antonym:
rational, logical, reasonable, sensible

Etymology:
anti- (prefix meaning "against, opposite") + rational (from Latin rationalis, "of or belonging to reason")
