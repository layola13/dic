misproduced:/ˌmɪsprəˈdjuːst/ | /ˌmɪsprəˈduːst/
adjective | 制造不良的；生产不当的；制作拙劣的
Produced badly or incorrectly; of poor quality due to errors in production.
The misproduced batch of toys had to be recalled due to safety concerns. 这批玩具因生产不良，存在安全隐患，不得不被召回。
The play was misproduced and poorly received by critics.  这部戏剧制作拙劣，受到评论家的差评。
近义词: faulty, defective, flawed, substandard
反义词: well-made, high-quality, perfect
词性: adjective

词源:
From mis- (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + produced (past participle of produce).  "Mis-" comes from Old French "mes-" from Latin "minus" meaning "less." "Produce" comes from Latin "producere" meaning "to lead forth, bring out."
