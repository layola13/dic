batch's:/bætʃɪz/| n.|一批的，一组的|Possessive form of "batch," meaning belonging to or associated with a particular batch.|The batch's quality was inconsistent.  这一批的质量不一致。|近义词:group's, set's, lot's|反义词:individual's, single's|词性:名词所有格

词源:
From Middle English *bacche, probably from Old English *bæcce (“baking”). Cognate with Dutch bag (“baking”). More at bake.
