mutteringly:/ˈmʌtərɪŋli/ | adv. | 喃喃地；咕哝地 | In a low, indistinct voice; with a mutter.  Expressing something in a quiet and unclear way, often complaining. | He answered mutteringly, clearly unhappy with the situation.  他咕哝着回答，显然对情况很不满。| Example: She mutteringly agreed to help, but she wasn't happy about it. 她咕哝着同意帮忙，但她并不高兴。 | Synonyms: murmuringly, under one's breath, indistinctly | Antonyms: clearly, distinctly, aloud | word type: adverb
