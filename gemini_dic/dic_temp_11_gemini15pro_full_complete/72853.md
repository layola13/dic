folklore:/ˈfoʊklɔːr/ (US) /ˈfəʊklɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |民间传说，民俗|The traditional beliefs, customs, and stories of a community, passed through the generations by word of mouth.|Irish folklore is rich with tales of fairies and leprechauns. 爱尔兰民间传说中有许多关于仙女和小妖精的故事。|近义词：tradition, myth, legend, lore|反义词:fact, history|noun
