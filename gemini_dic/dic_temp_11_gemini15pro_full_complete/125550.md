multistory:/ˈmʌltiˌstɔːri/ /ˈmʌltiˌstɔːri/| adj.|多层的，多楼层的|Having multiple stories or levels.  A building with more than one story.|例句：They live in a multistory apartment building. 他们住在一栋多层公寓楼里。

例句：The multistory car park offers ample parking space. 这座多层停车场提供充足的停车位。|近义词：multilevel, multi-storey, high-rise (for tall buildings)|反义词：single-story, one-story, bungalow|词性:形容词
