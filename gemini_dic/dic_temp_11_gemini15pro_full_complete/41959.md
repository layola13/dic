cosplayer:/ˈkɒspleɪə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːspleɪər/ (US)| n. |角色扮演者|A person who dresses up as and portrays a character, especially from anime, manga, video games, or other fictional works.|例句：The cosplayer's costume was incredibly detailed and accurate. 这位角色扮演者的服装非常精致和准确。|例句：Many cosplayers attend conventions to show off their creations and meet other fans. 许多角色扮演者参加展会来展示他们的作品并与其他粉丝见面。|近义词：role-player, costume player|反义词: None|词性: Noun
