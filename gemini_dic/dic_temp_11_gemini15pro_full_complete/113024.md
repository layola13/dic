lumic:/ˈluːmɪk/| adj.|发光的，光致的|Relating to light or luminescence.  Pertaining to light; luminous.  Often used in scientific contexts.|The lumic properties of the material were studied extensively.  对这种材料的发光特性进行了广泛的研究。|近义词：luminous, bright, shining, radiant|反义词：dark, dim, opaque|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin "lumen" (light) + "-ic" (suffix forming adjectives).
