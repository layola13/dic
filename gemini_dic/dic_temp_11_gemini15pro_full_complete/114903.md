man-sard:/ˈmænˌsɑːrd/ | n. | 阁楼; 屋顶房间 | A mansard or mansard roof (also called a French roof or curb roof) is a four-sided gambrel-style hip roof characterized by two slopes on each of its sides with the lower slope, punctured by dormer windows, at a steeper angle than the upper. | The old house had a mansard roof with small windows peeking out.  那栋老房子有一个屋顶房间，上面有一些小窗户探出来。 | synonyms: attic, garret, loft | antonyms: basement, cellar | noun
