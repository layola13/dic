dioecism: /daɪˈiːsɪzəm/ | n. | 雌雄异株 | The condition of having the male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals. | Holly trees exhibit dioecism, meaning you need both a male and a female plant for berry production. 冬青树表现出雌雄异株，这意味着你需要雄性和雌性植物才能生产浆果。| synonym: gonochorism | antonym: monoecism | noun

dioecy: /daɪˈiːsi/ | n. | 雌雄异株 | The state of being dioecious.  | Dioecy is common in certain plant families. 雌雄异株在某些植物科中很常见。| synonym: gonochorism | antonym: monoecy | noun
