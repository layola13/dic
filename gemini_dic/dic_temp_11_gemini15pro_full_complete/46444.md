daguerrean:/dəˈɡɛriən/
adjective|达盖尔银版法的；早期的|Relating to the daguerreotype process, an early photographic method.
The daguerrean image was remarkably detailed for its time.|这幅达盖尔银版照片以其时代而言非常精细。
近义词:early photographic, primitive photographic
反义词:modern photographic
词性:adjective

daguerrean:/dəˈɡɛriən/
noun|达盖尔银版照片|A photograph taken by the daguerreotype process.
The museum displayed a collection of daguerreans from the 19th century.|博物馆展出了一批19世纪的达盖尔银版照片。
近义词:daguerreotype
反义词: (No specific antonym, could use a modern photograph type like "digital photo")
词性:noun
