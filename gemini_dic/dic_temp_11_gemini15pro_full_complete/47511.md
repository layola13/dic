dears:/dɪrz/| n.|亲爱的们；各位；|A term of endearment used to address a group of people affectionately.|My dears, I have some exciting news! 亲爱的们，我有一些激动人心的消息！|近义词：loved ones, darlings|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "dēore," meaning "precious, valuable, beloved."  Related to Old High German "tiuri" (expensive, precious) and Gothic "dius" (wild animal, beast), reflecting the original sense of something highly valued.
