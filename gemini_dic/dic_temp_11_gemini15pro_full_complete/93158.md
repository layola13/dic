ignitability:/ɪɡˌnɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可燃性，易燃性 | The ignitability of a material is a measure of how easily it catches fire.  |  The ignitability of dry leaves is high, especially in autumn. 干树叶的易燃性很高，尤其是在秋天。| flammability, combustibility | non-flammability, incombustibility | noun
