bibulous:/ˈbɪbjʊləs/
adj.
嗜酒的; 吸水的;
Excessively fond of alcoholic beverages; absorbent.
He became quite bibulous after his wife left him.  他妻子离开他之后，他变得非常嗜酒。
The bibulous paper towels quickly soaked up the spill.  吸水的纸巾很快吸干了溢出的液体。
近义词: alcoholic, absorbent, porous
反义词: abstemious, teetotal, impermeable
词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin bibulus (“drinking, absorbent”), from bibō (“I drink”).
