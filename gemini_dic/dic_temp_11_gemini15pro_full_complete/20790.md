blare: /blɛr/ /bler/ | n. v. | 刺耳的声音，发出刺耳的声音 | a loud, harsh sound | The car horn blared loudly. 汽车喇叭发出刺耳的声音。 | 近义词: blast, roar, bellow | 反义词: murmur, whisper | 词性:名词,动词
词源:  Probably imitative.  Possibly from Middle Dutch *blaren "to blow, boast" or Middle Low German *blerren "to bellow."
