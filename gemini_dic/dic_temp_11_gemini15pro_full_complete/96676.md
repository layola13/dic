induviate:/ɪnˈdjuːvieɪt/ /ɪnˈduːvieɪt/
verb
蜕皮；脱落
to shed or cast off (a covering, such as skin, feathers, or hair).
The snake induviated its skin. 蛇蜕掉了它的皮。
近义词:shed, molt, exuviate
反义词:grow, develop
词性:v.

induviate:/ɪnˈdjuːvieɪt/ /ɪnˈduːvieɪt/
noun
蜕皮; (蛇、昆虫等的)蜕; 蜕下的皮
The cast-off skin, feathers, or hair of an animal.
The researcher studied the induviate of the insect. 研究人员研究了昆虫的蜕皮。
近义词:exuviae, slough
反义词:skin, covering
词性:n.
