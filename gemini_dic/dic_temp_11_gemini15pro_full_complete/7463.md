anisotropy:/ˌænɪˈsɒtrəpi/ | n. |各向异性 |The property of being directionally dependent, as opposed to isotropy, which implies uniformity in all directions.  |The anisotropy of the wood caused it to split easily along the grain. 木材的各向异性导致它很容易沿着纹理裂开。|近义词：nonuniformity, inhomogeneity |反义词：isotropy, uniformity |词性:名词

anisotropic:/ˌænɪsəˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. |各向异性的 |Exhibiting different properties in different directions. |Liquid crystals are anisotropic materials. 液晶是各向异性的材料。|近义词：nonuniform, inhomogeneous |反义词：isotropic, uniform |词性:形容词
