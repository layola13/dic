homogentisic:/ˌhɒmədʒɛnˈtɪsɪk/ |adj.|尿黑酸的；褐黑色素的|Relating to or designating homogentisic acid.|Homogentisic acid is an intermediate in the metabolism of tyrosine and phenylalanine.|近义词:alkaptonuric (related to alkaptonuria, a condition caused by the buildup of homogentisic acid)|反义词: None|词性: Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From homo- (same) + gentisic (from gentisin, a yellow pigment found in gentian root) + -ic (adjectival suffix).  Gentisic acid was originally thought to be related to homogentisic acid.
