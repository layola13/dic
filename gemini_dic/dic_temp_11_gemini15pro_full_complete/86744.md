help oneself to:/ˌhɛlp wʌnˈsɛlf tuː/
|phrasal verb|
|自取；自行取用|
|To serve oneself with something, typically food or drink, without asking permission.|
|例句：There's plenty of food, so please help yourself to whatever you want. 食物很充足，请随意取用你想要的。|

|例句：Feel free to help yourself to the coffee and biscuits.  请随意取用咖啡和饼干。|

|近义词：serve oneself, take, grab|
|反义词:|
|词性:phrasal verb|
