hibernation:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 冬眠 | A state of inactivity and metabolic depression in endotherms (warm-blooded animals).  Characterized by lower body temperature, slower breathing, and lower metabolic rate. | Bears go into hibernation during the winter. 熊在冬天进入冬眠状态。 | The chipmunk entered hibernation as the weather grew colder.  花栗鼠随着天气变冷进入冬眠。 |近义词：dormancy, sleep, torpor |反义词：activity, wakefulness | noun
