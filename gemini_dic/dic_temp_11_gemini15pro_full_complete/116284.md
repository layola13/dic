masseurs:/mæˈsɜːrz/ /məˈsɜːz/
n.
按摩师（通常指男性）
People who give massages, typically men.
The masseurs at the spa were highly skilled.  这家水疗中心的按摩师技术娴熟。
近义词:massage therapists, masseuses
反义词:None
词性:Noun

Etymology:
From French masseur, masculine of masseuse, from masser "to massage," from Arabic massa "to touch, to handle, to feel."
