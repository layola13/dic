interungular:/ˌɪntərˈʌŋɡjʊlər/
adjective
趾间的，爪间的
Situated between the toes or claws.
Example: Interungular tinea is a common fungal infection. 趾间癣是一种常见的真菌感染。

Example:  The vet found a small lesion in the interungular space of the dog's paw.  兽医在狗爪的趾间隙发现了一个小病灶。
Synonym: interdigital
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "dorsal" or "plantar" for surfaces of the paw/foot)
Word type: Adjective
