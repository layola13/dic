dimorphic:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|二形的，二态的|Occurring in or representing two distinct forms.  Having two different forms, especially during a life cycle.|The dimorphic fungus exists as both a yeast and a mold.  这种二形真菌以酵母和霉菌两种形式存在。|dimorphous, biform|monomorphic|adjective
dimorphism:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n.|二态性，二形性|The existence of two different forms of individuals within the same species or group.  The instance or condition of having two different forms.|Sexual dimorphism is common in birds. 鸟类的性别二态性很常见。|dimorphy|monomorphism|noun
