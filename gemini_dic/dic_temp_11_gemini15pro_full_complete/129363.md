nolo:/ˈnoʊloʊ/
adjective
不愿的，不情愿的
unwilling, reluctant; specifically, disinclined to prosecute further
The district attorney entered a *nolo contendere* plea. 地方检察官提出了不抗辩的请求。
Near Antonyms: eager, keen, willing, avid
Antonyms: voluntary, willing
词性: adjective
