non-migratory:/ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrətri/ | /ˌnɑːnˈmaɪɡrətɔːri/ | adj. | 非迁徙的，不迁徙的 | Not migratory; not migrating.  Referring to animals that do not move from one region to another with the change of seasons. | Robins are non-migratory birds in some areas. 知更鸟在某些地区是不迁徙的鸟类。| The park is home to several non-migratory species of birds.  这个公园是几种不迁徙鸟类的家园。 |  resident, sedentary | migratory, nomadic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + migratory.  "Migratory" comes from the Late Latin "migratorius," from "migrare" (to migrate).
