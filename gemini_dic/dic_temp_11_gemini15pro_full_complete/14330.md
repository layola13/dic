automats:/ɔːˈtɒmət/ /ɔːˈtɑːmət/ | n. | 自动售货机; 自动机 | A machine that automatically performs a sequence of operations or responds to encoded instructions. |  I bought a snack from the automats. 我从自动售货机买了一份零食。| synonyms: vending machine, automated machine, robot, apparatus | antonyms: manual machine | noun

automaton:/ɔːˈtɒmətən/ /ɔːˈtɑːmətɑːn/ | n. | 自动机; 机器人; 行尸走肉 | A moving mechanical device made in imitation of a human being.  A person who acts in a mechanical or unemotional way. | The factory uses automatons for welding. 工厂使用机器人焊接。 He moved like an automaton, his face expressionless. 他像个机器人一样移动，面无表情。| synonyms: robot, machine, android, bot | antonyms: human | noun
