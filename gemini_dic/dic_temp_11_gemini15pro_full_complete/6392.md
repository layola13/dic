amphigony: /æmˈfɪɡəni/ | n. | 两性生殖，有性生殖 | Sexual reproduction involving the fusion of two gametes, one from each parent. | Amphigony is the most common form of reproduction in animals. 两性生殖是动物中最常见的繁殖方式。| The process of amphigony ensures genetic diversity. 两性生殖的过程确保了遗传多样性。 | synonyms: sexual reproduction, syngamy | antonyms: asexual reproduction | noun
