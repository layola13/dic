monopolize:/məˈnɒpəlaɪz/ (US) /məˈnɒpəlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |垄断，独占|To obtain exclusive possession or control of.| The company monopolized the market for that particular software.  这家公司垄断了该特定软件的市场。| The giant corporation effectively monopolized the telecommunications industry.  这家巨型公司实际上垄断了电信行业。|近义词：dominate, control, corner|反义词: share, distribute |词性:动词

monopolization:/məˌnɒpəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (US) /məˌnɒpəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |垄断，独占|The act of monopolizing something.| The monopolization of the market stifled competition and innovation. 对市场的垄断扼杀了竞争和创新。|  The government is investigating the company for monopolization of resources. 政府正在调查该公司垄断资源的行为。|近义词： domination, control |反义词: sharing, distribution |词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *monopoliser*, from *monopole* ("monopoly"), from Greek *mónos* ("alone, single") + *póleos* ("to sell").
