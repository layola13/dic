dwarfs:/dwɔːrfz/| n.|矮人；侏儒；矮小的动植物|A mythical being like a small man, who is usually said to live underground and have magic powers.  A person, animal, or plant that is much smaller than the usual size.|Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs.  白雪公主和七个小矮人|synonyms: gnome, midget, pygmy|antonyms: giant, titan|noun
dwarfs:/dwɔːrfz/| v. (dwarfs, dwarfing, dwarfed)|使显得矮小；使相形见绌|To make something seem small or unimportant by comparison.|The new skyscraper dwarfs all the surrounding buildings.  这座新的摩天大楼使周围所有建筑物都显得矮小。|synonyms: overshadow, eclipse, diminish|antonyms: enhance, magnify, amplify|verb
