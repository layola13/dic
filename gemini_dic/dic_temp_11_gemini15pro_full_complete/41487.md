cornshuck:/ˈkɔːrnʃʌk/ |/ˈkɔːrnˌhʌsk/
n.|玉米壳，玉米皮|The dry outer covering of an ear of corn.  The cornshuck can be used for various purposes, including animal feed, mulch, and crafts.|例句：The farmer collected the cornshucks after harvesting the corn. 农民在收完玉米后收集了玉米壳。|近义词：husk, shell|反义词: kernel (referring to the edible part)|noun
