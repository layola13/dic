coniine:/ˈkəʊniːn/ /ˈkoʊniːn/ | n. |毒芹碱|A poisonous alkaloid, C₈H₁₇N, extracted from poison hemlock (Conium maculatum). It is a neurotoxin, causing paralysis of motor nerve endings.|例句：Coniine is a potent neurotoxin that can be fatal if ingested. 毒芹碱是一种强效神经毒素，如果摄入可能会致命。

例句：Socrates was famously executed by drinking a hemlock concoction containing coniine.  苏格拉底因饮用含有毒芹碱的毒芹汁而被处决，这是众所周知的。|近义词：neurotoxin, poison |反义词:antidote, remedy |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Conium (the hemlock genus) + -ine (a suffix used to form the names of alkaloids).  Conium comes from the Greek word *kṓneion* meaning "hemlock".
