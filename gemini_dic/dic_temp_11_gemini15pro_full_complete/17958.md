befogged:/bɪˈfɒɡd/ | adj. | 迷雾笼罩的；困惑的 | Mentally confused; obscured as if by fog. |  The hikers were befogged in the dense mountain mist.  徒步旅行者在浓密的山雾中迷失了方向。

The complex instructions left her feeling utterly befogged. 复杂的指示让她感到十分困惑。| 近义词：confused, bewildered, perplexed, hazy, obscure | 反义词：clear, lucid, distinct | 词性:形容词
