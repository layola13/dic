nonperfections:/ˌnɒnpərˈfɛkʃənz/
n.
缺陷，瑕疵，不完美之处
Flaws, imperfections, or shortcomings.  The state of being imperfect.
The artist embraced the nonperfections in the pottery, seeing them as adding character.  这位艺术家欣然接受陶器中的不完美之处，认为它们增添了特色。
Synonyms: flaws, defects, imperfections, shortcomings, blemishes
Antonyms: perfections, flawlessness, impeccability
词性: 名词
