incredulity:/ˌɪnkrəˈduːləti/ | n. |怀疑，不相信|the state of being unwilling or unable to believe something|He stared at me in incredulity. 他难以置信地盯着我。|近义词：disbelief, skepticism, doubt|反义词：belief, faith, trust|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin incredulitas, from incredulus ‘unbelieving’ (see incredulous).
