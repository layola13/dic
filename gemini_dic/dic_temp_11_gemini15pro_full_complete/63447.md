entrench:/ɪnˈtrɛntʃ/ /ɛnˈtrɛntʃ/| v. | 壕固；保卫；牢固确立；根深蒂固 | To establish something firmly so that it is very difficult to change. | The government's policies are now deeply entrenched. 政府的政策现在根深蒂固。|  The soldiers entrenched themselves behind the hill. 士兵们在山后构筑了防御工事。 | synonyms: establish, fortify, secure, embed, root | antonyms: dislodge, undermine, weaken, uproot | verb
