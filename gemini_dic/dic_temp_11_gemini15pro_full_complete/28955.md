carry-ons: /ˈkæriˌɑːnz/ (US), /ˈkæriˌɒnz/ (UK) | n. | 手提行李 | Small bags or suitcases that passengers are allowed to take with them onto an airplane or other form of public transport. | Example:  Please place your carry-ons in the overhead compartment.  请把您的随身行李放在头顶的行李舱内。

Example:  The airline has strict regulations about the size and weight of carry-ons.  这家航空公司对随身行李的尺寸和重量有严格的规定。 | 近义词: hand luggage, cabin baggage | 反义词: checked baggage, checked luggage | 词性: noun
