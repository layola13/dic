jambalaya:/ˌdʒʌmbəˈlaɪə/ | n. |什锦饭，印第安什锦饭|A Creole and Cajun dish of rice, vegetables, and meat or shellfish.  A spicy mixture. |例句：We had a delicious jambalaya for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了一顿美味的什锦饭。

|例句：The jambalaya was full of shrimp, chicken, and sausage. 这份什锦饭里有满满的虾、鸡肉和香肠。|近义词：gumbo, paella, stew|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain rice") | 词性: noun
