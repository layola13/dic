henpeck: /ˈhɛnˌpɛk/| v. | 责骂，不断挑剔(尤指妻子对丈夫) | To continually nag and criticize (especially a wife to her husband). | He was constantly henpecked by his wife for not doing enough chores. 他经常被他妻子责骂，说他做的家务不够多。|  nag, criticize, carp, scold | support, encourage, praise | verb

henpeck: /ˈhɛnˌpɛk/| adj. | 惧内的，怕老婆的 |  Continually nagged and criticized by one's wife. |  He's so henpecked he won't even go out with his friends without her permission. 他太怕老婆了，甚至没有她允许都不敢和朋友出去。 | timid, submissive, cowardly | dominant, assertive, bold | adjective
