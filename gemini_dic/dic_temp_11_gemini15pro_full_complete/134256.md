ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not".
inflammable (adjective): From Late Latin inflammabilis "easily set on fire," from Latin inflammare "to set on fire," from in- "in, into" + flamma "flame," from Proto-Italic *flagma-, from Proto-Indo-European *bhleg- "to shine, flash, burn."
-ity (suffix):  From Latin -itas, a suffix forming abstract nouns of quality.
-ies (suffix): Plural form of nouns ending in -y.
