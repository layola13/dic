gurglingly:/ˈɡɜːrɡlɪŋli/ | adv. |汩汩地，咕噜咕噜地 |  Describes an action or sound resembling the bubbling and murmuring of water. It is formed by adding the adverbial suffix "-ly" to the word "gurgling," which is itself derived from the verb "gurgle." | The brook gurgled gurglingly over the rocks. 小溪汩汩地流过岩石。| The baby drank his milk gurglingly. 婴儿咕噜咕噜地喝着牛奶。| 近义词: bubblingly, murmuringly | 反义词: silently, quietly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From gurgle + -ly.  "Gurgle" is imitative of the sound.  It first appeared in the late 16th century.
