multi-cultural:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 多元文化的 | Relating to or containing several cultural or ethnic groups within a society. |  London is a multi-cultural city. 伦敦是一个多元文化的城市。|  The school has a multi-cultural student body. 这所学校的学生群体具有多元文化背景。 | multicultural, diverse, cosmopolitan | monocultural, homogeneous | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
"multi-" (prefix): from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much"
"-cultural" (suffix): from "culture" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives)
"culture": from Latin "cultura" meaning "cultivation, agriculture," later broadened to encompass intellectual and artistic development.
