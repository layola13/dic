agonistical:/ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 竞争的，好斗的 | Relating to contests, competition, or struggling; combative. |  He displayed an agonistical spirit throughout the tournament, refusing to give up even when he was down. 他在整个比赛中都表现出一种好斗的精神，即使在落后的情况下也拒绝放弃。

The two companies engaged in an agonistical battle for market share.  这两家公司为争夺市场份额展开了一场激烈的竞争。 | 近义词: competitive, combative, antagonistic | 反义词: cooperative, peaceful, conciliatory | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Greek *agōnistikos*, from *agōnistēs* "competitor," from *agōn* "contest." 源自希腊语 *agōnistikos*，来自 *agōnistēs* “竞争者”，来自 *agōn* “竞赛”。
