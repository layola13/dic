deadish:/ˈdɛdɪʃ/| adj.|有点死的，接近死的，死气沉沉的|Resembling or suggesting death; somewhat dead, or appearing nearly dead.  It can also describe something lacking liveliness or excitement.|The fish looked deadish, floating belly-up. 那条鱼看起来快死了，肚子朝上漂着。|Near death, lifeless, dull|Lively, vibrant, energetic|adjective
