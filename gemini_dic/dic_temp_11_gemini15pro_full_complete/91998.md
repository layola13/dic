hypernymy:/ˌhaɪpərˈnɪmi/| n.|上位词，上义词|A word or concept that is more general than a more specific word or concept.  It represents a category or class to which more specific terms belong.  For example, "fruit" is a hypernym of "apple," "banana," and "orange."|Fruit is a hypernym of apple. 水果是苹果的上位词。|synonyms: superordinate, generic term|antonyms: hyponym, subordinate term|noun
