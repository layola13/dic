has-beens:/ˈhæzˌbiːnz/
noun|过气的人，曾经辉煌过的人|people or things that were once successful or important but are no longer so|例句：Many of the actors at the convention were has-beens, clinging to past glories.  许多参加大会的演员都是过气的人物， clinging着过去的荣耀。|例句：The team is full of has-beens who are past their prime. 这个团队充满了巅峰已过的老将。|近义词：wasted, washed-up, former, ex-|反义词：up-and-comers, rising stars|词性:n.
