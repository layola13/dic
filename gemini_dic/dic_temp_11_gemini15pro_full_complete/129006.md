nirvana:/nɪrˈvɑːnə/ /nɪrˈvænə/| n. |涅槃，极乐世界，天堂；解脱|A state of perfect happiness and peace; a state of freedom from pain and worry, especially in Buddhism.  A place or state characterized by freedom from or oblivion to pain, worry, and the external world. |例句：He has reached a state of nirvana. 他已达到了涅槃的境界。

例句：Nirvana is often described as a state of enlightenment. 涅槃常被描述为一种觉悟的状态。


|近义词：paradise, heaven, bliss, enlightenment|反义词:suffering, torment, hell|词性:noun
