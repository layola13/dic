floes:/floʊz/
n.
浮冰群；大块浮冰
A sheet of floating ice, especially one broken off from a larger mass.
The ship navigated carefully through the dangerous floes. 这艘船小心地在危险的浮冰群中航行。
近义词: ice floes, ice sheets, pack ice
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Perhaps "open water" could be considered a contextual antonym.)
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

n.
浮冰；大块浮冰
