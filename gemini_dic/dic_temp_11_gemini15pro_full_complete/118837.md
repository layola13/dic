metaphoric:/ˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪk/ /ˌmɛtəˈfɑːrɪk/| adj.|隐喻的，比喻的|Figurative; using a metaphor.  Expressing one thing in terms normally denoting another.  Not literal.|Her language was rich and metaphoric, filled with vivid imagery. 她的语言丰富而隐喻，充满了生动的意象。|Synonyms: figurative, allegorical, symbolic, poetic|Antonyms: literal, prosaic|adjective

metaphorical:/ˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪkəl/ /ˌmɛtəˈfɑːrɪkəl/| adj.|隐喻的，比喻的|Same as metaphoric.|He used the image of a caged bird as a metaphorical representation of his feelings of being trapped. 他用笼中鸟的形象来比喻他被困住的感觉。|Synonyms: figurative, allegorical, symbolic, poetic|Antonyms: literal, prosaic|adjective
