The correct spelling is "efficiency".  "Efficience" is an archaic and now largely obsolete form.  Here's the breakdown for "efficiency":

efficiency: /ɪˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. | 效率，效能 | the ability to do something successfully and without wasting time or energy | The company is striving to improve efficiency.  这家公司正在努力提高效率。 |  The new machine operates with remarkable efficiency. 这台新机器运转效率非常高。 | synonyms: effectiveness, productivity, competency | antonyms: inefficiency, incompetence, wastefulness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *efficientia, from Latin ēfficiēns (“effecting, efficient”), present participle of ēfficiō (“I effect, bring about”).  Equivalent to efficient +‎ -cy.
