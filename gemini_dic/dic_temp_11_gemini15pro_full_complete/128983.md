nipperkin: /ˈnɪpərkɪn/ | n. |小酒杯，一小杯（尤指啤酒或烈酒）| A small measure of liquor, especially beer or spirits. | He took a hearty swig from his nipperkin. 他痛饮了一口他的小酒杯里的酒。|

例句：
The old sailor filled his nipperkin with rum. 老水手在他的小酒杯里倒满了朗姆酒。
She offered him a nipperkin of ale to warm him up. 她给了他一小杯麦芽酒让他暖暖身子。

近义词：
small glass, tot, measure

反义词：
flagon, tankard (These are much larger containers for drink)

词性：
noun
