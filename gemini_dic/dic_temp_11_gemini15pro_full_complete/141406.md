ophiological:/ˌɒfiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
蛇学的；与蛇有关的
relating to the branch of zoology concerned with snakes.
The ophiological collection at the museum was impressive.  博物馆的蛇类收藏令人印象深刻。

近义词:herpetological (爬虫学的)
反义词:None

词性:adjective
词源: From Greek ὄφις (óphis, “snake”) + -logical.
