browsers:/ˈbraʊzərz/| n. |浏览器|A software application used to access and view websites and other internet resources.|I use different browsers depending on the task. 我根据不同的任务使用不同的浏览器。|Example: I updated my browser to the latest version for better security. 我将浏览器更新到最新版本以提高安全性。|Synonyms: web browser, internet browser|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

browse:/braʊz/| v. |浏览，随意翻阅|To look through or glance at casually, without a specific purpose.|I like to browse through bookshops on weekends. 我喜欢在周末逛书店。|Example:  She browsed the internet for information on the topic. 她在网上浏览有关该主题的信息。|Synonyms: skim, scan, glance at, peruse|Antonyms: study, scrutinize, pore over|Verb
