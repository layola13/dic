defragmentation:/diːˌfræɡmənˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 磁盘碎片整理；整理碎片 | The process of reorganizing files on a hard drive so that they are stored contiguously, improving performance. | Regular defragmentation can improve your computer's speed. 定期整理磁盘碎片可以提高计算机的速度。| synonyms: consolidation, optimization | antonyms: fragmentation, scattering | noun

defragmentations:/diːˌfræɡmənˈteɪʃnz/ | n. | 磁盘碎片整理（复数） | Multiple instances of the process of reorganizing files on a hard drive for better performance. | The scheduled defragmentations improved the server's response time.  预定的磁盘碎片整理提高了服务器的响应时间。| synonyms: consolidations, optimizations | antonyms: fragmentations, scatterings | noun
