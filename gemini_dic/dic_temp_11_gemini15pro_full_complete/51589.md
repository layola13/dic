devalue:/diːˈvæljuː/
|v.|贬值；降低…的价值；贬低；轻视|To reduce the value of something, especially a currency, or to cause someone or something to seem less valuable or important.|The government decided to devalue the currency. 政府决定将货币贬值。|近义词:depreciate, downgrade, undervalue, diminish, belittle|反义词:appreciate, enhance, upgrade, overvalue|词性:动词

devalue:/diːˈvæljuː/
|n.|贬值|The reduction or underestimation of the worth or importance of something.|The devaluation of the currency caused economic chaos. 货币贬值导致了经济混乱。|近义词:depreciation, devaluation|反义词:appreciation, enhancement|词性:名词
