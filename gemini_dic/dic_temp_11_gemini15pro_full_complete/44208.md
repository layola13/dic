crochet:/kroʊˈʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkrəʊʃeɪ/ (UK)| n. v.|钩针编织，钩针织物|A method of creating fabric from yarn or thread using a crochet hook.|She made a beautiful baby blanket using crochet. 她用钩针编织了一条漂亮的婴儿毯。|The intricate lace was crocheted by hand. 这精美的蕾丝是用手工钩针编织的。|近义词：knit, weave|反义词: unravel, untangle|词性:名词,动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *crochet* "small hook," diminutive of *croc* "hook," from Old Norse *krókr*.
