cotransporting:/kəʊˈtrænsˌpɔːtɪŋ/ |v. | 共同转运，协同运输 |The protein is responsible for cotransporting sodium and glucose into the cell.  这种蛋白质负责将钠和葡萄糖共同转运到细胞内。| Example: The sodium-glucose cotransporter is an example of secondary active transport. 钠-葡萄糖协同转运蛋白是继发性主动转运的一个例子。|  Synonyms: symporting, coupled transport | Antonyms: antiporting, counter-transporting | Verb (present participle)

cotransport:/kəʊˈtrænsˌpɔːt/ |n. v. | 共同转运，协同运输 |Cotransport is the coupled movement of two or more different types of molecules across a cell membrane. 共同转运是指两种或多种不同类型的分子跨细胞膜的偶联运动。| Example:  Glucose cotransport with sodium allows for efficient absorption in the intestine. 葡萄糖与钠的协同转运使其在肠道中被有效吸收。| Synonyms: symport, coupled transport | Antonyms: antiport, counter-transport | Noun/Verb
