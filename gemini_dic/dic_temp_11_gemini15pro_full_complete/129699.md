non-dom:/ˌnɒnˈdɒm/ (UK), /ˌnɑːnˈdɑːm/ (US)| n. | 非 domiciled 居民，非英国税务居民 | A person who lives in the UK but is not legally domiciled there, often for tax purposes.  A non-dom is someone who lives in the UK but declares their permanent home to be outside the UK. |  Example 1:  He's a non-dom, so he doesn't pay UK tax on his overseas earnings. 他是非英国税务居民，所以他的海外收入不用在英国缴税。

Example 2:  Many wealthy individuals choose to become non-doms to avoid paying high taxes. 许多富有的个人选择成为非英国税务居民以避免缴纳高额税款。 | 近义词:  tax exile (税务流亡者), offshore resident (离岸居民) | 反义词:  domiciled resident (税务居民), UK resident (英国居民) | 词性: noun (名词)
