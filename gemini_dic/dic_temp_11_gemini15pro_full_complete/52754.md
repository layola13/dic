dilatable:/daɪˈleɪtəbl/ /dɪˈleɪtəbl/
adjective
可扩张的，可膨胀的
Capable of being dilated or expanded; able to increase in size or diameter.
The pupil of the eye is dilatable.  瞳孔是可扩张的。
The blood vessels are dilatable. 血管是可扩张的。
近义词: expandable, extensible, enlargeable
反义词: constricting, contracting
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin "dilatare" (to spread wide) + "-able" (suffix denoting capability).
