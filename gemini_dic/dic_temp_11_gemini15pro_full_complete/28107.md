capitalists:/ˈkæpɪtəlɪsts/
n.
资本家；资本主义者
People who own and control the means of production and operate them for profit.
The capitalists invested heavily in the new technology. 资本家们在新技术上投入了巨资。
The early capitalists of the Industrial Revolution greatly changed society. 工业革命早期的资本家极大地改变了社会。

synonyms: investors, financiers, business magnates, entrepreneurs, tycoons
antonyms: workers, laborers, proletariat, socialists, communists
noun
