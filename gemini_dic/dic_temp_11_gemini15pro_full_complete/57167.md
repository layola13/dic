dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| n. |销钉，木钉，暗榫|A short wooden or metal rod used for fastening things together, typically by fitting into a corresponding hole in each piece.|例句：He used a dowel to reinforce the joint. 他用销钉加固了接头。

例句：The shelves are held up by dowels.  这些架子是用木钉支撑的。|近义词：peg, pin, rod|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与连接方式相关的词，例如：glue(胶水))|词性:noun


dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| vt. |用销钉固定|To fasten or join with a dowel.|例句：He dowelled the two pieces of wood together. 他用销钉把两块木头连接在一起。

例句：The frame was dowelled and glued for extra strength.  为了增加强度，框架用销钉固定并粘合。|近义词：pin, peg, fasten|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与连接方式相关的词，例如：separate(分开)，unfasten(解开))|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Low German *dōvel, diminutive of dōve (“dove”), referring to the dovetail joint. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dūbō (“dove”).
