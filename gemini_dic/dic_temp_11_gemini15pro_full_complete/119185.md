mezzotint: /ˈmetsəʊtɪnt/ (US) /ˈmedzəʊtɪnt/ (UK) | n. | 铜版雕刻，中调版画 | A method of engraving on copper or steel, producing prints in shades of gray.  A print made using this method. |  例句：The artist was known for her exquisite mezzotints.  这位艺术家以她精美的铜版雕刻而闻名。

例句：The gallery exhibited a collection of 18th-century mezzotints.  画廊展出了一系列18世纪的铜版画。| 近义词：engraving, etching | 反义词: None (No direct opposite for a specific printing technique) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *mezzotinto*, from *mezzo* "half" + *tinto* "tinted".
