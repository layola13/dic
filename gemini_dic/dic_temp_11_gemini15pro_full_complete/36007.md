codicillary:/ˌkɒdɪˈsɪləri/ | adj. |遗嘱附录的，遗嘱修改附录的| Relating to or contained in a codicil (an addition or supplement that explains, modifies, or revokes a will or part of one).| The codicillary provision added a bequest of a valuable painting.  遗嘱附录的条款增加了一幅珍贵画作的遗赠。|The lawyer advised her client to make a codicillary addition to his will. 律师建议他的客户在他的遗嘱中添加一个附录。 |近义词: supplementary, amending|反义词: original, primary|词性:形容词
