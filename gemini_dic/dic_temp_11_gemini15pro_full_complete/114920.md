manageably:/ˈmænɪdʒəbli/| adv.|易处理地，可管理地|In a way that can be managed or controlled effectively.  It describes something that can be dealt with or handled without too much difficulty.|The project was large, but manageably so.  这个项目很大，但还是可以管理的。|Synonyms: easily, conveniently, handily, practically|Antonyms: unmanageably, difficultly, impractically|Adverb
