aggression:/əˈɡrɛʃən/| n.|侵略，攻击，进攻；敌对情绪，攻击性|The act of initiating hostilities or invasion.|The country's aggression led to war.  这个国家的侵略行为导致了战争。|近义词：invasion, encroachment, offense|反义词：defense, retreat, withdrawal|词性:名词

词源：From Latin *aggressio*, from aggress-, past participle stem of aggredi ‘attack’, from ad- ‘towards’ + gradi ‘go’.
