miniwarehouse:/ˈmɪni ˈwɛərˌhaʊs/| n. |迷你仓库，小型仓库|A small, individually rented storage space, often used by businesses or individuals for storing goods or personal belongings.|Example 1:  We rented a miniwarehouse to store our excess inventory. 我们租了一个迷你仓库来存放我们多余的库存。

Example 2: Many people use miniwarehouses to store seasonal items like holiday decorations. 很多人用迷你仓库来存放季节性物品，比如节日装饰品。|Synonyms: self-storage unit, storage unit, storage locker|Antonyms: warehouse (in general sense, not necessarily opposite but indicative of scale difference)|Noun
