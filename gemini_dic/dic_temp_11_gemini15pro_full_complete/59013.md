dvergr:/ˈdvɛrɡr/| n. |矮人,侏儒|A dwarf, especially one of the supernatural beings in Scandinavian mythology who dwelt in mountains and were skilled in metalworking.  Dvergar are associated with earth, crafting, and wisdom. They are often depicted as bearded, short in stature, and sometimes grumpy.|The ancient Norse sagas tell of dvergr crafting magical weapons for the gods. 古老的北欧萨迦讲述了矮人为神 crafting 神奇武器的故事。|Synonyms: dwarf, gnome|Antonyms: giant|noun
