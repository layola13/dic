neats:/niːts/ | n. | 牛; 牛群; 整洁的人 | Cattle, especially oxen. Also, a tidy or well-organized person. | The farmer yoked his neats to the plow. 农民把他的牛套到犁上。 He keeps his desk remarkably neat; he's one of the neats. 他把桌子收拾得非常整洁；他是一个非常爱整洁的人。 | 近义词: cattle, oxen, tidy person | 反义词: slob, messy person | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *netes, *netis, from Old English *nēat, from Proto-Germanic *nautą (“cattle, beast of burden”). Cognate with Scots nowt (“cattle”), West Frisian neat (“cattle”), Dutch rund (“cattle”), German Rind (“cattle”), Old Norse naut (“cattle”).
