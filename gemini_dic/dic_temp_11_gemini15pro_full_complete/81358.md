graphs:/ɡræfs/ | n. | 图表；曲线图 | A diagram showing the relation between variable quantities, typically of two variables, each measured along one of a pair of axes at right angles. | The graphs show a clear upward trend in sales.  这些图表显示销售额明显呈上升趋势。 | charts, diagrams | tables, lists | noun

graphs:/ɡræfs/ | v. (third-person singular simple present graphs, present participle graphing, simple past and past participle graphed) | 绘制…的图表 | To represent (something) by means of a graph. | He graphed the results of the experiment. 他绘制了实验结果的图表。 | chart, plot | tabulate, list | verb
