cowrites:/ˌkoʊˈraɪts/
verb
共同写作
writes something together with someone else
She cowrites most of her songs with her husband. 她和丈夫共同创作了她的大部分歌曲。

cowrites:/ˌkoʊˈraɪts/
noun
共同作者
a person who writes something with someone else
The play had two cowrites.  这出戏有两个共同作者。
