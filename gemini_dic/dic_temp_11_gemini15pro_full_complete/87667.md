hexagonally:/hɛkˈsæɡənəli/
adverb
六边形地，六角形地
in the shape of a hexagon; with six sides.
The tiles were arranged hexagonally on the floor.  瓷砖在地板上以六边形排列。
The crystal structure is hexagonally close-packed. 这种晶体结构是六角密排的。
近义词:  in a hexagonal pattern, six-sidedly
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider: irregularly, asymmetrically)
词性: adverb

hexagonally:/hɛkˈsæɡənəli/
词源:  From hexagon + -al + -ly.  Ultimately from Greek  ἕξ (héx, “six”) + γωνία (gōnía, “angle, corner”).
