inviolate:/ɪnˈvaɪəleɪt/| adj.|神圣不可侵犯的；未受损害的；纯洁的|free or safe from injury or violation; pure|例句：Their ancestral home remained inviolate throughout the war.  他们的祖屋在整个战争期间都完好无损。

例句：Her reputation remained inviolate despite the rumors. 尽管有谣言，她的声誉仍然完好无损。|近义词：sacred, untouchable, unblemished, pristine|反义词：violated, defiled, corrupted|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inviolatus*, from in- 'not' + violatus, past participle of violare 'to violate', from vis 'force, violence'.
