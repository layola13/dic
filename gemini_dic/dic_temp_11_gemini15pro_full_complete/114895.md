man-eaters:/ˈmænˌiːtərz/
| n. |食人者，吃人动物（通常指动物）|An animal that devours human beings.|例句：The villagers feared the man-eaters in the jungle. 村民们害怕丛林里的食人动物。|近义词：cannibals, predators|反义词:prey, herbivores|词性:名词
man-eater:/ˈmænˌiːtər/
| n. |食人者，吃人动物（通常指动物），比喻凶狠的人|An animal that devours human beings, or figuratively, a fierce or destructive person.|例句：She's a real man-eater in the boardroom. 她在董事会议室里真是个狠角色。|近义词：cannibal, predator, shark (figuratively)|反义词:prey, herbivore|词性:名词

词源: Compound of "man" + "eater."  "Eater" comes from the Old English "etere," from "etan" (to eat).
