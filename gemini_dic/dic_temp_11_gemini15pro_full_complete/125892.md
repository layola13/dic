muscularis:/ˌmʌskjʊˈlɛərɪs/
noun
肌层
The muscularis is the layer of muscle in the wall of a hollow organ.
The muscularis of the stomach contracts to churn food.  胃的肌层收缩以搅拌食物。
近义词: muscular coat, muscular layer
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a tissue layer)
词性: noun
