antifebrile: /ˌæntiˈfaɪbraɪl/ /ˌæntiˈfiːbraɪl/ | adj. n. |退热的，退热药 |Reducing fever; an agent that reduces fever.|  The doctor prescribed an antifebrile for the child's high temperature. 医生给孩子开了退烧药。|  She took an antifebrile to bring down her fever. 她服用了退烧药来退烧。| Synonyms: antipyretic, febrifuge | Antonyms: pyrogenic | adjective, noun

Etymology:
From anti- + febrile.  Anti- is a prefix meaning "against," "opposite of," or "opposed to."  Febrile comes from the Latin word "febris," meaning "fever."
