nonimperiousnesses:/ˌnɑːnɪmˈpɪriəsnəsɪz/
n.
lack of imperiousness; humility; modesty
The quality or state of not being imperious; absence of arrogance or domineering manner.
Her nonimperiousness made her a beloved leader. 她不专横的性格使她成为一位受人爱戴的领导者。
Synonyms: humility, modesty, meekness, humbleness, unassumingness
Antonyms: imperiousness, arrogance, haughtiness, domineeringness, authoritativeness
词性: 名词
