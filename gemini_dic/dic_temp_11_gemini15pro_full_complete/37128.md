come:/kʌm/ (US) /kʌm/ (UK)| vi. | 来，来到；出现；发生；变成；达到；开始；来源于 |To move or travel toward the speaker or a specified place or position. | She comes to work by bus. 她乘公共汽车来上班。

He came to see me yesterday. 他昨天来看我了。


近义词：arrive, approach, appear | 反义词: go, leave, depart | 词性: 动词
