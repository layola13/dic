moory:/ˈmʊəri/| adj.|沼泽的，泥炭的，荒凉的|Resembling or characteristic of a moor; boggy.  Covered with or consisting of peat.|The ground was soft and moory.  地面柔软且泥泞。|Near synonyms: marshy, boggy, swampy, fenny, peaty |Antonyms: dry, arid, desert|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From moor + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mōra- (bog, swamp).
