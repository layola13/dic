amnicolist: /ˌæmnɪˈkɒlɪst/
n.
淡水螺研究者; 栖息于淡水螺中的生物研究者
A person who studies amnicolids (small freshwater snails) or organisms that inhabit them.
Amnicolists have made significant contributions to our understanding of freshwater ecosystems. 淡水螺研究者为我们理解淡水生态系统做出了重大贡献。
The amnicolist carefully examined the snail's shell.  淡水螺研究者仔细检查了蜗牛的壳。
近义词: malacologist (软体动物学家),  freshwater biologist (淡水生物学家)
反义词:  无
词性: noun
