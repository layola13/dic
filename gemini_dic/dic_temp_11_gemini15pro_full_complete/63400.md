entozoologist:/ˌɛntoʊzoʊˈɑlədʒɪst/
n.
动物体内寄生虫学家
A scientist who studies entozoa, or internal parasites, especially parasitic worms.
The entomozoologist studied the life cycle of the tapeworm.  动物体内寄生虫学家研究了绦虫的生命周期。

The entomozoologist discovered a new species of roundworm in the intestines of the pig.  动物体内寄生虫学家在猪的肠道中发现了一种新的蛔虫。
