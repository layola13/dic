mythologer:/ˌmɪθɒlədʒər/| n.|神话学家|A person who studies or writes about mythology.  A scholar of myths and legends.|例句：The mythologer explained the origins of the Greek gods.  这位神话学家解释了希腊诸神的起源。

例句：She's a renowned mythologer specializing in Norse mythology. 她是一位著名的北欧神话专家。|近义词：mythologist, student of mythology, folklorist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
