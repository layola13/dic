elizabethans:/ɪˌlɪzəˈbiːθənz/
noun
伊丽莎白时代的人；伊丽莎白女王时代的作品
People who lived during the reign of Queen Elizabeth I (1558-1603). Also refers to the artistic and literary works of that period.
Shakespeare was one of the most famous Elizabethans.  莎士比亚是最著名的伊丽莎白时代人物之一。
The Globe Theatre is a reconstruction of an Elizabethan playhouse.  环球剧场是伊丽莎白时代剧院的重建。
Synonym:Elizabethan people, Elizabethan era figures
Antonym:N/A
