nondualistically:/ˌnɒnˌdʒuːəˈlɪstɪkli/
adv.
非二元地，非对立地
In a way that does not perceive a separation or duality between subject and object, self and other, or mind and matter.
She experienced the world nondualistically, seeing the interconnectedness of all things.
近义词: holistically, integrally, unifiedly
反义词: dualistically, separately
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From non-dualistic + -ally.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Dualistic comes from dual + -istic. Dual refers to "two," from Latin *dualis, related to duo "two."  The suffix -istic forms adjectives.  Finally, -ally forms adverbs from adjectives.
