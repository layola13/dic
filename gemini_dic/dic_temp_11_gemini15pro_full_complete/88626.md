hm:/hm/, /m̩/ | interj. | 嗯，哼 | A sound made to express hesitation, doubt, or disagreement, or to indicate that one is thinking. | Hm, I'm not sure about that. 嗯，我不确定。 | 近义词: uh, huh, um | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound) | 词性: interjection


hm:/m̩/, /hm/ | n. | 哼声 | The sound represented by "hm." |  She gave a noncommittal hm. 她不置可否地哼了一声。 | 近义词: murmur, grunt | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound) | 词性: noun

词源:  Imitative origin, representing a thoughtful murmur.
