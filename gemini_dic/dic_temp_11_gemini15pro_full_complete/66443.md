exemplar:/ɪɡˈzɛmplər/ /ɪɡˈzæmplər/ | n. |范例，样本，典型|A person or thing serving as a typical example or excellent model. |例句：These students are exemplars of hard work and dedication. 这些学生是勤奋和奉献的典范。

例句：The teacher used the student's essay as an exemplar for the class. 老师用这个学生的文章作为全班的范例。|近义词：example, model, paradigm, archetype, prototype |反义词: anomaly, exception |词性:noun
