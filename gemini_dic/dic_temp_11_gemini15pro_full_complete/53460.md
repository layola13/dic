disapproving:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvɪŋ/ | adj. | 不赞成的，表示反对的 | Showing that you do not approve of something or someone. | She gave me a disapproving look when I mentioned I was quitting my job. 她听说我要辞职时，给了我一个不赞成的眼神。| synonyms: critical, censorious, condemnatory, reproving, | antonyms: approving, supportive, encouraging | adjective

disapproving:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvɪŋ/ | adj. | 不以为然的 | Showing that you feel something or someone is not good enough. | He made a disapproving noise when he saw the messy room. 当他看到凌乱的房间时，发出了一声不以为然的声音。| synonyms: displeased, dissatisfied, | antonyms: pleased, satisfied | adjective
