archlet: /ˈɑːrtʃlɪt/
n.
软件架构师
A software designer or architect.
Archlets encapsulate decisions about the structure and behavior of architectural elements.  架构组件封装了关于架构元素的结构和行为的决策。
The archlet specifies the interaction between the client and the server. 这个架构组件指定了客户端和服务器之间的交互。
近义词: architect, designer
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "developer" in a limited context.)
词性: noun

Etymology:  A blend of "architect" and "-let" (a diminutive suffix).
