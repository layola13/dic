outdesign:/ˌaʊtdɪˈzaɪn/
verb
胜过…的设计; 设计得比…更好
to design something that is better or more advanced than something else
例句：They've completely outdesigned their competitors with this new model.  他们用这款新模型彻底在设计上胜过了竞争对手。

例句：The company is constantly striving to outdesign and outperform its rivals.  该公司不断努力在设计和性能上超越其竞争对手。

近义词：surpass, outdo, outperform, eclipse
反义词：be outdesigned, be inferior in design


词性：v.


词源：out- + design.  "Out-" prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing.
