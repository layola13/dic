isometrical:/ˌaɪsəˈmetrɪkl/ | adj. | 等距的，等轴测的 | Relating to or characterized by equality of measure, especially in three dimensions; having equal dimensions or measurements.  Relating to isometric projection. | The drawing was done in an isometrical projection. 这幅图是用等轴测投影法绘制的。 |  The crystal structure is isometrical. 晶体结构是等距的。| synonyms: isometric, equidimensional | antonyms: anisometrical | adjective

isometry:/aɪˈsɒmɪtri/ | n. | 等距 | Equality of measure. | The isometry of the two figures was remarkable. 这两个图形的等距性非常显著。| synonyms: equality, congruence | antonyms: inequality, incongruence | noun
