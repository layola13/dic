词源:
From Old English *gōdspel (unattested), from gōd "good" + spel "story, message".  Displaced Old English godspell, which is cognate with Old Saxon godspell, Old Frisian godspell, Dutch godspel, Old High German gotspel, German Gottesspiel.  Literally "good news".
