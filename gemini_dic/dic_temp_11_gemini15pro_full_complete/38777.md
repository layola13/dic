conducive:/kənˈd(j)uːsɪv/
adjective
有益的，有帮助的
making a certain situation or outcome likely or possible.
例句：Regular exercise is conducive to good health.  规律的运动有益于健康。

例句：A quiet environment is conducive to studying. 安静的环境有利于学习。

近义词：beneficial, helpful, favorable, advantageous
反义词：detrimental, harmful, unfavorable, disadvantageous
词性：adjective

词源:  From Late Latin conducivus "leading, helpful," from Latin conducere "to lead or bring together, contribute, be advantageous," from com- "together" (see com-) + ducere "to lead" (see duke).
