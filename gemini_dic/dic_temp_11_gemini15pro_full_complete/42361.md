counterassassination:/ˌkaʊntərəˌsæsɪˈneɪʃən/
|n.|反暗杀，阻止暗杀|The counterassassination plot was foiled by the secret service. 反暗杀阴谋被特勤局挫败了。|
|英文解析: The act of assassinating someone in order to prevent them from assassinating someone else, or the act of preventing an assassination.|
|例句：The president's security detail planned a counterassassination should an attempt on his life be made. 如果有人企图暗杀总统，总统的安保人员计划进行反暗杀。|

|例句：The agency specializes in counterassassination and protecting high-profile individuals. 该机构专门从事反暗杀和保护重要人物。|
|近义词：preemptive strike, preventive measure|
|反义词: assassination|
|词性: noun|
