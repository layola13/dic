chaliced:/ˈtʃælɪst/| adj.|圣杯形的，杯状的|Resembling or relating to a chalice, a large cup or goblet, often used in religious ceremonies.  Like a goblet; shaped like a cup. |The knight drank from the chaliced cup. 骑士从圣杯状的杯子中喝水。|近义词：cup-shaped, goblet-shaped|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'flat' or 'unshaped')|词性: adjective

词源: From "chalice" + "-ed."  "Chalice" comes from Old French "calice," ultimately from Latin "calix," meaning "cup," "goblet."
