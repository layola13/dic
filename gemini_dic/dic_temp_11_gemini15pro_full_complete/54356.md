dishonorable:/dɪsˈɑːnərəbl/ | adj. | 不光彩的，可耻的 | Not deserving respect; shameful.  Lacking honor or integrity. |  His dishonorable conduct led to his dismissal from the army. 他的不光彩行为导致他被军队开除。 |  His dishonorable actions brought shame to his family. 他的可耻行为给他的家人带来了耻辱。| 近义词: shameful, disgraceful, ignoble, base, contemptible | 反义词: honorable, respectable, noble, virtuous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From dis- (expressing negation) + honorable, from Old French honorable, from Latin honorabilis "worthy of honor," from honor "honor."
