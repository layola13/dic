icositetrahedron:/aɪˌkɒsɪˌtɛtrəˈhiːdrən/ |n.|二十四面体；斜方二十四面体|A polyhedron with 24 faces, especially one in which all the faces are congruent deltoids (also called a trapezoidal icositetrahedron or a tetragonal icositetrahedron).|The mineral analcime often forms icositetrahedra.| 近义词:trapezoidal icositetrahedron, tetragonal icositetrahedron|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific geometric shape)|Noun
