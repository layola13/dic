beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/ | v. | 欺骗；使着迷；消磨(时间) | To charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way. To trick (someone) into doing something. To help (time) pass pleasantly. |  She beguiled them with her beauty and wit. 她用她的美貌和智慧吸引了他们。|  He beguiled me into lending him money. 他骗我借钱给他。| synonyms: charm, enchant, captivate, entice, allure, deceive, trick | antonyms: repel, disgust, disenchant, bore | verb

beguiling:/bɪˈɡaɪlɪŋ/ | adj. | 迷人的；诱人的 | Charming or enchanting, often in a deceptive way. |  She had a beguiling smile. 她有着迷人的微笑。| synonyms: charming, enchanting, captivating, alluring, tempting | antonyms: repulsive, disgusting, repellent | adjective
