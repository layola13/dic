integration: /ˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃn/ | n. | 整合，集成，一体化 | The act or process of combining two or more things so that they work together.  The process of becoming a full member of a group or society. |  The integration of refugees into society can be challenging.  难民融入社会可能具有挑战性。

The integration of different software systems is a complex task.  不同软件系统的集成是一项复杂的任务。 | synonyms: unification, combination, amalgamation, synthesis | antonyms: separation, division, segregation, fragmentation | noun
