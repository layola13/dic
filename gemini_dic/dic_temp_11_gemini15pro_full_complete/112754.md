ltd:/ˌɛl tiː ˈdiː/
abbreviation
limited 有限的
A company whose owners are legally responsible for only a limited amount of its debts.
例句：The company is registered as a ltd. 这家公司注册为有限公司。
例句：Many small businesses operate as ltds. 许多小型企业以有限公司的形式运营。
近义词：Inc., Corp.
反义词: unlimited
词性: abbreviation
