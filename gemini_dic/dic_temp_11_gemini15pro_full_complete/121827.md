misrelation:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪʃn/
n.
错误关系；不协调；不适当的关系
A wrong or unsuitable connection or relationship between things or people.
The report highlighted the misrelation between educational attainment and income levels.  这份报告强调了教育程度和收入水平之间的错误关系。
The architect pointed out the misrelation between the building's design and its intended function.  建筑师指出了建筑设计与其预期功能之间的不协调。
近义词: disproportion, imbalance, incongruity, disparity
反义词:  harmony, balance, agreement, correlation
词性: 名词
