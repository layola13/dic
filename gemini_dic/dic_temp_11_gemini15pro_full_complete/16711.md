barrister:/ˈbærɪstər/| n. |大律师，律师|A lawyer who is qualified to plead a case in a higher court.|例句：The barrister presented a compelling argument to the jury.  大律师向陪审团提出了令人信服的论点。

例句：She hired a prominent barrister to represent her in court. 她聘请了一位著名的律师在法庭上代表她。|近义词：lawyer, advocate, counsel, attorney|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
