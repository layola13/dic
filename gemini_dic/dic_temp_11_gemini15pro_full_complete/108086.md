latewake: /ˈleɪtweɪk/
noun
晚起; 晚醒
the act of waking up late, or a person who wakes up late.
Example: I enjoy a latewake on weekends.  我周末喜欢晚起。
Example: He's a latewake and always misses breakfast. 他是个晚起的人，总是错过早餐。
Synonyms:  lie-in, sleep-in, late riser
Antonyms: early riser, early bird
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From "late" (meaning occurring after the usual or expected time) + "wake" (meaning the act of ceasing to sleep).  由"late"（意为迟于通常或预期的时间）+ "wake"（意为停止睡觉的行为）组成。
