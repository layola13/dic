confucians:/kənˈfjuːʃənz/ | n. | 儒家学者，儒士 | Followers or advocates of the teachings of Confucius. |  Confucians believe in the importance of education and moral principles. 儒家学者相信教育和道德原则的重要性。|  Confucians emphasized filial piety and respect for elders. 儒家强调孝顺和尊重长辈。 | synonyms: followers of Confucius, disciples of Confucius | antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider those who oppose Confucianism) | noun
