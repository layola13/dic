overpollinated:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːləneɪtɪd/
adj.
过度授粉的
Having received excessive amounts of pollen, often resulting in reduced fruit or seed set.
The squash plants were overpollinated, resulting in misshapen fruit. 西葫芦植株过度授粉，导致果实畸形。
over-pollinated
overfertilized, excessively pollinated
underpollinated
adjective
