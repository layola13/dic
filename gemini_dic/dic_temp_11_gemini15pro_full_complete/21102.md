blip:/blɪp/| n.| 短暂的信号中断；暂时的问题；小光点；（雷达屏幕上的）光点|A brief interruption in a signal; a temporary problem; a small dot of light; a spot of light on a radar screen|The radar screen showed a blip, then it disappeared. 雷达屏幕上出现了一个光点，然后消失了。|近义词：glitch, anomaly, spike|反义词: continuity, stability|名词

blip:/blɪp/| v.|发出哔哔声；短暂出现|To make a short, high-pitched sound; to appear briefly|The machine bliped and then shut down. 机器发出哔哔声，然后关机了。|近义词：beep, flash, flicker|反义词: continue, persist|动词
