monon:/ˈmoʊnɒn/ /ˈmoʊnɑːn/ | n. |单轨铁路，单轨列车 |A single rail serving as a track for a wheeled vehicle, or a railway system using such a track.  A vehicle that runs on such a track. |例句：The monon connects the airport to the city center. 单轨铁路连接机场和市中心。

例句：We took the monon to the theme park. 我们乘坐单轨列车去了主题公园。|近义词：monorail, elevated railway |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps:  conventional railway, subway) |词性: noun
