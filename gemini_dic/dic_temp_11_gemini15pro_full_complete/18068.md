beguilingly:/bɪˈɡaɪlɪŋli/| adv.|迷人地，诱人地|In a charming or enchanting way that attracts and holds the attention, often deceptively.  It suggests a captivating quality that can be misleading or subtly persuasive.|She smiled beguilingly, and he was instantly smitten. 她迷人地一笑，他立刻就被迷住了。|Synonyms: charmingly, enticingly, alluringly, seductively|Antonyms: repulsively, off-puttingly|adverb
