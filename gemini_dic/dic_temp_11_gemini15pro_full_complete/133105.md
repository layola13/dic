nonerratics:/ˌnɒnɪˈrætɪks/ | n. | 原地岩, 非漂移岩 | Rocks and deposits that have not been transported from their place of origin by geological action such as glaciers. | The nonerratics in this region are primarily limestone.  这个地区的原地岩主要是石灰岩。|  The geologist carefully distinguished the nonerratics from the glacial erratics.  地质学家仔细地区分了原生的岩石和冰川漂砾。 | Synonyms:  indigenous rocks, bedrock, autochthonous rocks | Antonyms: erratics, glacial drift | noun
