oozing:/ˈuːzɪŋ/
verb
渗出；流出；充分显露
slowly flowing or leaking out of something.  Also, to display a quality or feeling strongly.
The wound was oozing blood. 伤口在渗血。
He was oozing confidence. 他浑身散发着自信。
近义词：seeping, leaking, exuding, emitting
反义词：absorbing, solidifying, drying
词性：verb


oozing:/ˈuːzɪŋ/
adjective
渗出的；流出的；洋溢的
used to describe something that is slowly flowing or leaking out, or that strongly displays a quality or feeling.
The oozing wound needed to be bandaged. 渗血的伤口需要包扎。
His oozing charm captivated the audience. 他洋溢的魅力吸引了观众。
近义词：seeping, dripping, exuding
反义词：dry, solid
词性：adjective


词源：From Old English *wōs "juice, sap," of Germanic origin.
