costarring:/ˌkoʊˈstɑːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 联合主演的 | Appearing in a movie or play with one or more other leading actors. | Example:  She was costarring with Tom Hanks in the new romantic comedy. 她和汤姆·汉克斯共同主演了这部新的浪漫喜剧。

Example: The movie, costarring Meryl Streep and Robert De Niro, was a critical success. 这部由梅丽尔·斯特里普和罗伯特·德尼罗联合主演的电影获得了评论界的好评。 | 近义词: featuring, sharing the screen with | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective


costar:/ˈkoʊstɑːr/ | n. | 联合主演 | A performer who shares a leading role with another actor or actress. | Example:  Who is her costar in the upcoming film?  谁是她在即将上映的电影中的联合主演？

Example: He met his future wife when she was his costar in a Broadway play. 他在百老汇的一部戏剧中遇到了他未来的妻子，当时她是他的联合主演。| 近义词: fellow actor, leading actor/actress, supporting actor | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


costar:/ˌkoʊˈstɑːr/ | v. | 联合主演 | To appear as a leading actor or actress with someone else. | Example: He costarred with Julia Roberts in the movie "Ocean's Eleven". 他和茱莉亚·罗伯茨在电影《十一罗汉》中联合主演。

Example:  She's costarring in a new play with her husband. 她和她丈夫在新剧中共同主演。 | 近义词: act alongside, share the stage/screen with | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb

词源: co- (together) + star (leading performer)
