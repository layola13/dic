living-room: /ˈlɪvɪŋ ruːm/ (US), /ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌruːm/ (UK)| n. |客厅；起居室|A room in a house used for general relaxation and socializing.|例句：We spent the evening chatting in the living-room. 我们在客厅里聊了一个晚上。|例句：The living-room has a large fireplace and comfortable sofas. 客厅里有一个大壁炉和舒适的沙发。|近义词：sitting room, lounge, family room|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From "living" (present participle of "live") + "room".  Originally referring to a room where everyday living took place, as opposed to bedrooms or formal dining rooms.
