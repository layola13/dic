mantled:/ˈmæntəld/ | adj. | 覆盖的，披风的 | Covered with a mantle; cloaked. | The mountains were mantled in snow. 群山被雪覆盖。| The queen, mantled in velvet, addressed her subjects.  女王身披天鹅绒斗篷，向她的臣民讲话。 |近义词：covered, draped, cloaked, enveloped |反义词：bare, exposed, uncovered | 词性: 形容词

mantle:/ˈmæntl/ | n. | 斗篷，覆盖物；责任，职位 | 1. A loose sleeveless garment worn over other clothes. 2. Something that covers, envelops, or conceals. 3. A position of responsibility or authority. |  She wore a velvet mantle. 她穿着一件天鹅绒斗篷。 | The mantle of leadership fell upon her. 领导的责任落在了她身上。 |近义词：cloak, cape, covering, responsibility, role |反义词：(for covering) bareness, exposure | 词性: 名词
