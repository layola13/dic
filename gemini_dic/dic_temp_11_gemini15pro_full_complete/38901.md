confessable:/kənˈfɛsəbəl/
adj.
可承认的，可坦白的
capable of being confessed; admitting of confession.
He had committed some confessable sins in his youth.  他年轻时犯了一些可以坦白的罪过。

近义词:admissible, acknowledgeable, avowable
反义词:unconfessable, inadmissible, deniable
词性:adjective
