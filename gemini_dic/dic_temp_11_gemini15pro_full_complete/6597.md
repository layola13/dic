anachronistically:/əˌnækrəˈnɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 过时地，时代错误地 | Belonging or appropriate to an earlier period, especially so as to seem conspicuously old-fashioned.  Existing out of its proper time in history. | He used anachronistically formal language. 他使用了过时地正式语言。| The film anachronistically places a cell phone in the hands of a medieval knight.  这部电影时代错误地让一个中世纪骑士拿着手机。 | archaically, obsoletely, unseasonably | contemporarily, modernly | adverb
