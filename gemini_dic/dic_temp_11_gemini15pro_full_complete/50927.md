desiccation:/ˌdesɪˈkeɪʃn/
noun
干燥，脱水
the process of removing moisture from something
The desiccation of the landscape was evident after the long drought. 长期干旱后，地貌的干燥显而易见。
dehydration, drying
hydration, moistening
