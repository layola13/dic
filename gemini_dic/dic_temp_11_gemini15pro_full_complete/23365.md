bracelets:/ˈbreɪslɪts/| n.|手镯，手链|An ornamental band or chain worn around the wrist.|She wore a charm bracelet on her left wrist. 她左手腕上戴着一条手链。|synonyms: bangles, wristbands, cuffs|antonyms: necklace (by location, not function) |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "bracel" (armlet, bracelet), diminutive of "bras" (arm), from Latin "brachium" (arm).
