mosone:/ˈmoʊˌsoʊn/ | n. |季风|A seasonal prevailing wind in the region of South and Southeast Asia, blowing from the southwest between May and September and bringing rain (the wet monsoon ), or from the northeast between October and April (the dry monsoon ).|The summer monsoon brings heavy rains to India. 夏季季风给印度带来了暴雨。|Synonyms: seasonal wind, prevailing wind|Antonyms: calm, stillness|noun
