cuds:/kʌdz/| n. |反刍的食物| Partly digested food returned from the first stomach of ruminants to the mouth for further chewing. |The cow chewed its cuds contentedly.  母牛心满意足地咀嚼着反刍的食物。|近义词：bolus |反义词: None |Noun
