acidophilous:/əˌsɪdəˈfɪləs/ | adj. | 嗜酸的，喜酸的 | Thriving in or preferring acidic environments.  Relating to or denoting cells and tissues that stain readily with acid dyes. |  Acidophilous plants thrive in acidic soil. 嗜酸性植物在酸性土壤中茁壮成长。|  The acidophilous staining of the cells indicated a specific type of infection. 这些细胞的嗜酸性染色表明了一种特定类型的感染。 | 近义词: acid-loving, oxyphilous | 反义词: basophilous, alkaliphilic | 词性: 形容词

词源: From acid + -philous (loving).  Acid comes from Latin *acidus "sour, sharp, tart," related to acere "be sour."  -philous comes from Greek philos "loving."
