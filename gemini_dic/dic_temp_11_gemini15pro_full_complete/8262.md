anti-integration:/ˌæntiˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃən/
|adj.|反融合的，反一体化的|Opposing or resisting integration, especially racial or social integration.|The politician's anti-integration stance drew criticism from many. 这位政治家的反融合立场引来了很多批评。|例句：
Anti-integration policies were common in the past. 过去，反融合政策很普遍。
Her anti-integration views are outdated and harmful. 她的反融合观点已经过时且有害。
|近义词：segregationist, separatist|反义词：integrationist, pro-integration|adjective
