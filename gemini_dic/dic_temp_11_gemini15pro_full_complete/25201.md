buddle:/ˈbʌd(ə)l/| n. | 蝶形花属植物，醉鱼草属植物 | A shrub of the genus *Buddleja*, especially *Buddleja davidii* (butterfly bush), which has spikes of fragrant flowers.| The butterflies were attracted to the fragrant buddle. 蝴蝶被芬芳的醉鱼草吸引。 | synonym: butterfly bush | antonym: N/A | noun


buddle:/ˈbʌd(ə)l/| v. | 聚集，拥抱 | To huddle or nestle together.| The kittens buddled together for warmth. 小猫们挤在一起取暖。| synonym: huddle, cuddle, nestle | antonym: separate, disperse | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From a probable alteration of Middle English *bothel*, meaning "bundle," from Old English *boþl*.  The sense of "huddle" likely developed from the image of things bundled together.
