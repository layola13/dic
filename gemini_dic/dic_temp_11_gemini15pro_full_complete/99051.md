interantagonisms:/ˌɪntərænˈtæɡənɪzəmz/
|n.|相互对抗，相互敌对|Mutual antagonisms or hostilities between individuals or groups within a larger entity.|The interantagonisms within the political party ultimately led to its downfall.  党内相互敌对最终导致其垮台。|Synonyms:  infighting, internal conflict, dissension, friction, strife|Antonyms: cooperation, harmony, unity, concord|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "inter-" (between, among) + "antagonism" (active hostility or opposition).  "Antagonism" itself comes from the Greek "antagonistes" meaning opponent, rival, competitor.
