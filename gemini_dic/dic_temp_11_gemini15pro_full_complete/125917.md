muset:/ˈmjuːzeɪ/ /mjuːˈzeɪ/| n. vi.|沉思；冥想；(诗人等的)灵感；(诗歌的)主题|A state of dreamy meditation or contemplation.|She was lost in muset, pondering the mysteries of the universe. 她陷入了沉思，思考着宇宙的奥秘。|近义词：meditation, contemplation, reflection, reverie, daydream|反义词:action, work|词性:名词, 动词

muset:/ˈmjuːzeɪ/ /mjuːˈzeɪ/| vi.|沉思；冥想|To become absorbed in thought; to ponder.|He museted on the meaning of life. 他沉思着生命的意义。|近义词：meditate, contemplate, reflect, ponder|反义词:act, work|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old French *muser "to ponder, dream," perhaps from Vulgar Latin *musare "to loiter with one's nose in the air," literally "to smell about," from Latin mus "mouse" (from the image of a mouse sniffing around).
