edenized:/ˈiːdənˌaɪzd/
adjective
伊甸园化的；天堂般的
Transformed into or resembling the Garden of Eden; paradisiacal.  Made blissful or perfect.
The couple sought an edenized existence, far from the city's clamor. 这对夫妇寻求一种伊甸园般的生活，远离城市的喧嚣。
The garden, with its lush foliage and fragrant flowers, was truly edenized.  这个花园，郁郁葱葱的树叶和芬芳的花朵，真像伊甸园。
synonyms: paradisiacal, blissful, utopian, heavenly
antonyms: hellish, dystopian, infernal
