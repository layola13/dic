make ends meet:/meɪk ɛndz mit/| idiom |量入为出；收支相抵|to earn just enough money to pay for one's living expenses|例句：It's hard to make ends meet with rising prices.  物价上涨，收支平衡很困难。

例句：They're struggling to make ends meet on a single income.  他们依靠一份收入难以维持生计。

近义词：get by, scrape by, live within one's means, break even|反义词:live extravagantly, spend lavishly|词性:idiom (习语)
