consign:/kənˈsaɪn/ | v. |交付；托运；寄售；打发；放逐 |to deliver something to a person’s custody, typically in order for it to be sold|The goods were consigned to the care of the auctioneer. 这些货物被委托给了拍卖师保管。|Synonyms: entrust, commit, deliver, transfer |Antonyms: retain, withhold, keep|verb

consign:/kənˈsaɪn/ | v. |把...打发到；把...置于 |to put someone or something in an unpleasant place or situation|He was consigned to a life of poverty. 他被打发到贫困的生活中。|Synonyms: relegate, banish, exile |Antonyms: welcome, embrace, accept|verb
