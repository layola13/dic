menispermine:/mɛˈnɪspərmɪn/
noun
美尼斯波明（一种生物碱）
A poisonous alkaloid, C18H24N2O2, found in the seeds of several species of Menispermum.  It acts as a central nervous system stimulant.
Menispermine is extracted from the seeds of the moonseed plant.  美尼斯波明是从防己科植物的种子中提取的。
Menispermine can be toxic in high doses.  大剂量的美尼斯波明可能是有毒的。
Synonyms: alkaloid, toxin (in high doses)
