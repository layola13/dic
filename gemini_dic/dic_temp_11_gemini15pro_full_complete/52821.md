diluteness:/daɪˈluːtnəs/ | n. |稀释度，淡薄，稀薄|The diluteness of the solution made it ineffective.  该溶液的稀释度使其无效。|例句：The diluteness of the color made it almost invisible.  颜色的淡薄使它几乎看不见。|近义词：thinness, weakness, rarity |反义词: concentration, strength, thickness |词性:名词

Etymology: From dilute + -ness.  Dilute comes from Latin *dilutus, past participle of diluere "to wash away, dissolve," from di- "away" + -luere, related to lavere "to wash."
