mixes:/ˈmɪksɪz/
verb
混合，掺和
combines two or more substances into one.
She mixes flour, sugar, and eggs to make a cake. 她将面粉、糖和鸡蛋混合在一起做蛋糕。

mixes:/ˈmɪksɪz/
noun
混合物
a combination of different things.
The DJ played a mix of old and new songs.  DJ播放了新旧歌曲的混合曲。


Etymology: From Middle English *mixen, from Old English *miscian (unattested), from Proto-West Germanic *miskōn, from Proto-Germanic *miskōną (“to mix”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)meyḱ- (“to mix, scatter”).  Cognate with West Frisian miksje (“to mix”), Dutch mixen (“to mix”), German mischen (“to mix”).

Synonym: blends, combines, mingles
Antonym: separates, divides, sorts
Word type: Verb (primarily), Noun (less common)
