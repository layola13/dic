misspell:/ˌmɪsˈspel/ | v. | 拼写错误 | To spell a word incorrectly. | Example: I often misspell "necessary". 我经常拼错“necessary”。 | Example: He misspelled my name on the form. 他在表格上拼错了我的名字。 | Synonyms: miswrite, spell wrongly | Antonyms: spell correctly, spell right | Verb

misspell:/ˌmɪsˈspel/ | n. | 拼写错误 | An incorrect spelling of a word. | Example: There are several misspellings in this essay.  这篇文章里有好几个拼写错误。| Example: The teacher circled the misspellings in red ink. 老师用红笔圈出了拼写错误。| Synonyms: misspelling, error, mistake | Antonyms: correct spelling | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *misspellen*, from mis- + spellen (“to spell”). Equivalent to mis- +‎ spell.
