discapacitate:/ˌdɪskəˈpæsɪteɪt/| vt.|使丧失能力，使残疾|To deprive of capability or fitness for action; disable.  To make legally incapable; disqualify.|The accident incapacitated him from working.  这场事故使他丧失了工作能力。|Synonyms: disable, cripple, incapacitate, immobilize|Antonyms: enable, empower|Verb

disability:/ˌdɪsəˈbɪləti/| n.|残疾，无能|A physical, mental, cognitive, or developmental condition that impairs, interferes with, or limits a person's ability to engage in certain tasks or participate in typical daily activities and interactions.|His disability prevents him from walking. 他的残疾使他无法行走。|Synonyms: impairment, handicap, affliction|Antonyms: ability, capability|Noun
