diarrhoea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (UK) /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US)| n. |腹泻|frequent and watery bowel movements|例句：He suffered from diarrhoea after eating contaminated food. 他吃了被污染的食物后腹泻了。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diarrhoia, from Greek διαρροια (diarrhoia), from διαρρέω (diarrheō, “I flow through”), from διά (dia, “through”) + ῥέω (rheō, “I flow”).
