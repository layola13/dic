lombardi: /lɒmˈbɑːrdi/ (US) /lɒmˈbɑːdi/ (UK) | n. | 隆巴迪 |  Refers to Vince Lombardi, a legendary American football coach, or things named after him, like the Vince Lombardi Trophy awarded to the Super Bowl winner.  It can also refer to the Lombardy region of Italy. | The Green Bay Packers won the first two Super Bowls under the leadership of Vince Lombardi. 绿湾包装工队在文斯·隆巴迪的带领下赢得了前两届超级碗。| synonyms:  (for the trophy) Super Bowl trophy, championship trophy; (for the person) coach, leader, mentor | antonyms:  (for the person) follower, loser  | noun
