nonstainers:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪnərz/
n.
不染色剂，不沾污物
Substances or materials that do not cause stains.
These new markers are nonstainers, perfect for kids.  这些新记号笔是不染色剂，非常适合孩子使用。
Nonstaining carpet fibers are a boon for busy households. 不沾污的地毯纤维对忙碌的家庭来说是一大福音。
近义词: stain-resistant materials, non-staining substances
反义词: stainers, staining agents
词性: noun (plural)
