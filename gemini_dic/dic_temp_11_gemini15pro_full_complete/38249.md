computing:/kəmˈpjuːtɪŋ/ | n. | 计算；计算机的使用 | the process of using computers to carry out or calculate something | Example: Advances in computing have revolutionized the way we live. 计算技术的进步彻底改变了我们的生活方式。 | Example:  He studied computing at university. 他在大学学习计算机科学。 | Synonym: calculation, computation, data processing | Antonym:  N/A | noun

compute:/kəmˈpjuːt/ | v. | 计算；估算 | calculate or reckon (a figure or amount) | Example: The cost of the project was computed at $5 million.  该项目的成本估算为500万美元。 | Example: I tried to compute the total cost of the trip. 我试图计算这次旅行的总费用。 | Synonym: calculate, reckon, estimate | Antonym: guess, estimate (although estimate can also be a synonym depending on context) | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "computare," meaning "to count, sum up, reckon," from "com-" (together) + "putare" (to think, reckon, prune). The modern sense of using computers emerged in the mid-20th century.
