asslike:/ˈæsˌlaɪk/
adjective
像驴子一样的；愚蠢的
Resembling a donkey in appearance or behavior; stupid or obstinate.
His asslike stubbornness prevented any compromise. 他像驴子一样的固执阻碍了任何妥协。
近义词: asinine, foolish, stupid, obstinate
反义词: intelligent, smart, wise, compliant
词性: adjective

词源：
From "ass" (donkey) + "-like" (suffix indicating resemblance).  "Ass" comes from Old English "assa," which ultimately derives from Latin "asinus." "-like" comes from Old English "-lic."
