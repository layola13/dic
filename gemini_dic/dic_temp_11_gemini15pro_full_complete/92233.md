hypertoxicity:/ˌhaɪpərˌtɑːkˈsɪsəti/ | n. | 过度毒性；超毒性 | The state of being excessively toxic; extreme toxicity.  |  The hypertoxicity of the new pesticide led to its ban.  这种新杀虫剂的过度毒性导致它被禁止使用。 |  extreme toxicity,  overtoxicity,  excessive toxicity |  nontoxicity, harmlessness | noun

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, above, beyond"
toxic- (root): from Latin toxicus, meaning "poisonous"
-ity (suffix): forms nouns indicating state or quality
