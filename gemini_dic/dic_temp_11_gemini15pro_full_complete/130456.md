nonabsorbents:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbənts/
| n. |非吸收性物质；非吸收体|Materials that do not absorb liquids or gases are nonabsorbents.|These nonabsorbents are ideal for use in wet environments. 这些非吸收性材料非常适合在潮湿环境中使用.|Synonyms: impermeable materials, water-resistant materials, non-porous materials|Antonyms: absorbents, porous materials|Noun


nonabsorbent:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbənt/
| adj. |非吸收性的；不吸收的|Not able to absorb liquids or gases.|The jacket is made of a nonabsorbent fabric. 这件夹克是用非吸收性面料制成的。|Synonyms: impermeable, water-resistant, non-porous|Antonyms: absorbent, porous|Adjective
