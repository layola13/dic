metathorax:/ˌmetəˈθɔːræks/ | n. |后胸|The metathorax is the posterior segment of the thorax of an insect, bearing the hindwings and the hindlegs.|The grasshopper's powerful metathorax allows it to jump long distances. | 蚱蜢强壮的后胸使其能够跳跃很远的距离。|Synonym: hind-thorax | Antonym: prothorax | noun
