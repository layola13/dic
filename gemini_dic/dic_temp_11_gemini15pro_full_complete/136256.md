nonrepellence:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpɛl.əns/ | n. | 不排斥性，无驱避性 | The nonrepellence of the insecticide allows it to work effectively.  这种杀虫剂的不排斥性使其能够有效地发挥作用。|  Absence of repellent properties; inability to repel.  缺乏驱避特性；无法驱避。| 例句：The nonrepellence of the new formula makes it ideal for treating sensitive areas. 新配方的无驱避性使其成为处理敏感区域的理想选择。

例句：The nonrepellence of the treatment meant the insects continued to forage in the treated area, unknowingly ingesting the poison.  这种处理的无驱避性意味着昆虫继续在处理过的区域觅食，不知不觉地摄入了毒药。

近义词：nondeterrence,  nonresistance | 反义词：repellence, deterrence | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + repellence.  Repellence from Latin repellere "drive back, push back," from re- "back" + pellere "to drive, push."
