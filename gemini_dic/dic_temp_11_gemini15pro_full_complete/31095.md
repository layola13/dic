champion:/ˈtʃæmpiən/ (US), /ˈtʃæmpɪən/ (UK)| n. vt. adj.|冠军，拥护者，捍卫者|A person who has defeated or surpassed all rivals in a competition, especially in sports.  A person who fights or argues for a cause or on behalf of someone else.|
He is the reigning world champion. 他是卫冕世界冠军。

She championed the rights of the disabled. 她捍卫残疾人的权利。

He is a champion swimmer. 他是一位游泳冠军。|近义词：winner, victor, advocate, supporter, defender|反义词：loser, opponent, detractor|词性: 名词, 动词, 形容词
