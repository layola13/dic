apostolically:/ˌæpəˈstɒlɪkli/ | adv. | 使徒地，像使徒一样地 | In a manner resembling or characteristic of an apostle, especially in being devoted to the spread of Christianity. | He lived and preached apostolically, traveling from town to town spreading the gospel. 他过着使徒般的生活并传教，在城镇之间旅行传播福音。|  Synonym: evangelically, missionarily | Antonym: secularly | Part of Speech: Adverb

apostolically:/ˌæpəˈstɒlɪkli/ | adv. | 使徒般地 |  Acting with the authority or in the manner of an apostle. |  She spoke apostolically about the importance of forgiveness. 她以使徒般的口吻谈论宽恕的重要性。 | Synonym: authoritatively, divinely | Antonym: irreverently | Part of Speech: Adverb
