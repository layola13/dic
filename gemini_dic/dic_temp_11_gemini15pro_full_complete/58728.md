dunderhead:/ˈdʌndərˌhɛd/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A stupid or slow-witted person. |例句：He's such a dunderhead, he can't even tie his shoes. 他真是个笨蛋，连鞋带都不会系。

例句：Don't be a dunderhead, think before you speak. 别犯傻了，说话前先想想。|近义词：blockhead, numskull, dunce, idiot, fool|反义词：genius, intellectual, mastermind|词性:noun
