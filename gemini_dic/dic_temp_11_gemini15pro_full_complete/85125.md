hardies:/ˈhɑːrdiːz/
n.
耐寒植物; 强壮的人或动物
Hardy plants can survive cold winters.  耐寒植物能够在寒冷的冬天生存下来。
These mountain people are a hardy lot. 这些山里人是一群强壮的人。
hardy plants, hardy souls,  robust individuals, resilient people
tender plants, fragile creatures, weak individuals
