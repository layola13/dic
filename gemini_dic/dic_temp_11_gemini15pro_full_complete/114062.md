magnetization:/ˌmæɡnɪtɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 磁化，磁化强度 | The process of making a substance magnetic or the degree to which a substance is magnetic. |  The magnetization of the iron rod increased with the strength of the magnetic field.  铁棒的磁化强度随着磁场强度的增加而增加。 | 近义词: magnetic induction, magnetic polarization | 反义词: demagnetization | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magnetizatio, from magnetizare  "to magnetize," from Latin magnes "magnet" (see magnet) + -izare  "-ize."
