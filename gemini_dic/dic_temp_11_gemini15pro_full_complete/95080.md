inaffected:/ˌɪnəˈfɛktɪd/ | adj. |未受影响的，自然的，不矫揉造作的 | Not influenced or changed by something; natural and sincere. |例句：She seemed inaffected by the news. 她似乎没被这消息影响。|例句：His inaffected manner was refreshing. 他不做作的态度令人耳目一新。| 近义词：unmoved, untouched, genuine, natural, artless | 反义词：affected, pretentious, artificial | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + affected.
