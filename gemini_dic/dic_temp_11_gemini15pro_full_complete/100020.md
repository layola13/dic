interpermeates:/ˌɪntərˈpɜːmieɪts/
verb
互相渗透，弥漫，充满
spreads or flows throughout something, mixing with it
例句：The scent of flowers interpermeates the air. 花香弥漫在空气中。

例句：Her work interpermeates her life.  她的工作充满了她的生活。

近义词：permeates, pervades, infuses, saturates
反义词：separates, divides

词性：v.


Etymology: From Latin *interpermeare*, from *inter-* (between, among) + *permeare* (to pass through).
