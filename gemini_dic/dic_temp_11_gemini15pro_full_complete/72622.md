flyers:/ˈflaɪərz/ (US), /ˈflaɪəz/ (UK)| n. |传单；飞行物；飞鸟|A small handbill advertising an event or product. Also, something or someone that flies.|They handed out flyers for the concert. 他们分发了音乐会的传单。|The bird is a high flyer. 这只鸟飞得很高。|synonyms: handbill, leaflet, circular, pamphlet, brochure, advertisement, flyer (US), flier (US)|antonyms: grounded (for objects that fly)|noun

flyers:/ˈflaɪərz/ (US), /ˈflaɪəz/ (UK)| n. |经常飞行的人|Someone who frequently travels by air.|He's a frequent flyer and gets upgraded often. 他经常坐飞机，而且经常被升舱。|synonyms: frequent flyer, air traveler |antonyms: non-flyer|noun
