bountiful:/ˈbaʊntɪfl/
adjective
丰富的，慷慨的
Giving freely and generously; plentiful.
The land produced a bountiful harvest. 这片土地获得了丰收。
近义词: abundant, plentiful, copious, ample, generous, lavish
反义词: meager, scarce, scanty, sparse
词性: adjective
词源: From Middle English *bountifull, from Anglo-Norman *buntifull, from bountié (“goodness, kindness, generosity, bounty”) + -full.
