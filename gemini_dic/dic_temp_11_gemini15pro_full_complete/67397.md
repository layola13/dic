exterminate:/ɪkˈstɜːmɪneɪt/
verb
消灭，根除
to kill all the members of a group of people or animals
The Nazis tried to exterminate the Jews during World War II.  纳粹在二战期间试图灭绝犹太人。

近义词: eradicate, annihilate, destroy, wipe out
反义词: preserve, conserve, protect
词性: verb
