counterintuitively:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtuːɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 反直觉地 | In a way that seems to contradict one's intuition or expectations. |  Surprisingly, and contrary to what one might expect, the stock market rose after the negative news. 令人惊讶的是，与人们的预期相反，股市在负面消息传出后上涨了。 | Surprisingly, unexpectedly, paradoxically | intuitively, predictably | adverb
