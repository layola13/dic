floundering:/ˈflaʊndərɪŋ/| adj. |挣扎的，不知所措的|Struggling clumsily or helplessly; having difficulty doing or understanding something.|The company is floundering in the current economic climate.  这家公司在当前的经济环境中苦苦挣扎。|近义词：struggling, thrashing, faltering|反义词: thriving, succeeding|形容词

flounder:/ˈflaʊndər/| v. |挣扎，不知所措|To struggle clumsily or helplessly; to have difficulty doing or understanding something.|He floundered for a moment, unsure how to answer the question. 他犹豫了一下，不知道该如何回答这个问题。|近义词：struggle, thrash, falter|反义词: thrive, succeed|动词


词源: From Middle English *floundren, probably of Scandinavian origin; compare Norwegian flunra (“to flounder”).
