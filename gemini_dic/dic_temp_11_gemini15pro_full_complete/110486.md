lily-whites:/ˈlɪli waɪts/ | n. | 百合白；(指保守的)白人；(尤指)共和党白人 |  A term, often derogatory, referring to a group of white people, especially in the context of politics, implying exclusivity and conservatism.  Originally referring to white cricket uniforms. |  The lily-whites dominated local politics for decades.  百合白统治了当地政坛数十年。 |  conservatives, right-wingers | liberals, progressives | noun

lily-white:/ˈlɪli waɪt/ | adj. | 纯白的；洁白的；(政治上)保守的 |  Of a pure white color; also used figuratively to describe a group as being exclusively white, often with implications of political conservatism. |  The bride wore a lily-white gown.  新娘穿着一件洁白的礼服。 The neighborhood remained lily-white for years. 多年来，这个社区一直保持着纯白人的状态。 | pristine, spotless, pure white | stained, dirty, mixed | adjective
