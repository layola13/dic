hyperurbanism:/ˌhaɪpərˈɜːrbənɪzəm/ | n. | 超级城市化；过度城市化 | The condition of excessive urban growth and concentration, often leading to social and environmental problems.  A high degree of urbanization, typically characterized by high population density, extensive infrastructure, and a complex social organization. |  The rapid growth of megacities in developing countries is a clear example of hyperurbanism. 在发展中国家，特大城市的快速发展是超级城市化的一个明显例子。

Hyperurbanism often leads to a strain on resources and infrastructure. 超级城市化经常导致资源和基础设施的紧张。 | 近义词: overurbanization, mega-urbanization | 反义词: ruralization | 词性: noun
