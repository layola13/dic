cristallology: /ˌkrɪstəˈlɒlədʒi/ | n. | 晶体学 | The study of crystals, their forms, structures, and properties. |  Crystallography plays a vital role in understanding the properties of materials. 晶体学在理解材料特性方面起着至关重要的作用。|  X-ray crystallography is used to determine the 3D structure of molecules. X射线晶体学用于确定分子的三维结构。| 近义词: crystal science | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *cristallographie*, from *cristal* (crystal) + *-logie* (-logy). Ultimately from Ancient Greek *krýstallos* (ice) + *-logia* (study of).
