decompose:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊz/ (US), /ˌdiːkəmˈpəʊz/ (UK)| v. |分解，腐烂| to break down or be broken down into simpler parts or substances by a chemical or biological process |  The dead leaves will decompose and enrich the soil. 枯叶会分解并使土壤肥沃。

The body began to decompose after death.  尸体死后开始腐烂。|近义词：decay, rot, break down, disintegrate |反义词: synthesize, compose, combine |词性: verb
