mariachis:/ˌmɑːriˈɑːtʃiːz/
noun
墨西哥流浪乐队
A group of Mexican musicians, typically playing violins, trumpets, guitars, and a guitarrón, and wearing traditional charro costumes.
The mariachis filled the plaza with lively music. 墨西哥流浪乐队在广场上演奏着欢快的音乐。
