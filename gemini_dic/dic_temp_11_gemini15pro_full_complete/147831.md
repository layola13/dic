overpunch:/ˈoʊvərˌpʌntʃ/| n. |超冲孔，附加穿孔|A method of representing alphabetic characters or special symbols by adding a second punch to a digit punch in a punched card.  A method of data storage used with punched cards in which an extra hole is punched in a column to indicate a sign or a letter. |Example: The overpunch in column 72 indicated a positive value. 第72列的超冲孔表示正值。|Synonym:  zone punch|Antonym: N/A|noun


overpunch:/ˌoʊvərˈpʌntʃ/| vt. |超冲孔|To add an extra punch to a digit punch in a punched card to represent alphabetic characters or special symbols. To punch an extra hole in (a punched card column).|Example:  They used to overpunch the cards to represent letters. 他们过去常常对卡片进行超冲孔来表示字母。|Synonym:  zone punch|Antonym: N/A|verb
