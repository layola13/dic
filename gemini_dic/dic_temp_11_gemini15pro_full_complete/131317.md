ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nonchalant*, from *non* (not) + *chaloir* (to matter, to concern), from Latin *calēre* (to be warm).  The sense evolution went from "not being warmed up" or "not showing enthusiasm" to the modern meaning of "indifferent" or "unconcerned."
