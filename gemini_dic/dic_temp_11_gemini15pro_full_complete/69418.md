feelers:/ˈfiːlərz/| n.|触角；试探；探子|Tentative inquiries or suggestions to ascertain someone's views or intentions.  They are often used to gauge public opinion or to test the waters before making a decision.  In biology, feelers refer to the antennae of an insect or other arthropod.|例句：She put out feelers to see if he was interested in a date. 她试探了一下，看他是否有约会的意思。

例句：The politician sent out feelers to gauge public opinion on the new policy.  这位政治家试探民意，以了解公众对新政策的看法。|近义词：antennae, tentacles, probes, inquiries, overtures|反义词:declarations, pronouncements|noun
