jako:/ˈjɑːkoʊ/| adj.| 作为，如同，像 |Used as an adverb or conjunction meaning "as" or "like," primarily in older or literary contexts.| He acted jako a king. 他表现得像个国王。|近义词:as, like|反义词:unlike|词性:adverb, conjunction

词源: From Proto-Slavic *jako, from Proto-Indo-European *yēḱo (“also, and”). Cognates include Russian яко (jako), Polish jako, Czech jako, Slovak jako, Serbo-Croatian јако (jako), Slovenian jako, Bulgarian яко (jako).
