neoliberalism:/ˌniːoʊˈlɪbərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 新自由主义 | A modified form of liberalism tending to favor free-market capitalism.  It is generally associated with policies of economic liberalization, including privatization, deregulation, globalization, free trade, austerity, and reductions in government spending in order to increase the role of the private sector in the economy and society. |  Many economists argue that neoliberalism has led to increased global inequality. 许多经济学家认为新自由主义导致了全球不平等加剧。

Neoliberalism has been a dominant economic ideology for the past few decades. 新自由主义在过去几十年一直是占主导地位的经济意识形态。 | 近义词: laissez-faire capitalism, free market economics | 反义词: socialism, communism, interventionism | 词性: noun
