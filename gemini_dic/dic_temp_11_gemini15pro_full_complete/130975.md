nonastringent:/ˌnɒnəˈstrɪndʒənt/ | adj. | 不涩的，收敛性弱的 | Not astringent; not causing contraction of body tissues, especially of the mouth.  A nonastringent wine, for example, would not leave a dry, puckering feeling in the mouth. | This wine is delightfully nonastringent and easy to drink. 这款酒非常爽口，易于饮用。

The lotion is nonastringent and gentle on sensitive skin.  这款乳液不涩，对敏感肌肤很温和。 | 近义词: mild, smooth, soft | 反义词: astringent, harsh, sharp, bitter | 词性: adjective

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + astringent (from Latin *adstringent-, present participle of adstringere "to bind fast," from ad- "to" + stringere "to draw tight")
