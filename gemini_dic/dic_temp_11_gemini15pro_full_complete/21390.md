blotting:/ˈblɒtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈblɑːtɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |吸墨，印迹，污点|The act of absorbing excess ink or liquid, or a mark made by doing so. Also refers to a technique used in molecular biology. |She used a piece of blotting paper to absorb the excess ink. 她用一张吸墨纸吸干多余的墨水。|近义词：absorbing, soaking, wiping|反义词:smearing, spreading|noun

blotting:/ˈblɒtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈblɑːtɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |吸干，涂污，弄脏|To absorb excess ink or liquid, or to mark something by doing so. Also refers to obscuring or hiding something. |He blotted the signature with a tissue. 他用纸巾吸干了签名上的墨水。|近义词：drying, wiping, obscuring|反义词:wetting, soaking, revealing|verb
