melt:/mɛlt/| v. n.|融化，熔解；软化，感动|To change from a solid to a liquid state, usually by heat. To become softened in feeling; be moved to tenderness or pity.|The snow began to melt as the sun came out.  雪一出来就开始融化了。| 近义词：liquefy, dissolve, thaw | 反义词: freeze, solidify |词性:动词,名词

melt:/mɛlt/| n.|融化；熔化物|The process of changing from a solid to a liquid state. The molten material resulting from this process.|The melt from the volcano flowed down the mountainside. 火山熔化的物质顺着山坡流了下来。| 近义词：liquefaction, thaw | 反义词: freezing, solidification |词性:名词
