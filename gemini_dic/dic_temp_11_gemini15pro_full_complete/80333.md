goldfield:/ˈɡoʊldˌfiːld/ | n. |金矿区；金矿产地|A district where gold is mined.|The Klondike was a famous goldfield during the Gold Rush. 克朗代克在淘金热时期是一个著名的金矿区。|Example:  Many people rushed to the goldfield hoping to strike it rich. 许多人涌向金矿区，希望能发财。

Example:  The old goldfield is now a ghost town.  这片旧金矿区现在是一座鬼城。|Synonyms: gold mine, gold region, digging(s) |Antonyms:  N/A | noun
