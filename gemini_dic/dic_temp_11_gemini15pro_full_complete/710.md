absentations: /ˌæbsənˈteɪʃənz/
n.
缺席; 缺席次数
the act of being absent or the fact of being away from a place
例句：His frequent absentations from class were noticed by the teacher. 他经常缺课，老师注意到了。

例句：The company's policy on absentations is very strict. 公司的缺勤政策非常严格。

近义词：absences, nonattendances
反义词：presences, attendances
词性：名词
