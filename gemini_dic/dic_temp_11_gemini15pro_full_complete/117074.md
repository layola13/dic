meacock:/ˈmiːkɒk/| n.|懦夫，胆小鬼|A timid or cowardly person. | He called him a meacock for refusing to join the fight. 他因为他拒绝加入战斗而称他为懦夫。| 近义词: coward, wimp, milksop | 反义词: hero, brave | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *mekecok, equivalent to meek + cock (in the sense of "fool").  The word suggests a person as meek as a cock (male chicken) which was considered a cowardly bird.
