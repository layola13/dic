flammables:/ˈflæməblz/
noun
易燃物
materials or substances that are easily set on fire.
Store flammables away from heat sources.  将易燃物远离热源存放。
Flammable materials should be handled with care. 易燃材料应小心处理。
近义词:combustibles, inflammables
反义词:non-flammables, fire-resistant materials
词性:n. (noun, 名词)
词源:From Latin flammabilis (“flaming, burning”), from flammō (“I set on fire”) + -bilis. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₁- (“to blow, blaze, shine”).
