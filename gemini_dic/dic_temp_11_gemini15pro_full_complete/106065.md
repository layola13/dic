kneipp:/knaɪp/| n. adj.|水疗法；克奈圃疗法|A system of hydrotherapy involving alternating hot and cold baths and other water treatments, developed by Sebastian Kneipp.|She swears by Kneipp treatments for her arthritis. 她坚信克奈圃疗法能治疗她的关节炎。|Near synonyms: hydrotherapy, water cure|Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific treatment)|noun, adjective


Etymology: Named after Sebastian Kneipp (1821–1897), a Bavarian priest and naturopath who developed the system.
