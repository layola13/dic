biasing:/ˈbaɪəsɪŋ/| n.|偏置，偏压|The act of introducing bias or systematic error into something, especially statistical samples or testing procedures.|The biasing of the sample led to inaccurate results. 样本的偏差导致结果不准确。|近义词：prejudice, influencing, skewing, weighting, distorting|反义词：neutralizing, balancing|noun

biasing:/ˈbaɪəsɪŋ/| v.|使...偏向，加偏压于|Influencing or prejudicing someone or something in favor of a particular outcome or viewpoint; applying a bias voltage to an electronic component.|The judge was accused of biasing the jury. 法官被指控对陪审团施加偏见。  |近义词：prejudicing, influencing, slanting, predisposing|反义词：neutralizing, balancing|verb
