conserver's:/kənˈsɜːrvərz/
| n.|保护者，守护者的（所有格）|A conserver is someone who protects something, often natural resources.  Conserver's refers to something belonging to or associated with a conserver.  |The conserver's passion for the environment was inspiring. 这位环保主义者的热情令人鼓舞。|近义词：protector's, guardian's, preservationist's|反义词：destroyer's, waster's|词性:名词所有格

conserve:/kənˈsɜːrv/
| v.|保护，保存|To conserve something means to protect it from harm, loss, or decay, especially natural resources. It comes from the Latin word "conservare," meaning "to keep, preserve."|We must conserve water during the drought. 我们在干旱期间必须节约用水。|近义词：preserve, protect, save|反义词：waste, squander, deplete|词性:动词
