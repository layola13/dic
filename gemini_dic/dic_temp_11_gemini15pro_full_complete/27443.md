camonflet: /ˈkæmənˌflɛt/ | n. | 挫败；羞辱；奚落 | A sudden disappointment or humiliation; a snub. | He suffered a severe camonflet when his proposal was rejected.  他被拒绝了提议，这让他很受挫。| Synonyms: setback, rebuff, humiliation | Antonyms: triumph, victory | Noun

Etymology: From French *camonflet*, from Italian *camonfalato*, past participle of *camonfare* "to bend down the muzzle," from *camuffo* "muzzle" (of uncertain origin) + *-are*.
