baseboards:/ˈbeɪsbɔːrdz/
n.
踢脚线，护壁板
A narrow board that runs along the base of an interior wall.
The baseboards in the old house were intricately carved. 老房子的踢脚线雕刻精美。

近义词:skirting boards, mopboards
反义词:None

词性:noun (plural)

词源:base + board.  "Base" refers to the bottom part, and "board" refers to a flat piece of wood.
