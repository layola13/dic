outblessed: /ˌaʊtˈblɛst/
verb
超越祝福；比...更受祝福
to surpass in being blessed or fortunate
He outblessed his brother by inheriting the entire fortune. 他比他兄弟更幸运，继承了全部财产。
近义词: outmatched, surpassed, exceeded
反义词: cursed, damned
词性: verb
