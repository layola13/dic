distributive:/dɪˈstrɪbjətɪv/| adj.|分配的，分布的，散布的| relating to or denoting the distribution of resources or goods among a group or area.  Characterized by or producing distribution.|The distributive property in mathematics allows us to multiply a sum by multiplying each addend separately and then add the products.  |近义词：allocative, apportioning, dispensing |反义词: collective, cumulative |词性: adjective
Etymology:  Late Middle English (in the sense 'tending to distribute'): from late Latin distributivus, from Latin distribut- 'distributed', from the verb distribuere (see distribute).
