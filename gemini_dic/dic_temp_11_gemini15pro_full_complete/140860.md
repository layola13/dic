on-the-jump:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈdʒʌmp/ | adj. | 非常忙碌的，奔波的，随时准备行动的 | Very busy and active; constantly moving or ready to act. |  The reporters were on the jump, trying to get the latest news on the developing crisis. 记者们非常忙碌，试图获得危机发展的最新消息。

The emergency services are always on the jump, ready to respond to any situation. 紧急服务部门总是随时待命，准备应对任何情况。 | 近义词: busy, active, restless, alert | 反义词: idle, inactive, relaxed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  This phrase likely originated from the imagery of someone literally jumping from one task to another, reflecting a state of constant motion and alertness.  It's an idiomatic expression.
