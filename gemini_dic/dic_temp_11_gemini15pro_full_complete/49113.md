deintegrate:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
|v.|分解，分离，取消合并|To separate something that has been integrated, especially to end racial segregation.|The government decided to deintegrate the school system.|Synonyms: separate, disintegrate, dismantle, uncombine, segregate|Antonyms: integrate, combine, unite, merge|verb

deintegrate:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
|n.|分解，分离，取消合并|The act of separating something that has been integrated.|The deintegration of the school system caused a lot of controversy.|Synonyms: separation, disintegration, dismantling|Antonyms: integration, combination, unification, merger|noun
