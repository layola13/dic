lame-brained:/ˌleɪmˈbreɪnd/
| adj. |愚蠢的，笨的|Stupid or foolish; lacking intelligence or good judgment.|He made a lame-brained decision to invest all his money in that failing company. 他愚蠢地决定把所有的钱都投资在那家快要倒闭的公司。|She came up with some lame-brained scheme to get rich quick. 她想出了一个愚蠢的快速致富计划。|近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, idiotic, witless, brainless|反义词：intelligent, smart, clever, bright, wise|词性：形容词

Etymology:
"lame" (crippled) + "brained" (having a brain). The word implies that the person's brain is not functioning properly, like a crippled limb.
