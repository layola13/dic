emigrationist: /ˌɛmɪˈɡreɪʃənɪst/
noun
移民主义者；支持移民的人
A person who advocates or supports emigration.
The emigrationist argued that moving abroad would offer better opportunities for young people.  移民主义者认为，移居国外将为年轻人提供更好的机会。
The government's policy was criticized by emigrationists for making it harder for citizens to leave. 政府的政策因加大公民离境难度而受到移民主义者的批评。
synonyms:  pro-emigration, advocate for emigration
antonyms: anti-emigrationist, nativist
n.
