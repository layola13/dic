annul:/əˈnʌl/| vt.|取消，废除，宣告无效|To declare officially that something is no longer valid. |The marriage was annulled after two years. 这段婚姻两年后被宣告无效。|Synonyms: invalidate, nullify, abolish, repeal, revoke, cancel|Antonyms: validate, confirm, ratify, establish, authorize|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *anuller*, from Latin annullare "to make void, reduce to nothing," from ad- "to" + nullus "not any, none."
