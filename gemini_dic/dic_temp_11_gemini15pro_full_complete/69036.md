fatuousness:/fætʃuːəsnəs/
|n.|愚蠢；愚昧；空洞|The fatuousness of her remarks was evident to everyone.|她言论的愚蠢显而易见。|
|近义词：foolishness, stupidity, silliness, inanity|
|反义词：wisdom, intelligence, sagacity|
|词性：名词|
|词源：From Latin *fatuus* "foolish, silly, insipid," related to *fari* "to speak" (compare infant). The notion is perhaps of "babbling nonsense."|
