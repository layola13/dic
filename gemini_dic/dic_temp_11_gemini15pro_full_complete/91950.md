hypermobile:/ˌhaɪpərˈmoʊbəl/ (US), /ˌhaɪpəˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|过度活动的，活动性过强的|Having joints that bend further than is normal or desirable.  This means that the ligaments and other soft tissues around the joint are looser than usual.|例句：Her fingers are hypermobile, allowing her to bend them backwards at an unusual angle. 她的手指过度活动，可以以不寻常的角度向后弯曲。

例句：Hypermobile joints can be a sign of an underlying connective tissue disorder. 过度活动的关节可能是潜在结缔组织疾病的征兆。|近义词：flexible, loose-jointed, double-jointed|反义词：stiff, rigid|词性: adjective
