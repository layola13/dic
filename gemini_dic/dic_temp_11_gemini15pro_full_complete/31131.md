A person who exploits opportunities without regard for moral principles.|He's a bit of a chancer, always looking for a quick way to make money. 他有点像个投机分子，总是想快速赚钱。|近义词：gambler, risk-taker, opportunist|反义词：planner, strategist|词性: noun
