non-unionist: /ˌnɒnˈjuːnjənɪst/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈjuːnjənɪst/ (American)| n. adj.|非工会会员，非工会主义者|A person who is not a member of a trade union, or who opposes trade unions.  A worker who doesn't belong to or support a labor union, or someone who is against the principles of unionism.|The factory owner preferred to hire non-unionist workers.  工厂老板更喜欢雇佣非工会工人。|Synonyms: non-member, anti-unionist|Antonyms: unionist, union member|Noun, Adjective
