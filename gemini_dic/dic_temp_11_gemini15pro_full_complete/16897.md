basipterygium: /ˌbeɪsɪptəˈrɪdʒiəm/
noun
基翼骨，基鳍骨
A bone or cartilage supporting the pelvic fins in some fishes.
The basipterygium plays a crucial role in the locomotion of these fish species.  基翼骨在这些鱼类的运动中起着至关重要的作用。
The scientist studied the basipterygium of the shark.  科学家研究了鲨鱼的基翼骨。
近义词: pelvic fin support, basal pterygiophore
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical term)
词性: n.

Etymology:
From basi- (base) + pterygium (fin)
