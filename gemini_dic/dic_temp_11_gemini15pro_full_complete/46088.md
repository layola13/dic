cyclosis:/saɪˈkloʊsɪs/
n.
细胞质环流，原生质环流
The streaming movement of the cytoplasm within a living cell.
例句：Cyclosis helps distribute nutrients and organelles throughout the cell.  细胞质环流有助于在整个细胞中分配营养物质和细胞器。

例句：The researchers observed cyclosis in the Elodea leaf cells under the microscope. 研究人员在显微镜下观察了伊乐藻叶细胞中的细胞质环流。
近义词：cytoplasmic streaming
反义词：(None - This is a biological process without a direct opposite)
词性：noun
