enhearten:/ɪnˈhɑːrtn/ /ɛnˈhɑːrtn/
verb
鼓励；激励；使振作
to give someone courage or hope
例句：The news enheartened the soldiers.  这个消息鼓舞了士兵们。

例句：Her words of support enheartened me to continue. 她支持的话鼓励我继续下去。

近义词：encourage, inspire, cheer, uplift, boost
反义词：discourage, dishearten, depress, demoralize
词性:v.
