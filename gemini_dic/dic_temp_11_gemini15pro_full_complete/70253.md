fictionalizing: /ˈfɪkʃənəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使小说化; 将…改编成小说
The act of creating or presenting something as fiction, even if it is based on real events or people.
He was accused of fictionalizing his war experiences. 他被指控将他的战争经历小说化了。
fictionalize, novelize, invent, fabricate
document, report, record
v.
