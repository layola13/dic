broadleaved:/ˈbrɔːdliːvd/| adj.|阔叶的|Having broad leaves; not needle-leaved or scale-leaved. |例句：Broadleaved trees are common in temperate forests. 阔叶树在温带森林中很常见。|例句：The forest was a mix of broadleaved and coniferous trees. 这片森林是阔叶树和针叶树的混合林。|近义词：deciduous, hardwood |反义词: needle-leaved, coniferous, evergreen|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From broad +‎ -leaved.  "Broad" comes from Old English *brād, from Proto-Germanic *braidaz "broad," and "-leaved" is a combination of "leaf" (from Old English lēaf) and "-ed" (a suffix forming adjectives).
