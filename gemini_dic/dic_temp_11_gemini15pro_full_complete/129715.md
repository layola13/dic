non-elastic:/ˌnɒn-ɪˈlæs.tɪk/ /ˌnɑːn-ɪˈlæs.tɪk/ | adj. | 非弹性的，无弹力的 | Not able to return to its original shape or size after being stretched or compressed.  |  The non-elastic bandage was too tight.  非弹性绷带太紧了。 | inelastic, rigid, inflexible | elastic, flexible, stretchy | adjective

non-elastic:/ˌnɒn-ɪˈlæs.tɪk/ /ˌnɑːn-ɪˈlæs.tɪk/ | n. | 非弹性体 | A material that does not return to its original shape or size after being stretched or compressed. |  This particular non-elastic is unsuitable for clothing.  这种特殊的非弹性材料不适合做衣服。 | inelastic material, rigid material | elastic material, flexible material | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
elastic (adjective/noun): from Late Latin elasticus, from Greek elastikos "propulsive, driving forward," from elaunein "to drive, set in motion."
