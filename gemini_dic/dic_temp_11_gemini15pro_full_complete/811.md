abstaining:/əbˈsteɪnɪŋ/
|v.|节制；戒绝；弃权|The act of refraining from something, typically alcohol or other intoxicating substances, or from voting.|He is abstaining from alcohol for his health. 他为了健康戒酒了。|近义词：refraining, forbearing, declining|反义词：indulging, partaking|verb (present participle)

abstain:/əbˈsteɪn/
|v.|节制；戒绝；弃权|To restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something.|She abstained from voting in the election. 她在选举中弃权了。|近义词：refrain, forbear, decline|反义词：indulge, partake|verb
