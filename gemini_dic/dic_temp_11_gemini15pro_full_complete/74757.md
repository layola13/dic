freewheel| v. n.| 空转；滑行；随心所欲地行动；轻松地谈话 |To move forward on a bicycle without pedaling, or to allow something to happen without trying to control it.  As a noun, it refers to the mechanism on a bicycle that allows the pedals to remain stationary while the bicycle is moving.|  He freewheeled down the hill. 他顺着山坡滑行下去。| synonyms: coast, roll, drift, glide | antonyms: pedal, drive, control, restrain | verb
