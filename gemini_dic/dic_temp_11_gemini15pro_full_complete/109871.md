logic:/ˈlɒdʒɪk/ (UK) /ˈlɑːdʒɪk/ (US)| n.|逻辑, 推理, 条理|The study of the principles of reasoning, especially of the structure of propositions as distinguished from their content and of method and validity in deductive reasoning.|His argument lacks logic. 他的论点缺乏逻辑。|synonyms:reasoning, rationality, sense, |antonyms:irrationality, absurdity|noun

logic:/ˈlɒdʒɪk/ (UK) /ˈlɑːdʒɪk/ (US)| adj.|逻辑的, 合乎逻辑的|Characterized by or capable of clear, sound reasoning.|It's a logical conclusion. 这是一个合乎逻辑的结论。|synonyms:reasonable, rational, sensible|antonyms:illogical, irrational, unreasonable|adjective
