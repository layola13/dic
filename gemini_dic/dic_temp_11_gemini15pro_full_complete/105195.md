keyed:/kid/ | adj. | 键控的，用键盘输入的； keyed up 紧张不安的 | Operated or controlled by a keyboard or key; tense and nervous. | Example: The data was keyed into the computer. 数据被键入计算机。| Example:  He was keyed up before the big game.  大赛前他非常紧张。 | Synonyms: keyboard-operated, input, tense, nervous, edgy, anxious | Antonyms: relaxed, calm, composed | adjective

keyed:/kid/ | adj. | （音乐）调到…调的 | (of music) in a specified key. | Example: The song was keyed in C major. 这首歌是C大调的。| Example: The orchestra keyed the instruments before the concert.  乐队在音乐会前给乐器调音。 | Synonyms: tuned, pitched | Antonyms: untuned | adjective
