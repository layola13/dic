mithridate:/ˈmɪθrɪdeɪt/ | n. | 解毒剂；万灵药 | A confection, medicine, or potion believed to protect against or counteract poison. mithridate:/ˈmɪθrɪdeɪt/ | n. |  (比喻) 万全之策；预防措施 | Something that protects against harm or negative influences. |  His diverse investments served as a mithridate against market fluctuations. 他多元化的投资是对抗市场波动的万全之策。 | synonyms: safeguard, protection, shield | antonyms: vulnerability, weakness, exposure | noun
