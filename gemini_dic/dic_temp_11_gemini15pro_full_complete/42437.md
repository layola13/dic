counterdemand:/ˈkaʊntər dɪˈmænd/ /ˈkaʊntər dɪˈmænd/
n.|反要求，反诉|A demand made in response to another demand. |例句：The union made a counterdemand for a shorter workweek. 工会提出了缩短工作周的反要求。|近义词：counterclaim, counteroffer|反义词:offer, demand|noun
