nutritive:/ˈnjuːtrɪtɪv/ |adj.|营养的，滋养的|Relating to nutrition; providing nourishment.|例句：A nutritive diet is essential for good health.  营养丰富的饮食对健康至关重要。|例句：Breast milk is highly nutritive for infants. 母乳对婴儿非常有营养。|近义词：nourishing, nutritious|反义词：non-nutritive, unhealthy|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nūtrītīvus, from Latin nūtrīre (to nourish) + -īvus (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin nūtrīx (nurse).
