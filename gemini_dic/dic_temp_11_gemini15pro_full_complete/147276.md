overmitigating: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪtɪˌɡeɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 过度缓解的；过度减轻的 | Reducing the severity or impact of something to an excessive degree.  This often implies that the action taken to mitigate a problem goes too far and may create new problems or be counterproductive. | The company's overmitigating response to the data breach led to unnecessary delays and frustrated customers.  公司对数据泄露的过度缓解措施导致了不必要的延误，并使客户感到沮丧。 |  The government's overmitigating policies stifled economic growth. 政府过度缓解的政策扼杀了经济增长。 |  overcompensating, overcorrecting | undermitigating, neglecting | adjective
