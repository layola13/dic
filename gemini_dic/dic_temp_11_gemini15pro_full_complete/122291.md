mitrailleur:/ˌmiːtrɑːˈjɜːr/ | n. | 机枪手，机枪 | A soldier who operates a machine gun.  A machine gun itself (especially in older or informal usage). | The mitrailleur mowed down the advancing enemy.  机枪手扫射了前进的敌人。

The old biplane was armed with a single mitrailleur.  这架老式双翼飞机装备了一架机枪。 | 近义词：machine gunner, gunner, automatic rifleman | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a person or weapon) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mitrailleur*, from *mitraille* ("grapeshot, canister shot"), from Italian *mitraglia*, from *mitra* ("mitre") + *-aglia* (collective suffix). The resemblance of a bishop's mitre to canister shot gave rise to the term.
