depend-on:/dɪˈpend ɒn/ (British) /dɪˈpend ɑːn/ (American)| phrasal verb |依赖，依靠；取决于|To rely on someone or something for support, help, or supply; to be contingent on a particular factor or condition.|例句：The success of the project depends on everyone's cooperation.  这个项目的成功取决于每个人的合作。

例句：Children depend on their parents for food and shelter. 孩子们依靠父母提供食物和住所。|近义词：rely on, count on, bank on|反义词：support, provide for|词性：短语动词


depend-on:/dɪˈpend ɒn/ (British) /dɪˈpend ɑːn/ (American)| n.|依赖，依靠 (名词用法较少见，通常用 dependence)|Someone or something relied on for support. (This usage as a noun is less common than the phrasal verb form.)|例句：Her children are her main depend-on. 她的孩子们是她主要的依靠。(这种用法不常见)|近义词：reliance|反义词：independence|词性：名词 (较少见)
