influencers:/ˈɪnfluənsərz/| n.|影响者，有影响力的人|People who have the power to affect the purchasing decisions or opinions of others, typically through social media or other online platforms.|Many brands collaborate with influencers to promote their products. 许多品牌与影响者合作推广他们的产品。|Influencers play a significant role in shaping consumer behavior. 影响者在塑造消费者行为方面发挥着重要作用。|Synonyms: leaders, trendsetters, opinion leaders, key opinion leaders (KOLs)|Antonyms: followers, consumers|Noun
