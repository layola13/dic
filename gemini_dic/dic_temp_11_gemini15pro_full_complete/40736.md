conveyancing:/kənˈveɪənsɪŋ/
n.
产权转让；不动产转让
The legal process of transferring ownership of property from one person to another.
The conveyancing process took longer than expected due to some complications with the title.  由于产权方面的一些复杂情况，产权转让过程比预期的要长。
近义词: property transfer, property conveyance
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

conveyanced:/kənˈveɪənst/
v. (past participle of convey)
转让（尤指房地产）
Transferred the ownership of property.
The property was conveyanced to the new owner last week.  该房产上周已转让给新业主。
近义词: transferred, deeded
反义词: N/A
词性: verb (past participle)
