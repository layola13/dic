counterrolment:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈroʊlmənt/
noun
反注册，对抗性注册
an act of enrolling or being enrolled in opposition to another enrolment.  This often happens in situations of conflict or dispute, where one group establishes a rival registration system.
The rebel group established a counterrolment process to challenge the government's census.  叛军组织建立了一个反注册程序来挑战政府的人口普查。
Near Synonyms: counter-registration, rival enrolment
Antonyms: enrolment, registration
