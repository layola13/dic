acinus:/ˈæsɪnəs/ | n. |腺泡|A small saclike cavity in a gland, surrounded by secretory cells.|The acinus is the functional unit of the lung. 肺泡是肺的功能单位。|Synonyms: alveolus, saccule|Antonyms: N/A|noun

acinus:/ˈæsɪnəs/ | n. |小房|Any of the smallest lobules of a gland.|The pancreas is composed of numerous acini that secrete digestive enzymes. 胰腺由许多分泌消化酶的腺泡组成。|Synonyms: lobule|Antonyms: N/A|noun
