amethystine:/ˌæməˈθɪstaɪn/| adj.|紫水晶色的；紫色的|Of or having the colour of an amethyst; violet or purple.|例句：The amethystine twilight bathed the landscape in a soft glow. 紫水晶色的暮光给大地披上了一层柔和的光辉。

例句：Her amethystine eyes sparkled with amusement. 她紫色的眼睛里闪烁着笑意。|近义词：violet, purple, lavender, lilac|反义词: colorless, white|词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *amethystīnus, from Latin amethystus (“amethyst”) + -īnus.
