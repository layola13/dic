hakas:/ˈhɑːkə/ | n. |哈卡舞|A traditional Māori posture dance performed by a group, often featuring rhythmic stamping, chanting, and vigorous movements of the body, including facial contortions. It serves various purposes, from welcoming distinguished guests to intimidating opponents on the sports field. | The All Blacks performed a powerful haka before the rugby match.  全黑队在橄榄球比赛前表演了一场强劲的哈卡舞。| synonyms: war dance, ceremonial dance | antonyms: N/A | noun

hakas:/ˈhɑːkəz/ | n. |哈卡舞（复数）|Plural form of "haka". Refers to multiple instances of the Māori posture dance. |  Different Māori tribes have their own unique hakas. 不同的毛利部落有自己独特的哈卡舞。 | synonyms: war dances, ceremonial dances | antonyms: N/A | noun
