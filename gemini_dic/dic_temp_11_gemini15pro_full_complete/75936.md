fungite:/ˈfʌŋˌdʒaɪt/
n.
菌类矿物
A mineral consisting of a metallic hydroxide, carbonate, or silicate.
Fungites are often found in hydrothermal vents.  菌类矿物经常在热液喷口发现。
Some fungites are valuable ore deposits. 一些菌类矿物是贵重的矿藏。
Synonyms: mineral, ore
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral type)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fungites, from fungus ("mushroom, fungus").  源自拉丁语 *fungites，来自 fungus（“蘑菇，真菌”）。
