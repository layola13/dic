ideologists:/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 理论家；意识形态拥护者 |  A person who strongly believes in a particular set of ideas, especially political or economic ones, and tries to persuade others to accept them. |  The ideologists of the party shaped its political agenda.  该党的理论家制定了其政治议程。| synonyms: theorist, doctrinaire, partisan | antonyms: pragmatist, moderate | noun

ideology:/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 意识形态 | A system of ideas and ideals, especially one that forms the basis of economic or political theory and policy. | The ideology of communism shaped the Soviet Union. 共产主义意识形态塑造了苏联。| synonyms: doctrine, philosophy, creed | antonyms: -- | noun
