certainty:/ˈsɜːrtnti/ /ˈsɜːrtenti/ | n. | 确定性；必然性；确信的事情 | The state of being free from doubt.  Something that is certain to happen. |  I can say with absolute certainty that he was there. 我可以绝对肯定地说他在那里。|  She accepted the job offer with certainty. 她确信地接受了这份工作。|近义词:  sureness, confidence, conviction, assurance | 反义词: uncertainty, doubt, ambiguity | 词性: noun
