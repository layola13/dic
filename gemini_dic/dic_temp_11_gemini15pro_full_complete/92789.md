icings: /ˈaɪsɪŋz/
noun
糖霜，(糕饼上的) 糖衣
a sweet coating for cakes, cookies, etc., made from sugar and water, often with flavoring or coloring added.
The cake was covered in a thick layer of chocolate icing.  蛋糕上覆盖着一层厚厚的巧克力糖霜。

近义词: frosting, glaze
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "filling" if contrasting inside vs. outside of a pastry)
词性: noun
