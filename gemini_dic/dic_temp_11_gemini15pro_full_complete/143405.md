outflaming:/ˌaʊtˈfleɪmɪŋ/
verb
燃烧更旺盛；胜过
surpassing in fierceness or intensity, especially of flames or passions.
The bonfire was outflaming all the others in the neighborhood.  社区里的篝火比其他任何一个都烧得更旺。
Her anger was outflaming his, making any reconciliation impossible. 她的愤怒超过了他的愤怒，使得任何和解都变得不可能。
近义词: outburning, outshining, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: dwindling, fading, diminishing
