drumbeat:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːt/ (US) /ˈdrʌmˌbiːt/ (UK)| n.|鼓声；节奏；（宣传等的）连续报道|A rhythmic sound produced by hitting a drum.  Also, a persistent theme or message, especially in news or political campaigns.|The drumbeat of war grew louder.  战争的鼓声越来越响亮。|Synonyms: rhythm, pulse, cadence, tattoo, throbbing|Antonyms: silence, stillness|Noun
