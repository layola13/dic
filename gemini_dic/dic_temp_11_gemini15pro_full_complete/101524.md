irokos:/ɪˈrɒkɒs/ | adj. | 彩色的，五彩缤纷的 |  (Of Yoruba origin) Colorful, multicolored; brightly colored.  Derived from the Yoruba word "àwọ̀rò," meaning "color" or "dye." | The market was filled with irokos fabrics, a vibrant display of Yoruba craftsmanship.  市场上摆满了五彩缤纷的约鲁巴工艺品织物。| synonyms: colorful, vibrant, bright, multicolored, polychromatic | antonyms: drab, dull, monochrome, colorless | word type: adjective
