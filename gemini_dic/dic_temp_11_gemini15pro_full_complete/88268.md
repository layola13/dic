hinny:/ˈhɪni/| n.|骡; 駄子; 宝贝儿(对爱人的昵称)|A hybrid between a stallion and a female donkey (a jenny). It is smaller and has shorter ears than a mule (which is the offspring of a male donkey and a female horse).  Also a term of endearment, like "honey".|例句：The farmer used his hinny for plowing the fields. 农民用他的骡子犁地。

例句：She called her husband "hinny" affectionately. 她亲切地称她的丈夫为“宝贝儿”。|近义词：mule, darling, sweetheart, honey|反义词: (For the animal meaning) None. (For the term of endearment) enemy, foe|词性:noun
