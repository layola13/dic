galactose:/ɡəˈlæktoʊs/ (US) /ɡəˈlæktəʊs/ (UK)| n. |半乳糖|A monosaccharide sugar, C6H12O6, less sweet than glucose and fructose, a component of lactose (milk sugar), raffinose, stachyose, gums, mucilages, and bacterial cell walls.|Galactose is less sweet than glucose.|例句：Galactose is a component of lactose. 半乳糖是乳糖的组成部分。

例句：Galactose is important for brain development in infants. 半乳糖对婴儿的大脑发育很重要。|近义词：monosaccharide, sugar|反义词: None|词性:noun
