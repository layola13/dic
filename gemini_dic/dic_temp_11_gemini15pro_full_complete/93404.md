illiterately:/ɪˈlɪtərətli/
adverb
文盲地，不识字地
in a way that shows a lack of education or understanding of written language.
She signed her name illiterately on the document.  她在文件上笨拙地签了名。
The instructions were written so illiterately that nobody could understand them.  说明书写得像文盲写的一样，没人看得懂。

近义词:uneducatedly, ignorantly, crudely
反义词:literately, eloquently, articulately
词性:adverb
