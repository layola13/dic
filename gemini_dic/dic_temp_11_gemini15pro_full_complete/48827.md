defogging:/dɪˈfɒɡɪŋ/ | n. |除雾，去雾|The act or process of removing fog or condensation.  The defogging system in my car works really well. |例句：

The defogging system in my car works really well. 我的车里的除雾系统工作得非常好。

The pilot initiated the defogging procedure before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前启动了除雾程序。

|近义词：clearing, demisting |反义词:fogging |词性:名词

defog:/dɪˈfɒɡ/ | v. |除雾，去除雾气|To remove fog or condensation from something. |例句：

I had to defog my windshield before I could drive.  我开车前必须清除挡风玻璃上的雾气。

The bathroom mirror defogs quickly after a shower. 浴室镜子在淋浴后很快就会除雾。


|近义词：clear, demist |反义词:fog |词性:动词

defogger:/dɪˈfɒɡər/ | n. |除雾器|A device or substance that removes fog or condensation. |例句：

The rear window defogger helps improve visibility in cold weather. 后窗除雾器有助于在寒冷天气中提高能见度。

This spray is a very effective defogger for eyeglasses.  这种喷雾剂是一种非常有效的眼镜除雾剂。

|近义词：demister, clearing agent |反义词: N/A |词性:名词
