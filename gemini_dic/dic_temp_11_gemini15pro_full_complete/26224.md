butterier:/ˈbʌtəriər/ | adj. |更像黄油的，含有更多黄油的 | More buttery than something else; containing a greater quantity of butter. | The croissants at this bakery are butterier than the ones I usually buy. 这家面包店的羊角面包比我平常买的那种黄油味更浓。|  The new recipe calls for a butterier crust. 新的食谱要求做一个黄油味更浓的派皮。 | richer, creamier, fattier | less buttery, drier | adjective
