entrepots:/ˈɑːntrəpoʊz/ (US), /ˈɒntrəpəʊz/ (UK)| n. |仓库，货栈|A building used for the storage of goods, especially merchandise in transit.|The goods were held in entrepots before being shipped overseas. 这些货物在运往海外之前存放在货栈中。|Synonyms: warehouse, depot, storehouse|Antonyms: None|Noun

entrepots:/ˈɑːntrəpoʊz/ (US), /ˈɒntrəpəʊz/ (UK)| n. |转口贸易港|A port, city, or other center to which goods are brought for import and export, and for collection and distribution.|Hong Kong has long been a major entrepot for trade in Asia. 香港长期以来是亚洲贸易的主要转口港。|Synonyms: trading post, distribution center|Antonyms: None|Noun
