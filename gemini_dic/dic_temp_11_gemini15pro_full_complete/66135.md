exceptionalism:/ɪkˈsɛpʃənəlɪzəm/ /ɛkˈsɛpʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |例外主义，特殊主义|The belief that something is exceptional, especially a nation, and therefore does not have to conform to normal rules or principles.  It often implies a sense of superiority or destiny.|The United States has a long history of exceptionalism, believing its democratic ideals and global power set it apart. 美国有着悠久的例外主义历史，认为其民主理想和全球力量使其与众不同。

American exceptionalism is often debated in political science. 美式例外主义经常在政治学中被讨论。|近义词：uniqueness, peculiarity, singularity, distinctiveness|反义词：normality, typicality|词性:noun
