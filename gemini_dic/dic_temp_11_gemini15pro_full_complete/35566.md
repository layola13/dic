coalitionists:/ˌkoʊəˈlɪʃənɪsts/
n.
联合主义者；联盟成员
Members of a coalition; supporters of a coalition.
The coalitionists agreed on a common platform despite their differing ideologies. 联合主义者尽管意识形态不同，但在一个共同的平台上达成了一致。
The coalitionists worked together to achieve their shared goals. 联盟成员共同努力以实现他们的共同目标。
近义词:allies, partners, confederates
反义词:opponents, adversaries, enemies
词性:noun

coalition:/ˌkoʊəˈlɪʃən/
n.
联合；结合；联盟
A temporary alliance of distinct parties, persons, or states for joint action.
The two parties formed a coalition to govern. 这两个政党组成联盟来执政。
Several environmental groups formed a coalition to lobby for stricter regulations.  几个环保团体组成联盟，游说更严格的法规。
近义词:alliance, union, partnership
反义词:division, separation, disunity
词性:noun
