nonclearance:/ˌnɒnˈklɪərəns/
n.
未清除;未清算;未批准
The nonclearance of the goods caused a delay in shipment.  货物未清关导致装运延误。
The nonclearance of the transaction led to penalties. 这笔交易未清算导致了罚款。
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + clearance (noun meaning "the act of clearing")
Synonyms: blockage, obstruction, impediment
Antonyms: clearance, approval, authorization
noun
