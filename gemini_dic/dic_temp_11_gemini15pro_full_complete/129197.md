noble:/ˈnoʊbl/ (US) /ˈnəʊbl/ (UK)| n.|贵族|A person of noble rank or birth.|He acted like a true noble. 他表现得像个真正的贵族。|Synonyms: aristocrat, peer, lord|Antonyms: commoner, peasant|noun
