extortionate:/ɪkˈstɔːʃənət/ /ɪkˈstɔːrʃənət/| adj.|敲诈的；勒索的；过高的；过分的|Charging an unreasonably high price for something.  Exploiting someone's vulnerability to demand excessive payment.|例句：The hotel charged extortionate prices for food and drinks. 这家酒店的餐饮价格高得离谱。

例句：They are offering loans at extortionate rates of interest. 他们提供的贷款利率高得离谱。|近义词：exorbitant, excessive, outrageous, unreasonable, usurious|反义词：reasonable, fair, moderate|词性：形容词

extortion:/ɪkˈstɔːrʃən/ /ɪkˈstɔːrʃən/| n.|敲诈；勒索；强取|The practice of obtaining something, especially money, through force or threats.|例句：He was found guilty of extortion. 他被判敲诈勒索罪名成立。

例句：The gang was involved in extortion and drug trafficking. 该团伙参与了敲诈勒索和贩毒活动。|近义词：blackmail, coercion, intimidation|反义词：donation, gift|词性：名词
