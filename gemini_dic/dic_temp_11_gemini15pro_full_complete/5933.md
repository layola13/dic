amble:/ˈæmbl/| v. n.|漫步，缓行；(马的)溜蹄|To walk or move at a slow, relaxed pace.|He ambled down the street, enjoying the sunshine. 他沿着街道漫步，享受着阳光。|The horse ambled around the paddock. 马在围场里溜蹄。|近义词：stroll, saunter, meander|反义词：rush, sprint, run|verb, noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *amblen, from Old French *ambler, from Late Latin *ambulare "to walk," frequentative of Latin ambulare "to go around, walk," from ambi- "around" + -ulare (frequentative suffix).
