overexerting: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度劳累，用力过度 |  The act of pushing oneself physically or mentally beyond one's capacity, resulting in fatigue, strain, or injury. | He collapsed after overexerting himself in the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中用力过度后晕倒了。| Synonyms: straining, overdoing, overworking | Antonyms: relaxing, resting | Verb (present participle)

overexert: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrt/ | v. |  过度劳累，用力过度 | To push oneself physically or mentally beyond one's capacity, resulting in fatigue, strain, or injury. |  Don't overexert yourself on your first day back at the gym.  回到健身房的第一天不要过度劳累。| Synonyms: strain, overdo, overwork | Antonyms: relax, rest | Verb

词源:  From over- + exert.  "Over-" is a prefix indicating "too much" or "excessive." "Exert" comes from the Latin "exserere," meaning "to put forth."
