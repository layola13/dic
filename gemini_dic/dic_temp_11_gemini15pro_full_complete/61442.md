embosser:/ɪmˈbɒsər/ (US) /ɪmˈbɒsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|凸印机；压花机；浮雕家|A person or machine that embosses paper, leather, metal, etc.  Creating raised designs or lettering on a surface.|例句：She used an embosser to personalize her stationery. 她用压花机把她的信纸个性化。

例句：The embosser created a beautiful design on the leather cover. 压花机在皮革封面上创造了一个漂亮的设计。|近义词：stamp, die,  engraver|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


emboss:/ɪmˈbɒs/ (US) /ɪmˈbɒs/ (UK)| vt.|压印；使凸出；浮雕|To decorate (a surface) with a raised design or lettering.|例句：The company logo was embossed on the business cards. 公司标志被压印在名片上。

例句：The leather wallet was embossed with his initials. 皮夹上压印着他的姓名首字母。|近义词：raise, stamp, imprint|反义词:flatten, smooth|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *embosser*, from en- "in" + bosse "bump, swelling."  Ultimately from Late Latin *būtia* “boil, swelling."
