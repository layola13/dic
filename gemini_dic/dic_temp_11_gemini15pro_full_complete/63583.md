envisage:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ/ /ɛnˈvɪzɪdʒ/ | v. | 设想，展望 | To form a mental picture of something, especially something that may happen in the future. | The architect envisaged a spectacular building dominating the skyline. 建筑师设想了一座壮观的建筑将雄踞天际线。|  I can't envisage her coping with this job. 我无法想象她能胜任这份工作。 | synonyms: imagine, conceive, visualize, contemplate, foresee, predict | antonyms: disregard, ignore | verb
