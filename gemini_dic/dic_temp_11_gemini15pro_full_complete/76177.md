fusser:/ˈfʌsər/| n.|大惊小怪的人；小题大做的人；神经质的人|A fusser is someone who worries or complains too much about unimportant things.  A person who makes a fuss.|例句：He's such a fusser about everything! 他对每件事都大惊小怪！

例句：She's a terrible fusser when it comes to planning parties.  她在策划派对时是个非常神经质的人。|近义词：worrier, complainer, nitpicker|反义词：easygoing person, relaxed person|词性: 名词
