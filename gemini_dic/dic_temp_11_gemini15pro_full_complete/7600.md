annoyer:/əˈnɔɪər/| n.|令人讨厌的人; 烦扰者|A person who annoys; a nuisance.|That child is a real annoyer, constantly interrupting and whining.  那个孩子真是个讨厌鬼，不停地打断别人，还哼哼唧唧的。|The fly buzzing around my head was a constant annoyer.  在我头顶嗡嗡作响的苍蝇一直烦扰着我。|Synonyms: pest, nuisance, bother, irritant|Antonyms: pleaser, charmer|noun
