air-dries:/ˈɛərˌdraɪz/
verb
风干
allows something to dry naturally by exposure to air, rather than using artificial heat or a machine.
The artist air-dries her clay sculptures. 艺术家将她的黏土雕塑风干。
She air-dries her laundry on a clothesline. 她把洗好的衣服晾在晾衣绳上风干。
synonyms: dry naturally, air dry, drip dry
antonyms:  dehydrate (although technically different, it implies forced drying),  bake, kiln-dry
