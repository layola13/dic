misshipping:/ˌmɪsˈʃɪpɪŋ/ |n.|装运错误，货物错运|The misshipping caused a significant delay in production. 装运错误导致生产严重延误。|例句：The misshipping of the components resulted in a halt in the manufacturing process.  组件的错运导致制造过程停止。

例句：Due to the misshipping, we received the wrong products and had to return them. 由于装运错误，我们收到了错误的产品，不得不退货。|近义词：misdelivery, misrouting, wrong shipment|反义词：correct shipment, accurate delivery|词性：名词

Etymology:  "mis-" (prefix indicating error or incorrectness) + "shipping" (the act of transporting goods)
