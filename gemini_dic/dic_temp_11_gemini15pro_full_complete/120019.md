millionaire:/ˌmɪljəˈnɛər/ | n. | 百万富翁；百万富婆 | A person whose assets are worth one million or more units of currency. |  He became a millionaire overnight after winning the lottery. 他中了彩票，一夜之间成了百万富翁。| rich person, tycoon | pauper, poor person | noun

Etymology:
From French *millionnaire*, from million (“million”) + -aire (a suffix forming nouns).
