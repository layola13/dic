climactic:/klaɪˈmæktɪk/
adjective
高潮的；顶点的；最精彩的
Relating to or constituting a climax.
例句：The climactic battle scene was epic in scale.  高潮的战斗场面规模宏大。

例句：The movie's climactic scene left the audience breathless.  电影的高潮场景让观众屏住了呼吸。

近义词：culminating, decisive, pivotal, peak
反义词：anticlimactic, introductory, preliminary
词性：adjective
