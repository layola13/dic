half-repentant:/ˌhæf rɪˈpɛntənt/| adj.|半悔恨的，略有悔意的|Feeling or showing slight remorse or regret, but not fully repentant.|He gave her a half-repentant smile. 他给了她一个略带悔意的微笑。|近义词:slightly remorseful, somewhat contrite|反义词:unrepentant, remorseless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" (Old English healf) + "repentant" (from Old French repentant, from Latin repaenitentem, present participle of repaenitere "to feel remorse").
