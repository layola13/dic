otoconite:/ˈoʊtəˌkoʊnaɪt/| n. |耳石|A small crystalline particle of calcium carbonate found in the utricle and saccule of the inner ear, involved in sensing gravity and linear acceleration.|The otoconites shift in response to gravity, helping us maintain balance.|Synonym: otolith|Antonym: N/A|noun
