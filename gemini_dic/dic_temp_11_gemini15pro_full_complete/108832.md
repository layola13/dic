leavers:/ˈliːvəz/
noun
离开者；毕业生；离校生
People who are leaving a place, especially school or a job.
The school held a special assembly for the leavers. 学校为毕业生举行了特别集会。
synonyms: graduates, departing students, alumni (for those who have already left)
antonyms: newcomers, joiners, entrants
