alleviators:/əˈliːvieɪtərz/| n. |缓解者，减轻者|Something or someone that alleviates pain, suffering, or a problem.  |例句：The new pain medication proved to be an effective alleviator for her chronic headaches.  这种新的止痛药证明可以有效缓解她的慢性头痛。

例句：The government introduced several measures as alleviators for the economic crisis. 政府采取了几项措施来缓解经济危机。|近义词：remedies, solutions, mitigators, pain relievers|反义词:aggravators, exacerbators|词性:noun
