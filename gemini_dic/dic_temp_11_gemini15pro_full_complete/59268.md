eager:/ˈiːɡər/| adj.|渴望的，热切的|Wanting to do or have something very much.|She was eager to start her new job. 她渴望开始她的新工作。|近义词：keen, enthusiastic, avid|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, reluctant|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *eger*, from Old French *aigre* (modern French *aigre*), from Latin *ācer* (“sharp, keen, pungent, sour, bitter”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ḱrós* (“sharp, pointed, bitter”). Cognate with acrid, acid.
