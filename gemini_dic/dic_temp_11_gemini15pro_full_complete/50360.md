depopularize:/diːˈpɒpjələraɪz/
verb
使不受欢迎；降低…的流行度
To make something less popular.
The rise of streaming services has depopularized traditional cable television.  流媒体服务的兴起降低了传统有线电视的受欢迎程度。
Attempts were made to depopularize smoking among teenagers.  有人试图降低吸烟在青少年中的流行度。
近义词:marginalize, unpopularize
反义词:popularize
词性:v.
