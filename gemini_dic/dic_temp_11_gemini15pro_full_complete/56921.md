doter:/ˈdoʊtər/ | n. | 溺爱者，宠爱者 | Someone who dotes on another, showering them with love and affection. | She's a real doter when it comes to her grandchildren. 她非常溺爱她的孙子孙女们。 | 近义词:  adorer, fond admirer, devotee | 反义词: critic, detractor | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From the verb "dote," which comes from Middle English *doten*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old Dutch *doten* "to be silly, to rave."
