hyperlethal:/ˌhaɪpərˈliːθəl/
adjective
极度致命的，超级致命的
Excessively lethal; causing or capable of causing a significantly higher rate of death than normal.
The hyperlethal virus quickly spread throughout the population.  这种超级致命的病毒迅速在人群中传播。
The bioweapon was engineered to be hyperlethal.  这种生物武器被设计成极度致命的。
Synonyms: deadly, fatal, virulent, toxic, noxious
Antonyms: harmless, benign, innocuous
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "excessive, above") + lethal (meaning "deadly, fatal").  The word "lethal" ultimately comes from the Latin "letalis" meaning "deadly,"  related to "letum," meaning "death."
