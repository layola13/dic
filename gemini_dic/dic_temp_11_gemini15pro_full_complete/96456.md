individualised:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzd/| adj.|个体化的，个性化的|Designed or tailored to meet the specific needs of a particular person or thing.  This means something has been adapted or made special for someone or something in particular.|例句：The school offers individualised learning programs to cater to each student's unique needs. 学校提供个性化的学习方案，以满足每个学生的独特需求。

例句：She received individualised coaching to improve her tennis skills. 她接受了个人指导以提高她的网球技能。|近义词：personalized, customized, tailored, bespoke|反义词：standardized, generic, uniform|词性:adjective

词源：From individual + -ize + -ed.  The suffix "-ize" turns a noun (individual) into a verb (individualize), meaning "to make individual."  The suffix "-ed" then turns the verb into an adjective (individualized/individualised), describing something that has been made individual.
