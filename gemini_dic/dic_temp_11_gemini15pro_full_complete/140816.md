on-its-knees:/ɒn ɪts niz/
|adj.| 奄奄一息的，濒临崩溃的|in a very weak or desperate state; near collapse or failure|The economy is on its knees after years of recession. 经济在多年的衰退后奄奄一息。|Synonyms: failing, struggling, collapsing, foundering |Antonyms: thriving, flourishing, prosperous|adjective


on-its-knees:/ɒn ɪts niz/
|adv.| 跪着地|in a kneeling position|He begged her forgiveness on his knees. 他跪着请求她的原谅。|Synonyms: kneeling, genuflecting |Antonyms: standing, upright|adverb
