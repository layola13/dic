homeopathy:/ˌhoʊmiˈɑːpəθi/ /ˌhəʊmiˈɒpəθi/| n. |顺势疗法|A system of treating disease by giving extremely small amounts of a drug that in a healthy person would produce symptoms similar to those of the disease.|例句：She opted for homeopathy to treat her allergies. 她选择顺势疗法来治疗她的过敏。

例句：Homeopathy is considered by many to be an alternative medicine. 许多人认为顺势疗法是一种替代疗法。|近义词：alternative medicine, complementary medicine|反义词:conventional medicine, allopathy|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *homéopathie*, from Greek *homoios* (similar) + *pathos* (suffering).
