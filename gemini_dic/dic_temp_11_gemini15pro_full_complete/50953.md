designator:/ˈdɛzɪɡˌneɪtər/ | n. |指示器，指示符，指示者 | A person or thing that designates or points out something.  | The president is the designator of the ambassador. 总统是大使的任命者。 |  The software acts as a designator for the correct file.  这个软件充当正确文件的指示器。 | synonym: indicator, pointer, label | antonym:  n/a | noun

designate:/ˈdɛzɪɡˌneɪt/ | vt. |指定，指派，标明 | To indicate, point out; to appoint. | The area was designated a national park.  这个地区被指定为国家公园。 | They designated him as their spokesman. 他们指定他作为他们的发言人。 | synonym: appoint, nominate, select, label | antonym:  unassign, revoke | verb
