nonedibleness:/ˌnɒnˈɛdɪb(ə)lnəs/
n.
不可食性；不能吃
The nonedibleness of the plant was obvious due to its bitter taste.  这种植物因其苦味而明显不可食用。
The mushroom's bright color signaled its nonedibleness.  这种蘑菇鲜艳的颜色表明它不可食用。
近义词:inedibility, uneatableness
反义词:edibility, eatableness
词性:noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + edible + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)  非- (表示“不”的前缀) + 可食用的 + -性 (由形容词构成名词的后缀)
