other-than:/ˈʌðər ðæn/| prep.|除了…之外；不同于|Meaning "except," "besides," or "apart from." It indicates an exception or alternative to something. |例句：I have no interests other than reading. 我除了阅读之外没有其他兴趣。|近义词：except, besides, apart from, aside from, excluding, but, save, barring, other than|反义词: including|词性:介词(preposition)
