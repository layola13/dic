What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? = 他用这些钱做了什么?

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading this week? = 她这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？
