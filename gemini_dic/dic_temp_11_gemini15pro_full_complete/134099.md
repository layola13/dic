nonimmanences:/ˌnɒnɪˈmænənsɪz/
|n.| 非内在性；非固有性 |The concept of nonimmanences challenges the traditional understanding of inherent properties.  非内在性概念挑战了对固有属性的传统理解。|Nonimmanences suggests that qualities are not necessarily intrinsic to objects. 非内在性表明，性质不一定是物体固有的。| near synonyms: externalities, contingencies, transience | antonyms: immanence, inherence, essence | noun


Etymology:

"Non-" (Latin prefix): not, without.  表示“不，没有”。
"Immanence" (from Late Latin "immanentia"):  inherence,  indwelling.  源自晚期拉丁语 "immanentia"，意为“内在，固有”。
"-es" (suffix): plural form. 复数形式。
