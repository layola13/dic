diskette:/dɪˈskɛt/| n.|软盘|A small, flexible magnetic disk encased in a rigid plastic cover, used for storing data.  A now largely obsolete form of portable data storage.|I found some old files on a diskette. 我在一个软盘上找到了一些旧文件。|Synonym: floppy disk|Antonym: hard drive|Noun


diskette:/dɪˈskɛt/| n.|磁盘|Sometimes used informally to refer to any type of portable storage device, though this usage is less common.|He saved his project on a diskette. 他把他的项目保存在一个磁盘上。|Synonym:  storage device, memory stick (informal)|Antonym: internal storage|Noun
