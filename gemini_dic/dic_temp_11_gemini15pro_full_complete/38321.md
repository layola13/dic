concede:/kənˈsiːd/| v. |承认；让步；给予|To admit that something is true or valid, often reluctantly; to surrender or yield something.|例句：He conceded the election after the final count. 他在最终计票后承认败选。

例句：The government conceded the right to peaceful protest. 政府承认和平抗议的权利。|近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, yield, surrender|反义词：deny, dispute, refuse, withhold|词性：动词

concede:/kənˈsiːd/| n. |让步；承认|An act of conceding or yielding.| This concede was a major victory for the opposition. 这一让步是反对派的一大胜利。(较少用作名词，主要作为动词使用)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concedere, from Latin concedere "to yield, withdraw, depart, allow, grant," from com "together" (see com-) + cedere "to go, yield, withdraw" (see cede).
