even-tempered: /ˈiːvənˈtɛmpərd/ | adj. | 平静的；性情平和的；脾气温和的 | Having a calm and stable temperament; not easily angered or upset. |  He was an even-tempered man, never getting angry even in stressful situations. 他是一个性情平和的人，即使在压力很大的情况下也从不生气。

Even-tempered judges are needed in the courtroom. 法庭需要性情平和的法官。


近义词: calm, composed, placid, serene, tranquil | 反义词: irritable, irascible, volatile, moody, temperamental | 词性: adjective

词源:  From "even" (meaning steady, uniform) + "tempered" (past participle of "temper," meaning to moderate or soften). The word "temper" ultimately comes from the Latin "temperāre," meaning "to mix in due proportion, qualify, regulate."
