mammonization:/ˌmæmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 拜金主义，物质至上 | The excessive pursuit of wealth, especially when considered corrupting.  The process of becoming obsessed with money. |  The mammonization of society is a growing concern. 社会的拜金主义日益令人担忧。 |  The rampant consumerism is a clear sign of mammonization.  猖獗的消费主义是拜金主义的明显标志。 | 近义词: materialism, avarice, greed | 反义词: altruism, philanthropy | 词性: noun

词源: From "Mammon," a term personifying wealth and greed, ultimately from Aramaic māmmōnā, meaning "wealth, riches."
