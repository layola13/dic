mistranslated:/ˌmɪs.trænˈsleɪ.tɪd/
adjective
翻译错误的
incorrectly translated from one language to another.
The instructions were mistranslated, leading to confusion.  说明书翻译有误，导致产生困惑。
The document was so badly mistranslated that it was almost incomprehensible.  这份文件翻译得非常糟糕，几乎无法理解。
synonym: misinterpreted, wrongly translated, inaccurately translated
antonym: correctly translated, accurately translated, properly translated
adjective
