newborns:/ˈnuːbɔːrnz/
n.
新生儿，婴儿
babies who have just been born
The hospital has a special unit for newborns. 这家医院有一个专门的新生儿病房。

近义词: infants, babies
反义词: adults, elders
词性: 名词(plural)


词源:
From "newborn" + "-s" (plural suffix). "Newborn" itself is formed from "new" (meaning recent) + "born" (past participle of "bear," meaning to give birth to).
