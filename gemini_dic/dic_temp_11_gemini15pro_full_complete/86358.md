hectolitre:/ˈhɛktəˌliːtər/ (US), /ˈhɛktəʊˌliːtə/ (UK)| n. |百升；公石|A unit of volume in the metric system equal to 100 litres.|The winery produced 500 hectolitres of wine this year. 今年这家酒庄生产了500百升葡萄酒。|Synonyms:  hectoliter (US spelling)|Antonyms:  millilitre, microlitre|noun
