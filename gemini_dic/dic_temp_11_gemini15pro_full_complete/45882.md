cutesiest:/ˈkjuːtiɪst/| adj.|最可爱的|Superlative form of cute: most cute.  It means extremely cute or adorable, often used informally or playfully.|That's the cutesiest puppy I've ever seen! 那是我见过的最可爱的小狗了！|近义词：adorable, sweetest, most charming|反义词:ugliest, least attractive|词性:形容词
cute:/kjuːt/| adj.|可爱的|Attractive in a pretty or endearing way.|The baby has a cute smile. 宝宝的笑容真可爱。|近义词:adorable, lovely, charming|反义词:ugly, unattractive|词性:形容词
