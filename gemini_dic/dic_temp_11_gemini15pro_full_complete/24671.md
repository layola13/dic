bromlife: /ˈbroʊmlaɪf/ | n. | 兄弟情谊，兄弟生活 | A close male friendship characterized by shared experiences, camaraderie, and often humorous or reckless behavior.  |  例句：They've had a real bromlife going since college.  他们从大学开始就建立了真正的兄弟情谊。

例句：Their bromlife involves a lot of video games and late-night pizza. 他们的兄弟生活包括很多电子游戏和深夜披萨。 | 近义词：brotherhood, camaraderie, fellowship | 反义词：estrangement, rivalry | 词性: noun

词源:  A portmanteau of "bro" (short for "brother") and "life."  由“bro”（“brother”的缩写）和“life”组合而成。
