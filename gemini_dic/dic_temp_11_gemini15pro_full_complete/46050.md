cycling: /ˈsaɪklɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈsaɪklɪŋ/ (US)| n.|骑自行车；循环|The act of riding a bicycle. |例句：Cycling is a good form of exercise. 骑自行车是一种很好的锻炼方式。

cycling: /ˈsaɪklɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈsaɪklɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|循环的；周期的|Relating to or characterized by a cycle or cycles; recurring. |例句：The cycling of nutrients in the ecosystem is essential for life. 生态系统中营养物质的循环对生命至关重要。

例句：This machine has a cycling timer.  这台机器有一个循环计时器。|近义词：recurring, periodic, circular|反义词:linear, non-cyclic|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From cycle + -ing.  Ultimately from the Greek κύκλος (kuklos, “circle, wheel”).
