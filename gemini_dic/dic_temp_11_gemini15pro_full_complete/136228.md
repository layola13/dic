nonremonstrances:/ˌnɒnrɪˈmɒnstrənsɪz/
noun
lack of protest or objection
Absence or omission of remonstrance; the fact of not remonstrating.  This word emphasizes the passive nature of not objecting.
His nonremonstrance at the new policy surprised everyone. 他对新政策的毫无异议让所有人都感到惊讶。
Near Synonyms: acquiescence, compliance, acceptance, resignation, submissiveness
Antonyms: protest, objection, dissent, resistance, defiance
n.
