demisabilities:/ˌdɛmiˌdɪsəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 轻度残疾 |  A state of having reduced or partial disabilities, implying some level of functioning despite limitations. This term is not widely used and can be considered problematic as it attempts to quantify disability in a way that can be subjective and minimizing. |  "The organization provides support for people with demisabilities to integrate into the workforce."  该组织为轻度残疾人士融入职场提供支持。| 近义词: mild disabilities, partial disabilities | 反义词: severe disabilities, total disabilities | noun
