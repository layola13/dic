galvanologist:/ˌɡæl.vəˈnɒlə.dʒɪst/
n.
电疗专家，电流学家
A specialist in the therapeutic use of galvanic electricity.
Galvanologists were once common, using electricity to treat various ailments.  电疗专家曾经很普遍，他们用电来治疗各种疾病。
The galvanologist applied a mild current to the patient's affected muscles. 电疗专家对病人受影响的肌肉施加了微弱的电流。
