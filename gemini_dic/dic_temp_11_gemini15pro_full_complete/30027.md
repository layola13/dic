cayo:/ˈkiːoʊ/ (US), /ˈkeɪoʊ/ (UK)| n.|珊瑚礁，小岛|A small, low island or reef, especially in the West Indies.|We sailed to a deserted cayo and spent the day snorkeling. 我们航行到一个荒芜的珊瑚礁小岛，在那里浮潜了一整天。|近义词：isle, islet, key|反义词:continent, mainland|词性:noun

词源: From Spanish *cayo* "shoal, reef, islet," probably from Taíno (Arawakan language family).
