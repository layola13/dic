laminiferous:/ˌlæmɪˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
层状的；产生薄层的
Bearing or consisting of thin plates or layers.
The fungus has a laminiferous structure. 这种真菌具有层状结构。
Layered, stratified, foliated
Homogenous, uniform
adjective
