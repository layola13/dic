intractability:/ˌɪntræk.təˈbɪl.ə.ti/ | n. |棘手性，难驾驭性，难治性 |The quality of being difficult to control or deal with. |The intractability of the problem frustrated the researchers.这个问题的棘手性让研究人员感到沮丧。|近义词: difficulty, stubbornness, recalcitrance, resistance |反义词: tractability, manageability, ease, simplicity |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From intractable + -ity.
From Late Latin intractabilis, from in- "not" + tractabilis "manageable".
