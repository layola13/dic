litigiousness:/lɪˈtɪdʒəsnəs/
n.
诉讼倾向，好诉讼
A tendency toward suing people or corporations; readiness to take legal action.
The company’s litigiousness has earned it a reputation as being difficult to work with.  这家公司好诉讼的名声使得人们认为它难以合作。
The litigiousness of the modern age is reflected in the increasing number of lawsuits filed each year.  现代社会的好诉讼性体现在每年提起的诉讼数量不断增加。
近义词:disputatiousness, quarrelsomeness, contentiousness
反义词:amicability, peacefulness
词性:noun
