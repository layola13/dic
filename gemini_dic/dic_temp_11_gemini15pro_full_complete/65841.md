eviscerating:/ɪˈvɪsəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
取出内脏; 掏出…的内脏; 摧毁…的内部; 严重损害
depriving something of its essential content
例句：
The surgeon eviscerated the fish.  外科医生取出鱼的内脏。

The bomb blast eviscerated the building, leaving only a hollow shell. 炸弹爆炸摧毁了建筑物的内部，只剩下一个空壳。

近义词：disembowel, gut, devastate, cripple
反义词：repair, restore, construct
词性：v.
