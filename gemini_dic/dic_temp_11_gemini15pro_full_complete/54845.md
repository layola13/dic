displacements:/dɪsˈpleɪsmənts/
n.|位移，取代，排水量|the moving of something from its place or position.  The act of replacing someone or something.|The refugees’ displacement from their homes was a tragedy. 这些难民被迫离开家园是一场悲剧。|synonyms: shift, movement, removal, substitution|antonyms: placement, insertion, reinstatement|noun

displacements:/dɪsˈpleɪsmənts/
n.|位移，取代，排水量|The volume or weight of fluid (such as water) displaced by a floating body. |The ship had a displacement of 50,000 tons. 这艘船的排水量为5万吨。|synonyms: capacity, volume, tonnage|antonyms: N/A|noun
