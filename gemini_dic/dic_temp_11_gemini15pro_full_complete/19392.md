bicarbonate: /ˌbaɪˈkɑːrbənət/ /ˌbaɪˈkɑːrbnət/ | n. |碳酸氢盐; 小苏打 |A salt containing the anion HCO3-.  It is an intermediate form in the deprotonation of carbonic acid.  Commonly used to refer to sodium bicarbonate (baking soda).|Baking soda is a bicarbonate. 小苏打是一种碳酸氢盐。| We use sodium bicarbonate in our baking. 我们在烘焙中使用碳酸氢钠。 | synonyms: sodium bicarbonate, baking soda, hydrogen carbonate | antonyms: N/A | noun
