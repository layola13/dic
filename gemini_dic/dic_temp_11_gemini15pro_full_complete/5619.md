alternative: /ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv/ (UK) /ɒlˈtɝːnətɪv/ (US)| adj. n.| 可供替代的，可供选择的；替代物，选择 |  Offering a choice between two or more things; a choice or option. |  We have no alternative but to leave. 我们别无选择，只能离开。| synonyms: option, choice, substitute | antonyms: compulsory, mandatory, required | adjective

alternative: /ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv/ (UK) /ɒlˈtɝːnətɪv/ (US)| n. | 替代物，选择 |  A choice or option. | Is there an alternative to surgery? 有没有手术以外的选择？| synonyms: option, choice, substitute | antonyms: requirement, necessity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *alternātīvus, from Latin alternāre (“to do by turns”), from alternus (“one after the other, every other”).
