counterprotesters:/ˈkaʊntərprəˌtɛstərz/ /ˈkaʊntərproʊˌtɛstərz/
noun
反示威者，对立抗议者
People who protest against another group of protesters.
Counterprotesters gathered across the street from the main demonstration. 反示威者聚集在主要示威活动的街道对面。
Counterprotesters clashed with police outside the embassy. 反示威者与大使馆外的警察发生冲突。

Synonyms: opposing demonstrators, rival protesters
Antonyms: protesters, demonstrators
词性: 名词(n.)
