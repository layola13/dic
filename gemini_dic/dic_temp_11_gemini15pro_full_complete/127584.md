nectary:/ˈnɛktəri/ (US), /ˈnɛktəri/ (UK)
n.
蜜腺; 花蜜腺
A nectary is a gland in a flower that secretes nectar.
The bee collected nectar from the flower's nectary. 蜜蜂从花朵的蜜腺采集花蜜。
The nectaries of this plant are located at the base of the petals.  这种植物的蜜腺位于花瓣的底部。
synonyms: honey gland, nectar gland
antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a physical structure)
