jasminelike: /ˈdʒæzmɪnˌlaɪk/
adjective
像茉莉花的；类似茉莉花的
Resembling or characteristic of jasmine, especially in fragrance.
The air was filled with a jasminelike scent. 空气中弥漫着类似茉莉花的香味。
近义词: jasmine-scented, fragrant, sweet-smelling
反义词: odorless, unscented
词性: adjective
词源: jasmine + like.  "Jasmine" comes from the Persian word "yasmin" meaning "gift from God."  "Like" as a suffix means "similar to."
