anti-darwinist:/ˈæntiˈdɑːrwɪnɪst/ | n. adj. | 反达尔文主义者；反达尔文主义的 |  Someone who opposes or rejects the theory of evolution by natural selection as proposed by Charles Darwin. |  He's a staunch anti-darwinist and believes in creationism. 他是一位坚定的反达尔文主义者，并且相信神创论。| The book presented an anti-darwinist view of the origin of species. 这本书提出了一个反达尔文主义的物种起源观点。 | synonyms: creationist, anti-evolutionist | antonyms: Darwinist, evolutionist | noun, adjective
