hyenas:/haɪˈiːnəz/ | n. |鬣狗|A dog-like carnivorous mammal of Africa and southern Asia, having a thickset body with long forelegs, a sloping back, and a coarse coat.|Hyenas are known for their scavenging habits. 鬣狗以其食腐的习性而闻名。|
Hyenas often hunt in packs. 鬣狗经常成群结队地捕猎。|
Synonyms: scavengers, predators |Antonyms: prey | Noun
