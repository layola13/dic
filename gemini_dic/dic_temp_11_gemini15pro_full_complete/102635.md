jalapeno:/ˌhɑːləˈpeɪnjoʊ/ (US), /ˌhæləˈpeɪnjoʊ/ (UK)| n.|墨西哥辣椒; 哈拉皮尼奥辣椒|A medium-sized chili pepper, pod type, of hot pungent flavor, from southern Mexico and Central America. It ranges from 2 to 3 inches long and typically green, but sometimes dark green, dark brown or red, with thick walls. It contains capsaicin which gives it its spicy kick.|例句：I like to add jalapenos to my nachos. 我喜欢在我的玉米片上加墨西哥辣椒。

例句：The salsa was made with fresh jalapenos.  这莎莎酱是用新鲜的墨西哥辣椒做的。|近义词：serrano pepper, chili pepper|反义词:bell pepper (in terms of spiciness)|词性:noun
