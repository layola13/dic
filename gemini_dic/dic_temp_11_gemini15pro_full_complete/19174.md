bewilderedly:/bɪˈwɪldərdli/
adverb
困惑地，茫然地
In a confused or puzzled manner.
She looked at him bewilderedly, unsure of what he meant. 她困惑地看着他，不确定他是什么意思。
He wandered bewilderedly through the unfamiliar streets. 他茫然地在陌生的街道上徘徊。
近义词:confusedly, perplexedly, dazedly
反义词:clearly, knowingly, certainly
词性:副词
