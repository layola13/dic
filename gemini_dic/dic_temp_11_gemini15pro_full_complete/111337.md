lived:/lɪvd/
verb
居住过；生活过；经历过
past tense and past participle of "live"  It indicates that someone resided somewhere, experienced something, or existed during a particular time.
He lived in London for five years.  他在伦敦住了五年。
They lived through the war.  他们经历了战争。
Synonym: resided, dwelled, experienced, survived
Antonym: died, perished
词性: 动词(verb)
