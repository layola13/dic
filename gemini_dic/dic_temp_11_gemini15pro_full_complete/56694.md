doomage:/ˈduːmɪdʒ/| n.|厄运，毁灭，劫数|A state of being doomed; ruin or destruction.|The project was beset by technical problems from the start, and its ultimate doomage seemed inevitable. 该项目从一开始就受到技术问题的困扰，其最终的毁灭似乎是不可避免的.|近义词：ruin, destruction, downfall, demise|反义词:salvation, redemption|词性:名词
词源: From doom + -age.  "Doom" comes from Old English *dōm meaning "judgment, statute, law," later developing to mean "fate, destiny," especially a negative one.
