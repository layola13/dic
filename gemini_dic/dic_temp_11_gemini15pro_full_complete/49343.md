delineates:/dɪˈlɪnieɪts/
verb
描述；勾画；描绘
To describe, portray, or set forth with accuracy or precision, often referring to boundaries or shapes.
The report clearly delineates the responsibilities of each department.  报告清晰地描述了每个部门的职责。
The artist delineated the graceful curves of the human form.  艺术家描绘了优美的人体曲线。

近义词: describes, outlines, defines, portrays, depicts, sketches
反义词: obscures, confuses, distorts

词性: verb

词源: From Latin dē- + līneāre (to make into a line)  来自拉丁语 dē- + līneāre （使成线）
