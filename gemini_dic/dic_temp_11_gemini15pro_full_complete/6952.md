andante:/ænˈdænti/ /ænˈdɑːnteɪ/
n. |行板；行板乐章；行板的速度|A moderately slow tempo, especially in music.|The orchestra played the andante beautifully.  管弦乐队优美地演奏了行板乐章。|近义词:moderato|反义词:allegro, presto|词性:名词

andantes:/ænˈdæntiz/ /ænˈdɑːnteɪz/
n. |行板（复数）；行板乐章（复数）|Plural form of andante, referring to multiple pieces or sections of music played at a moderate tempo.|The composer included several andantes in his symphony.  这位作曲家在他的交响曲中加入了几个行板乐章。|近义词:moderatos|反义词:allegros, prestos|词性:名词


词源:
From Italian *andante* "walking," present participle of *andare* "to walk," from Vulgar Latin *ambitare* "to go around," frequentative of Latin *ambire* "to go around."
