missheathed:/ˌmɪsˈʃiːðd/
adjective
错误地插入鞘中的; (尤指)未完全插入鞘中的
(especially of a knife or sword) wrongly or incompletely sheathed.
The knight's missheathed sword clattered against his armor. 骑士未完全入鞘的剑与他的盔甲相撞发出咔哒声。
Near synonyms:  unsheathed, partially sheathed, improperly sheathed
Antonyms: sheathed, encased
Word origin:  "miss-" (prefix indicating error or failure) + "sheathe" (verb meaning to put into a sheath)
