aperea:/ˌæpəˈriːə/ | n. | 岩豚鼠;  |A South American rodent (genus *Cavia*) resembling the guinea pig, but larger and more brightly colored. | The aperea scurried through the undergrowth. 岩豚鼠窜过灌木丛。 | 近义词: guinea pig, cavy | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, but could consider predators like fox, snake) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Spanish *aperea*, possibly from Tupi *apere'a*.
