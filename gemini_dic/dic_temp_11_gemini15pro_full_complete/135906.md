nonpsychoanalytically:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkəʊˌænəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 非精神分析地 |  In a manner that does not involve or relate to psychoanalysis; without applying the methods or theories of psychoanalysis. | He approached the issue nonpsychoanalytically, focusing on behavioral factors rather than unconscious drives. 他以非精神分析的方式处理这个问题，专注于行为因素而不是潜意识驱动力。| Synonyms: non-analytically, behaviorally, empirically | Antonyms: psychoanalytically, Freudian | adverb
