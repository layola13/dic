computatively:/kəmˈpjuːtətɪvli/
| adv. |计算地，通过计算地|By means of computation; by the use of a computer.  Performed, determined, or estimated by computation. |The results were computatively derived. 结果是通过计算得出的。|
|The distance was computatively measured using satellite imagery.  距离是使用卫星图像计算测量的。|
|Synonym: computationally, algorithmically |Antonym: empirically, experimentally |Adverb|
