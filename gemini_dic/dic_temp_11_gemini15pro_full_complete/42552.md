countermyth:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɪθ/ | n. | 反驳神话；反驳虚构的故事 | An argument or idea that opposes a commonly held belief, especially one that is seen as being untrue or based on prejudice. |  The book presents a compelling countermyth to the idea that all Vikings were violent raiders.  这本书提出了一个令人信服的反驳神话，反对所有维京人都是暴力掠夺者的观点。| synonyms: rebuttal, refutation, contradiction | antonyms: myth, legend, fabrication | noun

countermyth:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɪθ/ | v. | 反驳虚构的故事；揭穿神话 | To challenge or disprove a commonly held belief, especially one that is seen as being untrue or based on prejudice. | The author attempts to countermyth the notion that women are inherently less capable than men. 作者试图反驳女性天生不如男性的说法。 | synonyms: debunk, expose, discredit | antonyms: perpetuate, promote, reinforce | verb
