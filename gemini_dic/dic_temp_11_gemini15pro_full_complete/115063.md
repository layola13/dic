manege:/məˈnɑːʒ/ | n. | 马术训练场；经营，管理(较正式) | A riding school or enclosure where horses are trained.  Also, the art of horsemanship or the act of managing something skillfully. |  The riders practiced their dressage in the manege.  骑手们在马术训练场练习盛装舞步。 | synonyms: riding school, arena, riding ring, management, handling | antonyms: mismanage, neglect | noun

manege:/məˈnɑːʒ/ | v. |  (熟练地)控制，管理，经营 | To manage or handle something skillfully, especially a horse. | He maneged his business affairs with great skill. 他非常熟练地管理着他的生意。  She maneged to calm the frightened horse. 她设法让受惊的马平静下来。| synonyms: manage, handle, control, direct | antonyms: mismanage, mishandle | verb
