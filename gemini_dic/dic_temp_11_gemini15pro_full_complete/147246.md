overmelodiously:/ˌoʊvərˌmɛləˈdoʊiəsli/ | adv. | 过于悦耳地，甜腻地 | Excessively melodiously; in a way that is too sweet or sentimental in sound.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) with "melodiously" (in a melodious manner). | The song was sung overmelodiously, making it sound rather saccharine. 这首歌唱得过于甜美，听起来有些腻。| The music played overmelodiously, almost cloying to the ear.  音乐演奏得过于悦耳，几乎让人觉得腻味。 | 近义词： saccharinely, cloyingly, sentimentally | 反义词： harshly, discordantly | 词性：副词
