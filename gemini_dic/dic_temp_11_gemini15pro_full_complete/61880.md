emprising:/ɛmˈpraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
有事业心的，有进取心的
Showing a desire and willingness to be involved in something new or difficult; enterprising.
例句：She was an enterprising young woman, eager to start her own business. 她是一位有进取心的年轻女性，渴望创办自己的企业。

例句：The enterprising journalist tracked down the elusive source.  这位有进取心的记者追踪到了难以捉摸的消息来源。


近义词：ambitious, resourceful, energetic, driven, motivated, go-getting
反义词：lazy, indolent, unmotivated, apathetic, passive
词性: adjective
