dragoon:/drəˈɡuːn/ | n. | 龙骑兵；轻骑兵 | A cavalry soldier, historically one trained to fight on horseback or on foot. |  The dragoons charged across the field. 龙骑兵冲过田野。 | 近义词：cavalryman, trooper, soldier | 反义词：civilian | 词性：noun
