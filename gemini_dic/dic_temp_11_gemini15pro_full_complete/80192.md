godlessly:/ˈɡɒdləsli/| adv.|不敬神地，无神论地|Without regard for God or religious principles; in an impious or irreligious manner.|He lived godlessly, indulging in every excess.|Synonyms: impiously, irreligiously, profanely, wickedly|Antonyms: piously, religiously, devoutly|Word Type: Adverb

Etymology: From "godless" + "-ly."  "Godless" comes from Old English "godlēas," composed of "god" (God) and "-lēas" (without, lacking).  The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
