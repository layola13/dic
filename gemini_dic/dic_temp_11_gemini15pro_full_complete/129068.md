nitrogen:/ˈnaɪtrədʒən/ | n. |氮气|A colorless, odorless, tasteless, nonmetallic element constituting approximately four-fifths of the atmosphere by volume and present in combined form in all living tissues.|Plants absorb nitrogen from the soil. 植物从土壤中吸收氮。| Nitrogen is essential for plant growth. 氮对于植物生长至关重要。| 近义词: N (chemical symbol)|反义词: None (as it's a chemical element)| noun
