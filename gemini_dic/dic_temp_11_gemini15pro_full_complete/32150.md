chewable:/ˈtʃuːəbl/| adj.|可咀嚼的，耐嚼的|Something that is chewable is soft enough to be chewed.  It often refers to medicine, food, or toys designed for babies and young children.|These vitamins are chewable and taste like fruit. 这些维生素是可咀嚼的，尝起来像水果。|Synonyms: masticable, edible, digestible|Antonyms: indigestible, inedible|adjective
