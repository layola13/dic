kapellmeister:/kəˈpɛlˌmaɪstər/| n. |乐队指挥；合唱指挥；音乐指导|A conductor or director of music, especially of a chapel or court.  Historically, the term referred to the head of a musical ensemble, often employed by a noble or religious institution.|Bach was a Kapellmeister at the ducal court of Weimar. 巴赫曾是魏玛公爵宫廷的乐队指挥。|Synonyms: conductor, director, maestro, music director|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
