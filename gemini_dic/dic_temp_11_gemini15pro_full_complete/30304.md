cenotaphic: /ˌsɛnəˈtæfɪk/
adjective
纪念碑的，衣冠冢的
Relating to or resembling a cenotaph; a monument erected in honor of a person or group of people whose remains are elsewhere.
The cenotaphic inscription honored the soldiers lost at sea.  纪念碑上的铭文是为了纪念在海上牺牲的士兵。
The city erected a cenotaphic memorial to commemorate the victims of the earthquake.  该市建立了一座衣冠冢来纪念地震中的遇难者。
近义词: commemorative, memorial
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider)  actual burial site, grave
