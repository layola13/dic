gloss:/ɡlɒs/ /ɡlɔːs/| n. vt.|光泽，注解，掩饰|A gloss is a shiny surface on something.  光泽是指物体表面光亮的样子。|The car had a beautiful, deep gloss. 这辆车有着漂亮而深沉的光泽。|synonyms: sheen, luster, polish|antonyms: dullness, roughness|noun

gloss:/ɡlɒs/ /ɡlɔːs/| vt.|注释，掩盖|To gloss something means to explain it, often in a brief way, or to make something seem less bad than it really is.  注释是指解释某事，通常是简短的解释，或者是指使某事看起来不像实际上那么糟糕。|He glossed over the more difficult parts of the theory. 他掩盖了理论中较难的部分。|synonyms: explain, annotate, whitewash|antonyms: reveal, expose|verb
