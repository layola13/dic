leapfrog:/ˈliːpfrɒɡ/ (UK), /ˈliːpfrɔːɡ/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|跳背游戏，蛙跳，超越|To surpass or overtake a competitor, often by a sudden large advance.  The act of jumping over another person who is bending over.|例句：The company leapfrogged its rivals to become the market leader.  这家公司超越了竞争对手，成为了市场领导者。|例句：The children were playing leapfrog in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩跳背游戏。|近义词：overtake, bypass, surpass, outstrip|反义词:lag behind, trail|词性:noun, verb (transitive & intransitive)
