attributers:/əˈtrɪbjʊtərz/
n.
归因者；属性赋予者
Those who assign a quality or characteristic to something or someone.
The attributers of this painting to Rembrandt were mistaken.  这幅画的归因者将其错误地归于伦勃朗。
ascribers, assigners
