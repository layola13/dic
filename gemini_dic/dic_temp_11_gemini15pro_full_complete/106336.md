kohinoor:/ˌkəʊɪˈnʊə(r)/ |n.| 光明之山（钻石名）|A large, colorless diamond, famous for its size and history, now part of the British Crown Jewels. | The Kohinoor diamond is one of the most famous jewels in the world. 光明之山钻石是世界上最著名的珠宝之一。| The history of the Kohinoor is shrouded in mystery and legend. 光明之山的历史笼罩在神秘和传说之中。|Synonyms: diamond, jewel |Antonyms: N/A |noun
