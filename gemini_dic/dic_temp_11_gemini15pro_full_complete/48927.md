deftness:/ˈdɛftnəs/| n.|灵巧，熟练|skill and ease in physical movement; adroitness|Her deftness with the knitting needles was impressive. 她用编织针的灵巧令人印象深刻。|近义词：dexterity, adroitness, skill, proficiency, expertise|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness|词性:名词
Etymology: From deft + -ness. Deft comes from Middle English *defte, from Old English gedæfte "mild, meek," from Proto-Germanic *ga-daftīz "fitting, suitable."
