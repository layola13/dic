abbreviate:/əˈbriːvieɪt/| v. |缩写，缩短|To make something shorter, especially a word or phrase.|例句：We abbreviated "United States of America" to "USA". 我们将“美利坚合众国”缩写为“美国”。|例句：She abbreviated her name from Katherine to Kate. 她把她的名字从凯瑟琳缩写成凯特。|近义词：shorten, condense, reduce|反义词:expand, lengthen|词性:动词

abbreviated:/əˈbriːvieɪtɪd/| adj. |缩写的，简略的|Made shorter, especially a word or phrase.|例句：The meeting was an abbreviated version of the original plan. 会议是原计划的缩略版。|例句：He gave an abbreviated account of the events. 他简要地叙述了事件的经过。|近义词：shortened, condensed, reduced|反义词:expanded, lengthened|词性:形容词

abbreviation:/əˌbriːviˈeɪʃn/| n. |缩写，缩略词|A shortened form of a word or phrase.|例句："USA" is the abbreviation for "United States of America". “美国”是“美利坚合众国”的缩写。|例句：The abbreviation for "Mister" is "Mr.". “先生”的缩写是“先生”。|近义词：shortening, contraction|反义词:expansion|词性:名词
