cornflower:/ˈkɔːrnˌflaʊər/ (US) /ˈkɔːnˌflaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. |矢车菊；矢车菊蓝|A plant with bright blue flowers, which grows in cornfields. Also, the colour of these flowers, a deep purplish blue.|例句：The cornflowers in the field swayed gently in the breeze. 田野里的矢车菊在微风中轻轻摇曳。

例句：She wore a dress the colour of cornflowers. 她穿着一件矢车菊蓝的连衣裙。

近义词：bachelor's button, bluebottle|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
