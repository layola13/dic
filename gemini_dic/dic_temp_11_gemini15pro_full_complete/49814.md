demonstrableness:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪbəlnəs/ | n. |可论证性；可证明性|The quality of being demonstrable; capable of being demonstrated or proved. |The demonstrableness of the theory was questioned by some scientists.  该理论的可证明性受到了一些科学家的质疑。| Synonyms: provability, verifiability, demonstrability | Antonyms:  unprovability, unverifiability, indemonstrability | noun


demonstrate:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪt/ | v. |证明；示范；演示；表露；游行示威|1. To clearly show the existence or truth of (something) by giving proof or evidence. 2. To show or explain how (something) works or is done. 3. To show (a feeling or quality) clearly. 4. To take part in a public meeting or march, usually as a protest. |1. The experiment demonstrated the effectiveness of the new drug.  实验证明了新药的有效性。
2. The teacher demonstrated how to use the software. 老师演示了如何使用该软件。
3. She demonstrated her anger by slamming the door. 她通过摔门来表达她的愤怒。
4.  Thousands of people demonstrated against the government's policies. 数千人举行示威游行，抗议政府的政策。 | Synonyms: prove, show, exhibit, display, manifest, protest, march | Antonyms: disprove, refute, conceal, hide | verb
