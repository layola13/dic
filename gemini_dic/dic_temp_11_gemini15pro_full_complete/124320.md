motherlessnesses:/ˈmʌðərləsnɪsɪz/
n.
没有母亲的状态；失去母亲的悲惨境遇
The state of being without a mother; the condition of being orphaned of one's mother.
The child's motherlessnesses affected her deeply, leaving her with a sense of abandonment.  这个孩子的失去母亲的境遇深深地影响了她，让她感到被遗弃。
Her poems often explored themes of motherlessnesses and longing. 她的诗歌经常探讨失去母亲和渴望的主题。
Synonyms: orphanhood, bereavement, loss
Antonyms: motherhood, maternal care
