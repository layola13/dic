autodynamic:/ˌɔːtəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. |自动动态的| Relating to or characterized by self-powered movement or change.  It often refers to systems that adjust dynamically based on their own internal state or feedback. | The autodynamic system adjusted the car's suspension based on the road conditions.  自动动态系统根据路况调整了汽车的悬架。|  The robot's autodynamic navigation system allowed it to adapt to changing environments.  机器人的自动动态导航系统使其能够适应不断变化的环境。| 近义词: self-regulating, adaptive, dynamic | 反义词: static, fixed |词性:形容词
