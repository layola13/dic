happier:/ˈhæpiər/ | adj. | 更快乐的，更幸福的 | comparative form of "happy," indicating a greater degree of happiness. | She is happier now than she was last year. 她现在比去年更快乐了。|  I feel much happier after a good workout.  好的锻炼之后，我感觉快乐多了。 | 近义词: more cheerful, more joyful, more content | 反义词: sadder, more miserable, less content | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: From Middle English *happier, *happior, from Old English *happigra, comparative of *hapig (“happy, fortunate”). Cognate with Middle Low German happiger (“happier”), Dutch happiger (“happier”), German happiger (“happier”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *happīgaz, from Proto-Indo-European *kob- (“to heed, notice”).
