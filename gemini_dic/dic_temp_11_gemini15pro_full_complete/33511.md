cicatrix: /ˈsɪkətrɪks/ (US), /ˈsɪkətrɪks/ (UK)| n. |疤痕，瘢痕|A mark left on the skin or within body tissue where a wound, burn, or sore has not healed completely and fibrous connective tissue has developed.|The surgeon pointed to the faint cicatrix on her knee.  外科医生指了指她膝盖上淡淡的疤痕。|Synonyms: scar, blemish, mark|Antonyms: clear skin, unblemished skin|noun
