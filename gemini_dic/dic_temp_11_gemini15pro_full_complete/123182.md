mono-unsaturated:/ˌmɑːnoʊʌnˈsætʃəˌreɪtɪd/ /ˌmɒnəʊʌnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/| adj.|单不饱和的|Containing only one double or triple bond per molecule.  Specifically referring to fats or fatty acids having one double or triple bond per molecule.|Olive oil is rich in mono-unsaturated fats.  橄榄油富含单不饱和脂肪。|Synonyms: monounsaturated|Antonyms: saturated, polyunsaturated|adjective
