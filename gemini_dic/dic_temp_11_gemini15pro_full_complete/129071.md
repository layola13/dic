nitrogens:/ˈnaɪtrədʒənz/
noun
氮气(复数)
a colorless, odorless unreactive gas that forms about 78 percent of the earth's atmosphere.
Nitrogen is essential for plant growth. 氮对植物生长至关重要。

近义词:N2 (chemical formula)
反义词:(No direct antonym for a chemical element)
词性:noun
