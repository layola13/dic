narcissists: /ˈnɑːrsɪsɪsts/ (US) /ˈnɑːsɪsɪsts/ (UK)| n. |自恋者|A person who has an excessive admiration of themselves.  A narcissist is characterized by an inflated sense of self-importance, a need for constant attention and admiration, and a lack of empathy for others.|例句：He's such a narcissist; he only talks about himself. 他真是个自恋狂，他只谈论他自己。

例句：Many narcissists have fragile egos. 许多自恋者都有脆弱的自我。


|近义词：egotists, self-centered people|反义词: humble people, altruists|词性:noun
