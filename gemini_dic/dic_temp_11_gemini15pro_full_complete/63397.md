entozoic:/ˌɛntoʊˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
体内寄生的；肠内寄生的
Living within the body of an animal, especially in the intestines.
The entozoic amoeba can cause severe dysentery.  这种体内寄生的阿米巴原虫会导致严重的痢疾。
近义词: endoparasitic, internal
反义词: ectozoic, external
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *entos* (within) + *zōikos* (pertaining to animals or life).
