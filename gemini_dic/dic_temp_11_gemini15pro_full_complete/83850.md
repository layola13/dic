halberd:/ˈhɔːlbərd/ (US) /ˈhɔːlbɜːd/ (UK)| n. |戟|A combined spear and battle-axe, a weapon consisting of an axe blade and a spike mounted on a long shaft.|The knights charged, their halberds glinting in the sun. 骑士们冲锋陷阵，他们的戟在阳光下闪闪发光。|Example: The Swiss Guard at the Vatican still carry halberds ceremonially.  梵蒂冈的瑞士卫队仍然在仪式上携带戟。|Synonyms: poleaxe, glaive, partisan|Antonyms: N/A (Weapon, no direct opposite)|noun
