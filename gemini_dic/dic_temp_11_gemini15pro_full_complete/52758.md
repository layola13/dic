dilatative:/daɪˈleɪtətɪv/ /dɪˈleɪtətɪv/
adjective
扩张的; 膨胀的
Causing dilation; expanding or enlarging.  Relating to or characterized by dilation.
The doctor prescribed dilatative eye drops to examine the patient's retina. 医生开了扩张瞳孔的眼药水来检查病人的视网膜。
The dilatative effects of the medication caused his pupils to widen.  药物的扩张作用使他的瞳孔放大。
近义词: expansive, expanding, distending, widening
反义词: constrictive, contracting, narrowing
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *dīlātātīvus, from dīlātātus, past participle of dīlātāre (“to spread out, extend”), from dī- + lātus (“wide, broad”).
