atropous:/ˈæt.rə.pəs/| adj.|倒生的，直生的|An atropous ovule has the micropyle directed downwards and the chalaza and funiculus at the opposite end. (倒生胚珠的珠孔朝下，合点和珠柄在另一端。)|The orchid has an atropous ovule. (这株兰花有一个倒生胚珠。)|近义词: orthotropous (some contexts)|反义词: anatropous|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *ἀτροπος (átropos) "not turning, inflexible," from *ἀ- (a-) "not" + *τροπή (tropḗ) "a turning, turn."
