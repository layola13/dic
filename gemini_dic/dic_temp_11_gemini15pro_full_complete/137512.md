nontrader:/ˌnɒnˈtreɪdər/ | n. | 非交易者，非贸易商 |  A person or entity that does not engage in trading, especially in financial markets. |  Many nontraders are surprised by the volatility of the stock market. 许多非交易者对股票市场的波动感到惊讶。| The museum is a nontrader and relies on donations.  这家博物馆是非贸易机构，依靠捐赠维持运营。 | synonym: non-professional, amateur, outsider | antonym: trader, professional, investor | noun
