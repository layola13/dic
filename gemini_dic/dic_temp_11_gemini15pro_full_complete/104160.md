jungly: /ˈdʒʌŋɡli/ | adj. | 丛林般的，野生的，混乱的 | Resembling a jungle; overgrown and tangled; wild and uncivilized. |  The garden was jungly and overgrown.  花园杂草丛生，像丛林一样。| 近义词: overgrown, wild, tangled, lush, dense | 反义词: cultivated, tame, orderly, manicured | 词性: 形容词
词源: From jungle + -y.  来自jungle（丛林）+ -y（形容词后缀）。
