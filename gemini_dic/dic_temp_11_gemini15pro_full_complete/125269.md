multiexhausts:/ˈmʌltiɪɡˈzɔːsts/
noun
多排气管，多排气口
A vehicle or engine having multiple exhaust outlets.  This usually applies to performance vehicles or engines where multiple outlets are used to reduce back pressure and increase power output.
The sports car boasted dual multiexhausts, adding to its aggressive look. 这辆跑车拥有双排多排气管，增加了它的侵略性外观。
synonyms: multiple exhaust pipes, multiple exhaust outlets
antonyms: single exhaust, single exhaust pipe
n.
