implanted: /ɪmˈplɑːntɪd/ /ɪmˈplæntɪd/
Verb (past participle)| 植入的，嵌入的 | Fixed or inserted into the body surgically or by other means. | The doctor implanted a pacemaker in the patient's chest. 医生在病人的胸腔植入了一台起搏器。| embedded, inserted, ingrained | removed, extracted | v.
implanted: /ɪmˈplɑːntɪd/ /ɪmˈplæntɪd/
Adjective | 植入的，嵌入的 | Existing as a result of being implanted. | The implanted device monitors the patient's heart rate.  这个植入的装置监测病人的心率。| embedded, inserted, ingrained | removed, extracted | adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin implantare "to plant in, engraft," from in- "in" + plantare "to plant."
