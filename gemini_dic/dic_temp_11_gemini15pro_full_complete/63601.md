envy:/ˈɛnvi/ | n. vt. | 羡慕；嫉妒；令人羡慕的人或物 | A feeling of discontented or resentful longing aroused by someone else's possessions, qualities, or luck.  | She felt a pang of envy when she saw her friend's new car. 她看到朋友的新车时感到一阵嫉妒。|  He envied his brother's athletic ability. 他羡慕他哥哥的运动能力。 | 近义词: jealousy, covetousness, resentment | 反义词: admiration, generosity | 词性: 名词, 动词
