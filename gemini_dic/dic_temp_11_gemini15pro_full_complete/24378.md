brinies:/ˈbraɪniz/
noun
鹽水蝦卵，豐年蝦卵
Brine shrimp eggs, typically used as fish food.
The fish tank was stocked with newly hatched brinies.  鱼缸里放满了新孵化的丰年虾。
synonyms: brine shrimp eggs, Artemia eggs
antonyms: None (lack of a direct opposite for a specific type of fish food)
