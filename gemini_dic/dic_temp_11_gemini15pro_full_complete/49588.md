demerited: /diːˈmɛrɪtɪd/
verb (past tense)|被贬值的，被降级的|Reduced in value or merit; having lost merit.|His demerited performance led to his demotion.  他表现不佳导致他被降职。|Example: The athlete was demerited for his unsportsmanlike conduct.  这个运动员因其不运动的行为而受到处罚。|Synonyms: downgraded, demoted, devalued, penalized |Antonyms: promoted, upgraded, valued, commended|v.
