biseriate:/baɪˈsɪəriət/
adjective
双列的；排成两列的
Arranged in two rows or series.
The plant's leaves are biseriate, growing in two distinct rows along the stem.  这种植物的叶子是双列的，沿着茎长成两排。
The stomata are biseriate. 气孔呈双列排列。
近义词: two-rowed, two-ranked
反义词: uniseriate (单列的)
词性: adjective


biseriate:/baɪˈsɪəriət/
noun
双列物
Something arranged in two rows or series. (Less common usage)
This fossil reveals a biseriate arrangement of scales.  这个化石揭示了鳞片呈双列排列。
近义词: two-rowed structure, double row
反义词:  N/A (Noun form less common, therefore antonym less relevant)
词性: noun
