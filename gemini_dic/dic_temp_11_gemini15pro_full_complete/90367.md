how-so:/ˌhaʊ ˈsoʊ/ | adv. | 怎么会这样；真是如此吗 | Used to ask someone to explain something more clearly, especially when you doubt what they are saying. | "He's very intelligent, you know." "How so?" “你知道，他很聪明。” “怎么说？” | 近义词: How come?, In what way?, Really? | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with phrases like "I understand" or "That's clear.") | 词性: 副词

PHRASE->
(How-so is generally not used in phrases, it stands alone as a question.  Therefore, providing alternative phrasing using similar meanings)

1.  How is that possible?  那怎么可能？
2.  Explain yourself. 解释一下。
3.  In what way? 以什么方式？
4.  Can you elaborate? 你能详细说明吗？
5.  Give me an example. 举个例子。
