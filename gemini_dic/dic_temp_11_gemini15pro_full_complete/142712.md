otiose:/ˈoʊʃiˌoʊs/ | adj. | 冗余的，多余的，无效的 | Serving no practical purpose; useless; futile; superfluous.  | The committee considered the new regulation otiose, as it merely duplicated existing rules.  该委员会认为新规是多余的，因为它只是重复了现有的规则。 |  例句：His lengthy explanation was otiose, as everyone already understood the situation. 他冗长的解释是多余的，因为每个人都已经了解情况了。 | 近义词：superfluous, redundant, unnecessary, futile, pointless | 反义词：essential, necessary, crucial, vital, important | 词性:形容词

词源:  From Latin *otiosus* "at leisure," from *otium* "leisure."
