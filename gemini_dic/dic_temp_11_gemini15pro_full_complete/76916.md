gantries: /ˈɡæntriz/ (US), /ˈɡæntriːz/ (UK)| n. |龙门架；高架起重机；（摄影棚）天桥；（机场）登机桥|A gantry is a bridge-like overhead structure with a platform supporting equipment such as a crane, signals, or cameras.|The gantry crane lifted the heavy container onto the ship. 龙门吊将沉重的集装箱吊上了船。|Synonyms: crane, framework, scaffold, platform|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "ground level" in certain contexts)
