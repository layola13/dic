coolie: /ˈkuːli/ (US), /ˈkuːli/ (UK)| n. |苦力；劳工 (offensive)|An unskilled laborer, especially in or from the Indian subcontinent or South China, hired for low wages.  This term is now considered offensive due to its association with exploitation and forced labor.|

例句：
The coolies were forced to work long hours in harsh conditions.
苦力们被迫在恶劣的条件下长时间工作。

The term "coolie" is a derogatory term for an unskilled laborer.
“苦力”一词是对非熟练工人的贬义词。


近义词：laborer, worker, unskilled worker|
反义词: employer, boss, manager|
词性: noun
