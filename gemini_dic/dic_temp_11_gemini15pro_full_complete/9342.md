antipriesthood:/ˌæntiˈpriːsthʊd/
adjective
反神职人员的；反对神权统治的
Opposed to or hostile towards the priesthood or priestly power.
The antipriesthood movement gained momentum during the Reformation. 反神职人员运动在宗教改革时期获得了动力。
The author's antipriesthood sentiments are evident throughout the novel. 作者的反神职人员情绪在整部小说中都很明显。
Synonyms: anticlerical
Antonyms: pro-clerical
词性: adjective


Etymology:
anti- (prefix meaning "against, opposed to") + priesthood (n.)

From "anti-" + "priesthood".  "Anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of, instead of, in place of". "Priesthood" comes from Old English "prēosthād" meaning "state or condition of being a priest".
