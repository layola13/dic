anti-inflation:/ˌænti-ɪnˈfleɪʃən/ | adj. | 反通货膨胀的，抑制通货膨胀的 |  Relating to or intended to prevent or control inflation. | The government introduced new anti-inflation measures. 政府推出了新的反通货膨胀措施。 |  The central bank implemented anti-inflation policies to stabilize the economy. 中央银行实施了反通货膨胀政策以稳定经济。 | Synonyms: deflationary, counter-inflationary | Antonyms: inflationary | adjective

(rarely used) | 反通货膨胀措施（很少使用复数形式）| Actions or policies designed to combat inflation. |  The government implemented various anti-inflations to curb rising prices. (Less common usage) 政府实施了各种反通货膨胀措施来抑制物价上涨。(不太常见的用法) |  Multiple anti-inflations were proposed, but none were effective. Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix) from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of"
"inflation" from Late Latin "inflatio" meaning "a swelling, inflation," from Latin "inflare" meaning "to blow into, inflate."
