homogenous:/ˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs/| adj.|同质的，同类的，均匀的|Of the same kind or nature; consisting of similar parts or elements.|The population of the village is remarkably homogenous. 这个村庄的人口惊人地同质。|Synonyms: uniform, similar, alike, consistent|Antonyms: heterogeneous, diverse, varied|adjective

homogenous:/ˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs/| adj.|同种的，同源的|(biology) Having a common descent.|The homogenous nature of the island's flora suggests a single ancestral species. 岛上植物群的同源性表明它们来自同一个祖先物种。|Synonyms: homologous|Antonyms: heterologous|adjective
