dualing:/ˈduːəlɪŋ/
noun
决斗
a prearranged combat between two persons, fought with deadly weapons according to an accepted code of procedure, especially to settle a private quarrel.
The two men engaged in a duel at dawn. 两个男人在黎明时分进行决斗。
近义词:combat, fight
反义词:peace, reconciliation
词性:n.

Etymology:  From duel + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin duellum (“war”).
