dentalization:/ˌdɛntələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |齿音化；齿音现象|The pronunciation of a sound as a dental consonant, especially when it is not normally pronounced that way.  It occurs when the tongue makes contact with the teeth during articulation.|Example: The pronunciation of /n/ as [n̪] before /θ/ in "tenth" is an example of dentalization.  “tenth”中/n/在/θ/前发音为[n̪]是齿音化的一个例子。|Example: Dentalization of /s/ can be observed in some speakers.  在一些说话者中可以观察到/s/的齿音化。|Synonym:  dental articulation |Antonym: N/A |noun
