lay-aside:/ˈleɪ əˈsaɪd/ | n. | 储蓄; 搁置物; | Something that is saved or put away, especially money. |  She had a small lay-aside for emergencies. 她有一小笔应急储蓄。| synonyms: savings, reserve, nest egg | antonyms: expenditure, spending | noun
lay-aside:/ˈleɪ əˈsaɪd/ | v. | 搁置; 储蓄; 留出; | To put something aside, typically for later use; to save. |  He laid aside some money each month for his daughter's education. 他每个月都留出一些钱供女儿上学。| synonyms: put aside, set aside, save, reserve | antonyms: spend, use, squander | verb
