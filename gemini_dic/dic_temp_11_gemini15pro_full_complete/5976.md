ambulatory:/ˈæmbjʊlətɔːri/ (US), /ˈæmbjʊlətri/ (UK)| adj. n.|步行的，流动的；门诊病人；流动诊所|Able to walk; relating to or adapted for walking; of or relating to patients who are able to walk.|The hospital has a new ambulatory care center. 这家医院有一个新的门诊中心.|近义词：walking, mobile, perambulatory|反义词：bedridden, immobile|词性:形容词, 名词

ambulant:/ˈæmbjʊlənt/| adj.|可走动的，步行的|Able to walk about; not confined to bed.|The patient was ambulant after surgery. 手术后病人能够走动。|近义词：walking, mobile, ambulatory|反义词：bedridden, immobile|词性:形容词

ambulate:/ˈæmbjʊleɪt/| vi.|行走，移动|To walk about; move about.|The patient is encouraged to ambulate after surgery. 鼓励病人在手术后走动。|近义词：walk, move, perambulate|反义词：sit, lie down|词性:动词

词源:  From Late Latin *ambulātōrius, from Latin ambulō (“I walk”).
