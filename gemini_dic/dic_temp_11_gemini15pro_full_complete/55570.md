disturbed:/dɪˈstɜːbd/ (US), /dɪˈstɜːbd/ (UK)| adj.|不安的，心烦意乱的，精神错乱的|Feeling worried or upset, mentally ill.|例句：She was deeply disturbed by the news. 她被这个消息深深地困扰了。|近义词：upset, troubled, agitated, worried, anxious, distressed, unstable, mentally ill|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene, stable, sane|词性:形容词

disturb:/dɪˈstɜːb/ (US), /dɪˈstɜːb/ (UK)| vt.|打扰，扰乱，使不安|To interrupt, bother, or make someone feel worried or upset.|例句：I didn't want to disturb her while she was working. 我不想在她工作的时候打扰她。|近义词：interrupt, bother, disrupt, upset, worry|反义词：soothe, calm, comfort|词性:动词

词源: From Late Middle English *distourben, from Old French destourber, from Vulgar Latin *disturbāre, from Latin disturbō (“I throw into disorder”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + turbō (“I disturb, confuse”).
