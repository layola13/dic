hindsighted:/ˈhaɪndˌsaɪtɪd/| adj.|后知之明的，事后诸葛亮的|Characterized by or possessing hindsight; understanding of a situation or event only after it has happened or developed.|Looking back, it's easy to be hindsighted about the financial crisis. 回过头来看，很容易对金融危机进行事后诸葛亮的分析。|近义词：wise after the event, with hindsight, in retrospect|反义词：foresighted, prescient|词性:形容词
