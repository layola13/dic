detachments:/dɪˈtætʃmənts/
n.
分离, 分遣队, 超然
the state of being objective or aloof.  A group of soldiers, ships, etc. sent away from the main body on a special mission.  The action or process of detaching; separation.
The detachment of the placenta after childbirth is a crucial process.  胎盘在分娩后的剥离是一个至关重要的过程。
A small detachment of soldiers was sent to scout the area.  一小队士兵被派去侦察该地区。
Synonyms: separation, disconnection, objectivity, impartiality, aloofness, indifference, neutrality
Antonyms: attachment, connection, involvement, engagement, bias, partiality
