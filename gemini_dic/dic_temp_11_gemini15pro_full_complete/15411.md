bacteria:/bækˈtɪəriə/ (plural) /bækˈtɪriəm/ (singular)| n.|细菌|A member of a large group of unicellular microorganisms that have cell walls but lack organelles and an organized nucleus, including some that can cause disease.|例句：Bacteria can cause food poisoning. 细菌会导致食物中毒。

例句：The bacteria are multiplying rapidly. 这些细菌正在迅速繁殖。


|近义词：microorganism, germ, microbe|反义词:None (Bacteria don't have a direct opposite, but one might refer to the absence of bacteria as "sterile" or talk about things that kill bacteria, such as "antibiotics" or "disinfectants").|词性:noun
