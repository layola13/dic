homeopathy:/ˌhoʊmiˈɑːpəθi/ /ˌhəʊmiˈɒpəθi/| n. |顺势疗法|A system of treating diseases using very small amounts of substances that would in larger amounts produce the same symptoms as the disease being treated.|She tried homeopathy to treat her allergies. 她尝试用顺势疗法治疗她的过敏。|近义词:alternative medicine, complementary medicine|反义词:conventional medicine, allopathy|词性:noun

homoeopathy:/ˌhoʊmiˈɑːpəθi/ /ˌhəʊmiˈɒpəθi/| n. |顺势疗法|(British English spelling of homeopathy) A system of treating diseases using very small amounts of substances that would in larger amounts produce the same symptoms as the disease being treated.|He's a strong believer in homoeopathy. 他是顺势疗法的坚定信徒。|近义词:alternative medicine, complementary medicine|反义词:conventional medicine, allopathy|词性:noun
