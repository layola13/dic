enigmatize:/ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪzaɪz/ | v. | 使迷惑；使成谜 | To make something puzzling or obscure; to express something in a way that is difficult to understand. | The author deliberately enigmatized the ending of the novel, leaving readers to speculate.  作者故意把小说的结局写得很神秘，让读者去猜测。 | The detective tried to enigmatize the clues to mislead the criminal.  侦探试图把线索弄得扑朔迷离以误导罪犯。 | 近义词: mystify, puzzle, obscure, bewilder | 反义词: clarify, explain, elucidate | 词性: 动词

词源:  From enigma + -ize. Enigma comes from the Greek αἴνιγμα (aínigma), meaning "riddle."
