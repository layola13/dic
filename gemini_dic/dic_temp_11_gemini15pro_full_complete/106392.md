kook:/kʊk/ | n. |怪人；疯子；行为古怪的人|A person regarded as strange, eccentric, or crazy. |例句：He's a bit of a kook, but he's harmless. 他有点怪，但 harmless。

例句：She's become a real health kook, eating only organic food. 她变成了一个真正的健康狂，只吃有机食品。|近义词：eccentric, crank, oddball, weirdo|反义词：conformist, normal person|词性: 名词
