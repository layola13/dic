antipestilential:/ˌæntiˌpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəl/
adj.
防瘟疫的，抗瘟疫的
Preventing or counteracting pestilence or plague.
The doctor prescribed an antipestilential potion. 医生开了一种防瘟疫的药水。
近义词:antiplague, prophylactic
反义词:pestilential, infectious
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From anti- "against" + pestilential (from Late Latin pestilentialis, from Latin pestilentia "plague").
