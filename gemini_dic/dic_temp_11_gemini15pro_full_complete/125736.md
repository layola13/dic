muntin:/ˈmʌntɪn/| n. |竖框，窗棂，灯格|A strip of wood or metal separating and holding panes of glass in a window.|The muntins in the old window were intricately carved.  这扇旧窗户上的竖框雕刻精美。|近义词:glazing bar, sash bar|反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym, perhaps "pane" as the opposite element)|词性: Noun


muntin:/ˈmʌntɪn/| n. |竖框，窗棂，灯格|A vertical or horizontal bar that separates and holds panes of glass in a window or door.|The craftsman carefully replaced the broken muntin in the antique door.  工匠小心地更换了古董门上破损的竖框。|近义词:mullion (often used interchangeably, though mullions are typically heavier and more structural)|反义词: None|词性: Noun
