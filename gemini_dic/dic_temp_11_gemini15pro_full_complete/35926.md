cocultivations:/ˌkoʊˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃənz/
n.
共同培养，共培养，协同栽培
The process of growing two or more different types of organisms together in a shared environment.
Cocultivations of bacteria and fungi can lead to the discovery of novel antibiotics.  细菌和真菌的共同培养可以导致发现新的抗生素。
Cocultivations of different cell types are often used in biological research. 不同细胞类型的共培养常用于生物学研究。
近义词:cocultures
反义词: monoculture
词性: 名词 (plural)
