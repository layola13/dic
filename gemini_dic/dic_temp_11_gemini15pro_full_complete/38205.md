compulsory:/kəmˈpʌlsəri/ | adj. | 必须的，强制的，义务的 | Required by law or a rule; obligatory. |  Attendance at school is compulsory for children of certain ages.  某些年龄段的儿童必须上学。 |  mandatory, obligatory, required | optional, voluntary | adjective

compulsory:/kəmˈpʌlsəri/ | adj. | 必修的 | Forming a required part of a course of study. |  compulsory subjects like math and English  必修科目如数学和英语 | required, mandatory | optional, elective | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compulsorius, from Latin compulsus, past participle of compellere (“to compel, force, drive”). Equivalent to compel +‎ -sory.
