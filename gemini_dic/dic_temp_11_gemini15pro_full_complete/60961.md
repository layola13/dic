elfins:/ˈɛlfɪnz/| adj. n.|小精灵的，小精灵|Of or relating to elves; resembling an elf; delicate and otherworldly.|The child had an elfin face with large, curious eyes. 这个孩子有一张精灵般的脸，长着大而好奇的眼睛。|The elfin creatures danced in the moonlight. 小精灵般的生物在月光下跳舞。|近义词：fairy-like, pixie-like, delicate, ethereal|反义词：coarse, rough, earthly|词性:形容词, 名词

词源:  From elf + -in, diminutive suffix. Ultimately from Old English ælf.
