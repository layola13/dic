desirability:/dɪˌzaɪərəˈbɪləti/
n.
 desirability
the quality of being desirable; attractiveness
The desirability of the location made the property very expensive.  这个位置的吸引力使得房产非常昂贵。
近义词: attractiveness, appeal, allure
反义词: undesirability, unattractiveness, repulsion
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin *desiderabilitat- , from desiderabilis ‘desirable’.
