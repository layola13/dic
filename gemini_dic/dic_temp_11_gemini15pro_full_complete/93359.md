illegibility:/ɪˌlɛdʒɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
不可读性，难以辨认
the quality of being difficult or impossible to read.
Her handwriting's illegibility makes it hard to grade her tests.  她的笔迹难以辨认，很难给她的试卷评分。
Near synonyms: unreadable, indecipherable
Antonyms: legibility, readability, clarity
noun

illegible:/ɪˈlɛdʒɪbəl/
adj.
难以辨认的，不可读的
difficult or impossible to read.
The doctor's handwriting was so illegible that the pharmacist had to call him to verify the prescription.  医生的笔迹难以辨认，药剂师不得不打电话给他确认处方。
Near synonyms: unreadable, indecipherable, scribbled
Antonyms: legible, readable, clear
adjective
