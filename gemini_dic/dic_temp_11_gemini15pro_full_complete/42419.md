countercries:/ˈkaʊntərkraɪz/
n.
反对的呼声，反驳的喊叫
Shouts or cries made in response to other cries, often in opposition or defiance.
The crowd's countercries drowned out the speaker.  人群的反对声淹没了演讲者。
The protesters met the police with angry countercries. 抗议者用愤怒的喊叫回应警察。
Synonyms:  retorts, rejoinders, responses, ripostes
Antonyms: agreements, approvals, assents
noun
