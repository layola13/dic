daydreamy: /ˈdeɪdriːmi/ | adj. | 恍惚的，爱幻想的，梦幻的 | Given to daydreaming; dreamy.  Characterized by or suggestive of a daydream; fanciful. | She had a daydreamy look in her eyes. 她眼神里带着梦幻般的神情。|  He's always been a bit daydreamy. 他总是有点儿爱幻想。 | 近义词: dreamy, fanciful, whimsical, absent-minded | 反义词: realistic, practical, down-to-earth | 词性: adjective | 词源: From daydream + -y.
