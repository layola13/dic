half-dressed:/ˌhæf ˈdrest/ | adj. | 半裸的，衣衫不整的 | Partially clothed; not fully dressed. |  She answered the door half-dressed, clearly having just woken up. 她衣衫不整地开了门，显然是刚醒。|  partially clothed, scantily clad, underdressed | fully dressed, properly dressed | adjective

Etymology:
"half" (Old English healf) + "dressed" (past participle of "dress," from Old French dresser)
