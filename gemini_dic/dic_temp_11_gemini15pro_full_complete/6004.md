ameba:/əˈmiːbə/ (US), /əˈmiːbə/ (UK)| n. |变形虫，阿米巴|A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.  A microscopic organism belonging to the rhizopodan order Amoebida.|The ameba is a simple, single-celled organism. 变形虫是一种简单的单细胞生物。| synonym: amoeba, protozoan | antonym: multicellular organism | noun

ameba:/əˈmiːbə/ (US), /əˈmiːbə/ (UK)| n. |(比喻)不断变化的事物|Something that changes shape or form frequently.|The city is an ameba, constantly growing and changing. 这座城市就像变形虫，不断发展变化。| synonym:  protean, mutable | antonym: static, fixed | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *amoeba, from Greek amoibē, meaning "change, alteration."
