basin:/ˈbeɪsn/| n.|盆；盆地；流域|A basin is a large or deep bowl-shaped container used for holding liquids, typically water, or a natural depression in the earth's surface, typically containing water.|例句：She filled the basin with warm water. 她把盆里装满了温水。

|例句：The Amazon basin is the largest in the world. 亚马逊盆地是世界上最大的盆地。|近义词：bowl, sink, container, depression|反义词:peak, summit, hill|noun
