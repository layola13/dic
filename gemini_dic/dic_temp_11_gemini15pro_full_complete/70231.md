fickleness:/ˈfɪk(ə)lnəs/
n.
反复无常，变化无常
The fickleness of the weather made it difficult to plan a picnic.
天气变化无常，很难计划野餐。
The fickleness of public opinion can be frustrating for politicians.
公众舆论的变化无常可能会让政客们感到沮丧。
capriciousness, volatility, instability, variability
constancy, steadfastness, stability
noun
