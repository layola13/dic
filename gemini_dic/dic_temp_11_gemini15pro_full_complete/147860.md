overrating:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 高估 | The act of estimating something or someone to be better, more important, or more valuable than they actually are. |  Many critics believe the movie was overrated. 许多评论家认为这部电影被高估了。| overestimation, overvaluing | undervaluing, underestimating | noun

overrate:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪt/ | vt. | 高估 | To have too high an opinion of (someone or something); to overestimate. | Don't overrate your abilities; you're not invincible.  不要高估自己的能力；你不是无敌的。 | overestimate, overvalue | underestimate, undervalue | verb
