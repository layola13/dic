ETYMOLOGY->
From "hydrologic" + "-ally".
"Hydrologic" comes from "hydro-" (water) + "-logic" (study of).
"Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" (water).
"-logic" comes from Greek "-logia" (study).
"-ally" forms adverbs from adjectives.
