overfertilization: /ˌoʊvərˌfɜːrtələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度施肥 | The excessive application of fertilizers to soil, resulting in negative environmental impacts. |  Excessive nitrogen from overfertilization can leach into waterways. 过度施肥产生的过量氮会渗入水道。 | 近义词: overmanuring | 反义词: underfertilization | 词性: noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "fertilize" (from French fertiliser, from Late Latin fertilisare "make fruitful") + "-ation" (suffix forming nouns of action or process).
