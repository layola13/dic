overpreoccupation: /ˌoʊvərpriːˌɑːkjəˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度关注，过分专注 | An excessive absorption or fixation on a particular subject, thought, or feeling, often to the detriment of other aspects of life. |  He showed an overpreoccupation with his health. 他过分关注自己的健康。| synonyms: obsession, fixation, preoccupation | antonyms: indifference, apathy, detachment | noun

overpreoccupation: /ˌoʊvərpriːˌɑːkjəˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 过分担忧，过度忧虑 |  An excessive and often irrational worry or anxiety about something. | Her overpreoccupation with work led to burnout. 她对工作的过度担忧导致了倦怠。| synonyms: anxiety, worry, solicitude | antonyms: calmness, tranquility, serenity | noun
