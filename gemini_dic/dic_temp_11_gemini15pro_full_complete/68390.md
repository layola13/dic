fallowness:/ˈfæləʊnəs/| n. |休耕；闲置；荒芜|The state of being fallow; uncultivated or inactive.|The fallowness of the fields allowed the soil to regenerate. 田地的休耕使土壤得以再生。|Near Synonyms: inactivity, idleness, dormancy, barrenness|Antonyms: activity, fertility, productiveness|noun


fallow:/ˈfæləʊ/| adj. n. v. |休耕的；闲置的；荒芜的；休耕地；休耕；使休耕|Left unplanted or uncultivated for a period in order to restore fertility; inactive.|The farmer left the field fallow for a year. 农民让这块地休耕一年。|Near Synonyms: (adj.) uncultivated, idle, dormant; (n.) wasteland, barren land; (v.) rest, idle|Antonyms: (adj.) cultivated, active, fertile; (v.) cultivate, plant|adjective, noun, verb
