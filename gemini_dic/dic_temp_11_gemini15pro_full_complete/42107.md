cottagely: /ˈkɒtɪdʒli/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtɪdʒli/ (US)
adjective
小屋似的，乡村式的; 朴素的
Resembling or suggestive of a cottage; rustic and simple.
The room had a cottagely charm, with its exposed beams and floral curtains. 房间有着小屋般的魅力，裸露的横梁和花卉窗帘。
She wore a cottagely dress, perfect for a summer picnic. 她穿着一件乡村风格的连衣裙，非常适合夏季野餐。
近义词: rustic, simple, homely, quaint
反义词: modern, sophisticated, urban
