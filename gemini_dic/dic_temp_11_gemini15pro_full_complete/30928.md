chaired:/tʃɛərd/| v. |担任主席；主持|acted as chairperson of a meeting or other gathering. |例句：She chaired the meeting effectively. 她有效地主持了会议。

例句：The committee was chaired by a renowned scientist. 该委员会由一位著名科学家担任主席。|近义词：presided over, led, moderated, directed, officiated |反义词:attended, participated |词性:动词

词源: From the noun "chair" (meaning "seat of authority") + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  The noun "chair" comes from Old French "chaiere," ultimately from Latin "cathedra."
