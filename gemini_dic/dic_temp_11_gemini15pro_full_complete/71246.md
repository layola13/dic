fitweed:/ˈfɪtwiːd/
n.
fitweed; fumewort; Scoparia dulcis
A tropical American plant (Scoparia dulcis) of the figwort family, having small white flowers and a sweet, slightly bitter taste. It is used in traditional medicine.
Fitweed is a common weed found in tropical regions.  灯笼草是一种常见于热带地区的杂草。
Fitweed is used in some cultures for its medicinal properties.  在一些文化中，灯笼草因其药用价值而被使用。
Synonyms: sweet broom, licorice weed
Antonyms: N/A
