avoidably:/əˈvɔɪdəbli/
adjective
可避免地
Capable of being avoided.
It was an avoidably awkward situation. 这是一个本来可以避免的尴尬局面。
近义词:preventable, escapable
反义词:inevitable, unavoidable
词性:adjective

词源:
From avoid + -able + -ly.  "Avoid" comes from Old French "esvuidier" meaning "to empty out, get rid of."  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being." "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
