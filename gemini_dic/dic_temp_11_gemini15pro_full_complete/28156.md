caplin:/ˈkæplɪn/| n. |毛鳞鱼|A small fish of the smelt family, found in large shoals in the North Atlantic and Arctic oceans. It is an important food source for many larger fish and seabirds. |Caplin are a vital part of the ecosystem. 毛鳞鱼是生态系统的重要组成部分。

Caplin are often dried and used as fertilizer. 毛鳞鱼常被晒干用作肥料。|近义词：sprat, smelt|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species)|noun
