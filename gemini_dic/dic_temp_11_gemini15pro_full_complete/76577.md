gallican:/ɡəˈlɪkən/ | adj. |高卢的，法国的，法国教会的 | Of or relating to Gaul or France, especially to the historical French Church and its assertion of certain liberties from papal authority. | The Gallican Church historically maintained a degree of independence from Rome. 法国教会历史上保持着一定程度的独立于罗马教廷。| The king implemented Gallican liberties to restrict papal influence. 国王实行高卢自由以限制教皇的影响力。 | 近义词: French, Frankish | 反义词: Roman, papal | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Gallicanus*, from Latin *Gallicus* "of Gaul," from *Gallia* "Gaul," from Gaulish *Gallia*, probably meaning "power" or "strength".
