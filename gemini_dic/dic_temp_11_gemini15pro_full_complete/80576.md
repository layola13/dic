goofball:/ˈɡuːfbɔːl/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋，搞笑的人|A goofball is a silly or foolish person, often in a funny or endearing way.  They are known for their clumsiness, eccentricity, or unconventional behavior.|He's such a goofball, always making us laugh. 他真是个傻瓜，总是逗我们笑。|近义词：clown, klutz, dork, goof, silly person|反义词：serious person, genius|词性: 名词

goofball:/ˈɡuːfbɔːl/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的|Used informally to describe something silly or foolish.|That was a goofball mistake. 那真是个愚蠢的错误。|近义词：silly, foolish, goofy|反义词：serious, sensible|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From *goof* (meaning a foolish or silly person) + *ball* (possibly influenced by words like "screwball"). The term likely originated in American slang in the mid-20th century.
