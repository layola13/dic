brunetto:/bruˈnɛtoʊ/ | adj. | 栗色的，深褐色的 |  Italian origin, referring to a dark brown or brownish color, typically of hair.  Used mainly to describe a person's hair color as dark brown. | Example: He had thick, brunetto hair. 他有一头浓密的深褐色头发。 | Example:  The brunetto stallion galloped across the field.  那匹深褐色的种马飞奔过田野。 |Synonym:  brown, dark brown, chestnut | Antonym: blond, light, fair | Adjective
