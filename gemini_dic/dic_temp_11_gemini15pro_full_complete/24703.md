brontograph:/ˈbrɒntəɡrɑːf/ | n. | 雷电记录仪；雷暴记录器 | An instrument for recording the phenomena of thunderstorms, especially the time of occurrence, duration, and intensity of thunder. |  The museum displayed a vintage brontograph, showcasing early attempts to understand thunderstorms.  博物馆展出了一台老式雷电记录仪，展示了早期人们试图了解雷暴的努力。| 近义词: ceraunograph | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
Etymology: From Greek *brontē* (thunder) + *-graph* (write, record).
