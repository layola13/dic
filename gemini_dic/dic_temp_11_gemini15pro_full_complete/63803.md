epidemiologists: /ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒlədʒɪsts/ (UK) /ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɑːlədʒɪsts/ (US) | n. | 流行病学家 | Scientists who study the patterns, causes, and effects of health and disease conditions in defined populations. | Epidemiologists are working to understand the spread of the new virus. 流行病学家正在努力了解这种新病毒的传播。| Epidemiologists tracked the source of the food poisoning outbreak. 流行病学家追踪了食物中毒爆发的源头。 |  Synonyms: disease detectives, public health researchers | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
