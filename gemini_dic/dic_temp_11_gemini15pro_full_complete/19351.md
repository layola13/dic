bibliophiles:/ˈbɪblɪəˌfaɪlz/ | n. | 藏书家；爱书的人 | A person who collects or has a great love of books. |  Example 1: My uncle is a true bibliophile, his house is overflowing with books. 我叔叔是一个真正的藏书家，他的房子里堆满了书。

Example 2:  Bibliophiles often spend hours browsing in secondhand bookstores. 爱书的人经常花几个小时在二手书店里浏览。 | 近义词: bookworm, book lover, bibliomaniac | 反义词:  anti-intellectual, philistine | 词性: noun
