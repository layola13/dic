kippers:/ˈkɪpəz/| n.|熏鱼，腌鱼，通常指鲱鱼|A whole kipper, split open like a butterfly, grilled over a gentle heat.|一整条像蝴蝶一样剖开的熏鱼，用文火烤。|近义词：smoked fish, bloaters (specifically smoked herring)|反义词:fresh fish|noun

kipper:/ˈkɪpər/| n.|熏鱼，腌鱼，通常指鲱鱼|I had kippers for breakfast.|我早餐吃了熏鱼。|近义词：smoked fish, bloaters (specifically smoked herring)|反义词:fresh fish|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kipper*, perhaps from *kippe* (“small pointed hill, peak, jutting point”), referring to the pointed belly of a male herring ready to spawn.  The male herring develops a pointed belly during the breeding season. These fish were traditionally caught and then kippered.
