multilocation:/ˌmʌltiˈloʊˈkeɪʃən/ |adj. n.|多地点的，多位置的；多分支机构|Having or occurring in multiple locations. |Our company has a multilocation presence, with offices in New York, London, and Tokyo. 我们的公司在多个地点设有分支机构，在纽约、伦敦和东京都设有办事处。|multi-site, multiple locations| single location| adjective, noun
Etymology: multi- (prefix meaning "many") + location (noun) 词源：multi-（前缀，意为“许多”）+ location（名词）
