noncoalescences:/ˌnɑːnkoʊəˈlɛsənsɪz/ | n. | 非聚结，不合并 | The process or state of not coalescing; failure to merge or combine. | The noncoalescences of the small droplets led to a stable emulsion. 小液滴的不合并导致形成了稳定的乳液。 | The noncoalescences of the oil droplets prevented the formation of a single layer. 油滴的不合并阻止了单层的形成。

| 近义词: nonunion, separation, disjunction | 反义词: coalescence, merger, fusion | 词性: 名词
