faddishnesses:/ˈfædɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
时尚癖; 一时的狂热
The tendency to follow fads; the state of being faddish.  A collection or manifestation of fads.
Her faddishnesses extended from clothes to diets to spiritual gurus. 她对时尚的狂热从服装延伸到饮食，再到精神导师。
Synonyms: trendiness, vogue, craze, fashionableness
Antonyms: classicism, timelessness, permanence
