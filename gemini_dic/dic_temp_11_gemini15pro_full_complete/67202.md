expressionistically:/ɪkˌspreʃənˈɪstɪkli/
adv.|表现主义地|In a way that uses strong emotion rather than precise representation.  用强烈的情感而不是精确的描绘来表达的方式。|例句：The painting was rendered expressionistically, with bold colors and distorted figures. 这幅画是用表现主义手法绘制的，色彩大胆，人物扭曲。|近义词：emotionally, intensely, dramatically|反义词：realistically, objectively, accurately|词性: 副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From expressionism (n.) + -istically (adv. suffix).  来自表现主义 (名词) + -istically (副词后缀)。
