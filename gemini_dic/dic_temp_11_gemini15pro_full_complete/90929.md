hurdy-gurdy:/ˈhɜːrdi ˌɡɜːrdi/| n.|手摇风琴，轮擦提琴|A stringed musical instrument played by turning a crank that rubs against the strings.|The hurdy-gurdy man played a lively tune. 手摇风琴演奏者演奏了一首轻快的曲子。|近义词：barrel organ, street organ|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑其他乐器如piano 钢琴, violin 小提琴)|名词
