hibernacle:/ˈhaɪbərnækl/ |n.|冬眠处，藏身处|A place of shelter or refuge during the winter, especially for animals; a winter dwelling.  |The bear retreated to its hibernacle for the winter. 熊回到了它的冬眠处过冬。|Synonyms: den, lair, burrow, shelter|Antonyms: exposure|noun

hibernacle:/ˈhaɪbərnækl/ |n.|教堂，圣所，神龛|A place of worship or a sacred place. (Often capitalized when referring to the tabernacle in the Bible.)|The pilgrims visited the Hibernacle to pray. 朝圣者们前往圣所祈祷。|Synonyms: sanctuary, shrine, temple, church|Antonyms:  profane place|noun
