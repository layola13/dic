nonincriminatory:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkrɪmɪnətɔːri/
adjective
无罪的；不构成犯罪的；不牵连犯罪的
Not incriminating; not tending to establish guilt.
His lawyer advised him to make a nonincriminatory statement to the police. 他的律师建议他对警方做一份无罪的陈述。

The witness provided nonincriminatory testimony. 证人提供了不构成犯罪的证词。

近义词:exculpatory, absolving, vindicating
反义词:incriminatory, accusatory, implicating
词性:adjective
