nickeling:/ˈnɪkəlɪŋ/| n. |少量收费；斤斤计较; 用镍处理|The act of charging small amounts of money, often perceived as excessive or petty. Also, the process of plating with nickel.|

例句：
The constant nickeling and diming of customers by the airline led to many complaints.
这家航空公司不断地向顾客收取小额费用，导致了很多投诉。

例句：
The company specializes in the nickeling of metal components for increased durability.
这家公司专门从事金属部件的镀镍处理，以提高耐用性。


近义词：penny-pinching, chiseling, nickel-and-diming|
反义词: generosity, extravagance|
词性: noun|


nickeling:/ˈnɪkəlɪŋ/| v. |少量收费；斤斤计较; 用镍处理|To charge small amounts of money, often perceived as excessive or petty. Also, to plate with nickel.|

例句：
They were nickeling us for every little extra, like baggage fees and seat selection.
他们对我们收取各种小额额外费用，比如行李费和选座费。

例句：
The factory is nickeling the steel parts to prevent rust.
工厂正在给钢制零件镀镍以防止生锈。


近义词：penny-pinching, chiseling, nickel-and-diming|
反义词: being generous, splurging|
词性: verb (gerund or present participle)|
