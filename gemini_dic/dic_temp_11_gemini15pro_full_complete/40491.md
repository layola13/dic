contriteness:/kənˈtraɪtnəs/ /kənˈtraɪtnɪs/
|n.|痛悔；悔悟|The state of feeling or expressing remorse or penitence; repentance.|He expressed contrition for his sins. 他对自己的罪孽表示忏悔。|
近义词: remorse, penitence, repentance, regret, shame, compunction
|反义词: impenitence, unrepentance, indifference, apathy, remorselessness
|词性:noun
|词源: From Middle English *contritnes, from Old French contrite + -ness, from Latin contrītus, past participle of conterere (“to grind, bruise, rub together, wear away”), from com- (“together”) + terere (“to rub”).
