dowagerly:/ˈdaʊədʒərli/| adj.|像寡妇的；老年贵妇的；一本正经的|Behaving in a way that is like a dowager, especially in being dignified but also severe or old-fashioned.  Having the appearance or demeanor of a dowager; often used to describe a woman who is elderly, wealthy, and somewhat imperious.|She swept into the room with a dowagerly air, surveying everyone with a critical eye. 她带着一副老贵妇人的气势走进房间，用挑剔的目光打量着每个人。|Synonyms: matronly, dignified, stately, imperious, haughty|Antonyms: youthful, girlish, humble, unassuming|词性:形容词

dowager:/ˈdaʊədʒər/| n.|寡妇；遗孀（尤指继承亡夫财产的）；受人尊敬的老年贵妇人|A widow who holds a title or property derived from her late husband.  A dignified elderly woman.|The dowager queen still held considerable influence in the court.  太后在朝廷中仍然拥有相当大的影响力。|Synonyms: widow, matron|Antonyms: bachelor, spinster|词性:名词
