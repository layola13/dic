hobblingly:/ˈhɒblɪŋli/ (US) /ˈhɑːblɪŋli/| adv. |一瘸一拐地，蹒跚地|In a hobbling manner; with a limp.  It describes the way someone moves when they are injured or have difficulty walking.|例句：He walked hobblingly down the street after twisting his ankle.  他扭伤了脚踝，一瘸一拐地走在街上。

例句：The old dog moved hobblingly across the yard.  老狗一瘸一拐地穿过院子。


近义词：haltingly, limpingly, awkwardly, falteringly|反义词: smoothly, gracefully, easily, effortlessly |词性: adverb
