bleated:/ˈbliːtɪd/ | v. | 咩咩叫；(人)发出微弱的声音；抱怨 |The lamb bleated pitifully. 小羊羔可怜地咩咩叫。|例句：The lost sheep bleated for its mother.  迷路的小羊咩咩地叫着找妈妈。

例句：He bleated about the unfairness of it all. 他抱怨这一切太不公平了。|近义词：baaed, cried, whimpered, complained |反义词: roared, shouted |词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *bleten, from Old English *blǣtan (“to bleat”), from Proto-Germanic *blētąną (“to bleat”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰléh₁- (“to blow, bleat”). Cognate with Scots bleit (“to bleat”), Saterland Frisian blöägje (“to bleat”), West Frisian bliêgje (“to bleat”), Dutch blaten (“to bleat”), German blöken (“to bleat”), Swedish bläka (“to bleat”), Icelandic blekja (“to bleat”).
