illinois:/ˌɪləˈnɔɪ/| n. |伊利诺伊州|A Midwestern state in the United States.  It borders Wisconsin to the north, Indiana to the east, Kentucky to the southeast, Missouri to the southwest, and Iowa to the west. Its capital is Springfield. Chicago is its largest city.|I grew up in Illinois. 我在伊利诺伊州长大。|Synonym: IL (abbreviation), Prairie State (nickname)|Antonym: N/A|Noun
