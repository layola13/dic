interinfluencing:/ˌɪntərˈɪnfluənsɪŋ/
adjective
相互影响的
exerting influence on each other.
例句：The interinfluencing factors of climate and vegetation are complex. 气候和植被的相互影响因素很复杂。

例句：The two companies have an interinfluencing relationship, each impacting the other's market share.  这两家公司有着相互影响的关系，各自影响着对方的市场份额。

近义词：interacting, interdependent, interconnected
反义词：independent, separate, unrelated
词性: adjective

词源:  "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "influence" + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  The word "influence" comes from Old French "influence," from Late Latin "influentia" ("flowing in"), from Latin "influere" ("to flow into"), from "in-" ("in, into") + "fluere" ("to flow").
