daggy:/ˈdæɡi/ | adj. |邋遢的，过时的，土气的 |Unfashionable, outdated, or shabby in appearance.  Characterized by clothes or accessories that are out of style or worn-out.  Can also refer to a person who is socially awkward or uncool. |例句：His daggy old sweater had holes in the elbows.  他那件邋遢的旧毛衣肘部有洞。


例句：She thought his haircut was a bit daggy. 她觉得他的发型有点土。


近义词：unfashionable, outdated, shabby, frumpy, dorky, nerdy |反义词：stylish, fashionable, trendy, chic, cool |词性: Adjective
