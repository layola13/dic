ahh:/ɑː/ | int. | 啊; 呀; 哎哟 | An exclamation expressing a range of emotions, from pleasure to pain. | Example: Ahh, that feels good.  啊，感觉真好。| Example: Ahh, I stubbed my toe! 啊，我的脚趾撞到了！| 近义词: oh, ouch, ah | 反义词: (Interjections typically don't have antonyms) | 词性: interjection
