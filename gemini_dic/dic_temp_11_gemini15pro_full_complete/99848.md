intermixt:/ˌɪntərˈmɪkst/| adj. v.|混合的，混杂的；混合，混杂|Mixed together; blended.|The poem intermixes humor and pathos. 这首诗将幽默和伤感交织在一起。|The artist intermixed various colors to create a unique shade.  这位艺术家混合各种颜色以创造一种独特的色调。|近义词：mix, blend, mingle, combine, intermingle|反义词：separate, divide, sort|词性:形容词/动词
词源: From Middle English *intermyxt, from Latin intermixtus, past participle of intermiscere (“to mix between, intermix”), from inter (“between”) + miscere (“to mix”).
