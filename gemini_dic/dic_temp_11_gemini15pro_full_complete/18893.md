beslaver:/bɪˈsleɪvər/| n.|谄媚者；奴颜婢膝的人|A person who beslavers; a sycophant.|The king was surrounded by beslavers. 国王身边都是些阿谀奉承的人。|近义词：sycophant, flatterer, toady|反义词：critic, detractor|noun
