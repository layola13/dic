midtowns:/ˈmɪdtaʊnz/| n.|市中心区，中心商业区|The area of a city that is between the central business district and the suburbs.  It often contains a mix of residential, commercial, and entertainment venues.|Many young professionals choose to live in midtowns because of their vibrant atmosphere and proximity to work and leisure activities. 许多年轻的专业人士选择住在市中心，因为那里充满活力，而且靠近工作和休闲场所。|近义词：city centers, downtown areas|反义词: suburbs, outskirts|noun
