edacious:/ɪˈdeɪʃəs/
adjective
贪吃的；狼吞虎咽的
Habitually eating to excess; voracious.
The edacious boy ate all the cookies in the jar. 这个贪吃的男孩把罐子里的所有饼干都吃光了。
Synonyms: ravenous, voracious, gluttonous, greedy
Antonyms: abstemious, temperate, moderate
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin edax, edac- (gluttonous, devouring) from edere (to eat).
