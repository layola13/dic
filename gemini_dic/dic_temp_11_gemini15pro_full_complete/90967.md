hurried:/ˈhɜːrid/ (US) /ˈhʌrid/ (UK)| v. |催促（hurry的过去式和过去分词）|to urge or cause (someone) to do something more quickly|  He hurried her into the car. 他催促她上车。| synonyms: rushed, hastened, sped up | antonyms: delayed, slowed down | verb
