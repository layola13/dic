miosis:/maɪˈoʊsɪs/ /miˈəʊsɪs/| n. |瞳孔缩小，缩瞳|Constriction of the pupil of the eye. |例句：The doctor observed miosis in the patient's eyes after administering the medication. 医生给病人用药后观察到病人瞳孔缩小。

例句：Miosis is a common side effect of certain opioid medications. 缩瞳是某些阿片类药物的常见副作用。|近义词：pupillary constriction|反义词: mydriasis (瞳孔散大)|词性:noun
