colliding:/kəˈlaɪdɪŋ/
v.|碰撞，冲突，抵触|The two cars collided at the intersection. 两辆车在十字路口相撞。|
colliding:/kəˈlaɪdɪŋ/
adj.|碰撞的，冲突的|Their interests are colliding. 他们的利益相冲突。|
colliding:/kəˈlaɪdɪŋ/
词源: From the Latin "collidere," meaning "to strike together," from "com-" (together) + "laedere" (to strike).|
colliding:/kəˈlaɪdɪŋ/
近义词:crashing, clashing, bumping, impacting|
colliding:/kəˈlaɪdɪŋ/
反义词:avoiding, separating, diverging|
colliding:/kəˈlaɪdɪŋ/
词性: present participle of "collide"
