healthfully:/ˈhɛlθfəli/| adv.|健康地；有益健康地|In a way that promotes good health.  She eats healthfully and exercises regularly.|例句：She lives healthfully, eating plenty of fruits and vegetables. 她生活得很健康，吃很多水果和蔬菜。

例句：He tries to eat healthfully, avoiding processed foods. 他尽量吃得健康，避免加工食品。|近义词：healthily, wholesomely|反义词：unhealthily, poorly|词性:副词
词源: From health + -ful + -ly.  Ultimately from Old English *hǣlþ.
