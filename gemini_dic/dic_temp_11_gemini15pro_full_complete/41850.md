corymbosely:/ˌkɔːrɪmˈboʊsli/ | adv. | 以伞房花序状地 | In a corymbose manner; arranged in corymbs.  | The flowers were arranged corymbosely at the top of the stem.  花朵以伞房花序状排列在茎的顶部。| 近义词: cymosely | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps racemosely, paniculately depending on the specific contrasting inflorescence type) | 词性: adverb

corymb:/ˈkɔːrɪmb/ | n. | 傘房花序 | A flat-topped or convex indeterminate inflorescence in which the outer flowers open first. | The yarrow plant has a corymb of white flowers.  蓍草植物有一个白色的伞房花序。 | 近义词: cyme | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could be raceme, panicle, etc. depending on the contrasting inflorescence) | 词性: noun
