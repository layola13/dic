overreward:/ˌōvər-rɪˈwɔːrd/ |v.| 过度奖励；奖励过高 | To give someone a reward that is greater than they deserve or that is more than necessary to achieve the desired effect. | The company overrewarded its executives with exorbitant bonuses.  这家公司过度奖励其高管，给予了过高的奖金。|Synonyms: overcompensate, overpay | Antonyms: underreward, underpay | Verb

overreward:/ˌōvər-rɪˈwɔːrd/ |n.| 过度的奖励；过高的报酬 | A reward that is greater than deserved or necessary. | The overreward created resentment among the other employees.  过高的奖励引起了其他员工的不满。|Synonyms: excessive reward, overpayment | Antonyms: inadequate reward, underpayment | Noun
