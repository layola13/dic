alohas:/əˈloʊhɑːz/ | n. |再见，祝福 | A Hawaiian word used to express affection, hello, or goodbye. It carries a deeper meaning than a simple greeting, encompassing love, peace, and compassion. |  Example 1:  We exchanged alohas as we parted ways. 我们分别时互相道别(祝福)。

Example 2: She sent her alohas to her family back home. 她向家乡的家人送去祝福。 | Synonym: farewell, goodbye, greetings | Antonym: hello (in the sense of greeting) | noun
