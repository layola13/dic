joyed: /dʒɔɪd/
adjective
高兴的，快乐的
Feeling or expressing great happiness.
She was overjoyed at the news of her son's graduation. 她听到儿子毕业的消息欣喜若狂。
近义词: delighted, elated, thrilled, jubilant
反义词: sad, sorrowful, unhappy, miserable
词性: adjective

词源: From joy + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives from nouns, indicating "having, characterized by, or filled with."  "Joy" itself comes from Old French "joie," ultimately from Latin "gaudia," plural of "gaudium" meaning "joy, delight."
