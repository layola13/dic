overindividualisms:/ˌoʊvərˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪzəmz/
n.|过度个人主义|Excessive emphasis on individual rights and needs, often at the expense of the community or collective good.|The rise of overindividualisms in modern society has led to a decline in social cohesion.|近义词:egoism, narcissism, self-centeredness|反义词:collectivism, altruism|词性:名词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "individualism" (from "individual" + "-ism")
