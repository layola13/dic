mistitled:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪt(ə)ld/
adjective
错误命名的；标题不当的
incorrectly or inappropriately titled
The book was mistitled, leading to confusion among readers. 这本书的标题不当，导致读者感到困惑。
misnamed, wrongly titled
correctly titled
adjective
