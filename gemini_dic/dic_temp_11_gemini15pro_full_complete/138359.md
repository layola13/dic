notified: /ˈnoʊtɪfaɪd/
verb
已通知的，被告知的
past participle of notify, meaning having been informed or given notice.
The police were notified of the incident. 警察接到了事件的通知。
I was notified of the meeting by email.  我通过电子邮件收到了会议通知。
synonyms: informed, advised, told, apprised
antonyms: uninformed, unaware, ignorant
verb (past participle)
