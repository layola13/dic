hyper-emotive:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊtɪv/ | adj. |过度情绪化的，极度情绪化的 | Excessively emotional; readily displaying extreme emotions. |  Her hyper-emotive response to the news surprised everyone. 她对这个消息过度情绪化的反应让每个人都感到惊讶。

The hyper-emotive child cried at the slightest provocation.  这个情绪化过度的孩子一被激怒就哭。 | 近义词：overemotional, overly sensitive, excitable, dramatic | 反义词：calm, composed, stoic, impassive | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ (hupér) meaning "over, beyond") + "emotive" (from Latin "emovere" meaning "to move out, remove, excite").
