compressibleness:/kəmˌpresəˈbɪlnəs/ |n.|可压缩性；压缩性|The compressibleness of air allows for its use in pneumatic tools.  空气的可压缩性使其可用于气动工具。| The compressibleness of the gas is a key factor in its storage. 这种气体的可压缩性是其储存的关键因素。|近义词:compactability, compressibility|反义词:incompressibility, rigidity|词性:名词

compress:/kəmˈpres/ |v.|压缩；精简|He compressed the files into a zip folder. 他把文件压缩成一个zip文件夹。|She compressed her presentation into a shorter time slot. 她把她的演讲压缩到一个更短的时间段。|近义词:condense, compact, squeeze|反义词:expand, enlarge, decompress|词性:动词

compressible:/kəmˈpresəbl/ |adj.|可压缩的|Air is a compressible fluid. 空气是一种可压缩流体。|Sponges are highly compressible materials. 海绵是高度可压缩的材料。|近义词:squeezable, condensable|反义词:incompressible, rigid|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From compress + -ible + -ness.  Compress comes from Latin comprimere (to press together), from com- (together) + premere (to press).
