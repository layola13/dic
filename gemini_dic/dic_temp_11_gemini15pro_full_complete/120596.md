mirrorlike:/ˈmɪrərlaɪk/
adjective
像镜子的；光滑如镜的
Resembling a mirror; smooth and reflective.
The lake was mirrorlike, reflecting the surrounding mountains. 湖面像镜子一样，倒映着周围的山脉。

The highly polished floor had a mirrorlike sheen.  高度抛光的地板有着像镜子一样的光泽。

近义词:reflective, glassy, polished, smooth
反义词:dull, rough, opaque, matte
词性:adjective
