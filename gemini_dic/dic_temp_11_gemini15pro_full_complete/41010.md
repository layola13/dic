coparcenary:/koʊˈpɑːrsənəri/| n.|共同继承；共同继承权；共同继承人|The state of being a coparcener; joint heirship.  A joint inheritance.|The property was held in coparcenary by the three sisters.|Synonyms: joint inheritance, joint tenancy, joint heirship|Antonyms: sole ownership, individual ownership|noun

coparcener:/koʊˈpɑːrsənər/| n.|共同继承人|One of several heirs who inherit jointly, especially under common law.|Each of the daughters was a coparcener in their father's estate.|Synonyms: coheir, joint heir|Antonyms: sole heir|noun
