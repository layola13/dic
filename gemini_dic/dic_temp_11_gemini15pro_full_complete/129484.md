non-analogous:/ˌnɒn əˈnæləɡəs/ | adj. | 非类似的，不相似的 | Not analogous; dissimilar; not corresponding. | The two situations are non-analogous and therefore comparing them is misleading. 这两种情况并不相似，因此比较它们会产生误导。|  The development of the internet is non-analogous to any prior technological advancement. 互联网的发展与以往任何技术进步都不相似。 | synonyms: dissimilar, different, unlike, disparate, divergent | antonyms: analogous, similar, alike, comparable, parallel | adjective
