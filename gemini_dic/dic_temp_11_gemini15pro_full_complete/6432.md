ampicillins:/æmˌpɪˈsɪlɪnz/ | n. |氨苄西林（复数）|A semisynthetic penicillin antibiotic used to treat a broad range of bacterial infections.  It is usually administered intravenously or intramuscularly.|The doctor prescribed ampicillins to combat the infection. 医生开了氨苄青霉素来对抗感染。|Synonyms:  penicillin, antibiotic|Antonyms: None (medications don't typically have direct antonyms)|noun
