composedly:/kəmˈpoʊzɪdli/
adverb
镇定地；沉着地
in a calm and controlled manner, especially in a difficult situation.
She accepted the bad news composedly. 她镇定地接受了这个坏消息。
composedly handled the crisis. 她沉着地处理了危机。
近义词:calmly, coolly, serenely
反义词:nervously, anxiously, agitatedly
词性:adverb

词源:
From compose + -ed + -ly.  "Compose" comes from the Old French "composer" and ultimately from the Latin "componere," meaning "to put together."  The "-ed" suffix forms the past participle, and "-ly" creates the adverb form.
