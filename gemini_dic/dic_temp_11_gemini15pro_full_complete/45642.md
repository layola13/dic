cursorial:/kɜːrˈsɔːriəl/ | adj. | 适应奔跑的；善于奔跑的 | Adapted for running; having limbs adapted for running. | The cheetah is a highly cursorial animal. 猎豹是一种非常善于奔跑的动物。|  The horse's long legs make it a cursorial mammal. 马的长腿使它成为一种善于奔跑的哺乳动物。| 近义词：running, swift, fleet, agile | 反义词: slow, sluggish, sedentary | 词性: adjective
