insinuatory:/ɪnˈsɪnjuːətɔːri/ /ɪnˈsɪnjuːəˌtɔːri/
adjective
暗示的，含沙射影的，影射的
Suggesting or implying something bad or unpleasant in an indirect way.
例句：He made an insinuatory remark about her past. 他含沙射影地说了她过去的事。

例句：Her insinuatory tone made me uncomfortable. 她那影射的语气让我很不舒服。

近义词：suggestive, implying, hinting, allusive, innuendo
反义词：direct, explicit, straightforward
词性: adjective
