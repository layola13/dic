gushes:/ˈɡʌʃɪz/ | v. | 涌出，喷涌；迸发；滔滔不绝地说 | To flow or issue suddenly and copiously.  (Of a person) express (an emotion) effusively. | Water gushed from the broken pipe. 水从破裂的管道中喷涌而出。 She gushed about how much she loved the movie. 她滔滔不绝地讲述她有多么喜欢这部电影。 | synonyms: pours, streams, spouts; effuses, raves | antonyms: trickles, drips;  restrains | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *guschen*, frequentative of *gissen* (“to gush”), from Old Norse *geysa* (“to gush”). Cognate with Icelandic *gjósa* (“to gush”).
