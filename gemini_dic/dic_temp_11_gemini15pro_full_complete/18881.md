besieged:/bɪˈsiːdʒd/
verb|被围困的，被包围的|Surrounded and under attack; beset with difficulties.|The city was besieged by enemy forces for months.  这座城市被敌军围困了数月。|例句：The besieged city was running out of food. 被围困的城市粮食快用完了。|近义词：surrounded, encircled, beleaguered, blockaded|反义词：liberated, free|词性:v.
