creatureless:/ˈkriːtʃərlɪs/
adjective
无生物的; 没有生物的
Without creatures; devoid of living beings.
The landscape was barren and creatureless.  这片土地荒芜，寸草不生。
The astronaut landed on a creatureless planet. 宇航员降落在一个没有生物的星球上。
近义词: lifeless, barren, desolate, uninhabited
反义词: teeming, populated, inhabited
词性: adjective
