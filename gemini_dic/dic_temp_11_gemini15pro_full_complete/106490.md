krait:/kraɪt/ | n. | 金环蛇; 环蛇 | Any of several highly venomous Asian snakes of the genus Bungarus, having black and white or yellow rings. | Example: The krait's venom is neurotoxic, paralyzing its prey.  金环蛇的毒液具有神经毒性，会使其猎物瘫痪。

Example:  Kraits are nocturnal hunters, often found near water. 金环蛇是夜间活动的猎手，经常在水边被发现。 | 近义词: snake, viper, cobra | 反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a specific snake type) | noun
