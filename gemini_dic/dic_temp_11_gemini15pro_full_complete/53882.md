discord:/ˈdɪskɔːrd/ (US), /ˈdɪskɔːd/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|不和，纷争；不一致；使不和，使不一致|Lack of agreement or harmony; conflict.|例句：There was much discord among the members of the committee. 委员会成员之间有很多分歧。|近义词：conflict, disagreement, strife, contention|反义词：harmony, agreement, concord, unity|词性：名词，动词

discord:/dɪˈskɔːrd/ (US), /dɪˈskɔːd/ (UK)| vi. vt.|不和，不一致；使不和，使不一致|To disagree or clash.|例句：His views discord with ours. 他的观点与我们的不一致。|近义词：clash, conflict, disagree|反义词：harmonize, agree, accord|词性：动词

词源：From Old French *descord*, from Latin discord-, discors (“disagreeing, different, harsh-sounding”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + cord-, cors (“heart”).  Originally referred to a lack of musical harmony.
