castellation:/ˌkæstəˈleɪʃn/
n.
城堡式建筑；城垛；牙状突起；【电子】(印刷电路板边缘的)方型缺口
A projecting part of a fortification. A series of indentations resembling the battlements of a castle, especially those cut into the edge of a printed circuit board.
The castle was well-defended by its strong castellations. 这座城堡凭借其坚固的城垛得到了很好的防御。
The castellations on the circuit board allow for easy connection to other devices. 电路板上的牙状缺口可以方便地连接到其他设备。
近义词:battlement, crenelation, indentation, notch
反义词:smoothness
