embrew:/ɪmˈbruː/ | vt. | 浸透；使充满；使沾染 | To permeate or saturate, especially with moisture or a quality; to steep or soak. |  The cloth was embrewed with the rich dyes. 这块布浸透了丰富的染料。

The air was embrewed with the scent of pine. 空气中弥漫着松树的香味。 | 近义词: imbue, saturate, permeate, infuse, steep, soak | 反义词: drain, dry | 词性: 动词
