hesitant:/ˈhezɪtənt/| adj.|犹豫的，迟疑的|Inclined to hesitate; uncertain or slow in acting or speaking.|She was hesitant to answer the question. 她犹豫着要不要回答这个问题。|近义词：uncertain, reluctant, dubious, wavering|反义词：decisive, resolute, confident|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *haesitant- (stem of haesitans), present participle of haesitare "to stick fast, remain fixed, stammer, hesitate," frequentative of haerere "to stick, adhere, cling."
