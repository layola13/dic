onondagas:/ˌɒnənˈdɑːɡəz/ | n. | 奥农达加人 |  A member of a North American Indian people originally inhabiting the region around Onondaga Lake, New York. They were one of the original five (later six) nations of the Iroquois Confederacy. | The Onondagas were known for their skilled diplomacy within the Iroquois Confederacy. 奥农达加人以其在易洛魁联盟中的娴熟外交而闻名。|  Iroquois, Haudenosaunee |  None | Noun
