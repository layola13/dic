jointer:/ˈdʒɔɪntər/ (US), /ˈdʒɔɪntə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 接合器，连接器；(木工)接缝刨 |A tool or machine for making a joint, especially one for planing the edges of boards so that they will make a tight joint.  A person or thing that joins.| He used a jointer to smooth the edges of the two boards before gluing them together. 他用接缝刨把两块板的边缘磨光滑后再把它们粘在一起。 |近义词：joiner, connector, assembler |反义词: separator, divider |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *joyntour, *joygnour, from Anglo-Norman joignour, from Old French joindre (“to join”), from Latin jungere (“to join, yoke”).
