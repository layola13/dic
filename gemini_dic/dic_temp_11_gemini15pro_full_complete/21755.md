boating:/ˈboʊtɪŋ/| n.|划船，乘船游览|The act of traveling in a boat, especially for pleasure.|We went boating on the lake last weekend.  我们上周末去湖上划船了。|近义词：sailing, yachting, canoeing, kayaking|反义词:hiking, driving|noun

Etymology: From the verb "boat," which comes from the Old English "bāt" (boat).  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a noun representing the activity.
