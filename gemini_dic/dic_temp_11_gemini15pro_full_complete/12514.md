asphalt:/ˈæsˌfɔːlt/, /ˈæsˌfɒːlt/| n. |沥青，柏油路|A dark bituminous substance obtained from petroleum or by destructive distillation of coal, used for paving and roofing.|The asphalt on the road was melting in the hot sun. 路上的沥青在烈日下融化了。|synonyms: bitumen, tarmacadam, blacktop|antonyms: concrete, cobblestone|noun
asphalt:/ˈæsˌfɔːlt/, /ˈæsˌfɒːlt/| vt. |用沥青铺|To cover with asphalt.|They asphalted the driveway yesterday. 他们昨天用沥青铺了车道。|synonyms: pave, surface, blacktop|antonyms: excavate, dig up|verb
