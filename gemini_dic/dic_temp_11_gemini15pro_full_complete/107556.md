landlady:/ˈlændˌleɪdi/ (US), /ˈlændˌleɪdi/ (UK)| n.|女房东，女业主|A woman who owns and rents out land or buildings to tenants.|例句：The landlady was very kind and helpful. 女房东非常友善和乐于助人。|近义词：owner, proprietor|反义词:tenant|noun
