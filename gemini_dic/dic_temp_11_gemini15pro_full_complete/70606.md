filmically:/fɪlˈmɪkli/
adverb
电影地；像电影一样地
in a way that relates to or resembles film
The story unfolds filmically, with dramatic close-ups and sweeping panoramic shots.  这个故事以电影般的方式展开，带有戏剧性的特写镜头和广阔的全景镜头。

例句：
The director captured the scene filmically, using a variety of camera angles and lighting techniques. 导演以电影手法拍摄了这一幕，运用了各种各样的摄像角度和灯光技巧。

The author described the events filmically, making the reader feel like they were watching a movie.  作者用电影化的方式描述了这些事件，让读者感觉像在看电影。


近义词：cinematically
反义词: prosaically, unfilmically (less common)
词性: adverb
