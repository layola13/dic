formulae:/ˈfɔːrmjʊliː/ (also formulas)
noun
公式，配方
A set of instructions for making or doing something.
The formula for the area of a circle is πr².  圆的面积公式是πr²。
synonyms: equation, rule, method, recipe, prescription
antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a set of instructions)
