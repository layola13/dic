octodecimo:/ˌɒktəˈdɛsɪmoʊ/ /ˌɑːktəˈdɛsɪmoʊ/
|n.|十八开本|A size of book page resulting from folding each printed sheet into eighteen leaves (36 pages).|This rare book was printed in octodecimo.|近义词:18mo|反义词:folio|词性:名词
Etymology: From Latin *octōdecimus* (eighteenth), from *octō* (eight) + *decimus* (tenth).
