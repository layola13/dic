hypural:/ˈhaɪpərəl/| n. |尾下骨；下尾骨|A flattened, modified hemal spine at the posterior end of the vertebral column in some fishes, supporting the caudal fin rays.|The hypurals are key elements in the caudal fin structure of teleost fish.  尾下骨是硬骨鱼尾鳍结构的关键元素。|The hypurals of this species are uniquely shaped.  这个物种的尾下骨形状独特。|Synonyms: hypural bone|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical structure)|noun
