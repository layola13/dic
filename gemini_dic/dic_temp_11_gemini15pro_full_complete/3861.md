agorot's:/əˈɡɔːrɒts/
noun
阿戈罗(以色列货币单位)的复数所有格形式
plural possessive form of agora, the smallest unit of Israeli currency.  It indicates ownership or possession by multiple agorot.
The price increase was only a few agorot's worth. 价格只上涨了几阿戈罗。
Synonym:  agora's (alternative spelling), agorot's (less common)
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a unit of currency)
词性: noun
