gutturality:/ˌɡʌtəˈrælɪti/ | n. |喉音, 粗哑的声音|The quality of being guttural; a guttural sound.|His voice had a certain gutturality that added to his intimidating presence. 他的声音有种喉音，让他更令人畏惧。|Example: The gutturality of his laugh made everyone in the room uncomfortable. 他粗哑的笑声让房间里的每个人都不舒服。|Synonyms: harshness, hoarseness, roughness |Antonyms: smoothness, melodiousness | noun
