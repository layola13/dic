ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lætest, from Proto-Germanic *laista- (“following after, slowest”), superlative of *lat- (“slow, late”). Cognate with Old Norse lestr (“footprint, track”), Dutch laatst (“last”), German letzt (“last”). The semantic development is from "slowest" to "coming last" to "final".
