layaway:/ˈleɪəˌweɪ/ /ˈleɪəweɪ/ | n.| 分期付款;预订商品服务 |A system of paying a deposit to secure an item and then making regular payments until the full price is paid. The item is kept by the store until the final payment is made.| I put the dress on layaway so I could pay for it over a few months. 我把这件衣服分期付款了，这样我就可以在几个月内付清。| Synonyms: installment plan, payment plan | Antonyms: outright purchase | Noun

layaway:/ˈleɪəˌweɪ/ /ˈleɪəweɪ/ | vt.| 分期付款购买;预订 |To purchase something on a layaway plan.|  She layawayed a new bicycle for her son's birthday. 她为儿子生日预订了一辆新自行车。| Synonyms: put on layaway, reserve | Antonyms: buy outright | Verb
