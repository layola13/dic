muddiness:/ˈmʌdinəs/ | n. | 泥泞；浑浊；模糊不清 | The muddiness of the water made it difficult to see the fish.  水的浑浊使得很难看到鱼。|  The muddiness of his explanation left me confused. 他的解释含糊不清，让我感到困惑。 | 近义词：murkiness, turbidity, cloudiness | 反义词：clarity, clearness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *mudinesse, equivalent to muddy +‎ -ness.
