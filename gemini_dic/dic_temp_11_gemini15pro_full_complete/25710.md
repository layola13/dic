bunny:/ˈbʌni/ | n. |兔子；可爱的女郎|A small rabbit, especially a young one.  Also, sometimes used as a term of endearment for a woman.|例句：The children were delighted to see a fluffy bunny hopping in the garden. 孩子们很高兴看到一只毛茸茸的小兔子在花园里跳来跳去。

例句：He called his girlfriend "bunny" as a term of endearment. 他亲昵地称他的女朋友为“小兔子”。|近义词：rabbit, hare (more formal), coney|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
