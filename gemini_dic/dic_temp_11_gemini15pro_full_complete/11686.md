armour-plated:/ˈɑːməˌpleɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈɑːrmərˌpleɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|装甲的，坚固的，防护严密的|Covered or protected with armour plating.  Figuratively, it means something is heavily protected or resistant to criticism.|例句：The car was armour-plated for the president's safety. 这辆车为了总统的安全而加装了装甲。

例句：He has an armour-plated self-confidence. 他有着无比坚定的自信。|近义词：reinforced, fortified, protected, impregnable|反义词：vulnerable, unprotected, defenseless|词性:形容词

armour-plating:/ˈɑːməˌpleɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːrmərˌpleɪtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|装甲，装甲板|Protective metal plating.|例句：The armour-plating on the tank deflected the bullets. 坦克上的装甲板弹开了子弹。

例句：The ship's armour-plating was several inches thick.  这艘船的装甲板有几英寸厚。|近义词：armour, plating, protection|反义词：(No direct antonym)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From "armour" (from Old French "armure," ultimately from Latin "arma" - weapons) + "plated" (past participle of "plate," from Old French "plat" - flat).  Essentially, it refers to being covered in flat pieces of protective metal.
