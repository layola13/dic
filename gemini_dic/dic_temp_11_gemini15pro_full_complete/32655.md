chitin: /ˈkaɪtɪn/ (US) /ˈkaɪtɪn/ (UK)| n. |几丁质；壳质|A fibrous substance consisting of polysaccharides and forming the major constituent in the exoskeleton of arthropods and the cell walls of fungi.|The exoskeleton of insects is made of chitin.|近义词:  exoskeleton (in some contexts)|反义词:  None|词性:noun

chitinous: /ˈkaɪtɪnəs/ (US) /ˈkaɪtɪnəs/ (UK) | adj. |几丁质的；含几丁质的|Containing or resembling chitin.|The chitinous shell of the beetle protected it from predators.|近义词:  horny, shell-like|反义词:  None|词性:adjective
