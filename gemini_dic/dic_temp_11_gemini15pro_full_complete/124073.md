morphotic:/mɔːrˈfɒtɪk/ /mɔːrˈfɑːtɪk/| adj.|形态的，形态学的|Relating to the form or structure of things, especially in biology or linguistics.  Pertaining to morphology.|The morphotic features of the virus were studied under a microscope. 在显微镜下研究了该病毒的形态特征。|Near synonyms: structural, formal, morphological|Antonym: amorphous|Word type: Adjective
