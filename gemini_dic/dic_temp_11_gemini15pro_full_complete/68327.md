fall-flat:/fɔːl ˈflæt/| phrasal verb |彻底失败；未达到预期效果|To fail completely to produce the desired effect or result. |例句：His joke fell flat. 他的笑话很冷场。

例句：The new advertising campaign fell flat. 新的广告活动彻底失败了。


近义词：fail miserably, flop, bomb, tank, backfire|反义词:succeed, triumph|词性:动词短语
