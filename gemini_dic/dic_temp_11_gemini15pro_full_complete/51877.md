dezincification:/ˌdiːzɪŋkɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
脱锌，去锌
The selective corrosion of zinc from brass, leaving a porous, copper-rich structure.
The dezincification of the pipes caused a significant leak. 管道的脱锌造成了严重的泄漏。
corrosion, erosion, leaching
