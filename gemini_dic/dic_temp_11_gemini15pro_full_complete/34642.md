clench:/klɛntʃ/ /klɛnʧ/ | v. n. | 紧握，咬紧，握紧，攥紧，确定（胜利）| to close tightly; to grasp firmly | He clenched his fists in anger. 他愤怒地握紧了拳头。 | synonyms: grip, grasp, clutch, tighten | antonyms: loosen, release, relax | verb

clench:/klɛntʃ/ /klɛnʧ/ | n. | 紧握，咬紧 | a tight grip |  The clenching of his jaw showed his determination. 他紧咬的下巴显示了他的决心。 | synonyms: grip, grasp, clutch | antonyms: release, relaxation | noun
