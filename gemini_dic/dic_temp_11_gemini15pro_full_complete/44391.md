cross-fertilize:/ˌkrɔːsˈfɜːrtɪlaɪz/
verb
交叉施肥，异花授粉；相互影响，互相促进
To fertilize a plant or animal with pollen or sperm from a different variety; to cause (ideas, influences, etc.) to have an effect on each other.
The bees cross-fertilize the flowers in the garden. 蜜蜂给花园里的花进行异花授粉。
New ideas are cross-fertilized when people from different backgrounds work together. 当不同背景的人一起工作时，新的想法就会相互融合。
Synonyms: interbreed, hybridize, crossbreed, pollinate, intermingle, blend
Antonyms: inbreed, self-fertilize
v.
