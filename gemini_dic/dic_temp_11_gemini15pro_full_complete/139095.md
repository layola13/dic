obdiplostemonous: /ˌɒbdɪpləˈstɛmənəs/
adjective
对瓣二强雄蕊的；外轮对瓣雄蕊的
Having stamens in two whorls, the outer whorl opposite the petals.
The obdiplostemonous arrangement of the flower's stamens is a key characteristic for identification.  这朵花对瓣二强雄蕊的排列是识别的关键特征。
The botanist identified the rare plant by its obdiplostemonous flowers.  植物学家通过其对瓣二强雄蕊的花朵识别出了这种稀有植物。
近义词: diplostemonous (though not exactly the same, it refers to having two whorls of stamens)
反义词:  No direct antonym exists.
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *ob-* "against, opposite" + *diplo-* "double" + *stemon* "thread, warp (in weaving), stamen" + *-ous* (adjective forming suffix).
