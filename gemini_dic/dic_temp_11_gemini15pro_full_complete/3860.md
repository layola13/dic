agorot:/ɑːˈɡɔːrɒt/ |n.|阿哥拉,以色列货币单位|A subunit of the Israeli new shekel (NIS). One hundred agorot equal one shekel.  There are coins of 10 and 50 agorot.|The price was 4 shekels and 50 agorot.|Synonyms: agora (less common, sometimes pluralized as agorot), cent (general term for a hundredth of a currency unit)|Antonyms: shekel (the main unit, not a direct opposite but represents a larger denomination)|noun
