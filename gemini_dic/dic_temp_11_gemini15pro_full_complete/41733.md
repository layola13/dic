corrosibility:/kəˌroʊzəˈbɪləti/ | n. |腐蚀性，可腐蚀性 | The corrosibility of the metal is a major concern.  This property describes how easily a material can be corroded. | The corrosibility of iron in a salty environment is high. 铁在含盐环境中的腐蚀性很高。|Synonyms: corrosiveness, susceptibility to corrosion | Antonyms: resistance to corrosion, inertness | noun
