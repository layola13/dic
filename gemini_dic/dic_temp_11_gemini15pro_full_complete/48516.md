deerstalking: /ˈdɪərstɔːkɪŋ/
noun
猎鹿; 潜近猎鹿
The act of stalking deer, typically as a form of hunting.
He went deerstalking in the Scottish Highlands. 他到苏格兰高地猎鹿去了。

近义词: hunting, stalking
反义词:  conservation

词性: noun
