comper:/ˈkɒmpə(r)/ (UK) /kəmˈpɛr/ (US)| n.|主持人，司仪|A person who introduces the acts in a variety show or other entertainment.|例句：The comper kept the audience entertained between acts.  司仪在节目表演的间隙让观众保持愉悦。

例句：The comper announced the next singer with great enthusiasm.  司仪满怀热情地宣布了下一位歌手。|近义词：host, emcee, master of ceremonies|反义词: audience, spectator|词性:noun

词源:  "Comper" comes from the verb "compere," which is a back-formation from "compère," borrowed from French, meaning "compare."  The shift in meaning likely occurred because a master of ceremonies often compares or links different parts of a show.
