noun

illiberal: /ɪˈlɪbərəl/
adjective
吝啬的，小气的，不慷慨的
not generous; mean.
He made a very illiberal offer for the house. 他对这栋房子给出了一个非常吝啬的报价。
近义词: stingy, mean, tight-fisted, parsimonious, miserly
反义词: generous, liberal, magnanimous, bountiful

词源:
From Late Latin illiberalis, from in- ‘not’ + liberalis (see liberal).
