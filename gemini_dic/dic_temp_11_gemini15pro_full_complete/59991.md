editionize:/ɪˈdɪʃənaɪz/ |v. |版本化，使成为版本|To produce or publish in different editions.  To make something available in different versions or formats, often for different markets or audiences. |The publisher decided to editionize the textbook for the American and British markets. 出版商决定将教科书版本化，以适应美国和英国市场。|The software company editionized their product to offer different features at various price points. 这家软件公司对其产品进行了版本化，以不同的价位提供不同的功能。| synonym: adapt, modify, version | antonym: standardize, unify |verb
