grownness:/ˈɡroʊnnɪs/
n.
成年；成熟
The state of being fully grown or developed.
His grownness surprised his parents, who still thought of him as a child. 他的成熟让他的父母很惊讶，他们仍然认为他是个孩子。
maturity, adulthood, development
childishness, immaturity
