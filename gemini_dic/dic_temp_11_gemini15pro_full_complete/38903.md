confessary:/kənˈfɛsəri/| n.|忏悔室，告解室|A small enclosed space in a church where people go to confess their sins to a priest. |例句：He entered the confessional and knelt down. 他走进忏悔室跪了下来。

例句：The priest listened patiently in the confessary. 神父在忏悔室里耐心地听着。|近义词：confessional|反义词:None|词性:noun

confessory:/kənˈfɛsəri/| adj.|忏悔的，告解的|Relating to confession or penance. (Less common usage than the noun form)|例句：He wrote a confessory letter to his friend. 他给他的朋友写了一封忏悔信。

例句：The confessory nature of the ritual was evident.  仪式的忏悔性质显而易见。|近义词：penitential, contrite|反义词:unrepentant|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *confessarius, from Latin confessus (past participle of confiteri "to confess"), from com- "together" + fateri "to acknowledge."
