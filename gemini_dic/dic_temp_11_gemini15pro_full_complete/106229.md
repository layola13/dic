knotwort:/ˈnɒtwɜːt/| n. | 鹅肠菜，萹蓄 |A common weedy plant (Polygonum aviculare) with jointed stems and small, inconspicuous flowers, often found in disturbed ground. It's also known as prostrate knotweed, common knotgrass, or bird knotgrass.|The farmer tried to eradicate the knotwort from his field.  农民试图从他的田地里清除萹蓄。|Synonyms: knotweed, knotgrass, prostrate knotweed, common knotgrass, bird knotgrass|Antonyms: N/A|noun
