co-owners:/koʊˈoʊnərz/| n.|共同所有人，共同业主|People who share ownership of something, such as property or a business.|Example 1:  The co-owners of the restaurant decided to renovate the dining area.  餐厅的共同所有人决定翻新用餐区。

Example 2: My brother and I are co-owners of a small apartment building.  我和我哥哥是一栋小型公寓楼的共同业主。|近义词：joint owners, shared owners|反义词: sole owner, sole proprietor|Noun
