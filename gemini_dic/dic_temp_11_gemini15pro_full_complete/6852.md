anatocism: /əˈnætəsɪzəm/ | n. | 复利盘算，高利贷 |The charging of interest on interest, especially at an exorbitant rate.  The practice of adding unpaid interest to the principal balance, upon which interest is then calculated.  This can lead to rapidly increasing debt. |  The lender was accused of anatocism after adding the unpaid interest to the loan principal.  放贷人因将未付利息添加到贷款本金中而被指控复利盘算。

Anatocism is prohibited in many jurisdictions. 在许多司法管辖区，复利盘算是被禁止的。 | 近义词：compound interest, usury | 反义词: simple interest | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *anatocismus, from Ancient Greek ἀνατοκισμός (anatokismós, “payment of compound interest”), from ἀνα- (ana-, “again, back”) + τόκος (tókos, “interest”).
