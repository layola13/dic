misevaluation:/ˌmɪsɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 误判，错误评估 | An inaccurate or incorrect assessment of something's worth, importance, or quality. |  The misevaluation of the company's assets led to significant financial losses.  公司资产的误判导致了巨大的经济损失。 | Example:  His misevaluation of the opponent's strength cost him the match. 他对对手实力的误判让他输掉了比赛。 | Synonyms: misjudgment, miscalculation, underestimation, overestimation | Antonyms: accurate assessment, correct evaluation | Noun
