disemploy:/ˌdɪsɪmˈplɔɪ/ | vt. | 使失业，解雇 | To deprive (someone) of employment; put out of work. |  The factory closed and disemployed hundreds of workers. 工厂倒闭，导致数百名工人失业。

The economic downturn has disemployed many skilled professionals. 经济衰退导致许多熟练的专业人员失业。 | 近义词：dismiss, fire, lay off, make redundant, discharge | 反义词：employ, hire, recruit | 词性：动词
