ababa:/əˈbɑːbə/ | n. | 阿巴巴（一种织物） | A coarse handwoven fabric made from the fibers of the abaca plant, often used for making ropes, mats, and other similar items.  It's primarily produced in the Philippines. | The abaca fiber is strong and durable, making ababa a popular choice for various applications.  阿巴巴纤维结实耐用，使其成为各种用途的流行选择。| Example: The trader displayed rolls of ababa in his stall.  商人在他的摊位上展示了成卷的阿巴巴。| Synonym: Manila hemp, abaca cloth | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of fabric) | noun
