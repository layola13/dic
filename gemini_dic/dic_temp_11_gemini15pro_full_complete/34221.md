clangous:/ˈklæŋəs/| adj.|发出叮当声的，响亮的|Characterized by or producing a loud, resonant, metallic sound, like that of metal striking metal.|The clangous bells of the cathedral echoed through the square. 大教堂响亮的钟声在广场上回荡。|近义词：resonant, resounding, sonorous, clangorous|反义词：muffled, quiet, soft|词性:形容词
Etymology: From clang + -ous (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by").  Ultimately from Latin *clangere* "to resound."
