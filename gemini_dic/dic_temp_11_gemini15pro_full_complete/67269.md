exquisiteness:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪtnəs/| n. |精致，优雅，细腻|The exquisiteness of the craftsmanship is remarkable.  这工艺的精湛程度非凡。| 近义词:delicacy, refinement, elegance| 反义词:coarseness, crudeness, vulgarity| noun

exquisite:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪt/| adj. |精致的，精美的，剧烈的|She wore an exquisite gown to the ball. 她穿着一件精美的礼服去参加舞会。  He felt exquisite pain after the surgery.  手术后他感到剧烈的疼痛。| 近义词:refined, elegant, delicate, intense| 反义词:crude, coarse, rough, mild| adjective
