anchylosis:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊsɪs/ /ˌæŋkaɪˈləʊsɪs/
n.
僵硬，强直
Stiffness or fixation of a joint.
The patient experienced anchylosis of the knee after the injury.  这个病人在受伤后膝盖僵硬。
近义词：stiffness, rigidity, immobility
反义词：flexibility, mobility

anchylose:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊz/ /ˌæŋkaɪˈləʊz/
v.
(使)僵硬，(使)强直
To make or become stiff or fixed.
The arthritis caused his joints to anchylose. 关节炎导致他的关节僵硬。
近义词：stiffen, rigidify, immobilize
反义词：loosen, mobilize
