nondarkest:/nɒnˈdɑːrkɪst/ | adj. | 非最黑暗的；不那么黑暗的 | Not the darkest; less dark than the darkest. | The nondarkest corner of the room was still dimly lit. 房间里不那么黑暗的角落仍然光线昏暗。|  The nondarkest shade of blue was chosen for the wall.  墙面选择了不那么深的蓝色。|  lighter, brighter | darkest | adjective

Etymology:
"Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Darkest" is the superlative form of the adjective "dark."  Therefore, "nondarkest" literally means "not the darkest."
