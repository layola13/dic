intertwine:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪn/| v. |交织，缠绕，紧密联系|To twist or twine together; interlace.  To be or become mutually involved.|例句：The vines intertwined around the trellis. 藤蔓缠绕在棚架上。|例句：Their destinies were intertwined. 他们的命运交织在一起。|近义词：interweave, interlace, entwine, tangle|反义词：separate, untangle, disentangle|词性:动词
