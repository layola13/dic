fins:/fɪnz/
n.
鱼鳍，鳍状物，散热片
thin membranous processes or projections of various shapes and uses, especially on aquatic animals for propulsion, balance, or steering; a similar projection on an aircraft, car, or missile.
The fish uses its fins to swim. 这条鱼用它的鳍游泳。
The car has fins for stability. 这辆车的尾翼是为了保持稳定性。

近义词:flippers, wings
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:noun


fins:/fɪnz/
n.
(人的)手
(informal) hand
He grabbed the money with his fins. 他用他的手抓住了钱。

近义词:hands, paws
反义词:(No direct antonym)
