beelining:/ˈbiːlaɪnɪŋ/
n.|直奔，直线前进|Going directly and quickly towards something or someone, like a bee flying straight to its hive.|He made a beeline for the buffet as soon as he arrived. 他一到就直奔自助餐台。|近义词：heading straight for, making a dash for, rushing towards|反义词：meandering, wandering, strolling|词性：名词

beelining:/ˈbiːlaɪnɪŋ/
v.|直奔，直线前进|To go directly and quickly towards something or someone.|She beelined for the exit as soon as the meeting was over.  会议一结束，她就直奔出口。|近义词：head straight for, make a dash for, rush towards|反义词：meander, wander, stroll|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "bee" + "line."  Bees are known for flying in a straight line back to their hive after collecting pollen.  This efficient, direct route became the basis for the word "beeline."
