bicorn:/ˈbaɪkɔːrn/| adj. n.|两角的，双角的；两角帽；双角形|Having two horns or horn-like projections.  A hat with two points, like those worn by some military officers. A crescent-shaped figure.|The general wore a bicorn hat. 将军戴着一顶双角帽。|近义词：two-horned, crescent, bicorne|反义词:unicorn|词性:adjective, noun

bicorn:/ˈbaɪkɔːrn/| n.|两角帽；双角形|A hat with two points, like those worn by some military officers. A crescent-shaped figure.|Napoleon was often depicted wearing his bicorn hat. 拿破仑经常被描绘成戴着他的双角帽。|近义词：bicorne, cocked hat|反义词:|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin bicornis (“two-horned”), from bi- (“two”) +‎ cornū (“horn”).
