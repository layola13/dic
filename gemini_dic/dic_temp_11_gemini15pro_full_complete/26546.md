cabinetries:/ˈkæbɪnətriz/
n.
橱柜; 橱柜制造业
The craft or business of designing and installing cabinets.  The cabinets of a particular room or building collectively.
The kitchen's new cabinetry is stunning.  厨房的新橱柜令人惊叹。
近义词:cabinets, cupboards
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly open shelving)
词性:noun
