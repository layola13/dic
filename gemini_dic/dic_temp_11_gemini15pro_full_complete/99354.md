interdepartmental:/ˌɪntərdɪˌpɑːrtˈmentl/| adj.|部门间的，跨部门的|Involving or existing between two or more departments within an organization.|例句：Interdepartmental cooperation is crucial for the success of this project.  这个项目的成功需要跨部门合作。|例句：The company organized an interdepartmental meeting to discuss the new strategy. 公司组织了一次跨部门会议来讨论新的战略。|近义词：cross-departmental, interdivisional|反义词:intradepartmental|词性:形容词

词源:
inter- (prefix): between, among (来自拉丁语)
departmental (adjective): relating to a department
