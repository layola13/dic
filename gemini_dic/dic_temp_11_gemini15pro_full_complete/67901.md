facadist:/fəˈsɑːdɪst/| n. |外观主义者；注重表面的人|A person who is preoccupied with facades or superficial appearances, often neglecting the underlying substance or reality.|He's such a facadist, always concerned with how things look rather than how they function. 他真是个外观主义者，总是关心事物的外表而不是它们的功能。|近义词：superficialist, poseur|反义词: pragmatist, realist|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *façade* (face, front) + *-ist* (one who practices or is concerned with).
