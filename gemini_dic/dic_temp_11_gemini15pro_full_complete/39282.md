congruent:/ˈkɒŋɡruənt/ (British) /ˈkɑːŋɡruənt/ (American)| adj.|全等的，一致的，适合的|Identical in form; coinciding exactly when superimposed.  Also, in agreement or harmony.|例句：The two triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形全等。|近义词：identical, similar, matching, corresponding, compatible, consistent|反义词：incongruent, dissimilar, incompatible, inconsistent|词性:形容词
congruence:/ˈkɒŋɡruəns/ (British) /ˈkɑːŋɡruəns/ (American)| n.|全等，一致，适合|The state of being congruent; agreement or harmony.|例句：There is a congruence between his beliefs and his actions. 他的信仰和行动是一致的。|近义词：agreement, harmony, consistency, compatibility|反义词：incongruence, disagreement, inconsistency, incompatibility|词性:名词
