cistercian:/sɪˈstɜːʃən/ | adj. n. | 西多会的；西多会修士 |  Relating to or denoting a monastic order founded in 1098 at Cîteaux in eastern France, known for its strict observance of the Benedictine rule and for its emphasis on manual labor and austerity.  | The Cistercian monks are known for their simple lifestyle. 西多会修士以其简朴的生活方式而闻名。 | The abbey is a beautiful example of Cistercian architecture. 这座修道院是西多会建筑的美丽典范。 |  Benedictine, monastic |  secular | adjective, noun
