menobranch:/ˈmɛnəˌbræŋk/ | n. | 美西螈，泥螈 | A large, permanently larval, aquatic salamander (Necturus maculosus) of the eastern North America, having bushy external gills.  It is also called a mud puppy. |  例句：The menobranch, with its feathery gills, is a fascinating creature to observe in its aquatic habitat.  美西螈，带着它羽毛状的鳃，在水生环境中是一种迷人的生物。

例句：Menobranches are often mistaken for axolotls, but they are distinct species. 美西螈经常被误认为是蝾螈，但它们是不同的物种。 | 近义词：mud puppy, water dog | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mēn* (moon) + *bránchia* (gills), referring to the feathery gills which somewhat resemble a crescent moon.
