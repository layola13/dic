mesophyllum:/meˈsɒfɪləm/ | n. | 中叶藻属；中叶藻 | A genus of red algae (family Corallinaceae) that are heavily calcified, especially in the lower portion, and form crusts or nodules.|  Mesophyllum lichenoides is a common species found in the Mediterranean Sea. 中叶藻属地衣状珊瑚藻是地中海地区的常见物种。 |  Synonym:  Lithothamnion (sometimes used interchangeably but taxonomically distinct),  Phymatolithon (closely related) | Antonym: N/A (It's a genus of algae, so direct antonyms are not applicable.) | noun
