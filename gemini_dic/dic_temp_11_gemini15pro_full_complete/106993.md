lacquerers:/ˈlækərərz/
n.
喷漆工；涂漆工人
People who apply lacquer, a type of varnish or finish.
The lacquerers carefully applied several coats to the furniture.  喷漆工小心地在家具上涂了几层漆。
近义词：varnishers, finishers, painters
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun


lacquerers:/ˈlækərərz/
词源：From lacquer + -er (agent suffix). Ultimately from Middle French lacre.
用法说明：Lacquerers refers to individuals who apply lacquer, a hard, glossy varnish, to objects. It's typically used in the context of furniture making, automotive finishing, or decorative arts.
