depurant:/ˈdɛpjʊrənt/| n. adj.|净化剂，净化的|A substance that cleanses or purifies, especially the blood or body.|Example: The doctor prescribed a depurant to help cleanse the patient's system. 医生给病人开了一种净化剂来帮助清洁病人的身体系统。|Example:  He took a herbal depurant to detoxify his body after the holidays.  他假期过后服用草药净化剂来排毒。|近义词:purifier, cleanser, detoxifier|反义词:contaminant, pollutant|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *depurant-, present participle stem of depurare "to cleanse, purify," from Latin de- "from, down, away" + purare "to purify."
