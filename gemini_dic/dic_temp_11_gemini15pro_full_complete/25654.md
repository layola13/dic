bundly:/ˈbʌndli/| adj.| 成捆的，捆绑式的 | bundled, tied together | The newspapers were bundly delivered. 报纸成捆地送来了。 | 近义词：bundled, packaged, grouped | 反义词: loose, separate | 词性:形容词

词源:  "Bundly" is likely a  non-standard or colloquial formation based on "bundle" (n. & v.), which comes from Middle English *bundel, possibly from Low German or Dutch *bundel, ultimately of uncertain origin.  It's not a formally recognized word in standard dictionaries.  It functions as an adjective describing something that is in the form of or characteristic of a bundle.
