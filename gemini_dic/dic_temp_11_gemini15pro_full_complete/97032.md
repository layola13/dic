infective:/ɪnˈfɛktɪv/| adj.|传染性的，感染性的|Capable of causing infection; contagious.|The infective agent was identified as a novel coronavirus.  这种传染性病原体被确定为一种新型冠状病毒。|近义词：contagious, transmissible, communicable|反义词：noninfective, non-contagious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *īnfectīvus, from Latin īnfectus (past participle of īnficere “to taint, stain, infect”), from in- “in, into” + facere “to make, do”.
