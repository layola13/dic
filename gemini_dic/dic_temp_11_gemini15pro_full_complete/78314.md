gerontocracy:/ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒkrəsi/ | n. |老年统治；老人政治|A form of social organization in which the leaders are old people.|The small town was effectively a gerontocracy, run by a council of elders.|Synonyms: gerontocratic rule, rule by elders |Antonyms:  youthocracy, rule by the young | noun
