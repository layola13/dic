mundane:/mʌnˈdeɪn/ /mʌnˈdeɪn/| adj.|世俗的；平凡的；单调的|Lacking interest or excitement; dull.  Relating to or characteristic of the world or worldly matters.|例句：She found the mundane tasks of housework boring. 她觉得做家务琐事很无聊。|近义词：ordinary, commonplace, routine, humdrum, banal|反义词:extraordinary, remarkable, exciting, thrilling|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from Old French mondain, from monde ‘world’, from Latin mundus.
