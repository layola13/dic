midwit:/ˈmɪdwɪt/| n.|中等智力者；庸才|A person of average or slightly above average intelligence, often used pejoratively to describe someone who overestimates their intellectual abilities.|He considers himself a genius, but most people see him as a midwit. 他自认为是天才，但大多数人认为他只是个中等智力者。|近义词:mediocre, average, philistine|反义词:genius, intellectual, highbrow|词性:名词
