beldame:/ˈbeldeɪm/ | n. |老妇人；丑老太婆 | An offensive term for an old woman, especially one considered to be ugly or malicious. |  The beldame cackled gleefully as she stirred her cauldron.  丑老太婆一边搅动着大锅，一边高兴地咯咯笑着。| synonyms: hag, crone, witch | antonyms: maiden, ingenue | noun

Etymology:
From Middle French *beldame*, from Old French *bele dame* "beautiful lady."  The meaning shifted over time from "fine lady" to "old woman," and eventually acquired negative connotations.
