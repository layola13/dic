needlepointed:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪntɪd/| adj.|针绣的；用针绣装饰的|Decorated with needlepoint.  A needlepointed design often involves yarn stitched onto canvas.|例句：She gave me a needlepointed pillow for my birthday. 她送给我一个针绣的枕头作为生日礼物。

例句：The chair was covered in a beautiful needlepointed tapestry. 椅子上覆盖着漂亮的针绣挂毯。|近义词：embroidered, stitched|反义词:plain, undecorated|词性:形容词

词源:  From "needlepoint" (noun), referring to the type of embroidery, + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Needlepoint" itself comes from "needle" + "point."
