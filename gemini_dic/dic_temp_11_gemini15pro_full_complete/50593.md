derangement:/dɪˈreɪndʒmənt/ | n. |错乱，紊乱，精神失常|A state of mental disturbance and disorder. |The stress of the situation led to a temporary derangement of his mind. 这种情况的压力导致他精神暂时错乱。| Synonyms: disorder, disarray, chaos, confusion, madness, insanity | Antonyms: order, organization, sanity, stability | noun

derangement:/dɪˈreɪndʒmənt/ | n. |排列错乱|A permutation of the elements of a set, such that no element appears in its original position. |The number of derangements of a set of n elements is given by the subfactorial of n.  n个元素集合的错排数由n的次阶乘给出。| Synonyms: permutation, transposition | Antonyms: N/A | noun
