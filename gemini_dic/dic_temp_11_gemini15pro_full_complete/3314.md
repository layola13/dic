ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affluentness, from Latin affluent-, stem of affluens (“abounding, rich, copious”), present active participle of affluere (“to flow to, abound”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + fluere (“to flow”).  See also affluent (adj.).
