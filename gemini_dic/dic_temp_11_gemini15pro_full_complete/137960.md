adjective

Not fading or decaying; remaining fresh and vibrant.
The florist created a bouquet of nonwithering flowers for the wedding.  花商为婚礼制作了一束不凋谢的花。
She gave him a nonwithering rose, symbolizing their eternal love. 她送给他一朵不凋谢的玫瑰，象征着他们永恒的爱。
近义词:everlasting, unfading, imperishable, permanent
反义词:withering, fading, decaying, perishable
词性:adjective

词源:

withering (present participle of wither): from Old English *wiðrian, meaning "to dry up"
