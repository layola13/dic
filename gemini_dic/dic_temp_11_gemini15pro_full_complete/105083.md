kerfuffle:/kərˈfʌf(ə)l/
n.
混乱，骚动
a commotion or fuss, especially one caused by conflicting views.
例句：There was a bit of a kerfuffle over the seating arrangements.  对于座位的安排，大家有些小骚动。

例句：The sudden change in policy caused a kerfuffle among the employees. 政策的突然改变在员工中引起了骚动。

近义词：fuss, commotion, uproar, disturbance, hubbub, brouhaha, fracas, melee
反义词：calm, peace, tranquility, order
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain; perhaps related to Scottish Gaelic *car-fuffle ‘disordered heap’. First recorded in the mid 19th century. 可能源于苏格兰盖尔语 *car-fuffle “杂乱堆”。 首次记录于19世纪中期。
