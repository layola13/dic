mensurate:/ˈmenʃərɪt/ /ˈmenʃərət/| adj.|相称的，相当的，相等的|Corresponding in size, amount, extent, or degree; proportionate.|例句：Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。|例句：The punishment should be commensurate with the crime. 惩罚应该与罪行相称。|近义词：proportionate, appropriate, corresponding, equivalent, comparable|反义词：disproportionate, inadequate, unequal, incommensurate|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin commensuratus ‘measured together’, from com- ‘together’ + mensuratus (past participle of mensurare ‘to measure’), from mensura ‘measure’.
