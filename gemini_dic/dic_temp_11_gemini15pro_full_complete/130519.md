nonaccompanying:/ˌnɒnəkʌmpəniɪŋ/
adjective
非陪伴的，无陪同的
Not going with someone else; traveling or attending an event alone.
The nonaccompanying baggage will be sent separately.  非随身行李将单独发送。
Children traveling nonaccompanying require special attention from airline staff.  无人陪伴的儿童需要航空公司工作人员的特别照顾。
近义词: unescorted, solo, alone
反义词: accompanied, escorted
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "lack of" (from Latin)
accompanying (present participle of accompany): meaning "to go with" (from Old French acompaniier)
