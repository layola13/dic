codesigns:/ˈkoʊdsaɪnz/| n.|共同设计，联合签署|The codesigns on the document indicated a collaborative effort. 文件上的共同签名表明这是一项合作成果。|例句：The codesigns on the contract confirmed the agreement between the two companies. 合同上共同的签名确认了两家公司之间的协议。

codesigns:/ˈkoʊdsaɪnz/| v.|共同设计，联合签署|They codesigned the proposal for the new project. 他们共同设计了新项目的提案。|例句：Both parties codesigned the peace treaty, ending the long conflict. 双方共同签署了和平条约，结束了长期冲突。


近义词：joint signatures, collaborative signatures|反义词:sole signature, individual signature|词性:名词,动词

Etymology:  "co-" (together) + "design" (to create a plan or drawing) or "sign" (to mark with one's signature).  The prefix "co-" indicates joint or shared action.  "Design" comes from the Latin "designare" (to mark out). "Sign" comes from the Latin "signum" (a mark, token, or sign).
