gait:/ɡeɪt/| n.|步态，步法|A manner of walking or moving on foot.|Her gait was slow and deliberate. 她的步态缓慢而从容。|Example: The horse's gait was smooth and rhythmic. 这匹马的步态平稳而有节奏。|Synonyms: walk, stride, bearing, carriage|Antonyms: - |noun
