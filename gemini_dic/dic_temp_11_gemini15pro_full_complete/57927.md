drink:/drɪŋk/ | n. v. | 饮料，喝酒 |  A liquid that is swallowed for nourishment or enjoyment. To swallow a liquid. |  I like to drink orange juice in the morning. 我喜欢早上喝橙汁。  She drank a whole bottle of water. 她喝了一整瓶水。 | 近义词：beverage, sip, gulp, swallow | 反义词： abstain | 词性:名词,动词

drink:/drɪŋk/ |  v. | 喝 | To consume liquid. | He drank all the milk. 他喝光了所有的牛奶. | 近义词：imbibe, consume, swallow | 反义词： abstain | 词性:动词
