despot's:/ˈdespɑts/ |n.|暴君的，专制者的| belonging to a despot; characteristic of a despot. |The despot's cruelty was legendary. 暴君的残暴是传奇性的。|Synonyms: tyrant's, dictator's, autocrat's|Antonyms: democrat's, leader's|Possessive noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *despote*, from Old French *despote*, from Late Latin *despotes*, from Ancient Greek *δέσποτης* (despótēs) meaning "master, lord".
