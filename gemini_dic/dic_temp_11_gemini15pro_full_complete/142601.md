Example 2:  Osteolysis around the implant was a significant complication. 植入物周围的骨溶解是一个严重的并发症。|近义词：bone resorption, bone loss|反义词：bone formation, ossification|词性：名词

Etymology: osteo- (Greek, bone) + -lysis (Greek, loosening, dissolution)
