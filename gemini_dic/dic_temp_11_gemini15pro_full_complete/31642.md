chatrooms:/ˈtʃætˌruːmz/ /ˈtʃætˌrʊmz/
noun
聊天室
A place, especially on the internet, where people can exchange messages in real time.
They met in online chatrooms dedicated to their shared hobby. 他们在致力于他们共同爱好的在线聊天室里相遇。
The chatrooms are monitored for inappropriate language. 聊天室会被监控，以防出现不恰当的语言。
synonyms: chat groups, forums, message boards
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "face-to-face meeting")
