ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dynyng rome*, equivalent to *dining* + *room*.  *Dining* comes from Old French *disner* (to dine), from Vulgar Latin *disjūnāre* (to break one's fast), from Latin *dis-*, *jējunāre* (to fast). *Room* comes from Old English *rūm*.
