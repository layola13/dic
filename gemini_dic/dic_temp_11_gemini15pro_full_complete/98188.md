inseam:/ˈɪnsiːm/| n.|内缝；内侧骨缝；裤裆长|The inseam of a pair of trousers is the seam that runs from the crotch to the bottom of the leg. 裤子的内缝是从裆部到裤腿底部的缝线。|Example 1:  I need trousers with a 32-inch inseam. 我需要一条内缝长32英寸的裤子。

Example 2: The inseam of these jeans is too short for me. 这条牛仔裤的内缝对我来说太短了。|Synonym: inside leg|Antonym: outseam|noun
