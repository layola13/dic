gash:/ɡæʃ/| n. v. |深长的伤口，划伤；砍，切开|A long, deep cut or wound. To make a long, deep cut in something. |The tree had a deep gash in its trunk.  树干上有一道很深的伤口。 He gashed his leg on a sharp rock. 他的腿被一块锋利的岩石划伤了。|近义词：cut, laceration, wound, slash|反义词:heal, mend|词性:名词,动词

Etymology: From Old French *gasche ("scar"), probably a variant of Old North French *gache ("notch, flaw"), from Frankish *gaska, from Proto-Germanic *gaskaz ("split, gap, opening, crack"). Cognate with Middle Dutch gasche ("cut, notch"), German Gasche ("notch, mortise, scarf joint").
