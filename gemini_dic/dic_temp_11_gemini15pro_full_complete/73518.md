foreshop: /ˈfɔːrʃɒp/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrʃɑːp/ (US) | n. | 前店，尤指小杂货店; (理发店的)等候室 | A small shop, especially one selling groceries or other small items, typically located at the front of a larger building or in a separate small building. Also, a waiting room in a barber shop. |  例句：He bought a newspaper at the foreshop. 他在小杂货店买了一份报纸。

例句：The men waited in the foreshop for their haircuts. 男人们在理发店的等候室等着理发。| 近义词：shop, store, newsagent, kiosk | 反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以根据语境选择，例如：warehouse 仓库, backroom 后屋) | 词性: 名词
