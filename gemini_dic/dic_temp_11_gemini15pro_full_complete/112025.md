long-sightedly:/ˌlɒŋˈsaɪtɪdli/ /ˌlɔːŋˈsaɪtɪdli/| adv. |远见卓识地，有远见地|In a way that considers the future and its consequences.|He invested his money long-sightedly, ensuring a comfortable retirement.|近义词：prudently, wisely, farsightedly|反义词：short-sightedly, myopically|词性:副词

词源:
From long-sighted + -ly.  Long-sighted itself comes from long + sighted.  "Sighted" relates to the ability to see, and "long" in this context refers to seeing far (into the future).
