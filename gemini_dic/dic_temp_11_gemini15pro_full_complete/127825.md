nematode:/ˈnɛməˌtoʊd/ (US), /ˈnɛmətəʊd/ (UK)| n. |线虫；蛔虫|A worm of the large phylum Nematoda, such as a roundworm or threadworm.|例句：The nematodes are causing damage to the crops. 这些线虫正在损害庄稼。

例句：Many nematodes are parasites of plants or animals. 许多线虫是动植物的寄生虫。|近义词：roundworm, threadworm, eelworm|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a biological organism)|词性:noun
