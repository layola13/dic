desiredly:/dɪˈzaɪərəbli/ | adv. | 理想地，如所希望地 | In a desired or desirable manner; preferably. |  She desiredly obtained a position at the prestigious company. 她如愿以偿地在 prestigious 公司获得了一个职位。| It would be desiredly to finish the project before the deadline.  最好在截止日期前完成这个项目。 | 近义词：preferably, ideally, favorably | 反义词：undesirably, unfavorably | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "desirable" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Desirable" comes from the verb "desire" (from Old French "desirer"), ultimately from Latin "desiderare" (to long for, wish for).
