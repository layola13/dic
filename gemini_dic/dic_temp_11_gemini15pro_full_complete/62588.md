enfeeble:/ɪnˈfiːbl/ |v. |使衰弱；使无力|To make weak or feeble. |The illness enfeebled him for weeks.  这种病使他虚弱了好几个星期。|Synonyms: weaken, debilitate, incapacitate, devitalize |Antonyms: strengthen, invigorate, empower|verb


enfeeble:/ɪnˈfiːbl/ |adj. (rare, obsolete) |虚弱的；无力的|Weak or feeble.|An enfeebled old man. 一个虚弱的老人。|Synonyms: weak, frail, debilitated, infirm|Antonyms: strong, robust, vigorous|adjective (rare, obsolete)
