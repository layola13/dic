legion:/ˈliːdʒən/| n. |军团，众多，大量|A large group of soldiers, or a vast number of people or things.|The Roman legions conquered much of Europe. 罗马军团征服了欧洲的大部分地区.|Synonyms: multitude, host, throng, army, mass|Antonyms: few, handful, minority|noun

legion:/ˈliːdʒən/| adj. |大量的，众多的|Numerous, vast.|Her fans were legion. 她的粉丝众多。|Synonyms: numerous, countless, myriad|Antonyms: few, scarce|adjective
