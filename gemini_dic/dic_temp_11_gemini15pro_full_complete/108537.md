laze:/leɪz/
verb
懒散；无所事事
To spend time idly or in a relaxed way.
He lazed by the pool all afternoon. 他整个下午都在泳池边懒洋洋地度过。
近义词: lounge, loaf, relax, idle
反义词: work, toil, hustle
词性: verb

laze:/leɪz/
noun
一段懒散的时光
A period of idleness or relaxation.
We enjoyed a long laze on the beach. 我们在海滩上享受了一段悠闲的时光。
近义词: relaxation, rest, downtime
反义词: work, activity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lasen, from Old English *lǣs, from Proto-Germanic *laisaz (“weak, sluggish”). Cognate with Norwegian las (“sluggish”), Danish lad (“lazy”). More at late.
