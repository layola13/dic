marginate:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪt/ | adj. | 有边缘的；边缘化的 | having a margin |  The marginate leaves of the plant were a striking feature.  这种植物有边缘的叶子是一个显著的特征。| synonyms: bordered, edged | antonyms: central, core | adjective
