gazetteer: /ˌɡæzɪˈtɪr/ | n. |地名词典；地名索引|A geographical dictionary or directory. |例句：I consulted the gazetteer to find the exact location of the village. 我查阅了地名词典以找到该村庄的确切位置。

例句：The gazetteer provided detailed information about the region's topography. 这本地名索引提供了关于该地区地形的详细信息。|近义词：atlas, directory, index|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From French *gazette*, newspaper + -eer. Ultimately from Venetian *gazeta*, name of a small Venetian coin, the price of a newspaper.
