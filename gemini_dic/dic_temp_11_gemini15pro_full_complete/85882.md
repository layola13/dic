headbutt:/ˈhedˌbʌt/| v. n.|用头撞击；头槌|To strike someone with the head, usually in the forehead area.|He headbutted the other player and got a red card.  他用头撞了另一名球员，得到了一张红牌。|近义词：butt, ram|反义词: embrace, caress|词性:verb (动词), noun (名词)

Etymology:
From head + butt (verb), meaning "to strike with the head or horns." The word "butt" in this sense comes from the Old French word "boter," meaning "to strike, push, thrust."
