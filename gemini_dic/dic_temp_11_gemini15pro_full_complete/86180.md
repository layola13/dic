hearty:/ˈhɑːrti/ /ˈhɑːrdi/| adj.|衷心的，热情的；健壮的；丰盛的|Showing warm friendliness or enthusiasm;  (of a person) strong and healthy;  (of a meal) large and satisfying.|例句：She gave us a hearty welcome. 她热情地欢迎我们。|近义词：warm, enthusiastic, sincere, vigorous, robust, substantial, generous|反义词：cold, unenthusiastic, insincere, weak, frail, meager|词性:形容词

hearty:/ˈhɑːrti/ /ˈhɑːrdi/| n.|精力充沛的人；热心人|A hearty and enthusiastic person.|例句：He was one of the hearties of the village. 他是村里最热心的人之一。|近义词：enthusiast, extrovert|反义词：introvert, recluse|词性:名词
