impaired:/ɪmˈpeərd/| adj.|受损的，削弱的|Weakened or damaged.  Having a disability of a specified kind.|例句：His hearing was impaired by the loud noise. 他的听力被巨大的噪音损害了。|近义词：damaged, weakened, hampered, hindered, compromised|反义词:improved, enhanced, strengthened|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *imparāre, from in- + parāre ‘prepare’.  Originally meant "made worse," later "diminished in strength, function or ability."
