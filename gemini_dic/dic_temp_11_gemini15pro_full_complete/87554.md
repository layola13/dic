heteronereis:/ˌhɛtərəˈnɪəriːs/
noun
异沙蚕体
The reproductive form of certain polychaete worms, characterized by morphological changes, especially in the parapodia, adapted for swimming and swarming.
Many marine polychaetes, such as the Palolo worm, have a heteronereis stage. 许多海洋多毛类动物，例如帕罗洛虫，都有异沙蚕体阶段。
synonyms:epitoke, epitokous form
antonyms:none
n.
