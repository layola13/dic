freighted: /ˈfreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 充满的；担心的；焦虑的 | Filled with a particular atmosphere or tone, often negative; burdened; worried. | The atmosphere in the room was freighted with tension. 房间里的气氛充满了紧张。|Synonyms: laden, charged, burdened, anxious, worried | Antonyms: unburdened, carefree, lighthearted | adjective

freighted: /ˈfreɪtɪd/ | v. | 装货于；使充满；使担忧 | (past tense of freight) To load with goods for transportation; to fill with something, especially something unpleasant or negative; to cause to feel worried. | The ship was freighted with cargo. 这艘船装满了货物。 | Synonyms: load, burden, charge, worry | Antonyms: unload, unburden, relieve | verb
