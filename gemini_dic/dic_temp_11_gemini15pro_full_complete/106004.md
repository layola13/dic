knar:/nɑːr/
词性:n.
中文翻译:节, (尤指树木的) 瘤, 木节
英文解析:A knot in wood, especially one that is large and distorted.
例句:The old oak tree was covered in knars.  老橡树上布满了节疤。

例句:The gnarled branches of the tree reached towards the sky.  树木多节的树枝伸向天空。
近义词:knot, burl, node
反义词:smoothness
