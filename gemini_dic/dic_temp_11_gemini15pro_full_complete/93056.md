idolatrous:/aɪˈdɑːlətrəs/ | adj. | 偶像崇拜的，盲目崇拜的 | Characterized by or given to the worship of idols; excessively devoted or admiring. |  The king's idolatrous practices angered the priests. 国王的偶像崇拜行为激怒了祭司们。|  Her idolatrous devotion to her favorite band bordered on obsession. 她对她最喜欢的乐队的盲目崇拜近乎痴迷。 | 近义词:worshipping, adoring, revering, venerating | 反义词: iconoclastic, irreverent | 词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French idolatros, via late Latin from Greek eidōlolatrēs, from eidōlon ‘idol’ + latreia ‘worship’.
