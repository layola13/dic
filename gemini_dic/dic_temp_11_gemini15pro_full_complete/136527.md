nonruling:/ˌnɒnˈruːlɪŋ/
adjective
非统治的，非执政的
Not ruling; not pertaining to or characteristic of rulers.
The nonruling members of the royal family often engage in philanthropic work.  非统治的皇室成员经常从事慈善工作。
The nonruling party criticized the government's handling of the economy.  非执政党批评政府对经济的处理方式。
Synonyms: non-governing, powerless
Antonyms: ruling, governing
