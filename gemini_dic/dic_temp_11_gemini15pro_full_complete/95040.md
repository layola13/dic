inactivated: /ɪnˈæktɪveɪtɪd/
| adj. | 失活的，灭活的 | made inactive or no longer effective | The inactivated virus is used in the vaccine.  灭活病毒用于疫苗中。| synonyms: deactivated, neutralized, disabled | antonyms: activated, active, live | adjective

inactivate: /ɪnˈæktɪveɪt/
| v. | 使…失活，使…无效 | to make something inactive or no longer effective |  Heating the serum will inactivate the enzymes.  加热血清会使酶失活。 | synonyms: deactivate, neutralize, disable | antonyms: activate, enable | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From in- (not) + activate (to make active).  "Activate" ultimately comes from Latin *activātus, past participle of *activāre, from actus "act, deed,"  from agere "to do, drive, lead, act."
