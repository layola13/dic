arights:/əˈraɪts/ | n. |权利；正确的事情| Something that is due to anyone by just claim, legal guarantees, moral principles, etc.; that which justly belongs to one.|  He stood up for his arights. 他捍卫自己的权利。| 近义词: rights, entitlements, prerogatives | 反义词: wrongs, injustices | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Arights" is a non-standard plural form of "right" (in the sense of "entitlement" or "that which is due").  It likely arose through dialectal or informal usage. The standard plural is "rights."
