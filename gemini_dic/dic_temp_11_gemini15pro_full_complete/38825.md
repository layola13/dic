conepatl:/koʊˈneɪpɑːtl/| n. |墨西哥甜筒; 一种仙人掌果实|A type of cactus fruit native to Mexico, often used to make ice cream and other desserts.  It is the fruit of the prickly pear cactus.|Example 1:  The conepatl ice cream was refreshing on a hot day.  墨西哥甜筒冰淇淋在炎热的天气里令人神清气爽。

Example 2: She bought some fresh conepatl at the market. 她在市场上买了一些新鲜的墨西哥甜筒。|Synonyms: prickly pear fruit, tuna (fruit), xoconostle (for a sour variety) |Antonyms: N/A|noun
