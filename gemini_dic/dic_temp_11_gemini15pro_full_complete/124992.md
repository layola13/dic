muggy:/ˈmʌɡi/| adj.|闷热的，潮湿的|Characterized by warm, moist, and oppressive weather, often with little or no breeze.|It was a muggy day, and the air felt thick and heavy. 天气闷热，空气感觉又闷又重。|The muggy weather made it difficult to sleep. 闷热的天气让人难以入睡。|近义词：humid, sticky, sultry, close|反义词：arid, dry, crisp, fresh|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From mug¹ + -y¹; ultimately imitative.  Relating to the feeling of oppressive closeness in the air.
