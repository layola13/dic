deflux:/diːˈflʌks/ | n. | 下流；向下流动；流出物 |  A flowing down; a downward flow, especially of humors. The discharge of a fluid or humor from a natural opening or sore. | The deflux of rheum from his nose was constant. 他鼻子里的粘液不断流出。 | 近义词：discharge, flow, effusion | 反义词：influx | 词性: 名词

deflux:/diːˈflʌks/ | v. |  (使)向下流动；(使)流出 | To flow down; to cause to flow down. |  The medicine caused his sinuses to deflux.  这种药使他的鼻窦流出分泌物。 | 近义词：drain, discharge | 反义词：inflow | 词性: 动词
