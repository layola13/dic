balaustine:/ˈbæləˌstaɪn/ /ˈbæləˌstiːn/| adj. |石榴红的，深红色的|Of a deep red color, like that of a pomegranate.|She wore a balaustine gown to the gala. 她穿着石榴红的礼服参加了晚会。|Synonyms: crimson, scarlet, ruby |Antonyms: pale, light|adjective
