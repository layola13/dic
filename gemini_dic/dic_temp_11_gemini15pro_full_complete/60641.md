elected:/ɪˈlɛktɪd/| adj.|当选的，被选出的|Chosen by vote.|The newly elected president will take office next month. 新当选的总统将于下个月就职。|Synonyms: chosen, appointed, selected|Antonyms: unelected, defeated|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēlēctus*, past participle of *ēligere* ("to choose out, pick out, select"), from *ē-* ("out") + *legere* ("to choose, gather, read").
