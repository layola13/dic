bodywork: /ˈbɒdiwɜːk/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiwɜːrk/ (US)
noun
车身修理；车身；身体护理
The therapeutic manipulation of the muscles and other soft tissues of the body.  Also, the structure or repair of vehicle bodies.
例句：The car's bodywork was badly damaged in the accident. 这辆车的车身在事故中严重受损。

例句：She specializes in a type of bodywork called deep tissue massage. 她专攻一种叫做深层组织按摩的身体护理。

近义词：carrosserie, coachwork (for vehicles), massage, therapy (for body)

词性: noun
