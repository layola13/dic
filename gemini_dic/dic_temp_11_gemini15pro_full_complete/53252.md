direful:/ˈdaɪərfəl/| adj.|可怕的，悲惨的|Causing or involving great fear or suffering; dreadful.|The survivors told a direful tale of their escape from the sinking ship. 幸存者讲述了他们从沉船中逃生的可怕故事。|近义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, horrific, appalling|反义词：wonderful, excellent, delightful|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *direful, from dire + -ful.  Ultimately from Latin dīrus (“terrible, awful, ill-omened”).
