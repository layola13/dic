naturalistically:/ˌnætʃrəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 自然主义地；逼真地 |  In a way that is based on natural appearances or descriptions and not imagined or invented; in a way that is true to life. | The artist painted the scene naturalistically, capturing the light and shadows perfectly.  这位艺术家自然主义地描绘了这个场景，完美地捕捉了光线和阴影。|  realistically, authentically, truly, faithfully | artificially, synthetically, unnaturally | adverb
