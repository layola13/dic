flagrate:/ˈfleɪɡreɪt/| v. |燃烧；爆发；变得炽热|To burn or burst into flames; to become suddenly and intensely active.|The scandal flagrated across the headlines.  这桩丑闻在头条新闻中爆发开来。|
The dry forest flagrated after a lightning strike.  闪电击中后，干燥的森林燃烧起来。|近义词：blaze, burn, erupt, flare|反义词：extinguish, quench|词性:动词

词源:
From Latin *flagratus*, past participle of *flagrare* "to burn, blaze," from *flagro* "I burn, blaze."
