flashbulb: /ˈflæʃˌbʌlb/ | n. |闪光灯泡；闪光记忆| A flashbulb is a small, electrically powered lamp designed to produce a single, intense flash of light, typically for taking photographs. It can also refer to a vivid, clear memory of an emotionally significant moment or event. |  I have a flashbulb memory of where I was when I heard about 9/11. 我对听到9/11事件时我在哪里有一个清晰的记忆。 | synonyms: flash, flash lamp, photographic flash | antonyms: continuous light | noun
