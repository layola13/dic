counterclaims:/ˈkaʊntərkleɪmz/
noun
反诉，反驳，抗辩
A claim made to offset another claim, especially in a legal action.
The defendant filed counterclaims against the plaintiff.  被告对原告提出了反诉。
The company issued a statement refuting the counterclaims. 公司发表声明驳斥了这些反驳。
synonyms: countercharges, counterarguments, rebuttals, defenses
antonyms: claims, allegations, accusations
n.
