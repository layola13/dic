maxim:/ˈmæksɪm/| n.|格言；箴言；座右铭|A short, pithy statement expressing a general truth or rule of conduct.|例句： "Actions speak louder than words" is a well-known maxim. “行动胜于雄辩”是一句众所周知的格言。|例句：He lived by the maxim "early to bed, early to rise". 他遵循“早睡早起”的座右铭生活。|近义词：proverb, adage, aphorism, saying|反义词:|词性:noun
