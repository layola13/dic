outrowing:/ˌaʊtˈroʊɪŋ/
verb
胜过划船,在划船比赛中胜过
to row faster or better than
例句：He outrowed his competitors in the race. 他在比赛中胜过了他的对手。

例句：She easily outrowed the other boats. 她轻松地划得比其他船快。

近义词：outpace, surpass, beat, defeat
反义词：lose to

词性：v.
