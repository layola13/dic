histolytic:/ˌhɪstəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|组织溶解的，破坏组织的|Causing the dissolution or destruction of tissues.  Relating to or causing histolysis.|例句：The histolytic enzyme caused the breakdown of the surrounding tissue. 组织溶解酶导致周围组织的分解。|例句：The venom of certain snakes has histolytic properties.  某些蛇的毒液具有溶解组织的特性。|近义词：cytolytic, tissue-destructive|反义词：histogenetic, tissue-building|词性:形容词

词源:histo- (tissue) + -lytic (dissolving)  来自希腊语 "histos" (tissue) 和 "lytikos" (capable of dissolving).
