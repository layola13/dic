harmonium:/hɑːrˈmoʊniəm/ | n. |簧风琴，脚踏式风琴 |A keyboard instrument resembling a small organ, with metal reeds blown by air forced from bellows worked by pedals. |  The harmonium played a mournful tune. 簧风琴演奏了一曲悲伤的调子。

The church service was accompanied by the soft sounds of a harmonium. 教堂礼拜伴随着簧风琴柔和的声音。 | 近义词:reed organ, organ, melodeon | 反义词: None (乐器通常没有直接的反义词) | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *harmonium*, from Greek *harmonia* ("harmony")
