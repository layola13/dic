divestments:/daɪˈvɛstmənts/| n.|撤资，剥离|The act of selling off or disposing of assets, such as stocks, bonds, or property.  This can be done for various reasons, including financial gain, risk management, or ethical considerations.|Example 1: The company announced a series of divestments to focus on its core business.  该公司宣布了一系列撤资以专注于其核心业务。

Example 2:  The university's divestments from fossil fuel companies sparked a debate on campus.  该大学从化石燃料公司撤资引发了校园辩论。|近义词：sales, disposals|反义词:acquisitions, investments|词性:名词
