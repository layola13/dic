disfiguring:/dɪsˈfɪɡərɪŋ/ | adj. | 毁容的，丑陋的，有损外观的 | Spoiling the appearance of something or someone, especially their face. | The accident left him with a disfiguring scar.  这场事故给他留下了一个毁容的疤痕。 | Synonyms: deforming, marring, mutilating, scarring | Antonyms: beautifying, enhancing, improving | adjective

disfigure:/dɪsˈfɪɡər/ | v. | 毁容，损毁…的外观 | To spoil the appearance of something or someone, especially their face. | The fire badly disfigured the building.  大火严重损毁了这座建筑的外观。 | Synonyms: deform, mar, mutilate, scar | Antonyms: beautify, enhance, improve | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + figure (shape, form).  Ultimately from Latin dis- and figura.
