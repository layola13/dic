centrobaric:/ˌsɛntrəˈbærɪk/
adjective
重心的，与重心有关的
Relating to the center of gravity or center of mass.
The centrobaric method is used to calculate the center of gravity of complex shapes.  重心法用于计算复杂形状的重心。
The engineer calculated the centrobaric position of the aircraft. 工程师计算了飞机的重心位置。
近义词: center-of-gravity, center-of-mass
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially peripheral)
词性: adjective
