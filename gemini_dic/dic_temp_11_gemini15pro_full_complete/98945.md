inter-choking:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊkɪŋ/
adjective
相互窒息的; 互相阻塞的
The term "inter-choking" describes a situation where two or more things are blocking or obstructing each other, often in a way that restricts flow or movement. It can be used literally or figuratively.
The overgrown vines were inter-choking, preventing sunlight from reaching the smaller plants below.  茂盛的藤蔓相互缠绕，阻挡了阳光照射到下面的较小植物。
Their arguments became so heated and convoluted, they ended up inter-choking any productive discussion.  他们的争论变得如此激烈和复杂，最终阻碍了任何富有成效的讨论。
近义词:intertwined, entangled, obstructing, blocking
反义词:separated, disentangled, clearing, freeing
词性:adjective

词源:  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Choking" comes from the Old English word "ceocian," meaning "to suffocate."  The combined meaning is "choking each other" or "choking between/among."
