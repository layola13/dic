emboguing:/ɪmˈboʊɡɪŋ/
verb
陷入困境；使陷入泥沼
The car became embogued in the mud. 汽车陷入了泥潭。
The project became embogued in a series of delays. 该项目陷入了一系列的延误中。
近义词:miring, bogging down, stagnating
反义词:extricating, freeing, liberating
词性:v.

embogued:/ɪmˈboʊɡd/
adjective
陷入困境的；陷入泥沼的
The embogued vehicle had to be towed out. 陷入泥潭的车辆不得不被拖出来。
The company was embogued in debt. 公司深陷债务之中。
近义词:mired, bogged down, stuck
反义词:free, liberated, extricated
词性:adj.

词源: From en- + bog, meaning "in a bog."
