anticigarette:/ˌæntiˈsɪɡəret/ | adj. | 反吸烟的，反对吸烟的 | Opposed to smoking or the use of cigarettes. |  The anticigarette campaign aimed to educate young people about the dangers of smoking. 反吸烟运动旨在教育年轻人吸烟的危害。|  Example 2:  He had a strong anticigarette stance, refusing to even be around smokers. 他有强烈的反吸烟立场，甚至拒绝与吸烟者在一起。 | Synonyms: antismoking, nonsmoking | Antonyms: pro-smoking, prosmoking | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix) + cigarette (noun)

"anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against" or "opposite."
"cigarette" comes from French "cigarette," diminutive of "cigare," from Spanish "cigarro."
