medullin:/ˌmɛˈdʌlɪn/| n.|肾上腺髓质素|A hormone produced in the kidney and other tissues that has vasodilatory effects and other biological actions.|例句：Medullin levels are elevated in patients with heart failure. 肾衰竭患者的肾上腺髓质素水平升高。

例句：Medullin is being investigated as a potential therapy for pulmonary hypertension. 肾上腺髓质素正在被研究作为肺动脉高压的潜在疗法。|近义词：adrenomedullin (alternative spelling), vasodilator|反义词:vasoconstrictor|词性:noun
