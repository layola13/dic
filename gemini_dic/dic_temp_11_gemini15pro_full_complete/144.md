abase:/əˈbeɪs/ /əˈbeɪs/ | vt. | 降低，贬低，使卑下 | To lower in rank, prestige, or esteem; to humble or degrade. | He abased himself before the king, begging for mercy. 他在国王面前卑躬屈膝，乞求怜悯。

He felt abased after failing the important exam.  考试不及格后，他感到很羞愧。

近义词：humiliate, degrade, demean, lower, subdue | 反义词：elevate, exalt, praise, aggrandize | 词性：动词
