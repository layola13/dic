hawkbill:/ˈhɔːkbɪl/| n.|玳瑁，鹰嘴龟，玳瑁壳|A hawkbill is a sea turtle with a sharply pointed beak-like mouth, similar to a hawk's beak.  It is known for its beautiful shell, which is used to make jewelry and other decorative items. | The hawkbill turtle is critically endangered due to poaching for its shell. 玳瑁龟由于其龟壳的偷猎而极度濒危。

The craftsman carved a beautiful comb from a piece of hawkbill.  工匠用一块玳瑁壳雕刻了一把美丽的梳子。|近义词：tortoiseshell turtle, carey|反义词:|n.
