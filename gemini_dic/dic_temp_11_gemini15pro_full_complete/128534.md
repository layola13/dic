/nɛksts/ | n. | 下一个，接下来 | The next step is to gather more data. 下一步是收集更多数据。 |  Example: What comes next? 接下来是什么？| synonyms: following, subsequent | antonyms: previous, preceding | noun

/nɛksts/ | adj. | 下一个的，接下来的 | The next train leaves in 30 minutes. 下一班火车30分钟后出发。 | Example:  Who is the next batter? 谁是下一个击球手？| synonyms: following, subsequent | antonyms: previous, preceding | adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *nexte*, from Old English *nīehst* (“nearest, next”), superlative of *nēah* (“near”). Cognate with Dutch *naast* (“next to”), German *nächst* (“next”), Gothic *𐌽𐌴𐍈𐍅𐌰* (nēhw).
