imprisoning:/ɪmˈprɪzənɪŋ/
verb
监禁，关押
The act of putting someone in prison or confining them.
They were imprisoned for their beliefs. 他们因其信仰而被监禁。
confining, incarcerating, jailing
releasing, freeing
