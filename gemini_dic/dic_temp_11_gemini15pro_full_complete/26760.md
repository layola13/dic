caecilian:/siːˈsɪliən/| n. |蚓螈|A legless amphibian resembling a worm or snake, typically burrowing and found in tropical regions.|例句：The caecilian burrowed into the soft earth. 蚓螈钻进了松软的泥土。

例句：Caecilians are often mistaken for snakes. 蚓螈经常被误认为是蛇。|近义词：amphibian,蚓螈|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps reptile or mammal based on differences)|词性:noun
