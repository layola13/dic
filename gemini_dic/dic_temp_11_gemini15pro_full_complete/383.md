abiogenetic:/ˌeɪbaɪoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非生物成因的，自然发生的 | Relating to abiogenesis; not originating from living organisms.  |  Example: Scientists are studying abiogenetic theories of the origin of life. 科学家们正在研究关于生命起源的非生物成因理论。

Example:  The abiogenetic formation of amino acids is a key step in understanding the early development of life. 氨基酸的非生物形成是理解生命早期发展的关键一步。 | 近义词: abiotic, inorganic | 反义词: biogenic, biotic | 词性: adjective


词源:  From a- (not) + bio- (life) + genetic (relating to origin).  来自 a- （非） + bio- （生命） + genetic （与起源相关的）。
