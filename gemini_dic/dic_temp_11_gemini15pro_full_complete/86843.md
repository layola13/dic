hemerobid:/hɪˈmɛrəbɪd/ | n. adj. | 褐蛉，褐蛉科的 | A lacewing insect of the family Hemerobiidae.  Also used as an adjective to describe something related to this family of insects. |  例句：The hemerobid larva is a voracious predator of aphids. 褐蛉幼虫是蚜虫的贪婪捕食者。

例句：The entomologist specialized in hemerobid taxonomy.  这位昆虫学家专攻褐蛉科的分类学。 | 近义词：lacewing, brown lacewing | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hēmeróbios*  "living for a day,"  from *hēméra* "day" + *bios* "life".  This refers to the perceived short lifespan of the adult insect.
