apocalyptically:/əˌpɑːkəˈlɪptɪkli/
adverb|末世般地；灾难性地；启示性地|in a way that relates to the end of the world, or a great disaster, or revelation.|The world seemed to be changing apocalyptically. 世界似乎正在发生翻天覆地的变化。|Synonyms: catastrophically, disastrously, ruinously|Antonyms:  beneficially, favorably, positively|词性: 副词

apocalyptically:/əˌpɑːkəˈlɪptɪkli/
adverb|末世般地；灾难性地；启示性地|Relating to or resembling an apocalypse; prophetic of devastation or ultimate doom.|He spoke apocalyptically about the future of the economy. 他对经济的未来做出了灾难性的预言。|Synonyms: catastrophically, disastrously, ruinously|Antonyms:  beneficially, favorably, positively|词性: 副词
