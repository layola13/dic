diehard:/ˈdaɪhɑːrd/| n. adj.|顽固分子，死硬派；顽固的，死硬的|A person who strongly opposes change or who continues to support something that most people have abandoned.  Also used as an adjective to describe such a person or their beliefs.|例句：He's a diehard fan of the team, even though they haven't won a game in years. 他是球队的铁杆球迷，即使他们多年来一场比赛都没赢过。

例句：She has a diehard belief in traditional medicine. 她对传统医学有着坚定的信念。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, resolute, inflexible, tenacious|反义词：flexible, adaptable, open-minded|词性:名词，形容词
