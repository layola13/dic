downrightly:/ˌdaʊnˈraɪtli/ | adv. | 完全地，彻底地 |  In a thorough and complete manner; absolutely. | She was downrightly rude to the waiter. 她对服务员非常粗鲁。|  He downrightly refused to help. 他断然拒绝帮忙。 | 近义词: absolutely, completely, totally, utterly, entirely | 反义词: partially, slightly, somewhat | 词性:副词

词源: From "downright" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Downright" itself comes from "down" (indicating completeness or intensity) + "right" (originally meaning "straight" or "direct").
