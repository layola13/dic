defiant:/dɪˈfaɪənt/| adj.|挑衅的，蔑视的，不服从的|Showing resistance or disobedience; boldly challenging or opposing.|She gave him a defiant look. 她挑衅地看了他一眼。|The protesters stood defiant against the police. 抗议者们对抗警察，毫不畏惧。|近义词：rebellious, resistant, disobedient, challenging|反义词：obedient, submissive, compliant, yielding|词性:形容词

defiant:/dɪˈfaɪənt/| n.|违抗者，挑战者|(Rarely used) A person who defies or challenges authority.|He was a defiant of social norms. 他是一个社会规范的挑战者。|The defiants were punished severely. 违抗者受到了严厉的惩罚。|近义词：rebel, insurgent, dissident|反义词：conformist, follower|词性:名词
