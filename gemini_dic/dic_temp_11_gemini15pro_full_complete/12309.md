ascribable:/əˈskraɪbəbl/
adjective
可归因的，可归于...的
capable of being ascribed or attributed to a specified cause, source, or origin.
The success is ascribable to hard work.  这个成功归因于努力工作。
The decline in sales is ascribable to several factors. 销售额下降可归因于多种因素。
synonyms: attributable, imputable, creditable, assignable
antonyms: unascribable, unattributable
