lambaste:/læmˈbeɪst/ /læmˈbæst/| vt.|严厉批评，痛斥|To criticize (someone or something) harshly.|The coach lambasted the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。|Synonyms: criticize, censure, condemn, denounce, castigate, excoriate|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, extol|verb

lambaste:/læmˈbeɪst/ /læmˈbæst/| n.|严厉批评，痛斥 (较少用作名词)|A harsh criticism.|He delivered a furious lambaste to the board of directors. 他向董事会发起了猛烈的抨击。|Synonyms: criticism, censure, condemnation, denunciation, castigation, excoriation|Antonyms: praise, commendation, laudation, extolment|noun
