noncollective:/ˌnɒnkəˈlɛktɪv/
adjective
非集体的，个体的
Not collective; individual.
例句：Noncollective bargaining is the process by which a single employee negotiates their terms of employment with their employer. 非集体谈判是指单个雇员与其雇主谈判其雇佣条款的过程。

例句：The artist preferred a noncollective exhibition, showcasing only their own works.  这位艺术家更喜欢非集体展览，只展示自己的作品。

近义词：individual, separate, independent
反义词：collective, group, joint
词性：adjective
