mullis:/ˈmʌlɪs/ | n. | 马利斯（姓氏） | Mullis is a surname, most famously associated with Kary Mullis, the American biochemist who invented the polymerase chain reaction (PCR) technique. |  Kary Mullis won the Nobel Prize. 卡里·马利斯获得了诺贝尔奖。 | 近义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 反义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 词性: 名词


mull:/mʌl/ | v. | 仔细考虑，反复思考 | To think about (something) deeply and at length. | He mulled over the problem for days. 他对这个问题思考了好几天。|  She mulled the offer before accepting it.  她在接受这份工作前仔细考虑了一番。| 近义词: ponder, consider, contemplate, deliberate | 反义词: ignore, dismiss, disregard | 词性: 动词
