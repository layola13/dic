nonabstainer:/ˌnɒnəbˈsteɪnər/ | n. | 不戒酒者；不节制者 |  A person who does not abstain from something, especially alcohol. | He's a nonabstainer and enjoys a glass of wine with dinner. 他不戒酒，喜欢晚餐时喝一杯葡萄酒。 |  drinker, imbiber, indulger | abstainer, teetotaler | noun

Etymology->
From non- + abstainer.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," and abstainer comes from the verb "abstain," which means to refrain from something, especially alcohol.  "Abstain" ultimately derives from the Latin word "abstinere," composed of "abs" (from) + "tenere" (to hold).
