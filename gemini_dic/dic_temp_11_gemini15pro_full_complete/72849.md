folker:/ˈfoʊkər/| n.| 民谣歌手；民间音乐爱好者|A person who performs or enjoys folk music.|例句：The folker played a beautiful ballad on his acoustic guitar.  这位民谣歌手用原声吉他演奏了一首优美的民谣。

例句：The festival attracted folkers from all over the country.  这个节日吸引了来自全国各地的民谣爱好者。|近义词：folk musician, folksinger|反义词:|noun
