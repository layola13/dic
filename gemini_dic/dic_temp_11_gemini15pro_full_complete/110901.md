lipase:/ˈlaɪpeɪs/ /ˈlaɪpeɪz/| n. |脂肪酶|An enzyme produced by the pancreas and other tissues that hydrolyzes fats into fatty acids and glycerol. |Pancreatic lipase is essential for the digestion of fats. 胰脂肪酶对脂肪的消化至关重要。| The lipase in saliva helps to begin the process of fat digestion.唾液中的脂肪酶有助于启动脂肪消化过程。|synonyms: esterase| antonyms: inhibitor| noun
