hedges:/ˈhedʒɪz/ /ˈhɛdʒɪz/| n.|树篱；障碍；防备措施；套期保值|A row of bushes or small trees planted close together, typically to form a boundary.|The garden is surrounded by a thick hedge. 花园被厚厚的树篱包围着。|synonyms: barrier, fence, screen, windbreak|antonyms: opening, gap|noun


hedges:/ˈhedʒɪz/ /ˈhɛdʒɪz/| v.|用树篱围住；避免正面回答；限制；套期保值|To enclose or bound with a hedge; to avoid giving a direct answer; to limit or restrict; to protect oneself against financial loss.|He hedged his bets by investing in a variety of stocks. 他通过投资各种股票来对冲风险。|synonyms: evade, avoid, equivocate, qualify|antonyms: confront, face, commit|verb
