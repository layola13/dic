continuously:/kənˈtɪnjuəsli/ | adv. | 连续地，不断地 | Without interruption; constantly. | The machine has been running continuously for three days.  这台机器已经连续运转三天了。

The rain fell continuously for hours. 雨连续下了几个小时。


近义词: constantly, incessantly, perpetually, uninterruptedly
反义词: intermittently, sporadically, occasionally
词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *continuosus, from Latin continuus (“holding together, uninterrupted”).
