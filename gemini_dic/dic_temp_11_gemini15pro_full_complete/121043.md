misconducting:/ˌmɪskənˈdʌktɪŋ/
adjective
行为不端的，管理不善的
Behaving or managing something badly, especially in a way that is dishonest or illegal.
The investigation found evidence of serious misconducting by several police officers.  调查发现几名警官存在严重不当行为的证据。
Several employees were dismissed for misconducting themselves at the office party. 几名员工因在办公室聚会上的不当行为而被解雇。
synonyms:  wrongdoing, malpractice, misbehavior, impropriety
antonyms:  good conduct, propriety, ethical behavior
词性: adjective
