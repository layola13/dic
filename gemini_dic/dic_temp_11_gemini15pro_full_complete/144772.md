over-effort:/ˌoʊvərˈɛfərt/| n.|过度努力，用力过猛|An act of exerting excessive effort, often leading to exhaustion or injury.  The athlete's over-effort resulted in a pulled hamstring.|例句：He suffered from burnout due to over-effort at work. 他因为工作过度劳累而精疲力竭。

例句：The over-effort in lifting the heavy box caused him back pain.  抬那个重箱子用力过猛导致他背痛。|近义词：overexertion, strain, overwork|反义词:under-exertion, relaxation|词性:noun
