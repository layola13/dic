boldish:/ˈboʊldɪʃ/| adj.|略显大胆的，有点鲁莽的|Somewhat bold; slightly impudent or forward.  Having a degree of boldness, but not excessively so.  Approaching boldness but not quite there.|She made a boldish move by quitting her stable job to start her own business. 她辞去稳定的工作去创业，这是一个略显大胆的举动。|近义词：adventurous, daring, audacious, spirited, forward|反义词：timid, shy, reserved, cautious, hesitant|词性:形容词
