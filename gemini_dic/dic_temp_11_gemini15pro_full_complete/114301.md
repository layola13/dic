mainstay:/ˈmeɪnsteɪ/| n.|支柱，主要依靠，中流砥柱|A mainstay is a key element or part of something that provides essential support. It often refers to a person or thing that is central to the stability or success of something else.|Tourism is the mainstay of the local economy. 旅游业是当地经济的支柱。|synonyms: cornerstone, pillar, support, anchor, linchpin|antonyms: weakness, vulnerability|noun

mainstays:/ˈmeɪnsteɪz/| n.|支柱，主要依靠，中流砥柱（复数）|The plural form of "mainstay," referring to multiple key elements or parts providing essential support.|Exports are the mainstays of the national economy. 出口是国民经济的支柱。|synonyms: cornerstones, pillars, supports, anchors, linchpins|antonyms: weaknesses, vulnerabilities|noun
