divisible:/dɪˈvɪzəbl/| adj.|可分的，可整除的|Capable of being divided, usually with no remainder.|The number 10 is divisible by 2 and 5.  10可以被2和5整除。|
|Example: 12 is divisible by 3. 12可以被3整除。|
|Example: Any even number is divisible by 2. 任何偶数都可以被2整除。|
|Synonyms: dividable, factorable|
|Antonyms: indivisible, prime (when referring to numbers)|
|Word type: Adjective|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *divisibilis*, from Latin divisus (past participle of dividere "to divide") + -ibilis "-ible".  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *deyw- "to separate".
