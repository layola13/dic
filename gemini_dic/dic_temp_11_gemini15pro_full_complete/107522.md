landers:/ˈlændərz/| n.|登陆者；着陆器；登陆部队|People or spacecraft that land on a surface, especially another planet or the moon.  It can also refer to troops that disembark from ships onto land.|The landers successfully touched down on the lunar surface.  着陆器成功地在月球表面着陆。|Synonyms:  lunar module, landing craft, descent module|Antonyms:  takeoff craft, launch vehicle|noun
