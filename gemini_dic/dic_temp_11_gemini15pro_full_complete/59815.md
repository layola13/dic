ectal:/ˈɛktəl/
adjective
外胚层的；体外的
Relating to the ectoderm (the outermost layer of cells in an embryo).  Situated or occurring on the outside.
The ectal layer of the embryo develops into the skin and nervous system. 胚胎的外胚层发育成皮肤和神经系统。
Ectal surfaces are exposed to the environment. 外胚层表面暴露于环境中。
近义词: outer, external, exterior
反义词: internal, inner, endodermal
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *ektos* (outside) + -al (suffix forming adjectives). 源于希腊语 *ektos*（外部）+ -al（构成形容词的后缀）。
