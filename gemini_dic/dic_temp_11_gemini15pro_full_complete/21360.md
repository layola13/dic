blossom:/ˈblɒsəm/ (UK) /ˈblɑːsəm/ (US)| n. vi. |花朵；开花；兴旺，发展|A flower or a mass of flowers, especially on a fruit tree or bush; the state of flowering.  The process of developing or maturing.|The cherry tree is covered in blossom. 櫻花樹上開滿了花。|近义词：bloom, flower, flourish|反义词：wither, fade, decline|noun

blossom:/ˈblɒsəm/ (UK) /ˈblɑːsəm/ (US)| vi. |开花；兴旺，发展|To produce or yield blossoms; to develop or mature successfully.|The apple trees blossomed in the spring. 蘋果樹在春天開花。|近义词：bloom, flourish, thrive|反义词：wither, fade, decline|verb
