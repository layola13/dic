fallen:/ˈfɔːlən/ (US) /ˈfɔːlən/ (UK)| adj. | 堕落的；倒下的；落下的；阵亡的 | past participle of the verb "fall."  Indicates a state of having fallen, either literally or figuratively. | The leaves had fallen from the trees. 树叶从树上落了下来。| The fallen hero was remembered for his bravery.  这位牺牲的英雄因其英勇而被人们铭记。|近义词：dropped, toppled, collapsed, deceased (if referring to a person), ruined|反义词：risen, standing, upright, living|词性:形容词
