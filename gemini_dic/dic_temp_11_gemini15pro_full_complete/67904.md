face-offs:/ˈfeɪsˌɔːfs/ /ˈfeɪsˌɑːfs/
noun
对峙，对抗
a direct confrontation between two people or groups
例句：The hockey game began with a face-off in the center of the rink.  冰球比赛以在溜冰场中央的争球开始。

例句：The presidential debate was a tense face-off between the two candidates. 总统辩论是两位候选人之间紧张的对抗。
synonym: confrontation, showdown, clash
antonym: agreement, compromise, cooperation
n.
