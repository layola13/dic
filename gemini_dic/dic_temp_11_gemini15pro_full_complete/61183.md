eludes:/ɪˈluːdz/
verb
逃避，躲避
To escape the understanding or grasp of; to baffle.
The cause of the illness eludes researchers.  这种疾病的病因令研究人员感到困惑。
Synonyms: evades, avoids, escapes
Antonyms: confronts, faces, meets
词性: 动词

eludes:/ɪˈluːdz/
verb
使…达不到
To fail to be attained or remembered by.
Victory eluded them.  胜利与他们失之交臂。
Synonyms: escapes, misses
Antonyms: achieves, attains
词性: 动词
