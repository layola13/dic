forman:/ˈfɔːrmən/| n.|工头，领班|A worker who is in charge of a group of other workers, especially in a factory or on a building site.|The forman directed the crew to start pouring the concrete. 工头指示工人开始浇筑混凝土。|近义词：supervisor, overseer, boss, leader|反义词: worker, subordinate|noun
