dispender:/dɪˈspɛnsər/| n.|分配器，施予者，药剂师|A device that dispenses a specific amount of something, such as a liquid or powder.  A person who distributes or administers something.|例句：The soap dispenser was empty. 肥皂分配器空了。

|例句：He worked as a dispenser in a pharmacy. 他在药店当药剂师。|近义词：distributor, vendor, giver, provider|反义词：receiver, collector|noun


dispense:/dɪˈspɛns/| v.|分配，施予，免除，配药|To distribute or provide something, often in measured amounts. To administer (medication). To exempt or release someone from a duty or obligation.|例句：The machine dispenses snacks and drinks. 这台机器分发零食和饮料。

|例句：The doctor dispensed the medication to the patient. 医生给病人配了药。|近义词：distribute, administer, provide, allocate|反义词：collect, gather, withhold|verb
