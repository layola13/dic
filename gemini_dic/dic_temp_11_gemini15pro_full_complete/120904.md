miscaptioned:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃənd/
adjective
配有错误说明的；贴错标签的
incorrectly captioned; having a wrong caption
The photo was miscaptioned as a picture of the president, when it was actually his bodyguard. 这张照片被错误地标注为总统的照片，实际上是他保镖的照片。
mislabeled, wrongly captioned
correctly captioned
adjective
