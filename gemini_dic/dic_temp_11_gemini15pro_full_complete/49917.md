denationalised:/diːˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/
adjective
国有化被取消的；私有化的
Having been changed from being owned and controlled by the government to being privately owned and controlled.
The denationalised railway system is now more efficient.  私有化后的铁路系统现在效率更高了。
Synonyms: privatized, deregulated
Antonyms: nationalized, state-owned
词性: adjective


denationalise:/diːˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/
verb
取消国有化；私有化
To change (an industry or company) from being owned and controlled by the government to being privately owned and controlled.
The government plans to denationalise the postal service. 政府计划将邮政服务私有化。
Synonyms: privatize, deregulate
Antonyms: nationalize, state-own
词性: verb
