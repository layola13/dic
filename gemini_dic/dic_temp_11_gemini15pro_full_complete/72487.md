fluorated:/ˈflʊərɪˌteɪtɪd/
adjective
氟化的；加氟的
Treated or infused with fluorine.
例句：Fluorated water helps prevent tooth decay.  氟化水有助于预防蛀牙。

例句：Many plastics are fluorinated to increase their resistance to chemicals and heat. 许多塑料经过氟化处理以增强其耐化学性和耐热性。

近义词：fluoridized
反义词：non-fluorinated
词性: adjective
