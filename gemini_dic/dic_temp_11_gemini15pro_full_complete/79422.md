glissette:/ɡlɪˈsɛt/| n. |滑行; 滑行轨迹; 滑行曲线|A curve traced by a point moving under specified conditions.|The glissette of the airplane's descent was steep and rapid. 飞机下降的滑行轨迹陡峭而迅速。|Example: The artist created a beautiful glissette by dragging a charcoal stick across the canvas. 艺术家通过在画布上拖动一根炭笔，创造出一条美丽的滑行曲线。|Synonyms: glide, slide, slip|Antonyms: stick, adhere|noun
