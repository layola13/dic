inklike:/ˈɪŋklaɪk/
adjective
像墨水的；墨水般的
Resembling ink in color, consistency, or other properties.
The dark, inklike substance stained the tablecloth.  深色的、像墨水一样的物质弄脏了桌布。
The squid released an inklike cloud into the water.  鱿鱼在水中释放了一团像墨水一样的云雾。
Near synonyms: inky, inky-black
Antonyms: colorless, transparent
词性: 形容词
