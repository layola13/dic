kitesurfing:/ˈkaɪtsɜːfɪŋ/
n.
风筝冲浪
The sport of riding on a board that is propelled across water by a large kite.
例句：Kitesurfing is a thrilling water sport. 风筝冲浪是一项刺激的水上运动。

例句：She went kitesurfing in Hawaii. 她去夏威夷玩风筝冲浪了。

近义词：kiteboarding
反义词: (No direct opposite)
词性: noun


kitesurfing:/ˈkaɪtsɜːfɪŋ/
v.
进行风筝冲浪运动
To engage in the sport of kitesurfing.
例句：He loves kitesurfing on windy days.  他喜欢在大风天玩风筝冲浪。

例句：They were kitesurfing near the coast. 他们在海岸附近进行风筝冲浪。


近义词：kiteboard
反义词: (No direct opposite)
词性: verb (gerund/present participle)
