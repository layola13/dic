做作;装腔作势
The quality or state of being artsy, often in a pretentious or affected way.  It implies an excessive or superficial display of artistic or creative interests.
Her Instagram feed is full of artsinesses, like pictures of her latte art and blurry photos of sunsets. 她的Instagram动态充满了各种装腔作势的艺术感，比如她的拿铁拉花照片和模糊的日落照片。

Antonyms: genuineness, authenticity, sincerity
