lukewarm:/ˈluːkwɔːrm/ (US) /ˈljuːkwɔːm/ (UK)| adj.|微温的，不热情的|Moderately warm; tepid. Showing little enthusiasm.|The coffee was lukewarm and disappointing. 咖啡是温的，令人失望。|synonyms: tepid, mild, indifferent|antonyms: hot, boiling, enthusiastic|adjective
