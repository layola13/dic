chinchona: /tʃɪŋˈkoʊnə/ /sɪnˈkoʊnə/ | n. | 金鸡纳树；金鸡纳皮| A South American tree whose bark yields quinine, an alkaloid used to treat malaria. |  The bark of the chinchona tree is a source of quinine.  金鸡纳树的树皮是奎宁的来源。

The discovery of chinchona bark was a major breakthrough in the treatment of malaria. 金鸡纳树皮的发现是疟疾治疗方面的一个重大突破。| 近义词: quinine tree | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps diseases it treats like malaria?) | 词性: noun
