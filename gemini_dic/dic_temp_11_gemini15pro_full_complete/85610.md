haughtiest:/ˈhɔːtiɪst/ | adj. | 最傲慢的，最自大的 |  Superlative form of haughty: having or showing arrogant superiority to and disdain of those one views as unworthy. |  She gave him her haughtiest stare. 她傲慢地瞪着他。 | 近义词: arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, condescending | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *hautiest, *hawtiest, from Old French altis (*hautis), haltis (*halts), from halt (haut) (“high”), from Latin altus (“high, deep, tall”).  The -y suffix was added later by analogy with other adjectives.
