eulogist: /ˈjuːlədʒɪst/
n.
颂扬者，致悼词者
A person who delivers a eulogy, a speech praising someone who has died.
He was a gifted eulogist, moving the entire congregation to tears. 他是一位才华横溢的悼词家，让整个会众感动得流下了眼泪。
近义词: speaker, orator, praiser
反义词: critic, detractor
词性: noun

eulogize: /ˈjuːlədʒaɪz/
v.
颂扬，致悼词
To praise highly in speech or writing, especially someone who has died.
The mayor eulogized the fallen firefighter as a hero. 市长称赞这位牺牲的消防员是英雄。
近义词: praise, extol, laud, commend
反义词: criticize, condemn, denigrate
词性: verb

eulogistic: /ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪk/
adj.
颂扬的，赞美的
Expressing or containing eulogy; praising.
The eulogistic obituary celebrated her life and accomplishments.  这篇颂扬性的讣告赞扬了她的一生和成就。
近义词: laudatory, complimentary, praising
反义词: critical, derogatory
词性: adjective
