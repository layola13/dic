devious:/ˈdiːviəs/| adj.| 狡猾的；迂回的；不光明正大的| Showing a skillful use of underhanded tactics to achieve goals.| He took a devious route through back streets. 他绕着偏僻的街道走了一条迂回的路线。|Synonyms: cunning, sly, deceitful, underhand, indirect, roundabout|Antonyms: straightforward, direct, honest, aboveboard|adjective

devious:/ˈdiːviəs/| adj.| 狡猾的；迂回的；不光明正大的|Straying from a straight course; circuitous.|The river follows a devious course through the mountains. 这条河沿着一条曲折的路线穿过群山。|Synonyms: winding, meandering, rambling, tortuous|Antonyms: straight, direct|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēvius* "out of the way," from *dē* "off, away from" + *via* "way, road".
