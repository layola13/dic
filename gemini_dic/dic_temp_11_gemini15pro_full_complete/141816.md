orbiter:/ˈɔːrbɪtər/ (US) /ˈɔːbɪtə/ (UK)| n.|轨道飞行器；人造卫星|A spacecraft designed to orbit a celestial body, such as a planet or moon, without landing on it.|The orbiter sent back high-resolution images of Mars. 轨道飞行器发回了火星的高分辨率图像。|例句：
The Mars orbiter has been circling the planet for several years.  火星轨道飞行器已经绕火星运行好几年了。

The lunar orbiter mapped the moon's surface in detail.  月球轨道飞行器详细绘制了月球表面图。

|近义词：satellite, spacecraft|反义词: lander|词性: noun
