outseeking:/ˌaʊtˈsiːkɪŋ/
adjective
外求的；向外寻求的
Actively searching for something outside of oneself or one's usual environment.  Engaged in a quest or pursuit beyond familiar boundaries.
She has an outseeking personality, always looking for new experiences and adventures. 她有着外求的性格，总是寻求新的体验和冒险。
He adopted an outseeking approach to problem-solving, consulting experts from various fields. 他采用了一种外求的问题解决方法，咨询了来自不同领域的专家。
近义词: adventurous, exploring, inquisitive, searching
反义词: introspective, inward-looking, withdrawn
词性: adjective

词源:  A combination of "out" (meaning outside, beyond) and "seeking" (present participle of "seek," meaning to search for).  Relatively modern coinage, not found in older dictionaries.
