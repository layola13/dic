facings:/ˈfeɪsɪŋz/
n.
饰面，贴面，朝向
a layer of material covering part of a garment for decoration or strength; the direction in which something faces.
The velvet facings on the collar gave the coat a luxurious feel.  天鹅绒衣领贴面让这件外套很有奢华感。
The building has south-facing windows. 这栋楼的窗户朝南。
covering, lining, veneer, surface, aspect, orientation
back, rear, underside
