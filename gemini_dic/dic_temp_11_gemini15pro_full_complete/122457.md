mobilises:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzɪz/ /ˈməʊbəˌlaɪzɪz/| v. |动员，调动|To organize and prepare something, such as a group of people, for a particular purpose.|The government mobilised the army for war. 政府动员军队准备战争。|近义词：activate, assemble, deploy, marshal, rally|反义词: demobilize, disband|verb

mobilises:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzɪz/ /ˈməʊbəˌlaɪzɪz/| v. |动员，调动|To make something movable or capable of movement.|The doctor worked to mobilise the patient's stiff joints. 医生努力活动病人僵硬的关节。|近义词：activate, stimulate|反义词: immobilize|verb
