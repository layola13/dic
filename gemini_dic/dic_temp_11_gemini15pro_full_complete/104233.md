jurisconsult:/ˈdʒʊərɪskɒnsʌlt/ /ˈdʒʊrɪskənˌsʌlt/| n. | 法律顾问；法学家 | A jurisconsult is a legal expert, often one who gives advice on legal matters. |  The king sought the advice of his jurisconsult before declaring war. 国王在宣战之前征求了他的法律顾问的意见。| synonyms: legal expert, legal advisor, lawyer, jurist, counselor, attorney | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps layman, non-expert) | noun
