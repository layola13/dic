nonreviewability:/ˌnɒnˌrɪvjuːəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可复审性，不可审查性
The nonreviewability of the judge's decision caused frustration among the appellants.  法官的决定不可复审，这使得上诉人感到沮丧。
The principle of nonreviewability protects certain decisions from being challenged in court. 不可复审原则保护某些决定免于在法庭上受到质疑。
Synonym: finality, conclusiveness
Antonym: appealability, reviewability
noun

nonreviewable:/ˌnɒnˌrɪvjuːəbəl/
adj.
不可复审的，不可审查的
The arbitrator's decision is nonreviewable, except in cases of fraud. 仲裁员的决定是不可复审的，除非存在欺诈行为。
The contract contained a clause making certain disputes nonreviewable.  合同中包含一项条款，规定某些争议不可复审。
Synonym: final, conclusive, unappealable
Antonym: reviewable, appealable
adjective
