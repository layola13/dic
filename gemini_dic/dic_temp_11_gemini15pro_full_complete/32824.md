choke:/tʃoʊk/ (US) /tʃəʊk/ (UK)| v. n.|窒息，噎住；阻塞；抑制；（因紧张等）失常，哽咽；（植物）枯萎；减小，缩小（孔径等）|

*Verb:*

The smoke was thick and I started to choke. 浓烟很呛，我开始窒息。
He choked on a piece of bread. 他被一块面包噎住了。
The weeds choked the flowers in the garden. 杂草扼杀了花园里的花。
He was choking back tears as he spoke. 他说话时强忍着眼泪。
Fear choked his words. 恐惧让他哽咽。
She choked down her anger. 她抑制住了愤怒。
The engine choked when I tried to start it. 我试图启动引擎时，它熄火了。
He choked the dog with its own leash. 他用狗自己的皮带勒死了它。
The government choked off the supply of arms to the rebels. 政府切断了对叛军的武器供应。
The company choked the market by buying up all the available supplies. 这家公司通过收购所有可用物资来垄断市场。


*Noun:*

The carburetor choke is stuck. 化油器的阻风门卡住了。


近义词: suffocate, strangle, stifle, gag, suppress, inhibit, obstruct, clog|
反义词: breathe, unclog, release, express|
词性: verb, noun|
