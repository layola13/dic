malware:/ˈmælweər/ | n. | 恶意软件 | Software designed to damage or disable computer systems. |  My computer is running very slowly; I think it might have malware. 我的电脑运行非常缓慢；我想它可能感染了恶意软件。 | My antivirus software detected and quarantined the malware. 我的杀毒软件检测到并隔离了恶意软件。 | 近义词: virus, spyware, adware, Trojan horse | 反义词: antivirus software | 词性: noun
