co-determine:/ˌkoʊdɪˈtɜːrmɪn/
| v. |共同决定；共同参与决策|To decide or control something together with someone else.|The workers co-determine the company's future. 工人们共同决定公司的未来.|Synonyms: co-decide, share in decision-making|Antonyms: dictate, impose|Verb

co-determination:/ˌkoʊdɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/
| n. |共同决定；共同参与决策|The act of deciding or controlling something together with someone else.|Co-determination is an important part of German corporate governance. 共同决定是德国公司治理的重要组成部分。|Synonyms: joint decision-making, shared control|Antonyms: autocracy, dictatorship|Noun
