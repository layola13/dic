falcula:/ˈfæl.kjʊ.lə/ | n. | 小镰刀; 小刀; 爪; |A small, curved, sharp-pointed blade or claw, often used for cutting or tearing.  Derived from Latin "falx, falcis" meaning "sickle."|  The surgeon used a falcula to make the incision.  外科医生用小镰刀做了切口。|  The cat extended its falcula to catch the mouse. 猫伸出爪子抓老鼠。| synonyms: blade, knife, claw, talon | antonyms: blunt instrument | noun
