erysipelous:/ˌɛrɪˈsɪpələs/| adj.|丹毒的，患丹毒的|Relating to or affected with erysipelas, a bacterial infection of the skin.|Her face was erysipelous, red and swollen. 她的脸得了丹毒，又红又肿。|近义词:inflamed, infected|反义词:healthy, uninfected|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *erysipelatosus, from Greek *erusipelatōdēs, from erusipelas 'erysipelas'. Ultimately from Greek eruthros 'red' + pella 'skin'.
