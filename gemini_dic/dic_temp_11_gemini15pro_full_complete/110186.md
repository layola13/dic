lieutenant:/luːˈtɛnənt/ (US), /lɛfˈtɛnənt/ (UK)| n.|中尉；副职官员|A commissioned officer in the armed forces, ranking next below a captain.  A person who acts in place of or represents a superior.|The lieutenant led his platoon into battle. 中尉带领他的排投入战斗。|Synonyms: deputy, second-in-command, assistant|Antonyms: commander, chief, leader|noun
