off-the-beam:/ˌɒf ðə ˈbiːm/ /ˌɔːf ðə ˈbiːm/| adj. | 错误的，不正确的；偏离目标的 | Incorrect; mistaken; deviating from the intended course. |  The project went off-the-beam when the team leader resigned.  项目在团队领导辞职后偏离了方向。

The politician's speech was completely off-the-beam and failed to address the public's concerns. 这位政客的演讲完全跑题了，没有回应公众的关切。 | 近义词: wrong, incorrect, inaccurate, misguided, astray | 反义词: correct, accurate, on target, on track | 词性: 形容词
