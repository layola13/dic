lockage:/ˈlɒkɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkɪdʒ/ (US)| n.|水闸，船闸；通过船闸的费用；船闸通行费|The lockage fee for the Panama Canal is substantial. 巴拿马运河的船闸通行费很高。|Synonyms: lock dues, canal toll|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "open passage" in some contexts)|noun
Etymology: From "lock" (referring to a canal lock) + "-age" (suffix forming nouns denoting a charge or fee).  "Lock" ultimately comes from Old English *loc, meaning a fastening or barrier.
