mauve:/moʊv/ (US), /məʊv/ (UK)| adj. n.|淡紫色，紫红色；淡紫色的东西|A pale purple colour.  Also, something of this colour.|例句：She wore a mauve dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件淡紫色的连衣裙去参加婚礼。

例句：Mauve is my favorite color. 淡紫色是我最喜欢的颜色。

近义词：lavender, lilac, violet|反义词: (No direct antonym for a color, could consider complementary colors like yellow-green)|词性: adjective, noun
