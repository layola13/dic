interquarter:/ˌɪntərˈkwɔːrtər/
adjective
季度间的；季度与季度之间的
Occurring between quarters (of a year).
Interquarter sales growth has been impressive this year. 今年季度与季度之间的销售增长令人印象深刻。
近义词: between quarters
反义词: intraquarter
词性: adjective
