dignifying:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
使高贵的，使有尊严的
Making something or someone seem worthy and impressive.
He gave her a dignifying burial, despite her crimes.  他给了她一个有尊严的葬礼，尽管她犯了罪。
The ceremony was a dignifying affair.  这个仪式是一个庄严的场合。
Synonyms: ennobling, elevating, honoring
Antonyms: degrading, humiliating, demeaning
词性: adjective

dignify something as something (将...尊称为...): They dignified the building as a "national treasure".  他们将这座建筑尊称为“国宝”。
dignify oneself (举止庄重): He always managed to dignify himself, even in the most difficult situations.  即使在最困难的情况下，他总是设法保持自己的尊严。
