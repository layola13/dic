hard-line:/ˈhɑːrdlaɪn/ | adj. n. |强硬的，强硬路线的 |Uncompromising and unyielding in one's views or actions. Refers to a strict, inflexible stance, especially in political or social contexts. |The government took a hard-line stance against the protesters. 政府对示威者采取了强硬立场。|Synonyms: unyielding, inflexible, uncompromising, firm, rigid, strict, tough |Antonyms: flexible, compromising, lenient, moderate, soft |adjective

hard-line:/ˈhɑːrdlaɪn/ | n. |强硬路线，强硬立场 |A firm, uncompromising policy or position. |The party's hard-line on immigration alienated many voters. 该党在移民问题上的强硬路线疏远了许多选民。|Synonyms: stance, position, policy, approach |Antonyms: compromise, concession |noun
