gluers:/ˈɡluːərz/| n.|粘合工；胶水使用者|People who use glue, especially in a professional capacity, are called gluers.  These individuals might work in factories assembling products, in bookbinding, or in various crafts.|The gluers in the factory worked quickly and efficiently. 工厂里的粘合工工作迅速高效。
The gluers carefully applied adhesive to each piece of wood. 粘合工小心地将粘合剂涂在每块木头上。|近义词：adhesive appliers, bonders, cementers|反义词: N/A|词性:Noun
Etymology: From "glue" + "-er" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a specified action).  "Glue" itself comes from Old French "glu" (birdlime), ultimately from Late Latin "glutem" (glue).
