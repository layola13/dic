osteoid:/ˈɒstiːɔɪd/| adj. n.|类骨质的，骨样组织|Resembling bone; the organic matrix of bone tissue which becomes mineralized.|例句：The osteoid is secreted by osteoblasts.  骨样组织由成骨细胞分泌。

例句：The biopsy revealed an excess of unmineralized osteoid. 活检显示未矿化的骨样组织过多。|近义词：osseous, bony|反义词: None|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From osteo- + -oid.  Osteo- comes from the Greek word "osteon" meaning "bone".  -oid is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "having the form of".
