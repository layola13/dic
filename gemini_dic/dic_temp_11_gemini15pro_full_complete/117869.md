melodious:/məˈləʊdiəs/ (UK) /məˈloʊdiəs/ (US)| adj.|悦耳的，旋律优美的|Producing or having a pleasant tune; musical.|Her voice was soft and melodious. 她的声音轻柔悦耳。|The melodious sounds of the birds filled the air. 鸟儿悦耳的歌声充满了空气。|近义词：tuneful, harmonious, musical, sweet-sounding|反义词：discordant, harsh, cacophonous|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English *melodious, from Old French melodieux, from Late Latin melōdiōsus (“melodious”), from Latin melōdia (“melody, song”) + -ōsus.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek μελῳδία (melōidía, “singing, chanting, a choral song”).
