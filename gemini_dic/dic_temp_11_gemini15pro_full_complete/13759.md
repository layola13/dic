audacities: /ɔːˈdæsɪtiz/ | n. |大胆；鲁莽；放肆的行为 (plural of audacity) |  Bold or daring acts, often considered disrespectful or rude. |  His audacities at the meeting shocked everyone. 他在会议上的放肆行为震惊了所有人。|  The sheer audacities of the plan were breathtaking. 该计划的绝对大胆令人叹为观止。 | synonyms: boldness, daring, nerve, temerity, impudence, effrontery | antonyms: timidity, cowardice, shyness, meekness | noun

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French audacite, from Latin audacitas, from audax, audac- ‘bold’.
