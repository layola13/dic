cytoid:/ˈsaɪtɔɪd/| adj.|类细胞的，细胞样的|Resembling a cell, especially in having a readily stainable nucleus surrounded by a small amount of cytoplasm.  This term usually refers to cells that resemble lymphocytes.|Cytoid cells were observed in the blood sample.|Synonyms: cell-like, lymphocytoid|Antonyms: None|Adjective
