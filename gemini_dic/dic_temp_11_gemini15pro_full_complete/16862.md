basihyal: /ˈbeɪsiˌhaɪəl/ | n. | 基舌骨 | The basihyal is a bone or cartilaginous element forming the base, or body, of the hyoid apparatus in most vertebrates. | The basihyal is a small, U-shaped bone in the frog. 基舌骨是青蛙体内一块小的U形骨。| The basihyal of the bird is elongated. 鸟类的基舌骨是细长的。 |  synonym: body of hyoid, hyoid bone (broadly) | antonym: N/A | noun
