notochordal:/ˌnoʊtəˈkɔːrdl/
adjective
脊索的；具有脊索的
Relating to or having a notochord, a flexible rod-like structure that forms the main support of the body in the embryos of all chordates and in the adult forms of certain lower chordates.
The notochordal process is crucial in the development of the nervous system. 脊索突在神经系统的发育中至关重要。
The lancelet retains its notochordal structure throughout its life. 文昌鱼终生保留其脊索结构。
Synonyms: chordal
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, can use terms describing lack of a notochord, such as "invertebrate")
词性: adjective
