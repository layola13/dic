injudicious:/ˌɪndʒʊˈdɪʃəs/| adj.|不明智的，欠考虑的，不审慎的|Not showing good judgment; unwise.|Her injudicious remarks caused a lot of offense. 她不明智的言论引起了很多人的反感。|Example: Investing all your savings in one stock is an injudicious decision. 把你所有的积蓄投资于一只股票是不明智的决定。|Synonyms: unwise, imprudent, ill-advised, impolitic|Antonyms: wise, prudent, judicious, sensible|adjective
