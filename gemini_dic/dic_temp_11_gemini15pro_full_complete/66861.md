/ɪkˈspɛktɪd/ | adj. | 预期的，预料的 | anticipated, foreseen, predicted |  The expected arrival time is 3 PM. 预计到达时间是下午3点。| anticipated, foreseen, predicted | unexpected, surprising | adjective

/ɪkˈspɛktɪd/ | n. | 被期待的人或事 | something that is expected | The expected was a victory, but they lost.  预期会胜利，但他们输了。| anticipation, prospect | surprise, shock | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exspectāre* (“to look out for, await, expect”), from *ex* (“out”) + *spectāre* (“to look”).  The spelling with “-e-” is a later modification.
