departs:/dɪˈpɑːrts/ /dɪˈpɑːrts/| v. |离开，出发，启程|To leave, especially to start a journey.|The train departs at 10:00 a.m. 火车上午10点出发。|The flight departs from London Heathrow. 这架航班从伦敦希思罗机场起飞。|近义词：leave, go, exit|反义词：arrive, come, return|词性：动词
Etymology: From Old French *departir, from Latin dēpartīrī (“to divide, distribute, separate, depart”), from dē (“from”) + partīrī (“to divide, share, distribute”).
