cystlike: /ˈsɪstˌlaɪk/
adjective
囊肿样的，像囊肿的
Resembling a cyst; having the characteristics of a cyst.  A cyst is a closed sac, having a distinct membrane and developing abnormally in a cavity or structure of the body. Cystlike formations can appear on various parts of the body and can be filled with fluid, air, or other substances.
