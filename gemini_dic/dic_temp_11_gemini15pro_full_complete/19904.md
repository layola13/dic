bins:/bɪnz/| n.|箱子，容器，垃圾箱|Large plastic bins are used for storage.  大型塑料箱用于存储。|The bins were overflowing with rubbish. 垃圾箱里堆满了垃圾。|containers, receptacles, boxes| |noun
Etymology: From Old English *binne (“crib, manger”), from Proto-Germanic *binnjō (“basket, crib”). Cognate with Dutch binde (“band, tie”), German Binde (“sling, bandage”).
