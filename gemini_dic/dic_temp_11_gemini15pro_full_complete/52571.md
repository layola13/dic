diffuses:/dɪˈfjuːzɪz/
| v.|扩散，传播，散布|spreads out over a large area; becomes less concentrated|The scent of flowers diffused through the garden. 花香弥漫在整个花园里。|近义词：spreads, disperses, dissipates|反义词：concentrates, gathers, collects|词性:动词

diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːs/
| adj.|弥漫的，扩散的，不清楚的|spread out over a large area; not concentrated; lacking clarity|The report was long and diffuse, making it hard to understand the main points.  这份报告冗长而散漫，很难理解要点。|近义词：spread out, widespread, rambling, vague|反义词：concentrated, concise, clear, focused|词性:形容词

diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːs/
| v.|扩散，传播，散布|(third-person singular simple present diffuses, present participle diffusing, simple past and past participle diffused)|To spread or cause to spread over a wide area or among a large number of people.|The speaker diffused his message of hope to the crowd. 演讲者向人群传播了他的希望之声。|近义词：spread, disseminate, disperse|反义词：concentrate, gather, collect|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin diffusus, past participle of diffundere "to pour out, spread, scatter," from dis- "out, apart" + fundere "to pour."
