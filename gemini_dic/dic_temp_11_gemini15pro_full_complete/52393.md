didactyl:/daɪˈdæktɪl/| adj. n.|二指的，二趾的；二趾动物|Having only two digits (fingers or toes) on each hand or foot.  Relating to or characteristic of such an animal.|The aye-aye is a didactyl primate. 指猴是一种二指灵长类动物。|The didactyl foot of the ostrich is a unique adaptation. 鸵鸟的二趾足是一种独特的适应性特征。|近义词:two-toed, two-fingered|反义词:polydactyl, pentadactyl|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek *di- "two" + *daktylos* "finger, toe".
