dissemblances:/dɪˈsɛmblənsɪz/
noun
不同, 差异
lack of resemblance or similarity
The dissemblances between the twins were striking, despite their identical genes. 这对双胞胎尽管基因相同，但差异却很明显。

The report highlighted the dissemblances between the two proposals. 报告强调了两个提案之间的不同之处。

近义词:differences, discrepancies, dissimilarities, variations
反义词:similarities, resemblances, likenesses
词性:n. (noun)
