ophiuroid:/ˈɒfiˌjʊərɔɪd/ /ˌoʊfiˈjʊrɔɪd/ | n. | 蛇尾纲动物，蛇尾 |A marine invertebrate animal with a flat, star-shaped body and five slender, flexible arms, belonging to the class Ophiuroidea. |  Example: The ophiuroid clung to the coral reef with its delicate arms.  蛇尾用它纤细的手臂紧紧抓住珊瑚礁。

Example:  Researchers studied the feeding habits of the ophiuroid. 研究人员研究了这种蛇尾的摄食习性。 | 近义词: brittle star | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
