cryer:/ˈkraɪər/| n.| 哭泣者，传令员，叫喊者 |A person who cries, especially habitually; a town crier. |The town crier announced the king's arrival.| 近义词: weeper, announcer, herald | 反义词: laugher | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English criour, from Anglo-Norman criour, from Old French criere, from crier (“to shout, cry out”), from Latin quiritare (“to call for help, scream, shriek”).
