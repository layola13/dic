joint-venturers:/ˌdʒɔɪnt ˈvɛntʃərərz/
n.
合资企业家，合营者
People or companies who participate in a joint venture.
Several joint-venturers collaborated on the massive construction project.

近义词:partners, collaborators, co-venturers
反义词:competitors, rivals
词性:名词(plural)


joint-venturer:/ˌdʒɔɪnt ˈvɛntʃərər/
n.
合资企业家，合营者
A person or company who participates in a joint venture.
The Chinese joint-venturer provided the land for the factory.
中方合资企业家提供了工厂用地。

近义词:partner, collaborator, co-venturer
反义词:competitor, rival
词性:名词(singular)


词源:
From "joint venture" + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns). "Joint venture" comes from "joint" (combined, shared) and "venture" (a risky or daring undertaking).
