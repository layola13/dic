flexiblenesses:/ˌflɛksɪˈbɪlɪtɪz/
n.
柔韧性，灵活性
the quality of being flexible; the ability to bend easily without breaking.
The flexiblenesses of the young gymnasts were impressive.  年轻体操运动员的柔韧性令人印象深刻。
近义词:pliability, suppleness, elasticity
反义词:stiffness, rigidity, inflexibility
词性:noun


Etymology:
From flexible + -ness.  Flexible comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin flexibilis, from Latin flectere ‘to bend’.
