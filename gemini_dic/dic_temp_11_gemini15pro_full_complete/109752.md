leviathanic:/ləˌvaɪəˈθænɪk/| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|Of, relating to, or resembling a leviathan; of immense size and power.  Resembling a sea monster in size, power, or appearance.|例句：The leviathanic cruise ship dwarfed the smaller vessels in the harbor.  这艘巨大的游轮使港口中的其他船只相形见绌。

例句：The company faced a leviathanic task in restructuring its operations.  公司面临着重组业务的艰巨任务。|近义词：gigantic, enormous, colossal, immense, massive|反义词：tiny, small, minuscule, diminutive|词性:adjective
词源:From Leviathan, a sea monster mentioned in the Bible, via Late Latin leviathan. Ultimately from Hebrew livyāthān.
