lanced:/lænst/
|v.|刺破，切开|pierced or cut open with a lancet or other sharp instrument.|The doctor lanced the boil to drain the pus. 医生用柳叶刀切开了疖子以排出脓液。|近义词：pierced, punctured, incised|反义词:sutured, healed|verb

lanced:/lænst/
|adj.|矛状的，披针形的|shaped like a lance; tapering to a point.|The lanced leaves of the plant were a vibrant green.  这种植物的披针形叶子是鲜绿色的。|近义词：pointed, tapered, spear-shaped|反义词:rounded, blunt|adjective
