brize:/braɪz/| v.|烤，烧烤 (通常指在炭火上)|To cook something by grilling it over coals, typically outdoors.|例句：They brized the chicken over an open fire. 他们在明火上烤鸡肉。

例句：We brized the sausages for our picnic. 我们为野餐烤了香肠。|近义词：grill, barbecue, roast|反义词:freeze, chill|词性：动词
