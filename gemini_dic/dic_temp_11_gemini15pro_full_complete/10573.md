apprehend:/ˌæprɪˈhend/ | v. | 理解；逮捕；担忧 | To understand something; to arrest someone; to fear something. |
The police apprehended the suspect. 警察逮捕了嫌疑人。

The student struggled to apprehend the complex theory.  这个学生努力理解这个复杂的理论。

I apprehend that there will be trouble. 我担心会有麻烦。 | 近义词：understand, grasp, seize, capture, arrest, fear, dread | 反义词：misunderstand, release, liberate, ignore | 词性: 动词

apprehended:/ˌæprɪˈhendɪd/ | adj. | 被逮捕的；感到焦虑的 | Arrested; feeling anxious or fearful. |  The apprehended criminal was taken to the police station. 被捕的罪犯被带到了警察局。

He felt apprehended about the upcoming exam. 他对即将到来的考试感到焦虑。 | 近义词：arrested, anxious, worried, fearful | 反义词：released, calm, relaxed | 词性: 形容词
