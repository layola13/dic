corradial:/kɔːˈreɪdiəl/
adjective
同径的，共径的
Having the same radius. Two circles are corradial if they have the same center and different radii. Two spheres are corradial if they have the same center and different radii.
The two corradial circles shared the same center point. 这两个同径的圆拥有同一个圆心。
Two corradial spheres are concentric. 两个同径的球面是同心的。
近义词: concentric, homocentric
反义词: eccentric
词性: adjective
