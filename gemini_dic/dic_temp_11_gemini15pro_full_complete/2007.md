actualize: /ˈæktʃuəlaɪz/ (US), /ˈæktʃʊəlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |实现，实行，将…付诸实施|To make something real or concrete; to put something into action or effect.|She finally actualized her dream of opening a bakery. 她终于实现了开面包店的梦想。|He struggled to actualize his vision for the company. 他努力将他对公司的愿景付诸实践。|近义词: realize, achieve, fulfill, complete|反义词: neglect, abandon, postpone|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *actualis* "active, practical," from Latin *actus* "an act, impulse, a doing," past participle of *agere* "to drive, lead, act, do."  The English word was formed from the adjective *actual* with the suffix *-ize*.
