ETYMOLOGY->
crowd (n.) + fund (v.)

crowd (n.): From Old English *crūd "crowd, throng," of uncertain origin.

fund (v.): From French *fonder "to found, establish, base," from Latin fundus "bottom, base, piece of land, farm."  The financial sense developed in the 18th century.
