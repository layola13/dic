committedness:/kəˈmɪtɪdnəs/
n.|承诺；奉献；献身；忠诚|The state of being committed or dedicated to a cause, activity, etc.  A strong sense of dedication and loyalty.|Her committedness to the project was admirable. 她对这个项目的奉献精神令人钦佩。|近义词：dedication, commitment, devotion, loyalty|反义词：apathy, indifference, disloyalty|词性:名词

Etymology:
From commit + -ed + -ness.  "Commit" comes from the Latin "committere," meaning "to join together, entrust, or perpetrate." "-ed" is a suffix forming the past participle of verbs. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
