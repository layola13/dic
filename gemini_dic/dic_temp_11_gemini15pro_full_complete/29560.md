Etymology:
From catching + -ness + -'s (possessive).  "Catching" originates from the Middle English "cacchen," from the Old North French "cachier," meaning "to chase, hunt, seize." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality.  "-'s" is the possessive marker.
