nonrepression:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈprɛʃən/
noun
非抑制，无抑制
The state or quality of not being repressed; absence of repression.
The nonrepression of emotions can lead to a healthier psychological state.  情绪的非抑制可以带来更健康的心理状态。
nonrepression of anger can be detrimental.  愤怒的非抑制可能是有害的。
Synonyms:  derepression, liberation, uninhibitedness
Antonyms: repression, suppression, inhibition
