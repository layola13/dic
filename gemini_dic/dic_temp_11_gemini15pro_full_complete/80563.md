goody-goody:/ˈɡʊdi ˈɡʊdi/| n.|伪君子|A goody-goody is someone who is self-righteously good. 伪君子是一个自以为是的好人。|Don't be such a goody-goody! 别那么装腔作势!  The goody-goodies were always complaining about the noise. 那些伪君子总是抱怨噪音。|近义词：prig, prude|反义词：rebel, scoundrel|词性:名词

词源:  A reduplication of "goody," an archaic term of address for a woman, especially a married woman of humble station, implying virtue or respectability.  由“goody”重复构成，“goody”是一个过时的称呼女性的词语，尤指地位低下的已婚妇女，暗示其贤惠或值得尊敬。  The reduplication creates a sense of disapproval and mockery. 重复则创造了一种不赞成和嘲弄的意味。
