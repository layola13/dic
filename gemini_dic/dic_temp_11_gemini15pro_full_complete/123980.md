mormoness:/ˈmɔːrmənnəs/| n.| 摩尔门性，摩尔门教徒的特质 |The degree to which someone embodies the perceived characteristics or principles of Mormonism.  It can refer to the adherence to doctrines, cultural practices, or a general sense of belonging within the Mormon community. |Her mormoness was evident in her commitment to family and community service. 她对家庭和社区服务的奉献体现了她的摩尔门教徒特质。|Synonyms: Mormon identity, Mormon character, Mormon spirit|Antonyms: secularism, worldliness|noun
