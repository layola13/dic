had:/hæd/
|v.|有(have的过去式和过去分词); 经历; 遭受; 允许; 必须; 应该| I had a dream last night. 我昨晚做了一个梦。|近义词：possessed, owned, experienced, underwent|反义词: lacked|verb

had:/hæd/
|aux. v.|（构成完成时）已经，曾经| I had finished my work before he arrived.  在他到达之前我已经完成了我的工作。|近义词: - |反义词: - |auxiliary verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I have a car. = 我有一辆车。
She has a beautiful voice. = 她有一副好嗓子。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am having a great time. = 我玩得很开心。
She is having dinner with her family. = 她正在和家人吃晚饭。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have had enough. = 我受够了。
He has had a cold for a week. = 他感冒一个星期了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been having trouble sleeping lately. = 我最近一直有睡眠问题。
They have been having meetings all day. = 他们开了一整天会。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I had a dog when I was young. = 我小时候有一条狗。
She had a headache yesterday. = 她昨天头痛。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was having lunch when he called. = 他打电话时我正在吃午饭。
They were having a party when the police arrived. = 警察到达时他们正在举行派对。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already had dinner when she arrived. = 她到达时我已经吃过晚饭了。
He had had a long day at work. = 他工作了一整天，很辛苦。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been having nightmares for weeks before I saw a doctor. = 我看医生前几周一直做噩梦。
They had been having financial problems for years. = 他们多年来一直有财务问题。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will have a party for my birthday. = 我会为我的生日举办一个派对。
She will have a new car next year. = 她明年将有一辆新车。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be having dinner with my family tomorrow. = 我明天将和家人一起吃晚饭。
They will be having a meeting next week. = 他们下周将举行会议。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next month, I will have had this car for five years. = 到下个月，我拥有这辆车就五年了。
She will have had her baby by then. = 到那时她将已经生了孩子。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been having piano lessons for ten years. = 到明年，我将已经上了十年钢琴课。
They will have been having discussions for months by the time a decision is made. = 到做出决定时，他们将已经讨论了几个月。



虚拟语气:
If I had had more time, I would have gone to the party. = 如果我有更多时间，我就会去参加聚会了。
I wish I had had more money. = 我希望我有更多钱。



被动语态 (一般过去时):
The decision had been made before the meeting. = 会议前决定已经做出了。
The car had been repaired before I picked it up. = 我取车之前，车已经修好了。


疑问句:
Did you have a good time? = 你玩得开心吗？
Had she finished her work? = 她完成工作了吗？


否定句:
I didn't have any money. = 我没有钱。
She hadn't seen him before. = 她以前没见过他。
