overconcerned:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˈsɜːrnd/| adj.|过分关心的，过分担心的|Excessively concerned or worried about something or someone.|He was overconcerned about his daughter's safety. 他过分担心女儿的安全。|近义词：anxious, solicitous, worried, apprehensive|反义词：indifferent, apathetic, neglectful|adjective
