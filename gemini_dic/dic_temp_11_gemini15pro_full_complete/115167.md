maniacal:/məˈnaɪək(ə)l/
adjective
疯狂的；狂热的；着魔的
exhibiting or suggestive of mania.
The killer had a maniacal laugh. 杀手发出疯狂的笑声。
近义词:insane, crazy, mad, lunatic, demented, deranged
反义词:sane, rational, calm
词性:adjective

maniacal:/məˈnaɪək(ə)l/
adjective
狂躁的；躁狂的
affected with or suggestive of mania.
His maniacal energy was both inspiring and exhausting. 他狂躁的精力既鼓舞人心又令人疲惫。
近义词:frenzied, frantic, wild, rabid, berserk
反义词:calm, peaceful, tranquil
词性:adjective
