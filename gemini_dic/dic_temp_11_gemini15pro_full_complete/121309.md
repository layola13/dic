misfolded:/ˌmɪsˈfoʊldɪd/ | adj. | 错误折叠的，折叠错误的 | Incorrectly folded, especially referring to proteins. |  Prions are misfolded proteins that can cause disease. 朊病毒是错误折叠的蛋白质，会导致疾病。| Misfolded proteins can accumulate in the brain. 错误折叠的蛋白质会在脑中累积。 | synonyms: malformed, deformed, distorted | antonyms: correctly folded, properly folded | adjective
