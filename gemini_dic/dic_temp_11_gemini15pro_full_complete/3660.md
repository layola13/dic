agglomerating:/əˈɡlɒməreɪtɪŋ/
verb
聚集，凝聚
forming or grouping into a mass or cluster.
The dust particles are agglomerating into larger clumps. 灰尘颗粒凝聚成更大的团块。
近义词: aggregating, accumulating, clustering, coalescing
反义词: dispersing, separating, scattering
词性: verb (present participle)

agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/
noun
聚集体，团块
a mass or collection of things gathered together.
The rock is an agglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物聚集而成的。
近义词: cluster, mass, collection, aggregate
反义词: scattering, dispersion
词性: noun

agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/
verb
聚集，凝聚
to gather into a mass or ball.
The snow agglomerated into large drifts. 雪堆积成大雪堆。
近义词: aggregate, accumulate, cluster, coalesce
反义词: disperse, separate, scatter
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *agglomeratus*, past participle of *agglomerare* "to wind into a ball," from *ad-* "to" + *glomus* "ball."
