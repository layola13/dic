PHRASE->
flow from: 源于，来自  Creativity flows from inspiration.  创造力源于灵感。
flow into: 流入，注入  The river flows into the ocean. 河流注入海洋。
flow with: 充满，富于  She flowed with confidence. 她充满自信。
go with the flow: 顺其自然  Just go with the flow and see what happens. 顺其自然，看看会发生什么。
ebb and flow:  潮起潮落;  盛衰，起伏  Life has its ebbs and flows. 人生有起有落。
