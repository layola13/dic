goal-line:/ˈɡoʊl laɪn/| n. |球门线|The line marking the end of the playing field in sports like soccer, rugby, and American football.  It is the line a team must cross to score a goal or touchdown.|The striker's shot just crossed the goal-line.  前锋的射门刚好越过了球门线。|Synonyms: goal line, end line, try line (rugby)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

goal-line:/ˈɡoʊl laɪn/| adj. |球门线的|Relating to or situated on the goal line.|It was a goal-line clearance that saved the game.  那是一次在球门线上的解围，拯救了比赛。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
