ethnological:/ˌɛθnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 人种学的，民族学的 | Relating to ethnology, the study of the characteristics of different peoples and the relationships between them. |  Ethnological research often involves fieldwork and participant observation. 人种学研究通常包括实地考察和参与观察。|  The museum has an impressive collection of ethnological artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了令人印象深刻的人种学文物。 | 近义词: anthropological, ethnographic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "ahistorical") | 词性: adjective
