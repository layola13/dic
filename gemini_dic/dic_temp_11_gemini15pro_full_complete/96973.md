infanthoods:/ˈɪnfəntˌhʊdz/| n. |婴儿时期（复数）|The state or period of being an infant; early childhood.  The plural form is less common but can be used to refer to the infant periods of multiple individuals or groups.|

例句：
Researchers are studying the impact of nutrition on infanthoods in different cultures.
研究人员正在研究营养对不同文化中婴儿时期的影响。


例句：
Their infanthoods were spent in poverty.
他们的婴儿时期是在贫困中度过的。


近义词：babyhood, infancy|
反义词:adulthood, maturity|
词性:noun|

词源: From infant + -hood.  Infant comes from the Latin infans, meaning "unable to speak."|
