abjuring:/əbˈdʒʊərɪŋ/
verb
正式放弃；发誓放弃；公开宣布放弃
the act of formally renouncing or rejecting something, often under oath
He abjured his former beliefs. 他放弃了他以前的信仰。
近义词：renouncing, forswearing, repudiating, recanting
反义词：embracing, affirming, upholding, endorsing
词性: 动词(present participle)


abjure:/əbˈdʒʊər/
verb
正式放弃；发誓放弃；公开宣布放弃
formally reject or disavow a formerly held belief, usually under oath
She abjured her allegiance to the king. 她放弃了对国王的效忠。
近义词：renounce, forswear, repudiate, recant
反义词：embrace, affirm, uphold, endorse
词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *abjuren, from Old French abjurer, from Latin abiūrāre (“to deny on oath”), from ab (“from, away from”) + iūrāre (“to swear”).
