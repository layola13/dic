despatching:/dɪˈspætʃɪŋ/
verb|派遣; 发送; 迅速处理; 杀死|The company is despatching goods to customers all over the world. 公司正在向世界各地的客户发送货物。|近义词：sending, shipping, dispatching, killing|反义词:receiving, keeping, delaying|v.

despatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/
noun|派遣; 发送; 迅速处理; (公文)急件|The despatch of the goods was delayed due to bad weather. 货物的发送由于天气恶劣而延误了。|近义词：sending, shipment, dispatch|反义词:receipt, delay|n.

despatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/
verb|派遣; 发送; 迅速处理; 杀死|The general despatched a messenger to headquarters. 将军派了一名信使去总部。|近义词：send, ship, dispatch, kill|反义词:receive, keep, delay|v.


Etymology:
From Old French *despechier, from des- "un-, out" + piece "piece".  The spelling with "i" is older but now less common, especially in British English.
