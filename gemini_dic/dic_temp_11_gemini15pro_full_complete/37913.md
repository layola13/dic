complemented:/ˈkɒmplɪmentɪd/ |ˈkɑːmplɪmentɪd/| adj. |补充的，补足的|Made complete or perfect by the addition of something else.|Her dress was complemented by a beautiful necklace. 她的连衣裙配上一条漂亮的项链，显得更加完美。|The wine perfectly complemented the meal.  这酒与这顿饭完美搭配。|近义词: supplemented, enhanced, improved|反义词: lacking, deficient|词性:形容词

complemented:/ˈkɒmplɪmentɪd/ |ˈkɑːmplɪmentɪd/| v. |补充，补足 (过去分词)|Past participle of "complement," meaning to add something to make it complete or perfect.|The chef carefully selected wines that complemented each dish. 厨师精心挑选了与每道菜相得益彰的葡萄酒。|The soft lighting complemented the romantic atmosphere. 柔和的灯光增添了浪漫的气氛。|近义词: supplemented, enhanced, improved|反义词: detracted from, diminished|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin complementum "that which fills up or completes," from Latin complere "to fill up, complete," from com- "together" + plere "to fill."
