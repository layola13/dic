malingered:/məˈlɪŋɡərd/| v. |装病；逃避责任|To pretend to be ill or incapacitated in order to avoid work or duty.|He malingered for weeks, claiming a back injury. 他装病好几个星期，声称背部受伤了。|The soldier malingered to avoid combat duty.  这个士兵为了逃避战斗任务而装病。|近义词：feign, sham, simulate, fake|反义词: persevere, attend, work|词性:动词

词源: From French *malingrer*, from *malingre* "sickly, ailing," of uncertain origin.
