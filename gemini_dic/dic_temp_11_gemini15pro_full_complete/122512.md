mockingbird:/ˈmɑːkɪŋbɜːrd/ (US) /ˈmɒkɪŋbɜːd/ (UK)| n.|嘲鸫|A North American bird known for its ability to mimic the songs of other birds.|The mockingbird perched on the branch and sang a medley of familiar tunes. 嘲鸫栖息在树枝上，唱着一连串熟悉的曲调。|Synonyms: mimic, imitator|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

mockingbird:/ˈmɑːkɪŋbɜːrd/ (US) /ˈmɒkɪŋbɜːd/ (UK)| adj. (rare, figurative)|嘲弄的，模仿的|Resembling or characteristic of a mockingbird, especially in its mimicking behavior.  |He gave a mockingbird laugh, echoing the speaker's words. 他发出了嘲弄般的笑声，模仿着演讲者的话。|Synonyms: mimicking, imitative|Antonyms: original, genuine|Adjective
