braggartly: /ˈbræɡərtli/ | adv. | 吹嘘地，自夸地 | In a boastful or arrogant manner.  Characterized by bragging. | He braggartly announced his victory, much to the annoyance of his competitors. 他吹嘘地宣布了他的胜利，这让他的竞争对手们很恼火。|  boastfully, arrogantly, vaingloriously | humbly, modestly | adverb

Etymology:
From braggart (n.) + -ly (adv. suffix).  "Braggart" comes from Middle French "bragard" meaning "boastful."
