hypersusceptibility:/ˌhaɪpər səˌsɛptəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 过敏性，超敏性 |  Increased susceptibility or sensitivity to a substance (such as a drug or antigen) or to an environmental influence.  An exaggerated reaction to a stimulus. | Example 1: The patient exhibited hypersusceptibility to the medication, developing a severe rash. 病人对药物表现出过敏性，出现了严重的皮疹。

Example 2:  Hypersusceptibility to certain allergens can lead to anaphylactic shock. 对某些过敏原的过敏反应会导致过敏性休克。 | Synonym: hypersensitivity, oversensitivity | Antonym: hyposensitivity, resistance | Noun
