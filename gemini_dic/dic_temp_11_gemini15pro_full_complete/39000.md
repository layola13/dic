confirmer:/kənˈfɜːrmər/ | n. | 确认者；证实者 |Someone or something that confirms something. | The lab results were the confirmer of his diagnosis. 实验室结果证实了他的诊断。|  The lawyer acted as a confirmer for the witness's testimony. 律师充当了证人证词的确认者。 |  validator, corroborator, verifier | denier, contradictor | noun

confirm:/kənˈfɜːrm/ | v. | 确认；证实；批准；使坚定 | Establish the truth or validity of something; state or prove the truth of; strengthen or make firm. |  Please confirm your attendance by email. 请通过电子邮件确认您的出席。 | The evidence confirmed our suspicions. 证据证实了我们的怀疑。 | validate, corroborate, verify, ratify, endorse | deny, refute, contradict | verb
