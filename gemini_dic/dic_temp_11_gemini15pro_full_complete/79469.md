gloat:/ɡləʊt/ (UK) /ɡloʊt/ (US)| vi. |幸灾乐祸，沾沾自喜|dwell on one's own success or another's misfortune with smugness and malicious pleasure.|例句：He gloated over his rival's failure. 他对竞争对手的失败幸灾乐祸。

例句：She couldn't resist gloating over her exam results. 她忍不住为自己的考试成绩沾沾自喜。

近义词：revel, crow, exult, rejoice (maliciously)|反义词：commiserate, sympathize|词性：动词
