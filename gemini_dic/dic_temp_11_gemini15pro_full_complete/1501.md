acetification:/əˌsiːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
醋化；酸化
The process by which alcohol is converted into vinegar by the action of bacteria.
例句：The acetification of wine can occur if it is exposed to air for too long.  如果葡萄酒暴露在空气中太久，就会发生醋化。

例句：The acetification process is essential for making vinegar. 醋化过程对于制作醋至关重要。

近义词：acidification, souring
反义词: alkalization, neutralization
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acétification*, from *acétifié*, past participle of *acétifier* "to acetify," from *acétique* "acetic" + *-fier* (from Latin *-ficare*).  Ultimately from Latin *acetum* "vinegar."
