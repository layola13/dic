disregardfully:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdfəli/
adv.
不尊重地，轻蔑地
showing a lack of respect
He disregardfully tossed the invitation aside. 他轻蔑地把邀请函扔到一边。
近义词：contemptuously, disdainfully, disrespectfully
反义词：respectfully, politely, courteously
词性：副词

disregard:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrd/
n.
漠视，忽视
the act of ignoring something or someone
Her disregard for the rules was astonishing. 她对规则的漠视令人震惊。
vt.
漠视，忽视
to ignore something or someone
He disregarded the doctor's advice. 他无视医生的建议。
近义词：ignore, neglect, overlook
反义词：heed, observe, respect
词性：名词，动词

词源:  From dis- "not" + regard "consideration, respect".  Ultimately from Old French regarder "to look at," from re- "back" + garder "to guard, keep."
