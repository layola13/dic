brame:/breɪm/| n. |渴望, 嘶鸣 |A loud, harsh cry, typically of a deer during the mating season.  A yearning or longing. |The stag let out a loud brame across the glen.  雄鹿在峡谷中发出一声响亮的嘶鸣。|近义词：bellow, roar, cry, yearn, long|反义词:whisper, silence, apathy|noun
brame:/breɪm/| v. |渴望, 嘶鸣 |To utter a loud, harsh cry, typically of a deer during the mating season. To yearn or long for. |The stags bramed throughout the autumn nights.  整个秋夜，雄鹿都在嘶鸣。  He bramed for a simpler life. 他渴望过上更简单的生活。|近义词：bellow, roar, cry, yearn, long|反义词:whisper, be quiet, be indifferent|verb
