nutritionists: /njuˈtrɪʃənɪsts/ | n. | 营养学家；营养师 | A person who studies or advises others on matters of food and nutrition in relation to health.  |  Many nutritionists believe breakfast is the most important meal of the day. 很多营养学家认为早餐是一天中最重要的一餐。| dieticians, food scientists | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun


Etymology:

From *nutrition* + *-ist*.  *Nutrition* comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin *nutritio*, *nutrion-*, from Latin *nutrire* ‘nourish’.  The suffix *-ist* denotes "one who does or makes, one who practices or is skilled in, one who believes in or advocates".
