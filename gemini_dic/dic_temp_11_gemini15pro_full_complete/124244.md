mossinesses:/ˈmɒsɪnəsɪz/
n.
苔藓覆盖的状态; 长满苔藓的状态
The state or quality of being mossy; a surface covered with moss.
The old stone walls were covered in mossinesses, giving the garden a mysterious atmosphere.  古老的石墙上长满了苔藓，给花园增添了神秘的气氛。
近义词: moss, moss growth
反义词: bareness, smoothness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From mossy + -ness.  Mossy ultimately comes from Old English *mos* (compare Dutch *mos*, German *Moos*).  The suffix *-ness* forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
