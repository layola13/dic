counselling: /ˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkaʊnslɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 咨询；辅导；忠告| The process of listening to someone and giving them advice about their problems. |She sought counselling after her divorce. 她离婚后寻求了咨询。| guidance, advice, therapy | dissuasion | noun
counsel: /ˈkaʊnsəl/ (UK) /ˈkaʊnsl/ (US)| n. v.| 劝告，忠告；法律顾问；商议；建议| Advice, especially that given formally. To give advice or guidance to (someone).| He gave me some good counsel. 他给了我一些很好的建议.  The lawyer counseled his client to plead guilty. 律师建议他的客户认罪。| advice, guidance, recommend | dissuade, discourage | noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "conseil"  (advice, counsel, council), from Latin "consilium" (consultation, deliberation, advice), from "consulere" (to consult).
