cannula:/ˈkænjuːlə/ (US), /ˈkænʊlə/ (UK)| n. |套管;插管|A thin tube inserted into a vein or body cavity to administer medicine, drain off fluid, or insert a surgical instrument.|The surgeon inserted a cannula into the patient's vein.  外科医生将套管插入病人的静脉。|Synonyms: catheter, tube|Antonyms: None|noun

cannulas:/ˈkænjuːləz/ (US), /ˈkænʊləz/ (UK)| n. |套管;插管（cannula的复数形式）|Plural form of cannula, referring to multiple thin tubes inserted into veins or body cavities.|The nurse prepared several cannulas for the IV drips. 护士准备了几个套管用于静脉滴注。|Synonyms: catheters, tubes|Antonyms: None|noun
