attrition:/əˈtrɪʃən/| n.|损耗，磨损，消耗;人员自然缩减|A gradual reduction in the number of employees through retirement, resignation, or other reasons, rather than through layoffs or other forms of involuntary termination.  A gradual weakening or wearing down.|例句：The company experienced a high rate of attrition due to low salaries. 由于工资低，公司员工流失率很高。

例句：The attrition of the enemy's forces was significant. 敌军的力量被显著削弱了。|近义词：depletion, erosion, weakening, decline|反义词:growth, increase, strengthening|词性:noun
