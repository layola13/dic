lambswool:/ˈlæmzˌwʊl/| n. |羊羔毛，羊毛衫|Soft wool from a lamb, often used for clothing.  The term can also refer to a garment made from this wool.|例句：She bought a lambswool sweater for the winter. 她买了一件羊毛衫过冬。

|例句：The lambswool blanket was incredibly soft. 羊羔毛毯子非常柔软。

|近义词：wool, fleece|反义词:synthetic fiber|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From lamb + wool.  "Lamb" comes from Old English *lamb, from Proto-Germanic *lambaz. "Wool" comes from Old English wull, from Proto-Germanic *wullo.
