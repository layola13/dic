organism:/ˈɔːrɡənɪzəm/ | n. |生物，有机体 |A living thing, such as an animal, plant, fungus, or bacterium, that can function independently. |例句：A single-celled organism. 单细胞生物。

例句：The pond is teeming with tiny organisms. 池塘里充满了微小的生物。 | 近义词：being, creature, living thing, life form | 反义词：inanimate object |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French organisme, from organe (“organ”), from Latin organum (“instrument, tool”), from Ancient Greek ὄργανον (órganon, “instrument, tool, organ”).
