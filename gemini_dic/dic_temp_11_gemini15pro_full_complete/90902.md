hunting-grounds:/ˈhʌntɪŋ ɡraʊndz/| n.|狩猎场，猎场|A place where animals are hunted.|The Serengeti is a famous hunting-ground for lions.  塞伦盖蒂是著名的狮子狩猎场。|Synonyms: hunting area, game preserve, hunting range|Antonyms: sanctuary, refuge|noun
