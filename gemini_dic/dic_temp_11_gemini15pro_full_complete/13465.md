atrociously:/əˈtroʊʃəsli/
adverb
极其恶劣地，糟糕地
in a terribly cruel and violent way; extremely badly or unpleasantly
The play was atrociously acted. 这部戏演得糟透了。

Her singing was atrociously off-key. 她的歌声跑调跑到可怕的程度。

近义词: terribly, awfully, dreadfully, appallingly, horribly, shockingly
反义词: wonderfully, excellently, superbly, magnificently
词性: adverb
