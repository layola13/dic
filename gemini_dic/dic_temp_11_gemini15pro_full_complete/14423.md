autumn: /ˈɔːtəm/ (British) /ˈɑːtəm/ (American)| n. |秋季，秋天|The season after summer and before winter, when the leaves change color and fall from the trees. |例句：The leaves turn beautiful colors in autumn. 秋天树叶的颜色变得很漂亮。

例句：Autumn is my favorite season. 秋天是我最喜欢的季节。

近义词：fall (American English)|反义词：spring|词性：名词
