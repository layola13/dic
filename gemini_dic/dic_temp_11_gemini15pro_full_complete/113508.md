m-learning:/ˈɛmˌlɜːrnɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɛmˌlɜːnɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |移动学习|Learning that takes place using mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets.|Many universities now offer m-learning resources to supplement traditional classroom teaching. 许多大学现在提供移动学习资源来补充传统的课堂教学。|Example: The student used m-learning to study for the exam while commuting on the train.  这个学生在火车上通勤时使用移动学习来准备考试。|Synonyms: mobile learning,  e-learning (broader), distance learning (broader)|Antonyms: traditional learning, classroom learning|Noun
