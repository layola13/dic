honeyish:/ˈhʌniɪʃ/ | adj. | 略带蜂蜜味的；蜂蜜似的；甜蜜的 | Somewhat like honey in taste, color, or texture; sweet. | The tea had a honeyish aroma. 这茶略带蜂蜜的香味。| The child gave her mother a honeyish smile. 孩子给了她妈妈一个甜蜜的微笑。| 近义词: sweet, honeyed, nectarous | 反义词: bitter, sour | 词性: 形容词

词源: honey + -ish.  "Honey" comes from Old English "hunig," and "-ish" is a suffix meaning "somewhat like," from Old English "-isc."
