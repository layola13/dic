grammarless: /ˈɡræmərləs/ | adj. | 无语法规则的，不通顺的 | Lacking grammatical correctness or structure; ungrammatical. |  The student's essay was grammarless and difficult to understand.  学生的作文不通顺，难以理解。| ungrammatical, incoherent | grammatical, fluent | adjective

Etymology:  "grammar" + "-less". "Grammar" comes from Old French "gramaire"  ultimately from Greek "gramma" meaning "letter". "-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without".
