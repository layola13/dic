farrier:/ˈfæriər/ | n. |蹄铁匠；装蹄师 |Someone who trims and shoes horses' hooves.|The farrier carefully examined the horse's hoof before applying the new shoe.  蹄铁匠在钉新蹄铁之前仔细检查了马蹄。|Synonyms: horseshoer, blacksmith |Antonyms: None |Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ferrier (from Latin ferrārius ‘iron worker’), from ferrum ‘iron’.
