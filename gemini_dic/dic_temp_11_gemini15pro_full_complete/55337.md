dissweeten:/dɪsˈswitən/
|v.|
|去除甜味; 使不甜;|
|To remove the sweetness from something; to make less sweet.|
|The chef dissweetened the sauce by adding a pinch of salt.  厨师加了一撮盐去掉了酱汁的甜味。|

|They dissweetened the cranberry sauce with a little lemon juice.  他们用一点柠檬汁去掉了蔓越莓酱的甜味。|

|近义词: unsweeten, de-sweeten|
|反义词: sweeten|
|词性: 动词(verb)|
