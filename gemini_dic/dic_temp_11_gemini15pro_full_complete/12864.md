assortment:/əˈsɔːrtmənt/| n.|各种各样，混合物，分类|A collection of different things or a variety of something.|The shop sells a wide assortment of cheeses. 这家商店出售各种各样的奶酪。|近义词：variety, selection, mixture, collection, array|反义词: uniformity, sameness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French assortiment, from assortir "to sort, match," from Old French asortir, from a- "to" + sortir "to go out, issue, allot," from Latin sortiri "to draw lots, obtain by lot, allot, assign," from sors "lot, fate, destiny."
