chapen:/ˈtʃeɪpən/| v.|买卖；讨价还价 (Archaic -  Now chiefly Scottish)|To bargain; to haggle over a price. |He chapens with the merchant for a lower price on the tapestry. 他与商人就挂毯的价格讨价还价。|They were chapining over the price of the cattle. 他们就牛的价格讨价还价。|近义词：bargain, haggle, barter|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "pay full price") |v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chappen*, from Old North French *cheapier*, from Late Latin *caupo, -onis* ("merchant, innkeeper"). Ultimately related to Latin *caupō* ("innkeeper, shopkeeper").
