harper:/ˈhɑːrpər/ (US), /ˈhɑːpə/ (UK)| n.|竖琴演奏者，游吟诗人|A person who plays the harp.  Historically, a harper was also a poet and storyteller who recited verses and often accompanied themselves on the harp.|例句：The harper played a beautiful melody. 竖琴演奏者演奏了一曲优美的旋律。

例句：The ancient harper entertained the king with stories and songs. 古代的游吟诗人用故事和歌声娱乐国王。|近义词：minstrel, bard, musician|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of a non-musician)|词性:noun
