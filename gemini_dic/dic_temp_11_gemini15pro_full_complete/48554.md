defame:/dɪˈfeɪm/ | vt.| 诽谤，中损，诋毁 |to harm someone's reputation by saying or writing bad things about them that are not true | The article defamed him and his family. 这篇文章诽谤了他和他的家人。| synonyms: slander, libel, malign, disparage | antonyms: praise, commend, extol | verb

defame:/dɪˈfeɪm/ | n.| 诽谤 |The act of defaming someone. | He sued the newspaper for defamation. 他起诉这家报纸诽谤. | synonyms: slander, libel, calumny | antonyms: praise, commendation, eulogy | noun
