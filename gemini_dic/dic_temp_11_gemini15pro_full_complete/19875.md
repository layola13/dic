bingos:/ˈbɪŋɡoʊz/| n.|宾果游戏（的获胜）; 宾果游戏（的获胜喊叫声）|A game of chance in which each player has a card printed with different numbers on which to place markers when the numbers are called out. The winner is the first to cover a row, column, or diagonal. Also, the exclamation shouted by the winner.|例句：Bingo! I've got a full house. 宾果！我赢了！

例句：He shouted "Bingos!" and collected his winnings. 他喊了声“宾果！”，然后领取了他的奖金。

|近义词：lotto, raffle|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "loss" in the context of the game)|词性:noun
