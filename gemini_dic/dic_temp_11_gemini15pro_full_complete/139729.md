ochre: /ˈoʊkər/ (US) /ˈəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|赭色的|Having a moderate orange-yellow color.|The ochre cliffs glowed in the setting sun.|近义词:yellowish-brown, brownish-yellow|反义词:blue, green, white|adjective
