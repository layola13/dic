norite:/ˈnɔːraɪt/| n.|苏长岩，紫苏辉长岩|A coarse-grained igneous rock composed primarily of plagioclase feldspar (labradorite) and orthopyroxene (hypersthene).|The geologist identified the sample as norite.  地质学家将样品鉴定为苏长岩。|The norite cliff face was impressive.  苏长岩悬崖面令人印象深刻。|Synonyms: hypersthene-gabbro|Antonyms: None|Noun
