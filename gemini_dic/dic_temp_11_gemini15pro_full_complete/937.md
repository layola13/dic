abyssinia:/ˌæbɪˈsɪniə/ | n. | 阿比西尼亚 (旧称，现称埃塞俄比亚) | An old-fashioned name for Ethiopia, a country in East Africa. |  He traveled to Abyssinia in search of adventure. 他前往阿比西尼亚探险。| 近义词: Ethiopia | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin Abyssinia, from Arabic al-Habash, meaning "the Habesha people". The Habesha people are a group of ethnic groups inhabiting the Horn of Africa.
