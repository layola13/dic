mesosauria:/ˌmɛzoʊˈsɔːriə/ | n. | 中龙目 | An order of extinct reptiles from the early Permian period, known for being among the first reptiles to return to an aquatic lifestyle.  They are characterized by their long, slender bodies and paddle-like limbs. |  Mesosauria fossils are often found in freshwater deposits. 中龙目化石经常在淡水沉积物中发现。|  Mesosaurs were among the first amniotes to adapt to an aquatic environment. 中龙是羊膜动物中最先适应水生环境的。 | synonyms: (no direct synonyms, as it's a specific taxonomic order)  | antonyms: (not applicable to taxonomic classifications) | noun
