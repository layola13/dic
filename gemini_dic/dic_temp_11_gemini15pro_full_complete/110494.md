limaçon:/ˈlɪməsɒn/ (UK), /ˈlaɪməkɑːn/ (US)| n. |蚶线，蜗线；利马松曲线|A plane curve formed by the path of a point fixed to a circle as the circle rolls around the circumference of another circle of equal radius.|The equation of the limaçon is r = a + b cos θ.|近义词: cardioid (when a=b), conchoid |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *limaçon*, literally "snail," from Old French *limazon*, from Latin *limax*, "slug, snail."
