brickline:/ˈbrɪklaɪn/
n.
砖砌线，砖层，砖列
A line formed by bricks, often referring to a single row or course in a wall or structure.
The brickline of the old house was uneven. 这栋老房子的砖砌线参差不齐。

The brickline was meticulously laid. 这排砖砌得非常细致。

近义词:row of bricks, course of bricks
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially gap, void)
词性:noun
