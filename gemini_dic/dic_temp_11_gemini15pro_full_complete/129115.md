nixie:/ˈnɪksi/| n.|数码管; (神话) 女水妖; 错误; 废片|A nixie tube is an electronic device for displaying numerals or other information using glow discharge. 例句：The letter was returned as a nixie because of an incorrect address. 这封信因为地址不正确而被退回，成为了一封废信。
