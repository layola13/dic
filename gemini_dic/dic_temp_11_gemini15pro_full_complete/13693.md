attributing:/əˈtrɪbjʊtɪŋ/
v.|归因；归属；认为…是…所为|The painting is attributed to Leonardo da Vinci. 这幅画被认为是莱昂纳多·达·芬奇的作品。|Synonyms: assigning, ascribing, crediting|Antonyms: denying, rejecting|Verb

attributing:/əˈtrɪbjʊtɪŋ/
adj.|归因的；归属的|The attributing factor in the accident was driver error. 事故的起因是驾驶员失误。|Synonyms: causative, responsible|Antonyms: resultant, consequential|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *attribuere*, from Latin ad- "to" + tribuere "to assign, bestow".
