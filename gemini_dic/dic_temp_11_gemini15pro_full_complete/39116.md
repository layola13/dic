confucianism:/kənˈfjuːʃənɪzəm/| n.|儒家思想，儒学|A system of philosophical and ethical teachings founded by Confucius and developed by Mencius.|例句：Confucianism has profoundly influenced Chinese culture for centuries. 儒家思想深刻地影响了中国文化数个世纪。

例句：Many principles of Confucianism emphasize social harmony. 儒家的许多原则都强调社会和谐。|近义词：Confucian doctrine, Confucian philosophy|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially contrasting philosophies like Legalism or Taoism)|词性:noun
