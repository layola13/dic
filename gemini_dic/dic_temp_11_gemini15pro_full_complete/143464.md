outgabble:/ˌaʊtˈɡæbl/
verb
胜过…的饶舌；比…更健谈
to talk more loudly and rapidly than (someone)
He outgabbled her in the argument. 他在辩论中说得比她更大声更急促。
Synonyms: outtalk, overpower
Antonyms: be quiet, listen
词性: 动词
