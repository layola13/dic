definition:/ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 定义，释义 | A statement of the exact meaning of a word, especially in a dictionary. | Example: The definition of "love" is complex and varied.  爱的定义复杂且多样。 | Example:  Can you give me a precise definition of this term?  你能给我这个术语的精确定义吗？| Synonyms: meaning, explanation, interpretation | Antonyms: ambiguity, vagueness | noun

etymology:
From Middle French *definition*, from Latin *dēfīnītiō*, from *dēfīnīre* (“to define, limit, determine”), from *dē* (“from, down from”) + *fīnīre* (“to finish, limit”), from *fīnis* (“end, limit, boundary”).
