metemptosis:/ˌmetəmˈtəʊsɪs/ (US) /ˌmetəmˈtəʊsɪs/ (UK)| n.|灵魂转世;  再生|The concept of metemptosis suggests a soul's journey through different lives, unlike metempsychosis which implies transmigration into different bodies.| Belief in metemptosis offers solace to those who grieve the loss of loved ones, imagining them reborn into new lives. | 近义词: reincarnation, rebirth, transmigration|反义词: death, extinction | noun
