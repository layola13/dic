dhurrie:/ˈdʌri/ /ˈdʊri/| n. |粗毛地毯，印度地毯|A thick flat-woven cotton rug or carpet, typically from India.|例句：She bought a beautiful dhurrie for her living room. 她为她的客厅买了一块漂亮的地毯。

例句：The dhurrie added a touch of color to the otherwise minimalist decor.  这块地毯给原本简约的装饰增添了一抹色彩。

近义词：rug, carpet, mat|反义词:|词性:noun
