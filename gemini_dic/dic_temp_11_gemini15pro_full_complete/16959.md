bassoons:/bəˈsuːnz/
noun
巴松管（复数）
A double-reed woodwind instrument with a low range, often used in orchestras and bands.
The bassoons played a mournful melody. 巴松管演奏了一段悲伤的旋律。

The orchestra has two bassoons.  这个管弦乐队有两支巴松管。

Synonyms:double reeds (泛指双簧乐器)
Antonyms:N/A (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他乐器类别，例如brass instruments 铜管乐器)
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *bassone*, augmentative of *basso* (low). Ultimately from Late Latin *bassus*.
