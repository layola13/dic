chub:/tʃʌb/| n.| 胖乎乎的人（尤指小孩）；鲤鱼的一种|A short, thickset, typically rounded person or animal, often a child. Also, a type of fish, specifically a European freshwater cyprinid fish.|例句：The little chub giggled as he splashed in the paddling pool.  小胖墩在戏水池里嬉戏，咯咯地笑着。

例句：He caught a large chub while fishing in the river. 他在河里钓鱼时钓到了一条大鲤鱼。|近义词：pudgy, plump, rotund, stocky (for a person); dace, roach (for the fish)|反义词:thin, slender, skinny (for a person)|词性:noun
