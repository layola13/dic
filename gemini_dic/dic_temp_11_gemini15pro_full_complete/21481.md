Blu-ray: /ˈbluːˌreɪ/ (US), /ˈbluːˌreɪ/ (UK) | n. | 蓝光光盘 | An optical disc format for high-definition video and data storage. It gets its name from the blue-violet laser used to read the disc. | I bought a Blu-ray of my favorite movie. 我买了我最喜欢的电影的蓝光光盘。 |  Synonyms:  HD DVD (obsolete), UHD Blu-ray | Antonyms: DVD, VHS | noun
