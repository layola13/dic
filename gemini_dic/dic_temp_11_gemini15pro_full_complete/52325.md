diclinic: /daɪˈklɪnɪk/ | adj. | 雌雄异株的；单性异株的 | Having stamens and pistils in separate flowers on different plants. |  The willow tree is diclinic, with male and female flowers on separate trees. 柳树是雌雄异株的，雄花和雌花分别生长在不同的树上。 | synonyms: dioecious | antonyms: monoecious | adjective

diclinous: /daɪˈklaɪnəs/ | adj. | 雌雄异株的；单性异株的 | Having stamens and pistils in separate flowers on different plants. |  Diclinous plants rely on wind or pollinators to transfer pollen between male and female flowers. 雌雄异株的植物依靠风或传粉者在雄花和雌花之间传递花粉。 | synonyms: dioecious | antonyms: monoecious | adjective
