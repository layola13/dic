inductors:/ɪnˈdʌktərz/| n.|电感器|A passive electronic component that stores energy in a magnetic field when electric current flows through it.  It resists changes in current.|The circuit uses several inductors to filter out unwanted frequencies.  这个电路使用多个电感器来滤除不需要的频率。|近义词：coil, reactor|反义词:capacitor|noun
