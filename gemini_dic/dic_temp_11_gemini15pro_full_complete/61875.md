empress:/ˈemprɪs/| n.|女皇，皇后|A woman who is the ruler of an empire.|例句：The empress ruled her vast empire with wisdom and strength. 女皇以智慧和力量统治着她广阔的帝国。

例句：Catherine the Great was a powerful empress of Russia.  凯瑟琳大帝是俄罗斯一位强大的女皇。

近义词：queen, ruler, monarch, sovereign|反义词:subject, commoner|词性:noun
