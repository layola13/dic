hyperawareness:/ˌhaɪpərəˈwɛrnəs/| n. |过度警觉，过度意识|An abnormally heightened state of awareness of one's surroundings and thoughts, often associated with anxiety or trauma.|After the robbery, she experienced a period of hyperawareness, constantly scanning her environment for potential threats. 她被抢劫后经历了一段时间的过度警觉，不断地审视周围环境，以防潜在的威胁。|近义词：hypersensitivity, oversensitivity|反义词：unawareness, obliviousness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From hyper- + awareness.  "Hyper-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "over, above, beyond" and "awareness" comes from the Old English word "geweornian" meaning "to perceive, to become aware of".
