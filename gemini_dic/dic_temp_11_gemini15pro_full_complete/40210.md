contesting:/kənˈtɛstɪŋ/ /kɑːnˈtɛstɪŋ/
verb
竞争；争辩；对…提出异议
Engaging in a competition or challenge; disputing or arguing against something.
The two teams are contesting the championship. 这两支队伍正在争夺冠军。
They are contesting the validity of the election results. 他们正在对选举结果的有效性提出异议。
近义词: challenging, disputing, opposing
反义词: accepting, conceding, agreeing
词性: verb

contested:/kənˈtɛstɪd/ /kɑːnˈtɛstɪd/
adjective
有争议的；竞争激烈的
Disputed or contended for; subject to competition.
The election results were highly contested. 选举结果备受争议。
It was a closely contested race. 这是一场竞争激烈的比赛。
近义词: disputed, controversial, debatable
反义词: undisputed, uncontroversial, agreed upon
词性: adjective

词源: From the Latin word "contestari," meaning "to call to witness," which evolved from "com-" (together) and "testari" (to bear witness).
