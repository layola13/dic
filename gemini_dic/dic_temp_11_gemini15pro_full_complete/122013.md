misstate:/ˌmɪsˈsteɪt/
verb
错误陈述；谎报
to state something wrongly or falsely.
The company misstated its earnings in the financial report.  该公司在财务报告中谎报了收益。
The witness misstated the facts of the case.  证人错误陈述了案件的事实。
Synonyms: misrepresent, falsify, distort, exaggerate
Antonyms: state correctly, report accurately, represent truthfully
词性: 动词
