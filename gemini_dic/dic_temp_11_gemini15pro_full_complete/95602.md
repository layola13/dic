incommoding: /ˌɪnkəˈmoʊdɪŋ/
verb
打扰，妨碍；使不便
causing inconvenience or trouble to someone.
例句：I hope I'm not incommoding you by asking you to move.  我希望请你挪一下不会打扰到你。

例句：The construction work outside is really incommoding us. 外面的施工真的给我们带来了不便。

近义词：disturbing, bothering, inconveniencing, troubling
反义词：accommodating, assisting, helping
词性：v.
