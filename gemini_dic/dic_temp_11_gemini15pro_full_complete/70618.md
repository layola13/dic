filmographies:/ˌfɪlmˈɒɡrəfiz/
n.
电影作品集；影片目录；电影目录学
A list of films related by some criteria, such as the films featuring a particular actor, director, or genre.
例句：
The website provides comprehensive filmographies of all major Hollywood actors.
该网站提供所有主要好莱坞演员的完整电影作品集。

例句：
Her filmography includes several award-winning documentaries.
她的电影作品包括几部获奖的纪录片。

近义词：filmography (singular)
反义词: 无

词性: noun (plural)
