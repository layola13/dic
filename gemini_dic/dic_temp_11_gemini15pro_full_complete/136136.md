nonreflectors:/ˌnɒnrɪˈflɛktəz/ (US) /ˌnɒnrɪˈflektəz/ (UK)| n.|非反射器，不反射物体|Objects or surfaces that do not reflect light or other radiation. |The stealth aircraft was coated with nonreflectors to avoid radar detection.  隐形飞机涂上了非反射材料以避免雷达探测。|Synonyms: non-reflective materials, opaque materials|Antonyms: reflectors, reflective surfaces|noun
