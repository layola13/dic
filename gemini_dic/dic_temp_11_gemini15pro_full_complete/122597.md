modernistically:/ˌmɒdəˈnɪstɪkli/ /ˌmɑːdərˈnɪstɪkli/
adverb
现代主义地
In a manner characterized by modernism; in a way that reflects the principles and styles of modernism.
The building was designed modernistically, with clean lines and large windows. 这座建筑设计得非常现代化，线条简洁，窗户很大。
The artist painted modernistically, rejecting traditional techniques. 这位艺术家以现代主义风格绘画，拒绝传统技法。
Synonyms: contemporarily, avant-gardely
Antonyms: traditionally, classically
词性: 副词
