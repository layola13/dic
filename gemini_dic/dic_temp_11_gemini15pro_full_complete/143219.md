outculls:/ˈaʊtkʌlz/
noun
剔除物，淘汰品
Items of lower quality that have been removed from a larger group.
The farmer sold the outculls at a discounted price. 农民以折扣价出售了剔除品。
Synonyms: rejects, culls, discards
Antonyms:  selections, keepers
词性: 名词

outcull:/ˈaʊtkʌl/
verb
剔除，淘汰
To remove items of lower quality from a larger group.
The factory outculled the damaged products. 工厂剔除了损坏的产品。
Synonyms: reject, cull, discard
Antonyms: select, keep
词性: 动词
