oversauces:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːsɪz/
verb
浇过多的酱汁; 酱汁放得太多
To put too much sauce on (food).
The pasta was oversauced and quite salty.  这意大利面酱汁太多，而且很咸。
oversauce, overseason, overdo
undersauce
