The continued presence of complex structures or procedures that could be streamlined. |  The team's insistence on nonsimplifications in the code made it difficult to debug.  该团队坚持代码的非简化，导致调试困难。

The nonsimplifications in the legal document made it incomprehensible to the average person.  法律文件中不简化的地方使普通人无法理解。 | synonyms: complexities, intricacies, elaborations | antonyms: simplifications, reductions, streamlinings | noun
