gloomfully: /ˈɡluːmfʊli/ | adv. | 忧郁地，闷闷不乐地 | In a gloomy manner; sadly and despondently.  | He stared gloomfully out the window at the rainy street. 他忧郁地望着窗外雨淋淋的街道。|  She shook her head gloomfully. 她闷闷不乐地摇了摇头。 | 近义词：sadly, dejectedly, despondently, morosely | 反义词：cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

gloom:/ɡluːm/ | n. | 昏暗；忧郁；沮丧 | Partial or total darkness; a state of depression or despondency. |  A heavy gloom settled over the room. 房间里弥漫着沉重的忧郁气氛。| The news cast a gloom over the entire city.  这个消息给整个城市蒙上了一层阴影。 | 近义词：darkness, sadness, depression | 反义词：brightness, happiness, cheer | 词性: 名词
