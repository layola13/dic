clearstarch: /ˈklɪərstɑːrtʃ/ | n. | 透明淀粉，浆洗淀粉 | A type of starch used to stiffen fabrics, often sprayed onto clothes before ironing.  It gives a crisp, smooth finish. |  Example: She used clearstarch to iron her shirt. 她用透明淀粉熨烫她的衬衫。

Example:  The tablecloth was starched with clearstarch for a crisp look.  桌布用透明淀粉浆洗过，看起来很挺括。| 近义词: starch, sizing | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


词源:

clear + starch

* clear: From Middle English *clere, from Old French cler, ultimately from Latin clārus (“clear, bright”).
* starch: From Middle English sterche, from Old English stearc, from Proto-Germanic *sterkaz (“stiff, strong”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ter- (“stiff”).
