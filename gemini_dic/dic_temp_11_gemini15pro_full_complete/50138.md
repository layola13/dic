denudement:/dɪˈnjuːdmənt/ /diˈnuːdmənt/
n.
剥夺，褫夺；暴露；结局，收场
the act of stripping something bare of its covering, possessions, or assets; the state of being stripped bare; the final resolution of the intricacies of a plot, as of a drama or novel.
The denudement of the forest by excessive logging caused severe soil erosion. 过度砍伐导致森林被剥夺，造成严重的水土流失。

The denudement of the play left the audience feeling unsatisfied.  这出戏的结局让观众感到不满意。

近义词: stripping, baring, uncovering, exposure, resolution, outcome
反义词: covering, concealment, enrichment

词性: noun
