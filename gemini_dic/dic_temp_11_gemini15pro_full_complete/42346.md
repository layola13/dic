counteractor:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktər/ | n. |抵消者；对抗者；中和剂| something that reduces or prevents the bad effect of something else |  The drug acts as a counteractor to the poison. 这种药物可以中和毒药。| synonyms: neutralizer, antidote, remedy | antonyms:  intensifier, catalyst, aggravator | noun


counteract:/ˌkaʊntərˈækt/ | v. |抵消；对抗；中和| to reduce or prevent the bad effect of something |  Drinking plenty of water counteracts the dehydrating effects of hot weather.  多喝水可以抵消炎热天气造成的脱水。| synonyms: neutralize, offset, negate | antonyms: intensify, exacerbate, worsen | verb
