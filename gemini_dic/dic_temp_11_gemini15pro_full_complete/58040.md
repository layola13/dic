droll:/droʊl/| adj.|古怪有趣的，滑稽的|Having a humorous, whimsical, or odd quality that provokes amusement.|His droll sense of humor made him popular at parties. 他古怪的幽默感使他在聚会上很受欢迎。|近义词：funny, amusing, comical, whimsical, quirky|反义词：serious, solemn, humorless|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *drolle*, "funny, comical," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Middle Dutch *drol*, "imp, goblin, buffoon."
