demarcated:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪtɪd/ | adj. |  划定的；界定的 | clearly marked or defined; set the boundaries or limits of |  The boundary between the two countries is clearly demarcated.  两国之间的边界划分得很清楚。| synonyms: delimited, bounded, outlined | antonyms: undefined, blurred, vague | word type: adjective

demarcate:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪt/ | vt. | 划定；界定 | set the boundaries or limits of; separate or distinguish from | The police demarcated the crime scene with tape. 警察用胶带划定了犯罪现场。| synonyms: delimit, bound, outline | antonyms: merge, blend, confuse | word type: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *démarquer*, from *dé-* (meaning "off, away from") + *marquer* (meaning "to mark"). Ultimately from Latin *marcare* (to mark).
