outgushed:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌʃt/
verb
涌出，喷出
(of liquid) gushed or poured out.
Tears of joy outgushed from her eyes. 喜悦的泪水从她的眼中涌出。
Synonyms: pour, spurt, stream, gush
Antonyms: trickle, seep, drip
词性: 动词
