outgrowing:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊɪŋ/
verb
长得太大穿不下; 长大成熟而不再
growing too large for (something); growing up and no longer doing or needing (something)
She has outgrown her childhood clothes. 她已经长得太大，穿不下她小时候的衣服了。
He has outgrown his childish fears. 他已经长大，不再害怕那些幼稚的事情了。
outgrow, surpass, exceed
retain, keep
v.
