experientialism:/ɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəlɪzəm/
n.
经验主义，经验论
A philosophy that emphasizes the role of experience in acquiring knowledge.  It asserts that knowledge is primarily or solely derived from sensory experience.
Experientialism is central to many modern educational theories. 经验主义是许多现代教育理论的核心。
The artist's work is rooted in experientialism, focusing on the sensory impact of her installations. 这位艺术家的作品植根于经验主义，注重装置的感官冲击。
近义词:empiricism, pragmatism
反义词:rationalism, idealism
词性:noun

Etymology:
From experience + -ial + -ism.  Ultimately from Latin experientia.
