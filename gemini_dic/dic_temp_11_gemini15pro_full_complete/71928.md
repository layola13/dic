flibustier:/ˌflɪbəˈstɪər/| n.|海盗，暴徒，掠夺者|A pirate, especially one operating in the Caribbean in the 17th century.  A person who engages in unauthorized warfare against a foreign country.|The flibustier captured a Spanish galleon laden with gold.|Synonyms: pirate, buccaneer, privateer, corsair, freebooter|Antonyms: lawman, officer|noun
