healthless:/ˈhelθləs/| adj.|不健康的，有害健康的|Lacking health; unhealthy.  Characterized by a lack of health or by practices or conditions that are detrimental to health.|例句：A healthless diet can lead to various diseases. 不健康的饮食会导致各种疾病。

例句：Living in a healthless environment can compromise your immune system.  生活在一个不健康的环境中会损害你的免疫系统。|近义词：unhealthy, insalubrious, noxious, detrimental|反义词:healthy, wholesome, salubrious|词性:adjective
