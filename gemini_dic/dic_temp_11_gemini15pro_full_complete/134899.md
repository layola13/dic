nonmultiples:/ˌnɒnˈmʌltɪplz/ | n. | 非倍数 | Numbers that are not multiples of a specific number. | Example:  The nonmultiples of 3 in the set {1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8} are 1, 2, 4, 5, 7, and 8.  在集合 {1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8} 中，3 的非倍数是 1、2、4、5、7 和 8。 | Example:  Identifying the nonmultiples of 10 is easy – they are simply numbers that don't end in a zero. 识别 10 的非倍数很容易——它们就是不以 0 结尾的数字。| Near Synonyms: non-divisors | Antonyms: multiples, divisors | noun
