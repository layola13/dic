criticizer:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzər/| n. |批评者，评论者|A person who expresses disapproval of someone or something.|例句：He is a harsh criticizer of the government's policies. 他是政府政策的严厉批评者。

例句：The play attracted many criticizers. 这部戏吸引了很多批评者。


近义词：critic, faultfinder, detractor|反义词:supporter, advocate|词性:noun
