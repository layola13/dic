disheveled: /dɪˈʃevəld/ (US) /dɪˈʃɛvəld/ (UK)| adj.|蓬乱的，凌乱的|Being in a hurry, her hair was disheveled and her clothes were rumpled.|例句：He looked pale and disheveled. 他看起来脸色苍白，头发凌乱。|例句：After the long journey, she arrived looking disheveled. 长途旅行后，她到达时看起来很凌乱。|近义词：untidy, messy, rumpled, disarranged, tousled|反义词：tidy, neat, ordered, groomed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deschevelé, past participle of descheveler "to disarrange the hair," from des- "dis-" + chevel "hair," from Latin capillus "hair."
