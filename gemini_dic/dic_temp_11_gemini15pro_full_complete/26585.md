cabriole:/ˈkæbrɪˌoʊl/ (US), /ˈkæbrɪɔːl/ (UK)| n. |跳跃，腾跃 (芭蕾)；桌椅腿的一种弯曲式式样|A ballet leap in which the dancer jumps into the air, extending one leg horizontally forward at the height of the jump and bringing the other leg up to meet it, before landing on the first leg; a curved furniture leg.|The dancer executed a perfect cabriole.  这位舞者完美地完成了腾跃动作。

The antique chair had elegantly carved cabrioles. 这把古董椅子的弯腿雕刻精美。|近义词：leap, jump, bound (for the movement); curved leg (for the furniture style)|反义词: landing, descent (for the movement) |词性:noun
