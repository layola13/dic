nonreactionary:/ˌnɑːnriˈækʃəneri/
adjective
非反动派的；不反对变革的
Not reactionary; not opposing change or progress.
例句：The government adopted a nonreactionary policy towards the protests. 政府对这些抗议活动采取了非反动政策。

例句：He has a nonreactionary approach to education, embracing new technologies and teaching methods. 他对教育的态度不保守，乐于接受新技术和教学方法。

近义词：progressive, liberal, reformist, open-minded
反义词：reactionary, conservative, traditionalist
词性: adjective
