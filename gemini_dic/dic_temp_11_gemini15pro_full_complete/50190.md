deoxidization:/diːˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/ /diːˌɑːksɪˈdeɪʃən/
n.
脱氧，去氧
The process of removing oxygen from a substance.
The deoxidization of steel is a crucial step in its production. 钢铁的脱氧是其生产中的关键步骤。
Deoxidization is important in metallurgy. 脱氧在冶金中很重要。
reduction, oxygen removal
oxidation
noun
