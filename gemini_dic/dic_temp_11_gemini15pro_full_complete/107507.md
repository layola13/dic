land:/lænd/ | n. v. | 土地，陆地；降落，着陆 |A piece of ground, especially one used for a particular purpose. To come down to the ground after moving through the air. |The plane landed safely. 飞机安全着陆。|近义词：ground, soil, earth; alight, touch down|反义词：sea, sky, air; take off, ascend|n. v.

land:/lænd/ | v. | 获得，得到 |To succeed in getting something, especially something good. |He landed a job as a teacher. 他找到了一份教师的工作。|近义词：obtain, acquire, secure, win|反义词：lose, forfeit|v.
