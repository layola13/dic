exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | v. n. | 利用，剥削；功绩，功勋 | To make full use of a resource or situation, sometimes in a way that is considered unfair or unethical.  |  The company exploited its workers by paying them low wages.  这家公司剥削工人，支付低工资。 | synonyms: utilize, capitalize on, abuse, misuse | antonyms: protect, conserve, neglect | verb

exploit:/ˈeksplɔɪt/ | n. | 功绩，功勋；英勇事迹 | A bold or daring feat. | His wartime exploits were legendary. 他的战时功勋是传奇性的。 | synonyms: feat, accomplishment, achievement | antonyms: failure, defeat | noun
