acetabular:/ˌæsəˈtæbjʊlər/ | adj. | 髋臼的，杯状的 | Relating to the acetabulum (the socket of the hip joint). |  The acetabular fracture required surgery.  髋臼骨折需要手术。 |  The prosthetic hip joint included an acetabular component.  人造髋关节包括一个髋臼组件。 |  Synonymous: cotyloid  | Antonymous: (No direct antonym, but one could consider terms related to other parts of the hip joint like femoral or trochanteric as conceptually opposite.) | adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *acētabulāris, from Latin acētabulum (“vinegar cup, hip socket”) + -āris (adjective suffix). The Latin word is from acētum ("vinegar") + -bulum (diminutive suffix).
