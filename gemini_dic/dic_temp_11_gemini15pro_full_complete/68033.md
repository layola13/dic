factorizable:/ˈfæktəraɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可分解的，可因式分解的 |  Able to be factored or resolved into factors, especially of a number or polynomial. | The number 12 is factorizable into 2, 2, and 3.  12可以分解成2、2和3。| The polynomial x² - 4 is factorizable. 多项式x²-4是可因式分解的。 | factorable, resolvable | irreducible, indecomposable | adjective
