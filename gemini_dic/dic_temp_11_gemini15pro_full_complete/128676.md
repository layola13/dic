nicotines:/ˈnɪkətiːnz/
noun
尼古丁（复数）
Nicotine is a highly addictive stimulant found primarily in tobacco plants.  It acts as both a stimulant and a relaxant.  The plural form "nicotines" can be used to refer to different forms or concentrations of nicotine, though "nicotine" is often used as a mass noun.
Nicotine is the addictive substance in cigarettes. 尼古丁是香烟中的成瘾物质。
Different nicotines are used in vaping products.  不同的尼古丁被用在电子烟产品中。
Synonym:  (No direct synonym for the plural form, but for singular) stimulant, addictive substance
Antonym:  None
词性: 名词
