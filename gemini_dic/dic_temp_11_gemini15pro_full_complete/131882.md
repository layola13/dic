noncontemplatively:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛmplətɪvli/ | adv. | 非沉思地，不加思索地 | Without contemplation or deep thought; in a way that is not reflective or meditative. | He acted noncontemplatively, grabbing the first item he saw. 他不假思索地行动，抓起了他看到的第一件物品。|  He spoke noncontemplatively, the words tumbling out without pause. 他不假思索地说，话语不停地涌出。 | Synonyms: thoughtlessly, impulsively, instinctively, automatically | Antonyms: thoughtfully, contemplatively, reflectively, deliberately | Word Type: Adverb
