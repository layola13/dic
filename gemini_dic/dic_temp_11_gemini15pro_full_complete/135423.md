nonpersecutive:/ˌnɑːnpərˈsɛkjətɪv/
adjective
非连续的，不连续的
Not following in uninterrupted succession; not consecutive.
The meetings were nonpersecutive, taking place over several months. 这些会议并非连续举行，而是持续了几个月。
The nonpersecutive nature of the data made it difficult to analyze.  数据的非连续性使其难以分析。
近义词:nonconsecutive, discontinuous, intermittent, sporadic
反义词:consecutive, continuous, sequential
