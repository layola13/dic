conjuration:/ˌkɒndʒəˈreɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːndʒəˈreɪʃn/ (US)| n.| 符咒；咒语；魔术；恳求 |The magician performed a conjuration to make the rabbit disappear. 魔术师表演了一个咒语，让兔子消失了。| The act of conjuring, the calling forth of spirits or the performance of magic.  The earnest requesting of something.| She tried every conjuration she knew to bring him back. 她试了她所知道的一切咒语想让他回来。| 近义词：incantation, spell, charm, invocation, plea, appeal | 反义词: dispelling, banishment | 词性: noun
