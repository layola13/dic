detasselled:/diːˈtæsəld/
|v. |去雄；除去（玉米等植物的）雄穗| to remove the tassels from (a plant, especially corn) to prevent self-pollination and promote cross-pollination.  |The corn had been detasselled by hand. 玉米已经人工去雄了。|
| |The crew detasselled thousands of acres of corn this summer.  今年夏天，工作人员为数千英亩的玉米去雄。|
| |Synonyms: emasculate |
| |Antonyms: pollinate |
| |Verb|
