dekabrist:/dɪˈkæbrɪst/| n. |十二月党人|A member of a group of Russian revolutionaries who led an unsuccessful uprising in December 1825.  They sought to establish a constitutional monarchy or republic after the death of Tsar Alexander I.|The Decembrist revolt was a pivotal moment in Russian history. 十二月党人起义是俄罗斯历史上的一个关键时刻。|  The Decembrists were inspired by the ideals of the Enlightenment. 十二月党人受到了启蒙运动思想的启发。|Synonyms: revolutionary, rebel, insurgent|Antonyms: loyalist, conservative|Noun
