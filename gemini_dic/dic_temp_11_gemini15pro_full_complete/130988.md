nonanatomical:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈtɒmɪkəl/ | adj. | 非解剖的；非解剖学上的 | Not related to anatomy or the structure of the body. |  The artist's drawings were nonanatomical, focusing more on emotion than physical accuracy.  这位艺术家的绘画是非解剖学的，更多地关注情感而不是物理上的准确性。| Near Synonyms: non-anatomical, nonstructural | Antonyms: anatomical, structural | adjective
