looed:/luːd/
verb
上厕所（非正式，主要用于英国）
went to the toilet (informal, chiefly British).
例句：
I need to loo.
我需要上厕所。

He just looed before we left.
在我们离开之前他刚上过厕所。

近义词：
pee, urinate, piddle, wee, go to the bathroom/restroom/lavatory/toilet

反义词:
(Looed lacks a direct antonym as it's the act of using a toilet.  Holding it or refraining from urination could be considered conceptually opposite.)


词性
verb


词源
From a shortening of lavatory or water closet.
