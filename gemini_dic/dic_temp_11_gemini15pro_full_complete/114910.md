manacled:/ˈmænəkld/ | adj. | 被铐住的，被束缚的 | Restrained with manacles; shackled. | The prisoner was brought into the courtroom, manacled and guarded.  囚犯被带进法庭，戴着手铐，有人看守。| 近义词: shackled, chained, fettered, bound | 反义词: free, liberated, unrestrained | 词性: 形容词

manacle:/ˈmænəkl/ | n. | 手铐，镣铐 | A metal band, chain, or shackle for fastening someone's hands or ankles. | The police placed manacles on the suspect. 警察给嫌疑人戴上了手铐。 | 近义词: handcuff, shackle, fetter, chain | 反义词: key, release | 词性: 名词

manacle:/ˈmænəkl/ | v. | 给…戴上手铐，束缚 | To restrain with manacles. |  His wrists were manacled behind his back. 他的手腕被铐在背后。 | 近义词: handcuff, shackle, fetter, restrain | 反义词: free, liberate, release | 词性: 动词
