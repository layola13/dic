auxetic:/ɔːkˈsɛtɪk/
adjective
拉胀的，负泊松比的
Having a negative Poisson's ratio, which means that the material becomes thicker perpendicular to the applied force when stretched.
Example 1: Auxetic materials expand when stretched, unlike conventional materials.  拉胀材料在被拉伸时会膨胀，这与传统材料不同。
Example 2: Researchers are exploring the potential applications of auxetic foams in protective gear.  研究人员正在探索拉胀泡沫在防护装备中的潜在应用。
近义词: expansive, dilational
反义词: contractive, compressive
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *αὔξησις (*auxēsis, “increase”) + -ic.  源自希腊语 *αὔξησις (*auxēsis，“增加”) + -ic。
