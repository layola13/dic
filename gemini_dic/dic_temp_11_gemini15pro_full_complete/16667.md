barras:/ˈbærəs/ (US), /ˈbɑːrəs/ (UK)| n.| 栏杆，栅栏，酒吧，(西班牙语) 酒吧，杆|A structure formed of parallel bars.|例句：The bull charged at the barras of the enclosure. 公牛向围栏的栏杆冲去。

例句：We leaned against the barras and watched the sunset. 我们倚着栏杆看日落。|近义词：rails, bars, fence, railing|反义词:opening, gap|词性:noun
