nougat:/ˈnuːɡɑːt/ (US), /ˈnuːɡæt/ (UK)| n. |牛轧糖|A chewy candy made with sugar or honey, nuts, and egg whites.|例句：She loves almond nougat. 她喜欢杏仁牛轧糖。

例句：The nougat was soft and chewy. 这牛轧糖又软又嚼劲。


近义词：confection, candy|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of candy)|词性: Noun
