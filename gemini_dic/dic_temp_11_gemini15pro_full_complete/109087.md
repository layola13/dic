legators:/ˈleɡətərz/
noun
遗赠人;立遗嘱者
People who make a will and leave property or money to others.
The legators stipulated that the funds be used for educational purposes.  遗赠人规定这些资金应用于教育目的。
testators, donors, benefactors
inheritors, beneficiaries
n.
