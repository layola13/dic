clearwater:/ˈklɪrˌwɔːtər/ (US), /ˈklɪəˌwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|清水，清澈的水；地名|Clear water is essential for healthy aquatic life. 清澈的水对健康的水生生物至关重要。|近义词：freshwater, pure water, limpid water|反义词:murky water, polluted water|noun
clearwater:/ˈklɪrˌwɔːtər/ (US), /ˈklɪəˌwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|清澈的|The lake was clearwater and we could see the fish swimming below.  湖水清澈，我们可以看到鱼在下面游来游去。|近义词：clear, transparent, crystalline|反义词:murky, cloudy, opaque|adjective
