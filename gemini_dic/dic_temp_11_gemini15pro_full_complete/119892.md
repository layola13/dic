militarist:/ˈmɪlɪtərɪst/| n. |军国主义者|A person who believes in the importance of military strength and advocates an aggressive military policy.|The militarist urged the country to increase its weapons stockpile.|近义词：warmonger, jingoist|反义词：pacifist|noun


militaristic:/ˌmɪlɪtəˈrɪstɪk/| adj. |军国主义的|Advocating or characteristic of militarism; aggressively promoting military values and policies.|The country's militaristic regime was a constant threat to its neighbors.|近义词：hawkish, bellicose|反义词：dovish, pacifistic|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *militariste*, from Latin *militaris* "of a soldier, military," from *miles* "soldier."
