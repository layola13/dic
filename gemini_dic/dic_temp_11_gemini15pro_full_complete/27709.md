candying:/ˈkændiɪŋ/
v.
蜜饯；糖渍
The process of preserving fruit by coating or immersing it in sugar.
例句：She was candying orange peel for the Christmas cake. 她正在为圣诞蛋糕蜜饯橙皮。

例句：The candying process takes several days. 蜜饯过程需要几天时间。

近义词：preserving, conserving, sugaring
反义词：N/A
词性：动词(gerund or present participle)
