accelerator:/əkˈseləreɪtər/ (US), /əkˈsɛləreɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|加速器; 油门|A device, especially the pedal in a motor vehicle, used to increase speed.|She pressed down on the accelerator and the car sped forward. 她踩下油门，汽车向前疾驰。|Synonyms: gas pedal (US), throttle, catalyst|Antonyms: brake, decelerator|noun
