isagogical:/ˌɪsəˈɡɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌɪsəˈɡɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
入门式的，导论的，绪论的
Introductory, pertaining to an introduction or preliminary instruction, especially to a branch of knowledge.
The professor's isagogical lecture provided a helpful overview of the course material. 教授的绪论课对课程资料提供了一个有益的概述。
Near Synonyms: introductory, preliminary, prefatory, prolegomenary
Antonyms: concluding, final, summary, epilogic
词性: adjective
