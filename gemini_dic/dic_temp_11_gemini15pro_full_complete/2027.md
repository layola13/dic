actuose:/ˈæktʃuˌoʊs/
adjective
活跃的；多活动的
Characterized by much action or activity; energetic.
The actuose child never seemed to tire.  这个活跃的孩子似乎永远不知疲倦。

The actuose volcano has been erupting for months. 这座活跃的火山已经喷发了几个月了。
近义词:active, energetic, vigorous, lively
反义词:inactive, inert, passive, sluggish
词性:adjective

词源:From Latin actuosus (“active, busy”), from actus (“act, deed”), past participle of agō (“I do, act, drive”).
