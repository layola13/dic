gratifying:/ˈɡrætɪfaɪɪŋ/| adj.|令人满足的；令人高兴的|Giving pleasure or satisfaction.|例句：It's gratifying to see your hard work pay off. 看到你的努力得到回报是令人欣慰的。|例句：It was a gratifying victory after so many defeats.  在经历了多次失败后，这是一场令人满足的胜利。|近义词：satisfying, rewarding, pleasing, fulfilling|反义词：disappointing, frustrating, disheartening|词性:形容词
词源: From gratify + -ing.  Gratify comes from the Latin gratificārī "to oblige, to please," from gratus "pleasing, agreeable, thankful" + -ficārī, a combining form denoting "to make, to do."
