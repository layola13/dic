determent:/dɪˈtɜːrmənt/ | n. | 阻碍；损害；妨碍 | Something that deters; a hindrance or obstacle. |  His lack of experience was a determent to his career. 他的经验不足阻碍了他的职业生涯。| The heavy rain proved a serious determent to our travel plans.  大雨严重阻碍了我们的旅行计划。| 近义词：hindrance, obstacle, impediment, disadvantage | 反义词：advantage, benefit, aid, assistance | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "deter" (to discourage or prevent from acting), which comes from the Latin "deterrere," formed from "de-" (away from) + "terrere" (to frighten).
