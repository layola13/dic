denser:/ˈdɛnsər/| adj.|更密集的，更稠密的|Comparative form of dense: more dense.  Having parts or molecules packed closely together; crowded or compact.|The fog grew denser as the evening wore on. 傍晚时分，雾越来越浓了。|Synonyms: thicker, heavier, more compact|Antonyms: thinner, lighter, less dense|adjective

dense:/dɛns/| adj.|密集的，稠密的|Closely compacted in substance; crowded together.|The forest was dense with trees. 森林里树木茂密。|Synonyms: thick, compact, crowded|Antonyms: thin, sparse, scattered|adjective
