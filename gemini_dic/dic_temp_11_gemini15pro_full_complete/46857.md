Dardanelles:/ˌdɑːrdəˈnɛlz/ | n. | 达达尼尔海峡 | A narrow strait connecting the Aegean Sea with the Sea of Marmara. |  The Dardanelles is a strategically important waterway. 达达尼尔海峡是一条具有战略意义的水道。 |  Ships pass through the Dardanelles to reach Istanbul. 船只通过达达尼尔海峡到达伊斯坦布尔。 | synonyms: Hellespont | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Medieval Greek *Δαρδανέλλια (Dardanéllia), from Δαρδανέλλια κάστρα (Dardanéllia kástra, “Dardanelles castles”), named after the ancient city of Dardanus nearby.
