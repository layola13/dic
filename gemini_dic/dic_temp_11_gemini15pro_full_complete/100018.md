interpermeate:/ˌɪntərˈpɜːrmiˌeɪt/
verb
互相渗透，弥漫
spread or diffuse through something; pervade
例句：The smell of baking bread interpermeated the house.  烤面包的香味弥漫了整个房子。

例句：The two cultures have interpermeated each other over the centuries.  这两个文化几个世纪以来一直相互渗透。
近义词：pervade, permeate, infuse, saturate, imbue
反义词：separate, isolate, divide
词性:v.
