dealable:/ˈdiːləbəl/
adjective
可商量的，可交易的
able to be dealt with or negotiated.
This price isn't ideal, but it's dealable. 这个价格不理想，但可以商量。
Negotiable, tradable, bargainable
Non-negotiable, intractable, fixed
