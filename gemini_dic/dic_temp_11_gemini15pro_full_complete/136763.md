nonseparating:/nɒnˈsɛpəˌreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不可分的，非分离的 | Not capable of being separated or divided.  A nonseparating curve on a surface does not divide the surface into two disconnected pieces. |  A nonseparating closed curve on a sphere can be continuously deformed to a point. 一个球体上的非分离闭合曲线可以连续变形为一个点。 | The theorem states that a nonseparating curve on a torus bounds a disk. 该定理指出，环面上的非分离曲线界定了一个圆盘。 |  connected, inseparable, indivisible | separating, divisible | adjective
