convulsion:/kənˈvʌlʃən/| n.|抽搐，痉挛，震动，剧变|A convulsion is a sudden, involuntary contraction of muscles, often accompanied by loss of consciousness. It can be a symptom of various medical conditions, including epilepsy, fever, and drug withdrawal.  A convulsion can also refer to a violent social or political upheaval.|例句：The child suffered a convulsion due to the high fever.  这个孩子因为高烧而抽搐。

例句：The country is in a state of political convulsion. 这个国家正处于政治动荡之中。|近义词：spasm, seizure, fit, upheaval, turmoil|反义词:stability, calmness|noun
