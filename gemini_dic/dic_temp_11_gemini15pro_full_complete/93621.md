imbibing:/ɪmˈbaɪbɪŋ/
verb
吸收，吸取；喝；接受（思想、知识等）
absorbing or assimilating something, especially drink or ideas
He was imbibing the culture of the local people. 他在吸收当地人的文化。

例句：
They were imbibing copious amounts of beer.
他们正在喝大量的啤酒。

The students were imbibing knowledge from their teacher.
学生们正在从老师那里汲取知识。

近义词：
absorbing, drinking, taking in, assimilating
反义词：
excreting, emitting, rejecting
