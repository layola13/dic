mummery:/ˈmʌməri/ | n. |  滑稽可笑的举动；虚伪的仪式；装腔作势 | A performance considered ridiculous or hypocritical.  A set of ceremonies or customs that are considered meaningless and outdated. |  The elaborate mummery of the coronation bored the young prince.  加冕礼上繁琐的仪式让年轻的王子感到厌烦。 | synonyms: charade, pretense, farce, sham, ritual, performance | antonyms: sincerity, genuineness, authenticity | noun
