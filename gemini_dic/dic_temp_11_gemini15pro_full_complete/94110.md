impearl:/ɪmˈpɜːrl/
verb
使成珍珠；用珍珠装饰；使…完美
to decorate with pearls; to make something perfect or beautiful.
The dress was impeccably tailored and impearled with tiny seed pearls. 这件衣服剪裁完美，并用小珍珠装饰。
近义词:adorn, embellish, decorate, ornament
反义词:strip, mar, disfigure
词性:v.
