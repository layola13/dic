amplitudes:/ˈæmplɪtjuːdz/| n.|振幅；幅度|The strength of a wave signal, such as sound or light, measured from its center to its peak.|The amplitudes of the sound waves were measured with a sensitive instrument. 声波的振幅是用灵敏的仪器测量的。|Synonyms: magnitude, size, extent|Antonyms: smallness, insignificance|Noun
