challenge: /ˈtʃælɪndʒ/
n. |挑战，质疑|Something that tests strength, skill, or ability. |Climbing Mount Everest is a huge challenge. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一个巨大的挑战。|近义词：test, trial, dare, obstacle|反义词: walkover, breeze, cinch
词性: 名词

challenge: /ˈtʃælɪndʒ/
vt. |挑战，质疑|To invite someone to compete or take part in a contest. |He challenged me to a game of chess. 他向我挑战下棋。|近义词：dare, question, dispute|反义词: accept, concede
词性: 动词
