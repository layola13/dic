loutish:/ˈlaʊtɪʃ/| adj.|粗鲁的，笨拙的，没教养的|Resembling or characteristic of a lout; awkward, clumsy, and unmannerly.|He behaved in a loutish manner, spilling his drink and shouting loudly. 他举止粗鲁，洒了饮料，还大声喊叫。|近义词：boorish, uncouth, clumsy, awkward, oafish|反义词：refined, graceful, polite, courteous|词性:形容词
词源: From lout + -ish.  Lout comes from Middle English *louten, from Old Norse *lūta (“to bend, stoop”).
