delusionist:/dɪˈluːʒənɪst/
n.
魔术师；骗子；(尤指)使人产生错觉的人
A person who practices or specializes in delusion, often by creating illusions or deceptive appearances.
The delusionist amazed the audience with his incredible tricks.  这位魔术师以其令人难以置信的戏法使观众惊叹。
The politician was accused of being a delusionist, manipulating the public with false promises.  这位政客被指控是一个骗子，用虚假的承诺操纵公众。
近义词：illusionist, magician, trickster, charlatan, deceiver
反义词：realist
词性：名词
