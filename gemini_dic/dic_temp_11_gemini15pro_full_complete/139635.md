occluding:/əˈkluːdɪŋ/| adj. | 闭塞的，遮挡的 |  Blocking or obstructing something from view or passage.  The process of one thing hiding another. | The tall building was occluding the sunlight.  高大的建筑遮挡了阳光。| synonyms: blocking, obstructing, covering, obscuring | antonyms: revealing, exposing, uncovering | adjective

occlude:/əˈkluːd/| v. | 闭塞，遮挡 | To stop something from being seen or to block something. | The clouds occluded the sun. 云遮住了太阳。 | synonyms: block, obstruct, cover, obscure | antonyms: reveal, expose, uncover | verb
