atmidometer:/ˌætmɪˈdɒmɪtər/| n. |湿度计；蒸发计|An instrument for measuring the rate of evaporation of water. |The atmidometer showed a high rate of evaporation due to the strong winds. 由于强风，蒸发计显示蒸发率很高。|Synonym: evaporimeter, atmometer|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps hygrometer for measuring humidity which is the opposite of dryness)|noun
