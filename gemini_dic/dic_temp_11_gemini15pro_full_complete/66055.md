exasperating:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人恼怒的；使人烦躁的
intensely annoying; infuriating
Her constant complaining was exasperating. 她不停地抱怨，真是令人恼火。
Synonyms: annoying, irritating, frustrating, infuriating
Antonyms: soothing, calming, pleasing, delightful
词性: 形容词
