ornithichnology:/ˌɔːrnɪθɪkˈnɒlədʒi/
n.
鸟迹学
The study of bird tracks and other trace fossils left by birds.
Ornithichnology can reveal details about the behavior and distribution of extinct bird species. 鸟迹学可以揭示已灭绝鸟类物种的行为和分布细节。
Fossil footprints provide valuable insights into the lives of dinosaurs, a field of study known as ornithichnology. 化石足迹为了解恐龙的生活提供了宝贵的线索，这一研究领域被称为鸟迹学。
近义词:paleoornithology

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὄρνις (órnis, “bird”) + ἴχνος (íkhnos, “track, trace”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
