Etymology->
The name Alena has several possible origins, but the most accepted is that it's a Slavic name, potentially derived from the Proto-Slavic root *elen-, meaning "bright," "shining," or "torch." It is related to names like Helen, Helena, and Elaine, which share a similar origin.  It's particularly popular in Czech Republic, Slovakia, Belarus, Russia, and other Slavic countries. It also sees some usage in Germany and other parts of Europe.
