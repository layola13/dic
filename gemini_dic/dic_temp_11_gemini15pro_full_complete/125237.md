multicuspidate:/ˌmʌltɪˈkʌspɪdeɪt/| adj.|多尖的，多齿的|Having many cusps or points, especially referring to a tooth with multiple cusps or projections.|The dentist identified the molar as multicuspidate. 牙医确认这颗臼齿是多尖齿。|The multicuspidate premolar had several distinct points. 这颗多尖的前磨牙有几个明显的尖头。|Synonyms: multipointed, polycuspidate|Antonyms: unicuspidate, bicuspidate|adjective
