gungn:/ˈɡʌŋ.ən/ | n. | 炮,枪 | A large, heavy gun, typically used in warfare; a firearm. | Example: The gungn fired a powerful shot. 大炮发射了强有力的一击。| Example: He cleaned his gungn carefully after the hunt.  狩猎结束后，他仔细地清洗了他的枪。 | Synonym: cannon, firearm, weapon, artillery | Antonym: peace, disarmament | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *gunna, meaning "woman, wife" (referring to a large cannon named "Mons Meg" in Edinburgh Castle, which was personified as female).  The word evolved to refer to cannons in general, and later to firearms.
