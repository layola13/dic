NVQ:/ˌɛnˌviːˈkjuː/
noun
National Vocational Qualification (英国) 国家职业资格证书
A qualification in a specific occupational area, awarded on the basis of competence demonstrated in the workplace.
She gained an NVQ Level 3 in Childcare. 她获得了三级儿童保育国家职业资格证书。
Synonyms: qualification, certification, diploma
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) lack of qualification, incompetence
