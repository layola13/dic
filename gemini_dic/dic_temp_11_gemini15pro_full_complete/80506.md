good-looking:/ˌɡʊdˈlʊkɪŋ/ | adj. |好看的，漂亮的|Pleasing to the eye; attractive.|例句：He's a good-looking young man. 他是一个英俊的年轻人。

例句：She's always been very good-looking. 她一直都很漂亮。

近义词：attractive, beautiful, handsome, pretty, stunning|反义词：ugly, unattractive, plain, homely|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From good + looking (present participle of look).  The sense evolved from "having a good or kind appearance" to "handsome, attractive" in the 18th century.
