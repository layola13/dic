ornately:/ˈɔːr.neɪt.li/ /ɔːrˈneɪtli/
adjective
华丽地，装饰精美地
in a way that is decorated with complex patterns or shapes
The ornately carved door was impressive.  这扇雕刻精美的门令人印象深刻。
The palace was ornately decorated with gold and jewels.  宫殿装饰华丽，金碧辉煌，珠光宝气。

近义词: elaborately, intricately, lavishly, richly
反义词: plainly, simply, austerely

词性: adverb
词源: From ornate + -ly.  Ornate comes from the Latin word "ornatus," past participle of "ornare" (to adorn, decorate).
