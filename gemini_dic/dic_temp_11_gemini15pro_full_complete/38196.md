compulsiveness:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvnəs/| n.|强迫性，强制性|The state of being compulsive; an irresistible urge to behave in a certain way, especially against one's conscious wishes.|Her compulsiveness led her to check the locks five times before leaving the house. 她的强迫性让她在出门前检查了五次门锁。|近义词：obsession, compulsion|反义词:control, moderation|词性:名词

compulsive:/kəmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj.|强迫性的，强制的|Resulting from or relating to an irresistible urge, especially one that is against one's conscious wishes.|He's a compulsive gambler. 他是个嗜赌成魔的人。|近义词：obsessive, uncontrollable|反义词:controlled, moderate|词性:形容词

compulsively:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvli/| adv.|强迫地，强制地|In a compulsive manner; characterized by compulsion.|She compulsively washes her hands. 她强迫性地洗手。|近义词：obsessively, uncontrollably|反义词:moderately, restrainedly|词性:副词
