indemnified:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪd/
verb|已赔偿的，已保障的|protected against loss or other financial burden;|The contract indemnified the company against any legal action.  合同保障公司免受任何法律诉讼。|compensated, reimbursed, secured, protected|liable, responsible|v.
indemnify:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪ/
verb|赔偿，保障|compensate for loss or damage; secure against future loss or damage;|The insurance company will indemnify you for the damage to your car. 保险公司将赔偿你的汽车损失。|compensate, reimburse, repay| penalize, charge|v.
indemnity:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪti/
noun|赔偿，保障|security or protection against a loss or other financial burden;|The company paid an indemnity to the farmer for his lost crops.  公司向农民支付了赔偿金，以弥补他损失的庄稼。|compensation, restitution, reimbursement|penalty, forfeiture|n.
