caulk: /kɔːk/ | n. vt. | 填缝；填隙料；堵缝 | A waterproof filler and sealant, typically used in building work and repairs. |  He used caulk to seal the gap around the window. 他用填缝料密封了窗户周围的缝隙。 | sealant, filler, putty | gap, opening | noun, verb

caulks: /kɔːks/ | v. | 填塞；堵缝  | Seals (a gap or seam) with caulk.  | He caulks the seams of the boat every year. 他每年都给船的接缝填塞。 | seals, fills | opens | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Northern French *cauquer, from Latin *calicare "to whitewash," from calx "lime."
