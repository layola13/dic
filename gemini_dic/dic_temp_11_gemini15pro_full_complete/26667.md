cactiform:/ˈkæktɪfɔːrm/| adj.|仙人掌状的，类似仙人掌的|Resembling a cactus in form; having a fleshy, succulent stem with spines or scales.|The plant has a distinctive cactiform appearance. 这种植物具有独特的仙人掌状外观。|Synonyms: cactus-like, succulent|Antonyms: leafy, deciduous|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cactus* + *-formis* "having the form of". *Cactus* itself comes from Greek *kaktos*, referring to a spiny plant. *-formis* is a suffix meaning "having the form of," related to *forma* "form, shape."
