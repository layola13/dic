halfasleep: /ˌhɑːfəˈsliːp/ (US), /ˌhɑːfəˈsliːp/ (UK)| adj.|半睡半醒的，昏昏欲睡的|Being in a state between sleeping and waking; drowsy.|例句：I was halfasleep during the movie and missed most of the plot. 我在看电影时半睡半醒，错过了大部分情节。

例句：She stumbled into the kitchen, halfasleep and still in her pajamas. 她跌跌撞撞地走进厨房，半睡半醒，还穿着睡衣。|近义词：drowsy, sleepy, dozy, semiconscious|反义词：awake, alert, wide awake|词性:形容词
