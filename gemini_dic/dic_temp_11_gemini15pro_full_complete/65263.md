eucalyptic:/ˌjuːkəˈlɪptɪk/| adj.|桉树的，有桉树气味的|Relating to or resembling a eucalyptus tree or its leaves; having the smell of eucalyptus. | The air was thick with a eucalyptic aroma. 空气中弥漫着桉树的香味。|The koala's fur smelled distinctly eucalyptic.  考拉的皮毛散发着明显的桉树气味。|近义词：aromatic, fragrant|反义词:odorless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Eucalyptus (the genus of trees) + -ic (forming adjectives). Ultimately from Greek eu- (well, good) + kalyptos (covered), referring to the flower buds being covered by a cap-like operculum.
