cuttingly:/ˈkʌtɪŋli/
adv.
尖刻地，刻薄地
in a way that is hurtful and unkind
Her words were cuttingly sarcastic. 她的话尖酸刻薄。
近义词：sharply, bitterly, cruelly, harshly
反义词：kindly, gently, sympathetically
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "cutting" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Cutting" itself comes from the verb "cut," with roots in Old English "cuttian."
