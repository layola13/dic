caravaneer:/ˌkærəvəˈnɪər/| n.| 旅行队商人；商队队员| A person who leads or travels with a caravan, especially for trade. |  The caravaneer led his camels across the desert. 商队队员带领着他的骆驼穿越沙漠。|Synonyms: trader, merchant, caravan leader|Antonyms: settler, resident|noun

caravaneer:/ˌkærəvəˈnɪər/| v. |带领商队；以商队旅行| To travel with or lead a caravan.  | They caravaneered across the Silk Road. 他们沿着丝绸之路以商队旅行。|Synonyms: journey, trek, travel|Antonyms: settle, stay|verb
