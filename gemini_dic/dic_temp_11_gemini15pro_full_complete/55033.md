disquietedness:/dɪsˈkwaɪɪtɪdnəs/
n.
不安；焦虑；忧虑
A state of unease or anxiety.
Her disquietedness about the upcoming exam was evident. 她对即将到来的考试感到焦虑不安。
The disquietedness in the room was palpable. 房间里的不安情绪是可以感觉到的。
anxiety, uneasiness, worry, perturbation, distress
calm, composure, tranquility, serenity, peace
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From disquiet + -ed + -ness.  Disquiet comes from the Old French *desqueter, from des- (expressing negation) + quiet (quiet).
