catty's:/ˈkætiz/ | n. | 猫咪的; 刻薄女人的; | Belonging to or associated with a catty person (a spiteful or malicious woman). It's the possessive form of "catty," which acts as a noun referring to such a woman or her behavior. |  Example: That was a catty's remark; she's always trying to stir up trouble. 那是一句刻薄的话；她总是试图挑起事端。| Synonym: spiteful, malicious, bitchy | Antonym: kind, compassionate, generous | noun

catty:/ˈkæti/ | adj. | 刻薄的; 恶意的; |  Like a cat; sly, stealthy, or spiteful.  It describes behavior characterized by malicious gossip or backbiting. | Example:  She made a catty comment about her rival's dress. 她对竞争对手的着装发表了刻薄的评论。 | Synonym:  spiteful, malicious, bitchy | Antonym: kind, compassionate, generous | adjective
