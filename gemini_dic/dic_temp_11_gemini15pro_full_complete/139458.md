obstacle:/ˈɑːbstəkl/ (US) /ˈɒbstəkl/ (UK)| n. |障碍，阻碍，妨碍|Something that blocks or hinders progress.|
The biggest obstacle to peace is the lack of trust between the two sides.  双方缺乏信任是和平的最大障碍。

She overcame many obstacles to achieve her goals. 她克服了重重障碍，最终实现了目标。


近义词：barrier, hindrance, impediment, obstruction
反义词：aid, assistance, help, advantage
词性：noun
