gump:/ɡʌmp/
noun
笨人，傻瓜，蠢货
a foolish or stupid person.
例句：He's such a gump, he locked his keys in the car. 他真是个笨蛋，把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be a gump, wear a coat, it's freezing! 别傻了，穿件外套，天冷死了！

近义词：fool, idiot, dolt, simpleton, dunce
反义词：genius, intellect, savant
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain; perhaps related to Low German gump 'lump, bumpkin'. Earliest documented use is from the late 19th century.
