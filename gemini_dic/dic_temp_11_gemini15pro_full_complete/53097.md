diphthonging:/dɪfˈθɒŋɪŋ/ /daɪfˈθɑːŋɪŋ/ | n. |双元音化|The process by which a single vowel sound changes into a diphthong (a combination of two vowel sounds).|The Great Vowel Shift involved a lot of diphthonging of Middle English vowels.|近义词：vowel shift, vowel change|反义词：monophthongization|词性:名词

diphthongize:/dɪfˈθɒŋaɪz/ /daɪfˈθɑːŋaɪz/ | v. |使成双元音|To change a single vowel sound into a diphthong.|The vowel in "house" diphthongized over time.|近义词：change, alter|反义词：monophthongize|词性:动词
