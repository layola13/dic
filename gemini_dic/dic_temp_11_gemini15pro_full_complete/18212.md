beleaguered:/bɪˈliːɡərd/
adjective
被围困的；饱受批评的；处于困境的
Surrounded and under attack; beset with difficulties.
The beleaguered city finally fell to the enemy. 被围困的城市最终落入敌人手中。

The beleaguered prime minister faced calls for his resignation.  饱受批评的首相面临辞职的呼声。

besieged, troubled, harassed, under pressure, under siege
unconcerned, untroubled, carefree, secure

词性: adjective
