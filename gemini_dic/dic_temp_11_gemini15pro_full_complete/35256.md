clunkily:/ˈklʌŋkɪli/
adverb|笨拙地；粗糙地|in a clunky way; awkwardly and noisily.|The robot moved clunkily across the floor.  机器人笨拙地在地板上移动。|The old car clunkily made its way up the hill. 这辆旧车笨拙地爬上了山。|awkwardly, heavily, gracelessly|smoothly, gracefully|词性:副词

clunky:/ˈklʌŋki/
adjective|笨重的；粗糙的；难用的|awkward, heavy, and inefficient|The software is a bit clunky and needs some improvements.  这个软件有点笨重，需要一些改进。|These shoes are clunky and uncomfortable.  这些鞋子笨重且不舒服。|awkward, cumbersome, unwieldy|sleek, elegant, streamlined|词性:形容词
