bribable:/ˈbraɪbəbl/
adjective
可贿赂的；可以收买的
Susceptible to bribery; easily persuaded or influenced by gifts or money.
The official was bribable and accepted the money in exchange for favors.  这位官员可以收买，并接受了钱以换取好处。
The guard was known to be bribable, which made smuggling easier.  这个警卫是出了名的容易收买，这使得走私更容易。
Synonyms: corruptible, venal, dishonest, purchasable
Antonyms: incorruptible, honest, upright, principled
词性: 形容词
