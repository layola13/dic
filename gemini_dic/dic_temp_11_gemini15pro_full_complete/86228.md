heathlike:/ˈhiːθlaɪk/
adjective
类似荒野的，荒地似的
Resembling or characteristic of a heath; heath-like vegetation.
The landscape was barren and heathlike.  这片土地荒芜，像荒野一般。
The garden had a wild, heathlike appearance.  花园里有一种荒凉的，类似荒野的外观。
Synonyms: moorland, heathy, barren, desolate
Antonyms: fertile, lush, verdant
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From heath + like.  Heath refers to an area of open uncultivated land, typically with sandy soil and covered with shrubs and low-growing vegetation. The suffix "-like" means resembling or characteristic of.
