eurobabble:/ˈjʊərəʊˌbæbəl/
noun
空洞的欧洲政治言论;官样文章
Jargon and clichés used in discussions about European politics, often perceived as meaningless or evasive.
The meeting descended into eurobabble, with no concrete decisions being made. 会议沦为空洞的欧洲政治言论，没有做出任何具体的决定。
The politician's speech was full of eurobabble and offered no real solutions.  这位政治家的演讲充满了官样文章，没有提供任何真正的解决方案。
近义词: jargon, clichés, officialese, doublespeak
反义词: plain speaking, straightforward language
词性: n.
