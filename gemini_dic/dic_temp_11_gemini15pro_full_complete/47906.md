decentralized: /diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 分散的，去中心化的 | Distributed or spread out over a wider area or among several individuals or organizations, rather than concentrated in one location or controlled by a single entity.  |  Example:  A decentralized network is more resistant to attacks than a centralized one. 分散式网络比集中式网络更能抵御攻击。

Example: The company decided to decentralize its operations to empower regional offices. 公司决定分散运营，赋予区域办事处更多权力。 | Synonyms: distributed, dispersed, scattered, diffused | Antonyms: centralized, concentrated, unified | adjective


decentralise: /diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. | 分散，去中心化 |  To distribute or spread out power, control, or resources away from a central authority or location. | Example: They plan to decentralise the decision-making process. 他们计划将决策过程分散化。


Example:  Many companies are decentralising their operations to reduce costs and increase efficiency. 许多公司正在分散运营以降低成本并提高效率。 | Synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve, delegate | Antonyms: centralize, concentrate, consolidate | verb
