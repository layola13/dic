dinnerwares:/ˈdɪnərˌwɛərz/ | n. |餐具|Dishes, plates, cups, and other utensils used for serving and eating a meal.  A collective term referring to all items used for setting a table for dining. |We need to buy new dinnerwares for our new apartment. 我们需要为我们的新公寓买新的餐具。 |Synonyms: tableware, crockery, dishware |Antonyms: None (lack of a true opposite; one might consider "bare table" as a conceptual antonym)| noun
