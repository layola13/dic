disposition:/ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃən/ /ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃn/
n.|性情，性格；倾向；支配，处置|A person's inherent qualities of mind and character.  The power to deal with something as one pleases.|He has a cheerful disposition. 他性格开朗。|Synonyms: temperament, character, personality, nature, bent, inclination, tendency|Antonyms:  indisposition, aversion|noun

disposition:/ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃən/ /ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃn/
n.|安排；部署|The arrangement or placing of things in a particular order.|The disposition of troops was carefully planned. 部队的部署经过精心策划。|Synonyms: arrangement, placement, ordering, organization|Antonyms: disorganization, disorder|noun
