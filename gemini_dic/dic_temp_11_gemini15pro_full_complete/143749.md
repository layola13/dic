outmanipulates:/ˌaʊtməˈnɪpjuleɪts/| v. | 巧妙地胜过，以策略战胜 | To defeat or gain advantage over someone by using clever or devious tactics, especially in a manipulative way. | She outmanipulated her opponents in the negotiations and secured a favorable deal. 她通过谈判中的策略，战胜了对手，获得了一份有利的协议。

He outmanipulated the competition by spreading false rumors. 他通过散布谣言，以策略战胜了竞争对手。

| 近义词: outsmart, outmaneuver, outwit | 反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted | 词性: 动词
