lumbering:/ˈlʌmbərɪŋ/| adj.| 笨拙的，缓慢移动的|Moving in a slow, heavy, and awkward way. |The elephant made its lumbering way across the savanna. 大象笨拙地穿过大草原。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, ponderous, slow|反义词：agile, nimble, graceful|词性:形容词

lumbering:/ˈlʌmbərɪŋ/| n.|伐木；木材|The process of felling trees and cutting and preparing the timber.|Lumbering is a major industry in this region. 伐木是这个地区的主要产业。|近义词：logging, timbering|反义词: 无|词性:名词


词源:  From the verb "lumber" (meaning "to move heavily and clumsily"), which is of uncertain origin, possibly from Middle Dutch lommeren ("to rumble").
