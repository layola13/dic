miming:/ˈmaɪmɪŋ/ | n. | 模仿；哑剧；假唱 | the act of using movements of your body and face to express something or tell a story without speaking |  He entertained the children by miming a monkey. 他模仿猴子来逗孩子们开心。 | synonyms:  imitation, impersonation, pantomime | antonyms: speaking, verbalizing | noun

miming:/ˈmaɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 模仿；做哑剧；假唱 | to use movements of your body and face to express something or tell a story without speaking | She mimed eating an apple. 她模仿吃苹果的动作。 | synonyms: imitate, impersonate, pantomime | antonyms: speak, verbalize | verb
