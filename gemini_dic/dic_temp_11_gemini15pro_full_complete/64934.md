estates:/ɪˈsteɪts/| n.|房地产；财产；遗产；身份，地位|A large area of land in the country, usually with a large house on it, or all the land and buildings owned by someone.|He owns several estates in Scotland. 他在苏格兰拥有几处庄园。|近义词：property, land, possessions, holdings|反义词:debts|名词

estates:/ɪˈsteɪts/| n.|（尤指作为政治阶层的）社会等级，阶级|One of the groups of people who had power in a country in the past, such as the nobles, the clergy, and the ordinary people.|The three estates of the realm were the nobles, the clergy, and the commons. 王国的三个等级是贵族、神职人员和平民。|近义词：class, rank, social order|反义词:none|名词
