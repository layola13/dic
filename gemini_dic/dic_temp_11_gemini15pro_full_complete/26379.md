by-lines:/ˈbaɪ laɪnz/ | n. | 作者署名；(文章)署名行 | A line at the beginning or end of a piece of writing in a newspaper or magazine that gives the writer's name. | The article appeared with no by-line. 这篇文章没有署名发表。| synonyms: attribution, credit line | antonyms: anonymous, uncredited | noun

by-lines:/ˈbaɪ laɪnz/ | n. | 副业；兼职 | A secondary occupation. | He makes some extra money through various by-lines. 他通过各种副业赚些外快。| synonyms: sideline, side hustle, second job | antonyms: main job, primary occupation | noun
