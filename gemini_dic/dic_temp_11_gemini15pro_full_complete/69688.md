fenestrate:/ˈfɛnɪˌstreɪt/| adj. v. |有窗的，开窗的；穿孔的|Having windows or openings like windows; pierced with openings.|The architect designed a fenestrated facade for the building.|近义词:perforated, pierced, windowed|反义词:solid, unperforated|词性:形容词, 动词


Etymology: From Latin *fenestratus*, past participle of *fenestrare* "to furnish with windows," from *fenestra* "window."
