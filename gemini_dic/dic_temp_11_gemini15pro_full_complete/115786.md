marionettish:/ˌmærɪəˈnɛtɪʃ/ |adj.|傀儡般的；木偶似的|Resembling or characteristic of a marionette, especially in being jerky, stiff, or unnatural in movement or manner.|Her marionettish movements made her look like a toy soldier. 她的傀儡般的动作使她看起来像个玩具士兵。|The dancer's marionettish gestures seemed forced and unnatural.  舞者的木偶般的手势显得做作而不自然。|Synonyms: puppet-like, jerky, stiff, mechanical, robotic, wooden|Antonyms: fluid, graceful, natural, supple|adjective
