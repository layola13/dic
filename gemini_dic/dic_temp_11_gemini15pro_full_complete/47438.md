deafen:/ˈdefn/ (US), /ˈdefən/ (UK)| v. |使聋，使听不见，震耳欲聋|To make someone temporarily or permanently unable to hear, or to make a loud noise that is unpleasant or overwhelming. |The explosion deafened several people. 爆炸使几个人失聪。

The loud music deafened me. 吵闹的音乐震耳欲聋。| synonyms: stun, overwhelm, drown out | antonyms: quiet, soothe | verb
