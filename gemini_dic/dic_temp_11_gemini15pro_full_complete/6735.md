analyzer:/ˈænəlaɪzər/ (US)| n.|分析器，分析仪|A device or program used for analyzing data or materials.|The lab uses a network analyzer to monitor internet traffic.|近义词: analyser (UK spelling), evaluator, interpreter|反义词: synthesizer|词性:名词


词源: From the verb "analyze" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments).  "Analyze" comes from the French "analyser," from the Late Latin "analysare," from the Greek "analyein," meaning "to loosen up, dissolve."
