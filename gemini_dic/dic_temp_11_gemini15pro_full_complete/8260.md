anti-institutional:/ˌæntiˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənl/
adjective
反体制的，反制度的
Opposed or hostile to established institutions or the system in general.
His anti-institutional stance made him unpopular with the university administration.  他反体制的立场使他不受大学行政部门的欢迎。
The punk rock movement was known for its anti-institutional lyrics and rebellious attitude.  朋克摇滚运动以其反体制的歌词和叛逆的态度而闻名。
近义词:  nonconformist, rebellious, iconoclastic, dissident, radical
反义词:  conformist, establishment, orthodox, traditional
词性:  adjective
