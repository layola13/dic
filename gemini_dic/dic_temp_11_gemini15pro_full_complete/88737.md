hobos:/ˈhoʊboʊz/ (US) /ˈhəʊbəʊz/ (UK)| n.|流浪汉，游民|A homeless person, especially one who travels from place to place.|Example 1:  The hobos gathered around the fire to share stories and warmth. 流浪汉们围在火堆旁分享故事和温暖。

Example 2: During the Great Depression, many people became hobos, riding the rails in search of work. 在大萧条时期，许多人变成了流浪汉，乘坐火车四处寻找工作。|近义词：vagrant, tramp, drifter|反义词:resident, settler|词性:noun
