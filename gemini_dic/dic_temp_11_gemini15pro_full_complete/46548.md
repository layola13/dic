dallasite:/ˈdæləsaɪt/
n.
达拉斯陨石，橄榄陨铁
A type of stony-iron meteorite composed primarily of olivine and nickel-iron.
Dallasite is a rare type of pallasite meteorite. 达拉斯陨石是一种稀有的橄榄陨铁。
Synonyms: pallasite
Antonyms: N/A
