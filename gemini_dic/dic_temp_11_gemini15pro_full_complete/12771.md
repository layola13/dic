assignment:/əˈsaɪnmənt/|/əˈsaɪnmənt/| n.|任务，作业，分配|The teacher gave us a reading assignment. 老师给我们布置了一项阅读作业。|Synonyms: task, duty, job, project, exercise|Antonyms: leisure, freedom|noun

assignment:/əˈsaɪnmənt/|/əˈsaɪnmənt/| n.|分配，指派|The assignment of tasks was done fairly. 任务分配得很公平。|Synonyms: allocation, allotment, distribution|Antonyms: collection, gathering|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English assignement, from Middle French assignement, from assigner (“to assign”), from Latin assignare (“to mark out, allot, assign, ascribe, attribute”), from ad + signare (“to mark, seal”), from signum (“a mark, sign, seal”).
