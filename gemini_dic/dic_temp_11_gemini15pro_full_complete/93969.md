imod:/ˈaɪmɒd/ (UK) /ˈaɪmɑːd/ (US)| n. | internet moderator | An internet moderator is someone who oversees online communities and forums, ensuring that discussions remain civil and within the rules. | Example: The imod quickly deleted the offensive post.  这位网络管理员迅速删除了冒犯性的帖子。| Example:  The forum's imods work hard to keep the community a positive space.  论坛的管理员努力工作，以保持社区的积极氛围。|Synonym: moderator, online moderator, forum moderator, admin| Antonym:  troll (someone who deliberately disrupts online discussions), spammer | Noun
