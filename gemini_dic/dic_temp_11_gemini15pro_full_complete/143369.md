outfeasted: /ˌaʊtˈfiːstɪd/
verb
胜过宴请;  比…宴请得更丰盛
To have provided a more lavish or impressive feast than someone else.
He outfeasted all his rivals, serving a seven-course meal with exotic delicacies. 他设宴款待所有的对手，胜过他们所有人，菜肴是七道菜的异国珍馐。
The king was outfeasted by the visiting emperor, whose banquet featured a hundred different dishes. 国王的宴会被来访的皇帝比了下去，皇帝的宴会上有一百道不同的菜肴。
近义词: outdo, surpass, eclipse
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed
