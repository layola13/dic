letterers:/ˈlɛtərərz/| n.|字体设计师，写字员，题写者|People who draw, paint, or inscribe letters, especially in an artistic or stylized way. They may work on signs, posters, book covers, or other projects that require lettering.|The letterers worked tirelessly to complete the elaborate sign for the new theater.  字体设计师们不知疲倦地为新剧院完成了精心设计的招牌。


The experienced letterers carefully crafted each letter of the manuscript.  经验丰富的写字员精心制作了手稿中的每一个字母。|近义词：calligraphers, sign painters, typographers, scribes|反义词: N/A|Noun
