contraremonstrant:/ˌkɒntrəˈrɛmənstrənt/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈrɛmənstrənt/
n.
抗议者，反对者
A person who opposes a remonstrance or protest; one who argues or protests against something.
The contraremonstrants argued fiercely against the proposed changes to the church doctrine.  抗辩者强烈反对教会教义的拟议修改。
He was a vocal contraremonstrant against the king's policies. 他是一位直言不讳地反对国王政策的抗议者。
opponent, dissenter, protestor
supporter, advocate
noun
