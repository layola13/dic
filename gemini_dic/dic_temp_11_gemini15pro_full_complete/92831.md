iconomania: /ˌaɪkɒnəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 肖像狂；圣像崇拜；肖像画收藏癖 | An excessive fondness for images or icons, especially religious ones.  It can also refer to a passion for collecting images or portraits. |  He had an iconomania, filling his house with religious icons from around the world. 他患有肖像狂，把家里摆满了来自世界各地的宗教圣像。

|  Her iconomania extended beyond religious images to include portraits of historical figures. 她的肖像狂热延伸到宗教图像之外，还包括历史人物的肖像。 | 近义词: iconolatry, image worship | 反义词: iconoclasm | 词性: noun
