例句：We planted a forsythia bush near the front door for a splash of color in early spring. 我们在前门附近种了一丛连翘，为早春增添一抹色彩。

词源:

*  **forsythia:** Named after William Forsyth (1753-1804), a Scottish botanist.
* **Forsyth:**  A Scottish and Irish surname, likely derived from a place name.  Possibly meaning "from the ford by the willows" (from elements *ford* and *saileach* "willow").
