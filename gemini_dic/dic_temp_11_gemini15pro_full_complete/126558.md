nadir:/ˈneɪdɪr/ /ˈnædɪr/ |adj. n.|最低点，最底点|The nadir of his career came when he was fired.  他的职业生涯的最低谷出现在他被解雇的时候。|例句：The stock market reached its nadir in March. 股市在三月份跌到了最低点。|近义词：bottom, lowest point, rock bottom|反义词：zenith, peak, apex, summit|词性：名词，形容词
Etymology: From Arabic نظير naẓīr  "opposite, counterpart," specifically referring to the nadir as the opposite of the zenith.
