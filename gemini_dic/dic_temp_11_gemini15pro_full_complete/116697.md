maturest:/məˈtʊərɪst/ /məˈtʃʊərɪst/
adjective
最成熟的
Superlative form of mature: most mature.  Having reached the highest stage of development.
例句：He exhibited the maturest judgment in a crisis.  他在危机中展现出了最成熟的判断力。

例句：She chose the maturest of the available options. 她选择了现有方案中最成熟的那一个。

近义词：ripened, developed, perfected, seasoned
反义词：immature, undeveloped, green, raw
词性: adjective
