affiliable:/əˈfɪliəbəl/| adj.|可加入的，可附属的|Capable of being affiliated; suitable for affiliation.  Relating to or allowing affiliation.|The local club is affiliable to a national organization.  这个地方俱乐部可以加入一个全国性的组织。|近义词:associable, attachable, joinable|反义词:independent, unaffiliated|词性:形容词
词源:From affiliate + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *affiliāre, from ad- + filius (“son”).
