dromaeognathous:/ˌdroʊmiːˈɒɡnəθəs/
adjective
奔龙类的；驰龙类的
Having a jaw structure characteristic of dromaeognathous birds, a group that includes modern birds and their closest extinct relatives, characterized by a joint in the lower jaw that allows for increased mobility and a wider gape.
The *Velociraptor*, a dromaeognathous dinosaur, was a fearsome predator.  迅猛龙，一种驰龙类恐龙，是一个可怕的捕食者。
Fossil evidence suggests that *Archaeopteryx* was dromaeognathous. 化石证据表明始祖鸟是驰龙类的。
Synonym:  avian (in a broader sense)  更广义的：鸟类的
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical classification) 没有直接的反义词，因为它是一个特定的解剖学分类
词性: adjective
