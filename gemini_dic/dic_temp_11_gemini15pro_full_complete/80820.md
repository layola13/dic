gotool:/ˈɡoʊtuːl/| n.| 走工具; 首选工具; 常用工具 |A go-to tool is one that is regularly used or relied upon, especially because it is effective or easy to use. |Example:  My go-to tool for graphic design is Photoshop. 我进行图形设计的常用工具是Photoshop。

Example:  For quick calculations, my phone is my go-to tool.  对于快速计算，我的手机是我的首选工具。 |Synonyms:  preferred tool, favorite tool, essential tool, main tool |Antonyms:  unused tool, neglected tool |noun
