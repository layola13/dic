hypertechnicality:/ˌhaɪpərˌtɛknɪˈkælɪti/ | n. | 过于技术性的细节；钻牛角尖 | An excessive focus on minor technical details, often to the detriment of understanding the bigger picture. | The lawyer's argument was bogged down in hypertechnicalities, obscuring the main issue. 律师的论点陷入了过于技术性的细节，模糊了主要问题。 |  The contract was filled with legal hypertechnicalities that made it difficult to understand.  合同中充满了法律上的过于技术性细节，使其难以理解。 |近义词：nitpicking, hairsplitting, quibbling |反义词: simplification, generalization | 词性: noun
