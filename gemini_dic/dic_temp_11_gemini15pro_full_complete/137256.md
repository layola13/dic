nonsweetened:/ˌnɒnˈswiːtənd/ /ˌnɑːnˈswiːtənd/
adjective
未加糖的；无糖的
Not having any sugar added.
Example: I prefer nonsweetened tea. 我更喜欢无糖茶。
Example:  She always buys nonsweetened yogurt. 她总是买无糖酸奶。
Synonym: unsweetened, sugar-free
Antonym: sweetened, sugary
词性: 形容词
