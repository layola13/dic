disclaunder:/dɪsˈklɔːndər/
verb
为...洗清污名；澄清；辩白
to deny responsibility for; disavow. To clear from accusation or blame.
例句：He disclaundered himself from the accusations of theft. 他为自己洗清了偷窃的指控。

例句：The lawyer worked tirelessly to disclaunder her client. 律师不知疲倦地工作，为他委托人洗清罪名。

近义词：vindicate, acquit, exonerate, absolve, clear
反义词：incriminate, accuse, blame, condemn
词性：v.
