inventive:/ɪnˈvɛntɪv/ | adj. | 有创造力的；有发明才能的 | Having the ability to create or design new things or think original thoughts. |  She has a very inventive mind and is always coming up with new ideas. 她有着非常有创造力的头脑，总是能想出新点子。|  creative, imaginative, ingenious | uninspired, unimaginative | adjective

Etymology->
From Late Latin *inventivus, from invent- the past participle stem of invenire "to come upon, find, discover, devise," from in- "in, on" + venire "to come."
