disestablished:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃt/
| adj. |被剥夺特权的，被取消建制的 | Deprived of its established status, especially of a church no longer recognized as the official church of a state; abolished as an established institution. | The Church of England was disestablished in Wales in 1920.  威尔士教会于1920年被取消了国教地位。| synonyms: abolished, revoked, withdrawn, repealed | antonyms: established, instituted, authorized | adjective


disestablish:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃ/
| vt. |取消…的建制，剥夺…的特权 | To deprive (an established church) of its official status; to abolish as an established institution. | The government voted to disestablish the state church. 政府投票决定取消国教的地位。| synonyms: abolish, dismantle, dissolve, terminate | antonyms: establish, found, institute, set up | verb
