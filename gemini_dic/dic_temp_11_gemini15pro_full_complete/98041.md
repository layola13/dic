inosite:/ˈɪnəˌsaɪt/| n. |肌醇|A cyclic alcohol often found in plants and animals, important for cell function.  It exists in several isomeric forms, the most prevalent being myo-inositol.|Myo-inositol is often used as a dietary supplement.|Synonym: cyclohexanehexol|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound like inositol)|Noun
