interclashing:/ˌɪntərˈklæʃɪŋ/
adjective
相互冲突的；互相碰撞的
Characterized by conflict or collision between different elements or groups.
The interclashing opinions of the committee members made it difficult to reach a decision. 委员会成员相互冲突的意见使得难以达成决议。
The interclashing swords of the duelists created a shower of sparks.  决斗者相互碰撞的剑产生了一阵火花。
近义词: conflicting, clashing, colliding, opposing
反义词: harmonious, agreeing, concordant
词性: adjective

词源:
"Inter-" (prefix): between, among, mutually. From Latin "inter" (between, among).
"Clashing" (present participle):  meeting and hitting with force and a loud noise. From Old Norse "klask" (to clap, dash).
