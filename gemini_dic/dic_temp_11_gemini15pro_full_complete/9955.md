apheliotropic:/ˌæfɪliˈəʊtrɒpɪk/ /ˌeɪfɪliˈoʊtrɑːpɪk/
adjective
背光的；避光的
Turning away from or avoiding sunlight.
The apheliotropic plant grew away from the direct sunlight.  这种背光植物生长方向远离阳光直射。
The fungus exhibited apheliotropic behavior, retreating into the shade.  这种真菌表现出避光性，躲到阴凉处。
negative phototropic, negatively phototropic
phototropic, heliotropic
adjective
