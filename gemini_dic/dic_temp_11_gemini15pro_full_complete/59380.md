earplug:/ˈɪə(r)ˌplʌɡ/| n.|耳塞|A device, typically made of soft material like foam or silicone, that is inserted into the ear canal to reduce noise levels or to protect the ear from water, dust, or other foreign bodies.  It can be used in various situations, including sleeping, studying, working in noisy environments, swimming, and attending concerts.  |I needed earplugs to block out the snoring of my roommate. 我需要耳塞来阻挡室友的鼾声。|synonyms: ear protector, ear defender|antonyms: N/A|noun
