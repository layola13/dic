designedly:/dɪˈzaɪnɪdli/
adverb
故意地
Intentionally; on purpose.
She designedly left the door open. 她故意把门开着。
He designedly avoided mentioning her name. 他故意不提她的名字。
近义词: intentionally, purposely, deliberately, knowingly, wittingly
反义词: accidentally, unintentionally, unknowingly, unwittingly
词性: 副词


词源:
From design + -ed + -ly.  "Design" comes from the Old French "desseignier" (to mark out, designate), ultimately from Latin "designare" (to mark out).
