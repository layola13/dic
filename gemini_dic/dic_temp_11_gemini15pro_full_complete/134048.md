nonidentity:/ˌnɒnaɪˈdɛntɪti/
n.|非同一性，不同一|The principle of nonidentity states that A is not not-A.  非同一性原则指出A不是非A。|
nonidentity expresses the lack of sameness or equivalence. 非同一性表达了缺乏相同性或等价性。|
Synonyms: difference, dissimilarity, distinctness, inequality, divergence|
Antonyms: identity, sameness, equivalence, similarity|
词性: noun
