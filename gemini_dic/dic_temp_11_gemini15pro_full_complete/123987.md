morn:/mɔːrn/
noun
早晨，清晨
The morning period of time.  The time from sunrise to noon.
The hunter awoke at morn. 猎人清晨醒来。
Synonyms: morning, dawn, daybreak, sunrise
Antonyms: evening, night, dusk, sunset
词性: 名词
