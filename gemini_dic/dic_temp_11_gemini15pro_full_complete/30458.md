centricity:/senˈtrɪsɪti/ | n. |中心性；以…为中心 |The state or quality of being centric; having or revolving around a center. | The company's centricity on customer needs has been key to its success. 该公司以客户需求为中心的策略是其成功的关键。 | synonyms: centrality, focus, concentration | antonyms: periphery, marginality | noun


centricity:/senˈtrɪsɪti/ | n. |中心地位；核心地位 |The quality of being central or dominant in importance or influence. | The city's centricity in the region's economy is undeniable. 该城市在区域经济中的中心地位不可否认。 | synonyms: dominance, prominence, importance | antonyms: insignificance, unimportance | noun
