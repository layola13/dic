democratizations: /dɪˌmɒkrətɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |民主化（democratization的复数形式）| The process of making something more democratic, involving greater participation by the people in political decision-making. | The democratizations of Eastern Europe after the fall of the Soviet Union were a significant historical event.  东欧在苏联解体后的民主化进程是一个重大的历史事件。|  The recent democratizations in some African countries have been met with mixed reactions.  一些非洲国家近期的民主化进程受到了褒贬不一的反应。 | synonyms:  liberalizations, reforms, transformations | antonyms:  authoritarianism, oppression, repression | noun
