crang:/kræŋ/
n.|狭窄的岩石裂缝，峭壁|A narrow fissure in rock, especially on a cliff face.|The climbers found a handhold in a crang in the cliff face. 登山者在悬崖峭壁的裂缝中找到了一个抓手。|fissure, crevice, crack|chasm, canyon|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *krang, likely related to krankr ("crooked, bent").  Cognate with Norwegian krang ("crooked timber, frame").
