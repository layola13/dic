antarchistic: /ˌæntɑrˈkɪstɪk/ | adj. | 反对等级制度的，反对统治的 | Opposed to hierarchies and rule; against any form of government or authority.  It describes a belief system that rejects all forms of imposed authority and hierarchy. |  "His antarchistic views led him to believe that all forms of government were inherently corrupt."  他的反对等级制度的观点使他相信所有形式的政府本质上都是腐败的。 | Synonyms: anarchistic, anti-authoritarian | Antonyms: authoritarian, hierarchical | adjective

antarchistic: /ˌæntɑrˈkɪstɪk/ | n. | 反对等级制度者，反对统治者 | A person who opposes hierarchies and rule; against any form of government or authority. |  "He was considered an antarchistic by his peers due to his constant questioning of authority." 他因不断质疑权威而被同龄人视为反对等级制度者。 | Synonyms: anarchist, anti-authoritarian | Antonyms: authoritarian, statist | noun
