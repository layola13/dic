disposited:/dɪˈspɒzɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有…倾向的；被安排好的 | Placed or arranged in a particular way; inclined or willing. | The funds were disposited according to the terms of the will. 资金按照遗嘱的条款进行分配。|  The troops were disposited strategically along the border. 部队沿着边界战略性地部署。| 近义词: arranged, placed, inclined, predisposed | 反义词: disinclined, unwilling | 词性: adjective

词源：From Latin *dispositus*, past participle of *disponere* (“to arrange, dispose”), from *dis-* (“apart”) + *ponere* (“to place, put”).
