interorganizational:/ˌɪntərˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃənl/
adjective
组织间的；跨组织的
Existing or occurring between organizations.
例句：Interorganizational collaboration is crucial for tackling complex social problems. 组织间的合作对于解决复杂的社会问题至关重要。

例句：The interorganizational network facilitated the sharing of best practices.  跨组织网络促进了最佳实践的共享。

近义词：inter-agency, cross-organizational, collaborative
反义词：intraorganizational
词性：adjective
