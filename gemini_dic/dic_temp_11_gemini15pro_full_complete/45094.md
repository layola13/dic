cuca:/ˈkuːkə/ | n. |鬼怪，妖怪；虫子(主要指巴西葡萄牙语)|A mythical creature, often used to scare children in Brazilian folklore. Also, a general term for bug or insect (primarily in Brazilian Portuguese).|Example: The children were afraid of the cuca that supposedly lived in the woods. 孩子们害怕据说住在树林里的鬼怪。

Example:  There's a cuca on your arm!  你胳膊上有个虫子！|Synonym:  (mythical creature) boogeyman, monster, goblin; (insect) bug, insect |Antonym: (mythical creature) hero, protector | noun
