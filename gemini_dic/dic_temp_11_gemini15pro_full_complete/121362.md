misgrounded:/ˌmɪsˈɡraʊndɪd/
adjective
根据错误的理由；基于错误的理解
based on incorrect reasons or on a misunderstanding

The argument was completely misgrounded, relying on outdated statistics and flawed logic.
这个论点完全是基于错误的理解，依赖于过时的统计数据和有缺陷的逻辑。

His fears were misgrounded; the company was actually doing very well.
他的恐惧是没有根据的；公司实际上做得很好。


synonym: unfounded, baseless, fallacious, ill-founded, spurious
antonym: well-founded, justified, valid, sound, legitimate
词性: adjective
