merostomata:/mɛrəˈstɒmətə/ | n. | 肢口纲 | An arthropod class of chelicerates, characterized by having book gills on the underside of the opisthosoma.  This class includes horseshoe crabs. |  Merostomata are ancient marine creatures. 肢口纲是古老的海洋生物。| synonym: Xiphosura (Note: Some sources consider Xiphosura a subclass within Merostomata) | antonym: N/A | noun

merostomate:/mɛrəˈstɒmeɪt/ | n. | 肢口纲动物 | An animal belonging to the Merostomata class, such as a horseshoe crab. | The horseshoe crab is a well-known merostomate.  鲎是著名的肢口纲动物。| synonym: horseshoe crab | antonym: N/A | noun
