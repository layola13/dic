mercedes:/mərˈseɪdiːz/ | n. |梅赛德斯|A brand of luxury automobiles produced by Daimler AG.  It is named after Mercédès Jellinek, the daughter of Emil Jellinek, who was a European automobile entrepreneur who worked with Daimler Motoren Gesellschaft.| He named the 1901 Mercedes 35 hp after her. |例句：He drives a Mercedes. 他开一辆梅赛德斯。

例句：The Mercedes is known for its luxurious features. 梅赛德斯以其豪华的配置而闻名。|近义词：car, automobile, vehicle|反义词: (No direct antonym for a brand name, could consider cheaper car brands)|词性:noun
