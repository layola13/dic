incooerdination:/ɪnkɔːˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/
n.|不协调，失调|Lack of coordination, especially of muscular movements; inability to make different parts of the body work smoothly together.|His incoordination made it difficult for him to play the piano. 他的不协调让他很难弹钢琴。|incoordination, clumsiness, awkwardness|coordination, dexterity|noun
