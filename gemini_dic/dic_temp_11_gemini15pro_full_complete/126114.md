muted:/ˈmjuːtɪd/ (US), /ˈmjuːtɪd/ (UK)| adj.|柔和的，减弱的，沉默的|Reduced in volume or intensity; softened; silent or subdued.|The muted colors of the painting created a calming atmosphere.  这幅画柔和的色彩营造出一种宁静的氛围。|The trumpet player played a muted note. 小号手吹奏了一个弱音。|近义词：subdued, softened, hushed, quiet, silent|反义词：bright, loud, vibrant, strong|词性:形容词

词源: From the past participle of *mute* (verb), from Old French *mut* "mute, silent," ultimately from Latin *mutus* "dumb, silent."
