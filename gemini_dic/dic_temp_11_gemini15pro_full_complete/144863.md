over-process:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːses/ |v.|过度加工; 过度处理|To process something excessively, often to the point of detriment.  To subject something to too many steps in a process, resulting in a negative outcome.|The coffee beans were over-processed, resulting in a bitter flavor. 咖啡豆过度加工，导致味道苦涩。|The photographer over-processed the image, making it look unnatural. 摄影师过度处理了图像，使其看起来不自然。|overdo, overwork|under-process|verb
