dangle:/ˈdæŋɡəl/| v. n.|悬挂，吊着；诱惑|To hang loosely or swing back and forth.  To offer something desirable to someone.|
The earrings dangled from her ears. 耳环从她的耳朵上垂下来。

He dangled the prospect of promotion in front of her. 他用升职的前景来诱惑她。

|近义词：hang, swing, suspend, tempt, entice|反义词:fix, secure|词性:动词, 名词


dangle:/ˈdæŋɡəl/| n.|悬挂物|Something that hangs loosely or swings back and forth.|
The mobile was a collection of colorful dangles. 这个风铃是一堆五颜六色的悬挂物。
