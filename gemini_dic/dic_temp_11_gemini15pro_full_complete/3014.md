aerial:/ˈɛəriəl/ (US), /ˈeəriəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|空中的，航空的；天线|Existing or occurring in the air or atmosphere; from a plane or other flying vehicle; a radio or television antenna.|例句：The aerial view of the city was breathtaking. 城市的鸟瞰图令人叹为观止。


例句：We installed a new aerial on the roof. 我们在屋顶上安装了一个新的天线。


近义词：airborne, atmospheric, antenna (n.)|反义词:ground, terrestrial|词性:adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aereus, from Latin aer "air," from Greek aer (ἀήρ).  The noun sense "antenna" first appeared in the early 20th century.
