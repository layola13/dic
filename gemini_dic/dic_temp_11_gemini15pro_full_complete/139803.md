octile:/ˈɒktaɪl/ (US) /ˈɒktaɪl/ (UK)| adj. n.|八分位数的，八分相|Of or relating to an aspect of 1/8th of a circle; an octagon.  Relating to every eighth; occurring every eighth.  (Noun) An aspect of 1/8th of a circle; an octagon.|The octile aspect between planets is considered challenging in astrology. 行星之间的八分相位在占星学中被认为是具有挑战性的。
