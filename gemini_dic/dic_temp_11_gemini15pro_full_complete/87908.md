hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. n. | 象形文字的；象形符号 | relating to a system of writing that uses pictures or symbols to represent words or sounds; a picture or symbol used in this way | The ancient Egyptians used hieroglyphics to record their history. 古埃及人使用象形文字记录他们的历史。 |The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphics. 罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。| synonyms: ideogram, pictogram | antonyms: letter, alphabet |词性: adjective, noun

hieroglyph:/ˈhaɪərəɡlɪf/ | n. | 象形文字；象形符号 | a picture or symbol used in a system of writing, such as that of ancient Egypt |  The walls of the tomb were covered in hieroglyphs. 墓穴的墙壁上覆盖着象形文字。 | Each hieroglyph represented a word or concept. 每个象形文字代表一个单词或概念。 | synonyms: ideogram, pictogram | antonyms: letter, alphabet |词性: noun
