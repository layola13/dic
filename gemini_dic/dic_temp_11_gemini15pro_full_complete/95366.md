incessant:/ɪnˈsesənt/| adj.|不停的，不断的|Continuing without interruption; ceaseless.|The incessant noise of the traffic kept me awake all night. 交通不停的噪音让我整夜无法入睡.|近义词：constant, continuous, perpetual, unremitting|反义词:intermittent, occasional, sporadic|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin incessantem (nominative incessans) "unceasing, not ceasing," present participle of incessare "not to cease, to continue, proceed," from in- "not" (from PIE root *en "in") + cessare "to cease, stop, give up" (from PIE root *ked- "to fall, yield").
