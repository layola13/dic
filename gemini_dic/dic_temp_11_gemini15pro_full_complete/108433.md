lawyerlike: /ˈlɔːjərlaɪk/ | adj. | 像律师的；精明而谨慎的；严谨的 | Resembling or characteristic of a lawyer, especially in being precise, argumentative, or cautious. |  He had a lawyerlike approach to the problem, carefully considering all the angles. 他以律师般的方式处理这个问题，仔细考虑了所有角度。|例句：Her lawyerlike attention to detail ensured the contract was flawless. 她像律师一样注重细节，确保合同完美无缺。| 近义词：precise, meticulous, cautious, scrupulous, discerning | 反义词：careless, negligent, reckless, impulsive | 词性: 形容词

词源: lawyer + like.  "Lawyer" comes from the Anglo-French *lawer,  from Old French loier "to hire, engage in legal business," from Latin locare "to hire, let, lease, place."  "Like" comes from Old English *gelic "similar, equal, like," from Proto-Germanic *galikaz.
