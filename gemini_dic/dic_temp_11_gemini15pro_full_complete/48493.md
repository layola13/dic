deepest:/ˈdiːpɪst/| adj.|最深的，最深厚的|At its deepest point, the ocean is nearly seven miles deep. 在最深处，海洋将近七英里深。|近义词：profoundest, furthest, most intense|反义词：shallowest, slightest|词性:形容词
词源：From Old English *dēopest, superlative of dēop (“deep”). Cognate with West Frisian djipst, Dutch diepst, German tiefst.
