deleting:/dɪˈliːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 删除中，删除的 | Present participle of "delete," meaning in the process of removing or erasing something, typically data. |  例句：The system is currently deleting temporary files. 系统目前正在删除临时文件。

例句：He is deleting the photos from his phone. 他正在从手机里删除照片。


近义词：removing, erasing, expunging | 反义词：restoring, recovering, saving | 词性：形容词
