hectostere:/ˈhɛktəstɪər/ | n. |一百立方米|A unit of volume in the metric system equal to 100 cubic meters.  It's primarily used for measuring quantities of firewood.|The forestry report measured the harvested timber in hectosteres. 该林业报告以百立方米为单位测量采伐的木材。| synonym: stere (though representing a different quantity), cubic meter | antonym: N/A (units of volume don't typically have direct opposites) | noun
