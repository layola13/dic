handywork:/ˈhændiwɜːrk/ | n. | 手工制品；手工艺；杰作（常指不好的）| Something made by hand, especially something showing skill;  (often disapproving) the results of someone's actions, especially when they are unpleasant |例句：The intricate carvings are a testament to the artisan's handywork. 这些精美的雕刻证明了工匠的精湛技艺。

例句：The broken window and graffiti were clearly the handywork of vandals. 破碎的窗户和涂鸦显然是破坏者的杰作。| 近义词：craft, handiwork, workmanship, creation | 反义词：destruction, ruin | 词性: 名词
