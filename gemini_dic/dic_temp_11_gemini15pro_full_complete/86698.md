hellsgate:/ˈhɛlzɡeɪt/| n. |地狱之门，极其危险或混乱的地方|A place of turmoil or great danger.  It often refers to a narrow, turbulent strait or waterway.|例句：The rapids in that section of the river are a veritable hells gate. 那一段河流的急流简直就是地狱之门。|例句：Navigating the crowded streets during rush hour felt like going through hellsgate. 在高峰时段穿过拥挤的街道感觉就像穿过地狱之门。|近义词：danger zone, death trap, inferno, maelstrom|反义词:safe haven, sanctuary|词性:noun

词源:
Compound of "hells," the possessive form of "hell," and "gate," meaning an entrance or passage.  It evokes the imagery of a gateway to hell, a place of torment and destruction.
