inoperculate:/ɪnɒˈpɜːkjʊlət/ | adj. | 无盖的，无厣的 | Lacking an operculum; having an opening that is not closed by a lid or flap. | The inoperculate snail is vulnerable to predators.  这只无盖的蜗牛很容易受到捕食者的攻击。 | Synonyms: lidless, coverless | Antonyms: operculate | adjective
