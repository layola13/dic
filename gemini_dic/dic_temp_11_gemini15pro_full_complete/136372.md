nonresponsibility:/ˌnɒnrɪˌspɒnsɪˈbɪləti/
n.
无责任，不负责任，免责
lack of responsibility; the state of not being responsible.
The contract included a clause of nonresponsibility for damage caused by natural disasters.  合同中包含一项关于自然灾害造成的损害免责的条款。
The company claimed nonresponsibility for the faulty product. 公司声称对有缺陷的产品不负责任。
近义词: irresponsibility, unaccountability, blamelessness
反义词: responsibility, accountability, liability
词性: 名词
