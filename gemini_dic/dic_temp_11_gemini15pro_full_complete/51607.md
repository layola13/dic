devastators:/ˈdevəˌsteɪtərz/
noun
破坏者，毁灭者
People or things that cause widespread destruction.
The hurricane proved to be one of the most powerful devastators in the region's history.  飓风被证明是该地区历史上最强大的破坏者之一。
The devastators of the environment must be held accountable.  环境的破坏者必须被追究责任。
synonyms: destroyers, ravagers, wreckers
antonyms: creators, builders, preservers
词性: 名词(plural)

devastate:/ˈdevəˌsteɪt/
verb
摧毁，毁灭
To destroy or ruin something completely.
The earthquake devastated the city, leaving thousands homeless. 地震摧毁了这座城市，使数千人无家可归。
The news devastated her. 这消息让她崩溃了。
synonyms: ruin, destroy, wreck
antonyms: build, create, restore
词性: 动词

devastating:/ˈdevəˌsteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
毁灭性的，极具破坏力的
Causing widespread destruction or damage.
The fire had a devastating effect on the local wildlife.  这场大火对当地野生动物造成了毁灭性的影响。
She received devastating news about her family. 她收到了关于她家人的毁灭性消息。
synonyms: destructive, ruinous, catastrophic
antonyms: beneficial, helpful, constructive
词性: 形容词
