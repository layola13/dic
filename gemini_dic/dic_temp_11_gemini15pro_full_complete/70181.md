fibrillary:/ˈfɪbrɪləri/ /faɪˈbrɪləri/| adj.|纤维状的；原纤维的|Composed of or resembling fibrils; characterized by fibrils.|Amyloid plaques, composed of fibrillary aggregates of misfolded proteins, are a hallmark of Alzheimer's disease.  淀粉样斑块，由错误折叠蛋白质的纤维状聚集体组成，是阿尔茨海默病的标志。|The doctor identified fibrillary structures in the biopsy sample. 医生在活检样本中发现了纤维状结构。|近义词：fibrous, filamentous|反义词: non-fibrous|词性: adjective

fibril:/ˈfaɪbrɪl/| n.|原纤维；细丝|A small or slender fiber or filament, often one of a bundle or group that forms a larger fiber or structure.|Collagen fibrils provide structural support to connective tissues. 胶原纤维为结缔组织提供结构支撑。|Muscle fibers are made up of smaller myofibrils. 肌纤维由更小的肌原纤维组成。|近义词：fiber, filament|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fībra* ("fiber") + *-illus* (diminutive suffix) + *-āris* (adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ser- (“to join, tie”).*
