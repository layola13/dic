cluelessly:/ˈkluːləsli/| adv.|茫然地，无知地|Without any understanding or awareness; in a clueless manner.  It describes an action performed without knowledge or awareness.|She stared at the complex equation cluelessly. 她茫然地盯着复杂的方程式。|Synonyms: blankly, bewilderedly, vacantly, ignorantly, naively|Antonyms: knowingly, intelligently, perceptively, astutely|adverb
