maxillipedary:/mækˈsɪlɪˌpedəri/ | adj. n.| 颚足的；颚足 |Relating to or denoting a maxilliped; a maxilliped.  A maxilliped is an appendage modified for feeding, located near the mouth of a crustacean. They are typically the first three pairs of thoracic appendages. |例句：The crayfish uses its maxillipeds to manipulate food towards its mouth. 小龙虾用它的颚足将食物送到嘴里。

|例句：The maxillipedary structure is crucial for the crab's feeding habits. 颚足结构对螃蟹的摄食习性至关重要。|近义词：None (highly specific term) |反义词: None (highly specific term)|词性: Adjective, Noun
