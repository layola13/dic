lithargyrum:/lɪˈθɑːrdʒərəm/ | n. |  密陀僧，氧化铅 | Lead oxide (PbO), a yellowish or reddish crystalline or amorphous powder, used in making flint glass, as a pigment, and in the manufacture of lead salts. |  Litharge is used in the production of some types of glass. 密陀僧用于生产某些类型的玻璃。|  The ancient Egyptians used litharge in cosmetics. 古埃及人用密陀僧制作化妆品。 | synonyms: lead monoxide, lead(II) oxide | antonyms: N/A | noun
