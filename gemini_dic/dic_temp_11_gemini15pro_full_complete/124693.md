movingly:/ˈmuːvɪŋli/| adv.|动人地，感人地|In a way that causes strong feelings of sadness or sympathy.  |She spoke movingly about her experiences. 她动情地讲述了她的经历。|近义词：touchingly, poignantly, emotionally|反义词：apathetically, indifferently|词性:副词
词源: From "moving" (present participle of "move") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "movēre" (to move).
