cheerled:/ˈtʃɪərlɛd/ | v. | 欢呼，喝彩 | Past tense of "cheerlead".  The act of leading cheers, typically at a sporting event. |  She cheerled for her team at the championship game. 她在冠军赛上为她的队伍加油助威。 |  She had cheerled for him in all his races.  她在他的所有比赛中都为他加油。 | synonyms: rooted for, supported, encouraged | antonyms: booed, jeered, heckled | verb
