leucosphere:/ˈljuːkəsfɪər/ | n. |白圈; 白层; |The leucosphere is the innermost layer of the Sun's atmosphere, lying above the photosphere and below the corona.  它是太阳大气层的最内层，位于光球层上方和日冕下方。|The temperature of the leucosphere increases with altitude. 白圈的温度随着高度的增加而升高。|Near synonyms: chromosphere, inner corona|Antonym: None|Noun
