outpushing:/ˌaʊtˈpʊʃɪŋ/
|n.|外推，向外推进|The outpushing of the company's influence into new markets was rapid.  公司影响力向新市场的扩张非常迅速。|
|v.|（向外）推进，扩张|The company is outpushing its rivals in the electric car market. 该公司在电动汽车市场上正在超越其竞争对手。|
|Etymology:| out- (prefix) + push (verb) + -ing (suffix).  "Out-" indicates direction away from a center or beyond a limit. "Push" means to exert force on (someone or something) in order to move them away from oneself. "-ing" forms the present participle or gerund of the verb.|
|Synonyms:| expanding, extending, broadening, spreading, reaching out|
|Antonyms:| contracting, shrinking, withdrawing, retreating|
|Word Type:| Noun/Gerund (primarily),  Verb (less common)|
