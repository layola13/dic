nonascetically:/nɒnəˈsɛtɪkli/
adv.
非禁欲地，不节制地
not ascetically; in a way that is not characterized by abstinence from worldly pleasures.
He lived nonascetically, indulging in fine food and wine. 他过着非禁欲的生活，沉溺于美酒佳肴。
Synonyms: indulgently, luxuriously, hedonistically
Antonyms: ascetically, austerely, abstemiously
词性: 副词
