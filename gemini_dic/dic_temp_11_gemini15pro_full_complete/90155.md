hotfoot:/ˈhɑːtfʊt/ /ˈhɒtfʊt/| v. |匆匆离去，急忙赶往|to go somewhere quickly, especially because you are in a hurry|例句：He hotfooted it out of the room when he saw his boss coming. 他看到老板来了，就匆匆离开了房间。

例句：She hotfooted it to the store to get the ingredients for dinner. 她急忙赶到商店去买晚餐的食材。|近义词：hurry, rush, dash, hasten|反义词：dawdle, linger|词性：动词

hotfooted:/ˈhɑːtfʊtɪd/ /ˈhɒtfʊtɪd/| adj. |匆忙的，急匆匆的|done or acting in a hurried way|例句：He arrived, hotfooted and breathless, just as the meeting was starting. 他气喘吁吁地赶到，正好会议开始。

例句：She made a hotfooted escape from the paparazzi. 她匆忙逃离了狗仔队。|近义词：hurried, rushed|反义词：leisurely, unhurried|词性：形容词
