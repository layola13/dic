douce:/duːs/| adj.| 甜美的，温柔的，温和的|Sweet, gentle, and agreeable in disposition or manner.  Referring to a person, it describes a pleasant and amiable personality.  Can also refer to a mild or soft flavor or smell.|She had a douce smile and a calming presence. 她有着甜美的笑容和令人平静的气质。|The air was filled with the douce scent of honeysuckle. 空气中弥漫着忍冬花的芬芳。|近义词：sweet, gentle, mild, pleasant, amiable|反义词：harsh, severe, unpleasant, disagreeable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *douls, *dous, from Latin *dulcis, from dulcis “sweet.”  Related to English "dulcet."
