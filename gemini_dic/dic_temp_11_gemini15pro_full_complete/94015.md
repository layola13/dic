impanate: /ɪmˈpeɪneɪt/ | adj. | 包上面包屑的；裹上面糊的 | Covered with breadcrumbs or batter, typically before being fried. | The chef impanated the chicken breasts before frying them.  厨师在煎鸡胸肉之前裹上面包屑。 | Example: The impanated fish fillets were crispy and golden brown. 裹上面包屑的鱼片酥脆金黄。 | Synonym: breaded, crumbed | Antonym: unbreaded, plain | Adjective


impanate: /ɪmˈpeɪneɪt/ | vt. | 在...上撒面包屑；裹上面糊 | To cover something with breadcrumbs or batter. | He impanated the eggplant slices and fried them until golden. 他把茄子片裹上面糊，炸至金黄色。 | Example:  She impanated the pork chops before baking them in the oven. 她把猪排裹上面包屑，然后在烤箱里烤。 | Synonym: bread, crumb | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but the process can be reversed by removing the breading) | Verb
