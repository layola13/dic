miscreates:/ˌmɪskrɪˈeɪts/
verb
vt.
错误地创造；歪曲；滥用
creates wrongly; distorts; misuses.
The artist felt he had miscreated his vision, the final product a pale imitation of what he'd intended.  这位艺术家觉得他错误地创造了他的愿景，最终的成品与他预想的相去甚远。
He felt the director had miscreated his play, turning a subtle drama into a farce. 他觉得导演歪曲了他的戏剧，把一出微妙的戏剧变成了闹剧。
近义词:distort, pervert, deform, corrupt
反义词:create, perfect, enhance
词性:verb
