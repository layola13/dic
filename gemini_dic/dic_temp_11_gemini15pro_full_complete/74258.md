foxish:/ˈfɒksɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksɪʃ/ (US)| adj.|狡猾的，诡计多端的|Resembling or suggestive of a fox, especially in cunning, slyness, or alertness.|He had a foxish grin that made me distrust him. 他狡黠地一笑，让我不信任他。|The lawyer used a foxish strategy to win the case.  这位律师用了一个狡猾的策略赢得了这场官司。|近义词：cunning, sly, crafty, wily, artful, deceitful|反义词：honest, naive, ingenuous, artless|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *foxisshe, equivalent to fox + -ish.  Related to Old English fox “fox” and -ish.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fuhsaz.
