intricacies: /ˈɪntrɪkəsiːz/
noun
复杂性，错综复杂，细微之处
the complicated details of something
例句：She was fascinated by the intricacies of the clock mechanism. 她对钟表机制的复杂性很着迷。

例句：I don't want to get involved in the intricacies of their family arguments. 我不想卷入他们家庭纠纷的复杂细节中。

近义词：complexities, complications, subtleties
反义词：simplicity, straightforwardness
词性：n. (plural)
