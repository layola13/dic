endogamy:/ˈɛndɒɡəmi/ (UK) /ˈɛndoʊɡəmi/ (US)| n. |族内婚，同族结婚|The custom of marrying only within the limits of a local community, clan, or tribe.|The isolated community practiced strict endogamy.|Synonyms: in-marriage, intermarriage (within a specific group)|Antonyms: exogamy, out-marriage|noun
endogamy:/ˈɛndɒɡəmi/ (UK) /ˈɛndoʊɡəmi/ (US)| n. |内婚制|Marriage within a specific group as required by custom or law.|Endogamy was practiced to maintain the purity of the royal bloodline.|Synonyms: inmarriage|Antonyms: exogamy|noun
