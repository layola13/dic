crotalaria: /krɒtəˈleəriə/ | n. | 猪鹿豆；野百合| A large genus of chiefly tropical or subtropical herbs and shrubs (family Leguminosae) having usually yellow flowers and inflated pods. |  Crotalaria is used as a cover crop to improve soil health. 猪鹿豆被用作覆盖作物以改善土壤健康。| Crotalaria is grown as a green manure in some regions.  在一些地区，猪鹿豆被种植用作绿肥。 | synonyms: rattlebox, rattlepod | antonyms: N/A | noun
