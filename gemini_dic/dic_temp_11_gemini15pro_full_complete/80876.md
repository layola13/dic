governability:/ˌɡʌvərnəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可 управляемость; 可治理性; 受控性 | The quality or state of being governable; the extent to which a state or other political unit is capable of being governed. | The country's governability is in question due to the ongoing political crisis.  这个国家的可治理性由于持续的政治危机而受到质疑。 | Synonyms: controllability, manageability, tractability | Antonyms: ungovernability, unmanageability, intractability | noun
