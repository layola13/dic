extruder:/ɪkˈstruːdər/| n.|挤出机，压出机|A machine that forces material through a shaped opening to produce a continuous length of product.|The extruder is used to create plastic pipes.|近义词:expeller, press|反义词:N/A|名词

extrude:/ɪkˈstruːd/| vt.|挤出，压出，推出|To shape (a material such as metal or plastic) by forcing it through a die.|The plastic is extruded into thin sheets.|近义词:expel, force out|反义词:inject, insert|动词

extrusion:/ɪkˈstruːʒən/| n.|挤压，挤出，挤出物|The process or action of extruding something.|The extrusion of molten plastic forms various shapes.|近义词:expulsion, ejection|反义词:injection, insertion|名词
