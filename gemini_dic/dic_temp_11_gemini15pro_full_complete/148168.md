overservices:/ˌōvərˈsɜːrvɪsɪz/
verb
过度服务；服务过剩
to provide too many services, often unnecessarily
The hotel staff overserviced the guests, constantly interrupting them to ask if they needed anything.  酒店员工过度服务客人，不断打断他们询问是否需要什么。
overserved, overserving
underserved
