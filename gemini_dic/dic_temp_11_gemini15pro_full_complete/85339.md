My friend is a talented harpsichordist and performs regularly. 我的朋友是一位才华横溢的大键琴演奏家，经常演出。 | 近义词: harpsichord player | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From harpsichord + -ist.  "Harpsichord" comes from the Italian "arpicordo," a shortened form of "arpa a corda," meaning "harp with strings."
