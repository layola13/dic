foreflow:/ˈfɔːrfloʊ/| n.|前流，预流，先行流 (computing, technical)|A flow of data or information that precedes the main flow.|Example: The foreflow of data from the sensors allows the system to anticipate potential issues.  传感器数据的预流允许系统预测潜在问题。|Example:  A foreflow of information about the market trends helped the company make better investment decisions.  关于市场趋势的先行信息流帮助公司做出了更好的投资决策。|近义词：preliminary flow, advance flow, leading edge|反义词: afterflow, backflow|词性:noun
