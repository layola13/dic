admirer:/ədˈmaɪərər/ | n. | 钦佩者；仰慕者；爱慕者 |  A person who has a high opinion of someone or something and often expresses praise or affection. |  She has many secret admirers. 她有很多秘密的仰慕者。

He is a great admirer of Picasso's work. 他是毕加索作品的狂热崇拜者。

近义词: fan, devotee, follower, supporter | 反义词: critic, detractor | 词性: noun
