alternately:/ˈɔːltərneɪtli/ /ˈɑːltərnətli/| adv.|交替地，轮流地|By turns; one after the other.  Changing repeatedly between two contrasting states or things.|The two teams alternately attacked and defended.  两支队伍交替进攻和防守。|Synonyms: by turns, in rotation, one after the other, in succession, consecutively|Antonyms: simultaneously, concurrently, at the same time|adverb
