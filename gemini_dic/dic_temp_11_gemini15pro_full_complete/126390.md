myroxylon: /ˌmaɪrɒkˈsaɪlɒn/ | n. | 香脂树，秘鲁香脂树 | A tropical American tree (Myroxylon balsamum) of the legume family, yielding balsam of Peru.  It's a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae. |  Myroxylon is the source of balsam of Peru, a fragrant resin used in perfumes and some medicines. 秘鲁香脂树是秘鲁香脂的来源，这是一种用于香水和一些药物的芳香树脂。| synonym: balsam tree | antonym: N/A | noun
