handsomer:/ˈhændsəmər/ | adj. | 更英俊的 | Comparative form of handsome. Used to describe someone (typically male) as more attractive in appearance than another person or than they were previously. | He's handsomer than his brother. 他比他哥哥更英俊。|  He looks handsomer with the beard. 他留着胡子看起来更英俊。 | 近义词:more attractive, better-looking, finer | 反义词: uglier, less attractive | 词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *handsomere, comparative of handsom; equivalent to hand + -some.
