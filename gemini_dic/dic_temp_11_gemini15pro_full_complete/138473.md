novellas:/nəˈvɛləz/ | n. | 中篇小说 | A short novel, longer than a short story but shorter than a typical novel.  | Example: She enjoyed reading novellas because they offered a more satisfying narrative than short stories but didn't require the time commitment of a full-length novel. 她喜欢读中篇小说，因为它们比短篇小说更有叙事性，但不需要像长篇小说那样花费时间。

| Example: He published several novellas before attempting his first full-length novel. 在尝试他的第一部长篇小说之前，他出版了几部中篇小说。 | 近义词: novelette, short novel | 反义词: novel, epic | 词性: noun
