bardo:/ˈbɑːrdoʊ/ | n. | 中阴身；过渡期；中间状态 | A state of existence between death and rebirth, varying in length according to a person's conduct in life and manner of, or age at, death. Also, any transitional period or state of existence. |  Example 1:  The Tibetan Book of the Dead describes the bardo state. (《西藏度亡经》描述了中阴身的状态。)

Example 2:  He felt he was in a kind of bardo, waiting for his life to begin again. (他觉得自己处于一种过渡期，等待着人生重新开始。) | Synonyms: limbo, purgatory, transition, interregnum | Antonyms: permanence, stability | noun
