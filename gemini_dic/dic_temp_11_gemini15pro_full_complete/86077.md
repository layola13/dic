ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *herkenen*, from Old English *heorcn(i)an* (“to hearken, listen, obey”), from Proto-Germanic *hauzjaną* (“to hear, listen”). Cognate with Dutch *horken* (“to listen, eavesdrop”), German *horchen* (“to listen”), Swedish *harka* (“to rake, scrape”).  The ending "-ing" indicates the present participle form of the verb.
