monospace:/ˈmɑːnəˌspeɪs/ /ˈmɒnəˌspeɪs/ | adj. n.|等宽的，等宽字体|A typeface in which all characters occupy the same amount of horizontal space.|The document is printed in monospace.|近义词：fixed-width, non-proportional|反义词:proportional|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: "mono" (Greek: μόνος, mónos, "single, alone") + "space"
