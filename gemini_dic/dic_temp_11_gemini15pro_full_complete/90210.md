hounding:/ˈhaʊndɪŋ/| v.| 追踪；纠缠；不断骚扰|The paparazzi were hounding the celebrity relentlessly. 狗仔队不断地骚扰着这位名人。|例句：The creditors were hounding him for the money. 债主们一直缠着他要钱。

例句：She was hounded by the press after the scandal broke.  丑闻曝光后，她被媒体穷追猛打。|近义词：harassing, pursuing, persecuting|反义词: ignoring, leaving alone|词性:动名词(gerund) or present participle

hounding:/ˈhaʊndɪŋ/| adj.| 持续不断的；折磨人的|The constant hounding of the press was taking its toll.  媒体持续不断的骚扰让她付出了代价。|例句：The hounding pain in her back made it difficult to sleep. 背部持续的疼痛使她难以入睡。

例句：He lived in hounding fear of being discovered. 他一直生活在被发现的恐惧之中。|近义词：relentless, persistent, unremitting|反义词: intermittent, occasional|词性:形容词

词源：From the verb "hound," which comes from Old English "hund" (dog).  源自动词“hound”（猎犬），它来自古英语“hund”（狗）。
