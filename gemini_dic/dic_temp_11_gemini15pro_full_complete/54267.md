disgraduate:/dɪsˈɡrædʒueɪt/ | vt.|剥夺学位；降级|To deprive of a degree or academic rank. |He was disgraduated for plagiarism. 他因为剽窃被剥夺了学位。|The university disgraduated him for cheating on his exams.  大学因为他考试作弊而取消了他的学位。|近义词：expel, dismiss, degrade|反义词：graduate, promote|Verb

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + graduate (to grant an academic degree or diploma).
