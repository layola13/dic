decongregate:/diːˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/
verb
分散，疏散
To break up a gathering or crowd; to cause people to disperse.
The police decongregated the protesters. 警察驱散了抗议者。
The crowd decongregated after the concert. 音乐会结束后，人群散开了。
synonyms: disperse, scatter, break up, disband
antonyms: congregate, assemble, gather, collect
词性: 动词
