non-resident:/ˌnɒnˈrezɪdənt/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈrezɪdənt/ (US)| adj. n.|非居民的，非定居的；非居民|A person who does not live permanently in a particular place.|例句：Non-resident students pay higher tuition fees. 非居民学生支付更高的学费。|近义词：foreigner, alien, outsider|反义词:resident, local, native|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + resident.  "Resident" comes from the Latin "residentem," the present participle of "residēre" meaning "to remain behind."
