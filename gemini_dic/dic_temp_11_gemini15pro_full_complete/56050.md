docent:/ˈdoʊsənt/ | n. | 讲解员，博物馆讲解员 | A person who acts as a guide, typically on a voluntary basis, in a museum, art gallery, or zoo. | The docent led the tour group through the museum, explaining the history of each exhibit.  讲解员带领旅游团参观博物馆，讲解每个展品的来历。| synonyms: guide, lecturer, instructor, teacher | antonyms: student, pupil | noun

docent:/ˈdəʊs(ə)nt/ | n. | 大学讲师(尤其指美国) | A university teacher, especially in the US, who ranks below assistant professor. |  The docent gave a fascinating lecture on the history of medieval architecture.  这位讲师做了一个关于中世纪建筑史的精彩讲座。 | synonyms: lecturer, instructor, professor, teacher | antonyms: student, pupil | noun
