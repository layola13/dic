disestablishment:/dɪsɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/
n.
撤销；废除；解除
The act of depriving a church or other religious organization of its official status.
The disestablishment of the Church of England is a complex issue.  英国教会的废除是一个复杂的问题。
The disestablishment of the state religion was a major political event. 国教的废除是一项重大的政治事件。
synonyms: abolition, revocation, annulment, termination
antonyms: establishment, institution, founding
