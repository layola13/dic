cost-benefit:/ˌkɔːst ˈbɛnɪfɪt/ |adj. n.|成本效益，利弊|A cost-benefit analysis is a process used to measure the benefits of a decision or taking action minus the costs associated with taking that action.|Example:  A cost-benefit analysis showed that the project was not financially viable. 成本效益分析表明该项目在财务上不可行。|Synonym: cost-effectiveness,  pros and cons|Antonym: unprofitable, wasteful|adjective, noun

cost-benefits:/ˌkɔːst ˈbɛnɪfɪts/ |n.|成本效益，利弊 |The cost-benefits of the new system are still being evaluated.  新系统的成本效益仍在评估中。|Synonym: advantages and disadvantages,  trade-offs|Antonym: drawbacks, disadvantages|noun
