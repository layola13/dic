inventive:/ɪnˈvɛntɪv/| adj.|有发明才能的；有创造力的|Having the ability to create or design new things or think original ideas.|She has a very inventive mind and always coming up with new ideas for projects.|近义词：creative, imaginative, ingenious, resourceful|反义词：unimaginative, unoriginal, uninventive|词性:形容词
inventious:/ɪnˈvɛnʃəs/| adj.|善于发明创造的；足智多谋的|Skillful at inventing; having or showing an unusual aptitude for inventing, devising, or contriving.|He was an inventious cook, always experimenting with new ingredients and flavors.|近义词：inventive, creative, ingenious, resourceful|反义词：unimaginative, unoriginal, uninventive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inventitiosus, from inventus (past participle of invenire ‘to find’), from in- ‘into’ + venire ‘to come’.  Related to invent. The -ious suffix forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
