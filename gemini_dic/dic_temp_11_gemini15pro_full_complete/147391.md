overnice:/ˈoʊvərˌnaɪs/| adj.|过分友善的，过分殷勤的|Excessively nice; too polite or agreeable, often to the point of seeming insincere or affected.|She was so overnice to the new boss that it seemed fake. 她对新老板过分殷勤，显得有些虚伪。|近义词：excessively polite, overly kind, unctuous, obsequious|反义词：rude, impolite, unfriendly|词性:形容词
词源: over- + nice.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, indicating an excess of the quality denoted by "nice."
