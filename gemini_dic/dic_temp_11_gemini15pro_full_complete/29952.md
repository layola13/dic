cavemen:/ˈkeɪvmen/ (US), /ˈkeɪvmən/ (UK)| n.|穴居人|A person who lived in a cave in prehistoric times.|例句：Cavemen used simple tools made of stone and bone. 穴居人使用由石头和骨头制成的简单工具。

例句：The museum exhibit depicted the daily life of cavemen. 博物馆展览描绘了穴居人的日常生活。|近义词：troglodyte, prehistoric human|反义词:modern human|词性:noun
