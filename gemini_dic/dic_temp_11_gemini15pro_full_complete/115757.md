marinade:/ˈmærɪneɪd/| n. vt.|腌泡汁，腌泡|A sauce, typically made of oil, vinegar, spices, and herbs, in which meat, fish, or other food is soaked before cooking in order to flavor or tenderize it.  The act of soaking food in a marinade.|I marinated the chicken in a mixture of lemon juice, garlic, and herbs. 我把鸡肉浸泡在柠檬汁、大蒜和香草的混合物中。|近义词：sauce, dressing|反义词:|n. & vt.
