magnetic:/mæɡˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. |磁的，有磁性的，有吸引力的|Relating to magnetism; having the properties of a magnet, able to attract. Also, very attractive or alluring.|例句：The magnetic field of the Earth protects us from harmful radiation. 地球的磁场保护我们免受有害辐射。|例句：She has a magnetic personality; everyone is drawn to her. 她很有魅力，每个人都被她吸引。|近义词：attractive, alluring, charismatic|反义词：repellent, unappealing|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *magneticus*, from *magnes* (“magnet, lodestone”), from Greek *Μάγνης* (*Magnēs*, “Magnesian (stone)”).  The stone was named after Magnesia, a region in Thessaly, Greece, where it was found.
