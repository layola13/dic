oversee:/ˌoʊvərˈsiː/ /ˌoʊvərˈsiː/
verb
监督，监管
to watch or organize a job or an activity to make certain that it is being done correctly
The teacher oversaw the students' work. 老师监督学生们的作业。

例句：
The manager oversees the daily operations of the store.  经理监督商店的日常运营。

The committee was appointed to oversee the implementation of the new policy.  该委员会被任命监督新政策的实施。


近义词：supervise, monitor, manage, direct, control
反义词：neglect, ignore
词性：v.
