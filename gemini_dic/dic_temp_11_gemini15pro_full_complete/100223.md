interrogatively:/ˌɪntəˈrɒɡətɪvli/ | adv. | 疑问地，探询地 | In a manner that asks a question; questioningly. | She looked at him interrogatively. 她用 вопросительный взгляд 看着他。| synonym: inquiringly, questioningly | antonym: declaratively, affirmatively | adverb

interrogative:/ˌɪntəˈrɒɡətɪv/ | adj. | 疑问的，质问的 | Having the form or force of a question. |  An interrogative sentence ends with a question mark. 疑问句以问号结尾。| synonym: questioning, inquisitive | antonym: declarative, affirmative | adjective

interrogative:/ˌɪntəˈrɒɡətɪv/ | n. | 疑问词 | A word used to ask a question. | "Who," "what," and "where" are interrogatives.  “谁”、“什么”和“哪里”都是疑问词。| synonym: question word, wh-word | antonym:  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin interrogātīvus, from Latin interrogātus (past participle of interrogāre ‘to question’), from inter- ‘between’ + rogāre ‘to ask’.
