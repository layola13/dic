dungan:/ˈdʌŋɡən/| n. |东干人；东干语|A member of a group of Muslims of Chinese origin, living mainly in Kyrgyzstan and Kazakhstan, or their language (a form of Mandarin Chinese written in Cyrillic).  东干族，主要居住在吉尔吉斯斯坦和哈萨克斯坦的中国血统穆斯林群体，或指他们的语言（一种用西里尔字母书写的官话变体）。|例句：The Dungan people have a rich culture. 东干族有着丰富的文化。

例句：He is studying the Dungan language. 他正在学习东干语。|近义词：Hui (sometimes), Chinese Muslim (general term)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
