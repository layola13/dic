conversos:/kənˈvɛərsəʊz/ (UK), /kənˈvɜːrsoʊz/ (US)| n.|皈依者（尤指14-15世纪西班牙和葡萄牙被迫皈依基督教的犹太人及其后裔）|People who have converted, especially Jewish people in 14th and 15th century Spain and Portugal who were forced to convert to Christianity, and their descendants.|Many conversos continued to practice Judaism in secret. 许多皈依者仍然秘密地信奉犹太教。|Synonyms: converts, proselytes|Antonyms: None (inherently describes a change, lacks a direct opposite)|Noun
