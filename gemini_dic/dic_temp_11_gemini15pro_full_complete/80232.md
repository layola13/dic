goel: /ɡoʊɛl/ | n. | 血亲复仇者; 为被杀的亲人复仇的人 |  A person who is responsible for avenging the death of a relative, especially in traditional or customary law. |  In some cultures, the goel is obligated to pursue and punish the killer. 在某些文化中，血亲复仇者有义务追捕并惩罚凶手。|  The family appointed a goel to avenge their son's murder.  这个家庭指定了一个血亲复仇者为他们儿子的被杀复仇。 | synonyms: avenger, blood avenger | antonyms: protector, guardian | noun
