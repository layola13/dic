indissolubly:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbli/ | adv. | 不可分解地，牢不可破地 | In a manner that cannot be dissolved or separated. |  The two concepts are indissolubly linked. 这两个概念紧密相连。| 近义词: inseparably, permanently, inextricably | 反义词: solubly, separably | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *indissolubilis, from in- "not" + dissolubilis "dissoluble," from dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen."
