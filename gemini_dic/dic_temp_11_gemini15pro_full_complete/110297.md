light: /laɪt/ | adj. n. v. | 轻的，光，点燃 | Something that is light doesn't weigh very much; the natural agent that stimulates sight and makes things visible; to ignite something | The feather is light. 这根羽毛很轻。| 近义词: slight, bright, kindle | 反义词: heavy, dark, extinguish | 词性: 形容词, 名词, 动词

light: /laɪt/ | adj. n. v. | 浅色的，光线，变亮 | Pale in color; electromagnetic radiation; to become brighter | She wore a light blue dress. 她穿着一件浅蓝色的连衣裙。| 近义词: pale, beam, lighten | 反义词: dark, shadow, darken | 词性: 形容词, 名词, 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lēoht (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *leuhtą (“light”), from Proto-Indo-European *leuk- (“light, brightness”). Cognate with Scots licht (“light”), West Frisian ljocht (“light”), Dutch licht (“light”), German Licht (“light”), Danish lys (“light”), Swedish ljus (“light”), Icelandic ljós (“light”).
