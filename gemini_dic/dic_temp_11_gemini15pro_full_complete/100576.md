interzones:/ˈɪntərˌzəʊnz/ | n. |区间，中间地带|A region between two or more zones, often implying a transitional or neutral area.|例句：The demilitarized interzones between the warring countries became a haven for refugees. 交战国之间的非军事区成了难民的避难所。

例句：Cultural exchange often flourishes in the interzones between different communities.  文化交流经常在不同社群之间的交汇地带蓬勃发展。|近义词：buffer zone, neutral zone, intermediate area|反义词: core, center|词性:名词
