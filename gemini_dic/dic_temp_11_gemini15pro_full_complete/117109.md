mealtime:/ˈmiːltaɪm/ (US), /ˈmiːltaɪm/ (UK)| n. |用餐时间|A time at which a meal is regularly eaten.|例句：We always talk about our day at mealtime. 我们总是在用餐时间谈论我们的一天。|例句：Mealtime can be a special time for families to connect. 用餐时间对于家庭来说可以是建立联系的特殊时刻。|近义词：dinnertime, lunchtime, suppertime, breakfast time|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *mēl-tīm*, equivalent to *mēl* (meal) + *tīm* (time).  The word "meal" comes from Old English *mǣl*, meaning "measured portion of food," from Proto-Germanic *matiz*. The word "time" comes from Old English *tīma*, from Proto-Germanic *tīmaz*.
