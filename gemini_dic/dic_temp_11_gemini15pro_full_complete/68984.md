fatigues:/fəˈtiːɡz/
n.
military uniform
军服；作战服
The soldiers were wearing their fatigues. 士兵们穿着军服。
Synonyms: uniform, combats
Antonyms: civilian clothes

fatigues:/fəˈtiːɡz/
n.
(usually plural)
a feeling of extreme tiredness, typically resulting from mental or physical exertion or illness.
疲劳；劳累
After a long day's work, he was in his fatigues.  一天辛苦工作后，他感到非常疲惫。
Synonyms: exhaustion, tiredness, weariness
Antonyms: energy, vigor, freshness

fatigues:/fəˈtiːɡz/
v. (transitive, often passive)
cause (someone) to feel exhausted
使疲劳；使劳累
The long journey fatigued the travellers. 长途旅行使旅客们疲惫不堪。
Synonyms: tire, exhaust, weary
Antonyms: energize, invigorate, refresh
