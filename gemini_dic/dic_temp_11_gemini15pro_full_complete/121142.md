misdemeanor:/ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːnər/ | n. | 轻罪；行为失检；轻微犯罪 | A misdemeanor is a minor wrongdoing.  A crime less serious than a felony. | He was convicted of a misdemeanor. 他被判犯有轻罪。|  Example:  Parking illegally is considered a misdemeanor in most cities. 在大多数城市，非法停车被认为是一种轻罪。 | Synonyms: infraction, offense, petty crime | Antonyms: felony, capital crime | noun
