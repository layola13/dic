apocalyptic:/əˌpɒkəˈlɪptɪk(əl)/ /əˌpɑːkəˈlɪptɪk(əl)/| adj.|末日的；启示录的；预示灾难的|Relating to or involving the end of the world or a disaster on a huge scale; resembling the end of the world as described in the biblical book of Revelation.|The film depicts an apocalyptic vision of the future. 这部电影描绘了对未来的末日景象。|Synonyms: catastrophic, disastrous, cataclysmic, devastating, doomful|Antonyms: hopeful, promising, auspicious|adjective
