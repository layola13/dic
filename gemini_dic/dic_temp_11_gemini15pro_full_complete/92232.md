hypertoxicities:/ˌhaɪpərˌtɑːkˈsɪsətiz/ | n. | 超毒性，过度毒性 |  The state of being excessively toxic; extreme toxicity. This refers to instances where toxicity levels are significantly higher than typical or expected.  | The patient experienced unexpected hypertoxicities from the new chemotherapy regimen.  病人对新的化疗方案产生了意想不到的超毒性。 |  Extreme toxicity, severe toxicity | Mild toxicity, low toxicity | noun
