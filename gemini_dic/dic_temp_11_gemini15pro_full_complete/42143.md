cotyledonal:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəl/ | adj. | 子叶的；胚胎的 | Relating to or resembling a cotyledon; of the nature of a cotyledon. | The cotyledonal leaves of the bean plant are thick and fleshy. 豆类植物的子叶又厚又多肉。| Near Synonyms: embryonic, germinal, seminal | Antonyms: mature, developed | adjective

cotyledon:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. | 子叶 | The embryonic leaf in seed-bearing plants, one or more of which are the first leaves to appear from a germinating seed. |  The bean seed has two cotyledons.  豆种子有两片子叶。| Near Synonyms: seed leaf, plumule | Antonyms:  None (Cotyledons are a unique structure without a direct opposite.) | noun
