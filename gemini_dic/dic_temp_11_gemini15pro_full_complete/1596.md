acicular: /əˈsɪkjʊlər/
adj. |针状的，针形的|Shaped like a needle; slender and pointed.|例句：Acicular crystals of gypsum were found in the cave. 在洞穴中发现了石膏的针状晶体。|例句：The plant has acicular leaves that resemble pine needles.  这种植物有针状的叶子，类似于松针。|近义词：needle-like, needle-shaped, pointed, slender|反义词：blunt, rounded|词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *acicula*, diminutive of *acus* "needle".
