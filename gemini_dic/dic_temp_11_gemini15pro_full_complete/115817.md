markednesses:/ˈmɑːrkɪdnəsɪz/
n.|显著性；标记性|The markednesses of the grammatical features help linguists understand language acquisition. 语法特征的显著性帮助语言学家理解语言习得。|例句：The researchers studied the markednesses of different phonetic features. 研究人员研究了不同语音特征的显著性。|近义词：salience, prominence, distinctiveness|反义词：unmarkedness, neutrality|词性:名词
