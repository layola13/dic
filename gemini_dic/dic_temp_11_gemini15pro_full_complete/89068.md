例句：Scientists are studying the composition of the holosiderite to learn more about the early solar system. 科学家正在研究这块全铁陨石的成分，以了解更多关于早期太阳系的信息。|近义词：iron meteorite|反义词: stony meteorite, stony-iron meteorite|词性:noun
