dentex:/ˈdɛntɛks/| n. |齿鲷；鲷科鱼类|A fish of the Sparidae family, commonly found in the Mediterranean Sea and the eastern Atlantic Ocean. They are known for their large teeth and are popular as game fish and for food.|例句：The dentex is a prized catch for many anglers.  齿鲷是许多垂钓者梦寐以求的捕获物。

例句：Dentex is often served grilled in Mediterranean restaurants. 在地中海餐厅，齿鲷通常是烤着吃的。|近义词：snapper, sea bream|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "dēns, dentis" meaning "tooth".  The name refers to the fish's prominent teeth.
