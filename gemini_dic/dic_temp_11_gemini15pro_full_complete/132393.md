nondemonstrable:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrəbl/ | adj. | 不可论证的，无法证明的 | Not capable of being demonstrated or proved.  |  The existence of ghosts is nondemonstrable. 鬼魂的存在是无法证明的。 |  His claim about the cure was nondemonstrable without further research.  如果没有进一步的研究，他关于这种疗法的声明是无法证明的。 | synonyms: unprovable, indemonstrable, unsubstantiable | antonyms: demonstrable, provable, verifiable | adjective
