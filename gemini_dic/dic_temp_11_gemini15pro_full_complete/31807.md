checkpoint: /ˈtʃɛkˌpɔɪnt/ (US) /ˈtʃekpɔɪnt/ (UK) | n. | 检查站；关卡；控制点；存档点 | A place where people or vehicles are stopped and checked. A point in a computer program where information can be saved so that the program can be restarted from that point. |  We were stopped at three checkpoints along the road. 我们在沿途的三个检查站被拦了下来。 | synonyms: control point, roadblock, stopping place, milestone, way station, save point | antonyms: open road, free passage | noun
