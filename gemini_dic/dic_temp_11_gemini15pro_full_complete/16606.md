barometrical:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective

relating to the barometer or to atmospheric pressure
例句：Barometrical pressure is falling, indicating an approaching storm. 气压正在下降，预示着暴风雨即将来临。

例句：Barometrical readings are crucial for weather forecasting. 气压读数对天气预报至关重要。

近义词：atmospheric
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially "non-barometrical")
