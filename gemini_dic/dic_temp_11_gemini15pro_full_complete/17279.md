bazaars:/bəˈzɑːrz/| n.|集市，市场|A bazaar is a marketplace or street of shops and stalls, especially one in the Middle East or Asia. |We wandered through the bustling bazaars of Marrakech. 我们漫步在马拉喀什熙熙攘攘的集市中。|The Grand Bazaar in Istanbul is a must-see. 伊斯坦布尔的Grand Bazaar是必游之地。|近义词：markets, souks, marketplaces|反义词: supermarket, mall | noun
