materially:/məˈtɪriəli/
adverb
实质上，重大地，在物质方面
In a way that relates to physical objects or money; significantly.
例句：The information was not materially different from what had been previously published.  这些信息与之前发布的内容没有实质性区别。

例句：The company's profits have increased materially this year. 公司的利润今年大幅增长。

近义词：significantly, substantially, considerably, importantly, essentially
反义词：immaterially, insignificantly, trivially
词性: adverb

materially:/məˈtɪriəli/
adjective
物质的，肉体的
Relating to physical objects or money rather than to spiritual or intellectual matters.
例句：Material possessions do not bring lasting happiness. 物质财富并不能带来持久的幸福。

例句：The witness provided material evidence that helped solve the case.  证人提供了有助于破案的物证。

近义词：physical, corporeal, tangible
反义词：spiritual, immaterial, intangible
词性: adjective
