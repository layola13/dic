nonredemptions:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈdɛmp.ʃənz/
|n.|未赎回;未兑现|The company reported a high number of nonredemptions of coupons.  该公司报告了大量的优惠券未兑现。|
|英文解析|The act of not redeeming something, such as bonds, coupons, or shares. It refers to the situation where the holder chooses not to exchange or cash in something that is redeemable.|
|例句|The high rate of nonredemptions suggests customers are satisfied with the product and are not seeking refunds.  高未兑换率表明客户对产品感到满意，并没有寻求退款。|

|例句|Nonredemptions of gift cards can be a significant source of revenue for retailers. 礼品卡的未兑换可以成为零售商的重要收入来源。|
|近义词|non-payment, default, lapse, forfeiture|
|反义词|redemption, payment, cashing in|
|词性|noun (plural)|
