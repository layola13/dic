nonhyperbolically:/ˌnɒnˌhaɪpəˈbɒlɪkli/ | adv. | 非夸张地，不夸张地说 |  In a way that avoids exaggeration or hyperbole. |  He described the situation nonhyperbolically, sticking to the verifiable facts. 他如实地描述了情况，坚持可验证的事实。 |  realistically, truthfully, accurately, soberly, plainly  | hyperbolically, exaggeratedly | adverb
