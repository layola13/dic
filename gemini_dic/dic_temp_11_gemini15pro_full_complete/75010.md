friendlessnesses:/ˈfrendləsnɪsɪz/
noun
没有朋友的状态；孤寂；无依无靠
The state of having no friends; isolation; lack of support.
Her friendlessnesses deepened after she moved to a new city.  她搬到新城市后更加孤单了。
His friendlessnesses made him vulnerable. 他的孤獨使他很脆弱。
