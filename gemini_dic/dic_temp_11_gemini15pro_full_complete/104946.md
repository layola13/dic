kelping:/ˈkɛlpɪŋ/| n.|海藻养殖; 收集海藻; 海带肥料|The act of gathering kelp from the sea, especially for use as fertilizer or a source of iodine.  The process or business of cultivating kelp.|例句：Kelping provided a vital source of income for coastal communities.  海藻养殖为沿海社区提供了重要的收入来源。

例句：The kelping industry is growing rapidly due to the demand for sustainable food sources.  由于对可持续食物来源的需求，海藻养殖业正在快速增长。|近义词：seaweed harvesting, kelp farming|反义词: None|词性:Noun
