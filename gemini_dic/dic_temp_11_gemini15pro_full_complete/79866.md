gnar:/nɑːr/ | adj. | 非常棒的，极好的；困难的，极端的 |  Slang term, especially in surfing and skateboarding culture, used to describe something as excellent, impressive, or challenging. It can also refer to something twisted, knotty, or gnarled.  |  That wave was totally gnar! 那个浪真是太棒了！ | 近义词：awesome, rad, sick, wicked, challenging, difficult | 反义词:lame, easy, boring | 词性:形容词

gnar:/nɑːr/ | n. | 瘤，节；困难，挑战 | A knotty protuberance, especially on a tree. A difficult or challenging situation. | The old oak tree was covered in gnar. 那棵老橡树上长满了节瘤。 | 近义词：knot, burl, difficulty, challenge | 反义词:smoothness, ease | 词性:名词
