captivity:/kæpˈtɪvɪti/| n. |束缚，禁锢 (比喻义) |The state of being restricted or controlled.|He felt held in captivity by his job. 他感觉被他的工作束缚住了。|Synonyms: constraint, restriction, limitation|Antonyms: liberation, release|noun
