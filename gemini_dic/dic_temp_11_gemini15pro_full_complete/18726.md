bergander:/bɜːrˈɡændər/ | n. | 秋沙鸭（雄鸟）| A male shelduck.  A brightly colored duck with reddish-brown plumage, a white breast, and a dark green head. |例句：The bergander stood out amongst the other ducks with its vibrant plumage.  秋沙鸭在其他鸭子中以其鲜艳的羽毛脱颖而出。

|例句：The bergander preened its feathers, displaying its striking markings. 秋沙鸭整理羽毛，展示其醒目的斑纹。 |近义词：sheldrake (male), duck|反义词:  female shelduck (specifically referring to the female) |词性: noun
