irresponsibles:/ˌɪrɪˈspɒnsəblz/ | n. |不负责任的人；不负责任的行为（复数）| People who or actions that are not responsible. |The irresponsibles left the party a mess. 那些不负责任的人把派对现场弄得一团糟。| 近义词： delinquents, wrongdoers | 反义词: responsible people/actions | 词性: 名词

irresponsible:/ˌɪrɪˈspɒnsəbl/ | adj. |不负责任的，不承担责任的 | Not showing a proper sense of responsibility. |It was irresponsible of him to leave the children alone. 他把孩子们单独留下是很不负责任的。| 近义词： careless, negligent, reckless | 反义词: responsible | 词性: 形容词
