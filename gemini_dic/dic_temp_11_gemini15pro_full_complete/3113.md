aerostat:/ˈɛərəstæt/| n. |气球；飞艇；浮空器|A lighter-than-air craft that gains its lift through the use of a buoyant gas, such as helium or hydrogen.  It can be tethered (blimp) or untethered (balloon).|例句：The aerostat provided a unique aerial view of the festival.  飞艇提供了独特的节日鸟瞰图。

例句：Military forces have used aerostats for surveillance for centuries.  几个世纪以来，军队一直使用气球进行监视。|近义词：blimp, dirigible, airship|反义词:airplane, aeroplane, jet|词性:noun
