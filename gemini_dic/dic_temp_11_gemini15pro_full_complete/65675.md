even-though:/ˈiːvən ðoʊ/| conjunction|即使；尽管；虽然|Used to introduce a subordinate clause expressing something that contrasts with the main clause, but does not prevent the main clause from being true.|Even though it was raining, we went for a walk. 即使下雨了，我们还是去散步了。|Even though I was tired, I finished the project. 虽然我很累，但我还是完成了项目。|Although, though, while, albeit|But, yet|conjunction
