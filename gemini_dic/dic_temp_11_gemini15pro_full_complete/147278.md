overmix:/ˌōvərˈmɪks/| v. |过度混合，搅拌过久|To mix or blend something too much, typically resulting in a negative effect on the texture or consistency.|The cake was dense because I overmixed the batter.  蛋糕很硬，因为我把面糊搅拌过久了。|The chef cautioned against overmixing the dough, as it would make the bread tough. 厨师告诫不要过度搅拌面团，因为这会使面包变硬。|近义词：overbeat, overblend|反义词: undermix|词性:动词
