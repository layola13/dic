delapse:/dɪˈlæps/
verb
失效，终止，期满
to lapse; to end, often of a right or privilege
The contract will delapse at the end of the year.  合同将在年底失效。
His insurance policy delapses if he doesn't pay. 如果他不付款，他的保险单将失效。
Synonyms: expire, terminate, lapse
Antonyms: renew, extend
