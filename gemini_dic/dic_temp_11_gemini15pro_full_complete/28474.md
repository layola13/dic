carcassed:/ˈkɑːrkəst/| adj.|屠体化的，被宰杀的，像尸体的|Having the appearance of a carcass; slaughtered.  Reduced to a carcass; dead.|The battlefield was carcassed with the bodies of fallen soldiers. 战场上散落着士兵的尸体。|近义词：slaughtered, butchered, dead, lifeless|反义词:alive, living|词性:形容词
Etymology: From carcass + -ed,  carcass from Old North French *carcois "skeleton, framework"
