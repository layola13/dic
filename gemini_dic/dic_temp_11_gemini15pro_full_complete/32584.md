chirology:/kaɪˈrɒlədʒi/
n.
手相术，手相学
The study of the lines and formations of the hand, especially as an indication of character or future.
She consulted a chirologist to have her palm read. 她咨询了一位手相学家来解读她的掌纹。
Chirology is often considered a pseudoscience. 手相学通常被认为是伪科学。
近义词: palmistry
反义词:  None (No direct antonym exists)
词性: noun
