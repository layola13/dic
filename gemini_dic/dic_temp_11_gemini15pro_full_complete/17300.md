bcc'ing: /ˌbiː siː siːɪŋ/ | v. | 密送；暗送 | The act of sending a copy of an email to someone without the primary recipients knowing.  | Example: I'm bcc'ing John on this email so he's aware of the situation. 我正在将这封邮件密送给约翰，让他了解情况。

Example: She bcc'ed her boss on all client communications. 她把所有与客户的通信都密送给了她的老板。 | 近义词: blind copying | 反义词: cc'ing (carbon copying),  to'ing (directly addressing) | 词性: 动词 (present participle of "bcc")
