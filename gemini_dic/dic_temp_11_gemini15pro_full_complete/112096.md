longitudinal:/ˌlɒndʒɪˈtjuːdɪnl/ /ˌlɑːndʒɪˈtuːdɪnl/ | adj. | 纵向的，纵的 |  Extending lengthwise; in the direction of the length of a thing. |  A longitudinal study followed the group of participants over twenty years.  这项纵向研究对这组参与者进行了二十年的跟踪调查。 | synonyms: lengthwise, lengthways, running, | antonyms: transverse, crosswise, latitudinal | adjective

longitudinal:/ˌlɒndʒɪˈtjuːdɪnl/ /ˌlɑːndʒɪˈtuːdɪnl/ | n. | 纵线，经线 | A line of longitude. (Less common usage) | The longitudinal marked the boundary between the two territories. 这条经线标志着两个领土之间的边界。 | synonyms: meridian | antonyms: latitude, parallel | noun
