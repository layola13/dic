alarming:/əˈlɑːmɪŋ/
adjective
令人惊恐的，引起恐慌的
Causing worry and fear; frightening.
The alarming rise in crime has caused many people to move out of the city.  犯罪率的惊人上升导致许多人搬离了这座城市。

The news of the impending hurricane was alarming.  即将到来的飓风的消息令人担忧。

近义词:disturbing, worrying, frightening, concerning, unsettling, troubling
反义词:reassuring, comforting, calming, soothing
词性:adjective
