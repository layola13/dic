enoptomancy:/ɛˈnɒptəmænsi/ |n.|镜占;  水晶球占卜|The practice of divination by examining reflections in mirrors or other polished surfaces, such as water or crystal balls.|She practiced enoptomancy, seeking answers in the depths of her crystal ball. 她练习镜占，在水晶球的深处寻找答案。|Near synonyms: scrying, crystal gazing|Antonym:  None (Lack of a specific antonym for this specialized divination method)|Noun
