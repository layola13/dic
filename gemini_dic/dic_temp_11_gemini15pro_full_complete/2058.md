acupuncturist:/ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃərɪst/| n. |针灸师，针灸医生|A person who practices acupuncture, a system of complementary medicine in which thin needles are inserted into the skin at specific points on the body to relieve pain or for therapeutic purposes.|例句：I saw an acupuncturist for my back pain. 我去看针灸师治疗我的背痛。

例句：The acupuncturist explained the procedure in detail. 针灸师详细解释了治疗过程。|近义词：acupuncture practitioner, Chinese medicine practitioner|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "conventional doctor" or "Western medicine practitioner" for contrast) |词性:noun
