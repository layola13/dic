lucarne: /luːˈkɑːrn/ | n. | 天窗；屋顶窗 | A small window in a roof, especially a gable, or in the side of a spire, tower, or dome. |  The lucarne offered a picturesque view of the snowy rooftops. 天窗提供了雪屋顶的如画景色。

The old house had several lucarnes that let in the morning light. 这栋老房子有几个天窗，可以让早晨的光线照进来。| 近义词: dormer, skylight | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle French *lucarne, from Old French lucarne, luiserne, from Vulgar Latin *lucerna, from Latin lucerna (“lamp, lantern”), from lux (“light”).  The connection is that the window admits light, like a lamp.
