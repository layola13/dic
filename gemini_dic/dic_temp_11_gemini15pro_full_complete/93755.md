immeasurability:/ɪˌmɛʒərəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可估量，无法计量
The quality of being impossible to measure.
The immeasurability of the universe makes it a constant source of wonder and study. 宇宙的浩瀚无垠使它成为不断探索和研究的源泉。
The immeasurability of human potential is inspiring. 人类潜力的不可估量性令人鼓舞。
immenseness, vastness, boundlessness
measurability, finiteness
noun
