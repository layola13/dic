interdictive:/ˌɪntərˈdɪktɪv/
adjective
禁止的，阻止的
Having the power to prohibit or forbid.
The government issued an interdictive order against the illegal trade. 政府发布了禁止非法贸易的命令。
The court issued an interdictive injunction to prevent the company from polluting the river. 法院发布了强制令，阻止该公司污染河流。

synonyms: prohibitive, prohibitory, restrictive, forbidding
antonyms: permissive, allowing, authorizing
