liaise:/liˈeɪz/| vi.|联络，联系，接洽|To communicate or cooperate with someone, especially in order to work together effectively.|The marketing team needs to liaise with the sales team to ensure a successful product launch. 市场团队需要与销售团队联络，以确保产品成功发布。

The company appointed a manager to liaise between the different departments. 公司任命了一位经理来协调不同部门之间的工作。|Synonyms: coordinate, collaborate, communicate, connect, network|Antonyms: disconnect, separate, isolate|Verb
