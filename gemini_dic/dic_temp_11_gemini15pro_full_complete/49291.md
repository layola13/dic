delicatenesses:/ˈdɛlɪkətɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
精妙之处；微妙之处；细致之处
The quality of being delicate; fineness of texture, workmanship, or constitution; fragility.
The painting captured the delicatenesses of the flower petals.  这幅画捕捉到了花瓣的精妙之处。
delicacy, subtleties, nuances, refinements
coarseness, crudeness, roughness
noun
