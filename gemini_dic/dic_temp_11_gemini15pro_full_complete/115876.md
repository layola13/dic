what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if we go fishing for marlins tomorrow? 如果我们明天去钓马林鱼会怎么样？

what for = 为了什么
What are you keeping that marlin for? 你留着那条马林鱼是为了什么？

no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what, I'm going to catch a marlin. 无论如何，我都要钓到一条马林鱼。

what's more = 而且；更重要的是
Marlins are fast swimmers; what's more, they are very strong. 马林鱼是游泳健将；而且，它们非常强壮。

What about going deep-sea fishing for marlin? 去深海钓马林鱼怎么样？
