noncommitment:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtmənt/ |n.| 不承诺，不承担义务，不明确的态度 |Lack of commitment or a definite decision. |例句：His noncommitment to the project frustrated the team.  他对这个项目不置可否的态度使团队感到沮丧。

例句：She maintained a policy of noncommitment on controversial issues.  她对有争议的问题保持着不表态的策略。|近义词：indecision, neutrality, reluctance |反义词：commitment, dedication, engagement |词性: 名词
