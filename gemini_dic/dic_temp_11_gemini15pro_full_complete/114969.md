manchet:/ˈmæntʃɪt/
n.
小圆白面包，上等白面包
A small loaf or roll of fine white bread.
The baker prepared a fresh manchet for the king's table.  面包师为国王的餐桌准备了一个新鲜的小圆白面包。
Synonyms: roll, loaf, bun
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "coarse bread")
Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mainchet, diminutive of main "hand," because of its small size.
