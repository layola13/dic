barless:/ˈbɑːrlɪs/| adj.|无栏的，无栅栏的|Without bars or a barrier.  The barless enclosure allowed the animals to roam freely.  无栏的围栏让动物可以自由漫步。|例句：The barless cage gave the bird a sense of freedom.  无栏的笼子给了鸟儿一种自由的感觉。

例句：They created a barless zoo exhibit to mimic the animals' natural habitat. 他们创建了一个无栏的动物园展览，以模仿动物的自然栖息地。|近义词：open, unfenced, unobstructed|反义词：barred, caged, fenced|词性:形容词
