crispish:/ˈkrɪspɪʃ/| adj.|略脆的，有点脆的|Somewhat crisp; slightly crisp.  This describes something that has a texture approaching crispness but not fully crisp.|The apples were crispish, not quite as crisp as I like them.  这些苹果略脆，不像我喜欢的那样脆。|例句：The air was crispish this morning, hinting at the coming autumn. 今晨的空气略带凉意，预示着秋天的到来。

例句：The bacon was crispish, but could have been cooked a little longer. 培根有点脆，但是可以再煮久一点。|近义词：crispy, crunchy, firm|反义词：soft, soggy, mushy|词性:形容词
