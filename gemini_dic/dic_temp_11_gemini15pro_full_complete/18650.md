benzoline:/ˈbɛnzəliːn/ /ˈbɛnzəˌlin/
noun|汽油; 挥发油|A volatile flammable liquid mixture of hydrocarbons, obtained from petroleum and used as a fuel in internal combustion engines; petrol.|The car ran out of benzoline. 这辆车没汽油了。|synonyms: petrol, gasoline, gas|antonyms: none
