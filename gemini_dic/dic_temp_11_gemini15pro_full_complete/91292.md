hydrology:/haɪˈdrɒlədʒi/ /haɪˈdrɑːlədʒi/
n.|水文学|The science that deals with the properties, distribution, and circulation of water on and below the earth's surface and in the atmosphere.|The hydrology of the area is complex, with numerous underground rivers.|近义词: geohydrology|反义词: None|词性: Noun
Etymology: From the Greek ὕδωρ (hýdōr, “water”) + -λογία (-logia, “study of”).
