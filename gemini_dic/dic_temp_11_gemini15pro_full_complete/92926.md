identifiableness:/aɪˌdɛntɪfaɪəbəlˈnɛs/ | n. |可识别性，可辨认性 | The quality or state of being identifiable; the ability to be recognized or distinguished. | The identifiableness of the suspect was compromised by the poor quality of the CCTV footage. 监控录像质量差，损害了嫌疑人的可辨认性。 | Synonyms: distinguishability, discernibility, recognizability | Antonyms: anonymity, indistinguishability, obscurity | noun

identifiable:/aɪˌdɛntɪˈfaɪəbl/ | adj. |可识别的，可辨认的 | Able to be recognized or distinguished. | The car was easily identifiable by its bright pink paint. 这辆车很容易通过其鲜艳的粉红色油漆来识别。 | Synonyms: recognizable, distinguishable, discernible | Antonyms: unrecognizable, indistinguishable, obscure | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From identifiable + -ness.  Identifiable comes from identify + -able. Identify comes from French identifier, from Late Latin identificare, from Latin idem (the same) + facere (to make).
