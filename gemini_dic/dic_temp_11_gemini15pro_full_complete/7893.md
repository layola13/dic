antelope:/ˈæntɪloʊp/ (US) /ˈæntɪləʊp/ (UK)| n. |羚羊|A swift-running deer-like ruminant with smooth hair and upward-pointing horns, native to Africa and Asia. |例句：The antelope leaped gracefully across the savanna.  羚羊优雅地跳跃过大草原。

例句：A herd of antelope grazed peacefully in the distance. 一群羚羊在远处平静地吃草。|近义词：gazelle, hartebeest, impala |反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider predator like lion, leopard)|Noun
