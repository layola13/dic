co-ruler:/ˈkoʊˌrulər/| n.|共同统治者，共同执政者|A person who rules jointly with another. |例句：The two brothers were co-rulers of the kingdom. 这两兄弟是王国的共同统治者。

例句：She served as co-ruler with her husband for over a decade. 她和她的丈夫共同执政了十多年。|近义词：coruler, joint ruler, co-regent|反义词:subject, subordinate|词性:noun
