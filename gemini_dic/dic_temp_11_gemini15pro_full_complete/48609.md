defectively:/dɪˈfɛktɪvli/
adv.
有缺陷地，不完美地
in a way that is faulty or not working properly
The product was defectively manufactured and had to be recalled. 该产品制造存在缺陷，不得不被召回。
The bridge was defectively designed, leading to its collapse. 这座桥的设计有缺陷，导致其倒塌。

近义词:faultily, imperfectly, inadequately, improperly
反义词:perfectly, flawlessly, impeccably
词性:副词
