abdications: /ˌæbdɪˈkeɪʃənz/
| n. |退位，放弃|The act of formally giving up a high office or responsibility.|例句：The history books are full of royal abdications. 历史书上充满了皇室退位的记载。

例句：His abdication of responsibility shocked everyone. 他放弃责任的行为震惊了所有人。

|近义词：resignations, relinquishments, renunciations|反义词: accessions, assumptions|词性:名词(复数)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abdicātiō (“renunciation”), from Latin abdicō (“disown, renounce, abdicate”), from ab- (“away from”) + dicō (“say, declare”).
