notarizes:/ˈnoʊtəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
公证，证明
certifies or attests something, typically a document, as genuine, usually by signature and official seal.
The lawyer notarizes the will.  律师公证了遗嘱。
certifies, attests, validates, authenticates
falsifies, rejects, denies
v.
