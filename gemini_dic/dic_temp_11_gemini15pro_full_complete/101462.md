例句：Her irenical approach to conflict resolution often resulted in positive outcomes. 她以和平的方式解决冲突，往往会带来积极的结果。|近义词：peaceful, pacific, conciliatory, appeasing, diplomatic|反义词：belligerent, hostile, antagonistic, aggressive|词性:形容词
