aerostatics:/ˌɛroʊˈstætɪks/ | n. |空气静力学|The branch of physics that deals with the equilibrium of gaseous fluids and of solid bodies immersed in them.  Specifically, the study of the equilibrium conditions of balloons and airships.|The principles of aerostatics explain how hot air balloons can fly.  空气静力学原理解释了热气球是如何飞行的。| 近义词:pneumatics | 反义词:aerodynamics | 词性:noun
