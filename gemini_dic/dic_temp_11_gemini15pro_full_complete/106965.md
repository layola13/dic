lacked:/lækt/ | v. | 缺乏，缺少 |  past tense of "lack" meaning to be without or deficient in something. |  He lacked the necessary skills for the job. 他缺乏这项工作所需的技能。|  miss, want, need | possess, have | verb

lacked:/lækt/ | adj. | 缺乏的，不足的 |  (less common usage as an adjective, usually used as the past participle of the verb "lack")  |  The food was lacked in flavor.  食物缺乏味道。(This usage is less common and considered somewhat awkward. "The food lacked flavor" is preferable.) |  deficient, insufficient, wanting |  sufficient, ample | adjective
