devotions:/dɪˈvoʊʃənz/| n.|虔诚；祈祷；宗教仪式|acts of religious worship or prayer|例句：She attended her morning devotions. 她参加了早祷。

例句：His devotions to his family are admirable.  他对家庭的奉献令人钦佩。|近义词：prayers, worship, piety, dedication|反义词：disregard, neglect|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French devotion, from late Latin devotio(n-), from devotus ‘devoted’.
