disposingly:/dɪˈspəʊzɪŋli/
adv.
乐意地，愿意地
in a willing or positive manner.
She smiled disposingly at him. 她乐意地对他笑了笑。
近义词:willingly, readily, favorably
反义词:reluctantly, unwillingly
词性:副词


词源: From dispose + -ing + -ly. Dispose comes from Old French *disposer, from Latin dispōnere "put in different places, arrange, distribute," from dis- "apart" + pōnere "to put, place."
