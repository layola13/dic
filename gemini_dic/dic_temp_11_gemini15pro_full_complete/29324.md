castigation:/ˌkæstɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 严厉的惩罚；责骂；鞭挞 | Severe criticism or punishment. |  The politician faced castigation from the media after the scandal broke.  这位政治家在丑闻曝光后受到了媒体的严厉批评。| Synonyms: reprimand, rebuke, censure, chastisement | Antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim | noun

castigate:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 严厉批评；惩罚；鞭挞 | To criticize or punish severely. | The teacher castigated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师严厉批评了考试作弊的学生。| Synonyms: reprimand, rebuke, censure, chastise | Antonyms: praise, commend, laud | verb
