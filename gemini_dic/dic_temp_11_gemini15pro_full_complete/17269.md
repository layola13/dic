bayonetted:/ˌbeɪəˈnɛtɪd/
verb|用刺刀刺；装上刺刀|
英文解析:Past participle of bayonet.  Having been stabbed with a bayonet or having a bayonet attached.
例句：The soldier bayonetted the enemy.  士兵用刺刀刺杀了敌人。
例句：The rifles were bayonetted and ready for action.  步枪装上了刺刀，准备战斗。
近义词：stabbed, pierced, impaled
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "unbayonetted" - though uncommon)
词性:Verb (past participle)


bayonetted:/ˌbeɪəˈnɛtɪd/
adjective|装有刺刀的；被刺刀刺伤的|
英文解析:Describing something that has been stabbed with a bayonet or has a bayonet attached.
例句：A bayonetted rifle lay on the ground. 地上躺着一支装有刺刀的步枪。
例句：The bayonetted victim succumbed to his injuries. 被刺刀刺伤的受害者因伤势过重而死亡。
近义词：armed (with a bayonet), pierced, impaled
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "unbayonetted"- though uncommon)
词性:Adjective
