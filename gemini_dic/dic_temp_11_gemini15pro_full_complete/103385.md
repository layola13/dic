jingoistic:/ˌdʒɪŋɡoʊˈɪstɪk/
jingoistic:|adj.|侵略主义的；沙文主义的；极端爱国主义的|Characterized by extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy.|The politician's jingoistic rhetoric stirred up the crowd.  这位政客的沙文主义言论煽动了人群。|Example:  His jingoistic speeches increased international tensions. 他的沙文主义演讲加剧了国际紧张局势。|Synonyms: chauvinistic, nationalistic, patriotic, hawkish|Antonyms: pacifistic, dovish, internationalist|adjective
