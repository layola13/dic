old-maidenhood:/ˌoʊld ˈmeɪdn̩hʊd/
n.
老处女身份，未婚老年女性的状态
The state of being an old maid; spinsterhood.
She accepted her old-maidenhood with grace and dignity. 她优雅而有尊严地接受了自己老处女的状态。
Near Synonyms: spinsterhood, celibacy
Antonyms: marriage, matrimony
