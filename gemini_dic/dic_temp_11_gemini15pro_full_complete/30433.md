centralizer:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzər/| n.|中心化器，中心化装置，扶正器，集中器，[数]中心化子|A device that centers something. In mathematics, the centralizer of a subset of a group consists of all elements of the group that commute with every element of the subset.|The centralizer ensures the drill bit stays centered.  中心化器确保钻头保持在中心位置。| The centralizer of an element x in a group G is the set of all elements in G that commute with x. 在群 G 中元素 x 的中心化子是 G 中所有与 x 交换的元素的集合。|synonyms: stabilizer, normalizer|antonyms: decentralizer|noun
