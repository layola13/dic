nonirrationals:/ˌnɒnɪˈræʃənəlz/ | n. |非无理数；有理数| Numbers that can be expressed as a ratio of two integers (i.e., a fraction where the numerator and denominator are both integers, and the denominator is not zero). |  The set of nonirrationals includes integers, fractions, and terminating or repeating decimals. 非无理数的集合包括整数、分数和有限小数或循环小数。|  Rational numbers and nonirrationals together make up the set of real numbers.  有理数和非无理数共同构成了实数集。 |Synonyms: rationals| Antonyms: irrationals | Noun
