givee:/ɡɪˈviː/ | n. | 受赠者；接受者 |  A person who receives something, especially a gift or a benefit. | Example: The givee was thrilled with the unexpected present. 受赠者对这份意外的礼物感到非常激动。 Example:  The program provides training to the givees.  这个项目为受赠者提供培训。 | 近义词: recipient, beneficiary | 反义词: giver, donor | 词性: 名词

词源:  Back-formation from "give," influenced by words like "grantee" and "donee."  由“give”逆构词法构成，受“grantee”和“donee”等词的影响。
