demerger:/diːˈmɜːrdʒər/
noun
公司分拆；业务剥离
The separation of a company into two or more independent companies.
The demerger of the company resulted in two separate entities.  公司分拆导致形成了两个独立的实体。
The demerger created a new company focused on software development. 分拆创建了一家专注于软件开发的新公司。
synonyms:  spin-off, divestiture, separation, split-off
antonyms:  merger, acquisition, consolidation
n.
