lepidodendroid:/ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈdɛndrɔɪd/ | n. | 鳞木类植物 | An extinct tree-like lycopod of the genus Lepidodendron or a related genus, dominant during the Carboniferous period. | Lepidodendroids contributed significantly to the formation of coal deposits. 鳞木类植物对煤炭沉积的形成做出了重大贡献。 |  The lepidodendroids thrived in the swampy environments of the Carboniferous. 鳞木类植物在石炭纪的沼泽环境中繁盛生长。 | 近义词:Lepidodendron, scale tree | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "modern tree" as a contrasting concept) |词性: noun


词源： From Greek  *lepido*  (scale) + *dendron* (tree) + *-oid* (resembling).
