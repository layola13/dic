overfunctioning:/ˌoʊvərˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/
adjective
过度运作的；过度承担责任的
Taking on too many responsibilities and tasks, often to the detriment of oneself or others.  This can stem from anxiety, a need for control, or a difficulty setting boundaries.
She was overfunctioning in the relationship, always taking care of his needs while neglecting her own. 她在这段关系中过度付出，总是照顾他的需求而忽略了自己的需求。

He was overfunctioning at work, taking on extra projects even though he was already overwhelmed. 他在工作中过度运作，即使他已经不堪重负，仍然承担额外的项目。

近义词: overworking, overextending, hyperfunctioning
反义词: underfunctioning, lazy, negligent
词性: adjective
