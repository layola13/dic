dislikers:/dɪsˈlaɪkərz/
noun
不喜欢的人；讨厌的人
People who dislike something or someone.
The band has as many dislikers as fans.  这个乐队有多少歌迷就有多少不喜欢他们的人。
The president’s dislikers are very vocal. 总统不喜欢他的人非常直言不讳。
synonyms: haters, critics, detractors, opponents
antonyms: fans, admirers, supporters
