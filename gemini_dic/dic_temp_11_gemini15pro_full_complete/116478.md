materialist:/məˈtɪəriəlɪst/ | n. |唯物主义者；物质主义者 | A person who believes that only physical matter exists and that there is no spiritual world. | He's a committed materialist and doesn't believe in anything beyond the physical world. 他是一个坚定的唯物主义者，不相信任何超乎物质世界的东西。|Synonyms: consumer, hedonist, epicurean | Antonyms: idealist, spiritualist | noun


materialistic:/məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. |物质主义的；唯物论的 | Excessively concerned with material possessions; relating to materialism. |  She's become very materialistic lately, always wanting the latest gadgets. 她最近变得非常物质主义，总是想要最新的电子产品。| Synonyms: acquisitive, greedy, mercenary | Antonyms: spiritual, idealistic, ascetic | adjective
