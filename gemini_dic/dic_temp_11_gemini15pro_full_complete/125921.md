museums:/mjuːˈziːəmz/
noun
博物馆（museum 的复数形式）
A museum is a building where objects of historical, scientific, artistic, or cultural interest are stored and exhibited.
We visited several museums during our trip to Italy.  我们在意大利之旅中参观了几家博物馆。
The city's museums are world-renowned. 这座城市的博物馆享誉世界。
synonyms: galleries, collections, archives, repositories
antonyms: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 private collections 私人收藏)
n.
