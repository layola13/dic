fall-offs:/ˈfɔːl ɒfs/ (US), /ˈfɔːl ɒfs/ (UK)| n.|下降，减少，衰退|A decrease or decline in something, such as quality, quantity, or activity. |例句：There has been a significant fall-off in sales this quarter. 本季度销售额大幅下降。

例句：We noticed a fall-off in attendance at the weekly meetings. 我们注意到每周例会的出席率有所下降。|近义词：decline, decrease, drop, slump, downturn|反义词：increase, rise, growth, surge, upturn|词性:名词
