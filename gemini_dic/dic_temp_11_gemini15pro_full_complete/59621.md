ecchymotic: /ˌɛkiˈmɑtɪk/ | adj. | 瘀斑的，呈现瘀斑的 | Relating to or resembling an ecchymosis; marked by ecchymosis. |  The patient presented with ecchymotic bruising around the eye socket. 病人的眼窝周围出现了瘀斑。| Synonyms: bruised, contused, black-and-blue | Antonyms: unblemished, clear | adjective

ecchymosis: /ˌɛkɪˈmoʊsɪs/ | n. | 瘀斑 | The escape of blood into the tissues from ruptured blood vessels marked by a livid black-and-blue or purple spot or area; also : the discoloration of the skin caused by such an extravasation. | The ecchymosis on his arm was a result of the fall. 他手臂上的瘀斑是摔倒造成的。| Synonyms: bruise, contusion | Antonyms:  None (absence of bruising) | noun
