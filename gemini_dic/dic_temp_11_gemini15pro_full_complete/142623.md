osteosarcoma: /ˌɒstioʊsɑːrˈkoʊmə/ | n. | 骨肉瘤 | A malignant tumor of bone in which there is a proliferation of osteoblasts. |  Osteosarcoma is the most common type of bone cancer. 骨肉瘤是最常见的骨癌。|  He was diagnosed with osteosarcoma in his left leg. 他被诊断出左腿患有骨肉瘤。| 近义词: bone cancer | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用“healthy bone tissue”健康骨组织来表示对比) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From osteo- (bone) + -sarcoma (malignant connective tissue tumor).  The "osteo-" part comes from the Greek word "osteon," meaning "bone." The "-sarcoma" part comes from the Greek word "sarx," meaning "flesh."
