concordist:/kənˈkɔːrdɪst/| n.|协调者，和谐主义者|A person who advocates or promotes concord or harmony, especially between different groups or factions, particularly in religious contexts. |例句：The concordist attempted to reconcile the conflicting views of the two denominations.  协调者试图调和两个教派之间的冲突观点。

例句：She acted as a concordist between the management and the union. 她在管理层和工会之间扮演了协调者的角色。|近义词：mediator, arbitrator, peacemaker, reconciler|反义词：dissenter, antagonist, disputant|词性: noun
