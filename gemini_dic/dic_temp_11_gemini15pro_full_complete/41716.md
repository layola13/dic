corroboratory:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtəri/ (US) /kəˈrɒbərətəri/ (UK)
adjective
确证的，证实的
serving to support or confirm
例句：The witness provided corroboratory evidence.  证人提供了确凿的证据。

例句：Her statement was corroborated by two other witnesses.  她的陈述得到了另外两名证人的证实。
近义词：confirming, supporting, substantiating, validating
反义词：contradictory, refuting, disproving
词性：adjective

词源: Late 16th century: from late Latin corroboratorius, from corroborat- ‘strengthened’, from the verb corroborare (see corroborate).
