equilibrial:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəl/
adjective
平衡的，均衡的
Relating to or characterized by equilibrium; balanced.
The equilibrial state of the ecosystem was disrupted by the introduction of a new species. 生态系统的平衡状态被新物种的引入打破了。
The tightrope walker maintained an equilibrial posture.  走钢丝的人保持着平衡的姿势。

近义词:balanced, stable, steady
反义词:unstable, unbalanced, unsteady
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *aequilibrium*, from *aequi-* (equal) + *libra* (balance).
