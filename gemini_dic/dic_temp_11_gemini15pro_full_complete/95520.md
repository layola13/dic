include:/ɪnˈkluːd/ (US), /ɪnˈkluːd/ (UK)| vt.|包括，包含|To contain something as a part of something else, or to make something part of something else.|The price includes breakfast. 价格包含早餐。|Synonyms: comprise, encompass, incorporate, contain|Antonyms: exclude, omit|Verb

include:/ɪnˈkluːd/ (US), /ɪnˈkluːd/ (UK)| n.|包含，包括（常用于计算机编程）|A directive or command that tells a program to insert text from another file.|The #include directive in C++ inserts the contents of a header file.  C++中的#include指令插入头文件的内容。|Synonyms: incorporation, insertion|Antonyms: exclusion, omission|Noun
