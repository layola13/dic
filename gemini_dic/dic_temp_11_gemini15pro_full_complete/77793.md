generalize:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/ (US), /ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |概括，归纳；推广；普及|To infer or form a general principle or conclusion from particular instances; to make something more widespread or common.| Scientists are careful not to generalize from limited data. 科学家们小心翼翼地避免从有限的数据中概括出结论。| synonyms: summarize, abstract, extrapolate, universalize | antonyms: specify, particularize | verb

generalization:/ˌdʒɛnərələˈzeɪʃən/ (US), /ˌdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |概括，归纳；推广；普遍化|A general statement or concept obtained by inference from specific cases; the act or process of generalizing.|  His generalization about women was offensive. 他对女性的概括性说法带有冒犯性。| synonyms: summarization, abstraction, conclusion | antonyms: specification, particularization | noun
