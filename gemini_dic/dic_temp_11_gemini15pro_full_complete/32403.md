chilling:/ˈtʃɪlɪŋ/| adj.|令人恐惧的，令人不寒而栗的；寒冷的|Causing a feeling of fear or unease; very cold.|The chilling story kept me awake all night.  这个令人毛骨悚然的故事让我彻夜未眠。|synonyms: frightening, terrifying, scary, eerie, cold, freezing|antonyms: comforting, reassuring, warm, pleasant|adjective


chilling:/ˈtʃɪlɪŋ/| n.|冷却，冷藏|The act of cooling something, especially food or drinks.|The chilling of the wine took longer than expected.  葡萄酒的冷却时间比预期的要长。|synonyms: cooling, refrigeration|antonyms: heating, warming|noun
