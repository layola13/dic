decompartmentalized:/ˌdiːkəmˌpɑːrtˈmɛntəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 去隔间的，去部门化的，跨部门的 |  Removed from compartments or departmental divisions; integrated or unified. It refers to breaking down traditional barriers between different groups, departments, or areas of knowledge. | The company adopted a decompartmentalized approach to project management, allowing team members from different departments to collaborate more effectively.  公司采用了一种去部门化的项目管理方法，允许来自不同部门的团队成员更有效地协作。| integrated, unified, cross-functional, interdisciplinary, holistic | compartmentalized, segregated, isolated, divided | adjective
