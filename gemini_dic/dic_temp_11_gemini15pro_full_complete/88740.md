hobson:/ˈhɒbsən/| n.|霍布森选择，没有选择的选择|A Hobson's choice is a free choice in which only one thing is offered. Because there is no alternative, the choice is not really free.|You can have any car you like, as long as it's a Ford – it's a Hobson's choice really.|近义词：no choice, dilemma (in some contexts)|反义词: multiple choices, options|名词
