guidepost:/ˈɡaɪdpoʊst/| n.|路标，指导原则，指标|A guidepost is a signpost, often at a road junction, that gives directions to places.|The factory closure served as a guidepost for future economic policy. 工厂关闭成为未来经济政策的指标。|synonyms: signpost, marker, indicator, beacon, landmark|antonyms:  misdirection, distraction|noun

guidepost:/ˈɡaɪdpoʊst/| n.|路标，指导原则，指标|A guidepost is something that serves as a guide or indicator for future action or planning.|Her experiences served as a guidepost for her career choices. 她的经历成为她职业选择的指导原则。|synonyms: guideline, principle,  rule of thumb|antonyms:  misguidance,  confusion|noun
