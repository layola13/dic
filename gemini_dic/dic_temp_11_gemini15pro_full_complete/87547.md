heteromera:/ˌhɛtərɒmərə/ | n. |异节类甲虫| Beetles having five tarsal segments on the first and second legs and four on the third and fourth.  A group of beetles characterized by having five tarsal segments on the front and middle legs, and four on the hind legs.| The Darkling Beetle belongs to the heteromera group. 黑暗甲虫属于异节类甲虫。|  Heteromera beetles are known for their diverse habitats. 异节类甲虫以其多样的栖息地而闻名。| synonyms: Tenebrionoidea (superfamily encompassing heteromera) | antonyms: None (there isn't a direct opposite for a taxonomic group) | noun
