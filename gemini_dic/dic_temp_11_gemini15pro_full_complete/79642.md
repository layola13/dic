glossing:/ˈɡlɒsɪŋ/ | n. |注释，注解；光泽，光彩| The act of providing a gloss or explanation, especially a brief one, for a word or phrase. |  The editor provided glossing for the more obscure terms in the text. 编辑为文本中较为晦涩的术语提供了注释。| synonyms: annotation, explanation, commentary, interpretation | antonyms: obfuscation, confusion | noun


glossing:/ˈɡlɒsɪŋ/ | v. |注释，注解；掩盖，粉饰|  To provide a gloss or explanation for something. | He glossed over the difficult parts of the theory. 他掩盖了理论中困难的部分。| synonyms: explain, interpret, annotate, elucidate, whitewash | antonyms: obscure, confuse, complicate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From gloss (n.) + -ing. The noun "gloss" comes from Old French "glose" (explanation), ultimately from Late Latin "glossa" (obscure word needing explanation), from Greek "glōssa" (tongue, language, obscure word).
