lookalike:/ˈlʊkəˌlaɪk/| n.|相似的人或物；替身；酷似的人|A person or thing that closely resembles another.|He hired a lookalike to stand in for him at the press conference. 他雇了一个替身代替他出席记者招待会。|近义词：double, twin, imitator, impersonator|反义词:opposite, contrast|noun

lookalike:/ˈlʊkəˌlaɪk/| adj.|相似的；酷似的|Resembling someone or something else.|The two sisters are so lookalike that it's hard to tell them apart.  这两个姐妹长得太像了，很难分辨她们。|近义词：similar, resembling, alike|反义词:different, dissimilar, unlike|adjective
