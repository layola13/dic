curling:/ˈkɜːrlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɜːlɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |冰壶运动；卷曲，卷发|A sport in which two teams of four players slide heavy stones across a sheet of ice toward a target area.|例句：Curling is a popular winter sport. 冰壶是一项很受欢迎的冬季运动。

例句：Her hair has a natural curling. 她的头发自然卷曲。|近义词：coiling, twisting, waving (for the hair)|反义词: straightening (for the hair)|词性:名词

curling:/ˈkɜːrlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɜːlɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |卷曲的|Having a tendency to curl.|例句：She has long, curling hair. 她有一头卷曲的长发。

例句：The curling smoke rose from the chimney. 卷曲的烟雾从烟囱里升起。|近义词：curly, coiling, twisting, waving|反义词: straight|词性:形容词


词源: From the verb "curl," which originates from the Old Norse "krulla" meaning "to twist" or "to curl."
