gorily:/ˈɡɔːrɪli/ /ˈɡɔːrəli/
adjective
血腥地，残暴地
in a gory or bloodthirsty manner; involving or depicting violence and bloodshed in a gruesome or explicit way.
The movie depicted the battle gorily, showing every wound in detail.  这部电影血淋淋地描绘了这场战斗，详细地展现了每一处伤口。

The crime scene was described gorily in the newspaper. 报纸上对犯罪现场进行了血腥的描述。

近义词:bloodily, gruesomely, violently
反义词:peacefully, gently
词性:adjective
