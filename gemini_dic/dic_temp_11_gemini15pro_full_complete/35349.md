co-existence:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstəns/| n.|共存，共处|the state of existing together or at the same time, especially peacefully.|例句：Peaceful co-existence between different ethnic groups is essential for social harmony. 不同族群之间的和平共处对社会和谐至关重要。

例句：The co-existence of traditional and modern values is a hallmark of this society.  传统价值观和现代价值观的共存是这个社会的特征。|近义词：cohabitation, coexistence|反义词:conflict, antagonism|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin co- (together) + existentia (existence).
