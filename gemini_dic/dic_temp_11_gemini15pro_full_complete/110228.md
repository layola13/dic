lifehold:/ˈlaɪfˌhəʊld/| n.| 生命维持，生命控制（权）|A device or system that sustains life, often in a precarious situation; the power or control over someone's life.|The climbers' lifehold was a single, fraying rope. 攀登者的生命维持全靠一根磨损的绳子。|近义词：lifeline, support, sustenance|反义词: death grip, stranglehold|noun
