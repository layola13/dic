mussel:/ˈmʌsəl/ (US), /ˈmʌs(ə)l/ (UK)| n. |贻贝；蚌类|A marine bivalve mollusk with a dark, elongated shell, often found attached to rocks in coastal areas.  It is edible and considered a delicacy in many cultures.|例句：We ate fresh mussels at the seaside restaurant. 我们在海边餐厅吃了新鲜的贻贝。

例句：Mussels are often farmed for food. 贻贝经常被养殖作为食物。|近义词：clam, oyster, bivalve|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of shellfish)|词性:noun
