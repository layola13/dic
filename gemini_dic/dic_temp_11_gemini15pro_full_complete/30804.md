ceviche:/səˈviːtʃeɪ/ (US), /seˈviːtʃe/ (UK)| n.|酸橘汁腌鱼(生鱼片)|A dish of marinated raw fish cured in citrus juices, such as lemon or lime, and spiced with chili peppers.|We had ceviche for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚饭吃了酸橘汁腌鱼。|近义词: sashimi, crudo|反义词: cooked fish|noun
