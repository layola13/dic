inhale:/ɪnˈheɪl/ /ɪnˈhɛɪl/ | v. |吸入，吸气| To breathe in; to take air into the lungs.|  She closed her eyes and inhaled deeply. 她闭上眼睛，深深地吸了一口气。

The climber inhaled the crisp mountain air. 登山者吸入清新的山间空气。

|近义词：breathe in, respire|反义词:exhale|词性:动词

inhale:/ˈɪnheɪl/ /ˈɪnhɛɪl/ | n. |吸入, 吸气|The act of inhaling.|  Take a deep inhale and hold it for a few seconds. 深吸一口气，屏住几秒钟。

With each inhale, she felt calmer. 每次吸气，她都感觉更平静了。

|近义词：breath, respiration|反义词:exhalation|词性:名词
