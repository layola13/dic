lead:/liːd/ (n.) /liːd/ (v.)
noun|领导, 领先, 线索, 铅|A person or thing that guides or directs others; the position of being ahead or in front; a clue or piece of information that helps solve a problem; a heavy, soft, gray metal.|The tour guide led the group through the museum. 导游带领团队参观了博物馆。|synonyms: guide, chief, clue, hint|antonyms: follower, lag,  following|n.

lead:/liːd/ (v.)
verb|领导, 引导, 指引, 通向|To guide or direct; to be in charge of; to go in a particular direction.|The path leads to the garden. 这条路通向花园。|synonyms: guide, direct, conduct, escort|antonyms: follow, trail|v.
