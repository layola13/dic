barning: /ˈbɑːrnɪŋ/
adjective
燃烧的; 热烈的; 紧急的
burning describes something on fire, or a feeling that is very strong.
The house was burning fiercely. 房子猛烈地燃烧着。
She had a burning desire to travel the world. 她有着强烈的环游世界的愿望。
synonyms: ablaze, fiery, fervent, intense
antonyms: extinguished, cold, apathetic
word type: adjective
