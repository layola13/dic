criticaster:/ˈkrɪtɪˌkæstər/| n. |批评家（贬义），爱挑剔的人|A criticaster is a person who criticizes, especially someone who habitually criticizes harshly and unfairly.  They are often seen as petty or superficial in their judgments.|He dismissed his online detractors as mere criticasters. 他把网上的批评者斥为只不过是一些爱挑剔的人。|近义词：faultfinder, nitpicker, carper|反义词：supporter, admirer|词性:noun
