haloxyline:/ˌhæloʊˈzaɪliːn/ | n. |卤代木质素|A halogenated lignin derivative, often used in polymers.  It's formed by reacting lignin with a halogenating agent. |  Haloxyline exhibits improved flame resistance compared to unmodified lignin. 卤代木质素比未改性的木质素具有更好的阻燃性。|  Researchers are exploring the use of haloxyline in creating new bio-based plastics. 研究人员正在探索使用卤代木质素来制造新型生物基塑料。 |Synonyms: halogenated lignin | Antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym) | noun
