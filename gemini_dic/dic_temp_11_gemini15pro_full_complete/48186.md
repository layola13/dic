decomposition:/ˌdiːkɑːmpəˈzɪʃən/ |n.|分解，腐烂|The decomposition of organic matter creates compost. 有机物的分解产生堆肥。| The archaeologist studied the decomposition of the ancient mummy. 考古学家研究了古代木乃伊的分解。|近义词：decay, disintegration, breakdown, rotting|反义词：synthesis, composition, formation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *decompositionem, from decompositus, past participle of decomponere (“to decompose”), from de- (“down, away”) + componere (“to put together”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kom- (“beside, near, by, with”).
