gismo:/ˈɡɪzmoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡɪzməʊ/ (UK)| n. |小玩意儿；小装置；(尤指不知如何称呼的)新奇的小玩意儿|A gadget, especially one whose name the speaker does not know or cannot recall.  A mechanical or electronic device, especially an ingenious or novel one.|例句：
This car is full of gizmos. 这辆车里装满了各种小玩意儿。

例句：
He showed me a new gismo he'd bought for his car. 他给我看他为他的车买的一个新玩意儿。


|近义词：gadget, device, contrivance, contraption, thingamajig, doodad|反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "basic tool" or "simple implement")|词性:noun
