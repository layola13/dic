fussed:/fʌst/ | adj. | 烦躁的，大惊小怪的 | Feeling or showing excessive or anxious concern about something, typically something trivial. | She got all fussed about the seating arrangements. 她对座位安排大惊小怪。| 近义词：worried, anxious, bothered, agitated, flustered | 反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, unperturbed | 词性: 形容词

fussed:/fʌst/ | v. | 忙乱，大惊小怪 | To behave in an agitated way, especially about something unimportant. |  Don't fuss, it's only a small scratch. 别大惊小怪，只是一个小划痕。| 近义词：worry, fret, fidget | 反义词：relax, calm down | 词性: 动词
