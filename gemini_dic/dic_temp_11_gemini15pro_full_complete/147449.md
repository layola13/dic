overobsequiousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˌɒbsɪˈkwiːəsnəsɪz/
noun
过分殷勤，过分恭顺
Excessive obsequiousness; fawning behavior.
His overobsequiousnesses made his colleagues uncomfortable.  他过分的殷勤让他的同事感到不舒服。
Near Synonyms: subservience, servility, fawning, sycophancy
Antonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, independence, assertiveness
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- (excessive) + obsequiousness (from obsequious, from Latin obsequiosus "compliant, obedient," from obsequi "to comply with, obey," from ob- "toward" + sequi "to follow").  The plural form is created by adding -es to the singular noun.
