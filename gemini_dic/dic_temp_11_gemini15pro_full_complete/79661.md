glost:/ɡlɒst/ /ɡlɑːst/| n. |釉，光泽，釉料|A thin, glassy coating fused onto pottery or porcelain to give it a smooth, impervious, and often decorative surface.  The process of applying this coating.|The potter applied a beautiful blue glost to the vase. 陶艺家在花瓶上涂了一层漂亮的蓝色釉料。|Synonyms: glaze, enamel, varnish|Antonyms: matte, dull|noun
