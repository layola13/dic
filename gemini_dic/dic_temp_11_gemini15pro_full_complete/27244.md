calorie:/ˈkæləri/ (US), /ˈkæləri/ (UK)| n. |卡路里；卡(热量单位)|A unit of energy, often used to measure the energy content of food. |例句：This chocolate bar contains 200 calories. 这块巧克力含有200卡路里。

例句：A balanced diet should provide enough calories for your daily activities. 均衡的饮食应该提供足够的卡路里来满足你的日常活动。

近义词：energy unit|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
