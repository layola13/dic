一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to help the situation? 她做了什么来帮助解决这种情况？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? 你对他说了什么？
What happened at the party last night? 昨晚的派对发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

What had they decided before the announcement? 他们在宣布之前决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):

What will happen if we don't take action? 如果我们不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
