crookbill:/ˈkrʊkbɪl/| n. |弯嘴鸟; 歪嘴鸟|A bird with a curved bill.  Specifically refers to certain birds, particularly crossbills. |The crookbill expertly extracted seeds from the pine cone. 弯嘴鸟熟练地从松果中取出种子。|近义词：crossbill|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "straight-billed bird")|noun

crook-billed:/ˌkrʊkˈbɪld/| adj. |弯嘴的; 歪嘴的|Having a curved or hooked bill. |The crook-billed bird perched on the branch. 弯嘴鸟栖息在树枝上。|近义词：curved-billed, hooked-billed|反义词: straight-billed|adjective
