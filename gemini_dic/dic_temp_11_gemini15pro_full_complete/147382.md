overnervousness:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrvəsnəs/| n. |过度紧张，神经质|The state of being excessively nervous.  It's characterized by heightened anxiety, worry, and tension.|Her overnervousness made it difficult for her to perform well in the exam. 她的过度紧张让她很难在考试中发挥好。|近义词：anxiety, apprehension, tension, jitters|反义词：calmness, composure, tranquility|词性: noun

词源:
over- (prefix) + nervous + -ness (suffix)
"over-" 表示 "过度"， "nervous" 表示 "紧张的"，"-ness" 名词后缀，表示状态或性质.
