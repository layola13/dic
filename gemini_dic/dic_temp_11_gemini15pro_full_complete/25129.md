bucket:/ˈbʌkɪt/ (US) /ˈbʌkɪt/ (UK)| n. |桶; 容器; (一)桶(的量); 大量|a container with an open top and a handle, typically used for carrying liquids or other materials. It can also refer to the amount held by such a container or a large quantity of something.|例句：She filled the bucket with water. 她把桶装满了水。

|例句：He brought a bucket of sand to the beach. 他带了一桶沙子去海滩。|近义词：pail, container, vessel|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "nothing" in the sense of quantity) |noun
