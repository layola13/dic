fastens:/ˈfɑːsnz/
verb
系紧，固定
to close or join securely
She fastens her coat with a button. 她用纽扣系上外套。
He fastened the rope to the tree. 他把绳子系在树上。
secure, attach, connect
unfasten, loosen, detach
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fastnen, from Old English *fæstnian, from Proto-Germanic *fastinōną (“to fasten”), from Proto-Indo-European *pastH- (“firm, solid”). Cognate with Dutch vasten (“to fasten”), German festnen (“to tighten”).
