ashamed:/əˈʃeɪmd/| adj.|羞愧的，惭愧的|Feeling shame or guilt; embarrassed.|例句：I felt ashamed of my behavior. 我对自己的行为感到羞愧。|近义词：embarrassed, guilty, humiliated, mortified|反义词：proud, unashamed|词性:形容词

ashamed:/əˈʃeɪmd/| adj.|羞愧的，惭愧的|Originating from Old English "æscamod," past participle of "æscamian" meaning "to feel shame,"  ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*skamōną."  The word reflects a deep sense of personal disgrace or humiliation related to one's actions or character.|例句：She was ashamed to admit she had lied. 她羞于承认自己撒了谎。|近义词：contrite, remorseful|反义词：boastful, arrogant|词性:形容词
