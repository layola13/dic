bodyboards:/ˈbɒdiˌbɔːdz/ (UK), /ˈbɑːdiˌbɔːrdz/ (US)| n.|趴板，身体冲浪板|A rectangular piece of foam used for riding waves close to shore.|He bought a new bodyboard for his trip to Hawaii. 他买了一个新的趴板去夏威夷旅行。|近义词：boogie board, surf mat|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps surfboard for a different type of wave riding)|noun

bodyboard:/ˈbɒdiˌbɔːd/ (UK), /ˈbɑːdiˌbɔːrd/ (US)| v.|趴板冲浪|The act of riding waves using a bodyboard.|He loves to bodyboard in the summer. 他喜欢在夏天趴板冲浪。|近义词：boogie board, surf|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb
