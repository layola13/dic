diphthongic:/dɪfˈθɒŋɪk/ /daɪˈθɑːŋɪk/| adj.|双元音的；包含双元音的|Containing or consisting of a diphthong (a vowel sound that glides from one quality to another within a single syllable).|The word "coin" contains a diphthongic sound.  单词“coin”包含一个双元音。|

The sound /aɪ/ in "eye" is diphthongic. /aɪ/在“eye”中是双元音。|近义词: two-voweled |反义词: monophthongic|词性:形容词
