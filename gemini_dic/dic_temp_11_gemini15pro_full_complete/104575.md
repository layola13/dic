kangaroo:/ˌkæŋɡəˈruː/ | n. | 袋鼠 | A large marsupial with strong hind legs for hopping, a long powerful tail for balance, and a pouch on the abdomen of the female. |例句：The kangaroo hopped across the field.  袋鼠跳过田野。

例句：We saw a baby kangaroo in its mother's pouch. 我们看到一只小袋鼠在它妈妈的育儿袋里。

近义词：wallaby, wallaroo | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun
