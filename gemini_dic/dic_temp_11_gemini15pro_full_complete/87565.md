heteropathy:/ˌhɛtərˈɒpəθi/
noun
异疗法；异种疗法
A system of medical treatment by remedies that produce in healthy persons effects different from those produced in the person who is diseased.
Example: Heteropathy, unlike homeopathy, uses remedies that produce different effects in healthy individuals compared to those who are ill. 异疗法与顺势疗法不同，它使用的药物在健康人身上产生的效果与在病人身上产生的效果不同。

Example: Some practitioners believe heteropathy offers a more targeted approach to treating specific ailments. 一些医生认为异疗法提供了一种更有针对性的治疗特定疾病的方法。
近义词: allopathy 对抗疗法
反义词: homeopathy 顺势疗法
词性: noun

Etymology:
From hetero- (different) + -pathy (suffering, disease).  From Greek *héteros* (different) + *páthos* (suffering).
