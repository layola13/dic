octohedral:/ˌɒktəˈhiːdrəl/ | adj. |八面体的；八面晶系的| Having the shape of an octahedron, a solid figure with eight plane faces.| The diamond crystal has an octohedral structure. 钻石晶体具有八面体结构。|  Synonyms: octahedric | Antonyms: N/A | Adjective

octohedrical:/ˌɒktəˈhiːdrəl/ | adj. |八面体的；八面晶系的| Relating to or shaped like an octahedron.  An octahedron is a polyhedron with eight faces.  In chemistry, it often refers to a molecular geometry where a central atom is bonded to six atoms, forming eight faces. |  Iron pyrite often forms octohedral crystals. 黄铁矿常形成八面体晶体。| Synonyms: octahedric | Antonyms: N/A | Adjective
