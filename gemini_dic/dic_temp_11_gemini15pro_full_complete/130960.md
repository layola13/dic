nonassociative:/ˌnɒnəˈsəʊʃiətɪv/ | adj. | 非结合的 | Not associative; (of a binary operation) not satisfying the associative law, which states that the order of combining three or more elements does not affect the result. |  Octonions are nonassociative. 八元数是非结合的。| Noncommutative | Associative | adjective
