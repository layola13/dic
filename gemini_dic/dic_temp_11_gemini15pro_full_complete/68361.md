fallenness:/ˈfɔːlənɪs/
n.
堕落; 罪恶;  堕落状态
The state of being fallen, especially from a state of grace or innocence; a condition of moral or spiritual decline.
Human fallenness is a central theme in Christian theology. 人类的堕落是基督教神学中的一个核心主题。
Synonyms: depravity, degradation, corruption, sinfulness
Antonyms: grace, innocence, purity, righteousness
词性: 名词
