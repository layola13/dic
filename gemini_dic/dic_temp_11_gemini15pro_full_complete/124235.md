mossbunker:/ˈmɔːsbʌŋkər/ (US) /ˈmɒsbʌŋkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |鲱鱼;油鲱;门海登|A type of oily fish (Brevoortia tyrannus) found in the western Atlantic Ocean, often used as bait, fertilizer, and for fish oil.
