nonregistrable:/ˌnɒnˈrɛdʒɪstrəbəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈrɛdʒɪstrəbəl/
adjective
不可注册的；无法登记的
Not eligible to be registered; incapable of being registered.

例句：
The name chosen for the new company was deemed nonregistrable because it was too similar to an existing trademark.
新公司选定的名称被认为不可注册，因为它与现有商标太相似了。

The invention was nonregistrable as it lacked novelty and inventiveness.
由于缺乏新颖性和创造性，该发明无法注册。

近义词：unregistrable
反义词：registrable

词性: adjective
