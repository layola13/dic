go-for-it:/ɡoʊ fɔːr ɪt/ |idiomatic expression|加油；努力争取；放手一搏|An expression of encouragement to try something, take a risk, or pursue a goal.|例句：If you really want that promotion, go for it! 如果你真的想要那个晋升，就去争取吧！

例句：He was hesitant to start his own business, but his friends told him to go for it. 他犹豫要不要自己创业，但他的朋友们告诉他放手一搏。|近义词：give it a try, take a chance, go all out|反义词：hold back, hesitate, give up|phrase
