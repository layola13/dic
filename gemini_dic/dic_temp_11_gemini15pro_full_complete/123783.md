mootest:/ˈmuːtɪst/| adj.|最无意义的；最不实际的|Having the least practical significance; purely academic.|The mootest point in the entire debate was whether the team should wear blue or red socks.  整个辩论中最无意义的一点是球队应该穿蓝色还是红色的袜子。|近义词：irrelevant, inconsequential, trivial, academic|反义词：significant, important, relevant, crucial|词性:形容词

词源: From *moot* (adj.) + *-est* (superlative suffix).  *Moot* originally referred to an assembly held for discussion, particularly of legal matters.  Over time, the meaning shifted to describe something debatable, and then to something of little practical value.
