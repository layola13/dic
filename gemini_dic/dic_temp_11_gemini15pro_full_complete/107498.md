launch:/lɔːntʃ/ /lɑːntʃ/ | n. v. |发射，启动，发行，下水|An act of sending something out, propelling something into motion, or introducing something new.|

例句：
The launch of the new product was a great success.  新产品的发布非常成功。

The rocket launch was delayed due to bad weather.  火箭发射由于恶劣天气而推迟。


近义词：start, begin, initiate, release, introduce
反义词:land, terminate, withdraw

词性: noun, verb
