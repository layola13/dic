barstool: /ˈbɑːrˌstuːl/ | n. | 吧凳，高脚凳 | A tall stool with a footrest, typically used for sitting at a bar. |  He perched himself on a barstool and ordered a beer. 他坐在高脚凳上，点了一杯啤酒。

She swiveled on the barstool to face him. 她转动吧凳面向他。


| 近义词：stool, high stool, counter stool | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps chair or armchair) | 词性: noun
