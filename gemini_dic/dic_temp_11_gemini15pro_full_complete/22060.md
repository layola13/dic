boletus:/boʊˈliːtəs/ /bəˈliːtəs/| n. |牛肝菌；伞菌|A genus of mushroom, many edible, with pores rather than gills on the underside of the cap.  A type of edible mushroom with tubes and pores instead of gills beneath its cap.|例句：I found a beautiful boletus while foraging in the woods. 我在树林里觅食时发现了一个美丽的牛肝菌。

例句：Boletus edulis is considered a delicacy by many mushroom enthusiasts. 牛肝菌被许多蘑菇爱好者视为美味佳肴。|近义词：porcini, cep, king bolete|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps poisonous mushroom as a contrasting concept)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bōlētus*, from Ancient Greek βωλίτης (bōlítēs, “a kind of mushroom”).
