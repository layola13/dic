jelliest:/ˈdʒɛliɪst/| adj.|最像果冻的；最软的|Having the consistency of jelly, or being the most like jelly; extremely soft and wobbly.|The jelliest dessert at the buffet was the panna cotta.  自助餐中最像果冻的甜点是意式奶冻。|The old man's hands were jelliest I'd ever seen. 老人的手是我见过的最软的。|Synonyms:  wobbly, shaky, flabby, soft, yielding |Antonyms: firm, solid, hard, rigid|Adjective

jelly:/ˈdʒɛli/| n.  |果冻；胶状物|A sweet, clear food made with gelatin and fruit juice, or a substance similar to this.|I love strawberry jelly. 我喜欢草莓果冻。|The substance had a jelly-like consistency.  这种物质呈胶状。|Synonyms: gelatin, jam|Antonyms: solid|Noun
