例句：He admired the different cursivenesses displayed in the antique manuscripts. 他欣赏古籍手稿中展示的不同草书风格。

近义词：calligraphy, handwriting, script |反义词: print |词性: 名词

词源:  From cursive + -ness + -es.  "Cursive" ultimately derives from the Latin "currere" meaning "to run".
