macaronism:/ˈmækərəˌnɪzəm/ |n.|滑稽模仿;  穿插外语的写作风格;  混合语|The use of macaronic language, especially the mixing of vernacular words with Latin words or other learned terms, often for humorous effect. It can also refer to a style of writing that mixes two or more languages.|His poem was full of macaronism, blending English slang with lines of Latin.|近义词:code-switching, linguistic borrowing|反义词:purism|词性:名词
