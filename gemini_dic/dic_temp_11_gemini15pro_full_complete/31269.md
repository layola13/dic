chape:/tʃeɪp/| n.|包铁，金属箍，扣环，（剑鞘的）尖|A metal tip or fitting, especially one that strengthens or protects the end of something, like the tip of a scabbard or the metal plate on the toe or heel of a shoe.|The chape of his sword was intricately carved. 他的剑鞘尖端雕刻精美。|Synonyms: cap, ferrule, tip, |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "blunt end" in context)|noun

chape:/ʃæp/| v.|给...装包铁，用金属箍住|To furnish with a chape.|He carefully chaped the end of the walking stick. 他仔细地给拐杖的末端装上包铁。|Synonyms: cap, tip, protect|Antonyms: expose|verb
