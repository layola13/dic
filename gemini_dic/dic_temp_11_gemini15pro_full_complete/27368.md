camcorders:/ˈkæmˌkɔːrdərz/| n. |摄像机|A portable video camera that records both sound and moving images.  It combines a camera and a video recorder into a single unit.|I used my camcorder to film my daughter's graduation ceremony. 我用我的摄像机拍摄了我女儿的毕业典礼。|近义词：video camera, camcorder |反义词: None|名词
