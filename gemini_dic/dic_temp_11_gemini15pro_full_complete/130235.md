non-skid:/ˌnɑːnˈskɪd/ /ˌnɒnˈskɪd/| adj.|防滑的|Designed to prevent skidding or slipping.  Providing increased traction or grip to prevent sliding. |The non-skid tires helped the car maintain control on the icy road. 防滑轮胎帮助汽车在结冰的道路上保持控制。|The non-skid surface of the bath mat prevents falls. 防滑的浴垫表面可以防止摔倒。| 近义词：anti-slip, slip-resistant, tractioned| 反义词: slippery, slick|词性:形容词
