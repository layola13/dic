fusted:/ˈfʌstɪd/ | adj. | 发霉的；腐朽的；过时的；古板的 | Having a stale, musty smell; moldy; decayed. Also, old-fashioned or outdated in ideas or style; stuffy. | The fusted old books in the attic smelled of damp and decay. 阁楼里发霉的旧书散发着潮湿和腐烂的气味。 | The professor's fusted ideas about education were not well-received by the students.  教授关于教育的过时想法并没有受到学生们的欢迎。 | 近义词: musty, stale, moldy, antiquated, outdated | 反义词: fresh, modern, contemporary | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *fusten, from Old French fust "wine cask," from Latin fustis "cudgel, club." The original sense was likely related to the musty smell of old wine casks.
