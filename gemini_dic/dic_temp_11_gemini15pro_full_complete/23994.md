breathiest:/ˈbriːθiɪst/| adj.|最透气的，最气喘吁吁的|Having the quality of being airy or light; characterized by heavy or labored breathing.|Her breathiest voice sounded almost like a whisper. 她气喘吁吁的声音听起来几乎像耳语。|The sheer curtains allowed for a breathiest atmosphere in the room. 薄纱窗帘使房间里的空气最流通。|近义词：airy, light, gasping, panting|反义词：stuffy, airless, calm|词性:形容词
Etymology: From breath + -y + -est.  "Breath" comes from Old English "bræth," meaning "odor, scent, exhalation." The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."  The suffix "-est" is the superlative ending.
