misassignment:/ˌmɪs əˈsaɪnmənt/
n.
错误分配；分配错误
the act of assigning something incorrectly.
The misassignment of the funds caused a delay in the project.  资金分配错误导致项目延误。
The misassignment of tasks led to confusion among the team. 任务分配错误导致团队成员之间产生混淆。
近义词: misallocation, incorrect assignment
反义词: correct assignment, proper allocation
词性: noun

misassign:/ˌmɪs əˈsaɪn/
vt.
错误地分配；分配错
to assign something incorrectly.
The manager misassigned the task to a junior employee. 经理把这项任务错误地分配给了一名初级员工。
They misassigned the blame for the accident. 他们错误地归咎了事故的责任。
近义词: misallocate, incorrectly assign
反义词: correctly assign, properly allocate
词性: verb
