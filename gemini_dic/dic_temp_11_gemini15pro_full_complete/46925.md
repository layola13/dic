darlingly:/ˈdɑːrlɪŋli/ | adv. | 亲爱的；可爱地；讨人喜欢地 | In a darling or endearing manner; sweetly or affectionately. | She smiled darlingly at him. 她对他可爱地笑了笑。|  She spoke darlingly to the baby. 她用甜蜜的语气对婴儿说话。 | 近义词：sweetly, affectionately, endearingly, charmingly | 反义词：nastily, unkindly, cruelly | 词性: 副词

词源: From darling + -ly. "Darling" comes from Old English *deorling, from deore "dear, precious, beloved" + -ling (diminutive suffix).
