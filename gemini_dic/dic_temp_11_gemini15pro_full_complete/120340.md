minimalize:/ˈmɪnɪməlaɪz/| v. |最小化，使减到最小|To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree.|She tried to minimalize the risk by investing cautiously. 她试图通过谨慎投资将风险最小化。|The company minimalized its losses by selling off unprofitable assets. 公司通过出售不盈利的资产，将损失降到最低。|近义词：minimize, reduce, decrease|反义词：maximize, increase, enlarge|词性:动词

词源: From minim + -al + -ize.  The word "minimal" comes from the Latin "minimus" meaning "smallest."
