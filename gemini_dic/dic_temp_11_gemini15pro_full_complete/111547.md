lobber:/ˈlɒbə(r) (UK), ˈlɑːbər (US)| n. |高抛球手，吊高球的人；投掷手|A person who throws something, especially a ball, in a high arc. |He's a good lobber, always able to get the ball over the net. 他是一个优秀的吊高球手，总是能把球打过网。

The lobber tossed the grenade over the wall. 投掷手把手榴弹扔过了墙。|近义词：thrower, pitcher, hurler |反义词:catcher, receiver|noun


lobber:/ˈlɒbə(r) (UK), ˈlɑːbər (US)| v. |吊高球，高抛|To throw something, especially a ball, in a high arc. |He lobbed the ball over the defender's head. 他把球吊过了防守队员的头顶。

She lobbed a handful of peanuts to the squirrels. 她把一把花生扔给了松鼠。|近义词：toss, throw, fling |反义词:catch, receive|verb
