bioluminescence:/ˌbaɪoʊˌlʊmɪˈnɛsəns/ |/ˌbaɪəʊˌlʊːmɪˈnɛsəns/| n. |生物发光|The production and emission of light by a living organism as a result of a chemical reaction during which chemical energy is converted to light energy. |Fireflies use bioluminescence to attract mates. 萤火虫利用生物发光来吸引配偶。|Synonyms:  phosphorescence, light emission|Antonyms:  darkness, blackening |noun
