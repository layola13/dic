lapel:/ləˈpɛl/| n. |翻领；西装领|The folded-back collar of a coat or jacket, especially the part that is folded back below the collar and buttoned back to the coat or jacket.|He adjusted his lapel before entering the room. 他在进房间前整理了一下他的翻领。|Near synonyms: collar, facing|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "collarless")|noun

lapels:/ləˈpɛlz/| n. |翻领（复数）|The plural form of lapel, referring to the two folded-back parts of a coat or jacket collar.|His lapels were wide and stylish. 他的翻领很宽，很时尚。|Near synonyms: collars, facings|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
