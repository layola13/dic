mediastinum:/ˌmiːdiːəˈstaɪnəm/
noun
纵隔
The mediastinum is the central compartment of the thoracic cavity surrounded by loose connective tissue, as an undelineated region that contains a group of structures within the thorax.
The surgeon opened the patient's mediastinum to access the heart.  外科医生打开病人的纵隔以接触心脏。
The tumor was located in the mediastinum.  肿瘤位于纵隔内。
Synonyms:  mediastinal space
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an anatomical term like mediastinum)
