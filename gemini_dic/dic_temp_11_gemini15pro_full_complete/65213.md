etiolate:/ˈiːtiəˌleɪt/ /ˈiːtiəleɪt/| v. adj.|（使）变白；（使）变弱；（使）柔弱|To cause (a plant) to whiten or grow pale by excluding light. To make or become pale or sickly.|The celery stalks etiolated in the dark cellar.  芹菜茎在地窖里变白了。|近义词：blanch, bleach, pale, weaken|反义词: strengthen, invigorate|词性:verb, adjective

etiolated:/ˈiːtiəˌleɪtɪd/ /ˈiːtiəleɪtɪd/| adj.|变白的；柔弱的|Whitish or pale through lack of light.  Pale and sickly.|The etiolated seedlings desperately needed sunlight.  白化的幼苗迫切需要阳光。|近义词：blanched, bleached, pale, sickly|反义词: healthy, robust|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *étioler* "to blanch, grow pale," from Old French *estioler* "prop up (a plant)," from *estiole* "stalk, prop," from Latin *stipula* "stalk, straw."  The shift in meaning occurred because blanching of plants was done by tying them to stakes.
