homogenesis:/ˌhɒməˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. |同源发生，同型生殖|The development of different organs from the same germ cell layer is an example of homogenesis. 不同器官从同一胚层发育是同源发生的一个例子。|例句：The homogenesis of the various species within this genus is a topic of ongoing research.  这个属内不同物种的同源发生是一个持续研究的课题。

例句：Scientists are studying the homogenesis of certain plant structures to understand their evolutionary history. 科学家们正在研究某些植物结构的同源发生，以了解它们的进化历史。|近义词：homology, orthogenesis |反义词: heterogenesis |词性: noun
