ambitiousnesses:/æmˈbɪʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.|野心，雄心壮志（复数，罕用）|The state of being ambitious; a strong desire and determination to succeed.  It is generally used in the singular form (ambition).|His ambitiousnesses were evident in his relentless pursuit of power. 他的雄心壮志体现在他对权力的不懈追求上。|近义词: ambitions, aspirations, drives|反义词: apathy, indifference, lack of motivation
|词性:名词
