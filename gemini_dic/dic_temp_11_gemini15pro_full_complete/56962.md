double-act:/ˈdʌbəl ækt/| n.|双人表演，二人转，相声|A performance by two people, especially comedians or singers.|Laurel and Hardy were a famous double-act.  劳雷尔和哈迪是一对著名的双人表演艺术家。|例句：Their double-act has been entertaining audiences for years.  他们的双人表演多年来一直娱乐着观众。|近义词：duo, pair, twosome|反义词:|noun

double-act:/ˈdʌbəl ækt/| n.|串通，勾结|A collusive or collaborative effort between two people, often deceptive.|The police suspected the two men were a double-act, pretending to be strangers.  警察怀疑这两个人是串通好的，假装是陌生人。|例句：It turned out their argument was a double-act to distract us. 原来他们的争吵是串通好的，目的是为了分散我们的注意力。|近义词：conspiracy, collusion|反义词:|noun
