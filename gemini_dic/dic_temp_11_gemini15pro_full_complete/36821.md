colonialness:/kəˈləʊniəlnəs/ | n. |殖民性，殖民状态|The state or quality of being colonial, often referring to the lasting impact of colonial rule on a society, culture, or mindset. | The film explores the colonialness inherent in the education system. 这部电影探讨了教育体系中固有的殖民性。 | 近义词: colonialism, imperialness | 反义词: independence, autonomy | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "colonial" + "-ness". "Colonial" derives from "colony" (from Latin "colonia," meaning "farm," "settlement," later "colony"), ultimately from "colonus" ("farmer," "settler").  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality.
