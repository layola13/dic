cobber:/ˈkɒbə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːbər/ (US)| n.|朋友，伙伴，老兄（主要用于澳大利亚和新西兰）|A close friend or companion, primarily used in Australia and New Zealand.  It originates from the practice of Cornish miners sharing their "crib" or lunch, thus becoming "crib mates" which evolved into "cobber".|例句：G'day cobber, how are you going? 你好，伙计，最近怎么样？

|例句：He's a good cobber of mine. 他是我的好朋友。|近义词：mate, pal, friend, buddy, chum|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
