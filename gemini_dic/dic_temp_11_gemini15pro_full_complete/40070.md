contagion:/kənˈteɪdʒən/| n.|传染，蔓延，感染；传染病|The contagion of fear spread through the crowd. 恐慌的情绪在人群中蔓延开来。|例句：The doctor worried about the contagion spreading to other patients. 医生担心这种传染病会传播给其他病人。

例句：The contagion of violence is spreading throughout the city. 暴力事件正在全城蔓延。

近义词：infection, contamination, spread|反义词:immunity, resistance|词性:noun

contagion:/kənˈteɪdʒən/| adj. (rare)|传染性的|His enthusiasm was contagious. 他的热情很有感染力。|例句：His laughter was contagious, and soon everyone was joining in. 他的笑声很有感染力，很快大家就都跟着笑了。

例句：Her contagious optimism brightened everyone's day. 她富有感染力的乐观精神照亮了每个人的一天。


近义词：infectious, catching|反义词:non-contagious|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contagio, from Latin contingere "to touch," from com- "together" + tangere "to touch."
