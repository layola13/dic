exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | n. |功绩，业绩；英勇事迹|A brilliant, daring, or heroic feat or action.|例句：He was rewarded for his exploits during the war. 他因在战争中的功勋而受到嘉奖。|近义词：feat, achievement, accomplishment|反义词:failure, defeat|词性:名词

exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | v. |利用，剥削；开发|Make full use of and derive benefit from (a resource).  Take advantage of (a person or situation) in a way considered unfair or underhand.|例句：The company exploited cheap labor in developing countries.  这家公司在发展中国家剥削廉价劳动力。|近义词：utilize, harness, capitalize on, take advantage of|反义词:conserve, neglect|词性:动词
