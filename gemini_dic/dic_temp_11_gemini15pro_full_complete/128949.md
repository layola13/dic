ninja:/ˈnɪndʒə/| n.|忍者|A person trained in ancient Japanese martial arts and employed especially for espionage and assassinations.  A person skilled in stealth and secrecy.|例句：The ninja moved silently through the shadows. 忍者在阴影中悄无声息地移动。|例句：He's a real ninja in the kitchen, chopping vegetables with lightning speed. 他在厨房里像个忍者一样，以迅雷不及掩耳之势切菜。|近义词：assassin, spy, warrior, fighter|反义词: civilian, noncombatant |词性:noun
