manurers:/məˈnʊərərz/
n.
施肥者；施肥器
People who apply manure to land or devices used for spreading manure.
Farmers are the primary manurers of their fields.  农民是他们田地主要的施肥者。
Manure spreaders are mechanical manurers used in modern agriculture.  施肥机是现代农业中使用的机械施肥器。
fertilizers, spreaders
