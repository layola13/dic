handmaiden:/ˈhændˌmeɪdn/ | n. |侍女；女仆；婢女 | A female servant, especially one who attends a queen or princess.  A person or thing that helps to bring about something else. |  The queen was always surrounded by her handmaidens. 女王身边总是围绕着她的侍女们。| The internet has proved a useful handmaiden to democracy. 互联网已被证明是民主的有用工具。 | synonyms: maid, servant, attendant, abigail, ancillary, assistant | antonyms: master, lord, mistress | noun
