overprogramming:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræmɪŋ/
noun
过度编程，过度计划
The act of creating too many programs or activities, especially for children, leading to stress and a lack of free time.  Overprogramming can also refer to writing excessively complex or redundant code in software development.
例句：Overprogramming children with extracurricular activities can lead to burnout.  给孩子们安排过多的课外活动会导致倦怠。

例句：The software suffered from overprogramming, making it slow and inefficient.  该软件存在过度编程的问题，导致运行缓慢且效率低下。
近义词：overscheduling, overplanning
反义词：underprogramming, underscheduling
词性:n.
