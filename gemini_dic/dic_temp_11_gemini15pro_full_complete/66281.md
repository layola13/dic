excommunicates:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪts/
|v.|逐出教会；开除教籍|Officially exclude (someone) from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.|The Pope excommunicated him for heresy. 教皇因他异端邪说而将他逐出教会。|Synonyms: exclude, banish, oust, expel|Antonyms: admit, include, welcome|verb

excommunicate:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪt/
