janglers:/ˈdʒæŋɡlərz/
n.
喋喋不休的人;饶舌者;空谈者
People who talk at length but without making much sense.  Those who engage in idle or meaningless chatter.
The meeting was disrupted by a group of janglers who wouldn't let anyone else speak.  会议被一群喋喋不休的人扰乱了，他们不让其他人发言。
The bar was full of janglers, making it difficult to have a proper conversation. 酒吧里满是饶舌的人，很难进行一次合适的交谈。
近义词: chatterboxes, gossips, windbags
反义词: quiet people, listeners
