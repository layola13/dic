binning:/ˈbɪnɪŋ/| n.|装箱，分箱，数据分级|The process of grouping data into discrete intervals, or "bins," for analysis or visualization.  This simplifies the data and can reveal patterns.|Data binning was used to group the participants by age.|近义词：grouping, classifying, categorizing|反义词: scattering, separating|noun
