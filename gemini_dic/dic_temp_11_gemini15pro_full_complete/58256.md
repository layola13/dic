drumly:/ˈdrʌmli/| adv.|沉闷地；像鼓声一样地|Resembling the sound of a drum; monotonously; dully.  Characterized by a dull, monotonous, or rhythmic sound like a drum.|The rain drummed drumly on the roof. 雨沉闷地敲打着屋顶。|Synonyms: monotonously, dully, hollowly|Antonyms: melodically, brightly, cheerfully|Adverb
