interstrive: /ˌɪntərˈstraɪv/
verb
互相竞争，互相斗争
to strive or contend with one another; to compete internally
例句：The different factions within the company constantly interstrive for dominance.  公司内部的不同派系不断争夺主导地位。

例句：The conflicting desires within him interstrive for control. 他内心相互冲突的欲望争夺着控制权。
近义词：compete, contend, vie, rival
反义词：cooperate, collaborate
词性：v.
