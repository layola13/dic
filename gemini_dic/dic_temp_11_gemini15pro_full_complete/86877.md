hemiplegia:/ˌhɛmɪˈpliːdʒə/ (US), /ˌhɛmɪˈpliːdʒɪə/ (UK)| n.|偏瘫，半身不遂|Paralysis of one side of the body.|After the stroke, he suffered from hemiplegia and could no longer use his right arm and leg. 中风后，他患上了偏瘫，无法再使用他的右臂和右腿。|synonyms: unilateral paralysis, paralysis of one side|antonyms:  full mobility, no paralysis|noun
