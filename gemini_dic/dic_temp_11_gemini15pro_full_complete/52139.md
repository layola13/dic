diaper:/ˈdaɪpər/ (noun)|n.|尿布|A piece of absorbent material wrapped around a baby's bottom and between its legs to absorb and retain urine and feces.|The baby needs a diaper change.  婴儿需要换尿布。|近义词:nappy|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps toilet)|词性:名词

Etymology: From the Old French word *diapre*, meaning "ornamental cloth," later referring to the white linen cloth used as diapers.
