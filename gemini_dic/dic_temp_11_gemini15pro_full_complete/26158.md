businesses /ˈbɪznəsɪz/
noun
企业，生意
Commercial enterprises or activities, often involving the exchange of goods or services for profit.
例句：Many small businesses have been affected by the pandemic. 许多小企业受到了疫情的影响。

例句：She runs three successful businesses. 她经营着三家成功的企业。

近义词：companies, firms, enterprises, ventures
反义词: unemployment, inactivity

词性: noun (plural)
