hawkweed:/ˈhɔːkwiːd/| n. |山柳菊，鬼针草|A plant of the genus *Hieracium*, typically having yellow dandelion-like flowers and hairy leaves. Hawkweeds are known for their ability to reproduce both sexually and asexually, leading to a large number of microspecies.|The field was covered in yellow hawkweed.|近义词：dandelion, wildflower|反义词:|noun
