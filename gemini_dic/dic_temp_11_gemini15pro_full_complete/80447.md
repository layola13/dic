gonfalonier:/ˌɡɒnfəˈləʊniə(r)/ /ˌɡɑːnfəˈloʊniər/| n.|旗手；执旗官；（旧时意大利一些城邦的）最高行政长官|A gonfalonier was a standard-bearer or chief magistrate in medieval and Renaissance Italy. 中世纪和文艺复兴时期的意大利，旗手是掌旗官或首席行政官。|The gonfalonier led the procession, carrying the city's banner. 旗手带领着队伍，举着城市的旗帜。|Synonyms: standard-bearer, flag-bearer, ensign|Antonyms: follower, subordinate|Noun

gonfalon:/ˈɡɒnfəlɒn/ /ˈɡɑːnfəlɑːn/| n.|旗；（尤指）宗教或政治游行队伍的旗帜|The gonfalon of the city was paraded through the streets. 城市的旗帜在街道上游行。|The knights carried their gonfalons into battle. 骑士们带着他们的旗帜进入战斗。|Synonyms: banner, standard, flag|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain cloth")|Noun
