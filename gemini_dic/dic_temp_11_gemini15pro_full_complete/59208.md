dyspnoea: /dɪsˈpniːə/ (British), /dɪsˈpniːə/ (American)
dyspnoea: n.
dyspnoea: 呼吸困难，气促
dyspnoea: Difficult or labored breathing; shortness of breath.  It's a subjective experience of breathing discomfort.
dyspnoea:  The patient complained of dyspnoea after climbing the stairs. 病人爬楼梯后抱怨呼吸困难。
dyspnoea:  His dyspnoea was so severe he had to be hospitalized. 他的呼吸困难非常严重，不得不住院治疗。
dyspnoea: shortness of breath, breathlessness,  labored breathing
dyspnoea: eupnea (normal breathing)
