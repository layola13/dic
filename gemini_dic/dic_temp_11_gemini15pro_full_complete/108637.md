leady:/ˈliːdi/| adj.|像铅的；沉闷的；铅灰色的；含铅的|Resembling lead; dull, heavy, gray, or containing lead.|The sky was a leady gray, promising rain. 天空是铅灰色的，预示着要下雨。|近义词：gray, dull, heavy, leaden|反义词：bright, vibrant, colorful|词性:形容词

词源: From lead (n.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lauda-.
