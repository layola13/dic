mutch:/mʌtʃ/ | n. |  snuff; a small or insignificant person or thing (often used contemptuously)| A blend of tobacco and other ingredients ground into a powder, usually inhaled through the nose.  Can also be used as a derogatory term for a person or thing considered unimportant or insignificant. | He took a pinch of mutch. 他吸了口鼻烟。| synonym: snuff, rappee | antonym:  (For the tobacco meaning, there isn't a direct antonym.  For the derogatory meaning, perhaps something like "giant," "important person," "significant thing") | noun
