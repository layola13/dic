nonferventnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈfɜːrvəntnəsɪz/ | n. | 冷淡，缺乏热情 | Lack of fervor or enthusiasm; the state of being nonfervent. |  His nonferventness about the project worried his colleagues. 他对这个项目的冷淡让他的同事们很担心。| Synonyms: apathy, indifference, coolness, lukewarmness | Antonyms: fervor, enthusiasm, passion, zeal | noun

Etymology:
From non- + fervent + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Fervent" comes from the Latin "fervere" meaning "to boil, glow."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
