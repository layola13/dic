nonirrevocably:/ˌnɑːnɪˈrɛvəkəbli/
adv.
非不可撤销地，可撤回地
in a way that can be revoked or changed; not irrevocably
The contract stated that the agreement was entered into nonirrevocably.  合同规定该协议并非不可撤销地签订。
The decision was made nonirrevocably, allowing for future adjustments. 这个决定并非不可撤销，允许未来进行调整。
Synonyms: reversibly, changeably, conditionally
Antonyms: irrevocably, permanently, definitively
词性: 副词
