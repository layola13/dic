heterogenesis:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n.|异源生成，异代生殖|The development of one type of organism from a different type, as opposed to homogenesis.|Example 1:  Heterogenesis is a fascinating biological process observed in some insects.  异源生成是一种在某些昆虫身上观察到的迷人的生物过程。

Example 2: The scientist studied heterogenesis in aphids.  这位科学家研究了蚜虫的异代生殖。|Synonyms:  alternation of generations, heterogony|Antonyms: homogenesis|noun
