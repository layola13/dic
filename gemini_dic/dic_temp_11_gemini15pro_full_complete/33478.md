chylopoetic:/ˌkaɪləʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk/
adjective
乳糜生成的，与乳糜形成有关的
Relating to the formation of chyle.
Chyle is a milky bodily fluid consisting of lymph and emulsified fats, formed in the small intestine during digestion of fatty foods and passed into the lymphatic system.  Therefore, chylopoetic refers to the processes or organs involved in its creation.
The small intestine plays a crucial chylopoetic role, absorbing fats and forming chyle. 小肠在吸收脂肪和形成乳糜方面起着至关重要的作用。
The lymphatic system is essential for chylopoetic transport. 淋巴系统对乳糜的运输至关重要。
近义词:  chyle-producing
反义词: None
