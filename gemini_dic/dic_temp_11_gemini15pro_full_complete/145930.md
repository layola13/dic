overdisciplined: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪsəplɪnd/ | adj. | 过度自律的，管教过严的 |  Excessively disciplined; controlled too rigidly.  Characterized by excessive adherence to rules and regulations, often to the detriment of creativity, spontaneity, and well-being. | He was an overdisciplined child, never stepping out of line. 他是一个过度自律的孩子，从不逾矩。 |  The overdisciplined athlete pushed himself too hard and suffered an injury.  这个过度自律的运动员把自己逼得太紧，受了伤。 | 近义词: rigid, inflexible, strict, regimented | 反义词: undisciplined, lax, permissive | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "disciplined" (past participle of the verb "discipline," from Latin "disciplina" meaning "instruction, knowledge").
