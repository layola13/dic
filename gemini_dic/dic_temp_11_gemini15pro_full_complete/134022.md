nonhumanists:/ˌnɒnˈhjuːmənɪsts/ |n.|非人类中心主义者；非人本主义者|People who do not subscribe to humanist philosophies, which emphasize human values, reason, and agency, and often prioritize human interests above other considerations.  They might include posthumanists, those who believe in animal rights, or those with certain religious beliefs. |例句：Some nonhumanists argue that human needs shouldn't always take precedence over the needs of the environment. 一些非人类中心主义者认为，人类的需求不应该总是优先于环境的需求。

例句：The conference attracted a diverse group of scholars, including several prominent nonhumanists.  这次会议吸引了来自不同领域的学者，包括几位杰出的非人类中心主义者。 |近义词：anti-humanists|反义词：humanists|词性: 名词
