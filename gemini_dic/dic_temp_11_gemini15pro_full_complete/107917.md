larvated:/ˈlɑːrveɪtɪd/ /ˈlɑːrvˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|隐蔽的；假装的；未充分发展的；戴着面具的|Concealed, masked, or disguised, especially of a disease or symptom; undeveloped or immature.  Also, wearing a mask.|The larvated symptoms made the diagnosis difficult. 隐蔽的症状使诊断变得困难。|Synonyms: masked, concealed, disguised, hidden, dormant, latent, undeveloped, immature|Antonyms: overt, apparent, manifest, obvious, developed, mature|adjective

larva:/ˈlɑːrvə/ /ˈlɑːrvə/| n.|幼虫；幼体|The immature, wingless, feeding stage of an insect that undergoes metamorphosis.|The larva of a butterfly is called a caterpillar. 蝴蝶的幼虫叫做毛毛虫。|Synonyms: grub, caterpillar, maggot, wriggler|Antonyms: adult, imago|noun
