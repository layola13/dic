knarry:/ˈnɑːri/| adj.|多节的，多瘤的; (指树木)多节的，多瘤的|A knarry tree trunk. 一棵多节的树干。|例句：The old oak tree had a knarry trunk. 那棵老橡树的树干多瘤。

例句：The knarry branches reached out like twisted fingers. 多节的树枝像扭曲的手指一样伸出来。

近义词：gnarled, knotty, lumpy|反义词:smooth, straight|词性:形容词

词源:  From *knurr (“knot, lump, protuberance”), of imitative origin.  Related to gnarl.
