impanel:/ɪmˈpæn(ə)l/| vt.|组成陪审团；选定（陪审员）；登记（陪审员名单）|to enroll and swear in (a jury); to list as members of a jury|The judge impaneled a jury of twelve people. 法官组成一个十二人的陪审团。|synonyms: enlist, enroll, swear in|antonyms: dismiss, discharge|verb

impanel:/ɪmˈpæn(ə)l/| n.|陪审员名单；陪审团|a list of jurors; a jury|The impanel was made up of local citizens. 陪审团由当地公民组成。|synonyms: jury, panel|antonyms: N/A|noun
