ledbetter:/ˈlɛdbɛtər/ | proper n. | 莉莉·莱德贝特（人名）; 莉莉·莱德贝特公平薪酬法案（美国法律）| Ledbetter is a surname, and also refers to the Lilly Ledbetter Fair Pay Act of 2009, a U.S. federal statute that restored worker protections against pay discrimination. |  Lilly Ledbetter fought for equal pay. 莉莉·莱德贝特为同工同酬而奋斗。| The Ledbetter Act helps close the pay gap.  莉莉·莱德贝特公平薪酬法案有助于缩小薪酬差距。 | 近义词: (For the person) no direct synonym; (For the act) equal pay act, fair pay act | 反义词: (For the act) discriminatory act | 词性: 名词 (proper noun)
