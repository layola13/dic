faulted:/ˈfɔːltɪd/ | adj. |有缺点的；有故障的；断层的| Containing a fault or faults; defective.  Characterized by a fault; imperfect. |The software was faulted and crashed frequently.  该软件有缺陷，经常崩溃。|Synonyms: defective, flawed, imperfect, faulty |Antonyms: perfect, flawless, intact|adjective

faulted:/ˈfɔːltɪd/ | v. |找出…的缺点；弄错；产生断层（fault 的过去式和过去分词）|Past tense and past participle of "fault," meaning to criticize or find fault with something or someone; to make a mistake; to cause a geological fault. |He faulted her for not being prepared. 他责备她没有做好准备。|Synonyms: criticized, blamed, condemned, erred |Antonyms: praised, commended, lauded|verb
