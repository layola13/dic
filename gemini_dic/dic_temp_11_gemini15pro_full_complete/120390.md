minishment:/ˈmɪnɪʃmənt/
n.
减少，减损；惩罚
The minishment of his reputation caused him great distress.
他名誉的受损使他非常痛苦。
The minishment of their power was a gradual process.
他们权力的削弱是一个渐进的过程。
近义词: reduction, decrease, diminution, lessening, curtailment,  penalty, punishment
反义词: increase, augmentation, enlargement, growth, expansion, reward

词性: 名词 (noun)

词源:  From the verb "minish" (to make smaller or less), which comes from Old French "minuissier," ultimately from Latin "minuere" (to lessen).
