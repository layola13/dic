come-to-light:/kʌm tə laɪt/ | phrasal verb | 暴露；真相大白；为人所知 | To become known; to be revealed. |  The truth came to light years after the incident.  真相在事件发生多年后才大白。

The hidden documents finally came to light.  隐藏的文件最终被曝光。| 近义词: emerge, surface, be revealed, be discovered, become known | 反义词: be hidden, be concealed, be suppressed | 词性: phrasal verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From the literal meaning of something moving out of darkness and into the light, hence becoming visible or known.  The phrase is attested from the late 16th century.
