lard-asses:/ˈlɑːrdˌæsɪz/
noun
肥屁股; 胖子 (贬义, 粗俗)
A derogatory and vulgar term referring to overweight people, implying laziness and gluttony.
Example: "Those lard-asses are always hogging the couch." 那些肥猪总是霸占着沙发。
Example: "He called them a bunch of lard-asses." 他叫他们一群肥猪。

Synonyms: fatso, tub of lard, slob, couch potato
Antonyms: thin person, slim person, fit person
词性: 名词 (plural)
