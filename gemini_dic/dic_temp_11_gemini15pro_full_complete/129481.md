non-amenable:/ˌnɒn əˈmiːnəbl/ /ˌnɑːn əˈmenəbl/ | adj. | 不服从的，难处理的，不顺从的 | Not willing to comply; uncooperative. Also, not susceptible to treatment or remedy. |  The tumor proved non-amenable to chemotherapy.  这个肿瘤被证明对化疗没有反应。| Synonyms: intractable, unyielding, resistant, recalcitrant | Antonyms: compliant, cooperative, amenable, responsive | adjective

non-amenable:/ˌnɒn əˈmiːnəbl/ /ˌnɑːn əˈmenəbl/ | adj. | 不服管教的，倔强的 |  The non-amenable child refused to follow instructions. 这个不服管教的孩子拒绝听从指示。 | Synonyms: disobedient, unruly, defiant | Antonyms: obedient, compliant, docile | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + amenable.  Amenable comes from Old French amener "to bring, lead," from a- "to" + mener "to lead."
