noncomplacence:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪsəns/ |n.|不满足，不 complacency|The company's noncomplacence about its market share led to innovative product development.  这家公司对自己的市场份额并不满足，从而促进了产品的创新发展。|Near synonyms: dissatisfaction, unease, disquiet, restlessness|Antonyms: complacency, satisfaction, contentment|noun


Etymology:  From non- + complacency.  "Complacency" comes from the Latin "complacere" (to please greatly), from "com-" (with, together) + "placere" (to please).  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
