multitubular:/ˌmʌltiˈtjuːbjʊlər/ | adj. | 多管的 | Having many tubes or tubular structures. | The multitubular boiler is more efficient than the older single-tube design.  多管式锅炉比旧的单管式设计更高效。| The multitubular heat exchanger efficiently transfers heat between two fluids. 多管式换热器有效地在两种流体之间传递热量。 | 近义词: polytubular | 反义词: monotubular | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "much, many"
tubular (adjective): from Latin tubulus "small pipe," diminutive of tubus "pipe"
-ar (suffix): forming adjectives from nouns
