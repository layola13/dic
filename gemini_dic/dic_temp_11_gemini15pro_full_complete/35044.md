closeable:/ˈkloʊzəbl/ (US) /ˈkləʊzəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可关闭的，可合上的|Able to be closed.  Something that can be closed, such as a window, door, or file.|例句：The window is closeable. 这扇窗户可以关上。|例句：The application has several closeable panels.  这个应用程序有几个可以关闭的面板。|近义词：shuttable, sealable|反义词: open, uncloseable|词性:形容词
词源: close (v.) + -able (suffix) From the verb "close," meaning "to shut," and the suffix "-able," meaning "capable of being."
