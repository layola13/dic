antisleep:/ˌæntiˈsliːp/
adjective
抗睡眠的，防止睡眠的
Preventing or counteracting sleepiness.
She took an antisleep medication to help her stay awake during the long drive. 她服用了一种抗睡眠药物，以帮助她在长途驾驶中保持清醒。
近义词:wakefulness-promoting, stimulant
反义词:sleep-inducing, soporific
词性:adjective

antisleep:/ˌæntiˈsliːp/
noun
抗睡眠药物或设备
A drug or device that prevents or counteracts sleepiness.
The truck driver relied on antisleeps to maintain alertness on overnight deliveries. 卡车司机依靠抗睡眠药物来保持夜间送货时的警觉性。
近义词:stimulant, wakefulness aid
反义词:sedative, hypnotic
词性:noun
