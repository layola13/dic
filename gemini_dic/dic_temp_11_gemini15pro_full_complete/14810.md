axeman:/ˈæksmən/| n.|樵夫，用斧子的人，(尤指)刽子手|A person who cuts down trees with an axe, or (especially) an executioner.|The axeman swung his axe and felled the tree. 樵夫挥动斧头，砍倒了树。|synonyms: lumberjack, woodcutter, logger, executioner|antonyms:  (For executioner) savior, protector|noun
