falanges: /fəˈlændʒiːz/
n.
指骨;趾骨
The bones of the fingers and toes.
The surgeon expertly repaired the broken phalanges in her hand.  外科医生熟练地修复了她手上的断裂指骨。
Synonyms: finger bones, toe bones
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for body parts)
noun


falange:/ˈfælæŋks/
n.
方阵;密集队形
A body of troops or police officers, standing or moving in close formation.
The Roman legions advanced in a tight falange. 罗马军团以密集的方阵前进。
Synonyms: phalanx, formation, array
Antonyms: disarray, disorder
noun
