bakeries:/ˈbeɪkəriz/| n. |面包店（复数）|A place where bread, cakes, and other baked goods are made or sold.  The plural form of "bakery".| There are many bakeries in this town.  这个镇有很多面包店。|  There are two new bakeries on this street. 这条街上有两家新面包店。 | 近义词:bakehouses| 反义词: 无| 词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bakerie, from bakier (“baker”) +‎ -erie. Ultimately from Late Latin *pistrinarius, from Latin pistrīnum (“bakehouse”).
