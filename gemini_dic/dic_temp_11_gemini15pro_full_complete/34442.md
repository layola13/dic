clave:/klɑːveɪ/ (US), /kleɪv/ (UK)| n.| 钥匙；线索；关键；（音乐）节奏型|A rhythmical pattern, usually consisting of two bars repeated over and over, that forms the basis of many Afro-Cuban dance styles.|The clave rhythm is the heartbeat of salsa music.|Synonyms: key, clue, code, rhythm, beat|Antonyms: insignificance, irrelevance|noun

clave:/klɑːveɪ/ (US), /kleɪv/ (UK)| v.| 用钥匙开（锁）；用楔子敲入；插入|To strike or split something with a wedge.|He claved the wood to make kindling.|Synonyms: wedge, split, insert|Antonyms: extract, remove|verb
