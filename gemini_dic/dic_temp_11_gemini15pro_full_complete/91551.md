hyper-immunity:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. | 超级免疫，过度免疫 |  A condition of enhanced immunity, often artificially induced, characterized by a heightened immune response to specific antigens.  It implies an exceptionally strong or exaggerated immune reaction. | The researchers are exploring ways to induce hyper-immunity to certain viruses. 研究人员正在探索诱导对某些病毒的超级免疫的方法。| Hyper-immunity protects the body against many infections. 超级免疫力保护身体免受多种感染。 | synonyms: heightened immunity, increased immunity, superimmunity | antonyms: immunodeficiency, immunosuppression | noun
