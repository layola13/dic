carefulnesses:/ˈkɛərfʊlnɪsɪz/
n.
谨慎；小心；细心
The state of being careful; the quality of taking precautions or paying close attention to detail.
Her carefulnesses in checking the figures ensured the accuracy of the report. 她仔细检查数字确保了报告的准确性。
近义词: caution, prudence, diligence, meticulousness
反义词: carelessness, negligence, recklessness
词性: 名词
