episperm:/ˈɛpɪˌspɜːrm/| n. |外种皮|The outer layer of a seed coat, derived from the outer integument of the ovule.|The episperm of this seed is quite thick.  这种种子的外种皮很厚。|Synonym: testa (in general usage, though testa can include both layers of the seed coat)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps tegmen, which refers to the inner seed coat) |noun
