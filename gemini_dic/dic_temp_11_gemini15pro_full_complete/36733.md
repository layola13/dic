colloidal:/kəˈlɔɪdəl/| adj.|胶体的，胶质的|Relating to or consisting of a colloid.  A colloid is a substance microscopically dispersed evenly throughout another substance.|Milk is a colloidal solution. 牛奶是一种胶体溶液。|synonyms:  gel-like, gluey, viscous|antonyms: crystalline, solution|adjective
