gynandromorphous:/ˌɡaɪnænˈdrɒmɔːfəs/ |adj.|雌雄同体的，雌雄嵌合的|Having both male and female characteristics, especially with regard to external sexual characteristics.  This term is typically used in biology to describe organisms, particularly insects, that exhibit a mosaic of male and female traits.|例句：The researchers discovered a gynandromorphous butterfly with one wing displaying male coloration and the other displaying female coloration. 研究人员发现了一只雌雄嵌合的蝴蝶，它的一只翅膀显示雄性颜色，另一只翅膀显示雌性颜色。

例句：Gynandromorphous individuals are rare but provide valuable insights into sex determination and development. 雌雄同体的个体很少见，但它们为性别决定和发育提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：hermaphroditic, intersexual|反义词: unisexual, dioecious|词性: adjective
