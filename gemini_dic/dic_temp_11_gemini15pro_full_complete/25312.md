bugging:/ˈbʌɡɪŋ/| n.|窃听；安装窃听器|the act of secretly listening to someone's conversations using a listening device; the act of installing a listening device|The police were bugging the suspect's phone. 警方正在窃听嫌疑人的电话。|Synonyms: eavesdropping, wiretapping|Antonyms: None|noun
