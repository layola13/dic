gabbiest:/ˈɡæbiɪst/
adjective
最健谈的
Superlative form of gabby: most gabby.  Meaning excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters.
She was the gabbiest person at the party, talking to everyone about everything. 她是在派对上最健谈的人，跟每个人谈论所有事情。
The gabbiest member of the group dominated the conversation.  小组中最健谈的成员主导了谈话。
近义词:talkative, loquacious, chatty, voluble
反义词:quiet, reticent, taciturn
词性:adjective
词源:From gab + -iest.  Gab, meaning idle talk, is of unknown origin.
