non-partisan: /ˌnɒnˈpɑːtɪzæn/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈpɑːrtɪzən/ (US) | adj. |无党派的；不偏不倚的| Not biased or partisan, especially toward any particular political group.  Showing no allegiance to a particular political party or cause.|例句：The organization strives to maintain a non-partisan stance on all issues.  该组织努力在所有问题上保持中立的立场。|近义词：neutral, impartial, objective, unbiased |反义词: partisan, biased, prejudiced |词性: adjective

non-partisan: /ˌnɒnˈpɑːtɪzæn/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈpɑːrtɪzən/ (US) | n. |无党派人士| A person who is not affiliated with or biased toward a particular political party. |例句：He is a respected non-partisan and is often sought out for his balanced perspective. 他是一位受人尊敬的无党派人士，经常有人向他寻求均衡的观点。|近义词：independent |反义词: partisan |词性: noun
