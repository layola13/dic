hyperconformities:/ˌhaɪpərˌkənˈfɔːrmɪtiz/
n.
高度整合, 超整合（地层学）
Surfaces in the rock record that represent significant gaps in time, often encompassing multiple unconformities.  These surfaces are characterized by anomalously high stratigraphic relief.
The hyperconformity represents a gap of over 100 million years in the geological record. 超整合面代表了地质记录中超过1亿年的空白。
The geologist identified a hyperconformity in the exposed rock face. 地质学家在裸露的岩壁上发现了一个超整合面。
Synonyms: unconformity (broadly), paraconformity, disconformity, nonconformity, angular unconformity
Antonyms: conformity (generally refers to continuous deposition)
noun
