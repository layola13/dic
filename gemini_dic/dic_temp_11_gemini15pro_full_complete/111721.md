locomotiveness:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtɪvnəs/| n. |移动力; 运动力; 机动性|The quality of being locomotive; the ability to move from place to place.|The locomotiveness of the new robot surprised everyone.  这个新机器人的移动能力让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：mobility, motility|反义词：immobility, stasis|词性:名词
Etymology: From locomotive + -ness.  Locomotive comes from Latin "locus" (place) and "motivus" (moving).
