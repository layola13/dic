esoterically:/ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪkli/
adverb|深奥地；秘密地；秘传地|Understood by or meant only for the select few who have special knowledge or interest; confined to a small group.|He smiled esoterically, as if sharing a secret joke. 他神秘地笑了笑，好像在分享一个秘密笑话。|Synonym: obscurely, cryptically, privately, secretly|Antonym: exoterically, publicly, openly|词性: 副词
