half-deservedly:/ˌhæf dɪˈzɜːrvɪdli/ | adv. | 部分应得地，一半 заслуженно |  Meaning "in a way that is only partly deserved". It combines "half" and "deservedly". | He half-deservedly won the prize, as his contribution was significant, but not solely responsible for the success. 他一半应得地赢得了奖项，因为他的贡献很重要，但并非是成功的唯一原因。|  partially deservedly, partly earned | undeservedly, fully deservedly | adverb
