overregulate:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛɡjʊleɪt/
verb
过度监管，过度管制
to regulate something too much or too strictly
The government has been criticized for overregulating the banking industry. 政府因过度监管银行业而受到批评。
近义词: overcontrol, micromanage
反义词: deregulate
词性: verb
