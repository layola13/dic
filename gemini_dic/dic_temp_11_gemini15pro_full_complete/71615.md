flatbreads:/ˈflætˌbrɛdz/
n.
flatbreads
扁面包；薄饼
A type of bread made with flour, water, and sometimes yeast or other leavening agents, rolled into a thin, flat shape and baked or cooked on a griddle.
We had various flatbreads with hummus and other dips. 我们吃了各种各样的扁面包，配以鹰嘴豆泥和其他蘸酱。
近义词：pita, tortilla, naan, roti
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly "loaf" as it implies a shaped, risen bread)
