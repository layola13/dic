intercoordinated:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
相互协调的
arranged or structured so that the constituent elements function together effectively.
例句：The intercoordinated movements of the dancers were mesmerizing.  舞者们相互协调的动作令人着迷。

例句：The team's intercoordinated efforts led to a successful project completion. 团队相互协调的努力促成了项目的成功完成。
近义词：coordinated, synchronized, harmonized, integrated
反义词：uncoordinated, disjointed, fragmented
词性: adjective
