kennel:/ˈkɛnəl/ (US), /ˈkɛn(ə)l/ (UK)| n. v.|狗窝，犬舍；养狗场|A small shelter or cage for a dog or cat.  A place where dogs are bred, boarded, or trained.|例句：My dog loves sleeping in his kennel. 我的狗喜欢睡在他的狗窝里。|近义词：doghouse, cage, shelter|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb

kennel:/ˈkɛnəl/ (US), /ˈkɛn(ə)l/ (UK)|v.|把…关进狗舍|To put or keep (a dog) in a kennel.|例句：We kennelled our dog while we were on vacation. 我们去度假时把狗寄养在狗舍。|近义词：board, lodge|反义词:release|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *kenel (modern chenil), from Latin canile "dog house," from canis "dog."
