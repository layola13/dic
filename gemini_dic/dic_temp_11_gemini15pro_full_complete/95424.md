incinerable:/ɪnˈsɪnərəbl/
adj.
可焚化的；可烧尽的
Capable of being incinerated; combustible.
Medical waste is incinerable.医疗废物是可焚化的。
incinerable waste should be put in the designated container.可焚烧的垃圾应该放在指定的容器内。
combustible, burnable
non-incinerable, noncombustible
adjective
