bougainvillaea:/ˌbuːɡɪnˈvɪliə/ /ˌboʊɡənˈvɪliə/| n. |九重葛，叶子花|A tropical climbing plant with showy bracts that resemble petals. |例句：The bougainvillaea climbed up the wall, adding a splash of color to the garden. 九重葛爬满了墙壁，为花园增添了一抹色彩。

例句：She planted a vibrant pink bougainvillaea in a pot on the patio. 她在露台上的一个花盆里种了一株鲜艳的粉红色九重葛。|近义词：paperflower (less common)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of Louis Antoine de Bougainville (1729-1811), a French admiral and explorer.
