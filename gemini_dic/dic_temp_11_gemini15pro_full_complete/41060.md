coplanar:/koʊˈpleɪnər/| adj.|共面的|Lying in the same plane; points, lines, or figures are coplanar if they lie in the same plane.|The two lines are coplanar. 这两条线共面。|近义词：concurrent, coincident|反义词：noncoplanar|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + planar (pertaining to a plane). Ultimately from Latin planus (flat, level).
