coalfish:/ˈkoʊlfɪʃ/| n. |黑线鳕，绿青鳕|A North Atlantic fish (Pollachius virens) related to and resembling the cod, valued as a food fish. It is also known as saithe or coley.|例句：The fishermen brought in a large haul of coalfish. 渔民们捕获了大量的黑线鳕。

例句：Coalfish is a popular fish in many European countries. 黑线鳕在许多欧洲国家是一种很受欢迎的鱼。|近义词：saithe, coley, pollock|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From coal + fish.  "Coal" likely refers to the dark coloration of the fish's back.
