befuddle:/bɪˈfʌdəl/| v.|使迷惑，使糊涂|To confuse or perplex someone; to make someone unable to think clearly.|The complex instructions completely befuddled me. 复杂的指令让我完全糊涂了。|Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, perplex, mystify|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, illuminate|verb

Etymology: From be- + fuddle (to make muddy or cloudy, to confuse).  Ultimately from Old English *fudlian, likely imitative.
