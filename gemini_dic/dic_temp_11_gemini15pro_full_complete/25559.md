bulwarked:/ˈbʊlwə(r)kt/
adjective
设防的，有堡垒保护的，加固的
fortified or protected by a bulwark (a defensive wall).
The city was bulwarked against attack. 这座城市筑起了防御工事以防袭击。
The castle is bulwarked by massive stone walls. 这座城堡被巨大的石墙加固。

近义词: fortified, defended, protected, secured, shielded, reinforced
反义词: vulnerable, exposed, undefended, unprotected

词性: adjective

词源: From the noun "bulwark" + "-ed."  "Bulwark" comes from Middle Dutch "bolwerc," from "bole" (tree trunk) + "werc" (work). Originally referred to a defensive wall made of tree trunks.
