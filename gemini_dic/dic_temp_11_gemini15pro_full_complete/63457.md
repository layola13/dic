entrepreneurial:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːriəl/| adj.|创业的；企业家的；有创业精神的|Characterized by the taking of financial risks in the hope of profit; enterprising.|例句：She has a very entrepreneurial spirit. 她有着非常强的创业精神。|近义词：enterprising, business-minded, innovative,  |反义词:risk-averse, cautious, unadventurous|词性:adjective
词源: From French *entrepreneur*, from entreprendre 'undertake'.
