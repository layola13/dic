cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/
adjective
宣泄的，净化心灵的
Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; relating to catharsis.
The movie provided a cathartic experience for the viewers. 这部电影为观众提供了一次情感宣泄的体验。
cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/
noun
宣泄剂，净化心灵的事物
Something that provides psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions.
Writing poetry can be a cathartic for some people. 写诗对某些人来说是一种情感宣泄的方式。

近义词: cleansing, purifying, purgative, therapeutic
反义词: repressive, suppressive
词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Greek kathartikos, from kathairein ‘cleanse, purge’.
