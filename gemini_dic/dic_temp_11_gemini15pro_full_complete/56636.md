donkeys:/ˈdɒŋkiz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːŋkiz/ (US)| n.|驴子；傻瓜；顽固的人|A domesticated hoofed mammal of the horse family, with long ears and a braying call.  It is typically smaller and stockier than a horse and is often used as a beast of burden.  Informally, it can also refer to a stupid or stubborn person.|
The farmer used two donkeys to carry his goods to market.|农夫用两头驴把货物运到市场。|
He's such a donkey, he never listens to advice.|他真是个傻瓜，从来不听劝告。|
近义词：ass, mule, burro (for the animal); fool, idiot (for the person)|
反义词:genius (for the person - as an opposite to fool)|
词性:noun (plural)
