non-tarnishing:/ˌnɒnˈtɑːnɪʃɪŋ/| adj.|不失去光泽的，抗变色的|Not liable to become tarnished; resistant to tarnishing.  It describes something that doesn't lose its shine or discolor over time due to exposure to air or other elements.|This necklace is made of non-tarnishing silver. 这条项链是由不会失去光泽的银制成的。|The company uses a non-tarnishing coating on its products. 这家公司在其产品上使用了抗变色涂层。|近义词：stainless, corrosion-resistant, untarnishable|反义词: tarnishable, oxidizing|词性:形容词
