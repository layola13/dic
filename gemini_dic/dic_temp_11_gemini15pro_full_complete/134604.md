nonlugubrious:/ˌnɒnləˈɡuːbriəs/
adjective
不悲伤的，不忧郁的
Not lugubrious; cheerful, happy.
Her nonlugubrious demeanor surprised everyone at the funeral. 她并不悲伤的态度让葬礼上的每个人都感到惊讶。
Synonyms: cheerful, merry, joyful, lighthearted, jovial, happy, upbeat
Antonyms: lugubrious, mournful, sorrowful, sad, gloomy, depressed
词性: 形容词
