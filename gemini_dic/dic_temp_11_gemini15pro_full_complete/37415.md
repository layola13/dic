commercializers: /kəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪzərz/ | n. | 商业化者，商业推广者 | People or companies that bring products or services to market.  Those who adapt something for sale or profit. |  The commercializers of the new drug hope to see a large return on their investment.  这些新药的商业推广者希望看到他们的投资有很大的回报。 | The commercializers successfully launched the product into a new market. 商业推广者成功地将产品推向了一个新的市场。 | synonyms: marketers, promoters, merchandisers, entrepreneurs | antonyms: researchers, developers, inventors | noun
