coloboma:/kəˌlɒˈbəʊmə/ /kəˌloʊˈboʊmə/| n. |缺损，先天性发育缺陷|A congenital defect or fissure of the eye, typically involving the iris, choroid, or optic disc.|The child was born with a coloboma of the iris, giving her a keyhole-shaped pupil.|Synonym: defect, fissure, gap|Antonym: completeness, wholeness|noun

colobomata (plural):/kəˌlɒˈbəʊmətə/ /kəˌloʊˈboʊmɑːtə/| n. |缺损（复数）|Plural form of coloboma, referring to multiple congenital defects of the eye.|The patient presented with multiple colobomata affecting both eyes.|Synonym: defects, fissures, gaps|Antonym: - |noun
