cezar:/ˈseɪzər/ | n. | 凯撒（人名；尤指罗马帝国的统治者）| A title of Roman emperors, derived from Julius Caesar.  It also refers to a type of salad or a form of haircut. | Julius Caesar was a Roman general and statesman.  尤利乌斯·凯撒是一位罗马将军和政治家。|  Caesar salad is one of my favorite dishes.  凯撒沙拉是我最喜欢的菜之一。 | synonyms: emperor, ruler, autocrat | antonyms: subject, commoner | noun
