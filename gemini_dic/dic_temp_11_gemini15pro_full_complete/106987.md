laconicism: /ləˈkɒnɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 简洁, 简练; 言简意赅 |  The quality or style of using few words, expressing much in few words; brevity of speech. |  His laconicism was legendary; he never used two words when one would do.  他的简洁是出了名的；能用一个词表达的他绝不用两个词。 |  brevity, terseness, conciseness, succinctness | verbosity, prolixity, wordiness | noun

laconic: /ləˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 简洁的, 简练的; 言简意赅的 | Using or involving the use of a minimum of words; concise to the point of seeming rude or mysterious.  |  His laconic reply suggested he wasn't interested. 他简短的回答表明他不感兴趣。 |  brief, concise, terse, succinct | verbose, prolix, wordy | adjective
