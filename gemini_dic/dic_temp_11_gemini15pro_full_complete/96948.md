infabricated:/ɪnˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪd/
adj.
捏造的，伪造的
fabricated; made up; invented.
例句：The evidence presented was completely infabricated. 提出的证据完全是伪造的。

例句：He infabricated a story about being robbed. 他编造了一个关于被抢劫的故事。

近义词：fabricated, concocted, forged, invented, made-up, trumped-up
反义词：genuine, real, authentic, true
词性：形容词
