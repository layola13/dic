nonbearing:/ˌnɒnˈbeərɪŋ/ | adj. | 非承重的，不生息的 | Not load-bearing; not producing income or fruit. |  The nonbearing walls can be removed easily.  非承重墙可以很容易地拆除。

The investment was nonbearing for the first few years. 前几年这项投资没有收益。 | 近义词：unproductive, barren, sterile, infertile | 反义词：bearing, productive, fertile, fruitful | 词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + bearing (present participle of bear).  "Non-" prefix meaning "not". "Bear" in this context can mean "to support" or "to produce".
