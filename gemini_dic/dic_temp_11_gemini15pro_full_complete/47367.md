deactivated: /diːˈæktɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
已停用的，已失效的
made inactive or no longer effective.
The security system was deactivated after the alarm was triggered.  安全系统在警报触发后被停用了。
Her account was deactivated due to inactivity.  她的帐户由于不活动而被停用。
近义词: disabled, inoperative, off, inactive
反义词: activated, active, enabled, operational
词性: adjective

deactivated: /diːˈæktɪveɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
停用，使失效
past participle of deactivate.
The bomb was deactivated by the bomb squad.  炸弹被拆弹小组拆除了。
He deactivated his Facebook account temporarily.  他暂时停用了他的脸书账户。
近义词: disabled, neutralized, switched off
反义词: activated, enabled, switched on
词性: verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (indicating reversal or removal) + activate + -ed (past participle suffix).  "Activate" comes from Late Latin "activatus," past participle of "activare," meaning "to make active," from Latin "activus," meaning "active."
