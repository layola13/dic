dolby:/ˈdoʊlbi/ (US), /ˈdɒlbi/ (UK)| n. adj.|杜比|A trademark for a system of noise reduction used in recording and reproducing sound, especially in films and on tape; also, the sound produced by this system.|The movie was shown in Dolby Surround Sound. 这部电影是用杜比环绕声播放的。|近义词:stereo, surround sound|反义词:mono|noun, adjective
