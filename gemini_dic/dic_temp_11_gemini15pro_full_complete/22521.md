boomerah:/ˈbuːməræŋ/ | n. | 回飞棒；自食其果的行为 | A curved wooden missile configured to return to the thrower.  Something that returns to its originator, often with negative consequences. | He threw the boomerang and it came spiraling back. 他扔出回飞棒，它旋转着飞了回来。 |  The policy backfired on the government like a boomerang. 这项政策对政府来说就像回飞棒一样适得其反。 | synonyms: comeuppance, backlash, recoil | antonyms:  gift, blessing | noun
