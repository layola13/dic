homonymity:/ˌhɒməˈnɪmɪti/ | n. |同音异义, 同形异义|The state or quality of being a homonym.  The coexistence of two or more words with the same spelling and/or pronunciation but different meanings.|The homonymity of "bat" (animal) and "bat" (sports equipment) can sometimes cause confusion. “bat”（动物）和“bat”（运动器材）的同音异义有时会造成混淆。|近义词：homonymy, polysemy|反义词: - |词性: noun

Etymology:  From French *homonymité*, from Ancient Greek ὁμωνυμία (homōnumía, “sameness of name”), from ὁμώνυμος (homṓnumos, “having the same name”).
