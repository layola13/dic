ecstatic: /ɪkˈstætɪk/ /ekˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 狂喜的；欣喜若狂的 | Feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness. |  She was ecstatic when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息欣喜若狂。|  He was ecstatic about his team's victory.  他为他的团队的胜利而欣喜若狂。 |  thrilled, elated, overjoyed, rapturous |  disappointed, depressed, miserable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin ecstaticus, from Greek ekstatikos "beside oneself," from ekstasis "displacement, trance," literally "a putting out of place," from ek "out" + histanai "to cause to stand."
