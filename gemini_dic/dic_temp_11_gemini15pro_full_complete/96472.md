individualizes:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使个性化；个别化；区别对待
To make something suitable for individual people or things; to treat something as different from others.
The teacher individualizes instruction to meet each student's needs. 老师根据每个学生的需要进行个性化教学。
individualize, personalize, customize, tailor
standardize, generalize
v.
