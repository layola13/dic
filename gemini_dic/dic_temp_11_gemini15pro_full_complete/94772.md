imprudently: /ɪmˈpruːdəntli/ | adv. | 不谨慎地，轻率地 | Not wisely or carefully; lacking prudence. | He imprudently invested all his savings in a single stock. 他不谨慎地把所有积蓄都投资在一支股票上。 | 近义词: rashly, recklessly, unwisely, foolishly | 反义词: prudently, cautiously, wisely, carefully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Latin *imprudent-, from in- "not" + prudent- "prudent," present participle stem of prudēre "to be prudent."
