crampon:/ˈkræmpɒn/ (US) /ˈkræmpɒn/ (UK)| n.|冰爪，防滑钉|A metal frame with spikes attached to boots for walking on ice or snow.|He strapped on his crampons before tackling the icy slope. 他在攀登冰坡之前系好了冰爪。|近义词：spikes, cleats|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth soles")|名词


crampon:/ˈkræmpɒn/ (US) /ˈkræmpɒn/ (UK)| v.|用冰爪攀登; 装上冰爪|To climb using crampons; to attach crampons to.|They cramponed their way up the glacier. 他们用冰爪爬上了冰川。|近义词：climb, ascend|反义词: descend|动词
