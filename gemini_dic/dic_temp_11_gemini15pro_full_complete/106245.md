know-how:/ˈnoʊhaʊ/
词性:n.
中文翻译:专门技能，专业知识，诀窍
英文解析:Practical knowledge and skill, especially in a particular job or activity.
例句:She has the know-how to run a successful business. 她拥有成功经营企业的专业知识。

例句:He lacks the know-how to fix the computer. 他缺乏修理电脑的技能。

近义词:expertise, skill, experience, proficiency
反义词:ignorance, inexperience, incompetence
