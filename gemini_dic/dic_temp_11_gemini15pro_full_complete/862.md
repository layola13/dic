abstractnesses:/ˈæbˌstræktnəsɪz/
n.
抽象性；抽象的概念
The abstractnesses of the philosophical debate left many audience members confused.
哲学辩论的抽象性让许多听众感到困惑。
The artist explored the abstractnesses of time and memory in her work.
这位艺术家在她的作品中探索了时间和记忆的抽象性。
近义词:abstractions, generalities
反义词:specifics, concreteness
词性:名词

abstractness:/ˈæbˌstræktnəs/
n.
抽象性；抽象的概念
The abstractness of the concept made it difficult to grasp.
这个概念的抽象性使它难以理解。
He preferred the abstractness of mathematics to the complexities of human relationships.
比起人际关系的复杂性，他更喜欢数学的抽象性。
近义词:abstraction, generality
反义词:specificity, concreteness
词性:名词

词源: From abstract + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *abstractus*, past participle of abstrahere "to draw away".
