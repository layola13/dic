ethyls:/ˈɛθɪlz/| n.|乙基|A univalent hydrocarbon radical, C2H5, derived from ethane by removal of one hydrogen atom.|Ethyl groups are common in organic chemistry. 乙基在有机化学中很常见。|近义词: alkyl|反义词: 无|词性:名词

词源: From ethyl + -yl.  "Ethyl" comes from ether + -yl, ultimately from Greek *aithēr (bright, clear sky, upper air).
