binocular:/bɪˈnɒkjʊlə(r) (UK), /baɪˈnɑːkjələr (US)| adj. n.|双筒的，双目的；双筒望远镜|relating to, using, or adapted for both eyes; a device having two tubes containing lenses and prisms, used for looking at distant objects with both eyes|I watched the birds through my binoculars. 我用双筒望远镜观察鸟类。|近义词：field glasses, opera glasses|反义词:monocular|词性:adjective, noun
