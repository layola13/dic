hulky:/ˈhʌlki/| adj.|笨重的，庞大的|Large, heavy, and clumsy.|例句：The hulky furniture made the room feel cramped.  笨重的家具使房间感觉很狭窄。

例句：He was a hulky man with broad shoulders. 他是一个有着宽阔肩膀的魁梧男人。|近义词：massive, bulky, hefty, ponderous|反义词：small, slight, petite|词性:形容词

词源:  From *hulk* + *-y*.  The adjective *hulk* itself dates to the 16th century and originally referred to a large, unwieldy ship.  Over time, the meaning extended to anything large and clumsy.
