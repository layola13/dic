flashback:/ˈflæʃbæk/| n.|闪回；倒叙|A scene in a movie, novel, etc., set in a time earlier than the main story.|The movie uses flashbacks to tell the story of the character's childhood. 这部电影用闪回讲述了角色的童年故事。|近义词：regression, recall|反义词: - |noun

flashback:/ˈflæʃbæk/| v.|闪回；倒叙|To have a sudden, vivid memory of a past event.|She flashed back to the day she graduated from college. 她突然回忆起大学毕业那天。|近义词：reminisce, remember|反义词: - |verb
