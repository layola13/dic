assertiveness:/əˈsɜːrtɪvnəs/| n.|自信; 坚定; 果断|The quality of being confident and not frightened to say what you want or believe.|She spoke with assertiveness, clearly stating her demands. 她坚定地发言，清楚地表明了自己的要求。|Synonyms: confidence, self-assurance, firmness, decisiveness|Antonyms: timidity, shyness, meekness, submissiveness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From assert (v.) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  The verb "assert" comes from Latin "asserere," meaning "to claim, maintain, affirm."
