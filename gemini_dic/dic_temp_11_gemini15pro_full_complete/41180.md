copying: /ˈkɒpiɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiɪŋ/ (US) | n. | 复制；抄写；临摹 | the act of making a copy of something |  Example: The copying of the document took several hours.  复制这份文件花了几个小时。 | Synonyms: duplication, replication, imitation | Antonyms: originating, creating | noun


copying: /ˈkɒpiɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiɪŋ/ (US) | adj. | 复制的；抄写的 | relating to or involving the act of copying | Example:  He was using a copying machine. 他正在使用复印机。 | Synonyms: duplicating, replicating | Antonyms: original, unique | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *copiyng, *copyinge, from Anglo-Norman copier, from Latin copiāre (“to copy, transcribe”), from copia (“abundance, plenty, supply, multitude; transcript, copy”).
