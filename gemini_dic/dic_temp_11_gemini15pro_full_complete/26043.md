bushest:/ˈbʊʃɪɪst/ | adj. | 最灌木丛生的；灌木最茂密的 |  Superlative form of 'bushy':  having a lot of bushes.  | The bushest part of the garden was home to a family of rabbits. 花园里灌木丛最茂密的地方，住着一窝兔子。 |  densest, thickest, most overgrown | sparsest, thinnest, barest | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bysc (recorded only as bysc-sceat "thicket") and *busk, of uncertain origin. Related to Middle Dutch busch, Old High German busk, German Busch "bush." The superlative suffix "-est" is a common Germanic ending used to form the highest degree of comparison.
