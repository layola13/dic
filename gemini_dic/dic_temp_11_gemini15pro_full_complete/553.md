aborted:/əˈbɔːr.tɪd/ | adj. v. |中止的，夭折的；流产的|Stopped before it was finished or successful; miscarried. |The mission was aborted due to bad weather.  任务因恶劣天气而中止。|The launch of the new product was aborted at the last minute.  新产品的发布在最后一刻被取消了。|Synonyms: abandoned, cancelled, halted, terminated, |Antonyms: completed, finished, continued, successful|verb

aborted:/əˈbɔːr.tɪd/ | adj. v. |流产的|Ended a pregnancy deliberately. |She aborted the pregnancy in the first trimester. 她在孕早期终止了妊娠。|In some countries, it is legal to have an aborted pregnancy. 在一些国家，终止妊娠是合法的。|Synonyms: terminated, ended|Antonyms: carried to term, delivered|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abortus*, past participle of *aborīrī* ("to miscarry, fail to be born"), from *ab* ("from, away") + *orīrī* ("to arise, be born").
