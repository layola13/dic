misintention:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛnʃən/
|n.|
|恶意；不良意图|
|An intention to do something wrong or harmful.  A wrongful or improper purpose.|
|His misintention was clear from the start; he wanted to sabotage the project. 他从一开始就心怀叵测，想要破坏这个项目。|

|The lawyer argued that the defendant's actions were a result of misintention, not negligence. 律师辩称被告的行为是恶意而非疏忽造成的。|
|近义词：malice, ill will, malevolence|
|反义词：benevolence, goodwill, kindness|
|词性：名词|
|词源：mis- (bad, wrong) + intention|
