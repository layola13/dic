escritorial:/ˌeskritɔ́ːriəl/ | adj. n. | 书斋的；写作的；著述的；书房；办公室 | Relating to writing or a writer's study; a writing room or office. |

例句：
He spent hours in his escritorial sanctuary, crafting his next novel.
他在他的书房圣殿里待了几个小时，精心创作他的下一部小说。

例句：
The room had an escritorial feel, with bookshelves lining the walls.
这个房间有一种书房的感觉，书架沿着墙壁排列。

近义词：literary, scholarly, academic, writerly |
反义词：illiterate, unscholarly |
词性：adjective, noun |

词源: From Spanish *escritorio* "writing desk, office," from Latin *scriptorium* "a place for writing," from *scribere* "to write." |
