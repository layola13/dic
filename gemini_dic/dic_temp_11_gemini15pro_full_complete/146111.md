overembroider: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈbrɔɪdər/
verb
过度刺绣；绣得过多
To embroider excessively or too elaborately; to cover with too much embroidery.
The bodice of the dress was overembroidered with pearls and sequins.  这件衣服的胸衣上绣满了珍珠和亮片。
She overembroidered the cushion until it was stiff and uncomfortable. 她给垫子绣了太多，直到它变得僵硬不舒服。
近义词: overdecorate, embellish
反义词: simplify, understate
词性: verb

Etymology:
From over- + embroider.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much."  "Embroider" comes from the Old French word "embroidier," meaning "to decorate with needlework."
