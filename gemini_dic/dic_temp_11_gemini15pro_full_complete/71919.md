flexuous:/ˈflɛkʃuəs/| adj.|弯曲的，蜿蜒的，柔韧的|Characterized by regular curves or bends; sinuous; not straight.|The river flowed in a flexuous course through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。|近义词：winding, sinuous, tortuous, serpentine|反义词：straight, rigid, inflexible|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flexuosus*, from *flexus*, past participle of *flectere* "to bend."
