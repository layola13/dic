nonaggressions:/ˌnɑnəˈɡreʃənz/ | n. | 非侵略行为（复数） | Multiple instances of refraining from aggressive behavior or intentions; multiple policies of not initiating hostilities. | The historian documented several nonaggressions between the tribes. 历史学家记录了部落间的几次互不侵犯行为。 | synonym: peaceful coexistences, truces | antonym: attacks, offenses | noun
