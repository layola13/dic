corundum: /kəˈrʌndəm/ | n. |刚玉|A very hard mineral, aluminium oxide, that occurs in several forms, including ruby and sapphire. |Rubies and sapphires are varieties of corundum. 红宝石和蓝宝石是刚玉的变种。|近义词:  alumina,  oxide |反义词: None (as it's a specific mineral) |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Tamil *kurundam*, ultimately from Sanskrit *kuruvinda*, meaning "ruby".
