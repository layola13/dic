neocolonial:/ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəl/ | adj. n.|新殖民主义的；新殖民主义者|Relating to or characteristic of neocolonialism; a person who supports or participates in neocolonialism. |  Many developing nations accuse multinational corporations of being neocolonial. 许多发展中国家指责跨国公司是新殖民主义者。| Near synonyms: postcolonial, imperialist |Antonyms: independent, autonomous | adjective, noun

neocolonials:/ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəlz/ | n. |新殖民主义者|People who support or practice neocolonialism. | The neocolonials exploit the resources of developing countries. 这些新殖民主义者剥削发展中国家的资源。| Near synonyms: imperialists, exploiters | Antonyms: liberators, anti-imperialists | noun
