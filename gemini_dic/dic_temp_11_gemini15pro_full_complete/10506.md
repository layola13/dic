appointive:/əˈpɔɪntɪv/
adjective
任命的；委任的
Relating to or filled by appointment rather than election.
The president has the power to make numerous appointive offices.  总统有权任命许多官员。
Several appointive positions on the board remain vacant. 董事会中几个任命的职位仍然空缺。
Synonyms: appointed, designated, nominated
Antonyms: elected, elective
