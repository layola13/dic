contemptible:/kənˈtɛmptɪbl/ | adj. | 卑鄙的，可鄙的，令人轻视的 | Deserving contempt; despicable. |  His contemptible behavior disgusted everyone. 他的卑鄙行为令每个人都感到恶心。

Her treatment of her staff was contemptible. 她对待员工的方式令人不齿。


近义词: despicable, loathsome, detestable, abject, base, mean, vile, low
反义词: admirable, respectable, honorable, praiseworthy
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *contemptibilis, from Latin contemnere (“to despise”) + -ibilis.  Equivalent to contemn + -ible.
