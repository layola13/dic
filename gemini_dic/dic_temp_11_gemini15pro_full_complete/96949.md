infallibilist:/ɪnˈfælɪbəlɪst/
|n.|绝对正确论者；无错论者|A person who believes in infallibility, especially papal infallibility.|He considered himself an infallibilist, convinced his judgment was always sound. 他自认为是一个绝对正确论者，确信自己的判断总是正确的。|Synonyms: doctrinaire, dogmatist|Antonyms: doubter, skeptic|Noun
