irrational:/ɪˈræʃ(ə)nəl/ | adj. |不合理的，无理性的|Not based on reason or logic; unreasonable.|例句：His irrational fear of spiders made him avoid going into the basement. 他对蜘蛛的非理性恐惧使他不敢去地下室。

例句：It's irrational to expect to win the lottery every time you buy a ticket. 每次买彩票都期望中奖是不理智的。|近义词：illogical, unreasonable, absurd, senseless|反义词：rational, logical, reasonable, sensible|词性:形容词

irrationality:/ɪˌræʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. |不合理，无理性|The state or quality of being irrational.|例句：The irrationality of her decision surprised everyone. 她决定的不合理性让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：He displayed a certain irrationality in his obsession with collecting bottle caps. 他对收集瓶盖的痴迷表现出某种非理性。|近义词：unreasonableness, absurdity, folly|反义词：rationality, reason, logic|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin irrationalis, from in- ‘not’ + rationalis (see rational).
