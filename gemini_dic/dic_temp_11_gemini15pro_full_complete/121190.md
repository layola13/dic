misdo:/mɪsˈduː/ |n.| 错事，过失 |A misdo is a wrong or improper act; a misdeed. |例句：His youthful misdos came back to haunt him. 他年轻时的错误回来困扰他了。

例句：She confessed her misdo to the priest. 她向神父忏悔了她的过错。 |近义词：misdeed, wrongdoing, offense, transgression |反义词：good deed, virtue |词性: 名词


词源:  From mis- + do.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English origin, meaning "wrongly" or "badly." "Do" is from Old English *dōn, of Germanic origin, from a base meaning "put, place."
