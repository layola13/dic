humanify:/ˌhjuːmənɪfaɪ/ /ˌjuːmənɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使人性化；赋予人性 | To give something non-human human qualities or characteristics; to make something more humane. |  Example 1:  The company tried to humanify its brand by creating a friendly mascot.  公司试图通过创造一个友好的吉祥物来使它的品牌人性化。

Example 2:  The author humanified the animals in her story, giving them the ability to speak and reason.  作者将她故事中的动物人性化，赋予它们说话和推理的能力。 | 近义词: anthropomorphize, personalize | 反义词: dehumanize, objectify | 词性: 动词
