heresy:/ˈherəsi/ /ˈhɛrəsi/| n.|异端邪说，谬见|An opinion or belief that is very different from accepted beliefs, especially in religion.  A belief that goes against Christian doctrine.|例句：The church regards this teaching as heresy. 教会认为这种教义是异端。

例句：He was burned at the stake for heresy. 他因异端邪说被绑在火刑柱上烧死。|近义词：dissidence, unorthodoxy, blasphemy, apostasy, nonconformity|反义词：orthodoxy, conformity|词性:noun
