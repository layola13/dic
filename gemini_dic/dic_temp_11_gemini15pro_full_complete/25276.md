buffoon:/bəˈfuːn/| n.|小丑，滑稽可笑的人|A buffoon is a person who amuses others by clowning, by tricks, jokes, and antics.  A buffoon is a clown, jester, or fool.|The king employed a buffoon to entertain the court.|Synonyms: clown, jester, fool, comedian, harlequin|Antonyms: serious person, sage, scholar|Noun
buffoonery:/bəˈfuːnəri/| n.|滑稽可笑的举止，愚蠢的行为|Buffoonery refers to behavior that is ridiculous but amusing. It's often characterized by silly antics, jokes, and exaggerated gestures.|His buffoonery entertained the children, but annoyed the adults.|Synonyms: clowning, tomfoolery, silliness, antics, absurdity|Antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gravity, dignity|Noun
