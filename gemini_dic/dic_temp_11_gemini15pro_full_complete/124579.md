mournings:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋz/
n.
悲痛，哀悼，丧服
expressions of sorrow for someone's death
The queen wore black mournings for a year after her husband died. 女王在她丈夫去世后穿了一年的黑色丧服。
Synonyms: grief, sorrow, bereavement, lamentation
Antonyms: celebrations, rejoicings
