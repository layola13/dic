multipersonal:/ˌmʌltiˈpɜːrsənəl/ | adj. | 多人参与的；涉及多重人格的 | Relating to or involving several people or multiple personalities. |  This multiplayer game is designed for a multipersonal experience.  这款多人游戏旨在提供多人参与的体验。| Synonyms:  multiple, collective, shared | Antonyms:  individual, solitary, single | adjective

multipersonal:/ˌmʌltiˈpɜːrsənəl/ | adj. | 多重人格的| Relating to or characteristic of multiple personality disorder (now usually called dissociative identity disorder). | The therapist specialized in treating multipersonal disorders. 这位治疗师专门治疗多重人格障碍。 | Synonyms:  dissociative | Antonyms: unified, integrated | adjective
