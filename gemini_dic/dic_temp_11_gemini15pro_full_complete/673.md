abs:/ˈæbzəˌluːt/ | adj. | 绝对的; 完全的 |  Complete; total; not limited in any way.  | The party was an absolute disaster. 派对完全是一场灾难。 | synonyms: complete, total, utter, perfect, pure | antonyms: relative, partial, incomplete, imperfect | adjective

abs:/əbˈzɒlv/ | v. |  赦免; 宣布…无罪 | To declare someone free from guilt or responsibility. | The jury absolved the defendant of all charges. 陪审团宣布被告所有罪名不成立。 | synonyms: exonerate, acquit, pardon, forgive | antonyms: convict, condemn, blame | verb

Etymology: From Latin *absolvere* (to loosen from, acquit), from *ab-* (from, away from) + *solvere* (to loosen, release).
