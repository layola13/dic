fessed:/fest/ | adj. | 供认的，承认的 | Having confessed to something.  Derived from the verb "confess" with the suffix "-ed" indicating a past participle often used adjectivally. | He stood there, fessed up to the crime. 他站在那里，承认了罪行。 | The fessed criminal awaited sentencing.  已被定罪的罪犯等待判决。 | Synonyms: admitted, acknowledged, avowed | Antonyms: denied, concealed, unacknowledged | adjective
