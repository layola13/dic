guadalupe:/ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpeɪ/ | n. |瓜达卢佩|A female given name, also the name of various places, including a river in Texas, a mountain range in New Mexico, and a town in Spain.  Ultimately from Arabic وادي (wādī) meaning "valley, river" and Latin lupus meaning "wolf".| Guadalupe is the patron saint of Mexico. 瓜达卢佩是墨西哥的守护神。

The Guadalupe River flows through Texas. 瓜达卢佩河穿过德克萨斯州。|Synonym: (for the name) Lupe|Antonym: N/A|noun


guadaloop:/ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːp/ | n. |瓜达卢普|An island in the eastern Caribbean Sea, part of the French overseas region of Guadeloupe. Also sometimes used to refer to the entire French overseas region, which also includes the islands of Basse-Terre, Grande-Terre, Marie-Galante, Désirade, and Les Saintes. Its name comes from the Spanish "Guadalupe", ultimately from Arabic وادي (wādī) meaning "valley, river" and Latin lupus meaning "wolf".|We spent our honeymoon in Guadaloop. 我们在瓜达卢普度蜜月。

Guadeloupe is known for its beautiful beaches. 瓜达卢普以其美丽的海滩而闻名。|Synonym: (for the region) French West Indies|Antonym: N/A|noun
