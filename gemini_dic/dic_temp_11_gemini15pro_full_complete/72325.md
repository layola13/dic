flouts:/flaʊts/
| v. |蔑视，嘲笑，公然违抗|To show contempt for something, especially a rule or convention, by openly disobeying or defying it.|She flouts the school dress code by wearing ripped jeans. 她穿着破洞牛仔裤，无视学校的着装规定。|近义词：defy, scorn, disregard, disobey|反义词：obey, respect, comply, observe|词性：动词

flout:/flaʊt/
| n. |蔑视，嘲笑，公然违抗（行为）|An act of showing contempt for something, especially a rule or convention, by openly disobeying or defying it.|Her flout of the dress code resulted in detention. 她对服装规定的蔑视导致她被留校。|近义词：defiance, scorn, disregard, disobedience|反义词：obedience, respect, compliance, observance|词性：名词
