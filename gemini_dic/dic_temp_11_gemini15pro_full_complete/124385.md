motley:/ˈmɒtli/ | adj. | 杂色的，五颜六色的；混杂的，各式各样的 |  Exhibiting great diversity of elements; composed of many distinct parts. | The jester wore a motley tunic. 小丑穿着一件杂色的外衣。  | Synonyms: varied, diverse, heterogeneous, miscellaneous | Antonyms: uniform, homogeneous, similar | adjective
