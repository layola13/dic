cornily:/ˈkɔːrnɪli/| adv.|老土地；陈腐地；伤感地|In a corny or sentimental way.  Characterized by sentimentality that is excessive or insincere.|He cornily declared his undying love. 他老套地宣布了他永恒的爱。|近义词：sentimentally, mawkishly, cheesily|反义词：sophisticatedly, subtly|词性:副词
Etymology: From *corny* (1930s American slang, meaning "rustic, unsophisticated") + *-ly* (adverbial suffix). The origin of *corny* is uncertain, but it may be related to *corn* (the grain) in the sense of something common or commonplace.|
