mesodont:/ˈmɛsəˌdɒnt/ (US), /ˈmɛzəʊˌdɒnt/ (UK)| adj. n.|中齿兽的；中齿兽|Having teeth of moderate height, between brachydont (low-crowned) and hypsodont (high-crowned), typically with a moderate amount of crown exposed above the gum line.  Referring to or belonging to the extinct mammalian order Mesodonta.|The rhinoceros exhibits mesodont dentition.  犀牛展现出中齿兽的牙列。|近义词:medium-crowned|反义词:brachydont, hypsodont|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Greek *mesos* "middle" + *odous* (genitive *odontos*) "tooth".
