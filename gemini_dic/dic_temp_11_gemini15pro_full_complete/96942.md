inextricable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstrɪkəbl/ /ˌɪnɛkˈstrɪkəbl/
adjective
无法解脱的，纠缠不清的
Impossible to disentangle or separate.
例句：The two lovers found themselves in an inextricable embrace. 这两个恋人发现他们拥抱在一起无法分开。

例句：The country's economic problems are inextricably linked to its political instability.  这个国家的经济问题与其政治不稳定密不可分。

近义词：inseparable, tangled, intertwined
反义词：separable, extricable, detachable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inextricabilis, from in- 'not' + extricabilis (from extricare 'disentangle,' from ex- 'out' + tricae 'perplexities, hindrances').
