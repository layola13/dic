boletic:/boʊˈlɛtɪk/| adj.|牛肝菌的，有关牛肝菌的|Relating to or characteristic of boletes, a group of fleshy fungi.|The forest floor was covered with boletic mushrooms. 森林的地面上覆盖着牛肝菌。|近义词：fungal, mushroomy|反义词:|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *boleticus, from Latin boletus (“mushroom”), from Ancient Greek βωλίτης (bōlítēs, “a kind of mushroom”).
