derogation:/ˌdɛrəˈɡeɪʃən/ |n.| 减损， derogations, 偏离， 减损 |  a partial revocation or suspension of a law; lessening of power, status, or character. |  The derogation of human rights during wartime is a serious concern.  战时人权的减损是一个严重的问题。 | synonyms: lessening, weakening, diminishment, impairment, detraction, depreciation | antonyms:  enhancement, improvement, strengthening | noun

derogation:/ˌdɛrəˈɡeɪʃən/ |n.| 贬低， derogations,  |  the act of speaking about someone or something in a way that shows you do not have a good opinion of them or it. |  He regarded the comments as a derogation of his dignity.  他认为这些评论贬低了他的尊严。 | synonyms: disparagement, belittlement, denigration | antonyms: praise, commendation | noun
