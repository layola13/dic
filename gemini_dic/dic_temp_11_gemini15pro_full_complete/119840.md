mildew:/ˈmɪlduː/| n. |霉菌，霉|A thin whitish coating produced by fungi on organic matter, especially in humid conditions.| The old books were covered in mildew. 旧书上长满了霉菌。| 近义词:mold, fungus | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可用cleanliness表示干净的状态) | noun

mildew:/ˈmɪlduː/| v. |发霉，使发霉| To become or cause to become coated with mildew.| The damp air mildewed the clothes left outside. 潮湿的空气使留在外面的衣服发霉了。| 近义词:mold, rot | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | verb
