flamboyant:/flæmˈbɔɪənt/ | adj. |艳丽的，炫耀的， flamboyant |  Strikingly bold or brilliant; showy: "flamboyant colors"; "a flamboyant personality". |  The peacock strutted around, displaying its flamboyant plumage.  孔雀昂首阔步地走来走去，炫耀着它艳丽的羽毛。 | synonyms:  ostentatious, showy, flashy, extravagant, flamboyant  | antonyms: subdued, understated, modest, restrained | adjective

flamboyants:/flæmˈbɔɪənts/ | n. |凤凰木（复数）|  A type of tree known for its vibrant red flowers, scientifically known as Delonix regia.  | The flamboyants lining the street were in full bloom, creating a fiery spectacle.  街道两旁的凤凰木盛开着，构成一幅火红的景象。| synonyms: royal poinciana, flame tree | antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a specific tree) | noun
