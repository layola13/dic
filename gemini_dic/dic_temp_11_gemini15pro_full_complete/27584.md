canary:/kəˈnɛəri/ | n. |金丝雀；告密者；女歌手|A small, brightly colored finch native to the Canary Islands, often kept as a cage bird. Also, a person who informs on others, especially to the police. Can also refer to a female singer, particularly in a nightclub or musical. |例句：The canary in the cage sang beautifully.  笼子里的金丝雀唱得很好听。

例句：He was known as the canary for the police in the gang. 他在帮派里被称为警方的告密者。|近义词：informer, snitch, songbird (for singer)|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the meaning used) |词性: noun
