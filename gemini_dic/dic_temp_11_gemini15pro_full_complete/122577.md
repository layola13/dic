moderator:/ˈmɒdəreɪtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtər/ (US)| n.|主持人，仲裁人，调解人|A person who presides over a discussion or debate, ensuring that it is conducted fairly and orderly.  A person who monitors and regulates content or behavior in an online forum or community.|例句：The moderator skillfully guided the panel discussion. 主持人巧妙地引导了小组讨论。

例句：The online forum moderators deleted the offensive posts.  在线论坛版主删除了攻击性帖子。|近义词：chairman, chairperson, facilitator, adjudicator, arbiter, controller|反义词：participant, instigator|词性：名词

moderator:/ˈmɒdəreɪtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtər/ (US)| n.|版主|A person who monitors and regulates content or behavior in an online forum or community.|例句：The moderator banned the user for violating the forum rules. 版主因违反论坛规则而封禁了该用户。

例句：The moderator pinned the important announcement to the top of the discussion thread. 版主将重要公告置顶在讨论帖的顶部。|近义词：administrator, manager, supervisor|反义词：user, member|词性：名词
