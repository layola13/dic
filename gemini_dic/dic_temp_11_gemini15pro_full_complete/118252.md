merchandised: /ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzd/ | v. |推销，销售，商品化 | To promote or sell (goods) using advertising, product placement, and other marketing techniques. | The company successfully merchandised its new line of clothing.  该公司成功地推销了它的新服装系列。 | Synonyms: marketed, promoted, sold, retailed | Antonyms: withheld, suppressed | verb

merchandised: /ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzd/ | adj. | 商品化的 | Made into or treated as merchandise; commercialized. | The band's music became increasingly merchandised, appearing on everything from t-shirts to coffee mugs.  这支乐队的音乐越来越商业化，从T恤到咖啡杯，到处都是。 | Synonyms: commercialized, commodified | Antonyms: non-commercial | adjective
