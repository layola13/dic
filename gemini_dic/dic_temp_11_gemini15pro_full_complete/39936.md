constructability:/kənˌstrʌktəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可施工性，可建造性 | The ease with which something can be constructed, taking into account factors such as design, materials, and site conditions.  |  The architect considered the constructability of the design before finalizing the plans.  建筑师在最终确定计划之前考虑了设计的可施工性。 | synonyms: buildability, feasibility, practicality | antonyms: impracticability, unfeasibility | noun
