degradabilities:/dɪˌɡreɪdəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可降解性；降解能力|The plural form of degradability, referring to the capability of materials to be broken down by natural processes.|The researchers are studying the degradabilities of different types of plastic. 研究人员正在研究不同类型塑料的可降解性。|近义词：biodegradabilities, decomposabilities|反义词：non-degradabilities, resistances to degradation|词性: noun
