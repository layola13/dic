bewit:/bɪˈwɪt/| v.|迷惑，使着魔；使神魂颠倒|To charm or fascinate someone completely.|She was utterly bewitched by his smile. 她完全被他迷住了。|近义词：enchant, captivate, fascinate|反义词：repel, disgust|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *biwicchen*, from Old English *bewiccian* (“to enchant, bewitch”), from *be-* (“thoroughly”) + *wiccian* (“to practice witchcraft”), from *wicca* (“wizard, witch”).
