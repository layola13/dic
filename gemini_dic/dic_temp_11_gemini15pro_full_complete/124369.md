motivator:/ˈmoʊtɪveɪtər/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪveɪtər/ (UK)| n. |激励因素，动力，鼓舞人心的人或事物|Something that encourages a person to do something.|The main motivator for people to work hard is money.  人们努力工作的主要动力是金钱。|incentive, stimulus, inspiration, encouragement|deterrent, discouragement|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "motivate," which comes from the Latin word "motivus" meaning "moving," and ultimately from "movere" meaning "to move."
