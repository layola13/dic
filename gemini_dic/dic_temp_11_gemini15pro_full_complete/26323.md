buyouts:/ˈbaɪaʊts/| n.|收购；买断；控制股权收购|The company announced a management buyout.  这家公司宣布了一项管理层收购。|例句：The leveraged buyout left the company with significant debt.  杠杆收购使该公司背负了巨额债务。

例句：Many employees opted for the buyout package offered by the company. 许多员工选择了公司提供的买断方案。|近义词：acquisitions, takeovers|反义词:sale, divestiture|词性:名词

词源:  Derived from "buy" + "out".  "Buy" comes from Old English "bycgan," meaning "to acquire by payment." "Out" comes from Old English "ūt," meaning "outward, outside."
