PHRASE->
what constitutes (构成什么) What constitutes a healthy diet? 什么构成健康饮食？
be constituted of/by (由...组成) The team is constituted of experts from various fields.  该团队由来自各个领域的专家组成。
constitute a threat/danger/problem (构成威胁/危险/问题) His actions constitute a threat to public safety. 他的行为对公共安全构成威胁。
constitute a crime/offense (构成犯罪/违法行为)  Driving under the influence of alcohol constitutes a serious offense. 酒后驾驶构成严重违法行为。
constitute a breach of (构成违反)  His behavior constituted a breach of contract. 他的行为构成违反合同。
