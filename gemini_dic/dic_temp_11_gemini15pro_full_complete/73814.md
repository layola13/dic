formell:/fɔrˈmɛl/ | adj. | 正式的，礼仪的 |  done in accordance with rules of convention or etiquette; suitable for or constituting an official or important situation. | The invitation requested formal attire.  邀请函要求穿着正式服装。| formal, ceremonial, official | informal, casual | adjective

formell:/fɔrˈmɛl/ | adj. | 形式上的，表面的 | following or according with established form, custom, or rule. Often contrasted with actual or effective. |  The president held only formal power, the real decisions being made by her advisors.  总统只拥有形式上的权力，真正的决定是由她的顾问做出的。 | nominal, superficial, perfunctory | substantive, genuine | adjective
