glorious:/ˈɡlɔːriəs/ /ˈɡlɔːrɪəs/| adj.|辉煌的，光荣的，极好的|Having, worthy of, or bringing glory; magnificent.  Characterized by great beauty and splendor; delightful. |例句：It was a glorious victory. 这是一场辉煌的胜利。|例句：We had a glorious time on vacation. 我们假期玩得很开心。|近义词：magnificent, splendid, wonderful, excellent, delightful|反义词：inglorious, shameful, dismal, terrible|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Middle English *glorious*, from Old French *glorios* (modern French *glorieux*), from Latin *gloriōsus* (“full of glory, glorious, famous, vainglorious”), from *glōria* (“glory, fame, renown”) + *-ōsus* (suffix forming adjectives denoting abundance).
