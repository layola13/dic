fluvial:/ˈfluːviəl/| adj.|河流的，河成的|Of, relating to, or produced by rivers.|例句：Fluvial deposits are sediments transported and deposited by rivers. 河流沉积物是由河流搬运和沉积的沉积物。

例句：The fluvial geomorphology of the area is shaped by the meandering river. 该地区的河流地貌是由蜿蜒的河流塑造的。|近义词：riverine, alluvial|反义词:marine, oceanic|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fluvialis, from Latin fluvius (“river, stream”) + -alis (adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *plew- (“to flow, swim”).
