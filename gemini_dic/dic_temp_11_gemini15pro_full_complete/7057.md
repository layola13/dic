anemometer:/ˌænɪˈmɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌænɪˈmɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |风速计，风力计|An instrument for measuring the speed of the wind.|The anemometer on the roof indicated wind speeds of over 50 mph. 屋顶上的风速计显示风速超过每小时50英里。|Synonyms: wind gauge, wind meter|Antonyms: None|Noun
