future-proofing:/ˈfjuːtʃəˌpruːfɪŋ/| n.|未来防护，面向未来设计|The act of designing or developing something (e.g., a product, system, or process) in a way that anticipates future needs or changes and minimizes the risk of obsolescence.|Investing in renewable energy is a form of future-proofing your business against rising fossil fuel costs.  为应对不断上涨的化石燃料成本，投资可再生能源是对您的业务进行未来防护的一种形式。|synonyms: forward planning, anticipation, foresight|antonyms: short-sightedness, unpreparedness|noun

future-proof:/ˈfjuːtʃəˌpruːf/| adj. v.|面向未来的，防过时的；使面向未来|Designed or developed in a way that anticipates future needs or changes and minimizes the risk of obsolescence; to make something future-proof.|This new software is future-proof, so it should be compatible with upcoming operating systems.  这款新软件是面向未来的，因此它应该与即将推出的操作系统兼容。  They are future-proofing the building by installing the latest technology. 他们通过安装最新技术来确保建筑的未来适用性。|synonyms: adaptable, resilient, forward-looking|antonyms: obsolete, outdated, short-sighted|adjective, verb
