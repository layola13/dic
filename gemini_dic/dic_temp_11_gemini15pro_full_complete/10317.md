appealable:/əˈpiːləb(ə)l/
adjective
可上诉的
Subject to appeal; able to be appealed.
The judge's decision is appealable to a higher court. 法官的判决可以向更高级法院上诉。
The verdict was not appealable.  这项判决不可上诉。
近义词:reviewable, challengeable
反义词:final, conclusive
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Middle English *appelable, from Old French apelable, from apeler (“to appeal”) + -able.
