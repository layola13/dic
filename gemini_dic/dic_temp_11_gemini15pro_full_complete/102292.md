USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? 他以什么为生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? 他用这笔钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? 我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? 会议前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the interruption?  被打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
