gaslights: /ˈɡæslaɪts/ (US) /ˈɡæslaɪts/ (UK)| v. (third-person singular simple present gaslights, present participle gaslighting, simple past and past participle gaslighted) |心理操纵; 煤气灯操纵|To manipulate someone by psychological means into questioning their own sanity.|She gaslighted him into believing he was losing his mind. 她对他进行心理操纵，让他相信自己正在失去理智。|近义词: manipulate, brainwash, deceive|反义词: support, empower, validate|动词
