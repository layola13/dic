beestings: /ˈbiːstɪŋz/
noun
蜂刺；牛初乳
The first milk produced by a cow after giving birth, also known as colostrum.  Can also refer to the act of a bee stinging.
The calf eagerly drank the beestings. 小牛急切地喝着牛初乳。
The farmer collected the beestings to feed to the newborn calves. 农民收集牛初乳喂养新生的小牛。
近义词: colostrum (for cow's milk), stings (for bee stings)
反义词: None
