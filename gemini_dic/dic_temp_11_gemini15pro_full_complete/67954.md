facile:/ˈfæsaɪl/, /ˈfæsɪl/| adj.|容易的，流畅的，肤浅的| Appearing neat and comprehensive only by ignoring the true complexities of an issue; superficial. |Her facile solution to the problem ignored many important factors. 她对这个问题的肤浅的解决方案忽略了许多重要因素。| The pianist’s facile performance impressed the audience.  这位钢琴家流畅的演奏给观众留下了深刻的印象。|Synonyms: effortless, simple, easy, superficial, glib |Antonyms: difficult, laborious, arduous, profound, complex|adjective
