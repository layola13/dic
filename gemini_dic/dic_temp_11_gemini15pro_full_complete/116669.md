mattock:/ˈmætək/| n. |鹤嘴锄; 阔嘴锄|A mattock is a tool that is similar to an axe, but with a broad blade instead of a pointed one. It is used for digging and breaking up soil, especially clay. |The gardener used a mattock to break up the hard clay soil. 园丁用鹤嘴锄来破碎坚硬的粘土。

He used a mattock to dig out the roots of the tree. 他用鹤嘴锄挖出树根。|近义词：pickaxe, pick, adze|反义词: (No direct antonym for a tool. Perhaps "shovel" if contrasting digging vs. moving earth) |词性: noun
