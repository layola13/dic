antinihilism:/ˌæntiˈnaɪɪlɪzəm/| n.|反虚无主义|The philosophical doctrine opposing nihilism, which asserts that life is without objective meaning, purpose, or intrinsic value.  It often emphasizes the importance of finding or creating meaning and value in life.|His art is a powerful expression of antinihilism, celebrating the beauty and wonder of the natural world.|近义词:optimism,  life-affirmation|反义词:nihilism|词性:noun
