fastidiosity:/ˌfæstɪˈdɪəsɪti/ | n. |一丝不苟；挑剔；讲究； | Excessive attention to detail; fastidiousness. | Her fastidiosity was evident in the perfectly arranged flowers. 她的讲究体现在摆放得整整齐齐的花朵上。|  He approached the task with extreme fastidiosity, checking every detail meticulously. 他以极其一丝不苟的态度处理这项任务，仔细检查每一个细节。 | Synonyms: meticulousness, punctiliousness, scrupulousness, precision | Antonyms: carelessness, sloppiness, negligence | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fastidiositas*, from Latin *fastidiosus* ("disgusting, disdainful, squeamish"), from *fastus* ("arrogance, pride") + *-idiosus*, a suffix forming adjectives.
