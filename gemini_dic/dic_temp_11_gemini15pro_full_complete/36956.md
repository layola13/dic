colourists:/ˈkʌlərɪsts/| n.|调色师|A person who specializes in coloring, especially in film or photography.  They adjust the colors of a film or photograph to achieve a particular aesthetic effect.|The colourists worked tirelessly to give the film a vintage look. 调色师不知疲倦地工作，赋予了这部电影复古的视觉效果。

colourists:/ˈkʌlərɪsts/| n.|着色师|A person who adds color to something, such as a drawing or a piece of fabric.|The colourists carefully applied the dyes to the silk scarves. 着色师小心翼翼地将染料涂在丝巾上。
