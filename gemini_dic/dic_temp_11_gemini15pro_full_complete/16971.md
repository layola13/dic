bastardism:/ˈbæstərdɪzəm/
n.
私生子身份;非婚生子女状态;庶子身份
The state of being a bastard; illegitimate birth.
His bastardism prevented him from inheriting the title. 他的私生子身份使他无法继承爵位。

n.
劣质的仿制品;粗劣的模仿品
Something that is a poor imitation or a debased version of something else.
The film was a bastardism of the original novel. 这部电影是对原著拙劣的模仿。

近义词:illegitimacy, bastardy
反义词:legitimacy

词性: noun

词源: From bastard + -ism.  "Bastard" itself comes from Old French *bastard, meaning "pack saddle," implying a child conceived on a makeshift bed, hence illegitimate.
