occultness:/əˈkʌltnəs/ |n.|神秘，奥秘，隐秘|The occultness of the ancient rituals fascinated her. 古老仪式的奥秘让她着迷。|例句：The occultness of his motives made everyone suspicious.  他动机的神秘性让每个人都起了疑心。

The occultness of the group's practices added to their mystique.  该团体做法的神秘性增加了他们的神秘感。|近义词：mystery, secrecy, enigma, hiddenness|反义词：obviousness, clarity, openness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *occulte + -ness, from Latin occultus (“hidden, secret”), past participle of occulere (“to cover over, conceal”), from ob- (“over, against, toward”) + -celere, related to celare (“to hide”).
