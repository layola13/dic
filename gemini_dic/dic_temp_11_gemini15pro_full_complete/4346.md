akinetic: /ˌeɪkaɪˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌeɪkɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
运动不能的；运动迟缓的
Relating to or suffering from akinesia, the loss or impairment of the power of voluntary movement.
The patient exhibited akinetic symptoms, including muscle rigidity and slowness of movement.  病人表现出运动不能的症状，包括肌肉僵硬和运动迟缓。
Parkinson's disease can cause akinetic mutism, a state where the patient is unable to speak or move voluntarily. 帕金森病会导致运动不能性缄默症，患者无法自主说话或活动。

近义词: motionless, immobile, still, rigid
反义词: kinetic, mobile, active

词性: adjective
词源: From a- (not, without) + kinetic (of or relating to motion), from Greek kinētikos, from kinein (to move).
