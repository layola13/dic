neatness:/ˈniːtnəs/| n.|整洁，干净，利落|The neatness of her handwriting impressed me. 她的字迹工整给我留下了深刻的印象。|例句：She always keeps her house in a state of perfect neatness. 她总是把她的房子保持得非常整洁。

例句：He admired the neatness and order of the garden. 他欣赏花园的整洁有序。

近义词：tidiness, orderliness, cleanliness, precision|反义词：messiness, disorder, untidiness, sloppiness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *netnesse, from Old English *nēatnes (“neatness, elegance, refinement”), equivalent to neat +‎ -ness. Cognate with Scots neitnes (“neatness”).
