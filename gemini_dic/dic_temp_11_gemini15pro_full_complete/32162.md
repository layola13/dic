chews:/tʃuːz/
verb
咀嚼；咬
bites and grinds food with the teeth.
The cow chews its cud. 牛反刍。
近义词:munch, gnaw, masticate
反义词:swallow, gulp
词性:v.

词源:Old English cēowan "to chew," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch kauwen and German kauen.
