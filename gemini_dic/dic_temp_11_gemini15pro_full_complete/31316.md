chaprasi: /tʃəˈprɑːsi/ | n. | 茶房，勤杂工 | An office attendant or messenger, especially in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.  Derived from Persian "chahar" (four) and "pars" (piece, portion), possibly referring to a servant responsible for four tasks. |  The chaprasi brought us tea. 茶房给我们送来了茶。|  The chaprasi delivered the mail. 茶房送来了邮件。 | Synonyms: peon, orderly, messenger | Antonyms: executive, manager | noun
