macerate:/ˈmæsəreɪt/| v. |浸渍, 沤软, 消瘦|To soften or break down by soaking in liquid; to become soft or separated into parts by steeping in liquid.|The chef macerated the dried mushrooms in warm water before adding them to the stew.  厨师在将干蘑菇加入炖菜之前，先将其在温水中浸泡软化。

The berries were macerated in sugar and brandy.  这些浆果被浸泡在糖和白兰地中。|近义词：soak, steep, soften, ret|反义词:harden, dry|词性：动词

macerater:/ˈmæsəreɪtər/| n. |浸渍器, 沤软器|A device or container used for macerating.|The pharmacist used a macerater to prepare the herbal tincture. 药剂师用浸渍器来配制草药酊剂。

The lab uses a specialized macerater for tissue samples. 实验室使用专门的浸渍器来处理组织样本。|近义词：soaker|反义词:dryer|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *macerāre* "to make soft, soak, steep," related to *macer* "lean, thin."
