bacule: /ˈbækjul/ | n. | 小棒; 杆状体; |A small rod or staff; a rod-shaped body. |The diatoms have an outer layer of silica forming a bacule. 硅藻有一个形成杆状体的外层二氧化硅。|  The insect's leg is composed of several bacules.  这种昆虫的腿由几个杆状体组成。 |synonyms: rod, staff, stick, bar | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider "sphere" or "orb" for contrasting shapes) | noun
