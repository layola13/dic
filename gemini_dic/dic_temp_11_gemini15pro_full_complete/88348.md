hippopathology:/ˌhɪpoʊpəˈθɒlədʒi/ | n. |马病学；马的病理学|The study of diseases of horses.|例句：Hippopathology is a specialized branch of veterinary medicine. 马病学是兽医学的一个专门分支。

例句：The veterinarian specialized in hippopathology and treated many racehorses.  这位兽医专攻马病理学，治疗过许多赛马。|近义词：equine pathology|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
From hippo- (Greek: horse) + pathology (Greek: study of suffering).
