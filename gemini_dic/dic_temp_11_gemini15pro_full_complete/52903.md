dimmest:/ˈdɪməst/| adj.|最昏暗的|Superlative form of dim.  Meaning having the least amount of light.|The dimmest star in the sky was barely visible. 天空中最暗淡的星星几乎看不见。|近义词：faintest, darkest, weakest|反义词:brightest, strongest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *dimmest, superlative of dim, from Old English *dim, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse *dimma (“mist, darkness”).
