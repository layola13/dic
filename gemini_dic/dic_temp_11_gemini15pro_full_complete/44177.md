critique:/krɪˈtiːk/ /krɪˈtik/| n. vt.| 批评，评论；评论文章| A detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, artistic, or musical work. | The professor offered a critique of the student's essay. 教授对学生的论文作了评论。| synonyms: analysis, assessment, evaluation, review, commentary, appraisal | antonyms: praise, commendation | noun

critique:/krɪˈtiːk/ /krɪˈtik/| vt.| 批评，评论| To evaluate (a theory, practice, or performance) in a detailed and analytical way. |  She critiqued his performance in the play. 她评论了他在剧中的表演。| synonyms: analyze, assess, evaluate, review, appraise | antonyms: praise, commend | verb
