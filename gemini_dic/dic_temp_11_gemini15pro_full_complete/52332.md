dicrotic:/daɪˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 双脉冲的，重搏的 | Relating to or characterized by a double pulse or beat, especially in the arterial pulse. | The patient exhibited a dicrotic pulse, suggesting a particular cardiovascular condition. 病人表现出重搏脉，提示一种特殊的心血管疾病。| 近义词: bifid, double-beat | 反义词: monocrotic | 词性: 形容词
