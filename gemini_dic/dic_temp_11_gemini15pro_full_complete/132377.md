nondelivery:/ˌnɑːndɪˈlɪvəri/ /ˌnɒndɪˈlɪvəri/
n.
未送达；未交付
failure to deliver something that has been ordered or promised.
The customer complained about the nondelivery of his order. 顾客投诉他的订单未送达。
近义词：non-shipment, failure to deliver
反义词：delivery, shipment
词性：名词
