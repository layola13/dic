half-hessian:/ˌhɑːf ˈhɛsiən/ | adj. | 半麻布的；由黄麻和另一种纤维混合制成的 | Made of hessian combined with another fibre. | The sack was made of half-hessian, combining jute with cotton for increased durability. 这个麻袋是用半麻布做的，黄麻和棉花混合在一起，增加了耐用性。|  half-linen, blended | pure, unblended | adjective


Etymology->
"half-" + "hessian". "Half-" denotes a partial composition or mixture. "Hessian" refers to a coarse fabric made from jute, named after the German state of Hesse.
