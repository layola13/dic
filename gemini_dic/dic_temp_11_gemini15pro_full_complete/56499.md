domesticant:/dəˈmɛstɪkənt/
noun
家仆，佣人
A person employed to perform domestic duties, such as cleaning, cooking, or childcare.
例句：The wealthy family employed several domesticants to maintain their large estate.  富裕的家庭雇佣了几个佣人来维护他们的大庄园。

例句：She worked as a domesticant for many years before starting her own business.  她做了多年的佣人，然后才开始自己创业。
近义词：servant, maid, housekeeper, domestic
反义词: employer, master
