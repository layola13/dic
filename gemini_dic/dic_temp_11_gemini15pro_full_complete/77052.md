garner:/ˈɡɑːrnər/| v. |收集，积累，获得|to gather or collect something, especially information or approval|例句：The police are trying to garner information about the robbery. 警察正在努力收集有关抢劫案的信息。|例句：She garnered several awards for her performance. 她的表演获得了多个奖项。|近义词：gather, collect, amass, accumulate, obtain, acquire|反义词：scatter, disperse, lose|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *gernier (modern grenier “granary, attic”), from Latin granarium “granary”, from granum “grain”.
