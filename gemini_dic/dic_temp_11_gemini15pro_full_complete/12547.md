aspirant:/ˈæspərənt; əˈspɑɪrənt/| n. adj.|有抱负的人；雄心勃勃的；|A person who has ambitious hopes or desires, typically to achieve a particular position of importance or success.  A person who eagerly seeks some high position, honor, or achievement.|例句：He was an aspirant to the throne. 他是王位的觊觎者。|近义词：candidate, applicant, contender, seeker, hopeful, ambitious|反义词：incumbent, established, unambitious, apathetic|词性:名词

aspirant:/ˈæspərənt; əˈspɑɪrənt/| adj.|有抱负的；渴望的；|Having or showing a strong desire and determination to succeed.|例句：An aspirant writer.  一位胸怀抱负的作家。|近义词：ambitious, aspiring, hopeful, eager, desirous|反义词：unambitious, apathetic, indifferent, unconcerned|词性:形容词
