hyperemotiveness:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənəsnɪs/| n. |过度情绪化；情绪亢奋|The state of being excessively emotional; heightened emotional sensitivity.|Her hyperemotiveness made it difficult for her to cope with everyday stress. 她过度的情绪化使她难以应对日常压力。|近义词：hypersensitivity, emotionalism, excitability|反义词：apathy, indifference, calmness|词性:noun
Etymology:  From hyper- (meaning "excessive, above") + emotiveness (from emotion + -ive + -ness).
