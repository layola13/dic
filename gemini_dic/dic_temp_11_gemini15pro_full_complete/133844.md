| adv. | 不礼貌地，粗鲁地 | In a way that is not gracious; rudely or ungraciously. | He nongraciously shoved her out of the way.  他粗鲁地把她推开。|  She nongraciously accepted the gift without a word of thanks. 她毫不客气地收下了礼物，连句谢谢都没有。 | rudely, ungraciously, impolitely, discourteously | graciously, politely, courteously | adverb
