nanobots:/ˈnænoʊbɒts/ (US), /ˈnænəʊbɒts/ (UK)| n. |纳米机器人|Microscopic robots, typically on a nanometer scale (one billionth of a meter), designed to perform specific tasks at a molecular level.|Scientists are exploring the potential of nanobots to deliver drugs directly to cancer cells. 科学家们正在探索纳米机器人将药物直接输送到癌细胞的潜力。

|Near synonyms: nanorobots, nanomachines|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "macromachines" in a relative sense)|Noun
