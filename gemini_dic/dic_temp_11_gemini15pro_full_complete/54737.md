disparate:/ˈdɪspərət/| adj.|不同的，不相似的，迥异的|Fundamentally different; entirely unlike.|例句：The two groups held disparate views on the issue. 这两个团体对这个问题持有截然不同的观点。|例句：They came from disparate backgrounds but shared a common goal. 他们来自不同的背景，但有着共同的目标。|近义词：different, dissimilar, diverse, various, distinct|反义词：similar, alike, homogeneous, uniform|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *disparātus*, past participle of *disparāre* "to separate," from *dis-* "apart" + *parāre* "to prepare."
