aspiringly:/əˈspaɪərɪŋli/
| adv. | 渴望地，有抱负地 | In a manner that shows ambition or a strong desire to achieve something. | She looked aspiringly at the Olympic podium. 她渴望地望着奥运领奖台。|  He aspiringly reached for the highest shelf. 他满怀抱负地伸手去够最高的架子。 | 近义词: ambitiously, hopefully, eagerly | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "aspire" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Aspire" comes from Old French "aspirer"  (to breathe, aspire to), from Latin "aspirare" (to breathe upon, long for), from "ad-" (to) + "spirare" (to breathe).
