maternalistic:/məˈtɜːrnəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
母性的；母权主义的；大母神崇拜的
Relating to or characteristic of a mother, especially in a caring or protective way, sometimes excessively so; also relating to or advocating the system of mother-right.
She had a very maternalistic attitude towards her employees, always making sure they had everything they needed. 她对她的员工有一种非常母性的态度，总是确保他们拥有所需的一切。
The government's maternalistic policies aimed to support families with young children. 政府的母性政策旨在支持有幼儿的家庭。
近义词: motherly, protective, nurturing
反义词: neglectful, uncaring
词性: adjective
