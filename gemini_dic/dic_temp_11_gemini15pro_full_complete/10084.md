apocynaceous:/əˌpɒsɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
夹竹桃科的
Relating to or belonging to the Apocynaceae family of plants, which includes dogbane, oleander, and milkweed.  Characterized by milky sap, often poisonous, and typically having simple, opposite leaves.
The oleander is an apocynaceous shrub commonly found in warm climates. 夹竹桃是一种夹竹桃科的灌木，常见于温暖的气候。
The periwinkle is another example of an apocynaceous plant. 长春花是夹竹桃科植物的另一个例子。
Synonym:dogbane-like, oleander-like
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic adjective)
词性:adjective
