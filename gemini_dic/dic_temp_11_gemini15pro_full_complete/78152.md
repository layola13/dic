geoplanarian:/ˌdʒiːoʊpləˈnɛəriən/| n. |土居涡虫|A type of land planarian, a free-living flatworm found in terrestrial habitats.|Geoplanarians are important predators in some ecosystems. 土居涡虫在一些生态系统中是重要的捕食者。|近义词:land planarian, flatworm|反义词: None|Noun

词源:  "geo-" (Greek, earth) + "planarian" (from "planarius," Latin, flat). 指的是生活在陆地上的涡虫。
