electrostrictive:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstrɪktɪv/
adjective|电致伸缩的|Relating to or exhibiting electrostriction, the phenomenon in which a material changes shape in response to an applied electric field.|Electrostrictive materials are used in actuators and sensors.|近义词:piezoelectric (similar but different mechanism)|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps non-responsive to electric fields)
词性:adjective
