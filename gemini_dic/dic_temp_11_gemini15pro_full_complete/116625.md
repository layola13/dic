matron:/ˈmeɪtrən/ | n. | 女舍监，护士长，已婚妇女 | a woman who is in charge of the domestic staff of a hospital, school, or other institution; a married woman, usually one who is mature and dignified | The matron of the hospital was known for her strictness.  医院的女舍监以严格著称。| synonyms: superintendent, supervisor, director, housekeeper, married woman, wife | antonyms: bachelor (for married woman) | noun
