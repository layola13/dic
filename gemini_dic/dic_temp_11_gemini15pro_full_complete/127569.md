nectared:/ˈnɛktərd/| adj.|充满花蜜的，甜蜜的|Containing or sweetened with nectar; honeyed.  Figuratively, extremely sweet or delightful.|The nectared wine flowed freely at the feast.  宴会上的甜酒源源不断。|近义词：honeyed, sweet, sugary, luscious|反义词：bitter, sour|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From nectar + -ed.  "Nectar" comes from the Greek "νέκταρ (nektar)," the drink of the gods, often associated with immortality.
