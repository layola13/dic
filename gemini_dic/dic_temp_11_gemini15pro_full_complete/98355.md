insisted:/ɪnˈsɪstɪd/| v. |坚持，强调|past tense and past participle of *insist*, meaning to demand something forcefully, not accepting refusal, or to state something firmly and repeatedly.|He insisted that he was innocent. 他坚持说他是无辜的.|近义词：persisted, maintained, asserted, emphasized|反义词: yielded, conceded, acquiesced|词性:动词
