mineralize:/ˈmɪnərəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 矿化；使矿化；在…中沉淀矿物 | To convert into or deposit minerals; to supply with minerals. |  The bones of the dinosaur were completely mineralized. 恐龙的骨头已经完全矿化了。

| The farmer mineralized his fields to improve the soil. 农民给田地施加矿物质以改良土壤。 | 近义词: petrify, fossilize | 反义词: decompose, decay | 词性: 动词

mineralize:/ˈmɪnərəˌlaɪz/ | n. | 矿化；矿化作用 | The process of converting into or depositing minerals. | The mineralization of the wood took millions of years. 木材的矿化过程花了数百万年。

|  Scientists study mineralization to understand geological processes. 科学家研究矿化作用以了解地质过程。| 近义词: petrification, fossilization | 反义词: decomposition, decay | 词性: 名词


词源: From mineral + -ize.  From French *minéraliser, from minéral ("mineral").
