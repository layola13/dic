hydrochlorofluorocarbons:/ˌhaɪdrəˌklɔːrəˈflʊərəʊˌkɑːrbənz/ /ˌhaɪdroʊˌklɔːroʊˈflʊrəˌkɑːrbənz/ | n. | 氢氯氟烃 |  Any of a class of inert compounds of carbon, hydrogen, chlorine, and fluorine, used in refrigerants and aerosols. They are known to cause depletion of the atmospheric ozone layer.|  Hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFCs) were developed as replacements for chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs). 氢氯氟烃 (HCFC) 是作为氯氟烃 (CFC) 的替代品而开发的。|  The use of hydrochlorofluorocarbons has been phased out. 氢氯氟烃的使用已被逐步淘汰。| synonyms: HCFCs, freons (泛指) | antonyms:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 ozone-friendly refrigerants 臭氧友好型制冷剂) | noun
