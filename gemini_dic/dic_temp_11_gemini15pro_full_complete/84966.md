ha'porth:/ˈheɪpəθ/ (UK), /ˈhæpərθ/ (US) |n.|少量，少许，一点点；价值很少的东西|A ha'porth is a very small amount of something, especially something of little value.  It originally referred to something worth a halfpenny.|I don't care a ha'porth what they think. 我一点也不在乎他们怎么想。|Synonyms: little, bit, trifle, smidgen, jot, iota|Antonyms: lot, much, abundance|noun
