keynote:/ˈkiːnoʊt/ (US) /ˈkiːnəʊt/ (UK)| n. adj.|主旨，基调；主旨演讲；主题；|A keynote is the central idea or theme of something, especially a speech or a piece of writing. It can also refer to a speech that sets the main theme of a conference or meeting.  As an adjective, it means central or most important.|
例句：
The keynote of his speech was the importance of education.
他的演讲主旨是教育的重要性。

The CEO delivered a powerful keynote at the annual conference.
首席执行官在年会上发表了强有力的主旨演讲。

The keynote speaker inspired the audience with her vision.
主题演讲者以她的远见卓识激励了观众。

The keynote issue in the election was the economy.
选举中的核心问题是经济。

近义词：theme, essence, main idea, core, central, principal, primary|
反义词：minor, secondary, unimportant, peripheral|
词性：noun, adjective|
