overindustrialisms: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəmz/
noun
过度工业化
The excessive development of industry in a region or country, typically leading to negative consequences such as pollution and social disruption.
The city's overindustrialisms led to severe air pollution. 这个城市的过度工业化导致了严重的空气污染。
Overindustrialization has resulted in widespread environmental damage in some areas. 在某些地区，过度工业化导致了广泛的环境破坏。
近义词: overdevelopment, industrial excess
反义词: underdevelopment, sustainable development
词性: noun

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "industrialism" (noun from "industrial" + "-ism")
