inequitableness:/ɪnˈɛkwɪtəbəlnəs/
n.
不公平，不公正
lack of fairness or justice
The inequitableness of the legal system was a major cause of social unrest.  法律制度的不公正是社会动荡的主要原因。

The inequitableness of the distribution of resources led to widespread poverty. 资源分配的不公平导致普遍贫困。

近义词: unfairness, injustice, partiality, bias
反义词: fairness, justice, equity, impartiality
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From inequitable + -ness.  Inequitable from in- + equitable. Equitable from equity + -able. Equity from Old French equite, from Latin aequitas (“equality, fairness”), from aequus (“equal, level, fair, just”).
