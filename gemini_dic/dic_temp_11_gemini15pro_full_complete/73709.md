forgotten:/fərˈɡɒtn/ (UK), /fərˈɡɑːtn/ (US)| adj.|被遗忘的，忘记了的|Past participle of "forget".  Something that is forgotten is no longer remembered or thought about. |She found a forgotten letter in the drawer. 她从抽屉里找到了一封被遗忘的信。|Synonyms: unremembered, lost, neglected, overlooked|Antonyms: remembered, cherished, recalled|verb (past participle)

forgotten:/fərˈɡɒtn/ (UK), /fərˈɡɑːtn/ (US)| adj.|被遗忘的，忘记了的|Can also describe a person or group of people who are neglected or ignored.  |The forgotten heroes of the war. 被遗忘的战争英雄们。|Synonyms: abandoned, forsaken, deserted|Antonyms: celebrated, honored, acknowledged|adjective
