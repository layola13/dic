verb
在民意调查中胜过
defeats someone by receiving more votes in a poll.
Candidate A outpolls Candidate B by a wide margin in the latest survey.  候选人A在最新的民意调查中以较大优势胜过候选人B。
The incumbent mayor outpolled the challenger by a small margin.  现任市长以微弱优势在民意调查中胜过挑战者。
