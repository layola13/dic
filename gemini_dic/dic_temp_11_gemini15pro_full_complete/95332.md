incense-breathing:/ˈɪnsɛns briːðɪŋ/
adjective
焚香呼吸的；吸入香气的
The act of inhaling the smoke or scent of burning incense.  This can be part of a religious or spiritual practice, or simply for enjoyment of the fragrance.
The incense-breathing ritual filled the temple with a mystical aroma. 寺庙里焚香呼吸的仪式充满了神秘的香气。
Near synonyms:  aromatherapy, fumigation
No direct antonym, perhaps "exhaling" or "air-freshening" in a very loose sense.
word type: adjective
