hydrogenide:/ˈhaɪdrədʒəˌnaɪd/
noun
氢化物
A binary compound of hydrogen with another element, especially a more electropositive element.
Sodium hydrogenide (NaH) is a strong base. 氢化钠是一种强碱。
Lithium aluminum hydride is a powerful reducing agent in organic chemistry. 氢化铝锂是有机化学中一种强还原剂。
synonyms: hydride
antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a chemical compound)
