normalizing:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 标准化的，正常化的 |  Relating to the process of normalizing. | The normalizing effect of routine can be beneficial. 常规的正常化作用可能是有益的。 | standardizing, regularizing | abnormal, irregular | adjective
