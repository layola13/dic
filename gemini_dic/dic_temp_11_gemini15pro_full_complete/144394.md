outstrike: /ˈaʊtˌstraɪk/
n.
罢工; 停工
A strike by workers.  Specifically, a strike exceeding in extent or effectiveness a concurrent strike.

The miners’ outstrike lasted for months, crippling the industry. 矿工的罢工持续了数月，使该行业陷入瘫痪。


The autoworkers' outstrike crippled production nationwide. 汽车工人的大罢工使全国的生产陷于瘫痪。


近义词: strike, walkout, work stoppage
反义词: work, operation


词性: noun
