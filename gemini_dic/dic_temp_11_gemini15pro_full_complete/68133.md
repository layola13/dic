fahlunite:/ˈfɑːlˌjuːˌnaɪt/ | n. |黝帘石|A mineral of the chlorite group, a hydrated silicate of aluminum, iron, and magnesium, usually occurring in hexagonal prisms.  It is a variety of clinochlore containing iron. |Fahlunite is often found in metamorphic rocks. 黝帘石常发现于变质岩中。| Synonyms: chlorite, clinochlore | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
