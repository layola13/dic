informedly:/ɪnˈfɔːrmɪdli/ | adv. | 知情地，见多识广地 |  In a way that shows knowledge or understanding; knowingly. | She spoke informedly about the current political situation. 她对当前的政治局势侃侃而谈。| 近义词: knowledgeably, wisely, knowingly | 反义词: ignorantly, foolishly, blindly | 词性: 副词

词源: informed (知情的) + -ly (副词后缀)  "Informed" comes from the Old French "enformer" meaning "to train, instruct, teach."
