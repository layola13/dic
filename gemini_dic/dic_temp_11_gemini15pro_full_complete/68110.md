faffle:/ˈfæfəl/| v.| 笨手笨脚地做；瞎忙；小题大做；浪费时间| to act or work clumsily or inefficiently; to fuss or potter about; to waste time on trivial matters | He faffled around the kitchen, trying to find the coffee filter. 他在厨房里瞎忙活，想找到咖啡滤纸。| 近义词：fumble, fiddle, potter, dawdle | 反义词: focus, concentrate | 词性: 动词

faffle:/ˈfæfəl/| n.| 忙乱；小题大做；浪费时间| a state of clumsy or inefficient activity; a fuss; a waste of time |  There was a lot of faffle before the meeting actually started. 会议真正开始前有很多忙乱。| 近义词：fuss, commotion, palaver | 反义词: efficiency, organization | 词性: 名词
