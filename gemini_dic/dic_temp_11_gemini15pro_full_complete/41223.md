corallian: /kɒˈræliən/ (UK) /kɔːˈreɪliən/ (US)| adj. n.|珊瑚的；珊瑚礁的；科拉利期（侏罗纪晚期的一个时期）|Relating to or denoting the Corallian, the upper stage of the Jurassic period, between the Oxfordian and Kimmeridgian stages, or the system of rocks deposited during it. It can also mean consisting of or resembling coral.|

例句：
The Corallian rocks are rich in fossils.
科拉利时期的岩石富含化石。

The corallian limestone formed a natural barrier.
珊瑚质的石灰岩形成了天然屏障。

近义词：coral, Jurassic|
反义词: (无明确反义词)|
词性: adjective, noun
