colony:/ˈkɒləni/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləni/ (US)| n.|殖民地，群体，聚居地|A country or area under the full or partial political control of another country and occupied by settlers from that country.  A group of people of one nationality or race living in a foreign place. A group of animals or plants of the same type living close together.|
The British Empire had many colonies around the world.  大英帝国在世界各地拥有许多殖民地。

Many Portuguese settled in Brazil during the colonial period.  许多葡萄牙人在殖民时期定居巴西。

There's a colony of ants in my garden.  我的花园里有一个蚂蚁群。|
近义词：settlement, outpost, community, group|
反义词：homeland, motherland|
词性: noun
