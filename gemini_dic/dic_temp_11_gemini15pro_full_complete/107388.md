lamest:/ˈleɪməst/| adj.|最差劲的，最蹩脚的|Superlative form of lame: most lame.  Weak, unconvincing, or disappointing, especially of an excuse or argument.|That was the lamest excuse I've ever heard. 那是我听过的最蹩脚的借口。|Synonyms:  weakest, poorest, feeblest, most pathetic|Antonyms: best, strongest, most convincing, most effective|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From lame + -est (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *lama, from Proto-Germanic *lama- (meaning "crippled, paralyzed"), related to Old Norse lami ("crippled").
