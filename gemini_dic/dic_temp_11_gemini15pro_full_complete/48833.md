defoliated:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
落叶的；被摘掉叶子的
stripped of leaves
The defoliated trees looked barren and lifeless. 光秃秃的树木看起来荒凉而毫无生气。
近义词:bare, leafless, denuded
反义词:leafy, verdant
