condom:/ˈkɒndɒm/ (UK), /ˈkɑːndəm/ (US)| n. |避孕套; 保险套|A thin rubber sheath worn on the penis during sexual intercourse to prevent conception or the transmission of sexually transmitted diseases.|Example 1:  Always use a condom to protect yourself against STDs.  总是使用避孕套来保护自己免受性传播疾病的侵害。

Example 2:  Condoms are readily available at most pharmacies.  大多数药店都可以买到避孕套。|近义词：prophylactic, sheath, rubber|反义词: None (This word has no direct opposite)|词性: noun
