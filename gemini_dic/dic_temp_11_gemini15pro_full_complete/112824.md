lucidnesses:/ˈluːsɪdnəsɪz/
noun
清醒，明晰
The state of being lucid; clarity of thought or expression.
His occasional lucidnesses gave them hope. 他偶尔的清醒给了他们希望。
lucidity, clarity, transparency
confusion, obscurity, vagueness
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin luciditas, from Latin lucidus (lucid), from lucēre (to shine).
