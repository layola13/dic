altarless:/ˈɔːltərlɪs/| adj.|无祭坛的，没有祭坛的|Lacking an altar; without an altar.  This term describes a space or place of worship that does not have a traditional altar.|例句：The altarless chapel felt modern and minimalist.  无祭坛的小教堂感觉现代而简约。
