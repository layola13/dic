conciliable:/kənˈsɪliəbl/
adjective
可调和的，可安抚的
capable of being conciliated; placable.
例句：The dispute proved to be conciliable, and both parties reached an agreement. 争议最终得以调和，双方达成了协议。

例句：His conciliable nature made him a successful mediator. 他和蔼可亲的性格使他成为一名成功的调解员。

近义词：appeasable, pacifiable, reconcilable, amenable
反义词：intransigent, unyielding, implacable

词性: adjective
