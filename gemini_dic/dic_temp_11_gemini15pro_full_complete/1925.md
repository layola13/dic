actinolite:/ækˈtɪnəˌlaɪt/| n. |阳起石|A bright green or greyish-green amphibole mineral occurring in metamorphic rocks, especially schists and gneisses. It is a variety of tremolite containing iron. |例句：Actinolite is a common mineral found in metamorphic rocks. 阳起石是一种常见的变质岩矿物。

例句：The rock sample contained crystals of actinolite.  岩石样本中含有阳起石晶体。|近义词：tremolite, asbestos (some varieties) |反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *actinote*, from Ancient Greek ἀκτίς (aktís, “ray”) + λίθος (líthos, “stone”), referring to its fibrous or radiating habit.
