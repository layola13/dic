blessingly:/ˈblɛsɪŋli/| adv.|幸运地，幸福地，谢天谢地|Something that happens blessingly is fortunate and makes you feel happy and grateful. |It was, blessingly, a cool evening.  谢天谢地，那是一个凉爽的夜晚。| Thankfully, fortunately, luckily | unfortunately, unluckily | adverb

blessing:/ˈblɛsɪŋ/| n.|祝福，好事，幸事|A blessing is something that is good or helpful.  It can also refer to a prayer asking for God's favour and protection. |The priest gave the couple his blessing. 牧师为这对新人送上了祝福。| boon, gift, advantage | curse, misfortune | noun
