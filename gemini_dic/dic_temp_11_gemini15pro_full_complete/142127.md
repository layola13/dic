originable:/əˈrɪdʒɪnəbl/
adjective
可起源的；可發源的
That which can originate or be originated; capable of originating.
例句：Is such a complex system originable from random processes?  这样一个复杂的系统有可能起源于随机过程吗？

例句：The report traced the disease as originable from contaminated water.  报告指出这种疾病起源于受污染的水。
近义词：derivable, traceable, attributable
反义词：unoriginable, untraceable
词性: adjective

词源:  From origin + -able.  来自 "origin"（起源）+ "-able"（可...的）后缀.
