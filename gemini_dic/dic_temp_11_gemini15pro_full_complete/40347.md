contractible:/kənˈtræktəbl/ | adj. |可收缩的，可缩小的，可订立契约的 |Able to be made smaller or shorter by being contracted; able to be reduced in size or pulled together.| The muscle is contractible, allowing for movement. 这块肌肉是可收缩的，可以运动。 | The balloon is contractible.  这个气球是可以收缩的。 | synonyms: shrinkable, reducible, compressible | antonyms: expandable, extensible, non-contractible | adjective

contractible:/kənˈtræktəbl/ | n. | 可收缩的东西 | Something that can be contracted. |  A contractible is often used in topology. 可收缩的物体常用于拓扑学。 | This material is a contractible. 这种材料是可收缩的。| synonyms: retractable | antonyms: non-contractible | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contractibilis, from Latin contractus, past participle of contrahere "to draw together, conclude," from com- "together" + trahere "to draw."
