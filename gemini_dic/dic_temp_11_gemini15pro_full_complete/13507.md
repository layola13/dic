attagen:/ˈætədʒən/ | n. | 黑琴鸡 | A large grouse, *Tetrao tetrix*, found in northern Europe and Asia.  It is known for its elaborate courtship displays. | The male attagen performs a lekking display to attract females. 雄性黑琴鸡进行求偶炫耀以吸引雌性。|  Black grouse, Heathcock (male), Greyhen (female) |  Capercaillie | noun
Etymology: From Swedish *orre*, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *araz*.
