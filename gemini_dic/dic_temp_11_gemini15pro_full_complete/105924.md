Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她去婚礼穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before changing her major? =  她在换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the end of the century? = 到本世纪末会发生什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
