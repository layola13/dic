emergencies:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒənsiːz/
n.|紧急情况，突发事件|A serious, unexpected, and often dangerous situation requiring immediate action.|Many hospitals are overwhelmed by the number of emergencies. 许多医院都被大量的紧急情况压垮了。|The pilot made an emergency landing. 飞行员进行了紧急迫降。|近义词：crises, exigencies, calamities|反义词:normality, calmness|词性:名词(复数)


词源:
From Old French *emergence, from Late Latin emergentia "emergence, a rising up," noun of action from past-participle stem of emergere "to rise out, come forth, bring to light," from ex "out" (see ex-) + mergere "to dip, plunge, immerse" (from PIE root *merg- "to dip, plunge").
