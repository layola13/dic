avicular:/ˌævɪˈkjʊlər/| adj.|鸟形的，鸟状的；像小鸟的|Resembling a small bird, especially in shape or form.  Relating to or characteristic of small birds.  (Often used in biological contexts to describe structures or features.)|The plant's avicular appendages looked like tiny bird beaks.  这种植物的鸟状附属物看起来像微小的鸟喙。

The avicular zooid of a bryozoan is specialized for defense.  苔藓虫的鸟头状个虫专门用于防御。|近义词：birdlike,  avian|反义词:|adjective
