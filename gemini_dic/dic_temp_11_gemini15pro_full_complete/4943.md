alisphenoid: /ˌælɪˈsfɪnɔɪd/
n.|蝶骨翼状突; 蝶翼骨|A wing-like process of the sphenoid bone. Specifically, refers to either of two bones forming part of the sphenoid bone in many vertebrates.|The alisphenoid forms a significant part of the vertebrate skull. |近义词: pterygoid|反义词: (None - anatomical term)|词性: noun
