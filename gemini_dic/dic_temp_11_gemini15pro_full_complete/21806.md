bobsleighs:/ˈbɒbsleɪz/ (US) /ˈbɑːbsleɪz/| n.|雪橇，长橇，大雪橇|A bobsleigh is a large sled used for racing down a special ice track. It typically carries two or four people.|Example 1:  The Jamaican bobsleigh team became famous after competing in the Winter Olympics.  牙买加雪橇队在参加冬奥会后声名鹊起。

Example 2:  The bobsleighs hurtled down the icy track at incredible speeds.  雪橇以惊人的速度沿着冰道飞驰而下。|Synonym: sled, luge|Antonym: N/A|Noun
