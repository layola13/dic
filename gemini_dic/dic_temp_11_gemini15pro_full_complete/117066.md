me-too:/miː tuː/
| adj. n.|我也是；#我也是运动|A social movement against sexual harassment and sexual assault where people publicize allegations of sex crimes. Also used as an adjective to describe such allegations. |Many women came forward with their me-too stories.|Synonyms: me too movement|Antonyms: N/A|adjective, noun

me-too:/miː tuː/
| interj.|我也是|Used to express agreement or identification with a previous speaker's statement, especially one expressing a similar experience or feeling. |"I'm tired." "Me too." “我累了。” “我也是。”|Synonyms: same here, so am I|Antonyms: N/A|interjection
