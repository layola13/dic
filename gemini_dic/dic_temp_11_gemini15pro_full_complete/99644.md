interlayered:/ˌɪntərˈleɪərd/
adjective
交错层的，互层的
Consisting of alternating layers of different materials.
The rock formation was interlayered with shale and sandstone.  这岩层由页岩和砂岩交错而成。
The cake was interlayered with cream and jam.  蛋糕中夹着奶油和果酱。
Synonyms: laminated, stratified, layered
Antonyms: homogeneous, uniform
词性: adjective
