delinquencies: /dɪˈlɪŋkwənsiz/
| n. | 违规行为，拖欠，失职 | An act of failing to do what is required; the state of being delinquent. |  The report highlighted the rising delinquencies in loan repayments. 该报告强调了贷款偿还拖欠的增加。 | 近义词: offenses, violations, defaults, lapses | 反义词: compliances, payments, observances | 词性: 名词 (plural)
delinquency: /dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi/
| n. | 违规行为，拖欠，失职，青少年犯罪 | An act of failing to do what is required; the state of being delinquent; juvenile crime. |  His delinquency in paying his rent led to his eviction. 他拖欠房租导致他被驱逐。| 近义词: offense, violation, default, lapse, misbehavior | 反义词: compliance, payment, observance, obedience | 词性: 名词 (singular)


词源: Late 16th century: from French délinquance, from late Latin delinquentia ‘offense’, from delinquere ‘fail in one's duty’.
