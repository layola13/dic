multicapsular:/ˌmʌltiˈkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
多囊的，多蒴的
Having many capsules or compartments.  A multicapsular fruit is composed of numerous carpels, each forming a capsule.
The botanist examined the multicapsular structure of the plant.  植物学家检查了这种植物的多囊结构。
The poppy is a multicapsular plant. 罂粟是多蒴植物。
