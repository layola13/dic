dividant: /ˈdɪvɪdənd/
noun
股息；红利；回报；好处
A sum of money paid regularly (typically annually) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits (or reserves).  A benefit, reward, or advantage that is gained or obtained.
例句：The company declared a dividend of $2 per share. 公司宣布每股派息2美元。

例句：Education pays rich dividends. 教育带来丰厚的回报。

synonym:  payment, share, return, reward, gain, profit
antonym: debt, loss, penalty
