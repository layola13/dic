劝告的；告诫的；警告的

例句：The teacher's monitive words helped the student avoid making the same mistake again. 老师的劝诫帮助学生避免再次犯同样的错误。

例句：The flashing lights on the dashboard served as a monitive signal that the car needed maintenance. 仪表盘上闪烁的灯光是一个警告信号，表明汽车需要保养。

近义词：admonitory, cautionary, warning
反义词：encouraging, laudatory
词性：adjective
