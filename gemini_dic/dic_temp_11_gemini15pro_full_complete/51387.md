determinatenesses:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪtnəsɪz/
|n.|确定性；决断；坚定|The state or quality of being determinate; resoluteness.|His determinateness to finish the project was admirable. 他完成项目的决心令人钦佩。|近义词：resoluteness, firmness, decisiveness|反义词：indecision, vacillation|词性:名词

Etymology: From determinate + -ness.  Determinate traces back to Late Latin *dēterminātus, past participle of dētermināre "to limit, determine," from Latin dē- "completely" + termināre "to bound, limit."
