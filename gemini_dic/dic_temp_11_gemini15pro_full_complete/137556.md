nontransitional:/ˌnɒntrænˈzɪʃənl/
| adj. |非过渡性的，非转变的|Not involving or relating to a transition or change from one state or form to another.|The evolution of this species appears nontransitional, lacking clear intermediate forms in the fossil record.  这个物种的进化似乎是非过渡性的，化石记录中缺乏明显的中间形态。|例句：The architect chose a nontransitional design, opting for a starkly modern aesthetic.  这位建筑师选择了一种非过渡性的设计，选择了鲜明的现代美学。|近义词：unchanging, static, stable, abrupt|反义词：transitional, evolving, changing|词性:形容词
