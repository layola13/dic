orogastric: /ˌɔːroʊˈɡæstrɪk/ /ˌɒrəʊˈɡæstrɪk/
adjective
经口胃的；口胃的
Relating to or involving passage through the mouth and stomach.
The patient received orogastric feeding due to difficulty swallowing.  病人由于吞咽困难，接受了口胃管喂食。
The doctor inserted an orogastric tube. 医生插入了一根口胃管。
近义词:  oral-gastric
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially parenteral)
词性: adjective
