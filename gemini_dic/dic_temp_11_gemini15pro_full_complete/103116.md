jerky:/ˈdʒɜːrki/ (US) /ˈdʒɜːki/ (UK)| n. |牛肉干；（尤指人或动物）抽搐的，急促而不连贯的|Dried strips of meat, often beef, that have been cured and seasoned.|例句：He snacked on some beef jerky. 他吃了一些牛肉干。|例句：The car made jerky movements as it pulled away. 汽车启动时颠簸了一下。|近义词：jerked (meat), dried meat, spasms (for movements)|反义词:smooth (for movements)|词性:名词

jerkies:/ˈdʒɜːrkiz/ (US) /ˈdʒɜːrkiz/ (UK)| n. |牛肉干（jerky的复数形式）；（尤指人或动物）抽搐，急促而不连贯的动作（jerk的复数形式）|Plural form of jerky, referring to multiple pieces of dried meat or multiple jerky movements.|例句：He bought several bags of jerkies for the road trip. 他为公路旅行买了几个牛肉干。|例句：The dancer's jerkies added a unique element to the performance. 舞者的抽搐动作给表演增添了独特的元素。|近义词：jerked meats, dried meats, spasms|反义词:smooth movements|词性:名词
