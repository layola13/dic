anenterous: /ænˈɛntərəs/ | adj. | 无肠的，没有肠子的 | Lacking an intestine, or having a rudimentary intestine.  |  Some parasitic organisms are anenterous, absorbing nutrients directly through their body walls. 一些寄生生物是无肠的，直接通过体壁吸收营养。 |  Example 2: The anenterous flatworm relies on diffusion for nutrient uptake.  这种无肠的扁虫依靠扩散来吸收营养。|  Synonyms: gutless, intestine-less | Antonyms:  intestinal, enteric | adjective
