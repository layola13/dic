ear-piercing:/ˈɪər ˌpɪərsɪŋ/| adj.| 尖锐的，刺耳的；震耳欲聋的|  (of a sound) loud and high-pitched enough to hurt the ears. | The music was ear-piercing, and I had to leave the club.  音乐震耳欲聋，我不得不离开俱乐部。 | 近义词：shrill, piercing, deafening, earsplitting | 反义词: soft, quiet, muted | 词性: 形容词
