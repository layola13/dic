equinities:/ɪˈkwɪnɪtiz/
n.
平等；均等；公平
The state or quality of being equal or equitable.
例句：He argued for greater equinities between men and women. 他主张男女之间更大的平等。

例句：The company is committed to equinities in hiring and promotion. 该公司致力于招聘和晋升方面的平等。

近义词：equality, fairness, justice, parity, impartiality
反义词：inequality, disparity, injustice, bias, discrimination
词性：名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French equite, from Latin aequitas ‘evenness, fairness’, from aequus ‘even, level, equal’.
