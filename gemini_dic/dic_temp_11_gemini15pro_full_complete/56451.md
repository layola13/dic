dolomize:/ˈdɒləmaɪz/ /ˈdoʊləmaɪz/| v. |白云石化|To convert (limestone) into dolomite by replacement of part of the calcium by magnesium.|The limestone was dolomitized over millions of years. |近义词:metasomatize|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用decompose分解)|词性:verb
dolomite:/ˈdɒləmaɪt/ /ˈdoʊləmaɪt/| n. |白云石，白云岩|A carbonate mineral having the chemical composition CaMg(CO3)2. Also, a sedimentary rock composed primarily of the mineral dolomite.|The cliffs are made of dolomite. 这悬崖是由白云岩构成的。|近义词:dolostone|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用limestone石灰石)|词性:noun
