falls:/fɔːlz/| n. |瀑布; 秋天; 落下; 下降|A steep descent of the water of a river.|Niagara Falls is a famous tourist attraction. 尼亚加拉大瀑布是一个著名的旅游景点。|近义词：cascade, cataract, autumn, decline|反义词:rise, ascent|名词

falls:/fɔːlz/| v. |落下; 跌倒; 降低; 属于; 变成|To descend freely by the force of gravity.|The rain falls from the sky. 雨从天上落下。|近义词：drop, descend, decline, tumble|反义词:rise, ascend|动词
