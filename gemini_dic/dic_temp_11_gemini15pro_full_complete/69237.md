feasibleness:/ˈfiːzəbəlnəs/
n.
可行性
the state or degree of being easily or conveniently done
The feasibility of the project is still under discussion.  这个项目的可行性仍在讨论中。

近义词:
practicability, viability, possibility

反义词:
infeasibility, impracticality, impossibility

词性:
noun

词源:
From feasible + -ness. Feasible comes from the Old French faisable "doable," from faire "to do," from Latin facere "to make, do."
