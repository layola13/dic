dasyurine:/ˌdæsiˈjʊəraɪn/ | n. | 袋鼠尿 | The nitrogenous constituents of the urine of the wombat (or other marsupial) when the animal is fed on a flesh diet.  (Specifically relating to the high concentration of urea.) |  Dasyurine was studied to understand the unique metabolic processes of marsupials. 袋鼠尿被研究以了解有袋动物独特的代谢过程. | 近义词: urea (in context) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *dasy- "hairy, thick" + urine.  Refers to the thick, concentrated urine produced by wombats (and other marsupials) when on a meat diet.
