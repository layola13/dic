jezebel:/ˈdʒɛzəbəl/| n.| 耶洗别；放荡的女人；大胆的女人|A shameless, immoral, or manipulative woman.  The name comes from the biblical figure Jezebel, the wife of King Ahab of Israel, who was known for her wickedness and idolatry. |例句：She was labeled a jezebel for her flamboyant lifestyle and numerous affairs. 她因其华丽的生活方式和众多的婚外情而被称为荡妇。

例句：Her manipulative tactics earned her the reputation of a jezebel.  她耍弄权术的手段使她赢得了荡妇的恶名。|近义词：hussy, harlot, vamp, temptress|反义词：saint, madonna|词性: 名词
