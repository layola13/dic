eventuate:/ɪˈvɛntʃueɪt/
verb
最终发生；结果是
To occur as a result; to turn out in a specified way.
His efforts eventually eventuated in success. 他的努力最终取得了成功。
synonyms: result, culminate, end, lead to, conclude
antonyms: begin, commence, start, originate, initiate
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin ēventuātus, past participle of ēventuāre (“to come out, happen, result”), from ēventus (“outcome, event”) + -ātus (past participle suffix).
