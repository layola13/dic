allochroic:/ˌæləˈkroʊɪk/
adjective
变色的，多彩的
Exhibiting allochroism; having a different color by transmitted light than by reflected light.  A mineral is allochroic if it changes color when viewed in different directions under polarized light.
Alexandrite is a famous example of an allochroic gemstone.  亚历山大石是著名的变色宝石的例子。
Synonyms: pleochroic, dichroic
Antonyms: monochromatic, homochromatic
词性: adjective
