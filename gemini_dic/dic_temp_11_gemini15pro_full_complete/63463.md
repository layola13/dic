entrepreneurship: /ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːʃɪp/ (US) /ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːʃɪp/ (UK)| n.|创业精神；企业家精神|The process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business.  The capacity and willingness to develop, organize and manage a business venture along with any of its risks in order to make a profit.| Elon Musk's entrepreneurship is evident in his founding of companies like Tesla and SpaceX. 伊隆·马斯克的创业精神体现在他创立了特斯拉和SpaceX等公司。|synonyms: enterprise, initiative, innovation| antonyms:  inaction, stagnation| noun
