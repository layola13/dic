antipathogens:/ˌæntiˈpæθədʒənz/ | n. | 抗病原体 | Substances or agents that inhibit or destroy pathogens (disease-causing organisms). |  Example: Researchers are developing new antipathogens to combat drug-resistant bacteria. 研究人员正在开发新的抗病原体来对抗耐药细菌。

Example:  Natural antipathogens, like certain herbs, have been used for centuries in traditional medicine.  像某些草药这样的天然抗病原体，几个世纪以来一直被用于传统医学。| 近义词: antimicrobials, anti-infectives | 反义词: pathogens | 词性: 名词
