fittingly:/ˈfɪtɪŋli/| adv.|适当地，恰当地|In a way that is suitable or appropriate to the particular circumstances.|She was fittingly dressed for the occasion. 她穿着得体，适合这个场合。|appropriately, suitably, properly|inappropriately, unsuitably, improperly|adverb
Etymology->
From fitting + -ly.  Fitting itself comes from the Old English "fitt" (a division of a song or poem), which evolved to mean "suitable" or "appropriate."  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending derived from Old English "-lice."
