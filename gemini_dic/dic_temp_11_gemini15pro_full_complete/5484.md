alongside-of:/əˌlɔːŋˈsaɪd əv/
preposition
在...旁边, 与...一起
close beside; side by side with.  Considered redundant by many usage guides, who recommend simply using "alongside."
The new ship was moored alongside-of the old one. 新船停泊在旧船旁边。
近义词:alongside, beside, next to
反义词:across from, opposite
