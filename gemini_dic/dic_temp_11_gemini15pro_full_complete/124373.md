motived: /ˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有动机的，有积极性的 | Driven by a motive or motives; inspired or determined to do something. | She is a highly motivated student. 她是一个非常有动机的学生。|  He was motivated by a desire to help others. 他帮助别人的愿望激励着他。 |近义词：driven, ambitious, inspired, determined | 反义词：unmotivated, apathetic, lethargic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "motivate," which comes from the Latin word "motivus," meaning "moving."
