octaemeron:/ˌɑktəˈmɪrən/ | n. | 八日集; 八日谈 | A literary work consisting of eight parts, typically covering eight days.  It is modeled after Boccaccio's Decameron. |  The author penned a fascinating octaemeron, each day unveiling a new set of interconnected stories.  作者写了一部引人入胜的八日集，每天都揭开一系列相互关联的故事。 | Example:  Inspired by the Decameron, she decided to write her own octaemeron set in a futuristic world. 受十日谈的启发，她决定写一部以未来世界为背景的八日集。 | Synonym:  collection, anthology, compilation | Antonym:  monograph, single work | noun
