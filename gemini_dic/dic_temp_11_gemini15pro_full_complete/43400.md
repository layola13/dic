crankish: /ˈkræŋkɪʃ/ | adj. |古怪的，脾气坏的，易怒的 | Having strange and unusual beliefs or behavior; easily annoyed or irritated. | He's getting a bit crankish in his old age. 他年纪大了，脾气变得有点古怪。 | eccentric, crotchety, grumpy, irritable, cantankerous | agreeable, amiable, good-natured | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From crank (n.) + -ish (suffix).  "Crank" originally referred to a person with eccentric or impractical ideas, particularly in relation to mechanical inventions.  The "-ish" suffix adds the sense of "somewhat" or "having the qualities of."
