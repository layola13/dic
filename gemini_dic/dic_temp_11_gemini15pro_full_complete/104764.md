kayaks:/ˈkaɪæks/ | n. |皮划艇，皮船|A small, narrow boat with a covered deck and a seat where the paddler sits with legs outstretched, propelled by a double-bladed paddle. |例句：We rented kayaks and paddled down the river. 我们租了皮划艇，沿着河划了下去。

例句：The kayaks were brightly colored and easy to maneuver. 这些皮划艇颜色鲜艳，易于操作。|近义词：canoes, boats |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑ship, yacht等大型船只作为相对的概念) |词性:noun


kayak:/ˈkaɪæk/ | v. |划皮划艇|To travel in a kayak. |例句：We kayaked down the river for hours. 我们划着皮划艇在河上玩了几个小时。

例句：They plan to kayak around the island next weekend.  他们计划下周末划皮划艇环岛游。|近义词：canoe, paddle, boat |反义词: (没有直接的反义词) |词性:verb

词源: From Inuit *qajaq (meaning "hunter's boat" or "man's boat").
