dracunculus: /drəˈkʌŋkjʊləs/ | n. | 龙线虫，麦地那龙线虫 | A parasitic nematode worm (Dracunculus medinensis) of tropical regions, the female of which, when gravid, produces a painful ulcer in the skin of the legs or feet, through which the larvae are discharged into water. | Example: Dracunculus medinensis is a parasitic worm that causes dracunculiasis. 麦地那龙线虫是一种寄生虫，会导致麦地那龙线虫病。

Example:  The eradication of dracunculus is a major public health goal. 根除麦地那龙线虫是一项重要的公共卫生目标。 | 近义词: Guinea worm, fiery serpent | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific parasitic worm) | 词性: noun
