decontaminates:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪts/
verb
净化，清除污染，消毒
To remove or neutralize contaminants (harmful substances) from something.
The cleaning crew decontaminates the lab after each experiment.  实验结束后，清洁人员对实验室进行消毒。
The hospital staff worked quickly to decontaminate the operating room. 医院工作人员迅速对手术室进行消毒。
