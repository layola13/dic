bloodshedding:/ˈblʌdʃɛdɪŋ/
n.|流血, 殺戮|The act of causing a lot of death and violence, especially during a war or other conflict.|The bloodshed in the city continued for days. 城市里的杀戮持续了数日。|近义词：carnage, slaughter, massacre, gore|反义词：peace, reconciliation|词性:名词
Etymology: blood + shedding (from shed, Old English *sceadan "to separate, divide, distinguish")
