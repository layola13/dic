cut-and-run:/ˌkʌt ən ˈrʌn/ | idiom | 匆忙撤退；溜之大吉；落荒而逃 | To quickly abandon a difficult or unpleasant situation.  | The company cut and ran when the project started losing money.  公司在这个项目开始亏损时就溜之大吉了。 | synonyms: abandon, desert, flee, retreat | antonyms: stay, persevere, endure | idiom

cut-and-run:/ˌkʌt ən ˈrʌn/ | n. | 匆忙撤退；溜之大吉；落荒而逃 | The act of quickly abandoning a difficult or unpleasant situation. | His cut and run left his team in a difficult position. 他的不辞而别让他的团队陷入了困境。| synonyms: desertion, flight | antonyms: perseverance | noun
