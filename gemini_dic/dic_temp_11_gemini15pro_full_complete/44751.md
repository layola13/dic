crumbliest:/ˈkrʌmbliɪst/
adjective
最易碎的
Superlative form of crumbly: most crumbly.  Meaning easily broken into small fragments.
The crumbliest cake I've ever eaten fell apart as soon as I touched it. 我吃过的最易碎的蛋糕，我一碰就碎了。
The baker tried a new recipe, but the result was the crumbliest pie crust imaginable.  面包师尝试了一个新配方，但结果是做出了一个想象中最易碎的馅饼皮。

近义词:
brittle, fragile, friable

反义词:
tough, firm, solid

词性
adjective

词源
From *crumb* + *-ly* (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'having the qualities of') + *-est* (suffix forming the superlative degree of adjectives).
