counterdemonstrations:/ˌkaʊntərˌdemənˈstreɪʃənz/
|n.|反示威，抗议|A public display of opposition to another public display.  A protest against another protest.|Thousands of people joined the counterdemonstrations against the proposed law.  数千人参加了反对拟议法律的反示威活动。|
|Synonyms: counterprotests, protests|Antonyms: demonstrations, rallies, support|noun|
