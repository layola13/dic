geronimo:/dʒəˈrɒnɪmoʊ/ | int. | 振奋人心的战斗口号（通常在跳伞或采取大胆行动时喊）| A battle cry used to express exhilaration or excitement, especially when jumping from a height or taking a daring action. | Example: He yelled "Geronimo!" and jumped off the cliff. 他大喊一声“Geronimo！”然后跳下了悬崖。| Synonym: battle cry, war cry, yell | Antonym: whisper, silence | interjection

geronimo:/dʒəˈrɒnɪmoʊ/ | n. | 杰罗尼莫（美国土著阿帕奇部落领袖的名字）| Geronimo (c. 1829 – 1909) was a prominent leader and medicine man from the Bedonkohe band of the Chiricahua Apache tribe. | Example: Geronimo led his people in resistance against the U.S. and Mexican armies. 杰罗尼莫领导他的人民抵抗美国和墨西哥军队。| Synonym: Apache leader, warrior | Antonym: N/A | noun
