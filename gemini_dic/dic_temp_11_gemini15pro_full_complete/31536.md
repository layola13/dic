charterers:/ˈtʃɑːrtərərz/
n.
承租人；租船人
A person or company that hires or leases a vessel or aircraft.
The charterers were responsible for loading and unloading the cargo. 承租人负责装卸货物。

The charterers decided to extend their lease for another year. 租船人决定将租约再延长一年。

近义词:lessees, renters, hirers
反义词:owners, lessors

词性:名词(复数)

词源: From the verb "charter," which comes from Old French "chartre" and ultimately from Latin "charta," meaning "paper" or "document."
