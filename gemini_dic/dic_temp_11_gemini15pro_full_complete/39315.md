This beak shape is an adaptation for cracking seeds. | Example 1: The coniroster perched on the branch, cracking a sunflower seed with its powerful beak. 厚嘴鸟栖息在树枝上，用它强有力的喙啄开葵花籽。

Example 2:  Conirosters are common visitors to backyard bird feeders. 厚嘴鸟是后院鸟食器的常客。 | 近义词: finch, grosbeak | 反义词:  (No direct antonym as it refers to a specific beak shape) | 词性: noun
