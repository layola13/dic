dragger:/ˈdræɡər/| n.|拖拽者；拖拉机；拖网渔船|Someone or something that drags.|The fisherman used a dragger to catch fish. 渔夫用拖网捕鱼。|近义词：puller, hauler, trawler|反义词: pusher|noun

dragger:/ˈdræɡər/| n.|累赘；障碍；令人厌烦的人或事|Something or someone that is tedious, slow, or burdensome.|That old car is a real dragger. 那辆旧车真是个累赘。|近义词：burden, hindrance, nuisance|反义词: help, asset|noun
