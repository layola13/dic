heraldries: /ˈhɛrəldriz/
n.
纹章学；纹章；徽章
The study of coats of arms and other heraldic bearings.  It also refers to the coats of arms themselves, collectively.
The book provides a detailed overview of European heraldries. 这本书详细概述了欧洲纹章。
Medieval heraldries are a fascinating subject. 中世纪纹章学是一个引人入胜的课题。
近义词:  coats of arms, armory, insignia
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "anonymity" or "plainness" in certain contexts)
词性: noun (plural)

词源:
From Old French *heraldie, from herault "herald".
