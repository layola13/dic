bequeathal:/bɪˈkwiːðəl/
n.
遗产；遗赠；遗赠物
The act of bequeathing something, or something that is bequeathed.
His father's bequeathal of the family estate came with certain conditions.  他父亲遗赠的家族财产附带某些条件。
My grandfather's bequeathal included a collection of rare books. 我祖父的遗产包括一套珍本书。

近义词:legacy, inheritance, bequest
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用夺取相关的词) seizure, confiscation
词性: noun
