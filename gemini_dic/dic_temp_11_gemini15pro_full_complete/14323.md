automatisms:/ɔːˈtɒmətɪzəmz/ | n. | 自动症；无意识动作 | An involuntary action or behavior performed without conscious awareness, often as a result of neurological or psychological conditions. |  He exhibited automatisms like lip smacking and hand wringing during the seizure.  他在癫痫发作期间表现出咂嘴和绞手的自动症。 | synonyms: involuntary movements, reflex actions, unconscious actions | antonyms: conscious movements, voluntary actions, deliberate actions | noun

automatism:/ɔːˈtɒmətɪzəm/ | n. | 自动症；无意识动作；自动性 | The performance of actions without conscious thought or intention. | The sleepwalker's actions were pure automatism.  梦游者的行为纯粹是无意识的。| synonyms: involuntary action, reflex, unconscious behavior | antonyms: conscious action, voluntary behavior, deliberate action | noun
