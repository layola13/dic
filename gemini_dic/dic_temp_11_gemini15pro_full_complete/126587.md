nagarjuna:/ˌnɑːɡɑːrˈdʒuːnə/
n.|龙树菩萨；那加尔朱纳|A major figure in Mahayana Buddhism, particularly known for his work on emptiness (sunyata) and the Madhyamaka school of philosophy.|Nagarjuna is considered one of the most important Buddhist philosophers. 龙树菩萨被认为是最重要的佛教哲学家之一。|Nagarjuna's teachings have had a profound impact on Buddhist thought. 龙树菩萨的教义对佛教思想产生了深远的影响。|synonyms: none (unique figure)|antonyms: none (unique figure)|noun
