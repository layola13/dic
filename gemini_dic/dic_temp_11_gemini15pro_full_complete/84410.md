hallow:/ˈhæləʊ/ (UK) /ˈhæloʊ/ (US)| vt. |使神圣；尊为神圣；把…视为神圣|To make holy; to regard or treat as holy.|例句：The ground where the saint was buried was hallowed.  圣人安葬的地方被视为圣地。

例句：Time hallows certain customs. 时间赋予某些习俗以神圣性。|近义词：consecrate, sanctify, venerate|反义词：desecrate, profane|词性：动词

词源：From Old English *hālgian, from hālig "holy."  源自古英语 *hālgian，来自 hālig “神圣的”。
