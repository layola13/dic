housemen:/ˈhaʊsmən/ | n. | 住院实习医生；（英国）男住院医生 | A physician who lives in a hospital and cares for its patients, especially one who is still in training after graduating from medical school.  A male resident in a hospital (British English). |  The housemen were exhausted after a 36-hour shift. 住院实习医生们在值了36个小时的班后筋疲力尽。 | The housemen in the UK are responsible for patient care. 英国的男住院医生负责病人护理。 | Synonyms: resident, intern, junior doctor | Antonyms: consultant, attending physician | noun
