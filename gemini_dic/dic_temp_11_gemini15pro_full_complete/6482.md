amputative:/ˈæmpjʊˌteɪtɪv/
adjective
截肢的; 切断的
Relating to, or characterized by, amputation; performing amputation.
The surgeon discussed the amputative procedure with the patient.  外科医生与病人讨论了截肢手术。
The patient required amputative surgery due to severe infection.  由于严重感染，病人需要截肢手术。
近义词: ablative, excisional
反义词: restorative, reconstructive
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *amputātīvus, from Latin amputātus, past participle of amputāre (“to cut around, prune, lop off”), from ambi- (“around”) + putāre (“to prune, think”).
