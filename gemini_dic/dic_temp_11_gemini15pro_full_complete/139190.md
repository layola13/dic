objecting:/əbˈdʒektɪŋ/
adjective
反对的；不赞成的
Expressing or feeling disapproval or opposition.
He was objecting to the proposed changes. 他反对提议的变更。
近义词: opposing, dissenting, protesting
反义词: approving, consenting, agreeing
词性: adjective

objecting:/əbˈdʒektɪŋ/
noun
反对；异议
The act of expressing disapproval or opposition.
There were several objections to the plan. 对该计划有几点反对意见。
近义词: opposition, dissent, protest
反义词: approval, consent, agreement
词性: noun

词源: From the Latin verb "obiicere," meaning "to throw against" or "to oppose."  The word evolved through Old French "objet" and Middle English "objecten" before arriving at its modern form.
