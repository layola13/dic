counterfeiter:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪtər/| n.|伪造者；假冒者|A person who makes or sells counterfeit goods.  A person who copies something illegally. |The police arrested the counterfeiter who was producing fake banknotes. 警方逮捕了制造假钞的伪造者。|Synonyms: forger, faker, imitator, impersonator|Antonyms: authenticator, verifier|noun

counterfeit:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪt/| n. adj. v. | 伪造品；假冒的；伪造；假冒|Made in exact imitation of something valuable or important with the intention to deceive or defraud. |The handbag was a counterfeit of a designer brand.  这个手袋是名牌的仿制品。|Synonyms: fake, forged, spurious, imitation|Antonyms: genuine, authentic, real|noun, adjective, verb
