femalist:/ˈfɛmɪlɪst/ | n. | 女性主义者；女权主义者 | A person who supports the belief that women should have the same rights and opportunities as men. |  She's a strong feminist and advocates for equal pay. 她是一位坚定的女权主义者，倡导男女同工同酬。| Synonyms: women's rights advocate, suffragette, egalitarian | Antonyms: misogynist, sexist | noun

femalist:/ˈfɛmɪlɪst/ | adj. | 女性主义的；女权主义的 | Relating to or supporting feminism. |  She has a feminist perspective on the issue. 她对这个问题持有女权主义的观点。| Synonyms: womanist, pro-feminist | Antonyms: anti-feminist, misogynistic | adjective
