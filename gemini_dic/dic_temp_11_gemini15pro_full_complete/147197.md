overmagnifies: /ˌoʊvərˈmæɡnɪˌfaɪz/
verb
过度放大；过分夸大
to magnify something to an excessive degree.
The media overmagnifies the importance of celebrity gossip. 媒体过分夸大了名人八卦的重要性。
The microscope overmagnifies the image, making it blurry. 显微镜过度放大了图像，使其变得模糊。
synonyms: exaggerate, overstate, overemphasize, amplify, inflate
antonyms: understate, minimize, downplay
词性: 动词
