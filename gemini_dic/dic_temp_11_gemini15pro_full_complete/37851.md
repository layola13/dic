competitivenesses:/kəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
竞争力（复数）
The state or quality of being competitive; the ability to compete successfully.
Many factors contribute to a company's competitivenesses in the global market.
许多因素促成了公司在全球市场的竞争力。
The competitivenesses of different economies are compared using various indices. 
不同经济体的竞争力是用各种指数来比较的。
synonyms: abilities, capabilities, competencies, strengths
antonyms: weaknesses, inadequacies, deficiencies
noun
