nonmusicians:/ˌnɒn.mjuːˈzɪʃənz/ /ˌnɑːn.mjuːˈzɪʃənz/
noun
非音乐家
People who are not musicians.  Those who do not play a musical instrument professionally or with a high degree of skill.
The concert was attended by musicians and nonmusicians alike.  这场音乐会吸引了音乐家和非音乐家。
synonyms: non-professionals, amateurs, laypersons
antonyms: musicians, professionals, experts
词性: noun
