agentic:/əˈdʒɛntɪk/
|adj.|施动的，能动的|Having the capacity to act as an agent; characterized by agency.|She felt very agentic in her new role as project manager, taking charge and making decisions. 她作为项目经理的新角色让她感到非常有能动性，能够掌控并做出决策。|例句：The agentic state is characterized by a reduced sense of personal responsibility. 施动状态的特点是个人责任感降低。|近义词：active, proactive, self-determining, autonomous, independent|反义词：passive, submissive, dependent|词性:形容词

agentic:/əˈdʒɛntɪk/
|n.(rarely used)|施动性|The capacity to act as an agent; agency.|Her agentic qualities made her a successful entrepreneur.  她的能动性使她成为一名成功的企业家。|例句：Further research is needed to understand the development of agentic thinking. 需要进一步的研究来理解施动思维的发展。|近义词：agency, autonomy, self-determination|反义词：passivity, dependence|词性:名词
