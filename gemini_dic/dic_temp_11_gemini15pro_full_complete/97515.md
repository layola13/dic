ingloriousnesses:/ɪnˈɡlɔːriəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|不光彩；耻辱；不名誉|The state or quality of being inglorious; lack of glory or honor.|His ingloriousnesses were well documented in the press. 他的不光彩事迹在媒体上都有详细记载。|近义词:shame, disgrace, dishonor, ignominy|反义词:glory, honor, fame, renown|词性:名词

inglorious:/ɪnˈɡlɔːriəs/
|adj.|不光彩的；耻辱的；不名誉的|Not glorious; shameful; disgraceful.|He suffered an inglorious defeat. 他遭受了耻辱的失败。|近义词:shameful, disgraceful, dishonorable, ignominious|反义词:glorious, honorable, famous, renowned|词性:形容词
