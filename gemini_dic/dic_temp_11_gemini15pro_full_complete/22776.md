boruret: /ˈbɔːrəˌrɛt/ | n. | 硼化物 | A binary compound of boron with a more electropositive element.  A compound formed by the union of boron with a more electropositive element. | Boron carbide (B₄C) is a very hard boruret. 碳化硼 (B₄C) 是一种非常坚硬的硼化物。|  Magnesium diboride (MgB₂) is another example of a boruret. 二硼化镁 (MgB₂) 是硼化物的另一个例子。 | synonym: boride | antonym: N/A | noun
