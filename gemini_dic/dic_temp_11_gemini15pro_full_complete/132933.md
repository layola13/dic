nonemancipative:/ˌnɒnɪˈmænsɪpeɪtɪv/
adjective
非解放的；不授予自主权的
Not emancipative; not granting freedom or autonomy.
The nonemancipative laws of the time kept many people in servitude.  当时的非解放法律使许多人处于奴役状态。
The regime maintained a nonemancipative policy towards its colonies. 该政权对其殖民地保持着不授予自主权的政策。
Synonyms: restrictive, oppressive, suppressive, controlling
Antonyms: emancipatory, liberating, freeing, empowering
word type: adjective
