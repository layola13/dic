nonactivists:/ˌnɒnˈæktɪvɪsts/
n.|非活动分子；不积极分子|People who are not involved in activities, especially political or social ones. Those who do not participate in protests, campaigns, or other forms of activism.|Many nonactivists still care about social issues, but they prefer to express their views in other ways. 许多非活动人士仍然关心社会问题，但他们更喜欢以其他方式表达自己的观点。|
synonyms:bystanders, onlookers, observers
antonyms:activists, campaigners, advocates
词性:noun
