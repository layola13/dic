harmonize:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/ /ˈhɑːr.mə.naɪz/ | v. | 使和谐，使协调；协调，相配 | To bring into or cause to be in harmony; to make things consistent or compatible. |

例句：
The colors in the painting harmonize beautifully.
这幅画中的颜色搭配得非常和谐。

The government's policies need to harmonize with the needs of the people.
政府的政策需要与人民的需求相协调。


近义词：coordinate, reconcile, blend, integrate, match, accord
反义词：clash, conflict, disagree, jar
词性: verb
