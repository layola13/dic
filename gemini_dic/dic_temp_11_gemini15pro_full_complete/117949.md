membraniform:/ˈmɛmbrənɪfɔːrm/| adj.|膜状的，膜形的|Resembling a membrane; having the form of a membrane. |The bacteria formed a thin, membraniform layer on the surface of the liquid. 细菌在液体表面形成了薄薄的膜状层。|The artist created a membraniform sculpture using thin, translucent sheets of plastic.  艺术家用薄而透明的塑料片创作了一个膜状雕塑。|近义词：membranous, film-like, sheet-like|反义词: thick, solid|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin "membrana" (membrane) + "-form" (having the form of).
