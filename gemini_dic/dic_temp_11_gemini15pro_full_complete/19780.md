billowiest:/ˈbɪliəʊɪɪst/| adj.|最波涛汹涌的，最鼓胀的|Most billowy; characterized by large, swelling waves or masses.|The billowiest clouds drifted lazily across the summer sky.  最波涛汹涌的云朵懒洋洋地飘过夏日的天空。|近义词：puffiest, fullest, most voluminous|反义词:flattest, smoothest|词性:形容词
词源: From billow + -y + -est.  Billow comes from Old Norse *bylgja "wave."
