ostrich-like:/ˈɒstrɪtʃˌlaɪk/
adjective
像鸵鸟的；逃避现实的
Resembling an ostrich, especially in behavior; avoiding reality or difficult situations.
The politician buried his head in the sand in an ostrich-like manner, refusing to acknowledge the growing scandal.  这位政客像鸵鸟一样把头埋在沙子里，拒绝承认日益增长的丑闻。
She adopted an ostrich-like attitude towards her financial problems, hoping they would simply disappear. 她对自己的财务问题采取了鸵鸟式的态度，希望它们会自行消失。
Synonyms:  evasive, head-in-the-sand, avoidance,  irresponsible
Antonyms:  confrontational, responsible, proactive, realistic
詞性: adjective
