lovelessnesses:/ˈlʌvləsnəsɪz/
noun
无爱；缺乏爱；冷酷
The state or quality of being loveless; absence of love or affection.
His lovelessnesses made his children feel neglected. 他的冷漠让他的孩子们感到被忽视。
近义词: coldness, heartlessness, indifference, apathy
反义词: love, affection, warmth, tenderness
词性: noun

loveless:/ˈlʌvlɪs/
adjective
无爱的；缺乏爱意的；冷酷的
Without love or affection; cold and unfeeling.
She grew up in a loveless home. 她在一个缺乏爱的家庭中长大。
近义词: unloving, unaffectionate, cold, harsh
反义词: loving, affectionate, warm, tender
词性: adjective
