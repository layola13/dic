jettiest:/ˈdʒɛtɪɪst/
adjective
最像喷射的; 最突出部分的
having the characteristics of a jet or jets to the greatest degree; projecting furthest or most prominently.
The jettiest part of the cliff offered the best views of the ocean.  悬崖最突出的部分提供了最佳的海景。
The jettiest rock formations were popular with climbers.  最像喷射状的岩层很受攀岩者的欢迎。
近义词: projecting, protruding, jutting, prominent
反义词: receding, withdrawn, sunken, indented
词性: adjective
