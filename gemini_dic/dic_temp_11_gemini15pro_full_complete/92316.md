hypnotic:/hɪpˈnɒtɪk/ (US) /hɪpˈnɑːtɪk/| adj. n.|催眠的；催眠药|inducing or tending to induce sleep or hypnosis; a drug that induces sleep|例句：The rhythmic sound of the waves had a hypnotic effect. 海浪有节奏的声音有催眠的效果。|近义词：soporific, somnolent, slumberous|反义词：stimulating, invigorating|词性: adjective

hypnotic:/hɪpˈnɒtɪk/ (US) /hɪpˈnɑːtɪk/| n.|催眠药|a drug that induces sleep|例句：The doctor prescribed a hypnotic to help her sleep. 医生给她开了安眠药来帮助她睡眠。|近义词：sleeping pill, sedative|反义词：stimulant|词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin hypnoticus, from Greek hypnōtikos, from hypnoun "to put to sleep," related to hypnos "sleep."
