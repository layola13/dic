charterages:/ˈtʃɑːrtərɪdʒɪz/
n.
包租费，租船费
The cost of hiring a vessel or aircraft.
The charterages for the yacht were exorbitant.  游艇的租船费过高。
近义词：charter fees, hire charges, rental fees
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "earnings" from the owner's perspective)
