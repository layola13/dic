adding:/ˈæ.dɪŋ/ | v. | 添加; 加; 增加; 附加 |  The process of combining numbers or amounts to get a total. |  She is adding more sugar to her coffee. 她正在往咖啡里加糖。 | synonyms: summing, totaling, plussing | antonyms: subtracting, deducting, removing | verb

adding:/ˈæ.dɪŋ/ | adj. | 附加的，额外的 | Something that is extra or supplementary. | The adding burden of childcare made it difficult for her to work. 额外的育儿负担使她难以工作。 | synonyms: additional, supplementary, extra | antonyms: essential, necessary, integral | adjective
