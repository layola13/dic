colonialists:/kəˈloʊniəlɪsts/ | n. |殖民者，殖民主义者 | People who support or participate in colonialism, the policy or practice of acquiring full or partial political control over another country, occupying it with settlers, and exploiting it economically. | The colonialists exploited the natural resources of the conquered land. 殖民者剥削了被征服土地的自然资源。

The colonialists imposed their culture and language on the indigenous population. 殖民者将他们的文化和语言强加于土著居民。

| 近义词: colonizers, imperialists | 反义词: natives, indigenous people | 词性: Noun
