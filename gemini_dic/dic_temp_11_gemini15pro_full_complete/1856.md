acronym's:/ˈækrənɪmz/ |n.| 首字母缩略词 |A word formed from the initial letters of a name or phrase, such as "NATO" from "North Atlantic Treaty Organization." | Example: LASER is an acronym for "Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation." 激光（LASER）是“受激辐射光放大”的首字母缩略词。

| 近义词: abbreviation, initialism | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
