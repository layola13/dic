electro-optics:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɒptɪks/ |n.|电光学|The branch of physics that deals with the interactions between light and electric fields, especially as utilized in devices such as lasers, photodiodes, and liquid crystal displays.|Electro-optics plays a crucial role in modern telecommunications. 电光学在现代通信中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: optoelectronics, photonics|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

electro-optical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɒptɪkəl/ |adj.|电光的|Relating to or involving electro-optics; utilizing the interaction of light and electric fields.|Electro-optical devices are used in a wide range of applications. 电光设备广泛应用于各种领域。|Synonyms: optoelectronic, photonic|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
