noma:/ˈnoʊmə/| n. |游牧;游牧生活;游牧民族|A way of life in which a group of people travel from place to place, rather than living in one fixed location.|The nomads moved their herds to new pastures each season. 游牧民族每个季节都把他们的牲畜迁徙到新的牧场。|近义词：migration, wandering|反义词:settlement, sedentariness|noun

Etymology: From Greek *νομάς (nomas) "roaming, wandering," from *νέμειν (nemein) "to distribute, allot, pasture cattle."
