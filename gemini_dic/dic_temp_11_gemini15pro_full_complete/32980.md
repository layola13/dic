choreic:/kɔːˈriːɪk/ | adj. | 舞蹈症的；不规则的，痉挛的 | Relating to or resembling chorea, a nervous disorder characterized by involuntary, jerky movements. |  His choreic movements made it difficult for him to perform everyday tasks. 他的舞蹈症动作使他难以进行日常工作。 | The patient exhibited choreic limb movements. 病人表现出肢体舞蹈症样的动作。 | synonyms: jerky, spasmodic, twitchy, involuntary | antonyms: controlled, smooth, steady | adjective

choreic:/kɔːˈriːɪk/ | n. | 舞蹈症患者 | A person affected with chorea. | While less common now, choreics were once a more visible part of society. 虽然现在不太常见了，但舞蹈症患者曾经是社会中更明显的一部分。 |  The support group provided a safe space for choreics and their families.  这个互助小组为舞蹈症患者及其家人提供了一个安全的空间。 | synonyms:  (no direct synonym for a person with the condition) | antonyms: (not applicable) | noun
