craziness:/ˈkreɪzinəs/
|n.|疯狂；精神错乱|The state of being crazy; insane or chaotic behavior.|The craziness of the situation was overwhelming. 这种情况的疯狂程度令人难以承受。|Synonyms: insanity, lunacy, folly, absurdity|Antonyms: sanity, lucidity, rationality|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From crazy + -ness.  Crazy comes from Middle English *crasen*, from Old Norse *kreisa* "to bend, twist."
