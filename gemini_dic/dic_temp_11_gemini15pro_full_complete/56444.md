dolman:/ˈdoʊlmən/ (US), /ˈdɒlmən/ (UK)| n.|多尔曼袖长袍, 多尔曼袖,  匈牙利轻骑兵的短上衣|A loose, robelike outer garment with dolman sleeves. A dolman sleeve is a sleeve cut very wide at the armhole and tapering to the wrist.  Also, a hussar's short jacket.|She wore a stunning velvet dolman to the opera.|近义词: robe, caftan, kimono|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fitted jacket")|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dolman*, from Turkish *dolaman*, meaning "robe." Ultimately of Hungarian origin.
