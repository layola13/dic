comeliness:/ˈkʌmlɪnɪs/| n.|美丽，漂亮，清秀|The comeliness of the bride captivated everyone.  新娘的美丽吸引了所有人。|例句：She was renowned for her comeliness and grace. 她以美丽和优雅闻名。|近义词：beauty, attractiveness, loveliness, prettiness|反义词：ugliness, homeliness, unattractiveness|noun


Etymology->
From Middle English *comelynesse*, equivalent to *comely* + *-ness*.  *Comely* comes from Old English *cymlic* (“suitable, appropriate, beautiful”), from *cyme* (“suitable, appropriate”) + *-lic* (suffix forming adjectives).
