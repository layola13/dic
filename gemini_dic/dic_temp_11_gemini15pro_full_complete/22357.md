boo:/buː/
interjection|嘘（表示不满、轻蔑等）|An exclamation used to express disapproval or contempt, or to startle someone.|例句：The crowd booed the politician off the stage.  人群把政客嘘下台。|近义词：hiss, jeer|反义词：cheer, applaud|词性:感叹词
boo:/buː/
noun|嘘声|A sound made to express disapproval or contempt.|例句：The team received loud boos from the disappointed fans. 球队受到了失望的球迷的嘘声。|近义词：hiss, jeer|反义词：cheer, applause|词性:名词
boo:/buː/
verb|发出嘘声|To express disapproval or contempt by uttering “boo.”|例句：The audience booed the comedian off the stage. 观众把喜剧演员嘘下台。|近义词：hiss, jeer|反义词：cheer, applaud|词性:动词
