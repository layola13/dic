nondigestibilities:/ˌnɒndaɪˌdʒɛstɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
无法消化; 不消化性; 消化不良
The inability of a substance to be broken down and absorbed by the digestive system.
Certain types of fiber contribute to the nondigestibilities of food.  某些类型的纤维导致食物无法消化。
The researchers studied the nondigestibilities of various artificial sweeteners. 研究人员研究了各种人造甜味剂的消化不良性。
近义词: indigestibility, indigestibleness
反义词: digestibility
