apophlegmatism:/ˌæpəˈflɛɡməˌtɪzəm/
n.
冷静；镇定；沉着
The state of being calm and unemotional.
He maintained his apophlegmatism throughout the crisis.  他在整个危机中保持冷静。
composure, equanimity, imperturbability, serenity
excitability, nervousness, anxiety
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *apophlegmatismus, from Ancient Greek ἀποφλεγματισμός (apophlegmatismos, “clearing of phlegm”), from ἀποφλεγματίζω (apophlegmatizō, “I clear of phlegm”), from ἀπό (apó, “off, away”) + φλέγμα (phlégma, “phlegm”).
