automagically:/ˌɔːtəˈmædʒɪkli/ | adv. | 自动地；神奇地 | Like magic; automatically.  It implies something happens effortlessly and mysteriously, often related to technology. | The computer automagically fixed the problem. 电脑神奇地解决了这个问题。|  The program automagically generates reports. 程序自动生成报告。 | 近义词: automatically, magically, miraculously | 反义词: manually, laboriously | 词性: 副词

词源:  A portmanteau of "automatic" and "magically," coined in the early days of computing.
