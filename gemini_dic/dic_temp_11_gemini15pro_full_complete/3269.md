affirmative action:/əˈfɜːrmətɪv ˈækʃ(ə)n/| n.|平权行动|A set of policies and practices intended to address past and present systemic discrimination by providing advantages to individuals and groups who have historically been marginalized or underrepresented, especially in areas like employment and education.|The university's affirmative action policy aims to increase the number of minority students. 这所大学的平权行动政策旨在增加少数族裔学生的数量。

The company implemented an affirmative action plan to promote diversity in its workforce.  这家公司实施了一项平权行动计划，以促进其员工队伍的多样性。|近义词：positive discrimination, equal opportunity measures|反义词：reverse discrimination|词性: noun
