outyelping:/ˌaʊtˈjɛlpɪŋ/
|v. | 比...叫得更响亮;  在叫喊声上胜过|
|The coyotes were outyelping each other in the distance.  远处的郊狼互相叫嚣，比谁的声音更大。|
|Example:  The fans were outyelping the opposing team's supporters. 球迷们的叫喊声盖过了对方球队的支持者。|
|Synonyms: outshouting, outscreaming, drowning out|
|Antonyms: being drowned out, being outshouted|
|Verb|

outyelping:/ˌaʊtˈjɛlpɪŋ/
|adj. |  (用于描述声音)盖过其他声音的，叫喊声更大的|
|The outyelping cries of the children filled the playground. 孩子们震耳欲聋的叫喊声充满了操场。|
|Example: The outyelping noise of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate. 建筑工地震耳欲聋的噪音使人难以集中注意力。|
|Synonyms: deafening, earsplitting, piercing|
|Antonyms: quiet, hushed, muffled|
|Adjective|
