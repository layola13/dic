hydriodide:/ˌhaɪdrɪˈaɪdaɪd/
n.
氢碘酸盐；碘化物
A binary compound of iodine with another element or group.  A salt of hydriodic acid.
Potassium hydriodide is used in the preparation of organic compounds. 碘化钾用于制备有机化合物。
