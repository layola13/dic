ageist:/ˈeɪdʒɪst/| adj. n.|歧视老年人的；年龄歧视者|Discriminating against or prejudiced against older people; a person who discriminates on the basis of age.|例句：It's ageist to assume that older people can't use technology.  认为老年人不会使用科技是年龄歧视。

|例句：The company was accused of ageist hiring practices.  这家公司被指控在招聘过程中存在年龄歧视。|近义词：discriminatory, prejudiced, biased|反义词:accepting, tolerant|词性:形容词/名词

ageism:/ˈeɪdʒɪzəm/| n.|年龄歧视；年龄主义|Prejudice or discrimination on the basis of age, typically against older people.|例句：Ageism is a serious problem in the workplace.  年龄歧视是工作场所的一个严重问题。

|例句：She encountered ageism when she tried to re-enter the workforce after raising her children.  当她抚养孩子后试图重新进入职场时，她遇到了年龄歧视。|近义词：discrimination, prejudice, bias|反义词:tolerance, acceptance|词性:名词
