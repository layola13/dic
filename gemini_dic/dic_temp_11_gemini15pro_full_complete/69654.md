fence-mending:/ˈfɛnsˌmɛndɪŋ/
noun
修补栅栏；改善关系
The act of repairing a fence, often used metaphorically to describe the act of repairing a damaged relationship.
He engaged in some fence-mending after his argument with his neighbor.  他与邻居争吵后，努力修补关系。
synonyms: reconciliation, rapprochement, appeasement, making amends, bridge-building
antonyms:  alienation, estrangement, division
