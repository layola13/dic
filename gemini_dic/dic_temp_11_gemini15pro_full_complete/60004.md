editorializes:/ˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
发表社论；评论；表达意见
expresses opinions in an editorial; introduces personal opinions into a factual report.
The news anchor editorialized on the recent political scandal.  这位新闻主播就最近的政治丑闻发表了评论。
The reporter editorialized about the need for stricter gun control laws. 记者就加强枪支管制法律的必要性发表了评论。
Synonyms: comments, opines, pontificates
Antonyms: reports, states, relates
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From editorial + -ize.  "editorial" comes from Late Latin editorialis, from Latin editor "publisher," from editus, past participle of edere "to give out, publish," from ex- "out" + -dere, combining form related to dare "to give."  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become," "to subject to," "to engage in," etc.
