legitimizers:/ləˈdʒɪtɪˌmaɪzərz/
|n.|合法化者；使…合法化的人（或事物）|People or things that make something legitimate or acceptable.  They provide justification or authority for something.|The new law served as a legitimizer for the previously controversial practice.|近义词: validators, ratifiers, authorizers, justifiers|反义词: delegitimizers, invalidators|词性: 名词 (plural)

legitimize:/ləˈdʒɪtɪˌmaɪz/
|v.|使合法化；使…显得正当|To make something legal or acceptable; to justify something.|The government's actions legitimized the rebels' cause.|近义词: validate, ratify, authorize, justify|反义词: delegitimize, invalidate|词性: 动词

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
|adj.|合法的；正当的；合理的|Conforming to the law or to rules; justifiable; reasonable.|The government's concern for national security is legitimate.|近义词: legal, valid, lawful, justifiable|反义词: illegitimate, illegal, unlawful, invalid|词性: 形容词

词源: From the French légitimer, from Latin legitimus 'lawful,' from lex, leg- 'law.'
