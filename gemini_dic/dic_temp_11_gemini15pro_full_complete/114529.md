malambo:/maˈlamboʊ/ (US) /maˈlæmboʊ/ (UK)| n.| 马兰博舞; 马兰博鼓|A lively Argentinian folk dance and music genre, traditionally performed by men, featuring intricate footwork, rhythmic stomping, and often accompanied by drumming on a bombo legüero (a type of drum).|The malambo dancers showcased their incredible skill and stamina. 马兰博舞者展示了他们惊人的技巧和耐力。

The malambo performance was a highlight of the festival.  马兰博表演是节日的一大亮点。|近义词：zamba (another Argentinian folk dance), chacarera|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From Quechua *malampu.
