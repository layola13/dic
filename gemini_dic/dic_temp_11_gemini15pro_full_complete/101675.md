Example 2:  He identified himself as an irreligionist on the census form. 他在人口普查表上将自己定义为无宗教信仰者。 | Near Synonyms: atheist, agnostic, nonbeliever, skeptic, freethinker | Antonyms: believer, religious person, devout person | Noun
