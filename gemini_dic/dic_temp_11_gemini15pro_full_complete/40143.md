contemporarinesses:/kənˌtɛmpəˈrɛərɪnɪsɪz/
|n.|当代性，同时代性|The state of existing or occurring at the same time.|The exhibition explores the contemporarinesses of various art forms.  这个展览探索了各种艺术形式的当代性。|

contemporariness:/kənˌtɛmpəˈrɛərɪnɪs/

|近义词：modernity, currency, presentness|反义词：antiquity, archaism, pastness|词性:名词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From contemporary + -ness. Contemporary comes from the Late Latin contemporarius, from com- "together" + temporarius "of time," from tempus "time."
