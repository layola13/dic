disarray:/dɪsəˈreɪ/ /ˌdɪsəˈreɪ/| n. |混乱，紊乱|A state of disorder; confusion.|The room was in complete disarray after the party. 房间在派对后一片狼藉。|近义词：disorder, chaos, confusion, mess|反义词：order, organization, tidiness|词性:名词

disarray:/dɪsəˈreɪ/ /ˌdɪsəˈreɪ/| vt. |使混乱，使紊乱|To throw into disorder; to disarrange.|The sudden storm disarrayed the carefully arranged garden. 突如其来的暴风雨把精心布置的花园弄得乱七八糟。|近义词：disorganize, disorder, disrupt|反义词：organize, arrange, order|词性:动词
