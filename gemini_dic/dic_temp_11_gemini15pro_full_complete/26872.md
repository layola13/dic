cajun:/ˈkeɪdʒən/| adj. n.|卡津人（的），卡津文化的|A descendant of French-speaking Acadians who were deported by the British from Acadia (Nova Scotia, Canada) in the 18th century and settled in southern Louisiana. Also: relating to or characteristic of Cajuns or their culture, especially with reference to their folk music or cuisine.|例句：Cajun music is a vibrant and soulful genre. 卡津音乐是一种充满活力和灵魂的音乐流派。

例句：We tried some authentic Cajun cuisine in Louisiana. 我们在路易斯安那州尝试了一些正宗的卡津菜。|近义词：Acadian|反义词:|noun, adjective
