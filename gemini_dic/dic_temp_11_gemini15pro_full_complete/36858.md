colophonite:/ˈkɒləfəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 钙铁榴石 | A brown, yellow, or green garnet, especially a massive, granular variety of andradite.  It has a resinous luster. | The jeweler identified the stone as a colophonite due to its granular texture and resinous appearance.  这名珠宝商根据其颗粒状纹理和树脂状外观将这块石头鉴定为钙铁榴石。| Synonym: andradite, garnet | Antonym: N/A | noun
