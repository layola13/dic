gnomically:/noʊˈmɪk(ə)li/
adv.
格言式地，警句式地
In a way that resembles or suggests a gnome, especially in being wise or perceptive; expressed as a short, pithy saying.
He spoke gnomically, offering cryptic pronouncements on the nature of existence. 他说话像格言一样，对存在的本质发表了一些神秘的声明。
Near synonyms: aphoristically, sententiously, pithily, succinctly
Near antonyms: verbosely, prolixly, lengthily
词性: 副词
