coagent:/ˈkoʊˌeɪdʒənt/| n.|共同代理人；合作者；共剂|A person or thing that acts with another, especially in bringing about a result.|The virus acts as a coagent in the development of the disease. 这种病毒是该疾病发展的共剂。|Synonyms: collaborator, partner, associate|Antonyms: opponent, adversary|noun

coagent:/ˈkoʊˌeɪdʒənt/| adj.|共同作用的|Acting together with another agent.|The coagent effects of the two drugs produced a stronger response.  两种药物的共同作用产生了更强的反应。|Synonyms: synergistic, collaborative|Antonyms: antagonistic, opposing|adjective
