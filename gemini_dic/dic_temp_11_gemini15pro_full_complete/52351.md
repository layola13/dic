dictator:/ˈdɪkteɪtər/ | n. | 独裁者；专制者 | A ruler with total power over a country, typically one who has obtained power by force. |  He ruled the country as a dictator for over 30 years. 他以独裁者的身份统治了这个国家30多年。

The dictator suppressed all opposition.  这个独裁者压制了所有的反对派。


| 近义词: autocrat, tyrant, despot | 反义词: democrat | 词性: noun
