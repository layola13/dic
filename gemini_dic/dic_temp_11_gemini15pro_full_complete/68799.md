facet:/ˈfæsɪt/ | v. |刻面 |To cut facets on. |The jeweler carefully faceted the diamond.  珠宝商仔细地给钻石刻面。| synonyms: cut, shape, polish | antonyms: smooth, round, unfaceted | verb
