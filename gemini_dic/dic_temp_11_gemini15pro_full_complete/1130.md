accessorize: /əkˈsesəˌraɪz/ | v. |  搭配配饰，装饰 | To add accessories to something, typically clothing, to enhance its appearance. |  She accessorized her little black dress with a pearl necklace and earrings. 她用珍珠项链和耳环来搭配她的小黑裙。 | 近义词: adorn, embellish, ornament | 反义词: strip, simplify | verb
