overdiscuss:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌs/| v. |过度讨论，过分讨论|To discuss something excessively or to a point where it becomes unproductive or tedious. |We overdiscussed the topic and ended up going in circles. 我们过度讨论了这个话题，结果一直在兜圈子。|They overdiscussed the minor details and missed the deadline. 他们过度讨论了细枝末节，错过了截止日期。|近义词：analyze to death, belabor, rehash|反义词：skim, gloss over, summarize|词性:动词

词源：The prefix "over-" means "excessively" or "too much," combined with the verb "discuss."  "Discuss" comes from the Latin "discutere," meaning "to shake apart" or "examine."  Thus, "overdiscuss" literally means to examine something excessively.
