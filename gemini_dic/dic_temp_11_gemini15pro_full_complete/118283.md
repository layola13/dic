mercifulnesses:/ˈmɜːrsɪfʊlnɪsɪz/
noun
仁慈，慈悲（复数）
The quality or state of being merciful; compassion or forgiveness shown toward someone whom it is in one's power to punish or harm.
The mercifulnesses of God are infinite. 上帝的慈悲是无限的。
近义词:compassion, clemency, leniency, mercy
反义词:cruelty, ruthlessness, mercilessness
