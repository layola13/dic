out-trick: /ˌaʊtˈtrɪk/
verb
以计胜过，用诡计战胜
to defeat someone by using a trick
He out-tricked his opponent in the final round of the card game. 他在纸牌游戏的最后一轮以计胜过了对手。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
词性: verb

out-trick: /ˌaʊtˈtrɪk/
noun
高明的计谋，取胜的策略
a clever trick used to defeat someone
His out-trick secured him the championship title. 他的高明计谋为他赢得了冠军头衔。
近义词: ruse, stratagem, ploy
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "blunder" or "mistake")
词性: noun
