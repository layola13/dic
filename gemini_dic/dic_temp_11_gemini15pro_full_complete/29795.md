caucus:/ˈkɔːkəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːkəs/ (UK)| n. vi.| 核心会议，预选会议；党团会议|A meeting of the members of a political party or movement, especially to select candidates or decide policy.|例句：The Democratic caucus selected their nominee for president. 民主党核心会议选出了他们的总统候选人。

例句：The party held a caucus to discuss the upcoming election. 该党召开了一次预选会议来讨论即将举行的选举。|近义词：meeting, conference, assembly, gathering|反义词: N/A
词性: 名词, 动词

caucus:/ˈkɔːkəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːkəs/ (UK)| vi.| 举行核心会议，参加预选会议|To hold or participate in a caucus.|例句：The senators caucused to choose their leader. 参议员们召开核心会议选举他们的领导人。

例句：Party members will caucus next week to select a candidate. 党员们将在下周举行预选会议，选出一名候选人。|近义词：meet, confer, assemble, gather|反义词: N/A
