"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "brightness" (from "bright" (Old English beorht, meaning "shining, radiant") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)) + "-es" (plural suffix)
