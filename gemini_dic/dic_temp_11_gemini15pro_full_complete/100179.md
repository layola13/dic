interreigned:/ˌɪntərˈreɪnd/
verb
相互统治，交替统治，（两个国王）先后统治
A period during which two or more monarchs reigned in succession.  The period between the reigns of two rulers.
The five years that interreigned between the two world wars were a time of uncertainty and fear.  两次世界大战之间交替统治的五年充满了不确定性和恐惧。
Synonyms:  alternate, succeed, follow
Antonyms: precede, predate
