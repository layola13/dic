outdespatching:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈspætʃ.ɪŋ/
verb
超越派遣，胜过派遣
Surpassing in speed or efficiency of dispatching; sending out something faster or more effectively than another.
The new courier service is outdespatching all its competitors. 新的快递服务在速度和效率上都超过了所有竞争对手。
The company prided itself on outdespatching its rivals in delivering goods to customers.  这家公司以比竞争对手更快地向客户交付货物而自豪。
近义词: outpacing, outrunning, exceeding
反义词: lagging, trailing
