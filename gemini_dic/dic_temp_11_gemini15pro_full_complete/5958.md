ambrosial: /æmˈbroʊʒəl/ /æmˈbroʊziəl/ | adj. | 美味的，芳香的；神食的 | Exceptionally pleasing to taste or smell; worthy of the gods; divine. | The bakery was filled with the ambrosial aroma of freshly baked bread. 面包店里充满了新鲜出炉的面包的美味香气。 | The Thanksgiving feast was an ambrosial delight. 感恩节盛宴是一场美味的盛宴。 | 近义词: delicious, heavenly, divine, delectable, savory | 反义词: unpleasant, unappetizing, foul, disgusting | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *ambrosialis, from Latin ambrosia (“food of the gods”), from Ancient Greek ἀμβροσία (ambrosía, “immortality; food of the gods”), from ἀμβρόσιος (ambrósios, “immortal”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + *μβροτός (*mbrotos, “mortal”).
