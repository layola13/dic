fibr:/ˈfaɪbər/ (US) /ˈfaɪbə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 纤维; 纤维组织; 质地; 基本成分; 品格; 道义; 勇气|A thread or filament from which a vegetable tissue, mineral substance, or textile is formed.  A slender and greatly elongated thread-like structure. |例句：The muscle fibr was torn. 肌肉纤维撕裂了。|例句：The rope is made of natural fibr. 这绳子是由天然纤维制成的。|近义词：filament, strand, thread, fiber|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
