miszealous: /ˌmɪsˈzɛləs/ | adj. | 过分热心的；狂热的 | Excessively zealous; marked by excessive or misguided zeal.  Derived from "mis-" (wrongly) + "zealous" (enthusiastic). |  His miszealous attempts to help actually hindered the project. 他过分热心的帮助实际上阻碍了项目。 |  Example:  Her miszealous pursuit of fame led to her downfall. 她对名声过分狂热的追求导致了她的垮台。 | Synonyms: overzealous, fanatical, immoderate, excessive  | Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | adjective
