live up to:/lɪv ˈʌp tuː/
|phrasal verb|
|不辜负；做到；实践(诺言等)|
|To fulfill expectations or to reach a certain standard.|
|He failed to live up to his parents' expectations.| 他没能达到父母的期望。

|She always lives up to her promises.| 她总是信守诺言。

|近义词：fulfill, meet, satisfy, achieve|
|反义词：disappoint, fail, fall short of|
|词性：动词短语|
