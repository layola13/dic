ivory-towerists: /ˈaɪvəri ˈtaʊərists/
noun
象牙塔里的人; 不切实际的人
people who live or exist in an “ivory tower”;  isolated from the practical world and unconcerned with its problems.
Those ivory-towerists have no idea what it's like to work a minimum wage job. 那些象牙塔里的人根本不知道拿着最低工资工作是什么滋味。
Synonyms: academics, intellectuals, theorists, idealists,  dreamers
Antonyms: pragmatists, realists, practical people, down-to-earth people
n.
