corticine:/ˈkɔːtɪsɪn/ /ˈkɔːrtɪsiːn/
adjective
皮质的;关于皮质的
Relating to or consisting of cortex.
Corticine pathways are essential for higher-level cognitive functions. 皮质通路对于高级认知功能至关重要。
The corticine layers of the brain play a crucial role in information processing. 大脑的皮质层在信息处理中起着至关重要的作用。
近义词: cortical
反义词: medullary
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *corticīnus, from cortex (“bark, rind, shell, husk”) + -īnus.
