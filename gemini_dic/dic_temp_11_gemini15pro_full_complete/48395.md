dedesignating:/ˌdiːˈdɛzɪɡneɪtɪŋ/
verb
取消指定；撤销指定
removing a designation or appointment from something or someone.
The committee is dedesignating the area as a protected wetland. 委员会正在取消该地区作为受保护湿地的指定。
The governor dedesignated the judge to a lower court. 州长将法官改派到下级法院。
Synonyms: revoking, rescinding, canceling, withdrawing, annulling
Antonyms: designating, appointing, assigning, nominating
词性: 动词
