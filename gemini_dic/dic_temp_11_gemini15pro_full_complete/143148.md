outcharged:/ˌaʊtˈtʃɑːrdʒd/
verb
多收…的费用；收费超过
charged someone more than they should have been charged
例句：The customer complained that they had been outcharged for their meal. 顾客抱怨他们的餐费被多收了。
The taxi driver outcharged the tourists. 出租车司机多收了游客的钱。
近义词：overcharge, overbill
反义词：undercharge
词性：动词
