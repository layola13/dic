interlocated:/ˌɪntərˈloʊkeɪtɪd/ | adj. |相互关联的，互相联系的 | placed or fitted together | The two companies' operations are so interlocated that separating them would be difficult.  这两家公司的运营相互关联，以至于很难将它们分开。| 近义词：interconnected, intertwined, interlinked | 反义词：separate, independent | 词性:形容词

interlocate:/ˌɪntərˈloʊkeɪt/ | v. |互相联系，互相连接 | to connect or lock together | The pieces of the puzzle interlocate to form a complete picture. 拼图的碎片互相连接形成一幅完整的图画。| 近义词：connect, join, link | 反义词：separate, disconnect | 词性:动词

词源:  From inter- + locate.  "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among," and "locate" means "to place." Thus, "interlocate" literally means to place between or among.
