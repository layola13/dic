chilly:/ˈtʃɪli/| adj.|寒冷的，冷飕飕的；冷漠的，不友好的|Feeling uncomfortably cold; unfriendly or distant in manner. |例句：It's a bit chilly outside today. 今天外面有点冷。|例句：She gave me a chilly reception. 她对我的接待很冷淡。|近义词：cold, cool, frosty, unfriendly|反义词：warm, friendly, hot|词性:形容词
词源: From chill + -y.  Chill itself comes from Old English cele ("cold").
