lamboys:/ˈlæmˌbɔɪz/ | n. | 小伙子们，小羊羔们 (尤指活泼的) |  A playful or affectionate term for boys, often suggesting youthful energy and sometimes a bit of mischief, similar to "lads" or "rascals."  It can also literally refer to young male lambs. |  The lamboys were racing around the field.  这些小伙子们在田野里跑来跑去。 | Synonyms: lads, rascals, boys, youngsters | Antonyms: old men, elders | noun
