nonassumptions:/ˌnɒnəˈsʌmpʃənz/ | n. | 无假设，不预设 |  The principle of nonassumptions dictates that we should approach each situation with an open mind, free from preconceived notions. |  We proceeded with the investigation based on nonassumptions, considering all possibilities. | synonyms: open-mindedness, neutrality, objectivity | antonyms: prejudice, bias, preconception | noun

Etymology: "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "assumption" (from Late Latin "assumptio," meaning "a taking up").
