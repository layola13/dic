mitotically:/mɪˈtɒtɪkli/
adverb|有丝分裂地|In a manner relating to or characteristic of mitosis (cell division).|The cells were dividing mitotically.|近义词: asexually, by mitosis|反义词: meiotically|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mitosis + -ally.  Mitosis comes from the Greek mitos "thread" + -osis "condition, process."
