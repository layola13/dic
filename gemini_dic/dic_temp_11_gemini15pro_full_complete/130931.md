nonaspirated:/ˌnɒnˈæspəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 非送气的 | Pronounced without an audible release of breath.  Relating to a sound, especially a plosive consonant, produced without aspiration. |  The /p/ in "spin" is nonaspirated, while the /p/ in "pin" is aspirated.  旋转中的/p/是非送气的，而pin中的/p/是送气的。 |  unaspirated, unvoiced | aspirated | adjective
