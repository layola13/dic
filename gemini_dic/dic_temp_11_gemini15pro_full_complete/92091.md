hyperrealizes:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度现实化；超现实化
to cause something to seem excessively or overly real, often to the point of artificiality or distortion.  It implies an intensified or exaggerated sense of reality.
The film hyperrealizes the violence of war, making it both disturbing and captivating.  这部电影过度渲染了战争的暴力，使其既令人不安又引人入胜。
The VR experience hyperrealizes the feeling of flying, making it almost too intense. 这个VR体验过度现实化了飞行的感觉，使其几乎过于强烈。
intensifies, exaggerates, amplifies
understates, minimizes, downplays
