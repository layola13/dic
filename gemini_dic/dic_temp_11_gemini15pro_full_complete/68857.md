fashionless:/ˈfæʃənˌləs/| adj.|缺乏时尚感的；不时髦的；土气的|Lacking fashion or style; not fashionable. |例句：He wore a fashionless old suit. 他穿着一套老式过时的西装。

例句：Her fashionless attire made her stand out in the stylish crowd.  她那毫无时尚感的服装让她在时髦的人群中显得格格不入。|近义词：unfashionable, outdated, frumpy, dowdy, styleless|反义词：fashionable, stylish, trendy, chic|词性:形容词

词源: fashion + -less.  "Fashion" comes from Old French "façon" meaning "making, shape, form." "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking."
