chillnesses:/ˈtʃɪlnɪsɪz/
n.
疾病，病痛
A state of being ill or unhealthy.
She suffered from various childhood illnesses. 她患有各种儿童疾病。

近义词:sicknesses, ailments, diseases, maladies
反义词:health, wellness

词性:noun (plural)
词源:From "chillness," meaning a feeling of coldness, later extended to refer to the feeling of being unwell, and eventually to illness itself.  The plural form "chillnesses" is less common than "illnesses."
