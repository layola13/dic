nonbeatific:/ˌnɒnbiːəˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
非至福的，不快乐的
Not beatific; not imparting or experiencing supreme happiness or blessedness.
His nonbeatific expression showed his disappointment. 他那不快乐的表情显示了他的失望。

The long, arduous journey left them in a nonbeatific state. 漫长而艰苦的旅程使他们处于不快乐的状态。

Synonyms: unhappy, joyless, miserable, wretched, sorrowful
Antonyms: beatific, blissful, joyful, happy, ecstatic
词性: 形容词
