griffinish:/ˈɡrɪfɪnɪʃ/| adj.|狮鹫般的；像狮鹫的|Resembling or characteristic of a griffin; having the qualities of a griffin, such as fierceness, vigilance, or a combination of leonine and aquiline features.|The statue had a griffinish appearance, with the head and wings of an eagle and the body of a lion. 这座雕像有着狮鹫般的外观，鹰的头和翅膀，狮子的身体。|近义词：leonine, aquiline, fierce, vigilant|反义词:gentle, meek|词性:形容词
