dietitian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. |营养师；饮食学家|A health professional who gives advice about food and eating. |例句：The dietitian recommended a balanced diet. 营养师建议均衡饮食。

例句：I consulted a dietitian about my weight loss goals. 我咨询了一位营养师关于我的减肥目标。|近义词：nutritionist |反义词: (无直接反义词) |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin diaeta "diet," from Greek diaita "manner of living," including diet, from diaitasthai "lead one's life," from dia "through" + aithein "to burn," from PIE root *aidh- "to burn." The ending "-ician" denotes a specialist.
