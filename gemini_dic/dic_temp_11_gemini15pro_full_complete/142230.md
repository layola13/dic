orogen:/ˈɔːrədʒən/ | n. |造山带；山脉 |A region of the Earth's crust involved in mountain formation.  A belt of the earth's crust involved in the formation of mountains. |例句：The Himalayas are a vast orogen. 喜马拉雅山脉是一个巨大的造山带。

|例句：The geologists studied the ancient orogen for clues about Earth's history. 地质学家研究了古老的造山带，以寻找有关地球历史的线索。|近义词：mountain range, mountain belt, orogenic belt |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'plain' or 'basin' in a geological context) |词性: noun
