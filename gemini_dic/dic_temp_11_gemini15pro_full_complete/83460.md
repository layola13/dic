habitude:/ˈhæbɪtjuːd/ (US), /ˈhæbɪtʃuːd/ (UK)| n.|习惯，习性|A settled or regular tendency or practice, especially one that is hard to give up.|She has a habitude of biting her nails when she's nervous. 她紧张的时候有咬指甲的习惯。| 近义词: custom, practice, habit, wont| 反义词: unfamiliarity, inexperience| noun
