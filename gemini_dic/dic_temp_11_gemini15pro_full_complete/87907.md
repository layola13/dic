hieroglyph:/ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪf/ | n. |象形文字 |A picture or symbol used in hieroglyphic writing, especially in ancient Egyptian writing. | Example: The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphs.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。

| Example:  The walls of the tomb were covered in intricate hieroglyphs.  墓穴的墙壁上覆盖着复杂的象形文字。| Synonym: symbol, ideogram, pictogram | Antonym: letter, alphabet | noun


hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 象形文字的 | Relating to or written in hieroglyphs. | Example: Hieroglyphic writing is a complex system. 象形文字书写是一个复杂的系统。

| Example: The archaeologist specialized in deciphering hieroglyphic inscriptions.  这位考古学家专门破译象形文字铭文。| Synonym: symbolic, pictorial | Antonym: alphabetic | adjective
