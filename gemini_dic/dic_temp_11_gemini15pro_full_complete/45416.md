cuppings:/ˈkʌpɪŋz/
n.
拔罐; 杯吸法; 拔火罐疗法;
The practice of placing heated cups on the skin to create suction. This is believed to draw out toxins, improve blood flow, and relieve pain.
Example 1:  She tried cuppings to relieve her back pain. 她尝试拔罐来缓解背痛。
Example 2: Cuppings is a traditional Chinese medicine therapy. 拔罐是一种传统的中医疗法。

近义词:  cupping therapy, vacuum therapy
反义词:  None (No direct antonym)
词性: Noun
