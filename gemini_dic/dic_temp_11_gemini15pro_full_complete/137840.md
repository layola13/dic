nonvictim:/ˌnɒnˈvɪktɪm/ | n. |非受害者|A person who is not a victim, especially in a situation where others are.  This term can sometimes carry a connotation of someone who avoids being a victim by taking proactive measures or by having a certain mindset.|例句：He considered himself a nonvictim, believing he had control over his own destiny. 他认为自己不是受害者，相信自己能掌控自己的命运。

例句：The program aimed to empower individuals to become nonvictims.  这个项目旨在赋能个人，使他们成为非受害者。|近义词：survivor, thriver|反义词：victim|词性: 名词
