ciao:/tʃaʊ/ |interj. n.|你好，再见| An informal Italian greeting or farewell.| Example: "Ciao, Marco!" (你好，马可！) | Example: "Ciao, bella!" (再见，美女！) |Synonym: hello, goodbye, farewell| Antonym: N/A | Interjection, Noun

词源: From Italian *ciao*, from Venetian *s-ciào* "I am your slave", from Vulgar Latin *sclavus* (compare Italian *schiavo*, French *esclave*) "slave".  Originally a very formal greeting, akin to saying "I am at your service," its meaning has shifted to become informal.
