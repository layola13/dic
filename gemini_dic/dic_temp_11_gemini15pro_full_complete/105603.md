kindredships:/ˈkɪndrɪdʃɪps/| n. |亲属关系；血缘关系；精神上的契合|A state of being related or connected by blood, or a sharing of similar interests, values, or characteristics. |Example 1: The two families felt a strong sense of kindredship after going through the same hardship.  这两个家庭经历了同样的苦难后，产生了强烈的亲属关系。

Example 2:  There is a deep kindredship between the two artists, evident in their shared artistic vision.  两位艺术家之间有着深厚的精神契合，这在他们共同的艺术愿景中显而易见。|近义词：kinship, relationship, affinity, connection|反义词：estrangement, alienation|词性:名词
