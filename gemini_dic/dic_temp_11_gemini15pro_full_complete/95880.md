increasement:/ɪnˈkriːsmənt/ | n. |增加, 增量|The act or process of increasing; the amount by which something increases.|The increasement of taxes has angered the public. 税收的增加激怒了公众。|近义词: increase, rise, growth, augmentation |反义词: decrease, reduction, decline, diminution |词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *encresement, *encresment, from Old French encresment, encreissement, from encrescier (“to increase, grow”), from en- + creistre, creissier (“to grow”), from Latin crescere (“to grow, increase”).
