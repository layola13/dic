overpleases:/ˌoʊvərˈpliːziz/
verb
使过分高兴；过分取悦
To please excessively; to cater to someone's desires to an excessive degree.
He overpleased his guests with extravagant gifts and constant attention. 他用奢华的礼物和不断的关注过分取悦了他的客人。
The restaurant overpleased its customers by offering free desserts with every meal. 这家餐厅通过每餐都提供免费甜点来过分取悦顾客。
近义词: pamper, indulge, spoil
反义词: disappoint, neglect
词性: verb

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "please" (verb meaning "to cause pleasure or satisfaction")
