melodiousness:/mɛˈloʊdiəsnɛs/ /məˈləʊdiəsnɪs/
noun
优美；悦耳；旋律性
The quality of being melodious; sweetness of sound.
Her voice was filled with a captivating melodiousness. 她的声音充满了迷人的旋律。
The melodiousness of the music filled the air.  音乐的优美旋律充满了空气。
近义词:euphony, harmony, musicality, sweetness
反义词:discordance, dissonance, harshness, cacophony
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From melodious + -ness.  Melodious from melody + -ous. Melody from Middle French melodie, from Late Latin melodia, from Greek melōidia, from melos "song" + oidē "song".
