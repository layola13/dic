accountable:/əˈkaʊntəbl/| adj.|有责任的，应负责任的；可解释的|Subject to the obligation to report, explain, or justify something; responsible; answerable.|例句：The government should be accountable to the people. 政府应该对人民负责。|近义词：responsible, liable, answerable|反义词：unaccountable, irresponsible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *accontable, from Late Latin accomptabilis (“computable”), from accomptare (“to compute”), from Latin ad (“to”) + computare (“to compute”).
