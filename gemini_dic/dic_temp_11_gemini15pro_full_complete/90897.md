hunter-gatherers:/ˈhʌntər ˈɡæðərərz/ | n. |狩猎采集者| People who live by hunting wild animals and gathering wild plants, fruits, and nuts.| Early humans were hunter-gatherers. 早期人类是狩猎采集者。|  Hunter-gatherers moved from place to place in search of food. 狩猎采集者为了寻找食物而四处迁徙。| synonyms: foragers, nomads | antonyms: farmers, settlers | noun

hunter:/ˈhʌntər/ | n. |猎人| A person who hunts wild animals or game.| The hunter tracked the deer through the forest. 猎人追踪着穿过森林的鹿。| The hunter bagged a pheasant. 猎人猎获了一只野鸡。| synonyms: huntsman, sportsman | antonyms: prey, quarry | noun

gatherer:/ˈɡæðərər/ | n. |采集者| A person who gathers something, especially plants, fruits, etc., for food.| The gatherers collected berries and nuts. 采集者收集浆果和坚果。| The gatherers brought back a basket full of mushrooms. 采集者带回了一篮子蘑菇。| synonyms: collector, forager | antonyms: - | noun
