dichromic:/daɪˈkroʊmɪk/| adj.|二色的，双色的|Exhibiting dichromatism; having two colors.  Appearing different colors under different lighting conditions.|The dichromic mineral appeared green in sunlight but blue under incandescent light.  这种二色矿物在阳光下呈绿色，但在白炽灯下呈蓝色。|近义词:two-colored, bicoloured, iridescent|反义词:monochromatic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From di- (two) + -chromic (related to color), ultimately from Greek di- (two) + chrōma (color).
