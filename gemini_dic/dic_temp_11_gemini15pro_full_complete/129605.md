non-concur:/ˌnɒn kənˈkɜːr/ (US: /ˌnɑːn kənˈkɝː/)| v.|不同意，不一致，不并发|To disagree or not be in agreement; to refuse to approve or sanction.|The board non-concurred with the CEO's proposal.|近义词：disagree, dissent, object|反义词:agree, concur, approve|verb

non-concurrence:/ˌnɒn kənˈkʌrəns/ (US: /ˌnɑːn kənˈkɝːəns/)| n.|不一致，不同意，不并发|The lack of agreement or concurrence; a refusal to approve or sanction.|The non-concurrence of the committee stalled the project.|近义词：disagreement, dissent, objection|反义词:agreement, concurrence, approval|noun
