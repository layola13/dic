merlon:/ˈmɜːrlən/ |n.| 城垛；雉堞；|A solid upright section of a battlement alternating with an embrasure (open space).|The castle walls were topped with merlons and crenels. 城墙顶部有城垛和堞口。|Synonyms: battlement, crenel, parapet|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "gap" in the context of fortifications)|Noun
