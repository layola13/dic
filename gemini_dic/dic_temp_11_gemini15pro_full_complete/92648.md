ibis:/ˈaɪbɪs/| n.|朱鷺|A wading bird with a long down-curved bill, related to the spoonbill.|We saw an ibis fishing in the shallows. 我们看到一只朱鹭在浅滩捕鱼。|近义词：wading bird, shorebird|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些与其习性相反的鸟类，例如猛禽 birds of prey)|noun
