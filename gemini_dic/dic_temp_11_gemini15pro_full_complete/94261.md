impermeably:/ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbli/
adverb
不可渗透地；不透水地； непроницаемо
In a manner that is not permeable; so as to be impassable to fluids.
The virus was sealed impermeably inside the container. 病毒被密封在容器内，无法渗透。
The plastic sheeting covered the furniture impermeably. 塑料布将家具完全覆盖，不透水。
近义词: imperviously, impenetrably
反义词: permeably, porous
词性: adverb
