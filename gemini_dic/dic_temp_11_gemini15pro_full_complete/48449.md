deedless:/ˈdiːdlɪs/
adjective
无行动的；不作为的；无功绩的
Inactive; having performed no deeds; without achievement.
He lived a deedless life, contributing nothing to society.  他过着无所事事的生活，对社会没有任何贡献。
The knight felt ashamed of his deedless existence.  这位骑士为自己无所作为的存在感到羞愧。
近义词:inactive, idle, inert, feckless
反义词:active, productive, effective
词性:adjective

deedless:/ˈdiːdlɪs/
词源:From deed + -less.  由deed(行为，事迹) + -less(无，没有)构成。
