crampness:/ˈkræmpnəs/| n. |痉挛，抽筋|A state of being cramped; a painful involuntary contraction of a muscle or muscles.|The crampness in my calf muscle made it difficult to walk. 我小腿肌肉的痉挛使我难以行走。|Synonyms: tightness, stiffness, spasm, contraction|Antonyms: looseness, relaxation|Noun
