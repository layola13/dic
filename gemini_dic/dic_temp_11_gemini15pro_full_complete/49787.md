demonist:/ˈdiːmənɪst/| n.|恶魔崇拜者，信奉恶魔的人|A person who believes in demons or worships them. |例句：The villagers believed the old woman was a demonist and shunned her. 村民们认为老妇人是恶魔崇拜者，并避开她。

例句：In the fantasy novel, the demonist summoned a powerful creature from the underworld. 在这部奇幻小说中，恶魔崇拜者从地下世界召唤出一个强大的生物。|近义词：devil worshipper, Satanist|反义词:theist, believer (in a benevolent god)|词性:noun
