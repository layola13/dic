nonparticulate:/ˌnɒnpɑːˈtɪkjʊlət/ |adj.|非颗粒状的，非微粒的|Not consisting of particles; not particulate.  Relating to or denoting a pollutant that is not in the form of particles, such as a gas.|The nonparticulate pollutants in the air, such as ozone and nitrogen oxides, can also cause respiratory problems.| 近义词: non-granular, continuous | 反义词: particulate, granular| adjective
