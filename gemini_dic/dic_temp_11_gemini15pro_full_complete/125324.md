multijugous: /ˌmʌltɪˈdʒuːɡəs/ | adj. | 多轭的；多对小叶的 | Having many pairs of leaflets (in botany). Refers to a compound leaf structure where the leaf is divided into multiple pairs of leaflets.  | Example: The multijugous leaves of the plant were a striking feature. 这种植物的多对小叶的叶子是一个显著的特征。 Example:  The botanist identified the species by its characteristic multijugous foliage.  植物学家通过其特有的多对小叶的叶子识别了该物种。 | Near Synonyms: pinnate, compound | Antonyms: simple (leaf) | Word Type: adjective
