mahonia:/məˈhoʊniə/ | n. |十大功劳属植物；十大功劳|A genus of evergreen shrubs belonging to the barberry family, native to North America and eastern Asia, having spiny leaves and clusters of yellow flowers followed by blue-black berries.| The mahonia in my garden is blooming beautifully. 我花园里的十大功劳开得很漂亮。|Synonyms: Oregon grape, hollygrape|Antonyms: None|Noun


mahonia:/məˈhoʊniə/ | n. |十大功劳 (尤指该属植物的果实)|The berry of the mahonia plant.| The mahonia berries are edible but quite tart. 十大功劳的果实可以食用，但很酸。|Synonyms: Oregon grape, hollygrape|Antonyms: None|Noun
