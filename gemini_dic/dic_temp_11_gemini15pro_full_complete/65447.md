eurasian:/jʊˈreɪʒən/ /jʊˈreɪʃən/
adjective|欧亚的；欧亚大陆的|Relating to or characteristic of both Europe and Asia, or the people of this region.|例句：Eurasian trade routes have existed for centuries. 欧亚贸易路线已经存在了几个世纪。|近义词：Euro-Asian|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be specific continental like European, Asian)|词性: adjective

eurasian:/jʊˈreɪʒən/ /jʊˈreɪʃən/
noun|欧亚混血儿|A person of mixed European and Asian descent.|例句：She identifies as Eurasian, embracing both her European and Asian heritage. 她认为自己是欧亚混血儿，同时拥抱她的欧洲和亚洲血统。|近义词：mixed-race (more general)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
