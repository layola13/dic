bullshitty:/ˈbʊlʃɪti/| adj.|胡说八道的，废话连篇的，不真实的|Of poor quality, untrue, or insincere; nonsense.|例句：That's a bullshitty excuse! 那真是个胡扯的借口！|例句：He gave a bullshitty explanation for his absence. 他对他的缺席给出了一个不靠谱的解释。|近义词：bogus, phony, spurious, nonsensical|反义词：genuine, authentic, truthful|词性:形容词|词源:From "bullshit" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Bullshit" itself likely originated in the early 20th century, combining "bull" (meaning nonsense) and "shit" (excrement), emphasizing the worthless nature of the thing described.|
