essayer:/ɛseje/ (French), /ɛˈseɪ/ (anglicized)| v. |尝试，试验|To attempt or try something; to make an effort to do something.  Derived from Old French "essaier" meaning "to test, try". Ultimately from Latin "exagium" meaning "weighing, a trial".| I will essayer to bake a cake today. 我今天要尝试烤一个蛋糕。| try, attempt, test | refrain, avoid | verb
