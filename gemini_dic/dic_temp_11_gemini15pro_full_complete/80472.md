gonochorism: /ˌɡɒnəˈkɔːrɪzəm/ | n. | 雌雄异体; 雌雄异株 | The separation of male and female reproductive organs in different individuals. | Most mammals exhibit gonochorism. 大多数哺乳动物都表现出雌雄异体。|  Gonochorism is a common reproductive strategy in many animal species. 雌雄异体是许多动物物种中常见的繁殖策略。 | synonyms: dioecy (in plants), bisexuality (opposite in a way) | antonyms: hermaphroditism, monoecy (in plants) | noun
