outmigration:/ˌaʊtmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/
n.
向外迁移，迁出
The outmigration from rural areas to cities is a common phenomenon.
农村地区向城市的迁出是一个普遍现象。
The region experienced significant outmigration due to the economic downturn.
由于经济衰退，该地区经历了大量人口迁出。
emigration, exodus
immigration, influx
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + migration.
