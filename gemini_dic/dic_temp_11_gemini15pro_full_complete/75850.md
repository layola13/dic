funambulist:/fəˈnæmbjʊlɪst/
n.
走钢丝者；走绳索的人
A person who walks on a tightrope or slack rope.
The funambulist gracefully crossed the high wire.  走钢丝者优雅地走过了高空钢丝。
The funambulist captivated the audience with their daring feats.  走钢丝者以其大胆的技艺吸引了观众。
Synonyms: tightrope walker, ropewalker, acrobat
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *funambulus, from Latin funis (“rope”) + ambulare (“to walk”).
