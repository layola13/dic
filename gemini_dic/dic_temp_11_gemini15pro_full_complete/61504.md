embroilment:/ɪmˈbrɔɪlmənt/
n.
纠纷，卷入，牵连
the state of being involved in a difficult or complicated situation, especially a dispute or quarrel.
The country's embroilment in the war lasted for years.  这个国家卷入战争持续了数年。
The two families were embroiled in a bitter feud. 这两个家族卷入了激烈的世仇。
近义词：involvement, entanglement, complication
反义词：disentanglement, extrication
词性：名词

Etymology:
From French *embrouillement*, from *embrouiller* "to confuse, mix up," from en- "in" + brouiller "to mix, stir," of uncertain origin.
