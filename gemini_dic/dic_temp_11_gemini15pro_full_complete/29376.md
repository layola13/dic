casually:/ˈkæʒuəli/ /ˈkæʒjʊəli/| adv.|随意地，随便地，偶然地|In a relaxed or informal manner; without giving much attention or effort.|She casually mentioned she was moving to Paris. 她漫不经心地提到她要搬去巴黎了。|Synonyms: informally, nonchalantly, effortlessly, offhandedly|Antonyms: formally, deliberately, carefully, intentionally|词性:副词

casually:/ˈkæʒuəli/ /ˈkæʒjʊəli/| adj.|非正式的，随便的|Characterized by informality and lack of seriousness.|He wore a casually elegant outfit. 他穿着一套休闲而优雅的服装。|Synonyms: informal, relaxed, easygoing|Antonyms: formal, serious, stiff|词性:形容词
