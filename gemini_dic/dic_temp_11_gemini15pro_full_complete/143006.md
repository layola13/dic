outbellow:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊ/
verb
发出比…更大的声音；胜过
to shout louder than; to outdo in loudness
The angry protesters outbellowed the police, their chants echoing through the streets. 抗议者愤怒的喊叫声盖过了警察，他们的口号声在街道上回荡。
The opera singer easily outbellowed the rest of the cast.  这位歌剧演唱家轻易地盖过了其他演员的声音。
synonym: outshout, drown out, overpower
antonym: whisper, murmur, mumble
词性: 动词
