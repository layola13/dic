ambivalent:/æmˈbɪvələnt/
adjective
矛盾的； ambivalent about
Having mixed feelings or contradictory ideas about something or someone.
She was ambivalent about accepting the job offer. 她对于接受这份工作邀约感到矛盾。
近义词: uncertain, hesitant, unsure, doubtful, indecisive
反义词: certain, sure, decisive, resolute, convicted
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin ambi- 'both' + valentem (present participle of valēre 'be strong').
