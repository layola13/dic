indissolvableness:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒlvəb(ə)lnəs/ |n.| 不可溶解性；牢固性；永久性|The indissolvableness of their marriage was important to them.  他们的婚姻牢不可破对他们很重要。|The indissolvableness of the chemical bond makes it very stable.  这种化学键的不可溶解性使其非常稳定。|近义词: permanence, stability, inseparability|反义词: dissolvability, instability, separability|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + dissolvable + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Dissolvable comes from dissolve (from Latin dissolvere, from dis- (apart) + solvere (to loosen)).
