gelatinization:/dʒəˌlætɪnɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |糊化，胶凝化|The process by which starch granules absorb water and swell, forming a viscous solution or gel.| The gelatinization of the starch is crucial for the texture of the pudding.| 近义词:gelling, thickening | 反义词:liquefaction | noun

gelatinize:/dʒəˈlætɪnaɪz/ | v. |糊化，胶凝|To convert into gelatin or a gelatinous form.| The cornstarch gelatinized when heated with the milk. | 近义词:gel, thicken, congeal | 反义词:liquefy, thin | verb

词源: From French gélatinisation, from gélatine (gelatin) + -isation (-ization). Ultimately from Latin gelare (to freeze).
