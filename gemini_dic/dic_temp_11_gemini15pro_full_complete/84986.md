happy-go-lucky: /ˌhæpi ɡoʊ ˈlʌki/ | adj. | 无忧无虑的; 随遇而安的 | carefree and easygoing; not worrying about the future | He has a happy-go-lucky attitude to life. 他对生活有一种无忧无虑的态度。|  She's a happy-go-lucky person, always smiling. 她是个无忧无虑的人，总是面带微笑。 | 近义词: carefree, easygoing, lighthearted, optimistic | 反义词: anxious, worried, pessimistic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  The phrase originated in the early 19th century, likely as a descriptive term for someone with a cheerful and unconcerned disposition.  The "go-lucky" part might be related to the idea of relying on luck or chance.
