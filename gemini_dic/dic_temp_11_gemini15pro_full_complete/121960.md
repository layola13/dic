missioning:/ˈmɪʃənɪŋ/
n.|派遣; 赋予使命;|The act of assigning a mission or task to someone or something.|The missioning of the spacecraft was successful.  航天器的派遣很成功。|近义词：deployment, assignment, commissioning|反义词: recall, withdrawal|词性:名词

Etymology: From "mission" + "-ing" suffix, ultimately from Latin "missio" (sending).
