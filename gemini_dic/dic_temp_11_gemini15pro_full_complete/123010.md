monarchy: /ˈmɒnəki/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnərki/ (US)| n. |君主制，君主政体；君主国|A system of government in which one person reigns, usually a king or queen.  The monarch may have absolute power or limited power sharing with a legislature. |The United Kingdom is a constitutional monarchy. 英国是一个君主立宪制国家。

The monarchy has ruled this country for centuries.  这个国家由君主统治了几个世纪。| 近义词: kingdom, empire, sovereignty | 反义词: republic, democracy | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *monarchie*, from Latin monarchia, from Ancient Greek μοναρχία (monarkhía, “rule by one”), from μόνος (mónos, “single, alone”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule”).
