gelable:/ˈdʒɛləbl/ | adj. | 可凝胶的 |  Capable of forming a gel.  This describes a substance that can transition from a liquid to a gel-like state. | This new material is gelable under certain conditions. 这种新材料在特定条件下可以凝胶。| The pectin is gelable when mixed with sugar and acid. 果胶与糖和酸混合后可凝胶。 |  gelling, solidifying, coagulating | liquefying, melting, dissolving | adjective
