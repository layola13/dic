greatish: /ˈɡreɪtɪʃ/ | adj. | 相当好的，还不错的 | Moderately great; fairly good. |  The food was greatish, not amazing, but good enough.  这食物还不错，不算惊艳，但足够好了。 |  例句：The movie was greatish.  这部电影还不错。| 近义词: good, decent, reasonable, acceptable | 反义词: terrible, awful, poor, bad | 词性:形容词

词源: great + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat" or "approximately".
