dedecoration:/diːˌdekəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 去除装饰；装饰的去除 | The dedecoration of the room made it look bare and uninviting.  房间的装饰被去除后，看起来光秃秃的，一点也不吸引人。|  The dedecoration process involved stripping the wallpaper and removing the ornate light fixtures. 去除装饰的过程包括剥掉壁纸和移除华丽的灯具。 | 近义词: stripping, removal | 反义词: decoration, ornamentation | 词性: noun

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning removal or reversal) + "decoration"
