confusions:/kənˈfjuːʒənz/ | n. |困惑，混乱，混淆|  A state of being unclear or uncertain; a lack of understanding; The act of mixing things up so they are difficult to distinguish.| The confusions surrounding the new tax law led to many errors. 新税法的混乱导致了很多错误。| synonyms: uncertainties, misunderstandings, bewilderment, disorientation, perplexities | antonyms: clarity, certainty, order, understanding | noun
