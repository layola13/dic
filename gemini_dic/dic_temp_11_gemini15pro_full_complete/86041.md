healthily:/ˈhelθɪli/| adv.|健康地|In a healthy manner; in a way that promotes good health.|She eats healthily and exercises regularly. 她健康饮食，经常锻炼。|近义词：well, fitly|反义词:unhealthily, poorly|词性:副词
词源: From health + -ly.  Ultimately from Old English *hǣlþu.
