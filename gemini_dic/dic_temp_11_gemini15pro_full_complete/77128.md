gasalier:/ˌɡæsəˈlɪr/
n.
煤气吊灯
An ornate hanging light fixture, usually with multiple branches, designed to be fueled by gas.
The grand ballroom was illuminated by a magnificent gasalier.  宏伟的舞厅被一盏华丽的煤气吊灯照亮。

The gasalier cast flickering shadows on the walls. 煤气吊灯在墙上投下闪烁的阴影。

近义词:chandelier, light fixture
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for a specific type of lighting)
词性:noun
