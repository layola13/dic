blarney:/ˈblɑːrni/
n.|奉承；甜言蜜语；胡说八道|Flattery; smooth, persuasive talk; nonsense.|He used a lot of blarney to get what he wanted. 他用了很多甜言蜜语才得到他想要的。|近义词：flattery, cajolery, sweet talk, nonsense|反义词：honesty, frankness|词性:名词

blarneys:/ˈblɑːrniz/
n.|奉承；甜言蜜语；胡说八道（blarney的复数形式，较少使用）|Multiple instances of flattery or smooth talk.|He showered her with blarneys, but she saw through his deception. 他对她说了很多甜言蜜语，但她看穿了他的欺骗。|近义词：flatteries, cajoleries, sweet talks|反义词：honesty, frankness|词性:名词
