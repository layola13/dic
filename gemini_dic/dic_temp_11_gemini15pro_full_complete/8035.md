n.|人类世|Relating to or denoting the current geological age, viewed as the period during which human activity has been the dominant influence on climate and the environment.|The Anthropocene epoch is marked by widespread human impact on Earth's geology and ecosystems.|Synonym: Human Age|Antonym: Holocene|Noun

/ˈæn.θrə.pə.siːn/ | /ˈæn.θrə.pəˌsiːn/
adj.|人类世的|Relating to or denoting the current geological age, viewed as the period during which human activity has been the dominant influence on climate and the environment.|Scientists debate the official start of the Anthropocene era. |Synonym: Human-influenced|Antonym: Pre-human|Adjective
