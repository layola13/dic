gainsaying:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪɪŋ/
n.
否认，反驳
the act of denying or contradicting something.
There is no gainsaying the fact that he is a talented musician.  无可否认，他是一位才华横溢的音乐家。
近义词：denial, contradiction, disputation, contesting
反义词：agreement, acceptance, acknowledgment, confirmation
词性:名词


gainsaying:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪɪŋ/
adj.
无可争辩的，不可否认的
that cannot be denied or contradicted.
His talent is gainsaying. 他的才华是无可争辩的。
近义词：undeniable, indisputable, irrefutable, unquestionable
反义词：disputable, questionable, debatable, contestable
词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *gaynsayng, *gaynseyng, from Old English *gēansecg, equivalent to gain- + saying. Cognate with Dutch geinzeggen (“to contradict, deny”), German gegensprechen (“to contradict”).
