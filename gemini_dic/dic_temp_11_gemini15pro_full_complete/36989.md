columbary:/ˈkɒləmˌbɛəri/ (US) /kəˈlʌmbəri/ (UK)| n.|鸽舍，骨灰存放处|A structure, especially one with niches, for keeping pigeons or doves, or a place for storing urns containing cremated remains.|The monks kept pigeons in the columbary behind the monastery. 修道士们把鸽子养在修道院后面的鸽舍里。|The columbary offered a peaceful resting place for the ashes of loved ones. 骨灰存放处为亲人的骨灰提供了一个宁静的安息之所。|synonyms: dovecote, pigeon house, cinerarium, niche|antonyms: N/A|noun
