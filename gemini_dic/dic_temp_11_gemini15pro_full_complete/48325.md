noun
公开谴责，强烈反对
a public condemnation of something
例句：The politician's decrial of the new law was met with applause.  这位政治家对新法律的公开谴责赢得了掌声。

例句：Her decrial of the government's policies was widely reported. 她对政府政策的强烈反对被广泛报道。

synonym
denunciation, condemnation, criticism, attack, censure
antonym
praise, commendation, approval, endorsement, support
词性
n.
