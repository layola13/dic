luge:/luːʒ/| n. v.|雪橇，平底雪橇；乘雪橇滑行|A sport in which one sleds down a track of ice or snow, lying face up and feet first.  The sled used in this sport. |例句：He competed in the luge at the Winter Olympics. 他参加了冬奥会的雪橇比赛。

例句：She's learning to luge down the hill. 她正在学习滑下山坡。|近义词：sled, toboggan|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用ascend(上升)表示相反的动作)|名词, 动词
