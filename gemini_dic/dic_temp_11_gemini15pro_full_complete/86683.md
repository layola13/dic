hellishly:/ˈhɛlɪʃli/| adv.|极其地，非常糟糕地，可怕地|Resembling or characteristic of hell; extremely unpleasant.  It intensifies the adjective or adverb it modifies to a very high degree, implying a connection to the negative connotations of hell.|例句：The traffic was hellishly congested. 交通拥堵得可怕。|近义词：infernal, devilish, fiendish, extremely, terribly, awfully|反义词:heavenly, divinely, wonderfully|词性 副词
词源: From "hellish" + "-ly." "Hellish" comes from "hell" (Old English *hel) + "-ish."
