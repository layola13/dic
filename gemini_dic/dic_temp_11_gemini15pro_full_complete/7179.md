anginose:/ˈændʒɪˌnəʊs/ /ˈændʒɪˌnoʊs/| adj.|心绞痛的；似心绞痛的|Resembling or characteristic of angina pectoris; marked by choking or suffocating sensations.|例句：He experienced anginose pain in his chest. 他胸口感到心绞痛般的疼痛。|例句：The patient described an anginose feeling in his throat. 病人描述喉咙有窒息感。|近义词：sore, painful, constricting|反义词:painless, comfortable|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Modern Latin *anginosus, from Latin angina "quinsy, angina," from Greek ankhein "to strangle, choke."
