millenarian:/ˌmɪləˈnɛəriən/| adj. n.|千禧年的，千年一次的；千禧年主义者|Relating to a millennium, especially to a belief in a future golden age of peace and prosperity.  A person who believes in millenarianism. |例句：The preacher's sermons were filled with millenarian prophecies.  牧师的布道充满了千禧年的预言。

The group was identified as a millenarian cult.  这个团体被认定为一个千禧年邪教。|近义词：chiliastic, apocalyptic|反义词:secular|词性:adjective, noun

millenary:/mɪˈlɛnəri/| n. adj.|千禧年，一千年；千禧年的|A period of one thousand years; relating to a period of one thousand years.|例句：We are celebrating the millenary of the town's founding. 我们正在庆祝该镇成立一千周年。

The millenary celebrations lasted for a week.  千禧年庆典持续了一周。|近义词：millennium|反义词: |词性:noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *millenarius, from Latin mille "thousand".  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sm̥-gʰesli- "one thousand".
