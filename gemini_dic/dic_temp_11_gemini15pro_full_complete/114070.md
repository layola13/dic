magneto:/mæɡˈniːtoʊ/ | n. | 磁发电机，磁电机 |A small electrical generator that uses a permanent magnet to produce alternating current, especially one used in the ignition system of some internal-combustion engines. | The magneto on my motorcycle needs replacing. 我的摩托车的磁电机需要更换。|  The old tractor wouldn't start because the magneto was faulty.  这辆旧拖拉机启动不了，因为磁电机坏了。 | synonyms: generator, alternator, dynamo | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *magnéto*, from *magnéto-électrique* "magneto-electric," a shortening of *machine magnéto-électrique*, from *magnétique* "magnetic" (see magnetic) + *électrique* "electric" (see electric).
