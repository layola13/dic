damns:/dæmz/
| v.| 诅咒；谴责；使注定失败 |A strong expression of anger or frustration, or to condemn someone or something to a bad fate. | He damns the government for its inaction. 他谴责政府不作为。|  condemns, curses | blesses, praises | verb


damns:/dæmz/
| n.|  诅咒；咒骂 |  An expression of anger or frustration.  (Less common as a noun) | His damns were loud and frequent. 他的咒骂声又响又频繁。 | curses, condemnations | blessings, praises | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dēman "to judge, deem," from Proto-Germanic *dōmijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- "to set, put."  The sense of "condemn to hell" developed later.
