adipocerous:/ˌædɪpəˈsɪərəs/
adjective
尸蜡的，脂肪蜡状的
Resembling or consisting of adipocere, a waxy substance formed from the decomposition of body fat.
The body was found in an adipocerous state.  这具尸体被发现时呈尸蜡状。
The adipocerous remains were difficult to identify.  这些尸蜡化的遗骸很难辨认。
近义词: waxy, fatty
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider using descriptions like "decomposed" or "putrefied" for contrasting states of decay)
词性: adjective
