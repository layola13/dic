lieutenants:/luːˈtenənts/ /ljuːˈtenənts/| n.|中尉；副官；助理；代理人|An officer in the army, navy, or air force, ranking immediately below a captain or, in the US air force, a major.  A person who acts as a deputy or substitute for another.|The lieutenant led his platoon into battle. 中尉带领他的排投入战斗。|近义词：deputy, assistant, representative|反义词:leader, superior|noun
