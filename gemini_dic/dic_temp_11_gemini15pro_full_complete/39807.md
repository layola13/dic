consortium's:/kənˈsɔːrʃiəmz/
noun
财团的，联合体的
belonging to or associated with a consortium (a group of companies or organizations that have joined together for a specific purpose)
The consortium's proposal was accepted by the government. 
财团的提案被政府接受了。
Synonyms: syndicate's, alliance's, coalition's
Antonyms: individual's, lone's, separate's
