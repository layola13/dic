否定句: (Malgré is rarely used in negative sentences.  It emphasizes the presence of an obstacle, not its absence.)

注意事项:  Malgré is a more formal and less common preposition than "despite" or "in spite of".  It is more frequently used in written than spoken English, and retains a somewhat French flavor.  It is not typically used in phrases or idioms.
