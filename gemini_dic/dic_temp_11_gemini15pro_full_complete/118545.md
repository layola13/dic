mesomyodian:/ˌmiːzəˈmaɪəʊdiən/ | adj. | 中肌的，中肌型的 | Relating to or denoting the Mesomyaria, a suborder of sea anemones characterized by a well-developed retractor muscle system. |  Mesomyodian anemones are known for their ability to quickly retract their tentacles. 中肌海葵以其快速收缩触手的能力而闻名。| Near Synonyms: Actiniarian, Anthozoan | Antonyms: None | Adjective
