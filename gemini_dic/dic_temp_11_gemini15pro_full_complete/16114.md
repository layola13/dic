bane:/beɪn/
noun
祸根，灾星，毒药
A cause of great distress or annoyance.
例句：The bane of his existence was his noisy neighbor.  他生活中最大的祸根就是他那吵闹的邻居。

例句：Traffic jams are the bane of city life. 交通堵塞是城市生活的祸根。

近义词：curse, affliction, scourge, plague, pest
反义词：blessing, boon, advantage

词性: n.

Etymology: From Old English *bana "murderer, slayer," from Proto-Germanic *banon "that which kills," from PIE root *gwhen- "to strike, kill."
