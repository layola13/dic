interchoked:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊkt/
adjective
互相扼住的，互相阻塞的
mutually choked or obstructed; strangled
The roots of the two trees were so interchoked that neither could grow properly.  两棵树的根互相缠绕，以至于都不能正常生长。
intertwined, entangled, interlocked
free, separate
词性：形容词
