centrode:/ˈsɛntrɔɪd/| n.|质心，形心|The point at which the lines of action of forces intersect, especially of the weight of a body or system.|The centrode of the pendulum is located just below the bob.|Synonym: center of mass, center of gravity|Antonym: N/A|noun
