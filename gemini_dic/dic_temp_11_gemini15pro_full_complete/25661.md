bung:/bʌŋ/
noun|塞子, 栓, 桶塞; (尤指啤酒桶的) 塞子|A bung is a stopper, especially one for closing a cask or barrel.|The bung popped out of the wine barrel.  酒桶的塞子蹦了出来。|stopper, cork, plug|lid, cap|n.
bung:/bʌŋ/
verb|塞住, 堵住(孔); 扔, 丢, 抛|To close with a bung; throw or toss casually.|He bunged the bottle with a cork. 他用软木塞塞住了瓶子。|close, seal, stop up|open, unseal|v.
