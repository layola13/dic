ivoried:/ˈaɪvərid/
adjective
象牙色的；乳白色的；用象牙装饰的
Made of or resembling ivory; having a creamy white color.
The king sat on an ivoried throne. 国王坐在象牙宝座上。
Near Synonyms: ivory, creamy, off-white, bone-colored
Antonyms: dark, black, ebony
词性: 形容词
