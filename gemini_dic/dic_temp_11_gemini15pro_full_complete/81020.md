gradually:/ˈɡrædʒuəli/| adv.|逐渐地，渐渐地|in a gradual way; slowly over a period of time|The weather gradually got warmer. 天气逐渐变暖了。|例句：The pain gradually subsided. 疼痛逐渐消退了。|近义词：slowly, progressively, steadily|反义词：suddenly, abruptly, rapidly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gradualis*, from Latin gradus (“step, degree”) + -alis.
