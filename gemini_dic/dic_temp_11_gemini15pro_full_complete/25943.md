burp:/bɜːrp/ (US) /bɜːp/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|打嗝，嗳气|The act of expelling air from the stomach through the mouth, often with a characteristic sound.  |He let out a loud burp after drinking the soda. 他喝完汽水后打了一个响亮的嗝。|She tried to suppress a burp during the meeting.  她在会议期间试图抑制打嗝。|近义词：belch|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun, verb
