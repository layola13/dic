inflaters:/ɪnˈfleɪtərz/
n.|充气机；充气泵；打气筒|A device used to inflate something, such as a tire or balloon.|He used an electric inflater to pump up the tires. 他用电动充气泵给轮胎打气。|Synonyms: pump, air pump|Antonyms: deflator|noun

inflaters:/ɪnˈfleɪtərz/
n.|（使某物）膨胀的人或物|Something or someone that causes something to inflate.|The Federal Reserve's policies were seen as the main inflater of the housing bubble. 美联储的政策被认为是房地产泡沫的主要推手。|Synonyms: expander, amplifier|Antonyms: contractor, reducer|noun
