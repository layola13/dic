In rhetoric, it refers to an added word or phrase, often expressing emotion or emphasis.  |例句：The epithema on the leaves of the plant glistened in the sun.  植物叶子上的滴液在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：The speaker used the epithema "alas" to express his sorrow. 演讲者用插入语“唉”来表达他的悲伤。|近义词：exudate, secretion, effusion; interjection, parenthesis |反义词:  (No direct antonym for the botanical sense).  |词性: Noun
