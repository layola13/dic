llanero: /ljɑːˈneɪroʊ/ /ʝaˈneɾo/
noun
牛仔，牧人；拉诺斯草原居民
A cowboy or herdsman of the llanos of Venezuela and eastern Colombia.
The llanero skillfully rode his horse across the vast plains.  牛仔熟练地骑着马穿过广阔的平原。
The llaneros are known for their horsemanship and traditional music. 拉诺斯草原居民以其精湛的骑术和传统音乐而闻名。
synonyms: cowboy, herdsman, gaucho
antonyms: city dweller


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish llanero, from llano (“plain”) + -ero (masculine agent suffix). Ultimately from Latin planus (“flat”).
