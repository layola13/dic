endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/| n. |捐赠，天赋，才能|Something that is bestowed; a gift.  The act of bestowing or investing.  Natural abilities or qualities.|例句：The university received a large endowment from a wealthy alumnus. 这所大学收到了一位富有校友的大笔捐赠。

例句：She has a natural endowment for music. 她有音乐天赋。|近义词：gift, grant, donation, talent, aptitude, faculty|反义词：deprivation, lack|词性: noun

endow:/ɪnˈdaʊ/| vt. |捐赠，赋予|To provide with a permanent fund or source of income.  To furnish, as with some talent, faculty, or quality; equip.|例句：The philanthropist endowed the hospital with a new wing.  这位慈善家为医院捐赠了一个新侧楼。

例句：Nature has endowed her with both beauty and intelligence. 大自然赋予了她美貌和智慧。|近义词：bestow, donate, grant, provide, furnish|反义词：deprive, divest|词性: verb
