chastise:/ˈtʃæstaɪz/ (US), /tʃæˈstaɪz/ (UK)| vt.| 惩罚；责备；严厉批评|To rebuke or reprimand severely; to punish, especially by beating.|The teacher chastised the student for disrupting class. 老师责备了扰乱课堂秩序的学生。

The coach chastised the team for their lack of effort. 教练因队员缺乏努力而严厉批评了他们。|近义词：reprimand, rebuke, scold, punish, castigate|反义词：praise, commend, reward|词性: verb
