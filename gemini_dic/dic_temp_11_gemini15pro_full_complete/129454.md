non-addictive:/ˌnɒn əˈdɪktɪv/ /ˌnɑːn əˈdɪktɪv/| adj. |非成瘾性的|Not causing addiction; unable to cause a habit or compulsion.|This new pain reliever is marketed as non-addictive.  这种新的止痛药被宣传为非成瘾性的。|This game is fun and non-addictive, perfect for casual play.  这个游戏很有趣，而且不会上瘾，非常适合休闲娱乐。|近义词：non-habit-forming, harmless|反义词：addictive, habit-forming|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + addictive (from addict + -ive)
