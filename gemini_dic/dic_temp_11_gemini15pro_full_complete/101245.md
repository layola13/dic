invocate:/ˈɪnvəˌkeɪt/| v.|祈求，恳求，调用|To call upon (a deity or spirit) for aid, protection, inspiration, or the like; to appeal to or cite as authority.|The shaman invocated the spirits of the ancestors.  萨满祈求祖先的灵魂。|近义词：invoke, appeal to, call upon, conjure, beseech|反义词: disregard, ignore|词性:动词

词源:
From Late Latin *invocatus*, past participle of *invocare* "to call upon, invoke," from *in-* "upon" + *vocare* "to call."
