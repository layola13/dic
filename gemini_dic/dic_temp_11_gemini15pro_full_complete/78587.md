giantism:/ˈdʒaɪənˌtɪzəm/
n.
巨大症；巨人症
Excessive growth and height, especially due to overproduction of growth hormone in childhood.
The boy's giantism was diagnosed early, allowing doctors to intervene.  这个男孩的巨人症很早就被诊断出来了，这让医生得以进行干预。
synonyms:  acromegaly, gigantism, macrosomia
antonyms: dwarfism
词性: noun
