aisles:/aɪlz/| n. |通道，走廊|A passage between rows of seats in a building such as a church or theater, an airplane, or between rows of shelves in a supermarket.|例句：The bride walked down the aisle.  新娘沿着通道走过来。

例句：I prefer window seats to aisle seats on airplanes.  在飞机上，我喜欢靠窗的座位而不是靠过道的座位。|近义词：passageways, corridors, walkways|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑：rows, walls)|词性:noun
