japonica:/dʒəˈpɒnɪkə/ | adj. n.|日本的，日本产的；山茶|Of or relating to Japan, or native to Japan. Also, a specific epithet for species originating in Japan, often used in taxonomy. |The Camellia japonica is a beautiful flowering plant. 日本山茶是一种美丽的开花植物。| The japonica rice is a short-grain variety.  日本粳稻是一种短粒品种。|近义词：Japanese|反义词:|adjective, noun
