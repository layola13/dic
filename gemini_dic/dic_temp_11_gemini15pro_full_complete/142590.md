osteogenic:/ˌɒstiəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 成骨的，生骨的 | Relating to or capable of producing bone. |  Osteogenic cells are responsible for bone formation. 成骨细胞负责骨骼的形成。

The implant was coated with an osteogenic material to promote bone growth.  这种植入物涂有一种成骨材料以促进骨骼生长。


|近义词：bone-forming, osteogenetic |反义词: osteoclastic |词性: adjective
