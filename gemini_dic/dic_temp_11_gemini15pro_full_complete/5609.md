alternate:/ˈɔːltərneɪt/ (US) /ˈɒltəneɪt/ (UK)| v.| 交替，轮流 | to happen or exist one after the other repeatedly | 例句：She alternates between hope and despair. 她时而充满希望，时而感到绝望。|近义词：rotate, interchange|反义词:persist, continue|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alternatus*, past participle of *alternare* "to do by turns," from *alter* "other (of two)."
