hanuman:/ˌhʌnʊˈmɑːn/ | n. |哈奴曼|A Hindu deity, the monkey god and devoted servant of Rama. |Hanuman is revered for his strength, devotion, and courage. 哈奴曼因其力量、奉献和勇气而受到尊敬。| Example: Hanuman leaped across the ocean to Lanka to find Sita. 哈奴曼飞跃大海到楞伽岛寻找悉多。| Synonyms:  monkey god,  Vayuputra | Antonyms:  demon,  rakshasa | noun

hanuman:/ˌhʌnʊˈmɑːn/ | n. |哈努曼|In the epic Ramayana, Hanuman is a central figure who helps Rama rescue his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana. | Statues of Hanuman are common throughout India and Southeast Asia. 哈奴曼的雕像在印度和东南亚很常见。 | Synonyms:  Rama's devotee,  son of Vayu | Antonyms:  Ravana,  enemy of Rama | noun
