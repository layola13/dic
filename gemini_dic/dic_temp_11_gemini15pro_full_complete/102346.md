ixtle: /ˈɪkstəl/, /ˈɪʃtlə/ | n. | 龙舌兰纤维, 伊克斯特尔纤维 | A strong fiber obtained from several species of Agave, used for making rope, brushes, etc. |  The ixtle brush is stiff but effective.  龙舌兰纤维刷子很硬，但很有效。 |  The ancient Mayans used ixtle to make rope and sandals.  古代玛雅人用龙舌兰纤维制作绳索和凉鞋。 | synonyms: fiber, agave fiber, sisal | antonyms: N/A | noun
