conviction:/kənˈvɪkʃən/ | n. | 定罪；确信；信念 |  A formal declaration that someone is guilty of a criminal offense, made by the verdict of a jury or the decision of a judge in a court of law.  A firmly held belief or opinion. |  He had a previous conviction for robbery.  (他先前有过抢劫罪的定罪记录。) She spoke with conviction. (她讲话很有说服力。) | synonyms:  belief, certainty, confidence, assurance, persuasion, sentence, judgment  | antonyms: doubt, uncertainty, disbelief, acquittal | noun
