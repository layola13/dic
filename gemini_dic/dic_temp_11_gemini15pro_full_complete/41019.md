copartnery:/koʊˈpɑːrtnəri/
n.
合伙；合伙企业；合伙关系
A partnership; joint ownership or operation of a business.
The brothers formed a copartnery to run the family farm. 兄弟俩成立了一个合伙企业来经营家族农场。
synonyms: partnership, alliance, association, collaboration, joint venture
antonyms: sole proprietorship, independence

copartnery:/koʊˈpɑːrtnəri/
n.
合伙人
Partners collectively. (Often treated as singular)
The copartnery decided to expand into new markets. 全体合伙人决定拓展新市场。
synonyms: partners, associates, collaborators
antonyms: competitor, opponent
