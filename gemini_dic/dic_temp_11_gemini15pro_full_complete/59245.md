e-float:/ˈiːfloʊt/ | n. | 电子浮筒; 电子浮子;  电子浮标 | An electronic device designed to float on water and transmit data, often used for monitoring water quality or tracking objects. |  The researchers deployed several e-floats in the ocean to gather data on currents and temperature. 研究人员在海洋中部署了几个电子浮筒来收集洋流和温度的数据。| synonyms: electronic buoy, floating sensor, telemetering buoy | antonyms: N/A | noun
