dissemblance:/dɪˈsɛmbləns/ | n. |不像，相异，伪装|lack of resemblance; pretense; feigning; hypocrisy| Example:  The dissemblance between their public personas and their private lives was striking.  他们公开形象和私生活的巨大差异令人吃惊。

Example: He used dissemblance to hide his true intentions. 他用伪装来掩盖他的真实意图。| 近义词: difference, disparity, dissimilitude, pretense, deception | 反义词: resemblance, similarity, likeness, honesty, candor | 词性: noun

Etymology:  Late Middle English: from Old French dissemblance, from dissembler ‘disguise’, based on Latin dissimulare ‘make unlike’, from dis- (expressing reversal) + simulare ‘simulate’.
