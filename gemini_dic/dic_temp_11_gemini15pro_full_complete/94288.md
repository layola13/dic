impertinence: /ɪmˈpɜːrtɪnəns/ /ɪmˈpɝːtənəns/ | n. | 无礼，鲁莽；不恰当 | Lack of respect; rudeness; being inappropriately bold. | Example:  His impertinence to the judge earned him a night in jail. 他对法官的无礼让他在监狱里待了一夜。

Example: She had the impertinence to interrupt the professor mid-lecture.  她竟然无礼地打断教授的讲课。

Synonyms: rudeness, insolence, impudence, disrespect, audacity

Antonyms: respect, courtesy, politeness, deference, humility

Etymology:  From Late Latin *impertinentia, from impertinent- ‘not pertinent’, from in- ‘not’ + pertinent- ‘pertaining to’.
