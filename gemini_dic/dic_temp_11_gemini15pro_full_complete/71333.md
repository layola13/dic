flabbily:/ˈflæbɪli/| adv.|松弛地；软弱无力地|in a flabby way; lacking firmness or resilience. |His arm waved flabbily. 他的手臂无力地挥舞着。|近义词：loosely, limply, weakly|反义词：firmly, tightly, strongly|词性:副词

flabby:/ˈflæbi/| adj.|松弛的；软弱无力的|lacking firmness or resilience; soft and yielding. |He has flabby muscles. 他的肌肉松弛。|近义词：loose, limp, weak|反义词：firm, tight, strong|词性:形容词

词源: From *flab* + *-y*.  *Flab* is likely of imitative origin, suggesting something soft and yielding.
