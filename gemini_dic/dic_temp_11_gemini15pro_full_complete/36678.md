collieries:/ˈkɒliəriz/
noun
煤矿（colliery的复数）
Coal mines; places where coal is extracted.
The collieries in the region employed thousands of men. 该地区的煤矿雇佣了数千名男子。
近义词:coal mines, pits
反义词:None (No direct opposite for a place of extraction)
词性:n. (noun)

colliery:/ˈkɒliəri/
noun
煤矿
A coal mine.
The colliery closed down after the last seam of coal was extracted.  在最后一层煤被开采出来后，煤矿关闭了。
近义词:coal mine, pit
反义词:None (No direct opposite for a place of extraction)
词性:n. (noun)
