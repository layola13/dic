misgovernment:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvənmənt/
n.| 施政不当；治理不善；腐败| Bad or dishonest administration of government.
The country suffered greatly under years of misgovernment.|这个国家在多年的腐败统治下遭受了巨大的苦难。
近义词: mismanagement, maladministration, corruption
反义词: good governance, sound administration
