liquidises:/ˈlɪkwɪˌdaɪzɪz/
verb
清算；变卖；将…变成现金；（用果汁机等）将…打成糊状
Converts assets into cash, typically by selling them; blends something into a liquid.
The company was forced to liquidise its assets to pay off its debts. 公司被迫变卖资产以偿还债务。
She liquidised the strawberries to make a smoothie. 她把草莓打成糊状做冰沙。
synonyms: sell off, cash in, dissolve, blend, purée
antonyms: invest, acquire, solidify
词性: 动词
