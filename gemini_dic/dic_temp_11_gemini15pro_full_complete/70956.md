fireguard:/ˈfaɪəɡɑːrd/
n.|防火罩，火炉围栏|A metal guard placed in front of a fireplace to prevent sparks or embers from flying out and causing a fire.  It can also refer to a person responsible for preventing and extinguishing fires.|We have a sturdy fireguard in front of our fireplace to protect the children.|近义词:firescreen, spark guard|反义词:(None - the absence of a fireguard doesn't have a direct antonym)|名词


fireguards:/ˈfaɪəɡɑːrdz/
n.|防火罩，火炉围栏（复数）|The plural form of fireguard, referring to multiple metal guards placed in front of fireplaces.|They sell a variety of fireguards at the hardware store.|近义词:firescreens, spark guards|反义词:(None)|名词

词源:
fire + guard.  "Fire" from Old English "fyr" meaning "fire". "Guard" from Old French "garder" meaning "to protect, keep, watch".
