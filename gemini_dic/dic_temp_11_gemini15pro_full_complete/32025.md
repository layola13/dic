cherbourg: /ˈʃɜːrbɜːrɡ/ (UK), /ˈʃɜːrbʊərɡ/ (US) | n. |瑟堡|A port in northwestern France, on the English Channel. It is the largest town in the department of Manche.  Historically and culturally important, it is a major cross-channel port, with ferries to and from the UK and Ireland.  |例句：The ferry sailed from Portsmouth to Cherbourg.  渡轮从朴茨茅斯航行到瑟堡。

例句：Cherbourg suffered heavy damage during World War II. 瑟堡在二战期间遭受了严重的破坏。 |近义词：port, harbor|反义词: (No direct antonym for a place name. Could consider inland, rural as antonyms to its characteristic as a port city.)|词性:noun
