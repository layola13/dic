antireformists:/ˌænti rɪˈfɔːrmɪsts/
| n. | 反改革者，反对改革的人 | People who oppose reforms or changes, especially in political or social systems. | The antireformists blocked the passage of the new law. 反改革者阻止了新法律的通过。 | The antireformists feared that change would threaten their power. 反对改革的人担心变革会威胁到他们的权力。 |  conservatives, reactionaries | reformers, progressives | Noun

antireformist:/ˌænti rɪˈfɔːrmɪst/
| adj. | 反改革的 | Opposing or resisting reform. | The antireformist movement gained momentum. 反改革运动势头强劲。 |  They took an antireformist stance on the issue. 他们在这个问题上采取了反改革的立场。 | conservative, reactionary | reformist, progressive | Adjective
