abligate: /ˈæblɪɡeɪt/
verb
强迫；迫使；使负有义务
to bind morally or legally; to obligate
例句：
He felt abligated to help his family. 他觉得有义务帮助他的家人。

The law abligates parents to send their children to school. 法律规定父母有义务送孩子上学。

近义词：oblige, compel, force, bind
反义词：exempt, excuse, release
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abligatus, past participle of abligare (“to tie up, bind fast, fasten, fetter”), from ab (“from, away”) + ligare (“to tie, bind”).
