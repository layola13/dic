co-operatively:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪvli/| adv.|合作地，协作地|in a way that involves mutual assistance in working toward a common goal.|例句：The team worked co-operatively to complete the project. 团队合作完成了这个项目。|近义词：collaboratively, jointly, together|反义词:independently, separately|词性:副词
Etymology: From "co-operative" + "-ly". "Co-operative" comes from "co-operate," which is from Latin "co-" (together) + "operari" (to work). The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs.
