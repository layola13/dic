half-successfully:/ˌhæf səkˈsɛsfəli/
| adv. | 部分成功地；半成功地 | Partly or incompletely successful.  | The project was half-successfully implemented, meeting some but not all of its goals.  该项目部分成功实施，实现了一些但并非所有目标。 | Example: He half-successfully climbed the mountain, reaching a high point but not the summit. 他攀登这座山只成功了一半，到达了一个高点，但没有到达山顶。 | Synonyms: partially successfully, moderately successfully, somewhat successfully | Antonyms: completely successfully, totally successfully, entirely successfully | word property: adverb

half:/hæf/
| adj. n. adv. | 一半的；一半 | One of two equal parts of a whole; 50%. | I ate half the pizza. 我吃了一半披萨。| Example:  Half of the students passed the exam. 一半学生通过了考试。 | Synonyms: one-half, moiety | Antonyms: whole, double | word property: adjective, noun, adverb

successfully:/səkˈsɛsfəli/
| adv. | 成功地 | In a successful manner; with success. | She successfully completed the marathon. 她成功地完成了马拉松。 | Example: The mission was successfully accomplished. 任务成功完成。 | Synonyms: triumphantly, effectively, efficiently | Antonyms: unsuccessfully, fruitlessly, vainly | word property: adverb
