headmold:/ˈhɛdməʊld/ (UK) /ˈhɛdˌmoʊld/ (US)| n.|头部模型，头模|A mold taken from the head, especially of a baby, usually to create a doll or other likeness.|The artist used a headmold of the child to sculpt a realistic portrait. 艺术家用孩子的头模雕刻了一幅逼真的肖像画。|Synonyms: head cast, mold of the head|Antonyms: N/A|noun
