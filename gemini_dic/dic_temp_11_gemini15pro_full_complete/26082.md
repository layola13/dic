bushwhacking:/ˈbʊʃˌwækɪŋ/
n.
伏击，丛林游击战
The act of ambushing someone from a concealed position in a wooded area, or traveling through dense woods or underbrush.
He went bushwhacking through the dense forest. 他在茂密的森林里进行丛林探险。

The guerrillas engaged in bushwhacking against the occupying forces. 游击队对占领军进行了丛林游击战。

近义词: ambushing, attacking, raiding
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "open warfare")
词性: noun, adjective (when used as "bushwhacking tactics")

词源:  From "bushwhack," a blend of "bush" and "whack" (to strike or cut with a sharp blow).  The original meaning referred to clearing a path through bushes.
