beachcomber:/ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊmər/ (US), /ˈbiːtʃˌkəʊmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|海滩拾荒者；海滩流浪汉|A person who walks along beaches looking for things that have been washed ashore, especially valuable or interesting things.|The beachcomber found an old message in a bottle. 海滩拾荒者在一个瓶子里发现了一张旧纸条。|Synonyms: scavenger, forager, collector|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'landowner', 'resident')|noun
