hatchettine:/ˈhætʃɪˌtiːn/| n. |斧石|A soft, yellowish-white, waxy hydrocarbon mineral, essentially a native paraffin. It is found in coal and lignite deposits.|Hatchettine was discovered in the early 19th century. 斧石是在19世纪早期被发现的.|Synonym: mineral wax, ozocerite|Antonym: N/A (lacks a direct opposite)|noun
