overdominate:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːmɪneɪt/
verb
过度支配，过分控制
To dominate excessively; to have overwhelming influence or control over.
The CEO overdominated the board, stifling any dissenting opinions.  首席执行官过度控制董事会，压制任何异议。
His desire for control overdominated their relationship. 他对控制的渴望过度支配了他们的关系。
近义词: overwhelm, overpower, control, dominate
反义词: submit, yield, concede
词性: v.

overdominance:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːmɪnəns/
noun
过度支配，过分控制
The state of dominating excessively; excessive influence or control.
The company's overdominance in the market stifled competition.  该公司在市场上的过度支配扼杀了竞争。
Her overdominance in the relationship led to its eventual breakdown. 她在恋爱关系中的过度支配最终导致了关系的破裂。
近义词: supremacy, ascendancy, dominance
反义词: subordination, subservience
词性: n.
