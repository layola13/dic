hadrosaurus:/ˌhædrəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |鸭嘴龙|A large duck-billed dinosaur of the late Cretaceous period in North America.|例句：The Hadrosaurus was a herbivore that lived during the Late Cretaceous period. 鸭嘴龙是生活在白垩纪晚期的食草动物。

例句：The museum's exhibit featured a complete Hadrosaurus skeleton.  博物馆的展览以一个完整的鸭嘴龙骨架为特色。|近义词：dinosaur, ornithopod|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἁδρός (hadrós, “bulky, thick”) + σαῦρος (saûros, “lizard”).
