left-wingism:/ˌleftˈwɪŋɪzəm/
n.
左翼主义
The belief in or support of the political ideals of the left wing.
Left-wingism advocates for social equality and government intervention in the economy.  左翼主义主张社会平等和政府干预经济。
The party's platform embraced left-wingism, advocating for increased social programs and wealth redistribution. 该党的纲领拥护左翼主义，主张增加社会项目和财富再分配。
近义词: socialism, radicalism, progressivism
反义词: right-wingism, conservatism
词性: noun
