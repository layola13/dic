festschrift:/ˈfɛstʃrɪft/ | n. |  纪念文集； Festschrift | A collection of essays or articles written by colleagues and admirers to honor a scholar, often upon retirement or a significant anniversary. |  A festschrift was published in honor of Professor Smith's 70th birthday.  史密斯教授70岁生日时，出版了一本纪念文集。|  commemorative volume, testimonial | N/A | noun

Etymology->
From German *Festschrift*, from *Fest* "festival, celebration" + *Schrift* "writing".
