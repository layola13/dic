answerphone:/ˈɑːnsəfəʊn/ (UK) /ˈænsərfəʊn/ (US)| n.| 留言机，电话答录机 |A machine that answers the telephone automatically and records messages from callers. |I left a message on her answerphone. 我在她的答录机上留言了。|Synonyms: answering machine, voicemail|Antonyms: None|Noun


answerphones:/ˈɑːnsəfəʊnz/ (UK) /ˈænsərfəʊnz/ (US)| n.| 留言机（复数）|Plural form of answerphone, referring to multiple answering machines.|Many businesses still use answerphones to take messages after hours. 许多企业在下班后仍然使用留言机来接收留言。|Synonyms: answering machines, voicemails|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "answer" + "phone".  "Answer" comes from Old English *andswaru and "phone" is a shortened form of "telephone" which comes from Greek tēle (far) + phōnē (sound).
