outspeeds:/ˌaʊtˈspiːdz/
verb
超过…的速度；比…更快
to move faster than someone or something else
例句：The new car easily outspeeds the old one. 这辆新车轻松地超过了旧车。

例句：The cheetah outspeeds all other land animals. 猎豹比所有其他陆地动物都快。

近义词：outrun, surpass, exceed
反义词：lag behind, trail

词性：v.


词源: out- + speed,  "out-" prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding," + "speed" meaning swiftness.
