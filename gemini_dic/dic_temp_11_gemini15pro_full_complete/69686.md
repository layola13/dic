fenestra:/fɪˈnɛstrə/
noun
窗，孔, (解剖学) 颅骨上的窗
an opening, especially in a bone, such as the fenestra ovalis of the ear.
The surgeon carefully examined the fenestra in the patient's skull.  外科医生仔细检查了病人颅骨上的窗。
Synonyms: aperture, opening, orifice, hole, gap
Antonyms: closure, blockage
