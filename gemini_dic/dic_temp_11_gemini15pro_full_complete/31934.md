cheka:/ˈtʃɛkə/| n. |契卡 (Cheka),  全俄肃反委员会|The Cheka was the first Soviet secret police organization. 契卡是苏联最早的秘密警察组织。|近义词:secret police, KGB, OGPU |反义词: N/A |noun
Etymology: From the Russian abbreviation ЧК (Cheka), standing for Всероссийская чрезвычайная комиссия по борьбе с контрреволюцией и саботажем (Vserossiyskaya chrezvychaynaya komissiya po bor'be s kontrrevolyutsiyey i sabotazhem), meaning "All-Russian Extraordinary Commission for Combating Counter-revolution and Sabotage".
