initialled:/ɪˈnɪʃəld/ | adj. | 已签名的，首字母缩写的 | Bearing someone's initials, typically in the form of a monogram.  Marked with initials. |  The contract was initialled by both parties. 合同双方都已签字。|  signed, autographed | unsigned | adjective


initialled:/ɪˈnɪʃəld/ | v. | 用首字母签名，草签 | To mark or sign with initials. | She initialled each page of the document. 她在文件的每一页上都签上了姓名首字母。| sign, autograph | abstain | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From initial + -ed.  "Initial" comes from the Late Latin "initialis," meaning "of or belonging to the beginning," from "initium," meaning "beginning."
