frondless:/ˈfrɒndləs/ | adj. | 无叶的；没有复叶的 | Lacking fronds; without fronds (typically referring to ferns or palm-like plants). | The harsh winter left the ferns frondless and brown. 严冬过后，蕨类植物的叶子都掉光了，变成了褐色。| The frondless palm tree looked stark against the vibrant sunset.  无叶的棕榈树在鲜艳的日落映衬下显得格外荒凉。 | leafless, bare | leafy, frondose | adjective
