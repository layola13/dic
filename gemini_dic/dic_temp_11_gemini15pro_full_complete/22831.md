bossier:/ˈbɑːsiər/ (US), /ˈbɒsiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更专横的，更跋扈的|More assertive and domineering; inclined to give orders.|例句：He became bossier after his promotion.  他升职后变得更加专横了。

例句：She's much bossier than her sister. 她比她姐姐要强势得多。

近义词：domineering, overbearing, autocratic, dictatorial, imperious|反义词：submissive, meek, compliant, docile|词性:形容词

bossy:/ˈbɒsi/ (US), /ˈbɒsi/ (UK)| adj.|专横的，跋扈的|Fond of giving orders; domineering.|例句：She has a bossy personality. 她性格很强势。

例句：Don't be so bossy! 别那么专横！


词源: From "boss" (meaning "master" or "overseer") + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Dutch "baas".
