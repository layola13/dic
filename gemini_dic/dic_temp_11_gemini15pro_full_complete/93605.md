imbeciles:/ˈɪmbəsɪlz/ | n. |低能者，笨蛋|A person of profound intellectual disability, having a mental age of three to seven years.  This term is now considered offensive. | It's offensive to call people imbeciles.  叫人低能儿是无礼的。|  They acted like complete imbeciles. 他们表现得像十足的笨蛋。|  synonyms: fools, idiots, morons, dolts, simpletons | antonyms: geniuses, intellectuals | noun
