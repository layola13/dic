nonalienating:/nɒnˈeɪliəneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非异化的，不疏远的
Not causing someone to feel alienated or estranged.  Creating a sense of belonging and connection.
例句：The company fostered a nonalienating work environment where employees felt valued.  公司营造了一种让员工感到被重视的非异化工作环境。

例句：The therapist used nonalienating language to build rapport with the patient.  治疗师使用不疏远的语言与患者建立融洽的关系。

近义词：inclusive, welcoming, engaging, connecting, integrating
反义词：alienating, estranging, isolating, excluding, marginalizing
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"
alienate (verb): from Latin alienare "to estrange," from alienus "belonging to another, foreign," from alius "other."
-ing (suffix):  forms present participles and adjectives.
