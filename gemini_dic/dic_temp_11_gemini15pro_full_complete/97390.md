infraspinous:/ˌɪnfrəˈspaɪnəs/| adj.|冈下肌的，肩胛冈下的|Situated below the spine of the scapula.|The infraspinous fossa is a shallow depression on the back of the scapula.|近义词:subscapular|反义词:supraspinous|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *infra-*, meaning "below" + *spina*, meaning "spine".  Therefore, "below the spine (of the scapula)."
