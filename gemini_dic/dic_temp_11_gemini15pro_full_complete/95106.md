inane:/ɪˈneɪn/ | adj. | 空洞的；愚蠢的；无意义的 | Lacking sense, substance, or significance; silly, empty, pointless. |  The politician's speech was full of inane platitudes. 这位政客的演讲充满了空洞的陈词滥调。 |  His inane chatter was starting to annoy me. 他愚蠢的喋喋不休开始让我恼火。 | 近义词: silly, foolish, vapid, empty, pointless | 反义词:  meaningful, significant, profound, sensible | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inanis*, "empty," from *in-* "not" + *anis* related to *anus* "ring, circle"
