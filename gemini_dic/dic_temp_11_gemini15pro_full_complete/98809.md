intellection:/ˌɪntəˈlɛkʃən/| n.|智力活动，理解，思考|The process of understanding or comprehending something through the use of the intellect.|例句：Deep intellection is required to solve this complex problem. 解决这个复杂问题需要深入的思考。|近义词：thinking, reasoning, contemplation, cogitation|反义词：ignorance, mindlessness|noun


intellect:/ˈɪntəlekt/| n.|智力，理解力|The faculty of reasoning and understanding objectively, especially in abstract matters.|例句：He possesses a keen intellect. 他拥有敏锐的智力。|近义词：mind, intelligence, reason|反义词：stupidity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intellectio*, from Latin *intellectus*, past participle of *intellegō* (“I understand”), from *inter* (“between”) + *legō* (“I gather, choose, read”).
