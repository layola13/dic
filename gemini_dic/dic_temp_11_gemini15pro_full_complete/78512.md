gherkin:/ˈɡɜːrkɪn/| n.|小黄瓜，嫩黄瓜|A small cucumber pickled in vinegar or brine. |例句：We added some gherkins to our sandwiches. 我们在三明治里加了一些小黄瓜。

|例句：Gherkins are often served as a side dish. 小黄瓜通常作为配菜。|近义词：cornichon, pickle|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑)cucumber (广义的黄瓜)|词性:noun
