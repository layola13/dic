childbearing:/ˈtʃaɪldˌberɪŋ/ /ˈtʃaɪldˌbɛərɪŋ/| n. adj.|生育，生孩子; 适于生育的|The process of bearing children.  It can also be used adjectivally to describe the years during which a woman is typically able to bear children.|例句：She decided to postpone her career goals during her childbearing years.  她在生育年龄期间决定推迟她的职业目标。|近义词：reproduction, procreation, parturition |反义词:infertility, barrenness|词性:noun, adjective
childbearing:/ˈtʃaɪldˌberɪŋ/ /ˈtʃaɪldˌbɛərɪŋ/| adj.|适于生育的|Suitable for bearing children; of or relating to the bearing of children.|例句：The clinic specializes in childbearing issues. 这家诊所专门处理生育问题。|近义词：fertile, reproductive |反义词:infertile, barren|词性:adjective
