bombproofing:/ˈbɒmpruːfɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɒmpruːfɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|防爆; 防空; 加固|The act or process of making something resistant to bombs or explosions.  It can also refer to the state of being bombproof.|The government invested heavily in bombproofing key government buildings.|近义词: fortification, reinforcement|反义词: weakening|词性:名词

bombproof:/ˈbɒmppruːf/ (US) /ˈbɒmppruːf/ (UK)| adj.|防弹的; 防爆的;  不受炸弹破坏的|Designed or constructed to withstand the force of a bomb explosion. Can also metaphorically refer to something unshakeable or impervious to criticism.|The shelter was bombproof.|近义词: impenetrable, invulnerable,  impervious|反义词: vulnerable, fragile|词性:形容词
