developmentary:/dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəri/| adj.|发展的，发育的|Relating to or involved in development, especially the gradual growth of something.|例句：The child is at a crucial developmental stage.  这个孩子正处于一个关键的发育阶段。

例句：Developmental psychology is the study of how people change over the course of their lives.  发展心理学是研究人们在其生命过程中如何变化的学科。|近义词：evolving, progressive, growing, maturing|反义词：regressive, stagnant, declining|词性:形容词
