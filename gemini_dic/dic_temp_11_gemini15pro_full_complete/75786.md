fumarine:/ˈfjuːməriːn/
n.
延胡索素，富马碱
An alkaloid, C21H19NO4, found in several species of *Fumaria* (fumitory) and *Corydalis*.
Fumarine is a potent anti-inflammatory agent. 延胡索素是一种有效的消炎药。
近义词:protopine, alkaloid
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)
词性:noun
