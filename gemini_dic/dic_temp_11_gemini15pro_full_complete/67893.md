faburden:/ˈfeɪbərˌdən/ /ˈfæbərˌdən/
n.|福伯顿,一种中世纪的复调音乐形式|A technique of musical harmonization used in the 14th and 15th centuries, in which a plainchant melody is sung with two other voices, one a perfect fourth below and the other a perfect fourth above.|The choir sang the hymn in faburden.  唱诗班用福伯顿的唱法演唱了赞美诗。|Synonyms: fauxbourdon, faulx bourdon|Antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a specific musical technique)|noun
