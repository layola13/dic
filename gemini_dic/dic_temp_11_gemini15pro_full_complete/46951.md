darraign:/əˈreɪn/| vt.|传讯；控告；责难|to call (someone) before a court to answer to an indictment; to accuse; to censure|The prisoner was darraigned on charges of theft. 囚犯因盗窃罪被传讯。|Synonyms: indict, accuse, charge, arraign|Antonyms: acquit, exonerate|verb

darraign:/əˈreɪn/| vt.|责备；谴责|to find fault with; to criticize or condemn|He was darraigned for his irresponsible behavior. 他因不负责任的行为受到谴责。|Synonyms: reprimand, rebuke, censure|Antonyms: praise, commend|verb
