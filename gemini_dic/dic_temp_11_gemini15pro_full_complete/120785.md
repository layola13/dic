misappropriating:/ˌmɪsəˈproʊprieɪtɪŋ/
verb
盗用，挪用，滥用
Dishonestly or unfairly taking (something, especially money or other assets belonging to another person or company) for one's own use.
The CEO was accused of misappropriating company funds for personal gain.  首席执行官被指控盗用公司资金以谋取私利。
He misappropriated the charity's funds. 他挪用了慈善基金。
近义词: embezzling, pilfering, purloining, diverting, misusing
反义词: returning, restoring, donating

词性: verb (present participle of misappropriate)

词源: From mis- (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + appropriate (meaning "to set aside for a particular purpose").  "Mis-" 源于古法语 "mes-"，最终源于拉丁语 "minus"（意为“更少”）。 "Appropriate" 源于拉丁语 "appropriare"，意为“使成为自己的”。
