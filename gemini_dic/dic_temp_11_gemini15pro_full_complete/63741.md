epicardium:/ˌɛpɪˈkɑːrdiəm/ | n. | 心外膜 | The outer layer of the heart wall. | The surgeon carefully examined the epicardium for any signs of damage. 外科医生仔细检查了心外膜是否有任何损伤迹象。|  The epicardium is a thin, transparent membrane. 心外膜是一层薄而透明的膜。| Synonym: visceral pericardium | Antonym: endocardium | noun
