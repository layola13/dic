carbonisable:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzəbl/
adjective
可碳化的
Capable of being carbonised; that can be converted into carbon by partial burning.
Coal is a carbonisable material. 煤是一种可碳化的物质。
The plastic is not carbonisable.  这种塑料不可碳化。
synonyms: carbonizable
antonyms: non-carbonizable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From carbonize + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *carbōnāre, from carbō (“coal, charcoal”).
