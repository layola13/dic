mallee:/ˈmæli/ | n. | 马利灌木；尤加利树的一种 | A type of eucalyptus tree or shrub that grows in dense thickets, typically having multiple stems arising from an underground lignotuber.  Native to Australia. |  The mallee roots are used by Aboriginal people for making tools.  马利灌木的根被澳大利亚土著居民用来制作工具。 | The fire swept through the mallee scrub. 大火席卷了马利灌木丛。 | synonyms: scrub, thicket, brushwood | antonyms: forest, woodland | noun

mallee:/ˈmæli/ | adj. | 马利灌木的；生长在马利地区的 | Relating to or characteristic of the mallee region of Australia. | Mallee fowl build large mounds for their eggs.  马利冢雉为它们的卵筑起大堆。 | The mallee landscape is harsh and dry. 马利地区的景观是荒凉干燥的。 | synonyms: arid, semi-arid, scrubland | antonyms: lush, verdant | adjective
