outwandering:/ˈaʊtˌwɑːndərɪŋ/ | n. | 漫游，徘徊；（尤指老年痴呆症患者的）外出游荡 | The act of wandering outside, especially used in the context of people with dementia leaving their homes or care facilities unsupervised. |  The nursing home implemented new security measures to prevent outwandering.  养老院实施了新的安全措施，以防止老人外出游荡。 | 近义词：wandering, roaming, straying | 反义词: staying put, remaining | 词性: 名词

outwandering:/ˈaʊtˌwɑːndərɪŋ/ | adj. | 漫游的，徘徊的 |  Used to describe someone who wanders, especially someone with dementia. |  The outwandering patient was found safe and sound a few blocks away.  外出游荡的病人被发现安然无恙地在几个街区之外。 | 近义词：wandering, roaming, straying | 反义词: settled, stationary | 词性: 形容词
