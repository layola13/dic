overpollinate:/ˌōvərˈpɑːləˌneɪt/| v.|过度授粉|To pollinate excessively, resulting in reduced fruit or seed production.|The farmer overpollinated the apple blossoms, which led to a smaller crop than expected. 农民对苹果花过度授粉，导致产量比预期要低。|The bees overpollinated the squash plants this year, and we ended up with fewer, but larger, squash. 今年蜜蜂对南瓜植株过度授粉，结果我们得到的南瓜数量减少了，但个头变大了。|近义词: overfertilize|反义词: underpollinate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "pollinate" (from Latin "pollen," meaning "fine flour")
