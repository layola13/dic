deflect:/dɪˈflɛkt/ /diˈflekt/ | v. | 转偏，使转向;  避开，挡开; | To cause something to change direction by bouncing off something; to prevent something from succeeding; to divert attention away from something. |  The ball deflected off the defender's leg and went into the goal. 球打在防守队员的腿上偏转进了球门。 | synonyms: divert, deviate, swerve, avert | antonyms: attract, focus, maintain,  straighten | verb

deflection:/dɪˈflɛkʃən/ /diˈflekʃən/ | n. | 转向，偏斜; 偏转; | The act of deflecting or the state of being deflected. | The deflection of the missile prevented a major catastrophe. 导弹的转向避免了一场大灾难。| synonyms: deviation, divergence, digression | antonyms: adherence, directness,  straightness | noun
