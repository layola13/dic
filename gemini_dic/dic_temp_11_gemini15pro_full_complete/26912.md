calamiferous:/ˌkæləˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
灾难性的；带来灾祸的
producing or containing calamity; disastrous
例句：The calamiferous storm destroyed many homes. 灾难性的风暴摧毁了许多家园。

例句：The region suffered a calamiferous drought that lasted for months. 该地区遭受了持续数月的灾难性干旱。

近义词：disastrous, catastrophic, ruinous
反义词：fortunate, beneficial, advantageous
词性: adjective
词源：Latin calamifer, from calamitas "disaster" + -fer "bearing"
