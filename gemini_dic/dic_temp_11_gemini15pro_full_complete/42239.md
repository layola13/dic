countableness:/ˈkaʊntəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
可数性
The quality of being countable or capable of being counted.
The countableness of nouns is a key concept in English grammar.  名词的可数性是英语语法中的一个关键概念。
Nouns have the property of countableness. 名词具有可数性的属性。
近义词:numerability, calculability
反义词:uncountableness, innumerability
词性:noun
