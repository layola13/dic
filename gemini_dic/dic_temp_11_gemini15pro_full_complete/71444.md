flamboyance:/flæmˈbɔɪəns/
n.
华丽；炫耀； flamboyant的特性
The quality of being flamboyant; extravagant elaborateness.
Her outfits were known for their flamboyance. 她的服装以其华丽而闻名。

flamboyance:/flæmˈbɔɪəns/
n.
艳丽；鲜艳；火焰般的样子
Strikingly bold or brilliant display or behavior.
The flamboyance of the sunset was breathtaking. 日落的艳丽令人叹为观止。


近义词:ostentation, showiness, extravagance, flourish, fanfare
反义词:understatement, modesty, restraint, simplicity, plainness
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *flamboyance*, from *flamboyer* "to flame," from Old French *flambe* "flame," from Latin *flamma*.
