numismatician:/ˌn(j)uːmɪzˈmætɪʃən/
n.
钱币学家，钱币收藏家
A person who studies or collects coins, medals, and paper money.
例句：The numismatician carefully examined the ancient Roman coin.  钱币学家仔细地检查了这枚古罗马硬币。

例句：She became a renowned numismatician after years of studying rare currency.  经过多年的稀有货币研究，她成了一位著名的钱币学家。

近义词：coin collector, philatelist (for stamps)
反义词: None (No direct antonym exists)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French numismatique, from Latin numisma "coin, money," from Greek nomisma "current coin, established usage, custom, law," from nomizein "to hold or own by usage, to use customarily, to adopt a custom, to practise, to be in use," from nomos "custom, usage, law."
