laxations:/lækˈseɪʃənz/
noun
泻药；通便；放松；减轻
the act of loosening or relaxing, especially of the bowels.
The doctor prescribed laxations to relieve the patient's constipation. 医生开了泻药来缓解病人的便秘。
synonyms: purgatives, aperients, cathartics
antonyms: constrictions, tightenings
n.
