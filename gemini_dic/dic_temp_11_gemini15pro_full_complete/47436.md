deaf:/dɛf/ | adj. | 聋的 | Unable to hear, either completely or partly. |
She was born deaf. 她天生耳聋。
He is deaf in one ear. 他一只耳朵聋了。
| 近义词: hearing-impaired | 反义词: hearing | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *dēaf, from Proto-Germanic *daufaz (compare West Frisian dôf, Dutch doof, German taub, Gothic 𝑑𝑎𝑢𝑓𝑠), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewbh- (“to be cloudy, dusty, numb, deaf”).
