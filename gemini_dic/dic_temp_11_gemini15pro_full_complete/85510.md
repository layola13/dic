hastily:/ˈheɪstɪli/| adv.|匆忙地，草率地|Done or acting with excessive speed and urgency, often without due care or consideration.|He hastily packed his bags and left. 他匆忙地收拾行李离开了。|She hastily signed the contract without reading it carefully. 她草率地签了合同，没有仔细阅读。|近义词：hurriedly, quickly, rapidly, swiftly|反义词：slowly, deliberately, carefully|词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hastifliche*, from Old French *hastivement*, from *hastif* (Modern French *hâtif*), from Late Latin *hastīvus* (“hasty, early”), from Latin *hasta* (“spear, shaft”). The notion of haste is thus connected with the speed of a spear thrust.
