floppy disk:/ˈflɒpi dɪsk/ (UK) /ˈflɑːpi dɪsk/ (US)| n.|软盘|A thin, flexible magnetic disk encased in a square plastic or cardboard cover, used for storing computer data. |I remember using floppy disks to save my school projects. 我记得用软盘来保存我的学校项目。|Synonym: diskette|Antonym: hard drive|noun

floppy:/ˈflɒpi/ (UK) /ˈflɑːpi/ (US)| adj.|松软的，下垂的|Soft and flexible; tending to droop or hang loosely. |The dog had long, floppy ears. 这只狗有着长长的、松软的耳朵。|Synonym: limp, droopy|Antonym: stiff, rigid|adjective
