gravitater:/ˈɡrævɪˌteɪtər/
n.
引力体，倾向于…的人
One who or that which gravitates. Something which attracts.
例句：The large city acts as a gravitater, drawing people from rural areas.  这个大城市像一个引力体，吸引着来自农村地区的人们。

例句：He is a gravitater towards new ideas and innovative projects. 他是一个倾向于新想法和创新项目的人。

近义词：attractor, magnet
反义词：repeller
词性：名词
