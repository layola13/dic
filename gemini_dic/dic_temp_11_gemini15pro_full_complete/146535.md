overglamorizing: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度美化
Making something seem more glamorous or exciting than it actually is.
The advertisement was overglamorizing the life of a rock star.  这则广告过度美化了摇滚明星的生活。
Hollywood often overglamorizes the lives of celebrities. 好莱坞经常过度美化名人的生活。
近义词: romanticizing, idealizing, exaggerating
反义词: deglamorizing, downplaying, understating
词性: verb (present participle)
