masque: /mɑːsk/ /mæsk/ | n. | 面具；假面舞会 | A covering for all or part of the face, worn as a disguise, or for protection.  A masked ball. |  She wore a beautiful masque to the ball. 她戴着漂亮的面具去参加舞会。

The king held a grand masque at the palace. 国王在宫殿里举行了一场盛大的假面舞会。 | 近义词: mask, disguise, masquerade | 反义词: face, reveal | 词性: 名词
