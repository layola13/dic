matured:/məˈtʃʊərd/ /məˈtʊərd/
adjective
成熟的
Fully developed physically; emotionally or mentally developed.
例句：He's a very matured young man for his age. 他年纪轻轻就非常成熟。

例句：The wine has matured nicely over the years.  这酒多年来已经醇化得很好了。

近义词：ripe, developed, grown, adult, mellow
反义词：immature, undeveloped, green, young
词性：adjective


matured:/məˈtʃʊərd/ /məˈtʊərd/
verb
(使)成熟
To become or cause to become fully developed physically; to become or cause to become emotionally or mentally developed.
例句：The cheese has matured beautifully.  奶酪已经熟成了，味道很好。

例句：She matured quickly after she left home.  她离家后很快就成熟了。

近义词：develop, ripen, age, grow
反义词：regress, deteriorate
词性：verb
