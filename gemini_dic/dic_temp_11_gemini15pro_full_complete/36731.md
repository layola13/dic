collogue:/kəˈloʊɡ/ | n.|密谋，秘密会议；(非正式)闲谈，聊天|A secret or private discussion, often with improper motives. ETYMOLOGY->
From French *colloquer*, from Latin *colloqui* "to speak with, converse," from *com-* "together" + *loqui* "to speak."
