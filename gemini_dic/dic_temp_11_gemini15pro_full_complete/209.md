abbrev:/əˈbriːv/ |v. |缩写，简写|To shorten (a word or phrase) by omitting letters or syllables; abridge.|  例句：We decided to abbrev. “et cetera.” 我们决定缩写“等等”。 |例句：It is common to abbrev. long or complicated names. 缩写长或复杂的名字是很常见的。|近义词：shorten, condense, abridge|反义词：expand, lengthen|词性:动词 (verb)
abbrev.:/əˈbriːv/ |adj. |缩写的，简写的|Shortened; abridged.| 例句：The abbrev. form of "Mister" is "Mr." “先生”的缩写形式是“Mr.”。|例句：The company used an abbrev. name on its logo.  这家公司在商标上使用了缩写名称。|近义词：shortened, condensed, abridged|反义词：expanded, lengthened|词性:形容词 (adjective)
abbrev.:/əˈbriːv/ |n. |缩写，缩写词|A shortened form of a word or phrase.| 例句：The abbrev. for "United Kingdom" is "UK". “英国”的缩写是“UK”。|例句：Common abbrevs. save time and space. 常见的缩写可以节省时间和空间。|近义词：abbreviation, shortening, contraction|反义词：expansion, lengthening|词性:名词 (noun)


词源：Late Middle English: from Latin abbreviat- ‘shortened’, from the verb abbreviare, from brevis ‘short’.
