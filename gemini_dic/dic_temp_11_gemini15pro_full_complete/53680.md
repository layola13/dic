disciplinarian's:/ˌdɪsəplɪˈnɛəriənz/
noun
训练者；纪律严明的人的；执行纪律者
belonging to or associated with a disciplinarian.
The disciplinarian's strict rules were unpopular with the students. 执行纪律者的严格规定不受学生欢迎。
近义词: enforcer, stickler, martinet, taskmaster
反义词: pushover, softie

词性: noun (possessive form)
词源: From discipline + -arian + -'s (possessive suffix).  "Discipline" comes from Latin *disciplīna* "instruction, knowledge," from *discipulus* "pupil."
