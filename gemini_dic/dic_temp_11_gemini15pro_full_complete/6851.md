anatine:/ˈænəˌtaɪn/| adj. n.|鸭子的，鸭科动物|Relating to, resembling, or characteristic of ducks; duck-like.  Also, a duck or any of various duck-like birds.|The bird had an anatine bill.  这只鸟有鸭子一样的喙。|The anatine species are diverse. 这些鸭科物种多种多样。|近义词：duck-like, anserine|反义词: (No clear opposite exists, perhaps "non-anatine")|词性: adjective, noun
