ignites:/ɪɡˈnaɪts/| v. |点燃；激起| to cause something to start burning or to cause a strong emotion or reaction |The match ignited the dry leaves. 火柴点燃了干树叶。

The speaker's words ignited the crowd's enthusiasm. 演讲者的话激起了群众的热情。|近义词：kindle, spark, inflame |反义词: extinguish, quench |动词
词源: From Late Latin *ignitāre, from Latin ignītus, past participle of ignīre (“to set on fire”), from ignis (“fire”).
