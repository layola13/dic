flax:/flæks/
n.|亚麻；亚麻纤维；亚麻制品|A plant with blue flowers, cultivated for its seeds and for the fibres in its stem, used for making linen cloth. |例句：The farmer sowed the field with flax. 农民在地里播种了亚麻。|近义词：linen|反义词:|词性:名词

flax:/flæks/
n.|淡黄色；亚麻色|A pale yellowish gray color like that of processed flax.|例句：Her hair was a beautiful shade of flax. 她的头发是美丽的亚麻色。|近义词：blonde, flaxen, golden|反义词:dark, black|词性:名词
