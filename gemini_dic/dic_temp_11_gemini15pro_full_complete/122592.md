modernize:/ˈmɒdənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪz/ (US)| v. | 使现代化 | To make something modern or up-to-date. | They plan to modernize the factory by installing new equipment. 他们计划通过安装新设备来使工厂现代化。| Update, innovate, renovate, upgrade | Obsolete, antiquate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From modern + -ize/-ise.  "Modern" comes from Old French "moderne," ultimately from Late Latin "modernus," meaning "of now."  The suffix "-ize/-ise" comes from Greek "-izein" and means "to make or become."
