indefatigability:/ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəˈbɪləti/
n.
不屈不挠,不知疲倦
The quality of being tireless and persistent.
Her indefatigability and tireless work ethic impressed everyone. 她不知疲倦的工作热情给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
Synonyms: tirelessness, persistence, tenacity, perseverance
Antonyms: fatigue, weariness, lethargy, laziness
noun
