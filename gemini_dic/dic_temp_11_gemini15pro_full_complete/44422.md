cross-pollinate:/ˌkrɒs ˈpɒləneɪt/ /ˌkrɑːs ˈpɑːləneɪt/ | v. |异花授粉；交叉融合| To fertilize a plant with pollen from another plant, or to cause this to happen.  To combine ideas or principles from different sources or disciplines.| Bees cross-pollinate apple blossoms. 蜜蜂给苹果花异花授粉。|  The author cross-pollinates ideas from several different cultures in her novel. 作者在小说中融合了几种不同文化的思想。| Synonyms: hybridize, interbreed, cross-fertilize | Antonyms: self-pollinate, inbreed | verb

cross-pollination:/ˌkrɒs ˌpɒləˈneɪʃn/ /ˌkrɑːs ˌpɑːləˈneɪʃn/ | n. |异花授粉；交叉融合| The transfer of pollen from one plant to another, or the fusion of ideas or principles from different sources or disciplines. | Cross-pollination is essential for the production of many fruits and vegetables. 异花授粉对许多水果和蔬菜的生产至关重要。 | The conference encouraged cross-pollination of ideas between different scientific fields. 会议鼓励不同科学领域之间的思想交流。| Synonyms: hybridization, interbreeding, cross-fertilization | Antonyms: self-pollination, inbreeding | noun
