osmosis:/ɑːzˈmoʊsɪs/ /ɒzˈməʊsɪs/
noun
渗透；渗透作用
The process by which a solvent passes through a semipermeable membrane from a less concentrated solution into a more concentrated one, equalizing the concentrations on each side of the membrane.
Water enters the roots of plants by osmosis. 水通过渗透作用进入植物的根部。

近义词: diffusion, infiltration
反义词: reverse osmosis
