contrasting:/kənˈtrɑːstɪŋ/ | adj. | 对比的，截然不同的 | Strikingly different; exhibiting a marked difference. | The photograph showed the contrasting colors of the desert and the sky. 这张照片展现了沙漠和天空对比鲜明的颜色。 |  The artist used contrasting shades of blue and green. 艺术家使用了对比鲜明的蓝色和绿色阴影。 | synonyms: differing, dissimilar, divergent, distinct, diverse, unlike, varying | antonyms: similar, alike, identical, uniform | adjective
