convincer:/kənˈvɪnsər/ | n. | 说服者；劝服者 |  A person or thing that convinces someone of something. | The lawyer was a skilled convincer, swaying the jury with his arguments. 律师是一位技艺高超的说服者，用他的论点说服了陪审团。| 近义词: persuader, advocate | 反义词: dissuader, opponent | 词性: noun

convince:/kənˈvɪns/ | vt. | 说服；使确信；使信服 | To persuade someone to believe something or do something. |  I managed to convince him to come with us. 我设法说服了他和我们一起来。| 近义词: persuade, assure | 反义词: dissuade, deter | 词性: verb
