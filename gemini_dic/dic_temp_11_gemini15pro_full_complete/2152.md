add-in:/ˈædɪn/ | n. | 插件，附加组件 | A software program or hardware component that adds a specific feature or capability to a larger system. |  This add-in provides extra grammar checking features for your word processor.  这个插件为你的文字处理器提供了额外的语法检查功能。| Example: I installed a new add-in for my email client to manage my contacts more efficiently. 我为我的电子邮件客户端安装了一个新的插件，以便更有效地管理我的联系人。 | Synonyms: extension, plug-in, supplement | Antonyms: base, core | noun
