hygienism:/ˈhaɪdʒiːənɪzəm/
n.
卫生主义;保健法
A system of principles for preserving health, especially public health.
Hygienism was a popular health movement in the 19th century.  19世纪，卫生主义是一场流行的健康运动。
Hygienism advocates for clean living and a healthy diet.  卫生主义提倡清洁的生活和健康的饮食。
Synonyms:  sanitarianism, health promotion, preventive medicine
Antonyms:  neglect, filth, unsanitary conditions
