bicentenaries: /ˌbaɪsɛnˈtɛnəriːz/ /ˌbaɪsɛnˈtɛnəriːz/
n.
两百周年纪念; 二百周年纪念日
The plural form of bicentenary, referring to multiple occasions marking a 200th anniversary.
The city is celebrating its two bicentenaries this year, one for its founding and one for a key historical event.  这个城市今年庆祝两个两百周年纪念，一个是建市纪念日，一个是重要的历史事件纪念日。
The museum hosted exhibitions for several important bicentenaries.  博物馆为几个重要的两百周年纪念举办了展览。
近义词: bicentennials
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific anniversary)
词性: Noun (plural)
