disavaunce: /dɪsəˈvɑːns/
verb
损害；使处于不利地位；阻碍
to cause someone or something to be less successful or to make less progress
His ill health disavaunced his career. 他的健康状况阻碍了他的事业发展。
近义词: hinder, impede, obstruct, disadvantage
反义词: advance, promote, benefit, aid
词性：v.


Etymology:
From dis- (expressing reversal) + avaunce (obsolete form of advance). Ultimately from Latin ab ante (“from before”).
