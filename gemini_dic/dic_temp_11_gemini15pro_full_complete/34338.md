clasp:/klɑːsp/ /klæspt/
verb
紧握，扣住
to grasp something firmly with your hand
He clasped the medal tightly in his hand. 他紧紧地握住奖牌。
近义词: grip, clutch, hold
反义词: release, open
词性: Verb

词源: From Middle English claspen,  from Old English *clæps, from Proto-Germanic *klapsōną (“to clap, beat, strike”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʰlew- (“to stick”).
