balderdash:/ˈbɔːldərdæʃ/| n.|胡说八道；废话|Nonsense; senseless talk.  It's often used to express disbelief or contempt for what someone has said.|例句：That's a load of balderdash!  那是一派胡言！

例句：He dismissed her claims as utter balderdash. 他认为她的说法完全是胡说八道。

|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, twaddle, hogwash, gibberish|反义词：truth, fact, sense|词性:名词

balderdashes:/ˈbɔːldərdæʃɪz/| n.|胡说八道；废话（复数形式，较少使用）|Plural form of balderdash, less commonly used.|例句：He filled the air with his balderdashes. 他满口胡言乱语。

例句：All those balderdashes are getting on my nerves. 所有这些胡言乱语都让我心烦意乱了。

|近义词：nonsenses, rubbishes, twaddles, hogwashes, gibberishes|反义词：truths, facts|词性:名词
