floweriest: /ˈflaʊəriɪst/ (US) /ˈflaʊəriɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最华丽的，最辞藻华丽的|Having the most elaborate or ornate language; excessively embellished with figures of speech.|Her prose was dense and floweriest, filled with obscure metaphors. 她的散文密集而华丽，充满了晦涩的隐喻。|The acceptance speech was the floweriest I've ever heard. 这篇获奖感言是我听过的最华丽的。|近义词：ornate, elaborate, flamboyant, flowery|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From flower + -y + -est.  The "-y" suffix forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by," as in "cloudy."  The "-est" suffix is the superlative ending, indicating the highest degree of a quality.
