nativity: /nəˈtɪvɪti/ /neɪˈtɪvɪti/
n.
出生;诞生;耶稣诞生;圣诞节
The act or process of being born.  The Nativity is the birth of Jesus Christ, celebrated on December 25.
例句：
They celebrated the nativity of their first child.  他们庆祝了第一个孩子的出生。

The painting depicts the Nativity. 这幅画描绘了耶稣诞生。

近义词：birth, origin, genesis
反义词：death, demise
词性：名词
