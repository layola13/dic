disempower:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpaʊər/| vt.|使丧失权力，使失去自主权，剥夺权力|To deprive of power; make weak and ineffectual.|The new laws disempower local communities. 新法律剥夺了当地社区的权力。|Synonyms: weaken, undermine, cripple, disable|Antonyms: empower, strengthen, enable|Verb

disempowerment:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpaʊərmənt/| n.|丧失权力，失去自主权|The process of losing power or control over one's own life or destiny.|The disempowerment of the working class is a major concern. 工人阶级丧失权力是一个主要问题。|Synonyms: weakness, powerlessness, helplessness|Antonyms: empowerment, enablement, strengthening|Noun
