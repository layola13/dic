benefaction:/ˌbɛnɪˈfækʃən/ | n. | 善行，捐赠，恩惠 | An act of kindness or generosity, especially a charitable gift or donation. |  The library was built thanks to a generous benefaction from a local businessman.  这图书馆的建成得益于当地一位商人的慷慨捐赠。

|  His benefactions to the university totalled over a million dollars. 他对这所大学的捐款总额超过一百万美元。 | Synonyms: donation, contribution, gift, endowment, grant | Antonyms:  malefaction, detriment, injury | noun
