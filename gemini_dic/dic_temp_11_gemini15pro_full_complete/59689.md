echometer:/ˌekəˈmɒmɪtər/ (US: /ˌekəˈmɑːtər/) | n. | 回声探测仪；回声测深仪 | An instrument that measures depth or determines the location of objects underwater by measuring the time taken for sound waves to travel from the instrument to the object and back again. | The echometer indicated a depth of 30 meters. 回声测深仪显示深度为30米。 | The fishing boat used an echometer to locate schools of fish. 渔船使用回声探测仪来定位鱼群。 | synonyms: sonar, depth sounder, fathometer | antonyms: None | noun
