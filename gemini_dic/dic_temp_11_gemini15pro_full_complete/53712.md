discloser:/dɪˈskloʊzər/ | n. |披露者；揭发者；公开, 透露| A person or thing that discloses something. The act of disclosing something. |例句：The discloser of the secret information was never found.  秘密信息的泄露者从未被找到。

例句：The discloser of the company's financial troubles led to a drop in stock prices.  公司财务问题的披露导致股价下跌。|近义词：revealer, informant, exposer |反义词: concealer, suppressor |词性: noun

词源: From the verb "disclose," which comes from the Old French "desclore" (to uncover, reveal), ultimately from the Latin "discludere" (to shut off, separate, open).
