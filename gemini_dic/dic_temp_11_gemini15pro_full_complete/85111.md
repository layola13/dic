hardener:/ˈhɑːrdnər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|硬化剂，固化剂|A substance added to another substance to make it harder.|The resin needs a hardener to set properly.  树脂需要硬化剂才能正常凝固。|Synonyms: curing agent, setting agent|Antonyms: softener, thinner|noun

hardener:/ˈhɑːrdnər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|硬化剂，固化剂|A substance that causes another substance to become hard or solid.|This hardener will help the concrete dry faster.  这种硬化剂将有助于混凝土更快干燥。|Synonyms:  setting agent, catalyst|Antonyms:  softener, thinner|noun
