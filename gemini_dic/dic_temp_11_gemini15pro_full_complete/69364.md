federators:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪtərz/
n.
联盟者；联邦成员；联合者
Those who unite in a federation or league.
The federators signed a treaty to ensure mutual defense. 联盟者签署了一项条约，以确保共同防御。
Synonyms: allies, partners, confederates, associates
Antonyms: adversaries, opponents, enemies
noun
