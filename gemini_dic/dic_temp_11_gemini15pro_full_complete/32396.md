chillier:/ˈtʃɪliər/ | adj. | 更冷的，更寒冷的 |Comparative form of chilly: more chilly.  Colder than chilly, but not freezing. | The evenings are getting chillier.  晚上越来越冷了。|  It's much chillier today than it was yesterday. 今天比昨天冷得多。|近义词：colder, cooler, frostier |反义词：warmer, hotter |词性:形容词
词源: From chilly + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from chill (n.) + -y (adjective suffix).
