capitols: /ˈkæpɪtlz/ | n. | 首府（复数）; 国会大厦（复数）| The capitols of all 50 states are unique in their own ways.  所有 50 个州的首府都各有特色。| The protesters marched to the capitols of several European countries. 抗议者游行到几个欧洲国家的国会大厦。 | 近义词：capital cities (for 首府),  legislative buildings (for 国会大厦) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Middle English *capitol, from Old French *capitol, from Latin *Capitolium (“Temple of Jupiter on the Capitoline Hill; the Capitoline Hill itself”), from *caput (“head”).  The meaning "building housing a state legislature" is from 1795 American English.
