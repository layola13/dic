dowered:/ˈdaʊərd/
adjective
赋予的，有天赋的，有嫁妆的
provided or supplied or equipped with (especially as by inheritance or nature)
例句：She was dowered with both beauty and intelligence. 她天生丽质，又聪明过人。

例句：The newly established university is dowered with a generous grant from the government. 这所新成立的大学获得了政府慷慨的拨款。

近义词：endowed, gifted, talented, privileged
反义词：deprived, lacking, deficient
词性: adjective
