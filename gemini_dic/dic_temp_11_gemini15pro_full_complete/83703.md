hagiographal:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfəl/
adjective
理想化的传记的；圣徒传记的
Relating to or resembling a hagiography; excessively flattering or idealizing.
The biography was so hagiographal that it omitted any mention of the subject's flaws. 这本传记过于理想化，以至于完全忽略了主人公的缺点。
Synonyms: adulatory, laudatory, eulogistic, panegyrical, fawning
Antonyms: critical, objective, impartial, unbiased
词性: adjective
