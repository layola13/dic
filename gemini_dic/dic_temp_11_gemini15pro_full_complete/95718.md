unconscious: /ʌnˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK) /ʌnˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| adj.| 无意识的，失去知觉的，未察觉的|Not aware of something; or in a state similar to sleep, usually as the result of a head injury, illness, or drug.|She was knocked unconscious by the falling tree. 她被倒下的树砸晕了。|Synonyms: senseless, insensible, comatose, unaware|Antonyms: conscious, aware, awake|adjective

unconscious: /ʌnˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK) /ʌnˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| n.| 无意识|The part of the mind that is inaccessible to conscious awareness.|His unconscious desires manifested in his dreams. 他无意识的欲望在他的梦中显现。|Synonyms: subconscious|Antonyms: conscious|noun
