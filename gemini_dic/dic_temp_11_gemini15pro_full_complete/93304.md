ill-considered:/ˌɪl kənˈsɪdərd/
adjective
欠考虑的；考虑不周的
Not properly or sufficiently thought through.
例句：
It was an ill-considered remark that I immediately regretted.
这是一句欠考虑的话，我立刻就后悔了。

His ill-considered investment cost him dearly.
他不经考虑的投资让他付出了沉重的代价。

近义词：injudicious, unwise, rash, imprudent, foolish, ill-advised
反义词：well-considered, prudent, judicious, wise


词性: adjective

词源:  "ill-" (badly) + "considered" (thought about)
