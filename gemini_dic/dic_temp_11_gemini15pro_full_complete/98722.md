insures:/ɪnˈʃʊrz/ | v. |投保，给…保险；确保，保证| to arrange for compensation in the event of damage to or loss of property, or injury to or the death of someone, in exchange for regular payments to a company or to the state; to make certain that something will happen |  The policy insures your house against fire. 这项保险为你的房屋投保火灾险。| synonyms: protect, assure, guarantee | antonyms: endanger, jeopardize | verb

insures:/ɪnˈʃʊrz/ | v. |确保，保证| to make certain that something will happen | Eating a balanced diet insures good health. 均衡的饮食确保身体健康。| synonyms: ensure, guarantee, secure | antonyms: endanger, risk | verb
