nonentitious:/ˌnɒnɛnˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
虚构的，不存在的
Having no actual existence; unreal; not existing.
Her fears were nonentitious, nothing bad ever happened. 她害怕的事情都是虚构的，实际上什么坏事也没发生。

The novel described a nonentitious land with strange creatures. 这部小说描述了一个虚构的、生活着奇异生物的国度。
近义词: unreal, imaginary, fictitious, nonexistent, illusory
反义词: real, actual, existent, tangible, substantial
词性: adjective
