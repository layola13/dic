decrew:/diːˈkruː/
verb
卸下船员;使（船）失去船员
To remove the crew from a ship; to deprive a ship of its crew.
The pirates decrewed the captured vessel, leaving the passengers adrift.  海盗们把俘获的船上的船员卸下，让乘客们漂流。
Synonyms: unman, disarm
Antonyms: man, crew
词性: 动词
