nonpigmented:/nɒnˈpɪɡməntɪd/ /nɑːnˈpɪɡməntɪd/| adj.|未着色的，无色素的|Not having pigment; lacking normal coloration.|Albino animals are nonpigmented. 白化动物是没有色素的。|The fungus produces nonpigmented spores. 这种真菌产生无色的孢子。|近义词：achromic, colorless, unpigmented|反义词: pigmented, colored|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".
pigment (noun): from Latin pigmentum "paint, color, dye," from pingere "to paint."
-ed (suffix):  forms adjectives from nouns, indicating possession of the quality denoted by the noun.
