noncoagulative:/ˌnɒnkoʊˈæɡjʊlətɪv/
adjective
非凝固性的
Not causing or involving coagulation; not clotting.
Example: Noncoagulative necrosis can occur due to ischemia.  非凝固性坏死可能由于局部缺血引起。
Example: The patient received noncoagulative laser treatment. 病人接受了非凝固性激光治疗。
Synonyms: nonclotting, incoagulable, non-coagulating
Antonyms: coagulative, clotting, coagulating
词性: 形容词
