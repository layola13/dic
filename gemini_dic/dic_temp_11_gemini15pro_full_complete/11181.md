archilute:/ˈɑːrkɪˌluːt/ (US), /ˈɑːkɪˌluːt/ (UK)| n. |大提琴|A large lute with extended bass strings.|The archilute produced a deep, resonant sound, favored in Baroque music.  大提琴发出了一种深沉而洪亮的声音，在巴洛克音乐中很受欢迎。| Near synonyms: theorbo, chitarrone| Antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a musical instrument)| Noun
