monosporous:/ˌmɑːnəˈspɔːrəs/| adj.|单孢子的|Producing or containing only one spore.  Relating to or characterized by the production of a single spore, typically within an ascus or sporangium.|The monosporous development of the ascus is a key characteristic of this fungal species.  这种真菌物种的子囊单孢子发育是一个关键特征。|monosporic|polysporous|adjective
