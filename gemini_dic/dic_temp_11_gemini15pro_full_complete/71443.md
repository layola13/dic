flambé:/flɑːmˈbeɪ/ /flæmˈbeɪ/
verb
火焰烹调，淋上白兰地等烈酒后点燃
To pour liquor over food and ignite it.
The chef flambéed the cherries jubilee.  厨师用火焰烹调了樱桃甜点。

flambé:/flɑːmˈbeɪ/ /flæmˈbeɪ/
adjective
火焰烹调的
Prepared or served flambé.
We ordered the flambé bananas for dessert. 我们点了火焰香蕉作为甜点。


flambés:/flɑːmˈbeɪz/ /flæmˈbeɪz/
verb (third-person singular simple present flambés, present participle flambéing, simple past and past participle flambéed)
火焰烹调，淋上白兰地等烈酒后点燃
To pour liquor over food and ignite it.
He flambés the steak to give it a smoky flavor. 他用火焰烹调牛排以赋予它烟熏味。


Etymology:  From French *flamber*, "to flame."


Synonym: ignite, blaze, fire
Antonym: extinguish, quench
