froglet:/ˈfrɒɡlɪt/ | n. | 小青蛙，幼蛙 | A young frog, especially one that has recently developed from a tadpole. |  The pond was teeming with froglets. 池塘里到处都是小青蛙。| synonyms: young frog, tadpole (in later stages), polliwog (in later stages) | antonyms: adult frog, mature frog | noun
