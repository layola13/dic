cougar: /ˈkuːɡər/ (US), /ˈkuːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. |美洲狮，中年女子(尤指追求比自己年轻男子的) |A large American wild cat with a plain tawny to grayish coat. Also, an older woman who seeks a romantic relationship with a younger man. |例句：The cougar stalked its prey through the long grass. 美洲狮在高高的草丛中跟踪猎物。


例句：She's a cougar who dates men half her age. 她是一个追求比自己年轻一半男人的中年女子。|近义词：(for animal) mountain lion, puma, panther; (for woman) maneater, predator|反义词: (for animal) prey; (for woman)  No direct antonym exists, perhaps "younger woman" in the specific context. |词性:noun
