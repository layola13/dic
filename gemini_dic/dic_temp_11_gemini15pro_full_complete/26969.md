calcigerous:/kælˈsɪdʒərəs/
adjective
产钙的；含钙的
Bearing or producing calcium salts, calcareous.
The calcigerous glands secrete calcium carbonate to form the shell of a bird's egg.  这些产钙的腺体分泌碳酸钙形成鸟蛋壳。
The soil in this region is calcigerous, making it suitable for growing certain types of crops. 这个地区的土壤富含钙，适合种植某些类型的作物。
Synonyms: calcareous, chalky
Antonyms: non-calcareous


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calciger* "lime-bearing," from *calc-* (stem of *calx* "lime, limestone") + *-iger* "-bearing" (from PIE root *-ag- "to drive, draw, move").
