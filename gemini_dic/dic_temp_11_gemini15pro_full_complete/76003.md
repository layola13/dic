furbelow:/ˈfɜːrbəloʊ/ (US), /ˈfɜːbɪləʊ/ (UK)| n.|饰边，褶边，褶皱|A pleated or gathered piece of fabric used as a trimming or decoration, especially on clothing.|The dress was adorned with elaborate furbelows.|Synonyms: frill, ruffle, flounce, ornament, embellishment|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|Noun

furbelow:/ˈfɜːrbəloʊ/ (US), /ˈfɜːbɪləʊ/ (UK)| v.|饰以褶边，用褶边装饰|To decorate with furbelows.|She furbelowed the sleeves of her gown.|Synonyms: adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, trim|Antonyms: strip, simplify|Verb
