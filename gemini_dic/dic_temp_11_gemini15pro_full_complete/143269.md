outdistancing:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstənsɪŋ/
verb
超越，把…远远抛在后面
Surpassing or leaving someone or something far behind, especially in a race or competition.
The Kenyan runner outdistanced his rivals in the marathon. 肯尼亚选手在马拉松比赛中远远超过了他的对手。
outstrip, surpass, outrun, leave behind
lag behind, trail, fall behind
v.
