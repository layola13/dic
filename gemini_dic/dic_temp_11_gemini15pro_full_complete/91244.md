hydrodynamic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. | 流体动力学的；水力的 | Relating to the forces of liquids in motion, or the study of these forces. |  The ship's hydrodynamic design minimizes drag and maximizes fuel efficiency. 这艘船的流体动力学设计最大限度地减少了阻力并提高了燃油效率。

The hydrodynamic properties of the submarine allow it to dive to great depths. 潜水艇的流体动力学特性使其能够潜入很深的地方。 | 近义词:hydraulic | 反义词:aerodynamic | 词性:形容词
