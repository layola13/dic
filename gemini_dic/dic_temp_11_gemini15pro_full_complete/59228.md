dysuric:/dɪsˈjʊərɪk/ | adj. | 排尿困难的，尿痛的 | Relating to or experiencing dysuria (painful or difficult urination). |  The patient reported dysuric symptoms, including burning and frequency. 病人报告了排尿困难的症状，包括灼痛和尿频。| Example: The doctor asked if I was experiencing any dysuric symptoms. 医生问我是否有排尿困难的症状。|  Synonyms: painful urination, difficult urination | Antonyms: painless urination, easy urination | adjective
