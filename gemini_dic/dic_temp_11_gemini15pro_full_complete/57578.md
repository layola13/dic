dramatizing: /ˈdræməˌtaɪzɪŋ/
verb
戏剧化；夸张；编写剧本
present participle of dramatize
The movie dramatizes the events of the war. 这部电影戏剧化地表现了战争的事件。
synonyms: exaggerating, overstating, sensationalizing, embellishing, heightening, amplifying
antonyms: understating, downplaying, minimizing
v.
