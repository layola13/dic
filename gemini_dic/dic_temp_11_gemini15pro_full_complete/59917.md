edentate:/iːˈdɛnteɪt/ | adj. n.| 无齿的，贫齿目动物 | Lacking teeth; belonging to the order Edentata (now considered obsolete), which formerly included sloths, anteaters, and armadillos. |  The giant anteater is an edentate mammal. 巨食蚁兽是一种无齿的哺乳动物。 |  The sloth is an edentate animal.  树懒是一种贫齿目动物。| 近义词: toothless | 反义词: toothed | 词性: adjective, noun

edentata:/iːdɛnˈteɪtə/ | n. | 贫齿目（现已废弃）| A former taxonomic order of mammals that included sloths, anteaters, and armadillos, now considered obsolete because the group is polyphyletic. | Edentata was once used to classify various toothless or near-toothless mammals. 贫齿目曾被用来给各种无齿或近乎无齿的哺乳动物分类。 |  Modern taxonomy has superseded the use of Edentata. 现代分类法已经取代了贫齿目的使用。 | 近义词: Xenarthra (partially) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *edentatus*, from *e-* (out of, without) + *dentatus* (toothed), from *dens* (tooth).
