ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *grōf, from Proto-Germanic *grōbō (“groove, ditch”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghrebh- (“to dig, scratch, scrape”). Cognate with Dutch groef (“groove”), German Grube (“pit, mine”).
