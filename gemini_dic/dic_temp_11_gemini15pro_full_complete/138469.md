novelistically:/ˌnɒvəlˈɪstɪkli/ /ˌnɑːvəlˈɪstɪkli/
adverb
小说式地；像小说一样
In a manner characteristic of or resembling a novel; fictitiously.
She described the events novelistically, adding dramatic details and emotional depth. 她以小说般的方式描述了这些事件，添加了戏剧性的细节和情感深度。
The biographer novelistically recreated the scene of their first meeting.  传记作家以小说般的方式重现了他们第一次见面的场景。
Near Synonyms: fictitiously, imaginatively, creatively, dramatically
Antonyms: factually, realistically, plainly, simply
Word Origin: From novelist + -ically.
