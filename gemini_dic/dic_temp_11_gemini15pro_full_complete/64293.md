equivalency: /ɪˈkwɪvələnsɪ/ /ɪˈkwɪvələntsi/
n.
等价；等效；相等
The state or fact of being equivalent.
High school diplomas are not always seen as having equivalency to a GED. 高中文凭并不总是被认为与GED(普通教育发展证书)等同。
Her computer skills have the equivalency of a master’s degree in computer science. 她的计算机技能相当于计算机科学硕士学位。
equivalence, parity, equality
inequality, disparity, difference
noun
