abaddon:/əˈbædən/ | n. | 毁灭, 深渊, 地狱 | A place of destruction or perdition; hell.  It comes from the Hebrew word "אֲבַדּוֹן" (’Ǎḇaddōn), meaning "destruction" or "place of destruction". In the New Testament, it is personified as an angel of the abyss. |  The preacher spoke of the evils of abaddon and the importance of redemption. 传教士谈到了地狱的罪恶和救赎的重要性。 |  The missile strike left the city in a state of abaddon. 导弹袭击使这座城市沦为一片废墟。 | synonyms: hell, abyss, underworld, inferno, perdition | antonyms: heaven, paradise | noun
