eteostic:/ˌiːtiˈɒstɪk/
adjective
藏头诗的; 以姓名首字母组成的
Composed of the initial letters of a name or names.
Example:  He wrote an eteostic poem for his wife's birthday, each line starting with a letter from her name.  他为妻子的生日写了一首藏头诗，每一行都以她名字中的一个字母开头。

Example:  Eteostic poems were a popular form of tribute in the Victorian era.  在维多利亚时代，藏头诗是一种流行的致敬方式。
synonym: acrostic
antonym: (No direct antonym exists)
词性: adjective
