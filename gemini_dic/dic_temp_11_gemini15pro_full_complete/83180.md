gutturals:/ˈɡʌtərəlz/
| n. |喉音；咽喉音|Sounds produced in the throat.  These sounds are often harsh or grating.|The language is full of gutturals, making it difficult for foreigners to pronounce.  这种语言充满了喉音，让外国人很难发音。|近义词:throaty sounds, guttural consonants|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps consider "vowel sounds," "sibilants," or "fricatives" depending on context)|词性:noun
