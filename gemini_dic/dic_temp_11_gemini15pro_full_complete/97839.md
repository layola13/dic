inlace:/ɪnˈleɪs/ | v. | 编织；使交织；插入 | To interweave, intertwine, or insert something into something else. |  The artisan inlaced gold thread into the fabric. 工匠将金线编织到织物中。

The story inlaced elements of fantasy with historical fact.  这个故事将奇幻元素与史实交织在一起。 | 近义词: interweave, intertwine, interlace, weave, braid | 反义词: disentangle, separate, unravel | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *inlacen, from in- + lace (“to fasten with a lace or cord”). Equivalent to in- +‎ lace.
