fugitivenesses:/ˌfjuːdʒɪˈtɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|逃亡，逃匿；短暂，易逝|The fugitivenesses of youth are often regretted in later life. 青春的短暂常常在晚年令人惋惜。|The fugitivenesses of memories can be frustrating.  记忆的易逝令人沮丧。|
|近义词: transience, ephemerality, brevity, impermanence|
|反义词: permanence, eternity, durability|
|词性: noun (plural)|


ETYMOLOGY->
From fugitive (adj. & n.) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *fugitīvus, from fugere "to flee."
