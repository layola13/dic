honeyless:/ˈhʌnilɪs/| adj.|无蜜的，缺乏甜蜜的|Lacking honey; without honey.  Figuratively, lacking sweetness or pleasantness.|The honeyless bees struggled to survive the winter.  这些没有蜂蜜的蜜蜂难以熬过冬天。|近义词：barren, sterile, unproductive|反义词:honeyed, sweet, rich|adjective
