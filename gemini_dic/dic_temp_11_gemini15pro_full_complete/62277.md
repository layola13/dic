encyst:/ɪnˈsɪst/ | v. | 包囊，形成囊肿；使…被包在囊内 | to enclose in a cyst or as if in a cyst; to become enclosed in a cyst |  The parasite will encyst itself in the muscle tissue. 寄生虫会在肌肉组织中形成包囊。| synonyms: encapsulate, enclose, encase | antonyms: excyst, release | verb


encystation:/ɪnˌsɪˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 包囊形成，囊肿形成 | the process of becoming enclosed in a cyst | The encystation of the amoeba allows it to survive harsh conditions. 变形虫的包囊形成使其能够在恶劣的条件下生存。| synonyms: encapsulation | antonyms: excystation | noun
