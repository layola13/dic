enrockment: /ɪnˈrɑːkmənt/ /ɛnˈrɒkmənt/ | n. |  围石, 石砌, 用岩石加固 | The act of encasing something in rock, or something that has been encased in rock.  Also, a structure made of rocks, especially one used to protect a shoreline or other area from erosion. | The enrockment of the riverbank prevented further flooding. 河岸的石砌防止了进一步的洪水泛滥。|  The enrockment protected the harbor from the storm surge.  石砌保护了港口免受风暴潮的侵袭。 | synonyms: riprap, revetment, rock fill | antonyms: excavation, dredging | noun
