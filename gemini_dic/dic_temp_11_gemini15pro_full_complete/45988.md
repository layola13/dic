cyanotic: /ˌsaɪəˈnɒtɪk/ /ˌsaɪəˈnɑːtɪk/
adjective
发绀的；青紫的
Marked by or causing a bluish or purplish discoloration of the skin and mucous membranes due to deficient oxygenation of the blood.
The patient's lips were cyanotic, indicating a lack of oxygen. 病人的嘴唇发绀，表明缺氧。
近义词: blue, purplish, livid
反义词: rosy, pink, flushed
词性: adjective
