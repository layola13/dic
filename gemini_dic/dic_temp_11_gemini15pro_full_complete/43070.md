cowpeas:/ˈkaʊˌpiz/ | n. |豇豆，黑眼豆|A type of bean (Vigna unguiculata) cultivated in warm regions, having edible pods and seeds.|例句：Cowpeas are a staple food in many parts of Africa. 豇豆是非洲许多地区的主食。

例句：I'm growing cowpeas in my garden this year. 我今年在我的花园里种植豇豆。|近义词：black-eyed peas, field peas|反义词:|noun
