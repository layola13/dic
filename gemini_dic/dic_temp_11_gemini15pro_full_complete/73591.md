foreteller:/fɔːrˈtelər/ |/fɔːrˈtɛlər/| n. |预言者，预卜者|A person who foretells future events; a prophet or soothsayer.|The ancient foreteller predicted the fall of the kingdom. 古代的预言家预言了王国的覆灭。|Synonyms: prophet, seer, soothsayer, oracle, diviner|Antonyms:  None (The concept lacks a direct opposite, though one might consider "skeptic" or "doubter" as loosely antonymous.)|Noun
