actinostome:/ˈæktɪnəˌstoʊm/| n.|放线孔；辐射孔|An actinostome is the mouth of a sea anemone or other similar invertebrate. It is typically located at the center of the oral disc and is surrounded by tentacles.|The actinostome of the anemone closed tightly when touched.|近义词:mouth, oral aperture|反义词:|n.
