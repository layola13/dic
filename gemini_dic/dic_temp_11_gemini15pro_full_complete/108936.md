leeks: /liːks/ | n. |韭菜|A long, cylindrical vegetable with flat, dark green leaves and a mild onion-like flavor. It is a member of the allium family, which also includes onions, garlic, and chives.  |例句：I added leeks to the soup for extra flavor. 我在汤里加了韭菜来提味。

|例句：Leeks are a common ingredient in French cuisine. 韭菜是法国菜中常见的食材。|近义词：scallions, green onions |反义词: (No direct antonym for a vegetable) |词性: noun
