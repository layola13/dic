multicasting:/ˈmʌltɪˌkɑːstɪŋ/ | n. |多播，组播|The act of transmitting a message to a group of recipients simultaneously.| Example: Multicasting is an efficient way to distribute video streams to multiple viewers. 多播是将视频流分发给多个观众的有效方式。|Example: The network supports IP multicasting for efficient data distribution. 该网络支持IP多播以实现高效的数据分发。|Synonyms: groupcasting|Antonyms: unicasting, broadcasting|noun
