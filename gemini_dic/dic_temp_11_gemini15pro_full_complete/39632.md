consequential:/ˌkɑːn.səˈkwen.ʃəl/ |/ˌkɒn.səˈkwen.ʃəl/| adj.|重要的；后果严重的；随之而来的|Following as a result or effect. Important, significant.|例句：The decision had consequential impacts on the company's future.  这个决定对公司的未来产生了重大影响。|例句：Her resignation was a consequential event that led to a major restructuring. 她的辞职是一个重要的事件，导致了重大的重组。|近义词：significant, important, momentous, far-reaching|反义词:insignificant, trivial, unimportant|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin consequencialis, from consequentia ‘consequence’.
