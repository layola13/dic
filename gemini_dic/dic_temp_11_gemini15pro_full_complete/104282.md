justiciable:/ˈdʒʌstɪʃəbl/ /dʒʌˈstiʃiəbl/
adjective
可司法审查的；可裁决的
Capable of being settled by a court of law; appropriate for judicial determination.
The issue of voting rights is a justiciable one.  投票权问题是一个可以由法院裁决的问题。
The question of whether the president acted within his authority is justiciable. 总统的行为是否在其权限范围内是一个可以司法审查的问题。

近义词:reviewable, actionable, triable
反义词:nonjusticiable

词性:adjective

词源:Late Middle English: from Old French justiciable, from justicier ‘judge’, from justice.
