outseek:/ˌaʊtˈsiːk/ | vt. | 比...更努力地寻找；寻找更有效率 | To search more diligently or effectively than someone else. |  She outseeked everyone in the library, determined to find the rare manuscript.  她在图书馆里比任何人都更努力地寻找，决心找到那份珍稀的手稿。| 近义词：outsearch, outhunt | 反义词：be outdone, be surpassed | verb
