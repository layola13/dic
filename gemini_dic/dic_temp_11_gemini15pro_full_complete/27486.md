campanula:/kæmˈpænjuːlə/ | n. |风铃草；钟花 |A plant with bell-shaped flowers, typically blue or purple. |例句：The campanula in my garden are blooming beautifully. 我花园里的风铃草开得很漂亮。

例句：She wore a dress adorned with delicate campanula prints. 她穿着一件装饰着精致风铃草图案的连衣裙。| 近义词：bellflower | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin campanula, diminutive of campana "bell".
