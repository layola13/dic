His gawkiness made him feel self-conscious at the dance. 他的笨拙让他在舞会上感到局促不安。|近义词：awkwardness, clumsiness, ungainliness|反义词: grace, elegance, poise|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gawky + -ness.  Gawky, meaning awkward or clumsy, likely originated in the 18th century, possibly related to the dialectal word "gawk," meaning to stare stupidly.
