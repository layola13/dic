morbidity:/mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/ /mɔːrˈbɪdɪɾi/| n. |发病率，病态，不健全|The morbidity rate for this disease is quite high. 这种疾病的发病率相当高。|近义词：illness, sickness, prevalence|反义词: health, wellness|noun

morbidity:/mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/ /mɔːrˈbɪdɪɾi/| n. |病态，不健全|His writings were filled with a disturbing morbidity. 他的作品充满了令人不安的病态。|近义词：unhealthiness, abnormality|反义词: normality, health|noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *morbiditas, from Latin morbidus (“diseased, sickly”), from morbus (“disease, sickness”).
