identity:/aɪˈdɛntɪti/ | v. | 认同，识别 | to recognize or establish as being a particular person or thing | The police identified the body as that of a local businessman. 警方确认尸体是当地一名商人的。| synonyms: recognize, pinpoint, distinguish, determine | antonyms: confuse, mistake | verb
