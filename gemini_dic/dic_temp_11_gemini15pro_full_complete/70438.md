figurability:/ˌfɪɡjərəˈbɪləti/
n.
可塑性; 形象化能力;  塑造能力
The quality of being figurable; capable of being shaped or molded.  The capacity to be represented in a figure or image, or to be understood figuratively.
Her artistic figurability allowed her to create stunning sculptures from clay.  她的艺术塑造能力使她能用黏土创作出令人惊叹的雕塑。
