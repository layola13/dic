functionary:/ˈfʌŋkʃəˌnɛri/| n.| 官员，公务员，职员|A person who holds an office or performs a function, especially in government or an organization.|The low-level functionary shuffled the papers on his desk.  这个低级官员整理了他桌子上的文件。|Synonyms: official, bureaucrat, administrator, agent|Antonyms: civilian, nonofficial|noun

functionary:/ˈfʌŋkʃəˌnɛri/| adj.| 职务上的，公务的|Relating to or characteristic of a functionary or their duties.|His functionary duties included processing paperwork and answering phones. 他的公务职责包括处理文书工作和接听电话。|Synonyms: official, bureaucratic|Antonyms: unofficial, personal|adjective
