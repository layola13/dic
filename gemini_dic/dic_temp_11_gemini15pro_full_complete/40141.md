contemporaries:/kənˈtɛmpəˌrɛriz/
n.
同时代的人；同辈
People or things existing or occurring at the same time.
Shakespeare and his contemporaries wrote plays in blank verse. 莎士比亚和他的同时代的人都用无韵诗写剧本。
peers, coevals
predecessors, successors
noun
