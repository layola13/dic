chiseled: /ˈtʃɪzəld/ (US), /ˈtʃɪzəld/ (UK)
adjective
轮廓分明的，凿刻般的
clearly defined, as if cut with a chisel
His chiseled features and piercing blue eyes made him strikingly handsome. 他轮廓分明的五官和锐利的蓝眼睛使他格外英俊。
近义词: sculpted, sharp, defined, angular
反义词: round, soft, undefined, pudgy
词性: adjective


chiseled: /ˈtʃɪzəld/ (US), /ˈtʃɪzəld/ (UK)
verb (past participle of chisel)
凿刻的
carved or shaped with a chisel
The statue was chiseled from a single block of marble. 这座雕像是由一整块大理石凿刻而成的。
近义词: carved, sculpted, engraved
反义词: molded, cast
词性: verb (past participle)


词源: From the verb "chisel" + "-ed" (past participle suffix), ultimately from Old French "cisel", meaning "chisel".
