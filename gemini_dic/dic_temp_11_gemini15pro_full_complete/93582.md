imated: /ˈɪmɪteɪtɪd/
adjective | 模仿的，效仿的 | Copied or following a model or example. | Her style was often imitated but never equaled. 她的风格经常被模仿，但从未被超越。|  The painting was so expertly imitated that it fooled the experts. 这幅画模仿得如此精妙，连专家都被骗了。| 近义词: copied, emulated, replicated, mimicked | 反义词: original, unique | 词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin imitatus, past participle of imitari "to copy, portray, imitate," from imago (genitive imaginis) "image, likeness."
