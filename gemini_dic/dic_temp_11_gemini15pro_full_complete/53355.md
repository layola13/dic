disadvise:/ˌdɪsədˈvaɪz/
verb
劝阻，不建议
To recommend against; to counsel someone not to do something.
I strongly disadvise going out in this weather. 我强烈建议你不要在这种天气外出。
The doctor disadvised her from lifting heavy objects. 医生建议她不要提重物。
synonyms: caution, warn, recommend against, deprecate, discourage
antonyms: advise, encourage, recommend, support, advocate
词性: 动词(verb)
