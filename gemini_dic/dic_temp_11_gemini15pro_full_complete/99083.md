interbrachial:/ˌɪntərˈbreɪkiəl/| adj.|肱间的，臂间的|Situated between the arms or forelimbs.  Relating to the region between the upper arms.|例句：The interbrachial membrane of the bat allows it to fly. 蝙蝠的臂间膜使它能够飞行。

例句：The interbrachial veins are located in the upper arm. 肱静脉位于上臂。|近义词：axillary, brachial|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "dorsal" if referring to back vs. front)|词性: adjective
