babushka: /bəˈbʊʃkə/ (US) /bəˈbuʃkə/ (UK)| n. |巴布什卡，俄式头巾，老太太|A headscarf tied under the chin, typically worn by Russian peasant women. Also refers to an elderly woman, especially one perceived as being old-fashioned.|例句：She wore a bright red babushka. 她戴着一个鲜红色的巴布什卡头巾。

例句：My babushka used to bake the most delicious bread. 我的祖母过去常常烤最美味的面包。|近义词：headscarf, kerchief, grandmother, old woman|反义词: young woman|词性: 名词
