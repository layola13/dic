autology:/ɔːˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 套套逻辑; 同义反复 | A statement that is true by necessity or by virtue of its logical form. A self-evident truth.  | Example: "A bachelor is an unmarried man" is an autology.  一个单身汉就是一个未婚男人（这是同义反复）. | Synonym: truism, tautology, pleonasm | Antonym: contradiction, fallacy | noun

autology:/ɔːˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 自我学; 自身论 | The study of oneself or of one's own work.  | Example: His autobiography was a fascinating exercise in autology. 他的自传是一次迷人的自我剖析. | Synonym: self-study, introspection | Antonym: N/A | noun
