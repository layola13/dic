earthquakes:/ˈɜːrθkweɪks/ | n. |地震|A sudden violent shaking of the ground, typically causing great destruction, as a result of movements within the earth's crust or volcanic action. |例句：The city was devastated by a series of powerful earthquakes. 这座城市被一系列强烈的地震摧毁了。

例句：Earthquakes are common in this region. 地震在这个地区很常见。|近义词：tremors, temblors|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用stability（稳定性）)|词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *erthequake, equivalent to earth +‎ quake.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *erþō- + *kwakōną (“to shake”).
