outfitted:/aʊtˈfɪtɪd/
adjective
配备的，装备的
supplied with equipment or clothes, especially for a particular purpose
The expedition was outfitted with the latest climbing gear. 探险队配备了最新的攀登装备。
equipped, furnished, geared up, decked out
unequipped, unfurnished, bare
词性:形容词
