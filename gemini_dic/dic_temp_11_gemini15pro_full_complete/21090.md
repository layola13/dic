blink:/blɪŋk/ /blɪŋk/| v. n.|眨眼，闪光|To close and open the eyes quickly.  A quick flash of light.|She blinked rapidly in the bright sunlight. 她在明亮的阳光下快速地眨着眼睛。

The light blinked on and off. 灯一闪一闪的。|近义词：wink, flicker, flash|反义词：stare, gaze|词性:动词/名词

blink:/blɪŋk/ /blɪŋk/| n.|眨眼，瞬间|The act of blinking; a very short time.|In the blink of an eye, the bird had flown away.  一眨眼的功夫，鸟就飞走了。

It all happened in a blink. 一切都发生在一瞬间。|近义词：instant, moment, jiffy|反义词：eternity, age|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blinken*, from Old English *blincian* (“to gleam, shine, glitter, twinkle”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse *blykkja* (“to blink”) and Middle Low German *blinken* (“to shine”).
