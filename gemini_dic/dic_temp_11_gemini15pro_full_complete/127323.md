naysayers:/ˈneɪˌseɪərz/ | n. |反对者，唱反调的人 | A person who criticizes, objects to, or opposes something. |例句：The naysayers were quick to point out the flaws in the plan. 反对者很快就指出了该计划的缺陷。

例句：Despite the naysayers, she persevered and achieved her goal. 尽管有人反对，她还是坚持不懈，最终实现了目标。|近义词：critics, dissenters, objectors, opponents |反义词：supporters, proponents, advocates | 词性: 名词
