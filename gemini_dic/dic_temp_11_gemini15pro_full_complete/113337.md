luxuriant:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/ | adj. | 茂盛的，繁茂的；丰富的，华丽的 | (of vegetation) growing thickly and strongly;  (of hair, fur, etc.)  thick and healthy;  elaborate and richly ornamented |  The jungle was filled with luxuriant vegetation.  丛林里长满了茂盛的植物。 | synonyms: abundant, lush, profuse, opulent, lavish | antonyms: sparse, meager, barren, scanty | adjective

luxuriant:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/ | adj. | 奢华的，丰富的 |  She has a luxuriant head of hair. 她有一头浓密的头发。 | synonyms: rich, abundant, opulent | antonyms: poor, meager, scanty | adjective

luxuriant:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/ | adj. | （语言、风格）华丽的，辞藻丰富的 | His writing style is somewhat luxuriant. 他的写作风格有点华丽。 | synonyms:  florid, ornate, elaborate | antonyms: plain, simple, austere | adjective
