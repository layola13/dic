abnormals: /æbˈnɔːrməlz/ | n. |异常，异常情况，异常事物| Things that are not typical, usual, or regular; deviations from the norm. |  The doctor noted several abnormals in the patient's blood test. 医生注意到病人的血液检测中有几项异常。|  The factory discarded the abnormals from the production line. 工厂将生产线上的不合格产品丢弃了。| 近义词: anomalies, irregularities, deviations, exceptions | 反义词: normals, standards, regulars | 词性: 名词 (plural)


abnormal: /æbˈnɔːrməl/ | adj. |异常的，反常的| Not typical, usual, or regular; deviating from the norm. | The patient's heart rate was abnormal. 病人的心率异常。|  The weather has been abnormal this summer. 今年夏天的天气反常。| 近义词: atypical, unusual, irregular, deviant, anomalous | 反义词: normal, typical, usual, regular, standard | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin abnormalis, from ab- ‘away from’ + normalis (see normal).
