anthophagous:/ænˈθɒfəɡəs/
adjective
食人的；以人肉为食的
Feeding on human flesh; cannibalistic.
例句：Some ancient tribes were believed to be anthophagous.  一些古代部落被认为是食人的。

例句：Anthophagous practices are considered taboo in most cultures.  在大多数文化中，食人行为都被视为禁忌。

近义词：cannibalistic, man-eating
反义词：herbivorous, carnivorous (referring to other diets)
词性：adjective

词源: From Greek *ánthrōpophagos*, from *ánthrōpos* "human being" + *-phagos* "eating," from *phageîn* "to eat."
