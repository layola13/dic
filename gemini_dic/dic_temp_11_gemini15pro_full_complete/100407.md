intertentacular:/ˌɪntərtenˈtækjʊlər/| adj.|触手间的，位于触手之间的|Situated between tentacles.  It describes something located or occurring between the tentacles of an animal, typically a cephalopod like a squid or octopus.|例句：The intertentacular membrane helps the octopus to swim.  章鱼的触手间膜帮助它游泳。|例句：The intertentacular space is where the octopus holds its prey. 章鱼在触手间的空间里抓住猎物。|近义词:interbrachial (specifically for arms/brachia, not tentacles)|反义词: N/A |词性:adjective
