husbandage:/ˈhʌzbəndɪdʒ/
n.
管理，经营；节俭；精打细算
The act or practice of managing resources economically; thrift.
He showed excellent husbandage of his limited resources. 他精打细算地利用了他有限的资源。
economy, thrift, management, frugality
prodigality, wastefulness
noun
