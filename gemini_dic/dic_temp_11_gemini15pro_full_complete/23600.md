bramblier:/bræmbli.eɪ/ /ˈbræmbliər/
n.
荆棘丛，黑莓丛
A thorny shrub, especially a blackberry bush.
The path was overgrown with brambliers, making it difficult to pass.  这条小路被荆棘丛覆盖，很难通行。
The birds nested deep within the bramblier. 鸟儿在荆棘丛深处筑巢。
近义词: thicket, bramble, thornbush
反义词: clearing, meadow
