narcs:/nɑːrks/
n.
禁毒警察；告密者；自恋者
(plural of narc)  A shortened form of "narcotics agent" (a law enforcement officer who specializes in drug-related crimes) or "narcissist" (someone with an excessive sense of self-importance).
例句：The narcs busted a major drug ring. 禁毒警察破获了一个主要的贩毒集团。

例句：He's such a narc, always telling on everyone. 他真是个告密者，总是打小报告。

近义词：informant, snitch (for "narc" meaning informant), egotist, narcissist (for "narc" meaning narcissist)
反义词：N/A
词性：noun (plural)
