overdaintily:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪntɪli/| adv.|过精致地；过分讲究地|In an excessively dainty or fastidious manner; with an exaggerated delicacy or refinement.|She picked at her food overdaintily, barely touching the vegetables. 她过分讲究地挑剔着食物，几乎没碰蔬菜。|近义词：fussily, meticulously, primly|反义词：sloppily, carelessly, roughly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessively) + "daintily" (in a dainty manner). "Dainty" comes from Old French "daintie" meaning "delicacy, something pleasing." Ultimately from Latin "dignitas" meaning "worthiness, dignity."
