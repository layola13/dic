disvest:/dɪsˈvɛst/ /daɪˈvɛst/| vt.|剥夺，使脱去，出售，放弃|To deprive or strip of clothing, rank, power, or property; to sell off or dispose of (assets, investments, etc.).|The company is divesting its holdings in the tobacco industry.  该公司正在出售其在烟草行业的股份。|The king was divested of his power. 国王被剥夺了权力。|synonyms: sell off, dispose of, liquidate, unload, relinquish, forfeit|antonyms: invest, acquire, accumulate|verb

disvest:/dɪsˈvɛst/ /daɪˈvɛst/| n.|撤资，剥夺|The act of divesting.|The divestment of the company's assets was completed last month. 公司资产的出售已于上个月完成。|synonyms: divestiture, sale, disposal|antonyms: investment, acquisition|noun
