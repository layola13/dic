eulogistical:/ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪkəl/
adjective
赞美的，颂扬的
Characterized by or containing eulogy; praising highly.
The eulogistical speech moved the audience to tears.  这充满赞美的演讲使观众感动得流下了眼泪。
The mayor delivered a eulogistical address in honor of the fallen firefighters. 市长发表了颂扬牺牲的消防员的讲话。
近义词:laudatory, commendatory, complimentary
反义词:critical, derogatory, defamatory
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek eulogia "praise" + -istic + -al.
