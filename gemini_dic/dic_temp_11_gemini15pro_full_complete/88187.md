hilus:/ˈhaɪləs/| n. | 肺门；脾门；肾门| A hilum is the point of entry and exit for blood vessels, nerves, lymphatic vessels, and other structures in organs such as the lungs, kidneys, spleen, and ovaries.  It's essentially the "root" of the organ where everything connects. | 例句：The radiologist noted enlargement of the hilum on the chest x-ray. 放射科医生注意到胸部X光片上肺门增大。

例句：The hilum of the spleen is where the splenic artery and vein enter and exit. 脾门是脾动脉和脾静脉出入的地方。 | 近义词：root (in the context of organ anatomy) | 反义词: N/A |词性:noun
