loanword: /ˈloʊnˌwɜːrd/ (US) /ˈləʊnˌwɜːd/ (UK)
n.
外来词，借词
A word adopted from a foreign language with little or no modification.
例句：
"Sushi" is a loanword from Japanese.
“寿司”是一个来自日语的外来词。

"Kindergarten" is a loanword from German.
“幼儿园”是一个来自德语的外来词。

近义词：
borrowing, adopted word
反义词:
native word, inherited word
词性
noun
