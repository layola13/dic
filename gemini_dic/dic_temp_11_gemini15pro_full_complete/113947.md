magazinish: /ˌmæɡəˈziːnɪʃ/
| adj. |类似杂志的；杂志风格的|Resembling or characteristic of a magazine, especially in being superficial or sensational.|Her writing style is a bit magazinish, focusing more on style than substance. 她的写作风格有点像杂志，注重风格胜过实质。|例句：The website has a very magazinish layout.  这个网站的排版很像杂志。|近义词：glossy, journalistic|反义词：scholarly, academic|词性：形容词

词源: magazine + -ish.  "Magazine" comes from the Arabic word "makhazin," meaning storehouses. The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of."
