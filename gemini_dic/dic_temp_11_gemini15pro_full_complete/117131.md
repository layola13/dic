meandrous:/miˈændrəs/| adj.|蜿蜒的，曲折的|Following a winding or intricate course. Characterized by many twists and turns.|The river flowed in a meandrous course through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。|近义词：winding, sinuous, tortuous, serpentine|反义词：straight, direct|词性:形容词
meandering:/miˈændərɪŋ/| adj.|蜿蜒的，曲折的|Wandering aimlessly or indirectly.|We took a meandering walk through the woods. 我们在树林里漫步。|近义词：winding, rambling, indirect|反义词：straight, direct|词性:形容词

词源:
From the Greek "Maiandros" (Μαίανδρος), the name of a winding river in Phrygia (now Turkey), known for its many turns.  The word evolved into the Latin "Maeander" and then into English as "meander" (both noun and verb), and finally "meandrous" (adjective).
