methylate:/ˈmeθɪˌleɪt/ (US), /ˈmiːθɪleɪt/ (UK)| v. | 甲基化 | To add a methyl group (CH3) to a molecule. |  Scientists methylated the DNA to study gene expression. 科学家们将DNA甲基化以研究基因表达。| The company methylated the compound to improve its stability. 公司对该化合物进行甲基化以提高其稳定性。| 近义词: alkylate | 反义词: demethylate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From methyl + -ate.  Methyl from methylene (from Greek *methy* "wine" + *hyle* "wood") + -ene (a suffix used in forming the names of hydrocarbons).  -ate is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to cause to be characterized by" or "to treat, combine, or impregnate with".
