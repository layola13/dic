ghoulishness:/ˈɡuːlɪʃnəs/ | n. |  可怕；残忍；令人毛骨悚然 | The state or quality of being ghoulish; gruesome or morbid fascination. | The ghoulishness of the crime scene shocked even the seasoned detectives. 犯罪现场的恐怖景象即使是经验丰富的侦探也感到震惊。| synonyms: gruesomeness, morbidness, macabreness | antonyms: pleasantness, agreeableness, cheerfulness | noun


Etymology: From ghoulish + -ness.  "Ghoulish" comes from "ghoul," which originates from Arabic "ghūl," a desert demon that robs graves and feeds on corpses.
