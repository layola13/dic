co-operativeness:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪvnəs/| n.|合作性，协作性|The success of the project depended on the co-operativeness of all team members.  这个项目的成功取决于所有团队成员的合作性。|例句：The team's co-operativeness ensured the project's success.  团队的合作精神确保了项目的成功。|近义词：collaboration, teamwork, synergy|反义词：uncooperativeness, antagonism, resistance|词性:名词
Etymology: From co-operative + -ness.  Co-operative is from co- + operative, with operative ultimately from Latin *operativus.
