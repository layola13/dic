inflammatory:/ɪnˈflæmətɔːri/ (US) /ɪnˈflæmətəri/ (UK)| adj.| 炎症的；煽动性的 |Relating to or causing inflammation of a part of the body; tending to excite anger, disorder, or tumult.| The politician's inflammatory rhetoric incited violence.  这位政客煽动性的言论引发了暴力。| Synonyms: provocative, incendiary, rabble-rousing | Antonyms: soothing, calming, conciliatory | Adjective

Etymology->

From Late Latin *inflammātōrius*, from Latin inflammō (“I set on fire, kindle, inflame”), from in (“in, on, upon”) + flammō (“I flame”), from flamma (“flame, blaze”).
