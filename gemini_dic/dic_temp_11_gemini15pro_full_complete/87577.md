heteropoda:/ˌhɛtərˈɒpədə/ /ˌhɛtəˈrɑːpədə/ | n. |异足类，蟹蛛科| A taxonomic group of spiders, commonly known as huntsman spiders, characterized by their large size and crab-like legs. |例句：Heteropoda venatoria is a common species of heteropoda found in tropical regions.  白额高脚蛛是常见于热带地区的异足类蜘蛛。

例句：Some species of heteropoda can grow to have a leg span of up to 30 centimeters. 有些种类的异足类蜘蛛腿展可达30厘米。|近义词：huntsman spider, giant crab spider |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group) |词性: noun
