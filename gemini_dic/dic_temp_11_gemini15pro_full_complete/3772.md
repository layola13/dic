agitato:/ˌædʒɪˈtɑːtoʊ/ | adj. | 激动地，不安地 |  A musical direction indicating that the piece should be played in an agitated or excited manner. It derives from the Italian verb "agitare," meaning "to agitate" or "to shake." | The pianist played the agitato passage with furious energy. 钢琴家以狂暴的能量演奏了那段激动人心的乐段。| synonyms: restless, disturbed, frantic, frenzied | antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene | adjective
