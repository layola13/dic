galactin:/ɡəˈlæktɪn/| n.|半乳糖蛋白|A protein that binds and agglutinates galactose-specific receptors on the surface of cells, particularly macrophages and neutrophils, and promotes a variety of cellular activities, including phagocytosis, chemotaxis, and cytokine release. Galactin is also known as galactose-binding protein.|Galectin-3 plays a role in cell adhesion and activation of immune cells.|近义词:galactose-binding protein|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a protein with such specific function) |词性:noun
