continentals:/ˌkɒntɪˈnɛntlz/ /ˌkɑːntɪˈnɛntlz/
noun
大陆人；欧洲大陆人；(尤指美国独立战争时期)大陆军士兵；大陆纸币
People who live on a continent, especially the continent of Europe; (especially during the American Revolutionary War) soldiers in the Continental Army; paper money issued by the Continental Congress.
The continentals were outnumbered but fought bravely. 欧洲大陆的士兵人数上处于劣势，但他们英勇作战。
synonyms:mainlanders, Europeans
antonyms:islanders
n.

continental:/ˌkɒntɪˈnɛnt(ə)l/ /ˌkɑːntɪˈnɛnt(ə)l/
adjective
大陆的；大陆性的；欧洲大陆的；(尤指美国独立战争时期)大陆军的；大陆会议的
Relating to or characteristic of a continent, especially the continent of Europe; (especially during the American Revolutionary War) relating to the Continental Army or the Continental Congress.
The hotel offers a continental breakfast. 这家酒店提供欧陆式早餐。
synonyms:European, mainland
antonyms:insular, island
adj.
