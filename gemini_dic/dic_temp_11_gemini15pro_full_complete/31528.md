charte:/ʃɑːrt/| n.|宪章，特许状，航海图|A written document outlining the rights and privileges of a town, corporation, or other body.|The town's ancient charte guaranteed its citizens certain trading rights. 该镇古老的宪章保障了其公民的某些贸易权利。|近义词：charter, constitution, statute|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology: From Old North French *charte (Old French chartre), from Latin charta (“paper, papyrus; page, leaf”), from Ancient Greek χάρτης (khártēs, “sheet of papyrus”). Doublet of card.
