nonapportionable:/ˌnɒnəˈpɔːʃənəbəl/
| adj. |不可分摊的，不可分配的 |Not capable of being apportioned or divided proportionally.  Relating to costs or expenses that cannot be assigned to specific departments, products, or activities.|The overhead costs of the head office are considered nonapportionable and are shared equally among the branches.| 近义词: indivisible, unallocable | 反义词: apportionable, divisible |词性:形容词
