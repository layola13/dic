nonsupposed:/ˌnɒnsəˈpoʊzd/ | /ˌnɑːnsəˈpoʊzd/ | adj. | 非预期的，不该发生的，未被假定的 | Not supposed to happen or exist; unexpected or inappropriate. | She found herself in a nonsupposed meeting. 她发现自己身处一个她不该参加的会议。|  He entered a nonsupposed area of the building. 他进入了大楼里一个不允许进入的区域。 | 近义词：unexpected, inappropriate, unforeseen, unintended | 反义词：expected, appropriate, intended, foreseen | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "supposed" (past participle of "suppose").  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "supposed," creating the sense of something not being as it should be or was expected to be.
