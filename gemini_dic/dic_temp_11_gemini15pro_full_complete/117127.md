meanderings:/miˈændərɪŋz/
| n. | 漫游，曲折的路径，迂回曲折的谈话 |  A journey involving winding turns; rambling or aimless wandering; digressive or circuitous discourse. |  His speech was full of meanderings, never getting to the point. 他的演讲充满了迂回曲折，从未切中要害。| 近义词：wanderings, ramblings, digressions | 反义词: directness, straightness | 词性: 名词

meandering:/miˈændərɪŋ/
| adj. | 蜿蜒的，曲折的，漫无目的的 | Winding, twisting, or rambling. | The river followed a meandering course through the valley. 这条河沿着蜿蜒的河道流经山谷。| 近义词：winding, twisting, rambling, circuitous | 反义词: straight, direct | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the Greek "Maiandros," the name of a winding river in Phrygia (now Büyük Menderes in Turkey).
