breechloader:/ˈbriːtʃˌləʊdə(r)/ | n. | 后膛装填枪 | A firearm that is loaded through the breech (the rear end of the barrel) rather than the muzzle. | The breechloader replaced the muzzleloader as the standard infantry weapon in the 19th century.  后膛装填枪在19世纪取代了前膛装填枪，成为标准的步兵武器。| synonym: breech-loading rifle, breech-loading gun | antonym: muzzleloader | noun
