eulogization:/ˌjuːlədʒɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
颂扬，赞美
The act of praising someone or something highly, typically in a formal speech.
The eulogization of the war hero was both moving and inspiring.  这位战争英雄的颂词既感人又鼓舞人心。
praise, commendation, tribute, extolment, laudation
criticism, condemnation, censure, denunciation
noun
