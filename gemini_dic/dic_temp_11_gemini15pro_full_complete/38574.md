concomitant:/kənˈkɒmɪtənt/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtənt/| adj. n.|伴随的，共存的；伴随物|Existing or occurring with something else, often in a lesser way; something that accompanies something else.|The fever was concomitant with the infection.  发烧伴随着感染。|近义词：accompanying, attendant, concurrent, coincident, related|反义词：unrelated, separate, independent|词性: adjective, noun

concomitant:/kənˈkɒmɪtənt/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtənt/| n.|伴随物|Something that accompanies something else.|Loss of appetite is a frequent concomitant of the disease. 食欲不振是这种疾病常见的伴随症状。|近义词：accompaniment, attendant, corollary|反义词：None|词性: noun
