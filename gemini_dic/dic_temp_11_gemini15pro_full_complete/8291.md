anti-malthusian:/ˌæntiˈmælθjuːziən/ | adj. n. | 反马尔萨斯主义的；反马尔萨斯主义者 |  Opposed to the views of Thomas Robert Malthus, particularly his theory that population growth will outstrip resources.  |  The anti-Malthusian argued that technological advances would prevent widespread famine. 反马尔萨斯主义者认为，技术进步将阻止大范围饥荒的发生。| synonyms:  cornucopian, optimistic (regarding population growth) | antonyms:  Malthusian, pessimistic (regarding population growth) | adjective, noun


anti-malthusian:/ˌæntiˈmælθjuːziən/ | n. | 反马尔萨斯主义者 | A person who opposes the views of Thomas Robert Malthus. |  Many anti-Malthusians believe that human ingenuity will solve resource scarcity problems. 许多反马尔萨斯主义者认为，人类的智慧将解决资源短缺问题。 | synonyms: cornucopian | antonyms: Malthusian | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + Malthusian, referring to Thomas Robert Malthus (1766–1834), English economist and demographer who argued that population growth would eventually outstrip food supply.
