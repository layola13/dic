abiotic:/ˌeɪbaɪˈɒtɪk/ /ˌeɪbaɪˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
非生物的；无生命的
Characterized by the absence of life or living organisms. Relating to non-living things in the environment, such as sunlight, temperature, or water.
Abiotic factors like temperature and rainfall influence the distribution of plant species.  非生物因素，如温度和降雨，影响植物物种的分布。
The abiotic components of an ecosystem include rocks, water, and air. 生态系统的非生物组成部分包括岩石、水和空气。
non-living, inanimate
biotic, living
adjective
