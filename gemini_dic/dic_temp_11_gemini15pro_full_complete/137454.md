nonthirstier:/nɒnˈθɜːstiər/
adjective
不更渴的；更不渴的
Not more thirsty; less thirsty.
He felt nonthirstier after the small sip of water.  他喝了一小口水后，感觉不更渴了。
She was nonthirstier than her brother after the hike.  徒步旅行后，她比她哥哥更不渴。
Near Antonyms: thirstier, more thirsty
Antonyms: less thirsty, not thirsty
词性: adjective
