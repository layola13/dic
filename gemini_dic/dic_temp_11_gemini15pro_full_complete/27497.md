campering:/ˈkæmpɪŋ/ | n. | 露营，野营 | The act of staying and sleeping outdoors, usually in a tent. | We went camping in the mountains last weekend. 我们上周末去山上露营了。| camping trip:  We're planning a camping trip to Yosemite. 我们正计划去约塞米蒂露营。| 近义词: camping out | 反义词: staying indoors

campering:/ˈkæmpɪŋ/ | adj. | 露营的，野营的 | Related to or used for camping. | We bought some new camping equipment. 我们买了一些新的露营设备。 |  We packed our camping gear and headed out.  我们收拾好露营装备出发了。 | 近义词: outdoorsy | 反义词: indoor

词性:  名词 (n.) 和 形容词 (adj.)
