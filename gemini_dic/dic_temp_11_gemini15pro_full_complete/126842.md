naphthol:/ˈnæfθɑːl, ˈnæfθɒl/| n. |萘酚|An aromatic organic compound derived from naphthalene by replacing one hydrogen atom with a hydroxyl group. It exists in two isomeric forms, alpha-naphthol and beta-naphthol, and is used in making dyes and perfumes.|Naphthol is used as an intermediate in the manufacture of some dyes.|近义词:hydroxynaphthalene|反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
