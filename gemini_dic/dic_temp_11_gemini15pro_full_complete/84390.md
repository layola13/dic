hallelujatic:/ˌhælɪluːˈjætɪk/ | adj. | 赞颂上帝的；狂喜的；欢欣鼓舞的 | Characterized by or expressing praise of God; ecstatically joyful. | The choir sang a hallelujatic anthem.  唱诗班演唱了一首赞美上帝的圣歌。|  The crowd erupted in hallelujatic cheers when the team won. 当球队获胜时，人群爆发出欢呼声。 | 近义词: joyful, ecstatic, celebratory, jubilant | 反义词: mournful, sorrowful, depressed | 词性: 形容词

词源：From Late Latin hallelujaticus, from Greek allelouiatikos, from allelouia (see hallelujah).
