innervation:/ˌɪnərˈveɪʃn/
n.
神经支配；神经分布
The process of supplying nerves to a body part or organ.
The innervation of the heart allows it to beat regularly.  心脏的神经支配使其能够规律地跳动。

The loss of innervation to the muscles can cause paralysis. 肌肉失去神经支配会导致瘫痪。

近义词: nerve supply
反义词: denervation

词性: noun
