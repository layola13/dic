morphologists:/mɔːrˈfɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 形态学家 |  A person who studies morphology, the study of the forms of things, in particular:
* **Linguistics:** the study of the formation of words.
* **Biology:** the study of the form and structure of organisms and their specific structural features. |  The morphologists analyzed the complex word formation processes of the ancient language.  形态学家们分析了古代语言复杂的构词过程。 |  linguists, biologists, scientists, researchers, analysts | N/A | noun
