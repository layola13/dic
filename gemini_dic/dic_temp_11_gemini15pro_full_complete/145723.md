overcredulity:/ˌoʊvərkrəˈduːləti/
|n.|过度轻信，过分相信|An excessive readiness to believe things, even without sufficient evidence.|His overcredulity made him an easy target for scammers. 他过于轻信使他很容易成为骗子的目标。|Synonyms: gullibility, naivety, credulousness|Antonyms: skepticism, cynicism, disbelief|noun
