oedipal:/ˈiːdɪp(ə)l, ˈɛdɪp(ə)l/| adj.|俄狄浦斯的，恋母情结的|Relating to or characteristic of the Oedipus complex, in which a child has unconscious sexual desires for the parent of the opposite sex and feelings of rivalry with the parent of the same sex.|His oedipal feelings manifested in a strong attachment to his mother and a resentment towards his father. 他对母亲的强烈依恋和对父亲的怨恨体现了他的俄狄浦斯情结。|近义词：incestuous|反义词：None|词性:形容词
