loom:/luːm/
verb
出现，隐约出现；逼近
appear as a shadowy form, especially one that is large or threatening.
A dark shape loomed out of the fog. 一个黑影从雾中隐约出现。
emerge indistinctly; come into view in indistinct and enlarged form.
Dangers loomed ahead. 危险隐约地出现在前面。
近义词: emerge, appear, materialize, take shape
反义词: disappear, recede, vanish
词性: verb


loom:/luːm/
noun
织布机
a machine for weaving cloth.
The weaver sat at her loom. 织布工坐在她的织布机前。
近义词: weaving machine
反义词: 无
词性: noun


词源:
From Middle English *lomen, from Old English *gelōma (“tool, implement”), from Proto-Germanic *lōman (“tool, utensil”), from Proto-Indo-European *(H)lew- (“to cut off, divide”). Cognate with Old Frisian lōm (“tool”), Old Saxon lōma (“utensil, tool”), Old High German luoma (“tool, utensil”), Icelandic lómi (“loom”).
