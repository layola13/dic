nonchronical: /ˌnɒnˈkrɒnɪkəl/ | adj. | 非慢性的，非长期的 | Not chronic; not lasting a long time. |  This is a nonchronical illness, so you should recover soon. 这不是慢性病，所以你应该很快就会康复。|  The pain was nonchronical, lasting only a few days.  疼痛不是慢性的，只持续了几天。 | 近义词: acute, temporary, transient | 反义词: chronic, persistent, long-term | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "chronical" (from Greek "khronikos" meaning "of time").
