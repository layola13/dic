crouches:/ˈkraʊtʃɪz/| v.|蹲伏，蜷缩|Third-person singular present tense form of "crouch".  It describes the action of lowering one's body close to the ground by bending one's legs.|The cat crouches in the grass, waiting to pounce. 猫蹲在草丛里，等待着猛扑。|近义词：stoops, bends, squats|反义词:stands, rises|词性:verb

crouch:/kraʊtʃ/| v. n.|蹲伏，蜷缩；蹲伏的姿势|To lower one's body close to the ground by bending one's legs.|He crouched down to tie his shoelace. 他蹲下来系鞋带。|近义词：stoop, bend, squat|反义词:stand, rise|词性:verb and noun

词源:
From Old French *crouchir, from croche "hook, bend," from Old Norse krókr "hook."  The sense evolution is from "to be bent like a hook" to "to stoop or crouch."
