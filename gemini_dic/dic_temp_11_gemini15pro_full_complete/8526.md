anticensorious:/ˌæntiˌsɛnˈsɔːriəs/
adjective
不爱批评的；不吹毛求疵的
Not inclined to criticize or find fault; tolerant and forgiving.
Her anticensorious nature made her a beloved teacher. 她不爱批评的性格使她成为一位受人爱戴的老师。
The anticensorious editor allowed the controversial article to be published.  这位宽容的编辑允许发表这篇有争议的文章。

近义词: tolerant, forgiving, lenient, indulgent, broad-minded, understanding, accepting
反义词: censorious, critical, fault-finding, judgmental, hypercritical, disapproving

词性: adjective
