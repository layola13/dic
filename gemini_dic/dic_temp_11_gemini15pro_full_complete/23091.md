bounteousness:/ˈbaʊntiəsnəs/| n.|慷慨；大方；丰富|The bounteousness of the harvest ensured no one went hungry. 丰收的慷慨确保没有人挨饿。|The king was known for his bounteousness to the poor.  国王以对穷人的慷慨而闻名。|近义词：generosity, liberality, abundance, bounty|反义词：stinginess, meanness, scarcity|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bountevousnes*, from bountevous +‎ -nes, equivalent to bounteous +‎ -ness.  Bounteous itself comes from Old French *bonteus*, from bonté (“goodness”) (from Latin bonitas).
