marc:/mɑːrk/ | n. |酒渣，残渣，渣滓；（尤指葡萄、橄榄压榨后的）果渣，皮渣 | The marc is used as fertilizer.  酒渣被用作肥料。 |例句：The marc was used to make grappa.  果渣被用来制作格拉巴酒。

例句：After pressing the grapes, the marc is discarded. 葡萄压榨后，果渣被丢弃。|近义词：residue, remains, lees, pomace |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *marc, from Frankish *marka (“dregs, residue”), from Proto-Germanic *markō (“border, boundary, mark”). Cognate with Old English mearc (“boundary, border”), Old Norse mörk (“forest, woodland, border”), Middle Dutch marc (“border”), Old High German marcha (“border, boundary”), German Mark (“border, march”), Gothic marka (“border, limit”). The semantic development likely proceeded from "border" to "that which is left at the border or edge" to "residue, dregs."
