meantime:/ˈmiːntaɪm/| adv. n.|期间，与此同时| meanwhile; in the intervening time| I'll call you later. In the meantime, try to relax. 我稍后给你打电话。在此期间，试着放松一下。 |She continued to work as a waitress in the meantime. 她同时继续做服务员的工作。|近义词：meanwhile, interim|反义词: never|词性: 副词/名词

词源: From Middle English *menetyme, equivalent to mean (“middle, intervening”) +‎ time.
