disabilities:/ˌdɪsəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|缺陷，残疾，无能力|The Americans with Disabilities Act prohibits discrimination based on disability.  《美国残疾人法案》禁止基于残疾的歧视。|例句：The school is equipped to handle students with various disabilities.  这所学校有能力照顾各种残疾的学生。

例句：Her disabilities prevent her from driving. 她的残疾使她无法开车。|近义词：impairments, handicaps, limitations|反义词：abilities, capabilities|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: plural of disability, from Old French disabilite, from des- (expressing reversal) + habilite (see ability).
