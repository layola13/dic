clingier:/ˈklɪŋ.i.ər/ /ˈklɪŋ.ɡi.ər/| adj.|更粘人的，更依赖的|Comparative form of clingy.  Meaning more inclined to stay close to someone physically or emotionally, more dependent. |例句：He's become much clingier since the accident.  他自从事故之后变得更加粘人了。

例句：The child was clingier than usual today.  这孩子今天比平时更粘人了。|近义词：needier, more dependent, more attached|反义词：more independent, more detached, less needy|词性:形容词

clingy:/ˈklɪŋ.i/ /ˈklɪŋ.ɡi/| adj.|粘人的，依赖的|Inclined to stay close to someone physically or emotionally, dependent. |例句：My dog is very clingy and follows me everywhere. 我的狗很粘人，到处跟着我。

例句：She became clingy after her parents divorced. 她父母离婚后，她变得很依赖别人。|近义词：needy, dependent, attached|反义词：independent, detached|词性:形容词
