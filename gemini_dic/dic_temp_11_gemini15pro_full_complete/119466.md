micrometer: /maɪˈkrɒmɪtər/ (UK) /maɪˈkrɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n. |千分尺，测微计|An instrument for measuring small distances, objects, or angles with precision, often used in engineering and manufacturing. |The engineer used a micrometer to measure the thickness of the metal sheet. 工程师用千分尺测量金属板的厚度。|Synonyms: caliper, gauge |Antonyms: N/A | noun

micrometer: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌmiːtər/ (US)| n. |微米|A unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter, often used in scientific contexts. |The diameter of the human hair is about 75 micrometers.  人类头发的直径约为75微米。|Synonyms: micron |Antonyms: N/A | noun
