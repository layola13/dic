fatlike:/ˈfætˌlaɪk/| adj.|类似脂肪的；脂肪状的|Resembling fat in appearance, texture, or properties.|The substance had a fatlike consistency.  这种物质有一种类似脂肪的稠度。|Example: The lab created a fatlike substance for their experiment.  实验室为他们的实验创造了一种类似脂肪的物质。|Synonyms:  lipoid, fatty, greasy, oily|Antonyms: lean, thin, watery|adjective
