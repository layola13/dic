fallings-out:/ˈfɔːlɪŋz aʊt/
|n.|争吵，不和|Disagreements or quarrels between people, often leading to a temporary or permanent break in their relationship.  It's usually used in the plural form.|
例句：There have been several fallings-out between the two families over the years.  多年来，这两个家庭之间发生过几次争吵。

例句：Their recent falling-out stemmed from a misunderstanding about a business deal.  他们最近的不和源于对一笔商业交易的误解。
|近义词：disagreements, quarrels, arguments, disputes, rifts|
反义词：reconciliations, agreements, harmonies|词性:名词
