fabella:/fəˈbɛlə/| n.|籽骨，外侧籽骨|A small sesamoid bone located in the lateral head of the gastrocnemius muscle behind the lateral femoral condyle. It is often asymptomatic but can cause pain and limited range of motion in the knee.|The patient's knee pain was attributed to an inflamed fabella.|Synonym:  sesamoid bone (general term)|Antonym: N/A (no direct antonym for a specific bone)|noun
