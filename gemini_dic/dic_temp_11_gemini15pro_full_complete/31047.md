chambray: /ˈʃæmbreɪ/ (US), /ˈʃɒmbreɪ/ (UK)| n. | 钱布雷布，一种轻薄的纯棉或亚麻织物，类似于牛仔布，但比牛仔布更轻更细。|A lightweight plain-weave fabric of cotton or linen resembling denim but finer and lighter.|  She wore a chambray shirt with white jeans. 她穿着一件钱布雷衬衫和白色牛仔裤。

The chambray dress was perfect for the summer weather. 这条钱布雷连衣裙非常适合夏天穿。
