confute:/kənˈfjuːt/ | v. |驳倒；反驳；证明…错误 |To prove a person or an argument wrong. |例句：The lawyer skillfully confuted the witness's testimony. 律师巧妙地驳倒了证人的证词。

例句：His theory was confuted by the latest experimental results. 他的理论被最新的实验结果驳倒了。|近义词：refute, disprove, debunk, invalidate |反义词：prove, confirm, validate, corroborate |词性:动词
