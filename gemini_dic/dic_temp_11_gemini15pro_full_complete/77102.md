garrulous: /ˈɡærələs/ (US), /ˈɡærjələs/ (UK)| adj. |喋喋不休的，絮叨的| Excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters.| She's a garrulous old woman who loves to gossip. 她是一位爱说闲话的絮叨老妇人。|  He became quite garrulous after a few drinks.  几杯酒下肚后，他变得十分健谈。| 近义词：loquacious, talkative, voluble| 反义词：reticent, taciturn, laconic |词性: 形容词
