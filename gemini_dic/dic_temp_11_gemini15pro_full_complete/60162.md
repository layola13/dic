effeminating: /ɪˈfɛmɪneɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 使女性化的；使柔弱的 | Making someone or something appear or behave more feminine; weakening someone's strength or spirit. |  His effeminating mannerisms made him the target of bullies.  他女性化的举止使他成为欺凌的目标。| Synonyms: weakening, unmanning, emasculating | Antonyms: strengthening, masculinizing, invigorating | adjective

effeminate: /ɪˈfɛmɪnət/
| adj. |  (of a man) having or showing characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly. | (指男人) 具有或表现出被认为是女性特征的；没有男子气概的。| He had an effeminate way of walking. 他走路的样子很女性化。| Synonyms: womanly, unmanly, sissy | Antonyms: manly, masculine, virile | adjective

词源: From Latin *effeminatus*, past participle of effeminare (“to make womanish, weaken”), from ex (“out of, from”) + femina (“woman”).
