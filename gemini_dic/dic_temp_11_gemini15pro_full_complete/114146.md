mahatma:/məˈhɑːtmə/ | n. | 圣雄，（尤指印度的）伟人，尊者 | A person regarded with reverence or loving respect, especially a holy person or sage in India. |  Example 1: Mahatma Gandhi led India to independence. 圣雄甘地带领印度走向独立。

Example 2:  He was considered a mahatma by his followers.  他的追随者将他视为圣雄。 | Synonym: sage, saint, guru, apostle | Antonym: villain, scoundrel | noun
