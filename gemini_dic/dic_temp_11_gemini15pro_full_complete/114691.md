malingerer:/məˈlɪŋɡərər/| n. |装病者，逃避责任者|A person who pretends to be ill or incapacitated in order to avoid work or duty.|He was accused of being a malingerer after calling in sick for the fifth time that month. 他那个月第五次打电话请病假后，被指责为装病逃避工作。|近义词：shirker, slacker, dodger|反义词：hard worker, diligent worker|noun

malinger:/məˈlɪŋɡər/| v. |装病，逃避责任|To pretend to be ill or incapacitated in order to avoid work or duty.|He was suspected of malingering to get out of military service.  他被怀疑装病以逃避兵役。|近义词：shirk, feign illness|反义词：work diligently|verb
