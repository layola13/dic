honeys:/ˈhʌniz/| n.|蜂蜜（复数）；甜心，宝贝（复数）|A sweet, viscous fluid produced by bees from the nectar of flowers, often used as a food.  Also a term of endearment.|The honeys from different flowers have distinct flavors. 不同花的蜂蜜有不同的味道。|She called all her children "honeys". 她称呼她所有的孩子为“宝贝”。|近义词：(as food) nectar, syrup; (as endearment) darling, sweetheart, sweetie|反义词:(as food)  (no direct opposite, perhaps "vinegar" in a metaphorical sense)|词性:noun


honeys:/ˈhʌniz/| n.|亲爱的们，宝贝们（称呼）|Used as a term of endearment, often in the plural, addressing a group.|Okay, honeys, let's get started. 好吧，亲爱的们，我们开始吧。|Listen up, honeys, I have an announcement.  听着，宝贝们，我有个通知。|近义词:darlings, sweethearts, sweeties, dears|反义词:(no direct opposite)|词性:noun
