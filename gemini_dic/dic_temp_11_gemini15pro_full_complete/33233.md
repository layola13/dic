chronographies: /krəˈnɒɡrəfiz/ | n. | 编年史; 年代记 |  A descriptive record or listing of events in chronological order. It refers to the practice or method of compiling chronicles or historical accounts arranged according to the order in which events occurred. |  His chronographies of the war meticulously detailed every battle. 他对这场战争的编年史细致地描述了每一场战斗。 |  chronicles, annals, histories |  None | noun
