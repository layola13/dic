lay-off:/ˈleɪˌɔːf/ (US), /ˈleɪˌɒf/ (UK)| n.|裁员；解雇；临时解雇；停工期|A lay-off is a temporary or permanent discharge of a worker or workers.|The company announced a lay-off of 500 employees due to declining sales.  公司因销售额下降宣布裁员500人。|Synonyms: redundancy, dismissal, discharge|Antonyms: hiring, recruitment|noun

lay-off:/ˈleɪˌɔːf/ (US), /ˈleɪˌɒf/ (UK)| n.|停工期；失业期|A lay-off is a period of time when someone is not working, usually because their employer has told them to stop.|He used his lay-off to travel around Europe. 他利用失业期去欧洲旅行。|Synonyms: unemployment, downtime, break|Antonyms: employment, work|noun
