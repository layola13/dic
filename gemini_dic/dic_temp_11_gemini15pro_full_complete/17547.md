beatifies:/biˈætɪfaɪz/
v.
使享福; 使幸福; 祝愿…幸福;册封…为真福（天主教）
to make blissfully happy. In Roman Catholicism, to declare (a deceased person) to be among the blessed and thus entitled to limited public veneration.
The Pope beatified Mother Teresa in 2003.  教宗于2003年册封德蕾莎修女为真福。
Her smile beatified her face. 她的笑容使她显得格外幸福。
近义词:exalt, glorify, bless, sanctify
反义词:condemn, curse
词性:动词
