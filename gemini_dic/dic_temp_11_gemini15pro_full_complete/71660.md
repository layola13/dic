flattest:/ˈflætɪst/ | adj. | 最平的，最平坦的 |  superlative form of flat; having an even surface, without slopes or bumps. | This is the flattest pancake I've ever made. 这是我做过的最平的煎饼。| The Bonneville Salt Flats are known for their flattest terrain. 博纳维尔盐滩以其最平坦的地形而闻名。| 近义词: levelest, smoothest | 反义词: steepest, hilliest | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Old Norse *flatr, from Proto-Germanic *flataz. Related to Old English flett (“floor, hall, dwelling”), Old High German flaz (“floor”).
