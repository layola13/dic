nagging:/ˈnæɡɪŋ/| adj.|唠叨的，烦人的，挥之不去的|persistently annoying or troubling; bothering or urging someone repeatedly.|The nagging pain in her shoulder wouldn't go away. 她肩膀上挥之不去的疼痛一直不好。|Synonyms: persistent, bothersome, irritating, harassing|Antonyms: soothing, calming, comforting|adjective

nagging:/ˈnæɡɪŋ/| n.|唠叨，不断抱怨|the act of nagging someone; persistent complaints.|Her constant nagging about my diet is driving me crazy. 她对我饮食的不断唠叨快把我逼疯了。|Synonyms: complaining, carping, badgering|Antonyms: praising, complimenting|noun
