counseling: /ˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkaʊnslɪŋ/ (UK)
n.
咨询，辅导
The process of giving advice and guidance to someone to help them solve problems, especially personal ones.
She received counseling after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她接受了心理咨询。
advice, guidance, therapy, support
direction, instruction, teaching
noun

counsel: /ˈkaʊnsəl/ (US), /ˈkaʊnsl/ (UK)
n.
建议，忠告
Advice and guidance given to someone.
He sought counsel from his lawyer. 他向他的律师寻求建议。
advice, guidance, recommendation, suggestion
vt.
劝告，忠告
To give advice and guidance to someone.
The doctor counseled her to stop smoking. 医生劝她戒烟。
advise, guide, recommend, suggest
verb, noun
