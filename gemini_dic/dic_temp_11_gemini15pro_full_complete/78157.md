geopolitist:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈpɑːlɪtɪst/
n.|地缘政治学家；地缘政治论者|A specialist in geopolitics; one who interprets world affairs in terms of geographic factors.|Example: The geopolitist predicted the conflict based on the region's resources.  这位地缘政治学家根据该地区的资源预测了这场冲突。|Example:  Geopolitists often analyze the interplay of geography, economics, and power. 地缘政治学家经常分析地理、经济和权力之间的相互作用。|Synonym: strategist, analyst, expert|Antonym:  N/A (Lack of a direct antonym)|noun
