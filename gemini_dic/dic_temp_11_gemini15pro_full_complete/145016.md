overadvances:/ˌoʊvərədˈvænsɪz/| n. |透支；垫款；过度预支|An amount of money advanced beyond the agreed limit or in excess of collateral.|The company's overadvances caused a strain on their cash flow. 公司的过度预支给他们的现金流造成了压力。|synonyms: overdraft, excess advance|antonyms: repayment, deposit|noun


overadvance:/ˌoʊvərədˈvæns/| vt. |过度预支；垫付过多|To advance more money than is warranted or secured.|The bank overadvanced the client, leading to financial difficulties. 银行给客户过度垫款，导致了财务困难。|synonyms: overlend, overdraw|antonyms: underlend, withhold|verb
