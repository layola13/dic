freshens:/ˈfreʃənz/| v. |使…清新；使…凉爽；使…精神焕发；更新；变新鲜|Makes something fresher, cooler, or more lively; renews; becomes fresh.|The cool breeze freshens the air. 凉爽的微风使空气清新。|近义词：revives, refreshes, invigorates, renews|反义词：stagnates, wilts, fades, deteriorates|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *fresshen, from Old English *ferscian, from fersc (“fresh”).
