larkingly:/ˈlɑːrkɪŋli/
adv.
嬉戏地，开玩笑地
In a playful and mischievous manner; jokingly.
He larkingly suggested they should elope. 他开玩笑地建议他们私奔。
近义词: playfully, jokingly, mischievously, teasingly
反义词: seriously, solemnly
词性: 副词
