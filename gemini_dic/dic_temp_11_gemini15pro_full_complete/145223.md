overbeaten:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːtn/ | adj. | 打得过度的，过度搅拌的，疲惫不堪的 |  Excessively beaten, whether physically or figuratively, also overmixed (as in cooking). | The batter was overbeaten and the cake came out tough. 面糊被过度搅拌，导致蛋糕烤出来很硬。 | Synonyms: overworked, exhausted, overwhipped, overmixed | Antonyms: underbeaten, fresh, energetic | adjective

overbeaten:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːtn/ | adj. | 陈腐的，缺乏新意的 | Figuratively, lacking freshness or originality. | The plot of the movie was overbeaten and predictable.  这部电影的情节老套且可预测。| Synonyms: stale, clichéd, hackneyed, trite | Antonyms: original, fresh, innovative | adjective
