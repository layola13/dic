deity:/ˈdiːɪti/ (US), /ˈdeɪɪti/ (UK)| n.|神，神性，女神|A god or goddess; a supernatural being considered divine.|The ancient Greeks worshipped a pantheon of deities. 古希腊人崇拜众神。|synonyms: god, goddess, divinity, immortal, supreme being|antonyms: mortal, human|noun

deity:/ˈdiːɪti/ (US), /ˈdeɪɪti/ (UK)| n.|神性，神格|The state or quality of being divine.|The deity of the emperor was proclaimed throughout the land. 皇帝的神性被宣布到全国各地。|synonyms: divinity, godhead, holiness, sacredness|antonyms: mortality, humanness|noun
