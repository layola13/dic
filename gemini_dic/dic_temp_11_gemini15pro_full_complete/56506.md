domesticative:/dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪtɪv/| adj.|喜爱家庭的，专注家务的|Of or relating to the running of a home or to family relations.  Enjoying home or family life.|例句：She leads a quiet, domesticative life. 她过着安静的家庭生活。

例句：He's not very domesticative; he prefers to eat out. 他不怎么喜欢做家务；他更喜欢外出就餐。|近义词：home-loving, housewifely, homebody, family-oriented|反义词：gregarious, outgoing, social|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin domesticativus, from Latin domesticus (“of the house, native”), from domus (“house, home”).
