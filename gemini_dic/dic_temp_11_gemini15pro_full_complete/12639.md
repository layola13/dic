assay:/əˈseɪ/ /ˈæsˌeɪ/| n. vt.|化验，分析；试验，考验；尝试；(贵金属的)成色鉴定|An assay is a test, especially a scientific or medical one, that is done to find out how much of a particular substance is in something.  It can also refer to an attempt to do something difficult.|
例句：The blood samples were sent to the lab for assay. 血液样本被送往实验室进行化验。
例句：His first assay at writing a novel was a dismal failure. 他第一次尝试写小说就惨败了。
|近义词：test, analysis, examination, trial, attempt|反义词: |-
词性: 名词, 动词


assay:/əˈseɪ/ /ˈæsˌeɪ/| vt.|化验，分析；试验；考验；尝试|To assay something means to test or analyze it, especially to determine its components or quality.  It can also mean to try or attempt something.|
例句：The chemist assayed the ore to determine its gold content. 化学家化验矿石以确定其黄金含量。
例句：He assayed to climb the mountain, but failed due to bad weather. 他试图攀登这座山，但由于天气恶劣而失败了。
|近义词：test, analyze, examine, try, attempt|反义词: |-
词性: 动词
