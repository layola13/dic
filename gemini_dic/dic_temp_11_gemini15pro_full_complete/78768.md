gilet:/ˈdʒɪleɪ/ (US), /ˈʒiːleɪ/ (UK)| n. |马甲，背心|A sleeveless jacket, often padded or quilted, worn over other clothes.|He wore a fleece gilet under his coat for extra warmth. 他在大衣里穿了一件羊毛背心以保暖。|Synonyms: vest, waistcoat, body warmer|Antonyms: coat, jacket|noun

gilet:/ˈdʒɪleɪ/ (US), /ˈʒiːleɪ/ (UK)| n. |胸甲（用于击剑或其他运动）|A protective padded garment worn on the chest, especially in fencing or other sports.|The fencer's gilet protected him from his opponent's blade.  击剑运动员的胸甲保护他免受对手的剑刃伤害。|Synonyms: chest protector, plastron|Antonyms: N/A|noun
