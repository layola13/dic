highroller:/ˈhaɪˌroʊlər/| n. |豪赌客；挥金如土的人|A person who gambles or spends large sums of money.|例句：The casino caters to highrollers, offering them complimentary suites and private gaming tables.  这家赌场迎合豪赌客的需求，为他们提供免费套房和私人赌桌。

例句：Highrollers often receive special treatment from businesses eager for their patronage.  挥金如土的人经常会受到渴望得到他们惠顾的企业的特殊待遇。|近义词：big spender, whale, VIP|反义词:cheapskate, penny-pincher|词性:noun


词源:  "High" (meaning large amount) + "roller" (originally referring to someone who rolls dice). The term gained popularity in the mid-20th century.
