confronter:/kənˈfrʌntər/| n.|对抗者，面对者，质问者|A person who confronts someone or something, especially someone who challenges or accuses someone else.|She was a confronter, never afraid to speak her mind. 她是一个敢于直言的人，从不害怕表达自己的想法。|例句：The confronter accused the politician of corruption. 质问者指控该政客腐败。

例句：He acted as a confronter, challenging the status quo. 他扮演了一个对抗者的角色，挑战现状。|近义词：challenger, opponent, accuser, antagonist|反义词：supporter, defender, ally|词性: noun
