indiretin: /ɪndɪˈrɛtɪn/ | n. |  间接视黄素; 间接视黄醛 | A carotenoid pigment found in green algae, acting as a light-harvesting pigment for photosynthesis.  It's converted to retinoids in animals. |  Indiretin is a precursor to vitamin A in some algae. 间接视黄素是某些藻类中维生素A的前体。|  Researchers discovered indiretin plays a crucial role in the algae's ability to thrive in low-light conditions. 研究人员发现间接视黄素在藻类在弱光条件下茁壮成长的能力中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: carotenoid, pigment | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
