carried-forward:/ˈkærid ˈfɔːrwərd/ | adj.|结转的，转入下期的| An accounting term used to describe an amount or balance that is transferred from one period to the next.  | The carried-forward losses reduced the company's tax burden this year.  结转的亏损减少了公司今年的税负。| synonyms: brought forward, transferred, deferred | antonyms:  expired, closed, settled | adjective

carried-forward:/ˈkærid ˈfɔːrwərd/ | n.|结转金额，转入下期余额| The amount that is carried forward from one period to the next. | The carried-forward from last quarter helped us start the year strong. 上个季度的结转金额帮助我们强势开年。| synonyms: balance brought forward, transferred balance | antonyms: current balance, closing balance | noun
