overindulge:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ/| v. |过分放纵；沉溺；纵容|To allow oneself to have or do too much of something enjoyable, especially food or drink.|He overindulged in rich food and wine during the holidays. 他在假期里过度沉溺于丰富的食物和葡萄酒。|She overindulged her children, giving them everything they wanted. 她溺爱她的孩子们，给了他们想要的一切。|近义词：pamper, spoil, coddle|反义词：abstain, moderate|词性：动词

overindulgence:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒəns/| n. |过分放纵；沉溺|The action or fact of having or doing too much of something enjoyable.|His overindulgence in alcohol led to health problems. 他过度饮酒导致了健康问题。|Overindulgence in sweets can cause weight gain.  过度沉溺于甜食会导致体重增加。|近义词：excess, intemperance|反义词：moderation, abstinence|词性：名词
