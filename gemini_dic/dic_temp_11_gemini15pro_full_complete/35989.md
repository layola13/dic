codesigning:/ˈkoʊdˌsaɪnɪŋ/| n.|代码签名|The process of digitally signing software to confirm its origin and guarantee that it hasn't been tampered with.|Example: Codesigning is essential for distributing iOS apps. 代码签名对于分发iOS应用程序至关重要。|Example:  Without proper codesigning, your application might be blocked by the operating system. 没有正确的代码签名，你的应用程序可能会被操作系统阻止。|Synonym: digital signing, code signature|Antonym: N/A|Noun
