infusionism:/ɪnˈfjuːʒənɪzəm/| n.|文化渗透论，文化传播论|The theory that cultural traits can spread from one society to another, leading to similarities in different cultures.  This contrasts with theories that emphasize independent invention.|The museum's exhibit challenged the simplistic notion of infusionism, showing how similar technologies developed independently in various parts of the world.|近义词：diffusionism, cultural transmission|反义词:independent invention|noun
