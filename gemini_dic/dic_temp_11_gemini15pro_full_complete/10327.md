appeals:/əˈpiːlz/ | n. | 吸引力；感染力；呼吁；上诉 |  The quality of being attractive or interesting; an earnest request for something; an application to a higher court for a reversal of the decision of a lower court. | The movie appeals to a broad audience. 这部电影吸引了广大观众。| synonyms: attractions, charms, requests, petitions | antonyms: repulsions, deterrents, denials | noun

appeals:/əˈpiːlz/ | v. | 呼吁；恳求；上诉；有吸引力 | Make a serious or urgent request, typically to the public; be attractive or interesting. | He appealed to the court to overturn his conviction. 他向法院上诉，要求推翻他的定罪。| synonyms:  pleads, begs, entreats, attracts | antonyms:  repels, dissuades, rejects | verb
