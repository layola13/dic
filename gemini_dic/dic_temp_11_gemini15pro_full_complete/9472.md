antiresonance:/ˌæntɪˈrɛzənəns/ | n. | 反共振，反谐振 |The condition of a system in which an applied oscillatory force produces a response of unusually small amplitude.  A frequency at which the impedance of a system is very high, approaching infinity.|  The bridge exhibited antiresonance at a specific frequency. 这座桥梁在一个特定频率下表现出反共振。 | synonyms: anti-resonance,  | antonyms: resonance | noun

antiresonant:/ˌæntɪˈrɛzənənt/ | adj. | 反共振的 | Relating to or exhibiting antiresonance.|  The antiresonant frequency of the circuit was carefully measured.  电路的反共振频率被仔细测量。| synonyms: anti-resonant | antonyms: resonant | adjective
