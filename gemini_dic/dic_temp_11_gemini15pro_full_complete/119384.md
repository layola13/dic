microdensitometric:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌdɛnsɪtəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
显微光密度测定的
Relating to or using microdensitometry, a technique for measuring the optical density of very small areas.
Microdensitometric analysis of the X-ray revealed subtle variations in bone density.  显微光密度分析显示了骨密度中的细微变化。

Microdensitometric scans were used to quantify the protein bands on the gel. 用显微光密度扫描来量化凝胶上的蛋白质条带。

近义词: densitometric
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider "qualitative" as opposed to the quantitative nature of microdensitometry)
词性: adjective
