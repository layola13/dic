helmet:/ˈhɛlmɪt/ | n. | 头盔；安全帽 | A hard or padded protective hat, typically worn by motorcyclists, construction workers, or soldiers. |  Always wear a helmet when riding a motorcycle.  骑摩托车时一定要戴头盔。 | synonyms: hard hat, safety helmet, headgear | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "bareheaded") | noun
