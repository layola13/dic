caple:/ˈkeɪpəl/| n.|马匹；劣马； (苏格兰) 工作马|A horse, especially a workhorse or one of poor quality. |例句：The old caple plodded along the dusty road. 那匹老马沿着尘土飞扬的道路缓缓走着。

例句：He couldn't afford a thoroughbred, so he bought a sturdy caple instead. 他买不起纯种马，所以买了一匹强壮的劣马。|近义词：horse, nag, jade|反义词: thoroughbred (纯种马) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *caple, from Old North French caple, from Late Latin caballus (horse), from Gaulish *caballos.  Related to Spanish caballo and Italian cavallo.
