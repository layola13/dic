batsmanship: /ˈbætsmənʃɪp/ | n. | 击球技巧；击球水平 |  The skill or art of batting, especially in cricket.  A player's ability to score runs and avoid being dismissed. | His batsmanship was magnificent in the final match. 他的击球技巧在决赛中非常出色。|  skill, technique, artistry, proficiency | ineptitude, clumsiness | noun

batsmanship: /ˈbætsmənʃɪp/ | n. | 击球水平 | The quality of a batsman's play, judged by how many runs they score and how long they stay at the wicket. | The team's batsmanship let them down in the second innings. 该队的击球水平在第二局让他们失望了。|  performance, form, ability, expertise | incompetence, weakness | noun
