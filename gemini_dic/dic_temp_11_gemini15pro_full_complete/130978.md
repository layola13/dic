nonastronomical:/ˌnɒnˌæstrəˈnɒmɪkəl/
adjective
非天文数字的；正常的；合理的
Not related to astronomy; (of a price or amount) not extremely large.
The price was nonastronomical, so we decided to buy it.  价格并非天文数字，所以我们决定买下它。
The cost of the repairs was nonastronomical, thankfully.  谢天谢地，维修费用并非天文数字。
Synonym: reasonable, moderate, affordable, modest
Antonym: astronomical, exorbitant, excessive
词性: adjective
