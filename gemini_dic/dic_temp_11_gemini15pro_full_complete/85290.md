harmonizer:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzər/ | n. | 和声器，协调者，调和者 | A device or person that produces or creates harmony.  A person who tries to resolve conflict. | The barbershop quartet sang beautifully, their voices blending together thanks to the lead singer's skill as a harmonizer. 理发店四重唱唱得很优美，由于主唱的和声技巧，他们的声音融合在一起。 The mediator acted as a harmonizer between the two feuding families. 调解员在两个不和的家庭之间充当了调和者的角色。 | 近义词: coordinator, mediator, conciliator, unifier | 反义词: agitator, instigator, divider | 词性: noun

harmonize:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/ | v. |  使和谐，使协调，调和 | To bring into or be in accord; make harmonious or consistent. To sing or play in harmony. | The interior designer harmonized the colors of the room. 室内设计师协调了房间的颜色。  The singers harmonized beautifully during the chorus. 歌手们在合唱部分配合得非常和谐。 | 近义词: coordinate, blend, reconcile, integrate | 反义词: clash, conflict, disagree, discord | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *harmonisen*, from Old French *harmoniser*, from Late Latin *harmonizāre*, from Ancient Greek *ἁρμονίζειν (harmonízein)*, from *ἁρμονία (harmonía, “harmony”)*.
