defaulting:/dɪˈfɔːltɪŋ/ | n. | 违约，拖欠 | The act of failing to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in court. |  The company is defaulting on its loan payments.  这家公司拖欠贷款。| synonyms: failing, neglecting, omitting, breaching | antonyms: fulfilling, honoring, upholding | noun

defaulting:/dɪˈfɔːltɪŋ/ | adj. | 违约的，拖欠的 | Failing to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in court. | The defaulting borrower was contacted by the bank.  违约的借款人被银行联系了。| synonyms: delinquent, overdue, non-compliant | antonyms: compliant, current, paid-up | adjective
