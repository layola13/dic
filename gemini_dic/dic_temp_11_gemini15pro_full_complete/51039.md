desmognathous: /dɛzˈmɒɡnəθəs/
adjective
颌骨联合的，固颌型的
Having the maxillae immovably united with the skull and the pterygoids articulating with the quadrate, as in most amphibians, reptiles, and birds.
例句：The desmognathous skull structure is a key characteristic of many reptiles. 固颌型颅骨结构是许多爬行动物的一个关键特征。

例句：Desmognathous articulation allows for limited jaw movement. 固颌型连接方式使得颌骨运动受限。

近义词：akinetic (referring to the skull)
反义词：streptostylic (having a movable quadrate bone)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *desmos* (bond, ligament) + *gnathos* (jaw)
