calumniate:/kəˈlʌmnieɪt/| v. |诽谤，中伤|To make false and malicious statements about someone in order to damage their reputation.|He was calumniated by his political rivals. 他被他的政敌诽谤了。|Synonyms: slander, libel, defame, vilify|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud|verb

calumnize:/kəˈlʌmnaɪz/| v. |诽谤，中伤|To make false and malicious statements about someone in order to damage their reputation.|The newspaper article calumnized the actor, falsely accusing him of theft. 这篇报纸文章诽谤了这位演员，诬告他偷窃。|Synonyms: slander, libel, defame, vilify|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin calumniārī, from Latin calumnia (“false accusation, trickery”), from calvi (“to deceive”).
