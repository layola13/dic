clustering:/ˈklʌstərɪŋ/| n.|聚类，集群|The process of grouping similar objects or data points together.  The clustering of galaxies. 星系的聚集|例句：The clustering of these symptoms suggests a specific diagnosis. 这些症状的聚集表明了一种特定的诊断。

例句：Data clustering is a common technique in machine learning. 数据聚类是机器学习中的一种常见技术。|近义词：grouping, aggregation, assembling|反义词:separation, dispersal, isolation|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cluster + -ing.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klustrōną (“to clot, coagulate”).
