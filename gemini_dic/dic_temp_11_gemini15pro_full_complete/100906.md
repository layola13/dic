It refers to the multiple instances or aspects of something being easily understood or operated without conscious reasoning.|The design of the software prioritizes intuitivenesses for a seamless user experience.  该软件的设计优先考虑直觉性，以提供无缝的用户体验。|Near Synonyms: insights, instincts, comprehensibilities|Antonyms: complexities, difficulties, obscurities|noun
