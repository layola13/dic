iqs:/aɪ kjuː ɛs/ | n. | intelligence quotient 智商 | A number representing a person's reasoning ability (measured using problem-solving tests) as compared to the statistical norm or average for their age, taken as 100. |  Her IQ is very high. 她的智商很高。| synonyms: intelligence, mental ability | antonyms: stupidity, unintelligence | noun
