hyperobtrusive:/ˌhaɪpərəbˈtruːsɪv/| adj.|极其突兀的，过分惹眼的|Excessively obtrusive; unduly noticeable or prominent, often in a way that is irritating or unwelcome.  It's formed by adding the prefix "hyper-" (meaning "over, excessive") to "obtrusive."|The hyperobtrusive advertising billboards along the highway were a distraction to drivers. 公路旁极其突兀的广告牌分散了司机的注意力。

The hyperobtrusive neon sign clashed with the historic architecture of the street.  过于惹眼的霓虹灯招牌与街道的历史建筑格格不入。|近义词：garish, blatant, conspicuous, intrusive, prominent|反义词：subtle, understated, discreet, inconspicuous, unnoticeable|词性: adjective
