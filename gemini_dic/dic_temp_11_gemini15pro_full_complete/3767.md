agitating:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人不安的，焦虑的
Causing emotional disturbance; exciting.
The agitating news of the impending war caused widespread panic.  令人不安的战争即将爆发的消息引起了广泛的恐慌。

近义词:disturbing, unsettling, upsetting, worrying, distressing, nerve-racking
反义词:calming, soothing, relaxing, peaceful, tranquil
词性:adjective


agitating:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪŋ/
verb
煽动，鼓动
To stir up public opinion for or against a cause.
He was agitating for better working conditions. 他在鼓动改善工作条件。

近义词:stirring, rousing, provoking, inciting, fomenting
反义词:calming, soothing, appeasing, pacifying
词性:verb (present participle of agitate)


词源:
From Latin *agitatus*, past participle of *agitare* "to drive, urge on," frequentative of *agere* "to do, set in motion, drive, lead."
