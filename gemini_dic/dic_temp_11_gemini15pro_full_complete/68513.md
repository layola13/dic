fanaticize:/fəˈnætɪsaɪz/
verb
使狂热；使入迷
to make someone become a fanatic
The propaganda is clearly designed to fanaticize the population. 这宣传显然是为了煽动民众。

近义词:obsess, infatuate, indoctrinate
反义词:moderate, temper, calm
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From fanatic + -ize.  Fanatic itself comes from Latin fanaticus "inspired by a deity, frenzied, enthusiastic," from fanum "temple."
