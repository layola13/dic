lo-res:/ˌləʊˈrɛz/ | adj. | 低分辨率的 |  Short for "low resolution," referring to images or displays with a relatively small number of pixels, resulting in less detail and clarity. | The image was lo-res and pixelated. 这张图片分辨率低，而且有像素块。 | Synonyms: low-resolution, pixelated | Antonyms: hi-res, high-resolution | adjective

lo-res:/ˌləʊˈrɛz/ | n. | 低分辨率图像或显示 | An image or display with low resolution. | The lo-res made it hard to identify the suspect. 低分辨率使得难以识别嫌疑人。 | Synonyms: low-resolution image/display | Antonyms: hi-res image/display, high-resolution image/display | noun
