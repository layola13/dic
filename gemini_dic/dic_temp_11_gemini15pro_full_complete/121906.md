missee:/mɪˈsiː/ | n. | 小姐，女士 (尤指未婚女性，现已过时) |  A young unmarried woman; (archaic) a girl or woman. |  In old novels, you often see the term "missee" used to refer to unmarried women. 在旧小说中，你经常看到用“missee”来指代未婚女性。 | 近义词: miss, young lady | 反义词: madam, Mrs. | 词性: 名词

词源:
From the French "mademoiselle" (my young lady), shortened to "mam'selle," which in turn was anglicized to "missee." 源自法语“mademoiselle”（我的小姐），缩写为“mam'selle”，然后被英语化为“missee”。
