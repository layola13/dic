bugger:/ˈbʌɡər/ |n. v.|讨厌鬼，混蛋; 弄坏，搞砸|
1. (n.) A mildly offensive term used to express annoyance or frustration.
2. (v.) To damage or ruin something.
|1. (n.)  That bugger stole my parking space!  那个混蛋偷了我的停车位！
2. (v.) I've completely buggered up this project. 我完全搞砸了这个项目。|近义词：(n.) rascal, scoundrel, blighter; (v.) ruin, spoil, damage|反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:名词，动词

词源:
From Middle English *bugren* ("commit sodomy"), from Old French *bougre* ("heretic of Bulgaria, sodomite").  The meaning shifted over time to become a general term of abuse.
