forwardness:/ˈfɔːrwərdnəs/ | n. | 大胆;鲁莽;冒昧;唐突 | The quality or state of being forward; boldness; confidence.  | Her forwardness in expressing her opinions sometimes offends people. 她直言不讳的表达方式有时会冒犯别人。 |  例句：His forwardness surprised her. 他的唐突让她感到惊讶。

例句：She showed unusual forwardness for her age. 以她的年纪来说，她表现得异常大胆。

近义词：boldness, audacity, presumption, assertiveness, impudence | 反义词：shyness, timidity, reserve, reticence | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From forward + -ness.  Forward (adj.) comes from Old English *foreweard, from fore "before" + -weard "toward." The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
