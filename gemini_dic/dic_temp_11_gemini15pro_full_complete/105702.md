kinkless:/ˈkɪŋklɪs/
adjective
无扭结的，平滑的
Smooth and without kinks or bends.
The ribbon was kinkless and flowed gracefully.  这缎带很平滑，优雅地飘动。
The hose was kinkless, allowing for easy water flow.  这水管没有扭结，方便水流。
smooth, straight, unbent, even
kinky, twisted, knotted, bent
