masthead:/ˈmæstˌhed/ | n. | 旗杆顶; 报头; (公司)领导层 |  The information about the newspaper's publisher and editors is printed in the masthead. 报纸出版商和编辑的信息印在报头上。| The company masthead includes the CEO and other top executives. 公司的领导层包括首席执行官和其他高管。 | synonyms: banner, heading, title, leadership, top management | antonyms: staff, employees, subordinates | noun

masthead:/ˈmæstˌhed/ | n. | (尤指帆船的)桅顶 | The masthead snapped off in the storm. 桅顶在暴风雨中折断了。| The sailor climbed to the masthead to get a better view. 水手爬到桅顶以便更好地观察。| synonyms: topmast, truck | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly base, bottom) | noun
