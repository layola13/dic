nor:/nɔːr/ /nɔr/
conjunction
也不；也没有
Used before a word or phrase that states a second negative idea in a sentence, after a first negative idea has been introduced by “neither” or “not”.
Neither he nor I went to the party. 他和我都没去参加派对。
She doesn't like fish, nor does she like chicken. 她不喜欢鱼，也不喜欢鸡肉。
近义词: and not, neither
反义词: or, and
