blasphemer:/ˈblæsfəmə(r)/| n. |亵渎神明者，渎神者|Someone who commits blasphemy; a person who speaks irreverently about God or sacred things.|The blasphemer was banished from the kingdom.|近义词：heretic, iconoclast, infidel|反义词：believer, devotee, worshipper|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *blasfemour, from Old French blasfemeor, blasfémateur, from blasfemer (“to blaspheme”) + -eur, -or.  Ultimately from Late Latin blasphēmātor, from Ancient Greek βλασφημητής (blasphēmētḗs).
