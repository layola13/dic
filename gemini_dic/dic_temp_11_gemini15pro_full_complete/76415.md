gaggle:/ˈɡæɡl/| n. |一群（鹅）；咯咯声|A flock of geese, especially when on the water.  A noisy or disorderly group of people.|例句：A gaggle of geese waddled across the road. 一群鹅摇摇摆摆地走过马路。

例句：A gaggle of tourists crowded around the famous painting. 一群游客围在那幅名画周围。|近义词：flock, group, crowd, throng|反义词: (没有确切的反义词，可以考虑个体 individual)|词性:名词
