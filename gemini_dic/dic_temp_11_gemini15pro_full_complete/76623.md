galloping:/ˈɡæləpɪŋ/| adj.| 飞驰的，疾驰的；(价格等)飞涨的| moving or progressing very quickly.| The galloping inflation is eroding people's savings.  飞涨的通货膨胀正在侵蚀人们的储蓄。| 近义词：rapid, swift, speedy, fast, racing, runaway| 反义词：slow, gradual| 词性：形容词

galloping:/ˈɡæləpɪŋ/| n.| 奔驰，飞驰| the act of galloping.| The horse's galloping across the field was a majestic sight.  马儿在田野里飞奔，景象十分壮观。| 近义词：run, sprint, dash, race| 反义词：walk, stroll| 词性：名词
