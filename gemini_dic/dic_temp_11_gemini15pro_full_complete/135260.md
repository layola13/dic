nonparalysis:/ˌnɑːnpəˈræləsɪs/ | n. | 非瘫痪，无瘫痪 | Absence of paralysis; the state of not being paralyzed. |  The patient showed signs of nonparalysis after the surgery. 手术后病人表现出无瘫痪的迹象。| Near synonyms: mobility, movement, function. | Antonyms: paralysis, immobility. | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "paralysis" (from Greek "paralusis" meaning "loosening at the side," from "para-" meaning "beside" + "luein" meaning "to loosen").
