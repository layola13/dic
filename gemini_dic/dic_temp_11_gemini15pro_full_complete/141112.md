ontogenesis:/ˌɒn.təˈdʒɛn.ə.sɪs/ /ˌɑːn.təˈdʒɛn.ə.sɪs/ | n. | 个体发生，个体发育 | The development of an individual organism or anatomical or behavioral feature from the earliest stage to maturity. | Example 1:  The ontogenesis of the human brain is a complex process. 人类大脑的个体发育是一个复杂的过程。

Example 2:  Scientists are studying the ontogenesis of language in children. 科学家正在研究儿童语言的个体发育。 | 近义词: development, maturation, growth | 反义词:  phylogenesis | 词性: 名词
