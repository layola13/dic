bannable:/ˈbænəbəl/
adjective
可被禁止的; 可被封禁的
Susceptible to being banned or prohibited.
His online behavior was so egregious, it was bannable. 他的网络行为非常恶劣，足以被封禁。
The forum moderators determined that his post was bannable due to its hate speech. 论坛版主认定他的帖子由于包含仇恨言论而应被禁止。
近义词:prohibitable, forbiddable
反义词:permissible, allowable
词性:adjective

词源:
From ban (v.) + -able.  "Ban" comes from Old French "banir" meaning "to proclaim, summon, or banish."  "-able" is a suffix indicating capability or worthiness.
