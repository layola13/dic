Lübeck: /ˈlyːbɛk/ | n. | 吕贝克 | A historic port city in northern Germany on the Baltic Sea. |  Lübeck is a UNESCO World Heritage site. 吕贝克是联合国教科文组织世界遗产地。

Lübeck was once the head of the Hanseatic League. 吕贝克曾是汉萨同盟的盟主。 | 近义词: port, city | 反义词: (无) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle Low German *Lūbeke, from Old Saxon *Liubiki ("lovely place"), from *liubo ("dear, pleasant") + *-ik.  The name likely refers to the location's beauty and strategic importance.
