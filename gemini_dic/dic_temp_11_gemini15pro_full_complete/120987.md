miscitations:/ˌmɪssaɪˈteɪʃənz/
n.
错误引用；引文不确
The article was full of miscitations and factual errors.  这篇文章充满了错误的引用和事实错误。
The book contains several miscitations of Shakespeare. 这本书里有几处莎士比亚作品的错误引用。
Synonyms: misquotes, misquotations, incorrect citations
Antonyms: accurate citations, correct quotations
noun
