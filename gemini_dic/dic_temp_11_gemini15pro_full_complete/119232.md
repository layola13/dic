miasmatic:/maɪˈæzmætɪk/ | adj. | 瘴气的；污浊的；腐臭的 | Relating to or characterized by miasma, a noxious or poisonous atmosphere formerly thought to rise from swamps and putrid matter and cause disease. | The miasmatic swamp gave off a foul odor. 瘴气弥漫的沼泽散发出一股恶臭。 |  The air in the crypt was thick and miasmatic. 地窖里的空气又浓又臭。 | 近义词: noxious, pestilential, foul, fetid, putrid | 反义词: pure, clean, fresh, wholesome | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin miasma (“pollution”), from Ancient Greek μίασμα (míasma, “stain, pollution”), from μιαίνω (miaínō, “I stain, pollute”).
