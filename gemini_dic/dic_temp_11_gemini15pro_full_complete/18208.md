belching:/ˈbɛltʃɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɛltʃɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 打嗝，嗳气 | the expulsion of gas from the stomach through the mouth. |  The baby's belching was loud. 婴儿打嗝的声音很大。

The belching of the volcano sent ash into the sky. 火山的喷发将火山灰送上了天空。| 近义词：burping, eructation | 反义词: None | noun


belch:/bɛltʃ/ (US), /bɛltʃ/ (UK)| v. | 打嗝，嗳气；喷出 | to expel gas from the stomach through the mouth; to emit something violently. | He belched loudly after drinking the soda. 他喝完汽水后大声地打了个嗝。

The factory belched smoke into the air. 工厂向空中喷出浓烟。 | 近义词：burp, eructate | 反义词: None | verb
