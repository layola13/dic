nondoctrinaire: /ˌnɒnˌdɒktrɪˈnɛː(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˌdɑːktrɪˈnɛːr/ | adj. | 非教条的，不拘泥于教条的 | Not rigidly adhering to a set of doctrines or theories; pragmatic. |  The senator took a nondoctrinaire approach to the issue, considering all sides before making a decision.  这位参议员对这个问题采取了非教条主义的方法，在做出决定之前考虑了所有方面。

|  She is known for her nondoctrinaire views on economic policy. 她以其对经济政策的非教条主义观点而闻名。| 近义词: pragmatic, flexible, open-minded, empirical | 反义词: doctrinaire, dogmatic, rigid, inflexible | 词性: adjective


词源: non- (not) + doctrinaire (doctrinal, adhering to a doctrine)  来自法语，non- 表示“非”，doctrinaire 表示“教条主义的”。
