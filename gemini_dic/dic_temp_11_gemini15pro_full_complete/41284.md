cordierite:/ˈkɔːrdiəraɪt/ | n. |堇青石|A mineral, (Mg,Fe)2Al4Si5O18, occurring in metamorphic rocks. It is typically blue to violet in color and is used in ceramics and as a gemstone.|Cordierite is often found in contact metamorphic zones. 堇青石常在接触变质带中发现。|Cordierite ceramics have excellent thermal shock resistance. 堇青石陶瓷具有优异的抗热震性。|Synonyms: iolite, dichroite|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
