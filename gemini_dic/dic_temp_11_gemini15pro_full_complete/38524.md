concionatory: /kənˈsɪʃəˌnətɔːri/ /kənˈsaɪʃəˌnətɔːri/
adjective
劝诫的，讲道的，说教的
Relating to or characterized by preaching; hortatory.  Marked by a formal, pompous, and often extended and tedious moral discourse.
The politician delivered a concionatory speech about the importance of family values.  这位政治家发表了一篇关于家庭价值重要性的说教性演讲。
He adopted a concionatory tone when speaking to his children about their behavior. 他在对孩子们谈论他们的行为时，采取了一种说教的语气。
Synonyms: hortatory, didactic, preachy, moralistic, sermonic, homiletic
Antonyms: encouraging, supportive, conversational, informal
词性: adjective
