comtist:/ˈkɒmtɪst/ (US) /ˈkɑːmtist/| n. |孔德主义者；实证主义者|A follower of the philosophy of positivism as formulated by Auguste Comte.| He was a devout Comtist, believing strongly in the power of observation and scientific method. 他是一位虔诚的孔德主义者，坚信观察和科学方法的力量。|Synonyms: positivist, empiricist|Antonyms: idealist, metaphysician|noun
