misrouting:/ˌmɪsˈraʊtɪŋ/
n.
错误路由，误送
the act of sending something to the wrong destination.
The misrouting of the package delayed its delivery by a week. 包裹的错误路由导致其递送延迟了一周。
misdirection, rerouting
correct routing
noun
