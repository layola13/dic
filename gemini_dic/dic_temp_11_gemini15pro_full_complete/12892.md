assuming: /əˈsuːmɪŋ/ (US), /əˈsjuːmɪŋ/ (UK) | prep. conj.| 假设，假定 | Accepting something to be true without proof. | Assuming it rains tomorrow, the picnic will be canceled. 假设明天下雨，野餐将被取消。 | Example: Assuming you're correct, what should we do next?  假设你是对的，我们下一步应该怎么做？ | Synonyms: supposing, presuming, if, provided | Antonyms: doubting, questioning | preposition, conjunction

assuming: /əˈsuːmɪŋ/ (US), /əˈsjuːmɪŋ/ (UK) | adj. | 傲慢的，自负的 | Arrogant or presumptuous. | He had an assuming air about him. 他一副傲慢的样子。 | Example: Her assuming manner made her unpopular. 她傲慢的态度让她不受欢迎。| Synonyms: arrogant, presumptuous, haughty | Antonyms: humble, modest | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *assumptus, past participle of assumere "to take up, adopt, usurp," from ad- "to" + sumere "to take."
