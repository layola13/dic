midtown:/ˈmɪdtaʊn/ | n. |市中心，闹市区|The central part of a town or city, especially the main business or commercial area.|We had lunch at a restaurant in midtown. 我们在市中心的一家餐馆吃了午饭。|近义词：downtown, city center|反义词：suburbs, outskirts|词性:名词

词源:  mid + town.  "Mid" meaning middle and "town" referring to the urban area.  A straightforward compound word indicating the middle of the town.
