dealerships: /ˈdiːlərʃɪps/ | n. |经销商；代理店；经销权|A business that sells the products of a particular company, especially cars.  A dealership is authorized by the manufacturer to sell its products.|

例句：
He works at a car dealership.
他在一个汽车经销店工作。

She bought her new car from the local Ford dealership.
她从当地的福特经销店买了她的新车。


近义词：dealers, distributors, agencies, retailers|
反义词: N/A |
词性: noun |

dealership: From *dealer* + *-ship*.|
