commentation:/ˌkɒmənˈteɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːmənˈteɪʃn/| n.|评论；注释；评论文章|A series of explanatory notes or comments, especially on a literary work. commentation:/ˌkɒmənˈteɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːmənˈteɪʃn/| n.|实况报道；现场解说|A spoken description of an event as it happens, especially on radio or television.|The sports commentator's commentation was lively and engaging.  体育评论员的现场解说生动有趣。|近义词：narration, description, account, report|反义词: silence, muteness|词性:noun
