overnourished: /ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃt/ | adj. | 营养过剩的，营养过度的 | Excessively nourished; having consumed more nutrients than necessary, often leading to obesity or other health problems.  The state of having received more nutrients than the body needs, usually resulting in excessive weight gain. | The overnourished child struggled with his weight.  这个营养过剩的孩子为他的体重而苦恼。

The cat became overnourished from too many treats.  这只猫因为吃太多零食而营养过剩。 | Synonyms: overfed, obese, overweight | Antonyms: undernourished, malnourished, thin, underweight | adjective
