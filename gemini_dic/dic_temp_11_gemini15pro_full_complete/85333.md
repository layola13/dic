harpoonlike:/hɑːrˈpuːnlaɪk/
adjective
像鱼叉的，鱼叉状的
Resembling a harpoon; barbed and pointed.  Shaped or functioning like a harpoon.
The creature's teeth were harpoonlike, perfectly designed for catching fish.  这个生物的牙齿像鱼叉一样，非常适合捕鱼。
The thorns on the rose bush were surprisingly harpoonlike and difficult to remove. 玫瑰丛上的刺出奇地像鱼叉，很难去除。
近义词: barbed, pointed, spearlike
反义词: blunt, rounded
