madrigalianism:/ˌmædrɪˈɡeɪliənɪzəm/ | n. | 牧歌风格；牧歌体裁；牧歌精神 | The quality or style characteristic of a madrigal, especially a complex polyphonic style. |  The composer's later works show a marked shift away from madrigalianism towards a simpler, homophonic texture.  作曲家后期的作品明显地从牧歌风格转向了更简单、更主音化的织体。|Synonyms: madrigal style, polyphony (in music) | Antonyms: homophony (in music) | noun
