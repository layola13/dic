innerly:/ˈɪnəli/| adv.|内在地，精神上，|Inwardly; in the heart or mind.  It describes something happening within a person's thoughts or feelings, rather than externally.|She wept innerly at the news. 她听到这个消息内心哭了。|近义词：inwardly, privately, secretly, spiritually|反义词: outwardly, externally|词性:副词

词源:  From "inner" + "-ly."  "Inner" comes from Old English "innera," comparative of "inne" (within).
