diacoustic:/ˌdaɪəˈkuːstɪk/ | adj. | 透声的；双耳声的 | Relating to the transmission of sound through or by means of two ears. | Diacoustic tests are used to determine directional hearing.  双耳听力测试用于确定方向性听力。|  binaural, biauricular | monaural | adjective
