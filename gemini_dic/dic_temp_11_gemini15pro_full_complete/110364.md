lightweight:/ˈlaɪtweɪt/| adj. n.|轻量级的，轻量级选手|Of less than average weight; designed to be easily portable or movable. |This laptop is lightweight and easy to carry. 这款笔记本电脑很轻，便于携带。|Synonyms: light, portable, flimsy|Antonyms: heavy, bulky, hefty|adjective

lightweight:/ˈlaɪtweɪt/| n.|轻量级选手|A person who competes in a lightweight category, especially in boxing or wrestling. |He's a lightweight boxer. 他是一位轻量级拳击手。|Synonyms: contender, competitor|Antonyms: heavyweight|noun
