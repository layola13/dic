ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blowsen, from Old Norse *blausa (“to puff, blow, swell”), from Proto-Germanic *blausaną (“to blow”).  Related to Old English blāwan (“to blow”). The sense development is from the idea of being puffed up or disheveled.
