free-falls:/ˈfriːfɔːlz/
free-falls: v. |自由落体，垂直落下|To drop or fall rapidly under the force of gravity, without any resistance.|The skydiver free-falls for a few seconds before opening his parachute. 跳伞运动员在打开降落伞之前自由落体几秒钟。|近义词：plummet, plunge, drop|反义词:ascend, rise|词性:动词
free-fall:/ˈfriːfɔːl/
free-fall: n. |自由落体，垂直落下|The state of falling freely under the force of gravity.|The economy is in free-fall. 经济正在直线下滑。|近义词：plunge, drop|反义词:rise, ascent|词性:名词
