machining:/məˈʃiːnɪŋ/| n.|机械加工，机加工|The process of shaping metal or other materials by removing material using machine tools such as lathes, milling machines, and drills.|The machining of this part requires high precision.  这个零件的加工需要很高的精度。|Synonyms:  metalworking, fabrication, tooling|Antonyms:  casting, molding|noun

machining:/məˈʃiːnɪŋ/| adj.|(rare) 用于机加工的|Suitable for or related to machining. |Machining tolerances are very tight. 机加工公差非常严格。|Synonyms: machinable|Antonyms: non-machinable|adjective
