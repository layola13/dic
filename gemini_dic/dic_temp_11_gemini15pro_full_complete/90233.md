house-broken:/ˈhaʊsˌbroʊkən/
adjective
家养的，习惯室内生活的；（尤指宠物）训练好的
Trained to urinate and defecate outside or in a designated area inside.  A house-broken dog will not soil the carpet.
My new puppy is almost house-broken. 我的新小狗几乎已经训练好了不在家里大小便了。
近义词:house-trained, potty-trained
反义词:untrained, not house-trained
词性:adjective

house-broken:/ˈhaʊsˌbroʊkən/
adjective
（指人）驯服的，温顺的；缺乏独立精神的
Subdued and compliant; lacking in independent spirit.  He felt house-broken after years in a dead-end job.
He felt house-broken after years in that restrictive environment.  在那种压抑的环境中待了多年之后，他感觉自己变得温顺听话了。
近义词:docile, tamed, subdued
反义词:rebellious, independent, spirited
词性:adjective


词源:  "house" + "broken".  "Broken" in this context refers to the breaking of a habit (of relieving oneself indoors).
