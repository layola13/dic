bear-out:/ˌber ˈaʊt/| v.|证实，支持|to confirm the truth or accuracy of something|例句：The evidence bears out his story. 证据证实了他的说法。

例句：The facts bear out my theory. 事实证明我的理论是正确的。

近义词：confirm, corroborate, substantiate, verify, validate|反义词：contradict, refute, disprove|词性：动词
