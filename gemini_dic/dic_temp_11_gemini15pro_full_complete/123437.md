monopolization:/məˌnɒpəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /məˌnɑːpəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/| n. |垄断，独占|The act of monopolizing; the condition of being monopolized.  The exclusive possession or control of something. |The monopolization of the oil industry by a few large companies led to higher prices.  少数几家大型公司对石油行业的垄断导致价格上涨。|Synonyms: cornering the market, domination, exclusivity|Antonyms: competition, sharing|noun
