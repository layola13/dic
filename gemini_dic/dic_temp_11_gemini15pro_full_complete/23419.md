bracket:/ˈbrækɪt/ (US), /ˈbrækɪt/ (UK)| n. v.|支架，括号，等级，托架; 用括号括起，把…归入同一类|A bracket is a wall or shelf fixture used to support something, typically something decorative or functional, like a shelf or a light. It can also refer to a punctuation mark ( ) used to enclose parenthetical information or to group mathematical operations. As a verb, it means to place something within brackets or to categorize it.|The shelf was held up by decorative brackets.  这个架子由装饰性支架支撑着。
He bracketed the two numbers together in the equation. 他在方程式中将这两个数字括在一起。|近义词：support, brace, parenthesis, category, group|反义词:separate, divide|noun, verb

bracket:/ˈbrækɪt/ (US), /ˈbrækɪt/ (UK)| n.|等级;档次|A bracket often refers to a grouping of similar things, typically based on income, age, or skill level.|They fall into the highest tax bracket. 他们属于最高税级。|近义词：range, group, tier, level|反义词: individual|noun
