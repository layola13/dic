nonconversions:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɜːrʒənz/| n.|未转化，未转换，不转化（通常指在营销或宗教领域）|The marketing team is analyzing the nonconversions to understand why potential customers didn't complete the purchase.| The nonconversions represented a significant loss of potential revenue.  The evangelist was concerned about the high number of nonconversions after the revival.|近义词：failures, non-responders|反义词:conversions, successes|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "conversion" (from Latin "conversio," meaning "a turning around, alteration, transformation").
