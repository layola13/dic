greenest:/ˈɡriːnɪst/| adj.|最绿的，最环保的|Superlative form of green.  Means having the most green color, or being the most environmentally friendly.|例句：This is the greenest valley I've ever seen. 这是我见过的最绿的山谷。

例句：They live in the greenest house in the neighborhood, powered entirely by solar energy. 他们住在附近最环保的房子里，完全由太阳能供电。|近义词：most eco-friendly, most environmentally conscious|反义词：brownest, least green, least environmentally friendly|词性:形容词
