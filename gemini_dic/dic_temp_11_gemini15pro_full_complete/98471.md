instability:/ˌɪnstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不稳定，不稳定性 | Lack of stability; the state of being unstable. | The political instability of the region is a major concern. 该地区政治的不稳定是一个主要问题。 | instability is a major factor in the current economic crisis.  不稳定是当前经济危机的一个主要因素。 | 近义词：unsteadiness, precariousness, volatility, fluctuation | 反义词：stability, firmness, steadiness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *instabilitatem, from Latin instabilis (“unstable”), from in- (“not”) + stabilis (“stable”).
