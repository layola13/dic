concrement:/ˈkɒŋkrɪmənt/ (US) /ˈkɑːŋkrɪmənt/| n. |结石; 凝结物|A hard, solid mass formed in a body cavity, such as a kidney stone or gallstone.  It is formed by the local accumulation of matter.|例句：The doctor discovered a concrement in the patient's bladder. 医生在病人的膀胱里发现了一块结石。

例句：Kidney concrements can cause severe pain. 肾结石会引起剧烈疼痛。|近义词：calculus, stone|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concrementum, from Latin concrescere "to grow together," from com- "together" + crescere "to grow."
