arbitration:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃn/ | n. |仲裁，调停|The dispute was settled by arbitration.  争议通过仲裁解决。|例句：The two companies agreed to binding arbitration.  两家公司同意具有约束力的仲裁。

例句：They chose arbitration over litigation. 他们选择仲裁而不是诉讼。|近义词：mediation, conciliation, adjudication |反义词：litigation, lawsuit|词性:名词
