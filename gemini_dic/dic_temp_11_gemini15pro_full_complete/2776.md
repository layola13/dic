adumbratively:/ˌædʌmˈbreɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 略述地，概括地，模糊地 | In a way that indicates or outlines something vaguely or roughly; sketchily. | He adumbratively described his plans for the future, leaving many details unclear. 他粗略地描述了他对未来的计划，很多细节都没有交代清楚。 |  The artist adumbratively sketched the scene, capturing its essence with a few quick strokes.  这位艺术家概略地勾勒了这个场景，用几笔快速的笔触捕捉到了它的精髓。 | 近义词: sketchily, vaguely, roughly, imprecisely | 反义词: precisely, explicitly, detailedly, clearly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adumbrare* "to sketch, outline," from *ad-* "to" + *umbra* "shadow."  The sense evolved from "to represent faintly, as with a shadow" to "to outline or sketch."
