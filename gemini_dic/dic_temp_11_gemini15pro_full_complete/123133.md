monist: /ˈmɒnɪst/ (UK) /ˈmoʊnɪst/ (US) | n. | 一元论者 | A person who believes in monism, the doctrine that only one supreme being exists. | Spinoza is considered a prominent monist philosopher. 斯宾诺莎被认为是一位杰出的一元论哲学家。|  The monist argued that mind and matter are ultimately one.  这位一元论者认为精神和物质最终是一体的。 | 近义词:  unitarian | 反义词:  dualist, pluralist | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek μόνος (mónos, “alone, single, only”) + -ist.
