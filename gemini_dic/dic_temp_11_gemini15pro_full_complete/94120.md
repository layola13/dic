impecunious:/ˌɪmpɪˈkjuːniəs/
adjective
贫穷的；没钱的
Having little or no money.
He was a charming but impecunious artist. 他是一位迷人但贫穷的艺术家。
impecunious:/ˌɪmpɪˈkjuːniəs/
adjective
身无分文的
Not having any money.
She was forced to live an impecunious life after losing her job.  失业后她被迫过着身无分文的生活。
near synonyms:  penniless, indigent, destitute, poverty-stricken
antonyms: wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
词性: adjective
