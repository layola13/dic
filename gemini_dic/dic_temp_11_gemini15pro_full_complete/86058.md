heapier:/ˈhiːpiər/| adj.|更大堆的，更丰富的|More heaping; containing a larger heap.  Having a greater quantity, especially of something loose and bulky. |The farmer had a heapier harvest this year than last. 今年农民的收成比去年更多。|The second serving of mashed potatoes was heapier. 第二份土豆泥更大份。|近义词：larger, bigger, more abundant, fuller|反义词:smaller, less, scanter|adjective
