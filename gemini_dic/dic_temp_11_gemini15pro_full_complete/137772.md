nonvassals:/nɒnˈvæsəlz/
|n.| 非附庸者；非诸侯|People or groups who are not vassals, meaning they are not subject to the feudal system and don't owe allegiance or service to a lord.|The nonvassals were free to trade and travel as they pleased. 非附庸者可以自由贸易和旅行。|Near synonyms: freemen, commoners, citizens|Antonyms: vassals, subjects, serfs|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "vassal" (from Old French "vassal," ultimately from Medieval Latin "vassallus," meaning "servant," "dependent").
