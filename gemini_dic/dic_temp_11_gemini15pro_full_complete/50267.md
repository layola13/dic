词源:  From the verb "depersonalize," which is formed by adding the prefix "de-" (meaning "remove" or "reverse") to "personalize." "Personalize" comes from "personal" + "-ize" (a suffix meaning "to make" or "to cause to be"). Ultimately, the root is the Latin word "persona" (mask, character, role).
