ipos:/ˈaɪpɒs/ | n. |  Initial Public Offering (IPO)的缩写 | An initial public offering (IPO) is the process of offering shares of a private corporation to the public in a new stock issuance. This allows a company to raise capital from public investors. |  The ipos was highly successful, with the share price doubling on the first day.  该公司的首次公开募股非常成功，股价在第一天就翻了一番。 | synonym: listing, flotation | antonym: delisting, going private | abbreviation
