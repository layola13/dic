neptune:/ˈnɛptjuːn/ (US), /ˈnɛptʃuːn/ (UK)| n.|海王星；（罗马神话）海神|A giant planet in the outer solar system, eighth planet from the sun. Also the Roman god of the sea.|例句：Neptune is the furthest planet from the sun in our solar system. 海王星是太阳系中距离太阳最远的行星。

例句：In Roman mythology, Neptune is the god of the sea. 在罗马神话中，海王星是海神。|近义词：(for the planet) gas giant, ice giant |反义词: N/A|词性:noun
