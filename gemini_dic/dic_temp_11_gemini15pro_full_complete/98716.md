insure:/ɪnˈʃʊr/ (US), /ɪnˈʃɔːr/ (UK)| vt.|投保，给…保险；确保，保证|To protect against risk of loss by guaranteeing compensation in the event the loss occurs; to make certain of something.|I insured my car against theft. 我给我的汽车投了盗窃险。|近义词: guarantee, protect, ensure, assure|反义词: endanger, jeopardize|Verb
