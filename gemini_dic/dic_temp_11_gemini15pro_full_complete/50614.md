deregulator:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪtər/ | n. |解除管制者，撤销管制规章者| A person or entity that removes or reduces regulations. | The government acted as a deregulator in the telecommunications industry. 政府在电信行业扮演了放松管制的角色。| synonyms: liberalizer, reformer | antonyms: regulator, controller | noun

deregulate:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪt/ | v. |解除管制，撤销管制规章| To remove or reduce government regulations. | The government deregulated the airline industry. 政府解除了对航空业的管制。| synonyms: liberalize, free | antonyms: regulate, control | verb
