obsessed:/əbˈsɛst/| adj.|痴迷的，着迷的|Preoccupied with or constantly thinking about something or someone.|例句：She is obsessed with her weight. 她非常在意自己的体重。|例句：He's obsessed with the idea of becoming a famous actor. 他一心想成为一名著名演员。|近义词：fixated, preoccupied, consumed, captivated|反义词：indifferent, apathetic|词性:形容词

词源: From the Latin word *obsessus*, past participle of *obsidere*  ("to besiege, beset, block, occupy"), from *ob* ("against, towards") + *sedere* ("to sit").  Originally meant "besieged by an evil spirit."
