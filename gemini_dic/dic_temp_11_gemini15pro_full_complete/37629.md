communitarian:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈtɛəriən/
| adj. n.|社群主义的；社群主义者|Relating to or characteristic of a communitarian; a person who advocates communitarianism.|例句：Communitarian values emphasize the importance of social responsibility. 社群主义价值观强调社会责任的重要性。|近义词：collective, social, communal|反义词：individualistic, libertarian|adjective
communitarian:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈtɛəriən/
| n.|社群主义者|A person who advocates communitarianism.|例句：The communitarian argued for greater social cohesion.  这位社群主义者主张加强社会凝聚力。|近义词：socialist, collectivist|反义词：individualist, libertarian|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From community + -arian.  "Community" ultimately derives from Latin "communis" (common, shared). "-arian" is a suffix used to form nouns and adjectives denoting adherence to a particular doctrine or system.
