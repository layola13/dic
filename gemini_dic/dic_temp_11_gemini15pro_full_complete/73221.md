forces:/ˈfɔːrsɪz/ | n. |力量，武力，军队|  A group of people organized and trained for a particular purpose, especially war; strength or energy exerted or brought to bear; coercion or compulsion, especially with the use or threat of violence. |The armed forces were deployed to the region. 武装部队被部署到该地区。| synonyms: troops, army, power, strength, might, energy, pressure, coercion | antonyms: weakness, impotence, peace | noun

forces:/ˈfɔːrsɪz/ | v. |强迫，迫使| To compel by physical, moral, or intellectual means. |He was forced to resign. 他被迫辞职。| synonyms: compel, oblige, coerce, pressure | antonyms: allow, permit, persuade | verb
