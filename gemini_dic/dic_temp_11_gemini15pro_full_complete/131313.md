nonchalant:/ˌnɑːnʃəˈlɑːnt/ | adj. | 冷淡的，漠不关心的，若无其事的 | Appearing or behaving in a calm and relaxed way, often in a way that suggests you are not interested or do not care. |  He was surprisingly nonchalant about winning the lottery.  他中了彩票却表现得异常冷静。 | Synonyms: casual, indifferent, unconcerned, apathetic | Antonyms: anxious, concerned, worried, eager | adjective
