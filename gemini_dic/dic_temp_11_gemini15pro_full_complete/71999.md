flint:/flɪnt/| n.|燧石；打火石；极硬的东西|A very hard gray or black stone that produces a spark when struck against steel.  Used in prehistoric times for making tools and weapons, and later in firearms to ignite gunpowder.|例句：The early humans used flint to make tools. 早期人类使用燧石制作工具。

例句：He struck the flint against steel to make a fire. 他用燧石敲击钢材生火。|近义词：stone, rock, chert|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something soft like "clay" or "cotton")|词性:noun
