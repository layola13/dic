chaetopoda:/kiːtəˈpoʊdə/ | n. |环节动物门|A phylum of segmented worms, including earthworms, leeches, and marine polychaetes, characterized by bristles (chaetae) on their bodies.|Earthworms, members of the Chaetopoda, are beneficial to soil health. 蚯蚓，环节动物门的一员，对土壤健康有益。| Annelida |None | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek χαίτη (khaítē, “long hair, mane”) + πούς (poús, “foot”).
