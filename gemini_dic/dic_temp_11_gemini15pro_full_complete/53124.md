diploetic:/dɪploʊˈɛtɪk/
adjective
板障的；板障层的
Relating to or situated in the diploe, the spongy layer of bone between the inner and outer layers of the skull.
The diploetic veins are located within the spongy bone of the skull.  颅骨板障静脉位于颅骨的海绵骨内。
The skull fracture extended into the diploetic layer. 头骨骨折延伸到了板障层。
近义词: cancellous, trabecular
反义词: compact, cortical
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek diplóos (double) + -etic (relating to).  源于希腊语diplóos（双重的）+ -etic（与...相关的）。
