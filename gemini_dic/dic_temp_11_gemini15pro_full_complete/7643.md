annuloid:/ˈæn.jʊ.lɔɪd/| adj. n.|环状的，环形的；环状体|Resembling a ring; ring-shaped.  A ring-shaped object or structure.|The planet Saturn has a magnificent annuloid system. 土星有一个壮观的环状系统。|The cross-section of the pipe is annuloid. 这根管子的横截面是环形的。|近义词：ring-shaped, toroidal, annular|反义词:linear, straight|词性:adjective, noun|词源:From Latin *annulus (ring) + -oid ("resembling").|
