lovingly:/ˈlʌvɪŋli/| adv.|充满爱意地，亲切地|In a way that shows love and care.  She looked at her children lovingly. 她充满爱意地看着她的孩子们。|例句：She held the baby lovingly in her arms. 她充满爱意地把婴儿抱在怀里。|近义词：affectionately, tenderly, warmly|反义词：coldly, hatefully, indifferently|词性:副词
词源：From love + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Old English lufu.
