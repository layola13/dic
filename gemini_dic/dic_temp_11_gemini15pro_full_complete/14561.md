aves:/ˈeɪviːz/ /ˈɑːveɪz/
n.
birds
Birds are feathered, winged, two-legged, warm-blooded, egg-laying vertebrates.
例句：Many aves migrate south for the winter. 许多鸟类冬天向南迁徙。
例句：The study of aves is called ornithology. 鸟类的研究被称为鸟类学。
近义词：birds, fowl
反义词: N/A
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Latin *avēs, plural of avis (“bird”).
