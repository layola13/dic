mellifluence:/məˈlɪfluəns/| n.|流畅; 甜美;  蜜一样的流淌|A quality of smooth, sweet-flowing eloquence or sound.|Her mellifluence captivated the audience. 她的流畅的口才吸引了观众。|近义词：euphony, sweetness, smoothness, fluency|反义词:harshness, dissonance, cacophony|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mellifluus* "flowing with honey," from *melli-* (combining form of *mel*, *mellis* "honey") + *fluus* "flowing."
