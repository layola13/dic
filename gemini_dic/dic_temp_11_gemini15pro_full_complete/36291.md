cohere:/koʊˈhɪr/ (US), /kəʊˈhɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 粘合，凝聚，连贯 | to stick together; to form a united whole; to be logical and consistent | The particles cohered to form a solid mass. 这些粒子凝聚成固体。 The different parts of the story didn't cohere. 故事的不同部分不连贯。 | 近义词：stick, adhere, unite, bind, connect, integrate | 反义词: separate, disintegrate, fall apart | 词性: 动词

coherent:/koʊˈhɪrənt/ (US), /kəʊˈhɪərənt/ (UK)| adj. | 连贯的，一致的，条理清楚的 | logical and consistent; able to speak clearly and understandably |  She gave a coherent account of the events. 她对事件作了连贯的叙述。 He was too drunk to be coherent. 他醉得语无伦次。 | 近义词：logical, consistent, rational, lucid, articulate | 反义词: incoherent, illogical, rambling, confused | 词性: 形容词

coherence:/koʊˈhɪrəns/ (US), /kəʊˈhɪərəns/ (UK)| n. | 连贯性，一致性 | the quality of being logical and consistent; the quality of forming a united whole  | The essay lacked coherence. 这篇文章缺乏连贯性。  There was no coherence to her argument. 她的论点没有条理性。| 近义词：consistency, unity, cohesion, logic | 反义词: incoherence, inconsistency, disunity | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Latin cohaerēre "to stick together," from co- "together" + haerēre "to stick."
