bushido:/ˈbuːʃiːdoʊ/ /ˈbʊʃiːdoʊ/
n.
武士道，武士道精神
The way of the warrior, referring to the samurai code of conduct in feudal Japan, emphasizing honor, courage, loyalty, and self-discipline.
The samurai lived by the strict code of bushido.  武士们遵循严格的武士道准则。
He demonstrated true bushido in the face of death.  面对死亡，他展现了真正的武士道精神。
近义词：chivalry, code, ethic
反义词：cowardice, dishonor
