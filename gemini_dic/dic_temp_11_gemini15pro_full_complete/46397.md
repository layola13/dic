daemon:/ˈdiːmən/ /ˈdeɪmən/ | n. | 守护进程，后台程序 | A computer program that runs as a background process, rather than being under the direct control of an interactive user.  |  The email server runs as a daemon, constantly checking for new messages.  邮件服务器作为守护进程运行，不断检查新邮件。| Synonyms: background process, service, agent | Antonyms: foreground process | noun

daemon:/ˈdiːmən/ /ˈdeɪmən/ | n. |  精灵，守护神（常指不好的） |  An evil spirit or inner nature.  |  His inner daemons drove him to commit terrible acts.  他内心的恶魔驱使他犯下可怕的行为。 | Synonyms: demon, evil spirit, devil | Antonyms: angel, guardian angel | noun
