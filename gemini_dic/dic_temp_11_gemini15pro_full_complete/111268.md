litora:/ˈlɪtərə/ /ˈlɪtɔːrə/ | n. | shores; seashore; coast | The plural of "litus," a Latin word meaning "shore" or "seaside."  It refers to the region between the high and low tide marks. | Vergil's Georgics speaks of "washing the litora with his waves."  维吉尔的《农事诗》提到“用他的波浪冲刷海岸”。| Near synonyms: coasts, shores, beaches | Antonym: inland | noun
