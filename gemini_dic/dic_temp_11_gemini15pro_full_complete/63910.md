epiphoneme:/ˈɛpɪfəʊˌniːm/ | n. |警句；结语；格言 | A short, pithy, and often antithetical saying or remark at the end of a discourse.  A concluding summarizing sentence. |  The speaker ended his address with the epiphoneme, "Let us therefore go forth with courage and determination." 演讲者以“让我们带着勇气和决心前进”这句警句结束了他的演讲。
