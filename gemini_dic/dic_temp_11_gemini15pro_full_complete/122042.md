mist:/mɪst/| n.|薄雾，水汽，迷蒙|A thin fog, often with condensation.  A cloud of tiny water droplets suspended in the air near or at the earth's surface.|The early morning mist hung heavy over the fields. 早晨的薄雾笼罩着田野。|近义词：fog, haze, vapor|反义词:clearness, sunshine|noun
