haliography:/ˌheɪlɪˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌheɪlɪˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |海水盐度研究；海水盐度记述|The study and description of the salinity of seawater.  It encompasses the measurement, distribution, and variation of salt content in the oceans, seas, and other bodies of saltwater.  It also includes the investigation of the factors influencing salinity, such as evaporation, precipitation, river runoff, and ice formation.|Oceanographers use haliography to understand the complex dynamics of ocean currents and the distribution of marine life. 海洋学家利用海水盐度研究来理解复杂的洋流动态和海洋生物的分布。|Synonym:  Oceanography (in part),  Marine Chemistry (in part)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider freshwater studies/limnology)|noun
