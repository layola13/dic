defreezing:/dɪˈfriːzɪŋ/ |n./v. (gerund)|解冻|The process of thawing or becoming free from ice.  This can refer to food, land, or other things that have been frozen.|
The defreezing of the river in spring allows boats to travel again. 春天河流解冻后，船只又能航行了。
Defreezing the chicken takes several hours in the refrigerator.  鸡肉在冰箱里解冻需要几个小时。|近义词:thawing, melting|反义词:freezing, solidifying|词性:名词/动词(动名词)

Etymology:  de- (prefix meaning "removal or reversal") + freeze + -ing (suffix forming present participles and gerunds).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *friusan.
