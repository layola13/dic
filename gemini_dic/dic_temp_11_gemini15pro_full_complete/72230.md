floribundas:/ˌflɔːrɪˈbʌndəz/
noun
丰花月季（复数）
A group of garden roses known for their profuse blooming, typically bearing clusters of relatively small flowers.
The floribundas in her garden were a riot of color. 她花园里的丰花月季色彩缤纷。
synonyms: cluster-flowered roses, polyantha roses
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) single-flowered roses, sparsely blooming roses
n.
