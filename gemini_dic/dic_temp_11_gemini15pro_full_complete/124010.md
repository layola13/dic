morology: /məˈrɒlədʒi/ (UK) /məˈrɑːlədʒi/ (US)
|n.|愚蠢；愚蠢的行为；愚蠢的话|The study of foolishness or foolish behavior.  It can also refer to the foolish behavior or speech itself.|His constant morology exasperated his colleagues. 他不断的愚蠢行为惹恼了他的同事们。|近义词：foolishness, stupidity, absurdity, silliness|反义词：wisdom, intelligence, sense, sagacity|词性: noun


Etymology:
From Greek *μωρολογία* (mōrologia), from *μῶρος* (mōros, “foolish”) + *-λογία* (-logia, “-logy”).
