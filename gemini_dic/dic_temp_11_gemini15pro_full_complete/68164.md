faineancy:/ˈfeɪniːənsi/| n.|懒惰，无所事事|A disposition to avoid exertion; laziness; idleness.|He was fired for faineancy. 他因为懒惰被解雇了。|近义词：laziness, indolence, idleness|反义词：diligence, industry|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *faineantise*, from *faineant* "idle," from *faire neant* "to do nothing," from *faire* "to do" + *neant* "nothing."
