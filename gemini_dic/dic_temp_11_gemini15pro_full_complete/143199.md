outcourting: /ˌaʊtˈkɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的求爱；在求爱方面超过
surpassing in courtship; courting more successfully than another.
He was outcourting his rival for her affections. 他在追求她的过程中胜过他的情敌。
近义词: outmaneuver, outdo, surpass
反义词: lose to, be surpassed by
词性: verb

outcourting: /ˌaʊtˈkɔːrtɪŋ/
noun
胜过…的求爱；在求爱方面超过(行为)
the act of surpassing someone in courtship.
His outcourting of his rival led to their engagement.  他胜过情敌的求爱最终促成了他们的订婚。
近义词:  superior courtship, winning courtship
反义词: losing courtship, failed courtship
词性: noun
