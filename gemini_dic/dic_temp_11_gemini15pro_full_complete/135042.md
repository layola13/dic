nonnoble:/nɒnˈnəʊbl/ | adj. | 非贵族的，卑贱的 | Not noble; of low birth or rank; base, ignoble. |  The nonnoble classes often struggled for basic rights. 非贵族阶级经常为基本权利而斗争。|  He engaged in nonnoble pursuits, like gambling and petty theft. 他从事卑鄙的勾当，比如赌博和小偷小摸。 | 近义词: base, ignoble, common, plebeian | 反义词: noble, aristocratic, highborn | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- + noble. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Noble" comes from Old French, ultimately from Latin "nobilis," meaning "well-known, famous, high-born, excellent."
