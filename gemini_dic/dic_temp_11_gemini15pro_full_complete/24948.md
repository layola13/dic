brush-off:/ˈbrʌʃ ɒf/ (US) /ˈbrʌʃ ɒf/ (UK)| n.|冷落，拒绝，打发|An abrupt or offhand dismissal of a person or request.|She gave him the brush-off when he asked her for a date.  她拒绝了他约会她的请求。|Synonyms: rejection, snub, dismissal, cold shoulder|Antonyms: acceptance, welcome, embrace|noun
