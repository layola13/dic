havener: /ˈheɪvənər/ | n. | 港务员；码头管理员 | A person who works at a haven or port, managing the arrival and departure of ships and other vessels. | The havener directed the ship to its designated berth. 港务员指挥船只停泊到指定的泊位。|  The havener collected the docking fees from the captain. 港务员向船长收取了停泊费。 |Synonyms: port worker, dockworker, longshoreman, stevedore | Antonyms: N/A | noun
