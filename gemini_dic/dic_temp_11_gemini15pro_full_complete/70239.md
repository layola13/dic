fictional:/ˈfɪkʃənəl/| adj.|虚构的；小说的|Of, relating to, or characterized by fiction; invented by the imagination.  Created for the purposes of a story, play, or film; not based on fact.|She's a fictional character in a popular TV series. 她是一个热门电视剧中的虚构人物。|The story is entirely fictional.  这个故事完全是虚构的.|synonyms: invented, imaginary, made-up, fabricated, unreal, mythical, legendary, apocryphal|antonyms: real, factual, true, actual, authentic|adjective
