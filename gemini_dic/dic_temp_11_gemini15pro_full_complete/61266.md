emarginately:/iˈmɑːrdʒɪnətli/ | adv. | 边缘缺刻地，边缘有凹陷地 | In a way that has a notched or indented margin. | The petals were emarginately shaped, giving the flower a delicate appearance.  花瓣的形状边缘有凹陷，使花朵看起来很精致。 | notchedly, indentedly | entirely, smoothly | adverb
