caravansary:/ˌkærəˈvænsəri/| n.| 商队旅馆，大客栈|A large inn or roadside hotel in the Middle East or Asia, with a central courtyard for accommodating caravans.|The travelers rested for the night at the caravansary. 旅行者们在商队旅馆过夜。|Synonyms: inn, hostel, lodge, hotel|Antonyms: house, home|noun
