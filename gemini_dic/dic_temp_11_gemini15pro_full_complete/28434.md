carborundum:/ˌkɑːrbəˈrʌndəm/| n. |金刚砂，碳化硅|A very hard, black, crystalline compound of silicon and carbon (SiC), used as an abrasive and in refractories.|  Carborundum is used in grinding wheels. 金刚砂用于砂轮。|Synonyms: silicon carbide, abrasive|Antonyms: N/A|noun
