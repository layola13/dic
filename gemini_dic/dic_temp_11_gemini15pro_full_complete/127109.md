nationalizers:/ˈnæʃənəˌlaɪzərz/
noun|国有化者，国有化支持者|People who support or carry out the nationalization of industries or resources.|The nationalizers argued that key industries should be under state control.|近义词：statists, socialists (in some contexts)|反义词:privatizers, capitalists|n.
