beambird:/ˈbiːmbɜːrd/| n.|束流鸟，光束鸟（指同步辐射装置中用来引导和控制光束的装置）|A beambird is a complex system of optical elements, such as mirrors, slits, and monochromators, used at synchrotron radiation facilities to extract, focus, and manipulate the intense X-ray beams produced by the synchrotron. |Example 1:  The researchers used a specialized beambird to study the structure of a protein crystal. 研究人员使用专门的光束鸟来研究蛋白质晶体的结构。

Example 2: The new beambird allows for higher resolution experiments. 新的光束鸟可以进行更高分辨率的实验。|近义词: beamline (更常用), experimental station|反义词: 无|词性: 名词
