lagting: /ˈlɑːɡθɪŋ/ | n. | 拉格廷（挪威议会下议院）| The Lagting is one of the two chambers of the Norwegian Parliament, responsible for approving or rejecting laws proposed by the Odelsting. | The Lagting rejected the proposed amendment to the law. 拉格廷否决了这项法律修正案。| The Lagting played a crucial role in the passing of the new healthcare legislation. 拉格廷在新医疗保健法规的通过中发挥了关键作用。 | synonym: lower house, legislative chamber | antonym: upper house | noun
