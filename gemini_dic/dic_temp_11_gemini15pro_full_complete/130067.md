non-positivistic:/ˌnɒnˈpɒzɪtɪˈvɪstɪk/
adjective
非实证主义的
Not based on or believing in positivism (a philosophical system that holds that every rationally justifiable assertion can be scientifically verified or is capable of logical or mathematical proof, and that therefore rejects metaphysics and theism).  It emphasizes the importance of interpretation and context in understanding social phenomena.
Her research takes a non-positivistic approach, focusing on qualitative interviews and ethnographic studies. 她采用了非实证主义的研究方法，侧重于定性访谈和人种学研究。
The professor argued for a non-positivistic methodology in the social sciences.  这位教授主张在社会科学中采用非实证主义的方法论。
近义词:interpretive, hermeneutic, constructivist
反义词:positivist, empirical
词性:adjective
