lidge:/lɪdʒ/
n.
ledge
岩架；突出部分
The climber found a narrow lidge to rest on.
登山者找到了一个狭窄的岩架休息。
Synonyms: shelf, ledge, projection
Antonyms:  recess, cavity
noun

Etymology:  From Middle English ligge, legge (“ledge, shelf, crossbar”), probably from Old Norse *liggja, from liggja (“to lie”). Related to lay.
