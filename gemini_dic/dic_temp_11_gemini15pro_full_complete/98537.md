instigative:/ɪnˈstɪɡətɪv/ | adj. | 煽动的，教唆的，挑拨的 | Tending to instigate; provocative. |  The instigative speech riled up the crowd.  煽动性的演讲激怒了人群。 |  provocative, inflammatory, inciting, abetting |  pacifying, conciliatory | adjective

instigate:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/ | vt. | 唆使，煽动，教唆 | Bring about or initiate (an action or event). |  He instigated a rebellion against the government. 他煽动了一场反政府叛乱。| incite, provoke, goad, urge |  prevent, deter | verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *instigatus, past participle of instigare "to incite, urge on," from in- "toward" + stigare "to prick, goad."
