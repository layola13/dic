jazzes:/ˈdʒæzɪz/
verb|使...具有爵士乐风格;演奏爵士乐| To play or arrange music in a jazz style. |He jazzes up the song with some syncopated rhythms. 他用切分音律使这首歌更具爵士风格。|近义词:improvises, swings|反义词:classicizes|词性:v.

jazzes:/ˈdʒæzɪz/
noun|爵士乐(jazz的复数)|A style of music characterized by improvisation, syncopation, and a distinctive rhythmic feel.|The 1920s were a golden age for jazzes. 20世纪20年代是爵士乐的黄金时代。|近义词:blues, ragtime|反义词:classical music|词性:n.
