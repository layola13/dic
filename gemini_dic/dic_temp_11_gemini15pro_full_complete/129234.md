noctilucine:/ˌnɒktɪˈluːsɪn/ | n. |夜光素; 发光素 | A substance that exhibits bioluminescence, the emission of light by living organisms. It's typically found in organisms like fireflies and certain marine creatures. | The firefly's glow is due to the presence of noctilucine in its abdomen. 萤火虫的闪光是因为它腹部存在夜光素。|  The noctilucine in the dinoflagellates caused the ocean to sparkle at night.  夜光藻中的夜光素使海洋在夜间闪闪发光。 | synonym: luciferin, photoprotein | antonym: N/A | noun
