bioluminescence:/ˌbaɪoʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsəns/| n.|生物发光|The emission of light by living organisms as a result of a chemical reaction.|例句：Fireflies use bioluminescence to attract mates. 萤火虫利用生物发光来吸引配偶。

例句：The deep ocean is full of creatures that exhibit bioluminescence. 深海中充满了展现生物发光的生物。|近义词：phosphorescence|反义词:|名词

bioluminescences:/ˌbaɪoʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsənsiz/| n.|生物发光现象（复数）|Multiple instances or types of bioluminescence.|例句：The researchers studied various bioluminescences in marine organisms. 研究人员研究了海洋生物中各种各样的生物发光现象。

例句：The bioluminescences of the fungi created a magical glow in the forest.  真菌的生物发光在森林中营造出一种神奇的光芒。|近义词:|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + luminescence. Bio- comes from Greek bios "life". Luminescence comes from Latin lumen "light".  "life-light"
