face-saving:/ˈfeɪsˌseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
保全面子的，顾及颜面的
Intended to protect someone's reputation or dignity.
He resigned in a face-saving gesture. 他辞职是为了保全面子。
近义词:dignified, respectable, honorable
反义词:humiliating, embarrassing
词性:adjective

face-saving:/ˈfeɪsˌseɪvɪŋ/
noun
保全面子，顾全颜面
The action of protecting someone's reputation or dignity.
The agreement allowed both sides some measure of face-saving. 该协议让双方都保住了一些面子。
近义词:dignity, prestige, honor
反义词:humiliation, disgrace
词性:noun
