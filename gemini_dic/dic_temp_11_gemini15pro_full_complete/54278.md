disguisedness:/dɪsˈɡaɪzdnɪs/
n.
伪装的状态；被掩饰的状态
the state of being disguised or concealed.
His disguisedness was so complete that no one recognized him. 他的伪装如此完美，以至于没有人认出他。
The spy's disguisedness allowed him to infiltrate the enemy base.  间谍的伪装使他能够渗透到敌人的基地。
近义词：concealment, camouflage, disguise
反义词：obviousness, openness, exposure
词性: noun

词源: From disguise + -ed + -ness.  Disguise itself comes from Old French desguisier, meaning "to alter the guise or appearance of."
