nonrecuperative:/ˌnɒnriˈkʌpərətɪv/ | adj. | 非 recupera的，非恢复性的，非蓄热的 | Not recuperative; not recovering or reclaiming heat, energy, or materials. |  The process was nonrecuperative, leading to significant energy loss.  这个过程是非恢复性的，导致了巨大的能量损失。| Example: The nonrecuperative furnace design resulted in higher operating costs.  这个非蓄热式熔炉设计导致了更高的运营成本。 | 近义词: nonregenerative, wasteful | 反义词: recuperative, regenerative | 词性: adjective

词源: non- + recuperative.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "recuperative" comes from the Latin "recuperare," meaning "to recover."
