jumpable:/ˈdʒʌmpəbl/| adj.|可跳跃的，可以跳过的|Able to be jumped over or from.|The fence was low and jumpable. 这篱笆很矮，可以跳过去。|近义词：leapable, crossable|反义词: unjumpable, impassable|词性: adjective
Etymology: From "jump" (v.) + "-able" (suffix). "Jump" comes from Old French "jumper," likely of imitative origin. "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," from Old French "-able" and ultimately from Latin "-abilis."
