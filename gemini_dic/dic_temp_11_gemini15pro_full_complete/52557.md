diffractogram: /dɪˈfræktəɡræm/ | n. | 衍射图；衍射花样 | A graphical representation of the intensity of scattered radiation (such as X-rays or neutrons) as a function of scattering angle. It is used to analyze the structure of materials. | The diffractogram showed sharp peaks, indicating a crystalline structure. 衍射图显示出尖锐的峰，表明其具有晶体结构。

The diffractogram revealed the presence of impurities in the sample.  衍射图揭示了样品中存在杂质。 | Synonyms: diffraction pattern | Antonyms: None | Noun

diffractogram: /dɪˈfræktəɡræm/ | n. | 衍射图谱 | A plot of the intensity of diffracted radiation versus the diffraction angle.  |  The diffractogram of the crystal showed a regular pattern. 晶体的衍射图谱显示出规则的图案。

The analysis of the diffractogram provided information about the crystal structure. 对衍射图谱的分析提供了有关晶体结构的信息。 | Synonyms: diffraction pattern, X-ray diffraction pattern (if using X-rays) | Antonyms: None | Noun
