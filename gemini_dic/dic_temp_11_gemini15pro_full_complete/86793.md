hemadynamometer:/ˌhiːməˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/ /ˌhiːməˌdaɪnəˈmɑːmɪtər/
noun|血流动力计；血压计|An instrument for measuring the force of the blood current. It typically measures blood pressure.|The doctor used a hemadynamometer to check the patient's blood pressure.|近义词: sphygmomanometer, blood pressure monitor|反义词: None|词性:n.

Etymology:
hema- (Greek: αἷμα, haîma, "blood") + dynamo- (Greek: δύναμις, dýnamis, "power, force") + -meter (Greek: μέτρον, métron, "measure")
