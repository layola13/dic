cpr:/ˌsiː piː ˈɑːr/ (US), /ˌsiː piː ˈɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.|心肺复苏；心脏复苏术|Cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) is an emergency lifesaving procedure performed when the heart stops beating.  It combines chest compressions, rescue breaths, and defibrillation (if available) to restore blood circulation and breathing.|A bystander performed CPR on the heart attack victim until the paramedics arrived. 路人对心脏病发作的受害者进行了心肺复苏，直到医护人员到达。|近义词:resuscitation|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 death 死亡)|名词
