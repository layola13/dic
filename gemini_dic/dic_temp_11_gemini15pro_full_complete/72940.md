fondue: /fɒnˈduː/ (UK), /fɑːnˈduː/ (US) | n. | 奶酪火锅；巧克力火锅 | A Swiss dish of melted cheese served in a communal pot over a small burner, into which pieces of bread are dipped. Also, similar dishes using chocolate or other sauces. |  例句：We had a cheese fondue for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了奶酪火锅。

例句：They dipped strawberries into the chocolate fondue. 他们把草莓蘸进巧克力火锅里。

近义词：dip (as in dipping sauce) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性：noun


fondus: /fɒnˈduːz/ (UK), /fɑːnˈduːz/ (US) | n. |  奶酪火锅（复数）; 巧克力火锅（复数）| Plural form of "fondue". | 例句：We tried several different fondus at the restaurant. 我们在餐厅尝试了几种不同的火锅。

例句：The fondus were kept warm over small burners. 这些火锅都用小炉子保温。


近义词：dips | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性：noun


词源: From French "fondu," past participle of "fondre" (to melt), from Latin "fundere" (to pour, melt).
