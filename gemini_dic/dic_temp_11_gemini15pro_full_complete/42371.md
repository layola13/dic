counterattacking:/ˌkaʊntərəˈtækɪŋ/
| adj. |反击的，反攻的|
An act of attacking in response to being attacked.  Responding to an attack by attacking back. |
The army is counterattacking on the eastern front.  这支军队正在东部战线反攻。|
The team's counterattacking strategy proved effective. 该队的反击策略证明是有效的。|
近义词: offensive, aggressive, retaliating |
反义词: defensive, retreating, withdrawing |
词性:  adjective


counterattack:/ˈkaʊntərətæk/
| n.  v. |反击，反攻|
An attack made in response to an attack by an enemy or opponent.  To attack back after being attacked.  |
The general ordered a swift counterattack. 将军下令迅速反击。|
The boxer counterattacked with a powerful right hook. 拳击手以一记强有力的右勾拳进行反击。|
近义词: retaliation, riposte, offensive |
反义词: defense, retreat, withdrawal |
词性: noun, verb

词源:
From counter- + attack.  "Counter-" comes from the Old French word "contre-" meaning "against".
