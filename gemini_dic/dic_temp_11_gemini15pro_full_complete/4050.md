aides:/eɪdz/| n.|助手，副官，辅助物|A person who acts as an assistant or helper, typically to a person of higher rank or status.  Someone who provides support or assistance.|The president's aides briefed the press. 总统的助手向新闻界做了简报。|Synonyms: assistant, helper, supporter, attendant|Antonyms: opponent, adversary|noun
