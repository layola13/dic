chaptrel:/ˈtʃæptrəl/| n. |墙板，壁柱|A small pillar or pilaster, often decorative, used to support or adorn a wall, especially near a door or window opening. It's a type of wall pier.  | The chaptrels flanking the arched doorway were intricately carved.  拱形门口两侧的壁柱雕刻精美。|  Near synonyms: pilaster, pier, column, support | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider words implying lack of support or decoration like gap, void, plain) | noun
