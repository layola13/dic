half-smilingly: /ˌhɑːfˈsmaɪlɪŋli/ /ˌhæfˈsmaɪlɪŋli/
adverb
半笑着地，带着一丝微笑地
in a way that involves smiling slightly
He half-smilingly acknowledged the applause. 他半笑着回应了掌声。
She half-smilingly shook her head. 她带着一丝微笑摇了摇头。
Synonyms: almost smilingly, faintly smilingly
Antonyms: frowningly, scowlingly
词性: 副词


half-smilingly: /ˌhɑːfˈsmaɪlɪŋli/ /ˌhæfˈsmaɪlɪŋli/
Etymology:
From 'half' + 'smiling' + '-ly'. 'Half' indicates a partial or incomplete action, 'smiling' refers to the act of smiling, and '-ly' is a suffix used to form adverbs.
