deejayed:/ˌdiːˈdʒeɪd/
verb
担任DJ；做DJ工作
worked as a disc jockey
He deejayed at the club last night. 他昨晚在俱乐部担任DJ。
近义词:DJ'd, spun records
反义词:None
词性:v.

deejayed:/ˌdiːˈdʒeɪd/
adjective
由DJ播放的
played by a DJ
The music was deejayed live. 音乐是由DJ现场播放的。
近义词:DJ'd, mixed
反义词:pre-recorded
词性:adj.

词源:  "deejay" comes from "DJ," which is an abbreviation of "disc jockey."  "Disc" refers to vinyl records, and "jockey" refers to someone who operates or controls something.  The "-ed" ending transforms the noun into a verb (past tense or past participle) or an adjective.
