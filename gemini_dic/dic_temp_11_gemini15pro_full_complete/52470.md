diet:/ˈdaɪət/ | n. vi. vt.| 饮食，节食 |  The kind and amount of food prescribed for a person or animal for a special reason. To regulate the food of, especially in order to improve the physical condition. | He's dieting to lose weight. 他正在节食减肥。 | synonyms: reduce, slim, lose weight | antonyms: gorge, overeat, indulge | verb
