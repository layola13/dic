bombast:/ˈbɑːmbæst/ /ˈbɒmbæst/| n.  vi.|夸大的言辞；夸夸其谈|High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people.|The politician's speech was full of bombast, but lacked substance. 这位政客的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，但缺乏实质内容.|Synonyms: bluster, rhetoric, grandiloquence|Antonyms: understatement, simplicity, conciseness|noun, verb


bombast:/ˈbɑːmbæst/ /ˈbɒmbæst/| vi.|夸夸其谈|To speak pompously or boastfully.|He bombasted about his achievements, but no one was impressed. 他吹嘘自己的成就，但没有人留下深刻印象。|Synonyms: bluster, rant, brag|Antonyms: understate, downplay, be modest|noun, verb
