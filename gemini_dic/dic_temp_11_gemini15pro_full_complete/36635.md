collectivizations:/kəˌlɛktɪvɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
集体化
The process of organizing rural populations and agricultural production into collectively owned and controlled units, often enforced by the state.
The collectivizations of the 1930s in the Soviet Union led to widespread famine.  苏联20世纪30年代的集体化导致了 widespread 的饥荒。
Collectivization of farms was a key component of communist ideology.  农场集体化是共产主义意识形态的一个关键组成部分。
Synonym:  communization, consolidation
Antonym: privatization, individualization
noun
