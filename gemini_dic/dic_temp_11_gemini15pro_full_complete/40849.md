cooed:/kuːd/| v. | 咕咕叫；轻声细语 |To make a soft murmuring sound, like a dove or a baby.  A gentle murmuring sound expressing happiness or affection.| The baby cooed happily in her mother's arms. 婴儿在母亲的怀里幸福地咕咕叫。| The doves cooed softly in the trees. 鸽子在树上轻轻地咕咕叫。|近义词：murmured, whispered, crooned|反义词：shouted, yelled|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *couwen, from Old English *cūwan, of imitative origin. Cognate with West Frisian koeje (“to coo”), Dutch koejen (“to coo”), German kauen (“to chew”).
