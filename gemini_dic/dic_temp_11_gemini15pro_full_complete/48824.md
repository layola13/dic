defog:/diːˈfɒɡ/ /diːˈfɑːɡ/| v. | 除雾，使清晰 |To remove fog or condensation from something, such as a windshield or eyeglasses. Also, figuratively, to clarify or make something easier to understand.|I used the defogger to clear the windshield. 我使用了除雾器来清除挡风玻璃上的雾气。|The speaker tried to defog the complex issue for the audience. 演讲者试图为观众阐明这个复杂的问题。|synonyms: clarify, clear, demist, explain, illuminate|antonyms: confuse, obscure, complicate|verb
