conciliar:/kənˈsɪliər/ | adj.| 和解的，调和的; 安抚的，调停的 | intended to reconcile or pacify; relating to or of the nature of a council, especially an ecclesiastical council |  The new CEO adopted a conciliatory approach to labor relations. 新任首席执行官采取了安抚的劳工关系策略。| synonyms: appeasing, mollifying, pacifying, placatory, propitiatory | antonyms: antagonistic, hostile, uncompromising | adjective

conciliar:/kənˈsɪliər/ | n.|宗教会议| An assembly of bishops and other clergy, especially a general council of the Roman Catholic Church. | The Pope convened a conciliar meeting to discuss the reforms. 教皇召集了一次宗教会议来讨论改革事宜。| synonyms: council, synod, assembly | antonyms: N/A | noun
