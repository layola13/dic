overmoisten:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔɪsən/ | v. | 过度湿润；使…过于潮湿 | To make something excessively moist or damp. | The baker overmoistened the cake batter, resulting in a dense, heavy cake.  面包师把蛋糕糊弄得太湿了，导致蛋糕又密又重。| The gardener overmoistened the soil, which caused the roots to rot. 园丁把土壤浇得太湿了，导致根部腐烂。 | overhydrate, oversaturate, waterlog | dry, dehydrate | Verb
