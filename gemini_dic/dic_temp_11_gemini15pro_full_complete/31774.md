checkbooks: /ˈtʃɛkˌbʊks/
n.
支票簿；支票本
A book containing checks and stubs, used for making payments from a checking account.
I need to order new checkbooks from the bank. 我需要从银行订购新的支票簿。
She balanced her checkbook every month. 她每个月都结算她的支票簿。
Synonyms: chequebook (British English)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "debit card," "credit card," or "online banking" as alternative payment methods.)
