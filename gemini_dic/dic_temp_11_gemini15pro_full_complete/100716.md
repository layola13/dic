intralobular:/ˌɪntrəˈlɒbjʊlə(r)/
adjective
小叶内的
Situated within a lobule.
例句：Intralobular ducts are small tubes located within the lobules of glands like the liver or breast. 小叶内导管是位于肝脏或乳腺等腺体小叶内的小管。

例句：The intralobular injection of the drug caused localized necrosis.  药物的小叶内注射导致局部坏死。

近义词:  intra-acinar
反义词: interlobular
词性: adjective
