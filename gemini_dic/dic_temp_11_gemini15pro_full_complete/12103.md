artists:/ˈɑːrtɪsts/| n.|艺术家；美术家|A person who creates visual art, such as paintings, sculptures, or drawings.  A person skilled in any of the fine arts, such as a musician, actor, or dancer.|例句：The artists displayed their work in the gallery. 艺术家们在画廊里展示他们的作品。

例句：Many famous artists lived in Paris during the 20th century. 许多著名的艺术家在20世纪住在巴黎。|近义词：creators, performers, designers, painters, sculptors, musicians, actors, dancers|反义词:audience, viewer, spectator|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French artiste (“artist, artisan”), from Italian artista, from Late Latin artista, from Latin ars (“art, skill”).
