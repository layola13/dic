lithographs:/ˈlɪθəɡræfs/
n.
石版画；平版印刷品
A lithograph is a print made from a design drawn on a specially prepared stone.
The museum has a large collection of lithographs by Toulouse-Lautrec.  博物馆收藏了大量的图卢兹-洛特雷克的石版画。
Synonyms: prints, engravings
Antonyms: originals, paintings
noun


lithograph:/ˈlɪθəɡræf/
v.
用石版印刷
To lithograph something means to print it using a lithographic process.
The images were lithographed onto high-quality paper.  这些图像被石版印刷在高质量的纸张上。
Synonyms: print
Antonyms: handwrite, hand-paint
verb
