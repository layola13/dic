febrifuge:/ˈfebrɪfjuːdʒ/| n. adj.|退烧药，解热药；退热的|A medicine or treatment that reduces fever.  A substance or procedure that reduces fever.|Aspirin is a common febrifuge.|近义词：antipyretic, antifebrile|反义词:|noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *febrifugium, from Latin febris (“fever”) + fugāre (“to put to flight”).
