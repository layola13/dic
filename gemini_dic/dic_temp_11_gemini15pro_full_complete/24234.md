bridie:/ˈbraɪdi/| n.|肉馅饼，新娘|A savory pastry filled with meat, often served hot.  A Scottish word originally referring to a wedding feast, later applied to the pie served there.|例句：The bridie was warm and flavorful, filled with seasoned ground beef. 肉馅饼很热乎，味道鲜美，里面塞满了调味好的碎牛肉。

例句：She ordered a bridie and a cup of tea for lunch. 她午餐点了一份肉馅饼和一杯茶。|近义词：pie, pasty, turnover|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "cake" for a sweet contrast)|词性: noun
