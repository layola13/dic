一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think about this movie? 你觉得这部电影怎么样？
What does she usually do on weekends? 她周末通常做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? 你在看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么才赢得这个奖项？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
