hoodooed:/ˌhuːˈduːd/
verb
中文翻译：被施魔法；被诅咒；倒霉；遭遇厄运
英文解析：Affected by a hoodoo; brought bad luck to; jinxed.  To hoodoo someone is to bring bad luck upon them, often through some sort of curse or spell.  It can also be used more generally to mean simply to have bad luck.
例句：
The team felt hoodooed after losing five games in a row.
球队连续输了五场比赛后感觉被诅咒了。

He believed he was hoodooed after breaking a mirror.
他打破镜子后相信自己倒霉了。


近义词：cursed, jinxed, plagued, bewitched
反义词：blessed, lucky, fortunate
词性：v.
