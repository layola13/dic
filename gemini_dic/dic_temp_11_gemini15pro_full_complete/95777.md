incontinence:/ɪnˈkɒntɪnəns/ (US: /ɪnˈkɑːntɪnəns/)| n. |失禁，不能自制|Inability to control bodily functions, especially urination or defecation.|She suffers from incontinence after the surgery. 她手术后患有失禁。|近义词：inability to control, loss of control|反义词: continence, control|词性:名词

incontinent:/ɪnˈkɒntɪnənt/ (US: /ɪnˈkɑːntɪnənt/)| adj. |失禁的，不能自制的|Lacking self-restraint; uncontrolled.|He was incontinent of laughter. 他忍不住大笑。|近义词：unrestrained, uncontrolled|反义词: continent, restrained|词性:形容词
