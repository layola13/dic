hydrachnid:/haɪˈdræknɪd/ | n. | 水蜘蛛 |  An aquatic arachnid, also known as a water mite.  These tiny creatures are typically found in freshwater environments and are related to spiders and ticks. |  Hydrachnids are common in ponds and lakes. 水蜘蛛在池塘和湖泊中很常见。| The hydrachnid scurried across the surface of the water. 水蜘蛛掠过水面。 | synonyms: water mite | antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider terrestrial arachnids like spiders or scorpions) | noun
