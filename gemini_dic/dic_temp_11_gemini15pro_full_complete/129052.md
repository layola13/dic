nitrifier:/ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪər/| n. |硝化细菌|An organism, especially a bacterium, that oxidizes ammonia to nitrites or nitrites to nitrates, as part of the nitrogen cycle.|Nitrifiers play a crucial role in the nitrogen cycle, converting ammonia into nitrates, which plants can absorb. 硝化细菌在氮循环中起着至关重要的作用，将氨转化为植物可以吸收的硝酸盐。|近义词:nitrogen-fixing bacteria|反义词:denitrifier|词性:noun
