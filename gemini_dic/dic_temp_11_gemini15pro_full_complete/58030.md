drogue:/droʊɡ/| n.|减速伞，阻力伞，海锚|A drogue is a device used to slow the movement of something, especially an aircraft or a boat.|
The pilot deployed the drogue to slow the aircraft down. 飞行员展开了减速伞以降低飞机速度。

The fishing boat used a drogue to stabilize itself in the rough seas.  渔船使用海锚在汹涌的海面上保持稳定。|近义词：anchor, brake, drag, sea anchor|反义词:accelerator|词性:名词
