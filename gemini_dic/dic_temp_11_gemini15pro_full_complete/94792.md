impugn:/ɪmˈpjuːn/ | v. | 质疑，非难，攻击 | To challenge the validity, honesty, or motives of something or someone. | The lawyer impugned the witness's testimony. 律师质疑证人的证词。| Synonyms: challenge, question, dispute, attack, criticize | Antonyms: support, defend, uphold, endorse | verb
