darking:/ˈdɑːrkɪŋ/
Verb
变暗; 使变暗
The sky was darking rapidly. 天空迅速变暗。
近义词: dimming, darkening, obscuring
反义词: brightening, lightening
词性: v.

darking:/ˈdɑːrkɪŋ/
Noun
昏暗; 变暗
The darking of the room made it difficult to see.  房间变暗使得很难看清东西。
近义词: dimming, darkening
反义词: brightening, lightening
词性: n.


词源:  From the verb "darken," which comes from the Old English "dearcian" meaning "to become dark."  Ultimately related to the Proto-Germanic "*derkaz" (dark).
