kranging:/ˈkreɪndʒɪŋ/
verb
发出刺耳的金属摩擦声;发出嘎吱嘎吱的声音
making a harsh grating metallic sound.
例句：The rusty gate kranged as it opened. 生锈的大门打开时发出嘎吱嘎吱的声音。
例句：The old machinery kranged and groaned throughout the night.  老旧的机器整夜发出嘎吱嘎吱和呻吟的声音。
近义词：grating, grinding, scraping, screeching
反义词：smooth, silent
词性：v.
