ineffaceably:/ˌɪnɪˈfeɪsəbli/
adverb
不可磨灭地，永久地
In a way that cannot be erased or forgotten.
The experience was ineffaceably etched in her memory. 这段经历深深地铭刻在她的记忆中。

Her kindness is ineffaceably imprinted on my heart. 她的善良永远铭刻在我的心中。


Synonym: permanently, indelibly, lastingly
Antonym: temporarily, fleetingly, transiently
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + efface (to erase) + -able (capable of being) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Efface comes from Old French effacer, from ex- (out) + face (face). Ultimately from Latin ex- (out) and facies (face).
