Etymology:
From Late Latin *dyscrasia, from Ancient Greek δυσκρασία (dyskrasía, “bad temperament, intemperateness”), from δυσ- (dys-, “bad, difficult”) + κρᾶσις (krâsis, “mixture, blending”).
