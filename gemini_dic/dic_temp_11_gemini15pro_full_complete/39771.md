consoling:/kənˈsoʊlɪŋ/
adjective
安慰的，抚慰的
reducing or trying to reduce someone's grief or disappointment
例句：She offered consoling words to her grieving friend. 她向悲伤的朋友 offering 安慰的话。

例句：The consoling hug from her mother made her feel better.  来自母亲安慰的拥抱让她感觉好些了。

近义词：comforting, soothing, reassuring
反义词：distressing, upsetting, disheartening
词性: adjective
