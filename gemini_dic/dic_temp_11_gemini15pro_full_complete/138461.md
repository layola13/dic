novelesque:/ˌnɒvəˈlɛsk/ | adj. | 小说般的，像小说的 |  Resembling or characteristic of a novel, especially in being romantic, improbable, or exciting. |  Her life was so novelesque, full of dramatic twists and turns. 她的人生充满了戏剧性的波折，像小说一样。|近义词：fictional, romantic, dramatic, improbable, fantastic |反义词：realistic, mundane, ordinary |词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *nouvelesque*, from *nouvelle* ("novella") + *-esque* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "in the style of, resembling"). Ultimately from Latin *novellus* ("new, young").
