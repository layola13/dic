gallinule:/ˈɡælɪˌnjuːl/ | n. | 秧鸡；水鸡 | A small water bird with a short bill, brightly colored plumage, and long toes, related to rails and coots. |  例句：The gallinule darted across the lily pads. 水鸡飞快地穿过睡莲叶子。

例句：We observed a purple gallinule foraging in the marsh. 我们观察到一只紫水鸡在沼泽地里觅食。

近义词：moorhen, coot, rail | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gallinula, diminutive of gallina "hen," from Latin gallus "rooster."  So literally, "little hen."
