limestone:/ˈlaɪmˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈlaɪmˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|石灰石，石灰岩|A sedimentary rock consisting mainly of calcium carbonate, CaCO3, mostly formed from the remains of marine organisms.|The White Cliffs of Dover are made of limestone. 多佛白崖是由石灰岩构成的。|Near synonyms: calcite, chalk, marble|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of rock)|Noun
