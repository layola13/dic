detains:/dɪˈteɪnz/
verb
拘留，扣留
keeps someone in official custody, typically for questioning about a crime or in politically sensitive situations.
The police detained him for questioning. 警察把他拘留问话。
Synonyms: hold, confine, imprison, arrest
Antonyms: release, free, liberate
词性: 动词

detain:/dɪˈteɪnz/
verb
耽搁，使迟延
delay someone from proceeding
I was detained by a phone call. 一个电话耽搁了我。
Synonyms: delay, hinder, impede
Antonyms: expedite, hasten, accelerate
词性: 动词
