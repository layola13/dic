cannicula:/kəˈnɪkjʊlə/ | n. | 天狼星; 犬日; 三伏天 |The period of greatest heat, typically around the time of the heliacal rising of Sirius (the Dog Star).  |  The canicula is often the hottest part of summer.  三伏天通常是一年中最热的时候。 |  The farmers struggled to work during the canicula. 农民们在酷暑中艰难地工作。 | synonyms: dog days, midsummer, heatwave | antonyms: winter, cold snap | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin canicula ("little dog," diminutive of canis, "dog"), referring to Sirius, the Dog Star.  The hottest days of summer coincide with the period when Sirius rises and sets with the sun.
