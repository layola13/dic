drizzly:/ˈdrɪzli/ | adj. | 毛毛雨的，细雨蒙蒙的 | Characterized by light rain; raining lightly. | It was a cold and drizzly morning. 那是一个寒冷又下着毛毛雨的早晨。 | The drizzly weather made the football game unpleasant to watch. 毛毛雨的天气使得观看足球比赛很不愉快。 | 近义词: rainy, showery, misty | 反义词: sunny, dry, clear | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From drizzle + -y.  Drizzle itself comes from Middle English *driselen, frequentative of Middle English drislen, from Old English *drisian "to fall in fine drops."
