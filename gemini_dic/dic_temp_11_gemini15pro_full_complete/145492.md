overclocked:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːkt/ /ˌəʊvəˈklɒkt/| adj.|超频的|Increased in operating speed beyond the manufacturer's specifications.  A component, such as a CPU or GPU, is said to be overclocked when it is forced to run at a higher clock speed than intended by the manufacturer.|I overclocked my CPU to get better gaming performance. 我超频了我的CPU以获得更好的游戏性能。|Near Synonyms: boosted, supercharged, enhanced|Antonyms: underclocked, throttled|adjective

overclock:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːk/ /ˌəʊvəˈklɒkt/| vt.|超频|To increase the operating speed of a component, such as a CPU or GPU, beyond the manufacturer's specifications.|He overclocked his graphics card for better frame rates. 他超频了他的显卡以获得更好的帧率。|Near Synonyms: boost, supercharge, enhance|Antonyms: underclock, throttle|verb
