contemporaneity:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪnɪti/ | n. | 同时代性；同时代；同时期 | the quality of existing or happening at the same time |  The exhibition explores the contemporaneity of these two artists.  这个展览探讨了这两位艺术家的时代性。| synonyms:  simultaneity, synchronicity, coexistence | antonyms: anachronism, non-contemporaneity | noun

contemporaneous:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ | adj. |  同时代的；同时期的 | existing or occurring in the same period of time | The two writers were contemporaneous, though they never met. 这两位作家是同时代的，尽管他们从未见过面。 | synonyms:  coeval, concurrent, synchronous, simultaneous | antonyms: non-contemporaneous, asynchronous | adjective
