belemnite:/ˈbɛləmˌnaɪt/| n. |箭石|an extinct cephalopod mollusk of the order Belemnoidea that existed from the Triassic to the Cretaceous periods, having a cigar-shaped internal shell.  The fossilized shells are common and are typically shaped like a bullet or dart.|The museum displayed a collection of belemnites from the Jurassic period.  博物馆展出了一批侏罗纪时期的箭石化石。|Synonyms:  None (specific to the extinct organism)|Antonyms:  None|noun

belemnite:/ˈbɛləmˌnaɪt/| n. |箭石的内壳|the fossilized internal shell of a belemnite.|He found a perfect belemnite while walking on the beach. 他在海滩散步时发现了一个完整的箭石内壳。|Synonyms:  None (specific to the fossilized shell)|Antonyms:  None|noun
