apode:/ˈæpoʊd/| n. |无足动物；无肢动物|An animal without feet or limbs, like a snake, caecilian, or some lizards. |例句：The snake, an apode, slithered across the grass. 蛇，一种无足动物，滑过草地。

例句：Some lizards are apodes, having lost their limbs over evolutionary time.  一些蜥蜴是无足动物，在进化过程中失去了它们的四肢。|近义词：limbless animal|反义词:tetrapod|词性:noun
