coraly:/ˈkɔːrəli/ | adj. | 珊瑚色的；珊瑚红的 | Of a coral color or resembling coral. |  The dress shimmered with a coraly hue. 这条裙子闪着珊瑚色。| 近义词: coral, coral-colored, coral-red | 反义词: (No direct opposite, consider colors like blue, green) | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "coral" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Coral" comes from Old French "coral," ultimately from Latin "coralium."
