amoebous:/əˈmiːbəs/| adj.|变形虫似的；不定形的|Resembling an amoeba in character or form, especially in being shapeless or able to change shape freely.|The company's structure is somewhat amoebous, constantly adapting to market demands.|近义词：amorphous, shapeless, formless, protean|反义词：rigid, fixed, structured|词性:形容词
词源: From Modern Latin *amoeba* + -ous. Ultimately from Greek *amoibḗ* (change).
