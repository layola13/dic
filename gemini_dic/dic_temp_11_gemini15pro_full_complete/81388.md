graspingnesses:/ˈɡræspɪŋnɪsɪz/
| n. |贪婪，贪得无厌|The graspingnesses of the company's CEO led to his downfall. 该公司首席执行官的贪婪导致了他的垮台。|A state of being excessively desirous of acquiring wealth or possessions; greed.  An instance of this. |His graspingnesses were evident in his relentless pursuit of wealth. 他的贪婪在他对财富的无情追求中显而易见。|近义词：greed, avarice, acquisitiveness, covetousness|反义词：generosity, altruism|词性:名词

Etymology:  From grasping + -ness.  "Grasping" itself comes from the verb "grasp," which has roots in Old Norse *graspa.
