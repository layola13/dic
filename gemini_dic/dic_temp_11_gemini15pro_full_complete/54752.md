dispassionate:/dɪsˈpæʃənət/
adjective
冷静的；不带感情的；客观的
Not influenced by strong emotion, and so able to be rational and impartial.
The judge gave a dispassionate summing up of the case.  法官对案件做了冷静的总结。

She delivered a dispassionate analysis of the situation. 她对局势进行了客观的分析。

近义词: impartial, objective, unbiased, unemotional, detached, calm, cool, collected
反义词: passionate, emotional, biased, prejudiced, subjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + passionate.  来自dis-（表示否定）+ passionate（充满激情的）。
