intransitivities:/ˌɪntrænsɪˈtɪvɪtiz/| n. |不及物性；不及物动词|The property of a verb that does not take a direct object.  It describes actions that are not done *to* something or someone. |The verb "sleep" demonstrates intransitivity; one cannot "sleep something".  睡觉这个动词展现了不及物性；你不能“睡某物”。|近义词：intransitiveness|反义词:transitivities|词性:noun
