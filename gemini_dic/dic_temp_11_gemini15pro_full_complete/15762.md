baldy:/ˈbɔːldi/| n.|秃头，秃子|A baldy is a person who is bald or has very little hair.  秃头是指秃顶或头发很少的人。|例句：He's a bit of a baldy these days. 他最近有点秃了。

例句：Look at that baldy over there! 看看那边那个秃子！|近义词：baldhead, chrome dome|反义词: hairy person|词性:noun
