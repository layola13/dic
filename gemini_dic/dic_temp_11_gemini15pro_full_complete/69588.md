felting:/ˈfɛltɪŋ/| n.|毡化；制毡；毛毡|The process of matting, condensing, and pressing fibers together to create a non-woven fabric.  The felting process involves moisture, heat, and agitation, causing the fibers to interlock and form a dense material.|She enjoys felting wool into hats and scarves. 她喜欢把羊毛毡化成帽子和围巾。|Synonyms:  matting, fulling|Antonyms:  separating, unraveling|noun

felting:/ˈfɛltɪŋ/| adj.|毡化的；用于毡化的|Made of felt or relating to the process of felting.  Felting needles are special barbed needles used to create felt. |She bought some felting wool for her next project. 她为她的下一个项目买了一些用于毡化的羊毛。|Synonyms:  felted|Antonyms:  woven, knitted|adjective
