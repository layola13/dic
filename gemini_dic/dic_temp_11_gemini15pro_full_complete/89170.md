homeopathic:/ˌhoʊmiəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|顺势疗法的|Of or relating to homeopathy; a system of treating disease by minute doses of natural substances that in a healthy person would produce symptoms of disease.|例句：She opted for a homeopathic remedy for her allergies. 她选择了顺势疗法来治疗她的过敏。

例句：Homeopathic medicine is based on the principle of "like cures like." 顺势疗法基于“相似相治”的原则。|近义词：alternative, naturopathic|反义词：conventional, allopathic|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Modern Latin *homeopathicus*, from Greek *homoios* "similar" + *pathos* "suffering, disease."  Coined by Samuel Hahnemann, the founder of homeopathy, in the early 19th century.
