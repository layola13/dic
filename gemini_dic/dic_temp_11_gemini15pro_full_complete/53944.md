discoverably:/dɪˈskʌvərəbli/
adv.
可发现地，明显地
in a way that can be discovered or noticed
The flaws in the system were discoverably by careful inspection.  这个系统的缺陷通过仔细检查是可以发现的。
Her disappointment was discoverably in her downcast eyes. 她的失望从她低垂的双眼中可以明显地看出。
近义词: noticeably, perceptibly, detectably, observably
反义词: imperceptibly, undetectably, invisibly
词性: 副词

词源:
From discoverable + -ly. Discoverable is from discover + -able. Discover comes from Old French descovrir, from des- "un-, dis-" + covrir "to cover."
