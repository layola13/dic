hyper-romantic:/ˌhaɪpər roʊˈmæn.tɪk/ | adj. | 过度浪漫的，极度浪漫的 | Excessively or unrealistically romantic; characterized by an idealized or exaggerated view of love and relationships. | She has a hyper-romantic view of love, expecting grand gestures and constant affection. 她对爱情有着过度浪漫的看法，期待着盛大的姿态和不断的爱慕。 |  He wrote her hyper-romantic poems filled with flowery language and unrealistic promises. 他给她写了充满华丽辞藻和不切实际承诺的过度浪漫的诗歌。 | 近义词: excessively romantic, overly sentimental, starry-eyed | 反义词: cynical, pragmatic, realistic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek "hyper" meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
romantic: from Late Latin "romanicus" meaning "of or pertaining to the Roman language or people," later evolving to mean "characteristic of or suggestive of romance or chivalry"
