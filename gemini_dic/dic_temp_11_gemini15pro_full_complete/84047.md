half-fictitiously:/ˌhæf fɪkˈtɪʃəsli/
| adv. |
| 半虚构地 |
| Partly fictitious; based partly on fact and partly on imagination. |
| The story was half-fictitiously based on her own life experiences.  这个故事半虚构地基于她自己的生活经历。|
| The biography was half-fictitiously written, blending truth with embellishment. 这部传记是半虚构的，将事实与润色融合在一起。|
| semi-fictional, partly invented |
| factual, real, true |
| adverb |
