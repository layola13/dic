nonadmirer:/ˌnɒnədˈmaɪərər/ (US) /ˌnɒnədˈmaɪərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |非仰慕者，不喜欢的人|A person who does not admire someone or something.|He's a nonadmirer of the current political regime. 他对现行的政治体制不感冒。|近义词：critic, detractor, opponent|反义词: admirer, fan, supporter|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- + admirer
* non-:  a prefix meaning "not," from Latin non "not"
* admirer: from French admirer, from Latin admirari "to wonder at," from ad- "to" + mirari "to wonder"
