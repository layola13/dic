hokum:/ˈhoʊkəm/| n.|胡说八道；废话；老套的噱头；陈词滥调|nonsense; insincere or sentimental talk or writing, especially in a play or film.|The plot of the movie was pure hokum, but the special effects were impressive. 这部电影的情节完全是胡说八道，但特效令人印象深刻。|近义词：nonsense, claptrap, drivel, twaddle, baloney|反义词：truth, fact|词性:名词

hokum:/ˈhoʊkəm/| adj. (informal, less common)|老套的；陈腐的|corny; clichéd|The dialogue was hokum and predictable. 对话老套且可预测。|近义词：corny, clichéd, hackneyed|反义词：original, fresh|词性:形容词
