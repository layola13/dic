finalization:/ˌfaɪnələˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|最终确定；定稿；完成|The finalization of the contract took several weeks.  合同的最终确定花了几个星期。|例句：The finalization of the agreement is expected next week. 协议预计下周完成。|近义词：completion, conclusion, settlement|反义词：inception, beginning, commencement|词性: 名词

Etymology: From finalize + -ation.  Finalize comes from final + -ize.  Final traces back to Latin *finālis, from finis "end, boundary."
