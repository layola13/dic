illutation: /ˌɪljuːˈteɪʃən/
noun
阐明；说明；例证
an explanation or example that makes something clear
The professor used several illutations to explain the complex theory.  教授用几个例证来解释这个复杂的理论。
近义词: illustration, explanation, clarification, elucidation
反义词: obfuscation, confusion

Etymology:
From Late Latin *illutatio, from Latin illutare (“to mock, play upon”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + ludere (“to play”)
