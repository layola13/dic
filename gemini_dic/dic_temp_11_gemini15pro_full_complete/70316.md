fief:/fiːf/
noun
封地；采邑
An estate of land, especially one held on condition of feudal service.
The knight received a fief from the king. 骑士从国王那里得到一块封地。
synonyms: estate, manor, domain, barony, territory
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical piece of land; perhaps conceptually: common land, public land)
n.
