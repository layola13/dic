outyielding:/ˌaʊtˈjiːldiŋ/
adjective
产量更高的，超过...产量的
Producing a greater yield than something else.
This new variety of wheat is outyielding all other strains. 这种新的小麦品种比所有其他品种的产量都高。
Synonyms: more productive, higher-yielding
Antonyms: less productive, lower-yielding
词性: 形容词
