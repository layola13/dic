aspidistral: /ˌæspɪˈdɪstrəl/
adjective
盾形的; 像盾的; (指植物的叶子)单侧的, 仅向一侧生长的
(of a plant, especially its leaves) developing on one side only.  Relating to or resembling an aspidistra.
The fern exhibited aspidistral growth, its fronds unfurling only towards the light source.  这种蕨类植物呈现出盾形的生长方式，它的叶子只朝着光源展开。
The aspidistral arrangement of the leaves gave the plant a distinctive, fan-like appearance.  叶子的盾形排列使这种植物具有独特的扇形外观。
近义词: unilateral, one-sided
反义词: bilateral, two-sided
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *ἀσπιδιστρά (aspidistra), from ἀσπίς (aspis, “shield”) + -ιστρά (-istra, a feminine suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
