bridged:/brɪdʒd/| adj. v. |桥接的，架桥的|Past participle of "bridge", meaning to build a bridge over something or to connect two things. |They bridged the gap between the two buildings with a walkway. 他们在两栋楼之间建了一条走廊。|近义词：connected, linked, spanned, joined|反义词:separated, divided, disconnected|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *brycgian, from brycg "bridge."
