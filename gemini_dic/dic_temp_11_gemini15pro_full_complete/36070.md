coefficacy:/ˌkoʊˈɛfɪkəsi/
n.
共同功效; 协同效应
The combined power of two or more agents, substances, or organisms to produce a greater effect than the sum of their individual effects.
The coefficacy of the two drugs resulted in a significant improvement in patient outcomes. 两种药物的协同作用使患者的预后得到显著改善。
synergy, combined effect, joint action
antagonism, counteraction, opposition
noun
