noncrediblenesses:/ˌnɑːnˌkrɛdəˈbɪlɪtɪz/
n.
不可信性；难以置信之处
the state or quality of being not credible; things that are not credible
His constant lies led to a perception of noncredibleness. 他不断的谎言导致人们认为他不可信。

The numerous noncrediblenesses in his story made it difficult to believe. 他故事中许多难以置信的地方让人难以相信。
近义词: incredibility, unbelievability, implausibility
反义词: credibility, believability, plausibility
词性: noun (plural)
