dissent:/dɪˈsɛnt/ | n. | 异议，不同意 | Disagreement with the majority opinion. |  Two justices dissented from the majority opinion. 两位法官对多数意见表示异议。

dissent:/dɪˈsɛnt/ | vi. | 持不同意见，表示异议 | To disagree with the majority opinion. |  Several members dissented from the party line. 几位成员对党的路线表示异议。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dissentir*, from Latin dissentire ("to disagree"), from dis- ("apart, away") + sentire ("to feel, think").

SYNONYMS->
disagreement, objection, opposition, protest, defiance

ANTONYMS->
agreement, consent, acceptance, approval, concurrence
