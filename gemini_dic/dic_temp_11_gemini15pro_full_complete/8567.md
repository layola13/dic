anticipative:/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪv/
adjective
预期的；预先的；抢先的
Characterized by anticipation; expecting something to happen or preparing for something ahead of time.
She gave an anticipative smile, clearly excited about the upcoming trip. 她露出了期待的微笑，显然对即将到来的旅行感到兴奋。
The company adopted an anticipative strategy to stay ahead of the competition.  该公司采取了预期策略以保持领先于竞争对手。
近义词:expectant, prospective, anticipatory
反义词:retrospective, reactive
词性:adjective
