izvestia:/ɪzˈvɛstɪjə/ | n. | 消息，新闻，(尤指)官方新闻  |  News or information, especially official news.  Derived from the Russian word "Известия" (Izvestiya), meaning "news" or "reports".  It originally referred specifically to the official newspaper of the Soviet government. | The Izvestia reported on the latest developments in the peace talks. 伊兹维斯蒂亚报报道了和平谈判的最新进展。 | The Izvestia published an article about the economic reforms. 伊兹维斯蒂亚报发表了一篇关于经济改革的文章。 |  synonyms: news, report, bulletin, announcement, dispatch | antonyms:  silence, secrecy | noun
