apoplectoid:/ˌæpəˈplɛktɔɪd/
adjective
类中风的, 似中风的
Resembling apoplexy; exhibiting symptoms suggestive of apoplexy.  Relating to or resembling apoplexy, a condition of sudden loss of consciousness or feeling and voluntary movement caused by hemorrhage into the brain.
His face turned an apoplectoid red. 他的脸涨得通红，像是要中风一样。
She went into an apoplectoid rage when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后勃然大怒，像是要中风一样。
near synonyms: apoplectic, stroke-like
antonyms: healthy, asymptomatic
词性: adjective
