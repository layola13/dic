hasht:/hæʃt/| n.|哈希值，散列值|A hash is a fixed-size string of characters generated from arbitrary data using a mathematical function. It acts as a digital fingerprint or unique identifier for the original data. Hashes are commonly used in cryptography and data integrity verification.|The password is stored as a hash, not in plain text. 密码存储为哈希值，而不是明文。|synonyms: hash value, checksum, digest|antonyms: N/A|noun
