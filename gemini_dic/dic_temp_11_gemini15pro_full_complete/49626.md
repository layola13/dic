demigod:/ˈdemɪɡɒd/ (UK) /ˈdemɪɡɑːd/ (US)| n. |半神，神之子|A being who is partly divine and partly human, often the offspring of a god and a mortal.|Heracles, son of Zeus and a mortal woman, is a famous demigod in Greek mythology.  赫拉克勒斯，宙斯和凡人女子之子，是希腊神话中著名的半神。|Synonyms: semigod, divinity, deity|Antonyms: mortal, human|Noun

demigod:/ˈdemɪɡɒd/ (UK) /ˈdemɪɡɑːd/ (US)| n. |神化的人物|A person who is greatly admired or revered, as if they were a god.|The rock star was treated like a demigod by his fans.  这位摇滚明星被他的粉丝们奉若神明。|Synonyms: idol, icon, hero|Antonyms: nobody, commoner|Noun
