noncertainties:/nɒnˈsɜːrtntiz/
noun
不确定性；不确定因素
lack of certainty; things that are not definite or sure.
The project faces numerous noncertainties due to the volatile market.  由于市场动荡，该项目面临诸多不确定性。
synonyms: uncertainties, doubts, ambiguities, indeterminacies
antonyms: certainties, assurances, guarantees
n.
