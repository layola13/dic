agenting:/ˈeɪdʒəntɪŋ/
n.
代理；经纪；代理业务
the business of representing actors, musicians, writers, etc.
Example: He's involved in the agenting of young actors. 他从事年轻演员的经纪业务。
Example:  She made a fortune in property agenting. 她通过房地产经纪赚了一大笔钱。
近义词: representation, agency, brokering
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: client work, self-representation)
词性: noun
