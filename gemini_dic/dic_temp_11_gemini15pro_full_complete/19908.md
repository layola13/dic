binuclear:/baɪˈnjuːkliər/ /baɪˈnuːkliər/
adjective
双核的；二核的
Having two nuclei.  Relating to or containing two nuclei, especially two atomic nuclei.
例句：
The binuclear complex plays a crucial role in catalysis.  双核络合物在催化中起着至关重要的作用。

Scientists have synthesized a new binuclear iron compound. 科学家们合成了一种新的双核铁化合物。

近义词：dinuclear
反义词：mononuclear (单核的)
词性：adjective
