n.|哥特式风格，哥特式特征|The gothicity of the architecture was striking.  这栋建筑的哥特式风格令人印象深刻。|
例句：The film captured the gothicity of the novel perfectly. 这部电影完美地捕捉到了小说的哥特式风格。
例句：She loved the gothicity of the old cathedral. 她喜欢那座古老教堂的哥特式风格。
近义词：Gothicism, Gothic character, Gothic style
反义词：Modernism, Classicism
词性:noun


Etymology: From Gothic + -ity.  "Gothic" ultimately derives from the East Germanic tribe of the Goths.  The term was initially used pejoratively to describe medieval art and architecture considered "barbaric" by Renaissance thinkers.  The suffix "-ity" forms abstract nouns denoting a state or quality.
