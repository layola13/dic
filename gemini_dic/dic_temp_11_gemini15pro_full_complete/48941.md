defund:/diːˈfʌnd/ | vt. | 撤资，停止拨款 | To deprive of funds, especially as a means of protest or punishment. |  The city council voted to defund the police department. 市议会投票决定停止对警察部门的拨款。

The activist group is campaigning to defund fossil fuel companies.  这个激进组织正在开展运动，呼吁停止对化石燃料公司的投资。| 近义词：divest, deprive | 反义词：fund, finance | 词性: 动词

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + fund (verb meaning "to provide with money")  来源于前缀"de-"（意为“移除”或“反转”）和动词"fund"（意为“提供资金”）。
