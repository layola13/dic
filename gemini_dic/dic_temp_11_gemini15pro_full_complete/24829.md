brown-bagger:/ˈbraʊnˌbæɡər/
n.
自带午餐者；自带午餐的上班族
A person who brings their own lunch, typically in a brown paper bag, to work or school, often to save money.
He's a brown-bagger – he always brings his own lunch. 他是一个自带午餐的人——他总是自带午餐。
She's a brown-bagger to save money. 她自带午餐是为了省钱。
近义词:lunch-packer
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider someone who always eats out)
词性: noun
