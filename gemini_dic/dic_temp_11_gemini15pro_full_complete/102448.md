jackfruits:/ˈdʒækˌfruːts/ | n. | 菠萝蜜 |Large, tropical fruit with a spiky exterior and sweet, fleshy interior. |  Jackfruits are becoming increasingly popular in Western cuisine. 菠萝蜜在西方菜肴中越来越受欢迎。

Jackfruits can weigh up to 80 pounds. 菠萝蜜的重量可达80磅。 | 近义词:  (in terms of texture) durian, breadfruit | 反义词:  (Generally no direct opposite for a fruit) | 词性: noun
