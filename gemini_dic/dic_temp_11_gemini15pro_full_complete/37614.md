communing:/kəˈmjuːnɪŋ/
| n. |交流，交感，亲近|The act of sharing one's thoughts or feelings with another person or with a higher power, especially in a spiritual or intimate way.|She found solace in communing with nature. 她在大自然中找到了慰藉。|近义词：connecting, interacting, conversing, meditating|反义词：isolating, withdrawing|词性:Noun (Gerund or present participle of 'commune')

commune:/kəˈmjuːn/
| v. |交流，交感，亲近|To share one's thoughts or feelings with another person or with a higher power, especially in a spiritual or intimate way.|He communed with nature, finding peace in the solitude. 他与自然交流，在孤独中找到了平静。|近义词：connect, interact, converse, meditate|反义词：isolate, withdraw|词性:Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French comuner "to share," from Latin communicare "to share, communicate," from communis "common."
