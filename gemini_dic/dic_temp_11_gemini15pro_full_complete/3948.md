agroforesters:/ˌæɡroʊˈfɔːrɪstərz/
n.
农林复合经营者，农林复合工作者
People who practice agroforestry, integrating trees and shrubs into crop and/or livestock farming systems.
Agroforesters are working to develop sustainable farming practices that benefit both the environment and local communities. 农林复合经营者正在努力开发既有利于环境又有利于当地社区的可持续农业实践。
Agroforesters play a crucial role in combating deforestation and promoting biodiversity. 农林复合经营者在对抗森林砍伐和促进生物多样性方面发挥着至关重要的作用。
Synonyms:  agroforestry practitioners, forest farmers
Antonyms:  conventional farmers, monoculture farmers
