flashinesses:/ˈflæʃɪnɪsɪz/
noun
炫耀，浮华，俗丽
The state or quality of being flashy; ostentatious display.
Her flashinesses were out of place at the academic conference. 她的炫耀在学术会议上显得格格不入。
ostentation, showiness, gaudiness
subtleness, modesty, understatement
