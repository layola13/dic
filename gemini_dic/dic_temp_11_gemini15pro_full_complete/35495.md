coadapted:/ˌkoʊəˈdæptɪd/
adjective
共同适应的
(of two or more species) having adapted to each other through reciprocal evolutionary change.
例句：The ants and the acacia tree are coadapted, each benefiting from the other's presence. 蚂蚁和金合欢树共同适应，彼此都受益于对方的存在。

例句：Coadapted species often exhibit intricate symbiotic relationships. 共同适应的物种通常表现出复杂的共生关系。

近义词：coevolved, mutually adapted
反义词：maladapted, unadapted
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + adapted.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together" or "jointly." "Adapted" comes from the Latin "adaptare," meaning "to fit."
