goering:/ˈɡɜːrɪŋ/ | n. |戈林| Hermann Wilhelm Göring (1893-1946), a German political and military leader and a prominent member of the Nazi Party. He played a key role in the Nazi rise to power and held several important positions during World War II. | Hitler appointed Goering as commander-in-chief of the Luftwaffe. 希特勒任命戈林为空军的总司令。|Synonym: Nazi leader |Antonym:  anti-Nazi |noun
