equivocatory:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtəri/
adjective
含糊其辞的，模棱两可的，故意误导的
Using ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.
例句：
His equivocatory response left me wondering what he really meant.
他含糊其辞的回答让我怀疑他真正的意思。

例句：
The politician gave an equivocatory answer to the journalist's question.
这位政治家对记者的提问给出了一个模棱两可的答案。

近义词：ambiguous, evasive, noncommittal, vague
反义词：clear, direct, straightforward, unambiguous
词性：adjective


词源:
From Late Latin aequivocator, from aequivocat- ‘used words ambiguously’, from the verb aequivocare, from aequivocus ‘having a double meaning’ (see equivocal).
