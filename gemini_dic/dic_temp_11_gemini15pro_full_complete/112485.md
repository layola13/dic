lounge: /laʊndʒ/ | vi. | 闲逛，懒散地躺卧 | to spend time relaxing or idly | He lounged on the sofa all afternoon. 他整个下午都懒洋洋地躺在沙发上。| synonyms: relax, loll, loaf | antonyms: work, hustle | verb
