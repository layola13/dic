ileocolic:/ˌɪliəˈkɒlɪk/| adj.|回盲部的，回结肠的|Relating to the ileum (the final section of the small intestine) and the colon (the large intestine). It specifically refers to the valve or area where these two parts of the digestive system join.|The ileocolic valve prevents backflow of contents from the colon into the small intestine.|Synonyms: ileocecal|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
