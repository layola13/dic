horizontally:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntəli/ /ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntəli/ | adv. | 水平地；横向地 | In a horizontal direction; from side to side. | The lines run horizontally across the page.  这些线条水平地穿过页面。 |  The picture was hung horizontally. 这幅画是横着挂的。| synonyms: sideways, across, lengthwise | antonyms: vertically, upright, perpendicular | adverb

horizontally:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntəli/ /ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntəli/ | adj. | 水平的; 横向的 |  Positioned from side to side rather than up and down. | This is a horizontally scrolling website. 这是一个水平滚动的网站。 | The company has a horizontally integrated structure. 该公司拥有水平整合的结构。 | synonyms: level, flat, prone | antonyms: vertical, upright, perpendicular | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *horizontalis*, from Latin horizon (“boundary, horizon”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek ὁρίζων (horízōn, “bounding, defining”).
