lapser:/ˈlæpsər/| n.| 失效者；背教者；堕落者；犯错者|Someone who lapses, especially in their religious faith or moral standards, or allows something such as a subscription or agreement to lapse.|He was a lapsed Catholic who hadn't been to church in years. 他是一个背教的天主教徒，多年没去过教堂了。|近义词：backslider, defaulter, offender|反义词：believer, adherent|词性: noun


Etymology: From the verb "lapse", from Latin *lapsus*, past participle of *lābī* "to slip, glide, fall".
