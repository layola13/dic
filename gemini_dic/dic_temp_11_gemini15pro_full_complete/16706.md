barring out: /ˈbɑːrɪŋ aʊt/ | n. |关学生在外; 把…关在外面（尤指学生将老师关在教室外以示抗议）| The act of students locking their teachers out of the school building, typically as a form of protest. |  The pupils staged a barring out to protest the school's new uniform policy. 学生们举行了一次关老师在外的活动，抗议学校的新校服政策。| 近义词: rebellion, protest, revolt | 反义词: obedience, compliance | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "to bar" (meaning to obstruct or prevent entry) and the adverb "out" (meaning outside or away).
