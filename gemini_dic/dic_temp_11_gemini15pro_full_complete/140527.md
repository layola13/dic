oleic:/oʊˈliːɪk/ | adj. | 油的；油酸的 | Relating to, derived from, or containing oleic acid. |  Oleic acid is a common unsaturated fatty acid. 油酸是一种常见的单元不饱和脂肪酸。|  Olive oil is rich in oleic acid. 橄榄油富含油酸。| synonyms: unsaturated, fatty | antonyms: saturated | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *oléique*, from Latin *oleum* ("oil") + *-icus*. Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἔλαιον (élaion, “olive oil”)*.
