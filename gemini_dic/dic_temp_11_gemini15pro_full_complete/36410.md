cojuror:/kəˈdʒʊərər/ | n. | 共谋者；帮凶；同谋 |  A person who swears jointly with another; a joint party in an oath or affirmation.  Someone who assists in a plot or scheme, especially a dishonest one. | The cojuror helped the magician with the disappearing act.  那个帮凶协助魔术师完成了消失的表演。| synonyms: accomplice, collaborator, confederate, assistant | antonyms: opponent, adversary, enemy | noun
