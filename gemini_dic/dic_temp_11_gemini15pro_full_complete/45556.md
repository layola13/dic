curledly:/ˈkɜːrlɪdli/| adv.|卷曲地，蜷曲地|Having curls or being shaped into curls.  Characterized by curls or a curling shape.|Her hair hung curledly around her shoulders. 她的头发卷曲地垂在肩上。|近义词：curly, coiling, spirally,  waving|反义词:straight, uncurled|词性:副词
Etymology: From curl + -ed + -ly.  "Curl" originates from Middle Dutch "crul," meaning "to curl or crisp."  The "-ed" suffix transforms the verb into an adjective (curled), and the "-ly" suffix further transforms the adjective into an adverb (curledly).
