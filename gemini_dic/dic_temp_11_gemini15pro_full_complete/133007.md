nonentailed:/ˌnɒnɪnˈteɪld/
adjective
非蕴涵的
Not logically entailed or necessarily following from something else.  A conclusion is nonentailed if it does not follow logically from the premises.
The committee's decision was nonentailed by the evidence presented. 委员会的决定并非由提出的证据所蕴涵。
His outburst was completely nonentailed by the conversation. 他的爆发与之前的对话完全没有逻辑关系。
近义词:unrelated, disconnected, illogical
反义词:entailed, implied, consequent
词性:adjective
