non-movable:/ˌnɒnˈmuːvəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈmuːvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|不可移动的，固定的|Not able to be moved; fixed in place.|例句：The antique desk was non-movable due to its size and weight. 这张古董桌子由于其尺寸和重量而不可移动。|例句：Non-movable hospital equipment includes items like surgical lights and operating tables.  不可移动的医院设备包括手术灯和手术台等物品。|近义词：fixed, immobile, permanent, stationary|反义词：movable, mobile, portable|词性:形容词

non-movable:/ˌnɒnˈmuːvəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈmuːvəbəl/ (UK)| n.|不动产|Property that cannot be moved, such as land or buildings. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.)|例句：The non-movables included the land and the house built upon it.  不动产包括土地和建在上面的房子。|例句：They considered the factory a non-movable asset. 他们将工厂视为不动产。|近义词：real estate, property, fixtures|反义词：movables, chattels|词性:名词
