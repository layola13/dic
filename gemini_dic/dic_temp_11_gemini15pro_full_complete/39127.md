confusably:/kənˈfjuːzəbli/ | adv. | 容易混淆地，令人困惑地 | In a way that is easy to confuse with something else. | The twins are so alike they are confusably similar.  这对双胞胎长得太像了，很容易混淆。 | 近义词: misleadingly, ambiguously | 反义词: clearly, distinctly | 词性: 副词

词源: From confuse (v.) + -able (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Latin confundere "to pour together, mix up."
