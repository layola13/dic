mis-parsed:/ˌmɪsˈpɑːrst/ | adj. | 错误解析的 | Incorrectly analyzed or interpreted, typically referring to data or language by a computer program. | The sentence was mis-parsed by the software, leading to a nonsensical translation.  这句子被软件错误地解析了，导致翻译毫无意义。| The algorithm mis-parsed the user's query and returned irrelevant results.  算法错误解析了用户的查询，返回了不相关的结果。 | 近义词: misinterpreted, misconstrued, misread | 反义词: correctly parsed, accurately interpreted | 词性: adjective
