ascidium:/əˈsɪdiəm/ | n. | 海鞘；【植】囊状体；|A small sac-like or pitcher-shaped structure, especially in a plant, such as the pouch forming the trap of a pitcher plant, or in certain tunicates. It can also refer to a single zooid of a colonial ascidian.| The ascidium of the pitcher plant is filled with digestive fluids.  猪笼草的囊状体充满了消化液。|Synonym: pitcher, bladder, utricle | Antonym: N/A | Noun

ascidia:/əˈsɪdiə/ | n. | 海鞘（复数）| Plural of ascidium, referring to multiple sac-like or pitcher-shaped structures or to colonial ascidians.| The ascidia of these tunicates are brightly colored. 这些海鞘的囊状体颜色鲜艳。| Synonym: pitchers, bladders, utricles| Antonym: N/A | Noun
