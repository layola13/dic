nonreversions:/ˌnɒnriˈvɜːʃənz/ | n. |非回复，非逆转，不恢复原状|The lack of nonreversions suggests the mutation is stable.|  After the treatment, nonreversions were observed in the majority of the patients, indicating its effectiveness. | 近义词:  non-returns,  continuations | 反义词: reversions, returns, relapses | noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
reversion (noun): From Late Latin *reversionem (nominative reversio) "a turning back," from past participle stem of Latin revertere "to turn back," from re- "back" + vertere "to turn."
