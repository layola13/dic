noninterpretability:/ˌnɒnɪnˌtɜːprɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可解释性，无法理解性 | The noninterpretability of the model's output is a major drawback.  This refers to the inability to understand how a model arrives at its predictions or decisions. | The black box nature of some AI algorithms leads to noninterpretability, making it difficult to trust their outputs.  人工智能的某些算法的黑盒性质导致了不可解释性，使得人们难以信任其输出结果。 |  near synonyms: inexplicability, opacity, unintelligibility | near antonyms: interpretability, understandability, clarity | noun

noninterpretable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːprɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可解释的，无法理解的 |  The results of the experiment were noninterpretable due to the flawed methodology. This means the results couldn't be understood or explained meaningfully. | The complex equation yielded a noninterpretable result.  这个复杂的方程式得出了一个无法解释的结果。 | near synonyms: inexplicable, opaque, unintelligible | near antonyms: interpretable, understandable, clear | adjective
