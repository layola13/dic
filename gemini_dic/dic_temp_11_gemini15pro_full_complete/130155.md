non-representative:/ˌnɒn reprɪˈzentətɪv/ (US) /ˌnɑːn reprɪˈzentətɪv/| adj.|非代表性的，不典型的|Not typical of a particular group or thing; not adequately representing something.|例句：The survey sample was non-representative of the population as a whole.  这项调查样本不具有代表性，不能代表整个人口。

例句：This small group is non-representative of the student body. 这个小组不代表全体学生。|近义词：atypical, uncharacteristic, untypical|反义词: representative, typical|词性:形容词
