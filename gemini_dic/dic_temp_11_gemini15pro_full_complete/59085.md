dyadically:/daɪˈædɪkli/ | adv. | 二元地，成对地 |  In a manner relating to, consisting of, or involving two elements or parts; in pairs.  |  The two companies collaborated dyadically on the project, sharing resources and expertise equally.  两家公司在这个项目上进行了二元合作，平等地共享资源和专业知识。

|  The dancers moved dyadically, mirroring each other's movements across the stage. 舞者们成对地移动，在舞台上互相呼应对方的动作。| 近义词:  pairwise, in twos | 反义词: individually, singly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From Greek *dyas* ("two") + *-ikos* (adjective-forming suffix) + *-ly* (adverb-forming suffix).
