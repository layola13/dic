nonpersistencies:/ˌnɒnpəˈsɪstənsiz/ | n. | 非持久性；非持续性 | Lack of persistence; the state of not continuing to exist or occur. | The nonpersistencies of the software glitches made it difficult to track the problem.  软件故障的非持久性使得难以追踪问题。 |  The frequent nonpersistencies in the network connection frustrated users. 网络连接频繁的非持久性让用户感到沮丧。 | Synonyms: transience, ephemerality, instability | Antonyms: persistence, permanence, stability | noun

nonpersistency:/ˌnɒnpəˈsɪstənsi/ | n. | 非持久性；非持续性 | Lack of persistence; the state of not continuing to exist or occur. | The nonpersistency of the data made it unreliable for long-term analysis. 数据的非持久性使其对于长期分析来说不可靠。 | Due to the nonpersistency of the symptoms, the doctor struggled to make a diagnosis. 由于症状的非持久性，医生难以做出诊断。 | Synonyms: transience, ephemerality, instability | Antonyms: persistence, permanence, stability | noun
