floorcloths:/ˈflɔːrˌklɔːθs/ /ˈflɔːrˌklɑːθs/
n.
油布；地毯；擦地板布
A heavy, durable piece of fabric, often painted or decorated, used to cover and protect a floor.
The kitchen floor was covered with brightly colored floorcloths. 厨房地板上铺着色彩鲜艳的油布。

近义词:linoleum, rugs, carpets, mats
反义词:(No direct antonym, but perhaps "bare floor")
词性:noun
