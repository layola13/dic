mootness's:/ˈmuːtnəsɪz/
noun
无实际意义；争论的结束
The state or quality of being moot.  The fact that a legal case or issue is moot.
The mootness's of the issue was apparent to everyone.  这个问题的无实际意义对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
近义词: irrelevance, inconsequentiality
反义词: relevance, significance
词性: noun

Etymology:
From moot + -ness. Moot itself comes from Old English *mōt (noun), meaning "meeting, assembly," and (adjective) "subject to discussion, debatable."
