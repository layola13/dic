herded:/ˈhɜːrdɪd/ | v. | 放牧；驱赶；聚集 | moved or controlled a large group of animals or people, often forcing them to go somewhere | The farmer herded the sheep into the pen. 农民把羊群赶进羊圈。 | The tourists were herded into the museum. 游客们被赶进了博物馆。| 近义词：drove, shepherded, guided, rounded up | 反义词：scattered, dispersed | 词性: 动词

词源: From herd (n.) + -ed (past tense suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *herdo, meaning "herd, flock."
