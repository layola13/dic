alumnae: /əˈlʌmniː/
n.
女毕业生；女校友
female graduates or former students of a particular school, college, or university
The alumnae of the college were invited back for a reunion.  该学院的女毕业生被邀请回来参加聚会。

近义词: graduates, alumni (if including all genders)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "current students")
词性: noun (plural)
