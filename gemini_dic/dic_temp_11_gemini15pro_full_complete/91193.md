hydraulically:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪkli/
adv.
液压地，用水力地
Operated or moved by means of liquid, typically oil under pressure.
The gate is hydraulically operated.  这扇门是液压操作的。
hydraulically powered system 液压动力系统  The landing gear is retracted hydraulically. 起落架是用液压装置收回的。
Synonyms:  by hydraulics, using hydraulic power
Antonyms: manually, mechanically
词性: 副词
