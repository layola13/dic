overcrediting: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度授信，过高评价
Giving someone or something too much credit or recognition.
The bank was accused of overcrediting risky borrowers.  这家银行被指控向高风险借款人过度放贷。
Some critics believe the young artist is being overcredited by the media. 一些评论家认为这位年轻的艺术家被媒体过誉了。
Synonyms: overestimating, overpraising, overvaluing
Antonyms: undercrediting, underestimating, undervaluing
