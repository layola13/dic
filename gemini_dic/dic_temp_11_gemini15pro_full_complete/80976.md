graciously: /ˈɡreɪʃəsli/ (US), /ˈɡreɪʃəsli/ (UK) | adv. |  亲切地，和蔼地，优雅地 | In a kind, polite, and pleasant way, often showing that you are grateful or willing to do something. | She graciously accepted the gift. 她优雅地接受了礼物。|  She graciously offered to help me with my luggage. 她亲切地主动帮我拿行李。| kindly, politely, courteously, generously | rudely, ungraciously, impolitely
词性: 副词
