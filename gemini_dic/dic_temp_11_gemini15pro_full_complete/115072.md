manetho:/məˈniːθoʊ/
Proper noun|曼涅托|Manetho was an Egyptian historian and priest who lived during the Ptolemaic Kingdom. He is known for his *Aegyptiaca*, a history of ancient Egypt written in Greek.|Manetho's *Aegyptiaca* is a crucial source for our understanding of ancient Egyptian history, though it only survives in fragmentary form through later writers.|近义词: (No direct synonym for a proper noun, but related terms could include) Egyptian historian, chronicler, scribe |反义词: N/A|词性: Proper Noun
