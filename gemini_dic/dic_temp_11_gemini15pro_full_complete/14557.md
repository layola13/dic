avertissement:/əˈvɛrtɪsmənt/ |n.|警告，告诫|A cautionary advice or warning. | The government issued an avertissement about the dangers of smoking. 政府发布了关于吸烟危害的警告。|  The product label contained a clear avertissement about the potential side effects. 产品标签上包含了关于潜在副作用的明确警告。|synonym: warning, caution, admonition| antonym: encouragement, approval |noun
