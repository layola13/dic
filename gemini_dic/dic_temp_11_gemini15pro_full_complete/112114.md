longshoremen:/ˈlɔːŋˈʃɔːrmən/ | n. | 码头工人，港口装卸工人 | A longshoreman is a worker who loads and unloads ships in a port. | The longshoremen were busy unloading cargo from the ship. 码头工人们正忙着从船上卸货。| The longshoremen went on strike for better wages. 码头工人们举行罢工，要求提高工资。 | 近义词: docker, stevedore, port worker | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑shipowner, shipping company等) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
long + shore + men

"longshore" dates back to the 16th century, referring to activities along the shoreline. "Men" simply denotes the individuals engaged in such work. The combined term "longshoremen" became common in the 19th century.
