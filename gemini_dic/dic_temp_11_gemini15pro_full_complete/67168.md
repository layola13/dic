expostulate:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊleɪt/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəˌleɪt/| v. | 抗议；劝诫；反对；告诫 | To reason earnestly with someone against something that person intends to do or has done; remonstrate. | She expostulated with him about his rudeness. 她劝诫他不要粗鲁。 | 近义词：remonstrate, protest, object, argue, complain | 反义词：agree, consent, approve | 词性: 动词

expostulating:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊleɪtɪŋ/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəˌleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |  抗议的；劝诫的；反对的；告诫的 | Expressing strong disapproval or disagreement. |  He gave her an expostulating look. 他给了她一个表示抗议的眼神。| 近义词：remonstrating, protesting, objecting | 反义词：approving, consenting | 词性: 形容词
