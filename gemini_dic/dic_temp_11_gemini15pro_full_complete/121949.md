mission-critical:/ˈmɪʃən ˌkrɪtɪkəl/| adj.|关键任务的，至关重要的|Essential for the success of a mission or project; crucial. Describing something that is vital to an operation or organization, and whose failure would cause serious disruption or damage.|例句：The server is mission-critical to our business operations. 这台服务器对我们的业务运营至关重要。|例句：The power supply is a mission-critical component of the spacecraft. 电源是航天器的关键部件。|近义词：essential, crucial, vital, indispensable|反义词:non-essential, unimportant, trivial|词性:adjective
词源: "mission" (from Old French "mission," from Latin "missionem," meaning "act of sending") + "critical" (from French "critique," from Greek "kritikos," meaning "able to judge")
