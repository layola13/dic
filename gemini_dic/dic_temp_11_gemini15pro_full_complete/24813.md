brouhaha:/ˈbruːhɑːhɑː/ /bruːˈhɑːhɑː/
brouhaha: n. |骚动，喧闹，吵闹；| a noisy and overexcited reaction or response to something. | Example: There was a big brouhaha when the star player was traded to another team.  明星球员被交易到另一支球队时，引起了很大的骚动。| Example: The media created a brouhaha about the politician's personal life. 媒体对这位政治家的私生活大肆渲染，引起了轩然大波。 | Synonym: commotion, uproar, hubbub, fuss, furor | Antonym: peace, quiet, calm | noun
