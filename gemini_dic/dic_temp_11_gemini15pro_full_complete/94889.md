in-depth:/ˌɪnˈdɛpθ/| adj.|深入的，彻底的|Extensive and thorough.|例句：We need an in-depth analysis of the market. 我们需要对市场进行深入的分析。|例句：She conducted in-depth research on the topic. 她对这个主题进行了深入的研究。|近义词：thorough, comprehensive, detailed, exhaustive|反义词：superficial, cursory, shallow|词性:形容词
词源: From "in" + "depth". "Depth" comes from Old English "deopþu".
