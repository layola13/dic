inferiae:/ɪnˈfɪəriːiː/
noun
祭祀; 阴间的; 献给亡灵的祭品
sacrifices offered to the dead; rites for the dead
The ancient Romans performed inferiae to honor their deceased ancestors. 古罗马人举行inferiae来纪念他们已故的祖先。
The family held elaborate inferiae for their departed loved one. 这家人为他们逝去的亲人举行了精心准备的inferiae。
synonyms: obsequies, rites, ceremonies
antonyms: celebration, festivity
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inferiae*, plural of *inferius* ("belonging to the lower world"), from *inferus* ("low, below"). Related to English *inferior*.
