approvement: /əˈpruːvmənt/
n.
批准；认可；赞同
An official act of approving.  The feeling of having a positive opinion of someone or something.
例句：The project won the approvement of the board.  这个项目获得了董事会的批准。

例句：He desperately sought his father's approvement. 他迫切地想得到父亲的认可。

近义词：approval, sanction, endorsement, consent
反义词：disapproval, rejection, condemnation
词性：名词
