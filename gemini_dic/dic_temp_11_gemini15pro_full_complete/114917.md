manageability:/ˌmænɪdʒəˈbɪləti/| n.|可管理性，易处理性|The manageability of the project depends on having a clear plan and sufficient resources.  这个项目的可管理性取决于有一个清晰的计划和充足的资源。|例句：The new software improved the manageability of the company's data.  新软件提高了公司数据的可管理性。|近义词：controllability, tractability, handiness|反义词: unmanageability, intractability, difficulty|词性:noun
Etymology: From manage + -ability.  "Manage" comes from Italian maneggiare "to handle," especially "to control a horse," from mano "hand," from Latin manus "hand."
