例句：She met a mysterious stranger at the masked ball. 她在化妆舞会上遇到了一位神秘的陌生人。


| 近义词：masquerade, costume party | 反义词: N/A | 词性：名词

Etymology:
"masked" comes from the Old French "masquer" meaning "to wear a mask, to disguise oneself." "Ball" comes from the Late Latin "ballare" meaning "to dance," which in turn comes from the Greek "ballizein."
