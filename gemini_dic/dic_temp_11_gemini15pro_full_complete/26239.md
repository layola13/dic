butternuts:/ˈbʌtərˌnʌts/ (US), /ˈbʌtənʌts/ (UK)| n.|冬南瓜; 白胡桃树; 白胡桃木|A type of winter squash with a tan-colored, pear-shaped fruit, or the tree that produces this fruit, or the wood of this tree.|例句：I made butternut squash soup for dinner. 我晚饭做了冬南瓜汤。

ETYMOLOGY->
From butter + nut.  The "butter" part refers to the smooth, buttery texture of the squash or nut meat.
