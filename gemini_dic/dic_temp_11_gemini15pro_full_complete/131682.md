nonconclusive:/ˌnɒnkənˈkluːsɪv/
adjective
非结论性的；无定论的
Not leading to a firm conclusion; not decisive.
The evidence was nonconclusive, so the jury couldn't reach a verdict.  证据不确凿，所以陪审团无法作出裁决。
The meeting ended with a nonconclusive agreement. 会议以一项无定论的协议结束。
inconclusive, indecisive, ambiguous
conclusive, decisive, definitive
词性：形容词
