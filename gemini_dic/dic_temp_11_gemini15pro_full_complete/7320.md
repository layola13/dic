angulo:/ˈæŋɡjʊloʊ/ (US), /ˈæŋɡjʊləʊ/ (UK)| n.|角，角度|An angle formed by two lines meeting. 两条线相交形成的角度。|The angle of the roof is too steep. 屋顶的角度太陡了。|近义词: corner, bend|反义词: straight|词性:名词

词源: From Spanish *ángulo*, from Latin *angulus* (“corner, angle”), related to *angere* ("to press tightly").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\ank-* (“to bend, angle”).
