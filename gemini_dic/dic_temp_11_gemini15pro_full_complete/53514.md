disassociation:/dɪsəˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/ /dɪsəˌsəʊʃiˈeɪʃən/
n.|脱离，分离，分裂|The act of separating or disuniting; the state of being separated or disunited.  A disconnection or lack of association between things usually associated.|After the trauma, she experienced a period of disassociation, feeling disconnected from reality. 创伤过后，她经历了一段时间的脱离现实感，感觉与现实脱节。|近义词：separation, detachment, disconnection|反义词：association, connection, union|词性:名词

dissociation:/dɪˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/ /dɪˌsəʊʃiˈeɪʃən/
n.|游离，解离|In chemistry, the separation of a molecule into simpler molecules, atoms, radicals, or ions.|The dissociation of water into hydrogen and oxygen ions is a fundamental chemical process. 水游离成氢离子和氧离子是一个基本的化学过程。|近义词：decomposition, ionization, separation|反义词：association, combination, synthesis|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dissociationem, from dissociare "to disunite," from dis- "apart" + sociare "to unite, associate," from socius "companion."
