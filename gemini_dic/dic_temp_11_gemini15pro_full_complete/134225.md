nonindustries:/ˌnɒnˈɪndəstriːz/ | n. | 非产业；非工业部门 |  Sectors of the economy that are not involved in manufacturing or production of goods.  Refer to activities not traditionally classified as industry, such as services, research, and education. | The growth of nonindustries is crucial for a balanced economy.  非工业部门的增长对经济均衡发展至关重要。

The government is investing heavily in nonindustries like tourism and education. 政府正在大力投资旅游和教育等非工业部门。| 近义词: non-industrial sectors, service sector | 反义词: industries, manufacturing sector | 词性: 名词
