chelerythrine: /ˌkɛlɪˈrɪθriːn/ | n. |  血根碱；白屈菜红碱 | An alkaloid, C₂₁H₁₇NO₄, extracted from the roots of *Sanguinaria canadensis* (bloodroot) and other members of the poppy family. It has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties and is used in some traditional medicines.  |  Chelerythrine has shown promising results in laboratory studies against certain cancer cells. 血根碱在实验室研究中对某些癌细胞显示出有希望的结果。

Chelerythrine is a potent inhibitor of protein kinase C. 血根碱是一种有效的蛋白激酶C抑制剂。 | 近义词: sanguinarine (血根碱，结构类似的生物碱) | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑与其作用相反的物质，例如某些蛋白激酶激活剂，但这需要具体情况具体分析) | 词性: noun
