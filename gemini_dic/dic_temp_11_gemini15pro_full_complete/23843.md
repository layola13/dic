breaded:/ˈbredɪd/| adj.|裹面包屑的；油炸的|coated in breadcrumbs before cooking|例句：We enjoyed the breaded chicken. 我们很喜欢这道炸鸡排。|例句：She prepared breaded fish for dinner. 她晚饭准备了炸鱼排。|近义词：crumbed, fried|反义词：unbreaded, plain|词性:形容词
词源:From bread + -ed,  past participle suffix indicating a state or condition.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *braudis.
