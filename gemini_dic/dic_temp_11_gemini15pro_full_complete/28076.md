capias:/ˈkeɪpiæs/| n.| судебный приказ о задержании; ордер на арест (legal)|A writ ordering the arrest of a named person.|The judge issued a capias for the defendant's arrest. 法官签发了对被告的逮捕令。|Synonyms: warrant, writ|Antonyms: release, pardon|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *capiās*, second-person singular present active subjunctive of *capiō* (“I take, seize, capture”).
