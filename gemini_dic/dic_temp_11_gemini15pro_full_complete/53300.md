disruption:/dɪsˈrʌpʃən/| n. |中断，扰乱，破坏|A disruption is something that interrupts the normal course or continuity of something.|The heavy snow caused a disruption to train services. 大雪导致了火车服务的 disruption. |近义词：interruption, disturbance, disorder|反义词:order, continuity, stability|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *disruptio*, from Latin disruptus, past participle of disrumpere (“to break apart”).  Dis- + rumpere ("to break").
