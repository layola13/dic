misfashions:/ˌmɪsˈfæʃənz/
|n.|过时的式样；不时髦的服装|A style or manner of dress that is out of fashion or unbecoming.|The misfashions of the 1980s are now considered retro. 20世纪80年代的过时款式现在被认为是复古的。|Near synonyms: outdated styles, unfashionable attire|Opposite: fashions, trends|Noun

misfashion:/ˌmɪsˈfæʃən/
|vt.|把…做得不合时宜；把…弄得不漂亮|To fashion or make in an unbecoming or unsuitable manner.|The dressmaker completely misfashioned the gown.  裁缝把礼服做得完全不合时宜。|Near synonyms:  mar, spoil, ruin|Opposite: fashion, style|Verb
