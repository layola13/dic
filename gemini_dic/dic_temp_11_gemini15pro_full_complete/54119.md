disenchant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃænt/ | vt. |使清醒，使不再抱幻想|To free from illusion or false belief; disillusion.|例句：The experience disenchanted her with politics.  这段经历使她对政治不再抱有幻想。

例句：He became disenchanted with his job. 他对自己的工作感到失望。


近义词：disillusion, disappoint, undeceive|反义词: enchant, charm, fascinate |词性: verb
