belly:/ˈbeli/ (US) /ˈbɛli/ (UK)| n. |肚子，腹部|The part of the body between the chest and the thighs, containing the stomach and bowels.  |He rubbed his belly contentedly after the feast.  他酒足饭饱后心满意足地揉了揉肚子。|Synonyms: abdomen, stomach, gut, tummy|Antonyms: back, spine|noun

belly:/ˈbeli/ (US) /ˈbɛli/ (UK)| v. |(使)鼓起，(使)胀满|To swell or cause to swell outwards.|The sails bellied out in the wind.  风吹得船帆鼓了起来。|Synonyms: swell, bulge, distend|Antonyms: deflate, shrink|verb
