motoneuron:/ˈməʊtəʊˌnjʊərɒn/ (UK), /ˈmoʊt̬əˌnʊrɑːn/ (US)| n. |运动神经元|A nerve cell that transmits signals from the brain or spinal cord to muscles, stimulating them to contract.|The motoneurons control the movement of your arm. 运动神经元控制你手臂的运动。|Synonym: motor neuron, efferent neuron|Antonym: sensory neuron|noun
