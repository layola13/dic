fonne:/fɔn/ | n. | fool; simpleton | A person who is easily tricked or deceived. | He was such a fonne, he believed everything she told him. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信她所说的一切。|  近义词: fool, idiot, simpleton, dupe | 反义词: sage, genius, wise man | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *fonne, from Old French *fon, *fol, ultimately from Latin *follis "bellows, windbag", implying someone empty-headed.
