feasibly:/ˈfiːzɪbli/ | adv. | 可行地，切实可行地 | Possible to do easily or conveniently. | It's not feasibly possible to complete the project in two weeks.  在两周内完成这个项目是不可行的。 | Synonyms: practically, possibly, realistically | Antonyms: impossibly, impractically | adverb

Etymology ->
From Late Middle English *fesible*, from Old French *faisable*, from *faire* (“to do, make”) (from Latin *facere*), + *-able* (from Latin *-bilis*).
