complainingly: /kəmˈpleɪnɪŋli/ | adv. | 抱怨地，发牢骚地 | in a complaining manner; with complaints | He grumbled complainingly about the cold weather. 他抱怨着寒冷的天气。|Synonyms: querulously, grumblingly, peevishly, whiningly | Antonyms: cheerfully, contentedly, happily | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From complain + -ing + -ly.  "Complain" comes from Old French "complaindre,"  meaning "to bewail, lament," from Latin "complangere," meaning "to beat one's breast," from "com-" (together) + "plangere" (to lament).
