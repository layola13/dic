genre:/ˈʒɑːnrə/ (US), /ˈʒɒnrə/ (UK)| n. |类型，体裁|A category of artistic composition, as in music or literature, characterized by similarities in form, style, or subject matter. |例句：This book belongs to the science fiction genre. 这本书属于科幻小说类型。

例句：The genre of the movie is romantic comedy.  这部电影的类型是浪漫喜剧。


近义词：category, type, kind, sort, style, form |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
