etiology's:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒiz/
noun
病因学（的）
The study of the causes or origins of diseases or abnormal conditions.  It investigates all the factors that contribute to the occurrence of a disease, including genetic predisposition, environmental influences, and lifestyle choices.
The etiology of the disease is still unknown.  这种疾病的病因仍然未知。
synonyms:causation, origin, root, source
antonyms: effect, result, outcome
n.
