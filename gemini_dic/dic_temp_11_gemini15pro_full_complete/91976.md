hypermysticalness:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɪstɪkəlnəs/
noun
极端神秘性；超级神秘感
The state or quality of being extremely or excessively mystical.
Her poetry is filled with a sense of hypermysticalness, exploring themes of ancient rituals and hidden knowledge. 她的诗歌充满了极端神秘感，探索着古代仪式和隐藏的知识。
The author's hypermysticalness borders on the absurd. 作者的极度神秘主义近乎荒谬。
Near synonyms:  extreme mysticism,  intense spirituality, profound mystery, esotericism
Antonyms:  mundanity,  ordinariness,  pragmatism
n.
