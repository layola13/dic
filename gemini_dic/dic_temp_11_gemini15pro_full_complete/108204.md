laudable:/ˈlɔːdəbl/ (US), /ˈlɔːdəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|值得称赞的，值得赞扬的|Deserving praise and commendation.|Her dedication to helping the homeless is truly laudable. 她致力于帮助无家可归者的奉献精神是 truly laudable.|近义词：praiseworthy, commendable, admirable, meritorious|反义词：blameworthy, reprehensible, deplorable|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English *laudable*, from Late Latin *laudabilis* (“praiseworthy”), from Latin *laudō* (“I praise”).
