interterminal:/ˌɪntərˈtɜːrmɪnəl/ | adj. | 航站楼间的，终端之间的 | Situated or operating between terminals, especially in an airport. |  Passengers can take the interterminal shuttle bus. 乘客可以乘坐航站楼间的穿梭巴士。| synonym: inter-terminal, connecting, linking | antonym: intraterminal | adjective
