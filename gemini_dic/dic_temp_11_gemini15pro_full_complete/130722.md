nonalliance:/ˌnɒnəˈlaɪəns/ |n.|不结盟，不联盟|The country adopted a policy of nonalliance during the war.  这个国家在战争期间采取了不结盟政策。|例句：Switzerland's traditional policy of nonalliance has served it well. 瑞士传统的保持中立的政策对它很有利。

例句：Many smaller nations favored a policy of nonalliance during the Cold War.  冷战期间，许多小国倾向于不结盟政策。|近义词：neutrality, nonalignment|反义词：alliance, coalition|词性:名词

词源: non- (not) + alliance (联盟)  "non-"是前缀，表示“不”，“非”。 "alliance" 源自古法语 "aliance"，意为“联合，结合”。
