bondage:/ˈbɒndɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːndɪdʒ/ (US)| n.|奴役，束缚，捆绑|The state of being bound or restrained, either physically or metaphorically.  A lack of freedom.|
She felt trapped in a life of domestic bondage. 她感觉被困在家庭奴役的生活中。

He was finally released from the bondage of debt. 他终于摆脱了债务的束缚。

近义词：captivity, confinement, imprisonment, restraint, slavery, subjugation|
反义词：freedom, liberty, liberation|
词性：名词

词源：From Old French *bondage*, from bonde ‘fetter,’ ultimately of Germanic origin.
