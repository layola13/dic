eap:/iːˈæp/ | n. |  Employee Assistance Program (员工援助计划) | A confidential service offered by employers to help employees deal with personal or work-related problems. | Example: The company's EAP provides counseling services for stress management. 公司的员工援助计划提供压力管理咨询服务。| Example:  She accessed the EAP for help with her financial difficulties. 她通过员工援助计划寻求帮助来解决她的财务困难。 | Synonym: staff support program, employee well-being program | Antonym: N/A | noun
