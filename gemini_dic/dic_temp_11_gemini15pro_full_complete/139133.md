obelus: /ˈɒbələs/ (US) /ˈəʊbələs/ (UK)| n. | 除号，斜线号 |A mark (÷ or †) used in ancient manuscripts to mark a questionable passage or to indicate a spurious addition.  It is now used chiefly to indicate division. |The teacher used an obelus to indicate the division problem on the board. 老师用除号来表示黑板上的除法问题。|Synonym: division sign, slash |Antonym: N/A | noun
