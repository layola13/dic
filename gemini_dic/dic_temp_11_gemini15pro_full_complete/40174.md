contentednesses:/kənˈtɛntɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
满足感，满意度
The state of being contented; satisfaction.
She expressed her contentednesses with the outcome. 她对结果表示满意。
近义词:contentment, satisfaction, happiness, pleasure
反义词:discontent, dissatisfaction, unhappiness, displeasure
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From contentedness + -es (plural suffix).  Contentedness comes from contented + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Contented comes from content (satisfied) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives). Content ultimately derives from the Latin word "contentus" (past participle of "continere," meaning "to hold together, contain").
