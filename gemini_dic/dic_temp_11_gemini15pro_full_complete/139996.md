odyles:/ˈɑːdɪˌlaɪlz/ | n. | 髁 | Rounded, knuckle-like projections at the end of a bone, typically where it forms a joint. | The distal end of the femur has two odyles that articulate with the tibia to form the knee joint. |  股骨远端有两个髁与胫骨连接形成膝关节。|  condyles, epicondyles |  (No direct antonym, perhaps "shaft" of the bone) | noun
