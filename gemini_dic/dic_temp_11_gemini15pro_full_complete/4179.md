airdromes:/ˈɛərdrəʊmz/ (UK), /ˈɛərdroʊmz/ (US)
|n.|飞机场|A place where aircraft regularly land and take off, with buildings and equipment for their use.  Essentially synonymous with "airport," though sometimes implying a smaller or less developed facility.|Several airdromes were built during the war.  战争期间建造了几个飞机场。|synonyms: airport, airfield, airstrip|antonyms: None (lack of an airdrome isn't a specific place)|noun
