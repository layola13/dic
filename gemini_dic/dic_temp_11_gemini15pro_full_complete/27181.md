calligraphist: /kəˈlɪɡrəfɪst/
n.
书法家
A person who produces decorative handwriting or lettering with a pen or brush.
The calligraphist spent hours perfecting each stroke of the character.  这位书法家花了几个小时完善每个字的笔画。
The calligraphist's work was admired for its elegance and precision.  这位书法家的作品因其优雅和精准而备受赞赏。

近义词:scribe, penman, letterer
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "amateur" or "tyro" in a comparative context)
词性:noun
