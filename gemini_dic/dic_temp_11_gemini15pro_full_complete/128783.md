nightbird:/ˈnaɪtbɜːrd/ | n. |夜莺，夜行动物|A bird that is active at night, such as an owl or nightingale.  A person who enjoys staying up late at night.| The nightbird sang a haunting melody. 夜莺唱着一首令人难忘的旋律。| 近义词：night owl, nocturnal animal | 反义词: day bird, diurnal animal |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *nightbrid*, equivalent to night + bird.
