emphasized: /ˈɛmfəsaɪzd/ | adj. | 强调的，着重的 | Given special importance or prominence. | The teacher emphasized the importance of doing homework. 老师强调了做作业的重要性。 | synonyms: stressed, highlighted, underscored | antonyms: downplayed, de-emphasized | adjective

emphasize: /ˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | vt. | 强调，着重 | To give special importance or prominence to (something) in speaking or writing. |  She emphasized the need for cooperation. 她强调了合作的必要性。 | synonyms: stress, highlight, underscore | antonyms: downplay, de-emphasize | verb

词源: From Late Latin *emphasizare, from Greek emphainein ‘exhibit’, from en- ‘in’ + phainein ‘to show’.
