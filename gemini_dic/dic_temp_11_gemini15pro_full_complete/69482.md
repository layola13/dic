feldspar:/ˈfeldˌspɑːr/ /ˈfɛldˌspɑːr/
n.
长石
Any of a group of crystalline minerals that consist of aluminosilicates of potassium, sodium, calcium, and occasionally barium, and that are the most abundant constituents of rocks such as granite and gneiss.
Feldspar is the most common mineral in the Earth's crust.  长石是地壳中最常见的矿物。
Many different types of ceramics use feldspar as a flux. 许多不同类型的陶瓷都使用长石作为助熔剂。
近义词: albite, anorthite, orthoclase, microcline, plagioclase, sanidine
反义词: None (Being a mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
