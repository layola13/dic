hemispherical:/ˌhemɪˈsfɛrɪkəl/
adjective
半球形的
Shaped like a half of a sphere.
The dome of the building is hemispherical.  这栋建筑的穹顶是半球形的。
The planetarium projector displayed images on the hemispherical ceiling. 天文馆的放映机将图像投射到半球形的天花板上。

近义词:dome-shaped, semiglobular
反义词:flat, planar
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hemi- (half) + spherical (shaped like a sphere). Ultimately from Greek hēmi- (half) and sphaira (sphere).
