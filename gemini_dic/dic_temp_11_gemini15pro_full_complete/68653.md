faraday:/ˈfærədeɪ/ | n. |法拉第（电容单位）; 法拉第（人名）| A unit of electrical capacitance equal to the capacitance of a capacitor that has an equal and opposite charge of 1 coulomb on each plate and a potential difference of 1 volt between the plates. Also, the surname of Michael Faraday, a British scientist who contributed significantly to the study of electromagnetism and electrochemistry. | The capacitance of the capacitor is measured in farads.  这个电容器的电容是用法拉测量。

Michael Faraday discovered electromagnetic induction. 迈克尔·法拉第发现了电磁感应。 | near synonyms:  unit (for capacitance), scientist (for the person) | antonyms: N/A | noun

faradaic:/ˌfærəˈdeɪɪk/ | adj. | 法拉第的; 与法拉第有关的 | Relating to Michael Faraday or his work, especially in electromagnetism and electrochemistry. |  Faradaic current is the current that flows during an electrochemical reaction. 法拉第电流是指在电化学反应过程中流动的电流。

The faradaic cage protects the equipment from external electromagnetic fields. 法拉第笼保护设备免受外部电磁场的影响。| near synonyms: electrochemical, electromagnetic | antonyms: N/A | adjective
