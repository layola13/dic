oligopetalous:/ˌɒlɪɡəˈpɛtələs/| adj.|少瓣的；寡瓣的|Having few petals (especially fewer than the typical number for the family or genus).|The oligopetalous flower had only four petals, unlike its close relatives with five.  这种少瓣的花只有四片花瓣，不像它的近亲有五片。|Few-petaled, having few petals.|Polypetalous|adjective
Etymology: From oligo- "few" + -petalous "petaled" (from Greek *petalon "leaf").
