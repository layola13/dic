out-bellowing:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊɪŋ/
verb
大声吼叫，胜过…的吼叫
to shout louder than someone or something else
The captain out-bellowed the storm, urging his crew to keep rowing. 船长的声音盖过了风暴的呼啸，敦促他的船员继续划船。
The rival fans were out-bellowing each other throughout the match.  比赛中，双方的球迷都在互相叫喊，试图盖过对方的声音。
synonyms: out-shouting, drowning out, over-roaring
antonyms: whispering, murmuring, muttering
