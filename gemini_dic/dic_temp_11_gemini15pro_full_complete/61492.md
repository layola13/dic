embroiderer:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərər/| n. | 刺绣者，绣工 | A person who embroiders.  A person who decorates cloth by sewing patterns on it with thread. |  The embroiderer skillfully crafted a delicate floral design.  这位刺绣者巧妙地制作了一个精致的花卉图案。| Synonym: needleworker, stitcher | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider someone who removes embroidery -  "seam ripper" in a specific context) | noun
