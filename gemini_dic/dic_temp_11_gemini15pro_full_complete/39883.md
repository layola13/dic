constituently:/kənˈstɪtʃuəntli/| adv.| 组成地，作为成分地| In a way that relates to the parts or elements that form something. |The cake was constituently made of flour, sugar, and eggs.  这个蛋糕是由面粉、糖和鸡蛋组成的。| synonym: componently, elementally | antonym: inseparably, wholly | adverb

constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/| n. adj.| 成分，构成要素；选民 | a. Being a part of a whole;  n. A person who authorizes another to act in his or her behalf, as a voter to an elected official. | Water is a constituent of many drinks. 水是许多饮料的成分。| synonym: component, element, ingredient; voter, elector | antonym: whole, entirety; nonvoter | noun, adjective
