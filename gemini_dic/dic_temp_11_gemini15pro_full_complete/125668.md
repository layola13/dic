munch:/mʌntʃ/| v. n.|大声咀嚼，吃；嘎吱嘎吱地嚼|To chew something steadily and often noisily.|例句：He munched on an apple. 他嚼着苹果。|近义词：chew, champ, nibble|反义词: sip, swallow|词性:动词, 名词
munch:/mʌntʃ/| n.|吃东西，咀嚼|The act of munching.|例句：I heard the loud munch of the horse eating hay. 我听到马吃干草大声咀嚼的声音。|近义词：bite, chewing|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
