acorned:/əˈkɔːrnd/ | adj. |橡果累累的；橡子状的 | Bearing or resembling acorns. | The ancient oak was acorned and majestic. 古老的橡树结满了橡子，雄伟壮观。 |  The path was littered with acorned branches. 小径上散落着橡子状的树枝。 | 近义词: oaky, nut-bearing | 反义词: barren, fruitless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From acorn + -ed.  "acorn" comes from Old English "æcern", meaning "fruit of the oak tree".  The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives meaning "having" or "characterized by".
