mule:/mjuːl/| n.|骡子；杂交动物；顽固的人；拖鞋|A hybrid offspring of a male donkey and a female horse, known for its strength and surefootedness.  A stubborn person. A type of backless slipper.|例句：The mule carried heavy loads across the mountain pass. 骡子驮着重物穿过山口。

例句：He's as stubborn as a mule. 他像骡子一样固执。|近义词：donkey, hybrid, stubborn person, slipper|反义词:horse, ass, compliant person|noun


mule:/mjuːl/| v.|运送（毒品）|To smuggle (drugs).|例句：He was arrested for muling cocaine across the border. 他因跨境贩运可卡因而被捕。

例句：She risked her life muling drugs for the cartel. 她冒着生命危险为贩毒集团运送毒品。|近义词：smuggle, traffic|反义词: N/A|verb
