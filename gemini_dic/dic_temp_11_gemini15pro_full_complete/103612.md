jointest:/ˈdʒɔɪntɪst/ | adj. | 共同的，连接的 |  Superlative form of "joint," meaning shared or done together.  While grammatically correct, it's extremely rare and archaic. It’s more common to use “most joint” or another phrasing like “most shared” or “most collaborative.”  |  The jointest effort of the two kingdoms resulted in a lasting peace.  两个王国共同的努力带来了持久的和平。 | 近义词: shared, combined, collaborative, united | 反义词: separate, individual, divided | 词性: 形容词
