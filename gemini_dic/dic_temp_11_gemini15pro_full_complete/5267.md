allomorphism:/ˌæləˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ | n. | 同质异形；同质异象 | The variation in the shape of morphemes based on the surrounding phonemes is called allomorphism.  词素的形状基于周围音素的变化称为同质异形。 |  The plural morpheme in English exhibits allomorphism, appearing as /-s/, /-z/, or /-ɪz/ depending on the final sound of the noun. 英语中的复数词素表现出同质异形，根据名词的最后一个音，它表现为 /-s/、/-z/ 或 /-ɪz/。 |Synonyms:  variation, alternation | Antonyms:  uniformity, invariance | noun
