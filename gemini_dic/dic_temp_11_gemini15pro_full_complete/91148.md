hydage:/ˈhaɪdɪdʒ/
n.
水税; 水力发电量;
A tax formerly levied for the scouring of ditches or waterways.  A toll or due for water passage. The amount of power generated by hydraulic machinery.
The hydage for using the canal was quite high. 使用运河的水税很高。
The hydage from the dam powered the entire town. 大坝的水力发电量为整个城镇供电。
Synonyms: water tax, water toll, water rate, hydropower, hydraulic power
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could consider concepts like "water subsidy" or "free water usage")
