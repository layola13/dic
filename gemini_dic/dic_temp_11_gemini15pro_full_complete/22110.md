bolshiness:/ˈbɒlʃɪnəs/ |n.| 粗暴; 蛮横; 盛气凌人 |The bolshiness of the teenager made it difficult for his parents to communicate with him. 这个青少年的粗暴态度使他的父母很难与他沟通。|Bolshiness is characterized by a defiant and uncooperative attitude. 盛气凌人的特点是反抗和不合作的态度。|近义词: aggressiveness, defiance, rebelliousness|反义词: compliance, obedience, submissiveness|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Bolshevik + -ness.  "Bolshevik" referred to members of the majority faction of the Russian Social Democratic Labour Party, which seized power in the October Revolution of 1917.  The term became associated with revolutionary and often aggressive or defiant behavior.
