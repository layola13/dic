outwatching:/ˌaʊtˈwɒtʃɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
外出观看；户外观看
The act of watching something while outdoors.
We were outwatching the meteor shower last night. 我们昨晚在户外观看流星雨。
outwatching the birds in the garden 在花园里观鸟
近义词: observing, viewing, watching outdoors
反义词: indoors watching, inside viewing
词性: verb (present participle)
