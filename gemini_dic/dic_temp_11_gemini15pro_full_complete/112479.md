louk:/luːk/ | n. | look; appearance | The general outward appearance of something or someone.  It can also refer to a facial expression or the act of looking.  Derived from Middle English *loken*, from Old English *lōcian* "to see, look at." |  She had a worried look on her face. 她脸上带着担心的表情。 |  appearance, aspect, guise |  invisibility, blindness | noun
