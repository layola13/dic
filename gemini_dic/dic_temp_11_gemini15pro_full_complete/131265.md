noncategoricalnesses:/ˌnɒnkætəˈɡɒrɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 非绝对性; 非明确性 |  The state or quality of not being categorical; lack of definiteness or absoluteness.  | The noncategoricalnesses of the guidelines allowed for flexibility in their interpretation. 指导方针的非绝对性使得对其解释具有一定的灵活性。|  ambiguity, vagueness, indefiniteness |  certainty, definiteness, clarity | noun
Etymology->
From non- + categorical + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Categorical" comes from the Late Latin "categoricus," meaning "affirmative, predicative," from Greek "katēgorikos," from "katēgorein" meaning "to accuse, to predicate, to affirm."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
