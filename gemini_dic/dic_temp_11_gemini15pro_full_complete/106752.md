la-di-da:/ˌlɑː.diˈdɑː/
n./adj.
装腔作势的；矫揉造作的；故作姿态的
Affectedly elegant or sophisticated; pretentious.
例句：She spoke in a la-di-da accent that annoyed everyone. 她说话带着一种矫揉造作的口音，惹恼了所有人。

例句：He dismissed their la-di-da lifestyle as superficial and meaningless. 他认为他们故作姿态的生活方式肤浅而毫无意义。

近义词：pretentious, affected, pompous, snobbish
反义词：down-to-earth, unpretentious, natural, genuine
词性：名词/形容词
