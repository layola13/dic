feather:/ˈfeðər/ (US) /ˈfeðə(r)/ (UK)| n. |羽毛|A lightweight, flat, hollow structure that grows from a bird's skin, providing insulation and enabling flight.|The bird preened its feathers.  这只鸟梳理它的羽毛。|Synonyms: plume, plumage|Antonyms: scale, fur|noun

feather:/ˈfeðər/ (US) /ˈfeðə(r)/ (UK)| v. |用羽毛装饰；长羽毛|To cover or decorate with feathers; to grow feathers.|The nest was feathered with soft down.  鸟巢里铺满了柔软的绒毛。|Synonyms: adorn, decorate|Antonyms: pluck, strip|verb
