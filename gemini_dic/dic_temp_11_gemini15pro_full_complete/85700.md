haversack: /ˈhævərsæk/ | n. | 背包，干粮袋 | A bag, typically made of canvas or other strong material, carried on the back or over the shoulder and used for carrying provisions or personal belongings. |  The soldier carried his rations in a haversack. 士兵把他的口粮装在一个背包里。

The hiker's haversack was filled with supplies for the weekend.  徒步旅行者的背包里装满了周末所需的用品。 | Synonyms: backpack, rucksack, knapsack | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
