countersniper:/ˈkaʊntərˈsnaɪpər/
n.|反狙击|The act or practice of countering enemy snipers.|The countersniper operation was successful. 反狙击行动很成功。|Synonyms: sniper hunting, anti-sniping|Antonyms: sniping|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
counter- (prefix): from Old French *contre-, from Latin contra "against"
sniper: from snipe (v.) "to shoot from a hidden position," from snipe (n.) "a bird difficult to shoot," of uncertain origin.
