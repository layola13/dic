distrusters:/dɪˈstrʌstərz/
n.
不信任者，怀疑者
People who distrust others; skeptics.
The distrusters questioned the motives of the new CEO.  不信任者质疑新任首席执行官的动机。
The distrusters were wary of the government's promises.  怀疑者对政府的承诺持谨慎态度。
近义词: skeptics, doubters, cynics
反义词: believers, trusters, optimists
词性: noun


distrust:/dɪˈstrʌst/
v.
不信任，怀疑
To lack confidence or faith in; to regard with suspicion.
She distrusted his motives for helping her. 她不信任他帮助她的动机。
He distrusted politicians and their empty promises. 他不信任政客和他们的空洞承诺。
近义词: doubt, suspect, question
反义词: trust, believe, confide
词性: verb


distrustful:/dɪˈstrʌstfəl/
adj.
不信任的，怀疑的
Feeling or showing distrust; suspicious.
She gave him a distrustful look. 她给了他一个怀疑的眼神。
He was distrustful of anyone in authority. 他不信任任何当权者。
近义词: suspicious, wary, skeptical
反义词: trusting, confident, believing
词性: adjective


词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + trust.
