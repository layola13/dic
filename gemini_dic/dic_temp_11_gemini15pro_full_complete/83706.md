hagiographies:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfiz/| n. |圣徒传记；（理想化、溢美之词的）传记|An idealized or worshipful biography, often of a saint.  A biography that treats its subject with undue reverence.|The new biography of the president reads more like a hagiography than a serious historical work.| 近义词：biography, life story, memoir, vita|反义词: exposé, denunciation|noun
