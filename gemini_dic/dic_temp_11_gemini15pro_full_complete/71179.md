fissureless:/ˈfɪʃərˌlɛs/| adj.|无裂缝的，无裂隙的|Without fissures or cracks; smooth and unbroken.|The surgeon was pleased to find the bone fissureless after the accident.  外科医生很高兴地发现事故后骨头没有裂缝。|例句：The fissureless surface of the obsidian was perfect for the mirror.  黑曜石光滑无裂缝的表面非常适合做镜子。|近义词：smooth, unbroken, seamless, intact|反义词: fissured, cracked, fractured|词性:形容词
Etymology: fissure + -less.  "fissure" comes from Latin "fissura," meaning "a cleaving or splitting." "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
