icteritious:/ˌɪktəˈrɪʃəs/| adj.|黄疸的，患黄疸的|Affected by jaundice, having a yellowish discoloration of the skin, whites of the eyes, etc., due to excess bilirubin in the blood.|The patient's skin appeared icteritious, a sign of liver problems.|近义词：jaundiced, yellowed|反义词: healthy, normal|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *icterus* (jaundice) + *-itious* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by").  Ultimately from Greek *ikteros* (jaundice).
