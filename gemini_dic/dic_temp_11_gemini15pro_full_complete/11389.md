argan:/ˈɑːrɡən/ | n. |阿甘油，阿甘树| A tree native to Morocco, whose nuts yield an oil used in cosmetics and cooking.  The oil extracted from the kernels of the argan tree.|  The argan oil is prized for its moisturizing properties. 阿甘油因其保湿特性而备受珍视。|  The argan trees are crucial to the ecosystem of the region.  阿甘树对该地区的生态系统至关重要。| synonyms:  argan tree,  | antonyms: N/A | noun
