credentialling: /krɪˈdɛnʃəlɪŋ/ | n. | 资格认证；资格审查 | The process of verifying and evaluating the qualifications and credentials of individuals or organizations.  | The hospital has a rigorous credentialling process for all physicians. 这家医院对所有医生都有严格的资格认证程序。 |  The credentialling process for nurses can be lengthy and complex.  护士的资格认证过程可能漫长而复杂。| Synonyms: certification, accreditation, licensing | Antonyms: disqualification | noun
