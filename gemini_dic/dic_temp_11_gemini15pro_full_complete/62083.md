encephalocele: /ɛnˈsɛfələˌsil/ /ɛnˈkɛfələˌsil/
noun
脑膨出
a birth defect in which part of the brain protrudes from the skull.
Babies born with an encephalocele typically require surgery.  婴儿出生时患有脑膨出，通常需要手术。
The doctor diagnosed the baby with an encephalocele. 医生诊断婴儿患有脑膨出。
synonym: craniocele
antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific birth defect. One could consider "normal cranial development" as a contrasting concept)
