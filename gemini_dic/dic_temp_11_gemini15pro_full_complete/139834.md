octonary:/ˈɒktənəri/ (UK) /ˈɑːktəneri/ (US)| adj. n.|八进制的，八元的；八进制数|Relating to or based on the number eight.  A system of numerical notation that has 8 rather than 10 as a base.|The octonary system is used in some computer systems.|近义词：base-8, octal|反义词: binary, decimal|词性: adjective, noun

octonary:/ˈɒktənəri/ (UK) /ˈɑːktəneri/ (US)| n.|八进制数|A number expressed in the octonary system.|The computer stored the data as an octonary.|近义词：octal number|反义词: binary number, decimal number|词性: noun
