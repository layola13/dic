cameo:/ˈkæmioʊ/ (US), /ˈkæmiəʊ/ (UK)| n. |客串；小饰品；浮雕宝石|A brief appearance of a well-known person in a film or play. A small piece of jewelry, typically oval in shape, with a carved design in relief.|例句：The film director makes a cameo appearance in his own movie.  这位电影导演在他的电影里客串了一下。
