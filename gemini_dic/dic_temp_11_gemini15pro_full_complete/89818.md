horned:/ˈhɔːrnd/ (US) /ˈhɔːnd/ (UK)| adj.|有角的；角状的|Having horns; shaped like a horn.|The horned owl perched silently on the branch. 有角鸮静静地栖息在树枝上。|The museum displayed a collection of horned helmets. 博物馆展出了一批角盔。|近义词：antlered, cornute|反义词：hornless|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English horned, from Old English hornig (“horny, horned”), equivalent to horn +‎ -ed. Cognate with Dutch gehoornd, German gehörnt.
