outthrob:/aʊtˈθrɒb/
verb
剧烈跳动，悸动
pulsate or beat strongly, especially in a rapid or irregular way.
Her heart outthrobbed with fear and excitement. 她的心跳因恐惧和兴奋而剧烈跳动。

近义词: pulsate, throb, palpitate
反义词: subside, calm

词性: v.

词源:  out- (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + throb (verb meaning to beat or pulsate rhythmically)
