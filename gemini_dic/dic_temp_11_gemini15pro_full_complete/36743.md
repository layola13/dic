colloquiality:/ˌkɒləʊkwiˈælɪti/ /ˌkɑːləʊkwiˈælɪti/
n.
口语性；通俗性；俗语
The quality of being colloquial; informal language or style.
Her writing style is known for its colloquiality, making it relatable to a wide audience. 她的写作风格以口语化著称，使其更容易被广大读者接受。

Colloquiality makes the dialogue in the play sound more natural. 口语使得这部戏的对话听起来更自然。

近义词:informality, casualness, vernacular
反义词:formality, stiffness
