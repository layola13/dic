misframe:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪm/
verb
错误地表达；歪曲；断章取义
To present or interpret (something, such as an issue or argument) incompletely or incorrectly, especially in a way that is misleading or unfair.
The politician claimed his words were misframed by the media. 这位政治家声称他的话被媒体歪曲了。
The study misframed the issue, leading to false conclusions. 这项研究错误地表述了这个问题，导致了错误的结论。
synonyms: misrepresent, distort, misinterpret, mischaracterize, falsify
antonyms: represent accurately, portray truthfully, interpret correctly

词性: verb
