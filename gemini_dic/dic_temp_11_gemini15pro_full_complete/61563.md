emendator:/ɪˈmɛndeɪtər/ | n. | 校订者；修订者 | A person who corrects or improves texts, especially literary works. | The emendator carefully reviewed the manuscript, correcting errors and suggesting improvements. 校订者仔细地审阅了手稿，纠正了错误并提出了一些改进建议。 | Synonyms: editor, reviser, corrector, amender | Antonyms:  corruptor, distorter | noun
