elect's:/ɪˈlekts/
noun
被选中的人的；当选者的
belonging to or associated with a person or group that has been elected
The elect's duties begin immediately after the inauguration. 当选者的职责在就职后立即开始。
近义词:chosen, selected
反义词:rejected, unelected
词性:noun (possessive form)

elect:/ɪˈlekt/
adjective
当选的；被选中的
chosen or selected
The elect mayor will address the city tomorrow. 当选的市长明天将向全市发表讲话。
近义词:chosen, selected
反义词:rejected, unelected
词性:adjective

elect:/ɪˈlekt/
verb
选举；选择
to choose someone for a position by voting
They elected a new president. 他们选举了一位新总统.
近义词:choose, select, vote for
反义词:reject
词性:verb
