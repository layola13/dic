ETYMOLOGY->
From cute + -ness + -es (plural suffix). "Cute" originally meant "clever, shrewd, sharp," later evolving to mean "attractive, pretty, especially in a dainty way."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition. "-es" is a plural suffix.
