multichromes:/ˌmʌltiˈkroʊmz/
| n. |多色、多变色、多色颜料|A substance, especially a pigment, that exhibits several different colors depending on the angle of view or the angle of illumination.|The makeup artist used multichromes to create a dazzling, otherworldly effect on the model's eyelids.|近义词：iridescent, opalescent, chameleon, pearlescent|反义词:monochromatic|词性:noun


Etymology:
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "-chrome" (from Greek "chrōma" meaning "color")
