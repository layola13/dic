misinferring:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɜːrɪŋ/
verb
错误地推断，误解
Drawing a wrong conclusion from available information.
She misinferred his silence as agreement. 她把他的沉默误解为同意。
misinterpret, misconstrue, misunderstand
correctly inferring, accurately understanding
