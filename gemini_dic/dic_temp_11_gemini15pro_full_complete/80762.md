gossip: /ˈɡɒsɪp/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːsɪp/ (US)
n.|流言蜚语，闲聊|Casual or unconstrained conversation or reports about other people, typically involving details that are not confirmed as being true.|The office was rife with gossip about the new manager.|rumor, talk, chatter|truth, fact
n.|爱说闲话的人|A person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others.|She's such a gossip!|rumormonger, busybody|recluse, introvert
vi.|说闲话，传播流言|Engage in gossip.|They gossiped about their neighbors.|chatter, tattle, prattle|be silent, keep quiet
