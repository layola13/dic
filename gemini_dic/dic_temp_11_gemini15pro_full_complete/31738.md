cheapo:/ˈtʃiːpoʊ/| adj. n.|便宜的，廉价的；便宜货|Something that is cheapo is cheap and of poor quality.  It can be used as both an adjective and a noun.|例句：I bought a cheapo phone, and it broke after a week. 我买了个便宜手机，一周就坏了。|近义词：cheap, inexpensive, bargain, budget|反义词:expensive, costly, pricey|adjective

cheapo:/ˈtʃiːpoʊ/| n.|便宜货|A cheapo is something that is cheap and of poor quality.|例句：That car is a real cheapo. 那辆车真是个便宜货。|近义词：cheapskate|反义词:luxury item|noun
