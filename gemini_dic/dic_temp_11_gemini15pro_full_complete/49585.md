demerging:/diːˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/
|n.|拆分，分拆|The act of separating a company into two or more independent companies.|The demerging of the company resulted in two separate entities. 公司的拆分导致了两个独立的实体。|Synonym: splitting, separating, dividing, divestiture|Antonym: merging, combining, consolidating|noun
demerging:/diːˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/
|v.|拆分，分拆|To separate a company into two or more independent companies.|They are demerging the telecommunications business from the main company. 他们正在将电信业务从总公司中拆分出来。|Synonym: split, separate, divide, divest|Antonym: merge, combine, consolidate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (expressing reversal) + merging (from merge, from Old French remergier, from re- "again" + mergier "to immerse").
