bye's:/baɪz/| n.|告别，再见（bye 的非正式复数形式，较少使用）|A casual, often childish, plural form of "bye," used to express farewell.  It's grammatically unconventional. |Example: "Bye's, see you later!" 再见，回头见！|近义词：goodbyes, farewells|反义词: greetings, hellos|Noun
