agriculture:/ˈæɡrɪkʌltʃər/ | n. |农业，农学|The practice of farming, including cultivation of the soil for the growing of crops and the rearing of animals to provide food, wool, and other products. |例句：Modern agriculture relies heavily on technology. 现代农业严重依赖科技。|例句：Agriculture is the backbone of many developing economies. 农业是许多发展中经济体的支柱。|近义词：farming, husbandry, cultivation |反义词: - |名词
