词源：
"hemangio" comes from Greek "haima" meaning blood and "angeion" meaning vessel. "blastoma" refers to a tumor composed of embryonic tissue.  Therefore, "hemangioblastoma" literally means a tumor originating from embryonic blood vessel cells.
