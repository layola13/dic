clayish:/ˈkleɪɪʃ/| adj.|黏土的；似黏土的；含黏土的|Resembling clay; containing clay.|The soil in the garden is rather clayish. 花园里的土壤相当黏土。|近义词：clayey, argillaceous|反义词：sandy, loamy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From clay + -ish.  Clay comes from Old English clæg. The suffix -ish denotes "somewhat like" or "having the quality of".
