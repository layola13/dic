incentivized:/ɪnˈsɛntɪvaɪzd/| v. |激励；刺激；|To provide (someone) with an incentive. To motivate or encourage (someone) to do something.|The company incentivized its employees with bonuses for exceeding sales targets.  这家公司用奖金来激励员工超额完成销售目标。|Synonyms: motivated, encouraged, stimulated, spurred, prompted|Antonyms: discouraged, demotivated, hindered|verb

incentivized:/ɪnˈsɛntɪvaɪzd/| adj. |受激励的|Provided with an incentive. Motivated or encouraged to do something.|The incentivized employees worked harder to achieve their goals. 受到激励的员工更加努力地工作以实现他们的目标。|Synonyms: motivated, encouraged, stimulated, spurred, prompted|Antonyms: discouraged, demotivated, hindered|adjective
