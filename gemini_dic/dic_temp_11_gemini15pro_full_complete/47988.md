decimate: /ˈdɛsɪmeɪt/ | vt. | 大量毁灭，大大削弱，严重破坏 | to destroy a large part of something; to kill or destroy a large number of people, animals, or things | Disease decimated the population. 疾病使人口大量减少。 | synonyms: destroy, devastate, ravage, ruin | antonyms:  preserve, protect, save | verb

decimate: /ˈdɛsɪmeɪt/ | vt. | (旧时军队对叛乱者或战俘)每十人抽杀一人 | to kill every tenth person in a group of soldiers, as a punishment for the whole group | The Roman army decimated the rebellious legion. 罗马军队对反叛的军团施行了十抽一的处决。 | synonyms: execute, slaughter | antonyms: pardon, spare | verb
