neuropteran: /njʊəˈrɒptərən/ | adj. n. | 脉翅目的；脉翅目昆虫 | An insect of the order Neuroptera, such as a lacewing or antlion. | Lacewings are delicate neuropteran insects with large, net-veined wings. 草蛉是脉翅目昆虫，有着精致的大网状翅膀。| The antlion is a fascinating neuropteran known for its larval traps. 蚁蛉是一种迷人的脉翅目昆虫，以其幼虫陷阱而闻名。 | 近义词: lacewing, antlion, dobsonfly | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group) | 词性: adjective, noun
