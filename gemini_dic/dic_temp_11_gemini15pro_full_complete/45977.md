cyanine:/ˈsaɪənɪn/ | n. |花青素，靛蓝染料|A class of synthetic dyes characterized by their intense colors, often used in photography and as fluorescent probes in biology. |例句：Cyanine dyes are widely used in biological imaging. 花青素染料广泛用于生物成像。

例句：The researchers used a cyanine dye to label the cells. 研究人员使用花青素染料标记细胞。|近义词：dye, colorant |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
