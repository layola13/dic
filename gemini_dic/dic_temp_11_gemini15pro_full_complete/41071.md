词源：
From Middle English coperose, from Old French couperose, from Medieval Latin cuperosa, from Latin cuprum (“copper”) (so called because it was obtained from water draining from copper mines). Ultimately from Ancient Greek Κύπρος (Kúpros, “Cyprus”), where copper was mined in antiquity.
