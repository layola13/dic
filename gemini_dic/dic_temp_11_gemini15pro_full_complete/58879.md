duration:/djʊˈreɪʃən/ /dʊˈreɪʃən/| n.|持续时间，期间|The duration of the flight is approximately 10 hours.  飞行时间大约是10个小时。|length, term, span, period| shortness, brevity|noun

例句：

The duration of the concert was two hours.
音乐会的持续时间是两个小时。

The course has a duration of six weeks.
这门课程为期六周。


近义词：length, term, span, period

反义词：shortness, brevity


词源：

From Middle English *duracioun, from Anglo-Norman duraciun, Old French duration, and directly from Latin dūrātiō (“a hardening, continuance”), from dūrō (“to harden, last, endure”) + -tiō.
