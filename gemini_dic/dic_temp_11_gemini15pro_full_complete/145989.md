overdogmaticalness:/ˌoʊvərˌdɒɡˈmætɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 过于教条主义 | The state of being excessively dogmatic; an insistence on strict adherence to doctrine or belief, often to an unreasonable or inflexible degree. | His overdogmaticalness made it difficult to have a productive conversation. 他的过分教条主义使得很难进行富有成效的对话。| Near synonyms: rigidity, inflexibility, dogmatism, doctrinaireism | Near antonyms: open-mindedness, flexibility, pragmatism | noun
