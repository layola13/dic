geophysicists:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪsɪsts/ | n. | 地球物理学家 | A scientist who studies the physics of the earth, including its structure, composition, and processes. | Geophysicists use a variety of tools and techniques to study the Earth. 地球物理学家使用各种工具和技术来研究地球。| synonyms: earth scientists, geoscientists | antonyms: N/A | Noun


Etymology: geo- (earth) + physics + -ist (one who practices)
