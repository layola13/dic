droughtier:/ˈdraʊ.ti.ər/ | adj. | 更干旱的 |  Comparative form of "droughty," meaning experiencing or susceptible to drought.  | The land is much droughtier this year than last. 今年的土地比去年干旱得多。 | The droughtier conditions led to crop failure.  更干旱的条件导致了作物歉收。 |近义词: drier, more arid | 反义词: wetter, more humid | 词性: adjective

drought:/draʊt/ | n. | 干旱 | A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water. | The drought has lasted for several months.  干旱已经持续了几个月。 | The farmers are struggling because of the severe drought. 农民们正因为严重的干旱而苦苦挣扎。 |近义词: aridity, dryness | 反义词: flood, deluge | 词性: noun

词源: From Old English *drugaþ, from Proto-Germanic *drugaz. Related to dry.
