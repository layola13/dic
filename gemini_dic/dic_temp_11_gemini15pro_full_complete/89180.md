homeostatic:/ˌhoʊmiːəˈstætɪk/ | adj. |体内平衡的; 恒定的 | Relating to homeostasis; tending to maintain internal stability. | The body's homeostatic mechanisms regulate temperature and blood sugar levels.  人体体内平衡机制调节体温及血糖水平。 | 近义词: regulatory, stabilizing, self-regulating | 反义词: unstable, fluctuating | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From homeo- (same, alike) + -static (standing, staying). Ultimately from Greek homoios (similar) and statikos (causing to stand).
