cocas:/ˈkoʊkəs/| n. |古柯叶; 古柯树|Coca leaves are the dried leaves of two coca species, Erythroxylum coca and Erythroxylum novogranatense. Coca leaves contain alkaloids, including cocaine.|The coca plant is native to South America. 古柯植物原产于南美洲。|Synonyms: coca leaves|Antonyms: N/A|noun
