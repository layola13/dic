mingy:/ˈmɪndʒi/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|Unwilling to give or spend; stingy.  Characterized by meanness or lack of generosity.|He was too mingy to leave a tip for the waiter. 他太吝啬了，没给服务员小费。|近义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious, tight-fisted|反义词：generous, liberal, open-handed|词性:形容词
词源:  Likely a blend of "mean" and "stingy," both referring to a lack of generosity.
