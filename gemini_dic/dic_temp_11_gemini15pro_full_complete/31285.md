chaperonage:/ˈʃæpərəʊnɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈʃæpərˌoʊnɪdʒ/ (US)| n.|陪伴；监护；伴护|The act of chaperoning someone, or the state of being chaperoned.  The social system or practice of chaperoning young unmarried people.|例句：The strict chaperonage of young girls was common in the 19th century.  19世纪，对年轻女孩的严格监护是很普遍的。

例句：She resented the chaperonage of her aunt. 她讨厌她姑妈的陪伴。|近义词：supervision, guardianship, escort, guidance|反义词: freedom, independence|词性: noun
