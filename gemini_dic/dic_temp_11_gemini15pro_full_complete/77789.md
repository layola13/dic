generalistic:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
普遍性的; 通才的; 泛化的
Having or involving a wide variety of skills or interests rather than specialized expertise in a particular area.
A generalistic approach to learning can be beneficial in the early stages of education.  通才式的学习方法在教育初期可能是有益的。

The company adopted a generalistic marketing strategy to reach a wider audience.  这家公司采取了普遍性的营销策略以覆盖更广泛的受众。

近义词:versatile, adaptable, all-around, multi-skilled, broad-based
反义词:specialized, specialist, specific, narrow, expert
词性:adjective
