effed:/ɛft/ | adj. | 糟糕的，搞砸的 | A euphemism for "fucked," meaning ruined, damaged, or in a bad state. |  例句： "I'm totally effed. I failed the exam." 我完蛋了。我考试不及格了。| 近义词：screwed, ruined, messed up, fucked | 反义词: fine, okay, good | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Effed" is a euphemism derived from the past participle of the verb "fuck." It replaces the vowel sound with a less offensive one while retaining the original meaning.
