humiliating:/hjuːˈmɪlieɪtɪŋ/ /juːˈmɪlieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
羞辱性的，丢脸的
Making someone feel ashamed and foolish.
It was a humiliating defeat for the team. 这对团队来说是一场耻辱的失败。

It was deeply humiliating to have to ask for money.  不得不开口要钱真是太丢脸了。


近义词:embarrassing, shameful, mortifying, degrading
反义词:exalting, uplifting, honoring

词性:adjective
