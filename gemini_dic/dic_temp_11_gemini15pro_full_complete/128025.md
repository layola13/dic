neptunicentric:/ˌnɛptjuːnɪˈsɛntrɪk/
adjective
以海王星为中心的
Having Neptune as the center.
例句：A neptunicentric orbit would be one that revolves around Neptune.  一个以海王星为中心的轨道就是一个绕海王星旋转的轨道。

例句：From a neptunicentric perspective, Triton appears to be the largest moon. 从以海王星为中心的视角来看，海卫一似乎是最大的卫星。

近义词: N/A (This is a highly specific term and doesn't have direct synonyms)
反义词: heliocentric (以太阳为中心的)
词性: adjective


词源:  Neptune (海王星, Roman god of the sea) + -centric (centered, from Greek kentron, meaning "center")
