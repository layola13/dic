curvilinear:/ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪniər/| adj.|曲线形的，曲线的|Having or represented by curved lines.  Characterized by curved lines.|The architect favored curvilinear designs, incorporating sweeping arches and rounded walls.  这位建筑师喜欢曲线设计，融合了拱形和圆形墙壁。|例句：The artist employed curvilinear forms throughout the painting.  这位艺术家在整幅画中都使用了曲线形式。

例句：The building's curvilinear facade stood out among the more traditional structures. 这座建筑的曲线形外观在更传统的建筑中脱颖而出。|近义词：curved, bending, sinuous|反义词:straight, linear|词性:adjective
