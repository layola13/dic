enlighten:/ɪnˈlaɪtn/ | vt. | 启发，启迪，开导 | To give someone greater knowledge and understanding about a subject or situation. | The teacher enlightened the students about the history of the Roman Empire. 老师给学生们讲解了罗马帝国的历史。| synonyms: educate, inform, illuminate, instruct | antonyms: confuse, mislead, obscure | verb

enlighten:/ɪnˈlaɪtn/ | vt. | 使…明白，使…了解 | To make something clear or understandable. |  Could you enlighten me on this matter? 你能给我解释一下这件事吗？ | synonyms: clarify, explain, elucidate | antonyms: complicate, confuse, obscure | verb
