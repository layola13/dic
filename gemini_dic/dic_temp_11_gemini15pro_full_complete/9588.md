antisocietally:/ˌæntiˈsəʊʃəli/ | adv. | 反社会地 | In a manner that harms or disrupts society.  Acting against the norms and values of society. |  His antisocietally motivated pranks caused significant damage. 他的反社会恶作剧造成了重大损失。|  The graffiti was deemed antisocietally offensive.  这些涂鸦被认为是反社会的冒犯行为。 |  harmfully, destructively, detrimentally, irresponsibly |  prosocially, constructively, helpfully | adverb
