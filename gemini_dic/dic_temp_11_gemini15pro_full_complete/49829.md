demonstrative:/dɪˈmɒnstrətɪv/ (UK), /dəˈmɑːnstrətɪv/ (US)| adj. n.|指示的，指示词|serving to demonstrate or prove; showing clearly; openly expressive of emotions|She gave a demonstrative hug to her friend. 她热情地拥抱了她的朋友。|近义词：indicative, illustrative, expressive|反义词：reserved, unemotional|词性:形容词

demonstrative:/dɪˈmɒnstrətɪv/ (UK), /dəˈmɑːnstrətɪv/ (US)| n.|指示词|a word or morpheme that points to a referent|“This,” “that,” “these,” and “those” are demonstratives.  “这”、“那”、“这些”和“那些”都是指示词。|近义词：indicator|反义词: (No direct antonym for the noun form)|词性:名词
