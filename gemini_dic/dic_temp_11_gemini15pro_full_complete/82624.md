governance:/ˈɡʌvərnəns/ | n. | 治理；管理；支配；统治 | The process of governing a country, organization, or group, especially the way that power is used and decisions are made. | The company's governance is weak and needs to be improved. 公司的治理薄弱，需要改进。 |  The new administration promised to improve governance and reduce corruption. 新政府承诺改善治理，减少腐败。 | synonyms: administration, management, control, rule, authority | antonyms: misrule, anarchy, chaos | noun
