outloves:/ˌaʊtˈlʌvz/
verb
超过…的爱；比…更爱
To love someone or something longer, more intensely, or more faithfully than someone or something else.
She outloved him by many years, cherishing his memory long after he was gone. 她比他多活了很多年，在他去世很久之后仍然珍惜着对他的记忆。
Synonyms: surpasses in love, loves more deeply, loves longer
Antonyms: is outloved by, loves less
词性:动词
