overcivilizing: /ˌoʊvərˈsɪvəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
过度文明化；过分教化
Making someone or something excessively civilized, often to the point of losing naturalness or vitality.
He lamented the overcivilizing influence of modern technology on children. 他哀叹现代科技对儿童的过度文明化的影响。
Overcivilizing our pets can lead to behavioral problems.  过分教化我们的宠物会导致行为问题。

近义词: refining, over-refining, over-cultivating
反义词: barbarizing, brutalizing


词性: Verb (动词)


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "civilize" (from Latin "civilis" meaning "relating to a citizen, courteous, polite") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
