interradiations:/ˌɪntərˌreɪdiˈeɪʃənz/
|n.|相互辐射，相互影响|The act or process of radiating or spreading out from a common center, especially in a complex or intricate way, often involving mutual influence or interaction.  It can refer to the spreading of light, ideas, influence, or other immaterial things.|The interradiations of Eastern and Western cultures have enriched both.|Synonyms: interaction, interplay, intermingling, fusion, blending|Antonyms: separation, isolation, divergence|noun
