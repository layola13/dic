fritter-away:/ˈfrɪtər əˈweɪ/
verb
浪费，挥霍
to waste time or money on unimportant things
He frittered away his inheritance on fast cars and expensive holidays. 他把遗产浪费在跑车和昂贵的假期上。
She frittered away the whole afternoon watching TV. 她浪费了整个下午看电视。
squander, waste, dissipate
save, conserve, preserve
词性:动词
