aristarchian:/ˌærɪˈstɑːrkiən/| adj. n.|阿里斯塔克斯的，过度批评的；阿里斯塔克斯学派的信徒|Relating to Aristarchus of Samothrace, a renowned grammarian and critic of ancient Greece, or to his rigorous methods of textual criticism; excessively critical.|His aristarchian approach to literature made him unpopular with many authors. 他对文学的过度批评态度让他不受许多作家的欢迎。|近义词：hypercritical, captious, censorious|反义词：uncritical, accepting, tolerant|词性:形容词,名词
