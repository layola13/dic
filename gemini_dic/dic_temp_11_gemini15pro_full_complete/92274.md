hyperviscous:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪskəs/| adj.|高粘度的，超粘性的|Having unusually high viscosity; extremely thick and sticky.|The patient's blood was hyperviscous, making it difficult to draw.|近义词:thick, viscous, gelatinous|反义词:thin, watery, fluid|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "hyper-" (Greek: over, excessive) + "viscous" (Latin: viscosus, sticky, glutinous)
