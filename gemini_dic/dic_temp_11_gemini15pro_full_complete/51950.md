diacap:/daɪˈækæp/ |n.|透析帽；透析膜|A semipermeable membrane used in dialysis, especially for hemodialysis.|The patient's blood was filtered through a diacap during the dialysis treatment. 病人的血液在透析治疗期间通过透析帽过滤。|Synonyms: dialysis membrane, semipermeable membrane|Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From dia- (through) + -cap (cap, covering)
