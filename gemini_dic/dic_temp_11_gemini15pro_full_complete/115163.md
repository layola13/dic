mania: /ˈmeɪniə/ (US), /ˈmeɪnɪə/ (UK) | n. | 狂热，躁狂 | An excessive enthusiasm or desire; an obsession.  |  He had a mania for collecting stamps.  他有收集邮票的狂热。 | synonyms: craze, obsession, passion, infatuation | antonyms: apathy, indifference, calmness | noun

mania: /ˈmeɪniə/ (US), /ˈmeɪnɪə/ (UK) | n. | 躁狂症（精神病学术语） | A phase of bipolar disorder characterized by intense excitement, elation, and overactivity. |  She experienced a manic episode last year. 她去年经历了一次躁狂症发作。| synonyms:  hypomania, frenzy | antonyms: depression | noun
