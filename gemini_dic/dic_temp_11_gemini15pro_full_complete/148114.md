oversecure:/ˌoʊvər sɪˈkjʊr/| adj.|过分安全的，过于谨慎的|Excessively secure; overly cautious and anxious about safety and security, often to the point of being impractical or counterproductive.|例句：He was so oversecure about his bike that he had five different locks on it. 他对他的自行车过于谨慎，用了五把不同的锁。

例句：Her oversecure nature made it difficult for her to relax and enjoy life. 她过分谨慎的天性使她难以放松和享受生活。|近义词：overprotective, overcautious, anxious, paranoid|反义词：careless, reckless, negligent|词性:形容词

词源：The prefix "over-" means "excessively" or "too much."  "Secure" comes from the Latin "securus," meaning "free from care, safe."  Therefore, "oversecure" means "excessively secure."  前缀“over-”表示“过度”或“太多”。 “Secure”来自拉丁语“securus”，意思是“无忧无虑，安全”。 因此，“oversecure”的意思是“过分安全”。
