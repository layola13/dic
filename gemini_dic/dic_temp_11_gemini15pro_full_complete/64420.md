ergonomics:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪks/ (US) /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɑːmɪks/| n.| 人体工程学；工效学|The study of people's efficiency in their working environment.  It aims to design workplaces, equipment, and tasks to fit the worker, thereby maximizing efficiency and minimizing discomfort or injury.| This office chair is ergonomically designed to provide maximum lumbar support. 这把办公椅采用人体工学设计，提供最大的腰部支撑。| synonyms: human factors, human engineering | antonyms: N/A | noun

ergonomic:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪk/ (US) /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɑːmɪk/| adj.| 人体工程学的；工效学的| Designed to minimize physical strain and maximize efficiency. |  An ergonomic keyboard can help prevent carpal tunnel syndrome. 人体工学键盘有助于预防腕管综合症。| synonyms: user-friendly, comfortable, efficient | antonyms: uncomfortable, inefficient | adjective
