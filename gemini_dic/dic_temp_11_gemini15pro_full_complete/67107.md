explosibility:/ɪkˌsploʊzɪˈbɪləti/ /ɪkˌsploʊzɪˈbɪləti/
n.|爆炸性，易爆炸性|The explosibility of the gas mixture was a major concern.  这种气体混合物的爆炸性是一个主要问题。|Explosibility refers to the ease with which a substance can explode or detonate. 爆炸性是指一种物质爆炸或引爆的容易程度。|The explosibility of the chemicals stored in the warehouse posed a significant risk.|近义词：inflammability, volatility|反义词:stability, inertness|词性:名词

explosibility:/ɪkˌsploʊzɪˈbɪləti/ /ɪkˌsploʊzɪˈbɪləti/
