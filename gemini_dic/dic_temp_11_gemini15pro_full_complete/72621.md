flyer:/ˈflaɪər (US), ˈflaɪə (UK)| n.|传单；飞行物；飞鸟；冒险家|A piece of paper, often advertising something, that is given to people.|They were handing out flyers for a new restaurant. 他们正在散发一家新餐厅的传单。|Synonyms: leaflet, handbill, circular, pamphlet, brochure|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "poster" if affixed, or "advertisement" if broadcasted)
词性: 名词

flyer:/ˈflaɪər (US), ˈflaɪə (UK)| n.|飞行员；飞行器|A person who flies an aircraft or spacecraft; an aircraft or spacecraft.|He's training to be a commercial flyer. 他正在接受培训，成为一名商业飞行员。|Synonyms: pilot, aviator (for person); aircraft, plane (for machine)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a person; for a machine, consider "grounded vehicle")
词性: 名词
