gusted:/ˈɡʌstɪd/ | adj. | 受强风吹袭的；（感情）迸发的 | Affected by sudden, strong gusts of wind; (of a feeling) expressed suddenly and strongly. |  She gave a gusted sigh of relief. 她如释重负地重重叹了口气。 | Synonyms: windy, blustery, impetuous | Antonyms: calm, peaceful | adjective
