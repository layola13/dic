gotten:/ˈɡɑːtn/| v. (past participle of get)|获得，得到|gotten is the past participle of the verb "get," primarily used in American English. It means "obtained," "acquired," or "become."|例句：I've gotten used to the weather here. 我已经习惯了这里的天气。|近义词：obtained, acquired, received|反义词:lost, forfeited|词性:动词(过去分词)
