droned:/drəʊnd/
verb
发出嗡嗡声；无人驾驶飞机飞行；单调地说
make a continuous low humming sound; fly a drone; speak tediously in a monotonous tone
The refrigerator droned in the background.  冰箱在背景里嗡嗡作响。
The drone droned overhead. 无人机在头顶嗡嗡作响。
He droned on about his work. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的工作。
近义词:hum, buzz, murmur, ramble
反义词:be quiet, be silent
词性:v.

droned:/drəʊnd/
adjective
单调的，嗡嗡作响的
monotonous and boring
His droned voice put me to sleep. 他单调的声音让我睡着了。
The droned lecture seemed to last forever.  单调的讲座似乎永远持续下去。
近义词:monotonous, tedious, boring
反义词:interesting, exciting, stimulating
词性:adj.
