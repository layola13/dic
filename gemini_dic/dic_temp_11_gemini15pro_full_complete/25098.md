bubonocele: /ˌbjuːbəˈnəʊˌsiːl/ /ˌbʊbənoʊˈsiːl/
n.
腹股沟淋巴结肿大;腹股沟疝
A hernia or rupture in the groin.  Specifically, a protrusion of intestine through a weakness in the abdominal wall in the inguinal canal.
例句：The doctor diagnosed the patient with a bubonocele.  医生诊断病人患有腹股沟疝。

例句：The bubonocele required surgical intervention. 腹股沟疝需要手术干预。

近义词：inguinal hernia, hernia
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists for a specific medical condition like bubonocele.  One could consider "healthy inguinal canal" as a contrasting state.)
词性：noun
