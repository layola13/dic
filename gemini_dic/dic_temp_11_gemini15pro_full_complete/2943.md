advocate: /ˈædvəkət/ (US) /ˈædvəkeɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.| 提倡者，拥护者；主张，拥护|A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.  To publicly recommend or support.|例句：She is a strong advocate for animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利倡导者。|近义词：supporter, proponent, champion|反义词：opponent, critic|词性: noun, verb

advocate: /ˈædvəkeɪt/ (US/UK)| vt.| 提倡，拥护|To publicly recommend or support.|例句：He advocates for stricter gun control laws. 他主张更严格的枪支管制法律。|近义词：support, promote, champion|反义词：oppose, criticize|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *advocatus, past participle of advocare (“to summon, call to one's aid”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + vocare (“to call”).
