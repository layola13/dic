monkfish:/ˈmʌŋkˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 鮟鱇鱼 | A bottom-dwelling fish with a large head and mouth, found in the Atlantic and Pacific oceans. It is considered a delicacy in some cuisines. | Monkfish is often served in high-end restaurants.  鮟鱇鱼经常在高档餐厅供应。

Monkfish liver is considered a delicacy, much like foie gras.  鮟鱇鱼肝被认为是一种美味佳肴，很像鹅肝。 | 近义词: anglerfish, goosefish | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish) | 词性: noun
