aptest:/ˈæptɪst/
adjective
最合适的，最恰当的
most suitable or appropriate
例句：He is the aptest candidate for the job. 他是这份工作的最佳人选。
例句：She always finds the aptest words to express her feelings. 她总能找到最恰当的词语来表达她的感受。

近义词：suitable, appropriate, fitting, proper, relevant
反义词：unsuitable, inappropriate, unfit, improper, irrelevant
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aptest, *aptiste, from Old French apte, habile (“apt, suitable”), from Latin aptus (“fit, suited”), past participle of apere (“to fasten, attach, connect”).
