outwrangle:/ˌaʊtˈræŋɡəl/
verb
胜过；以智取胜
to defeat or outsmart someone by arguing or persuading more skillfully
He outwrangled his opponents in the debate. 他在辩论中以智取胜，击败了对手。
近义词: outsmart, outmaneuver, outwit, defeat
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb

outwrangle:/ˌaʊtˈræŋɡəl/
vt.
胜过；以智取胜

近义词: outsmart, outmaneuver, outwit
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
