lamprey:/ˈlæm.pri/ (US), /ˈlæm.pri/ (UK)| n. |七鳃鳗|A jawless fish-like aquatic vertebrate with a sucker mouth lined with teeth, parasitic on other fish.|The lamprey attached itself to the salmon. 七鳃鳗吸附在鲑鱼身上。|近义词:hagfish|反义词:None (Lampreys don't have direct opposites as they occupy a unique ecological niche.)|noun
