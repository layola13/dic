meliaceous:/ˌmɛliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
楝科的
belonging to the Meliaceae family (the mahogany family), which includes trees and shrubs with typically pinnate leaves, fragrant flowers, and fruit that is a capsule, berry, or drupe.
The meliaceous tree provided welcome shade in the tropical heat.  这棵楝科树木在热带的酷暑中提供了受欢迎的阴凉。
The botanist specialized in meliaceous plants, particularly mahogany and neem trees.  这位植物学家专门研究楝科植物，特别是桃花心木和印度楝树。
synonyms: mahogany family (as a noun),  楝科的 (using scientific classification)
antonyms:  None (There's no direct opposite for a taxonomic adjective.)
词性: adjective
