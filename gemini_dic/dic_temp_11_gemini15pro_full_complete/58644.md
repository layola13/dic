dumbfounders:/dʌmˈfaʊndərz/ | n. | 使人惊愕的事；令人哑口无言的事物 | Something that dumbfounds or astonishes someone. | The magician's tricks were complete dumbfounders, leaving the audience speechless. 魔术师的戏法令人叹为观止，让观众哑口无言。 | 近义词: shocker, stunner | 反义词: commonplace, bore | 词性: 名词

dumbfound:/dʌmˈfaʊnd/ | v. | 使惊呆；使哑口无言 | To greatly astonish or amaze someone. |  The news of the lottery win completely dumbfounded her.  彩票中奖的消息让她完全惊呆了。 | 近义词: astonish, amaze, astound | 反义词: bore, tire | 词性: 动词
