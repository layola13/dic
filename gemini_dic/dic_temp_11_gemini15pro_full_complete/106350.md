kokom:/ˈkoʊkoʊm/ | n. |祖母，外祖母 (Ojibwe)|A term of endearment and respect for a grandmother or grandmother figure, particularly in Ojibwe culture. |My kokom told me stories of our ancestors. 我的祖母给我讲了我们祖先的故事。| grandmother, grandma, nana | grandfather, grandpa | noun

kokom:/ˈkoʊkoʊm/ | n. |女性长者 (Ojibwe)|A respectful term for an older woman, especially within the Ojibwe community, not necessarily a blood relative. |The kokoms of the community shared their wisdom. 社区的女性长者分享了她们的智慧。| elder, matriarch | young woman, girl | noun
