nonelectiveness:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktɪvnəs/
|n.| 非选择性，非选举性 |The nonelectiveness of the position means that the appointment is based on merit, not popular vote.  这个职位的非选择性意味着任命是基于能力，而不是民众投票。|  This course has a nonelectiveness policy, meaning all students enrolled must complete it. 这门课程是非选择性的，意味着所有注册的学生都必须完成它。| 近义词: obligatoriness, compulsion | 反义词: electiveness, optionality | 词性: noun

nonelective:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktɪv/
|adj.| 非选举的，非选择性的，必修的 | Students must take a certain number of nonelective courses. 学生必须修读一定数量的必修课。| The nonelective nature of the training program ensured everyone received the same basic skills. 该培训项目的非选择性确保了每个人都获得相同的  基本技能。 | 近义词: compulsory, required, mandatory | 反义词: elective, optional | 词性: adjective


词源:  "non-" (not) + "elective" (chosen by vote).  由"non-"(不)和"elective"(选举的)组成。
