grazioso:/ɡratˈtsjoʊzoʊ/ /ɡræˈtsɪəʊsoʊ/
adjective|优美的，优雅的；典雅的|Graceful, elegant; smoothly flowing, usually referring to a piece of music or a style of performance.|The pianist played the piece with a grazioso touch. 钢琴家以优雅的触感演奏了这首曲子。|Synonyms: graceful, elegant, stylish, refined, delicate, flowing, smooth|Antonyms: awkward, clumsy, ungainly, rough, jerky|adjective

grazioso:/ɡratˈtsjoʊzoʊ/ /ɡræˈtsɪəʊsoʊ/
