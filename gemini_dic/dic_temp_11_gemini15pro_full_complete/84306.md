half-wickets:/ˌhɑːf ˈwɪkɪts/ | n. | 半个三柱门，指板球比赛中以保龄球击倒的半个三柱门数 | A half-wicket refers to the number of wickets taken by a bowler in cricket, counted in halves because two bowlers are required to dismiss a batsman in partnership. | England won by six wickets, and Australia won by four and a half wickets. 英格兰队以六个三柱门获胜，澳大利亚队以四个半三柱门获胜。| Synonym: wickets (in general context) | Antonym:  (no direct antonym, perhaps "runs" in the context of scoring) | noun


half:/hɑːf/ | n. adj. adv. | 一半，半个 | One of two equal parts of something. | I ate half of the pizza. 我吃了一半的披萨。| Synonym: moiety, semi- | Antonym: whole, double | noun, adjective, adverb

wickets:/ˈwɪkɪts/ | n. | 三柱门，指板球比赛中的三柱门 |  The set of three stumps and two bails that a batsman defends in cricket. | The bowler took five wickets in the innings. 投球手在这局中拿下了五个三柱门。| Synonym: stumps (less common) | Antonym: (no direct antonym) | noun
