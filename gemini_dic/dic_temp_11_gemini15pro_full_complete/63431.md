entreatable:/ɪnˈtriːtəbl/
adjective
可恳求的，易求情的
Able to be influenced by entreaty; susceptible to pleading or persuasion.
He was an entreatable man, always willing to listen to a reasonable request. 他是一个容易求情的人，总是愿意倾听合理的请求。
近义词:persuadable, susceptible, approachable
反义词:intransigent, unyielding, implacable
词性:adjective
