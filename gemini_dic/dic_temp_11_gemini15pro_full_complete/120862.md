misbelief:/ˌmɪsbɪˈliːf/
n.
错误的信念；误信
An incorrect belief.
例句：His misbelief about the effectiveness of the treatment led to a worsening of his condition. 他对治疗效果的误信导致他的病情恶化。

例句：Common misbeliefs about healthy eating can be harmful. 对健康饮食的常见误解可能是有害的。

近义词：fallacy, delusion, misconception
反义词：truth, belief, fact
词性：名词
