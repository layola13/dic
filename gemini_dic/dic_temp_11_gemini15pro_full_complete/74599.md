freaking:/ˈfriːkɪŋ/| adj. adv.|该死的，非常的，吓人的|Used as a milder form of "fucking," often to express annoyance, surprise, or emphasis.|例句：This freaking traffic is making me late!  这该死的交通让我迟到了！

例句：That movie was freaking awesome! 那部电影真是太棒了！

近义词：damn, bloody, very, extremely|反义词:not at all, slightly|词性:adjective, adverb|词源:Derived from "freaking out," meaning to have a strong emotional reaction, often of fear or excitement. Over time, "freaking" became a standalone intensifier.|
