libethenite: /lɪˈbiːθəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 铜磷矿 | A naturally occurring mineral composed of hydrated copper phosphate, typically green in color. | Libethenite is a relatively rare secondary copper mineral. 铜磷矿是一种相对罕见的次生铜矿物。|  Libethenite crystals are often found in oxidized copper deposits. 铜磷矿晶体经常在氧化铜矿床中被发现。 | Synonyms: pseudomalachite, cornetite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
