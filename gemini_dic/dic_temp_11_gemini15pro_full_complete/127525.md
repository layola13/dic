necrologist:/nəˈkrɒlədʒɪst/
n.
讣告撰写人；专门撰写讣告的人
A writer of obituaries, often a journalist specializing in this field.
The necrologist wrote a moving tribute to the deceased scientist. 讣告撰写人为已故科学家写了一篇感人的悼词。
Synonyms: obituarist, death notice writer
Antonyms: biographer (focuses on the life of a living or recently deceased person)
词性: noun
