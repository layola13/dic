noncommittal:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtl/ /ˌnɑːnkəˈmɪtl/| adj.|不置可否的，不表态的，含糊的|Not expressing or revealing a commitment to a definite opinion or course of action.|Her response to the proposal was noncommittal. 她对这个提议的回应不置可否。|近义词：evasive, ambiguous, vague, indifferent, neutral|反义词:committed, definite, clear, decisive|词性:形容词
noncommittally:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtəli/ /ˌnɑːnkəˈmɪtəli/| adv.|不置可否地，含糊地|In a noncommittal manner.|He smiled noncommittally. 他不置可否地笑了笑。|近义词：evasively, ambiguously, vaguely|反义词:definitely, clearly, decisively|词性:副词
