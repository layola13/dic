headquartered:/ˈhedˌkwɔːrtərd/| adj.|总部设在…的|Having its headquarters in a specified place.  The company's main offices are located there. |Example: The company is headquartered in New York City. 这家公司的总部设在纽约市。|Example:  Many multinational corporations are headquartered in tax havens. 许多跨国公司的总部设在避税天堂。|近义词：based, located, situated|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'headquarter' (noun) + '-ed' (suffix forming adjectives). 'Headquarter' comes from 'head' (chief) + 'quarter' (lodging, place of residence).  Originally a military term referring to the location of a commanding officer's lodging.
