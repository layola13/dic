jasperize:/ˈdʒæspəˌraɪz/ |v. |碧玉化；使像碧玉|To make something resemble jasper, typically by imparting a variegated or spotted appearance.|The artist jasperized the clay pot, giving it a beautiful mottled green finish. 艺术家将陶罐碧玉化，使其呈现出美丽的斑驳绿色。|近义词: mottle, variegate, marbleize|反义词: homogenize|词性:动词
