coction:/ˈkɒkʃən/ (US) /ˈkɑːkʃən/| n.|烹煮; 煮熟; 煎熬; 混合物|the process of cooking or boiling something; something made by cooking or boiling ingredients together|The coction smelled of herbs and spices.  这种混合物闻起来有香草和香料的味道。|The chef's special coction was a delicious stew.  厨师的特色菜肴是一道美味的炖菜。|近义词：concoction, brew, mixture|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *coctiōn-, from Latin coctiō  "a cooking," from coctus, past participle of coquere "to cook, ripen."
