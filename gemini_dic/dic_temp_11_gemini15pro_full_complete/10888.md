arachnophobia:/əˌræknəˈfoʊbiə/| n. |蜘蛛恐惧症|An extreme or irrational fear of spiders.|例句：She has arachnophobia and can't even look at a picture of a spider. 她有蜘蛛恐惧症，甚至不能看蜘蛛的照片。

例句：His arachnophobia prevented him from going camping in the woods. 他的蜘蛛恐惧症使他无法去森林里露营。|近义词： fear of spiders |反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑 spiderphilia 喜爱蜘蛛) |词性: noun
