benediction:/ˌbenɪˈdɪkʃən/| n.|祝福，祈祷|An utterance of good wishes, especially a blessing.  A benediction is a prayer asking for God's blessing, usually at the end of a religious service.|The priest delivered a benediction at the end of the mass.|近义词：blessing, invocation|反义词：curse, malediction|noun
