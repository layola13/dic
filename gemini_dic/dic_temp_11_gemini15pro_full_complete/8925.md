antihegemonies:/ˈæntiˌhɪˈdʒɛməniz/
n.
反霸权；反霸权主义
forces that oppose hegemony or dominance.
例句：The conference focused on the various antihegemonies arising in response to globalization.  这次会议的重点是应对全球化而出现的各种反霸权力量。

例句：The rise of antihegemonies in the region posed a challenge to the established powers.  该地区反霸权主义的兴起对现有强权构成了挑战。
近义词：counter-hegemony, resistance, opposition
反义词：hegemony, dominance, control
词性：名词 (plural)
