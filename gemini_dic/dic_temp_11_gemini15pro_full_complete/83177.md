gutturally:/ˈɡʌtərəli/| adv.|喉音地，粗声地|Produced in the throat; harsh-sounding.  Relating to or produced in the throat.|He spoke gutturally, his voice rough and low. 他粗声粗气地说，声音低沉沙哑。|Synonyms: hoarsely, huskily, roughly, gruffly|Antonyms: melodically, smoothly, softly|adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *gutturalis, from Latin guttur ("throat").
