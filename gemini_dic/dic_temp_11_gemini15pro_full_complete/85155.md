hardto:/ˈhɑːrd tuː/ (American) /ˈhɑːd tə/ (British)| adj. |难以; 难于| difficult; challenging; not easy| It's hard to say what will happen next. 很难说接下来会发生什么。|  It's hard to learn a new language. 学习一门新的语言很难。|近义词：difficult, tough, challenging, arduous, strenuous|反义词：easy, simple, effortless|词性: 形容词 (Adjective)
词源:  From Old English *heard, from Proto-Germanic *harduz, meaning "hard, firm, strong, severe."
