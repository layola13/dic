authentic:/ɔːˈθentɪk/ | adj. | 真实的，真正的；可靠的；可信的 | Genuine; real; not fake or copied.  Of undisputed origin and not a copy; genuine. |  The document is authentic.  这份文件是真的。| 近义词: genuine, real, bona fide | 反义词: fake, counterfeit, spurious | 词性: 形容词

authentics: /ɔːˈθentɪks/ | n. | 真品，真迹 | Genuine articles or items, typically those having historical or cultural value. |  These authentics are highly prized by collectors. 这些真品受到收藏家的高度珍视。| 近义词: originals, genuine articles | 反义词: fakes, replicas, copies | 词性: 名词
