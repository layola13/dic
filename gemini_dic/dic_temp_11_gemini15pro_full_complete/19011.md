bestseller:/ˈbɛstˌsɛlər/ (US), /ˈbɛstˌsɛlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|畅销书，畅销货|A book or other product that sells in very large numbers.|The new thriller quickly became a bestseller. 这部新的惊悚小说很快成为畅销书。|synonyms: hit, blockbuster, smash hit|antonyms: flop, failure|noun
best-selling:/ˌbɛstˈsɛlɪŋ/ (US), /ˌbɛstˈsɛlɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|畅销的|Selling in very large numbers.|She is the author of several best-selling novels. 她是几部畅销小说的作者。|synonyms: popular, in-demand, hot|antonyms: unpopular, slow-selling|adjective
