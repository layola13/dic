methadone:/ˈmɛθəˌdoʊn/ (US) /ˈmɛθədən/ (UK)| n.|美沙酮|A synthetic opioid drug used as a pain reliever and as a treatment for heroin addiction. It acts as a substitute for heroin, reducing withdrawal symptoms and cravings.|The doctor prescribed methadone to help him overcome his heroin addiction. 医生给他开了美沙酮来帮助他克服海洛因成瘾。|近义词:Dolophine (brand name), opioid analgesic|反义词:naloxone (antagonist)|noun
