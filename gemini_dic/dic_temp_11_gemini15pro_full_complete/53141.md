diplomatic:/ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 外交的；圆滑的；有策略的 |  Of or concerning the profession, activity, or skill of managing international relations. Also, tactful and skillful in dealing with people. |  He gave a diplomatic answer to avoid offending anyone. 他给出了一个圆滑的答案，以免得罪任何人。 | Synonyms: tactful, discreet, polite, judicious, sensitive | Antonyms: undiplomatic, tactless, blunt, clumsy, insensitive |词性: 形容词
