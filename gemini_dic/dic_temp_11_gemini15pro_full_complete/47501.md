dearer:/ˈdɪərər (US), ˈdɪərə(r) (UK)| adj.|更昂贵的，更亲爱的|Comparative form of "dear".  Means more expensive or more beloved.|例句：This car is dearer than the other one. 这辆车比另一辆贵。

例句：My dearer friend, I miss you so much. 我亲爱的朋友，我非常想念你。|近义词：pricier, costlier, more expensive, more beloved, fonder|反义词:cheaper, less expensive, less beloved|词性:adjective
