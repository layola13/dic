depth-of-focus:/ˌdepθ əv ˈfoʊkəs/| n.|焦深，焦点深度|The depth of focus is the range of distance along the optical axis where the image appears acceptably sharp. 焦深是指沿着光轴的距离范围，在该范围内图像显得清晰可接受。|The microscope has a large depth of focus, allowing us to see more of the sample in focus at once. 这台显微镜焦深很大，可以让我们一次看到更多对焦的样品。|近义词：focal range, focal depth|反义词: N/A|名词
