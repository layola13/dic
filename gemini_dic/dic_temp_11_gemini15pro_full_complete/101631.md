irreducibilities:/ˌɪrɪˌduːsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |不可约性；不可化简性|The quality or state of being irreducible.  The fact that something cannot be reduced to a simpler form.|The irreducibilities of certain mathematical equations are key to understanding their solutions. 某些数学方程的不可约性是理解其解的关键。|irreducibility (singular form)|reducibility (often used in context, not a direct antonym)|noun

irreducible:/ˌɪrɪˈduːsəbl/| adj. |不可约的；不可化简的|Not capable of being reduced or simplified further.|This fraction is irreducible. 这个分数是不可约的。|indecomposable, unsimplifiable|reducible|adjective
