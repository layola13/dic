An ostentatious display of opinions or beliefs.  |  His attitudinarianism made him unpopular with his colleagues, who found him overly concerned with appearing virtuous. 他装腔作势的态度让他不受同事们的欢迎，同事们觉得他过分在意显得有道德。 |  dogmatism, rigidity, self-righteousness | flexibility, open-mindedness, tolerance | noun
