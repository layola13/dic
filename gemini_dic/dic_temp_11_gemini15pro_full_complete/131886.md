noncontemporaneously:/ˌnɒnkənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsli/
adverb
非同时地，不同时地
not at the same time
The two events occurred noncontemporaneously, one happening years after the other.  这两个事件发生在不同的时间，一个比另一个晚了几年。
The documents were written noncontemporaneously, reflecting different periods in history. 这些文件写于不同时期，反映了历史上的不同时期。
近义词: asynchronously, nonsimultaneously
反义词: contemporaneously, simultaneously
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + contemporary (meaning "existing, occurring, or living at the same time") + -ly (adverbial suffix)
