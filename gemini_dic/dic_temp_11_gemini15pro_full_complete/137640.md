nonubiquitous:/ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəs/
adjective
非普遍存在的，非无所不在的
Not present everywhere; not ubiquitous.
Example: Unlike oxygen, clean drinking water is nonubiquitous and requires significant infrastructure for distribution.  不像氧气，干净的饮用水并非无处不在，需要重要的基础设施来分配。
Example:  The species is nonubiquitous, found only in specific isolated regions.  这个物种并非普遍存在，只在特定的孤立区域被发现。
synonym: rare, scarce, localized, limited, uncommon, infrequent
antonym: ubiquitous, omnipresent, widespread, universal, pervasive
词性: adjective
