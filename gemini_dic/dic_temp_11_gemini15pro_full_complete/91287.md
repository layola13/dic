hydrological:/ˌhaɪdrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌhaɪdrəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/| adj.|水文的；水文学的|Relating to the study of water, especially its movement in relation to land.|The hydrological cycle is crucial for maintaining life on Earth. 地球上的水文循环对维持生命至关重要。|近义词：water-related, hydrographic|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

Etymology: From hydro- (water) + -logical (relating to the study of).  "Hydro-" comes from the Greek "hydōr" (water). "-logical" comes from the Greek "logos" (word, reason, study).
