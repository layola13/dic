moodiest: /ˈmuːdiɪst/ | adj. | 心情最差的，最喜怒无常的 |  Superlative form of "moody," meaning experiencing or expressing a lot of changes in mood.  It describes someone who exhibits mood swings more frequently or intensely than others. | She was the moodiest person I'd ever met; one minute she was laughing, the next she was in tears. 她是我见过的最喜怒无常的人；一会儿大笑，一会儿又哭了起来。|  gloomiest, sullenest, temperamental | cheerful, stable, even-tempered | adjective
