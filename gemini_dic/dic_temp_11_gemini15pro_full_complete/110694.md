linear:/ˈlɪniər/| adj.|线的，直线的，线性的|Relating to a line; straight; progressing uniformly.  Characterized by lines or resemblance to lines; of or in lines; lineal.|例句：The graph shows a linear relationship between the two variables.  图表显示了两个变量之间的线性关系。

例句：The architect preferred a more linear design for the building.  这位建筑师更喜欢这座建筑的线性设计。|近义词：straight, direct, even, uniform|反义词：nonlinear, curved, crooked, irregular|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lineāris, from Latin līnea (“line, thread”).
