commandlet:/kəˈmændlɪt/| n. |小型命令; Cmdlet; 命令小程序|A lightweight command that is used in Windows PowerShell.  A cmdlet performs a single function. |Example 1:  The `Get-Process` cmdlet retrieves a list of running processes. 获取正在运行的进程列表。

Example 2:  You can create your own custom cmdlets using C#. 你可以使用 C# 创建自己的自定义 cmdlet。|Synonym: command, script, applet|Antonym: N/A|Noun
