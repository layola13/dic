chronographically:/ˌkrɒnəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 按时间顺序地 | In a manner that arranges events in the order of their occurrence. | The events were listed chronographically in the report.  报告中按时间顺序列出了事件。|  The museum exhibits artifacts chronographically, from the Stone Age to the present day.  博物馆按时间顺序展出文物，从石器时代到现代。 | 近义词: sequentially, chronologically | 反义词: randomly, haphazardly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From chrono- (time) + -graphically (in a descriptive way).  Ultimately from Greek khronos (time) and graphein (to write).
