enfleurage:/ˌɒ̃fləˈrɑːʒ/ |n.| 花香提取法 |The process of extracting fragrances from flowers by using odorless fats.  It's a traditional method used in perfumery.| The perfumer used enfleurage to capture the delicate scent of jasmine.  调香师使用花香提取法来捕捉茉莉花的精致香味。| 近义词: extraction, infusion | 反义词: N/A | noun
