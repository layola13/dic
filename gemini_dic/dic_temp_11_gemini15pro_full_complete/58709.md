dumpy:/ˈdʌmpi/| adj.|矮胖的，粗短的|Short and stout; podgy.|She was a dumpy little woman with a kind face.|近义词：chubby, stout, plump, squat, thickset|反义词：tall, slender, slim, thin|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From dump (n.1) "heap, mound," + -y (2).  Originally "resembling a dump or mound," hence "short and thick."
