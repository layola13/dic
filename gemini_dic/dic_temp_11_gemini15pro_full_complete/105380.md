kidan:/ˈkiːdɑːn/| n.|欺骗；谎言；恶作剧|A deliberate falsehood; a hoax. |He told me some kidan about needing money for his sick mother. 他编了个谎言，说他需要钱给他生病的母亲。|例句：The story about the unicorn was a complete kidan. 关于独角兽的故事完全是个骗局。|近义词：hoax, lie, fabrication, deception|反义词：truth, fact|词性:noun

Etymology: From Amharic ክድን (kidän).
