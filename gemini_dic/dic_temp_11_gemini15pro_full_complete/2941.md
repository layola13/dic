advocatory:/ˈædvəkətɔːri/
adjective
拥护的，倡导的
Characterized by or supporting advocacy; pleading for or on behalf of a cause or person.
The organization played an advocatory role in promoting human rights.  该组织在促进人权方面发挥了倡导作用。
She delivered an advocatory speech about the importance of education. 她发表了一篇拥护教育重要性的演讲。
近义词:supportive, championing,  proponent,  pleading
反义词:oppositional, antagonistic,  dissenting
词性:adjective

advocate:/ˈædvəkət/
noun
拥护者，提倡者
A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.
He is a strong advocate for animal rights. 他是一位坚定的动物权利拥护者。
She's a passionate advocate of free speech.  她是一位充满激情的言论自由倡导者。
近义词:supporter, proponent, champion
反义词:opponent, antagonist, critic
词性:noun

advocate:/ˈædvəkeɪt/
verb
提倡，拥护
Publicly recommend or support.
The group advocates for stricter gun control laws.  该团体倡导更严格的枪支管制法。
They advocate a more balanced approach to education.  他们提倡一种更均衡的教育方式。
近义词:support, promote, champion, recommend
反义词:oppose,  contest,  resist
词性:verb


词源:
From Late Latin *advocatorius, from Latin advocatus (past participle of advocare ‘summon’), from ad- ‘to’ + vocare ‘to call’.
