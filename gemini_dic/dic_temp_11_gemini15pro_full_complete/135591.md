nonporousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈpɔːrəsnɪsɪz/
noun
无孔性，不透气性
The state or quality of being nonporous; impenetrability.
The nonporousnesses of the material made it ideal for underwater use. 这种材料的不透气性使其成为水下使用的理想选择。
Near Synonyms: impenetrability, impermeability, tightness
Antonyms: porosity, permeability, porousness
n.
