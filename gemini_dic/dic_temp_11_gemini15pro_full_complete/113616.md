macheted:/məˈʃɛtɪd/
|v.|用弯刀砍；被弯刀砍伤|
|The explorers macheted their way through the dense jungle. 探险家们用弯刀在茂密的丛林中开路。|
|The victim was brutally macheted to death. 受害者被残忍地砍死。|
|近义词: hacked, chopped, slashed|
|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 repaired, healed (指伤口))|
|词性: 动词 (past participle)|


macheted:/məˈʃɛtɪd/
|adj.|被弯刀砍过的|
|The macheted sugarcane stalks lay scattered on the ground. 被砍过的甘蔗茎散落在地上。|
|His macheted arm bled profusely. 他被砍伤的手臂血流如注。|
|近义词: chopped, hacked, cut|
|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用unharmed, intact)|
|词性: 形容词|



词源:  From machete + -ed.  Machete itself comes from Spanish *machete*, a diminutive of *macho* "male, sledgehammer," from Latin *masculus* "male."
