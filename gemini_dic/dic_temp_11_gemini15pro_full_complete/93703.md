imitators:/ˈɪmɪteɪtərz/| n.|模仿者，效仿者|A person who copies the behavior or actions of another.|Many young artists start out as imitators of their favorite painters. 许多年轻的艺术家一开始都是他们最喜欢的画家的模仿者。|Example:  The comedian was so talented that even his imitators couldn't match his wit.  这个喜剧演员才华横溢，即使是模仿他的人也无法比拟他的机智。

Example:  The market was flooded with imitators selling cheap knock-offs of the popular brand.  市场上充斥着模仿者，他们出售廉价仿冒的流行品牌。|近义词：copycats, emulators, mimics|反义词：originators, innovators|词性:noun
