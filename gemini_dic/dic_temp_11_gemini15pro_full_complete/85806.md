hazardously:/ˈhæzərdəsli/

Example 2: The hiker hazardously climbed the steep cliff without any safety equipment.  这名徒步旅行者在没有任何安全设备的情况下危险地攀登了陡峭的悬崖。|Synonym: dangerously, precariously, riskily, recklessly|Antonym: safely, securely, cautiously|词性:副词
