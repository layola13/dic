epiphonema:/ˌepɪˈfɪːnɪmə/ | n. | 警句；格言；结语；（尤指）演说中总结性的精辟语句 | A concluding summarizing sentence of a speech, especially one adding emphasis or emotional appeal.  |  At the end of his speech, he delivered a powerful epiphonema that left the audience in awe. 在他演讲的结尾，他发表了一句强有力的警句，让观众肃然起敬。| Example:  The speaker concluded with a stirring epiphonema about the importance of education. 演讲者以一句关于教育重要性的激动人心的警句结束了演讲。 | Synonyms:  maxim, adage, aphorism, apothegm | Antonyms:  preface, introduction | noun
