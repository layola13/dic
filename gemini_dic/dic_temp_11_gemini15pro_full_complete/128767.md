nighed:/naɪd/
verb
(of a horse) neighed; made the characteristic sound of a horse.
The horse nighed impatiently as its rider prepared to mount.  马在骑手准备上马时不耐烦地嘶鸣。
近义词: whinnied, snorted
反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal sound)
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *neighen, *neien, from Old English *hnǣgan, from Proto-Germanic *խնægōną (“to neigh”). Cognate with West Frisian neije (“to neigh”), Dutch neighen (“to neigh”), German wiehern (“to neigh”), Swedish gnägga (“to neigh”), Icelandic hneggja (“to neigh”).
