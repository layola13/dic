mammonisms:/ˈmæmənɪzəmz/
n.
拜金主义；贪婪行为
Excessive devotion to or pursuit of wealth, often associated with materialism and greed.
He condemned the mammonisms of modern society.  他谴责现代社会的拜金主义。
Near Synonyms: materialism, greed, avarice, cupidity, acquisitiveness
Antonyms:  altruism, generosity, philanthropy, selflessness
