epicedium:/ˌɛpɪˈsiːdiəm/ | n. | 挽歌；悼词；哀歌 | A song of mourning or praise for the dead. |  The poet composed a moving epicedium for his deceased friend. 这位诗人为他去世的朋友创作了一首感人的挽歌。 |  elegy, lament, dirge |  encomium, panegyric | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *epicedium*, from Greek *epikēdeion*, from *epi* (upon) + *kēdeos* (mourning).
