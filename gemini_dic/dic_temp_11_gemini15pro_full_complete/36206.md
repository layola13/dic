cogitable:/ˈkɒdʒɪtəbəl/ (US) /ˈkɑːdʒɪtəbl/| adj.|可思考的，可以想的|Something that is cogitable is capable of being thought or considered.|例句：The existence of extraterrestrial life is a cogitable concept.  外星生命的存在是一个可以思考的概念。

例句：While the consequences are dire, they are cogitable and we must prepare. 虽然后果很可怕，但它们是可以预见的，我们必须做好准备。|近义词：thinkable, conceivable, imaginable|反义词：inconceivable, unimaginable|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *cogitabilis, from Latin cogitō (“I think, consider, ponder, reflect”), frequentative of cogō (“I compel, force, collect”). Equivalent to cogit(ate) + -able.
