gaged:/ɡeɪdʒd/ | adj. | 已订婚的；被测量的；被雇佣的 | Engaged means having formally agreed to marry. It can also mean busy or occupied, or having made a commitment to do something. |  例句：They are gaged to be married next spring. 他们已订婚，明年春天结婚。

例句：The engineer was gaged in measuring the depth of the river. 工程师忙于测量河的深度。| 近义词：betrothed, affianced (for marriage); employed, hired (for work); occupied, busy (for general use) | 反义词：single, unattached (for marriage); unemployed (for work); free, unoccupied (for general use) | 词性:形容词


gaged:/ɡeɪdʒd/ | v. | 测量；雇佣；与…啮合 |  The past participle form of "gage."  It can mean having measured something, employed someone, or caused something to interlock or mesh with something else. | 例句：The carpenter had gaged the wood before cutting it. 木匠在切割木头之前已经测量好了。

例句：The company gaged ten new employees last month. 公司上个月雇佣了十名新员工。 | 近义词：measured, employed, hired, interlocked | 反义词：unmeasured, unemployed, disengaged | 词性:动词

词源：From Old French "gagier" meaning "to pledge."
