homelesses:/ˈhoʊmlɪsɪz/| n.|无家可归的人（复数，罕用）|A group of people who are homeless.  This plural form is considered non-standard and "homeless people" is generally preferred. |There were many homelesses huddled together for warmth. (Non-standard usage) 许多无家可归的人挤在一起取暖。|近义词：homeless people, the homeless, people experiencing homelessness|反义词: housed people, residents|词性:Noun


homeless:/ˈhoʊmlɪs/| adj.|无家可归的|Without a home.|The homeless man slept on a park bench.  这个无家可归的男人睡在公园的长椅上。|近义词：destitute, vagrant|反义词: housed, sheltered|词性:Adjective
