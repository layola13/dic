caprylic:/kəˈprɪlɪk/ | adj. | 辛酸的；羊脂酸的；辛基的 | Relating to or derived from caprylic acid (octanoic acid), a saturated fatty acid found in the milk of goats and other mammals, as well as coconut oil and palm kernel oil. | Caprylic acid is used in the production of esters used in perfumery and also in the manufacture of dyes. 辛酸用于生产用于香料的酯，也用于制造染料。|  Caprylic acid triglycerides are being investigated for their potential health benefits. 辛酸甘油三酯的潜在健康益处正在研究中。| synonyms: octanoic | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider unsaturated fatty acids as a contrasting group) | word type: adjective
