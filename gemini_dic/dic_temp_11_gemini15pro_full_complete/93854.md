immobilize: /ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbəlaɪz/ (UK) | v. | 使不动；使固定；使无法活动 | To prevent something or someone from moving or operating. | The doctor immobilized his broken leg with a cast. 医生用石膏固定了他骨折的腿。|  The heavy snow immobilized traffic. 大雪使交通瘫痪。|  Synonyms: disable, cripple, paralyze | Antonyms: mobilize, activate, free | Verb

Etymology:
From im- (not) + mobilis (movable) + -ize (to make).  Ultimately from Latin *mobilis, from movēre (to move).
