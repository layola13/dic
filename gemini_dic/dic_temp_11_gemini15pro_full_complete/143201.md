outcrafty: /ˌaʊtˈkræfti/
verb
以智取胜，用计谋战胜
to defeat someone by being more clever or cunning than they are
He outcrafted his opponents in the negotiations and secured a favorable deal. 他在谈判中以智取胜，达成了有利的协议。
outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
be outwitted by, be outsmarted by
