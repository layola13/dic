luteous:/ˈluːtiəs/| adj.|金黄色的，黄色的|Of a deep golden yellow color; saffron-colored.|例句：The luteous flowers brightened the meadow. 金黄色的花朵照亮了草地。|例句：The butterfly's wings were a striking luteous hue. 蝴蝶的翅膀呈现出醒目的金黄色。|近义词：golden, yellow, flaxen, aureate|反义词：dark, dull|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *luteus*, from *lūtum* "yellow weed, weld" (used as a yellow dye), related to Greek *khlōros* "pale green, yellowish".
