chicanery:/ʃɪˈkeɪnəri/
n.
诡计；欺骗；狡辩
The use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose.
例句：The lawyer used legal chicanery to get his client off. 律师运用法律上的诡计使他的客户脱罪。

例句：The company's success was based on chicanery and deceit. 这家公司的成功建立在诡计和欺骗之上。

近义词：trickery, deception, subterfuge, guile, fraud, deceit
反义词：honesty, integrity, forthrightness
词性：noun
