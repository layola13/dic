countersteps:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛps/| n. |反击；对策；应对措施| Actions taken to oppose or counteract something else. | The government took countersteps to prevent the spread of the virus. 政府采取了应对措施来防止病毒传播。| The company developed countersteps to address the competitor's aggressive marketing campaign.  公司制定了对策来应对竞争对手咄咄逼人的营销活动。 |countermeasures, responses| reactions, inaction |noun|

counterstep:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛp/| v. |采取反击；采取对策| To take action to oppose or counteract something. |The army counterstepped the enemy's advance. 军队采取反击阻止了敌人的前进。|  The company counterstepped the negative publicity with a series of positive advertisements.  公司通过一系列正面广告来反击负面宣传。|counteract, oppose|  submit, yield|verb|
