lutose:/ˈluːtoʊs/ | adj. | 泥泞的；多泥的 | Covered in or full of mud; muddy. | The farmland was lutose after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，农田泥泞不堪。|  The path became lutose and slippery. 小路变得泥泞而滑。| 近义词: muddy, miry, swampy | 反义词: dry, arid | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *lutosus, from lutum (“mud”).
