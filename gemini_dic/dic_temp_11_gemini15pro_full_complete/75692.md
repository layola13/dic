fulgury:/ˈfʌlɡjʊri/ /ˈfʊlɡəri/ | n. |闪电，雷电；闪光；辉煌|A sudden flash or burst of light, especially lightning; brilliance. | The fulgury of the explosion lit up the night sky. 爆炸的闪光照亮了夜空。| 近义词：flash, lightning, brilliance, gleam, glitter | 反义词：darkness, dullness | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin *fulgur* "lightning," from the same root as *fulgere* "to shine, flash, glitter."
