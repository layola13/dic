nan's:/nænz/ | n. | 奶奶的（东西）; 老奶奶（的） |  Contraction of "nan's", meaning something that belongs to or is associated with a grandmother (nan).  This is primarily a British English term. | That's nan's favorite chair. 那是奶奶最喜欢的椅子。| Nan's knitting is beautiful. 奶奶的编织很漂亮。| 近义词: grandma's, grandmother's | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially grandpa's/grandfather's if contrasting ownership) | 词性: noun (possessive)
