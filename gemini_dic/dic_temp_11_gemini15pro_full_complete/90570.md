huia:/ˈhuːiə/ | n. | 惠亚鸟（一种已灭绝的新西兰鸟类） | An extinct wattlebird (Heteralocha acutirostris) of New Zealand, the male of which had a short, stout beak, and the female a long, curved beak. |  The huia was once a treasured symbol of New Zealand. 惠亚鸟曾经是新西兰珍贵的象征。| The last confirmed sighting of a huia was in 1907.  最后一次确认看到惠亚鸟是在1907年。 | 近义词: wattlebird (垂肉鸟), extinct bird (灭绝的鸟) | 反义词: extant bird (现存的鸟) | 词性: noun
