dazedly:/ˈdeɪzɪdli/ | adv. | 茫然地，恍惚地 | In a dazed and confused manner, as if stunned or bewildered. | He looked around dazedly, trying to remember where he was. 他茫然地环顾四周，试图想起自己在哪里。|  She wandered dazedly through the streets after the accident. 事故发生后，她恍惚地走在街上。| 近义词: confusedly, bewilderedly, blankly | 反义词: alertly, clearly, attentively | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "dazed" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Dazed" comes from the Old Norse word "dasa" meaning "to become exhausted or weary."
