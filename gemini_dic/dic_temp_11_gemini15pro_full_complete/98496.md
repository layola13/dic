instancing:/ˈɪnstənsɪŋ/ | n. | 实例化，创建实例 | The process of creating multiple copies of an object, often in computer graphics or programming.  This allows for efficient rendering or processing of many similar items without needing to store each individually. | In this game, instancing is used to render the thousands of trees in the forest.  在这个游戏中，实例化被用来渲染森林里的数千棵树。 | synonyms: replication, duplication, instantiation | antonyms: singularity, uniqueness | noun

instanced:/ˈɪnstənst/ | adj. |  被引为例的，被提及的 |  Given as an example. | This problem, instanced by the recent data breach, highlights the need for better security.  最近的数据泄露事件就是这个问题的一个例子，它突出了加强安全性的必要性。 | synonyms: exemplified, cited, mentioned | antonyms: unmentioned, overlooked, ignored | adjective
