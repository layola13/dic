nonexploitable:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可利用的，无法被剥削的 | Not able to be exploited or taken advantage of. |  The company's security systems are nonexploitable, making it difficult for hackers to gain access.  这家公司的安全系统是不可利用的，这使得黑客难以获得访问权限。

The vulnerability discovered in the software was thankfully nonexploitable.  幸运的是，在软件中发现的漏洞是不可利用的。

| 近义词：secure, invulnerable, safe,  impregnable | 反义词：exploitable, vulnerable, susceptible | 词性: adjective


词源:

non- + exploitable

*   non-:  prefix meaning "not"
*   exploitable: from exploit (v.) -  from Old French *esploitier, from Latin explicitus "unfolded, set forth," past participle of explicare "unfold, explain"
