behalf:/bɪˈhɑːf/ /bɪˈhæf/| n.|代表，利益|A person's behalf is their interest or their representation. |例句：On behalf of the entire company, I welcome you. 我代表全公司欢迎你。

例句：She spoke on her daughter's behalf. 她代表她的女儿发言。

近义词：interest, benefit, part, side, stead|反义词:|词性:名词
