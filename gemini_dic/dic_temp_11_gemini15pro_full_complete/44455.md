cross-tongue:/ˈkrɒsˌtʌŋ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌtʌŋ/ (US)| n. |交叉舌奏；交叉吹奏；(演奏管乐器时)用舌头快速断续地控制气流|A technique used in playing wind instruments, especially brass instruments, involving a rapid fluttering of the tongue against the mouthpiece or reed to produce a trill or other special effect.|The trumpet player used cross-tonguing to create a distinctive sound. 小号手使用交叉舌奏来创造一种独特的声音。| 近义词:double-tonguing, flutter-tonguing|反义词:single-tonguing|词性:名词

cross-tongue:/ˈkrɒsˌtʌŋ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌtʌŋ/ (US)| v. |(演奏管乐器时)交叉舌奏；交叉吹奏|(Of a wind instrument player) to use cross-tonguing.|He cross-tongued the passage, creating a shimmering effect. 他用交叉舌奏法演奏了这段乐曲，营造出一种闪烁的效果。|近义词:double-tongue, flutter-tongue|反义词:single-tongue|词性:动词
