interspersedly:/ˌɪntərˈspɜːrsɪdli/
adv.
散布地，点缀地
scattered or distributed among other things; with intervals between
The text is interspersedly with illustrations.  正文中散布着插图。
近义词: here and there, sporadically, occasionally, at intervals
反义词: continuously, constantly, without interruption
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From intersperse + -ly.  Intersperse comes from Latin interspersus, past participle of interspergere "to scatter among," from inter- "among" + spargere "to scatter."
