conifers:/ˈkɑːnɪfərz/ (US), /ˈkɒnɪfəz/ (UK)| n. |针叶树|A tree that bears cones and evergreen needlelike or scalelike leaves.  Most conifers are cone-bearing seed plants with vascular tissue; these are woody plants, the great majority being trees with just a few being shrubs.|例句：Conifers are a dominant plant form in the northern hemisphere. 针叶树是北半球主要的植物类型。

|近义词：evergreens, softwoods|反义词:deciduous trees|词性:noun
