blooper:/ˈbluːpər/| n.|失误，洋相，穿帮镜头|An embarrassing mistake, especially one made in public or on television or radio.|例句：The blooper reel at the end of the movie was hilarious.  电影结尾的穿帮镜头集锦非常搞笑。

例句：He made a terrible blooper when he called the prime minister by the wrong name. 他把首相的名字叫错了，犯了一个严重的错误。|近义词：mistake, error, gaffe, slip-up|反义词:success, triumph|词性:noun
