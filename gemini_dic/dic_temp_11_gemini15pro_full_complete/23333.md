boyo:/ˈbɔɪ.oʊ/| n.|小子，伙计 (often used as a term of endearment or familiarity, sometimes slightly condescending)|A term of address for a boy or young man, often used affectionately or playfully, but can sometimes be perceived as patronizing. |"Hey there, boyo, how's it going?" 嘿，小子，最近怎么样？|"Well, boyo, looks like you've got yourself in a bit of a pickle." 好吧，伙计，看来你有点麻烦了。|近义词：lad, chap, fellow, guy|反义词: (No direct opposite, but perhaps "girl" in certain contexts)|noun
