controller:/kənˈtroʊlər/ (US) /kənˈtrəʊlə(r)/ (UK)| n. |控制器；管理员；主计员|The air traffic controller guided the plane to a safe landing.  空中交通管制员引导飞机安全着陆。|近义词：administrator, manager, regulator|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


**词源:**

From Old French *controleor*, from *controler* ("to check, verify, compare with a duplicate register"), from Medieval Latin *contrarotulus* (“counter-roll”), from *contra- + rotulus* (“roll”), from Latin *rota* ("wheel").  The original sense was of checking against a duplicate register to verify accounts.
