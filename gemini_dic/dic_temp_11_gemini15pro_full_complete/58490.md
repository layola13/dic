due:/duː/
adj.|到期的，预期的，应得的|owed as a debt or obligation; expected or scheduled.|The rent is due at the end of the month. 月底房租到期。|近义词: payable, owing, expected, scheduled|反义词: overdue, early, unscheduled
adj.|适当的，合适的|appropriate or fitting.|After due consideration, we decided to accept the offer.  经过适当的考虑，我们决定接受这个提议。|近义词: appropriate, proper, fitting, suitable|反义词: inappropriate, unsuitable, improper
adv.|正对着|directly; exactly.|The ship sailed due east. 船向正东航行。|近义词: directly, straight, exactly|反义词: indirectly


due:/duː/
n.|应得之物，应付款|something that is owed or due.|He received his due reward. 他得到了应有的回报。|近义词: right, entitlement, debt|反义词: undue


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deu, *du, past participle of devoir “to owe,” from Latin dēbēre “to owe.”
