hyperperistalsis:/ˌhaɪpərˌperɪˈstɔːlsɪs/ (US), /ˌhaɪpəˌpɛrɪˈstɒlsɪs/ (UK)
noun
亢进的蠕动
Excessive rapidity of the passage of food through the intestines.
Example 1:  The patient experienced hyperperistalsis after eating the contaminated food. 病人吃了受污染的食物后，经历了肠蠕动亢进。

Example 2: Hyperperistalsis can be a symptom of various digestive disorders. 肠蠕动亢进可能是各种消化系统疾病的症状。

synonym
increased peristalsis, rapid intestinal transit
antonym
hypoperistalsis, sluggish bowels
n.
