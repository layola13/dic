incapsulated: /ɪnˈkæpsjəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被封装的，被包裹的 | Enclosed in a capsule or other protective covering; summarized concisely. | The radioactive material was incapsulated in a lead container. 放射性物质被封装在一个铅容器中。 | The main points of the lecture were incapsulated in a handout. 演讲的要点被概括在一份讲义里。 |  近义词: enclosed, encased, encapsulated, summarized, condensed | 反义词: exposed, unenclosed, open | 词性: 形容词

incapsulate: /ɪnˈkæpsjəleɪt/ | v. | 封装，概括 | To enclose in a capsule or other protective covering; to summarize or condense. |  The company incapsulates the vitamins in a gel. 这家公司将维生素封装在凝胶中。| He incapsulated the entire story in a single sentence. 他用一句话概括了整个故事。 | 近义词: enclose, encase, summarize, condense, epitomize | 反义词: expose, open | 词性: 动词
