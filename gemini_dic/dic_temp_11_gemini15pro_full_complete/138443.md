nouveau:/nuːˈvoʊ/ (US), /ˈnuːvəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|新颖的，新式的|Modern; recently introduced, especially of a fashionable but often short-lived kind.| The restaurant features a nouveau cuisine menu. 这家餐厅以新式菜肴为特色。|近义词：modern, contemporary, newfangled|反义词：old, traditional, established|词性:形容词

Etymology: French, 'new'.  Ultimately from Latin 'novus'.
