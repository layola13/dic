leaves-off:/liːvz ɒf/
| phrasal verb |停止，中断，不再继续|The rain leaves off and the sun comes out. 雨停了，太阳出来了。|The story leaves off at a crucial moment. 故事在一个关键时刻中断了。|cease, stop, discontinue|continue, persist, carry on|verb phrase

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The rain often leaves off in the afternoon. 下午雨经常停。
He leaves off work at 5 pm every day. 他每天下午5点下班。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The rain is leaving off now. 雨现在正在停。
He is leaving off work early today. 他今天早点下班。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The rain has left off. 雨已经停了。
He has left off smoking. 他已经戒烟了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
It has been leaving off and on all day.  雨断断续续地下了一整天。
He has been leaving off work early this week. 他这周一直早下班。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The rain left off suddenly. 雨突然停了。
He left off work early yesterday. 他昨天早退了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The rain was leaving off when we arrived. 我们到达时，雨正在停。
He was leaving off work when I called. 我打电话时他正要下班。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The rain had left off before we arrived. 我们到达之前雨已经停了。
He had left off smoking before he got sick. 他生病前已经戒烟了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
It had been leaving off and on all day before the sun finally came out.  太阳终于出来之前，雨断断续续地下了一整天。
He had been leaving off work early for a week before his boss noticed.  在他老板注意到之前，他已经早退一个星期了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The rain will leave off soon. 雨很快就会停。
He will leave off work early tomorrow. 他明天会早点下班。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time tomorrow, the rain will be leaving off. 明天这个时候，雨将会停。
He will be leaving off work early next week. 他下周将会早点下班。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, he will have left off smoking. 到下周，他就戒烟了。
The rain will have left off by the time we arrive. 我们到达时，雨就会停了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, he will have been leaving off work early for a year. 到下个月，他早退就一年了。
By tomorrow, the rain will have been leaving off and on for three days. 到明天，这雨断断续续地下三天了。


虚拟语气:
If the rain left off, we could go for a walk. 如果雨停了，我们就可以去散步了。
I wish the rain would leave off. 我希望雨停了。


被动语态 (一般来说，"leave off" 不用于被动语态, 但可以有类似的表达)
Work was discontinued for the day. 当天的工作停止了。
The project was abandoned.  这个项目被放弃了。




疑问句:
When did the rain leave off? 雨是什么时候停的？
Why did he leave off work early? 他为什么早退？



否定句:
The rain didn't leave off until late at night. 雨直到深夜才停。
He didn't leave off smoking, despite his doctor's advice.  他不顾医生的劝告，没有戒烟。


PHRASE->
leave off work  下班
leave off doing something  停止做某事
leave off talking  停止说话
leave something off  遗漏某事
leave off complaining  停止抱怨


注意事项:  "leaves off" 主要用于指停止一个活动或进程，而非停止在一个位置。  虽然可以用来指雨停，但通常不用来指车辆停止。
