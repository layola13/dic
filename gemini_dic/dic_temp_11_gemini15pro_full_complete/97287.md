influx:/ˈɪnflʌks/| n.|涌入；汇集；流入|A sudden arrival or increase in the number or amount of something.|例句：The influx of tourists during the summer months strains the local resources. 夏季游客的涌入给当地资源带来了压力。

例句：There has been a large influx of immigrants into the city.  该市涌入了大量的移民。|近义词：inflow, arrival, increase, surge, rush|反义词：outflow, departure, decrease, decline|名词
