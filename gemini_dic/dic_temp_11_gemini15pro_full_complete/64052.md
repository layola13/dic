epitomic:/ˌɛpɪˈtɒmɪk/ | adj. | 典型的，摘要的；代表性的 | Representing the essential characteristics of something; being a typical or perfect example. |  This painting is epitomic of the Impressionist style. 这幅画是印象派风格的典型代表。| The dancer's graceful movements were epitomic of the ballet. 舞者的优雅动作是芭蕾舞的缩影。 |  representative, typical, exemplary, quintessential | atypical, unusual, anomalous | adjective

epitome:/ɪˈpɪtəmi/ | n. | 典型，缩影，摘要 | A person or thing that is a perfect example of a particular quality or type; a summary of a written work. |  She is the epitome of elegance. 她优雅的典范。 | This passage is the epitome of the entire book. 这段文字概括了整本书的内容。|  essence, embodiment, quintessence, archetype |  expansion, enlargement,  opposite | noun
