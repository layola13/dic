mediative:/ˈmiːdiətɪv/| adj.|调解的，斡旋的；沉思的，冥想的| tending to mediate or reconcile; conducive to meditation|例句：The monk led a mediative life, spending hours in quiet contemplation.  和尚过着冥想的生活，在安静的沉思中度过数小时。

例句：The community center offers mediative services for disputing neighbors. 社区中心为有争议的邻居提供调解服务。|近义词：contemplative, reflective, thoughtful, arbitrating, conciliatory|反义词：aggressive, confrontational|词性:形容词
