nonpenetrating:/ˌnɒnˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非穿透的，非渗透的 | Not entering or passing through something; unable to penetrate. |  The doctor confirmed it was a nonpenetrating head injury. 医生确认这是非穿透性头部损伤。|  The nonpenetrating stain was easy to clean.  这种非渗透性污渍很容易清洗。 | 近义词: noninvasive, superficial, external | 反义词: penetrating, invasive, piercing | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + penetrating (from Latin penetrare, "to penetrate")
