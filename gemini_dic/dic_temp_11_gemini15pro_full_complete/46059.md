cycloganoid:/ˌsaɪkləˈɡeɪnɔɪd/ | n. |环类化合物|A natural substance with a cyclic structure, often found in plants and having physiological activity.  Relating to or denoting a class of natural products characterized by a cyclic structure.  |Example: Researchers isolated a novel cycloganoid from the bark of a rainforest tree. 研究人员从一种雨林树的树皮中分离出一种新的环类化合物。

Example: Cycloganoids are a promising area of research for new pharmaceuticals. 环类化合物是新药研究的一个有前景的领域。|近义词:cyclic compound, cycloalkane (in some contexts) |反义词:acyclic compound (broadly), linear compound (in some contexts) |词性:noun
