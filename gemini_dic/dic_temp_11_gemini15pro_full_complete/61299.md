embargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US) /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. vt.|禁运，禁令；禁止发表或出版|An official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.|The US imposed an embargo on trade with Cuba.|近义词：ban, prohibition, restriction, sanction|反义词: allowance, permission|noun, verb


embargoes:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊz/ (US) /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊz/ (UK)| n.|禁运，禁令（embargo的复数形式）|Plural form of embargo, referring to multiple official bans on trade or other commercial activity with particular countries.|The UN has imposed several embargoes on arms sales to certain nations.|近义词：bans, prohibitions, restrictions, sanctions|反义词: allowances, permissions|noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *embargar* "to impede, restrain," from *em-*, here perhaps intensive, + *barra* "bar."
