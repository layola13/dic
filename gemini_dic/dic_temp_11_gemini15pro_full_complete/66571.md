exhilarate: /ɪɡˈzɪləreɪt/ (US) /ɪɡˈzɪləreɪt/ (UK)
verb
使高兴；使兴奋；使激动
To make someone feel very happy, animated, or elated.
The news of their victory exhilarated the entire team.  他们的胜利的消息让整个团队兴奋不已。

The roller coaster ride exhilarated the children. 过山车让孩子们兴奋不已。


近义词: thrill, excite, stimulate, invigorate, enliven
反义词: depress, discourage, dishearten, dampen, bore
词性: verb
