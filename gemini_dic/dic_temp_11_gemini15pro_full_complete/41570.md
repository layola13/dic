corporatenesses:/ˈkɔːrpərɪtnɪsɪz/
n.
公司特性；企业文化
The quality or state of being corporate, especially in a large company or group.  This often refers to the culture, values, and behaviors associated with a corporation.
The corporateness of the company stifled creativity and innovation. 这家公司的企业文化扼杀了创造力和创新。

近义词: corporate culture, corporate identity, corporate image
反义词: individuality, independence
