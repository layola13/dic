goshawk:/ˈɡɒs.hɔːk/ (US: /ˈɡɑːs.hɑːk/) | n. |苍鹰|A medium-sized to large bird of prey in the family Accipitridae.  Known for its powerful flight and hunting prowess.|例句：The goshawk soared high above the forest canopy. 苍鹰在森林树冠上空翱翔。

|例句：A goshawk swooped down and caught a rabbit. 一只苍鹰俯冲下来，抓住了一只兔子。

|近义词：raptor, hawk, falcon|反义词:prey|词性:noun
