dough:/dō/ | n. | 面团；钱；<俚>生面团似的东西 | A mixture of flour, water, and sometimes yeast, fat, or sugar, that is kneaded and baked to make bread, pastry, etc.  Also used informally to refer to money. |  Example 1: She kneaded the dough for the pizza. 她揉披萨面团。

Example 2: He doesn't have much dough to spend on a new car. 他没多少钱买新车。 | 近义词: money (俚语), batter (用于较稀的面糊), paste | 反义词: None (没有明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dāg, from Proto-Germanic *daigaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeyǵʰ- (“to knead”). Cognate with Old Frisian dei (West Frisian daai), Middle Dutch deech (Dutch deeg), Old High German teic (German Teig), Old Norse deig.
