nurselike:/ˈnɜːrslaɪk/ | adj. | 像护士的；有护士风范的；体贴的 | Resembling or characteristic of a nurse, especially in being caring and attentive. |  She had a nurselike way about her, always ready to help and comfort others. 她有一种像护士一样的气质，总是乐于助人，安慰他人。|  He spoke to the injured child in a gentle, nurselike tone. 他用温柔的、像护士一样的语气对受伤的孩子说话。| 近义词: caring, compassionate, attentive, solicitous | 反义词: uncaring, inconsiderate, neglectful | 词性: 形容词

词源: nurse (n.) + -like (suffix).  The suffix "-like" forms adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
