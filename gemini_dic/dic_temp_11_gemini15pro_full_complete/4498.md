albinotic:/ˌælbɪˈnɒtɪk/ | adj. |白化的，患白化病的 | Affected by albinism, a genetic condition characterized by a lack of melanin pigment, resulting in white or pale skin, hair, and eyes. |  The albinotic rabbit stood out against the dark green grass. 白化的兔子在深绿色的草地上很显眼。

The albinotic child was sensitive to sunlight.  白化病儿童对阳光敏感。| 近义词: amelanistic, amelanic | 反义词: pigmented | 词性: 形容词
