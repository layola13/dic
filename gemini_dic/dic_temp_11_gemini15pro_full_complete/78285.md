germicidal:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈsaɪdəl/
adjective
杀菌的
Capable of killing germs (bacteria, viruses, etc.).
This hand sanitizer is germicidal and effective against a wide range of bacteria.  这种洗手液具有杀菌作用，可有效对抗多种细菌。
The UV light has germicidal properties.  紫外线具有杀菌特性。
synonyms:antibacterial, antimicrobial, antiseptic, disinfectant
antonyms:bacteriostatic (抑制细菌生长,但不杀死)
