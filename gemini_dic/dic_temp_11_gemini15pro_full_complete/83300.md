gyneceum:/ɡaɪˈniːsiəm/ /dʒɪˈniːsiəm/
n.
雌蕊群; 雌蕊; 妇女的房间
the female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of one or more pistils.  A room or building reserved for women, especially in ancient Greece.
The botanist examined the gyneceum of the orchid.  植物学家检查了兰花的雌蕊群。
The women gathered in the gyneceum to discuss household matters.  妇女们聚集在妇女的房间里讨论家务事。
synonym: pistil, carpel
antonym: androecium


词性: noun


词源:  From Ancient Greek γυνή (gunḗ, “woman”) + οἶκος (oîkos, “house”).
