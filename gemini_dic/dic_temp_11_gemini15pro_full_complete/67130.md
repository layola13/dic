exportable:/ɪkˈspɔːrtəbl/ /ekˈspɔːrtəbl/| adj.|可出口的，适合出口的|Suitable or legally permitted to be exported.|例句：The country's exportable surplus of grain has increased this year. 该国今年可出口的谷物盈余有所增加。

例句：These products are highly exportable because of their low cost. 这些产品因成本低廉而极易出口。|近义词：tradeable, marketable, shippable|反义词：unexportable, non-exportable|词性:形容词
