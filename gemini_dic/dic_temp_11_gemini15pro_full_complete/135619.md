nonpracticability:/ˌnɒnpræktɪkəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可行性，不切实际|The nonpracticability of the plan was evident from the start. 该计划从一开始就显而易见不可行。|Example: Due to the nonpracticability of the proposed solution, the team had to rethink their strategy.  由于提议的解决方案不可行，团队不得不重新考虑他们的策略。|Synonyms: infeasibility, impossibility, impracticality|Antonyms: feasibility, practicality, possibility|Noun
