interavailable:/ˌɪntərəˈveɪləbəl/
adjective
相互可用的；可互换的
able to be used or accessed by each other or in different situations.
The two systems are interavailable, allowing seamless data transfer. 这两个系统相互可用，可以无缝传输数据。
The software is interavailable on both Windows and Mac platforms.  该软件在Windows和Mac平台上都可用。
近义词: compatible, interchangeable, exchangeable
反义词: incompatible, non-interchangeable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
inter- (prefix) + available

inter-:  Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
available: from avail + -able.  Avail is from Old French *availlir "to be of use, profit," from Latin advalere "to be strong, be worth," from ad- "to" + valere "be strong."
