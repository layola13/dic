carcel:/ˈkɑːrsɛl/| n.|监狱，牢房|A prison, a jail.  A type of lamp, especially one used in lighthouses. |The king threw him in the carcel for treason. 国王以叛国罪将他投入监狱。 The carcel lamp was a significant advancement in lighthouse technology. 卡塞尔灯是灯塔技术的一项重大进步。|近义词：prison, jail, gaol, penitentiary, lockup, dungeon (for older prisons) |反义词:freedom, liberty|noun
