marigold: /ˈmærɪɡoʊld/ (US), /ˈmærɪɡɒld/ (UK) | n. | 万寿菊; 金盏花 | A plant with bright yellow or orange flowers, often used in gardens.  It is also used in some cultures for medicinal purposes and as a dye. |  She planted marigolds along the border of the garden. 她沿着花园的边界种了万寿菊。

Marigolds are known for their vibrant colors. 万寿菊以其鲜艳的颜色而闻名。


近义词: calendula, pot marigold | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
