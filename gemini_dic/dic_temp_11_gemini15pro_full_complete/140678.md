omigod:/oʊ maɪ ˈɡɑːd/ |interj.|我的天哪；哦，我的上帝| An exclamation of surprise, shock, or dismay.  It's a shortened and informal version of "Oh my God." | Omigod, I forgot my keys! 我的天哪，我忘了带钥匙了！| 近义词: oh my god, oh my gosh, goodness me, heavens above | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation, but could consider expressions of calmness like "no problem," "that's alright") | 词性: interjection
