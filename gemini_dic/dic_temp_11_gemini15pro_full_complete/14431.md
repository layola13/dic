aux:/ɔːɡz/ |auxiliary|辅助的; 辅助人员; 辅助设备|Used to describe something that provides additional help or support.  It can also refer to a person who provides such help, or a device designed for a supporting function.|The aux cable connects your phone to the car stereo. 辅助线缆连接你的手机和汽车音响。| synonyms:  supplementary, additional, reserve, backup | antonyms: primary, main, principal | adjective
