deductively:/dɪˈdʌktɪvli/
adverb
演绎地
by way of deduction; by reasoning from general principles to particular instances.
The detective deductively reasoned that the killer must have known the victim.  侦探通过演绎推理，认为凶手一定认识受害者。
Sherlock Holmes solved the mystery deductively. 夏洛克·福尔摩斯通过演绎推理解决了这个谜团。
Synonym: logically, rationally, analytically
Antonym: inductively, empirically
词性: 副词
