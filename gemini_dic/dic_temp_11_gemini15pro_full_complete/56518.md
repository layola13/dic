domicile:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK), /ˈdɑːməsaɪl/ (US)| n. v.|住所，居所；(v.)使定居|A person's domicile is the place where they live permanently or for a considerable period of time, regardless of where they may temporarily be staying.|He established his domicile in France. 他在法国定居。|近义词：residence, abode, dwelling, home|反义词：N/A|词性: noun, verb
domicile:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK), /ˈdɑːməsaɪl/ (US)| v.|使定居|To establish a domicile means to make a place one's permanent residence.|The company is domiciled in Bermuda. 该公司位于百慕大。|近义词：settle, reside, inhabit|反义词：emigrate|词性: verb
