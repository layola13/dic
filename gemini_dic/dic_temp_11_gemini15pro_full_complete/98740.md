insurrectionary:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃəˌnɛri/
adjective
暴动的；起义的；叛乱的
Relating to or engaging in insurrection; rebellious.
例句：The insurrectionary group plotted to overthrow the government.  这个暴动团体密谋推翻政府。

例句：Insurrectionary sentiments were brewing among the oppressed population. 被压迫的人民中酝酿着起义的情绪。

近义词：rebellious, revolutionary, mutinous, seditious
反义词：loyal, obedient, submissive
词性：adjective


insurrectionary:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃəˌnɛri/
noun
暴动者；起义者；叛乱者
A person who engages in insurrection; a rebel.
例句：The insurrectionaries stormed the government building. 暴动者冲进了政府大楼。

例句：Several insurrectionaries were arrested after the failed coup.  几次未遂政变后，几名暴动者被捕。

近义词：rebel, revolutionary, mutineer
反义词：loyalist
词性：noun


词源:
From Late Latin *insurrectionarius, from Latin insurrectionem (nominative insurrectio) "a rising up, rebellion," noun of action from past-participle stem of insurgere "to rise up" (see insurgent). Meaning "rebellious" is from 1794.
