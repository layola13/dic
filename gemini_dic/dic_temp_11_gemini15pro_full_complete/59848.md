/ˈɛktəˌproʊktə/ | n. | 外肛动物 |  A phylum of aquatic invertebrate animals, also known as Bryozoa, characterized by a lophophore (a crown of tentacles used for feeding) and a U-shaped digestive tract. The anus is located outside the lophophore, hence the name "ectoprocta" (outside anus). |  Ectoprocta are commonly found encrusting rocks and other surfaces in marine environments. 外肛动物通常在海洋环境中包裹岩石和其他表面。|  These ectoprocta colonies are creating beautiful patterns on the seafloor. 这些外肛动物群落在海底形成了美丽的图案。 | Synonyms: Bryozoa | Antonyms: N/A | noun

/ˈɛktəˌproʊktə/ | n. | 外肛动物门 |  Referring to the phylum itself, encompassing all ectoproct species. | The Ectoprocta are a diverse phylum with a rich fossil record. 外肛动物门是一个多样化的门，拥有丰富的化石记录。 | Scientists are still discovering new species within the phylum Ectoprocta. 科学家们仍在外肛动物门中发现新物种。 | Synonyms: Bryozoa | Antonyms: N/A | noun
