lipaemia:/laɪˈpiːmiə/ /lɪˈpiːmiə/
noun
脂肪血症
The presence of excess lipids (fats) in the blood.
High levels of triglycerides in the blood, sometimes causing it to appear milky.
例句：Lipaemia can interfere with certain blood tests. 脂肪血症会干扰某些血液测试。

例句：The patient presented with severe lipaemia after a fatty meal. 病人在吃了一顿油腻的饭后出现了严重的脂肪血症。

近义词：hyperlipidemia, hyperlipemia
反义词：(None -  The opposite would be having abnormally low blood lipids, which is also a medical condition but doesn't have a single, common antonym word like lipaemia.)
词性: noun
