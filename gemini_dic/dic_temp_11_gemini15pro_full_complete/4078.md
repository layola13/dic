ail:/eɪl/
verb
使…苦恼；使…不适
to cause sickness, pain, or trouble
例句：
What ails you? 你怎么了？

He looked pale and seemed to be ailing. 他脸色苍白，好像身体不适。


近义词：trouble, bother, afflict, affect
反义词: comfort, soothe, heal
词性: verb
