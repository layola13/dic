fair-mindedness:/ˌfeərˈmaɪndɪdnəs/
n.
公平；公正；不偏不倚
The quality of being fair and impartial in one's judgments and actions.
例句：The judge was known for his fair-mindedness.  法官以其公正而闻名。

例句：Her fair-mindedness made her an excellent mediator.  她的公正使她成为一名优秀的调解员。

近义词：impartiality, objectivity, neutrality, justice, fairness
反义词：bias, prejudice, partiality, unfairness
