escalate:/ˈɛskəleɪt/| v. | 升级，扩大，加剧 | To increase or intensify in extent, seriousness, or intensity. | The conflict escalated quickly into a full-blown war. 冲突迅速升级为全面战争。|  The tension escalated after the announcement. 宣布之后，紧张局势加剧了。|近义词：increase, intensify, worsen, aggravate, heighten |反义词: de-escalate, decrease, diminish, lessen, reduce |词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *scalare "to climb," from scala "ladder, staircase."  The prefix e- adds intensity.  Originally referring to climbing stairs, the meaning broadened to include any kind of increase in intensity or magnitude.
