nonamputation:/ˌnɒnˌæmpjuˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 非截肢，保肢 | The preservation of a limb or body part that might otherwise be amputated. |  The surgeon performed a complex nonamputation surgery to save the patient's leg.  外科医生进行了一次复杂的保肢手术，以保住病人的腿。

The focus on nonamputation techniques has improved outcomes for patients with severe limb injuries.  对保肢技术的关注改善了严重肢体损伤患者的预后。| 近义词: limb salvage, limb preservation | 反义词: amputation | 词性: noun
