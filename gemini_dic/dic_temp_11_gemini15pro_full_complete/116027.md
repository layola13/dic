marsupialian: /mɑːrˈsuːpiəlɪən/ | /mɑːrˈsuːpiəlɪən/ | n. adj. | 有袋动物；有袋类的 | A mammal of an order whose members are born incompletely developed and are typically carried and suckled in a pouch on the mother's belly. |  例句：Kangaroos are marsupials. 袋鼠是有袋动物。| 近义词：pouched mammal | 反义词: placental mammal | 词性: noun, adjective

marsupialian: /mɑːrˈsuːpiəlɪən/ | /mɑːrˈsuːpiəlɪən/ | adj. | 有袋类的 | Relating to or resembling a marsupial. | 例句：The koala has characteristically marsupialian features.  考拉具有典型的有袋动物特征。 | 近义词：pouched | 反义词: placental | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *marsupial*, from Late Latin *marsupium* "pouch," from Greek *marsippion* (diminutive of *marsipos* "bag, pouch").
