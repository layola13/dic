antimilitaristic:/ˌæntiˌmɪlɪtəˈrɪstɪk/
| adj. | 反军国主义的；反对黩武主义的 | Opposed to militarism; against the glorification of military power and the belief that a country should maintain a strong military capability and be prepared to use it aggressively to defend or promote national interests. |  Many antimilitaristic protesters gathered outside the parliament. 许多反军国主义抗议者聚集在议会大楼外。| synonyms: pacifist, peace-loving, dovish | antonyms: militaristic, hawkish, pro-war | adjective
