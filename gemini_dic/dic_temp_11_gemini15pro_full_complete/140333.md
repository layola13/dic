oh-oh:/oʊ oʊ/ |interj.| 糟糕；啊哦（表示意识到出了问题或麻烦）| An exclamation used to express dismay or the realization that something has gone wrong. |例句：Oh-oh, I think I left the oven on. 啊哦，我好像忘记关烤箱了。

例句：Oh-oh, looks like we're in trouble. 啊哦，看来我们有麻烦了。|近义词：uh-oh, oops, whoops |反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider expressions of relief like "phew" or "thank goodness") |词性: interjection
