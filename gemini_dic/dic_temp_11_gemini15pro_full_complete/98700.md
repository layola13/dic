insuperably:/ɪnˈsuːpərəbli/
adverb
不可克服地
in a way that cannot be overcome
Her courage was insuperably strong. 她的勇气无比强大。
insuperable difficulties 不可逾越的困难

近义词:unconquerably, invincibly, insurmountably
反义词:surmountably, conquerably
词性:adverb
