hematocrystallin:/ˌhiːmətoʊˈkrɪstəlɪn/| n. |血晶蛋白|A crystalline protein found in red blood cells, identical to hemoglobin.|Hemoglobin, also known as hematocrystallin, carries oxygen in the blood. 血红蛋白，也称为血晶蛋白，在血液中携带氧气。|Synonym: hemoglobin|Antonym: None|Noun
