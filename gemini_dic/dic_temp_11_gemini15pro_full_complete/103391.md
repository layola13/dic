jink:/dʒɪŋk/ | n. vi. vt. | 急转弯，躲闪，改变方向；使急转弯 | A sudden quick change of direction or course. | The hare jinked to the right to avoid the fox. 野兔为了躲避狐狸向右急转弯。 | 近义词: swerve, dodge, veer, sidestep | 反义词: straighten, continue | 词性: 名词, 动词

jink:/dʒɪŋk/ | n. vi. vt. | (苏格兰) 恶作剧，戏弄，诅咒 | A spell, hex, or curse.  (Mostly Scottish) |  She blamed the jink for her bad luck. 她把自己的厄运归咎于诅咒。 | 近义词: hex, curse, jinx | 反义词: blessing | 词性: 名词, 动词
