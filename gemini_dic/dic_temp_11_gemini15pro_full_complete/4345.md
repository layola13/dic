akinesic: /ˌeɪkaɪˈniːsɪk/ | adj. | 运动不能的；无表情的 | Relating to akinesia, the loss or impairment of the power of voluntary movement. |  He had an akinesic face, showing no emotion. 他面无表情，毫无情绪波动。| The patient exhibited akinesic symptoms, struggling to initiate movement.  病人表现出运动不能的症状，难以启动运动。 |  Synonyms: motionless, expressionless, impassive, inexpressive | Antonyms: mobile, expressive, animated | adjective
