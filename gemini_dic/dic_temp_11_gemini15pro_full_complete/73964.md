fortifiers:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪərz/
noun
防御工事; 设防者; 防御者
People who or things that fortify something, making it stronger or more resistant.
The engineers acted as fortifiers, strengthening the city's defenses against the impending attack. 工程师充当设防者，加强城市防御以应对即将到来的袭击。
Synonyms: strengtheners, reinforcers, bulwarks
Antonyms: weakeners, saboteurs, destroyers
