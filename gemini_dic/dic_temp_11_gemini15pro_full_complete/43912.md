crevasses: /krəˈvæsɪz/ | n. | 裂缝，裂隙 | A deep open crack, especially in a glacier or ice sheet. |  The climbers had to be careful of the hidden crevasses in the glacier. 登山者必须小心冰川中隐藏的裂缝。


 The crevasses were so deep they couldn't see the bottom. 这些裂缝很深，他们看不到底部。

| 近义词: cracks, fissures, chasms | 反义词: closure, seal | 词性: 名词
