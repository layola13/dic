endochondral:/ˌɛndoʊˈkɒndrəl/| adj.|软骨内,软骨内的|Formed or occurring within cartilage; relating to the process of endochondral ossification where bone develops from a cartilage precursor.|Endochondral ossification is the process by which most bones in the body are formed.  软骨内成骨是人体大多数骨骼形成的过程。|The endochondral bones of the limb develop from cartilage templates. 四肢的软骨内骨骼由软骨模板发育而来。|近义词: cartilaginous|反义词: intramembranous|词性: adjective
