clodpate: /ˈklɒdpeɪt/ (UK), /ˈklɑːdpeɪt/ (US) | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 | A foolish or stupid person.  A clodpate is someone considered slow-witted or lacking in common sense. It's a rather informal and somewhat old-fashioned term. | He acted like a complete clodpate, forgetting his own address. 他表现得像个十足的笨蛋，竟然忘记了自己的地址。|  You utter clodpate! You've locked us out! 你这个十足的笨蛋！你把我们锁在外面了！ |  Synonyms: idiot, fool, dunce, dolt, blockhead | Antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant | noun
