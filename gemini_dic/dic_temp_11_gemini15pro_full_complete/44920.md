cryptobranchiata: /ˌkrɪptoʊbræŋkiˈɑːtə/ | n. | 隐鳃鲵科 | A family of primitive salamanders characterized by external fertilization and larval gills retained throughout life.  These fully aquatic salamanders are found in eastern North America and eastern Asia. |  Cryptobranchiata are fascinating creatures that have changed little over millions of years. 隐鳃鲵科是数百万年来变化很小的迷人生物。 | The Hellbender is a member of the Cryptobranchiata. 地狱弯腰蝾螈是隐鳃鲵科的成员。 | synonyms: giant salamanders, primitive salamanders | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic family) | noun
