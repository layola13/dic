demystifier:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪər/
n.|揭秘者，使不再神秘的人(或物)|A person or thing that demystifies something.
|The book is a demystifier of complex scientific concepts. 这本书揭开了复杂科学概念的神秘面纱。
|synonyms: explainer, clarifier, interpreter, elucidator
|antonyms: obfuscator, mystifier
|noun


demystify:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪ/
vt.|揭开…的神秘面纱，使不再神秘|To make something easier to understand by explaining it in a clear and simple way.
|The teacher demystified the complex mathematical equation for the students. 老师为学生们揭开了复杂数学方程的神秘面纱。
|synonyms: clarify, explain, simplify, elucidate, interpret
|antonyms: obfuscate, mystify, confuse
|verb
