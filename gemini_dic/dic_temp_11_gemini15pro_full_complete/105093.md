kerneled:/ˈkɜːrnəld/ | adj. | 有核的；似核的；颗粒状的 | Having a kernel or kernels; resembling a kernel; granular. | The farmer planted kerneled corn. 农民种植了有核的玉米。| The mixture contained small, kerneled particles. 这种混合物含有小的颗粒状物质。 | 近义词: seeded, nucleated, granular | 反义词: seedless, smooth | 词性: adjective

词源: From kernel + -ed.  "Kernel" comes from Old English *cyrnel, from Proto-Germanic *kurnila-, diminutive of *kurnam "grain, seed."
