liquidizes:/ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪz/
verb
清算；变现；液化
converts assets into cash; turns something into a liquid state
The company liquidizes its assets to pay off its debts.  公司变卖资产以偿清债务。
The heat liquidizes the butter.  高温使黄油融化。
Synonyms: dissolve, melt, liquefy, cash in
Antonyms: solidify, freeze
词性: 动词
