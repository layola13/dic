annuity:/əˈnjuːɪti/ /əˈnuːɪti/| n.|年金；养老金；年金保险|A fixed sum of money paid to someone each year, typically for the rest of their life.|例句：She receives an annuity of $10,000 per year. 她每年收到一万美元的年金。

例句：He invested his savings in an annuity. 他把积蓄投资于年金。


近义词：pension, allowance, payment|反义词:lump sum|词性:noun
