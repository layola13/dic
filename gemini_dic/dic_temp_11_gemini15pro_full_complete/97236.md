inflectional:/ɪnˈflɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 屈折的，词尾变化的 | Relating to or characterized by inflection; pertaining to the modification of words to express grammatical relations such as tense, person, number, case, etc. |  The inflectional ending "-ed" indicates past tense. 词尾变化“-ed”表示过去式。| inflectional, grammatical | derivational | adjective
