nazirite: /ˈnæzəˌraɪt/ | n. | 拿细耳人 | A person who took a vow described in Numbers 6 of the Hebrew Bible to abstain from alcohol and cutting their hair, among other things, as a sign of devotion to God. | Samson was a famous Nazirite. 参孙是一位著名的拿细耳人。 | 近义词: ascetic, abstainer | 反义词: hedonist, libertine | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *Nazaraeus, from Ancient Greek Ναζαραῖος (Nazaraîos), from Hebrew נזיר (nazir, “one separated; consecrated; Nazirite”).
