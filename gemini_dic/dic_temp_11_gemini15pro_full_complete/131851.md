nonconstricted:/ˌnɒnkənˈstrɪktɪd/
adjective
未收缩的，未缩窄的
Not constricted; not narrowed or compressed.
The nonconstricted blood vessels allowed for easy blood flow.  未收缩的血管使血液流动通畅。
The patient's airways were nonconstricted, allowing for normal breathing.  病人的呼吸道没有收缩，可以正常呼吸。
近义词: dilated, expanded, open, unblocked
反义词: constricted, narrowed, blocked, obstructed
词性: adjective
