league: /liːg/ | n. | 联盟，联赛；等级，级别 | A group of people, organizations, or countries that have united for a common purpose, especially for mutual help or defense. | The two teams are in the same league. 这两支队伍在同一个联盟。|  The company is in a league of its own.  这家公司独树一帜。 | synonym: alliance, association, federation, union | antonym: division, separation, disunity | noun


league: /liːg/ | v. | 结盟，联合 | To form a league with. |  Several countries leagued together against the common enemy.  几个国家联合起来对抗共同的敌人。 | They leagued with other groups to lobby for change. 他们与其他团体结盟，共同游说变革。 | synonym: ally, unite, combine, join forces | antonym: divide, separate, disunite | verb
