misalignment:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnmənt/ | n. |失准，未对准，错位|A failure of parts or systems to be properly positioned in relation to each other. | The misalignment of the wheels caused the car to pull to the left. 车轮未对准导致汽车向左偏移。

The misalignment of the telescope resulted in blurry images. 望远镜的失准导致图像模糊。|近义词：maladjustment, discrepancy, disparity, discordance|反义词：alignment, adjustment, harmony, concord|词性: 名词
