leucocytogenesis: /ˌluːkoʊˌsaɪtoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs/ | n. | 白细胞生成 |  The formation and development of leukocytes (white blood cells). It is the process by which the body produces white blood cells, which are essential components of the immune system. |  Leucocytogenesis is stimulated during infection. 感染期间会刺激白细胞生成。|  leukopoiesis, leukocytopoiesis |  leucocytopenia | noun
