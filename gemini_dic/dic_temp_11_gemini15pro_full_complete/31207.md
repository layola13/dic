changu: /ˈtʃɑːŋɡuː/ | n. | 长颈鹿;  (东非斯瓦希里语) | A tall African mammal with a very long neck and legs, patterned with brown patches separated by lighter lines.  It is the tallest living animal. |  We saw a changu on our safari in Kenya. 我们在肯尼亚的狩猎之旅中看到了一只长颈鹿。|  Synonyms: giraffe | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
