gardening:/ˈɡɑːrdnɪŋ/ /ˈɡɑːrdənɪŋ/| n.|园艺；从事园艺|The act of cultivating gardens.  The practice of growing and cultivating plants as part of horticulture. |She finds gardening a relaxing hobby. 她觉得园艺是一种放松的爱好。|My grandmother enjoys gardening and spends hours tending her roses. 我的祖母喜欢园艺，花几个小时照料她的玫瑰。|近义词：horticulture, cultivation|反义词:neglect|词性:noun
