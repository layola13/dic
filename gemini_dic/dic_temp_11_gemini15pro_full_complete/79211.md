glandless:/ˈɡlændləs/| adj.|无腺的，无分泌腺的|Lacking glands, especially oil glands.  Specifically refers to varieties of cotton plants bred to produce seeds without gossypol glands, making the seeds safe for human and animal consumption.|例句：Glandless cottonseed is a valuable source of protein. 无腺棉籽是宝贵的蛋白质来源。

例句：The glandless cotton variety is preferred for its edible seeds.  无腺棉籽品种因其可食用的种子而受到青睐。


|近义词：non-glandular|反义词:glandular|词性:adjective
