degum:/diːˈɡʌm/ | v. | 脱胶; 去除树胶 | To remove gum or gummy substances from something, especially silk or other natural fibers. | The silk is degummed before it can be dyed.  丝绸在染色之前需要脱胶。| Synonyms: remove gum, extract gum | Antonyms: gum, apply gum | verb

degumming:/diːˈɡʌmɪŋ/ | n. | 脱胶; 去除树胶（的过程） | The process of removing gum or gummy substances from something. | Degumming is an important step in silk processing. 脱胶是丝绸加工的重要步骤。| Synonyms: gum removal, gum extraction | Antonyms: gumming, application of gum | noun
