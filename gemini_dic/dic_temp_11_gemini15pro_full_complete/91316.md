hydropath:/ˈhaɪdrəpæθ/ | n. | 水疗法；水疗医师 | A person who practices hydropathy (treatment of disease by the internal and external use of water). |  The hydropath recommended drinking mineral water for better health. 水疗医师建议饮用矿泉水以增进健康。|  Synonym: hydrotherapist, water cure practitioner | Antonym: allopath | noun
