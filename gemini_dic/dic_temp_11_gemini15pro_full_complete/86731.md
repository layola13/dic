helmsmanship:/ˈhɛlmzmənʃɪp/| n.|掌舵术，舵艺；领导才能|The captain's helmsmanship was praised by all. 船长的掌舵技术受到了所有人的称赞。|例句：His skillful helmsmanship saved the ship from the storm. 他娴熟的掌舵技术使船只免于风暴的袭击。

例句：The company's success is a testament to the CEO's helmsmanship. 公司的成功证明了首席执行官的领导才能。|近义词：leadership, guidance, direction, navigation|反义词:mismanagement, incompetence|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From helm (n.) + -ship.  "Helm" comes from Old English *helma, from Proto-Germanic *helmaz.  "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting "state, condition, office, skill."
