babying:/ˈbeɪbiɪŋ/| v. (gerund)|溺爱，娇惯，像对待婴儿一样对待|Treating someone as if they were a baby, often by being overprotective or doing things for them that they are capable of doing themselves.| She's always babying her youngest son, even though he's already twelve. 她总是溺爱她的小儿子，即使他已经十二岁了。|
He resented being babied by his older sister. 他讨厌被姐姐像婴儿一样对待。|近义词：pampering, coddling, spoiling|反义词：neglecting, ignoring|词性:动词(gerund)

babying:/ˈbeɪbiɪŋ/| n. |溺爱，娇惯|The act of treating someone like a baby.|The constant babying had made him overly dependent on his parents.  不断的娇惯使他过度依赖父母。|Stop all this babying! He needs to learn to do things for himself.  别再溺爱他了！他需要学会自己做事。|近义词：pampering, coddling, overprotectiveness|反义词：neglect, indifference|词性:名词
