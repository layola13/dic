cantering:/ˈkæntərɪŋ/| n. vi.|慢跑，轻快地跑|The horse was cantering across the field.  马在田野里慢跑。|近义词：trotting, loping|反义词: galloping, standing|词性：noun, verb (present participle)
Etymology:  From the verb "canter," which likely originated from Canterbury gallop, a moderate gallop said to have been popular among pilgrims traveling to Canterbury Cathedral.
