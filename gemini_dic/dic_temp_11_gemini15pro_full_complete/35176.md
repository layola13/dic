cloying:/ˈklɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. |甜得发腻的；令人腻烦的|Excessively sweet, rich, or sentimental, to the point of being unpleasant.|The dessert was so cloying that I couldn't finish it. 这道甜点太腻了，我吃不完。|近义词：sickly, saccharine, oversweet, nauseating|反义词：savory, tart, refreshing|词性:形容词

cloying:/ˈklɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. |令人腻烦的|Excessively sentimental or flattering to the point of being repulsive.|His cloying compliments made me uncomfortable. 他那令人腻烦的恭维让我很不舒服。|近义词：fulsome, excessive, insincere|反义词：genuine, sincere, understated|词性:形容词
