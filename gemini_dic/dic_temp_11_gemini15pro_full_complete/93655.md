imbroglio:/ɪmˈbroʊljoʊ/ (US), /ɪmˈbrəʊljəʊ/ (UK)| n.|纠纷，混乱，复杂局面|A confused or complicated situation, typically one involving a disagreement or misunderstanding.|The inheritance became an imbroglio when the deceased's will was found to be invalid. 遗产继承变成了一场纠纷，因为死者的遗嘱被发现是无效的。|例句：The political imbroglio threatened to destabilize the entire region. 政治纠纷有可能破坏整个地区的稳定。|近义词：muddle, entanglement, predicament, quandary, confusion|反义词：order, clarity, solution, harmony|词性:名词

词源: From Italian *imbroglio* "confusion, entanglement," derived from *imbrogliare* "to entangle, confuse," from *broglio* "confusion." Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *brogilus,* of uncertain origin.
