hand-it-to:/ˈhænd ɪt tuː/
idiom
承认(某人)的优点; 佩服(某人)
To acknowledge someone's skill or achievement; to admire someone for something.
例句：You have to hand it to her, she's a brilliant businesswoman. 你不得不佩服她，她是一位出色的女商人。

例句：I'll hand it to him; he knows how to get things done. 我不得不承认他的能力，他知道如何把事情做好。

近义词：give credit to, acknowledge, admire, respect
反义词：criticize, belittle, disparage
词性：idiom (idiomatic expression)
