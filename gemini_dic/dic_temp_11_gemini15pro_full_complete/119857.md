milepost:/ˈmaɪlpoʊst/| n.|里程碑，里程标志|A milepost is a marker, typically a post, placed at intervals along a road or railway to indicate the distance to a particular place.|We passed a milepost that said "100 miles to Chicago." 我们经过了一个写着“距离芝加哥100英里”的里程碑。|Synonyms: milestone, marker, signpost|Antonyms: N/A|noun
