horrifically:/hɒˈrɪfɪkli/ /hɔːˈrɪfɪkli/
adverb
可怕地；极其地
in a horrific manner; in a way that causes horror.
The child was horrifically injured in the accident.  这个孩子在事故中受了重伤。
The play was horrifically bad.  这出戏糟透了。
近义词:terribly, dreadfully, awfully, frightfully, appallingly
反义词:wonderfully, beautifully, delightfully
词性:adverb

词源:From horrific + -ly.  来自 horrific + -ly.
