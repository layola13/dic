bowled:/boʊld/ | v. | 保龄球；打保龄球；投球（尤其指板球或保龄球）；击倒；使震惊；使倾倒 |  The batsman was bowled out for a duck.  那个击球手一分未得就被淘汰出局了。 | synonyms: knocked down, defeated, overwhelmed, bowled over | antonyms: supported, encouraged, built up | verb

bowled:/boʊld/ | adj. | (指人)被击倒的；被征服的；被倾倒的 | I was completely bowled over by the news. 我完全被这个消息震惊了。| synonyms: astonished, amazed, impressed, overwhelmed | antonyms: unimpressed, unmoved, indifferent | adjective
