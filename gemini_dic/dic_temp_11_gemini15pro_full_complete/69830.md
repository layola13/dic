ferreted:/ˈfɛrɪtɪd/ | v. | 搜寻，探查; 驱赶(用雪貂); | To search out persistently; to hunt with ferrets. |  The detective ferreted out the crucial evidence. 侦探搜寻出了关键证据。

The reporter ferreted the information out of a reluctant source. 记者从一个不愿透露消息的来源那里探查到了信息。| 近义词：searched, unearthed, rummaged, hunted, explored | 反义词：hid, concealed, buried | 词性: 动词
