anti-militarism:/ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪzəm/| n. |反军国主义|Opposition to war and military policies and preparedness, often involving advocacy of peaceful methods of resolving international disputes.|  Pacifists often subscribe to anti-militarism.  和平主义者通常支持反军国主义。|  Anti-militarism is a growing sentiment in the country. 反军国主义情绪在这个国家日益高涨。|Synonyms: pacifism, peace advocacy|Antonyms: militarism, bellicosity|noun
