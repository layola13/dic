one-for-the-book:/wʌn fɔːr ðə bʊk/
idiom
非同寻常的；值得纪念的；破纪录的
An unusual, remarkable, or record-breaking act or event.
His 90th birthday was certainly one for the book.  他的90岁生日当然值得纪念。
The team’s stunning victory was one for the book.  这支队伍惊人的胜利破了纪录。
近义词:remarkable, extraordinary, memorable, unforgettable
反义词:ordinary, common, usual, typical
词性:idiom (习语)
