conservatively:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪvli/| adv.|保守地，谨慎地|In a way that avoids risk or extravagance; cautiously.|She dresses conservatively for work. 她上班穿着保守。|Synonyms: cautiously, prudently, carefully, moderately|Antonyms: radically, liberally, extravagantly|adverb

conservatively:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪvli/| adv.|保守地，谨慎地|Estimating something at a low value to avoid overstatement; in a way that is deliberately understated.|The damages were conservatively estimated at $1 million. 损失保守估计为100万美元。|Synonyms: cautiously, modestly, understatedly|Antonyms: optimistically, exaggeratedly|adverb
