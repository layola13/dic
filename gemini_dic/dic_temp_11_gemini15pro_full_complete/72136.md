floodless:/ˈflʌdlɪs/| adj.|无洪水的，不受洪水影响的|Free from floods; not subject to flooding.  Not having or experiencing floods.|The town is situated on high ground, making it relatively floodless.  这个小镇地势较高，相对来说没有洪水。|近义词：flood-free, immune to flooding|反义词：flood-prone, susceptible to flooding|词性:形容词

Etymology:
flood (n.) + -less (suffix)

flood (n.) Old English *flod "a flowing, stream, flood," from Proto-Germanic *floduz (source also of Old Saxon flod, Old Frisian flod, Dutch vloed "flood, tide," Old High German fluot, German Flut "flood, flow, tide," Old Norse flod "flood, river," Gothic flodus "flood"), from PIE root *pleu- "to flow."

-less (suffix) Old English -leas "free from, without, devoid of," from Proto-Germanic *-lausaz (source also of Old Saxon, Old Frisian -los, Dutch -loos, Old High German -los, German -los, Gothic -laus), from PIE root *leudh- "to grow up, sprout."
