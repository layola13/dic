indoctrinator:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪtər/ /ɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪtər/| n. |灌输者；教导者|A person who indoctrinates others, especially with a specific ideology or doctrine.|The cult leader was a skilled indoctrinator, convincing his followers to abandon their families and possessions.|Synonyms: brainwasher, propagandist, proselytizer|Antonyms: freethinker, skeptic, dissenter|Noun

indoctrinate:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪt/ /ɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪt/| vt. |向…灌输；教导|To teach (someone) to fully accept the ideas, opinions, and beliefs of a particular group and to not consider other ideas, opinions, and beliefs.|The regime indoctrinates its citizens from an early age.|Synonyms: brainwash, propagandize, instill|Antonyms: enlighten, educate (in a broader sense), free|Verb
