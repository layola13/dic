bedaggle: /bɪˈdæɡəl/ | vt. | 弄湿，弄脏，使泥泞 | To wet or soil by dragging through mud or water. |  The dog's fur was bedaggled after running through the muddy field.  狗跑过泥泞的田野后，毛发都被弄脏了。

The hem of her dress was bedaggled from walking in the rain.  她的裙摆因为在雨中行走而被弄湿了。| 近义词: soak, drench, muddy, soil | 反义词: clean, dry | 词性: 动词
