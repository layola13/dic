countlessly:/ˈkaʊntləsli/| adv.|无数地，数不清地|In a way that is too numerous to be counted.  It emphasizes the sheer number or frequency of something, often to the point of being overwhelming or immeasurable.|The stars in the night sky seemed countlessly numerous. 夜空中的星星看起来数不胜数。|Synonyms: innumerably, endlessly, infinitely, without number|Antonyms: finitely, limitedly|adverb

countlessly:/ˈkaʊntləsli/| adv.|无数地，数不清地|Describes something so numerous it cannot be counted.|She sighed countlessly throughout the tedious meeting.  在冗长的会议中，她不停地叹气。|Synonyms:  innumerable, myriad, numerous, immeasurable|Antonyms: few, limited, countable|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From count + -less.  "Count" ultimately derives from the Latin "computare" (to calculate).  "-less" is a suffix of Old English origin meaning "without."
