mesne:/miːn/
adjective
中间的；居间的
intermediate; intervening.  Existing in the middle; intervening.  Specifically in law, a mesne lord is a lord who holds land from a superior but grants a portion of it to another.
Example: He held the land in mesne tenure. 他以中间保有权持有土地。
Example: The mesne profits of the estate were substantial. 这片地产的中间收益相当可观。
Synonym: intermediate, intervening, middle
Antonym: extreme, ultimate, direct
word class: adjective

mesne:/miːn/
noun
中间领主；中间利润
intermediate lord; intermediate profits.  A lord who holds land from a superior but grants a portion of it to another.  The profits derived from land by a tenant in wrongful possession between two periods.
Example: The mesne lord collected rents from the subtenant. 中间领主向次承租人收取租金。
Example: The court awarded the rightful owner the mesne profits. 法院将中间利润判给了合法的所有者。
Synonym: intermediary
Antonym: principal, owner
word class: noun
