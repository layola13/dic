ingenerable:/ɪnˈdʒɛnərəbl/
adjective
不能产生的，无法生成的
Not able to be generated or produced; that cannot be brought into existence.
例句：Some scientists believe that consciousness is ingenerable from purely physical processes. 一些科学家认为，意识无法从纯粹的物理过程中产生。

例句：The artist claimed that true inspiration is ingenerable and cannot be forced.  这位艺术家声称真正的灵感是无法产生的，也不能强求。

近义词：uncreatable, unproducible, impossible to generate
反义词：generable, producible, creatable
词性：adjective
