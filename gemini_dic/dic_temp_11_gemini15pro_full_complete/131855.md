nonconstructively:/ˌnɒnkənˈstrʌktɪvli/
adverb|非建设性地，无益地|In a way that does not help to improve a situation or find a solution.|He criticized the project nonconstructively, offering no helpful suggestions. 他非建设性地批评了这个项目，没有提供任何有益的建议。|unhelpfully, negatively|constructively, helpfully
词性: 副词
