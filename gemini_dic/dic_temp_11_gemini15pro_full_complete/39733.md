consistently:/kənˈsɪstəntlɪ/
adverb
始终如一地，一致地
in a way that does not vary; in every case or occasion
She has consistently ranked among the top students in her class. 她一直名列班级前茅。
He consistently performs at a high level. 他始终保持高水平的发挥。
近义词:regularly, steadily, uniformly, invariably, constantly
反义词:inconsistently, erratically, sporadically
词性:副词

consistently:/kənˈsɪstəntlɪ/
adverb
一致地；连续地
in agreement or harmony; without contradiction
The witness's story was not consistently accurate.  证人的说法前后不一致。
The results of the experiment were consistently positive. 实验结果始终是积极的。
近义词:compatibly, congruently, harmoniously
反义词:inconsistently, incompatibly, discordantly
词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *consistenter, from Latin consistens, present participle of consistere "to stand firm, stand still, consist of," from con- "together" (see con-) + sistere "to cause to stand," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm." The adverbial use developed in English c. 1600.
