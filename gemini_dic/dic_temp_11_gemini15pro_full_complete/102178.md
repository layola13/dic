itacist:/ˈɪtəsɪst/| n.|伊塔主义者；滥用字母i的人|A person who mispronounces or misspells words, especially by substituting the letter "i" for other vowels, or who overuses the letter "i".|The itacist confused "e" and "i" constantly, making his writing difficult to decipher.|近义词：cacographer, misspeller|反义词: grammarian|词性:noun
