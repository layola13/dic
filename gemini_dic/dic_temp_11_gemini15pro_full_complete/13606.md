attestants:/əˈtɛstənts/| n. |证人；证明人|People who attest something, especially in a formal or legal context.  They bear witness to an event or verify the authenticity of a document.|The attestants signed the will in the presence of a lawyer. 律师在场的情况下，证人在遗嘱上签了字。|Synonyms: witnesses, verifiers, corroborators|Antonyms:  challengers, disputants|Noun
