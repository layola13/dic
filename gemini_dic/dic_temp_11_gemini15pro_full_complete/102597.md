jailbreaking:/ˈdʒeɪlˌbreɪkɪŋ/
n.
越狱(指软件)，破解
Removing restrictions imposed by the manufacturer or operator of a device, especially a mobile device, to allow the installation of unauthorized software or access to unauthorized features.
Many users resort to jailbreaking their iPhones to install apps not available on the official App Store. 许多用户选择越狱他们的iPhone来安装官方应用商店里没有的应用程序。

近义词:rooting, unlocking
反义词:restricting, locking
词性:名词
