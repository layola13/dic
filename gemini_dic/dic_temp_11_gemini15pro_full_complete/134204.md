nonindependence:/ˌnɒnɪndɪˈpɛndəns/ | n. | 非独立性，不独立 |  The lack of independence; the state of being dependent or interconnected.  This term often appears in statistical contexts, referring to the situation where variables or events are not independent of each other. | The nonindependence of the two variables made the statistical analysis more complex.  这两个变量的非独立性使统计分析更加复杂。 | The study acknowledged the nonindependence of the samples. 这项研究承认了样本的非独立性。| dependence, interdependence, correlation | independence, autonomy | noun
