convict:/ˈkɒnvɪkt/ (UK) /kənˈvɪkt/ (US)| n. |罪犯|A person found guilty of a crime and sentenced by a court.|The convict escaped from prison.  这个罪犯越狱了。|Synonyms: prisoner, inmate, criminal, felon|Antonyms: law-abiding citizen|Noun

convict:/kənˈvɪkt/ (US) /kənˈvɪkt/ (UK)| vt. |定罪；证明…有罪|To prove or declare (someone) to be guilty of a crime, especially by the verdict of a jury or the decision of a judge in a court of law.|The jury convicted him of murder. 陪审团裁定他犯有谋杀罪。|Synonyms: condemn, sentence, incriminate|Antonyms: acquit, exonerate|Verb
