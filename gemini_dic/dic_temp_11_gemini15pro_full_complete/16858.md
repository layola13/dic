basifier: /ˈbeɪsɪfaɪər/
n.
碱化剂；碱化器
A substance that makes something more basic or alkaline.
The basifier was added to the solution to increase its pH.  碱化剂被添加到溶液中以增加其pH值。
The soil needs a basifier to neutralize the acidity. 土壤需要碱化剂来中和酸度。
近义词: alkalizer, neutralizer
反义词: acidifier
词性: 名词
