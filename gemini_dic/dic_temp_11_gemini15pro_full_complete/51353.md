detente:/deɪˈtɑːnt/ /deɪˈtɑːnt/| n.|缓和，缓和时期，（国际关系）缓和|A period of lessening tension between two countries, especially the US and Soviet Union during the Cold War.|Example: The period of détente between the United States and the Soviet Union began in the late 1960s.  美苏之间的缓和时期始于20世纪60年代末。

Example: The two countries agreed to a period of détente after years of conflict.  两国在多年冲突后同意进入一段缓和时期。|近义词：rapprochement, relaxation, thaw|反义词：tension, hostility, conflict|词性:名词
