nonmethodical:/ˌnɒn məˈθɒdɪkəl/ | /ˌnɑːn məˈθɑːdɪkəl/ | adj. | 非系统化的，没有条理的 | Not methodical; lacking method or order; unsystematic. |  He has a nonmethodical approach to his work, often jumping between tasks. 他工作起来没有条理，经常在任务之间跳来跳去。

|  Her nonmethodical packing resulted in a suitcase full of wrinkled clothes. 她没有条理的打包方式导致行李箱里全是皱巴巴的衣服。

| 近义词: unsystematic, disorganized, haphazard, random, chaotic | 反义词: methodical, systematic, organized, orderly | 词性: 形容词
