dualistically:/ˌduːəlˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 二元地，二元论地 |  In a manner characterized by dualism, which is the division of something conceptually into two opposed or contrasted aspects. |  He viewed the world dualistically, seeing a constant struggle between good and evil. 他以二元论的观点看待世界，认为善与恶之间存在着不断的斗争。 | synonym:dichotomously | antonym:monistically | adverb

dualistically:/ˌduːəlˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 二元地，二元论地 |  Referring to a way of thinking that divides things into two separate and opposing categories.  | She tends to think dualistically, seeing everything in terms of black and white. 她倾向于二元思维，把一切都看成非黑即白。 | synonym: binarily | antonym:holistically | adverb
