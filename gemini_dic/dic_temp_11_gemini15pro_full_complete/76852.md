ganger:/ˈɡæŋə(r)/| n.| 工头；领班；一组工人；暴徒|A foreman or leader of a gang of workers, especially in construction or mining; a member of a gang, especially a violent one.|The ganger directed the crew to start laying the track. 工头指示工人开始铺设轨道。|The gang of gangers roamed the streets, looking for trouble. 一群暴徒在街上游荡，寻找麻烦。|近义词：foreman, supervisor, leader, overseer, thug, hoodlum|反义词: subordinate, follower|noun
