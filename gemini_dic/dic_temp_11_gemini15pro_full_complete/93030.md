idle-away:/ˈaɪdl əˈweɪ/ | phrasal verb | 虚度；闲混 | to spend time doing nothing or very little |  He idled away the whole afternoon watching TV. 他一下午都在看电视，虚度光阴。 | Synonyms: waste, fritter away, kill time, laze around | Antonyms: utilize, make the most of, be productive | verb phrase

idle:/ˈaɪdl/ | adj. | 空闲的，懒散的 | not working or being used | The factory stood idle during the strike. 工厂在罢工期间处于闲置状态。| Synonyms: inactive, unemployed, lazy | Antonyms: busy, active, employed | adjective

away:/əˈweɪ/ | adv. | 离开，远离 | to or at a distance from a particular place or person |  She went away for the weekend. 她周末外出了。 | Synonyms: off, aside, apart | Antonyms: near, close, toward | adverb
