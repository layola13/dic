bitzer:/ˈbɪtsər/| n.|比泽尔（人名）；（尤指狄更斯小说《艰难时世》中的）毕业生，实用主义者|A bitzer is a character, especially from Charles Dickens's novel Hard Times, who is a product of a utilitarian system of education, prioritizing facts over imagination and critical thinking.  |例句：He was a real bitzer, only interested in the facts and figures. 他是一个真正的实用主义者，只对事实和数据感兴趣。

例句：The bitzer in the story represents the dangers of a purely factual education.  故事中的这个人物代表了纯粹事实教育的危险。|近义词：pragmatist, utilitarian, Gradgrind|反义词：idealist, dreamer, romantic|词性:noun
