foxtail:/ˈfɒksteɪl/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksteɪl/ (US)| n.|狐尾，狐尾草|A spike of flowers resembling a fox's tail, especially that of the foxtail grasses (genus *Alopecurus*).  Also refers to things shaped like a fox's tail.|The dog was playing with a foxtail he found in the field. 这只狗在田里玩耍，它找到了一根狐尾草。|近义词：spike, plume, brush|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "base" if referring to the plant's structure) |词性:noun
