ashamedly:/əˈʃeɪmɪdli/ | adv. |羞愧地，惭愧地 | In a manner showing shame or embarrassment.  Feeling or showing guilt or embarrassment about something. | She ashamedly admitted she had lied. 她羞愧地承认她撒谎了。| He ashamedly looked down at his shoes after being caught cheating. 他作弊被抓后，羞愧地低头看着自己的鞋子。 | 近义词: guiltily, sheepishly, apologetically | 反义词: proudly, boldly, confidently | 词性: 副词 | 词源: From "ashamed" + "-ly". "Ashamed" comes from Old English "æscæmed," past participle of "æscæmian," meaning "to feel shame."
