comparator:/kəmˈpærətər/| n.|比较器; 比较电路|A comparator is a device that compares two input voltages and outputs a digital signal indicating which is larger. |The comparator circuit flagged an error when the input voltage exceeded the threshold. 比较器电路在输入电压超过阈值时标记了一个错误。|Synonyms: comparer, differentiator|Antonyms: integrator|noun

comparator:/kəmˈpærətər/| n.|比较器; 比较电路|A comparator is a circuit or device that compares two input signals and produces an output signal indicating the relationship between them. |This comparator is used to determine which of the two signals is stronger. 这个比较器用来确定两个信号中哪一个更强。|Synonyms: comparer, differentiator|Antonyms: integrator|noun
