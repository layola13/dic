grouchy: /ˈɡraʊtʃi/ | adj. | 易怒的，脾气坏的 | Easily annoyed and complaining; bad-tempered. |  He's been grouchy all day because he didn't get enough sleep. 他一整天都脾气暴躁，因为他没睡够。

| Example:  The old man was grouchy and yelled at the children playing in his yard.  那个老人脾气暴躁，对着在他院子里玩耍的孩子们大喊大叫。
| 近义词: grumpy, irritable, bad-tempered, cantankerous, cross | 反义词: cheerful, good-natured, pleasant, amiable | 词性: adjective
