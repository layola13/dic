lecithins:/ˈlesɪθɪnz/ | n. | 卵磷脂 |  A group of fatty substances found in animal and plant tissues, and egg yolks. They are essential for the proper functioning of cell membranes. |  Soy lecithins are widely used as emulsifiers in food processing. 大豆卵磷脂被广泛用作食品加工中的乳化剂。| synonyms: phospholipids | antonyms: None | noun

lecithin:/ˈlesɪθɪn/ | n. | 卵磷脂 | Any of a group of phospholipids, especially those present in egg yolk and nerve tissue, consisting of diglycerides of fatty acids combined with phosphoric acid and choline. | Lecithin is vital for healthy cell function. 卵磷脂对健康的细胞功能至关重要。| synonyms: phosphatidylcholine | antonyms: None | noun
