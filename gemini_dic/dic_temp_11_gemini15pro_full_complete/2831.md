adventurers:/ədˈvɛntʃərərz/| n.|冒险家；探险者|People who seek out and engage in exciting and often dangerous experiences.|例句：The adventurers set off into the uncharted jungle. 探险者们出发进入了未知的丛林。

例句：Many adventurers have attempted to climb Mount Everest. 许多冒险家都尝试攀登珠穆朗玛峰。|近义词：explorers, pioneers, daredevils|反义词：cowards, stay-at-homes|词性:Noun (Plural)

adventurer:/ədˈvɛntʃərər/| n.|冒险家；探险者|A person who enjoys or seeks adventure.|例句：He was a seasoned adventurer who had travelled the world. 他是一位经验丰富的冒险家，游历过世界各地。

例句：She fancies herself an adventurer, always seeking new thrills. 她自认为是一个冒险家，总是寻求新的刺激。|近义词：explorer, pioneer, daredevil|反义词：coward, stay-at-home|词性:Noun (Singular)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aventureer, *aventurer, from Old French aventureor, aventurier (“adventurer”), from aventure (“adventure”), from Latin adventūra (“that which happens; chance, fortune”).
