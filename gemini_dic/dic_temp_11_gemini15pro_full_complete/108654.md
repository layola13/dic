leafinesses:/ˈliːfɪnɪsɪz/
n.
叶茂盛，多叶的状态
The state or quality of being leafy; abundance of leaves.
The leafinesses of the trees provided ample shade.  树木的茂密叶子提供了充足的阴凉。
近义词：foliage, luxuriance
反义词：bareness, sparseness
词性: noun


Etymology:
From leafy + -ness + -es.  Leafy comes from the Old English word *lēafī, meaning "having leaves."  The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state or quality.  The suffix -es creates the plural form.
