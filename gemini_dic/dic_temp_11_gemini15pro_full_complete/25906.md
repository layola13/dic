burn oneself out:/bɜːrn wʌnˈsɛlf aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 耗尽体力；精疲力竭 | To become extremely tired or sick by working too hard. |  He burned himself out working too many hours. 他因工作时间过长而精疲力竭。

She burned herself out trying to please everyone. 她为了取悦每个人而筋疲力尽。

近义词: exhaust oneself, wear oneself out, overwork | 反义词: relax, rest, recuperate | 词性: 动词短语
