misbecome:/ˌmɪsbɪˈkʌm/
verb
失当，不合适，不相称
to be unsuitable or inappropriate for; to not suit
Her gaudy dress misbecame the solemnity of the occasion. 她那件花哨的连衣裙与庄严的场合不相称。
Synonyms:  ill-suit, be inappropriate, be unbecoming, clash with
Antonyms: suit, become, befit
词性:动词
