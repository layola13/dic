fireback:/ˈfaɪərbæk/| n.|后挡板，炉壁，铁板|A plate at the back of a fireplace, reflecting heat into the room.|The fireback was intricately carved with a hunting scene.  壁炉的后挡板精雕细琢地刻着狩猎的场景。|Example: The old fireback was made of cast iron and radiated warmth efficiently.  这个旧的壁炉后挡板是用铸铁制成的，散热效率很高。|Synonyms: backplate, fire plate|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun


fireback:/ˈfaɪərbæk/| n.|（枪炮的）后焰|The backblast or recoil of a firearm.|The fireback from the cannon knocked him off his feet.  大炮的后焰把他从地上掀了起来。|Example: The powerful fireback of the rifle surprised the inexperienced shooter.  这把步枪强大的后坐力让这位缺乏经验的射手感到惊讶。|Synonyms: recoil, kickback, backblast|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun


Etymology:  From "fire" + "back," literally the back of a fire.
