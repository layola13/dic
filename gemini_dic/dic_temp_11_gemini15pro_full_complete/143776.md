outmodedness:/ˌaʊtˈmoʊdɪdnəs/ | n. | 过时，陈旧 | The outmodedness of their equipment put them at a disadvantage.  | Their marketing strategies have an air of outmodedness. | 近义词：obsolescence, antiquatedness, old-fashionedness | 反义词：modernity, currency, contemporaneity | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From outmoded + -ness. Outmoded from out- + mode.  Mode from Old French mode, from Latin modus “measure, manner, fashion”.
