fairly:/ˈferli/ | adv. | 相当地，公平地，完全地 |  moderately; to a certain extent; justly; completely | She's a fairly good singer. 她唱歌还不错。| fairly accurately = 꽤 정확하게 (Korean) | very, quite, rather | unfairly | adverb

Etymology:
From Middle English *fairely*, from Old English *fæġerlice* (“fairly, beautifully, handsomely, suitably, fitly”), equivalent to fair +‎ -ly.
