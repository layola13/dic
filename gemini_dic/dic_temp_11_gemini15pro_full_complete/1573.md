achlamydate:/əˈklæmɪdeɪt/ | adj. | 无被的，裸露的 | Lacking a perianth (the outer part of a flower, consisting of the calyx and corolla).  A flower lacking sepals and petals. | Example: Achlamydate flowers are characteristic of some wind-pollinated plants.  无被花是一些风媒植物的特征。

Example: The achlamydate inflorescence was easily recognizable.  无被花序很容易辨认。 | Synonym: naked, aperianthous | Antonym: chlamydeous, perianthate | adjective
