echometry:/ɛˈkɒmətri/ | n. |回声测距，回声测深|The use of ultrasound waves to measure the size of or distance to an object, or to map the sea floor.  |例句：Echometry is used to determine the depth of the ocean. 回声测距用于确定海洋的深度。

|例句：Doctors use echometry to examine internal organs. 医生使用回声测距来检查内部器官。|近义词：sonometry, echo sounding, ultrasound |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
