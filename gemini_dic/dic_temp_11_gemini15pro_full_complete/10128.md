apollonic:/ˌæpəˈlɒnɪk/| adj.|阿波罗式的，和谐的，完美的，俊美的|Relating to the Greek god Apollo, characterized by beauty, harmony, intellect, and reason; serene and self-possessed.|例句：He had an Apollonic physique. 他拥有阿波罗般健美的体格。|近义词：beautiful, harmonious, graceful, elegant, serene, composed, classical|反义词：dionysian, chaotic, wild, passionate|词性:形容词
词源:From Greek *Apollōnios*, from *Apollōn* (Apollo).
