institutionary: /ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənəri/ | adj. | 机构的；制度的；习俗的 | Relating to, or characteristic of an institution; established by institution or custom.  | The report criticized the institutionary racism prevalent in the justice system.  这份报告批评了司法系统中普遍存在的制度性种族主义。| The orphanage followed strict institutionary rules. 这家孤儿院遵循严格的机构规定。 |  近义词: institutional, conventional, established, organizational | 反义词: unconventional, unorthodox, individual | 词性: adjective

词源: From "institution" + "-ary".  "Institution" comes from the Latin "institutio," meaning "establishment" or "instruction."
