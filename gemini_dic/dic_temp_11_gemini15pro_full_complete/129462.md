non-advertence:/ˌnɒn ədˈvɜːtəns/ | n. |  疏忽，不注意 | Lack of attention; failure to notice something. |  His non-advertence to the warning signs led to the accident.  他因疏忽警告标志而导致了事故。 | synonyms: inattention, heedlessness, oversight, negligence | antonyms: attention, heedfulness, vigilance, care | noun


non-advertent:/ˌnɒn ədˈvɜːtənt/ | adj. |  疏忽的，不注意的 | Not resulting from or achieved through deliberate planning. | The error was non-advertent, but still had serious consequences.  这个错误是无意的，但仍然造成了严重的后果。| synonyms: unintentional, accidental, unintended, unplanned | antonyms: intentional, deliberate, planned, advertent | adjective


词源:

From non- + advertence.  Advertence itself comes from Latin *advertere*, meaning "to turn to".
