neckland: /ˈnɛklənd/
noun
颈饰；颈部区域
An area around the neck, often decorated with jewelry or other adornments.  It can also refer to a necklace itself, especially a prominent or elaborate one.
The queen's neckland glittered with diamonds. 女王的颈饰闪耀着钻石的光芒。
Synonyms: necklace, gorget, collar
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly bare neck)
词性: 名词
