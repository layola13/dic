fire-eater:/ˈfaɪərˌiːtər/ | n. |吞火者；敢于冒险的人；好斗的人|A performer who pretends to eat fire.|The fire-eater swallowed a flaming torch.  吞火者吞下了一根燃烧的火把。|Synonyms: daredevil, stuntman, risk-taker|Antonyms: coward, timid person|Noun

fire-eater:/ˈfaɪərˌiːtər/ | n. |好争吵的人；喜欢吵架的人|A person who is aggressive and argumentative.|He was known as a fire-eater, always ready for a fight. 他被称为好斗者，总是准备打架。|Synonyms:  rabble-rouser, warmonger,  provocateur|Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator|Noun
