daze:/deɪz/
n.|昏迷，茫然|A state of stunned confusion or bewilderment.|He walked in a daze after being hit on the head. 他被打中头部后茫然地走着。
v.|使茫然，使昏迷|To stun or bewilder someone.|The bright lights dazed her.  明亮的灯光使她眼花缭乱。
近义词:stun, bewilder, confuse
反义词:enliven, invigorate
词性:名词, 动词

dazes:/ˈdeɪzɪz/
n.|昏迷，茫然（daze的复数形式）|Plural form of daze, referring to multiple instances of stunned confusion.|He experienced several dazes after the concussion. 脑震荡后，他经历了几次昏迷。
近义词: (For daze) stun, bewilder, confuse
反义词: (For daze) enliven, invigorate
词性:名词

词源:From Old Norse *dasask, "to become tired, exhausted."
