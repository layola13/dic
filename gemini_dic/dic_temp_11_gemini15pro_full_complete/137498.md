nontraceability:/ˌnɒnˌtreɪsəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可追溯性|The state or quality of being impossible to trace or track.|The nontraceability of cryptocurrency transactions is a concern for some governments. 加密货币交易的不可追溯性令一些政府担忧。|Synonyms: anonymity, untraceability|Antonyms: traceability, identifiability|noun
