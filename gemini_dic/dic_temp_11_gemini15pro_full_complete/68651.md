far-sighted:/ˌfɑːrˈsaɪtɪd/ /ˌfɑːrˈsʌɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有远见的；远视的 | Able to see things clearly that are far away but not things that are close. Also, able to understand what will or might happen in the future and to make wise plans. | She's a far-sighted leader who always considers the long-term consequences of her decisions. 她是一位有远见的领导者，总是考虑她决策的长期后果。

He made a far-sighted investment that paid off handsomely years later. 他进行了一项有远见的投资，几年后获得了丰厚的回报。


近义词：prescient, visionary, long-sighted | 反义词：short-sighted, myopic | 词性：形容词
