exquisitely:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪtli/
adverb
精巧地；优美地；精致地
In an exquisite manner; to a high or elaborate degree of excellence or refinement.
The cake was exquisitely decorated with delicate sugar flowers.  蛋糕用精致的糖花装饰得非常精美。
She sang exquisitely, moving the audience to tears. 她唱得优美动听，感动得观众热泪盈眶。
近义词:beautifully, delicately, elegantly, perfectly, superbly
反义词:crudely, roughly, poorly, clumsily
词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Latin exquisitus, past participle of exquirere "to search out," from ex- "out" + quaerere "to seek, ask."  The original sense was "carefully sought out," hence "choice, excellent."
