bucky:/ˈbʌki/ | n. |巴基，雄鹿，美元（俚语）| A male deer, antelope, hare, or rabbit, especially in its first year. Also a slang term for a dollar. | He saw a young bucky grazing in the meadow. 他看到一只年轻的雄鹿在草地上吃草。|  The cab fare was five bucky.  出租车费是五美元。 | synonyms: buck, stag, dollar | antonyms: doe (for deer), nothing specific for dollar | noun

buckyball:/ˈbʌkibɔːl/ | n. |巴基球| A spherical molecule of sixty carbon atoms (C60) having a structure resembling a geodesic dome. | Buckyballs have potential applications in nanotechnology. 巴基球在纳米技术中具有潜在的应用。| Scientists are exploring the properties of buckyballs. 科学家正在探索巴基球的特性。 | synonyms: fullerene, C60 | antonyms: N/A | noun
