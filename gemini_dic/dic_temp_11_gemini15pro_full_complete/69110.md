favelas:/fəˈvɛləz/| n. |贫民窟|A shantytown in or near a Brazilian city, especially Rio de Janeiro or São Paulo.  A low-income, informal urban area in Brazil characterized by substandard housing and limited access to basic services.|The documentary offered a glimpse into the harsh realities of life in the favelas of Rio de Janeiro. 这部纪录片让我们得以一窥里约热内卢贫民窟生活的严酷现实。

|近义词：slums, shantytowns|反义词: affluent neighborhoods, upscale communities|词性:noun

favelas:/fəˈvɛləz/| n. |贫民窟|A densely populated urban area characterized by substandard housing, often built by the residents themselves, and lacking in basic infrastructure and services.  The term is primarily used in Brazil.| Many people living in the favelas struggle with poverty and lack of opportunity. 许多住在贫民窟的人们与贫困和缺乏机会作斗争。
