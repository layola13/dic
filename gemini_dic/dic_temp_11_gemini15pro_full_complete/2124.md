adaptedness:/əˈdæptɪdnəs/
n.
适应性，适合性
The adaptedness of an organism to its environment is crucial for survival.
一个生物体对环境的适应性对于生存至关重要。
The camel's adaptedness to desert conditions is remarkable.
骆驼对沙漠环境的适应性非常显著。
Near synonyms: suitability, fitness, adaptability, adjustment, accommodation
Near antonyms: unsuitability, maladaptation, inadaptability
