frustratedly:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪdli/| adv.|沮丧地，懊恼地|In a frustrated manner; with frustration.  Expressing or showing frustration.|She sighed frustratedly and threw her pen on the desk. 她沮丧地叹了口气，把笔扔在桌子上。|Synonyms: angrily, irritably, crossly, peevishly|Antonyms: calmly, patiently, contentedly|词性:副词

词源: From the adjective "frustrated," which comes from the past participle of the verb "frustrate."  "Frustrate" originates from the Latin word "frustratus," the past participle of "frustrare," meaning "to deceive, disappoint, or make void."
