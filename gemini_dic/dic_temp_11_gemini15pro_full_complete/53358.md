disaffectedly:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛktɪdli/| adv.|不满地，不耐烦地|In a manner showing discontent and lack of trust; resentfully.|He sighed disaffectedly and slumped back in his chair. 他不满地叹了口气，瘫倒在椅子上。|Synonyms: resentfully, sullenly, rebelliously, grudgingly, unwillingly|Antonyms: enthusiastically, willingly, eagerly, contentedly, happily|词性:副词

disaffected:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛktɪd/| adj.|不满的，不耐烦的| Dissatisfied with the people in authority and no longer willing to support them.|Many young people feel disaffected by the current political system. 许多年轻人对目前的政治体制感到不满。|Synonyms: alienated, disgruntled, discontented, rebellious, resentful|Antonyms: loyal, contented, satisfied, supportive, enthusiastic|词性:形容词
