leprosies:/ˈlɛprəsiːz/
n.
麻风病（复数）
Leprosy is a chronic infectious disease caused by Mycobacterium leprae.
例句：Leprosies were once a widespread and feared disease.  麻风病曾经是一种广泛传播且令人恐惧的疾病。

例句：In the past, people with leprosies were often ostracized. 过去，麻风病人经常被排斥。
近义词：leprosy (单数)
反义词：health
词性：noun
