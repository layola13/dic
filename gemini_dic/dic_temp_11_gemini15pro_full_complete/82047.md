gristed:/ˈɡrɪstɪd/ | adj. | 懊恼的，愤怒的 | Feeling annoyed or irritated.  |  He was gristed about the traffic jam. 他对交通堵塞感到很恼火。 |  Example: She felt gristed after her flight was delayed. 她因为航班延误感到很懊恼。 | Synonyms: annoyed, irritated, vexed, irked, peeved | Antonyms: pleased, happy, content | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely a blend of "gritted" (past participle of "grit," meaning to clench one's teeth) and "pissed" (a vulgar slang term for angry). This etymology suggests a sense of suppressed anger or annoyance.
