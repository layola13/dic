bite:/baɪt/| n. v.|咬；一口；咬伤；上钩|An act of biting something.  The wound made by biting. A small meal. |The dog took a bite out of the steak. 狗咬了一口牛排。|Synonyms: nip, gnaw, munch, snack |Antonyms: release|noun, verb

bite:/baɪt/| v.|咬；刺痛；上当|To grip, cut into, or tear something with the teeth. To cause a smarting or stinging sensation.|The mosquito bite itched. 蚊子叮咬的地方很痒。|Synonyms: sting, nip |Antonyms: soothe|verb
