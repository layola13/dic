misoccupy: /ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪ/ /ˌmɪsˈɑːkjupaɪ/
verb
占用错误；使用不当；滥用
to occupy wrongly or improperly; to put to a wrong or improper use.
He misoccupied his time playing video games instead of studying. 他把时间浪费在玩电子游戏而不是学习上。
The funds were misoccupied by the corrupt officials. 资金被腐败官员滥用了。
misuse, abuse, misapply
use properly, employ correctly
v.
