ego-trip:/ˈiːɡoʊ trɪp/ | n. | 自我膨胀之旅；自我陶醉的行为 | An act or course of action that boosts one's self-esteem, often in a way that is perceived as excessive or arrogant by others. | He went on an ego trip after winning the award, bragging about it to everyone. 他获奖后自我膨胀，逢人就吹嘘。 |  She's always posting selfies - it's such an ego trip. 她总是发自拍 - 简直太自恋了。 |  近义词: self-aggrandizement, vanity, narcissism | 反义词: humility, modesty | 词性: noun
