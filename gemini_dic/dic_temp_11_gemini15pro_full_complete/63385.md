entoprocta:/ˈɛntoʊprɒktə/| n. |内肛动物门|A phylum of small, sessile, aquatic animals characterized by a crown of ciliated tentacles surrounding the mouth and anus.|Entoprocts are often found attached to rocks or other submerged surfaces. 内肛动物通常附着在岩石或其他水下表面。|近义词:bryozoan (苔藓虫, 较为接近但并非同义)|反义词:None (无明确反义词)|词性:noun (名词)

Etymology: From Greek *entos* (within) + *prōktos* (anus).
