fetidly:/ˈfetɪdli/ | adv. | 发臭地，恶臭地 |  In an offensive or disgusting manner, especially by emitting a foul odor.  Describes how something smells or is done in a way that relates to a foul smell. | The garbage fetidly filled the alleyway. 垃圾恶臭地堆满了小巷。| The wound fetidly oozed pus. 伤口散发着恶臭，流出脓液。 | Synonyms: offensively, disgustingly, putridly, rankly, foully | Antonyms: fragrantly, sweetly, pleasantly, aromatically | 词性: 副词

fetid:/ˈfetɪd/ | adj. |  发臭的，恶臭的 | Smelling extremely unpleasant; stinking. | The fetid swamp gave off a noxious odor.  发臭的沼泽散发出一股难闻的气味。|  The fetid air in the basement made me gag. 地下室里污浊的空气让我作呕。| Synonyms: stinking, foul, rank, putrid, noisome | Antonyms: fragrant, sweet, pleasant, aromatic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *fetidus*, from *fetēre* "to stink".
