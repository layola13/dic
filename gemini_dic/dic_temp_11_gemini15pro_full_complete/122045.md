Simple Present:
I make mistakes sometimes. 我有时会犯错误。
He often mistakes me for my brother. 他经常把我错认成我弟弟。

Present Continuous:
I am making a mistake by trusting him. 我正在犯一个错误，那就是相信他。
She is mistaking my kindness for weakness. 她把我的善良误解为软弱。

Present Perfect:
I have made a terrible mistake. 我犯了一个可怕的错误。
He has mistaken my intentions. 他误解了我的意图。

Present Perfect Continuous:
I have been making the same mistake over and over. 我一直在犯同样的错误。
She has been mistaking me for someone else all day. 她一整天都把我错认成别人。

Simple Past:
I made a mistake in my calculations. 我的计算出错了。
She mistook the house for her own. 她把那房子误认为是她自己的。

Past Continuous:
I was making a mistake when I interrupted him. 我打断他时犯了一个错误。
She was mistaking my silence for anger. 她把我的沉默误解为生气。

Past Perfect:
I had made a mistake before I realized it. 在我意识到之前，我已经犯了一个错误。
She had mistaken his politeness for affection. 她把他的礼貌误解为喜爱。

Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been making the same mistake for years. 多年来我一直犯着同样的错误。
She had been mistaking his intentions for months. 她几个月来一直误解他的意图。


Simple Future:
I will make mistakes, but I will learn from them. 我会犯错误，但我会从中吸取教训。
He will mistake me for someone else if I wear this hat. 如果我戴上这顶帽子，他会把我错认成别人。


Future Continuous:
I will be making dinner when you arrive. 你到的时候我正在做晚饭。
She will be mistaking my meaning if she doesn't read the whole letter. 如果她不读完整封信，她会误解我的意思。


Future Perfect:
By next week, I will have made a decision. 到下周，我将已经做出决定。
By then, she will have mistaken his silence for disapproval. 到那时，她会把他的沉默误解为不赞成。


Future Perfect Continuous:
By next year, I will have been working here for five years. 到明年，我将已经在这里工作五年了。
By the time she realizes it, she will have been mistaking his intentions for years. 等她意识到的时候，她已经误解他的意图好几年了。


Subjunctive Mood:
If I had known it was a mistake, I wouldn't have done it. 如果我知道这是一个错误，我就不会这样做了。
I wish I hadn't made that mistake. 我希望我没有犯那个错误。


Passive Voice:
A mistake was made in the report. 报告中有一个错误。
His intentions were mistaken. 他的意图被误解了。


Interrogative Sentence:
What mistake did you make? 你犯了什么错误？
What did you mistake it for? 你把它误认为是什么了？


Negative Sentence:
I didn't make a mistake. 我没有犯错。
She didn't mistake his meaning. 她没有误解他的意思。
