calculable:/ˈkælkjələbl/| adj.|可计算的，可估计的|Able to be calculated or estimated.  Something is calculable if its value or amount can be determined by calculation or estimation.|The risks involved are not easily calculable.  这些涉及的风险不容易计算。|Synonyms: computable, estimable, predictable, foreseeable|Antonyms: incalculable, unpredictable, immeasurable|Adjective


calculate:/ˈkælkjuleɪt/| v.|计算，估计，预测|1. To determine or estimate something by mathematical processes. 2. To make a judgment about what is likely to happen or what is the best course of action.|We calculated the cost of the project. 我们计算了项目的成本。|Synonyms: compute, reckon, estimate, figure, determine|Antonyms: guess, estimate (ironically, as estimation can be both a synonym and an antonym depending on the context)|Verb


calculation:/ˌkælkjuˈleɪʃn/| n.|计算，估计，预测|The process of calculating something; a mathematical determination of something.|The calculation of the total cost took several hours. 计算总成本花了几个小时。|Synonyms: computation, reckoning, estimation, figuring|Antonyms: guess, conjecture|Noun
