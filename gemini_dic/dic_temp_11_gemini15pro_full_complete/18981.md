例句：She bested all the other competitors and won the championship. 她战胜了所有其他选手，赢得了冠军。|近义词：defeating, conquering, overcoming, surpassing, triumphing over|反义词：losing to, being defeated by, succumbing to|词性: 动词 (verb) - present participle of "best"
