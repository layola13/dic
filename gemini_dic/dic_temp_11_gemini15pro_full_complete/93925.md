immunities:/ɪˈmjuːnɪtiz/
n.
免疫力；豁免权
The state of being exempt from or protected against something, especially a disease or the action of something harmful.
He has developed an immunity to the virus. 他对这种病毒产生了免疫力。
The diplomats enjoy immunity from prosecution. 外交官享有豁免起诉权。
Synonyms: resistance, protection, exemption
Antonyms: susceptibility, vulnerability
Noun
