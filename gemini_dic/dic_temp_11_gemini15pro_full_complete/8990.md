antiliberalnesses:/ˌæntiˈlɪbərəlnəsɪz/
|n.| 反自由主义；非自由主义 |The state of being opposed to liberal principles or policies.  This word highlights the various ways in which someone or something can be illiberal.|His antiliberalnesses manifested in his strict adherence to traditional values. 他的反自由主义体现在他对传统价值观的严格遵守。|近义词: illiberality, intolerance, conservatism|反义词: liberalism, tolerance, progressivism|词性: noun


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek "anti-")
liberal (adjective):  favorable to progress or reform, open-minded (from Latin "liberalis")
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns indicating a state, quality, or condition
