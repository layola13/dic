frontwise:/ˈfrʌntwaɪz/
adverb
向前地，正面朝前地
Relating to or seen from the front; in a forward direction.
The car was parked frontwise against the wall. 汽车车头朝前停靠在墙边。
近义词：forward, forwards, frontward
反义词：backward, backwards
词性：副词


frontwise:/ˈfrʌntwaɪz/
adjective
前面的，正面的
Situated at or relating to the front. (Less common usage)
The frontwise section of the house needs repainting.  房子的正面部分需要重新粉刷。
近义词：front, frontal, anterior
反义词：rear, back, posterior
词性：形容词

词源:  "front" + "-wise"  "front" 源自古法语 "front," 最终来自拉丁语 "frons" (额头，前面)。 "-wise" 是一个古英语后缀，表示“方向”或“方式”。
