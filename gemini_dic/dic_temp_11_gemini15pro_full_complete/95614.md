incompacted:/ˌɪnkəmˈpæktɪd/
adjective
未压实的，未夯实的
not compacted; loose.
The soil was incompacted and easily eroded by the rain. 雨水很容易侵蚀未压实的土壤。
Synonyms: loose, unconsolidated, uncompressed
Antonyms: compacted, compressed, consolidated
词性: 形容词
