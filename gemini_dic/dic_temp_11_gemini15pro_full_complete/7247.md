anglo-norman:/ˌæŋɡloʊ ˈnɔːrmən/
| adj. n.|盎格鲁-诺曼的；盎格鲁-诺曼人|Relating to the French dialect spoken by the Normans in England, especially from the time of the Norman Conquest until about 1400; a person of mixed Norman and English descent.|The Anglo-Norman language heavily influenced Middle English.|近义词:Norman French|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:adjective, noun
