kited:/ˈkaɪtɪd/ | v. | 使用空头支票欺骗;放风筝 | cheated or defrauded, especially using bad checks; flew a kite | He kited a check to pay for the car. 他开空头支票买车。| synonyms: defrauded, swindled, cheated, tricked | antonyms: paid, reimbursed | verb

kited:/ˈkaɪtɪd/ | adj. |  (支票)为空头的;像风筝的;兴奋的 | (of a check) worthless; resembling a kite; exhilarated, high-spirited |  The kited check bounced.  这张空头支票被退票了。  | synonyms: (for check) worthless, bad, fraudulent; (for resembling a kite) kite-like, airy; (for exhilarated) elated, joyful | antonyms: (for check) valid, good; (for exhilarated) depressed, sad | adjective
