impost:/ˈɪmpəʊst/ (US) /ˈɪmpoʊst/| n.|税;  (尤指)进口税;  负担;  强加|A tax, especially a tax on imported goods; a burden or something that is imposed.|The government imposed a new impost on imported cars. 政府对进口汽车征收新的税。|Synonyms: tax, duty, levy, tariff, excise, toll, tribute|Antonyms: exemption, deduction|noun

impost:/ˈɪmpəʊst/ (US) /ˈɪmpoʊst/| v.|征税;  强加|To impose something such as a tax, duty, or charge|The government has imposed a new tax on cigarettes. 政府对香烟征收了新的税。|Synonyms: levy, charge, assess, inflict, enforce|Antonyms: remove, repeal, abolish|verb
