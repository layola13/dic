bigeye:/ˈbɪɡˌaɪ/| n. |大眼鱼；金枪鱼的一种|A type of tuna fish characterized by its large eyes. |例句：We caught a bigeye on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼之旅中捕到了一条大眼鱼。

例句：Bigeye tuna are prized for their firm, flavorful flesh.  大眼金枪鱼因其紧实而美味的肉质而备受推崇。


近义词：tuna, ahi (specifically yellowfin tuna when referring to the fish used in sushi) |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a type of fish) |词性:noun
