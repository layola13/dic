gruner:/ˈɡruːnər/ | adj. n. |  绿色的（尤指葡萄酒）；格鲁纳·维特利纳（一种奥地利白葡萄品种）|  From German, meaning "green."  Refers to both the color of the grapes and the wine made from them. Can also be used as a noun to refer to the Grüner Veltliner grape variety. | The sommelier recommended a crisp gruner.侍酒师推荐了一杯清爽的格鲁纳葡萄酒。 | Synonyms: (for wine) greenish, herbaceous, vegetal; (for grape) Veltliner | Antonyms: (for wine) red, ripe, fruity | Adjective, Noun

gruner:/ˈɡruːnər/ | n. | 格鲁纳·维特利纳（一种奥地利白葡萄品种）| Shortened form of "Grüner Veltliner," an Austrian white wine grape variety.  | We enjoyed a bottle of gruner with dinner. 我们晚餐喝了一瓶格鲁纳葡萄酒。 | Synonyms: Veltliner, Grüner Veltliner | Antonyms:  N/A (grape variety) | Noun
