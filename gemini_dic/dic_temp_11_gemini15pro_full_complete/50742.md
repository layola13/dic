Derrida: /dəˈrɪdə/ | n. |德里达| Refers to Jacques Derrida (1930-2004), a French philosopher best known for developing a form of semiotic analysis known as deconstruction.  It can also refer to his philosophical theories and methods. |  "Derrida's work has had a profound impact on literary theory."  德里达的著作对文学理论产生了深远的影响. | 近义词: deconstructionist, post-structuralist | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "structuralist" in a philosophical context) | 词性: Noun
