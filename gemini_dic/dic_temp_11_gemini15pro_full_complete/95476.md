incivil:/ɪnˈsɪvəl/| adj.|不礼貌的，粗鲁的|Not polite; rude.|例句：His incivil behavior embarrassed everyone at the party. 他不礼貌的行为让派对上的每个人都很尴尬。

例句：It's incivil to interrupt someone while they are speaking. 打断别人说话是不礼貌的。|近义词：rude, impolite, disrespectful, discourteous|反义词：polite, courteous, respectful, civil|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + civil.
