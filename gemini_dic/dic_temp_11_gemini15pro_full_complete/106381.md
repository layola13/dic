konnichiwa:/kɔːn.nɪ.tʃi.wɑː/
interjection
你好(用于白天)
A Japanese greeting, typically used during the daytime.  It's a more formal version of "hello."
例句：Konnichiwa, Tanaka-san.  你好，田中先生。

例句：When I visited Japan, I greeted everyone with "Konnichiwa."  当我访问日本时，我用“Konnichiwa”问候每个人。

近义词：hello, hi (in English contexts), ohayou gozaimasu (Japanese, more formal, used in the morning), こんにちは (Japanese, same word in different writing system)
反义词:konbanwa (Japanese, good evening)
词性:interjection
