outwings:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋz/ | v. | 胜过，超越（尤指在速度或技巧方面）| To surpass or defeat, especially in speed or skill. | The new plane outwings all its competitors.  这架新飞机在速度上胜过所有竞争对手。|
The experienced pilot easily outwinged the novice.  经验丰富的飞行员轻易地超过了新手。| 近义词：surpass, outperform, outdo, excel, eclipse | 反义词：lag behind, trail, fall behind | 词性：动词

词源： "out-" (prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding") + "wing" (referring to flight or speed)
