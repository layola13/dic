backsliding: /ˈbækslaɪdɪŋ/
backsliding: n.
backsliding: 倒退，故态复萌，重新陷入坏习惯
backsliding: The act of returning to bad habits or a worse state after a period of improvement.
backsliding: After years of sobriety, he experienced a period of backsliding and began drinking again.  多年戒酒后，他又故态复萌，开始酗酒。
backsliding:  The country's backsliding on human rights is a cause for concern.  该国在人权方面倒退令人担忧。
backsliding: 近义词: relapse, regression, reversion, lapse, deterioration
backsliding: 反义词: progress, improvement, advancement
backsliding: 词性: 名词
