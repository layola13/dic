bloggers: /ˈblɒɡəz/ (UK) /ˈblɑːɡərz/ (US)| n. | 博主；写博客的人 | People who regularly write and publish content on a blog.  |  Example: Many bloggers write about their personal experiences. 许多博主写他们个人的经历。

Example:  Fashion bloggers often showcase new clothing trends.  时尚博主经常展示新的服装趋势。

| 近义词: online writers, web authors, content creators | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional journalists" in some contexts) | 词性: noun (plural)
