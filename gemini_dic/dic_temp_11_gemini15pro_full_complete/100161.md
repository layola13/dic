interreceiving:/ˌɪntərɪˈsiːvɪŋ/
| adj. |相互接收的，互相接收的 |The process of two or more entities receiving from each other simultaneously or reciprocally.  This implies a continuous or ongoing exchange. |The two computers were interreceiving data, constantly updating each other's files. 两台计算机正在相互接收数据，不断更新彼此的文件。|reciprocal, interactive, exchanging | unidirectional, one-way | adjective
