complex:/ˈkɒmplɛks/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmplɛks/ (US)| adj. n.|复杂的，复合的；综合体|A complex system is difficult to understand because it has many parts.  |This is a complex issue with no easy solutions. 这是一个复杂的问题，没有简单的解决办法。|complicated, intricate, involved|simple, straightforward|adjective
complex:/ˈkɒmplɛks/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmplɛks/ (US)| n.|综合体，复合体；情结|A complex is a group of buildings or facilities. |The sports complex includes a swimming pool and a gym. 这个体育中心包括一个游泳池和一个健身房。|compound, system, network|individual, single, separate|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin complexus, past participle of complecti (“to embrace, encompass, surround, comprise”), from com- (“together”) + plectere (“to weave, braid, plait, interweave”).
