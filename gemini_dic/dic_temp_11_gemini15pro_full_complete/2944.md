advection:/ædˈvɛkʃən/| n.| 平流，水平对流|The advection of warm air from the south caused the temperature to rise. 南方暖空气的平流导致气温上升。|例句：The advection of pollutants by the wind caused widespread contamination.  风对污染物的平流造成了大范围的污染。

例句：Ocean currents play a crucial role in the advection of heat around the globe. 洋流在地球周围热量的平流中起着至关重要的作用。

|近义词：convection, transport|反义词: diffusion|noun
