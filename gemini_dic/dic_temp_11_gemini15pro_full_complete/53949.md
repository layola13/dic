例句：Her discoverment of a hidden talent for painting surprised everyone. 她发现自己隐藏的绘画天赋让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：discovery, finding, unearthing, revelation|反义词：concealment, hiding|词性: 名词

词源:  From late Middle English *discoverment, from discover + -ment. Equivalent to discover + -ment.
