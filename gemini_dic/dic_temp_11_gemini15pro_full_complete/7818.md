antagonistical:/ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪkəl/
adjective
对抗性的；敌对的；反对的
Showing or feeling active opposition or hostility towards someone or something.
The two countries have had an antagonistic relationship for decades. 这两个国家几十年来一直关系敌对。
He displayed an antagonistic attitude towards the new policy. 他对新政策表现出敌对的态度。
近义词：hostile, opposed, inimical, adversarial
反义词：friendly, amicable, supportive, cooperative
词性：adjective
