insurancer:/ɪnˈʃʊərənsər/ | n. |保险人; 投保人|A person or entity that provides insurance.  Someone who takes out an insurance policy.| Example: The insurancer paid the claim promptly. 保险人迅速支付了索赔。

Example:  As an insurancer, you are responsible for paying your premiums. 作为投保人，您有责任支付保费。|Synonym: insurer, policyholder, underwriter (when referring to the company providing insurance) |Antonym: insured (when referring to the individual or entity being insured) | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From insure + -er.  Insure comes from Old French enseurer "to make certain, guarantee, assure," from en- "make" + seur "sure."
