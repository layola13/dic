ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chafer "to warm, heat," from Vulgar Latin *calefare "to make warm," from Latin calefacere "to make warm," from calere "to be warm" + facere "to make." The sense shifted from "to warm by rubbing" to "to make sore by rubbing."
