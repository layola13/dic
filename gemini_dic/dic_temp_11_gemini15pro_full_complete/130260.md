non-stick:/ˌnɒnˈstɪk/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈstɪk/ (US)| adj.|不粘的，防粘的|Covered with a special material that prevents food from sticking to it.|例句：I always use a non-stick pan for frying eggs. 我煎鸡蛋总是用不粘锅。|近义词：non-adhesive|反义词:stick, sticky|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".  It comes from Latin *nōn*.
"stick" comes from Old English *stician*, meaning "to pierce, stab, adhere".
