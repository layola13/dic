hang-gliding:/ˈhæŋˌɡlaɪdɪŋ/
n.|滑翔运动|The sport of flying using a hang-glider.|He went hang-gliding in the Swiss Alps. 他在瑞士阿尔卑斯山玩滑翔运动。|近义词:gliding, soaring|反义词:None
词性:名词


hang-glide:/ˈhæŋˌɡlaɪd/
v.|滑翔|To fly using a hang-glider.|She loves to hang-glide over the valley. 她喜欢在山谷上空滑翔。|近义词:glide, soar|反义词:None
词性:动词

词源: "hang-gliding" and "hang-glide" are derived from the action of hanging from a glider and gliding through the air. "Hang" comes from Old English *hangian, and "glide" comes from Old English *glīdan.
