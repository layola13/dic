dandling:/ˈdændlɪŋ/
v.
摇晃，轻晃；逗弄
moving something gently up and down, especially a baby
She was dandling the baby on her knee. 她把婴儿放在膝盖上摇晃。

近义词: swinging, rocking, swaying
反义词: stilling, holding steady
词性: Verb (present participle)


dandle:/ˈdændl/
vt.
摇晃，轻晃；逗弄
to move a baby or young child up and down gently in your arms or on your knee
He dandled the child on his knee. 他把孩子放在膝盖上摇晃。

近义词: bounce, jiggle, rock
反义词: drop, ignore
词性: Verb


词源:
From Middle English *dandlen, frequentative of *danden (“to dance”), from Old French dandiner, of uncertain origin, probably from Frankish *dandōn (“to swing, sway”), from Proto-Germanic *dandōną (“to stretch, extend”).
