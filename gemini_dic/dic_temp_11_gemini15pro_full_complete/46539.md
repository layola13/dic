dalesman:/ˈdeɪlzmən/| n.|山谷居民，住在谷地的人|A man who lives in a dale (valley).|例句：The dalesman led us through the winding paths of the valley.  这位山谷居民带领我们穿过山谷蜿蜒的小路。

例句：The dalesman knew every nook and cranny of the hills. 这位山谷居民对这片山丘的每个角落都了如指掌。|近义词：valley dweller, highlander, mountaineer|反义词:lowlander, plainsman|词性:noun

Etymology: From 'dale' (Old English *dæl  meaning "valley") + 'man' (Old English mann).
