cypseliform:/sɪpˈsɛlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
形似瘦果的，菊果形的
Resembling a cypsela, a type of dry, single-seeded fruit formed from a double ovary, of which only one develops a seed, as in the dandelion or sunflower.
The dandelion seed head is a classic example of a cypseliform structure.  蒲公英的种子头是形似瘦果结构的典型例子。
The plant produces small, cypseliform fruits that are dispersed by the wind. 这种植物会结出细小、形似瘦果的果实，并由风传播。
Near Synonyms: achene-like, cypsela-like
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, as it describes a specific shape)
Word Origin: From Greek "kypsele" (a hollow vessel) + Latin "-formis" (shaped like).
