motive's:/ˈmoʊtɪvz/| n.|动机，目的|The detective was trying to understand the killer's motives.  侦探试图理解凶手的动机。|Synonyms: reasons, intentions, aims, objectives|Antonyms: disincentives, deterrents|noun, possessive form of "motive"

Etymology:
From Late Middle English motif, from Old French motif, from Late Latin motivus (“moving, impelling”), from Latin moveō (“I move”).
