acrylamide:/əˈkrɪləmaɪd/ | n. | 丙烯酰胺 | A colorless, crystalline, water-soluble solid used in the production of various polymers and resins. It is also formed in some foods when cooked at high temperatures. |  Acrylamide is a chemical compound that can form in starchy foods when they are cooked at high temperatures. 丙烯酰胺是一种化学化合物，在淀粉类食物高温烹饪时会形成。

Acrylamide is considered a potential carcinogen. 丙烯酰胺被认为是一种潜在的致癌物。 | 近义词: 2-propenamide | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
