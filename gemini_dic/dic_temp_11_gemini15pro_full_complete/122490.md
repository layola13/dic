mocha:/ˈmoʊkə/ (US) /ˈmɒkə/ (UK)| n.|摩卡咖啡；摩卡色|A type of coffee made with espresso, chocolate syrup, steamed milk, and often whipped cream.  Also, a reddish-brown color similar to that of the coffee.|

例句：
I ordered a mocha latte with extra whipped cream.
我点了一杯摩卡拿铁，加了很多奶油。

例句：
She wore a mocha-colored sweater.
她穿了一件摩卡色的毛衣。

近义词：caffe mocha, mochaccino|
反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of coffee or color. Could use contrasting colors like "white" for mocha the color.)|
词性: noun
