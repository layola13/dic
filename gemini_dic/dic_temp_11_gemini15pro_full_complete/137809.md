nonverminousness:/ˌnɒnvɜːrˈmɪnəsnəs/
noun
无害性；非害虫性
The state or quality of not being verminous; freedom from vermin.
The inspector certified the nonverminousness of the warehouse. 检查员证明了仓库没有害虫。
Near synonyms: cleanliness, hygiene, sanitation
Antonyms: verminousness, infestation
词性: noun
