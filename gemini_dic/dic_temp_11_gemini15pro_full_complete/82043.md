grisly:/ˈɡrɪzli/ | adj. |  可怕的，恐怖的，令人毛骨悚然的 | Causing horror or disgust; gruesome. | The scene of the accident was grisly.  事故现场惨不忍睹。| 近义词：gruesome, horrific, macabre, ghastly | 反义词：pleasant, delightful, agreeable | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *grislich, *griislich (“horrible, dreadful, terrible”), from Old English grislic (“terrible, horrible, dreadful, inspiring terror”), from grīs (“horror, terror”) +‎ -lic (“-ly”).  More at agrise.
