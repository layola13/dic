commenced:/kəˈmɛnst/| v.|开始，着手|to begin, to start|The meeting commenced at 9 am. 会议上午九点开始。|The construction of the new building commenced last month. 新大楼的建设于上个月开始。|近义词：begin, start, initiate, inaugurate|反义词:end, finish, conclude, terminate|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old French *comencier, from Late Latin *cominitiare, from Latin cominitiō (“a beginning”), from com (“with, together”) + initium (“beginning”).
