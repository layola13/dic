adversarial:/ˌædvərˈsɛriəl/ | adj. | 对抗性的，敌对的 | Characterized by or involving conflict or opposition.  Relating to or characteristic of an adversary or adversaries. | The two countries have an adversarial relationship. 这两个国家的关系是对抗性的。|  The lawyer adopted an adversarial stance during the cross-examination.  律师在盘问时采取了对抗的姿态。 | antagonistic, hostile, opposed, conflicting |  cooperative, friendly, supportive | adjective

adversary:/ˈædvərˌsɛri/ | n. | 对手，敌手 | One's opponent in a contest, conflict, or dispute. | He defeated his old adversary in the election. 他在选举中击败了他的老对手。|  She faced her adversary in court. 她在法庭上面对她的对手。 | opponent, rival, foe, antagonist, enemy | ally, friend, supporter, partner | noun
