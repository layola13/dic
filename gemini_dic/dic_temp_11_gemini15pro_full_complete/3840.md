agonizing:/ˈæɡənaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|极度痛苦的；令人痛苦的|Causing great physical or mental pain.|例句：She described the agonizing pain in her back. 她描述了背部极度的疼痛。|例句：It was an agonizing decision to make.  这是一个令人痛苦的决定。|近义词：excruciating, tormenting, harrowing|反义词：pleasurable, comforting|词性:形容词
agonize:/ˈæɡənaɪz/| v.|（使）极度痛苦；苦思冥想|To suffer extreme physical or mental pain; to worry excessively about something.|例句：He agonized over the decision for weeks. 他为这个决定苦恼了几个星期。|例句：She agonized about whether to tell him the truth. 她苦苦思索是否要告诉他真相。|近义词：worry, fret, anguish|反义词：relax, rejoice|词性:动词
