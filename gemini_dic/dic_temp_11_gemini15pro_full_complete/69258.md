feather-brained: /ˈfeðərˌbreɪnd/ | adj. | 轻浮的；愚蠢的；头脑简单的 |  Having or showing a lack of intelligence or good judgment; silly and frivolous.  Derived from the image of a bird with a head full of feathers, implying lightness and lack of substance. |  She made a feather-brained decision to quit her job without another offer. 她轻率地决定在没有其他工作机会的情况下辞职。 |  He's so feather-brained, he forgets where he parked his car almost every day. 他太糊涂了，几乎每天都忘记把车停在哪里了。 |  silly, frivolous, foolish, giddy, flighty, scatterbrained, dizzy |  sensible, intelligent, wise, prudent, level-headed | adjective
