chartomancy:/ˈkɑːrtəʊmænsi/ (US) /ˈtʃɑːrtəʊmænsi/ (UK)| n.|纸牌占卜|Divination of the future using playing cards. It involves interpreting the meanings of the cards and their combinations to gain insight into a person's life or future events.|She practiced chartomancy to predict the future.|近义词：cartomancy, card reading|反义词: None|noun
