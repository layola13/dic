obstructivity:/əbˌstrʌkˈtɪvɪti/
noun
阻塞性；阻碍性
The quality or state of being obstructive.  This refers to the degree to which something hinders or blocks flow, typically in a physiological context like airflow in the lungs.
Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) is characterized by increased airway obstructivity. 慢性阻塞性肺疾病的特点是气道阻塞性增加。
The doctor measured the obstructivity of her airways. 医生测量了她气道的阻塞性。
近义词: blockage, obstruction, impedance, resistance
反义词: patency, openness, permeability
词性: noun

obstructive:/əbˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
阻塞的；阻碍的
Tending to obstruct; hindering. Something that blocks or impedes progress, development, or flow.
The obstructive nature of the tumor made breathing difficult. 肿瘤的阻塞性使呼吸困难。
His obstructive behavior was preventing the project from moving forward. 他的阻碍行为使项目无法推进。
近义词: hindering, impeding, blocking, inhibiting
反义词: helpful, facilitating, aiding, assisting
词性: adjective

词源: From obstruct + -ive + -ity.  Obstruct comes from Latin obstructus, past participle of obstruere "to build in front of, block up," from ob "against" + struere "to pile, build."
