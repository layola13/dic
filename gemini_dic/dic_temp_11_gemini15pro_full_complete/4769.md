algarot:/ˈælɡərɒt/ | n. | 阿尔加罗特粉，氧化锑 | A white powder formerly used as an emetic and purgative, consisting of a mixture of antimonious oxide and oxychloride. It is produced by the hydrolysis of antimony trichloride. |  Algarot was a popular medicine in the 17th century.  阿尔加罗特粉在17世纪是一种流行的药物。 | Synonym:  powder of Algarot, antimonious oxychloride | Antonym:  N/A | noun
