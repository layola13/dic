corpusculous:/kɔːrˈpʌskjʊləs/ | adj. |微小的；由微粒组成的| Relating to corpuscles or consisting of corpuscles; minute. |The corpusculous theory of light suggests that light is made up of tiny particles. 光的微粒理论认为光是微小粒子组成的。|The scientist examined the corpusculous matter under the microscope. 科学家在显微镜下检查了微粒物质。|近义词：minute, tiny, microscopic, particulate |反义词: huge, massive, macroscopic |词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin corpusculum (small body), diminutive of corpus (body).
