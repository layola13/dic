magistratical:/ˌmædʒɪˈstreɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 行政裁判的；地方官员的；有地方官员权力的 | Relating to a magistrate or the office of a magistrate; having the authority of a magistrate. |  The magistrate took a magistratical approach to the case. 地方官员对这个案子采取了地方官员的处理方式。| Synonyms: judicial, official, authoritative | Antonyms: unofficial, unauthorized | adjective

magistratical:/ˌmædʒɪˈstreɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 似法官的；威严的；有权威的 | Resembling or characteristic of a magistrate, especially in being authoritative or dignified. |  He delivered his pronouncements with a magistratical air. 他以一种威严的姿态发表了他的声明。| Synonyms: dignified, stately, imposing, august | Antonyms: undignified, informal, humble | adjective
