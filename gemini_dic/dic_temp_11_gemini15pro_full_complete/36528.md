collaborate:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/| v. |合作，协作|To work together with someone or a group of people, especially on a project or artistic creation.|We collaborated on a research project. 我们在一个研究项目上合作。|近义词：cooperate, partner, team up|反义词: compete, oppose|verb

collaborate:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/| v. |勾结，通敌（通常指与敌人）|To cooperate traitorously with an enemy.|He was accused of collaborating with the enemy during the war. 他被指控在战争期间与敌人勾结。|近义词：conspire, collude|反义词: resist, defy|verb
