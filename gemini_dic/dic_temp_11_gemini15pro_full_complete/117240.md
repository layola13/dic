mecate:/məˈkɑːteɪ/ | n. |  measuring rope, measuring chain; especially a rope about 100 meters long used in Mexico and the southwestern U.S. | A mecate is a rope or chain used for measuring, particularly a rope approximately 100 meters long used in Mexico and the southwestern U.S.  |  The surveyor used a mecate to measure the boundaries of the property.  测量员用一根mecate来测量地产的边界。 | Synonyms: rope, chain, cord, line | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

mecate:/məˈkɑːteɪ/ | n. |  (Mexico, southwestern US) a unit of land area equal to 100 square varas (about 70 sq m/ 83 sq yd). | A mecate can also refer to a unit of land area, specifically 100 square varas. |  He owns several mecates of land in the valley.  他在山谷里拥有几块mecate的土地。| Synonyms:  area, land measure, unit of area | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
