antirationalistic:/ˌæntiˌræʃəˈnəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反理性主义的
Opposed to or distrustful of reason and rationalism, emphasizing intuition, feeling, or faith.
His antirationalistic worldview led him to reject scientific explanations in favor of mystical interpretations.
近义词: irrational, nonrational, mystical, intuitive
反义词: rational, rationalistic, logical, empirical
词性: adjective
