noncorrective:/ˌnɒnkəˈrɛktɪv/
adjective
非矫正的，不纠正的
Not intended to correct or rectify something.
The teacher used a noncorrective approach, allowing students to explore their own ideas without immediate feedback. 老师采用了一种非纠正的方法，允许学生在没有立即反馈的情况下探索自己的想法。
The software provides noncorrective feedback, simply highlighting potential issues without suggesting changes.  该软件提供非矫正反馈，只是突出潜在问题而不建议更改。
Synonyms: non-intervening, passive, permissive
Antonyms: corrective, remedial, rectifying
词性: adjective
