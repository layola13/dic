exossation:/ˌɛksɒˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 骨骼化，骨骼生长 |The process of bone formation, especially the ossification of cartilage.  Also, the abnormal development of bone in soft tissue.|The exossation of the cartilage began in the third trimester.  软骨的骨化在妊娠晚期开始。|Near synonyms: ossification, osteogenesis| Antonyms:  decalcification, resorption|noun
