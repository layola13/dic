disbeliefs:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːfs/
n.
不相信，怀疑
The state of not believing something; lack of belief.
Her statement was met with widespread disbeliefs. 她的陈述遭到广泛的怀疑。
synonyms: doubts, skepticism, incredulity
antonyms: beliefs, faith, trust
noun
