elucidatory:/ɪˈluːsɪdəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 说明性的，阐明的 | Making something clear; explanatory. |  The professor's elucidatory comments helped the students understand the complex theorem. 教授的解释性评论帮助学生理解了这个复杂的定理。|  The accompanying diagram was elucidatory, making the instructions easy to follow.  附图具有说明性，使说明书易于遵循。| 近义词: explanatory, clarifying, illuminating, interpretive | 反义词: confusing, obscure, ambiguous | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *elucidatorius, from Latin elucidatus (past participle of elucidare “to make clear, explain”), from e- “out” + lucidus “clear, bright, shining” (from lux “light”).
