diaeretic:/ˌdaɪəˈrɛtɪk/| adj. n.|分音符号的；分音符号|A diaeretic is a mark, such as two dots ( ¨ ), placed over a vowel to indicate a different pronunciation.|The word "naïve" uses a diaeretic.|近义词：umlaut, trema|反义词:|adjective, noun
