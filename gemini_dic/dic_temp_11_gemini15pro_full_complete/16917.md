basking:/ˈbæskɪŋ/| v. adj.| 曝晒，享受温暖；沉浸于；|  Lie exposed to warmth and light, typically from the sun, for relaxation and pleasure; reveling or luxuriating in something.| The lizards were basking in the sun on the rocks. 蜥蜴在岩石上晒太阳。|近义词：sunbathing, luxuriating, wallowing, reveling|反义词:shivering, freezing|词性: verb, adjective (present participle of "bask")

basking:/ˈbæskɪŋ/| adj.| 曝晒的，沉浸于的| Exposed to warmth and light, typically from the sun; reveling or luxuriating in something.|  A basking shark filtered plankton from the water. 一条姥鲨正在水中过滤浮游生物。|近义词：sun-drenched, sunlit|反义词:chilled, cold|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *baðask "to bathe oneself," reflexive of baða "to bathe."
