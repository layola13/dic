iodothyrin:/ˌaɪoʊdoʊˈθaɪrɪn/
n.
碘甲状腺原氨酸; 甲状腺激素
A hormone of the thyroid gland containing iodine.
Iodothyrin plays a crucial role in metabolism. 碘甲状腺原氨酸在新陈代谢中起着至关重要的作用。
Iodothyrin deficiency can lead to hypothyroidism. 碘甲状腺原氨酸缺乏会导致甲状腺功能减退。
近义词: thyroid hormone, thyroxine
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider antagonists like thyroid hormone inhibitors)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *iodo-* (combining form of iodine) + *-thyrin* (related to thyroid).
