ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "chop," meaning to cut into pieces.  The "-er" suffix denotes an agent or tool that performs the action of the verb. Ultimately from Old English *ceapian "to buy, sell, barter," from Proto-Germanic *kaupōną. The meaning shifted to "to cut" in Middle English.
