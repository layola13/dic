nonadministrative:/ˌnɑːnədˈmɪnɪstreɪtɪv/
adjective
非行政的
Not related to administration; not concerned with the management of an organization.
The nonadministrative staff focuses on research and development.  非行政人员专注于研究和开发。
The project required both administrative and nonadministrative personnel.  这个项目需要行政和非行政人员。
Synonyms: non-managerial, non-executive, operational
Antonyms: administrative, managerial, executive
词性: adjective
