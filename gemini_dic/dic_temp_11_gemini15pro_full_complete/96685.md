inebriant:/ɪnˈbriːənt/
n. adj.|醉人的东西；令人陶醉的东西|Something that causes intoxication or drunkenness. Can be used as a noun to refer to the intoxicating substance itself, or as an adjective to describe something that has intoxicating properties.|The potent inebriant quickly caused him to lose his senses.  这强效的醉人剂很快使他失去了知觉。|
The sweet scent of the flowers was almost inebriant. 花的甜香味几乎令人陶醉。|
近义词：intoxicant, narcotic, stimulant|
反义词：sobering, calming|
词性: noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inebriant-, present participle stem of inebriare "to intoxicate," from in- "in, into" + ebriare "to make drunk," from ebrius "drunk."
