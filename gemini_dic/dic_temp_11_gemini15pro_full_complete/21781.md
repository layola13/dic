bobbies:/ˈbɒbiz/| n.|警察 (尤指英国的)|A British slang term, often affectionate, for police officers.  It originates from the name of Sir Robert Peel, who established the Metropolitan Police Force in London.|例句：The bobbies were on patrol in the city centre.  市中心的警察正在巡逻。

例句：The local bobbies knew everyone in the neighbourhood. 当地的警察认识附近的每个人。


|近义词：police officers, cops, constables|反义词:criminals, lawbreakers|词性:noun
