depopulated:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
人口减少的，荒无人烟的
Reduced in population; deserted.
The war depopulated the village.  战争使这个村庄人口减少。
Synonyms: deserted, barren, empty, uninhabited
Antonyms: populated, crowded, teeming, bustling
词性: 形容词


depopulate:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪt/
verb
使人口减少；减少…的人口
To reduce the population of; to deprive of inhabitants.
The plague depopulated the entire region. 瘟疫使整个地区人口减少。
Synonyms: devastate, deplete, decimate
Antonyms: populate, inhabit
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (indicating removal or reversal) + populate (from Latin populus, "people").
