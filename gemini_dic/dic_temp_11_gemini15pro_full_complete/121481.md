mislabels:/ˌmɪsˈleɪblz/
|v. (third-person singular simple present mislabels, present participle mislabelling, simple past and past participle mislabelled)|错误标记，贴错标签|To apply a wrong label to something; to label incorrectly.|The factory mislabelled the boxes, sending the wrong products to the stores. 工厂贴错了标签，把错误的产品送到了商店。|misnames, misidentifies|correctly labels|verb

mislabel:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbl/
|n.|错误标签|A label that is incorrect.|The mislabel on the package caused confusion. 包装上的错误标签引起了混乱。|misnomer, error|correct label|noun
