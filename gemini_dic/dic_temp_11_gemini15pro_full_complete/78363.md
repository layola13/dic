geste:/ʒɛst/| n.|姿态，手势，举动，行为；(尤指)装腔作势的举动|A gesture is a movement of part of the body, especially a hand or the head, to express an idea or meaning.|He made a welcoming gesture with his hand. 他用手做了一个欢迎的手势。|Synonyms: action, motion, movement, sign, signal|Antonyms: inaction, stillness|noun
