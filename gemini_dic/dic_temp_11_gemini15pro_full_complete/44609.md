crowdedly:/ˈkraʊdɪdli/| adv.|拥挤地，密集地|In a crowded manner; with many people close together.  The concert hall was crowdedly filled with enthusiastic fans.|近义词：densely, thickly, compactly|反义词: sparsely, thinly|词性:副词|词源: From "crowded" + "-ly"  "Crowded" comes from the Old English "crudan" meaning "to press, crowd."
