cherisher:/ˈtʃerɪʃər/| n. |珍爱的人；爱护者；怀有（美好想法）的人|Someone who holds something dear and treats it with affection.  A person who protects and cares for something. |She is a cherisher of old traditions. 她是一位珍视古老传统的人。|近义词：protector, guardian, custodian, nurturer, supporter|反义词：neglecter, abuser, destroyer|noun
