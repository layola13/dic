defrauding:/dɪˈfrɔːdɪŋ/
verb
欺骗，诈取
The act of illegally obtaining money or goods from someone by deception.
He was found guilty of defrauding the company of millions of dollars. 他被判犯有诈骗公司数百万美元的罪行。
The lawyer was disbarred for defrauding his clients.  这名律师因诈骗客户被取消了律师资格。
cheating, swindling, deceiving
honest dealing, fair play
v.
