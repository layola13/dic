overshadowingly:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædoʊɪŋli/
adv.
压倒性地，使黯然失色地
in a way that overshadows something else; so as to overshadow something.
Her success was overshadowingly impressive. 她的成功令人印象深刻，使其他一切黯然失色。
Synonym: dominantly, overwhelmingly, eclipsingly
Antonym: insignificantly, subtly, unimpressively
词性: 副词
