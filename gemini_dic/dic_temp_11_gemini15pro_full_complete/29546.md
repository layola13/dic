catchdrain:/ˈkætʃˌdreɪn/| n.|集水沟，截水沟|A drain installed to intercept and carry away surface water.|The catchdrain prevented the parking lot from flooding during heavy rain. 集水沟防止了停车场在大雨期间被淹。|近义词：drain, gutter, culvert|反义词: (no direct antonym)|noun

词源:  "catch" (from Old French "cachier," meaning "to chase, hunt, capture") + "drain" (from Old English "drennan," meaning "to cause to flow").  The word combines the idea of capturing or intercepting water and then draining it away.
