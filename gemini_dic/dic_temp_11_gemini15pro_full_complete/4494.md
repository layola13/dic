albino:/ælˈbaɪnoʊ/ | n. adj. |白化病患者，白化体的 |A person or animal with albinism.  A genetic condition characterized by a deficiency or absence of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes, resulting in pale skin, white or light-colored hair, and pink or red eyes.|  The albino rabbit stood out against the dark grass. 白化兔子在深色的草地上很显眼。 |  synonyms: amelanistic, amelanic | antonyms: pigmented | noun, adjective
