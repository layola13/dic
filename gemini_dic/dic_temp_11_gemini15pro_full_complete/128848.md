nigritude:/ˈnɪɡrɪtjuːd/ /ˈniːɡrɪtjuːd/ | n. | 黑人性; 黑人文化 | The quality or fact of being of black African origin; the affirmation or consciousness of the value of black or African culture, heritage, and identity. |  The concept of negritude was developed by francophone intellectuals and writers in Paris in the 1930s.  这些讲法语的知识分子和作家在20世纪30年代的巴黎发展了黑人性概念。|  Negritude became a significant literary and ideological movement celebrating Black identity. 黑人性成为一场重要的文学和意识形态运动，歌颂黑人身份。 | synonyms: Blackness, Afrocentrism,  Black consciousness | antonyms: Whiteness, Eurocentrism | noun
