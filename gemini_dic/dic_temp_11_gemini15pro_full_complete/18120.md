behindhand:/bɪˈhaɪndhænd/ | adj. | 落后的，迟到的，拖欠的 | late or overdue, especially in payments or work; not up to date |  Example: He was behindhand with the rent. 他拖欠了房租。| Example:  She is behindhand in her studies. 她的学习落后了。 | Synonyms: overdue, late, delayed, tardy, lagging | Antonyms:  ahead, early, prompt, punctual | adjective

behindhand:/bɪˈhaɪndhænd/ | adv. | 落后地，迟到地 | in arrears; late | Example:  We're a bit behindhand with the preparations for the party.  我们的派对准备工作有点落后了。| Example: The project is running behindhand because of bad weather.  由于天气不好，项目进度落后了。| Synonyms: late, tardily | Antonyms:  early, promptly | adverb
