nags:/næɡz/| v.| 唠叨，不断抱怨|  To repeatedly criticize or complain to someone about something in a way that is annoying. |She nags him constantly about his messy habits. 她总是抱怨他不整洁的习惯。|My mom nags me about cleaning my room. 我妈妈总是唠叨我打扫房间。|近义词：pester, harass, badger|反义词: praise, commend|词性:动词

nags:/næɡz/| n.| 唠叨，抱怨| Repeated criticisms or complaints.| Her constant nags about his weight were starting to annoy him. 她不断地抱怨他的体重开始让他感到厌烦。|The child's nags for a new toy finally wore down his parents. 孩子不断地要新玩具最终让他的父母妥协了。|近义词：carping, complaints, criticisms|反义词: compliments, praise|词性:名词
