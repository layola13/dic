laurates:/ˈlɔːreɪts/| n.|月桂酸盐；十二酸盐|Salts of lauric acid, a saturated fatty acid.  These are often used in soaps, shampoos, and other personal care products because of their cleansing and foaming properties.|Sodium laurate is a common ingredient in soap. 月桂酸钠是肥皂中的常见成分。

Potassium laurate is used in some shampoos. 月桂酸钾用于一些洗发水中。|近义词：esters (酯类, though not always interchangeable), fatty acid salts (脂肪酸盐)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
