counter-questioning:/ˌkaʊntərˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 反诘；反问 | The act of responding to a question with another question, often to challenge the original questioner or to avoid answering directly. |  "His counter-questioning revealed the inconsistencies in her story." 他的反问揭示了她故事中的矛盾之处。 | synonyms: cross-examination, interrogation, quizzing | antonyms: answering, replying, responding | noun

counter-question:/ˌkaʊntərˈkwɛstʃən/ | n. | 反问; 反诘 | A question posed in response to another question, often to challenge the original questioner or to avoid answering directly. | "She met his accusation with a counter-question: 'Were you there?'" 她用反问回应他的指责：“你在那里吗？” | synonyms: retort, rejoinder, riposte | antonyms: answer, reply, response | noun

counter-question:/ˌkaʊntərˈkwɛstʃən/ | v. | 反问；反诘 | To respond to a question with another question, often to challenge the original questioner or to avoid answering directly. | "Instead of answering directly, the politician counter-questioned the reporter."  这位政客没有直接回答，而是反问了记者。 | synonyms: cross-examine, interrogate, quiz | antonyms: answer, reply, respond | verb
