nonromantic:/ˌnɑːnroʊˈmæntɪk/ |adj.| 非浪漫的；不浪漫的；缺乏浪漫色彩的|Not characterized by or suggestive of romance.|例句：They have a very nonromantic relationship. 他们的关系很不浪漫。|例句：The setting for the dinner was surprisingly nonromantic.  晚餐地点出奇地不浪漫。|近义词：unromantic, practical, pragmatic|反义词：romantic, sentimental|词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + romantic (from Old French romanz "verse narrative," later "prose narrative of love and adventure")
