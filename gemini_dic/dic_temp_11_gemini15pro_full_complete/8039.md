anthropocentricity: /ˌænθrəpəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪti/ | n. | 人类中心主义 |  The belief that human beings are the most important entity in the universe and that all other things are considered in relation to humankind. |  The anthropocentricity of our worldview prevents us from fully appreciating the intrinsic value of other species.  人类中心主义的世界观阻碍了我们充分认识其他物种的内在价值。 |  human-centeredness, homocentrism | ecocentrism, biocentrism | noun
