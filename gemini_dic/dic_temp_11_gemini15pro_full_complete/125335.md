multilateralists:/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪsts/| n. |多边主义者|People who support multilateralism, the principle of cooperation between multiple countries. |例句：Multilateralists believe that global challenges require global solutions. 多边主义者认为，全球挑战需要全球解决方案。

例句：The conference attracted multilateralists from all over the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的多边主义者。|近义词：internationalists, cosmopolitans|反义词：unilateralists, isolationists| Noun
