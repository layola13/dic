leadenly:/ˈlɛdənli/ | adv. | 沉重地；迟钝地；呆滞地 | In a heavy, dull, or sluggish manner, like lead.  |  He walked leadenly across the room, his shoulders slumped. 他沉重地走过房间，肩膀耷拉着。 |  She spoke leadenly, her voice barely above a whisper. 她说话迟钝，声音几乎听不见。 | heavily, dully, sluggishly, listlessly | lightly, briskly, energetically | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From leaden + -ly.  "Leaden" comes from Old English "lēaden," meaning "made of lead." The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
