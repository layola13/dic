freethinking:/ˈfriːˌθɪŋkɪŋ/
adjective
自由思考的
Characterized by independent and unconventional thinking, especially on religious or social matters.
She was known for her freethinking views on marriage. 她以其对婚姻的自由思考的观点而闻名。
synonyms: independent-minded, unorthodox, radical, liberal, progressive, open-minded, unprejudiced, tolerant
antonyms: conventional, orthodox, conservative, dogmatic, narrow-minded, prejudiced, intolerant
词性: adjective

freethinking:/ˈfriːˌθɪŋkɪŋ/
noun
自由思想
The practice of forming opinions on the basis of reason independently of authority, especially in matters of religious belief.
Freethinking was once considered a dangerous activity. 自由思想曾经被认为是一种危险的活动。
synonyms: independent thought, rationalism, skepticism, nonconformity
antonyms: conformity, orthodoxy, dogmatism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From free + thinking.  The adjective dates back to the late 17th century, originally referring to those who rejected religious dogma.  The noun form emerged slightly later.
