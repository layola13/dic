furrowless:/ˈfɜːroʊləs/| adj.|无皱纹的，光滑的|Smooth and unwrinkled.|Her furrowless brow showed no signs of worry. 她光滑的额头没有一丝担忧的痕迹。|例句：The baby's skin was soft and furrowless. 婴儿的皮肤柔软光滑。|近义词：smooth, unlined, unwrinkled|反义词: wrinkled, lined, furrowed|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From 'furrow' (a trench or groove) + '-less' (suffix indicating absence). 'Furrow' comes from Old English *furh, from Proto-Germanic *furhō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *perkʷ- (to cut, scratch).
