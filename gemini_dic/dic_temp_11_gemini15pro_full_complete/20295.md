bisect:/baɪˈsekt/ /baɪˈsɛkt/| v. |二等分，平分|To divide something into two equal parts.|The line bisects the angle. 这条线平分这个角。|例句：
The road bisects the town.  这条路把城镇一分为二。

The surgeon bisected the tumor.  外科医生将肿瘤切成两半。
|近义词：divide, halve, split|反义词: join, unite|词性:动词

bisected:/baɪˈsektɪd/ /baɪˈsɛktɪd/| adj. |被二等分的，被平分的|Divided into two equal parts.|The bisected angle measured 45 degrees.  被平分的角为45度。|例句：
The bisected circle revealed two semicircles. 被平分的圆显示出两个半圆。

The bisected line segment was precisely measured. 被平分的线段被精确测量。

|近义词：divided, halved, split|反义词: joined, united|词性:形容词


词源: From Latin bisectus, past participle of bisecare "to cut in two," from bi- "two" + secare "to cut."
