otro:/ˈo.tɾo/| adj. pron.|other, another, different|Used to refer to a different person or thing from one already mentioned or known; another. Also used as a pronoun.|Example: Tengo otro libro. (I have another book.)|Synonyms: another, different, alternative|Antonyms: same|Adjective, Pronoun

PHRASE->
otro día = another day
de otro modo = otherwise
en otro lugar = elsewhere
otro más = another one
por otro lado = on the other hand


词源: From Latin *alteru(m), from alter (“other”).  Related to Spanish alterno.


注意事项:  "Otro" changes gender and number to agree with the noun it modifies.  For example, "otra casa" (another house - feminine), "otros libros" (other books - masculine plural), "otras casas" (other houses - feminine plural).
