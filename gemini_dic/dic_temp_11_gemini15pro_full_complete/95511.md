inclining:/ɪnˈklaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 倾向于...的；倾斜的 | Having a tendency or disposition towards something; sloping or leaning. | The land is inclining gently towards the river. 这片土地缓缓向河流倾斜。|  The government is inclining towards stricter regulations. 政府倾向于更严格的规章制度。 | 近义词: leaning, sloping, tending, predisposed | 反义词: upright, vertical, level, averse | 词性: adjective
词源: From Middle English *enclinen, from Old French encliner, from Latin inclināre (“to bend, lean, incline”), from in- (“in, into, on, upon, towards”) + clināre (“to bend, lean”).
