cliquishnesses:/ˈkliːkɪʃnəsɪz/
n.
排外性，小集团主义
The tendency to form or associate in small exclusive groups.
Her cliquishness made it difficult for new people to join the group. 她的排外性使新成员很难加入这个团体。
exclusivity, clannishness
inclusiveness, openness
noun

cliquish:/ˈkliːkɪʃ/
adj.
小集团的，排外的
Tending to form or associate in small exclusive groups.
The office was very cliquish and it was hard to fit in. 办公室里小集团林立，很难融入进去。
exclusive, clannish
inclusive, open
adjective

clique:/kliːk/
n.
小集团，派系
A small, exclusive group of people.
The popular girls formed a clique and wouldn't let anyone else sit with them. 受欢迎的女孩们组成了一个小团体，不让其他人和她们坐在一起。
group, coterie, faction
individual
noun
