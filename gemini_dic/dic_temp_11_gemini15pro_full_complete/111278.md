litterateur:/ˌlɪtəˈrəˈtɜːr/ /ˌlɪtəˈrəˈtʊər/
n.
文学家；文人；著作家
A person who is interested in and knowledgeable about literature.  A writer of literary works, especially one noted for stylistic skill.
He was a renowned litterateur, respected for his insightful essays and captivating novels. 他是一位著名的文学家，以其富有洞察力的散文和引人入胜的小说而备受尊敬。
The litterateur's latest work explores themes of love and loss in the modern world.  这位文学家的最新作品探讨了现代世界中的爱与失去的主题。

synonym: writer, author, man of letters, intellectual
antonym: philistine, ignoramus
