messiahships:/məˈsaɪəʃɪps/ |n. | 救世主身份（复数）| The state or quality of being a messiah; the office or role of a messiah.  It often implies a belief in multiple individuals fulfilling this role or multiple instances of a single individual acting as a messiah. |  The various messiahships proclaimed throughout history have often led to conflict and division.  历史上宣称的各种救世主身份常常导致冲突和分裂。|  Synonyms: saviorships, redeemerships | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "false prophetships") | noun
