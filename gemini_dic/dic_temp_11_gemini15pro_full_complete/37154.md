come to one's senses:/kʌm tə wʌnz ˈsɛnsɪz/
verb phrase
清醒过来；恢复理智；
To begin thinking and behaving reasonably after a period of foolishness or irrationality.
He finally came to his senses and realized he was making a huge mistake. 他终于清醒过来，意识到自己犯了一个大错。
She was so angry, but she eventually came to her senses and apologized. 她非常生气，但最终她还是恢复了理智并道歉。
近义词: regain consciousness, come around, see reason, wise up
反义词: lose one's mind, go crazy, be irrational
