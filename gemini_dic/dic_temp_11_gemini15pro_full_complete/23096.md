bountifulness:/ˈbaʊntɪfəlnəs/
n.
慷慨; 丰饶; 丰富
The quality of being bountiful; generosity or abundance.
The bountifulness of the harvest ensured no one went hungry.  丰收的富饶保证了没有人挨饿。
近义词: generosity, abundance, plentifulness, copiousness, profusion
反义词: scarcity, meagerness, stinginess, parsimony
词性: 名词

bountiful:/ˈbaʊntɪfʊl/
adj.
丰富的；慷慨的；大量的
Giving freely and generously; plentiful.
We were grateful for the bountiful harvest. 我们对丰收充满感激。
近义词: generous, abundant, plentiful, copious, profuse
反义词: scarce, meager, stingy, parsimonious
词性: 形容词
