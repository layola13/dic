desolaters:/ˈdesəleɪtərz/
noun
荒芜者；使荒凉的人或物
Those who cause desolation; those who lay waste.
The desolaters swept across the land, leaving only ruin in their wake. 荒芜者席卷了这片土地，只留下了废墟。
The war was a desolater of cities and lives. 战争摧毁了城市和生命。
