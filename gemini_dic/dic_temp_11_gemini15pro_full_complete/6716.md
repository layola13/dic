analogize:/əˈnælədʒaɪz/
verb
类比；作类推
to compare one thing with another in order to explain or clarify
He analogized the human brain to a computer. 他把人脑比作电脑。
synonyms: compare, liken, equate, parallel, correlate
antonyms: differentiate, distinguish, contrast
v.
