chimed:/tʃaɪmd/ | v. | 响，鸣，插话 | to make a clear ringing sound, or to say something that interrupts a conversation | The clock chimed three times. 钟敲了三下。 He chimed in with a joke. 他插了一句笑话。 | ring, clang, toll | be silent | verb

chime:/tʃaɪm/ | n. | 钟声，和谐的声音 | a set of bells, especially in a church tower, or the sound they make | The chimes of Big Ben rang out across London. 大本钟的钟声响彻伦敦。 | ringing, pealing, clang | silence | noun
