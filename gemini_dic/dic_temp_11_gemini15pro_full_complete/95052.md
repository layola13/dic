inactuate:/ɪnˈæktʃueɪt/ | v. | 使不活动；使无效 | To make inactive or ineffective; to deprive of force or activity. |  The emergency brake was designed to inactuate the engine in case of failure.  紧急制动器设计用于在发生故障时使发动机失效。

The drug inactuated the virus, preventing it from replicating.  这种药物使病毒失效，阻止其复制。 | 近义词: deactivate, disable, neutralize | 反义词: activate, enable | 词性: 动词
