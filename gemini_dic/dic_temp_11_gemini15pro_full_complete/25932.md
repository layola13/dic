burnoose:/bərˈnuːs/| n.|伯努斯；一种带帽斗篷|A long, loose hooded cloak worn especially by Arabs.|The traveler wore a burnoose to protect himself from the desert sun. 旅行者穿着一件伯努斯来保护自己免受沙漠阳光的照射。|cloak, cape, mantle| |noun
词源->
From French *burnous*, from Arabic *burnūs*. Ultimately from Late Latin *byrrus*.
