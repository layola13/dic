burl:/bɜːrl/| n. | 节瘤，树瘤；（尤指用作装饰的）树瘤切片 | A burl is a rounded outgrowth on the trunk or branch of a tree, often used for decorative woodworking. | The burl on the walnut tree was prized for its intricate grain patterns. 这棵核桃树上的树瘤因其复杂的纹理图案而备受珍视。| synonym: knot, outgrowth | antonym: smooth patch | noun

burl:/bɜːrl/| v. | 去除树瘤；起球，起毛 | To remove a burl from a tree or to form small balls of fiber on a fabric. | The carpenter carefully burled the wood before shaping it into a bowl. 木匠在将木头塑造成碗之前，小心地去除了树瘤。  The sweater started to burl after just a few washes. 这件毛衣洗了几次后就开始起球了。 | synonym: remove, fuzz | antonym: smooth, flatten | verb
