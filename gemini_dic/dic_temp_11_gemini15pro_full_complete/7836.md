antanagoge:/ænˌtænəˈɡoʊdʒi/ | n. | 反衬法，以褒贬两方面说明事情 | A rhetorical device in which, not being able to answer the accusation of an adversary, one offers a counter-accusation. | Example 1: He acknowledged his lack of experience, but emphasized his willingness to learn. 他承认自己缺乏经验，但强调他愿意学习。

Example 2: The car is not very fuel-efficient, but it is extremely spacious. 这辆车不是很省油，但它非常宽敞。 | 近义词: compensation, counterpoise | 反义词: apophasis (explicit denial) | noun
