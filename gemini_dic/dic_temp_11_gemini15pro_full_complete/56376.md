dolabra:/doʊˈlɑːbrə/ /dəˈleɪbrə/
noun
锄头; 斧头; 柴刀; 一种罗马工具，一端是斧头，另一端是锄头或镐
A Roman tool with an axe head at one end and a mattock or hoe at the other.
The legionaries used dolabrae to construct fortifications. 军团士兵用锄头建造防御工事。
Synonyms: axe, hatchet, mattock, pickaxe, adze
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a tool.  Consider antonyms for its function, like "build" vs. "destroy")
