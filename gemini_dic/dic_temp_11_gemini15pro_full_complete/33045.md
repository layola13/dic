ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *choghe*, *choe*, from Old English *ceo*, from Proto-Germanic *kawwō*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂w-*. Related to Dutch *kauw*, German *Kähe*, and Latin *cavus* ("hollow").  The red bill is likely the source of the association with the word "chough."
