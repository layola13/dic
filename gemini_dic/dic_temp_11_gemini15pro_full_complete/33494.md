ciabatta:/tʃəˈbætə/ /tʃɑːˈbɑːtə/
|n.|拖鞋面包|A long, flat Italian bread made with a wet dough and characterized by large holes in the crumb.|I love a crispy ciabatta sandwich. 我喜欢吃脆皮拖鞋面包三明治。|Synonyms: Italian bread, slipper bread|Antonyms: None|Noun

ciabattas:/tʃəˈbætəz/ /tʃɑːˈbɑːtəz/
|n.|拖鞋面包（复数）|Plural form of ciabatta, referring to more than one of these Italian breads.|We bought two ciabattas from the bakery. 我们从面包店买了两个拖鞋面包。|Synonyms: Italian breads, slipper breads|Antonyms: None|Noun
