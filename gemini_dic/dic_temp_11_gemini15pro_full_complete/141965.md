oreographic: /ˌɔːriəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 地形的，山岳的 | Relating to mountains or mountainous regions; depicting mountains. |  The oreographic effect causes heavy rainfall on the windward side of mountains.  地形效应对迎风坡造成大量降雨。

The oreographic lift of the air created lenticular clouds.  空气的地形抬升形成了荚状云。| 近义词: mountainous, alpine | 反义词: flat, lowland | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *oros* (mountain) + *-graphikos* (relating to writing or drawing).
