encryption:/ɪnˈkrɪpʃən/ | n. | 加密，编码 | The process of converting information into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access. |  Data encryption is essential for protecting sensitive information. 数据加密对于保护敏感信息至关重要。

| The website uses 256-bit encryption to secure online transactions. 该网站使用256位加密来保护在线交易安全。 | 近义词: encoding, ciphering | 反义词: decryption, deciphering | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "to put into") + crypt (meaning "hidden" or "secret") + -ion (suffix forming nouns of action or process).  The word "crypt" ultimately comes from the Greek word "kryptos" meaning "hidden."
