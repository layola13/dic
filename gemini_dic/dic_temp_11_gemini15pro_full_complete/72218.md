florescence:/flɔːˈrɛsəns/ /flɔːˈrɛs(ə)ns/
n.
开花,开花期;繁荣,兴盛时期
the state or time of flowering
例句：The florescence of the cherry blossoms is a beautiful sight.  樱花盛开是一道美丽的风景。

例句：The Renaissance marked a florescence of art and literature. 文艺复兴标志着艺术和文学的繁荣。
近义词：bloom, blossoming, flowering, efflorescence, peak, prime
反义词：decline, decay, withering
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *florescent- (stem of *florescens), present participle of *florescre, from Latin floresco (“begin to blossom, bloom”), inchoative of Latin floreo (“blossom, bloom, flourish”), from Latin flos (“flower”).
