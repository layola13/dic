carbon-dating:/ˈkɑːrbən ˌdeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːrbən ˌdeɪtɪŋ/
noun
碳定年法，放射性碳定年法
The process of determining the age of organic material by measuring the amount of carbon-14 remaining in it.
The carbon-dating of the artifact showed it to be over 2000 years old. 这件文物的碳定年法显示它已有2000多年的历史。
radiocarbon dating, radiometric dating
