missampling:/ˌmɪsˈsæmplɪŋ/
noun
错误采样，抽样偏差
The act of taking a sample that is not representative of the population from which it is drawn, leading to inaccurate or biased results.
The survey suffered from missampling, as it only included people from urban areas.  这项调查存在抽样偏差，因为它只包括了来自城市地区的人。
Missampling can lead to inaccurate conclusions. 错误采样会导致不准确的结论。
近义词: sampling bias, sampling error
反义词: representative sampling, accurate sampling
词性:n.


词源:
"mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "sampling" (from "sample")
