diffidence:/ˈdɪfɪdəns/| n.|缺乏自信；胆怯；羞怯|Lack of self-confidence; shyness; modesty. |She overcame her natural diffidence and spoke with great conviction. 她克服了天生的胆怯，非常有把握地发言。|近义词: shyness, timidity, hesitancy, insecurity, self-doubt|反义词: confidence, boldness, assertiveness|词性:名词

diffident:/ˈdɪfɪdənt/| adj.|缺乏自信的；胆怯的；羞怯的|Lacking self-confidence; shy; modest. |He was diffident about speaking up in class. 他在课堂上不敢发言。|近义词: shy, timid, hesitant, insecure, self-doubting|反义词: confident, bold, assertive|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *diffidentia, from Latin diffidens, diffident- ‘distrustful’, present participle of diffidere, from dis- ‘not’ + fidere ‘to trust’.
