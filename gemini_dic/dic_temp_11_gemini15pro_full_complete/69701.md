fenlands:/ˈfɛnləndz/ | n. | 沼泽地，低洼地，芬兰 | Low-lying, marshy land, typically drained for agriculture.  The Fens, a region in eastern England, are a prime example. | The fenlands of East Anglia are known for their fertile soil.  东安格利亚的沼泽地以其肥沃的土壤而闻名。|  marshes, wetlands, bogs | uplands, highlands | noun
