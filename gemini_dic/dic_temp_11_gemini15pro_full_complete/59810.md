ecstatical: /ɪkˈstætɪkəl/ /ekˈstætɪkəl/
|adj.|欣喜若狂的，狂喜的|Overwhelmed by ecstatic joy; extremely happy.|She was ecstatical when she heard she'd won the lottery. 她听到自己中了彩票欣喜若狂。|
近义词: overjoyed, rapturous, blissful, euphoric
|反义词: miserable, dejected, depressed
|词性: adjective


ecstasy: /ˈɛkstəsi/ /ˈekstəsi/
|n.|狂喜，欣喜若狂|A state of overwhelming joy; intense delight.|The news of their engagement sent them into ecstasies. 订婚的消息让他们欣喜若狂。|近义词: rapture, bliss, euphoria
|反义词: misery, despair, depression
|词性: noun


词源: From Late Latin ecstaticus, from Greek ekstatikos “beside oneself,” from existanai "put out of place, displace," from ek "out" + histanai "to cause to stand."
