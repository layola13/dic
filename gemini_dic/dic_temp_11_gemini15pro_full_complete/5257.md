allodium:/əˈloʊdiəm/
n.
完全保有地; 自由保有地
Land owned absolutely, without any rent or service due to a superior.  Freehold land.
The family's farm was an allodium passed down through generations.  这家人世代相传的农场是一块完全保有地。
近义词：freehold, freehold estate
反义词：fief, leasehold
词性：noun


allodium:/əˈloʊdiəm/
Etymology: From Medieval Latin *allodium, from Frankish *allōd, from Proto-Germanic *alaudaz (“entire property, inheritance”), from *al- (“all”) + *audaz (“wealth, heritage”). Cognate with Old High German allōt, Old English ealod.
词源：源于中世纪拉丁语 *allodium，来自法兰克语 *allōd，来自原始日耳曼语 *alaudaz（“全部财产、遗产”），来自 *al-（“全部”）+ *audaz（“财富、遗产”）。 与古高地德语 allōt、古英语 ealod 同源。
