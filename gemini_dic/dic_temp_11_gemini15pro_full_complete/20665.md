blackspots:/ˈblækˌspɒts/ (UK) /ˈblækˌspɑːts/ (US)| n. |黑点；污点；事故多发地段|A particular place or area where accidents frequently occur.  A mark or flaw that detracts from something's appearance or reputation.|例句：This road is known as a blackspot for accidents. 这条路是有名的事故多发地段。

blackspot:/ˈblækˌspɒt/ (UK) /ˈblækˌspɑːts/ (US)| n. |（植物的）黑斑病|A fungal disease of plants characterized by black spots on the leaves.|例句：The roses were suffering from blackspot.  这些玫瑰得了黑斑病。

Blackspot can severely damage rose bushes. 黑斑病会严重损害玫瑰丛。|近义词：leaf spot, fungal disease|反义词：healthy foliage|词性:名词
