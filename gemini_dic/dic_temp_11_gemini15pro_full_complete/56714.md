doomster:/ˈduːmstər/ | n. | 末日预言者；悲观主义者 | A person who predicts disaster or misfortune; a pessimist. | The doomster warned of impending economic collapse.  末日预言者警告即将发生的经济崩溃。| synonym: pessimist, prophet of doom, alarmist | antonym: optimist, Pollyanna | noun
