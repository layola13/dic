compeer:/kəmˈpɪr/ | n. | 同辈，同僚，伙伴 | A person of equal status, rank, or ability; a colleague or associate. | He discussed the issue with his compeers at the conference. 他在会议上与他的同僚讨论了这个问题。|  He found solace in the company of his compeers. 他在同伴的陪伴下找到了慰藉。| 近义词: peer, colleague, associate, contemporary | 反义词: superior, subordinate | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Old French *comper, from Late Latin compar “equal, like,” from Latin com- “with, together” + par “equal.”
