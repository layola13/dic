overglut:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌt/ | vt. n. | 过度供应；过饱；充斥 | To supply with far more than is needed or wanted; to fill to excess. | The market was overglutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。| The holiday season overglutted stores with seasonal merchandise. 假日期间商店里充斥着季节性商品。| 近义词: glut, surfeit, inundate, overload | 反义词:  lack, shortage, scarcity | verb, noun
