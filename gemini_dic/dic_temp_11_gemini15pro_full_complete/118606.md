messaged:/ˈmɛsɪdʒd/| adj.|已发送消息的|Adjectival form of "message". Describes something that has been marked or affected by messages.  Less common than the verb form.|The messaged inbox showed the extent of the communication.  已发送消息的收件箱显示了通信的程度。|近义词：communicated, marked|反义词: unmarked, blank |adjective
