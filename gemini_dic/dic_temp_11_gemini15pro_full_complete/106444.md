kosi:/ˈkoʊsiː/| n.| 科西河; 科西族;  |A river in Nepal and India, a tributary of the Ganges. Also, an ethnic group primarily located in Nepal.|The Kosi river is known for its flooding. 科西河因洪水泛滥而闻名。| The Kosi people are indigenous to Nepal. 科西人是尼泊尔的土著居民。|近义词: Koshi (alternative spelling), Saptakoshi (another name for the river)|反义词: None|词性: 名词

词源: "Kosi" likely derives from local languages in the region where the river flows, potentially related to words describing its characteristics.  Further etymological research is needed for a definitive answer.
