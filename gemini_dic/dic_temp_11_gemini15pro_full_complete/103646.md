jokiest:/ˈdʒoʊkiɪst/
adjective
最爱开玩笑的；最幽默的
superlative adjective of "jokey": most jokey;  Humorous, playful, given to joking.
He told the jokiest stories I've ever heard. 他讲了我听过的最幽默的故事。
近义词:funniest, most humorous, wittiest
反义词:most serious, least funny
词性:adjective
