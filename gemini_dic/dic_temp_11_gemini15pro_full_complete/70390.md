fifths:/fɪfθs/
noun
五份之…; 五分之几
Representing five of five equal parts of a whole.
Two fifths of the students are girls.  五分之二的学生是女孩。

近义词:five parts out of five, five of five equal parts
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "other fractions" or "whole")
词性:n.
