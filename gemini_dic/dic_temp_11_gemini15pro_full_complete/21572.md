bluestone:/ˈbluːˌstoʊn/| n. |青石，蓝灰砂岩|A type of hard, bluish-grey sandstone, used for building and paving.|The patio was paved with bluestone. 露台是用青石铺成的。|Synonyms: flagstone, paving stone|Antonyms: None|Noun

bluestone:/ˈbluːˌstoʊn/| adj. |青石制的，蓝灰砂岩的|Made of bluestone.|A bluestone wall. 一堵青石墙。|Synonyms: flagstone, paving stone|Antonyms: None|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From blue + stone.  The "blue" refers to the bluish-grey color of the stone.
