nonmovable:/nɒnˈmuːvəbəl/ (British), /ˌnɑːnˈmuːvəbəl/ (American)
adjective
不可移动的；固定的
Not able to be moved; fixed.
The antique desk was a nonmovable fixture in the library. 这张古董书桌是图书馆里一件不可移动的固定装置。
The large stone statue was nonmovable.  这尊巨大的石像是不可移动的。
近义词: fixed, immobile, stationary, permanent
反义词: movable, mobile, portable
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not
movable (adjective): capable of being moved
