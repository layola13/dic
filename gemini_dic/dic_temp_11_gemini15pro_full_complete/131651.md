noncomputations:/ˌnɒnkɒmpjuˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 非计算，非运算 |  Processes or tasks that do not involve computation or calculations. This refers to activities that don't require mathematical or logical processing by a computer or a person. |  Many creative processes, like brainstorming and freewriting, are noncomputations. 许多创意过程，如头脑风暴和自由写作，都是非计算的。|  The artist's process was largely noncomputation, relying on intuition and emotion. 这位艺术家的创作过程很大程度上是非计算的，依赖于直觉和情感。 | synonyms: non-calculations, non-algorithmic processes | antonyms: computations, calculations | noun
