guerdonless:/ˈɡɜːrdənˌləs/
adjective
无报酬的，未受奖赏的
Without reward or recompense.
The knight errant, though guerdonless, continued his quest.  这位骑士尽管没有得到任何报酬，仍然继续他的探险。
近义词: unrewarded, uncompensated, unpaid
反义词: rewarded, compensated, paid
词性: adjective

词源:
From 'guerdon' (reward) + '-less' (without).  'Guerdon' comes from Old French 'guerredon', ultimately from Frankish 'widarlōn'.
