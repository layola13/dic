outeating: /ˈaʊtiːtɪŋ/
n.
外出就餐;在外用餐
The act of eating meals at restaurants or other establishments outside the home.
We do a lot of outeating because we don't have time to cook. 我们经常外出就餐，因为我们没有时间做饭。
dining out, eating out
home cooking, cooking at home
