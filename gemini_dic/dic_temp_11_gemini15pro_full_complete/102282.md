yttria:/ˈɪtriə/|n.|氧化钇; 钇土|A white powder, yttrium oxide (Y2O3), used in making phosphors for color television tubes and other products.|Yttria is a key component in many high-tech materials. 氧化钇是许多高科技材料的关键组成部分。|Synonyms: yttrium oxide|Antonyms: None|Noun
