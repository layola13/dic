besmirch:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
verb
玷污，败坏，损害(名誉)
To damage the reputation of (someone or something) in the opinion of others.
The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire company. 这桩丑闻玷污了整个公司的名誉。
His name was besmirched by the false accusations. 他的名誉被这些不实的指控所玷污。

synonyms: tarnish, sully, taint, smear, disgrace, dishonor, defame, slander, libel
antonyms: honor, praise, extol, laud, commend, clear, vindicate
词性: 动词
