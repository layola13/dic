euroscepticism: /ˌjʊərəʊˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/ /ˌʊrəˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/
|n.| 欧洲怀疑主义|Criticism of or opposition to the European Union and European integration.|例句：Euroscepticism is rising in some European countries. 一些欧洲国家的欧洲怀疑主义情绪正在上升。|例句：The party's euroscepticism resonated with voters concerned about national sovereignty.  该党的欧洲怀疑主义情绪引起了关注国家主权的选民的共鸣。|近义词：anti-EU sentiment, EU skepticism|反义词：pro-Europeanism, European integrationism|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From euro- + skepticism.  "Euro-" is a combining form representing "Europe" or "European." "Skepticism" comes from the Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring" or "thoughtful," which evolved to mean "doubtful."
