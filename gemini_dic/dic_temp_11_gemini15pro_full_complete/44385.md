cross-fenced:/ˌkrɒsˈfɛnst/ /ˌkrɔːsˈfɛnst/ | adj. | 交叉围栏的，分成小块的 | Divided into smaller sections by fences intersecting each other. | The pasture was cross-fenced to allow for rotational grazing. 牧场用交叉围栏隔开，以便进行轮牧。 | The farm is cross-fenced into several paddocks.  农场被交叉围栏分成几块围场。 | 近义词: subdivided, partitioned | 反义词: open, undivided | 词性: 形容词


词源: "cross-" (meaning across or intersecting) + "fenced" (past participle of "fence").  "Cross-" comes from Old English "cros" and "fence" comes from Old French "fense".
