backfire:/ˌbækˈfaɪər/ (US) /ˌbækˈfaɪə(r)/ (UK)| vi. n.|适得其反，逆火，回火| (of a plan or action) have an unintended and unwelcome result that is the opposite of what was intended; (of an engine) undergo a mistimed explosion in the cylinder or exhaust.|

backfire:/ˈbækˌfaɪər/ (US) /ˈbækˌfaɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|逆火，回火，适得其反的后果|an instance of a plan or action having an unintended and unwelcome result that is the opposite of what was intended; a mistimed explosion in the cylinder or exhaust of an engine.|
The policy change resulted in a serious backfire. 政策的改变导致了严重的适得其反的后果。

The loud backfire from the truck echoed through the street. 卡车巨大的回火声在街道上回荡。

近义词:repercussion, setback|
反义词:success, achievement|
词性:noun


词源: "back" + "fire"，最初指内燃机中的“逆火”现象，后来引申为比喻义“适得其反”。
