munificent:/mjuːˈnɪfɪsnt/
adjective
慷慨的；丰厚的
very generous, especially with money; giving large sums of money
例句：A munificent benefactor donated one million dollars to the hospital. 一位慷慨的捐助者向医院捐赠了一百万美元。

例句：The king gave the artist a munificent reward for his masterpiece. 国王给了这位艺术家丰厚的报酬，以表彰他的杰作。

近义词：generous, lavish, bountiful, philanthropic
反义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *munificus*, from *munus* "gift, service, duty" + *-ficus*,  related to *facere* "to make, do."  Literally "gift-making."
