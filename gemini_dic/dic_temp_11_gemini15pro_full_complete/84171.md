half-profile:/ˌhɑːf ˈproʊfaɪl/ | n. | 半侧面；侧面像 | A side view of a face, showing only one side of the features. |  She posed for a half-profile portrait, showcasing her elegant jawline. 她摆姿势拍了一张半侧面肖像，展现了她优雅的下颌线。 | Example: The coin featured a half-profile of the emperor.  这枚硬币上有皇帝的半侧面头像。| Synonym: profile, side view | Antonym: full-face, frontal view | noun

half-profile:/ˌhɑːf ˈproʊfaɪl/ | adj. | 半侧面的 | Seen or depicted from a side view, showing only one side of the features. | The artist sketched a half-profile view of the model. 这位艺术家画了一幅模特儿的半侧面素描。| Example: The security camera captured a half-profile image of the suspect. 监控摄像头拍到了嫌疑人的半侧面图像。 | Synonym: profile, side-view | Antonym: full-face, frontal | adjective
