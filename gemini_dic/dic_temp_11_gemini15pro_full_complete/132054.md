noncosmopolitan:/ˌnɒnkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ | adj. | 非世界性的，非世界主义的 | Not cosmopolitan; not worldly or sophisticated; limited in outlook or experience, often to a local area. |  She had a rather noncosmopolitan upbringing in a small rural town.  她在一个小的乡村小镇长大，相当缺乏世界性。 | Example: His noncosmopolitan views made it difficult for him to adapt to life in the diverse city. 他狭隘的视野使他难以适应这个多元化的城市生活。 | Synonyms: parochial, provincial, insular, narrow-minded | Antonyms: cosmopolitan, worldly, sophisticated, open-minded | Adjective
