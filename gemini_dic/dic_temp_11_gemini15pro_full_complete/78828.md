gimmickers:/ˈɡɪmɪkərz/| n.|耍花招的人，玩噱头的人|People who use gimmicks, often to deceive or attract attention.  Those who employ tricks or schemes, especially for attention or profit. |The gimmickers tried to sell their product with flashy advertising but little substance. 那些耍花招的人试图用华而不实的广告来销售他们的产品，但产品本身没什么实质内容。|synonyms: tricksters, schemers, con artists, fraudsters|antonyms: honest dealers, straightforward people|noun
