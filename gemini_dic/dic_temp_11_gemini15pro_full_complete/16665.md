barramundi:/ˌbærəˈmʌndi/ | n. | 尖吻鲈，澳洲肺鱼 | A large, primarily freshwater fish (Lates calcarifer) native to Australia and the Indo-Pacific region, prized as a food and game fish.  It is also known as Asian sea bass. |  We caught a huge barramundi in the river. 我们在河里钓到了一条巨大的尖吻鲈。| synonyms: Asian sea bass, giant perch | antonyms: N/A | noun
