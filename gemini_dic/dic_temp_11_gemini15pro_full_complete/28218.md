capsaicin:/kæpˈseɪsɪn/ | n. |辣椒素|A colorless, crystalline, pungent compound, C₁₈H₂₇NO₃, found in various capsicums, especially in hot peppers, that produces a burning sensation when it comes in contact with skin or mucous membranes. |例句：Capsaicin is the active component of chili peppers. 辣椒素是辣椒中的活性成分。

例句：Researchers are studying the potential medicinal uses of capsaicin. 研究人员正在研究辣椒素的潜在药用价值。|近义词：capsicum oleoresin|反义词: None|词性:noun
