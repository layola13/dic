gamely:/ˈɡeɪmli/| adv.|勇敢地；坚决地；不屈不挠地|In a spirited or plucky manner; with courage and determination, even in the face of difficulty or defeat.|Despite being injured, she gamely continued the race. 尽管受伤了，她还是勇敢地继续比赛。|例句：He gamely fought on despite his injuries. 他不顾伤势，顽强地战斗下去。

例句：She gamely accepted the challenge, even though she knew it would be difficult. 她勇敢地接受了挑战，即使她知道这会很困难。|近义词：bravely, courageously, pluckily, spiritedly|反义词：cowardly, timidly|词性：副词
