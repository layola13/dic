assuring:/əˈʃʊrɪŋ/
adjective
令人安心的，肯定的
making you feel less worried or doubtful about something
Her assuring smile calmed my nerves. 她令人安心的微笑使我平静下来。
synonyms: comforting, encouraging, reassuring
antonyms: alarming, disturbing, unsettling
词性:形容词

assuring:/əˈʃʊrɪŋ/
verb (assures, assured, assuring)
使确信，向…保证
tell someone something positively or confidently to dispel any doubts they may have.
He assured me that everything would be alright. 他向我保证一切都会好起来的。
synonyms: guarantee, promise, pledge
antonyms:  deny, contradict, oppose
词性:动词 -ing形式 (现在分词)
