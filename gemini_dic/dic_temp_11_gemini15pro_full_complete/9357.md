antiprotectionist:/ˌæntiprəˈtɛkʃənɪst/
| adj. n.|反保护主义的；反保护主义者|opposed to protectionism (the theory or practice of shielding a country's domestic industries from foreign competition by taxing imports).|
例句：The antiprotectionist senator argued for free trade.  反保护主义的参议员主张自由贸易。

例句：He's a staunch antiprotectionist and believes in open markets. 他是一位坚定的反保护主义者，信奉开放市场。
|近义词：free trader, liberal (in economics), advocate of free trade|反义词：protectionist, mercantilist|词性:形容词, 名词
