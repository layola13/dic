famousness:/ˈfeɪməsnəs/
n.
名声，名气
The state or quality of being famous.
Her famousness grew after the release of her new movie.  她新电影上映后名气大增。
近义词: celebrity, renown, fame
反义词: obscurity, anonymity
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From famous + -ness.  Famous derives from Middle English famous, ultimately from Latin famosus (“famous, notorious”). The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
