archeology: /ˌɑːrkiˈɑːlədʒi/ (US) /ˌɑːkiˈɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n. |考古学|The study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：She has a degree in archaeology. 她拥有考古学学位。

例句：Archaeology can reveal a lot about past civilizations. 考古学可以揭示很多关于过去文明的信息。

近义词：paleontology, anthropology|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
