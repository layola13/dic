metachloral:/ˈmɛtəklɔːræl/
noun
偏氯醛；三聚氯醛
A polymeric form of chloral hydrate, used as a sedative and hypnotic.
Metachloral was once used as a sedative, but is now obsolete due to its toxicity.  偏氯醛曾经用作镇静剂，但现在由于其毒性已被淘汰。
Metachloral is a less soluble form of chloral hydrate.  偏氯醛是水合氯醛的一种溶解度较低的形式。
Synonym:  chloral hydrate
Antonym:  stimulant
