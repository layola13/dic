foresight:/ˈfɔːrsaɪt/| n.|远见，先见之明|The ability to anticipate future needs or events.|She had the foresight to book the tickets in advance. 她有先见之明，提前预订了门票。|近义词：vision, prudence, anticipation|反义词：shortsightedness, myopia|名词

foresight:/ˈfɔːrsaɪt/| n.|远见，先见之明|The act of looking forward; the power of seeing beforehand what is likely to happen.|He demonstrated remarkable foresight in predicting the economic downturn. 他在预测经济衰退方面展现了非凡的远见。|近义词：forethought, prescience|反义词：hindsight, improvidence|名词

词源:  From Old English *foresīeþ "foresight," from fore- "before" + sīeþ "sight."
