battle-fatigued: /ˈbætl fəˈtiːɡd/ | adj. | 战争疲劳的，身心俱疲的 | Exhausted and demoralized from prolonged exposure to the stresses of combat or other highly stressful situations.  It can manifest as physical and mental exhaustion, emotional numbness, and a reduced capacity to function effectively. |  The battle-fatigued soldiers were withdrawn from the front lines.  身心俱疲的士兵从前线撤了下来。

The doctor diagnosed the soldier as battle-fatigued. 医生诊断这名士兵患有战争疲劳症。 | 近义词: shell-shocked, traumatized, exhausted, burned-out | 反义词: refreshed, invigorated, resilient | 词性: adjective
