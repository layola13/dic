caffs:/kæfs/
n.
(informal) cafes; coffeehouses
Small, informal restaurants where you can get simple meals and drinks, especially coffee.
We stopped at one of the local caffs for a quick lunch. 我们在当地的一家小餐馆吃了顿快餐。
近义词: cafes, coffee shops, diners
反义词: restaurants (more formal)
词性: noun


caffs:/kæfs/
n.
(British informal) cafes; coffeehouses
Small, informal restaurants, typically in Britain, where you can get simple meals and drinks, especially coffee.
They met up for a chat in one of their favourite caffs. 他们在他们最喜欢的一家小咖啡馆里见面聊天。
近义词: cafes, greasy spoons (British), tea rooms (British)
反义词: restaurants (more formal)
词性: noun
