argumentatious: /ˌɑːrɡjʊmənˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 好争辩的，爱争论的 | Given to argument; disputatious; fond of arguing.  Inclined to argue or debate; often in a petty or contentious way. |  He became increasingly argumentatious as the evening wore on.  他随着夜越来越深变得越来越爱争论。 |  例句：She has an argumentative personality. 她个性好争辩。 | 近义词：disputatious, contentious, quarrelsome | 反义词：agreeable, amiable, conciliatory | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French argumenteux, from argument (see argument).
