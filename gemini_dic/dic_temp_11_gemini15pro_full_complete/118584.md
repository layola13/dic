mesothoracic:/ˌmɛzəʊθəˈræsɪk/
adj.
中胸的
Relating to the middle segment of the thorax, which bears the second pair of legs and the first pair of wings in insects.
The mesothoracic legs of the grasshopper are adapted for jumping.  蚱蜢的中胸足适于跳跃。

The beetle's mesothoracic shield was brightly colored. 甲虫的中胸背板颜色鲜艳。
近义词:midthoracic
反义词:prothoracic, metathoracic
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From meso- (middle) + thoracic (pertaining to the thorax).  来源于 meso-（中间的） + thoracic（与胸廓有关的）。
