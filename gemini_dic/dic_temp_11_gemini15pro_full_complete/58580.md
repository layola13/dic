dulciloquy: /ˈdʌlsɪˌloʊkwi/ /ˈdʌlsɪləkwi/ | n. |甜言蜜语；温柔的谈话|sweet, soothing speech or talk; a gentle, melodious way of speaking|Her dulciloquy charmed everyone in the room. 她温柔的谈话迷住了房间里的每个人。|The lovers exchanged dulciloquies under the starry sky. 恋人们在星空下互诉衷肠。|近义词: sweet talk, soft words, flattery, blandishments|反义词: harsh words, reprimand, criticism|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dulciloquium*, from *dulcis* (sweet) + *loquium* (speech), from *loqui* (to speak).
