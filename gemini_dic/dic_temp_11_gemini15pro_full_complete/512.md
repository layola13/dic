abolishments: /əˈbɒlɪʃmənts/
n.
废除；取消
The act of abolishing something, or the state of being abolished.  The complete destruction of something.
The abolition of slavery marked a turning point in American history.  奴隶制的废除标志着美国历史的转折点。

The abolition of the death penalty is a controversial topic. 死刑的废除是一个有争议的话题。

Synonyms: eradication, elimination, extermination, annihilation
Antonyms: establishment, institution, creation, enactment
词性: 名词 (Noun)
