nonpayment:/ˌnɒnˈpeɪmənt/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈpeɪmənt/ (UK)| n.|未支付，不付款|Failure to fulfill a financial obligation.  没有履行财务义务。|Example: The company's nonpayment of debts forced them into bankruptcy. 公司未偿还债务，被迫破产。|Synonyms: non-remittance, arrears|Antonyms: settlement, discharge|noun
