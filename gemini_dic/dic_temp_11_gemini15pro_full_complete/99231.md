intercombats:/ˌɪntərˈkɒmbæts/ | n. |交战，相互战斗| Fights or battles between different groups or factions within a larger entity.  | The intercombats between the rival gangs resulted in numerous casualties.  敌对帮派之间的交战导致大量人员伤亡。 |  The protracted intercombats weakened the kingdom, making it vulnerable to invasion. 长期不断的内战削弱了王国的力量，使其容易受到入侵。 | synonyms: infighting, internal strife, internal conflict, factionalism | antonyms: peace, harmony, unity, cooperation | noun
