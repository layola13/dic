dimorphism:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ /daɪˈmɔːrˌfɪzəm/
noun|二态性，二形性|The existence of two different forms of individuals within the same species or population. |Sexual dimorphism in birds is often expressed in differences in plumage. 鸟类的性别二态性通常表现在羽毛的差异上。|synonym:dimorphism, polymorphism|antonym:monomorphism|n.

dimorphic:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪk/ /daɪˈmɔːrˌfɪk/
adjective|二态的，二形的|Having two different forms. |Many species of deer are sexually dimorphic, with males possessing antlers and females not. 许多鹿种是性别二态的，雄鹿有鹿角而雌鹿没有。|synonym:bimodal, biform|antonym:monomorphic|adj.
