antiorganization:/ˌæntiˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|反组织；非组织；反对组织|A stance or entity that actively opposes established organizations or the concept of organization itself.  An antiorganization might resist hierarchical structures, formalized rules, or centralized control.|The antiorganization protested the corporate takeover of the local farm.|Synonyms:  dissident group, nonconformist movement, counterculture, anarchist collective|Antonyms: organization, establishment, institution|Noun

antiorganization:/ˌæntiˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃən/
adj.|反组织的|Opposed to or disruptive of organizations or the principles of organization.|His antiorganization views made it difficult for him to work within the company's structure.|Synonyms:  iconoclastic, rebellious, subversive, dissident|Antonyms: organized, structured, conventional, conformist|Adjective
