endoscopic:/ˌɛndəˈskɒpɪk/ (US) /ˌɛndəˈskɒpɪk/ (UK)| adj.|内窥镜的；用内窥镜检查的|Relating to or performed using an endoscope, a thin, flexible tube with a camera and light source used to examine the internal organs or cavities of the body.|The doctor performed an endoscopic procedure to examine the patient's stomach. 医生进行了内窥镜检查以检查病人的胃。|Synonyms: minimally invasive, laparoscopic|Antonyms: open surgery|adjective
