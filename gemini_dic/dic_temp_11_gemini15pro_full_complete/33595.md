cimer:/ˈsɪmər/| informal contraction | 再见 (See you later 的缩写) | "Cimer" is a shortened, informal way of saying "See you later," often used in online communication or casual settings. It originates from the French phrase "à plus tard," which also means "see you later." While not considered standard English, it is understood by many English speakers, especially younger generations familiar with internet slang. | Cimer, gotta go! 再见，我得走了！ | 近义词：See ya, Later, Bye | 反义词: Hi, Hello | contraction
