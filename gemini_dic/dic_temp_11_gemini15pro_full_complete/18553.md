benefiting:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
受益，得益于
Receiving an advantage; experiencing something good as a result of something.
The charity is benefiting from the increased donations.  这个慈善机构受益于捐款的增加。
Local businesses are benefiting from the influx of tourists. 当地企业受益于游客的涌入。
Synonyms: profiting, gaining, thriving, flourishing
Antonyms: suffering, losing, declining, deteriorating
词性:动词的现在分词形式

benefit:/ˈbɛnɪfɪt/
noun
益处，好处；优势
An advantage or profit gained from something.
There are many benefits to eating healthy foods.  吃健康食品有很多好处。
The benefit of regular exercise is improved health.  规律锻炼的好处是改善健康状况。
verb
受益；得益于
Receive an advantage; profit.
Students will benefit from the new library. 学生将受益于新的图书馆。
The company benefited from the tax cuts.  该公司受益于减税。
Synonyms: advantage, gain, profit, boon
Antonyms: disadvantage, loss, detriment, drawback
词性:名词；动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French bienfait (“good deed, kindness”), from bien (“well”) and fait (“deed, fact”), ultimately from Latin bene factum (“well done, a good deed”).  The English word retains the sense of a “good deed” as well as the more general sense of “advantage” or “profit.”
