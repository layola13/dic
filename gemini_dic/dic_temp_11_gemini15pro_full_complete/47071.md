datetime: /ˈdeɪtˌtaɪm/ | n. | 日期时间 | A single point in time, usually represented by a date and a time. |  The datetime of the event is July 4th, 2024, 10:00 AM.  事件的日期时间是2024年7月4日上午10点。 | synonyms: timestamp, point in time | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:  A compound word formed from "date" and "time". "Date" comes from Old French "date"  and ultimately from Latin "datum" (something given). "Time" comes from Old English "tīma" and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*tīmaz".
