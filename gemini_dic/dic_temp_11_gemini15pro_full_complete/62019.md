enantiosis:/ɪˌnæntiˈoʊsɪs/ /ɪˌnæntiˈɒʊsɪs/
noun
对映性，镜像异构性
The process or condition of being enantios, or of exhibiting enantiomorphism; the condition of being reversed or of reversing, as in a mirror image.
Crystals of quartz exhibit enantiosis.  石英晶体呈现对映性。

enantiosis:/ɪˌnæntiˈoʊsɪs/ /ɪˌnæntiˈɒʊsɪs/
noun
对映现象，对映体形成
The process by which a substance becomes enantiomorphic.
The enantiosis of tartaric acid results in two distinct forms. 酒石酸的对映现象导致两种不同的形式。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐνάντιος (enántios, “opposite”) + -osis.

SYNONYMS->
enantiomorphism, mirror-image isomerism, chirality

ANTONYMS->
achirality, meso

PART OF SPEECH->
noun
