dissimilate:/dɪˈsɪməleɪt/
verb
同化；吸收
To make unlike or dissimilar; to cause to become different.  To break down (nutrients) into simpler products that can be absorbed and used by the body.
Plants dissimilate organic matter into simpler substances. 植物将有机物分解成更简单的物质。
近义词:differentiate, diverge, separate, break down, catabolize
反义词:assimilate, homogenize
词性:verb

dissimilation:/dɪˌsɪməˈleɪʃən/
noun
异化；异化作用；同化作用
The act or process of dissimilating; the state of being dissimilated.  The metabolic process by which organisms break down nutrients into simpler substances.
The dissimilation of fats produces energy. 脂肪的分解产生能量。
近义词:differentiation, divergence, separation, catabolism
反义词:assimilation, homogenization
词性:noun
