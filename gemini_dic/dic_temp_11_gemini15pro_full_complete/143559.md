outhire:/ˌaʊtˈhaɪər/
verb
外包；外聘
to hire or contract out work to an external provider.
Many companies outhire their IT services to specialist firms. 许多公司将其IT服务外包给专业公司。
The company decided to outhire some of its manufacturing processes.  这家公司决定将其部分制造流程外包。
近义词: outsource, subcontract
反义词: insource
