actionabilities:/ˌækʃənəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可操作性，可行动性|The quality of being actionable; the extent to which something can be acted upon or implemented.|The report highlighted the actionabilities of the recommendations, ensuring they could be easily implemented. 这份报告强调了这些建议的可操作性，确保它们能够轻松实施。|The actionabilities of the plan are crucial for its success.  这个计划的可操作性对它的成功至关重要。|synonyms: practicality, feasibility, viability|antonyms: impracticality, infeasibility, unworkability|noun
