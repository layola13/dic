frying:/ˈfraɪɪŋ/| adj. v.|油炸的；油炸|The process of cooking food in hot oil or fat. |例句：The smell of frying bacon filled the kitchen. 厨房里弥漫着煎培根的香味。|例句：She is frying some potatoes for dinner. 她正在炸一些土豆做晚餐。|近义词：sautéing, deep-frying|反义词:boiling, steaming|词性:形容词, 动名词

Etymology->
From Middle English *fryen*, from Old French *frire*, from Latin *frīgĕre* (“to roast, fry, parch”).
