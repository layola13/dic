cherublike: /ˈtʃɛrəblaɪk/ | adj. | 天使般的，像小天使的 | Resembling a cherub, especially in innocence, beauty, or plumpness. |  The baby had a cherublike face with rosy cheeks.  这个婴儿有一张天使般的脸，有着玫瑰色的脸颊。 |  The painting depicted a cherublike child with golden curls. 这幅画描绘了一个有着金色卷发的像小天使一样的孩子。 | 近义词：angelic, sweet, innocent | 反义词：devilish, impish | 词性：形容词

词源:
From cherub + -like.  "Cherub" comes from the Hebrew word "kərūb," referring to a winged celestial being. "-like" is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
