engraftment:/ɪnˈɡræftmənt/ /ɛnˈɡræftmənt/
n.|移植, 接枝, 嫁接|The successful engraftment of the new tissue was crucial for the patient's recovery.  新组织的成功移植对病人的康复至关重要。|
engraftment:/ɪnˈɡræftmənt/ /ɛnˈɡræftmənt/
n.| (比喻) (思想、习惯等的)灌输|The engraftment of new ideas into the company culture took time and effort. 将新思想灌输到公司文化中需要时间和精力。|
Synonyms: implantation, transplantation, insertion, grafting
Antonyms: rejection, expulsion, removal
Etymology: From en- + graft + -ment.  "Graft" comes from the Old French "graffe," a cutting used for grafting.
Word Type: Noun
