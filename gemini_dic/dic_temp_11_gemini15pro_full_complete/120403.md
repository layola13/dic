ministry:/ˈmɪnɪstri/ | n. | 部；事工；牧师的职务；全体牧师 |  A government department headed by a minister. The work or vocation of a minister of religion. |  The Ministry of Defence has announced cuts in spending. 国防部宣布削减开支。  He felt a calling to the ministry. 他感到蒙召从事牧师的职务。 | synonyms: department, agency, office, administration, portfolio, pastorate, priesthood | antonyms: laity | noun

ministry:/ˈmɪnɪstri/ | n. | (ministries) 服务；帮助 | The act of serving or helping.  | They offer a ministry to the homeless. 他们为无家可归者提供服务。 The church has several ministries to help those in need. 教会开展多项事工来帮助有需要的人。 | synonyms: service, aid, assistance, support | antonyms: neglect, indifference | noun
