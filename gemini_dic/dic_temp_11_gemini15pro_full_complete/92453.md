hypoptilum:/ˌhaɪpəˈtɪləm/ | n. | 绒羽，下层羽毛 | The soft, fluffy feathers that lie beneath the contour feathers of a bird, providing insulation. | The hypoptilum of the duck kept it warm in the icy water. 鸭子的绒羽使它在冰冷的水中保持温暖。 | synonyms: down feather, underplumage | antonyms: contour feather, flight feather | noun
