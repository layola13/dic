diarrheal:/ˌdaɪəˈriːəl/ | adj. | 腹泻的 | relating to or characterized by diarrhea |  The doctor diagnosed the patient with a diarrheal illness. 医生诊断该病人患有腹泻性疾病。|  The child suffered from diarrheal symptoms.  这个孩子出现了腹泻症状。 | synonymous: loose, watery, runny | antonymous: constipated, solid | adjective


diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ | n. | 腹泻 | an illness in which the body's solid waste is more liquid than usual and is discharged from the bowels frequently | The traveler experienced severe diarrhea after eating contaminated food. 旅行者吃了受污染的食物后，腹泻严重。| The baby's diarrhea was a cause for concern. 婴儿的腹泻令人担忧。 | synonymous: the runs, the trots, loose bowels | antonymous: constipation | noun
