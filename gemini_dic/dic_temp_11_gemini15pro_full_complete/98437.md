inspectorial:/ɪnˌspɛkˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
检查的；视察的
of or relating to an inspector or inspection.
The report highlighted the inspectorial powers of the agency.  该报告强调了该机构的检查权力。
The board has inspectorial oversight of the school.  董事会对学校有视察监督权。
Synonyms: supervisory, overseeing, regulatory, monitoring
Antonyms: unregulated, unmonitored
词性: 形容词
