obsessionally: /əbˈseʃənəli/
adverb
痴迷地；着魔地
in a way that shows you are obsessed with something
He checks his phone obsessionally. 他不停地查看手机。
近义词: compulsively, fixatedly
反义词: moderately, casually
词性: 副词

词源: From obsession + -ally.  Obsession comes from the Late Latin *obsessionem (nominative obsessio) "a siege, blockade," from Latin obsessus, past participle of obsidere "to besiege, occupy," from ob- "against" + sedere "to sit".
