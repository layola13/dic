injected:/ɪnˈdʒɛktɪd/| adj.|注射的，注入的|Introduced into something, typically by injection.  The word is the past participle of the verb "inject."|The doctor injected the patient with a painkiller. 医生给病人注射了止痛药。|Synonyms: administered, introduced, inserted|Antonyms: withdrawn, extracted|verb (past participle)

inject:/ɪnˈdʒɛkt/| v.|注射，注入|To introduce a substance into something, typically by using a syringe.  Can also mean to introduce a new, exciting, or vital element into something.|The nurse will inject the vaccine into your arm. 护士会把疫苗注射到你的胳膊上。|Synonyms: administer, introduce, insert|Antonyms: withdraw, extract|verb
