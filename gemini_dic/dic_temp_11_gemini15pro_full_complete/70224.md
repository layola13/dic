fibula:/ˈfɪbjələ/ (US), /ˈfɪbjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |腓骨|The fibula is the outer and usually smaller of the two bones between the knee and the ankle in vertebrates.|She fractured her tibia and fibula in a skiing accident. 她滑雪时摔断了胫骨和腓骨。|Synonyms: calf bone, outer bone (of lower leg)|Antonyms: tibia|Noun

fibulae:/ˈfɪbjʊliː/ (plural)| n. |腓骨（复数）|Fibulae are the outer and usually smaller of the two bones between the knee and the ankle in vertebrates (plural form of fibula).|The surgeon carefully examined both fibulae.  外科医生仔细检查了两根腓骨。|Synonyms: calf bones, outer bones (of lower leg)|Antonyms: tibiae|Noun
