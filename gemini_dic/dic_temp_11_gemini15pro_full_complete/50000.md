denl: /ˈdɛnl/
noun
a small, secluded valley; a lair or den, especially of a wild animal.
A denl is a small, secluded valley, often used by animals as a lair or den.
The fox retreated to its denl in the hillside.  狐狸撤退到山坡上的窝里。
近义词: den, lair, burrow
反义词:  exposure, openness
