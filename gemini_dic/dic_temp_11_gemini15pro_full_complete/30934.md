chairmaned:/ˈtʃɛəmənːd/
verb
担任主席；主持
acted as chairman of a meeting or organization.
She chairmaned the meeting effectively. 她有效地主持了会议。
The committee was chairmaned by Professor Smith.  该委员会由史密斯教授担任主席。
Synonyms: presided over, headed, led, moderated
Antonyms:  attended, participated (as a non-chair)
词性: 动词
