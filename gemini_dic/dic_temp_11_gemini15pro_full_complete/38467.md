concha:/ˈkɒntʃə/ (UK), /ˈkɑːntʃə/ (US)| n. |贝壳，海螺; (墨西哥甜面包)康查|A spiral shell, especially of a snail or other mollusk.  A sweet bread roll of Mexican origin, typically shaped like a shell and coated with a sugary topping.|例句：The child found a beautiful concha on the beach. 孩子在沙滩上发现了一个美丽的贝壳。

例句：I bought a delicious concha from the panaderia. 我从面包店买了一个美味的康查。

近义词：shell, seashell (for the seashell meaning); pan dulce (for the bread meaning)|反义词: None|词性:Noun


concha:/ˈkɒntʃə/ (UK), /ˈkɑːntʃə/ (US)| n. |(解剖学)耳廓; 外耳|The hollow part of the external ear.|例句：The doctor examined the patient's concha. 医生检查了病人的耳廓。

例句：An infection in the concha can be painful. 耳廓感染会很痛。

近义词：auricle, pinna|反义词: None|词性:Noun
