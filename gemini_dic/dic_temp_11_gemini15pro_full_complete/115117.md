mangier:/ˈmeɪndʒɪər/ | adj. | 更瘦的，更骨感的 |  comparative form of "mangy": having a thin or patchy coat of fur or hair, typically due to disease or malnutrition; looking shabby and neglected. | The rescued dog looked even mangier than before after a week on the streets. 这只获救的狗在街上待了一周后，看起来比以前更瘦了。| 近义词: thinner, scrawnier, gaunt, emaciated | 反义词:  healthier, fatter, plumper | 词性: 形容词
