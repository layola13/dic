libation:/laɪˈbeɪʃən/ | n. | 奠酒，祭酒；饮料（尤指酒） |  A drink poured out as an offering to a god or spirit or in memory of those who are dead. Also, a drink, especially an alcoholic one. |  The priest made a libation to the gods.  祭司向众神献上了奠酒。

They made a libation to the memory of their fallen comrades. 他们为悼念牺牲的战友而举杯。

We enjoyed libations and lively conversation at the party.  我们在派对上享用了酒水，并进行了热烈的交谈。 | 近义词：drink, beverage, offering, toast | 反义词: abstinence | 词性: noun
