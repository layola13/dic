do-time:/ˈduː taɪm/
verb phrase
服刑；坐牢
To serve a prison sentence.
He did time for armed robbery.  他因持械抢劫而服刑。
She's done time before. 她以前坐过牢。
近义词:serve time, be in prison, be incarcerated
反义词:be released, be free

PHRASE->
do hard time  服重刑；在艰苦的条件下服刑
He did hard time for murder. 他因谋杀而服重刑。

do easy time  服轻松的刑；在条件较好的监狱服刑
Compared to others, he did easy time. 与其他人相比，他服刑很轻松。

do one's time  服完刑期
He did his time and is now a free man. 他服完了刑期，现在是一个自由人。

first time doing time  第一次服刑
It was his first time doing time and he was scared. 这是他第一次服刑，他很害怕。

do time on the installment plan  分期服刑 (指多次短期服刑)
He seemed to be doing time on the installment plan. 他似乎在分期服刑。
