apprenticed:/əˈprɛntɪst/
verb (past tense)|学徒的，apprenticed to sb.| bound as an apprentice; employed as an apprentice.|He was apprenticed to a carpenter.|近义词：trainee, intern, novice, learner|反义词：master, expert, professional|v.

apprenticed:/əˈprɛntɪst/
adjective|学徒的|placed or bound as an apprentice.|an apprenticed carpenter.|近义词：trainee, intern, novice, learner|反义词：master, expert, professional|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French apprentiz, from apprendre “to learn,” from Latin apprehendere “to grasp, seize, learn.”
