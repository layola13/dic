non-depletable:/ˌnɒn dɪˈpliːtəbl/
adjective
非消耗性的；不可耗尽的
Not able to be depleted; inexhaustible.
Solar energy is a non-depletable resource.  太阳能是一种非消耗性能源。
The use of non-depletable resources like wind and solar energy is crucial for sustainable development. 使用风能和太阳能等不可耗尽的资源对于可持续发展至关重要。
近义词: inexhaustible, renewable, sustainable
反义词: depletable, exhaustible, finite
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + depletable.  "Depletable" comes from the verb "deplete," which means to reduce something in size or amount. "Deplete"  originates from the Latin word "deplēre" which means "to empty out."  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning, resulting in "non-depletable" meaning "not able to be emptied out" or "inexhaustible."
