compatible:/kəmˈpætɪbl/ /kəmˈpætəbl/ | adj. |兼容的；相容的；和谐共处的；可共存的|able to exist, live together, or work successfully with something or someone else |This software is compatible with all versions of Windows.  这个软件与所有版本的Windows兼容。| synonym: consistent, harmonious, agreeable | antonym: incompatible, conflicting, discordant |adjective

compatibles:/kəmˈpætɪblz/ /kəmˈpætəblz/ | n. |兼容物；兼容设备；能和睦相处的人|things that are compatible, especially computer hardware or software| The new software has been designed to ensure its compatibles with older systems. 新软件的设计是为了确保与旧系统的兼容性。| synonym: counterparts, complements | antonym: incompatibles | noun
