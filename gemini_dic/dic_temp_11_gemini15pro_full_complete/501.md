abodement:/əˈboʊdmənt/
n.
预兆，预感
an omen, prediction, or presentiment, especially of something evil.
The dark clouds seemed an abodement of the coming storm.  乌云似乎预示着暴风雨即将到来。
Synonyms:foreboding, presage, portent, augury
Antonyms:assurance, certainty

Etymology:
From Middle English *abodement, from aboden + -ment, from Old French aboder.
