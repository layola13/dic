drastically:/ˈdræstɪkli/
adverb
彻底地；激烈地；大幅地
radically, severely, significantly altering something.
The company's profits fell drastically. 公司的利润大幅下降。
The new law drastically changed the way businesses operate. 新法律彻底改变了企业运营的方式。

近义词: severely, radically, significantly, dramatically
反义词: slightly, moderately, gradually, mildly
词性: adverb (副词)

词源:
From Late Latin *drasticus, from Ancient Greek δραστικός (drastikós, “active, efficacious”), from δράω (dráō, “I do, act”).
