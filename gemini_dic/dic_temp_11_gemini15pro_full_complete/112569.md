lovely: /ˈlʌvli/ (UK) /ˈlʌvli/ (US)| adj.|可爱的；美丽的；令人愉快的|Pleasing, delightful, beautiful, and amiable.|例句：She has a lovely voice. 她有一副好嗓子。|例句：We had a lovely time at the beach. 我们在海滩上玩得很开心。|近义词：beautiful, pretty, charming, delightful, pleasant|反义词：ugly, unpleasant, horrible, awful|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *loveli, *luveli, from Old English luflic (“loving, friendly, amiable, lovely”), equivalent to love + -ly. Cognate with Scots luvely (“lovely”), West Frisian leaflik (“lovely”), Dutch lieflijk (“lovely”), German lieblich (“lovely”), Swedish ljuvlig (“lovely”).
