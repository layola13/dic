mesoamerica:/ˌmɛzoʊəˈmɛrɪkə/ | n. | 中美洲 | A region and cultural area in the Americas, extending approximately from central Mexico to Belize, Guatemala, El Salvador, Honduras, Nicaragua, and northern Costa Rica, within which pre-Columbian societies flourished. | The Maya civilization thrived in Mesoamerica. 玛雅文明在中美洲繁荣昌盛。| 近义词: Central America | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  meso- (from Greek mesos "middle") + America
