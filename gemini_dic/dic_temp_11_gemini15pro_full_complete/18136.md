behooveful:/bɪˈhuːvfʊl/
adjective
合适的，适当的，有益的
Suitable, proper, and advantageous; incumbent.
It is behooveful for a young man to show respect to his elders.  对年轻人来说，尊重长辈是应该的。
It is behooveful that he attend the meeting. 他有必要参加这次会议。
近义词: fitting, proper, appropriate, suitable, apt, seemly, decent, right, correct, meet, convenient, expedient, beneficial, advantageous, useful, helpful, desirable, advisable, prudent, wise, sensible, politic
反义词: inappropriate, unsuitable, improper
词性: adjective
