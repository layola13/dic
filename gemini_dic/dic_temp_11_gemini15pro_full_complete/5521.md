alphabetics:/ˌælfəˈbɛtɪks/| n. |字母表; 字母学|The study of alphabetics is crucial for learning a language. 字母学研究对于学习一门语言至关重要。|  Alphabetics is a fundamental part of literacy. 字母表是读写能力的基础部分。 |近义词: alphabet, letters |反义词: None |词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *alphabēticus, from Greek ἀλφάβητος (alphábētos, “alphabet”), from the first two letters of the Greek alphabet, ἄλφα (álpha) and βῆτα (bêta).
