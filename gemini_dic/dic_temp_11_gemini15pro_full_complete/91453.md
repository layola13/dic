hygroscope:/ˈhaɪɡrəskoʊp/ | n. |吸湿器，湿度计|An instrument used for measuring the humidity of the air or a gas.|  The hygroscope indicated a sharp rise in humidity. 吸湿器显示湿度急剧上升。| synonym:hygrometer | antonym:N/A | noun

hygroscopic:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈskɑːpɪk/ | adj. |吸湿的|  Having the ability to absorb moisture from the air.|  Salt is a hygroscopic substance. 盐是一种吸湿性物质。| synonym:absorbent, moisture-absorbing | antonym:waterproof, water-repellent | adjective
