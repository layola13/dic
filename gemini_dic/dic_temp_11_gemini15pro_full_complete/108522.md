laystall:/ˈleɪstɔːl/| n.|垃圾堆，粪堆|A place where refuse or dung is deposited; a dunghill. |The farmer dumped the manure onto the laystall. 农民把粪肥倒在粪堆上。|Near the laystall, the air was thick with the smell of decay. 在垃圾堆附近，空气中弥漫着腐烂的味道。|近义词：dunghill, midden, dump, refuse heap|反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly compost heap if considering reuse)|词性:noun
