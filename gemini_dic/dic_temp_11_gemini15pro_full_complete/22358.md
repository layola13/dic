boo-boo:/ˈbuːbuː/
n.
小错误；轻伤；小麻烦
a minor injury or mistake
例句：It's just a little boo-boo, don't worry. 这只是个小伤，别担心。

例句：He made a boo-boo on the report. 他在报告里犯了个小错误。
近义词：mistake, error, slip-up, ouch, owie (chiefly childish)
反义词:  success, triumph (referring to mistake); healing, recovery (referring to injury)
词性: 名词


boo-boo:/ˈbuːbuː/
int.
哎呦
an exclamation of pain or dismay, typically used by children
例句：Boo-boo! I bumped my head! 哎呦！我撞到头了！

例句：Boo-boo! That hurt! 哎呦！好疼！
近义词：ouch, owie
反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection expressing pain)
词性: 感叹词
