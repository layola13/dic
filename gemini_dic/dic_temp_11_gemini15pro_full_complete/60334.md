egoity: /ˈiːɡəʊɪti/ | n. | 自我中心; 自负;  |  The quality of being excessively focused on oneself and one's own importance. A synonym for egotism and self-centeredness. |  His egoity prevented him from seeing the needs of others. 他的自我中心让他看不到别人的需要。|Synonyms: egotism, self-centeredness, narcissism, vanity | Antonyms: humility, modesty, selflessness | Noun
