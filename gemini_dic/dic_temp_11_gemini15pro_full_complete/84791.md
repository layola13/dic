handsome:/ˈhænsəm/| adj.|英俊的，漂亮的；数量可观的；慷慨的；美观的|Pleasing in appearance, especially a man; of considerable size or amount; generous; having an appearance appropriate to its use or purpose.|例句：He is a handsome young man. 他是一个英俊的年轻人。|例句：She received a handsome sum of money. 她收到了一大笔钱。|近义词：attractive, good-looking, beautiful, generous, ample|反义词：ugly, unattractive, meager, paltry|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *handsum, *hondsom (“easy to handle, convenient, apt, ready; pleasant, courteous”), equivalent to hand +‎ -some.  Originally meant "easy to handle, convenient." The sense shifted to "appropriate, fitting" and then to "of pleasing appearance."
