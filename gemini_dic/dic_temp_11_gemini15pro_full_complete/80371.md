golet:/ˈɡɒlət/ | n. |小海湾，狭窄的水道|A small bay or narrow inlet of water. |The boat navigated the golet carefully. 船小心地航行过小海湾。|近义词: inlet, creek, cove, bayou |反义词: ocean, sea |词性: 名词

词源:
From French *goulet*, diminutive of *goule*, from Latin *gula* "throat."
