bleary-eyed:/ˈblɪəri aɪd/
adjective
睡眼惺忪的；视线模糊不清的
Having or showing eyes that are red or watery, especially from tiredness or lack of sleep.  Characterized by blurred vision or drowsiness.
He stumbled into the kitchen, bleary-eyed and disoriented. 他跌跌撞撞地走进厨房，睡眼惺忪，神志不清。
She was bleary-eyed after studying all night. 她熬夜学习后，双眼惺忪。
近义词: sleepy, tired, exhausted, bloodshot
反义词: alert, awake, refreshed
词性: adjective
词源: From "blear" (dim, blurred) + "eyed" (having eyes of a specified kind). "Blear" comes from Middle English *bleren*, of uncertain origin.
