complimenters:/ˈkɒmplɪmentəz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmplɪmentərz/ (US)| n. |称赞者，恭维者|People who give compliments. Those who express praise or admiration.|She received many compliments on her new dress, and the complimenters were all sincere. 她收到很多关于她新裙子的赞美，而且所有称赞者都是真诚的。|
The complimenters were effusive in their praise of her performance. 称赞者们对她的表演赞不绝口。|
Synonyms: praisers, lauders, admirers|
Antonyms: critics, detractors, disparagers|
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From compliment + -er (agent suffix). Ultimately from Latin complēre "to fill up, complete."
