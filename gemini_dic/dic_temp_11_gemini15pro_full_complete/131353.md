nonchivalrously: /nɒnˈʃɪvəlrəsli/ (UK) /nɑːnˈʃɪvəlrəsli/ (US)
| adv. | 不骑士地，不绅士地，无礼地 | In a manner lacking the qualities of chivalry, such as courtesy, honor, and valor, especially towards women. | He nonchivalrously pushed past the elderly woman to get on the bus. 他很不绅士地推开老妇人上了公共汽车。 | Examples: 
He nonchivalrously interrupted her while she was speaking.  他不绅士地打断了她说话。

She felt he acted nonchivalrously by not offering to pay for dinner. 她觉得他不主动付晚餐钱很不绅士。


| 近义词: discourteously, rudely, ungallantly, inconsiderately | 反义词: chivalrously, courteously, politely, gallantly | 词性: 副词 (Adverb)
