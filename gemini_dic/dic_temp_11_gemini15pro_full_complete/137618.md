nontrusts:/ˈnɒnˈtrʌsts/ | n. | 非信托机构/非信托资产 | Entities or assets that are not held in trust; organizations or properties not governed by the legal framework of a trust. |  Example: The government is investigating the movement of funds into nontrusts. 政府正在调查资金流入非信托机构的情况。| Example: He manages both trusts and nontrusts for his clients. 他为客户管理信托和非信托资产。 | Near synonyms: non-trust entities, non-fiduciary assets | Antonyms: trusts, trust funds | Noun
