nonphilologic:/ˌnɒnˌfɪləˈlɒdʒɪk/
adjective
非文献学的；不属于文献学的
Not pertaining to philology; not based on the study of language and literature.
His approach to the text was surprisingly nonphilologic, focusing more on the historical context than the linguistic nuances.  他处理文本的方法令人惊讶地是非文献学的，更多地关注历史背景而不是语言细微差别。
The historian employed a nonphilologic method, prioritizing archaeological evidence. 这位历史学家采用了一种非文献学的方法，优先考虑考古证据。
近义词: nonlinguistic
反义词: philological
词性: adjective
