miscaptions:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃənz/| n.|错误的标题或说明|An incorrect or misleading caption accompanying a picture or video.  It's formed by combining "mis-" (meaning wrong or bad) with "captions."|The photo had several miscaptions, identifying the wrong people.|Synonyms: mislabeling, misidentification, inaccurate captioning|Antonyms: accurate captioning, correct labeling, proper identification|noun
