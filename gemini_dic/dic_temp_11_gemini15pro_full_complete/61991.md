enal:/ˈiːnæl/ | adj. | 烯醛的; 含烯醛基的 | Relating to or containing an enal group (a combination of an alkene and an aldehyde). |  This compound is an enal, possessing both a double bond and an aldehyde group.  这种化合物是一种烯醛，同时具有双键和醛基。| Example:  Acrolein is a simple enal. 丙烯醛是一种简单的烯醛。| Synonym:  α,β-unsaturated aldehyde | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with saturated aldehydes) | Word Type: Adjective
