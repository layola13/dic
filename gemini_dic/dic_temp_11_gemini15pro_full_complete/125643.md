mummers:/ˈmʌmərz/ | n. | 扮演哑剧的人；化妆游行者 | Actors who wear masks and costumes, especially during traditional festivals, and perform in dumb shows or plays. |  The mummers paraded through the streets, their costumes bright and colorful. 化妆游行者在街上游行，他们的服装鲜艳夺目。|  The mummers' play told the story of St. George and the Dragon. 哑剧演员的表演讲述了圣乔治和龙的故事。 | synonyms: masqueraders, guisers, performers | antonyms: audience, spectators | noun
