nonprosperously:/ˌnɑːnprɑːˈspɛrəsli/
adverb
不繁荣地，不顺利地
Not prosperously; unsuccessfully; in a way that does not thrive or flourish.
The business venture concluded nonprosperously, leaving them in debt.  这家企业的风险投资以失败告终，让他们负债累累。
The small town fared nonprosperously after the factory closed down.  工厂倒闭后，小镇的经济不景气。
Synonym: unsuccessfully, unfavorably, poorly, badly, unfortunately
Antonym: prosperously, successfully, favorably, well, fortunately
词性: adverb (副词)
