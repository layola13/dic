overking:/ˈoʊvərˌkɪŋ/| n.|僭位者，篡位者|A person who usurps the throne or authority of a king.|例句：The overking ruled with an iron fist, oppressing the people. 篡位者用铁腕统治，压迫人民。

例句：The overking was eventually overthrown and the rightful heir restored to the throne.  篡位者最终被推翻，合法继承人重登王位。|近义词：usurper, pretender|反义词: rightful king, legitimate ruler|词性: noun

词源: From 'over' (meaning 'above' or 'excessive') + 'king' (meaning 'ruler').  Indicates someone who rules 'above' or in place of the legitimate king.
