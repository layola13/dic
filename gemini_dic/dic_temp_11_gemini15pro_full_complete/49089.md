deictics:/ˈdaɪktɪks/| n.|指示词|Words or expressions whose meaning depends on the context in which they are used, such as "here," "now," "I," "you," "this," "that."|Example: The word "here" is a deictic expression because its meaning depends on the speaker's location.|Synonyms: indexicals, pointing words|Antonyms: None|Noun

deictic:/ˈdaɪktɪk/| adj.|指示的|Relating to or characteristic of deictics; showing or pointing out directly.|Example:  "This," "that," "these," and "those" are deictic adjectives. |Synonyms: indexical, demonstrative|Antonyms: None|Adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I use this pen every day. = 我每天用这支笔。
She lives here now. = 她现在住在这里。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am holding this book right now. = 我现在正拿着这本书。
He is pointing at that building over there. = 他正指着那边那栋楼。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have lived here for five years. = 我在这里住了五年了。
We have already seen that movie. = 我们已经看过那部电影了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been living here since 2018.  = 我从2018年开始就住在这里。
She has been pointing at that picture for a minute. = 她指着那张照片已经有一分钟了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I met him here yesterday. = 我昨天在这里遇见了他。
She said that she would be here tomorrow. = 她说她明天会在这里。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was standing here when it happened. = 事情发生时我正站在这里。
They were discussing that issue at the meeting. = 他们在会议上讨论那个问题。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already left when she arrived here. = 她到这里时我已经离开了。
He had seen that movie before. = 他以前看过那部电影。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been living there for two years before I moved here. = 我搬到这里之前在那里住了两年。
They had been discussing that problem for hours before they reached a solution. = 他们在找到解决方案之前已经讨论了几个小时的问题。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be here tomorrow. = 我明天会在这里。
They will go there next week. = 他们下周会去那里。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be working here this time tomorrow. = 明天这个时候我将在这里工作。
She will be living there next year. = 明年她将住在那里。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have lived here for six years. = 到明年，我将在这里住了六年。
By the time you arrive, I will have finished this. = 等你到的时候，我将已经完成了这个。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been living here for five years and six months. = 到下个月，我就住在这里五年零六个月了。
By the end of the year, she will have been working there for ten years. = 到年底，她就在那里工作十年了。


虚拟语气:
If I were there, I would help you. = 如果我在那里，我会帮助你。
If she had been here, she would have known what to do. = 如果她当时在这里，她就会知道该做什么。


被动语态:
This is where the meeting was held. = 会议就是在这里举行的。
That problem was discussed yesterday. = 那个问题昨天讨论过了。


疑问句:
Where are you now? = 你现在在哪里？
When did you see that? = 你什么时候看到那个的？

否定句:
I am not here now. = 我现在不在这里。
She didn't see that yesterday. = 她昨天没有看到那个。

词源：From Greek *deiktikos, from deiknynai "to show," from PIE root *deik- "to show," also "pronounce solemnly."
