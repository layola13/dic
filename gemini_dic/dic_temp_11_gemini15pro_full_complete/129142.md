no-doubt:/noʊ daʊt/| adv.|毫无疑问地，肯定地|Used to emphasize that something is almost certainly true.  Expressing strong certainty or conviction.|例句：No doubt she'll call us when she arrives. 她到了肯定会给我们打电话。

例句：He's no doubt the best player on the team.  他毫无疑问是队里最好的球员。

近义词：certainly, definitely, undoubtedly, without a doubt, assuredly|反义词：doubtfully, possibly, perhaps, maybe|词性:副词
