knoblike:/ˈnɑːblaɪk/| adj.|像旋钮的，球形的|Resembling a knob, especially in shape; rounded. |The plant had knoblike flowers.  这种植物的花朵呈球形。|The fungus had a knoblike cap. 这种真菌的菌盖呈旋钮状。|近义词：rounded, spherical, bulbous|反义词：pointed, sharp, elongated|词性:形容词

词源:  From knob +‎ -like.  来自名词 knob（旋钮）和形容词后缀 -like（像...的）。
