mischievously:/ˈmɪstʃɪvəsli/| adv.|顽皮地；淘气地；恶意地|In a way that shows a fondness for causing trouble in a playful or harmless way, or intended to cause minor harm or annoyance.|The child mischievously grinned as he hid his sister's toy.  这孩子顽皮地笑着，藏起了姐姐的玩具。|

The cat mischievously batted at the dangling string.  猫淘气地拍打着悬挂的绳子。|Synonyms: playfully, naughtily, impishly, roguishly|Antonyms: seriously, obediently, respectfully|Adverb
