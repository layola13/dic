disinterest:/dɪsˈɪntrɪst/ | n.| 不感兴趣，漠不关心 | Lack of interest; indifference.  |  He showed a complete disinterest in the project. 他对这个项目完全不感兴趣。 | 近义词: indifference, apathy, unconcern | 反义词: interest, enthusiasm, zeal | noun

disinterested:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪd/ | adj.|  无私的；公正的 |  Not influenced by personal interest or advantage; unbiased. | A disinterested judge is essential for a fair trial.  公正的法官对公平审判至关重要。 | 近义词: impartial, unbiased, neutral | 反义词: interested, biased, prejudiced | adjective
