franchisee:/ˌfræn.tʃaɪˈziː/| n.|特许经营人；加盟商|A person or company that is granted a license to operate a business under the franchisor's trademark and business model.|例句：The franchisee agreed to pay a royalty fee to the franchisor.  加盟商同意向特许人支付特许权使用费。

例句：The franchisee opened a new restaurant in the city center.  加盟商在市中心开了一家新餐厅。|近义词：licensee, dealer, operator|反义词：franchisor|词性：名词
