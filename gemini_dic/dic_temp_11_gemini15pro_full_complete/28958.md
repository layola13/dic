carry-overs:/ˈkæriˌoʊvərz/
n.
结转，延滞，遗留物
Amounts carried over from one column, page or book to the next.  从一列、一页或一本书结转到下一列、下一页或下一本书的金额。
The carry-overs from last year's budget helped fund the new project.  去年预算的结转资金帮助资助了这个新项目。
carryover, surplus, remainder
deficit, shortfall
noun
