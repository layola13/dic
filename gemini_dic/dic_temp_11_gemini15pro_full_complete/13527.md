attal: /ˈætəl/
attal| adjective |附属的，依附的|Attached or pertaining to; connected as a subordinate part or branch.  Originating from the Arabic word "aṭ-ṭāl", meaning "the vanguard" or "the front."  It later evolved in Sicilian and Italian to refer to things attached or dependent. |The attal buildings were considered part of the main complex. 附属建筑被认为是主要建筑群的一部分。|近义词: attached, subordinate, auxiliary, dependent, affiliated|反义词: independent, detached, separate|词性: adjective
