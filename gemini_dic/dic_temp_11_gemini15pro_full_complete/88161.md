hillinesses:/ˈhɪlɪnɪsɪz/
noun
丘陵起伏；多山的地形
The state of being hilly; a series of hills.  The terrain was marked by its pronounced hillinesses.  该地区以其显著的丘陵起伏为特征。
The hillinesses of the region made farming difficult. 该地区的多山地形使得耕作困难。
undulation, unevenness, rolling terrain
flatness, levelness
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From hilly + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Hilly" originates from the Old English "hyll" meaning "hill," with "-y" added to form an adjective.  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns denoting a state or quality. "-es" is a plural suffix.
