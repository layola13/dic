incurrable:/ɪnˈkjʊərəb(ə)l/
adjective
无法治愈的；不可救药的
not able to be cured
例句：He has an incurrable disease. 他患有不治之症。
例句：She suffers from an incurrable optimism. 她乐观得无可救药。
近义词：irremediable, incurable, terminal, hopeless
反义词：curable, treatable, remediable
词性: adjective


incur:/ɪnˈkɜːr/
verb
招致；遭受
To experience something unpleasant as a result of one's own actions or behavior.
例句：I incurred a lot of expenses on my trip. 我的旅行花了很多钱。
例句：She incurred the wrath of her boss. 她招致了老板的愤怒。
近义词：suffer, sustain, experience, attract, provoke
反义词：avoid, evade, prevent
词性: verb
