inosinic:/ˌɪnəˈsɪnɪk/| adj.|肌苷酸的|Relating to or derived from inosine, a nucleoside that is formed by the deamination of adenosine.|Inosinic acid is important in muscle metabolism. 肌苷酸在肌肉代谢中很重要。|Synonyms: hypoxanthine-ribose monophosphate|Antonyms: N/A|adjective


inosinate:/ɪˈnɒsɪneɪt/| n.|肌苷酸盐|A salt or ester of inosinic acid.|Sodium inosinate is commonly used as a flavor enhancer. 肌苷酸钠通常用作增鲜剂。|Synonyms: IMP|Antonyms: N/A|noun
