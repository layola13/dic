lans:/lænz/ | n. | 局域网 (Local Area Network) |  A computer network that interconnects computers within a limited area such as a residence, school, laboratory, university campus or office building. |  Our office lans allows us to share files easily. 我们的办公室局域网使我们可以轻松共享文件。| synonyms: network, intranet | antonyms: internet, wide area network | noun

lans:/lænz/ | v. (informal, often used in gaming) | 登陆; 加入 (游戏) | To connect to and join a game or online platform, especially in a multiplayer context. | He's lansing into the game right now. 他现在正在登陆游戏。 | synonyms: log in, join, connect | antonyms: log out, disconnect, leave | verb
