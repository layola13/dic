outrigs: /ˈaʊtrɪɡz/
n.
支腿，舷外支架
A framework that extends outward from the side of a boat or canoe to stabilize it.
The outriggers kept the canoe from tipping over in the rough water.  舷外支架使独木舟在汹涌的水中不会翻倒。
stabilizers, supports
n/a
noun
