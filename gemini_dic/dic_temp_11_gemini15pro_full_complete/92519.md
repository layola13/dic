hypothyroidisms:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈθaɪrɔɪdɪzəmz/
n.
甲状腺功能减退（症）的复数形式
A condition in which the thyroid gland doesn't produce enough thyroid hormone.  This can lead to a variety of symptoms, such as fatigue, weight gain, and depression.
She suffers from hypothyroidisms and has to take medication daily. 她患有甲状腺功能减退症，必须每天服药。
Hypothyroidism can be treated with medication. 甲状腺功能减退症可以通过药物治疗。
近义词:thyroid deficiency, underactive thyroid
反义词:hyperthyroidism, overactive thyroid
词性:noun


词源:
hypo- (Greek: under, below) + thyroid (Greek: shield-shaped, referring to the thyroid cartilage) + -ism (suffix forming nouns of condition or state) + -s (plural suffix)
