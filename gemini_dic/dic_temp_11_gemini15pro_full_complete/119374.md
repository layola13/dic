microcosms: /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌkɒzəmz/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊˌkɑːzəmz/ (US)| n. |缩影，微观世界|A community, place, or situation regarded as encapsulating in miniature the characteristics of something much larger.|例句：The family is a microcosm of society.  这个家庭是社会的缩影。

例句：The school is a microcosm of the wider world. 这所学校是更广阔世界的缩影。|近义词：epitome, miniature, model|反义词：macrocosm, universe|词性：名词
