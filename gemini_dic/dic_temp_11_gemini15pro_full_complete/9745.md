antiwelfare:/ˌæntiˈwɛlfeər/| adj.|反福利的，反对社会福利的|Opposed to government-provided social welfare programs.|He holds an antiwelfare stance, believing such programs discourage self-reliance. 他持反福利立场，认为这样的计划会阻碍人们的自立。|近义词：anti-social security, against welfare|反义词: pro-welfare, supportive of welfare|词性:形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  (from Greek anti-)
welfare (noun): the state of being healthy, happy, or prosperous; financial or other assistance given to those who are unable to support themselves. (from Middle English wel fare, meaning "good fortune")
