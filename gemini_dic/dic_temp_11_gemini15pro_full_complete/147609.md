overpleased: /ˌoʊvərˈpliːzd/
adjective
过分高兴的，过分满意的
Excessively pleased; too pleased.  Feeling or expressing more pleasure than is appropriate or warranted.
She was overpleased with her new car, bragging about it to everyone. 她对自己的新车过分满意，逢人就夸。
He was overpleased with the outcome, almost to the point of being smug. 他对结果过分满意，几乎到了自鸣得意的地步。
近义词: delighted, overjoyed, elated, thrilled
反义词: displeased, dissatisfied, unhappy
