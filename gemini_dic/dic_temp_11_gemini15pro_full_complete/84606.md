hamstrings:/ˈhæmˌstrɪŋz/ | n. | 腿筋；腘绳肌 | The muscles at the back of the upper leg. | I pulled a hamstring during the race. 我在比赛中拉伤了腿筋。 | synonyms: hamstring muscles | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a muscle group) | noun

hamstring:/ˈhæmˌstrɪŋ/ | vt. | 使无力；使难有作为 | To severely restrict the activity or effectiveness of someone or something. | The company was hamstrung by lack of funds.  公司因缺乏资金而受阻。 | synonyms: cripple, disable, incapacitate, impede | antonyms: empower, enable, facilitate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hamm + string.  "Hamm" referred to the back of the knee, and "string" referred to tendons.
