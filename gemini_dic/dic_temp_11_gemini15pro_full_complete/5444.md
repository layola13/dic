almses: /ɑːmzɪz/ | n. |施舍物，救济品|Something given freely to relieve the poor.|The king distributed almses to the beggars. 国王向乞丐分发施舍物。|Synonyms: charity, donations, offerings|Antonyms: earnings, income|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English ælmesse, from Late Latin eleemosyna, from Greek eleēmosynē ("pity, alms"), from eleēmōn ("pitiful, compassionate").
