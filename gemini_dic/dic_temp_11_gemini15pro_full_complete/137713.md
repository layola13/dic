nonutopians:/ˌnɒnjuːˈtəʊpiənz/ | n. |非乌托邦主义者；非理想主义者| People who do not believe in or subscribe to utopian ideals; those who are realistic or pragmatic about the limitations of achieving a perfect society. |  The nonutopians argued that the proposed social reforms were impractical and ultimately doomed to fail. 非乌托邦主义者认为，拟议的社会改革不切实际，最终注定要失败。 | realists, pragmatists | utopians, idealists | noun

nonutopian:/ˌnɒnjuːˈtəʊpiən/ | adj. |非乌托邦的；非理想化的|  Not characteristic of a utopia; realistic or pragmatic. |  The author presented a nonutopian view of the future, acknowledging the persistence of social problems. 作者提出了对未来的非乌托邦式的观点，承认社会问题的持续存在。 | realistic, pragmatic | utopian, idealistic | adjective
