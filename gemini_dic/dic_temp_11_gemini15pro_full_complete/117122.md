meanderer:/miˈændərər/ | n. | 漫步者；闲逛者；蜿蜒曲折的人/物 | someone who meanders; a person who wanders aimlessly or casually |  The meanderer strolled through the park, enjoying the sunshine. 漫步者在公园里闲逛，享受着阳光。| synonyms: wanderer, rambler, roamer, stroller | antonyms:  rusher, sprinter | noun


meander:/miˈændər/ | v. | 漫步；蜿蜒流动；迂回曲折 | to wander aimlessly or casually; to follow a winding course | The river meanders through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。| synonyms: wander, ramble, roam, stroll, wind, snake | antonyms:  rush, sprint, straighten | verb
