dandy:/ˈdændi/ | n. adj. | 花花公子；纨绔子弟；极好的 | A man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress and appearance. / Excellent; first-rate. |  He was quite the dandy in his youth, always impeccably dressed. 他年轻时是个十足的花花公子，总是穿着无可挑剔。/ That's a dandy new car you've got! 你那辆新车真棒！ | 近义词：fop, swell, exquisite, excellent | 反义词：slob, slovenly, terrible | 词性: 名词，形容词

dandy:/ˈdændi/ | 词源 |  Origin uncertain, perhaps from Andrew, a common Scottish personal name. |  来源不确定，可能来自安德鲁，一个常见的苏格兰人名。
