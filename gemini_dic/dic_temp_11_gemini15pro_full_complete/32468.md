chinampas:/tʃɪˈnɑːmpəs/ |n.|奇南帕斯，浮岛|Artificial islands built on freshwater lakes for agricultural purposes, particularly in the Valley of Mexico.  These "floating gardens" were created by the Aztecs and other pre-Columbian peoples. |The chinampas provided fertile land for growing crops. 奇南帕斯为种植作物提供了肥沃的土地。

The Aztecs built a vast network of chinampas to feed their growing population.  阿兹特克人建造了庞大的奇南帕斯网络来养活他们不断增长的人口。|近义词:floating gardens, raised fields|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Nahuatl *chināmitl, meaning "fence of canes" or "place enclosed with canes."
