landau:/ˈlændɔː/ (US), /ˈlændɔː/ (UK)| n.|四轮马车；兰道车|A horse-drawn carriage with a folding top over the rear passenger compartment.|

例句：The wealthy family arrived at the opera house in a stylish landau.  富裕的家庭乘坐一辆时髦的四轮马车抵达歌剧院。

例句：The landau offered a comfortable ride through the cobblestone streets.  四轮马车在鹅卵石街道上提供了舒适的乘坐体验。

近义词：carriage, coach|

ETYMOLOGY->
From German Landau, named after the city of Landau in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany, where these carriages were first made.
