hypermanias:/ˌhaɪpərˈmeɪniəz/
|n.|
|躁狂症|
|A state of abnormally elevated, expansive, or irritable mood and persistently increased activity or energy lasting at least one week.|
|He experienced periods of hypermanias followed by deep depressions.|他经历了躁狂症发作，随后是严重的抑郁症。|
|mania, euphoria, frenzy|
|depression, lethargy, apathy|
|noun|


hypermania:/ˌhaɪpərˈmeɪniə/
|n.|
|躁狂症|
|A state of abnormally elevated, expansive, or irritable mood and persistently increased activity or energy lasting at least one week. It's a more severe form of mania.|
|Her hypermania led her to make impulsive decisions.|她的躁狂症导致她做出冲动的决定。|
|mania, euphoria, frenzy|
|depression, lethargy, apathy|
|noun|
