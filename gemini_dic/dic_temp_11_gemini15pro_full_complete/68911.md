例句：The new product is a fasttracker in the market and has already gained significant market share.  这款新产品在市场上发展迅速，已经获得了很大的市场份额。|近义词：high-flyer, go-getter, quick learner|反义词:slowpoke, laggard, plodder|词性:noun
