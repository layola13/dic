interdistinguish:/ˌɪntər dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/
verb
区分，辨别
To distinguish between; to differentiate.
It is difficult to interdistinguish between the two species of birds.  区分这两种鸟类很困难。
He could interdistinguish the subtle flavors in the wine. 他能分辨出葡萄酒中微妙的味道。

近义词: differentiate, distinguish, discriminate
反义词: confuse, conflate
