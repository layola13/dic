broomy: /ˈbruːmi/ | adj. | 多金毛的，似扫帚的 | Covered with or resembling broom; overgrown with broom. | The hillside was broomy, covered in yellow flowers.  山坡上长满了金雀花，像一把把扫帚。 | The path was narrow and broomy.  小路狭窄，两旁都是金雀花。 | 近义词：bushy, scrubby | 反义词：bare, clear | 词性: 形容词
词源：From broom + -y.  来自名词broom（金雀花，扫帚）加上形容词后缀-y.
