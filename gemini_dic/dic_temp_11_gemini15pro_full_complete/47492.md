deans:/diːnz/
n.
院长们；系主任们
The deans of several colleges met to discuss the new curriculum.
几位学院的院长开会讨论新课程。
plural of dean
dean 的复数形式
The deans are meeting tomorrow.
院长们明天要开会。
近义词:heads, directors, administrators
反义词:students, faculty (泛指)
词性:名词(plural noun)

dean:/diːn/
n.
院长；系主任
The dean of the medical school is a renowned surgeon.
医学院院长是一位著名的外科医生。
From Old French *deien (modern French doyen), from Medieval Latin decanus "leader of ten," from Latin decem "ten". Originally referred to a monk in charge of ten others.
源自古法语 *deien（现代法语 doyen），来自中世纪拉丁语 decanus “十人领袖”，来自拉丁语 decem “十”。最初指的是负责十个其他僧侣的僧侣。
近义词:head, director, principal
反义词:student, faculty (泛指)
词性:名词(noun)
