lucidness:/ˈluːsɪdnəs/| n. |清醒；明晰；清晰易懂|The state of being lucid; clarity of thought and expression.|Her writing is remarkable for its lucidity and conciseness. 她的写作以清晰简洁著称。|近义词：clarity, clearness, transparency, perspicuity|反义词：vagueness, obscurity, confusion, muddle|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *luciditas, from Latin lucidus (“light, bright, clear”), from lux (“light”).
