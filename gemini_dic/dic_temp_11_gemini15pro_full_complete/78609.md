gibbing:/ˈɡɪbɪŋ/
n.|去内脏;去除鱼鳃|The process of removing the gills and entrails of a fish. |Example: The gibbing of the salmon was quick and efficient.  三文鱼的去内脏过程快速而有效。|Synonyms: gutting, cleaning, eviscerating|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
gibbing:/ˈɡɪbɪŋ/
v.|去内脏;去除鱼鳃|To remove the gills and entrails of a fish. |Example: He gibbed the fish as soon as he caught it. 他一抓到鱼就去掉了内脏。|Synonyms: gut, clean, eviscerate|Antonyms: N/A|Verb
