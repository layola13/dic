compress:/kəmˈprɛs/ /kəmˈprɛs/| v. n.|压缩，浓缩，压迫|To press or squeeze together; to reduce in size or volume.  Also, a folded cloth or pad applied to an injury.|She compressed the files into a zip folder. 她把文件压缩成一个zip文件夹。|synonyms: condense, compact, squeeze, constrict|antonyms: expand, enlarge, decompress|verb

compress:/ˈkɑːmˌprɛs/ /ˈkɒmˌprɛs/| n.|敷布，压布|A soft pad or dressing applied firmly to a part of the body to stop bleeding or relieve pain.|The nurse applied a cold compress to his forehead. 护士在他的额头上敷了一块冷敷布。|synonyms: poultice, dressing, pad|antonyms: N/A|noun
