dreamier:/ˈdriːmiər (US)/ /ˈdriːmiə(r) (UK)| adj.|更梦幻的，更爱幻想的|The comparative form of "dreamy"; more dreamy; given to or characterized by daydreaming; vague and impractical.|例句：He became dreamier as he listened to the music. 听着音乐，他变得更加恍惚了。

例句：The sunset painted the sky in dreamier colors than usual. 日落把天空染成了比平时更梦幻般的色彩。|近义词：more ethereal, more romantic, more wistful|反义词：more pragmatic, more grounded, more hard-headed|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dremerye, *dremery, from Old English drēam + -er, -ory.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *draumaz.
