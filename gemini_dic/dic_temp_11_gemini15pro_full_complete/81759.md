greeze:/ɡriːz/ /ɡriz/ | n. |油脂，润滑油 | A thick, oily substance used as a lubricant. |  The mechanic applied grease to the axle. 机械师给车轴上了润滑油。| Synonyms: lubricant, oil, fat | Antonyms: solvent, cleaner | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *greisse, from Late Latin *crassus, from Latin crassus (“thick, fat”).
