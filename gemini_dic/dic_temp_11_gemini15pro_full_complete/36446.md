cold:/kəʊld/ (British) /koʊld/ (American)| adj. n.|寒冷的；冷淡的；感冒|Feeling a low temperature; not friendly or emotional; a common illness that makes you sneeze and cough.|

例句：
The weather is cold today. 今天天气很冷。
She gave him a cold stare. 她冷冷地瞪了他一眼。

例句：
I have a cold. 我感冒了。
He caught a cold last week. 他上周感冒了。

近义词：chilly, frigid, icy, indifferent, aloof|反义词:warm, hot, friendly, passionate|词性:形容词, 名词
