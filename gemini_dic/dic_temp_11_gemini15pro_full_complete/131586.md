noncompetencies:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːmpɪtənsiz/
|n.|无能；不胜任；不合格|Lack of necessary skills or knowledge to perform a task or fulfill a role effectively.  The plural form of noncompetency.|His noncompetencies in management led to the company's downfall. 他的管理方面的无能导致了公司的垮台。|近义词:inabilities, inadequacies, shortcomings, deficiencies|反义词:competencies, abilities, skills, capabilities|词性:名词
