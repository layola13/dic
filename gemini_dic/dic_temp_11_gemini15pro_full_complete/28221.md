capsicin:/kæpˈseɪsɪn/ | n. |辣椒素|A pungent crystalline compound present in chilli peppers.|Capsaicin is the active component of chilli peppers, giving them their heat. 辣椒素是辣椒的活性成分，赋予它们辣味。|
Capsaicin creams can be used to relieve muscle pain. 辣椒素乳膏可以用来缓解肌肉疼痛。|Synonyms: chilli pepper extract, hot pepper extract|Antonyms: None|Noun
