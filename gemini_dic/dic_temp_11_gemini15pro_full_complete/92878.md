idealising:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
理想化；将…理想化
The process of making something or someone seem perfect or better than reality.
She was idealising her boyfriend, ignoring all his flaws. 她一直在理想化她的男朋友，忽略了他所有的缺点。
He tends to idealise his childhood, remembering only the good times. 他倾向于理想化他的童年，只记得美好的时光。
glorifying, romanticizing, exalting
vilifying, denigrating, disparaging
verb

idealized:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzd/
adjective
理想化的
Having been made to seem perfect or better than reality.
She had an idealized view of marriage. 她对婚姻抱有理想化的看法。
The painting presented an idealized version of rural life. 这幅画呈现了理想化的乡村生活。
romantic, perfect, utopian
realistic, flawed, imperfect
adjective
