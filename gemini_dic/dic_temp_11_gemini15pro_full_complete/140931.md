one:/wʌn/ | num. pron. adj.|一，一个，某个|A single unit or thing; the first cardinal number.  Also used as a pronoun or adjective.|例句：I have one apple. 我有一个苹果。|近义词：single, individual, a|反义词: two, many, several|词性:数词

one:/wʌn/ |pron.|一个人，任何人|An unspecified person; anyone.|例句：One should always be polite. 一个人应该始终保持礼貌。|近义词：someone, anyone, a person|反义词: none, nobody|词性:代词

one:/wʌn/ |adj.|同一的，唯一的|Being a single entity; united.|例句：They share one goal. 他们拥有一个共同的目标。|近义词：same, single, united|反义词: different, separate, many|词性:形容词
