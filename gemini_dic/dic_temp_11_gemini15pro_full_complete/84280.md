half-theatrical:/ˌhæf θiˈætrɪkəl/
adj.
半戏剧化的；略带戏剧性的
Having some of the qualities of theater or dramatic performance, but not fully or genuinely so.
Her reaction was a little half-theatrical, as if she was playing to an audience. 她的反应有点半戏剧化，好像她在演戏给观众看。

Her farewell was half-theatrical, complete with a dramatic flourish of her hand. 她的告别略带戏剧性，最后还夸张地挥了挥手。

近义词:histrionic, dramatic, melodramatic
反义词:understated, subdued, natural
词性:adjective
