demonises:/ˈdiːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
妖魔化; 将…描绘成恶魔
to portray someone or something as wicked or threatening.
The media often demonises teenagers, portraying them as lazy and irresponsible. 媒体经常妖魔化青少年，把他们描绘成懒惰和不负责任的人。
The politician demonised his opponent, accusing him of corruption and treachery.  这位政治家妖魔化他的对手，指责他腐败和背叛。
synonyms: vilifies, denounces, condemns, criticizes harshly
antonyms: praises, glorifies, extols, celebrates
v.
