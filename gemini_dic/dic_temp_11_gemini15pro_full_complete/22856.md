bosun:/ˈbəʊsən/ (US) /ˈbəʊzn/ (UK)| n. | 水手长；甲板长 | A ship's officer in charge of equipment and the crew on deck.  A non-commissioned officer responsible for the ship's anchors, ropes, rigging, boats, flags, and general deck maintenance. |  The bosun piped all hands on deck. 水手长用哨子召集全体船员到甲板上。 | Synonyms: boatswain, deck officer, first mate (sometimes) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps: landlubber, passenger) | noun
