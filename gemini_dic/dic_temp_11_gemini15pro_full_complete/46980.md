darwinism:/ˈdɑːrwɪnɪzəm/| n.|达尔文主义，进化论|The theory of the evolution of species by natural selection advanced by Charles Darwin.|例句：Darwinism revolutionized the study of biology. 达尔文主义彻底改变了生物学的研究。

例句：Social Darwinism is a misapplication of Darwin's theories. 社会达尔文主义是对达尔文理论的误用。

近义词：evolution, natural selection|反义词:creationism|词性:noun
