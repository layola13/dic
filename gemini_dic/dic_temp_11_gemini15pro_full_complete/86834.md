hematotherma:/ˌhiːmətoʊˈθɜːrmə/ | n. | 变温动物，冷血动物 | An animal whose body temperature varies with the temperature of its surroundings; a poikilotherm.  A cold-blooded animal.|  Example: Reptiles, like snakes and lizards, are hematotherms.  爬行动物，如蛇和蜥蜴，是变温动物。| Example:  The hematotherma basked in the sun to raise its body temperature.  变温动物晒太阳以提高体温。| Synonym: poikilotherm, ectotherm | Antonym: homeotherm, endotherm | noun
