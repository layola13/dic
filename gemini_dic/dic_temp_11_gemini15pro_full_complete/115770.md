marine:/məˈriːn/ /mərˈiːn/| adj. n.| 海的；海产的；航海的；海军陆战队的；海运业 | relating to the sea; found in or produced by the sea; of or relating to marine shipping | The marine environment is being polluted by plastic waste. 海洋环境正被塑料垃圾污染。 | 近义词：nautical, maritime, oceanic | 反义词：terrestrial, land | 词性：形容词

marine:/məˈriːn/ /mərˈiːn/| n.| 海军陆战队队员；海运业 | a member of a body of troops trained to serve on land or at sea; shipping |  He is a marine in the United States armed forces. 他是美国海军陆战队队员。 | 近义词：soldier, sailor | 反义词：civilian | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *marin* (Modern French *marin*), from Latin *marinus* "of the sea," from *mare* "sea."
