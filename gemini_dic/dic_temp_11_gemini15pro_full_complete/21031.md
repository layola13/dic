blight:/blaɪt/ | n. vt. |枯萎病；荒芜；衰败；使枯萎；破坏；摧残|A disease that causes plants to wither and die.  A condition of deterioration or ruin.| The blight killed all the tomatoes in the garden. (枯萎病杀死了花园里所有的西红柿). He blighted his career with a foolish mistake. (他因为一个愚蠢的错误毁掉了自己的事业). |近义词: disease, decay, ruin, spoil, damage |反义词: growth, health, prosperity |词性: 名词, 动词
词源: From Middle English *bliȝten*, from Old English *blīȝan* (“to shine, rejoice, gladden”).  The original sense was “to pale or bleach,” then “to blast, wither,” and finally “to infect with blight.”
