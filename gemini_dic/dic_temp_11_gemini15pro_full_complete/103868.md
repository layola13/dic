jubate:/ˈdʒuːbeɪt/| adj.|有鬃毛的，有流苏的|Having a mane or a crest of hair or feathers.|The jubate lizard puffed out its frill. 有鬃毛的蜥蜴鼓起了它的褶边。|近义词：crested, maned|反义词:bald, smooth|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *jubatus*, past participle of *jubare* "to have a mane," from *juba* "mane, crest."
