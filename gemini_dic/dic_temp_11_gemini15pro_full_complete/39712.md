consigner:/kənˈsaɪnər/ | n. |发货人；委托人；寄售人|A person or company that consigns goods to another.  Someone who delivers something to another party for sale. |例句：The consigner shipped the goods by rail.  发货人通过铁路运送了货物。

例句：The consigner agreed to a 50/50 split of the profits with the auction house. 委托人同意与拍卖行五五分成利润。|近义词：shipper, sender, consignor |反义词：consignee |词性: noun
