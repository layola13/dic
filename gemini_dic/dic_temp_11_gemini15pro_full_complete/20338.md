bismar:/ˈbɪzmɑːr/ | n. |俾斯麦，脾斯麦（一种棕色染料）| A brown dye derived from Bismarck brown, used especially for staining leather and wood. |  He used bismar to stain the leather of his saddle. 他用俾斯麦染料给马鞍皮革上色。| Near synonyms: dye, stain, pigment | Antonyms: bleach | noun

bismar:/ˈbɪzmɑːr/ | n. |俾斯麦（人名）|  Referring to Otto von Bismarck, the first Chancellor of the German Empire.  | The Bismarck Tower commemorates the Iron Chancellor. 俾斯麦塔是为了纪念铁血宰相而建的。| Near synonyms:  Otto Eduard Leopold, Fürst von Bismarck, Duke of Lauenburg | Antonyms: N/A | noun
