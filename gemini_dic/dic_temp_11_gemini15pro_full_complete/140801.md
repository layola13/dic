on-boards:/ˈɔnˌbɔrdz/ /ˈɑːnˌbɔrdz/
verb | 使…上飞机（或火车、船等）; 使…加入| The airline on-boarded the passengers quickly. 航空公司迅速安排乘客登机。|近义词：embark, board |反义词: disembark, deboard
词性: verb

on-boards:/ˈɔnˌbɔrdz/ /ˈɑːnˌbɔrdz/
adjective | 机载的; 车载的; 船载的| The plane has on-board entertainment systems. 飞机上有机载娱乐系统。|近义词：built-in, integrated |反义词: external, off-board
词性: adjective

on-boards:/ˈɔnˌbɔrdz/ /ˈɑːnˌbɔrdz/
