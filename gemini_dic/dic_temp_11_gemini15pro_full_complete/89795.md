hormogonium:/ˌhɔːrməˈɡəʊniəm/ |n.|藻殖段；连锁体|A motile filament of cells formed by some cyanobacteria, which detaches from the parent filament and grows into a new one.|The hormogonia are released into the water column and can establish new colonies. 藻殖段被释放到水体中，可以建立新的菌落。|Synonyms: filament, trichome|Antonyms: N/A|noun
