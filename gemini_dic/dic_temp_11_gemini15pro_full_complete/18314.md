belles-lettres:/ˌbel ˈletrə/ | n. | 纯文学；文学作品 |  Literature regarded as a fine art, especially as having a purely aesthetic function.  Refers to literary works valued for their artistic merit and style rather than their informative or practical value. |  Her interests were centered on belles-lettres and philosophy. 她对纯文学和哲学感兴趣。 | synonyms: literature, belles lettres, literary study, literary works, letters | antonyms: non-fiction, technical writing | noun
