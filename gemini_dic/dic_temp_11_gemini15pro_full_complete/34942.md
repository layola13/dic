clock: /klɑːk/ (US) /klɒk/ (UK)| n. v. |时钟；计时；记录（时间或速度）|A device for measuring and showing time, typically one showing the time of day.  To measure or record the time or speed of something. |例句：The clock on the wall chimed midnight. 墙上的钟敲响了午夜。|近义词：timepiece, chronometer, watch|反义词: None (Clocks don't have a direct opposite. Perhaps 'sundial' could be considered a contextual antonym, as it relies on the sun rather than a mechanism.)|词性: noun, verb
