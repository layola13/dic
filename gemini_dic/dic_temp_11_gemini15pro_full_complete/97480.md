ingeniousness:/ˌɪnˈdʒiːniəsnəs/
n.|独创性；机敏；精巧|The ingeniousness of the design impressed everyone.  这个设计的独创性给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|
近义词:inventiveness, creativity, cleverness, originality, resourcefulness
反义词:stupidity, dullness, banality
词性:名词

ingeniousness:/ˌɪnˈdʒiːniəsnəs/
n.|心灵手巧；足智多谋|He showed remarkable ingeniousness in solving the problem. 他在解决这个问题时表现出了非凡的机智。|
例句:Her ingeniousness allowed her to create a successful business from scratch. 她的足智多谋使她白手起家创办了一家成功的企业。|
近义词:cleverness, brilliance, resourcefulness, inventiveness
反义词:incompetence, ineptitude
词性:名词


词源:
From ingenious + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *ingeniōsus (“gifted by birth, innate, native”), from ingenium (“natural quality, innate talent”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives).
