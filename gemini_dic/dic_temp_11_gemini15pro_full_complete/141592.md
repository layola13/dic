opprobrium:/əˈproʊbriəm/| n.|耻辱，责难，咒骂|harsh criticism or censure. Public disgrace arising from shameful conduct.|His actions brought opprobrium on his family. 他的行为给他的家庭带来了耻辱。|Synonyms: disgrace, shame, dishonor, ignominy, infamy, obloquy|Antonyms: honor, praise, acclaim, commendation|noun
