misdoing:/ˌmɪsˈduːɪŋ/
n.
 wrongdoing, offense, transgression
An act that is illegal or morally wrong.
His misdeeds were eventually discovered. 他的不法行为最终被发现了。
近义词: wrongdoing, offense, transgression, sin, crime
反义词: good deed, virtue, righteousness
词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *misdoing, equivalent to mis- + doing.
