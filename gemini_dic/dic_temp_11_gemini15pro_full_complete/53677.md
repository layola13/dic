disciplinal:/ˌdɪsəˈplɪnəl/
adjective
纪律的；惩戒的
Relating to discipline, either in maintaining order or administering punishment.
Disciplinal measures were taken against the unruly student. 对这个不守规矩的学生采取了纪律措施。
Disciplinal action was necessary to maintain order in the classroom.  为了维持课堂秩序，必须采取纪律处分。
近义词: corrective, punitive, disciplinary
反义词: lenient, permissive
词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin disciplinalis, from disciplina ‘instruction, discipline’ (see discipline).
