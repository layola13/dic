gazed:/ɡeɪzd/| v.|凝视，注视|To look steadily and intently, especially with great curiosity, admiration, or thoughtfulness.|She gazed at the beautiful sunset. 她凝视着美丽的日落。|近义词: stared, peered, contemplated, observed|反义词: glanced, glimpsed|词性:动词
词源: From Middle English gasen, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse gapa (“to gape”).
