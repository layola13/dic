extrude:/ɪkˈstruːd/| vt.|挤压；突出；逐出|To push or force something soft and thick out of a container or through a small opening. |The machine extrudes plastic into various shapes.  这台机器将塑料挤压成各种形状。

The volcano extruded molten lava. 火山喷出了熔岩。|近义词：force out, press out, squeeze out, eject|反义词: intrude, insert|verb

extrude:/ɪkˈstruːd/| n.|挤压出的东西|Something that has been extruded.|The extrude was then cut into smaller pieces. 挤压出的东西随后被切成小块。|近义词: product, output|反义词: N/A|noun
