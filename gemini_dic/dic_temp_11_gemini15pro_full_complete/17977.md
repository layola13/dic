befrill: /bɪˈfrɪl/ | v. |装饰以褶边，给…加上褶边|To decorate with a frill or frills. |She befrilled the cuffs of her blouse. 她给衬衫袖口加了褶边。| The dressmaker befrilled the neckline of the gown.  裁缝给礼服的领口加了褶边。 |近义词：decorate, adorn, ornament, trim |反义词:strip, simplify |词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + frill.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "to make" or "to furnish with". "Frill" comes from the Middle English word "frillen", meaning "to wrinkle or curl".
