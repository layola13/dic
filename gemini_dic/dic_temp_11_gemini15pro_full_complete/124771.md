mses:/ˌɛmɛsˈiːz/ | n. | 均方误差 (Mean Squared Errors) |  A measure of the average squared difference between the estimated values and the actual value.  It's commonly used in statistics and machine learning to evaluate the performance of a model. | The researcher calculated the MSEs to compare the accuracy of the different prediction models. 研究人员计算了均方误差来比较不同预测模型的准确性。 | 近义词: RMSE (Root Mean Squared Error), MAE (Mean Absolute Error) | 反义词:  N/A | noun
