half-obliterated:/ˌhæf əˈblɪtəreɪtɪd/
adjective
半抹掉的；几乎消失的
partially erased or destroyed, almost imperceptible
The inscription on the tombstone was half-obliterated by weathering. 墓碑上的铭文因风化而几乎消失不见了。
The faded photograph showed a half-obliterated image of a young woman.  褪色的照片上是一个几乎看不清的年轻女子影像。
Near Synonyms:faded, indistinct, obscured, worn, eroded
Antonyms: clear, distinct, visible, preserved
词性:形容词
