aliquot: /ˈælɪkwɒt/ (US) /ˈælɪkwɒt/ (UK)| adj. n.|等分的，整除的；等分，整除部分|An aliquot part of a number or quantity is one that will divide it without a remainder.  A factor or divisor of a number that divides exactly into that number.|
例句：
10 is an aliquot part of 30. 10是30的等分部分。

A 10ml aliquot of the solution was used for the experiment.  10毫升的等分溶液被用于实验。

近义词：divisor, factor|反义词: multiple|词性: adjective, noun


aliquot: /ˈælɪkwɒt/ (US) /ˈælɪkwɒt/ (UK)| n.|等分，整除部分|A portion of a larger whole, especially a sample taken for chemical analysis or other treatment.|
例句：
A 50ml aliquot was taken from the sample.  从样品中取出了50毫升的等分试样。

The chemist analyzed several aliquots of the solution.  化学家分析了溶液的几个等分部分。

近义词：portion, sample|反义词: whole|词性: noun
