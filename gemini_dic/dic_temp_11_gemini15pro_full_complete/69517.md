fellable:/ˈfɛləbəl/
adjective
可砍伐的，可击倒的
Capable of being felled (cut down).  A tree that is likely to fall or be blown over is also described as fellable.
The old oak, weakened by disease, was deemed fellable.  那棵被疾病削弱的老橡树被认为可以砍伐了。
Several trees along the roadside were marked as fellable due to the risk they posed to traffic. 路边有几棵树被标记为可砍伐的，因为它们对交通构成威胁。
近义词:cuttable,  loggable
反义词:unfellable
词性:adjective
