downweed:/ˈdaʊnˌwiːd/| n.|绒毛草，棉毛草|A type of plant covered with downy or cottony hairs, often considered a weed.  A plant, typically considered undesirable, that has soft, fine hairs.|Example: The field was overrun with downweed, making it difficult to walk through. 这片田地长满了绒毛草，很难穿过。

Example:  The gardener struggled to remove the persistent downweed from the flowerbeds.  园丁努力清除花坛里顽固的绒毛草。|近义词：weed, fluffweed|反义词:cultivated plant|词性:noun
