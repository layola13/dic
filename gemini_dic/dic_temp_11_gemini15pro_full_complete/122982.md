monamide:/ˈmɒnəmaɪd/ /ˈmɑːnəmaɪd/ | n. |  单酰胺 | An organic compound derived from a carboxylic acid by replacing the hydroxyl group (-OH) with an amino group (-NH2).  A molecule containing one amide group (-CONH2). |  Nylon is a synthetic polyamide, essentially a chain of linked monamides. 尼龙是一种合成聚酰胺，本质上是一条连接的单酰胺链。| synonyms:  acid amide, carboxamide | antonyms:  N/A | noun
