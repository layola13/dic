discradle: /dɪsˈkreɪdəl/
verb
剥夺…的保护；使…失去支持；使…离开舒适的环境
To deprive of nurture, protection, or support; to remove from a comfortable or secure place.
The economic downturn discradled many families, forcing them into poverty. 经济衰退使许多家庭失去保障，迫使他们陷入贫困。
The war discradled countless children, leaving them orphaned and vulnerable. 战争使无数儿童失去保护，使他们成为孤儿，处境危险。

近义词: displace, unsettle, dislodge, uproot
反义词: protect, shelter, support, nurture
词性: verb
