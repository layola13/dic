howdied: /haʊˈdaɪd/ | n./phrase (informal, internet slang) | 如何去世，怎么死的 |  A non-standard and often humorous way of asking how someone (typically a fictional character or historical figure) died.  It originated online and is not considered formal language. | Example: "Howdied Caesar?"  凯撒是怎么死的？ | Synonym: (formal) How did they die?, How did he/she die? | Antonym: N/A | part of speech: pronoun, verb, adverb combination (acting as noun phrase)
