auratic:/ɔːˈrætɪk/ | adj. | 光环的，灵气的，超凡魅力的 | Having a distinctive atmosphere or quality that seems to surround and be generated by a person, thing, or place.  Relating to or possessing an aura. |  例句：The auratic presence of the Dalai Lama filled the room. 达赖喇嘛充满灵气的存在感弥漫了整个房间。

例句：She had an auratic beauty that captivated everyone. 她拥有一种超凡脱俗的美丽，迷住了所有人。 | 近义词：charismatic, magnetic, radiant, luminous | 反义词：dull, ordinary, mundane | 词性: adjective
