nonmenacing: /nɒnˈmɛnɪsɪŋ/ (British) /nɑːnˈmɛnɪsɪŋ/ (American) | adj. | 无威胁的；不构成威胁的 | Not posing a threat; not intimidating. |  The dog's tail wagged in a nonmenacing way. 狗的尾巴以一种没有威胁性的方式摇摆着。|  He gave a nonmenacing smile. 他露出了一个没有威胁性的微笑。| 近义词: harmless, innocuous, inoffensive | 反义词: menacing, threatening, intimidating | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".  This prefix negates the meaning of the word it is attached to.
menacing (adjective): from the verb "to menace," which comes from Old French "menacier" meaning "to threaten."  Ultimately derived from Latin "minaciae" meaning "threats."
