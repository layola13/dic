foreganger:/ˈfɔːrˌɡəʊər/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrˌɡoʊər/ (US)| n. |前辈，先驱，前任|A person or thing that came before another of the same kind.| Isaac Newton's work was influenced by the findings of his forerunners in the field of physics. 牛顿的工作受到了物理学领域前辈们的发现的影响。

 Copernicus is considered a foreganger of Galileo. 哥白尼被认为是伽利略的先驱。|近义词：predecessor, ancestor, precursor|反义词：successor, descendant|词性: 名词
