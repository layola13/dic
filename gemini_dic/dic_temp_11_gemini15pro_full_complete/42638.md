countershaft: /ˈkaʊntərˌʃæft/ (US) /ˈkaʊntəʃɑːft/ (UK)| n. | 副轴；中间轴 | A short shaft that receives motion from a mainshaft and transmits it to a working part, as in a lathe or a vehicle. | The countershaft of the lathe was damaged during operation. 车床的副轴在操作过程中损坏了。| The countershaft helps to reduce the speed of the output shaft. 副轴有助于降低输出轴的转速。| synonyms: jackshaft, layshaft | antonyms: mainshaft | noun
