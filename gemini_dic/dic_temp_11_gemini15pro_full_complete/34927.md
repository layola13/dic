cloak-and-dagger:/ˌkloʊk ən ˈdæɡər/ | adj. | Cloak-and-dagger; 秘密的; 神秘的; 诡秘的 |  Relating to or characteristic of espionage or intrigue; involving or suggestive of mystery and secrecy.  It derives from the stereotypical image of spies or assassins concealing daggers beneath their cloaks. |  The whole affair had a cloak-and-dagger feel to it. 整个事件都有一种神秘诡异的感觉。|  The spy was involved in a cloak-and-dagger operation to steal the enemy's plans.  这名间谍参与了一项秘密行动，旨在窃取敌人的计划。| synonyms: clandestine, secretive, surreptitious, covert, furtive | antonyms: open, overt, public, transparent | adjective
