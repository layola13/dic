bastardizing:/ˈbæstərˌdaɪzɪŋ/
v.
窜改; 歪曲; 降低…的格调; 败坏;
Corrupting or debasing something, typically something artistic or academic, by adding inferior elements or altering it in a way that violates its original nature.
He was accused of bastardizing the language by using slang.  他被指责使用俚语败坏了语言。
synonyms: corrupting, debased, debasing, adulterating, degrading, perverting, distorting
antonyms: purifying, refining, improving, enhancing
verb
