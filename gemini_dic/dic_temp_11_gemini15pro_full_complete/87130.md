herbarist:/ˈɜːrbəlɪst/ | n. | 草药学家；草药医生 | A person who is an expert in the identification and uses of medicinal herbs. |  She learned about herbal remedies from a local herbarist. 她向当地一位草药学家学习草药疗法。 | herbalist, herbal practitioner | None | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *erbariste, from Old French herbariste, from Medieval Latin herbārius (“herbalist”), from Latin herba (“herb”).
