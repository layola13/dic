borrowable:/ˈbɒrəʊəbl/ (US) /ˈbɑːroʊəbl/| adj.|可借的，可借用的|Something that is borrowable can be borrowed.  It is available to be lent out temporarily.|例句：Are your notes borrowable? 你的笔记可以借吗？|例句：The library has many borrowable books. 图书馆有很多可以借阅的书籍。|近义词：loanable, lendable|反义词：unborrowable, non-lendable|词性:形容词

词源: borrow (v.) + -able (suffix).  "Borrow" comes from Old English *borgian, from Proto-Germanic *borgan, meaning "to borrow, lend, save, protect." "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of being."
