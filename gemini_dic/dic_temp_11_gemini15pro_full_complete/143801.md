outorganize:/ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡənaɪz/
verb
胜过组织；在组织方面胜过
To organize more effectively or efficiently than someone else.
Our team outorganized the competition and secured the victory.  我们的团队在组织方面胜过了竞争对手，从而确保了胜利。
The non-profit outorganized the government agency in the relief effort. 这个非营利组织在救援工作中比政府机构组织得更好。
近义词: outmaneuver, outperform, surpass
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed

词性: verb

词源:  A combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) and the verb "organize."
