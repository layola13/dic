maneuvering:/məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ /məˈnʊvərɪŋ/
n.|调遣，机动，策略|The maneuvering of the troops was crucial to the victory. 部队的调遣对胜利至关重要。
v.|调动，操纵， maneuvering (present participle of maneuver)|He skillfully maneuvered the car into the tight parking space. 他熟练地把车开进了狭窄的停车位。
adj.|用于机动的， maneuverable|The new tank is highly maneuvering and can traverse difficult terrain. 新型坦克机动性很强，可以穿越复杂的地形。
词源: From French *manœuvrer*, from *manœuvre* "manual labor," from Latin *manu opera* "with the hand and work."
近义词: manipulating, strategizing, navigating, maneuvering
反义词: inaction, stillness,  passivity
词性: noun, verb (present participle), adjective (less common)
