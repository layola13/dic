authoritarian: /əˌθɔːrɪˈtɛːriən/ /əˌθɑːrɪˈtɛːriən/
authoritarian: adj. n.|专制的，独裁的；专制主义者|Favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority, especially that of the government, at the expense of personal freedom.|The government was becoming increasingly authoritarian.|近义词: dictatorial, totalitarian, despotic, tyrannical, autocratic|反义词: democratic, liberal, libertarian|词性: adjective, noun

authoritarian: /əˌθɔːrɪˈtɛːriən/ /əˌθɑːrɪˈtɛːriən/

ETYMOLOGY->
