overcomers:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌmərz/| n. |克服者，战胜者|Those who overcome obstacles or difficulties are called overcomers.  战胜障碍或困难的人被称为克服者。|例句：The overcomers of the challenge will be rewarded.  克服挑战的人将获得奖励。

例句：These overcomers demonstrated resilience and determination. 这些克服者展现了韧性和决心。


近义词：victors, winners, conquerors, champions|反义词:losers, defeated, vanquished|词性:名词(plural)
