olivite:/ˈɒlɪvaɪt/ /ˈɑːlɪvaɪt/| n.|橄榄石|A mineral, magnesium iron silicate, (Mg,Fe)2SiO4, occurring in a variety of colours (typically olive green to brownish) and common in igneous and metamorphic rocks.  It is also found in some meteorites.|Olivine is a common mineral in Earth's mantle. 橄榄石是地球地幔中常见的矿物。

Olivine crystals can be found in some types of volcanic rock.  在某些类型的火山岩中可以发现橄榄石晶体。|近义词：peridot (when gem quality), chrysolite (an older name)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
