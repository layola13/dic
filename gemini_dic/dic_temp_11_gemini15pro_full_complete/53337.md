disadorn:/ˌdɪsəˈdɔːrn/
verb
剥去…的装饰；使…朴素
to remove the ornaments or decorations from; to make plain
The room was disadorned, stripped of its usual festive decorations.  房间被剥去了装饰，没有了往日的节日气氛。
The artist disadorned the sculpture, preferring a simpler, more austere aesthetic.  艺术家去掉了雕塑的装饰，更喜欢简单、朴素的美感。
近义词:strip, denude, divest
反义词:adorn, decorate, embellish
词性:v.
