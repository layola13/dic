heard:/hɜːrd/ (UK) /hɝːd/ (US)| v. (past tense and past participle of hear)|听见，听说|The past tense and past participle of "hear". It indicates that someone perceived sound or received information aurally in the past.|I heard a strange noise last night. 我昨晚听到一个奇怪的声音。|I heard that you're moving to London. 我听说你要搬到伦敦了。|近义词：listened to, perceived, learned, discovered|反义词:missed, ignored|动词
