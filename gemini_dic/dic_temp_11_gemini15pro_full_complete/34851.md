clinkered:/ˈklɪŋkərd/| adj.|熔渣的；烧结的；结块的|Covered or coated with clinker, a hard, stony residue formed during the burning of coal or other substances.  It can also mean fused together by heat.|The bottom of the furnace was clinkered with molten slag. 炉底结满了熔化的炉渣。|Synonyms: slaggy, fused, vitrified|Antonyms: smooth, clear, unburned|Adjective

clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/| n.|熔渣；煤渣；熟料|A hard, stony residue formed during the burning of coal or other substances. Also refers to bricks made from this material.|The clinker from the fire was difficult to remove. 火炉里的煤渣很难清除。|Synonyms: slag, cinder, residue|Antonyms: ash, dust|Noun
