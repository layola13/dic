freemen's:/ˈfriːmənzk/| n.|自由民的；公民的|A possessive form of freemen, meaning "belonging to or associated with freemen."  It refers to something owned by or relating to people who are not slaves or serfs.|The freemen's hall was the center of civic life. 自由民大厅是公民生活的中心。|近义词：citizens', independent's|反义词:slave's, serf's|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:
From Old English *frēomanna (genitive plural of frēoman "freeman"),  from frēo "free" + mann "man."  The suffix -'s is the possessive marker.
