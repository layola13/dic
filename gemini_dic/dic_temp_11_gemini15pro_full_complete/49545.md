dematerialised:/ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪzd/ /ˌdiːməˈtɪriəlaɪzd/| adj.|非物质化的，无实体的|Converted from physical certificates or documents to electronic form.|Example: Dematerialised shares are held electronically rather than in paper form.  非物质化股票以电子方式持有，而不是纸质形式。

Example: The company has dematerialised all its records.  该公司已将其所有记录非物质化。|近义词：electronic, digital, intangible, virtual|反义词：physical, tangible, material|词性:形容词

dematerialise:/ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪz/ /ˌdiːməˈtɪriəlaɪz/| v.|非物质化，使无实体|To convert from physical certificates or documents to electronic form.|Example:  The company is planning to dematerialise its share certificates. 公司计划将其股票证书非物质化。

Example: Many companies are now dematerialising their records. 许多公司现在正在将其记录非物质化。|近义词：digitize, electronify|反义词：materialize|词性:动词

dematerialisation:/ˌdiːməˌtɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌdiːməˌtɪriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|非物质化|The process of converting from physical certificates or documents to electronic form.|Example: The dematerialisation of share certificates has simplified the trading process. 股票非物质化简化了交易流程。

Example:  The company is undergoing a process of dematerialisation.  该公司正在进行非物质化进程。|近义词：digitization, electronification|反义词：materialization|词性:名词
