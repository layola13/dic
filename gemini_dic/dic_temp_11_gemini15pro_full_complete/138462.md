novelette:/ˌnɒvəˈlet/ | n. |中篇小说；中篇故事|A short novel, typically one that is between 7,500 and 17,500 words in length. |  Example: She wrote a charming novelette about a young woman's journey of self-discovery. 她写了一部关于一个年轻女子自我发现旅程的迷人中篇小说。

Example: The magazine published a novelette by a promising new author. 这本杂志发表了一位有前途的新作家的中篇小说。| 近义词: novella, short novel, long story | 反义词: novel (though not a true opposite, it indicates a difference in length) | 词性: noun
