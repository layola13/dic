louse:/laʊs/ (plural lice /laɪs/) | n. |虱子；讨厌的家伙|A small, wingless, parasitic insect that lives on the skin of mammals and birds and sucks their blood. | The dog was infested with lice. 这只狗身上长满了虱子。| synonym: parasite, vermin | antonym:  (no direct antonym for a biological organism) | noun

louse (verb -  /laʊz/ -  louses, lousing, loused) | v. |去除虱子； (infml) 弄糟，搞砸 |To rid (someone or something) of lice; (informal) to ruin or spoil something. | He loused up the whole project. 他把整个项目都搞砸了。 | synonym:  infest (with lice), ruin, spoil, mess up | antonym: fix, repair, improve | verb
