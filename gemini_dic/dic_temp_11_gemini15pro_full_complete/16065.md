banditism:/ˈbændɪtɪzəm/
n.
土匪行为；盗匪活动；匪患
The practice of highway robbery; robbery by bandits.
The country was plagued by banditism.  这个国家饱受土匪之苦。
banditry, outlawry, brigandage
lawfulness, order

Etymology:
From bandit + -ism.  Ultimately from Italian bandito.
