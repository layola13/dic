demedicated: /diːˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
已停药的；未服药的
No longer taking medication; not medicated.
The patient was demedicated after showing signs of recovery.  病人出现康复迹象后停药了。
He entered the trial demedicated. 他在未服药的状态下参加了试验。
近义词: drug-free, off medication, unmedicated
反义词: medicated, on medication
词性: adjective

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "removal" or "reversal") + "medicated" (past participle of "medicate," meaning "to treat with medicine").
