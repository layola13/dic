curler:/ˈkɜːrlər/ (US) /ˈkɜːlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|卷发器；卷发的人；（冰壶运动的）运动员|A device used to curl hair, a person who curls hair, or a person who plays the sport of curling.|She uses heated curlers to create bouncy curls. 她用加热的卷发器打造蓬松的卷发。|近义词：roller, curling iron|反义词:straightener|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "curl" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Curl" comes from Middle English *curle*, *crulle*, from Old North French *croller*, of uncertain origin.
