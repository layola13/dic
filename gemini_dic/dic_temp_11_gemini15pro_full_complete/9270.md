antiperspirant: /ˌæntiˈpɜːrspərənt/ | n. |止汗剂，防汗剂 | A substance designed to inhibit or reduce perspiration. |  I apply antiperspirant every morning. 我每天早上都涂止汗剂。

|  This antiperspirant keeps me dry all day.  这款止汗剂让我整天保持干爽。 | 近义词: deodorant (除臭剂，有时也兼具止汗功能) | 反义词:  N/A (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 sweat/perspiration 出汗/汗水) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + perspire (to sweat) + -ant (forming adjectives and nouns from verbs).  Ultimately from Latin anti- (against) + perspirare (to breathe through, exhale, sweat).
