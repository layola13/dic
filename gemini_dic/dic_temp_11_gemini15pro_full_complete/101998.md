isoclinal:/ˌaɪsəˈklaɪnəl/
adjective
等斜的；同斜的
Relating to or denoting a line on a map or chart connecting points of equal inclination, especially of a magnetic or other geophysical quantity.
The isoclinal lines on the map showed areas of equal magnetic dip.  地图上的等斜线显示了磁倾角相同的区域。
Isoclinal folding is common in intensely deformed metamorphic rocks.  等斜褶皱在强烈变形的变质岩中很常见。
近义词:  equally inclined
反义词:  anisocline (rarely used)
词性: adjective
