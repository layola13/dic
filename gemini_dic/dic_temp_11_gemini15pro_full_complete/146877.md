overinform:/ˌōvərɪnˈfɔːrm/ |v. |过度告知；提供过多信息|To provide someone with more information than is necessary or desirable, often causing confusion or anxiety. |例句：The doctor overinformed the patient about the potential side effects of the medication, making her anxious. 医生向病人提供了过多的药物副作用信息，使她感到焦虑。

例句：He tends to overinform his clients, burying the important details in a flood of minutiae. 他倾向于向客户提供过多的信息，把重要的细节淹没在一堆琐碎的事情中。|近义词：overshare, overload |反义词: underinform |词性:动词
