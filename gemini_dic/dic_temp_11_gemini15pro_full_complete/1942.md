actinozooen:/ˌæktɪnəˈzoʊən/
n.
Actinozoan (plural Actinozoa)
腔肠动物; 珊瑚虫纲动物
An animal belonging to the Actinozoa, a class of coelenterates which includes sea anemones and corals.
Actinozoa are marine invertebrates characterized by a radially symmetrical body with a central mouth surrounded by tentacles. These tentacles are armed with stinging cells called nematocysts, which they use for capturing prey and defense.
例句：The coral reefs are built by colonies of actinozooen. 珊瑚礁是由珊瑚虫群体建造的。

例句：Actinozooen, like sea anemones and corals, are vital components of marine ecosystems.  像海葵和珊瑚这样的珊瑚虫是海洋生态系统的重要组成部分。
