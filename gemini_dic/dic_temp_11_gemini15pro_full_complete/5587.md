alterabilities:/ˌɔːltərəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
可变性，可更改性
The quality or state of being alterable; capability of being altered.
The alterabilities of the software make it adaptable to various needs.  该软件的可变性使其能够适应各种需求。
Synonyms: modifiability, changeability, flexibility, plasticity
Antonyms: immutability, unchangeability, inflexibility, rigidity
