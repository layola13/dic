dewclaw:/ˈdjuːklɔː/ (US), /ˈdʒuːklɔː/ (UK)| n. |悬爪，上爪|A claw on the inside of a dog's or cat's leg, higher up than the other claws. It does not usually touch the ground when the animal walks. |例句：The dog's dewclaw was removed to prevent injury. 这只狗的悬爪被移除以防止受伤。

例句：My cat has dewclaws on her front paws. 我的猫前爪上有悬爪。|近义词：vestigial claw, rudimentary claw|反义词: (None - no true opposite exists) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "dew" + "claw".  "Dew" in this context likely refers to the claw's perceived delicacy and its tendency to be slightly off the ground, as if it only touched the dew.
