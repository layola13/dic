nontemperable:/ˌnɒnˈtɛmpərəbl/
adjective
不可回火的，不能回火的
Not capable of being tempered (toughened or strengthened by heating and then cooling).
This type of steel is nontemperable and therefore unsuitable for applications requiring high strength. 这种钢不可回火，因此不适用于需要高强度的应用。
The alloy proved to be nontemperable, frustrating our attempts to improve its durability.  这种合金被证明是不可回火的，使我们提高其耐久性的尝试受挫。
近义词:unhardenable, non-hardenable
反义词:temperable, hardenable
词性:adjective
