fuselages:/ˈfjuːsəlɑːʒɪz/ /ˈfjuːsəlɑːdʒɪz/
noun
(飞机的)机身
The main body of an aircraft.
The fuselage of the aircraft was severely damaged in the crash.  飞机的机身在坠机事故中严重受损。

Synonym: body, frame, hull
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a physical component like fuselage.  One might consider "wings" or "tail" as contrasting parts, but not true antonyms.)
词性: noun (名词)
