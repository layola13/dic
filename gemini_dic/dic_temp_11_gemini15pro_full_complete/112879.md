luculent:/ˈluːsjələnt/ | adj. |清晰的，明白易懂的；流畅的；优美的；明亮的 |  Characterized by clarity of expression; easily understood; also, clear and bright; luminous.  Derived from Latin "luculentus," meaning "full of light," from "lux" (light). | Her luculent prose made even complex topics accessible to all readers. 她的清晰的散文使所有读者都能理解 even 复杂的主题。| The luculent moon bathed the landscape in silver light.  明亮的月亮给大地披上了一层银色的光辉。 |  Synonyms: clear, lucid, perspicuous, articulate, eloquent, luminous, bright  | Antonyms: obscure, opaque, ambiguous, inarticulate, murky, dim | adjective
