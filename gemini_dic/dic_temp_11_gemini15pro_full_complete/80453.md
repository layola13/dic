gonging: /ˈɡɒŋɪŋ/ | n. | 敲锣声；锣声 | The gonging announced the start of the ceremony. 锣声宣告仪式开始。 |  The constant gonging was starting to give me a headache.  持续不断的锣声开始让我头疼。 | 近义词: clanging, ringing, chiming | 反义词: silence | 词性: 名词

词源:  Derived from the verb "gong" (to strike a gong), which itself comes from the Malay word "gong".
