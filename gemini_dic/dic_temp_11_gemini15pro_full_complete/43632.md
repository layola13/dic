creationary:/kriˈeɪʃəˌnɛri/
adjective|创造的，与创造有关的|Relating to creation; having the power to create.|例句：Creationary power is often attributed to deities.  创造力通常被认为是神灵的属性。

例句：The creationary aspects of the universe are still being explored by scientists.  宇宙的创造性方面仍在被科学家们探索。|近义词：creative, generative, inventive|反义词：destructive, ruinous|词性:adjective

Etymology: From the Late Latin *creationarius*, derived from Latin *creatio* ("creation") + *-arius* (suffix forming adjectives relating to a thing or place). The word entered English in the 17th century.
