immaturely: /ˌɪməˈtʃʊrli/ (US), /ˌɪməˈtjʊəli/ (UK) | adv. | 不成熟地，幼稚地 | In a way that shows a lack of experience or development; not fully formed or developed. | He behaved immaturely, throwing a tantrum when he didn't get his way.  他表现得很不成熟，没达到目的就大发脾气。 | 近义词: childishly, foolishly, prematurely | 反义词: maturely, responsibly, wisely | 词性: 副词

immature: /ˌɪməˈtʃʊr/ (US), /ˌɪməˈtjʊə(r)/ (UK)| adj. | 不成熟的，幼稚的 | Not fully developed; behaving in a way that is not appropriate for someone of their age. |  He's a bit immature for his age.  就他的年龄来说，他有点不成熟。| 近义词: childish, juvenile, undeveloped | 反义词: mature, grown-up, developed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *immaturus, from in- ‘not’ + maturus ‘ripe’.
