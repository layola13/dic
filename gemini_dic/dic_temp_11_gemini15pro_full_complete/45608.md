currier:/ˈkʌriər/| n.|皮革工，鞣革匠|A person who curries leather, that is, dresses and prepares animal hides for use in making leather goods.|The currier skillfully worked the hide, transforming it into supple leather.|近义词：tanner, leatherworker|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology: From Old French *correor, from correier "to curry, prepare leather," from Late Latin conredare, from Latin com- "together" + reda "chariot, carriage." Originally referring to preparing leather for harnesses and other parts of horse-drawn vehicles.
