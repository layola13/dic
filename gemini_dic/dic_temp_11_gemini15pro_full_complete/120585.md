mirr:/mɪr/ | n. | 镜子; 反映; 典型; 榜样 | A reflecting surface, typically of glass coated with a metal amalgam, that reflects a clear image.  Something regarded as accurately representing something else. | She looked at her reflection in the mirr. 她照了照镜子里的自己。|  The lake was a perfect mirr of the sky. 湖面完美地映照着天空。 | synonyms: looking-glass, reflector, speculum, example, model, epitome | antonyms: none | noun

mirr:/ˈmɪrər/ | v. | 反映; 映照; 镜像 | To reflect, as in a mirr; to correspond to. |  His mood mirred the gloomy weather. 他的心情反映了阴沉的天气。 | The server mirrs the website's content. 服务器镜像了网站的内容。 | synonyms: reflect, echo, replicate, duplicate | antonyms: distort, misrepresent | verb
