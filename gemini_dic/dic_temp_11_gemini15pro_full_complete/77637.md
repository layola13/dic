gelatinization:/ˌdʒɛlətɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 胶凝化，糊化 | The process of forming a gel, typically by cooling a hot, colloidal solution, such as gelatin in water.  This transformation involves the formation of a three-dimensional network within the liquid, resulting in a semi-solid state. | The gelatinization of the starch mixture created a thick pudding. 淀粉混合物的糊化形成了浓稠的布丁。 | Near synonyms: gelling, solidification, thickening | Antonym: liquefaction | noun


gelation:/dʒɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 胶凝(作用) | The process of a liquid transforming into a gel.  This can occur through various mechanisms, including cooling, chemical reactions, or the addition of gelling agents. | The gelation of the agar solution happened quickly after it cooled.  琼脂溶液冷却后迅速凝胶化。 | Near synonyms: gelatinization, gelling, setting | Antonym: liquefaction | noun
