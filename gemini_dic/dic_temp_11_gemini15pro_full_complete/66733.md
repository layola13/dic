exorcisements:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzmənts/
n.
驱魔;驱邪;去除(坏念头等)
The act or process of exorcising; expulsion of evil spirits from a person or place.
The priest performed exorcisements on the possessed man.  神父对被附身的人进行了驱魔。
The exorcisements seemed to have worked, and the house felt peaceful again.  驱魔似乎奏效了，房子又恢复了平静。
近义词:expulsions, purgations, castings out
反义词:blessings, consecrations
词性:名词

exorcisement:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzmənt/
词源: From Late Latin exorcismus, from Greek exorkismos, from exorkizein "to adjure (an evil spirit) to depart," from ex- "out" + horkizein "to administer an oath, to bind by oath," from horkos "oath."
