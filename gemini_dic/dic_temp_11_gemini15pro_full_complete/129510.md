non-aspirated:/ˌnɒnˈæs.pɪ.reɪ.tɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈæs.pə.reɪ.tɪd/| adj.|非送气的|Not aspirated; pronounced without a puff of air.  This term is used in phonetics to describe sounds, particularly plosives (like p, t, k), that are produced without a noticeable release of air.|The /p/ in "spin" is non-aspirated, while the /p/ in "pin" is aspirated.|近义词:unaspirated|反义词:aspirated|词性:形容词
