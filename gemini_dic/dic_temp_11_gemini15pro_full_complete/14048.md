ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *austerne, from Old French austere, from Latin austērus (“harsh, sour, rough, severe, stern, grave, strict, frugal”), from Ancient Greek αὐστηρός (austērós, “harsh to the taste, astringent; rough to the touch; harsh, severe, stern, rigid”).
