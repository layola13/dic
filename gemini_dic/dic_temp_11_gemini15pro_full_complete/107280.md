lallation:/læˈleɪʃən/ /ləˈleɪʃən/| n. |牙牙学语；不清楚的发音；（尤指）将“l”发成“r”的语音缺陷|The child's lallation was charming. 这孩子的牙牙学语很可爱。|Example:  The toddler's lallation consisted mostly of repeated syllables.  这个蹒跚学步的孩子牙牙学语主要由重复的音节组成。

Example:  His lallation made it difficult to understand him.  他口齿不清，让人难以理解。|Synonyms:  babbling, prattling, lisp (specifically for 'l' to 'r' substitution)|Antonyms:  articulate speech, clear pronunciation|Noun
