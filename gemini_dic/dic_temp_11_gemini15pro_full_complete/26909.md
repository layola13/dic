calamary:/ˈkæləməri/ (US), /kəˈlæməri/ (UK)| n. |乌贼；鱿鱼|A cephalopod mollusc with eight arms and two long tentacles, typically eaten as food.|例句：The calamari was fried to perfection.  鱿鱼炸得恰到好处。

例句：We ordered calamari for an appetizer. 我们点了一份鱿鱼作为开胃菜。|近义词：squid, cuttlefish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *calamarium "ink horn, pen case," from Latin calamus "reed, pen" (see calamus).  The association is with the ink sac of the squid.
