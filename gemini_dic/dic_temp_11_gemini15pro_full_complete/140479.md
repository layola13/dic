old-time:/ˈoʊldˌtaɪm/ | adj. | 旧时的，过去的；老式的 | Characteristic of earlier times, especially in a way regarded as quaintly attractive. |  He was an old-time movie star. 他是一位老时代的电影明星。 | 近义词: old-fashioned, traditional, vintage, classic, nostalgic | 反义词: modern, contemporary, new, current | 词性: 形容词

old-time:/ˈoʊldˌtaɪm/ | n. |  过去的好时光 | A period in the past, especially one regarded as better or more interesting than the present.  |  Those were the old-times, when life was simpler. 那是过去的好时光，生活更简单。| 近义词: past, olden days, good old days | 反义词: present, future | 词性: 名词


词源:  Compound of 'old' and 'time'.  'Old' comes from Old English 'ald', ultimately from Proto-Germanic *alþaz. 'Time' comes from Old English 'tīma', from Proto-Germanic *tīmaz.
