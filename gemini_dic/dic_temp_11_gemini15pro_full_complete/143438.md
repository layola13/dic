outflow:/ˈaʊtfloʊ/
n.
流出；外流；流出物
The movement of a large amount of money, liquid, or people out of a place.
Example: There has been a significant outflow of capital from the country.  这个国家出现了严重的资本外流。

Example: The dam controls the outflow of water from the reservoir.  大坝控制着水库的出水量。

近义词: efflux, discharge, exit, exodus
反义词: inflow, influx

词性: noun

词源: From out + flow
