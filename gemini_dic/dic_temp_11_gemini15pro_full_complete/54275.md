disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/ | n. vt. |伪装，掩饰；假扮，乔装|The act of concealing the identity of something by modifying its appearance, or the altered appearance itself.  |She wore a disguise to avoid being recognized. 她乔装打扮以免被认出来。|近义词：camouflage, mask, conceal, cloak |反义词:reveal, expose| noun/verb

disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/ | vt. |伪装，掩饰；假扮，乔装|To give a different appearance to something, especially in order to conceal its identity. |He disguised his voice on the phone. 他在电话里伪装了自己的声音。|近义词：camouflage, mask, conceal, cloak |反义词:reveal, expose| verb
