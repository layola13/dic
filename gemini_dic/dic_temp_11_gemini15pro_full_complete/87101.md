heraldic:/həˈrældɪk/ | adj. | 纹章的；纹章学的；传令官的 | Relating to heraldry; the practice of devising, granting, and blazoning arms; armorial bearings, especially as a branch of genealogy. |  The heraldic symbols on the shield denoted the family's lineage. 盾牌上的纹章符号表明了这个家族的血统。 | Synonyms: armorial, genealogical | Antonyms: plain, undecorated | adjective


heraldic:/həˈrældɪk/ | n. | 纹章学；传令官 |  The study or art of heraldry. | He is an expert in heraldic traditions. 他是纹章学传统的专家。 | Synonyms: heraldry | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, potentially "commonality" in a figurative sense) | noun
