enceinte:/ɒ̃ˈsæ̃t/ /ˌɑːnˈsænt/ | adj. |怀孕的；围住的；城墙环绕的|Pregnant (used of women); surrounded by walls or other fortifications.|例句：The queen is enceinte. 女王怀孕了。|例句：The enceinte city was well protected. 这座有城墙环绕的城市得到了很好的保护。|近义词：pregnant, expecting (for pregnant); fortified, walled (for surrounded by walls)|反义词：non-pregnant; undefended|词性:形容词

词源: From French enceinte, from Latin incinctus, past participle of incingere "to gird, surround," from in- "in, on" + cingere "to gird."
