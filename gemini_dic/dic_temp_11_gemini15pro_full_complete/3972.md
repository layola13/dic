agrotourists:/ˈæɡroʊˌtʊərɪsts/ | n. | 农业旅游者 | Tourists who visit agricultural areas and participate in activities related to farming and rural life. |  Example: The agrotourists enjoyed picking strawberries at the local farm. 农业旅游者喜欢在当地农场采摘草莓。

Example: The rise of agrotourism has benefited many rural communities. 农业旅游的兴起使许多农村社区受益。 |  Synonyms: farm tourists, rural tourists | Antonyms: urban tourists | Noun
