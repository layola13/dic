bombardman:/bɒmˈbɑːdmən/ | n. | 投弹手，炮手；轰炸员 | A person who operates a bomber or artillery. | The bombardier sighted the target through the crosshairs. 投弹手通过十字准线瞄准目标。|  The young bombarman was nervous on his first mission.  年轻的轰炸员第一次执行任务时很紧张。 | synonyms: bomber, gunner, artilleryman | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps civilian, pacifist) | noun


bombard:/bɒmˈbɑːd/ | v. | 轰炸，炮击；大量提问；向…连续提出问题 | To attack with bombs or artillery; to assail vigorously with questions or criticisms. |  The enemy bombarded the city relentlessly. 敌人无情地轰炸了这座城市。| They bombarded the speaker with questions after his presentation. 演讲结束后，他们向演讲者提出了一连串问题。| synonyms: shell, barrage, pepper, assail | antonyms: defend, protect, shield | verb
