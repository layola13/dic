curule:/ˈkjʊrjuːl/ /ˈkɜːr(j)uːl/| adj.|  库鲁尔的；古罗马高级官员的 | Of or relating to a curule chair, an official chair used in ancient Rome by magistrates of high rank. | The curule aedile oversaw public games and festivals.  古罗马市政官负责监督公共游戏和节日。|  The consul sat in his curule chair.  执事坐在他的库鲁尔椅上。 | 近义词: official, magisterial | 反义词: unofficial, plebeian | 词性: adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *cūrilis*, from *currus* (“chariot, car”).
