interknit:/ˌɪntərˈnɪt/
verb
交织，紧密结合
to knit or weave together; to connect closely
例句：The various plot lines of the novel are skillfully interknitted. 这部小说的各种情节线巧妙地交织在一起。

例句：The lives of the two families were closely interknitted.  这两个家庭的生活紧密地结合在一起。
近义词：interweave, intertwine, interlace, connect, link
反义词：separate, disentangle, disconnect
词性：v.

Etymology:
From inter- + knit.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "knit" refers to the process of looping yarn together to create fabric. Thus, "interknit" literally means "knitted together."
