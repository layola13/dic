bituminous:/bɪˈtʃuːmɪnəs/ /bɪˈtuːmɪnəs/
adjective
沥青的；含沥青的
Containing or resembling bitumen.
The road was paved with bituminous asphalt. 这条路是用沥青铺成的。
The bituminous coal burned with a smoky flame. 烟煤燃烧时冒着浓烟。
synonyms: asphaltic, tarry, pitchy
antonyms: non-bituminous, anthracite (referring to coal)
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *bituminōsus, from Latin bitūmen (“bitumen”) + -ōsus.
