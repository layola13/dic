expertly:/ˈɛkspərtli/| adv.|熟练地，内行地|In a skillful and knowledgeable manner, demonstrating expertise.|She expertly navigated the complex legal system. 她熟练地驾驭了复杂的法律体系。|例句：He expertly repaired the antique clock. 他熟练地修理了那台古董钟。|近义词：skillfully, proficiently, adeptly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, amateurishly|词性:副词

词源: From "expert" + "-ly." "Expert" comes from the Old French "expert," past participle of "experir," meaning "to try, test," from the Latin "experiri" ("to try").
