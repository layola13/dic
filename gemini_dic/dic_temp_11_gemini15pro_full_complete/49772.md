demoniacal:/ˌdiːməˈnaɪək(ə)l/| adj.|恶魔的；残忍的；疯狂的|Of or like a demon; very cruel or wicked; frenzied.|例句：He had a demoniacal gleam in his eyes. 他的眼神里闪烁着恶魔般的光芒。|例句：The killer's demoniacal laughter echoed through the house. 凶手的恶魔般的笑声回荡在房子里。|近义词：fiendish, satanic, hellish, devilish|反义词：angelic, divine, saintly|词性:adjective
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin demoniacus, from Greek daimōniakos, from daimōn ‘demon’.
