antisyphilitic:/ˌæntiˌsɪfɪˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
抗梅毒的
Used to describe something that prevents or treats syphilis.
Penicillin is an effective antisyphilitic drug. 青霉素是一种有效的抗梅毒药物。
The doctor prescribed an antisyphilitic medication. 医生开了抗梅毒的药物。
Synonyms: antiluetic
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider "pro-syphilitic" as a theoretical, though rarely used, antonym meaning something that promotes or exacerbates syphilis.  This term is highly discouraged and should not be used in any clinical or professional context.)
