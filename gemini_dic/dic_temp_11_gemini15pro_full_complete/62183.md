encomion:/ɛnˈkoʊmiən/ |n.|颂词，赞美词，赞扬|A formal expression of praise; a eulogy. |He delivered a moving encomion at his mentor's funeral. 他在导师的葬礼上发表了感人的颂词。|
The book is filled with encomions to nature. 这本书充满了对大自然的赞美。|Synonym: eulogy, panegyric, tribute, accolade |Antonym: criticism, condemnation, censure|noun
