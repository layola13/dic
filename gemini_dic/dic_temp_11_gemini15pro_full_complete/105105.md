kerosene:/ˈkerəsiːn/ (US), /ˈkerəsiːn/ (UK)| n.|煤油|A thin, clear liquid fuel distilled from petroleum or shale oil, used in jet engines and domestic heaters and lamps.|The old lamp used kerosene for fuel. 这盏旧灯用煤油做燃料。|Synonyms: paraffin (UK), lamp oil|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but alternatives include electricity, natural gas, propane)|noun
