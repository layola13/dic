nonconscientiously: /ˌnɑːnkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsli/ | adv. | 不认真地，不尽责地 |  In a manner that lacks conscientiousness; without due care or attention to detail.  It describes actions performed without a strong sense of duty or thoroughness. | He completed the task nonconscientiously, leading to several errors.  他不认真地完成了任务，导致了几个错误。| Synonyms: carelessly, negligently, irresponsibly, perfunctorily | Antonyms: conscientiously, diligently, meticulously, thoroughly | adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," "opposite of," from Latin non "not."
conscientious (adjective): From Latin conscientia "knowledge within oneself, consciousness," from consciens, present participle of conscire "to be conscious of," from com- "with," intensive prefix + scire "to know."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
