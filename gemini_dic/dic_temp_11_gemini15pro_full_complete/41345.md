corinne:/kəˈrɪn/| n. |科琳（女子名）|A female given name, likely derived from the Greek "kore," meaning maiden.| Corinne is a beautiful name. 科琳是一个漂亮的名字。|Corinne won the spelling bee. 科琳赢得了拼字比赛。|近义词：girl's name, female name|反义词:boy's name, male name|词性:noun

Etymology:
From the Greek κόρη (korē), meaning "maiden" or "girl."  It gained popularity as a given name in the 19th century, possibly influenced by the novel "Corinne, or Italy" by Madame de Staël.
