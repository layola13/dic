convoy:/ˈkɒnvɔɪ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnvɔɪ/ (US)| n. v. |护航，车队；护送|A group of vehicles, typically motor vehicles or ships, traveling together for mutual support and protection.  |The army convoy made its way slowly across the desert. 陆军车队缓慢地穿过沙漠。|近义词：escort, guard, fleet, caravan|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "disperse" for the verb form) |noun, verb


convoy:/ˈkɒnvɔɪ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnvɔɪ/ (US)| v. |护送|To accompany (a group of vehicles or ships) for protection.|Warships were sent to convoy merchant vessels.  军舰被派去护航商船。|近义词：escort, accompany, protect|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
