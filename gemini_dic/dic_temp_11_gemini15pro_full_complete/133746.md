nonfuturistic:/ˌnɒnˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非未来主义的；非未来派的 | Not futuristic; relating to the present or past rather than the future. |  The building's design was surprisingly nonfuturistic, resembling a classic Roman temple.  这栋建筑的设计令人惊讶地非未来主义，类似于一座经典的罗马神庙。 | The movie's setting was decidedly nonfuturistic, taking place in a small rural town in the 1950s.  这部电影的背景设定明显是非未来主义的，发生在20世纪50年代的一个乡村小镇。| 近义词: traditional, conventional, contemporary, current | 反义词: futuristic, advanced, modern | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- + futuristic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "futuristic" refers to the future.
