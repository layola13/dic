lure:/lʊr/ (US), /ljʊər/ (UK)| n. vt.|诱惑；诱饵；诱惑物；引诱|Something that tempts or is used to tempt a person or animal to do something.|The fisherman used a brightly colored lure to attract fish. 渔夫用颜色鲜艳的诱饵来吸引鱼。|
近义词:temptation, bait, enticement, attract, seduce|反义词:deterrent, repellent, repulsion|词性:noun, verb


lure:/lʊr/ (US), /ljʊər/ (UK)| vt.|引诱；诱惑|To tempt someone to do something or to go somewhere, especially by offering some form of reward.|The company lured him away from his old job with a higher salary. 这家公司用更高的薪水把他从原来的工作岗位上挖走了。|近义词:entice, tempt, attract, seduce|反义词:deter, repel, dissuade|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *luerre "decoy, bait," of uncertain origin.
