boracite:/ˈbɔːrəsaɪt/ | n. |硼镁石|A white, gray, yellow, or green mineral consisting of a magnesium borate and chloride.  It is used as a source of boric acid. |Boracite is a relatively rare mineral found in evaporite deposits.|近义词:stassfurtite (a massive variety)|反义词:  None (as it's a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|noun
