alegge:/əˈlɛdʒeɪ/
adjective
所谓的；声称的
Claimed to be true, but not necessarily so.
The alleged thief was seen near the bank.  所谓的窃贼在银行附近被看到。
He denied the alleged offense. 他否认了所谓的罪行。
近义词:supposed, purported, claimed, asserted
反义词:proven, confirmed, verified
词性:adjective
