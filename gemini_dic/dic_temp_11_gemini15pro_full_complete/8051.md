anthropolatry:/ˌænθrəˈpɒlətri/
n.
崇拜人，把人当作神崇拜
The worship of a human being as a god.
Some critics accused the pop star's fans of anthropolatry. 一些评论家指责这位流行歌星的粉丝把他当神一样崇拜。
Anthropolatry is a form of idolatry. 崇拜人是偶像崇拜的一种形式。
近义词:deification, apotheosis
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) +  -λατρεία (-latreía, “worship”).
