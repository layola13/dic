lazinesses:/ˈleɪzinɪsɪz/
n.
懒惰，懈怠
The state of being lazy; inactivity resulting from a dislike of work.
His lazinesses resulted in him failing the class. 他的懒惰导致他考试不及格。
laziness, indolence, idleness, sloth
industriousness, diligence, activity
noun
