abusable:/əˈbjuːzəbl/
adjective
可滥用的；可被虐待的
Open to or susceptible to abuse; capable of being misused or mistreated.
That system is abusable if users aren't careful.  该系统如果用户不小心，很容易被滥用。
The readily abusable nature of the internet. 互联网易于被滥用的性质。
近义词: exploitable, mistreatable, misusable
反义词: protected, safeguarded
