counterpoint:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɔɪnt/| n.|对位；对照物；反驳|A contrasting but complementary element, idea, or argument.|The lawyer skillfully presented counterpoints to the prosecutor's accusations. 律师巧妙地提出了反驳控方指控的论点。|Synonyms: antithesis, contrast, rebuttal, rejoinder|Antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord, corroboration|noun

counterpoint:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɔɪnt/| v.|构成对位；形成对照|To set in contrast or opposition; to provide a counterpoint to.|The sweetness of the melody is counterpointed by the bitterness of the lyrics.  旋律的甜美与歌词的苦涩形成了对比。|Synonyms: contrast, juxtapose, offset|Antonyms: harmonize, blend, unify|verb
