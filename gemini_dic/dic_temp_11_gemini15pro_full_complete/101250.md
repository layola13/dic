invocatory:/ɪnˈvɒkətəri/ | adj. | 祈求的，祈愿的 | Relating to or containing invocation; making or expressing a plea or prayer. |  The priest began the ceremony with an invocatory prayer.  神父以祷告开始了仪式。 | Synonyms: supplicatory, imploring, beseeching, appealing | Antonyms: demanding, commanding, dictatorial | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *invocatorius, from Latin invocatus, past participle of invocare "to call upon, invoke," from in- "in, on" + vocare "to call."
