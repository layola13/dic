blitz:/blɪts/| n. v.|闪电战，突击，快速行动|A sudden, intensive military attack; a concentrated effort to deal with something quickly and decisively.|The advertising blitz for the new product was very successful.  这个新产品的广告闪电战非常成功。|Synonyms: assault, attack, onslaught, bombardment|Antonyms: defense, retreat|noun

blitz:/blɪts/| v.|以闪电战攻击，突击，快速完成|To attack suddenly and intensively; to do something quickly and intensively.|We blitzed through the housework so we could go out early. 我们快速地完成了家务，以便早点出门。|Synonyms: attack, assault, bombard|Antonyms: defend, protect|verb
