condores:/ˈkɑːndɔrz/
noun
秃鹰（复数）
Large birds of prey found in the Andes mountains.
The condores soared high above the mountain peaks.  秃鹰在山峰上空翱翔。
synonyms:vultures, raptors
antonyms:prey
