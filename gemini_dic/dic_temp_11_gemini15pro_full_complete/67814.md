eyewall:/ˈaɪwɔːl/| n. |眼墙|The eyewall is the ring of thunderstorms surrounding the eye of a tropical cyclone, characterized by the strongest winds and heaviest rainfall.|The plane flew through the eyewall of the hurricane.|近义词: core, center|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps outer bands of a hurricane)|noun
