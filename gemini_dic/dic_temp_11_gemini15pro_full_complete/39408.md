conking:/ˈkɒŋkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 梳理 (特指非裔美国人的头发)；抛锚；停止运转 |The act of styling, especially straightening, tightly curled hair (typically associated with African American men's hairstyles); breaking down; ceasing to function.|  He spent hours conking his hair. 他花了几个小时梳理他的头发。| synonyms: straightening, styling, breaking down, stalling, quitting | antonyms: curling, starting, running, working | noun

conk:/kɒŋk/ (UK) /kɑːŋk/ (US) | v. | (引擎或机器) 抛锚，停止运转；梳理 (头发)；打 (某人的头部) | (of an engine or machine) break down; cease to function; style (hair), especially by straightening it; hit (someone) on the head.| The engine suddenly conked out. 引擎突然熄火了。| synonyms: break down, stall, die, quit, style, hit | antonyms: start, run, work, curl | verb
