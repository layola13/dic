isicle:/ˈaɪsɪkl/ | n. |冰柱，冰垂|A hanging piece of ice formed by the freezing of dripping water.|The icicles hanging from the roof sparkled in the sunlight. 屋顶上悬挂的冰柱在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：ice formation, ice pendant|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *īsģicel (attested only as īs-gicel), from īs "ice" + gicel "icicle". Cognate with German Eiszapfen.
