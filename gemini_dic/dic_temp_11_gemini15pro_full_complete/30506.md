ETYMOLOGY->
From century + -ed.  "Century" comes from the Latin "centuria," meaning a company of one hundred soldiers, later broadened to mean a period of one hundred years.  The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives from nouns, indicating possession or having the characteristics of the noun.
