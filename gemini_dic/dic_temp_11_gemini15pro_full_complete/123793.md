mopboard:/ˈmɒpbɔːd/ (UK), /ˈmɑːpbɔːrd/ (US)| n. |踢脚板；护壁板|A board running along the bottom of a wall, covering the join between the wall and the floor.|The mopboard needs repainting. 踢脚板需要重新粉刷。|Synonyms: baseboard (US), skirting board (UK), skirting|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "exposed wall-floor joint")|noun

mopboard:/ˈmɒpbɔːd/ (UK), /ˈmɑːpbɔːrd/ (US)| n. |护壁板|A board fixed to the bottom of an interior wall to protect it from damage and to cover the joint between the wall and the floor, especially from being scuffed by mops.|We installed new mopboards in the living room. 我们在客厅安装了新的护壁板。|Synonyms: baseboard (US), skirting board (UK), skirting|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
