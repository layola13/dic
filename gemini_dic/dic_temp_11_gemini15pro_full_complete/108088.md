latex: /ˈleɪtɛks/ (US) /ˈleɪtɛks/ (UK) | n. | 乳胶；胶乳；（LaTeX）排版系统 | A milky fluid found in many plants, such as poppies and spurges, which exudes when the plant is cut and coagulates on exposure to the air. Also, a synthetic material resembling this.
