flagstone:/ˈflæɡˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈflæɡˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|石板，铺路石|A flat piece of hard stone used for paving.|The patio was paved with large flagstones. 露台是用大块石板铺成的。|
近义词: paving stone, slab, bluestone |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps gravel or asphalt as alternative paving materials)| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *flaga ("slab of stone") + stone.  Related to flag ("a piece of cloth used as a symbol"). The sense evolved from "a slab of rock" to specifically a paving stone.
