antidynastic:/ˌænti daɪˈnæstɪk/ | adj. | 反王朝的，反对王朝统治的 | Opposed to the rule of a dynasty or hereditary succession of rulers. |  The antidynastic movement gained momentum after the king's controversial decision.  反王朝运动在国王做出有争议的决定后获得了动力。 | Example: The antidynastic sentiment in the country led to the establishment of a republic.  这个国家的反王朝情绪导致了共和国的建立。 | Synonyms: anti-monarchical, republican | Antonyms: dynastic, monarchist | adjective
