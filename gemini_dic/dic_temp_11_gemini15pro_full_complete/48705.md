defibrillator:/diːˈfɪbrɪleɪtər/ | n. | 除颤器 | A device that delivers an electric shock to the heart to restore its normal rhythm. | The paramedics used a defibrillator to revive the heart attack victim.  医护人员使用除颤器使心脏病发作的受害者苏醒过来。 | synonym: AED (Automated External Defibrillator) | antonym: N/A | noun

defibrillators:/diːˈfɪbrɪleɪtərz/ | n. | 除颤器（复数） | Plural form of defibrillator. |  The hospital has several defibrillators available in case of emergencies. 医院备有几台除颤器，以防万一。| synonym: AEDs (Automated External Defibrillators) | antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + fibrillation + -or.  "De-" means "removal or reversal," "fibrillation" refers to the chaotic quivering of the heart muscles, and "-or" denotes an agent or instrument.
