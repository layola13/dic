nonabstractnesses:/ˌnɒnæbˈstræktnɪsɪz/
noun
非抽象性；具体性
The quality or state of being nonabstract; concreteness.
The artist's focus on nonabstractnesses, depicting everyday objects with meticulous detail, resonated with viewers.  这位艺术家对具体性的关注，用细致的细节描绘日常物品，引起了观众的共鸣。
His photography celebrated the nonabstractnesses of the natural world, capturing the textures and forms of rocks, leaves, and water. 他的摄影作品颂扬了自然界的具体性，捕捉了岩石、树叶和水的纹理和形态。
Synonyms: concreteness, tangibility, palpability
Antonyms: abstractness, generality
