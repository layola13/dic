chloritic:/klɔːˈrɪtɪk/ |adj.|绿泥石的，含绿泥石的|Containing or resembling chlorite, a green mineral.|The soil sample was chloritic, indicating the presence of weathered volcanic rock. 土壤样本中含有绿泥石，表明存在风化的火山岩。|Synonyms:chlorite-rich, chlorite-bearing|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
