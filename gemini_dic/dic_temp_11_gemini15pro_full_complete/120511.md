minutemen:/ˈmɪnɪtmɛn/ | n. |民兵| Members of the American militia during and before the Revolutionary War who were ready to fight at a minute's notice. | The minutemen played a crucial role in the battles of Lexington and Concord.  民兵在列克星敦和康科德战役中发挥了至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: militiamen, patriots | antonyms: loyalists, redcoats | noun
