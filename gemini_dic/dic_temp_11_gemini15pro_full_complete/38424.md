concertedness:/kənˈsɜːrtɪdnəs/
n.
共同性，一致性，协调性
The state of being concerted; joint action or effort.
The concertedness of the musicians created a beautiful symphony.  乐手们齐心协力，共同创造了一曲优美的交响乐。
The team's concertedness was key to their victory.  团队的团结一致是他们获胜的关键。
Synonyms:  collaboration, cooperation, coordination, harmony, teamwork, unity
Antonyms:  discord, disunity, disagreement, conflict, opposition
noun
