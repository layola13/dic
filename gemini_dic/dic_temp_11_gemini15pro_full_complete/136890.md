nonsocialistic:/ˌnɒnˌsəʊʃəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非社会主义的
Not adhering to or based on the principles of socialism.
Example:  The country adopted a nonsocialistic economic policy.  这个国家采取了非社会主义的经济政策。

Example:  His views were decidedly nonsocialistic. 他的观点明显是非社会主义的。

Synonym: capitalist, free-market, anti-communist
Antonym: socialist, communist
词性: adjective
