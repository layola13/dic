half-title:/ˈhɑːfˌtaɪtl/ (US), /ˈhɑːfˌtaɪtl/ (UK)
n.
半书名页，副书名页
A page at the beginning of a book, usually preceding the title page, that contains only the title or a shortened version of the title.
The half-title simply read "The Secret Garden". 半书名页上只写着“秘密花园”。

近义词: bastard title
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider the full title page as a contrasting element)
词性: noun
