augite:/ˈɔːdʒaɪt/ | n. | 辉石 |A common rock-forming mineral of the pyroxene group, usually black, greenish-black, or dark green in color, and composed of a complex silicate of calcium, magnesium, iron, and aluminum. | Augite is a common constituent of igneous and metamorphic rocks.  辉石是火成岩和变质岩的常见成分。|  The geologist identified the dark crystal as augite. 地质学家将深色晶体鉴定为辉石。 | Synonyms: pyroxene, diopside | Antonyms: N/A | noun
