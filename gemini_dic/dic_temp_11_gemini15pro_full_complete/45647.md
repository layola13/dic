cursory:/ˈkɜːrsəri/ (US) /ˈkɜːsəri/ (UK)| adj.|粗略的，草率的，仓促的|Performed with haste and superficiality, without attention to detail.  A cursory glance/examination/inspection suggests that everything is in order.|He gave the report a cursory glance and signed it.|superficial, hasty, perfunctory, brief, rapid, quick, hurried, fleeting, desultory|thorough, meticulous, comprehensive, detailed, in-depth|adjective
