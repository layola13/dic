burler:/ˈbɜːrlər/| n.| 粗纱工，粗梳毛机操作工 |A worker who operates a burling machine, which removes knots and imperfections from cloth, especially wool.| The burler meticulously inspected the fabric for flaws.  粗纱工仔细检查织物上的瑕疵。| 近义词: textile worker, mill worker, cloth finisher | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fabric damager" as a humorous opposite) | 词性: noun

词源: From the verb "to burl," which means to remove knots and imperfections from cloth.  The verb likely originates from the noun "bur," referring to a rough or prickly seed case or a knot in cloth.
