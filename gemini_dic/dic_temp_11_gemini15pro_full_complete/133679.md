nonfraternization: /ˌnɒnˌfrætərnɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌnɑːnˌfrætərnɪˈzeɪʃn/
| n. | 不 fraternization; 不亲善;  禁止 fraternization|  The act or policy of discouraging or prohibiting social interaction between different groups, especially within a military or other hierarchical organization.  |  The company has a strict policy of nonfraternization between managers and their subordinates. 公司有严格的经理和下属之间禁止 fraternization 的政策。|Synonyms: non-association, segregation, aloofness | Antonyms: fraternization, association, socializing| Noun
