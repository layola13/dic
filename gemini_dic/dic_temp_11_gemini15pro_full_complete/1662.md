acknowledgment:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒmənt/ |/əkˈnɑːlɪdʒmənt/|
n.|承认；确认；感谢；收据|the act of accepting that something is true or that something exists.  The expression of thanks or gratitude. A formal declaration that something has been received.|
The company issued an acknowledgment of the receipt of the complaint.|公司发出了一份确认收到投诉的声明。|
He received an acknowledgment for his donation to the charity.|他收到了慈善机构对他捐款的感谢信。|
synonyms: acceptance, admission, recognition, confirmation, gratitude, thanks|
antonyms: denial, rejection, repudiation|
noun
