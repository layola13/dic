deceptive:/dɪˈsɛptɪv/
adjective
欺骗性的；误导性的
giving an appearance or impression different from the true one; misleading.
Her performance was deceptive; she wasn't as nervous as she appeared. 她的表演很具有欺骗性；她不像看起来那么紧张。
misleading, deceitful, false, fallacious, spurious
honest, genuine, truthful, sincere, candid
adjective
