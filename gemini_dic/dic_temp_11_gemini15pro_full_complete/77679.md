gelseminic:/ˌdʒɛlsɪˈmɪnɪk/ | adj. | 钩吻属的；钩吻碱的 | Relating to, or derived from, the genus *Gelsemium* (jasmine family), especially the species *Gelsemium sempervirens* (yellow jessamine). | The gelseminic acid found in the plant is highly toxic.  这种植物中发现的钩吻酸毒性很强。| Synonym: gelsemial | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-gelseminic" in a scientific context) | adjective

gelseminic:/ˌdʒɛlsɪˈmɪnɪk/ | n. | 钩吻碱 | A crystalline alkaloid, C20H22N2O2, obtained from plants of the genus *Gelsemium*, notably *Gelsemium sempervirens*; used medicinally as a sedative and antispasmodic. | Gelseminic is a potent neurotoxin. 钩吻碱是一种强效神经毒素。 | Synonym:  gelsemine, gelsemium alkaloid | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
