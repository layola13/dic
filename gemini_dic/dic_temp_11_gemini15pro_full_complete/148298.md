oversoftness:/ˌoʊvərˈsɒftnəs/ | n. | 过度柔软；过度温和 | Excessive softness or mildness. | The oversoftness of the mattress made it difficult to get a good night's sleep. 床垫太软了，很难睡个好觉。|  The oversoftness of his approach to discipline resulted in unruly children. 他过于温和的管教方式导致孩子们不服管教。| 近义词：flabbiness, sponginess, tenderness | 反义词：firmness, hardness, rigidity | 词性: 名词

词源: over- + softness

over-:  prefix meaning "excessive," "too much"  表示“过度”、“太多”

softness: noun form of soft, from Old English "sōfte"  名词形式，来自古英语 "sōfte"
