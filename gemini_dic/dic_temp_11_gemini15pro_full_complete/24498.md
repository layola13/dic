broacher:/ˈbroʊtʃər/| n.|小册子，宣传册|A small booklet or pamphlet containing information about a product, service, or company.|The travel agent gave me a brochure about holiday packages in Hawaii. 旅行社给了我一本关于夏威夷度假套餐的小册子。|leaflet, pamphlet, booklet, flyer|book, novel|noun

broach:/broʊtʃ/| vt.|提出，开启；钻孔；打开|To raise (a sensitive or difficult subject) for discussion.|He finally broached the subject of divorce. 他终于提出了离婚的话题。|introduce, raise, mention, bring up|avoid, ignore, conceal|verb
