antineutral:/ˌæntiˈnjuːtrəl/
adjective
反中性的；非中性的
Opposing or hostile to neutrality, especially in international affairs.
During wartime, many countries found it difficult to remain antineutral, forced to take sides.  在战时，许多国家发现很难保持中立，被迫站队。
The antineutral stance of the newspaper led to accusations of bias. 这家报纸的反中立立场导致了偏见的指责。
近义词: biased, partisan, unneutral
反义词: neutral, impartial, objective
词性: adjective
