arrhizal:/əˈraɪzəl/ | adj. | 非根生的，无根的 | Lacking true roots. Refers to plants that do not obtain nutrients through roots, such as some orchids. | Example: Some orchids are arrhizal, obtaining nutrients from the air and rain.  一些兰花是非根生的，从空气和雨水中获取营养。| Example:  Arrhizal plants often rely on symbiotic relationships with fungi. 非根生植物通常依靠与真菌的共生关系。 | Synonym: rootless | Antonym: rooted | Part of Speech: Adjective
