blankest:/ˈblæŋkɪst/ | adj. | 最空白的；最茫然的 |  superlative adjective of blank; most devoid of expression, understanding, or information. |  Her face was the blankest I'd ever seen. 她的脸是我见过最茫然的。| synonyms: emptiest, vacantest, expressionless | antonyms: most expressive, most intelligent, most informed | adjective


Etymology->

From blank + -est.  "Blank" itself comes from Old French "blanc" meaning white, and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*blankaz." The semantic shift from "white" to "empty" likely occurred through the notion of an unwritten page.
