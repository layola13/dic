colorations:/ˌkʌləˈreɪʃənz/
n.
着色,染色,颜色,色彩
the act or process of coloring something or of becoming colored; the colors or patterns characteristic of a particular animal, plant, etc.
The artist experimented with different colorations of the paint.  这位艺术家尝试了不同的颜料颜色。
The bird's vibrant colorations helped it attract a mate.  这只鸟鲜艳的色彩帮助它吸引配偶。
synonyms: colors, hues, tints, shades, tones, pigmentation
antonyms: achromatism, monochrome
noun
