martlet:/ˈmɑːtlɪt/
noun
小燕子，(纹章上的)无足燕子，马丁
A small, swift-flying bird, typically dark blue or black, similar to a swallow but with shorter wings and tail. In heraldry, a martlet is depicted without feet, symbolizing landlessness or constant motion.
The crest of the university features a martlet. 这所大学的校徽上有一只小燕子。
synonyms: swift, swallow, house martin
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider contrasting birds like ostrich, penguin which are flightless)
