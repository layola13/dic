inchamber:/ɪnˈtʃeɪmbər/| adj.|在密室中的；秘密的；私下的|Held or done in private, especially by judges or other officials.|The judges inchambered to decide the verdict. 法官们在密室里裁决。|近义词：private, secret, confidential|反义词:public, open|词性:形容词
词源:  From 'in' + 'chamber'.  "Chamber" refers to a room, especially a judge's private room.  Therefore, "inchamber" literally means "inside the chamber".
