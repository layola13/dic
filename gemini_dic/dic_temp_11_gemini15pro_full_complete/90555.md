hugger:/ˈhʌɡər/| n.|拥抱者；喜欢拥抱的人|A person who hugs other people frequently and enthusiastically.|She's a real hugger, always greeting people with open arms. 她真是个爱拥抱的人，总是张开双臂迎接别人。|Example: He's not much of a hugger, preferring a handshake or a nod. 他不太喜欢拥抱，更喜欢握手或点头致意。|Synonym: embracer|Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) aloof person, distant person|noun
