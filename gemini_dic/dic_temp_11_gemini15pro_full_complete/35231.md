clump:/klʌmp/
noun
团，块，丛，簇
A compact mass or cluster of something.
The daffodils grew in clumps.  水仙花丛生。
cluster, bunch, mass, lump
scattering, dispersal

clump:/klʌmp/
verb
聚集，丛生
To form or gather into a clump or clumps.
The bushes clumped together at the edge of the forest. 灌木丛聚集在森林边缘。
cluster, gather, bunch
scatter, disperse
