allowed: /əˈlaʊd/ | adj. | 允许的，准许的 | permitted, having permission to do something |  Smoking is not allowed in the restaurant.  餐厅内禁止吸烟。| permitted, authorized | forbidden, prohibited | adjective

allowed: /əˈlaʊd/ | v. | 允许，准许（allow的过去式和过去分词） |  gave permission to do something | He allowed me to use his car. 他允许我用他的车。 | permitted, authorized | forbade, refused | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *allowen*, from Old French *alouer*, from Latin *allaudāre* (“to praise, commend, approve”), from *ad-* (“to, toward”) + *laudāre* (“to praise”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lewdh-* (“to speak well of, praise, honor”).
