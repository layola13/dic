intimacy:/ˈɪntɪməsi/| n.|亲密，密切，亲昵的行为|Close familiarity or friendship; closeness.|例句：The intimacy between the two friends was evident. 这两个朋友之间的亲密关系显而易见。|例句：She shared her deepest fears with him in a moment of intimacy. 在亲密的时刻，她与他分享了她内心最深处的恐惧。|近义词：closeness, familiarity, nearness|反义词：distance, remoteness, formality|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *intimitas, from Latin intimus "inmost, innermost," superlative of *interus/*intus "within"  (from PIE root *en "in").
