如果你是想问 "cat"，以下是解析：

cat:/kæt/
n.|猫|A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, a short snout, and retractile claws. It is widely kept as a pet or for catching mice, and many breeds have been developed.|例句：The cat sat on the mat. 猫坐在垫子上。|例句：My cat likes to chase laser pointers. 我的猫喜欢追逐激光笔。|近义词：feline, kitty|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑dog, predator的天敌prey)|词性:名词
