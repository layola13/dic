impression:/ɪmˈprɛʃən/| n.|印象，感觉，影响，压痕|An idea, feeling, or opinion about something or someone, especially one formed without conscious thought or on the basis of little evidence.  |例句：I got the impression that she wasn't happy. 我感觉她不开心。|例句：The book made a strong impression on me. 这本书给我留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：feeling, sense, notion, idea, belief, opinion, view|反义词：reality, fact|词性：noun

词源: From Old French *empresion, from Late Latin impressio (“a pressing into”), from Latin imprimere (“to press into”), from in- + premere (“to press”).
