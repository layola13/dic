ethicalness:/ˈɛθɪkəlˌnɛs/| n.|道德; 伦理性; 合乎道德|The ethicalness of his decision is questionable. 他的决定的伦理性值得怀疑。|例句：The ethicalness of animal testing is often debated. 动物实验的伦理性经常受到争论。  The committee will review the ethicalness of the proposed research. 委员会将审查拟议研究的伦理性。|近义词：morality, righteousness, virtuousness|反义词：immorality, wickedness, unethicalness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ethical + -ness.  Ethical comes from the Greek word "ethikos," meaning "relating to character."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
