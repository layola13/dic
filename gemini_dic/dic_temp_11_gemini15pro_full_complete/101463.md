irenicon:/aɪˈriːnɪkɒn/ | n. | 和平倡议；和解的提议；促进和平的措施 | A proposal or measure intended to promote peace and reconciliation. |  例句：The king offered an irenicon to the rebels in hopes of ending the civil war. 国王向叛军提出和解方案，希望结束内战。

例句：Her latest book serves as an irenicon in the often-fractious debate about immigration. 她的新书在经常充满争议的移民辩论中起到了和解的作用。| 近义词：peace offering, olive branch, overture, compromise, conciliation | 反义词：provocation, challenge, ultimatum, casus belli | 词性: noun
