manipulates:/məˈnɪpjəˌleɪts/| v.|操纵，控制，影响|influences, controls, or skillfully handles something or someone, often in a dishonest or unfair way. |例句：The politician manipulates public opinion to his advantage.  这位政客操纵公众舆论为自己谋利。|近义词：exploits, controls, influences, maneuvers, directs, guides|反义词：helps, assists, supports, empowers, facilitates|词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *manipulāre*, frequentative of *manipulus* "handful, company of soldiers," from *manus* "hand" + *-plus* (akin to *plēnus* "full").  The original meaning referred to handling objects skillfully.  Over time, the meaning shifted to include skillful handling of situations and people, often with negative connotations.
