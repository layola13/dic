afflictednesses:/əˈflɪktɪdnɪsɪz/
|n.|苦难，痛苦，折磨|The state of being afflicted; a condition of suffering or distress.|He bore his afflictednesses with patience and fortitude. 他以耐心和毅力忍受着他的痛苦。|近义词: sufferings, miseries, tribulations|反义词: blessings, comforts, joys|词性: 名词

词源:  From afflicted + -ness.  "Afflicted" comes from the Late Latin *afflictus*, past participle of affligere "to strike down, damage," from ad- "to, toward" + fligere "to strike."
