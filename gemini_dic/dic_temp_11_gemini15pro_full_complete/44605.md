crowd-pulling:/ˈkraʊdˌpʊlɪŋ/
adjective
吸引人群的；叫座的
Something that is crowd-pulling attracts large numbers of people.
The circus is a real crowd-pulling event.  马戏团是一个真正吸引人群的活动。
The museum's new dinosaur exhibit is proving to be a major crowd-pulling attraction.  博物馆新的恐龙展览被证明是一个主要的吸引人群的景点。
近义词: popular, attractive, successful
反义词: unpopular, unattractive, unsuccessful
词性: adjective
词源:  A compound adjective formed from "crowd" (a large number of people gathered together) and "pulling" (exerting a force to draw something towards oneself).  Implies an ability to draw a crowd.
