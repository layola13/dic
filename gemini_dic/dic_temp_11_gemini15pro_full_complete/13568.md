attendee:/əˈtenˈdiː/| n.|出席者，参加者|A person who attends a meeting, event, etc.|例句：There were over 200 attendees at the conference. 会议上有200多名出席者。

例句：All attendees are requested to register at the front desk. 所有与会者都被要求在前台登记。|近义词：participant, attender, goer|反义词: absentee, non-attendee|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From attend + -ee. Attend comes from the Latin attendere "to stretch toward, give heed to," from ad- "to" + tendere "to stretch."  The suffix -ee denotes one who receives or is affected by the action of the verb.
