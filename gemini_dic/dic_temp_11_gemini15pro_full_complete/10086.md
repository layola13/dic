apocynin:/əˈpɒsɪnɪn/ | n. |  阿朴西宁 |  A naturally occurring organic compound found in the roots of the medicinal herb *Apocynum cannabinum* (Canadian hemp), known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. It inhibits the activity of NADPH oxidase, an enzyme involved in the production of reactive oxygen species. | Apocynin has shown promise in treating various inflammatory diseases. 阿朴西宁在治疗各种炎症性疾病方面显示出希望。 | Apocynin is being investigated for its potential use in cardiovascular diseases.  阿朴西宁正在被研究用于心血管疾病的可能性。 |  Synonyms: acetovanillone, 4'-hydroxyacetophenone | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a specific chemical compound. One might list pro-inflammatory agents as functional opposites, but not true antonyms.) | noun
