meowing:/miˈaʊɪŋ/ | n. | 猫叫声；喵喵叫 | The sound a cat makes. |  The kitten's constant meowing kept me awake all night. 小猫不停地喵喵叫让我整夜没睡。 | 近义词: purring, caterwauling | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 silence 安静) | 词性: 名词 (也可作为动名词)

词源:  From meow + -ing.  Ultimately from the imitative word *miau*.
