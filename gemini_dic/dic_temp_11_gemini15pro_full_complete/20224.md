birdseed:/ˈbɜːrdˌsiːd/| n. |鸟食|A mixture of seeds, nuts, and other foods suitable for birds to eat. |例句：I filled the bird feeder with birdseed. 我把鸟食装满了喂鸟器。

例句：The birdseed attracted a variety of birds to the garden. 鸟食吸引了各种各样的鸟来到花园。|近义词：bird food|反义词: None|词性: Noun
