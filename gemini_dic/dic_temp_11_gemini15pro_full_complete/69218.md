fearing:/ˈfɪərɪŋ/| v.|害怕，担忧|Feeling or showing fear or apprehension about something that might happen.|She was fearing for her safety. 她担心自己的安全。|Synonyms: dreading, apprehensive, anxious, worried|Antonyms: confident, unafraid, fearless|Verb (present participle)

fearing:/ˈfɪərɪŋ/| adj.|令人害怕的，可怕的 (rare usage)|Inspiring fear or apprehension.|The fearing storm approached quickly.  令人恐惧的风暴迅速逼近。|Synonyms: frightening, terrifying, menacing, intimidating|Antonyms: comforting, reassuring, calming|Adjective
