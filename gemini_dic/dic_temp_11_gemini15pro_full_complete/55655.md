dithyramb:/ˈdɪθɪræmb/ /daɪˈθɪræmb/| n.|酒神颂；狂热赞美诗；热情奔放的讲话|A wild, enthusiastic, or ecstatic speech or writing, usually in praise of something.|例句：The poet launched into a dithyramb about the beauty of nature. 诗人开始热情洋溢地赞美大自然的美丽。

例句：His speech was a dithyramb to the glory of the Roman Empire. 他的演讲是对罗马帝国辉煌的热情赞颂。|近义词：eulogy, panegyric, encomium, accolade, tribute|反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure, denunciation|词性:noun
