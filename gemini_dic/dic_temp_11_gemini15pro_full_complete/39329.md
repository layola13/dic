conjecturalist:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərəlɪst/
n.
推测者，猜测者
A person who makes conjectures; someone who bases their opinions or beliefs on guesses or incomplete information.
The conjecturalist proposed a theory about the origin of the universe, but it lacked substantial evidence.  这位推测者提出了一个关于宇宙起源的理论，但缺乏实质性证据。
Synonyms: theorist, speculator, hypothesizer, surmiser
Antonyms: pragmatist, realist, empiricist
noun


conjectural:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərəl/
adj.
推测的，猜测的
Based on or involving conjecture; speculative.
His conclusions were highly conjectural and based on limited data. 他的结论很大程度上是推测性的，并且基于有限的数据。
Synonyms: speculative, theoretical, hypothetical, suppositional
Antonyms: factual, proven, certain, definite
adjective
