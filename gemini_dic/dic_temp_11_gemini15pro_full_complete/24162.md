brickbats:/ˈbrɪkbæts/| n.|猛烈抨击，严厉批评|Insults, criticisms, or verbal attacks.  A barrage of angry or hostile comments.|The politician received a shower of brickbats after the scandal broke.  这位政治家在丑闻曝光后遭到猛烈抨击。|synonyms: criticisms, censures, condemnations, attacks, insults, abuse, flak, vitriol, aspersions|antonyms: praise, compliments, accolades, applause|noun

brickbats:/ˈbrɪkbæts/| n.|碎砖块|Pieces of broken brick, often used as missiles.|Rioters hurled brickbats at the police. 暴徒向警察投掷碎砖。|synonyms: rubble, debris, fragments|antonyms: whole bricks, complete structures|noun
