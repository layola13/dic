griseous:/ˈɡrɪsiəs/| adj.|灰色的；略带灰色的；浅灰色的；青灰色的|Of a gray or grayish color, often with a slightly bluish or greenish tinge.  Relating to, or resembling the color of something that has been burned to ashes.|The old house stood on a hill, its griseous walls blending with the stormy sky.  老房子矗立在山上，灰色的墙壁与暴风雨的天空融为一体。|Synonyms: gray, greyish, ashen, ash-colored, smoky, pearly, dove-colored, slate-colored|Antonyms: colorful, bright, vibrant, vivid|adjective


Etymology->
From Latin *griseus*, from French *gris* "gray".  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grīsaz*.
