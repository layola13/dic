laqueary: /ləˈkwiri/ | n. |  侍从，随从 | A person who attends to someone, especially a person of high rank.  |  The king's laqueary held his crown. 国王的侍从拿着他的王冠。| 近义词: attendant, servant, squire | 反义词: master, lord | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "lackey," which comes from Middle French "laquais" and ultimately from Spanish "lacayo."
