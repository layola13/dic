comeuppance:/ˌkʌmˈʌpəns/| n. |应得的惩罚，报应|A punishment or fate that someone deserves.  A deserved retribution.|例句：After years of bullying his classmates, he finally got his comeuppance when they all stood up to him.  多年来一直欺负同学的他，终于在他们都站起来反抗他时得到了报应。

例句：The corrupt official's comeuppance was swift and severe.  腐败官员的报应来得迅速而严厉。|近义词：retribution, deserts, just deserts, punishment, payback, reckoning|反义词:reward,  impunity|词性:noun
