clearing-out:/ˈklɪərɪŋ aʊt/
noun
清除；清理；清仓大甩卖
the act of getting rid of unwanted things
Example: I'm having a clearing-out of my wardrobe. 我正在清理我的衣柜。

Example: The shop is having a clearing-out sale. 这家商店正在进行清仓大甩卖。
synonyms: clean-out, purge, removal, clearance
antonyms: accumulation, hoarding, collecting
n.
