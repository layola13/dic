herbarian:/hɜːrˈbeəriən/
n.|标本管理员；植物学家|A person who specializes in the study of plants or maintains a herbarium.  A specialist in herbs.| The herbarian meticulously cataloged the newly discovered species.  这位标本管理员一丝不苟地将新发现的物种编入目录。| botanist, plant scientist| taxonomist, zoologist
词性: 名词

herbarian:/hɜːrˈbeəriən/
adj.|与植物标本室有关的；与植物学有关的|Relating to a herbarium or the study of plants.|The herbarian collection contained thousands of dried specimens. 这个植物标本室的收藏包含了数千个干燥的标本。|botanical, plant-related|zoological, animal-related
