cdw:/ˌsiː diː ˈdʌb(ə)ljuː/ |abbr.| Collision Damage Waiver (汽车租赁的碰撞损失减免)| Collision Damage Waiver is a type of optional insurance offered by car rental companies. | If you decline CDW and the rental car is damaged, you will be responsible for the cost of repairs.  | synonyms: Loss Damage Waiver (LDW) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but declining CDW means assuming full financial responsibility for damage.) | abbreviation
