irrisible:/ˈɪrɪsɪbl/| adj.|易笑的，忍不住笑的|Capable of laughter; easily provoked to laughter.  Disposed to laugh. |Her irrisible nature made her a joy to be around. 她爱笑的性格让她周围的人都感到快乐。|例句：His irrisible chuckle filled the room. 他忍不住的笑声充满了整个房间。|近义词：laughable, amusing, humorous|反义词：serious, solemn, grave|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *irrisibilis, from Latin irrīsus (past participle of irrīdēre, “to laugh at, mock”) + -ibilis (“-able”). Equivalent to ir- (variant of in-, not) + risible.
