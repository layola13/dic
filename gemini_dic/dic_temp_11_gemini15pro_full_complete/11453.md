argulus:/ˈɑːrɡjʊləs/| n. |鱼虱|A crustacean parasite of fish, belonging to the subclass Branchiura.  It attaches itself to the fish's skin or gills and feeds on its blood.|The argulus is a common parasite in freshwater fish. 鱼虱是淡水鱼中常见的寄生虫。| Argulus infestation can cause significant damage to fish populations. 鱼虱感染会对鱼类种群造成重大损害。|近义词:fish louse, branchiuran|反义词: (No direct antonym for a parasite) |词性:noun
