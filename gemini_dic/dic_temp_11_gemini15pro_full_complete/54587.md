dismal:/ˈdɪzməl/ | adj. | 凄凉的，阴沉的，忧郁的 | causing gloom or depression; dreary; cheerless |  The weather was dismal and rainy. 天气阴沉多雨。 | synonyms: gloomy, dreary, bleak, somber, depressing | antonyms: cheerful, bright, sunny, joyful | adjective

dismals:/ˈdɪzməlz/ | n. | 忧郁，沮丧；低落的情绪 (通常用复数形式) | feelings of sadness or depression | He suffered from the dismals after losing his job.  他失业后情绪低落。| synonyms: blues, doldrums, dumps | antonyms: elation, joy, happiness | noun
