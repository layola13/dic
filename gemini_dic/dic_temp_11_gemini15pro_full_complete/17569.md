beauish:/ˈboʊɪʃ/| adj.|有点漂亮；有点英俊|Somewhat handsome or attractive, in a rather affected or superficial way.  Often used with a slightly ironic or deprecating tone. |He's beauish, I suppose, but not really my type.  他算是有点帅吧，但不是我喜欢的类型。|近义词：handsome, pretty, attractive|反义词：ugly, unattractive, plain|词性:形容词

Etymology:  A combination of "beau" (meaning a boyfriend or admirer, from French) and the suffix "-ish" (meaning somewhat or having the quality of).
