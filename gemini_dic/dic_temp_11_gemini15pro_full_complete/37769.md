compatibility:/kəmˌpætɪˈbɪlɪti/ | n. |兼容性，相容性 |The ability of two or more things to exist or work together without causing problems.|  This software has compatibility issues with older operating systems.  这个软件与较旧的操作系统存在兼容性问题。| synonyms:  interoperability,  congruity,  accord,  harmony | antonyms: incompatibility,  conflict,  discord | noun
