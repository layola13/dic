buttressless:/ˈbʌtrəsləs/
adjective
无扶壁的，无支撑的
lacking buttresses; unsupported.
The buttressless wall eventually crumbled under the pressure. 这堵没有扶壁的墙最终在压力下倒塌了。
The old barn, buttressless and weathered, seemed ready to collapse.  这栋古老的谷仓，没有扶壁而且饱经风霜，看起来快要倒塌了。
Near synonyms: unsupported, unbraced, unfortified, weak
Antonyms: buttressed, supported, reinforced, strong
词性: 形容词
