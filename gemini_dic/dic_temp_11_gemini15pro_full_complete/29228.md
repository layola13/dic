casque:/kæsk/ /kɑːsk/ | n. |头盔，钢盔|A helmet, especially one of metal. |The knight wore a plumed casque. 骑士戴着有羽饰的头盔。|Synonyms: helmet, helm, headpiece|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps bareheadedness)|noun

Etymology->
From Old French *casque*, from Spanish *casco* "skull, helmet, potsherd," probably from Vulgar Latin *cascus*, perhaps ultimately of Iberian origin.
