chariotlike:/ˈtʃæriətˌlaɪk/| adj.|像战车的，战车似的|Resembling a chariot, especially in magnificence or imposing appearance.  This describes something that looks or feels like a chariot, often implying grandeur or power.|例句：The king rode in a chariotlike carriage, pulled by four white horses. 国王乘坐一辆战车般的马车，由四匹白马拉着。

例句：The clouds moved across the sky in a chariotlike procession. 云彩像战车队伍一样在天空中移动。|近义词：majestic, imposing, grand, stately|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性:adjective
