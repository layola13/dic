noncensoriously:/ˌnɒnsenˈsɔːriəsli/
adverb
不加批判地；不加审查地；宽容地
in a way that does not criticize or condemn something, especially something that others might consider morally wrong
He listened noncensoriously to her confession. 他不带批判地倾听了她的忏悔。

近义词:tolerantly, open-mindedly, acceptingly, liberally, permissively
反义词:critically, judgmentally, censoriously, disapprovingly
词性:adverb

词源:
From non- + censorious + -ly.  "Censorious" comes from Late Latin "censoriosus",  meaning "apt to blame or criticize," from Latin "censor," a Roman official who maintained public morals.
