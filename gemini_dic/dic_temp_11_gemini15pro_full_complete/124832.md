muckiest:/ˈmʌkiɪst/
adjective
最脏的；最污秽的
superlative adjective of mucky: covered with or containing muck; very dirty.
例句：That's the muckiest pigsty I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最脏的猪圈。

例句：His boots were the muckiest of the group after the hike. 徒步旅行后，他的靴子是小组里最脏的。

近义词：dirtiest, filthiest, grimiest
反义词：cleanest, purest, tidiest
词性：形容词
