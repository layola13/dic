aurally:/ˈɔːrəli/| adv.|听觉上地，通过听觉|Perceived by the ear.  Relating to the sense of hearing or the organs of hearing.|例句：The information was presented aurally. 信息是通过听觉呈现的。|例句：She learned the song aurally, by listening to it repeatedly. 她通过反复听来学习这首歌，也就是通过听觉学习。|近义词：acoustically, audibly|反义词: visually|词性: 副词

词源: From Latin *aurālis*, from auris (“ear”).
