austronesian:/ˌɔːstrəˈniːʒən/ | adj. n. | 南岛语系的；南岛语系民族的；南岛语系的人 | Relating to or denoting a family of languages spoken in an area extending from Madagascar in the west to the Pacific islands in the east.  A member of any of the peoples speaking an Austronesian language. | The Tagalog language is Austronesian.  他加禄语属于南岛语系。|  The cultural similarities suggest an Austronesian origin. 文化上的相似性表明了南岛语系的起源。 | 近义词: Malayo-Polynesian (旧称) | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Austro- (south) + -nesian (islands).  源自 Austro- (南) + -nesian (岛屿)。
