hamsters:/ˈhæmstərz/ | n. |仓鼠|Small rodents with large cheek pouches, often kept as pets. They are known for their hoarding behavior.| My daughter has two hamsters named Fluffy and Squeaky. 我的女儿有两只仓鼠，分别叫Fluffy和Squeaky。| synonyms: rodent, gerbil | antonyms: (none specific, perhaps predator) | noun
