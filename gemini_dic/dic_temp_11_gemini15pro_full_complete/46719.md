dancingly:/ˈdænsɪŋli/ | adv. | 舞蹈般地，轻盈地 | In a way that suggests dancing; gracefully and lightly. | She moved dancingly across the floor. 她轻盈地穿过地板。| Synonym: gracefully, lightly, nimbly, airily | Antonym: clumsily, awkwardly | adverb

Etymology: From dance + -ing + -ly.  "Dance" comes from Old French "dancier."  "-ing" forms a present participle, and "-ly" forms an adverb.
