nonsaving:/ˌnɒnˈseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
非储蓄的；不储蓄的
Not pertaining to saving; not accumulating savings.
The family had significant nonsaving expenses related to healthcare.  这个家庭在医疗保健方面有大量的非储蓄支出。
The nonsaving nature of their spending habits led to financial instability.  他们不储蓄的消费习惯导致了财务不稳定。
Near Synonyms: spending, consuming, expending
Antonyms: saving, thrifty, frugal
词性: 形容词
