locking: /ˈlɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkɪŋ/ (US) | n. adj. | 锁定；锁住；联锁 |The act of fastening something with a lock; the state of being locked or fastened.  Also used as an adjective to describe something related to locking.|
例句：The locking mechanism is broken. 锁定装置坏了。
例句：Locking the door is important for security. 锁门对于安全很重要。
近义词：fastening, securing, bolting | 反义词：unlocking, opening, releasing | 词性: noun, adjective

locking: /ˈlɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkɪŋ/ (US) | v. | 锁定；锁住；联锁  |Present participle of "to lock".  Describes the action of fastening something with a lock.|
例句：He is locking the gate. 他正在锁门。
例句：She was locking up the shop when the alarm went off.  当警报响起时，她正在锁店门。
近义词：fastening, securing, bolting | 反义词：unlocking, opening, releasing | 词性: verb


词源: From Old English *loccian,  frequentative of *lūcan "to close, lock," from Proto-Germanic *lukjanan.
