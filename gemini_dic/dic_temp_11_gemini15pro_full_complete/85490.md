hastate:/ˈheɪsteɪt/| adj.|戟形的，矛状的|Shaped like a spearhead with outward-pointing lobes at the base.|The leaves of the plant are hastate, resembling small spearheads.|近义词：spear-shaped, lanceolate, sagittate|反义词:rounded, ovate|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *hastatus*, from hasta (“spear”).
