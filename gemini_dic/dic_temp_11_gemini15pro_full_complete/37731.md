compartment:/kəmˈpɑːrtmənt/ /kəmˈpɑɚtmənt/| n. | 隔间；车厢；分隔间 |A separate section or part of a larger space, container, or vehicle.| The train was crowded, and we had to stand in the compartment. 火车很拥挤，我们不得不站在隔间里。| synonym: section, chamber, bay, cubicle | antonym: whole, entirety | noun

compartment:/kəmˈpɑːrtmənt/ /kəmˈpɑɚtmənt/| v. | 分隔；划分 |To divide into separate sections or categories.| The suitcase is compartmentalized for easy packing.  这个手提箱被分隔开以便于打包。| synonym: divide, partition, segregate | antonym: unite, combine | verb
