corallike:/ˈkɔːrəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 珊瑚似的，像珊瑚的 | Resembling coral, in appearance or form. | The intricate, corallike formations were breathtaking.  这些错综复杂的珊瑚状构造令人叹为观止。| The vase had a corallike texture.  这个花瓶有着像珊瑚一样的纹理。 | 近义词：coral-shaped, coralline | 反义词: smooth, flat | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: coral + like.  From "coral" referring to the marine invertebrates and "like" indicating resemblance.
