exceptionality:/ɪkˌsɛpʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 例外性；特殊性；非凡性 | The quality of being exceptional; the state of being unusual, extraordinary, or atypical. |  Her exceptionality as a musician was evident from a young age. 她作为音乐家的非凡才能从小就显而易见。| synonyms: singularity, uniqueness, rarity, unusualness, extraordinariness, remarkableness, distinction | antonyms: typicality, normality, ordinariness, commonness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exceptionalis*, from Latin exceptio (“exception”), from exceptus, past participle of excipio (“I take out, except”), from ex (“out”) + capio (“I take”).
