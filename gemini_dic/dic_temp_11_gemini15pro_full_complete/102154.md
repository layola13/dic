issuer:/ˈɪʃuːər/ (US) /ˈɪʃʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|发行人，签发者|A person or organization that issues something, especially documents or money.|The issuer of the bond is a well-known financial institution.  这家知名的金融机构是该债券的发行人。|synonyms: provider, supplier, distributor, originator|antonyms: receiver, holder, bearer|noun
