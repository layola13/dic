overbrutally: /ˌoʊvərˈbruːtəli/ | adv. | 过于残暴地，极其野蛮地 |  Formed by combining "over" (meaning excessively) and "brutally" (in a cruel and violent manner).  It describes an action performed with excessive brutality. | The dictator overbrutally suppressed the peaceful protest.  独裁者极其野蛮地镇压了和平抗议。|  The soldiers overbrutally treated the prisoners of war. 士兵们过于残暴地对待战俘。 |  savagely, viciously, ruthlessly | gently, kindly, humanely | adverb
