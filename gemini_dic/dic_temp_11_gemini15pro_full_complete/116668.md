mattock: /ˈmætək/ (US), /ˈmætɒk/ (UK) | n. | 锄头;鹤嘴锄 | A digging tool with a broad blade and a pointed end, used for breaking up hard ground and rocks. |  He used a mattock to break up the hard soil in the garden. 他用锄头把花园里的硬土翻松。

The workers used mattocks to dig the foundations for the new building. 工人们用鹤嘴锄挖新建筑的地基。


|近义词：pickaxe, pick, adze |反义词: None |词性: noun
