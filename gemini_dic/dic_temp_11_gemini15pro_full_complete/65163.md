ethnocentric: /ˌeθnoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 民族中心主义的 |  Believing that one's own ethnic group or culture is superior to all others and judging other cultures by the standards of one's own. |  Example 1: His ethnocentric views made it difficult for him to appreciate other cultures.  他的民族中心主义观点使他难以欣赏其他文化。

Example 2: The company's ethnocentric marketing campaign offended many people. 该公司以民族中心主义为导向的营销活动冒犯了许多人。 | 近义词: culturally biased, prejudiced, parochial | 反义词: culturally sensitive, tolerant, cosmopolitan | 词性: adjective
