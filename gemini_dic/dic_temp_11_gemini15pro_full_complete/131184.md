nonbrutal:/ˌnɑːnˈbruːtl/ | adj. | 非野蛮的，不残酷的 | Not brutal; gentle, kind, or merciful. |  The police employed nonbrutal methods to disperse the crowd. 警方采用了非暴力手段驱散人群。|  The teacher used nonbrutal discipline techniques in the classroom. 老师在课堂上使用非残酷的纪律技巧。| 近义词: gentle, kind, humane, compassionate, merciful | 反义词: brutal, cruel, savage, violent, vicious | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "brutal" (from Late Latin "brutalis," meaning "of beasts," from Latin "brutus," meaning "dull, senseless, irrational," also used of animals).
