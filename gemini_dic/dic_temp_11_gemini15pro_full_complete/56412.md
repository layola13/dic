doling:/ˈdoʊlɪŋ/
verb
施舍；少量地发放
distributing something sparingly or in small amounts.
The government was doling out food to the refugees. 政府正在向难民分发食物。
synonyms: distributing, dispensing, allocating, allotting, apportioning, rationing
antonyms: hoarding, withholding, keeping, accumulating, stockpiling
词性：动词
