hemipterous:/ˌhɛmɪˈptərəs/| adj.|半翅目的|Of or relating to the Hemiptera (an order of insects comprising the true bugs). Characterized by forewings that are leathery or horny at the base and membranous at the tip, and by piercing and sucking mouthparts.|例句：Aphids are hemipterous insects that are common garden pests. 蚜虫是常见的半翅目害虫。|近义词：hemelytrous|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

hemipterous:/ˌhɛmɪˈptərəs/| adj.|半翅目的|Relating to or belonging to the insect order Hemiptera, characterized by piercing-sucking mouthparts and usually two pairs of wings, the forewings being partly thickened. |例句：The shield bug is a hemipterous insect known for its protective covering. 盾蝽是一种以其保护性外壳而闻名的半翅目昆虫。|近义词：hemelytrous|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
