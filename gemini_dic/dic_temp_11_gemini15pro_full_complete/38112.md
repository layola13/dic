compradors:/kəmˈprɑːdɔːrz/ |n. |买办|A person, especially in Southern China, Hong Kong, and other parts of East Asia, who acted as an agent for Western commercial interests, often holding a position of authority within a foreign business. | The compradors played a crucial role in the early days of Western trade in China. 这些买办在西方与中国早期贸易中扮演着至关重要的角色。|Synonyms: agents, intermediaries, brokers|Antonyms: principals, owners| Noun
