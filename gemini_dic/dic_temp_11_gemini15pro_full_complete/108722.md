leans:/liːnz/
verb
倾斜，倚靠，倾向
To incline or bend from a vertical position.
The Leaning Tower of Pisa leans at an angle. 比萨斜塔倾斜一个角度。
incline, bend, tilt
stand, straighten
v.

leans:/liːnz/
noun
倾向，偏好
An inclination or preference.
He has leanings towards socialism. 他倾向于社会主义。
inclination, preference, tendency
aversion, dislike
n.
