fielding:/ˈfiːldɪŋ/ | n. | 接球；守备；防守 | The act of catching or stopping a ball, especially in cricket, baseball, or softball.  |  Example: The team's fielding was excellent, preventing the other team from scoring.  这个队的防守非常好，阻止了对方得分。

Example: His fielding has improved significantly this season.  他的接球能力本赛季有了显著提高。 | 近义词: defense, catching | 反义词: batting, hitting | 词性: noun


fielding:/ˈfiːldɪŋ/ | adj. |  (通常指板球队员)守备的;防守的 | Relating to the act of fielding. | Example:  He's a brilliant fielding player. 他是一位优秀的防守球员。

Example:  Their fielding positions were strategically chosen. 他们的防守位置经过了战略性的选择。| 近义词: defensive | 反义词: offensive | 词性: adjective
