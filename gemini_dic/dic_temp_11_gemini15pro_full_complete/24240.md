bridler:/ˈbraɪdlər/| n.|约束者，控制者；马勒|A person or thing that restrains or controls something, especially a horse's head by means of a bridle.|The experienced rider used a bridler to control the spirited stallion.|近义词：restrainer, controller, checker, curb|反义词:liberator, emancipator|noun

bridle:/ˈbraɪdl/| n. vt. vi.|马勒；约束，控制；(表示愤怒、轻蔑等)绷脸，板着脸|1.(n.) A piece of equipment used to control a horse, consisting of straps around its head and a bit in its mouth. 2.(vt.) To put a bridle on (a horse). 3.(vt.) To restrain or control. 4.(vi.) To show anger or resentment, typically by throwing back the head and tightening the lips.|1. The bridle was too tight on the horse.  马勒对马来说太紧了。 2. He bridled the horse before the ride. 他在骑马前给马套上马勒。 3. She bridled her anger. 她抑制住了自己的愤怒。 4. He bridled at the suggestion. 他对这个建议嗤之以鼻。|近义词：(n.) harness, reins; (v.) restrain, curb, control|反义词：(v.) release, unleash, liberate|noun, verb, verb, verb
