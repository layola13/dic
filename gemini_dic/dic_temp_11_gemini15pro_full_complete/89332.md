homogeny:/həˈmɒdʒəni/ /hoʊˈmɑːdʒəni/| n.|同质性，同源性，均匀性|The homogeny of the student population made it difficult to have diverse perspectives. 学生群体的同质性使得难以拥有多样化的观点。|The homogeny of the milk was ensured by the pasteurization process. 巴氏杀菌法确保了牛奶的均匀性。|近义词：uniformity, similarity, consistency|反义词：heterogeneity, diversity, variety|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *homogeneus, from Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (homogenḗs, “of the same kind”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”).
