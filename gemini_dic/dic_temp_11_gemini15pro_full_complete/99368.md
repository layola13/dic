interdestructive:/ˌɪntərdiˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
相互破坏的；互相摧毁的
Causing mutual destruction.  Involving actions or processes that destroy each other.
The interdestructive forces of the two rival gangs resulted in significant losses for both sides. 这两个敌对帮派的相互破坏力量导致双方都损失惨重。
The colliding particles engaged in an interdestructive process, annihilating each other upon impact. 碰撞的粒子进行了一个相互破坏的过程，在撞击时相互湮灭。
近义词: mutually destructive, counterdestructive
反义词: constructive, beneficial
词性: adjective

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): between, among, mutually, reciprocally (from Latin)
"destructive" (adjective): causing destruction (from Latin "destruct-" - to pull down, demolish)
