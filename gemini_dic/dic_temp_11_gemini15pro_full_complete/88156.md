hiller:/ˈhɪlər/| n.| 堆土机; 起垄机; 做丘者; 种植者 |A machine or person that makes hills, especially for planting crops.|The farmer used a hiller to create rows for planting potatoes.| 近义词: ridger, planter | 反义词: leveler | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the verb 'to hill' + '-er' (agent suffix). The verb 'to hill' means 'to form into a hill or mound', from the noun 'hill'.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hulniz, meaning "hill, elevation."
