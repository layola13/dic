gem:/dʒem/ | n. | 宝石；珍品；精华 | A precious or semiprecious stone, especially when cut and polished or engraved.  Something that is highly valued or prized. | This ring features a stunning ruby gem. 这枚戒指上有一颗令人惊艳的红宝石。 | Synonyms: jewel, treasure, pearl, masterpiece | Antonyms: defect, flaw, imperfection | noun


gem:/dʒem/ | v. |  点缀；用宝石装饰 | To adorn or decorate with gems. | The crown was gemmed with diamonds and emeralds.  王冠上镶嵌着钻石和祖母绿。 | Synonyms: adorn, decorate, embellish, bejewel | Antonyms: strip, denude | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *geme (modern French gemme), from Latin gemma "bud, precious stone," related to germen "bud, sprout."
