law's:/lɔːz/
noun
法律的；法律条文的
possessive form of law, meaning "belonging to law" or "of the law"
The law's reach extends to all citizens. 法律的管辖范围涵盖所有公民。
Near Synonyms: legal, juridical
Antonyms: illegal, unlawful
词性: noun (possessive)
