nonfortuitousness:/ˌnɒnfɔːrtʃuˈɪtəsnəs/ (British), /ˌnɑːnfɔːrtʃuˈɪtəsnəs/ (American)| n. |非偶然性，非意外性|The quality or state of not being fortuitous; planned or designed.|The nonfortuitousness of the meeting suggests it was prearranged.  这次会议的非偶然性表明它是预先安排好的。|Synonyms: intentionality, deliberateness, design, purposefulness|Antonyms: fortuitousness, randomness, chance, accident|noun
