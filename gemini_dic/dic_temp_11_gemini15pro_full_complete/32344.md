childhoods:/ˈtʃaɪldhʊdz/| n. |童年（复数）|The state or period of being a child.  The plural form emphasizes multiple or different experiences of childhood.|

例句：Their childhoods were very different. 他们的童年非常不同。

例句：He studied the childhoods of famous artists. 他研究了著名艺术家的童年时期。


近义词：youth, infancy, adolescence|反义词:adulthood, maturity|词性:noun
