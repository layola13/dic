outsmelling:/ˌaʊtˈsmɛlɪŋ/
adjective
胜过…的气味; 比…难闻
surpassing in smell; smelling worse than
例句：The durian was outsmelling the garbage.  榴莲的气味比垃圾还要难闻。

例句：His cologne was outsmelling everyone else's perfume. 他的古龙水比其他人的香水都难闻。

近义词：outstinking, overpowering (in smell)
反义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling

词性
adjective
