obtainabilities:/əbˌteɪnəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可获得性；可得到性|The obtainabilities of these rare books are very low. 这些珍稀书籍的可获得性非常低。| This study focuses on the obtainabilities of affordable housing in major cities. 这项研究的重点是大城市经济适用房的可获得性。|近义词：availability, accessibility, attainability|反义词：unavailability, inaccessibility, unattainability|词性: 名词

词源:  From "obtainable" + "-ity". "Obtainable" comes from the Old French "obtenir," from Latin "obtinere," meaning "to hold, possess, maintain, or obtain," from "ob-" (toward) + "tenere" (to hold).  The suffix "-ity" forms nouns of quality or state.
