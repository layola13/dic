dispeller:/dɪˈspɛlər/ | n. |驱散者，消除者 |Someone or something that dispels something unpleasant, such as fear, doubt, or ignorance. |The sunshine acted as a dispeller of the morning gloom. 阳光驱散了清晨的阴郁。| 近义词:remover, eliminator, erradicator | 反义词:creator, instigator | noun

dispell:/dɪˈspɛl/ | vt. |驱散，消除 |To make something, such as a feeling, thought, or belief, go away. |She needed to dispel the lingering doubts in her mind. 她需要消除心中挥之不去的疑虑。| 近义词:banish, eliminate, disperse | 反义词:gather, collect, accumulate | verb
