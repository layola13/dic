lamellibranchiata:/ləˌmɛlɪˈbræŋkiˌɑːtə/ /ləˌmɛlɪˈbræŋkiˌeɪtə/
noun
双壳纲动物；瓣鳃类
A class of aquatic mollusks, including clams, oysters, mussels, scallops, etc., having a laterally compressed body enclosed in a shell consisting of two hinged valves.
Clams and oysters are lamellibranchiata. 蛤蜊和牡蛎是双壳纲动物。
近义词:bivalve, pelecypod
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific biological classification)
词性:n.
