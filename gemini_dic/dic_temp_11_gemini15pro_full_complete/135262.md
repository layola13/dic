nonparalytics:/ˌnɒnpærəˈlɪtɪks/ | n. | 非麻痹性药物 | Medications that do not cause paralysis.  These drugs may still affect muscle function, but they don't completely paralyze the muscles. | The patient was given nonparalytics to manage muscle spasms.  病人服用非麻痹性药物来控制肌肉痉挛。| The doctor prescribed nonparalytics for the patient's mild muscle stiffness. 医生为病人轻微的肌肉僵硬开了非麻痹性药物。 | Synonyms: non-depolarizing muscle relaxants | Antonyms: paralytics, neuromuscular blocking agents | noun
