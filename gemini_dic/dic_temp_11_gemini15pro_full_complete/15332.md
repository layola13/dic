backspaced:/ˈbækˌspeɪst/| v. |退格，删除|To move the cursor back one space at a time, usually deleting the preceding character.|She backspaced to correct the typo. 她退格纠正了打字错误。|Synonyms: delete, erase|Antonyms: insert, type, write|verb

backspaced:/ˈbækˌspeɪst/| adj. |被退格的，被删除的|(Rarely used as an adjective)  Describes text that has been deleted using the backspace key. |The backspaced words were still faintly visible on the screen.  屏幕上被删除的文字仍然依稀可见。|Synonyms: deleted, erased|Antonyms: inserted, typed, written|adjective
