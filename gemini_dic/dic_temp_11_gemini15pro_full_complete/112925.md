lug's:/lʌɡz/| n.|把手；耳状物；笨人；（非正式）耳朵；（非正式）一耳光；（非正式，通常作 lugs）肺；（非正式）笨蛋；（非正式）沉重的脚步；|lug's is the possessive form of "lug," meaning "belonging to a lug." A "lug" can refer to a handle, an ear-like projection, a clumsy person (informally), an ear (informally), a slap (informally), a lung (informally), or a heavy footstep (informally).  Because "lug" has so many informal meanings, context is essential to understanding the specific meaning of "lug's."|例句：The lug's handle was broken.  那个把手坏了。

例句：He gave the lug's ear a playful tug. 他开玩笑地拽了一下那个笨蛋的耳朵。|近义词：(depending on the meaning of lug) handle, ear, fool, idiot, clutz|反义词:(depending on the meaning of lug) genius, expert|词性:noun (possessive)
