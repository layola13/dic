insnarl:/ɪnˈsnɑːrl/ /ɪnˈsnɑːrl/
verb
缠住，使陷入困境
to cause to become entangled or trapped in something as in a net
The fishing net insnarled a large number of salmon.  渔网缠住了一大群鲑鱼。

近义词:entangle, trap, enmesh
反义词: disentangle, free, release
词性:v.
