combs:/kəʊmz/ (British) /koʊmz/ (American)| n.|梳子；蜂窝；鸡冠|A toothed implement used to smooth, arrange, or fasten the hair.|She used a comb to untangle her long hair. 她用梳子梳理她长长的头发。|近义词：brush|反义词:|noun

combs:/kəʊmz/ (British) /koʊmz/ (American)| v. |梳理；搜索；仔细检查|To smooth, arrange, or fasten (hair) with a comb; to search carefully and systematically.|He combed his hair neatly before the interview.  他在面试前仔细梳理了头发。|近义词：search, scour, scrutinize|反义词:|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *camb, from Proto-Germanic *kambaz, from Proto-Indo-European *gombh- (“tooth”). Cognate with German Kamm (“comb”), Dutch kam (“comb”).
