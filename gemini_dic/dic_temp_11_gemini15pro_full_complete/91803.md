hyperfemininity:/ˌhaɪpərˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. | 过度女性化；极端女性化 |  An exaggerated adherence to stereotypically feminine gender roles, often involving an excessive focus on appearance, submissiveness, and nurturing behavior.  It can be a conscious performance or an unconscious internalization of societal expectations. | Her hyperfemininity, with the frilly dresses and constant need for reassurance, seemed almost a caricature. 她极度女性化的表现，穿着褶边连衣裙，不断需要 reassurance，看起来就像一个漫画人物。|  The movie explores themes of hyperfemininity and its impact on young women. 这部电影探讨了极端女性化及其对年轻女性的影响。 | 近义词: ultra-femininity, exaggerated femininity | 反义词: masculinity, androgyny | 词性: noun


Etymology:
hyper- (Greek): over, excessive
femininity (Latin): femina (woman) + -ity (state or quality of)
