mis-cite:/ˌmɪsˈsaɪt/
verb
错误引用
to quote (someone or something) incorrectly
The author miscited several sources in his paper.  作者在他的论文中错误地引用了几个来源。
misquote, cite incorrectly
cite correctly, quote accurately
v.
