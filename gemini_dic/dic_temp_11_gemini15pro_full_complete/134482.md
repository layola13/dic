nonlabor:/nɒnˈleɪbər/ |adj.| 非劳力的；非劳动力的 |  Relating to or involving activities or costs that are not directly related to labor or workforce.  | The company's nonlabor expenses, such as rent and utilities, increased significantly this quarter.  该公司本季度的非劳动力支出（例如租金和水电费）大幅增加。| Synonyms: non-labor, nonlabour | Antonyms: labor, labour | adjective
