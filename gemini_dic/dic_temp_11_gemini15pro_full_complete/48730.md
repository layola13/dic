defilers:/dɪˈfaɪlərz/| n.|玷污者，亵渎者|People who defile something sacred or pure; those who corrupt or contaminate.  Those who make something impure or unclean.|The defilers of the temple were banished from the city.  玷污神殿的人被驱逐出城。|Synonyms: polluters, contaminators, desecrators, profanes, violators|Antonyms: purifiers, cleansers, protectors, guardians|Noun

defile:/dɪˈfaɪl/| v.|玷污，亵渎|To make something impure or unclean, often something sacred or respected; to corrupt or contaminate.|The vandals defiled the monument with graffiti.  破坏者用涂鸦玷污了纪念碑。|Synonyms: contaminate, pollute, desecrate, profane, violate|Antonyms: purify, cleanse, protect, sanctify|Verb
