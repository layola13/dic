guildhall: /ˈɡɪldhɔːl/ | n. | 行会会馆；市政厅 | A building used by a guild or corporation, often a town hall.  | The mayor gave a speech at the guildhall. 市长在市政厅发表了讲话。 |  The annual banquet was held in the city's guildhall. 年度宴会是在该市的市政厅举行的。 | synonyms: town hall, city hall, municipal building | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps: private residence) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gildehalle*, from Old English *gildheall*  ("guild hall"), from *gild* ("guild, payment, contribution, association") + *heall* ("hall"). Cognate with German *Gildehalle*.
