individuating:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒueɪtɪŋ/
adjective
赋予个性的，使个性化的
Giving an individual character to something; distinguishing.
The artist's unique style individuates his work.  这位艺术家的独特风格使其作品个性鲜明。
近义词: distinguishing, characterizing, particularizing, specifying
反义词: generalizing, standardizing
词性: adjective

individuate:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒueɪt/
verb
区分，使个性化
1. To make or become individual or distinct.
2. To perceive or recognize as distinct and individual.
The program individuates students based on their learning styles.  这个项目根据学生的学习风格对他们进行个性化区分。
近义词: differentiate, distinguish, separate, singularize
反义词: generalize, homogenize
词性: verb


词源：From Late Latin individuatus, past participle of individuare "to make indivisible, distinguish," from Latin individuus "indivisible."
