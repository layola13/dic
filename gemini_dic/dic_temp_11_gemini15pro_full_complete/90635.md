humanitarians:/hjuːˌmænɪˈteəriənz/
|n.|人道主义者|People who are actively involved in promoting human welfare and reducing suffering; philanthropists.|例句：Many humanitarians work for NGOs. 许多人道主义者在非政府组织工作。

例句：The humanitarians rushed to the disaster zone to provide aid.  这些人道主义者冲向灾区提供援助。
|近义词：philanthropists, altruists, benefactors|反义词：misanthropes, sadists|词性:名词(plural)


humanitarian:/hjuːˌmænɪˈteəriən/
|adj.|人道主义的|Concerned with or seeking to promote human welfare.|例句：The organization provides humanitarian aid to refugees. 该组织向难民提供人道主义援助。

例句：He dedicated his life to humanitarian work. 他毕生致力于人道主义工作。
|近义词：charitable, benevolent, compassionate|反义词：inhumane, cruel, merciless|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *humanitarius, from Latin humanitas (“human nature, culture, kindness”), from humanus (“human”).
