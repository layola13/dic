outsole: /ˈaʊtˌsoʊl/ | n. | 外底 | The outermost layer of the sole of a shoe or boot, in direct contact with the ground. |  The cobbler replaced the worn outsole of my hiking boots. 修鞋匠更换了我登山靴磨损的外底。


 The outsole of these running shoes provides excellent traction.  这些跑鞋的外底提供了极好的抓地力。


| 近义词: sole, bottom | 反义词: insole | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From out + sole.  "Out" indicating the outer part, and "sole" referring to the bottom of a shoe.
