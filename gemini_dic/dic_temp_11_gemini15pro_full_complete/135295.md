nonpassionateness:/ˌnɒnˈpæʃənətnəs/
noun
缺乏热情；冷静；冷漠
lack of passion or strong emotion; calmness; indifference.
His nonpassionateness about the project worried his colleagues.  他对这个项目的冷淡让他的同事们感到担忧。
Synonyms: apathy, indifference, coolness, detachment, impassivity
Antonyms: passion, fervor, zeal, enthusiasm, ardor
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + passionateness.  Passionateness from passionate + -ness. Passionate from Old French passionat, from Late Latin passionatus "having suffered,"  from Latin passio "suffering, enduring," from past participle stem of pati "to suffer, endure."
