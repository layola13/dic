monarchically:/məˈnɑːrkɪkəli/
adverb
君主地；专制地
In a manner resembling or characteristic of a monarch; autocratically.
The country was ruled monarchically for centuries.  这个国家被君主统治了几个世纪。
He spoke monarchically, as if his word were law. 他专制地说着，好像他的话就是法律。
Synonyms:regally, royally, imperially, sovereignly, despotically, autocratically
Antonyms:democratically, republically
词性: 副词
