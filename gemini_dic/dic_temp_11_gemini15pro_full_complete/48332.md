decriminalised:/diːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzd/
verb
使合法化；非刑事化
To make something that was previously a crime legal.
Marijuana has been decriminalised in several states.  一些州已经将大麻合法化。
The government has decriminalised certain minor offences. 政府已经将某些轻微的犯罪行为非刑事化。
Synonyms: legalized, legitimized
Antonyms: criminalized, prohibited
词性: 动词

decriminalisation:/diːˌkrɪmɪnələˈzeɪʃən/
noun
合法化；非刑事化
The act of making something that was previously a crime legal.
The decriminalisation of marijuana is a controversial issue.  大麻合法化是一个有争议的问题。
There are arguments for and against the decriminalisation of drugs.  关于毒品合法化的正反两方面的论点都有。
Synonyms: legalization, legitimation
Antonyms: criminalization, prohibition
词性: 名词
