easy:/ˈiːzi/| adj. adv.|容易的，轻松的；容易地，轻松地|Something that is easy is not difficult to do or understand.  It can also describe a person who is relaxed and not worried.|The exam was easy. (考试很简单。)  He's an easy person to get along with. (他是一个很容易相处的人。)|近义词：simple, effortless, straightforward, relaxed|反义词：difficult, hard, complex, challenging|词性:形容词, 副词
词源:From Middle English *esie, from Old English ēaðe (“easy, comfortable, gentle, mild, peaceful, pleasant, prosperous, smooth, fortunate, ready, prepared, willing, kind, good”), from Proto-Germanic *auþiz (“empty, desolate, deserted”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ówiths (“empty, vacant”). Cognate with Scots aise (“easy”), West Frisian aezje (“easy”), Dutch aaien (“to pamper, to indulge”), German öde (“desolate, deserted”).|
