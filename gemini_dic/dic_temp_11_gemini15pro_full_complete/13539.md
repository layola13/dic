attemperance:/ˈætɛmp(ə)rəns/
n.|节制，克制；温和|The practice of moderating or abstaining from indulgence, especially in alcohol; moderation or self-restraint.|He preached attemperance in all things. 他提倡凡事都要节制。|moderation, sobriety, abstinence, self-control|excess, intemperance, indulgence|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French attemperance, from Late Latin attemperantia, from Latin attemperare "to moderate, temper," from ad- "to" + temperare "to mix, blend, moderate."
