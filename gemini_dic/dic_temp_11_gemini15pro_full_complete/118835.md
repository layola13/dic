metaphony:/ˈmɛtəˌfoʊni/ | n. | 元音音变，变音 | A vowel change in which one vowel is influenced by another, especially a vowel of a following syllable. | The metaphony in this dialect changes the 'o' to a 'u' sound.  这个方言中的元音音变将“o”变成了“u”音。 |  Metaphony can be observed in many Romance languages. 在许多罗曼语系语言中可以观察到元音音变。 | umlaut, vowel harmony | - | noun
