noncutting:/ˌnɒnˈkʌtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈkʌtɪŋ/
adjective
非切割的；非剪切的；不切割的
Not involving cutting or incision.  Relating to a procedure or tool that does not cut.
The doctor opted for a noncutting procedure to minimize scarring. 医生选择了一种非切割手术以减少疤痕。
They used a noncutting laser to stimulate the cells. 他们使用非切割激光来刺激细胞。
近义词:noninvasive, nonsurgical
反义词:cutting, invasive, surgical
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + cutting (present participle of the verb "to cut").  "Cut" comes from the Old English *cutt- (noun), and *ceorfan (verb).
