crystal's:/ˈkrɪstlz/ | n. | 水晶的；晶体的；水晶制品 |  Belonging to or associated with a crystal or crystals.  |  Example: The crystal's facets reflected the light beautifully. 水晶的刻面漂亮地反射着光。 |  Example:  I admired the crystal's clarity. 我欣赏水晶的清澈。 | Synonyms: crystalline, glassy, clear | Antonyms: opaque, cloudy |  Possessive noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English cristal, from Old French cristal, from Latin crystallum, from Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krústallos, “ice, crystal”), from κρύος (krúos, “icy cold, frost”).
