cissy:/ˈsɪsi/| n. |娘娘腔，胆小鬼，懦夫|A derogatory term for a boy or man considered effeminate or cowardly. |He was teased for being a cissy because he preferred reading to sports. 他因为喜欢读书而不是运动而被嘲笑为娘娘腔。|近义词：wimp, coward, milksop|反义词：tough guy, macho man|名词

cissy:/ˈsɪsi/| adj. |娇气的，胆怯的|Effeminate or cowardly (used pejoratively). |He had a cissy way of walking. 他走路的样子很娘。|近义词：effeminate, timid, cowardly|反义词：masculine, brave, bold|形容词
