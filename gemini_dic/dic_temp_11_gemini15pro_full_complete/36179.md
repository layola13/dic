coffinless:/ˈkɒfɪnlɪs/| adj.|无棺的，不用棺材埋葬的|Not buried in a coffin; without a coffin.  This refers to a burial practice where the body is interred directly into the earth, or cremated, without using a coffin.|He requested a coffinless burial, wanting to return to the earth as naturally as possible. 他要求不用棺材埋葬，希望尽可能自然地回归大地。|The family chose a coffinless cremation for environmental reasons. 出于环境原因，这家人选择火化，不使用棺材。|近义词:Uncoffined|反义词:Coffined|词性:形容词
