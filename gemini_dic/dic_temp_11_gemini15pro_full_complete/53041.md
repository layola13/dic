dioecious: /daɪˈiːʃəs/ | adj. | 雌雄异株的 | Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals. |  The ginkgo tree is dioecious, meaning some trees have only male flowers and others have only female flowers. 银杏树是雌雄异株的，这意味着有些树只有雄花，而另一些树只有雌花。

The papaya plant is dioecious, with separate male and female trees.  木瓜是雌雄异株的植物，雄树和雌树是分开的。

| 近义词: unisexual (in plants) | 反义词: monoecious, hermaphroditic | 词性: adjective
