exhortatively:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtətɪvli/ | adv. | 劝告地，鼓励地 | In a manner intended to urge or encourage someone to do something. |  He spoke exhortatively to the team, urging them to give their best effort. 他劝勉团队，鼓励他们尽最大努力。|  The coach addressed the players exhortatively, reminding them of the importance of teamwork. 教练劝告队员们，提醒他们团队合作的重要性。 | encouragingly, persuasively, urgently | discouragingly, dissuasively | adverb
