murmuringly:/ˈmɜːrmərɪŋli/ | adv. | 低声地，喃喃地 | In a murmuring way; with a low, continuous sound. | She murmured the words murmuringly, barely audible to anyone else. 她喃喃自语，几乎没有人能听到。|  He thanked her murmuringly, his voice thick with emotion.  他低声地感谢她，声音里充满了感激之情。 | 近义词: softly, quietly, under one's breath, in a low voice | 反义词: loudly, clearly, distinctly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "murmur" + the adverbial suffix "-ingly."  "Murmur" comes from the Latin "murmurare," meaning "to murmur, grumble."
