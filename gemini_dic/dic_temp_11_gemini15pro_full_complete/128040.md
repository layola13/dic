nereid:/ˈnɪəriɪd/ /ˈnɪriɪd/ | n. | 海仙女；海精 | A sea nymph; a female spirit of the sea.  |例句：The nereid combed her long, flowing hair. 海仙女梳理着她飘逸的长发。

例句：Legends tell of nereids luring sailors to their doom with enchanting songs.  传说中海仙女用迷人的歌声引诱水手走向毁灭。 |近义词：sea nymph, mermaid, oceanid |反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑land spirit 陆地精灵作为对比) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek Νηρεΐς (Nēreís), from Νηρεύς (Nēreús, “Nereus”). Nereus was a sea god in Greek mythology, and the Nereids were his fifty daughters.
