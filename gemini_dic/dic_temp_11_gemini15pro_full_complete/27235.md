calmnesses:/ˈkɑːmnɪsɪz/ /ˈkɑːmnəsɪz/
noun
平静，镇静，沉着
The state of being calm; absence of excitement or disturbance.
Her calmness in the face of danger was remarkable.  她在危险面前的镇定令人敬佩。
calm, tranquility, serenity, composure
agitation, anxiety, nervousness, restlessness
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From calmness + -es (suffix forming plural nouns). Calmness itself comes from calm (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality). Ultimately from Middle French calme.
