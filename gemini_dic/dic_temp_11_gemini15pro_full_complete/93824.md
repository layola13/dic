immigrations:/ˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃənz/
n.
移民，移居；移入
The process of people entering a country to live there permanently.
Large-scale immigrations from Europe to America occurred in the 19th century.
19世纪发生了从欧洲到美洲的大规模移民。
Immigration from Asia has enriched the cultural landscape of the United States.
来自亚洲的移民丰富了美国的文化景观。

近义词：migration, influx
反义词：emigration
