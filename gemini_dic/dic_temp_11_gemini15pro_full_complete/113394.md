lyric: /ˈlɪrɪk/ | n. adj.| 歌词；抒情的 |A lyric is a short poem that expresses personal feelings, often set to music.|  The lyrics of the song are very moving. 这首歌的歌词很感人。|近义词：poem, song, verse |反义词: epic, narrative |词性: 名词, 形容词


lyric: /ˈlɪrɪk/ | adj.| 抒情的；充满感情的 |Lyric poetry expresses personal feelings, often about love or nature. | The music had a lyric quality that made me feel peaceful.  这音乐有一种抒情的特质，让我感到平静。|近义词：poetic, emotional, expressive |反义词: unemotional, prosaic |词性: 形容词
