brightest:/ˈbraɪtɪst/| adj.|最明亮的，最聪明的，最快乐的|Superlative form of "bright," meaning shining with the most light, showing the most intelligence, or full of the most cheer.|例句：She had the brightest smile I'd ever seen. 她拥有我见过的最灿烂的笑容。|近义词：most brilliant, most radiant, smartest, most intelligent, most cheerful, happiest|反义词：dullest, dimmest, darkest, stupidest, gloomiest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English "beorht," meaning "shining," "clear," "beautiful."  The "-est" suffix is a superlative ending.
