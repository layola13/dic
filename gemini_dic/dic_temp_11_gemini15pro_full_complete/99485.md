interfertility:/ˌɪntərˈfɜːrtɪləti/ | n. |杂交可育性；互交可育性 | The ability of two varieties or species to interbreed and produce fertile offspring. |  Horses and donkeys can interbreed, but their offspring (mules) are sterile, so they are not interfertile. 马和驴可以杂交，但它们的后代（骡子）是不育的，所以它们不杂交可育。| synonym: cross-fertility | antonym: intersterility | noun

interfertile:/ˌɪntərˈfɜːrtaɪl/ | adj. |可杂交生育的；互交可育的 |  Capable of interbreeding and producing fertile offspring. | Different varieties of rice are often interfertile. 不同品种的水稻通常是可杂交生育的。| synonym: cross-fertile | antonym: intersterile | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + fertile (capable of producing offspring) + -ity (noun suffix) / -ile (adjective suffix)
