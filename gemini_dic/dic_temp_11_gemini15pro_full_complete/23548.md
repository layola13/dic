brainlike:/ˈbreɪnlaɪk/
adjective
像大脑的; 类似大脑的
Resembling or characteristic of a brain, especially in function or complexity.
The computer's brainlike ability to process information amazed the scientists.  这台电脑类似大脑的信息处理能力使科学家们感到惊讶。
The AI demonstrated brainlike activity in its response to stimuli.  人工智能在对刺激的反应中表现出类似大脑的活动。
近义词: intelligent, cerebral, sophisticated
反义词: mindless, unintelligent, simple
词性: adjective
