botulisms:/ˈbɒtʃʊlɪzəmz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtʃʊlɪzəmz/ (US)| n.|肉毒中毒|A serious and sometimes fatal illness caused by a toxin produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum.|例句：Botulisms can be a life-threatening condition. 肉毒中毒可能危及生命。

例句：The symptoms of botulisms can include blurred vision and difficulty swallowing. 肉毒中毒的症状可能包括视力模糊和吞咽困难。|近义词：food poisoning (in some cases) |反义词: (None -  it's a specific disease)|词性:noun
