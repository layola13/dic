gipsy:/ˈdʒɪpsi/ (US) /ˈdʒɪpsi/ (UK)| n. |吉普赛人；流浪者；骗子|A member of a traditionally nomadic people originating in northern India, now living mostly in Europe and North America.|The gipsy caravan travelled across the country. 吉普赛大篷车穿越了整个国家。|Synonyms: Roma, Romani, traveller, nomad|Antonyms: settler, resident|noun

gipsy:/ˈdʒɪpsi/ (US) /ˈdʒɪpsi/ (UK)| adj. |吉普赛的；流浪的|Relating to or characteristic of Gypsies or their way of life.|She had a gipsy soul, always yearning for new adventures. 她有一颗吉普赛人的灵魂，总是渴望新的冒险。|Synonyms: nomadic, wandering, itinerant|Antonyms: settled, sedentary|adjective
