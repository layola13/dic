non-essential:/nɒnɪˈsɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 非必要的；不重要的 | Not absolutely necessary; not essential. |  Many people lost their jobs in non-essential businesses during the pandemic.  许多人在疫情期间失去了非必要行业的工作。

non-essential:/nɒnɪˈsɛnʃəl/ | n. | 非必需品 | Something that is not essential. | Luxuries like expensive cars are considered non-essentials.  像昂贵的汽车这样的奢侈品被认为是非必需品。


Etymology->
From non- + essential.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "essential" comes from the Late Latin "essentialis," meaning "pertaining to the essence."

Synonyms->
unnecessary, dispensable, inessential, nonobligatory, optional

Antonyms->
essential, necessary, crucial, vital, indispensable
