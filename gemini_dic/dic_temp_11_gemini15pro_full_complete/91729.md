hyperdelicate:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɛlɪkət/| adj.|极其精细的，非常脆弱的|Extremely delicate; very fragile and easily damaged.|The hyperdelicate instruments required a steady hand and a controlled environment. 这些极其精密的仪器需要稳定的手和受控的环境。|The artist's hyperdelicate brushstrokes brought the painting to life. 艺术家极其精细的笔触使这幅画栩栩如生。|近义词：fragile, frail, sensitive, subtle, dainty|反义词：robust, sturdy, tough, resilient|词性:形容词
Etymology: From hyper- "over, excessive" + delicate. "hyper-" comes from Greek "hyper" (over, beyond). "delicate" comes from Latin "delicatus" (pleasant, delightful, dainty), past participle of "delicare" (to allure, entice).
