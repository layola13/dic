overrated:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪd/| adj.|被高估的；评价过高的|Regarded as better or more important than it actually is.|That movie was so overrated; the plot was predictable and the acting was terrible. 那部电影被高估了；情节可以预见，演技也很糟糕。|近义词：overestimated, overhyped, overvalued|反义词：underrated, undervalued|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (过度) + "rated" (评价).  "Rate" comes from Old French "rater"  meaning to assess or estimate.
