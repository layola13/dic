logjam:/ˈlɒɡdʒæm/ (US) /ˈlɒɡdʒæm/ (UK)| n.| 木材阻塞；僵局；停滞状态|A blockage or standstill, especially in business or traffic, caused by a large accumulation of logs in a river or waterway.  Figuratively, it refers to any situation where progress is stalled due to an overwhelming backlog or obstacle.|The heavy rains caused a logjam in the river, preventing boats from passing through.  大雨导致河道木材堵塞，船只无法通行。|Synonyms: blockage, bottleneck, deadlock, gridlock, impasse, standstill|Antonyms: breakthrough, progress, solution|Noun
