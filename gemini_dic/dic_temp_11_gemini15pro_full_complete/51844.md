dexterical:/dɛkˈstɛrɪkəl/
adjective
灵巧的，惯用右手的
Relating to or done with the right hand; skillful with the hands.
She displayed dexterical skill in playing the piano. 她展现了弹钢琴的灵巧技巧。
synonyms: deft, adroit, nimble, skillful, handy
antonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept, left-handed (specifically for hand dominance), unskilled
词性: adjective
