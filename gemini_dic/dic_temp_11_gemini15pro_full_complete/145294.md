overbravado:/ˌoʊvərbrəˈvɑːdoʊ/
|n.|过分虚张声势; 过分逞强|Excessive display of bravado; exaggerated boasting or swagger.|He showed a lot of overbravado, but inside he was terrified. 他表现得非常逞强，但内心却很害怕。|近义词：swagger, bluster, braggadocio|反义词：humility, modesty|词性: 名词


词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "bravado" (noun)

"over-" 表示 "过度", "超过"
"bravado" 源自意大利语 "bravata"，意思是 "bragging, boasting" (吹嘘，自夸)
