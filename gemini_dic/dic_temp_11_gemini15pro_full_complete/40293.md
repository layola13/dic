continuo:/kənˈtɪnjuoʊ/ /kənˈtɪnjuːəʊ/
n.|持续低音|A bass line played continuously throughout a piece to support the melody, characteristic of Baroque music.|The cello played the continuo throughout the sonata.|近义词:basso continuo, thoroughbass|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a musical technique)|词性:名词

continuo:/kənˈtɪnjuoʊ/ /kənˈtɪnjuːəʊ/
adj.|持续的|Played continuously throughout a piece, typically referring to a bass line.|The continuo part provided a harmonic foundation for the other instruments.|近义词:continuous, sustained|反义词:interrupted, broken|词性:形容词

词源:
From Italian *continuo*, from Latin *continuus* ("continuous").
