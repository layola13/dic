gracile:/ˈɡræsɪl, ˈɡreɪsɪl/ /ˈɡreɪsəl/| adj.|纤细的，优雅的|Slender and graceful.|例句：The ballerina's gracile movements captivated the audience.  芭蕾舞演员优雅的动作吸引了观众。

gracillent:/ˈɡræsɪlənt/ /ˈɡreɪsɪlənt/| adj.|非常纤细的，非常优雅的|Extremely slender and graceful; delicate.|例句：The artist drew gracillent figures in the margin of the manuscript.  艺术家在手稿的边缘画了非常纤细的人物。

例句：The gracillent branches of the willow tree swayed in the breeze. 柳树纤细的枝条在微风中摇曳。


近义词：very slender, extremely graceful, very delicate, ethereal|反义词：clumsy, awkward, ungainly, stout, robust|词性:形容词


词源：From Latin *gracillentus, diminutive of gracilis ("slender, thin").  来自拉丁语 *gracillentus，是 gracilis（“细长的，瘦的”）的指小词。
