larded:/ˈlɑːrdɪd/ | v. |点缀；加肥肉于；使丰富；润色 | To insert strips of fat into (lean meat) before cooking, typically to add moisture and flavour; to embellish or enrich something, often excessively. | The roast was larded with bacon. 烤肉中加入了培根。The speech was larded with technical jargon. 演讲中充斥着专业术语。| 近义词: enriched, embellished, interlarded, garnished | 反义词: plain, simple, unadorned | verb

larded:/ˈlɑːrdɪd/ | adj. |点缀的；加肥肉的 | Enriched or embellished, often excessively. | a larded prose style  一种华丽的散文风格 | 近义词: enriched, embellished, interlarded, garnished | 反义词: plain, simple, unadorned | adjective
