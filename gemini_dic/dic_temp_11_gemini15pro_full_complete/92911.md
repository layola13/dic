ideating:/aɪˈdiːeɪtɪŋ/
verb
形成想法；构思
the process of forming ideas
例句：The team is currently ideating on a new marketing campaign.  团队目前正在构思一个新的营销活动。

例句：We spent the afternoon ideating potential solutions to the problem. 我们一下午都在思考解决这个问题的潜在方案。

近义词：brainstorming, conceptualizing, generating ideas, innovating
反义词：procrastinating, delaying, stagnating
词性：Verb (present participle)
