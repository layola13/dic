agog:/əˈɡɒɡ/ (US) /əˈɡɑːɡ/ (UK)| adj.|兴奋的，急切的|Full of intense excitement and curiosity.|例句：The children were agog with excitement as they waited for Santa Claus. 孩子们兴奋地等待着圣诞老人。

例句：We were all agog to hear the latest news. 我们都急切地想听到最新消息。


|近义词：eager, excited, expectant, impatient, keen, thrilled|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, uninterested, bored|词性:形容词
