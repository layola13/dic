indelicately:/ˌɪnˈdɛlɪkɪtli/
adverb
不雅地，粗俗地
in a way that is not delicate or refined; coarsely or rudely.
He indelicately slurped his soup. 他粗鲁地喝着汤。
近义词:rudely, coarsely, crudely, unrefinedly, tastelessly
反义词:delicately, subtly, refinedly, tastefully
词性:adverb

indelicate:/ˌɪnˈdɛlɪkɪt/
adjective
不雅的，粗俗的
not delicate or refined; coarse or rude.
It was an indelicate question to ask. 这是一个粗鲁的问题。
近义词:rude, coarse, crude, unrefined, tasteless
反义词:delicate, subtle, refined, tasteful
词性:adjective
