nontannin:/nɑːnˈtænɪn/| n. adj.|非单宁|A substance in plant tissue, such as cellulose or lignin, that is not a tannin and is not extracted with the tannins.|The nontannin content of the bark was analyzed.  分析了树皮中的非单宁含量。|Near synonyms: non-tannin, non-tanning|Antonyms: tannin, tanning|noun, adjective

nontannin:/nɑːnˈtænɪn/| adj.|非单宁的|Not containing or derived from tannin.|Nontannin materials in tea contribute to its flavor. 茶叶中的非单宁物质有助于其风味。|Near synonyms: non-tannic|Antonyms: tannic|adjective
