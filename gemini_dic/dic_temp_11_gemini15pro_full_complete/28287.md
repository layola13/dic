capturer:/ˈkæptʃərər/| n.|捕获者，捕捉者|A person or thing that captures something or someone.|The photographer was a skilled capturer of fleeting moments.  这位摄影师是一位善于捕捉稍纵即逝瞬间的高手。|近义词：trapper, hunter, collector|反义词:liberator, releaser|noun

Etymology:
From the verb "capture," which comes from the Old French "capturer," and ultimately from the Latin "capere" (to take, seize).
