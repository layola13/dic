nontheosophical:/ˌnɒnˌθiːəˈsɒfɪkəl/
adj.
非神智学的
Not related to or concerned with theosophy (a system of esoteric religious thought based on a blend of philosophy, religion, and occultism).
His nontheosophical views on spirituality often clashed with his family's beliefs. 他非神智学的灵性观点经常与他家人的信仰冲突。
Her research focused on nontheosophical interpretations of ancient texts. 她的研究集中于对古代文献的非神智学解释。
近义词:non-esoteric, secular
反义词:theosophical
