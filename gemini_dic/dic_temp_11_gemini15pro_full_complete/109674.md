leucophlegmatic: adj.
leucophlegmatic: 冷淡的；迟钝的；冷漠的；粘液质的
leucophlegmatic:  Characterized by apathy and sluggishness, phlegmatic; having an excess of phlegm, as per the ancient theory of humors.  From Greek *leukophlegmatos*, from *leukos* (white) + *phlegma* (phlegm).
leucophlegmatic:  He had a leucophlegmatic temperament, rarely showing any strong emotion. 他性格冷淡，很少流露强烈的情感。
leucophlegmatic: 近义词：apathetic, listless, phlegmatic, indifferent
leucophlegmatic: 反义词：enthusiastic, passionate, energetic
