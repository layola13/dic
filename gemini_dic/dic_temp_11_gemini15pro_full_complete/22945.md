bottlenecked: /ˈbɒtlˌnekt/ (British) /ˈbɑːtlˌnekt/ (American)
bottlenecked: adj.
bottlenecked: 狭窄的，瓶颈的；受阻的
bottlenecked:  Constricted or obstructed, like the neck of a bottle; hindering smooth flow or progress.  Referring to a situation where progress is slowed or stopped because of a limited capacity at a particular point.
bottlenecked: Traffic was bottlenecked due to the accident.  由于事故，交通堵塞了。
bottlenecked: The production process is bottlenecked by the slow speed of one machine. 生产过程由于一台机器的速度慢而受阻。
bottlenecked: restricted, hampered, congested, obstructed, limited
bottlenecked: unimpeded, free-flowing, open, clear
