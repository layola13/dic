indentment:/ɪnˈdɛntmənt/| n.|缩进；凹陷；订货单|An indentment is a space left at the beginning of a line of writing or printing. It can also refer to an official order for goods. |The indentment at the beginning of each paragraph made the text easier to read. 每段开头的缩进使文本更易于阅读。

The company sent an indentment for 1000 units of the product.  公司发出了1000件产品的订货单。|近义词：indentation, recession, order|反义词:projection, protrusion|noun
