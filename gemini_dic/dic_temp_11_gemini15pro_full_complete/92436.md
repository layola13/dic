hyponyms:/ˈhaɪpənɪmz/| n. |下义词|Words that are more specific than a given word; a word whose meaning is included in the meaning of a more general word. For example, "spoon," "fork," and "knife" are hyponyms of "cutlery."|Example: "Red," "blue," and "green" are hyponyms of "color." 红色、蓝色和绿色是“颜色”的下义词。
Example: "Cat," "dog," and "horse" are hyponyms of "animal." 猫、狗和马是“动物”的下义词。|近义词: subordinate terms|反义词: hypernyms|词性:noun
