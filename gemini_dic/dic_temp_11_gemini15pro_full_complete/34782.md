climatically:/klaɪˈmætɪkli/
adverb
气候上地
In a way that relates to climate.
例句：The region is climatically diverse, with both arid deserts and lush rainforests.  这个地区气候多样，既有干旱的沙漠，也有茂密的雨林。

例句：Climatically, the area is not suitable for growing grapes.  从气候上讲，这个地区不适合种植葡萄。
近义词：meteorologically, environmentally
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, consider phrases like "non-climatically" or focusing on specific contrasting conditions like "geographically")
词性: adverb
