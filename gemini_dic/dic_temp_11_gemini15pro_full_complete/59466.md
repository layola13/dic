eased:/iːzd/
|v.|缓和，减轻，放松|Made less severe, less painful, or less difficult. |The medicine eased his pain.  这药减轻了他的疼痛。|Synonyms: alleviated, mitigated, relieved, lessened, diminished|Antonyms: intensified, aggravated, worsened, exacerbated|verb

eased:/iːzd/
|adj.|感到舒适的，放松的|Feeling relaxed and comfortable. |He looked eased after the massage. 他按摩后看起来很放松。|Synonyms: relaxed, comfortable, relieved, at ease|Antonyms: tense, stressed, anxious, uneasy|adjective
