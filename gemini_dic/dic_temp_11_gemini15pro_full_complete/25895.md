burl:/bɜːrl/| n.|节疤，树瘤；（尤指羊毛上的）毛球|A burl is a rounded outgrowth on the trunk or branch of a tree.|The woodworker prized the burl for its intricate grain patterns. 木工珍视这种树瘤，因为它有复杂的纹理。|Synonyms: knot, gnarl, outgrowth|Antonyms: smooth patch|noun

burl:/bɜːrl/| v.|去除（毛球）|To remove burls (small knots or lumps) from fabric, especially wool.|She carefully burled the sweater before putting it away. 她小心翼翼地去除了毛衣上的毛球才把它收起来。|Synonyms: pick, remove, clean|Antonyms: attach, add|verb
