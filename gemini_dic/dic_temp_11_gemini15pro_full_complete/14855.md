axogenic:/ˌæksəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adj.
轴突发生的，轴突生成的
Relating to or stimulating the growth of axons.  Axons are the long, slender projections of nerve cells that conduct electrical impulses away from the neuron's cell body.  Therefore, something that is axogenic promotes or facilitates the growth or development of these axons.
Axogenic factors play a crucial role in nerve regeneration after injury. 轴突生成因子在损伤后的神经再生中起着至关重要的作用。
The researchers discovered a new axogenic compound that could potentially treat spinal cord injuries. 研究人员发现了一种新的轴突生成化合物，可能用于治疗脊髓损伤。
近义词: axon-promoting, neurotrophic
反义词:  axon-inhibiting
