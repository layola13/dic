novilunar:/ˌnoʊvɪˈluːnər/ | adj. | 新月形的；朔望月的 | Relating to the new moon, or the period of the new moon.  The novilunar cycle is approximately 29.5 days long. | The ancient Druids held ceremonies during the novilunar phase.  古代德鲁伊教徒在新月期间举行仪式。 | synonyms: crescent, lunar | antonyms: full moon | adjective
