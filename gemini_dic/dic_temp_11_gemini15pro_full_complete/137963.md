nonwork:/ˈnɑːnˌwɜːrk/ | n. | 非工作时间，非工作活动 | Time or activities not related to one's job or profession |  I spend most of my nonwork time reading. 我大部分的非工作时间都用来阅读了。|Synonyms: leisure, free time, downtime | Antonyms: work, job, labor | noun

nonwork:/ˈnɑːnˌwɜːrk/ | adj. | 非工作的 | Not related to one's job or profession |  She enjoys her nonwork activities, like painting and hiking. 她喜欢她的非工作活动，例如绘画和徒步旅行。|Synonyms:  off-duty, leisure, recreational | Antonyms: work-related, professional, occupational | adjective
