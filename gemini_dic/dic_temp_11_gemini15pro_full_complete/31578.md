chasmiest:/ˈtʃæzmiɪst/ | adj. |  最深不可测的，最难以理解的 |  Superlative form of "chasmlike," meaning resembling a chasm, especially in depth or impenetrability.  It describes something very deep, profound, or difficult to understand. |  His chasmiest thoughts were a mystery even to himself.  他最深邃的思想即使对他自己来说也是一个谜。 |  例句：Her chasmiest expressions hinted at a hidden world of emotion. 她最深不可测的表情暗示了一个隐藏的情感世界。 | 近义词：profound, abysmal, unfathomable, inscrutable | 反义词：shallow, superficial, transparent, obvious | 词性：形容词

词源:  From "chasm" (from Greek *χάσμα (khásma), from χαίνω (khaínō, “I gape, yawn”)) + "-like" + "-est" (superlative suffix).
