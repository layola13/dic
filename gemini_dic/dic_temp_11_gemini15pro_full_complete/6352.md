amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. |两栖动物，两栖的|An animal, such as a frog or toad, that can live both on land and in water.  Relating to or characteristic of an amphibian.|例句：The frog is an amphibian. 青蛙是两栖动物。|例句：Amphibian aircraft can land on both water and land. 水陆两用飞机既能在水上着陆也能在陆地着陆。|近义词：frog, toad, newt, salamander (n.)|反义词:mammal, reptile, bird (n.)|词性: noun, adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin amphibius, from Greek amphibios, from amphi "both, on both sides" + bios "life".
