diptera:/ˈdɪptərə/ (US), /ˈdɪptərə/ (UK)| n. |双翅目昆虫|An order of insects having only two wings, including flies, mosquitoes, gnats, and midges.  These insects typically have a single pair of membranous wings for flight and a pair of halteres, which are modified hindwings that act as balancing organs.|例句：Diptera are a large order of insects. 双翅目是一个很大的昆虫目。|例句：Many Diptera are vectors of disease. 许多双翅目昆虫是疾病的传播媒介。|近义词：fly (informal, for some species)|反义词: None (as it's a taxonomic classification)|词性:Noun
