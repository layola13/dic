kopeck:/ˈkoʊpɛk/| n.|戈比（俄罗斯货币单位）|A kopeck is a unit of currency in Russia, equal to one hundredth of a ruble. |例句：He found a kopeck on the street. 他在街上发现了一戈比。

例句：A loaf of bread cost several kopecks.  一个面包要几戈比。|近义词:cent, penny|反义词:ruble (in a relative sense - as a larger unit)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian копейка (kopejka), from копьё (kopʹjó, “spear”), referring to the depiction of Saint George slaying a dragon on the coin.
