disconfidence:/dɪsˈkɒnfɪdəns/| n. |不信任，缺乏信心|Lack of confidence; distrust.|He expressed disconfidence in the government's ability to handle the crisis. 他对政府处理危机能力表示不信任。|近义词：distrust, mistrust, suspicion, doubt|反义词：confidence, trust, faith|词性:名词
Etymology: dis- (expressing negation) + confidence (from Latin confidentia, from confidentem, present participle of confidere "to be confident," from com- "with" + fidere "to trust")
