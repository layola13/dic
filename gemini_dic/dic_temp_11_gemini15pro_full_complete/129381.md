nomarchy: /ˈnɒmɑːki/ | n. | 省长管辖区；省的统治 | The area of jurisdiction of a nomarch, or governor of a nome in modern Greece, or ancient Egypt.  A system of government by nomarchs. |  The nomarchy of Thebes was particularly prosperous during the New Kingdom. 底比斯省在新王国时期尤其繁荣。|  The nomarchy faced several challenges due to the Nile's fluctuating water levels.  这个省由于尼罗河水位波动而面临若干挑战。| 近义词: province, prefecture, district | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek νομαρχία (nomarkhía), from νομός (nomós, “district, province”) +‎ -αρχία (-arkhía, “rule, command”). Ultimately from νέμω (némō, “I distribute, allot”).
