bagman:/ˈbæɡmən/| n. |提包人；（尤指）为组织收取或分发非法资金的人；|A person, especially one who collects or distributes illicit funds for an organization. |The bagman delivered the payoff to the corrupt official. 提包人把酬金送给了腐败的官员。|Synonyms: courier, messenger, go-between, intermediary|Antonyms: law enforcement officer, police, investigator|noun
