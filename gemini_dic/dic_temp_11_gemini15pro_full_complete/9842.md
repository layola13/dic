USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for breakfast? = 她早餐想吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they doing in the garden? = 他们在花园里做什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她怎么处理那笔钱了？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading all afternoon? = 她整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她婚礼上穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for lunch? = 他们午饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before you called me? = 你打电话给我之前一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? = 他们几个小时以来一直在讨论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? = 到2033年，你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to say. = 我希望我知道该说什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He didn't tell me what happened. = 他没有告诉我发生了什么。
