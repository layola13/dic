in-respect:/ɪn rɪˈspekt/ | prep. | 关于，就…而言 |  Concerning; with regard to.  Relating to a particular aspect or detail of something.  | Example 1: In respect to your request, we have decided to grant it. 就你的请求而言，我们决定批准。

Example 2:  The two cars are similar in respect to size and fuel efficiency. 这两辆车在尺寸和燃油效率方面相似。 | 近义词: regarding, concerning, with respect to, in regard to, as regards, pertaining to | 反义词: irrespective of, regardless of | 词性: 介词


词源:  From Middle English *in respect*, from in + respect (noun), from Old French *respect* and directly from Latin *respectus* (“regard, consideration”).
