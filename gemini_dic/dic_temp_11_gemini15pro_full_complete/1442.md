ace-up-their-sleeve:/ˌeɪs ʌp ðɛər ˈsliːv/
ace-up-their-sleeve: idiom
ace-up-their-sleeve: 秘密武器；锦囊妙计；藏有绝招
ace-up-their-sleeve:  A secret advantage or resource that can be used when needed.  It refers to a playing card, an ace, hidden in someone's sleeve, ready to be used to win a game dishonestly.  Figuratively, it means a hidden plan or advantage that someone keeps secret until the right moment.
ace-up-their-sleeve: He had an ace up his sleeve - a surprise witness who could corroborate his story. 他有一个秘密武器——一个可以证实他故事的意外证人。

ace-up-their-sleeve: She knew she had an ace up her sleeve going into the negotiation. 她知道自己在谈判中有一个秘密武器。

ace-up-their-sleeve: 近义词: secret weapon, hidden advantage, trump card
ace-up-their-sleeve: 反义词: disadvantage, weakness
ace-up-their-sleeve: 词性 idiom
