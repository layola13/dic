contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ (UK) /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/ (US)| adj.|矛盾的，对立的，不一致的|Containing or expressing a contradiction; mutually opposed or inconsistent.|The witness gave contradictory statements to the police.  证人向警方提供了相互矛盾的陈述。|Synonyms: conflicting, opposing, inconsistent, incompatible, clashing, antithetical|Antonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious, agreeing, concordant|adjective

contradictional:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkʃənəl/ (UK) /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkʃənəl/ (US)| adj.|矛盾的，对立的，不一致的|Containing or involving a contradiction; inconsistent.|His account of the events was highly contradictory. 他对事件的描述非常矛盾。|Synonyms: conflicting, opposing, inconsistent, incompatible, clashing, antithetical|Antonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious, agreeing, concordant|adjective
