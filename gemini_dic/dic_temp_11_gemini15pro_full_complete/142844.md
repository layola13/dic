out-fences: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnsɪz/ | v. | 超越，胜过，击剑胜过 | To surpass or defeat someone in fencing, or more generally, to outdo or outperform someone in any competitive activity. | He out-fenced his opponent in the final match and won the gold medal. 他在决赛中击剑胜过对手，赢得了金牌。 | She out-fenced all the other candidates and got the job. 她胜过所有其他候选人，得到了这份工作。 | 近义词: outdo, outperform, surpass, excel, beat | 反义词: lose to, be defeated by | 词性: 动词
