holophrastic:/ˌhɑːləˈfræstɪk/ | adj. | 单词句的，一言句的 | Expressing a complex of ideas in a single word; pertaining to the use of single words to express complex ideas. |  Example: A baby's utterance of "mama" can be holophrastic, expressing a range of meanings from "I want my mother" to "Where is my mother?".  婴儿说“妈妈”可以是一个单词句，表达的意思从“我想要妈妈”到“妈妈在哪里？”都有可能。| Synonym: one-word, single-word | Antonym: multi-word, polysyllabic | word class: adjective
