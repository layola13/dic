balaniferous: /ˌbælənɪfərəs/
adjective
产橡实的；多橡实的
Bearing acorns; producing acorns.
The balaniferous oak tree provided ample food for the squirrels.  这棵结满橡实的橡树为松鼠提供了充足的食物。
The forest was dense and balaniferous, perfect for the wild boar. 这片森林茂密且多橡果，非常适合野猪栖息。
近义词: acorn-bearing, nut-bearing
反义词: barren, infertile
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *balanifer, from balanus (“acorn”) + -fer (“bearing”).
