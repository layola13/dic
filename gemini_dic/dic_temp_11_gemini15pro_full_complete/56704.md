dooming:/ˈduːmɪŋ/| adj.| 注定的，劫数难逃的；厄运的 |Condemning to a disastrous fate; causing ruin or failure.  A sense of impending doom. |The dark clouds seemed dooming for the picnic.  乌云密布，看来野餐要泡汤了。| synonyms: damning, condemning, fated, ill-fated, disastrous, catastrophic, ruinous, fatal, lethal, destructive, devastating, calamitous|antonyms: promising, auspicious, encouraging, hopeful, propitious| adjective
