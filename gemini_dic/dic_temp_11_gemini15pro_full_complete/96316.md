indigestibles:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstəblz/ | n. | 难以消化的事物；难理解的事物 | Something that is difficult or impossible to digest, either physically or mentally. |  Many mushrooms are indigestibles for some people. 许多蘑菇对某些人来说难以消化。| synonyms: inedible, unpalatable, unassimilable | antonyms: digestible, palatable, assimilable | noun

indigestible:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstəbl/ | adj. | 难以消化的；难理解的 | Difficult or impossible to digest, either physically or mentally. | The steak was tough and indigestible. 牛排很硬，难以消化。| synonyms: inedible, unpalatable, unassimilable | antonyms: digestible, palatable, assimilable | adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *indigestibilis, from in- "not" + digestibilis "digestible," from the past participle stem of digerere "to digest," from dis- "apart, thoroughly" + gerere "to carry, bear, manage."
