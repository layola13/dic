bouse:/baʊs/| v. n.| 豪饮，狂饮；痛饮；酒宴 |To drink large quantities of alcohol; a drinking bout.  A carouse.|(v.) He boused all night and woke up with a terrible hangover. 他整晚狂饮，醒来后宿醉得厉害。|(n.) They went on a three-day bouse after receiving their bonuses.  他们拿到奖金后开始了为期三天的狂欢痛饮。|近义词：drink heavily, carouse, binge, guzzle|反义词: abstain, be temperate|词性:动词,名词
词源: From Middle Dutch *būsen, from būse (“drinking vessel, pot, jug”). Cognate with German Bauche (“belly, paunch”).
