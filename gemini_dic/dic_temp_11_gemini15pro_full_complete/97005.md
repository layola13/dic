infarction:/ɪnˈfɑːrkʃən/ /ɪnˈfɑːrkʃn/ | n. |梗塞，梗死|The obstruction of the blood supply to an organ or region of tissue, typically by a thrombus or embolus, causing local death of the tissue.| He suffered a myocardial infarction. 他患有心肌梗塞。| 近义词：blockage, obstruction, occlusion | 反义词: perfusion | 词性: 名词
