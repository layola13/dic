dysphoric:/dɪsˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 难受的，烦躁不安的 | Characterized by dysphoria, a state of unease or generalized dissatisfaction with life. |  She felt dysphoric and couldn't shake the feeling of impending doom. 她感到烦躁不安，无法摆脱即将到来的厄运感。| Example: After the surgery, he experienced a period of dysphoria and struggled with his new identity.  手术后，他经历了一段烦躁不安的时期，并与他的新身份作斗争。 |  Synonyms: uneasy, anxious, depressed, melancholic, irritable | Antonyms: euphoric, happy, content, comfortable | adjective
