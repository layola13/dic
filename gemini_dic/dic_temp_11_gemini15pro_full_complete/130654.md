nonadvantageous:/ˌnɒnˌædvənˈteɪdʒəs/
adjective
无优势的，不利的
Not advantageous; unfavorable or unhelpful.
The nonadvantageous position of their troops led to a swift defeat.  他们的军队处于不利位置，导致迅速溃败。
近义词: disadvantageous, unfavorable, detrimental, unhelpful
反义词: advantageous, favorable, beneficial, helpful
词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "advantageous" (from "advantage" - ultimately from Latin "abante" meaning "from before, in front")
