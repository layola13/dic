inflatable:/ɪnˈfleɪtəbl/ | adj. n. | 膨胀的，可充气的；可充气物 | Capable of being inflated with air or gas; something that can be inflated. | The inflatable boat was easy to pack and carry.  这艘充气船很容易打包携带。| synonyms: blow-up, expansible | antonyms: rigid, solid | adjective

inflatable:/ɪnˈfleɪtəbl/ | n. | 充气物 | An object that can be inflated. |  We bought an inflatable mattress for the camping trip. 我们为露营旅行买了一个充气床垫。 | synonyms: blow-up, airbed | antonyms: N/A | noun
