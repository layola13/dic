counter-title:/ˈkaʊntərˌtaɪtl/ | n. | 副标题，对题，反题 | A subordinate or contrasting title; a title set against another title. |  The book had the main title "Love and Loss," with the counter-title "A Journey Through Grief." 这本书的主标题是“爱与失去”，副标题是“穿越悲伤的旅程”。 | synonym: subtitle, alternative title | antonym: main title, primary title | noun


counter-title:/ˈkaʊntərˌtaɪtl/ | v. | 加上副标题，起反题 | To give a counter-title to. |  They decided to counter-title the film with a more provocative phrase. 他们决定给这部电影加上一个更具煽动性的副标题。 | synonym: subtitle | antonym:  (No direct antonym for the verb form) | verb
