confess:/kənˈfɛs/ (US), /kənˈfɛs/ (UK)| v. |承认，坦白，忏悔|To admit or acknowledge something, typically something wrong or embarrassing.|He confessed to stealing the money. 他承认偷了钱。

She confessed her love for him. 她坦白了她对他的爱。|Synonyms: admit, acknowledge, own up, reveal, divulge|Antonyms: deny, conceal, hide|verb

PHRASE->
confess to (doing) something: 承认(做了)某事  He confessed to stealing the car. 他承认偷了车。
confess that: 承认…  She confessed that she had lied. 她承认她撒谎了。
confess one's sins: 忏悔罪过  He went to church to confess his sins. 他去教堂忏悔他的罪过。
confess all: 全盘招供  The suspect eventually confessed all. 嫌疑人最终全盘招供。
confess love for someone: 向某人表白爱意 She confessed her love for him. 她向他表白了爱意。
