flanged:/flændʒd/
adjective
法兰的；带凸缘的
Having a flange or flanges.
The pipe was flanged to connect to the valve.  这个管道用法兰连接到阀门上。
近义词: rimmed, edged
反义词: unflanged
词性: adjective

flange:/flændʒ/
noun
凸缘；轮缘；法兰
A projecting flat rim, collar, or rib on an object, serving to strengthen or attach or (on a wheel) to maintain position on a rail.
The flange of the wheel kept it on the track.  车轮的凸缘使其保持在轨道上。
近义词: rim, edge, lip
反义词: none
词性: noun
