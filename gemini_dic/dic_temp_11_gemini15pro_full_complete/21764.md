boatnecked:/ˈboʊtˌnekt/| adj.|船领的|Having a wide, open neckline resembling the shape of a boat's hull. This neckline typically follows a curved line from shoulder to shoulder, exposing the collarbone and often part of the upper chest.|She wore a boatnecked sweater that accentuated her elegant shoulders. 她穿了一件船领毛衣，突出了她优雅的肩膀。|The dress had a flattering boatnecked design. 这条裙子采用了讨人喜欢的船领设计。|近义词：scoop-necked, wide-necked|反义词：high-necked, turtleneck|词性:adjective

词源:
boat + neck + -ed.  "boat" referring to the shape of a small boat's open top, "neck" referring to the part of a garment around the neck, and "-ed" forming an adjective.
