investigative: /ɪnˈvɛstɪɡətɪv/ | adj. | 研究的，调查的 |  Relating to or involved in investigation.  Characterized by a tendency to investigate; inquisitive. | The journalist's investigative work uncovered a major political scandal.  这位记者的调查工作揭露了一起重大的政治丑闻。| synonyms: inquiring, searching, probing, analytical, curious | antonyms: incurious, uninquiring, superficial | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin investigativus, from investigare "to track, trace out," from in- "in, into" + vestigare "to track, trace," from vestigium "footprint, track."
