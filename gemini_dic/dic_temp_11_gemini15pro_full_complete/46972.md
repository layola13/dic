dartos:/ˈdɑːrtɒs/ (US) /ˈdɑːtɒs/ (UK)| n. | 肉膜 | A thin layer of smooth muscle beneath the skin of the scrotum. It is responsible for the wrinkled appearance of the scrotum and helps to regulate the temperature of the testes. | The dartos muscle contracts in cold temperatures, drawing the scrotum closer to the body to conserve heat. | 近义词:  tunica dartos | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek δαρτός (dartós, “flayed, skinned”), from δέρειν (dérein, “to flay”).
