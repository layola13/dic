annoyful:/əˈnɔɪfʊl/
adjective
令人讨厌的；恼人的

Her annoyful habit of tapping her pen drove me crazy. 她不停地敲笔的恼人习惯快把我逼疯了。
annoying, irritating, vexing, bothersome
pleasant, agreeable, delightful
