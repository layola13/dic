barde:/bɑːrd/ | n. |吟游诗人|A poet, traditionally one reciting epics and associated with a particular oral tradition.|例句：The barde sang of heroic deeds and ancient legends. 吟游诗人歌颂英雄事迹和古老的传说。

例句：The wandering barde captivated the audience with his tales.  这位流浪的吟游诗人用他的故事吸引了观众。|近义词：poet, minstrel, troubadour |反义词: None (no direct opposite) |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *barde, from Gaulish *bardos.  Cognate with Irish bárd, Welsh bardd. Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *bardos.
