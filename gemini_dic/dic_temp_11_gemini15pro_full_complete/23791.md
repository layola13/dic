brawny: /ˈbrɔːni/ | adj. | 健壮的，强壮的 | Physically strong and muscular. |  He was a brawny blacksmith with powerful arms. 他是一位强壮的铁匠，有着强健的臂膀。|  muscular, strong, powerful, burly, sturdy | weak, frail, feeble | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *brauny, from Old English *brāwn, from Proto-Germanic *brūnaz (“brown”). More at brown. The original sense was "brown-haired or brown-skinned," later shifting to "muscular, strong," perhaps due to the association of brown with the tanned skin of outdoor laborers.
