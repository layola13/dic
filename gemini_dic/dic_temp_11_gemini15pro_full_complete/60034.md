educated:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd/| adj.|受过教育的；有教养的|Having undergone education; possessing knowledge or skills acquired through learning. | She is a highly educated woman with multiple degrees. 她是一位受过高等教育的女性，拥有多个学位。| He was educated at Eton and Oxford. 他在伊顿公学和牛津大学接受教育。 |近义词：learned, knowledgeable, cultured, schooled, literate|反义词：uneducated, ignorant, illiterate|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin "educatus," past participle of "educare" (to bring up, rear, educate), from "ex-" (out) + "ducere" (to lead).
