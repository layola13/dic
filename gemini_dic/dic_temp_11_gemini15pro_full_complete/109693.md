leukoplast: /ˈluːkəˌplæst/ | n. | 白色体；无色体 | A colorless plastid in plant cells, commonly found in roots and underground stems, that stores starch. |  Leukoplasts are essential for storing starch in plant cells. 白色体对植物细胞储存淀粉至关重要。

Leukoplasts are different from chloroplasts, which contain chlorophyll. 白色体不同于含有叶绿素的叶绿体。| 近义词: amyloplast (specifically for starch storage), plastid | 反义词: chloroplast, chromoplast | 词性: noun
