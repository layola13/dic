marlite:/ˈmɑːrlaɪt/
noun
玛丽板，一种装饰性墙板
A hard, smooth, usually plastic-finished wall panel, often used in bathrooms and kitchens.
The bathroom walls were covered in marlite. 浴室的墙壁上覆盖着玛丽板。
近义词: wall panel, wallboard, paneling
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with materials like tile, wallpaper)
词性: n.
