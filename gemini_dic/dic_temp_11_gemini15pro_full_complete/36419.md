词源:  "cokier" is a comparative adjective derived from the noun "cookie." "Cookie" itself comes from the Dutch word "koekje," meaning "little cake." The "-ier" suffix is used to form the comparative degree of adjectives, indicating a greater degree of the quality described by the adjective.
