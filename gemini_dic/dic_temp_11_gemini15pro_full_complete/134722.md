nonmaturities:/ˌnɒnməˈtʃʊərətiz/ | n. | 未到期债务，短期债务 | Liabilities or debts that are not yet due or mature.  These are often short-term obligations. | The company's nonmaturities increased significantly this quarter, indicating a potential cash flow issue in the near future. 该公司本季度未到期债务大幅增加，表明近期可能出现现金流问题。| Example 2:  Investors are increasingly concerned about the company's ability to manage its growing nonmaturities. 投资者越来越担心该公司管理其不断增长的短期债务的能力。 | Synonym: short-term liabilities, current liabilities | Antonym: long-term liabilities, long-term debt | noun
