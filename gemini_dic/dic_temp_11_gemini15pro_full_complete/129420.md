nominatival: /ˌnɒmɪˈneɪtɪvəl/
adjective|主格的，格位的|Relating to or belonging to the nominative case.|The nominatival form of the noun is used as the subject of the verb.|近义词: subjective|反义词: objective|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *nominativus*, from Latin nominare (“to name”), from nomen (“name”).
