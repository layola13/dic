grovel:/ˈɡrɒvəl/ (UK), /ˈɡrɑːvəl/ (US)| vi.| 卑躬屈膝，匍匐，奴颜婢膝|To behave in a way that shows you are very eager to please someone, especially someone who is more important than you, usually in a way that is not sincere or respectful.| He groveled at his boss's feet, begging for forgiveness. 他卑躬屈膝地跪在老板的脚下，乞求原谅。|近义词：crawl, fawn, cringe|反义词：stand up to, defy|词性：动词

grovel:/ˈɡrɒvəl/ (UK), /ˈɡrɑːvəl/ (US)| n.| 卑躬屈膝，匍匐，奴颜婢膝（的行为）|The act of groveling.| His constant groveling was embarrassing to watch.  他不停地卑躬屈膝，让人看着尴尬。|近义词：fawning, subservience|反义词：defiance, resistance|词性：名词
