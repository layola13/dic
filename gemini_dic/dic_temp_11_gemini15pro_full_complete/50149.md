denunciation:/dɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |谴责，告发|  The act of publicly condemning something as wrong or evil.  A public statement that strongly criticizes someone or something. | The government issued a denunciation of the terrorist attacks. 政府发表声明谴责恐怖袭击。| The witness's denunciation of the suspect led to his arrest.  证人对嫌疑人的告发导致了他的被捕。| synonyms: condemnation, accusation, indictment, censure, reprimand | antonyms: praise, commendation, approval, endorsement | noun

denounce:/dɪˈnaʊns/ | v. |谴责，告发| To publicly declare that something or someone is wrong or evil. To inform against someone. |  He denounced the government's policies as discriminatory. 他谴责政府的政策具有歧视性。 | She denounced her neighbor to the police for drug dealing. 她向警方举报她的邻居贩毒。 | synonyms: condemn, criticize, attack, censure, reprimand | antonyms: praise, commend, approve, endorse | verb
