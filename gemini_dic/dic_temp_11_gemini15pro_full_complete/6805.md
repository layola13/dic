anarchal: /ˈænərkəl/ (US), /ˈænɑːkɪkəl/ (UK)| adj.|无政府的，混乱的|Characterized by a lack of government or authority and often by social disorder and chaos.|The country descended into an anarchal state after the coup. 政变后，这个国家陷入了无政府状态。|The classroom was anarchal without a teacher. 没有老师，教室里一片混乱。|近义词: lawless, chaotic, unruly, disordered|反义词: orderly, organized, lawful, governed|词性: adjective

Etymology: From the Greek *anarkhos*, meaning "without a ruler," from *an-* ("without") + *arkhos* ("ruler").
