antidotal:/ˌæntiˈdəʊtl/ (UK) /ˌæntiˈdoʊtl/ (US)| adj.|解毒的；抵消的；轶事性质的|Used to counteract something unpleasant or unwanted; of the nature of an anecdote.|例句：There is much anecdotal evidence about the dangers of this new drug. 有很多关于这种新药危险性的轶事证据。|例句：He offered anecdotal evidence of the effectiveness of the treatment. 他提供了轶事证据证明治疗的有效性。|近义词：counteractive, remedial, corrective, narrative, story-like|反义词：harmful, exacerbating, analytical, statistical|词性：形容词
