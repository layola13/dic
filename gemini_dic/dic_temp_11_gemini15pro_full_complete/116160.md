mascotry: /ˈmæskətri/ | n. | 吉祥物行为，吉祥物文化; 吉祥物身份 | The act of serving as a mascot; the state or condition of being a mascot.  Mascotry is a relatively new area of study in sports marketing. |  The university's increasing emphasis on mascotry has led to the creation of a dedicated mascot team. 大学越来越重视吉祥物文化，促成了专门的吉祥物团队的建立。 | 近义词: mascot representation, mascot activities | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "anti-mascot sentiment") | 词性: noun


Etymology:  Derived from "mascot" + "-ry".  "Mascot" comes from French "mascotte," meaning "lucky charm," originally from Provençal "mascoto," a diminutive of "masco," meaning "witch" or "sorcerer."
