congressional:/kənˈɡrɛʃənəl/| adj.|国会的，议会的|Relating to a congress, especially the US Congress.  Pertaining to or emanating from a congress.|例句：Congressional hearings on the matter are scheduled for next week.  关于此事，国会听证会定于下周举行。

例句：The congressional budget process is complex and often contentious. 国会预算过程复杂且经常引起争议。|近义词：legislative, parliamentary|反义词:executive, judicial|词性:形容词
