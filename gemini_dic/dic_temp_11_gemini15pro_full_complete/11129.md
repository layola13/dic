ETYMOLOGY->
From archduke + -dom.  "Archduke" comes from the German "Erzherzog," a compound of "erz-" (meaning "chief" or "principal") and "Herzog" (meaning "duke").  "-dom" is a suffix of Germanic origin meaning "state, condition, or domain."
