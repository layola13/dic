boonest: /ˈbuːnɪst/ | adj. | 最有益的，最有利的 |  Superlative form of *boon* meaning "most beneficial" or "most helpful." This word is archaic or dialectal and rarely used in modern English. | The boonest thing that happened to me was meeting my wife.  遇见我的妻子是我遇到的最有益的事情。 | 近义词：most beneficial, most advantageous, most helpful | 反义词：most harmful, most detrimental, least helpful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *bonest*, superlative of *bon* ("good, beneficial"), from Old Norse *bón* ("prayer, request, boon").
