mamma:/ˈmɑːmə/ (US), /ˈmæmə/ (UK)| n.|妈妈；乳房|A term of endearment for mother, or referring to breasts, especially in a child's language. |例句：The little girl called out for her mamma. 小女孩喊着要妈妈。

|例句：The baby wanted its mamma's milk. 婴儿想要妈妈的奶。|近义词：mom, mommy, mother, breast (when referring to the anatomical part)|反义词: (No direct antonym, context-dependent) |词性:noun
