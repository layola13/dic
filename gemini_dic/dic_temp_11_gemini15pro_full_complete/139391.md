observant:/əbˈzɜːrvənt/| adj.|善于观察的；机警的；细心的|Paying close attention to details and surroundings; quick to notice things.|例句：She is an observant child and noticed the small details that others missed. 她是一个善于观察的孩子，注意到别人忽略的小细节。

例句：An observant police officer noticed the suspect's nervousness. 一位观察敏锐的警官注意到了嫌疑人的紧张情绪。|近义词：perceptive, astute, attentive, watchful, vigilant|反义词：inattentive, unobservant, oblivious, unaware|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *observaunt*, from Old French *observant*, present participle of *observer* "to observe," from Latin *observāre* "to watch, observe," from *ob-* "toward" + *servāre* "to watch, keep, guard."
