boilingly:/ˈbɔɪlɪŋli/
adverb
滚烫地，沸腾地，激情澎湃地
in a boiling manner; with intense emotion or passion.
She spoke boilingly about the injustices she had suffered. 她激情澎湃地讲述了她所遭受的不公正待遇。
The water in the pot was boilingly hot. 锅里的水滚烫。
近义词:furiously, passionately, intensely, heatedly
反义词:calmly, coolly, placidly
词性:adverb

词源:From boil + -ing + -ly.  The root "boil" comes from the Old French "boillir," ultimately from Latin "bullire" meaning "to bubble."
