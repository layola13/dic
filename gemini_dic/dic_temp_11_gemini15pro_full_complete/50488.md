depressor:/dɪˈprɛsər/| n.|抑制器，减压器，降肌|A muscle that lowers a part of the body. depressor:/dɪˈprɛsər/| n.|镇静剂，抗抑郁药|A drug that reduces functional activity or a medication used to treat depression.|The patient was prescribed a depressor to help manage their anxiety.  病人被开了镇静剂来帮助控制他们的焦虑。|synonyms: tranquilizer, sedative, antidepressant|antonyms: stimulant, upper|noun
