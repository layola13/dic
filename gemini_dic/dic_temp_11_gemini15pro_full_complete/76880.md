gangrenescent:/ˌɡæŋɡrɪˈnɛsənt/
adjective
坏疽的；腐败的
becoming gangrenous; showing signs of gangrene.  (变得坏疽的；显示出坏疽的迹象)
The wound on his leg became gangrenescent. 他腿上的伤口开始腐烂。
Near synonyms:  gangrenous, putrid, necrotic, decaying, mortifying
Antonyms: healthy, vibrant, vital, flourishing
词性: adjective
