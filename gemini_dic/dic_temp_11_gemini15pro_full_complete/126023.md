musselled:/ˈmʌsəld/ | adj. | 长满贻贝的；像贻贝的 | Covered with or resembling mussels. | The rocks were musselled with a thick layer of the shellfish.  岩石上长满了厚厚一层贻贝。| 近义词: shellfish-covered, barnacled | 反义词: bare, clean | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From mussel + -ed.  "Mussel" comes from Middle Dutch "mossele," ultimately from Latin "musculus" (little mouse), due to the resemblance of the bivalve's shape to a mouse.
