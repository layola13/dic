heathy:/ˈhɛði/ | adj. |健康的，健壮的|Characterized by good health and physical condition; free from disease or illness.  Relating to or promoting good health. | She has a heathy appetite. 她胃口很好。|  He maintains a heathy lifestyle. 他保持着健康的生活方式。| 近义词:healthy, well, robust, fit, sound | 反义词:unhealthy, sick, ill, frail, weak |词性:形容词

**词源:**

Likely a misspelling of "healthy," from Old English *hǣlþ* (health).
