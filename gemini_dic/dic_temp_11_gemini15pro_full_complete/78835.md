gimmicks:/ˈɡɪmɪks/| n. | 花招，噱头，小玩意儿 | A trick or device intended to attract attention, publicity, or business. |  The sales promotion was just a gimmick to get people into the store.  促销活动只是一个吸引人们进店的噱头。 | synonyms: trick, device, scheme, ploy, stunt, ruse, dodge, contrivance, artifice, wile, stratagem | antonyms: genuineness, authenticity, sincerity | noun

gimmicks:/ˈɡɪmɪks/| n. | 小玩意儿；不值钱的小东西 | A gadget, especially a cheap or novelty item. | He sold cheap gimmicks to tourists. 他向游客兜售廉价的小玩意儿。 | synonyms: gadget, novelty, trinket, doodad | antonyms: necessity, essential | noun
