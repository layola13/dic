outshaping:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪpɪŋ/
verb
胜过；在…方面超越
surpassing in shaping or creating; exceeding in form or design.
Tesla's innovative designs are outshaping the competition in the electric vehicle market.  特斯拉的创新设计在电动汽车市场上胜过了竞争对手。
The new sculptor is outshaping his mentor with his intricate and evocative pieces.  这位新的雕塑家以其复杂而引人入胜的作品超越了他的导师。
近义词: surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, outdoing, outstripping
反义词: lagging, falling behind, underperforming
词性: verb

词源:  A combination of "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) and "shaping" (the process of giving form or structure to something).  "Out-" is a common prefix used to indicate surpassing or exceeding, as in "outdo" or "outperform". "Shaping" comes from the Old English word "sceppan" meaning "to create" or "to form".
