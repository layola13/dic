kindles:/ˈkɪndlz/| v. |点燃；激起；照亮|To set something on fire; to arouse or excite a feeling or emotion. |The sunset kindled the sky with vibrant colors. 夕阳用鲜艳的色彩点燃了天空。

The teacher's words kindled a passion for learning in the students. 老师的话激起了学生们学习的热情。|近义词：ignites, sparks, arouses, inspires|反义词：extinguishes, dampens, suppresses|词性:动词

kindle:/ˈkɪnd(ə)l/| n. |点火器；电子阅读器|A device used to start a fire; an electronic device for reading books. |She used a kindle to start the campfire. 她用点火器点燃了篝火。

I downloaded a new book onto my Kindle. 我在我的Kindle上下载了一本新书。|近义词：(for fire starter) lighter, match; (for e-reader) e-reader|反义词：(for e-reader) printed book|词性:名词
