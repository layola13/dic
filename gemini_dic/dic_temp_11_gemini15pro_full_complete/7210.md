anglesite:/ˈæŋglɪsaɪt/| n.|角铅矿|A sulfate mineral, lead sulfate (PbSO4), that is an important ore of lead. It forms as a secondary mineral from the oxidation of galena.|Anglesite is often found in the oxidized zones of lead ore deposits. 角铅矿常在铅矿床的氧化带中发现。|The museum displayed a beautiful specimen of anglesite.  博物馆展出了一块美丽的角铅矿标本。|近义词:lead sulfate, cerussite|反义词:None|词性:noun
