discession:/dɪˈsɛʃən/ | n. | 切割; 裂开; 讨论; 论述 | The act of cutting apart or dissecting; discussion or debate. | The discession of the topic was thorough and insightful.  对这个话题的讨论是深入和有见地的。 | 近义词: discussion, debate, analysis | 反义词: agreement, consensus | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *discessio, from discedere "to depart, go away," from dis- "apart" + cedere "to go, yield."  源自拉丁语 *discessio，来自 discedere “离开，走开”，来自 dis- “分开” + cedere “去，屈服”。
