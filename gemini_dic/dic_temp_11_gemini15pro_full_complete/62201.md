encounterers:/ɪnˈkaʊntərərz/
noun
相遇者；遭遇者
Those who encounter something or someone.
例句：The encounterers exchanged a brief greeting before parting ways.  相遇者在分开前互相简短地打了招呼。

例句：The encounterers of the strange phenomenon were baffled and intrigued.  遭遇这种奇怪现象的人感到困惑和好奇。

近义词：meeters, experiencers
反义词: avoiders

词性:n.
