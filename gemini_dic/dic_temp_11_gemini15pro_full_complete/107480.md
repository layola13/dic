lancastrians:/læŋˈkæstriənz/
noun
兰开斯特王朝的支持者；兰开斯特郡人
Supporters of the House of Lancaster during the Wars of the Roses; people from Lancashire, England.
The Lancastrians lost the Battle of Towton. 兰开斯特王朝的支持者在陶顿战役中失利。
Many Lancastrians still feel a strong connection to the Red Rose. 很多兰开斯特郡人仍然对红玫瑰有着强烈的认同感。
synonyms: Lancastrian adherents, Lancashire residents
antonyms: Yorkists
n.
