bondages:/ˈbɒndɪdʒɪz/| n. |束缚，奴役|The state of being bound or restrained, either physically or metaphorically.  It can refer to literal imprisonment or enslavement, but also to oppressive circumstances or limiting situations.|She felt trapped in the bondages of tradition. 她感觉被困在传统的束缚中。|近义词：captivity, confinement, restraint, servitude, slavery|反义词：freedom, liberty, liberation|noun
