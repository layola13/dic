officeless:/ˈɒfɪsləs/ /ˈɑːfɪsləs/
adjective
无办公室的；不在办公室工作的
Not having or relating to an office; working remotely or without a fixed office space.
Many companies are embracing officeless work models. 许多公司正在接受无办公室的工作模式。
The officeless lifestyle allows for greater flexibility. 无办公室的生活方式允许更大的灵活性。
近义词:remote, virtual, decentralized
反义词:traditional, office-based
词性:adjective
