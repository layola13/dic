claro:/ˈklɑːroʊ/ (US) /ˈklɑːrəʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv.|清晰的，明白的；当然，当然可以|Clear, bright; obviously, certainly; of course (used as an affirmative reply).|The instructions were written in claro and simple language. 说明书是用清晰简洁的语言写的。|Synonyms: clear, obvious, evident, distinct, manifest, patent, apparent, unmistakable, transparent, limpid, pellucid, crystalline, bright, luminous, shining, light, distinct, obvious, evident, certain, sure, undoubtedly, definitely, easily, readily, of course, certainly, naturally, by all means|Antonyms: unclear, obscure, vague, ambiguous, indistinct, murky, opaque, fuzzy, blurred, dim, dull, dark, uncertain, doubtful, dubious, questionable|adjective/adverb

词源: From Latin clārus (“clear, bright, loud”).
