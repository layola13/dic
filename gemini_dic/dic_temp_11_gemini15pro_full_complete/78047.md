genuineness:/ˈdʒɛnjuɪnnəs/
n.
真实性；真诚；真挚
The genuineness of his remorse is unquestionable. 他的悔恨是真诚的，毋庸置疑。
genuineness is the quality of being authentic and true.  Genuineness implies sincerity and honesty, without pretense or hypocrisy.
例句：The painting was tested for genuineness. 这幅画被鉴定真伪。

The genuineness of her apology was evident in her tears.  她道歉的真诚从她的眼泪中可以明显看出。

近义词：authenticity, sincerity, honesty, truthfulness
反义词：falseness, artificiality, insincerity, dishonesty
