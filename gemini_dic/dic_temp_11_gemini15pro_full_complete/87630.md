hewable:/ˈhjuːəbl/| adj.|可砍伐的，可凿的|Suitable for hewing or shaping by cutting, chopping, or other similar actions.|The large block of granite was hewable into a variety of shapes. 这块巨大的花岗岩可以凿成各种形状。| The sculptor searched for a hewable piece of wood for his next project.  这位雕塑家正在寻找一块可雕刻的木头用于他的下一个项目。|近义词：carvable, workable, malleable|反义词:unworkable, inflexible|词性:adjective
Etymology: From "hew" (Old English *hēawan, to cut) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).
