midnight:/ˈmɪdnaɪt/| n.|午夜，半夜|The stroke of midnight. 午夜的钟声。|Example 1:  They arrived at the airport at midnight. 他们午夜到达了机场。

Example 2: The curfew starts at midnight.  宵禁从午夜开始。|Synonyms:  midday, noon, twelve o'clock|Antonyms: noon, midday|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *midniht (attested as *middnihte), from midde "mid" + niht "night".  Literally "middle of the night".
