nonpassionatenesses:/ˌnɒnˈpæʃənətnəsnɪs/
noun
缺乏热情，冷漠
lack of passion; absence of strong feeling or emotion
His nonpassionateness towards the project worried his colleagues. 他对这个项目缺乏热情，这让他的同事们很担心。
The artist's nonpassionateness was reflected in his bland paintings.  这位艺术家缺乏热情，这反映在他平淡无奇的画作中。
近义词: apathy, indifference, coldness
反义词: passion, enthusiasm, fervor
词性: noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + passionate (adjective meaning "having or showing strong feeling or emotion") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
