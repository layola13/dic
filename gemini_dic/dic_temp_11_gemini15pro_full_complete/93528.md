imagine:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ | v. | 想象，设想 | To form a mental image or concept of something. | I can't imagine life without you. 我无法想象没有你的生活。

| Example: She imagined herself winning the lottery. 她想象自己中了彩票。| Synonyms: envision, visualize, conceive, fancy | Antonyms:  disbelieve, doubt | Verb


imagine:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ | v. | 认为，猜想 | To believe or suppose something to be true. | I imagine they'll be arriving soon. 我想他们很快就会到了。

| Example:  I imagine you're tired after your long journey. 我想你长途旅行后一定很累了。 | Synonyms: suppose, assume, guess, presume | Antonyms: know, prove | Verb
