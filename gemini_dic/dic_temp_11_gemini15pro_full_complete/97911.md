innkeepers:/ˈɪnˌkiːpərz/
n.
旅店老板，客栈老板
A person who owns or manages an inn.
The innkeepers welcomed the weary travelers with a warm fire and a hearty meal.  旅店老板用温暖的炉火和丰盛的餐点欢迎疲惫的旅客。
The innkeepers charged exorbitant rates during the festival. 节日期间，旅店老板们收费过高。
Synonyms: hoteliers, landlords, publicans
Antonyms: guests, lodgers, tenants
