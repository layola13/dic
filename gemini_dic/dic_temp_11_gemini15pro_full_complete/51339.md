detecting:/dɪˈtɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 检测，发现，查明 | Discovering the presence of something, usually something hidden or subtle. | The software is designed for detecting computer viruses.  该软件旨在检测计算机病毒。| Synonyms: discovering, finding, identifying, noticing, sensing, perceiving | Antonyms: missing, overlooking, ignoring | verb

detecting:/dɪˈtɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 探测的，检测的 |  Used to describe something that detects.  (Less common usage) |  A detecting device was used to find the gas leak.  使用探测装置找到了气体泄漏。 | Synonyms: sensing, perceptive | Antonyms:  unaware, oblivious | adjective
