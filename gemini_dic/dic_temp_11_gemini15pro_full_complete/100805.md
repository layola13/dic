intrigues:/ɪnˈtriːɡz/ | n. | 阴谋，诡计；密谋 | a secret plan to do something, especially something that will harm another person | The book is full of political intrigue. 这本书充满了政治阴谋。 | synonyms: conspiracy, plot, scheme, machination | antonyms: honesty, openness | noun
intrigues:/ɪnˈtriːɡz/ | v. | 激起…的兴趣；引发…的好奇心；(尤指因奇怪或神秘而)迷住 | to interest someone a lot, especially by being strange, unusual, or mysterious | The story intrigued me.  这个故事引起了我的兴趣。 | synonyms: fascinate, captivate, interest, absorb | antonyms: bore, repel | verb
