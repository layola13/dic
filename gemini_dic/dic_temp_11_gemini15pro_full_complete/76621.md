galloper:/ˈɡæləpər/ (US), /ˈɡæləpə/ (UK)| n.| 驰骋的马；飞驰的人（或物）；快马；快船 |A horse that gallops; a person or thing that moves at a gallop; a fast horse; a fast ship.| The galloper thundered down the track. 那匹快马沿着跑道飞驰而过。 | 近义词: racer, courser, steed | 反义词: dawdler, slowpoke | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "gallop," which comes from Old French "galoper," of uncertain Germanic origin.
