collaborateur:/kəˌlæbəˈrɑːtər/ /kəˌlæbəˈreɪtər/| n. |合作者， collaborators (复数)|A collaborator is a person who works jointly with others, especially in an intellectual endeavor. |例句：He is a frequent collaborator with other musicians. 他经常与其他音乐家合作。|例句：The project involved collaborators from several different universities. 这个项目涉及来自几所不同大学的合作者。|近义词：partner, associate, colleague, co-worker|反义词：opponent, adversary|Noun
