excrementitiously:/ˌɛkskrɪmɛnˈtɪʃəsli/
adjective
排泄物地；污秽地；粪便似地
in a way that relates to excrement; offensively
His prose style is dense and excrementitious. 他的散文风格浓厚而污秽。
近义词:fecal, filthy, foul, obscene
反义词:clean, pure, pristine
词性:adjective
