armadas:/ɑːrˈmɑːdəz/
noun
舰队；(尤指大型的)海军舰队
A fleet of warships, especially a large one.
The Spanish Armada was defeated in 1588. 西班牙无敌舰队于1588年被打败。
synonyms: fleet, navy, flotilla
antonyms: single ship, small craft
