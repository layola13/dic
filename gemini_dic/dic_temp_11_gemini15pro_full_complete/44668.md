cruciferous: /kruːˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
十字花科的
Relating to or denoting plants of the family Brassicaceae (formerly Cruciferae), which have flowers with four petals in the shape of a cross.  These plants include cabbage, broccoli, cauliflower, kale, Brussels sprouts, collard greens, and radishes.
Broccoli is a cruciferous vegetable.  西兰花是一种十字花科蔬菜。

近义词: brassica
反义词: (No direct antonym as it's a classifying adjective)
词性: adjective
