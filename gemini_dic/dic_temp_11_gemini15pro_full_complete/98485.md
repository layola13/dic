installment: /ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (UK) | n. | 分期付款；部分；（分期连载的）一期 |  A payment made as one of a series for a purchase, debt, or other obligation.  A portion of something presented in parts. |  She paid for the car in monthly installments. 她按月分期付款买了这辆车。 |  The next installment of the story will be published next month. 故事的下一期将在下个月出版。 | synonyms: payment, portion, part, chapter, episode | antonyms: lump sum, whole | noun
