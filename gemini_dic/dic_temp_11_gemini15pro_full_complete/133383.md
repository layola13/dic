nonexultant:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzʌltənt/
adjective
不兴高采烈的，不喜悦的
Not feeling or expressing triumphant joy or jubilation.
Her nonexultant acceptance of the award surprised everyone. 她并不喜悦地接受了这个奖项，让每个人都感到惊讶。
The team's nonexultant reaction to their victory suggested they had expected to win.  该队对胜利没有表现出喜悦，表明他们原本就预料会赢。
近义词: subdued, restrained, unemotional, impassive
反义词: exultant, jubilant, triumphant, joyous
词性: adjective

词源:  "Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "exultant" (from Latin "exsultare," meaning "to leap up, rejoice").
