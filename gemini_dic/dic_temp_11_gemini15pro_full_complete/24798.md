brotherliness:/ˈbrʌðərlɪnɪs/
n.
兄弟情谊，友爱
The quality or state of being brotherly; brotherly affection.
He showed great brotherliness towards his teammates.  他对他的队友表现出极大的兄弟情谊。
近义词：fraternity, camaraderie, fellowship
反义词：hostility, animosity, antagonism
词性：名词

brotherly:/ˈbrʌðəli/
adj.
兄弟般的，友爱的
Of, relating to, or characteristic of a brother or brothers; like that of brothers.
He gave me a brotherly hug. 他给了我一个兄弟般的拥抱。
近义词：fraternal, amicable, friendly
反义词：unfriendly, hostile, antagonistic
词性：形容词
