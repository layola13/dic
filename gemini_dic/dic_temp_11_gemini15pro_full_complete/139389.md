observancy:/əbˈzɜːrvənsi/
n.|遵守；奉行；仪式；庆祝|The strict observance of the Sabbath is important to many Jews.  对许多犹太人来说，严格遵守安息日是很重要的。|例句：His religious observancy impressed everyone. 他的宗教虔诚给每个人留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：compliance, adherence, obedience|反义词：disregard, neglect, violation|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *observance*, from Anglo-Norman *observance*, *observaunee*, and Old French *observance*, from Late Latin *observantia* ("observance, respect"), from Latin *observans*, present participle of *observāre* ("to observe, watch, keep, comply with").
