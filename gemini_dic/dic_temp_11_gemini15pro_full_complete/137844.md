nonviewer:/ˌnɒnˈvjuːər/ | n. | 非观众；不观看者 |  Someone who does not watch a particular television program or type of program.  | Example: The marketing campaign aimed to reach both viewers and nonviewers. 这场营销活动旨在覆盖观众和非观众。

| Example:  The survey targeted nonviewers to understand why they didn't watch the show.  这项调查针对不观看者，以了解他们为什么不看这个节目。 | 近义词: non-watcher | 反义词: viewer, watcher | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "viewer" (from the verb "view").
