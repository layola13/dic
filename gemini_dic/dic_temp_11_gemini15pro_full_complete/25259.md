buffest:/ˈbʌfɪst/| adj.|最强壮的，肌肉最发达的|Superlative form of "buff," meaning having a well-toned physique, especially with prominent muscles.|He was the buffest guy in the gym, easily lifting the heaviest weights.|近义词：most muscular, strongest, fittest|反义词:weakest, skinniest|词性:形容词

词源:
From "buff," which originally referred to the color of buffalo hide, then to a strong, sturdy leather, and later to a person with a similar physique.
