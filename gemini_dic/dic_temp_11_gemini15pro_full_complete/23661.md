brands:/brændz/ | n. | 品牌; 商标; 类型;烙印 | a type of product manufactured by a particular company under a particular name. |  Many consumers are loyal to particular brands. 许多消费者忠于特定的品牌。| synonyms: trademarks, makes, labels | antonyms: generics, no-names | noun

brands:/brændz/ | v. | 标记; 污辱; 打烙印于 | mark with a hot iron. | The cattle were branded with the owner's initials. 牛被打上了主人的姓名首字母烙印。| synonyms: mark, stigmatize, label | antonyms: erase, clear | verb
