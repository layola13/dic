hydraulic:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪk/ | adj. n.| 水力的，液压的；液压装置 | Operated by, moved by, or employing liquid (water or oil) in motion under pressure. |  例句：The car's brakes are hydraulic. 这辆车的刹车是液压的。| 近义词：fluid-powered, pneumatic | 反义词: manual, mechanical | 词性: adjective

hydraulic:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪks/ | n. | 液压学，水力学 | The science of liquids in motion and their application in engineering. | 例句：He specialized in hydraulics. 他专门研究水力学。| 近义词：fluid mechanics | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hydraulique*, from Latin *hydraulica* and Greek *ὑδραυλικός (hydraulikos)*, from *ὕδωρ (hydōr)* meaning "water" and *αὐλός (aulos)* meaning "pipe," referring originally to a musical instrument operated by water pressure.
