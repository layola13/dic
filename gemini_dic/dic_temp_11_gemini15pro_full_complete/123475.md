monostrophe:/ˈmɑːnəˌstroʊfi/ /ˈmɒnəˌstrəʊfi/| n. |单节诗; 独段体诗|A poem or strophe consisting of a single stanza.  A poem or song in which all stanzas are sung to the same melody. |Sappho's lyric poems are often composed as monostrophs.  萨福的抒情诗通常是由单节诗组成的。

The hymn was a simple monostrophe, easy for the congregation to learn. 这首赞美诗是一首简单的独段体诗歌，易于会众学习。|近义词：stanza, verse|反义词:|noun
