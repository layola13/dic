liquefiers:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪərz/
n.
液化器；液化装置
A device or substance that liquefies something, typically gases.
The liquefiers are essential for converting natural gas to LNG for transport. 这些液化器对于将天然气转化为液化天然气进行运输至关重要。
synonyms: condensers, liquefaction units
antonyms: vaporizers, gasifiers
noun
