maim:/meɪm/| vt. n.|使残废，残害；残废，伤残|To wound or injure someone so severely that a part of their body is permanently damaged.|The soldier was maimed in battle and lost his leg.  这个士兵在战斗中致残，失去了一条腿。|例句：The accident maimed him for life.  这起事故使他终身残疾。

例句：He suffered a maim in the explosion. 他在爆炸中受了伤残。|近义词：cripple, disable, mutilate, injure|反义词：heal, cure|词性：动词，名词
