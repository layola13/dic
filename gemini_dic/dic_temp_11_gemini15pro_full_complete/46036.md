cycas: /ˈsaɪkæs/ | n. |苏铁，西葛|A genus of palmlike evergreen gymnosperms (family Cycadaceae) with pinnate leaves and large terminal cones, widely distributed in warm regions.  They are considered living fossils, having changed very little over millions of years.|The cycas in the botanical garden is over 100 years old. 这座植物园里的苏铁已经超过100岁了。|Synonym: sago palm|Antonym: N/A|Noun
