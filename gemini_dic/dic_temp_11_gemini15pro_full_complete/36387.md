cointension:/ˌkoʊɪnˈtɛnʃən/ | n. |共同内涵，共同外延|The cointension of two terms refers to the attributes or properties they share. 两个术语的共同内涵是指它们共有的属性或特性。|The cointension of "dog" and "mammal" includes being warm-blooded and having fur. “狗”和“哺乳动物”的共同内涵包括温血和有毛。|Cointension is crucial in logic and knowledge representation. 共同内涵在逻辑和知识表示中至关重要。|Synonyms: connotation, implication |Antonyms: denotation, extension |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + intension (inner meaning, from Latin intendere, to stretch out, aim at).
