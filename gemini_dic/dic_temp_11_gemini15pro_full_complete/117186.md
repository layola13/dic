measurably:/ˈmeʒərəbli/
adverb
可测量地，显著地
in a way that can be measured or noticed
例句：The air quality has measurably improved. 空气质量已得到显著改善。

例句：Her health has measurably declined in recent weeks. 最近几周，她的健康状况明显下降。

近义词：noticeably, significantly, perceptibly, appreciably
反义词：imperceptibly, slightly, insignificantly
词性：adverb
