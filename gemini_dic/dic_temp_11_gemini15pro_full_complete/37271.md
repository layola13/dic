comma:/ˈkɑːmə/ | n. | 逗号 |A punctuation mark (,) indicating a pause between parts of a sentence. It is also used to separate items in a list and to mark the thousands in a large numeral.|  He forgot to put a comma in the sentence. 他忘记在这句话里加逗号了。| synonym:separation, pause | antonym: conjunction | noun

comma:/ˈkɑːmə/ | v. | 命令，指挥 | To give an order or instruction to (someone). | The general commaed his troops to attack. 将军下令部队进攻。| synonym: order, instruct, direct | antonym: obey, follow | verb
