onyx:/ˈɑːnɪks/| adj.|玛瑙色的，漆黑的|Having the color of onyx; jet black.|She wore an onyx necklace with her black dress. 她戴着一条玛瑙项链，配着她的黑色连衣裙。|Synonyms: black, ebony, jet|Antonyms: white, light|Adjective

词源：
From Old French *oniche, from Latin onyx, from Ancient Greek ὄνυξ (ónux, “claw, fingernail, hoof”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₃óŋgh₂us (“nail, hoof”).  The stone was so named from its resemblance to a fingernail in color and translucency.
来自古法语 *oniche，源自拉丁语 onyx，来自古希腊语 ὄνυξ (ónux，“爪子、指甲、蹄子”)，来自原始印欧语 *h₃óŋgh₂us（“指甲、蹄子”）。这种石头因其颜色和半透明度与指甲相似而得名。
