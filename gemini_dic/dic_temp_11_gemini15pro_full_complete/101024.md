invariant:/ɪnˈveəriənt/| n. adj.|不变式，不变量；不变的，恒定的|Something that remains unchanged under a particular transformation or set of operations.  An invariant property or value.|例句：The speed of light in a vacuum is an invariant. 真空中光速是一个不变量。|近义词：constant, unchanging, fixed, stable|反义词:variable, changing, fluctuating|n.
invariant:/ɪnˈveəriənt/| adj.|不变的，恒定的|Remaining constant under a specified set of transformations or conditions.|例句：The temperature remained invariant throughout the experiment. 在整个实验过程中，温度保持不变。|近义词：constant, unchanging, fixed, stable|反义词:variable, changing, fluctuating|adj.
