courted:/ˈkɔːrtɪd/ (US) /ˈkɔːtɪd/ (UK)| v.|追求；讨好；招致；试图获得|To seek the affections of; woo. To attempt to gain; solicit.  To invite or attract (something undesirable).|He courted her for two years before she finally agreed to marry him. 他追求她两年，她最终同意嫁给他。|The company courted controversy with its latest advertising campaign.  这家公司最新的广告宣传活动招致了争议。|近义词：woo, pursue, seek, solicit|反义词：spurn, reject, shun, avoid|词性:动词
