noncommunist:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːmjənɪst/ | /ˌnɒnˈkɒmjənɪst/| adj. n.|非共产主义的；非共产主义者|Not communist; opposed to or not in accordance with the principles of communism. |Many noncommunist countries are democracies. 许多非共产主义国家是民主国家。|The noncommunist world condemned the invasion. 非共产主义世界谴责了这次入侵。|近义词：anticommunist, non-Marxist|反义词:communist, Marxist|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology:
non- + communist

non-:  prefix meaning "not," from Old French non-, Latin non "not," from Old Latin noenum, neuter of *noinus, from PIE *ne- "not."

communist: from French communiste (1841), from commun "common" (see common (adj.)) + -iste.  Ultimately from Latin communis "in common, general, public, shared by all or many."
