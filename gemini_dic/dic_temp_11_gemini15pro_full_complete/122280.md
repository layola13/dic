mitochondria:/ˌmaɪtəˈkɒndriə/ (UK) /ˌmaɪtəˈkɑːndriə/ (US)| n. |线粒体|An organelle found in large numbers in most cells, in which the biochemical processes of respiration and energy production occur. It has a double membrane, the inner layer being folded inward to form layers (cristae).|Mitochondria are the powerhouses of the cell. 线粒体是细胞的能量工厂。|Near synonyms: chondriosome|No commonly used antonym|noun

mitochondrion:/ˌmaɪtəˈkɒndriən/ (UK) /ˌmaɪtəˈkɑːndriən/ (US)| n. |线粒体 (单数)|Singular form of mitochondria.  A single organelle responsible for energy production within a cell.|The mitochondrion is essential for cellular respiration.  这个线粒体对细胞呼吸至关重要。|Near synonyms: chondriosome|No commonly used antonym|noun
