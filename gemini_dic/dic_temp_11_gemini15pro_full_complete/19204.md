bewreke: /bɪˈriːk/
Verb
报仇；复仇
To avenge or revenge.
He swore he would bewreke his father's death. 他发誓要为他父亲的死报仇。
Synonyms: avenge, revenge
Antonyms: forgive, pardon
词性: 动词
