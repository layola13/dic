magniloquous: /ˌmæɡˈnɪləkwəs/ | adj. | 浮夸的，夸大其词的 | Using high-flown or bombastic language; pompous. Characterized by lofty, often bombastic language. |  The politician delivered a magniloquous speech, full of grand promises and lofty rhetoric.  这位政客发表了一篇夸夸其谈的演讲，充满了宏伟的承诺和崇高的言辞。

|  His magniloquous style of writing often obscured the meaning of his work. 他夸夸其词的写作风格常常掩盖了他作品的意义。 | 近义词：bombastic, pompous, grandiloquent, lofty, inflated | 反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious, modest, humble | 词性：形容词

词源:
From Latin *magniloquus*, from *magnus* ("great") + *loquor* ("to speak").
