illegalize:/ɪˈliːɡəlaɪz/
verb
使非法化；宣布…为非法
To make something illegal; to prohibit something by law.
The government is considering illegalizing certain types of fireworks. 政府正在考虑将某些类型的烟花爆竹定为非法。
The sale of such products has been illegalized. 此类产品的销售已被宣布为非法。
prohibit, ban, outlaw
legalize, permit, allow
v.
