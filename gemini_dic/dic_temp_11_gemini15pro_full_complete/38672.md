condemnatory:/kənˈdɛmnətɔːri/
adjective
谴责的，责备的
Expressing strong disapproval; censuring.
His condemnatory tone made it clear he disapproved of her actions. 他的谴责语气清楚地表明他不赞成她的行为。
The judge delivered a condemnatory speech before sentencing the criminal. 法官在判决罪犯之前发表了谴责性讲话。
synonyms: censorious, critical, disapproving, accusatory, denunciatory
antonyms: approving, commendatory, laudatory, praising, complimentary
词性: adjective
