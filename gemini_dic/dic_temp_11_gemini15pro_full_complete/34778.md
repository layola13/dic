climates:/ˈklaɪməts/| n.|气候（复数）|The long-term average weather conditions of a region, including temperature, precipitation, humidity, wind, and sunshine.|The Earth's climates are changing rapidly. 地球的气候正在迅速变化。|近义词：atmospheres, environments|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French climat, from Latin clima, climat-, from Greek klima "region, zone; slope, inclination," from klinein "to slope, lean."  Originally referring to the angle of the sun's rays at a particular location on Earth.
