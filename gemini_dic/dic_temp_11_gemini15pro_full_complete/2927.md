advisors:/ədˈvaɪzərz/| n. |顾问，指导者|A person who gives advice or guidance, especially in a particular field.|例句：The company's financial advisors recommended investing in the stock market. 该公司的财务顾问建议投资股票市场。

例句：The president sought the advice of his advisors before making a decision.  总统在做决定之前征求了顾问们的意见。|近义词：consultants, counselors, mentors, guides|反义词: None (No direct opposite, perhaps "advisee" in a specific context)|noun
