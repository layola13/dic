kittys:/ˈkɪtiz/ | n. | 小猫（复数，非正式） | Informal plural of "kitty," which itself is a diminutive of "cat."  Refers to young cats. | Example:  The kittys are playing with a ball of yarn. 小猫们正在玩毛线球。| Example: Look at all those cute kittys! 看看所有这些可爱的小猫！ | 近义词：kittens, cats | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a young animal) | 词性: Noun
