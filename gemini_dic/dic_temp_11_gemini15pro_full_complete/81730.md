greensboro:/ˈɡriːnzˌbɜːroʊ/ (US) /ˈɡriːnzbərə/ (UK)| n. |格林斯伯勒|A city in North Carolina, USA.  It is the third-most populous city in the state and the county seat of Guilford County.| Greensboro is known for its universities and its history in the textile industry. 格林斯伯勒以其大学和纺织业历史而闻名。|近义词:city, municipality|反义词:rural area|词性:noun

Etymology: Named after Nathanael Greene, a major general of the Continental Army in the American Revolutionary War.
