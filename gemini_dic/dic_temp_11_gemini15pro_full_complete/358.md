abides by:/əˈbaɪdz baɪ/| v.|遵守，遵循|To accept or obey a rule, decision, or agreement.|例句：The company abides by all local and federal regulations. 这家公司遵守所有地方和联邦法规。|例句：He abides by the principles of fairness and honesty. 他遵循公平诚实的原则。|近义词：complies with, adheres to, follows, observes, respects|反义词：violates, breaches, ignores, disregards|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *abiden bi*, from *abiden* (to abide, remain) + *bi* (by).  The original sense was "to remain near," later evolving to "remain faithful to" and then "to obey."
