origenist:/ˈɔːrɪdʒənɪst/
n.|奥利金主义者；奥利金派信徒|A follower of the theological doctrines of Origen.
|Origenists believed in the pre-existence of souls.|奥利金主义者相信灵魂的先存。
|Many of Origen's doctrines were later condemned as heretical.|奥利金的许多教义后来被谴责为异端。
|近义词:follower, disciple|反义词:opponent, critic|词性:n.
