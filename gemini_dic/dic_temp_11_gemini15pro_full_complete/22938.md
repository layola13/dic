bottled:/ˈbɒtld/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtld/ (US)| adj.|瓶装的；抑制的|Contained in a bottle; (of feelings) suppressed.|例句：Bottled water is convenient but expensive. 瓶装水很方便，但是很贵。|近义词：canned, packaged, suppressed (for feelings)|反义词:draft, on tap (for drinks), expressed (for feelings)|词性:形容词
bottled:/ˈbɒtld/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtld/ (US)| v.|装瓶；抑制（感情）|(of a drink) be put into bottles; suppress (feelings).|例句：The factory bottled thousands of liters of juice every day. 这家工厂每天将数千升的果汁装瓶。|近义词：contain, restrain, repress (for feelings)|反义词:release, vent, express (for feelings)|词性:动词
