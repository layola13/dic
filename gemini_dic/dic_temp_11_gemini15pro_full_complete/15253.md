backhanded:/ˌbækˈhændɪd/ /ˌbækˈhændəd/| adj.|间接的，反讽的，虚情假意的；(球类运动)反手的|A backhanded compliment appears to be a compliment but is actually an insult.  一个反讽的恭维表面上是赞美，实际上是侮辱。|例句：He gave her a backhanded compliment about her dress. 他拐弯抹角地挖苦了她的裙子。|近义词：sarcastic, ironic, indirect, left-handed (for sports)|反义词：direct, sincere, genuine|词性:形容词

backhanded:/ˌbækˈhændɪd/ /ˌbækˈhændəd/| n.|反手击球|He hit a powerful backhand down the line. 他沿着边线打了一个强有力的反手球。|例句：Her backhand is much stronger than her forehand. 她的反手比正手强得多。|近义词：backhand stroke, backhand shot|反义词：forehand|词性:名词
