intrude:/ɪnˈtruːd/ | v. | 侵入，打扰，闯入 | To come or go in without permission or welcome. |  The loud music from the party next door intruded on our quiet evening. 隔壁派对传来的喧闹音乐打扰了我们宁静的夜晚。

She hated to intrude on their privacy. 她不愿侵犯他们的隐私。 | 近义词：interfere, interrupt, encroach, impinge, trespass | 反义词：withdraw, retreat, leave | 词性：动词
