hurly-burly:/ˈhɜːrli ˈbɜːrli/ /ˈhərli ˈbərli/| n.|喧闹，骚动，混乱|A state of commotion, uproar, and confusion. |The marketplace was a hurly-burly of sights and sounds. 市场一片喧闹，充满了各种景象和声音。|The hurly-burly of city life can be overwhelming. 城市生活的喧嚣有时会让人难以承受。|近义词：commotion, uproar, tumult, chaos, pandemonium|反义词：peace, quiet, tranquility, calm, order|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From late Middle English *hurly burly*, probably of imitative origin, perhaps influenced by *hur*, to snarl or growl, and *burly*, large and strong.
