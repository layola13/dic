afterword:/ˈæftərwɜːrd/ | n. | 后记；结语；编后记 | A concluding section in a book, typically by a person other than the author. |  The author added an afterword to the second edition of his book, explaining the changes he'd made. 作者在他的书的第二版中增加了一个后记，解释了他所做的修改。

After the tumultuous events, she wrote an afterword to her biography, reflecting on the lessons learned. 经历了动荡的事件之后，她在自己的传记中写了一篇后记，反思了从中汲取的教训。 | 近义词: epilogue, postscript, concluding remarks | 反义词: foreword, preface, introduction | 词性: 名词
