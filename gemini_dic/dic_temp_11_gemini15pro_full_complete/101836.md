irrupting:/ɪˈrʌptɪŋ/
verb
突然爆发，闯入
suddenly entering a place or bursting out
例句：Protesters irrupting into the capitol building caused chaos. 抗议者闯入国会大厦造成了混乱。

例句：The volcano irrupted, spewing lava and ash into the sky. 火山爆发，向空中喷出熔岩和火山灰。

近义词：bursting, erupting, exploding, surging
反义词：seeping, trickling, oozing
词性：Verb, present participle of "irrupt"
词源：From Latin *irruptus*, past participle of irrumpere (“to break in, burst in”), from in- (“in, into, on, against”) + rumpere (“to break”).
