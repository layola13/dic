hypodermic:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. n. |皮下注射的；皮下注射器 | administered or used to administer something beneath the skin. |例句：The nurse gave the patient a hypodermic injection. 护士给病人进行了皮下注射。

例句：Hypodermic needles are used to administer insulin. 皮下注射针头用于注射胰岛素。

近义词：subcutaneous, injection |反义词: - |词性: adjective, noun

hypodermics:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪks/ | n. | 皮下注射；皮下注射器（复数） | hypodermic injections or needles. |例句：Hypodermics are commonly used in diabetes management.  皮下注射常用于糖尿病管理。

例句：The doctor prepared the hypodermics for the vaccinations. 医生准备了疫苗接种用的皮下注射器。


近义词：injections, needles |反义词: - |词性: noun
