iding:/ˈaɪdɪŋ/ |  n. | 行为，活动; (尤指)骑马 | The act of riding, especially on horseback. |  She enjoys riding in the countryside. 她喜欢在乡下骑马。| 近义词：horseback riding, equitation | 反义词: dismounting, walking | 词性:名词


iding:/ˈaɪdɪŋ/ | adj. |  骑马的；供骑乘的 | Used for or relating to riding a horse. | They went on a riding holiday. 他们去骑马度假了。 |  近义词：equestrian, mounted | 反义词: pedestrian | 词性:形容词


词源: From Middle English *ryding, from Old English rīding, from rīdan (“to ride”).
