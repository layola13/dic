charm:/tʃɑːrm/ (US) /tʃɑːm/ (UK)| n. vt.|魅力，吸引力；护身符；咒语|A quality that attracts or fascinates by its pleasing or delightful nature.  To attract or influence by magic or charm.|例句：Her charm captivated everyone in the room. 她的魅力迷住了房间里的每一个人。|近义词：allure, charisma, magic, spell, amulet|反义词：repulsion, unpleasantness|词性:名词,动词

charm:/tʃɑːrm/ (US) /tʃɑːm/ (UK)| vt.|用魔法迷惑；吸引|To attract or influence by magic or charm.|例句：He charmed the snake into leaving the basket. 他用咒语让蛇离开了篮子。|近义词：bewitch, enchant, captivate|反义词：repel, disgust|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "charme"  (magical charm, incantation), from Latin "carmen" (song, verse, incantation).
