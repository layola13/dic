identical:/aɪˈdɛntɪkəl/| adj.|相同的，完全一样的|Being exactly alike; the same in every detail.|The twins are identical. 这对双胞胎长得一模一样.|近义词：same, alike, indistinguishable, equal|反义词:different, dissimilar, distinct, unlike|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin identicalis, from ident- ‘same’ (from idem ‘the same’) + -icalis.
