abundantly:/əˈbʌndəntli/
| adv. |丰富地，大量地|in great quantity; plentifully; copiously|例句：The land was abundantly supplied with water. 这片土地水源充足。|近义词：plentifully, copiously, profusely, amply, richly|反义词:scarcely, sparsely, meagerly|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *abundant- (stem of abundant-em), present participle of abundāre ‘to overflow, abound’, from ab- ‘from’ + undāre ‘to surge, overflow’ (from unda ‘wave’).
