manichaeanisms:/ˌmænɪˈkiːənɪzəmz/
|n.|摩尼教思想；二元论；绝对善恶观|A dualistic philosophy dividing the world into good and evil principles, typically with an emphasis on the conflict between light and darkness, derived from the teachings of Mani.|His worldview, with its rigid separation of good and evil, could be seen as a form of Manichaeanism. 他的世界观，严格区分善恶，可以被视为一种摩尼教思想。|Synonyms: dualism, absolutism|Antonyms: pluralism, relativism|noun

manichaean:/ˌmænɪˈkiːən/
|adj.|摩尼教的；二元论的；绝对善恶观的|Relating to Manichaeism, a dualistic religious system.|The Manichaean struggle between light and darkness is a recurring theme in his work.  光明与黑暗之间的摩尼教斗争是他作品中反复出现的主题。|Synonyms: dualistic, absolutist|Antonyms: pluralistic, relativistic|adjective
