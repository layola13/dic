ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fierthra (attested only as fierr and fierrest), comparative of feorr "far". Cognate with Old Frisian ferra, Old Saxon ferri, Old High German ferriro, Old Norse ferr (whence Icelandic fjarr).
