inerrability:/ˌɪnɛrəˈbɪləti/
n.|无谬误性，绝对正确|The quality of being free from error; infallibility.  The inerrancy of scripture is a central tenet of some Christian denominations.|The doctrine of biblical inerrancy is a complex one. 圣经无误的教义是一个复杂的教义。|近义词：infallibility, impeccability, accuracy|反义词：fallibility, errancy, imperfection|词性:名词

inerrancy:/ɪˈnɛrənsi/
n.|无误，正确|The quality or state of being without error; inerrancy.|The pastor preached about the inerrancy of the Bible. 牧师布道讲述了圣经的无误性。|近义词：infallibility, accuracy|反义词：fallibility, error|词性:名词
