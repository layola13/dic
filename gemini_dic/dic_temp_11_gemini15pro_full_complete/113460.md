lynx-like:/ˈlɪŋksˌlaɪk/| adj.|像猞猁的，猞猁似的|Resembling a lynx, especially in appearance or behavior.  Having qualities characteristic of a lynx, such as sharp eyesight, stealth, or a wild nature.|The cat moved with a lynx-like grace through the tall grass. 这只猫像猞猁一样优雅地在高高的草丛中穿行。|The detective had lynx-like perception and noticed the smallest details.  这位侦探有着像猞猁一样敏锐的洞察力，注意到了最细微的细节。|近义词：feline, catlike, stealthy, sharp-sighted, keen, perceptive|反义词：clumsy, dull, unobservant|词性:形容词
