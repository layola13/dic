amendatory:/əˈmɛndətɔːri/
adjective
修正的，修订的
Serving to amend; making an amendment.
The legislature passed an amendatory bill to correct an error in the previous law.  立法机关通过了一项修正法案，以纠正先前法律中的错误。
The amendatory act clarified several ambiguous provisions.  修正案澄清了几个模棱两可的条款。
synonyms:  revising, modifying, correcting, emendatory
antonyms:  original, unamended, unchanged
词性: adjective
