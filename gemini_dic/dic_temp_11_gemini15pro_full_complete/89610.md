hoodwinker:/hʊdˈwɪŋkər/
n.
骗子；欺诈者
A person who deceives or tricks someone.
The hoodwinker convinced the elderly woman to give him her life savings.  这个骗子说服了老妇人把她的毕生积蓄都给了他。
synonyms:  trickster, swindler, charlatan, fraudster, deceiver, cheat
antonyms:  honest person, dupe
noun

hoodwinker:/hʊdˈwɪŋkər/
vt.
欺骗；蒙蔽
To deceive or trick someone.
He hoodwinked investors into believing his company was profitable. 他欺骗投资者，让他们相信他的公司是有利可图的。
synonyms:  deceive, trick, dupe, swindle, cheat, defraud
antonyms:  enlighten, inform, be honest with
verb
