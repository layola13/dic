cedes:/siːdz/
verb
割让，放弃(权利、领土等)
to surrender possession of, especially by treaty
例句：France ceded the territory to Germany after the war.  法国在战后把这块领土割让给了德国。

例句：He ceded his position as CEO to his son. 他把首席执行官的职位让给了他的儿子。
近义词：yield, relinquish, surrender
反义词：retain, acquire
词性：v.
