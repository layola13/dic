jadedness's:/ˈdʒeɪdɪdnəsɪz/ | n. |厌倦，疲惫，麻木 |The state of being jaded; weariness, apathy, or cynicism resulting from overexposure or overindulgence.|  Her jadedness's was evident in her lack of enthusiasm for anything. 她的厌倦从她对任何事情都缺乏热情中显而易见。 | 近义词: weariness, apathy, cynicism, exhaustion, ennui | 反义词: enthusiasm, excitement, vigor, energy, freshness | 词性: 名词

词源: From jaded + -ness.  Jaded itself comes from the now obsolete verb jade "to tire, weary," perhaps related to the noun jade (the hard stone), in the sense of something worn out or exhausted.
