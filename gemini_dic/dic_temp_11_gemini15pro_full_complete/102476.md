jacksnipe:/ˈdʒæksnaɪp/| n.|沙锥鸟；(俚)骗子；狡猾的人|A small wading bird with a long straight bill, found in marshes and wetlands.  一种小型涉禽，喙长而直，常见于沼泽和湿地。|例句：The hunter flushed a jacksnipe from the reeds. 猎人从芦苇丛中惊起一只沙锥鸟。

|例句：He's a real jacksnipe, always trying to trick people out of their money. 他真是个骗子，总是想方设法骗取别人的钱。|近义词：snipe, con man, trickster|反义词:honest person|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From jack + snipe, perhaps from the male bird's aggressive behaviour during the breeding season.  来自 jack + snipe，可能源于雄鸟在繁殖季节的攻击性行为。
