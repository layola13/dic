denigrating:/ˈdɛnɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
诋毁的，贬低的
criticizing someone or something unfairly; speaking damagingly of; belittling.
His denigrating remarks about her appearance were completely uncalled for. 他对她外貌的贬低言论完全没有必要。
近义词: disparaging, belittling, defamatory, slanderous, vilifying
反义词: praising, laudatory, complimentary, extolling
词性: adjective

denigrate:/ˈdɛnɪɡreɪt/
verb
诋毁，贬低
to criticize unfairly; speak damagingly of; belittle.
The article denigrated the achievements of the athletes. 这篇文章贬低了运动员的成就。
近义词: disparage, belittle, defame, slander, vilify
反义词: praise, laud, compliment, extol
词性: verb

词源: From Late Latin *denigrāre, from Latin dē- + nigrāre (“to blacken”), from niger (“black”).
