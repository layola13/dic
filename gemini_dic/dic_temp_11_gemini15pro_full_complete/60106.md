eery:/ˈɪəri/ (US), /ˈɪəri/ (UK)| adj.|怪诞的，阴森的，可怕的|Strange and frightening, causing a feeling of mystery and unease.|The empty house had an eery silence. 空荡荡的房子里有一种怪诞的寂静。|近义词：creepy, spooky, uncanny, weird|反义词：normal, ordinary, comforting|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *eerie*, from Old English *earg*, meaning "cowardly."  The sense shifted from "timid" to "inspiring fear" and then to "weird, uncanny."
