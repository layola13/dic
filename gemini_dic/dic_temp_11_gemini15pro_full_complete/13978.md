aureoled: /ɔːriˈoʊld/ /ɔːrɪˈoʊld/
adjective
光环笼罩的，aureoled with glory
Surrounded by a halo of light; glorified.
The saint was depicted in an icon, aureoled in gold.  圣徒被描绘在一个圣像上，笼罩在金色的光环中。
The rising sun, aureoled in mist, cast a magical glow over the landscape.  冉冉升起的太阳，笼罩在薄雾中，给大地投下了一层神奇的光辉。

近义词: haloed, nimbused, radiant, glorious
反义词: dull, dim, dark

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin aureolus "golden," diminutive of aureus "golden."
