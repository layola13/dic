amethystlike:/ˈæmɪθɪstˌlaɪk/| adj.|紫水晶般的，像紫水晶的|Resembling or characteristic of an amethyst in color or brilliance.  Having a violet or purple color like that of an amethyst.|Her eyes were an amethystlike purple. 她的眼睛是紫水晶般的紫色。|The ring had an amethystlike stone. 这枚戒指上有一颗像紫水晶的宝石。|近义词：amethystine, violet, purple|反义词: colorless, clear|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From amethyst + -like.  "Amethyst" comes from the Greek *amethystos*, meaning "not drunken", from *a-* (not) + *methyein* (to be drunk).  It was believed that the stone could prevent intoxication.
