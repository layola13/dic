labouring:/ˈleɪbərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈleɪbərɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 辛苦的，费力的；呼吸困难的 | working hard and strenuously; breathing with difficulty |  The labouring man wiped sweat from his brow.  辛苦工作的男人擦了擦额头的汗。| synonyms: toiling, struggling, strenuous, arduous | antonyms: effortless, easy, simple | adjective

labouring:/ˈleɪbərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈleɪbərɪŋ/ (US)| v. |  劳动；苦干；致力于；呼吸困难；（指引擎、车辆等）运转吃力；（指观点表达）笨拙费劲  | present participle of labour (verb) | He was labouring under the misapprehension that he was popular. 他误以为自己很受欢迎。 | synonyms: toiling, struggling, striving | antonyms: resting, relaxing | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *labouringe*, present participle of labouren, from Old French labourer, from Latin labōrāre (“to work, toil, labor”), from labor (“labor, toil, hardship”).
