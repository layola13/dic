consignment:/kənˈsaɪnmənt/| n.|寄售；委托；托付的货物|An arrangement where goods are left with a business to sell, and the business pays the supplier only when the goods are sold.|The shop received a new consignment of shoes.  这家商店收到了一批新鞋。|synonyms: shipment, delivery, batch, load|antonyms: retention, withholding|noun

consign:/kənˈsaɪn/| vt.|寄售；托运；交付|To deliver or give something over to another person's care, often for sale.|She consigned the painting to an auction house. 她把这幅画交给了拍卖行。|synonyms: entrust, commit, deliver|antonyms: retain, withhold|verb
