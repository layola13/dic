outfumbled:/ˌaʊtˈfʌmbəld/
verb
失球；(比……)更多地失球；笨拙地处理
To fumble the ball more than an opponent, leading to a loss of possession.  Also, to handle something clumsily.
The quarterback outfumbled his opponent, costing his team the game. 四分卫比对手失球更多，导致他的球队输掉了比赛。
He outfumbled the delicate vase, and it shattered on the floor. 他笨手笨脚地弄掉了精致的花瓶，花瓶摔碎在地板上。
近义词: mishandled, botched, bungled
反义词: controlled, mastered, skillfully handled
词性: verb (past tense)
