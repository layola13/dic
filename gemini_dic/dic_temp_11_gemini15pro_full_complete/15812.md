balkinesses:/ˈbɔːlkɪnəsɪz/
| n. | 倔强，不愿合作，迟疑 | The balkinesses of the old mule made plowing the field a slow and frustrating process.  老骡子的倔强使得耕地变成一个缓慢而令人沮丧的过程。 | The project was delayed due to the balkinesses of several key investors. 该项目由于几位关键投资者的迟疑而被推迟。 |  近义词：reluctance, hesitation, resistance, unwillingness | 反义词：willingness, cooperation, eagerness | 词性: 名词

balkiness:/ˈbɔːlkɪnəs/
| n. | 倔强，不愿合作，迟疑 | The balkiness of the horse made it difficult to control. 这匹马的倔强使它难以控制。 | His balkiness to accept the offer surprised everyone.  他不愿接受这份提议让每个人都感到惊讶。 | 近义词：reluctance, hesitation, resistance, unwillingness | 反义词：willingness, cooperation, eagerness | 词性: 名词

balky:/ˈbɔːlki/
| adj. | 倔强的，不愿合作的，迟疑的 | The balky engine refused to start. 这台发动机启动不了。 | He was balky about signing the contract. 他对签署合同犹豫不决。| 近义词：reluctant, hesitant, resistant, unwilling | 反义词：willing, cooperative, eager | 词性: 形容词


词源：balk (v.) + -y (adj. suffix) + -ness (n. suffix) + -es (plural suffix).  "balk" 源于古挪威语 "balkr"，意为 "田埂" 或 "障碍物"。  最初指马匹拒绝跨越障碍物，后来引申为不愿合作或迟疑。
