USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has he bought at the store? = 他在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened at the party last night? = 昨晚的派对发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 决定做出之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon? = 到明天下午他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
