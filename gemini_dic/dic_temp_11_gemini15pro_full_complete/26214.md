butterers:/ˈbʌtərərz/
n.
奉承者，拍马屁的人
People who flatter others excessively, especially to gain favor.
He's surrounded by butterers who tell him what he wants to hear. 他周围都是些奉承者，只会说他想听的话。
synonyms: flatterers, sycophants, yes-men
antonyms: critics, dissenters

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "to butter up" (meaning to flatter), which originates from the idea of spreading butter on bread, making it more palatable.  The "-er" suffix denotes a person who performs the action.
