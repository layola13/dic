disinter:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːr/ /ˌdɪsɪnˈtər/
verb
掘出; 挖出
To dig up (something, such as a corpse) from the earth; unearth.  To bring back from obscurity or oblivion.
The archaeologists disinterred the ancient king's remains. 考古学家挖出了古代国王的遗骸。

The historian disinterred long-forgotten documents relating to the scandal.  历史学家挖掘出了与丑闻相关的早已被遗忘的文件。
近义词: exhume, unearth, excavate
反义词: bury, inter
词性: verb

disinter:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːr/ /ˌdɪsɪnˈtər/
noun (rare)
掘出; 挖出 (名词形式较少使用)
The act of disinterring.
The disinterment of the body caused a public outcry. 尸体的挖掘引起了公众的强烈抗议。
近义词: exhumation, unearthing, excavation
反义词: burial, interment
词性: noun
