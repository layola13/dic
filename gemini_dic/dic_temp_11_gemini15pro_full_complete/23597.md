bramble:/ˈbræmbəl/| n.|荆棘，黑莓|A prickly shrub, especially a blackberry or raspberry bush.  A tangled mass of prickly shrubs.|The path was blocked by brambles.|近义词：thornbush, thicket, prickle|反义词: smooth, clear|noun


bramble:/ˈbræmbəl/| v.|被荆棘缠住，困难重重地前进|To scramble or clamber over rough, overgrown terrain.|We had to bramble through the dense undergrowth.|近义词：scramble, clamber|反义词: glide, sail|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bræmel, from Proto-Germanic *brēm-, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhrem- (“to project, point”). Cognate with Dutch braam (“blackberry”), German Brombeere (“blackberry”), Swedish björnbär (“blackberry”).
