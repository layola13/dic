nonavailability:/ˌnɒnəveɪləˈbɪləti/ |n.|不可用性，缺乏，无法获得|The nonavailability of key resources hampered the project. 关键资源的缺乏阻碍了该项目。|例句：The nonavailability of the witness delayed the trial.  证人无法出庭导致审判延期。

The report cited the nonavailability of affordable housing as a major problem. 报告指出，经济适用房的缺乏是一个主要问题。|近义词：unavailability, lack, absence, scarcity|反义词：availability, presence, abundance|词性:名词


Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + availability (from available, from Old French avail- "to be of use, profit, value,"  ultimately from Latin valēre "to be strong, have power")
