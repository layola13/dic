modelers:/ˈmɑːdələrz (US), ˈmɒdələz (UK)| n.| 建模者，建模人员|People who create models, often mathematical or computational, to represent and understand complex systems or processes.  They can work in various fields like engineering, finance, science, etc.|The modelers used sophisticated software to simulate the effects of climate change.  建模人员使用先进的软件来模拟气候变化的影响。| The modelers predicted a significant increase in sea levels. 建模人员预测海平面会显著上升。|synonyms: simulators, analysts, designers|antonyms: observers, measurers|noun
