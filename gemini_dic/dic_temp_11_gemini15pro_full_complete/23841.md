breadcrumb:/ˈbredkrʌm/ (US), /ˈbredkrʌm/ (UK)| n. |面包屑，线索，路径导航|A small piece of bread, especially one accidentally dropped or thrown down; a series of clues or pieces of information used to find something, especially on the internet. |例句：
The children left a trail of breadcrumbs in the forest. 孩子们在森林里留下了一串面包屑。

Hansel and Gretel dropped breadcrumbs to find their way back home.  韩塞尔和格莱特撒下面包屑以便找到回家的路。

The website uses breadcrumbs to help users navigate its complex structure.  这个网站使用面包屑导航来帮助用户浏览其复杂的结构。

Clicking on a breadcrumb takes you to the corresponding page. 点击面包屑导航可以带你到相应的页面。|近义词：clue, hint, trail, navigation, pathway|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "dead end" in a metaphorical sense)|noun
