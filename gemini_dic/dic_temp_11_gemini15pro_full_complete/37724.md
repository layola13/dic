comparers:/kəmˈpeərərz/ | n. | 比较者；比较器 | People or things that compare something. | The comparers analyzed the two products carefully. 比较者仔细分析了这两种产品。 |  The comparers found significant differences between the two brands. 比较者发现这两个品牌之间存在显著差异。| synonyms:  analysts, assessors, evaluators | antonyms:  N/A | noun

compare:/kəmˈpeər/ | v. | 比较 | To examine the similarities and differences between two or more things. |  She compared the prices of several different laptops before buying one. 她比较了几款不同笔记本电脑的价格才买了一台。 | He compared his handwriting to his father's. 他把自己的笔迹和他父亲的笔迹作了比较。| synonyms: contrast, juxtapose,  liken,  match | antonyms: differentiate, distinguish | verb
