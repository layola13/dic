behead:/bɪˈhed/| vt.|斩首，砍头|To cut off the head of; decapitate.|The king ordered the traitor to be beheaded. 国王下令将叛徒斩首。|Synonyms: decapitate, execute, guillotine|Antonyms: spare, pardon|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + head.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "to make," "to cause to be," or "completely."  "Head" refers to the top part of the body.  Therefore, "behead" literally means "to cause to be without a head."
