kma:/ˈkeɪmˌeɪ/ |abbr.| Kilometer, Area|公里，区域| KMA represents "kilometer area". It's a less common abbreviation and often used in specialized contexts like meteorology or geography to denote an area measured in square kilometers. For example, a weather radar might cover a certain KMA. |The storm covered a wide KMA.|近义词: sq km, km²|反义词: N/A|缩写词
