overcivility: /ˌoʊvərˌsɪˈvɪlɪti/ | n. | 过分礼貌，过分客气 | Excessive politeness or courtesy, sometimes to the point of being insincere or obsequious. |  Her overcivility made him suspicious. 她的过分客气让他起了疑心。|  He greeted me with such overcivility that I wondered what he wanted. 他对我如此客气，让我怀疑他想要什么。 | 近义词: obsequiousness, excessive politeness, fawning, sycophancy | 反义词: rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, disrespect | 词性: noun

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "civility" (from Middle French "civilité," from Latin "civilitas," meaning "citizenship, courtesy")
