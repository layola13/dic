cetacean:/sɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |鲸类动物|A marine mammal of the order Cetacea ; a whale, dolphin, or porpoise.|例句：Whales and dolphins are cetaceans. 鲸鱼和海豚都是鲸类动物。|例句：The documentary focused on the migration patterns of various cetaceans.  这部纪录片重点关注各种鲸类动物的迁徙模式。|近义词：whale, dolphin, porpoise|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑用terrestrial mammal 陆生哺乳动物)|词性:noun
