nonimpedimental:/ˌnɒnɪmˌpedɪˈmentl/
adjective
无障碍的，不阻碍的
Not presenting any impediment; not hindering or obstructive.
The council's decision was nonimpedimental to the project's progress. 委员会的决定没有阻碍项目的进展。
His nonimpedimental attitude made working with him a pleasure. 他不阻挠的态度使与他共事成为一种乐趣。
Synonyms: unobstructed, unhindered, facilitating, conducive
Antonyms: obstructive, hindering, impeding, inhibiting
词性: adjective
