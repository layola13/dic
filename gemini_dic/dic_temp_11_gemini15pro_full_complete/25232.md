budlet:/ˈbʌdlɪt/
n.
小芽，花蕾
A small bud, especially a developing leaf bud.
The rose bush was covered in tiny budlets, promising a beautiful bloom in the spring. 玫瑰丛上覆盖着细小的花蕾，预示着春天会开出美丽的花朵。

The budlets on the trees are starting to swell. 树上的小芽开始膨胀了。

synonym: sprout, shoot,  germ
antonym:  flower, blossom (成熟的花)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bud + -let (diminutive suffix).  "Bud" comes from Middle English *budde, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Dutch bot "bud."  The suffix "-let" comes from Old French -elet, a diminutive suffix.
