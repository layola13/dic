fugued:/fjuːɡd/
adj.
神志不清的，精神错乱的；(尤指)处于神游状态的
Resembling or suggestive of a fugue, especially in being disordered or incoherent.
例句：He wandered the streets in a fugued state, unable to remember his name or where he lived. 他在神志不清的状态下在街上游荡，记不起自己的名字和住处。

例句：The patient appeared fugued and disoriented after the head injury.  头部受伤后，病人显得精神错乱，方向感不清。

近义词：confused, disoriented, bewildered, dazed, delirious
反义词：lucid, clearheaded, coherent

词性: adjective
词源: From the musical term "fugue," from Italian *fuga* "flight," from Latin *fugere* "to flee." The psychological sense derives from the idea of thoughts "fleeing" or becoming disconnected.
