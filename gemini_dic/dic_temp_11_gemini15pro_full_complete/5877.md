ambassadresses:/æmˈbæsədrɪsɪz/
n.
女大使；大使夫人
a female ambassador; the wife of an ambassador
The ambassadresses from several countries attended the summit.
几位女大使出席了峰会。
The president greeted the ambassadresses warmly.
总统热情地迎接了大使夫人。
synonyms: female ambassadors, envoys (female), diplomats (female)
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a person's role)
