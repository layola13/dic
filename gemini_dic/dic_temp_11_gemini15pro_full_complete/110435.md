likelinesses:/ˈlaɪklihʊdnɪsɪz/| n. |可能性，概率|The plural form of "likeliness," referring to the probabilities or chances of something happening.  It implies multiple potential outcomes or scenarios being evaluated for their probability. |The likelinesses of rain are increasing as the clouds gather.  下雨的可能性随着云的聚集而增加。|The book explored the various likelinesses of different economic scenarios. 这本书探讨了不同经济情景的各种可能性。|近义词：probabilities, chances, possibilities|反义词: improbabilities, unlikelihoods|词性:noun (plural)
