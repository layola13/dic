farrows:/ˈfæroʊz/| n.|一窝小猪; 产仔;分娩|A litter of pigs; the act of giving birth to piglets.|The sow farrowed ten healthy piglets. 这头母猪产下了十头健康的小猪。|近义词：litter, offspring|反义词: None (No direct opposite for the birthing process of pigs)|词性:noun

farrows:/ˈfæroʊz/| v.|(母猪)产仔; 分娩| (of a sow) to give birth to piglets.|The sow is expected to farrow next week. 预计这头母猪下周会产仔。|近义词：give birth, deliver|反义词: None (No direct opposite for the birthing process of pigs)|词性:verb
