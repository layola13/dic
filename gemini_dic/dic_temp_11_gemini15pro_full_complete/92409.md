hypogene:/ˈhaɪpədʒiːn/
adjective
深成的；地下生成的
formed or originating under the surface of the earth.
例句：Hypogene mineral deposits are formed deep within the Earth. 深成矿床形成于地球深处。

例句：The hypogene caves were formed by rising hydrothermal fluids.  这些深成洞穴是由上升的热液形成的。
近义词：subterranean, plutonic
反义词：supergene, exogenetic
词性: adjective
