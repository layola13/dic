outscolding:/ˌaʊtˈskoʊldɪŋ/
|n.|责骂，斥责|an act of scolding someone angrily and at length|She gave him a thorough outscolding for his carelessness.|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, scolding|反义词:praise, commendation|词性:名词
outscold:/ˌaʊtˈskoʊld/
|v.|大声责骂，胜过…的责骂|to scold someone loudly and angrily, or to scold someone more severely than someone else does|She outscolded him for his messy room.|近义词：berate, castigate, reprimand|反义词:praise, commend|词性:动词
