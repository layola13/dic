lusciously:/ˈlʌʃəsli/ | adv. | 美味地，芬芳地，诱人地 | in a way that is pleasing to the senses, especially taste or smell.  |The cake was lusciously decorated with fresh berries and whipped cream. 蛋糕上装饰着新鲜的浆果和鲜奶油，看起来非常诱人。|  The steak was lusciously seasoned and cooked to perfection. 牛排的调味非常美味，而且烹饪得恰到好处。 | 近义词: deliciously, delectably, scrumptiously, temptingly | 反义词: blandly, unappetizingly, distastefully | 词性: 副词

词源: From luscious + -ly.  Luscious comes from Middle English *luscius, from Old French *luxurieux "luxurious," from Latin luxuriosus, from luxuria "luxury, abundance."
