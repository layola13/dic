catlinite: /ˈkæt.lɪ.naɪt/
noun
红管石，烟斗泥
A reddish clay mineral, consisting of iron-rich palygorskite, found in southwestern Minnesota and used by Native Americans for making pipes.
The Native Americans carved intricate designs into the catlinite pipes.  美国原住民在红管石烟斗上雕刻复杂的图案。
Synonyms: pipe clay, pipestone
Antonyms: N/A
