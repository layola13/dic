outkings:/ˌaʊtˈkɪŋz/
verb
胜过；在…中称王
To surpass or defeat someone, especially a king or ruler, in power, skill, or achievement.
He outkinged all the other monarchs in wisdom and diplomacy. 他在智慧和外交方面胜过所有其他君主。
She outkinged her rivals in the business world. 她在商界胜过她的竞争对手。
surpass, outdo, outperform, eclipse
