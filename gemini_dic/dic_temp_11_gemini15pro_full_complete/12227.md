asbestosis:/ˌæsbɛsˈtoʊsɪs/
n.
石棉肺，石棉沉着病
A chronic lung disease caused by inhaling asbestos fibers.  It is characterized by scarring and inflammation of the lung tissue.
He developed asbestosis after years of working in a shipyard.  他在造船厂工作多年后患上了石棉肺。

近义词:
pulmonary fibrosis, pneumoconiosis
反义词:
(No direct antonym for a specific disease.  Could consider "healthy lungs" as a contrasting concept.)  没有针对特定疾病的直接反义词。可以将“健康的肺”视为对比概念。
词性: noun
