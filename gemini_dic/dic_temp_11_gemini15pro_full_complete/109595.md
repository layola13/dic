lethargy:/ˈleθərdʒi/| n.| 昏睡；无精打采；倦怠；迟钝|A state of tiredness, inactivity, and a lack of enthusiasm.|Example 1:  The summer heat induced a state of lethargy in everyone.  夏天的炎热使每个人都昏昏欲睡。

Example 2: He emerged from the illness in a state of profound lethargy. 他从病中恢复过来，却感到极度倦怠。|Synonyms: sluggishness, inertia, lassitude, apathy, torpor|Antonyms: energy, vigor, activity, enthusiasm|noun
