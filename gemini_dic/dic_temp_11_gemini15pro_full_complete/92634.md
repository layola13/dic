ibc:/ˈaɪ biː siː/ | n. | 中间散货集装箱; 集装箱; |Intermediate Bulk Container (IBC) is a reusable industrial container designed for the transport and storage of bulk liquid and granular substances, such as chemicals, food ingredients, solvents, pharmaceuticals, etc. |  Example 1: The IBC was filled with a hazardous chemical and carefully transported.  这个中间散货集装箱装满了危险化学品，经过仔细运输。

Example 2:  Several IBCs of olive oil were delivered to the factory.  几箱橄榄油被运送到工厂。| 近义词: container, tote, tank | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "package" for smaller quantities) | 词性: noun
