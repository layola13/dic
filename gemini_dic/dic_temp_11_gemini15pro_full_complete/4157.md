airborne:/ˈɛərbɔːrn/ (US), /ˈɛːbɔːn/ (UK)| adj.|空运的；空气传播的；在空中的|Carried by air; transported by aircraft. Also refers to something that is in the air, or capable of flying.|例句：The airborne troops landed behind enemy lines. 空降部队在敌后着陆。

例句：The virus is airborne, so wear a mask. 病毒是空气传播的，所以要戴口罩。

近义词：aerial, aloft, flying|反义词:grounded, landed|词性:形容词


airborne:/ˈɛərbɔːrn/ (US), /ˈɛːbɔːn/ (UK)| n.|空降兵|Troops transported by air, typically paratroopers.|例句：The airborne were dropped into the combat zone at dawn. 空降兵在黎明时分被空投到作战区。

例句：The airborne suffered heavy casualties during the operation. 空降兵在行动中伤亡惨重。


近义词：paratroopers|反义词: infantry|词性:名词


词源：air + borne (past participle of bear, meaning "to carry")
