octillion:/ɒkˈtɪljən/ /ɑːkˈtɪljən/| n. |百万的八次方；千万亿；10^27（美式）/ 10^48（英式）|A number equal to 1 followed by 27 zeros (American system) or 48 zeros (British system).|The US national debt is now in the octillions. (美式) 美国国债现在已经达到了千万亿级别。

The number of atoms in the universe is estimated to be in the octillions. (美式) 宇宙中的原子数量估计有千万亿之多。

An octillion is a truly massive number, difficult to even comprehend. (英式) 千万亿是一个非常大的数字，甚至难以理解。

He inherited an octillion pounds, making him unbelievably wealthy. (英式) 他继承了千万亿英镑，这使他富得难以置信。|近义词：(depending on system) quadrillion, sextillion, septillion, nonillion, decillion|反义词: (no direct antonym, consider "small number," "fraction," "infinitesimal")|词性:noun
