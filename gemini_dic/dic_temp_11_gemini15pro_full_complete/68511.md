fanaticism:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəm/
n.
狂热; 盲信; 入迷
Excessive and single-minded zeal or devotion to a cause, belief, or pursuit.
His fanaticism for the cause led him to extreme actions. 他对这项事业的狂热导致他采取了极端行动。

fanaticism:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəm/
n.
狂热; 盲信; 入迷
The quality of being fanatical.
Religious fanaticism can be dangerous. 宗教狂热可能很危险。


近义词:zealotry, extremism, bigotry
反义词:moderation, tolerance
词性:noun
