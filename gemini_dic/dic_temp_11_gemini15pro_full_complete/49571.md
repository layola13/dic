dementedness:/dɪˈmɛntɪdnəs/ | n. |痴呆，精神错乱|The state of being demented; dementia.  A severe decline in mental abilities, including memory, thinking, and judgment, that interferes with daily life.|His dementedness made it difficult for him to recognize his own family.|Synonyms: dementia, insanity, madness, lunacy|Antonyms: sanity, lucidity, rationality|noun
