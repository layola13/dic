drip-dried:/ˈdrɪp draɪd/
adjective
滴干的；晾干的
hung up to dry, without being wrung out or tumble-dried.  Allowed to dry by dripping.
The drip-dried shirts were stiff and uncomfortable. 这些滴干的衬衫又硬又不舒服。
My drip-dried laundry hung on the clothesline. 我的滴干的衣服挂在晾衣绳上。
近义词:air-dried, line-dried
反义词:tumble-dried, spin-dried
词性:adjective


drip-dried:/ˈdrɪp draɪd/
verb
滴干；晾干
to dry something by hanging it up and letting the water drip off.
I drip-dried my swimsuit after swimming in the ocean. 我在海里游泳后把泳衣滴干了。
We drip-dried the towels on the balcony. 我们把毛巾放在阳台上滴干。
近义词:air-dry, line-dry
反义词:tumble-dry, spin-dry
词性:verb
