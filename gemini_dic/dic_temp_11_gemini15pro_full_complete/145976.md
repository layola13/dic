overdo:/ˌoʊvərˈduː/ /ˌəʊvəˈduː/| v.|过度；做过头；夸张|To do something to an excessive degree; to exaggerate.|例句：She overdid the makeup and looked like a clown. 她妆化得太浓了，看起来像个小丑。

例句：Don't overdo the exercise, or you'll hurt yourself. 不要过度运动，否则会伤到自己。

近义词：exaggerate, overact, overstate|反义词:underdo, understate|词性:动词

overdo:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌn/ /ˌəʊvəˈdʌn/| adj.|过度的；做得过分的|Cooked for too long.|例句：The steak was overdone and dry. 牛排烤得太老了，很干。

例句：Her acting was a bit overdone. 她的表演有点过火。


近义词：overcooked, exaggerated|反义词:underdone, undercooked|词性:形容词
