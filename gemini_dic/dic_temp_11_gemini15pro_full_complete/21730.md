boastance: /ˈboʊstəns/
n.
自夸; 吹嘘; 夸耀
An act of boasting; something boasted about.  The act or an instance of boasting.
He spoke with boastance about his achievements. 他夸耀着自己的成就。
The general's boastance about his invincibility proved to be premature. 将军的自夸最终证明为时过早。
近义词: boasting, bragging, vaunting, swaggering
反义词: modesty, humility
词性: noun
