mortify:/ˈmɔːrtɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈmɔːtɪfaɪ/ (UK)| vt.|使感屈辱；使感羞愧；克制；抑制（尤指肉体上的欲望）|To cause (someone) to feel extremely embarrassed or humiliated.  To subdue or deaden (the body, bodily appetites, etc.) especially by abstinence or asceticism.|例句：She was mortified by her child's behavior in the restaurant. 她对孩子在餐厅里的行为感到羞愧。

例句：He tried to mortify the flesh by fasting. 他试图通过禁食来克制肉体的欲望。|近义词：humiliate, shame, embarrass, subdue, repress|反义词：exalt, praise, gratify|词性：动词
