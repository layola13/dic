marriable:/ˈmærɪəbl/| adj.|适婚的，可以结婚的|Suitable for marriage; of a suitable age or condition to get married.|She's a perfectly marriable young woman. 她是一位完全适婚的年轻女性。|He considered himself marriable after landing a stable job. 他找到一份稳定的工作后认为自己可以结婚了。|近义词：eligible, suitable, nubile|反义词：unmarriable, ineligible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From marry + -able.  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being".  "Marry" comes from Old French marier, from Latin marītāre "to wed," frequentative of marītus "married, husband."
