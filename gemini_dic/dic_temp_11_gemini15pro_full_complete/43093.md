cowslipped:/ˈkaʊslɪpt/| adj.|像西洋樱草花的，淡黄色的|Covered or colored like the Cowslip flower, typically pale yellow. |The meadows were cowslipped with early spring flowers.|近义词：primrose-colored, pale yellow, buttery yellow|反义词: dark, vibrant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "cowslip" (noun), which comes from Old English *cūslyppe, possibly meaning "cow dung," referring to the places where cowslips often grow.
