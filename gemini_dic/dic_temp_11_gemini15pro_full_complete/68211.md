faired:/fɛəd/ | v. |  进展；成功；遭遇；表现 |  Performed or progressed in a specified way. | The company faired well despite the economic downturn.  这家公司尽管经济不景气，但仍然表现良好。|  The new product faired poorly in the market.  这个新产品在市场上表现不佳。 | synonyms: performed, progressed, fared | antonyms: failed, floundered | verb
