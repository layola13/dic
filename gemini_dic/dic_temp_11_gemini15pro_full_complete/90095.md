hostlerships:/ˈhɑːstlərʃɪps/
n.
马房管理(总称); 旅店老板的职位(总称)
The provision or management of stables, especially at an inn.  Positions of innkeepers collectively.
The decline of stagecoach travel led to a decrease in hostlerships across the country.  驿站马车旅行的衰落导致全国各地的旅店老板职位减少。
The hostlerships along the old trade route were once thriving businesses.  沿着古老贸易路线的旅店曾经是繁荣的生意。
近义词: innkeeping, stable management
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider words like "unemployment," "abandonment" if referring to the decline of the profession.)
