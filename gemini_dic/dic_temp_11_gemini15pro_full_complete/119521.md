micropyle:/ˈmaɪkrəˌpaɪl/| n. |珠孔|A small opening in the surface of an ovule, through which the pollen tube penetrates, or in the shell of an insect egg, through which sperm enter.|The pollen tube enters the ovule through the micropyle. 花粉管通过珠孔进入胚珠。|Synonyms: aperture, opening, pore|Antonyms: N/A|noun

micropyle:/ˈmaɪkrəˌpaɪl/| n. |卵壳孔|A small opening in the shell of an insect egg, through which sperm enter for fertilization.|The micropyle allows sperm to reach the egg for fertilization. 卵壳孔允许精子到达卵子进行受精。|Synonyms: aperture, opening, pore|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mīkros* (small) + *púlē* (gate, opening)
