nonpoisonously:/ˌnɒnˈpɔɪzənəsli/
adverb
无毒地
in a way that is not poisonous.
The mushrooms were identified as nonpoisonously and safe to eat. 这些蘑菇被鉴定为无毒的，可以安全食用。
The snake, though resembling a venomous species, behaved nonpoisonously. 虽然这条蛇长得像毒蛇，但它的行为表明它没有毒。
Synonyms: harmlessly, safely, innocuously
Antonyms: poisonously, toxically, venomously
词性: 副词
