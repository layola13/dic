maidishnesses:/ˈmeɪdɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
少女气质；处女情态
The quality of being maidish; excessive modesty or primness.
She maintained a certain maidishnesses even in her thirties. 她即使到了三十多岁，仍然保持着某种少女气质。
近义词: modesty, demureness, primness, coyness
反义词: boldness, immodesty, forwardness
词性: noun


maidish:/ˈmeɪdɪʃ/
adj.
少女的；处女的；适于少女的
Resembling or characteristic of a maid; modest or demure.
She had a maidish charm about her. 她有一种少女的魅力。
近义词: modest, demure, virginal, innocent
反义词: immodest, bold, experienced
词性: adjective
