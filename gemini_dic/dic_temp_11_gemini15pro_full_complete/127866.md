neoclassically:/ˌniːoʊˈklæsɪkli/ | adv. | 新古典主义地 | In a way that recalls or uses elements of the neoclassical style or period. | The building was neoclassically designed with columns and a pediment. 这座建筑采用新古典主义设计，带有柱子和山形墙。|  The music was composed neoclassically, incorporating Baroque and Classical elements.  这首音乐是用新古典主义风格创作的，融合了巴洛克和古典元素。 | 近义词: classically | 反义词: romantically | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
neo- (prefix): new, recent (from Greek "neos")
classical (adjective): relating to the classical period in art, literature, etc.
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
