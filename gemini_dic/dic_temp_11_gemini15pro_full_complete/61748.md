emparadise:/ɪmˈpærədaɪs/| vt.|使……无比快乐；使……置身天堂|To make someone extremely happy; to put someone in a state of bliss.|She was emparadised by the beautiful music. 她被美妙的音乐陶醉了。|The spa treatment emparadised her.  水疗护理让她无比快乐。|近义词：delight, enchant, bliss, transport|反义词：torment, torture|词性:动词

词源:  From em- (put into) + paradise (paradise).  由em-（置于…中）+ paradise（天堂）组成。
