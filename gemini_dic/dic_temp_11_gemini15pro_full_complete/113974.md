词源:
From Late Latin magi, plural of magus (“magician, astrologer”), from Greek μάγος (mágos), from Old Persian *maguš (whence also Avestan 𐬨𐬀𐬔𐬀𐬌𐬥 (maguš, “member of the Median tribe of Magi”)). Doublet of mage.

注意事项:
Magi 始终是复数形式，单数形式是 magus.
