calluses:/ˈkæləsɪz/ (US) /ˈkæləsɪz/ (UK)| n. |老茧；硬结；胼胝|A thickened and hardened part of the skin or soft tissue, especially in an area that has been subjected to friction.|Example:  The guitarist had calluses on his fingertips.  吉他手的手指上有老茧。
