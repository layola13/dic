fezzes:/ˈfɛzɪz/
noun
红缨帽（复数）
A brimless, cone-shaped felt hat with a tassel, worn primarily by men in some Middle Eastern and North African countries.
The tourists bought fezzes as souvenirs. 游客们买了红缨帽作为纪念品。
近义词:tarboosh
反义词:(No direct antonym for a specific type of hat)
词性:n.
