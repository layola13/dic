enlighteningly:/ɪnˈlaɪtnɪŋli/
adverb
启发地；启迪地；使人领悟地
in a way that provides insight or understanding
Her book enlighteningly explores the complexities of human relationships. 她的书以启迪的方式探讨了人际关系的复杂性。

The professor enlighteningly explained the theory of relativity. 教授深入浅出地解释了相对论。

Synonym: informatively, instructively, revealingly
Antonym: obscurely, confusingly
词性: 副词
