half-Aristotelian:/ˌhæf ˌærɪstəˈtiːliən/ | adj. | 半亚里士多德式的 | Partially or incompletely Aristotelian; resembling or influenced by Aristotle's philosophy but not fully adhering to it. | This philosopher's approach can be characterized as half-Aristotelian, incorporating elements of empiricism while rejecting his teleological views.  这位哲学家的方法可以被描述为半亚里士多德式的，它融合了经验主义的元素，同时又拒绝了他的目的论观点。 | Near Synonyms: semi-Aristotelian, quasi-Aristotelian | Antonyms: non-Aristotelian, anti-Aristotelian | Adjective
