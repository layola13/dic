jaded:/ˈdʒeɪdɪd/
adjective
厌倦的，疲惫不堪的
tired, bored, or lacking enthusiasm, typically after having had too much of something
例句：He became jaded with city life and moved to the countryside. 他厌倦了城市生活，搬到了乡下。

例句：Travel can sometimes leave you feeling jaded rather than refreshed. 旅行有时会让你感到疲惫不堪，而不是神清气爽。

近义词：worn-out, weary, exhausted, bored, cynical, disillusioned
反义词：enthusiastic, eager, keen, refreshed, invigorated
词性: adjective
