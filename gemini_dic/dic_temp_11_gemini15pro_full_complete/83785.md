hairily:/ˈhɛərɪli/ | adj. | 多毛地，毛茸茸地 | Covered with hair, especially in a noticeable or dense manner. | The dog was hairly and shed a lot. 这只狗毛很多，掉的也很多。|  The caterpillar was surprisingly hairly.  这条毛毛虫毛茸茸的，令人惊讶。 | 近义词: furry, shaggy, downy | 反义词: smooth, bald, hairless | 词性: adjective

词源: From *hair* + *-ly*, a common suffix forming adjectives. Old English *hǣr*, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *haar* and German *Haar*.
