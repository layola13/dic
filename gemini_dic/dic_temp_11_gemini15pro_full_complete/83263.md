gymnolaemata:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈliːmətə/ | n. | 裸唇綱 | A class of Bryozoa characterized by a circular lophophore and a lack of an operculum.  These are mostly marine, colonial animals. | Example: Gymnolaemata are a diverse group of aquatic invertebrates. 裸唇綱是水生無脊椎動物的一個多樣化群體。 | Example:  Scientists are studying the reproductive strategies of gymnolaemata. 科學家正在研究裸唇綱的繁殖策略。 | 近义词:  Bryozoa (in a broader sense) | 反义词:  None (It's a specific taxonomic classification) | 词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek γυμνός (gumnós, “naked, bare”) + λαιμός (laimós, “throat”).  Refers to the lack of an operculum, a covering for the "throat" or mouth area.
