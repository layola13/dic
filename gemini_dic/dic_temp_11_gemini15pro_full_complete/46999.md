dashed-off:/ˈdæʃt ˈɒf/ | adj. | 倉促完成的；草草寫成的；匆忙的 | Done quickly and carelessly; hastily written or composed. |  She sent a dashed-off email before leaving for the airport. 她赶在去机场前匆忙发了一封电子邮件。|  He gave a dashed-off performance, clearly unprepared.  他匆忙完成了一场演出，显然没有做好准备。 | hasty, hurried, cursory, perfunctory |  meticulous, careful, thorough | adjective
