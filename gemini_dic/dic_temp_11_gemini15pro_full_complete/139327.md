obole:/ˈɒbəʊl (UK), ˈoʊboʊl (US)| n.| 小额钱币；微薄的捐献；小东西|A small coin or sum of money, especially one given as an offering or contribution; something small or insignificant. |例句：He offered an obole to the beggar. 他给了乞丐一个小钱。

例句：Her contribution was a mere obole compared to the vast sum needed. 与所需巨款相比，她的贡献不过是杯水车薪。|近义词：mite, pittance, trifle, modicum |反义词: fortune, wealth |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *obole*, from Latin *obolus*, from Ancient Greek *ὀβολός (obolos)*, originally a small spit or skewer of metal used as currency, later a small silver coin, one-sixth of a drachma.
