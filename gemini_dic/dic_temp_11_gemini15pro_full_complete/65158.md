ethnicist:/ˈɛθnɪsɪst/| n.|种族主义者；民族学家|A person who studies or specializes in ethnicity and ethnic relations, sometimes with a bias towards their own group. |例句：The ethnicist argued that the conflict was rooted in historical grievances.  这位民族学家认为，这场冲突的根源在于历史上的积怨。

例句：Some critics accused the ethnicist of promoting a divisive agenda.  一些批评者指责这位种族主义者鼓吹分裂议程。|近义词：ethnologist, anthropologist, sociologist|反义词:racist (sometimes, depending on context), integrationist|noun
