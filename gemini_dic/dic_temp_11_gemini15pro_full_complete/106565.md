kruppize:/ˈkrʊpɪzaɪz/
verb
克虏伯化，使像克虏伯公司那样经营
To organize (an industry, company, etc.) along the lines of the Krupp works, especially with regard to large-scale, centralized, and integrated operations; to build up (a business, etc.) into a large and powerful concern.
The government was accused of trying to Kruppize the steel industry. 政府被指控试图将钢铁工业克虏伯化。
Synonyms: monopolize, centralize, consolidate
Antonyms: decentralize, fragment
词性: 动词
