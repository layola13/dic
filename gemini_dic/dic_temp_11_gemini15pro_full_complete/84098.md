half-intellectual:/ˌhæf ɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/ | adj. | 半吊子知识分子；附庸风雅的 | Someone who is considered to have some intellectual pretensions but not genuine intellectual depth.  A person who appears or tries to appear intelligent but is not truly so. |  He dismissed them as half-intellectuals, pretending to understand things they didn't. 他认为他们只是一群半吊子知识分子，假装理解他们不懂的事情。

He filled his apartment with books to appear half-intellectual. 他把自己的公寓里堆满了书，以显得自己有点文化。 | 近义词：pseudo-intellectual, dilettante,  | 反义词：intellectual, scholar, learned | 词性：形容词
