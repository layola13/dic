calcareous:/kælˈkeəriəs/
adjective
钙质的; 含钙的
Containing calcium carbonate; chalky.
The soil in this area is calcareous.  这个地区的土壤是钙质的。
近义词: chalky, limy
反义词: siliceous


calcariferous:/ˌkælkəˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
含钙的；产生碳酸钙的
Containing or producing calcium carbonate.
Calcariferous glands are found in some invertebrates.  一些无脊椎动物体内有钙质腺。
近义词: calcareous, chalky
反义词: siliceous


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin calcarius (“of lime”) + -ferous.  Ultimately from Latin calx (“lime”).
