finesseful:/fɪˈnɛsfʊl/
adjective
巧妙的；精致的；优雅的
Having or showing great skill and sensitivity.
She handled the delicate situation with finesseful diplomacy. 她以巧妙而老练的外交手腕处理了这一微妙的局势。
finesseful:/fɪˈnɛsfʊl/
adjective
灵巧的；娴熟的
Exhibiting grace and skill in performance or execution.
The pianist's finesseful performance captivated the audience.  钢琴家娴熟的演奏迷住了观众。

近义词: skillful, adroit, deft, subtle, refined, elegant, polished
反义词: clumsy, awkward, inept, crude, unsubtle

词性: adjective
词源: From finesse + -ful.  Finesse itself comes from French, ultimately from Latin *finititia, from finitus "finished," past participle of finire "to limit, finish."
