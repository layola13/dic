multiplying:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 乘；使相乘；使增加；使繁殖 |  The process of increasing something in number or quantity. | We are multiplying our efforts to reach a wider audience. 我们正在加倍努力以接触更广泛的受众。| synonyms: increasing, amplifying, augmenting, expanding | antonyms: dividing, reducing, decreasing, diminishing | verb (present participle)

multiplying:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 倍增的；乘法的 | Having the ability to multiply or increase. | The multiplying effect of social media can be significant. 社交媒体的倍增效应可能非常显著。| synonyms: reproductive, proliferating, expanding | antonyms: reductive, diminishing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French multiplier, from Latin multiplicare "to increase," from multiplex "having many folds," from multus "many" + plicare "to fold."
