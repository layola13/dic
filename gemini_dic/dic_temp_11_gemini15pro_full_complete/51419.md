deterrers:/dɪˈtɜːrərz/
noun
威慑物；制止物；阻吓物
Something that deters people from doing something.
Nuclear weapons are seen as the ultimate deterrent.  核武器被视为最终的威慑力量。
The high price of the tickets was a deterrent to many fans.  高昂的票价让很多球迷望而却步。
Synonyms: obstacle, impediment, discouragement, curb, restraint
Antonyms: incentive, encouragement, inducement
n.
