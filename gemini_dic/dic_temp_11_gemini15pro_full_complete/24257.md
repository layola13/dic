briefly:/ˈbriːfli/ | adv. | 简要地，短暂地 | In a short and concise manner; for a short time. | I briefly explained the situation. 我简要地解释了情况。| shortly, concisely, quickly, in short | lengthily, at length | adverb

Etymology: From Middle English *brefliche, from Old French brievement, from brief (“short, brief”) + -ment (“-ly”).
