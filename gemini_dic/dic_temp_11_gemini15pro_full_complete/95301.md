incarnationist:/ɪnkɑːrˈneɪʃənɪst/
n.
道成肉身论者；化身论者
A believer in the incarnation of a deity or spirit in some earthly form.
The incarnationist believed that the divine being had taken human form.  道成肉身论者相信神灵已化身为人。
Synonyms: incarnation believer, embodiment theorist
Antonyms: non-incarnationist, spiritualist
noun
