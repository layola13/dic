brassed:/bræst/
adjective
厚脸皮的，无耻的；黄铜色的
Having the appearance of brass; impudent, shameless.
例句：He had a brassed off look on his face after being caught cheating.  他被抓到作弊后，脸上露出厚颜无耻的表情。

例句：The antique clock had brassed gears that still functioned perfectly.  这个古董钟的黄铜齿轮仍然运转良好。

近义词：brazen, shameless, impudent, bold, audacious, brassy
反义词：ashamed, embarrassed, modest, humble, shy
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From brass + -ed.  The sense of "impudent" likely arises from the association of brass with boldness or loudness.
