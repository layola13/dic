jogs:/dʒɑːɡz/| n. |慢跑，慢跑裤|A pair of sweatpants or other loose-fitting trousers worn for jogging or casual wear.  |She put on her jogs and went for a run. 她穿上慢跑裤去跑步了。|近义词：sweatpants, track pants, joggers |反义词:jeans, dress pants|Noun


jog:/dʒɑːɡ/| v. |慢跑，轻推，唤起|To run at a slow, steady pace.  To nudge or push gently. To cause someone to remember something. | I jog every morning for exercise. 我每天早上慢跑锻炼。|近义词：run, trot, nudge, prompt |反义词:sprint, race, hinder|Verb
