muffishness:/ˈmʌfɪʃnɪs/
n.
闷闷不乐；脾气坏；不友好
A state of being sullen, bad-tempered, or unfriendly.
He exhibited a certain muffishness after his team lost the game.  他在球队输掉比赛后表现出一种闷闷不乐的样子。
sullenness, grumpiness, surliness
amiability, friendliness, affability
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From muffy + -ness.  Muffy itself is likely related to muff (to handle clumsily), perhaps implying a clumsy or awkward social demeanor leading to perceived sullenness.
