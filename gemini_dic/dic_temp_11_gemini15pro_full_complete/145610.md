overconservatism: /ˌoʊvərˈkənˈsɜːrvətɪzəm/ | n. | 过度保守主义 | Excessive adherence to traditional practices or values; a reluctance to accept change or new ideas. |  The company's overconservatism prevented them from adapting to the changing market.  这家公司的过度保守阻碍了他们适应变化的市场。| synonyms:  reactionism, traditionalism, diehardism | antonyms: radicalism, progressivism, liberalism | noun


overconservative: /ˌoʊvərˈkənˈsɜːrvətɪv/ | adj. | 过度保守的 | Excessively cautious and resistant to change or innovation. |  Their overconservative investment strategy yielded minimal returns. 他们过度保守的投资策略收益甚微。 | synonyms: reactionary, hidebound, unprogressive | antonyms: radical, progressive, liberal | adjective


Etymology:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "conservative" (from Latin "conservare" - to preserve).
