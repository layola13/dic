disjunctions:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋkʃənz/| n.|分离，分裂，析取|A disjunction is a statement formed by combining two or more statements using the word "or."|The disjunction of "it is raining" and "it is sunny" is "it is raining or it is sunny."  下雨或晴天是“下雨”和“晴天”的析取。|synonyms: separation, division, disunion|antonyms: conjunction, union, connection|noun

disjunction:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋkʃən/| n.|逻辑上的“或”|In logic, disjunction is a logical connective that joins two or more propositions and is true if and only if at least one of the propositions is true.|The disjunction of P and Q is written as P ∨ Q. P和Q的析取写成P∨Q。|synonyms: or, either/or|antonyms: conjunction, and|noun
