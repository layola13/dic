intercepted:/ˌɪntərˈseptɪd/| v. |拦截，截获|to stop and catch something or someone before that thing or person is able to reach a particular place|The police intercepted a message from the criminals. 警方截获了罪犯的一条消息。|Synonyms: seize, catch, block, interrupt|Antonyms: release, allow, permit|verb

intercepted:/ˌɪntərˈseptɪd/| adj. |被拦截的，被截获的|stopped and caught before reaching a particular place|The intercepted letter revealed a secret plot.  被截获的信件揭露了一个秘密阴谋。|Synonyms: seized, caught, blocked|Antonyms: released, allowed, permitted|adjective
