blessing:/ˈblesɪŋ/| n.|祝福，恩赐，幸事|Something that is good or helpful; a prayer asking for God's favour.|例句：Children are a blessing. 孩子们是上天的恩赐。|近义词：gift, boon, advantage|反义词:curse, misfortune|noun

blessing:/ˈblesɪŋ/| n.|赐福，祝福礼|The act of asking for God's favour.|例句：The priest gave the blessing.  牧师进行了赐福。|近义词：benediction, consecration|反义词:curse, malediction|noun
