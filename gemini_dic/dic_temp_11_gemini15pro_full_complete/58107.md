droplight: /ˈdrɒplaɪt/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːplaɪt/ (US) | n. | 吊灯 | A single light fixture hung from the ceiling. | The droplight above the dining table cast a warm glow. 餐桌上方吊灯发出温暖的光芒。| 近义词: pendant light, hanging light | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑) wall light, floor lamp | 词性: noun

词源:  "drop" (meaning to fall or hang down) + "light" (referring to a source of illumination).  The word describes a light fixture that "drops" or hangs from the ceiling.
