bindingnesses:/ˈbaɪndɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
约束力，拘束力
The state or quality of being binding; the degree to which something is binding.
The bindingnesses of the contract are clear.  合同的约束力是明确的。
The various bindingnesses of the agreements need to be reviewed. 这些协议的不同约束力需要审查。
Synonym: obligation, commitment, constraint
Antonym: freedom, liberty
Noun
