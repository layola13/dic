例句：She has beautiful noisette colored eyes. 她有一双美丽的淡褐色眼睛。 |近义词：hazelnut, filbert (for the nut), light brown, beige (for the color)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the nut. For the color) dark, black |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *noisette*, diminutive of *noix* "nut," from Latin *nux* "nut."
