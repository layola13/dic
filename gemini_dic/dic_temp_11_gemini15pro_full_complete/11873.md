arrives:/əˈraɪvz/
verb
到达，抵达
comes to a place, reaches a destination
The train arrives at 8:00 am.  火车早上八点到达。
The package arrived in the mail yesterday. 包裹昨天邮寄到了。
近义词：reaches, comes, appears
反义词: departs, leaves
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *arriver, from Vulgar Latin *arripare "to come to shore," from Latin ad "to" + ripa "shore, bank."  源自古法语 *arriver，来自通俗拉丁语 *arripare “到达岸边”，由拉丁语 ad “到” + ripa “岸边，河岸” 组成。
