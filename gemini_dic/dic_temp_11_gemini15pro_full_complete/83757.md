hair-raisingly:/ˈherˌreɪzɪŋli/
adverb
令人毛骨悚然地；极其惊险地
in a way that makes you feel very frightened or excited
The car chase was hair-raisingly exciting. 汽车追逐场面惊险刺激。
近义词:terrifyingly, alarmingly, chillingly
反义词:calmingly, soothingly
词性:副词
