outfigure:/ˌaʊtˈfɪɡər/| v. |胜过；超过；在…方面比…高明|to surpass in skill or wisdom; to defeat by superior tactics or cleverness|例句：He outfigured his opponents in the chess tournament. 他在象棋锦标赛中智胜了对手。|例句：The experienced detective outfigured the criminal and solved the case.  经验丰富的侦探比罪犯技高一筹，破了案。|近义词：outwit, outsmart, surpass, excel, defeat|反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted, lose to|词性：动词

词源：out- + figure.  "Out-" prefix meaning to exceed or surpass. "Figure" in this context refers to calculating or planning.
