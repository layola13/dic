backsplash: /ˈbækˌsplæʃ/ (US), /ˈbækˌsplæʃ/ (UK) | n. | 防溅板；后挡板 | A panel or surface located behind a sink, stove, or countertop to protect the wall from splashes. |  Example: We installed a new tile backsplash in the kitchen. 我们在厨房安装了新的瓷砖防溅板。

Example:  The backsplash behind the stove was covered in grease.  炉子后面的防溅板满是油渍。

backsplash: | n. | 反溅；后浪 |  A splash or spray of something, especially water, that is thrown back.  | Example: The backsplash from the wave soaked my shoes. 海浪的反溅弄湿了我的鞋子。

Example: The backsplash from the boat’s propeller churned the water. 船螺旋桨的反溅搅动了水面。


backsplash: | n. | （尤指负面）反应，强烈反对 | A strong negative reaction or backlash. | Example:  The company faced a backsplash after announcing the price increase. 公司在宣布提价后，面临强烈反对。

Example: The politician's controversial comments caused a significant backsplash on social media.  这位政客有争议的言论在社交媒体上引发了重大反响。


backsplash:| n. |词性|
