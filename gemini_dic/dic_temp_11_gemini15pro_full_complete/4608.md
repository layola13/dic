alcyonic: /ælˈsaɪənɪk/ | adj. |平静的，宁静的；繁荣的|Relating to the halcyon, a mythical bird identified with the kingfisher, said to nest at sea about the winter solstice and calm the waves; hence, peaceful, tranquil; prosperous. |例句：The alcyonic days of summer vacation flew by. 夏日假期平静的日子飞逝而过。

例句：The company experienced an alcyonic period of growth. 公司经历了一段繁荣的增长期。|近义词：peaceful, tranquil, serene, calm, prosperous, thriving |反义词:turbulent, chaotic, troubled, tumultuous, declining, failing |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alcyoneus*, from Greek *alkuōnios*, relating to the halcyon bird (kingfisher).
