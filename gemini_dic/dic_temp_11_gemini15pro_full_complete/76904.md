ganocephala:/ˌɡænoʊˈsɛfələ/| n. |厚头龙类|A group of ornithischian dinosaurs characterized by thick skulls.|The ganocephala, like the pachycephalosaurus, were known for their head-butting behavior. 厚头龙类，像肿头龙一样，以其相互撞头的行为而闻名。|Synonyms: pachycephalosaurid, marginocephalian|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)|noun
Etymology: From Greek *ganos* "brightness, sheen" + *kephale* "head".  指头部骨骼的厚度和光泽。
