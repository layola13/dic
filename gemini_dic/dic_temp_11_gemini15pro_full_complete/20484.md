bituminization:/bɪˌtʃuːmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|沥青化|The process by which organic matter is transformed into bitumen or bitumen-like substances.|The bituminization of plant matter over millions of years can lead to the formation of coal.  植物在地下经过数百万年的沥青化作用可以形成煤。|synonyms: asphaltation, bituminising|antonyms: None|noun

bituminizations:/bɪˌtʃuːmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ |n.|沥青化（复数）|Plural form of bituminization, referring to multiple instances of the process.|The study analyzed various bituminizations occurring in different geological formations. 这项研究分析了不同地质构造中发生的各种沥青化作用。|synonyms: asphaltations, bituminisings|antonyms: None|noun
