here-or-there:/ˌhɪər ɔːr ˈðɛər/ | adv. | 无论哪里；随便哪里；这里或那里 | In a general location, not exactly specified.  Expressing indifference to the specific location. | You can put the book here or there; it doesn't matter to me. 你可以把书放在这里或那里；我无所谓。 | Example:  He could be here or there, I'm not sure.  他可能在这里或那里，我不确定。|Synonyms: anywhere, anyplace, wherever | Antonyms: specifically, definitely | adverb

here-or-there:/ˌhɪər ɔːr ˈðɛər/ | adj. | 不重要的；无关紧要的 | Of little importance or significance. | It was just a here-or-there comment, nothing to take seriously.  这只是个无关紧要的评论，不必当真。| Example: The exact date is here-or-there, as long as it's within the next month. 确切日期无关紧要，只要在下个月内就行。 | Synonyms: unimportant, insignificant, trivial | Antonyms: important, significant, crucial | adjective
