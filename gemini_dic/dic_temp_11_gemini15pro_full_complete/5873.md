ambassadors-at-large:/æmˈbæsədərz æt lɑːrdʒ/
| n. |无任所大使；特使|A diplomatic official of high rank who is accredited to represent their country, but not assigned to a particular post or mission.|The United Nations appointed several ambassadors-at-large to address the global refugee crisis. 联合国任命了几位无任所大使来处理全球难民危机。|Synonyms: roving ambassadors, special envoys|Antonyms: resident ambassadors|noun
