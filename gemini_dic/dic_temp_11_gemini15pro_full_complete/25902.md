burmite:/ˈbɜːrmaɪt/
noun
缅甸琥珀
A fossilized resin from Myanmar (formerly Burma), often containing inclusions such as insects and plants.
Burmite is prized for its unique reddish hue and the variety of inclusions it can contain. 缅甸琥珀因其独特的红色调和其中包含的各种内含物而备受珍视。
Burmite often contains well-preserved insect fossils. 缅甸琥珀通常包含保存完好的昆虫化石。
Synonym: amber
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider 'copal' which is a younger, less fossilized resin)
