lodestar: /ˈloʊdˌstɑːr/ (US) /ˈləʊdˌstɑː(r)/ (UK)
n.| 指导原则；指路之星；北极星|something that serves as a guide or inspiration|例句：The principles of equality and justice serve as our lodestar. 平等和正义的原则是我们的指导原则。|例句：Her lodestar throughout her career has been her unwavering commitment to helping others. 在她的整个职业生涯中，她始终不渝地致力于帮助他人，这是她的指路之星。|近义词：guide, beacon, north star, compass, polestar|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可用表示迷失或误导的词) distraction, misdirection|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English lode sterre, literally, "leading star," from lode (course, journey) + sterre (star). The "lode" part is related to Old English lād (way, journey).
