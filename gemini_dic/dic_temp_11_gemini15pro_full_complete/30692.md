certifiably:/ˌsɜːrtɪˈfaɪəbli/ | adv. | 可证明地，无疑地 | In a manner that can be officially verified; undoubtedly. | He is certifiably insane. 他无疑是疯了。| certifiably crazy. 他疯得无可置疑。 | 近义词: undoubtedly, definitely, demonstrably, undeniably | 反义词: questionably, doubtfully | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From certify + -ably.  Certify comes from the Late Latin certificāre, from certus "certain" + -ficāre "-fy".
