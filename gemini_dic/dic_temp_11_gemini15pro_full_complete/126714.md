names:/neɪmz/ | n. |名字，名称；名声，名誉 |A word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known or referred to. |例句：What are their names?  他们的名字是什么？

例句：His name is known throughout the land. 他的名字闻名全国。|近义词：titles, appellations, designations |反义词: anonymity | noun
