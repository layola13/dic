overground:/ˈəʊvəɡraʊnd/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərɡraʊnd/ (US)| adj. n. adv.|地上（的），地面上（的）；地上铁路系统|Existing, situated, or operating above ground; an above-ground railway system, especially that of London.|

例句：
The overground section of the train line offers scenic views.
地上部分的铁路线提供了优美的风景。

The London Overground is a convenient way to travel around the city.
伦敦地上铁路是环游城市的一种便捷方式。

近义词：above ground, surface, elevated
反义词：underground, subterranean, below ground
词性: adjective, noun, adverb

overground:/ˈəʊvəɡraʊnd/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərɡraʊnd/ (US)| adv.|在地上，在地面上|In, on, or to a position above ground.|

例句：
The cables are run overground.
电缆在地上铺设。

After the flood, they moved everything overground.
洪水过后，他们把所有东西都搬到了地面上。

近义词：above ground
反义词：underground
词性: adverb


词源:  'over' + 'ground'. 'Over' meaning "above" and 'ground' referring to the earth's surface.
