overcharge:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒ/ /ˌəʊvəˈtʃɑːdʒ/| n. vt.|过度收费，超额收费；过量装载|An overcharge is an amount of money that you are asked to pay that is too high.  过高的收费|The restaurant overcharged us by $10.  这家餐馆多收了我们10美元。|近义词：overprice, gouge|反义词: undercharge, discount|noun
