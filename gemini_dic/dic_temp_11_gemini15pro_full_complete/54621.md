dismemberment:/dɪsˈmɛmbəmənt/
n.
肢解; 分割
The act of cutting, tearing, or pulling a body apart, separating limbs from the torso.
The dismemberment of the empire followed the death of the emperor. 帝国在皇帝死后分裂了。
dissection, mutilation, fragmentation
integration, unification
noun
