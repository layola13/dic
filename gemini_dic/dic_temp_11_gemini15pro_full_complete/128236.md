neurofilament: /ˌnjʊərəˈfɪləmənt/ | n. | 神经丝 |  A type of intermediate filament found in neurons, providing structural support to axons and regulating axon diameter.  They are a major component of the neuronal cytoskeleton. | Example 1:  Neurofilaments are essential for maintaining the shape and integrity of nerve cells.  神经丝对于维持神经细胞的形状和完整性至关重要。

Example 2:  Accumulation of neurofilaments can be a sign of certain neurological diseases. 神经丝的积累可能是某些神经系统疾病的征兆。 | Synonym: intermediate filament (in the context of neurons) | Antonym: N/A | noun
