nonfrustration:/ˌnɒnfrʌˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 无挫折，无挫败 | The state of not being frustrated; absence of frustration.  This refers to a situation where someone is not prevented from achieving a desired goal or fulfilling a need. | The design of the toy emphasized nonfrustration, allowing children to easily assemble it.  这个玩具的设计强调无挫折感，让孩子们可以轻松组装。 |  The company's policy of open communication promotes nonfrustration among employees. 公司的开放沟通政策促进了员工之间的无挫折感。 |  synonyms: satisfaction, contentment, fulfillment | antonyms: frustration, disappointment, dissatisfaction | noun
