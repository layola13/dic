outbantered:/ˌaʊtˈbæntərd/
verb
胜过(某人)的玩笑;以玩笑胜过
outdo someone in banter; surpass someone in witty repartee.
He outbantered his opponent during the debate. 他在辩论中以玩笑胜过了对手。
Synonyms: outwit, outsmart, surpass, defeat, trounce
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
