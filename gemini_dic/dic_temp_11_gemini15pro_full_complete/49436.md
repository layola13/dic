delphian:/ˈdelfiən/ | n. | 德尔斐人；预言者 | An inhabitant of Delphi; a prophet.  | The delphian offered cryptic advice to the king. 预言者向国王提供了神秘的建议。 | The delphian's pronouncements were highly regarded in ancient Greece.  在古希腊，这位德尔斐人的预言受到高度重视。| 近义词：oracle, seer, prophet | 反义词：None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Delphi, the location of a famous oracle in ancient Greece.  The word ultimately derives from the Greek word δελφίς (delphis), meaning "dolphin," possibly due to the association of dolphins with Apollo, the god of Delphi.
