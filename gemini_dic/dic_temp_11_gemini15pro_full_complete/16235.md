ban:/bæn/
n.|禁令，禁止|An official or legal prohibition.|例句：There is a ban on smoking in public places. 公共场所禁止吸烟。|例句：The government imposed a ban on the import of certain goods. 政府禁止进口某些商品。|近义词：prohibition, restriction, embargo|反义词：allowance, permission|词性:名词

ban:/bæn/
vt.|禁止，取缔|To prohibit or forbid something, especially officially.|例句：The book was banned in several countries. 这本书在几个国家被禁。|例句：The city banned the use of fireworks. 该市禁止燃放烟花爆竹。|近义词：prohibit, forbid, outlaw|反义词：allow, permit|词性:动词

bans:/bænz/
n.|禁令（复数）|Plural form of "ban".|例句：The bans on certain pesticides are designed to protect the environment.  对某些杀虫剂的禁令旨在保护环境。|例句：Several bans were put in place after the incident.  事件发生后，多项禁令生效。|近义词：prohibitions, restrictions|反义词：permissions, allowances|词性:名词
