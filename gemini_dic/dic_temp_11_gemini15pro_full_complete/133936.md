nonharmoniously:/ˌnɒnhɑːˈməʊniəsli/ /ˌnɑːnhɑːrˈmoʊniəsli/| adv. |不和谐地，不协调地|In a manner lacking harmony or agreement; discordantly.  It describes actions, sounds, or situations that are not in sync or agreement with each other.  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "harmoniously," which means "in a harmonious way."|The orchestra played nonharmoniously, with instruments clashing against each other.  管弦乐队演奏得不和谐，乐器之间相互冲突。

The colors clashed nonharmoniously, creating a jarring effect. 这些颜色不和谐地冲突，产生了刺耳的效果。|Synonyms: discordantly, inharmoniously, jarringly, clashingly, cacophonously|Antonyms: harmoniously, melodically, concordantly, agreeably|Word Type: Adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + harmoniously.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Harmoniously" derives from "harmony," ultimately from Greek "harmonia" (agreement, concord).
