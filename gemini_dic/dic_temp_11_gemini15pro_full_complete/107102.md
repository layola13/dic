ladino:/ləˈdinoʊ/| adj. n.| 拉迪诺语的；拉迪诺人；狡猾的；精明的 |A Romance language derived from Old Spanish, spoken by Sephardic Jews expelled from Spain and Portugal at the end of the 15th century.|  He speaks Ladino fluently. 他拉迪诺语说得非常流利。|Synonyms:  cunning, shrewd, artful, sly, wily|Antonyms:  naive, ingenuous, guileless|adjective

ladino:/ləˈdinoʊ/| n.| 拉迪诺人；拉迪诺语 |A Sephardic Jew, especially one who speaks Ladino.| The ladino community maintains its unique traditions. 拉迪诺社区保持着其独特的传统。|Synonyms: Sephardi, Sephardic Jew|Antonyms: None|noun
