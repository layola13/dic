noninsurance:/ˌnɒnɪnˈʃʊərəns/| n.|无保险，非保险|Absence or lack of insurance.  The state of not having insurance coverage.|Many freelancers struggle with the high cost of noninsurance healthcare.|近义词: uninsured, uncovered|反义词: insured, covered|词性:名词

词源: non- + insurance

non-:  Prefix meaning "not," from Old French non-, nun- "not, no" (12c.), from Latin non "not," originally ne oinom (ne + oinom "one thing"), from PIE *ne "not" + *oi-no- "one."

insurance: from ensure (v.) + -ance. Meaning "act of insuring" is from 1680s; that of "business of insuring" is from 1728. The commercial sense (short for insurance policy) is from 1744.
