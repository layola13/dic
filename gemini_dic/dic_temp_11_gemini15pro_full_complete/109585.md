lethalities: /liːˈθælɪtiz/
noun
致命性；致死率
The quality or state of being lethal; deadliness.
The lethalities of the new virus strain are alarming.  这种新病毒株的致死率令人担忧。
Near synonyms: fatality rates, mortality rates, deadliness
Antonyms: survival rates, non-lethality
n.
