bobble:/ˈbɒbəl/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbl/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|小球, 绒球,  (尤指运动员)失误, 弄错|A small ball of wool or other material used as a decoration.  To drop or fumble (a ball). To make a mistake, especially in sport.|The cat played with the bobble on the string. (猫玩着绳子上的绒球。) He bobbled the catch and the other team scored. (他接球失误，对方得分了。) He bobbled the easy question and looked foolish. (他把简单的问题搞错了，看起来很愚蠢。)|近义词：pompom, tassel, fumble, blunder|反义词:catch, grab|词性:noun, verb

Etymology:  Likely from an earlier form *bobbel, of imitative origin, suggesting a light bouncing movement.
