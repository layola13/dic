overgreedily: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːdəli/
adverb
过度贪婪地
Excessively greedily; with extreme greed.  Characterized by wanting more than is necessary or fair.
He overgreedily grabbed all the candy, leaving none for the other children. 他过度贪婪地抓走了所有的糖果，一点也没留给其他孩子。
The businessman overgreedily pursued profits, ignoring ethical considerations.  这个商人过度贪婪地追求利润，无视道德方面的考虑。
Synonyms: avariciously, rapaciously, covetously
Antonyms: generously, selflessly
词性: adverb
