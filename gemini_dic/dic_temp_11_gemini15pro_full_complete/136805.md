nonshatter:/nɒnˈʃætər/ | adj. | 防碎的，不易碎的 | Designed to resist shattering; made of a material that does not easily break into pieces. | The car's nonshatter windshield prevented serious injury in the accident. 汽车的防碎挡风玻璃在事故中防止了严重伤害。| The nonshatter glass is ideal for children's rooms. 防碎玻璃非常适合儿童房。 | 近义词: shatterproof, shatter-resistant, safety | 反义词: shatterable, brittle | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "shatter" (verb meaning "to break suddenly and violently into pieces")
