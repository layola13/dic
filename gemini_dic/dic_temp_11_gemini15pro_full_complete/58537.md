dufrenite:/ˈduːfrəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 绿磷铁矿 | A hydrous basic phosphate of iron, occurring as small, dark green, orthorhombic crystals. | Dufrenite is a relatively rare mineral. 绿磷铁矿是一种相对稀有的矿物。| Dufrenite can be found in oxidized iron deposits. 绿磷铁矿可以在氧化的铁矿床中找到。 |Synonyms:  rockbridgeite, beraunite (similar minerals) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
