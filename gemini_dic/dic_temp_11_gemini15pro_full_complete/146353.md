overfish:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪʃ/| v. |过度捕捞|To catch too many fish so that the population becomes too small to reproduce effectively.|例句：The lake has been overfished, and now there are very few fish left. 这个湖泊被过度捕捞了，现在鱼很少了。

例句：Overfishing can lead to the collapse of fish populations. 过度捕捞会导致鱼类种群的崩溃。|近义词：deplete, exhaust|反义词：conserve, replenish|词性:动词


overfishing:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪʃɪŋ/| n. |过度捕捞|The act of catching too many fish so that the population becomes too small to reproduce effectively.|例句：Overfishing is a serious problem that threatens the world's oceans. 过度捕捞是一个威胁世界海洋的严重问题。

例句：The decline in fish stocks is largely due to overfishing. 鱼类资源的减少主要是由于过度捕捞造成的。|近义词：depletion, exhaustion|反义词：conservation, replenishment|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + fish.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much".  "Fish" refers to the act of catching fish.  Thus, "overfish" literally means to fish excessively.
