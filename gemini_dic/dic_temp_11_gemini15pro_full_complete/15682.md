baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/| n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in countries where it is expected or demanded.|Example 1: He offered the porter a small baksheesh to carry his bags. 他给了搬运工一点小费来搬运行李。

Example 2:  She had to pay baksheesh to the officials to get her paperwork processed quickly. 她不得不向官员行贿以快速办理她的文件。|Synonym: tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but the concept opposes legitimate payment or salary) |noun
