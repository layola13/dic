citizenly:/ˈsɪtɪzənli/
adjective
公民的；市民的；像公民的
characteristic of a citizen; behaving as a good citizen should
例句：He acted in a citizenly manner by reporting the crime.  他以公民应有的方式举报了犯罪行为。

例句：She displayed citizenly virtue by volunteering at the local hospital. 她通过在当地医院做志愿者展现了公民美德。

近义词：civic, public-spirited, responsible
反义词：irresponsible, uncivic, antisocial
词性: adjective


词源:
From citizen + -ly.  由citizen（公民）+ -ly（形容词后缀）构成。
