atrophied:/ˈætrəfid/
adjective
萎缩的，退化的
(of a part of the body) reduced in size; wasted away, typically as a result of disease or lack of use.
His arm was atrophied from lack of use after the accident.  他的手臂在事故后因缺乏使用而萎缩了。
The muscles in his leg atrophied after he was in a cast for six months.  他的腿部肌肉在他打石膏六个月后萎缩了。
shrunken, withered, wasted, deteriorated, diminished, weakened
developed, strengthened, grown, enlarged
