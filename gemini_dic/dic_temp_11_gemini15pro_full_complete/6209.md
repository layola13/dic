ammonitiferous:/ˌæməˌnɪˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含菊石的；产菊石的
Containing ammonites; yielding ammonites.
The cliffs are ammonitiferous, revealing the fossilized remains of these ancient creatures. 这些悬崖峭壁富含菊石，露出了这些古老生物的化石遗迹。
Near synonyms: fossiliferous
Antonyms: barren, unfossiliferous
词性: adjective
