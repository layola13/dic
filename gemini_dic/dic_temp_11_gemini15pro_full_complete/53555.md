disbarment:/dɪsˈbɑːrmənt/
n.
取消律师资格，逐出律师界
The act of expelling a lawyer from the bar, revoking their license to practice law.
The lawyer faced disbarment after being convicted of fraud.  该律师因被判犯有欺诈罪而面临被取消律师资格。
His disbarment followed a series of ethical violations. 在一系列违反职业道德的行为之后，他被取消了律师资格。
expulsion, exclusion, removal
reinstatement, admittance, readmission
noun
