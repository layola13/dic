androdioecious:/ˌændrəʊdaɪˈiːʃəs/| adj.|雄性两性异株的|Having male individuals and hermaphrodite individuals.|This rare breeding system, androdioecy, is found in some plants and invertebrates.|近义词: (No close synonym, but related terms include) dioecious, monoecious|反义词: N/A|词性: Adjective

Etymology:  From Greek  *andr-*  "man" + *di-* "two" + *oik-*  "house" + *-ous* adjective ending.  Essentially meaning "two houses (male and hermaphrodite) within the male sex".
