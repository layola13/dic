nonaccumulatively:/ˌnɑːnəˈkjuːmjələtɪvli/ | adv. | 非累积地 |  In a manner that does not accumulate or build up over time. | The interest is applied nonaccumulatively, meaning it doesn't compound. 利息是非累积应用的，意味着它不会复利。|  The penalties are applied nonaccumulatively, so each violation is treated individually.  处罚是非累积的，因此每次违规都单独处理。| synonym: individually, separately, discretely | antonym: cumulatively, progressively, increasingly |  adverb
