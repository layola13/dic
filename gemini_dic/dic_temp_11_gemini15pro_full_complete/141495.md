opisthocoelian: /ˌɒpɪsθəˈsiːliən/ | adj. | 后凹的 | Having a vertebral centrum that is concave posteriorly and convex anteriorly. |  The opisthocoelian vertebrae of certain dinosaurs are a distinguishing characteristic.  某些恐龙的后凹椎骨是一个显著特征。 |  近义词: concave posteriorly | 反义词: procoelous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Greek *opistho-* (behind, posterior) + *-koilos* (hollow).
