millennialism:/mɪˈlɛniəlɪzəm/| n. |千禧年主义，千年至福说|The belief that a golden age or paradise will occur on Earth, especially in association with the second coming of Christ.|Many cults are based around millennialism. 许多邪教都围绕着千禧年主义。|近义词：chiliasm|反义词:|名词

millenarian:/ˌmɪləˈnɛəriən/| n. adj. |千禧年主义者；千禧年的|A person who believes in millennialism; relating to millennialism.|He was a millenarian who predicted the end of the world. 他是一个千禧年主义者，预言了世界末日。|近义词：chiliast|反义词:|名词，形容词

millennium:/mɪˈlɛniəm/| n. |一千年，千禧年|A period of one thousand years, especially as calculated from the traditional date of the birth of Christ.|The new millennium began on January 1, 2001.  新的千禧年始于2001年1月1日。|近义词:|反义词:|名词
