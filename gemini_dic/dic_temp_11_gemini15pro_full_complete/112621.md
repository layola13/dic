low-end:/ˌloʊ ˈɛnd/| adj.|低端的，廉价的|Relating to the cheaper or less powerful products in a range.  Referring to products or services that are less expensive and of lower quality than other products of the same type. |例句：They specialize in low-end electronics. 他们专门经营低端电子产品。

例句：The company targets the low-end market. 这家公司瞄准低端市场。|近义词：cheap, budget, inexpensive, bottom-of-the-line|反义词：high-end, top-of-the-line, premium, luxury|词性: adjective
