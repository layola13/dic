anticults:/ˈæntiˌkʌlts/
n.|反邪教组织，反邪教运动|Groups or movements that oppose cults and their practices.  They often work to educate the public about the dangers of cults and to help people leave them.|The anticults are working hard to expose the group's harmful practices.|近义词：cult awareness groups, countercult movement|反义词:pro-cult movements|词性:名词

anticult:/ˈæntiˌkʌlt/
adj.|反邪教的|Opposed to cults or their practices.|The anticult movement gained momentum in the 1980s.|近义词：countercult|反义词:pro-cult|词性:形容词
