词源:
From apostolical + -ness.  Ultimately from Late Latin *apostolicālis, from Latin apostolicus (“apostolic”), from Ancient Greek ἀποστολικός (apostolikós, “of or belonging to an apostle”), from ἀπόστολος (apóstolos, “apostle”).
