flatlet:/ˈflæt.lət/ | n. | 小公寓；套房 | A small apartment, often consisting of a single room or a few rooms. |  She couldn't afford a large apartment, so she rented a flatlet. 她租不起大公寓，所以租了一套小公寓。|

Example 1: He found a cheap flatlet near the university. 他在大学附近找到了一个便宜的小公寓。
Example 2: The flatlet was small but had everything she needed. 这套小公寓虽小，但五脏俱全。

|Synonyms: studio, apartment, bedsit | Antonyms: house, mansion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From flat + -let (diminutive suffix).  The suffix "-let" is used to create a noun meaning "a small version of" something.  Therefore, a "flatlet" is a small flat.
