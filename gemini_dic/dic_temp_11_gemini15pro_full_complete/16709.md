barrister:/ˈbærɪstər/ (UK), /ˈbærɪstər/ (US)| n.|大律师，出庭律师|A lawyer who is qualified to plead a case in a higher court.|例句：The barrister argued persuasively for his client's acquittal.  这位大律师雄辩地为他的客户争取无罪释放。

例句：She hired a top barrister to represent her in the appeal. 她聘请了一位顶级大律师代表她上诉。|近义词：lawyer, advocate, counsel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
