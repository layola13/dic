cline:/klaɪn/| n.|渐变群，斜坡，倾斜|A gradual change in a character or feature across the distributional range of a species or population, usually along a geographical transect.|The cline in flower color runs from white in the north to pink in the south.  花的颜色由北方的白色逐渐过渡到南方的粉色。|gradient, slope, incline|decline, level|noun
