conscriptional:/kənˈskrɪpʃənəl/
adjective
征兵的；与征兵有关的
Relating to or involving conscription (compulsory enlistment for military service).
Conscriptional policies are often controversial. 征兵政策通常是有争议的。
The government introduced conscriptional measures during the war. 政府在战争期间实行了征兵措施。
近义词: draft, compulsory
反义词: voluntary
词性: adjective
