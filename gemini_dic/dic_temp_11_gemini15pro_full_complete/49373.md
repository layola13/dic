delirament:/dɪˈlɪrəmənt/
n.|谵妄，胡言乱语|A state of confused or uncontrolled excitement or emotion, often accompanied by incoherent speech.|The patient in the ICU was experiencing delirament due to the high fever.|近义词：ravings, incoherence, babbling|反义词：lucidity, coherence|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *dēlīrāmentum*, from dēlīrāre (“to be out of one's furrow, rave”), from dē (“from, away from”) + līra (“furrow, ridge between furrows”).
