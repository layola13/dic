nonliable:/ˌnɒnˈlaɪəbl/ | adj. | 无责任的，无需承担责任的 | Not legally responsible or obligated; exempt from liability. | Example 1: The landlord is nonliable for any damages caused by natural disasters.  房东对自然灾害造成的任何损失概不负责。

Example 2:  The company was found to be nonliable for the employee's actions.  该公司被认定对该雇员的行为不承担责任。 | Synonyms: exempt, immune, not responsible | Antonyms: liable, responsible | adjective
