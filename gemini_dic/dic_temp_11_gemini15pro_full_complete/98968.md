ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French entrelacer, from entre- "between" + lacer "to lace, tie."  The prefix "inter-" signifies "between" or "among," while "lace" refers to a cord or string used for fastening or decorating.  Therefore, "interlace" literally means "to lace between" or "to intertwine."
