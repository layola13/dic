castellations:/ˌkæstəˈleɪʃənz/
n.
城垛；雉堞；锯齿状；【建】窗孔
A series of battlements. Openings, usually rectangular, in a wall or parapet. A formation resembling a series of battlements.
The castle walls have impressive castellations. 城堡的墙壁有令人印象深刻的城垛。
The architect designed the building with decorative castellations along the roofline. 建筑师沿着屋顶线设计了带有装饰性雉堞的建筑物。

The arrangement of slots and openings in a cardboard box.
The castellations in the packaging allowed for easy ventilation. 包装上的开孔可以方便通风。
The castellations on the side of the box made it easy to tear open. 盒子侧面的锯齿状设计使其易于撕开。

synonyms: battlements, crenellations, indentations, notches
antonyms: smoothness, flatness
noun
