hyperdeliciously:/ˌhaɪpər dɪˈlɪʃəsli/ | adv. |极其美味地，超级美味地 |  An adverb formed by adding the prefix "hyper-" (meaning "excessive" or "beyond") to the word "deliciously". It intensifies the meaning of "deliciously" to express an extremely high degree of deliciousness. | The cake was hyperdeliciously moist and flavorful. 这蛋糕极其美味，湿润可口。|Synonyms: extremely deliciously, incredibly deliciously, exceptionally deliciously | Antonyms: unpleasantly, disgustingly | Word Type: Adverb
