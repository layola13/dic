discontentedness:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdnəs/
n.
不满, 不满足
A state of being dissatisfied or unhappy with one's circumstances.
The discontentedness of the workers led to a strike. 工人们的不满导致了罢工。
dissatisfaction, unhappiness, malcontentment
contentment, satisfaction
noun
