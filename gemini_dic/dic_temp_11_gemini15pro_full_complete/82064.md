gritter:/ˈɡrɪtər/ (US), /ˈɡrɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|撒沙机，撒沙车；撒沙工人|A vehicle or person that spreads grit (small stones) on roads, especially in icy conditions to improve traction.|The gritter was out all night because of the black ice. 由于夜间出现黑冰，撒沙车工作了一整夜。|The council employs several gritters during the winter months. 市政会在冬季雇佣几名撒沙工人。|Synonyms: sand spreader, salt spreader|Antonyms: N/A|noun

gritter:/ˈɡrɪtər/ (US), /ˈɡrɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|咬牙切齿的人|A person who deals with adversity with courage and resolve.|He's a real gritter, never giving up even when things get tough. 他是一个真正坚韧不拔的人，即使在情况艰难时也永不放弃。|She showed her gritter side by overcoming numerous obstacles to achieve her goals. 她通过克服重重障碍来实现自己的目标，展现了她坚韧的一面。|Synonyms:  determined person, tenacious individual|Antonyms: quitter, defeatist|noun
