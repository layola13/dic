overingenuity:/ˌoʊvərˌɪndʒəˈnjuːɪti/
noun
过度精巧; 过于巧妙; 过分精巧
Excessive or misplaced ingenuity; the use of overly clever or elaborate methods.
His plan failed due to overingenuity; it was so complex that it fell apart. 他的计划由于过度精巧而失败了；它太复杂了，以至于崩溃了。
The overingenuity of the plot made the movie less enjoyable.  剧情的过度巧妙使得电影不那么令人愉快。
Synonyms: overelaboration, overcomplication, over-sophistication
Antonyms: simplicity, straightforwardness, directness
词性: 名词
