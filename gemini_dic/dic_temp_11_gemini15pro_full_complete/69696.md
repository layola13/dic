fenianism:/ˈfiːniənɪzəm/
n.
爱尔兰共和主义；芬尼党主义
The principles and practices of the Fenians; support for Irish republicanism, often involving revolutionary methods.
The government cracked down on fenianism in the late 19th century. 政府在19世纪后期镇压了芬尼党主义。
Synonyms: republicanism, nationalism, patriotism
Antonyms: loyalism, imperialism, colonialism
noun
