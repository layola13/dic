breezeway:/ˈbriːzweɪ/ | n. |有屋顶的通道，走廊|A covered passage, often open on the sides, connecting two buildings or parts of a building.|
例句：We walked through the breezeway to get from the house to the garage. 我们穿过走廊从房子走到车库。

例句：The breezeway provided a sheltered area to sit and enjoy the fresh air. 走廊提供了一个可以坐着享受新鲜空气的遮蔽区域。


近义词：passage, walkway, corridor, hallway |
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "enclosed space") |
词性: noun
