explainable:/ɪkˈspleɪnəbl/
adjective
可解释的，可说明的
capable of being explained or accounted for.
The phenomenon is easily explainable. 这种现象很容易解释。

近义词:understandable, comprehensible, interpretable
反义词:inexplicable, mysterious, unaccountable
词性:adjective
