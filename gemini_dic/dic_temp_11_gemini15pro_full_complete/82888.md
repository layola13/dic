gulpin:/ˈɡʌlpɪn/| n.|大口吞咽，一大口（尤指液体）|A gulpin is a large mouthful, especially of liquid.|He took a huge gulpin of water after his run. 他跑步后猛灌了一大口水。|近义词：gulp, swallow, mouthful|反义词:sip|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "gulp" + "-in" (suffix forming nouns).  "Gulp" comes from Middle Dutch "gulpen."
