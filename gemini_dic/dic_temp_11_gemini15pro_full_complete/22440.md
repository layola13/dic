bookfuls:/ˈbʊkfʊlz/
noun
满书，相当于许多书的内容
A quantity that fills a book or books.
She had read bookfuls of romantic novels. 她读了很多浪漫小说。
synonyms: lots of books, a large number of books
antonyms: few books, a small number of books
