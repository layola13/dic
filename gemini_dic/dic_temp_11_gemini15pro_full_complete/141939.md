ordnances:/ˈɔːrdnənsɪz/
n.
军械，大炮；(尤指)火炮；条例，法令
Military supplies, especially weapons and ammunition; cannons, especially large caliber guns; a law or regulation.
The army deployed its heavy ordnances along the border. 军队沿着边界部署了重型火炮。
The city council passed a new ordnance regarding noise pollution. 市议会通过了一项关于噪音污染的新法令。
synonyms: weaponry, artillery, munitions, armament, statute, decree
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a concrete noun like ordnance.  One could consider concepts like "peace treaty" or "disarmament" as antonymic ideas.)
