lugubrious:/luˈɡuːbriəs/| adj.|悲哀的，忧郁的|Looking or sounding sad and dismal.|例句：The dog gazed at its departed owner with a lugubrious expression.  这只狗悲伤地看着它逝去的主人。

例句：He told the story in a lugubrious tone. 他用忧郁的语气讲述了这个故事。|近义词：mournful, gloomy, somber, melancholy|反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lūgubris*, from lūgēre (“to mourn”), related to Greek λύγος (lúgos, “grief”)
