cantabile:/kænˈtɑːbileɪ/ /kænˈtɑːbɪleɪ/
adjective|歌唱性的，如歌的|In a smooth singing style. Characterized by a smooth, flowing melody.|The piece has a beautiful, cantabile melody.  这首曲子有着优美流畅的旋律。|
synonyms: melodious, lyrical, flowing, smooth|antonyms: disjointed, jerky, staccato|adjective
cantabile:/kænˈtɑːbileɪ/ /kænˈtɑːbɪleɪ/
adverb|歌唱地，如歌地|In a smooth, singing manner.|Play this passage cantabile.  请用歌唱般的风格演奏这段。|synonyms: lyrically, melodiously, smoothly|antonyms: disjointedly, jerkily, staccato|adverb
cantabile:/kænˈtɑːbileɪ/ /kænˈtɑːbɪleɪ/
noun|歌唱部分，如歌的乐段|A piece or passage marked to be performed in a smooth, flowing style.|The cantabile of the second movement is particularly moving.  第二乐章的歌唱部分特别感人。|synonyms: aria, melody|antonyms: - |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *cantabile*, from *cantare* "to sing," from Latin *cantare* "to sing," frequentative of *canere* "to sing."
