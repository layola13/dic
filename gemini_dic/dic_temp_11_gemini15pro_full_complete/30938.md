chairmans:/ˈtʃɛərmənz/
noun
主席们；董事长们
Plural form of "chairman," referring to multiple individuals holding the position of chairman.
The chairmans of the various committees met to discuss the budget. 各个委员会的主席们开会讨论预算。
chairpersons, chairs, presidents, heads
