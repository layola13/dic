intrepidly:/ɪnˈtrepɪdli/ | adv. |勇敢地，无畏地 |  In a fearless and adventurous manner; resolutely. |  She intrepidly climbed the steep mountain face. 她勇敢地攀登陡峭的山崖。|  bravely, courageously, fearlessly, daringly, boldly | timidly, cautiously, fearfully, cowardly | adverb

intrepid:/ɪnˈtrepɪd/ | adj. | 无畏的；勇敢的 |  fearless; adventurous (often used for rhetorical or humorous effect). | He was an intrepid explorer who ventured into uncharted territories. 他是一位勇敢的探险家，敢于踏入未知的领域。 | brave, courageous, fearless, daring, bold | timid, cautious, fearful, cowardly | adjective

词源: From Latin intrepidus, from in- ‘not’ + trepidus ‘alarmed, trembling’.
