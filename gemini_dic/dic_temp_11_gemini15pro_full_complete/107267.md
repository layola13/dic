lakeview: /ˈleɪkvjuː/
n.
湖景；湖畔住宅
A view of a lake; a house or building with a view of a lake.
We have a beautiful lakeview from our bedroom window. 我们卧室的窗户可以看到美丽的湖景。
The Lakeview Hotel offers stunning views of the lake. 湖景酒店提供迷人的湖景。
synonyms: lakeside view, lake vista, lake prospect
antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider) obstructed view, city view
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "lake" (Old English "lacu," meaning "pond, pool, stream") + "view" (Old French "veue," from "veoir," meaning "to see").
