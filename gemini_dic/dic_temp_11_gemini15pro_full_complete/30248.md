cellulose:/ˈseljʊloʊs/| n. |纤维素|A complex carbohydrate that is the main constituent of the cell walls of most plants.|例句：Cotton is almost pure cellulose. 棉花几乎是纯纤维素。

例句：Cellulose is the most abundant organic polymer on Earth. 纤维素是地球上最丰富的有机聚合物。|近义词：fiber, roughage|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cellulose*, from *cellule* (“small cell”) + *-ose* (suffix forming names of carbohydrates). *Cellule* is from Latin *cellula* ("small room, chamber, cell"), diminutive of *cella* (“storeroom, cell”).
