fat-chance:/ˌfæt ˈtʃæns/ /ˌfæt ˈtʃɑːns/| idiom |  (informal) 没门儿；不可能；想都别想 |  Used ironically to say that there is very little or no possibility of something happening.  It expresses strong disbelief or disagreement. | "Fat chance I'm going to help him after the way he treated me!"  他那样对我之后，我才不会帮他呢！(想都别想！) | 近义词: no way, unlikely, slim chance, not a chance | 反义词: good chance, likely, definitely | idiom
