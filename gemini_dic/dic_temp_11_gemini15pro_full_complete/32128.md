chevelure:/ʃəˈvɛljʊər/ |n.|头发，尤指女子的秀发|A woman's head of hair, especially if long and beautiful. |She had a magnificent chevelure that cascaded down her back. 她有一头顺着后背倾泻而下的华丽秀发。|Synonyms: hair, tresses, locks|Antonyms: baldness|noun

Etymology->
From French *chevelure*, from Old French *chevelure*, from *chevel* (“hair”), from Latin *capillus* (“hair”).
