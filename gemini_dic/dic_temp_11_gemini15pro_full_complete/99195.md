interclashes:/ˌɪntərˈklæʃɪz/
n.
冲突，矛盾
Clashes or conflicts, especially between different groups within a larger entity.
例句：The interclashes between different departments hindered the project's progress.  不同部门之间的冲突阻碍了项目的进展。

例句：Political interclashes often lead to instability. 政治冲突往往导致不稳定。

近义词：conflicts, clashes, disputes, frictions
反义词：harmony, agreement, concord
词性:名词


interclash:/ˌɪntərˈklæʃ/
v.
（使）冲突，（使）矛盾
To clash or conflict, especially between different groups within a larger entity.
例句：The two tribes interclashed over territory.  两个部落因领土问题发生冲突。

例句：Differing opinions interclashed, creating a tense atmosphere.  不同的意见相互冲突，制造了紧张的气氛。

近义词：clash, conflict, collide, disagree
反义词：harmonize, agree, cooperate
词性:动词


词源:
"inter-" (prefix) + "clash"
"inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"clash" likely originates from imitative words representing the sound of metallic objects striking together.
