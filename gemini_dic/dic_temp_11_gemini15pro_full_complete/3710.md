aggressions: /əˈɡrɛʃənz/
n.
侵略，攻击，侵犯
hostile or violent behavior or attitudes toward another; readiness to attack or confront.
The country's aggressions towards its neighbors led to war.  这个国家对其邻国的侵略导致了战争。

His aggressions stemmed from deep-seated insecurities. 他的攻击性源于根深蒂固的不安全感。

近义词: attacks, assaults, offenses, hostilities, incursions
反义词: peace, pacifism, nonviolence, submission, retreats
词性: 名词(plural)
