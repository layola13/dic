appulsion:/əˈpʌlʃən/
n.
冲积；撞击；牙齿移位
the sudden tearing away of a part or structure
例句：The avulsion of the cliff face caused by the storm was dramatic. 暴风雨引起的悬崖面的剥落是戏剧性的。

例句：Dental appulsion is a serious dental emergency. 牙齿移位是一种严重的牙科急症。
近义词：avulsion, tearing, detachment
反义词：accretion, addition, attachment
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin appulsio, from Latin appellere (to drive to, bring to), from ad- (to) + pellere (to drive).
