nonathletic:/ˌnɒnæθˈlɛtɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒnæθˈlɛtɪk/ (UK)
adj.
不擅长运动的；没有运动细胞的
Not good at or interested in sports or athletic activities.
He's always been a bit nonathletic. 他一直都不太擅长运动。
Near Synonyms: unathletic, unsporting, clumsy, awkward
Antonyms: athletic, sporty, agile
词性: 形容词
