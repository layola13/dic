agapeically:/ˌæɡəˈpeɪɪkli/
|adv.|
|博爱地，无私地|
|In a manner characterized by agape, or selfless, unconditional love.|
|She served the homeless agapeically, driven by a deep compassion for others.|
|近义词：selflessly, altruistically, charitably|
|反义词：selfishly, egotistically|
|词性: 副词|


Etymology:

From agape (ἀγάπη) + -ic + -al + -ly.  The Greek word *ἀγάπη (agapē)* refers to a selfless, unconditional love, often used in a spiritual or philosophical context.
