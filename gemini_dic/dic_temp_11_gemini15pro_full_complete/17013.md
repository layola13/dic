batata:/bəˈtɑːtə/ /bɑːˈtɑːtə/ | n. |  甜薯，甘薯 | A starchy tuberous root vegetable widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.  From the Taino *batata*.| Example: Batata is a staple food in many parts of the world.  甜薯是世界许多地方的主食。

Example:  She baked the batata until it was soft and sweet. 她把甜薯烤到软糯香甜。| 近义词：sweet potato, yam | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
