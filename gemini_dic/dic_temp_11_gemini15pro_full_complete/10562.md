appreciation:/əˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 欣赏; 感激; 理解; 认识; [经] 增值 | An increase in value over time.  The act of recognizing the good qualities of something.  A feeling of thankfulness. |  例句：She expressed her appreciation for his help. 她表达了对他的帮助的感谢。

例句：The painting has increased in appreciation over the years. 多年来，这幅画的价值一直在增长。 | 近义词：gratitude, recognition, understanding, increase, valuation | 反义词：depreciation, criticism, disregard, ingratitude | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *appretiationem, noun of action from past participle stem of appretiare "to set a price to, appraise," from ad- "to" + pretium "price, worth, value."
