cheiropterous:/ˌkaɪˈrɒptərəs/| adj.| 翼手的，蝙蝠似的|Relating to or resembling bats; having wings like a bat.  Derived from Greek *kheir* (hand) and *pteron* (wing).| Bats are cheiropterous mammals. 蝙蝠是翼手类哺乳动物。|synonyms: bat-winged, batlike|antonyms: wingless|adjective
