irresuscitable:/ˌɪrɪˈsʌsɪtəbl/
adjective
不可复苏的，无法抢救的
Not capable of being resuscitated; unable to be revived.
The patient was declared irresuscitable after multiple attempts at CPR.  这位病人在多次心肺复苏后被宣布无法抢救。
The damage to the brain was so severe that he was deemed irresuscitable.  他的大脑损伤非常严重，被认为无法复苏。
近义词: unrevivable, irrecoverable
反义词: revivable, resuscitable (in rare cases where spontaneous recovery is possible after being declared clinically dead)
词性: adjective
