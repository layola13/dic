hype:/haɪp/| n. vt.|炒作；大肆宣传；兴奋剂；骗局|Intense or exaggerated publicity, often used to promote a product or idea.  Also, a state of excitement or energy.|The company used a lot of hype to promote their new phone. 这家公司用大量的炒作来宣传他们的新手机。|synonyms: promotion, publicity, ballyhoo, exaggeration, buzz|antonyms: understatement, downplaying|noun

hype:/haɪp/| vt.|大肆宣传；使兴奋；欺骗|To promote or publicize intensely, often exaggerating the benefits or importance.|The media hyped the upcoming movie as the best of the year. 媒体将即将上映的电影炒作为年度最佳。|synonyms: promote, publicize, boost, plug|antonyms: downplay, understate|verb
