inscrutableness:/ɪnˈskruːtəbəlnəs/
n.
不可测度，难以理解，神秘莫测
the quality of being impossible to understand or interpret.
His face was a mask of inscrutableness. 他的脸上带着一种难以捉摸的表情。
inscrutability, impenetrability, unfathomability, incomprehensibility
transparency, clarity, obviousness, perspicuity
noun
