olde:/oʊld/| adj.|古老的，旧的|Archaic form of "old."  Meaning "ancient, former, of long standing." Derived from Old English "ald."|例句：In olde tymes, people travelled by horse. 在古代，人们骑马旅行。

例句：Ye olde shoppe sold many curious items.  这家古老的商店出售许多稀奇古怪的商品。|近义词：ancient, antique, old, former|反义词:new, modern, recent|词性:形容词
