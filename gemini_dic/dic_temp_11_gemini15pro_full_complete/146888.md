overinhibit:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪt/| v. |过度抑制，过分压抑|To inhibit excessively; to restrain or suppress beyond what is necessary or desirable.|He tends to overinhibit his emotions, rarely expressing joy or sadness. 他倾向于过度压抑自己的情绪，很少表达快乐或悲伤。|例句：She overinhibited her natural exuberance. 她过度压抑了她天生的热情奔放。|近义词：suppress, repress, stifle, constrain|反义词：indulge, liberate, express|词性：动词

overinhibit:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪt/| adj. |过度抑制的，过分压抑的|Excessively inhibited or restrained.|His overinhibited personality made it difficult for him to make friends. 他过度压抑的性格使他难以交朋友。|例句：The child seemed overinhibited and withdrawn.  这孩子似乎过度压抑且孤僻。|近义词：repressed, suppressed, constrained|反义词：uninhibited, expressive, liberated|词性：形容词
