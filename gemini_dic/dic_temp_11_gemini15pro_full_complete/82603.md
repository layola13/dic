guardrails:/ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪlz/ | n. | 护栏，防护栏 |A barrier erected along a road, bridge, or other elevated structure to prevent people or vehicles from falling off. |  The bridge's guardrails prevented the car from plunging into the river. 桥上的护栏阻止了汽车坠入河中。

guardrails:/ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪlz/ | n. | 保障措施，安全措施 (比喻义) | Protective measures or guidelines that prevent undesirable outcomes. | The company implemented strict financial guardrails to prevent excessive spending. 公司实施了严格的财务保障措施，以防止过度支出。
