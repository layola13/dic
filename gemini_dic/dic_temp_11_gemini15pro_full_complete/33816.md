circumforanean: /ˌsɜːrkəmˌfɔːrəˈniːən/ | adj. | 流浪的，巡回的，江湖的 |  Wandering from place to place, especially as a performer or trader; itinerant.  Derived from Latin "circum" (around) + "forum" (marketplace).  Referring to those who traveled around marketplaces, performing or selling goods. | The circumforanean troupe of actors delighted the villagers with their lively performance.  这群巡回演出的演员以他们生动的表演使村民们感到高兴。|  The circumforanean preacher moved from town to town, spreading his message. 这位巡回传教士辗转于各个城镇之间，传播他的信息。 | Synonyms: itinerant, nomadic, wandering, peripatetic | Antonyms: settled, stationary, resident | adjective
