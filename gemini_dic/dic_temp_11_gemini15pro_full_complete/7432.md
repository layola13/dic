animuses: /ˈænɪməsiːz/
n.
敌意，憎恨；仇恨；蓄意，恶意
a feeling of strong dislike or hatred
例句：He felt no animus toward his critics. 他对批评他的人没有恶意。

例句：She harbored a deep animus against her former employer. 她对她的前任雇主怀有深深的敌意。

近义词：hostility, animosity, antagonism, antipathy, rancor, enmity
反义词：benevolence, goodwill, kindness, amity
词性：名词 (plural noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin animus (“mind, spirit, courage, passion, wrath”), related to anima.
