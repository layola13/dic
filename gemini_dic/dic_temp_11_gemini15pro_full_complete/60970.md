elgen: /ˈɛldʒən/ |  n. |  特征向量；本征向量 | A vector that does not change its direction when a linear transformation is applied to it. It changes only by a scalar factor, called an eigenvalue. | The elgen of the matrix A are v1 and v2.  矩阵A的特征向量是v1和v2。| synonyms: eigenvector, characteristic vector | antonyms: N/A | noun
