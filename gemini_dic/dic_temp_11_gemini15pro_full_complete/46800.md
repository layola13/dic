ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *Danis, *Dannys, from Old Norse *danskr (masculine), *dönsk (feminine), from Proto-Germanic *daniskaz, derived from the name of the Danes (*Daniz). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰenh₂- (“to stretch, extend”).
