heresiographer:/ˌhɛrɪsiˈɒɡrəfər/ |/ˌhɛrɪsiˈɑːɡrəfər/| n. |异端学研究者；异端史学家|A person who writes about or studies heresies.|The heresiographer meticulously documented the various splinter groups that emerged from the main religious movement.| 近义词: historian of heresy, scholar of heresies | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "orthodox theologian") | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek αἵρεσις (haíresis) "choice, sect, heresy" + -grapher (from γράφειν gráfein, "to write").
