nonstocks:/ˈnɑːnˌstɑːks/ | n. | 非股票，非股份 | Securities other than stocks, such as bonds, options, and commodities. | Example:  He invested heavily in nonstocks, preferring the stability of bonds. 他大量投资于非股票，更喜欢债券的稳定性。

Example:  Her portfolio was diversified with a mix of stocks and nonstocks. 她的投资组合由股票和非股票构成，实现了多元化。 | Synonyms:  non-equities, fixed-income securities | Antonyms: stocks, equities | noun
