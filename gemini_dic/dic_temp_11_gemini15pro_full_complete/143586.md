outhunt:/ˌaʊtˈhʌnt/
verb
胜过狩猎; 在狩猎中超过
to hunt more successfully than someone else
例句：He outhunted his rivals and bagged the biggest deer. 他比对手猎得更多，猎到了最大的鹿。

例句：She consistently outhunts her brothers, bringing home more game. 她总是比她的兄弟们猎得更多，带回更多的猎物。

近义词：outperform, surpass, excel, outdo
反义词：underperform, lose to

词性: verb
