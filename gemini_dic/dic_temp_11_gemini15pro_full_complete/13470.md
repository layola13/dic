atrophia:/əˈtroʊfiə/ | n. |萎缩，衰退|Wasting away or decrease in size of a part of the body, caused by disease or lack of use. | Muscle atrophia can occur after prolonged bed rest.  长时间卧床休息后可能会出现肌肉萎缩。| synonyms: degeneration, decline, deterioration | antonyms: growth, development, hypertrophy | noun

atrophied:/ˈætrəˌfaɪd/ | adj. |萎缩的，衰退的|Wasted away or decreased in size. | His leg muscles were atrophied after being in a cast for so long.  他的腿部肌肉在打石膏很长时间后萎缩了。| synonyms: shrunken, wasted, withered | antonyms: developed, enlarged, grown | adjective
