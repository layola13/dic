demining:/diːˈmaɪnɪŋ/
|n.|扫雷，排雷|The demining process is slow and dangerous. 扫雷过程缓慢而危险。|
|英文解析: The process of removing landmines from an area.|
|例句：Demining is crucial for the safety of civilians.  扫雷对于平民的安全至关重要。|
|例句：The organization specializes in demining operations in war-torn countries. 该组织专门在饱受战争蹂躏的国家开展排雷行动。|
|近义词：minesweeping, bomb disposal|
|反义词：mining (laying mines)|
|词性：noun|
