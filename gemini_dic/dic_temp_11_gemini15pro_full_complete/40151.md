contemptibly:/kənˈtɛmptɪbli/
| adv. |轻蔑地，卑鄙地|In a manner deserving of contempt; despicably.|He behaved contemptibly towards the waiter. 他对服务员的态度十分轻蔑。|Synonyms: disdainfully, scornfully, despicably, meanly|Antonyms: respectfully, admirably, honorably|词性: 副词

contemptibly:/kənˈtɛmptɪbli/
| adv. |可鄙地|In a manner deserving of contempt; shamefully.|She treated the homeless man contemptibly. 她对那个无家可归的人的态度很可鄙。|Synonyms: shamefully, disgracefully, basely|Antonyms: nobly, virtuously, commendably|词性: 副词
