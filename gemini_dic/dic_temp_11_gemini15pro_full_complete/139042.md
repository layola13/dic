oarfish:/ˈɔːrfɪʃ/| n. |皇带鱼|A large, elongated, deep-sea fish of the family Regalecidae, known for its ribbon-like body and long dorsal fin.|例句：The oarfish is the longest bony fish alive. 皇带鱼是现存最长的硬骨鱼。

例句：An oarfish was found washed ashore after the storm.  暴风雨过后，人们发现一条皇带鱼被冲上了岸。|近义词：ribbonfish, king of herrings|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |词性:noun
