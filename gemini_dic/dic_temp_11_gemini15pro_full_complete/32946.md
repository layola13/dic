choppiest:/ˈtʃɒpiɪst/| adj.|最波涛汹涌的，最不稳定的|superlative adjective of choppy; having many short, irregular waves |The lake was choppiest near the shore. 湖边浪最大。|近义词：rough, turbulent, agitated|反义词：calm, smooth, tranquil|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From chop + -y + -est.  "Chop" originally referred to striking or cutting something, and in the context of water, it came to describe the short, jerky movements of waves.
