child-proof:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːf/ | adj. | 防儿童的；儿童安全的 | Designed to prevent children from opening or using something potentially dangerous. |  The medicine bottle is child-proof.  药瓶是防儿童开启的。| Synonyms: child-resistant, tamper-proof, safe for children | Antonyms: unsafe for children, accessible to children | adjective

child-proof:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːf/ | vt. | 使…防儿童开启/使用 | To make something safe for children by preventing them from opening or using it. | They child-proofed the cabinets under the sink. 他们在水槽下的橱柜做了防儿童开启的措施。 | Synonyms: secure, protect from children | Antonyms: expose to children | verb
