calligrapher: /kəˈlɪɡrəfər/ | n. | 书法家 | A person who is skilled in the art of calligraphy (beautiful handwriting). |  He is a renowned calligrapher, known for his elegant script. 他是一位著名的书法家，以其优雅的书法而闻名。

The calligrapher spent hours perfecting each stroke.  这位书法家花了几个小时完善每一笔。 | 近义词: penman, scribe | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "typisti" for someone who types rather than writes beautifully) | 词性: noun
