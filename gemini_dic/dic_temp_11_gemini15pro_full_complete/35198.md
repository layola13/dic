clubfooted:/ˈklʌbˌfʊtɪd/| adj.|畸形的，内翻足的|Having a deformed foot that is twisted so that the sole cannot be placed flat on the ground.  A congenital deformity in which the foot is twisted out of shape or position.|The child was born clubfooted.  这个孩子出生时就患有畸形足。|Synonyms: taliped,  deformed, misshapen|Antonyms: normal, healthy|adjective
