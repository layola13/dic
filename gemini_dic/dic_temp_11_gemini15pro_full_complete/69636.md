feminizes:/ˈfɛmɪnaɪzɪz/
verb
使女性化,使具有女性特征
To make or become feminine in appearance, characteristics, or behavior.
The hormone therapy feminizes the male body.  激素疗法使男性身体女性化。
The author feminizes the main character in the novel.  作者将小说中的主角女性化。
synonyms: emasculates, effeminates
antonyms: masculinizes, virilizes
词性:v.
