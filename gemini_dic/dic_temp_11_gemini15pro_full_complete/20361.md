bisque:/bɪsk/ | n. |浓汤；淡黄褐色 |A thick, creamy soup, typically made of shellfish or puréed vegetables. Also, a pale yellowish-brown color. |例句：The lobster bisque was rich and flavorful. 龙虾浓汤味道浓郁。

例句：She chose a bisque-colored dress for the wedding. 她选择了一件淡黄褐色的连衣裙参加婚礼。|近义词：soup, potage, cream soup (for food); beige, buff, cream (for color) |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thin broth" for the food sense) |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "bisque," perhaps from "Biscaye" (Baycay), a region in the Bay of Biscay, though the connection is uncertain. Another theory suggests it relates to "bis cuit" (twice cooked).
