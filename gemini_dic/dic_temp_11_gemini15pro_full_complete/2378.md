adipocerate:/ˌædɪpəˈsɪəreɪt/ | adj.  n.| 脂肪化的，尸蜡 | A waxy or fatty substance formed by the decomposition of animal tissues in moist environments, also called grave wax.  | The body was adipocerate, preserved by the peculiar conditions of the bog.  这具尸体呈脂肪化状态，被沼泽的特殊环境保存了下来。 |近义词: waxy, fatty,saponified |反义词: decomposed, putrefied | adjective, noun

adipocerate:/ˌædɪpəˈsɪəreɪt/ | v. | (使)脂肪化，(使)变成尸蜡 | To convert into adipocere. | The damp conditions of the tomb adipocerated the body.  潮湿的墓穴环境使尸体脂肪化了。|近义词: saponify |反义词: decompose, putrefy | verb
