's:/z/ or /s/ | possessive marker |  的 (表示所属关系) |  Indicates possession or belonging.  It is a contraction of "is" or "has" when used with pronouns (he's, she's, it's) but functions as a possessive marker with nouns.  Derived from Old English "es," genitive case ending. | John's car is blue.  约翰的车是蓝色的。

My sister's house is big. 我姐姐的房子很大。

近义词: of (in some cases) | 反义词: None | 词性: possessive marker
