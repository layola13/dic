congestion:/kənˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 拥堵，堵塞 | An excessive or abnormal accumulation of something, typically people or traffic, in a limited space.  | The traffic congestion in the city center is terrible during rush hour. 城市中心的交通拥堵在高峰时段非常严重。 |  Synonyms: overcrowding, blockage, gridlock | Antonyms: flow, clearance | noun

congested:/kənˈdʒɛstɪd/ | adj. | 拥堵的，堵塞的 | Excessively or abnormally full of something, typically people or traffic. | The roads are congested with holiday traffic. 道路被假日交通堵塞了。| Synonyms: overcrowded, jammed, packed | Antonyms: clear, open, flowing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congestionem, from Latin congestus, past participle of congerere "to heap together," from com- "together" + gerere "to carry."
