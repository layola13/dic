judicata:/ˌdʒuːdɪˈkeɪtə/ /ˌdʒuːdɪˈkɑːtə/ | n. | 已判决事项，既判力 |  A matter that has been adjudicated by a competent court and may not be pursued further by the same parties. | The principle of res judicata prevents the same parties from relitigating the same issue.  这既判力原则阻止相同当事人就同一问题再次提起诉讼。| Synonyms: adjudicated, decided, settled | Antonyms: pending, undecided, unresolved | noun
