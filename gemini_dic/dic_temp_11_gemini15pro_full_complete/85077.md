hard-pressed:/ˌhɑːrdˈprɛst/ | adj. | 陷入困境的，拮据的，很难的 | Under great difficulty or stress, especially financial stress; facing a very difficult situation. |  Many families are hard-pressed to pay their bills. 许多家庭难以支付账单。 | synonyms: strained, stressed, struggling, in difficulty | antonyms: affluent, prosperous, wealthy | adjective


hard-pressed:/ˌhɑːrdˈprɛst/ | adj. | 被催逼的，被紧迫的 | Forced to do something, usually because of time constraints. |  The team was hard-pressed to finish the project on time. 团队被迫加紧完成项目以按时交付。 | synonyms: pressured, hurried, rushed, urged | antonyms: relaxed, unhurried, leisurely | adjective
