doctorer:/ˈdɑːktərər/ | n. | 篡改者，伪造者；修理者 | Someone who alters something secretly or dishonestly; someone who mends or repairs something. |  The antique doctorer made the old chair look brand new.  这个古董修理者使旧椅子看起来像新的一样。| Synonyms: falsifier, forger, repairer, restorer | Antonyms: authenticator, verifier | noun

doctorer:/ˈdɑːktərər/ | n. | 庸医 | A person who pretends to be a physician; a quack. |  He went to a doctorer who prescribed him useless medicine. 他去找了个庸医，那庸医给他开了没用的药。 | Synonyms: quack, charlatan | Antonyms: physician, doctor | noun
