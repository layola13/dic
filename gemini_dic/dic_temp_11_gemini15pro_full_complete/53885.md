discordance:/dɪˈskɔːrdəns/ | n. |不一致，不和谐，不协调 |Lack of agreement or harmony; conflict. |例句：There is a discordance between his words and his actions. 他的言行不一致。

例句：The discordance between the two studies is puzzling.  这两项研究之间的不一致令人费解。

|近义词：disagreement, conflict, disharmony, variance, discrepancy, inconsistency |反义词：agreement, harmony, concord, consistency, accordance |词性: 名词
