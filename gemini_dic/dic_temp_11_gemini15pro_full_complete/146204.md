overexpect:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspekt/ | v. | 过度期望，期望过高 | To expect too much; to have unrealistic expectations. |  He overexpected his son's performance in the exam, leading to disappointment. 他对他儿子在考试中的表现期望过高，导致了失望。

| Example:  She overexpected the return on her investment and was disappointed. 她对投资回报期望过高，结果很失望。| Synonym:  overestimate, anticipate excessively | Antonym: underestimate, underexpect | Verb
