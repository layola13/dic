antherozooid:/ˌænθərəˈzəʊɪd/| n.|游动精子，精子体|A motile male gamete of algae, ferns, mosses, and some other plants.|例句：The antherozooid swims through water to reach the archegonium. 游动精子游过水到达颈卵器。

例句：Ferns and mosses rely on antherozooids for fertilization. 蕨类植物和苔藓依靠游动精子进行受精。|近义词：spermatozoid|反义词:egg cell|词性:noun
