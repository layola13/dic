godforsaken:/ˈɡɒdˌfəˈseɪkən/| adj.|被上帝遗弃的，荒凉的，凄凉的|Desolate, remote, and wretched; as if abandoned by God.  It often implies a place that is bleak, barren, and unpleasant. |例句：They lived in a godforsaken village in the mountains. 他们住在一个荒凉的山村里。|例句：The travelers found themselves stranded in a godforsaken desert.  旅行者发现自己被困在一个荒凉的沙漠里。|近义词：desolate, bleak, barren, forsaken, remote, wretched|反义词：blessed, fortunate, prosperous, thriving|词性:形容词
