figeater:/ˈfɪɡˌiːtər/
noun
无花果食者；(尤指)食蜜鸟
A bird that eats figs, especially a honey eater.
Figbirds are figeaters and are important seed dispersers. 食蜜鸟吃无花果，是重要的种子传播者。
Synonyms: frugivore, fruit-eater
Antonyms: carnivore, herbivore (if considering only diet excluding fruits)
n.
