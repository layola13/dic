breer: /briːr/
n. |荆棘，野蔷薇|A prickly shrub or plant.|The path was overgrown with breers.|近义词: thorn, briar, bramble|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑smooth, soft)|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *brere, from Old English brǣr (“briar”), from Proto-Germanic *brēzō (“thorn bush”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰars- (“point, bristle”). Cognate with West Frisian brar (“briar”), Dutch braam (“bramble”), German Brombeere (“blackberry”).
