ganglands:/ˈɡæŋˌlændz/
n.
黑社会活动猖獗的地区；犯罪猖獗的地区
Areas dominated by criminal gangs, often associated with violence and illegal activities.
The detective spent years infiltrating the city's ganglands.  这名侦探花了数年时间渗透到该市的犯罪团伙中。
The film portrays the brutal realities of life in the ganglands. 这部电影描绘了黑社会生活的残酷现实。
synonyms: underworld, slums, badlands
antonyms: safe neighborhoods, affluent areas
noun
