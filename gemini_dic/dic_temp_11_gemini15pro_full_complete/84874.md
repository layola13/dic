hangings: /ˈhæŋɪŋz/
n.
悬挂物；(尤指)绞刑
objects that are hung, especially curtains or tapestries; execution by hanging
The museum displayed medieval tapestries and other hangings.  博物馆展出了中世纪的挂毯和其他悬挂物。
Public hangings were common in the 18th century. 18世纪，公开绞刑很常见。
Synonyms: drapes, curtains, tapestry (for decorative hangings); execution, capital punishment (for execution by hanging)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the object sense. For the execution sense, perhaps:  pardon, reprieve, acquittal)
词性: noun
