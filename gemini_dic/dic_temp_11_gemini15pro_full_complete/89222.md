homeworks:/ˈhoʊmwɜːrks/ | n. | 家庭作业（复数，不常用）|  Usually, "homework" is used in its uncountable form, meaning it doesn't typically have a plural. It refers to tasks assigned to students to be completed outside of class. While grammatically possible, using "homeworks" to refer to multiple sets of homework assignments is uncommon.  | I have a lot of homework to do tonight.  我今晚有很多家庭作业要做。| Near synonyms: assignments, exercises, studies| Antonym:  leisure, free time | noun
