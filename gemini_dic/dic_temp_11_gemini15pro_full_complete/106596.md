kudin:/ˈkuːdɪn/ | n. | money; cash (Nigerian slang) |Nigerian slang term for money.  It is derived from the Hausa word "kudi" which means money. | Example:  I need to get some kudin to pay my bills. 我需要弄点钱来付账单。 Example:  She's got plenty of kudin. 她很有钱。 | Synonym:  cash, dough, bread, money | Antonym: debt, poverty | noun
