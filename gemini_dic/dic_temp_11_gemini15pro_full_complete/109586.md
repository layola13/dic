lethality:/liːˈθælɪti/
n.|致命性，杀伤力|The lethality of the virus is a major concern. 病毒的致命性是一个主要问题。|The new weapon has a high degree of lethality.  这种新武器杀伤力很强。|
近义词:deadliness, fatality, mortality
反义词:harmlessness, safety
词性:noun

lethality:/liːˈθælɪti/
n.|致命性，杀伤力|The study examined the lethality of different snake venoms. 这项研究检测了不同蛇毒的致命性。|The lethality of the disease has decreased significantly with the new treatment.  由于新的治疗方法，这种疾病的致命性已显著降低。|
近义词:potency, destructiveness
反义词:innocuousness, harmlessness
词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *lēthālitās, from Latin lēthālis (“lethal”) + -itās (“-ity”). Equivalent to lethal +‎ -ity.
