disinhibitory:/dɪsɪnˈhɪbɪtəri/
adjective
去抑制的，解除抑制的
Reducing or eliminating inhibition; tending to remove a restraint, social or psychological.  Disinhibitory effects can manifest in different ways, such as increased impulsivity, reduced social anxiety, or heightened aggression.
Alcohol has a disinhibitory effect on some people, making them more talkative and less reserved.  酒精对某些人有去抑制作用，使他们更健谈，更不拘谨。
The medication can have disinhibitory side effects, leading to impulsive behavior.  这种药物可能有去抑制作用，导致冲动行为。
近义词:  releasing, liberating, freeing
反义词:  inhibiting, restraining, suppressing
词性: adjective
词源:  dis- (prefix meaning "removal or reversal") + inhibit (verb meaning "to restrain or prevent") + -ory (suffix forming adjectives)
