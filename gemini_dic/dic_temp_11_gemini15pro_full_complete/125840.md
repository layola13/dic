词源:
From Middle English *murthre, from Old French murtre, from Late Latin morthrum, from Frankish *murthra (“secret killing”), from Proto-Germanic *murþrą (“murder, secret killing”). Cognate with Old English morþor, Old High German mord, Old Norse morð.  The spelling with -th- was influenced by death.
