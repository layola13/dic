mortgageable:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒəbl/
adjective
可抵押的
Able to be used as security against a loan.
The house is mortgageable and can be used to secure a loan. 这房子可以抵押，可以用来获得贷款。
My car is mortgageable. 我的车子可以抵押。
Synonyms: securable, pledgeable
Antonyms: unsecurable, non-pledgeable
词性: adjective
