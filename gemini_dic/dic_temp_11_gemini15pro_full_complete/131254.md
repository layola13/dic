noncarrier:/ˌnɒnˈkæriər/ /ˌnɑːnˈkæriər/ | n. |非携带者，非运营商|A person, organism, or substance that does not harbor or transmit a particular disease or characteristic, especially a recessive gene.|例句：She is a noncarrier for cystic fibrosis. 她不是囊性纤维化的携带者。

|近义词：non-transmitter, unaffected individual |反义词:carrier|词性:名词


noncarrier:/ˌnɒnˈkæriər/ /ˌnɑːnˈkæriər/ | adj. | 非运营商的|Relating to a company that does not operate a transportation network. |例句：A noncarrier airline sells tickets but doesn't operate its own flights.  一家非运营商航空公司出售机票，但不运营自己的航班。|近义词：independent, third-party |反义词:carrier, operator|词性:形容词
