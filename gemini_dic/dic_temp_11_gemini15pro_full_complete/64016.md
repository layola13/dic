epitaphian:/ˌepɪˈtæfiən/| adj.|墓志铭的；适合做墓志铭的；（风格）严肃而悲伤的|Of, relating to, or resembling an epitaph; suitable for an epitaph; (of style) solemn and mournful.|Her epitaphian poem moved everyone to tears.  她那首墓志铭式的诗让每个人都感动得流泪。|Synonyms:  funereal, elegiac, mournful, somber|Antonyms:  joyful, celebratory, cheerful|adjective

epitaphian:/ˌepɪˈtæfiən/| n.|墓志铭体诗|A poem in the style of an epitaph.|He wrote an epitaphian for his beloved dog. 他为心爱的狗写了一首墓志铭体诗。|Synonyms:  elegy, lament|Antonyms:  paean, ode|noun
