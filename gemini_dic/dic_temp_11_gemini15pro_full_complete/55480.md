distractedness:/dɪˈstræktɪdnəs/| n. |心烦意乱，注意力分散|The state of being unable to concentrate or focus because one's mind is preoccupied. |Her distractedness during the meeting led to several important details being missed.  她在会议期间心不在焉，导致遗漏了几个重要细节。|近义词：inattention, absent-mindedness, preoccupation|反义词：focus, concentration, attentiveness|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From distract (v.) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives). Distract ultimately comes from the Latin word "distractus," past participle of "distrahere," meaning "to draw apart" or "to divert."
