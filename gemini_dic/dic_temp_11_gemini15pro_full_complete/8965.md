antihypnotic:/ˌænti.hɪpˈnɒt.ɪk/ /ˌænti.haɪpˈnɑː.tɪk/| adj. n.|抗眠的，抗催眠的；抗眠药|A substance or agent that counteracts or reduces the effects of hypnotics or sleep-inducing drugs. It can also refer to the property of being antihypnotic.|
The doctor prescribed an antihypnotic medication to help him stay awake during the day. 医生给他开了抗眠药，以帮助他在白天保持清醒。

Caffeine is a well-known antihypnotic. 咖啡因是一种众所周知的抗眠物质。|
近义词：stimulant, wakefulness-promoting|
反义词：hypnotic, soporific, sleep-inducing|
词性: adjective, noun

词源:
From anti- + hypnotic.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite of," and "hypnotic" refers to sleep or hypnosis.
