directed:/dɪˈrektɪd/ | adj. | 指导的，定向的 |  guided, aimed, or focused in a particular direction or at a particular person or thing | The teacher directed the students to complete the assignment. 老师指导学生完成作业。 | guided, aimed, focused | undirected, aimless | adjective

directed:/dɪˈrektɪd/ | v. | (direct的过去式和过去分词) 指导，管理，指挥 | to manage, control, or oversee |  The film was directed by Steven Spielberg. 这部电影是由史蒂文·斯皮尔伯格导演的。 | managed, controlled, oversaw | neglected | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *directen*, from Latin *dīrectus*, past participle of *dīrigō* (“I arrange, set straight, guide, align”), from *dī-* (“apart, asunder, between”) + *regō* (“I rule, guide, govern”).
