一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
Where do you live? = 你住在哪里？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching? = 你在看什么？
Why is he crying? = 他为什么哭？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
Where has she been? = 她去过哪里？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
Why has he been sleeping so much? = 他为什么一直睡这么多？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
Where did she go yesterday? = 她昨天去哪里了？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
Why was he driving so fast? = 他为什么开那么快？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
Why had she left so early? = 她为什么走得这么早？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed majors? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
Why had he been complaining so much? = 他之前为什么一直抱怨那么多？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
Where will they go for vacation? = 他们要去哪里度假？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
Where will she be living next year? = 明年她会住在哪里？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
How much money will he have saved by then? = 到那时他将存了多少钱？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
How long will she have been studying English by then? = 到那时她将已经学习英语多久了？
