befuddled:/bɪˈfʌdld/| adj.|困惑的，糊涂的|Confused and unable to think clearly.|例句：He was completely befuddled by the complex instructions. 他完全被复杂的指示弄糊涂了。|例句：The long and tedious journey left her feeling befuddled and disoriented. 漫长而乏味的旅程让她感到困惑和迷失方向。|近义词：confused, bewildered, perplexed, addled|反义词：clear-headed, lucid, coherent|词性:形容词

词源: From be- (making a verb causative) + fuddle (“to intoxicate”).  Essentially meaning "to make drunk".
