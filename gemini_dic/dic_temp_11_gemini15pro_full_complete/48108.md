decliners:/dɪˈklaɪnərz/
n.
下降者；衰退者；拒绝者
People or things that are declining in number, value, or strength.
The number of churchgoers has been amongst the decliners in recent years. 近年来，去教堂的人数一直在下降。
Synonyms: losers, fallers, dropouts
Antonyms: risers, gainers, improvers
noun

decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/
v.
下降；衰退；拒绝
(of something) to become smaller, fewer, or less; to decrease
The company's profits have declined sharply this year.  今年公司利润急剧下降。
Synonyms: decrease, diminish, lessen
Antonyms: increase, rise, grow
verb

decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/
n.
下降；衰退；斜坡
a gradual and continuous loss of strength, numbers, quality, or value
There has been a steady decline in the number of smokers.  吸烟者人数持续下降。
Synonyms: decrease, downturn, slump
Antonyms: increase, upturn, rise
