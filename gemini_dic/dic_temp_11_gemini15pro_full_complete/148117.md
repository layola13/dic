oversecures:/ˌoʊvər səˈkjʊrz/ | v. | 过度保护，过分防护 | To secure something excessively or unnecessarily; to make too safe. |  He oversecures his bicycle with three different locks. 他用三种不同的锁过度保护他的自行车。

The company oversecures its data, making access difficult even for authorized personnel.  这家公司过度保护其数据，即使是授权人员也很难访问。 | Near synonyms: overprotect, coddle, shield, cosset | Antonyms: neglect, endanger, expose | verb

oversecured:/ˌoʊvər səˈkjʊrd/ | adj. | 被过度保护的，被过分防护的 |  Secured excessively or unnecessarily. | The house was oversecured with alarms and cameras. 这所房子装满了警报器和摄像头，被过度保护了。

Feeling oversecured in his position, he stopped putting in effort.  由于感到自己的职位过于安全，他不再努力工作了。 | Near synonyms: overprotected, shielded, cosseted | Antonyms: neglected, endangered, exposed | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively," "too much") + "secure" (from Latin "securus" meaning "free from care, safe").
