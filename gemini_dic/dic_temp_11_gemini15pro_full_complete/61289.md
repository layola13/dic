embalms:/ɪmˈbɑːmz/ /ɪmˈbɑːms/
verb
防腐处理；涂香油
treats (a corpse) with preservatives in order to prevent decay.
The ancient Egyptians embalmed their pharaohs. 古埃及人对他们的法老进行防腐处理。
preserve, mummify
decay, decompose
