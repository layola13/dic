loord:/lɔːrd/ | n. | 乡巴佬；笨蛋；懒汉 | A rustic, clumsy, or lazy person. | He called him a loord for not helping with the chores. 他因为他没帮忙做家务而叫他懒汉。| 近义词：bumpkin, yokel, oaf, lout | 反义词: gentleman, sophisticate | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "lurd," itself possibly a shortening and alteration of "sluggard."  Its precise origins are uncertain, and it is considered dialectal or informal.
