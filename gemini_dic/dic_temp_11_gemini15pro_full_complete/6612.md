anacreontic:/ˌænəkriˈɒntɪk/
adjective
放荡享乐的；酒神精神的；阿那克里翁式的
Relating to or suggestive of the Greek lyric poet Anacreon; convivial, especially in celebrating love and wine.
The party had a decidedly anacreontic atmosphere, with wine flowing freely and guests reciting poetry.  这个派对充满了酒神精神的氛围，美酒畅饮，宾客们吟诵着诗歌。
Near synonyms: bacchanalian, carousing, convivial, hedonistic, jovial, merry
Near antonyms: ascetic, austere, sober, temperate
词性: adjective

anacreontic:/əˌnækriˈɑːntɪk/
noun
阿那克里翁体诗；以酒和爱情为主题的诗歌
A poem in the style of Anacreon, typically dealing with love and wine.
He penned a witty anacreontic for the birthday of his beloved. 他为爱人的生日写了一首诙谐的阿那克里翁体诗。
Near synonyms: lyric, ode, poem, verse
Near antonyms: N/A
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Anakreōn* (name of a Greek lyric poet) + *-ikos* (suffix forming adjectives).
