densities:/ˈdɛnsɪtiz/
n.
密度;密集度
The measurement of how much mass is contained within a given volume.
The population density of Tokyo is very high.  东京的人口密度非常高。

近义词: concentrations, thicknesses
反义词: sparseness, thinness
词性: 名词(plural of density)

density:/ˈdɛnsɪti/
n.
密度;密集度
The degree of compactness of a substance.
The density of lead is higher than that of aluminum.  铅的密度比铝高。

近义词: compactness, thickness
反义词: sparseness, thinness
词性: 名词
