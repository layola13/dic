heteroousian:/ˌhɛtərəˈuːsiən/
adjective
不同实体的；不同物质的
Of or relating to a doctrine, especially of the nature of Christ, maintaining that the Father and the Son are of different substances.  This contrasted with the homoousian view, which asserted they were of the same substance.
The Council of Nicaea condemned the heteroousian view.  尼西亚会议谴责了异质说。
Near Antonyms: homoousian, consubstantial
Antonyms:  Not commonly used in everyday language, so direct antonyms are rare.  Conceptually, "identical in substance" could be considered an antonym.
