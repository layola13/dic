disjointedly:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪdli/
| adv. |不连贯地，支离破碎地|Lacking connection or consistency; in a disjointed manner. |Her speech was rambling and disjointedly delivered, making it difficult to follow. 她的演讲漫无边际，断断续续，很难理解。|The patient spoke disjointedly, jumping from one topic to another. 病人说话语无伦次，从一个话题跳到另一个话题。|incoherently, disconnectedly, ramblingly|coherently, smoothly, connectedly|词性:副词
