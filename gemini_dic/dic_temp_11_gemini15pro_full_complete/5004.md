all-american:/ˌɔːl əˈmerɪkən/| adj. n.| 全美明星（运动员）；典型的美国人（事物）|  Relating to or characteristic of an ideal or exemplary American, especially a wholesome, successful, and popular athlete chosen for an all-star team; also, a person or thing that is typically American. | He's an all-American boy. 他是一个典型的美国男孩。|  Synonyms: exemplary, ideal, quintessential, classic, representative | Antonyms: atypical, unconventional, nonconformist| adjective, noun

all-american:/ˌɔːl əˈmerɪkən/| n.| 全美明星运动员 | A player selected for an all-star team composed of outstanding athletes in a particular sport. | She was named an all-American in basketball. 她被评为篮球全美明星运动员。| Synonyms: star athlete, champion, top player | Antonyms: mediocre player, benchwarmer | noun
