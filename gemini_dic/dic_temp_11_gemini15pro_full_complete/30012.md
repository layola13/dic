cawky:/ˈkɔːki/| adj.|嗓音粗哑的；刺耳的；令人不悦的|Having a harsh, grating, or unpleasant voice, like the caw of a crow.|Her cawky laugh echoed through the hallway. 她粗哑的笑声回荡在走廊里。|近义词：raucous, strident, harsh|反义词：melodious, soothing, pleasant|词性:形容词

词源:  From "caw" (the cry of a crow) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately imitative in origin.
