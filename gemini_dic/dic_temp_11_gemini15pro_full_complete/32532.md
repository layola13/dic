chip's:/tʃɪps/| n.|薯片；芯片；碎片；筹码 (复数通常指薯条或炸薯片)|Belonging to or associated with a chip. It usually refers to potato chips in the context of food, or to a microchip in technical contexts.  Example: "The chip's processing power is impressive."  芯片的处理能力令人印象深刻。 |近义词：(for potato chips) crisps, fries; (for microchip) integrated circuit, semiconductor |反义词:(no direct opposite, depends on context) |词性:noun (possessive form)
