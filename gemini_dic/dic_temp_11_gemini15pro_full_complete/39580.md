conscriptionist:/kənˈskrɪpʃənɪst/
n.
征兵主义者；支持义务兵役制者
A person who advocates or supports conscription.
The conscriptionist argued that a strong military required compulsory service.  征兵主义者认为强大的军队需要义务兵役。
The politician, a staunch conscriptionist, believed a draft was essential for national security. 这位政治家是一位坚定的征兵主义者，他认为征兵对国家安全至关重要。
近义词：draft advocate, pro-conscription
反义词：pacifist, anti-conscriptionist
词性: noun

Etymology:
From conscription + -ist. Conscription from French *conscription*, from Latin conscrīptiō (“enrolment, levy”), from conscrībō (“enroll, levy”), from con- (“together”) + scrībō (“write”).
