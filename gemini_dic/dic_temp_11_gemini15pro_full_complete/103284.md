jiaqing:/ˌdʒɑːˈtʃɪŋ/| n.|嘉庆|Jiaqing refers to the Jiaqing Emperor (1760-1820), the sixth emperor of the Qing dynasty in China.  It can also refer to the era of his reign (1796-1820).|The Jiaqing Emperor faced numerous challenges during his reign. 嘉庆皇帝在其统治期间面临诸多挑战。|The Jiaqing era saw a decline in the Qing dynasty's power. 嘉庆时期见证了清朝国力的衰落。|Synonym:  Qing Dynasty (for the era), Emperor Renzong (formal name)|Antonym: N/A|Noun
