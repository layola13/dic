词源:  From "cheese" + "paring" (the act of cutting away the rind or outer layer of something).  Originally referred to the frugal practice of paring away the rind of cheese very thinly to avoid waste.  Over time, the term expanded to refer to any kind of excessive frugality or stinginess.
