manilas:/məˈnɪləz/
noun
马尼拉麻绳；马尼拉雪茄
Manila hemp ropes or cigars made in Manila, Philippines.
The sailors used strong manilas to secure the cargo. 水手们用结实的马尼拉麻绳固定货物。
Synonyms:  Manila rope, abaca rope
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific type of rope)
词性: noun
