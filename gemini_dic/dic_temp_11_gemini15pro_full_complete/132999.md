nonengagements:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/
noun
不参与，不约定，不交战
lack of engagements or commitments; a state of not being involved or committed.
例句：His nonengagement in the project caused delays. 他对这个项目的不参与导致了延误。

例句：The policy of nonengagement with the enemy proved disastrous.  不与敌人交战的政策最终证明是灾难性的。

近义词：inaction, aloofness, detachment, neutrality
反义词：engagement, involvement, commitment, participation
词性:n.
