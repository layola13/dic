indigometer: /ˌɪndɪˈɡɒmɪtər/ (UK) /ˌɪndɪˈɡɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n. |靛蓝计，靛蓝测定仪|An instrument used to measure the amount of indigo dye in a solution.  It determines the quality and strength of the indigo dye.|例句：The indigo farmer used an indigometer to assess the quality of his latest batch.  靛蓝种植者使用靛蓝计来评估他最新一批靛蓝的质量。

例句：The textile factory relied on the indigometer for accurate dye measurements.  纺织厂依靠靛蓝计进行精确的染料测量。|近义词：dye meter, colorimeter|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

indigometer: |Etymology: From indigo + -meter. Indigo comes from the Greek word *indikon*, meaning "Indian dye," as indigo was originally imported from India. The suffix "-meter" derives from the Greek metron, "measure."|
