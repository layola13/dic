colorize: /ˈkʌləˌraɪz/ (US)| v. |着色，上色|To add color to something, typically a black and white film or photograph.|They plan to colorize the classic film. 他们计划为这部经典电影上色。|近义词：colorise (UK), tint, paint, dye|反义词: bleach, decolorize|词性:动词


词源:  From "color" + "-ize," a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or cause to be."  The British spelling retains the "s" from "colorise."
