cannibally:/ˌkænɪˈbælɪkli/ | adv. | 食人般地，残忍地 | In a manner resembling cannibalism; savagely. |  He attacked his competitor cannibally, trying to destroy his reputation. 他凶狠地攻击他的竞争对手，试图摧毁他的名声。| 近义词：savagely, ferociously, brutally | 反义词：kindly, gently, mercifully | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From cannibal + -ly. Cannibal comes from the Spanish word *caníbal*, derived from the name of the Caribs, a West Indies tribe formerly reputed to practice cannibalism.
