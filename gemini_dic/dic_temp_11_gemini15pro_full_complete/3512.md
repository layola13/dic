afterthought:/ˈæftərθɔːt/ (US), /ˈɑːftəθɔːt/ (UK)| n.|事后想法；补充；附言|An idea, thought, or plan that was not originally intended but is thought of or added later.|I added a postscript to the letter as an afterthought. 我在信后又补充了几句。|synonyms: reconsideration, addition, supplement, appendix|antonyms: forethought, premeditation|noun
