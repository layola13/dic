domesticism: /dəˈmɛstɪˌsɪzəm/
n.
专注于家庭事务，家庭生活；对家庭生活的热爱
Devotion to home and domestic affairs; an interest in home life.

She embraced domesticism and found joy in creating a comfortable and loving home for her family. 她热爱家庭生活，并乐于为家人创造一个舒适而充满爱的家。

Domesticism was a common ideal for women in the 19th century.  在19世纪，专注于家庭事务是女性的普遍理想。

近义词: homemaking, domesticity
反义词: careerism, worldliness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From domestic + -ism.  "domestic" ultimately derives from Latin "domus" (house).
