blameless:/ˈbleɪmləs/| adj.|无辜的，无可指责的|Free from blame; innocent; irreproachable.  Not deserving censure or criticism.|She led a blameless life, dedicated to helping others. 她过着无可指责的生活，致力于帮助他人。|innocent, impeccable, unblemished, faultless, guiltless|guilty, culpable, blameworthy|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blāmlēas, from blāme (blame) + -lēas (without).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *blaimaz (disgrace, injury).
