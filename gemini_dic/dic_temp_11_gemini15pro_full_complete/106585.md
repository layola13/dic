kubernetes:/ˌkuːbərˈnɛtɪs/ | n. | Kubernetes（容器编排系统） | An open-source container-orchestration system for automating computer application deployment, scaling, and management.  It was originally designed by Google, and is now maintained by the Cloud Native Computing Foundation. | Example: We use Kubernetes to manage our containerized applications.  我们使用 Kubernetes 来管理我们的容器化应用程序。

| Example: Kubernetes simplifies the deployment of complex microservices architectures.  Kubernetes 简化了复杂微服务架构的部署。 | Synonym: container orchestrator,  | Antonym: N/A | noun
