diwali:/dɪˈwɑːliː/ |n.|排灯节; 印度灯节; 万灯节; 光明节|A Hindu festival of lights, celebrated over five days in late autumn.|We celebrate Diwali every year. 我们每年都庆祝排灯节。| Diwali is a time for family and feasting. 排灯节是家人团聚和盛宴的时刻。|Synonyms: Deepavali, Festival of Lights|Antonyms: None|Noun
