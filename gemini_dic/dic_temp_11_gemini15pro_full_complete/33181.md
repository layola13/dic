chromogenic:/ˌkrəʊməˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 产色的，显色的 | Producing color; relating to the production of color.  A chromogenic substance is one that can produce color, often through a chemical reaction. | Chromogenic bacteria are used in water testing.  产色细菌用于水质检测。| The chromogenic substrate produced a blue color indicating the presence of the enzyme.  显色底物产生蓝色，表明存在这种酶。| 近义词: color-producing, colorimetric, chromogenous | 反义词: achromic, colorless | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
chromo- (Greek) = color
-genic (Greek) = producing, generating
