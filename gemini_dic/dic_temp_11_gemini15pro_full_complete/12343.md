ashame:/əˈʃeɪm/| n.|羞愧，耻辱，憾事|A feeling of guilt, regret, or sadness because you know you have done something wrong.|I felt a deep sense of shame after I lied to my friend. 我对我的朋友撒谎后感到深深的羞愧。|近义词：guilt, disgrace, humiliation|反义词:pride, honor|名词
