compounder:/kəmˈpaʊndər/ (US), /ˈkɒmpaʊndə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 配方师；调配师；混合者；（使问题等）加重的人（或事物）；妥协者| someone who prepares medicines by mixing different ingredients; something that makes a problem or difficult situation worse; someone who settles a debt or other matter by agreeing to pay less than the full amount| The pharmacist carefully measured the ingredients as a skilled compounder.  药剂师像一位熟练的配方师一样仔细地测量配料。| synonyms: mixer, blender, combiner, aggravator, intensifier | antonyms: separator, divider, simplifier| noun

compounder:/kəmˈpaʊndər/ (US), /ˈkɒmpaʊndə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 使恶化；加重；混合；调配；妥协，和解（通过少付一些钱）| to make something bad worse; to mix something together; to settle a debt or other matter by agreeing to pay less than the full amount | His financial problems were compounded by the loss of his job. 他失业加剧了他的经济问题。| synonyms: worsen, aggravate, exacerbate, combine, mix | antonyms: alleviate, improve, separate | verb
