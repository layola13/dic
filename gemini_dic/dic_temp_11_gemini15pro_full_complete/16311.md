baramati:/bəˈrɑːmətiː/| n.|巴拉马蒂|Baramati is a city and a municipal council in Pune district in the state of Maharashtra, India. It is a tehsil headquarters.|Baramati is known for its agricultural produce. 巴拉马蒂以其农产品而闻名。|Near Pune| |noun
Etymology: From Marathi बारामती (bārāmatī).
