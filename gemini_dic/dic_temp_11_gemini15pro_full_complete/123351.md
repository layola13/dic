monolobular:/ˌmɒnəˈlɒbjʊlə(r)/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɑːbjʊlər/
adjective
单叶的；单瓣的
Having a single lobe.  Referring to a structure, typically an organ or part of an organ, that is composed of just one lobe.
The monolobular lung of some reptiles contrasts sharply with the multilobed lung of mammals.  一些爬行动物的单叶肺与哺乳动物的多叶肺形成鲜明对比。
The liver is normally multilobed, but in some species it can be monolobular. 肝脏通常是多叶的，但在某些物种中它可以是单叶的。
synonym: unilobar
antonym: multilobar
词性: adjective
