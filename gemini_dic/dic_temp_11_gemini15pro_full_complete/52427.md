didrachm:/ˈdaɪdrækm/ | n. | 双德拉克马（古希腊钱币） | An ancient Greek silver coin equivalent to two drachmas. |  Example: The didrachm was a common currency in ancient Athens.  双德拉克马是古雅典的通用货币。

Example:  He paid the temple tax with a didrachm. 他用一枚双德拉克马支付了圣殿税。 | 近义词: stater (another ancient Greek coin), coin, currency | 反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific coin. Could consider "debt" in a loose sense if referring to a lack of coins) | 词性: noun
