fancifulnesses:/ˈfænsifʊlnəsɪz/
n.
奇思妙想；想象力丰富；异想天开
The state or quality of being fanciful; a tendency to indulge in fantasy or whimsical ideas.
Her writing is full of fancifulnesses, creating a world of magical creatures and talking trees. 她的作品充满了奇思妙想，创造了一个充满神奇生物和会说话的树木的世界。
近义词:imaginativeness, creativity, whimsy
反义词:practicality, realism
