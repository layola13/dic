highbrow:/ˈhaɪbraʊ/| adj.|高雅的；知识分子的；阳春白雪的|Appealing to or appreciated by intellectuals.|例句：The museum's new exhibit is rather highbrow.  博物馆的新展览相当高雅。|近义词：intellectual, sophisticated, cultured, refined|反义词：lowbrow, unsophisticated, popular|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From the now outdated phrenological theory that a high forehead indicated high intelligence.  "High" + "brow" (forehead).
