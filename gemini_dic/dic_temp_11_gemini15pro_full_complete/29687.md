catheterize:/ˈkæθɪtəraɪz/| vt.|插管，导尿|To insert a catheter into (a body cavity, such as the bladder) for evacuating fluids or injecting medication. |The nurse catheterized the patient to drain his bladder.| 近义词: intubate |反义词: N/A |词性: verb

catheterization:/ˌkæθɪtərəˈzeɪʃən/| n.|插管，导尿（术）|The process of inserting a catheter. |The patient underwent catheterization to relieve urinary retention.| 近义词: intubation |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
