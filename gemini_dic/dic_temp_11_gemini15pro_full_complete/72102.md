flocculent:/ˈflɒkjʊlənt/ (UK) /ˈflɑːkjələnt/ (US)| adj.|絮状的；绒毛状的；羊毛状的|Having or resembling loose fluffy tufts like wool or cotton.  Characterized by a loosely aggregated, woolly, or cloudy appearance.|The beer had a flocculent yeast sediment at the bottom.|近义词：fluffy, woolly, downy, fleecy, feathery|反义词:compact, dense, solid|adjective
