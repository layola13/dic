love-sick:/ˈlʌvˌsɪk/| adj.|因单恋而精神恍惚的|Mentally distracted because of unrequited love; pining for someone.|He wandered around love-sick and distracted after she rejected him. 她拒绝他之后，他四处游荡，精神恍惚，饱受单恋之苦。|Synonyms: lovestruck, besotted|Antonyms: uncaring, detached|adjective
