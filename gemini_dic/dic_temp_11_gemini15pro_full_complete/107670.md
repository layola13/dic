laniard:/ˈlænjərd/ (US), /ˈlænɪəd/ (UK)| n.|挂绳，系索，吊带|A rope or cord worn around the neck, shoulder, or wrist for carrying something, such as a whistle, ID card, or keys.|例句：The sailor wore a lanyard with his whistle attached. 水手戴着挂着哨子的挂绳。|例句：She lost her keys because the lanyard broke.  因为挂绳断了，她丢了钥匙。|近义词：cord, rope, strap, tether|反义词: N/A|noun
