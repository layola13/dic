aphasia: /əˈfeɪʒə/ (US), /əˈfeɪziə/ (UK)| n. |失语症|Loss of ability to understand or express speech, caused by brain damage.|After the stroke, she suffered from aphasia and struggled to communicate. 中风后，她患上了失语症，难以沟通。|Synonyms: speechlessness, dysphasia|Antonyms: fluency, eloquence|noun
