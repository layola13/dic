chivalrous:/ˈʃɪvəlrəs/| adj.| 骑士精神的；侠义的；殷勤的；对女人彬彬有礼的|Marked by or exhibiting the qualities traditionally ascribed to a knight, such as courage, honor, courtesy, justice, and a readiness to help the weak.|He opened the door for her with a chivalrous gesture. 他以骑士般的姿态为她开门。|例句：He was known for his chivalrous behavior towards women.  他以对女性彬彬有礼的行为而闻名。

例句：It was chivalrous of him to offer his seat to the elderly lady. 他主动给老妇人让座，真是侠义之举。

近义词：gallant, courteous, honorable, noble|反义词:rude, cowardly, ungentlemanly|词性:adjective
