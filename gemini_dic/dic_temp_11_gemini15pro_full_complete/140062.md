oestrogenicity: /ˌiːstrədʒəˈnɪsɪti/ /ˌɛstrədʒəˈnɪsɪti/
noun
雌激素性，雌激素活性
The oestrogenicity of a compound refers to its ability to mimic the effects of oestrogen in the body.  一种化合物的雌激素性是指它在体内模拟雌激素作用的能力。
Soy products have some oestrogenicity. 大豆制品具有一定的雌激素活性。
Synonyms: estrogenic activity, estrogenic potency
Antonyms: anti-oestrogenicity
n.
