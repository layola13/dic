antipopularizations:/ˌæntiˌpɒpjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 反普及化；通俗化之反动 |  The act or process of making something less popular or accessible to the general public, often by making it more complex or obscure.  It can also refer to works that oppose or critique the simplification or popularization of complex ideas. |  His dense academic prose could be seen as a form of antipopularization. 他的佶屈聱牙的学术散文可以被视为一种反普及化的形式。

The author's refusal to simplify his complex theories led to accusations of antipopularization. 作者拒绝简化他复杂的理论，导致他被指责为反普及化。| 近义词：obscuration, mystification | 反义词：popularization, simplification | 词性: 名词
