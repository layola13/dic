crispier:/ˈkrɪspɪər/| adj.|更脆的|Comparative form of crispy: more crispy.  Having a firm, brittle, and easily broken texture.|The chips were crispier than I expected. 薯片比我想象的要脆。|近义词：crunchier, firmer, more brittle|反义词：soggy, soft, chewy|词性:形容词
词源: From crisp + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Old English "crisp" meaning curly or wavy.
