jettisonable:/ˈdʒɛtɪsənəbl/
adjective
可抛弃的，可投弃的
able to be jettisoned.
The aircraft had jettisonable fuel tanks for emergencies.  飞机有可在紧急情况下抛弃的油箱。
The capsule carrying the astronauts back to earth had a jettisonable heat shield. 返回地球的太空舱有一个可抛弃的隔热罩。
近义词:disposable, expendable
反义词:essential, indispensable
词性:adjective

词源: From jettison + -able.  Jettison itself comes from the Old French "getaison" meaning "act of throwing overboard."
