hypernaturalness:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃrəlnəs/| n.| 超自然性，极度自然感，矫枉过正的自然|A state of artificiality that appears, paradoxically, natural due to its extremity.  It refers to something that is designed to appear natural but is actually exaggerated or amplified to a degree that it becomes unnatural or even surreal. |The filter gave her selfie a disturbing hypernaturalness – her skin was too smooth, her eyes too bright.| 近义词: artificiality, unnaturalness, contrivedness,  overdone, exaggerated | 反义词: naturalness, authenticity, genuineness |词性: noun
