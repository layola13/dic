isometrically: /ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪkli/ | adv. | 等距地 | In a way that maintains equal dimensions or measurements.  Relating to or denoting muscular action in which tension is developed without contraction of the muscle. | The crystal expanded isometrically when heated.  晶体受热后等距膨胀。| The lifter held the weight isometrically for ten seconds.  举重运动员等距支撑重物十秒钟。 | synonym: uniformly, evenly | antonym: anisometrically | word type: adverb
