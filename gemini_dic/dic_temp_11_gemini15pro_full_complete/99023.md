interagents:/ˌɪntərˈeɪdʒənts/
|n.| 代理人之间；相互作用的代理 |Agents that interact with each other or operate within a shared environment.| The interagents of the two companies worked together to negotiate the deal. 两家公司的代理人共同协商这笔交易。|近义词: collaborators, partners |反义词: competitors, adversaries |词性: 名词

Etymology:
inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
agent (noun): a person or thing that takes an active role or produces a specified effect (from Latin agere, "to do, drive, lead, act")
