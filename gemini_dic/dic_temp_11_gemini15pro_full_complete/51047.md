desolately:/ˈdesələtli/ | adv. | 凄凉地，荒凉地 | In a desolate manner; lonely; sadly. | The wind howled desolately across the empty plains.  风凄凉地呼啸过空旷的平原。 | Near Synonyms: forlornly, bleakly, miserably | Near Antonyms: cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

desolate:/ˈdesələt/ | adj. | 荒凉的，凄凉的 |  (of a place) deserted of people and in a state of bleak and dismal emptiness. | The desolate landscape stretched for miles.  荒凉的景色绵延数英里。 | Near Synonyms: barren, bleak, deserted | Near Antonyms: populated, cheerful, lively | 词性: 形容词
