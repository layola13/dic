okapi:/əˈkɑːpi/ | n. | 霍加狾，㺢㹢狓 |A central African mammal (Okapia johnstoni) related to the giraffe but having a shorter neck, striped hindquarters, and relatively short horns. It is the only living relative of the giraffe. |例句：The okapi is a shy and elusive creature, rarely seen in the wild.  霍加狾是一种害羞且难以捉摸的生物，在野外很少见到。

例句：The okapi's distinctive stripes help it blend in with the dappled sunlight of the rainforest. 霍加狾独特的条纹有助于它融入雨林斑驳的阳光中。 | 近义词：giraffe (distant relative) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
