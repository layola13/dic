inarticulation:/ɪnɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. |  表达不清，不清晰的吐字，口齿不清 |Inability to articulate speech clearly; indistinctness of utterance; inarticulateness. |  His inarticulation made it difficult to understand him. 他的口齿不清让人很难理解他。 | 近义词：inarticulateness, slurring, mumbling, stammering | 反义词：articulation, clarity, eloquence | 词性: 名词

inarticulate:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/ | adj. |  表达不清的，不善言辞的，说不出的 | Not articulate; unable to express oneself clearly or effectively in speech; not uttered with distinctness; not jointed or articulated. | He was inarticulate with rage. 他气得说不出话来。 |  He felt inarticulate in the face of such beauty.  面对如此美景，他感到难以言表。|近义词：speechless, tongue-tied, dumb, silent | 反义词：articulate, eloquent, fluent | 词性: 形容词


词源:
Late Middle English: from medieval Latin inarticulatus, from in- ‘not’ + articulatus (see articulate).
