narrower:/ˈnæroʊər/| adj.|更窄的，更狭隘的|Comparative form of narrow.  Meaning more limited in space, scope, or view.|The path became narrower as we climbed higher.  这条路随着我们爬得越高变得越窄。|近义词：thinner, slighter, smaller, tighter|反义词:wider, broader, larger|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *nearwa, from Proto-Germanic *narwaz.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *hner-, meaning "inward, below."
