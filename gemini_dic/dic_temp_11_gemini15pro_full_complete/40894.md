cookshop:/ˈkʊkʃɒp/ | n. |小餐馆，饮食店|A small restaurant or eating place, typically one that serves simple, inexpensive food. |例句：We grabbed a quick lunch at the local cookshop. 我们在当地的小餐馆里吃了一顿快餐。

例句：The cookshop was known for its hearty portions and affordable prices. 这家小餐馆以其丰盛的份量和实惠的价格而闻名。|近义词：café, diner, eatery, snack bar |反义词: restaurant (especially a more formal one)|词性: noun
