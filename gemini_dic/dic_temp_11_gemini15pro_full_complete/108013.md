last-ditchers:/ˈlæst dɪtʃərz/
|n.|最后一搏的人；死守待援者|People who make a final desperate attempt to achieve something, especially when all other options have failed.|The last-ditchers held out against the enemy until reinforcements arrived. 死守待援者抵抗敌人直到援军到达。|Synonyms: diehards, stalwarts|Antonyms: quitters, defeatists|Noun


last-ditch:/ˈlæst dɪtʃ/
