jambed:/dʒæmd/ | adj. | 堵塞的；卡住的；拥挤的 | pressed or squeezed tightly together, so that movement is difficult or impossible. | The traffic was jambed solid for miles. 交通拥堵了好几英里。| synonyms: stuck, wedged, jammed, blocked, clogged | antonyms: free, loose, unstuck | adjective
