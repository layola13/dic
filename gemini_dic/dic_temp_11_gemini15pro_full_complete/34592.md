clearway:/ˈklɪəweɪ/ |n.| 清除道路;畅通无阻的道路|A stretch of road where stopping or parking is prohibited, especially during rush hour, to allow traffic to flow freely. |  The police diverted traffic onto the clearway. 警方将车辆改道至畅通无阻的道路。

 The clearway helps to reduce congestion during peak hours. 清除道路有助于减少高峰时段的拥堵。 | synonyms: throughway, expressway, arterial road | antonyms: side street, back road | noun
