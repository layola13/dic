irresolvedly:/ˌɪrɪˈzɒlvdli/ /ˌɪrɪˈzɑːlvdli/
adverb|未解决地，犹豫不决地|in a way that is not settled or solved; hesitantly|He stared irresolutely at the closed door. 他犹豫不决地盯着那扇关着的门。|近义词:indecisively, hesitantly, doubtfully|反义词:resolutely, decisively, determinedly|词性:副词
Etymology: From irresolute + -ly. Irresolute from in- (not) + resolute (determined).  Resolute from Latin resolutus, past participle of resolvere (to loosen, dissolve, resolve).
