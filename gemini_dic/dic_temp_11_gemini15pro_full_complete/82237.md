grouching:/ˈɡraʊtʃɪŋ/| n. |抱怨，牢骚|The act of complaining or grumbling persistently. |He's been grouching all day about the weather.  他整天都在抱怨天气。|近义词：complaining, grumbling, moaning, whining|反义词：praising, complimenting|词性:noun (gerund)
grouchy:/ˈɡraʊtʃi/| adj. |脾气坏的，爱抱怨的|Inclined to complain or grumble; bad-tempered. |She's been very grouchy lately. 她最近脾气很暴躁。|近义词：grumpy, bad-tempered, irritable, cantankerous|反义词：cheerful, good-natured, pleasant|词性:adjective

词源: From the verb "grouch," which originated in the early 20th century, possibly as a variant of "grutch."  Ultimately related to Old French "grouchier" (to grumble).
