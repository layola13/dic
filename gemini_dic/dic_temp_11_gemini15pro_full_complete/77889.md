genial:/ˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. |友善的；亲切的；和蔼的|Friendly and cheerful.|例句：He was a genial host, making everyone feel welcome. 他是一位和蔼可亲的主人，让每个人都感到宾至如归。

例句：The genial sunshine warmed the garden. 温暖的阳光照耀着花园。|近义词：amicable, affable, cordial, pleasant, agreeable|反义词：unfriendly, hostile, cold, unpleasant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *genialis* "festive, joyous, pertaining to marriage," also "of or belonging to a genius," from *genius* "guardian deity or spirit which watched over each person from birth; innate ability or inclination" (see genius). The sense of "conducive to growth or comfort" (of climate, air, etc.) is first recorded 1660s, and that of "kindly, sympathetic" is from 1774.
