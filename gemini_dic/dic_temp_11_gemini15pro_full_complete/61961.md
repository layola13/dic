emulsion:/ɪˈmʌlʃən/ | n. | 乳状液；乳剂；感光乳剂 |A mixture of two or more liquids that are normally immiscible (unmixable) owing to liquid-liquid phase separation. One liquid is dispersed in the other in the form of small droplets.  |  Milk is an emulsion of fat in water. 牛奶是一种脂肪在水中的乳状液。| synonyms: suspension, colloid, mixture | antonyms: solution, compound | noun

emulsify:/ɪˈmʌlʃɪfaɪ/ | v. | 乳化 | To combine two or more liquids that are normally immiscible into an emulsion. |  The chef emulsified oil and vinegar to make a salad dressing. 厨师将油和醋乳化成沙拉酱。 | synonyms: blend, mix, homogenize | antonyms: separate, divide | verb
