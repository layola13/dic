hastive:/ˈheɪstɪv/| adj.|仓促的，草率的|Done or made too quickly, without enough thought or care. |例句：He made a hasty decision and later regretted it. 他做了一个草率的决定，后来后悔了。

例句：She cast a hasty glance at her watch and hurried out. 她匆匆瞥了一眼手表，然后急忙出去了。|近义词：hurried, rushed, impetuous, precipitate|反义词：deliberate, careful, considered, thoughtful|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *haestivus, from Latin *haestus (“heat, passion, excitement”). Related to haste.
