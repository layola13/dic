ETYMOLOGY->
From Polari (slang used by some actors, circus and fairground showmen, professional wrestlers, criminals, and gay subcultures): *nāk*, from Romany *nak* ("nose").  The connection is likely through the idea of "sticking one's nose into other people's business."
