husbanding:/ˈhʌzbəndɪŋ/ | n. | 节约，保存，管理 |  The careful management and conservation of resources. |  The farmer practiced careful husbanding of his water supply during the drought.  这位农民在干旱期间谨慎地管理他的水供应。 | synonyms: conserving, preserving, economizing, managing | antonyms: wasting, squandering, depleting | noun

husbanding:/ˈhʌzbəndɪŋ/ | v. | 节约，保存，管理 | To use sparingly or economically; to conserve. |  She husbanded her remaining energy for the final sprint. 她为最后的冲刺保存了她剩余的能量。 | synonyms: conserve, preserve, economize, manage | antonyms: waste, squander, deplete | verb
