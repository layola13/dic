fidelities:/fɪˈdɛlətiz/
n.|忠诚，忠实；保真度；尽责；精确度|The plural form of fidelity, referring to multiple instances of faithfulness or accuracy.|例句：He swore his fidelities to the king. 他宣誓效忠国王。

例句：The recording has remarkable fidelities to the original performance. 这张录音对原始演奏的还原度非常高。
|近义词：loyalties, allegiances, accuracies, precisions|反义词：treacheries, betrayals, infidelities, inaccuracies|词性:名词

fidelity:/fɪˈdɛləti/
n.|忠诚，忠实；保真度；尽责；精确度|Faithfulness to a person, cause, or belief, demonstrated by continuing loyalty and support.|例句：He swore his fidelity to the king. 他宣誓效忠国王。

例句：The recording has remarkable fidelity to the original performance. 这张录音对原始演奏的还原度非常高。
|近义词：loyalty, allegiance, accuracy, precision|反义词：treachery, betrayal, infidelity, inaccuracy|词性:名词
