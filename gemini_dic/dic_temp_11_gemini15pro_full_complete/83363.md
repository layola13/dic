gyrodus: /ˈdʒaɪrədʌs/ | n. | 旋转齿鱼 | An extinct genus of ray-finned fish that lived from the Middle Triassic to the Early Cretaceous.  Characterized by their spiral-shaped tooth plates. |  Gyrodus fossils have been found in Europe and North America. 旋转齿鱼化石已在欧洲和北美发现。 | The Gyrodus is known for its unique dentition.  这种旋转齿鱼以其独特的齿列而闻名。|  近义词:  (No close synonyms as it's a specific genus)  没有近义词（因为它是一个特定的属）| 反义词:  (Not applicable to a genus) 不适用 | 词性: noun
