overcorruption:/ˌoʊvərkrʌpʃən/ | n. | 过度腐败，极度腐败 | Excessive corruption; corruption that is widespread and deeply ingrained. | The overcorruption in the government led to widespread public unrest. 政府的极度腐败导致了公众的广泛不安。|  The overcorruption within the organization made it impossible to function effectively.  该组织内部的过度腐败使其无法有效运作。 | 近义词: extreme corruption, rampant corruption | 反义词: integrity, honesty | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "corruption" (from Latin "corruptio," meaning "a spoiling, bribing").
