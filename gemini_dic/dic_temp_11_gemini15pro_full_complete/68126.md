fagotto:/fəˈɡɒtəʊ/ (UK), /fəˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US)| n.|巴松管，低音管|A double-reed woodwind instrument with a long tube doubled back on itself, producing a deep, mellow tone.  It is also known as a bassoon.|The orchestra's fagotto player is renowned for his virtuosity.  管弦乐队的巴松管演奏家以其精湛的技艺而闻名。|Synonyms: bassoon|Antonyms: N/A|noun


fagotti:/fəˈɡɒti/ (UK), /fəˈɡɑːti/ (US)| n.|巴松管（复数）|Plural form of fagotto (bassoon).|The fagotti section played beautifully during the concerto.  协奏曲中，巴松管声部演奏得非常优美。|Synonyms: bassoons|Antonyms: N/A|noun
