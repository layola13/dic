dehort:/dɪˈhɔːrt/
verb
劝阻，告诫不要做
To strongly advise or urge (someone) against doing something.
The doctor dehorted her from smoking. 医生劝她戒烟。
dissuade, advise against, warn against
encourage, urge, persuade
