macrobiotically: /ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɑːtɪkli/
| adv. |以长寿饮食法地；从长寿饮食法的观点| In a way that relates to macrobiotics, a diet and lifestyle based on the principles of Zen Buddhism that emphasizes the consumption of whole grains and vegetables and avoids processed foods. |She eats macrobiotically, following a strict diet of grains and vegetables.| 近义词: holistically, naturally | 反义词: unhealthily, conventionally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From macrobiotic + -ally.  Macrobiotic from macro- "large, long" + bio- "life" + -tic "relating to". Ultimately from Greek makros "long" and bios "life".
