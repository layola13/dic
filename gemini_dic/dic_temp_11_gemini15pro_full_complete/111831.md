loggia:/ˈlɒdʒə/ (UK), /ˈlɑːdʒə/ (US)| n.|凉廊，走廊|A gallery or corridor open on one or more sides, especially one that forms part of a house and has a roof or ceiling.|We sat in the loggia, enjoying the cool evening breeze. 我们坐在凉廊里，享受着凉爽的晚风。|balcony, veranda, porch, gallery| courtyard, interior|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *loggia*, from Old French *loge*, from Frankish *laubja* "shelter," related to Old High German *louba* "shelter." Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *laubijō* "covered walkway, gallery."
