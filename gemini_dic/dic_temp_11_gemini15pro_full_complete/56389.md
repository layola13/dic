dole:/doʊl/| n. vt.|施舍；发放救济；失业救济金；悲哀；份额|A portion or allotment of something, especially money, food, or other necessities given as charity or as an allowance.|He received a weekly dole from the government. 他每周领取政府的失业救济金。|近义词：alms, charity, handout, benefit, allowance|反义词：earnings, income|词性:名词,动词

dole:/doʊl/| vt.|发放；施舍|To distribute something sparingly or as a charity.|The government doled out food to the refugees. 政府向难民分发了食物。|近义词：distribute, dispense, allot|反义词：withhold, deny|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *dāl (noun), *dǣlan (verb), from Proto-Germanic *dailiz, *dailjaną (“to divide, share, distribute”), from Proto-Indo-European *dail- (“to divide”). Cognate with Old Saxon dēlian, Old Frisian dēla, Middle Dutch delen, Old High German teilen (German teilen), Old Norse deila, Gothic dailjan.
