forfeit:/ˈfɔːrfɪt/ (US) /ˈfɔːfɪt/ (UK)| n. vt. adj.|罚金，丧失，没收； 罚款；被没收的|Lost or given up as a penalty. |His claim to the throne was forfeit. 他对王位的要求被取消了。|Synonyms: lost, surrendered, relinquished|Antonyms: retained, kept, maintained|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *forfait* "crime; penalty for a crime,"  from *forfaire* "to transgress, forfeit," from *for-* "outside, beyond" + *faire* "to do" (from Latin *facere* "to make, do").
