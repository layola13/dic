cymballike:/ˈsɪmbəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像铙钹的，像钹的声音的|Resembling a cymbal in sound or appearance. | The sound was cymballike, a sharp metallic clang. 声音像铙钹一样，发出尖锐的金属碰撞声。| The crashing waves made a cymballike noise.  海浪撞击发出像铙钹一样的声音。 | 近义词：clanging, resonant, metallic | 反义词：muted, soft, dull | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
cymbal (n.) + -like (suffix)
cymbal: from Old French cimbale, from Late Latin cymbalum, from Greek kymbalon "cymbal," from kymbe "bowl, cup"
-like: Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-likan "having the form or likeness of," from PIE root *-leikos, adjectival suffix.
