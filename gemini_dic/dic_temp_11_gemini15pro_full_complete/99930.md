interneural:/ˌɪntərˈnjʊərəl/
adjective
神经间的,神经元间的
Located or occurring between neurons.
The interneural connections are complex and not fully understood. 神经元间的连接很复杂，尚未完全被理解。
近义词: interneuronal
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


Etymology:
inter- (Latin, between) + neural (Greek, nerve)
