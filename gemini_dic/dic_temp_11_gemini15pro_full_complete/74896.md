freshman:/ˈfrɛʃmən/ (US) /ˈfrɛʃmən/ (UK)| n.|新生；一年级学生|A student in the first year of high school or college.|She's a freshman at Harvard. 她是一名哈佛大学的新生。|Synonym: newcomer, fresher (UK)|Antonym: senior, graduate|Noun

freshman:/ˈfrɛʃmən/ (US) /ˈfrɛʃmən/ (UK)| adj.|一年级的；新生的|Relating to or characteristic of a freshman.|The freshman dorm is across the street. 新生宿舍在街对面。|Synonym: first-year|Antonym: senior|Adjective
