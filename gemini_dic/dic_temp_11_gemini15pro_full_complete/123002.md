monarchies: /ˈmɒnəkɪz/ | n. | 君主制；君主国（monarchy的复数） | A system of government in which one person reigns, usually a king or queen.  The term can also refer to the countries ruled by a monarch. | Many European countries were monarchies until relatively recently. 许多欧洲国家直到最近还是君主制国家。|  The United Kingdom is one of the few remaining monarchies in Europe. 英国是欧洲少数几个仍然保留君主制的国家之一。| synonyms: kingdoms, empires, reigns | antonyms: republics, democracies | noun
