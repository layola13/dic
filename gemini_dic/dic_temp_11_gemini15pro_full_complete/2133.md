adaptively:/əˈdæptɪvli/ | adv. | 适应地，顺应地 | In a way that adjusts to changing circumstances or conditions. | The system adaptively learns from user interactions.  该系统通过用户交互进行自适应学习。|
adaptively:/əˈdæptɪvli/ | adv. | 适应地，顺应地 |  The software can adaptively change its settings based on the user's needs.  该软件可以根据用户需求自适应地更改其设置。|
adaptively:/əˈdæptɪvli/ | adv. | 适应地，顺应地 | The chameleon adaptively changes its skin color to match its surroundings. 变色龙会根据周围环境的变化来改变皮肤颜色。|近义词: flexibly, dynamically, responsively | 反义词: rigidly, inflexibly, statically | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From adapt (v.) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs). Adapt comes from Latin adaptare, meaning "to fit, adjust."
