anodizations:/ˌænədəˈzeɪʃənz/| n.| 阳极氧化处理; 阳极氧化(处理);|The anodizations of the aluminum parts improved their corrosion resistance.  铝制零件的阳极氧化处理提高了它们的耐腐蚀性。|例句：The quality of the anodizations was crucial for the product's longevity. 阳极氧化处理的质量对产品的寿命至关重要。

The anodizations on these components prevent them from rusting.  这些组件上的阳极氧化层防止它们生锈。|近义词：oxidation, surface treatment|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 coating removal 涂层去除)|词性:名词 (plural noun)

词源:  From anodize + -ation.  Anodize comes from anode (positive electrode) + -ize (verb-forming suffix). Ultimately from Greek anodos "way up".
