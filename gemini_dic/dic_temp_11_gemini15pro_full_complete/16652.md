barracking: /ˈbærəkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbærəkɪŋ/ (US)
n.|起哄，喝倒彩，加油|Loud cheering or shouting, typically to support a team or to express disapproval of an opponent.|The barracking from the home crowd was deafening.  主场观众的喝倒彩震耳欲聋。| The constant barracking from the opposition fans unnerved the players.  对方球迷不断的起哄让球员们感到不安。| cheering, jeering, rooting (US), supporting|criticism, disapproval|noun
