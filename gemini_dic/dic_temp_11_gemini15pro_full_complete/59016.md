dvr:/ˌdiː viː ˈɑːr/ (also /ˈdiː viː ɑːr/)| n.|数字视频录像机;硬盘录像机|A digital video recorder (DVR) is an electronic device that records video in a digital format to a disk drive, USB flash drive, SD memory card, or other local or networked mass storage device. |Example 1: I set the DVR to record my favorite show. 我设置了数字视频录像机来录制我最喜欢的节目。

Example 2:  The DVR has a large hard drive that can store many hours of recordings. 这台硬盘录像机有一个很大的硬盘，可以存储很多小时的录像。|近义词：recorder, PVR (Personal Video Recorder)|反义词: None|词性:noun
