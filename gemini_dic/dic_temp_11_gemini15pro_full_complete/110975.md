liquidates:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪts/
verb
清算，变卖; 清偿（债务）; 除掉，杀掉;
To settle the affairs of (a business) by determining the liabilities and applying the assets to their discharge. To convert (assets) into cash. To pay off (a debt). To kill or abolish.
The company was liquidated after it went bankrupt.  这家公司破产后被清算了。
The assassin was hired to liquidate the CEO.  刺客受雇暗杀CEO。
Synonyms: dissolve, close down, wind up, eliminate, kill, abolish
Antonyms: establish, found, create, build, preserve
词性:v.
