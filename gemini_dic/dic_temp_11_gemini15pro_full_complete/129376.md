nomadize:/ˈnoʊməˌdaɪz/| v. |游牧化，过游牧生活|To live as a nomad; to travel from place to place rather than living in one fixed location.|Many tribes in Mongolia still nomadize across the vast steppes. 蒙古的许多部落仍然在广阔的草原上过着游牧生活。|They nomadized through the desert for many years. 他们在沙漠中游牧了很多年。|近义词：migrate, wander, roam, rove|反义词: settle, settle down|词性:动词

Etymology:  From nomad + -ize.  "Nomad" ultimately comes from Greek *νομάς (nomas), meaning "roaming, wandering, pasturing," from νέμω (nemō), meaning "I distribute, allot, pasture, graze."
