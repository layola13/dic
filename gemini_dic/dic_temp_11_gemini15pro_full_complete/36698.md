collins:/ˈkɒlɪnz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlɪnz/ (US)| n.|柯林斯; 科林斯|A surname; also, a given name. It can refer to the publishing house HarperCollins, known for its dictionaries and other language resources.  It can also refer to Michael Collins, the Irish revolutionary leader.|例句：I looked up the word in my Collins dictionary. 我在我的柯林斯词典里查了这个词。

例句：Michael Collins played a key role in Irish independence. 迈克尔·柯林斯在爱尔兰独立中扮演了关键角色。|近义词：None (as a proper noun)|反义词:None (as a proper noun)|词性:Noun
