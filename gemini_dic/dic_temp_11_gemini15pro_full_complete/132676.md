nondispensations:/ˌnɒndɪspɛnˈseɪʃənz/ | n. |  不可豁免，不可免除 | The absence of dispensations; the inability to be excused or exempted from a rule, obligation, or requirement. |  There are no nondispensations for this rule, everyone must comply.  这项规则没有豁免，每个人都必须遵守。| synonyms: non-exemptions, non-exceptions | antonyms: dispensations, exemptions, exceptions | noun
