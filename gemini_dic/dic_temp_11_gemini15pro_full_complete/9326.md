antipopularization:/ˌæntiˌpɒpjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌæntiˌpɑːpjələraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
反普及，反推广
The act of discouraging the widespread adoption or understanding of something.
The antipopularization of complex scientific concepts often leads to public misunderstanding.  反普及复杂的科学概念常常导致公众的误解。
近义词:obscuration, suppression
反义词:popularization, promotion
词性:名词


词源:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposite of  反，相反
popularization (noun): the act of making something popular or easily understood  普及，推广
