intercreate:/ˌɪntərkriˈeɪt/
verb
互相创造；共同创造；交互创造
To create together or reciprocally; to bring into being through mutual or reciprocal action.
例句：The artists sought to intercreate a performance that blurred the lines between audience and performer. 艺术家们试图共同创造一个模糊观众和表演者界限的表演。

例句：Through dialogue and collaboration, the team intercreated a dynamic and innovative marketing campaign. 通过对话和合作，团队共同创造了一个充满活力和创新的营销活动。
近义词：co-create, collaborate, synergize
反义词：destroy, dismantle
词性：v.
