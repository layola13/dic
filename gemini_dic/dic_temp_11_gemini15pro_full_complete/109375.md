lenslike: /ˈlɛnzˌlaɪk/
adjective
透镜状的，像透镜的
Resembling a lens in shape or function.
The ice crystals formed a lenslike structure, magnifying the sunlight. 冰晶形成了透镜状的结构，放大了阳光。
The telescope uses a series of lenslike mirrors to gather light.  这架望远镜使用一系列透镜状的反射镜来收集光线。
近义词: lenticular, lens-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes unlike a lens, e.g., cubic, shapeless)
词性: adjective
