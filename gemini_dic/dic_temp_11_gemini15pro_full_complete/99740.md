intermaxillary: /ˌɪntərˈmæksɪləri/| adj.| 颌间的，上颌间的| Situated between the maxillary bones, specifically between the two halves of the maxilla (upper jawbone).| The intermaxillary suture is the junction between the two maxillary bones. 上颌骨间缝是两个上颌骨之间的连接处。| The cleft lip often involves the intermaxillary segment.  唇裂通常涉及颌间段。|近义词: mid-maxillary|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + maxillary.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Maxillary" refers to the maxilla, the upper jawbone.
