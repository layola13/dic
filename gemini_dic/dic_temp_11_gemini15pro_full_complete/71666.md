flatulence: /ˈflætʃʊləns/ (US) /ˈflætjʊləns/ (UK)| n. |胀气；肠胃气胀|The state of having excessive gas in the digestive tract. |He suffered from flatulence after eating too much spicy food. 他吃了太多辛辣食物后，胃胀不舒服。|Synonyms: gas, bloating, wind, flatus|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but one could consider "regularity" or "good digestion" as loosely opposite concepts) |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *flatulentia, from Latin flatulentus (“blowing, puffing”), from flatus (“a blowing, blast, puff of wind”), from flare (“to blow, puff”).
