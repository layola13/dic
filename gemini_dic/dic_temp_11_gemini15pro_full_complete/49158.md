dejeuner:/deʒøne/ (French pronunciation)| n.|午餐, 早午餐|A midday meal; lunch.  It comes directly from French, where it means "lunch" or "breakfast".  In English, it retains a somewhat formal or literary connotation, often suggesting a leisurely or elegant meal.|We had a delightful déjeuner at a charming bistro. 我们在一家迷人的小酒馆享用了美味的午餐。|近义词：lunch, midday meal|反义词:dinner, supper|Noun
