broke away:/broʊk əˈweɪ/
verb
脱离，逃脱，摆脱
The cyclist broke away from the peloton.  这位自行车手从主车群中突围而出。
A small group broke away from the main party and formed a new political party.  一小群人脱离了主要政党，组成了一个新的政党。
近义词：escape, flee, secede, depart
反义词：join, unite, merge
词性：verb

broke away:/broʊk əˈweɪ/
adjective
脱离的，分离的
a breakaway group  一个脱离的团体
The breakaway republic declared its independence.  脱离出去的共和国宣布独立。
近义词：independent, separate, secessionist
反义词：united, joined, merged
词性：adjective
