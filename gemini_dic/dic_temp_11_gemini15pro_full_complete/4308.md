aitchless:/ˈeɪtʃləs/
adjective
无H音的；不发H音的
lacking the sound of the letter “h”
She has an aitchless accent, pronouncing "hello" as "ello." 她说话不发H音，把“hello”发成“ello”。
近义词: non-rhotic (applied specifically to the sound /r/)
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

aitchless:/ˈeɪtʃləs/
adjective
(of a word) not beginning with the letter “h”
(指单词) 不以字母“h”开头
The word "on" is aitchless.  单词 "on" 不以h开头。
