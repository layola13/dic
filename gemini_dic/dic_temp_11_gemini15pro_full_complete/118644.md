messmate:/ˈmesmeɪt/| n. |伙伴，同伴，室友|A person who shares a mess, especially a soldier or sailor who shares meals and living quarters with another.|例句：He's been my messmate for the past three years. 他过去三年一直是我的室友。

例句：During the war, soldiers often developed close bonds with their messmates. 战争期间，士兵们经常与他们的同伴建立密切的联系。|近义词：companion, comrade, friend, buddy, pal, mate|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:名词

词源:  From mess (a group of people who regularly eat together) + mate (a companion or friend).  The word originated in the military, particularly the navy, where sailors ate together in messes.
