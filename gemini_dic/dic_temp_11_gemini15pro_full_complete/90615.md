humaner:/ˈhjuːmeɪnər/ | adj. | 更人道的，更仁慈的 | More humane; more compassionate. Showing more concern for the welfare of other beings, especially humans or animals. |  The prison implemented humaner methods of rehabilitation.  监狱实施了更人道的改造方法。|  The organization advocates for humaner treatment of farm animals.  该组织倡导更人道地对待农场动物。 | 近义词: more compassionate, more merciful, kinder, gentler | 反义词:  crueler, more brutal, less humane, harsher | 词性: 形容词

词源: From humane + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Latin *humanus* "human," from *homo* "man."
