babe-in-the-wood:/ˈbeɪb ɪn ðə wʊd/| n.|涉世未深的人；天真无邪的人|A naive or innocent person, easily tricked or deceived.  Someone who is inexperienced and trusting, like a baby abandoned in a forest.|She was a babe in the woods when she first moved to the big city, and she quickly learned that not everyone is trustworthy. 她刚搬到大城市时就像个涉世未深的孩子，很快她就明白了并非每个人都值得信任。

He's a babe in the woods when it comes to politics. 在政治方面，他是个天真无邪的人。|近义词：innocent, naive, gullible, ingenuous, unsuspecting|反义词:  worldly, sophisticated, experienced, shrewd, cunning|词性:noun
