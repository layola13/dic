gangue:/ˈɡæŋjuː/
n.
矿脉；脉石；尾矿；(喻)渣滓
The worthless rock in which valuable minerals are found.
The mine produces a lot of gangue. 这个矿产生大量的尾矿。

近义词:waste, tailings, debris, dross
反义词:ore

词性: 名词

词源: From French *gangue*, from Middle High German *gange* (“vein, stratum”).
