dumetose:/ˈdjuːmɪˌtoʊs/| adj.|灌木状的，多灌木的|Covered with bushes; bushy; shrubby.|例句：The dumetose hillside provided excellent cover for the rabbits.  灌木丛生的山坡为兔子提供了极好的掩护。

例句：The explorers struggled through the dense, dumetose vegetation. 探险者们艰难地穿过茂密的灌木丛。|近义词：bushy, shrubby, thicketed|反义词：sparse, clear, open|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dumetosus*, from dumetum ("thicket"), from dumus ("bush, bramble").
