brooches:/ˈbroʊtʃɪz/
n.
胸针，饰针
A small piece of jewelry with a pin at the back that is fastened to clothes
She wore a beautiful diamond brooch on her lapel. 她在外套翻领上佩戴了一枚漂亮的钻石胸针。

近义词: pins, clips, ornaments
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词(plural)

brooch:/broʊtʃ/
n.
胸针，饰针
A small piece of jewelry with a pin at the back that is fastened to clothes
She wore a beautiful diamond brooch on her lapel. 她在外套翻领上佩戴了一枚漂亮的钻石胸针。

近义词: pin, clip, ornament
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词(singular)


词源: From Old French broche "a spit, pointed tool," from Vulgar Latin *brocca, of unknown origin.  The meaning shifted from "pointed tool" to "ornamental clasp."
