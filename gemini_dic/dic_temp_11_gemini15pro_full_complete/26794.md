caffeinated:/ˈkæfɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
含咖啡因的；加了咖啡因的
Containing caffeine; stimulated with caffeine.
例句：I need a caffeinated beverage to wake up in the morning.  我早上需要一杯含咖啡因的饮料来提神。

例句：He felt more alert after drinking a caffeinated soda.  喝了含咖啡因的汽水后，他感觉更清醒了。

近义词：stimulated, energized
反义词：decaffeinated, caffeine-free
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From caffeine + -ated.  Caffeine ultimately derives from Arabic *qahwah*, meaning "coffee."
