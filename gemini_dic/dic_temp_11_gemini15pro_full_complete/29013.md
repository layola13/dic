cartograph: /ˈkɑːrtəɡræf/ | n. |地图;海图|A map or chart, especially one that is detailed and accurate. |例句：The cartograph showed the intricate network of rivers and streams. 这张地图展示了错综复杂的河流网络。

例句：Ancient cartographs often depicted mythical creatures and unexplored territories. 古代地图通常描绘神话生物和未探索的领土。|近义词：map, chart, plan |反义词: None |词性: noun

Etymology: From French *cartographe*, from Italian *cartografo*, from Late Latin *charta* (paper, map) + Greek *graphein* (to write).
