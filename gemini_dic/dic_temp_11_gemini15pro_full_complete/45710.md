curvature:/ˈkɜːrvətʃər/ /ˈkɜːrvəˌtʃʊr/| n. | 曲率，弯曲，弧度 |The degree to which a curve deviates from a straight line.  |The curvature of the lens affects the focus of the image.  镜头的曲率会影响图像的焦距。|Synonyms: bend, curve, arc, bow, camber |Antonyms: straightness, linearity |noun
