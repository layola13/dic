constitutionalist:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlɪst/ /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪst/| n.|宪政主义者；立宪主义者|A person who strongly supports or advocates for a constitution, especially adherence to its principles.|The constitutionalists argued against expanding executive power. 宪政主义者反对扩大行政权力。|Synonyms: constitutional advocate, adherent of the constitution|Antonyms: dictator, authoritarian, tyrant|noun


constitutionalist:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlɪst/ /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪst/| adj.|宪法的；拥护宪法的|Relating to or supporting a constitution, especially adherence to its principles.|The group held constitutionalist views on the separation of powers.  该团体对权力分立持有宪法主义的观点。|Synonyms: constitutional, charterist|Antonyms: unconstitutional, anti-constitutional|adjective
