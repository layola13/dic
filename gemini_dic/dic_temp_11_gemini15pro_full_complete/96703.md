ineffaceable:/ˌɪnɪˈfeɪsəbl/ | adj. | 无法消除的，抹不掉的 | Not able to be erased or forgotten; permanent. | The experience left an ineffaceable mark on his memory. 这段经历在他的记忆中留下了不可磨灭的印记。| synonyms: indelible, permanent, lasting, enduring | antonyms: temporary, fleeting, ephemeral, erasable | adjective

Etymology: From in- (not) + effaceable (capable of being effaced), from French effacer (to erase), from Late Latin exfaciare (to destroy the surface of), from ex- (out) + facies (face).
