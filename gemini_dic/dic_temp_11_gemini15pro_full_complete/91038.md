huskinesses:/ˈhʌskɪnɪsɪz/
n.
沙哑，嘶哑(复数)
a rough quality or tone of voice, often caused by a sore throat or emotion.
Her voice was filled with huskinesses from crying all night. 她哭了一整夜，声音充满了嘶哑。
synonyms: hoarseness, roughness, raspiness
antonyms: clarity, smoothness, mellowness
noun
