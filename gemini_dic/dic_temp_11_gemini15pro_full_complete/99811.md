intermigration:/ˌɪntərmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/ |n.|互相移居，相互迁移|The intermigration between rural and urban areas has significantly increased in recent years. 城乡之间的人口互相迁移近年来显著增加。|  Movement of people between two or more areas, often involving reciprocal exchange.|例句：The intermigration between the two countries has led to a blending of cultures.  两国之间的人口互相迁移导致了文化的融合。

例句：Intermigration within the European Union has become increasingly common. 欧盟内部的互相迁移变得越来越普遍。|近义词：migration, exchange, movement|反义词:stasis, stagnation|词性:名词
