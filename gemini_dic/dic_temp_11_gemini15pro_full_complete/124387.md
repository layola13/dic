motleys:/ˈmɒt.liz/ (UK), /ˈmɑːt.liz/ (US)| n. |杂色衣服；混杂的人群|A collection of different elements, often brightly colored and diverse; a heterogeneous group of people.|例句：The market was a motley of sights, sounds, and smells.  市场上充斥着各种景象、声音和气味。

例句：The traveling circus employed a motley crew of performers.  这个巡回马戏团雇佣了一群形形色色的表演者。|近义词：assortment, mixture, medley, variety, hodgepodge|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity|词性:名词


motley:/ˈmɒt.li/ (UK), /ˈmɑːt.li/ (US)| adj. |杂色的；混杂的|Exhibiting great diversity of elements; variegated in color.|例句：The jester wore a motley tunic.  小丑穿着一件杂色的外衣。

例句：A motley collection of furniture filled the antique shop.  古董店里摆满了各式各样的家具。|近义词：variegated, diverse, heterogeneous, multicolored|反义词：uniform, homogeneous, monochromatic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *motely, *medle, from Old French *meslé, past participle of mesler (“to mix”), from Vulgar Latin *misculāre, from Latin misceō (“I mix”).  Related to miscellaneous.
