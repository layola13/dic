confessing:/kənˈfɛsɪŋ/| v.|承认，坦白，忏悔|The act of admitting or acknowledging something, typically a fault, wrongdoing, or secret.|He was confessing his sins to the priest. 他在向神父忏悔他的罪过。|近义词：admitting, acknowledging, owning up, avowing|反义词：denying, concealing, hiding|词性：动词(present participle of confess)
Etymology: From Old French confesser, from Latin confessus, past participle of confiteri "to confess," from com- "together" + fateri "to acknowledge, confess."
