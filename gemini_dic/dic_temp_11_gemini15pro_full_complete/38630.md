concubinary:/kɒnˈkjuːbɪnəri/ /kɑːnˈkjuːbɪneri/
adjective
姘居的，同居的，非正式婚姻关系的
Relating to or involving a concubine; characteristic of a concubine or concubinage.  A relationship resembling marriage but not formally recognized.
He lived in a concubininary relationship with her for many years. 他和她姘居多年。
Synonyms:  cohabiting, unmarried, common-law, de facto
Antonyms:  married, legitimate, wedded
adjective
