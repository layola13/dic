half-farthing:/ˌhɑːf ˈfɑːrðɪŋ/
n.
半法寻 (一种旧英国货币，价值四分之一便士)
A half-farthing was a British coin worth one eighth of a penny.
The half-farthing was so small it was easily lost.  半法寻非常小，很容易丢失。
近义词: mite (少量)
反义词: fortune (一大笔钱)
词性: 名词

Etymology:
"half" + "farthing"
"half" from Old English "healf"
"farthing" from Old English "fēorþing," meaning "fourth part," referring to a quarter of a penny.
