flecklessly:/ˈflɛklɪsli/
Adverb
无斑点地，完美无瑕地
Without flecks or spots; flawlessly.
The newly polished car shone flecklessly in the sunlight.  新抛光的汽车在阳光下闪闪发光，完美无瑕。
Near Synonyms: immaculately, flawlessly, spotlessly, perfectly
Antonyms: spotted, stained, blemished, flawed
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fleckless + -ly.  Fleckless from fleck + -less. Fleck (noun) from Old Norse *flekkr.
