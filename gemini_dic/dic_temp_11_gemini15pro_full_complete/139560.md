obtuseness:/əbˈtuːsnəs/ /ɑːbˈtuːsnəs/
|n.|迟钝，愚钝；不尖，钝|The obtuseness of the student frustrated the teacher.  学生的迟钝使老师感到沮丧。|Lack of sharpness or quickness of perception or intellect; dullness.  缺乏敏锐的感知或智力；迟钝。|His obtuseness to social cues made him seem rude. 他对社交暗示的迟钝让他显得粗鲁。|dullness, insensitivity, stupidity|sharpness, acuity, intelligence|noun

obtuse:/əbˈtuːs/ /ɑːbˈtuːs/
|adj.|迟钝的，愚钝的；不尖的，钝的|He made an obtuse remark that offended everyone. 他说了一句愚蠢的话，冒犯了所有人。|Not quick or alert in perception, feeling, or intellect; not sensitive or observant; dull. 在感知、感觉或智力方面不敏捷；不敏感或不善于观察；迟钝。|The obtuse angle of the corner made it difficult to navigate. 这个角落的钝角使通行变得困难。|dull, insensitive, stupid|sharp, acute, intelligent|adjective
