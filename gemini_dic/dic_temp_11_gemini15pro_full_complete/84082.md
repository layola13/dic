half-indignantly:/ˌhɑːf ɪnˈdɪɡnəntli/
adverb
半愤慨地，略带愤怒地
Expressing mild indignation; a mixture of anger and annoyance, but not fully expressed.
"She half-indignantly tossed her hair and walked away." 她略带愤怒地甩了甩头发，走开了。
近义词: resentfully, slightly angrily, with mild indignation
反义词: calmly, placidly, approvingly
词性: adverb
