intimidated: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 害怕的，胆怯的，畏惧的 | Feeling frightened or nervous because you are not confident in a situation. |  I felt intimidated by all the successful people at the party. 我在派对上被所有那些成功人士吓到了。 | synonyms: frightened, nervous, scared, apprehensive, daunted | antonyms: confident, bold, fearless | adjective

intimidate: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪt/ | v. | 恐吓，威胁 | To frighten or threaten someone, usually in order to persuade them to do something that you want them to do. |  They tried to intimidate him into confessing. 他们试图恐吓他让他招供。| synonyms: frighten, threaten, menace, browbeat, bully | antonyms: encourage, support, reassure | verb
