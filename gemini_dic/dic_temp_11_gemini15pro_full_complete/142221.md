ornithopoda:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒpədə/ | n. |鸟脚亚目|A diverse group of herbivorous dinosaurs characterized by their "bird-like" feet and complex chewing mechanisms.  They ranged in size from small, bipedal runners to large, quadrupedal grazers.| Ornithopods like Iguanodon were common during the Cretaceous period.  禽龙这样的鸟脚类动物在白垩纪时期很常见。|近义词: dinosaur group, herbivore group |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider carnivorous dinosaur groups like Theropoda)| noun
