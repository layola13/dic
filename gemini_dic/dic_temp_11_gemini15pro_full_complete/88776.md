hodgepodge:/ˈhɑːdʒpɑːdʒ/ /ˈhɒdʒpɒdʒ/ | n. | 大杂烩；混合物 | A mixture of different things, often a confused or disorderly mixture. | The room was a hodgepodge of furniture from different eras. 房间里摆放着不同年代的家具，显得杂乱无章。| synonyms: mixture, medley, jumble, assortment, miscellany | antonyms: uniformity, consistency | noun

hodgepodge:/ˈhɑːdʒpɑːdʒ/ /ˈhɒdʒpɒdʒ/ | adj. (less common) | 杂乱的；混合的 |  Made up of a variety of things. |  The script was a hodgepodge collection of ideas, lacking a coherent theme.  这个剧本是各种想法的大杂烩，缺乏一个连贯的主题。 | synonyms: mixed, miscellaneous, assorted | antonyms: uniform, homogeneous | adjective
