frothily:/ˈfrɒθɪli/ (US) /ˈfrɔːθɪli/ (UK)| adv.|轻浮地，起泡地|In a light, bubbly, or frivolous manner; also, covered with or resembling froth.|She laughed frothily and tossed her hair. 她轻浮地笑着，甩了甩头发。|Synonyms: airily, frivolously, flippantly, skittishly|Antonyms: seriously, solemnly, gravely|Part of Speech: Adverb

Etymology:
From froth + -ily.  "Froth" comes from Old Norse *froða, from Proto-Germanic *frōþōną.
