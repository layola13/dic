agglomerator:/əˈɡlɒməreɪtə(r)/ /əˈɡlɑːməreɪtər/ | n. | 团聚机，烧结机，凝聚器 |A machine that causes small particles to gather into a mass. | The agglomerator combines fine iron ore particles into larger pellets.  烧结机将细小的铁矿石颗粒聚集成更大的球团。| synonym: aggregator, collector, compiler | antonym: separator, disperser | noun

agglomerators:/əˈɡlɒməreɪtəz/ /əˈɡlɑːməreɪtərz/ | n. | 团聚机（复数），烧结机（复数），凝聚器（复数） | Machines that cause small particles to gather into masses. | Several agglomerators are used in the factory to process the raw materials. 工厂里使用了几台凝聚器来处理原材料。| synonym: aggregators, collectors, compilers | antonym: separators, dispersers | noun
