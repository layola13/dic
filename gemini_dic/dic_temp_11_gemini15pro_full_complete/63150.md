ensue:/ɪnˈsuː/
verb
接着发生；因而产生
to happen after something else, especially as a result of it
例句：After the heavy rains, floods ensued.  暴雨过后，发生了洪水。

例句：The riot police clashed with the protestors, and chaos ensued. 防暴警察与抗议者发生冲突，随后一片混乱。
近义词：follow, result, arise, stem, proceed
反义词：precede, cause
词性:v.
