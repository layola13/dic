niggler:/ˈnɪɡlər/| n.|吹毛求疵的人；琐碎地争论的人；斤斤计较的人|A person who raises petty objections or finds trivial faults; someone who quibbles over minor details.|He's such a niggler, always finding something to complain about. 他真是个吹毛求疵的人，总是能找到抱怨的东西。|She's a niggler about grammar. 她对语法斤斤计较。|近义词：quibbler, nitpicker, caviler, faultfinder|反义词:easygoing person, tolerant person|词性:noun

niggle:/ˈnɪɡəl/| v.|吹毛求疵；琐碎地争论；斤斤计较|To raise petty objections or find trivial faults; to quibble over minor details.|He keeps niggling about the price. 他一直对价格斤斤计较。|It niggles me that she never apologizes. 她从不道歉，这让我很恼火。|近义词：quibble, nitpick, cavil|反义词: overlook, ignore|词性:verb
