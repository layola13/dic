foolship:/ˈfuːlʃɪp/| n.|愚蠢；愚蠢的行为；傻事|The act of being foolish; folly; a foolish act or idea.|His decision to invest all his money in one stock was an act of pure foolship. 他把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上，真是愚蠢至极。|近义词：folly, stupidity, silliness, foolishness|反义词：wisdom, prudence, sagacity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *folischipe*, equivalent to *fool* +‎ *-ship*.
