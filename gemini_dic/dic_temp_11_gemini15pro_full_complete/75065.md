frightfulnesses:/ˈfraɪtfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
可怕的事物；令人恐惧的性质或状态
The state or quality of being frightful; terribleness.
The frightfulnesses of war are well documented.  战争的恐怖之处都有详细的记录。
Near synonyms: horrors, terrors, atrocities
Opposite: pleasantness, beauty
noun

fright:/fraɪt/
n.
惊吓；恐怖
Sudden intense fear.
She got a terrible fright when the dog barked.  狗叫的时候她吓了一大跳。
Near synonyms: scare, shock, terror
Opposite: calmness, composure
noun

v.
使惊恐；吓唬
To fill with sudden and intense fear; terrify.
The loud noise frightened the children. 巨大的噪音吓坏了孩子们。
Near synonyms: scare, terrify, startle
Opposite: calm, soothe
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fryghtfulnes*, equivalent to frightful +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *frihtą (“fear”)
