morgay:/ˈmɔːrɡeɪ/ | n. |  morganatic marriage | A morganatic marriage is one between people of unequal social rank, in which the lower-ranking spouse and any children of the marriage do not inherit the higher-ranking spouse's title or possessions. |  He entered into a morgay with a commoner. 他与一位平民缔结了贵贱通婚。 | 近义词: left-handed marriage | 反义词: regular marriage | 词性: noun
