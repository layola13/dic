bedstaff:/ˈbɛdstæf/| n.|床棍，尿壶|A stick, often with a chamber pot at one end, kept by the bed in former times and used as a weapon or to summon help.|He grabbed the bedstaff and used it to fend off the intruder. 他抓起床棍用来抵御入侵者。|近义词:cudgel, staff|反义词: None|Noun
