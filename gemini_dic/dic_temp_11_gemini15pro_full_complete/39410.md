conman:/ˈkɒnmæn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnmæn/ (US)| n. |骗子；诈骗犯|A man who cheats or tricks people by gaining their trust and persuading them to believe something that is not true.|He posed as a financial advisor, but he was really just a conman. 他伪装成财务顾问，但实际上他只是一个骗子。|近义词：fraudster, swindler, cheat, trickster|反义词: honest person, philanthropist| noun
