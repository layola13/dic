balloons:/bəˈluːnz/
noun
气球
A small, inflatable rubber bag filled with air or gas, often used for decoration or entertainment.
The child clutched a bunch of colorful balloons.  孩子紧紧抓住一束五颜六色的气球。
synonyms: inflatables, blimps
antonyms: weights, anchors
词性: 名词(plural)

balloon:/bəˈluːn/
noun
气球
A small, inflatable rubber bag filled with air or gas.
He bought a balloon for his daughter. 他给女儿买了一个气球。
synonyms: inflatable, blimp
antonyms: weight, anchor
词性: 名词(singular)


balloon:/bəˈluːn/
verb
膨胀，激增
To swell or puff out; to increase rapidly.
The population of the city ballooned in the last decade.  这个城市的人口在过去十年里激增。
synonyms: expand, inflate, swell, surge
antonyms: shrink, contract, deflate, dwindle
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ballon* "large ball," from Italian *ballone* "large ball," augmentative of *balla* "ball," from Late Latin *balla*.
