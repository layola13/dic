generification:/ˌdʒɛnərɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 泛化，通用化 | The process of making something more general or applicable to a wider range of things.  It often refers to making something less specific or specialized. In computer science, it refers to the concept of writing code that can work with different data types without needing to be rewritten for each type. | The generification of the software allowed it to be used on various platforms.  该软件的泛化使其能够在各种平台上使用。 | generalization, abstraction | specialization, concretization | noun
