lapidescence:/ˌlæpɪˈdɛs(ə)ns/
n.
石化；化石作用
The process of turning into stone; petrification.
The slow lapidescence of the ancient tree eventually formed petrified wood.  这棵古树缓慢的石化最终形成了硅化木。
petrification, fossilization
softening, dissolution
noun
