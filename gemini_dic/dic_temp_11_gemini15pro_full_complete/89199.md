homesick:/ˈhoʊmsɪk/ (US) /ˈhəʊmsɪk/ (UK)| adj.|想家的，思乡的|Experiencing a longing for one's home.|例句：She felt homesick after being away for only a week. 她离开家才一个星期就想家了。

例句：He was so homesick that he decided to return home early. 他太想家了，所以决定提早回家。|近义词：nostalgic, lonesome|反义词:content, happy|词性:形容词

词源:
From home + sick (in the sense "having a longing for").  The word emerged in the late 17th century.  "Sick" in this context originally meant "filled with yearning or desire," a meaning now largely obsolete except in this compound.
