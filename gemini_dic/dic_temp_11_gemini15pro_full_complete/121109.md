miscultivated: /ˌmɪskʌltɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
未开垦的，教养不好的
Not cultivated; lacking education or refinement.
The miscultivated land lay fallow for years.  这片未开垦的土地荒芜了多年。
He displayed a miscultivated manner, interrupting others constantly. 他表现出一种缺乏教养的举止，不断打断别人说话。
近义词: uncultivated, unrefined, uneducated, crude, coarse
反义词: cultivated, refined, educated, cultured, polished
词性: adjective
词源: mis- (prefix meaning 'wrongly' or 'badly') + cultivated (from Latin cultivare, 'to till, cultivate')
