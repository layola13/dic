contributions:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃnz/ (UK), /ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃnz/ (US)
n.|贡献，捐款，捐赠|Something that is given or offered to help achieve a particular purpose. |例句：Her contributions to the project were invaluable. 她对这个项目的贡献是无价的。|例句：He made significant contributions to medical research. 他对医学研究做出了重大贡献。|近义词：donations, gifts, offerings, input|反义词：deductions, withdrawals|词性:名词

词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin contributio(n-), from the verb contribuere, from con- ‘together’ + tribuere ‘grant, bestow’.
