appay: /əˈpeɪ/ | vt. | 支付，偿还 | To satisfy or settle (a claim, debt, etc.); to make due return or recompense for (service, trouble, loss, etc.). |  He appays his debts regularly. 他定期偿还债务。

He appays his workers fairly for their labor. 他公平地支付工人工资。| 近义词: pay, compensate, reimburse, satisfy | 反义词: default, owe | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *apaier, *apayer, from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + paier ‘to pay’, from Latin pacare ‘to pacify, appease’, from pax, pac- ‘peace’.
