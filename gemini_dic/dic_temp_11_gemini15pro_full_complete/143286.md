outdrag:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡ/
verb
胜过；在变装比赛中胜过
to surpass or defeat someone in a drag competition or performance, typically by being more flamboyant or extravagant.
RuPaul outdragged all the other queens on the runway.  鲁保罗在T台上艳压群芳，胜过所有其他变装皇后。
She outdragged the competition with her stunning costume and impeccable lip-sync. 她以惊艳的服装和无可挑剔的唇语同步表演，在比赛中脱颖而出。
synonyms: outshine, surpass, eclipse, outperform
antonyms: lose to, be outdone by
