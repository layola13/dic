dens:/dɛnz/ | n. | 洞穴，窝 | A wild animal's den.  一个野生动物的窝。| The bear retreated to its den for the winter. 熊回到它的洞穴过冬。 | lair, burrow |  - | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēns* (genitive *dentis*), meaning "tooth".  The connection likely arises from the resemblance of a tooth's root to a cave or burrow.
