crapulent: /ˈkræpjələnt/ | adj. | 饮食过度引起的恶心或不适；暴饮暴食的 | Suffering from or marked by excessive drinking; given to gross intemperance. | After the all-you-can-eat buffet, I felt rather crapulent.  在自助餐后，我感到相当恶心。 | He woke up crapulent and regretful after the fraternity party. 他在兄弟会派对后醒来，感到恶心和后悔。 | 近义词: nauseated, sick, queasy, ill, indisposed | 反义词: temperate, sober, abstinent | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *crapulentus, from Latin crapula "intoxication," from Greek kraipale "hangover, headache after intoxication."
