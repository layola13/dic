disrobers:/dɪsˈroʊbərz/
n.
脱衣者，使人脱衣者
People who undress someone or cause someone to undress.
The disrobers in the play quickly and efficiently changed the actors' costumes between scenes.  剧中的脱衣者在场景之间快速有效地更换了演员的服装。
The king's disrobers prepared him for his nightly bath. 国王的脱衣者为他准备夜间沐浴。
Synonyms:  strippers, undressers
Antonyms: dressers, clothiers
noun
