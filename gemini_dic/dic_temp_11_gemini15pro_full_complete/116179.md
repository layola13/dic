masculinizing:/ˈmæskjʊləˌnaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
男性化的，使男性化的
Causing to have or develop more masculine characteristics.
Steroids can have a masculinizing effect on women. 类固醇会对女性产生男性化的影响。

近义词:virilizing,  emboldening
反义词:feminizing, softening
词性:adjective


masculinize:/ˈmæskjʊləˌnaɪz/
verb
使男性化，使更具男子气概
To cause someone or something to have or develop more masculine characteristics.
Social pressures can masculinize men's behavior. 社会压力会使男性的行为更加男性化。
近义词:virilize,  embolden
反义词:feminize, soften
词性:verb


Etymology: From French *masculinisant*, present participle of *masculinis*, from Latin masculinus (“masculine”).
词源：源自法语 *masculinisant*，*masculinis* 的现在分词，来自拉丁语 masculinus（“男性的”）。
