lair:/ler/ | n. | 藏身处，隐匿处 | A secret or hidden place where a person goes. | The police discovered the thief's lair. 警方发现了小偷的藏身处。| hideout, hideaway, den |  public place, open area | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lær "bed, resting place," from Proto-Germanic *laizaz "couch, bed" (source also of Old Norse legr "bed," Gothic ligrus "sickbed"). The sense of "animal's den" developed later.
