beak-like:/ˈbiːkˌlaɪk/
adjective
像喙的; 喙状的
Resembling a beak in shape or function.
The bird had a beak-like projection on its face.  这只鸟的脸上有一个像喙一样的突出物。
The platypus has a beak-like mouth.  鸭嘴兽的嘴巴像喙。
similar: beak-shaped, pointed, hooked
opposite: flat, rounded
Etymology: beak + -like
