apodous:/ˈeɪpədəs/| adj.|无足的，无脚的|Lacking feet or legs; footless.|例句：Apodous larvae are common in many insect species. 许多昆虫物种都有无足的幼虫。|例句：The apodous lizard slithered through the grass. 这只无足蜥蜴滑过草丛。|近义词：footless, legless|反义词：footed, legged|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Ancient Greek *ἄπους (ápous, “footless”) from *ἀ- (a-, “without”) + *πούς (poús, “foot”).
