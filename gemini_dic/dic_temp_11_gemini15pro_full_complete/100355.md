interstimulated:/ˌɪntərˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
互相刺激的；相互刺激的
stimulated between or among; mutually stimulated
例句：The researchers observed interstimulated neural activity in the brains of the test subjects. 研究人员观察到测试对象大脑中相互刺激的神经活动。

例句：Interstimulated muscle contractions were measured to assess the efficacy of the treatment.  测量相互刺激的肌肉收缩以评估治疗效果。

近义词：mutually stimulated, cross-stimulated, reciprocally stimulated
反义词：unstimulated, uninhibited, independent

词性: adjective
词源:  "inter-" (Latin, meaning "between, among") + "stimulated" (past participle of "stimulate," from Latin "stimulare," meaning "to goad, urge on").
