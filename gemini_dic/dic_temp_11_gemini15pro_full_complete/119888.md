militarises:/ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使军事化;军事化
To equip or organize (a country, area, etc.) for war, or to adopt military methods and characteristics
例句：The government is militarising the police force. 政府正在使警察部队军事化。

例句：The border region has become increasingly militarised. 边境地区日益军事化。

近义词：arm, fortify, strengthen
反义词：demilitarise, disarm
词性：verb
