nonolfactory:/ˌnɒnɒlˈfæktəri/ /ˌnɑːnɑlˈfæktəri/
adjective
非嗅觉的
Not related to the sense of smell; not perceived by the sense of smell.
The trigeminal nerve plays a role in nonolfactory sensations in the nose, such as the feeling of coolness from menthol.  三叉神经在鼻子的非嗅觉感觉中起作用，例如薄荷醇带来的凉爽感。

The study focused on the nonolfactory effects of certain airborne chemicals. 这项研究集中于某些空气传播的化学物质的非嗅觉效应。


近义词:non-odorous, scentless
反义词:olfactory

词性:adjective
