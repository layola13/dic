albigenses:/ælbɪˈdʒɛnsiːz/ | n. | 阿尔比派教徒 | The Albigensians were a religious group that flourished in southern France in the 12th and 13th centuries.  They held beliefs considered heretical by the Catholic Church. | The Albigensian Crusade was a brutal campaign against the Albigensians.  阿尔比十字军东征是一场残酷的针对阿尔比派教徒的运动。| Near synonyms: heretics, dissenters | Near antonyms: Catholics, orthodox believers | noun
