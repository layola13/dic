acrocephaly:/ˌækrəˈsɛfəli/
|n.|尖头畸形|A congenital condition in which the top of the skull is pointed or conical due to premature closure of the coronal suture.  The coronal suture is a fibrous joint that runs across the skull, separating the frontal bone from the parietal bones. Premature closure restricts the growth of the skull, forcing it to grow upwards and resulting in a pointed shape.|Acrocephaly can be associated with other craniofacial abnormalities.  尖头畸形可能与其他颅面畸形有关。

The baby was diagnosed with acrocephaly shortly after birth. 这个婴儿出生后不久就被诊断出患有尖头畸形。
|近义词:oxycephaly, turricephaly, tower skull|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

acrocephalic:/ˌækrəsəˈfælɪk/
|adj.|尖头的，尖头畸形的|Relating to or characterized by acrocephaly.  Having a pointed or conical shaped head due to premature fusion of the cranial sutures.|Acrocephalic babies may require surgery to correct the skull shape. 尖头畸形的婴儿可能需要手术来矫正颅骨形状。

The acrocephalic shape of the skull was evident in the X-ray.  X光片显示了颅骨的尖头形状。
|近义词:oxycephalic, turricephalic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
