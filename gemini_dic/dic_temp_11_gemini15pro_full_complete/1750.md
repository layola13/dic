acquire:/əˈkwaɪər/| v. |获得，取得，学到|To gain possession of something; to obtain something by one's own efforts or behavior.|She acquired a taste for fine wines during her time in France.  她在法国期间培养了对优质葡萄酒的品味。|近义词：obtain, gain, learn, earn|反义词:lose, forfeit, relinquish|词性:动词
词源: From Old French *aquerre (Modern French acquérir), from Latin acquirere "to get in addition, accumulate, acquire, gain, obtain," from ad- "to" + quaerere "to seek, gain, ask."
