depicts:/dɪˈpɪkts/
verb
描绘，描述
represents or shows something in a picture, story, movie, etc.
The painting depicts a scene from the Trojan War. 这幅画描绘了特洛伊战争的一个场景。
The novel depicts the lives of ordinary people in the 19th century. 这部小说描述了19世纪普通人的生活。
synonyms: portrays, represents, illustrates, describes
antonyms: misrepresents, distorts, obscures
