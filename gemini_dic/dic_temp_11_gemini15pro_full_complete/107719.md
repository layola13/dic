lanyard:/ˈlænjərd/ (US), /ˈlænɪəd/ (UK)| n.|挂绳，系索，吊带|A short rope or strap worn around the neck, wrist, or shoulder, typically to carry something such as a key or identification card.|例句：She wore a lanyard around her neck with her ID card. 她脖子上挂着一条挂绳，上面挂着她的身份证。

例句：The sailor secured the lanyard to the boat. 水手用系索把船系牢。|近义词：cord, strap, rope, tether|反义词: None (Lanyard doesn't have a direct opposite.)|词性:noun
