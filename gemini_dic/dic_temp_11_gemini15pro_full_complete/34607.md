cleaves:/kliːvz/ | v. | 劈开；粘附；坚持 |  To split or divide by or as if by a sharp blow; adhere strongly; cling. | The butcher cleaves the meat with a sharp knife. 肉贩用锋利的刀劈开肉。/ He cleaves to his principles. 他坚持自己的原则。 | 近义词：split, adhere, stick, cling | 反义词：join, separate, detach | 词性: 动词

cleaves:/kliːvz/ | v. | (cleave 的第三人称单数形式) |  Third-person singular simple present indicative form of cleave. | He cleaves the wood for the fire. 他劈柴生火。/  She cleaves to the traditional methods. 她坚持传统方法。| 近义词：splits, adheres, sticks, clings | 反义词：joins, separates, detaches | 词性: 动词


词源: From Old English *clēofan, from Proto-Germanic *kleubaną (“to cleave, split”), from Proto-Indo-European *gleubʰ- (“to stick, adhere, cleave”).
