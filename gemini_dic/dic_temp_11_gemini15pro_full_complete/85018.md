harass:/ˈhærəs/ /həˈræs/| vt. n.|骚扰，侵扰；反复袭击|To persistently annoy or bother someone, often to the point of distress or intimidation.  To subject to aggressive pressure or intimidation.|The manager was accused of harassing his employees.  经理被指控骚扰他的员工。|近义词：persecute, pester, badger, torment, hound|反义词：comfort, soothe, aid, assist|verb, noun

harassment:/ˈhærəsmənt/ /həˈræsmənt/| n.|骚扰，侵扰|Aggressive pressure or intimidation.|Workplace harassment is a serious issue.  职场骚扰是一个严重的问题。|近义词：persecution, pestering, badgering|反义词：comfort, solace|noun
