birthrights:/ˈbɜːθraɪts/ | n. |与生俱来的权利；天赋的权利| a right that you have from birth, especially your right to inherit your parents' property |  Everyone has a right to education, healthcare, and other basic birthrights. 每个人都有受教育、医疗保健和其他基本权利的权利。| synonyms:  prerogatives, entitlements, privileges | antonyms:  inabilities, restrictions, prohibitions | noun

birthright:/ˈbɜːθraɪt/ | n. |长子继承权| the right of the first-born child to inherit his or her parents' property | In many cultures, the birthright went to the eldest son. 在许多文化中，长子继承权属于长子。| synonyms:  inheritance, patrimony, legacy | antonyms:  disinheritance, forfeiture | noun
