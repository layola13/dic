heenan:/ˈhiːnən/ | n. | 恶棍，暴徒 | A ruffian; a thug.  A follower of the prize-fighter John C. Heenan. | John Morrissey defeated Heenan in a bare-knuckle fight. 約翰·莫里西在一場赤手拳擊賽中擊敗了希南。 | 近义词: ruffian, thug, hoodlum, villain | 反义词: hero, saint | 词性: noun

词源:  Derived from the name of John C. Heenan (1834-1873), an American prizefighter. His notoriety led to the use of his name as a generic term for a tough or violent person.
