outleapt:/ˌaʊtˈliːpt/ | v. |超越，跳得比...远| To jump further than (someone or something). |He outleapt his competitors in the long jump. 他在跳远比赛中跳得比他的竞争对手远。

The startled deer outleapt the fence. 受惊的鹿跳过了篱笆。

|近义词：surpass, exceed, outjump, outstrip|反义词: lag behind, fall short of |词性: 动词
