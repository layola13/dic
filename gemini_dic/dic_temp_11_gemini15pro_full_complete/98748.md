insusceptible:/ˌɪnsəˈsɛptəbl/
adjective
不受影响的；不敏感的；不易感染的
Not capable of being affected by something; not susceptible.
He seems insusceptible to colds, he never gets sick. 他似乎对感冒不敏感，从不生病。
The material is insusceptible to damage from water. 这种材料不易受水损害。

近义词: immune, resistant, impervious
反义词: susceptible, vulnerable, sensitive
词性: adjective
