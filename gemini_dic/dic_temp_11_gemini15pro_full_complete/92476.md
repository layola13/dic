hypotaxes:/ˈhaɪpəˌtæksiːz/
n.
下位结构，从属关系
A syntactic structure in which phrases or clauses are subordinated to others.  A sentence with multiple levels of subordination.
The long, complex sentence was a perfect example of hypotaxes, with multiple clauses embedded within each other.  这个冗长而复杂的句子是下位结构的完美例子，多个从句相互嵌套。
