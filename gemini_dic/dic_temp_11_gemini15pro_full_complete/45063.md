cubhood:/ˈkʌbhʊd/
n.
幼崽时期，幼兽时期
The period of being a cub; the state or time of being a young animal, especially a bear or lion.
The researchers observed the cubs throughout their cubhood to study their development. 研究人员在幼崽的整个幼年时期对它们进行观察，以研究它们的发育。
近义词：infancy, youth
反义词：adulthood, maturity
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cub + -hood.  "Cub" comes from Middle English *cubbe*, of uncertain origin. "-hood" is an Old English suffix denoting "state, condition, quality."
