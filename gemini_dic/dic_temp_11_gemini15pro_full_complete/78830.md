gimmicking:/ˈɡɪmɪkɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
耍花招；弄虚作假
using or employing a gimmick, often in a deceptive or manipulative way
The magician was gimmicking the cards. 魔术师在耍花招变牌。
Near Synonyms:  tricking, manipulating, cheating, deceiving, defrauding
Antonyms:  being honest, being straightforward, being truthful
verb

gimmick:/ˈɡɪmɪk/
noun
花招；噱头；小玩意儿
a trick or device intended to attract attention, publicity, or business
The free toy was just a marketing gimmick to get people to buy the cereal.  这个免费玩具只是一个营销噱头，为了让人们购买麦片。
Near Synonyms:  trick, device, scheme, ploy, stunt
Antonyms:  reality, truth, authenticity
noun

gimmicky:/ˈɡɪmɪki/
adjective
花哨的；噱头的；华而不实的
relying on or featuring gimmicks, often in a way that seems cheap or insincere
The movie's plot was gimmicky and predictable. 这部电影的情节很花哨，而且可以预见。
Near Synonyms:  contrived, artificial, cheap, tawdry, flashy
Antonyms:  genuine, authentic, sincere, substantial
adjective
