leakages:/ˈliːkɪdʒɪz/
n.
泄漏，漏损
The escape of something (liquid, gas, information, etc.) through a hole or crack.
The leakages in the pipes caused substantial water damage. 管道泄漏造成严重的水损。
synonyms: leaks, spills, escapes, losses
antonyms: containments, seals
