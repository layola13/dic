global:/ˈɡloʊbl/ (US) /ˈɡləʊbl/ (UK)| adj.|全球的，全世界的；总体的，全面的|Relating to the whole world; considering everything|例句：Climate change is a global issue. 气候变化是一个全球性问题。

例句：The company has a global reach. 这家公司的业务遍及全球。

近义词：worldwide, international, universal, overall|反义词：local, regional, national|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *globus, from Latin globus ("sphere, round mass, ball").  The adjective form developed in the mid-19th century.
