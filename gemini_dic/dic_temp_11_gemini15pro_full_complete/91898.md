hyperlinked:/ˌhaɪpərˈlɪŋkt/ | adj. | 超链接的 | Connected to another document or website by a hyperlink. |  Example: The website is hyperlinked to several other related sites.  该网站通过超链接连接到其他几个相关网站。

Example: Click on the hyperlinked word to access more information. 点击超链接的词语以访问更多信息。

| 近义词: linked, connected | 反义词: unlinked, disconnected | 词性: adjective

词源: "hyper-" (prefix meaning "over, beyond") + "link" (verb meaning "to connect").  The term gained popularity with the rise of the World Wide Web. 由前缀"hyper-"（意为“超过，超越”）和动词"link"（意为“连接”）组成。这个术语随着万维网的兴起而流行。
