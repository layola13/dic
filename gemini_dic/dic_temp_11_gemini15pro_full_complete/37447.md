commiserative:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪv/
adjective
表示怜悯的，同情的
feeling or expressing sympathy
例句：She gave him a commiserative pat on the shoulder. 她同情地拍了拍他的肩膀。

例句：He offered a few commiserative words. 他说了几句表示同情的话。

近义词：sympathetic, compassionate, empathetic
反义词：unsympathetic, indifferent, callous
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commiserativus, from Latin commiserari "to pity," from com- "with" + miserari "to pity," from miser "wretched."
