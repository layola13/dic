nontraction:/ˌnɒn.kənˈtræk.ʃən/ | n. | 非缩写形式 |  The formal essay required the use of nontractions, like "cannot" instead of "can't."  正式论文要求使用非缩略形式，例如使用“cannot”而不是“can't”。 | Example:  The teacher insisted on nontractions in formal writing. 老师坚持在正式写作中使用非缩略形式。  | 近义词: full form, expanded form | 反义词: contraction | 词性: noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The nontraction "do not" is preferred in formal writing.  在正式写作中，非缩写形式“do not”更受欢迎。
He does not understand the instructions. 他不明白这些指示。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is not working on the project right now. 她现在没有在做这个项目。
They are not attending the meeting today. 他们今天没有参加会议。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have not seen him since last week.  我从上周就没见过他了。
We have not finished the report yet. 我们还没有完成报告。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have not been sleeping well lately.  他们最近一直没有睡好。
He has not been attending classes regularly. 他没有经常上课。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would not do that. 如果我是你，我就不会那样做。
I wish I did not have to work tomorrow. 我希望我明天不用工作。

被动语态:
The decision was not made at the meeting.  这个决定不是在会议上做出的。
The problem is not being solved effectively. 这个问题没有得到有效解决。


疑问句:
Is it not a beautiful day?  今天难道不是个好天气吗？
Do you not understand? 你不明白吗？

否定句:
I do not like this movie. 我不喜欢这部电影。
She does not want to go. 她不想去。
