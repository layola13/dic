hoofiness:/ˈhuːfinɪs/
n.
蹄状, 有蹄的特征
The quality or state of being hoofy; resemblance to a hoof.
The farrier noted the hoofiness of the foal's legs.  蹄铁匠注意到小马驹腿部的蹄状特征。
近义词: ungulate, hoof-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "paw-like," "soft-footed")
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hoof + -iness.  "Hoof" comes from Old English "hōf," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*hōfaz."  "-iness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
