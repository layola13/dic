gavelkind:/ˈɡævəlkaɪnd/
n.
均分遗产制
The practice of dividing a deceased person's estate equally among all his or her sons.
In some cultures, gavelkind ensures all male heirs receive an equal share of the inheritance. 在某些文化中，均分遗产制确保所有男性继承人获得平等的遗产份额。
The ancient custom of gavelkind prevented the concentration of land ownership in the hands of a single heir.  古老的均分遗产制习俗防止了土地所有权集中在单个继承人手中。
Synonym: equal inheritance, partible inheritance
Antonym: primogeniture
