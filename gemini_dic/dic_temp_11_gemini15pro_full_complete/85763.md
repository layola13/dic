haya:/ˈhɑːjə/| n.|羞耻；谦逊；羞怯|A sense of modesty or shyness, particularly in Arab culture.|She showed haya by lowering her gaze. 她低下目光以示谦逊。|Synonyms: modesty, shyness, bashfulness, diffidence, humility|Antonyms: boldness, brazenness, impudence, arrogance|noun
