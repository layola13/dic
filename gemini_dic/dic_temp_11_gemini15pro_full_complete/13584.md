attentive:/əˈtɛntɪv/ | adj. | 专注的，细心的，周到的 | Paying close attention to something or someone; watchful. | She is an attentive student, always listening carefully to the teacher. 她是一个专注的学生，总是认真听老师讲课。 |  He was attentive to her needs, making sure she had everything she wanted. 他对她关怀备至，确保她拥有她想要的一切。 | 近义词: alert, observant, mindful, heedful, considerate | 反义词: inattentive, distracted, negligent, careless | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French attentif, -ive, from Latin attent- ‘heeded’, from the verb attendere (see attend).
