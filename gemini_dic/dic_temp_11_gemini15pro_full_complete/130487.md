nonaccedence:/ˌnɒnəkˈsiːdəns/
n.
不接受，不赞同
lack of acceptance or agreement; refusal to accept or agree.
His nonaccedence to the proposal caused a delay in the project. 他不接受这项提议导致项目延期。
近义词：rejection, refusal, declination, dissent
反义词：acceptance, agreement, approval, consent
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- + accede + -ence.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Accede comes from the Latin "accedere," meaning "to approach, to agree to."  The suffix -ence forms nouns from verbs.
