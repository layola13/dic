outspokenness:/ˌaʊtˈspoʊkənnəs/| n. |坦率，直言不讳|The quality of being outspoken; frankness or boldness in speech.|Her outspokenness about the company's policies surprised everyone. 她对公司政策的直言不讳让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: candor, frankness, directness, boldness, bluntness|Antonyms: reticence, reserve, shyness, taciturnity|noun

outspoken:/ˌaʊtˈspoʊkən/| adj. |坦率的，直言不讳的|Frank in stating one's opinions, especially if they are critical or controversial.|He was an outspoken critic of the government. 他是一位直言不讳的政府批评家。|Synonyms: frank, candid, direct, forthright, blunt|Antonyms: reticent, reserved, shy, inhibited|adjective
