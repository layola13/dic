epiclinal:/ˌɛpɪˈklaɪnəl/
adjective
上位斜的；顺斜的
Relating to or denoting an inclined geological stratum in which the angle of the dip decreases upwards.  The stratum slopes in the same general direction as the underlying rocks but is less steep.
The epiclinal structure of the region suggests a gentle uplift.  该地区的上位斜构造表明地势平缓抬升。
The geologist identified an epiclinal fold in the mountain range.  地质学家在山脉中发现了一个上位斜褶皱。
Near synonyms:  homoclinal,  conformable
Antonym:  anticlinal
Part of Speech: adjective
