completive:/kəmˈpliːtɪv/ | adj. | 完成的，完结的 | Serving to complete; making up a whole; fulfilling.  Bringing to an end; conclusive. | The signing of the treaty was a completive act, ending the war.  签署条约是一个完结的举动，结束了战争。| 近义词: final, concluding, terminating, finishing | 反义词: incomplete, unfinished, ongoing | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *complētīvus, from Latin complētus (past participle of complēre ‘fill up, complete’), from com- ‘together’ + plēre ‘fill’.
