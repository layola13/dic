all-American:/ˌɔːl əˈmerɪkən/
adj.|全美明星的；典型的美国人的|Relating to or characteristic of an ideal or typical American.
例句：He's an all-American boy. 他是一个典型的美国男孩。
例句：She has that all-American girl-next-door look. 她拥有那种典型的美国邻家女孩的外表。
近义词：typical, classic, representative
反义词：un-American, atypical
词性: 形容词

all-American:/ˌɔːl əˈmerɪkən/
n.|全美明星运动员；典型的美国人|A person who is considered to be a typical or ideal American, often a star athlete.
例句：He was named an all-American in football. 他被评为全美明星橄榄球运动员。
例句：She's the epitome of an all-American. 她就是典型的美国人的缩影。
近义词：star, champion, ideal
反义词：nobody, foreigner
词性: 名词

词源:  "all-" (meaning "completely" or "thoroughly") + "American".  The term originated in sports, referring to athletes selected for a national team.  It later broadened to describe people seen as embodying positive American qualities.
