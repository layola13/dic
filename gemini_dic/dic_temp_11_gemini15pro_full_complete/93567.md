imaginous:/ɪˈmædʒɪnəs/
adjective
想象的，虚构的
Existing only in the imagination; imaginary.
例句：The imaginous world of the novel captivated readers. 这部小说的虚构世界深深吸引了读者。

例句：She created an imaginous friend to cope with loneliness. 她创造了一个假想的朋友来应对孤独。

近义词：imaginary, fictional, fanciful, fabricated
反义词：real, actual, factual, true
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin imaginosus (“full of images, fanciful”), from Latin imago (“image, likeness”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ey- (“to go, to throw”).
