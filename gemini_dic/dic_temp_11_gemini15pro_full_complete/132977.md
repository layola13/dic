nonencyclopedic:/ˌnɒnɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. | 非百科全书式的；不适合百科全书的 | Not suitable for or characteristic of an encyclopedia; not objective or factual.  | This blog post is too personal and nonencyclopedic for Wikipedia. 这篇博客文章太个人化且不适合维基百科，因为它不客观。|  The information provided was highly subjective and therefore nonencyclopedic. 所提供的信息非常主观，因此不适合百科全书。 | 近义词: subjective, biased, informal, anecdotal | 反义词: encyclopedic, objective, factual, formal | 词性: adjective
