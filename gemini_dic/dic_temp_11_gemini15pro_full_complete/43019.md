cowboyed:/ˈkaʊbɔɪd/
verb
以牛仔的方式行事；粗暴地对待；
acted like a cowboy; dealt with roughly
The contractor cowboyed the job, leaving a mess behind.  承包商草草了事，留下一片狼藉。
近义词: bully, browbeat, intimidate, push around
反义词: coddle, pamper, assist, help
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From cowboy + -ed.  Meaning "to act like a cowboy" emerged in the late 19th century.
