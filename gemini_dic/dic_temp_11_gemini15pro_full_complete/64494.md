erode:/ɪˈroʊd/ (US) /ɪˈrəʊd/ (UK)| v. |侵蚀，腐蚀|To gradually wear away or destroy something by natural forces (such as water, wind, or ice) or by chemical action.|The coastline is slowly being eroded by the sea. 海岸线正慢慢地被海水侵蚀。|The foundations of the building were eroded by years of flooding.  建筑物的地基由于多年的洪水侵蚀。|近义词：wear away, corrode, abrade|反义词：build up, strengthen, deposit|词性:动词
