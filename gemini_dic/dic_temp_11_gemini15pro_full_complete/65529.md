eutectic:/juːˈtɛktɪk/
adjective|低共熔的，共晶的|Relating to or denoting a mixture of substances (in fixed proportions) that melts and solidifies at a single temperature that is lower than the melting points of the separate constituents or of any other mixture of them.|The alloy is eutectic.  这种合金是低共熔合金的。|
eutectic:/juːˈtɛktɪk/
noun|低共熔物，共晶体|A mixture of substances (in fixed proportions) that melts and solidifies at a single temperature that is lower than the melting points of the separate constituents or of any other mixture of them.|Solder is a eutectic mixture of tin and lead.  焊料是锡和铅的低共熔混合物。|synonym: eutectoid|antonym: N/A
