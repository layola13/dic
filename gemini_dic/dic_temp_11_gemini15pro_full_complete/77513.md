gazpacho:/ɡəˈspɑːtʃoʊ/ (US), /ɡəˈspætʃoʊ/ (UK)| n. |西班牙冷汤|A cold Spanish soup made of raw blended vegetables.|We had a refreshing gazpacho for lunch on the patio. 我们在露台上享用了清爽的西班牙冷汤。|例句：The gazpacho was a welcome relief on a hot summer day.  在炎热的夏天，西班牙冷汤是一种受欢迎的解暑佳肴。|近义词：cold soup, chilled soup|反义词:hot soup|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish gazpacho, probably ultimately from Mozarabic Arabic *caspáčo, from Late Latin *caspācium "fragments, leftovers," from Latin caspa "chaff, fragments."  The original dish likely consisted of stale bread soaked in water with vinegar and oil.
