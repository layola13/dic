journeyers:/ˈdʒɜːrniərz/
n.
旅行者；旅人
People who travel, especially over long distances.
The weary journeyers finally reached their destination.  疲惫的旅行者们终于到达了目的地。

The journeyers shared stories around the campfire. 旅行者们围着篝火分享故事。

近义词: travelers, voyagers, pilgrims
反义词: residents, locals, stay-at-homes
词性: noun

词源:
From journey + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting persons performing an action or associated with a thing).  Ultimately from Old French *journee "a day's work, a day's travel," from jour "day."
