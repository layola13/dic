hdr:/ˈeɪtʃˌdiːˈɑːr/|abbr.|高动态范围（High Dynamic Range）|A technique used in imaging and photography to reproduce a greater dynamic range of luminosity than is possible with standard digital imaging or photographic techniques.|The photographer used HDR to capture the full range of light in the scene. 摄影师使用HDR来捕捉场景中的全部光线范围。|近义词：Wide Dynamic Range (WDR)|反义词：Standard Dynamic Range (SDR)|abbreviation
