cocosette:/ˌkoʊkəˈsɛt/| n. |椰丝巧克力卷; 椰蓉巧克力棒|A sweet treat consisting of shredded coconut covered in chocolate.  A type of candy bar popular in several Latin American countries and Spain.|I brought back a box of cocosettes from my trip to Venezuela. 我从委内瑞拉旅行带回了一盒椰丝巧克力卷。|Synonyms: coconut bar, chocolate coconut candy, bounty (similar but not identical)|Antonyms: N/A (It's a type of food, so direct antonyms don't really apply.)|noun
