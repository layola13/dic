auriform:/ˈɔːrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
耳形的，耳状的
Shaped like an ear.
The plant had auriform leaves.  这种植物有耳形的叶子。
近义词: ear-shaped
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *aurifōrmis, from auris (“ear”) + -fōrmis (“-formed”).
