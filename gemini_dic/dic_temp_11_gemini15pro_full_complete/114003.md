ETYMOLOGY->
From "magistrate" + "-ship".  "Magistrate" comes from Old French "magistrat," and ultimately from Latin "magistratus" (office of a magistrate), from "magister" (master, chief). The suffix "-ship" forms abstract nouns denoting state, condition, office, skill, etc.
