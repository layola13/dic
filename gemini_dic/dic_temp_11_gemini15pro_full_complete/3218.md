affectionately:/əˈfekʃənətli/
adv.
深情地，亲切地
in a way that shows love and care
She looked at him affectionately. 她深情地望着他。
近义词：lovingly, tenderly, warmly
反义词：coldly, indifferently, harshly
词性：副词
