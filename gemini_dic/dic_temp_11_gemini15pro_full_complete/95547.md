incogitance:/ɪnˈkɒdʒɪtəns/ /ɪnˈkɑːɡɪtəns/| n. |欠考虑；轻率；无思想|Lack of thought; thoughtlessness.  The state of not thinking; lack of awareness or attention.|He acted in a moment of incogitance and later regretted it. 他一时欠考虑，后来后悔了。|Synonyms: thoughtlessness, heedlessness, carelessness, rashness, recklessness, imprudence|Antonyms: thoughtfulness, care, prudence, circumspection|noun

incogitant:/ɪnˈkɒdʒɪtənt/ /ɪnˈkɑːɡɪtənt/| adj. |欠考虑的；轻率的；无思想的|Not thinking; thoughtless; inconsiderate.|Her incogitant remark offended several people. 她欠考虑的话冒犯了几个人。|Synonyms: thoughtless, heedless, careless, rash, reckless, imprudent|Antonyms: thoughtful, careful, prudent, circumspect|adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *incogitantia, from in- "not" + cogitare "to think".
