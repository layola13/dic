overorganize:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪz/ | v. | 过度组织；过分安排 | To organize something excessively, often to the point of inefficiency or inflexibility. |  She overorganized the trip, creating a minute-by-minute itinerary that left no room for spontaneity. 她把旅行安排得过于细致，制定了一个精确到分钟的行程，完全没有留下任何即兴发挥的空间。 |  He overorganized his closet, categorizing his clothes by color, season, and fabric. 他把衣橱整理得过于井井有条，按颜色、季节和面料对衣服进行分类。 |  micromanage, overmanage | underorganize | verb
