ophiuroidea:/ˌɒfiʊˈrɔɪdiə/| n. |蛇尾纲|A class of marine echinoderms with a central disc and five radiating arms, commonly known as brittle stars or basket stars. They are closely related to starfish.|例句：Ophiuroidea are found in all oceans, from the intertidal zone to the abyssal depths. 蛇尾纲动物存在于所有海洋中，从潮间带到深海区。

例句：The ophiuroidea have a remarkable ability to regenerate lost arms. 蛇尾纲动物具有非凡的再生失去手臂的能力。|近义词：brittle star, basket star|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
