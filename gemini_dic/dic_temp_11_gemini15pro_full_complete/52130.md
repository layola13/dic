dianthus:/daɪˈænθəs/ | n. | 石竹花；石竹属植物 | A flowering plant of the genus Dianthus, native to Europe and Asia, with fringed or pink flowers. |  Dianthus flowers are popular for their vibrant colors. 石竹花因其鲜艳的色彩而广受欢迎。

Dianthus barbatus is also known as Sweet William. 石竹又名须苞石竹或美国石竹。 | 近义词：pink, carnation | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun
