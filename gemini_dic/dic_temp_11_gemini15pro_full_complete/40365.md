contracts:/ˈkɒntrækts/ (British) /ˈkɑːntrækts/ (American)| n. v.|合同; 契约; 收缩; 感染|A legally binding agreement between two or more parties.|

例句：
The company signed several contracts with overseas suppliers.
这家公司与海外供应商签订了几份合同。

The muscles in his arm contracted.
他手臂上的肌肉收缩了。

近义词：agreements, pacts, deals, compacts, shrinks, reduces, shortens|
反义词：expands, lengthens, grows|
词性：noun (plural), verb (third person singular present)|
