antimedication:/ˌæntiˌmedɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.| 反药物治疗；药物反对|Opposition to the use of medication, especially conventional medicine. |Some people believe in antimedication, preferring natural remedies.  有些人相信反药物治疗，更喜欢自然疗法。|Synonyms: antipharmacy, drug opposition|Antonyms: pro-medication, pharmacophilia|noun


antimedication:/ˌæntiˌmedɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.| 反药物情绪；抗药性|A feeling of opposition or resistance towards taking medication. |His antimedication made it difficult for doctors to treat him. 他的抗药情绪使医生很难治疗他。|Synonyms: drug aversion, medication resistance|Antonyms: drug compliance, medication adherence|noun
