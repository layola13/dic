exuberance:/ɪɡˈzjuːbərəns/| n.|丰富;茂盛;热情洋溢;兴高采烈|The exuberance of the vegetation in the tropics is overwhelming.  热带地区的植被茂盛得令人叹为观止。|近义词：enthusiasm, ebullience, lavishness, profusion|反义词：apathy, dullness, lifelessness|词性:名词

exuberant:/ɪɡˈzjuːbərənt/| adj.|丰富的;茂盛的;热情洋溢的;兴高采烈的|Her exuberant personality filled the room. 她热情洋溢的个性感染了整个房间。|近义词：enthusiastic, ebullient, lively, lavish, profuse|反义词：apathetic, dull, lifeless, restrained|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exuberantia, from Latin exuberans, present participle of exuberare (“to be abundant, overflow”), from ex (“out of, from”) + uber (“fertile, abundant”).
