nonchalance:/ˌnɒnʃəˈlɑːns/ (US) /ˌnɒnʃəˈlɑːns/ (UK)| n.| 冷淡；漠不关心；若无其事|the state of being nonchalant; a casual lack of concern|He reacted to the news with surprising nonchalance. 他对这消息的反应出奇地冷淡。|Synonyms: indifference, apathy, coolness, composure, calmness|Antonyms: anxiety, eagerness, excitement, nervousness|noun

nonchalant:/ˌnɒnʃəˈlɑːnt/ (US) /ˌnɒnʃəˈlɑːnt/ (UK)| adj.| 冷淡的；漠不关心的；若无其事的|feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety, interest, or enthusiasm|She gave a nonchalant shrug. 她漫不经心地耸了耸肩。|Synonyms: indifferent, apathetic, cool, composed, calm|Antonyms: anxious, eager, excited, nervous|adjective
