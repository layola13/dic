mentors:/ˈmɛntɔːrz/ (US), /ˈmɛntɔːz/ (UK)| n.| 导师，指导者 | A mentor is an experienced and trusted advisor. |  Experienced engineers mentored the interns. 经验丰富的工程师指导实习生。| synonyms: advisors, guides, tutors, coaches | antonyms:  protégés, mentees | noun

mentors:/ˈmɛntɔːrz/ (US), /ˈmɛntɔːz/ (UK)| v.| 指导，做…的良师 | To mentor someone is to guide and advise them. | She mentored many young women in the field of technology.  她在科技领域指导了许多年轻女性。| synonyms: advise, guide, tutor, coach | antonyms: learn from, follow | verb
