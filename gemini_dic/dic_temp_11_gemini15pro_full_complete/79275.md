glaucescent:/ɡlɔːˈsɛsənt/| adj.| 稍带白霜的，微绿色的|Covered with a pale bloom; slightly glaucous.  A glaucous surface is often smooth and waxy, and the bloom can be rubbed off.|The glacescent leaves of the plant shimmered in the sunlight. 这种植物略带白霜的叶子在阳光下闪闪发光。|Synonyms: glaucous, frosted, powdery|Antonyms: shiny, glossy|adjective
