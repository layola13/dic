Jamestowns refers to places named Jamestown, most commonly the first permanent English settlement in North America, located in Virginia.
Jamestown, Virginia, was founded in 1607.  詹姆斯敦，弗吉尼亚，建立于1607年。
The historic site of Jamestowns offers a glimpse into colonial life.  詹姆斯敦的历史遗址让人得以一窥殖民时期的生活。
