geosphere:/ˈdʒiːəsfɪə(r)/
n.
地球的岩石圈；固体地球
The solid portion of the Earth (distinguished from atmosphere, hydrosphere, and biosphere).
The geosphere is constantly changing due to various geological processes. 地球的岩石圈由于各种地质作用而不断变化。
Volcanoes are part of the geosphere. 火山是地球岩石圈的一部分。
lithosphere, Earth's crust, solid Earth
atmosphere, hydrosphere, biosphere
noun
