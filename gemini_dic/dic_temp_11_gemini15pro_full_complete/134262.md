noninflation:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfleɪʃən/ | n. | 非通货膨胀 |  The absence of inflation, a state where the general price level of goods and services is stable or even declining. |  The government's policies aimed at achieving noninflation were successful. 政府旨在实现非通货膨胀的政策是成功的。 | Synonyms: price stability, deflation | Antonyms: inflation, hyperinflation | noun

noninflationary:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 非通货膨胀的 | Not causing or characterized by inflation.  | The central bank implemented noninflationary monetary policies. 中央银行实施了非通货膨胀的货币政策。| Synonyms: stable, deflationary | Antonyms: inflationary | adjective
