aculeiform:/əˈkjuːliɪfɔːrm/
adjective
针形的，刺状的
Shaped like a needle or thorn; pointed.
The aculeiform leaves of the yucca plant can be quite sharp.  丝兰植物的针形叶子非常锋利。
近义词:needle-shaped, acicular, spiniform
反义词:obtuse, blunt
词性:adjective
