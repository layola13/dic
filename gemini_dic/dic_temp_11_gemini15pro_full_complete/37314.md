commeasure:/kəˈmɛʒər/| v.|（使）相称，相等，相当|To make proportionate; to adjust to a suitable measure or standard.|例句：Salaries should be commensurate with skills and experience. 薪水应与技能和经验相称。

例句：The punishment should be commensurate with the crime.  惩罚应该与罪行相称。|近义词：proportion, match, equal, correspond|反义词：disproportionate, unequal, mismatch|词性：动词

commensuration:/kəˌmɛnʃəˈreɪʃən/| n.|相称，相等，相当|The act of making proportionate or corresponding.|例句：The commensuration of salaries and performance is important for employee morale. 薪酬与绩效的相称对于员工士气至关重要。

例句：The principle of commensuration applies to rewards as well as punishments. 相称原则适用于奖励和惩罚。|近义词：proportion, balance, equivalence|反义词：disproportion, imbalance, inequality|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commensurare, from Latin com- "together" + mensurare "to measure," from mensura "measure."
