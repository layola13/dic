cheekier:/ˈtʃiːkiər/| adj.|更厚颜无耻的，更放肆的|Comparative form of cheeky: more cheeky.  Showing a lack of respect or politeness in a way that is amusing or appealing.|He gave her a cheekier grin than usual. 他给了她一个比平时更厚颜无耻的笑容。|Synonyms: bolder, impudent, saucier, impertinent, disrespectful|Antonyms: respectful, polite, reserved, shy, timid|adjective
