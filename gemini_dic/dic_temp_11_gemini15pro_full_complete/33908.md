circumventive:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛntɪv/ | adj. | 迂回的，规避的 | Designed to avoid or bypass something, especially by using clever or indirect methods. |  He took a circumventive route to avoid the traffic jam. 他绕道行驶以避开交通堵塞。| The lawyer used a circumventive strategy to get his client acquitted. 律师使用迂回策略使其客户被判无罪。 | 近义词: evasive, indirect, roundabout, devious | 反义词: direct, straightforward | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin circumventus, past participle of circumvenire (“to surround, encompass, entrap”), from circum (“around”) + venire (“to come”).
