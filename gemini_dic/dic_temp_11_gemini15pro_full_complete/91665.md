hypercellularity:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛljʊˈlærɪti/
n.
细胞过多症，细胞增多
An excessive number of cells in a tissue or organ.
The biopsy showed marked hypercellularity of the bone marrow.  活检显示骨髓细胞明显增多。

Hypercellularity is a hallmark of this disease. 细胞增多是这种疾病的特征。
近义词: hyperplasia, proliferation
反义词: hypocellularity, hypoplasia
词性: noun


词源:
hyper- (prefix meaning "excessive, above") + cellular (relating to cells) + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)
