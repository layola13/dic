barrage:/bəˈrɑːʒ/ /bəˈrɑːʒ/
noun
阻塞，拦河坝；弹幕；堰
a structure built across a river to control the flow of water
例句：The barrage protects the city from flooding. 这座拦河坝保护城市免受洪水侵袭。

例句：The Three Gorges Dam is the world's largest power station and barrage. 三峡大坝是世界上最大的水电站和拦河坝。

近义词：dam, barrier, obstacle
反义词：opening, passage
词性：n.

barrage:/bəˈrɑːʒ/ /bəˈrɑːʒ/
noun
弹幕；齐射式攻击
a rapid, large-scale outpouring of something
例句：The celebrity faced a barrage of questions from reporters.  这位名人面临记者连珠炮般的提问。

例句：He received a barrage of criticism for his controversial remarks. 他因其有争议的言论而受到猛烈抨击。

近义词：bombardment, volley, onslaught
反义词：trickle, dribble
词性：n.


barrage:/bəˈrɑːʒ/ /bəˈrɑːʒ/
verb
以密集火力攻击；（用言语）攻击
to bombard someone with something
例句：The troops were barraged with enemy fire. 部队遭到敌人的猛烈炮火攻击。

例句：She was barraged with complaints from angry customers. 她被愤怒的顾客的投诉淹没。

近义词：bombard, pepper, inundate
反义词：protect, shield
词性：v.
