lady:/ˈleɪdi/ (US) /ˈleɪdi/ (UK)| n.|女士，夫人，小姐，贵妇人|A polite term for a woman; a woman of high social position; a woman who behaves in a polite and refined way.|例句：The lady in red is my mother. 穿红色衣服的那位女士是我母亲。

例句：She is a true lady. 她是一位真正的淑女。


近义词：woman, female, gentlewoman|反义词:gentleman, man|词性:noun
