hepatization:/ˌhɛpətɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌhepətɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|肝化|The process by which lung tissue becomes liver-like in consistency, usually as a result of pneumonia.  It refers to the solidification of the lung tissue, making it resemble the liver in texture.|The doctor diagnosed hepatization of the lung due to severe pneumonia. 医生诊断出由于严重的肺炎导致的肺肝化。|consolidation, solidification|resolution, aeration|noun
