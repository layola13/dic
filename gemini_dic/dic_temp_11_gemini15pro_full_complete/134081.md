nonillustrative:/ˌnɒnɪˈlʌstrətɪv/ | adj. | 非例证性的，不具说明性的 | Not serving as an example or explanation; not illustrative. |  This nonillustrative example doesn't help clarify the concept. 这个不具说明性的例子无助于阐明这个概念。 | The presentation included nonillustrative slides with only bullet points. 演示文稿中包含的幻灯片只有要点，没有插图，不具说明性。 | 近义词: unexemplifying, nonexemplary, unrepresentative | 反义词: illustrative, exemplary, representative | 词性: adjective

词源:  non- (not) + illustrative (serving to illustrate)
