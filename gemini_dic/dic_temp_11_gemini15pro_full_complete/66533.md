exhaustive:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪv/| adj.|详尽的，彻底的，全面的|Leaving no aspect unexamined; thorough.  Fully comprehensive.|例句：The police conducted an exhaustive search of the area. 警方对该地区进行了彻底搜查。|例句：She made an exhaustive list of all the possible outcomes. 她列出了所有可能结果的详尽清单。|近义词：thorough, complete, comprehensive, in-depth|反义词：incomplete, partial, superficial|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exhaustīvus, from Latin exhaustus (past participle of exhaurire ‘to drain out, empty’), from ex- ‘out’ + haurire ‘to draw’.
