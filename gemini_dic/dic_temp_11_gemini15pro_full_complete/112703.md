low-quality:/ˌloʊ ˈkwɑːləti/ |adj.|低质量的；劣质的|of poor quality; not up to acceptable standards|例句：The low-quality image was pixelated and blurry.  低质量的图像像素化且模糊。

例句：They sell low-quality goods at inflated prices. 他们以虚高的价格出售劣质商品。

|近义词：inferior, poor, shoddy, substandard, second-rate, cheap|反义词：high-quality, superior, excellent, premium, top-notch|词性: 形容词
