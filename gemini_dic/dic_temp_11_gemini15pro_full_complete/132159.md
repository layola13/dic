noncuriousness:/ˌnɒnˈkjʊəriəsnəs/ | n. |缺乏好奇心，漠不关心|Lack of curiosity or interest; indifference.|He showed a surprising noncuriousness about the world around him. 他对周围的世界表现出令人惊讶的漠不关心。|She displayed a marked noncuriousness towards the news. 她对这消息表现出明显的漠不关心。|近义词：indifference, apathy, lack of interest|反义词：curiosity, inquisitiveness, interest|词性:名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + curiousness (noun form of curious)
