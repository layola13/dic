chlorotic:/kləˈrɒtɪk/ /klɔːˈrɑːtɪk/
adjective
萎黄的，褪绿的
(of a plant or plant part) pale yellow or white as a result of chlorophyll deficiency.
The chlorotic leaves of the plant indicated a nutrient deficiency.  这种植物萎黄的叶子表明它缺乏营养。
The prolonged drought left the crops chlorotic and stunted.  长期的干旱使农作物变得萎黄且发育不良。

近义词:pale, yellowed, blanched, etiolated
反义词:green, healthy, vibrant
词性:adjective
