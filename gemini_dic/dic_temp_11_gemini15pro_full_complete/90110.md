hot-desking:/ˌhɒt ˈdɛskɪŋ/ /ˌhɑːt ˈdɛskɪŋ/ | n. | 办公桌轮用；流动办公；共享办公 | The practice of having a pool of desks available to be used by any employee on a rotating system, rather than giving each employee their own designated desk. |  Our company has recently introduced hot-desking to save office space.  我们公司最近引入了办公桌轮用制度以节省办公空间。| 近义词：flexible working, agile working | 反义词: fixed desks, assigned seating | 词性: noun
