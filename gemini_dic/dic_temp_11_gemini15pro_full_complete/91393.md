hydrotrope:/ˈhaɪdrəˌtroʊp/| n. |增溶剂，水溶助长剂|A compound that increases the solubility in water of another substance, which is normally insoluble or slightly soluble.|Sodium xylene sulfonate is a common hydrotrope used in detergent formulations.  二甲苯磺酸钠是一种常用的增溶剂，用于洗涤剂配方中。

Hydrotropes can enhance the solubility of perfumes in water-based products. 增溶剂可以提高香水在水基产品中的溶解度。|近义词：solubilizer, cosolvent|反义词:precipitant|词性:noun
