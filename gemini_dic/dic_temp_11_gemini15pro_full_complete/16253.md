banteringly:/ˈbæntərɪŋli/
adverb
戏谑地，开玩笑地
In a playful, teasing, or joking manner.
He banteringly suggested that she try wearing a clown suit to the interview. 他开玩笑地建议她面试时穿小丑服。
She banteringly accused him of stealing the last cookie. 她开玩笑地指责他偷吃了最后一块饼干。
Synonyms: playfully, teasingly, jokingly, humorously
Antonyms: seriously, sternly, gravely, solemnly
词性: 副词
