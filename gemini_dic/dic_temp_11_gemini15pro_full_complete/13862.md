augmentatively: /ɔːɡˌmɛntətɪvli/
| adv. |增强地，扩大地|In a way that augments; so as to increase or enlarge.  It describes an action or process that is done in a way that increases or enhances something.|He spoke augmentatively about the company's future prospects. 他夸大地谈论了公司未来的前景。|近义词: increasingly, expansively, enhancingly |反义词: decreasingly, reductively, diminishingly|词性: 副词


词源:
From augmentative + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin augmentare.
