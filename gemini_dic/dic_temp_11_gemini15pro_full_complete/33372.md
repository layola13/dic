chunji:/ˈtʃʌndʒiː/| n.|春季,春天 (Specifically referring to the first month of spring in the lunisolar calendar used in East Asia)|Chunji is the beginning of spring in the traditional East Asian calendar. 春季是东亚传统历法中春天的开始。|Synonym: spring, springtime|Antonym: autumn, fall|noun

/ˈtʃʌndʒiː/| n.|春意,春天的气息|The air held the chunji of new beginnings. 空气中弥漫着春天的气息，预示着新的开始。|Synonym: spring feeling, vernal feeling|Antonym: autumnal feeling, wintry feeling|noun
