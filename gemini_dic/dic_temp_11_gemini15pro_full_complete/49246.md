deleteriousness:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəsnəs/
n.
有害；危害性
The quality or state of being deleterious; harmfulness.
The deleteriousness of smoking is widely known.  吸烟的危害性是众所周知的。
deleteriousness of the drug 药物的危害性


近义词: harmfulness, noxiousness, perniciousness
反义词: harmlessness, beneficence, advantageousness
词性: noun


deleterious:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/
adj.
有害的；有毒的
Causing harm or damage.
Smoking has a deleterious effect on health. 吸烟对健康有害。
The chemical is deleterious to the environment.  这种化学物质对环境有害。

近义词: harmful, damaging, injurious, detrimental
反义词: beneficial, advantageous, helpful, wholesome
词性: adjective
