acosmism:/ˈeɪˌkɒzmɪzəm/ /əˈkɒzmɪzəm/
n.
宇宙虚无主义; 非宇宙论
The denial or disbelief in the reality of the physical universe, the belief that the material world is illusory.
Acosmism is a philosophical view that denies the reality of the physical universe, seeing it as an illusion or a misinterpretation of true reality.

Acosmism asserts that the physical world we perceive is not real.  这个理论断言我们感知的物质世界并非真实。

Some mystical traditions embrace acosmism.  一些神秘主义传统信奉宇宙虚无主义。


近义词:illusionism, nihilism, nondualism
反义词:realism, materialism
词性:noun
