cashless:/ˈkæʃləs/| adj.|无现金的|Relating to, made for, or involving a system where money is not physically exchanged but rather transactions are made electronically.|Example:  We are moving towards a cashless society. 我们正在走向一个无现金社会。|Example:  The cashless payment system is very convenient. 无现金支付系统非常方便。|近义词:electronic, digital, card-based|反义词:cash, cash-based|词性:adjective
Etymology: cash + -less.  "Cash" comes from Middle French "casse" meaning "money box."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
