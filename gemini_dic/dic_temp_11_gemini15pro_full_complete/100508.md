interventricular:/ˌɪntərvenˈtrɪkjələr/| adj.|心室间的，室间的|Located between the ventricles (especially of the heart).  位于心室之间（尤指心脏）。|The interventricular septum separates the left and right ventricles of the heart. 心室间隔将心脏的左右心室分开。|Interventricular conduction delay can be a sign of a heart condition. 室间传导阻滞可能是心脏疾病的征兆。|interventricular groove, interventricular foramen|atrioventricular|adjective
