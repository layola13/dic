beeswaxed:/ˈbiːzwækst/
adjective
涂过蜂蜡的；擦亮的
coated with beeswax; polished.
The antique table was beeswaxed to a gleaming shine.  古董桌子被涂上蜂蜡，闪闪发亮。
polished, waxed, buffed
dull, unpolished
词性: 形容词


beeswaxed:/ˈbiːzwækst/
verb
用蜂蜡涂抹；用蜂蜡擦亮
to coat or polish with beeswax.
He beeswaxed the floor until it shone. 他用蜂蜡擦地板，直到它闪闪发光。
polish, wax, buff
词性: 动词


词源:
From bee + wax + -ed.  由“bee”（蜜蜂）+“wax”（蜡）+过去分词后缀“-ed”构成。
