astounded: /əˈstaʊndɪd/ | adj. | 震惊的，惊骇的 | Feeling or showing great surprise or wonder; amazed. |  I was astounded by the news of her sudden death. 我对她的突然去世的消息感到震惊。

The magician astounded the audience with his incredible tricks. 魔术师以其令人难以置信的技巧使观众震惊。


近义词: amazed, shocked, surprised, stunned, bewildered

反义词: unsurprised, indifferent, unimpressed

词性: adjective

词源:  From the verb "astound," which comes from Old French "estoner" meaning "to stun, daze, amaze," ultimately from Latin "extonare" meaning "to thunder at."
