antinomy:/ænˈtɪnəmi/ | n. |矛盾，对立，二律背反 |A contradiction between two beliefs or conclusions that are in themselves reasonable; a paradox. |例句：He explored the antinomy between free will and determinism. 他探讨了自由意志和决定论之间的矛盾。

例句：There is a fundamental antinomy between the pursuit of individual happiness and the demands of society.  在追求个人幸福和社会需求之间存在着根本的矛盾。|近义词：contradiction, paradox, conflict, dichotomy, opposition |反义词：agreement, harmony, consistency, concord |词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *antinomia*, from Ancient Greek ἀντινομία (antinomia, “contradiction of the law”), from ἀντί (anti, “against”) + νόμος (nomos, “law, custom”).
