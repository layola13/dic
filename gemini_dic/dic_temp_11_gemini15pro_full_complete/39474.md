conniver:/kəˈnaɪvər/| n.|共谋者，阴谋家，纵容者|A conniver is someone who secretly makes plans to achieve something, usually something dishonest or immoral. |He's a conniver and a cheat, and I don't trust him. 他是一个阴谋家和骗子，我不信任他。|The CEO was accused of being a conniver in the accounting scandal. 首席执行官被指控在这起会计丑闻中充当共谋者。|Synonyms: schemer, plotter, intriguer, manipulator|Antonyms: honest person, straight shooter|noun
