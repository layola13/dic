collyrium:/kəˈlɪriəm/
noun
眼药水
a liquid used as an eyewash, typically to treat an infection.
The doctor prescribed a collyrium for her eye infection. 医生给她开了眼药水治疗眼部感染。
synonyms: eyewash, eye drops
antonyms: N/A
