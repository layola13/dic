braise:/breɪz/| v. | 焖；炖；蒸 |To cook (food) by browning it in fat, then simmering it in a small amount of liquid in a closed container.| I braised the beef in red wine for three hours. 我用红酒焖了三个小时牛肉。|  She braised the short ribs until they were fall-off-the-bone tender. 她将牛肋骨炖至脱骨的程度。 | simmer, stew | fry, roast | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *braisier, from braise (“live coals”), from Frankish *brasa, from Proto-Germanic *brasǭ (“burning embers”). Cognate with Old High German brasa (“burning embers”), Middle Dutch braese (“fire, heat”). More at brand.
