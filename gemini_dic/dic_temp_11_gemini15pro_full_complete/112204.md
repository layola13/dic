looped:/luːpt/| adj.|环状的，循环的，醉醺醺的|forming a loop or loops; (informal) drunk or intoxicated.|The movie featured a looped roller coaster.  这部电影的特色是一个环状过山车。|The partygoers were looped by midnight.  参加聚会的人到午夜都喝醉了。|近义词：circular, round, drunk, intoxicated|反义词:straight, sober|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "loop," which originated from Middle Dutch "lopen" meaning "to run."  The past participle form "looped" eventually became an adjective.
