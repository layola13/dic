abord:/əˈbɔːrd/| adv. prep.|在船(飞机、火车)上，上船(飞机、火车)|On or into a ship, aircraft, train, or other vehicle.|例句：Welcome aboard! 欢迎登机！|近义词：on board, aboard, on|反义词:ashore, off, disembark|副词/介词
Etymology: From Middle English aborde, from Old French a bord (“on board, on shipboard”), from a (“to”) + bord (“ship's side, edge, border, bank”).|词源
