outcry:/ˈaʊtkraɪ/| n. |强烈抗议，大声疾呼|a strong expression of public disapproval or anger|例句：The new tax proposal caused a public outcry. 新的税收提案引起了公众的强烈抗议。

例句：There was an outcry over the government's decision. 政府的决定引起了强烈抗议。


近义词：protest, clamor, uproar, commotion, objection|反义词:  approval, acceptance, support|词性: noun
