卡林加 (历史地名，古印度东部一地区，今奥里萨邦)
A historical region of eastern India, corresponding roughly to the modern state of Orissa.
Kalinga was a powerful kingdom in ancient India. 卡林加是古印度一个强大的王国。
The Kalinga War was a significant event in Indian history.  卡林加战争是印度历史上的一件大事。
