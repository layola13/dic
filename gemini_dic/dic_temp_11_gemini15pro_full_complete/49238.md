deler:/ˈdiːlər/ | n. |经销商，交易商，发牌者| A person or company that buys and sells goods. |  He's a car dealer. 他是一个汽车经销商。|  vendor, trader, merchant | buyer, customer | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *delere*, from Anglo-Norman *deler*, from Old French *deleier*, *deloier* (“to divide, share out, distribute”), from Latin *dīlātāre* (“to spread out, extend, enlarge”), frequentative of *dīferre* (“to carry in different directions, spread, scatter”), from *dis-* (“apart, asunder”) + *ferre* (“to carry, bear”).  Doublet of *dilator*.
