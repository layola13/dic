maleficent:/məˈlɛfɪsənt/ | adj. n.| 有害的，恶毒的；邪恶的人 |  (adj.) causing harm or destruction, especially by supernatural means; (n.) a person who does evil or wishes harm to others. | The maleficent witch cast a spell on the princess.  邪恶的女巫对公主施了咒语。| Synonym: evil, wicked, malevolent, sinister | Antonym: benevolent, benign, good | adjective

maleficent:/məˈlɛfɪsənt/ | n. | 邪恶的人 | A person who does evil or wishes harm to others. |  Maleficent is a Disney character who was originally portrayed as a purely evil villain.  玛琳菲森是一个迪士尼角色，最初被描绘成一个纯粹邪恶的反派。| Synonym: villain, evildoer, antagonist | Antonym: hero, protagonist | noun

Etymology:
From Latin *maleficus*, from male (badly) + -ficus (doing, making), related to facere (to do, to make).
