lambent:/ˈlæmbənt/| adj.|柔和光亮的；闪烁的；轻柔的|Softly bright or radiant; flickering lightly; gently glowing.|(Of light or fire) glowing faintly with soft,wavering light. |例句：The lambent flames danced in the fireplace. 柔和的火焰在壁炉里跳动。

例句：Her lambent eyes met his across the crowded room. 在拥挤的房间里，她明亮的眼睛与他的相遇。|近义词：glowing, luminous, radiant, shimmering, flickering|反义词：dull, dark, dim|词性:形容词

lambent:/ˈlæmbənt/| adj.|轻快的；优美的（指文风或说话风格）|(esp. of wit) lightly brilliant or graceful.|Playing lightly and gracefully over a subject.|例句：His lambent wit charmed everyone at the party. 他轻快的机智迷住了派对上的每一个人。

例句：Her writing style is known for its lambent prose. 她的写作风格以其优美的散文而闻名。|近义词：witty, sparkling, elegant, refined|反义词：dull, heavy, clumsy|词性:形容词
