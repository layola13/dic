deserve: /dɪˈzɜːrv/ (British) /dɪˈzɜːrv/ (American)| v. |应得；值得|To be worthy of something, whether good or bad.|She deserved a promotion after all her hard work. 她辛勤工作后应该得到晋升。|He deserves to be punished for his crimes. 他应该因他的罪行而受到惩罚。|近义词: merit, earn, warrant, justify|反义词: forfeit|词性:动词
