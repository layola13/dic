guerrero:/ɡeˈrɛro/ | n. |战士，勇士|A soldier or warrior, especially one who is brave and skilled in combat.  Derived from Spanish, ultimately from Germanic *weraz "man."|例句：The Guerrero fought bravely in the battle.  这位勇士在战斗中英勇作战。

例句：He was a skilled guerrero, feared by his enemies. 他是一位技艺高超的勇士，令敌人闻风丧胆。|近义词：warrior, soldier, fighter, combatant|反义词:coward, pacifist|词性:noun
