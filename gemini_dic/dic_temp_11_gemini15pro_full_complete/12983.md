astir:/əˈstɜːr/
adjective
活跃的，忙碌的，骚动的
Out of bed and active;  moving around.
The whole town was astir with excitement about the upcoming festival.  整个城镇都因即将到来的节日而兴奋不已。
The house was astir at dawn.  黎明时分，这所房子里就开始有人活动了。
近义词: active, awake, bustling, lively, stirring, up
反义词: asleep, quiet, still
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From a- + stir.  From Old English styrian "to move, stir," from Proto-Germanic *sturjaną (source also of Old Saxon stirrian, Old Norse styrrja, Old Frisian stiria, Dutch storen "to disturb," Old High German stören, German stören "to disturb, interrupt"), from PIE root *stere- "to spread."
