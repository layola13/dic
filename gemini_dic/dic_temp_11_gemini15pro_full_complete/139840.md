octopoda:/ɒkˈtɒpədə/ | n. |章鱼|A member of the order Octopoda, which includes octopuses.  These cephalopods are characterized by their eight arms, large bulbous heads, and remarkable intelligence.|Octopuses, members of the Octopoda class, are fascinating creatures. 章鱼，属于八腕目，是迷人的生物。|Synonyms: octopus, cephalopod|Antonyms: None|Noun

octopodes:/ɒkˈtɒpədiːz/ | n. |章鱼（复数）|The plural form of octopus, less commonly used than "octopuses".|Some scientists prefer the term octopodes when referring to multiple species. 一些科学家在提到多个物种时更喜欢用octopodes这个词。|Synonyms: octopuses|Antonyms: None|Noun
