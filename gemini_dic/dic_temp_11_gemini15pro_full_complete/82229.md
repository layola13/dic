grouched:/ɡraʊtʃt/ | adj. | 抱怨的，不高兴的 | Feeling or expressing discontent or irritation. | He's been grouched all day because he lost his keys.  他一整天都很不高兴，因为他丢了钥匙。 | 近义词：grumpy, sullen, sulky, irritable, cranky | 反义词：cheerful, happy, content | 词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "grouch" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Grouch" originates from the early 20th century, possibly influenced by Yiddish "grutshen" (to grumble).
