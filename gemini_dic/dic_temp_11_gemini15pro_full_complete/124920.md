mudskipper:/ˈmʌdsˌkɪpər/ | n. | 弹涂鱼 | A fish that lives in intertidal habitats and can move on land using its pectoral fins. | Mudskippers can be seen skipping across the mudflats at low tide.  弹涂鱼在退潮时可以在泥滩上跳跃。|近义词: amphibious fish | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | noun
