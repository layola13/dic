feels:/fiːlz/| v.|感觉，觉得|Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "feel".  It describes a sensory experience or an emotional state.|She feels cold. 她觉得冷。|It feels like it's going to rain. 感觉要下雨了。|近义词：senses, experiences, believes, thinks|反义词:numb, insensible|verb
