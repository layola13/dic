anticlassicism:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪzəm/
|n.|反古典主义，非古典主义|Opposition to or rejection of the principles of classicism, especially in art, literature, and music.|The rise of anticlassicism in the late 18th century led to a renewed interest in the medieval period.|近义词:romanticism, modernism|反义词:classicism|词性:名词
