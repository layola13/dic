gesticulatingly:/dʒɛsˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪŋli/
adverb
做着手势地，以手势表达地
Characterized by or done with gestures, often animated or emphatic ones.
She spoke gesticulatingly, her hands flying through the air. 她说话时做着手势，双手在空中飞舞。
The conductor gesticulatingly directed the orchestra with passion.  指挥热情地做着手势指挥乐队。
Synonyms: dramatically, expressively, emphatically, animatedly
Antonyms: calmly, quietly, reservedly
词性: 副词
