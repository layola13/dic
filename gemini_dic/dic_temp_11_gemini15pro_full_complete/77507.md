gazing:/ˈɡeɪzɪŋ/| v. |凝视，注视|Looking steadily and intently, especially in admiration, thought, or wonder.|She was gazing out the window, lost in thought. 她凝视着窗外，陷入了沉思。|近义词：staring, watching, observing, contemplating|反义词：glancing, ignoring|词性: 动词 (present participle of gaze)

词源: From Old North French *gaiser "to gape, stare," perhaps of Germanic origin.
