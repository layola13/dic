non-hazardous:/nɒnˈhæzədəs/ | adj. |无害的，非危险的 | Not dangerous; unlikely to cause harm. | This cleaning product is non-hazardous and safe for use around children.  这种清洁产品无害，可在儿童周围安全使用。| The waste produced by this process is completely non-hazardous. 这个过程产生的废物是完全无害的。 | 近义词: safe, harmless, innocuous | 反义词: hazardous, dangerous, harmful | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
hazardous: from hazard + -ous (suffix forming adjectives). Hazard ultimately from Arabic az-zahr "the die," referring to games of chance.
