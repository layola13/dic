boast:/boʊst/ (US) /bəʊst/ (UK)| v. n.|吹嘘，自夸；拥有（值得自豪的东西）|To speak with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities.  Can also mean to possess something to be proud of.|He boasted about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。|synonyms: brag, crow, vaunt|antonyms: deprecate, downplay|verb

boast:/boʊst/ (US) /bəʊst/ (UK)| n.|吹嘘，自夸；值得骄傲的事物|An act of boasting; a proud statement about oneself or one's achievements.  Something that a person or place is proud to have.|His boast about winning the race was annoying.  他吹嘘赢得比赛的事很烦人。|synonyms: bragging, vaunting|antonyms: modesty, humility|noun
