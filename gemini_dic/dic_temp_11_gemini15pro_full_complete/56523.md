domiciliary: /ˌdɒmɪˈsɪliəri/ (UK), /ˌdɑːməˈsɪliəri/ (US)
| n.| 上门服务的医生、护士等|A person, such as a doctor or nurse, who provides care in a patient's home.| The domiciliary checked the patient's vital signs. 上门服务的医生检查了病人的生命体征。|Synonyms: home healthcare provider, visiting nurse, home health aide|Antonyms: hospital staff, resident physician| noun
