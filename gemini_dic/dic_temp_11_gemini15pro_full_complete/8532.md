anticentralisms:/ˌæntiˈsɛntrəlɪzəmz/
|n.|反中心主义|Opposition to the concentration of power or control in a central authority or location. It often advocates for greater autonomy and decentralization in various spheres, such as politics, economics, or culture.|The conference explored various forms of anticentralisms, from political movements advocating for regional autonomy to online communities promoting decentralized networks.|近义词：decentralization, localism, regionalism, autonomy|反义词：centralism, centralization|词性: Noun
