cook-chill:/ˈkʊk tʃɪl/
noun|冷烹
|A system of food preparation where food is cooked, rapidly chilled and stored at refrigerated temperatures before being reheated for serving.|The hospital uses a cook-chill system for patient meals.  医院对病人餐食采用冷烹系统。|sous-vide, prepared meals|fast food|n.
cook-chill:/ˈkʊk tʃɪl/
adjective|冷烹的
|Relating to or denoting a system of food preparation where food is cooked, rapidly chilled and stored at refrigerated temperatures before being reheated for serving.|The cook-chill meals are convenient for busy people. 冷烹食品对于忙碌的人来说很方便。|pre-prepared, ready-made|freshly cooked|adj.
