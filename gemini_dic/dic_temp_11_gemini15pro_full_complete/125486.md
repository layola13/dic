multiprogrammed:/ˌmʌltiˈproʊɡræmd/ | adj. | 多道程序的 | Designed to run multiple programs seemingly simultaneously by rapidly switching between them. This allows the CPU to be kept busy while waiting for slower input/output operations to complete. |  The multiprogrammed operating system significantly increased the throughput of the computer system.  多道程序操作系统显著提高了计算机系统的吞吐量。| Near Synonyms: concurrent, multitasking | Antonyms: single-tasking | adjective

multiprogram:/ˌmʌltiˈproʊɡræm/ | v. | 多道程序设计 | To design or implement a system capable of multiprogramming. |  They multiprogrammed the mainframe to maximize its efficiency.  他们对大型机进行了多道程序设计，以最大限度地提高其效率。| Near Synonyms: schedule, manage, orchestrate | Antonyms: N/A | verb
