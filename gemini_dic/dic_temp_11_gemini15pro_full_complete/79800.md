glyceryl:/ˈɡlɪsərɪl/ | n. | 甘油基 | A radical formed from glycerol by the removal of one, two, or three hydroxyl groups.  It is a component part of many lipids. | Glyceryl tristearate is a common type of fat. 甘油三硬脂酸酯是一种常见的脂肪。| Glycerol, glyceride | N/A | noun
