mahayana:/ˌmɑːhəˈjɑːnə/| n. |大乘佛教|One of the two major traditions of Buddhism, often contrasted with Theravada Buddhism. It emphasizes the Bodhisattva ideal, universal compassion, and the possibility of enlightenment for all beings.|例句：Mahayana Buddhism is prevalent in East Asia. 大乘佛教在东亚盛行。

|例句：The concept of emptiness is central to Mahayana philosophy.  空的概念是大乘佛教哲学的核心。|近义词：Greater Vehicle|反义词：Hinayana (now considered pejorative, prefer Theravada)|词性：noun
