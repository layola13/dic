cowleeching:/ˈkaʊliːtʃɪŋ/
n.
放血疗法（用牛蛭）;  用牛蛭吸血
The practice of using cow leeches (a type of leech) for medicinal bloodletting.
Cowleeching was a common practice in medieval medicine.  牛蛭放血疗法是中世纪医学的常见做法。
近义词: bloodletting, leeching, hirudotherapy
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but potentially "transfusion," "blood donation")
