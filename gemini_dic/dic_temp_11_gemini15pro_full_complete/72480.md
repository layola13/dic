fluoboride:/ˌfluːəˈbɔːraɪd/ | n. |氟硼酸盐；四氟硼酸盐|A salt containing the BF4- anion.  A compound containing the fluoroborate ion, BF4−. | Potassium fluoboride is used in some fluxes for brazing and soldering. 氟硼酸钾用于一些钎焊和焊接的助熔剂。|Synonyms: tetrafluoroborate|Antonyms: N/A|noun
