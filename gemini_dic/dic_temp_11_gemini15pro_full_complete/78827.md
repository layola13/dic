The advertising campaign relied heavily on gimmickeries to attract customers. 这场广告宣传活动严重依赖各种噱头来吸引顾客。 | synonyms: tricks, ploys, stunts, ruses, contrivances | antonyms: authenticity, genuineness, sincerity | noun


gimmickry:/ˈɡɪmɪkri/| n. | 花招，噱头，小玩意 | The use of gimmicks; a gimmick.  | The film's plot relied too heavily on gimmickry. 这部电影的情节过于依赖噱头。

The salesman's gimmickry didn't impress the experienced buyer.  销售员的花招并没有给经验丰富的买家留下深刻印象。 | synonyms: trickery, chicanery, subterfuge | antonyms: honesty, straightforwardness | noun
