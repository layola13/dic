exporting:/ɪkˈspɔːtɪŋ/ /ˈekspɔːrtɪŋ/
n.|出口; 输出; |The exporting of goods to foreign countries is important for the economy.  向外国出口货物对经济很重要。|近义词:shipping, selling abroad|反义词:importing|词性:名词

exporting:/ɪkˈspɔːtɪŋ/ /ˈekspɔːrtɪŋ/
adj.|出口的|The exporting company specializes in handcrafted goods. 这家出口公司专门经营手工制品。|近义词:outbound, outward|反义词:importing|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exportare* "to carry out," from *ex* "out of" + *portare* "to carry."
