in-box:/ˈɪnˌbɑːks/ | n. | 收件箱；待办事项 | A tray, basket, or compartment, as in an office, for incoming mail or other material.  A place, such as a folder on a computer, where email messages are received and stored. | I have over 200 unread emails in my inbox. 我的收件箱里有超过200封未读邮件。| synonyms: mailbox, letterbox, inbox folder, incoming mail | antonyms: outbox, sent items | noun

in-box:/ˈɪnˌbɑːks/ | vt. | 将…放入收件箱；发送至收件箱 | To send or deliver something to someone's inbox. | I'll inbox you the details later. 我稍后会把细节发送到你的收件箱。| synonyms: send, email, forward, deliver | antonyms: send from, reply, respond | verb
