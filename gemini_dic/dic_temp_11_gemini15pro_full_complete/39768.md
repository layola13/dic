consolidator:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪtər/ /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪtər/| n. |合并者，整合者，统一者|A person or company that combines or unites several smaller things, especially businesses, into one larger unit.|The airline consolidator offers discounted fares by buying tickets in bulk. 这家航空公司整合商通过批量购买机票提供折扣票价。|Synonyms: integrator, merger, unifier|Antonyms: divider, separator|noun

consolidate:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪt/| v. |巩固，加强，合并，统一|To make something stronger or more secure; to combine several things into one.|The company consolidated its position in the market by acquiring its main rival.  这家公司通过收购其主要竞争对手巩固了其市场地位。|Synonyms: strengthen, reinforce, combine, merge|Antonyms: weaken, divide, separate|verb
