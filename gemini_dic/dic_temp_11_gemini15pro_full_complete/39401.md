conjurorship:/ˈkʌndʒərərʃɪp/
n.
魔术师的职业；魔术师的身份
The art or practice of a conjurer; the profession of a conjurer.
He abandoned his conjurorship and became a teacher. 他放弃了魔术师的职业，成为了一名教师。
Synonyms: magic, illusionism, prestidigitation, legerdemain
Antonyms: reality
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From conjurer + -ship.  Conjurer comes from Old French conjureur, from conjurer "to conjure," from Latin conjurare "to swear together, conspire, entreat earnestly," from com- "together" + jurare "to swear."
