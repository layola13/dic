extroversion: /ˈɛkstrəvɜːʃən/ (US) /ˈɛkstrəvɜːʒən/ (UK)| n. |外向性；外向；外倾|The act, state, or habit of being predominantly concerned with obtaining gratification from what is outside the self.|Her extroversion makes her a natural salesperson. 她的外向性使她天生就是个销售员。|近义词：outgoingness, sociability|反义词：introversion, shyness|词性: noun

Etymology:  From  extra-  + -version, from Latin extra "outside"  + versus "turned".  Literally "turned outwards".
