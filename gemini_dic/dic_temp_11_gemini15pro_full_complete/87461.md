hess:/ˈhɛs/ | n. | 黑森州 (德国中部的一个州) | Hesse is a state in central Germany. | Example: He comes from Hesse, Germany. 他来自德国黑森州。| Example: Frankfurt is the largest city in Hesse. 法兰克福是黑森州最大的城市。| Synonym: Hessen (German) | Antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Hessen*, derived from the name of the Germanic tribe, the *Hessians*, who inhabited the region.  The ultimate origin of the tribal name is uncertain.
