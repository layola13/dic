catelectrode:/ˈkætɪˌlektrəʊd/ (UK) /ˈkætɪˌlekˌtroʊd/ (US)| n. |阴极|The negative electrode in an electrolytic cell, towards which positive ions (cations) migrate.|

例句：
The catelectrode attracts positively charged ions.
阴极吸引带正电的离子。

The metal is deposited on the catelectrode during electrolysis.
在电解过程中，金属沉积在阴极上。


近义词：negative electrode, cathode|
反义词：anode, positive electrode|
词性：noun|

词源: cata- (Greek: down, against) + electrode (electric + -ode (path))  Meaning "downward path for electricity," referring to the flow of positive ions.
