cosmopolitan:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ /ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlɪtən/| adj. n.|世界性的，全球性的；世界主义者，四海为家的人|Familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures.|She has a very cosmopolitan outlook on life. 她的人生观非常国际化。|近义词：international, global, worldly, sophisticated|反义词：provincial, parochial, insular|adjective

cosmopolitan:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ /ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlɪtən/| n.|世界主义者，四海为家的人|A person who is familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures.|London is a city of cosmopolitans. 伦敦是一个世界主义者的城市。|近义词：internationalist, globe-trotter|反义词：local, native|noun
