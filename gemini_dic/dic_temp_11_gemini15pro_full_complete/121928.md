misshaping:/ˌmɪsˈʃeɪpɪŋ/
|n. |变形，畸形|The process of giving something an incorrect or distorted shape.|The misshaping of the clay made it unusable for pottery.  黏土的变形使它无法用于制陶。|deformation, distortion, contortion|formation, shaping
|v. |使变形，弄歪|To give something the wrong shape; to deform or distort.|Wearing ill-fitting shoes can misshape your feet. 穿不合脚的鞋子会使你的脚变形。|distort, deform, warp|form, shape
