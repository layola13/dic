observative: /əbˈzɜːrvətɪv/ | adj. | 善于观察的；观察力敏锐的 | Keenly observant; perceptive.  | She is an observative child, always noticing small details. 她是一个观察力敏锐的孩子，总是注意到一些小细节。 |  He made some observative comments about the painting's composition. 他对这幅画的构图提出了一些敏锐的见解。 |  perceptive, observant, insightful, astute, sharp-eyed, watchful, vigilant | unobservant, inattentive, oblivious | adjective

Etymology:  From the Latin "observare" (to watch, observe) + "-ative" (forming adjectives).
