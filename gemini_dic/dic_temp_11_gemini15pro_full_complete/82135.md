词源:  "Grommet" originally referred to a young surfer, particularly a pre-teen or early teenage beginner.  Its origin is uncertain, but one theory suggests it might be a playful alteration of "groom," implying someone being trained or groomed in the sport.  The term has broadened to encompass novices in other activities as well, often retaining a sense of youthful enthusiasm.
