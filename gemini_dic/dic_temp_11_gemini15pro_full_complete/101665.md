irrelevance:/ɪˈreləvəns/ | n. | 不相关，不切题，无关紧要 |The irrelevance of his comment was apparent to everyone.  他的评论毫不相关，每个人都看出来了。|例句：The chairman ruled the question out of order because of its irrelevance to the matter under discussion. 主席裁定该问题与讨论事项无关，因此无效。|近义词：inappositeness, immateriality, extraneousness |反义词: relevance, pertinence, significance | 词性: 名词

irrelevancy:/ɪˈreləvənsi/ | n. | 不相关，不切题，无关紧要 | He spoke at length on the irrelevancy of the current curriculum. 他详细阐述了当前课程的不相关性。|例句：The irrelevancy of her remarks made everyone uncomfortable. 她不相关的言论让每个人都感到不舒服。|近义词：inappositeness, immateriality, extraneousness |反义词: relevance, pertinence, significance | 词性: 名词
