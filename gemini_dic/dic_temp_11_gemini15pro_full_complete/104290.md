justifiable:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪəbl/ /ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪəbəl/ | adj. | 可辩护的，有理由的，情有可原的 | Capable of being shown to be right or reasonable; defensible. |  His actions were entirely justifiable given the circumstances.  鉴于当时的情况，他的行为完全合理。

| Example 1: Her anger was justifiable considering what he had said. 考虑到他所说的话，她的愤怒是情有可原的。

| Example 2: The use of force was justifiable in self-defense. 出于自卫，使用武力是正当的。

| Synonyms: defensible, excusable, legitimate, reasonable, valid, warranted | Antonyms: indefensible, inexcusable, unjustifiable, unreasonable, invalid, unwarranted | Word type: adjective
