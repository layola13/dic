elenctic:/ɪˈlɛŋktɪk/
adjective
辩驳的，反驳的，检验的
Serving to confute or disprove; of the nature of an elenchus.
Socrates' elenctic method forced his students to question their assumptions. 苏格拉底的辩驳法迫使他的学生质疑他们的假设。
elenctic:/ɪˈlɛŋktɪk/
noun
辩驳法，反驳法
A method of argument that aims to refute an opponent's proposition by showing that it leads to a contradiction or absurdity.
The philosopher employed an elenctic in his debate. 这位哲学家在辩论中运用了辩驳法。
synonym: refutative, disproving
antonym: supportive, confirming
