overkill:/ˈoʊvərˌkɪl/| n.|过度杀伤；过分；矫枉过正|More than what is necessary; excessive.  Using a sledgehammer to crack a nut is overkill.|He brought out all of his presentation materials for the five-minute meeting – it was complete overkill.|近义词：excess, superfluity, overkill, redundancy|反义词: underkill, insufficiency|noun


overkill:/ˈoʊvərˌkɪl/| adj.|过度的；过分的|More than is necessary; excessive. |Bringing a tank to a knife fight is overkill.|Her elaborate decorations for the small party were a bit overkill.|近义词：excessive, immoderate, extravagant|反义词: insufficient, inadequate|adjective
