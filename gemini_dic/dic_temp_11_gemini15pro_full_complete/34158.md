clambake:/ˈklæmbeɪk/| n.| 野餐，海滩野餐，(尤指)吃蛤蜊的野餐；热闹的聚会|A social gathering, typically outdoors, at which clams are baked or steamed, often along with other seafood, corn, and potatoes.  A clambake can also refer to any lively, festive gathering.| We're having a clambake on the beach this weekend.  这个周末我们要去海滩上野餐。

The annual company clambake is always a lot of fun. 公司一年一度的热闹聚会总是很有趣。|Synonyms: beach party, cookout, picnic, outing, get-together|Antonyms:  None (lacks a direct opposite, potentially "formal event")|Noun
