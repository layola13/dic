hyperaggressively:/ˌhaɪpərəˈɡrɛsɪvli/
| adv. |极具侵略性地，过度攻击性地 |  in an extremely aggressive manner, excessively combative |  He hyperaggressively pursued his business rivals, driving some into bankruptcy. 他极具侵略性地追击他的商业对手，导致一些人破产。| 近义词: excessively aggressively, over-aggressively, ultra-aggressively | 反义词: passively, defensively, submissively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessively"
aggressive (adjective): from Latin *aggredi* "to attack," from *ad-* "to" + *gradi* "to step, go"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
