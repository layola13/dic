lineally:/ˈlɪniəli/| adv.| 直系地，世系上地，线条地|In a lineal descent from father to son. 从父亲到儿子的直系血统。|例句：The throne passed lineally from father to son. 王位由父子直系相传。

例句：The design is simple, consisting of lineally arranged dots.  这个设计很简单，由线性排列的点组成。|近义词：directly, hereditarily, in direct line|反义词：collaterally, indirectly|词性：副词

词源：From Late Latin *linealis, from Latin linea (“line”).
