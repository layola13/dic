in-clear:/ɪnˈklɪər/| adj.|清楚的，明白的；无障碍的；畅通的|Free from obstruction; not blocked or congested.  Also, easily understood; plain; obvious.|The road is in-clear after the snowplows came through. 道路在扫雪车经过后畅通无阻了。|近义词：clear, unobstructed, open, transparent, evident, obvious|反义词：blocked, obstructed, unclear, vague, ambiguous|词性:形容词
