embay:/ɪmˈbeɪ/ | v. | 嵌入，包围，融入 | To fix something firmly in a surrounding mass; to enclose or surround. | The small pearl was embedded in the gold ring. 小珍珠被嵌在金戒指里。

The soldiers were embedded with the local population. 士兵们融入当地居民之中。 | 近义词：embed, insert, enclose, surround | 反义词：extract, remove | 词性: 动词
