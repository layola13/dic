outdeliver:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈlɪv.ər/
verb
超越交付；在交付方面胜过
To deliver more or better than someone or something else.
Example: Company A outdelivered its competitors by offering faster shipping and better customer service.  A公司通过提供更快的运输和更好的客户服务，在交付方面胜过了竞争对手。
Example:  They consistently outdelivered their sales targets. 他们持续超额完成销售目标。
Synonyms: surpass, exceed, outperform
Antonyms: underperform, lag behind
词性: 动词
