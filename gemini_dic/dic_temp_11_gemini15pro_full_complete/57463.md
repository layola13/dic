drafter:/ˈdræftər/ (US), /ˈdrɑːftər/ (UK)| n.|起草人；制图员；绘图员|A person who draws plans or designs, especially of buildings or machinery; a person who writes a first version of a document.|The architect's drafter carefully rendered the blueprints for the new skyscraper. 建筑师的绘图员仔细地绘制了新摩天大楼的蓝图。|近义词：designer, writer, author, composer, architect|反义词:None|Noun

drafter:/ˈdræftər/ (US), /ˈdrɑːftər/ (UK)| n.|应征入伍者；被征召者|A person who is drafted into military service.|Many young drafters were sent overseas during the war. 战争期间，许多年轻的应征入伍者被派往海外。|近义词：conscript, recruit|反义词:volunteer|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From draft + -er.  "Draft" comes from the Middle English *draught*, from Old Norse *dráttr* meaning "a drawing, pulling."
