oasis:/oʊˈeɪsɪs/ /əʊˈeɪsɪs/
n.
绿洲；舒适的地方；避难所
A fertile spot in a desert where water is found.  A pleasant or peaceful place or period in the midst of difficulty or unpleasantness.
The travelers found an oasis in the desert. 旅行者在沙漠中发现了一片绿洲。
The library was an oasis of calm in the busy city. 图书馆是繁忙城市中一片宁静的绿洲。
近义词：haven, refuge, sanctuary, retreat
反义词：desert, wasteland
词性：名词

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French, from Latin oasis, from Greek oasis, of Egyptian origin.
