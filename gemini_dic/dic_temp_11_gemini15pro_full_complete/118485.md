mesmerically:/ˌmezˈmerɪkli/ | adv. | 催眠地，迷人地 | In a way that completely holds your attention and fascinates you. |  Her performance was mesmerizing; the audience was captivated. 她的表演令人着迷，观众都被吸引住了。| The dancers moved mesmerically across the stage. 舞蹈演员在舞台上优美地移动，令人着迷。 | captivatingly, hypnotically, fascinatingly | dully, boringly, uninterestingly | 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mesmerize + -ically.  "Mesmerize" comes from the name of Franz Mesmer (1734-1815), an Austrian physician who practiced hypnotism.
