bedfellow:/ˈbɛdfɛloʊ/ (US), /ˈbedfeləʊ/ (UK)| n.|同床人；伙伴；盟友|A person who shares a bed with another; a person or group allied or associated with another, especially in a surprising or unexpected way.|例句：1.  He found himself sharing a bed with a complete stranger. 他发现自己和一个完全陌生的人睡在同一张床上。

2. Strange bedfellows politics makes. 政治会制造奇怪的盟友。|近义词：associate, ally, partner, colleague|反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy|词性:名词
