degarnishment:/diːˈɡɑːrnɪʃmənt/
n.
解除扣押； garnishment的解除
The legal process of releasing a garnishment.  This typically occurs after a debt has been paid or a court order is lifted.
The degarnishment of his wages finally allowed him to manage his finances properly.  他的工资解除扣押后，他终于可以妥善管理自己的财务了。

Synonym: release, discharge, liberation
Antonym: garnishment, seizure, attachment
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + garnishment.  Garnishment comes from Old North French garnissement, meaning "provision, supply," ultimately from Germanic roots.
