nonanemic:/ˌnɒnəˈniːmɪk/
adjective
非贫血的
Not anemic; having a normal blood count.
Her nonanemic complexion suggested she was in good health. 她面色红润，表明她身体健康。
Synonyms: healthy, robust, vigorous
Antonyms: anemic, pale, weak
