impanelled:/ɪmˈpænəld/ |v.| 被选入陪审团；被组成专门小组 |  The jury was impanelled and sworn in. 陪审团成员被选定并宣誓就职。 |  A special committee was impanelled to investigate the incident.  一个特别委员会被组成以调查该事件。 | 近义词：enrolled, listed, selected, chosen | 反义词：discharged, dismissed | 词性: 动词

词源:  From im- + panel + -ed.  "Im-" is a prefix meaning "in" or "into." "Panel" originally referred to a piece of parchment, then a list of jurors written on such parchment. Thus, "impanel" means to enter onto a panel or list, especially a jury list.
