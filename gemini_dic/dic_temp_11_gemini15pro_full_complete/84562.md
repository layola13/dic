词源:  From the verb "hammer" (Old English *hamor, from Proto-Germanic *hamaraz), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱem- (to cut, split).  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a noun, indicating the act or result of hammering.
