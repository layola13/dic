devoid:/dɪˈvɔɪd/
adjective
缺乏的，没有的
Entirely lacking or free from.
The landscape was devoid of life. 这片土地上毫无生机。
The room was devoid of furniture. 房间里没有任何家具。
synonyms: lacking, empty, barren, bereft, destitute
antonyms: full, replete, abounding, teeming
词性: 形容词


词源:
From Old French *desvoidier, from des- "out, from" + voidier "to empty".
