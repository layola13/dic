internets:/ˈɪntərˌnɛts/
n.
（非标准用法）互联网
(non-standard) plural form of internet; refers to multiple interconnected networks, often used humorously or ironically.
Many people mistakenly refer to "the internets" as if there are multiple internets. 很多人错误地将互联网称为“多个互联网”，就好像有多个互联网一样。
The meme about "surfing the internets" is a playful take on how people explore the vast online world.  关于“在互联网上冲浪”的表情包是对人们如何探索浩瀚网络世界的俏皮说法。
Synonym: internet, cyberspace, World Wide Web
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider) offline, real world
词性: noun
