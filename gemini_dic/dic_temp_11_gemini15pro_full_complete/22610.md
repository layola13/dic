boox:/buːks/ |n.|电子阅读器；电子书阅读器|An e-reader; a device designed for reading ebooks.|例句：I downloaded several new books onto my boox. 我在我的boox上下载了几本新书。|例句：The boox has a paper-like display that's easy on the eyes. 这款boox拥有类似纸张的显示屏，阅读起来很舒服。|近义词：e-reader, e-book reader, digital reader|反义词:physical book, paperback, hardcover|词性:名词

词源: "Boox" is a brand name for a series of e-readers produced by the company Onyx International.  It likely plays on the word "books" but stylizes it to evoke a more technological feel.  There's no deep etymological root beyond its connection to the word "books."
