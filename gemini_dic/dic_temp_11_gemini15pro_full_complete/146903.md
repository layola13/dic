overinstructed: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪd/
adjective
过度指导的; 指示过多的
given too many instructions or directions, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency.
The employees felt overinstructed and micromanaged, leading to decreased productivity.  员工们感觉被过度指导和微观管理，导致生产力下降。
The overinstructed child struggled to complete the simple task.  这个被过度指导的孩子很难完成简单的任务。
Synonyms: overmanaged, overdirected, micromanaged
Antonyms: underinstructed, unguided
词性: adjective
