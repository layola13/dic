busiest:/ˈbɪziɪst/ | adj. | 最忙的 | Superlative form of busy: most busy.  Having a great deal to do; occupied with many tasks or activities. | This is the busiest time of year for our company. 这是我们公司一年中最忙的时候。|  The airport is busiest during the holiday season.  机场在节假日最繁忙。 |  Near synonyms: most active, bustling, hectic  | Near antonyms: quietest, calmest, slowest | adjective
