lancing:/ˈlɑːnsɪŋ/| v. |刺破；穿刺；切开|(present participle of lance)  lance的现在分词|例句：He is lancing the infected area. 他正在切开感染的部位。


词源: From Middle English *launcen, from Anglo-Norman launcer, lancer (“to throw, hurl, launch, pierce”), from Vulgar Latin *lanceāre, from Latin lancea (“lance, spear”).

近义词: piercing, puncturing, incising, cutting
反义词: suturing, closing, sealing
词性:  Noun, Gerund or present participle
