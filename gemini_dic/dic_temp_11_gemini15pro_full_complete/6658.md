anaglyptography:/ˌænəˌɡlɪpˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |立体雕刻术，浮雕术 |The art or process of carving, chasing, or embossing in low relief.  A method of producing stereoscopic effects, especially in printing and photography, by superimposing two slightly offset images in contrasting colours, typically red and cyan, which are viewed through correspondingly coloured filters. | Anaglyptography was used to create 3D images long before the advent of modern technology. 立体雕刻术早在现代科技出现之前就被用来制作3D图像。|  Stereoscopy, embossing | Intaglio | noun
