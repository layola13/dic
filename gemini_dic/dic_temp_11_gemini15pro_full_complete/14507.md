avengers:/əˈvenʤərz/| n.|复仇者|Someone who takes vengeance. A person who exacts punishment for a wrong or injury, especially in the absence of legal retribution.|例句：The Avengers assembled to fight Thanos. 复仇者联盟集结起来对抗灭霸。

例句：They became avengers after their family was murdered.  在他们的家人被谋杀后，他们成为了复仇者。

近义词：vigilantes, retaliators|反义词:forgivers, pardoner|词性:noun
