harmonising:/ˈhɑːmənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使和谐；使协调；协调；相配 | Making different things go well together; being in agreement with other things. |

例句：
The government is harmonising its policies with those of its European partners. 政府正在使其政策与其欧洲伙伴的政策相协调。
They are working on harmonising the different legal systems. 他们正在努力协调不同的法律体系。

近义词： coordinating, unifying, integrating, synchronising, blending, merging
反义词： clashing, conflicting, disagreeing, jarring

词性: Verb (present participle of harmonise)
词源：From French *harmoniser*, from Late Latin *harmonizare*, from Ancient Greek *ἁρμονίζειν (harmonizein)*.
