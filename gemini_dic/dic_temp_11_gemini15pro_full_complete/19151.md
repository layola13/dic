bevyest:/ˈbevɪɪst/| adj.|最多的，最大的（一群）|This word is archaic and rarely used. It's a superlative form of "bevy" meaning the largest or most numerous group.  It describes the largest or most numerous bevy (group, especially of birds or women).|Example:  Of all the gatherings that season, the bevyest was the Duchess's ball.  那个季节的所有聚会中，人数最多的是公爵夫人的舞会。|近义词：largest, most numerous, greatest|反义词: smallest, fewest|词性:形容词

词源:
From "bevy" + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Bevy" itself has uncertain origins, possibly from the Anglo-French "bevee," meaning a drink or drinking company.
