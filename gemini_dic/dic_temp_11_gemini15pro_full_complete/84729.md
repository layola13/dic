handiwork:/ˈhændɪwɜːrk/| n.|手工, 手工艺品, 作品|Something made by hand; a product of someone's creative work.  The intricate carvings were clearly the handiwork of a master craftsman.|The beautiful quilt was her handiwork.|近义词:craft, workmanship, creation, product|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "machine-made")|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old English handgeweorc, from hand (hand) + geweorc (work).
