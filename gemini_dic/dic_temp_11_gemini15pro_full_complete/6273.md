amoralism:/eɪˈmɔːrəlɪzəm/
n.|非道德主义; 超道德; 道德虚无主义|The doctrine that moral distinctions are invalid or do not exist.  A doctrine that there is no moral order or basis for morality.| Some philosophers argue for amoralism, claiming that morality is a human construct with no objective basis. 一些哲学家支持非道德主义，声称道德是人类构建的，没有客观基础。|
近义词:unmorality, nihilism|
反义词:moralism|
词性:noun

amoral:/eɪˈmɔːrəl/
adj.|非道德的; 超道德的; 与道德无关的|Lacking a moral sense; unconcerned with the rightness or wrongness of something.|His amoral behavior shocked his colleagues. 他的非道德行为震惊了他的同事们.  The film presented an amoral view of the world. 这部电影呈现了一种非道德的世界观。|
近义词:unmoral, nonmoral, unethical|
反义词:moral, ethical|
词性:adjective
