mortuary: /ˈmɔːrtʃʊəri/ (US) /ˈmɔːtjʊəri/ (UK)| adj.| 死亡的，丧葬的|Relating to death or burial. |  The mortuary arrangements were handled by a local funeral director. 丧葬事宜由当地殡葬主管处理。 | Synonyms:  funereal, deathly | Antonyms: vital, living | adjective
