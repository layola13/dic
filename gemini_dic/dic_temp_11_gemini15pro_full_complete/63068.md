enrolment:/ɪnˈrəʊlmənt/ (UK) /ɪnˈroʊlmənt/ (US)|n.|登记；注册；入学；入会|The act of enrolling or being enrolled.  The number of people enrolled.|The university saw a sharp rise in enrolment this year. 今年大学的入学人数急剧上升.|近义词：registration, admission, enrollment (US spelling)|反义词：withdrawal, deregistration|词性:名词
enrol:/ɪnˈrəʊl/ (UK) /ɪnˈroʊl/ (US)|v.|登记；注册；招收；使入会|To officially register as a member of an institution or a student on a course.|I enrolled in an evening class to learn Spanish. 我报名参加了一个晚间西班牙语学习班。|近义词：register, sign up, join|反义词：withdraw, unregister|词性:动词
