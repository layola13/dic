disinfestation:/ˌdɪsɪnfɛˈsteɪʃən/
noun
清除害虫；灭虫
The act of ridding a person, place, or thing of insects or other pests.
The city undertook a disinfestation program to combat the rat problem.  该市开展了一项灭鼠计划以解决鼠患问题。
The refugees' clothes underwent disinfestation upon arrival.  难民的衣服在抵达时进行了消毒除虫。
