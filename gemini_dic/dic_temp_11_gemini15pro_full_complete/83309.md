gynno: /ˈɡɪnoʊ/ |  n. | 女人，女性 (Informal, sometimes offensive) |  A slang and often derogatory term for a woman or girl.  Derived from "gynecology," which relates to the female reproductive system. Its usage is generally considered disrespectful and should be avoided. |  "He called her a gynno, which was very rude." 他叫她“gynno”，这很粗鲁。 |  近义词: woman, girl, female (neutral), broad, chick (informal, potentially offensive) | 反义词: man, boy, male | 词性: noun
