abridgment:/əˈbrɪdʒmənt/| n.|缩短，删节，节略|A shortened version of a book, film, play, speech, etc.|例句：The abridgment of the novel made it more accessible to younger readers. 这部小说的删节本让年轻读者更容易理解。

例句：She read an abridgment of "War and Peace". 她读了《战争与和平》的缩写本。|近义词：condensation, summary, synopsis, abstract|反义词：expansion, elaboration, lengthening|词性:名词
