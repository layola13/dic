crustier:/ˈkrʌstiər/| adj.|更硬的，更易怒的|Comparative form of crusty: having a hard, crisp surface or being bad-tempered.|The bread was crustier than I expected. 这面包比我想象的要硬。|近义词：harder, firmer, grumpier, more irritable|反义词:softer, gentler, kinder|adjective

crustiness:/ˈkrʌstinəs/| n.|硬度，易怒|The quality or state of being crusty.|The crustiness of the old man's demeanor was intimidating. 老人的举止粗暴，令人胆怯。|近义词：hardness, firmness, grumpiness, irritability|反义词:softness, gentleness, kindness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crusted, from crust + -ed; akin to Old Norse kruptr (“crooked, bent”), from Proto-Germanic *kruptaz, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kreup- (“to curve, bend”). Related to creep.
