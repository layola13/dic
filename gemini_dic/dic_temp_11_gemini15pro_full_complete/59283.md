eaglestone:/ˈiːɡəlˌstoʊn/| n.|鹰石|A nodule of clay ironstone, often spherical and with a rough surface, found in some clays and shales.  It is believed to have been used as an amulet or charm.|He found an eaglestone on the beach and kept it as a good luck charm. 他在海滩上发现了一块鹰石，并把它作为好运符保存起来。|近义词:ironstone concretion|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源:
From 'eagle' + 'stone'.  The name derives from a folk belief that these stones were found in eagles' nests and had magical properties, especially in childbirth.
