manageable:/ˈmænɪdʒəbl/| adj.|易处理的，易管理的，可控的|Something that is manageable is easy to deal with or control. |The project is large but manageable. 这个项目很大，但是可控。|The workload is manageable. 工作量是可以承受的。|近义词：controllable, easy to handle, convenient|反义词: unmanageable, difficult, unwieldy|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From manage + -able.  "Manage" comes from the Italian *maneggiare* "to handle," especially horses, from *mano* "hand" from Latin *manus*.  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin meaning "capable of being."
