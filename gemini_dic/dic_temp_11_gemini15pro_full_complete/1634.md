acinaciform:/əˈsɪnəsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
弯刀形的，似弯刀的
Curved like a scimitar; shaped like a saber.
The acinaciform leaves of the eucalyptus tree are a distinctive feature.  桉树弯刀形的叶子是一个显著特征。
The ancient warrior carried an acinaciform blade.  古代武士携带一把弯刀形的刀刃。
近义词:scimitar-shaped, saber-shaped, falcate
反义词:straight, linear
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acinaces*, from Greek ἀκινάκης (*akinákēs*) (a short sword or dagger of Median or Persian origin), + -iform (meaning "having the form of").
