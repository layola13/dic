heil:/haɪl/
interj.|万岁；欢迎；祝贺|Used as a salutation or greeting, especially one associated with Nazi Germany.|Heil Hitler! (A historical example, not an endorsement.) 希特勒万岁！（一个历史例子，并非赞同。）
近义词:greet, salute
反义词:curse, condemn
词性:interjection


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *heil*, from Old High German *heil*, meaning "whole," "healthy," "fortunate," "prosperous."  Cognate with Old English *hāl* (whole, sound, healthy).  The use as a salutation developed from the wish for someone's good health and prosperity.
