engraved:/ɪnˈɡreɪvd/ | adj. | 雕刻的；铭记的 | Carved, etched, or impressed into a surface.  |  例句：The ring was engraved with their initials. 戒指上刻着他们的姓名首字母。 | 近义词：carved, etched, inscribed, imprinted | 反义词: plain, smooth | 词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French *engraver, from en- "in" + graver "to carve". Ultimately from Germanic *grabaną.
