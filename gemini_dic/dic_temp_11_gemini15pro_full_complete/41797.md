corsned:/ˈkɔːrsnɛd/| n. |神判面包，噎食面包|A piece of bread or cheese consecrated by a priest and given to a suspected person to swallow as a trial of guilt or innocence.  It was believed that if the person was guilty, they would choke on it.|例句：In medieval times, the corsned was used as a form of trial by ordeal.  在中世纪，神判面包被用作一种神判形式。

例句：The accused trembled as he attempted to swallow the corsned.  被告在试图吞下神判面包时颤抖着。|近义词：ordeal, trial|反义词: None.|词性: noun
