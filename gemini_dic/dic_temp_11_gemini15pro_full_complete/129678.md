non-destructive:/ˌnɑːn dɪˈstrʌktɪv/ | adj. | 非破坏性的 | Not causing damage or requiring that something be destroyed. |  They used non-destructive testing methods to check for cracks in the bridge. 他们使用非破坏性测试方法来检查桥梁是否有裂缝。
