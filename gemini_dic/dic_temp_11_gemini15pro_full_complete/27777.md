cannibalization:/ˌkænɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/
|n.|自相残杀，同类相食，（产品或服务）市场侵蚀|The process of a company's new product taking sales away from its existing products.  一个公司的新产品从其现有产品中夺走销售额的过程。|The introduction of the new model led to the cannibalization of sales of the older model. 推出新模型导致旧模型的销售额被蚕食。|market erosion, sales displacement|augmentation, synergy|noun
