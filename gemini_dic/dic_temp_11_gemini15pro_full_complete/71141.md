fishtail:/ˈfɪʃˌteɪl/ (US), /ˈfɪʃteɪl/ (UK)| n. v. |鱼尾；鱼尾状物；鱼尾形；摆动|A fishtail is the tail of a fish.  As a verb, it refers to the action of a vehicle's rear end sliding from side to side.|The car fishtailed on the icy road. 汽车在结冰的路上摆尾打滑。|近义词：swerve, skid, weave (as verbs)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider stabilize, straighten)|noun, verb
