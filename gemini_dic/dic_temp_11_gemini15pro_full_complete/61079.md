elliptically: /ɪˈlɪptɪkli/ | adv. | 椭圆地；省略地 | In a manner resembling an ellipse; with words omitted. | The Earth orbits the sun elliptically. 地球绕太阳椭圆运行。|  He spoke elliptically, leaving out important details. 他说话省略了很多，漏掉了重要的细节。 | 近义词: obliquely, indirectly | 反义词: directly, explicitly | 词性: 副词

elliptical: /ɪˈlɪptɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 椭圆的；省略的 | Having the form of an ellipse; omitting some words but easy to understand. | The elliptical shape of the stadium is impressive.  体育场的椭圆形令人印象深刻。 | His elliptical remarks left me confused. 他省略的话让我感到困惑。 | 近义词: oval, oblong; concise, terse | 反义词: circular, round; complete, detailed | 词性: 形形词

ellipse: /ɪˈlɪps/ | n. | 椭圆 | A regular oval shape, traced by a point moving in a plane so that the sum of its distances from two other points (the foci) is constant. | The comet follows an ellipse around the sun. 这颗彗星绕太阳运行的轨迹是椭圆形的。 | Draw an ellipse using a string and two pins. 用一根绳子和两个图钉画一个椭圆。 | 近义词: oval | 反义词: circle | 词性: 名词
