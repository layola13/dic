cleating: /ˈkliːtɪŋ/
noun
装夹板; 用夹板固定;  (尤指足球鞋的)鞋钉
The act of fastening or securing with cleats. Also refers to cleats themselves, especially on athletic shoes.
The workers were busy cleating the new deck. 工人们正忙着给新甲板装夹板。
The football player's cleats dug into the turf.  足球运动员的鞋钉扎进了草皮。
synonyms: fastening, securing, bolting, anchoring
antonyms: loosening, detaching, unfastening
n.
