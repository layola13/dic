excommunicable:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkəbl/
adjective
可逐出教会的；可被开除教籍的
Suitable or liable to be excommunicated.
例句：His heresy made him excommunicable. 他的异端邪说使他被逐出教会。

例句：The act was considered excommunicable in the eyes of the church.  在教会看来，这种行为是可以被开除教籍的。


近义词：banishable, ostracizable
反义词：acceptable, admissible
词性：adjective


excommunicate:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/
verb
逐出教会；开除教籍
Officially exclude (someone) from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
例句：He was excommunicated for refusing to obey the church's teachings. 他因拒绝服从教会的教义而被逐出教会。

例句：The priest excommunicated the heretic.  牧师将异教徒逐出教会。

近义词：banish, ostracize
反义词：accept, admit
词性：verb
