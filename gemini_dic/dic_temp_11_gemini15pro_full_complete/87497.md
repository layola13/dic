heterochlamydeous:/ˌhɛtərəkləˈmɪdiəs/
adjective
异被的
Having a perianth differentiated into calyx and corolla.  The perianth is the non-reproductive part of the flower, and in heterochlamydeous flowers, it's clearly divided into the sepals (calyx) and petals (corolla).
The rose is a heterochlamydeous flower, with distinct green sepals and colorful petals.  玫瑰是一种异被花，有明显的绿色萼片和彩色花瓣。
Synonym: dichlamydeous
Antonym: homochlamydeous, monochlamydeous
