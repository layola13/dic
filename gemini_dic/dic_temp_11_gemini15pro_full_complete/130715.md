nonalkaloid:/ˌnɒnˈælkəlɔɪd/
adjective
非生物碱的
Not being or containing an alkaloid; specifically : of, relating to, or being a plant constituent other than an alkaloid.
Herbal teas contain many nonalkaloid constituents.  草药茶含有许多非生物碱成分。
Near synonyms: nonalkaloidal
Antonyms: alkaloid, alkaloidal
词性: adjective
