garba:/ˈɡɑːrbə/| n.|伽尔巴舞，一种古吉拉特邦的民间舞蹈|A folk dance and music style from Gujarat, India, often performed during the nine-day Navratri festival.|例句：The vibrant colors and rhythmic movements of garba are captivating. 伽尔巴舞充满活力的色彩和节奏感十足的动作令人着迷。

例句：During Navratri, people gather in large numbers to perform garba. 在九夜节期间，人们聚集在一起表演伽尔巴舞。|近义词：dance, folk dance|反义词:|noun
