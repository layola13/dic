gelatiniform:/dʒɪˈlætɪnɪfɔːrm/
adj.
胶状的，似凝胶的
Resembling gelatin; jelly-like.
The substance had a gelatiniform appearance, jiggling slightly when the container was moved.  这种物质呈胶状，容器移动时会轻微晃动。
The bacterial colony exhibited a gelatiniform consistency.  这个细菌菌落呈现出胶状的稠度。
近义词:gelatinous, jelly-like, colloidal
反义词:solid, firm, rigid
词性:形容词
词源:gelatin(凝胶)+ -iform (形状的，像…的)  From Latin *gelātīniformis, from gelātīna (“jelly, gelatin”) + -formis.
