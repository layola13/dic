deforesting:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈɛstɪŋ/| n./v.|砍伐森林，森林开伐|The act of clearing forests by cutting down trees.|Deforesting the Amazon rainforest has devastating consequences for the global climate.  亚马逊雨林的砍伐对全球气候造成了毁灭性的后果。|近义词：logging, clear-cutting|反义词：reforestation, afforestation|词性：动名词/及物动词
Etymology: de- (Latin prefix meaning "down from, away from, off") + forest + -ing (present participle suffix)
