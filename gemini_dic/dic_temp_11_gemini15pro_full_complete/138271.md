notables:/ˈnəʊtəblz/| n.| 显要人物；名流；值得注意的人（或事物）|People of high social standing or people who are famous for their achievements.  These can also refer to noteworthy things.|The guest list included several notables from the world of finance.|近义词： dignitaries, luminaries, VIPs, celebrities, worthies |反义词: unknowns, nonentities|Noun

notable:/ˈnəʊtəb(ə)l/| adj.| 值得注意的；显著的；著名的|Something noteworthy, remarkable, or important.|The city is notable for its beautiful architecture.|近义词： remarkable, noteworthy, significant, prominent, outstanding, distinguished |反义词: unremarkable, insignificant, obscure|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French notable, from Latin notabilis "remarkable," from notare "to mark, note," from nota "mark, sign, letter."
