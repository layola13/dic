disruptiveness:/dɪsˈrʌptɪvnəs/| n. |破坏性，扰乱性|The state or quality of being disruptive; causing disorder or interruption.|The disruptiveness of the new technology changed the entire industry.  这种新技术的破坏性改变了整个行业。|Synonyms:  disorder, chaos, turbulence, upheaval, interruption, disturbance|Antonyms: order, stability, calmness, peacefulness|noun


disruptiveness:/dɪsˈrʌptɪvnəs/| n. |破坏性，扰乱性|The inherent tendency to cause disruption or upheaval.|The disruptiveness of social media is a subject of ongoing debate. 社交媒体的破坏性是一个持续争论的话题。|Synonyms: volatility, instability,  unpredictability|Antonyms:  predictability, stability,  consistency|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From disruptive + -ness.  Disruptive comes from disrupt + -ive. Disrupt comes from the Latin disrumpere "to break apart," from dis- "apart" + rumpere "to break."
