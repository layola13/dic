kyushu:/ˈkjuːʃuː/
n.|九州|Kyushu is the third largest of Japan's five main islands. 九州是日本五大岛屿中的第三大岛。
|Kyushu is known for its hot springs and active volcanoes. 九州以其温泉和活火山而闻名。
