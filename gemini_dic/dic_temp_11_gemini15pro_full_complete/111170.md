lithodomous:/lɪˈθɒdəməs/
adjective
凿石而居的，在岩石中生活的
Living in burrows or cavities that they excavate in rock.
Piddocks are lithodomous bivalves that bore into rocks.  石蛏是凿石而居的双壳类动物，它们会钻入岩石中。
Lithodomous mussels secrete acid to bore into rocks. 凿石贻贝会分泌酸性物质来钻入岩石。
近义词:rock-boring, rock-dwelling
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "free-swimming" or "pelagic" for animals that don't live in rocks)
词性:adjective
