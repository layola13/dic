judaism: /ˈdʒuːdeɪɪzəm/
n.
犹太教
The monotheistic religion of the Jewish people, tracing its origins to Abraham and emphasizing belief in one God and obedience to his will, especially as revealed to Moses.
Judaism is one of the oldest monotheistic religions. 犹太教是最古老的一神教之一。
My friend practices Judaism. 我的朋友信奉犹太教。
近义词: Jewish faith, Hebrew religion
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a religion, could list other religions like Christianity, Islam, etc. but not recommended as it implies opposition)
词性: noun
