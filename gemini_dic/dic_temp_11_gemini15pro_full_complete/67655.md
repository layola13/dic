extrovert: /ˈɛkstrəvɜːrt/ (US) /ˈɛkstrəvɜːt/ (UK)| n. adj.|外向的人；性格外向的|A person who is energized by being around other people, enjoys social interaction, and is often outgoing and enthusiastic.|She's a real extrovert and loves being the center of attention. 她是一个真正的外向者，喜欢成为关注的焦点。|近义词: outgoing, sociable, gregarious|反义词: introvert, shy, reserved|词性:名词，形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *extravertere* "to turn outwards," from *extra-* "outside" + *vertere* "to turn."  Coined by Carl Jung.
