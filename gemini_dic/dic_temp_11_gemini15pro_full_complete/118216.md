mera:/ˈmɛrə/ |pron.|我的 (Hindi/Urdu)| "Mera" is a possessive pronoun in Hindi and Urdu, meaning "my" or "mine." It is used to indicate ownership or possession.  It agrees with the gender and number of the noun it modifies.| Mera ghar bada hai. (My house is big.) | My, mine |Your, his, her, their, our |pronoun
