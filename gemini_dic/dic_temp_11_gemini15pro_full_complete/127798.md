neighbour:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə/ (UK)| n.|邻居；邻国；邻近的人或物|A person who lives next door or very near to you, or a country that shares a border with another.|例句：My neighbours are very friendly. 我的邻居们很友好。|近义词：resident, dweller|反义词:stranger, foreigner|noun

neighbour:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə/ (UK)| v.|邻接；靠近|To live or be situated next to or very near to.|例句：Our countries neighbour each other. 我们的国家彼此相邻。|近义词：adjoin, border|反义词: be distant from|verb
