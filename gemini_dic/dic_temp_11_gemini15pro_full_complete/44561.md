crotchety: /ˈkrɒtʃɪti/ (US), /ˈkrɒtʃɪti/ (UK) | adj. | 脾气暴躁的，易怒的 | Bad-tempered; easily irritated, especially by small annoyances. |  My grandfather gets very crotchety when he's tired. 我祖父累了的时候脾气会很暴躁。

The old woman became increasingly crotchety as the day wore on.  随着时间的推移，这位老妇人变得越来越暴躁。

近义词: grumpy, irritable, cantankerous, bad-tempered, irascible

反义词: good-natured, cheerful, amiable, pleasant

词性: adjective

词源:  From the earlier 'crotchet', meaning a whim or fancy, possibly influenced by 'crutch', suggesting frailty or irritability associated with old age.
