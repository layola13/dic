ovum:/ˈoʊvəm/ | n. | 卵子；卵细胞 | A mature female reproductive cell, especially of a human or other animal, that can divide to give rise to an embryo usually only after fertilization by a male cell. | The ovum is released from the ovary during ovulation. 卵子在排卵期从卵巢中排出。|  The fertilized ovum develops into a fetus. 受精卵发育成胎儿。 | synonyms: egg cell, gamete, oocyte | antonyms: sperm, spermatozoon | noun
