bidirectional:/ˌbaɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəl/
adj.
双向的
Operating or moving in two directions.
This street is bidirectional. 这条街是双向的。
The software supports bidirectional communication.  这个软件支持双向通信。
近义词: two-way, reciprocal
反义词: unidirectional, one-way
词性: adjective
词源: bi- (two) + directional (of or pertaining to direction)
