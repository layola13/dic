coccyx:/ˈkɒksɪks/ (UK) /ˈkɑːksɪks/ (US)| n. |尾骨|The small triangular bone at the base of the spinal column in humans and some apes, formed of fused vestigial vertebrae.  It is a remnant of a tail.|After her fall, she experienced persistent pain in her coccyx.|Synonym: tailbone|Antonym: (No direct antonym exists as it's an anatomical feature)|noun
