antiquated:/ˈæntɪˌkweɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过时的，陈旧的 | Old-fashioned and no longer suitable for modern needs or conditions; obsolete. |  Many of their business practices are now antiquated. 他们的许多商业惯例现在已经过时了。| The museum displayed an antiquated printing press.  博物馆展出了一台老式的印刷机。| 近义词：obsolete, outdated, archaic, old-fashioned | 反义词：modern, contemporary, current, up-to-date | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin *antiquatus, past participle of antiquare ‘make old’, from antiquus ‘old’.
