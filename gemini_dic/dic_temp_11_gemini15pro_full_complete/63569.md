enviousnesses:/ˈɛnviəsnɪsɪz/| n. |嫉妒，羡慕|The state of being envious; a feeling of discontent or resentment aroused by and desiring another's advantages, possessions, or qualities.|His enviousnesses of his brother's success was palpable. 他对他兄弟成功的嫉妒显而易见。|近义词：jealousy, envy, covetousness|反义词：admiration, generosity, goodwill|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from envious + -ness.
