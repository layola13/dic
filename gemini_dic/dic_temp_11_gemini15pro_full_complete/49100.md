deifying:/ˈdiːɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
神化；奉若神明
treating someone or something as a god.
The fans were deifying their favorite singer after the concert.  演唱会后，歌迷们把他们最喜欢的歌手奉若神明。
The ancient Egyptians deified many animals. 古埃及人神化了许多动物。

近义词: idolizing, worshipping, exalting, glorifying
反义词: denigrating, belittling, vilifying

词性: verb (present participle of deify)
词源: From Late Latin deificare, from Latin deus (“god”) + -ficare (“-fy”).
