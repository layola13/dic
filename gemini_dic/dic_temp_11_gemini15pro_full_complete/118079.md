mendole: /mɛnˈdoʊli/ |  n. | 曼陀林 | A musical instrument of the lute family with a pear-shaped body. |  He played a beautiful melody on his mendole. 他用他的曼陀林演奏了一曲优美的旋律。 | synonyms: mandolin | antonyms: None | noun


Etymology: From Italian *mandolino*, diminutive of *mandola* "almond," referring to the instrument's shape.
