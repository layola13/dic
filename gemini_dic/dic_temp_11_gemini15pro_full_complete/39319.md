Coniston: /ˈkɒnɪstən/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnɪstən/ (US)| n.|康尼斯顿（英国坎布里亚郡的一个村庄和湖泊）|Coniston is a village and lake in the Furness area of Cumbria, England. It is particularly famous for its association with Donald Campbell, who died on Coniston Water in 1967 while attempting to break the world water speed record.|例句：We spent a lovely week holidaying in Coniston. 我们在康尼斯顿度过了一个美好的假期。
