cetacea:/sɪˈteɪʃə/ | n. |鲸目动物|An order of marine mammals that includes whales, dolphins, and porpoises. They are characterized by their streamlined bodies, horizontal tail flukes, and blowholes for breathing.|例句：Cetacea are highly intelligent marine mammals. 鲸目动物是高度智能的海洋哺乳动物。

例句：The blue whale is the largest animal in the Cetacea order. 蓝鲸是鲸目中最大的动物。|近义词：whales, dolphins, porpoises|反义词:None|词性:Noun
Etymology: From Latin *cētus*, from Ancient Greek κῆτος (kêtos, “whale, sea monster”).
