millennialist:/ˌmɪˈlɛniəlɪst/ | n. adj. | 千禧年主义者；千禧年主义的 | A person who believes in millennialism, the doctrine of the millennium (a period of a thousand years of peace and prosperity).  A millennialist can also be used as an adjective to describe something related to this belief. |  Example: The millennialist preacher predicted the end of the world.  这位千禧年主义的传教士预言了世界末日。| Synonym: chiliast, premillennialist, postmillennialist | Antonym: secularist | Noun, Adjective

millenarian:/ˌmɪˈlɛnəriən/ | n. adj. | 千禧年主义者；千禧年主义的 |  A person who believes in millenarianism, the doctrine of the millennium (a period of a thousand years of peace and prosperity).  Millenarian can also be used as an adjective to describe something related to this belief. | Example: The preacher's millenarian prophecies attracted a large following.  这位传教士的千禧年预言吸引了大批追随者。 | Synonym: chiliast, premillennialist, postmillennialist | Antonym: secularist | Noun, Adjective
