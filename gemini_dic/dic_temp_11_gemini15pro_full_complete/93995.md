impairs:/ɪmˈpɛəz/| v. |损害，削弱，降低|To weaken or damage something, making it less effective or efficient.|例句：Alcohol impairs your judgment.  酒精会损害你的判断力。

例句：Lack of sleep impairs the immune system. 缺乏睡眠会削弱免疫系统。

近义词：damage, harm, weaken, undermine, compromise, deteriorate|反义词:improve, enhance, strengthen, boost|词性:动词
