nationalised: /ˈnæʃnəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 国有化的 | Converted from private to state ownership or control.  |  The government nationalised the railway system. 政府将铁路系统国有化了。|  The coal industry was nationalised in 1947. 煤炭行业在1947年被国有化了。 | Synonyms: state-owned, public, socialized | Antonyms: privatized, private, denationalised | adjective


nationalise: /ˈnæʃnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 国有化 | To transfer (a major branch of industry or commerce) from private to state ownership or control. | The government plans to nationalise the banks. 政府计划将银行国有化。 |  Many countries nationalised their airlines after World War II.  二战后许多国家将航空公司国有化。 | Synonyms: socialize, state-own, take over | Antonyms: privatize, denationalize, deregulate | verb

Etymology:
From nation + -al + -ize.  "Nation" comes from Old French "nacion"  ultimately from Latin "natio" (birth, race).  "-al" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns. "-ize" is a suffix meaning "to make or become."
