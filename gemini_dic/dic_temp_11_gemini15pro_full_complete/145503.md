overcloyed:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪd/
adjective
过分满足的，腻烦的
excessively satisfied or filled, especially with rich or sweet things; surfeited
The guests were overcloyed with rich food and drink. 客人们对丰盛的酒菜感到腻烦了。
近义词:satiated, surfeited, jaded
反义词:unsatisfied, hungry, eager
词性:adjective

overcloyed:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪd/
verb (past participle)
使过分满足，使腻烦
(past participle of overcloy) to supply with something to excess, especially something rich or sweet, so as to cause disgust or satiety.
He was overcloyed by the sweetness of the desserts. 他被甜点齁到了。
近义词:satiate, glut, surfeit
反义词:deprive, starve, crave
词性:verb (past participle)
