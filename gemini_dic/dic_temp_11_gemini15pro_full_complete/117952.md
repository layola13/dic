membranous:/ˈmɛmbrənəs/| adj.|膜的；膜状的|Relating to, consisting of, or resembling a membrane.  A thin, pliable sheet of tissue that covers or lines a part of the body.|The lungs are covered by a membranous sac called the pleura. 肺被一个称为胸膜的膜状囊覆盖着。|The butterfly's wings are membranous and delicate. 蝴蝶的翅膀是膜状的，很脆弱。|近义词：membranaceous, filmy, thin|反义词:thick, solid|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *membranosus, from Latin membrana (“membrane”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of, abounding in').
