cc'ing:/ˈsiːˌsiːɪŋ/ |v. |抄送|Present participle of "cc," which stands for "carbon copy" and refers to sending a copy of an email or letter to someone other than the primary recipient.  It's now mostly used in electronic communication.|I'm cc'ing John on this email so he's aware of the situation. 我把这封邮件抄送给约翰，让他知道情况。|近义词:copying, including, sending a copy to|反义词:excluding, omitting|词性:动词
