honking:/ˈhɒŋkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːŋkɪŋ/ (US)| n.  v. |鸣笛声，按喇叭|The sound a car horn makes.  The act of pressing a car horn.|
The honking of cars in the city is constant. 城市里汽车的喇叭声不断。
He was honking his horn at the slow driver. 他对那个开慢车的司机按喇叭。
近义词：beeping, tooting|反义词: silence|词性: noun, verb

honking:/ˈhɒŋkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːŋkɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |响亮的，刺耳的 (informal, often used to describe a large size or amount)|Loud or harsh; very large.|
We had a honking good time at the party. 我们在派对上玩得很开心。（指玩得非常尽兴）
They gave us a honking big portion of fries. 他们给了我们一大份薯条。(指份量很大)
近义词：huge, enormous, loud|反义词: small, quiet|词性: adjective
