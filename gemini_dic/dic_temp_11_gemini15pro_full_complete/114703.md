mallards:/ˈmælərdz/
n.
野鸭（复数）
A common wild duck (Anas platyrhynchos) of the Northern Hemisphere, the male of which has a glossy green head and white neck ring.
例句：A flock of mallards landed on the pond. 一群野鸭落在了池塘上。

例句：The mallards were dabbing in the shallows for food.  野鸭在浅水处觅食。

近义词：ducks, wild ducks
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
