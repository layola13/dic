anastaltic:/ˌænəˈstɔːltɪk/
adjective
止血的，收缩血管的
Having the property of contracting tissues or blood vessels; styptic.
This medicine has anastaltic properties, helping to stop bleeding quickly. 这种药具有止血特性，有助于快速止血。
synonyms: styptic, hemostatic, astringent
antonyms: vasodilator, haemorrhagic
词性: adjective
