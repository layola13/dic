emporia:/ɛmˈpɔːriə/
|n.| 商业中心，商场，集市|A place of trade, especially one with a wide variety of goods; a marketplace or shopping center.|The city became a thriving emporia for merchants from all over the world. 这座城市成为了来自世界各地商人的繁荣商业中心。|synonyms: marketplace, bazaar, mart, trading center|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider something like "ghost town" or "deserted area")|noun
emporia:/ɛmˈpɔːriə/
|n.|  (尤指某种商品的) 大商店，大公司|A large store or business, especially one dealing in a particular commodity.|The company owns a chain of furniture emporia across the country. 该公司在全国拥有一系列家具大商店。|synonyms: store, shop, retailer, outlet|antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "small business" or "independent store")|noun
