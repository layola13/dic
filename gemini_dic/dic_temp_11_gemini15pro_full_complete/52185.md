diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US) /ˌdaɪəˈrɪə/ (UK)| n. |腹泻|An abnormal, frequent discharge of fluid fecal matter from the bowels.|例句：He had a bad case of diarrhea after eating contaminated food. 他吃了受污染的食物后腹泻严重。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with diarrhea and prescribed medication. 医生诊断她腹泻并开了药。|近义词：the runs, loose bowels, the trots|反义词: constipation|词性:noun

diarrhoea (British English spelling):/ˌdaɪəˈrɪə/ | n. |腹泻|Same as diarrhea.|例句：She suffered from diarrhoea during her trip abroad. 她在国外旅行期间腹泻。

例句：The child's diarrhoea was a cause for concern.  孩子的腹泻令人担忧。|近义词：the runs, loose bowels, the trots|反义词: constipation|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diarrhoia, from Greek diarrhoia "a flowing through," from diarrhein "to flow through," from dia- "through" + rhein "to flow."
