nearish:/ˈnɪərɪʃ/| adj.|较近的，附近的；大约|Relatively near, but not very close; approximately.|例句：The shop is nearish - just a ten-minute walk.  这家商店比较近——步行只需十分钟。|近义词：nearby, close, approximately, sort of, kind of|反义词:far, distant|词性:形容词
Etymology: A combination of "near" and the suffix "-ish," which indicates approximation or resemblance.
