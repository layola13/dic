beclip:/bɪˈklɪp/ | vt. | 修剪，剪短 | To cut short or trim, especially with clippers or scissors. |  The gardener beclipped the hedges. 园丁修剪了树篱。

The barber beclipped his hair too short. 理发师把他的头发剪得太短了。 | 近义词: trim, clip, prune, shear | 反义词: lengthen, extend | 词性: 动词


词源:  A combination of the prefix "be-" (meaning "to make" or "to cause to be") and "clip" (meaning to cut or trim).  "Be-" prefixes are relatively rare in modern English but were more common in older forms of the language.
