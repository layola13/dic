cosh:/kɒʃ/ (UK), /kɑːʃ/ (US)| n.|双曲余弦|Hyperbolic cosine, a mathematical function that is the average of the exponential function and its reciprocal. It is defined as cosh(x) = (e^x + e^-x) / 2.|The cable hung in the shape of a hyperbolic cosine curve, also known as a catenary. |该电缆悬挂成双曲线余弦曲线的形状，也称为悬链线。|近义词: (No direct synonym, but related to exponential and trigonometric functions)|反义词: None|词性: Noun (primarily), can also function as a mathematical function.
