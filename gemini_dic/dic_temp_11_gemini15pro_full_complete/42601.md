counterprotest:/ˈkaʊntərˌproʊtest/ /ˈkaʊntəˌprəʊtest/| n. vi. |反抗议，对立抗议|An organized public demonstration expressing disagreement with another demonstration.|例句：A counterprotest was staged by the opposing political party.  反对党举行了反示威活动。

例句：They organized a counterprotest to voice their disagreement with the new policy. 他们组织了一场反抗议活动，以表达他们对新政策的不满。|近义词：demonstration, protest, rally|反义词:support, agreement|词性:名词, 动词
