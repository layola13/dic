beset:/bɪˈset/| v. |困扰，包围|To trouble or threaten persistently.|例句：The expedition was beset by storms and dwindling supplies. 探险队受暴风雨和日渐减少的补给困扰。|近义词：trouble, harass, plague, beleaguer, besiege|反义词:aid, assist, help|动词

beset:/bɪˈset/| adj. |镶嵌的，装饰的|(of a precious metal object) studded with jewels.|例句：A gold crown beset with diamonds. 镶嵌着钻石的金冠。|近义词：studded, inlaid, ornamented|反义词:plain, bare|形容词
