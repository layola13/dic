centauric: /sɛnˈtɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 人马座的；半人马的 | Relating to or resembling a centaur, a mythical creature with the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse. |  例句：The centauric figure in the painting was both majestic and terrifying.  画中的人马形象既雄伟又可怕。

例句：She imagined a centauric race inhabiting a distant planet. 她想象着一个人马种族居住在一个遥远的星球上。 | 近义词：mythological, chimerical | 反义词: real, actual | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *Centauricus, from Centaurus (the constellation Centaurus and the centaurs), from Greek Kéntauros.
