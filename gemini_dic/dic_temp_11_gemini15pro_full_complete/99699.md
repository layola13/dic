interlock:/ˌɪntərˈlɒk/ (British), /ˌɪntərˈlɑːk/ (American)| n. v.|连锁，互锁；联锁装置|A device or system that prevents a machine from operating unless certain conditions are met.  It can also refer to the act of joining or fitting things together closely.|
The safety interlock prevented the machine from starting while the guard was open. 安全联锁装置防止机器在防护装置打开时启动。
The bricks were laid in an interlock pattern.  这些砖块以互锁的样式铺设。|
Synonyms: link, connect, join, mesh, engage|
Antonyms: separate, disconnect, disengage|
noun, verb


interlocks:/ˌɪntərˈlɒks/ (British), /ˌɪntərˈlɑːk/ (American)| n. pl.|连锁装置，互锁装置|Plural form of interlock, referring to multiple devices or systems of interlocking.|
The complex machine had numerous safety interlocks. 这台复杂的机器有许多安全联锁装置。
These interlocks ensure the system operates safely. 这些联锁装置确保系统安全运行。|
Synonyms: connections, links, couplings|
Antonyms: separations, disconnections|
