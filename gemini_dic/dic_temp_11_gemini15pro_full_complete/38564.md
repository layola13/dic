concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ /kɑːnˈkɑːkt/ | v. |调制，配制，捏造 |To create or devise something, especially in a skillful or ingenious way; to fabricate or invent (a story, an excuse, etc.). | He concocted a delicious soup from leftover vegetables. 他用剩下的蔬菜调制了一碗美味的汤。| devise, invent, fabricate | destroy, dismantle | verb
