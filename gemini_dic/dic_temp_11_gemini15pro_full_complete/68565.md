fangless:/ˈfæŋləs/| adj.|无牙的，没有尖牙的|Lacking fangs; having no fangs.  It describes something or someone that normally has fangs, but doesn't.  It implies vulnerability or harmlessness.|The fangless snake was harmless.  这条无牙的蛇是无害的。|Synonyms: toothless, harmless, defenseless|Antonyms: fanged, toothed, dangerous|adjective

fangless:/ˈfæŋləs/| adj.|无力的，没有威慑力的|Lacking power or effectiveness; unable to inflict harm or enforce authority. Figuratively used to describe something that has lost its power or threat.|The fangless legislation failed to deter crime. 这项毫无威慑力的立法未能阻止犯罪。|Synonyms: powerless, ineffective, weak|Antonyms: powerful, effective, strong|adjective
