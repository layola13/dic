cross-infection:/ˌkrɒs ɪnˈfɛkʃən/ /ˌkrɑːs ɪnˈfɛkʃən/| n. |交叉感染|The transmission of a different infection from one person to another, especially in a hospital.|例句：Strict hygiene measures are necessary to prevent cross-infection in hospitals.  医院需要严格的卫生措施来防止交叉感染。

例句：The ward was closed due to a risk of cross-infection.  由于交叉感染的风险，病房被关闭了。|近义词：secondary infection, nosocomial infection|反义词:|词性:noun
