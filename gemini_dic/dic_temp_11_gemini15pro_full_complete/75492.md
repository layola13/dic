fruticant:/ˈfruːtɪkənt/| adj.|灌木状的；丛生的；结果实的|Resembling a shrub; shrubby; fruiting.|例句：The fruticant plant was covered in small berries. 灌木状的植物上结满了小浆果。

例句：The hillside was covered in fruticant vegetation. 山坡上覆盖着灌木丛生的植被。|近义词：shrubby, bushy, fruiting|反义词: herbaceous, non-fruiting|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *fruticans*, present participle of *fruticare* "to put forth shoots, sprout," from *frutex* "shrub, bush."
