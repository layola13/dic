anemically:/əˈniːmɪkli/ | adv. | 贫血地；无力地；缺乏活力地 | In a way that suggests anemia; lacking vigor or energy. |  The stock market performed anemically last quarter. 股市上个季度表现疲软。| Synonyms: weakly, feebly, listlessly | Antonyms: robustly, strongly, vigorously | adverb

Etymology: From anemia + -ically.  Anemia comes from Greek *anaimia* meaning "lack of blood," from *an-* "without" + *haima* "blood."
