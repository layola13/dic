goody-goody:/ˈɡʊdi ˈɡʊdi/| adj. n.|伪善的，装腔作势的；伪善者|Affecting or characterized by exaggerated virtue or piety, often sanctimonious and hypocritical.  A person who behaves in this way.|例句：He's such a goody-goody, always telling everyone how good he is. 他真是个伪君子，总是告诉大家他有多好。

例句：She avoided the goody-goodies in her class. 她避开了班上那些伪善的人。|近义词：sanctimonious, hypocritical, pious, self-righteous|反义词：bad, wicked, sinful, rebellious|词性:形容词，名词

goody-goodiness:/ˈɡʊdi ˈɡʊdinɪs/| n.|伪善，假正经|The quality or state of being goody-goody; sanctimoniousness or hypocrisy.|例句：Her goody-goodiness made her unpopular. 她的假正经让她不受欢迎。

例句：He couldn't stand the goody-goodiness of some of his colleagues. 他无法忍受一些同事的伪善。|近义词：sanctimony, hypocrisy, piety, self-righteousness|反义词：wickedness, sinfulness, rebellion|词性:名词
