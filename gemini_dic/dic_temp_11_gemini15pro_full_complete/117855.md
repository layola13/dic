mellowspeak:/ˈmɛloʊspiːk/
adjective
温和的说话方式；舒缓的语气
Speaking in a relaxed, soft, or soothing manner.
He used his mellowspeak to calm the frightened child. 他用温和的语气来安抚受惊的孩子。
近义词:soft-spoken, gentle, soothing, calm, peaceful
反义词:harsh, aggressive, strident, loud, boisterous
词性:adjective

Etymology:  A blend of "mellow" (meaning pleasantly smooth or soft) and "speak".  "Mellow" ultimately derives from Old English "mearu" meaning "soft, tender."
