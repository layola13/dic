kyriology: /ˌkɪriˈɒlədʒi/ /ˌkɪriˈɑːlədʒi/
kyriology| n. |主权论，对主权的研究|The study of lordship, dominion, or sovereignty.  It explores the nature, basis, and exercise of supreme power, whether in political, religious, or social contexts.|He's writing a dissertation on kyriology, focusing on the divine right of kings.| 近义词: sovereignty, dominion, lordship | 反义词: subjection, servitude | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek κύριος (kýrios, “lord, master”) + -logy.
