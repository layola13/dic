mishnic: /ˈmɪʃnɪk/ | adj. | 密西拿的，与密西拿犹太法典相关的 | Relating to the Mishnah, a written collection of the Jewish oral traditions. |  The rabbi gave a mishnic interpretation of the passage. 拉比对这段文字进行了密西拿式的解释。 |  The scholar dedicated his life to mishnic studies.  这位学者毕生致力于密西拿研究。 | 近义词: Mishnaic, Talmudic (in a broader sense) | 反义词:  Biblical (in a narrower sense), secular | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Hebrew Mishnah (משנה), meaning "repetition" or "study," referring to the oral law as it was repeated and studied.
