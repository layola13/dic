metachronism:/mɛˈtækrənɪzəm/ | n. |异步性，时间差|The sequential beating of cilia, as in paramecium, or the sequential contraction of body segments in some worms, in which the movements follow a wavelike progression.|The metachronism of the cilia created a current in the water. 纤毛的异步性在水中产生了一股水流。| 近义词:phase shift, time lag, asynchrony | 反义词:synchronism, synchrony | noun

metachronal:/mɛˈtækrənəl/ | adj. |异步的，时间差的| Relating to or exhibiting metachronism.|The metachronal wave of the cilia propelled the organism forward. 纤毛的异步波推动生物体前进。| 近义词:asynchronous, out of phase | 反义词:synchronous, in phase | adjective
