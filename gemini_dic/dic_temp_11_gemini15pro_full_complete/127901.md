neoliberal:/ˌniːoʊˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. n. | 新自由主义的；新自由主义者 | Relating to or denoting a modified form of liberalism tending to favor free-market capitalism. |  Many economists are now convinced that the neoliberal policies of the 1990s caused more harm than good. 许多经济学家现在确信，20世纪90年代的新自由主义政策弊大于利。|  The neoliberal agenda includes deregulation and privatization. 新自由主义的议程包括放松管制和私有化。 | Synonyms:  free-market, laissez-faire, capitalist | Antonyms: socialist, interventionist | noun, adjective

neoliberals:/ˌniːoʊˈlɪbərəlz/ | n. | 新自由主义者 |  People who support neoliberal policies. |  Neoliberals advocate for deregulation of industries. 新自由主义者主张放松对工业的管制。 | The neoliberals believe that the free market is the best way to allocate resources. 新自由主义者认为自由市场是配置资源的最佳方式。 | Synonyms: free marketeers,  deregulators | Antonyms: socialists, interventionists | noun
