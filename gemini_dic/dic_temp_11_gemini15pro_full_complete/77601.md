geeking:/ˈɡiːkɪŋ/| v. n.|沉迷于，痴迷于；像个极客一样|Acting like a geek; engaging in geeky activities.  Derived from "geek," meaning a person with an obsessive interest in a particular area, often technology or a specific hobby.|例句：He was geeking out over his new computer. 他对他的新电脑着迷不已。|近义词：obsessing, nerding out, indulging, enthusing|反义词:disliking, ignoring|词性:动词/名词
