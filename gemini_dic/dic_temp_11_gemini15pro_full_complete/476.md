abnegation:/ˌæbnɪˈɡeɪʃn/
n.
放弃，克制；拒绝；自制
the act of renouncing or rejecting something.
She demonstrated great abnegation by giving up her career to care for her sick mother. 她为了照顾生病的母亲放弃了自己的事业，表现出了极大的克己精神。
Synonyms: renunciation, rejection, sacrifice, self-denial, forgoing
Antonyms: acceptance, indulgence, gratification
noun

abnegations:/ˌæbnɪˈɡeɪʃnz/
n.
放弃，克制；拒绝；自制（复数）
plural form of abnegation: acts of renouncing or rejecting something.
His life was marked by numerous abnegations of worldly pleasures. 他的一生以多次放弃世俗享乐为特征。
Synonyms: renunciations, rejections, sacrifices, self-denials
Antonyms: acceptances, indulgences, gratifications
noun
