agrappes:/əˈɡrɑːps/| adj.|成串的，一簇簇的|Bunches of grapes hanging from the vine.  葡萄藤上挂着一串串葡萄。|例句：The grapes were agrappes on the vine. 葡萄成串地挂在藤上。

例句：The currants were agrappes, glistening in the sun.  醋栗成串地挂着，在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：clustered, bunched|反义词:scattered, dispersed|词性:形容词
词源: From French *agrappé*, past participle of *agrapper* "to fasten with a hook or clasp," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) + *grappe* "bunch of grapes, hook, clasp."  Ultimately from Frankish *krappa* "hook."
用法说明:This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.  It is most likely to be encountered in older texts or in specialized contexts related to viticulture or botany.  It describes fruit, particularly grapes, growing in bunches or clusters.
