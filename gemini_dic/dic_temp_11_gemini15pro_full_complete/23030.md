boulevard:/ˈbuːləvɑːrd/ (US), /ˈbuːlvɑːd/ (UK)| n.|林荫大道，大街|A wide, often tree-lined street, typically one that is considered an important thoroughfare.|例句：We strolled along the boulevard, enjoying the vibrant atmosphere. 我们沿着林荫大道漫步，享受着充满活力的氛围。

例句：The boulevard was lined with shops and cafes. 这条大道两旁林立着商店和咖啡馆。


近义词：avenue, street, thoroughfare|反义词:alley, lane|词性:noun
