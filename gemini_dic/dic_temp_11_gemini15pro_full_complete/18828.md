beryllia:/bəˈrɪliə/
n.
氧化铍
a white, powdery oxide of beryllium, BeO, used in ceramics and as a moderator in nuclear reactors.
Beryllia is a highly toxic substance. 氧化铍是一种剧毒物质。
synonyms: beryllium oxide
antonyms: None (as it's a specific chemical compound)
