disapprobation:/ˌdɪsæprəˈbeɪʃən/| n. |不赞成，反对，非难| strong disapproval, condemnation | 例句：He looked at me with disapprobation. 他带着不赞成的眼光看着我。|例句：The government's new policy met with widespread disapprobation. 政府的新政策遭到广泛反对。|近义词：disapproval, condemnation, censure, objection |反义词：approval, acceptance, praise, commendation |词性:名词

disapprove:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːv/| v. |不赞成，反对| to have or express an unfavorable opinion of | 例句：I strongly disapprove of his behavior. 我强烈反对他的行为。|例句：She disapproved of the way he was treating his sister.  她不赞成他对待他妹妹的方式。|近义词：object to, condemn, criticize |反义词：approve, endorse, support |词性:动词
