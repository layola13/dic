appetible: /ˈæpɪtəbl/ | adj. | 美味的，促进食欲的 | Appetising; stimulating or whetting the appetite. |  This dish is very appetible. 这道菜非常美味。| The aroma of freshly baked bread is always appetible.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味总是让人胃口大开。 | 近义词: tasty, delicious, palatable, savory | 反义词: unappetizing, unpalatable, distasteful | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *appetibilis, from Latin appetere "to strive after, desire," from ad- "to" + petere "to seek, go to, ask for, demand."
