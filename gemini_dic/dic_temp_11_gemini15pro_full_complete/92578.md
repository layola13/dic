一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I'll go to the store tomorrow. 我明天去商店。
I'll think about it. 我会考虑的。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I'll be working on this project all day tomorrow.  我明天一整天都会在做这个项目。
I'll be driving to the beach this weekend.  这个周末我会开车去海滩。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I'll have finished the report by Friday. 我会在星期五之前完成报告。
I'll have eaten dinner by the time you arrive. 你到的时候我应该已经吃完晚饭了。
