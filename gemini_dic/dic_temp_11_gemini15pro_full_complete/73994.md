fortuitously:/fɔːrˈtjuːɪtəsli/ | adv. | 偶然地，意外地 | Happening by chance, especially in a way that is lucky or convenient. |  I fortuitously ran into an old friend at the airport. 我在机场偶然碰到了一个老朋友。

We fortuitously discovered a shortcut that saved us hours. 我们意外发现了一条捷径，节省了我们几个小时的时间。| 近义词：accidentally, luckily, coincidentally, serendipitously | 反义词: deliberately, intentionally, purposely | 词性: 副词
