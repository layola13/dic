limeyest:/ˈlaɪmiɪst/
adjective
最像英国人的;最典型的英国人的
most resembling or characteristic of a British person, especially one considered stereotypically unsophisticated.
例句：He spoke in a limeyest accent I'd ever heard. 他说着我听过的最英国的口音。
例句：She dressed in the limeyest fashion imaginable, complete with a tweed jacket and wellies. 她穿着你能想象到的最典型的英国服装，包括一件粗花呢夹克和雨靴。
近义词：British, English
反义词：Un-British, foreign
词性：形容词


词源:
"limeyest" is the superlative form of "limey," a slang term, often derogatory, for a British person.  The origin of "limey" is attributed to the practice in the British Royal Navy of giving sailors lime juice to prevent scurvy during long voyages.
