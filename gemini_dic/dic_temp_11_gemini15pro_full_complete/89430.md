honcho:/ˈhɑːntʃoʊ/ /ˈhɒntʃəʊ/| n.|老板; 头儿; 负责的人|A person in charge, a leader, a boss. |例句：She's the honcho of this project. 她是这个项目的负责人。

例句：The honcho called a meeting to discuss the new strategy. 老板召集会议讨论新战略。|近义词：boss, chief, leader, manager, supervisor|反义词：subordinate, employee, worker|词性: 名词
