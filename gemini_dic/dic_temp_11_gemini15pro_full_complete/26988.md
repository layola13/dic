calcographer:/kælˈkɒɡrəfər/
n.
铜版雕刻师，制版工
A person who engraves, especially one who makes engravings on copper or other metal plates for printing.
William Blake was a skilled calcographer as well as a poet and painter. 威廉·布莱克是一位技艺精湛的铜版雕刻师，同时也是一位诗人和画家。
