hederic:/hɪˈdɛrɪk/ | adj. |遗传的，世袭的|Of or relating to inheritance or heredity.  Relating to or determined by genetic factors transmitted from parents to offspring.|Heritability is a measure of how well differences in people's genes account for differences in their traits. 遗传力衡量的是人们基因差异对性状差异的解释程度。|The disease is hederic, passed down through generations. 这种疾病是遗传的，世代相传。| 近义词: hereditary, inherited, genetic, inborn, congenital | 反义词: acquired |词性:形容词
