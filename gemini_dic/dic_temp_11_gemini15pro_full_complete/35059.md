closeout: /ˈkloʊzaʊt/ (US) /ˈkləʊzaʊt/ (UK)| n. adj.|清仓甩卖；停业；结算；关闭|A sale of goods at reduced prices to clear out remaining stock, or the process of discontinuing a product, business, or project.  A final settlement or conclusion.|The store had a closeout sale before going out of business. 这家商店在倒闭前进行清仓大甩卖。|近义词：clearance, liquidation, shutdown|反义词: opening, commencement|词性:名词，形容词

closeout: /ˈkloʊzaʊt/ (US) /ˈkləʊzaʊt/ (UK)| v.|停业；关闭；结算|To sell off remaining inventory at reduced prices, usually to discontinue a product line or business; to bring something to a final settlement or conclusion.|They're closing out the summer collection to make way for fall fashions. 他们正在清仓夏季系列，为秋季时装腾出空间。|近义词：liquidate, discontinue, wind up|反义词: open, begin|词性:动词
