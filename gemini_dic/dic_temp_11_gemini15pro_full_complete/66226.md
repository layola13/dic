exciting:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|令人兴奋的，刺激的|Causing a feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness.|例句：The roller coaster was an exciting ride. 过山车是一项刺激的游乐项目。

例句：The news of the discovery was very exciting.  这个发现的消息令人非常兴奋。


近义词：thrilling, stimulating, exhilarating, interesting|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting|词性:adjective
