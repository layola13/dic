noncustomer:/ˌnɒnˈkʌstəmər/ /ˌnɑːnˈkʌstəmər/ | n. |非客户，非顾客|A person who is not a customer of a particular business or company.  Someone who does not buy goods or services from a particular company. |Example 1: The survey targeted noncustomers to understand why they didn't use the company's services. 这项调查针对非客户，以了解他们为什么不使用该公司服务的原因。

Example 2:  Understanding the needs of noncustomers can help businesses expand their market. 了解非客户的需求可以帮助企业拓展市场。|Synonym: non-client, prospect (潜在客户) |Antonym: customer, client |noun
