ajog:/əˈdʒɒɡ/| adv. adj.|慢跑着, 微微颤动地|Slightly jogging or moving with a gentle rocking motion.|The carriage went ajog down the lane. 马车沿着小路慢跑着。|近义词：jogging, trotting, ambling|反义词:still, stationary|词性:副词，形容词

Etymology:
From a- + jog.  "A-" is a prefix meaning "in, on, at, to," or indicating motion.  "Jog" comes from the Middle English "joggen," meaning "to shake, jolt."
