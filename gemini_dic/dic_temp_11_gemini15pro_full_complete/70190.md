fibrinogenous:/ˌfaɪbrɪˈnɒdʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 产生纤维蛋白的；纤维蛋白原性的 | Relating to or producing fibrinogen.  Fibrinogen is a soluble protein present in blood plasma, from which fibrin is produced. |  The fibrinogenous activity of the thrombin was measured.  该凝血酶的纤维蛋白原活性进行了测定。| The fibrinogenous reaction is crucial for blood clotting. 纤维蛋白原反应对血液凝固至关重要。 | synonyms: fibrin-forming, coagulant | antonyms: anticoagulant | adjective
