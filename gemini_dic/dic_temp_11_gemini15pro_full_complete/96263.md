indictability:/ɪnˌdɪktəˈbɪləti/
n.
可起诉性，可被控告性
The quality or state of being indictable.  Being subject to being formally accused of a crime.
The indictability of the offense is in question.  该罪行的可起诉性存在疑问。
Near Synonyms: chargeability, accusable, prosecutability
Antonyms:  immunity, impunity
noun
