outglare:/ˌaʊtˈɡlɛər/| vt.| 瞪视，以目光压倒|To stare at someone angrily or fiercely until they look away.|He outglared his opponent across the negotiating table. 他在谈判桌上瞪着他的对手。|She outglared the photographer who was trying to take her picture. 她怒视着那个试图给她拍照的摄影师。|近义词：stare down, glare at, outstare|反义词: avoid eye contact, look away|词性:动词
