horometer: /ˌhɒˈrɒmɪtər/ (UK) /ˌhɔːˈrɑːmɪtər/ (US) | n. | 测时器，计时器 | An instrument for measuring time, especially short intervals.  A horometer is a device used to measure time, often associated with scientific experiments or precise timing in sports. | The scientist used a horometer to measure the duration of the reaction. 科学家用测时器测量反应的持续时间。

The horometer recorded a time of 2.5 seconds for the athlete's sprint. 测时器记录了运动员冲刺的时间为2.5秒。 | synonyms: timer, stopwatch, chronograph | antonyms: N/A | noun
