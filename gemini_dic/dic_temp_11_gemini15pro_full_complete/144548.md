outvoyaged:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪɪdʒd/
verb
航行胜过；远航超过
To surpass in voyaging; to sail further or more extensively than.
Magellan outvoyaged all his contemporaries. 麦哲伦的航行超过了他所有的同时代人。
