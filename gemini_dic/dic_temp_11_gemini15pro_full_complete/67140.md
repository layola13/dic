exposability:/ɪkˌspəʊzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可暴露性；易受影响性；曝光度 | The state or quality of being able to be exposed or made vulnerable to something.  It refers to the degree to which something is susceptible to being affected by external factors or influences. | The exposability of the coastal towns to hurricanes is a major concern. 沿海城镇易受飓风影响是一个主要问题。

The exposability of the data to hackers prompted a security review. 数据容易受到黑客攻击，这促使了安全审查。| 近义词：vulnerability, susceptibility, openness | 反义词：invulnerability, impenetrability, resistance | 词性: noun
