herodian:/hɪˈroʊdiən/ | adj. n. |希律党人的；希律党人|Of or relating to Herod the Great or his dynasty. A supporter of the dynasty of Herod, especially during the time of Jesus.|  Example: The Herodian dynasty was marked by political intrigue and violence. 希律王朝的特点是政治阴谋和暴力。| Example:  Some scholars believe the Herodians were a political faction, not a religious sect. 一些学者认为希律党人是一个政治派别，而不是一个宗教派别。|Synonym: Herod's supporter, partisan of Herod |Antonym: opponent of Herod| Adjective, Noun
