dodecagynous:/ˌdɒʊdɪˈkæɡɪnəs/ /ˌdoʊdɪˈkædʒɪnəs/
| adj. | 十二雌蕊的 | Having twelve pistils or styles. |  This rare orchid is dodecagynous, possessing twelve distinct styles.  这种稀有的兰花是十二雌蕊的，拥有十二个不同的花柱。 |  multipistillate | apistillate | adjective


Etymology->
From Ancient Greek *δώδεκα* (dṓdeka, “twelve”) + *γυνή* (gunḗ, “woman”) + *-ous*.  The "woman" part refers to the pistil, the female reproductive organ of a flower.
