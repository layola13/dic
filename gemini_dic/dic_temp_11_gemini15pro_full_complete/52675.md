diglossias:/daɪˈɡlɒsiəz/ /daɪˈɡloʊsiəz/
n.
双语现象，双言制
A situation in which two distinct varieties of a language are used in different social contexts.
Diglossia is common in many parts of the world, where a "high" variety is used for formal occasions and a "low" variety for everyday communication.
双语现象在世界许多地方都很常见，在这种情况下，“高级”变体用于正式场合，而“低级”变体用于日常交流。
近义词:bilingualism, multilingualism
反义词:monolingualism
