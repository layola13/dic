lakh:/læk, lɑːk/ | n. | 十万; 拉克(印度计数单位) | A unit of currency in India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Nepal, equal to one hundred thousand.  It is often used to denote a large, indefinite number. |  India's population is over a billion, which is more than ten thousand lakh.  印度的人口超过十亿，也就是超过一万拉克。
