barmy:/ˈbɑːmi/| adj.|愚蠢的，疯狂的，古怪的|Crazy, foolish, or eccentric.|例句：That's a barmy idea! 那真是个疯狂的主意！

例句：He's gone completely barmy. 他完全疯了。

近义词：crazy, mad, insane, daft, loopy|反义词：sane, sensible, rational|词性:形容词

词源:  The origin of "barmy" is uncertain, but it is thought to be derived from "barm," which refers to the froth or yeast on fermenting malt liquor.  The idea is that someone behaving erratically is like the frothy, bubbling, and unpredictable nature of fermenting liquid.
