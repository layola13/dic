noncoherent:/ˌnɒnkoʊˈhɪərənt/ /ˌnɒnkəʊˈhɪərənt/| adj.|非相干的，不连贯的|Lacking coherence or logical connection; not sticking together.|The speaker's noncoherent ramblings made it difficult to understand his point. 演讲者语无伦次的讲话让人难以理解他的观点。|incoherent, disjointed, rambling|coherent, connected, logical|adjective
noncoherent:/ˌnɒnkoʊˈhɪərənt/ /ˌnɒnkəʊˈhɪərənt/| adj.|不相干的，非相参的 (物理学)|Not having a constant phase relationship. (physics)|The noncoherent light sources made it difficult to create interference patterns. 非相干光源难以产生干涉图样。|incoherent, asynchronous|coherent, synchronous|adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of". From Latin *non*.
coherent (adjective): From Latin *cohaerēns*, present participle of *cohaerēre* "to stick together," from *co-* "together" + *haerēre* "to stick".
