olivines:/ˈɒlɪˌviːnz/ /ˈɑːlɪˌviːnz/| n.|橄榄石|A group of silicate minerals that are a major component of the Earth's mantle and are also found in some meteorites.  They are typically olive-green in color.|Olivine is a common mineral in basaltic rocks. 橄榄石是玄武岩中常见的矿物。|
olivines:| n.|橄榄石|Olivine crystals can be found in some volcanic rocks. 在一些火山岩中可以找到橄榄石晶体。|Synonyms: peridot (when gem-quality), chrysolite (an older term)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
