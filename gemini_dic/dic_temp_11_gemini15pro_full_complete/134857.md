nonmolecular:/ˌnɒn.məˈlɛkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌnɑːn.məˈlɛkjələr/
adjective
非分子的
Not consisting of molecules; not molecular.  Characterized by ionic or covalent bonding in a continuous lattice structure rather than discrete molecules.
Diamond is a nonmolecular solid.  钻石是一种非分子固体。
近义词:non-molecular, macromolecular
反义词:molecular
词性:adjective
