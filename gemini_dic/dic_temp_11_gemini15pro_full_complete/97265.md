inflorescence:/ˌɪnfləˈrɛsns/| n. |花序|The complete flower head of a plant including stems, stalks, bracts, and flowers.  The arrangement of flowers on a plant.| The plant has a beautiful inflorescence.  这种植物的花序很漂亮。| synonyms: flower cluster, bloom, blossom | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical structure.  Could consider "sterile" if the inflorescence doesn't produce fruit/seeds.) | noun
