canalization:/ˌkænələˈzeɪʃən/ |/ˌkænəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |渠道化；(生物)限渠性；(比喻)固定化|The canalization of a river helps control flooding.  这条河的渠道化有助于控制洪水。|The canalization of development means that, despite variations in environment, most individuals develop along predictable pathways.  发育的限渠性意味着，尽管环境存在差异，但大多数个体都沿着可预测的途径发育。|近义词：channeling, routing, directing|反义词：diversification, variation|词性:名词

Etymology: From French canalisation, from canaliser "to canalize," from canal "canal." Ultimately from Latin canalis "pipe, channel."
