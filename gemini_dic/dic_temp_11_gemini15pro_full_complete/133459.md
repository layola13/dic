nonfeasibilities:/ˌnɒnˌfiːzəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
不可行性；不可行之处
The state or quality of not being feasible; things that are not feasible.
Due to budget constraints, the project's nonfeasibilities became apparent. 由于预算限制，该项目的不可行性变得显而易见。
The report highlighted the nonfeasibilities of the proposed plan.  报告强调了拟议计划的不可行之处。
近义词:impossibilities, impracticabilities
反义词:feasibilities, possibilities
