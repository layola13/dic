electro-hydraulics:/ɪˈlektrəʊ hʌɪˈdrɔːlɪks/ /ɪˈlektroʊ haɪˈdrɔːlɪks/| adj. n.|电液压的，电液压技术|A technology that uses both electrical and hydraulic components for control or power transmission. It combines the advantages of electrical control with the power of hydraulics.|The aircraft's landing gear is operated by an electro-hydraulics system.|近义词：electrohydraulic|反义词:|adjective, noun
