dorkiest:/ˈdɔːrkiɪst/
adjective
最呆的，最笨拙的
superlative adjective of dorky; most socially inept, awkward, or unfashionable.
He wore the dorkiest glasses I've ever seen. 他戴着我见过的最呆的眼镜。
近义词:nerdiest, geekiest, awkwardest, clumsiest
反义词:coolest, hippest, most stylish
词性:adjective
