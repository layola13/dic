monophonically:/ˌmɒnəˈfɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 单音地，单声部地 | Performed or uttered in a single melodic line, without harmony or counterpoint. | The Gregorian chant was sung monophonically.  格里高利圣咏是以单音演唱的。|  The melody was played monophonically on the flute.  这首旋律是由长笛单音演奏的。|  homophonically, unison | polyphonically | adverb
