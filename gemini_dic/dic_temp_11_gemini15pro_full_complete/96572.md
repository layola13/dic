indubitableness:/ˌɪndʊˈbɪtəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
无可置疑，确实性
The state of being indubitable; unquestionableness.
The indubitableness of his guilt was clear to everyone.  他的罪行无可置疑，每个人都清楚。
近义词：certainty, certitude, unquestionableness
反义词：doubt, uncertainty, dubiety
词性：名词

词源:
From Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + dubitable (see dubitable).
