consortia's:/kənˈsɔːrʃəz/
|n.|财团的，联合体的|The consortia's investment in the project was substantial.  财团对该项目的投资巨大。|英文解析: The possessive form of "consortia," indicating ownership or belonging to a group of companies or organizations working together.  "Consortia" is the plural of "consortium."|例句：The consortia's decision to fund the project was unanimous. 财团一致决定资助该项目。

例句：The consortia's combined resources enabled them to undertake the ambitious venture.  财团的资源整合使他们能够承担这项雄心勃勃的事业。|近义词：syndicate's, alliance's, coalition's|反义词: individual's, company's|词性:名词所有格

consortium:/kənˈsɔːrtiəm/
|n.|财团，联合体|The consortium comprises several leading companies in the industry.  该财团由业内几家领先公司组成。|英文解析: A group of companies or organizations working together on a joint venture.|例句：The consortium was formed to bid on the large construction project.  该财团的成立是为了竞标大型建筑项目。

例句：The consortium pooled their expertise to develop the new technology.  该财团汇集了他们的专业知识来开发新技术。|近义词：syndicate, alliance, coalition|反义词: individual, company|词性:名词

词源:
From Latin *consortium*, from *consors* ("sharing, partner"), from *con-* ("together") + *sors* ("lot, fate, share").
