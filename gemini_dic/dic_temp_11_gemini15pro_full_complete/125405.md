multiovulated:/ˌmʌltiˈɒvjuːleɪtɪd/
adjective
多排卵的
Having released multiple ova (eggs) during a single ovulation cycle.
The multiovulated mare produced twins. 这匹多排卵的母马产下了双胞胎。
近义词:superovulated
反义词:anovulatory
词性:adjective
