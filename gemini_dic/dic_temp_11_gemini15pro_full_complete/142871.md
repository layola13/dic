out-of-hand:/ˌaʊt əv ˈhænd/
adjective
失控的，无法控制的；立即的，不假思索的
(of a situation) not under control; (of an action) done without taking time to think
The situation got completely out of hand. 局势完全失控了。
He made an out-of-hand rejection of the offer. 他不假思索地拒绝了提议。
Synonyms: uncontrollable, unmanageable, immediate, spontaneous
Antonyms: controlled, manageable, deliberate, considered
词性: 形容词


out-of-hand:/ˌaʊt əv ˈhænd/
adverb
失控地，无法控制地；立即地，不假思索地
without taking time to think; immediately
He dismissed the idea out of hand. 他不假思索地否定了这个想法。
The protests spiraled out of hand. 抗议活动迅速失控。
Synonyms: immediately, promptly, spontaneously, without hesitation
Antonyms: deliberately, thoughtfully, cautiously, after consideration
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
"Out of hand" originates from the practice of handling and controlling horses.  A horse "in hand" was under control, while a horse "out of hand" was not. This meaning extended metaphorically to situations and actions that are beyond control or done without proper consideration.
