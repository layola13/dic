downsizing: /ˈdaʊnsaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 缩小规模，裁员 | The act of reducing the size of a company or organization by reducing the number of employees. |  Many companies are downsizing their workforce due to the economic downturn. 许多公司由于经济衰退正在裁员。

The company announced a major downsizing, leaving many employees worried about their jobs. 公司宣布了大规模裁员，让很多员工担心自己的工作。 | 近义词:  layoff, reduction, restructuring | 反义词: expansion, growth | 词性: 名词

downsize: /ˈdaʊnsaɪz/ | v. | 缩小规模，裁员 | To reduce the size of a company or organization by reducing the number of employees. | The company downsized its operations last year.  这家公司去年缩减了业务规模。

They were forced to downsize their staff due to budget cuts. 由于预算削减，他们被迫裁员。 | 近义词: lay off, reduce, restructure | 反义词: expand, grow | 词性: 动词
