implausible: /ɪmˈplɔːzɪbl/ /ɪmˈplɑːzəbl/ | adj. |难以置信的，不像真实的 | Not seeming reasonable or probable; failing to convince. |  His explanation for the missing money was completely implausible. 他对丢失的钱的解释完全不可信。

The plot of the movie was so implausible that I couldn't suspend my disbelief. 这部电影的情节太令人难以置信了，我无法让自己相信。

| 近义词: unbelievable, improbable, unlikely, doubtful | 反义词: plausible, believable, credible, likely, probable | 词性: adjective
