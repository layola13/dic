eukaryotic: /juːˌkæriˈɒtɪk/ (UK) /juˌkæriˈɑːtɪk/ (US) | adj. | 真核的 | Relating to or belonging to the domain Eukarya; having cells with a membrane-bound nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles. |  Eukaryotic cells are more complex than prokaryotic cells. 真核细胞比原核细胞更复杂。|  Eukaryotic organisms include animals, plants, and fungi. 真核生物包括动物、植物和真菌。 |  Synonyms: nucleated | Antonyms: prokaryotic | adjective
