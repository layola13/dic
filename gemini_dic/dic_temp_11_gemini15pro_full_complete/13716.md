atween:/əˈtwiːn/
adv. prep.|在…之间，介于…之间|Between; betwixt. Used chiefly in literary or dialectal contexts.|The house stood atween two hills. 房子坐落在两座小山之间。|近义词:between, betwixt|反义词:outside, beyond|词性:副词, 介词
词源: From Middle English *atwene, from Old English betwēonan (between, among), from be- (by, about) + twēonum (two each, two).  Essentially a reinforced form of "between."
