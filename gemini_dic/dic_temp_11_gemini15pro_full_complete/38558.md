conclusory:/kənˈkluːsəri/ /kənˈkluːsɔːri/
adjective
结论性的；总结性的
serving to conclude; expressing or based on conclusions rather than supporting evidence.
The report was criticized for its conclusory statements and lack of supporting data.  这份报告因其结论性的陈述和缺乏支持数据而受到批评。
The judge dismissed the case, saying the evidence presented was merely conclusory. 法官驳回了此案，称提出的证据仅仅是结论性的。
近义词:summary, final, definitive, concluding
反义词:introductory, preliminary, supporting, detailed
词性:adjective
