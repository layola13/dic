defend:/dɪˈfend/| v. | 保护，保卫；辩护；防守 | to protect someone or something from attack or criticism; to support someone or something that is being criticized |  The soldiers defended the city against the enemy. 士兵们保卫城市抵御敌人。| synonyms: protect, guard, safeguard, uphold, support | antonyms: attack, assault, prosecute, oppose | verb

defend:/dɪˈfend/| v. | 为…辩护；辩解 | to speak or write in support of someone or something that is being criticized |  He defended his actions, saying he had no choice. 他为自己的行为辩护，说自己别无选择。| synonyms: justify, excuse, vindicate | antonyms: accuse, blame, condemn | verb
