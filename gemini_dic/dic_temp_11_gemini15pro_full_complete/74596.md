freakin:/ˈfriːkɪn/| adv. |非常地，极其地|Used as an intensifier, similar to "very" or "extremely," but more informal and often used to express annoyance or frustration. |例句：I'm freakin' tired of this. 我真是烦透了。|近义词：really, very, extremely|反义词: (No direct antonym, but using words like "slightly" or "a little" conveys the opposite effect)|词性: Adverb
