oscitantly:/ˈɒsɪtəntli/ /ˈɑːsɪtəntli/| adv.|打哈欠地，懒洋洋地，无精打采地|Characterized by yawning or drowsiness; in a sleepy or listless manner.|He listened oscitantly to the lecture, struggling to keep his eyes open. 他懒洋洋地听着讲座，努力不让眼睛闭上。|Synonyms: sleepily, drowsily, listlessly, lethargically|Antonyms: alertly, energetically, vigilantly|Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oscitant- (stem of oscitans), present participle of oscitare "to yawn," from oscitāre "to open the mouth wide," frequentative of oscītare "to gape, yawn."
