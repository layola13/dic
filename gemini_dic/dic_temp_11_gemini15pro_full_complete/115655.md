mardus:/'mɑːrdəs/ | n. | 马尔都斯 | Malthus, referring to Thomas Robert Malthus, an English cleric and scholar, influential in the fields of political economy and demography.  He is best known for his theory that population growth will tend to outrun the growth of the means of subsistence. |  "The mardus predicted widespread famine." 马尔都斯预言了大范围的饥荒。 | 近义词: Malthusian (adj.), doomsayer | 反义词: optimist | 词性: 名词
