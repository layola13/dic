instructedness:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪdnəs/
noun
受教导的状态；被指示的状态
the state of being instructed or having been given instructions
The instructedness of the team was evident in their precise execution of the plan.  团队受过良好训练的状态在他们精确执行计划的过程中显而易见。
近义词：education, training, guidance
反义词：ignorance, naiveté
词性：n.


instructedness:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪdnəs/
noun
知识；学识
knowledge or understanding acquired through instruction
His instructedness in the field of astrophysics was impressive. 他在天体物理学领域的知识令人印象深刻。
近义词：knowledge, erudition, scholarship
反义词：ignorance, illiteracy
词性：n.


词源:
From instruct + -ed + -ness.  来自 instruct (指导) + -ed (过去分词后缀) + -ness (名词后缀，表示状态或性质).
