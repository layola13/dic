foresay:/fɔːrˈseɪ/ /fɔrˈseɪ/
verb
预言，预测
to predict; to say in advance what will happen
The economist foresaw a recession. 经济学家预言了经济衰退。
Synonyms: predict, foretell, anticipate, prognosticate
Antonyms:  N/A (Antonyms for prediction are difficult to define directly; consider "deny", "disbelieve", or verbs related to unexpected outcomes.)
词性: verb
