misdates:/ˌmɪsˈdeɪts/
verb
错误地定日期; 给…定错日期
To assign an incorrect date to something.
The historian misdated the ancient manuscript by several centuries. 历史学家把这份古代手稿的年代弄错了好几个世纪。
