ossean:/ˈɒsiən/ /ˈɑːsiən/| adj.|奥西恩的；芬戈尔时代的；像奥西恩诗歌的；有关古代凯尔特或爱尔兰的|Relating to or resembling the poems of Ossian, a legendary Gaelic warrior and bard.  Characterized by melancholy, grandeur, and a focus on nature and ancient Celtic or Irish themes.|The rugged landscape evoked an Ossean sense of melancholy.  崎岖的景色唤起了一种奥西恩式的忧郁。|近义词：Gaelic, Celtic, Irish, melancholy, epic|反义词:modern, cheerful|词性:adjective
