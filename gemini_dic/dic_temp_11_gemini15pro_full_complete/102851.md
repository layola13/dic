jaspery: /ˈdʒæspəri/ | adj. | 碧玉的，像碧玉的 | Resembling jasper in appearance, especially in being variegated or spotted. | The artist used jaspery strokes to create a textured effect. 艺术家用碧玉般的笔触来创造一种纹理效果。 | The cliff face was a jaspery mix of greens and browns.  悬崖表面是绿色和棕色混合的碧玉状。 | 近义词: jade-like, mottled, marbled | 反义词: uniform, plain | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From jasper + -y.  Jasper itself comes from Old French jaspre, ultimately from Latin iaspidem, accusative of iaspis, from Greek ἴασπις (iaspis).
