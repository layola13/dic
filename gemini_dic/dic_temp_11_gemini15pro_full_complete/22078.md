bollard:/ˈbɒləd/ (UK) /ˈbɑːlərd/ (US)| n.|短柱; 系船柱; |A short, thick post on a wharf or ship to which mooring lines are attached. A similar post set in the ground to prevent the passage of motor vehicles. |例句：Metal bollards were installed to prevent cars from parking on the sidewalk.  金属短柱被安装以防止汽车停放在人行道上。

例句：The ship was secured to the bollard.  船被固定在系船柱上。|近义词：post, pillar, marker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
