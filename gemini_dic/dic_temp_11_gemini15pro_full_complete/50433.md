depravity:/dɪˈprævəti/| n. |堕落，腐败，邪恶|Moral depravity is the corruption of morality or virtue. | The city was known for its depravity and vice.  这座城市以其堕落和邪恶而闻名。| synonyms: corruption, wickedness, evil, sinfulness, immorality, vice, degradation, perversion | antonyms: virtue, goodness, morality, righteousness, decency, integrity | noun

depraved:/dɪˈpreɪvd/ | adj. |堕落的，腐败的，邪恶的 | He led a depraved life filled with drugs and violence. 他过着充满毒品和暴力的堕落生活。 | synonyms: corrupt, wicked, evil, sinful, immoral, vicious, degenerate, perverted | antonyms: virtuous, good, moral, righteous, decent, upright | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēprāvitās, from Latin dēprāvāre (“to corrupt, pervert”), from dē- (“from, away from”) + prāvus (“crooked, perverse”).
