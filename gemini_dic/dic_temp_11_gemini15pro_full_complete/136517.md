nonrudimentary:/ˌnɒnruːdɪˈmɛntəri/
adjective
非基本的，非初步的，发达的
Not rudimentary; developed beyond the basic or elementary stage.
The artist's skills were far from nonrudimentary; she possessed a sophisticated technique.  这位艺术家的技艺远非基本，她掌握着一种成熟的技法。
His understanding of quantum physics is nonrudimentary. 他对量子物理学的理解已经超出了基础阶段。
synonym: advanced, developed, sophisticated, complex, elaborate
antonym: rudimentary, basic, elementary, simple, primitive
词性: 形容词
