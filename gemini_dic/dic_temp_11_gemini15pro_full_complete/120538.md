mira:/ˈmiːrə/| n.|奇迹，奇观；圣母像|A surprising and welcome event that is not explicable by natural or scientific laws and is therefore considered to be the work of a divine agency.  Something that evokes wonder or astonishment. A picture, statue, or other representation of the Virgin Mary.|例句：The doctors said her recovery was a mira. 医生说她的康复是一个奇迹。

例句：We visited the mira of the ancient ruins. 我们参观了古代遗迹的奇观。|近义词：wonder, marvel, miracle|反义词:disaster, curse|词性:名词
