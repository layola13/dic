mesocarp: /ˈmɛzəˌkɑːrp/ | n. | 中果皮 | The fleshy middle layer of the pericarp of a fruit, between the exocarp and endocarp. | The mesocarp of a peach is the juicy part that we eat. 桃子的中果皮就是我们吃的多汁的部分。|  Synonym: middle layer | Antonym: exocarp, endocarp | noun
