nonsolvency:/nɒnˈsɒlvənsi/ /nɑːnˈsɑːlvənsi/
n.
无力偿付;破产
The state of being unable to pay one's debts.
The company declared nonsolvency after years of declining profits.  这家公司在连年利润下降后宣布破产。
The nonsolvency of the business led to its closure.  该企业的无力偿付导致其倒闭。
