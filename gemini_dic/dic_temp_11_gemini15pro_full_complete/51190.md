destainer:/diːˈsteɪnər/| n.|去污剂，脱色剂|A chemical or substance used to remove stains, especially from microscopic slides in laboratory settings.|The destainer removed the excess dye from the tissue sample.|近义词：cleaning agent, bleach, solvent|反义词：stain, dye|noun

destain:/diːˈsteɪn/| v.|脱色，去污|To remove a stain or color from something, especially from a microscopic slide.|The lab technician destained the slides after staining them with hematoxylin and eosin.|近义词：bleach, clean, remove color|反义词：stain, dye|verb
