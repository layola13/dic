imagining:/ɪˈmædʒɪnɪŋ/
n.|想象; 设想|The act of forming a mental image or concept of something.|She spent hours imagining her future life. 她花了几个小时想象她未来的生活。|近义词: visualizing, conceiving, fantasizing|反义词:  perceiving (感知真实事物，没有反义词可以完全对应“想象”)
v.|想象；设想|Form a mental image or concept of.|I can't imagine life without you. 我无法想象没有你的生活。|近义词: envisioning, picturing, supposing|反义词: knowing (知道，表示确定，与想象相反)
词性:  present participle of imagine

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I imagine a world without war. 我想象一个没有战争的世界。
He imagines himself as a superhero. 他把自己想象成一个超级英雄。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is imagining her wedding day. 她正在想象她的婚礼当天。
They are imagining what it would be like to live on Mars. 他们正在想象住在火星上会是什么样子。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have imagined this scenario many times. 我已经想象过这种情况很多次了。
She has imagined living in a different country. 她已经想象过住在另一个国家了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been imagining my future career. 我一直在想象我未来的职业。
He has been imagining winning the lottery. 他一直在想象赢得彩票。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I imagined a better life for myself. 我想象着自己更好的生活。
She imagined meeting her favorite celebrity. 她想象着与她最喜欢的名人见面。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was imagining what it would be like to be a bird. 我在想象成为一只鸟会是什么样子。
He was imagining winning the race. 他在想象赢得比赛。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had imagined a different outcome. 我曾想象过不同的结果。
She had imagined that she would be married by now. 她曾想象过现在已经结婚了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been imagining this moment for years. 我多年来一直在想象这一刻。
He had been imagining traveling the world. 他一直想象着环游世界。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):

She will imagine what her children will look like. 她会想象她的孩子会长什么样子。
