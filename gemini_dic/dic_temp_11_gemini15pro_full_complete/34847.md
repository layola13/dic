clink:/klɪŋk/ | n. v. |叮当声，碰杯声；发出叮当声；使发出叮当声 |A short, sharp ringing sound, such as that made by striking glass or metal; to make or cause something to make a short, sharp ringing sound. | The ice clinked in the glass. 冰块在玻璃杯里叮当作响。 | synonyms: jingle, tinkle, chink | antonyms: silence, hush | noun, verb

clink:/klɪŋk/ | n. v. |监狱；被关进监狱 | (slang) prison; to put someone in prison. | He spent a night in the clink. 他在监狱里过了一夜。 | synonyms: jail, prison, lockup | antonyms: freedom, liberty | noun, verb
