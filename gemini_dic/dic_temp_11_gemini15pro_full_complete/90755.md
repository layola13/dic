hummuses:/ˈhʌməsəz/ /ˈhʊməsəz/| n. |鹰嘴豆泥（复数）|A thick Middle Eastern dip or spread made from ground chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. |例句：The restaurant offers a variety of hummuses, including roasted red pepper and cilantro jalapeno. 这家餐厅提供各种口味的鹰嘴豆泥，包括烤红辣椒和香菜墨西哥辣椒口味。
