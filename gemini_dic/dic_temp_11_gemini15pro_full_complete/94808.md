impulsively:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪvli/| adv.|冲动地，一时兴起地|Acting or done without forethought.|He impulsively bought a sports car. 他一时冲动买了一辆跑车。|近义词：rashly, spontaneously, capriciously|反义词：deliberately, cautiously, thoughtfully|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *impulsivus, from Latin impulsus (“impulse”), perfect passive participle of impellō (“push, drive”).
