bedazzled:/bɪˈdæzəld/
adjective
眼花缭乱的，被深深吸引的
Impressed and amazed, especially by someone's wealth or glamour.
She was bedazzled by the city lights. 她被城市的灯光迷住了。
synonyms: dazzled, amazed, awestruck, fascinated, captivated, charmed, enchanted, enthralled, entranced, mesmerized, hypnotized, spellbound
antonyms: unimpressed, bored, indifferent
词性: adjective


bedazzle:/bɪˈdæzəl/
verb
使眼花缭乱，使着迷
To impress or overwhelm with brilliance or splendor; to dazzle.
The fireworks bedazzled the children. 焰火使孩子们眼花缭乱。
synonyms: dazzle, amaze, impress, overpower, overwhelm, stun, stupefy
antonyms: bore, tire, disillusion
词性: verb

词源:
From be- + dazzle.  The prefix "be-" intensifies the meaning of "dazzle".  "Dazzle" comes from Middle English *dasen, of uncertain origin.
