catsuits:/ˈkætˌsuːts/| n. |紧身衣|A close-fitting, one-piece garment that covers the torso and legs, often made of stretchy material like spandex or Lycra. It is commonly associated with superheroes, athletes, dancers, and performers.|例句：The singer wore a sparkling catsuit for her performance.  这位歌手穿着一件闪亮的紧身衣表演。

例句：Catsuits are popular costumes for Halloween parties. 紧身衣是万圣节派对的流行服装。

|近义词：bodysuit, jumpsuit, unitard|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider loose clothing, separates)|词性:noun
