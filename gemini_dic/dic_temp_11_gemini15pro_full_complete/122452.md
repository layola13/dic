mobile homes:/ˈmoʊbəl hoʊmz/ (US) /ˈməʊbaɪl həʊmz/ (UK)| n.|活动房屋，移动房屋|A prefabricated structure, built on a chassis and capable of being moved, designed for permanent habitation.  Essentially a trailer home designed to be lived in year-round. |Many retirees choose to live in mobile homes due to their affordability. 许多退休人员因为移动房屋价格实惠而选择住在里面。

The mobile home park offered a swimming pool and a playground.  这个移动房屋园区提供游泳池和游乐场。|近义词：trailer home, manufactured home, house trailer|反义词: house, apartment, condominium|词性:noun
