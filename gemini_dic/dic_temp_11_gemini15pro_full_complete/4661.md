aled: /əˈlɛd/ | adj. | 点燃的，燃烧的；（古）火焰形的 |  Archaic.  Set ablaze; lighted.  Derived from the Middle English "alen," meaning "to kindle" or "to set on fire," ultimately from Old English "ælan." | The aled torches illuminated the castle walls. 点燃的火炬照亮了城堡的墙壁。| synonyms: ignited, ablaze, kindled, lighted, flaming | antonyms: extinguished, unlit, dark, quenched | adjective
