multiparous:/ˌmʌltɪˈpærəs/
adjective
多胎的；经产的
(of a woman or female animal) having given birth to more than one offspring.
例句：Multiparous women have a slightly higher risk of certain complications.  经产妇患某些并发症的风险略高。

例句：The study focused on the health outcomes of multiparous cows.  这项研究的重点是经产奶牛的健康结果。

近义词：polytocous
反义词：nulliparous, primiparous
词性: adjective
