hoariest:/ˈhɔːriɪst/ /ˈhɔːriəst/| adj.|最古老的，最陈腐的，头发最白的|Covered with white, gray, or frosty hairs, especially of age; extremely old; ancient; stale from overuse.|例句：The hoariest joke I've ever heard. 我听过的最老掉牙的笑话。|例句：The library housed some of the hoariest manuscripts known to man.  这个图书馆收藏了一些人类已知的最古老的手稿。|近义词：oldest, ancient, venerable, antiquated, timeworn|反义词：newest, youngest, recent, modern, contemporary|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *hor, hore (“hoary, gray”), from Old English hār (“hoary, gray, white”), from Proto-Germanic *hairaz (“gray, hoary”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱey- (“gray”). Cognate with Scots hoar (“hoary”), West Frisian hoar (“hoary”), Dutch haar (“hair”), German haar (“hair”), Old Norse hár (“hair”), Gothic 𐌷𐌰𐌴𐍂 (haer, “hair”). More at hair.  The -y suffix is from Middle English -i, from Old English -ig.
