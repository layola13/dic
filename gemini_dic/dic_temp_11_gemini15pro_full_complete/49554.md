demeaned:/dɪˈmiːnd/ | adj. | 贬低的，卑贱的 |  Lowered in dignity or status; humiliated.  Characterized by low conduct or character. |  She felt demeaned by the way her boss spoke to her. 她觉得老板跟她说话的方式贬低了她。|  humiliated, degraded, belittled |  exalted, honored, respected | adjective

demean:/dɪˈmiːn/ | vt. |  贬低，降低…的身份 | To lower in dignity, status, or character; degrade. To behave or conduct oneself (usually followed by *oneself*). |  He demeaned himself by lying. 他撒谎降低了自己的身份. |  degrade, humiliate, lower | elevate, honor, exalt | verb
