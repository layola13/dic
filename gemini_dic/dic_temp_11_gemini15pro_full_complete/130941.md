nonassertions:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːʃənz/ | n. | 非断言，非声明 |  Statements that do not assert or claim something to be true. They can include questions, commands, exclamations, and expressions of wishes or doubts.  |  Example: "Is it raining?" is a nonassertion. "Go to the store!" is another nonassertion.  "If only I were taller!" is also a nonassertion. | Synonyms: non-affirmations, non-declarations | Antonyms: assertions, declarations, statements | noun

nonassertion:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːʃən/ | n. | 非断言，非声明 | A statement that does not assert or claim something to be true. It can be a question, command, exclamation, or expression of a wish or doubt. | Example:  "What time is it?" is a nonassertion. "Please close the door." is also a nonassertion. | Synonyms: non-affirmation, non-declaration | Antonyms: assertion, declaration, statement | noun
