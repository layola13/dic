hickest:/ˈhɪkɪst/| adj.|最土的，最乡巴佬的|Superlative form of hick: most hick.  Refers to someone or something considered unsophisticated, unfashionable, or characteristic of rural areas, often in a derogatory way.|He had the hickest accent I'd ever heard. 他说话的口音是我听过的最土的。|近义词：most rustic, most countrified, most provincial|反义词：most sophisticated, most cosmopolitan, most urbane|词性:形容词

词源:
From *hick*, a shortened form of *Hickok* (a common surname, especially in rural areas), used as a generic name for a country bumpkin.
