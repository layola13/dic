idiomorphous: /ˌɪdiəˈmɔːrfəs/
idiomorphous: adj.
idiomorphous: 自形的，特有的晶形的
idiomorphous: Having its own characteristic form; said of a mineral when it occurs in distinct crystals.  A mineral is said to be idiomorphous when its crystals are fully developed and not hindered by the growth of adjacent crystals.
idiomorphous: The quartz crystals in this rock are idiomorphous, showing perfect hexagonal prisms.  这块岩石中的石英晶体是自形的，呈现出完美的六棱柱。
idiomorphous: euhedral, automorphic
idiomorphous: anhedral, subhedral
idiomorphous: adjective
