inflamer:/ɪnˈfleɪmər/| n.|煽动者，激怒者|Someone or something that inflames or excites strong feelings, especially of anger or hostility.|His inflammatory rhetoric made him an inflamer of public opinion.|近义词：agitator, provocateur, instigator|反义词：pacifier, mediator, peacemaker|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inflammātor, from Latin inflammare (“to set on fire, kindle, inflame”), from in- + flamma (“flame”).
