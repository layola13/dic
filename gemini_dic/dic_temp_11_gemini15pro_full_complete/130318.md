non-tenant:/ˌnɒnˈtɛnənt/ | n. | 非租户；非承租人 |  Someone who does not hold a tenancy agreement for a particular property.  A person who does not rent or lease a property. | The non-tenant was asked to leave the premises. 非租户被要求离开该处。 | The landlord had a dispute with a non-tenant over access to the property. 房东与一位非租户就进入房产的问题发生了争执。 |近义词: outsider, non-resident, non-occupant |反义词: tenant, lessee, renter | 词性: 名词
