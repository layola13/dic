inharmoniously:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəsli/ | adv. |不和谐地，不协调地 | Not in a harmonious manner; discordantly.  Lacking harmony or agreement; conflicting. | The instruments played inharmoniously, creating a jarring sound.  这些乐器演奏得很不和谐，发出刺耳的声音。 |  The different design elements clashed inharmoniously. 不同的设计元素不协调地冲突着。 | 近义词: discordantly, dissonantly, jarringly, clashingly | 反义词: harmoniously, concordantly, melodically | 词性: 副词

词源:
From in- (not) + harmonious + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Harmonious" ultimately comes from the Greek "harmonia" meaning "joint, agreement, concord."
