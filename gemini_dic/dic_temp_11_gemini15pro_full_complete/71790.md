flectional:/flɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 屈折的，词形变化的 | Relating to inflection; undergoing inflection.  A flectional language uses changes in word form to express grammatical relations. |  English has several flectional endings, such as -s for plurals and -ed for past tense. 英语有一些屈折词尾，例如复数的 -s 和过去式的 -ed。 |  inflectional, derivational | analytical | adjective
