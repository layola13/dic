overpleasing:/ˌoʊvərˈpliːzɪŋ/
adjective
过度取悦的；讨好别人的
Trying too hard to please others, often to the detriment of oneself.
She had an overpleasing personality, always putting others' needs before her own. 她性格过度讨好，总是把别人的需求放在自己之前。
She was exhausted from her overpleasing tendencies. 她因过度讨好的倾向而精疲力竭。
近义词:ingratiating, fawning, subservient
反义词:assertive, independent
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "pleasing" (giving pleasure or satisfaction).  The word "please" comes from Old French "plaisir" meaning "to please," ultimately from Latin "placere."
