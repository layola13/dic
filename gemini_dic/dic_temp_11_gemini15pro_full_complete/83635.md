haemoglobinaemia: /ˌhiːməˌɡləʊbɪˈniːmiə/ (UK) /ˌhiməˌɡloʊbɪˈnimiə/ (US)| n. |血红蛋白血症|The presence of haemoglobin in the blood plasma, resulting from the breakdown of red blood cells.|Example:  Haemoglobinaemia can be a sign of various underlying medical conditions. 血红蛋白血症可能是各种潜在疾病的征兆。

Example: The doctor ordered tests to check for haemoglobinaemia after the patient experienced unusual bruising. 病人在出现异常瘀伤后，医生下令进行血红蛋白血症检查。|近义词: hemoglobinemia (US spelling)|反义词:  None|词性: noun
