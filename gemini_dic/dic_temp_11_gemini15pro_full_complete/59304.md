ear-splitting:/ˈɪərˌsplɪtɪŋ/
adjective
震耳欲聋的；刺耳的
Extremely loud and harsh, as if it could damage the ears.
The band played at an ear-splitting volume. 乐队演奏的声音震耳欲聋。
ear-piercing, deafening, loud, harsh
quiet, soft, muted
adjective
