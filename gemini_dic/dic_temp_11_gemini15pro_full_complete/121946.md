missingly:/ˈmɪsɪŋli/
adv.
思念地，怀念地
in a way that shows you miss someone or something
She looked at the old photo missingly. 她怀念地看着那张旧照片。
近义词: longingly, wistfully
反义词: indifferently, apathetically
词性: 副词
