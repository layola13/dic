compartments:/kəmˈpɑːrtmənts/ /kəmˈpɑːrtmənts/
n.
隔间；隔层；车厢
A separate section or part of a structure or container.
The train has compartments for smokers and non-smokers. 火车上设有吸烟者和非吸烟者的隔间。

compartments:/kəmˈpɑːrtmənts/ /kəmˈpɑːrtmənts/
n.
(知识、思想等的)领域，范围
A separate section or category of something, especially knowledge or thought.
Knowledge is not neatly divided into separate compartments. 知识并非整齐地划分成单独的领域。

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *compartiment*, from *compartir* "to divide, share", from Latin *compartiri*, from *com-* "together" + *partiri* "to divide".

SYNONYMS->
sections, chambers, divisions, partitions, cubicles

ANTONYMS->
whole, entirety, unit
