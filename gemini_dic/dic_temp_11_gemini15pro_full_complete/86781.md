helvite:/ˈhɛlvaɪt/| n.|锰铍硅酸盐矿物(赫尔维石)|A rare mineral, a complex beryllium silicate and sulfide, typically brown, yellow, or reddish in color. It is found in pegmatites and other igneous rocks.|Helvite crystals were found in the granite pegmatite. 在花岗伟晶岩中发现了赫尔维石晶体。|近义词:genthelvite, danalite|反义词:None (as it's a specific mineral name)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Helvin*, from Greek *hēlios* (sun), in allusion to its typically yellow color.
