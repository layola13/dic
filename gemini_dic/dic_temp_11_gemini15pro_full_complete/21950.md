boggy:/ˈbɒɡi/ (UK) /ˈbɔːɡi/ (US)| adj.|沼泽的；泥泞的|Resembling a bog; wet and spongy.|The ground was boggy after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，地面泥泞不堪。|近义词：marshy, swampy, miry, quaggy|反义词：dry, arid, firm, solid|词性:形容词
词源: From bog + -y.  Ultimately from the Proto-Celtic *bugō (“soft”). Cognate with Irish bogach (“marsh, bog”).
