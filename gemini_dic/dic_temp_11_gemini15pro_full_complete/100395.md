intersyllabic:/ˌɪntərˈsɪlæbɪk/ | adj. | 音节间的 | Occurring between syllables; pertaining to the sounds that occur between syllables. | The intersyllabic consonant was difficult to pronounce. 音节间的辅音很难发音。|  intersyllabic liaison occurred between the two words. 这两个词之间发生了音节间的连音。| 近义词: intersyllabical | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From inter- + syllabic.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," and "syllabic" relates to syllables.
