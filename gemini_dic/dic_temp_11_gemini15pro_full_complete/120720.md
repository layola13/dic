misallocated:/ˌmɪsˈæləkeɪtɪd/
adjective
分配不当的，错配的
incorrectly distributed or assigned.
Resources were misallocated, leading to inefficiencies. 资源分配不当，导致效率低下。
The funds were misallocated, resulting in a budget shortfall.  资金分配错误，导致预算短缺。
misused, misapplied, wrongly allocated
correctly allocated, properly allocated, appropriately allocated
词性: adjective
