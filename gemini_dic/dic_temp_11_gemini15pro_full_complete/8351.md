anti-polish's:/ˌæntiˈpɒlɪʃɪz/ | adj. | 反抛光的；抗抛光的 |  Relating to or denoting a substance or treatment that prevents or reduces the polish or shine of a surface.  It's formed by adding the prefix "anti-" (meaning against or opposing) to "polish."  While "anti-polish" is attested, the possessive form "anti-polish's" is grammatically unusual and likely only encountered in specific, limited contexts like "the anti-polish's effect was immediate."  | The anti-polish's effect dulled the shine of the car. 反抛光剂的效果使汽车的光泽变暗淡了。|  matting, dulling | polishing, shining | adjective
