brutalising:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使残暴化；使野蛮化；虐待
making someone brutal or cruel, or involving cruel and violent treatment
The war has brutalised the entire region. 战争使整个地区变得野蛮。
The soldiers were accused of brutalising the prisoners. 士兵们被指控虐待囚犯。
近义词:savaging, battering, brutalizing,  terrorizing
反义词:civilizing, humanizing
词性:v.
