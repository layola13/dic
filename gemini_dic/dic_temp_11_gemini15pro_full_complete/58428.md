duchesslike:/ˈdʌtʃɪsˌlaɪk/
adj.
像公爵夫人一样的；有公爵夫人风范的
Resembling or characteristic of a duchess, typically in manner or appearance.
She entered the room with a duchesslike air of authority. 她以一种公爵夫人般权威的姿态走进房间。
近义词:regal, stately, aristocratic, dignified
反义词:common, unrefined, vulgar
词性:形容词

duchess:/ˈdʌtʃɪs/
n.
公爵夫人；女公爵
The wife or widow of a duke, or a woman holding a rank equivalent to duke in her own right.
The Duchess of Cambridge is a popular royal figure.  剑桥公爵夫人是一位受欢迎的皇室成员。
近义词:noblewoman
反义词:commoner
词性:名词

like:/laɪk/
prep.
像；如同
Similar to; resembling.
He looks like his father. 他长得像他父亲。
adj.
相似的；同样的
Similar; alike.
They have like interests. 他们有相似的兴趣。
v.
喜欢；喜爱
To find agreeable or enjoyable.
I like swimming. 我喜欢游泳。
adv.
可能；大概
Probably; likely.
It's like to rain later. 今天晚些时候可能会下雨.
n.
相似的人或物；喜好
A similar person or thing; preference.
I've never seen the like of it. 我从未见过这样的东西。
近义词:(prep.) similar to, resembling; (adj.) alike, same; (v.) enjoy, love; (adv.) probably, likely
反义词:(v.) dislike, hate
词性:介词，形容词，动词，副词，名词


词源:
"duchess" comes from Old French "duchesse,"  feminine of "duc" ("duke"), from Latin "dux" ("leader").
"like" comes from Old English "gelic,"  meaning "similar," "equal,"  from Proto-Germanic "*galikaz."  The suffix "-like" is a common way to form adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
