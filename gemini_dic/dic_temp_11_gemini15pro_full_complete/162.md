abatable:/əˈbeɪtəbl/
adjective
可减轻的；可缓和的
Capable of being abated or reduced in degree or intensity.
The noise was loud, but thankfully abatable. 噪音很大，但幸好可以减轻。
The pollution from the factory is abatable with the right technology.  这家工厂的污染使用合适的技术可以减少。
近义词: reducible, mitigable, diminish, lessen
反义词: unyielding, intractable, ineradicable, persistent
词性: adjective
词源: From abate + -able.  Abate comes from Old French abatre "to beat down," from a- "down" + batre "to beat."
