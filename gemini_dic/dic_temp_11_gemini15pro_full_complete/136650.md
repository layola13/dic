nonscoring:/ˌnɒnˈskɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 非计分的，不计分的 | Not contributing to or part of a scoring system; not involving the keeping of a score. |  The exhibition match was nonscoring, played just for fun. 这场表演赛是非计分的，只是为了娱乐而进行的。

The nonscoring period of the game allowed the new players to practice their skills.  比赛的不计分时间让新球员有机会练习他们的技能。 | 近义词: noncompetitive, practice, unofficial | 反义词: scoring, competitive, official | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "scoring" (present participle of "score," from Old Norse "skor" meaning "notch, tally").  "Non-"是一个表示“不”的前缀，"scoring"是"score"的现在分词，源于古挪威语"skor"，意为“刻痕，计数”。
