louvring: /ˈluːvrɪŋ/
verb
卢浮宫式屋顶采光; 仿照卢浮宫式屋顶采光
Designing or constructing a roof with a louvre or series of louvres, similar to those found on the Louvre Museum.
The architect is louvring the roof of the new museum to allow natural light into the galleries.  建筑师正在为新博物馆的屋顶设计百叶窗，以便让自然光线进入展厅。
The new sports complex is being louvred to provide shade and ventilation.  新的体育中心正在安装百叶窗，以提供遮阳和通风。
Synonyms: installing louvres, adding louvres
Antonyms: sealing, closing
