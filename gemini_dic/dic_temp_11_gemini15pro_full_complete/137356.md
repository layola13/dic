nonteachability:/ˌnɒnˌtiːtʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可教性；无法被教导的状态
The nonteachability of certain concepts can be frustrating for educators.  某些概念的不可教性可能会让教育工作者感到沮丧。
The researcher explored the nonteachability of perfect pitch.  研究人员探讨了绝对音高的不可教性。
近义词: unteachability, ineducability
反义词: teachability, educability
词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- + teachability.  Teachability from teach + -ability.  Teach from Old English *tǣcan "to show, point out, direct; teach, instruct," of uncertain origin.
