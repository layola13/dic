demotivating:/diːˈməʊtɪveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人沮丧的，消极的
Causing someone to lose motivation or enthusiasm.
The constant criticism from my boss was demotivating. 我老板不断的批评令人沮丧。
Synonyms: discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting
Antonyms: motivating, inspiring, encouraging
词性:形容词
