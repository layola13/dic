devoiding:/dɪˈvɔɪdɪŋ/
verb
使缺乏；使 خالی
The process of making something empty or lacking in something.
The new policy is devoiding the city of its cultural heritage.  这项新政策正在使这座城市失去其文化遗产。
The drought is devoiding the land of its fertility.  干旱正在使土地失去肥力。
depriving, stripping, emptying
filling, replenishing
