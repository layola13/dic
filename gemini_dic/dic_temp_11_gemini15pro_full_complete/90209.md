houndiest:/ˈhaʊndɪɪst/
adjective
最猎犬般的，最像猎犬的
Most like a hound; most eager in pursuit.
The houndiest of the dogs tracked the scent relentlessly.  最猎犬般的狗无情地追踪着气味。
The detective, with his houndiest instincts, was determined to solve the mystery.  这位侦探凭借他最像猎犬的直觉，决心解开这个谜团。
近义词:keenest, most ardent, most zealous
反义词:apathetic, indifferent, lackadaisical
词性:adjective
词源:From hound + -y + -est.  Hound comes from Old English hund.
