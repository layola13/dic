answer-for:/ˈænsər fɔr, ˈɑːnsər fɔːr/
pron. | 对…负责；保证；代表；为…受责 | To be responsible for; to vouch for; to represent; to be held accountable for.
I will answer for his honesty. 我可以保证他的诚实。
The company must answer for the safety of its products. 公司必须对其产品的安全性负责。
近义词: be responsible for, vouch for, be accountable for, guarantee
反义词: disavow, repudiate
词性:  phrasal verb


answer-for:/ˈænsər fɔr, ˈɑːnsər fɔːr/
vt. | 对…负责；保证；代表；为…受责 | To be responsible for; to vouch for; to represent; to be held accountable for.
She had to answer for her mistakes. 她必须为她的错误承担责任。
He answered for the debts of his deceased father. 他为他已故父亲的债务负责。
近义词: be responsible for, vouch for, be accountable for, guarantee
反义词: disavow, repudiate
词性:  phrasal verb



词源:  "answer" comes from Old English *andswaru*, meaning "a reply." "For" comes from Old English *for*, meaning "on behalf of," "before," or "because of."  The combination "answer for" developed the sense of being responsible or accountable for something.
