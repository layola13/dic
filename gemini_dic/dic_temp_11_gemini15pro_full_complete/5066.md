all-the-better:/ɔːl ðə ˈbetər/
adjective
更好
even better; improved as a result of something.
The rain stopped, making the picnic all the better.  雨停了，野餐更好了。
It's a challenging job, but I’m all the better for it. 这是一项具有挑战性的工作，但我因此变得更好。
近义词:even better, still better, improved
反义词:worse, all the worse
词性:adjective

词源:
This phrase is a combination of "all" (meaning entirely) and "the better" (a comparative form of "good").  It emphasizes the improvement resulting from a specific action or circumstance.  The "the" before "better" acts as an intensifier.
