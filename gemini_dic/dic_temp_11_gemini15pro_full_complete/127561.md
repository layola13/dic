necroses:/nɛˈkroʊsiːz/
noun
坏死; 坏疽
the death of most or all of the cells in an organ or tissue due to disease, injury, or failure of the blood supply.
例句：The biopsies revealed areas of necrosis in the liver.  活检显示肝脏有坏死区域。

例句：Extensive necrosis of the skin and underlying tissues. 皮肤和皮下组织的大面积坏死。

近义词：gangrene, mortification, decay
反义词：growth, regeneration
词性:n.
