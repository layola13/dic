make bricks without straw:/meɪk brɪks wɪˈðaʊt strɔː/ | idiom |  巧妇难为无米之炊；无米之炊 | To attempt to do something without the necessary materials or resources.  It refers to a difficult or impossible task. |  The manager wanted us to increase sales by 50% this quarter, but with the current economic downturn, it's like making bricks without straw.  经理希望我们在本季度将销售额提高 50%，但在目前的经济低迷时期，这就像巧妇难为无米之炊。 | synonyms: attempt the impossible, work miracles, fight a losing battle | antonyms: have all the resources, be well-equipped, have everything you need | idiom
