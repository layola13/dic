lily-livered:/ˈlɪliˌlɪvərd/| adj.|胆怯的，懦弱的|Lacking courage and easily frightened; cowardly.  It suggests someone pale and weak, as if their liver is the color of a lily.|He was too lily-livered to stand up to the bully. 他太胆小了，不敢反抗那个恶霸。|近义词：cowardly, timid, faint-hearted, spineless|反义词：brave, courageous, bold, valiant|词性:形容词

lily-livered:/ˈlɪliˌlɪvərd/|词源|From the now obsolete belief that a pale liver was a sign of cowardice.|
