evanescence:/ˌɛvəˈnɛsəns/ /ˌiːvəˈnɛsəns/| n. |消逝，消失，短暂|The state of vanishing gradually from sight or memory; the quality of being fleeting or not lasting very long.|The evanescence of beauty is a common theme in poetry.  美是短暂的，这是诗歌中的常见主题。| synonyms: disappearance, fading, vanishing, transience | antonyms: permanence, persistence, endurance| noun
