anesthetized:/əˈnesθəˌtaɪzd/ | adj. | 麻醉的；失去知觉的 | deprived of feeling or awareness, especially by a drug | The patient was anesthetized before the surgery. 病人在手术前被麻醉了。|  The anesthetized tiger was easily transported.  被麻醉的老虎很容易就被运走了。| synonyms: numbed, insensible, unconscious | antonyms: conscious, aware, sensitive | adjective

anesthesia:/ˌænəsˈθiːʒə/ | n. | 麻醉；麻木 | loss of feeling or awareness, especially caused by drugs | The patient was given general anesthesia for the surgery. 病人手术时进行了全身麻醉。 | The dentist used local anesthesia to numb the tooth. 牙医使用局部麻醉来麻痹牙齿。 | synonyms: numbness, insensibility | antonyms: sensation, feeling, consciousness | noun
