federation:/ˌfedəˈreɪʃn/| n.|联盟，联邦|The United States is a federation of 50 states. 美国是由50个州组成的联邦。|A federation of trade unions represents the interests of its members. 工会联合会代表其成员的利益。|近义词：league, union, alliance, confederation|反义词：division, separation|词性: 名词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *foederatio (“alliance, confederacy”), from Latin foederatus (“leagued, allied”), from foedus (“league, treaty, covenant”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeidʰ- (“to trust, persuade, believe”).
