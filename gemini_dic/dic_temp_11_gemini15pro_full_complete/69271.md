featherlessness:/ˈfeðərləsnəs/
n.
无羽毛；秃
lack of feathers; the state of being without feathers
The featherlessness of the newborn bird made it vulnerable to the cold. 这只新生鸟没有羽毛，很容易受凉。

近义词:baldness, hairlessness
反义词:featheriness, plumage

词性:noun
