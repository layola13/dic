debilitatingly:/dɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪŋli/
adverb
使人衰弱地，严重削弱地
in a way that greatly reduces someone's strength or ability
The illness debilitated him so severely that he was barely able to walk.  这种疾病严重地削弱了他，以至于他几乎无法行走。
debilitatingly ill  病重到使人衰弱
The heat was debilitatingly intense.  高温酷暑令人虚弱。
synonyms: weakening, enervating, sapping, crippling, incapacitating
antonyms: strengthening, invigorating, empowering
词性: adverb (副词)
