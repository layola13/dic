nonvariation:/ˌnɒnˌveəriˈeɪʃn/ | n. |不变性，无变化，恒定性|The nonvariation of the results across multiple trials confirmed the stability of the experiment.  多项试验结果的恒定性证实了实验的稳定性。|例句：The nonvariation in temperature throughout the day was unusual.  全天温度不变是很不寻常的。|近义词：constancy, stability, invariance, uniformity|反义词：variation, change, fluctuation, variability|词性:名词
Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + variation (from Latin variatio, meaning "a changing, alteration")
