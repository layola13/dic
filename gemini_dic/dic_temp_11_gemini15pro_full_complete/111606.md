lobule:/ˈlɒbjuːl/ (UK) /ˈlɑːbjuːl/ (US)| n. |小叶; 小裂片|A small lobe or subdivision of a lobe, as in the lung, liver, or brain.  A clearly defined portion of an organ or gland.|The doctor identified a shadow on the lower lobule of her left lung. 医生在她左肺下叶发现了一个阴影。|Synonyms: segment, section, subdivision|Antonyms: whole, entirety|noun

lobule:/ˈlɒbjuːl/ (UK) /ˈlɑːbjuːl/ (US)| n. |耳垂|The fleshy lower part of the external ear.|Her earrings dangled from her ear lobules. 她的耳环从她的耳垂上垂下来。|Synonyms: earlobe|Antonyms: N/A|noun
