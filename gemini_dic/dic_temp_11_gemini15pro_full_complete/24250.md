briefer:/ˈbriːfər/| adj.|更简短的，更简洁的|Comparative form of "brief," meaning shorter in duration or length.|The president gave a briefer statement than usual. 总统发表了一份比平常更简短的声明。|The meeting was briefer than expected. 会议比预期的要短。|近义词：shorter, more concise, succincter|反义词：longer, more extensive, lengthier|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *bref*, from Old French *bref*, from Latin *brevis* ("short"). The "-er" suffix is a comparative ending.
