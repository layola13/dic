non-imposition:/ˌnɒnɪmpəˈzɪʃən/
n.
不强加；不强制
the fact of not forcing something on someone, or of not introducing it
The government's policy of non-imposition of tariffs helped boost international trade. 政府的不强加关税的政策有助于促进国际贸易。

The principle of non-imposition respects individual autonomy. 不强加原则尊重个人自主权。

tolerance, forbearance, noninterference, nonintervention
imposition, coercion, enforcement
