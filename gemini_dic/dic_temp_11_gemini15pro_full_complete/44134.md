cristae:/ˈkrɪstiː/
n.
嵴，褶
folds of the inner membrane of a mitochondrion
The cristae of the mitochondria are where cellular respiration occurs. 线粒体的嵴是细胞呼吸发生的地方。

近义词:folds, ridges
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "smooth inner membrane" in a specific context.)
词性:noun (plural)

词源: Latin, plural of crista, meaning crest or ridge.
