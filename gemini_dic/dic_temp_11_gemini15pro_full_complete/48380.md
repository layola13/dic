decussatively:/ˌdiːkʌˈseɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 交叉地，十字形地 |  in a decussate manner; so as to cross or intersect, often in the form of an X. |  The nerve fibers decussatively connect the two hemispheres of the brain.  神经纤维交叉连接大脑的两个半球。 | synonym: crosswise, intersectingly | antonym: parallel, longitudinally | adverb

decussate:/dɪˈkʌseɪt/ | adj. | 交叉的，十字形的 | arranged in the form of an X; intersecting. | The optic nerves decussate at the optic chiasm.  视神经在视交叉处交叉。 | synonym: crossed, intersecting | antonym: parallel, uncrossed | adjective

decussation:/ˌdiːkʌˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 交叉，十字形交叉 | an intersection or crossing, especially of two nerves or bands of muscle or tendon, in the form of an X. | The decussation of the pyramids is a prominent feature of the medulla oblongata. 锥体交叉是延髓的一个显著特征。| synonym: intersection, crossing | antonym: parallelism | noun
