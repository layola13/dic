assimilation:/əˌsɪməˈleɪʃn/
noun
同化，吸收
The assimilation of immigrants into American culture. 移民融入美国文化。
The process of assimilation can be challenging for newcomers.  同化的过程对新来者来说可能具有挑战性。
近义词: integration, absorption, incorporation
反义词: rejection, segregation, differentiation
词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin assimilātiōn- (stem of assimilātiō ), from assimilāre ‘make like’, from ad- ‘to’ + simil- ‘like’.
