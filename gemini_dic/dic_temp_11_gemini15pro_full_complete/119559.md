microsurgery:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɜːrdʒəri/ /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsɜːdʒəri/| n.|显微外科;显微手术|Surgery performed using a microscope and miniaturized instruments, typically to reattach severed blood vessels and nerves.|Microsurgery is often used to reattach severed limbs. 显微外科常用于重新连接断肢。|Synonyms:  minimally invasive surgery, delicate surgery|Antonyms:  traditional surgery, open surgery|noun
