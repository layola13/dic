mindblown:/ˈmaɪndbləʊn/| adj.| 震惊的，惊呆的；兴奋不已的| Extremely impressed or amazed, as if one's mind has been blown away by something.| Example: I was absolutely mindblown by the movie's special effects. 我完全被电影的特效惊呆了。

Example: The concert was mindblown!  这场音乐会真是太棒了！| 近义词: astonished, amazed, awestruck, stunned, impressed, thrilled| 反义词: unimpressed, bored, apathetic |词性:形容词
