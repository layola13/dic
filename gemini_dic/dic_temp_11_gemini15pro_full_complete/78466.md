geyser:/ˈɡaɪzər/ (US), /ˈɡiːzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|间歇泉|A hot spring that intermittently sends up fountain-like jets of water and steam.|The geyser erupted, sending a plume of hot water high into the air. 间歇泉喷发了，一股热水柱冲向高空。|例句：Yellowstone National Park is famous for its geysers. 黄石国家公园以其间歇泉而闻名。|近义词：hot spring, thermal spring|反义词: (None - no direct opposite for a natural phenomenon)|noun
