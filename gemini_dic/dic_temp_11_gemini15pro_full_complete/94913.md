in-groups:/ˈɪn ɡruːps/| n.| 内群体；所属群体 | A social group to which a person psychologically identifies as being a member. | Example:  She felt a strong sense of belonging to her in-group at school. 她在学校里对自己的小团体有很强的归属感。

Example:  In-group bias can lead to discrimination against out-groups. 内群体偏见会导致对外群体的歧视。| 近义词：clique, circle, inner circle | 反义词: out-groups | 词性: 名词
