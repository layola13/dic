disinfect:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkt/| vt.| 消毒，杀菌|To cleanse of infection, usually by chemical means; sterilize.|例句：The hospital staff disinfected the operating room before the surgery. 医院工作人员在手术前对手术室进行了消毒。|近义词：sterilize, sanitize, cleanse|反义词: contaminate, infect|verb
disinfected:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛk.tɪd/| adj.| 已消毒的，无菌的|Cleansed of infection, sterile.|例句：The instruments were disinfected before use. 这些器械在使用前已经过消毒。|近义词：sterilized, sanitized, cleansed|反义词: contaminated, infected|adjective
