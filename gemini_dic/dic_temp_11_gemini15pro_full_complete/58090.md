drop-a-brick:/ˌdrɒp ə ˈbrɪk/ /ˌdrɑːp ə ˈbrɪk/
idiom
犯尴尬的错误；说错话；失言
To say or do something unintentionally that causes embarrassment or offense.
He dropped a brick when he asked her how her husband was, forgetting he'd died last year. 他问她丈夫怎么样的时候说错话了，因为他忘了她丈夫去年去世了。
Synonyms: put one's foot in it, blunder, make a gaffe, make a faux pas
Antonyms: behave impeccably, be tactful
idiom
