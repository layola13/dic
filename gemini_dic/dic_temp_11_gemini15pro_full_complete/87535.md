heterogenist:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒɛnɪst/
n.
異質主義者；主張不同文化或族群融合的人
An advocate of heterogenesis.  Someone who believes in the generation of living organisms from other organisms of different kinds, or from inanimate matter; a believer in abiogenesis or spontaneous generation.
The heterogenist argued that the diverse cultures in the city should be celebrated, not suppressed.  這位異質主義者主張，城市中多元的文化應該被慶祝，而不是被壓制。
近义词: pluralist, multiculturalist, integrationist
反义词: segregationist, separatist, nativist
词性: noun
