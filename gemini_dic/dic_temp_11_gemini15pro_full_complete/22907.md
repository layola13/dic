bothering:/ˈbɒðərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːðərɪŋ/ (US)|v. |打扰，烦扰；费心，麻烦；使担心|Causing trouble or annoyance to someone; taking the trouble to do something.|例句：I didn't want to bother her with my problems. 我不想用我的问题打扰她。|近义词：annoying, disturbing, troubling, inconveniencing, pestering|反义词：soothing, calming, comforting|verb (present participle)

bothering:/ˈbɒðərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːðərɪŋ/ (US)|adj. |令人烦恼的，讨厌的|Causing trouble or annoyance.|例句：It's a bothering noise. 这是一个令人烦恼的噪音。|近义词：annoying, irritating, troublesome|反义词：pleasant, agreeable|adjective (present participle)
