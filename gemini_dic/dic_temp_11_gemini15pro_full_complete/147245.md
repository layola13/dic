overmelodious:/ˌōvərˈməˈlōdiəs/| adj.|过于悦耳的，甜腻的|Excessively or cloyingly melodious; too sweet or sentimental in sound.|The overmelodious music made me feel a bit sick.  这过于甜腻的音乐让我觉得有点恶心。|例句：The soundtrack was criticized for being overmelodious and distracting from the film's narrative.  这部电影的配乐被批评过于甜美，分散了人们对电影叙事的注意力。|近义词：saccharine, cloying, sentimental|反义词：discordant, harsh, cacophonous|词性:adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "melodious" (from "melody" + "-ous," meaning "full of").  "Melody" ultimately comes from Greek "melos" (song).
