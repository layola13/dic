commutator:/ˈkɒmjʊteɪtə(r)/ (US: /ˈkɑːmjəteɪtər/) | n. | 换向器; 整流子 | A device used in electric motors to reverse the direction of current flow in the rotor windings, allowing the motor to continue rotating. | The commutator in my vacuum cleaner is worn out. 我的吸尘器的换向器磨损了。| synonym: rectifier | antonym: N/A | noun


commutate:/kəˈmjuːt/ (US: /ˈkɑːmjuːt/) | v. |  通勤; 交换; 减刑 | 1. To travel regularly between work and home. 2. (Mathematics) To change the order of two operations. 3. (Law) To reduce a sentence. | 1. I commute to work by train. 我乘火车上下班。 2. The operations of addition and multiplication commute. 加法和乘法运算可交换。 3. His death sentence was commuted to life imprisonment. 他的死刑被减为无期徒刑。| synonym: travel, exchange, reduce | antonym: stay, remain, increase | verb
