impierceable:/ɪmˈpɪərsəbl/
adjective
不可穿透的；无法刺穿的
not able to be pierced
The knight's armor was impierceable to the enemy's swords. 骑士的盔甲敌人之剑无法刺穿。
The fortress boasted impierceable walls.  这座堡垒拥有坚不可摧的城墙。
近义词: impenetrable, impervious, invulnerable
反义词: penetrable, permeable, vulnerable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Middle English *imperceable, from Anglo-Norman empersable, from Old French imperçable, from in- + perçable (“pierceable”), equivalent to im- + pierceable.
