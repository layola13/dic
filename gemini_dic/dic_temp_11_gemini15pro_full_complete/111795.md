lofts:/lɔːfts/ /lɑːfts/| n.|阁楼，顶楼公寓，高尔夫球杆的杆面倾斜度|A large, open space at the top of a building, often converted into an apartment; the sloping face of a golf club.|例句：They converted the lofts into luxury apartments. 他们把阁楼改建成了豪华公寓。

例句：He checked the lofts on his golf clubs. 他检查了他的高尔夫球杆的杆面倾斜度。|近义词：attics, garrets (for living spaces), loft spaces|反义词:basement, cellar|词性:名词

loft:/lɔːft/ /lɑːft/| n. v.|阁楼，顶楼公寓，高尔夫球杆的杆面倾斜度；把球打高，使球升高|A large, open space at the top of a building, often converted into an apartment; the sloping face of a golf club; to hit or throw a ball high in the air.|例句：The old factory was converted into lofts.  这个旧工厂被改建成了阁楼公寓。

例句：He lofted the ball over the defender's head. 他把球高高地踢过了防守队员的头顶。|近义词：attic, garret (for space), chip, pitch (for hitting a ball high)|反义词:basement, cellar (for space), ground (for hitting a ball low)|词性:名词，动词
