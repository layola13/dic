bonnily:/ˈbɒnɪli/| adv.|美好地，愉快地，健康地|In a cheerful, pleasant, or healthy way.  Describing something done in a happy, attractive, or healthy manner.|She smiled bonnily at the camera. 她对着镜头愉快地笑了笑。|近义词：cheerfully, pleasantly, attractively, prettily|反义词：unhappily, gloomily, unpleasantly|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "bonny" (meaning attractive, beautiful, healthy) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Bonny" comes from the Old French "bon" (good).
