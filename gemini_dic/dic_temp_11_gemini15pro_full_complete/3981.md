aguelike:/ˈeɪɡlaɪk/| adj.|类似疟疾的，类似寒战的|Resembling or characteristic of an ague, a fit of shivering or shaking, typically associated with malaria or other illnesses.|She experienced aguelike symptoms after contracting the virus. 她感染病毒后出现了类似疟疾的症状。|The sudden chills felt aguelike. 突如其来的寒意感觉像是疟疾。|近义词：feverish, shivering, chilly|反义词: healthy, well|词性:形容词


Etymology:
From "ague" + "-like".  "Ague" comes from the Old French "ague," from Latin "acuta febris" meaning "sharp fever."  "-like" is a common suffix denoting resemblance.
