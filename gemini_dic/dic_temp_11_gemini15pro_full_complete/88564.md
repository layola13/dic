hitchhiker:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪkər/| n.|搭便车的人，免费搭车旅行的人|A hitchhiker is someone who travels by getting free rides in passing vehicles. |He picked up a hitchhiker on the highway. 他在高速公路上载了一名搭便车的人。|Synonyms: free rider, thumb traveler|Antonyms: driver, passenger|noun

hitchhike:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪk/| v.|搭便车旅行，免费搭车|To hitchhike is to travel by getting free rides in passing vehicles. |She hitchhiked across Europe last summer. 她去年夏天搭便车游遍了欧洲。|Synonyms: thumb, bum a ride|Antonyms: drive, pay for transportation|verb
