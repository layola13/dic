multi-national:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃnəl/ | n. | 跨国公司 |  A company with operations in multiple countries. | Example:  The multi-national announced record profits this year. 这家跨国公司今年宣布了创纪录的利润。


Etymology->
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many") + "national" (from "nation" ultimately from Latin "natio" meaning "birth, race, nation").

Synonyms->
international, global, transnational, cosmopolitan

Antonyms->
domestic, local, national

Word type->
Adjective, Noun
