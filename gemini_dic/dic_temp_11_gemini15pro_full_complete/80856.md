gourmandisms:/ˈɡʊərməndɪzəmz/
n.
美食主义；讲究饮食
Excessive indulgence in fine food; gourmet habits or tendencies.
He indulged in gourmandisms, savoring every bite of the exquisite meal. 他沉迷于美食，细细品味每一口精致的菜肴。
gourmet habits, culinary indulgences, epicureanism
asceticism, abstinence, moderation
noun
