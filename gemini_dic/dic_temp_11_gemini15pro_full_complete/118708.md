metaconule: /ˈmɛtəkɒnjuːl/
n.
后尖
A conical elevation on the posterior side of a molluscan shell.
The metaconule is a distinct feature on some species of snails.  后尖是某些蜗牛物种的一个显著特征。
近义词: cusp, prominence
反义词: depression, groove
词性: noun

Etymology: meta- (Greek, meaning "after, beyond") + conule (diminutive of cone)
