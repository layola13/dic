nonadvocacies:/nɒnˈædvəkəsiːz/
|n.| 非拥护；不提倡 |Lack of support or promotion for a particular cause or policy.  It refers to the absence of active advocacy.|Due to the company's nonadvocacies on environmental issues, they faced criticism from activists.| 近义词:inaction,  passivity, neutrality | 反义词:advocacies, support, promotion|词性:名词
