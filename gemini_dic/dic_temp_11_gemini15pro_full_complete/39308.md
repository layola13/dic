coniferous:/kəˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
松柏类的；针叶类的
Relating to or belonging to the conifers; bearing cones.  Characterized by evergreen, needle-like or scale-like leaves and bearing cones.
The mountains are covered in dense coniferous forest.  这些山脉覆盖着茂密的针叶林。
The scent of the coniferous trees filled the air. 针叶树的香味弥漫在空气中。
近义词: evergreen, cone-bearing
反义词: deciduous
词性: adjective
