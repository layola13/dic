crevassed: /krɪˈvæst/ | adj. | 有裂缝的，布满裂痕的 | Covered with crevasses (deep cracks or fissures, especially in a glacier). | The climbers carefully navigated the crevassed glacier.  登山者小心翼翼地穿过了布满裂缝的冰川。 | Example 2: The crevassed terrain made the hike extremely dangerous.  这片布满裂缝的地形使徒步旅行变得极其危险。 | Synonyms: fissured, cracked, fractured | Antonyms: smooth, unblemished | word type: adjective

crevasse: /krɪˈvæs/ | n. | (尤指冰川的)裂缝，裂隙 | A deep crack or fissure, especially in a glacier. |  The mountaineers had to rope themselves together to avoid falling into a crevasse. 登山者必须用绳子把自己系在一起，以免掉进裂缝里。 | Example 2:  The crevasse was so deep they couldn't see the bottom.  这个裂缝太深了，他们看不到底部。| Synonyms: fissure, chasm, rift, crack | Antonyms: closure, seal | word type: noun
