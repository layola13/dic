fictions:/ˈfɪkʃənz/
n.
虚构的事物；小说；杜撰的故事
prose narratives, typically representing character and action, that are imagined or invented rather than based on fact.
She writes historical fictions. 她写历史小说。
近义词：fabrications, inventions, stories, novels, tales
反义词：facts, realities, truths
词性：名词
