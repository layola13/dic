dickerer:/ˈdɪkərər/
noun
讨价还价者; 讲价者
Someone who dickers or barters.
The dickerer spent hours haggling over the price of the rug.  讨价还价者花了几个小时的时间讨价还价地毯的价格。
近义词: bargainer, haggler, negotiator
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider: buyer, seller, customer)
词性: noun


dicker:/ˈdɪkər/
verb
讨价还价; 讲价
To bargain or haggle, typically over a price.
They dickered over the price of the car for hours. 他们为了汽车的价格讨价还价了几个小时。
近义词: bargain, haggle, negotiate
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider: agree, concede)
词性: verb
