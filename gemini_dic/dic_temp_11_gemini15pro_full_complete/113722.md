macrocosmical:/ˌmækroʊˈkɒzmɪkəl/
adjective
宏观的; 宇宙的
Relating to the macrocosm; on a large scale, universal.  Concerning the whole universe.
例句：His macrocosmical view of history encompasses millennia. 他对历史的宏观视角涵盖了数千年。

例句：The philosopher's theories explored the macrocosmical order of the universe. 这位哲学家的理论探讨了宇宙的宏观秩序。

近义词：universal, cosmic, global, worldwide
反义词：microcosmical, local, limited

词性: adjective

词源:  From macrocosm + -ical.  Macrocosm from Greek *makros kosmos, "great world,"  from makros "long, large" + kosmos "order, world."
