lunches:/ˈlʌntʃɪz/
noun
午餐(复数)
The noontime meal.  A meal eaten in the middle of the day.
We packed our lunches for the picnic. 我们打包了午餐去野餐。
The school provides free lunches for eligible students. 学校为符合条件的学生提供免费午餐。
meals, dinners
