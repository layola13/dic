over-read:/ˌoʊvərˈriːd/ | v. | 过度解读；读错；忽略 | To interpret something too deeply or too literally, often finding meanings that were not intended; to misread or skip over (a word or passage) while reading. |  I think I over-read his email and got the wrong impression. 我想我过度解读了他的邮件，得到了错误的印象。| synonyms: misinterpret, misread, overanalyze | antonyms: understand, comprehend | verb


over-read:/ˌoʊvərˈriːd/ | v. |  读错；忽略| To fail to notice or to skip over (a word or passage) while reading. | He over-read the instructions and missed an important step. 他错读了说明书，漏掉了一个重要步骤。 | synonyms: skip, miss, overlook | antonyms: notice, observe | verb
