adjective
非渗出的
Not exuding; not discharging fluids or secretions.
The wound was clean and nonexuding.  伤口干净且没有渗出。
The bandage covered the nonexuding incision. 绷带覆盖着没有渗液的切口。
近义词: dry, non-oozing, non-secreting
反义词: exuding, oozing, weeping, suppurating
