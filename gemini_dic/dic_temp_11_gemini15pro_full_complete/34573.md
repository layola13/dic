clearers:/ˈklɪərərz/| n.|清算人，清算公司，清除者|People or organizations that settle transactions, debts, or clear goods. |The clearers processed the payments efficiently.  清算人员高效地处理了付款。|Synonyms: settlers, liquidators, processors |Antonyms: accumulators, hoarders|noun

clearers:/ˈklɪərərz/| n.|清除者，清理者|People or things that remove something. |The clearers removed the debris after the storm. 清理人员在暴风雨后清除了碎片。|Synonyms: removers, cleaners, eliminators |Antonyms: creators, builders|noun
