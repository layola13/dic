keelhaul:/ˈkiːlhɔːl/
|n. vt.|龙骨刑；拖船底；严厉惩罚|The act of punishing someone by dragging them under a ship's keel.  将某人拖过船底进行惩罚的行为。|The captain threatened to keelhaul any man caught stealing. 船长威胁要对任何被抓到偷窃的人施以龙骨刑。|近义词：punish, flog, chastise|反义词：reward, praise|词性:名词,动词

keelhauls:/ˈkiːlhɔːlz/
|vt.|对…施以龙骨刑；严厉惩罚|Third-person singular present tense form of keelhaul. keelhaul的第三人称单数现在时形式|He keelhauls anyone who disobeys his orders. 他对任何不服从他命令的人施以龙骨刑。|近义词：punishes, flogs, chastises|反义词：rewards, praises|词性:动词
