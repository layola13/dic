hackster:/ˈhæk.stər/ (US), /ˈhæk.stə/ (UK)| n.|黑客;  技术爱好者;  热衷于电子制作的人|A person who enjoys exploring and building electronic projects, often involving hardware and software.  Someone who likes to tinker with and modify technology. |例句：The hackster built a robot that could follow him around.  这个技术爱好者制作了一个可以跟着他到处走的机器人。

例句：She's a real hackster and loves taking apart old electronics to see how they work. 她是一个真正的技术爱好者，喜欢拆开旧电子产品看看它们是如何工作的。|近义词：maker, tinkerer, hobbyist, enthusiast|反义词:Luddite|词性:noun
