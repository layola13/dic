brezilin: /brəˈzɪlɪn/ /ˈbrɛzɪlɪn/
noun
巴西木素；苏木色素
A crystalline compound, C₁₆H₁₄O₅, extracted from brazilwood as yellowish or colorless crystals and used as a stain in microscopy and as a red dye.
Brazilin is a natural dye extracted from brazilwood. 巴西木素是从巴西木中提取的天然染料。
Brazilin stains cell nuclei red. 巴西木素将细胞核染成红色。
Synonyms: brazilin dye, Natural Red 24
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
