obloquies:/ˈɑːbləkwiz/
n.
责骂，诽谤
Strong public criticism or verbal abuse.
The politician resigned amidst a torrent of obloquies.  这位政治家在一片责骂声中辞职了。
Synonyms:  abuse, vituperation, condemnation, censure, defamation, slander, calumny, aspersions
Antonyms:  praise, commendation, acclaim, accolades
Noun
