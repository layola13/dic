biller's:/ˈbɪlərz/| n.|开账单的人的；开票员的|Something that belongs to or is associated with a biller.  It refers to a person or entity that issues bills or invoices for goods or services.|This is the biller's office, where they process all the invoices. 这是开票员的办公室，他们在这里处理所有发票。|Synonyms: invoicer's, accountant's (in some contexts)|Antonyms: payer's, customer's|noun (possessive)

Etymology: From "biller" + "'s" (possessive suffix). "Biller" comes from the verb "bill," which originated from the Old French word "bille," meaning a written document or note.
