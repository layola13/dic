例句：She hated being matronized by the nurses. 她讨厌护士们对她摆出一副恩赐的态度。|近义词：patronize, condescend|反义词：respect, admire|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From matron + -ize.  "Matron" comes from Old French matrone, ultimately from Latin māter (mother).  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs from nouns or adjectives.
