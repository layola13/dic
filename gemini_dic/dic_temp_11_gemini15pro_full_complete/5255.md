allodially:/ˌæləˈdaɪəli/
adverb
完全地；绝对地；无条件地
Owned completely and outright, without any feudal dues or other obligations.
He inherited the land allodially. 他完全继承了这片土地。
Synonyms: absolutely, outright, freely, unconditionally
Antonyms: conditionally, restrictedly
词性: 副词
