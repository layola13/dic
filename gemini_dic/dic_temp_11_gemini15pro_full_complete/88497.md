historiographer:/hɪˌstɔːriˈɒɡrəfər/ | n. | 史学家；历史编纂者 | A person who writes about history; a historian, especially one who produces a scholarly interpretation of history. |  Barbara Tuchman was a renowned historiographer of the American Civil War.  芭芭拉·塔奇曼是一位著名的美国内战史学家。

| Example: The historiographer spent years researching the archives.  这位史学家花了数年时间研究档案。 | Synonym: historian, chronicler, annalist | Antonym: N/A | noun
