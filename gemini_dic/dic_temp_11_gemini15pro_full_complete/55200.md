dissert:/dɪˈsɜːrt/ | v. | 论述，讲述（尤指正式地）| to speak or write authoritatively about a subject | He disserted at length on the merits of free trade. 他详细论述了自由贸易的好处。 | 近义词: discuss, expound, discourse | 反义词: summarize, condense | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Latin *dissertare*, frequentative of *disserere* "to discuss, debate," from *dis- "apart" + *serere* "to join, link, bind, interweave." Originally meaning "to examine, discuss," the sense shifted to "speak formally, deliver a dissertation."
