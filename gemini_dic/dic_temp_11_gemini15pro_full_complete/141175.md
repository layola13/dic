oogenesis:/ˌoʊəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
卵子发生
The process of formation and development of the ovum.
Oogenesis is the process by which female gametes, or ova, are created. 卵子发生是产生雌性配子或卵子的过程。
The hormones involved in oogenesis are complex. 参与卵子发生的激素很复杂。
近义词: ovogenesis, oögenesis
反义词: spermatogenesis
词性: noun
