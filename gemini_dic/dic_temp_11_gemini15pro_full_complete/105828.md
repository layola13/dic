kit-up:/kɪt ˈʌp/
verb
装备齐全，穿戴好装备
to put on special clothes or equipment for a particular activity
例句：The climbers kitted up and started their ascent. 登山者们穿戴好装备，开始攀登。

例句：We kitted up in our protective gear before entering the contaminated area. 我们在进入污染区之前穿戴好了防护装备。

近义词：gear up, equip, outfit
反义词：undress, disrobe
词性：verb (phrasal verb)

kit-up:/kɪt ˈʌp/
noun
装备，全套装备 (较少用作名词)
the act of putting on special clothes or equipment; the clothes or equipment themselves
例句：The kit-up took longer than expected. 穿戴装备的时间比预期的要长。(较少用)

例句：His kit-up was impressive and included all the latest technology. 他的装备令人印象深刻，包括了所有最新的技术。(较少用)

近义词：gear, equipment, outfit
反义词：N/A
词性：noun (informal, less common)

词源：kit (n.) + up (adv.)
"kit" 源自中古荷兰语 kitte，指“木桶，罐子”。"up" 作为副词表示完成或彻底的动作。 "kit-up"  作为短语动词，指的是将所有必要的装备穿戴或准备好。
