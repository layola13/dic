kirklike:/ˈkɜːrkˌlaɪk/| adj.|像教堂一样的，教堂似的|Resembling or characteristic of a church, especially in appearance or atmosphere.|The building had a kirklike quality, with its high arched windows and quiet solemnity.|近义词：churchlike, ecclesiastical, chapel-like|反义词:secular, worldly|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Scots "kirk" (church) + "-like" (suffix indicating resemblance). "Kirk" ultimately derives from Old Norse "kirkja" (church).
