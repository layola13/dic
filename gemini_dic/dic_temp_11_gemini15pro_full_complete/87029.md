hepatitides:/ˌhɛpətˈaɪtɪdiːz/
n.
肝炎（复数）
Inflammation of the liver; plural of hepatitis.  This refers to multiple instances or types of liver inflammation.
Several different viruses can cause hepatitides.  几种不同的病毒可以引起肝炎。
近义词:liver inflammations
反义词:None (absence of inflammation is not a direct antonym)
词性:noun


hepatitides:/ˌhɛpətˈaɪtɪdiːz/
词源:From Ancient Greek  ἧπαρ (hêpar, “liver”) +  -itis (“inflammation”) + -ides (plural suffix).
英文解析:The plural form of "hepatitis," referring to multiple instances or types of liver inflammation.
例句：The doctor diagnosed several different hepatitides in the patient population. 医生在患者群体中诊断出几种不同的肝炎。
The various hepatitides can have different causes and treatments. 各种肝炎可以有不同的病因和治疗方法。

近义词:liver inflammations (less common)
反义词:None.  Health or absence of inflammation are not direct opposites.
词性:Noun
