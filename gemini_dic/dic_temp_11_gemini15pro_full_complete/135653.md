nonpredictive:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈdɪktɪv/
adjective
不可预测的
Not able to be predicted; unpredictable.
The weather is notoriously nonpredictive, making it difficult to plan outdoor events.  天气是出了名的不可预测，使得计划户外活动很困难。
The stock market's recent nonpredictive behavior has investors worried.  股票市场最近不可预测的表现让投资者感到担忧。
synonym: unpredictable, unforeseeable, erratic
antonym: predictable, foreseeable, consistent
词性: adjective
