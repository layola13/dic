neurotransmitters:/ˌnʊərəʊtrænzˈmɪtərz/ /ˌnʊroʊtrænzˈmɪtərz/| n. |神经递质|Chemicals that transmit signals across a synapse from one neuron to another target neuron, muscle cell, or gland cell.|Neurotransmitters like dopamine and serotonin play a crucial role in mood regulation. 多巴胺和血清素等神经递质在情绪调节中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: chemical messengers, signaling molecules|反义词: None (no direct opposite exists)|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From neuro- + transmitter.  "Neuro-" comes from Greek "neuron" (nerve).  "Transmitter" comes from Latin "transmittere" (to send across).
