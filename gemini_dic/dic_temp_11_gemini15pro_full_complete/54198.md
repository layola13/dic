disfaith:/dɪsˈfeɪθ/ | n. |不信；不忠；背信|Lack of faith; disloyalty; treachery. | He showed disfaith to his friends by revealing their secrets. 他通过泄露朋友的秘密对他们表示不忠。 | 近义词: infidelity, treachery, perfidy, betrayal | 反义词: faith, loyalty, fidelity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From dis- + faith.  The prefix "dis-" indicates negation or reversal, combined with "faith" meaning trust or belief.
