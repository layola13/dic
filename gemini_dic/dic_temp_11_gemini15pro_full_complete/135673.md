nonpremiums:/ˌnɒnˈpriːmiəmz/
n.|非溢价商品/服务; 非赠品商品/服务|Goods or services offered without an extra charge or not as a promotional giveaway.|The store offered nonpremiums like basic pens and notepads alongside premium gift items.|Synonyms: standard products, regular merchandise|Antonyms: premiums, promotional items|noun

nonpremium:/ˌnɒnˈpriːmiəm/
adj.|非赠品的; 非溢价的|Not given away for free or as a bonus; not of superior quality or value.|The nonpremium version of the software has limited features.|Synonyms: standard, basic, regular|Antonyms: premium, high-end, superior|adjective
