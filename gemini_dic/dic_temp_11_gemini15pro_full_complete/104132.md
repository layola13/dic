juncite:/ˈdʒʌŋksaɪt/| n. |锰硬绿泥石|A dark green mineral, a hydrous silicate of iron, manganese, and aluminum, found in metamorphic rocks.|The geologist identified the green mineral as juncite. 地质学家鉴定这种绿色矿物为锰硬绿泥石。|近义词:chlorite, greenalite (these are similar minerals but not exact synonyms) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
