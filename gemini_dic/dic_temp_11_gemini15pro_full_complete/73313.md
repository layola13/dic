foreclose: /fɔːrˈkloʊz/ /fɔːrˈkloʊz/
verb
取消赎回权，排除；预先处理，预先阻止
To take possession of a mortgaged property as a result of the mortgagor's failure to keep up mortgage payments.  Also, to prevent something from happening.
The bank foreclosed on their house after they missed several mortgage payments. 银行在他们拖欠数月房贷后取消了他们的房屋赎回权。
His knee injury foreclosed any chance of him playing professional basketball.  他的膝伤让他失去了打职业篮球的任何机会。
synonyms: repossess, seize, preclude, prevent
antonyms: allow, permit, facilitate
词性: 动词 (v.)
