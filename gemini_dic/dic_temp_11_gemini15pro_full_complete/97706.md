inimicalness:/ˌɪnɪˈmɪklnəs/
n.|敌意，不利，有害|The state or quality of being inimical; hostility or unfriendliness.|The inimicalness of the environment hindered the growth of the plants.  环境的恶劣阻碍了植物的生长。|Synonyms: hostility, antagonism, animosity, unfriendliness, malevolence|Antonyms: friendliness, amity, benevolence, goodwill|noun

inimical:/ɪˈnɪmɪk(ə)l/
adj.|敌意的，不利的，有害的|tending to obstruct or harm.|The policy proved inimical to the interests of small businesses. 这项政策对小企业的利益有害。|Synonyms: hostile, antagonistic, unfriendly, harmful, detrimental|Antonyms: friendly, helpful, beneficial, advantageous|adjective
