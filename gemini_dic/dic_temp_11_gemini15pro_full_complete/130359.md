non-unanimous:/ˌnɒn juːˈnænɪməs/ | adj. | 非一致的，不全体一致的 | Not agreed upon by everyone; lacking unanimity. | The jury returned a non-unanimous verdict. 陪审团做出了非一致的裁决。 | The decision was non-unanimous, with two members dissenting.  这项决定并非一致通过，有两名成员表示异议。 | Near Synonyms: divided, split, disagreeing | Antonyms: unanimous, united, agreed | adjective
