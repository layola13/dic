eurhythmics:/jʊˈrɪðmɪks/
noun
韵律体操；韵律学
A system of coordinated exercises, especially for children, designed to develop rhythm and movement.  It also refers to the study or theory of rhythm.
She studied eurhythmics in college. 她大学时学习了韵律体操。
Eurhythmics helps children develop their coordination. 韵律体操帮助孩子们发展协调能力。
synonyms: rhythmic movement, rhythmic exercises,  rhythmics
antonyms: arrhythmia, dysrhythmia
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *eurhythmique*, from Greek *eurhythmos* "rhythmical, harmonious," from *eu-* "well" + *rhythmos* "rhythm."
