catechiser:/ˈkætɪˌkaɪzər/| n.|教导者，盘问者|Someone who catechises, or instructs by question and answer.|The catechiser asked the children about the Ten Commandments.|Synonyms: instructor, teacher, examiner, interrogator|Antonyms: student, pupil|noun

catechise:/ˈkætɪˌkaɪz/| v.|教导，盘问|To instruct by question and answer; to interrogate, often closely or formally.|The priest catechised the children before their first communion.|Synonyms: instruct, teach, examine, interrogate|Antonyms: learn, study|verb
