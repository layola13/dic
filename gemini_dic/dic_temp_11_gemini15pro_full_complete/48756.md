definitively:/dɪˈfɪnɪtɪvli/| adv.|明确地，最后地，决定性地|Conclusive; serving to settle or decide a question; final.|The doctor definitively diagnosed the illness as pneumonia. 医生最终确诊该疾病为肺炎。|近义词：conclusively, decisively, finally, absolutely|反义词：inconclusively, tentatively, indefinitely|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dēfīnītīvus, from Latin dēfīnītus (past participle of dēfīnīre ‘to define, limit’), from dē- ‘completely’ + fīnīre ‘to end, limit’.
