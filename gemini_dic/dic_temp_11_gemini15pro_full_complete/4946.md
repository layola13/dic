alitrunk: /ˈælɪˌtrʌŋk/ | n. | 融合胸部和腹部 | The fused thorax and abdomen in arachnids (spiders, scorpions, mites, etc.) and some crustaceans. | The spider's alitrunk bore its eight legs. 蜘蛛的融合胸腹部支撑着它的八条腿。| synonym: cephalothorax (for arachnids) | antonym: abdomen (in insects) | noun
