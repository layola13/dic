monoclinal:/ˌmɒnəˈklaɪnəl/ | adj. n. |单斜的，单斜层 |  A geological structure in which rock strata are inclined in a single direction, like a tilted plane.  A fold in rock strata in which both limbs dip in the same direction. | The monocline formed a gentle slope across the landscape. 单斜层在地表形成了一个缓坡。 The geologist studied the monocline to understand the region's tectonic history. 地质学家研究了这个单斜层，以了解该地区的构造历史。| 近义词: homocline | 反义词: anticline, syncline | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From mono- (one) + -cline (slope, inclination), from Greek *klinein (to lean, slope).
