money-lender:/ˈmʌniˌlɛndər/ | n. | 放债人；贷款人 | A person or institution that lends money, often at high interest rates. |  He borrowed money from a money-lender. 他从一个放债人那里借了钱。

The money-lender charged exorbitant interest.  这个放债人收取过高的利息。


| 近义词：loan shark, usurer, creditor | 反义词：borrower, debtor | 词性: noun
