disassiduity:/ˌdɪsæsɪˈdjuːɪti/ | n. | 懈怠，懒惰 | Lack of diligence or persistence; neglect of duty. |  His disassiduity in his studies led to failing grades.  他学习上的懈怠导致成绩不及格。| 近义词: negligence, indolence, laziness | 反义词: assiduity, diligence, industry | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *disassiduitas, from dis- + assiduitas "assiduity".  The English word was first recorded in the 17th century.
