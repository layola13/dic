arrogantness:/ˈærəɡəntnəs/
n.
傲慢；自大
The quality of being arrogant; overbearing pride.
His arrogantness alienated many of his colleagues. 他的傲慢疏远了许多同事。
Synonyms: haughtiness, pride, superciliousness, conceit, egotism
Antonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness
noun
