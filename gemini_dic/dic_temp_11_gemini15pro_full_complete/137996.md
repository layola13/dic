noodling:/ˈnuːdlɪŋ/| n. vi.| 捞面，(尤指)徒手捉鱼；漫无目的地思考；随意弹奏 |The act of fishing for catfish using only one's bare hands; also, to improvise on a musical instrument, or to think or consider something in a casual or desultory way.|例句：He went noodling for catfish in the river. 他去河里徒手捉鲶鱼。|近义词：fishing (for catfish), improvising, pondering, musing|反义词:planning, concentrating|词性:名词,动词
