delectate:/dɪˈlɛkteɪt/
verb
使高兴，使愉快
to give great pleasure or delight to
The chef delectated the guests with his culinary creations.  厨师用他的烹饪作品使客人们感到高兴。
近义词:delight, please, gratify
反义词:displease, annoy, bore
词性:v.

delectation:/ˌdiːlɛkˈteɪʃən/
noun
愉快，高兴
great pleasure or delight
Listening to classical music is a source of great delectation for me.  听古典音乐对我来说是一种很大的乐趣。
近义词:pleasure, delight, enjoyment
反义词:displeasure, annoyance, boredom
词性:n.
