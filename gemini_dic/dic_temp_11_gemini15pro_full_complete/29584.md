catechisation:/ˌkætɪkaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 教导；问答式教学；教义问答 |  The process of instructing someone in the principles of a Christian religion by means of questions and answers.  | The catechisation of young children is considered important in many churches.  孩子们接受教义问答的教育在许多教会中被认为是重要的。 | instruction, teaching, indoctrination |  secularization | noun


catechize:/ˈkætɪkaɪz/ | v. | 教导；盘问；以问答法教导 | To instruct someone in the principles of a Christian religion by means of questions and answers; to question someone thoroughly or aggressively. |  The priest catechized the children before their first communion.  神父在孩子们初领圣餐前对他们进行了教导。| instruct, teach, question, interrogate | neglect, ignore | verb
