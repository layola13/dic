bacterial:/bækˈtɪəriəl/
adjective|细菌的，与细菌有关的|Relating to or caused by bacteria.|Bacterial infection can be treated with antibiotics. 细菌感染可以用抗生素治疗。|近义词:microbial, germinal|反义词:viral, fungal|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Modern Latin *bacterialis*, from bacterium + -al.  "Bacterium" comes from the Greek baktērion "small staff, rod," diminutive of baktron "stick, staff."
