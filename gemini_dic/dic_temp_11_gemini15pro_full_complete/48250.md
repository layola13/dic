decorates:/ˈdekəˌreɪts/
verb
装饰，装点
makes something look more attractive by adding extra items or images to it.
The family decorates the Christmas tree every year.  这个家庭每年都会装饰圣诞树。
She decorated the cake with icing and sprinkles. 她用糖霜和糖屑装饰蛋糕。
synonyms: adorns, embellishes, ornaments, trims
antonyms: strips, bares, denudes
