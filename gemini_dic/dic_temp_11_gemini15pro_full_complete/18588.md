benignancy:/bɪˈnɪɡnənsi/
n.
仁慈；良性；温和
kindness, gentleness, mildness of nature or disposition.
The doctor assured her of the benignancy of the tumor. 医生向她保证肿瘤是良性的。
近义词：kindness, compassion, benevolence, gentleness, mildness
反义词：malevolence, malignity, cruelty, harshness
词性：名词

benignancy:/bɪˈnɪɡnənsi/
n.
有利条件；有利环境
favorable conditions; a beneficial influence.
The benignancy of the climate made it a popular vacation spot.  宜人的气候使它成为一个受欢迎的度假胜地。
近义词：advantage, benefit, boon
反义词：disadvantage, drawback, hindrance
词性：名词
