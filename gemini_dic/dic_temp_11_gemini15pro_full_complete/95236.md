近义词:confinement, imprisonment, captivity
反义词:freedom, liberation, release
词性:名词

词源:  From in- + cage + -ment.  "In-" signifies "in" or "into," "cage" refers to a structure of bars or wires used to confine, and "-ment" is a suffix forming nouns that denote an action, process, or result.
