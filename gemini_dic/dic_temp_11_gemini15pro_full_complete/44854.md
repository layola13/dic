crustless:/ˈkrʌstləs/| adj.|无壳的，去边的|Without a crust; having the crust removed.  Specifically referring to food items, typically pies or quiches, that are made without a pastry crust.|I prefer crustless quiche because it's lighter. 我更喜欢无壳的乳蛋饼，因为它更清淡。|We made a crustless apple pie for Thanksgiving. 我们感恩节做了无壳苹果派。|近义词:uncrusted|反义词:crusted|词性:形容词
