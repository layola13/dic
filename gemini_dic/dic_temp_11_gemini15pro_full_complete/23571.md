braised:/breɪzd/| v. | 焖；炖 | cooked by browning in fat and then simmering in a closed container with a small amount of liquid |  The chef braised the short ribs for hours until they were fall-off-the-bone tender.  厨师将牛小排炖了几个小时，直到它们变得入口即化。

The braised pork belly was rich and flavorful.  红烧肉肥而不腻，香味浓郁。


近义词：stewed, simmered, pot-roasted | 反义词：roasted, grilled, fried | 词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *braiser*, from Old French *braisier* "to burn, roast," from *braise* "embers, burning wood," from Frankish *brasa* "embers."
