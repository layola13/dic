multiplexor:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksər/ | n. | 多路复用器，多工器 | A device that selects one of several input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line. | The multiplexor routes the data from several sources to one destination.  多路复用器将来自多个源的数据路由到一个目的地。|  The network uses a multiplexor to combine multiple data streams onto a single fiber optic cable. 网络使用多路复用器将多个数据流组合到一根光纤电缆上。 | synonyms: mux, data selector | antonyms: demultiplexor, demux | noun

multiplexer:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksər/ | v. |多路复用，多路传输 | To combine multiple signals into one. | The system multiplexes several video channels onto a single cable. 系统将多个视频通道多路复用到一根电缆上。| The company multiplexes several data streams onto a single fiber optic line. 公司将多个数据流多路复用到一根光纤线路上。 | synonyms: combine, merge | antonyms: demultiplex, separate | verb
