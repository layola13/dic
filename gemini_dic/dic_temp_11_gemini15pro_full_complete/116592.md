matriculate:/məˈtrɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
入学，注册
to enroll as a member of a body and especially of a college or university
Example: She matriculated at the University of Oxford. 她就读于牛津大学。
Example: He matriculated in the fall semester. 他在秋季学期入学。

近义词: enroll, register, admit
反义词: graduate, withdraw, leave


matriculation:/məˌtrɪkjʊˈleɪʃn/
noun
入学，注册
the act of matriculating
Example:  Matriculation day is always exciting for new students. 入学日对新生来说总是令人兴奋的。
Example:  His matriculation was delayed due to missing paperwork. 由于缺少文件，他的注册被推迟了。


近义词: enrollment, registration, admission
反义词: graduation, withdrawal
