historiography:/ˌhɪstəˈriˈɒɡrəfi/ (US) /ˌhɪstɔːriˈɑːɡrəfi/ (UK)
n.|史学；史学著作|The study of the writing of history and of written histories.  It analyzes how history has been written, by whom, and why. |Example:  "Modern historiography has challenged many traditional narratives." 现代史学对许多传统叙事提出了挑战。|Example: "Her book offers a fresh perspective on the historiography of the French Revolution." 她的书为法国大革命的史学研究提供了新的视角。|Synonyms: historical writing, historical scholarship, history|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
