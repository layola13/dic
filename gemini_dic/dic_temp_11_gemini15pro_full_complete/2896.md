advertisement:/ˌædvərˈtaɪzmənt/ |n.|广告|A public promotion of some product or service.|I saw an advertisement for a new type of phone. 我看到一个新型手机的广告。|synonyms: commercial, ad, publicity, promotion|antonyms: criticism, condemnation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From advertise + -ment.  "advertise" comes from Middle French "advertir" (to warn, inform) ultimately from Latin "advertere" (to turn towards).
