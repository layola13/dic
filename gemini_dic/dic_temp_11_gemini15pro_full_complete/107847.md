larderer:/ˈlɑːrdərər/
n.
食品贮藏员，管理食品的人
A person in charge of a larder; one who keeps a larder.
The larderer ensured that the pantry was always well-stocked. 食品贮藏员确保食品储藏室总是储备充足。
The king's larderer was responsible for all the food in the castle. 国王的食品管理员负责城堡里所有的食物。
近义词:storekeeper, steward, provisioner
反义词:None
词性:名词
