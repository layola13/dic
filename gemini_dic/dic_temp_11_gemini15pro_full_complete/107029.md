lactific: /lækˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
产乳的; 促进乳汁分泌的
Producing milk; promoting the secretion of milk.
例句：Lactic acid bacteria are known for their lactific properties. 乳酸菌以其产乳特性而闻名。

例句：Certain herbs are believed to have lactific effects, aiding in breastfeeding.  某些草药被认为具有催乳作用，有助于母乳喂养。

近义词：galactagogue, lactogenic
反义词: N/A (没有直接的反义词)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lactificus, from lact-, stem of lac "milk" + -ficus "-making," from facere "to make, do."  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *dʰeh₁- "to put, place, do."
