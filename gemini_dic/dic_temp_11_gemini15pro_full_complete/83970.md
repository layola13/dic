half-convincingly:/ˌhæf kənˈvɪnsɪŋli/
|adv.|半信半疑地，不太令人信服地|Meaning "in a way that is only partially convincing," formed by combining "half" with "convincingly."|He smiled half-convincingly, trying to reassure her. 他半信半疑地笑了笑，试图让她安心。|She explained her absence half-convincingly. 她不太令人信服地解释了她缺席的原因。|近义词: unconvincingly, doubtfully, hesitantly|反义词: convincingly, persuasively|词性: 副词
