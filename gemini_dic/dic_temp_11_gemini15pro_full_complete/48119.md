declutter:/diːˈklʌtər/
verb
清除杂物，整理
To remove unnecessary items from a place, in order to make it more tidy.
I spent the weekend decluttering my house. 我花了整个周末整理我的房子。
The company needs to declutter its product line. 这家公司需要精简其产品线。
tidy, organize, clean up
clutter, mess
v.
