Also, to form an opinion or conclusion about. |  The judge sentenced the defendant to five years in prison. 法官判处被告五年徒刑。| Synonyms: arbiter, referee, umpire, adjudicator, assessor, critic, connoisseur, expert | Antonyms: defendant, accused, litigant | noun, verb
