entomolin:/ˌɛntəˈmoʊlɪn/ | n. |昆虫蜕皮激素; 昆虫蜕皮素|A steroid hormone, C27H44O7, that controls molting and metamorphosis in insects. |Entomolin plays a crucial role in the insect life cycle. 昆虫蜕皮激素在昆虫的生命周期中起着至关重要的作用。| The researchers studied the effects of entomolin on butterfly development. 研究人员研究了昆虫蜕皮激素对蝴蝶发育的影响。|Synonym: ecdysterone, 20-hydroxyecdysone |Antonym: None |noun
