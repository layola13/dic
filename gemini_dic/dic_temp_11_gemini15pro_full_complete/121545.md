mismated:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪtɪd/
adjective
不相配的，不合适的
ill-matched, unsuitably paired
The mismated couple argued constantly. 这对不相配的夫妇经常吵架。
近义词: ill-matched, incompatible, unsuitable, uncongenial
反义词: well-matched, compatible, suitable, congenial
词性: adjective

mismated:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪtɪd/
verb
使不相配，错误地组合
to match or pair unsuitably
The breeder mismated the two dogs, resulting in unhealthy puppies.  饲养员错误地将两只狗配对，导致幼犬不健康。
近义词: mismatch
反义词: match well
词性: verb (past participle)
