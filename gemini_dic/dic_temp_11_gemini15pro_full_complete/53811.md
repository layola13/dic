disconformably:/ˌdɪskənˈfɔːməbli/ | adv. |不整合地|In a manner showing unconformity between sedimentary strata, where the layers are parallel but separated by a period of erosion or non-deposition.|The younger sandstone rests disconformably on the older limestone.  较新的砂岩不整合地覆盖在较老的石灰岩上。|Synonym: unconformably|Antonym: conformably|Word Type: Adverb
