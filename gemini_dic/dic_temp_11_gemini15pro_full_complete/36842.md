colonized: /ˈkɒlənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzd/ (US)
verb
殖民的，被殖民的
established control over (a place or people), especially as an extension of state power.
例句：The British colonized many countries around the world.  英国在世界各地殖民了许多国家。

例句：The Americas were colonized by Europeans centuries ago.  美洲几个世纪前被欧洲人殖民。

近义词：settled, occupied, conquered, subjugated
反义词：liberated, freed, independent
词性: v.


colonized: /ˈkɒlənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzd/ (US)
adjective
殖民的，被殖民的
having been made a colony
例句：India was a colonized country under British rule. 印度在英国统治下是一个被殖民的国家。

例句：Many colonized peoples fought for their independence. 许多被殖民的人民为争取独立而战。

近义词：dependent, subject, controlled
反义词：independent, free, sovereign
词性: adj.
