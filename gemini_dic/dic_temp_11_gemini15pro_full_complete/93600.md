imbastardize:/ɪmˈbæstərdaɪz/
verb
使…堕落；降低…的品质；掺杂；污损
to corrupt or debase; to make something worse by adding inferior elements
The purebred dog was imbastardized by mixing it with a mongrel. 这只纯种狗因与杂种狗交配而血统不纯。
verb
宣布…为私生子；认定…为非婚生子女
to declare or pronounce illegitimate
He sought to imbastardize his rival's heir. 他试图宣布他对手的继承人为私生子。
近义词:debase, corrupt, degrade, contaminate, adulterate
反义词:refine, purify, enhance, improve, elevate
词性:v.
