hydrocephalic:/ˌhaɪdrəsəˈfælɪk/| adj.|脑积水的；患脑积水的|Relating to or affected with hydrocephalus, a condition characterized by an abnormal accumulation of cerebrospinal fluid within the brain.|The hydrocephalic child required immediate surgery. 这个脑积水的孩子需要立即手术。|Synonyms: hydrocephalous|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "neurologically healthy")|Adjective


hydrocephalus:/ˌhaɪdrəˈsɛfələs/| n.|脑积水|A condition in which fluid accumulates in the brain, typically in young children, enlarging the head and sometimes causing brain damage.|The infant was diagnosed with hydrocephalus. 这个婴儿被诊断出患有脑积水。|Synonyms: water on the brain|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun
