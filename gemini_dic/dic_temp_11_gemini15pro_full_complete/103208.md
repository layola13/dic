jet lag: /ˈdʒɛt læɡ/ (US) /ˈdʒɛt læɡ/ (UK)| n.|时差; 时差反应|A disruption of bodily rhythms due to rapid long-distance travel.|I'm suffering from jet lag after my trip to Tokyo. 我东京之行后正遭受时差反应的困扰。|Synonyms: jet lag disorder, time-zone change syndrome|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, one could consider "acclimatization")|Noun
