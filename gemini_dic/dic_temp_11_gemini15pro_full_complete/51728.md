devoice:/diːˈvɔɪs/ | v. |使无声，使失声；消除…的浊音|To deprive of voice or vocal sound; to make voiceless. |The surgery devoiced her completely.  手术使她完全失声了。|synonyms: mute, silence, hush | antonyms: vocalize, sound, voice | verb

devoice:/diːˈvɔɪs/ | v. |（语音学）使…成为清音；去浊| (Phonetics) To cause (a normally voiced sound) to become voiceless. |The /b/ in "absurd" is often devoiced. “absurd”中的/b/通常被去浊。|synonyms: unvoice, deaspiration | antonyms: voice, aspiration | verb
