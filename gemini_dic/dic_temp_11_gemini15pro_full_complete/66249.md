excluded:/ɪkˈskluːdɪd/| adj.|被排除的，不包括的|Not included or allowed to take part in something; removed from consideration.  Derived from the Latin "excludere," meaning "to shut out."|例句：He felt excluded from the group. 他感觉自己被排除在这个团体之外。

例句：Women are still excluded from many high-level positions. 女性仍然被排除在许多高层职位之外。

近义词：omitted, excepted, debarred, barred, rejected, precluded, prohibited|反义词:included, admitted, accepted|词性:形容词
