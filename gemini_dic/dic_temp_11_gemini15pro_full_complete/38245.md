computerizing: /kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzɪŋ/ (US) /kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzɪŋ/ (UK)
|v.|计算机化；用计算机处理|The process of equipping something with computers, or using computers to do something that was previously done manually.|例句：The company is computerizing its accounting system.  公司正在将其会计系统计算机化。|近义词：automating, digitizing|反义词: manualizing |verb
