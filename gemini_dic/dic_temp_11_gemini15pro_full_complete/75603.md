fluffy:/ˈflʌfi/| adj.|毛茸茸的，蓬松的|Covered with soft fur or fluff; light and airy.|The kitten was small and fluffy. 小猫又小又毛茸茸的。|近义词：downy, furry, soft, feathery|反义词:smooth, flat, hard|词性:形容词

词源:  From fluff + -y.  Fluff itself likely comes from imitative origin, related to words like "puff."
