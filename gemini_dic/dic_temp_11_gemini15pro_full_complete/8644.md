anticonformist:/ˌæntɪkənˈfɔːrmɪst/ | n. |反传统者，不墨守成规的人| A person who consciously and deliberately rejects the norms and conventions of society.  | She's a bit of an anticonformist; she dyed her hair blue and got a nose piercing. 她有点反传统；她把头发染成了蓝色，还穿了鼻环。 |  近义词: maverick, nonconformist, rebel, iconoclast | 反义词: conformist, traditionalist | 词性: 名词
