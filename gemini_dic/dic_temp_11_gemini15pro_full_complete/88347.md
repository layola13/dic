hippolith:/ˈhɪpəlɪθ/| n. |马粪石; 马粪化石|A concretion of phosphate of lime and magnesia, sometimes found in the intestines of a horse, and formerly esteemed a bezoar; also, a fossil of this kind.  A bezoar is a hard mass found in the stomachs of certain animals and formerly believed to have medicinal properties.|The museum displayed a rare hippolith from the Pleistocene epoch.  博物馆展出了一块来自更新世的稀有马粪石。|近义词：coprolite (粪化石)|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
