drainers:/ˈdreɪnərz/
noun
排水器; 排水管; 下水道工人
People or things that drain something.  Specifically, it often refers to tools or systems used for drainage, or, less commonly, the people who maintain them.
The drainers in the sink are clogged with hair. 水槽里的排水管被头发堵塞了。
The city employs drainers to keep the sewers clear.  这个城市雇佣了下水道工人来保持下水道的畅通。
synonyms: drains, outlets, channels, sewers, ditchers
antonyms: fillers, irrigators, blockages
