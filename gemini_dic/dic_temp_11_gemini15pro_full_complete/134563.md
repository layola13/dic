nonlitigiousness:/ˌnɒnlɪˈtɪdʒəsnəs/
|n.|无诉讼倾向；非诉讼性|The state of being unwilling or unlikely to engage in lawsuits.  It refers to a preference for resolving disputes through negotiation, mediation, or other non-legal means, rather than resorting to litigation.|The company's culture of nonlitigiousness is reflected in its strong focus on mediation and arbitration. 该公司的非诉讼文化体现在其对调解和仲裁的重视。|Synonyms: amicableness,  conciliatoriness, peacefulness|Antonyms: litigiousness, contentiousness, quarrelsomeness|noun
