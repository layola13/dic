cartilage:/ˈkɑːrtɪlɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈkɑːtɪlɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|软骨|A firm, flexible connective tissue found in various parts of the body, such as the joints, nose, and ears.|The cartilage in my knee is worn down. 我的膝盖软骨磨损了。|Synonyms: gristle|Antonyms: bone|noun

cartilaginous:/ˌkɑːrtɪˈlædʒɪnəs/ (US), /ˌkɑːtɪˈlædʒɪnəs/ (UK)| adj.|软骨的|Consisting of or resembling cartilage.|Sharks have cartilaginous skeletons.  鲨鱼有软骨骨骼。|Synonyms: chondral|Antonyms: bony|adjective
