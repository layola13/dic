governablenesses:/ˌɡʌvərnəˈbɪlnəsɪz/
n.
可治理性；可控性
The quality or state of being governable.  The ease with which a country or region can be governed.
The governablenesses of the newly formed nations were uncertain. 这些新成立国家的可治理性是不确定的。
Synonyms: manageability, controllability, tractability
Antonyms: ungovernability, unruliness, intractability
