metaphysical:/ˌmetəˈfɪzɪkəl/
adjective
形而上学的；抽象的；深奥的
Relating to metaphysics; concerned with abstract thought or subjects, as existence, causality, or truth.  Beyond the physical realm; supernatural.
例句：
He enjoyed reading metaphysical poetry. 他喜欢读形而上学诗歌。

The nature of consciousness is a metaphysical question. 意识的本质是一个形而上学的问题。

近义词：abstract, philosophical, transcendental, spiritual, esoteric
反义词：physical, concrete, empirical, mundane
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin metaphysica, neuter plural of metaphysicus (“metaphysical”), from Greek τὰ μετὰ τὰ φυσικά (tà metà tà physiká, “the things after the physical things”), referring to the arrangement of Aristotle's works by Andronicus of Rhodes.  meta- (“after, beyond”) + physics.
