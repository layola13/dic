demideify: /ˌdemiˈdiːɪfaɪ/ /ˌdɛmiˈdiːɪfaɪ/
verb
降低神性；使不再神圣；贬低
To reduce from the status of a god; to make less divine or sacred; to demythologize.

The movement sought to demideify the king, portraying him as a fallible human rather than a divinely appointed ruler.
这场运动试图降低国王的神性，将他描绘成一个容易犯错的人，而不是神授的统治者。

Modern scholarship has attempted to demideify the historical figure of Joan of Arc, separating fact from legend.
现代学术试图将圣女贞德的历史形象去神化，将事实与传说分开。


近义词: demythologize, humanize, secularize, desacralize
反义词: deify, apotheosize, elevate, exalt

词性: verb
