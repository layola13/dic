dorsiparous:/dɔːrˈsɪpərəs/
adjective
背部产卵的；在背部孵卵的
Producing or carrying young on the back.  Specifically, used to describe certain frogs and toads that carry eggs and/or developing young on their backs.
例句：The Surinam toad is a dorsiparous amphibian, with the female embedding fertilized eggs into the skin of her back.  苏里南蟾蜍是一种背部孵卵的两栖动物，雌性将受精卵嵌入背部皮肤中。

例句：Dorsiparous behavior is a fascinating adaptation that provides protection for developing offspring.  背部孵卵的行为是一种迷人的适应性，为发育中的后代提供了保护。
近义词：oviparous (for egg-laying in general)
反义词：(No direct antonym, but could contrast with viviparous - live-bearing)
词性: adjective
