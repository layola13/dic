chigger: /ˈtʃɪɡər/ (US), /ˈtʃɪɡə/ (UK)| n.|恙螨，沙蚤|A small mite larva (Trombiculidae) that sucks the blood of vertebrates, including humans, causing intense itching.|例句：I got bitten by chiggers while hiking in the woods. 我在树林里徒步旅行时被恙螨咬了。

例句：The chiggers were so bad, my ankles were covered in bites. 恙螨太厉害了，我的脚踝上全是叮咬的痕迹。

近义词：mite, harvest mite|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


chigger: /ˈtʃɪɡər/ (US), /ˈtʃɪɡə/ (UK)| n.|秋恙螨|Specifically, the larva of the harvest mite.|例句：Chiggers are particularly active in late summer and early fall. 秋恙螨在夏末和初秋特别活跃。

例句：Chigger bites can be extremely itchy and uncomfortable. 秋恙螨叮咬会非常痒和不舒服。
