autop:/ˈɔːtɒp/ (British) /ˈɑːtɑːp/ (American)| n. |验尸，尸体解剖|A post-mortem examination to discover the cause of death or the extent of disease.|例句：The autop revealed the cause of death was a heart attack. 验尸结果显示死因是心脏病发作。

例句：The police ordered an autop after the suspicious death.  警方在可疑死亡事件后下令进行尸检。|近义词：postmortem, necropsy|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
