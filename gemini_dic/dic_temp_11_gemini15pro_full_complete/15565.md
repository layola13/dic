baguette:/bæˈɡɛt/ | n. | 法式长棍面包 | A long, thin loaf of French bread with a crisp crust. |  例句：I bought a fresh baguette from the bakery this morning. 我今天早上从面包店买了一条新鲜的法式长棍面包。

例句：The baguette was still warm from the oven.  这法棍面包还是从烤箱里拿出来，热乎乎的。

近义词：French bread, French stick | 反义词: None (面包没有明确的反义词) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *baguette* "wand, stick, rod," diminutive of *bague* "ring."  The bread is named for its long, stick-like shape.
