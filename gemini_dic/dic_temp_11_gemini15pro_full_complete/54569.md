dislodge:/dɪsˈlɒdʒ/ /dɪsˈlɑːdʒ/| vt.|驱逐；移动；逐出|to force something out of its position|The earthquake dislodged several boulders from the cliff face. 地震使几块巨石从悬崖面上掉落下来。|
The wind dislodged a tile from the roof.  风把屋顶上的一块瓦刮掉了。|
synonyms: remove, displace, shift, eject, oust|antonyms: fix, secure, embed, lodge|verb
