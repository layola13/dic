momentary:/ˈmoʊmənˌteri/ (US) /ˈməʊməntəri/ (UK)| adj.|瞬间的，短暂的|lasting for a very short time; brief|例句：The pain was momentary, but intense. 疼痛短暂但剧烈。|例句：She felt a momentary pang of guilt. 她感到一阵短暂的内疚。|近义词：brief, fleeting, transient, ephemeral|反义词：lasting, enduring, permanent|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Late Latin *momentaneus*, from Latin momentāneus (“of a moment, momentary”), from momentum (“movement, motion; moving power; alteration, change; particle, element; importance, weight, influence; instant, moment”) + -āneus.  The English word was influenced by French momentané.
