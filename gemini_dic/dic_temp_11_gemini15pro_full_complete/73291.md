forebrain:/ˈfɔːrbreɪn/ | n. | 前脑 | The anterior part of the brain, including the cerebral hemispheres, the thalamus, and the hypothalamus. | The forebrain is responsible for higher-level cognitive functions. 前脑负责高级认知功能。| The development of the forebrain in humans allows for complex reasoning. 人类前脑的发育使得复杂的推理成为可能。 | synonyms: prosencephalon | antonyms: None | noun
