interjunction:/ˌɪntərˈdʒʌŋkʃən/| n.|交叉路口，汇合点|A place where two or more things, especially roads or railways, join or cross each other.|The accident occurred at the interjunction of two major highways. 事故发生在两条主要公路的交叉路口。|近义词：intersection, junction, crossroads|反义词:separation, divergence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From inter- (prefix meaning "between, among") + junction (from Latin junctio, meaning "a joining").
