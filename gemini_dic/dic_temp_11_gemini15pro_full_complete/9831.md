anytime:/ˈɛniˌtaɪm/ | adv.| 任何时候；随时| At any time; whenever. |Call me anytime you need help.  任何时候你需要帮助都可以给我打电话。|  Synonyms: whenever, at any time, any moment | Antonyms: never, at no time | adverb

anytime:/ˈɛniˌtaɪm/ | n.| 任何时候| An unspecified or open-ended time. | "Anytime after three is fine for me."  三点以后的任何时间对我来说都可以。 | Synonyms: whenever, any moment, any time | Antonyms:  specific time, fixed time | noun

anytime: /ˈɛniˌtaɪm/ | interj.| (Informal) Used to express agreement or willingness. | "Want to grab lunch?" "Anytime!" “想一起吃午饭吗？” “随时！” | Synonyms: certainly, sure, of course | Antonyms: no way, absolutely not | interjection
