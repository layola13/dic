misnavigate:/ˌmɪsˈnævɪɡeɪt/
verb
错误导航，导航错误
to navigate incorrectly or poorly; to go to the wrong place because of bad navigation.
The ship misnavigated and ended up hundreds of miles off course. 这艘船导航错误，最终偏离航线数百英里。
The driver misnavigated the city streets and got completely lost. 司机在城市街道上导航错误，完全迷路了。
近义词:get lost, lose one's way, go astray
反义词:navigate correctly, find one's way
