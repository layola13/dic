cashes:/ˈkæʃɪz/
| v. |兑现；支付；兑换| To exchange a check, money order, etc., for cash. |He cashed his paycheck and went shopping. 他兑现了工资支票，然后去购物了。|近义词：redeems, converts|反义词: deposits|词性:动词

cashes:/ˈkæʃɪz/
| n. |现金；钱款 (通常用复数形式)|Money in the form of coins or banknotes. |The business has very little cash on hand. 这家企业手头现金很少。|近义词：funds, money|反义词: debts|词性:名词
