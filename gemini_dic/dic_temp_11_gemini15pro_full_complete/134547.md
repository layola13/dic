nonliquidly:/ˌnɒnˈlɪkwɪdli/ | adv. | 非流动地，不流通地 | In a manner that is not easily converted to cash. | The assets were nonliquidly held, making it difficult for the company to quickly raise capital.  该公司非流动性地持有资产，使其难以快速筹集资金。 | 近义词：illiquidly | 反义词：liquidly | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, opposite of  (非，相反)
liquid (adjective/noun):  flowing freely like water; easily converted into cash (液体的；流动的；易变现的)
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs (构成副词)
