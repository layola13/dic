nonmanagement:/ˌnɒnˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | /ˌnɑːnˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | adj. n. | 非管理的；非管理人员；非管理层 |  Not related to or involved in management; referring to employees who are not in management positions. |  The nonmanagement staff were unhappy with the new policy. 非管理人员对新政策不满意。 |  non-executive, nonsupervisory, rank-and-file | management, executive | adjective, noun
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + management
