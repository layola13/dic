headlock: /ˈhɛdlɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɛdlɑːk/ (US) | n. | 头锁；夹头锁 |  A hold in which one person's head is held under the other person's arm, typically in wrestling. | The wrestler had his opponent in a headlock.  摔跤选手用夹头锁控制住了他的对手。|  Synonyms: stranglehold, chokehold | Antonyms: release, freedom | noun

headlock: /ˈhɛdlɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɛdlɑːk/ (US) | vt. | 用头锁夹住 | To put someone in a headlock. | He headlocked his brother playfully. 他开玩笑地用头锁夹住他弟弟。 | Synonyms:  grapple, wrestle | Antonyms: release, free | verb
