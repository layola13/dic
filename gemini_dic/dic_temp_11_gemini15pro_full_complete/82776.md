guilted:/ˈɡɪltɪd/| adj.|感到内疚的，负罪的|Made to feel guilty; conscience-stricken.|例句：He felt guilted into going to the party. 他感到被迫去了聚会，心里很内疚。

例句：She guilted him into buying her a new car. 她让他感到内疚，从而给他买了一辆新车。|近义词：ashamed, remorseful, culpable|反义词：innocent, blameless|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "guilt" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Guilt" comes from Old English "gylt" meaning "crime, sin, offense."
