awesome: /ˈɔːsəm/ (US) /ˈɔːs(ə)m/ (UK) | adj. | 极好的，令人惊叹的 | Inspiring awe or admiration; extremely impressive. | That concert was awesome!  那场音乐会棒极了！| The view from the mountain top was awesome. 从山顶看到的景色令人惊叹。 | 近义词：amazing, incredible, fantastic, wonderful, excellent, terrific | 反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, horrible | 词性：形容词

词源:  From awe + -some.  Awe dates back to Old English *ege, from Proto-Germanic *agiz.  The suffix "-some" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "producing."
