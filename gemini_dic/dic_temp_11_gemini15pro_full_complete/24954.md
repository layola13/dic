PHRASE->
brush up on: 复习，重新学习  She needs to brush up on her math skills. 她需要复习一下她的数学技能。
brush aside:  漠视，不理会 He brushed aside her concerns. 他对她的担忧置之不理。
brush off:  拒绝，不理睬 She brushed him off when he asked her out. 他约她出去时，她拒绝了他。
brush with:  与…的摩擦，冲突；与…的短暂接触  He had a brush with the law when he was younger. 他年轻时曾与法律有过冲突。
brush against:  擦过，掠过 The branches brushed against the window. 树枝擦过窗户。
