d-structure:/ˈdiː strʌktʃər/ |n.|深层结构|A theoretical underlying syntactic structure of a sentence, proposed in transformational grammar, from which the surface structure is derived by a series of transformations.|The linguist analyzed the d-structure of the sentence. 语言学家分析了句子的深层结构。|underlying structure, deep grammar | surface structure |noun
