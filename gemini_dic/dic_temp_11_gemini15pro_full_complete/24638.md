brokering:/ˈbroʊkərɪŋ/| n. |代理；斡旋；调停；协商|The act of arranging or negotiating something on behalf of others, typically a business deal.|The UN played a key role in brokering a peace agreement. 联合国在促成和平协议方面发挥了关键作用。|Synonyms: negotiating, mediating, arranging, intermediating|Antonyms: obstructing, hindering|noun

brokering:/ˈbroʊkərɪŋ/| v. |代理；斡旋；调停；协商|Acting as a broker; arranging or negotiating something on behalf of others.|He brokered a deal between the two warring factions. 他在两个交战派别之间斡旋达成了一项协议。|Synonyms: negotiate, mediate, arrange, intermediate|Antonyms: obstruct, hinder|verb
