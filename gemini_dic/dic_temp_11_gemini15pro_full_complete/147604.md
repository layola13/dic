overplay:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪ/| v.|过分强调；夸大；表演过火|To exaggerate the importance of something; to act a role too dramatically.|He overplayed his hand and lost the negotiation. 他把自己的牌打得太过了，输掉了谈判。|The actor overplayed the villain, making him seem comical rather than menacing.  这位演员把反派角色演得太过了，让他看起来滑稽而不是危险。|近义词：exaggerate, overemphasize, overstate, overact|反义词：underplay, downplay|词性:动词

overplay:/ˈoʊvərpleɪ/| n.|夸大的行为；过分的表演|An exaggerated act or performance.|The constant overplay of his injuries became tiresome. 他不断夸大自己的伤势，让人厌烦。|Her overplay of innocence was unconvincing. 她过分表现出无辜的样子，反而让人难以信服。|近义词：exaggeration, overemphasis, overstatement|反义词：understatement|词性:名词
