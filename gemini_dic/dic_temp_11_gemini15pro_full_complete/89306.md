homoeozoic:/ˌhoʊmiəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. |同生世的，属于同一生世的|Relating to or denoting the present geological era, during which the principal forms of life have been mammals.|The homoeozoic era began about 66 million years ago. 同生世大约始于6600万年前。|近义词: Cenozoic|反义词: Paleozoic, Mesozoic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *homoios* (similar) + *zōikos* (of life)
