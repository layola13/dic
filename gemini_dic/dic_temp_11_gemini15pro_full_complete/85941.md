headmasterly:/ˌhedˈmɑːstərli/ | adj. | 校长般的；专横的；威严的 | Like a headmaster; dictatorial; authoritative.  Resembling or characteristic of a headmaster, often in a stern or pompous manner. |  He delivered the news in a headmasterly tone. 他以校长般的口吻宣布了这个消息。 |Synonyms: authoritarian, dictatorial, magisterial, pompous, domineering | Antonyms: humble, meek, subservient, democratic | adjective
