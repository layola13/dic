gently:/ˈdʒɛntli/ | adv. |轻轻地，温柔地 | In a gentle way; softly; mildly; kindly. | She gently stroked the cat's fur. 她轻轻地抚摸着猫的皮毛。 |  He gently closed the door. 他轻轻地关上了门。 | 近义词: softly, tenderly, lightly, mildly | 反义词: roughly, harshly, violently | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Middle English *gentli, *genteli, from Old French gentilement (“nobly, courteously, gently”), from gentil (“noble, courteous, gentle”) + -ment (adverbial suffix). More at gentle.
