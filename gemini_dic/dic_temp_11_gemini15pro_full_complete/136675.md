nonsecrecy:/nɑːnˈsiːkrəsi/
n.
无保密性；公开性
the state or practice of not keeping things secret; openness.
The government's policy of nonsecrecy about the pandemic was praised. 政府在疫情期间的公开透明政策受到了赞扬。
openness, transparency, candidness, forthrightness
secrecy, concealment, confidentiality
noun
