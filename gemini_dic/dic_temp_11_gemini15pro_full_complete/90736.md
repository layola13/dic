humin:/ˈhjuːmɪn/ | n. |腐殖质|The dark organic material in soil, produced by the decomposition of plant and animal matter.|The soil was rich in humin. 土壤富含腐殖质。|Near synonyms: humus, organic matter|Antonym: inorganic matter|noun

humin:/ˈhjuːmɪn/ | n. |腐殖酸|A complex mixture of organic substances extracted from humus, insoluble in alkali.|Humin is a key component of soil organic matter. 腐殖酸是土壤有机质的关键组成部分。|Near synonyms: humic acid, fulvic acid|Antonym: inorganic matter|noun
