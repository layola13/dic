carried-on:/ˈkærid ɒn/ (US), /ˈkærid ɒn/ (UK)| adj.|继续的，持续的；进行中的|Continuing, prolonged, or in progress.  It implies an activity that has been happening for a period of time and is still happening.|The meeting carried on for another hour. 会议又继续进行了一个小时。|Synonyms: continued, ongoing, sustained|Antonyms: discontinued, stopped, interrupted|Adjective

carried-on:/ˈkærid ɒn/ (US), /ˈkærid ɒn/ (UK)| n.|行李，随身物品|Luggage, especially small bags or cases that can be carried onto an airplane or other form of transport.|Please place your carried-on luggage in the overhead compartment. 请把您的随身行李放在头顶的行李箱里。|Synonyms: hand luggage, cabin baggage, carry-on baggage|Antonyms: checked baggage, hold luggage|Noun
