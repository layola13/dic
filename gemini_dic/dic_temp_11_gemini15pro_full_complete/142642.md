ostracean:/ˌɒstrəˈseɪən/ | adj. n. |牡蛎的；似牡蛎的；牡蛎类动物|Relating to, resembling, or belonging to the Ostracea, a subclass of bivalve mollusks that includes oysters.|Oysters are ostracean mollusks. 牡蛎是牡蛎类软体动物。|The ostracean shells littered the beach. 海滩上到处都是牡蛎壳。|近义词:oyster-like, bivalve |反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑非双壳类 mollusks) |词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: From Latin *ostraceus* "of oysters," from *ostrea* "oyster" + *-aceus*, adjectival suffix.
