idolum:/ˈaɪdələm/| n. |偶像，幻象，谬误|An image or representation of something; a false or misleading image or idea; a fallacy.|例句：The idolum of perfection prevented her from ever finishing her novel.  完美主义的幻象让她始终无法完成她的小说。

例句：He chased after the idolum of fame and fortune, neglecting his true passions. 他追逐着名利的幻象，忽略了他真正的热情。|近义词：illusion, fallacy, phantom, misconception|反义词：reality, truth, fact|词性:noun
