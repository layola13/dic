hypocarp:/ˈhaɪpəʊkɑːrp/ | n. |下位子房，子房下位|The part of a flowering plant that bears the ovules and ripens into the fruit, especially when formed below the attachment of the other floral parts.|The apple has a hypocarp. 苹果有一个下位子房。|Synonyms: inferior ovary |Antonyms: superior ovary, epicarp| noun
