inter-animation:/ˌɪntərænɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 交互动画，动画间的过渡 | The smooth inter-animation between scenes made the video engaging. 场景之间流畅的交互动画使视频引人入胜。| Example:  The inter-animation of the characters' facial expressions was incredibly realistic.  人物面部表情的交互动画非常逼真。


Example:  The artist focused on the inter-animation of the different elements in the artwork. 艺术家专注于作品中不同元素之间的交互动画。 | 近义词: transition, morphing, tweening | 反义词: static, still | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among") + "animation" (from Latin "anima" meaning "soul, breath, life").  表示在不同动画之间或动画元素之间的交互。
