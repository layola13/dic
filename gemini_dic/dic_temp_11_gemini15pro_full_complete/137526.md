It describes something that lacks the elements of tragedy, such as suffering, destruction, or a disastrous outcome.|The play had a nontragic ending, much to the audience's relief.|近义词:untragic, happy, fortunate, positive|反义词:tragic, disastrous, catastrophic|词性:形容词
