alkane:/ˈælkeɪn/ | n. | 烷烃 |A saturated hydrocarbon with the general formula CnH2n+2.  A type of hydrocarbon in which the carbon atoms are connected only by single bonds. | Methane is the simplest alkane. 甲烷是最简单的烷烃。 | Ethane and propane are also alkanes. 乙烷和丙烷也是烷烃。 | synonym: paraffin | antonym: alkene, alkyne | noun
