misos: /ˈmiːsɒs/, /ˈmaɪsɒs/ | n. | 味噌 | A thick, fermented paste made primarily from soybeans, used in Japanese cooking as a flavoring agent, often in soups and sauces. | Example 1: I love the rich flavor of miso soup. 我喜欢味噌汤浓郁的味道。

Example 2: She added a spoonful of miso paste to the marinade. 她在腌料里加了一勺味噌酱。| Near synonyms: fermented soybean paste, soybean paste | Antonyms: None | Noun
