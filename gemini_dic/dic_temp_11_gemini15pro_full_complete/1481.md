acescency:/əˈsɛsənsi/
|n.|酸败；发酸|The acescency of the milk made it unusable. 牛奶变酸了，不能用了。|The process of becoming sour or slightly acid.  The acescency of the wine was a sign that it had gone bad.|例句：The acescency of the fruit was noticeable after it had been left out for too long. 水果在外面放太久后，酸败很明显。

例句：The wine's acescency was detected by its slightly vinegary smell.  葡萄酒的酸败可以从它略带醋味的气味中察觉出来。
|近义词：sourness, acidity|反义词:sweetness, alkalinity
|词性:名词
