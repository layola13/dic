mismeasured:/ˌmɪsˈmeʒərd/
|adj.|测量错误的，估量错误的|Incorrectly measured; having an incorrect measurement. |The tailor mismeasured the fabric and the suit was too small.  裁缝测量布料时出错，导致西装太小了。| The surveyor mismeasured the land, leading to a boundary dispute. 测量员错误地测量了土地，导致了边界纠纷。|近义词：inaccurate, wrongly measured, incorrect|反义词：accurate, correctly measured, precise|词性:形容词
词源: "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "measured" (past participle of the verb "measure")
