freeganism:/ˈfriːɡənɪzəm/
n.
拾荒主义；免费素食主义
The practice of reclaiming and eating discarded food.
Freeganism is a lifestyle that involves retrieving and consuming food that has been discarded.  拾荒主义是一种生活方式，包括回收和食用被丢弃的食物。
Freegans often dumpster dive for food to reduce waste and minimize their impact on the environment.  拾荒者经常在垃圾箱里寻找食物，以减少浪费，并最大限度地减少对环境的影响。
近义词:anti-consumerism, dumpster diving
反义词:consumerism
词性:noun
