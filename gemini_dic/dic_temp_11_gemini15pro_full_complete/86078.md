hearkens:/ˈhɑːrkənz/ (US), /ˈhɑːkənz/ (UK)| v.|倾听，留意；使人联想起|This music hearkens back to the 1940s. 这音乐使人想起20世纪40年代。|例句：The story hearkens back to a simpler time.  这个故事使人回想起更简单的时代。|近义词：listen, hark, recall, evoke|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *herknen, from Old English *heorcnian, from Proto-Germanic *hauzjaną (“to hear, listen”). Cognate with Dutch horken (“to listen, eavesdrop”), German horchen (“to listen”).
