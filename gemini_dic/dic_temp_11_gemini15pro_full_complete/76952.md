garages: /ɡəˈrɑːʒɪz/ /ɡəˈrɑːdʒɪz/
n.
车库；汽车修理厂
A building for storing a motor vehicle or vehicles.  A place where motor vehicles are repaired.
The car is parked in the garage.  汽车停在车库里。
He works at a garage. 他在一家汽车修理厂工作。
近义词：carports, workshops, service stations
反义词:  None (No single antonym for a physical structure like a garage)
词性: Noun (plural)


garages: /ɡəˈrɑːʒɪz/ /ɡəˈrɑːdʒɪz/
v.
把…放入车库
To put or store in a garage.
He garages his car every night. 他每晚都把车停放在车库里。
She garaged the lawnmower for the winter. 她把割草机存放在车库里过冬。
近义词：park, store, shelter
反义词: remove, take out
词性: Verb (third-person singular present)
