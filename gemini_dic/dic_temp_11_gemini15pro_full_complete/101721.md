irreproachably: /ˌɪrɪˈproʊtʃəbli/ | adv. |无可指责地，完美地 | In a manner that is beyond reproach; faultlessly. | She behaved irreproachably throughout the entire ordeal.  她在整个考验中表现无可指责。 |  The project was completed irreproachably, meeting all deadlines and exceeding expectations.  该项目完美完成，满足所有期限并超出预期。 | 近义词: blamelessly, impeccably, flawlessly | 反义词: culpably, reproachably | 词性: 副词

词源:
From  "irreproachable" + "-ly".  "Irreproachable" comes from  "in-" (not) + "reproachable" (deserving of reproach or blame).  "Reproachable" comes from Old French "reprochable", from "reprocher" (to reproach), from Latin "reprobare" (to disapprove, reject).
