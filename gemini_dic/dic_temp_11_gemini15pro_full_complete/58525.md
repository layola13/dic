duetto:/duˈɛtoʊ/ /djuˈɛtoʊ/
n.
二重唱，二重奏
A piece of music written for two performers, vocal or instrumental.
The opera featured a beautiful duetto between the two main characters.  这部歌剧以两位主角之间优美的二重唱为特色。
duet, duo
solo
