espouse:/ɪˈspaʊz/ /ɛˈspaʊz/| vt.|支持，拥护；信奉（主义、学说等）；娶（妻），嫁（夫）|To adopt or support (a cause, belief, or way of life).|She espoused the feminist cause. 她拥护女权主义事业。|Synonyms: adopt, embrace, support, champion, advocate, uphold|Antonyms: reject, oppose, renounce|verb

espouse:/ɪˈspaʊz/ /ɛˈspaʊz/| n.|拥护，支持|The act of adopting or supporting a cause, belief, or way of life.|Her espousal of the environmental movement led her to a career in conservation. 她对环保运动的支持使她从事了自然保护工作。|Synonyms: adoption, embrace, support|Antonyms: rejection, opposition|noun
