concedes:/kənˈsiːdz/| v. |承认，让步，认输|admits something is true or valid often unwillingly; grants as a right or privilege|例句：He conceded defeat. 他承认失败。|近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, yield|反义词：deny, dispute, refuse|词性:动词

concedes:/kənˈsiːdz/| v. |承认，让步，认输|surrenders or yields (something)|例句：The team conceded two goals in the first half.  该队在上半场丢了两球。|近义词：relinquish, surrender, yield|反义词：retain, defend, maintain|词性:动词
