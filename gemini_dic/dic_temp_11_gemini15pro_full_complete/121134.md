misdeliver:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvər/
verb
投递错误，误送
to deliver (something) to the wrong place or person
The package was misdelivered to the wrong address. 包裹被误送到错误的地址。
近义词: misdirect, missend
反义词: deliver correctly
词性: verb
