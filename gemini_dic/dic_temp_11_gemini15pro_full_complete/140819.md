on-line: /ˌɑːnˈlaɪn/ /ˌɒnˈlaɪn/ | adj. adv.|在线的，联网的；联机的|Connected to, served by, or available through a system and especially a computer or telecommunications system (such as the Internet).|例句：The system is now on-line.  系统现在在线了。|近义词：connected, networked, online|反义词: offline, disconnected|词性:形容词，副词

on-line: /ˌɑːnˈlaɪn/ /ˌɒnˈlaɪn/ | adv.|在线地，联网地|In an on-line manner; while connected to a system and especially a computer or telecommunications system (such as the Internet).|例句：He booked his tickets on-line. 他在线订了票。|近义词：connectedly, networkingly, online|反义词: offline, disconnectedly|词性:副词

Etymology:  From "on line," literally "on the line," referring to being connected to a telephone or other communication line.  The hyphenated form "on-line" is becoming less common, with "online" now generally preferred.
