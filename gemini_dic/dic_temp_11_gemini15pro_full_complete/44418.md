cross-path:/ˈkrɒsˈpɑːθ/ /ˈkrɔːsˈpæθ/ | adj. | 交叉路径的，横穿路径的 |  Describing something that intersects or crosses another's path, often unexpectedly or in a conflicting manner.  | The hikers had a cross-path encounter with a bear.  徒步旅行者与一只熊不期而遇。 | synonyms: intersecting, crossing, conflicting, clashing | antonyms: parallel, aligned, harmonious | adjective

cross-path:/ˈkrɒsˈpɑːθ/ /ˈkrɔːsˈpæθ/ | n. | 交叉路径，交集 | The point where two paths intersect or cross. |  Their cross-path was marked by a small cairn. 他们的交集处用一个小石堆标记。 | synonyms: intersection, junction, crossing | antonyms: divergence, separation | noun

cross-path:/ˈkrɒsˈpɑːθ/ /ˈkrɔːsˈpæθ/ | v. | 横穿路径，与...的路径交叉 | To cross another's path, often unexpectedly or in a conflicting manner. |  Our lives cross-pathed in unexpected ways. 我们的生活以意想不到的方式相遇。| synonyms: intersect, cross, encounter, clash | antonyms: diverge, separate, avoid | verb
