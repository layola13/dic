forbiddenness:/fərˈbɪdənɪs/ /fɔːrˈbɪdənɪs/
n.
禁忌, 禁止, 被禁止的状态
The state of being forbidden; prohibition.
The forbiddenness of the fruit only made it more tempting.  这种水果的禁忌性只会让它更诱人。
prohibition, taboo, interdiction
allowance, permission
