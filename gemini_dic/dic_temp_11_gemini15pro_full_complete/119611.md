midbrain:/ˈmɪdbreɪn/ | n. | 中脑 | The central part of the brainstem, developing from the middle vesicle of the embryonic brain. It is responsible for certain aspects of vision and hearing, and for controlling movement. |  The midbrain plays a crucial role in eye movement. 中脑在眼球运动中起着至关重要的作用。| synonym: mesencephalon | antonym: N/A | noun
