ecchymosis: /ˌɛkɪˈmoʊsɪs/ | n. | 瘀斑，青肿 | A discoloration of the skin resulting from bleeding underneath, typically caused by bruising. |  The patient presented with ecchymosis on the left arm after the fall. 病人摔倒后左臂出现瘀斑。

The boxer had severe ecchymosis around both eyes.  拳击手双眼周围有严重的瘀伤。 | Synonyms: bruise, contusion, hematoma | Antonyms:  None (The absence of ecchymosis would simply be unblemished skin.) | Noun
