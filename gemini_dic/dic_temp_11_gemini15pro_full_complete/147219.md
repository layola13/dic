overmatch:/ˌoʊvərˈmætʃ/ | v.  n. | 过度匹配，胜过，对手太强 | to be more powerful or skillful than someone or something |  The team was overmatched by their opponents.  这个队被对手击败了。| synonyms: outmatch, defeat, conquer, surpass | antonyms: undermatch, lose to, be inferior to | verb

overmatch:/ˌoʊvərˈmætʃ/ | n. |  实力悬殊的比赛，不匹配的对手 |  a contest in which one side is much stronger than the other | It was a complete overmatch; the smaller team didn't stand a chance. 这是一场实力完全悬殊的比赛；较弱的队伍毫无胜算。| synonyms: mismatch, unequal contest | antonyms: even match, fair fight | noun
