brays:/breɪz/
| v. | 驴叫；发出刺耳的声音 | to make a loud, harsh cry like a donkey; to speak or say something in a loud, unpleasant way | The donkey brays loudly. 驴大声地叫。|近义词：hee-haw, shriek, scream, yell |反义词: whisper, murmur
|词性: 动词

brays:/breɪz/
| n. | 驴叫声；刺耳的声音 | the loud, harsh cry of a donkey; a loud, unpleasant sound | The brays of the donkeys echoed through the valley. 驴的叫声在山谷中回荡。|近义词：hee-haws, shrieks, screams, yells |反义词: whispers, murmurs
|词性: 名词
