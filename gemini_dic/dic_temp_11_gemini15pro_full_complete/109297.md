词源：From French *lémur*, from Latin *lemures* (“shades, ghosts”).  So named supposedly because of the nocturnal habits, large reflective eyes, and ghostly cries of some species.  来自法语 *lémur*，源自拉丁语 *lemures*（“幽灵、鬼魂”）。  之所以这样命名，据说是因为某些物种的夜间习性、巨大的反光眼睛和幽灵般的叫声。
