compassionating:/kəmˈpæʃəneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
富有同情心的，表示怜悯的
Showing or feeling compassion.
She cast a compassionating glance at the beggar. 她对乞丐投以怜悯的目光。
近义词:sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring
反义词:uncaring, callous, indifferent, apathetic
词性:adjective

compassion:/kəmˈpæʃən/
noun
同情，怜悯
A strong feeling of sympathy and sadness for the suffering or bad luck of others and a wish to help them.
He was filled with compassion for the sick child. 他对生病的孩子充满了同情。
近义词:sympathy, empathy, pity, concern
反义词:indifference, apathy, cruelty, callousness
词性:noun
