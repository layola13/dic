autophagy: /ɔːˈtɒfədʒi/ (British) /ɑːˈtɑːfədʒi/ (American)| n. |细胞自噬|The natural, regulated mechanism of the cell that removes unnecessary or dysfunctional components.  It allows the orderly degradation and recycling of cellular components.|例句：Autophagy plays a crucial role in maintaining cellular health. 细胞自噬在维持细胞健康方面起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Dysfunctional autophagy is implicated in several diseases. 自噬功能障碍与多种疾病有关。|近义词：self-eating, self-digestion|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


Etymology: From the Greek  "auto" (self) + "phagein" (to eat).
