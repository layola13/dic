chirpily:/ˈtʃɜːrpɪli/| adv.|兴高采烈地，活泼地|In a cheerful and lively manner, often characterized by short, high-pitched sounds like a bird's chirp.|She chirped merrily, flitting like a robin. 她像知更鸟一样飞舞，欢快地叫着。|近义词：cheerfully, merrily, blithely, brightly|反义词：morosely, gloomily, dejectedly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From chirp + -ily.  Chirp itself imitates the sound of small birds.
