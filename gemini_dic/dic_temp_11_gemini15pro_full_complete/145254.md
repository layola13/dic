overblow:/ˌoʊvərˈbloʊ/| v.|过分吹嘘；（乐器）过度吹奏；（风暴）减弱|To exaggerate or inflate the importance of something; to play a wind instrument too forcefully; (of a storm) to lose force and die down.|He overblew the significance of his contribution to the project. 他夸大了他对这个项目的贡献。|Synonyms: exaggerate, overstate, inflate, overemphasize|Antonyms: downplay, understate, minimize|verb

overblow:/ˈoʊvərbloʊ/| n.|（过度吹奏产生的）刺耳的声音|A harsh, unpleasant sound produced by overblowing a wind instrument.|The overblow from the trumpet was jarring. 小号过度吹奏产生的刺耳声音令人很不舒服。|Synonyms: blast, shriek, screech|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly) soft note, gentle sound|noun
