bolting:/ˈboʊltɪŋ/| n. v.|急逃，拴紧，囫囵吞咽|The act of running away suddenly; fastening something with bolts; eating food quickly without chewing properly.|例句：The horse startled and began bolting across the field.  马受惊了，开始在田野里狂奔。|近义词：fleeing, escaping, dashing, fastening, securing, gobbling, gulping|反义词:remaining, staying, loosening, chewing|词性:noun, verb
bolting:/ˈboʊltɪŋ/| v.|快速上升，抽薹|The act of a plant prematurely producing seeds and flowers, usually due to stress such as heat or drought.|例句：The lettuce bolted in the hot weather.  生菜在炎热的天气里抽薹了。|近义词：flowering, seeding|反义词:none|词性:verb
