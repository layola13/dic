eelpot:/ˈiːlpɒt/ (US) /ˈiːlpɒt/ (UK)| n.|鳗鱼笼，鳗鱼罐|A trap for catching eels, typically a wicker or wire basket with a funnel-shaped entrance.|The fisherman hauled the heavy eelpot onto his boat. 渔夫把沉重的鳗鱼笼拖到他的船上。|Example: The eelpot was full of wriggling eels. 鳗鱼笼里装满了蠕动的鳗鱼。|Synonyms: fish trap, eel trap|Antonyms: N/A|noun
