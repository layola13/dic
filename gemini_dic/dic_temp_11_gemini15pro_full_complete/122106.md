mistitling: /ˌmɪsˈtaɪtlɪŋ/
n.
错误命名，贴错标签
giving a title that is not appropriate or accurate
The mistitling of the painting led to confusion among art historians. 这幅画的错误命名导致了艺术史学家之间的困惑。

The article suffered from mistitling, which made it difficult to find in online searches. 这篇文章的标题错误，导致在网上搜索时很难找到。

近义词: misnaming, mislabeling
反义词: correct titling, accurate labeling
词性: noun
