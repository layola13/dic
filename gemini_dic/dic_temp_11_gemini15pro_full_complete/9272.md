antipesticide:/ˌæntiˈpɛstɪsaɪd/| n. adj.| 抗农药；杀虫剂的拮抗物 |A substance that opposes the action of a pesticide.| The development of antipesticides is crucial for managing resistance.  抗农药的开发对于控制抗药性至关重要。| The farmer experimented with an antipesticide to counteract the effects of the overuse of chemicals. 农民试验了一种抗农药来抵消过度使用化学品的影响。|近义词：pesticide antagonist|反义词: pesticide, insecticide|词性:名词，形容词


antipesticide:/ˌæntiˈpɛstɪsaɪd/| adj.| 抗农药的 |Having properties that counteract or oppose the effects of pesticides.|  Antipesticide technologies are being explored to protect beneficial insects.  抗农药技术正在被探索以保护益虫。| The company is researching antipesticide coatings for seeds. 该公司正在研究种子的抗农药涂层。|近义词：pesticide-resistant|反义词: pesticide-susceptible|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite of, instead of, in place of"
pesticide: from pest + -icide (suffix forming nouns meaning "killer of," from Latin -cida "killer," from caedere "to cut, kill")
