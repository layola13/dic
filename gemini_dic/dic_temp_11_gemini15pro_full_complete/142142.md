originations: /əˌrɪdʒəˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |起源，创始，贷款发放|The process of creating something new, or the fact of being created.  In finance, it often refers to the process of creating new loans. |The bank's loan originations increased by 20% this year. 今年该银行的贷款发放量增加了 20%。|Synonyms: beginnings, commencements, inceptions, starts; (in finance) underwriting, issuance. |Antonyms: endings, conclusions, terminations. | noun
