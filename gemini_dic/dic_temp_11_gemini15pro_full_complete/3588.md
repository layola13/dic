agatize:/ˈæɡəˌtaɪz/| v.|玛瑙化；使变成玛瑙状|To convert or cause to resemble agate.|The petrified wood had begun to agatize. 这块石化木已经开始玛瑙化了。|Synonyms: petrify, fossilize|Antonyms: dissolve, melt|verb

agatized:/ˈæɡəˌtaɪzd/| adj.|玛瑙化的；像玛瑙的|Having been converted into or resembling agate.|The agatized wood was beautiful and durable. 这块玛瑙化的木头既漂亮又耐用。|Synonyms: petrified, fossilized|Antonyms: dissolved, melted|adjective
