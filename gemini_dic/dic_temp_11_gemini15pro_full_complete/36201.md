cogger:/ˈkɒɡər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡər/ (US)| n.| 欺骗者，骗子；齿轮；（旧）中间人，掮客|A person who cheats or deceives others, especially in business dealings; a wheel or bar with a series of projections on its edge, used to transmit motion by engaging with projections on another wheel or bar; (archaic) a middleman or broker.| He was known as a cogger in the local market, always trying to sell faulty goods. 他在当地市场上被称为骗子，总是试图出售劣质商品。

The cogger in the clock mechanism broke, causing it to stop working. 时钟机构中的齿轮坏了，导致它停止工作。|近义词：cheat, swindler, fraudster, deceiver, con artist, gear, wheel|反义词：honest person, dupe|词性: 名词
