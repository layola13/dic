awesomeness:/ˈɔːsəmnəs/| n.|令人敬畏，惊叹，极好|The awesomeness of the Grand Canyon is breathtaking.  大峡谷的壮丽景色令人叹为观止。|近义词：splendor, magnificence, greatness, brilliance|反义词:awfulness, terribleness|Noun
Etymology->From awesome + -ness.  "Awesome" originates from "awe," which traces back to Old English "ege" meaning "fear, terror, dread." Over time, the meaning shifted to include reverence and wonder.
