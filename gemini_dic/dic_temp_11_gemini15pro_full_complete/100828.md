introductions:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃənz/| n.|介绍；引进；引言；序言|the act of making something known or available for the first time; the part of a book or speech that leads up to the main part|The chairman made a brief introduction of the speaker. 主席简要介绍了演讲者。|synonyms: preface, foreword, preamble, prologue|antonyms: conclusion, epilogue|noun

introductions:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃənz/| n.|引见；介绍（人）|the act of formally telling two or more people each other’s names so that they can meet|He made the introductions and then left us to chat. 他介绍了我们之后就离开了，让我们聊天。|synonyms: presentation|antonyms: N/A|noun
