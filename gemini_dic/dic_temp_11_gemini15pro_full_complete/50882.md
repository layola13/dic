deserted:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪd/
adj.
荒芜的， deserted
empty of people, abandoned
The deserted town was eerie at night.  夜晚，空无一人的小镇显得阴森恐怖。
desolate, barren, abandoned
inhabited, populated
adjective


deserted:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪd/
verb (past participle of desert)
被遗弃的， deserted
left empty or alone
The child felt deserted by his parents.  孩子感到被父母遗弃了。
abandoned, forsaken
protected, cherished
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *desert*, from Old French *desert*, past participle of *deserter* (“to desert, abandon”), from Latin *dēserere* (“to abandon”), from *dē* (“down, away from”) + *serere* (“to join, link”).
