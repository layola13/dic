outwrung:/ˌaʊtˈrʌŋ/
adjective
拧干的，榨干的
completely wrung out; drained of emotional or physical strength.
Her face was pale and outwrung. 她的脸色苍白，精疲力竭。
近义词: wrung out, drained, exhausted, spent
反义词: refreshed, invigorated
词性: adjective

词源:
"Outwrung" is the past participle of the verb "outwring," which is formed from the prefix "out-" (meaning "thoroughly, completely") and the verb "wring" (meaning "to twist and squeeze to remove liquid").  The word "wring" comes from Old English "wringen," of Germanic origin.
