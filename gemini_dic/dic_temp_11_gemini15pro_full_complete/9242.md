antipathetic:/ˌænti.pəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 反感的，厌恶的，格格不入的 | Having or showing a strong aversion or dislike.  Feeling or showing antipathy. |  He was antipathetic to the idea of moving abroad.  他对移居国外的想法很反感。 |  She seemed antipathetic to any form of exercise. 她似乎对任何形式的锻炼都感到厌恶。 |  averse, hostile, antagonistic, opposed |  sympathetic, friendly, agreeable | adjective
