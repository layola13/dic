litao: /liːˈtaʊ/ | n. |李涛 (人名；俚语)|A transliteration of the Chinese name 李涛, sometimes used online as an interjection expressing a desire for someone to elaborate or explain further. It originates from a Chinese streamer of the same name known for his in-depth analysis and commentary.  While not a standard English word, it is gaining traction in certain online communities familiar with the streamer. |"Someone just posted cryptic hints. Litao!" (有人刚发布了一些神秘的提示。李涛！)| Near Synonyms: Explain, elaborate,  comment, analyze | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
