culicid:/kjuːˈlɪsɪd/| n. adj.|蚊；蚊科动物的|A mosquito or any other member of the family Culicidae.|例句：The culicid population increases dramatically during the rainy season. 雨季蚊子数量急剧增加。

例句：Culicid-borne diseases are a major health concern in tropical regions.  蚊媒疾病是热带地区的主要健康问题。|近义词：mosquito, gnat|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Latin *culex*, *culicis* "gnat, mosquito".
