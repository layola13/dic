cleronomy: /klɪˈrɒnəmi/
n.
世袭圣职; 继承圣职的制度
The inheritance of a sacred office by right of birth.
Cleronomy was common in ancient societies where priesthood was often passed down through families.  世袭圣职在古代社会很常见，那时的祭司职位通常是家族世袭的。
The cleronomy system ensured that religious knowledge and practices remained within specific lineages. 世袭圣职制度确保宗教知识和实践保留在特定的血统内。
近义词: hereditary priesthood,  primogeniture (in a broader sense)
反义词: appointed priesthood, elected priesthood
词性: noun
