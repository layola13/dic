ebulliently:/ɪˈbʌliəntli/ /ɪˈbʊliəntli/
adverb
兴高采烈地；热情洋溢地
In a way that shows overflowing enthusiasm and excitement.
She greeted her guests ebulliently. 她热情洋溢地迎接客人。
The team celebrated their victory ebulliently.  团队兴高采烈地庆祝他们的胜利。
近义词: enthusiastically, exuberantly, joyfully, merrily
反义词: listlessly, apathetically, gloomily
