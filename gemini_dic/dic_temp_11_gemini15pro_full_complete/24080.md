brethren's:/ˈbreðrənz/
noun
兄弟们的，同胞们的
Possessive form of brethren, meaning "brothers," especially used in a religious or fraternal context.
The brethren's hymns filled the church with joy. 兄弟们的赞美诗充满了教堂的喜悦。
The brethren's lodge hosted a charity event. 兄弟会的旅馆举办了一场慈善活动。
近义词:brothers', colleagues', comrades'
反义词:enemies', opponents'
词性:noun (possessive)


brethren:/ˈbreðrən/
noun
兄弟们，同胞们，教友们
Plural form of brother, often used in a religious or fraternal context, referring to members of a group or community.
The brethren gathered for their annual meeting.  教友们聚集在一起举行年会。
He greeted his brethren with a warm embrace. 他热情地拥抱了他的兄弟们。
近义词:brothers, colleagues, comrades
反义词:enemies, opponents
词性:noun


词源:
From Old English *brōðor, from Proto-Germanic *brōþēr, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰréh₂tēr. Cognate with Old Frisian brother, Old Saxon brōthar, Old High German bruodar, Old Norse bróðir, Gothic brōþar, Dutch broeder, German Bruder.
