geographical:/ˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkl/ | adj. | 地理的；地理学的 | relating to geography, the study of the Earth's physical features, climate, products, and population | The geographical location of the island made it difficult to access.  这个岛的地理位置使其难以到达。 | synonyms: spatial, topographic, locational | antonyms: non-spatial, non-geographical | adjective

geographically:/ˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 地理上；地理学上 | in a way that relates to geography |  The countries are geographically close but culturally very different. 这些国家地理位置相近，但文化差异很大。| synonyms: spatially, topographically | antonyms: non-spatially, non-geographically | adverb
