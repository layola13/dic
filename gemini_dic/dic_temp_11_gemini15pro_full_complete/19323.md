biblical:/ˈbɪblɪkəl/| adj.|圣经的；有关圣经的|Relating to the Bible or its teachings; having the qualities of a story from the Bible, especially of epic scale.|The story had biblical proportions.  这个故事规模宏大，如同圣经故事。|近义词：scriptural, religious|反义词：secular, worldly|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *biblicus, from Greek biblios ‘book’, (short for ta biblia hiera ‘the sacred books’), from biblos ‘papyrus’.
