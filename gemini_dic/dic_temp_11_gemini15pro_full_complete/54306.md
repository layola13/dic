It refers to a state of being disabled or incapacitated, often due to illness or injury. | Example: The stroke resulted in significant dishabilitation for the patient. 中风导致患者严重丧失能力。| Synonym: disablement, incapacity, impairment | Antonym: rehabilitation, enablement | Noun
