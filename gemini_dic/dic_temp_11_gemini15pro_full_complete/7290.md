angst:/æŋst/| n.|焦虑，担忧|A feeling of deep anxiety or dread, typically an existential one about the human condition or the state of the world.|She was filled with angst about her future. 她对自己的未来充满了焦虑。|Near synonyms: anxiety, dread, worry, apprehension, anguish|Near antonyms: calmness, tranquility, serenity, peace, composure|noun
