countersuing:/ˌkaʊntərˈsuːɪŋ/
n.
反诉
the action of suing someone who has already sued you
Example:  The company filed a countersuit against its former employee.  这家公司对其前雇员提起了反诉。

Example:  She responded to his lawsuit with a countersuit. 她以反诉回应了他的诉讼。

近义词: countersuit, cross-action, cross-claim
反义词: lawsuit, suit
词性: noun (gerund)
