duelings:/ˈduːəlɪŋz/
noun
决斗（duel的名词复数）
fights between two people, often according to particular formal rules
The duelings of the knights were legendary. 骑士们的决斗是传奇故事。
近义词： combats, fights, battles
反义词： truces, peace
词性:n.


duel:/ˈduːəl/
noun
决斗
a prearranged fight between two people with deadly weapons, typically in order to settle a point of honour
They fought a duel at dawn. 他们在黎明决斗。
近义词： contest, match
反义词： truce
词性:n.

duel:/ˈduːəl/
verb
决斗
fight a duel
He dueled with his rival. 他与他的对手决斗。
近义词： fight, combat
反义词： compromise
词性:v.


词源: From Old French duel, from Latin duellum "war," originally "war between two," from duo "two."
