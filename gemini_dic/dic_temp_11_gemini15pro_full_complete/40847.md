co-occurred:/ˌkoʊ əˈkɜːrd/| v.|共同发生，同时出现|The two events co-occurred, suggesting a possible connection. 这两件事同时发生，表明可能存在联系。|例句：The earthquake and tsunami co-occurred, causing widespread devastation. 地震和海啸同时发生，造成了广泛的破坏。

例句：Symptoms of anxiety and depression often co-occur. 焦虑和抑郁的症状经常同时出现。

近义词：coincided, happened together, occurred simultaneously|反义词: occurred separately, independently|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + occur.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together" or "jointly." "Occur" comes from the Latin "occurrere," meaning "to run towards," "to meet," or "to happen."
