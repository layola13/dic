mitis:/ˈmaɪtɪs/| adj.|温和的，柔软的，成熟的|Mild, soft, ripe; mellowed, softened (as by age or disease).  Referring to something gentle, not harsh, or having reached a state of maturity.  Often used in scientific contexts, particularly botany and medicine. |The mitis strain of the virus is less virulent.  这种病毒的mitis菌株毒性较弱。|Synonyms: mild, gentle, soft, mellow, lenient |Antonyms: harsh, severe, strong, virulent|adjective
