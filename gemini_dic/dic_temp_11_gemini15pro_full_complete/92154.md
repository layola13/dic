hypersensuousnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnʃʊəsnəsɪz/
n.
超感官知觉；极度敏感
Extreme or excessive sensuousness; heightened sensitivity to sensory stimuli.
Her hypersensuousnesses made her acutely aware of the subtle changes in the atmosphere. 她的极度敏感使她敏锐地意识到大气中细微的变化。
近义词：sensitivities, acuities
反义词：insensitivities, numbness
词性：名词
