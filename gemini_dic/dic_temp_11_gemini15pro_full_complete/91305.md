hydrometallurgy:/ˌhaɪdroʊˈmɛtəlɜːrdʒi/ | n. |湿法冶金|The extraction of metals from their ores by dissolving them in aqueous solutions. | Example 1: Hydrometallurgy is a more environmentally friendly method of metal extraction compared to pyrometallurgy. 湿法冶金是一种比火法冶金更环保的金属提取方法。

Example 2:  Copper is often extracted through hydrometallurgy using sulfuric acid. 铜通常是用硫酸通过湿法冶金提取的。|Synonym:  chemical metallurgy, solution mining |Antonym: pyrometallurgy | noun
