limbus:/ˈlɪmbəs/| n. |边缘，界限；地狱边境；（尤指眼角膜与巩膜间的）环状边缘|A region on the border of something, especially hell or the cornea.|The contact lens irritated the limbus of her eye.  隐形眼镜刺激了她的眼角膜边缘。|Synonyms: edge, border, fringe, periphery|Antonyms: center, core|Noun


limbus:/ˈlɪmbəs/| n. |忘却之境；不确定状态|A state of uncertainty or oblivion.|His mind was a limbus of forgotten memories and half-formed thoughts. 他的脑海里充满了被遗忘的记忆和未成形的思想。|Synonyms: oblivion, nothingness, void|Antonyms: awareness, consciousness|Noun
