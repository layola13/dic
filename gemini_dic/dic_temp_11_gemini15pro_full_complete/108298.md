laureations: /ˌlɔːriˈeɪʃənz/
n.
授予学位仪式; 毕业典礼; 桂冠诗人的作品
Ceremonies or the season of conferring degrees at a university. Also, the poems of a Poet Laureate.
The university president presided over the laureations. 大学的校长主持了毕业典礼。
The laureations of Tennyson are highly regarded.  丁尼生的桂冠诗作备受推崇。
近义词: commencement, graduation, convocation
反义词:  None (for ceremony sense);  None (for poems sense)
词性: Noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *laureationem*, accusative singular of *laureatio* ("act of crowning with laurel"), from Latin *laureatus* ("crowned with laurel"), past participle of *laureare* ("to crown with laurel"), from *laurus* ("laurel").
