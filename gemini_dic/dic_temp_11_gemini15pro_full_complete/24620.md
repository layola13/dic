broke-out:/broʊk aʊt/
verb
爆发，突然发生
suddenly started
World War II broke out in 1939. 第二次世界大战于1939年爆发。
近义词：erupted, flared up, commenced
反义词：subsided, died down
词性：动词

broke-out:/broʊk aʊt/
verb
(of a rash or other skin condition) suddenly appeared on the skin
（指皮疹或其他皮肤状况）突然出现
A rash broke out on her arms.  她的胳膊上突然起了皮疹。
近义词：appeared, emerged, developed
反义词：cleared, disappeared
词性：动词

broke-out:/broʊk aʊt/
verb
escaped from a place, especially a prison
逃脱，尤其指越狱
Several prisoners broke out of jail last night. 昨晚有几名囚犯越狱了。
近义词：escaped, fled, got away
反义词：surrendered
词性：动词
