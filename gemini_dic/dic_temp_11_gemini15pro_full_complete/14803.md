ax:/æks/ | n. | 斧头 | A tool with a bladed head fixed perpendicular to a handle, used for chopping wood, etc. |  He used an ax to chop down the tree. 他用斧头砍倒了那棵树。| synonyms: hatchet, tomahawk | antonyms:  (no direct antonym, perhaps saw as a contrasting tool) | noun

ax:/æks/ | v. | 砍，削减 | To cut or strike with an ax; to remove abruptly; to dismiss from a job. | The company axed 200 jobs. 公司裁掉了200个工作岗位。| synonyms: chop, cut, dismiss, fire | antonyms: hire, employ, keep | verb
