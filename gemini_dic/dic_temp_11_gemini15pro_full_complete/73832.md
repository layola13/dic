formidableness:/fɔːrˈmɪdəbəlnəs/| n.|令人生畏；难对付；强大|The formidableness of the task ahead made him hesitate. 前方任务的艰巨性使他犹豫了。|Etymology: From formidable + -ness.  "formidable" comes from French "formidable," from Late Latin "formidabilis"  "causing fear," from Latin "formidare"  "to dread," from "formido" "fear, terror."|Synonyms:  intimidation, dauntingness, awesomeness, power, strength|Antonyms: weakness, insignificance, powerlessness|noun
