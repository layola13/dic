openness:/ˈoʊpənnəs/| n.|开放；公开；坦率；易接受|The quality of being open, honest, and receptive to new ideas or experiences. |例句：Her openness about her struggles inspired others. 她坦诚讲述自己的奋斗经历，激励了其他人。

例句：The government has pledged greater openness. 政府承诺提高透明度。|近义词：frankness, candor, honesty, transparency, receptiveness|反义词：secrecy, closedness, reticence, guardedness|词性:名词
