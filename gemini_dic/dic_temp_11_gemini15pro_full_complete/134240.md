noninfecting:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛktɪŋ/
adjective
非感染性的
Not capable of causing infection.
This strain of the virus is noninfecting and therefore poses no threat to public health.  这种病毒株是非感染性的，因此不会对公众健康构成威胁。
non-infectious, non-contagious
infectious, contagious
