nonmelodramatically:/ˌnɒnˌmɛlədrəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 不夸张地，不戏剧性地 |  In a way that avoids excessive or exaggerated emotional display, typically associated with melodrama.  | She reacted to the news nonmelodramatically, simply stating the facts. 她对这个消息的反应并不夸张，只是简单地陈述了事实。 | He accepted his defeat nonmelodramatically, shaking his opponent's hand and congratulating him. 他不带戏剧性地接受了他的失败，握了握对手的手并祝贺他。 | calmly, quietly, soberly, restrainedly | dramatically, theatrically, hysterically | adverb
