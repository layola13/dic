outsucking:/ˌaʊtˈsʌkɪŋ/
adjective
向外吸的；吸出的
drawing outwards by suction; sucking out
The outsucking pump quickly removed the water.  向外吸的泵很快就把水抽走了。
近义词:extracting, withdrawing, pumping
反义词:injecting, inserting, infusing
词性:adjective


词源:
"Outsucking" is a combination of the adverb "out" and the present participle of the verb "suck." "Out" indicates direction away from a center or enclosure, while "suck" refers to drawing something in by creating a vacuum.  Therefore, "outsucking" describes the action of sucking something outwards.
