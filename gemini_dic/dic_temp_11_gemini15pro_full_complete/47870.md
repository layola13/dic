deceiving:/dɪˈsiːvɪŋ/
adjective
欺骗性的，误导性的
giving a false impression; misleading
The advertisement was deceiving, the product was not as good as it claimed to be. 这则广告具有欺骗性，产品不如它宣传的那么好。
Synonyms: misleading, deceptive, fallacious, dishonest, untruthful
Antonyms: honest, truthful, genuine, sincere, accurate
词性: 形容词

deceiving:/dɪˈsiːvɪŋ/
verb (present participle of deceive)
欺骗，蒙蔽
to cause someone to believe something that is not true
He was deceiving his clients by selling them fake products. 他通过向客户出售假冒产品来欺骗他们。
Synonyms: misleading, tricking, fooling, deluding, cheating
Antonyms:  being honest, truthful, forthright
词性: 动词 (deceive的现在分词)
