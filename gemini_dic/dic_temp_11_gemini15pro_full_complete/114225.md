I check my mail every morning. 我每天早上查看邮件。
She mails a letter to her grandmother every week. 她每周都给祖母寄一封信。

I am mailing a package right now. 我现在正在寄包裹。
He is reading his mail at the moment. 他此刻正在读邮件。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have mailed the invitations for the party. 我已经寄出了派对的邀请函。
She has received a lot of mail this week. 这周她收到了很多邮件。
