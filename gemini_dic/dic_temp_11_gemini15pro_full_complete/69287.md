feature-film:/ˈfiːtʃə fɪlm/ | n. | 故事片，正片 | A full-length film telling a story, as distinguished from a short film, documentary, etc. |  Example:  She starred in her first feature film at the age of 18. 她18岁时主演了她的第一部故事片。

Example: The festival showcases a diverse range of feature films from around the world.  这个电影节展示了来自世界各地的各种故事片。 | Synonyms:  motion picture, movie, film, full-length film | Antonyms: short film, documentary, trailer | noun
