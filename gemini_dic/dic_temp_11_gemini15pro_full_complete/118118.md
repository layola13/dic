menology: /mɪˈnɒlədʒi/ /məˈnɑːlədʒi/
n.
圣人传; 月志; 宗教日历;
A menology is a calendar or register of saints, arranged according to the day of the month on which their festivals are celebrated.
例句：The monastery library contained a rare illuminated menology.  修道院图书馆里有一本罕见的圣人传彩绘本。

例句：He consulted the menology to determine the saint's feast day. 他查阅了月志以确定圣人的节日。

近义词：calendar of saints, martyrology
反义词: None
词性：noun
