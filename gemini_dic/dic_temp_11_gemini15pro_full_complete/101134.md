investigations:/ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃənz/| n.|调查，侦查，研究|The police are carrying out investigations into the robbery. 警方正在对这起抢劫案进行调查。|例句：Several investigations are underway into the cause of the plane crash.  几项关于飞机失事原因的调查正在进行中。

例句：The FBI launched a series of investigations into the company's financial dealings.  联邦调查局对该公司的财务往来展开了一系列调查。|近义词：inquiries, probes, researches, studies|反义词:cover-up, concealment|词性:名词(plural of investigation)

investigation:/ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|调查，侦查，研究|The police are conducting an investigation into the murder. 警方正在对这起谋杀案进行调查。|例句：The investigation revealed a complex web of deceit and corruption. 调查揭露了一个由欺骗和腐败交织成的复杂网络。


例句：A thorough investigation is necessary to determine the cause of the fire. 需要进行彻底的调查以确定火灾原因。|近义词：inquiry, probe, research, study|反义词:cover-up, concealment|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin investigatio, from investigare "to track, trace out," from in- "into" + vestigare "to track, trace," from vestigium "footprint, track."
