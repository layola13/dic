ibex:/ˈaɪbɛks/
n.
野山羊
A wild goat with long, ridged horns, native to mountain areas of Eurasia and North Africa.
The ibex stood majestically on the mountain peak. 野山羊雄伟地站在山峰上。
近义词：wild goat, mountain goat
反义词：(无直接反义词)
词性：名词

词源:
From Latin ibex, from an unknown Iberian or Ligurian source. Ultimately probably of pre-Indo-European origin.
