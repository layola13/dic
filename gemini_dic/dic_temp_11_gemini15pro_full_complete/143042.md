outbluffed: /ˌaʊtˈblʌft/
verb
以虚张声势胜过，以更大的虚张声势压倒
to defeat someone by bluffing more effectively than they do.
He outbluffed his opponents in the poker game and won the pot. 他在扑克游戏中以虚张声势胜过对手，赢得了彩池。
outmaneuver, outwit, surpass
- (no clear opposite, perhaps "be outbluffed by")
