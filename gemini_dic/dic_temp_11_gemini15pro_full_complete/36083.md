coelenterata:/sɪˈlɛntəˈreɪtə/ | n. |腔肠动物|Aquatic invertebrate animals characterized by a radially symmetrical body with a single opening serving as both mouth and anus, and specialized stinging cells (cnidocytes) for capturing prey.  This term is outdated and now generally replaced by Cnidaria.|Example: Coelenterata, such as jellyfish and corals, are fascinating creatures. 腔肠动物，如水母和珊瑚，是迷人的生物。|Synonym: Cnidaria|Antonym: N/A|Noun
