antaean:/ænˈtiːən/ | adj. | 巨大的，力大无穷的;  从大地获得力量的 | Of or relating to Antaeus, a giant in Greek mythology who gained strength from contact with the earth.  Also used figuratively to describe someone or something that draws strength or inspiration from a particular source, especially one's homeland or roots. |  His antaean strength seemed to grow whenever he touched the ground.  他巨大的力量似乎每当他接触地面时就会增长。 |  powerful, strong, mighty, Herculean | weak, feeble, powerless | adjective
