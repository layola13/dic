convivial: /kənˈvɪviəl/ | adj. | 欢乐的；友好的；好交际的 |  Friendly, lively, and enjoyable, especially when people are eating and drinking together; fond of feasting, drinking, and good company. | We had a convivial evening with friends, sharing stories and laughter. 我们和朋友们度过了一个愉快的夜晚，分享故事和欢笑。|  The restaurant had a convivial atmosphere. 这家餐厅的氛围很热闹友好。 |  sociable, genial, jovial, affable, gregarious | unsociable, unfriendly, aloof, cold | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *convivialis* "of a feast," from convivium "a feast,"  from com-, intensive prefix + vivere "to live."
