indisciplinable:/ˌɪndɪˈsɪplɪnəbl/
adjective
难以管教的；不服约束的
Not able to be disciplined or controlled.
Example: The teacher found the class of five-year-olds utterly indisciplinable.  老师发现这个五岁孩子的班级完全无法管教。

Example:  He was an indisciplinable child, constantly getting into trouble. 他是个难以管教的孩子，总是惹麻烦。
近义词: unruly, unmanageable, uncontrollable, incorrigible
反义词: disciplined, obedient, controllable, manageable
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From in- (not) + disciplinable (capable of being disciplined).  The word "discipline" comes from Latin "disciplina" meaning "instruction, knowledge".
