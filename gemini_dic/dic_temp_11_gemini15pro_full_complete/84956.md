haplessly:/ˈhæpləsli/| adv.|不幸地；倒霉地|In an unlucky or unfortunate way.  Derived from "hapless" (unlucky) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  The root "hap" comes from Old Norse "happ" meaning luck or chance.|He haplessly tripped and spilled his coffee. 他不幸地绊了一跤，把咖啡洒了。|unluckily, unfortunately, disastrously|fortunately, luckily|adverb
