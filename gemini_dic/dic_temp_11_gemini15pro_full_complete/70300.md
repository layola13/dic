fidgeting:/ˈfɪdʒɪtɪŋ/| n. |坐立不安，烦躁不安|The act of making small, restless movements, especially with the hands or feet, typically as a sign of nervousness or impatience.|Her constant fidgeting showed how nervous she was. 她不停地坐立不安，显示出她有多紧张。|Synonyms: fiddling, twitching, squirming|Antonyms: stillness, calmness|noun

fidget:/ˈfɪdʒɪt/| v. |坐立不安，烦躁|To make small, restless movements, especially with the hands or feet, typically as a sign of nervousness or impatience.|He fidgeted nervously in his chair. 他在椅子上紧张地坐立不安。|Synonyms: fiddle, twitch, squirm|Antonyms: relax, be still|verb
