levitically:/ləˈvɪtɪkli/| adv.|依照利未记;像利未人一样|According to the laws and rituals described in the Book of Leviticus in the Bible.  Relating to the Levites, the priestly tribe in ancient Israel.|He examined the animal levitically to ensure it was fit for sacrifice. 他按照利未记的规定检查了这只动物，以确保它适合献祭。|Analogously, ritually, ceremonially|Secularly|adverb
