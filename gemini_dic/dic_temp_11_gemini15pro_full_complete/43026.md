cowen:/ˈkaʊɪn/| n.|母牛（苏格兰、英格兰北部方言）|A cowen is a female bovine, specifically a cow, primarily used in dialects of Scotland and Northern England.  It's an older term, less common in modern usage. |The farmer milked the cowen every morning.  农民每天早上挤牛奶。|Synonyms: cow, bovine|Antonyms: bull (opposite gender)|Noun
