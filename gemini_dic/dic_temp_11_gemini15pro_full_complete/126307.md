myoid:/ˈmaɪɔɪd/| adj. n.|类肌的，肌样的；类肌细胞|Resembling muscle; a cell resembling a smooth muscle cell.|例句1：Myoid cells contract similarly to smooth muscle cells. 类肌细胞的收缩方式类似于平滑肌细胞。

例句2：The tumor contained myoid tissue.  肿瘤包含类肌组织。|近义词：muscle-like, myoepithelial|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词, 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From myo- + -oid.  "Myo-" comes from Greek *mys* meaning "muscle". "-oid"  is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "like".
