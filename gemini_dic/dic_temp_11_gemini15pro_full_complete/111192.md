lithoidal:/lɪˈθɔɪdəl/
adjective
岩石的; 似岩石的
Resembling stone; stony.  Having the texture or appearance of stone.
The lithoidal tumor was hard and dense, mimicking the surrounding bone tissue.  这个岩石状的肿瘤坚硬而致密，模仿周围的骨组织。
The artist sculpted a remarkably lithoidal figure from clay.  这位艺术家用粘土雕刻了一个非常像岩石的人物。
近义词: stony, rocky, petrous
反义词: soft, malleable
词性: adjective

词源:
From litho- + -oidal.  "Litho-" comes from the Greek word "lithos," meaning "stone."  "-oidal" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "like."
