nonconsumer:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsuːmər/ |n.|非消费者，非用户|A person or organization that does not consume a particular product or service.| Example:  The marketing campaign failed to reach nonconsumers, leading to disappointing sales. 这场营销活动未能触及非消费者，导致销售额令人失望。

Example: The study focused on the attitudes of nonconsumers towards electric vehicles. 这项研究的重点是非消费者对电动汽车的态度。|Synonyms: non-user, abstainer|Antonyms: consumer, user|noun
