naga:/ˈnɑːɡə/ | n. |  那伽,  神龙,  眼镜蛇 | A mythical serpent-like creature, often depicted with a human head and the body of a cobra, prominent in Hinduism and Buddhism. |  Example: The naga guarded the entrance to the temple.  那伽守护着寺庙的入口。| Example:  Legends say the naga can control the weather.  传说那伽可以控制天气。| Synonym: serpent, dragon, cobra | Antonym:  None (mythical creature) | noun
