bypass:/ˈbaɪpæs/| n. vt.|旁路，绕过，回避|A road that passes around a town or city to avoid traffic congestion.  A way of avoiding something. To go around or avoid something.|例句：The new bypass will reduce traffic in the town center.  这条新的旁路将减少市中心的交通。  He bypassed the manager and went straight to the owner. 他绕过了经理，直接去找了老板。|近义词：detour, circumvent, avoid|反义词:confront, encounter|noun, verb
