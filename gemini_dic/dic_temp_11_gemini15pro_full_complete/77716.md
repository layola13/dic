gemmiferous:/dʒɛˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
多芽的；产芽的；生宝石的
Bearing gems; producing buds.
The gemmiferous plant produced a beautiful ruby.  这种产芽植物孕育出了一颗美丽的红宝石。
The cave was known for its gemmiferous walls. 这个洞穴以其蕴藏宝石的墙壁而闻名。
Synonyms: gem-bearing, bud-producing
Antonyms: barren, sterile
