nonstimulating:/ˌnɒnˈstɪmjuleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
无刺激性的; 不刺激的
Not stimulating; not exciting or rousing.
The lecture was so nonstimulating that many students fell asleep. 这场讲座枯燥乏味，许多学生都睡着了。
nonstimulating environment  无刺激的环境
近义词:uninspiring, boring, dull, tedious, monotonous, bland
反义词:stimulating, exciting, interesting, engaging
