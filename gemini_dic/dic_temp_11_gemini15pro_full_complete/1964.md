activates:/ˈæktɪveɪts/| v.|激活，启动|To make something active or operational; to trigger or start a process or function.|The enzyme activates the chemical reaction. 这种酶能激活化学反应。|Synonyms: triggers, starts, initiates, sets off|Antonyms: deactivates, inhibits, stops|verb

activates:/ˈæktɪveɪts/| v.|使活动，使活跃|To make someone or something more energetic, lively, or involved.|The music activates the dancers. 音乐使舞者活跃起来。|Synonyms: energizes, stimulates, enlivens|Antonyms: subdues, calms, pacifies|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *activatus, past participle of activare "to make active," from Latin activus "active" (see active).
