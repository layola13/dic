livor:/ˈlaɪvər/| n.|  尸斑，死后瘀血|Discoloration of the skin due to the pooling of blood in the dependent parts of the body after death, which occurs as a result of gravity.|Example: The livor mortis suggested the victim had been dead for several hours. 尸斑表明受害者已经死亡数小时了。|Example: The pathologist noted the livor in his report. 病理学家在他的报告中记录了尸斑。|Synonym: lividity, hypostasis|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a postmortem process)|noun
