enchant:/ɪnˈtʃænt/ (US), /ɛnˈtʃɑːnt/ (UK)| v.|使着迷，使陶醉；施魔法于|To fill someone with great delight; to put a magic spell on.|The beautiful music enchanted the audience. 美妙的音乐使观众陶醉。|近义词：captivate, charm, fascinate, bewitch|反义词：disenchant, repel, bore|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old French enchanter, from Late Latin incantare “to chant a magic formula over,” from in- “in, on” + cantare “to sing.”
