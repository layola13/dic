launderette: /ˌlɔːndəˈrɛt/ (British), /ˌlɑːndəˈrɛt/ (American)| n. |自助洗衣店；投币洗衣店|A self-service laundry where customers wash and dry their own clothes using coin-operated machines.|Example: I went to the launderette to wash my duvet. 我去自助洗衣店洗了我的羽绒被。|Synonym: laundromat, self-service laundry|Antonym: dry cleaner|Noun
