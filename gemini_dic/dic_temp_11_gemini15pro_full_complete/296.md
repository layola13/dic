aberrance:/əˈbɛrəns/ | n. | 异常，偏差，反常 |A state or condition markedly different from the norm; a deviation. |例句：His sudden outburst of anger was an aberrance from his usually calm demeanor. 他突然爆发的愤怒与他通常平静的风度截然不同。

例句：The genetic aberrance caused a rare disease. 遗传异常导致了一种罕见的疾病。

近义词：deviation, anomaly, abnormality, irregularity |反义词：normality, regularity, standard |词性：名词
