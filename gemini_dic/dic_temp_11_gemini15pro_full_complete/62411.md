endogeny:/enˈdɒdʒəni/ | n. |内生，内因性|The development or growth from within; internal formation.|例句：The endogeny of this tumor suggests it originated within the body.  这个肿瘤的内生性表明它起源于体内。

例句：Endogeny plays a crucial role in the formation of certain geological structures. 内生作用在某些地质构造的形成中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：internal development, internal growth, autogeny|反义词：exogeny|词性:名词
