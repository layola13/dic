monoxenous:/ˌmɒnɒkˈsiːnəs/ /ˌmɑːnɑːkˈsiːnəs/| adj.|单宿主的|Living or developing in only a single host organism throughout its life cycle.|The parasite is monoxenous, completing its life cycle within a single snail species.|近义词：monogenic|反义词：heteroxenous, polyxenous|词性:形容词

monoxeny:/ˌmɒnɒkˈsiːni/ /ˌmɑːnɑːkˈsiːni/| n.|单宿主性|The state of being monoxenous; having a life cycle restricted to a single host.|Monoxeny is a common characteristic of many intestinal parasites.|近义词：monogenesis|反义词：heteroxeny, polyxeny|词性:名词
