diffract:/dɪˈfrækt/ | v. | 衍射，绕射 | to cause (something, such as a beam of light) to bend and spread out : to separate (light) into colors : to undergo diffraction |  The prism diffracted the light into a rainbow of colors. 棱镜将光衍射成彩虹的颜色。 | synonyms: disperse, scatter | antonyms: focus, concentrate | verb

diffraction:/dɪˈfrækʃən/ | n. | 衍射，绕射 | the process by which a beam of light or other system of waves is spread out as a result of passing through a narrow aperture or across an edge, typically accompanied by interference between the wave forms produced. |  The diffraction of light through the grating created a pattern of bright spots. 光通过光栅的衍射产生了一个亮斑图案。 | synonyms: scattering, dispersion | antonyms: focusing, concentration | noun
