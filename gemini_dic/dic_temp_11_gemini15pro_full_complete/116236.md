masquerading:/ˌmæskəˈreɪdɪŋ/
|adj. |伪装的，假扮的| appearing or pretending to be something different from what one really is |  He was masquerading as a police officer. 他伪装成一名警察。 | synonyms: posing, impersonating, feigning, pretending | antonyms: revealing, exposing, unmasking | word type: adjective, present participle of 'masquerade'


masquerade:/ˌmæskəˈreɪd/
|n. |伪装，化装舞会|a false show or pretense| The party was a masquerade, with everyone in elaborate costumes.  这个派对是一个化装舞会，每个人都穿着精致的服装。| synonyms: disguise, pretense, charade | antonyms: reality, truth, genuineness | word type: noun

masquerade:/ˌmæskəˈreɪd/
|vi.|伪装，冒充|to appear or behave deceptively under a false appearance |He was masquerading as a doctor. 他冒充医生。| synonyms: pose, impersonate, feign, pretend | antonyms: reveal, expose, unmask | word type: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mascarade* "masked ball," from Italian *mascherata*, from *maschera* "mask," probably from Arabic *maskhara* "buffoonery, mockery."
