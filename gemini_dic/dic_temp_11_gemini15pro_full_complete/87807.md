hiccup:/ˈhɪkʌp/ (US) /ˈhɪkʌp/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|打嗝，呃逆；小故障，小问题|A sudden, involuntary contraction of the diaphragm that closes the glottis and causes a characteristic sound.|例句：I ate too fast and got the hiccups. 我吃得太快了，打嗝了。|近义词：spasm, glitch (for problems)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the physiological act)|noun, verb
