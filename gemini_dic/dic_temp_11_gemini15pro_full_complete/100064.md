interpolate:/ɪnˈtɜːrpəleɪt/ /ɪnˈtɜːrpoleɪt/ | v. |插入; 估算; 篡改; |To insert (something of a different nature) into something else. To estimate the value of (a function or series) between two known values.|The author interpolates modern slang into the Shakespearean dialogue. 作者在莎士比亚式的对话中插入了现代俚语。|synonyms: insert, interject, introduce, inject, add, enter, incorporate, include, weave in, work in, interpose, intercalate|antonyms: delete, remove, extract, expunge|verb

interpolate:/ɪnˈtɜːrpəleɪt/ /ɪnˈtɜːrpoleɪt/ | n. |内插值|A value or term located between other values or terms.|The graph shows the interpolates calculated from the given data points. 图表显示了根据给定数据点计算出的内插值。|synonyms: intermediate value|antonyms: N/A|noun
