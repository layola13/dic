gob:/ɡɒb/ (UK) /ɡɑːb/ (US)| n. v. |一口，大量；吐痰；吞食|A lump or a mouthful of something, especially thick or sticky.  Also, to spit.|
He took a large gob of peanut butter. 他吃了一大口花生酱。
He gobbed on the sidewalk. 他在人行道上吐痰。|
近义词：lump, mouthful, wad, spit |反义词: N/A |词性: noun, verb

gob:/ɡɒb/ (UK) /ɡɑːb/ (US)| n. |大量|A large amount of something.|
He inherited a gob of money from his uncle. 他从叔叔那里继承了一大笔钱。|
近义词：lot, heap, pile |反义词: little, bit |词性: noun
