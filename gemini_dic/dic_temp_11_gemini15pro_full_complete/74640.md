free-fallen:/ˈfriːˌfɔːlən/ | adj. | 自由落体的；自由下降的 | Having fallen freely; experiencing free fall.  This describes something that has fallen under the influence of gravity alone, without any other forces acting on it. | The free-fallen object accelerated towards the earth. 自由落体物体向地球加速。

The skydiver experienced a few seconds of free-fallen weightlessness. 跳伞运动员经历了几秒钟的自由落体失重状态。 | 近义词：dropped, plummeting, falling | 反义词：rising, ascending | 词性: 形容词
