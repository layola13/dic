ETYMOLOGY->
From 'broad' (widely) and 'cast' (to throw or scatter), originally referring to the scattering of seeds.  Later adopted for the dissemination of information via radio and television.  源自“broad”（广泛地）和“cast”（投掷或散播），最初指的是播撒种子。后来被用于指通过无线电和电视传播信息。
