overgrievousness:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvəsnəs/
noun
过度悲伤，极度悲伤
Excessive grief or sorrow; the state of being excessively or unduly grieved.
Her overgrievousness after the loss of her pet was understandable but concerning. 她失去宠物后过度悲伤，这是可以理解但令人担忧的。
The therapist helped him cope with his overgrievousness.  治疗师帮助他应对过度悲伤。
近义词: excessive grief, profound sorrow, intense sadness
反义词: resilience, acceptance, composure
词性: noun
