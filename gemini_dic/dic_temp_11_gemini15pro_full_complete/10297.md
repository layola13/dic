apparence:/əˈpærəns/ | n. | 外貌，外观，出现 | outward aspect; the way something looks |  The car has the appearance of being brand new. 这辆车看起来像全新的一样。| synonyms: look, aspect, semblance, guise | antonyms: reality, essence | noun

Etymology:  From Old French *aparence, from Late Latin *apparentia, from Latin apparens, present participle of apparēre "to appear," from ad- "to" + parēre "to come in sight, be visible, appear, seem, obey."
