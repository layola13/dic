humiliate: /hjuːˈmɪlieɪt/ (US) /hjuːˈmɪlɪeɪt/ (UK)
verb
使蒙羞，羞辱，丢脸
to make someone feel ashamed or lose respect for himself or herself
例句：He was humiliated by his failure in front of his colleagues. 他在同事面前的失败让他感到羞愧。

例句：She felt humiliated when her secret was revealed to everyone. 当她的秘密被所有人知道时，她感到很丢脸。

近义词：shame, disgrace, mortify, abase
反义词：honor, exalt, praise
词性：v.
