outweigh:/ˌaʊtˈweɪ/
verb
超过，胜过
to be greater or more important than something else
The advantages of this deal outweigh the disadvantages. 这笔交易的利大于弊。
Synonyms: surpass, exceed, overbalance
Antonyms: lose to, be inferior to, be outweighed by
词性: 动词
