disquietly:/dɪsˈkwaɪətli/
adverb
不安地，不平静地
In a manner that shows or causes worry, uneasiness, or anxiety.
She paced disquietly around the room, waiting for the news. 她不安地在房间里踱来踱去，等待着消息。
He coughed disquietly, sensing the tension in the room.  他不安地咳嗽了一声，感觉到了房间里的紧张气氛。
近义词: uneasily, restlessly, nervously, anxiously
反义词: calmly, peacefully, serenely
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From disquiet + -ly.  Disquiet comes from the Old French *desquiete, from des- (expressing negation) + quiet (quiet).
