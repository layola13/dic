north-western:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛstərn/ | adj. | 西北的，来自西北的 | Situated in or coming from the northwest.  |  例句：The north-western region of the country is known for its rugged coastline.  这个国家的西北地区以其崎岖的海岸线而闻名。| 近义词：northwesterly | 反义词：south-eastern | 词性: adjective

north-western:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛstərn/ | n. | 西北人，西北风 | A person from the northwest or a wind from the northwest. | 例句：He is a north-western, born and bred. 他是一个土生土长的西北人。 | 近义词：northwesterner | 反义词：south-easterner | 词性: noun
