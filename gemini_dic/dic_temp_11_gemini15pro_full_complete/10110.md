apogee:/ˈæpədʒiː/
n.
最高点；顶峰；远地点
The highest point in the development of something; the climax or culmination.  The point in the orbit of the moon or a satellite at which it is furthest from the earth.
The rocket reached its apogee and began to descend. 火箭到达了它的最高点，然后开始下降。
The apogee of her career was when she won the Nobel Prize. 她事业的顶峰是她获得诺贝尔奖的时候。
近义词：climax, peak, summit, zenith, culmination
反义词：nadir, perigee
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French apogée, from New Latin apogaeum, from Greek apogaion (diastēma) ‘(distance) away from earth’, from apo ‘from’ + gaia, gē ‘earth’.
