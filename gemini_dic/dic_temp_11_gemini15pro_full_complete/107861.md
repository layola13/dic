large:/lɑːrdʒ/ | adj. |大的，大量的|Of considerable or relatively great size, amount, or extent.|  The company made a large profit this year.  这家公司今年获利丰厚。|近义词：big, huge, enormous, massive, substantial, considerable|反义词：small, little, tiny, minute, minuscule|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *large*, from Old French *large*, from Latin *largus* ("abundant, copious, liberal, large"), of uncertain origin.
