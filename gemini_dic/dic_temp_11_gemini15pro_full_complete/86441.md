heemraad:/ˈheɪmˌrɑːt/| n.| 海姆拉德（南非荷兰语，指地方议员或市政委员）| A heemraad was a member of a local council in areas of Dutch colonial settlement, particularly in South Africa.  It was a position of local authority and responsibility. | The heemraad met to discuss local issues.  海姆拉德开会讨论地方问题。| council member, local official |  (No direct antonym) | noun
