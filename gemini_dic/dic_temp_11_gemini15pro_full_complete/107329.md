lambert:/ˈlæmbərt/| n.|兰伯特|A unit of luminance or brightness equal to one lumen per square centimeter.  It is a non-SI unit of luminance named after Johann Heinrich Lambert, a Swiss mathematician, physicist, and astronomer.|The screen's brightness was measured at 500 lamberts.|近义词:nit, candela per square meter|反义词:(No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
