capillarity:/ˌkæpɪˈlærəti/| n.|毛细作用，毛细现象|The tendency of a liquid in a capillary tube or absorbent material to rise or fall as a result of surface tension.|The capillarity of the soil allows water to reach the roots of plants. 土壤的毛细作用使水能够到达植物的根部。|近义词：capillary action, capillary attraction|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one might consider concepts like "gravity" or "repulsion" in specific contexts)|noun
