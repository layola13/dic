fiducial:/fɪˈduːʃəl/ | adj. n.| 基准的，基准点；受托的，信托的 | Used as a fixed point of reference.  Relating to or involving trust, especially with regard to the relationship between a trustee and a beneficiary. |  The surveyor used the fiducial marks on the map to determine the exact location. 测量员使用地图上的基准标记来确定准确位置。 | Synonyms: benchmark, reference, datum, trusted, fiduciary | Antonyms: unreliable, untrusted | adjective
