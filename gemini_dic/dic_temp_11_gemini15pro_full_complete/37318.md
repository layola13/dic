commemorates:/kəˈmɛməˌreɪts/
verb
纪念，庆祝
serves to recall and show respect for (someone or something)
This statue commemorates the soldiers who died in the war.  这座雕像纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。
celebrate, memorialize, honor
forget, ignore, neglect
v.
