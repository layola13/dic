boomest:/ˈbuːmɪst/ | adj. | 最繁荣的; 婴儿潮一代最多的; 经历过婴儿潮的 |  A humorous or ironic term referring to someone or something as being the most representative or quintessential of the baby boomer generation (typically born between 1946 and 1964), often implying stereotypical qualities associated with that generation. |  "That avocado toast obsession is so boomest."  他痴迷于牛油果吐司，真是太婴儿潮了。 | 近义词: boomer-like, stereotypical boomer | 反义词: millennial, Gen Z | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: A portmanteau of "boomer" (short for "baby boomer") and the superlative suffix "-est".
