agama:/əˈɡɑːmə/ | n. | agama; rock lizard |A type of lizard found in warm regions, especially in Asia and Africa.  It is known for its ability to change color. | The agama basked in the sun on a rock.  那只飞蜥在一块岩石上晒太阳。| Synonyms: lizard, reptile | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider mammal, bird as contrasting animal categories) | noun
