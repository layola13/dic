matriculators:/məˈtrɪkjʊˌleɪtərz/
noun
注册者；入学新生
People who matriculate, or are formally enrolled, especially in a college or university.
The matriculators gathered in the auditorium for orientation.  入学新生聚集在礼堂里参加迎新会。
The university welcomed the new matriculators with a ceremony.  大学为新入学的学生举行了欢迎仪式。
Synonyms: enrollees, registrants, freshmen (sometimes)
Antonyms: graduates, alumni
n.
