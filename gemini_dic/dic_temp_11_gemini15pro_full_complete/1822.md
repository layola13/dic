acrobacy: /ˈækrəˌbæsi/ (US) /ˈækrəˌbeɪsi/ (UK)| n. |杂技；特技；高难度动作|The performance involved impressive feats of acrobacy. 表演中包含了令人印象深刻的杂技表演。|Acrobacy requires great skill and balance. 杂技需要高超的技巧和平衡能力。|近义词：gymnastics, stunts, feats|反义词: clumsiness, awkwardness|词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin acrobatia, from Greek akrobateia "rope-walking, tightrope-walking," from akrobatēs "walking on tiptoe, rope-dancer," from akron "tip, summit, extremity" + bainein "to go, walk, step."
