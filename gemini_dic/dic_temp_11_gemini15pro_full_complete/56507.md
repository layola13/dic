domesticator:/dəˌmestɪˈkeɪtər/
n.|驯化者；驯养者|A person who tames or cultivates a plant or animal.|The domesticator of the wild horse transformed human history.  这位野马的驯化者改变了人类历史。|
domesticator:/dəˌmestɪˈkeɪtər/|noun|驯化者；驯养者|Someone who tames or cultivates a plant or animal, especially by generations of breeding.|Through selective breeding, the ancient domesticators transformed the wolf into the dog we know today. 通过选择性繁殖，古代的驯化者将狼变成了我们今天所知的狗。|
domesticator:/dəˌmestɪˈkeɪtər/|noun|驯化者|A person who tames or trains an animal, especially a wild one, so that it can live with or work for humans. |The domesticators of the horse revolutionized warfare and travel. 马的驯化者彻底改变了战争和旅行的方式。|近义词: tamer, trainer, breeder|反义词: hunter, poacher|
