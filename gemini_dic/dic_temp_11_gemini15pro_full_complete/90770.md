humorists:/ˈhjuːmərɪsts/ | n. |幽默家；滑稽人物|A person who uses humor in writing, speaking, or performance, especially to entertain or make social commentary. |例句：Mark Twain was one of America's greatest humorists. 马克·吐温是美国最伟大的幽默家之一。

例句：The humorists poked fun at the political situation. 这些幽默家们拿政治局势开玩笑。|近义词：comedians, wits, jokers, satirists|反义词：serious writers,  pessimists|词性:名词
