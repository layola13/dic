abassi:/əˈbæsi/ | n. | 阿巴斯，阿巴西（一种旧时波斯货币单位） | An abassi was a silver coin used in Persia (now Iran) especially during the 17th and 18th centuries.  It was named after Shah Abbas I. |  He paid ten abassis for the carpet. 他花了十个阿巴西买了那块地毯。 | synonyms: coin, currency | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a unit of currency) | noun
