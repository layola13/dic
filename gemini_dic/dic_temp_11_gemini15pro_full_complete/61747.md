empanoplied:/ɪmˈpænəplid/
adjective
披甲的，全副武装的
Dressed in full armor; clad in panoply.
The knight, empanoplied in shining steel, rode forth to battle.  这位骑士披着闪亮的钢甲，骑马出征。
The warrior was empanoplied for the coming battle.  这位战士为了即将到来的战斗做好了全副武装。
近义词: armored, clad, equipped, arrayed
反义词: unarmed, defenseless, unprotected
词性: adjective

词源:
From en- + panoply.  From the French *empanoplier*, derived from panoplie "full suit of armor."  Ultimately from the Greek panoplia "full armor," from pan- "all" + hopla "arms, armor."
