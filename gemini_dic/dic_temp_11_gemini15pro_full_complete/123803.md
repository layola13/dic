mopily:/ˈmoʊpɪli/| adv.|闷闷不乐地，沮丧地|In a sad and gloomy manner; dejectedly. |He sat mopily in the corner, staring at the floor. 他闷闷不乐地坐在角落里，盯着地板。|She walked mopily through the park, lost in thought. 她心事重重地、无精打采地在公园里走着。|近义词：sadly, glumly, dejectedly, despondently|反义词：cheerfully, happily, joyfully|词性:副词

词源:
From mope (v.) + -ly (adv. suffix).  "Mope" comes from the Middle English "mopen," meaning "to be silent or dull," of uncertain origin.
