inframundane:/ˌɪnfrəˈmʌndeɪn/
adjective
世俗的；尘世的；地上的
Of or relating to the earth or this world; terrestrial. Existing or operating in the physical world; worldly.
例句：
Inframundane concerns like paying bills and grocery shopping often take precedence over spiritual pursuits.
世俗的担忧，比如付账单和买菜，常常优先于精神追求。

例句：
Her poetry often explores the contrast between the inframundane and the divine.
她的诗歌经常探讨世俗与神圣之间的对比。

近义词：earthly, terrestrial, mundane, worldly, secular
反义词：celestial, heavenly, spiritual, ethereal, otherworldly
词性：adjective

词源：
From Latin infra ("below") + mundus ("world").  Literally, "below the world."  Originally referred to the classical elements of earth and water (as opposed to air and fire).
