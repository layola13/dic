migratory:/ˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔːri/ (US), /maɪˈɡreɪtəri/ (UK)| adj.|迁徙的，迁移的|Relating to migration; habitually moving from place to place especially at regular intervals, typically annually.|Many birds are migratory, flying south for the winter. 许多鸟类都是迁徙的，冬天会飞往南方。|Swallows are migratory birds. 燕子是候鸟。|近义词：nomadic, mobile, itinerant|反义词：settled, resident, stationary|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *migratorius*, from Latin migrat-, past participle stem of migrare "to move from one place to another," from migrare "to depart, change one's abode, migrate."
