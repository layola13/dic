miscoinage:/ˌmɪsˈkɔɪnɪdʒ/ | n. | 伪造的货币；杜撰的词语 | A word or phrase that has been incorrectly or inappropriately created. |  The word "irregardless" is considered a miscoinage. “irregardless”这个词被认为是杜撰的词。 | Synonyms: neologism (when unintentional), solecism, barbarism | Antonyms: correct usage, standard word | noun
