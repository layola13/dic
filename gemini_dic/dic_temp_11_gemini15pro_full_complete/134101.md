nonimmanency:/ˌnɒnɪˈmænənsi/
n.
非内在性；非固有性
The state or quality of not being immanent; transcendence.  Existing or operating separate from.  Not inherent or intrinsic.
The concept of God's nonimmanency suggests a divine being separate from the material world.  上帝非内在性的概念表明神圣的存在与物质世界是分离的。
transcendence, externality
immanence, inherence
noun
