bossily:/ˈbɒsɪli/ (UK), /ˈbɑːsɪli/ (US)| adv.|专横地，跋扈地|In a manner of a boss; domineeringly; dictatorialy.  Acting like someone in charge, often in a way that is too forceful or controlling.|She bossily ordered everyone around. 她专横地指挥着每个人。|"Don't just stand there," she said bossily, "get me a coffee." “别光站在那儿，”她跋扈地说，“给我冲杯咖啡。”|近义词：domineeringly, dictatorially, imperiously, overbearingly, autocratically|反义词：submissively, meekly, humbly|词性:副词
