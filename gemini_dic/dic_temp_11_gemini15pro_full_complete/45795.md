custardy: /ˈkʌstərdi/ | adj. |像蛋奶冻的；柔软滑腻的|Resembling custard in texture; soft and creamy. |The flan had a smooth, custardy texture. 这法兰糕口感顺滑，像蛋奶冻一样。| The rice pudding was deliciously custardy.  这米饭布丁美味极了，像蛋奶冻一样。 | 近义词: creamy, smooth, soft, velvety | 反义词: firm, hard, lumpy | 词性: 形容词

custardy:  Ultimately from Latin "crustum" (crust, pastry) via Old French "crouste" then Anglo-Norman "custard".  The original meaning referred to a pie crust, and later evolved to refer to the egg and milk mixture used in such pies. The adjectival form "custardy" describes something resembling this mixture. | 词源
