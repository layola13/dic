flavin:/ˈfleɪvɪn/ | n. | 核黄素; 黄素 | Any of a class of organic compounds that are based on the heterocyclic three-ring system isoalloxazine, and occur widely in nature as coenzymes of flavoproteins. | Riboflavin, also known as vitamin B2, is a flavin. 核黄素，也称为维生素B2，是一种黄素。| synonym: riboflavin | antonym: None | noun

flavine:/ˈfleɪviːn/ | adj. | 黄素的 | Of, relating to, or containing flavin. | Flavine nucleotides are essential for biological oxidation. 黄素核苷酸对生物氧化至关重要。| synonym: riboflavin-related | antonym: None | adjective
