dinked:/dɪŋkt/
adjective
已婚的，双职工，没有孩子的 (Referring to a couple who are both employed and have no children.)
A couple is considered "dinked" if they are double income, no kids.  如果一对夫妇是双职工，没有孩子，就被认为是“dinked”。
They're a dinked couple, so they have a lot of disposable income. 他们是丁克夫妻，所以有很多可支配收入。
Synonyms: child-free, childless
Antonyms: parents, family with children
词性: 形容词
