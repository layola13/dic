fadedness:/ˈfeɪdɪdnəs/| n.|褪色；衰退；暗淡|The fadedness of the photograph made it difficult to identify the people in it.  这张照片褪色严重，很难辨认出上面的人。|The fadedness of her memory worried her family. 她记忆力的衰退让她的家人很担心。|Synonyms: paleness, dullness, faintness |Antonyms: brightness, vividness, intensity|noun

fade:/feɪd/| v.|褪色；衰退；逐渐消失|The colors in the curtains faded over time. 窗帘的颜色随着时间的推移而褪色。|His enthusiasm for the project faded as he encountered more and more obstacles.  随着遇到越来越多的障碍，他对这个项目的热情逐渐消退。|Synonyms: diminish, disappear, wane |Antonyms: brighten, intensify, strengthen|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From fade + -ness.  "Fade" comes from the Old French word "fader," meaning "to grow weak or faint."
