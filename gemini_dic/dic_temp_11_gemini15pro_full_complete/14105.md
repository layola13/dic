authentically:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkli/
adverb
真正地；地道地；逼真地
in a genuine and true way
She spoke authentically about her experiences. 她真实地讲述了自己的经历。
genuinely, truly, really, legitimately
falsely, artificially, deceptively
词性: 副词

authentic:/ɔːˈθɛntɪk/
adjective
真的；真正的；可靠的
of undisputed origin and not a copy; genuine
Is this an authentic Ming vase? 这是真的明朝花瓶吗？
genuine, real, true, bona fide
fake, counterfeit, spurious, bogus
词性: 形容词

authenticate:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪt/
verb
证明…是真实的；证实；验证
prove or show something to be true, genuine, or valid
Experts authenticated the painting. 专家们证实了这幅画的真实性。
verify, validate, confirm, certify
disprove, invalidate, deny
词性: 动词

authenticity:/ɔːˌθɛnˈtɪsɪti/
noun
真实性；可靠性
the quality of being authentic
The authenticity of the document is beyond doubt.  这份文件的真实性不容置疑。
genuineness, validity, credibility, veracity
falseness, artificiality, spuriousness
词性: 名词


词源:
From Late Latin authenticus "authoritative," from Greek authentikos "original, genuine, first-hand," from authentes "one acting on one's own authority," from autos "self" + hentes "doer, being."
