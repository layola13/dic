mishears:/ˌmɪsˈhɪrz/| v.|听错|To hear something incorrectly, often misunderstanding what was said.|例句：I misheard her and thought she said she was leaving. 我听错了她的话，以为她说她要走了。|例句：He often mishears instructions because he doesn't pay attention. 他经常听错指示，因为他没有注意。|近义词：misinterpret, misunderstand|反义词：hear correctly, understand|词性：动词
