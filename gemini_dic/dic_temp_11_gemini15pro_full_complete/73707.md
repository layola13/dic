forgone:/fɔːrˈɡɒn/ /fɔːrˈɡɔːn/
adjective
放弃的；过去的；不可避免的
something that is forgone is certain to happen
例句：
A forgone conclusion.  一个必然的结论。
His election is a forgone conclusion. 他当选已成定局。

近义词：past, bygone, lost, irrecoverable, inevitable, unavoidable, certain
反义词： retrievable, avoidable
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From past participle of forgo.
