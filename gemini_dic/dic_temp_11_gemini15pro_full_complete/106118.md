knightliness:/ˈnaɪtlɪnɪs/
n.|骑士精神；侠义；豪侠|The quality of being knightly; chivalry. |He displayed great knightliness in defending the weak. 他在保护弱者方面展现了伟大的骑士精神。|近义词：chivalry, gallantry, valor, courage, nobility|反义词：cowardice, baseness, meanness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *knightlinesse*, from knight +‎ -liness.  Ultimately from Old English *cniht* (boy, servant)
