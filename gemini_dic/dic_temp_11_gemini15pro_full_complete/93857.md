immobilizer:/ɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzər/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbɪˌlaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|固定器；防盗装置|A device that prevents something from moving or operating, often used in vehicles to prevent theft. |例句：The car's immobilizer prevented the thief from starting the engine. 汽车的防盗装置使小偷无法启动引擎。|近义词：lock, restraint, brake|反义词：activator, starter|noun

immobilizer:/ɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzər/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbɪˌlaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|固定器；防盗装置|A device, often electronic, that prevents a vehicle engine from starting without the correct key or code, thus deterring theft. |例句：Modern cars are equipped with engine immobilizers as standard. 现代汽车标配发动机防盗装置。|近义词：anti-theft device, security system|反义词：key, starter|noun
