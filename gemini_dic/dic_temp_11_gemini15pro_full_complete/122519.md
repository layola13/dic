mocks:/mɑːks/ (US), /mɒks/ (UK)| n. v.|嘲弄，模仿；仿制品|Mocks are imitations, often done with the intent to ridicule or satirize.  As a verb, it means to tease or imitate someone in a disrespectful way.|He made mocks of all her suggestions. 他嘲笑了她所有的建议.|近义词：imitates, mimics, satirizes, ridicules, teases, jeers, scoffs|反义词:praises, respects, admires|词性: noun (plural), verb (third person singular present)
