drowsiness:/ˈdraʊzinəs/| n. |困倦，睡意|A feeling of tiredness and sleepiness.|例句：The drowsiness caused by the medication made it difficult to drive.  这种药物引起的困倦使驾驶变得困难。

例句：She struggled to fight off the drowsiness after a long flight.  长途飞行后，她努力抵抗睡意。|近义词：sleepiness, somnolence, lethargy|反义词：wakefulness, alertness|词性:名词

drowsy:/ˈdraʊzi/| adj. |困倦的，昏昏欲睡的|Sleepy and lethargic.|例句：The warm room made me feel drowsy. 温暖的房间让我感到昏昏欲睡。

例句：He was drowsy after taking the allergy medication.  服用抗过敏药物后，他昏昏欲睡。|近义词：sleepy, lethargic, somnolent|反义词：alert, awake, wakeful|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drowsyhed, equivalent to drowsy +‎ -head.  Drowsy itself comes from Old English drūsian (“to be sluggish, troubled, sorrowful”), from Proto-Germanic *drūsijaną (“to sink, droop, be troubled”).
