floating:/ˈfloʊtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfləʊtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|漂浮的，浮动的；流动的，不固定的；浮动汇率|Something that is floating rests on the surface of a liquid and does not sink. |The boat was floating gently down the river.  这条船缓缓地漂流而下。|近义词：drifting, buoyant|反义词：sinking, submerged|adjective

floating:/ˈfloʊtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfləʊtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|漂浮；浮动；发行股票|The act of floating on the surface of a liquid.|The floating of the company on the stock market was a success.  公司股票上市很成功。|近义词：drifting|反义词：sinking|noun
