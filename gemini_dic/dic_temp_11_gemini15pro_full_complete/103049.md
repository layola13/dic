jellying:/ˈdʒɛliɪŋ/
v.|（使）胶凝，（使）成果冻状|The chef is jellying the stock for the aspic. 厨师正在将高汤做成果冻状，用于制作肉冻。|近义词：congealing, setting, solidifying|反义词:melting, liquefying|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From jelly + -ing.  "Jelly" comes from Old French "gelee," meaning "frost, jelly," ultimately from Latin "gelare," meaning "to freeze."
