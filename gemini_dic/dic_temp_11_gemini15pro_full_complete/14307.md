automatable:/ˌɔːtəˈmeɪtəbl/
adjective
可自动化的
Capable of being automated; able to be done by a machine or computer program.
Many factory jobs are now automatable, leading to concerns about unemployment. 许多工厂的工作现在都可以自动化，导致人们对失业的担忧。
Automated tasks can improve efficiency and reduce human error. 自动化任务可以提高效率并减少人为错误。
近义词: automatic, mechanized, robotic
反义词: manual, labor-intensive
词性: adjective
词源: From automate + -able.  Automate comes from automatic, which ultimately derives from the Greek automatos, meaning "self-acting".
