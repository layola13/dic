ordained:/ɔːrˈdeɪnd/ | v. |任命；命令；注定|to officially make someone a priest or other religious leader; (of God or fate) to decide that something will happen |例句：He was ordained as a priest in 1985. 他于1985年被任命为牧师。

例句：Fate had ordained that they would meet again. 命运注定他们会再次相遇。|近义词：appointed, decreed, destined, predestined |反义词：dismissed, discharged|词性:动词

ordained:/ɔːrˈdeɪnd/ | adj. |受戒的；被任命的|having been officially made a priest or religious leader |例句：an ordained minister 受戒牧师 |例句：She felt ordained to this kind of work. 她觉得命中注定要做这种工作。|近义词：appointed, designated |反义词：unordained, secular|词性:形容词
