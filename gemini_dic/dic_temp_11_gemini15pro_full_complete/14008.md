aurist:/ˈɔːrɪst/
noun
耳科医生;耳病专家
A specialist in diseases of the ear.
An aurist can help with hearing loss.  耳科医生可以帮助治疗听力损失。
The aurist diagnosed an ear infection. 耳科医生诊断出耳朵感染。
Synonyms: otologist, ear doctor, hearing specialist
Antonyms: N/A
