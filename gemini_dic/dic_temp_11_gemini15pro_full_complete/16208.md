bannerless:/ˈbænərlɪs/
adjective
无旗帜的; 没有横幅的
lacking a banner or banners; without banners.
The protest was bannerless, but the participants' voices were loud and clear.  抗议活动没有旗帜，但参与者的声音响亮而清晰。
The troops marched bannerless through the streets. 部队没有旗帜地穿过街道。
synonym:unflagged, standardless
antonym:bannered, flagged
词性:形容词
