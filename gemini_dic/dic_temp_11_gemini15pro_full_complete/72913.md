fomo:/ˈfoʊmoʊ/| n. |害怕错过 (Fear Of Missing Out)|The fear of missing out, especially on social media, can lead to anxiety and compulsive checking of updates.|例句：She felt the fomo when she saw all her friends' vacation pictures on Instagram. 她看到所有朋友在Instagram上晒的度假照片，感到很害怕错过。

例句：He didn't want to experience fomo, so he went to the party even though he was tired. 他不想错过，所以即使很累也去了派对。|近义词：anxiety, unease|反义词:contentment, satisfaction|词性:noun (abbreviation)
