minimizing: /ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使最小化；将…减到最少 | reducing something to the smallest possible amount or degree. |  例句：The company is minimizing its environmental impact. 公司正在将其对环境的影响降到最低。

| 近义词：reducing, decreasing, lessening | 反义词：maximizing, increasing, enlarging | 词性：动词 -ing 形式 (present participle)

minimizing: /ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 最小化的 | having the characteristic of reducing something to the smallest possible amount or degree. | 例句：They are taking a minimizing approach to risk. 他们正在采取一种将风险最小化的策略。| 近义词：reductive, diminutive | 反义词：maximizing, expansive | 词性：形容词
