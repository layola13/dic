non-member:/ˌnɑːnˈmɛmbər/ |n.| 非会员；非成员 |A person who does not belong to a particular group or organization.|  Example: Non-members are not allowed to use the club's facilities. 非会员不得使用俱乐部的设施。| Example: The discount is only available to members, non-members must pay full price.  这个折扣只对会员有效，非会员必须支付全价。 |Synonyms: outsider, non-affiliate |Antonyms: member, insider| noun
