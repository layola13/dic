ethmoturbinal: /ˌɛθmoʊˈtɜːrbɪnəl/ | n. | 鼻甲骨 | One of the thin, scroll-shaped bones in the nasal cavity, covered with mucous membrane, that warm and moisten inhaled air. | The ethmoturbinals are delicate structures easily damaged during surgery. 鼻甲骨是手术过程中容易受损的精细结构。| The cat's ethmoturbinals are highly developed, contributing to their keen sense of smell. 猫的鼻甲骨高度发达，这有助于它们的敏锐嗅觉。 | Synonyms: turbinate, nasal concha | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
