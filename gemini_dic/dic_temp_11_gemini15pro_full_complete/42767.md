countrymen:/ˈkʌntrimən/ | n. | 乡下人，农村人 | A man who lives in the country, especially one engaged in agriculture. (Less common usage) | The countrymen were gathering for the annual harvest festival. 乡下人正在聚集参加一年一度的丰收节。| synonyms: rustic, peasant | antonyms: city dweller, urbanite | noun
