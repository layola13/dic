nonpuerilely:/ˌnɑːnpjuˈɪrɪlli/
adverb
不幼稚地，成熟地
In a manner that is not childish or immature; seriously.
She addressed the complex issue nonpuerilely, demonstrating a maturity beyond her years. 她以不幼稚的方式处理了这个复杂的问题，展现出超越她年龄的成熟。
He nonpuerilely explained the situation to his younger brother. 他成熟地向弟弟解释了情况。

近义词: maturely, seriously, soberly, thoughtfully
反义词: childishly, immaturely, puerilely, foolishly
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix): not
puerile (adjective): childish, immature
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
