mortuaries:/ˈmɔːrtʃʊɛriz/ | n. | 停尸房，太平间 | A place where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation. | The body was taken to the mortuary. 尸体被送往停尸房。| synonyms: morgue, funeral home | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider places associated with life like "hospital" or "birthing center" in a contrasting context) | noun

mortuary:/ˈmɔːrtʃʊɛri/ | adj. | 死亡的，丧葬的 | Relating to death or burial. |  Mortuary services were provided by the local funeral home. 当地的殡仪馆提供丧葬服务。| synonyms:  funereal, death, burial | antonyms:  natal, birth, living | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *mortuarie*, from Anglo-Norman *mortuarie*, Old French *mortuaire* "mortuary, gift to a parish priest on the death of a parishioner,"  from Medieval Latin *mortuarium* "a gift left at death to a church," noun use of neuter of *mortuarius* "pertaining to the dead," from Latin *mors* (genitive *mortis*) "death."
