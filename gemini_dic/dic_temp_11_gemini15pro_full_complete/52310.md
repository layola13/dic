dickered:/ˈdɪkərd/
verb
讨价还价；(尤指为小事)争论
engaged in petty argument or haggling, typically over a price.
They dickered over the price of the used car for hours. 他们为了这辆二手车的价格讨价还价了几个小时。
近义词：bartered, haggled, negotiated
反义词：agreed, conceded

词性:v.

dicker:/ˈdɪkər/
noun
讨价还价；交易
an instance of petty argument or haggling, typically over a price.
After much dicker, they finally agreed on a price. 经过一番讨价还价，他们最终达成了价格协议。
近义词：bargain, deal, negotiation
反义词：agreement, concession

词性:n.


词源: From Middle Dutch *dīkeren, frequentative of *dīken (“to poke, prod, point”). Related to Dutch dikken (“to dam, dike”).
