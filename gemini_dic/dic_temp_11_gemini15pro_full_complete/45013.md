csharp: /ˈsiː ʃɑːrp/ | n. | C# (programming language) |  A modern, object-oriented, and type-safe programming language developed by Microsoft. C#是一种流行的用于Unity游戏开发的语言。 | Synonym:  .NET language, C-sharp | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a programming language) | Noun
