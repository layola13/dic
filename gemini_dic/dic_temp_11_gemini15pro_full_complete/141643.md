optimism: /ˈɒptɪmɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmɪzəm/ (US) | n. |乐观，乐观主义| Hopefulness and confidence about the future or the successful outcome of something. |例句：She maintained her optimism despite the difficult circumstances.  她尽管处境艰难，但仍然保持乐观。

例句：His optimism is contagious. 他的乐观情绪很有感染力。

近义词：hopefulness, positivity, sanguinity |反义词: pessimism, negativity, despair |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *optimisme*, from Latin *optimus* "best". Coined by Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz.
