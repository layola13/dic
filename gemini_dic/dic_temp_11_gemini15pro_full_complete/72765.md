fogie:/ˈfoʊɡi/ | n. | 老古董，守旧的人 | An old-fashioned or conservative person, often seen as resistant to new ideas or change. |  Example 1: He's such a fogie; he still thinks women shouldn't work. 他真是个老古董，他仍然认为女性不应该工作。

Example 2:  My grandfather is a bit of a fogie when it comes to technology. 我祖父在科技方面有点守旧。 | 近义词:conservative, traditionalist, diehard, old fogey, fuddy-duddy | 反义词:modernist, progressive, radical | 词性:名词
