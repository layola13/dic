diathermanism:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmənɪzəm/
noun
透热性
The property of transmitting radiant heat; transparency to heat rays.
Rock salt exhibits high diathermanism, allowing infrared radiation to pass through. 岩盐表现出很高的透热性，允许红外辐射穿过。
Synonyms: transcalency, thermal transmittance
Antonyms: athermancy, opacity to heat
