evolute:/ˈiːvəluːt/ | n. | 渐屈线; 进化物; 展开线 |A curve formed by the centers of curvature of another curve.  The locus of the centers of curvature of a given curve.  Something evolved.| The evolute of a circle is a single point at its center. 圆的渐屈线是其中心的一个点。| near meaning:  result, outcome, product, development | opposite meaning: involute, ancestor, origin | noun


evolute:/ˈiːvəluːt/ | adj. | 展开的;  | Unrolled or unfolded. Developed, evolved. |  The fern's evolute leaves unfurled in the spring sunshine.  蕨类植物展开的叶子在春日阳光下舒展开来。| near meaning: unfolded, unfurled, developed, expanded | opposite meaning:  involute, furled, undeveloped, contracted | adjective
