intangibleness:/ɪnˈtændʒɪbəlnəs/
n.
无形性；难以捉摸；不可捉摸性
The intangibleness of the concept made it difficult to grasp.  这个概念的无形性使其难以理解。
The intangibleness of her fear made it hard to address. 她恐惧的无形性使得难以应对。
intangibility, impalpability, immateriality
concreteness, tangibility, palpability
noun
