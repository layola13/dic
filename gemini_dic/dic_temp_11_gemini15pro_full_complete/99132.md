intercepters:/ˌɪntərˈseptərz/ | n. |拦截器；截击者；窃听者|People or things that intercept something, especially communications or missiles.|The intercepters shot down the incoming missile. 拦截器击落了来袭的导弹。|Synonyms: interceptors, blockers, catchers|Antonyms: transmitters, senders|Noun

intercept:/ˌɪntərˈsept/ | v. |拦截；截击；截获|To stop or interrupt the course of something, especially communications or missiles.|The police intercepted a shipment of drugs. 警方截获了一批毒品。|Synonyms: catch, seize, block|Antonyms: transmit, send, release|Verb
