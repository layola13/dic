ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin encyclopaedia, from Ancient Greek ἐγκύκλιος παιδεία (enkýklios paideía, “general education”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + κύκλος (kýklos, “circle”) + παιδεία (paideía, “education, rearing of a child”).  Originally referred to the circle of arts and sciences considered essential for a liberal education.
