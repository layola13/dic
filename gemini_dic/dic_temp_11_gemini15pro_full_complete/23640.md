brand-name:/ˈbrænd neɪm/ |n.|品牌名称；商标名称|A brand name is a name, term, design, symbol, or other feature that distinguishes one seller's product from those of others.  A brand name may identify one item, a family of items, or all items of that seller. If used for the firm as a whole, the preferred term is trade name.|Example:  The brand name of this coffee is very famous.  这个咖啡的品牌名称非常有名。
Example:  Choosing the right brand name is crucial for a new product. 为新产品选择合适的品牌名称至关重要。|Synonym: trademark, brand, label|Antonym: generic, non-branded|noun
