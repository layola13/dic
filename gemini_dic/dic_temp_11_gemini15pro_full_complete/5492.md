alopecia:/ˌæləˈpiːʃə/ /ˌæləˈpiːʃiə/ | n. |脱发，秃头症|The partial or complete absence of hair from areas of the body where it normally grows.|例句：The medication caused alopecia as a side effect. 这种药物的副作用会导致脱发。

例句：She suffers from alopecia areata, which causes patchy hair loss. 她患有斑秃，会导致局部脱发。|近义词：baldness, hair loss|反义词: hair growth|词性: noun
