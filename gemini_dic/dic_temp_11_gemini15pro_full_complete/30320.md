censoriousness:/ˌsensɔːriəsnəs/
n.
苛刻，吹毛求疵
The quality of being overly critical and fault-finding.
Her censoriousness made it difficult for anyone to feel comfortable around her. 她吹毛求疵的性格让周围的人感到很不自在。
criticalness, fault-finding, carping, hypercriticism
tolerance, leniency, forbearance, indulgence
noun
