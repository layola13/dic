hexagon:/ˈheksəɡɑːn/ (US), /ˈheksəɡən/ (UK)| n.|六边形；六角形|A plane figure with six straight sides and angles.|A bee's honeycomb is made up of hexagons. 蜜蜂的蜂巢是由六边形组成的。|The board game is played on a hexagon.  这个棋盘游戏是在一个六边形上玩的。|synonyms: polygon, six-sided figure|antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hexagonum*, from Ancient Greek ἑξάγωνον (hexágōnon, “hexagon, six-cornered”), from ἕξ (héx, “six”) + γωνία (gōnía, “angle, corner”).
