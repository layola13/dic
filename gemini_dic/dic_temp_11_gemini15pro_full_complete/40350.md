contractibly:/kənˈtræktɪbli/
adjective
可收缩地
capable of being contracted or shortened
例句：The muscle is contractibly active. 这块肌肉具有收缩活性。

例句：The material is contractibly responsive to temperature changes.  这种材料对温度变化具有收缩反应。

近义词：reducibly, shrinkably
反义词：expandably, extensibly
词性：adjective

词源:
From contract + -ible.  "Contract" comes from the Latin "contractus," the past participle of "contrahere," meaning "to draw together, to reduce."  "-ible" is a suffix meaning "capable of being."
