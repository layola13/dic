affidavit:/ˌæfɪˈdeɪvɪt/
n.
宣誓书， affidavit
a written statement confirmed by oath or affirmation, for use as evidence in court.
例句：The witness signed an affidavit confirming what she had seen.  证人签署了一份宣誓书，确认她所看到的情况。

例句：He submitted several affidavits to support his claim. 他提交了几份宣誓书来支持他的主张。

近义词：declaration, oath, testimony, affirmation
反义词: None (An affidavit is a legal document; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
