bulbil:/ˈbʌlbɪl/ | n. | 小鳞茎；珠芽；零余子 |A small bulb-like structure produced in the leaf axils or on the flower stalks of some plants, capable of developing into a new plant. |  Example 1: The tiger lily produces bulbils in its leaf axils. 虎皮百合在其叶腋处会长出珠芽。

Example 2:  Propagating lilies from bulbils is an easy way to increase your stock.  用珠芽繁殖百合是增加你库存的一种简单方法。 | Synonym: bulblet, offset | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider seed as a contrasting method of reproduction) | Noun
