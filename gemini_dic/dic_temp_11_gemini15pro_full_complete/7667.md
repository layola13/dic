anodize:/ˈænədaɪz/ (US) /ˈænədaɪz/ (UK)| v.| 阳极氧化，阳极处理|To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process.| The company anodizes aluminum parts for various industries.  这家公司为各行业阳极氧化铝零件。|Synonyms: oxidize, coat|Antonyms: strip, uncoat|verb
