isonicotinic:/ˌaɪsoʊˌnɪkəˈtɪnɪk/
adjective
异烟酸的;
Relating to or derived from isonicotinic acid.  Specifically, referring to the isomer of nicotinic acid in which the carboxyl group is at position 4 of the pyridine ring.
Isoniazid is a derivative of isonicotinic acid hydrazide and is used to treat tuberculosis.  异烟肼是异烟酸酰肼的衍生物，用于治疗结核病。
Isoniazid is an isonicotinic acid hydrazide derivative. 异烟肼是一种异烟酸酰肼衍生物。

近义词: pyridinecarboxylic (less specific)
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it refers to a specific chemical structure.)
词性: adjective
