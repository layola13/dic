aphis: /ˈeɪfɪs/ (US), /ˈeɪfɪd/ (UK)| n. |蚜虫|A small, soft-bodied insect that sucks the sap from plants. |例句：Aphids can cause significant damage to crops. 蚜虫会对农作物造成重大损害。

|例句：The roses are covered in aphids. 玫瑰花上爬满了蚜虫。
|近义词：greenfly, blackfly, plant louse|反义词: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *aphis, from Greek ἀφίς (aphis, “louse”).
