bts:/ˌbiːˌtiːˈɛs/| n. |防弹少年团|A South Korean boy band that debuted in 2013.  The acronym stands for "Bangtan Sonyeondan" which literally translates to "Bulletproof Boy Scouts". They are known for their music, performances, and social media presence.|例句：BTS has a huge global fanbase. 防弹少年团拥有庞大的全球粉丝群。|例句：Their music often addresses social issues and mental health. 他们的音乐经常涉及社会问题和心理健康。|近义词：K-pop group, boy band|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "girl group" as a contrasting term)|词性:noun
