antipasto:/ˌæntiˈpɑːstoʊ/ (US), /ˌæntiˈpæstoʊ/ (UK)| n. |开胃菜|An appetizer typically consisting of an assortment of meats, cheeses, olives, and other small savory items, often served before the main course of a meal.  It's Italian in origin.|例句：We started our meal with a delicious antipasto platter. 我们以美味的开胃菜拼盘开始用餐。

|例句：The antipasto included cured meats, olives, and marinated artichoke hearts.  开胃菜包括腌肉、橄榄和腌制的朝鲜蓟心。|近义词：appetizer, starter, hors d'oeuvre|反义词:dessert, main course|Noun
