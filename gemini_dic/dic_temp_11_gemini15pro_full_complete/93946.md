immunosuppressed:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛst/| adj.|免疫抑制的，免疫力受损的|Having a weakened immune system, typically as a result of disease or medication.|Patients who are immunosuppressed are more vulnerable to infections.|近义词:immunocompromised, immunodeficient|反义词:immunocompetent|词性:形容词
Etymology: immuno- (prefix meaning "immune") + suppressed (past participle of suppress, meaning "to reduce the activity or strength of something")
