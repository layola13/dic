nimby:/ˈnɪmbi/| n. adj.|邻避症候群；邻避主义者|Not In My Back Yard (NIMBY) is an acronym for "Not In My Back Yard." It describes the phenomenon where people support a project or development in principle, but oppose it being located near them.  It often refers to things like landfills, power plants, or homeless shelters.|The residents displayed classic NIMBYism when they protested the new prison, despite acknowledging the need for more prison space. 居民们抗议新建监狱，尽管他们承认需要更多的监狱空间，这展现了典型的邻避症候群。|Synonyms: local opposition, parochialism|Antonyms: welcoming, receptive|noun, adjective
