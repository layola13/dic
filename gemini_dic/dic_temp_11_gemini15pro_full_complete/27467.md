campaigners:/kæmˈpeɪnərz/
noun
活动家；倡导者；运动参与者
People who actively participate in a campaign to achieve a particular social or political goal.
Environmental campaigners are protesting against the new oil pipeline.  环保活动家正在抗议新的输油管道。
Campaigners for animal rights are trying to close down the fur farms.  动物权利倡导者正试图关闭皮草养殖场。
synonyms: activists, advocates, supporters, promoters
antonyms: opponents, adversaries, detractors
n.
