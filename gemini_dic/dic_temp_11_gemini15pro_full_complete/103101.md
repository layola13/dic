jeremiads: /ˌdʒɛrɪˈmaɪædz/ | n. | 悲叹，哀歌 |  A long, mournful complaint or lamentation; a list of woes.  Derived from the Lamentations of Jeremiah in the Bible, a book expressing grief over the destruction of Jerusalem. | The politician's speech was filled with jeremiads about the state of the economy.  这位政治家的演讲充满了对经济状况的悲叹。 | synonyms: lamentations, complaints, moans, wailing,  | antonyms: celebrations, rejoicings, praises | noun
