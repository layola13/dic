disappoints:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnts/ | v. | 使失望 | To fail to fulfill the hopes or expectations of (someone). | The movie disappointed me; it wasn't as good as the reviews said.  这部电影让我很失望；它不像评论说的那么好。 | sadden, frustrate, let down | please, satisfy | verb

disappointment:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntmənt/ | n. | 失望 | The feeling of sadness or displeasure caused by the non-fulfillment of one's hopes or expectations. |  The cancellation of the concert was a huge disappointment. 音乐会取消了，真是太令人失望了。| letdown, frustration, dissatisfaction | satisfaction, contentment | noun
