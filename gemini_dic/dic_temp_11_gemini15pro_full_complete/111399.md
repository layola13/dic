livingness:/ˈlɪvɪŋnɪs/| n.|活力; 生动性; 生活气息|The quality or state of being alive or lively; vibrancy.|The painting captured the livingness of the bustling market. 这幅画捕捉到了熙熙攘攘的市场的活力。|近义词：liveliness, vitality, vibrancy, animation|反义词：dullness, lifelessness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From living + -ness.  "living" traces back to Old English "libban," meaning "to live."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
