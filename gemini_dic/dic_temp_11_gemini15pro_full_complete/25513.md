bullish:/ˈbʊlɪʃ/| adj.| 看涨的；乐观向上的；充满信心的|Feeling or showing confidence that something will be successful, especially that share prices will rise.|例句：Investors are bullish about the company's future. 投资者对公司的前景感到乐观。

例句：The market is bullish on technology stocks. 市场看好科技股。|近义词：optimistic, confident, positive, hopeful|反义词:bearish, pessimistic, negative|词性:形容词
词源:From bull (n.) + -ish. Originally in reference to the way a bull attacks, by thrusting its horns upward.  后来被引申用于金融市场，指对市场上涨的预期。
