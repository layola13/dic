babingtonite:/bæˈbɪŋtəˌnaɪt/
n.
硅灰石
A triclinic mineral, Ca₂Fe²⁺Fe³⁺Si₅O₁₄(OH), occurring in dark, greenish black prismatic crystals.
Babingtonite is a relatively rare mineral found in cavities in volcanic rocks. 硅灰石是一种相对罕见的矿物，存在于火山岩的空洞中。
Babingtonite crystals can be quite beautiful and are sought after by collectors. 硅灰石晶体非常漂亮，受到收藏家的追捧。
