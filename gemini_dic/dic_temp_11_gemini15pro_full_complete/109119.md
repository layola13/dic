legibility:/ˌledʒɪˈbɪləti/
n.|清晰度，易读性|The quality of being clear enough to read. |The legibility of her handwriting is poor. 她的字迹很难辨认。| clarity, readability, decipherability |illegibility, unclarity, unreadability|noun

legibilities:/ˌledʒɪˈbɪlətiz/
n.|清晰度，易读性（复数）|The qualities of being clear enough to read, often referring to multiple instances or aspects of readability. |The legibilities of the different fonts varied greatly.  不同字体的清晰度差异很大。| clarities, readabilities, decipherabilities| illegibilities, unclarities, unreadabilities|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *legibilitas, from Latin legibilis "that can be read," from legere "to read."
