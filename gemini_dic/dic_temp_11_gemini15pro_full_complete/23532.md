brained:/breɪndd/ | adj. | ...脑的；有...般头脑的；| Having a brain of a specified kind (used in combination). |例句：He's a small-brained man. 他是个头脑简单的人。

例句：She is known for being a quick-brained strategist. 她以思维敏捷的战略家而闻名。 | 近义词：intelligent, clever, smart, bright | 反义词：stupid, foolish, unintelligent, dull | 词性: adjective

词源:  From brain + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives from nouns, indicating possession of the quality denoted by the noun.
