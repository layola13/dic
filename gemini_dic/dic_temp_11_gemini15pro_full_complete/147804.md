overprovide:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvaɪd/| v. |过度提供，供应过剩|To supply more of something than is necessary or required.|The government overprovided subsidized housing, leading to a surplus of empty apartments. 政府过度提供了补贴住房，导致大量空置公寓。|近义词：oversupply, glut|反义词: underprovide, undersupply|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + provide. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much," or "above." "Provide" comes from the Latin word "providēre," meaning "to foresee, take care of, prepare."
