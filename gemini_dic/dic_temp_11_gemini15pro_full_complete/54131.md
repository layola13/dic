disendow:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdaʊ/
verb
剥夺…的基金；停止对…的捐赠
To deprive of endowment; to take away the funds, revenues, or property by which an institution, such as a college or church, is supported.
The college was disendowed during the economic downturn.  这所大学在经济衰退期间被剥夺了基金。
The king disendowed the monastery. 国王取消了对修道院的捐赠。
synonyms: divest, deprive, strip
antonyms: endow, fund, support
词性: 动词
