non-proficiency:/ˌnɒn prəˈfɪʃənsi/ /ˌnɑːn prəˈfɪʃənsi/| n. |不熟练，缺乏技能|Lack of skill or expertise in a particular area or activity.  The state of not being proficient.|His non-proficiency in French made it difficult for him to communicate in Paris. 他法语不熟练，这让他在巴黎难以沟通。|lack of skill, incompetence, inability, inadequacy|proficiency, skill, expertise, mastery|noun
