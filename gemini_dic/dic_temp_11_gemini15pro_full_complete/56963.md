double-action:/ˈdʌbəl ˈækʃən/| adj. n.|双动的，双重行动|Having two actions or functions; (of a firearm) requiring only a single pull of the trigger to both cock and fire the mechanism.|This revolver is double-action, meaning you don't have to cock the hammer manually. 这把左轮手枪是双动的，意味着你不需要手动扳动击锤。|近义词：dual-action|反义词:single-action|词性:形容词, 名词

double-action:/ˈdʌbəl ˈækʃən/| n.|双动机制|The mechanism of a double-action firearm.|The double-action of this pistol makes it quick to fire. 这把手枪的双动机制使其能够快速射击。|近义词：dual-action mechanism|反义词:single-action mechanism|词性:名词
