denitrify:/diːˈnaɪtrɪfaɪ/ /dɪˈnaɪtrɪfaɪ/| v. |脱氮，反硝化|To convert nitrates in (soil, air, or water) into nitrogen gas, especially by the action of bacteria.|Farmers are encouraged to use practices that denitrify nitrogen fertilizer before it reaches streams. 鼓励农民采用能够在氮肥到达河流之前使其反硝化的做法。|
The bacteria in the soil denitrify the nitrates into nitrogen gas. 土壤中的细菌将硝酸盐反硝化成氮气。|
Synonyms: reduce, convert, break down|Antonyms: nitrify|verb
