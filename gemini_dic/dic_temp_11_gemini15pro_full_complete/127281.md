navicular: /nəˈvɪkjʊlər/ | adj. n. |舟状的，舟骨 |Shaped like a boat; relating to the navicular bone (a boat-shaped bone in the wrist and ankle). | The navicular bone in her wrist was fractured. 她的腕舟骨骨折了。| The doctor pointed out the navicular area on the X-ray. 医生指出了X光片上的舟状骨区域。 | 近义词: boat-shaped, scaphoid (for the wrist bone) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could use descriptions like "irregularly shaped") | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Latin *navicula* ("small boat") + *-aris* (forming adjectives of relation). *Navicula* is a diminutive of *navis* ("ship").
