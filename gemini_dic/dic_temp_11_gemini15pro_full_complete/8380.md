anti-reducing:/ˌænti rɪˈdjuːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 抗还原的，防止还原的 | Preventing or inhibiting reduction (a chemical reaction involving the gain of electrons).  It describes a substance or agent that prevents another substance from being reduced. |  The anti-reducing agent protected the silver from tarnishing.  这种抗还原剂保护了银免受失去光泽。| synonyms: antioxidant, reducing inhibitor | antonyms: reducing, reductant | adjective

anti-reducing:/ˌænti rɪˈdjuːsɪŋ/ | n. | 抗还原剂 | A substance that prevents or inhibits reduction. | The chemist added an anti-reducing to the solution. 化学家在溶液中加入了一种抗还原剂。 | synonyms: antioxidant, reducing inhibitor | antonyms: reducing agent, reductant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite of"
reducing (present participle of reduce): from Latin reducere "to lead back," from re- "back" + ducere "to lead"
