naturists:/ˈneɪtʃərɪsts/
n.
自然主义者；裸体主义者
People who believe in naturism and often practice nudism.  Those who advocate or practice living in harmony with nature.
例句：The naturists enjoyed sunbathing on the secluded beach.  自然主义者们在僻静的海滩上享受日光浴。

例句：Some naturists believe that clothing is unnatural and restrictive. 一些自然主义者认为穿衣服是不自然的，而且会限制人。

近义词：nudists, naturalists
反义词：
词性：noun
