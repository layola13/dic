burghmote:/ˈbɜːrɡmoʊt/
n.
市民大会，市政会议
A town meeting in a borough, especially in Anglo-Saxon England.
The burghmote assembled to discuss the new trade regulations. 市民大会聚集在一起讨论新的贸易法规。
The annual burghmote was a key event in the town's calendar. 年度市民大会是镇上日历中的一个关键事件。
Synonyms: town meeting, assembly, council
Antonyms: N/A
