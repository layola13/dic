adieux:/əˈdjuːz/ | n. |告别，辞别; 告别词 |A farewell remark.  She made her final adieux and left the stage. 她做了最后的告别，离开了舞台。 | 近义词: farewells, goodbyes | 反义词: greetings, welcomes | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *adieu*, plural *adieux*, from *à Dieu* "to God," literally "to God (I commend you)."
