chemung:/kəˈmʌŋ/
n.
Chemung River/Chemung County/Chemung period
希芒河/希芒县/希芒期
A river in New York state, also a county in New York state, and a geologic period.
The Chemung River flows through southern New York. 希芒河流经纽约州南部。
Chemung County is known for its wineries. 希芒县以其葡萄酒厂而闻名。
The Chemung period was part of the Devonian period. 希芒期是泥盆纪的一部分。
近义词: (For the river) waterway, stream, tributary; (for the county) region, district, area; (for the period) era, epoch, age
反义词: None (Proper nouns generally don't have antonyms)
词性: noun
