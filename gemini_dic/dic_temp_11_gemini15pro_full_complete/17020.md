batcher:/ˈbætʃər/| n.|批处理程序，配料员，分批机|A person or machine that divides things into batches.  A batcher can also refer to a computer program that processes data in batches.|The batcher loaded the ingredients into the mixer.  配料员把原料装进搅拌机。|Synonyms: sorter, organizer, distributor, processor|Antonyms:  combiner, aggregator|noun


batcher:/ˈbætʃər/| n.|配料员|A person who prepares batches of ingredients, especially in cooking or industrial processes.|The batcher carefully measured the flour and sugar for the cake. 配料员仔细地称量蛋糕用的面粉和糖。|Synonyms: mixer, preparer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
