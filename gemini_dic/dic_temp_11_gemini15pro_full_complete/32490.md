chinglish:/ˈtʃɪŋɡlɪʃ/| n.|中式英语|A blend of Chinese and English vocabulary and grammar, typically used by Chinese speakers when attempting to communicate in English.  It often results in grammatically incorrect or nonsensical phrases from a native English speaker's perspective.|例句：His email was full of Chinglish, making it difficult to understand. 他的电子邮件充满了中式英语，很难理解。

例句：Using too much Chinglish can create confusion in cross-cultural communication. 使用过多的中式英语会在跨文化交流中造成混淆。|近义词：broken English, pidgin English|反义词:standard English|词性:noun
