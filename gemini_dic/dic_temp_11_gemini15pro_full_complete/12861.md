assort:/əˈsɔːrt/ /əˈsɔrt/| v. |分类，整理；协调，相配| To distribute into groups of a like kind; classify. To be in harmony or agreement; match or correspond.|  The librarian assorts the returned books by genre. 图书管理员按类型对归还的书籍进行分类。| synonyms: categorize, classify, arrange, organize, group, systematize, order, sort, separate, sift, grade| antonyms: mix, mingle, jumble, disorganize, scatter, confuse | verb


assortment:/əˈsɔːrtmənt/ /əˈsɔːrtmənt/| n. |各种各样；混合物| A collection of various kinds; a variety.| The store offers a wide assortment of candies. 这家商店提供各种各样的糖果。| synonyms: variety, selection, array, collection, mixture, mix, miscellany, medley, range, diversity| antonyms: uniformity, sameness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *assortir, from Vulgar Latin *assortire, frequentative of Latin assortire "to assign, allot," from ad- "to" + sors "lot, fate, share."
