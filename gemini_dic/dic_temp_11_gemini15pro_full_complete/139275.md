obliquing:/əˈbliːkɪŋ/
verb
倾斜，斜行，使倾斜
present participle of oblique
The sailboat was obliquing across the bay. 帆船斜穿过海湾。
近义词: slanting, inclining, sloping
反义词: straightening, leveling
词性: verb


oblique:/əˈbliːk/
adjective
斜的，倾斜的，间接的，不直截了当的
neither parallel nor at a right angle to a specified or implied line; slanting.
The sun's rays hit the Earth at an oblique angle.  太阳光线以倾斜的角度照射地球。
He gave her an oblique glance. 他斜了她一眼。
近义词: slanted, inclined, indirect
反义词: straight, direct
词性: adjective


obliqueness:/əˈbliːknəs/
noun
倾斜，斜度，间接
the state of being oblique.
The obliqueness of the roof made it difficult to walk on. 屋顶的倾斜度使其难以行走。
近义词: slant, inclination, indirectness
反义词: straightness, directness
词性: noun
