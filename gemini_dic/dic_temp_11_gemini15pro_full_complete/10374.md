appendant:/əˈpɛndənt/
adjective
附属的，附加的
belonging to something else, attached to something larger or more important
The small piece of land is appendant to the main estate. 这小块土地是附属主庄园的。
近义词: attached, annexed, subordinate, accessory
反义词: detached, separate, independent
词性: adjective

appendant:/əˈpɛndənt/
noun
附属物
something that is attached to something else, a secondary or subordinate part
The document was an appendant to the main contract.  该文件是主合同的附件。
近义词: addition, supplement, attachment, adjunct
反义词: main body, core
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *appendant, from Latin appendens, present participle of appendere (to hang on, attach).  Ultimately from ad- (to) + pendere (to hang).
