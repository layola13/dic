coexecutrix: /ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɛkjətrɪks/
coexecutrix | n. |女共同遗嘱执行人|A woman appointed jointly with one or more other persons to carry out the terms of a will.|Jane and Mary were named as coexecutrices of their father's will. 简和玛丽被任命为她们父亲遗嘱的共同执行人。|
coexecutrix | 近义词: joint executrix, co-executor (if gender-neutral) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "beneficiary") |词性: noun
coexecutrix | 词源: From co- + executrix.  "Executrix" comes from Late Middle English, from Latin *execūtrīx, feminine of execūtor (“executor”).|
