chibouque:/tʃɪˈbuːk/ | n. | 长管土耳其烟斗；水烟袋 | A long Turkish tobacco pipe with a long flexible tube. | He smoked a chibouque contemplatively. 他若有所思地抽着长管烟斗。 | 近义词：hookah, narghile, water pipe | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French *chiboque*, from Turkish *çibuk*, meaning "tube, pipe."
