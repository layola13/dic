coleader:/ˈkoʊˌliːdər/| n.|共同领导者，共同领袖|A person who shares the leadership of a group or organization with one or more other people.|例句：She and her colleague served as coleaders of the project. 她和她的同事担任该项目的共同负责人。|例句：The two coleaders presented a united front at the press conference.  两位共同领导人在新闻发布会上展现了团结一致的姿态。|近义词：co-leader, joint leader, shared leader|反义词:follower, subordinate|noun
