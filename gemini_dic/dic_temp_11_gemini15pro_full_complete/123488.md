monosymmetric:/ˌmɒnəʊsɪˈmetrɪk/ | adj. | 单对称的，单轴对称的 | Having only one plane of symmetry.  A monosymmetric section is any section that has only one axis of symmetry. | The monosymmetric I-beam is commonly used in steel construction. 单对称工字梁常用于钢结构。| The bridge design incorporates monosymmetric girders for increased stability. 桥梁设计采用单对称梁以提高稳定性。|  symmetrical, asymmetric, axisymmetric | nonsymmetrical, asymmetric | adjective
