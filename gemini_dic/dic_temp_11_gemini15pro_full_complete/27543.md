can-do-ism: /ˈkæn duːɪzəm/ | n. | 积极进取的态度；乐观能干的精神 | A belief that one can succeed at anything one attempts; a spirit of optimism and resourcefulness. |  Example: Her can-do-ism inspired the whole team. 她的积极进取的精神鼓舞了整个团队。 | Example:  He approached the challenge with characteristic can-do-ism. 他以特有的乐观能干的精神迎接挑战。 | 近义词: optimism, positive attitude, initiative, resourcefulness | 反义词: pessimism, negativity, defeatism | 词性: noun

词源:  A combination of "can do" (verb phrase expressing ability and willingness) + "-ism" (suffix forming nouns denoting doctrines, beliefs, or practices).  From the phrase "can do," popularized in the mid-20th century, particularly in American English.
