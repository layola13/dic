dormancy:/ˈdɔːrmənsi/ /ˈdɔːr-/| n. |休眠；蛰伏；潜伏|A state of inactivity or rest, especially in animals or plants during winter or periods of drought.|例句：The seeds remained in dormancy until the spring rains arrived. 种子一直处于休眠状态，直到春雨到来。

例句：The volcano had been in dormancy for centuries before the recent eruption. 这座火山在最近一次喷发之前已经休眠了几个世纪。|近义词：inactivity, quiescence, latency |反义词：activity, growth, wakefulness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dormientia, from Latin dormient-, dormiens, present participle of dormire "to sleep."  Related to dormitory.
