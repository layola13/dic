all-of-heap:/ɔːl əv hiːp/ | adj. | 全堆的，全部堆积的 |  Relating to a data structure where all elements are stored in a heap, a specialized tree-based data structure. This term is primarily used in computer science, specifically in the context of memory management and garbage collection. |  The all-of-heap garbage collection strategy is efficient for large datasets. 全堆垃圾回收策略对于大型数据集很有效。 |  The all-of-heap data structure allows for efficient retrieval of the minimum or maximum element. 全堆数据结构允许高效地检索最小或最大元素。 | 近义词: heap-based | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词
