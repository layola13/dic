nashy:/ˈnæʃi/| adj.|辛辣的，刺激性的；时髦的，漂亮的|Having a sharp, pungent taste or smell; stylish, smart.|The curry was wonderfully nashy, just how I like it. 这咖喱真是辣得过瘾，正合我意。|近义词：pungent, spicy, stylish, chic|反义词:bland, mild, dull|词性:形容词

词源:
Likely a variant of "natty," meaning neat and stylish. The meaning related to taste developed later, perhaps influenced by the sharp sensation associated with stylishness or trendiness.
