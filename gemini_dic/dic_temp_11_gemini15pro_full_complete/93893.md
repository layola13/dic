Etymology:
From Late Middle English *immortalite, from Old French immortalité, from Late Latin immortalitās (“deathlessness”), from Latin immortālis (“immortal”) + -itās (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  Equivalent to immortal +‎ -ity.
