illuministic:/ɪˌluːmɪˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
启蒙主义的；光明会的
Relating to or characteristic of the Enlightenment, a philosophical movement of the 18th century that emphasized reason and individualism rather than tradition.  Also, relating to or characteristic of the Illuminati, a secretive and controversial group often associated with the Enlightenment.
The professor gave an illuministic interpretation of the French Revolution. 教授对法国大革命进行了启蒙主义的解读。
The novel explores the illuministic ideals of liberty and equality. 这部小说探讨了自由和平等的启蒙理念。
Synonyms: enlightened, rational, progressive
Antonyms: obscurantist, reactionary, traditional
词性: 形容词
