housecoat:/ˈhaʊskoʊt/ (US) /ˈhaʊskəʊt/ (UK)| n. |家居服，晨衣|A loose, informal robe worn over nightclothes, typically by women, around the house.|She slipped on her housecoat before going downstairs for breakfast. 她下楼吃早餐前穿上了她的晨衣。|例句：My grandmother always wore a floral housecoat in the mornings.  我的祖母早上总是穿一件花卉晨衣。

例句：The housecoat kept her warm on chilly mornings. 这件晨衣让她在寒冷的早晨保持温暖。|近义词：dressing gown, robe, negligee |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) outdoor clothes, street clothes|词性:noun
