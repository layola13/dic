nondeformation:/ˌnɒndiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 非变形，不变形 |Absence of deformation; the state of remaining unchanged in shape or form.  The material exhibited nondeformation under stress. 这种材料在压力下表现出不变形。|例句：The bridge's nondeformation during the earthquake was remarkable. 这座桥在地震期间没有变形，非常引人注目。

例句：The nondeformation of the steel frame ensured the building's stability. 钢框架的非变形确保了建筑物的稳定性。

|近义词：invariability, stability, constancy |反义词：deformation, distortion, contortion |词性：名词
