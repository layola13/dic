escapingly:/ɪˈskeɪpɪŋli/| adv.|逃避地，躲避地|In a manner that allows or suggests escape; evasively.|She spoke escapingly about her past, avoiding any direct mention of the difficult times.|近义词：evasively, elusively, indirectly|反义词：directly, frankly, openly|词性:副词
词源: From escape + -ing + -ly.  "escape" comes from Old French "escaper," ultimately from Latin "excappāre" (to slip out of one's cape, hence, to escape).
