at a loss:/æt ə lɔs/ | adj. phrase | 困惑的，不知所措的，茫然的 |  Unable to understand or explain something; uncertain about what to do or say. |  I was at a loss to explain the sudden power outage. 我不知道该如何解释突然的停电。| synonyms: bewildered, perplexed, confused, baffled | antonyms: certain, sure, confident, knowledgeable | phrase

at a loss:/æt ə lɔs/ | adj. phrase | 亏损的 | Operating at a financial deficit. | The company was at a loss for the third quarter in a row.  该公司连续第三季度亏损。| synonyms: in the red, unprofitable, losing money | antonyms: profitable, in the black, making money | phrase
