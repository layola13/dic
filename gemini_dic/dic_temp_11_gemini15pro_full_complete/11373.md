areopagus:/ˌɛəriˈɒpəɡəs/| n.|最高法院，雅典的最高法院，阿雷奥帕格|The Areopagus was the supreme court of ancient Athens.  雅典的最高法院是古代雅典的最高法院。|The case was brought before the Areopagus.  这个案件被提交到了最高法院。|近义词：supreme court, council, tribunal|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Latin *Areopagus*, from Ancient Greek *Ἀρειος πάγος* (Areios págos, “Ares' rock”), from *Ἄρης* (Árēs, “Ares”) + *πάγος* (págos, “rock, hill”).  Ares was the Greek god of war. According to legend, Ares was tried here by the gods for the murder of Poseidon's son Halirrhothius.
