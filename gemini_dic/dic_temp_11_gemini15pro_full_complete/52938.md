dine:/daɪn/
verb
用餐；吃饭
to eat dinner
We dined at a fancy restaurant. 我们在一家高档餐厅用餐。
近义词: eat, sup
反义词: fast, starve
词性: verb

dine:/daɪn/
verb
宴请；招待…吃饭
to give dinner to
We dined our guests with a five-course meal. 我们用五道菜的晚餐招待客人。
近义词: entertain, feast
反义词:  N/A
词性: verb
