aesthesodic:/ˌɛsθɪˈsɒdɪk/ | adj. | 感觉的，知觉的 | Relating to or affecting sensation or perception.  Characterized by episodes of altered sensation or perception. | The patient reported aesthesodic disturbances, including tingling and numbness in his extremities. 病人报告了感觉障碍，包括四肢麻木和刺痛。| 近义词: sensory, perceptual | 反义词: anesthetic, insensitive | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *aisthēsis* "sensation, perception" + *-odic* "relating to, characterized by".
