cognoscible:/ˈkɒɡnɪsəbl/ /ˈkɑːɡnɪsəbl/| adj.|可知的，可认识的|Capable of being known or understood; perceptible.|例句：The truth is cognoscible. 真理是可知的。

例句：The existence of dark matter is not directly cognoscible. 暗物质的存在不是可以直接认识的。|近义词：knowable, perceivable, discernible, comprehensible|反义词：unknowable, imperceptible, indiscernible, incomprehensible|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin cognoscibilis, from cognoscere ‘get to know’, from co- ‘together’ + gnoscere (see know).
