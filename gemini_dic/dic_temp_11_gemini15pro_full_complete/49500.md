demagogging:/ˈdeməɡɑːɡɪŋ/
noun
煽动群众；蛊惑人心
The act of appealing to people's emotions and prejudices in order to gain power or support, rather than using rational argument.
His campaign was marked by blatant demagogging, playing on people's fears about immigration. 他的竞选活动以公然的煽动群众为特点，利用人们对移民的恐惧。
rabble-rousing, inflammatory rhetoric
reasoned discourse
