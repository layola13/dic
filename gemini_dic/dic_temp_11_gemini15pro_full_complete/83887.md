half-angrily:/ˌhɑːf ˈæŋɡrɪli/
adv.
半生气地，略带恼怒地
Partly or slightly angrily.
He responded half-angrily, half-jokingly. 他半是生气半是开玩笑地回答。
近义词:irritatedly, crossly, peevishly
反义词:happily, cheerfully, pleasantly
词性:副词
