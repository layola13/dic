agitates:/ˈædʒɪˌteɪts/| v. |使激动；使不安；搅动；煽动|To make someone worried or upset, or to make something move violently.|The news agitated her. (这消息让她很不安。)  The washing machine agitates the clothes. (洗衣机搅动衣服。)|Synonyms: disturb, upset, stir, shake, rouse, inflame, provoke|Antonyms: calm, soothe, pacify, tranquilize|Verb

PHRASE->

agitate against (something) (反对…而鼓动) They are agitating against the new law.

become agitated (变得激动/不安)  He became agitated when he heard the news.
