bilamellate:/ˌbaɪləˈmɛleɪt/ |n.|双层片状结构|A structure composed of two lamellae or thin plates.|The electron micrograph revealed a bilamellate structure within the cell. 电子显微照片显示细胞内有一个双层片状结构。|lamella, bilayer| monolayer|noun
