antitheologians:/ˌæntiθiˈɒlədʒənz/
n.
反对神学家；非神学家
People who oppose theology or theologians.
Some antitheologians argue that theology is irrelevant in the modern world.
一些反对神学家认为神学在现代世界中无关紧要。
The antitheologians questioned the validity of religious doctrines.
这些非神学家质疑宗教教义的有效性。
critics of theology, secularists, atheists, agnostics
theologians, believers, religious scholars
noun
