flinted:/ˈflɪntɪd/
adjective
燧石制的; 坚硬的; 冷酷无情的
Made of flint; resembling flint; (figuratively) hard, unfeeling.
His flinted gaze met mine. 他那冷酷的目光与我的相遇。
近义词:flinty, hard, stony, impassive
反义词:soft, compassionate, kind
词性:adjective

flinted:/ˈflɪntɪd/
verb (past participle of flint)
用燧石打火
Struck with flint; (of eyes) flashed or sparkled.
Her eyes flinted with anger. 她的眼睛因愤怒而闪闪发光。
近义词:sparked, flashed, glittered
反义词:dimmed, dulled
词性:verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From flint (n.) + -ed.  Flint itself comes from Old English *flint, from Proto-Germanic *flintaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)plei- "to split, splinter".
