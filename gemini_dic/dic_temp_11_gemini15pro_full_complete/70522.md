filets:/fɪˈleɪz/ | n. | 鱼片，肉片 |  A boneless piece of meat or fish, especially one cut from the tenderloin of beef or the flesh of a fish.  Thin strips of meat or fish. |  The restaurant served delicious grilled filets of sole.  这家餐厅供应美味的烤鳎鱼片。| synonyms: fillets, tenderloins, strips | antonyms: whole, uncut | noun

filets:/fɪˈleɪz/ | n. | (法语)线，细丝| A thread-like structure; a filament.  | The spider spun delicate filets to create its web.  蜘蛛吐出纤细的丝来织网。 | synonyms: filaments, threads, strands | antonyms: chunks, blocks | noun
