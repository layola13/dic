nasalizations:/ˌneɪzələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 鼻音化；鼻音化现象 | The process of making a sound nasal, typically by lowering the velum (soft palate) so that air escapes through the nose as well as the mouth.  |  The nasalizations in French are a key feature of the language's pronunciation.  法语中的鼻音化是该语言发音的一个关键特征。 |  nasality, nasal sounds | non-nasality | noun
