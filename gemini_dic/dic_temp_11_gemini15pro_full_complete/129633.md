non-contributory:/ˌnɒn kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kənˈtrɪbjətɔːri/| adj.|非缴费的；非供款的；无须供款的|Not requiring a contribution from the person or entity receiving a benefit or service.  Relating to a pension or other benefit scheme where the recipient does not make regular payments into the scheme.| Employees are automatically enrolled in the company’s non-contributory pension plan. 员工自动加入公司的非缴费养老金计划。|Synonyms: free, gratuitous, unearned, complimentary|Antonyms: contributory, funded, paid|adjective
