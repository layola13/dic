electant:/ɪˈlɛktənt/ | n. | 选民; 有选举权的人 | A person entitled to vote in an election. |  Example:  The electants gathered at the polling station. 选民们聚集在投票站。

Example: Each electant has a responsibility to be informed about the candidates. 每位选民都有责任了解候选人。| 近义词: voter, elector | 反义词: none | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *ēlectāns, present participle of ēlectāre (“to choose”), frequentative of ēligere (“to choose, elect”).
