overglad:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlæd/| adj.| 非常高兴，欣喜若狂 | Excessively glad; overjoyed.  A heightened form of "glad," implying a greater intensity of happiness. | She was overglad to see her family after so long. 她很久没见到家人了，见到他们非常高兴。 |  Example: He was overglad to hear the good news. 他听到这个好消息欣喜若狂。|Synonyms: overjoyed, delighted, ecstatic, thrilled, jubilant| Antonyms:  sad, unhappy, miserable, depressed, sorrowful |Word Class: Adjective
