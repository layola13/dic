kitbag:/ˈkɪtbæɡ/| n.|工具袋；旅行袋；士兵的个人用品包|A canvas bag used by soldiers to carry personal belongings.|He packed all his essentials in his kitbag before deployment. 他在部署前把所有必需品都装进了工具袋。|近义词：duffel bag, rucksack, backpack|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 suitcase 旅行箱 表示与kitbag不同的行李类型) |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From kit (equipment) + bag.  The word 'kit' itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to Middle Dutch 'kitte' (a wooden tub).
