non-contradictory:/ˌnɒn kɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/| adj.|无矛盾的，不自相矛盾的|Not contradictory; consistent; not containing any statements that conflict with each other.|His statements were clear and non-contradictory. 他的陈述清晰且没有矛盾。|The witness gave a non-contradictory account of the events. 目击者对事件的描述没有矛盾。|近义词：consistent, coherent, compatible, harmonious|反义词：contradictory, inconsistent, conflicting, incompatible|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + contradictory (adjective)
From Latin contra- (“against”) + dicere (“to say”)
