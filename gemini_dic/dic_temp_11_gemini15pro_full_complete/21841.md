bodges:/ˈbɒdʒɪz/| v. (third-person singular simple present bodges, present participle bodging, simple past and past participle bodged)| 拙劣地修补；粗制滥造| to repair or make something badly or clumsily | He bodged the repairs and the roof still leaked. 他修补得很糟糕，屋顶仍然漏水。|Synonyms: bungle, mess up, patch up, cobble together | Antonyms: repair properly, fix perfectly| verb


bodges:/ˈbɒdʒɪz/| n. (plural of bodge) | 拙劣的修补工作；粗制滥造的东西 | a badly made or repaired thing | The whole project was a complete bodge. 整个项目完全是粗制滥造的。| Synonyms: mess, botch, shambles | Antonyms: masterpiece, perfect work| noun
