drumlike:/ˈdrʌmlaɪk/| adj.|像鼓一样的，鼓形的|Shaped like a drum; resembling the sound of a drum.|The hollow log made a deep, drumlike sound when struck.  这根空心木头被敲击时发出低沉的、像鼓一样的声音。|The  cylindrical container had a drumlike appearance.  这个圆柱形的容器看起来像个鼓。|近义词：drum-shaped, resonant, hollow|反义词:flat, solid|词性:adjective
词源:drum + like.  'Drum' comes from Middle Dutch 'tromme' and 'like' from Old English 'lic'.
