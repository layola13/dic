afoot:/əˈfʊt/
adjective
进行中的，策划中的，徒步的
happening, in progress, or being planned; on foot
Plans are afoot to build a new shopping center.  正在计划建造一个新的购物中心。
He traveled afoot. 他徒步旅行。
Synonyms: underway, in motion, in progress, developing, ongoing, proceeding, in the works, happening, occurring, taking place
Antonyms: dormant, inactive, stalled, stopped, halted
adjective
