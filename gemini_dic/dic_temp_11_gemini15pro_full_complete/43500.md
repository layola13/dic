craunch:/krɔːntʃ/ /krʌntʃ/| n. v. |嘎吱作响，咬碎，压碎|A loud crunching sound, like the sound of something hard being crushed or broken.  |
The snow crunched under our feet. 雪在我们脚下嘎吱作响。
He crunched an apple. 他嘎吱嘎吱地吃了个苹果。
|近义词：crush, grind, munch, crunch |反义词: - |词性:名词，动词

craunchy:/ˈkrʌn.tʃi/ /ˈkrɔːn.tʃi/| adj. |脆的，嘎吱作响的|Pleasantly crisp or firm and making a sharp sound when bitten or crushed.|
The granola bar was delightfully crunchy. 燕麦棒非常脆。
These apples are nice and crunchy. 这些苹果又好又脆。
|近义词：crisp, brittle |反义词:soft, chewy |词性:形容词
