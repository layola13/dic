cornfield: /ˈkɔːrnfiːld/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrnfiːld/ (US)
n.
玉米田，麦田
A field planted with corn.
The children got lost in the vast cornfield. 孩子们在广阔的玉米田中迷路了。
The farmer harvested his cornfield in the fall. 农民在秋天收割了他的玉米田。
近义词: maize field, corn patch
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider barren field, empty field)
词性: noun
