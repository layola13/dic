caulk:/kɔːk/| n. vt.|填缝料，堵缝；用填缝料堵塞|A waterproof filler and sealant, used in building work and repairs.  To seal (a gap or seam) with caulk.|例句：He caulked the windows to keep out the draft. 他用填缝料堵塞窗户以防止穿堂风。

例句：The boat needed fresh caulk to make it watertight.  这艘船需要新的填缝料才能防水。|近义词：sealant, filler, putty|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Northern French *cauquer, *calquer (“to press, tread down”), from Latin calcare (“to tread, press, trample”).  Related to "chalk."
