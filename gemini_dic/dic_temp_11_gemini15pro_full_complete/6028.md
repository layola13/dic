ameliorator:/əˈmiːliəreɪtər/
n.
改良者，改进者
Someone or something that ameliorates a situation; an improver.
The new CEO proved to be an ameliorator of the company's declining sales.  这位新CEO被证明是公司销售额下降的改进者。
The software update includes several ameliorators designed to enhance performance.  该软件更新包含几个旨在提高性能的改进程序。
近义词:improver, reformer, enhancer, betterer
反义词:worsener, aggravator, deteriorator
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ameliorator, from Latin amelioratus, past participle of ameliorare "to make better,"  from ad- "to" + melior "better."
