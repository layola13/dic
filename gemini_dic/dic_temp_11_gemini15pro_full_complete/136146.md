nonrefueling:/ˌnɒnriˈfjuːəlɪŋ/
adjective
不加油的，无需加油的
Not requiring refueling; capable of operating without refueling.
The Air Force is developing nonrefueling aircraft. 空军正在研发不加油的飞机。
The navy is experimenting with nonrefueling nuclear submarines. 海军正在试验无需加油的核潜艇。
近义词:self-sustaining, continuous operation
反义词:refueling, requiring refueling
词性:adjective
