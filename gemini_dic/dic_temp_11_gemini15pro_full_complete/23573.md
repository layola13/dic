braises:/ˈbreɪzɪz/
verb
炖; 焖
cooks (food) by browning it in fat and then simmering it in a closed container with a small amount of liquid
She braised the beef in red wine for three hours. 她用红酒炖牛肉三个小时。
近义词: stews, simmers
反义词: roasts, grills
词性:v.

braise:/breɪz/
noun
炖肉; 焖肉
a dish of meat braised
The beef braise was tender and flavorful. 这道焖牛肉鲜嫩可口。
近义词: stew
反义词: roast
词性:n.
