anthocarpous:/ænθəˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
具花果的，假果的
Having a fruit-like body which includes the persistent perianth and receptacle, as in the mulberry or strawberry.
The mulberry is an example of an anthocarpous fruit. 桑葚是假果的一个例子。
The strawberry is also anthocarpous, with the fleshy part derived from the receptacle rather than the ovary. 草莓也是假果，其肉质部分来源于花托而不是子房。
近义词: accessory, pseudocarpous
反义词: eucarpous (though not a perfect antonym, it refers to fruits developed solely from the ovary)
词性: adjective
