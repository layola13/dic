outvote:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊt/
verb
以票数胜过;投票击败
to defeat in a vote; to win a vote against
The proposal to build a new stadium was outvoted by the residents.  建设新体育场的提案被居民投票否决了。
The union members outvoted the leadership on the new contract.  工会成员在新的合同上以投票击败了领导层。
近义词:defeat, overrule
反义词: lose (a vote), be defeated
词性:verb
