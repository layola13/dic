daunt:/dɔːnt/ | v. | 使气馁，使畏缩，使胆怯 | to lessen the courage of; intimidate; dishearten |  The sheer size of the task didn't daunt her.  这项任务的艰巨性并没有让她气馁。 | synonyms: intimidate, discourage, dismay, dishearten | antonyms: encourage, embolden, hearten | verb
daunting:/ˈdɔːntɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人畏惧的，使人氣餒的 | seeming difficult to deal with in a way that discourages you | Climbing Mount Everest is a daunting task. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项艰巨的任务。| synonyms: intimidating, formidable, discouraging | antonyms: encouraging, easy, simple | adjective
