decompressing:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 减压，解压 | Relieving or reducing pressure; returning to a normal state after being compressed. |  After a long day at work, I enjoy decompressing with a good book.  下班后我喜欢读一本好书来放松一下。| synonyms: relaxing, unwinding, chilling out | antonyms: stressing, tensing, pressurizing | verb

decompression:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 减压，解压 | The act or process of decompressing. | The decompression of the cabin after the plane landed was noticeable. 飞机着陆后机舱的减压很明显。 | synonyms: relaxation, release | antonyms: compression, pressurization | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + compress (to press together) + -ing (present participle suffix). Ultimately from Latin com- (together) + premere (to press).
