bajillion:/bəˈdʒɪljən/
noun|无数，极大量的|An indefinitely huge number.  A humorous exaggeration.|例句：I have a bajillion things to do today. 我今天有无数的事情要做。

例句：There were a bajillion people at the concert.  音乐会上人山人海。
informal|非正式的|Used in informal contexts to emphasize a large quantity.|
近义词：zillion, gazillion, countless, myriad|
反义词:few, little|
