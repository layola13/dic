horseshoes:/ˈhɔːrsʃuːz/ /ˈhɔːrʃuːz/
n.
马蹄铁；马蹄铁形物
U-shaped pieces of metal nailed to the bottom of a horse's hooves to protect them.
The blacksmith shaped the horseshoes with practiced ease. 铁匠熟练地打造马蹄铁。
synonyms:  U-shaped iron,  hoof protector
antonyms:  bare hoof
