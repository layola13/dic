carryovers:/ˈkæriˌoʊvərz/
n.
结转，延期，遗留物
Amounts carried over from one accounting period to the next.
从一个会计期间结转到下一个会计期间的金额。
The carryover from last year's budget helped fund the new project.  去年的预算结转帮助资助了这个新项目。
carryover, surplus, remainder
deficit, shortage
noun
