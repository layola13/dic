garçon:/ɡɑːrˈsɔ̃/ (UK), /ɡɑːrˈsoʊn/ (US)| n.| 服务员(男), 男孩|A male waiter or server, especially in a restaurant.  It can also mean "boy" in French, but this usage is less common in English.|例句：Garçon, another bottle of wine, please. 服务员，请再来一瓶酒。

例句：The garçon quickly took our order. 服务员很快记下了我们的点菜。|近义词：waiter, server, attendant |反义词: customer, client| noun
