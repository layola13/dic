mineralization:/ˌmɪnərəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 矿化，矿化作用 | The process by which organic matter is converted into inorganic matter, often involving the deposition of minerals.  The mineralization of bone is essential for its strength. |  The mineralization of the soil is important for plant growth. 土壤的矿化作用对植物生长很重要。 | 近义词: petrification, fossilization | 反义词: decomposition | 词性: noun

词源: From mineral + -ization.  Ultimately from Latin *minera, "mine, ore."
