dump:/dʌmp/| n. vt.|垃圾场，倾倒；倾销|A place where rubbish or waste material is taken and left.  To get rid of something unwanted, often by leaving it in a place where it should not be.|例句：The city council is planning to build a new rubbish dump. 市议会计划新建一个垃圾场。


He dumped his old clothes in a charity bin. 他把旧衣服扔进了慈善捐赠箱。|近义词：discard, dispose of, unload, tip, ditch, jettison|反义词:retain, keep, hoard|名词/动词
