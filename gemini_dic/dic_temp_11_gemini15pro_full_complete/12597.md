assailable:/əˈseɪləbl/| adj.|易受攻击的，可攻击的|Open to attack; vulnerable.|The castle's defenses were weak and assailable from the north side.  城堡的防御薄弱，北侧易受攻击。|The candidate's position on the issue was highly assailable.  候选人在这个问题上的立场很容易受到攻击。|近义词：vulnerable, susceptible, weak, defenseless|反义词：impregnable, invulnerable, invincible|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From Old French *assailable (modern French assillable) < assaiilir (“to assault”) + -able. Equivalent to assail + -able.  源自古法语 *assailable（现代法语 assillable）< assaiilir（“攻击”）+ -able。相当于 assail + -able。
