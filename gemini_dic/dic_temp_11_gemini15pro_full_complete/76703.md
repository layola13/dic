gambeer:/ɡæmˈbɪər/ | adj. | 苦涩的，辛辣的；（尤指啤酒）口味浓烈的；坚定的，不屈不挠的 | Having a sharp, pungent, and slightly bitter flavour or aroma, especially of beer; resolute and unwavering. | The beer had a distinctly gambeer flavour. 这啤酒有一种明显的苦涩味道。|  Synonyms: bitter, pungent, sharp, astringent, resolute, steadfast, unwavering | Antonyms: sweet, mild, bland, irresolute, wavering | adjective

gambeer:/ɡæmˈbɪər/ | n. | （尤指东南亚产的）藤黄 | A yellowish-brown resin obtained from certain trees in Southeast Asia. | Gambeer is used in tanning and dyeing. 藤黄用于鞣革和染色。| Synonyms: resin, gum | Antonyms: N/A | noun
