illegality:/ˌɪliːˈɡælɪti/
noun
违法；非法；不法行为
the state of being illegal or unlawful
The illegality of the transaction was clear. 这笔交易的非法性显而易见。
Synonyms: unlawfulness, illicitness, criminality
Antonyms: legality, lawfulness
