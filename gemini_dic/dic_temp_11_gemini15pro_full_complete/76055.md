furnaced:/ˈfɜːrnɪst/| adj.|加热的，炉子处理的|Treated in a furnace; heated intensely.  The metal was furnaced until it glowed red. 金属在炉中加热直到发出红光。|近义词：heated, fired, smelted|反义词:cooled, chilled|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "furnace" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives from nouns).  "Furnace" comes from Old French "fornais" (forge, furnace), ultimately from Latin "fornax" (oven).
