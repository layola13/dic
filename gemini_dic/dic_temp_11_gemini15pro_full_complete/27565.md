canalised:/ˈkænəlaɪzd/ /ˈkænəˌlaɪzd/
verb
引导; 疏导; 管控;限制
To make something follow a particular route or course, often one that is narrow and restrictive.  To make something develop in a particular, often limited, way.
The river was canalised to prevent flooding. 这条河被疏导以防止洪水泛滥。
His creativity was canalised into writing advertising copy. 他的创造力被引导到撰写广告文案上。
近义词: channelled, directed, controlled, restricted
反义词: undirected, unrestricted, free-flowing
词性:v.


canalised:/ˈkænəlaɪzd/ /ˈkænəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
被疏导的; 被限制的; 被管控的
Having been made to follow a particular route or course, often one that is narrow and restrictive.  Having been made to develop in a particular, often limited, way.
