astun:/əˈstʌn/
verb
使震惊；使惊骇
To surprise or shock someone very much.
The news astounded everyone.  这个消息震惊了所有人。
He was astounded by her beauty. 他被她的美貌惊呆了。
synonym: amaze, shock, surprise, startle, daze
antonym: bore, calm, expect
词性: 动词
