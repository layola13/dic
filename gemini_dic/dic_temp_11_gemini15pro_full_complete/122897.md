molokany:/məˈlɒkəni/ | n. | 摩洛坎人； молокане (俄语) | A member of a Russian Christian sect, similar to the Dukhobors, that rejected the established church and its rituals.  They were pacifists and vegetarians. |  The Molokany migrated to the Caucasus and later some to the United States to escape persecution. 摩洛坎人为了逃避迫害迁移到了高加索地区，后来一些人又迁移到了美国。 |  The Molokany community maintained their traditions even after migrating. 摩洛坎社区即使在迁移之后也保持着他们的传统。 |  Dukhobors, spiritual Christians | None | noun
