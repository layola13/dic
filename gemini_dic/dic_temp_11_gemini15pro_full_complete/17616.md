beaverteen:/ˈbiːvərtiːn/
noun
海狸绒，棉绒
A heavy, close-woven cotton fabric with a short, thick, velvety nap on one side.
The sofa was upholstered in a plush beaverteen. 这张沙发是用豪华的棉绒装饰的。
synonyms:  velveteen, fustian, moleskin, corduroy
antonyms:  satin, silk, linen
