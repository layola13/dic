christianization:/ˌkrɪʃtʃənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 基督教化 |The process of converting to Christianity or causing someone/something to convert to Christianity. The spread of Christian beliefs and practices. |The christianization of the Roman Empire was a gradual process. 罗马帝国的基督教化是一个渐进的过程。| The christianization of indigenous cultures often led to the suppression of traditional beliefs. 土著文化的基督教化经常导致传统信仰的压制。| 近义词: conversion, evangelization | 反义词: secularization | 词性: 名词
