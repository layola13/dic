alacritously:/əˈlækrɪtəsli/
| adv. | 轻快地，欣然地 | Cheerfully and eagerly; with brisk and cheerful readiness. | She accepted the invitation alacritously. 她欣然接受了邀请。|
近义词: eagerly, readily, willingly, cheerfully, briskly
| 反义词: reluctantly, hesitantly, unwillingly, apathetically, listlessly
| 词性: 副词

alacrity:/əˈlækrɪti/
| n. | 活泼，敏捷，轻快 | Brisk and cheerful readiness. | She accepted the new assignment with alacrity. 她欣然接受了新任务。|
近义词: eagerness, willingness, readiness, enthusiasm, zeal
| 反义词: reluctance, hesitation, unwillingness, apathy, listlessness
| 词性: 名词


词源:
From Latin *alacritās*, from alacer (“cheerful, brisk, lively”).
