doctorize:/ˈdɑːktəraɪz/| v. |使医学化；用医学术语表达；像医生一样对待|To make something medical or to treat something in a medical way; to use medical jargon. |例句：The politician tried to doctorize the issue of poverty, attributing it solely to mental health problems.  这位政客试图将贫困问题医学化，将其完全归因于心理健康问题。

例句：He tends to doctorize even simple ailments, rushing to the internet for diagnoses. 他倾向于把即使是小病也医学化，急于上网寻求诊断。|近义词：medicalize, pathologize|反义词: normalize, demedicalize|词性:动词
