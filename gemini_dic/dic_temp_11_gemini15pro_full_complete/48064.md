declarable:/dɪˈklerəbl/
adjective|可申报的，可声明的|able to be declared; that must be declared|All income is declarable. 所有收入都必须申报。|synonym: reportable, accountable|antonym: undeclared, unreportable
word origin:  From declare + -able.  Declare comes from Old French *declarer*, from Latin declarare "to make clear, explain, reveal," from de- "fully" + clarare "to make clear," related to clarus "clear, bright."
