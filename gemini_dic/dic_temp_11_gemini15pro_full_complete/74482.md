franking:/ˈfræŋkɪŋ/
n.
邮资特权，免费邮寄
The act of impressing a postage stamp on a letter or other postal matter; also, the stamp or mark itself.  The privilege of sending mail free of charge.
The CEO enjoys the franking privilege for business correspondence. 首席执行官享有商务信件的免费邮寄特权。
近义词：postage, free mailing
反义词：paying postage

franking:/ˈfræŋkɪŋ/
n.
坦白，真诚
Frankness or sincerity of expression; openness.
His franking about his past mistakes was admirable. 他坦诚自己过去的错误令人钦佩。
近义词：candor, honesty, openness
反义词：dishonesty, deception
