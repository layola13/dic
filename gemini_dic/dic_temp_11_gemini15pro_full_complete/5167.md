allerion: /ˈæləriən/ | n. | 欧洲纹章学中的小鹰图案; 小鹰| A small eagle without beak or feet, used in heraldry.  A young eagle, especially one still in the nest. | The knight's shield bore an allerion, signifying his youthful valor. 骑士的盾牌上有一只小鹰图案，象征着他年轻的勇气。 | synonym: eaglet | antonym: N/A | noun
