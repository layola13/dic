hoard:/hɔːrd/ (US) /hɔːd/ (UK)| n. v.|囤积物；贮藏，囤积| A supply or accumulation that is hidden or carefully guarded.  To amass (something) and hide or store it away, often secretly.|Example 1:  He had a hoard of old coins in his attic. 他在阁楼里藏有一堆旧硬币。

Example 2:  Squirrels hoard nuts for the winter. 松鼠会储存坚果过冬。|Synonyms: stockpile, cache, stash (n.); accumulate, amass, stockpile (v.)|Antonyms: squander, dissipate (v.)|noun, verb
