house-trained:/ˈhaʊs treɪnd/| adj.|受过室内排泄训练的，习惯于室内排泄的|An animal, especially a dog or cat, is house-trained if it has been trained to urinate and defecate outside or in a designated area inside, rather than soiling the house.|例句：My puppy is finally house-trained. 我的小狗终于学会了室内排泄。|近义词：potty-trained|反义词:untrained, not housebroken|词性:形容词

house-trained:/ˈhaʊs treɪnd/| adj.|习惯于室内生活的|A person, especially a child, is considered house-trained if they understand and follow the rules and customs of living inside a house, such as not making a mess or being too noisy.|例句：The children are quite house-trained and know not to run inside. 孩子们很懂规矩，知道不要在屋里跑。|近义词：well-behaved, housebroken|反义词:unruly, disruptive|词性:形容词
