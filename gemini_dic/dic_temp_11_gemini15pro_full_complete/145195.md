overbaits:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪts/
verb
过度放饵；用饵过多
To use too much bait, especially when fishing.
The fisherman overbaited his hook and scared away the fish. 渔夫在鱼钩上放了太多鱼饵，把鱼吓跑了。
