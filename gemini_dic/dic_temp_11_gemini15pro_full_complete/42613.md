counterraids:/ˈkaʊntərˌreɪdz/
noun
反突袭，反击
An attack made in response to an enemy raid.
The army launched a series of counterraids against the enemy bases. 军队对敌军基地发动了一系列反突袭。
近义词: counterattacks, reprisals
反义词: raids, attacks
词性:n.
