nonprotestation:/ˌnɒnˌprɒtɪˈsteɪʃn/ |n.| 不抗议，不反对; 默认; 缄默 |Absence or lack of protest; the act of not protesting or objecting.| His nonprotestation implied agreement. 他的不抗议意味着同意。 | 近义词: acquiescence, acceptance, compliance, silence | 反义词: protest, objection, dissent | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- + protestation. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "protestation" comes from the Late Latin "protestatio," meaning "a declaration, affirmation."
