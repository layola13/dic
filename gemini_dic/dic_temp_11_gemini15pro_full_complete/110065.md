lichen:/ˈlaɪkən/ (US), /ˈlɪtʃən/ (UK)| n.|地衣|A complex life form that is a symbiotic partnership of two separate organisms, a fungus and an alga.|Lichens grow on rocks and trees. 地衣生长在岩石和树木上。|
lichen:/ˈlaɪkən/ (US), /ˈlɪtʃən/ (UK)| n.|地衣|Lichens are sensitive to air pollution. 地衣对空气污染很敏感。|
lichen:/ˈlaɪkən/ (US), /ˈlɪtʃən/ (UK)| n.|地衣|Synonyms: moss (in some contexts), growth|Antonyms: none (no direct opposite for a specific organism)|noun
