jug:/dʒʌɡ/ | n. | 水壶，罐，监牢 | A container with a handle and a lip, for holding and pouring liquids.  A large earthenware or glass bottle with a narrow neck, typically for holding and serving milk, cream, or water. Slang for jail or prison. |  She poured the milk from the jug into a glass. 她把牛奶从壶里倒进杯子里。 He spent a night in the jug. 他在监狱里待了一夜。 | 近义词：pitcher, container, vessel, jar, prison, jail | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a jug) | 词性: 名词


jug:/dʒʌɡ/ | v. |  用罐子装, 监禁 | To put into a jug. To imprison. | They jugged the cider for fermentation. 他们把苹果酒装进罐子里发酵。  The police jugged him for the night.  警察把他关了一夜。| 近义词： imprison, confine, jail  | 反义词: release, free | 词性: 动词
