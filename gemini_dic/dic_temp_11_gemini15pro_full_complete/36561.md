collapsed:/kəˈlæpst/ | adj.| 倒塌的，崩溃的，折叠的 | Having fallen down or in; broken down; folded compactly. | The roof of the old barn collapsed under the weight of the snow. 老谷仓的屋顶在雪的重压下坍塌了。| synonyms: fallen, ruined, crumbled, folded, compact | antonyms: upright, standing, expanded, open | adjective

collapsed:/kəˈlæpst/ | v.| 倒塌，崩溃，折叠 | (of a structure) suddenly fall inwards or downwards. | The tunnel collapsed, trapping several miners. 隧道坍塌了，困住了几名矿工。| synonyms: cave in, fall down, crumple, give way, fold | antonyms: erect, build, expand, open | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *collāpsus*, past participle of *collābere*  "to fall together, fall down, sink down, decay,"  from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *lābi* "to slip, glide, fall"
