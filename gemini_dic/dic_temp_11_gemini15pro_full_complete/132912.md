noneligible:/ˌnɒnˈelɪdʒɪb(ə)l/
adjective
不合格的，不符合条件的
Not eligible; not entitled to participate or be chosen.
例句：
He was noneligible for the scholarship because his grades were too low.
他的成绩太低，没有资格获得奖学金。

例句：
Many small businesses are noneligible for government funding.
许多小企业没有资格获得政府资助。

近义词：ineligible, disqualified, unfit, unsuitable
反义词：eligible, qualified, suitable, fit
词性: adjective
