assagai:/ˈæsəɡaɪ/ /ˈæsəɡɑːi/ | n. |投矛，短矛|A slender, iron-tipped spear used by various peoples of southern Africa.  A light spear, usually thrown, used by Southern African peoples.|The Zulu warriors charged, brandishing their assagais.  祖鲁族战士挥舞着他们的投矛冲锋陷阵。|The hunter brought down the antelope with a well-aimed assagai. 猎人用精准的投矛猎杀了羚羊。|近义词：spear, javelin |反义词:shield, armor|noun
