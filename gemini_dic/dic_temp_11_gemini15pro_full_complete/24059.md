breme:/briːm/| n.|鱼的种类; 特别是各种鲤科鱼类|A type of fish; especially various cyprinid fishes.|The breme is a common fish in European rivers.  这种鲤科鱼类在欧洲河流中很常见。|The fisherman caught a large breme. 渔夫捕到了一条大鲤鱼。|近义词：carp, roach, dace|反义词: None|词性:名词


Etymology:
From Middle English *breme, from Old French *bresme, from Frankish *brahsima.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brahsmô. Cognate with Old High German bresam, German Brassen, Dutch brasem.
