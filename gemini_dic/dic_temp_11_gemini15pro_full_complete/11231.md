archivolt:/ˈɑːrkɪvɒlt/ | n. |拱券装饰，拱门饰，拱顶石|An archivolt is a molded or decorated band following the curve of an arch.  It often includes a series of sculpted figures or decorative motifs.| The archivolt of the cathedral entrance was intricately carved with scenes from the Bible.| 近义词: arch molding, extrados| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps intrados - the inner curve of an arch) | 词性: noun
