inelegantly:/ɪnˈɛlɪɡəntli/
adv.
不雅地，笨拙地
in an inelegant manner; without elegance; clumsily or awkwardly.
She inelegantly stumbled over the rug. 她笨拙地被地毯绊倒了。
近义词:awkwardly, clumsily, gracelessly, ungracefully
反义词:elegantly, gracefully, smoothly
词性:副词

inelegant:/ɪnˈɛlɪɡənt/
adj.
不雅的，笨拙的
lacking elegance; not graceful or refined in appearance, movement, or manner.
He made an inelegant gesture. 他做了一个不雅的手势。
近义词:awkward, clumsy, graceless, ungraceful
反义词:elegant, graceful, smooth
词性:形容词

词源:
From in- + elegant + -ly.  "In-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Elegant" comes from the Latin word "elegans," meaning "choice, tasteful, fine."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
