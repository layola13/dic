dunes:/djuːnz/
n.
沙丘
a ridge or hill of sand piled up by the wind
The wind sculpted the dunes into fantastic shapes. 风将沙丘雕刻成奇异的形状。

近义词:hills, mounds, ridges
反义词:valleys, depressions
