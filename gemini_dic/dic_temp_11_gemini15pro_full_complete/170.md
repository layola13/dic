abatis:/ˈæbətɪs/, /əˈbɑːtiː/ | n. |防御工事，障碍物 |An obstacle formed by felled trees with sharpened branches pointed outward, used for defense. | The soldiers constructed an abatis to slow the enemy's advance. 士兵们构筑了障碍物来延缓敌人的进攻。

The retreating troops left an abatis of fallen trees behind them. 撤退的部队在身后留下了一片倒下的树木作为障碍。 | 近义词: barricade, obstacle, barrier | 反义词: opening, passage | 词性: noun
