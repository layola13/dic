irascibilities:/ɪˌræsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n.|易怒，暴躁|The state of being easily angered; proneness to outbursts of temper.|His irascibilities were well known to his colleagues, who learned to avoid triggering his outbursts.  他的同事们都很清楚他的暴躁脾气，所以都学会了避免激怒他。|Synonyms: irritability, touchiness, petulance, cantankerousness, choler|Antonyms: patience, forbearance, tolerance, equanimity|Noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *irascibilitas, from Latin irascibilis (“irascible”), from irasci (“to grow angry”), from ira (“anger”).
