otc:/ˌoʊ tiː ˈsiː/ (US) /ˌəʊ tiː ˈsiː/ (UK)| adj. n.|非处方药；场外交易|Over-the-counter.  Referring to drugs sold without a prescription or financial transactions made directly between parties, outside of a formal exchange.|Example: You can buy many pain relievers OTC. 你可以买到很多非处方止痛药。|Synonym: nonprescription, unlisted|Antonym: prescription, listed|adjective/noun

otc:/ˌoʊ tiː ˈsiː/ (US) /ˌəʊ tiː ˈsiː/ (UK)| adj. n.|场外交易的；非处方药的|Over-the-counter. Referring to securities traded directly between two parties, rather than on a formal exchange.|Example:  OTC stocks are often considered higher risk. 场外交易的股票通常被认为风险更高。|Synonym: unlisted, off-exchange|Antonym: listed, exchange-traded|adjective/noun
