barbarousness:/ˈbɑːrbərəsnəs/
n.
野蛮；残暴；粗鲁
extreme cruelty or brutality; uncivilized behavior.
The barbarousness of the attack shocked the world.  这次袭击的残暴震惊了世界。

近义词:savagery, brutality, cruelty, inhumanity, atrocity
反义词:civilization, humanity, kindness, compassion, gentleness
词性:noun

barbarous:/ˈbɑːrbərəs/
adj.
野蛮的；残暴的；粗鲁的
extremely cruel or brutal; uncivilized.

反义词:civilized, humane, kind, compassionate, gentle
