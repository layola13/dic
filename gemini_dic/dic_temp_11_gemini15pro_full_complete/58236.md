druidish:/ˈdruːɪdɪʃ/
adjective
德鲁伊的；像德鲁伊的；与德鲁伊有关的
Relating to, characteristic of, or resembling the Druids.
The ancient stones had a druidish feel. 这些古老的石头有一种德鲁伊的感觉。
Near synonyms:  pagan, Celtic, ancient, mystical, priestly
Opposite: modern, secular
词性: 形容词


druidish:/ˈdruːɪdɪʃ/
noun
德鲁伊教的仪式或习俗
The rituals or customs of Druids.
The ceremony involved various druidish practices.  这个仪式包含各种德鲁伊的习俗。
Near synonyms: rites, rituals, ceremonies, customs, practices
Opposite: ---
词性: 名词
