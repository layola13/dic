mislabeling:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbəlɪŋ/
n.|错误标记，贴错标签|The mislabeling of the product led to a massive recall. 产品的错误标签导致了大规模的召回。|例句：The mislabeling of the medication could have serious consequences.  药物的错误标签可能会造成严重后果。|近义词：misbranding, incorrect labeling|反义词：correct labeling|词性:名词

mislabel:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbl/
vt.|贴错标签，错误标记|The factory mislabeled several boxes of cereal. 工厂错误地标记了几盒麦片。|例句：The clerk mislabeled the package, sending it to the wrong address. 店员贴错了包裹标签，把它寄到了错误的地址。|近义词：misbrand, label incorrectly|反义词：label correctly|词性:动词

词源:  "mis-" (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "label" (from Old French "label," meaning "a strip of parchment or paper") + "-ing" (present participle/gerund suffix)
