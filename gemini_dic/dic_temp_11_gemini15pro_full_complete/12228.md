asbestos:/æsˈbɛstəs/
noun|石棉|A fibrous mineral that can cause serious respiratory diseases when inhaled.|The old building was filled with asbestos. 这栋老建筑里充满了石棉。|Synonyms: amphibole, chrysotile|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a material, but perhaps "safe insulation" in a specific context)|n.

asbestous:/æsˈbɛstəs/
adjective|石棉的，含石棉的|Made of, containing, or resembling asbestos.|The asbestous material was carefully removed.  含石棉的材料被小心地移除了。|Synonyms:  asbestic|Antonyms: asbestos-free|adj.
