graduators:/ˈɡrædʒueɪtərz/
n.
毕业生

The graduators threw their caps in the air at the end of the ceremony.  毕业生们在典礼结束时将帽子抛向空中。
The university welcomed the new graduators to the alumni association. 大学欢迎新毕业生加入校友会。

词性: noun
