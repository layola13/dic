degradation:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 降解；退化；堕落 |  The process of something being degraded or the state of being degraded.  A decline to a lower condition, quality, or level. | The environmental degradation caused by pollution is a serious concern. 污染造成的环境恶化是一个严重的问题。| synonyms: deterioration, decline, decay, degeneration, worsening | antonyms: improvement, enhancement, elevation, amelioration | noun

degradation:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 降级；丢脸 |  A reduction in rank or status, often involving loss of respect or reputation. |  His degradation from captain to private was a humiliating experience. 他从上尉降到列兵的经历是一种耻辱。 | synonyms: demotion, humiliation, disgrace, dishonor | antonyms: promotion, elevation, honor | noun
