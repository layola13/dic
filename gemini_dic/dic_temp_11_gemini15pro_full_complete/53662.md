disciflorous:/ˌdɪskɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
花盘状的;盘状花的
Having the structure of a disc floret, as in the flower heads of the Asteraceae (Compositae).  Characterized by having flowers with a disc-shaped head composed of many small florets.
The sunflower, while appearing to be a single large flower, is actually a disciflorous head composed of hundreds of tiny florets.  向日葵虽然看起来像一朵单一的大花，但实际上是由数百朵小花组成的盘状花序。
The botanist identified the plant as a disciflorous species due to its flat flower head.  植物学家根据其扁平的花头将该植物鉴定为盘状花种。
近义词: discoid, radiate
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider terms like "ligulate" referring to ray florets)
