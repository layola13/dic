magneto:/mæɡˈniːtoʊ/ |n.|磁电机，磁发电机|A small electrical generator that uses a permanent magnet to produce an alternating current, especially one used in the ignition system of some internal combustion engines.|The magneto on my motorbike needs replacing. 我的摩托车的磁电机需要更换了。|Synonyms: generator, alternator, dynamo|Antonyms: N/A|noun

magnetos:/mæɡˈniːtoʊz/ |n.|磁电机（magneto的复数形式）|Plural form of magneto, referring to multiple small electrical generators using permanent magnets.|The magnetos in these older cars are often unreliable.  这些老式汽车的磁电机通常不可靠。|Synonyms: generators, alternators, dynamos|Antonyms: N/A|noun
