copy-cat: /ˈkɒpiˌkæt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiˌkæt/ (US) | n. | 模仿者，抄袭者 |  Someone who copies another person's actions, ideas, or work, often to gain an advantage or because they lack originality. | He called her a copy-cat because she bought the same dress as him. 他说她是模仿者，因为她买了和他一样的裙子。|

copy-cat: /ˈkɒpiˌkæt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiˌkæt/ (US) | adj. | 模仿的，抄袭的 |  Describing something that is copied from something else. | She made a copy-cat version of the popular toy. 她制作了一个模仿流行玩具的版本。|


近义词: imitator, mimic, impersonator | 反义词: innovator, original, pioneer | 词性: noun, adjective
