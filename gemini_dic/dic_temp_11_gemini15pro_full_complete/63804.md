epidemiology:/ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n.|流行病学|The study of the distribution and determinants of health-related states or events in specified populations, and the application of this study to the control of health problems.|Epidemiology plays a crucial role in understanding and controlling the spread of infectious diseases.|近义词: study of disease, public health|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

etymology: epi- (Greek: upon) + demos (Greek: people) + -logy (Greek: study of)
