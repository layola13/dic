disuniters:/dɪsjuːˈnaɪtərz/
|n.|分裂者，造成不团结的人或事物|People who cause disunity or things that break apart a group are disuniters.  Those who sow discord among team members are disuniters.|The politician's divisive rhetoric made him a disuniter in the country.|近义词：divisors, separatists, schismatics|反义词:uniters, harmonizers|词性:名词

disuniter:/dɪsjuːˈnaɪtər/
|n.|分裂者，造成不团结的人或事物|A person or thing that causes disunity.|The new policy proved to be a disuniter within the company, causing factions to form.|近义词：divider, splitter|反义词:uniter, joiner|词性:名词
