endower:/ɪnˈdaʊər/| v.| 捐赠，赋予|To provide with a permanent fund or source of income; to furnish with a gift, talent, or quality.|例句：The philanthropist endowed the university with a large sum of money.  这位慈善家向这所大学捐赠了一大笔钱。

例句：She was endowed with both beauty and intelligence. 她被赋予了美貌和智慧。|近义词：donate, bestow, grant, enrich|反义词：deprive, divest|词性:动词
