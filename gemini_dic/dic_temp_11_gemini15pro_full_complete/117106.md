mealinesses:/ˈmiːlinɪsɪz/
|n.|粉状; 淀粉质; |The mealiness of the potatoes ruined the dish. 土豆的粉状毁了这道菜。|
|n.|干巴巴的; 无味的; |The mealiness of the conversation made me want to leave.  谈话的枯燥乏味让我想要离开。|
|近义词: dryness, blandness, starchiness|
|反义词: moistness, flavorfulness|
|词性: 名词|
|词源: From mealy + -ness.  Ultimately from Middle English *melew, from Old English melu “meal, flour.”|
