neurenteric:/ˌnjʊərənˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. |神经肠的|Relating to both the neural tube and the gut.  Formed from both neural and enteric tissue.  Often refers to a congenital malformation involving a persistent connection between the neural canal and the digestive tract.| Neurenteric cysts are rare congenital lesions. 神经肠囊肿是罕见的先天性病变。| Synonym: neuroenteric | Antonym: N/A | Adjective
