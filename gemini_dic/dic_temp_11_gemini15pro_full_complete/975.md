acanthaceous:/ˌækænˈθeɪʃəs/
adjective
爵床科的; 有刺的
Belonging to the Acanthaceae family (a family of flowering plants).  Also, resembling or characteristic of plants in this family, often having thorny or spiny leaves.
The garden boasts a wide variety of acanthaceous plants.  这个花园里生长着各种各样的爵床科植物。
Near synonyms: thorny, spiny, prickly
Antonyms: smooth, soft
