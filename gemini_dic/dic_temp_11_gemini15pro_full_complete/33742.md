circuitous: /sərˈkjuɪtəs/ /sərˈkjuːɪtəs/
adjective
迂回的；绕行的
(of a route or journey) longer than the most direct way.
The bus took a circuitous route because of the traffic. 因为交通拥堵，公共汽车绕道行驶。

The writer's circuitous style made the article difficult to understand.  这位作家的迂回写作风格使得文章难以理解。

synonym: indirect, roundabout, meandering
antonym: direct, straight, straightforward
词性: 形容词
