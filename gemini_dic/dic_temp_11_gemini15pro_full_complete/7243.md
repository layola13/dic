anglo-catholicisms:/ˈæŋɡləʊ kəˈθɒlɪsɪzəmz/
|n.|盎格鲁天主教主义|A movement within the Church of England that emphasizes the Catholic aspects of its tradition and seeks to incorporate certain Catholic beliefs and practices, while remaining within the Anglican Communion.|The anglo-catholicisms of the parish were evident in the elaborate rituals and vestments.  这个教区的盎格鲁天主教主义在精细的仪式和法衣中显而易见。|Synonyms: High Churchmanship, Anglo-Catholicism|Antonyms: Evangelicalism, Low Churchmanship|noun
