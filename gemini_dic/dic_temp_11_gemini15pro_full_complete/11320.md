area-codes:/ˈɛəriə koʊdz/ (American English) /ˈeəriə kəʊdz/ (British English) | n. | 区域代码 | A three-digit number that precedes a seven-digit phone number, indicating a specific geographic area. | Example: The area code for Los Angeles is 213.  洛杉矶的区号是213。

Example: I need to know the area code for New York City. 我需要知道纽约市的区号。


近义词: dialing code, telephone code | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
