cuz: /kʌz/| informal contraction|因为，由于|Cuz is an informal, shortened version of "because". It's commonly used in casual conversation, texting, and online communication. | Cuz I'm tired. 因为我累了。|近义词：because, 'cause, as, since|反义词: (No direct antonym for a conjunction.  Consider antonyms for the implied meaning, like "therefore" - perhaps "however," "nevertheless")| conjunction
