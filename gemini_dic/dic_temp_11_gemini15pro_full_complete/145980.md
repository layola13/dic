overdocumenting:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjəˌmentɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|过度记录，过度文档化|The act of creating excessive documentation, often to the point of being counterproductive.|The team was overdocumenting every single step, which slowed down the project significantly.  团队过度记录了每一个步骤，这大大减慢了项目的进度。|over-document, document excessively|underdocumenting|v.
overdocumenting:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjəˌmentɪŋ/
noun (gerund)|过度记录，过度文档化|The practice of documenting something excessively.|Overdocumenting can sometimes hinder productivity rather than improve it. 过度记录有时会阻碍生产力，而不是提高生产力。|excessive documentation|insufficient documentation|n.
