gris:/ɡriː/ /ɡrɪs/| adj.|灰色；灰色的；灰白的|A color between black and white; a neutral color.|The walls were painted a soft gris.|近义词:gray, ashen, silver, slate|反义词:bright, colorful|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *gris, from Frankish *grīs, from Proto-Germanic *grīsa- (“gray”). Cognate with Old High German grīs (German grau), Old English græg (English gray).
