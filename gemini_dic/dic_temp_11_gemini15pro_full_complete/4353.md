akub:/ˈɑːkuːb/ | n. |  阿库布; 一种宽松的, 通常是白色的, 长及脚踝的衬衫或长袍，常见于尼日利亚的约鲁巴人穿着。 | A loose, typically white, ankle-length shirt or robe worn by Yoruba people in Nigeria. |  He wore a beautifully embroidered akub.  他穿着一件绣花精美的阿库布。| synonym:  robe, gown, dashiki | antonym:  trousers, pants | noun
