choriambic:/ˌkɔːriˈæmbɪk/
adjective
抑扬格与长短短格相间的
Relating to or composed of choriambs (a metrical foot consisting of a long syllable followed by two short syllables and then another long syllable).
The poet experimented with choriambic verse.  这位诗人尝试了抑扬格与长短短格相间的诗歌。
choriambic tetrameter  抑扬格与长短短格相间的四音步诗行
Near Synonyms: rhythmic, metrical
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a metrical term.  One could consider "prosaic" or "unmetrical" as loosely contrasting.)
词性: adjective
