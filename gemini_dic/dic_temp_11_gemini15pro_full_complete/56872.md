dos's:/duːz/
noun
规定; 注意事项; 行为准则
rules or customs concerning some activity or behavior.
Example: Here are some dos and don'ts for traveling abroad.  这里有一些出国旅行的注意事项。
Example: The company's dos and don'ts are clearly outlined in the employee handbook. 公司的规章制度在员工手册中都有明确规定。
Synonyms: rules, guidelines, regulations, etiquette
Antonyms: don'ts, prohibitions
