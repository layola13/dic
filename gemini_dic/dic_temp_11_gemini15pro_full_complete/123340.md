monolingualism:/ˌmɒnəʊˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm/ | n. |单语制，单一语言|The state of using only one language.  It refers to a situation where individuals or communities use only one language for communication in all aspects of their lives.|  Many isolated communities exhibit monolingualism. 许多孤立的社区都呈现出单语现象。|  Monolingual speakers often face challenges when traveling abroad. 单语使用者在出国旅行时经常面临挑战。| unilingualism | multilingualism, bilingualism | noun
