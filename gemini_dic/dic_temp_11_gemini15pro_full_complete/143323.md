outeaten:/ˌaʊtˈiːtn/
verb
吃得比…多; 比…吃得多
To have eaten more than someone or something else.
The caterpillar outeaten all the other insects in the garden. 这条毛毛虫比花园里所有其他昆虫都吃得多。
He outeaten his brother at the hot dog eating contest. 在热狗比赛中，他比他哥哥吃得更多。
Synonyms: outconsume, surpass (in eating)
Antonyms: be outeaten by
