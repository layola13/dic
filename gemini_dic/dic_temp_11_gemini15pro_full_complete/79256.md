glassily:/ˈɡlɑːsɪli/ | adv. | 光滑地；呆滞地；无表情地 | Smoothly and shinily, like glass; in a fixed or unfocused way, suggesting lack of awareness or emotion. |  She stared glassily at the wall, lost in thought. 她呆呆地盯着墙，陷入了沉思。|  The frozen lake shimmered glassily under the winter sun. 结冰的湖面在冬日阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词: blankly, vacantly, expressionlessly, glazedly | 反义词: expressively, animatedly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "glass" + "-y" (adjectival suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The root "glass" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *glasam.
