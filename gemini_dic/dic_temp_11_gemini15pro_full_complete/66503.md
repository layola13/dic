exfiltrate:/ˈɛksfɪltreɪt/ /ˈɛksfɪltreɪt/
verb
渗透；秘密撤离；过滤
To remove (someone or something, especially information) secretly from a dangerous or hostile place.
The spy exfiltrated classified documents from the embassy.  间谍从大使馆秘密窃取了机密文件。
The refugees were exfiltrated across the border at night. 难民在夜间被秘密转移过边境。
synonyms: smuggle out, spirit away, extract, remove, evacuate, withdraw
antonyms: infiltrate, introduce, import
词性: 动词


exfiltration:/ˌɛksfɪlˈtreɪʃən/ /ˌɛksfɪlˈtreɪʃən/
noun
渗透；秘密撤离；过滤
The act of exfiltrating someone or something.
The exfiltration of the hostages was planned meticulously. 人质的秘密撤离经过了周密的计划。
Data exfiltration is a major security concern for businesses. 数据泄露是企业面临的主要安全问题。
synonyms: smuggling, evacuation, removal, withdrawal
antonyms: infiltration, introduction, importation
词性: 名词
