exactnesses:/ɪɡˈzæktnəsɪz/
n.
精确性；准确性；一丝不苟

The lawyer questioned the exactnesses of the witness's testimony. 律师质疑证人证词的准确性。

近义词: precision, accuracy, correctness, meticulousness
反义词: inaccuracy, imprecision, vagueness, looseness

exactness:/ɪɡˈzæktnəs/
n.
精确性；准确性；一丝不苟
The state or quality of being exact; precision.
The scientist conducted the experiment with exactness.  科学家一丝不苟地进行了实验。

近义词: precision, accuracy, correctness, meticulousness

词源: From late Middle English *exactnes, from Old French exactesse, from Latin exactus (“exact, precise”), past participle of exigō (“I demand, require, weigh, measure”).
