marinades:/ˈmærɪneɪdz/| n.|腌泡汁，腌料|A marinade is a seasoned liquid in which food is soaked before cooking in order to flavor or tenderize it. |She marinated the chicken in a mixture of herbs and spices. 她把鸡肉浸泡在香草和香料的混合物中腌制。|The steak was delicious, thanks to the flavorful marinade. 多亏了美味的腌泡汁，牛排非常好吃。|近义词：sauce, dressing|反义词:|noun


marinade:/ˈmærɪneɪd/| v.|腌制|To soak food in a marinade. |I marinated the tofu for an hour before grilling it. 我把豆腐腌制了一个小时才烤。|She marinated the pork in a mixture of soy sauce, ginger, and garlic. 她用酱油、姜和大蒜的混合物腌制猪肉。|近义词：soak, steep|反义词:|verb
