extinct:/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/
adjective
灭绝的，绝种的
No longer existing; having no living members.
The dodo is an extinct bird. 渡渡鸟是一种已灭绝的鸟类。
The volcano is extinct. 这座火山是死火山。
Synonyms: died out, vanished, defunct, lost
Antonyms: extant, existing, living, surviving
adjective


extincts: /ɪkˈstɪŋkts/
This isn't a standard English word.  "Extinct" functions as both the singular and plural adjective. There is no noun form "extincts."  If you encountered this, it's likely a typo or misunderstanding.
