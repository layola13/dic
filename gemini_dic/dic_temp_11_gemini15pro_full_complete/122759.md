moisturized:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪzd/
adjective
滋润的，保湿的
Having had moisture added, making something soft and supple.
My skin feels so moisturized after using this lotion. 我的皮肤用完这款乳液后感觉非常滋润。
The cake was kept moist and delicious. 蛋糕保持湿润可口。

近义词:hydrated, damp, dewy, soft
反义词:dry, parched, arid
词性:adjective

词源:
From moisturize + -ed.  Moisturize comes from moist + -ize. Moist ultimately derives from the Latin *musteus, "fresh, new," related to mustum, "must" (new wine).
