nonprojecting:/ˌnɒn.prəˈdʒek.tɪŋ/ | adj. | 非突出的，不伸出的，内敛的 | Not projecting or protruding; recessed.  | The shelf was nonprojecting, fitting flush against the wall.  这个架子是非突出的，与墙壁齐平。|  The building features nonprojecting windows, minimizing heat loss.  这栋建筑的特点是非突出式窗户，最大限度地减少热量损失。 | 近义词: recessed, flush, inset | 反义词: projecting, protruding, jutting | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "projecting" (extending outward). "Projecting" comes from the Latin "projectus," the past participle of "proicere" (to throw forward), from "pro-" (forward) + "iacere" (to throw).
