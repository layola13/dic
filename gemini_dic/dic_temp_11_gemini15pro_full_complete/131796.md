nonconsciousness:/nɒnˈkɒnʃəsnəs/ /nɑːnˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/
n.
无意识，无知觉
lack of awareness or consciousness; the state of being unconscious.
The patient lapsed into a state of nonconsciousness after the surgery.  手术后，病人陷入了无意识状态。
Near-synonym: unconsciousness
Antonym: consciousness
noun
