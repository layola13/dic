homography:/həˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |单应性，同形异义词|A homography is a transformation (a warping) of one plane onto another plane. In computer vision, a homography is a matrix that maps the points in one image to the corresponding points in another image.|Example 1: The homography was used to stitch the two images together seamlessly.  单应性被用来无缝地拼接两张图像。

Example 2:  Calculating the homography allowed us to correct the perspective distortion in the image. 计算单应性使我们能够校正图像中的透视失真。| 近义词: transformation, mapping | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *homos* "same" + *-graphía* "writing".
