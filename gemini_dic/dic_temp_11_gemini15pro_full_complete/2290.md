adenose:/ˈædɪˌnoʊs/| n. |腺苷|A nucleoside that is a structural component of nucleic acids such as DNA and RNA. It is composed of adenine and ribose.|Adenosine plays a crucial role in energy transfer within cells. 腺苷在细胞内的能量转移中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: nucleoside |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific nucleoside) |词性: noun
