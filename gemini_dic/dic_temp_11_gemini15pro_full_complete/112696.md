lowliness:/ˈləʊlinɪs/
n.
谦逊，卑微，地位低下
the quality or state of being lowly in status or unimportant
He spoke of the lowliness of his birth. 他谈到了他出身的卑微。
近义词:humility, modesty, meekness, humbleness
反义词:haughtiness, arrogance, pride, loftiness
词性:noun
