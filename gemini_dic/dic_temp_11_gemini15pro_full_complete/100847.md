introspect:/ˌɪntrəˈspekt/ | v. |内省，反省|To examine one's own thoughts and feelings. |I spent some time introspecting on my recent behavior. 我花了一些时间反省我最近的行为。|  I needed to introspect about my own motivations. 我需要反省一下我自己的动机。 | 近义词: contemplate, reflect, ponder, meditate | 反义词: ignore, disregard |词性: 动词
