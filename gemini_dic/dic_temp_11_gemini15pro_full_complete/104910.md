noun
块豪石，钛硅钇钙石
A rare titanosilicate mineral containing yttrium, calcium, iron, and titanium.
Keilhauite is a complex mineral found in igneous rocks. 块豪石是一种在火成岩中发现的复杂矿物。
Keilhauite crystals can exhibit a range of colors, from brownish-black to reddish-brown. 块豪石晶体可以呈现出一系列颜色，从棕黑色到红棕色。
