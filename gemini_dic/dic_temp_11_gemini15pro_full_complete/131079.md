nonbelief:/ˌnɒnbɪˈliːf/ /ˌnɑːnbɪˈliːf/
n.
不信；无信仰；怀疑
Lack of belief, especially religious belief.
His nonbelief in God caused conflict with his religious family. 他对上帝的无信仰导致了他与他的宗教家庭的冲突。

Her nonbelief in traditional medicine led her to explore alternative therapies. 她对传统医学的不信任使她探索替代疗法。

近义词:disbelief, skepticism, unbelief, incredulity
反义词:belief, faith, credence, conviction
词性:名词
