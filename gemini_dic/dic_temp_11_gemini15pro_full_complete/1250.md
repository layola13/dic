accomptable: /əkˈkɔʊntəbl/ (British) /əˈkaʊntəbl/ (American)

| | | | Example: The government should be accomptable to the people. 政府应该对人民负责。


| Synonym: answerable, responsible, liable | Antonym: unaccountable, irresponsible, exempt |词性: 形容词
