invos: /ˈɪnvɒs/ (British English), /ˈɪnvɔːs/ (American English) | n. | 发票，发货单 | A document listing goods shipped or services provided, along with the charges and terms of sale. | Example: The supplier sent us an invos for the materials. 供应商给我们寄来了材料的发票。

Example: Please check the invos carefully before making the payment.  请在付款前仔细核对发票。

近义词: invoice, bill, statement | 反义词: receipt, payment | 词性: noun
