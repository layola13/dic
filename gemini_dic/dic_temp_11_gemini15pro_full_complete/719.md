absentest:/ˈæbsəntɪst/
adjective
缺席最多的
Most frequently absent.
例句：He was the absentest student in the class. 他是班上缺席最多的学生。
例句：She received the award for being the absentest employee. 她获得了缺席最多的员工奖。
近义词：truant, nonattendant
反义词：present, attendant
词性：adjective
