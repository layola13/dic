illusionists:/ɪˈluːʒənɪsts/
n.
魔术师；幻术师
A person who performs illusions, tricks that deceive the eye.
The illusionists amazed the audience with their incredible disappearing act.  魔术师们以令人难以置信的消失表演让观众惊叹。
The illusionists captivated the children with their sleight of hand. 魔术师们用他们的巧妙手法吸引了孩子们。

magicians, conjurers
charlatans, frauds
noun
