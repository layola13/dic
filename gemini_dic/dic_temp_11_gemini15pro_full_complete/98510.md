instantiation:/ɪnˌstænʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |实例化，具体化|The process of creating a specific instance or example of something, especially a class in object-oriented programming.|The instantiation of the class 'Dog' created a new object called 'Fido'.  实例化“Dog”类创建了一个名为“Fido”的新对象。|近义词：example, realization, materialization|反义词: abstraction, generalization |词性: noun
