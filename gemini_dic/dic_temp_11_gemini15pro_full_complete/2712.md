adsorbable:/ədˈsɔːr.bə.bəl/ /ədˈzɔːr.bə.bəl/
adj.
可吸附的
Capable of being adsorbed.  Adsorption is the adhesion of atoms, ions, or molecules from a gas, liquid, or dissolved solid to a surface.
Activated charcoal is adsorbable to many toxins.  活性炭可吸附许多毒素。
absorbable, adsorptive
non-adsorbable
adjective
