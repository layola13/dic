nontolerably: /nɒnˈtɒlərəbli/ | adv. | 不可容忍地；无法忍受地 |  Meaning "in a way that cannot be tolerated," formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the suffix "-ly" (forming adverbs) to the adjective "tolerable," which comes from the Latin "tolerabilis," meaning "bearable."  | The noise was nontolerably loud. 噪音大得令人无法忍受。|  The heat was nontolerably intense. 炎热酷暑令人难以忍受。| 近义词: unbearably, intolerably, insufferably | 反义词: tolerably, bearably, acceptably | 词性: 副词
