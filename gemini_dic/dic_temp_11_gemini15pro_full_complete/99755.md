intermeddlesome:/ˌɪntərˈmed(ə)lsəm/| adj.|爱管闲事的，好干预的|Given to interfering in other people's affairs; meddling.|例句：He's an intermeddlesome old man, always poking his nose into other people's business. 他是一个爱管闲事的老头，总是喜欢插手别人的事情。

例句：Her intermeddlesome mother-in-law was a constant source of friction in her marriage. 她爱管闲事的婆婆是她婚姻中持续不断的摩擦来源。|近义词：meddlesome, interfering, intrusive, officious|反义词：unintrusive, discreet, respectful|词性:形容词
