outstride:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪd/ | v. | 超越，大步超过 | To walk with longer steps than someone else and so go past them |  She easily outstrode her companions. 她轻松地超过了她的同伴。|  He outstrode everyone in the race. 他在比赛中超越了所有人。 | 近义词：outpace, surpass, overtake | 反义词：lag behind, trail | 词性：动词

词源:  From out- + stride.  "Out-" prefix indicates exceeding or surpassing. "Stride" refers to a long step in walking.
