clastic:/ˈklæstɪk/
adjective
碎屑的，由碎片组成的
Composed of fragments of pre-existing rock.
Clastic rocks are formed from weathered pieces of other rocks. 碎屑岩是由其他岩石的风化碎片形成的。
The geologist examined the clastic sediments. 地质学家检查了碎屑沉积物。
synonym:fragmental, detrital
antonym:nonclastic, crystalline
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *klastós ‘broken’, from klân ‘to break’.  源自希腊语 *klastós “破碎的”，来自 klân “打破”。
