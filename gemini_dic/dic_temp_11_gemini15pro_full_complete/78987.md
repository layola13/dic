girthing:/ˈɡɜːrθɪŋ/
n.
围长；测量周长；用带子捆绑
The act of measuring the circumference of something, especially the girth of a horse or other animal. Also, the act of fastening something with a girth.
The farmer was girthing the horse before riding.  农民在骑马前给马束上肚带。
measurement, circumference, binding
ungirthing
noun
