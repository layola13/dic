conflagration:/ˌkɒnfləˈɡreɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːnfləˈɡreɪʃn/| n. |大火，大火灾；冲突；暴乱|a large and destructive fire that threatens human life and property.  A situation that suddenly becomes violent or destructive.|例句：The conflagration swept through the city, destroying everything in its path. 大火席卷了整个城市，摧毁了沿途的一切。


例句：The political conflagration engulfed the entire region. 政治冲突席卷了整个地区。


|近义词：inferno, blaze, firestorm, holocaust|反义词:deluge (flood - contrasting element), peace, tranquility (opposite of conflict)|词性:noun
