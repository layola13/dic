legitimizes:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
使…合法化；使…正统化
To make something legitimate or lawful; to justify or validate something.
The government legitimizes the use of force in self-defense. 政府认可自卫使用武力是合法的。
The election legitimizes his presidency.  这次选举使他的总统职位合法化。
Synonyms: validates, justifies, authorizes, sanctions
Antonyms: invalidates, delegitimizes, condemns, prohibits
词性:动词
