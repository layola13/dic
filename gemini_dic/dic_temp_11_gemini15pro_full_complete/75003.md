friend: /frend/ | n. | 朋友 | A person whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection, typically exclusive of sexual or family relations. |  She's my best friend. 她是我最好的朋友。

| My friends and I went to the movies. 我和我的朋友们去看电影了。 | 近义词: companion, pal, buddy, mate | 反义词: enemy, foe | 词性: noun


friend: /frend/ | v. | 与...为友 | To add (someone) to a list of contacts associated with a social networking website. | I friended her on Facebook. 我在脸书上加了她为好友。

| He friended me on Twitter after we met at the conference.  我们在会议上认识后，他在推特上关注了我。 | 近义词: connect, befriend | 反义词: unfriend | 词性: verb
