assassin: /əˈsæsɪn/ | n. | 刺客，暗杀者 | A person who murders an important person for political or religious reasons. |  例句：The assassin was caught before he could reach his target.  刺客在接近目标之前被抓住了。

例句：The president was killed by an assassin. 总统被一名刺客暗杀。

近义词：murderer, killer, hitman | 反义词: protector, guardian | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *assassin*, from Arabic *ḥaššāšīn* "hashish-users," plural of *ḥaššāš*, from *ḥašīš* "hashish,".  Originally referring to members of the Nizari Ismaili sect, a Muslim group that allegedly carried out assassinations under the influence of hashish.  The term evolved to its general meaning over time.
