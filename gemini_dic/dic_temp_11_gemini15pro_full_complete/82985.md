gunnels:/ˈɡʌnəlz/ | n. | 船舷；甲板边；龙骨翼板；(复数)最底层 | The sides of a ship's hull at the deck level. Also, the lowest internal part of a vessel, where bilge water collects. | The ship listed so far over that the gunnels were almost underwater. 船倾斜得厉害，以至于船舷几乎都浸在水下了。| synonyms: gunwale, waterways, sheerstrake | antonyms: keel | noun

gunnel:/ˈɡʌnəl/ | n. |  玉筋鱼; 弹涂鱼 | A small, elongated, mostly marine fish of the family Pholidae, found in intertidal zones. |  The gunnel darted into a crevice in the rocks.  玉筋鱼迅速钻进岩石的缝隙里。 | synonyms: rock gunnel, butterfish |  | noun
