excel:/ɪkˈsɛl/ (v.) /ˈɛksəl/ (n.)| v. 擅长；胜过 n. 优秀；优异| to be very good at something; to do something better than most other people; the quality of being outstanding or extremely good|
例句：She excels at playing the piano. 她擅长弹钢琴。


例句：He excels in mathematics. 他擅长数学。


近义词：surpass, outdo, shine, triumph, exceed|
反义词：fail, underperform, lag behind|
词性：动词, 名词


excel:/ˈɛksəl/ | n. |优秀；优异| the quality of being outstanding or extremely good|
例句：Her academic excel is truly impressive. 她的学业成绩真的令人印象深刻。


例句：The school is known for its excel in sports. 这所学校以其在体育方面的优异而闻名。


近义词：excellence, superiority, merit, distinction|
反义词：mediocrity, inferiority, deficiency|
词性：名词


词源：From Old French *exceller, from Latin excellere (“to rise, surpass, excel”), from ex (“out”) + -cellere, related to celsus (“high”).
