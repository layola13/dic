mohr:/mɔːr/| n.|莫尔，摩尔硬度|A unit of hardness, especially of minerals, equal to 7 on the Mohs scale.  A value assigned to a material's scratch resistance relative to ten reference minerals.|Diamond has a Mohs hardness of 10. 钻石的莫氏硬度为10。|Synonyms: hardness, scratch resistance|Antonyms: softness|noun
