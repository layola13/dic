donorships:/ˈdoʊnərʃɪps/
n.
捐赠; 捐赠物; 捐赠行为; 捐助者身份
The act of donating something, or the status of being a donor.
The university relies heavily on alumni donorships to fund its research programs. 这所大学严重依赖校友捐赠来资助其研究项目。
Many charities depend on the generosity of individual donorships. 许多慈善机构依靠个人捐赠的慷慨解囊。
donation, contribution, gift, endowment
