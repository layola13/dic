dilettante:/ˌdɪləˈtænti/ /ˌdɪləˈtɑːnteɪ/ | n. adj. | 业余爱好者；浅尝辄止的人 | A person who cultivates an area of interest, such as an art or a branch of knowledge, without real commitment or knowledge.  A superficial and unserious approach to a subject. |  He's a bit of a dilettante as far as wine is concerned – he knows the basics but nothing in-depth.  就葡萄酒而言，他有点像个业余爱好者——他知道一些基础知识，但并不深入。| 近义词：amateur, dabbler, superficial | 反义词：expert, professional, connoisseur | 词性: noun, adjective

dilettante:/ˌdɪləˈtænti/ /ˌdɪləˈtɑːnteɪ/ | adj. | 业余爱好的；浅尝辄止的 | Superficial; not serious. |  He has a dilettante interest in photography. 他对摄影的兴趣是浅尝辄止的。 | 近义词：amateurish, superficial | 反义词：professional, expert | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *dilettante*, present participle of *dilettare* "to delight," from Latin *delectare*.
