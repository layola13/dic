noneuphoniously| adv.|不悦耳地，不和谐地|Not in a euphonious manner; unpleasantly or harshly to the ear.  The word is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "euphonious" (meaning "pleasant-sounding"), and then the adverbial suffix "-ly."  The root "euphonious" comes from the Greek "euphōnos," meaning "well-sounding."|Her voice, normally melodious, grated noneuphoniously on my ears that day. 那天，她通常悦耳的声音刺耳地传到我的耳朵里。|The rusty hinges creaked noneuphoniously as I opened the old gate. 当我打开旧大门时，生锈的铰链发出刺耳的声音。|近义词:cacophonously, discordantly, harshly, unmelodiously|反义词:euphoniously, melodiously, harmoniously|词性:副词
