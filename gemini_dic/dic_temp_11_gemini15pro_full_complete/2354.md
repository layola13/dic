adiabatic:/ˌeɪdiəˈbætɪk/ /ˌædiəˈbætɪk/| adj.|绝热的|Relating to a process or condition in which heat does not enter or leave the system concerned.|例句：The adiabatic expansion of a gas causes cooling. 气体的绝热膨胀导致冷却。|例句：An adiabatic process is one that occurs without transfer of heat or matter between a thermodynamic system and its surroundings. 绝热过程是指在热动力系统与其周围环境之间没有热量或物质传递的情况下发生的过程。|近义词：isentropic, heat-insulated|反义词：diathermic, isothermal|词性:形容词
adiabatic:/ˌeɪdiəˈbætɪk/ /ˌædiəˈbætɪk/| n.|绝热线|A curve on a graph representing the variation of temperature with pressure in an adiabatic process.|例句：The adiabatic on the chart shows the temperature change during the process. 图表上的绝热线显示了过程中温度的变化。|例句：The adiabatic for this specific gas is clearly marked.  这种特定气体的绝热线已明确标出。|近义词：isentropic curve|反义词：isothermal curve|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀδιάβατος (adiabatos, “impassable”) from *ἀ- (a-, “not”) + *διαβατός (diabatos, “passable”) from *διαβαίνω (diabainō, “I cross, pass through”) from *διά (dia-, “through”) + *βαίνω (bainō, “I go, walk, step”).
