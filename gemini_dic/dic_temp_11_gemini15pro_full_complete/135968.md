nonquantitativenesses:/nɒnˌkwɒntɪˈtætɪvnɪsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 非量化性；非定量性 |The nonquantitativenesses of certain aspects of human behavior make them difficult to study scientifically. 人类行为某些方面的非量化性使其难以进行科学研究。|  The report highlighted the nonquantitativenesses of the data collected, making statistical analysis challenging.  报告强调了收集数据的非量化性，使得统计分析具有挑战性。| near synonyms: imprecision,  qualitativeness | antonyms: quantifiability, measurability | noun
