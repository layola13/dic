karmic:/ˈkɑːrmɪk/
adjective
业力的；因果报应的
Relating to karma; resulting from or determined by karma.
She believes her illness is karmic retribution for past misdeeds. 她相信她的疾病是前世恶行的因果报应。
The band's sudden success felt karmic, like a reward for years of struggle.  这乐队的突然成功感觉是因果报应，像是对多年奋斗的回报。
近义词: destined, fated, preordained
反义词: coincidental, accidental, fortuitous
词性: adjective

karma:/ˈkɑːrmə/
noun
业；因果报应
(In Hinduism and Buddhism) the sum of a person's actions in this and previous states of existence, viewed as determining their fate in future existences.
He believes in karma and reincarnation. 他相信因果报应和轮回转世。
Bad karma will eventually catch up with you. 恶业最终会报应到你身上。
近义词: fate, destiny, retribution
反义词: (No direct antonym, but concepts like "grace" or "forgiveness" offer contrasting ideas)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Sanskrit *karma* कर्मन्  "deed, action, work, performance", from Proto-Indo-European *kʷer- (3) "to do, make, build".
