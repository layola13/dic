deputations:/ˌdɛpjuˈteɪʃənz/
n.
代表团，派遣，委托
A group of people appointed to represent others.
The deputation met with the mayor to discuss the new development project. 代表团会见了市长，讨论新的开发项目。
A deputation from the local community presented their concerns to the council.  当地社区的代表团向市议会提出了他们的担忧。
synonyms: delegation, mission, embassy, legation
antonyms: individual, single person
