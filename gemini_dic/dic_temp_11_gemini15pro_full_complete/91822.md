hypergamous:/haɪˈpɜːrɡəməs/| adj.| 超级嫁娶的，嫁娶高攀的 |  Relating to the practice of marrying or forming a relationship with someone of a higher social standing or greater wealth.  |  She comes from a hypergamous culture where women are expected to marry up. 她来自一个超级嫁娶的文化，女性被期望嫁给更高社会地位的人。

In some societies, hypergamous marriages are encouraged as a way to improve social standing. 在一些社会，超级嫁娶的婚姻被鼓励作为一种提高社会地位的方式。 | 近义词: social climbing, upwardly mobile | 反义词: hypogamous | 词性: adjective
