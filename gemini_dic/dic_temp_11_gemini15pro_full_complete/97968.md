innyard:/ˈɪnjɑːrd/ | n. | 内院；庭院；农家院落 | An enclosed yard within a larger property, typically associated with a farmhouse or other rural building.  |  The children played in the innyard while their parents worked in the fields. 孩子们在庭院里玩耍，而他们的父母在地里干活。 | The chickens pecked around the dusty innyard.  鸡在满是灰尘的庭院里啄食。 | 近义词: courtyard, farmyard, barnyard | 反义词:  (No clear antonym) | 词性: noun

词源:
From Middle English *inyerd, from Old English *inngeard (“inner yard”), equivalent to in + yard. Cognate with Dutch binnenplaats (“courtyard”), German Innenhof (“courtyard”).
