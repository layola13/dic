kaiyama:/kaɪˈjɑːmə/| n. |开山，开垦，创业| The act of starting something new, often involving overcoming significant obstacles, similar to pioneering or groundbreaking.  It derives from Japanese, combining "kai" (開, open) and "yama" (山, mountain), literally meaning "opening the mountain."  It can metaphorically refer to any challenging new endeavor.|

例句：His kaiyama in the tech industry led to a revolutionary new software.  他在科技行业的开创性工作带来了一款革命性的新软件。

例句：Her kaiyama of the new art gallery was met with critical acclaim. 她创办的新艺术画廊获得了评论界的好评。

近义词：pioneering, groundbreaking, innovation, initiation, undertaking|
反义词: stagnation, cessation, abandonment|
词性:noun
