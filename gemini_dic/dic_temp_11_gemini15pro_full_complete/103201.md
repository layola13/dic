jet-propelled:/ˌdʒɛt prəˈpɛld/| adj.|喷气推进的|Powered by a jet engine; propelled by the expulsion of hot gases from a nozzle.|例句：The jet-propelled airplane flew across the Atlantic. 喷气式飞机飞越大西洋。|近义词：rocket-powered, jet-driven|反义词: propeller-driven|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "jet" (from Old French "jetter" meaning "to throw") + "propelled" (from Latin "propellere" meaning "to drive forward").
