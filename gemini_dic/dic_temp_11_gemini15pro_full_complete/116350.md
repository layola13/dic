mastful:/ˈmæstfəl/ | adj. |精通的；熟练的；巧妙的| Showing great skill or mastery; expertly done. | She gave a masterful performance on the piano. 她钢琴演奏技艺精湛。|  近义词: skillful, adept, proficient, expert, accomplished | 反义词: clumsy, inept, unskilled, incompetent | 词性:形容词
词源:  From mast(n.) + -ful.  Originally referred to the skill of a ship's master.
