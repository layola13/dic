fragging:/ˈfræɡɪŋ/| n. |杀害（长官）; 自相残杀|The act of killing a superior officer, especially by a soldier. Also used more generally to refer to killing one's own side.|The reports of fragging during the Vietnam War were deeply disturbing.  越南战争期间关于自相残杀的报道令人深感不安。|Synonyms: murder, assassination, killing|Antonyms: protecting, saving|noun

fragging:/ˈfræɡɪŋ/| v. |杀害（长官）; 自相残杀|The act of killing a superior officer, especially by a soldier. Also used more generally to refer to killing one's own side.|He was accused of fragging his lieutenant. 他被指控杀害了他的中尉。|Synonyms: murder, assassinate, kill|Antonyms: protect, save|verb
