The documentary offered a nonsurrealistic portrayal of the war, avoiding sensationalism and focusing on the human impact of the conflict. 这部纪录片对战争进行了非超现实主义的描绘，避免了耸人听闻，而是关注冲突对人类的影响。| 近义词: realistic, representational, naturalistic | 反义词: surreal, surrealistic, dreamlike | 词性: 形容词
