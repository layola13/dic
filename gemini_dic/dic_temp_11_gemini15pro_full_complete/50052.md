denouncer:/dɪˈnaʊnsər/| n.|谴责者，告发者|A person who publicly accuses someone or something of being wrong, illegal, or immoral.  A person who informs against another.|The whistleblower denounced the company's corrupt practices to the authorities.  这个告密者向当局检举了公司的腐败行为。|synonyms: accuser, informant, critic, whistleblower|antonyms: supporter, defender, advocate|noun
