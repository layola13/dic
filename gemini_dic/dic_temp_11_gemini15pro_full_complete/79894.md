gnatling:/ˈnæt.lɪŋ/ | n. | 小昆虫；小烦人 | A small gnat or other similar tiny, irritating insect. |  The air was thick with gnatlings, making it difficult to enjoy the picnic. 空气中到处都是小飞虫，让人很难享受野餐。 | Synonyms: midge, mosquito, insect | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider larger animals like birds, mammals) | Noun

gnatling:/ˈnæt.lɪŋ/ | n. | 烦人精；讨厌鬼 (figurative) | An irritating or annoying person, especially a child. | That gnatling wouldn't stop whining until he got his candy. 那个烦人的小鬼直到拿到糖果才停止哭闹。| Synonyms: pest, nuisance, brat | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider pleasant person, angel) | Noun
