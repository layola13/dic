havenage:/ˈheɪvənɪdʒ/
n.
避难所，安全地
A place of safety or refuge.
例句：The old barn became a havenage for the homeless during the harsh winter.  这间旧谷仓在严冬期间成了无家可归者的避难所。

例句：The dense forest offered havenage from the pursuing soldiers. 茂密的森林为躲避追兵的士兵提供了庇护所。

近义词：sanctuary, refuge, asylum, shelter
反义词：danger, peril, threat
