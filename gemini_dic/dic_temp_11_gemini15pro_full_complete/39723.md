consilience:/kənˈsɪliəns/ | n. |  一致; 符合;  | The agreement between the results of different scientific studies; the linking together of principles from different disciplines, especially when forming a comprehensive theory. |  E.O. Wilson's book, "Consilience," argues for a unity of knowledge across disciplines.  E.O. Wilson的著作《一致》论证了跨学科知识的统一性。 |  convergence, coherence, agreement, unity, harmony |  discordance, disagreement, divergence, conflict | noun
