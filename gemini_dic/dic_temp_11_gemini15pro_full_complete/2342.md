adhesively:/ədˈhiːsɪvli/
adverb|粘合地，粘着地|in a way that sticks firmly|The label was adhesively attached to the bottle. 标签紧紧地贴在瓶子上。|近义词：stickily, firmly, tightly|反义词: loosely|词性:副词

Etymology: From "adhesive" + "-ly".  "Adhesive" comes from the Latin "adhaerere" meaning "to stick to".
