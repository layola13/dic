grotto:/ˈɡrɒtəʊ/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːtoʊ/ (US)|n.|洞穴，石窟|A natural or artificial cave, often a small picturesque one.|We explored a hidden grotto behind the waterfall. 我们探索了瀑布后面的一个隐蔽的洞穴。|cave, cavern, den|palace, mansion|noun
词源: From Italian *grotta*, from Late Latin *grupta*, from Greek *kryptē* "a vault, crypt," from *kryptein* "to hide."
