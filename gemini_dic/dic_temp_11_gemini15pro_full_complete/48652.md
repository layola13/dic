defenseman:/dɪˈfɛnsmən/| n. |防守员|A player on a sports team, especially in hockey or lacrosse, whose primary role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring.|例句：The defenseman blocked the shot and cleared the puck.  防守队员挡住了射门并将球击出。

例句：He's a skilled defenseman known for his physical play. 他是一位以身体对抗著称的技术娴熟的防守队员。|近义词：defender, back|反义词:forward, attacker|词性:noun
