asbestos:/æsˈbɛstɒs/ (UK), /æsˈbɛstəs/ (US)| n. |石棉|A heat-resistant fibrous silicate mineral that can be woven into fabrics, and is used in fire-resistant and insulating materials such as brake linings.  Exposure to asbestos fibers is a known cause of mesothelioma and lung cancer.|The workers wore protective gear when removing the asbestos. 工人们在清除石棉时穿戴着防护装备。|Synonyms: amphibole, chrysotile|Antonyms: None|Noun
