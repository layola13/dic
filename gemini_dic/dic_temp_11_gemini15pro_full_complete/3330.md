affording:/əˈfɔːrdɪŋ/
| v.|提供; 给予(机会);  买得起|
|Present participle of *afford*.  It describes the act of having enough money or resources to do something, or the act of providing something (like an opportunity).|
|The high balcony afforded a beautiful view of the city.  高高的阳台可以欣赏到美丽的城市景色。|
|provide, give, grant, offer, furnish|
|deny, refuse, withhold|
|verb|
