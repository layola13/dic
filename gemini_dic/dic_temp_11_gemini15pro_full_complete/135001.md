nonnecessities:/ˌnɑːnɪˈsɛsətiz/
n.
非必需品
things that are not essential for survival or basic comfort
例句：She cut back on spending on nonnecessities like new clothes and eating out. 她减少了在新衣服和外出就餐等非必需品上的开支。

例句：During the recession, many people had to forgo nonnecessities. 在经济衰退期间，许多人不得不放弃非必需品。

近义词：luxuries, nonessentials, frills
反义词：necessities, essentials, staples
词性：名词
