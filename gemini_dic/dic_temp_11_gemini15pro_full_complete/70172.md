fibre:/ˈfaɪbər/ (US) /ˈfaɪbə/ (UK)| n.|纤维；光纤；性格；素质|A thread or filament from which cloth, yarn, or rope is made. Also, a slender, elongated, thread-like structure. Can also refer to character or inherent quality.|例句：The sweater is made of natural fibre.  这件毛衣是由天然纤维制成的。

例句：He showed moral fibre in refusing the bribe. 他拒绝贿赂，表现出了高尚的品格。|近义词：thread, filament, strand, fiber (US spelling)|反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym for a physical material)|词性:noun
