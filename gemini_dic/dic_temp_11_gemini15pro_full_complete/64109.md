epoxy:/ɪˈpɒksi/ (US) /ˈɛpɒksi/ (UK)| n. adj.|环氧树脂；环氧的|A thermosetting resin made by polymerization using a reactive epoxide group.|例句：The boat was coated with epoxy resin for waterproofing. 这艘船涂了环氧树脂以防水。|近义词：resin, adhesive|反义词: (n.) solvent  (adj.) none |n. adj.
epoxy:/ɪˈpɒksi/ (US) /ˈɛpɒksi/ (UK)| adj.|环氧的|Containing or derived from epoxy resin.|例句：Epoxy paint is very durable. 环氧涂料非常耐用。|近义词：resinous|反义词: none|adj.
