impede:/ɪmˈpiːd/| v. |阻碍，妨碍|to delay or prevent (someone or something) by obstructing them; hinder.|The heavy traffic impeded our progress. 交通拥堵阻碍了我们的前进。|近义词：hinder, obstruct, block, delay|反义词：facilitate, aid, assist, expedite|词性:动词

impeded:/ɪmˈpiːdɪd/| adj. |受阻的，受妨碍的|slowed or prevented; obstructed.|The project's progress was impeded by a lack of funding.  这个项目的进展因缺乏资金而受阻。|近义词：hindered, obstructed, blocked|反义词：unimpeded, facilitated, aided|词性:形容词


词源: From Latin *impedīre, from in- (“in, on”) + pēs (“foot”).  Originally meant to shackle the feet.
