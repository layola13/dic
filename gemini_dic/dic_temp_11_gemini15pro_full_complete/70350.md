fiendishly:/ˈfiːndɪʃli/ | adv. | 恶魔般地，极其残忍地，极恶地 | In a very cruel or unpleasant way, like a fiend (an evil spirit). | He smiled fiendishly as he revealed his evil plan. 他恶魔般地笑着，揭示了他的邪恶计划。|  She laughed fiendishly at their misfortune. 她对他们的不幸遭遇幸灾乐祸地大笑。 |  diabolically, devilishly, wickedly, cruelly | kindly, gently | adverb

词源:
