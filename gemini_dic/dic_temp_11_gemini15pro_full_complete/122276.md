mitigatory:/ˈmɪtɪɡətɔːri/| adj.| 减轻的，缓和的|Making or intended to make something less harmful, serious, or painful.|The government is taking mitigatory steps to address the economic crisis. 政府正在采取缓解措施来应对经济危机。|近义词:alleviating, palliative, extenuating, remedial|反义词:aggravating, exacerbating|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *mitigatorius, from Latin mitigatus (past participle of mitigare ‘soften, alleviate’), from mitis ‘mild’ + -igare (frequentative suffix).
