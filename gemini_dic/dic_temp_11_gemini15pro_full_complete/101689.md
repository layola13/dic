irremissive:/ˌɪrɪˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
不可饶恕的; 不可撤销的
not capable of being forgiven or pardoned; unpardonable.  Not able to be changed, reversed, or revoked.
His irremissive attitude made reconciliation impossible. 他不可饶恕的态度使得和解不可能。
The judge handed down an irremissive sentence. 法官宣布了一项不可撤销的判决。
synonyms: unforgivable, unpardonable, inexcusable, implacable
antonyms: forgivable, pardonable, excusable, revocable
词性: adjective
