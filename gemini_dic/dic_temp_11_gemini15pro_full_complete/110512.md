limber: /ˈlɪmbər/ | adj. | 柔软的，灵活的 |  Supple; flexible. |  The dancer has a limber body. 舞者的身体很柔软。| synonyms: flexible, supple, agile, lithe | antonyms: stiff, rigid, inflexible | adjective
