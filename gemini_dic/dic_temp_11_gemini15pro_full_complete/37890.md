complaint: /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (US) /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (UK)| n.|抱怨，投诉，控告; 疾病|An expression of dissatisfaction or annoyance about something.  A formal accusation against somebody.| I filed a complaint with the airline about my lost luggage. 我向航空公司投诉了我的行李丢失问题.|synonyms: grievance, criticism, protest, objection|antonyms: praise, compliment|noun

complaint: /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (US) /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (UK)| adj.(古用法，现不常用)|爱抱怨的，不满的|Inclined to complain; querulous.| He had a complaint nature. 他生性爱抱怨 (古用法，现不常用).|synonyms: querulous, petulant, whining|antonyms: contented, satisfied|adjective
