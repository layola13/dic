cordelier:/kɔːrˈdɪlɪər/ |n.|科德利埃修士；系带；细绳|A Franciscan friar, especially one of the stricter observance. Also, a cord or rope, especially one worn as a belt or girdle.| Friar Tuck, a jovial cordelier, is a popular character in Robin Hood stories.  塔克修士，一位快乐的科德利埃修士，是罗宾汉故事中的一个受欢迎的角色。|Synonyms: Franciscan, monk, friar, rope, cord, girdle|Antonyms: layman|noun
