nonponderosity:/ˌnɒnpɒndəˈrɒsɪti/
noun
轻盈；无重状态
lack of ponderosity; lightness.
The nonponderosity of the balloon allowed it to float effortlessly into the sky.  气球的轻盈使它毫不费力地飘上了天空。
The dancer moved with a remarkable nonponderosity, seemingly defying gravity. 舞蹈演员的动作轻盈得惊人，似乎在对抗地心引力。
Synonyms: lightness, weightlessness, buoyancy, airiness
Antonyms: heaviness, weight, ponderosity
