brabbler:/ˈbræblər/| n. |饶舌者，喋喋不休的人|A person who talks incessantly and indiscreetly.|例句：He's such a brabbler, always gossiping about everyone. 他真是个饶舌的人，总是八卦每个人。

例句：The brabbler revealed the secret to everyone. 这个饶舌的人把秘密泄露给了所有人。|近义词：chatterbox, gossip, windbag|反义词：reticent person, quiet person|词性:noun

brabble:/ˈbræbəl/| v. |大声争吵，口角|To quarrel or argue noisily about unimportant things.|例句：They brabbled for hours over who should do the dishes. 他们为谁应该洗盘子吵了好几个小时。

例句：The neighbors were constantly brabbling about the noise. 邻居们不断地为噪音争吵。|近义词：squabble, bicker, wrangle|反义词：agree, concur, harmonize|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *brablen*, frequentative of *brabben* (“to brag, boast”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old French *braber* (“to cry, shout”).  First recorded use of "brabbler" (n.) was circa 1400.  First recorded use of "brabble" (v.) was circa 1530.
