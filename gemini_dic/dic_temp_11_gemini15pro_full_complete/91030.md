husker:/ˈhʌskər/ | v. | 剥(玉米等的)壳; | To remove the husks from ears of corn. |  We husked the corn and then cooked it. 我们剥了玉米然后煮了它。


She husked dozens of ears of corn for the market. 她为市场剥了几十个玉米棒子的壳。| 近义词: shuck, peel | 反义词: None | 词性: Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
