acclimatize: /əˈklaɪmətaɪz/ (US) /əˈklaɪmətaɪz/ (UK)| v.| 使适应；使习惯| To become accustomed to a new situation or condition.|  It took him a while to acclimatize to his new job. 他花了一段时间才适应新工作。| synonyms: adapt, adjust, accustom, habituate| antonyms: disadapt| verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acclimater*, from *a-* "to" + *climat* "climate," ultimately from Latin *climatis* (genitive of *clima*), from Greek *klima* "region, slope."
