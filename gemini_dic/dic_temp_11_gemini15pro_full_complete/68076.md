faddisms:/ˈfædɪzəmz/
noun
一时流行的狂热；时尚；风尚
Trends, especially short-lived ones, followed enthusiastically by many people.
Many teenagers are victims of faddisms. 很多青少年都是一时流行的狂热的受害者。
synonyms: trends, fashions, crazes, vogues, fancies
antonyms: traditions, classics, staples
