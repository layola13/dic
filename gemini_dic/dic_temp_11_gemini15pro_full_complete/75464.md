frumpish:/ˈfrʌmpɪʃ/
adjective
邋遢的；古板的；不修边幅的
(of a woman or her clothes) dowdy and old-fashioned.
例句：She looked rather frumpish in that old dress. 她穿着那件旧衣服看起来很不修边幅。

例句：He considered her hairstyle somewhat frumpish. 他觉得她的发型有点古板。

近义词：dowdy, drab, old-fashioned, unfashionable, shabby, messy, untidy
反义词：stylish, fashionable, chic, elegant, smart, sophisticated
词性：adjective
