noncompetitiveness:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnəs/
n.|无竞争力;缺乏竞争力|The noncompetitiveness of the company was due to its outdated technology and lack of innovation. 该公司的无竞争力是由于其技术过时且缺乏创新造成的。|例句：The noncompetitiveness of the local industry is a major concern for the government. 当地产业缺乏竞争力是政府主要关心的问题。|近义词：inability to compete, lack of competitiveness, weakness|反义词：competitiveness, strength|词性: 名词

Etymology:  From non- + competitiveness.  Competitiveness from competitive + -ness. Competitive from compete + -itive. Compete from Latin competere "to strive together," from com- "together" + petere "to strive for, seek."
