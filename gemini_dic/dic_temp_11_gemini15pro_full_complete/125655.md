mumming:/ˈmʌmɪŋ/ | n. | 假面剧；哑剧表演；乔装打扮 | The act of wearing a disguise and performing in a traditional play, often involving mime and visiting houses to solicit gifts.  It also refers to the disguises themselves. |  The village held a mumming play on Boxing Day. 村庄在节礼日那天举行了一场哑剧表演。 |  masquerade, disguise, charade |  disclosure, revelation | noun
