crusade:/kruːˈseɪd/| n. vi.| 十字军东征；（为理想或信念而进行的）斗争，运动|A medieval military expedition, one of a series made by Europeans between the 11th and 13th centuries to recover the Holy Land from the Muslims; a vigorous campaign for political, social, or religious change.|例句：He led a crusade against corruption. 他领导了一场反腐败运动。|近义词：campaign, struggle, fight|反义词：surrender, submission|noun
crusade:/kruːˈseɪd/| vi.| 参加十字军东征；为…而奋斗|To engage in a crusade.|例句：She crusaded for women's rights. 她为争取妇女权利而奋斗。|近义词：campaign, fight, advocate|反义词：yield, concede|verb
