confuter:/kənˈfjuːtər/
n.|反驳者；驳斥者|Someone who confutes another person's arguments or ideas. |The confuter presented compelling evidence to disprove the opponent's claims. 反驳者提出了令人信服的证据来反驳对手的主张。|Synonym: opponent, contradicter, refuter, gainsayer|Antonym: supporter, advocate, proponent|noun

confute:/kənˈfjuːt/
v.|驳斥；反驳；证明…有误|To prove (a statement, theory, or person) wrong. |The lawyer skillfully confuted the witness's testimony. 律师巧妙地驳斥了证人的证词。
He confuted the theory with a single, well-placed question. 他用一个恰当的问题驳倒了这个理论。|Synonym: disprove, refute, rebut, debunk|Antonym: prove, confirm, corroborate, validate|verb
