humourless:/ˈhjuːmələs/ (US) /ˈhjuːmələs/ (UK)| adj.|缺乏幽默感的；不开玩笑的|Lacking a sense of humor; unable to appreciate or express humor.|He's a humourless man who takes everything seriously. 他是一个缺乏幽默感的人，对一切都非常认真。|She told the joke, but he remained humourless. 她讲了笑话，但他仍然面无表情。|近义词:serious, stern, unamusing, dour, grim|反义词:humorous, funny, witty, amusing, lighthearted|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From humor + -less.  "Humor" comes from Middle French "humour"  meaning "moisture, fluid," from Latin "humor"  meaning "fluid." The meaning shifted to "temperament"  based on the ancient theory of the four humors influencing personality. "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
