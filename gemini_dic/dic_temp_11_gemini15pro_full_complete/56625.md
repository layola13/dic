dongola:/dɒŋˈɡoʊlə/ | n. |东戈拉皮革；一种浅棕褐色 |A fine, soft, flexible, and durable goatskin leather, originally from Dongola, Sudan, typically used for shoes and gloves.  Also refers to a light reddish-brown color. | Example: She wore a pair of elegant dongola shoes. 她穿着一双优雅的东戈拉皮鞋。

Example:  The walls were painted a warm shade of dongola.  墙被漆成了温暖的浅棕褐色。|Synonym:  (for leather) kidskin, goatskin; (for color)  tan, beige, fawn  |Antonym:  (for color) black, white | noun
