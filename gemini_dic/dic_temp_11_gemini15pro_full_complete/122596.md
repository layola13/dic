modernistic:/ˌmɑːdərˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 现代主义的；现代派的 |Of or relating to modernism; having the characteristics of modernism.  Characterized by a deliberate break with tradition and the exploration of new forms of expression. |  The building's modernistic design featured clean lines and geometric shapes. 这座建筑的现代主义设计以简洁的线条和几何形状为特色。

The museum's modernistic art collection showcased works from the early 20th century.  这家博物馆的现代主义艺术收藏展出了20世纪早期的作品。 | 近义词: modern, contemporary, avant-garde | 反义词: traditional, conventional, classic | 词性: adjective
