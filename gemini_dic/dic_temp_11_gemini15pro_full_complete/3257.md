affinity: /əˈfɪnɪti/ /əˈfɪnəti/ | n. |密切关系；吸引力；相似性 | A natural liking for or attraction to a person, thing, or idea; a close similarity between two things. | She has a natural affinity for water. 她天生喜欢水。| synonym: attraction, liking, rapport, sympathy, empathy, inclination, fondness, kinship, relationship | antonym: aversion, dislike, antipathy, repulsion | noun

affinity: /əˈfɪnɪti/ /əˈfɪnəti/ | n. |(尤指化学)亲合力 | (especially in chemistry) the force that causes substances to combine chemically. | The two chemicals have a strong affinity for each other. 这两种化学物质彼此之间有很强的亲和力。| synonym: attraction, chemical bond | antonym: repulsion | noun
