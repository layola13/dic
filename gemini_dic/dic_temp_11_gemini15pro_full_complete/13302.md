athetosis:/ˌæθəˈtoʊsɪs/| n. |手足徐动症|A condition characterized by involuntary, slow, writhing movements, especially of the hands and feet, often associated with cerebral palsy.|例句：The patient exhibited signs of athetosis, with constant twisting and turning of the fingers. 病人表现出手足徐动症的迹象，手指不断扭动。

例句：Athetosis can be a debilitating condition, making everyday tasks difficult. 手足徐动症可能是一种使人衰弱的疾病，使日常工作变得困难。|近义词：dyskinesia, dystonia|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "motor control" or "coordination")|词性:noun
