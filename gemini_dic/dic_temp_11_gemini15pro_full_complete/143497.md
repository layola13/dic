outgloom:/ˌaʊtˈɡluːm/
verb
使更阴沉，使更忧郁
to be gloomier than; to overshadow in gloom.
The dark clouds outgloomed the already gray sky.  乌云使本来就灰蒙蒙的天空更加阴沉。
The funeral procession outgloomed the entire town. 送葬队伍给整个城镇蒙上了一层阴影。
Synonyms: darken, overshadow, eclipse
Antonyms: brighten, lighten, cheer
词性:v.

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "gloom" (n. from Old English *glōm "twilight, dusk")
