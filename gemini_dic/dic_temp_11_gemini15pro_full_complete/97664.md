inhibition:/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. | 抑制，阻止；顾虑，拘束 | a feeling of embarrassment or worry that prevents you from saying or doing what you want |  After a few drinks, her inhibitions disappeared.  喝了几杯酒后，她不再拘束了。 | synonyms: restraint, constraint, reserve, suppression | antonyms:  freedom, spontaneity, impulsiveness | noun

inhibition:/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. | 禁止，阻止 | the act of inhibiting or the state of being inhibited | The government imposed inhibitions on trade with that country. 政府对与该国的贸易施加了限制。 | synonyms: prohibition, restriction, ban | antonyms: allowance, permission, authorization | noun
