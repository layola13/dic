medianly:/ˈmiːdiənli/
adv. |居中地，中间地|In a median position or manner; centrally.  Located in the middle. |The line divides the area medianly.  这条线将该区域平分。|近义词：centrally, midway|反义词: peripherally, edgewise|词性:副词

Etymology:
From median + -ly.  "Median" comes from Late Latin *mediānus, from Latin medius “middle”.
