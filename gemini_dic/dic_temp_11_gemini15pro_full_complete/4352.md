akrotiri:/əˈkrɒtɪri/| n.|阿克罗蒂里|A Bronze Age settlement on the volcanic Greek island of Thera (modern Santorini), destroyed in the Theran eruption sometime in the 17th century BC and rediscovered in 1867. It is often referred to as the "Minoan Pompeii" due to its remarkably preserved state, offering valuable insights into Minoan civilization.|The excavations at Akrotiri have revealed stunning frescoes and sophisticated architecture. 阿克罗蒂里的挖掘工作揭示了令人惊叹的壁画和复杂的建筑。|The volcanic ash that buried Akrotiri preserved it in much the same way as Pompeii.  火山灰掩埋了阿克罗蒂里，使其保存得像庞贝一样完好。|近义词:Thera, Minoan city|反义词:|n.
