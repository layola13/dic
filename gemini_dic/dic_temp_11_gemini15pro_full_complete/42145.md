cotyledonous: /ˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ | adj. | 子叶的；有子叶的 | Having cotyledons; relating to or resembling a cotyledon. | Example: Dicotyledonous plants have two cotyledons. 双子叶植物有两片子叶。| Example: The cotyledonous stage is crucial for seedling development. 子叶阶段对幼苗发育至关重要。| Synonyms:  seed-leaved | Antonyms: acotyledonous | word type: adjective

cotyledon: /ˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. | 子叶 |  The embryonic leaf in seed-bearing plants, one or more of which are the first leaves to appear from a germinating seed. | Example:  The bean's cotyledons store food for the developing plant.  豆类的子叶储存食物以供植物发育。| Example: Monocots have one cotyledon, while dicots have two. 单子叶植物有一片子叶，而双子叶植物有两片。| Synonyms: seed leaf | Antonyms: N/A | word type: noun
