glycerol:/ˈɡlɪsərɒl/ (UK) /ˈɡlɪsərɔːl/ (US)| n.|甘油；丙三醇|A sweet, syrupy liquid obtained as a by-product in soap manufacture, also used as an emollient and laxative and in making explosives.|Glycerol is used in many cosmetic products. 甘油用于许多化妆品中。

Glycerol is a common ingredient in lotions. 甘油是乳液中的常见成分。

近义词：glycerin|反义词:|词性:noun
