bilimbi:/bɪˈlɪmbi/ | n. | 比林比，杨桃 | A small, sour fruit of a tree native to Southeast Asia, used in curries and pickles. |  例句：Bilimbi is often used to make chutneys. 比林比经常被用来制作酸辣酱。

例句：She added bilimbi to the curry for a tangy flavor.  她在咖喱里加了比林比，以增加浓郁的味道。
