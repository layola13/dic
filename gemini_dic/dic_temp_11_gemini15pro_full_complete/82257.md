grounder:/ˈɡraʊndər/| n.| 地滚球；基本问题；接地球 |A batted baseball that rolls or bounces along the ground; a basic question or fact; an act of grounding a ball. |例句：The shortstop fielded the grounder cleanly.  游击手干净利落地接住了地滚球。

例句：He asked me a real grounder about my past. 他问了我一个关于我过去的很基本的问题。 |近义词：roller, bouncer, fundamental, basic |反义词: fly ball, pop-up (棒球方面) |词性:noun

grounder:/ˈɡraʊndər/| vt.| 使…接地；罚…不准外出 |To cause (a ball) to roll or bounce along the ground; to punish by forbidding to go out. |例句：He grounded the ball to the second baseman.  他把球击成地滚球到二垒手。

例句：His parents grounded him for a week. 他父母罚他一个星期不准出门。 |近义词：ground, confine |反义词: allow out |词性:verb
