loopiness:/ˈluːpinəs/| n.|古怪；傻气；疯癫|The state of being loopy; craziness or eccentricity.|Her loopiness was endearing to some, but irritating to others. 她疯疯癫癫的性格，有些人觉得可爱，有些人觉得讨厌。|Synonyms: craziness, eccentricity, silliness, zaniness, flakiness|Antonyms: sanity, normalcy, seriousness, stability|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From loopy + -ness.  Loopy itself likely comes from "loop" (referring to something not quite right, like a loop in reasoning or behavior).
