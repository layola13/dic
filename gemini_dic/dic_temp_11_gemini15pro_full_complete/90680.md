humbuckers:/ˈhʌmbʌkərz/
n.
双线圈拾音器
A type of electric guitar pickup that uses two coils to reduce noise and produce a thicker, more powerful sound.
My Les Paul has a set of vintage humbuckers. 我的莱斯·保罗吉他装有一套复古双线圈拾音器。

He replaced the single-coil pickups in his Stratocaster with humbuckers for a fatter tone. 他把他Stratocaster吉他上的单线圈拾音器换成了双线圈拾音器，以获得更丰满的音色。

近义词:double-coil pickups
反义词:single-coil pickups
词性:名词
