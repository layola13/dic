anodizing:/ˈænəˌdaɪzɪŋ/
n.|阳极氧化|An electrochemical process that forms a protective oxide layer on the surface of a metal, typically aluminum.  This layer increases corrosion resistance and can be dyed for decorative purposes.|The anodizing of the aluminum parts increased their lifespan significantly.  铝制零件的阳极氧化显着延长了它们的使用寿命。|近义词:oxidation|反义词:cathodizing|词性:名词

anodize:/ˈænəˌdaɪz/
vt.|阳极氧化|To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process.|We anodized the metal to protect it from rust. 我们对金属进行了阳极氧化处理以防止生锈。|近义词:oxidize|反义词:N/A|词性:动词
