dyewood:/ˈdaɪˌwʊd/| n. |染木；染料木|A tree or shrub yielding a dye.|The dyewood logwood was once a major export of several Caribbean countries.  染料木原木曾经是几个加勒比海国家的主要出口商品。|Example:  Many vibrant textiles were traditionally dyed using extracts from dyewoods. 许多鲜艳的纺织品传统上都是用染木提取物染色的。|Synonym: dye plant, dyestuff|Antonym: N/A|noun
