glengarry:/ɡlɛnˈɡæri/| n.|格伦加里帽，一种苏格兰帽|A glengarry is a traditional Scottish cap, usually made of wool, with a flat crown, a crease down the middle, ribbons at the back, and often a toorie (a small, pom-pom-like ball) on top.|He wore a glengarry at the Highland Games. 他在高地运动会上戴了一顶格伦加里帽。|Synonyms: Scottish cap, Balmoral bonnet|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Glengarry, a valley in the Scottish Highlands.
