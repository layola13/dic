incitant:/ɪnˈsaɪtənt/ /ɪnˈsaɪtənt/
noun|刺激物；煽动者|Something that incites or stimulates; a person who incites. |The politician's fiery speech acted as an incitant to the crowd.  这位政治家的激烈演讲对人群起到了煽动作用。|Synonyms: stimulus, instigator, provoker, agitator|Antonyms: suppressant, deterrent, inhibitor|n.

incitant:/ɪnˈsaɪtənt/ /ɪnˈsaɪtənt/
adjective|刺激的；煽动的|Tending to incite or stimulate.|His incitant words caused a riot. 他煽动性的话引发了一场骚乱。|Synonyms: stimulating, provocative, inflammatory|Antonyms: calming, soothing, pacifying|adj.
