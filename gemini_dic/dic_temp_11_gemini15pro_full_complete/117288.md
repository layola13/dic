mechatronics:/ˌmɛkəˈtrɒnɪks/ /ˌmiːkəˈtrɑːnɪks/| n. |机电一体化|A multidisciplinary field of engineering that integrates mechanical engineering, electronics, computer engineering, control engineering, systems design and technical management.| Mechatronics plays a crucial role in the development of advanced manufacturing systems. 机电一体化在先进制造系统的发展中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: robotics, automation, control systems engineering |Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider traditional engineering disciplines individually, e.g., purely mechanical systems)| noun
