nocturnary:/ˈnɑːkˌtɜːrnəri/  /nɒkˈtɜːnəri/ | adj. | 夜行的；夜间活动的 | Active at night. | Bats are nocturnal animals.  蝙蝠是夜行动物。 |  Nocturnal insects fly around the light. 夜行昆虫绕着灯光飞舞。 | 近义词：night-time, crepuscular | 反义词：diurnal | 词性：形容词

noctuary:/ˈnɑːkˌtʃʊəri/ /nɒkˈtʃʊəri/| n. | 夜曲；夜间祈祷 | A piece of music, especially for the piano, that is expressive and suitable for playing at night; a service of prayer held at night. | Chopin's nocturnes are beautiful and evocative. 肖邦的夜曲优美而动人。|  The monks gathered in the chapel for the noctuary.  僧侣们聚集在小教堂里做夜间祈祷。 | 近义词：serenade | 反义词： (无明确反义词) | 词性：名词
