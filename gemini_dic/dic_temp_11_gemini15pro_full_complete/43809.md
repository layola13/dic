crengle:/ˈkrɛŋɡəl/ | n. | 环; 圈; 金属环 | A loop, ring, or eye of metal or rope, especially one used on a ship. | The sailor secured the rope to the crengle on the deck. 水手把绳子固定在甲板上的金属环上。 |  The ship's sails were attached to the mast by strong crengles. 船帆通过结实的金属环固定在桅杆上。 | synonym: loop, ring, eye, grommet | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "straight line" in a metaphorical sense) | noun
