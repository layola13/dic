过度怀疑
Excessive skepticism or doubtfulness.
His overscepticalnesses prevented him from embracing new ideas.  他过度的怀疑让他无法接受新想法。
近义词: cynicism, distrust, incredulity
反义词: credulity, trust, faith
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- (excessive) + sceptical + -ness (forming nouns of state or quality).  Sceptical ultimately derives from the Greek skeptikos, meaning "inquiring" or "thoughtful".
