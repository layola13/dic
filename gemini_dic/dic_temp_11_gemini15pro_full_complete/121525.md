mismade:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪd/
adjective
做坏了的，拙劣的
badly or incorrectly made
The dress was mismade and had to be altered. 这件衣服做坏了，必须修改。
近义词：faulty, defective, flawed
反义词：well-made, perfect
词性：形容词

词源：
From mis- + made, past participle of make.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "badly," or "wrongly." "Made" comes from the Old English "macian," meaning "to make" or "to construct."
