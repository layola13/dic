gypsography: /dʒɪpˈsɒɡrəfi/
noun
石膏地貌学; 石膏地形学
The study and description of landforms composed of gypsum or influenced by gypsum processes.
Gypsography plays a crucial role in understanding karst landscapes in arid and semi-arid regions.  石膏地貌学在理解干旱和半干旱地区的喀斯特地貌中起着至关重要的作用。
The gypsography of the White Sands National Monument is fascinating. 白沙国家纪念碑的石膏地貌令人着迷。
近义词: karst topography, gypsum geomorphology
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

Etymology: From Greek "gypsos" (gypsum) + "-graphy" (description). 源于希腊语 "gypsos"（石膏）+ "-graphy"（描述）。
