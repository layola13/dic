neuropodium:/ˌnjʊərəˈpoʊdiəm/ | n. | 神经足 | One of the paired lateral processes on each segment of a polychaete worm, bearing chaetae.  A parapodium's ventral lobe. | The neuropodium of this species of worm is particularly well-developed.  这种蠕虫的神经足特别发达。 | Synonym: ventral parapodium | Antonym: notopodium | noun
