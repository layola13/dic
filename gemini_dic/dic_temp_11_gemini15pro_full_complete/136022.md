nonrealistically:/ˌnɒnriːəˈlɪstɪkli/
adverb
不切实际地
in a way that is not realistic or practical
例句：
The project was nonrealistically budgeted.  项目预算不切实际。

He nonrealistically expected to finish the work in a day. 他不切实际地期望一天内完成工作。

近义词:impractically, unrealistically, quixotically
反义词:realistically, practically, pragmatically
词性:adverb
