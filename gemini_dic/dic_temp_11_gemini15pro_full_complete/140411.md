ojibwe:/oʊˈdʒɪbweɪ/ | n. adj.|奥吉布瓦人；奥吉布瓦语|A member of a Native American people inhabiting the region around Lake Superior and westward to the Turtle Mountains. Also refers to their Algonquian language.|例句：He is an Ojibwe from Minnesota. 他是来自明尼苏达州的奥吉布瓦人。|近义词：Chippewa, Anishinaabe|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑其他族群如Sioux, Cree等)|词性:名词/形容词
