innovation:/ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən/ | n. |革新，创新；新方法|The introduction of new things, ideas or ways of doing something.| Electric cars are a great innovation. 电动汽车是一项伟大的创新。| synonyms: novelty, modernization, revolution, transformation | antonyms: stagnation, tradition, conventionalism | noun

innovation:/ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən/ | n. |新事物|Something newly introduced, such as a new method or device.| This innovation in technology has revolutionized communication.  这项技术创新彻底改变了通信方式。| synonyms: invention, discovery, breakthrough | antonyms: imitation, copy, duplication | noun
