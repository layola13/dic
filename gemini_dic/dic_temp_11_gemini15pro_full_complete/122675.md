modulator:/ˈmɑːdʒəleɪtər/ (US), /ˈmɒdjʊleɪtər/ (UK)| n. |调制器；调节器|A device or substance that modulates something, especially a carrier wave in telecommunications or a physiological process.|The modulator adjusts the signal strength for optimal transmission. 调制器调整信号强度以实现最佳传输。|The liver acts as a modulator of blood glucose levels. 肝脏充当血糖水平的调节器。|synonyms: regulator, controller, modifier|antonyms: demodulator|noun
