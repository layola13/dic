engaging:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ/| adj.|迷人的，引人入胜的，有魅力的|Attracting or delighting|Her engaging smile captivated the audience. 她迷人的微笑吸引了全场观众。|近义词：captivating, charming, interesting, appealing|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French "engaiger" (to pledge, pawn, mortgage, bind by oath), from "en-" (in) + "gage" (pledge). The meaning shifted to "occupy, involve" in the 16th century, and later to "attract and hold the attention."
