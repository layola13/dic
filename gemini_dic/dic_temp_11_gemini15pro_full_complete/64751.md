escritoire:/ˌɛskrɪˈtwɑːr/ /ˌɛskrɪˈtwɔːr/
noun
写字台，书桌
A writing desk, often with drawers and compartments, sometimes having a sloping top that can be opened to form a writing surface.
She sat at her escritoire, penning a letter to her sister. 她坐在写字台前，给妹妹写信。
The antique escritoire was inlaid with mother-of-pearl.  这古董写字台镶嵌着珍珠母。

近义词:desk, writing desk, writing table, bureau
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "table" in a general sense)
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *escritoire*, from Old French *escretoire*, from *escrire* "to write," from Latin *scrībō*. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\skreybh-* (to cut, write, draw).
