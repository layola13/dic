climbdown:/ˈklaɪmdaʊn/ (US), /ˈklaɪmˌdaʊn/ (UK)| n.|让步，妥协；退却|An act of admitting that you were wrong or of changing your position on something, especially to avoid a bad situation.|The government was forced into a climbdown on its tax proposals. 政府被迫在其税收提案上做出让步。|近义词：concession, retreat, backdown, compromise|反义词：insistence, refusal, holding firm|词性:名词

climbdown:/ˈklaɪmdaʊn/ (US), /ˈklaɪmˌdaʊn/ (UK)| n.|撤退；下降；|The act of climbing down.|The climbdown from the mountain was difficult. 从山上下来很困难。|近义词：descent|反义词：ascent, climb|词性:名词
