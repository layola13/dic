mesquite:/mɛˈskiːt/ | n. | 牧豆树；牧豆树木材 | A thorny shrub or small tree of the pea family, native to arid regions of the Americas and cultivated elsewhere for its edible pods and as a source of timber and fuel. | The mesquite tree is highly adapted to desert environments.  牧豆树非常适应沙漠环境。|  The carpenter used mesquite wood to build the table.  木匠用牧豆树木材制作桌子。 | synonyms:  prosopis, algarrobo | antonyms:  N/A | noun


Etymology->
From Spanish *mezquite*, from Nahuatl *mizquitl*.
