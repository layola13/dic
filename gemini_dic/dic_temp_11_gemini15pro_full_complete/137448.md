nonthermoplastic:/ˌnɒnˌθɜːməˈplæstɪk/
adjective
非热塑性的
Not capable of being softened or molded by heat and of returning to substantially the same state upon cooling.  These materials undergo chemical changes when heated, generally becoming permanently harder.
Epoxy resins are nonthermoplastic.  环氧树脂是非热塑性的。
Near Synonyms: thermoset, thermosetting
Antonyms: thermoplastic
词性: adjective
