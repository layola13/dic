outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/
verb
抢先报道；比…先得到独家新闻
To report a news story before (someone else), especially a competitor.
The local newspaper outscooped the national media on the story. 当地报纸抢在国家媒体之前报道了这条新闻。
outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/
近义词: beat, scoop
反义词: be scooped
