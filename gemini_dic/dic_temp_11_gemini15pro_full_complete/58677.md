dumont:/ˈduːmɒnt/ | n. | 杜蒙（姓氏）| A surname of French origin.  It can also refer to places named after people with this surname. |  There are several towns named Dumont.  有好几个城镇名叫杜蒙。 | 近义词: (As a surname, it has no direct synonyms.  作为姓氏，它没有直接的同义词。) | 反义词: (As a surname, it has no direct antonyms.  作为姓氏，它没有直接的反义词。) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old French, meaning "of the mountain". Ultimately derived from Latin *de monte*. 源于古法语，意为“来自山”。最终源于拉丁语 *de monte*。
