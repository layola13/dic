inclined-to:/ɪnˈklaɪnd tuː/
adjective
倾向于的，有…意向的
Having a natural tendency or disposition towards something; likely or wanting to do something.
She is inclined to worry too much. 她总是容易过度担心。
Synonyms: prone to, disposed to, apt to, given to, liable to
Antonyms: disinclined to, averse to, resistant to
词性: adjective
