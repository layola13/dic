moresk:/ˈmɔːrɪsk/| adj. n.|摩尔式的，摩尔式装饰；|Of or relating to the Moors or their art or culture.  A type of arabesque ornamentation. |The building was decorated with intricate moresk patterns. 这座建筑装饰着复杂的摩尔式图案。|近义词：arabesque, Moorish|反义词: None|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Middle French *moresque*, from Italian *moresca* (“Moorish dance”), from *moro* (“Moor”), ultimately from Latin *Maurus* ("inhabitant of Mauretania").
