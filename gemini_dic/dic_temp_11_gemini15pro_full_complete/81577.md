grease: /ɡriːs/ /ɡris/ | n. v. |油脂；润滑油；贿赂 | A thick oily substance, especially as used as a lubricant.  To lubricate with grease. | The mechanic greased the axle. 技工给车轴上了润滑油。| 近义词：lubricant, oil, fat | 反义词：cleaner, solvent | 词性：名词, 动词 | 词源：From Old French *gresse, from Late Latin crassa, feminine of crassus ‘fat’.

greasy: /ˈɡriːsi/ /ˈɡrisi/ | adj. |油腻的；滑溜溜的；谄媚的 | Covered with or containing grease; oily. | The food was too greasy for me.  这食物对我来说太油腻了。| 近义词：oily, slippery, unctuous | 反义词：clean, dry | 词性：形容词 | 词源：From grease + -y.
