overpurchases:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrtʃəsɪz/
n.
过度购买；过量采购
The act of buying more than is needed or can be used.
They had to mark down prices to clear out the overpurchases.  他们不得不降低价格来清理过量采购的商品。

Overpurchases can lead to financial difficulties. 过度购买会导致经济困难。

近义词: overbuying, excess purchases
反义词: underpurchases

词性: noun
