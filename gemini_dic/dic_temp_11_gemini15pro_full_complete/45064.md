cubic:/ˈkjuːbɪk/| adj.|立方的，三次的|A cubic equation involves the third power of an unknown quantity. 立方方程包含一个未知量的三次方。|The volume of the box is 10 cubic meters. 这个盒子的体积是10立方米。|近义词:three-dimensional|反义词:linear, square|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Late Latin *cubicus, from Latin cubicus (“cubical, cubic”), from cubus (“cube”), from Ancient Greek κύβος (kúbos, “cube, die”).
