nonsusceptibility:/ˌnɑːnsəˌsɛptəˈbɪləti/
n.
无敏感性；不易感性；不受影响
lack of susceptibility, especially to a disease or drug.  The state or quality of not being susceptible.
The patient demonstrated nonsusceptibility to the antibiotic.  这个病人对这种抗生素表现出无敏感性。
nonsusceptibility to persuasion  不易受劝说
resistance, immunity, imperviousness
susceptibility, sensitivity, vulnerability
noun
