The heterogeneous mixture contained various sizes of rocks and sand. 这种异质混合物包含各种大小的岩石和沙子。 | 近义词: diverse, varied, mixed, dissimilar, miscellaneous | 反义词: homogeneous, uniform, similar | 词性: 形容词
