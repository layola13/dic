coactively:/koʊˈæktɪvli/
adv.|共同地，强制地|Acting together or by joint action, especially by compulsion.|The government coactively imposed new regulations on businesses.|近义词: jointly, concurrently, compulsorily|反义词: individually, separately, voluntarily|词性:副词

coact:/koʊˈækt/
v.|共同行动，强制|To act together or in concert; to compel to action.|Several agencies coacted to implement the new policy.|近义词: collaborate, cooperate, compel|反义词: resist, oppose, obstruct|词性:动词

coaction:/koʊˈækʃən/
n.|共同行动，强制|The act of acting together or in concert; compulsion.|The coaction of the two companies led to the development of a new product.|近义词: collaboration, cooperation, compulsion|反义词: resistance, opposition, obstruction|词性:名词
