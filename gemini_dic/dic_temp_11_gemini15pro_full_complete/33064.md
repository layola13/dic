chowing-down:/ˈtʃaʊɪŋ daʊn/|Informal Verb|大吃特吃，狼吞虎咽|The act of eating enthusiastically and heartily.|例句：We were chowing down on pizza after the game.  我们在比赛后大吃披萨。

例句：They were chowing down on burgers and fries. 他们在狼吞虎咽地吃汉堡和薯条。|近义词：gobbling, devouring, pigging out, stuffing oneself|反义词: nibbling, picking at|词性:动词短语
