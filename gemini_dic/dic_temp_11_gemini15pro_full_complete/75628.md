fugitively:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪvli/ | adv. | 逃亡地，短暂地 | in a way that suggests someone is running away or hiding from the law, or lasting for only a short time | He glanced fugitively around the room. 他迅速地环顾了一下房间。 | 近义词：fleetingly, transiently, elusively, briefly | 反义词：permanently, enduringly | 词性：副词

fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪv/ | n. adj. | 逃犯，逃亡的；短暂的，易逝的 |  n. a person who has escaped from a place or is in hiding, especially to avoid arrest or persecution; adj. lasting for a short time; fleeting | The fugitive was apprehended by police. 逃犯被警察逮捕了。 | 近义词：(n.) escapee, runaway, deserter; (adj.) fleeting, transient, ephemeral | 反义词：(n.) captive; (adj.) permanent, enduring | 词性：名词，形容词

词源: From Late Latin *fugitīvus, from Latin fugitīvus (“fleeing, fugitive”), from fugit (“he flees”), third-person singular present active indicative of fugiō (“I flee”).
