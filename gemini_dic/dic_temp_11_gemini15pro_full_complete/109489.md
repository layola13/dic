leptiform:/ˈlɛptɪfɔːrm/
adjective
瘦长的，细长的；癫痫样的
Resembling or characteristic of epilepsy or epileptic seizures.  Having a slender or thin form.
The patient exhibited leptiform movements during the seizure. 病人在癫痫发作期间表现出癫痫样的动作。
The plant's leptiform leaves helped it conserve water. 这种植物细长的叶子有助于它保存水分。
近义词: slender, thin, elongated, epileptiform
反义词: thick, broad, stout
