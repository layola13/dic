epaulette:/ˈɛpəlɛt/ | n. | 肩章，肩饰 | An ornamental shoulder piece on an item of clothing, typically on the coat or jacket of a military uniform. | The general's epaulettes glittered in the sunlight. 将军的肩章在阳光下闪闪发光。 |  The captain adjusted his epaulettes before addressing the troops. 上尉在向部队讲话前调整了他的肩章。 | synonyms: shoulder piece, shoulder strap, shoulder board, shoulder knot | antonyms: N/A | noun
