exhausting:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪŋ/| adj.|使人精疲力竭的；令人疲惫不堪的|Making one feel very tired; draining one's physical or mental energy.|例句：The hike up the mountain was exhausting.  爬山真是累人。|近义词：tiring, draining, strenuous, demanding|反义词：refreshing, invigorating, energizing|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From exhaust + -ing.  Exhaust comes from the Latin "exhaurire," meaning "to draw out completely, drain, empty."
