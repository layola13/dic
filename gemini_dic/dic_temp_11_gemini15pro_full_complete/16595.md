barogram:/ˈbærəɡræm/ | n. |气压图；气压记录图 | A chart made by a barograph, showing the variations of atmospheric pressure over a period of time. | The barogram showed a steady drop in pressure, indicating an approaching storm. 气压图显示气压持续下降，预示着暴风雨即将来临。 |  近义词: barograph trace, pressure chart | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: baro- (pressure) + -gram (something written or recorded)  源自希腊语 báros（重量）+ gramma（书写的东西）
