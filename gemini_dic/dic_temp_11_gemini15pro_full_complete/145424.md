overcharger:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒər/| n.|超额收费者；敲竹杠者|A person or business that charges too much for goods or services.|The mechanic was an overcharger, adding unnecessary repairs to inflate the bill.  这个机修工是个敲竹杠的，他增加了不必要的维修项目来抬高账单。|Synonyms: profiteer, gouger|Antonyms: discounter|noun

overcharge:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒ/| vt.|索价过高；过量装填|To charge someone more than a fair price for goods or services.|The taxi driver tried to overcharge us, but we knew the correct fare.  出租车司机想敲我们竹杠，但我们知道正确的车费。|Synonyms: gouge, fleece, soak|Antonyms: undercharge, discount|verb
