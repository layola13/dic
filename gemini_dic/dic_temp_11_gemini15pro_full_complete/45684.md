curtness:/ˈkɜːrtnəs/ | n. | 简短；唐突；粗鲁 |  The curtness of her reply surprised me.  |  Her curtness bordered on rudeness. 她简短的回答让我很吃惊。| 近义词: abruptness, brevity, terseness, bluntness, brusqueness | 反义词: politeness, courtesy, graciousness | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *curtnes*, from *curt* (short) + *-nes* (suffix forming nouns of quality or state). Ultimately from Latin *curtus* (shortened, cut short).
