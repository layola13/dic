nonverminous:/ˌnɒnvɜːˈmɪnəs/
adjective
无寄生虫的；干净的
Free from vermin; clean.
The apartment was advertised as nonverminous and well-maintained. 这套公寓的广告上说没有寄生虫，维护良好。

The newly renovated house was thankfully nonverminous.  谢天谢地，这栋新装修的房子没有寄生虫。

Synonym: clean, sanitary, hygienic, pest-free
Antonym: verminous, infested, dirty, unsanitary
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + verminous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "verminous" means "infested with vermin."
