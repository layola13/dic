overperemptory:/ˌoʊvərˌpɛrˈɛmptəri/
adjective
过于专横的，盛气凌人的
too dictatorial or assertive; bossy
"He gave an overperemptory instruction to the staff." 他给员工下达了一条过于专横的指示。
"Her overperemptory manner made her unpopular." 她盛气凌人的态度让她不受欢迎。
近义词:dictatorial, imperious, autocratic, bossy, domineering
反义词:humble, meek, submissive, compliant

词性:adjective
