mustn't: /ˈmʌsnt/ | modal verb | 不准，禁止 | Contraction of "must not," indicating prohibition or strong advice against something.  It expresses obligation not to do something. | You mustn't smoke in here. 你不准在这里吸烟。| You mustn't tell anyone our secret. 你不能告诉任何人我们的秘密。 |  shouldn't, can't, may not | must, should, can
