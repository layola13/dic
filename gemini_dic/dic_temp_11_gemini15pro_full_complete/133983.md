nonhistoric:/ˌnɒnhɪˈstɒrɪk/ /ˌnɑːnhɪˈstɔːrɪk/
adjective
非历史的；没有历史意义的
Not of historical significance; not having a recorded history.
The building was deemed nonhistoric and therefore demolished.  这栋建筑被认为没有历史意义，因此被拆除了。
The committee decided that the event was nonhistoric and did not warrant a commemoration. 委员会认为该事件不具有历史意义，不值得纪念。
近义词:unhistorical, insignificant
反义词:historic, historical, significant
词性:adjective
