orchis:/ˈɔːrkɪs/| n. |兰花，睾丸|A terrestrial orchid of temperate regions, typically having brightly coloured flowers and underground tubers.|例句：The early purple orchis is one of the first orchids to bloom in the spring. 早春紫兰是春天最早开花的兰花之一。

例句：He discovered a rare orchis while hiking in the mountains. 他在山上徒步旅行时发现了一种稀有的兰花。|近义词：orchid|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin orchis, from Ancient Greek ὄρχις (órkhis, “testicle, orchid”), because of the shape of the root tubers of some species.
