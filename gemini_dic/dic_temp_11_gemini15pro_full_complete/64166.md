equanimous:/ˌiːkwəˈnɪməs/ | adj. | 平静的；镇定的；沉着的 |  calm and composed, especially in a difficult situation |例句：She remained equanimous in the face of danger. 她面对危险时仍然保持镇定。

例句：He accepted the news with an equanimous spirit. 他以平静的心情接受了这个消息。

近义词：calm, composed, serene, tranquil, imperturbable, unflappable | 反义词：agitated, anxious, nervous, distraught, frantic | 词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *aequanimus*, from aequus (“even, level, equal, calm”) + animus (“mind, spirit”).
