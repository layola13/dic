neurula:/ˈnʊərʊlə/ (US), /ˈnjʊərʊlə/ (UK)| n. |神经胚|The embryonic stage of a chordate in which the neural tube is forming.|The neurula stage is crucial for the development of the nervous system. 神经胚阶段对神经系统的发育至关重要。|Synonym: embryo |Antonym: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *νεῦρον (neûron, “sinew, tendon, nerve”) + -ula (diminutive suffix).
