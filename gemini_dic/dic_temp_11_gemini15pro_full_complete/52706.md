digresses:/daɪˈɡrɛsɪz/
verb
离题；跑题
to deviate or wander away from the main topic or purpose in speaking or writing.
The professor digressed from the main topic to tell a personal anecdote. 教授偏离主题，讲了一个个人轶事。
He digressed from the subject to discuss his own experiences. 他跑题讨论自己的经历。
synonyms: deviate, stray, wander, ramble, depart
antonyms: focus, concentrate, stick to the point
v.
