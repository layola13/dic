enchyma:/ˈɛŋkɪmə/| n. |薄壁组织|The cellular tissue, especially of plants, that forms the softer portions (such as the pulp of fruits, the pith of stems, etc.).| The parenchyma of the apple was soft and juicy. 苹果的薄壁组织柔软多汁。|近义词：tissue, ground tissue|反义词: sclerenchyma (厚壁组织)|noun
