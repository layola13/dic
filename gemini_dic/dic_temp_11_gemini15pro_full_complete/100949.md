inundatory:/ˈɪnʌndətɔːri/| adj.|泛滥的，洪水的，淹没的|Characterized by or resembling a flood; overwhelming.  Covering with a flood; overflowing.|The inundatory rains caused widespread damage. 泛滥的雨水造成了广泛的破坏。|Synonyms: flooding, overwhelming, deluging|Antonyms: arid, dry, draining|adjective


inundate:/ˈɪnʌndeɪt/| vt.|淹没，泛滥，使应接不暇|To cover with a flood; overflow. To overwhelm with a large quantity of something.|The river inundated the town after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，河流淹没了小镇。  We were inundated with requests for help. 我们收到了大量的求助请求。|Synonyms: flood, deluge, overwhelm, swamp|Antonyms: drain, dry, empty|verb
