adjective
过度装饰的
Excessively or elaborately decorated; adorned to an excessive degree.
The overadorning Christmas tree looked gaudy rather than festive.  这棵过度装饰的圣诞树看起来很俗气，而不是喜庆。
Her overadorning dress was inappropriate for the simple wedding ceremony.  她那件过度装饰的连衣裙不适合简单的婚礼仪式。
近义词: overdecorated, overembellished, gaudy, ostentatious
反义词: simple, plain, understated, minimalist
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "adorn" (verb meaning "to make more beautiful or attractive") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
