assentatory:/əˈsɛntətɔːri/ | adj. | 奉承的，谄媚的 | Excessively or insincerely complimentary; flattering. | He offered assentatory remarks to his boss, hoping for a promotion. 他对老板说了些奉承话，希望能升职。| 近义词: flattering, adulatory, fawning, servile, sycophantic | 反义词: critical, dissenting, objective, truthful | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin assentatorius, from assentari ‘agree with,’ from ad- ‘to’ + sentire ‘feel.’
