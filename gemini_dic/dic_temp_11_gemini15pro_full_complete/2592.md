admonitrix: /ədˈmɒnɪtrɪks/
n.
female admonisher; female monitor
a woman who admonishes; a female monitor
She acted as an admonitrix to her younger siblings, constantly reminding them of their chores. 她像个女训导员一样对待她的弟弟妹妹，不断提醒他们做家务。
Synonyms:  counselor, advisor, mentor, guide
Antonyms:  misleader, instigator
noun
