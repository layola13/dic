exhibitionisms: /ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪzəmz/
n.
暴露癖；炫耀行为
a tendency to attract attention to oneself by extravagant behavior or by dressing in unusual clothes, or the act of doing this.
His exhibitionism often embarrassed his family. 他的炫耀行为经常让他的家人感到尴尬。
Synonyms: ostentation, showiness, flamboyance, attention-seeking behavior
Antonyms: modesty, humility, reserve, discretion
Noun

exhibitionism: /ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪzəm/

The man was arrested for exhibitionism in the park.  这个男人因为在公园里暴露癖而被逮捕。
Synonyms: indecent exposure, flashing
Antonyms: modesty, decency
