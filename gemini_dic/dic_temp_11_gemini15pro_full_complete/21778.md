bobber:/ˈbɒbə(r) (UK), /ˈbɑːbər (US)| n.|浮子，浮标，钓鱼用的浮标|A bobber is a small, floating object attached to a fishing line that indicates when a fish has taken the bait. |例句：The bobber dipped below the surface, signaling a bite. 浮标沉了下去，表明有鱼咬钩了。

例句：He watched the bobber intently, waiting for a fish to strike. 他目不转睛地看着浮标，等着鱼上钩。|近义词：float, cork|反义词:sinker|词性:noun
