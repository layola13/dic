denigratory:/dɪˈnɪɡrəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 贬低的，诋毁的 | Showing a critical or disrespectful attitude; disparaging. |  His denigratory remarks about her appearance were completely uncalled for. 他对她外貌的贬低言论完全是不必要的。|  The article was full of denigratory comments about the local community. 这篇文章充满了对当地社区的诋毁性评论。 | Synonyms: disparaging, derogatory, belittling, defamatory, slanderous | Antonyms: complimentary, flattering, laudatory, praising | adjective
