hinds:/haɪndz/| n.| 后腿；臀部；雌鹿（hind 的复数）|The hinds of the deer were powerful and muscular. 鹿的后腿强壮有力。|例句：The farmer saw a group of hinds grazing in the field. 农夫看到一群雌鹿在田里吃草。

例句：The dog nipped at the hinds of the sheep. 狗咬了羊的臀部。|近义词：rear, rump, posterior, doe (for female deer)|反义词：forelegs, front (when referring to animals)|词性:名词

hind:/haɪnd/| adj.| 后面的；后部的|The hind legs of the rabbit are longer than its front legs. 兔子的后腿比前腿长。|例句：The hind wheels of the car got stuck in the mud. 汽车的后轮陷在泥里了。

例句：Please close the hind door. 请关上后门。|近义词：rear, back, posterior|反义词：front, fore|词性:形容词

(hinds 通常不与以下短语搭配使用，以下是用 hind 的例句)

hind legs:  The dog sat on its hind legs. 狗用后腿坐着。
hind quarters:  The butcher prepared the hind quarters of the beef. 屠夫准备了牛肉的后腿部分。
hind sight: In hind sight, I should have taken the other path. 事后看来，我应该走另一条路。
hind end: The car's hind end was damaged in the accident. 汽车的后部在事故中受损。
hinder: The heavy rain hindered our progress. 大雨阻碍了我们的前进。
