misperceptions:/ˌmɪspərˈsɛpʃənz/
|n.|误解，错误的认识|inaccurate or incorrect understandings or interpretations of something.|Many misperceptions exist about the safety of vaccines.  关于疫苗的安全性存在许多误解。|synonyms: misunderstandings, misinterpretations, misconceptions, fallacies|antonyms: accurate perceptions, correct understandings, truths|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrong' or 'bad') + perception (from Latin perceptio, from percipere 'to perceive').
