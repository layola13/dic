endosmotic:/ˌɛndoʊˈzmɒtɪk/ /ˌɛndoʊˈɑːzmɑːtɪk/
adjective
内渗的
Relating to or characterized by endosmosis; the inward passage of a fluid through a semipermeable membrane toward a fluid of greater concentration.
Water moves into the cell by endosmosis. 水通过内渗作用进入细胞。

近义词:  inward,  endooosmotic
反义词: exosmotic

词性: adjective
