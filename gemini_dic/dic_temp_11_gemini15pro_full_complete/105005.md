kenogenetic:/ˌkiːnədʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
具适应性的；后成性状的；非系统发育的
Relating to adaptation, especially to a non-phylogenetic character supposedly acquired by an individual or species during its lifetime and transmissible to offspring, or relating to features of an organism arising during development that are not present in the adult.
Kenogenetic adaptations allow the organism to survive in its specific environment. 这些后成性状的适应使生物体能够在其特定环境中生存。
Kenogenetic characters are not used in phylogenetic classifications. 后成性状不用于系统发育分类。
近义词: adaptive, acquired
反义词: phylogenetic, palingenetic
词性: adjective
