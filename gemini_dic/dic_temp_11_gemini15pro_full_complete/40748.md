conveys:/kənˈveɪz/| v. |传达，表达，传递|To make (an idea, impression, or feeling) known or understandable to someone.|Her expression conveyed her disapproval. 她的表情表达了她的反对。|近义词：communicates, transmits, imparts|反义词：withholds, suppresses|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English conveien, conveyen, from Anglo-Norman conveier, conveyer, from Old French convoier, from Late Latin *conviare (“to accompany on the way”), from Latin con- (“with, together”) + via (“way, road”).
