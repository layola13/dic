gestating:/ˈdʒɛsteɪtɪŋ/
verb
孕育，酝酿，(想法、计划等)逐渐形成
developing or maturing slowly; in the process of being formed.
例句：The artist spent months gestating his masterpiece.  艺术家花了几个月的时间酝酿他的杰作。

例句：She's gestating a new business idea. 她正在酝酿一个新的商业想法。
近义词：developing, maturing, incubating, brewing, formulating
反义词：completed, finished, realized, born, launched
词性：v.


gestation:/dʒɛˈsteɪʃən/
noun
怀孕期，孕育期，(想法、计划等)形成期
the process of carrying or being carried in the womb between conception and birth.  The development of something over a period of time.
例句：The gestation period of an elephant is about 22 months. 大象的妊娠期大约是22个月。

例句：The project is still in its gestation period.  这个项目还处于酝酿阶段。
近义词：development, incubation, maturation, formation
反义词：completion, conclusion, birth, launch
词性：n.
