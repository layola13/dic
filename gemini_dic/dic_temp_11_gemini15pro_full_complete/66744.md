exorcize:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ /ˈɛɡzɔːrsaɪz/| vt.|驱魔，驱除(坏事)，除去|To drive out an evil spirit from a person, place, or thing; to free from evil spirits or malign influences. |The priest exorcized the demons from the possessed man.  神父将恶魔从被附身的人身上驱除出去。

The therapist helped her exorcize the painful memories of her childhood.  治疗师帮助她驱除了童年痛苦的记忆。|近义词:expel, banish, purge, cleanse|反义词:invoke, summon, invite|verb
