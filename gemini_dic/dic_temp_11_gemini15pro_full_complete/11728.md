arolla:/əˈroʊlə/| n.|卷饼，通常指墨西哥卷饼|A flour tortilla rolled around a filling.  A type of Mexican food consisting of a tortilla wrapped around various fillings, such as meat, beans, and cheese.|例句：I had a delicious arolla for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的卷饼。

例句：The arolla was filled with seasoned beef, rice, and beans.  这个卷饼里面包着调味牛肉，米饭和豆子。|近义词：burrito, wrap|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
