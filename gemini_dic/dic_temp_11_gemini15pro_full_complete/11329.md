arecibo:/ˌɑːrəˈsiːboʊ/
proper noun
阿雷西博
Arecibo is a municipality and town on the north coast of Puerto Rico.  It is also known for the Arecibo Observatory, a radio telescope.
The Arecibo message was broadcast into space from the Arecibo Observatory. 阿雷西博信息是从阿雷西博天文台向太空广播的。
Synonym: (for observatory) radio telescope, telescope
Antonym: N/A
