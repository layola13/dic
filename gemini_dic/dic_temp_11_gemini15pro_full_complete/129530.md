non-believer:/ˌnɒn bɪˈliːvə(r)/ (US: /ˌnɑːn bɪˈliːvər/)| n.|不信教者；无神论者|A person who does not believe in a particular religion or in the existence of God or gods.|例句：He's a non-believer, but he respects other people's faiths. 他不信教，但他尊重别人的信仰。|例句：The group welcomed both believers and non-believers. 这个团体欢迎信徒和非信徒。|近义词：atheist, skeptic, infidel, agnostic|反义词：believer, devotee, theist|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + believer.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "believer" comes from the Middle English *bileven* meaning "to believe," ultimately from Old English *belīefan*.
