flete:/ˈfleɪteɪ/ (US), /ˈfleɪt/ (UK)| n.|运费，货物；船|The cost of transporting goods, especially by ship; freight.  Also can refer to the goods themselves being shipped.|例句：The flete for shipping the container was higher than expected.  集装箱的运费比预期的要高。

例句：The flete arrived at the port on schedule. 货物按时抵达港口。|近义词：freight, cargo, shipment|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
fletar:/fleˈtaɾ/ (Spanish)| v.|租用，包租（船只，车辆等）|To charter or hire (a ship, vehicle, etc.).|例句：Tuvimos que fletar un autobús para llevar a todos los invitados a la boda.  We had to charter a bus to take all the wedding guests.

例句：La empresa fletó un avión para transportar las mercancías. The company chartered a plane to transport the goods.|近义词：alquilar, arrendar (Spanish)|反义词: N/A|词性:verb
