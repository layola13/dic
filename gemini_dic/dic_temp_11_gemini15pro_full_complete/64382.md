erect:/ɪˈrekt/ | adj. v. | 直立的，竖立的；建造，建立 | Upright in position or posture. To build or establish something. | The soldiers stood erect. 士兵们直立站着。 They erected a statue in his honor. 他们为他竖立了一座雕像。 | 近义词: upright, vertical, build, construct | 反义词: horizontal, demolish, destroy | 词性: 形容词，动词
词源: From Latin ērigō, ērigere ("raise up, set upright"), from ē- ("out, up") + regō ("to guide, rule, keep straight").
