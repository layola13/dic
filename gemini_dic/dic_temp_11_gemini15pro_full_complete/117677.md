meiostemonous:/ˌmaɪoʊˈstɛmənəs/
adj.
少雄蕊的，雄蕊数少于花瓣或萼片数的
Having fewer stamens than petals or sepals.
The flower is meiostemonous, with only four stamens while it has five petals.  这朵花是少雄蕊的，只有四个雄蕊，而它有五个花瓣。
Synonyms: oligandrous
Antonyms: polyandrous
词性: adjective
