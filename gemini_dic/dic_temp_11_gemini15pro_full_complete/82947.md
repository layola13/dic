gunboat:/ˈɡʌnboʊt/ (US), /ˈɡʌnbəʊt/ (UK)| n. |炮舰|A small warship equipped with guns, typically used in rivers or shallow coastal waters.|The gunboat patrolled the river. 炮舰在河上巡逻。|The gunboat shelled the enemy position. 炮舰炮击了敌人的阵地。|近义词：warship, corvette|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

gunboat diplomacy:/ˈɡʌnboʊt dɪˈploʊməsi/ (US), /ˈɡʌnbəʊt dɪˈpləʊməsi/ (UK)| n. |炮舰外交|Foreign policy that is supported by the use or threat of military force.|The country used gunboat diplomacy to force the other nation to comply with its demands.  这个国家用炮舰外交迫使另一个国家服从其要求。|The threat of sending a gunboat was enough to resolve the dispute. 派遣炮舰的威胁足以解决争端。|近义词：coercive diplomacy, military intimidation|反义词:peaceful negotiation|词性:noun
