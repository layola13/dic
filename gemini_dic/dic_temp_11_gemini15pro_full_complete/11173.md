archidiaconal: /ˌɑːrkɪdaɪˈækənəl/ | adj. | 总执事(职位)的；副主教的 | Relating to an archdeacon (a senior clergy position in certain Christian churches). | The archdeaconal visitation is scheduled for next month. 总执事视察安排在下个月。| Synonym: episcopal, diocesan | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it relates to a specific office) | adjective

Etymology:

From Late Latin *archidiaconalis*, from *archidiaconus* (archdeacon), from Greek *arkhi* (chief) + *diakonos* (servant, minister).
