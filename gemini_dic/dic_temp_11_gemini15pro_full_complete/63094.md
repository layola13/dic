ensembled:/ɑːnˈsɑːmbəld/
|v.|被组合的，被集成的|Brought together as a group or collection; formed into an ensemble.  Specifically refers to something having been put together, often implying a sense of careful selection or arrangement.|The orchestra was expertly ensembled with musicians from around the world.  这支管弦乐队由来自世界各地的音乐家精心组合而成。|assembled, collected, gathered, combined|separated, dispersed|verb
