obstruent:/əbˈstruːənt/| n. adj.|阻塞音，阻塞的|A sound made by obstructing the airflow from the lungs.  As an adjective, it describes such sounds.|The sounds /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, and /g/ are obstruents.  浊塞音/p/、/b/、/t/、/d/、/k/和/g/都是阻塞音。|近义词：plosive, stop (as nouns), blocked, constricted (as adjectives)|反义词:sonorant, vowel (as nouns), open, unblocked (as adjectives)|词性:noun, adjective
Etymology->
From Latin *obstruēns*, present participle of *obstruere* ("to build against, block up"), from *ob-* ("against") + *struere* ("to pile up, build").
