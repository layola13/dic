overplotted: /ˌoʊvərˈplɒtɪd/ | adj. | 过度绘制的，过度标绘的 |  Containing too many plotted elements, making it difficult to distinguish individual data points or trends.  Used particularly in the context of graphs and charts. | The scatter plot was overplotted, making it hard to see the correlation.  这个散点图过度绘制了，很难看出相关性。 |  overcrowded, cluttered, busy | sparse, clear, uncluttered | adjective

overplot: /ˌoʊvərˈplɒt/ | vt. | 过度绘制，过度标绘 | To plot too much data on a single graph, obscuring the patterns. |  He overplotted the graph, making it difficult to interpret. 他过度绘制了图表，使其难以解读。 | overcrowd, clutter | clarify, simplify | verb
