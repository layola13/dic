baile:/ˈbɑːleɪ/ /ˈbeɪli/| n.|舞会，舞蹈，社交聚会|A social gathering for dancing. A festive occasion, often with music and dancing, especially in Hispanic cultures.|例句：The annual baile was the highlight of the social calendar. 年度舞会是社交日程的亮点。

例句：They held a lively baile in the village square. 他们在村庄广场上举行了一个热闹的舞会。|近义词：dance, party, celebration, fiesta, ball|反义词:|noun
