entitlement:/ɪnˈtaɪtlmənt/ /ɪnˈtaɪtəlmənt/| n. |权利，津贴，应得权益|The right to do or have something, or the things that you have a right to do or have. A government program providing benefits to members of a specified group.|She believes that she has a natural entitlement to a high-paying job. 她认为她天生就有权获得一份高薪工作。|right, privilege, prerogative|obligation, duty|noun

entitlements:/ɪnˈtaɪtlmənts/ /ɪnˈtaɪtəlmənts/| n. |（政府提供的）福利，权利，津贴|Government programs providing benefits to members of a specified group.  Often used in the plural to refer to such benefits collectively.|The government is cutting back on entitlements. 政府正在削减福利。|benefits, allowances, grants|restrictions, limitations|noun
