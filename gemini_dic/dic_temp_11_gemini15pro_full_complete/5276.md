allopathic:/ˌæləˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 对抗疗法的，对抗疗法的 | Relating to or being a system of medicine that combats disease by using remedies that produce effects different from or incompatible with those produced by the disease being treated. | Example: Allopathic medicine is the dominant system of medicine in the Western world. 对抗疗法是西方世界主要的医学体系。| Example:  He opted for an allopathic treatment for his infection. 他选择了对抗疗法治疗他的感染。 | 近义词: conventional, orthodox, traditional | 反义词: homeopathic, alternative, complementary | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek allos "other" + pathos "suffering."  The term was coined in the mid-19th century by Samuel Hahnemann, the founder of homeopathy, to contrast conventional medicine with his own system of treatment.
