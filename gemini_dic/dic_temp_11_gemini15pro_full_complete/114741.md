malnutritious: /ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃəs/ | adj. | 营养不良的 | Lacking proper nutrition; not nourished adequately. |  The malnourished children were given vitamin supplements. 营养不良的儿童得到了维生素补充。

Many refugees arrive at the camps malnourished and exhausted. 许多难民到达营地时营养不良，疲惫不堪。


Near Synonyms: undernourished, underfed, starved, emaciated, unhealthy

Antonyms: well-nourished, healthy, nourished, well-fed

Word Origin:  From mal- (bad, ill) + nutritious.
