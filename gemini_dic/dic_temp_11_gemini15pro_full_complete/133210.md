nonexaggerating: /ˌnɑːnɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |  不夸张的，写实的 |  Not exaggerating; representing something accurately and without embellishment. |  She gave a nonexaggerating account of the events. 她对事件进行了不夸大的描述。 |  The documentary provided a nonexaggerating portrayal of life in the refugee camp. 这部纪录片对难民营的生活进行了真实的刻画。 | 近义词:  accurate, truthful, realistic, factual, unembellished | 反义词: exaggerating, hyperbolical, overstated, inflated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + exaggerating (present participle of the verb "exaggerate")  来自拉丁语 exaggeratus，意思是“堆高，增高”。
