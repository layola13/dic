nonimpulsive:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpʌlsɪv/
adjective
非冲动的；慎重的
not impulsive; characterized by the ability to control one's emotions and actions.
She took a nonimpulsive approach to the problem, carefully considering all options. 她以非冲动的方式处理这个问题，仔细考虑了所有选择。
He made a nonimpulsive decision to invest his money wisely. 他做了一个不冲动的决定，明智地投资了他的钱。
近义词: thoughtful, deliberate, considered, cautious, measured, level-headed, calm, collected, composed, self-controlled
反义词: impulsive, rash, impetuous, reckless, hasty
