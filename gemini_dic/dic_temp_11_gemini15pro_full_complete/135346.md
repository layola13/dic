nonpensionable:/nɒnˈpɛnʃənəbl/
adjective
不可领养老金的; 不计入养老金的
Not eligible for a pension; not counting towards a pension.
His current job is nonpensionable, so he's investing privately for retirement.  他的目前的工作不可领养老金，所以他正在私人投资以备退休。
This part-time work is nonpensionable. 这份兼职工作不计入养老金。
近义词: non-pensioned
反义词: pensionable
词性: adjective
