comprehensive:/ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnsɪv/| adj.| 综合的，全面的，广泛的|Including or dealing with all or nearly all elements or aspects of something.|The study provided a comprehensive overview of the subject. 这项研究对该主题进行了全面的概述。|
We offer a comprehensive range of services. 我们提供全方位的服务。|Synonyms: extensive, thorough, complete, full, inclusive|Antonyms: limited, narrow, restricted, incomplete, partial|Adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin comprehensivus, from Latin comprehensus, past participle of comprehendere (“to grasp mentally, understand”).
