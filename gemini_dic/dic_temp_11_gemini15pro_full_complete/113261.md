lustrous: /ˈlʌstrəs/ | adj. | 有光泽的；光亮的；光辉的 | Having a sheen or glow; shining. | Her lustrous hair cascaded down her back. 她光亮的头发瀑布般倾泻而下。| synonyms: shiny, glossy, gleaming, radiant, brilliant | antonyms: dull, matte, lackluster | adjective

lustrous: /ˈlʌstrəs/ | adj. | 杰出的；辉煌的 | Illustrious; outstanding.  | He had a lustrous career as a diplomat. 他有着辉煌的外交生涯。 | synonyms: distinguished, eminent, illustrious, notable | antonyms: undistinguished, unremarkable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *lustrouse*, from Old French *lustros*, from Latin *lūstrōsus* (“bright, shining”), from *lūstrō* (“I purify, brighten”), from *lūstrum* (“purification, cleansing”).
