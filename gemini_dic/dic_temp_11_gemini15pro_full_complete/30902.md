chagrinned:/ʃəˈɡrɪnd/ /ʃəˈɡrind/| adj.|懊恼的，失望的|Feeling or showing annoyance or disappointment at having failed or been humiliated.|例句：He was chagrinned at his failure to win the race. 他因没能赢得比赛而懊恼。|例句：She felt chagrinned when she realized her mistake. 当她意识到自己的错误时，她感到很懊恼。|近义词：disappointed, annoyed, embarrassed, humiliated, mortified|反义词：pleased, delighted, happy|词性:形容词

詞源:
From French *chagrin*, from Old French *chagrin* (“sadness, grief, affliction; rough, hard”), probably from *chigre* (“itch mite, chigger”), which may be related to Old North French *crehin* (“crack, flaw”). More at chigger.
