humanises:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
赋予人性；使通俗易懂
Makes something more humane or understandable, gives something human characteristics.
The company tried to humanise its image by featuring employees in its advertisements.  该公司试图通过在广告中突出员工来使其形象更人性化。
The author humanised the historical figures by describing their personal struggles. 作者通过描述历史人物的个人奋斗，使他们更具人性。
humanize, personalize, civilize
dehumanize, brutalize
词性:v
