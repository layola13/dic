bulkest:/ˈbʌlkiɪst/
adjective
最庞大的，最笨重的
having the greatest bulk; largest and heaviest
The bulkest package was left by the front door. 最笨重的包裹被放在前门。
近义词:largest, heaviest, hugest, massivest
反义词:smallest, lightest, tiniest, slightest
词性:adjective

词源:
From bulk + -est.  Bulk itself comes from Middle English *bulke*, of uncertain origin, possibly from Old Norse *bulki* "cargo, heap".
