broadheads:/ˈbrɔːdˌhɛdz/ /ˈbrɑːdˌhɛdz/| n.|阔刃箭头|A type of arrowhead with multiple sharp blades, often used for hunting.  These arrowheads create large wounds, leading to quicker kills.|The hunter used broadheads for the deer hunt. 猎人用阔刃箭头猎鹿。|Synonyms: hunting points, blades|Antonyms: target points, field points|noun

broadheads:/ˈbrɔːdˌhɛdz/ /ˈbrɑːdˌhɛdz/| n.|阔箭头|Referring to arrows with broadheads attached.|He carried a quiver full of broadheads. 他带着一壶装满阔箭头的箭袋。|Synonyms: hunting arrows|Antonyms: target arrows, field arrows|noun
