Characterized by a strong belief in one's own moral superiority and a tendency to judge others harshly.|例句：He gave an overrighteous speech about the evils of drinking. 他就饮酒的罪恶发表了一篇自以为是的演讲。

例句：Her overrighteous attitude alienated her friends. 她自以为是的态度疏远了她的朋友。|近义词：sanctimonious, self-righteous, hypocritical, pietistic|反义词：humble, modest, unpretentious|词性: adjective
