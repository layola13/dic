motivating:/ˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪŋ/| adj.|激励的，鼓舞人心的|Providing incentive or encouragement.  Giving a reason for action.|Her motivating speech inspired the team to victory. 她鼓舞人心的演讲激励团队取得了胜利。|近义词：inspiring, encouraging, stimulating|反义词：demotivating, discouraging, disheartening|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the verb "motivate," which comes from the Late Latin "motivatus," past participle of "movare" (to move), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*meue-" (to push away).
