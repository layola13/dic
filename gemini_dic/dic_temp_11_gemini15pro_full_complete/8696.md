anticreationism:/ˌæntiˌkriˈeɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
反创造论
The opposition to the belief in creationism, specifically the belief that the universe and living organisms originated from specific acts of divine creation.
He dismissed anticreationism as a fringe movement. 他将反创造论斥为边缘运动。
近义词：evolutionism, Darwinism
反义词：creationism
