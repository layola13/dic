italo-celtic:/ˈɪtəloʊ ˈkɛltɪk/ | adj. | 伊塔洛-凯尔特语的，属于伊塔洛-凯尔特语族的 | Relating to a branch of the Indo-European language family that includes Italic and Celtic languages. Italo-Celtic is a hypothetical grouping, and its existence is debated among linguists. |  The Italo-Celtic hypothesis proposes a common ancestor for the Italic and Celtic branches. 伊塔洛-凯尔特语假说提出，伊塔利语族和凯尔特语族有一个共同的祖先。

The existence of an Italo-Celtic language family is not universally accepted. 伊塔洛-凯尔特语族的存在并未被普遍接受。 | 近义词: Indo-European (a broader category) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a linguistic classification) | 词性: adjective
