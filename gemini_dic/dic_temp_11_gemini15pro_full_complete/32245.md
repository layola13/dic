chickenshit:/ˈtʃɪkɪnˌʃɪt/| n. adj.|胆小鬼，懦夫；胆怯的，懦弱的|A cowardly person; cowardly.|例句：He called me a chickenshit for not jumping off the cliff. 他因为我没有从悬崖上跳下来而叫我胆小鬼。

例句：Don't be such a chickenshit!  别这么胆小！|近义词：coward, wimp, scaredy-cat|反义词：brave, courageous, heroic|词性: 名词，形容词

chickenshits:/ˈtʃɪkɪnˌʃɪts/| n.|胆小鬼们，懦夫们|Plural form of "chickenshit," referring to multiple cowardly individuals.|例句：He called them a bunch of chickenshits for refusing to fight.  他骂他们是一群胆小鬼，因为他们拒绝打架。

例句：Only chickenshits would run away from a challenge like this. 只有胆小鬼才会逃避这样的挑战。|近义词：cowards, wimps|反义词：heroes, braves|词性: 名词
