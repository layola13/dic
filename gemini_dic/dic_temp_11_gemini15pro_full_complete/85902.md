headhunter:/ˈhedˌhʌntər/| n. |猎头|A person who identifies and approaches suitable candidates employed elsewhere to fill business positions.|The company hired a headhunter to find a new CEO. 这家公司聘请了一位猎头来寻找新的首席执行官。|近义词：recruiter, executive search consultant|反义词: applicant, candidate|noun
