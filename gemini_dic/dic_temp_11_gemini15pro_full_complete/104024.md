jujuism:/ˈdʒuːdʒuːɪzəm/
n.
巫术；符咒；迷信；旧物
A West African system of beliefs and practices, especially those involving magic or witchcraft.  It also refers to an object used in these practices, such as a charm or amulet.
He attributed his success to jujuism. 他把他的成功归功于巫术。
近义词:witchcraft, sorcery, magic, fetishism
反义词:rationality, logic, science
