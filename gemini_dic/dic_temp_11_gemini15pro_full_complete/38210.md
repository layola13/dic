compunctious:/kəmˈpʌŋkʃəs/| adj.|内疚的，后悔的，良心不安的|Feeling or showing guilt or remorse for a wrongdoing.|She felt compunctious about leaving her dog alone all day. 她为把狗独自留了一整天而感到内疚。|例句：A compunctious pang shot through him as he remembered his harsh words.  当他想起自己那些刻薄的话时，一阵悔恨的刺痛感涌上心头。

例句：Her compunctious look made him feel even worse. 她悔恨的表情让他感觉更糟了。|近义词：remorseful, repentant, contrite, guilty|反义词：unrepentant, remorseless, unapologetic|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compunctiōsus*, from Latin compunctiō (“a pricking, stinging”), from compungō (“I prick sharply, sting”), from com- (“together”) + pungō (“I prick, sting”).
