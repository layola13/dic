bunk's:/bʌŋks/| n.|床铺（尤指双层床的上铺或下铺）,铺位|A bunk is a narrow bed that is fixed to a wall, often in a ship or a prison. Bunk's is the possessive form, indicating ownership or association with a bunk.  It usually refers to the space or belongings associated with a particular bunk.|例句：He fell out of his bunk's top bunk. 他从他的床铺的上铺摔了下来。|例句：She stored her belongings under her bunk's mattress. 她把她的东西储存在她的床铺的床垫下面。|近义词：bed, berth, cot|反义词:|词性:名词
