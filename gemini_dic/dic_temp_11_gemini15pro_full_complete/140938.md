one-liner:/ˈwʌnˌlaɪnər/ | n. |  单行笑话；妙语；警句 | A short joke or witty remark, typically only one sentence long. | He's famous for his one-liners. 他以妙语连珠而闻名。| synonym: quip, witticism, gag | antonym:  speech, monologue | noun


one-liner:/ˈwʌnˌlaɪnər/ | adj. | 单行的 | Consisting of a single line of text or code. |  It's a one-liner solution to a complex problem.  这是一个解决复杂问题的单行代码方案。| synonym: concise, brief, succinct | antonym: lengthy, verbose, prolix | adjective
