omelette:/ˈɑːmlɪt/ (US), /ˈɒmlɪt/ (UK)| n.|煎蛋卷，蛋饼|A dish of beaten eggs cooked in a frying pan until set, often with a filling of cheese, vegetables, meat, etc.|例句：I had a cheese omelette for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个奶酪煎蛋卷。

例句：She made a delicious mushroom omelette. 她做了一个美味的蘑菇煎蛋卷。

近义词：fritatta|反义词:|词性:noun
