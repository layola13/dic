frenzy:/ˈfrɛnzi/ /ˈfrɛnzi/ | n. | 狂热，疯狂，暴乱 | A state of violent mental agitation or wild excitement. | The shoppers were in a frenzy on Black Friday.  购物者在黑色星期五陷入了疯狂。

The crowd worked itself into a frenzy.  人群变得狂热起来。| 近义词：mania, hysteria, delirium, rage, fury | 反义词：calm, composure, tranquility | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *phrenēsis, from Ancient Greek φρένησις (phrénēsis, “frenzy, delirium”), from φρήν (phrḗn, “mind, heart, midriff”).
