flavorless:/ˈfleɪvələs/ | adj. | 无味的；平淡的 | Lacking flavor; bland. | The soup was disappointingly flavorless. 这汤淡而无味，令人失望。| insipid, bland, tasteless | flavorful, tasty, savory | adjective

flavorless:/ˈfleɪvələs/ | adj. | 平淡的；枯燥乏味的 | Lacking interest or excitement; dull. |  His presentation was flavorless and uninspired. 他的演讲平淡无奇，缺乏激情。 | dull, uninteresting, boring | exciting, interesting, stimulating | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From flavor + -less.  "Flavor" comes from Old French "flaur," meaning "smell, odor, fragrance," ultimately from Latin "flare," meaning "to blow."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
