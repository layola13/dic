chemigraphy:/kɛˈmɪɡrəfi/
n.
化学照相术，化学刻印术
A process of etching metal plates using chemicals, often for printing images.
Chemigraphy allows for greater detail than traditional etching methods. 化学照相术比传统的蚀刻方法更精细。
The artist used chemigraphy to create the intricate designs on the jewelry.  这位艺术家用化学照相术在珠宝上创作了复杂的图案。
近义词:photogravure, etching
反义词:None
词性:noun

Etymology:
From "chemi-" (chemical) + "-graphy" (writing or drawing). 源于 "chemi-" (化学) + "-graphy" (书写或绘画).
