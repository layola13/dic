leat:/liːt/| n.|谷仓；牲畜棚；草料；干草|A barn or other building for storing hay or straw, especially one attached to a stable.|The farmer stored the hay in the leat. 农民把干草储存在牲畜棚里。|近义词：barn, shed, stable|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old English *hlēat, from Proto-Germanic *hlaþą (“barn”), from Proto-Indo-European *kl̥h₂-tó- (“something laid, a layer”). Cognate with Old Frisian hliat (“floor, story, loft”), Old Saxon hleo, Old High German hlado (“barn”), Old Norse hlaða (“barn, shed”), Gothic 𐌷𐌻𐌰𐌸𐌰 (hlaþa, “barn”).
