noncapriciousness:/ˌnɒnkəˈprɪʃəsnəs/
noun
非反复无常；稳定性
The state or quality of not being capricious; steadiness.
Her noncapriciousness and steady hand made her a skilled surgeon. 她沉稳的性格和稳定的手使她成为一名技术娴熟的外科医生。
Synonyms: steadiness, stability, constancy, consistency
Antonyms: capriciousness, fickleness, volatility, instability
