habitableness:/ˈhæbɪtəblnəs/
n.
可居住性，适居性
The habitableness of a planet depends on several factors, including temperature and the presence of water. 一个星球的可居住性取决于几个因素，包括温度和水的存在。
The researchers are studying the habitableness of Mars. 研究人员正在研究火星的宜居性。
近义词:livability, inhabitability
反义词:uninhabitability
词性:noun

Etymology: From habitable + -ness.  Habitable comes from Late Middle English: from Old French habitable or late Latin habitabilis, from habitare ‘inhabit’, from Latin habitus, past participle of habere ‘to have’.
