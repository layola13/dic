overpromptness:/ˌoʊvərˈprɒmpt.nəs/| n.| 过于迅速；过分及时|The overpromptness of his reply suggested he had been expecting the question. 他的回答过于迅速，表明他一直在期待这个问题。|例句：Her overpromptness in offering help was sometimes interpreted as being pushy. 她过分积极主动地提供帮助，有时被解读为爱出风头。

例句：His overpromptness in agreeing to the deal made them suspicious. 他答应这笔交易过于迅速，这让他们起了疑心。|近义词：haste, alacrity, eagerness, precipitateness|反义词：tardiness, delay, procrastination, slowness|词性:名词

词源: over- + promptness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Promptness" is derived from the adjective "prompt," which means "done without delay." Thus, "overpromptness" literally means "excessive promptness."
