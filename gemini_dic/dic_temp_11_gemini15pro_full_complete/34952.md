clocking-out:/ˈklɒkɪŋ aʊt/ /ˈklɑːkɪŋ aʊt/| n.|下班打卡，结束工作|The act of recording the time one finishes work, typically by using a time clock or electronic system.  It signifies the end of a work shift or period.| I'm clocking-out early today. 我今天早点下班打卡。|近义词：leaving work, finishing work, signing off|反义词:clocking-in, starting work|词性:名词 (gerund, derived from the verb "clock out")

clock-out:/klɒk aʊt/ /klɑːk aʊt/| v.|下班打卡|To record the time one finishes work. |I clocked out at 5 pm. 我五点钟下班打卡。|近义词：leave work, finish work, sign off|反义词:clock-in, start work|词性:动词
