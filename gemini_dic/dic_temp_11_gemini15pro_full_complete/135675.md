nonpreparations:/ˌnɒnprɛpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 无准备，缺乏准备 | Lack of preparations; the state of being unprepared. |  The team's nonpreparations for the match resulted in a crushing defeat. 球队对比赛缺乏准备，导致惨败。

The nonpreparations for the hurricane left the coastal towns vulnerable.  对飓风的缺乏准备使沿海城镇变得脆弱。| 近义词: unpreparedness, lack of preparation | 反义词: preparedness, readiness | 词性: 名词


词源:

"non-" (prefix):  from Latin *non "not". 表示否定。

"preparation" (noun): from Late Middle English: from Old French preparacion, from Latin praeparatio(n-), from the verb praeparare. 源自拉丁语 praeparare，意为“预先准备”。
