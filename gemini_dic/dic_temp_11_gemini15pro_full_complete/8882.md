antiformalists:/ˌæntiˈfɔːrməlɪsts/
n.
反形式主义者
Those who oppose formalism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy.
Antiformalists believe that strict adherence to rules and conventions hinders creativity and expression.  反形式主义者认为，严格遵守规则和惯例会阻碍创造力和表达。
The antiformalists championed a more intuitive and emotional approach to art. 反形式主义者拥护一种更直观、更感性的艺术方法。
Synonyms: iconoclasts, nonconformists, rebels
Antonyms: formalists, traditionalists
