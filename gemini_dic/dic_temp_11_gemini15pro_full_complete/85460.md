hasidism: /ˈhɑːsɪdɪzəm/ /ˈhæsɪdɪzəm/
n.
哈西德主义，哈西德派
A Jewish religious movement characterized by its emphasis on mystical experience, joy, and devotion to God.
Example: Hasidism originated in Eastern Europe in the 18th century. 哈西德主义起源于18世纪的东欧。

Example: The hasidism movement emphasizes the importance of prayer and study. 哈西德派运动强调祈祷和学习的重要性。

近义词: Hasidic Judaism
反义词: (No direct antonym, but potentially opposing religious or philosophical viewpoints within Judaism)
词性: noun
