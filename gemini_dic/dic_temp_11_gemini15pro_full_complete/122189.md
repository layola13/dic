misunderstanders:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændərz/| n. |误解者；不理解的人|People who misunderstand something or someone.  Those who fail to grasp the correct meaning or intention. |例句：The misunderstanders took offense at her comment, even though she meant no harm. 误解她评论的人被冒犯了，即使她没有恶意。

例句：The speaker tried to clarify his point to avoid any further misunderstanders. 演讲者试图澄清他的观点，以避免更多的人误解。|近义词：misinterpreters, misconstruers|反义词: interpreters, comprehenders |词性: Noun (plural)
