observatory:/əbˈzɜːrvətɔːri/ (US) /əbˈzɜːvətri/ (UK)| n.|天文台；气象台；瞭望台|A building or place equipped with telescopes and other scientific instruments for observing astronomical phenomena, meteorological conditions, or other natural phenomena.|例句：The astronomer spent many nights at the observatory. 天文学家在天文台度过了许多夜晚。

例句：The observatory is located on a mountaintop for optimal viewing conditions.  天文台位于山顶，可以获得最佳的观测条件。

近义词：lookout, watchtower|反义词:None|词性:noun
