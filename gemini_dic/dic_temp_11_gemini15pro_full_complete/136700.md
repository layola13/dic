nonsegregable:/ˌnɒnˌsɛɡrɪˈɡeɪbəl/ | adj. | 不可分离的，不可隔离的 | Not able to be segregated or separated. |  This type of waste is nonsegregable and must be treated as a whole. 这种废物是不可分离的，必须整体处理。|  The components are so intertwined that they are nonsegregable. 这些组件相互交织，无法分离。 | inseparable, indivisible, unsegregable | separable, divisible, segregable | adjective
