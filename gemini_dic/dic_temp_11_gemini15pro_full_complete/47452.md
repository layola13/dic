deal:/diːl/
n.|交易，协议；待遇；大量；（纸牌）一手牌|A business deal. 一笔生意。|agreement, contract, transaction|disagreement|noun
deal:/diːl/
v.|处理；给予；分配；发牌|He dealt with the problem effectively. 他有效地处理了这个问题。|handle, manage, distribute|neglect|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dǣlan* "to divide, distribute, share," from Proto-Germanic *dailjanan* "to divide," from Proto-Indo-European *dail- "to divide."
