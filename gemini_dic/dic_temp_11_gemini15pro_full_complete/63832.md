epigeal:/ɛpɪˈdʒiːəl/
adjective
地上生的；子叶出土的
Growing or occurring above the ground.  Specifically, referring to seed germination where the cotyledons are carried above the ground.
The epigeal germination of the bean plant allows the cotyledons to photosynthesize.  豆类植物的子叶出土萌发使得子叶能够进行光合作用。

epigeal:/ɛpɪˈdʒiːəl/
adjective
表生的
Occurring on or near the surface of the earth.
Epigeal organisms play a crucial role in nutrient cycling. 表生生物在营养循环中起着至关重要的作用。

Synonym: aboveground, superficial
Antonym: hypogeal, subterranean
词性: adjective
