blocks:/blɑːks/ (US) /blɒks/ (UK)| n. |块; 街区; 障碍物; 大楼|A solid piece of hard material, typically with flat surfaces on each side; a section of a town or city enclosed by streets; an obstacle or impediment; a large building divided into separate apartments or offices.|

例句：
The child was playing with wooden blocks.
孩子正在玩积木。

The museum is just two blocks down the street.
博物馆就在这条街往下走两个街区。

近义词：pieces, sections, obstacles, buildings
反义词: openings, passages

词性: noun
