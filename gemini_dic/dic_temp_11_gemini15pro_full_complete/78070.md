geodephagous:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɛfəɡəs/
adjective
食土的，食泥的
Feeding on earth or soil.
例句：Some parrots exhibit geodephagous behavior, consuming clay to neutralize toxins in their diet.  一些鹦鹉表现出食土行为，吃粘土来中和它们饮食中的毒素。

例句：Geodephagous insects play a vital role in soil nutrient cycling. 食土昆虫在土壤养分循环中起着至关重要的作用。

近义词：earth-eating, soil-consuming
反义词：(None -  lack of a direct antonym)
词性: adjective
词源：From Greek  *gē* (earth) + *-ed-* (eat) + *-phagos* (eater)
