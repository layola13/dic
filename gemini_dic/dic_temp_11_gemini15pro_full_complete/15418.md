bacterio:/bækˈtɪəriəʊ/
combining form
细菌- (用于构成复合词)
A combining form meaning “bacteria,” used in the formation of compound words.
Bacteriophage is a virus that infects and replicates within bacteria. 噬菌体是一种感染并在细菌内复制的病毒。
Bacteriostatic substances inhibit bacterial growth. 抑菌物质抑制细菌生长。
近义词:  None (as a combining form)
反义词:  None (as a combining form)
词性: Combining form
