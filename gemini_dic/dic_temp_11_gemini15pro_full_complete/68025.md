factorial:/fækˈtɔːriəl/ | n. |阶乘 |The product of all positive integers less than or equal to a given positive integer, denoted by that integer followed by an exclamation mark. |Example 1: The factorial of 5 (5!) is 5 * 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 = 120.  5的阶乘（5！）是5 * 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 = 120。| Example 2: Factorials are used in many areas of mathematics, including probability and combinatorics. 阶乘用于数学的许多领域，包括概率和组合学。| Near Synonyms:  product, multiplication | Antonyms: division, quotient | noun
