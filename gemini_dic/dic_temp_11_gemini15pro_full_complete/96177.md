indescribably:/ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbli/
adverb
难以形容地；无法描述地
so extraordinary as to be impossible to describe
The sunset was indescribably beautiful.  日落美得难以形容。
The pain was indescribably intense.  这种痛苦难以言表。
Synonyms: inexpressibly, unutterably, ineffably
Antonyms: describably, expressibly
词性: 副词
