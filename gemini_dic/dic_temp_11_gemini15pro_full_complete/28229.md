capsizing:/ˈkæpsaɪzɪŋ/
verb|倾覆，翻船|The act of overturning or causing a boat or ship to overturn.|The boat is capsizing! 船要翻了！
capsize:/ˈkæpsaɪz/
verb|倾覆，翻船|(of a boat) overturn in the water.|The boat capsized in the storm. 船在暴风雨中翻了。|近义词：overturn, upset, keel over|反义词:right, upright|v.
capsize:/ˈkæpsaɪz/
noun|倾覆，翻船|An instance of a boat or ship overturning.|The capsize of the ferry resulted in many casualties. 渡轮的倾覆导致了许多人员伤亡。|近义词：overturning, upsetting|反义词:righting|n.
