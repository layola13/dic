malleability:/ˌmæliəˈbɪləti/
n.
可锻性，延展性，柔韧性
The quality of something that can be shaped into something else without breaking, or of someone who is easily influenced by others.
Gold is a metal of high malleability. 金是一种具有高延展性的金属。
The artist chose clay for its malleability. 艺术家选择粘土是因为它的可塑性。
近义词: flexibility, plasticity, ductility, suppleness
反义词: rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility, brittleness
词性: noun
词源: From Late Latin malleabilitās, from malleābilis (malleable) + -itās (-ity).
