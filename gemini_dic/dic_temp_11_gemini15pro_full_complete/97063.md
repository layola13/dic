inferior:/ɪnˈfɪəriər/ | adj. n. |低等的，劣等的；下级的；下属；次品|Lower in rank, status, or quality.|This product is inferior to the other one. 这个产品比另一个差。|近义词:subordinate, lesser, poorer, second-rate|反义词:superior, better, excellent|词性:形容词, 名词

inferior:/ɪnˈfɪəriər/ | n. |下属，下级|A person lower than another in rank, status, or ability.|He treats his inferiors with respect. 他尊重他的下属。|近义词:subordinate, junior|反义词:superior, senior|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *inferior* (comparative of *inferus* "low"), from *infra* "below".
