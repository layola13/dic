harriers:/ˈhæriərz/ (US), /ˈhærɪəz/ (UK)| n.|猎兔犬；鹞（猛禽的一种）；骚扰者|A breed of dog used for hunting hares, or a type of bird of prey.|例句：The harriers chased the hare across the field. 猎兔犬追逐野兔穿过田野。

例句：The hen harrier is a beautiful bird of prey.  雌鹞是一种美丽的猛禽。|近义词：hounds (for dogs), hawks, falcons (for birds), pursuers|反义词:prey (for birds)|词性:名词

harrier:/ˈhæriər/ (US), /ˈhærɪə/ (UK)| n.|猎兔犬；鹞；骚扰者|A single dog or bird, or a person who harasses.|例句：The harrier swooped down and caught a mouse. 鹞俯冲下来，抓住了一只老鼠。

例句：He felt like a harrier, constantly pursued by creditors. 他感觉自己像个被追债人不断骚扰的猎物。|近义词：hound, hawk, falcon, pursuer|反义词:prey|词性:名词
