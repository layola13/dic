disobeisance:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiəns/
n.
不服从，违抗
Refusal or failure to obey.
His disobeisance to the king led to his imprisonment. 他对国王的违抗导致了他的监禁。
Synonyms: disobedience, defiance, insubordination, noncompliance
Antonyms: obedience, compliance, submission
noun
