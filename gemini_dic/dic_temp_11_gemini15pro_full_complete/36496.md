coleridgian: /kəˈlɪrɪdʒɪən/ | adj. |柯勒律治的；柯勒律治风格的| Relating to Samuel Taylor Coleridge, his work, or his style.  Characterized by philosophical or imaginative contemplation, often with a mystical or supernatural element, and by a focus on the power of the imagination and the beauty of nature.|His poetry is distinctly Coleridgian in its use of imagery. 他的诗歌在运用意象方面明显具有柯勒律治的风格。|Near synonyms: Romantic, imaginative, transcendental, visionary |Antonyms: prosaic, mundane, realistic| adjective
