overlie:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪ/
verb
覆盖；躺在…上面；压在…上面
to lie over or upon; to cover superficially
The dark clouds overlie the city.  乌云笼罩着城市。

近义词: cover, overlay, overspread
反义词: uncover, expose
词性: verb
