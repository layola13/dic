fissiparism:/ˈfɪsɪpərɪzəm/
n.
分裂生殖，裂殖；分裂主义
Reproduction by spontaneous division of the body into two or more parts, each of which grows into a complete organism.  The tendency to break into small dissenting groups.
The amoeba reproduces by fissiparism.  变形虫通过分裂生殖进行繁殖。
The fissiparism within the party threatens its future.  党内的分裂主义威胁着它的未来。
近义词：division, schism, fragmentation, splitting
反义词：unity, unification, cohesion
词性: noun

fissiparous:/fɪˈsɪpərəs/
adj.
分裂生殖的；分裂的
Reproducing by fission. Tending to break up into small, separate groups.
Fissiparous organisms, like bacteria, multiply rapidly. 像细菌这样的分裂生殖生物繁殖迅速。
The fissiparous nature of the movement made it difficult to control.  这场运动的分裂性质使其难以控制。
近义词：divisive, schismatic, factional
反义词：uniting, cohesive
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fissiparus (“splitting, dividing”), from fissus (past participle of findere (“to split, cleave”)) + -parus (“bearing, producing”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bheid- (“to split, cleave”).
