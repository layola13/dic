affiliate:/əˈfɪliət/ /əˈfɪlieɪt/| n. |附属机构，分公司；隶属机构；分支机构|An organization that is officially connected with or controlled by another, larger organization.|Our New York office is an affiliate of our main office in London. |我们的纽约办事处是我们在伦敦总公司的分支机构。|近义词: branch, subsidiary, associate, chapter|反义词: parent company, headquarters|词性:noun

affiliate:/əˈfɪliət/ /əˈfɪlieɪt/| v. |使隶属；使并入|Officially attach or connect (a subsidiary group or a person) to an organization.|The college is affiliated with the University of London.|这所学院隶属于伦敦大学。|近义词: associate, connect, join, link|反义词: disaffiliate, separate, disconnect|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affiliatus*, past participle of affiliare "to adopt as a son," from ad- "to" + filius "son".
