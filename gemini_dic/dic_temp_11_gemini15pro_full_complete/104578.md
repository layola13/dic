kangaroo:/ˌkæŋɡəˈruː/| n.|袋鼠|A large marsupial that hops on its strong hind legs, found only in Australia and nearby islands.  It carries its young in a pouch.|例句：The kangaroo hopped across the field. 袋鼠跳过田野。

例句：We saw a mob of kangaroos at the wildlife park. 我们在野生动物园看到了一群袋鼠。|近义词：wallaby, wallaroo|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
