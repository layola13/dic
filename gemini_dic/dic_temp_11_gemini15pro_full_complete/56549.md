domine:/ˈdɒmɪniː/ /ˈdɑːmɪneɪ/| n.|老爷，先生；阁下；牧师|A title of respect, especially for a clergyman.|例句：The domine greeted his parishioners. 牧师向他的教区居民问好。|例句：The domine delivered a powerful sermon. 牧师发表了一篇强有力的布道。|近义词：sir, master, reverend, priest, minister|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dominus* ("lord, master").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*(d)éh₁mn̥* (“domestic animal, domesticated”), from *\*(d)emh₁-* (“to tame”). Related to "domain" and "dominate".
