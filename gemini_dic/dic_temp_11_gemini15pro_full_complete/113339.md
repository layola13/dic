luxuriate: /lʌɡˈʒʊəriˌeɪt/ | v. | 沉溺；尽情享受；生长茂盛 | To indulge oneself in something pleasurable, especially in a self-indulgent way; to grow luxuriantly. |  She luxuriated in a hot bath after a long day.  她劳累了一天后，尽情享受了热水澡。

The plants luxuriated in the warm sunshine.  植物在温暖的阳光下茂盛生长。| 近义词: revel, bask, indulge, wallow | 反义词: abstain, deprive | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin *luxuriari*, present active infinitive of luxuriō ("to be luxuriant, abound, be wanton, riot"), from luxus ("excess, extravagance, luxury").
