fontaine:/fɒnˈtɛn/ /fɑːnˈteɪn/ | n. |喷泉，泉水|A structure from which water is forced into the air in a decorative way, or the jet or stream of water itself. |例句：The children splashed and played in the fontaine. 孩子们在喷泉里嬉戏玩耍。

例句：The town square features a beautiful marble fontaine.  城镇广场上有一个漂亮的大理石喷泉。|近义词：fountain, spring|反义词: drain, sewer|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fontaine*, from Latin *fontāna* (“spring, source, fountain”), from *fōns* (“spring, fountain”). Doublet of *font*.
