词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *impostem*, from Old French *aposteme*, from Late Latin *apostema*, from Ancient Greek *ἀπόστημα* (apóstēma, “abscess”), from *ἀφίστημι* (aphístēmi, “I go away, revolt, separate”), from *ἀπό* (apó, “away from”) + *ἵστημι* (hístēmi, “I stand, place”).
