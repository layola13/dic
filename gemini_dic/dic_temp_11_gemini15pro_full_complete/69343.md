federal:/ˈfɛdərəl/ (US), /ˈfɛd(ə)rəl/ (UK)| adj.|联邦的；联邦制的|Relating to a system of government in which several states form a unity but remain independent in internal affairs.|The federal government has imposed new regulations. 联邦政府实施了新的规章制度。|近义词：national, central|反义词：state, local|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: via Old French from Latin foederālis, from foedus, foeder- ‘league, covenant’.
