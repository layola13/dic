intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/| v. |干预，介入，插手;  (指时间)介于，在其间;  |To come between so as to prevent or alter a result or course of events.|The central bank intervened in the currency markets today to try to stabilize the exchange rate. 中央银行今天对货币市场进行了干预，试图稳定汇率。|Synonyms: interfere, meddle, intercede, mediate|Antonyms: ignore, neglect, abstain|verb

intervent:/ˌɪntərˈvent/| n. |干预者，介入者|One who intervenes.|The UN acted as an intervent in the conflict. 联合国在冲突中充当了干预者。|Synonyms: mediator, intercessor|Antonyms: bystander, onlooker|noun
