audit-trails:/ˈɔːdɪt treɪlz/ | n. | 审计追踪，审计线索 | A chronological record of system activities that is sufficient to enable the reconstruction and examination of the sequence of events and/or changes in system activities. |  The company implemented comprehensive audit trails to ensure data integrity.  该公司实施了全面的审计追踪以确保数据完整性。 | synonyms: audit log, activity log, transaction log | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but a lack of audit trails implies a lack of accountability and transparency) | noun

audit-trail:/ˈɔːdɪt treɪl/ | n. | 审计追踪，审计线索 | A chronological record that shows who accessed a computer system, when they accessed it, and what actions they performed. |  The security breach was investigated by examining the server's audit trails. 通过检查服务器的审计追踪来调查安全漏洞。 | synonyms: audit log, activity log, transaction log | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but a lack of audit trails implies a lack of accountability and transparency) | noun
