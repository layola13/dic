bellicosely:/ˌbelɪˈkoʊsli/ | adv. | 好战地，挑衅地 | In a hostile or aggressive manner; showing a willingness to fight. | He spoke bellicosely, threatening to retaliate for any perceived slight. 他好战地说着，威胁要报复任何他认为的轻蔑。 |  The general bellicosely addressed the troops, urging them to conquer the enemy. 将军以好战的语气向部队发表讲话，敦促他们征服敌人。 | 近义词: aggressively, belligerently, pugnaciously, contentiously | 反义词: peacefully, amicably, diplomatically | 词性: 副词 | 词源: From Latin bellicosus ("warlike"), from bellum ("war").
