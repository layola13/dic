municipality:/mjuːˌnɪsɪˈpæləti/
|n.|市政当局；自治市；市辖区|A municipality is a city or town that has its own local government.|The municipality is responsible for maintaining the roads. 该市负责维护道路。|Synonyms: borough, city, town|Antonyms: county, state, province
|noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *municipalite, from Middle French municipalité, from Medieval Latin municipālitās (“citizenship, municipal freedom”), from Latin municipālis (“of a free town”), from municipium (“free town, free city”), from mūniceps (“citizen, inhabitant of a free town”), from mūnia (“duties, functions, offices, privileges”) + capō (“take, seize, receive, hold”).
