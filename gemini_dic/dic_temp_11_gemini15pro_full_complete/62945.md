enneagynous:/ɛniˈæɡɪnəs/ | adj. | 九性别的，具有九种性别的 | Relating to or characterized by having nine genders or sexes. |  This fictional alien species is described as enneagynous, possessing nine distinct sexes.  这个虚构的外星物种被描述为九性别的，拥有九种不同的性别。 | Example 2:  The enneagynous nature of their society challenged traditional human understandings of gender. 他们社会九性别的性质挑战了人类对性别的传统理解。 | 近义词: pangender, polygender | 反义词: bigender, binary | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek "ennea" (nine) + "gynos" (woman/female), extended to encompass multiple sexes beyond the traditional binary. 源自希腊语 "ennea"（九）+ "gynos"（女人/女性），扩展到包含传统二元性别之外的多种性别。
