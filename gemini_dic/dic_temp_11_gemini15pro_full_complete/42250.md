countenancers:/ˈkaʊntənənsərz/
n.
支持者，赞助者，容忍者
People who approve of or tolerate something, especially something wrong.
The countenancers of corruption must be held accountable.  腐败的支持者必须被追究责任。
synonyms: supporters, backers, patrons, tolerators
antonyms: opponents, adversaries, critics, objectors
noun
