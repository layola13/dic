misgraded: /ˌmɪsˈɡreɪdɪd/
Verb
错误评分的，评分不当的
incorrectly graded or assigned a grade.
The teacher misgraded several papers and had to recalculate the final scores. 老师错误地给几篇论文评分，不得不重新计算最终成绩。
The essays were misgraded due to a clerical error.  由于文书错误，这些文章被错误地评分了。
Near Synonyms: wrongly graded, incorrectly marked, inaccurately assessed
Antonyms: correctly graded, accurately assessed, properly marked
