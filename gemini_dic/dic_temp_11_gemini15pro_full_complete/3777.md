agitpropist:/ˈædʒɪtprɒpɪst/| n.|宣传鼓动员|A person who disseminates political propaganda, especially one employed by a government or political party.|The agitpropist distributed leaflets urging workers to join the strike.  宣传鼓动员散发传单，敦促工人参加罢工。|Synonyms: propagandist,  provocateur|Antonyms: pacifist, mediator|noun
