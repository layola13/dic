cag:/kæɡ/ | n. | 笼; 监狱; 牢笼 | A cage is a structure made of bars or wires used to confine animals or birds. | The bird escaped from its cag.  鸟从笼子里逃走了。 | 近义词: enclosure, pen, jail | 反义词: freedom, liberty | 词性: noun

词源：From Middle English *cage, from Old North French *cage, from Latin cavea (“cavity, cage, coop, den, enclosure, stall”), from cavus (“hollow”).
