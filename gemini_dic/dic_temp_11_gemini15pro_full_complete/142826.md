out-blossomed:/ˌaʊtˈblɑsəmd/
verb
开花超过；胜过…的花朵；繁花似锦地超越
to blossom more profusely than; to surpass in blossoming
The cherry tree out-blossomed all the others in the orchard. 这棵樱桃树比果园里所有其他树都开花更多。
surpass, exceed, outstrip, outflower
pale, fade, wither, shrivel
v.
