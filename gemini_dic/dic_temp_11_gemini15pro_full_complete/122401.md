mna:/ˈɛm ɛn ˈeɪ/ |n. | Mycenaean Linear A |  A syllabic script used for writing the Minoan language on the island of Crete from c. 1850 to 1400 BC. It has not yet been deciphered.  | The Phaistos Disc contains an inscription in Linear A. 法伊斯托斯圆盘上刻有线形文字A的铭文。 | synonyms: Minoan Linear A | antonyms: N/A | noun
