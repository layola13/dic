dithecal: /daɪˈθiːkəl/
adjective
双药囊的，有两个药室的
Having two thecae or sacs, especially pollen sacs.
The dithecal anther of the flower released pollen. 这朵花的双药囊花药释放了花粉。
Near Synonyms: bilocular, two-celled
Antonyms:  monothecal (rarely used), single-celled
Word Origin: From Greek *di- "two" + theke "case, box"
