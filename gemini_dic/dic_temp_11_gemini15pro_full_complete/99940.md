internodial:/ˌɪntərˈnəʊdiəl/ (US: /ˌɪntərˈnoʊdiəl/)
| adj. |节间的；位于节间的|Relating to or situated between nodes or joints, especially of a stem or axis.|The internodial growth of bamboo is remarkably fast. 竹子的节间生长速度非常快。|例句：The damage was concentrated in the internodial regions of the plant stem.  损伤集中在植物茎的节间区域。

例句：Internodal spacing is an important characteristic used to identify different bamboo species. 节间距是识别不同竹种的重要特征。|近义词：intersegmental|反义词: nodal|词性: 形容词
