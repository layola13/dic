noncrises:/ˌnɒnˈkraɪsiːz/ | n. |非危机，正常的挑战|Situations that are not crises, but may be perceived as such by some.  These are often normal challenges or difficulties that are manageable and don't pose a significant threat.| Many of the issues presented as crises by the media are really just noncrises that can be resolved with calm and rational thought. 媒体所提出的许多危机实际上只是非危机，可以通过冷静和理性的思考来解决。|Near synonyms:  normalcies, regularities|Antonyms: crises, emergencies|Noun
