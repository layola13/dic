aerophobia:/ˌeərəˈfoʊbiə/| n.|恐高症，飞行恐惧症|An extreme or irrational fear of flying.  It is a type of specific phobia, which is an anxiety disorder characterized by excessive or unreasonable fear triggered by a specific object or situation.|She couldn't go on the trip because of her aerophobia.|近义词:aviophobia, fear of flying|反义词:None|名词
