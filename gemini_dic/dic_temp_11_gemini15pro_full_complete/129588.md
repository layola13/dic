non-commission:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪʃən/ |adj.|非佣金的，无佣金的|Not receiving a commission, as in sales.  Referring to a role or position where payment is a fixed salary and does not include commission based on sales or performance.| This is a non-commission sales job.  这是一个无佣金的销售工作。 |Synonyms: fixed salary, salaried| Antonyms: commission-based, commissioned| adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (not) + "commission" (a fee paid for services, especially for sales). The word "commission" ultimately comes from the Latin "committere" (to commit, entrust).
