enzymatic:/ˌɛnzɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 酶的；酶促的 | Relating to or involving enzymes; catalyzed by or produced by an enzyme. |  The enzymatic activity of the liver is essential for detoxification. 肝脏的酶活性对解毒至关重要。|  The enzymatic breakdown of food begins in the mouth.  食物的酶分解从口腔开始。 |  catalytic, enzymatic |  non-enzymatic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From enzyme + -atic.  Enzyme comes from Greek enzumos "leavened," from en- "in" + zumē "leaven."
