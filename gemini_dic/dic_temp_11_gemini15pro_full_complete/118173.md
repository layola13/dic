menthene:/ˈmenθiːn/ | n. | 薄荷烯 | A cyclic monoterpene hydrocarbon, C₁₀H₁₈, obtained from menthol by dehydration. It is a colorless liquid with a minty odor, used as a flavoring agent and in perfumes. | Menthene is a component of peppermint oil. 薄荷烯是薄荷油的一种成分。| Menthene has a characteristic minty aroma. 薄荷烯有一种特有的薄荷香味。 | synonyms: p-Menthene, 1-methyl-4-(1-methylethyl)-cyclohexene | antonyms: N/A | noun
