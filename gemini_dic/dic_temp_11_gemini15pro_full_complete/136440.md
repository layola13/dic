nonrevenues:/ˌnɒnˈrevənjuːz/
|n.|非收入；非收益|Amounts of money received by an organization for activities not directly related to its primary business operations, or resources utilized that do not generate revenue.|The airline's nonrevenues from baggage fees have increased significantly.  该航空公司来自行李费的非收入显著增加。|Synonyms: non-operating income, other income|Antonyms: revenues, operating income|noun


nonrevenue:/ˌnɒnˈrevənjuː/
|adj.|非收入的；非收益的|Not generating revenue; related to activities or resources that do not produce income.|The nonrevenue flight carried employees and their families.  这趟非营收航班载着员工和他们的家人。|Synonyms: non-profit, unprofitable|Antonyms: revenue-generating, profitable|adjective
