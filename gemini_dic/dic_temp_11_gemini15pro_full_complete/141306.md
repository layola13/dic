open-neck:/ˈoʊpənˌnɛk/| adj.|开领的；敞领的|A garment, typically a shirt, with a collar that is open at the neck, not fastened with a button or tie.|He wore an open-neck shirt and jeans. 他穿着一件开领衬衫和牛仔裤。|Synonyms: open-collared, unbuttoned|Antonyms: buttoned-up, closed-neck|adjective

open-neck:/ˈoʊpənˌnɛk/| n.|开领衬衫|A shirt with an open neck.|He chose an open-neck from his wardrobe. 他从衣柜里选了一件开领衬衫。|Synonyms: open-collared shirt|Antonyms: button-down shirt|noun
