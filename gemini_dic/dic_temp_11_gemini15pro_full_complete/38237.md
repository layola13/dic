computerised:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzd/ (UK) /kəmˈpjuːtəˌraɪzd/ (US)| adj.|计算机化的，电脑化的|Controlled or operated by computer.  Formed by adding "-ised" (British English) or "-ized" (American English) to "computer".  The suffix "-ise/-ize"  means "to make or become". So, "computerise/computerize" means "to make something controlled or operated by computer".|The library has a computerised cataloguing system. 图书馆有一个计算机化的编目系统。|Synonyms: automated, electronic, digital, mechanized|Antonyms: manual, analog, mechanical|adjective
