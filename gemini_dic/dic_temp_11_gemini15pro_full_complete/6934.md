anˈkəʊniəl (UK), ænˈkoʊniəl (US)| adj.| 肘的，尺骨的|Relating to the elbow, specifically the region of the ulna (the larger of the two bones in the forearm).|The anconeal fossa is a triangular depression on the posterior surface of the elbow. 肘窝是肘部后表面上的一个三角形凹陷。|The patient complained of anconeal pain after the fall. 病人抱怨摔倒后肘部疼痛。|Synonyms: cubital, olecranal|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms relating to other body parts, e.g., carpal (腕的))|adjective
