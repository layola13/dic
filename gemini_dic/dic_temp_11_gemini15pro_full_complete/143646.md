outkilling:/ˌaʊtˈkɪlɪŋ/
verb
超越杀戮，在杀戮上胜过
Surpassing another in the act of killing; killing more than someone else.
The invaders were outkilling the defending forces two to one. 入侵者杀死的防御部队人数是后者的两倍。
近义词: outmatching, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: being outkilled, losing
词性: 动词
