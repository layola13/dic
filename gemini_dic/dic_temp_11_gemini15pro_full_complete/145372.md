overcapabilities:/ˌoʊvərˌkeɪpəˈbɪlətiz/| n.| 过度能力，过剩能力，超负荷能力|  A situation where an entity (individual, organization, system) possesses capabilities that exceed the demands or requirements placed upon it, potentially leading to inefficiency, redundancy, or underutilization. | The company invested heavily in new technology, resulting in overcapabilities that strained their budget.  这家公司在新技术上投入巨资，导致能力过剩，使其预算紧张。| synonyms: excess capacity, surplus capability, overcapacity, redundancy | antonyms:  incapacity, inadequacy, deficiency, shortage | noun
