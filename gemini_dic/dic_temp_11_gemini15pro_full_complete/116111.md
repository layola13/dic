marveling:/ˈmɑːrvəlɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːrvliŋ/| v.|感到惊奇，赞叹|being filled with wonder or astonishment.|例句：She was marveling at the beauty of the sunset. 她惊叹于日落的美丽。|例句：They were marveling at his skill. 他们对他的技术感到惊奇。|近义词：wondering, admiring, awestruck|反义词: unimpressed, indifferent|词性: 动词 (present participle of marvel)

marvel:/ˈmɑːrvl/ /ˈmɑːrvl/| n. v.|奇迹，惊奇；感到惊奇| something that causes wonder; be filled with wonder or astonishment.|例句：The Grand Canyon is a natural marvel. 大峡谷是一个自然奇观。|例句：I marvel at her strength and resilience. 我惊叹于她的力量和韧性。|近义词：wonder, miracle, phenomenon; wonder, admire|反义词: normality, commonplace;  disregard, ignore|词性: 名词，动词

词源: From Old French *merveille (Modern French merveille), from Vulgar Latin *miribilia (“wonderful things”), neuter plural of Late Latin mīrābilis (“wonderful, admirable”), from Latin mīror (“I wonder at, admire”), of unknown origin.
