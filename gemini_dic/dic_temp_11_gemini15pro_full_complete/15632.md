bairnishness:/ˈbɛrnɪʃnəs/
n.
幼稚，孩子气
The state or quality of being childish; childlike behavior or character.
His bairnishness was charming at first, but it soon became tiresome. 他一开始的孩子气很迷人，但很快就变得令人厌烦了。
childishness, immaturity, puerility, naivety
maturity, sophistication, worldliness
noun
