clenched:/klɛnʃt/| adj.|紧握的， clenched fist 紧握的拳头; 咬紧的，clenched teeth 咬紧牙关; 绷紧的，clenched muscles 绷紧的肌肉|firmly closed or squeezed together, often because of anger, determination, or fear|He stood there, his fists clenched, ready to fight. 他站在那里，双拳紧握，准备战斗。|Synonyms: gripped, clasped, tightened|Antonyms: relaxed, loosened, open|adjective

clenched:/klɛnʃt/| v.|握紧，咬紧，绷紧|(past tense and past participle of clench) to close or squeeze something tightly, especially your hand or teeth|She clenched her fists in anger. 她愤怒地握紧了拳头。|Synonyms: grip, clasp, tighten|Antonyms: relax, loosen, open|verb
