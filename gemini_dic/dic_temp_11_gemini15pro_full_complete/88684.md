hobanob:/hoʊbəˌnɑːb/ | n. | 交往，亲密来往 |  The act of associating familiarly with someone, especially someone of higher social status. | He enjoys hobanobbing with celebrities at parties. 他喜欢在派对上与名人交往。 | 近义词：socialize, fraternize, mingle | 反义词：isolate, shun | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a blend of "hob" (rustic, clumsy fellow) and "nob" (person of wealth or high social standing), suggesting a mixing of social classes.
