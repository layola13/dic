drought:/draʊt/| n. |干旱，旱灾|A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water.|The drought has severely impacted agricultural production.  这场旱灾严重影响了农业生产。|The region is experiencing a severe drought. 该地区正经历一场严重的干旱。|近义词：aridness, dryness|反义词: flood, deluge|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drought, *drouȝte, from Old English *drugoþ, from Proto-Germanic *druguþiz (“dryness, drought”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewgʰ- (“dry”). Cognate with Dutch droogte (“drought”), German Dürre (“drought”), droughtiness.
