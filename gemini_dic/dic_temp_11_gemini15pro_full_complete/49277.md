deliberations:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənz/
n.
考虑, 商议, 审议
Careful consideration or discussion of a subject.
The jury began deliberations on Friday. 陪审团在星期五开始审议。
The deliberations lasted for several days. 商议持续了好几天。

近义词: discussions, considerations, consultations
反义词: impulsiveness, recklessness

词性: 名词 (plural)
词源:  From Late Latin *deliberationem, accusative singular of deliberatio (“weighing, pondering”), from deliberare (“to weigh, ponder, consider”), from de- + librare (“to weigh”), from libra (“balance, scales, a pound”).
