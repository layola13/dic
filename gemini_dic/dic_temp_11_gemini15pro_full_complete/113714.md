macrobiotic:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɑːtɪk/| adj. n.|长寿的；长寿食物；|Relating to or following a diet thought to promote long life, typically one based on whole grains, vegetables, and avoiding meat and processed foods.|例句：She adopted a macrobiotic diet in hopes of improving her health. 她为了改善健康，开始采用长寿饮食。|例句：The macrobiotic restaurant offered a variety of dishes free of refined sugars and processed ingredients. 这家长寿饮食餐厅提供各种不含精制糖和加工成分的菜肴。|近义词：holistic, natural, healthy|反义词：unhealthy, processed|词性：形容词, 名词

macrobiotic:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɑːtɪk/| n.|长寿饮食；长寿法；|A way of life and especially a diet thought to promote long life, typically one based on whole grains, vegetables, and avoiding meat and processed foods.|例句：Macrobiotics emphasizes the balance of yin and yang in food choices. 长寿饮食强调食物阴阳的平衡。|例句：He studied macrobiotics and incorporated its principles into his daily life. 他学习长寿法并将它的原则融入到他的日常生活中。|近义词：health regimen, dietary plan|反义词：unhealthy lifestyle|词性：名词
