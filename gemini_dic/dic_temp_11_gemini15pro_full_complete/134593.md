nonlubriciously:/ˌnɑːnlubˈrɪʃəsli/
| adv. |不滑地，不顺畅地|Not smoothly or slipperily; lacking lubrication. |The door opened nonlubriciously, with a loud creak.  门打开得不顺畅，发出一声巨响。| The gears turned nonlubriciously, grinding against each other. 齿轮转动得不顺畅，相互摩擦。|近义词：roughly, unsmoothly, stickily |反义词: smoothly, lubricatedly, slickly
|词性: 副词
