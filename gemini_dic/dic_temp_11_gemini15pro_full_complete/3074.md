aeromechanic:/ˌɛroʊmɪˈkænɪk/ | n. | 航空机械师 | A person skilled in aeromechanics; a specialist in the design, construction, and maintenance of aircraft. |  The experienced aeromechanic quickly diagnosed the problem with the engine.  经验丰富的航空机械师很快诊断出了发动机的问题。 | Synonyms: aircraft mechanic, aviation mechanic | Antonyms: None | noun
