oratorical:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːrɪkl/
adjective
演说家的；雄辩的；演讲术的
Relating to the art of public speaking, or characteristic of an orator.
He delivered an oratorical masterpiece, captivating the audience with his powerful words. 他发表了一篇雄辩的杰作，用他强有力的话语吸引了观众。
The politician's oratorical skills were legendary. 这位政治家的演讲技巧是传奇性的。
近义词:eloquent, rhetorical, articulate
反义词:inarticulate, dull, uninspired
词性:adjective
