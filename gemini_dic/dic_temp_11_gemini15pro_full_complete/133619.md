nonforbearance:/ˌnɒnfɔːrˈbɛərəns/ | n. | 不宽容，不忍耐 | Lack of forbearance; refusal to tolerate or be patient. | The bank's nonforbearance on the late mortgage payments resulted in foreclosure. 银行对逾期抵押贷款的不宽容导致了丧失抵押品赎回权。|  The teacher's nonforbearance of cheating led to strict consequences for the student. 老师对作弊的不容忍导致了对学生严厉的处罚。| 近义词: intolerance, impatience, intransigence | 反义词: forbearance, tolerance, patience | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "forbearance" (from "forbear," meaning "to refrain from," ultimately from Old English "forberan")
