maceration:/ˌmæsəˈreɪʃn/| n.|浸渍，浸软; (水果等)在液体中浸泡变软; (尸体)腐烂|The process of softening or breaking down by soaking in a liquid.  The maceration of the fruit made it easier to puree. 水果的浸渍使其更容易制成泥。|Synonyms: soaking, steeping, softening, disintegration, decomposition|Antonyms: drying, dehydration, hardening|noun
