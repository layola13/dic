meteorography:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 气象学；气象记录 | The study of the atmosphere and its phenomena, especially as a means of forecasting the weather. |  Meteorography played a crucial role in early aviation.  气象学在早期航空中扮演着至关重要的角色。 | Synonym: meteorology | Antonym: N/A | Noun

Etymology: From Greek  *meteōro-* (μετεωρο-)  "high in the air" +  *-graphia* (-γραφία) "writing, description."
