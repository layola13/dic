duskily:/ˈdʌskɪli/| adv.|昏暗地，朦胧地|In a way that is dusky; dimly.|The room was duskily lit, creating a mysterious atmosphere. 房间里灯光昏暗，营造出一种神秘的气氛。|近义词：dimly, faintly, obscurely|反义词：brightly, clearly|词性:副词
词源: From dusk + -ly.  Dusk (from Proto-Germanic *duskaz) ultimately relates to Old English *dox, meaning "dark."
