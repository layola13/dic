ETYMOLOGY->
From crust + -y + -ness + -es.  "Crust" comes from the Old Norse "krusta" meaning "crust, shell."  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality. "-es" is a plural suffix.
