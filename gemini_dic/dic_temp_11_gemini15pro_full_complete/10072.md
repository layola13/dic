apocope:/əˈpɒkəpiː/ /əˈpɑːkəpiː/
noun
省略，截断（尤指词尾）
The shortening of a word by omitting the final sound or sounds.  The word "photo" is an apocope of "photograph".
Photography is often apocopated to photo. 摄影经常被缩写成照片。
This dictionary lists common apocopes. 这本词典列出了常见的省略形式。
