ligaturing:/ˈlɪɡətʃərɪŋ/
n.
连字；捆绑；结扎
The process of joining two or more letters together into a single character.  Also, the act of tying or binding something.
The manuscript used elaborate ligaturing of letters. 这份手稿使用了精心设计的字母连字。
The surgeon performed a ligaturing of the artery. 外科医生对动脉进行了结扎。
ligation, binding, tying, joining
separation, division
