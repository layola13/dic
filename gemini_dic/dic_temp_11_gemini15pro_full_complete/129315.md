注意事项:

* "Noi" is primarily used in more formal contexts or in specific regions of Italy.  "Ci" (us) and "Noi" are often interchangeable, but "ci" is generally more common in everyday spoken Italian.


The etymology of "noi" comes from the Latin "nos," which also means "we."  It's a direct descendant and retains the same fundamental meaning.
