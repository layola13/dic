foregather:/fɔːrˈɡæðər/ /ˌfɔrɡəˈðɛr/
verb
聚集，集合
To come together; assemble or meet.
We foregathered at the local pub for a drink. 我们在当地酒吧聚会喝酒。
The family foregathered for Thanksgiving dinner. 全家聚在一起吃感恩节晚餐。
近义词：assemble, gather, congregate, meet
反义词：disperse, scatter, separate
词性：v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From fore- + gather.  Fore- is an Old English prefix meaning "before," "in front," or "beforehand." Gather comes from Old English *gadrian, of Germanic origin.
