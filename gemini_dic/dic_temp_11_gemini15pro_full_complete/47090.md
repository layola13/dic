daubery:/ˈdɔːbəri/ (US), /ˈdɔːbəri/ (UK)| n.|涂抹；涂污；拙劣的艺术作品；粗制滥造的东西|The painting was so amateurish it looked like mere daubery.  这幅画太业余了，看起来就像涂鸦。|例句：The critic dismissed the exhibition as mere daubery. 评论家认为这次展览不过是些粗制滥造的东西。

例句：His early attempts at painting were little more than childish daubery. 他早期的绘画尝试只不过是幼稚的涂鸦。|近义词：smear, smudge, botch, mess|反义词：masterpiece, work of art|词性：noun
