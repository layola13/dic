major-leagues:/ˈmeɪdʒər liːɡz/| n.| 大联盟；最高级别；顶尖水平 | Refers to the highest level of professional sports, especially in baseball, but often used metaphorically for any field of endeavor.| He finally made it to the major leagues after years of hard work.  他经过多年的努力终于进入了大联盟。| synonyms: big leagues, top tier, elite level | antonyms: minor leagues, lower levels, amateur level | noun

major-leagues:/ˈmeɪdʒər liːɡz/| adj. (informal)| 顶尖的；一流的|  Used informally to describe something of the highest quality or caliber. | That new restaurant is major-leagues!  那家新餐厅真是一流的！| synonyms: top-notch, first-rate, excellent | antonyms: second-rate, mediocre, inferior | adjective
