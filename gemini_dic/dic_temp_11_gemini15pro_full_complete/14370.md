autopsied:/ˈɔːtɑːpsiːd/ |v. |验尸的；被解剖的 |  (Past participle of autopsy) Having been subjected to an autopsy; examined postmortem. | The body was autopsied to determine the cause of death.  尸体被解剖以确定死因。| Synonym: examined, dissected, postmortemed | Antonym:  N/A (autopsied describes a state, not an action with a direct opposite) | Verb (past participle)


Etymology: From autopsy + -ed.  Autopsy comes from the Greek "autos" (self) and "opsis" (sight), literally "seeing for oneself".
