calcimine:/ˈkælsɪmaɪn/ | n. | 刷墙粉；涂料 | A white or tinted wash for walls, ceilings, etc., consisting of whiting or zinc white, glue, and water. |  The walls were coated with a cheap calcimine. 墙壁上涂了一层廉价的刷墙粉。| 近义词: whitewash, distemper | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "paint" as a more durable alternative) | noun

calcimine:/ˈkælsɪmaɪn/ | vt. | 用刷墙粉涂 | To coat or wash with calcimine. | They calcimined the kitchen ceiling. 他们在厨房的天花板上涂了刷墙粉。| 近义词: whitewash, coat | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "strip" as in removing the coating) | verb
