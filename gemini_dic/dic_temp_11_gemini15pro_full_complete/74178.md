four-star:/ˈfɔːr stɑːr (US) / /ˈfɔː stɑː (UK)| adj.|四星级的；一流的|An adjective used to describe something of high quality, typically a hotel or restaurant, that has been awarded a rating of four stars.|例句：We stayed at a four-star hotel in Paris. 我们在巴黎住了一家四星级酒店。

例句：The restaurant received four-star reviews. 这家餐厅获得了四星好评。

近义词：top-rated, high-quality, deluxe, luxury|反义词：low-quality, budget, one-star|词性:形容词
