clerkless:/ˈklɜːrklɪs/
adjective
无职员的；没有办事员的
Lacking clerks or clerical staff; automated.
The new clerkless system allows customers to check themselves out.  新的无职员系统允许顾客自助结账。
The clerkless office relies heavily on automation.  这间无职员办公室高度依赖自动化。
近义词:automated, self-service, unmanned
反义词:staffed, manned
词性:adjective

词源:
clerk (n.) + -less (suffix)
clerk: From Old French clerc "clergyman, scholar," from Late Latin clericus "clergyman," from Greek klerikos "of the clergy," from kleros "lot, allotted portion, inheritance, office in the church."
-less: Old English -leas "free from, devoid of, without,"
