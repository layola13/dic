kidnapped:/kɪdˈnæpt/ /ˈkɪdnæpt/ | v. |绑架，劫持|To unlawfully seize and detain a person by force or fraud, typically for ransom or political coercion. |The businessman was kidnapped from his home.  这位商人从家中被绑架。

The kidnappers demanded a million dollars in ransom. 绑匪索要一百万美元的赎金。|近义词：abduct, seize, capture|反义词:release, free|词性:动词

kidnapped:/kɪdˈnæpt/ /ˈkɪdnæpt/ | adj. |被绑架的|Having been abducted or unlawfully seized and detained. |The kidnapped child was eventually found safe and sound. 被绑架的孩子最终安然无恙地被找到了。

The police are searching for the kidnapped heiress. 警方正在搜寻被绑架的女继承人。|近义词：abducted, captured|反义词:rescued, liberated|词性:形容词
