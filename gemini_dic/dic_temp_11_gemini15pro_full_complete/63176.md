entailing:/ɪnˈteɪlɪŋ/
verb
需要，意味着
involving (something) as a necessary or inevitable part or consequence.
Building a new fence entails tearing down the old one.  建造新的篱笆需要拆除旧的。
synonyms: involve, necessitate, require, demand, call for, mean
antonyms: exclude, preclude
词性: 动词
