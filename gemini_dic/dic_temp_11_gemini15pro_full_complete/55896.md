divorcement:/dɪˈvɔːrsmənt/
n.|离婚，分离，脱离|The divorcement of church and state is a fundamental principle of democracy. 政教分离是民主的基本原则。|The company announced its divorcement from the failing subsidiary. 该公司宣布脱离其亏损的子公司。|近义词：divorce, separation, detachment, disunion|反义词：union, marriage, connection|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *divorcement (Modern French divorcement), from divorcer (“to divorce”), from Latin divortium (“divorce”), from divertere (“to turn aside, separate”), from di- (“aside, away”) + vertere (“to turn”).
