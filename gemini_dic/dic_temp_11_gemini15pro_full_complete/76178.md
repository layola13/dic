fussers:/ˈfʌsərz/| n. |大惊小怪的人；小题大做的人；过于讲究的人|People who make a fuss about things; those who are overly concerned with minor details or who complain unnecessarily. |例句：The fussers complained about the slightest imperfections in the food. 那些大惊小怪的人抱怨食物中 slightest 的瑕疵。

例句：Don't be such a fusser; it's just a small scratch.  别这么小题大做了，这只是一个小划痕。|近义词：nitpickers, complainers, worriers|反义词:easy-going people,  laid-back people|词性:名词(plural)
