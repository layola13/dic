despised:/dɪˈspaɪzd/| adj.|被鄙视的，令人厌恶的|Past participle of despise.  Meaning strongly disliked or regarded with contempt.|He was a despised figure in the community. 他是社区里一个被鄙视的人物。|近义词：hated, loathed, detested, abhorred|反义词：loved, admired, respected|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *despis*, past participle of *despire*  "to look down on," from Latin *despicere* "look down on, despise," from *de-* "down" + *specere* "to look at."
