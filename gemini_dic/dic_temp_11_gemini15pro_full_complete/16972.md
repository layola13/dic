bastardization:/ˌbæstərdaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 歪曲，粗劣的模仿，劣化 |  The act of bastardizing something, especially a language or an artistic work, by adding new elements or altering it in a way considered improper or debased. | The movie was a bastardization of the original novel, changing key plot points and characters. 这部电影是对原著小说的歪曲，改变了关键的情节和人物。| 近义词: corruption, debasement, distortion, perversion | 反义词: purification, refinement, improvement | 词性: 名词

bastardize:/ˈbæstərdaɪz/ | vt. | 歪曲，粗劣地模仿，使劣化 | To corrupt or debase something, such as a language, artistic work, or idea, by adding new elements or altering it in a way considered improper. |  Don't bastardize the language with slang. 不要用俚语来歪曲这门语言。| 近义词: corrupt, debase, distort, pervert | 反义词: purify, refine, improve | 词性: 动词

词源: From bastard + -ize.  The word originally referred to children born out of wedlock, then extended to things considered impure or debased.
