Example:  He tripped and spilled coffee all over himself – lols. 他绊倒了，咖啡洒了一身 - 笑死我了。 | 近义词: lmao, lmfao, rofl | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider words expressing sadness or disapproval like "sad," "terrible," or "awful") | 词性: interjection, noun
