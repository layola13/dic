inopportunely:/ˌɪnˌɑːpərˈtuːnli/ /ˌɪnˌɒpəˈtjuːnli/
adverb|不合时宜地，不适当地|At an inopportune moment; inconveniently.|She arrived inopportunely, just as we were about to leave. 她很不凑巧地在我们正要离开时到达了。|
例句：He called at an inopportunely moment. 他在一个不合适的时机打电话来。
例句：The announcement was made inopportunely, right before the holiday weekend.  这个公告发布得不合时宜，正好在假期周末之前。
近义词：awkwardly, unfortunately, inconveniently, unseasonably|反义词: appropriately, opportunely, conveniently, suitably|词性:adverb
