nonassister:/ˌnɑːnəˈsɪstər/ |n.| 不协助者；不援助者 |Someone who does not assist or help others, especially in times of need.  A person who chooses not to participate or contribute to a collective effort.|  He was labeled a nonassister when he refused to help his neighbors during the flood.  他因在洪水期间拒绝帮助邻居而被称为不援助者。| 近义词: bystander, onlooker, observer | 反义词: helper, assistant, supporter | 词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non*, meaning "not"
assist (verb): from Latin *assistere*, meaning "to stand by, to help"
-er (suffix): forms nouns indicating a person who performs a particular action
