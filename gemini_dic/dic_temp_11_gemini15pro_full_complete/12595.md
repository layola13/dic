assai:/ɑˈsɑɪ/ | adv. | 非常，很，十分 | very; much; to a great extent. It is primarily used in music notation to indicate a fast tempo. | The allegro assai movement was breathtaking. 非常活泼的乐章令人叹为观止。 | 近义词: very, much, extremely, exceedingly | 反义词: little, slightly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Italian *assai*, from Vulgar Latin *ad satis* "to satisfaction," from Latin *ad* "to" + *satis* "enough."
