kemp:/kɛmp/| n.| 梳理（亚麻）,精梳机, 梳理过的亚麻纤维|To comb or disentangle (flax, wool, etc.) before spinning; to cleanse or dress (hair); cleaned and dressed flax; a coarse, rough, or tangled mass (as of hair).|The farmer used a kemp to prepare the flax for spinning. 农民用梳理机准备亚麻纺纱。|近义词：comb, card, hackle|反义词:tangle, mat|noun

kemp:/kɛmp/| v.| 梳理（亚麻）|To comb or disentangle (flax, wool, etc.) before spinning; to cleanse or dress (hair).|She carefully kemped her long hair before braiding it. 她仔细梳理了她的长发，然后编成辫子。|近义词：comb, card, hackle|反义词:tangle, mat|verb
