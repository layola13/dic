logics:/ˈlɒdʒɪks/ | n. | 逻辑学; 逻辑性; 推理方法; 逻辑电路; |  The study of the principles of reasoning, especially of the structure of propositions as distinguished from their content and of method and validity in deductive reasoning.  |  He studied philosophy and logics at university. 他在大学学习哲学和逻辑学。 | synonyms: reasoning, deduction, inference | antonyms: illogicality, irrationality | noun

logic:/ˈlɒdʒɪk/ | n. | 逻辑; 推理方法; 逻辑性; 合乎情理; | A system of reasoning.  | The logic behind this decision is flawed. 这个决定的逻辑有缺陷。 | synonyms: reasoning, rationality, sense | antonyms: irrationality, absurdity | noun
