esperance:/ˈɛspərəns/ | n. | 希望，期望；期待 |  A feeling of hope or expectation. |  She held onto the esperance that her lost dog would return. 她一直抱着她丢失的狗会回来的希望。| 近义词: hope, expectation, optimism, anticipation | 反义词: despair, hopelessness, pessimism | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French esperance, from Late Latin sperantia "hope," from Latin sperare "to hope."
