nutmeg:/ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ/ (US), /ˈnʌtmɛɡ/ (UK)| n.|肉豆蔻，肉豆蔻树|The spice nutmeg comes from the seed of the nutmeg tree. 肉豆蔻香料来自肉豆蔻树的种子。|例句：I added a pinch of nutmeg to the béchamel sauce. 我在白汁沙司中加了一撮肉豆蔻。|近义词：mace (referring to the aril of the nutmeg seed)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

nutmeg:/ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ/ (US), /ˈnʌtmɛɡ/ (UK)| vt.|用肉豆蔻调味|She nutmegged the eggnog before serving. 她在上蛋奶酒之前加了肉豆蔻。|例句：I like to nutmeg my sweet potatoes. 我喜欢在我的红薯中加入肉豆蔻。|近义词：spice, season|反义词: N/A|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *notemuge, from Old French nois muguete (“nut musk”), from nois (“nut”) + muguete (“musk”).
