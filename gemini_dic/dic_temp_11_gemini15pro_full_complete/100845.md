introrse:/ɪnˈtrɔːrs/ | adj. | 内向的，向内弯曲的 | (of anthers) opening inwards towards the center of the flower. | The lily has introrse anthers, shedding pollen towards the center of the flower. 百合花有内向的花药，花粉向花中心散落。| Example: The introrse anthers of the flower prevent pollen from being easily dispersed by the wind.  这朵花内向的花药阻止了花粉被风轻易吹散。| Synonym: inward-facing, inflexed | Antonym: extrorse | word type: adjective
