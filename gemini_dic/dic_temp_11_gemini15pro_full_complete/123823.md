mora:/ˈmɔːrə/ (US), /ˈmɔːrɑː/ (UK)| n. |音节；拍；莫拉|A unit of metrical structure in phonology, typically representing the concept of syllable weight. It is often used in the description of quantity languages such as Japanese. |例句：In Japanese, the word "kokoro" (heart) has three moras.  日语中，“kokoro”（心）这个词有三个音拍。

例句：The mora is a basic unit in the study of prosody. 音拍是韵律学研究的基本单位。|近义词：syllable (in some contexts), unit|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
