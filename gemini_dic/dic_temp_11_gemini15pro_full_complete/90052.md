hospitalize: /ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪz/ (British) /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪz/ (American)
hospitalize: v.
hospitalize: 住院治疗
hospitalize: To admit someone to a hospital for treatment.
hospitalize: He was hospitalized for a week after the accident.  他事故后住院治疗了一周。
hospitalize: admit, intern
hospitalize: discharge, release
hospitalize: verb
