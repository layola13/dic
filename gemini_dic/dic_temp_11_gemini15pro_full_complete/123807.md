moplah:/ˈmoʊplɑː/ /ˈmɒplɑː/| n. |马拉巴尔穆斯林，莫普拉人|A member of the Muslim community of the Malabar coast of southwest India, historically of Arab descent.  This term is now often considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its association with colonial-era classifications and the Moplah Rebellion of 1921.  Prefer using "Mappila" or "Malabar Muslim."|The moplah community has a rich history.  马拉巴尔穆斯林社区有着丰富的历史。

The term "moplah" is no longer widely used.  “moplah”一词不再广泛使用。|近义词：Mappila, Malabar Muslim|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific group) |Noun
