nonubiquitousnesses:/ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəsnəsɪz/
noun
非普遍性；非无处不在
The state or quality of not being ubiquitous; the lack of ubiquity.  The various instances or examples of not being present everywhere.
Due to the nonubiquitousnesses of charging stations, electric car owners still face range anxiety. 由于充电站的非普遍性，电动汽车车主仍然面临里程焦虑。
The study highlighted the nonubiquitousnesses of internet access in rural communities.  这项研究强调了农村地区互联网接入的非普遍性。
Near synonyms: rarity, scarcity, infrequency, uncommonness, unusualness
Antonyms: ubiquity, omnipresence, universality, pervasiveness
