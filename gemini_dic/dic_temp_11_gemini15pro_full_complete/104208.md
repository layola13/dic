juppon:/dʒuːpɒn/ | n. | 十分，满分;柔道服;（尤指女式）衬裙| A score of ten points, especially in judo; a petticoat, especially a full, underskirt worn by women. |  She received a juppon for her flawless performance. 她因完美的表现获得了满分。 |synonyms: perfect score, ten, petticoat, underskirt | antonyms: zero, failure, overskirt | noun

juppon:/dʒuːpɒn/ | n. | 柔道服（尤指上衣）|  A judo uniform, especially the jacket. | He carefully folded his juppon after the match.  比赛结束后，他仔细地叠好他的柔道服。 | synonyms: judo gi, judo uniform, keikogi | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "street clothes") | noun
