antimedical:/ˌæntiˈmɛdɪkəl/
|adj.|
|反对医学的；对抗医学的|
|Opposed to or hostile towards established medical practices.  Characterized by skepticism or distrust of conventional medicine.|
|He had a very antimedical attitude, preferring herbal remedies to prescription drugs. 他对医学持非常反对的态度，更喜欢草药而不是处方药。|

|Many cultures have antimedical traditions that emphasize spiritual healing. 许多文化都有反对医学的传统，强调精神治疗。|


|近义词：alternative, holistic, unorthodox, skeptical (of medicine)|
|反义词：pro-medical, conventional, orthodox|
|词性: adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->

From *anti-* (prefix meaning "against, opposed to") + *medical* (adjective relating to medicine).
