oversecurely:/ˌoʊvər səˈkjʊərli/
adjective
过于安全地；过分谨慎地
excessively secure; in a way that is too protected or cautious.
He wrapped the package oversecurely, using excessive amounts of tape. 他把包裹包得过分安全，用了过多的胶带。
The children were kept oversecurely indoors, rarely allowed to play outside. 孩子们被过度保护地关在室内，很少被允许在外面玩耍。
近义词:overcautiously, excessively cautiously, overprotectively
反义词: insecurely, carelessly, recklessly
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "securely" (adverb form of "secure").  "Secure" ultimately derives from Latin "securus" (free from care, safe).
