multicarinate:/ˌmʌltiˈkærɪneɪt/ | adj. | 多脊棱的；多隆线的 | Having many keels or ridges.  Specifically in biology, refers to shells or other structures with multiple prominent ridges. | The multicarinate shell of the ammonite was a striking feature.  这颗菊石的多脊棱外壳是一个显著的特征。| multicostate, polycarinate | smooth, unicarinate | adjective
