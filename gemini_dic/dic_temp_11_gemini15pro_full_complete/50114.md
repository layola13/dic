dentirostrate:/ˌdɛntɪˈrɒstreɪt/
adjective
齿嘴形的，具有齿状喙的
Having a toothed beak or bill-like projection.
The platypus is a dentirostrate mammal.  鸭嘴兽是一种齿嘴形的哺乳动物。
The extinct bird species had a dentirostrate beak for crushing shellfish.  这种已灭绝的鸟类有一个齿状的喙，用来压碎贝类。
近义词: toothed-billed
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly smooth-billed)
词性: adjective
