missyish:/ˈmɪsiɪʃ/| adj.|女子气的，娇气的|Resembling or characteristic of a missy; affectedly dainty or delicate.|She gave him a missyish slap on the arm. 她娇滴滴地拍了一下他的胳膊。|近义词：girlish, prissy, demure|反义词：boyish, tomboyish|词性:形容词
Etymology: From missy + -ish.  Missy is a diminutive of miss, used as a familiar term of address for a girl or young woman. The suffix -ish forms adjectives from nouns, indicating resemblance or characteristic.
