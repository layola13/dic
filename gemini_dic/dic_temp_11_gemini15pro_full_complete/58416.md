dubstep:/ˈdʌbˌstɛp/| n.|低音舞曲，回响贝斯|A genre of electronic dance music characterized by syncopated rhythms, heavy bass, and reverberant drum patterns. It originated in South London in the late 1990s.|I really like the heavy bass in dubstep music. 我很喜欢低音舞曲里的重低音。|Synonyms:electronic dance music, bass music|Antonyms:classical music, acoustic music|noun
