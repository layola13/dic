algonkin:/ælˈɡɒŋkɪn/ /ælˈɡɑːŋkɪn/| n. adj.|阿尔冈昆语; 阿尔冈昆人的|A family of North American Indian languages formerly spoken across a vast area from the Atlantic coast to the Great Plains, and now surviving mainly in eastern Canada.
