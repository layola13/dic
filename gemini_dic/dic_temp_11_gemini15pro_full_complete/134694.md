nonmasculineness:/ˌnɑːnˌmæskjʊˈlɪnɪsnəs/
noun
非男性气质，非阳刚之气
The state or quality of not being masculine.  Lacking traditionally masculine traits.
He embraced his nonmasculineness and pursued interests like ballet and flower arranging.  他欣然接受自己的非男性气质，追求像芭蕾和插花这样的兴趣爱好。
Synonyms: effeminacy, femininity, unmanliness
Antonyms: masculinity, virility
