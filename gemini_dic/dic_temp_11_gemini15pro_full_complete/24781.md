brose: /broʊz/ (US), /brəʊz/ (UK)| n. |肉汤，燕麦粥|A thin, watery porridge or broth made from oatmeal or other meal, sometimes with meat or vegetables added.|He made a simple brose for breakfast. 他早餐做了一份简单的燕麦粥。|The hungry travelers were grateful for the warm brose. 饥饿的旅行者对热腾腾的肉汤感激不尽。|近义词：porridge, gruel, broth|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid food")|名词
