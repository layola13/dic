blench:/blɛntʃ/ | v. | 畏缩，退缩；脸色变白 | To shrink or flinch; to become pale. | The sight of so much blood made him blench.  看到这么多血，他吓得脸色发白。| synonyms: flinch, recoil, wince, blanch | antonyms:  embark, advance, face, confront | verb

blench:/blɛntʃ/ | v. |  (使)变白; (使)褪色 | To make or become white or pale. | The harsh sunlight blenched the vibrant colors of the fabric.  强烈的阳光使织物的鲜艳颜色褪色了。 | synonyms: blanch, fade, whiten | antonyms: darken, color, stain | verb
