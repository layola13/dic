firmly:/ˈfɜːrmli/ (US) /ˈfɜːmli/ (UK)| adv.|坚定地，牢固地|In a firm or definite manner; securely; resolutely.|She held the child firmly by the hand. 她紧紧地拉着孩子的手。|近义词：strongly, securely, steadily, resolutely, decisively|反义词：loosely, weakly, hesitantly, uncertainly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *fermeliche*, from Old English *feormelice* (“firmly, strongly, constantly”), equivalent to firm +‎ -ly. Cognate with Dutch *fermelijk*, German *fermlich*.
