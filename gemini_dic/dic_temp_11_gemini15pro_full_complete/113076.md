lunacies: /ˈluːnəsiːz/
noun
疯狂行为；愚蠢举动
acts of extreme foolishness or insanity
His recent lunacies have caused concern among his friends.  他最近的疯狂行为让他的朋友们感到担忧。
The mayor's latest proposal is sheer lunacy. 市长最新的提议纯粹是疯了。
synonyms: insanities, follies, madness, craziness
antonyms: sanity, rationality, wisdom, sense
