broiling:/ˈbrɔɪlɪŋ/| v. adj. n.|烧烤，炙热，烤肉|To cook by exposing to direct radiant heat; intensely hot.|例句：I'm broiling some steaks for dinner.  我正在烤牛排作为晚餐。|近义词：grilling, roasting, scorching|反义词:freezing, chilling|词性:动词

broiling:/ˈbrɔɪlɪŋ/| adj.|炙热的，酷热的|Intensely hot.|例句：It was a broiling hot day. 今天天气酷热。|近义词：scorching, sweltering, burning|反义词:freezing, cold|词性:形容词

broiling:/ˈbrɔɪlɪŋ/| n.|烧烤|The process of cooking by exposing to direct radiant heat.|例句：The broiling of the meat made the kitchen smell delicious. 烤肉让厨房充满了香味。|近义词：grilling, roasting|反义词:freezing|词性:名词


词源: From Middle English *broilen, from Anglo-Norman bruiller, bruillir (“to roast, burn, scorch, broil”). Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *brūlire, from Latin prūrīre (“to itch, to burn”).
