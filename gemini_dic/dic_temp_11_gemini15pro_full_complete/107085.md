laden:/ˈleɪdn/| adj.|装满的，负载的| Heavily loaded or weighed down.|The ship was laden with goods.|近义词：loaded, burdened, fraught|反义词:empty, unladen|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hladan "to load," from Proto-Germanic *khladaną, from Proto-Indo-European *k’leto- "to lean, incline."
