labyrinthodont:/ˌlæbəˈrɪnθəˌdɑːnt/| n. |迷齿螈，迷齿类动物|An extinct amphibian-like vertebrate of the Paleozoic Era, characterized by complexly folded tooth enamel.|The museum displayed a fossil of a labyrinthodont.  博物馆展出了一块迷齿螈的化石。|Synonyms:  stegocephalian (more technical)|Antonyms:  N/A (extinct group, no direct opposite)|noun

labyrinthodont:/ˌlæbəˈrɪnθəˈdɑːnt/| adj. |迷齿类的|Of or relating to labyrinthodonts.|The labyrinthodont teeth were a key feature in their identification. 迷齿类的牙齿是识别它们的关键特征。|Synonyms: stegocephalian|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
