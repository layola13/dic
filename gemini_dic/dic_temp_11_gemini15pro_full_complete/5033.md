all-in-wind:/ɔːl ɪn wɪnd/ | adj. | 徒劳的，白费力气的 |  Exposed to the wind; wasted or useless, like efforts blown away by the wind.  Derives from the literal meaning of being completely exposed to the wind, implying vulnerability and lack of protection, thus extending to the metaphorical sense of wasted effort. |  All their efforts were all-in-wind, the project ultimately failed.  他们所有的努力都白费了，项目最终失败了。|  futile, vain, fruitless |  effective, successful | adjective
