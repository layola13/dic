non-condensing:/ˌnɒn.kənˈdensɪŋ/ | adj. | 非冷凝的 | Not condensing; specifically (of a heating appliance, such as a boiler or furnace) designed to vent exhaust gases directly to the outside without recovering latent heat of vaporization. | The non-condensing boiler is less efficient than the newer condensing model.  非冷凝式锅炉的效率低于新型冷凝式锅炉。|  The old furnace was non-condensing and wasted a lot of energy.  旧的熔炉是非冷凝式的，浪费了很多能源。 | near synonyms: conventional, traditional | antonyms: condensing | adjective
