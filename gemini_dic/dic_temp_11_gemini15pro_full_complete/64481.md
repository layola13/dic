ermit:/ˈɜːrmɪt/| n.|隐士，遁世者|A person who lives a solitary life and tends to avoid other people, especially for religious reasons. |例句：The hermit lived in a small cabin deep in the woods. 这位隐士住在森林深处的小木屋里。

例句：He became a hermit after the tragic loss of his family. 在痛失家人后，他成了一个隐士。|近义词：recluse, ascetic, anchorite|反义词:socialite, extrovert|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ermite*, from Late Latin *eremita*, from Greek *erēmítēs* "desert dweller," from *erēmos* "desert, lonely place."
