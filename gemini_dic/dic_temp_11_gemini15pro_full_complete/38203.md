compulsoriness:/kəmˈpʌlsərɪnəs/| n.|强制性，义务性|The state of being compulsory; the quality of being mandatory or obligatory.|The compulsoriness of wearing seatbelts has saved many lives.  安全带强制佩戴的规定拯救了很多生命。|近义词：obligation, necessity, requirement|反义词：optionality, voluntariness|词性:noun
Etymology:  From compulsory + -ness.  Compulsory comes from Late Latin compulsorius "obligatory," from Latin compulsus, past participle of compellere "to compel, force, drive together," from com- "together" + pellere "to drive."
