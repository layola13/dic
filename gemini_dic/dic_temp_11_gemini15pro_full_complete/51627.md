developmentally:/dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəli/| adv.|发展地，发育地|In a way that relates to the development of someone or something.|例句：Children develop developmentally at different rates. 孩子们的发育速度各不相同。|近义词：evolutionarily, progressively|反义词: regressively|词性:副词

developmentally:/dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəli/| adv.|发展上地，发育上地|Concerning or related to the process of growth and maturation, either physically, mentally, or emotionally.|例句：Developmentally, the child is ahead of his peers.  这个孩子在发育上领先于他的同龄人。|近义词：maturationally, ontogenetically|反义词: regressively|词性:副词
