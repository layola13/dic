jeez:/dʒiːz/
interjection
哎呀；我的天；(表示惊讶、不相信或恼怒)
A mild oath; a euphemism for "Jesus".  Used to express surprise, disbelief, annoyance, or exasperation.
Jeez, that's a lot of work! 哎呀，那工作量真大！
Jeez, I can't believe it! 我的天，我简直不敢相信！
近义词:geez, gosh, wow, oh my god, oh my
反义词:(No direct antonym, as it's an exclamation)
词性:感叹词
