limner:/ˈlɪmnər/| n.| 画家，彩绘师|A person who paints pictures, especially miniatures or portraits.|例句：The limner captured the queen's likeness perfectly.  这位画家完美地捕捉到了女王的肖像。

例句：The medieval limner illuminated manuscripts with intricate designs. 中世纪的彩绘师用复杂的图案装饰手稿。|近义词：artist, painter, portraitist, illustrator|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps art critic, or someone who destroys art) |词性: noun
