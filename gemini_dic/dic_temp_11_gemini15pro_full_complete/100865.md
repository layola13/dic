introverted: /ˈɪntrəvɜːtɪd/ (UK) /ˈɪntrəvɜːrtɪd/ (US)
adjective
内向的
Characterized by concern primarily with one's own thoughts and feelings.
She's quiet and introverted, preferring to spend time alone with a book. 她安静内向，喜欢独自一人看书。
He's always been introverted and finds social gatherings draining. 他一直都很内向，觉得社交聚会很累。
近义词: shy, reserved, withdrawn
反义词: extroverted, outgoing, sociable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin intro- "inwardly" + vertere "to turn".  The psychological sense dates from the early 20th century, popularized by Carl Jung.
