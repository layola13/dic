honky-tonking: /ˌhɑːŋkiˈtɑːŋkɪŋ/ /ˌhɒŋkiˈtɒŋkɪŋ/
honky-tonking: n. |在低级酒吧寻欢作乐；演奏乡村音乐|The act of frequenting or performing in honky-tonks (typically involving drinking, dancing, and listening to country music).|They went honky-tonking every weekend. 他们每个周末都去低级酒吧寻欢作乐。|Synonyms: bar-hopping, pub-crawling|Antonyms: relaxing at home, staying in|noun
honky-tonking: v. |在低级酒吧寻欢作乐；演奏乡村音乐|To frequent honky-tonks; to play honky-tonk music.|They were honky-tonking all night. 他们整晚都在低级酒吧寻欢作乐。|Synonyms: carousing, partying|Antonyms: relaxing, resting|verb
honky-tonking: adj. |低级酒吧的；乡村音乐的|Characteristic of a honky-tonk; related to honky-tonk music.|It was a real honky-tonking good time. 那真是一段在低级酒吧的美好时光。|Synonyms: rowdy, boisterous|Antonyms: quiet, peaceful|adjective
