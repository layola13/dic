cerargyrite:/səˈrɑːrdʒɪˌraɪt/| n.|角银矿|A mineral, silver chloride (AgCl), that is an important ore of silver. It occurs as a secondary mineral in the oxidized zone of silver deposits.  It is soft, sectile, and can be cut with a knife. It darkens on exposure to light.|Cerargyrite is a common ore found in silver mines.|近义词:horn silver, chlorargyrite|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *keras* (horn) + *arguros* (silver) + *-ites* (suffix denoting a mineral).
