gid:/ɡɪd/| adj.| 头晕的，轻浮的，渴望的| Feeling dizzy or lightheaded; frivolous and flighty; filled with a strong desire or craving. |  She felt gid after spinning around too many times. 她转太多圈后感到头晕。| 近义词：dizzy, lightheaded, frivolous, flighty, eager, keen | 反义词：steady, serious, apathetic | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *gíðr, meaning "possessed by a god," related to Old English gōd "good."  Originally referred to a mental state believed to be divinely inspired, later evolving to describe dizziness and then a more general sense of excitement or eagerness.
