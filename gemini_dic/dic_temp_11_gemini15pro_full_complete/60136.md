effector:/ɪˈfektər/| n. |效应器；作用物；执行器|A muscle, gland, or organ capable of responding to a stimulus, especially a nerve impulse.  A device that affects something else. |例句：The muscles are the effectors of the nervous system. 肌肉是神经系统的效应器。

例句：The robot arm is a key effector in the automated assembly line.  机器手臂是自动化装配线上的关键执行器。|近义词：agent, instrument, device, organ, muscle|反义词:receptor, sensor|词性:noun
