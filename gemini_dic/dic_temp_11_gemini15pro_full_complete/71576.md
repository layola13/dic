flashingly:/ˈflæʃɪŋli/
adverb
闪耀地，炫耀地
In a way that is bright and noticeable, often in a way that is intended to attract attention.
She smiled flashingly at the cameras. 她对着镜头闪耀一笑。
Synonyms: brightly, dazzlingly, glitteringly, sparklingly
Antonyms: dully, dimly, faintly
词性: 副词
