kingmaker:/ˈkɪŋˌmeɪkər/| n.|造王者，拥立国王的人，幕后操纵者|A person or group that has great influence on who gets elected to a powerful position, but does not hold that position themselves.|The wealthy businessman was a kingmaker in local politics, funding several successful campaigns.|近义词:power broker, influencer, manipulator|反义词:pawn, puppet|词性:名词

Etymology:
From king + maker.  The word dates back to the late 16th century.
