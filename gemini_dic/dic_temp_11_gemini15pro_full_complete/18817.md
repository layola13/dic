berthierite:/ˈbɛərθiəraɪt/ | n. |辉锑矿|A steel-gray metallic mineral, FeSb₂S₄, an important ore of antimony. |Berthierite is often found in hydrothermal veins. 辉锑矿经常在热液矿脉中发现。|Berthierite is a valuable source of antimony. 辉锑矿是锑的重要来源。|Synonyms: stibnite, jamesonite |Antonyms: N/A |noun
