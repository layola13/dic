conceptionist:/kənˈsɛpʃənɪst/
n.
构思者，概念主义者
One who specializes in the development of concepts, often in advertising or marketing.
The conceptionist came up with a brilliant idea for the new campaign.  这位构思者为新活动想出了一个绝妙的主意。
近义词:  idea generator, innovator, creative, designer, strategist
反义词:  imitator, copier
