decomposable:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊzəbl/ | adj. | 可分解的，可被分解的 |  Able to be broken down or separated into simpler components or substances. |  Organic waste is decomposable.  有机垃圾是可分解的。 | synonyms:  breakable, divisible, separable, analyzable | antonyms:  indecomposable, indivisible, inseparable | adjective

decompose:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊz/ | v. | 分解，腐烂 | To break down or separate into simpler components or substances, especially by natural processes. | The body began to decompose after death. 尸体开始死后腐烂。| synonyms: decay, rot, disintegrate, break down | antonyms: synthesize, combine, integrate | verb
