Malala:/məˈlɑːlə/ | n. | 玛拉拉 |  A Pakistani activist for female education and the youngest Nobel Prize laureate.  She is known for human rights advocacy, especially the education of girls and children, in her native Swat Valley in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, northwest Pakistan, where the local Taliban had at times banned girls from attending school. | Malala Yousafzai is a global icon. 玛拉拉·优素福扎伊是全球偶像。 |  Malala's bravery inspired millions. 玛拉拉的勇气鼓舞了数百万人民。 |  activist, advocate, champion | opponent, antagonist | noun
