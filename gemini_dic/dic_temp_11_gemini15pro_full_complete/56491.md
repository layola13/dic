domed:/doʊmd/ | adj. | 圆顶形的；拱形的 | Having a dome.  |  例句：The domed stadium gleamed in the sunlight.  圆顶体育场在阳光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词：arched, vaulted, rounded | 反义词：flat, concave | 词性: 形容词

domed:/dəʊmd/ | adj. | 注定失败的; 注定毁灭的; 注定要死的(多用于新闻报道) | certain to fail, die, or be destroyed, especially within a short period of time |  例句：The project was domed from the start due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，这个项目从一开始就注定要失败。 | 近义词：doomed, condemned, fated | 反义词：blessed, fortunate | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dome + -ed, ultimately from Latin domus "house."
