discommunity:/ˌdɪskəˈmjuːnɪti/
n.
缺乏共同体；社会失范；群体分裂
a lack of community or a state of social disorder and alienation
The rapid urbanization led to a sense of discommunity among the new residents. 快速的城市化进程导致新居民之间缺乏共同体意识。
The internet can both build communities and foster discommunity. 互联网既可以建立社群，也可以造成社群分裂。
近义词: disunity, division, alienation, fragmentation, atomization
反义词: community, unity, solidarity, cohesion, integration
词性: 名词
