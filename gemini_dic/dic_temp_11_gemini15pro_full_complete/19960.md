bioenergy:/ˈbaɪoʊˌɛnərdʒi/| n. |生物能源|Energy derived from biological sources, such as biomass.|Bioenergy is a renewable source of power. 生物能源是一种可再生能源。|The farm uses bioenergy to power its operations.  这个农场使用生物能源来为其运营提供动力。|Synonyms: biomass energy, renewable energy|Antonyms: fossil fuel, non-renewable energy|noun
