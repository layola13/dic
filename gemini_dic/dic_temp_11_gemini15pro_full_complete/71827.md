fleeter:/ˈfliːtər (US), ˈfliːtə(r) (UK)| adj.|更快速的，更敏捷的|moving or able to move more quickly.  A fleet-footed runner. |The new model is fleeter and more fuel-efficient than the old one.  这款新车型比旧车型更快，也更省油。|近义词：faster, quicker, swifter, speedier|反义词：slower, more sluggish|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *fleter, *fletre (“swift, nimble”), from Old English flēohtor (“more able to fly, swifter”), comparative of flēoht (“flight, flying”). Cognate with Scots fleeter (“nimbler, swifter”), German flüchter (“more timid, shyer”)
