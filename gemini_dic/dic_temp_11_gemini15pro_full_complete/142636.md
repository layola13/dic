ostler: /ˈɒstlə(r)/ (British) /ˈɑːstlər/ (American)| n. |马夫；旅店里的马夫|A person who takes care of horses at an inn or stable.|The ostler led the weary traveler's horse to the stable.  马夫把疲惫的旅行者的马牵到马厩。|Synonyms: stableman, groom, hostler |Antonyms: guest, traveler|noun


ostlers: /ˈɒstləz/ (British) /ˈɑːstlərz/ (American)| n. |马夫们；旅店里的马夫们|Plural form of ostler, referring to multiple people who take care of horses at an inn or stable.|The ostlers were busy tending to the horses of the arriving guests.  马夫们正忙着照料到达的客人的马匹。|Synonyms: stablemen, grooms, hostlers |Antonyms: guests, travelers|noun
