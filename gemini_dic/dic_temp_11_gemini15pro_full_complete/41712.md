corroboratively:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtɪvli/
adverb
确证地，证实地
in a way that confirms or supports a statement, theory, or finding.
The witness corroboratively testified that she saw the defendant at the scene of the crime.  证人确凿地证明她在犯罪现场看到了被告。
The scientist's findings were corroboratively supported by independent research.  这位科学家的发现得到了独立研究的确证。
Synonyms: confirmingly, supportively, substantively
Antonyms: contradictorily, disprovingly, refutingly
词性: 副词
