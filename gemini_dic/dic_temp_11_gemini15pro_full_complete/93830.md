imminentness:/ˈɪmɪnəns/| n. |即将发生; 临近|The imminentness of the storm prompted us to evacuate. 暴风雨即将来临，促使我们撤离。|The imminentness of the deadline caused a flurry of activity. 截止日期的临近引起了一阵忙乱。|近义词：proximity, nearness, closeness, impendence|反义词：remoteness, distance|词性:noun
词源: From Late Latin imminent- (stem of imminens present participle of imminēre 'to overhang, impend, be imminent'), from in- 'in, on' + minēre 'to project'.
