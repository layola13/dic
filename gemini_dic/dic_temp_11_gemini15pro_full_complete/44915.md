cryptics:/ˈkrɪptɪks/| n.| 谜题；隐晦的东西；密码|Something having a hidden or ambiguous meaning; a puzzle or enigma. |The newspaper's cryptics were notoriously difficult. 这家报纸的谜题非常难解。|近义词：puzzles, riddles, enigmas, mysteries|反义词: solutions, answers, explanations|noun

cryptic:/ˈkrɪptɪk/| adj.|隐秘的；含义模糊的；神秘的|Having a hidden or ambiguous meaning; mysterious. |She gave me a cryptic message about meeting at midnight. 她给了我一个关于午夜见面的神秘信息。|近义词：enigmatic, obscure, ambiguous, mysterious|反义词: clear, obvious, straightforward, explicit|adjective
