ethylenic:/ˌeθɪˈliːnɪk/ | adj. | 乙烯的，含乙烯基的 | Relating to or derived from ethylene.  | Ethylene is the simplest alkene. 乙烯是最简单的烯烃。|  The ethylenic bond is highly reactive. 乙烯键具有很高的反应活性。 | 近义词: unsaturated, olefinic | 反义词: saturated | 词性: adjective

词源: From ethylene + -ic.  Ethylene itself comes from ethyl + -ene, denoting the presence of a double bond.  Ultimately from Greek *aithēr (bright sky, upper air) + *hylē (wood) + -ene + -ic.
