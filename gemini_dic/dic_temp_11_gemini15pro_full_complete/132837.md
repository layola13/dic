noneducated:/ˌnɒnˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd/| adj.|未受教育的，缺乏教育的|Not having received formal education; uneducated.|例句：The noneducated population often faces greater challenges in finding employment. 未受教育的人口在找工作时往往面临更大的挑战。|例句：Access to education is crucial for empowering the noneducated. 获得教育对赋能未受教育的人至关重要。|近义词：uneducated, illiterate, unschooled|反义词：educated, literate, schooled|词性:形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + educated (past participle of educate, from Latin educatus, past participle of educare "to bring up, rear, educate," from ex- "out" + ducere "to lead").
