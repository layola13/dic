half-confederate:/ˌhæf kənˈfɛdərɪt/ | adj. | 半同盟的，半同谋的 |  Someone who is a half-confederate is partially involved in a plan or scheme, often with some reluctance or reservation. It implies a level of complicity that is less than full partnership but still significant. The prefix "half-" suggests a divided loyalty or a partial commitment to the shared goal.  |  He felt like a half-confederate in his friend's questionable business dealings. 他觉得自己在他朋友可疑的商业交易中像个半同谋。 |  semi-complicit, partially involved, half-hearted ally | reluctant participant, opponent, adversary | adjective

half-confederate:/ˌhæf kənˈfɛdərɪt/ | n. | 半同盟者，半同谋者 |  A person who is partially involved in a plan or scheme, often with some reluctance or reservation.  |  She acted as a half-confederate, providing information but not actively participating in the crime. 她充当了一个半同盟者，提供信息但没有积极参与犯罪。 |  semi-complicit, partially involved, half-hearted ally | reluctant participant, opponent, adversary | noun
