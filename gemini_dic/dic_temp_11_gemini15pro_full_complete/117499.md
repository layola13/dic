medusiform:/məˈdjuːsɪfɔːrm/ /məˈduːsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
伞状的；水母形的
Resembling a medusa; shaped like an umbrella or a jellyfish.
The jellyfish exhibited a classic medusiform shape.  这只水母展现出典型的伞状外形。
The creature had a translucent, medusiform body.  这个生物有一个半透明的，伞状的身体。
近义词:umbrella-shaped, bell-shaped
反义词: (No direct opposite, possibly elongated, tubular)
词性: adjective
