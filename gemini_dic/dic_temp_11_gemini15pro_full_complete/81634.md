greediest:/ˈɡriːdiɪst/| adj.|最贪婪的|Superlative form of greedy: most greedy.  Characterized by having or showing an excessive desire or rapacity, especially for wealth or possessions.|He was the greediest of the three brothers, always grabbing the largest share. 他是三兄弟中最贪婪的，总是抢最大的份额。|Synonyms: avaricious, covetous, rapacious, acquisitive|Antonyms: generous, altruistic, selfless|adjective
