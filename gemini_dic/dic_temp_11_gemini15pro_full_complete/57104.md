doulocracy:/duːˈlɒkrəsi/
n.
奴隶统治; 奴隶政体
A government or state in which slaves have power.  A hypothetical political system where slaves hold power.
The science fiction novel depicted a dystopian doulocracy where the human slaves had overthrown their alien masters. 这部科幻小说描绘了一个反乌托邦的奴隶统治社会，在那里人类奴隶推翻了他们的外星主人。

synonyms: slaveocracy,  slave state
antonyms: democracy, republic
