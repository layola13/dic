-inous:/ɪnəs/ | adj. | ...的；具有...性质的；充满...的 | Characterized by or resembling something specified; full of or abounding in something specified. | The air was luminous and resinous. 空气明亮，充满了树脂的香味。| 近义词: like, full of, characterized by | 反义词: lacking, devoid of | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin -inosus, from -inus (suffix forming adjectives) + -osus (suffix indicating abundance).
