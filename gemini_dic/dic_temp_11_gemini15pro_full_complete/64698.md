escapist:/ɪˈskeɪpɪst/
n.|逃避现实者；逃避主义者|A person who seeks distraction and relief from unpleasant realities, especially by seeking entertainment or engaging in fantasy.|He's a bit of an escapist, burying himself in video games all day. 他有点逃避现实，整天沉迷于电子游戏。|Synonyms: dreamer, fantasist, daydreamer|Antonyms: realist, pragmatist|Noun

escapist:/ɪˈskeɪpɪst/
adj.|逃避现实的；逃避主义的|Relating to or characteristic of escapism.|Escapist literature provides a temporary refuge from the stresses of everyday life. 逃避现实的文学作品提供了一个暂时摆脱日常生活压力的避难所。|Synonyms: escapism, unrealistic, dreamy|Antonyms: realistic, pragmatic|Adjective
