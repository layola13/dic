overpiteously: /ˌoʊvərˈpɪtiəsli/ | adv. | 过于可怜地，极其悲惨地 |  In an excessively piteous manner; with exaggerated or excessive expressions of sorrow or suffering.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) with "piteously" (meaning in a way that evokes pity). | She sobbed overpiteously, clutching her lost teddy bear. 她紧紧抓住丢失的泰迪熊，极其悲惨地抽泣着。| The dog whimpered overpiteously after being scolded.  小狗被责骂后可怜巴巴地呜咽着。| 近义词: pathetically, woefully, mournfully | 反义词: stoically, bravely, cheerfully | 词性: 副词
