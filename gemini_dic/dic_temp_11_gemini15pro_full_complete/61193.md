elute:/ɪˈluːt/ (US) /ɪˈljuːt/ (UK)| v. | 洗脱，提取 | To remove (an adsorbed substance) from an adsorbent by means of a solvent.  |  The protein was eluted from the column using a salt gradient.  用盐梯度从柱中洗脱蛋白质。

The gold was eluted from the ore. 从矿石中提取金。 | 近义词：extract, separate, desorb | 反义词：adsorb | 词性：动词

elute:/ɪˈluːt/ (US) /ɪˈljuːt/ (UK)| n. | 洗脱物，洗出液 | The material that has been eluted. | The elute was then analyzed by mass spectrometry. 洗脱液随后用质谱法分析。 | 近义词：eluent, extract | 反义词：adsorbate | 词性：名词
