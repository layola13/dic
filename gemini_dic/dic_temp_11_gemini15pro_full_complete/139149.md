obeyingly:/oʊˈbeɪɪŋli/
adverb
顺从地，服从地
in a way that shows that you are willing to obey

She obeyingly followed her mother's instructions. 她顺从地听从了母亲的指示。

The dog obeyingly sat down when commanded.  这只狗听话地按命令坐下。

近义词:submissively, compliantly, dutifully, obediently
反义词:defiantly, rebelliously, disobediently
词性:adverb
词源:From obey + -ing + -ly.  Obey comes from Old French obeir.
