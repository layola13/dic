nonilluminatingly:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋli/
adverb
无启发性地；没有解释清楚地
in a way that does not provide insight or clarity.
The professor responded nonilluminatingly to the student's question, leaving them more confused than before. 教授对学生的问题回答得没有启发性，让他们更加困惑了。
The report discussed the issue nonilluminatingly, offering no new perspectives. 报告对这个问题的讨论没有启发性，没有提供新的视角。
Synonyms: unclearly, vaguely, obscurely, confusingly
Antonyms: clearly, enlighteningly, informatively, helpfully
词性: 副词
