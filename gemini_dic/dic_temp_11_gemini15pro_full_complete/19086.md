betrothal:/bɪˈtrəʊθəl/ /bɪˈtrɒθəl/| n.|订婚，婚约|A mutual promise or contract for a future marriage.|The couple announced their betrothal at a family gathering. 这对夫妇在家庭聚会上宣布了他们的订婚。|Synonyms: engagement, affiance|Antonyms: divorce, separation|noun

betrothed:/bɪˈtrəʊðd/ /bɪˈtrɒðd/| adj.  n.|已订婚的，订婚者|Engaged to be married; the person to whom one is engaged.|They were betrothed for two years before the wedding. 他们在婚礼前订婚了两年。| My betrothed gave me a beautiful ring. 我的未婚夫给了我一枚漂亮的戒指。 |Synonyms: engaged, affianced, promised|Antonyms: single, unattached|adjective, noun
