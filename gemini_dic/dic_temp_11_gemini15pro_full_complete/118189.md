mentor-ships:/ˈmɛntɔːrʃɪps/ |n.| 师徒关系；指导关系；辅导关系 |A relationship in which a more experienced or knowledgeable person helps to guide a less experienced or knowledgeable person. | The company encourages mentor-ships between senior and junior employees.  这家公司鼓励资深员工和初级员工之间的师徒关系。| The university has a thriving mentor-ship program for its graduate students. 这所大学的研究生导师计划非常成功。 | synonyms: guidance, tutelage, coaching, apprenticeship | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider lack of guidance, neglect) | noun
