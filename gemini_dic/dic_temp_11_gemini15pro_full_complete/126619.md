nail-biting:/ˈneɪlˌbaɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人紧张的，扣人心弦的
Causing a great deal of tension or anxiety.
The final minutes of the game were nail-biting.  比赛的最后几分钟令人紧张。
The election results were nail-biting. 选举结果扣人心弦。
近义词:tense, suspenseful, gripping, thrilling
反义词:relaxing, calming, boring, uneventful
词性:adjective

nail-biting: /ˈneɪlˌbaɪtɪŋ/
noun
咬指甲(的习惯)
The habit of biting one's fingernails.
He has a nervous habit of nail-biting. 他有个紧张的咬指甲习惯。
Nail-biting can damage your nails and cuticles. 咬指甲会损害你的指甲和角质层。
近义词: onychophagy
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: noun


词源:  Compound of "nail" and "biting" (present participle of "bite").  由“nail”（指甲）和“biting”（“bite”的现在分词）组成。
