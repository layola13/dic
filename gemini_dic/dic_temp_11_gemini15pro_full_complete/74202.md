Fourier: /ˈfʊrieɪ/ (US), /ˈfʊrieɪ/ (UK)| n. adj.| 傅里叶; 傅里叶的 |  Referring to Jean Baptiste Joseph Fourier (1768-1830), a French mathematician and physicist known for his work on the representation of functions as sums of trigonometric functions (Fourier series) and its applications to the study of heat flow.  It can also refer to the transforms and analysis methods derived from his work. | The Fourier transform is a powerful tool in signal processing. 傅里叶变换是信号处理中的强大工具。|  Fourier analysis is used to decompose complex waveforms into simpler sinusoidal components. 傅里叶分析用于将复杂的波形分解成更简单的正弦分量。 | 近义词: (For Fourier transform) spectral | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective
