expiring:/ɪkˈspaɪərɪŋ/
adjective
即将到期的，失效的
coming to an end; ceasing to be valid
The expiring contract needs to be renewed.  即将到期的合同需要续签。
My driver's license is expiring soon. 我的驾照快过期了。
近义词:lapsing, terminating, ending
反义词:valid, current, continuing
词性:adjective

词源: From the Latin verb *expīrāre*, meaning "to breathe out, to die out."  The sense of "coming to an end" developed from the idea of a breath or life coming to an end.
