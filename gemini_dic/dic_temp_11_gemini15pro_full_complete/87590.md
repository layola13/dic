heterospecific:/ˌhetərəʊspəˈsɪfɪk/
adjective
异种的
Relating to or involving individuals of different species.  Belonging to a different species.
Heterospecific competition occurs when two or more species compete for the same limited resource. 异种竞争发生在两个或多个物种竞争相同的有限资源时。

Interspecific interactions, such as heterospecific pollination, can have significant impacts on ecosystem dynamics.  物种间的相互作用，例如异种授粉，会对生态系统动态产生重大影响。

近义词:interspecific
反义词:conspecific
词性:adjective
