noneccentrically:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsentrɪkli/
adverb
非偏心地，不古怪地
Not eccentrically; in a conventional or normal way.
She dressed noneccentrically in a simple blue suit. 她穿着简单蓝色套装，一点也不古怪。
The building was designed noneccentrically, blending in with its surroundings.  这座建筑设计得并不古怪，与周围环境融为一体。
近义词: conventionally, normally, ordinarily, typically
反义词: eccentrically, bizarrely, strangely, oddly
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + eccentric (adjective meaning "unconventional and slightly strange") + -ally (adverbial suffix)
