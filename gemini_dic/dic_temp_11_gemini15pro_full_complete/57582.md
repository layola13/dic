dramaturgically:/ˌdræmətɜːrˈdʒɪkli/
adverb
戏剧地；从戏剧构作的角度
In a manner relating to the dramatic composition and structure of a play or performance.
The play was dramaturgically complex, weaving together multiple timelines and perspectives. 这部戏在戏剧结构上很复杂，交织了多个时间线和视角。
The director approached the scene dramaturgically, focusing on the subtext and the characters' motivations. 导演从戏剧构作的角度处理这个场景，着重于潜台词和人物动机。
近义词: theatrically, dramatically
反义词: plainly, simply
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From dramaturgy + -ically.  Dramaturgy comes from French *dramaturgie*, from Greek *δρᾶμα* (drama, "deed, act, play") + *-ουργία* (-ourgia, "working, activity").
