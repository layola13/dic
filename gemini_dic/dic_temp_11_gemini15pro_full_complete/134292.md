noninhibitive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɪbɪtɪv/| adj.|非抑制性的，不抑制的|Not inhibiting or tending to inhibit; allowing or encouraging free expression or activity.|The noninhibitive atmosphere of the workshop allowed participants to share their ideas openly. 研讨会的自由氛围让参与者可以公开分享他们的想法。|The new drug has a noninhibitive effect on enzyme activity.  这种新药对酶活性没有抑制作用。|近义词:permissive,  facilitative,  encouraging|反义词:inhibitory, restrictive, suppressive|词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
inhibit (verb): from Latin inhibitus, past participle of inhibere "to restrain, check, prohibit," from in- "in, on" + habere "to hold"
-ive (suffix): forming adjectives meaning "having the nature or quality of," from French -if, -ive and directly from Latin -ivus.
