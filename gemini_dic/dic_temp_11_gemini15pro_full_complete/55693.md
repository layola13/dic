diuresis:/ˌdaɪjʊˈriːsɪs/
noun
利尿；利尿作用
Increased excretion of urine.  The increased production of urine.
The doctor prescribed a medication to induce diuresis. 医生开了药来促进利尿。
Diuresis is a common side effect of this medication. 利尿是这种药物的常见副作用。
synonyms: polyuria, micturition
antonyms: oliguria, anuria
