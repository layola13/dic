nonduality:/nɒn.djuːˈæl.ɪ.ti/ |n.|非二元性，不二|The concept of nonduality emphasizes the interconnectedness of all things and the illusion of separation.|  Nonduality is a core concept in Advaita Vedanta. 非二元性是吠檀多不二论的核心概念。| synonyms: oneness, unity, wholeness | antonyms: duality, separation, division | noun

Etymology:  From non- + duality.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "duality" refers to the state of being two or divided.
