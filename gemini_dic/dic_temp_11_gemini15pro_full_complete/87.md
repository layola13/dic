aaronic: /ˌeɪˈrɒnɪk/ /ˌɛrˈɑːnɪk/
adjective
亚伦的；祭司的
Relating to Aaron, the first high priest of the Israelites, or to the priesthood descended from him.
The Aaronic priesthood was responsible for the sacrificial system in the Tabernacle. 亚伦的祭司负责圣所的献祭制度。
The high priest wore special Aaronic vestments. 大祭司穿着特殊的亚伦祭司服。
近义词: priestly, sacerdotal
反义词: lay, secular
词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin Aaronicus, from Greek Aarōnikos, from Aaron + -ikos.
