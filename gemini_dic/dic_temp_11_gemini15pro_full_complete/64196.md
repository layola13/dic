| adj. | 等角的；所有角都相等的 | Having all angles equal. | An equiangled triangle is also equilateral. 等角三角形也是等边三角形。 |  synonym: isogonal, equilateral (for triangles) | antonym:  oblique, scalene (for triangles) |词性:形容词
