fatherlessness: /ˈfɑːðərləsnəs/ | n. | 无父状态，缺乏父爱 | The state of being without a father, either through death, abandonment, or other circumstances. This often has negative social and psychological impacts on children. |  The cycle of fatherlessness often perpetuates poverty and crime. 缺乏父爱的恶性循环常常导致贫困和犯罪。

 The study examined the correlation between fatherlessness and academic performance. 这项研究调查了无父状态与学业成绩之间的相关性。 | 近义词: father absence, paternal deprivation | 反义词:  paternal presence, two-parent family | 词性: noun
