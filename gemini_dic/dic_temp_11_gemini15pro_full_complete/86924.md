hemoradiotherapy:/ˌhiːmoʊˌreɪdioʊˈθɛrəpi/ | n. | 放化疗；血液放射治疗 | Treatment of disease, especially cancer, using both radiation therapy and chemotherapy. | The patient underwent concurrent chemoradiotherapy for advanced head and neck cancer. 该患者接受了同步放化疗治疗晚期头颈癌。 | Synonyms: combined modality therapy, chemo-radiation | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider single modality therapy, such as radiation therapy alone or chemotherapy alone) | noun
