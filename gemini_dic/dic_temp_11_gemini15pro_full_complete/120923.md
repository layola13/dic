miscataloguing:/ˌmɪskætəlɒɡɪŋ/ | n. | 错误分类 | The act of incorrectly classifying or categorizing something, especially in a library or archive. |  The librarian apologized for the miscataloguing of the book. 图书管理员为书籍的错误分类道歉。| misclassification, misfiling | correct cataloguing | noun


miscatalogue:/ˌmɪskætəlɒɡ/ | vt. | 错误分类 | To classify or categorize something incorrectly, especially in a library or archive. | The new assistant miscatalogued several important historical documents.  新来的助理把几份重要的历史文件错误分类了。| misclassify, misfile | correctly catalogue | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
mis- (prefix): from Old English, meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "incorrectly."
catalogue (verb/noun): from French catalogue, from Late Latin catalogus, from Greek katalogos ("list," "register").
