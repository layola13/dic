calibrators:/ˈkælɪˌbreɪtərz/
noun
校准器；校准员
Instruments used to standardize or adjust other instruments.  People who perform calibration.
The technicians used calibrators to ensure the accuracy of the pressure gauges. 技术人员使用校准器来确保压力表的准确性。
synonyms:  gauges, standards, meters, instruments, adjusters
antonyms:  distorters, misaligners

calibrate:/ˈkælɪˌbreɪt/
verb
校准；标定
To check, adjust, or determine by comparison with a standard.
The scientist calibrated the instrument before starting the experiment. 科学家在开始实验前校准了仪器。
They calibrated the scale to ensure accurate measurements. 他们校准了秤以确保测量准确。
synonyms:  adjust, standardize, measure, regulate, fine-tune
antonyms:  distort, misalign
词性: 动词
