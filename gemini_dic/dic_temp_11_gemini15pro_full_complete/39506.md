connusor:/kəˈnʌsər/ | n. | 鉴赏家；行家 | A person who has expert knowledge and keen discrimination in some field, especially in the fine arts or in matters of taste. |  例句：He is a connosor of fine wines. 他是一位葡萄酒鉴赏家。

例句：She is a connosor of modern art. 她是一位现代艺术鉴赏家。

近义词：expert, specialist, aficionado, connoisseur | 反义词：amateur, novice, beginner | 词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *connoisseur*, from Old French *conoisseor*, from *conoistre* "to know," from Latin *cognoscere* "to get to know, learn," from *co-* "together" + *gnoscere* "to know."
