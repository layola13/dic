dichotomous:/daɪˈkɑːtəməs/| adj.|二分的，二叉的|Divided or dividing into two parts or classifications.|The survey presented a dichotomous choice: agree or disagree.  这项调查提出了一个二分法的选择：同意或不同意。|The plant has dichotomous branching. 这种植物有二叉分枝。|Synonyms: binary, dual, twofold|Antonyms: multiple, diverse|adjective

dichotomy:/daɪˈkɑːtəmi/| n.|二分法， dichotomy |A division or contrast between two things that are or are represented as being opposed or entirely different.|There is a dichotomy between theory and practice. 理论和实践之间存在着脱节。|The dichotomy between good and evil is a common theme in literature. 善与恶的二分法是文学中的一个常见主题。|Synonyms: division, contrast, split|Antonyms: unity, agreement|noun
