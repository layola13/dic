attent:/əˈtɛnt/| adj.|专心的，留意的|Paying close attention to something; carefully observant.|例句：The students were very attent during the lecture. 学生们在讲座期间非常专心。|例句：Please be attent to the instructions. 请注意这些指示。|近义词：attentive, heedful, observant|反义词：inattentive, distracted, negligent|词性:形容词
attent:/əˈtɛnt/| v. (obsolete)|照料，护理|To take care of; to attend to.|例句：She attentively attented her sick mother. 她细心地照料她生病的母亲。(古语用法，现在很少使用)|例句：The nurse attented the patient's wounds.  护士护理病人的伤口。(古语用法，现在很少使用)|近义词：tend, care for, minister to|反义词：neglect|词性:动词

词源: From Middle French *attendant*, present participle of *attendre* ("to wait for, expect"), from Latin *attendere* ("to stretch toward, direct one's attention to, wait for"), from *ad-* ("to") + *tendere* ("to stretch").
