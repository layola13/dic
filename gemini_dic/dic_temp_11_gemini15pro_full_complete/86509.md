heirloom:/ˈerluːm/
n.
传家宝，祖传之物
a valuable object that has belonged to a family for many years
My grandmother left me a diamond ring as an heirloom.
我的祖母留给我一枚钻石戒指作为传家宝。

近义词:inheritance, legacy, bequest
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用modern artifact 或 mass-produced item)
词性: 名词
