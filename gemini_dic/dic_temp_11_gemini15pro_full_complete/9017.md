antimachine:/ˌænti məˈʃiːn/
adjective
反对机器的；反机械化的
opposed to machinery or automation, especially to industrialization or technological advancement.
The Luddites were an antimachine movement in 19th-century England.  19世纪的英国，卢德运动是一个反机器化的运动。

His antimachine stance made him unpopular in the technologically advanced society.  他反对机器的立场让他在这个技术先进的社会不受欢迎。

近义词: Luddite, technophobic
反义词: pro-technology, automation-friendly
词性: adjective
