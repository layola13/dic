countereffort:/ˈkaʊntərˌɛfərt/ | n. | 反击；对抗；应对 | An action or effort made to oppose or counteract something. | The government launched a countereffort to combat the spread of misinformation. 政府发起了一项反击行动，以打击错误信息的传播。 |  The team mounted a spirited countereffort in the second half. 球队在下半场发动了有力的反击。 |Synonyms: counteraction, countermove, countermeasure, response, reaction | Antonyms: effort, support, assistance | noun
