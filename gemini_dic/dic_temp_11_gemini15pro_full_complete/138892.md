nutation:/njuːˈteɪʃn/ | n. |章动;点头;摆动|A small, cyclic variation in the precession of the Earth's axis, caused by the gravitational attraction of the Moon and Sun.|The Earth's nutation causes a slight wobble in its rotation.|近义词:wobble, oscillation, fluctuation|反义词:stability, stillness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nutatio*, from Latin nutare (“to nod”), frequentative of nuere (“to nod”).
