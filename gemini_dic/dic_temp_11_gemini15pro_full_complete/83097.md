gusset:/ˈɡʌsɪt/| n. |三角形衬布，角撑板，腋下三角形插布|A piece of material sewn into a garment to strengthen or enlarge a part of it, such as the crotch of trousers or the underarm of a shirt.|The gusset under the arm of the shirt allows for greater freedom of movement. 衬衫腋下的三角形衬布可以提供更大的活动自由。|
The tent had extra gussets to reinforce it against strong winds.  帐篷有额外的角撑板来加强它抵御强风。|Synonyms: insert, reinforcement, patch|Antonyms: N/A|noun
