arbitratrix:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪtrɪks/
noun
女仲裁人，女裁决人
A female arbitrator; a woman who settles a dispute or controversy between parties.
The arbitratrix listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making her decision. 女仲裁人仔细听取了双方的论点，然后才做出决定。
Synonym: arbitrator, judge, mediator, referee
Antonym: disputant, litigant
n.
