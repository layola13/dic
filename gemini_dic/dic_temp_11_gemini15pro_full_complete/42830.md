courrier:/ˈkʊriər/ (US), /ˈkʌriə(r)/ (UK)| n.|信使，快递，邮递员，情报员|A person or company that carries messages, packages, or important documents.|例句：The courrier delivered the package this morning. 快递员今天早上送来了包裹。

例句：She worked as a courrier for a diplomatic mission. 她在一个外交使团担任信使。|近义词：messenger, carrier, envoy, courier (alternative spelling)|反义词:recipient|noun
