blasted:/ˈblɑːstɪd/ /ˈblæstɪd/| adj.|该死的；讨厌的；极坏的|Damaged or destroyed by an explosion. Also used as a mild curse word expressing annoyance or frustration.|例句：The blasted car won't start. 这该死的车发动不了了。|近义词：damned, cursed, darned, annoying, frustrating|反义词:blessed, wonderful, great|词性:形容词

blasted:/ˈblɑːstɪd/ /ˈblæstɪd/| v.|爆破；摧毁（blast的过去式和过去分词）|Past participle of "blast", meaning to damage or destroy by an explosion.|例句：The building was blasted by the terrorists. 这栋楼被恐怖分子炸毁了。|近义词：destroyed, exploded, demolished|反义词:built, constructed, repaired|词性:动词
