carefully:/ˈkɛərfəli/ (US), /ˈkeəfəli/ (UK)| adv.|仔细地，小心地|In a way that involves giving attention to detail and avoiding mistakes or harm.  It modifies a verb to describe how an action is performed.|She carefully placed the vase on the table. 她小心翼翼地把花瓶放在桌子上。|近义词：meticulously, cautiously, attentively, prudently|反义词：carelessly, recklessly, negligently|词性:副词
词源：From Middle English *carefully*, equivalent to *careful* + *-ly*.  Ultimately from Old English *cearu* (“care, anxiety”) + *-ful* + *-ly*.
