ETYMOLOGY->
From engaging + -ness.  "Engaging" comes from the Old French "engager," meaning "to pledge" or "to bind by a promise," ultimately from the Frankish "*wadius," meaning "pledge" or "security." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
