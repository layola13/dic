flatwise:/ˈflætwaɪz/| adv.|平放地，水平地|With the flat part down; horizontally.  Lying flat, especially with a broad surface in contact with something else.|He laid the boards flatwise on the ground. 他把木板平放在地上。|Synonyms: horizontally, flatly|Antonyms: edgewise, vertically|adverb

flatwise:/ˈflætwaɪz/| adj.|平的，扁平的|(Less common) Flat; having a broad, level surface.|A flatwise surface. 一个平坦的表面。|Synonyms: flat, level, planar|Antonyms: curved, uneven, bumpy|adjective
