advisablenesses:/ədˌvaɪzəbəlˈnɛsɪz/| n. |明智之举；适当；可取性|The state or quality of being advisable; propriety; expedience. |The advisablenesses of such a course of action are questionable. 采取这种行动的明智性值得怀疑。|近义词：propriety, expedience, suitability, wisdom, prudence|反义词：inadvisability, impropriety, inexpedience, unsuitability, folly, imprudence|词性:名词

词源: From advisable + -ness.  Advisable comes from the verb advise, which ultimately derives from the Latin ad- (to) + visum (seen, considered), hence "to give counsel to."
