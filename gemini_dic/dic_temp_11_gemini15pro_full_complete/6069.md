If you were looking for information on the word "what" as a question word, here's an analysis:

what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. |
Example: What is your name?  你叫什么名字？

Example: What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！


近义词: which (in some contexts)
