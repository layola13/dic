fructidor:/frʊkˈtiːdɔːr/
n.
果月（法国共和历的第十二个月，8月18日至9月16日）
The twelfth month of the French Republican Calendar (August 18-September 16).
The coup of 18 Fructidor resulted in the deportation of many prominent royalists.  果月十八日的政变导致许多著名的保皇党人被驱逐出境。
近义词:Thermidor, Messidor, Floréal (other months in the French Republican Calendar)
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fructidor*, from Latin *fructus* (fruit) + *-dor* (giving, bearing).  Coined during the French Revolution.
