dronte:/ˈdrɒntiː/| n.|渡渡鸟|An extinct flightless bird, Raphus cucullatus, that was endemic to the island of Mauritius. It was about three feet tall and weighed about 50 pounds, with blue-gray plumage, a large head, and a heavy hooked bill.  It became extinct in the late 17th century.|The dodo, or dronte, was a symbol of extinction.|近义词：dodo|反义词:N/A|noun
