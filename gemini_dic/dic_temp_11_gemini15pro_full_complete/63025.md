enragedness:/ɪnˈreɪdʒdnəs/| n. |愤怒，暴怒|The state of being enraged; extreme anger.|His enragedness was evident in his clenched fists and red face. 他的愤怒从他紧握的拳头和涨红的脸上一览无遗。|近义词：fury, rage, wrath, ire|反义词：calmness, tranquility, peacefulness|词性:名词
Etymology: From enrage + -ed + -ness. Enrage comes from the Old French enrager, from en- "in" + rage "rage."
