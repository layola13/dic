countercommercials: /ˈkaʊntər kəˈmɜːrʃəlz/ | n. | 反广告 | Advertisements that respond to and criticize other advertisements, often to promote a social cause or an opposing viewpoint. |

Example 1: The group created a series of countercommercials to challenge the tobacco industry's marketing tactics.  这个团体制作了一系列反广告来挑战烟草业的营销策略。

Example 2:  Several countercommercials highlighted the environmental damage caused by the company's products.  几个反广告突出了该公司产品造成的环境破坏。


Synonym: counter-advertising |
Antonym: advertisement, commercial |
Part of Speech: noun |
