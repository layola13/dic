outscore:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːr/
verb
超过…的得分，比分高于
To defeat (an opponent) by scoring more points, goals, etc.
The team outscored their opponents 3-1.  该队以3比1的比分战胜了对手。
Synonyms: outpoint, surpass, beat, defeat
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性:动词
