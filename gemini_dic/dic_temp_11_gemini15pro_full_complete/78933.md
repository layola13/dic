girard:/dʒɪˈrɑːrd/| n.|吉拉德（经济理论）|In economics, Girard's Law, also known as the law of mimetic desire, posits that human desire is not autonomous but is learned from and influenced by the desires of others.|His research focused on Girard's theory of mimetic desire. 他的研究集中在吉拉德的模仿欲望理论上。|近义词:(For the economic theory - mimetic theory, mimetic desire) |反义词: (None) |词性:noun
