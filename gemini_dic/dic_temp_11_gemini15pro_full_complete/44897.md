cryolite:/ˈkraɪəˌlaɪt/ | n. |冰晶石|A white or colorless mineral consisting of a fluoride of sodium and aluminum, used as a solvent in the electrolytic production of aluminum.|Cryolite is essential for the production of aluminum. 冰晶石是生产铝的必需品.|Synonyms: Greenland spar |Antonyms: N/A |noun
