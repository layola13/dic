neuropodous:/nʊəˈrɒpədəs/ | adj. | 神经足的；具有神经足的 | Having neuropods; furnished with neuropods.  Neuropods are small, leg-like appendages found on some invertebrates, often used for sensory purposes. |  "The neuropodous creature explored its surroundings."  这只有神经足的生物探索着周围的环境。| Near Synonyms:  sensitive, sensory (related to function), appendaged (related to structure) | Antonyms:  None (lack of a specific antonym) | Word type: Adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *neuron* (nerve) + *pous, podos* (foot)
