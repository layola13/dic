house-sit: /ˈhaʊsˌsɪt/ (US), /ˈhaʊsˌsɪt/ (UK)
| v. |照看房子; housesitting(n.) |To stay in someone's house while they are away to look after it and any pets. | I'm house-sitting for my neighbors while they're on vacation. 我在邻居度假期间帮他们照看房子。

| | | | We house-sat for our friends last summer and their dog was lovely. 去年夏天我们帮朋友照看房子，他们的狗很可爱。
|近义词: pet-sit, house mind |反义词:  (No direct opposite, but perhaps "vacate") |动词

house-sitter: /ˈhaʊsˌsɪtər/ (US), /ˈhaʊsˌsɪtər/ (UK)
| n. |房屋看护人 | A person who stays in someone's house while they are away to look after it and any pets. |  My sister is a reliable house-sitter. 我姐姐是一个可靠的房屋看护人。

| | | |  They hired a house-sitter to care for their plants while they were gone.  他们雇了一个房屋看护人来照顾他们的植物，而他们不在的时候。
|近义词: pet-sitter, house minder|反义词: (No direct opposite)|名词
