cryptophyte:/ˈkrɪptəˌfaɪt/ | n. |隐生植物；潜隐植物|A plant that survives unfavorable seasons by means of subterranean buds or bulbs, or by remaining dormant beneath the surface of water.  A plant whose perennating buds are situated below the surface of the soil or submerged in water.| Cryptophytes, like water lilies, are adapted to survive harsh conditions.| 近义词:geophyte, hydrophyte | 反义词:phanerophyte | Noun
