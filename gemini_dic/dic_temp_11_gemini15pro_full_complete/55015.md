disputeless:/dɪˈspjuːtləs/
adj.
无可争议的，无异议的
Without dispute; undisputed.
The evidence presented was so overwhelming that the verdict was disputeless.  提交的证据如此确凿，判决无可争议。
His claim to the throne was disputeless. 他对王位的继承权是无可争议的。
近义词:undisputed, unchallenged, incontestable, undeniable
反义词:disputed, contested, debatable, controversial
词性:形容词

disputeless:/dɪˈspjuːtləs/
词源: From dispute + -less.  由dispute（争议）加上否定后缀-less构成。
用法: 用于描述某事毫无争议或不容置疑。
