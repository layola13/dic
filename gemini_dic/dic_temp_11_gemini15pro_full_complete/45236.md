culpas:/ˈkʊlpɑːs/
noun
罪过，过错
Faults or offenses; sins.
例句：He confessed his culpas to the priest. 他向神父忏悔了他的罪过。

例句：She carried the weight of her culpas for years. 多年来，她一直背负着罪过的重担。

近义词：sins, offenses, transgressions, wrongdoings
反义词：virtues, merits, good deeds
词性：noun
