ingrained:/ɪnˈɡreɪnd/ /ɪnˈɡrend/| adj.|根深蒂固的；牢固的| Firmly fixed or established; difficult to change.|  His prejudice against foreigners was deeply ingrained. 他对外国人的偏见根深蒂固。| 近义词：deep-rooted, embedded, fixed, implanted, inherent | 反义词：superficial, shallow | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From in- + grain (n.) + -ed. Originally referring to dyeing that penetrates the fibers of a fabric, hence firmly fixed.
