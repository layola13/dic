hippodame:/hɪˈpɒdəmiː/
n.
驯马女，女骑士
A woman skilled in horse riding and training, especially in ancient Greece.
The hippodame rode her stallion with grace and skill.  驯马女骑着她的种马，姿态优雅，技艺娴熟。
Synonyms: horsewoman, equestrienne
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "pedestrian")
Noun


Etymology:
From Greek *hippodamos* (ἵπποδαμος) "tamer of horses," from *hippos* (ἵππος) "horse" + *damao* (δαμάω) "to tame."
