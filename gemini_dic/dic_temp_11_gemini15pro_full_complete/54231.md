disfurnishment:/dɪsˈfɜːrnɪʃmənt/
|n.|剥夺家具；家具被搬空的状态|The disfurnishment of the apartment left it feeling cold and empty. 公寓里的家具被搬空了，让它感觉又冷又空。|The sudden disfurnishment of the house made it look abandoned. 房子的家具突然被搬空，使它看起来像被遗弃了一样。|removal of furniture, stripping of furniture|furnishment|noun
Etymology: dis- (prefix meaning "reversal, removal") + furnish + -ment (suffix forming nouns of action or process)  From Old French des- (prefix indicating separation, removal) + furnir (to furnish) + -ment.
