ben:/bɛn/ | n. | 内室；卧室；山峰 |  A Scottish word for a mountain or peak.  An inner room or bedroom. |  Example: We climbed Ben Nevis, the highest ben in Scotland. 我们爬了本尼维斯山，苏格兰最高的山峰。

Example: The children were tucked away safe in the ben. 孩子们安全地藏在内室里。 | Synonym: peak, summit, mountain, inner room | Antonym: valley, plain, outer room | Noun

ben:/bɛn/ | adv. | 向内；在里面 | Inside; within. | Example: Come ben the house. 到房子里面来。(苏格兰方言) | Synonym: inside, within | Antonym: outside, without | Adverb
