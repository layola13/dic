inseparables:/ɪnˈsɛpərəblz/| n. |形影不离的人（或物）|People (or things) that are very close and cannot be separated.|例句：They were inseparables in college. 他们在大学时形影不离。|近义词：bosom friends, close friends, companions|反义词: strangers, enemies|词性:名词

inseparable:/ɪnˈsɛpərəbl/| adj. |不可分的，形影不离的|Not able to be separated.|例句：The two friends were inseparable. 这两个朋友形影不离。|近义词：indivisible, unbreakable, united|反义词: separable, divisible|词性:形容词
