laceman:/ˈleɪsmən/ | n. | 花边商人；饰带商人 | A person who makes or sells lace or ribbons. |  He was a laceman by trade, supplying fine goods to the wealthy. 他是一个花边商人，向富人供应优质商品。

The laceman had a stall in the market square.  这个花边商人在市场广场上有一个摊位。 | 近义词：trimmer, haberdasher | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fabric wholesaler" in a broad sense if contrasting with a specialist) | 词性: noun
