disseat:/dɪsˈsiːt/
verb
使离座；剥夺…的席位
To remove from a seat, especially of authority or privilege.
The rebel forces disseated the king. 叛军废黜了国王。
近义词: unseat, dethrone, oust
反义词: enthrone, install, seat
词性: verb


Etymology:
From dis- + seat.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," "away," "utterly," or having a privative, negative, or reversing force. "Seat" comes from Old English "setl" meaning "a seat, stall, throne."
