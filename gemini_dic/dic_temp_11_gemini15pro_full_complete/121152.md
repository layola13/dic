misdescriptions:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskrɪpʃənz/
|n.|错误描述，不准确的描述|An inaccurate or misleading description of something.|The advertisement contained several misdescriptions of the property, leading to a lawsuit.|近义词: misrepresentations, inaccuracies, distortions|反义词: accurate descriptions, correct representations|词性: 名词

Etymology:
mis- (prefix):  From Old English *mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly, astray, amiss," from Proto-Indo-European *mei- (1) "to change, go, move."  Indicates something done wrongly or badly.
description (noun): From Middle English descripcioun, from Old French descripcion, from Latin descriptiō (“a writing out, a description”), from dēscrībō (“I write down, copy, describe”), from dē (“down, from”) + scrībō (“I write”).
