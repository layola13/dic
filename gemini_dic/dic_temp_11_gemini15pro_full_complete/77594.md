geek-out:/ɡik aʊt/| n. vi.|极客狂欢，沉迷于某事|An instance of enthusiastically engaging in or discussing a specialized topic, typically one of technical or intellectual interest.  It implies a deep dive into a subject matter that excites the person, often to the exclusion of others who may not share the same level of interest.|例句：We had a total geek-out last night discussing the new Star Wars movie.  昨晚我们彻底地沉迷于讨论新的星球大战电影。

例句：He totally geeked out over the new computer he built. 他对新组装的电脑兴奋极了。|近义词：rave, enthuse, obsess |反义词: disinterest, apathy |词性: noun, intransitive verb

geek-out:/ɡik aʊt/| vi.|沉迷于，对…着迷|To become extremely enthusiastic and involved with something, especially a technical or intellectual subject. |例句：I really geeked out when I saw the new virtual reality headset.  当我看到新的虚拟现实头盔时，我真的很兴奋。

例句：She geeks out over anything related to astrophysics. 她对任何与天体物理学相关的东西都非常着迷。|近义词：obsess over, go crazy for, get carried away with |反义词: ignore, disregard |词性: intransitive verb
