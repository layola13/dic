embargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US) /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. vt.|禁运，禁止贸易；禁止发表或公开|An official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.|The US imposed an embargo on trade with Cuba.|近义词：ban, prohibition, restriction, sanction|反义词:allowance, permission|noun
embargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US) /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| vt.|禁止贸易；禁止发表或公开|To officially ban trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.|The government decided to embargo all shipments of arms to the region.|近义词：ban, prohibit, restrict, sanction|反义词:allow, permit|verb
