astonish:/əˈstɒnɪʃ/ (US) /əˈstɒnɪʃ/ (UK)| vt.|使惊讶，使震惊|To surprise or impress someone very much.|It astonished me to hear he’d resigned. 我听到他辞职的消息感到很震惊。|近义词：surprise, amaze, astound|反义词:bore, tire|verb
