imprenable:/ɪmˈprɛnəbəl/ | adj. | 攻不破的，坚不可摧的 | Not able to be captured or broken into.  Unable to be overcome or refuted. | The castle, situated on a high cliff, was considered impregnable.  这座城堡位于高高的悬崖上，被认为是攻不破的。 | The fortress proved impregnable to enemy attacks. 这个要塞被证明是敌人无法攻破的。 | 近义词: invincible, unassailable, secure, fortified | 反义词: vulnerable, weak, defenseless | 词性: 形容词

imprenable:/ɪmˈprɛnəbəl/ | adj. | 无可辩驳的，无法反驳的 |  Unable to be overcome or refuted. |  His argument was so well-constructed as to be almost impregnable. 他的论证如此严密，几乎无可辩驳。 | Her logic was impregnable, leaving no room for counter-arguments. 她的逻辑无懈可击，没有留下任何反驳的余地。| 近义词: irrefutable, indisputable, unchallengeable | 反义词: refutable, disputable, challengeable | 词性: 形容词
