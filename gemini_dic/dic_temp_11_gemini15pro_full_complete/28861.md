carpetbagger: /ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡər/ | n. | 投机政客；外来者；背包客 | A person from the North who went to the South after the Civil War to profit from the Reconstruction. 指的是美国内战后从北方到南方去，利用重建时期混乱的局面谋取私利的人。|  Example 1: Many carpetbaggers became wealthy by exploiting the South's weakened economy. 许多投机政客通过剥削南方虚弱的经济而变得富有。

Example 2:  He was accused of being a carpetbagger, only interested in making a quick profit. 他被指控是一个投机政客，只对快速获利感兴趣。| 近义词: opportunist, exploiter, profiteer | 反义词: native, local, resident | 词性: 名词
