incompetency:/ɪnˈkɒmpɪtənsi/ /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtənsi/| n. |无能力；不称职；不胜任|lack of ability to do something successfully; inability|His incompetency led to the project's failure. 他的无能导致了项目的失败。|The lawyer's incompetency resulted in his client losing the case.  这位律师的无能导致他的客户输掉了官司。|近义词：inability, incapacity, ineptitude|反义词：competency, ability, capability|词性：名词

incompetent:/ɪnˈkɒmpɪtənt/ /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtənt/| adj. |无能力的；不胜任的；不称职的|not having or showing the necessary skills to do something successfully|He was fired for being incompetent. 他因不称职而被解雇。|She felt incompetent to handle the complex task. 她觉得自己没有能力处理这项复杂的任务。|近义词：unable, incapable, inept|反义词：competent, able, capable|词性：形容词
