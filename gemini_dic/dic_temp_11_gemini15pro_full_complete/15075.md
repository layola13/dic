baby mama:/ˈbeɪbi ˈmɑːmə/ /ˈbeɪbi ˈmɒmə/| n.|孩子妈；（尤指非婚生子女的）母亲|A woman who has a child with a man but is not married to him. |例句：He has two baby mamas. 他有两个孩子的母亲（并未与她们结婚）。

例句：She's a proud baby mama. 她是一位自豪的单身母亲。


近义词：mother, mom (though these don't carry the same connotation), girlfriend (if they are still in a relationship)|反义词: father, dad|词性:noun
