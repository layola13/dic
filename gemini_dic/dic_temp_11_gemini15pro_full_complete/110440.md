likenesses:/ˈlaɪknəsɪz/
n.
相似之处，相似物；肖像，画像
Representations or images of a person, especially in the form of a painting, drawing, or photograph; points of resemblance.
The artist captured her likenesses perfectly in the portrait.  这位艺术家在肖像画中完美地捕捉了她的样貌。
There are striking likenesses between the two sisters. 这两姐妹之间有着惊人的相似之处。
近义词: similarities, resemblances, portraits, images
反义词: differences, dissimilarities, contrasts
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *likenesse*, from Old English *līcnes*, from *līc* (“like, similar”) + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns). Cognate with Old Frisian *līknesse*, Middle Dutch *līkenesse* (Dutch *lijkenis*), Old High German *līhnissa* (German *Gleichnis* (“parable”)), Old Norse *líkn* (“form, appearance”).
