decide:/dɪˈsaɪd/| v.|决定，决心；解决；裁决|to make a choice or judgment about something; to bring to a resolution|She decided to go to Paris. 她决定去巴黎。|determine, resolve, settle, conclude|hesitate, waver|verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin decidere "to cut off, determine," from de- "off" + caedere "to cut."

PHRASE->
decide on: 决定，选定  We decided on the red dress. 我们决定选红色的裙子。
decide against: 决定不…  He decided against going to the party. 他决定不去参加派对了。
decide for oneself:  自己做决定  You have to decide for yourself what you want to do. 你必须自己决定你想做什么。
decide between: 在…之间做出选择  She had to decide between the two job offers. 她必须在两个工作机会之间做出选择。
decide that: 决定…  They decided that they would go on vacation in July. 他们决定在七月去度假。
