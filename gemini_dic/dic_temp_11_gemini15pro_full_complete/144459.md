outtake:/ˈaʊtteɪk/| n.|花絮，NG片段，(电影、电视节目、书籍或录音的) 删除部分|A scene or sequence that is filmed or recorded but not included in the final version of a movie, television program, book, or recording.|The director showed us some hilarious outtakes from the movie. 导演给我们看了一些电影里滑稽的NG片段。|近义词：deleted scene, unused footage, blooper|反义词: final cut, finished product|词性:名词

词源:  From "out" + "take".  "Take" in this context refers to a single recording or filmed sequence.  Therefore, an "outtake" is something taken out of the final product.
