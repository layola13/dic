diodon:/ˈdaɪədɒn/ (US) /ˈdaɪədən/ (UK)| n. |刺豚，豪猪鱼|A type of fish characterized by its ability to inflate its body into a ball shape when threatened, covered in sharp spines.|Divers often encounter diodon while exploring coral reefs. 潜水员在探索珊瑚礁时经常会遇到刺豚。|The diodon puffed itself up into a spiky ball when it saw the predator.  当刺豚看到捕食者时，它会把自己膨胀成一个带刺的球。|近义词：pufferfish, porcupinefish|反义词:|noun
