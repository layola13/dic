hypermilitant:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɪlɪtənt/| adj.|极端好战的，极端激进的|Characterized by extreme militancy; excessively aggressive and combative, especially in support of a cause.|The hypermilitant group claimed responsibility for the bombing.  这个极端好战的组织声称对爆炸事件负责。|例句：The hypermilitant faction within the party was pushing for war.  党内极端好战的派系正在推动战争。|近义词：extremist, radical, fanatic, zealot|反义词：moderate, pacifist|词性:形容词
词源: "hyper-" (Greek: over, beyond) + "militant" (Latin: militantem, present participle of militare, to serve as a soldier)
