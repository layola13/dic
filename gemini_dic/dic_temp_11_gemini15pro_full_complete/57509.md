dragoness:/ˈdræɡənɪs/| n.|母龙，雌龙|A female dragon.  A large, serpentine, legendary creature that breathes fire. |例句：The dragoness guarded her hoard of gold jealously. 母龙警惕地守护着她的黄金宝藏。

例句：The knight faced the fearsome dragoness in mortal combat. 骑士在殊死搏斗中面对可怕的母龙。|近义词：female dragon, she-dragon|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) male dragon, dragon|词性:noun
