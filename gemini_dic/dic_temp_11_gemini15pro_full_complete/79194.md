glamping:/ˈɡlæmpɪŋ/| n.|豪华露营|A portmanteau of "glamorous" and "camping," referring to a style of camping with amenities and accommodations more luxurious than traditional camping.|We went glamping in Yosemite and stayed in a yurt with a king-size bed. 我们去优胜美地豪华露营，住在一个有特大号床的蒙古包里。|近义词：luxury camping, upscale camping|反义词: camping, backpacking|词性:名词

Etymology:  Blend of *glamorous* and *camping*, first recorded in 2005 in the UK.
