overcoached:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtʃt/
adjective
过度指导的；被过度训练的
Having been coached excessively, often to the point of detriment.  The player's performance declined because he was overcoached.  这个球员的表现下降了，因为他被过度指导了。
例句：The team was overcoached and burnt out by the end of the season.  这个队伍在赛季末被过度训练，精疲力竭。

例句：The young gymnast felt overcoached and pressured to perform perfectly.  这位年轻的体操运动员感到被过度指导，承受着完美表现的压力。

近义词：overtrained, overmanaged, overdirected
反义词：undercoached, underdeveloped
词性：adjective
