enrages:/ɪnˈreɪdʒɪz/
verb
激怒，使暴怒
to make someone extremely angry
The referee's decision enraged the home fans. 裁判的判罚激怒了主场球迷。
近义词: infuriates, angers, maddens
反义词: calms, soothes, placates
词性: verb
