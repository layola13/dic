aerate:/ˈeəreɪt/ /ˈɛəˌreɪt/| v. |通风；充气；使透气|To introduce air into a substance, such as soil or a liquid.|Example 1: The farmer aerated the soil to improve drainage. 农民给土壤通风以改善排水。

Example 2:  They aerate the wine before bottling it. 他们在装瓶前给葡萄酒充气。|Synonyms: ventilate, oxygenate, carbonate|Antonyms: suffocate, stifle|verb
