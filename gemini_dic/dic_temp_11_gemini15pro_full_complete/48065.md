declarant:/dɪˈklɛərənt/ | n. | 陈述人，申报人 |A person who makes a formal statement, especially in a legal context. | The declarant testified under oath.  陈述人在宣誓后作证。|  witness, testifier, affiant | opponent, adversary | noun

Etymology: From Old French *declarant, present participle of declarer "to declare," from Latin declarare "to make clear, reveal, explain," from de- "completely" + clarare "to make clear," from clarus "clear."
