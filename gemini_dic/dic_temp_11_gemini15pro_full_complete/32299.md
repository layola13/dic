chievance:/ˈtʃiːvəns/
noun
抱怨，牢骚，委屈
a complaint or grievance.

She aired her chievances about the new management.
她表达了对新管理层的不满。

chievance:/ˈtʃiːvəns/
noun
诉讼理由，控诉
a cause of complaint; a ground of action.

The lawyer listened carefully to his client's chievances.
律师仔细聆听了委托人的诉讼理由。


Etymology:
From Old French *chevance "business, affair, matter," from chevir "to accomplish, manage, achieve," from Late Latin *capire "to seize, take," alteration of Latin capere.

Synonyms:
complaint, grievance, grumble, grouse, beef, objection, protest, remonstrance, criticism, disapproval

Antonyms:
praise, commendation, approval, compliment, satisfaction, contentment

Word Type:
Noun
