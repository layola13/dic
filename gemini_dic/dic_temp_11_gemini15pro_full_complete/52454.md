dielectric:/ˌdaɪɪˈlɛktrɪk/ | n. | 电介质，绝缘体 | A dielectric material is a substance that does not conduct electricity easily. |  A high dielectric constant material is used in the capacitor.  电容器中使用高介电常数的材料。| 近义词: insulator, nonconductor | 反义词: conductor | 词性: noun
