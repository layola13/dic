avat:/ˈævɑːtɑːr/ |n.| 化身，头像；虚拟替身 |An avatar is a graphical representation of a user or their character or persona, typically used in video games, online forums, and social media platforms.  |Example:  She created a customized avatar for her online gaming profile. 她为她的在线游戏档案创建了一个自定义头像。

Example: His avatar in the virtual world was a powerful wizard.  他在虚拟世界中的化身是一个强大的巫师。 |Synonym: icon, profile picture, persona, embodiment|Antonym:  reality, physical form |noun
