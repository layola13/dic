outwaving:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvɪŋ/
adj.|向外挥舞的，挥舞超过的|extending or waving outward; surpassing in waving.  It describes the action of waving something outwards or surpassing someone else in the act of waving.|The outwaving branches of the willow tree danced in the breeze. 柳树向外挥舞的枝条在微风中摇曳。|The queen's outwaving gesture captivated the crowd. 女王的挥舞姿势吸引了人群。|近义词：waving, brandishing, flourishing|反义词：infolding, withdrawing|词性:形容词
词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outward, exceeding") + "waving" (present participle of "wave")
