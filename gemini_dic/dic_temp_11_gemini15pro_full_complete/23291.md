boxinesses:/ˈbɒksɪnəsɪz/
noun
方正(boxiness)的复数形式；许多像盒子一样的状态
The plural form of boxiness, referring to multiple instances of resembling a box-like shape or quality.
The new car designs emphasized sleekness over the boxinesses of previous models.  这些新车的设计强调的是时尚感，而不是以前车型的方正感。
The architect disliked the boxinesses of the buildings in the new development.  这位建筑师不喜欢新开发项目中建筑物的方正感。
Near Synonyms: angularities, blockinesses, squarenesses
Near Antonyms: curves, roundnesses, sleeknesses
