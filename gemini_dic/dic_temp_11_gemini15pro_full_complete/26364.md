by-guess-and-by-golly:/baɪ ˈɡɛs ænd baɪ ˈɡɑli/
adverb
凭猜测和运气；胡乱猜测；碰运气
Through trial and error, often with luck and improvisation rather than careful planning.  Indicates a haphazard or improvised approach.
例句：He fixed the broken clock by-guess-and-by-golly. 他凭猜测和运气修好了坏掉的钟。
例句：She found the right answer by-guess-and-by-golly. 她瞎猫碰死耗子找到了正确答案。
近义词：haphazardly, randomly, roughly, approximately, imprecisely, by trial and error
反义词：methodically, systematically, precisely, accurately
词性：副词

词源：This expression likely originated in the early 20th century. "Golly" is a euphemism for "God," so the phrase avoids taking the Lord's name in vain.  The alliteration and rhythm contribute to its memorability.
