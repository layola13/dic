geophagism:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfeɪdʒɪzəm/
n.
食土癖，食土症
The practice of eating earth, especially clay or chalk.
He suffers from geophagism and often craves clay. 他患有食土癖，经常想吃土。

Geophagism is sometimes observed in pregnant women. 有时在孕妇身上会观察到食土癖。

Synonym:
clay-eating, earth-eating

Antonym:
(No direct antonym for a specific eating disorder)


Etymology:
From geo- (earth) + -phagy (eating)
