noncuriosities:/ˌnɒnkjʊəriˈɒsɪtiz/| n. |缺乏好奇心，漠不关心|Lack of curiosity; things that do not arouse curiosity.|The child's noncuriosities about the world worried his parents. 这个孩子对世界缺乏好奇心，令他的父母担忧。| Her noncuriosities regarding her family history were surprising. 她对家族史的漠不关心令人惊讶。 | 近义词：indifference, apathy, disinterest | 反义词：curiosity, interest, inquisitiveness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- (not) + curiosity (the desire to know or learn something).  Ultimately from Latin *cūrōsus* (careful, inquisitive), from *cūra* (care).
