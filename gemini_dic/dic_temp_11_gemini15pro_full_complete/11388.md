argali:/ˈɑːrɡəli/| n.|盘羊，大角羊|A wild sheep (Ovis ammon) of the mountains of central Asia, having very large, curved horns.|例句：The argali is a magnificent animal, with its huge, spiraling horns. 盘羊是一种雄伟的动物，有着巨大的螺旋状角。

例句：Argali populations have declined due to hunting and habitat loss.  由于狩猎和栖息地丧失，盘羊的数量已经下降。|近义词：wild sheep, mountain sheep|反义词: (无明确反义词，可考虑 domesticated sheep 家养绵羊)|词性:noun

词源: From Mongolian "argali".
