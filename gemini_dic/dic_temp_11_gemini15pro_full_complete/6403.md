amphipodan:/ˈæmfɪpɒdən/ (US), /ˈæmfɪpədən/ (UK)| n. |片脚类动物|A small crustacean of the order Amphipoda, typically marine and laterally compressed.|例句：Amphipods are a crucial part of the marine food web.  片脚类动物是海洋食物网的重要组成部分。
