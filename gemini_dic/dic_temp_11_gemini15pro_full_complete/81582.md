greaseless:/ˈɡriːslɪs/| adj.|无油的，不油腻的|Free from grease; not greasy.  Containing no grease.  Specifically (of food) cooked or prepared without fat or oil.|The mechanic used a greaseless lubricant on the delicate parts.  技工在精密部件上使用了无油润滑剂。|The recipe called for greaseless chicken breast.  食谱要求用无油鸡胸肉。|Near synonyms:  non-greasy, oil-free, fat-free|Near antonyms: greasy, oily, fatty|adjective
