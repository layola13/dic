maledict:/ˌmæləˈdɪkt/ | v. | 诅咒，咒骂 | To speak evil of; curse. | He maledicted his enemies, wishing them ill fortune. 他诅咒他的敌人，祝愿他们厄运。|  She maledicted the day she was born. 她诅咒她出生的那一天。 | 近义词: curse, damn, execrate | 反义词: bless, praise | 词性: 动词

malediction:/ˌmæləˈdɪkʃən/ | n. | 诅咒，咒骂 | A curse; imprecation of evil. |  His words were a malediction upon their house. 他的话是对他们家的诅咒。| The witch uttered a malediction upon the kingdom. 女巫对王国施了诅咒。| 近义词: curse, anathema, imprecation | 反义词: blessing, benediction | 词性: 名词

词源：From Latin maledicere "to speak ill of, curse," from male "badly" (see mal-) + dicere "to say" (see diction).


注意事项：maledict is a more formal and literary word than curse.  It's less commonly used in everyday speech.
