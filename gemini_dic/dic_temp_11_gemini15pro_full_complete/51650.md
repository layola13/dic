deviation:/ˌdiːviˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 偏差，偏离，背离 | A departure from an established course or accepted standard. | The teacher noted a deviation from the student's usual behavior. 老师注意到这名学生的行为与其平常不同。| The recent price fluctuations represent a significant deviation from the norm. 最近的价格波动代表着与正常情况的显著偏差。 | synonyms: divergence, variation, departure, aberration, anomaly | antonyms: conformity, adherence, compliance | noun
