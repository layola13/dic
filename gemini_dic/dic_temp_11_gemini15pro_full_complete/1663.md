aclinic: /eɪˈklɪnɪk/
adjective
无临床症状的;亚临床的
Relating to or denoting a disease that is not severe enough to present definite or readily observable symptoms.
Her aclinic infection posed a risk to others. 她无临床症状的感染对其他人构成威胁。
近义词: subclinical, asymptomatic, inapparent
反义词: clinical, symptomatic
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From a- (not) + clinic (relating to a clinic or the bedside of a patient).  Ultimately from Greek *kline* (bed).
