outglow:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊ/
verb
发出比...更强的光; 比...更亮;
shine more brightly than; surpass in brightness
The setting sun outglowed the city lights. 落日比城市灯光更亮。
surpass, eclipse, outshine, overshadow
dim, pale, darken
