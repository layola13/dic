caroming:/kəˈroʊmɪŋ/
| v. | 撞击后反弹；斜飞；|The ball caromed off the wall and into the pocket.  球撞到墙上后弹进了球袋。|English explanation: To strike and rebound, typically at an angle.  | The billiard balls caromed off each other several times before settling. 台球互相撞击了好几次才停下来。| 近义词:ricocheting, glancing, bouncing, rebounding | 反义词:stopping, halting | 词性:动词

carom:/ˈkærəm/
| n. | 撞击后反弹；斜飞；碰击；|The carom of the ball off the cushion surprised him. 球从垫子上弹回来让他很吃惊。|English explanation: An impact and rebound, typically at an angle. |He made the shot with a difficult carom. 他用一个高难度的反弹球完成了射门。 | 近义词:ricochet, glance, bounce, rebound | 反义词:stop, halt | 词性:名词
