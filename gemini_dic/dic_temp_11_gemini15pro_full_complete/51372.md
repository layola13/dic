determent:/dɪˈtɜːrmənt/
n.|损害，障碍，不利因素|Something that causes damage, harm, or loss; a hindrance or disadvantage.|The pollution is a serious detriment to the environment. 污染是对环境的严重损害。|近义词：hindrance, obstacle, disadvantage, impediment, handicap|反义词：advantage, benefit, asset, boon
詞性: 名詞
