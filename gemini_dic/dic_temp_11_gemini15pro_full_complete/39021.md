confix:/ˈkɒnfɪks/ | n. | 缀合词素; 联结词素 | A confix is a morpheme that is attached to a base morpheme both initially and finally. | Example: enlighten (en-light-en), where "en-" and "-en" are confixes and "light" is the base.  另一个例子是德语单词 *ge-spiel-t* (played), 其中 *ge-* 和 *-t* 是缀合词素， *spiel* 是词干。 | Near synonyms: circumfix | Antonyms: N/A | noun
