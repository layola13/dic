editorships:/ˈedɪtərʃɪps/
n.
编辑职位；编辑工作
the position or responsibilities of an editor
She held several editorships during her career.  她在职业生涯中担任过多个编辑职位。
He was offered the editorship of the prestigious journal. 他获得了这份著名期刊的编辑职位。
近义词:editorial positions, editorial posts
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly consider "authorship" if contrasting roles)
词性:noun
