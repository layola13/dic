disenchant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃænt/ | vt. | 使清醒；使不再抱幻想；使失望 | To free from illusion or false belief; to disappoint. |  She was disenchanted with the job after only a week. 她只工作了一周就对这份工作感到失望了。
