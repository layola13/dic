nonrevenue:/ˌnɒnˈrevənjuː/
adjective
非营利的，不产生收益的
Not generating revenue; not intended to generate revenue.
The museum offers many nonrevenue programs for the community.  博物馆为社区提供许多非营利性项目。
The city council voted to increase funding for nonrevenue services. 市议会投票决定增加对非营利服务的资助。
近义词: nonprofit, non-profit, loss-making, unprofitable
反义词: profit-making, profitable, revenue-generating, for-profit
词性: adjective

nonrevenue:/ˌnɒnˈrevənjuː/
noun
非收益项目，非收益业务
An activity or service that does not generate revenue.
The hospital's nonrevenues include community health education programs. 这家医院的非收益项目包括社区健康教育项目。
Many airlines provide nonrevenues like free checked baggage for loyal customers. 许多航空公司为忠实客户提供非收益服务，例如免费托运行李。
近义词: loss leader, complimentary service
反义词: revenue generator, profit center
词性: noun
