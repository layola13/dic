maioid:/ˈmeɪɔɪd/| adj.|类似蟹的；属于蟹类的|Relating to or resembling crabs; belonging to the crab family.|The maioid crab scuttled across the ocean floor. 这只类似蟹的螃蟹匆匆穿过海底。|近义词：crab-like, brachyuran|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-crab-like)|词性:adjective
词源: From Latin "Maia" (a type of crab) + "-oid" (resembling).
