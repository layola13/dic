handbasket:/ˈhændˌbɑːskɪt/| n. | 手提篮; (尤指糟糕情况)迅速恶化 |A handbasket is a small basket with a handle, used for carrying things.  The phrase "going to hell in a handbasket" is an idiom that means deteriorating rapidly or becoming chaotic.|  The economy seems to be going to hell in a handbasket. 经济似乎正在急剧恶化。 | 近义词: basket, downfall (in the phrase)| 反义词: improvement (in the phrase) | noun
