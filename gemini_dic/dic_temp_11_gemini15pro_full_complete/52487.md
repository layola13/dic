dieting:/ˈdaɪətɪŋ/ | n. |节食，控制饮食|The practice of restricting oneself to small amounts or special kinds of food in order to lose weight.|She's been dieting for months. 她已经节食好几个月了。|Synonyms:  reducing, slimming, weight loss |Antonyms: gorging, overeating, feasting |Noun


dieting:/ˈdaɪətɪŋ/ | adj. |节食的|Relating to or characteristic of a diet.|She's on a dieting plan. 她正在执行一个节食计划。|Synonyms:  reducing, slimming |Antonyms: gorging, overeating |Adjective
