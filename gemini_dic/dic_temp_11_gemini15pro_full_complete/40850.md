cooee:/kʊˈiː/
n. int. vi. vt.|呼叫声；招呼声；用呼叫声招呼|A call used to attract attention, especially in the Australian bush.  Used to attract attention or call someone to you.|
例句：He gave a cooee to his friends across the valley. 他向山谷对面的朋友发出一声呼叫。
例句：She cooeed to her children to come in for dinner. 她大声呼唤孩子们进来吃晚饭。
近义词：shout, yell, call, holler|
反义词:whisper, murmur|
词性:名词, 动词, 感叹词|
词源:From Dharuk *guwii, meaning "come here".|
