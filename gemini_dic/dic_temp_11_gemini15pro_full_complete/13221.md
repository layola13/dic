ataghan:/ɑːtɑːˈɡɑːn/ | n. |  突袭，袭击; 掠夺 | A raid or incursion, especially one made by mounted nomads for plunder. |  The ataghan devastated the village, leaving nothing but scorched earth.  突袭摧毁了村庄，只留下了焦土。| raid, incursion, attack, onslaught | defense, protection | noun

Etymology: From Turkish *atağan* "saber," possibly related to *atmak* "to throw."
