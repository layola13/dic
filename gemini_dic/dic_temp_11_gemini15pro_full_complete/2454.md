adjurer:/əˈdʒʊərər/ | n. | 恳求者；告诫者 | Someone who adjures another person.  | The adjurer pleaded with the king to spare the prisoner's life.  恳求者恳求国王饶恕囚犯的性命。 |  supplicant, petitioner, pleader | opponent, adversary | noun

adjure:/əˈdʒʊər/ | vt. |  恳求；告诫；命令 | To urge or request (someone) solemnly or earnestly to do something. To command or enjoin solemnly. | I adjure you to tell the truth. 我恳求你讲真话。| beseech, entreat, implore, command, order | discourage, dissuade | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *adjuren*, from Old French *adjurer*, from Latin *adiūrāre* (“to swear to, take an oath to, bind by oath”), from *ad-* (“to”) + *iūrāre* (“to swear”).
