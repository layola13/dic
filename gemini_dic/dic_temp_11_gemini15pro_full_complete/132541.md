nondevotional:/ˌnɒndɪˈvəʊʃənəl/
adjective
非宗教的; 非虔诚的
Not religious or devotional; secular.
例句：
The museum houses a collection of nondevotional art from the 18th century.
这家博物馆收藏了18世纪的非宗教艺术作品。

Her nondevotional approach to life surprised her religious family.
她对生活的非虔诚态度使她虔诚的家人感到惊讶。

近义词：secular, worldly, profane, irreligious
反义词：religious, devout, pious, spiritual
词性：adjective
