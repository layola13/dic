garb:/ɡɑːrb/| n. |服装，装束|clothes, especially of a particular or distinctive kind|He wore the garb of a monk. 他穿着僧侣的服装。|近义词：clothing, attire, apparel, costume|反义词:nudity|名词

词源:
From Old North French *garber, of Germanic origin; related to Old Norse gervi ‘equipment’.
