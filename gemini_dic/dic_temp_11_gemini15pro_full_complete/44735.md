crumb:/krʌm/| n.|碎屑，面包屑，少量|a small fragment of something, especially bread, cake, or biscuit.|She brushed the crumbs from her lap. 她把腿上的碎屑拂去。|Synonyms: fragment, bit, morsel, particle, speck|Antonyms: whole, loaf, chunk|noun

crumb:/krʌm/| v.|弄碎，捏碎|to break or cut something into very small pieces.|She crumbled the bread into the bowl. 她把面包捏碎放进碗里。|Synonyms: crush, break up, fragment|Antonyms: combine, join, solidify|verb
