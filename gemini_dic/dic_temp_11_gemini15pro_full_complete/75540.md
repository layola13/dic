fubsy:/ˈfʌbzi/
adjective
矮胖的，肥胖的
short and stout; plump.  Covered with soft, short fur or down.
例句：The fubsy cat curled up on the rug. 矮胖的猫蜷缩在地毯上。

例句：The old teddy bear was fubsy and worn.  旧泰迪熊胖乎乎的，而且破旧了。

近义词：plump, chubby, portly, stout, dumpy
反义词：thin, slender, slim, lean
词性: adjective


fubsy:/ˈfʌbzi/
词源:  Likely a blend of "fuzzy" and "stubby," suggesting a combination of soft texture and short, stout build.  First recorded use is in the early 19th century.
词性: adjective
中文翻译：矮胖的，肥胖的；（尤指动物）毛茸茸的
英文解析：Short and stout; plump. Covered with short, soft fur or down.
例句：The fubsy hamster stuffed its cheeks with seeds.  胖乎乎的仓鼠把种子塞满了腮帮子。

例句：She knitted a fubsy little toy for her nephew. 她为侄子织了一个毛茸茸的小玩具。

近义词：chubby, plump, stout, podgy, roly-poly, downy, fluffy
反义词：thin, slender, slim, lean, skinny, bald
词性: adjective
