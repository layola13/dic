fishcake:/ˈfɪʃˌkeɪk/ | n. | 鱼饼，鱼糕 | A flat, round cake made of minced fish, often coated in breadcrumbs and fried or baked. |  例句：I love fishcakes with tartar sauce. 我喜欢蘸塔塔酱的鱼饼。

例句：Fishcakes are a popular dish in many coastal regions. 鱼饼是许多沿海地区的流行菜肴。| 近义词：fish ball, fish patty | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider meat patty/meatball as a contrasting dish) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fish + cake.  The word "cake" in this context refers to a shaped and cooked mass, not necessarily a sweet baked good. The term dates back to the early 19th century.
