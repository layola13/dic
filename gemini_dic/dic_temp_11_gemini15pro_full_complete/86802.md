hematein:/ˌhiːməˈtiːɪn/| n.|苏木精，苏木素|The oxidized form of hematoxylin, used as a stain in microscopy.|Hematein stains cell nuclei blue-violet. 苏木精将细胞核染成蓝紫色。|Near synonyms: hematoxylin, logwood dye|Antonyms: None|Noun
