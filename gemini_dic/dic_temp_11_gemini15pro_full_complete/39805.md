consortism:/kənˈsɔːrʃɪzəm/ |n.|共同体；联合体；合伙|A consortism is a partnership or association, typically of several companies or organizations.|The international consortism bid for the construction project. 这个国际联合体竞标了这个建设项目。|Example 2:  Several banks formed a consortism to finance the new stadium. 几家银行组成了一个财团为新体育场提供资金。|Synonyms: consortium, syndicate, alliance, coalition, partnership|Antonyms:  division, separation, disunity|Noun
