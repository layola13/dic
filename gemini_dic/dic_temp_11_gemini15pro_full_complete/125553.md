multistriate:/ˌmʌltiˈstraɪeɪt/
adjective
多纹的，多条纹的
Having many striae or stripes.  Relating to or involving multiple striate areas of the brain, especially those involved in visual processing.
The multistriate areas of the visual cortex process more complex aspects of visual information.  视觉皮层的多纹状区域处理更复杂的视觉信息。
Near Synonyms: striped, striated, multilineated
Antonyms: unstriped, smooth
词性: adjective
