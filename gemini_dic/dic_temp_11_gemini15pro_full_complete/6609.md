anacoluthon:/ˌænəkəˈluːθɒn/ | n. |  句法中断，语序混乱 | A sudden break in a sentence's grammatical structure, leaving the initial construction unfinished. |  She was about to explain, the phone rang, and then she forgot what she was going to say. 她正要解释，电话响了，然后她就忘了她要说什么。 | Example:  He started to tell me about his day, the traffic was terrible, and he ended up being late. 他开始跟我讲他的一天，交通很糟糕，结果他迟到了。 | Synonym:  syntactic break, grammatical interruption, aposiopesis | Antonym:  syntactic fluency, grammatical coherence | noun
