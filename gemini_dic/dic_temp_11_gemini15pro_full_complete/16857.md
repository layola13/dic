basidium:/bəˈsɪdiəm/ (plural basidia /bəˈsɪdiə/)| n. |担子|A microscopic, club-shaped spore-bearing structure produced by certain fungi.|The basidia of the mushroom release spores into the air.|Synonym: sporophore|Antonym: N/A|noun
