outmasters:/ˌaʊtˈmæstərz/
|v.|胜过，超过，技高一筹|To surpass or defeat someone in skill or mastery, especially in a craft, art, or profession.|He outmastered his rivals in the chess tournament. 他在国际象棋比赛中战胜了对手。|
|v.|以更大的权威或力量压倒|To overpower or subdue someone through greater authority or force.|The dictator outmastered the opposition and consolidated his power. 独裁者压制了反对派，巩固了他的权力。|
|近义词:|surpass, excel, outdo, defeat, conquer, subdue, overpower|
|反义词:|lose to, be defeated by, succumb to|
|词性:|verb|
