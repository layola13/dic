fringelike:/ˈfrɪndʒˌlaɪk/| adj.|像流苏的，边缘状的|Resembling a fringe; having a fringed appearance.  The plant had fringelike leaves.  这种植物有流苏状的叶子。|近义词：fringed, tasseled, fimbriate|反义词: smooth, solid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "fringe" + "-like". "Fringe" comes from Old French "frenge" ultimately from Latin "fimbria".
