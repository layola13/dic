megalomaniac: /ˌmɛɡəloʊˈmeɪniæk/ | n. adj. | 狂妄自大的人；夸大妄想的 | A person obsessed with their own power and importance; exhibiting delusions of grandeur. |  He was a megalomaniac who believed he could control the world. 他是一个夸大妄想狂，认为自己可以控制世界。|  She had megalomaniac tendencies, always exaggerating her accomplishments. 她有夸大妄想的倾向，总是夸大自己的成就。 | 近义词: egomaniac, narcissist, tyrant | 反义词: humble, modest | 词性: noun, adjective

megalomaniac: /ˌmɛɡəloʊˈmænɪæk/ | adj. | 夸大妄想的; 狂妄自大的 |  Exhibiting delusions of grandeur; obsessed with power. | His megalomaniac plans involved buying every newspaper in the country. 他的狂妄计划包括买下全国所有的报纸。 | The CEO's megalomaniac behavior alienated his employees.  这位首席执行官的狂妄行为疏远了他的员工。| 近义词: narcissistic, grandiose, delusional | 反义词: humble, realistic | 词性: adjective
