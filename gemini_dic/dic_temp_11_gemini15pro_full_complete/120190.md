mindshare:/ˈmaɪndʃɛr/| n. |市场份额; 品牌知名度; 心灵占有率 |The amount of attention or awareness a particular product, brand, or company has among consumers. It represents the level of influence a company has on its target audience's thoughts and purchasing decisions.| Apple has a huge mindshare among young consumers. 苹果在年轻消费者中拥有巨大的市场份额。|Synonyms: awareness, recognition, market penetration, top-of-mind awareness |Antonyms: obscurity, anonymity| noun
