dandle:/ˈdændl/| v.|轻摇，轻晃；逗弄，哄|to move a baby or young child up and down gently in your arms or on your knees|She dandled the baby on her knee. 她把婴儿放在膝盖上轻轻地摇晃。|近义词：bounce, rock, sway|反义词:ignore, neglect|动词

dandle:/ˈdændl/| v.|奉承，迎合|to treat someone in a way that is designed to make them happy and make them like you, often by praising them too much|He dandled the investors with promises of quick profits. 他用快速获利的承诺来奉承投资者。|近义词：flatter, cajole, coax|反义词:criticize, reprimand|动词
