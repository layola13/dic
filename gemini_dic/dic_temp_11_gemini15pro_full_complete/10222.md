apostolicisms:/ˌæpəˈstɒlɪsɪzəmz/
n.
使徒的教义、习俗或实践
Doctrines, customs, or practices of the apostles.
The book detailed the apostolicisms of the early church. 这本书详细描述了早期教会的使徒教义和习俗。
Near Synonyms:  apostolic doctrines, apostolic traditions, apostolic practices
Antonyms:  heresies, heterodoxies
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *apostolicismus*, from apostolicus (“apostolic”), from Greek ἀποστολικός (apostolikós, “of or pertaining to an apostle”), from ἀπόστολος (apóstolos, “apostle, messenger”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *stel- (“to put, stand”).
