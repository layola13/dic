nonpictorially:/ˌnɒnpɪkˈtɔːriəli/
adverb
非图像地，非图画地
Not using or involving pictures or other visual representations.
He described the scene nonpictorially, focusing on the sounds and smells. 他没有用图像的方式描述场景，而是专注于声音和气味。
non-visually, abstractly
visually, pictorially
adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + pictorially.  Pictorial comes from the Latin "pictorius" meaning "of or pertaining to painting".
