deviation:/ˌdɪːviˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 偏差，偏离，背离 | a variation from the common standard, type, etc.  |  The deviation from the plan caused some delays.  偏离计划造成了一些延误。 | synonym:  variance, aberration, digression, departure | antonym: conformity, adherence, normalcy | noun

deviation:/ˌdiːviˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 偏差，偏离，背离 | the difference between a measurement and the mean or expected value.  | The standard deviation is a measure of the spread of a dataset. 标准差是衡量数据集分布的指标。 | synonym: variance, dispersion | antonym:  convergence, similarity  | noun
