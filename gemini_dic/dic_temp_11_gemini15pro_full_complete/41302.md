cordovan:/ˈkɔːrdəvən/| n. adj.|科尔多瓦皮革；科尔多瓦（西班牙南部一城市）；科尔多瓦的|A type of fine leather originally made in Cordoba, Spain, from the hides of horses or goats.  Also, relating to the city of Cordoba.|例句：He wore expensive cordovan shoes. 他穿了昂贵的科尔多瓦皮鞋。

例句：The cordovan leather is known for its durability. 科尔多瓦皮革以其耐用性而闻名。|近义词：leather, horsehide|反义词:|n. adj.
