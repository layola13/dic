例句：The wedding was filled with allegresse. 婚礼充满了喜悦。

近义词：joy, mirth, gaiety, elation, exuberance | 反义词：sadness, gloom, melancholy, depression | 词性：名词

词源: From Old French *allegresse, from alegrer (“to make glad, rejoice”), from a- (“to”) + legrer (“to lighten”), from Latin laetus (“happy”).
