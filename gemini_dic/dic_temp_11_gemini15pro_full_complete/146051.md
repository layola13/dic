overdryly: /ˌoʊvərˈdraɪli/ | adv. | 过于干燥地，过于冷淡地 |  In an excessively dry or unemotional manner.  It describes a tone of voice or writing that lacks warmth or expression. | "He responded overdryly, 'That's nice,' clearly not impressed." 他过于冷淡地回答说：“那很好。” 显然没有被打动。| Synonyms: aridly, impassively, flatly, dully | Antonyms: enthusiastically, warmly, emotionally, expressively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "dryly" (adverb form of "dry")
