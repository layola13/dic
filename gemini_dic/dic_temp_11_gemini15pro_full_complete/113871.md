madling:/ˈmæd.lɪŋ/| n.|疯子；狂人|A person who behaves in a wild or uncontrolled way, especially because of mental illness.|The madling raved and tore at his clothes. 这个疯子胡言乱语，撕扯着自己的衣服。|Synonyms: lunatic, maniac, insane person|Antonyms: sane person, rational person|noun


madling:/ˈmæd.lɪŋ/| adj.|疯狂的；狂暴的|Behaving in a wild or uncontrolled way.|He had a madling look in his eyes. 他的眼神疯狂。|Synonyms: frantic, frenzied, rabid|Antonyms: calm, composed, sane|adjective
