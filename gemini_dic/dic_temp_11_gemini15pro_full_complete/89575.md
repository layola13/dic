hoo:/huː/
pron.|谁|who is shortened to hoo, used mostly in informal spoken English. |"Hoo's there?"  谁在那儿？|近义词:who|反义词: (None -  'who' asks for identity, it doesn't have an opposite)|词性:pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Who is at the door? = 谁在门口？
Who wants ice cream? = 谁想要冰淇淋？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Who is knocking on the window? = 谁在敲窗户？
Who is singing that song? = 谁在唱那首歌？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Who has eaten all the cookies? = 谁吃了所有的饼干？
Who has finished their homework? = 谁完成了他们的作业？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Who has been using my computer? = 谁一直在用我的电脑？
Who has been playing the piano all morning? = 谁一直在弹钢琴一上午？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Who called last night? = 昨晚谁打电话来了？
Who broke the vase? = 谁打碎了花瓶？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Who was talking to you on the phone? = 谁在和你打电话？
Who was watching TV when I came in? = 我进来的时候谁在看电视？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Who had already left before the party started? = 派对开始前谁已经离开了？
Who had taken the last piece of cake? = 谁吃了最后一块蛋糕？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Who had been waiting for the bus for an hour? = 谁等了一个小时的公交车？
Who had been living in that house before you? = 在你之前谁住在那所房子里？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Who will be the next president? = 谁将是下一任总统？
Who will go to the store with me? = 谁会和我一起去商店？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Who will be attending the conference tomorrow? = 明天谁将参加会议？
Who will be performing at the concert next week? = 下周谁将在音乐会上表演？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Who will have completed the project by Friday? = 到周五谁将完成这个项目？
Who will have graduated by next summer? = 到明年夏天谁将毕业？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Who will have been working on this case for five years by then? = 到那时谁将在这个案件上工作五年了？
Who will have been studying English for ten years by the time they graduate? = 到他们毕业的时候，谁将已经学习英语十年了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew who was responsible, I would tell you. = 如果我知道谁负责，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew who sent me this gift. = 我希望我知道是谁送了我这份礼物。


被动语态:
By whom was this letter written? = 这封信是谁写的？ (formal)
Who was this painting created by? = 这幅画是谁创作的？


疑问句:
Who are you? = 你是谁？
Who is that woman? = 那个女人是谁？


否定句:
I don't know who he is. = 我不知道他是谁。
She didn't say who told her. = 她没说谁告诉她的。


PHRASE->
Who cares? = 谁在乎？
Who's who? =  哪些人重要/名流录
Who knows? = 谁知道呢？
Who's there? = 谁在那儿？
Who are you kidding? = 你在开玩笑吧？

注意事项: 虽然 hoo 可以替代 who， 但它非常不正式，最好避免在正式场合或书面英语中使用。  主要用于一些方言或非常随意的对话中。
