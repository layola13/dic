avowee:/əˌvaʊˈiː/
n.|公开承认者；声明者|The avowee is the person to whom a solemn declaration or acknowledgment is made.|The lawyer acted as avowee for his client's statement.|近义词：recipient, receiver, beneficiary|反义词:declarant, affiant|词性:名词

Etymology: From Anglo-French *avoué, past participle of avouer "to avow," from Latin advocare "to summon, call to one's aid," from ad- "to" + vocare "to call." The ending -ee is a suffix denoting the recipient or beneficiary of an action.
