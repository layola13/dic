misestimations:/ˌmɪsɛstɪˈmeɪʃənz/| n. |错误估计，估算错误|An act of misestimating; an inaccurate or incorrect estimation.  The process of making a judgment about the size, amount, or value of something that turns out to be incorrect. |The project suffered from a series of cost misestimations.  这个项目遭受了一系列的成本估算错误。|miscalculations, misjudgments, underestimations, overestimations|accurate estimations, correct assessments|noun
