nonliabilities:/ˌnɒnlaɪəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|非负债，非债务|Amounts owed to others are liabilities, while assets and owner's equity are nonliabilities.  欠他人的金额是负债，而资产和所有者权益是非负债。|The company's nonliabilities included cash, buildings, and retained earnings.  该公司的非负债包括现金、建筑物和留存收益。|近义词：assets, equity|反义词:liabilities|词性:名词
