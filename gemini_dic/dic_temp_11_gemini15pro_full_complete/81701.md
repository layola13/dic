例句：Her greenhornism in the business world was quickly exploited by her competitors.  她在商界的稚嫩很快就被竞争对手利用了。|近义词：naivete, inexperience, awkwardness, gaucherie|反义词：sophistication, experience, expertise, worldliness|词性: noun
