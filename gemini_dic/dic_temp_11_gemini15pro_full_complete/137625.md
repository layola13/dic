nontumultuousnesses:/ˌnɒnˌtjuːˈmʌltʃʊəsnəsɪz/
n.
lack of tumult; peacefulness; calmness
The nontumultuousness of the library provided a welcome respite from the noisy city.  图书馆的宁静提供了远离喧嚣城市的休憩之所。
The meeting proceeded with surprising nontumultuousness, despite the contentious issues on the agenda. 尽管议程上有争议性问题，会议进行得异常平静。
Synonyms: tranquility, serenity, peace, quiet, calmness
Antonyms: tumult, chaos, disorder, turbulence, uproar
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + tumultuous (characterized by tumult; noisy and disorderly) + -ness (forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Tumultuous ultimately derives from Latin tumultus (“commotion, uproar”).
