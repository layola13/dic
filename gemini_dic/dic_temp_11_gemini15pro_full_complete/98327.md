insincerely:/ˌɪnsɪnˈsɪrli/
adverb
不真诚地，虚伪地
Not expressing genuine feelings.
He apologized insincerely for the mistake. 他不真诚地为这个错误道歉。

近义词:dishonestly, falsely, hypocritically
反义词:sincerely, genuinely, honestly
词性:adverb
