kilderkin:/ˈkɪldərkɪn/| n.|小桶，容量单位（等于18加仑或82升）|A kilderkin is a small barrel or cask, formerly used for liquids, and having a capacity varying in different localities but usually equal to about 18 gallons or half a hogshead.|The pub served its ale in kilderkins.|近义词:barrel, cask, keg|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "tank" for a very large container)|noun
