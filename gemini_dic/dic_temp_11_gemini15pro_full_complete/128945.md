ning-nong:/ˈnɪŋnɒŋ/ /ˈnɪŋnɑːŋ/ | interjection | 闲聊，胡扯，无意义的谈话 | Meaningless chatter or idle talk. Often used dismissively. | Example: He was just ning-nonging on about nothing in particular. 他只是在胡扯些无关紧要的事情。| Synonym: babble, prattle, chatter, waffle | Antonym:  meaningful conversation, articulate speech | 词性:感叹词

ning-nong:/ˈnɪŋnɒŋ/ /ˈnɪŋnɑːŋ/ | noun | 废话，闲扯 | Meaningless chatter or idle talk. | Example: I couldn't stand any more of his ning-nong. 我再也受不了他的废话了。 | Synonym: babble, prattle, chatter, waffle, nonsense | Antonym: sense, facts, truth | 词性:名词
