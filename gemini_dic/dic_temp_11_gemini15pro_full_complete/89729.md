hopes:/həʊps/ (UK) /hoʊps/ (US)| n. |希望，期望|Plural form of hope: a feeling of expectation and desire for a certain thing to happen. |She has high hopes for her children's future. 她对孩子们的未来寄予厚望。|Near Synonyms: expectations, aspirations, desires|Antonyms: despair, hopelessness|Noun
