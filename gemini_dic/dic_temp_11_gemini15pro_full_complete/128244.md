neurolinguistic:/ˌnʊəroʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/| adj.|神经语言学的|Relating to the relationship between the brain and language.  It combines the fields of neurology (the study of the nervous system) and linguistics (the study of language).|Neurolinguistic programming is a controversial technique. 神经语言程序学是一个备受争议的技术。|Synonyms: neuro-linguistic|Antonyms: None|adjective

neurolinguistics:/ˌnʊəroʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/| n.|神经语言学|The study of the neural mechanisms in the human brain that control the comprehension, production, and acquisition of language.  It is a branch of cognitive science.|Her research in neurolinguistics focuses on language acquisition in children. 她的神经语言学研究重点是儿童的语言习得。|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|noun
