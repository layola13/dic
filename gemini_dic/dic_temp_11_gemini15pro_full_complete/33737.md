circuiteer: /sɜːrˈkɪtɪər/
noun
巡回者；巡回传教士；巡回法官
A person who travels a circuit, especially a preacher, judge, or salesperson.
The circuiteer visited several small towns during his trip.  这个巡回者在他的旅途中访问了几个小镇。
synonyms: traveler, itinerant, journeyman, wandering preacher, circuit rider
antonyms: resident, settler, stay-at-home
词性: 名词
