cratered:/ˈkreɪtərd/
adj.
布满陨石坑的；形成坑的
Marked with craters; having formed a crater.
The moon's surface is cratered. 月球表面布满了陨石坑。
pockmarked, pitted
smooth, unblemished
adjective


cratered:/ˈkreɪtərd/
v.
形成坑；崩塌
To form a crater in; to collapse.
The bomb cratered the landscape. 炸弹把地面炸出了许多坑。
collapse, cave in
expand, bulge
verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From crater + -ed.  Ultimately from the Greek "krater" (mixing bowl).
