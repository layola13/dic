beakiron:/ˈbiːkˌaɪərn/
noun
喙铁，鸟嘴铁（一种用于修剪鸟喙的工具）
A tool used for trimming a bird's beak.
The farmer used a beak iron to trim the chickens' beaks. 农民用喙铁修剪鸡的喙。
Synonyms: beak trimmer, beak cutter
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词


Etymology:
"beak" + "iron". "Beak" comes from Old French "bec" meaning "beak, bill". "Iron" comes from Old English "īren".
