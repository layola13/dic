hansom:/ˈhænsəm/| n. |汉森马车（一种双轮马车）|A two-wheeled covered carriage with the driver's seat elevated behind.  It was a popular form of public transport in the 19th century.|例句：The hansom cab clip-clopped down the cobbled street. 汉森马车在鹅卵石街道上发出得得的响声。

例句：He hailed a hansom and told the driver to take him to the station.  他叫了一辆汉森马车，告诉车夫把他送到车站。|近义词：cab, carriage, hackney|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Joseph Hansom (1803-1882), the architect who patented the design in 1834.
