cross-culturally:/ˌkrɒsˈkʌltʃərəli/ | adv. | 跨文化地 | In a way that involves or relates to different cultures. |  She studies cross-culturally to understand different perspectives. 她进行跨文化研究以理解不同的观点。

Example 2: The program promotes cross-culturally understanding and cooperation.  这个项目促进了跨文化的理解与合作。

| 近义词: interculturally | 反义词: intraculturally | 词性: 副词(adverb)


ETYMOLOGY->

From "cross-" (meaning "across" or "between") + "culturally" (adverbial form of "cultural," relating to culture).  The prefix "cross-" indicates interaction or connection between different cultures.  "Cultural" ultimately derives from the Latin word "cultura" meaning "cultivation" or "growing."
