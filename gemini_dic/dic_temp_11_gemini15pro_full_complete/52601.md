digested:/daɪˈdʒestɪd/ /dɪˈdʒestɪd/
adjective|消化的；吸收的；领悟的|broken down by the digestive system; absorbed and understood|例句：The digested food is absorbed into the bloodstream. 消化后的食物被吸收到血液中。|近义词：absorbed, assimilated, processed, understood|反义词：undigested, raw|词性:adjective
digested:/daɪˈdʒestɪd/ /dɪˈdʒestɪd/
verb|消化；吸收；领悟|to break down food in the stomach and intestines; to absorb and understand information|例句：I have digested all the information you gave me. 我已经消化了你给我的所有信息。|近义词：absorbed, assimilated, processed, understood|反义词：undigested, raw|词性:verb

词源: From Latin *digestus*, past participle of digerere (“to separate, distribute, arrange, digest”), from dis- (“apart”) + gerere (“to carry, bear”).
