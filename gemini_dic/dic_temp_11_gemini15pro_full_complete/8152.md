anti-constitutional:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənl/
adjective
违宪的，反宪法的
Opposing or violating the principles of a constitution.
The court ruled that the law was anti-constitutional. 法院裁定该法律违宪。
The group protested the anti-constitutional policies of the government.  该团体抗议政府的违宪政策。
Synonyms: unconstitutional, illegal, unlawful
Antonyms: constitutional, legal, lawful
词性: adjective
