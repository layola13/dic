hagbut:/ˈhæɡbʌt/ (US), /ˈhæɡbət/ (UK)| n.|火绳枪;早期的一种步枪|A type of early firearm, a heavy musket fired from a rest.|The soldiers were armed with hagbuts and pikes. 士兵们装备了火绳枪和长矛。

The hagbut's accuracy surprised the enemy. 火绳枪的精准度让敌人吃惊。|近义词：arquebus, musket|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bow and arrow" as a contrasting older weapon)|noun
