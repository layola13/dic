detractors:/dɪˈtræktərz/
n.|批评者，诋毁者|A person who disparages someone or something. |The film attracted many detractors, but it also had its supporters.|Synonyms: critic, attacker, slanderer, denigrator|Antonyms: supporter, admirer, advocate
