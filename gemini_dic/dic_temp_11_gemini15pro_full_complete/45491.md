curdiest:/ˈkɜːrdiɪst/
adjective
最凝乳状的；最像凝乳的
Most resembling curd; most like curd.
The curdiest cheese I've ever tasted was from a small farm in the Alps. 我尝过最像凝乳的奶酪来自阿尔卑斯山的一个小农场。
Near synonyms:  thickest, densest,  coagulated, clotted
Opposite: thinnest, runniest
词性: adjective
