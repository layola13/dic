nonsententious:/ˌnɒnsenˈtenʃəs/ /ˌnɑːnsenˈtenʃəs/
adjective
简洁的，精炼的；不爱说教的
Expressing much in few words; concise; not given to pompous moralizing.
His nonsententious style was a welcome change from the usual academic prose.  他简洁的风格与通常的学术散文相比是一个令人欣喜的变化。
The speaker's nonsententious delivery kept the audience engaged. 演讲者简洁的表达方式吸引了观众。
concise, succinct, pithy, brief, terse
wordy, verbose, prolix, long-winded, rambling
词性: 形容词
