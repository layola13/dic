go-native:/ɡəʊ ˈneɪtɪv/ | v. | 入乡随俗；同化；采取当地人的生活方式 | To adopt the lifestyle and customs of the local inhabitants, especially when living in a foreign country. |  He went native after living in Africa for ten years. 他在非洲生活了十年后就入乡随俗了。 |  The tourists went native, wearing traditional clothes and eating local food.  游客们入乡随俗，穿着传统服装，吃着当地食物。 |  assimilate, acculturate, integrate | resist, maintain one's own culture | verb
