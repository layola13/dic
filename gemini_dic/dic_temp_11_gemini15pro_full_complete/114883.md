mammy-wagon:/ˈmæmiˌwæɡən/| n.|保姆车，婴儿车 (带有种族歧视色彩，现已弃用)|A car, typically a station wagon, used primarily for transporting children and household goods. This term is now considered offensive due to its association with racist stereotypes of Black women as domestic servants.|例句：In the past, some families used a mammy-wagon for road trips. (过去，有些家庭使用保姆车进行公路旅行。)

例句：The term "mammy-wagon" is outdated and offensive. (“保姆车”这个词已经过时且具有冒犯性。)


|近义词：station wagon, minivan, family car|反义词:sports car|词性:noun
