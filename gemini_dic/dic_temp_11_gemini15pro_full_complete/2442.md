adjugate:/ˈædʒəɡət/| n.|伴随矩阵;共轭矩阵|A square matrix obtained by replacing each element of a square matrix by its cofactor and then transposing the resulting matrix.  It is used in finding the inverse of a matrix.|The adjugate of matrix A is denoted as adj(A). 伴随矩阵A表示为adj(A).|The adjugate is useful in calculating the inverse of a matrix. 伴随矩阵在计算矩阵的逆矩阵时很有用。|近义词:classical adjoint|反义词:|n.
