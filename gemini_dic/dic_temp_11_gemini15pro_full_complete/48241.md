decontaminators:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtərz/
noun
净化人员，去污人员
People or devices that remove contaminants from something.
The decontaminators worked tirelessly to clean up the spill. 净化人员不知疲倦地清理泄漏物。
Specialized decontaminators were brought in to sanitize the lab after the experiment.  实验结束后，专门的净化人员被带进来对实验室进行消毒。
synonyms: cleaners, purifiers, sanitizers
antonyms: contaminators, polluters
