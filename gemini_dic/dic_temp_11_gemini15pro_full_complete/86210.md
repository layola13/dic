heathenizes:/ˈhiːðənaɪzɪz/
verb
使异教化，使不信教
To cause someone or something to become heathen or to adopt heathen practices or beliefs.
The missionaries worried that exposure to other cultures would heathenize their converts.  传教士担心接触其他文化会使他们的皈依者异教化。
The prolonged isolation heathenized the small community. 长时间的孤立使这个小社区异教化了。
synonyms: paganize, barbarize
antonyms: Christianize, evangelize
词性:v.
