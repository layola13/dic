conscientize:/ˈkɒnʃɪɛntaɪz/ /ˈkɑːnʃiɛntaɪz/| vt.|使…意识到；启发…的觉悟；|To make (someone) aware of social, political, or economic realities, especially so as to motivate action.|The workshops aim to conscientize teenagers about the dangers of drug abuse. 这些研讨会的目的是让青少年意识到滥用药物的危害。|近义词：raise awareness, awaken, educate, enlighten|反义词：suppress, deceive, mislead|动词

conscientize:/ˈkɒnʃɪɛntaɪz/ /ˈkɑːnʃiɛntaɪz/| n.|觉悟；|The process of developing a social conscience.|The movement focused on conscientization and empowerment of marginalized communities. 这场运动的重点是边缘化社区的觉悟和赋权。|近义词：awareness, consciousness|反义词：ignorance, apathy|名词
