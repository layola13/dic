faze:/feɪz/
verb
使慌乱；使担忧；使烦躁
to disturb the composure of; disconcert
例句：The pressure didn’t faze him at all. 压力一点也没有让他慌乱。
例句：He was totally fazed by the news. 他完全被这个消息弄得不知所措。
近义词：disconcert, unsettle, disturb, ruffle, fluster
反义词：calm, compose, reassure
词性:v.


fazes:/ˈfeɪzɪz/
verb(third-person singular present)
使慌乱；使担忧；使烦躁

例句：It doesn't faze him that people stare.  人们盯着他看，他一点也不在乎。
近义词：disconcerts, unsettles, disturbs, ruffles, flusters
反义词：calms, composes, reassures
词性:v.
