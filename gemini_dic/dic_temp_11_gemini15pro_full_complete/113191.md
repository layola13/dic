lurid:/ˈlʊrɪd/ /ˈlʊərɪd/| adj.|可怕的，耸人听闻的，耀眼的|Causing shock or horror; gruesome. Unpleasantly bright in colour, especially so as to create a harsh or unnatural effect.|The newspaper published lurid details of the murder. 这家报纸刊登了耸人听闻的谋杀细节。|近义词：sensational, shocking, gruesome, ghastly, vivid, garish|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, muted, subtle|词性:形容词

lurid:/ˈlʊrɪd/ /ˈlʊərɪd/| adj.|苍白的，病态的|Pale and unhealthy in appearance.|He had a lurid complexion. 他面色苍白，病态。|近义词：pale, wan, sickly|反义词：healthy, rosy|词性:形容词
