hydrators:/ˈhaɪdreɪtərz/
noun
补水剂，保湿剂
Substances or products that add or restore water or moisture.
These hydrators are perfect for dry skin. 这些保湿剂非常适合干性皮肤。
synonyms: moisturizers, humectants
antonyms: dehydrators, desiccants
n.
