correlated: /ˈkɒrəleɪtɪd/ (British) /ˈkɔːrəleɪtɪd/ (American) | adj. |相关的，相互关联的 | Statistically related; exhibiting a mutual relationship or connection. |  The study found that income and education levels are strongly correlated.  这项研究发现，收入和教育水平密切相关。

The amount of rainfall is correlated with the growth of crops. 降雨量与作物生长相关。

近义词: related, connected, associated, linked, interdependent

反义词: unrelated, unconnected, independent, dissociated

词性: adjective

词源: From the Latin word "correlātus," past participle of "correlāre," meaning "to bring together," from "com-" (together) + "referre" (to relate).
