lows:/loʊz/
n.
低点；低水平；低谷；低潮；牛叫声
a state or situation in which something is at a low level or intensity.
The stock market reached new lows this week. 这周股市跌到了新低。

lows:/loʊz/
n.
(Low 的复数)
plural of low.
The lows of his career were overshadowed by his later successes.  他职业生涯的低谷被他后来的成功所掩盖。

Etymology: From Middle English *lowe, from Old Norse *lāgr, from Proto-Germanic *lēgaz. Cognate with Old English lāg ("low").

Synonym: minimum, bottom, nadir
Antonym: high, peak, apex
Noun
