larixinic: /ˌlærɪˈksɪnɪk/
| adj.|落叶松的；落叶松酸的|Relating to or derived from larch trees, especially Larix decidua, or their resin.  It can also refer to larixinic acid, a constituent of larch resin.|Larixinic acid is found in larch resin. 落叶松酸存在于落叶松树脂中。| The larixinic forests are beautiful in the fall. 秋天的落叶松林很美。 |近义词:larch, coniferous |反义词:deciduous (in a broader sense, as larixinic trees *are* deciduous conifers) |词性:adjective
