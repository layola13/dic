hobbyhorse:/ˈhɑːbiˌhɔːrs/ (US), /ˈhɒbiˌhɔːs/ (UK)| n.|爱好；癖好；反复谈论的话题；竹马|A subject that someone enjoys talking or complaining about very often.  A child’s toy consisting of a stick with a horse’s head.|He's always on his hobbyhorse about the decline of Western civilization. 他总是反复谈论西方文明的衰落。 The child galloped around the room on his hobbyhorse.  孩子骑着竹马在房间里跑来跑去。|近义词：obsession, preoccupation, fad, quirk, stick horse|反义词：disinterest, aversion|noun
