disedge:/dɪsˈɛdʒ/
verb
使…丧失优势；使…处于不利地位；使…边缘化
To deprive of an advantage; to disadvantage; to marginalize.
The new trade agreement disedged local businesses. 新的贸易协定使当地企业处于不利地位。
近义词: disadvantage, marginalize, handicap
反义词: advantage, empower, support
词性: verb

disedged:/dɪsˈɛdʒd/
adjective
处于劣势的；边缘化的
Deprived of an advantage; disadvantaged; marginalized.
The disedged communities struggled to access resources. 边缘化的社区难以获得资源。
近义词: disadvantaged, marginalized, underprivileged
反义词: advantaged, privileged, empowered
词性: adjective
