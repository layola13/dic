mishear: /ˌmɪsˈhɪr/ | v. | 误听，听错 | To hear incorrectly; to misunderstand what is said. | I misheard him and thought he said he was leaving, but he was actually staying. 我听错了他的话，以为他说他要离开，但实际上他要留下来。|  I misheard the directions and ended up getting lost. 我听错了指示，结果迷路了。 | 近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret | 反义词: hear correctly | 词性: 动词
