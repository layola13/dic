bushranging:/ˈbʊʃˌreɪndʒɪŋ/
n.
丛林强盗行为；劫掠；拦路抢劫
The act of living as a bushranger; robbery under arms.
Bushranging was a serious problem in 19th-century Australia.  19世纪的澳大利亚，丛林强盗行为是一个严重的问题。
Ned Kelly is perhaps the most famous Australian bushranger.  奈德·凯利也许是澳大利亚最著名的丛林强盗。
近义词: outlawry, banditry, highway robbery
反义词: law enforcement
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "bush" (uncultivated country) + "ranging" (roaming, wandering).  源自“bush”（未开垦的乡村）+“ranging”（漫游、流浪）。
