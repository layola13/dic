cotyledon:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. | 子叶; （尤指双子叶植物的）第一片叶 | The cotyledon provides nutrients for the developing seedling. 子叶为发育中的幼苗提供营养。|  A bean has two cotyledons.  一颗豆子有两片子叶。| synonym: seed leaf | antonym: N/A | noun

cotyle:/ˈkɒtaɪl/ | n. | 髋臼; 杯状窝 | The cotyle is the socket in the hip bone. 髋臼是髋骨中的窝。| The head of the femur fits into the cotyle.  股骨头与髋臼相嵌合。| synonym: acetabulum | antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cotylēdon*, from Ancient Greek κοτυληδών (kotulēdṓn), meaning "cup-shaped hollow",  from κοτύλη (kotúlē, “cup, bowl”).
