disestablish:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃ/
verb
废除；取消；解除…的建制
To deprive (an established church) of its official status. To end the established status of something.
The Church of England was disestablished in Wales in 1920.  威尔士教会于1920年被取消了国教地位。
The government disestablished the national airline. 政府取消了国家航空公司的官方地位。
近义词: abolish, dissolve, dismantle
反义词: establish, found, institute
词性: verb
