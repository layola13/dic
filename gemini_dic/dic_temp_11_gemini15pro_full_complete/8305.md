anti-nuclear:/ˌæntiˈnjuːkliər/ /ˌæntiˈnuːkliər/
adjective
反核的；反对使用核武器的；反对核能的
Opposed to the use of nuclear weapons or nuclear energy.
例句：
The anti-nuclear movement gained momentum in the 1980s.
反核运动在20世纪80年代获得了发展势头。

She joined the anti-nuclear protest.
她参加了反核抗议。

近义词：antinuclear, non-nuclear
反义词：pro-nuclear

词性: adjective
