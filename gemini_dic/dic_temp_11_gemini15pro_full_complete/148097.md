overscutched:/ˌōvərˈskʌtʃt/
adjective
过度鞭打的；过度责备的；过度苛刻的
Overly criticized or punished; excessively harsh.  Derived from "overscutch," a now obsolete verb meaning to whip or flog excessively.  Figuratively, it means to criticize or punish too harshly.
The team felt overscutched after their coach's relentless criticism following the loss.  球队在输球后受到了教练无情的批评，感觉受到了过度责备。
The child was overscutched for a minor mistake.  这个孩子因为一个小错误而受到了过度的责备。
近义词: overcriticized, overpunished, castigated, lambasted
反义词: praised, commended, lauded
