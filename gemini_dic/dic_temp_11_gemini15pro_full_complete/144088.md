outsaint:/ˌaʊtˈseɪnt/
verb
胜过…的圣洁；在圣洁方面超过
to surpass in holiness or saintliness
例句：
Mother Teresa outsaintes most people. 特蕾莎修女比大多数人都更圣洁。

She tried to outsaint everyone in the convent. 她试图在修道院里胜过所有人。


近义词：surpass, excel, outdo
反义词：lag behind, fall short


词性：v.


词源：out- + saint
