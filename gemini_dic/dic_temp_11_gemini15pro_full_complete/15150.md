back-formation:/ˌbæk fɔːrˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 逆构词法；逆生词 | The creation of a new word (a neologism) by removing an affix from another word.  This usually happens when the affix looks like it's something it isn't, as in forming the verb *edit* from the older noun *editor*. |  The verb "donate" is a back-formation from "donation."  捐赠这个动词是从捐赠品这个名词逆构而来的。

The verb "lase" is a back-formation from the noun "laser". 激光这个动词是从激光器这个名词逆构而来的。 | 近义词: neologism, derivation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
