brobdingnagian:/ˌbrɒbdɪŋˈnæɡɪən/ /ˌbrɑːbdɪŋˈnæɡiən/
adjective
巨大的，庞大的
Of extraordinary size; gigantic.  Relating to the fictional land of giants in Jonathan Swift's *Gulliver's Travels*.
The company unveiled its brobdingnagian new headquarters, a skyscraper that dwarfed all others in the city.  公司公布了其巨大的新总部，一座让城市中所有其他建筑物都相形见绌的摩天大楼。

The athlete's brobdingnagian appetite allowed him to consume vast quantities of food.  这位运动员巨大的胃口让他可以吃掉大量的食物。

synonym
gigantic, colossal, immense, enormous, mammoth
antonym
tiny, minuscule, diminutive, Lilliputian
词性
