convertiblenesses:/kənˌvɜːrtɪˈbɪlɪtɪz/
n.
可兑换性（复数）
The quality or state of being convertible, especially of currency or securities, into cash or other assets.
例句：The convertibilities of different currencies vary depending on government regulations and market conditions. 不同货币的可兑换性取决于政府法规和市场条件。

例句：The convertibilities of these bonds into common stock are crucial for attracting investors. 这些债券转换成普通股的可兑换性对于吸引投资者至关重要。

近义词：exchangeability, tradability
反义词：inconvertibility, non-exchangeability
词性: noun (plural)


词源:  From convertible + -ness.  "Convertible" ultimately derives from the Latin "convertere" meaning "to turn around, to change."
