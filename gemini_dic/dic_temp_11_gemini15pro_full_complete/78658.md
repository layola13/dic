gie:/ɡiː/ | n. | 液体; 活力; 精力;  |A Scottish word meaning liquid, vitality, or energy.| He's got plenty of gie in him yet. 他还很有活力。 | 近义词: energy, vitality, spirit, pep, vim | 反义词: lethargy, apathy | 词性: noun

词源:  From Scots, probably a shortened form of *ingyne "ingenuity, natural talent," from Middle English ingenie, from Old French engin, from Latin ingenium "inborn quality, talent."
