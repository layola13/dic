bittor: /ˈbɪtər/ | adj. | 苦的；痛苦的；辛酸的；严酷的；充满仇恨的 | experiencing or showing strong feelings of sadness, anger, or disappointment |  The coffee was too bitter for my taste. 这咖啡对我来说太苦了。| synonyms: acrid, harsh, resentful, sour, rancorous | antonyms: sweet, pleasant, happy | adjective

bittor: /ˈbɪtər/ | n. | 苦味；苦的东西 | something that tastes bitter | The beer had a distinct bitter. 这啤酒有一种明显的苦味。| synonyms: bitterness, acrimony | antonyms: sweetness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "biter" (sharp, cutting, cruel), from Proto-Germanic "*bitraz" (to bite), from Proto-Indo-European "*bheid-" (to split, cleave). The sense of "tasting sharp" developed later.
