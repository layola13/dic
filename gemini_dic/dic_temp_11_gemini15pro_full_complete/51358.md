deter:/dɪˈtɜːr/ (US) /dɪˈtəːr/ (UK)| v. |阻止，威慑，使不敢|To discourage someone from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.|The heavy rain deterred us from going out. 大雨阻止了我们外出。|The high security cameras are intended to deter shoplifters.  高安全性摄像头旨在震慑商店扒手。|近义词：discourage, prevent, hinder, inhibit|反义词：encourage, promote, incite|词性:动词
