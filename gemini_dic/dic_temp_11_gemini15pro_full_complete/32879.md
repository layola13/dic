chondrigen:/ˈkɒndrɪdʒən/ | n. | 线粒体 | A mitochondrion, especially one in cartilage cells.  It refers specifically to mitochondria found within chondrocytes (cartilage cells). |  Chondrigens play a crucial role in the energy metabolism of cartilage. 线粒体在软骨的能量代谢中起着至关重要的作用。|  Damage to chondrigens can contribute to osteoarthritis. 线粒体的损伤会导致骨关节炎。 | Synonyms: mitochondrion (general term),  | Antonyms: N/A | noun
