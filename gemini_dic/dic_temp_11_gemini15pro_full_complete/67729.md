USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you see with your eyes? = 你用眼睛看到了什么？
What does he say when he sees you? = 他看到你时说了什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she pointing at with her finger? = 她用手指指着什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you seen today? = 你今天看到了什么？
What has she learned from this experience? = 她从这次经历中学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been staring at for so long? = 你一直盯着什么看了这么久？
What has he been working on all day? = 他一整天都在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you see yesterday? = 你昨天看到了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you watching when I came in? = 我进来的时候你在看什么？
What was she doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候她在做什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you seen before you came here? = 你来这里之前看到了什么？
What had she done before leaving? = 她离开前做了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been looking at before I interrupted? = 我打断你之前你一直在看什么？
What had she been working on before the meeting? = 会议前她一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you see tomorrow? = 你明天会看到什么？
What will she say when she finds out? = 她发现后会说什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference? = 她将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have seen by the end of the trip? = 到旅行结束时你将会看到什么？
What will she have achieved by next year? = 到明年她将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for ten years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么十年了？
What will she have been working on for so long? = 到那时她将已经从事什么工作那么久了？


虚拟语气:
If I saw what you saw, I would be amazed. = 如果我看到你看到的，我会感到惊讶。
I wish I knew what was going on. = 我希望我知道发生了什么。

被动语态:
What was seen by the witnesses? = 目击者看到了什么？
What is being done to address the issue? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What are you talking about? = 你在说什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
She didn't tell me what she wanted. = 她没有告诉我她想要什么。
