introductoriness:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌktərɪnɪs/
|n.|
|介绍性，引论性|
|The introductoriness of the first chapter makes it easy for readers to grasp the basic concepts. 第一章的介绍性使其易于读者掌握基本概念。|
|例句：The introductoriness of the preface helps readers understand the author's intent.  序言的介绍性帮助读者理解作者的意图。

|例句：Despite its introductoriness, the chapter still contained some complex ideas. 尽管具有介绍性，本章仍然包含一些复杂的概念。|
|近义词：preliminary nature, introductory character|
|反义词：conclusiveness, finality|
|词性：名词|
