nonfanatics:/ˌnɒnfəˈnætɪks/
noun
非狂热者；不狂热的人
People who are not fanatics; those who do not hold extreme or obsessive views.
The nonfanatics in the group tried to mediate between the warring factions.  非狂热者试图在交战派别之间进行调解。
The policy appealed to nonfanatics across the political spectrum. 这项政策吸引了所有政治派别的非狂热分子。
Synonyms: moderates, pragmatists, realists
Antonyms: fanatics, zealots, extremists
