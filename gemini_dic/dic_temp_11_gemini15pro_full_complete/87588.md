heterosis:/ˌhetəˈroʊsɪs/, /ˌhetərˈəʊsɪs/ | n. | 杂种优势，杂交优势 | The superiority of a hybrid over either parent in one or more traits. | The farmer saw significant heterosis in his corn crop after crossbreeding two varieties.  这位农民在杂交两个品种后，在他的玉米作物中看到了显著的杂种优势。|  hybrid vigor, outbreeding enhancement | inbreeding depression | noun

heterosis:/ˌhetəˈroʊsɪs/, /ˌhetərˈəʊsɪs/ | n. | 杂交活力 | The increased vigor or other superior qualities arising from the crossbreeding of genetically different plants or animals. |  The heterosis of the new strain of rice resulted in a higher yield. 这种新品种水稻的杂交活力带来了更高的产量。 | hybrid vigor, outbreeding enhancement | inbreeding depression | noun
