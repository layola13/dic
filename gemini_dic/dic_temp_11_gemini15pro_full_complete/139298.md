oblong:| /ˈɒblɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːblɔːŋ/ (US) | n. | 长方形，椭圆形 | A rectangular or oval shape. |  The mirror was an oblong. 镜子是长方形的。 | Cut the dough into an oblong. 把面团切成长方形。 | 近义词: rectangle, oval | 反义词: square, circle | 词性: noun

词源:
From Late Latin *oblongus, from Latin oblongus (“longer than broad”), from ob- (“before, towards”) + longus (“long”).
