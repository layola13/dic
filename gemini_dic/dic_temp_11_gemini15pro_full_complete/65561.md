eutrophy:/ˈjuːtrəfi/| n.|富营养化|The excessive richness of nutrients in a lake or other body of water, frequently due to runoff from the land, which causes a dense growth of plant life and death of animal life from lack of oxygen.|The algal bloom was a clear sign of eutrophy in the lake.  湖中的藻华是湖泊富营养化的明显标志。|近义词：overfertilization, hypertrophication|反义词:oligotrophy|名词
