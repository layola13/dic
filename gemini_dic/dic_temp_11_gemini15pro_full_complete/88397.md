hirohito:/ˌhiːrɔːˈhiːtoʊ/ | n. | 裕仁天皇 |  The 124th Emperor of Japan, reigning from December 25, 1926, until his death on January 7, 1989.  His reign was marked by Japan's expansionism, militarism, and involvement in World War II.  After the war, he became a symbol of Japan's transition to a peaceful, democratic nation. | Emperor Hirohito officially renounced his divine status after World War II. 裕仁天皇在二战后正式宣布放弃神圣地位。

Hirohito's reign saw significant changes in Japan. 裕仁天皇的统治时期见证了日本重大的变化。 | 近义词: Emperor of Japan, Showa Emperor | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
