isographic:/ˌaɪsəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
等值的；等压线的；等高线的
Relating to or denoting lines on a map or chart connecting points having the same value of a given quantity, such as barometric pressure or altitude.
Isographic lines on weather maps connect points of equal barometric pressure.  天气图上的等压线连接气压相等的点。
The isographic lines on this topographical map represent equal elevations. 这张地形图上的等高线代表相同的海拔高度。
synonyms:  isobaric, isometric, contour
antonyms:  anisobaric, anisometric
词性: adjective
