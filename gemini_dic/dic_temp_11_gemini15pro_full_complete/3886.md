agreeable:/əˈɡriːəbl/| adj.|令人愉快的；惬意的；易相处的；同意的|Pleasing, pleasant, or suitable; willing to agree or consent.|The weather was agreeable, and we spent the afternoon in the park. 天气宜人，我们在公园里度过了一个下午。|近义词：pleasant, amiable, congenial, acceptable|反义词：disagreeable, unpleasant, objectionable|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle French *agréable*, from Late Latin *agrābilis* (“pleasing”), from Latin *agrāre* (“to please”), from *ad* + *grātus* (“pleasing, thankful”).
