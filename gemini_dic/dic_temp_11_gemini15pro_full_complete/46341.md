dacoity:/dəˈkɔɪti/ | n. |抢劫；盗匪行为| The act of robbery by a band of armed dacoits.  It often involves violence and intimidation. |  The dacoity terrified the villagers.  这起抢劫案吓坏了村民们。|  robbery, banditry, brigandage | theft, larceny | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi डकैती (ḍakaiṭī), from डकैत (ḍakait, “robber”), probably from Sanskrit द्रुघट (dru-ghaṭa, “difficult access, defile”), from द्रु (dru, “difficult”) + घट (ghaṭa, “access, defile”).
