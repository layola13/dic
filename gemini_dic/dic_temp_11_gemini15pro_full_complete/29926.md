cavalierly:/ˌkævəˈlɪəli/
adverb
漫不经心地，轻率地
showing a lack of proper concern; offhand.
He cavalierly dismissed her worries. 他漫不经心地打消了她的顾虑。
Synonyms: offhandedly, airily, casually, lightly, nonchalantly, indifferently, carelessly, negligently
Antonyms: carefully, cautiously, thoughtfully, seriously, respectfully
词性: 副词
