antidemocracies:/ˌænti dɪˈmɒkrəsiːz/ | n. | 反民主国家；非民主国家 |  A political system that is opposed to democracy, often characterized by authoritarianism, suppression of dissent, and limited individual freedoms. | Many antidemocracies use propaganda to control the flow of information. 许多反民主国家利用宣传来控制信息流动。

Antidemocracies often restrict freedom of speech and assembly.  反民主国家通常限制言论和集会自由。 | 近义词: dictatorships, totalitarian states, authoritarian regimes | 反义词: democracies, republics | 词性: 名词(复数)
