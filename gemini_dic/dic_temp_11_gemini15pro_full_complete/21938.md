boggier:/ˈbɒɡɪə(r)/ | adj. | 更泥泞的，更沼泽的 | More boggy; wetter and softer, like a bog. | The ground was boggier after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，地面更加泥泞了。|  The trail became boggier and boggier the further we went.  我们走得越远，小路就越泥泞。| 近义词：marshier, swampier, wetter, softer | 反义词：drier, firmer, harder | 词性：形容词

boggy:/ˈbɒɡi/ | adj. | 泥泞的，沼泽的 | Wet and soft, like a bog. | The ground was boggy and difficult to walk on.  地面泥泞，难以行走。|  Be careful, the path is boggy in places.  小心，这条路有些地方很泥泞。 | 近义词：marshy, swampy, wet, soft | 反义词：dry, firm, hard | 词性：形容词

词源: From bog + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from the Old Irish "boc" meaning "soft."
