carousingly:/kəˈraʊzɪŋli/
adverb
狂欢地，纵情欢乐地
In a manner characterized by revelry and boisterous merriment.
They celebrated carousingly after winning the championship.
他们赢得冠军后纵情狂欢。
The sailors spent their shore leave carousingly, drinking and singing loudly.
水手们上岸休假时纵情欢乐，喝酒唱歌。
近义词: boisterously, convivially, festively, jovially, merrily
反义词: soberly, quietly, solemnly

词性: adverb

词源: From carouse + -ing + -ly.  "Carouse" comes from the German "Gar aus!" meaning "Drink up!" or "Empty it!"
