bitmapped:/ˈbɪtˌmæpt/
adjective
位图的
represented or displayed using a bitmap.
例句：A bitmapped image is made up of pixels. 位图图像由像素组成。

例句：Bitmapped fonts are less scalable than vector fonts. 位图字体比矢量字体可缩放性差。
近义词：raster, pixelated
反义词：vector
词性：adjective
