blabbered:/ˈblæbərd/| v.|胡言乱语；泄露秘密|talked foolishly or indiscreetly; revealed secrets.|He blabbered about the surprise party and ruined it. 他胡言乱语，把惊喜派对的事泄露了，搞砸了。|Synonyms: babbled, chattered, gossiped, prattled, tattled|Antonyms: clammed up, kept silent|verb

blabber:/ˈblæbər/| v. n.|胡言乱语；泄露(秘密)；饶舌；喋喋不休|to talk too much, usually without thinking about what you are saying; foolish or excessive talk|He blabbered on about nothing for hours. 他喋喋不休地说了几个小时的废话。|Synonyms: chatter, prattle, gabble|Antonyms: be quiet, shut up|verb, noun
