inditement:/ɪnˈdaɪtmənt/| n.|控告，起诉书；谴责，告发|a formal accusation that someone has committed a crime, presented to a court so that the person can be tried |The grand jury handed down an indictment against the mayor for corruption. 大陪审团对市长提出了腐败起诉。|synonyms: accusation, charge, prosecution|antonyms: acquittal, exoneration|noun
