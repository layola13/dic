comptroller:/kənˈtroʊlər/ (US) /kəmˈtrəʊlə(r)/ (UK)| n. |审计官，财务主管，主计长|A comptroller is a person who is in charge of the financial affairs of a company or government organization. |The comptroller presented the annual budget to the board of directors. 审计官向董事会提交了年度预算。|The city comptroller is responsible for overseeing all financial matters. 市财务主管负责监督所有财务事宜。|Synonym: auditor, treasurer, accountant|Antonym: N/A|Noun
