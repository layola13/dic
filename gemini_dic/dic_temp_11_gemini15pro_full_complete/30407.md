centipedal:/ˌsɛntɪˈpiːdəl/ | adj. | 百足的；像蜈蚣的 | Having or resembling a hundred feet; relating to or characteristic of a centipede.  | Example: The centipedal creature crawled across the damp forest floor.  这只百足生物爬过潮湿的森林地面。 | Example:  The artist's depiction of the dragon was distinctly centipedal, with countless legs writhing beneath its scaled body.  艺术家对龙的描绘明显具有蜈蚣的特征，无数的腿在其鳞片状的身体下蠕动。 | Synonym:  multi-legged | Antonym:  apodal (without feet) | adjective
