granitiform: /ɡrænˈɪtɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 花岗岩状的，似花岗岩的 | Resembling granite in texture or appearance.  | The rock displayed a granitiform texture, with interlocking crystals of various minerals. 这块岩石呈现出花岗岩状的结构，各种矿物的晶体相互交错。 | The cliff face was granitiform, composed of large, interlocking crystals. 悬崖表面呈花岗岩状，由大型交错晶体组成。 | 近义词: granitic, granitelike | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like: fine-grained, aphanitic) | 词性: adjective
