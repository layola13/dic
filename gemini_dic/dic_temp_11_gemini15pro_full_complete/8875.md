antifoaming:/ˌæntiˈfəʊmɪŋ/ | adj. n.| 抗泡沫的，消泡的；抗泡剂，消泡剂 |  Used to describe a substance or process that prevents or reduces the formation of foam.  Also used as a noun to refer to the substance itself. |  This detergent has excellent antifoaming properties.  这种洗涤剂具有优异的抗泡性能。| Synonyms: defoaming, antifoam | Antonyms: foaming, frothing | adjective, noun

antifoam:/ˈæntiˌfəʊm/ | n.| 抗泡剂，消泡剂 | A substance used to reduce or prevent the formation of foam. | We added antifoam to the mixture to control the foam. 我们在混合物中加入了消泡剂来控制泡沫。| Synonyms: defoamer, antifoaming agent | Antonyms: foaming agent | noun


Etymology:  "anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "foam" (from Old English "fam" meaning froth).
