abscission:/æbˈsɪʒən/ | n. |脱落，分离|The abscission of leaves in the autumn is a natural process. 秋天树叶的脱落是一个自然过程。|例句：The abscission of the fruit from the tree was caused by the strong winds.  强风导致果实从树上脱落。

例句：Surgical abscission of the damaged tissue was necessary.  需要手术切除受损组织。|近义词：separation, detachment, shedding |反义词: adhesion, attachment |词性:noun
