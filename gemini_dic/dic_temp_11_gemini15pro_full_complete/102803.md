jargoned:/ˈdʒɑːrɡənd/ | adj. | 使用术语的；行话的 | Filled with or expressed in jargon; technical or specialized vocabulary that is difficult for outsiders to understand. |  The contract was so jargoned that I couldn't understand it.  合同中充斥着术语，我看不懂。 | synonyms: technical, specialized, obscure, esoteric | antonyms: simple, clear, straightforward, understandable | adjective

jargoned:/ˈdʒɑːrɡənd/ | v. |  (rare) To express in jargon.  | He jargoned his way through the presentation, leaving the audience confused. 他在演讲中满口术语，让听众一头雾水。 | synonyms: obfuscate, confuse, mystify | antonyms: clarify, simplify, explain | verb
