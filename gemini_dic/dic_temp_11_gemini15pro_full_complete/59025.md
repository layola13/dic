dwarf:/dwɔːrf/ (UK) /dwɔːrf/ (US)| n. adj. v. |矮子，侏儒；矮小的|A person, animal, or plant that is much smaller than the usual size of its kind.  Also used as an adjective to describe something that is smaller than normal. As a verb, it means to cause to appear small or insignificant by comparison.|

例句：
The dwarf stood proudly despite his small stature.
这个矮人虽然身材矮小，却昂首挺胸。

Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs is a classic fairy tale.
《白雪公主和七个小矮人》是一个经典的童话故事。

His achievements dwarf those of his predecessors.
他的成就使他的前辈们相形见绌。

近义词：midget, pygmy, diminutive, small, tiny|
反义词：giant, enormous, huge|
词性: noun, adjective, verb|
