bedright:/ˈbɛdˌraɪt/
n.
床边; 床侧
The space immediately next to a bed.
She sat on the bedright, holding his hand. 她坐在床边，握着他的手。

He placed a glass of water on the bedright. 他在床边放了一杯水。

近义词: bedside
反义词: (没有明确的反义词)
词性: 名词
