overdramatically: /ˌoʊvərˌdrəmˈætɪkli/ | adv. | 过于戏剧化地，夸张地 | In a way that is excessively dramatic or exaggerated. | She reacted overdramatically to the news, bursting into tears and fainting. 她对这个消息反应过度，突然大哭起来，然后晕倒了。|  He overdramatically clutched his chest and pretended to have a heart attack. 他夸张地捂住胸口，假装心脏病发作。 | 近义词： theatrically, histrionically, melodramatically | 反义词： subtly, understatedly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "dramatic" + "-ally" (adverbial suffix).  From "over" + "drama" (from Greek "δρᾶμα" drama, "action, deed") + "-atic" + "-ally."
