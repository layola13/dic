over-run:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌn/ /ˌəʊvəˈrʌn/| n.| 超过；泛滥；蔓延；侵占| An act or instance of overrunning; the amount by which something overruns. | The overrun of locusts destroyed the crops. 蝗虫的泛滥摧毁了农作物。| 近义词：excess, surplus, overflow | 反义词：deficit, shortage | noun
