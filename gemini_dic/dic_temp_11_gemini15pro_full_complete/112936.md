lugged:/lʌɡd/| adj.|笨重的，拖沓的；装有提手的|Carrying or equipped with lugs or handles.  Having a heavy, cumbersome appearance. |例句：She lugged a heavy suitcase up the stairs. 她提着一个沉重的行李箱上楼。

例句：The old computer was a lugged, outdated machine. 那台旧电脑是一台笨重的、过时的机器。|近义词：cumbersome, heavy, bulky, unwieldy|反义词：lightweight, portable, compact|词性:形容词

lugged:/lʌɡd/| v.|（吃力地）拖，拉，搬；(lugging 的过去式和过去分词)|Past tense and past participle of "lug," meaning to carry or drag something heavy with effort.|例句：We lugged the furniture into the new apartment. 我们把家具搬进了新公寓。

例句：He lugged the box across the room. 他吃力地把箱子搬到房间的另一边。|近义词：haul, drag, carry, heave|反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑：leave, abandon) |词性:动词


词源: From the verb "lug," which originates from Scandinavian languages, possibly Old Norse "lugga" meaning "to pull by the hair."
