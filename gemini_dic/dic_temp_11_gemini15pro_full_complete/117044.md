mcmillan:/məkˈmɪlən/ | n. |麦克米伦|A surname, derived from a given name meaning "son of Millan". Millan is of uncertain origin, possibly from a Gaelic personal name.  It is also the name of a publishing company.| MacMillan Publishing is one of the largest American publishing companies.| 近义词: (As a surname, it has no direct synonyms.  As a publisher, synonyms could include: publisher, printing house, book company) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
