lonesomenesses:/ˈloʊnsəmˌnɪsɪz/
|n.|孤独，寂寞|The vastness of the desert emphasized her lonesomenesses.  沙漠的广阔更加突显了她的孤寂。|例句：The long winter nights intensified his lonesomenesses. 漫长的冬夜加剧了他的孤独感。

|例句：She experienced periods of intense lonesomenesses after her children left home. 孩子们离家后，她经历了一段非常孤独的时期。
|近义词：solitude, isolation, loneliness|反义词：companionship, togetherness|词性: 名词

lonesome:/ˈloʊnsəm/
|adj.|孤独的，寂寞的|She felt lonesome and abandoned. 她感到孤独和被遗弃。|例句：The lonesome howl of a wolf echoed through the valley. 孤独的狼嚎声在山谷中回荡。

|例句：He led a lonesome life in the remote cabin. 他在偏僻的小屋里过着孤独的生活。
|近义词：lonely, solitary, isolated|反义词：accompanied, sociable|词性: 形容词
