bumblepuppy:/ˈbʌmbəlˌpʌpi/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜，容易受骗的人|A naive, inexperienced, or gullible person, often easily misled or taken advantage of. |例句：He was such a bumblepuppy that he believed everything the con man told him. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信了骗子所说的一切。

例句：She's a bit of a bumblepuppy when it comes to business dealings. 在生意场上，她有点容易上当受骗。|近义词：dupe, fool, sucker, chump|反义词: shrewd, astute|词性:名词
