funky:/ˈfʌŋki/ | adj. |时髦的，节奏强烈的，有霉味的|Having a strong, distinctive, and typically rhythmic quality, especially in music; unconventional and stylish; having a moldy or musty smell.|例句：That band plays a funky kind of jazz. 那支乐队演奏一种节奏强烈的爵士乐。|近义词：groovy, hip, cool, stylish, earthy, musty |反义词：bland, dull, conventional, odorless |词性:形容词

funky:/ˈfʌŋki/ | n. |放克音乐|A style of music, usually for dancing, with a strong rhythm and repetitive bass line.|例句：He's really into funky these days. 他最近真的很喜欢放克音乐。|近义词：funk |反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑其他音乐类型，例如：classical music) |词性:名词
