asphaltite: /ˈæs.fɔːl.taɪt/ | n. |沥青岩，一种天然沥青|A hard, natural asphalt resembling albertite and gilsonite, occurring as veins in rocks.  It is a variety of solid bitumen.|Asphaltite was mined in the Uinta Basin during the early 20th century.  20世纪初，尤因塔盆地开采沥青岩。|近义词: bitumen, gilsonite, albertite, uintaite |反义词: None |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From asphalt + -ite (suffix forming names of minerals and rocks).  Ultimately from Greek *άσφαλτος (ásphaltos).
