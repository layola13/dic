helleborin:/ˈhɛlɪˌbɔːrɪn/| n. |嚏根草苷;嚏根草毒苷|A poisonous glycoside obtained from the rhizomes of black hellebore (Helleborus niger) and green hellebore (Helleborus viridis). It is a cardiac stimulant. |Helleborin can be a dangerous substance if ingested. 嚏根草苷如果摄入体内可能是一种危险物质。|Synonyms: hellebrin, hellebrigenin|Antonyms: None|Noun
