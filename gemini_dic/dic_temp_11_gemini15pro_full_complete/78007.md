gentlemen:/ˈdʒɛntlmən/ | n. | 先生们；绅士们 | A polite or formal way to address a group of men. | Gentlemen, welcome to the meeting. 先生们，欢迎来到会议。| Good evening, gentlemen. 晚上好，先生们。 | 近义词：sirs, men | 反义词：ladies, women | noun

词源:
From Old French gentilhomme, from gentil (see gentle) + homme "man" (from Latin hominem).
