humdrum:/ˈhʌmdrʌm/| adj. n.|单调的，平凡的；单调乏味的事|A tedious, monotonous, and uneventful routine.|例句：He hated the humdrum of his office job. 他讨厌办公室工作的单调乏味。|近义词：monotonous, dull, tedious, boring, mundane|反义词：exciting, interesting, stimulating|词性:形容词/名词

humdrums:/ˈhʌmdrʌmz/| n.|单调乏味的事，平凡的生活|The tedious, monotonous, and uneventful routine of daily life.|例句：She longed to escape the humdrums of suburban life. 她渴望逃离郊区生活的单调乏味。|近义词：monotony, dullness, tedium, boredom, mundanity|反义词：excitement, interest, stimulation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the reduplication of *hum* (onomatopoeic, representing a droning sound) + *drum*.  Originally referring to a monotonous drumming sound, the meaning extended to encompass any kind of tedious routine.
