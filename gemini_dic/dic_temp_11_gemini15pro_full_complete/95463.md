incitants:/ɪnˈsaɪtənts/
n.
煽动者；刺激物；激励因素
Something that incites or stirs to action; a stimulus.
The inflammatory speech acted as an incitant to the riot. 这场煽动性的演讲成了引发骚乱的刺激因素。

The scent of blood is an incitant to the shark. 血腥味会刺激鲨鱼。
Synonyms: stimulus, provocation, impetus, incentive, instigation
Antonyms: deterrent, discouragement, suppression, inhibitor
noun
