inculcation:/ɪnˌkʌlˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |灌输，谆谆教诲 |The inculcation of moral values is an important part of education.  道德价值观的灌输是教育的重要组成部分。| Example: The constant inculcation of company values helps create a unified workforce.  不断灌输公司价值观有助于创建一个团结的员工队伍。| Example:  The inculcation of good manners starts at a young age. 良好礼仪的培养从小就开始。 |Synonyms: instillation, implantation, indoctrination, infusion| Antonyms:  discouragement, dissuasion, neglect| noun
