fables:/ˈfeɪblz/
n.
寓言；传说；神话；谎言
Short stories that teach a moral lesson, often with animals as characters.
Aesop's fables are still read today.  伊索寓言至今仍在阅读。
The old fisherman told fables about enormous fish he had caught. 老渔夫讲述了他捕获的巨型鱼的传说。
synonyms: allegory, parable, myth, legend, story, tale, fabrication, lie, falsehood
antonyms: truth, reality, fact
noun
