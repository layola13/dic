corporation: /ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/ (US) /ˌkɔːpəˈreɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |公司，企业|A large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity and recognized as such in law.|Example:  Microsoft is a large multinational corporation. 微软是一家大型跨国公司。

Example: The corporation announced record profits this year.  这家公司今年宣布了创纪录的利润。

近义词: company, firm, business, enterprise|反义词: None (Corporations don't have a direct opposite, though a small business or sole proprietorship might be considered a contrasting entity.)|词性: noun
