outchide:/ˌaʊtˈtʃaɪd/ | vt. | 胜过，超过，在责骂上超过 | to scold or rebuke more severely than. |  He outchided his mother when she criticized his messy room. 他在母亲批评他的房间乱时，反而责骂了她。 | Example: The teacher outchided the student for disrupting class. 老师严厉斥责了扰乱课堂秩序的学生。

| Synonym: outscold, rebuke, reprimand, berate | Antonym: praise, commend, laud | verb
