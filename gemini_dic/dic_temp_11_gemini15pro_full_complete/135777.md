nonprogrammable:/ˌnɒnˈprəʊɡræməbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈproʊɡræməbl/| adj.|不可编程的，非程序控制的|Not capable of being programmed; fixed or inflexible in function.|This device is nonprogrammable, meaning its functions are preset and cannot be altered by the user.|近义词:fixed, hardwired, inflexible, preset|反义词:programmable, adaptable, flexible|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not," from PIE *ne "not."
programmable: from program + -able.  Program ultimately from Greek programma "a written public notice," from prographein "to write publicly," from pro- "forth" + graphein "to write."
