irrevocablenesses:/ɪˌrɛvəkəbəlˈnɛsɪz/| n. |不可撤销性；不可挽回性|The quality or state of being irrevocable; inability to be revoked or changed.|The irrevocableness of the decision filled him with regret.  这个决定不可挽回，让他充满了悔恨。|近义词：finality, conclusiveness, unchangeableness|反义词: reversibility, changeability|noun


irrevocable:/ɪˈrɛvəkəbl/| adj. |不可撤销的；不可挽回的|Not capable of being revoked or recalled; final.|His words were irrevocable. 他的话是不可撤销的。|近义词：unalterable, irreversible, final|反义词: reversible, changeable|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin irrevocabilis, from in- "not" + revocabilis "able to be recalled," from revocare "to recall," from re- "back" + vocare "to call."
