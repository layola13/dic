counterhypothesis:/ˌkaʊntərhaɪˈpɒθɪsɪs/
n.
反假设，对立假设
A hypothesis that contradicts another hypothesis.
The prosecution presented a counterhypothesis to the defense's claim of self-defense.  控方提出了一个与辩方自卫主张相矛盾的反假设。
Scientists developed a counterhypothesis to explain the unexpected experimental results. 科学家们提出了一个反假设来解释出乎意料的实验结果。

近义词:alternative hypothesis, competing hypothesis
反义词:hypothesis, supporting hypothesis
词性:noun
