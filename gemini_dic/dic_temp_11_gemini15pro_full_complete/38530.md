concisest:/kənˈsaɪsɪst/| adj.|最简洁的，最简明的|Expressing much in few words; brief and to the point.  Giving a lot of information clearly and in a few words.|This is the concisest explanation I can give. 这是我能给出的最简洁的解释了。|synonyms: brief, succinct, terse, compact, pithy|antonyms: lengthy, verbose, prolix|adjective
