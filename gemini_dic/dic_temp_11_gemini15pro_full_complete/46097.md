cydonin:/ˈsaɪdənɪn/ | n. | 榅桲粘胶; 柑橘果胶 | A polysaccharide found in the juice of quinces and other fruits of the genus Cydonia, used as a gelling agent, especially in the food industry.  It is a type of pectin. |  Cydonin is often used to make quince jelly.  榅桲粘胶常用来制作榅桲果冻。| Synonyms: quince gum, quince pectin | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific chemical compound.  One could consider substances that prevent gelling as functional antonyms.) | Noun
