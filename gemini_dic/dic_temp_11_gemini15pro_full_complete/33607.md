cinching:/ˈsɪntʃɪŋ/
verb
中文翻译：系紧，确定，赢得
英文解析：Securing something tightly, especially with a belt or strap; confirming or settling something decisively; winning something decisively.
例句：He cinched the saddle on the horse. 他把马鞍系紧在马上。

例句：Her victory in the final cinched the championship. 她在决赛中的胜利锁定了冠军。

近义词：fastening, securing, tightening, confirming, clinching, winning
反义词：loosening, unfastening, losing
词性：v.

cinching:/ˈsɪntʃɪŋ/
noun
中文翻译：系紧，确定
英文解析：The act of securing something tightly, or the fact of something being secured.
例句：The cinching of the deal brought a sigh of relief.  交易的敲定带来了一阵轻松。

例句：The cinching of the rope around the package made it secure.  用绳子把包裹系紧使其安全。

近义词：fastening, securing, tightening
反义词：loosening
词性：n.
