abearing:/əˈbɛərɪŋ/
n.
方位， bearings (常用复数)
The ship lost its bearings in the fog.  这艘船在雾中失去了它的方位。
The compass gives you your bearings. 指南针能告诉你你的方位。
relation; relevance
关系；关联
These facts have no bearing on the case. 这些事实与该案件无关。
behavior; conduct
举止；行为
His bearing was noble and dignified. 他的举止高贵而庄重。
近义词: location, position, direction, posture, demeanor
反义词: (For location/direction)  None. Bearing doesn't have a direct opposite in terms of location.  It describes a relative position or direction.
