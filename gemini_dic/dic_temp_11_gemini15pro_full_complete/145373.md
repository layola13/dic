overcapability:/ˌoʊvərˌkeɪpəˈbɪləti/
|n.|过度能力，过剩能力|The company's overcapability in production led to a surplus of goods. 该公司的生产能力过剩导致货物过剩。|Excess capacity, surplus capability, overcapacity|Undercapacity, inadequacy, insufficiency|noun
overcapable:/ˌoʊvərˈkeɪpəbəl/
|adj.|能力过剩的，过度胜任的|The team was overcapable for the task, finishing it in record time.  这个团队对于这项任务来说能力过剩，以创纪录的时间完成了它。|Excessively capable, overly competent, overqualified|Underqualified, incompetent, inadequate|adjective
