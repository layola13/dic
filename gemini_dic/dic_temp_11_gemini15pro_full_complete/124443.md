motormouth: /ˈməʊtəmˌmaʊθ/ (UK), /ˈmoʊtərˌmaʊθ/ (US) | n. | 健谈的人；喋喋不休的人 | Someone who talks incessantly. |  He's a real motormouth; he never stops talking. 他真是个话痨，从来没停过嘴。|  She's such a motormouth that it's hard to get a word in edgewise. 她太爱说话了，很难插上一句话。 | 近义词: chatterbox, windbag, blabbermouth | 反义词: reticent person, quiet person | 词性: 名词

Etymology: motor + mouth.  "Motor" referring to something that runs continuously, combined with "mouth" to indicate someone who talks constantly.
