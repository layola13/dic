moussakas:/ˌmuːˈsɑːkəs/ /ˌmuːˈsɑːkɑːs/| n. |慕萨卡，希腊烤茄子|A Greek dish consisting of layers of minced meat, eggplant (aubergine), and often other vegetables, topped with a creamy sauce, typically béchamel.|例句：The moussakas was rich and flavorful.  这道慕萨卡味道浓郁。

例句：We ordered moussakas for dinner. 我们晚餐点了慕萨卡。|近义词：casserole |反义词: None|词性:Noun
