cripus: /ˈkrɪpəs/ | n. | 莎草属植物; 扁穗莎草 |A genus of aquatic or semi-aquatic sedges, commonly known as flatsedges or club-rushes, belonging to the family Cyperaceae. They are characterized by their triangular stems and clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers. | The cripus along the riverbank provided shelter for small fish. 河岸边的莎草为小鱼提供了庇护所。| Synonyms: sedge, bulrush, club-rush | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plant genus) | noun
