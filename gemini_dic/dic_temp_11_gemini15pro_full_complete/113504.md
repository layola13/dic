m'lud:/məˈluːd/ |n.|大人；法官大人（my lord的缩写）|A respectful form of address to a judge, magistrate, or other high-ranking official, especially in a court of law.  It is a contraction of "my lord."|Example: "M'lud, I object to this line of questioning."  大人，我反对这种提问方式。|Example: "M'lud, I plead guilty." 大人，我认罪。|Synonym: Your Honor, Your Lordship|Antonym: (No direct antonym, as it is a form of address)|noun
