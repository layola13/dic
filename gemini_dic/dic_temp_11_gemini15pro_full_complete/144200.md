outsighed:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪd/| v.|叹气胜过；比…更叹息|She outsighed the wind in its sadness. 她在悲伤中比风更叹息。|近义词: outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词: be inferior to|词性:动词

Etymology:  "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "sigh" (to emit a long, deep audible breath expressing sadness, relief, tiredness, or a similar emotion).
