bluegown:/ˈbluːɡaʊn/
noun
蓝袍学者; (尤指苏格兰的) 大学教师
a scholar, especially a university teacher in Scotland, who traditionally wore a blue gown
The bluegown lectured on the history of philosophy.  这位蓝袍学者讲授了哲学史。

近义词:academic, scholar, professor, lecturer
反义词:student, pupil

词性:n.
