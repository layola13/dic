calumniate: /kəˈlʌmnieɪt/
calumniate: v.
calumniate: 诽谤，中伤
calumniate: To make false and malicious statements about someone in order to damage their reputation.
calumniate:  He was calumniated by his political rivals. 他被他的政敌诽谤了。
calumniate:  She was wrongly calumniated as a thief. 她被诬陷为小偷。

calumniate: 近义词: slander, libel, defame, vilify, smear
calumniate: 反义词: praise, commend, laud, extol
calumniate: 词性: 动词
