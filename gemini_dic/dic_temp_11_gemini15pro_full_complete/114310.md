maintainable:/meɪnˈteɪnəbl/ |adj.|可维护的，可维持的|Able to be maintained in good condition; capable of being supported or sustained.| This code is highly maintainable, making it easy for future developers to work with. 这段代码很容易维护，方便以后的开发人员使用。|  The garden is maintainable with minimal effort.  这个花园只需很少的精力就能维护。 | 近义词: sustainable, supportable,serviceable | 反义词: unmaintainable, unsustainable | 词性: adjective

词源:
From maintain + -able.  Ultimately from Latin manu tenere "to hold in the hand".
