mobilized:/ˈməʊbəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/ (US)| adj. |动员的，已动员的|Made ready and available for use or action, especially in a military context.|例句：The army was mobilized for war. 军队已动员备战。|近义词：deployed, activated, marshaled|反义词: demobilized, deactivated|词性:形容词

mobilize:/ˈməʊbəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ (US)| v. |动员，调动|To prepare and organize (troops, resources, etc.) for active service or other use.|例句：The government mobilized its resources to combat the epidemic. 政府调动资源以对抗疫情。|近义词：deploy, activate, marshal|反义词: demobilize, deactivate|词性:动词

Etymology: From French *mobiliser*, from *mobile* ("movable") and *-iser* (suffix forming verbs).  Ultimately from Latin *mobilis* ("movable").
