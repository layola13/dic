copatriot: /koʊˈpeɪtriət/ /kəʊˈpeɪtriət/ | n. | 同胞，同国人 | A person from the same country as another. |  He found several compatriots among the crowd. 他在人群中找到了几个同胞。

My compatriots and I were overjoyed at our team's victory. 我和我的同胞们为我们团队的胜利而欣喜若狂。

| 近义词：countryman, fellow citizen | 反义词：foreigner, alien | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compatriōta, from com- 'together' + patriōta 'fellow countryman', from patria 'native land'.
