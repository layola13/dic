attirer:/ɑːtiʀeɪ/ (UK), /əˈtaɪər/ (US)| v.|吸引，引起...的兴趣，引起注意|To draw, attract, or engage the interest or attention of someone or something. |The bright colours of the flowers attracted the bees.  鲜艳的花朵吸引了蜜蜂。

The advertisement is designed to attract young consumers. 这则广告旨在吸引年轻消费者。|近义词：allure, entice, tempt, charm, fascinate|反义词：repel, deter, discourage|词性:动词

词源：
From Old French *atireir, *ateirer (“to draw to oneself”), from Latin attrahere (“to draw to, attract”), from ad- (“to”) + trahere (“to draw, pull”).

注意事项:
在现代英语中，“attirer” 主要用于其过去分词形式“attiré”，意为“穿着...的”。例如： "She was attirée in a beautiful gown." (她穿着一件漂亮的礼服。)  动词原形 "attirer" 比较少用。
