outbribing: /ˌaʊtˈbraɪbɪŋ/
Verb
中文翻译：行贿超过（别人）; 比…行贿更多
英文解析：Giving or offering a bribe that surpasses another's bribe in value or influence.  The act of bribing someone more than someone else is bribing them.
例句：He succeeded in outbribing his competitors for the lucrative contract. 他成功地比竞争对手出了更高的贿赂，赢得了那份利润丰厚的合同。

例句：The company was accused of outbribing officials to secure the project.  该公司被指控向官员行贿以确保该项目。

近义词：outmaneuvering, outbidding, outsmarting
反义词：being outbribed
词性：Verb (Gerund or present participle)
