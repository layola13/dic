artificiality:/ˌɑːrtɪfɪʃiˈælɪti/
n.
人工；人为；不自然；矫揉造作
the quality of being made or produced by human beings rather than occurring naturally.
The flower arrangements had a certain artificiality.  这些插花带有一种人为的痕迹。
artificialness, unnaturalness, contrivedness, affectation
naturalness, genuineness, authenticity, sincerity
noun
