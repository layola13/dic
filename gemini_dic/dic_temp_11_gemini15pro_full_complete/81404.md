grassiest:/ˈɡræsɪɪst/| adj.|最多草的，草最茂盛的|Covered with grass to the greatest extent; having the most grass. |The grassiest field in the valley was chosen for the picnic. 山谷里草最茂盛的地方被选为野餐地点。|例句：The park boasts the grassiest lawns in the city.  这个公园拥有全市草最茂盛的草坪。|近义词：lushest, greenest, leafiest|反义词：barrenest, barest|词性:形容词
词源: From grass + -y + -est.  来源于 grass（草）+ -y（形容词后缀，表示“充满...的”）+ -est（形容词最高级后缀）。
