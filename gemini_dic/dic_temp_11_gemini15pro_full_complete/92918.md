ident:/ˈaɪdənt/| n. |标识符，身份，同一性|A sequence of characters used to name a variable, function, or other entity in a computer program.  It can also refer to the identity or sameness of something.| Example:  The "ident" command in some operating systems displays the current user's login name.  在某些操作系统中，“ident”命令显示当前用户的登录名。| Synonyms: identifier, name, label | Antonyms: anonymity, obscurity | Noun

/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/| vt. |识别，鉴定，确定|To recognize or establish the identity of someone or something.| Example: The police were able to identify the suspect from the surveillance footage. 警方能够从监控录像中识别嫌疑人。 Example:  Scientists identified the new species of insect. 科学家们鉴定出了这种新的昆虫。 | Synonyms: recognize, distinguish, pinpoint | Antonyms: confuse, mistake, misidentify | Verb
