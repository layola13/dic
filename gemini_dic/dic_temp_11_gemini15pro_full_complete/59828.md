ectocuneiform:/ˌɛktoʊkjuˈniːɪfɔːrm/
noun
外侧楔骨
The ectocuneiform bone is one of the tarsal bones in the foot.  It articulates with the metatarsals and other tarsal bones.
The ectocuneiform bone is located on the lateral side of the foot, articulating with the third metatarsal. 外侧楔骨位于足的外侧，与第三跖骨相连。
