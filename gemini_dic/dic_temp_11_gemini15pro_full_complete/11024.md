arcade:/ɑːrˈkeɪd/ | n. |拱廊，游乐中心，有遮盖的步行街|A covered passageway with arches along one or both sides, often with shops.  A place with coin-operated games. |例句：We spent hours playing games at the arcade. 我们在游戏厅玩了好几个小时。

例句：The shoppers strolled through the elegant arcade. 购物者在优雅的拱廊里漫步。|近义词：gallery, passageway, mall |反义词: open street, road
