outslid:/aʊtˈslɪd/
verb
超过滑行; 滑得比…更远
to slide further than
例句：He outslid his opponent to win the race. 他滑得比对手更远，赢得了比赛。

例句：The car outslid the truck on the icy road. 在结冰的道路上，轿车比卡车滑得更远。

近义词：outdistance, surpass, exceed
反义词：lag behind, trail

词性:v.


词源: out- + slide.  "Out-" prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing.
