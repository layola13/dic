dutifulnesses:/ˈdjuːtɪfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
责任感；尽职；忠实
The quality or state of being dutiful; conscientiousness in performing one's duties.
Her dutifulnesses extended to caring for her elderly parents and volunteering at the local hospital. 她对父母尽孝，并在当地医院做志愿者，展现了她的责任感。
Her dutifulnesses were evident in her meticulous attention to detail. 她的责任感体现在她对细节一丝不苟的关注上。
近义词: responsibility, conscientiousness, diligence, dedication
反义词: negligence, irresponsibility, carelessness, laziness
词性: Noun
