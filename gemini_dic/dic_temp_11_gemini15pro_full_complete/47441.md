deafeningly:/ˈdɛfənɪŋli/ | adv. |震耳欲聋地|  In a way that is extremely loud; so loud as to make it difficult to hear anything else. | The music played deafeningly loud, making conversation impossible.  音乐震耳欲聋，根本无法交谈。|  loudly, extremely loudly, thunderously, resoundingly | softly, quietly | adverb
