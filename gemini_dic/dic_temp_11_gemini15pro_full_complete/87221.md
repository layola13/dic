heremitical:/ˌhɛrɪˈmɪtɪkəl/
adjective
隐居的；遁世的；像隐士的
Characterized by seclusion from society; solitary and contemplative, like a hermit.
He lived a heremitical life in the mountains, far from the hustle and bustle of the city. 他在山上过着隐居的生活，远离城市的喧嚣。
The old cabin had a heremitical feel, miles from any other dwelling.  这座古老的小屋给人一种隐居的感觉，距离其他任何住所都有好几英里。
近义词:reclusive, solitary, secluded, ascetic
反义词:gregarious, sociable, outgoing
词性:adjective

hermit:/ˈhɜːmɪt/
noun
隐士；遁世者
A person living in solitude as a religious discipline.
The hermit lived in a cave, surviving on berries and roots.  这位隐士住在山洞里，靠浆果和树根生存。
Stories abound of hermits living in the wilderness, seeking spiritual enlightenment.  有很多关于隐士在荒野中寻求精神启迪的故事。
近义词:recluse, anchorite, solitary
反义词:socialite, extrovert
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *eremiticus, from Greek erēmītikos "of the desert," from erēmitēs "desert dweller," from erēmia "desert," from erēmos "lonely, desolate."
