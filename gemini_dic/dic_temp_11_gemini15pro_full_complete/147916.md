overregulated:/ˌoʊvərˈreɡjəˌleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度监管的
Subject to excessive regulation; controlled by too many rules or regulations.
The industry is overregulated, stifling innovation and competition.  这个行业监管过度，扼杀了创新和竞争。
overly regulated, excessively regulated
underregulated, deregulated
