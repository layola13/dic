kaolin: /ˈkeɪəlɪn/ | n. | 高岭土，瓷土 | A fine, white clay formed by the weathering of feldspar, used in making porcelain and china, as a filler in paper and textiles, and in medicine. | Kaolin is an important ingredient in porcelain production.  高岭土是瓷器生产中的重要成分。

Kaolin is often used to treat diarrhea. 高岭土常用于治疗腹泻。 | 近义词: china clay, white clay | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
