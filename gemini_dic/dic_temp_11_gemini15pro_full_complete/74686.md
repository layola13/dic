freedstool:/ˈfriːdstuːl/
noun
自由凳; 免税地
A freedstool was historically a seat or place of sanctuary, especially one offering protection from arrest.
The fugitive sought refuge at the freedstool within the church.  逃犯在教堂里的自由凳上寻求庇护。
近义词: sanctuary, refuge, asylum
反义词: prison, jail
词性: n.
