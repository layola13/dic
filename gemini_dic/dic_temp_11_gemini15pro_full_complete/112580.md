loverlike:/ˈlʌvərlaɪk/
adj.
像情人的；多情的；充满爱意的
Resembling or characteristic of a lover; amorous.
She gave him a loverlike look. 她给了他一个充满爱意的眼神。
近义词:amorous, affectionate, loving, passionate
反义词:cold, indifferent, unloving
词性:形容词

loverlike:/ˈlʌvərlaɪk/
n.
像情人一样的人；多情的人
A person who acts like a lover. (Rare usage)
He acted like a loverlike figure towards her.  他对她表现得像个情人一样。(罕用)
近义词:lover, paramour, sweetheart
反义词:enemy, foe, rival
词性:名词 (罕用)
