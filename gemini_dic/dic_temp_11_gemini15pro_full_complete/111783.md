loft:/lɒft/ (UK), /lɔːft/ (US)| n. v.|阁楼，顶楼；高尔夫球的击高球；存放；高飞|A room or space directly under the roof of a house or other building, used for storage or as living space. To hit or propel (a ball or projectile) high into the air. To store in a loft.|例句：
They converted the loft into a bedroom. 他们把阁楼改成了一间卧室。
He lofted the ball over the infield. 他把球高高地打过了内场。


近义词：attic, garret (n.); elevate, raise (v.)|反义词:basement, cellar (n.)|词性:noun, verb
