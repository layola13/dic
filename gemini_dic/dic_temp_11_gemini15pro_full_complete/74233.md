fowler:/ˈfaʊlər/| n.|捕鸟人；设陷阱捕鸟者|A person who hunts or traps wildfowl.|The fowler set his traps before dawn. 捕鸟人在黎明前设置了他的陷阱。|近义词：birdcatcher, hunter, trapper|反义词:game, prey|名词

fowler:/ˈfaʊlər/| n.|猎枪；猎鸟用具|A gun or other device used for hunting wildfowl.|He cleaned his fowler after a successful hunt.  他在一次成功的狩猎后清洁了他的猎枪。|近义词: shotgun, firearm|反义词: None|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fowlere*, from Old English *fugelere* "bird-catcher," equivalent to *fugol* "bird" + *-ere* agent suffix.  The word *fugol* is related to the modern word "fowl."
