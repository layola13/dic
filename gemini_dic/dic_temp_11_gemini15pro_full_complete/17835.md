bedust:/bɪˈdʌst/
verb
撒满灰尘，使蒙上灰尘
To cover with dust.
The old furniture was bedusted and forgotten in the attic.  阁楼里的旧家具积满了灰尘，被人遗忘了。
The car was bedusted after driving down the dirt road.  汽车在土路上行驶后，蒙上了一层灰尘。
近义词: dust, powder, cover with dust
反义词: clean, cleanse, wipe
词性: verb

词源:
From be- + dust, from Old English *dūst, akin to Old High German dust “dust,” from Proto-Germanic *dunstaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰew- “to rise, fly up; smoke, vapor, dust.”
