nontotalitarian:/ˌnɒnˌtəʊˌtælɪˈtɛərɪən/ | adj. | 非极权主义的 | Not totalitarian; relating to a government that does not attempt to control all aspects of its citizens' lives. |  Many Western democracies are considered nontotalitarian states. 许多西方民主国家被认为是非极权主义国家。|  The country transitioned from a totalitarian regime to a nontotalitarian one.  这个国家从极权主义政权过渡到非极权主义政权。| synonyms: democratic, liberal, pluralistic | antonyms: totalitarian, authoritarian, despotic | adjective
