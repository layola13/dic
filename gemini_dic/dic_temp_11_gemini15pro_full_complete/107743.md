laparoscopy:/ˌlæpəˈrɒskəpi/ /ˌlæpəˈrɑːskəpi/| n. |腹腔镜检查|A surgical procedure in which a small incision is made in the abdomen and a laparoscope (a thin, lighted tube) is inserted to view the abdominal organs or to perform minor surgery.|The doctor recommended a laparoscopy to investigate the cause of her abdominal pain. 医生建议做腹腔镜检查以查明她腹痛的原因。

Laparoscopy is a less invasive alternative to open surgery. 腹腔镜手术是开腹手术的一种微创替代方案。|近义词:minimally invasive surgery|反义词:open surgery|词性:noun
