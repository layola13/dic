asthmas:/ˈæzməz/| n.|哮喘（复数）|A chronic respiratory condition marked by spasms in the bronchi of the lungs, causing difficulty in breathing. It is usually connected with allergic reaction or other forms of hypersensitivity.|She suffers from severe asthmas. 她患有严重的哮喘。|Synonym: respiratory disease, bronchospasm|Antonym: healthy lungs|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ázma, "panting, short-drawn breath," related to áein, "to breathe, blow."
