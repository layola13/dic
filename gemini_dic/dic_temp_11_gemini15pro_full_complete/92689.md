icebound:/ˈaɪsbaʊnd/
adjective
冰封的，被冰困住的
Covered and immobilized by ice; surrounded by ice.
The ship was icebound in the Arctic Ocean.  这艘船被困在北冰洋的冰层中。
The harbor was icebound for weeks. 港口被冰封了数周。
近义词：frozen, ice-covered, ice-locked
反义词：thawed, ice-free
词性：adjective
词源：ice + bound (past participle of bind) - ice from Proto-Germanic *isaz "ice"; bind from Proto-Germanic *bindaną "to tie, bind"
