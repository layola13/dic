beltane: /ˈbɛl.teɪn/ | n. |五朔节|A pagan May Day festival, celebrated in Celtic countries. It marks the beginning of summer.|例句：Beltane is a time of celebration and fertility rituals. 五朔节是庆祝和生育仪式的时刻。

例句：The Beltane fire festival is a popular event in Edinburgh. 爱丁堡的五朔节火祭是一个很受欢迎的活动。|近义词：May Day, Walpurgis Night|反义词:Samhain|词性:名词
