cheetah:/ˈtʃiːtə/| n. |猎豹|A large, spotted cat known for its speed, native to Africa and parts of Iran.|例句：The cheetah is the fastest land animal. 猎豹是陆地上跑得最快的动物。

例句：A cheetah can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just a few seconds.  一只猎豹可以在几秒钟内从0加速到60英里/小时。|近义词：cat, feline|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "slow animal")|词性:Noun


Etymology: From Hindi चीता (cītā), from Sanskrit चित्रक (citraka), meaning "spotted."
