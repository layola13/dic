indecomposable:/ˌɪndɪkəmˈpoʊzəbl/ | adj. | 不可分解的，不可分割的 | Not able to be separated or broken down into simpler parts or components. | This protein is an indecomposable unit.  这种蛋白质是一个不可分解的单元。 | The family's bond is indecomposable.  这个家庭的纽带是不可分割的。 | 近义词: indivisible, inseparable, unbreakable | 反义词: decomposable, divisible, separable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From in- "not" + decomposable, from decompose + -able.  Ultimately from Latin de- "down" + componere "put together," from com- "together" + ponere "to put, place."
