bilbies:/ˈbɪlbiːz/
n.
兔耳袋狸（复数）
A small, nocturnal marsupial native to Australia, with long ears, a pointed snout, and a long, crested tail.  They are also known as rabbit-eared bandicoots.
Bilbies are endangered in the wild.  兔耳袋狸在野外濒临灭绝。
近义词:bandicoots
反义词:(None -  as it's a specific animal, there's no direct opposite)
