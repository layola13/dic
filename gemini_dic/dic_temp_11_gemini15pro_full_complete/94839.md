imputativeness:/ɪmˈpjuːtətɪvnəs/| n. |归罪性，归咎性|The quality or state of being imputative; the act of imputing or ascribing something, especially blame or fault.  The tendency to attribute or ascribe a quality, especially a fault or blame, to a person or thing.|His imputativeness led him to blame others for his own mistakes. 他的归罪性导致他把自己的错误归咎于他人。|Near Synonyms: attribution, ascription, imputation|Near Antonyms: exculpation, exoneration|noun
