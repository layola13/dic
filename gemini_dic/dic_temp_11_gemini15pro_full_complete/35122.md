cloudiest:/ˈklaʊdiɪst/| adj.|最多云的|Superlative form of cloudy: most cloudy.  Covered with the most clouds; having the least amount of sunshine.|例句：It was the cloudiest day of the entire trip. 这是整个旅程中最多云的一天。

例句：November is typically the cloudiest month of the year here. 十一月通常是这里一年中最多云的月份。|近义词：overcast, gloomy, gray|反义词：sunniest, clearest, brightest|词性：形容词
