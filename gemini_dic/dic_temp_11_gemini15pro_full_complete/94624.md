impresa:/ɪmˈpreːzə/ /ɪmˈpriːsə/

壮举; 功绩; 事业; 企业

He was involved in a daring impresa to climb Mount Everest. 他参与了一项攀登珠穆朗玛峰的大胆壮举。
This family-owned impresa has been successful for generations. 这家家族企业已经成功经营了几代。
近义词:feat, achievement, exploit, venture, undertaking, enterprise, company, firm, business
反义词:failure, inaction
词性:n.
