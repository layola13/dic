login:/ˈlɒɡɪn/ (UK) /ˈlɑːɡɪn/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|登录，登入|The act of gaining access to a computer system or online account by entering a username and password.  It can also refer to the username itself.|Example: I couldn't remember my login details. 我不记得我的登录信息了。

|Example: You need a valid login to access this website. 你需要一个有效的登录名才能访问这个网站。|Synonyms: sign in, sign on, access|Antonyms: log out, sign out, sign off|noun, verb
