outteases:/ˌaʊtˈtiːzɪz/
verb
胜过，在...方面超过(尤指戏弄)
outdo in teasing; surpass in cleverness or wit, especially in teasing.
He outteased his siblings with his witty remarks. 他用机智的言辞胜过了他的兄弟姐妹们。
Synonyms: outsmart, outwit, surpass
Antonyms: lose to, be outdone by
词性: 动词
