fax:/fæks/
fax:/fæks/
noun|传真；传真机|A system for transmitting graphic matter (text or images) over telephone lines.|Please send me the document by fax. 请通过传真机把文件发给我。|
近义词: facsimile
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "mail" or "email" as alternative communication methods)
词性: noun


fax:/fæks/
verb|传真|To send something by fax.|I'll fax you the contract tomorrow. 我明天把合同传真给你。|
近义词: transmit, telefax
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: verb


词源: Clipped from facsimile.  Facsimile comes from the Latin "fac simile" meaning "make similar".
