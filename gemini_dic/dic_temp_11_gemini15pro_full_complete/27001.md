calculatedly:/ˈkælkjuleɪtlɪ/| adv.|故意地，蓄意地，精打细算地|Done in a deliberate and often cunning way, especially to achieve a particular effect.|She calculatedly ignored his pleas for help. 她故意不理睬他寻求帮助的请求。|Example: He calculatedly flattered his boss to get a promotion. 他刻意奉承老板以获得晋升。|Synonyms: deliberately, intentionally, purposely, knowingly, designedly|Antonyms: unintentionally, accidentally, unknowingly, randomly|Word type: adverb
