benesh:/ˈbɛnɛʃ/| n.| 贝奈什动作符号系统；贝奈什记谱法|A system of movement notation developed by Rudolf Benesh for recording dance, particularly ballet.  It uses abstract symbols to represent positions and movements of the body.|The ballet master meticulously recorded the choreography in Benesh notation.|近义词: Benesh movement notation, dance notation|反义词: None|词性: noun
