gneiss:/naɪs/| n.|片麻岩|A common high-grade metamorphic rock. It is formed by high-temperature and high-pressure metamorphic processes acting on formations composed of igneous or sedimentary rocks.|The gneiss formations in the Swiss Alps are spectacular. 瑞士阿尔卑斯山的片麻岩地层非常壮观。|Synonyms: metamorphic rock, schist|Antonyms: igneous rock, sedimentary rock|noun
