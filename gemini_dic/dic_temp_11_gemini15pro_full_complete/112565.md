lovelock: /ˈlʌvˌlɒk/ (US) /ˈlʌvˌlɒk/ (UK) | n. | 爱情锁，定情锁 | A padlock fastened to a bridge, fence, or similar public fixture by sweethearts to symbolize their love. |  Example: They attached a lovelock to the Pont des Arts bridge in Paris. 他们在巴黎艺术桥上挂了一把爱情锁。

Example: The city had to remove thousands of lovelocks because of the weight on the bridge. 由于桥梁的承重问题，该市不得不拆除数千把爱情锁。

| 近义词：padlock, love token | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
