labourless: /ˈleɪbələs/ | adj. | 无需劳动的；不费力的 | Not requiring much physical effort; involving little or no hard work. |  The company offers a labourless service for furniture assembly.  该公司提供无需劳动的家具组装服务。| The invention promised a labourless future. 这项发明预示着一个无需劳作的未来。| 近义词: effortless, easy, simple | 反义词: laborious, strenuous, arduous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "labour" (from Old French "labour," ultimately from Latin "labor" meaning "work, toil, hardship") + "-less" (suffix indicating absence or lack).
