hoarinesses:/ˈhɔːrɪnɪsɪz/
n.
灰白；白霜；古老
The state of being hoary, typically with age.  A covering of white hair or a frost-like whiteness.  The appearance of great age.
The ancient stones were covered in hoarinesses, evidence of centuries of weathering.  这些古老的石头上覆盖着一层白霜，证明了几个世纪的风化。
Synonyms: grayness, whiteness, antiquity, agedness
Antonyms: youthfulness, newness
