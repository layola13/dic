grinding:/ˈɡraɪndɪŋ/| adj. n.|磨的，磨削的；枯燥乏味的；刺耳的；研磨，磨碎| characterized by or causing friction; tedious; harsh or grating; the act of reducing something to small particles or powder |The grinding poverty of the region is heartbreaking.  该地区的极端贫困令人心碎。|近义词：milling, crushing, pulverizing, grating, tedious, monotonous|反义词：soothing, pleasant, enjoyable|词性: adjective, noun
grinding:/ˈɡraɪndɪŋ/| v.|磨碎；磨快；折磨；压榨|reduce (something) to small particles or powder by crushing it; sharpen (a blade); oppress or cause to suffer; work (someone) relentlessly|He was grinding coffee beans early in the morning. 他一大早就开始磨咖啡豆了。|近义词：milling, crushing, pulverizing, grating, oppressing|反义词：soothing, relieving|词性: verb

词源: From Old English *grindan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch grinden and German greinden.
