freakier:/ˈfriːkiər/| adj.|更古怪的，更反常的|Comparative form of freaky: more freaky.  Means stranger or more unusual than usual, often in a way that is unsettling or disturbing.|例句：The party got freakier as the night went on.  派对随着夜幕降临变得越来越古怪。|例句：His new art is even freakier than his last exhibition. 他的新艺术作品比上次展览的还要古怪。|近义词：stranger, weirder, odder, more bizarre|反义词：normaler, more conventional, more ordinary|词性:形容词

词源:
From *freaky* + *-er* (comparative suffix).  *Freaky* originates from *freak* (n.), which has uncertain origins, possibly related to a dialectal word meaning "capricious, whimsical."
