oriya: /ɔːˈriːə/ /ɔːˈriːjə/ | n. | 奥里亚语；奥里亚人 | Oriya is an Indo-Aryan language spoken primarily in the Indian state of Odisha.  奥里亚语是一种印欧语系的语言，主要在印度的奥迪沙邦使用。|  Many people in Odisha speak Oriya. 许多奥迪沙邦的人说奥里亚语。| The Oriya people are known for their rich cultural heritage. 奥里亚人以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。 | synonyms: Odia | antonyms: N/A | noun
