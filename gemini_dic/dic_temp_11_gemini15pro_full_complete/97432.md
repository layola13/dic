infuriated:/ɪnˈfjʊərieɪtɪd/| adj.|被激怒的，暴怒的| feeling or showing extreme anger |例句：He was infuriated by the constant interruptions. 他被不断的打断激怒了。|例句：She was absolutely infuriated when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后勃然大怒。|近义词：enraged, furious, livid, outraged|反义词：calm, pleased, content|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *infuriatus, past participle of infuriare (“to enrage”), from in- (“in, into”) + furia (“fury, rage”).
