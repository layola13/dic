desensitization:/diːˌsɛnsɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|脱敏，减敏|The process of reducing sensitivity or reactivity to a stimulus, especially an allergen or emotional trigger.|Exposure therapy is a common method for desensitization to phobias. 暴露疗法是脱敏恐惧症的常用方法。|
近义词:habituation, acclimatization, adaptation
反义词:sensitization
词性:noun

desensitize:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/
v.|使脱敏，使减敏|To make someone less sensitive or reactive to something.|The therapist worked to desensitize the patient to the trauma they experienced. 治疗师努力使病人对他们经历的创伤脱敏。|
近义词:inure, harden, dull
反义词:sensitize
词性:verb

词源:de- (Latin prefix meaning "down from, away from") + sense + -ize (suffix forming verbs) + -ation (suffix forming nouns)
