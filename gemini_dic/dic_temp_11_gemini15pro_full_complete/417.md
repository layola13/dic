abjurer:/əˈbʒʊərər/| n.|放弃者，发誓放弃者|A person who formally renounces or rejects a belief, cause, or claim.|He was an abjurer of his former radical beliefs. 他放弃了他以前的激进信仰。|近义词：renouncer, repudiator, retractor|反义词：adherent, supporter, advocate|noun
