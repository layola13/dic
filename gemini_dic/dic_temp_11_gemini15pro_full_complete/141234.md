oozed:/uːzd/
verb
渗出，慢慢流出
(of a thick liquid) pass or flow slowly; (of a quality) be gradually and steadily revealed or emitted.
The wound oozed blood. 伤口渗出了血。
Thick, black oil oozed from the engine. 浓稠的黑色油从引擎中渗出。
近义词: seep, leak, exude, discharge
反义词: gush, spurt, pour
