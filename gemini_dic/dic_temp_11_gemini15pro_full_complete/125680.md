mundanely:/mʌnˈdeɪnli/
adverb|平凡地；世俗地；单调地|In a mundane way; ordinarily.  Referring to things that are ordinary and not particularly interesting or exciting.|She described her job mundanely, focusing on the routine tasks.|Synonyms: ordinarily, routinely, commonly, dully, plainly|Antonyms: excitingly, extraordinarily, remarkably, unusually|词性: 副词
