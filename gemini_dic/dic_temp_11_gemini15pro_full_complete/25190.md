buddhisms: /ˈbʊdɪzəmz/
n.
佛教（的各种形式）
Various forms of the Buddhist religion.
Buddhisms, in their various forms, share core beliefs but differ in practices.  佛教在其各种形式中，有着共同的核心信仰，但在实践上有所不同。
Many buddhisms emphasize meditation and mindfulness. 许多佛教强调冥想和正念。
近义词: Buddhist schools, Buddhist traditions
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider other religions like Christianity, Islam, etc.)
