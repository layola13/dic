bratsche:/ˈbrɑːtʃə/ (US), /ˈbrɑːtʃə/ (UK)| n.|中提琴；中提琴演奏者|A stringed instrument of the violin family, tuned a fifth lower than the violin. It can also refer to a person who plays the viola.|例句：The bratsche added a rich, mellow tone to the orchestra. 中提琴为管弦乐队增添了丰富而圆润的音调。

例句：He is a skilled bratsche player. 他是一位技艺娴熟的中提琴演奏者。|近义词：viola|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Bratsche*, short for *Viola da braccio* (Italian, literally "arm viol").
