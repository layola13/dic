honolulu:/ˌhɑːnəˈluːluː/ /ˌhɒnəˈluːluː/
n.
檀香山; 火奴鲁鲁
Capital and largest city of Hawaii, located on the island of Oahu. It is a major tourist destination known for its beaches, Waikiki being the most famous. Honolulu is also a significant port and a hub for transportation in the Pacific.
例句：Honolulu is a popular tourist destination. 檀香山是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。

例句：We spent our honeymoon in Honolulu. 我们在檀香山度蜜月。

近义词：Waikiki (specifically referring to the beach area), Oahu (the island where Honolulu is located)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a place name)
词性: 名词
