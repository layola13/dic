clinkstone:/ˈklɪŋkˌstoʊn/| n. |响岩，火山岩的一种|A hard, gray, fine-grained volcanic rock that makes a ringing sound when struck.  It is a type of phonolite.|The geologist identified the sample as clinkstone due to its characteristic ringing sound.  地质学家根据其特有的响声将样品鉴定为响岩。|Synonyms: phonolite, basalt, trap rock|Antonyms: None|noun
