instil:/ɪnˈstɪl/ /ɪnˈstɪl/| vt.|灌输，逐渐 instill(美式拼写)|To gradually but firmly establish an idea, attitude, or feeling in a person's mind.|例句：The coach instilled a sense of discipline in his players. 教练给他的队员们灌输了纪律意识。|例句：Parents try to instill good values in their children. 父母努力给孩子们灌输良好的价值观。|近义词：implant, infuse, inculcate|反义词：eradicate, uproot|词性:动词

instill:/ɪnˈstɪl/ /ɪnˈstɪl/| vt.|慢慢滴入 instill(美式拼写)|To introduce (something) gradually.|例句：The doctor instilled the medicine into the patient's ear. 医生将药物慢慢滴入病人的耳朵里。|例句：She instilled a few drops of oil into the lock. 她往锁里滴了几滴油。|近义词：inject, introduce|反义词：extract, remove|词性:动词
