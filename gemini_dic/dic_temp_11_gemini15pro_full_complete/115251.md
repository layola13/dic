manipulated:/məˈnɪpjuleɪtɪd/
adjective|被操纵的，被控制的|influenced or controlled by someone or something else often in a skillful or devious way|例句：The manipulated data gave a false impression of the company's performance. 被操纵的数据给公司业绩带来了错误的印象。

例句：He felt manipulated by his boss into accepting the new position. 他感觉被老板操纵接受了新职位。


synonym:controlled, influenced, exploited, used
antonym:independent, autonomous, free, liberated
词性:adjective
