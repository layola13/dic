improportionable:/ˌɪmprəˈpɔːrʃənəbl/
adjective
不成比例的，不相称的
not in proper proportion; too large or too small in comparison with something else.
The punishment was improportionable to the crime.  对这项罪行来说，惩罚太重了。
The kitchen is improportionably large for such a small house.  对于这么小的房子来说，厨房太大了。
近义词:disproportionate, excessive, inordinate, unreasonable
反义词:proportionate, appropriate, reasonable, commensurate
词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from Old French improportionable or late Latin improportionabilis, from in- ‘not’ + proportionabilis (see proportionable).
