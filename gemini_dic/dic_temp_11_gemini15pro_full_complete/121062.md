misconsecrated:/ˌmɪskɒnˈsɪkreɪtɪd/
adjective
亵渎的，未正确神圣化的
Wrongly or improperly consecrated.
The artifact was considered misconsecrated after the unorthodox ceremony.  这件神器在非正统的仪式后被认为是亵渎的。
The ground, having been used for unsavory purposes, was deemed misconsecrated. 这块土地由于被用于不光彩的目的，被认为是未被正确神圣化的。
Near synonyms: desecrated, profaned, unconsecrated
Antonyms: consecrated, hallowed, sanctified
词性: adjective
