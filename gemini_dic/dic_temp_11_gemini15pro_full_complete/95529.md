inclusive:/ɪnˈkluːsɪv/ | adj. | 包括的，包含的；包容性的 | Including everything or everyone; containing the stated limit or extremes. | The price is inclusive of tax.  价格包含税费。

The conference is inclusive of all meals and accommodation.  会议费用包含所有餐食和住宿。| 近义词: comprehensive, all-embracing, encompassing | 反义词: exclusive, excluding | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *includere*, from in- 'in, into' + claudere 'to shut'.
