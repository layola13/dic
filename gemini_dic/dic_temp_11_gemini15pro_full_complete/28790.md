carolingian:/ˌkærəˈlɪndʒiən/| adj. n.|加洛林王朝的；加洛林王朝的人|Relating to the Frankish dynasty founded by Charlemagne.|The Carolingian Renaissance saw a flourishing of art and literature. 加洛林文艺复兴时期见证了艺术和文学的繁荣。| Charlemagne's empire was the largest since the Roman Empire. 查理曼大帝的帝国是罗马帝国以来最大的帝国。|近义词：Frankish, medieval|反义词：modern|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *Carolingianus, from Carolus (Latinized form of Charlemagne's name, Karl) + -ing- + -ianus.  Ultimately from the Germanic name Karl, meaning "free man."
