munificently:/mjʊˈnɪfɪsəntli/| adv.|慷慨地，丰厚地|In a generous or lavish manner; giving or bestowing liberally.|He munificently donated to the charity. 他慷慨地向慈善机构捐款。|Synonyms: generously, lavishly, bountifully, liberally, charitably|Antonyms: stingily, meanly, meagerly, sparingly, parsimoniously|詞性:副詞

Etymology: From Latin *munificus* "generous, liberal, bountiful,"  from *munus* "gift, service, duty" + *-ficus* "making, doing."
