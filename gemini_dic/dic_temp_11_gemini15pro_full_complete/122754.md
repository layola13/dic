moisturise:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪz/ (US)| v.|滋润，保湿|To make something less dry, especially skin, by putting a cream or lotion on it.|I always moisturise my skin after showering. 我洗澡后总是会给皮肤保湿。|Synonyms: hydrate, condition, soften|Antonyms: dry, dehydrate|verb

moisturizer:/ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪzər/ (US) /ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪzə/ (UK)| n.|保湿霜，润肤霜|A cream or lotion that you put on your skin to make it less dry.|This moisturizer leaves my skin feeling soft and smooth. 这款保湿霜让我的肌肤感觉柔软光滑。|Synonyms: lotion, cream, balm|Antonyms: desiccant, drying agent|noun
