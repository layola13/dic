overhasten:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪsən/| v. |加速；操之过急；过分催促|To hasten excessively; to accelerate unduly. |He overhastened the project and ended up making costly mistakes. 他为了赶进度，结果犯了代价高昂的错误。

The company overhastened the product launch and it was riddled with bugs.  公司过分催促产品发布，结果产品漏洞百出。|近义词：rush, precipitate, accelerate |反义词:delay, postpone, decelerate |词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + hasten. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Hasten" comes from the Middle English "hasten," from the Old Norse "hasta," meaning "to hurry."
