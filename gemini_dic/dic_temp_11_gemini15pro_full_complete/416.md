abjurement:/əˈdʒʊrmənt/
n.|放弃，发誓放弃;正式放弃(权利、信仰等)|A formal renunciation or abandonment, especially of a belief or claim.|He signed an abjurement of his claim to the throne. 他签署了放弃王位继承权的声明。|近义词：renunciation, abandonment, repudiation, disclaimer|反义词：affirmation, assertion, avowal|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *abjurement*, from Late Latin *abjuramentum*, from Latin *abjurare* "to deny on oath," from *ab-* "away" + *jurare* "to swear."
