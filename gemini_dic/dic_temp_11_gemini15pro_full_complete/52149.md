diaphanoscope:/daɪˈæfənəskoʊp/
n.
幻灯机；透视镜
An apparatus for projecting images of transparent objects or photographs onto a screen.
The lecturer used a diaphanoscope to show images of microscopic organisms. 演讲者使用幻灯机展示微生物的图像。
Synonyms: projector, magic lantern, slide projector
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific device)
Noun
