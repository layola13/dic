glaze:/ɡleɪz/| n. vt. vi.|釉，光滑面；呆滞的目光；上釉，装玻璃；变呆滞|A thin, smooth, shiny coating on a surface, often used on pottery or food.  To coat with a glaze. To become glassy or dull.|The baker glazed the donuts with a sugar coating. 面包师在甜甜圈上涂了一层糖衣。|近义词：polish, varnish, coat, gloss|反义词:dull, roughen|n./v.

glaze:/ɡleɪz/| v. |变得呆滞无神，心不在焉|To lose expression and become dull or glassy, as of the eyes.|His eyes glazed over during the boring lecture.  在枯燥的讲座中，他的眼神变得呆滞无神。|近义词：stare blankly, zone out|反义词:focus, concentrate|v.
