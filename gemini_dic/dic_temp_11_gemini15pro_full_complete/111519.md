loanwords:/ˈloʊnˌwɜːrdz/ | n. |外来词| Words adopted from one language and incorporated into another language without translation. |例句：Many English loanwords come from French. 许多英语外来词来自法语。

例句：Japanese has adopted many loanwords from English.  日语吸收了很多英语外来词。|近义词：borrowed words, adopted words|反义词: native words, indigenous words | 词性: noun
