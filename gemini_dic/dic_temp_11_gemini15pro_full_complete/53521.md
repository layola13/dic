disastrous:/dɪˈzæstrəs/| adj.|灾难性的；极糟糕的|Causing great damage or harm; extremely bad or unsuccessful.|The earthquake had disastrous consequences for the region.  地震给该地区带来了灾难性的后果。|例句：The flood had a disastrous effect on the crops. 洪水对庄稼造成了灾难性的影响。|近义词：calamitous, catastrophic, devastating, ruinous|反义词：fortunate, beneficial, advantageous|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *disastrosus, from Latin dis- ‘apart, away’ + astrum ‘star’.  The original sense was ‘ill-starred’, referring to the belief that disasters were caused by an unfavorable position of the stars.
