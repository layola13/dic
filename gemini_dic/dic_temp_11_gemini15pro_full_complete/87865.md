hides:/haɪdz/ | v. (third-person singular simple present hides, present participle hiding, simple past hid, past participle hidden) | 隐藏，躲藏 | to put or keep something or someone in a place where they cannot be seen or found |  The cat hides under the bed. 猫躲在床底下。

The thief hid the stolen jewels in a secret compartment. 小偷把偷来的珠宝藏在一个秘密隔间里。

近义词: conceal, cover, secrete, stash | 反义词: reveal, expose, show | 词性: 动词

hides:/haɪdz/ | n. (plural) | （动物的）皮 | the skin of an animal, especially a large animal, when it has been removed to be used for leather |  The company imports hides and skins from various countries. 这家公司从各国进口兽皮。

They used hides to make tents and clothing. 他们用兽皮制作帐篷和衣服。


近义词: skins, pelts | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
