citrination:/ˌsɪtrɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|柠檬酸化，柠檬黄化|The process of making something yellow, especially by treating it with citric acid.  This can refer to the chemical process of turning a substance yellow, or the artistic technique of using a yellow glaze or pigment derived from citric acid.|The citrination of the ceramic gave it a beautiful, sunny hue.|近义词:yellowing,  coloration|反义词: bleaching, decolorization|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *citrinus* (“citron-colored”), from *citrus* (“citron tree”).
