acidimetry:/ˌæsɪˈdɪmətri/ | n. | 酸量滴定法 | The determination of the amount of acid present in a solution by titration with a standard alkali. |  Example 1: Acidimetry is a common technique used in analytical chemistry. 酸量滴定法是分析化学中常用的技术。

Example 2: The chemist performed acidimetry to determine the concentration of the acid. 化学家进行了酸量滴定法以确定酸的浓度。 | 近义词: acid-base titration | 反义词: alkalimetry | noun
