decanting:/dɪˈkæntɪŋ/
n.
倾析；滗析
The process of gently pouring a liquid from one container to another, leaving sediment or solids behind.
The wine benefits from decanting an hour before serving.  这葡萄酒在饮用前一小时滗析一下会更好。
近义词：pouring, separating, siphoning
反义词：mixing, combining
