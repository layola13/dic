fatiguing:/fəˈtiːɡɪŋ/| adj.|令人疲倦的，使人疲劳的|Causing tiredness or exhaustion.|The hike up the mountain was fatiguing.|近义词：tiring, exhausting, draining, wearying|反义词：refreshing, invigorating, energizing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French fatigant, present participle of fatiguer (“to tire”), from Late Latin fatigare (“to tire out”), from Latin fatīgo (“I weary”).
