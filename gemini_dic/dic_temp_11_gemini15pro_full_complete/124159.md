mortpay:/ˈmɔːrtpeɪ/| n.|按揭付款，按揭供款|A mortpay is a regular payment made towards a mortgage loan, typically comprising both principal and interest.|例句：The mortpay is due on the first of each month. 每月的第一天都要支付按揭供款。

例句：Rising interest rates have increased our monthly mortpay.  利率上升增加了我们每月的按揭供款。|近义词：mortgage payment, mortgage installment|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "rent" if contrasting owning vs. renting)|词性:noun
