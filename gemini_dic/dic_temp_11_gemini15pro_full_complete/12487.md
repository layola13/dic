asperate:/ˈæspəreɪt/ | adj. v. |送气的，粗糙的；使送气|Characterized by the audible breath that accompanies certain speech sounds, like the English 'h'; rough or harsh in sound.| The letter 'h' represents an aspirate sound. 字母“h”代表送气音。|近义词：breathy, rough, harsh|反义词：smooth, soft|词性：形容词，动词

asperate:/ˈæspəreɪt/ | adj. v. |送气的，粗糙的；使送气|To pronounce (a consonant) with an exhalation of breath; to make rough or harsh in sound.|He aspirated the 'p' in 'park'. 他在“park”中送气发“p”音。|近义词：breathe, roughen|反义词：smooth, soften|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin asperātus, past participle of asperāre "to make rough," from asper "rough."
