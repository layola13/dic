meagrely:/ˈmiːɡərli/| adv.|少量地，贫乏地，不足地|In a small quantity; sparsely.  Lacking in richness, fullness, or strength.|He ate meagrely, only consuming a small piece of bread. 他吃得很少，只吃了一小块面包。|Synonyms: sparsely, sparingly, skimpily, inadequately|Antonyms: abundantly, copiously, lavishly, generously|词性:副词

meagre:/ˈmiːɡər/| adj.|瘦弱的，贫乏的，不足的|Lacking in quantity or quality. Thin or emaciated.|The meagre rations were not enough to sustain the workers.  微薄的口粮不足以维持工人的生存。|Synonyms: scanty, sparse, insufficient, inadequate|Antonyms: abundant, plentiful, ample, sufficient|词性:形容词
