interrupter:/ˌɪntəˈrʌptər/| n.|打断者，干扰者|Someone or something that interrupts.  A person or thing that causes a break in the continuity of an activity or process.|The loud music was a constant interrupter to my concentration.  喧闹的音乐不断干扰我的注意力。|Synonyms: disrupter, obstructor, intruder|Antonyms: facilitator, enabler|noun

interrupt:/ˌɪntəˈrʌpt/| v.|打断，打扰|To stop the continuous progress of (an activity or process).|I hate to interrupt, but there's a phone call for you.  我很抱歉打断你，但是有你的电话。|Synonyms: disturb, disrupt, intrude|Antonyms: assist, facilitate|verb
