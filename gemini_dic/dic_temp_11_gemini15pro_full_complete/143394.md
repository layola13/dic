outfishes:/ˌaʊtˈfɪʃɪz/
verb
胜过…的捕鱼量; 比…捕到更多鱼
catches more fish than
例句：He outfishes everyone on the lake. 他比湖上任何人都捕到更多鱼。
例句：She consistently outfishes her competitors in the tournament.  她在比赛中一直比她的竞争对手捕到更多鱼。
近义词：outperforms, surpasses, beats
反义词：is outfished by, loses to
词性：v.
