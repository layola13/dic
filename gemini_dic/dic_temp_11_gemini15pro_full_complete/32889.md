chondrogenesis:/ˌkɒndrəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌkɑːndrəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
软骨形成，软骨发生
The process by which cartilage is formed.
例句：Chondrogenesis is essential for skeletal development. 软骨形成对于骨骼发育至关重要。

例句：Researchers are studying the factors that regulate chondrogenesis. 研究人员正在研究调节软骨形成的因素。

近义词：cartilage formation, cartilage development
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) cartilage degradation, cartilage resorption
词性: noun
