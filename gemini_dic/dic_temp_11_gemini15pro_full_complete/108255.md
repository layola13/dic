launcher:/ˈlɔːntʃər (UK), /ˈlɑːntʃər (US)| n.|发射器，启动器，发射装置|A device used to launch something, such as a rocket, missile, or spacecraft.  A program that starts other programs on a computer.|例句：The rocket launcher was positioned and ready to fire. 火箭发射器已就位，准备发射。

例句：I clicked the icon on my desktop to open the application launcher. 我点击桌面上的图标打开了应用程序启动器。|近义词：starter, initiator, trigger, catapult|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
