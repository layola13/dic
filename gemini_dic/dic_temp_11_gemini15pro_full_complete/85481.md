hassle:/ˈhæsəl/ (US), /ˈhæsəl/ (UK)| n. v.|麻烦，困难;  烦扰，纠缠|A hassle is a situation that is annoying because it involves doing something difficult or complicated that needs a lot of effort.|例句：It was such a hassle getting my visa.  我的签证真是麻烦。|近义词：trouble, difficulty, bother, inconvenience, annoyance|反义词:ease, convenience, simplicity|词性:名词, 动词

hassle:/ˈhæsəl/ (US), /ˈhæsəl/ (UK)| v.|烦扰，纠缠|To hassle someone means to annoy them repeatedly, especially by asking them to do something.|例句：Shopkeepers complained of being hassled by beggars. 店主抱怨被乞丐纠缠。|近义词：bother, annoy, pester, harass|反义词:leave alone, assist|词性:动词
