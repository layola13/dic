anesthetic:/ˌænɪsˈθɛtɪk/ (US), /ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|麻醉的；麻醉剂|A substance that causes loss of feeling or awareness.|The surgery was performed under general anesthetic. 手术是在全身麻醉下进行的。|近义词：narcotic, opiate|反义词: stimulant|词性: adjective, noun

anesthesia:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒə/ (US), /ˌænəsˈθiːziə/ (UK)| n.|麻醉|Loss of feeling or awareness, especially of pain, induced by drugs.|The patient was given local anesthesia for the procedure. 病人接受了局部麻醉以进行手术。|近义词：narcosis|反义词: - |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anesthésique*, from Greek *anaisthetos* "without sensation," from *an-* "without" + *aisthētos* "perceptible, capable of feeling."
