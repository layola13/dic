honeybees:/ˈhʌniˌbiːz/| n. |蜜蜂|A social bee that lives in colonies and produces honey and beeswax.  A type of bee known for its production of honey and beeswax, living in large colonies with a queen, drones, and workers. |例句：The honeybees were buzzing around the hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围嗡嗡叫。
