co-heritor:/ˌkoʊɪnˈherɪtər/| n.|共同继承人|A person who inherits property jointly with another or others.|例句：She and her brother are co-heritors of their father's estate. 她和她哥哥是她父亲遗产的共同继承人。

例句：The co-heritors disagreed on how to divide the property. 共同继承人在如何分割财产上意见不一。|近义词：joint heir, fellow heir|反义词:|noun
