demiurgeous:/ˌdɛmiˈɜːrdʒəs/
adjective
造物主的；宇宙创始神的；有创造力的
Of or relating to a demiurge; creatively formative.
例句：The demiurgeous power of nature is awe-inspiring.  大自然的造物主般的力量令人敬畏。

例句：He had a demiurgeous vision for the company's future. 他对公司的未来有着极富创造力的远见。
近义词：creative, formative, generative
反义词：destructive, uncreative
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *demiurgus, from Ancient Greek δημιουργός (dēmiourgós, “craftsman, artisan”), from δῆμος (dêmos, “people”) + ἔργον (érgon, “work”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *werǵ- (“to do, work”).
