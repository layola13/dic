charlatanism:/ˈʃɑːrlətənɪzəm/| n.|江湖骗术；招摇撞骗|The charlatanism of the so-called "expert" was quickly exposed.  这个所谓的“专家”的骗局很快就被揭穿了。|The presentation was full of charlatanism, with no real substance behind the flashy claims. 这场演讲充满了江湖骗术，华而不实的言辞背后没有任何实际内容。|近义词：fraud, deception, quackery, humbug|反义词：honesty, authenticity, genuineness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *charlatanisme*, from Italian *ciarlatanismo*, from *ciarlatano* ("charlatan"), from *ciarlare* ("to chatter"), of imitative origin.
