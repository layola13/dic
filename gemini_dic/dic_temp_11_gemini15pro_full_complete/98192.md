insect:/ˈɪnsekt/ (US), /ˈɪnˌsekt/ (UK)| n.|昆虫|A small arthropod animal that has six legs and generally one or two pairs of wings.|例句：Ants, bees, and butterflies are all insects. 蚂蚁、蜜蜂和蝴蝶都是昆虫。|例句：The insect bit him on the arm.  这只昆虫咬了他的胳膊。|近义词：bug, creepy-crawly|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
