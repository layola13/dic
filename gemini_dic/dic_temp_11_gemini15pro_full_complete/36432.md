colander:/ˈkɒləndə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləndər/ (US)| n.|滤锅，漏勺|A bowl-shaped kitchen utensil with holes in it used for draining food such as pasta or rice.|I used a colander to drain the spaghetti. 我用滤锅沥干意大利面。|近义词:sieve, strainer|反义词: None|词性: noun

colanders:/ˈkɒləndəz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləndərz/ (US)| n.|滤锅，漏勺（复数）|Plural form of colander, referring to more than one colander.|She has a collection of colanders in different sizes. 她收集了各种大小的滤锅。|近义词:sieves, strainers|反义词: None|词性: noun
