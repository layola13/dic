gravities: /ˈɡrævɪtiz/ | n. | 重力，引力；地心吸力（gravity 的复数）| The strength of gravities varies across different planets.  不同行星的重力强度不同。|  例句：The differing gravities of the planets affect the trajectories of spacecraft. 不同行星的不同引力会影响航天器的轨道。

gravities: /ˈɡrævɪtiz/ | n. |  严重性，重大性（gravity 的复数）| The report emphasized the gravities of the situation.  报告强调了局势的严重性。| 例句：The politician spoke about the gravities of the economic crisis. 这位政治家谈到了经济危机的严重性。| 近义词：seriousnesses, magnitudes, importances | 反义词： trivialities, insignificances | 词性：名词

词源: From Late Middle English *gravite, from Old French gravité, from Latin gravitās (“heaviness, weight”), from gravis (“heavy”).
