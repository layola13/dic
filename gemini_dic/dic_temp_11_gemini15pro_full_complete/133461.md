nonfeasible:/ˌnɒnˈfiːzɪbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfiːzəbl/
adjective
不可行的，办不到的
Not capable of being done or carried out; impracticable.
例句：The project was deemed nonfeasible due to the exorbitant costs.  这个项目由于成本过高被认为是不可行的。

例句：Building a bridge across the ocean is a nonfeasible idea.  在大洋上建一座桥梁是一个不可行的想法。
近义词：impracticable, unfeasible, impossible, unattainable
反义词：feasible, practicable, possible, achievable
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语，意为“不”
feasible (adjective): from Old French faisable "doable," from faire "to do," from Latin facere "to make, do."  来自古法语 faisable “可做的”，源于 faire “做”，来自拉丁语 facere “制造，做”。
