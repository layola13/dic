enclitical: /ɪnˈklɪtɪk(ə)l/ /ɛnˈklɪtɪk(ə)l/| adj.| 附加的，后接的，粘附的| Relating to or characteristic of an enclitic. An enclitic is a word that is phonetically unstressed and attached to a preceding word, such as ’s in “He’s going home.” | Example: The word "n't" in "didn't" is an enclitical element.  单词"n't"在"didn't"中是一个后接成分。| Synonym: unstressed, dependent | Antonym: stressed, independent|词性: 形容词


enclitic: /ɪnˈklɪtɪk/ /ɛnˈklɪtɪk/| n.| 附加词，后接词| A word that is phonetically unstressed and attached to a preceding word, such as ’s in “He’s going home.” | Example: The word "'s" in "he's" is an enclitic.  单词"'s"在"he's"中是一个后接词。 | Synonym: clitic | Antonym: proclitic |词性: 名词
