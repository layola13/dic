confrontational:/ˌkɑːnfrənˈteɪʃənl/ | adj. | 对抗性的，挑衅的 | Inclined to confront or challenge; tending to deal with situations in an aggressive way. |  She has a very confrontational style of management. 她有一种非常具有对抗性的管理风格。| The lawyer took a confrontational approach with the witness. 律师对证人采取了对抗性的态度。|  aggressive, antagonistic, belligerent, combative, hostile, provocative | conciliatory, cooperative, diplomatic, passive, peaceful | adjective

confront:/kənˈfrʌnt/ | vt. | 面对，对抗 |  Meet (someone) face to face with hostile or argumentative intent. | I confronted him with the evidence of his guilt. 我用他犯罪的证据与他对质。|  We need to confront the problem head-on. 我们需要正面应对这个问题。| challenge, face, oppose, tackle | avoid, evade, ignore, retreat | verb
