cinderous: /ˈsɪndərəs/| adj.|灰烬般的，像煤渣的|Resembling or containing cinders; ash-colored. Covered with ash or cinders.  |The cinderous landscape after the eruption was a haunting sight. 火山爆发后，遍布灰烬的景象令人难以忘怀。|The walls were painted a cinderous grey, adding to the somber atmosphere.  墙壁被漆成灰烬般的灰色，更增添了阴沉的气氛。|近义词：ashy, ashen, sooty|反义词：bright, colorful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "cinder" (burning piece of coal/wood) + "-ous" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "possessing the qualities of").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *sind-, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷend-.
