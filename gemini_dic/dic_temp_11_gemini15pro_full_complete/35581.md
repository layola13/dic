coanchor:/koʊˈæŋkər/| n. v.|共同主持人，联合主播；共同主持，联合主播|A person who coanchors a broadcast, such as a news program, with another person.|例句：She coanchors the evening news with her colleague. 她和她的同事共同主持晚间新闻。|例句：They will coanchor a special report on the election. 他们将联合主持关于选举的特别报道。|近义词：co-host, co-presenter|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "solo anchor")|词性: noun, verb
