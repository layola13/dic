antitype:/ˈæntitaɪp/ | n. |原型，预表，对应物 |Something that is represented by a type or symbol; a person or thing that corresponds to or fulfills a prefiguration, especially in the Bible. |  The Passover lamb is seen as an antitype of Christ.  逾越节的羔羊被视为基督的预表。| Synonyms:  prototype, archetype, exemplar, prefigurement | Antonyms: type, symbol, figure | noun

antitype:/ˈæntitaɪp/ | n. | 对立面，相反之物 | Something that is diametrically opposed to something else.  | His personality is the complete antitype of his brother's. 他的性格与他兄弟的性格截然相反。 | Synonyms: opposite, antithesis, contrary | Antonyms:  type, counterpart | noun
