folding-in:/ˌfəʊldɪŋ ˈɪn/ | n./v. | 折叠；拌入；掺入 | The act of gently combining a lighter ingredient into a heavier one, usually with a spatula, to avoid deflating the lighter mixture.  | Example: Carefully folding in the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture will create a light and airy mousse.  小心地将打发的奶油拌入巧克力混合物中，就能做出轻盈的慕斯。 | Synonym: incorporating, blending, mixing | Antonym: separating, dividing | noun/verb

folding-in:/ˌfəʊldɪŋ ˈɪn/ | v. | 融入，吸纳 |  Gradually integrating something into something else. | Example:  The company is folding in several smaller businesses into its operations. 这家公司正在将几家小企业并入其运营中。| Synonym: integrating, merging, incorporating | Antonym: separating, divesting | verb
