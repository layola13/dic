moley:/ˈmoʊli/| adj. n.|多痣的，鼹鼠似的；痣，胎记|Having many moles (small, dark spots on the skin); resembling a mole.  A mole. |例句：She has a very moley complexion. 她的肤色有很多痣。|例句：The baby had a small, dark moley on its back.  这个婴儿的背上有一个小小的、黑色的胎记。|近义词：freckled, spotted, birthmark (for n.)|反义词:clear, unspotted|词性：adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *moley*, from *mole* + *-y*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mul*, meaning "dust, soil".
