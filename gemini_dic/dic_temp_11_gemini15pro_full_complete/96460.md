individualism: /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪzəm/ | n. | 个人主义 | The habit or principle of being independent and self-reliant. |  A society that encourages individualism. 一个鼓励个人主义的社会。 |  independence, self-reliance, autonomy | collectivism, socialism | noun

Etymology:  Late 18th century: from French *individualisme*, from individu (see individual) + -isme (see -ism).
