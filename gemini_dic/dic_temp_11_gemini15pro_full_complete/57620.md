draughts:/drɑːfts/ | n. | 西洋跳棋；(英式)气流，通风；(美式少见，拼写为drafts)汇票| A game played on a checkered board with pieces that are moved diagonally. Also, a current of air. |例句：We played a game of draughts. 我们玩了一局西洋跳棋。

例句：There was a cold draught coming from under the door.  门底下有一股冷风。|近义词：(game) checkers, (air current) draft, breeze |反义词:(air current) stillness |词性:noun
