macrocosm:/ˈmækrəˌkɒzəm/ /ˈmækroʊˌkɑːzəm/| n. |宏观世界；宇宙|The universe is often described as a vast macrocosm. 宇宙常被描述为浩瀚的宏观世界。|例句：Studying the macrocosm helps us understand our place in the universe.  研究宏观世界有助于我们理解我们在宇宙中的位置。

macrocosm:/ˈmækrəˌkɒzəm/ /ˈmækroʊˌkɑːzəm/| n. |整体；总体|Society is a macrocosm of the family unit. 社会是家庭单元的整体。|例句：The company is a macrocosm of the industry, reflecting its trends and challenges.  这家公司是该行业的缩影，反映了其趋势和挑战。|近义词：universe, cosmos, world, entirety, whole|反义词:microcosm|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μακρόκοσμος (makrókosmos, “great world”), from μακρός (makrós, “long, large”) + κόσμος (kósmos, “order, world”).
