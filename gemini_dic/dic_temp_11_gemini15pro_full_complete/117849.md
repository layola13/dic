mellowest:/ˈmɛloʊɪst/| adj.|最成熟的；最醇厚的；最柔和的|Superlative form of mellow: most mellow.  Having reached the highest degree of mellowness; soft, sweet, and full-flavored from ripeness, as fruit; genial; free from harshness, etc.|The mellowest wines are often the most expensive.  最醇厚的葡萄酒通常也是最昂贵的。|Near Synonyms: ripest, softest, sweetest, mildest, gentlest|Antonyms: harshest, sourest, bitterest, greenest|adjective

mellow:/ˈmɛloʊ/| adj. n. v.|成熟的；醇厚的；柔和的；使成熟；使醇厚；使柔和|
(adj.) Soft, sweet, and full-flavored from ripeness, as fruit; genial; free from harshness.
(n.)  A mellow quality or state.
(v.) To make or become mellow.|
(adj.) The cheese has a mellow flavor.  这种奶酪味道醇厚。
(n.)  The wine develops a wonderful mellow with age.  随着时间的推移，这种葡萄酒会散发出美妙的醇香。
(v.) The sun mellowed the fruit.  阳光使水果成熟了。|Near Synonyms: (adj.) ripe, soft, sweet, mild, gentle; (v.) ripen, soften, mature|Antonyms: (adj.) harsh, sour, bitter, green; (v.) harden, sour|adjective, noun, verb
