ethnicism: /ˈeθnɪˌsɪzəm/
n.
种族主义；种族歧视
The belief in the inherent superiority of one's own ethnic group or culture.  A tendency to view alien cultures or people from other ethnic groups as inferior.
Ethnicism can lead to discrimination and conflict. 种族主义会导致歧视和冲突。
The politician's speech was full of ethnicism and hateful rhetoric.  这位政客的演讲充满了种族主义和仇恨言论。
近义词: racism, bigotry, prejudice, discrimination, xenophobia
反义词: tolerance, acceptance, multiculturalism, diversity
词性: noun
