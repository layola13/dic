bilander:/ˈbɪləndər/| n. |双桅帆船，荷兰平底货船|A small two-masted merchant vessel, especially one common in the Netherlands, with a characteristic rigging and leeboards.|The bilander sailed smoothly across the Zuiderzee. 这艘双桅帆船平稳地航行过须德海。|近义词：ketch, hoy|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Dutch *bilander*, from Middle Dutch *bylander*, probably related to *bij* "near, alongside" + *land* "land," suggesting a coastal trader.
