mnemosyne:/nɪˈmɒsɪniː/ | n. |  记忆女神，记忆力 | Mnemosyne is the Greek goddess of memory and the mother of the Muses. She represents the personification of memory. |  Mnemosyne is often depicted holding a poppy, a symbol of sleep and dreams, which are believed to be gateways to memory. 记忆女神通常被描绘成手持罂粟花，罂粟花是睡眠和梦境的象征，人们相信睡眠和梦境是通往记忆的大门。

Mnemosyne's blessings were sought by poets and orators who needed to remember long passages of text.  诗人和演说家会祈求记忆女神的祝福，因为他们需要记住长篇的文本。| 近义词: memory | 反义词: amnesia, forgetfulness | 词性: noun
