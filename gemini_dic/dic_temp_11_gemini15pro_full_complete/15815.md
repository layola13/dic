balkish:/ˈbɔːlkɪʃ/ | adj. | 略显笨重的；有点大的；体积略大的 | Somewhat bulky; slightly large in size or volume. | The balkish package was difficult to maneuver through the narrow doorway.  这个略显笨重的包裹很难通过狭窄的门口。 |  The furniture was a bit balkish for the small apartment. 这家具对于这间小公寓来说有点大了。 |  bulky, cumbersome, hefty, massive | compact, small, slight | adjective

Etymology->
From balk (meaning "beam, ridge") + -ish (suffix indicating "somewhat resembling").  The original sense related to the size and shape of a balk of timber.
