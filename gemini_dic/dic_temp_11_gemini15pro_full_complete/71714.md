flavouring:/ˈfleɪvərɪŋ/ | n. | 调味品；调味剂 | A substance added to food or drink to give it a particular flavour. |  This flavouring gives the cake a hint of almond.  这种调味剂使蛋糕带有一丝杏仁味。| synonyms: seasoning, spice, condiment, additive | antonyms: blandness, plainness | noun

Etymology: From the Old French *flavour*,  ultimately from Latin *flare* "to blow, breathe".  The "-ing" suffix indicates a substance used for flavouring.
