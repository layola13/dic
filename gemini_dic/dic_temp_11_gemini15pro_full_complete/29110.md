cascade:/kæˈskeɪd/ /kæsˈkeɪd/| n. v.|瀑布；倾泻；串联|A small waterfall, typically one of several that fall in stages down a steep rocky slope.  To fall or hang in copious quantities.|The water cascaded down the rocks. 水从岩石上倾泻而下。|近义词：waterfall, torrent, flood, avalanche, shower, deluge|反义词:trickle, dribble|noun, verb

cascade:/kæˈskeɪd/ /kæsˈkeɪd/| v.|倾泻；大量落下|To fall or hang in copious quantities.|Her hair cascaded down her back. 她的头发倾泻而下。|近义词：flow, pour, stream, gush|反义词:ascend, rise|verb
