colonel:/ˈkɜːrnl/ (US) /ˈkɜːnəl/ (UK)| n. |上校；(某些组织的)领袖|A commissioned officer in the army, air force, or marine corps holding a rank above lieutenant colonel and below brigadier general.|例句：The colonel addressed his troops before the battle. 上校在战斗前向他的部队讲话。

例句：Colonel Sanders founded Kentucky Fried Chicken.  桑德斯上校创立了肯德基炸鸡。|近义词：commander, officer|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑subordinate 下属)|词性: 名词

词源：
From Old Italian *colonello (whence also French colonel), from colonna "column," because the colonel originally commanded a column of soldiers. 最初来自古意大利语 *colonello（法语 colonel 也源于此），来自 colonna “纵队”，因为上校最初指挥士兵的纵队。
