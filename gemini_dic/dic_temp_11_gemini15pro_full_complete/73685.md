forgettingly:/fərˈɡetɪŋli/
adverb
健忘地；不经意地
In a forgetful manner; absentmindedly.
She forgettingly left her keys in the car. 她不经意地把钥匙忘在了车里。
absentmindedly, heedlessly
mindfully, attentively
词性: 副词
