detenu:/dɪˈteɪnjuː/ /dɪˈtɛnjuː/
n.
被拘留者；未经审判的囚犯
a person held in custody, especially for political reasons.
The government refused to release the detainees. 政府拒绝释放被拘留者。

例句：
The detainees were held without charge for several months.  这些被拘留者未经指控就被关押了几个月。

The Red Cross visited the detainees to ensure their humane treatment. 红十字会探视了被拘留者，以确保他们受到人道的待遇。


近义词：prisoner, captive, inmate
反义词：freeman, parolee
词性：noun
