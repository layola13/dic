haka:/ˈhɑːkə/ /ˈhɑːkɑː/| n.|哈卡舞; 哈卡战舞|A traditional Maori posture dance performed by a group, often including chanting, rhythmic movements, and facial expressions such as sticking out the tongue and widening the eyes.|The All Blacks performed a powerful haka before the rugby match. 全黑队在橄榄球比赛前表演了一场气势磅礴的哈卡舞。|Near synonyms: war dance, ceremonial dance|Antonyms: N/A|noun
