euosmitte:/juːˈɒsmɪti/ /juːˈɑːsmɪti/| n.|愉悦的香气，通常指海洋气息，有时也指泥土的芬芳|A pleasant smell, especially of the sea or sometimes of the earth.|例句：The euosmitte of the seaside air invigorated her. 海边空气的芬芳使她精神焕发。

例句：After the rain, the garden was filled with the euosmitte of wet earth. 雨后，花园里充满了湿润泥土的芬芳。|近义词：fragrance, aroma, scent, perfume|反义词:stench, odor, stink|词性:名词

词源: From Greek *εὖ (eu) "good" + *ὀσμή (osmē) "smell".  It was coined in the 19th century.
