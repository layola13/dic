depreciate:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/ /dɪˈpriːsiˌeɪt/
verb
贬值；折旧；贬低；轻视
To diminish in value over a period of time.  To belittle or undervalue.
The value of the car depreciated quickly. 这辆车的价值很快贬值了。

He tends to depreciate his own achievements. 他总是贬低自己的成就。

近义词:devalue, lessen, reduce, belittle, disparage
反义词:appreciate, increase, enhance, praise
词性:verb


depreciated:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪd/ /dɪˈpriːsiˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
贬值的；折旧的
Reduced in value.
The company's assets were depreciated over time. 公司的资产随着时间的推移而贬值。

The building was in a depreciated condition. 这栋建筑处于贬值状态。

近义词:devalued, reduced
反义词:appreciated, increased
词性:adjective
