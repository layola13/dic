dorsal: /ˈdɔːrsəl/ (UK) /ˈdɔːrsəl/ (US)| adj.| 背部的，背侧的| Relating to the back or posterior of a body or organ, especially of an animal or plant. | The dorsal fin of a shark helps it to maintain balance.  鲨鱼的背鳍帮助它保持平衡。

The dorsal side of a leaf is usually darker than the ventral side.  叶子的背面通常比腹面颜色更深。|近义词：posterior, back|反义词：ventral, anterior|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *dorsalis, from Latin dorsum (“back”).
