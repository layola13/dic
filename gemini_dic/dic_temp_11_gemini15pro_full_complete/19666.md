bilaterally:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəli/
adverb|双边地，两方面地|involving two groups or countries|The agreement was signed bilaterally. 该协议是双边签署的。|
Synonym: mutually, jointly|Antonym: unilaterally, individually|词性:副词

bilaterally:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəli/
adjective|双边的，两侧的|having or relating to two sides; affecting both sides of the body|Bilateral trade between the two countries has increased. 两国之间的双边贸易有所增加。|Synonym: two-sided, mutual|Antonym: unilateral, one-sided|词性:形容词
