flatulent:/ˈflætʃʊlənt/ (US), /ˈflætjʊlənt/ (UK)| adj.|胀气的；夸夸其谈的；空洞的|Suffering from or caused by gases generated in the alimentary canal; pompous or pretentious, especially in speech or writing; empty, inflated.|

例句：
He felt flatulent after eating too many beans.
他吃了太多豆子后感到胀气。

例句：
His flatulent speech impressed no one.
他夸夸其谈的演讲没有打动任何人。


近义词：gaseous, bloated, pompous, pretentious, bombastic|
反义词：humble, modest, down-to-earth|
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *flatulentus, from Latin flatus (“a blowing, blast, breath”), from flare (“to blow, puff”).
