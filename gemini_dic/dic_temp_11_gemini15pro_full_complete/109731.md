leventhal:/ˈlɛvənθɔːl/ | adj. n. | 莱文塔尔的;莱文塔尔(姓氏) |  Relating to or characteristic of Howard Leventhal, a psychologist known for his work on the commonsense model of self-regulation of health and illness, or his theories and models.  Also used as a surname. | Leventhal's research has greatly influenced health psychology. 莱文塔尔的研究对健康心理学产生了巨大的影响。 |  The Leventhal model emphasizes the individual's understanding of their illness. 莱文塔尔模型强调个人对其疾病的理解。 | synonyms:  (for the model) self-regulation model, commonsense model | antonyms: N/A | noun, adjective
