outreckons:/ˌaʊtˈrɛkənz/
v.
胜过，超过，在计算上超过
surpass in calculating; excel in reckoning or computing.
The team outreckoned their opponents and won the championship.  这个队在计算上胜过了他们的对手，赢得了冠军。
The accountant outreckoned the total profits for the year. 会计计算出了全年的总利润，超过了预期。
近义词: outcalculate, surpass, exceed, outstrip
反义词: underestimate, fall short of
词性: 动词


词源:  "out-" (超过) + "reckon" (计算)
