frown:/fraʊn/
n.|皱眉，蹙额|An expression of displeasure or concentration characterized by a furrowing of one's brows.|He gave me a frown of disapproval. 他不赞成地朝我皱了皱眉。|近义词：scowl, grimace|反义词：smile, grin|词性:名词
frown:/fraʊn/
vi.|皱眉，蹙额|To wrinkle one's brow, as in displeasure or thought.|She frowned at the complicated instructions. 她对着复杂的说明书皱起了眉头。|近义词：scowl, grimace|反义词：smile, grin|词性:动词
