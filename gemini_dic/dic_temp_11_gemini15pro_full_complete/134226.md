nonindustrious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌstriəs/ | adj. | 不勤劳的，懒惰的 | Not industrious; lazy. Characterized by a lack of diligence and aversion to work.  | He was a nonindustrious student, preferring to socialize than study. 他是一个不勤奋的学生，宁愿社交也不愿学习。|  She was fired for being nonindustrious and frequently late. 她因为懒惰且经常迟到而被解雇了。 | 近义词: lazy, indolent, slothful, idle | 反义词: industrious, diligent, hardworking, assiduous | 词性: 形容词
