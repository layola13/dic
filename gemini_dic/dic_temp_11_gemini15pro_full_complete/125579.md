multitracking:/ˌmʌltiˈtrækɪŋ/ | n. | 多轨录音 | The process of recording different instruments or vocals on separate tracks of a recording medium, which can then be mixed together. |  The band used multitracking to create a rich, layered sound.  这支乐队使用多轨录音技术来创造丰富的层次感。| synonyms: overdubbing, layering | antonyms: live recording | noun


multitrack:/ˈmʌltiˌtræk/ | adj. | 多轨的 |  Relating to or involving the use of multiple tracks for recording. | They bought a multitrack recorder for their home studio. 他们为家庭工作室买了一个多轨录音机。| synonyms: multichannel | antonyms: single-track | adjective


multitrack:/ˌmʌltiˈtræk/ | v. | 进行多轨录音 | To record using multiple tracks. | They multitracked the song in just a few hours. 他们在几个小时内就完成了这首歌的多轨录音。 | synonyms: overdub, layer | antonyms: record live | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "multi-" (meaning "many") + "track" (referring to the individual audio channels on a recording medium).
