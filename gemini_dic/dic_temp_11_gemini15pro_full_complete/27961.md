cantor:/ˈkæntər/ (US), /ˈkæntɔːr/ (UK)| n.|歌手；领唱者；圣诗领唱人|A cantor is a person who sings liturgical music and leads prayer in a synagogue.  A cantor is a lead singer, especially one who sings religious music.|例句：The cantor's voice resonated through the synagogue. 领唱者的声音在犹太教堂里回荡。

例句：He was chosen as the cantor for the High Holidays. 他被选为高级圣日的领唱者。|近义词：singer, vocalist, musician, choirmaster|反义词: listener, audience|词性:noun
