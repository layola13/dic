monopoly:/məˈnɒpəli/ /məˈnɑːpəli/| n.|垄断, 独占, 专卖|The exclusive possession or control of the supply or trade in a commodity or service.|The company has a monopoly on the production of this drug.  这家公司垄断了这种药物的生产。|near synonym: control, domination|near antonym: competition, sharing|noun

monopolistic:/məˌnɒpəˈlɪstɪk/ /məˌnɑːpəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj.|垄断的, 独占的|Relating to or constituting a monopoly.|Monopolistic practices are harmful to consumers. 垄断行为对消费者有害。|near synonym: exclusive, controlling|near antonym: competitive, open|adjective
