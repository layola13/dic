miasmatist:/maɪˈæzmətɪst/| n.|瘴气论者；污秽空气有害论者|A person who believes in the miasma theory, which held that diseases like cholera, chlamydia, or the Black Death were caused by a noxious form of "bad air" emanating from rotting organic matter.|The miasmatist argued that the cholera outbreak was due to the foul smells emanating from the river. 瘴气论者认为霍乱的爆发是由于河里散发出的恶臭。|近义词:contagionist|反义词:|noun

miasma:/maɪˈæzmə/| n.|瘴气；秽气|A noxious or poisonous atmosphere or influence.|The miasma of corruption hung heavy over the city. 腐败的瘴气笼罩着整个城市。|近义词:stench, effluvium|反义词:fragrance|noun

miasmic:/maɪˈæzmɪk/| adj.|瘴气的；秽气的|Relating to or resembling miasma; foul-smelling.|The miasmic swamp was a breeding ground for mosquitoes. 瘴气弥漫的沼泽是蚊子的滋生地。|近义词:noxious, pestilential|反义词:fresh, clean|adjective
