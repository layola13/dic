cowrite:/ˌkoʊˈraɪt/| v. |共同写作，合著|To write something together with another person or people.|例句：They cowrote a screenplay for a new movie. 他们共同为一部新电影编写了剧本。

例句：She cowrote the song with her bandmate. 她和她的乐队成员共同创作了这首歌。|近义词：collaborate, co-author|反义词: write alone, sole author|词性: verb
