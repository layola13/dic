guarish:/ˈɡwærɪʃ/ | adj. | 治愈的，恢复的；康复的 |  Cured or recovered from illness or injury; convalescent.  | 例句：He felt quite guarish after a week's rest. 他休息了一周后感觉完全康复了。 | 例句：She was guarish enough to return to work. 她已康复，可以回去工作了。 | 近义词：recovered, cured, healed, convalescent | 反义词：sick, ill, unwell, diseased | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle English *garis(s)hen, from Anglo-Norman *garis-, garir (“to heal, cure”), from Frankish *warjan (“to defend, protect”), from Proto-Germanic *warjaną (“to protect, defend, forbid”). Cognate with Old High German werien (“to protect, defend”), Old English werian (“to defend, protect, wear (clothes)”). More at wary.
