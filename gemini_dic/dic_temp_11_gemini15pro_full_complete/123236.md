monocrystalline:/ˌmɒnəʊkrɪˈstəlɪn/ /ˌmɑːnoʊkrɪˈstælɪn/| adj.|单晶的|Composed of a single crystal, as opposed to being polycrystalline (made up of many small crystals).|The company specializes in manufacturing monocrystalline silicon solar panels.  这家公司专门生产单晶硅太阳能电池板。|Synonyms: single-crystal|Antonyms: polycrystalline|adjective
Etymology:  From mono- (one) + crystalline (relating to crystals).  Ultimately from Greek monos (single, alone) and krystallos (ice, crystal).
