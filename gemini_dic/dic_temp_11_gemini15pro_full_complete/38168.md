compromise: /ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪz/ (US)
| n. |妥协，折衷；和解；妥协方案；危害，损害|An agreement in which each side gives up something it wants.|  We had to reach a compromise.  我们不得不达成妥协。|近义词：agreement, settlement, concession, bargain |反义词:disagreement, conflict, intransigence| noun
compromise: /ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪz/ (US)
| vt. |妥协，折衷；危及，损害|To give up something you want in order to reach an agreement.|  Don't compromise your principles. 不要放弃你的原则。|近义词：jeopardize, endanger, expose, undermine|反义词:maintain, uphold, protect| verb
