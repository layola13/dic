hydrotherapeutics:/ˌhaɪdrəθɛrəˈpjuːtɪks/
noun
水疗法
The treatment of disease by the internal or external use of water.
Hydrotherapeutics can be used to treat a variety of conditions, including arthritis and muscle pain. 水疗法可以用来治疗各种疾病，包括关节炎和肌肉疼痛。
