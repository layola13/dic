hypermetamorphic:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|超变质的|Undergoing multiple episodes of metamorphism, each of which may obliterate the effects of earlier metamorphism.  Rocks that have been subjected to multiple metamorphic events, resulting in complex and often obscured textures and mineral assemblages.|The hypermetamorphic rocks in this region tell a complex story of tectonic upheaval and deep burial.|近义词:polymetamorphic|反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider low-grade metamorphic)|词性:adjective
