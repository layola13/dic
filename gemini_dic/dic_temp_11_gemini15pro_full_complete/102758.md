janus:/ˈdʒeɪnəs/| n. |雅努斯，两面神|A Roman god depicted with two faces, one looking forward and the other backward.  Often used metaphorically to represent duality, two-facedness, or hypocrisy. |He's a real Janus, friendly to your face but quick to criticize behind your back. 他真是个两面派，当面友好，背后却很快批评你。|近义词：two-faced, hypocritical, duplicitous|反义词：sincere, honest|noun
