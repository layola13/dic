infanthood:/ˈɪnfənthʊd/| n.|婴儿时期，幼儿期|The period of a child's life from birth to about two years of age; early childhood.|She has very few memories of her infanthood. 她对婴儿时期几乎没有什么记忆。|近义词：babyhood, infancy|反义词：adulthood|词性:名词
Etymology: From infant + -hood.  "Infant" comes from the Latin "infans," meaning "unable to speak," from "in-" (not) + "fans" (speaking). "-hood" is an Old English suffix denoting a state or condition.
