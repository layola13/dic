cartoonlike:/kɑːrˈtuːnlaɪk/
adj.
像卡通的，卡通式的
Resembling or characteristic of a cartoon.
Her painting style is very cartoonlike, with exaggerated features and bright colors. 她的绘画风格很像卡通，特征夸张，色彩鲜艳。
The animation had a cartoonlike quality, with simple characters and backgrounds.  这部动画片有一种卡通般的品质，人物和背景都很简单。
近义词:animated, caricatured, comical
反义词:realistic, lifelike
词性:形容词
