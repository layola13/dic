cacolet: /ˈkækəˌlɛt/ | n. | 驮篮；驮椅 | A chair or litter secured on either side of a mule or other pack animal, used especially formerly for carrying passengers in mountainous regions. |  The travelers crossed the Andes Mountains in cacolets. 旅行者们乘坐驮椅穿越了安第斯山脉。 |  The guide secured the cacolet to the mule before the ascent. 向导在上山之前把驮椅固定在骡子身上。 |Synonyms: litter, sedan chair, palanquin | Antonyms: N/A | noun
