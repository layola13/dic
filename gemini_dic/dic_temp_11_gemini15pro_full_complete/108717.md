词源: lean + -ish.  "Lean" comes from Old English *hlæne "lean, meager," from Proto-Germanic *khlainaz (source also of Old Saxon hleni, Old Frisian hlene, Old High German hlina, German hlein "small, thin, lean").  The suffix "-ish" forms adjectives meaning "somewhat," "rather," or "like."
