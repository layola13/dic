droil:/drɔɪl/| v.| 苦干，辛苦工作 | to work hard, especially at a tedious or unpleasant task | He droiled away at the farm all day. 他整天在农场辛苦劳作。 | synonyms: toil, slave, labor, grind | antonyms: rest, relax, idle | verb

Etymology: From Middle English *droilen, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *drýgja "to prolong, delay," or to Dutch *druilen "to loiter, linger."
