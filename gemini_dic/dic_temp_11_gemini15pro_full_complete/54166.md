disenthrallment:/ˌdɪsɪnˈθrɔːlmənt/
n.
解放，释放
The act of freeing someone from bondage or oppression; liberation.
Her disenthrallment from the cult was a long and difficult process. 她从邪教中解脱出来是一个漫长而艰难的过程。

近义词: liberation, freedom, release, emancipation
反义词: enslavement, captivity, oppression, subjugation
词性: 名词

词源:  From dis- + enthrallment, from enthrall, from en- + thrall.  "Dis-" prefixes the word to give the opposite meaning.  "Thrall" originally meant "slave."  So, disenthrallment literally means freeing from slavery.
