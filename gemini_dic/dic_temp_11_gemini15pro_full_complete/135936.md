nonpunitive:/nɒnˈpʌnɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非惩罚性的 | Not involving or imposing punishment.  Characterized by the absence of punishment or penalties. | Example: The company has a nonpunitive attendance policy.  该公司实行非惩罚性的考勤政策。| Example:  The school adopted a nonpunitive approach to discipline, focusing on restorative justice. 学校采取了非惩罚性的纪律方法，注重恢复性司法。 | Synonyms:  non-penal, lenient, merciful, forgiving, tolerant | Antonyms: punitive, penalizing, disciplinary |词性: 形容词
