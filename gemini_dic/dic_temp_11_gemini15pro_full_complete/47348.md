de-stratifying: /diːˈstrætɪfaɪɪŋ/
| v. | 消除分层，使均匀 | The process of destratifying involves mixing the layers of a fluid, typically water, to achieve a uniform temperature and distribution of properties. |  The engineers are de-stratifying the lake to prevent oxygen depletion. 工程师们正在消除湖泊的分层，以防止氧气耗尽。

The city is de-stratifying the reservoir to improve water quality.  这座城市正在消除水库的分层，以提高水质。 | synonyms: mixing, homogenizing, blending | antonyms: stratifying, layering | verb

Etymology:
From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + stratifying (from stratum (Latin for "layer") + -fy (suffix meaning "to make")).
