overassurednesses:/ˌoʊvərəˈʃʊərdnəsɪz/
n.
过度自信; 过于自信的状态
The state of being excessively confident or self-assured.
His overassuredness led him to make several costly mistakes. 他的过度自信导致他犯了几个代价高昂的错误。
Near Synonyms: arrogance, presumption, conceitedness, hubris
Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence, self-doubt
noun
