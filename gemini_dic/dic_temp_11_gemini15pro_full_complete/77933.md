genocidal: /ˌdʒɛnəˈsaɪdl/
adjective
种族灭绝的
Relating to or involving genocide; characteristic of genocide.
The Rwandan genocide was a genocidal campaign against the Tutsi population.  卢旺达种族大屠杀是一场针对图西族人口的种族灭绝运动。
The regime's genocidal policies led to the deaths of millions. 该政权的种族灭绝政策导致数百万人的死亡。

近义词: murderous, homicidal, destructive
反义词: peaceful, protective, benevolent
词性: adjective

词源:
From genocide + -al.  来自genocide（种族灭绝）+ -al（形容词后缀）。
Genocide itself comes from Greek genos "race, kind" + -cide "killing."  Genocide本身来自希腊语genos“种族，种类”+ -cide“杀戮”。
