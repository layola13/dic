baned:/beɪnd/
adjective
被禁止的
officially or legally prohibited.
例句：The baned substance was confiscated at the border.  这种被禁的物质在边境被没收了。

例句：He was baned from driving for six months. 他被禁止驾驶六个月。
近义词：forbidden, prohibited, outlawed
反义词：permitted, allowed, authorized
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "ban" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Ban" comes from Old French "banir" (to proclaim, summon, banish), from Frankish "*bannan" (to summon, command). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*bannjaną" (to proclaim).
