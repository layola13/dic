noncontrollably:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrəʊləbli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈtroʊləbli/
adjective
无法控制地
in a way that cannot be controlled
His hands shook noncontrollably. 他的双手无法控制地颤抖。
The child cried noncontrollably after losing his toy.  孩子丢失玩具后无法控制地哭泣。
近义词: uncontrollably, involuntarily
反义词: controllably, voluntarily
词性: adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + controllable + -ly (adverb suffix)
From "controllable," which comes from "control" (from Old French "contrerolle," a duplicate register used to verify accounts) + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
