cheesily:/ˈtʃiːzɪli/| adv.|俗气地，庸俗地，不自然地|In a cheesy way; in a way that is exaggerated or sentimental and not fashionable or sophisticated. |He grinned cheesily at the camera. 他对着镜头咧嘴一笑，显得有些俗气。|近义词：corny, tacky, mawkish, sentimental|反义词：tasteful, sophisticated, refined|词性:副词

cheesily:/ˈtʃiːzɪli/| adv.|廉价地，劣质地|Made of or resembling cheese.  (Informal, often disapproving) Cheap or poor quality.|The set was cheesily constructed and looked like it would fall apart. 布景搭建得很廉价，看起来像是要散架一样。|近义词：cheap, tacky, flimsy|反义词：expensive, high-quality, durable|词性:副词
