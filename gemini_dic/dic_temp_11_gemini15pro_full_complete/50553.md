deputies:/ˈdepjʊtiz/ /ˈdɛpjəˌtiz/| n.|代表，副手，代理人|A person appointed to act for or represent another, especially in an official capacity.|The sheriff's deputies patrolled the county. 警长的副手在县里巡逻。|近义词：representatives, agents, substitutes, assistants|反义词:principal, leader|noun

deputies:/ˈdepjʊtiz/ /ˈdɛpjəˌtiz/| n.|议员，议会代表|A member of certain legislative bodies, such as the French National Assembly.|The deputies voted on the new law. 议员们对新法律进行了投票。|近义词：representatives, members, delegates|反义词:constituent|noun
