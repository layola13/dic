araceous:/əˈreɪʃəs/
adj.
天南星科的
belonging to the Araceae family (also known as the arum family), a large family of flowering plants.
The philodendron is an araceous plant popular for its large, attractive leaves.  龟背竹是一种天南星科植物，因其宽大而美丽的叶子而受欢迎。
Synonyms: arum, aroideous
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a plant family)
词性: 形容词
