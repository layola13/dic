burgeon:/ˈbɜːrdʒən/
verb
开始迅速发展; 蓬勃发展; 迅速增长
begin to grow or increase rapidly; flourish
例句：The city’s population has burgeoned in recent years.  近年来，该市人口迅速增长。

例句：New suburbs burgeoned all around the city. 新的郊区在城市周围蓬勃发展。

近义词：flourish, thrive, prosper, boom, expand, mushroom
反义词：decline, dwindle, shrink, contract, wither
词性：v.
