admonition:/ˌædməˈnɪʃən/| n.|警告，劝告，忠告|an act of admonishing; authoritative counsel or warning|The teacher gave the student an admonition about being late to class again. 老师警告学生不要再迟到了。|synonyms: warning, caution, advice, counsel, reproof, rebuke|antonyms: commendation, praise, encouragement|noun

admonish:/ədˈmɒnɪʃ/| vt.|警告，劝告，责备|to warn or reprimand someone firmly|He admonished his friend for driving too fast. 他告诫他的朋友不要开得太快。|synonyms: warn, caution, advise, counsel, reproof, rebuke|antonyms: commend, praise, encourage|verb
