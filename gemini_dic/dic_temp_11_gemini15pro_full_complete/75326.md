frostbird:/ˈfrɒstbɜːd/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstbɜːrd/ (US)| n. |冬候鸟；耐寒的鸟|A bird that thrives in cold climates or migrates to warmer regions during winter.  A bird that can withstand or prefers cold weather. | The frostbird flew south for the winter.  这只冬候鸟飞往南方过冬。| Near synonyms: winter bird, cold-hardy bird | Antonyms: tropical bird | Noun
