attenuator:/əˈtɛnjuˌeɪtər/ | n. |衰减器，减弱器|A device that reduces the strength of a signal, light, or sound. | The guitarist used an attenuator to lower the volume of his amplifier. 吉他手使用衰减器来降低放大器的音量。 | synonyms: damper, reducer, suppressor | antonyms: amplifier, booster, intensifier | noun


attenuator:/əˈtɛnjuˌeɪtər/ | n. |衰减器，减弱器|Something that diminishes the intensity or effect of something else.  |  Heavy curtains acted as an attenuator for the bright sunlight. 厚重的窗帘减弱了强烈的阳光。 | synonyms: moderator, mitigator | antonyms:  intensifier, catalyst | noun
