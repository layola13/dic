non-amortizable:/ˌnɒn əˈmɔːrtaɪzəbl/ /ˌnɑːn əˈmɔːrtaɪzəbl/| adj.|不可摊销的|Not able to be amortized; that is, the cost cannot be gradually written off over a period of time.|例句：Goodwill is considered a non-amortizable asset. 商誉被认为是不可摊销的资产。|例句：Non-amortizable expenses are deducted in the year they are incurred. 不可摊销的费用在发生的年度扣除。|近义词：non-depreciable|反义词: amortizable|词性:形容词
