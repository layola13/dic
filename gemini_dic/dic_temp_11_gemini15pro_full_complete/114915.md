manage:/ˈmænɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈmænɪdʒ/ (UK)| v.|管理；经营；设法做到|To control or be in charge of something; to succeed in doing something difficult.|I manage a team of 20 people. 我管理一个20人的团队。|近义词：control, direct, handle, administer, cope, succeed|反义词:fail, neglect|verb

manage:/ˈmænɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈmænɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|管理；经营|The act or skill of controlling or organizing something.|The manage of the hotel is excellent.  这家酒店的管理很棒。|近义词：administration, direction, control|反义词:mismanagement|noun
