batikking:/bæˈtɪkɪŋ/
n.
蜡染
The process of creating patterns on fabric using wax and dye.
She demonstrated the intricate art of batikking. 她展示了复杂的蜡染艺术。
dyeing, printing
n/a
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Javanese *batik, from *tik "dot, drop."  The word refers to the process of applying wax drop by drop to resist dye.
