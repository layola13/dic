counter-order:/ˈkaʊntərˌɔːrdər/ | n. | 反指令，取消订单 | An order that cancels or reverses a previous order. | The general issued a counter-order to stop the attack. 将军下达了反指令停止进攻。 | The company received a counter-order for the goods. 公司收到了取消商品订单的通知。|  synonym: cancellation, revocation | antonym: order, confirmation | noun
