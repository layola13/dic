cutworm:/ˈkʌtwɜːrm/ (US), /ˈkʌtwɜːm/ (UK)| n. |切根虫|The larva of certain moths that cut off the stems of young plants at ground level.  These larvae are agricultural pests.|The cutworms destroyed my tomato seedlings.|Synonyms: armyworm, grub|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific insect pest.  Beneficial insects like ladybugs could be considered a broad antonym.)|noun
