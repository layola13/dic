dialectal:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktl/
adjective
方言的
Relating to a dialect; characteristic of a particular dialect.
Her dialectal pronunciation gave her away as a foreigner. 她带有方言腔的语音暴露了她外国人的身份。
dialectal:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktl/
adjective
方言的
Used in or characteristic of a particular dialect.
The word "ain't" is dialectal. "Ain't" 这个词是方言词。

近义词: vernacular, regional
反义词: standard, formal
词性: adjective
