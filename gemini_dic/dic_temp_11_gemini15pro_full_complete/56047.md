dobson:/ˈdɒbsən/ (UK) /ˈdɑːbsən/ (US)| n. |多布森单位，多布森光学单元|A unit of measurement for the total amount of ozone in a column of air overhead. One Dobson unit is equivalent to a layer of pure ozone 0.01 mm thick at standard temperature and pressure.|例句：The ozone layer measured 350 Dobson units. 臭氧层测量值为350多布森单位。

例句：Scientists use Dobson spectrophotometers to measure ozone levels. 科学家使用多布森分光光度计来测量臭氧水平。|近义词:DU (abbreviation)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
