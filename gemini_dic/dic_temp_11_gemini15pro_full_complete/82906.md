gumbo:/ˈɡʌmboʊ/ (US), /ˈɡʌmbəʊ/ (UK)| n. |秋葵汤，浓汤；混合物；困境|A stew thickened with okra pods, often containing meat or shellfish. Also, a metaphorical mixture of things. |例句：
My grandmother makes the best gumbo in Louisiana. 我祖母做的秋葵汤是路易斯安那州最好吃的。

The current political situation is a real gumbo. 当前的政治局势真是一团糟。

|近义词：stew, soup, mixture, medley, jumble|反义词:|词性:名词


gumbos:/ˈɡʌmboʊz/ (US), /ˈɡʌmbəʊz/ (UK)| n. |秋葵汤（复数）|Plural form of gumbo.  |例句：
