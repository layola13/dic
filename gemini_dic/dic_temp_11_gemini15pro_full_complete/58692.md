dumpies:/ˈdʌmpiz/
| n. |矮胖子；矮而粗壮的人（通常指儿童）|Short, stout people, often used to refer to children.|The group of dumpies waddled down the street. 那群矮胖子摇摇摆摆地走在街上。|近义词：chubbies, toddlers, shorties|反义词:tall, lanky|词性:名词

词源:  "Dumpies" is likely derived from "dumpy," which itself comes from "dump,"  referring to something short and thick or a heap.  The "-ies" suffix is a diminutive, often used to indicate small size or affection.
