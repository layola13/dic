imperishablenesses:/ˌɪmpɛrˈɪʃəbəlnəsɪz/ | n. |不朽, 永久, 不灭 | The quality or state of being imperishable; inability to decay or be destroyed. |  The imperishablenesses of their love will be remembered for generations.  他们不朽的爱情将被世世代代铭记。 | 近义词:  immortality, eternity, permanence | 反义词: mortality, transience, ephemerality | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From imperishable + -ness.  Imperishable from im- (not) + perishable. Perishable from Old French perissable, from perir “to perish,” from Latin perire “to pass away, be ruined, perish."
