freewheeler:/ˈfriːˌwiːlər/| n.|自由自在的人，独立自主的人，我行我素的人|A person who acts independently and without constraint, often disregarding rules or conventions.|He's a bit of a freewheeler, doing whatever he pleases. 他有点我行我素，想做什么就做什么。|Synonyms: nonconformist, maverick, individualist, independent|Antonyms: conformist, follower|noun

freewheeler:/ˈfriːˌwiːlər/| n.|滑行自行车，飞轮自行车|A bicycle with a freewheel mechanism, allowing the pedals to remain stationary while the bicycle is in motion.|He rode his freewheeler down the hill. 他骑着滑行自行车下了山。|Synonyms: coaster bike|Antonyms: fixed-gear bicycle|noun
