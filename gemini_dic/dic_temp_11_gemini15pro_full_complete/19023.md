beta-carotene:/ˈbeɪtə ˈkærəˌtiːn/| n. |β-胡萝卜素|A red-orange pigment found in plants and fruits, especially carrots and colorful vegetables. It is a precursor to vitamin A, meaning the body can convert it into vitamin A. |Carrots are a good source of beta-carotene. 胡萝卜是β-胡萝卜素的良好来源。|Synonyms: provitamin A |Antonyms: None|noun


beta-carotene:/ˈbeɪtə ˈkærəˌtiːn/| n. |β-胡萝卜素|Beta-carotene is an important antioxidant that can protect the body from damage caused by free radicals. β-胡萝卜素是一种重要的抗氧化剂，可以保护身体免受自由基的损害。|Synonyms: provitamin A |Antonyms: None|noun
