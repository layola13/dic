disembowel:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/ /dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/
verb
取出内脏；剖腹
To remove the internal organs of, especially from a person or animal.
The rebels disemboweled their prisoners. 反叛者将他们的囚犯开膛破肚。

disembowel:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/ /dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/
verb
掏出…的内部；除去主要部分
To remove the essential contents of; gut.
Critics disemboweled the author’s latest novel. 批评家们把这位作者最新小说的主要内容都掏空了。

disemboweled:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəld/ /dɪsɪmˈbaʊəld/
adjective
被取出内脏的；被剖腹的
Having had the internal organs removed.
The disemboweled body was found in a ditch.  被剖腹的尸体在沟里被发现。


disembowel:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/ /dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/
Etymology:  From dis- (expressing separation, reversal) + embowel (to remove the bowels of).
Word Origin: Late Middle English: from dis- + embowel.
