orometer:/ɔːˈrɒmɪtər/ /ɔːˈrɑːmɪtər/
noun
高度计；测高仪
An instrument for measuring altitude, especially above sea level.
The orometer indicated an altitude of 3,000 meters.  高度计显示海拔3000米。
The pilot checked the orometer to confirm their height. 飞行员查看了高度计以确认他们的高度。
Synonyms: altimeter, altitude meter, hypsometer
Antonyms: depth gauge, bathymeter
n.
