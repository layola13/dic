oldly:/ˈoʊldli/| adv.|老式地，过时地|In a way that is characteristic of old age or something old; in an old-fashioned way.  It describes actions or states of being in a manner reminiscent of the past, often implying outdatedness or a sense of age.|He spoke oldly, using phrases no one had heard in years. 他说话老气横秋，用的都是多年没人听过的词句。|The house was oldly furnished with antique furniture. 这栋房子老式地布置着古董家具。|近义词: archaically, antiquatedly, |反义词: modernly, contemporarily|副词
