lackluster:/ˈlækˌlʌstər/| adj.|缺乏光泽的；黯淡的；无生气的|Lacking in vitality, force, or conviction; uninspired or uninspiring.|The performance was lackluster and failed to impress the audience. 表演缺乏生气，未能给观众留下深刻印象。|Synonyms: dull, drab, lifeless, flat, insipid, uninspired, mediocre|Antonyms: brilliant, vibrant, dynamic, exciting, stimulating|adjective
