communion:/kəˈmjuːnjən/ | n. |交流； comunión (Spanish), comunhão (Portuguese) |the sharing or exchanging of intimate thoughts and feelings, especially when the exchange is on a mental or spiritual level.  A close relationship with someone or something.  The act of receiving Holy Communion.|例句：They felt a deep communion with nature. 他们感受到与大自然深深的交流。

例句：He experienced a sense of communion with his fellow worshippers. 他感受到与其他礼拜者之间的心灵相通。


近义词：fellowship, intimacy, rapport, closeness, togetherness, bond, union |反义词: isolation, separation, distance, estrangement |词性: noun

communions:/kəˈmjuːnjənz/ | n. |交流（复数）；圣餐（复数）| Plural form of communion. |例句：The various communions within the Christian faith often hold different beliefs. 基督教信仰中的不同派别通常持有不同的信仰。

例句：The church held several communions throughout the Easter weekend. 教堂在整个复活节周末举行了多次圣餐仪式。

近义词：fellowships, intimacies, rapports |反义词: isolations, separations, distances |词性: noun
