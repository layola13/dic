fizzily:/ˈfɪzɪli/| adv.|嘶嘶地，冒泡地|In a manner characterized by fizzing or effervescence.|The soda fizzily poured into the glass. 苏打水嘶嘶地倒入杯中。|近义词：effervescently, sparklingly|反义词:flatly|词性:副词
fizz:/fɪz/| n. v.|嘶嘶声，起泡|A hissing or sputtering sound; to make a hissing or sputtering sound.|The fizz of the soda was refreshing. 苏打水的嘶嘶声令人神清气爽.  The tablet fizzed in the water. 药片在水中嘶嘶作响。|近义词：hiss, bubble, effervesce|反义词:-|词性:名词，动词

词源:  From the verb "fizz," which is imitative of the sound.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fisōną (“to break wind”).
