lightly:/ˈlaɪtli/| adv.|轻轻地；轻微地；少量地；轻松地；草率地|With little weight or force; gently.  Also, to a small degree; slightly.  Additionally, in a cheerful or carefree way; airily.  Finally, without proper care or seriousness; superficially.|She touched his arm lightly. 她轻轻地碰了碰他的手臂。

She lightly dusted the furniture. 她轻轻地掸了掸家具上的灰尘。

He travels lightly, carrying only a small backpack. 他轻装旅行，只带了一个小背包。

He took the criticism too lightly. 他对批评太不在意了。|近义词：gently, softly, slightly, mildly, superficially|反义词：heavily, firmly, strongly, seriously, thoroughly|词性:副词
