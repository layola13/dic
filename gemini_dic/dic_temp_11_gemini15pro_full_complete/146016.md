overdraft: /ˈəʊvədrɑːft/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərdræft/ (US)| n.|透支；透支额度|An overdraft is an agreement with your bank that allows you to spend more money than you have in your account, up to a certain limit. |I had to use my overdraft to pay the rent this month. 我这个月不得不动用透支额度来付房租。

My bank offers an interest-free overdraft for students. 我的银行为学生提供免息透支服务。

|Synonyms: overdrawn balance, deficit, shortfall|Antonyms: credit, surplus, positive balance|noun
