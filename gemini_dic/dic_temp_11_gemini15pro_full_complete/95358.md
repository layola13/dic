incerative: /ɪnˈsɛrətɪv/ | adj. | 不确定的，犹豫的，不果断的 | Not assertive or confident; hesitant or unsure. |  He spoke in an incerative tone, unsure of his facts. 他说话的语气犹豫不决，对自己的事实没有把握。|  She gave an incerative nod, not fully committing to the plan. 她犹豫地点了点头，没有完全承诺这个计划。 | 近义词: hesitant, tentative, unsure, doubtful, vacillating | 反义词: assertive, confident, decisive, resolute | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *incertivus, from Latin incertus (“uncertain”), from in- (“not”) + certus (“certain”).
