likin:/ˈlaɪkɪn/| n.| 喜好，嗜好|A liking for something is a feeling of enjoyment and pleasure that you get from it.  It can also refer to a preference or fondness for something.|She had a liking for strong coffee. 她喜欢浓咖啡。|近义词：fondness, preference, taste, inclination, penchant|反义词：dislike, aversion, distaste|noun
