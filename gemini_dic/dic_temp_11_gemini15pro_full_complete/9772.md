antonym:/ˈæntənɪm/
n.|反义词|A word that expresses a meaning opposed to the meaning of another word, in which case the two words are antonyms of each other.| "Big" and "small" are antonyms. “大”和“小”是反义词。|opposite word|synonym|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀντί (antí, “against, opposite”) + ὄνομα (ónoma, “name”).
