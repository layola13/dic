arrearage:/əˈrɪərɪdʒ/| n.|欠款，拖欠的款项；积欠的债务|Something that is overdue or unpaid, especially a debt or other financial obligation.|The company is struggling with a large arrearage of unpaid bills.  这家公司正努力应对大量未付账单的积欠。|The tenant was evicted for arrearage of rent.  该租户因拖欠租金而被驱逐。|synonyms: arrears, debt, default, liability|antonyms: credit, asset|noun
