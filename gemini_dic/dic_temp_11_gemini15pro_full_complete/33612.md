cinchonine:/ˈsɪŋkənɪn/ | n. |金奎宁，辛可宁|A white crystalline alkaloid, C₁₉H₂₂N₂O, obtained from cinchona bark and used in medicine, especially formerly, as an antimalarial and antipyretic.|Cinchonine was once a popular treatment for malaria. 金奎宁曾经是治疗疟疾的常用药物。|Synonyms: quinidine|Antonyms: None|Noun
