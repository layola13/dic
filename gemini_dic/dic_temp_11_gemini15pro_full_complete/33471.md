chylifaction:/ˌkaɪlɪˈfækʃən/ | n. | 乳糜化；乳糜形成 | The process of converting food into chyle (a milky fluid consisting of emulsified fats and lymph) in the small intestine.  The formation of chyle in the intestines during digestion. | Example 1: The chylifaction of fats is essential for their absorption into the lymphatic system.  脂肪的乳糜化对于它们吸收到淋巴系统至关重要。

Example 2:  Problems with chylifaction can lead to malabsorption of nutrients. 乳糜化问题会导致营养吸收不良。| 近义词: emulsification | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

chyle:/kaɪl/ | n. | 乳糜 | A milky fluid consisting of emulsified fats and lymph, formed in the small intestine during digestion and absorbed by the lacteals. | Example 1: Chyle is transported through the lymphatic system before entering the bloodstream. 乳糜在进入血液之前通过淋巴系统运输。

Example 2: The presence of chyle in the pleural cavity is known as chylothorax.  乳糜在胸膜腔中的存在被称为乳糜胸。 | 近义词: None | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
