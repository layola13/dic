lithophytic:/ˌlɪθəˈfɪtɪk/| adj.|石生的，岩生的|Growing on or inhabiting rocks.|例句：Lithophytic orchids are adapted to survive in harsh, rocky environments.  石生兰花适应在恶劣的岩石环境中生存。

例句：Many lithophytic plants have specialized roots for clinging to rock surfaces. 许多石生植物有专门的根系来附着在岩石表面。|近义词：rock-dwelling, saxicolous|反义词: terrestrial, aquatic|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek λίθος (líthos, “stone”) + φυτικός (phutikós, “of plants”).
