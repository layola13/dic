nondepartmentally:/ˌnɒndɪˌpɑːtmɛntəli/
adverb
非部门地，不属于任何部门地
Not connected with or restricted to any particular department.
The funds were administered nondepartmentally. 这些资金是由非部门机构管理的。
The project was overseen nondepartmentally to ensure fairness.  这个项目由非部门机构监督以确保公平。
近义词: independently, autonomously
反义词: departmentally
词性: adverb
