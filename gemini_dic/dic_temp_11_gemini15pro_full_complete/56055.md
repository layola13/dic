docetic:/doʊˈsɛtɪk/
adjective
幻影说的，幻影论的
Relating to or holding the docetic view that Christ's body was not human but only seemed to be so; relating to or characterized by docetism.
Docetic beliefs were declared heretical by the early Church.  幻影说的信仰被早期教会宣布为异端。
Docetism denied the true humanity of Jesus Christ. 幻影说否认耶稣基督真正的人性。
synonym:illusory, phantom, unreal, apparent
antonym:real, actual, genuine
