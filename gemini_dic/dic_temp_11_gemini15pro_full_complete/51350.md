deted:/dɪˈtɛktɪd/ |v. (past participle)|已检测到，已发现|Detected is the past participle of the verb "detect," which means to discover or notice something, especially something that is not easy to see, hear, etc. |The software detected a virus on my computer.  该软件在我的电脑上检测到病毒。| detected a problem in the system. 在系统中检测到一个问题。|近义词：discovered, found, identified, noticed, observed, perceived, sensed|反义词：missed, overlooked|verb (past participle)
