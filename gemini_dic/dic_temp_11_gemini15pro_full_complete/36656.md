collegiateness:/kəˌliːdʒiˈɪtnəs/
n.
大学生活气氛；大学精神；同学情谊
The quality of being collegiate; fellowship or spirit among college students.
The new student appreciated the collegiateness of his dorm mates.  这位新生很感激宿舍同学的友善。
He missed the collegiateness of his university days. 他怀念大学时代浓厚的同学情谊。
近义词： camaraderie, fellowship, esprit de corps
反义词： aloofness, detachment, isolation
词性: noun

Etymology: From collegiate + -ness.  Collegiate comes from college, which ultimately derives from the Latin word "collegium," meaning "society" or "association."
