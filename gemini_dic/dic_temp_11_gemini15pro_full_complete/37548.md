commons:/ˈkɑːmənz/ | n. | 公共用地；平民；下议院 | land or resources belonging to or affecting the whole of a community; the ordinary people, as distinct from those of higher rank; (in Britain) the House of Commons | The villagers grazed their cattle on the commons. 村民们在公共用地上放牧牛群。| synonyms: common land, common ground, communal land, public land | antonyms: private land, private property | noun

commons:/ˈkɑːmənz/ | n. | (大学的)食堂 | a dining hall in a college or university |  Students gathered in the commons for lunch. 学生们聚集在食堂吃午饭。 | synonyms: cafeteria, dining hall, refectory | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) private dining room | noun
