machete:/məˈʃɛti/ /məˈtʃeti/| n. |大砍刀; 粗笨的刀|A large, heavy knife with a broad blade, used for cutting vegetation or as a weapon. |例句：He used a machete to clear a path through the jungle. 他用一把大砍刀在丛林中开辟了一条道路。

例句：The machete was sharp and dangerous. 这把大砍刀锋利而危险。|近义词：knife, blade, cutlass, parang|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其功能的反面，例如) shield, protection |词性:noun
