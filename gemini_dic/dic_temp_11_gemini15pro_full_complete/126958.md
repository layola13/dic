narrow-minded: /ˈnærəʊˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 心胸狭窄的，固执己见的 | unwilling to accept or try to understand ideas that are new or different | It's narrow-minded to think that your way is the only way.  认为你的方法是唯一的方法是心胸狭窄的。 | 近义词：intolerant, bigoted, prejudiced, dogmatic | 反义词：open-minded, tolerant, broad-minded | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From narrow +‎ minded.  由narrow（狭窄的）和minded（有…思想的）组合而成.
