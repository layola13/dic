nonconscientiousness:/ˌnɒnˌkɒnʃɪˈɛnʃəsnəs/ |n.|缺乏责任心；不认真；不尽职|Lack of conscientiousness; the trait of being careless, irresponsible, and not diligent.|His nonconscientiousness led to several missed deadlines.|近义词: irresponsibility, negligence, carelessness, unreliability |反义词: conscientiousness, diligence, responsibility, meticulousness |词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
conscientiousness (noun): the quality of wishing to do one's work or duty well and thoroughly
