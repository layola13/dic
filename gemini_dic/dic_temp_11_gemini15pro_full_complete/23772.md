brawest:/ˈbrɔːɪst/ | adj. | 最勇敢的，最优秀的 |  Superlative form of "braw," meaning excellent, fine, or brave, especially in a showy or physical way.  | He was the brawest warrior in the kingdom. 他是王国里最勇敢的战士。 | The brawest knight faced the dragon. 最勇敢的骑士面对巨龙。 | 近义词: bravest, finest, most courageous, best | 反义词: weakest, most cowardly, worst | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Scots "braw," likely from French "brave," ultimately from Italian "bravo."
