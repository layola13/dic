hotel:/hoʊˈtɛl/ (US), /həʊˈtɛl/ (UK)| n. |酒店，旅馆|A building where travelers can pay for lodging and meals and other services.|We stayed at a luxury hotel during our vacation. 我们在假期期间住在一家豪华酒店。|Example: The hotel offers a wide range of amenities, including a swimming pool and a fitness center.  这家酒店提供各种各样的便利设施，包括游泳池和健身中心。|Synonyms: inn, motel, lodging, accommodation|Antonyms: home, residence|noun
