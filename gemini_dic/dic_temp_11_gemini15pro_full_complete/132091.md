noncreditor:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɛdɪtər/
n.
非债权人
a person or entity that is not a creditor; someone who has not lent money or extended credit to another party
The noncreditors were not involved in the bankruptcy proceedings. 非债权人没有参与破产程序。
Synonyms:  non-lender,  debtor (in some contexts)
Antonyms: creditor, lender
