overdoubting:/ˌoʊvərˈdaʊtɪŋ/| adj.|过度怀疑的；怀疑过度的|Experiencing excessive doubt or skepticism, often to an unnecessary or debilitating degree.  It suggests a level of doubt that goes beyond reasonable caution and can hinder action or decision-making.|He was overdoubting his abilities and missed the opportunity. 他对自己的能力过度怀疑，错失了机会。|Synonyms: overthinking, apprehensive, insecure, hesitant|Antonyms: confident, certain, resolute, trusting|adjective

overdoubting:/ˌoʊvərˈdaʊtɪŋ/| v.|过度怀疑；怀疑过度|(Rarely used as a verb) The act of doubting excessively.  It implies an active process of questioning and second-guessing, often to an extreme.|She overdoubted her instincts and made the wrong choice. 她过度怀疑自己的直觉，做了错误的选择。|Synonyms: second-guessing, questioning, agonizing, wavering|Antonyms: trusting, believing, accepting, embracing|verb
