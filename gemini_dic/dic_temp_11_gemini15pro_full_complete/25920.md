burning-out:/ˈbɜːrnɪŋ aʊt/ | adj.  | 精疲力竭的，耗尽的 | Experiencing a state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged or excessive stress. |  Many teachers are suffering from burnout. 许多老师都感到精疲力竭。 |  exhausted, worn out, spent, drained |  energetic, refreshed, invigorated | adjective

burning out:/ˈbɜːrnɪŋ aʊt/ | n. |  精疲力竭，耗尽 | The process of becoming emotionally, physically, and mentally exhausted due to prolonged or excessive stress.  | Burnout is a serious problem in many professions.  在很多职业中，精疲力竭是一个严重的问题。 | exhaustion, fatigue, breakdown | recovery, rejuvenation | noun

词源：From the verb phrase "burn out," literally meaning to stop burning because the fuel is exhausted.  Figuratively, it refers to the exhaustion of a person's energy and motivation.
