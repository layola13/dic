bursten:/ˈbɜːrstən/| v. |爆裂，胀破|To break open or apart suddenly and violently, especially as a result of internal pressure.  From the Old English *berstan, related to German bersten.  Primarily a literary or archaic past participle form of "burst". |The dam bursten and flooded the valley. 大坝决堤，淹没了山谷。|近义词：broken, ruptured, exploded|反义词:intact, whole|词性:动词(past participle)
