kissingers:/ˈkɪsɪŋərz/
noun|基辛格们；基辛格的支持者们|People who support or follow the policies of Henry Kissinger, a former US Secretary of State.|Many kissingers believed his approach to foreign policy was effective. 很多基辛格的支持者认为他的外交政策是有效的。|supporters, followers|opponents, critics|n.

kissingers:/ˈkɪsɪŋərz/
adjective|基辛格式的；基辛格主义的|Characteristic of or relating to Henry Kissinger or his political philosophy.|His kissingers approach to negotiation was praised by some and criticized by others. 他基辛格式的谈判方法受到了一些人的赞扬，也受到了一些人的批评。|Kissingerian, pragmatic|idealistic, dovish|adj.
