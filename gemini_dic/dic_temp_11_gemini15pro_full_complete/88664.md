hoarsely:/ˈhɔːrsli/ (US), /ˈhɔːsli/ (UK)| adv.|嘶哑地；粗声地|In a rough, grating voice, typically as a result of a sore throat or shouting.|He spoke hoarsely, his voice raspy from shouting all day. 他说话声音嘶哑，因为整天都在喊叫。|She coughed hoarsely, trying to clear her throat. 她嘶哑地咳嗽，试图清清嗓子。|Synonym: roughly, huskily, gratingly|Antonym: smoothly, clearly, melodically|词性:副词
