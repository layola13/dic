electioneer:/ɪˌlɛkʃəˈnɪər/ | v. | 参加竞选，拉票 | To work actively for a candidate or political party. |  The candidate spent weeks electioneering in rural areas.  这位候选人花了数周时间在农村地区进行竞选活动。| campaign, canvass, solicit votes | abstain | verb

electioneer:/ɪˌlɛkʃəˈnɪər/ | n. | 竞选者，拉票者 | A person who electioneers. | He's a seasoned electioneer, known for his persuasive speeches. 他是一位经验丰富的竞选者，以其有说服力的演讲而闻名。| campaigner, canvasser | non-participant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From election + -eer (agent suffix). Ultimately from Latin *electionem (nom. electio), "choice, selection," from electus, past participle of eligere, "to choose out, select."
