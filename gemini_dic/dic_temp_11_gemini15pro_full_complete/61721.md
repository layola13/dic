emotionally:/ɪˈmoʊʃənəli/ /ɪˈməʊʃənəli/| adv.|情感上，情绪上|In a way that relates to emotions. |She was emotionally drained after the ordeal. 她经历了那场磨难后精疲力竭。|近义词：sentimentally, passionately|反义词：rationally, logically|词性:副词

词源: From emotion + -ally.  "Emotion" comes from Old French "emouvoir" meaning "to stir up, excite," ultimately from Latin "emovere" meaning "to move out, remove, agitate."
