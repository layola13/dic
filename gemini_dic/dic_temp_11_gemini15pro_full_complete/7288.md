angst-ridden:/ˈæŋstˌrɪdn/| adj.|充满焦虑的，焦虑不安的|Experiencing or expressing a lot of anxiety, worry, and unhappiness.|She wrote angst-ridden poetry in her teenage years. 她十几岁时写了很多充满焦虑的诗歌。|The protagonist is an angst-ridden teenager trying to find his place in the world.  主角是一个充满焦虑的青少年，试图找到自己在世界上的位置。|近义词：anxious, worried, apprehensive, distressed, tormented|反义词：calm, peaceful, serene, content|词性:形容词
Etymology: From German *Angst* (fear, anxiety) + English *-ridden* (suffixed to nouns to indicate being dominated or oppressed by something).
