bullocks:/ˈbʊləks/| n.|废话；胡说；(表示愤怒、惊讶、不耐烦等)；阉牛|A dated, chiefly British term used to express disbelief, annoyance, or contempt.  It can also refer to castrated bulls, especially young ones.|例句：Bullocks! I don't believe a word of it! 胡说！我一个字都不信！

例句：He's been telling me bullocks about his qualifications. 他一直在胡说他的资历。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, baloney, crap|反义词:truth|词性:名词
