officerlike: /ˈɒfɪsəlaɪk/ (US) /ˈɒfɪsəlaɪk/ (UK)| adj. | 像军官的；有军官风度的 | Resembling or characteristic of an officer, especially in behavior or appearance. | He had an officerlike bearing, standing tall and straight. 他站得笔挺，很有军官风度。|  The young man's officerlike demeanor impressed the recruiters.  这个年轻人的军官风度给招聘人员留下了深刻印象。 | 近义词: soldierly, military, disciplined, formal | 反义词: sloppy, casual, undisciplined, informal | 词性: 形容词

词源: officer + -like.  "Officer" comes from Old French "officier," ultimately from Latin "officium" meaning "duty, service." "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
