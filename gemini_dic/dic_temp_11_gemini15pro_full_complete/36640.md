collector:/kəˈlɛktər/| n.|收藏家，收集者|A person who collects things of a particular type, professionally or as a hobby. | He's a collector of antique clocks. 他是一位古董钟收藏家。|Synonym: accumulator, gatherer|Antonym: distributor, dispenser|noun

collector:/kəˈlɛktər/| n.|收税员，收账员|A person who collects payments, taxes, or debts. | The tax collector will be here next week.  税务员下周会来。|Synonym: receiver|Antonym: payer|noun
