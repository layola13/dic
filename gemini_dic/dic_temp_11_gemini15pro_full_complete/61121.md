eloin:/ɪˈlɔɪn/ | vt. | 使远离，使疏远；隔离 | To remove or distance (someone or something) from a place or person. |例句：The judge eloined the child from her abusive parents. 法官将孩子与其虐待她的父母隔离。

例句：He was accused of eloining funds from the company. 他被指控挪用公司资金。|近义词：remove, separate, banish, exile, estrange |反义词：unite, join, reconcile |词性：动词

词源: From Old French *esloignier (Modern French éloigner), from Late Latin *elongare "to remove, put at a distance," from Latin longe "far off" + agere "to do, drive, lead, act."
