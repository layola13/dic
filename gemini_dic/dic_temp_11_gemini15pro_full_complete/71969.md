flimflammeries:/ˈflɪmˌflæməriz/
noun
欺骗；花招；胡言乱语
nonsense, deceitful or misleading talk or behavior
The salesman's flimflammeries finally caught up with him, and he was arrested for fraud.  这个推销员的骗局最终让他落网，他因诈骗被捕。
Near synonyms:  tricks, shenanigans, deception, chicanery, fraud, scams
Antonyms:  honesty, truthfulness, sincerity, genuineness
