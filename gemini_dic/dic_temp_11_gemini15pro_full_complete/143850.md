outplans:/ˌaʊtˈplænz/
verb
制定比...更周密的计划;  胜过…的计划
to make more or better plans than someone else
例句：She completely outplanned her competitors and secured the contract. 她彻底胜过竞争对手的计划，拿下了合同。

例句：He outplanned everyone in the chess tournament and won first place. 他在国际象棋比赛中计划胜过所有人，赢得了第一名。

近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, outstrategize
反义词：be outplanned, be outsmarted
