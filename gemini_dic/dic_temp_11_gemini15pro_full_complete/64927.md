estafet:/ˈestəfɛt/ | n. | 接力赛；接力棒；接力传递 | A race in which several team members take turns running or swimming a part of the distance carrying a baton.  Also refers to the baton itself or the act of passing it on. |  He ran the first leg of the estafet. 他跑了接力赛的第一棒。 | relay race, relay, baton | N/A | noun
