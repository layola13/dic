anjelica:/ænˈdʒɛlɪkə/ | n. |当归；欧白芷|A tall aromatic plant (Angelica archangelica) of the parsley family, with large leaves and greenish-white flowers. The stems and roots are candied and used in confectionery, and the oil is used to flavor liqueurs.|Angelica is a common ingredient in many traditional European desserts. 当归是许多欧洲传统甜点中的常见成分。| Angelica grows wild in damp meadows. 欧白芷生长在潮湿的草地上。|Synonyms: angelica plant, garden angelica, wild celery|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
