adulterators:/əˈdʌltəˌreɪtərz/| n. |掺杂者，伪造者|Someone who adulterates something, meaning they debase it by adding inferior materials or elements, especially to food or drink.|The FDA is cracking down on adulterators who sell contaminated food products.  美国食品药品监督管理局正在严厉打击销售受污染食品的掺假者。|Synonyms: falsifiers, debasers, corruptors|Antonyms: purifiers, refiners|noun
