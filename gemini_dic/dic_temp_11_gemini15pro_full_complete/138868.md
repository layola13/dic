nursing: /ˈnɜːsɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɜːrsɪŋ/ (US)| n. |护理；照料；哺乳|The act of caring for someone, especially someone who is sick or injured.  It can also refer to breastfeeding.|例句：She studied nursing at university. 她在大学学习护理。|例句：The nursing staff were very kind and attentive. 护理人员非常友善和细心。|近义词：care, tending, ministering|反义词：neglect|词性：名词

nursing: /ˈnɜːsɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɜːrsɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |护理的；哺乳的|Relating to the profession of nursing or to breastfeeding.|例句：She has a nursing degree. 她有护理学位。|例句：The baby was having its nursing time. 婴儿正在吃奶。|近义词：caring, nurturing|反义词：neglectful|词性：形容词
