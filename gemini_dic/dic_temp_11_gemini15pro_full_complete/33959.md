cis's:/sɪsɪz/ | n. | 顺式异构体的 | cis isomers; molecules with identical formulas but different structural arrangements due to the fixed positions of atoms or groups around a double bond or ring, where similar atoms or groups are on the same side of the bond or ring. |  The cis isomer is often less stable than the trans isomer. 顺式异构体通常比反式异构体不稳定。|  Cis and trans isomers have different physical and chemical properties. 顺式和反式异构体具有不同的物理和化学性质。 | isomer, Z isomer | trans | noun
