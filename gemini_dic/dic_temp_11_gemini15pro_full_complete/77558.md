gean:/ˈɡiːən/| n. |野生甜樱桃，欧洲酸樱桃|A wild or cultivated sweet cherry, especially Prunus avium. | The gean trees were laden with ripe fruit. 野生甜樱桃树上果实累累。 | 近义词:wild cherry, mazzard | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun

词源: From Old Northern French *guisne, from a Vulgar Latin form of Latin *avisnia, from avis “bird”. Related to Old Occitan aguisina, Old French guisine, French guigne. Doublet of *avisnia.
