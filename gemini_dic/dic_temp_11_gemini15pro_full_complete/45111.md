cuckooing:/ˈkʊkuːɪŋ/
| n. |杜鹃叫；鸠占鹊巢；（俚语）非法侵占他人住宅|The act of a cuckoo laying its eggs in another bird's nest, or (slang) the act of taking over someone's home by force or coercion.|The cuckooing of their elderly neighbour was a shocking crime. 他们的老邻居被鸠占鹊巢，这是一起令人震惊的罪行。|近义词：home invasion, exploitation, squatting|反义词: protection, safeguarding|词性:名词

cuckooing:/ˈkʊkuːɪŋ/
| v. |（杜鹃）下蛋于别巢；（俚语）非法侵占他人住宅|To lay one's eggs in another bird's nest, like a cuckoo, or (slang) to take over someone's home by force or coercion.|Drug dealers were cuckooing vulnerable people's homes. 毒贩正在鸠占鹊巢，侵占弱势群体的家园。|近义词：intrude, usurp, hijack|反义词: vacate, relinquish|词性:动词
