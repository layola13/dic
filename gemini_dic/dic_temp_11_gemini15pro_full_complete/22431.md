booked-up:/bʊkt ˈʌp/
adjective
预订满的；被预约满了
fully reserved or engaged; having no availability for bookings or appointments.
The hotel is booked-up for the weekend.  这家酒店周末已被预订满。
All the flights to London are booked-up for the holiday.  假期所有飞往伦敦的航班都订满了。
近义词:full, reserved, sold out
反义词:available, vacant
词性:adjective
