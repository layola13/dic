hoodlum:/ˈhuːdləm/ (US), /ˈhʊːdləm/ (UK)| n.| 流氓，暴徒，恶棍 |A young street rough; a thug or gangster. | The hoodlums vandalized the park after dark.  那些流氓天黑后破坏了公园。|synonyms: thug, gangster, ruffian, hooligan, delinquent| antonyms: law-abider, citizen, model citizen| noun


Etymology:
Origin uncertain, possibly from German *Hudilump, a blend of *Huder (“rag”) and Lump (“rascal, scoundrel”). First recorded in American English in the 1870s.
