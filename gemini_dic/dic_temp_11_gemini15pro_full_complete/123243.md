monocultures: /ˈmɒnəˌkʌltʃəz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəˌkʌltʃərz/ (US)| n. |单一栽培，单一文化|The agricultural practice of producing or growing a single crop, plant, or livestock species, variety, or breed in a field or farming system at a time.| Large-scale monocultures can deplete soil nutrients.  大规模的单一种植会耗尽土壤养分。

Monocultures can make crops more susceptible to disease. 单一栽培会使作物更容易患病。|近义词：monocropping|反义词: polyculture, diversified farming|Noun
