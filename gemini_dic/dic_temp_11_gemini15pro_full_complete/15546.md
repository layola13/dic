baggily:/ˈbæɡɪli/| adv.|松垮地；袋状地|in a loose and baggy way.  The trousers hung baggily on his thin legs.  这条裤子在他细腿上松松垮垮地挂着。|近义词：loosely, slackly|反义词：tightly, snugly|词性:副词
Etymology: From baggy + -ly.  Baggy itself comes from bag, referring to the resemblance to a loose sack.
