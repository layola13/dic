lustwort:/ˈlʌstwɜːrt/ | n. | 生石花，露珠草 | A succulent plant of the genus *Mesembryanthemum*, native to southern Africa, with fleshy leaves and daisy-like flowers.  It is also known as ice plant or stone plant.  Some species are used in traditional medicine. |  The lustwort in her garden thrived in the dry conditions.  她花园里的生石花在干燥的环境下茁壮成长。 | Synonyms: ice plant, stone plant, Mesembryanthemum | Antonyms: N/A | noun
