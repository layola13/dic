dollship:/ˈdɑːlˌʃɪp/ |n.|玩偶屋，玩具屋；微型复制品；袖珍模型|A dollship is a miniature house, often furnished, that is used as a toy or a decorative object. It can also refer to a miniature replica of a larger object, like a ship or a building.|She spent hours arranging the furniture in her dollship. 她花了几个小时布置她玩偶屋里的家具。|The museum displayed a dollship of the Queen Mary. 博物馆展出了一艘玛丽女王号的微型复制品。|synonyms: miniature house, doll house, model, replica|antonyms: full-scale model, real house|noun
