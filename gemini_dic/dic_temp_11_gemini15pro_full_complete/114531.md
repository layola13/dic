malamic:/məˈlæmɪk/
adjective
恶意的，怀有恶意的
Characterized by ill will; wishing evil or harm to another or others.
He gave her a malamic glare. 他恶狠狠地瞪了她一眼。
近义词:malevolent, malicious, spiteful, hostile, venomous
反义词:benevolent, kind, friendly, benign
词性:adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *malamicus, from Latin male (badly) + amicus (friend).  Essentially meaning "bad friend".
