game-planning:/ˈɡeɪm ˌplænɪŋ/
noun
游戏策划；比赛计划；战略规划
The process of developing a strategy or plan for a game or competition.
The coach spent hours on game-planning for the upcoming match. 教练花了几个小时为即将到来的比赛制定比赛计划。
The team's success was due in part to their meticulous game-planning. 该队的成功部分归功于他们细致的比赛计划。
Synonyms: strategizing, planning, scheming, plotting
Antonyms: improvising, winging it
