murenoid:/mjʊəˈrɔɪnɔɪd/ | adj. | 肽聚糖的，胞壁质的 | Relating to or consisting of murein, a peptidoglycan that forms the cell walls of many bacteria. |  Bacterial cell walls are typically murenoid structures. 细菌细胞壁通常是肽聚糖结构。| The murenoid layer is essential for bacterial survival. 肽聚糖层对细菌的生存至关重要。 |Synonyms: peptidoglycan, bacterial cell wall | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "protoplasmic" relating to the interior of the cell) | adjective
