excitingest:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋɪst/ | adj. | 最令人兴奋的 |  While grammatically incorrect (the superlative of "exciting" is formed with "most" - "most exciting"), "excitingest" is sometimes used informally for emphasis. It represents a non-standard form. | That was the excitingest roller coaster ride ever! 那是有史以来最刺激的过山车！| Near Synonyms: most exciting, thrillingest (also non-standard), most thrilling | Antonyms: least exciting, most boring, dullest | Word Class: Adjective
