defly:/ˈdɛfli/| adv.|灵巧地，熟练地|In a deft and quick manner; skillfully.|He defly caught the falling vase. 他熟练地接住了掉落的花瓶。|近义词：skillfully, adeptly, nimbly, expertly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly|词性:副词
Etymology: From *deft* + *-ly*.  *Deft* comes from Middle English *deft*, from Old English *gedæfte* "mild, gentle, meek," later "fitting, suitable," then "skillful, apt." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *daftjaz* "fitting, appropriate," from Proto-Indo-European *dhabh-* "to fit, join."
