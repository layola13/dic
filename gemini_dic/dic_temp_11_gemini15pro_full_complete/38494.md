conchyliology: /ˌkɒŋkɪliˈɒlədʒi/ (UK) /ˌkɑːŋkɪliˈɑːlədʒi/ (US)
| n. | 贝壳学 | The study of shells, especially mollusk shells.  | He devoted his life to conchyliology, amassing a vast collection of shells from around the world. 他毕生致力于贝壳学研究，收集了来自世界各地的大量贝壳。| 近义词: malacology | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *conchyliologia*, from  Latin *conchylium* (shell, shellfish) +  -logia from Greek -λογία (-logia, study of).
