deciduity:/dɪˈsɪdʒuɪti/ | n. | 落叶性；脱落性 | The quality of falling off at a particular season or stage of growth, as of leaves, petals, or teeth. |  The deciduity of the maple tree's leaves is a hallmark of autumn. 枫树叶子的落叶性是秋天的标志。|  abscission, shedding | persistence, evergreenness | noun
