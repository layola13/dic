crab:/kræb/| n. v.|螃蟹, 螃蟹(v.)|A crustacean with a broad flattened shell, five pairs of legs, and the front pair modified as pincers. (n.) To fish for crabs. (v.)|例句：The crab scuttled sideways across the sand. 螃蟹横着穿过沙滩。|例句：We went crabbing on the pier. 我们在码头上钓螃蟹。|近义词：crustacean|反义词:|n. v.
