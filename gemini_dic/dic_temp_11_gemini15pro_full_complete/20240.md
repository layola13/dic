Birkenau: /ˌbɜːrkənˌaʊ/ | n. | 比克瑙 | Birkenau was a Nazi concentration and extermination camp, part of the Auschwitz complex, located in Poland. It was the site of the mass murder of primarily Jewish people during the Holocaust. |  Many people were transported to Birkenau by train, unaware of their fate. 许多人被火车运送到比克瑙，并不知道他们的命运。

Birkenau became a symbol of the horrors of the Holocaust. 比克瑙成了大屠杀恐怖的象征。 | 近义词: Auschwitz (often used interchangeably although technically Birkenau was a part of the Auschwitz complex) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a place name representing such atrocities.  One could consider concepts like "sanctuary," "refuge," or "freedom" as antonymous concepts.) | 词性: 名词
