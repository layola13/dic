lucidity:/luːˈsɪdəti/| n.|清醒；明晰；清晰易懂|The lucidity of her writing makes her work accessible to a wide audience. 她的写作清晰易懂，使她的作品更容易被广大读者接受。|例句：He spoke with remarkable lucidity. 他讲话异常清晰。|例句：The lucidity of the argument impressed the judges.  论证的清晰性给评委们留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：clarity, clearness, transparency|反义词：vagueness, obscurity, confusion|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *luciditas*, from lucidus (“bright, clear, shining”), from lux (“light”).
