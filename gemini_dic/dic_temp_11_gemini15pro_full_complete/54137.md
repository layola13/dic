disenfranchiser:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfræntʃaɪzər/
|n.|剥夺公民权者；剥夺权利者|Someone who deprives others of a right or privilege, especially the right to vote.|The disenfranchiers sought to suppress the voices of the minority.|近义词：oppressor, tyrant|反义词：liberator, emancipator|词性: noun


disenfranchise:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfræntʃaɪz/
|vt.|剥夺（某人）的权利；剥夺公民权|To deprive someone of a right or privilege, especially the right to vote.|The new law disenfranchised many poor citizens.|近义词：deprive, dispossess|反义词：empower, enfranchise|词性: verb
