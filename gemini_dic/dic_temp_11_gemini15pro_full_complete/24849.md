brownings:/ˈbraʊnɪŋz/
n.
褐变; 焦化物; 褐色的食物
the process of becoming brown, typically through cooking or decay.  The brown products of such a process.
例句：The brownings on the toast added a nice flavor. 烤面包上的褐变增添了美味。

例句：The chef checked the brownings on the meat to ensure it was cooked properly. 厨师检查了肉的褐变情况，以确保它煮熟了。

近义词：browning, scorching, charring, roasting
反义词：blanching

词性：名词 (Noun)
