canned:/kænd/
adjective
罐装的；录音的；预先录制的
preserved in a sealed can or jar.
例句：We had canned peaches for dessert. 我们甜点吃了罐装桃子。

例句：The laughter on the TV show sounded canned.  电视节目里的笑声听起来是预先录制的。

近义词：tinned, preserved, bottled
反义词：fresh, homemade
词性：adjective

canned:/kænd/
动词（can的过去分词）
装罐; 解雇; 录音
past participle of can
例句：The factory canned the peaches at their peak ripeness. 工厂在桃子最成熟的时候把它们装罐。

例句：He was canned from his job for being late too often. 他因为经常迟到而被解雇。

近义词：dismissed, fired, sacked (for "fired") recorded (for "recorded")
反义词：hired, employed (for "fired")
词性：verb (past participle)
