obstructors:/əbˈstrʌktərz/
n.
阻碍者，妨碍者
Those who obstruct or block progress or passage.
The obstructors prevented the bill from passing in the legislature.  阻碍者阻止了该法案在立法机关通过。
The obstructors parked their cars to block the road. 阻碍者停车挡住了道路。

近义词:impeders, hinderers, blockers, opponents, antagonists
反义词:supporters, helpers, facilitators, promoters

词性:noun (plural)

词源: From the verb "obstruct" + "-or" (agent suffix).  "Obstruct" comes from Latin "obstruere,"  from "ob-" (against) + "struere" (to build).
