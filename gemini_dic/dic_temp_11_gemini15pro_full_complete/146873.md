overinfluenced:/ˌoʊvərˈɪnflʊənst/
adjective
过度受影响的
Excessively influenced by someone or something; having one's opinions or actions shaped too strongly by an external force.
He made some overinfluenced decisions based on his friend's advice. 他根据朋友的建议做出了一些过度受影响的决定。
The young artist's style was overinfluenced by her mentor.  这位年轻艺术家的风格过度受其导师的影响。
近义词: dominated, swayed, controlled
反义词: independent, autonomous, self-directed
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "influence" (verb meaning "to affect or change") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
