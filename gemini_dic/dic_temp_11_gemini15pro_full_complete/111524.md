loathers:/ˈloʊðərz/| n.|憎恶者；厌恶者|People who strongly dislike something or someone. Those who feel intense aversion or disgust.|例句：The loathers of the new policy protested vehemently.  新政策的反对者强烈抗议。

例句：He is one of the most vocal loathers of modern art. 他是最直言不讳的现代艺术憎恶者之一。|近义词：haters, despisers, abominators, detestors|反义词：lovers, admirers, devotees|词性:Noun (plural)

loathe:/loʊð/| v.|憎恶；厌恶|To feel intense dislike or disgust for something or someone.|例句：I loathe hypocrisy. 我厌恶虚伪。

例句：She loathes spiders. 她讨厌蜘蛛。|近义词：hate, detest, despise, abhor|反义词：love, adore, cherish|词性:Verb
