massoret:/ˈmæsəˌrɛt/| n.|马索拉学者，马索拉文本的编辑者|A Jewish scholar of the 6th–10th centuries AD who worked to reproduce the original text of the Hebrew Bible, adding vowels and accents.|The massoretes meticulously copied and preserved the Hebrew Bible.| 近义词: scribe, copyist| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "corrupter" in a figurative sense)|词性:noun
