dissipationless:/ˌdɪsɪˈpeɪʃənlɪs/
adjective
无耗散的
Characterized by the absence of dissipation, meaning energy is conserved and not lost to heat, friction, or other irreversible processes.
A dissipationless current flows with no resistance.  无耗散电流无阻力地流动。
Superconductors exhibit dissipationless flow of electricity. 超导体表现出无耗散的电流。
Synonyms: non-dissipative, conservative, lossless
Antonyms: dissipative, lossy
