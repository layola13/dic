gaus:/ɡaʊs/| n.|高斯 (磁感应强度的单位)|A unit of magnetic induction, equal to one maxwell per square centimeter.  It is named after the German mathematician and physicist Carl Friedrich Gauss.|The magnetic field strength of the Earth is about 0.5 gaus.|近义词: maxwell per square centimeter|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
