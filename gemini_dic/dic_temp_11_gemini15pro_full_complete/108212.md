laudative:/ˈlɔːdətɪv/| adj.|赞美的，赞扬的|Expressing or conveying praise.|Her laudative remarks about the project boosted the team's morale. 她对这个项目的赞扬提高了团队的士气。|The principal gave a laudative speech at the graduation ceremony. 校长在毕业典礼上发表了赞扬的讲话。|近义词：complimentary, praising, eulogistic|反义词：critical, derogatory, disparaging|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *laudativus*, from laudatus (past participle of laudare ‘to praise’), from laus, laud- ‘praise’.
