mafias:/ˈmɑːfiəz/| n. |黑手党，黑帮|Plural form of mafia, referring to multiple organized crime groups.  It refers to more than one Mafia organization, or to Mafia-like organizations.|The mafias of several countries cooperated in drug trafficking. 几个国家的黑手党在贩毒方面进行了合作。|近义词：gangs, syndicates, mobs|反义词: law enforcement|Noun
