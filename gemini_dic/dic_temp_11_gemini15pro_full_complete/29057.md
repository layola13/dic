caruncle:/ˈkɑːrʌŋkəl/ | n. | 肉垂，小瘤 | A small, fleshy outgrowth, typically red or pink, often near the eye or beak of a bird or other animal.  It can also refer to similar structures in humans, such as the lacrimal caruncle. | The turkey's caruncle turned bright red during courtship. 火鸡的肉垂在求偶期间变成了鲜红色。| Synonym: wattle, comb | Antonym:  (No direct antonym) | noun

caruncle:/ˈkɑːrʌŋkəl/ | n. | (植物) 种阜 | A small protuberance or appendage on a seed. | The caruncle on the castor bean aids in its dispersal. 蓖麻籽上的种阜有助于其传播。 | Synonym: aril, elaiosome | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
