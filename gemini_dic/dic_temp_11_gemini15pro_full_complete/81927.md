grilly:/ˈɡrɪli/| adj.|烧烤的，烤的; (非正式)令人不快的，讨厌的| Cooked on a grill; (informal) unpleasant, disagreeable.|例句：The grilly smell of barbecue filled the air. 烧烤的香味弥漫在空气中。

例句：He gave me a grilly look. 他给了我一个令人讨厌的眼神。|近义词：grilled, barbecued (for cooking sense); unpleasant, nasty (for informal sense)|反义词: pleasant, agreeable (for informal sense)|词性:形容词

词源: From "grill" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Grill" comes from Old French "greille" (grid, grating).
