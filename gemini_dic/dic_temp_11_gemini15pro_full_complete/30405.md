centinody: /ˌsɛntɪˈnoʊdi/
n.
一百周年纪念; 百年庆典
A centinody is the hundredth anniversary of a significant event.
The university celebrated its centinody with a series of lectures and events.  大学以一系列讲座和活动庆祝其百年校庆。
Synonyms: centenary, centennial
