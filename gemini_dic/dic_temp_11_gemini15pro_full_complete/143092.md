outbreathe:/ˌaʊtˈbriːð/
verb
呼出; 吐气
to breathe out; exhale.
The yogi instructed the class to slowly outbreathe. 瑜伽教练指导全班慢慢呼气。
She outbreathed a sigh of relief. 她吐了口气，如释重负。
synonyms: exhale, breathe out
antonyms: inhale, breathe in
词性: 动词
