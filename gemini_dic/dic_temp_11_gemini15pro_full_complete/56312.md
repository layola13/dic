doghouse:/ˈdɒɡhaʊs/ (UK), /ˈdɔːɡhaʊs/ (US)| n.|狗屋；困境；失宠|A small shelter for a dog to sleep in.  Figuratively, it refers to a state of disfavor or trouble. |The dog was banished to the doghouse after chewing up the rug. 狗把地毯咬坏了，被罚关进了狗屋。 He's in the doghouse with his wife after forgetting their anniversary. 他忘记了结婚纪念日，现在老婆正在生他的气。|近义词：kennel, dog hut|反义词: None|词性:noun
