darklier:/ˈdɑːrkliər/ | adv. | 更黑暗地，更阴沉地 | More darkly; in a darker manner.  It's a comparative form of "darkly," meaning to a greater degree of darkness. | The sky grew darklier as the storm approached.  天空随着暴风雨的临近变得更加黑暗。| The mood in the room became darklier with each passing hour. 房间里的气氛随着时间的流逝变得越来越阴沉。 | 近义词: more darkly, gloomily, somberly | 反义词: brighter, lighter, more cheerfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "dark" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix) + "-er" (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *derkaz.
