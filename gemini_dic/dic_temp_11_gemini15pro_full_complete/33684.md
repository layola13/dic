cinnamoned:/ˈsɪnəmən(d)/
adjective
撒了肉桂粉的；加了肉桂的
Flavored or sprinkled with cinnamon.
例句：The cinnamoned apples smelled delicious. 撒了肉桂粉的苹果闻起来很香。

例句：She enjoyed a cinnamoned latte on a cold morning. 在一个寒冷的早晨，她享用了一杯加了肉桂粉的拿铁咖啡。

近义词：spiced, flavored, seasoned
反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider) plain, unflavored, unsalted
词性: adjective
