coralligerous:/ˌkɒrəˈlɪdʒərəs/
adjective
珊瑚的；产珊瑚的；多珊瑚的
Containing, bearing, or producing coral.
The coralligerous limestone formations are stunning. 这些产珊瑚的石灰岩构造令人惊叹。
The shallow, warm waters are coralligerous and support a diverse ecosystem. 浅而温暖的海水孕育着珊瑚，并支持着多样化的生态系统。
Synonyms: coral-bearing, coral-producing
Antonyms: barren, lifeless
