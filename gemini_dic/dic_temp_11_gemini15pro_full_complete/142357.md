orthopedic:/ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪk/ /ˌɔːrθəˈpidɪk/| adj.|骨科的；矫形的|Relating to the branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles.|例句：She needs orthopedic shoes. 她需要矫形鞋。|例句：The hospital has an excellent orthopedic department. 这家医院有一个优秀的骨科。|近义词：orthopaedic|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

词源: From Greek *orthos* "straight, correct" + *pais* "child". Originally focused on correcting deformities in children.
