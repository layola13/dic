exaggeration:/ɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 夸张；夸大；言过其实 |  An exaggeration is a statement that represents something as better or worse than it really is. | He told us an exaggeration about the size of the fish he caught. 他跟他说的鱼的大小是夸大其词的。| synonyms: overstatement, hyperbole, magnification | antonyms: understatement, minimization | noun

exaggerate:/ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 夸张；夸大；言过其实 | To exaggerate is to represent something as being larger, greater, better, or worse than it really is. | Don't exaggerate your accomplishments on your resume.  不要在你的简历上夸大你的成就。| synonyms: overstate, amplify, embellish | antonyms: understate, downplay | verb
