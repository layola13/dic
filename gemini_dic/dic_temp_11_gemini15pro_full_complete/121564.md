misnarrated: /ˌmɪsnəˈreɪtɪd/
verb
讲述错误的，叙述错误的
told or narrated incorrectly or inaccurately.
The witness misnarrated the sequence of events, leading to confusion in the courtroom.  目击者错误地叙述了事件的顺序，导致法庭上出现了混乱。
The historian misnarrated the king's motives, portraying him as more benevolent than he actually was. 历史学家错误地叙述了国王的动机，把他描绘得比实际更仁慈。
misrepresented, misreported, misstated
correctly narrated, accurately reported
