caravanning:/ˈkærəvænɪŋ/
noun
旅居露营；房车旅行
The activity of travelling and living in a caravan, especially for a holiday.
We went caravanning in France last summer. 去年夏天我们去法国旅行，住在房车里。
近义词:camping, touring
反义词:staying home
