chunking:/ˈtʃʌŋkɪŋ/| n.|组块化，分块|The process of grouping individual pieces of information into larger, more meaningful units.  This is often used in language learning and memory improvement.|She improved her memorization by chunking the numbers into groups of three.|synonyms: grouping, organizing, segmenting|antonyms: separating, dividing|noun

chunking:/ˈtʃʌŋkɪŋ/| v.|组块化，分块 (作为动词较少使用)|The act of grouping things together into chunks.|He chunked the wood for the fire.|synonyms: grouping, clumping|antonyms: separating, dividing|verb
