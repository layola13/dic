nolle:/ˈnɒleɪ/
verb
不愿起诉; 拒绝起诉; 放弃诉讼
to decline to prosecute (a case); to abandon a suit
The prosecutor entered a nolle prosequi, effectively ending the case.  检察官作出了不起诉的决定，有效地结束了这个案子。
drop, dismiss, discontinue
prosecute, pursue
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nolle prosequi (“to be unwilling to prosecute”).
