drawloom:/ˈdrɔːluːm/
noun
提花织机
A loom for weaving figured fabrics, in which the warp threads are lifted in groups by a mechanical device.
The intricate patterns on the tapestry were created using a drawloom.  这幅挂毯上复杂的图案是用提花织机织成的。
Synonyms:  Jacquard loom, figured loom
Antonyms:  Plain loom, simple loom
n.

Etymology->
From "draw" (to pull or drag) + "loom" (a frame or machine for weaving).  The "draw" refers to the action of drawing up specific warp threads to create the pattern.
