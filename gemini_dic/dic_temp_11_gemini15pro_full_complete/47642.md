debitor:/ˈdɛbɪtər/ (US), /ˈdɛbɪtə/ (UK)| n.|债务人，借方|A person or entity that owes a debt to another; someone who is in debt.  This term is more common in legal or formal contexts.| The company listed its debitors and creditors in its annual report. 该公司在其年度报告中列出了其债务人和债权人。|Synonyms: borrower, debtor, ower|Antonyms: creditor, lender|noun
