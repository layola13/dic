multiformity:/ˌmʌltɪˈfɔːrmɪti/ | n. | 多样性，多种形式 | The multiformity of life in the rainforest is astounding.  雨林中生物的多样性令人惊叹。|  diversity, variety, multiplicity | uniformity, sameness | noun

multiform:/ˈmʌltɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 多种形式的，多样的 |  The artist expressed his creativity through multiform artistic mediums. 这位艺术家通过多种艺术媒介表达了他的创造力。 | diverse, varied, multiple | uniform, single | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *multiformis, from Latin multus (“much, many”) + forma (“form, shape”).
