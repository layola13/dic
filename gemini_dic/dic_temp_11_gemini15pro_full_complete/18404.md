beloved:/bɪˈlʌvɪd/ /bɪˈlʌvd/| adj. n.|亲爱的，心爱的；爱人|Deeply loved; cherished.|例句：My beloved wife is my best friend. 我亲爱的妻子是我最好的朋友。

例句：He wrote a poem to his beloved. 他为他心爱的人写了一首诗。|近义词：dear, darling, cherished, adored|反义词：hated, despised, detested|词性:形容词,名词

beloved:/bɪˈlʌvɪd/ /bɪˈlʌvd/| n.|爱人，心上人|A person who is dearly loved.|例句：She was his childhood beloved. 她是他童年时代的心上人。

例句：He sent flowers to his beloved. 他给他的爱人送了花。|近义词：sweetheart, darling, love|反义词：enemy, foe|词性:名词
