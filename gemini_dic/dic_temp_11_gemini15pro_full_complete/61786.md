empery:/ˈem.pə.ri/| n. | 统治；帝国；绝对控制（权）| A vast and powerful domain, often including multiple territories or peoples under a single supreme authority; absolute control or power. | The Roman empery stretched across much of Europe and North Africa. 罗马帝国的疆域横跨欧洲和北非的大部分地区。

The CEO ruled his company with an iron-fisted empery.  这位首席执行官以铁腕统治着他的公司。 | 近义词：empire, dominion, sovereignty | 反义词: anarchy, chaos | 词性: noun

词源:
From Middle English *emperie, from Old French emperie, from Latin imperium (“command, power, empire”), from imperare (“to command”), from in- (“in, on, upon, against, towards”) + parāre (“to prepare, procure, furnish, get ready”).
