falsification:/ˌfɔːlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 伪造；篡改；歪曲 | the action of falsifying information or a theory |  The scientist was accused of falsification of data.  这位科学家被指控伪造数据。| synonyms: forgery, fabrication, misrepresentation, distortion | antonyms: verification, validation, confirmation | noun

falsify:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪ/ | v. | 伪造；篡改；歪曲 |  to make something false; to change something in order to make people believe something that is not true | He falsified the documents to get a visa. 他伪造了文件以获得签证。| synonyms: forge, fabricate, counterfeit, doctor | antonyms: verify, validate, confirm | verb
