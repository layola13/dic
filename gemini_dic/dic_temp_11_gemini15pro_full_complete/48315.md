decrescent:/dɪˈkrɛsənt/
adjective
渐小的，减少的，衰退的
becoming gradually less; waning.
The decrescent moon shone faintly in the sky.  渐小的月亮在天空中发出微弱的光芒。
近义词: waning, diminishing, declining, decreasing
反义词: crescent, increasing, growing, waxing
词性: adjective

decrescent:/dɪˈkrɛsənt/
noun
下弦月
the waning moon; the period between the full moon and the new moon.
The decrescent appeared as a sliver in the night sky. 下弦月像夜空中的一条银线。
近义词: waning moon, old moon
反义词: crescent moon, new moon
词性: noun
