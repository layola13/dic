forstall:/fɔːrˈstɔːl/ /fɔːrˈstɑːl/| vt.|阻止，先发制人，预先阻止|To prevent something from happening by acting first.|The company took steps to forstall a hostile takeover.  该公司采取措施阻止恶意收购。|近义词：prevent, preclude, obviate, avert, anticipate|反义词:allow, permit, encourage, promote|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *forstallen*, from Old English *forsteallan* ("to hinder, prevent, anticipate"), from *for-* ("before, against") + *stellan* ("to place, put, stand").  Cognate with Dutch *voorstalen*, German *vorstellen* ("to introduce, present").
