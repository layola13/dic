bounce:/baʊns/| v. n.|弹跳，反弹，跳动，活力，(支票等)被拒付退回|To spring back from a surface after hitting it.  To move energetically.|The ball bounced off the wall.  球从墙上弹了回来。|近义词：rebound, spring, leap, bound, hop|反义词:sink, drop, plop|verb

bounce:/baʊns/| n.|弹跳，反弹，跳动，活力|The act of bouncing or the ability to bounce.|The ball had a good bounce.  球弹性很好。|近义词：rebound, spring, leap, bound, hop|反义词:stillness, inactivity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *bombon, of imitative origin.
