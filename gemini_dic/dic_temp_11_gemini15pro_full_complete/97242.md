inflesh:/ɪnˈflɛʃ/ | v. |  赋予肉体; 使具体化; 体现 | To embody in flesh; to give substance or reality to; to make concrete or manifest. |  He infleshed his ideas in a powerful speech. 他在一场强有力的演讲中将他的想法具体化了。 | 近义词: embody, incarnate, personify, realize, manifest | 反义词: abstract, spiritualize, etherealize | 词性: 动词
