lithoglypher:/lɪˈθɒɡlɪfər/
n.
石刻家，雕刻师
A person who engraves on stone.
The lithoglypher skillfully carved the intricate design into the granite monument.  石刻家巧妙地将复杂的图案雕刻在花岗岩纪念碑上。

The ancient lithoglypher's work can still be seen on the temple walls.  古代石刻家的作品至今仍能在寺庙墙壁上看到。
近义词: engraver, sculptor, carver
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a profession)
词性: Noun
