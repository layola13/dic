briefs:/briːfs/
n.
内裤， briefs
Underwear covering the groin and buttocks, typically having short legs.
He packed three pairs of briefs for the trip. 他为这次旅行打包了三条内裤。
近义词: underwear, panties, boxers
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性: 名词

briefs:/briːfs/
n.
摘要，简报
A concise summary or abstract of a document or case.
The lawyer read the briefs before the trial. 律师在审判前阅读了案情摘要。
近义词: summary, abstract, synopsis
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性: 名词

briefs:/briːfs/
v.
向...介绍基本情况, to brief
Instruct or inform someone thoroughly, especially in preparation for a task or event.
The general briefed his troops before the battle. 将军在战斗前向他的部队作了简报。
近义词: instruct, inform, prepare
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性: 动词


词源：来源于古法语 *brief, 来自拉丁语 brevis “短的”。
