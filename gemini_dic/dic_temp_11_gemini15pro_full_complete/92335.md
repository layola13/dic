hypo:/ˈhaɪpoʊ/| prefix |低于，次于，在...之下|A prefix meaning "under," "beneath," "less than normal," or "deficient."|例句：Hypothermia is a dangerous condition where the body loses heat faster than it can produce it. 体温过低是一种危险的状况，身体散热的速度超过了它产生热量的速度。
