flintiness:/ˈflɪntinəs/
noun
坚硬；冷酷无情；坚定
The quality of being like flint; hardness, inflexibility, or unyieldingness.
Her flintiness was evident in her refusal to compromise. 她的坚定不移体现在她拒绝妥协的态度上。
synonyms: hardness, inflexibility, firmness, toughness, rigidity, unyieldingness, obduracy, intransigence
antonyms: softness, flexibility, gentleness, compassion, yielding
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From flinty + -ness.  Flinty from flint (Old English *flint), of Germanic origin; related to Dutch and German flint ‘flint’.
