ETYMOLOGY->
From the Yiddish word גליטש (glitsh), meaning "slippery place," perhaps via the German glitschen, "to slip."  Originally referred to a surge of electrical current.  The current meaning, related to computer malfunctions, emerged in the mid-20th century.
