hidability:/ˌhaɪdəˈbɪləti/
n.
可隐藏性，藏匿性
The quality of being able to be hidden or concealed.
The hidability of the bunker was its main advantage.  这个掩体的可隐藏性是它的主要优势。
concealment, secrecy
discoverability, visibility
noun
