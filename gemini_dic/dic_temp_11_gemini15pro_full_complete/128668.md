nicotiana:/ˌnɪkəʊʃiˈeɪnə/ /ˌnɪkoʊʃiˈænə/ | n. |烟草属；烟草植物|A genus of herbaceous plants in the nightshade family (Solanaceae), native to the Americas and the Pacific, many of which are cultivated for their leaves, which are the source of tobacco. |例句：Nicotiana tabacum is the species most commonly used for commercial tobacco production.  普通烟草是商业烟草生产中最常用的品种。

|例句：Some species of Nicotiana are grown as ornamentals for their attractive flowers.  一些烟草属植物因其美丽的花朵而被作为观赏植物种植。|近义词：tobacco plant|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
