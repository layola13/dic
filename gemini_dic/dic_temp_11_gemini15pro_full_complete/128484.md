newspaperism:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərɪzəm/ | n. | 报纸上的文章风格；报刊体；新闻体 | A style of writing characteristic of newspapers, often emphasizing brevity, sensationalism, and a focus on current events. | Example: The article was a classic example of newspaperism, with its short, punchy sentences and dramatic headlines. 这篇文章是典型的报刊体，句子简短有力，标题引人注目。

Example:  He criticized the newspaperism of the report, finding it lacking in depth and analysis. 他批评了这份报告的新闻体风格，认为它缺乏深度和分析。 | 近义词: journalism, journalese | 反义词:  literary style, academic writing | 词性: noun
