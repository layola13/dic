anatropous:/əˈnætrəpəs/
adjective
倒生的，反向的
(of an ovule) inverted so that the micropyle is turned towards the funiculus and the chalaza is at the opposite end.  胚珠倒置，以使珠孔转向珠柄，而合点位于另一端。
The anatropous ovule is the most common type of ovule in flowering plants.  倒生胚珠是被子植物中最常见的胚珠类型。
synonym:inverted, resupinate
antonym:orthotropous, atropous
adjective
