evolutionism:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪzəm/
n.
进化论，演化论
The theory that all living things developed from earlier simpler forms of life.
例句：Evolutionism is a cornerstone of modern biology. 进化论是现代生物学的基石。

例句：Some people still question the validity of evolutionism.  有些人仍然质疑进化论的有效性。

近义词：Darwinism, transformism
反义词：creationism
词性：名词
