deritualizes:/diːˈrɪtʃuəlaɪzɪz/
verb
去除儀式化，使非儀式化
To divest of ritual; to make something no longer a ritual.
The new CEO deritualized the annual company picnic, replacing the formal speeches with casual games.  新任首席执行官取消了公司年度野餐的仪式化，用轻松的游戏取代了正式的讲话。
The museum attempted to deritualize the display of artifacts, encouraging interaction rather than passive observation.  博物馆试图去除展品的仪式化，鼓励互动而不是被动观察。
