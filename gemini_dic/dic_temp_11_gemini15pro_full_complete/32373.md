chiles:/ˈtʃɪliːz/
n.
辣椒(复数)
A pungent hot pepper of the genus *Capsicum*, used fresh or dried as a flavoring, especially in Mexican cooking.
I like my chili extra spicy with lots of chiles. 我喜欢我的辣椒非常辣，放很多辣椒。
