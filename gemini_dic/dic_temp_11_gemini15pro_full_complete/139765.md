octagonally:/ɑːkˈtæɡənəli/
adv.
八边形地，八角形地
in the shape of an octagon; with eight sides.
The pool was octagonally shaped.  游泳池是八角形的。
The fortress was built octagonally.  这座堡垒是八边形建造的。
近义词: eight-sidedly
反义词: (No direct opposite, consider shapes with different sides like "circularly," "squarely," etc.)
词性: adverb
