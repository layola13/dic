diplomate:/ˈdɪpləmæt/ (US), /ˈdɪpləˌmæt/ (UK)| n. |外交家；有外交手腕的人|A person appointed by a state to conduct diplomacy with another state or international organization.  A person who is skilled in dealing with sensitive matters or people.|The American diplomat negotiated a peace treaty.  美国外交官谈判了一项和平条约。|Synonyms: envoy, ambassador, negotiator, consul|Antonyms: warmonger, agitator|noun
