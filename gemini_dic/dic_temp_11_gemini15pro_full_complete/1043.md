accelerometer: /əkˌseləˈrɒmɪtər/ (UK) /əkˌseləˈrɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n. |加速度计|An instrument that measures the rate of change of velocity of an object. |例句：The accelerometer in your phone allows it to track your steps. 你手机里的加速度计可以让它追踪你的步数。

例句：The engineers used an accelerometer to measure the vibrations of the bridge. 工程师们用加速度计来测量桥梁的振动。|近义词：accelerograph, g-sensor|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From *accelerate* + *-meter*. *Accelerate* comes from Latin *accelerare*, from *ad-* ("to, towards") + *celer* ("swift"). *-meter* comes from Greek *metron* ("measure").
