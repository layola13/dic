outsonnet:/ˌaʊtˈsɑːnɪt/
n.
十四行诗的变体
A poem that is longer than a sonnet, or one that varies from the traditional sonnet form.
Some poets have experimented with outsonnets, pushing the boundaries of the traditional form.  一些诗人尝试了十四行诗的变体，突破了传统形式的界限。
The poet's outsonnet explored themes of love and loss in a unique structure.  这位诗人的十四行诗变体以独特的结构探讨了爱与失落的主题。
Near Synonyms:  long poem, extended sonnet, variant sonnet
Antonyms: sonnet
