coaxingly:/koʊˈæksɪŋli/
adverb
哄骗地，劝诱地
in a coaxing or persuasive manner
He coaxingly asked her to stay. 他哄着她留下来。
Synonyms: persuasively, winningly, charmingly, sweetly, flatteringly
Antonyms: forcefully, demandingly, threateningly
词性: 副词


coax:/ˈkoʊæks/
verb
哄骗，劝诱
to persuade someone gently and patiently to do something or go somewhere
She coaxed the child to take its medicine. 她哄着孩子吃药。
Synonyms: persuade, wheedle, cajole, entice, tempt
Antonyms: force, compel, demand, order
词性: 动词
