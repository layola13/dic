meio:/ˈmeɪoʊ/ (US), /ˈmaɪoʊ/ (UK)| n. |减数分裂|A type of cell division that results in four daughter cells each with half the number of chromosomes of the parent cell, as in the production of gametes and plant spores.|Meiosis is essential for sexual reproduction.|近义词: reduction division|反义词: mitosis|词性: noun
