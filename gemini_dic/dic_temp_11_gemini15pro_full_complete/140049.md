oenophile:/ˈiːnəfaɪl/ | n. | 葡萄酒爱好者；品酒行家 | A connoisseur of wines; a person who enjoys and appreciates wine. | He's a true oenophile, with a cellar full of vintage wines. 他是一位真正的葡萄酒爱好者，地窖里收藏了大量的年份葡萄酒。 |  Synonym: wine lover, wine connoisseur, vinophile | Antonym: teetotaler, abstainer | Noun

Etymology:
From Greek *oinos* (wine) + *-philos* (loving).
