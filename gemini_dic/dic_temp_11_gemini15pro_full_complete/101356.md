ionics:/aɪˈɑːnɪks/
noun
离子学
The scientific study of ions and their properties, especially in electrolytic solutions.
Ionics plays a crucial role in understanding electrochemical processes. 离子学在理解电化学过程中起着至关重要的作用。
electrochemistry,  physical chemistry
