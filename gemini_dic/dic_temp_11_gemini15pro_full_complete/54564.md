dislodgement:/dɪsˈlɒdʒmənt/ /dɪsˈlɑːdʒmənt/
n.
移位；排出；逐出
the act of removing something from a fixed position
The earthquake caused the dislodgement of several boulders. 地震导致几块巨石移位。

The heavy rains caused a dislodgement of soil on the hillside.  暴雨导致山坡上的土壤移位。

removal, displacement, eviction, ousting
fixation, anchoring, securing
