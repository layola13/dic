drawerfuls:/ˈdrɔːrfʊlz/ | n. | 抽屉（满满的若干抽屉） | A quantity that fills several or many drawers.  It implies a large, unspecified amount. | She had drawerfuls of old photographs. 她有满满几抽屉的旧照片。 | The antique shop had drawerfuls of vintage jewelry.  这家古董店有满满几抽屉的复古珠宝。 |  near synonyms: lots, heaps, piles, masses | antonym:  a few, a little | noun
