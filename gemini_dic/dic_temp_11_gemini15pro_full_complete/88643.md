ho-hum:/ˈhoʊ hʌm/| adj.  int. | 平淡的，无聊的；(表示厌烦或冷漠)哎呦，哼 | Expressing boredom or indifference. |例句：He gave a ho-hum response to the news. 他对这个消息的反应很平淡。|近义词：boring, dull, tedious, unexciting, monotonous, commonplace, ordinary |反义词: exciting, interesting, stimulating, thrilling |词性:形容词,感叹词
ho-hum:/ˈhoʊ hʌm/|  int. | 哎呦，哼 | An exclamation expressing boredom or indifference. |例句："Ho-hum," she sighed, "another day, another dollar." “哎呦，”她叹了口气，“又是新的一天，又赚了一块钱。”|近义词：meh, oh well, whatever |反义词:wow, great |词性:感叹词
