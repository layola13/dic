errata:/ɪˈrɑːtə/ (US), /ɪˈreɪtə/ (UK)
n.
勘误表；正误表
A list of corrected errors in a printed work.
The publisher included a sheet of errata with the book.
出版商在书中附了一张勘误表。

errata:/ɪˈrɑːtə/ (US), /ɪˈreɪtə/ (UK)
n.
错误；错字
Errors, especially in printing or writing.
The errata in the report were numerous and embarrassing.
报告中的错误很多，令人尴尬。

erratum:/ɪˈrɑːtəm/ (US), /ɪˈreɪtəm/ (UK)
n.
单数形式
Singular form of errata.
There was a single erratum on page 32.
在第32页有一个错误。


近义词:corrections, amendments, revisions
反义词:perfection, flawlessness
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *errata* (plural of *erratum*), neuter past participle of *errare* "to wander, stray, err."
