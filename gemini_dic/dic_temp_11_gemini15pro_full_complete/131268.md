noncausalities:/ˌnɒnˌkɔːˈzælɪtiz/ | n. | 非因果关系；非因果性 | The concept of noncausalities challenges traditional understandings of cause and effect.  非因果关系的概念挑战了对因果关系的传统理解。 |  Quantum entanglement demonstrates a form of noncausality, where two particles can influence each other instantaneously regardless of distance. 量子纠缠展示了一种非因果关系的形式，其中两个粒子可以瞬间相互影响，而不管距离如何。 | 近义词: acausality, non-linearity | 反义词: causality, linearity | 词性: 名词 (plural noun)
