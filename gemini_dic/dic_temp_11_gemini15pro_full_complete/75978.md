funneled:/ˈfʌn(ə)ld/ | v. | 漏斗状；汇集；集中 | to move or be moved through a narrow space, or to make something do this | The crowd funneled into the stadium. 人群涌入体育场。|近义词：channel, direct, guide |反义词: disperse, scatter |词性:动词

funneled:/ˈfʌn(ə)ld/ | adj. | 漏斗形的 | shaped like a funnel | a funneled cloud 漏斗状的云|近义词：cone-shaped, conical |反义词: broad, wide |词性:形容词


词源: From funnel + -ed.  "funnel" itself comes from the Old French "funel," ultimately from the Latin "infundibulum."
