campestrian:/kæmˈpɛstriən/
adjective
平原的；乡下的；平凡的；散漫的
Relating to, characteristic of, or inhabiting open fields; rural; also, undistinguished; prosaic.
例句：The campestrian landscape stretched out before them, flat and monotonous.  平坦而单调的乡村景色展现在他们面前。

例句：He delivered a rather campestrian speech, lacking in originality and inspiration. 他发表了一篇相当平淡无奇的演讲，缺乏创意和灵感。
近义词：rural, rustic, agrarian, commonplace, pedestrian
反义词：urban, sophisticated, original
词性：adjective
