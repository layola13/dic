billy-goats:/ˈbɪli ɡəʊts/| n.|公山羊（复数）|Male goats, especially young ones.  Refers to the characters in the fairy tale "Three Billy Goats Gruff".|例句：The billy-goats crossed the bridge. 公山羊们过了桥。

例句：The troll threatened the billy-goats. 巨魔威胁了那些公山羊。|近义词：goats, bucks|反义词:nanny goats (母山羊)|词性:noun
