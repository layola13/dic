over-grades:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdz/ | v. | 给予过高的等级；过高评价；高估 | To assign a grade that is too high, exceeding the actual quality or merit.  | The teacher over-graded the essays, giving everyone As. 老师对论文的评分过高，每个人都得了A。 | He over-graded the wine, thinking it was a vintage. 他高估了这瓶酒，以为它是年份酒。 | 近义词：overestimate, overrate, inflate | 反义词：underestimate, underrate, downgrade | 词性：动词

over-grades:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdz/ | n. | 过高的等级 | A grade that is too high, exceeding the actual quality or merit. | The student received over-grades in several subjects. 这个学生在几个科目中都获得了过高的分数。 | The over-grades inflated his GPA. 过高的分数虚抬了他的平均绩点。 | 近义词：inflated grades | 反义词：low grades, failing grades | 词性：名词
