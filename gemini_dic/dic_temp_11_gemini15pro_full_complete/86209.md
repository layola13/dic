heathenized:/ˈhiːðənaɪzd/
adjective
异教化的；使成为异教徒的
Converted to heathenism; made heathen.
The Roman Empire gradually heathenized the conquered territories.  罗马帝国逐渐将被征服的领土异教化。
近义词:paganized, barbarized
反义词:Christianized, evangelized
词性:adjective

词源:
From heathen + -ize.  由"heathen"(异教徒) + "-ize"(使成为)构成。
