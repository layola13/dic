mispolicy:/mɪsˈpɒləsi/ | n. |错误的政策，失策 |A course of action or set of decisions adopted by a government, business, etc. that is unwise or wrong. | The government's mispolicy led to economic hardship. 政府的错误政策导致了经济困难。|Synonym: blunder, mismanagement, error, mistake | Antonym: good policy, wise decision | noun

mispolicy:/mɪsˈpɒləsi/ | n. | 误政策；失策 |  A wrong or unsuitable policy. | The company's mispolicy of ignoring customer complaints led to a decline in sales. 该公司忽视客户投诉的错误政策导致了销售额下降。 | Synonym: blunder, gaffe, error, mistake, fallacy | Antonym: wise policy, sound policy, good policy | noun
