mused:/mjuːzd/| v. |沉思；默想；冥想|To think about something carefully and thoughtfully.|She mused over the offer for a while before making a decision. 她仔细考虑了一会这个提议才做出决定。|例句：He mused on the future. 他沉思着未来。|近义词：pondered, contemplated, reflected, meditated|反义词:ignored, disregarded|verb

mused:/mjuːzd/| adj. |(literary) having lost oneself in thought|deep in thought; meditative|He had a mused expression on his face. 他脸上带着沉思的表情。|例句：She sat there, mused and silent. 她坐在那里，陷入沉思，一言不发。|近义词：thoughtful, pensive, reflective|反义词:inattentive, distracted|adjective
