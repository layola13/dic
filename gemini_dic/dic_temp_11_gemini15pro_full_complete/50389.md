deposable:/dɪˈpoʊzəbl/
adjective
可罢免的；可处置的；可丢弃的
Capable of being deposed, disposed of, or discarded.
The CEO is deposable if the board votes against him. 如果董事会投票反对，首席执行官是可以被罢免的。
Disposable diapers are convenient for busy parents.  一次性尿布对忙碌的父母来说很方便。
近义词:removable, disposable, dispensable
反义词:irremovable, indispensable
词性:adjective

词源: From depose + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *dēpōnere* ("to lay down, deposit").
