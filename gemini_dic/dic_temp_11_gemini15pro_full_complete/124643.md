mouthiest:/ˈmaʊθiɪst/| adj.|最多嘴的，最爱说话的，最直言不讳的|Superlative adjective of mouthy: excessively or habitually talkative, especially in an impudent or opinionated way.|例句：He was the mouthiest kid in the class, always interrupting the teacher. 他是班上最爱说话的孩子，总是打断老师。

例句：She's known for being the mouthiest critic in the industry. 她以是业内最直言不讳的评论家而闻名。|近义词：talkative, loquacious, garrulous, outspoken, opinionated|反义词：quiet, reserved, reticent, taciturn|词性：形容词
