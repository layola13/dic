organizes:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪz/
verb
组织，安排
arranges systematically; puts into working order
She organizes her desk every morning. 她每天早上整理她的桌子。
The company organizes team-building activities every quarter.  这家公司每个季度都会组织团队建设活动。
synonyms: arranges, coordinates, structures, systematizes, manages
antonyms: disorganizes, scatters, disrupts, confuses
词性: 动词
