furzeling:/ˈfɜːrzəlɪŋ/
n.
混乱，小题大做，无谓的忙乱
A state of confusion, fuss, or unnecessary busy-ness.
The whole furzeling over the seating arrangements was completely unnecessary.  整个关于座位安排的忙乱完全没有必要。
After the fire alarm, there was a general furzeling as everyone tried to evacuate the building. 火警响起后，大家都在试图撤离大楼，一片混乱。
近义词:fuss, commotion, ado, to-do
反义词:order, calmness, tranquility
词性:名词


词源:  Likely a blend of "fuss" and "hustle," or perhaps an expressive coinage.  Its origin is uncertain and not well-documented.  可能由“fuss”和“hustle”混合而成，也可能是一个富有表现力的自造词。它的起源不确定，也没有很好的记录。
