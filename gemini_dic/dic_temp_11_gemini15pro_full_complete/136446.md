nonreverently:/ˌnɒnrɪˈvɛrəntli/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈvɛrəntli/
adverb
不敬地，非虔诚地
showing a lack of respect for somebody/something that is generally treated with respect
He spoke nonreverently about the church. 他不敬地谈论教会。

近义词:disrespectfully, irreverently, impiously, profanely
反义词:reverently, respectfully, piously

词性:adverb
词源:From non- + reverent + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Reverent" comes from the Latin "revereri" meaning "to stand in awe of, to respect deeply."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
