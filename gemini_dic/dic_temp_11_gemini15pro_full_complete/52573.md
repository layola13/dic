diffusible:/dɪˈfjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 可扩散的；易散播的 | Capable of being diffused or spread out; readily spreading through a medium. |  The scent of the flowers was diffusible throughout the entire garden.  花园里到处弥漫着花香。 | synonyms: dispersible, spreadable, pervasive | antonyms: contained, localized, concentrated | adjective

diffusible:/dɪˈfjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 可被理解的 | Easily understood or grasped. | His explanation of the complex theory was surprisingly diffusible. 他对复杂理论的解释出奇地易懂。 | synonyms: comprehensible, understandable, clear | antonyms: incomprehensible, obscure, confusing | adjective
