dowcet:/ˈdaʊsɪt/| n. | 甜点；美味佳肴 | A sweet or tasty morsel; a delicacy. | The baker surprised us with a dowcet of marzipan and candied fruit.  面包师用杏仁蛋白软糖和蜜饯水果做的甜点让我们惊喜。| 近义词：delicacy, treat, confection, goody | 反义词: none | 词性: noun
Etymology: From Middle English *dowcet, from Old French doucet "sweet," from  dous "sweet."
