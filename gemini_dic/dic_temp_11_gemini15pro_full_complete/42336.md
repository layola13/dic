counteract:/ˌkaʊntərˈækt/ | v. |抵消，对抗，中和 |to act against something in order to reduce its force or neutralize it. |  The medicine is designed to counteract the effects of the poison. 这种药是为了抵消毒药的作用而设计的。

Drinking plenty of water counteracts the dehydrating effects of hot weather. 多喝水可以抵消炎热天气造成的脱水作用。

| 近义词: neutralize, offset, counterbalance, oppose, combat | 反义词: enhance, augment, intensify, aggravate |词性:动词
