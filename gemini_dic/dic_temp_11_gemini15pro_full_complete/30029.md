cayugas:/keɪˈjuːɡəz/
n.
卡尤加人；卡尤加语
Cayugas are a Native American tribe, one of the six nations of the Iroquois Confederacy.  卡尤加人是美洲原住民部落，是易洛魁联盟六个国家之一。
Cayuga is also the name of their Iroquoian language. 卡尤加语也是他们的易洛魁语的名称。
The Cayugas lived primarily in central New York State. 卡尤加人主要居住在纽约州中部。
