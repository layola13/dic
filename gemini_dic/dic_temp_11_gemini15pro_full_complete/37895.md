complaisant:/kəmˈpleɪzənt/ /kəmˈpleɪsənt/| adj.| 顺从的，殷勤的，讨好的|Willing to please others; obliging; agreeable.|例句：She was always complaisant to her superiors, hoping to gain their favor. 她总是对上司毕恭毕敬，希望能得到他们的青睐。

例句：The complaisant waiter catered to the diner's every whim.  这位殷勤的侍者满足了用餐者的一切要求。

近义词：amenable, obliging, accommodating, agreeable|反义词：uncooperative, intractable, disagreeable, assertive|词性:adjective
