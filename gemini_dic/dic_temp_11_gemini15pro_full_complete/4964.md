alkali: /ˈælkəlaɪ/ (US) /ˈælkəli/ (UK)| n. |碱|A chemical substance that reacts with acids to form salts and neutralizes them.  It is typically a soluble base that has a pH greater than 7. |Sodium hydroxide is a strong alkali. 氢氧化钠是一种强碱。
|synonyms: base, hydroxide, |antonyms: acid| noun


alkaline: /ˈælkəlaɪn/ (US) /ˈælkəlaɪn/ (UK)| adj. |碱性的|Having the properties of an alkali; having a pH greater than 7. |Alkaline batteries are commonly used in electronic devices. 碱性电池常用于电子设备中。
|synonyms: basic, base-forming|antonyms: acidic, acid| adjective
