antacids:/ˌæntˈæsɪdz/ | n. | 抗酸剂；解酸药 |  Medicines that neutralize acid in the stomach.  |  She took some antacids after eating spicy food. 她吃完辛辣食物后服用了一些抗酸剂。 | Synonyms: acid neutralizers, antacid tablets | Antonyms: acids | noun

antacid:/ˌæntˈæsɪd/ | adj. | 抗酸的 | Having the effect of neutralizing acidity, especially in the stomach. |  Antacid medication can relieve heartburn. 抗酸药物可以缓解胃灼热。 | Synonyms: alkaline, basic | Antonyms: acidic | adjective

Etymology:  From anti- "against" + acid.  "Anti-" comes from Greek _anti_ "against, opposite of, instead of, in place of." "Acid" comes from Latin _acidus_ "sour, sharp, tart," from the root of _acere_ "to be sour."
