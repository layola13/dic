girondist: /ʒɪˈrɒndɪst/ (UK) /ʒɪˈrɑːndɪst/ (US)| n. |吉伦特派（成员）|A member of the moderate republican faction active in the French Revolution.  The Girondists were representative of the provinces, and they favored a policy of extending the Revolution throughout Europe.|The Girondists, representing a more moderate approach, clashed with the more radical Jacobins.| 近义词: moderate, republican | 反义词: Jacobin, radical | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *Girondist*, from *Gironde*, a department of France from which many of the deputies came.
