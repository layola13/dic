depeople:/diːˈpiːpəl/
verb
减少人口
To reduce the population of (a place), especially by forced removal or massacre.
The invaders depeopled the village, leaving only ruins.  侵略者使这个村庄人口减少，只留下废墟。
The plague depeopled the entire region. 瘟疫使整个地区人口减少。
synonyms:evacuate, displace, exile, expel
antonyms:populate, inhabit
词性:v.
