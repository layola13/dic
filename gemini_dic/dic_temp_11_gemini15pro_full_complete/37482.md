commit:/kəˈmɪt/ | v. | 犯（罪）；做（错事等）；承诺；保证；投入，奉献 | To perform an action, especially a bad or illegal one; to promise or pledge; to devote (oneself, one's time, etc.) to something | He committed a terrible crime. 他犯下了可怕的罪行。

She committed herself to helping the poor. 她致力于帮助穷人。 | 近义词: perpetrate, pledge, devote | 反义词: abstain, refrain | 词性: 动词

commit:/kəˈmɪt/ | n. | 承诺，保证；委托；实行 | A promise or pledge; the act of committing |  He made a firm commit to the project. 他对这个项目做出了坚定的承诺。 | 近义词: pledge, undertaking | 反义词:  renunciation | 词性: 名词
