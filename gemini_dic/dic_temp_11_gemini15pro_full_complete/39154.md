confute:/kənˈfjuːt/ | vt. | 反驳，驳倒 | Prove (a person or an assertion) to be wrong. | The lawyer skillfully confuted the witness's testimony. 律师巧妙地驳倒了证人的证词。| synonyms: refute, rebut, disprove | antonyms: prove, confirm, corroborate | verb
