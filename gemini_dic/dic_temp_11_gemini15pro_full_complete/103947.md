judicial:/dʒuːˈdɪʃəl/ | adj. | 司法的；审判的；公正的 | Relating to the administration of justice or the function of a judge.  | The judicial system needs reform.  司法系统需要改革。 | The judicial process can be lengthy and complex. 司法程序可能漫长而复杂。 | 近义词: legal, judiciary, juridical | 反义词: illegal, unlawful | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *judiciall*, from Old French *judical*, from Latin *iūdiciālis*  "of or belonging to a judge or court," from *iūdex* "judge,"  from *ius* "right, law" + *-dex* (from root of *dicere* "to say").
