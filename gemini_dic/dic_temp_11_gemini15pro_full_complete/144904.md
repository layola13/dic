over-speculate: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛkjʊleɪt/
verb
过度推测；过分臆测
To engage in excessive speculation, often leading to unrealistic expectations or risky investments.
He over-speculated in the stock market and lost a fortune. 他在股市过度投机，损失了一大笔钱。
The company over-speculated on the new product's success and ended up with a warehouse full of unsold inventory.  这家公司对新产品的成功过度推测，结果仓库里堆满了未售出的库存。
近义词: overestimate, gamble, conjecture
反义词: underestimate, play it safe
词性: verb
