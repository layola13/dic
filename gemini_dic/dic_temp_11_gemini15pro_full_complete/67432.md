extinguish:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/ | vt. | 消除，使消失 | To put an end to (a feeling or hope); to destroy |  His hopes were extinguished by the news.  这个消息使他的希望破灭了。 | synonyms: abolish, eliminate, eradicate | antonyms: create, establish, foster | verb
