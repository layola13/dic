foredispose:/ˌpriːdɪˈspəʊz/ /ˌpriːdɪˈspoʊz/| verb |使预先有倾向；使易受感染|to make someone more likely to suffer from a particular illness or condition, or to make someone more likely to behave in a particular way|例句：Genetic factors can predispose people to certain types of cancer. 遗传因素会使人易患某些类型的癌症。

例句：His upbringing predisposed him to distrust authority. 他的成长经历使他不信任权威。|近义词：influence, incline, make susceptible, make liable|反义词：immunize, protect, shield|词性:v.
