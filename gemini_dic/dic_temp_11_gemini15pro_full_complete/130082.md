non-pressure:/ˌnɒnˈprɛʃə(r)/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈprɛʃər/ (UK)| adj.|无压力的，非强迫的|Characterized by the absence of pressure or coercion; relaxed and informal.|This is a non-pressure sales environment. 这是一个无压力的销售环境。|Synonyms: relaxed, casual, informal, low-key|Antonyms: pressured, stressful, intense|adjective

non-pressure:/ˌnɒnˈprɛʃə(r)/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈprɛʃər/ (UK)| n.|无压力的情况|A situation or environment characterized by the absence of pressure or coercion.|The non-pressure of the situation allowed her to think clearly.  这种无压力的情况让她能够清晰地思考。|Synonyms: ease, relaxation, calmness|Antonyms: stress, pressure, tension|noun
