conditionalism:/kənˈdɪʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. | 条件主义 | The doctrine that the salvation of individuals is conditional, often upon faith and works.  |  Salvation is not guaranteed, but earned through faith and obedience. 这救赎不是必然的，而是通过信仰和服从获得的。 |  Conditionalism asserts that the wicked will not suffer eternally in hell, but will ultimately be annihilated. 条件主义认为恶人不会在地狱里永远受苦，而最终会被毁灭。 | synonyms: conditionality, provisionality | antonyms: absolutism, unconditionalism | noun
