carroll:/ˈkærəl/ | n. |卡罗尔（姓氏）; 卡罗尔（女子名） | A surname and a given name, derived from the Irish surname Ó Cearbhaill, meaning "descendant of Cearbhall". Cearbhall is composed of the elements *cear*, meaning "love", and *bhall*, meaning "member, limb", hence "love-limb" or "dear/precious one."| Lewis Carroll wrote "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland".  刘易斯·卡罗尔写了《爱丽丝梦游仙境》。| Carroll County is located in Maryland. 卡罗尔县位于马里兰州。 |synonyms: (for given name) Carol, Carolyn | antonyms: N/A | proper noun
