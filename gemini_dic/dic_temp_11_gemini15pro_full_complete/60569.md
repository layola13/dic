elasticity:/ɪˌlæˈstɪsɪti/ /ɪˌlæˈstɪsəti/ | n. |弹性，弹力；伸缩性| The ability of an object or material to resume its normal shape after being stretched or compressed; flexibility. | Rubber has a high degree of elasticity. 橡胶有很高的弹性。| synonyms: flexibility, resilience, springiness, suppleness, pliancy, give | antonyms: rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility | noun
