hostilities:/hɑːˈstɪlətiz/
n.
敌对行动，战争，战斗
acts of war or fighting between nations or groups; acts of aggression
例句：Hostilities between the two countries finally ceased after years of conflict.  两国之间的敌对行动在多年的冲突后终于停止了。

例句：The outbreak of hostilities took the world by surprise.  敌对行动的爆发令世界震惊。

近义词：fighting, warfare, combat, conflict, aggression
反义词：peace, truce, armistice
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
