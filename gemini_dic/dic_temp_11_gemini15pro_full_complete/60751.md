ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- + encephalon (brain) + -gram (record).  "Electro-" comes from Greek "elektron" meaning "amber" (referring to static electricity). "Encephalon" comes from Greek "enkephalos" meaning "brain." "-Gram" comes from Greek "gramma" meaning "something written."
