facsimile:/fækˈsɪməli/
n.|传真，复制品|An exact copy or reproduction of something, such as a document, a signature, or a work of art.|例句：Please send me a facsimile of the contract. 请把合同的传真件发给我。|例句：This painting is a near facsimile of the original. 这幅画几乎是原作的复制品。|近义词：copy, replica, duplicate, reproduction|反义词：original|词性:名词

facsimile:/fækˈsɪməli/
vt.|传真，复制|To send (a document or image) via facsimile transmission.|例句：I will facsimile the report to you as soon as it's ready. 报告一准备好我就传真给你。|例句：She facsimiled her signature to the bank. 她把自己的签名传真给了银行。|近义词：fax, transmit, copy, reproduce|反义词：-|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin 'fac simile' (make similar)
