inlands:/ˈɪnləndz/| n.|内陆地区，内地|The inlands of the country are less populated than the coastal areas.  这个国家的内陆地区人口比沿海地区少。|例句：The inlands of Australia are vast and arid. 澳大利亚的内陆地区广阔而干旱。|近义词：interior, hinterland|反义词:coast, coastline|词性:名词
inland:/ˈɪnlənd/| adj.|内陆的，内地的|Inland areas are often hotter than coastal areas. 内陆地区通常比沿海地区更热。|例句：The inland city experienced a severe drought. 这座内陆城市经历了严重的干旱。|近义词：interior, internal|反义词:coastal, maritime|词性:形容词
