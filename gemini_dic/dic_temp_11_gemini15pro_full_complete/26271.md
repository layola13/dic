buttonholers:/ˈbʌtnˌhoʊlərz/| n.|拉住别人讲话的人；强留别人谈话的人|Someone who buttonholes another person; someone who detains another person in conversation, often against their will.|The politician was surrounded by buttonholers as he left the building.  这位政治家离开大楼时被一群缠着说话的人围住了。|Synonyms: accosters, interceptors|Antonyms: avoiders, ignorers|noun
