insensibility's:/ɪnˌsɛnsəˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
无知觉；麻木；冷漠
lack of awareness or feeling; unresponsiveness
His insensibility's to pain worried the doctors. 他的对疼痛的无知觉让医生们担忧。
近义词:unconsciousness, numbness, apathy, indifference
反义词:sensitivity, awareness, responsiveness, consciousness
词性:noun (名词)

词源:
From Late Latin *insensibilitas, from Latin insensibilis (“imperceptible, insensible”) + -itas. Equivalent to insensible +‎ -ity.
