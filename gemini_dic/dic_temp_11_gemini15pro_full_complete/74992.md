fridstol:/ˈfriːdˌstuːl/ | n. | 和平椅; 友好长椅 | A bench intended to promote peace and conversation, often placed in a public space. |  Example 1: The community installed a fridstol in the park as a symbol of unity. 社区在公园里安装了一把和平椅，作为团结的象征。

Example 2:  They sat on the fridstol and discussed their differences calmly. 他们坐在友好长椅上，平静地讨论了他们的分歧。 | Synonym: peace bench, friendship bench | Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider) war memorial, monument of conflict | Noun
