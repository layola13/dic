congestive:/kənˈdʒɛstɪv/
adjective
充血性的
Characterized by or causing congestion, an abnormal accumulation of blood or other fluid in a body part or blood vessel.
Congestive heart failure is a serious condition. 充血性心力衰竭是一种严重的疾病。
She was diagnosed with congestive heart disease. 她被诊断出患有充血性心脏病。
Synonyms: blocked, obstructed, clogged
Antonyms: clear, open, unobstructed
词性: adjective
