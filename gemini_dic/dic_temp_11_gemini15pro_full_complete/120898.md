miscalculation:/ˌmɪskælkjuˈleɪʃn/
n.
错误估计；误算
an act of calculating something wrongly.
The project's failure was due to a miscalculation of the costs. 这个项目的失败是由于成本计算错误。
misjudgment, misestimate, error, blunder
correct calculation, accurate estimate

词性: 名词
