deplumate:/diːˈpluːmeɪt/
verb
拔毛；剥去羽毛
To strip of feathers; pluck.
The cruel farmer deplumatd the live goose for its down.  残忍的农场主为了鹅绒拔活鹅的毛。
近义词:pluck, feather, deplume
反义词:implant, insert
