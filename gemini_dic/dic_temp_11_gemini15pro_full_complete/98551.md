instimulate: /ɪnˈstɪmjuleɪt/
verb
刺激，激发
to stimulate or excite
The new policy instimulates investment. 新政策刺激了投资。
近义词: stimulate, excite, provoke, encourage
反义词: suppress, inhibit, discourage
词性: verb

词源:  A back-formation from *instimulation*, itself from in- + stimulation.  Ultimately from Latin *stimulus* (“goad, prick”).
