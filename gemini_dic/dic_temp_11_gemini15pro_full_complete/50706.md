dermopathic:/ˌdɜːrməˈpæθɪk/
adjective
皮肤病的；由皮肤病引起的
Relating to or caused by skin disease.
The dermopathic changes were evident on the patient's arms and legs. 病人的手臂和腿上出现了明显的皮肤病变。
The doctor prescribed a cream to treat the dermopathic condition. 医生开了一种药膏来治疗这种皮肤病。
Synonyms: cutaneous, dermatological
Antonyms: healthy (skin)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From derm(a)-  +‎ -pathic.  Derma- comes from Greek *dérma (“skin”). -pathic comes from Greek páthos (“suffering, disease”).
