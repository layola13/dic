munificent:/mjuːˈnɪfɪsənt/| adj.|慷慨的，丰厚的|Characterized by great generosity or lavishness.|例句：The king made a munificent donation to the charity. 国王向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。|例句：Her munificent gift allowed the library to build a new wing. 她慷慨的捐赠使图书馆得以建造一个新的侧厅。|近义词：generous, lavish, bountiful, liberal|反义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin munificus (“bountiful, liberal, generous”), from munus (“gift, service, duty”) + -ficus (making, doing).
