nonpassive:/nɒnˈpæsɪv/ | adj. | 非被动的，主动的 | Not passive; active.  Characterized by performing or causing an action rather than receiving or being subjected to it. | The CEO took a nonpassive role in the negotiations, actively shaping the terms of the deal.  首席执行官在谈判中扮演了积极主动的角色，积极地塑造了交易条款。 |  The teacher encouraged nonpassive learning, urging students to ask questions and participate in discussions. 老师鼓励主动学习，敦促学生提问并参与讨论。 | 近义词: active, assertive, dynamic | 反义词: passive, inactive, inert | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "passive" (from Latin "passivus," meaning "capable of suffering or feeling").
