inofficially:/ˌɪnəˈfɪʃəli/| adv.|非正式地，非官方地|Not officially sanctioned or recognized; privately or unofficially. |The deal was made inofficially, with no lawyers present.  这笔交易是非正式达成的，没有律师在场。|
The two leaders met inofficially to discuss the crisis. 两位领导人私下会面讨论了这场危机。|Synonyms: privately, informally, unofficially, personally|Antonyms: officially, formally, publicly|adverb
