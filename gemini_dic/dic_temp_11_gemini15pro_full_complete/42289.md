counter-incentive:/ˈkaʊntər ɪnˈsɛntɪv/
|n.|反激励；抑制因素|Something that discourages a particular action or behavior.  It is the opposite of an incentive.|High taxes on cigarettes are a counter-incentive to smoking.  香烟的高税收是对吸烟的一种反激励。|disincentive, deterrent|incentive|noun
counter-incentive:/ˈkaʊntər ɪnˈsɛntɪv/
|n.|反刺激；反诱因|Something that discourages a particular action or behavior by making it less attractive.  It acts against the desired outcome.|The fear of getting caught is a strong counter-incentive to cheating.  害怕被抓是作弊的强烈反刺激。|deterrent, discouragement|motivation, inducement|noun
