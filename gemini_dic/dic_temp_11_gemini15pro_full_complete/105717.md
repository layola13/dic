kinships:/ˈkɪnʃɪps/
n.
亲属关系，血缘关系
The bonds of kinships are strong.  亲属关系的纽带很牢固。
kinship between cultures  不同文化之间的亲缘关系
近义词：relationship, relation, family ties, blood ties, affinity
反义词：estrangement, alienation
词性：名词


kinship:/ˈkɪnʃɪps/
n.
(尤指因起源或态度相似而产生的)密切联系，亲切感
He felt a kinship with the other artists. 他对其他艺术家感到一种亲切感。
a kinship between the two writers  两位作家之间的密切联系
近义词：affinity, rapport, empathy, closeness, connection
反义词：distance, detachment
词性：名词
