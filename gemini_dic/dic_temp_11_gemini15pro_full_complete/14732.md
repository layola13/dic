awe:/ɔː/ | n. | 敬畏，惊叹 | A feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder. | The vastness of the Grand Canyon filled me with awe. 大峡谷的广阔让我充满了敬畏。 | synonyms: wonder, amazement, reverence | antonyms: disdain, contempt | noun

awe:/ɔː/ | vt. | 使敬畏，使惊叹 | To inspire with awe. | The magician's skill awed the audience. 魔术师的技艺使观众惊叹。 | synonyms: amaze, astound, impress | antonyms: bore, disappoint | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *agi, meaning "fear, terror."  Cognate with Old English ege "fear, dread." The meaning shifted from "fear" to "reverential fear" and then to "wonder, awe."
