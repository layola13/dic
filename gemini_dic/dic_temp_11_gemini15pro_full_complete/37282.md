commandeer:/ˌkɒmənˈdɪə(r)/ | /ˌkɑːmənˈdɪr/ | v. | 征用，强占 | To seize (something, especially a vehicle or building) for military or official use. | The army commandeered all the trucks to transport supplies. 军队征用了所有的卡车来运输物资。

The government commandeered the building for use as a temporary hospital. 政府征用了这栋建筑作为临时医院。


近义词：seize, confiscate, appropriate, expropriate | 反义词：return, release, relinquish | 词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Dutch *kommanderen*, from *kommando*, command.  Ultimately from Latin *commandāre*.
