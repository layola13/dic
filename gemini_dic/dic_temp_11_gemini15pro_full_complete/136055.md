nonrecalcitrant:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˈkælsɪtrənt/ | adj. | 不反抗的，温顺的，顺从的 | Not recalcitrant; compliant, yielding, or amenable. | The nonrecalcitrant students followed the teacher's instructions without complaint. 这些温顺的学生毫无怨言地服从了老师的指示。 | Example 2:  The negotiators were pleased with the nonrecalcitrant attitude of the opposing party. 谈判代表对对方不反抗的态度感到满意。 | 近义词: compliant, amenable, docile, obedient, submissive, yielding | 反义词: recalcitrant, resistant, defiant, rebellious, uncooperative, obstinate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + recalcitrant (from Latin recalcitrare "to kick back," from re- "back" + calcitrare "to kick," from calx "heel").
