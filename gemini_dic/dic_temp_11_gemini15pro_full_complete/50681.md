例句：She bought a dermatine jacket because it was cheaper than real leather. 她买了一件人造革夹克，因为它比真皮的便宜。|近义词：leatherette, vinyl, pleather, artificial leather, imitation leather|反义词: genuine leather, real leather|词性:noun

词源:  From Greek *derma* (skin) + *-ine* (forming adjectives and nouns).
