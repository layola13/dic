deja:/ˈdeɪʒɑː/ (US), /ˈdeɪʒɑː/ (UK)| n.|似曾相识|The feeling that one has experienced something before, although they know they haven't.|I had a strong sense of deja vu when I walked into the room. |我走进房间时有一种强烈的似曾相识的感觉。|近义词:paramnesia|反义词: jamais vu|词性:名词

词源: French, literally 'already seen,' past participle of voir 'to see.'
