oking:/ˈəʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 好的，可以的，不错的 |  "Oking" isn't a standard English word. It's likely a slang or informal shortening of "OKing," which is the present participle of the verb "to OK" (meaning to approve or authorize). |  "He's oking the proposal."
