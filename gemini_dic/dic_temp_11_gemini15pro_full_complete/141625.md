opticity:/ɒpˈtɪsɪti/ | n. | 光学性；不透明度；晦涩难懂 | The quality of being opaque or not transparent; the degree to which something is opaque.  It can also refer to something being difficult to understand. | The opticity of the fog made it difficult to see.  浓雾的不透明度使人难以看清。 | The opticity of the legal jargon made it difficult for the layman to understand the contract.  法律术语的晦涩难懂让外行人难以理解合同。 |  opacity, opaqueness | transparency, clarity, lucidity | noun
