deflective:/dɪˈflektɪv/| adj.|偏斜的，偏转的，防御的|Causing something to change direction or shielding from something harmful.|The car's deflective armor protected it from the blast. 汽车的偏转装甲保护它免受爆炸的冲击。|近义词：diverting, deviating, protective, shielding|反义词：attractive, receptive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *deflectivus, from Latin deflectere (to bend away, turn aside).  From de- (away from) + flectere (to bend).
