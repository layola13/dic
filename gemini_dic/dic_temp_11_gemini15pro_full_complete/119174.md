mezcal: /mɛsˈkæl/ | n. | 梅斯卡尔酒 | An alcoholic spirit made from the agave plant, similar to tequila but with a smokier flavor. |  I tried a new mezcal last night; it had a distinct smoky flavor. 我昨晚尝试了一种新的梅斯卡尔酒；它有一种独特的烟熏味。 |  My favorite mezcal has hints of citrus and spice.  我最喜欢的梅斯卡尔酒带有柑橘和香料的味道。 | 近义词: tequila, agave spirits | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider non-alcoholic beverages like water or juice) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Nahuatl *mexcalli [metʃˈkalːi], meaning "oven-cooked agave," from metl ("agave") and ixcalli ("cooked agave").
