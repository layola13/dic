hobber's:/ˈhɒbəz/ (UK), /ˈhɑːbərz/ (US)| n.|滚齿工的（东西）; 滚齿机的|Something that belongs to or is associated with a hobber (a person or machine that cuts gear teeth).  It's a possessive form of "hobber".|This is the hobber's toolbox. 这是滚齿工的工具箱。|Synonym: gear cutter's (more general)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider something like "finisher's" in certain contexts if referring to a different stage of manufacturing)|noun
