glose:/ɡloʊs/
noun
注解，注释，解释
An explanation or interpretation of a difficult or obscure word or passage.
例句：The editor added a glose to the difficult passage. 编辑在难懂的段落加了注解。
例句：He provided a helpful glose on the meaning of the poem. 他对这首诗的含义作了有益的注释。
近义词：annotation, commentary, explanation, interpretation, note
反义词：obfuscation, confusion

verb
注解，注释，解释；掩饰，辩解
To explain or interpret something, often in a way that makes it seem more positive or less embarrassing than it really is.
例句：She tried to glose over her mistake, but the damage was done. 她试图掩饰自己的错误，但损害已经造成。
例句：He glossed the awkward situation with a joke. 他用一个笑话掩盖了尴尬的局面。
近义词：explain, interpret, comment on, elucidate, gloss over, whitewash
反义词：obscure, confuse, complicate
