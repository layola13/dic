heptametrical:/ˌhɛptəˈmɛtrɪkəl/
adjective
七音步的
Consisting of or written in heptameter (a line of verse consisting of seven metrical feet).
The poem's heptametrical structure gives it a stately rhythm. 这首诗的七音步结构赋予它一种庄重的节奏。
The poet experimented with heptametrical verse.  这位诗人尝试了七音步的诗歌。
Synonyms: seven-foot, septenary
