centerpiece:/ˈsentərpiːs/| n.|中心装饰品，核心，最重要的部分|A decorative object placed in the middle of a table, often used for special occasions.  Also, the most important or central part of something.|The centerpiece of the dining table was a beautiful arrangement of flowers. 餐桌中央的装饰品是一束美丽的鲜花。

The new museum is the centerpiece of the city's cultural revitalization plan. 新博物馆是城市文化复兴计划的核心。|synonyms:  focal point, highlight, centerpiece,  main feature,  pièce de résistance|antonyms: periphery, edge, margin|noun
