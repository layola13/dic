murkiness:/ˈmɜːkɪnɪs/ | n. |黑暗；阴暗；朦胧 |The murkiness of the water made it impossible to see the bottom. 水的浑浊使人无法看到底部。|例句：The murkiness of the swamp made it a dangerous place to explore. 沼泽的阴暗使它成为一个危险的探索之地。|近义词：darkness, gloom, obscurity, dimness |反义词：brightness, clarity, light |词性:名词
Etymology: From murky + -ness. Murky itself comes from the Middle English *merke*, likely related to Old Norse *myrkr* (dark).
