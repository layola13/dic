mirador:/ˌmɪrəˈdɔːr/ | n. | 观景台，眺望台 | A raised structure, often a balcony or tower, affording extensive views; a lookout. | From the top of the mirador, we could see the entire city. 从观景台上，我们可以看到整座城市。|Synonyms: lookout, viewpoint, observation point, belvedere| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "hidden spot," "enclosed space") | noun

mirador:/ˌmɪrəˈdɔːr/ | n. |观景楼，瞭望塔|A tower or other high structure designed to command an extensive view.|We climbed the mirador to get a better view of the sunset. 我们爬上瞭望塔以便更好地欣赏日落。|Synonyms: watchtower, observation tower, turret| Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "basement," "dungeon") | noun
