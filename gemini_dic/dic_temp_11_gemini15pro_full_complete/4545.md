alcarraza:/ˌalkəˈræθə/ (US), /ˌælkəˈræzə/ (UK)| n.| 阿尔卡拉萨（一种西班牙多孔陶土罐，用于冷却水）|A porous earthenware jug used in Spain for cooling water. The evaporation of water through the jug's pores lowers the temperature of the water inside. |例句：The alcarraza kept the water cool even on the hottest days.  即使在最热的日子里，阿尔卡拉萨也能保持水凉爽。

例句：We filled the alcarraza with water and left it in the shade. 我们把阿尔卡拉萨装满水，放在阴凉处。|近义词：water jug, earthenware jug, porous pot |反义词: N/A|词性:noun
