grungemann:/ˈɡrʌndʒmən/ | n. | 垃圾摇滚乐迷；邋遢的人 | A fan of grunge music, often characterized by a messy, unkempt appearance.  | He looked like a typical grungemann, with his long, greasy hair and ripped jeans. 他留着油腻的长发，穿着破洞牛仔裤，看起来像个典型的垃圾摇滚乐迷。 | 近义词:slacker, grunge fan, alt-rock fan | 反义词:preppy, neatnik | 词性:noun

词源: "Grunge" (referring to a subgenre of rock music) + "man" (suffix denoting a male person).  The term originated in the early 1990s with the rise of grunge music's popularity.
