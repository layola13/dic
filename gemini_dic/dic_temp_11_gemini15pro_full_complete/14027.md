auscultation:/ˌɔːskəlˈteɪʃən/ | n. |听诊|The act of listening to sounds within the body, typically with a stethoscope, for diagnostic purposes. |The doctor performed an auscultation of the patient's chest. 医生对病人的胸部进行了听诊。|Synonyms: listening, examination, monitoring|Antonyms: N/A|noun

auscultations:/ˌɔːskəlˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |听诊（复数）|Multiple instances of the act of listening to sounds within the body. |Repeated auscultations revealed no change in the patient's heart rate.  反复听诊显示病人的心率没有变化。|Synonyms: listenings, examinations, monitorings|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin auscultātiō (“a listening to”), from auscultō (“I listen attentively”), from auls (“ear”) + cultō.
