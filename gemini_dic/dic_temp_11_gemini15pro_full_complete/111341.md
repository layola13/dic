livelihood:/ˈlaɪvlihʊd/| n.|生计，生活来源|A person's livelihood is the way they earn money in order to live.|Fishing is the main livelihood of many people in the village. 村里许多人的主要生计是捕鱼。|近义词：living, income, sustenance, maintenance|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 unemployment 失业)|Noun
