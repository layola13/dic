marblelike: /ˈmɑːrblˌlaɪk/ (US) /ˈmɑːblˌlaɪk/ (UK)| adj.|像大理石的，光滑冰冷的|Resembling marble in smoothness, coldness, or appearance.|The statue had a marblelike surface, smooth and cold to the touch. 这座雕像表面像大理石一样，光滑冰凉。|
The cake's icing was marblelike in its glossy perfection. 蛋糕上的糖霜光滑完美，像大理石一样。|近义词：marmoreal, stony, smooth, cold|反义词：rough, warm, porous|词性:形容词

词源:
marble (n.) + -like (suffix)

marble: From Old French marbre, from Latin marmor, from Greek mármaros (“shining stone, marble”).
-like: Old English -līc (“having the form or appearance of, resembling”), from Proto-Germanic *-līkaz (“like, having the form of”). Cognate with Dutch -lijk, German -lich, Gothic -leiks.
