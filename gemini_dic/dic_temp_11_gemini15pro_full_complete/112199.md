loony:/ˈluːni/| adj. n.|疯狂的，愚蠢的；疯子|Crazy or foolish.  A crazy person.|He had some loony idea about turning lead into gold. 他有一些关于把铅变成金子的疯狂想法。| The hospital is full of loonies. 这家医院里全是疯子。|近义词：crazy, insane, mad, daft|反义词：sane, rational|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology->
From *lunatic*, influenced by *loony bin* (a slang term for a mental hospital). Ultimately from Latin *luna* "moon," based on the ancient belief that changes of the moon caused intermittent insanity.
