delitigation:/diːˌlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 消除诉讼，庭外和解 | The process of avoiding, reducing, or settling legal disputes outside of court. |  The company pursued a strategy of delitigation to minimize legal costs. 该公司采取了消除诉讼的策略，以最大限度地减少法律费用。

The parties engaged in delitigation to resolve their contract dispute amicably.  双方进行庭外和解，以友好地解决合同纠纷。| 近义词：alternative dispute resolution, arbitration, mediation, settlement, compromise | 反义词：litigation, lawsuit, legal action | 词性: noun

Etymology: de- (prefix meaning "removal" or "reversal") + litigation (noun meaning "the process of taking legal action")
