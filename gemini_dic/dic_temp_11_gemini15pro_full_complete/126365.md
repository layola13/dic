myricyl alcohol: /ˈmɪrɪsɪl ˈælkəhɒl/ | n. |三十烷醇| A long-chain primary fatty alcohol with the molecular formula C30H62O, also known as triacontanol or melissyl alcohol. It is a white, waxy solid found in plant cuticle waxes and beeswax. | Myricyl alcohol is used in some cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. 三十烷醇用于一些化妆品和药品。| 近义词: triacontanol, melissyl alcohol | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μύριοι (múrioi, “ten thousand”) +‎ -cyl.  Coined by Brodie in 1848, referring to the large number of carbon atoms.
