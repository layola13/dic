hst:/ˌeɪtʃ ɛs ˈtiː/ |abbr.| Harmonized Sales Tax (加拿大统一销售税) | A consumption tax in Canada. It combines the federal Goods and Services Tax (GST) and the provincial sales tax of a participating province. | Example: The HST in Ontario is 13%. 安大略省的统一销售税是13%。 | 近义词: consumption tax, sales tax | 反义词: N/A | abbreviation
