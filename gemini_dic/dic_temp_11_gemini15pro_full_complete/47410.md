deadlily:/ˈdɛdlɪli/| adv.|致命地；极度；非常|In a manner likely to cause death; extremely; exceedingly.|The cobra struck deadlily. 眼镜蛇致命一击。|近义词：fatally, mortally, lethally, extremely, exceedingly|反义词：harmlessly, safely|词性:副词

Etymology: From 'deadly' + '-ly'.  'Deadly' comes from Old English 'dēadlic', from 'dēad' (dead) + '-lic' (like).
