dues:/djuːz/| n. |会员费，应付款，应得之物| fees or payments owed for membership in an organization or for services rendered; something that is owed or due | Example: He failed to pay his club dues. 他没有支付俱乐部会员费。

Example:  She finally received the dues owed to her. 她终于收到了她应得的款项。|Synonym: fees, charges, payments, subscriptions, levies|Antonym: credits, receipts, refunds|noun
