backslashes: /ˈbækslæʃɪz/ | n. | 反斜线 | A backslash is a typographical mark (\) used primarily in computing. It is used to escape special characters, denote directory paths, and in some programming languages.  |  The directory path is C:\Users\Documents.  该目录路径是 C:\Users\Documents。 | synonyms: reverse solidus, escape character | antonyms: N/A | noun
