fettled:/ˈfɛt(ə)ld/
adjective
准备就绪的，状态良好的
Made ready, put in order, or in good condition.
例句：
The car is now fettled and ready for the race. 汽车现在已经准备就绪，可以参加比赛了。

The engineer fettled the engine and it's running smoothly now.  工程师调整了引擎，现在运转顺畅了。


近义词：ready, prepared, fixed, repaired, adjusted
反义词：broken, malfunctioning, unprepared, out of order
词性：adjective


fettled:/ˈfɛt(ə)ld/
verb
修理，调整，使…准备好
To make ready, put in order, or repair something.
例句：
He spent the afternoon fettling his bicycle. 他花了一下午时间修理他的自行车。

We need to fettle the arrangements for the party. 我们需要为派对做好安排。



近义词：repair, fix, mend, adjust, prepare
反义词：break, damage, worsen
词性：verb



词源：From the Middle English *fetlen*, from Old English *fetel* ("belt, girdle, vessel"), of Germanic origin.  The original meaning was "to put a girdle on," hence "to prepare or make ready."
