misfiring:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪərɪŋ/
| adj.  n.| 失灵的，不发火的；失败的；未击中目标的 |Failing to fire or ignite properly. Also, failing to produce the intended result.| The misfiring engine caused the car to stall. 失灵的引擎导致汽车熄火。 |近义词：malfunctioning, failing, sputtering, backfiring, miscalculated, unsuccessful |反义词: functioning, working, successful
|adjective
misfire:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪər/
| v.  n.| 失灵，不发火；失败；未击中目标 |(of a gun or missile) fail to fire or explode properly. Also, (of a plan or project) fail to produce the intended result. | The plan misfired when the funding was withdrawn. 当资金被撤回时，该计划失败了。|近义词： malfunction, fail, backfire, flop, miscalculate |反义词: succeed, function, work
|verb, noun
