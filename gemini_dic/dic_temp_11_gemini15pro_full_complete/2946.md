adulteress: /əˈdʌltərɪs/
n.
(已婚)通奸的女人，淫妇
A woman who has committed adultery.
She was branded an adulteress after her affair was discovered. 她与人私通的事情败露后，她被打上了荡妇的烙印。
Synonyms:  unfaithful wife, cheating wife, two-timer, homewrecker
Antonyms: faithful wife, loyal wife
noun

being unfaithful (不忠)
two-timing someone (脚踏两条船)
