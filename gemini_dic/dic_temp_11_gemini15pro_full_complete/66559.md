exhibitionism:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪzəm/
noun
暴露癖，炫耀症
A mental condition characterized by the compulsion to display one's genitals in public.  The act or practice of behaving so as to attract attention to oneself.
He was arrested for exhibitionism after exposing himself in the park. 他因在公园里暴露自己而因暴露癖被捕。
Her flamboyant clothes were seen as a form of exhibitionism.  她艳丽的服装被视为一种炫耀。
synonyms:  immodesty, ostentation, showiness, flamboyance
antonyms:  modesty, humility, discretion, reserve
