catapultic:/ˌkætəˈpʌltɪk/| adj.|弹射的，投射的；突然的，飞跃的|Relating to a catapult; launching or projecting something with sudden force; sudden and dramatic.|The catapultic rise to fame of the young singer surprised everyone.  这位年轻歌手一跃成名，让所有人感到惊讶。|The company experienced a catapultic increase in sales after the new product launch.  新产品发布后，该公司销量猛增。|近义词：sudden, abrupt, dramatic, meteoric, explosive, rapid, swift|反义词：gradual, slow, steady|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin "catapulta" (catapult), from Greek "katapeltēs" (throwing down), from "kata-" (down) + "pallein" (to hurl).
