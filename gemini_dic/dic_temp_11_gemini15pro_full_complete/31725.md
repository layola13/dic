cheapened:/ˈtʃiːpənd/
verb
降低价值；贬低；使显得廉价
to make something seem less valuable or respected
Mass production has cheapened the image of handcrafted furniture.  批量生产降低了手工家具的形象。
devalued, degraded, lessened
enhanced, elevated, improved
词性:动词
