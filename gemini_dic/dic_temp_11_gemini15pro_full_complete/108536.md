lazarwort:/ˈleɪzərwɜːrt/ | n. | 拉撒路草，麻风草 | A European plant (Aristolochia clematitis) formerly used in treating leprosy and other skin diseases.  It is also known as birthwort. |  Lazarwort was once believed to be a powerful cure for leprosy. 拉撒路草曾被认为是治疗麻风病的良药。 |  birthwort,  aristolochia | N/A | noun
