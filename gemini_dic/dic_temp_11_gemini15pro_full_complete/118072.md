mendicate: /ˈmɛndɪkeɪt/| v. |乞讨，行乞|To ask for alms or charity; to beg. |He was forced to mendicate on the streets to survive. 他被迫在街上乞讨才能生存。|The homeless man mendicated for food and money.  这个无家可归的人乞讨食物和金钱。|近义词：beg, panhandle, implore|反义词：give, donate|词性: 动词

mendicate: /ˈmɛndɪkeɪt/| n. |乞讨，行乞（名词形式较少使用）|The act of begging. |Mendicate is unfortunately a common sight in many large cities.  在许多大城市，乞讨不幸是一种常见的景象。|His mendicate was a result of his unfortunate circumstances. 他的乞讨是由于他不幸的处境造成的。|近义词：begging, panhandling|反义词：giving, donating|词性: 名词
