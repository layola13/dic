banisher:/ˈbænɪʃər/| n.|放逐者，驱逐者|Someone who banishes another person or thing.|The king acted as judge, jury, and banisher, exiling his enemies from the kingdom. 国王身兼法官、陪审团和放逐者的角色，将他的敌人驱逐出王国。|Synonyms: expeller, exile, deportee|Antonyms: welcomer, greeter|noun
