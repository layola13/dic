gorhen:/ˈɡɔːrˌhɛn/| n. |水鸡，秧鸡|A wading bird of the rail family (Rallidae), typically inhabiting marshy areas and having short rounded wings and long toes.|例句：The gorhen scurried through the reeds. 水鸡快速穿过芦苇丛。|例句：We spotted a gorhen foraging near the pond. 我们看到一只水鸡在池塘边觅食。|近义词：rail, moorhen, coot|反义词:|名词
