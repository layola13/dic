blotchier:/ˈblɒtʃɪə(r)/ (US) /ˈblɑːtʃɪər/| adj.|更斑点的，污迹更多的|Covered with more or larger blotches than something else.  More uneven in color or tone.  |例句：Her skin was blotchier than usual after spending the day in the sun.  在太阳下待了一天后，她的皮肤比平时更斑点了。

例句：The paint job on the car was blotchier than I expected.  这辆车的喷漆比我预想的更不均匀。|近义词：patchier, spottier, more mottled, more uneven|反义词：clearer, smoother, more even, more uniform|词性:adjective
blotchy:/ˈblɒtʃi/ (US) /ˈblɑːtʃi/| adj.|斑点的，有污迹的|Marked with or characterized by blotches; uneven in color or tone.  |例句：His face was blotchy with tears. 他的脸上满是泪痕。

例句：The wall had a blotchy appearance after the damp set in.  潮湿过后，墙上出现了斑点。|近义词：spotted, patchy, mottled, uneven|反义词：clear, smooth, even, uniform|词性:adjective

词源: From blotch + -y/-ier.  "Blotch" comes from Middle English "blocche," of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse "blakkr" (pale, bleached).
