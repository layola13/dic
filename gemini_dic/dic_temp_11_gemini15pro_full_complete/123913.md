morel:/ˈmɔːrəl/ (US), /ˈmɒrəl/ (UK)| n. |羊肚菌|An edible mushroom (genus Morchella) having a pitted, spongelike cap.|例句：We found morels growing in the woods. 我们在树林里发现了羊肚菌。|例句：Morels are a prized delicacy in French cuisine. 羊肚菌是法国菜中珍贵的美味佳肴。|近义词：mushroom, fungus|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps poisonous mushroom)|词性:noun
