environmentalism:/ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmentəlɪzəm/
n.|环保主义，环境保护论|A social and political movement dedicated to protecting the natural world from the harmful effects of human activity.|例句：Environmentalism is gaining momentum as people become more aware of climate change.  环保主义随着人们对气候变化的认识越来越强而 gaining momentum。

例句：She dedicated her life to environmentalism, fighting for the preservation of forests. 她毕生致力于环保事业，为保护森林而奋斗。|近义词：conservationism, ecologism|反义词：anti-environmentalism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From environment + -al + -ism.  "Environment" comes from Old French environner (to surround), from environ (around).  "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns that denote actions, practices, doctrines, or adherence to a certain principle.
