nonapologetic:/ˌnɒnəˌpɒləˈdʒɛtɪk/ | adj. | 不道歉的，不后悔的，理直气壮的 | Not feeling or showing any regret or shame about one's actions or opinions. |  She was nonapologetic about her decision to leave the company. 她对离开公司的决定毫不后悔。 | Example: He gave a nonapologetic shrug. 他耸了耸肩，毫无歉意。| Synonyms: unapologetic, unrepentant, remorseless | Antonyms: apologetic, remorseful, repentant | adjective
