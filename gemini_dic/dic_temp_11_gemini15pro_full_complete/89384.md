homophilic:/ˌhɒməˈfɪlɪk/ (US) /ˌhoʊməˈfɪlɪk/| adj.|同嗜性的，同质相吸的|Tending to be attracted to those similar to oneself, especially in sexual orientation.|Homophilic interactions between cells contribute to tissue formation. 细胞间的同嗜性相互作用有助于组织的形成。|Synonyms: like-minded, similar, kindred, like attracts like|Antonyms: heterophilic, dissimilar|adjective

homophily:/ˌhɒməˈfɪli/ (US) /ˌhoʊməˈfɪli/| n.|同嗜性，同质相吸|The tendency for people to associate and bond with similar others.|Homophily is a common phenomenon in social networks. 同质相吸是社交网络中的常见现象。|Synonyms: like-mindedness, similarity, kindredness|Antonyms: heterophily, dissimilarity|noun
