laut:/laʊt/| adj.| loud; noisy (especially of music) |Loud or noisy, particularly in reference to music.  Derived from Middle High German "lūt". Originally meant "of the people, belonging to the laity," later evolving to "audible, clear," and then to its current meaning.| The music at the concert was too laut. 音乐会上的音乐太吵了。| The band played a laut and energetic set. 乐队演奏了一组响亮而充满活力的曲目。|synonyms: loud, noisy, boisterous, clamorous | antonyms: quiet, soft, silent | adjective
