cofinance:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnæns/
v. |共同出资; 联合融资|To finance something together with another or others.|The project was cofinanced by several European governments.|近义词：jointly finance, fund together|反义词：solely fund|词性:动词

cofinance:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnæns/
n. |共同融资; 联合筹资|The act of financing something together with another or others.|The bridge construction required cofinance from private investors.|近义词：joint financing, shared funding|反义词：sole funding|词性:名词

词源: co- (together) + finance
