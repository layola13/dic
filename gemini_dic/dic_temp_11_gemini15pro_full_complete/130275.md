non-subscribing:/nɒn-səbˈskraɪbɪŋ/
adjective
非订阅的，未订阅的
Not having subscribed; not agreeing with or supporting something.
例句：The non-subscribing members will not receive the newsletter.  非订阅会员将不会收到简报。

例句：He held a non-subscribing view on the new policy. 他对新政策持不赞同的态度。
synonyms:unsubscribed, dissenting, objecting
antonyms:subscribing, agreeing, supporting
词性:adjective
