banterers:/ˈbæntərərz/
noun
开玩笑的人，爱打趣的人
People who engage in playful teasing or exchange of witty remarks.
The banterers in the corner were having a lively discussion.  角落里那群爱开玩笑的人正在进行热烈的讨论。
jokers, wits, teasers
serious people, quiet people
