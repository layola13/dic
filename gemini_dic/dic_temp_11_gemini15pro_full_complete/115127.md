mangle:/ˈmæŋɡəl/ (US), /ˈmæŋɡl/ (UK)| v. n.| 损坏，毁坏；乱砍；轧布机 |To severely damage or destroy something by cutting, tearing, or crushing it; a machine with rollers used for pressing clothes or sheets.|The car was mangled in the accident. 这辆车在事故中被撞毁了。|近义词：damage, ruin, mutilate, destroy, crush|反义词：repair, fix, mend, construct|词性:动词,名词

mangle:/ˈmæŋɡəl/ (US), /ˈmæŋɡl/ (UK)| n.|轧布机；严重损坏的状态|A machine with rollers used for pressing clothes or sheets; a severely damaged state.|The wreckage of the plane was a mangled mess. 飞机残骸是一片狼藉。|近义词：wreck, mess, shambles|反义词：order, neatness|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *mangler, frequentative of mangoner "to traffic, trade," from Late Latin mango "dealer, trader," of uncertain origin.  Originally referred to damaging goods through rough handling in trade, later broadened to general damage.
