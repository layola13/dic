dimissory:/dɪˈmɪsəri/
adjective
解雇的；允许离开的；准予毕业的
granting leave to depart; of or relating to dismissal
The bishop gave him a dimissory letter, allowing him to join a different diocese. 主教给了他一封解雇信，允许他加入另一个教区。
Synonyms: releasing, discharging, freeing
Antonyms: retaining, holding, keeping
词性: adjective


dimissory:/dɪˈmɪsəri/
noun
解雇信；准予毕业证书
a letter of dismissal
He received his dimissory from the college, marking the end of his studies. 他收到了大学的毕业证书，标志着他的学习生涯结束。
Synonyms: dismissal, discharge, release
Antonyms: retention, holding
词性: noun
