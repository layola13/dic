enkennel:/ɪnˈkɛn(ə)l/
n.|狗舍，犬舍|A small shelter or house for a dog.|The dog sleeps in his kennel at night. 狗晚上睡在它的狗窝里。
vt.|把…关进狗舍|To confine in a kennel.|We kenneled the dogs during the storm.  暴风雨期间，我们把狗关进了狗舍。
近义词:doghouse, kennel
反义词: N/A
词性: noun, verb
词源: From Old French *kenil, probably from Vulgar Latin *canile "dog house," from Latin canis "dog."
