monarchian:/məˈnɑːrkiən/ |  adj. | 君主主义的 | Relating to or characterized by monarchianism. |  Monarchian theology emphasizes the oneness of God.  君主制神学强调上帝的唯一性。 | synonym: Unitarian | antonym: Trinitarian | adjective
