frees-up:/friːz ˈʌp/| v.|释放，腾出|to make something available for use by removing something else; to make (someone) available|例句：Cleaning out the garage freed up a lot of space.  清理车库腾出了很多空间。

例句：Finishing the project early freed me up to take some time off.  提前完成项目让我有时间休息一下。|近义词：release, liberate, clear|反义词:occupy, tie up|词性:动词
