gratitudes:/ˈɡrætɪtjuːdz/
n.
感激之情，感谢
feelings of thankfulness or appreciation.
She expressed her gratitudes to everyone who had helped her. 她向所有帮助过她的人表达了感激之情。
近义词:thankfulness, appreciation, gratefulness
反义词:ingratitude, resentment
词性:名词
