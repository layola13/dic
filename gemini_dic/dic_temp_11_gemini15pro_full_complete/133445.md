nonfatalistic:/ˌnɒnˌfeɪtəlˈɪstɪk/ /ˌnɑːnˌfeɪtəlˈɪstɪk/
adjective
非宿命论的
Not believing in fate; believing that one can control one's own destiny.
She has a nonfatalistic attitude toward life and believes she can shape her own future. 她对生活抱有非宿命论的态度，相信自己可以塑造自己的未来。
He took a nonfatalistic approach to his illness, researching treatments and remaining positive. 他以非宿命论的态度对待自己的疾病，研究治疗方法并保持积极的态度。
Synonyms: optimistic, hopeful, positive, proactive
Antonyms: fatalistic, pessimistic, resigned, defeatist
词性: adjective
