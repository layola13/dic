nonfarmers:/ˈnɑːnˌfɑːrmərz/
noun
非农人口；非农业人口
People who do not work in agriculture.
The government is trying to create more job opportunities for nonfarmers. 政府正在努力为非农人口创造更多就业机会。
Synonyms: non-agricultural workers, urban population
Antonyms: farmers, agricultural workers
词性: 名词
