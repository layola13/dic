factorability:/ˌfæktərəˈbɪləti/| n.|可分解性，可因式分解性|The quality of being able to be factored or resolved into factors.  It often refers to the ability of a mathematical expression, like a polynomial, to be expressed as a product of simpler expressions.|The factorability of the quadratic equation made it easy to solve.  这个二次方程的可因式分解性使它很容易解。|factorizability is crucial in simplifying complex expressions. 可因式分解性在简化复杂表达式中至关重要。|factorization, resolvability|indecomposability|noun
