belibel:/bɪˈlaɪbəl/| v.|诽谤，中伤|To falsely and maliciously accuse someone of something, often in writing or speech, that damages their reputation.|He was libelled in the newspaper article, which falsely claimed he was involved in criminal activity. 他在报纸文章中被诽谤，文章谎称他参与了犯罪活动。|The magazine had to pay substantial damages for libelling the actress. 这家杂志不得不为诽谤这位女演员支付巨额赔偿金。|近义词：slander, defame, smear, calumniate|反义词：extol, praise, commend|词性:动词
