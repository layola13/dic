friller:/ˈfrɪlər/| n.|褶边，饰边，多褶边的衣服|A decorative edging of cloth gathered into small folds, typically used on clothing or curtains.  A thing that is unnecessarily elaborate or showy. A person who likes or wears frills or frilly clothes.|The dress had a delicate lace friller around the neckline. 这件衣服领口有一圈精致的蕾丝褶边。|Synonyms: ruffle, flounce, furbelow, ornament, embellishment|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun

frilly:/ˈfrɪli/| adj.|多褶边的，饰边的|Decorated with frills or having many frills. Characterized by excessive ornamentation or fussiness.|She wore a frilly pink dress to the party. 她穿了一件多褶边的粉红色连衣裙去参加聚会。|Synonyms: ruffled, flounced, lacy, ornate, fussy|Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned|adjective
