abasedly:/əˈbeɪsɪdli/
adverb
卑躬屈膝地，低声下气地
in a manner that shows shame and humility; in a humiliated way.
He abasedly apologized for his mistake. 他低声下气地为自己的错误道歉。
近义词:humbly, meekly, subserviently
反义词:proudly, arrogantly, haughtily
词性:adverb

词源:
From abase + -ed + -ly.  Abase comes from the Old French *abesser, from the prefix a- "to" + base "low."
