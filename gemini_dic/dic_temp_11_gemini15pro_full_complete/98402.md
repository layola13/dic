insolvency's:/ɪnˈsɑːlvənsiz/
noun
无力偿还;破产
the state of not having enough money to pay debts
The company filed for insolvency after years of declining profits.  这家公司在连年亏损后申请破产。
近义词:bankruptcy, failure
反义词:solvency, affluence
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French insolvable, from Latin insolvens, insolent- ‘unable to pay’, from in- ‘not’ + solvere ‘to loosen, pay’.
