emulsic: /ɪˈmʌlsɪk/ | adj. | 乳化的，乳状的 |  Of or relating to an emulsion; having the properties of an emulsion. | The emulsic mixture was thick and creamy.  乳状混合物浓稠而滑腻。| 近义词: emulsified, milky | 反义词: separated, immiscible | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēmulsus*, past participle of *ēmulgēre* "to milk out," from *ex* "out" + *mulgēre* "to milk."
