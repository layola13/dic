information's:/ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. |信息的，情报的 (罕用，通常用information)|  Possessive form of information, meaning "belonging to information".  It's generally considered grammatically incorrect and awkward. Use "of information" instead. | The information's source was unknown. (Awkward - better: The source of the information was unknown.) | 近义词: (N/A -  possessive) | 反义词: (N/A - possessive) | 词性: noun (possessive)
