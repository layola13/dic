interrhyming:/ˌɪntərˈraɪmɪŋ/ | adj. | 内部押韵的 |  A type of rhyme involving words within the same line.  It's where two or more words within a single line rhyme with each other. |  "The cat in the hat sat on the mat." The words "cat," "hat," and "mat" are interrhyming. | Near synonyms: internal rhyme, leonine rhyme | Antonyms: end rhyme, external rhyme | adjective
