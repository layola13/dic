bardic:/ˈbɑːrdɪk/ | adj. |吟游诗人的，吟游诗人风格的|Relating to bards or their art; characteristic of bards or their poetry.|The bardic tradition is still alive in some parts of the world. 世界上的一些地方仍然保留着吟游诗人的传统。|The bardic tales were full of magic and wonder. 吟游诗人的故事充满了魔法和奇迹。|近义词：poetic, lyrical, musical|反义词：prosaic, mundane|词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *bardik, from Old French bardique, from Late Latin bardicus, from Latin bardus (“bard”) (from Gaulish *bardos).
