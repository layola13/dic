garlics:/ˈɡɑːrlɪks/| n.|大蒜（复数，通常用garlic）|The word "garlics" is the plural form of "garlic," but it's less common.  While grammatically correct, native speakers usually use "garlic" even when referring to multiple cloves or bulbs.  It refers to the pungent, edible bulb of the plant *Allium sativum*, used as a seasoning or condiment.|I used three cloves of garlic in the sauce. (More common than "I used three garlics.") 我在酱汁里用了三瓣大蒜。|近义词：(Usually none for the plural form. For the singular "garlic":  Allium sativum)|反义词: (None)|词性:Noun
