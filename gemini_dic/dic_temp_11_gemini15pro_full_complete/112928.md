luge:/luːʒ/ | n. vi.|雪橇，小型滑板;乘雪橇滑行|A luge is a small sled used for sliding down a track of ice or snow.  | He competed in the luge at the Winter Olympics. 他参加了冬奥会的雪橇比赛。 | 近义词:sled, toboggan | 反义词: None | 词性: noun, verb


luged:/luːʒd/ | v. |乘雪橇滑行|Past tense and past participle of "luge". | They luged down the hill at breakneck speed.  他们以惊人的速度乘雪橇滑下山。| 近义词:sledded, tobogganed | 反义词:None | 词性: verb


词源: From French *luge*, from Franco-Provençal *luge*, *lise* “sledge”, from Gaulish *litos*.
