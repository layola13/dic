naturalizers:/ˈnætʃrəˌlaɪzərz/
n.
归化者；入籍者；移植者
People who become citizens of a country they were not born in; things that help something adapt to a new environment.
Many naturalizers come to this country seeking better opportunities. 许多归化者来到这个国家寻求更好的机会。
The introduction of naturalizers like earthworms can improve soil health. 引进蚯蚓等移植者可以改善土壤健康。
synonyms:  new citizens, immigrants, settlers (for people); acclimatizers, adapters (for things)
antonyms: natives, indigenous people (for people)
noun


naturalize:/ˈnætʃrəˌlaɪz/
v.
使归化；使入籍；移植；适应
To grant full citizenship to a foreigner; to introduce a plant or animal to a new environment and help it thrive.
The government will naturalize several thousand immigrants this year. 政府今年将使几千名移民入籍。
They successfully naturalized the orchids in their greenhouse. 他们成功地将兰花移植到温室中。
synonyms:  acclimatize, adapt, assimilate
antonyms: expatriate, deport
verb
