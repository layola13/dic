discontentments:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntmənts/
n.
不满，不满足
a feeling of wanting something more or different from what you have; things that make you feel dissatisfied
The survey revealed widespread discontentments among the workers.  这项调查揭示了工人们普遍存在的不满情绪。
Near synonyms: dissatisfaction, disaffection, grievances, complaints
Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, happiness
noun
