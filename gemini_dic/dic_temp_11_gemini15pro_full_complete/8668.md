anticonsumerism:/ˌænti kənˈsuːmərɪzəm/
|n.|反消费主义|Opposition to consumerism, the equation of personal happiness with the purchase and consumption of material possessions.| Many young people are embracing anticonsumerism and choosing experiences over things. 许多年轻人正在拥抱反消费主义，选择体验而不是物质。|Synonyms: anti-materialism, simple living, voluntary simplicity|Antonyms: consumerism, materialism|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
consumerism (noun): the preoccupation of society with the acquisition of consumer goods.
