outpolling:/ˌaʊtˈpoʊlɪŋ/
verb
在民意调查中领先
receiving more votes than another candidate in an election poll.
例句：Candidate A is outpolling Candidate B by a significant margin.  候选人A在民意调查中大幅领先候选人B。

例句：Despite the negative press, the incumbent is still outpolling the challenger. 尽管负面新闻不断，现任官员仍在民意调查中领先挑战者。
近义词：surpassing, exceeding, beating
反义词：trailing, losing to
词性：v.
