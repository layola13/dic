hdri:/ˈeɪtʃ diː ɑːr ˈaɪ/| n.|高动态范围图像 (High Dynamic Range Image)|A high dynamic range image (HDRI or HDR image) is a photographic or computer-generated image that has a wider dynamic range than standard images.|Example 1: The artist used an HDRI to create a realistic lighting effect in the 3D scene.  艺术家使用 HDRI 在 3D 场景中创建逼真的照明效果。

Example 2: HDRIs are often used in architectural visualization to achieve photorealistic results. HDRI 常用于建筑可视化以获得逼真的效果。|近义词：HDR image, HDR photograph|反义词:LDRI (Low Dynamic Range Image), standard image|词性:名词
