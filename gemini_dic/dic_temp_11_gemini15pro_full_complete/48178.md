decomposabilities:/diːˌkɒmpəʊzəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可分解性；可降解性|The decomposabilities of different materials vary greatly. 不同材料的可分解性差异很大。| This study investigates the decomposabilities of various plastic types under different environmental conditions.  这项研究调查了各种塑料类型在不同环境条件下的可分解性。|近义词：degradabilities, biodegradabilities|反义词：inertness, stability|词性: Noun (plural)

decomposability:/diːˌkɒmpəʊzəˈbɪləti/| n. |可分解性；可降解性|The decomposability of organic matter is crucial for soil health. 有机物的可分解性对土壤健康至关重要。|The decomposability of this new plastic is a key selling point. 这种新型塑料的可分解性是一个关键卖点。|近义词：degradability, biodegradability|反义词：inertness, stability|词性: Noun (singular)

decomposable:/diːkəmˈpəʊzəbl/| adj. |可分解的；可降解的|Food waste is decomposable. 食物残渣是可分解的。|Many plastics are not easily decomposable. 许多塑料不易分解。|近义词：degradable, biodegradable|反义词：inert, stable, non-biodegradable|词性: Adjective


Etymology:
From decompose (分解) + -able (可...的) + -ity (名词后缀，表示性质或状态) + -ies (名词复数后缀)
