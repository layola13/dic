bevel:/ˈbevəl/ (US), /ˈbɛvəl/ (UK) | vt. |  将…切成斜面，使成斜角 | To cut or shape to a sloping edge or surface. | He beveled the edges of the glass to make it safer. 他把玻璃的边缘磨成斜角，使它更安全。 | synonyms: slant, angle, slope, chamfer | antonyms: straighten, flatten | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bivel, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old North French beivre "to drink," referring to the slanted edge of a drinking vessel.
