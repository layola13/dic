nonculturally:/nɒnˈkʌltʃərəli/
adjective
非文化地；不考虑文化因素地
Not influenced by or related to culture; in a way that does not consider cultural differences.
Her approach to the problem was nonculturally sensitive, leading to misunderstandings. 她处理这个问题的方式对文化不够敏感，导致了误解。
non-cultural, acultural
culturally
