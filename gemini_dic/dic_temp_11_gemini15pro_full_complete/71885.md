fleur-de-lis:/ˌflɜːr də ˈliː/ |n.|鸢尾花；百合花饰|A stylized lily or iris, used as a decorative motif or symbol, especially in French heraldry.|The fleur-de-lis is a common symbol in French architecture. 鸢尾花是法国建筑中常见的象征。|The fleur-de-lis adorned the king's robes.  鸢尾花装饰着国王的长袍。|Synonyms: iris, lily (as a symbol), emblem, insignia|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
