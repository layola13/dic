overarticulated: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度清晰发音的；矫揉造作的 | Pronounced with exaggerated clarity, often to an unnatural or affected degree.  This can sometimes make speech sound stilted or artificial. |  She overarticulated every word, as if speaking to a child or someone hard of hearing. 她每个字都发音过度清晰，好像在跟小孩或听力不好的人说话。

The actor overarticulated his lines, which made the performance seem insincere. 演员的台词发音过于清晰，使得表演显得不真诚。 | 近义词:  precise, affected, exaggerated, artificial | 反义词:  slurred, mumbled, indistinct, natural | 词性: 形容词
