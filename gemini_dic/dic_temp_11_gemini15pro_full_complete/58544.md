dugong:/ˈduːɡɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈduːɡɑːŋ/ (US)| n. |儒艮|A large, plant-eating marine mammal related to the manatee, found in warm coastal waters of the Indian and western Pacific Oceans.|例句：The dugong grazed peacefully on the seagrass. 儒艮平静地在海草上吃草。

例句：Dugongs are vulnerable to extinction due to habitat loss. 由于栖息地丧失，儒艮很容易灭绝。|近义词：sea cow|反义词: None|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Malay *duyuŋ, ultimately from Proto-Malayo-Polynesian *dujuŋ.
