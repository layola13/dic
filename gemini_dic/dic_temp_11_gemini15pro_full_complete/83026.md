gunsmiths:/ˈɡʌnsmɪθs/
n.
枪匠，修枪匠
A person who makes or repairs firearms.
The old gunsmiths crafted each rifle with meticulous care. 老枪匠们精心制作每一支步枪。
synonyms: armorer, gunmaker
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "pacifist" in a broader sense)
noun
