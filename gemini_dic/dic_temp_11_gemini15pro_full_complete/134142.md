nonimportation:/ˌnɑːnɪmpɔːrˈteɪʃən/ |n.| 不进口；进口抵制|The act of not importing goods, typically as a form of protest.  不进口商品的行为，通常作为一种抗议形式。|例句：The colonists agreed on nonimportation of British goods. 殖民者同意不进口英国商品。

例句：Nonimportation of luxury goods was encouraged to stimulate the local economy.  鼓励不进口奢侈品以刺激当地经济。


近义词：boycott, embargo|反义词：importation|词性：名词
