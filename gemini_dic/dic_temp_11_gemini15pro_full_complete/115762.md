marinara:/ˌmærɪˈnɑːrə/ | n. | 腌番茄酱，水手风味番茄酱 | A tomato sauce, usually seasoned with herbs, garlic, and onions, often served with pasta. |  I made spaghetti with marinara sauce for dinner. 我晚饭做了意大利面配腌番茄酱。

The pizza was topped with fresh mozzarella and marinara.  披萨上面放了新鲜的马苏里拉奶酪和腌番茄酱。| 近义词:tomato sauce | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Italian *marinara*, feminine of *marinaro* (“of or pertaining to sailors or the sea”), because it was supposedly a sauce commonly prepared by sailors' wives upon their return from sea.  来自意大利语 *marinara*，*marinaro*（“属于水手或海洋的”）的阴性形式，因为它据说是水手们的妻子在他们从海上回来后通常准备的一种酱汁。
