overloaded:/ˌəʊvərˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌoʊvərˈloʊdɪd/ | adj. | 过载的，超负荷的 | Having too much of something; loaded beyond capacity. |  The circuit is overloaded and may cause a fire. 电路过载，可能会引起火灾。 | synonyms: overburdened, overwhelmed, swamped, saturated | antonyms: underloaded, light | adjective

overloaded:/ˌəʊvərˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌoʊvərˈloʊdɪd/ | v. |  使超载，使过载  | To load with too great a burden or cargo. | Don't overload the washing machine, or it might break. 不要让洗衣机超载，否则可能会损坏。 | synonyms: overburden, overtax, overwhelm | antonyms: underload, lighten | verb
