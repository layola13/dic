nonsubordinating:/ˌnɒnsəˈbɔːdɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非从属的，并列的
Connecting two or more clauses or phrases of equal grammatical rank and not showing any relation of dependence.  It signifies a relationship where one clause doesn't depend on another for its meaning.
She was tired and hungry, so she went home. 她又累又饿，所以回家了。
The dog barked, and the cat ran away. 狗叫了，猫跑了。
Synonyms: coordinating, independent
Antonyms: subordinating, dependent
词性: adjective
