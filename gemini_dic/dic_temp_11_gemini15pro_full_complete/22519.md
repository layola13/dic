boomings:/ˈbuːmɪŋz/
| n. | 繁荣，兴旺；隆隆声 |A period of rapid growth; loud, resonant sounds.|The boomings of the construction site woke me up every morning.  建筑工地的隆隆声每天早上都把我吵醒。|近义词：booms, prosperities, roars |反义词:slumps, declines, silences |词性:名词(Noun), 复数形式

Etymology:
From the verb "boom,"  which originates from Middle Dutch "bōmen" (to resound, drum), imitative in origin. The "-ing" suffix creates a gerund or present participle, and the "-s" marks it as plural.  最终来自模仿鼓声或回响的拟声词。
