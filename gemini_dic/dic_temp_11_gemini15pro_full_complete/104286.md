justiciarship: /dʒʌˈstɪʃiərʃɪp/
n.
司法官的职位；司法官的任期
The office or tenure of a justiciar.  (A justiciar was a high-ranking judicial officer, especially in medieval England.)
The justiciarship was a powerful position in the king's absence.  司法官的职位在国王不在的时候拥有很大的权力。
His justiciarship lasted for five years. 他的司法官任期持续了五年。
Synonyms: judgeship, magistracy, office
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider words relating to lack of power or authority like powerlessness, subordination)
词性: noun
