hit-the-hay:/hɪt ðə heɪ/
verb phrase
去睡觉
To go to bed.  This idiom evokes the image of farmhands retiring to a bed of hay.
例句：I'm exhausted, I'm going to hit the hay. 我累坏了，我要去睡觉了。

例句：It's getting late, time to hit the hay. 天色晚了，该睡觉了。
近义词：go to bed, retire, turn in, sack out
反义词：get up, wake up
词性：动词短语
