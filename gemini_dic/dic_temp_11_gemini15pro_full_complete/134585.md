nonloyal:/nɒnˈlɔɪəl/ | adj. | 不忠诚的，不忠实的 | Not loyal; disloyal.  Failing to be faithful to commitments or obligations. | He was accused of being nonloyal to the company. 他被指责对公司不忠。|  The nonloyal employee leaked confidential information to a competitor.  这个不忠的员工向竞争对手泄露了机密信息。 | Synonyms: disloyal, faithless, treacherous, unfaithful | Antonyms: loyal, faithful, devoted, steadfast | adjective
