emissary: /ˈɛmɪsɛri/ (US), /ˈɛmɪsəri/ (UK)| n.|使者，特使|A person sent on a special mission, usually diplomatic.|The king sent an emissary to negotiate a peace treaty. 国王派了一名使者去谈判和平条约。|近义词: envoy, ambassador, delegate, representative, agent|反义词: receiver, recipient|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *emissarius*, from Latin ēmissus, past participle of ēmittere "to send out," from ē- "out" + mittere "to send."
