ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of the name "Maine" is uncertain.  One theory suggests it derives from the French province of Maine. Another posits it comes from a nautical term, "mainland," used by early explorers to distinguish the mainland from islands. A third theory suggests it comes from an Algonquian word.
