broomstick:/ˈbruːmstɪk/ | n. |扫帚柄；飞天扫帚|The handle of a broom, typically made of wood; A witch's flying broom. |She leaped onto her broomstick and soared into the night sky. 她骑上飞天扫帚，飞入夜空。|The witch flew on her broomstick. 女巫骑着她的扫帚飞走了。| synonyms: broomshaft, besom | antonyms: None | noun
