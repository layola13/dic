asceticism:/əˈsɛtɪsɪzəm/
n.
禁欲主义，苦行
severe self-discipline and avoidance of all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.
He practiced asceticism to purify his soul.  他实行禁欲主义来净化他的灵魂。
self-denial, abstinence, austerity, self-discipline,mortification
hedonism, indulgence, luxury, self-indulgence
noun
