orderable:/ˈɔːrdərəbl/| adj.|可订购的，可预订的|Able to be ordered or requested.  Something that can be placed in a sequence or arranged systematically.|The new phone is orderable online starting tomorrow.  这款新手机明天开始可以在线订购。|Synonyms: available, obtainable, procurable|Antonyms: unavailable, unobtainable|adjective
