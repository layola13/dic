discouraging:/dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
令人沮丧的；使人泄气的
Causing someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm; disheartening.
The news from the front lines was discouraging.  前线的消息令人沮丧。
synonyms: disheartening, demoralizing, dispiriting, depressing, sapping, dampening, weakening
antonyms: encouraging, heartening, inspiring, uplifting
词性: adjective


discourage:/dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒ/
verb
使气馁；劝阻
To cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm.  To try to prevent or dissuade (someone) from doing something.
The coach discouraged the team from celebrating prematurely. 教练劝队员们不要过早庆祝。
synonyms: deter, dissuade, hinder, prevent
antonyms: encourage, promote, support, urge
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + courage.  Ultimately from Latin cor (heart).
