inanimation:/ˌɪnænɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.
无生命; 无生气; 缺乏活力
lack of animation; lifelessness.
The statue stood in utter inanimation. 这座雕像完全一动不动。
近义词：lifelessness, inertia, inactivity
反义词：animation, life, vitality
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inanimatio, from in- (not) + animatio (animation).
