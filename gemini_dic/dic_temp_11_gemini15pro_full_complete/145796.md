overdecadent:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkədənt/ | adj. | 过于颓废的，过度放纵的 | Excessively decadent; characterized by excessive luxury, self-indulgence, and moral decay.  Going beyond the usual bounds of decadence. |  The overdecadent lifestyle of the Roman emperors ultimately contributed to the empire's decline. 罗马皇帝过度颓废的生活方式最终导致了帝国的衰落。

The party was an overdecadent affair, with mountains of caviar and champagne.  这场派对过于颓废，堆满了鱼子酱和香槟。| 近义词：excessively indulgent, overly luxurious, dissipated, profligate | 反义词：austere, ascetic, moderate, restrained | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->

From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "decadent" (from French "décadent," from Latin "decadens," present participle of "decadere" - "to decay, decline").
