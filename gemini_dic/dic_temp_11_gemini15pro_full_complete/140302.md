ogham: /ˈɒɡəm/ | n. | 欧甘文 | An ancient alphabet used in Ireland, consisting of a series of strokes and notches cut across a central stem line. | Example: The inscription on the stone was written in ogham. 石碑上的铭文是用欧甘文写的。 | Example:  Scholars are still deciphering some ogham inscriptions. 学者们仍在破译一些欧甘文铭文。 | Synonym:  script, alphabet | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a writing system) | noun
