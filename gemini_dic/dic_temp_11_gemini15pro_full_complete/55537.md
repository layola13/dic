distributist:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪst/ | n. adj. | 分配主义者；分配主义的 | A person who believes in distributism, a political and economic theory advocating the widespread ownership of property.  A system where productive property is owned by the widest possible number of people. |  Example: He's a staunch distributist, arguing for policies that empower small businesses and local communities. 他是一位坚定的分配主义者，主张赋能小型企业和地方社区的政策。| Example:  Distributist policies aim to create a more equitable society. 分配主义政策旨在创造一个更公平的社会。 | Synonyms: decentralist, localist | Antonyms: centralist, collectivist | noun, adjective
