insertable:/ɪnˈsəːtəbəl/| adj.|可插入的，可嵌入的|Something that is insertable can be inserted into something else.  It describes the ability of an object to be put or fitted into another object. |The leaflet is insertable into the magazine. 这张传单可以插入杂志中。|The USB drive is insertable into the computer port. 这个U盘可以插入电脑接口。|近义词：injectable, implantable, attachable|反义词：non-insertable, fixed, permanent|词性:形容词
