aloha:/əˈloʊhɑː/| n. int.|你好，再见，爱，感情，同情|A Hawaiian word used as a greeting or farewell, expressing affection, love, peace, compassion, and pity.|例句：Aloha! Welcome to Hawaii! 你好！欢迎来到夏威夷！|例句：We said our alohas and parted ways. 我们互相道别后就分开了。|近义词：hello, goodbye, love, affection|反义词:hate, animosity|词性:名词,感叹词
