inertial:/ɪˈnɜːʃ(ə)l/ /ɪˈnɝːʃəl/ | adj. |惯性的，惰性的| Relating to inertia; resisting change in motion or direction.  Possessing inertia.  |例句：An inertial navigation system uses gyroscopes to maintain its orientation.  惯性导航系统使用陀螺仪来保持其方向。

例句：The inertial mass of an object is a measure of its resistance to acceleration.  一个物体的惯性质量是衡量其抵抗加速度的指标。|近义词：passive, resistant, immobile |反义词：active, dynamic, mobile |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inertialis*, from iners (“idle, inactive”) + -alis (suffix forming adjectives from nouns).
