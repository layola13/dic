nonreflectiveness:/ˌnɑːnˌriːˈflɛktɪvnəs/
n.
无反射性；不反光；不反省
Lack of reflection, either literally (not reflecting light) or figuratively (not engaging in introspection).
The nonreflectiveness of the matte finish was ideal for the photographer.  哑光表面的无反射性对于摄影师来说非常理想。
The nonreflectiveness of his personality prevented him from learning from his mistakes. 他不反省的性格使他无法从错误中吸取教训。
near synonyms: opacity, dullness, unreflectiveness, thoughtlessness
antonyms: reflectivity, brilliance, reflectiveness, thoughtfulness
noun
