antiracists:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪsts/
|n.|反种族主义者|People who actively oppose racism and discrimination based on race.|Many antiracists marched in the protest against racial injustice. 许多反种族主义者参加了抗议种族不公的游行。| 近义词: egalitarian, civil rights activist | 反义词: racist, bigot | 词性: 名词

antiracist:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪst/
|adj.|反种族主义的|Opposing racism and promoting racial equality.|She supports antiracist policies. 她支持反种族主义政策。| 近义词: anti-discrimination, non-racist | 反义词: racist, discriminatory | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "anti-" (against) + "racist" (one who believes in racism).  "Anti-" comes from Greek, and "racist" is a relatively modern word derived from "race."
