lincture:/ˈlɪŋktʃər/| n.|药剂;尤指甜的或黏稠的药水|A liquid medicine, especially one that is sweet or syrupy.|The doctor prescribed a lincture for her cough. 医生给她开了一种治咳嗽的药水。|近义词:potion, elixir, syrup|反义词:None|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *lincture*, from Latin *linctūra* (“a licking, a sucking”), from *linct* (“licked”), past participle of *lingō* (“to lick”).
