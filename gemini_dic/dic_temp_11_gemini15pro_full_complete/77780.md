generalised:/ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 普遍的，广义的，概括的 | Made more general or applicable to a wider range; not specific or specialized. |  This is a generalised theory that can be applied to many different situations.  这是一个可以应用于许多不同情况的普遍理论。 | generalized (American spelling), generic, broad, comprehensive | specific, particular, specialized | adjective
