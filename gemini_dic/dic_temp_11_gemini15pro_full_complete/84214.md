half-romantically:/ˌhæf roʊˈmæntɪkli/ | adv. | 半浪漫地；略带浪漫地 |  Combining "half" (meaning partially) and "romantically" (in a romantic manner), it describes something done with a touch of romance, but not fully or wholeheartedly. |  He looked at her half-romantically, half-amused. 他半是浪漫半是好玩地看着她。|  She described their first meeting half-romantically, as if it were a scene from a movie. 她略带浪漫地描述了他们的第一次相遇，仿佛是电影里的一个场景。 | Near Synonyms:  semi-romantically, slightly romantically,  somewhat romantically | Antonyms:  unromantically, pragmatically |  adverb
