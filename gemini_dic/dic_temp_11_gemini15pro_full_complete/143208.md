outcrier:/ˈaʊtˌkraɪər/| n.|大声疾呼者，公开谴责者|A person who publicly denounces or protests something.|The outcrier warned the villagers of the approaching storm. 大声疾呼者警告村民即将到来的暴风雨。|近义词：protestor, objector, critic, denouncer|反义词:supporter, advocate|词性:noun
Etymology: From out- + cry + -er.  "Out-" indicates a loud or public action, "cry" refers to shouting or proclaiming, and "-er" denotes a person who performs the action.
