mandola:/mænˈdoʊlə/ | n. | 曼陀林 | A musical instrument of the lute family, similar to a mandolin but larger and with a deeper tone. It typically has four courses of doubled strings tuned in fifths. | He played a beautiful melody on his mandola. 他用他的曼陀林演奏了一曲优美的旋律。|  近义词: mandolin, lute | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | 词性: noun
