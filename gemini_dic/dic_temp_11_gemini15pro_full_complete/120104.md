mimetic:/mɪˈmɛtɪk/ | adj. | 模仿的，拟态的 | Characterized by, exhibiting, or of the nature of mimicry; imitative. |  The chameleon's mimetic abilities allow it to blend in with its surroundings. 变色龙的拟态能力使其能够与周围环境融为一体。

The comedian's mimetic performance of the president was hilarious.  这位喜剧演员对总统的模仿表演十分滑稽。| 近义词: imitative, mimicking, aping | 反义词: original, genuine | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *mīmētikós*, from *mīmeîsthai* "to imitate."
