ingrieve:/ɪnˈɡriːv/ | vt. | 使悲痛，使伤心 | To cause to feel grief or sorrow. |  He was ingrieved by the loss of his friend. 他因失去朋友而悲痛。

 The news ingrieved her deeply.  这消息使她深感悲痛。| 近义词: sadden, distress, upset, grieve | 反义词: comfort, console, gladden | 词性: 动词 (verb)

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- + grieve.  "In-" is a prefix meaning "in, into, on, upon, within." "Grieve" comes from Old French "grever," meaning "to burden, oppress, harm," ultimately from Latin "gravis," meaning "heavy."
