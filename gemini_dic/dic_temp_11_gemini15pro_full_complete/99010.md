interactiveness:/ˌɪntərˈæktɪvnəs/ | n. |交互性，互动性 |The quality or state of being interactive; characterized by interaction or exchange.  |The interactiveness of the museum exhibit made it a popular attraction for children.  博物馆展览的互动性使其成为吸引儿童的热门景点。|近义词：interaction, reciprocity, engagement |反义词：passivity, inactivity| 词性:名词

interact:/ˌɪntərˈækt/ | v. |互动，相互作用 |To act on one another. |The website allows users to interact with each other through forums and chat rooms.  该网站允许用户通过论坛和聊天室相互交流。|近义词：communicate, engage, react |反义词：ignore, disregard| 词性:动词

interactive:/ˌɪntərˈæktɪv/ | adj. |交互式的，互动的 |Involving or allowing two-way communication or exchange. |The interactive display allowed visitors to learn more about the history of the city.  互动式展览可以让游客更多地了解这座城市的历史。|近义词：responsive, reciprocal, engaging |反义词：passive, static| 词性:形容词
