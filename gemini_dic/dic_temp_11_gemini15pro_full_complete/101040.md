inveigh:/ɪnˈveɪ/| v.| 痛斥，猛烈抨击，强烈抗议|To speak or write strongly and critically about something or someone.|The newspaper editorial inveighed against the government's new policy. 这篇报纸社説痛斥政府的新政策。|近义词：denounce, condemn, criticize, rail, fulminate|反义词：praise, commend, laud|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *invehere* "to carry in, bring in, introduce," also "attack with words, assail," from *in-* "in, into" + *vehere* "to carry."
