demi-hag:/ˈdɛmiˌhæɡ/| n. | 半妖婆，小妖婆 | A lesser hag, often the offspring of a hag and another creature.  A demi-hag is less powerful than a full hag, but still possesses some magical abilities and a malevolent nature. | The adventurers encountered a demi-hag guarding the entrance to the cave.  探险者们遇到一个守卫洞穴入口的半妖婆。 | The demi-hag lured the children with promises of sweets. 半妖婆用糖果的承诺引诱孩子们。 | 近义词: hag, witch, sorceress | 反义词: fairy, angel | 词性: noun
