louting:/ˈlaʊtɪŋ/ | n. | 喧闹；大声叫嚷；吵闹的行为 | The act of making a loud noise, especially in a rude or aggressive way.  |  The louting of the football fans disturbed the neighborhood. 足球迷的喧闹扰乱了附近的安宁. | synonyms: shouting, yelling, clamoring, roaring | antonyms: whispering, murmuring, silence | noun

louting:/ˈlaʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 喧闹；大声叫嚷；吵闹 | To make a loud noise, especially in a rude or aggressive way. | The protesters were louting slogans outside the government building. 抗议者在政府大楼外大声喊着口号。| synonyms: shout, yell, clamor, roar | antonyms: whisper, murmur | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *louten, from Old Norse *hljóta (“to resound, make a noise”), from Proto-Germanic *hleutaną (“to make a sound, call”).  Related to Old English hlūd (“loud”).
