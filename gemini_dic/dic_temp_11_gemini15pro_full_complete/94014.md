impalsy:/ɪmˈpɔːlsi/ | adj. |冲动的，易冲动的 | Characterized by acting on impulse; rash. |  He has an impalsy nature and often regrets his hasty decisions. 他性格冲动，经常后悔自己草率的决定。|例句：Her impalsy reaction was to slap him. 她冲动的反应是给了他一巴掌。| 近义词: impulsive, rash, impetuous, reckless | 反义词: cautious, deliberate, thoughtful, prudent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Impalsy" is likely a misspelling or a non-standard variant of "impulsive."  "Impulsive" comes from the Late Latin *impulsivus, from Latin impulsus, past participle of impellere "to push against, urge," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + pellere "to push, drive."
