anemographic:/ˌænɪməˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
风速记录的；风速仪的
Of or relating to the recording of wind speed or direction.
The anemographic data showed a steady increase in wind speed throughout the day.  风速记录数据显示，全天风速都在稳步增加。

The museum displayed an anemographic instrument from the 19th century.  博物馆展出了一台19世纪的风速记录仪器。
近义词:wind-related,  wind-measuring
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "calm" for describing wind conditions)
