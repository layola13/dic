commissionary:/kəˈmɪʃəˌneri/ | n. adj.| 委员；代表；代理人；军需官；小卖部；委托；佣金 | a store that sells food and supplies, especially to members of the armed forces; an officer in charge of supplies, especially food; the position or duties of a commissioner; authorized by a commission; pertaining to a commission or commissioner |  The soldiers lined up at the commissary to buy snacks. 士兵们在小卖部排队买零食。| Synonyms: store, canteen, shop, deputy, agent, representative | Antonyms: principal, head, chief | noun

commissionary:/kəˈmɪʃəˌneri/ | adj.| 委托的；由佣金获得的 | authorized by a commission; pertaining to a commission or commissioner |  He earned a commissary fee for brokering the deal. 他因促成这笔交易而获得佣金。| Synonyms: commissioned, delegated, authorized | Antonyms: unauthorized, independent | adjective
