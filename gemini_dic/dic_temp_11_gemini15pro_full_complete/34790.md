climatology:/ˌklaɪməˈtɑːlədʒi/ | n. |气候学| The scientific study of climate, including its causes, variations, and effects. |例句：Climatology helps us understand long-term weather patterns. 气候学帮助我们理解长期的天气模式。

例句：He is studying climatology at university. 他在大学学习气候学。|近义词：meteorology |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *climatologie*, from Ancient Greek κλίμα (klíma, “region, zone, slope, inclination”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
