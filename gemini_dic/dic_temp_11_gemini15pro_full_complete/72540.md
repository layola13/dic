flute-like:/ˈfluːtˌlaɪk/| adj.|像长笛的，笛声似的|Resembling the sound or shape of a flute. |The bird's song was flute-like, high and clear.  鸟儿的歌声像笛声一样，高亢而清澈。|The instrument produced a flute-like tone. 这件乐器发出了一种类似笛子的音调。|近义词：melodious, high-pitched, sweet, silvery|反义词：raucous, harsh, grating|词性:形容词
