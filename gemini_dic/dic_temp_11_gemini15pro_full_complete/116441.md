matching:/ˈmætʃɪŋ/ | adj. n. | 相配的，匹配的；比赛，竞赛 | corresponding in pattern, color, or design; a contest in which people or teams compete against each other in a particular sport or activity | The curtains and carpet are a perfect matching. 窗帘和地毯非常相配。| synonyms: corresponding, complementary, identical, twin, parallel | antonyms: contrasting, different, dissimilar, clashing | adjective

matching:/ˈmætʃɪŋ/ | n. | 相配，匹配；比赛，竞赛 | the act of finding something that is similar to or suitable for something else; a contest in which people or teams compete against each other | The matching of wines and cheeses is an art.  葡萄酒和奶酪的搭配是一门艺术。| synonyms: pairing, coupling, combining, coordinating | antonyms: mismatching, separation | noun
