documentation's:/ˌdɑːkjəmənˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 文件；文档（documentation 的所有格形式）|  The documentation's completeness is crucial for the project's success.  该文档的完整性对项目的成功至关重要。 | Synonym: records, files, paperwork | Antonym:  N/A (Possessive forms don't typically have antonyms) | noun


Etymology:  From document + -ation, ultimately from Latin documentum "lesson, proof, example," from docere "to teach."  The -'s is the possessive marker.
