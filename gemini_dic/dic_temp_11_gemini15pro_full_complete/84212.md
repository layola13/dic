half-roasted:/ˌhɑːf ˈroʊstɪd/ |adj.|半烤的，烤得半熟的|Partially roasted; cooked until only partly browned or softened by dry heat.  It describes something that has been cooked by roasting, but not completely, retaining some rawness or a less-done texture inside.|The half-roasted chicken was still slightly pink near the bone. 半烤的鸡骨头附近仍然有点粉红色。|The chef took the half-roasted vegetables out of the oven to check their progress.  厨师把半烤的蔬菜从烤箱里拿出来检查进度。|Near-raw, undercooked, underdone|Overcooked, well-done, burnt|adjective
