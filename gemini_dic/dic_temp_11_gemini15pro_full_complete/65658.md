evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/ /ɪˈveɪzɪv/| adj.|逃避的，回避的，闪烁其词的|Tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly.|He gave evasive answers to the police's questions. 他对警察的提问闪烁其词。|近义词：elusive, ambiguous, equivocal|反义词：direct, forthright, candid|词性:形容词

evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/ /ɪˈveɪzɪv/| n.|回避, 逃避|The act of avoiding something or someone.|His evasiveness made the police suspicious. 他的回避让警察起了疑心。|近义词：avoidance, dodging, elusion|反义词：confrontation, directness|词性:名词 (rarely used)
