goatsucker:/ˈɡoʊtsʌkər/| n. |夜鹰|A nocturnal bird with a wide mouth, traditionally believed to suck the milk of goats.|例句：The goatsucker is a master of camouflage, blending seamlessly into its surroundings.  夜鹰是伪装大师，能完美地融入周围环境。

例句：Goatsuckers are known for their distinctive, churring calls. 夜鹰以其独特的、唧唧喳喳的叫声而闻名。|近义词：nightjar, nighthawk|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑diurnal bird - 昼行性鸟类)|词性:noun
