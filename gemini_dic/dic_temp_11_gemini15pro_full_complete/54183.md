dice:/daɪs/ /diːs/ | n. |骰子（单数）| A small cube with each face marked with one to six spots, used in games. | He threw the dice and hoped for a six. 他掷骰子，希望掷出个六。| 近义词: cube | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *dé, plural dez, from Latin datum "something given" (as in a throw of the dice).


注意事项: dice作为名词单复数同形，但dises也是可以接受的复数形式，尤其在一些游戏中出现。
