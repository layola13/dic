incumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/| v. |阻碍，妨碍，拖累|To burden or hinder someone or something; to weigh down or make movement difficult.|例句：The heavy backpack incumbered his progress up the mountain.  沉重的背包妨碍了他上山的进程。

例句：He was incumbered with debts. 他被债务拖累。|近义词：hinder, obstruct, impede, burden, hamper|反义词：aid, assist, help, facilitate|词性:动词

incumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/| n. |累赘，负担，妨碍物|Something that burdens or hinders; an impediment or obstacle.|例句：The old furniture was an incumbrance when they moved.  搬家时，旧家具成了累赘。

例句：She considered her large family an incumbrance to her career. 她认为她的大家庭是她事业的阻碍。|近义词：burden, obstacle, impediment, hindrance, obstruction|反义词：aid, help, advantage, asset|词性:名词
