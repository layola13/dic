deepnesses:/ˈdiːpnəsɪz/
noun
深度，深奥，深刻
The great deepnesses of the ocean remain largely unexplored. 海洋的深处很大程度上仍然未被探索。
The deepnesses of his thoughts were difficult to comprehend.  他思想的深奥之处难以理解。
The artist explored the deepnesses of human emotion in her work. 这位艺术家在她的作品中探索了人类情感的深度。
Synonyms: depths, profundities, abysses
Antonyms: shallows, superficiality
