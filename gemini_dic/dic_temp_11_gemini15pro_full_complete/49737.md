demographer:/dɪˈmɑːɡrəfər/ | n. |人口统计学家；人口学家|A specialist who studies the characteristics of human populations, such as size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics. | Demographers predict a decline in the birth rate over the next decade. 人口统计学家预测未来十年出生率会下降。| synonym: population scientist, population analyst | antonym: N/A | noun

demography:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |人口统计学；人口学|The study of statistics such as births, deaths, income, or the incidence of disease, which illustrate the changing structure of human populations. | The demography of the region has changed dramatically in the last 50 years. 该地区的人口结构在过去50年中发生了巨大的变化。| synonym: population studies, vital statistics | antonym: N/A | noun

demographic:/ˌdeməˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. n. |人口统计学的；人口学的；人口统计数据|Relating to the study of changes in the characteristics of a population.  Also, a section of the population sharing common characteristics, such as age, sex, or income. | The demographic data shows a shift towards an older population. 人口统计数据显示人口结构正在向老龄化转变。| synonym:  population-related, statistical | antonym: N/A | adjective, noun
