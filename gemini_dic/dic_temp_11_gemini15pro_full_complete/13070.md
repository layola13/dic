astrologer:/əˈstrɒlədʒər/ (US) /əˈstrɒlədʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. |占星家，星象学家|A person who studies the positions and aspects of celestial bodies in the belief that they have an influence on the course of natural earthly occurrences and human affairs.|She consulted an astrologer about her future. 她向一位占星家咨询了她的未来。|Synonym: stargazer, soothsayer|Antonym: scientist, skeptic|Noun
astrologer:|Etymology: From Middle English astrologien, from Old French astrologie, from Latin astrologia, from Ancient Greek ἀστρολογία (astrología), from ἄστρον (ástron, “star”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).|
