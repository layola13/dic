limbic:/ˈlɪmbɪk/| adj.|边缘的，大脑边缘系统的|Relating to the limbic system, a complex system of nerves and networks in the brain, involving several areas near the edge of the cortex concerned with instinct and emotion.|The limbic system plays a crucial role in regulating emotions.  边缘系统在调节情绪方面起着至关重要的作用。|The limbic brain is responsible for our "fight or flight" response. 边缘脑负责我们的“战斗或逃跑”反应。|近义词:emotional, instinctive|反义词:cognitive, rational|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *limbicus* “bordering, marginal,” from *limbus* “border, fringe, edge.”
