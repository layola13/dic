eugenics:/juːˈdʒɛnɪks/
n.
优生学
the study of how to arrange reproduction within a human population to increase the occurrence of heritable characteristics regarded as desirable.
例句：The eugenics movement of the early 20th century aimed to improve the human race through selective breeding.  20世纪初的优生运动旨在通过选择性育种来改善人类种族。

例句：He studied the history of eugenics and its impact on social policy. 他研究了优生学历史及其对社会政策的影响。

近义词：genetic engineering, selective breeding
反义词：dysgenics


词性: noun
