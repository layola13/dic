ochery:/ˈoʊkəri/ | adj. |赭色的，赭石色的 |Of a light brownish-yellow color like ocher. | The walls were painted an ochery yellow.  墙被漆成了赭黄色。| Synonyms:  yellowish-brown, brownish-yellow,  buff, sand,  tan | Antonyms: dark, black, white | Adjective

ochre:/ˈoʊkər/ | n. |赭石，赭色 |An earthy pigment containing hydrated iron oxide, varying in color from light yellow to deep orange or red-brown. |  He used ochre to paint the landscape. 他用赭石画风景。| Synonyms: umber, sienna, pigment, earth color | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ochre*, from Latin *ochra*, from Greek *ōkhra* (ὠχρά), feminine of *ōkhros* (ὠχρός) "pale yellow, pale".
