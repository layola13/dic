facetting:/ˈfæsɪtɪŋ/ | n. |刻面，琢面；分面，多面|The process of cutting facets into a gemstone to enhance its brilliance.  将宝石切割成多个刻面以增强其光彩的过程。|例句：The facetting of the diamond was exquisite. 这颗钻石的刻面精美绝伦。

例句：The facetting of the story revealed multiple perspectives.  这个故事的多面性展现了多种视角。|近义词：cutting, shaping, polishing, multi-faceted|反义词：smoothing, rounding|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From facet + -ing.  Facet comes from the French word "facette," meaning "little face," ultimately derived from the Latin "facies," meaning "face" or "appearance."
