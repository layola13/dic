interloper: /ˈɪntərˌloʊpər/ (US) /ˈɪntəˌləʊpə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 闯入者，干涉者，无理干涉他人事务者 |Someone who becomes involved in a place or situation where they are not wanted or are considered not to belong.| The manager viewed the consultant as an interloper in his department. 经理认为顾问是他部门里的不速之客。|  The board of directors resented the interloper's attempts to influence their decisions. 董事会对闯入者试图影响他们决定的行为感到不满。| synonyms: intruder, trespasser, meddler, interferer | antonyms: guest, host, owner, resident | noun
