overdecorations: /ˌoʊvərˌdɛkəˈreɪʃənz/
noun
过度装饰；过分装饰
Excessive or superfluous decoration; ornamentation that is too elaborate or fussy.
The overdecorations in the living room made it feel cluttered. 客厅里的过度装饰使它感觉很杂乱。
Synonyms: embellishments, ornamentation, adornment, frills
Antonyms: simplicity, minimalism
词性: 名词
