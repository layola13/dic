fertile:/ˈfɜːrtl/ (US) /ˈfɜːtaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|肥沃的；富饶的；能生育的|Producing or capable of producing abundant vegetation or crops; fruitful; able to conceive young or produce offspring.|The Nile valley is a fertile region. 尼罗河流域是一片肥沃的地区。|Synonyms: productive, fruitful, fecund, prolific|Antonyms: barren, infertile, sterile|adjective


fertile:/ˈfɜːrtl/ (US) /ˈfɜːtaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|丰富的；富于创造力的|Characterized by great resourcefulness of thought or imagination; inventive.|He has a fertile imagination. 他有着丰富的想象力。|Synonyms: imaginative, inventive, resourceful|Antonyms: unimaginative, uninspired|adjective
