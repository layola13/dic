clicking:/ˈklɪkɪŋ/ | n. v. adj. | 点击，咔哒声，发出咔哒声 | a short, sharp sound as of a switch being operated or of two hard objects coming quickly into contact; the act of making such a sound. |  The clicking of the mouse annoyed her. 鼠标的咔哒声让她很烦。 | 近义词: ticking, tapping, snapping | 反义词: silence | 词性: noun/verb/adjective

clicking:/ˈklɪkɪŋ/ | n. v. adj. | 点击，咔哒声，发出咔哒声 | suddenly becoming clear or understood. | It suddenly clicked why she was so upset.  她为什么这么沮丧，我突然明白了。 | 近义词: connect,  realize | 反义词: confuse, misunderstand | 词性: noun/verb/adjective
