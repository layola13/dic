ossuary: /ˈɒʃʊəri/ (UK) /ˈɑːsəˌri/ (US) | n. | 骨灰堂，藏骨堂 | A container or room for the bones of the dead. |  The ossuary held the remains of hundreds of soldiers. 藏骨堂里存放着数百名士兵的遗骸。

|  The catacombs contained an ossuary filled with skulls.  地下墓穴里有一个装满头骨的藏骨堂。

| Synonym: charnel house, bone house | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps cemetery as a place for intact bodies) | Noun
