kaleidophone:/kəˈlaɪdəfoʊn/| n.|万花筒音叉;声波振动演示器|A device that visually demonstrates sound waves and their vibrations, often consisting of a vibrating rod with a reflective surface that creates changing patterns when illuminated.|The physics teacher used a kaleidophone to show the students how sound waves travel.|Near synonyms:  Chladni plate, oscilloscope (for visualizing sound electronically), resonoscope |Antonyms: N/A|Noun
