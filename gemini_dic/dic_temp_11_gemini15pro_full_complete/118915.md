metazooen:/ˌmetəˈzoʊən/
n.
后生动物
A major division of the animal kingdom that comprises all animals except protozoans and sponges.  Metazoans are multicellular animals whose cells are differentiated into tissues and organs.
Metazoans are a diverse group, including everything from simple jellyfish to complex mammals. 后生动物是一个多样化的群体，包括从简单的水母到复杂的哺乳动物。
Synonyms: animal, creature, beast
Antonyms:  N/A (Since it's a broad classification, there isn't a direct antonym)
