bonedog:/ˈboʊnˌdɒɡ/| n.|骨狗; 瘦骨嶙峋的狗|A very thin dog, often used to describe a dog that is undernourished or starving.  It can also be used humorously or affectionately.|The stray looked like a bonedog, its ribs clearly visible.|近义词：scrawny dog, skinny dog, emaciated dog|反义词:fat dog, plump dog|词性:noun

词源:  A compound word formed from "bone" and "dog."  "Bone" refers to the visibility of the bones due to the dog's thinness.
