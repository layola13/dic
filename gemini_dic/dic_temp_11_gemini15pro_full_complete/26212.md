buttered:/ˈbʌtərd/| adj.|涂了黄油的；奉承的；油嘴滑舌的|Covered with butter; flattering in a servile way.|例句：He ate a buttered scone. 他吃了一个涂黄油的烤饼。|例句：She gave him a buttered smile. 她给了他一个谄媚的微笑。|近义词：oiled, greased, flattered|反义词：dry, unbuttered|词性:形容词

buttered:/ˈbʌtərd/| v.|涂黄油；奉承（butter的过去式和过去分词）|Past tense and past participle of butter.|例句：He buttered the toast thickly. 他在吐司上涂了厚厚一层黄油。|例句：He buttered up the boss to get a promotion. 他为了升职而奉承老板。|近义词：spread, oiled, flattered|反义词：scrape, clean|词性:动词
