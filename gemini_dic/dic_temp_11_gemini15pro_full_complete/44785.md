crunches: /krʌntʃɪz/ | n. |  crunches |  A set of exercises designed to strengthen the abdominal muscles.  It involves lying on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor, then curling your upper body towards your knees. | She does 50 crunches every morning. 她每天早上做50个仰卧起坐。 | Synonyms:  sit-ups, abdominal exercises | Antonyms:  rests, relaxation | Noun

crunches: /krʌntʃɪz/ | v. | crunches | Third-person singular present tense form of "crunch," meaning to crush something noisily between the teeth or to make a loud crushing noise. | The car crunches over the gravel. 汽车在碎石上发出嘎吱嘎吱的声音。| Synonyms:  grinds, crushes | Antonyms: munches, nibbles | Verb
