craber:/ˈkreɪbər/| n.|捕蟹人; 抱怨的人|A person who catches crabs, or someone who complains a lot.|He's a craber by trade, spending his days out on the boat. 他以捕蟹为生，每天都在船上度过。

She's such a craber, always finding something to complain about. 她真是个爱抱怨的人，总是能找到一些事情来抱怨。|近义词：complainer, whiner, fisherman (for catching crabs)|反义词: optimist (for someone who complains)|词性:noun
