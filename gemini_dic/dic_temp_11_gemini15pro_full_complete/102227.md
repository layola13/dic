It corresponds to the -ly suffix. | definitely (definitely /ˈdefɪnɪtli/ adv. 肯定地,  He will definitely come. 他一定会来。),  infinitely (infinitely /ˈɪnfɪnɪtli/ adv. 无限地,  The universe is infinitely vast. 宇宙浩瀚无垠。) | 近义词: -ly | 反义词: N/A | suffix
