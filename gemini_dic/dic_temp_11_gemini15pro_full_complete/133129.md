nonesterified:/ˌnɒnˈɛstərɪfaɪd/
adjective
未酯化的
Not converted into an ester.  Specifically referring to fatty acids that are not bound to glycerol as part of a triglyceride molecule.
Nonesterified fatty acids (NEFAs) are an important source of energy for the body. 未酯化脂肪酸 (NEFA) 是人体重要的能量来源。
Nonesterified cholesterol is transported in the bloodstream. 未酯化胆固醇在血液中运输。
近义词:free, unesterified, unbound
反义词:esterified, bound
词性:adjective
