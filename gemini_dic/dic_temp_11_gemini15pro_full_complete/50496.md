depressurized:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzd/
|v. |减压；卸压；使降压|To release the pressure from something that has been pressurized.|The cabin was depressurized before the emergency landing.  机舱在紧急着陆前进行了减压。|depressurize|pressurize|verb
depressurized:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzd/
|adj. |减压的|Having had pressure reduced or removed.|The depressurized cabin posed a risk to the passengers. 减压的机舱对乘客构成了风险。|depressurized|pressurized|adjective
