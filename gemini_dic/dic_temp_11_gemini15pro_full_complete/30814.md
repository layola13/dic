/wɒt/ /hwɑːt/ |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.  It can also be used as a determiner and an adverb to express surprise or emphasis.|
What is your name? 你叫什么名字？
What did you say? 你说什么？

/wɒt/ /hwɑːt/ |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to inquire about someone's identity, nature, or characteristics.|
What kind of music do you like? 你喜欢什么类型的音乐？
What is he like? 他是怎样一个人？


/wɒt/ /hwɑːt/ |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for a repetition or clarification of something not heard or understood.|
What? I can't hear you. 什么？我听不到你说什么。
What was that noise? 那是什么声音？
