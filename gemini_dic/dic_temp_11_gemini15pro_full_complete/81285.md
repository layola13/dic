granter's:/ˈɡræntərz/
noun
授予者
belonging to or associated with the granter; possessive form of granter, a person or entity that grants something (like a right, privilege, or property)
The granter's signature was required on the deed. 授予者需要在契约上签字。
近义词: donor's, giver's, bestower's
反义词: recipient's, receiver's
词性: possessive noun

Etymology:
From grant + -er + -'s.  "Grant" comes from Old French "graanter" (to agree, consent, promise), ultimately from Latin "credere" (to believe, trust).  "-er" is a suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or one who performs an action. "-'s" is the possessive suffix.
