intra-european:/ˌɪntrəˌjʊərəˈpiːən/
adjective
欧洲内部的
Occurring within Europe; involving only European countries.
Intra-European trade has increased significantly since the formation of the EU. 欧洲内部贸易自欧盟成立以来显著增长。
The intra-European flights are often cheaper than international ones. 欧洲内部航班通常比国际航班便宜。
近义词:internal, within Europe
反义词:extra-European, intercontinental
词性:adjective
