maclurin:/məˈklɔːrɪn/| n. |麦克劳林|A Scottish mathematician (1698-1746) known for his work in calculus, particularly the Maclaurin series, a special case of the Taylor series centered at zero.  He also made contributions to geometry and algebra.|Maclaurin's work laid the foundation for many aspects of modern calculus.| 近义词: mathematician, scientist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

maclurin series:/məˈklɔːrɪn ˈsɪəriːz/| n. |麦克劳林级数|A Taylor series expansion of a function about 0, often used to approximate the value of a function near 0.|The Maclaurin series for sin(x) is x - x³/3! + x⁵/5! - ...|近义词: Taylor series (at 0) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
