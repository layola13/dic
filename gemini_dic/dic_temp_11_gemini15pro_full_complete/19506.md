bids:/bɪdz/ | n. | 出价，投标 | Offers of prices, especially at an auction. | The company submitted its bids for the construction project.  这家公司提交了该建设项目的投标。 | synonyms: offers, tenders, proposals | antonyms:  rejections, withdrawals | noun

bids:/bɪdz/ | v. |  (bid, bade, bidden) 出价，投标；吩咐，命令 | To offer a price for something, especially at an auction; to command or order. |  She bid $500 for the antique vase. 她出价500美元买那个古董花瓶。| synonyms: offer, tender, propose, command, order | antonyms:  withdraw, reject, refuse | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *biddan "to ask, request, pray, beseech, entreat, summon," related to Old Frisian bidda, Old Saxon biddian, Old High German bitten "to ask, beg, pray," Gothic bidjan "to pray, beseech."  The meaning shifted from "to ask" to "to offer" (a price) over time.
