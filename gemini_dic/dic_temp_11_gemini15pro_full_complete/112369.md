lose ground:/luːz ɡraʊnd/
verb phrase
失去优势；退步；失利
to become less successful or less popular than someone or something else
Example: The company is losing ground to its competitors.  这家公司正在输给竞争对手。

Example:  He was losing ground in the polls. 他的民意支持率正在下降。

近义词: fall behind, decline, regress, retreat, recede, lose momentum
反义词: gain ground, advance, progress, improve


lose:/luːz/
verb
失去；输掉；遗失；亏损；错过；浪费
1. to no longer have something because you do not know where it is
2. to no longer have something because it has been taken from you or destroyed
3. to fail to win a game, argument, election, etc.
4. to waste something such as time or an opportunity
5. if you lose a particular amount of weight, you become lighter by that amount
6. to fail to keep or maintain something
7. to have something or someone taken away from you because they die

ground:/ɡraʊnd/
noun
地面；土地；立场；根据；范围
1. the surface of the earth
2. an area of land, or a particular type of land
3. the reason for something
4. an area of knowledge or discussion
