joshua: /ˈdʒɒʃuə/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːʃwə/ (US)| n. |约书亚（男子名）;《约书亚记》|A biblical name, meaning "God is salvation." Joshua was Moses' successor who led the Israelites into the Promised Land.  It also refers to the Book of Joshua in the Old Testament.|Example: Joshua is a popular name in many Christian communities. 约书亚在很多基督教社区都是一个受欢迎的名字。

Example:  I'm reading the Book of Joshua. 我正在读《约书亚记》。|近义词: None (as a proper noun)|反义词: None (as a proper noun)|词性: Noun
