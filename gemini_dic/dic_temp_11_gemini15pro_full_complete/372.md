abietinic:/ˌeɪbiˈɛtɪnɪk/ | adj. | 松香的，枞酸的 | Relating to or derived from abietic acid, a resin acid found in rosin, particularly from coniferous trees like pine and fir. |  Abietinic acid is a major component of rosin.  枞酸是松香的主要成分。|  The abietinic compounds in the resin gave it a sticky texture. 树脂中的枞酸成分使其具有粘性。 |  resinous, piny | N/A | adjective
