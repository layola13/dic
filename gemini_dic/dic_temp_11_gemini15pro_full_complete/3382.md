affusion:/əˈfjuːʒən/
n.
灌注，浇注
The act of pouring a liquid on something, especially as part of a religious ceremony.
The priest performed the affusion of holy water on the baby's head during baptism.
神父在婴儿洗礼时将圣水浇在婴儿头上。

近义词:pouring, sprinkling, baptism
反义词:desiccation, dehydration
