equisetiform: /ˌekwɪˈsiːtɪfɔːrm/
adjective
木贼形的，似木贼的
Resembling or having the form of a horsetail (Equisetum).  Shaped like the hollow, jointed stems of horsetails.
The fossilized plant had an equisetiform structure.  这株植物化石具有木贼形的结构。
The artist created an equisetiform sculpture from metal. 艺术家用金属创作了一个木贼形状的雕塑。
Near Synonyms:  horsetail-like, Equisetum-like, jointed
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but could use descriptions like) smooth, unjointed, solid
Word origin: From Latin "equus" (horse) + "seta" (bristle) + "-form" (shaped)
