metrotome:/ˈmɛtrəˌtoʊm/| n. |子宫切开器|A surgical instrument used for incising the uterus.|The surgeon used a metrotome to perform the Cesarean section.|近义词:hysterotome|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:  From metro- + -tome.  Metro- comes from Greek mētra "womb". -tome comes from Greek tomē "a cutting, slice, segment"  from temnein "to cut."
