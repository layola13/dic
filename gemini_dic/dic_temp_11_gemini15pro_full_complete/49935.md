dendritic:/dɛnˈdrɪtɪk/
adjective
树枝状的; 枝状晶体的
Like a tree, having branches or branch-like parts.
The neuron's dendritic branches receive signals from other neurons. 这个神经元的树突分支接收来自其他神经元的信号。
dendritic crystals formed on the surface. 表面上形成了枝状晶体。
