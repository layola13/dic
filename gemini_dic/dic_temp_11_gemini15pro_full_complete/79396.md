glike:/ɡlaɪk/ | adj. | 像胶一样的; 粘性的 | Resembling glue; sticky; viscous.  | The substance had a glike consistency, making it difficult to remove. 这种物质具有胶状稠度，难以去除。| Example: The honey had a glike texture.  蜂蜜有胶状的质地。|Synonyms: gluey, sticky, viscous, adhesive | Antonyms: runny, watery, thin | Word type: Adjective

Etymology:  Likely a combination of "glue" and the suffix "-like," indicating similarity.
