overhelpful:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfəl/| adj.|过分热心的，过于殷勤的|Excessively eager to help; offering help that is unwanted or unnecessary.|例句：He was so overhelpful that it became annoying. 他太殷勤了，让人感到厌烦。

例句：Her overhelpful suggestions made me feel incompetent. 她过分热心的建议让我觉得自己很无能。|近义词：officious, intrusive, meddlesome|反义词：unhelpful, inconsiderate|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From 'over-' (meaning "excessively") + 'helpful'.  'Over-' is from Old English 'ofer', ultimately from Proto-Germanic '*ubar'. 'Helpful' is from 'help' (Old English 'helpan') + '-ful' (Old English '-ful').
