curari: /kjʊˈrɑːri/ /kʊˈrɑːri/ | n. |箭毒，箭毒碱|A highly toxic extract from certain South American plants, used by indigenous peoples as an arrow poison. It causes paralysis by blocking nerve impulses to the muscles. |The hunter tipped his arrows with curari. 猎人用箭毒涂抹箭头。| The effects of curari were immediate and devastating. 箭毒的效果立竿见影，威力巨大。 | synonym: curare | antonym: antidote | noun
