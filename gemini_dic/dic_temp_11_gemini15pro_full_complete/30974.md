chalcidian:/kælˈsɪdiən/
adjective| 寄生蜂总科的； 类似寄生蜂的 | relating to or resembling the Chalcidoidea, a superfamily of parasitic wasps.| Chalcidian wasps are important for biological control of pests. 寄生蜂对害虫的生物防治很重要。|Synonyms: parasitoid, chalcid|Antonyms: host (of the parasite)|adjective

chalcidian:/kælˈsɪdiən/
noun|寄生蜂总科昆虫| a member of the Chalcidoidea, a superfamily of parasitic wasps.|  The chalcidian laid its eggs inside the caterpillar.  这只寄生蜂把卵产在毛毛虫体内。|Synonyms: chalcid, parasitoid wasp|Antonyms: host (of the parasite)|noun
