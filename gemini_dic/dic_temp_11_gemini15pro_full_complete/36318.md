cohort:/ˈkoʊhɔːrt/ (US) /ˈkəʊhɔːt/ (UK)| n.|同伙，一群人，队列，（尤指统计中）同龄群，一批人|A group of people who share a characteristic, usually age.  A band of warriors or supporters.|例句：The 1999 birth cohort is the group of people born in 1999. 1999年出生的一批人是指1999年出生的人。

例句：The CEO arrived with his cohort of advisors. 首席执行官和他的顾问团一起抵达。|近义词：group, band, company, posse, troop|反义词:individual|词性:名词
