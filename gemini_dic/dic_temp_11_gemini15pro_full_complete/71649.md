flattens:/ˈflæt(ə)nz/
verb
使变平; 压平; 击倒; 使失去光泽
makes something flat or flatter; knocks someone down
The steamroller flattens the road surface.  蒸汽压路机把路面压平了。
He flattened his opponent with a single punch. 他一拳就把对手打倒在地。
近义词: levels, smooths, crushes
反义词: raises, swells, expands
词性:v.
