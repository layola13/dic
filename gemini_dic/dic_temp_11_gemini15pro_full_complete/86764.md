helpline:/ˈhɛlplaɪn/| n.|求助热线；帮助热线|A telephone service that provides advice and information, especially on a particular subject.| I called the domestic violence helpline for advice. 我打电话给家庭暴力求助热线寻求建议。|Example: The helpline is open 24 hours a day. 这条求助热线24小时开通。|Synonyms: hotline, support line, advice line|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
