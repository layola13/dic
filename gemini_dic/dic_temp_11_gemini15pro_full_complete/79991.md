go-getting:/ˈɡoʊˌɡetɪŋ/
adjective
有进取心的，积极进取的
Energetic, ambitious, and determined to succeed.  Characterized by a willingness to pursue opportunities vigorously.
She's a real go-getting young lawyer. 她是一位真正积极进取的年轻律师。
He has a go-getting attitude. 他有一种积极进取的态度。
近义词: ambitious, enterprising, driven, motivated, resourceful
反义词: lazy, passive, unmotivated, lethargic, apathetic
词性: adjective

词源:  Compound adjective formed from the verb phrase "go and get," meaning to actively pursue something.  This phrase became shortened to "go-get" and then the "-ing" suffix was added to create the adjective form.
