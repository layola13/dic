ill-used:/ˌɪlˈjuːzd/ | adj. | 虐待的，受虐的，滥用的 | badly or unfairly treated |  The ill-used furniture showed signs of neglect. 这件受虐待的家具显示出被忽视的迹象。 |  The ill-used car had clearly seen better days.  这辆被滥用的汽车显然已经过了它的黄金时期。 | misused, abused, mistreated | cherished, cared for | adjective

词源:
ill- (prefix) from Old English 'yfele', meaning "badly, wrongly"
used (past participle of 'use') from Old French 'user', from Latin 'uti', meaning "to use"
