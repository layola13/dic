crotonine: /ˈkroʊtənɪn/ | n. |巴豆碱|A highly poisonous alkaloid, C13H14NO2, obtained from croton seeds.|Crotonine is a potent toxin that should be handled with extreme care.|近义词: toxin, poison |反义词: antidote |词性: noun

Etymology:  From croton + -ine (alkaloid suffix). Ultimately from Greek κρότων (krótōn, “tick”), referring to the shape of the seeds.
