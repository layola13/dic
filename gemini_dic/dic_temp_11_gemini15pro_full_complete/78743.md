gigolos:/ˈdʒɪɡəˌloʊz/
n.
男妓；小白脸
A man who is paid by women for companionship and often sexual relations.
例句：The wealthy widow was surrounded by gigolos vying for her attention.  这位富有的寡妇身边围绕着一群争宠的男妓。

例句：The movie portrays the glamorous, yet ultimately empty, life of a gigolo.  这部电影描绘了一个男妓光鲜亮丽但最终空虚的生活。
近义词：escort, paramour, kept man
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "faithful husband/partner")
词性: noun
