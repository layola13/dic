blue:/bluː/
adjective|蓝色的；沮丧的；忧郁的|of a colour intermediate between green and violet, as of the sky or sea on a sunny day; low in spirits; depressed|例句：The sky is blue. 天空是蓝色的。|近义词：azure, cerulean, depressed, downcast|反义词：red, yellow, happy, cheerful
noun|蓝色；蓝色物品|the colour intermediate between green and violet; something of this colour|例句：She was dressed in blue. 她穿着蓝色的衣服。|近义词：azure, cerulean|反义词：red, yellow
