chefing:/ˈʃɛfɪŋ/
n.|担任厨师;做饭;掌厨|The act of working as a chef, especially temporarily or in a non-professional setting.  This often implies a more casual or amateur approach to cooking, often for a group of people.|例句：He enjoys cheffing for his friends on weekends. 他喜欢在周末为朋友们做饭。|近义词：cooking, catering|反义词：(No direct opposite, potentially dining, eating)|词性:noun
