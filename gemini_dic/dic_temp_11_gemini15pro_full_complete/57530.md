dragsters:/ˈdræɡstərz/
noun
飙车族，直线竞速赛车
A dragster is a powerful car designed for drag racing, a type of motor racing in which automobiles or motorcycles compete, usually two at a time, to be first to cross a set finish line.
Two dragsters roared down the track. 两辆直线竞速赛车在赛道上呼啸而过。
Synonyms: racing car, hot rod
Antonyms: family car, minivan
