bedecking:/bɪˈdɛkɪŋ/
verb
装饰，修饰
adorning, decorating something, usually in a festive or elaborate way
The streets were bedecked with flags and banners for the celebration.  街道上为了庆祝活动装饰着旗帜和横幅。
synonyms: adorning, decorating, ornamenting, embellishing, arraying, gracing, trimming
antonyms: stripping, denuding, bare

詞性: verb
