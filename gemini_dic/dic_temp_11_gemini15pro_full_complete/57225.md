downgrade:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪd/| v. n.|降低，降级|to reduce something to a lower rank, position, or importance|The airline downgraded me from business class to economy. 航空公司将我从商务舱降级到经济舱。|They downgraded the country's economic growth forecast. 他们下调了该国的经济增长预测。|近义词：degrade, demote, lower, reduce|反义词: upgrade, promote, elevate|词性:动词, 名词
downgrade:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪd/| n.|降级，降低|a reduction in the importance or quality of something|The hotel received several downgrades from tourists. 这家酒店收到了游客的几次降级评价。|The company announced a downgrade of its profit forecast. 公司宣布下调其利润预期。|近义词：reduction, demotion, lowering|反义词: upgrade, promotion, elevation|词性:名词
