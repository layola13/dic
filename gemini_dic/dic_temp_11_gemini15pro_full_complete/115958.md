marring:/ˈmɑːrɪŋ/

marring |n. |损毁；玷污|The act of damaging or spoiling something.|The marring of the painting was a tragedy. 这幅画的毁坏是一场悲剧。|Synonyms: damage, spoilage, impairment, blemish, disfigurement|Antonyms: enhancement, improvement, beautification|noun
