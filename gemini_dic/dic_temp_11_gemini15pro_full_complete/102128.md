isotropous:/ˌaɪsəˈtrɒpəs/ | adj. |各向同性的| Having physical properties that are the same in all directions. |例句：The material is isotropous, meaning its properties are uniform regardless of direction. 这种材料是各向同性的，意味着它的特性在各个方向上都是一致的。

例句：Isotropous materials are often preferred in engineering applications where consistent performance is crucial.  在工程应用中，各向同性材料通常是首选，因为一致的性能至关重要。|近义词：uniform, homogeneous, isotropic |反义词: anisotropic, nonuniform, heterogeneous |词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *isos* "equal" + *tropos* "turning, direction".
