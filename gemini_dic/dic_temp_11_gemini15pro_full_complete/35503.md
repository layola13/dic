coadjutorship:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒʌtərʃɪp/
n.
助手职位；副手职位；助理主教职位
The position or office of a coadjutor.  A coadjutor is someone who assists another, particularly in a religious or official capacity.
He was appointed to the coadjutorship of the diocese. 他被任命为教区助理主教。
synonyms: assistantship, aideship, deputyship
antonyms: leadership, headship
noun
