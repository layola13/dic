intolerantness:/ɪnˈtɒlərəntnəs/ /ɪnˈtɑːlərəntnəs/| n. |不宽容，偏狭|The state of being unwilling to accept views, beliefs, or behavior that differ from one's own.|His intolerantness of other religions caused much conflict. 他对其他宗教的不宽容造成了很多冲突. |Near synonyms: bigotry, prejudice, narrow-mindedness, intolerance|Antonyms: tolerance, acceptance, open-mindedness|noun

intolerant:/ɪnˈtɒlərənt/ /ɪnˈtɑːlərənt/| adj. |不宽容的，偏狭的|Not tolerant of views, beliefs, or behavior that differ from one's own.|She was intolerant of anyone who didn't share her political views. 她不能容忍任何与她政见不同的人.|Near synonyms: bigoted, prejudiced, narrow-minded|Antonyms: tolerant, accepting, open-minded|adjective
