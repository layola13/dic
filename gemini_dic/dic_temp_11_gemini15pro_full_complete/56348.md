dogvane:/ˈdɒɡveɪn/ (UK), /ˈdɑːɡveɪn/ (US)| n.|风向标，风向袋|A vane, often in the shape of a dog, used to show the direction of the wind. It is typically placed on top of a building or ship's mast.|The old dogvane on the barn creaked in the wind. 谷仓上旧的风向标在风中吱吱作响。|Synonyms: weather vane, wind vane, windsock|Antonyms: N/A|noun
