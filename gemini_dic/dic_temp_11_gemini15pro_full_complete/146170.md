overevaluation:/ˌoʊvərɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 过高评价；高估 | An excessively high evaluation of someone or something. | He had an overevaluation of his own abilities and thought he could do anything. 他高估了自己的能力，认为自己无所不能。|  The company's overevaluation of the market led to disastrous losses. 公司对市场的高估导致了灾难性的损失。 |  overestimation, overappraisal | undervaluation, underestimation | noun
