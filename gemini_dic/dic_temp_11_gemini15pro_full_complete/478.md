abnegator:/ˈæbnɪɡeɪtər/| n.|放弃者，克己者，禁欲者|A person who denies themselves pleasures or comforts, especially for religious reasons; one who practices self-denial.|例句：The monk lived as an abnegator, renouncing all worldly possessions.  这位僧侣过着禁欲者的生活，放弃了所有的世俗财产。

例句：She was an abnegator of personal comfort for the sake of her family. 她为了家人放弃了个人舒适的生活。|近义词：ascetic, renunciant, self-denier|反义词:hedonist, sensualist, epicurean|词性:noun
