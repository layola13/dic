bump-against:/bʌmp əˈɡɛnst/| phrasal verb | 碰撞；遇到(困难等) | To physically collide with something or to encounter a difficulty or obstacle. |  The car bumped against the wall. 汽车撞到了墙上。

The project bumped against several unforeseen problems.  这个项目遇到了几个意想不到的问题。 | 近义词：collide with, encounter, run into, come up against | 反义词：avoid, bypass | 词性：动词短语
