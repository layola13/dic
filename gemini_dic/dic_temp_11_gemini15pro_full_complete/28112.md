capitalizer:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzər/| n.|大写字母转换器，资本化工具|A tool or program that converts lowercase letters to uppercase. |Example:  She used a capitalizer to format the title of her essay. 她使用了一个大写字母转换器来格式化她论文的标题。|Example: The online capitalizer quickly changed the text to all caps. 在线大写字母转换工具快速地将文本全部转换为大写。|Synonyms: uppercase converter, capitalization tool|Antonyms: lowercase converter|noun

capitalizer:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzər/| n.|利用…的人；资本家|Someone who takes advantage of something; a capitalist.  |Example: He was a capitalizer on the misfortunes of others. 他是一个利用他人不幸的人。|Example:  As a capitalizer, he invested heavily in the stock market.  作为一名资本家，他在股市投入了大量资金。|Synonyms: opportunist, exploiter, capitalist, investor|Antonyms: philanthropist, altruist|noun
