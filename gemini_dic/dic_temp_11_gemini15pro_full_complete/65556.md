eutizing: /juːtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使安乐死，使无痛苦地死去 | The act of euthanizing an animal or person, typically to relieve suffering. | The vet euthanized the suffering dog.  兽医对那只受苦的狗实施了安乐死。|  近义词: mercy killing | 反义词: resuscitation | 词性: 动词

eutizing: /juːtaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 安乐死 | The practice of intentionally ending a life to relieve pain and suffering. | Euthanizing a pet is a difficult decision. 对宠物实施安乐死是一个艰难的决定。 | 近义词: euthanasia | 反义词: resuscitation | 词性: 名词
