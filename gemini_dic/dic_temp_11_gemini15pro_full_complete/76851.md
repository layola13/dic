ganged:/ɡæŋd/
| adj. | 组合的，连接的 | Connected or working together as a group; especially of electrical components or machines. | The switches were ganged together so that they all operated at the same time. 开关连接在一起，以便它们同时操作。 | connected, coupled, linked, joined | separate, independent | adjective

| v.
