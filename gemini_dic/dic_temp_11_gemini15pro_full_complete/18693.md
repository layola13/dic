berate:/bɪˈreɪt/ /ˈbereɪt/| v. |严厉责备，痛斥|To criticize or scold someone angrily and at length.|The coach berated the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉斥责了队员们糟糕的表现。|He berated her for being late. 他因她迟到而痛斥她。|Synonyms: scold, rebuke, reprimand, chastise, upbraid|Antonyms: praise, commend, compliment|verb
