cosmopolitanly:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənli/ | adv. | 世界性地，国际化地 | In a way that includes people from many different countries and cultures. |  She dressed cosmopolitanly, favoring Parisian styles and Italian shoes. 她穿着打扮很有国际范儿，偏爱巴黎风格和意大利鞋子。| cosmopolitanly designed buildings have global influences. 世界性设计的建筑具有全球影响力。 | 近义词: internationally, globally, broadly | 反义词: narrowly, provincially, locally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "cosmopolitan" + "-ly".  "Cosmopolitan" comes from the Greek "kosmopolitēs" (κόσμοπολίτης), meaning "citizen of the world," from "kosmos" (κόσμος), meaning "world," and "politēs" (πολίτης), meaning "citizen."
