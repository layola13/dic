implanting:/ɪmˈplɑːntɪŋ/ |v.| 植入，嵌入 |The doctor is implanting a pacemaker in the patient's chest. 医生正在将起搏器植入病人的胸部。|The idea was firmly implanted in his mind. 这个想法牢牢地印在了他的脑海里。| 近义词: inserting, embedding, grafting | 反义词: removing, extracting | 词性: 动名词 (gerund)
词源: From implant + -ing.  "Implant" comes from the French "implanter," from Late Latin "implantare,"  "to plant in," from "in-" (in, into) + "plantare" (to plant).
