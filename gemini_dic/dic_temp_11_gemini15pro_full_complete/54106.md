disembowelment:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəlmənt/
|n.|取出内脏；剖腹|The act of removing or exposing the internal organs, especially the intestines.  The ritual disembowelment was a gruesome part of the ancient ceremony.  古代仪式中的剖腹仪式是一场可怕的景象。|evisceration, gutting| - |noun
disembowel:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/
|vt.|取出…的内脏；剖腹|To remove the internal organs of, especially the intestines. The samurai disemboweled himself rather than surrender.  武士剖腹自杀也不投降。|eviscerate, gut| - |verb
