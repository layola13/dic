hitch-hike: /ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪk/ (US) /ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪk/ (UK)| v. n.|搭便车；免费搭车旅行|To travel by asking for free rides in passing vehicles.|Example:  We hitch-hiked from Paris to Rome. 我们从巴黎搭便车到罗马。

Example:  He got a hitch-hike to the next town. 他搭便车到了下一个城镇。|近义词：thumb a ride, bum a ride|反义词: drive, pay for transportation |词性: verb, noun

hitch-hike: /ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪk/ (US) /ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪk/ (UK)| n.|搭便车旅行|A journey made by hitch-hiking.|Example:  They went on a hitch-hike across America. 他们搭便车游遍了美国。

Example:  The hitch-hike was long and tiring. 搭便车旅行漫长而疲惫。|近义词：thumbing a ride, bumming a ride|反义词: road trip, journey |词性: noun
