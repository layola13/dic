cacique:/kəˈsiːk/ | n. |酋长；头领；首领|A leader or chief, especially of a Native American tribe in the Caribbean or Central or South America.| The cacique ruled over a vast territory.  这位酋长统治着广阔的领土。|Synonyms: chief, chieftain, leader, head, ruler|Antonyms: follower, subordinate|noun

cacique:/kəˈsiːk/ | n. |地方政客；有权势的人|A local political boss or person of influence.|The cacique controlled all the businesses in the town.  这个地方政客控制着镇上所有的生意。|Synonyms: boss, leader, politician, power broker|Antonyms:  underling, nobody|noun
