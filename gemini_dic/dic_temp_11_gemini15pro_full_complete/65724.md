eventuality:/ɪˌvɛntʃuˈælɪti/
n.
可能性；可能发生的事情；不测事件
a possible event, occurrence, or result, especially an unpleasant one.
例句：
We must be prepared for all eventualities.
我们必须为所有可能发生的情况做好准备。

We had considered that eventuality.
我们已经考虑过那种可能性。

近义词：possibility, likelihood, chance, contingency, prospect
反义词：impossibility, improbability
