词源:
From Latin malevolens, present participle of malevŏlēre "to wish ill," from male "badly" + volēre "to wish."
来自拉丁语 malevolens，malevŏlēre 的现在分词，意思是“希望坏事发生”，来自 male “坏地” + volēre “希望”。
