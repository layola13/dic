acidified: /əˈsɪdɪfaɪd/
| adj. | 酸化的 | Made or become acid; converted into an acid. | The acidified soil is not suitable for most plants. 酸化的土壤不适合大多数植物生长。| The lake was acidified by industrial pollution.  这个湖泊被工业污染酸化了。| 近义词: sour, acidic | 反义词: alkaline, basic | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From acid + -ify (from French -ifier, from Latin -ficare) + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin acidus "sour".
