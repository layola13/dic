letout:/ˈlɛtaʊt/
n.|排气孔，排放口；（衣服的）放宽，放大；解脱，摆脱|A let-out is a means of escape or release, often referring to a vent or an alteration to clothing for a looser fit. It can also metaphorically represent a release from obligation or difficulty.|The tailor added a let-out to the waist of the trousers.  裁缝把裤子的腰部放宽了一些。|synonyms: vent, outlet, release, escape, relief|antonyms: constraint, restriction|noun

letout:/ˈlɛtaʊt/
n.|（秘密的）泄露|A let-out can refer to a disclosure of a secret.|His confession was a let-out for his accomplices. 他的供认让他的同伙得以解脱。|synonyms: disclosure, revelation, leak|antonyms: concealment, suppression|noun
