intersecting:/ˌɪntərˈsektɪŋ/
adjective|相交的，交叉的|meeting and crossing each other|The two roads are intersecting at a right angle. 这两条路以直角相交。|近义词：crossing, overlapping, converging|反义词:parallel, divergent|词性:adjective
intersecting:/ˌɪntərˈsektɪŋ/
noun|交叉点，交点|the point where two or more things meet and cross each other|The intersecting of the two lines creates a point. 两条线的交点形成了一个点。|近义词：intersection, junction, crossroads|反义词:separation, divergence|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *intersect-, from intersecare "to cut or divide in the middle, cut each other," from inter "between" + secare "to cut".
