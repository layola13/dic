nincompooperies: /nɪŋˈkɒmpuːpəriːz/
noun
愚蠢的行为；胡闹；蠢事
foolish or silly behavior or ideas.  Plural form of nincompoopery.
His latest business venture is just another one of his nincompooperies. 他最新的商业冒险不过是又一次愚蠢的行为。
近义词：foolishness, silliness, absurdity, nonsense
反义词：wisdom, sense, intelligence
