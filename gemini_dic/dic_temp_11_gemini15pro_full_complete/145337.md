overbrutalizing:/ˌoʊvərˈbruːtəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
过度残暴化；过度野蛮化
The act of brutalizing something or someone excessively.  It implies going beyond simply being brutal and reaching a level of extreme cruelty or violence.
The dictator was accused of overbrutalizing the protesting citizens.  独裁者被指控过度残暴地对待抗议的公民。

The regime was condemned for overbrutalizing its political opponents. 该政权因过度残酷对待其政治对手而受到谴责。

Synonyms:  savaging, tormenting, torturing,  manhandling
Antonyms:  protecting, cherishing, nurturing
