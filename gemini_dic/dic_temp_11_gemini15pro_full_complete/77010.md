garganey:/ˈɡɑːrɡəni/ | n. |  小麻鸭 | A small dabbling duck (Anas querquedula) breeding in Europe and Asia and wintering in Africa and India. It has a distinctive broad white stripe above the eye. |  The garganey is a migratory bird, spending its summers in the northern hemisphere. 小麻鸭是一种候鸟，夏季生活在北半球。

| We observed a garganey foraging in the shallows. 我们观察到一只小麻鸭在浅滩觅食。 |  近义词: teal, wigeon | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French 'gargate', meaning throat, referring to its call. 源自古法语“gargate”，意为喉咙，指的是它的叫声。
