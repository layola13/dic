itching:/ˈɪtʃɪŋ/| adj. n.|发痒的；渴望的|An uncomfortable sensation on the skin that causes a desire to scratch; a strong desire or urge.|
The itching from the mosquito bite was driving me crazy. 蚊子叮咬引起的瘙痒快把我逼疯了。|
近义词：scratchy, irritated, yearning, longing|
反义词: comfortable, satisfied|
词性: adjective, noun
