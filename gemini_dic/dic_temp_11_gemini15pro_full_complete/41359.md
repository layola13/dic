cork-like:/ˈkɔːrkˌlaɪk/
adjective
像软木的；软木似的
Resembling cork in texture, appearance, or lightness.
The tree bark was cork-like and easy to peel.  这棵树的树皮像软木一样，很容易剥落。
The material had a cork-like buoyancy.  这种材料具有像软木一样的浮力。
近义词: spongy, porous, buoyant
反义词: dense, compact, solid
词性: adjective


cork:/kɔːrk/
noun
软木；软木塞
The elastic outer bark of the cork oak, used for bottle stoppers and insulation.
He pulled the cork out of the wine bottle. 他把软木塞从酒瓶里拔了出来。
The floor was made of cork tiles. 地板是用软木砖铺成的。
近义词: stopper
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: noun
