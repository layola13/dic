flurries:/ˈflɜːriz/ | n. | 阵雪；一阵忙乱（flurry的复数形式） | A brief, light snowfall. Also, a sudden burst of activity. |  The weather forecast predicted snow flurries in the afternoon. 天气预报预测下午会有阵雪。| synonyms: bursts, gusts, showers, squalls | antonyms: calms, lulls | noun

flurry:/ˈflɜːri/ | n. | 一阵；忙乱 | A sudden, brief period of activity or excitement.  | There was a flurry of activity in the kitchen as the chefs prepared for the dinner rush.  厨师们为晚餐高峰做准备，厨房里一片忙碌。| synonyms: burst, rush, spate, outburst | antonyms: calm, lull, quiet | noun
