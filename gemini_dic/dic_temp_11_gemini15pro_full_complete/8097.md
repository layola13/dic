anthropomorphism: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ | n. |拟人化，人格化|The attribution of human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.|例句：The cartoon uses anthropomorphism to give the animals human-like qualities. 这部动画片运用拟人化赋予动物们类似人类的特质。

例句：Anthropomorphism is a common literary device used to make animals or inanimate objects relatable. 拟人化是一种常见的文学手法，用于使动物或无生命物体更容易让人产生共鸣。


近义词：personification |反义词: objectification| 词性: noun
