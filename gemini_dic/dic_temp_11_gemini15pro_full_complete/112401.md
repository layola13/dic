lostness:/ˈlɒstnɪs/ (UK) /ˈlɑːstnɪs/ (US)| n.| 迷失，困惑，失落感 |The state of being lost, either physically or emotionally; a feeling of disorientation and uncertainty.|  She felt a deep sense of lostness after her husband died. 她丈夫去世后，她感到深深的失落感。|  He wandered through the city streets, overwhelmed by a feeling of lostness. 他在城市街道上游荡，被一种迷失感淹没。| synonyms: bewilderment, confusion, disorientation, perplexity | antonyms: certainty, clarity, direction, orientation | noun
