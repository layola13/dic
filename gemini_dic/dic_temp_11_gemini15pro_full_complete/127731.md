negligibly:/ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbli/| adv.|微不足道地，可以忽略地|In such cases, the error is negligibly small. 在这种情况下，误差小到可以忽略不计。|The effect on the overall result was negligibly small. 对整体结果的影响微乎其微。|近义词：insignificantly, minimally, trivially, slightly|反义词：significantly, substantially, considerably|词性:副词

词源:
From negligible + -ly.  Negligible comes from Late Latin *negligibilis, from Latin neglectus (past participle of neglegere “to neglect”) + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives).
