cowan:/ˈkaʊən/ | n. | 石匠，建筑工人，干墙工 | A person who builds with stone, especially a drystone waller. |  He's a skilled cowan, able to build walls without mortar. 他是一位技艺精湛的石匠，能够建造不用砂浆的墙。| 近义词: mason, builder, stoneworker | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源:
From Middle English *cowen, probably of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Middle Dutch kouwen (“to beat, strike”)
