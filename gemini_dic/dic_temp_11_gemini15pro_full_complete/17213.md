bavin:/ˈbævɪn/| n.|柴火，引火物|Brushwood or light kindling used for starting a fire.|The campers used bavin to get their campfire going. 露营者用柴火引燃篝火。|近义词: kindling, tinder|反义词: fuel (in a broader sense)|词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *bavyne, from Old French *baïne (“brushwood, faggot”), from a Romance root akin to Latin *baca (“berry”).
