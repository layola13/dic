mucedin:/ˈmjuːsɪdɪn/| n. |麦胶蛋白|A type of protein found in wheat gluten that is responsible for some of the elastic properties of dough and is also a major allergen for people with celiac disease.|The mucedin in the flour gives the bread its chewy texture.| 近义词:glutenin (麦谷蛋白)|反义词: None (没有明确的反义词) | Noun
