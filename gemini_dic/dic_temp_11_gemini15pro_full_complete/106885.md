labyrinthine:/ˌlæbəˈrɪnθaɪn/ /ˌlæbəˈrɪnθɪn/
adjective
迷宫般的；复杂的；曲折的
Resembling a labyrinth; intricate and confusing.
The building's labyrinthine corridors made it easy to get lost.  这栋建筑迷宫般的走廊很容易让人迷路。
The plot of the novel was so labyrinthine that I couldn't follow it.  这部小说的情节如此复杂，我根本看不懂。
近义词:complex, intricate, convoluted, tortuous
反义词:simple, straightforward, clear
词性:adjective

labyrinth:/ˈlæbərɪnθ/
noun
迷宫；曲径
a complicated irregular network of passages or paths in which it is difficult to find one's way; a maze.
The old city's streets were a labyrinth of narrow alleys and winding roads.  这座老城的街道是由狭窄的小巷和蜿蜒的道路组成的迷宫。
She felt lost in a labyrinth of conflicting emotions. 她感觉迷失在相互冲突的情感迷宫中。
近义词:maze, network, tangle
反义词:path, road, straight line
词性:noun

词源: From Latin *labyrinthus*, from Ancient Greek λαβύρινθος (labúrinthos).
