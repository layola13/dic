overpeoples:/ˌoʊvərˈpiːpəlz/ | v. | 使人口过剩；移民于…并取代原住民 | To populate (a place) in excessive numbers, or to migrate to and displace the original inhabitants of (a place). |  The invading army overpeopled the conquered territory, driving out the native population. 入侵军队使被征服的领土人口过剩，驱逐了原住民。

The small island was quickly overpeopled due to the influx of refugees. 由于难民涌入，这个小岛很快人口过剩。 | 近义词: overcrowd, overpopulate | 反义词: depopulate | 词性: 动词
