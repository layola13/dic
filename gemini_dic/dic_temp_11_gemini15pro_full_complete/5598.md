altercation: /ˌɔːltərˈkeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌɒltəˈkeɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|争吵，争执|A noisy argument or disagreement, especially in public.|例句：The boys had a noisy altercation on the playground.  男孩们在操场上大声争吵。

例句：A heated altercation broke out between the two drivers after the accident.  事故发生后，两位司机之间爆发了激烈的争吵。|近义词：argument, quarrel, dispute, squabble, tiff, clash, wrangle|反义词：agreement, harmony, concord|词性: 名词
