concerti:/kənˈtʃɛrtiː/
n.
音乐会，协奏曲（concerto的复数形式）
plural of concerto, a musical composition for a solo instrument or instruments accompanied by an orchestra.
Last night, we attended a series of concerti featuring works by Mozart and Bach. 昨晚，我们参加了一系列以莫扎特和巴赫的作品为特色的协奏曲音乐会。
The orchestra performed three concerti during their European tour.  这支管弦乐队在他们的欧洲巡演中演奏了三首协奏曲。
近义词:concertos
反义词: 无
词性:名词
