lithonthryptic:/ˌlɪθɒnˈθrɪptɪk/ | adj. | 碎石的，溶石的 | Relating to or causing the breaking down or dissolving of stones, especially gallstones or kidney stones. | Lithonthryptic medications can help dissolve kidney stones. 碎石药物可以帮助溶解肾结石。 | synonyms: lithotriptic, antilithic | antonyms: lithogenic | adjective
