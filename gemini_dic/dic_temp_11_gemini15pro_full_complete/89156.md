homeliest:/ˈhoʊmliɪst/ (US), /ˈhəʊmliɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最朴素的，最不好看的，最不吸引人的|Plain or unattractive in appearance; lacking in elegance or refinement.  It suggests a simple, unpretentious, and perhaps even slightly unattractive appearance, often in a comforting or familiar way.|例句：She wore the homeliest dress to the party, but she had the warmest personality. 她穿着最朴素的裙子去参加聚会，但她有着最温暖的个性。

例句：He had the homeliest face I'd ever seen, but his kindness shone through. 他有着我见过的最不好看的脸，但他的善良闪耀其中。|近义词：plain, unattractive, unappealing, homely, simple, unadorned|反义词：beautiful, attractive, glamorous, elegant, sophisticated|词性:形容词
