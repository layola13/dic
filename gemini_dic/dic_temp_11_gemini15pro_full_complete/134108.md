nonimmigration:/ˌnɑnɪməɡˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 非移民，不移民 |  The study of nonimmigration focuses on the factors that prevent people from migrating.  非移民研究的重点是阻止人们迁移的因素。| Example 1: The government's policies on nonimmigration are complex. 政府的非移民政策很复杂。

Example 2:  Nonimmigration can be influenced by strong family ties.  非移民可能会受到强烈的家庭关系的影响。 |Near synonyms: non-migration, staying, remaining | Antonyms: immigration, emigration, migration | noun
