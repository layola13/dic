operationality:/ˌɒpəˈreɪʃəˈnælɪti/ /ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃəˈnælɪti/
noun
可操作性；实用性；操作性
The state or quality of being operational or capable of being put into operation; practicality.
The operationality of the new system is still being evaluated.  新系统的可操作性仍在评估中。
synonyms: practicality, feasibility, workability, usability, functionality
antonyms: impracticality, infeasibility, unworkability
