gashing:/ˈɡæʃɪŋ/| adj.| 划伤的；割伤的；深切的| inflicting or characterized by a deep cut; making a long, deep cut. |The earthquake left a gashing wound across the landscape. 地震在地表留下了一道深深的伤痕。|近义词：cutting, slashing, lacerating, tearing, ripping|反义词: healing, mending|词性: adjective
gash:/ɡæʃ/| n. vt.| 深长的伤口；划开；砍伤|a long, deep cut; to make a long, deep cut in something.| The falling tree branch gave him a nasty gash on his arm. 掉落的树枝在他的胳膊上留下了一道严重的伤口。  He gashed his hand while chopping wood. 他劈柴时砍伤了手。|近义词：cut, laceration, wound, slash, tear|反义词: heal|词性: noun, verb
词源: From Old French *gasche, variant of mache (“gap, notch”). Related to Old North French gache (“gap, notch”).
