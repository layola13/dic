micromanagement:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 微观管理 | The act of controlling every part of a situation, including small details, often in a way that is annoying or not helpful. |  Example: The employees resented the micromanagement of their boss, who insisted on approving every email. 员工们怨恨老板的微观管理，他坚持要批准每一封电子邮件。

Example: Excessive micromanagement can stifle creativity and innovation. 过度的微观管理会扼杀创造力和创新。| 近义词: over-management, excessive control | 反义词: delegation, empowerment | 词性: noun
