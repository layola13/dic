chic-est:/ˈʃiːkɪst/
adjective
最时髦的，最别致的
Superlative form of chic: most chic.  It describes something as being the most fashionable, stylish, and elegant.
She wore the chic-est outfit at the party. 她派对上穿的衣服最时髦。
Synonyms: most stylish, trendiest, most fashionable
Antonyms: dowdiest, frumpiest, least fashionable
词性: 形容词
