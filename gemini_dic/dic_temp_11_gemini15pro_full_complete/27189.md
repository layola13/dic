calliper: /ˈkælɪpər/ (US) /ˈkælɪpə/ (UK)
n.|卡尺；测径器；圆规|A device used to measure the thickness or diameter of something, typically a pair of curved arms that can be adjusted to grip the object being measured. |The engineer used a calliper to measure the diameter of the pipe. 工程师用卡尺测量管子的直径。|
synonyms: caliper, gage (US), gauge (UK)|
antonyms: None (measuring tool, doesn't have a direct opposite)
noun

calliper: /ˈkælɪpər/ (US) /ˈkælɪpə/ (UK)
v.|用卡尺测量|To measure something with a calliper. |He callipered the thickness of the metal sheet. 他用卡尺测量了金属板的厚度。|
synonyms: measure, gauge|
antonyms: None (action of measuring, doesn't have a direct opposite)
verb
