extender:/ɪkˈstendər/ | n. | 延长器；扩展器；补充物 |Something that extends or increases the size or scope of something else. |She bought an extender for her Wi-Fi router to improve coverage throughout the house. 她买了一个Wi-Fi路由器的扩展器来提高整个房子的覆盖范围。|  extender cables are useful for reaching distant outlets. 延长线对于够到远处的插座很有用。 | synonyms: expansion, extension, supplement, addition | antonyms: reducer, shortener | noun

extensible:/ɪkˈstɛnsɪbl/ | adj. | 可延长的；可扩展的 |Able to be extended or made larger. | The table has extensible legs, so it can accommodate more people. 这张桌子有可伸缩的腿，所以可以容纳更多的人。| This software is extensible and can be adapted to meet your specific needs. 这个软件是可扩展的，可以根据您的特定需求进行调整。 | synonyms: expandable, adjustable, flexible, adaptable | antonyms: fixed, rigid, inflexible | adjective
