enrobe:/ɪnˈroʊb/| vt.|穿上…，覆盖…; 授予…长袍(作为荣誉标志)|To dress (someone or something) in a robe or garment; to cover or surround.|The queen was enrobed in a velvet gown. 女王身穿天鹅绒长袍。|近义词：dress, clothe, drape, cover, adorn|反义词：undress, uncover, strip|verb

enrobe:/ɪnˈroʊb/| n.|（尤指作为荣誉标志的）长袍|A robe, especially one worn as a mark of honor.  (This noun usage is less common.)|The king's enrobe was adorned with jewels. 国王的礼袍上饰有珠宝。|近义词：robe, gown, vestment|反义词： (No direct antonym for the noun form)|noun
