domiciled:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪld/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsaɪld/ (US)| adj.|居住的，定居的|Having one's permanent residence in a particular place.|例句：He is domiciled in France. 他居住在法国。|近义词：resident, located, inhabiting, dwelling|反义词：non-resident, unsettled, nomadic|词性:形容词
domicile:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsaɪl/ (US)| n. vt.|住所，定居；使定居|A person's fixed, permanent, and principal home for legal purposes; to establish in a domicile.|例句：She established her domicile in New York. 她在纽约定居。|近义词：residence, abode, dwelling, home|反义词：none (noun); evict, displace (verb)|词性:名词/动词

词源: From Old French *domicilium, from Latin domicilium (“habitation, dwelling, abode”), from domus (“house, home”).
