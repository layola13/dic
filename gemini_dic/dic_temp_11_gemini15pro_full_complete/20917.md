blear:/blɪr/| v. adj.|使模糊，模糊的|To make dim or indistinct; to obscure.  As an adjective, it describes something dim or indistinct.|The rain bleared the windshield, making it difficult to see. 雨水模糊了挡风玻璃，使人难以看清。|近义词：blur, dim, obscure|反义词:clear, sharpen, distinct|词性:动词, 形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *bleren*, from Old English *blērian* (“to make dim, to darken, to deceive”), from Proto-Germanic *blairijaną* (“to make dim”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰley- (“to shine; to bloom; to grow”)|.
