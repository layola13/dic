mislactation:/ˌmɪslækˈteɪʃən/
|n.|泌乳异常，不当哺乳|
|A condition characterized by faulty, deficient, or abnormal secretion of milk.|
|The new mother experienced mislactation, causing her baby to be undernourished.|这位新妈妈经历了泌乳异常，导致她的宝宝营养不良。
|
|Mislactation can be a serious issue for newborns.|泌乳异常对新生儿来说可能是个严重的问题。
|
|Synonym: lactation failure, insufficient lactation, poor milk supply|
|Antonym: normal lactation, successful breastfeeding|
|noun|
