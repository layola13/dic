forgivingnesses:/fərˈɡɪvɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
宽恕，原谅
The act of forgiving someone or something multiple times.  Repeated instances of pardon or absolution.
He was known for his many forgivingnesses, always willing to give people a second chance. 他以多次宽恕他人而闻名，总是愿意给人们第二次机会。
Her repeated forgivingnesses eventually led to her being taken advantage of. 她的一再宽恕最终导致她被利用。
Synonyms: pardons, absolutions, remissions
Antonyms: vengefulness, resentments, unforgivingness
