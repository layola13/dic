nontemperamentally:/nɒnˌtɛmp(ə)rəˈmɛnt(ə)li/ | adv. | 非气质性地，不情绪化地 |  In a way that is not influenced by or related to temperament; calmly and rationally. | He handled the situation nontemperamentally, considering all the facts before reacting. 他冷静地处理了情况，在作出反应之前考虑了所有的事实。| 近义词: calmly, rationally, dispassionately, objectively | 反义词: temperamentally, emotionally, irrationally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + temperamentally (derived from temperament, from Late Latin temperamentum "a mixing in due proportion," from Latin temperare "to mix, blend, moderate")
