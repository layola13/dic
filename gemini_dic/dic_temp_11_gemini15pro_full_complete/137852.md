nonvillainous:/ˌnɒnˈvɪlənəs/
adjective
非邪恶的; 非反派的
Not villainous; not wicked or criminal.
The nonvillainous characters in the play provided a much-needed sense of hope.  这出戏中非邪恶的角色提供了急需的希望感。
The king, surprisingly, was portrayed as a nonvillainous, even sympathetic, character.  令人惊讶的是，国王被描绘成一个非邪恶的，甚至富有同情心的角色。
Synonyms: innocent, virtuous, righteous, good, moral
Antonyms: villainous, wicked, evil, malevolent, malicious
词性: 形容词
