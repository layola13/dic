antitheist:/ˌæntiˈθiːɪst/ | n. | 反神论者；积极反对有神论的人 | Someone who actively opposes theism; a person who believes there is no God and is against the idea of God or religion. |  Antitheists are more than just atheists; they actively campaign against religious belief.  反神论者不仅仅是无神论者；他们积极地反对宗教信仰。

He's a vocal antitheist who often debates religious leaders. 他是一位直言不讳的反神论者，经常与宗教领袖辩论。 | 近义词:militant atheist, strong atheist | 反义词:theist, believer | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + theist.  来自 anti- + theist.
anti-: against, opposed to  反对，对抗
theist: someone who believes in the existence of a god or gods  相信神存在的人
