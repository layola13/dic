ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hæfen (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *hafnaz (“haven, sea”), from Proto-Indo-European *kap- (“to seize, hold”). Cognate with Old Norse höfn (“haven”), Old Frisian hafene, Dutch haven, German Hafen.
