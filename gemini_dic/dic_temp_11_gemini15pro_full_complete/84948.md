haphazard:/ˌhæpˈhæzərd/| adj.|偶然的，随意的，杂乱无章的|marked by lack of plan, order, or direction|The books were piled on the shelves in a haphazard way. 书架上的书堆放得很随意。|Synonyms: random, arbitrary, unplanned, chaotic, disorganized|Antonyms: planned, organized, systematic, methodical|adjective

haphazard:/ˌhæpˈhæzərd/| adv.|偶然地，随意地|in a haphazard manner|The ingredients were haphazardly thrown into the pot.  食材被随意地扔进了锅里。|Synonyms: randomly, arbitrarily, carelessly|Antonyms: methodically, systematically, deliberately|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From hap (n.) "chance, fortune" + hazard (n.) "game of chance."  Originally meant "characterized by chance," later acquired the sense of "lacking order or planning."
