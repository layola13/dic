eels:/iːlz/| n.| 鳗鱼 |Long, snake-like fish that live in both fresh and salt water.|We saw some eels swimming in the river. 我们看到一些鳗鱼在河里游泳。|近义词:fish, morays|反义词:None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æl (plural *ælas), from Proto-Germanic *ēlaz, from Proto-Indo-European *el- "brown, yellow, green."  Related to German Aal.
