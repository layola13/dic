hell-around:/ˈhel əˈraʊnd/
verb phrase
到处乱跑；胡闹；狂欢作乐
To behave in a wild and reckless manner, often involving noisy or disruptive activities.  To engage in excessive revelry or carousing.
He spent his youth hell-arounding with his friends, going to parties and getting into trouble. 他年轻时和朋友们到处胡闹，参加派对，惹是生非。
They were hell-arounding in the streets after the big game. 大赛结束后，他们在街上狂欢作乐。
近义词: carouse, revel, party, raise hell
反义词: behave, relax, calm down
词性: verb phrase
