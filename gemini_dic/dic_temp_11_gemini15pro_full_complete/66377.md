exeat:/ˈɛksiæt/ | n. |  准假证；离校许可证 | A formal permission granted to a student to be absent from school or college, especially for a specific period. | The student obtained an exeat to visit his family for the weekend.  这个学生获得了一张周末回家探亲的准假证。 | 近义词: leave, permission, pass | 反义词: detention, confinement | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *exeat*, third-person singular present active subjunctive of *exire* "to go out," from *ex* "out" + *ire* "to go."
