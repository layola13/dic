macroscopically:/ˌmækrəˈskɒpɪkli/ | adv. |宏观地，肉眼可见地 | Observable with the naked eye; not microscopic.  Relating to large-scale properties and features, as opposed to microscopic details. |  The tumor was macroscopically visible.  肿瘤肉眼可见。| Synonyms: visibly, observably, grossly | Antonyms: microscopically, invisibly | adverb
