creche: /kreʃ/ (UK) /kreɪʃ/ (US)| n. |托儿所，日托中心|A nursery where babies and young children are cared for during the day, especially while their parents are at work. |例句：The company provides a creche for employees' children.  公司为员工的孩子提供托儿所。

例句：She dropped her son off at the creche before heading to work. 她把儿子送到托儿所后就去上班了。

近义词：nursery, daycare, day nursery |反义词: None |词性: noun
