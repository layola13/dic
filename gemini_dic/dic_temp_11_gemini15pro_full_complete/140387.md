oilmen:/ˈɔɪlmən/ | n. |石油工人，石油商|A person who works in the oil industry, especially one who drills for or produces oil. | The oilmen were working hard to control the spill. 石油工人正在努力控制石油泄漏。|  The oilmen gathered at the annual conference. 石油商们聚集在年度会议上。|Synonyms: oil worker, petroleum worker, oil producer, oil executive | Antonyms: None | Noun
