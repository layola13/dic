coleading:/ˌkəʊˈliːdɪŋ/
| adj. |共同领导的|Sharing the leadership of something; leading jointly.|Example: They are coleading the project. 他们在共同领导这个项目。|近义词:jointly leading, shared leadership|反义词:sole leadership, independent leadership|词性:形容词

coleading:/ˌkəʊˈliːdɪŋ/
| n. |共同领导|The act of leading jointly.|Example: Coleading can be challenging but rewarding. 共同领导可能具有挑战性，但也回报丰厚。|近义词:joint leadership, shared leadership|反义词:sole leadership, independent leadership|词性:名词
