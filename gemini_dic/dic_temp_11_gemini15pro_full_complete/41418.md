cornerback:/ˈkɔːrnərbæk/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəbæk/ (UK)| n. |角卫|A defensive player in American football positioned near the sidelines, responsible for covering wide receivers.|The cornerback intercepted the pass and ran it back for a touchdown. 角卫拦截了传球并带球触地得分。|近义词:defender, defensive back|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps offensively: wide receiver, quarterback)|词性:noun
