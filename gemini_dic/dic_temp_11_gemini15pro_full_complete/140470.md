old-maidism:/ˌoʊld ˈmeɪdɪzəm/ | n. |老处女行为，老处女作风|The state of being an old maid, or the attitudes and behaviors stereotypically associated with unmarried older women, often characterized by excessive neatness, fussiness, primness, and a preoccupation with trivial matters.| Her old-maidism was evident in her meticulously organized spice rack. 她那老处女作风从她一丝不苟的调味料架上可见一斑。|Synonyms: spinsterhood, prudery, primness |Antonyms: worldliness, sophistication| noun
