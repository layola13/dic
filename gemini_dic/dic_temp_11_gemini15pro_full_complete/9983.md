aphorist:/ˈæfərɪst/| n. | 格言家；警句家 | A person who writes or speaks aphorisms.  A skilled writer of aphorisms, known for expressing general truths or observations in concise, witty sayings. |  Mark Twain was a famous American aphorist. 马克·吐温是一位著名的美国格言家。

Her grandfather, a retired professor and amateur aphorist, always had a witty saying for every occasion. 她祖父是一位退休的教授和业余格言家，在每个场合总能说出机智的名言。 | 近义词：sage, maxim writer,  pundit | 反义词:  prolix writer | 词性: noun
