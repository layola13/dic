nettliest:/ˈnet(ə)liɪst/
adjective
最易怒的；最难相处的；最刺人的
Covered in stinging hairs; easily irritated or annoyed.  (Most + nettly)
例句：She was known for having the nettliest personality in the office. 她以办公室里最易怒的性格而闻名。

例句：He gave a nettliest response to the innocent question. 他对这个无辜的问题做出了最暴躁的回应。


近义词：irritable, touchy, prickly, irascible, cantankerous
反义词：calm, placid, affable, amiable, even-tempered
词性: adjective

词源：
From nettle + -y + -est.  Nettle comes from Old English *netele, of Germanic origin.  The suffix -y forms adjectives meaning 'characterized by' (e.g., thorny, dirty), while -est is the superlative suffix.
