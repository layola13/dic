hit-the-road:/ˌhɪt ðə ˈroʊd/| idiom |出发，上路|To begin a journey, especially by car.  Leaving a place, often quickly. |Example:  We need to hit the road early tomorrow if we want to arrive on time. 我们明天得早点出发，如果我们想准时到达。

Example: Time to hit the road!  It's getting late. 该上路了！时间不早了。|近义词：depart, leave, set off, get going |反义词: arrive, stay |词性: idiom
