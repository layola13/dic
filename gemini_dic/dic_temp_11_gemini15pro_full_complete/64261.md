equiponderate:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒndəreɪt/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːndəreɪt/
adjective
等重的；均衡的
Equal in weight, force, or importance; balanced.
The two sides in the conflict were equiponderate, with neither having a clear advantage.  冲突双方势均力敌，都没有明显的优势。
The arguments for and against the proposal were equiponderate.  支持和反对这项提案的论点势均力敌。
近义词:equal, balanced, equivalent, commensurate
反义词:unequal, unbalanced, disproportionate
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *aequiponderatus*, from aequi- (equal) + ponderatus (weighed), past participle of ponderare (to weigh).
