out-work: /ˌaʊtˈwɜːrk/
verb
胜过工作；工作更努力
To work harder or more effectively than someone else.
He out-worked his colleagues and quickly earned a promotion. 他比同事工作更努力，很快就升职了。
The team was out-worked and out-played in the final.  该队在决赛中工作效率和比赛技巧都不如对手。
近义词: outdo, surpass, outperform
反义词: underperform
词性: verb
