locofoco:/ˌloʊkəˈfoʊkoʊ/
noun
激进的民主党人，火把派
A member of a radical faction of the Democratic Party, especially during the 1830s and 1840s, who favored hard money, free trade, and abolition of monopolies and other special privileges.
The Locofocos, a radical faction of the New York Democratic Party, played a significant role in the politics of the 1830s. 火把派，纽约民主党的一个激进派别，在19世纪30年代的政治中扮演了重要角色。
During the Panic of 1837, the Locofocos advocated for hard-money policies. 在1837年的金融恐慌期间，火把派倡导硬通货政策。
Synonyms: radical, extremist, agitator
Antonyms: conservative, moderate, reactionary
