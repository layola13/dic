non-endemic:/ˌnɒn ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒn ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ (UK)| adj.|非地方性的，非流行的|Not native or restricted to a certain region or locality; not endemic.  It refers to something that is not confined to a particular place or population.|The virus is non-endemic to this region, meaning it originated elsewhere.|Synonyms: non-native, foreign, introduced, exotic|Antonyms: endemic, native, indigenous|adjective
