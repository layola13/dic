husking:/ˈhʌskɪŋ/ | n. |脱壳，去壳|The act of removing the husks from something, such as corn or nuts.|例句：The husking of the corn took several hours. 玉米脱壳花了几个小时。|近义词：shelling, shucking|反义词:covering, encasing|词性:名词

husking:/ˈhʌskɪŋ/ | adj. |用于脱壳的|Used for removing husks.|例句：A husking machine can process large quantities of corn. 脱壳机可以处理大量的玉米。|近义词：shelling, shucking|反义词:N/A|词性:形容词
