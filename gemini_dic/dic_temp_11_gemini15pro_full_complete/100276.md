intersected:/ˌɪntərˈsektɪd/
| v. |相交的，交叉的|divided by or meeting another line or surface. |The lines intersected at a right angle. 这两条线相交成直角。|近义词：crossed, converged, met, |反义词:parallel, divergent|verb

intersect:/ˌɪntərˈsekt/
| v. |相交，交叉|to divide something by passing or lying across it. |The roads intersect near the bridge. 道路在桥附近相交。|近义词：cross, converge, meet, |反义词:diverge, bypass|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *intersectus*, past participle of intersecare (“to cut between, divide”), from inter (“between”) + secare (“to cut”).
