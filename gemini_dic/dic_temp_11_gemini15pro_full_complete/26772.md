caesarians:/sɪˈzɛəriənz/| n.|剖腹产手术；剖腹产出生的婴儿|A surgical operation for delivering a child by cutting through the wall of the abdomen and the uterus.  Babies delivered by Caesarian section. |例句：The baby was delivered by caesarian section.  这个婴儿是剖腹产出生的。

例句：Many women now opt for caesarians. 现在很多女性选择剖腹产。|近义词：C-section, Cesarean section, Cesarean delivery|反义词:vaginal birth|词性:noun
