antidogmatic:/ˌæntiːdɒɡˈmætɪk/
adjective
反教条的，不教条的
Opposed to or free from dogma or dogmatism.
例句：The professor encouraged antidogmatic thinking, urging students to challenge established theories. 教授鼓励反教条的思考，敦促学生挑战既定的理论。

例句：Her antidogmatic approach to religion made her open to diverse spiritual perspectives. 她不教条的宗教观使她对不同的精神观点持开放态度。

近义词：open-minded, tolerant, liberal, flexible, undogmatic
反义词：dogmatic, doctrinaire, rigid, inflexible, authoritarian
词性：adjective
