castillo:/kæˈstiːjoʊ/ /kɑːˈstiːjoʊ/
n.
castle, fortress
A large fortified building or group of buildings, typically used as a royal or noble residence.
The king lived in a grand castillo overlooking the valley. 国王住在一座俯瞰山谷的宏伟城堡里。
synonyms: castle, fortress, stronghold, citadel
antonyms: hut, shack
noun
