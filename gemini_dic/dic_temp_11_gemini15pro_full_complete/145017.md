overadvancing: /ˌoʊvərədˈvænsɪŋ/
Verb
过度推进；过分前进
advancing too far or too quickly.
The army was overadvancing and vulnerable to a counterattack.  军队过度推进，容易受到反击。
Their overadvancing strategy put them at a disadvantage. 他们的过度推进战略使他们处于劣势。
近义词: overreaching, overextending
反义词: retreating, withdrawing
词性: v. (present participle of overadvance)
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "advance" (verb meaning "to move forward")
