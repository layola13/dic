etherealities:/ɪˌθɪəriˈælɪtiz/ | n. |空灵的事物；超凡脱俗的事物|The state or quality of being ethereal; intangible or delicate things.|Her paintings capture the etherealities of a dream world. 她的画捕捉到了梦境的空灵之美。| 近义词:abstractions, subtleties, impalpabilities |反义词: realities, concreteness, tangibilities |词性:名词

词源:  From ethereal + -ity + -ies.  "Ethereal" comes from the Greek "aithēr" (αἰθήρ) meaning "upper air" or "bright, pure air," later associated with the heavens.
