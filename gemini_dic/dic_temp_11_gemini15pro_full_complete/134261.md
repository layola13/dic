noninflammatory:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflæmətɔːri/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪnˈflæmətəri/ (UK)| adj.|非炎性的，非炎症性的|Not causing or characterized by inflammation; not tending to excite or inflame.|This cream is noninflammatory and suitable for sensitive skin.  这种乳霜是非炎性的，适合敏感肌肤。|Synonyms: non-phlogistic, anti-inflammatory|Antonyms: inflammatory, phlogistic|adjective
