anthologize:/ænˈθɑːlədʒaɪz/
v.
选录；编选入选集
to include something in an anthology
Her short story was anthologized in "The Best American Short Stories." 她的短篇小说被选入了《美国最佳短篇小说》。

anthologize:/ænˈθɒlədʒaɪz/
v.
选录；编选入选集
to include something in an anthology
Several of his poems were anthologized. 他的几首诗被选入了诗集。


近义词:compile, collect, gather
反义词:exclude, omit
词性:verb

词源:
From anthology + -ize.  Anthology comes from Greek *anthologia "collection of flowers, garland of flowers" (used figuratively of poems, etc.), from anthos "flower" + logia "collection."
