enlimn:/ɪnˈlɪm/ |v. |描绘；用鲜明色彩描绘；刻画|to depict or describe in vivid detail, often pictorially; to illuminate, as in a manuscript|The artist skillfully enlimned the bustling marketplace scene. 艺术家巧妙地描绘了熙熙攘攘的市场景象。|例句：The author enlimned the character with vivid descriptions.  作者用生动的描写刻画了这个人物。

例句：The manuscript was enlimned with gold leaf and bright pigments.  这份手稿用金箔和鲜艳的颜料装饰。|近义词：depict, portray, illustrate, illuminate, delineate |反义词：obfuscate, obscure|词性：动词


词源：From Middle English *enlymnen, from Anglo-Norman enluminer, enluminier (“to illuminate, to adorn with colors or designs”), from Old French enluminer, from en- + luminer (“to illuminate, to light up”), from Latin illuminare, from in- + lumen (“light”).
