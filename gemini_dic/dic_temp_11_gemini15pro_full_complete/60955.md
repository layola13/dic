elevenths:/ɪˈlevənθs/
n.
十一分之一
One of eleven equal parts of a whole.
Example: Cut the pie into elevenths. 把馅饼切成十一份。
Example: Eight elevenths of the class passed the test. 十一分之八的学生通过了考试。
Near Synonyms:one-eleventh
Antonyms:whole, entirety
