offender:/əˈfendər/ | n. | 罪犯，冒犯者 | A person who commits an illegal act or breaks the law.  |  The judge sentenced the repeat offender to a longer prison term. 法官判处惯犯更长的刑期。 |  Example:  Cybercriminals are offenders who operate online. 网络犯罪分子是在网上活动的罪犯。|Synonyms: culprit, criminal, lawbreaker, delinquent, transgressor | Antonyms: victim, law-abider | noun
