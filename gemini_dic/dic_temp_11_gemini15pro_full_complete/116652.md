mattering:/ˈmætərɪŋ/ | adj. | 重要的，有影响的 (通常用于 "that mattering" 结构) |  These are the that mattering details you need to focus on. 这些是你需要关注的重要细节。| 近义词: important, significant, crucial | 反义词: unimportant, insignificant, trivial | 词性: 形容词 (present participle)

词源:  From Middle English *materi, from Old French matere, from Latin materia (“timber, material, stuff; subject, topic, theme, argument, cause; occasion, opportunity”), from Proto-Italic *materā (“mother”), from Proto-Indo-European *méh₂tēr (“mother”).


注意事项:  "mattering" 作为名词，通常用单数形式，表示 "the quality or state of being important or significant". 作为形容词，它通常用于 "that mattering" 结构中，例如 "These are the that mattering details".
