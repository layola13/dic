inshrine:/ɪnˈʃraɪn/| vt.|珍藏；奉为神圣；铭记；|to enclose in or as if in a shrine|The document is enshrined in a glass case.  这份文件被珍藏在一个玻璃柜里。|enshrine the memory of a loved one  铭记挚爱的记忆|近义词：treasure, cherish, sanctify, consecrate|反义词: desecrate, profane|verb

inshrined:/ɪnˈʃraɪnd/| adj.|被珍藏的；被铭记的；|preserved or cherished as sacred|These rights are enshrined in the constitution. 这些权利被载入宪法。|His memory is enshrined in our hearts. 他的记忆被铭记在我们心中。|近义词：treasured, cherished, sacred, hallowed|反义词: desecrated, profaned|adjective
