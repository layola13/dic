majolica:/məˈdʒɒlɪkə/ /məˈjɑːlɪkə/| n. |彩色陶器，锡釉陶器|A type of Italian pottery glazed with a tin enamel and decorated with bright colors.|例句：The museum has a fine collection of majolica.  这家博物馆收藏了精美的彩色陶器。

例句：Her kitchen was adorned with colorful majolica plates. 她的厨房装饰着色彩鲜艳的锡釉陶盘。|近义词：faience, delftware |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of pottery) |词性:noun
