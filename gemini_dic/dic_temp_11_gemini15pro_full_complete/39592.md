consecration:/ˌkɒnsɪˈkreɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːnsəˈkreɪʃn/| n. |奉献，祝圣，神圣化|The act of dedicating something to a sacred purpose; the state of being so dedicated.|The consecration of the new cathedral was a grand ceremony. 新教堂的祝圣仪式非常隆重. |近义词：dedication, sanctification, hallowing|反义词：desecration, profanation|词性:名词

consecrate:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/ /ˈkɑːnsəˌkreɪt/| vt. |奉献，祝圣，使神圣|To dedicate something to a sacred purpose; to make holy.|The bishop consecrated the new church. 主教祝圣了新教堂。|近义词：dedicate, sanctify, hallow|反义词：desecrate, profane|词性:动词
