majestic:/məˈdʒɛstɪk/| adj.|雄伟的；庄严的；壮丽的|Having or showing impressive beauty or dignity.|The majestic mountains loomed over the valley. 雄伟的山脉俯瞰着山谷。|例句：The eagle soared majestically through the sky.  老鹰雄赳赳地翱翔在空中。

例句：The majestic cathedral inspired awe in all who visited. 壮丽的大教堂使所有参观者都感到敬畏。|近义词：grand, magnificent, stately, imposing, august|反义词：humble, insignificant, unimpressive|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle French *majestatif, from Latin maiestaticus (“of or pertaining to majesty”), from maiestas (“greatness, dignity, majesty”), ultimately from magnus (“great, large, big”).
