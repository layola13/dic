doubters:/ˈdaʊtərz/| n. |怀疑者，持怀疑态度的人|People who question or hold reservations about something or someone.  Those who lack belief or confidence.|Many doubters questioned the feasibility of the project. 许多怀疑者质疑该项目的可行性。|synonyms: skeptics, cynics, questioners|antonyms: believers, supporters, adherents|noun
