nontarnished:/ˌnɒnˈtɑːnɪʃt/ | adj. |未玷污的，未失去光泽的| Not having lost its shine or quality; unblemished. | The silver teapot remained nontarnished despite its age. 这把银茶壶尽管年代久远，但仍然光亮如新。| 近义词：untarnished, pristine, immaculate, spotless | 反义词：tarnished, stained, blemished | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + tarnished.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "tarnished" comes from the Old French "ternir," meaning "to make dull."
