ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hyren*, from Old English *hȳrian* (“to hire, pay wages to, reward”), from Proto-Germanic *hūrijaną* (“to hire”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱwer-* (“to buy”). Cognate with Old Frisian *huria* (“to hire”), Old Saxon *hūrian* (“to hire”), Old High German *hūren* (“to hire”), Old Norse *hýra* (“to hire”).
