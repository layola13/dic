branchiostoma: /bræŋkiˈɒstəmə/ | n. |文昌鱼|A small, translucent, fish-like marine animal that is a member of the subphylum Cephalochordata, also known as lancelets.  They are considered to be a primitive chordate and are studied for insights into the evolution of vertebrates. |Branchiostoma is an important organism in evolutionary studies. 文昌鱼在进化研究中是一个重要的生物。|  Branchiostoma belcheri is a common species found in Asian waters.  白氏文昌鱼是在亚洲水域发现的一个常见物种。 | synonyms: lancelet, amphioxus | antonyms: vertebrate (in a broad sense, as a contrasting group) | noun
