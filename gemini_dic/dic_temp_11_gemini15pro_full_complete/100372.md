interstratify: /ˌɪntərˈstrætɪfaɪ/ | v. | 交错层理，互层 | To arrange or be arranged in alternating layers or strata; to form or occur in interstratified deposits. | The rocks are interstratified with shale and sandstone.  这些岩石与页岩和砂岩交错成层。| synonym: interbed, intercalate | antonym: separate, segregate | verb

interstratify: /ˌɪntərˈstrætɪfaɪ/ | adj. | 交错层理的 | Arranged or existing in alternating layers or strata. | The cliff face revealed interstratified layers of volcanic ash and sedimentary rock.  悬崖表面露出了火山灰和沉积岩的交错层。| synonym: interbedded, intercalated | antonym: unstratified, homogeneous | adjective
