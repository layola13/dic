duckmeat:/ˈdʌkmiːt/| n.|浮萍，水萍|Small, free-floating aquatic plants, especially of the genus *Lemna*, often forming a green covering on still water.  These plants are commonly used as food for ducks and other waterfowl.|Ducks enjoy eating duckmeat in the pond. 鸭子喜欢吃池塘里的浮萍。|Synonyms: duckweed, water lentil|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps terrestrial plants?)|Noun
