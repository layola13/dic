jobless:/ˈdʒɒbləs/ (British), /ˈdʒɑːbləs/ (American)| adj.|失业的，无业的|Without a job; unemployed.|例句：Many people are jobless due to the economic downturn. 许多人由于经济衰退而失业。|例句：The jobless rate has increased significantly in recent months. 近几个月失业率显著上升。|近义词：unemployed, out of work, redundant|反义词：employed, working|词性:形容词
