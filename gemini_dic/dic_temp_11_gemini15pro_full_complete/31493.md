charmingest:/ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋɪst/
adjective
最迷人的，最有魅力的
Superlative form of charming: most charming.  Used less frequently than "most charming" in modern English, considered somewhat archaic or affected.
She had the charmingest smile I'd ever seen. 她拥有我见过的最迷人的笑容。
The prince found her to be the charmingest of all the princesses. 王子觉得她是所有公主中最有魅力的。
近义词: most charming, most delightful, most captivating, most attractive
反义词: least charming, most unpleasant, most repulsive, most unattractive
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *charmynge*, present participle of *charmen* (“to enchant, charm”), from Old French *charmer*, from Late Latin *carminare* (“to charm, enchant with song”), from Latin *carmen* (“song, verse, incantation, charm”).
