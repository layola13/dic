homopter:/ˈhɒmɒptər/ (US) /ˈhoʊmɑːptər/| n. |同翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Homoptera, such as an aphid, cicada, or leafhopper, typically having two pairs of similar wings.  They feed by sucking plant sap.| Aphids are common homopteran pests in gardens. 蚜虫是花园里常见的同翅目害虫。|近义词: hemipteran|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From the New Latin *Homoptera*, from Ancient Greek ὁμός (homós, “same, similar”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
