donates:/ˈdoʊneɪts/ (US) /dəʊˈneɪts/ (UK)| v. |捐赠，捐献|gives something (like money, goods, or time) to a person or organization, especially a charity.|He donates blood regularly. 他定期献血。|近义词：contributes, gives, bestows|反义词：receives, takes, withholds|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *dōnāre*, present active infinitive of *dōnō* (“I give, present, bestow”), from *dōnum* (“gift”).
