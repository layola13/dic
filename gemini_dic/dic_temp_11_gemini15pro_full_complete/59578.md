ebony: /ˈɛbəni/ | n. adj. |乌木；乌木制的；乌黑色的 |A hard, heavy, durable wood, most varieties of which are black.  Also used to describe something of a deep black color. |例句：The piano keys were made of ebony. 钢琴键是用乌木做的。

例句：Her ebony hair cascaded down her back. 她的乌黑秀发披散在背上。|近义词：black, dark, jet |反义词:white, light, pale |词性:名词, 形容词
词源: From Middle English *ebeny, from Old French ebenin, ebein, from Latin ebenus (“ebony; ebony tree”), from Ancient Greek ἔβενος (ébenos). Ultimately of Semitic origin. Compare Hebrew הָבְנִים‎ (hovenīm, “ebony wood”).
