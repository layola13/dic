argental:/ɑːrˈdʒɛntəl/
adjective
银色的，似银的
Of or relating to silver; silvery.
The argental sheen of the moon lit up the night sky. 银色的月光照亮了夜空。
近义词:silver, silvery, argent
反义词:golden, gold
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin argentum (“silver”) + -al.
