chevalier:/ˌʃɛvəˈlɪər/ | n. |骑士，武士；爵士|A knight; a gallant gentleman.  From Old French, meaning "horseman," ultimately from Late Latin caballarius. |例句：He was a true chevalier, always ready to defend the weak. 他是一位真正的骑士，时刻准备着保护弱者。

例句：The chevalier bowed low before the queen. 这位骑士在女王面前深深鞠了一躬。|近义词：knight, cavalier, gallant|反义词：villain, knave|词性：noun
