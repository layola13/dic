implicational:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃənəl/
adjective
蕴涵的；隐含的
Of, relating to, or characterized by implication.  Based on or having the nature of an implication.
This paper discusses the implicational hierarchy of politeness strategies. 这篇论文讨论了礼貌策略的蕴涵层级。
The study revealed an implicational relationship between word order and grammatical function. 这项研究揭示了词序和语法功能之间的蕴涵关系。
Synonyms:  implicit, inferred, suggested, tacit, understood
Antonyms: explicit, stated, overt, express
词性: 形容词
