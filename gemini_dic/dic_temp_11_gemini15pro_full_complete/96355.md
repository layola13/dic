indirected:/ˌɪndɪˈrɛktɪd/ | v. |间接引导，间接指向 (过去式/过去分词)|To guide or point indirectly. |例句：His words indirected me towards the solution. 他的话间接地引导我找到了解决方案。|近义词：guided, pointed, hinted|反义词: directed|verb
