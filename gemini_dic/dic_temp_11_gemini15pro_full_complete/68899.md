fastigiated:/fæˈstɪdʒieɪtɪd/
adjective
簇生的，紧密向上生长的
Having an erect, columnar, or spire-like form; tapering to a point.  Branches are nearly erect, parallel to the main stem, and clustered close together.
The fastigiated Lombardy poplar trees create a dramatic vertical accent in the landscape. 这些簇生的伦巴第杨树在景观中营造出引人注目的垂直效果。
The fastigiated branches of the Irish yew reach towards the sky.  爱尔兰紫杉的簇生枝条伸向天空。
synonym: columnar, erect, upright, spire-like
antonym: spreading, horizontal, weeping, prostrate
词性: adjective
