nationalizations:/ˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |国有化|The act of a government taking control of a privately-owned business or industry.|例句：The nationalizations of the 1970s caused a lot of controversy. 20世纪70年代的国有化引起了很多争议。

例句：Many industries faced nationalizations after the war. 战后，许多产业面临国有化。|近义词：state ownership, expropriation|反义词：privatization, denationalization|词性: 名词 (Noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From nationalize + -ation.  Nationalize comes from national + -ize. National from nation (from Old French nacion, from Latin natio "birth, race, nation," from natus, past participle of nasci "to be born").  The suffix -ize forms verbs meaning "to make or render," and -ation forms nouns denoting action or process.
