detacher:/dɪˈtætʃər/ | n. | 分离器，拆卸器 | A device or tool used for detaching something. | The surgeon used a special detacher to separate the fused bones.  外科医生用一种特殊的拆卸器来分离融合的骨骼。| synonyms: separator, disconnector, uncoupler | antonyms: attacher, connector, coupler | noun

detacher:/dɪˈtætʃər/ | n. | 分离者，派遣者 | A person who detaches something or someone. | The military detacher arranged for the troops to return home.  军事派遣者安排部队返回家园。| synonyms: dispatcher, sender, emissary | antonyms: attacher, receiver | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *détacher*, from *dé-* (prefix indicating separation) + *attacher* (to attach). Ultimately from Latin *ad* (to) + *captare* (to seize).
