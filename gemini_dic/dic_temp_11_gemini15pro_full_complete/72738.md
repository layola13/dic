foetus:/ˈfiːtəs/ (US) /ˈfiːtəs/ (UK)| n. |胎儿|An unborn offspring of a mammal, especially a human, from the eighth week after conception until birth.  A developing human in the uterus. |例句：The doctor examined the foetus using ultrasound. 医生用超声波检查了胎儿。

例句：The foetus is developing normally. 胎儿发育正常。|近义词：embryo, unborn baby, unborn child|反义词:newborn, infant|词性:noun
