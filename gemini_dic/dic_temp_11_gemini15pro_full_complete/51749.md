devolve:/dɪˈvɒlv/ /dɪˈvɑːlv/
verb
移交，转移（权力、责任等）；退化，衰落
to pass (responsibility, rights, or power) to a lower level; to gradually go back to an earlier or less developed state.
The central government devolved some of its powers to local authorities. 中央政府将其部分权力下放给地方当局。
Over time, the Roman Empire devolved into a collection of smaller states. 随着时间的推移，罗马帝国逐渐分裂成许多较小的国家。
synonym: delegate, transfer, deteriorate, decline
antonym: centralize, evolve, improve, develop
词性: 动词
