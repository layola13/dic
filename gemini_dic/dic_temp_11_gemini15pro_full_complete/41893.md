cosignatory:/ˌkoʊˈsɪɡnəˌtɔːri/ /ˌkəʊˈsɪɡnətəri/| n.|共同签署人，连署人|A person who signs a document along with another person or other people, especially a treaty or other formal agreement.|The President and the Prime Minister were cosignatories on the peace treaty. 总统和首相是和平条约的共同签署人。

The cosignatories of the letter included several prominent scientists. 这封信的共同签署者包括几位杰出的科学家。|Synonym: joint signatory, cosigner|Antonym: N/A|Noun
