noncorruptive:/ˌnɒnkəˈrʌptɪv/
adjective
不易腐败的; 不受腐蚀的
Not susceptible to corruption or decay; incorruptible.
The new mayor promised to establish a noncorruptive government. 新市长承诺要建立一个廉洁的政府。

The organization strives to maintain a noncorruptive environment. 该组织努力维持一个廉洁的环境。

Synonym: incorruptible, honest, upright, unbribable
Antonym: corruptible, dishonest, venal, bribable
词性: 形容词
