diadelphian:/ˌdaɪəˈdɛlfiən/
adj.
二体雄蕊的
Having stamens united into two groups or bundles by their filaments, with one stamen often free, as in many members of the pea family.
The diadelphian structure of the flower is characteristic of the Fabaceae family.  这种花的二体雄蕊结构是豆科植物的特征。
The sweet pea has diadelphian stamens. 香豌豆有二体雄蕊。
近义词:monadelphous (单体雄蕊的)
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑polyandrous - 多雄蕊的，但并非完全相反)
词性:adjective

From Greek *di- "two" + *adelphos "brother" + -ian.  源自希腊语 *di- “二” + *adelphos “兄弟” + -ian。
