dixies:/ˈdɪksiz/
n.
南方邦联, 南方各州, 南方歌曲
(often initial capital letter)Dixie, Dixie Land, Dixieland  the southern states of the U.S., especially those that formed the Confederate States of America.
The band played a medley of old Dixieland tunes.  这支乐队演奏了一组古老的南方歌曲。
synonyms: the South, the Confederacy
antonyms: the North, the Union
noun

dixies:/ˈdɪksiz/
n.
(dixie)小锅, 野营小锅
a small metal pot or kettle used for cooking over an open fire, especially when camping.
We heated some water in the dixie for tea. 我们在小锅里烧了些热水泡茶.
synonyms: pot, kettle, camp kettle
antonyms: N/A
noun
