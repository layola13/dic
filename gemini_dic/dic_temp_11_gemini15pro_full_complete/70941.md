firedrake:/ˈfaɪərdreɪk/ (US), /ˈfaɪərdreɪk/ (UK)| n.|火龙；火蛇；鬼火|A mythical fire-breathing dragon, especially one inhabiting marshy areas.  Also, a fiery meteor or an ignis fatuus (will-o'-the-wisp).|例句：Legends told of a fearsome firedrake guarding the treasure. 传说有一条可怕的火龙守护着宝藏。

例句：The firedrake soared through the night sky, leaving a trail of sparks. 火龙飞过夜空，留下一串火花。|近义词：dragon, wyvern, drake, fire-dragon|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "water serpent" as an elemental opposite)|词性:noun
