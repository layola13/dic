gutterlike:/ˈɡʌtərlaɪk/
adjective
像排水沟的; 类似沟槽的
Resembling a gutter, especially in being narrow, deep, and/or dirty.
The alley was narrow and gutterlike.  这条小巷狭窄得像排水沟。
The trench was deep and gutterlike.  这条沟很深，像排水沟一样。
近义词:ditchlike, trenchlike, channel-like
反义词:wide, spacious, open
