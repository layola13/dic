consoles:/ˈkɒnsəʊlz/ (UK) /kənˈsoʊlz/ (US)| n. v.|控制台；安慰；抚慰|A console is a cabinet for electronic equipment, or the electronic equipment itself. As a verb, to console means to comfort someone who is sad or disappointed.|
The new game console has amazing graphics. 新的游戏机画面令人惊叹。

He tried to console her after her loss. 他试图在她失去亲人后安慰她。

近义词：(n.) panel, dashboard, control panel; (v.) comfort, soothe, solace
反义词：(v.) distress, upset, grieve
词性: noun, verb
