mythicalnesses: /ˈmɪθɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 神话性；神话色彩 |  The state or quality of being mythical; the extent to which something is mythical.  It refers to the collection or aggregation of mythical qualities or characteristics. This word is not commonly used and might sound awkward.  It's better to use phrases like "mythical qualities" or "degree of mythicalness." | The mythicalnesses of ancient Greek stories are captivating. 古希腊故事的神话性令人着迷。 | 近义词: mythicality (less common), mythology (related but refers to the body of myths) | 反义词: reality, factuality | 词性: noun
