ETYMOLOGY->
From muscle + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives from nouns, meaning "having" or "characterized by."  The word "muscle" itself comes from the Latin "musculus," meaning "little mouse," because the shape of some muscles resembled mice.
