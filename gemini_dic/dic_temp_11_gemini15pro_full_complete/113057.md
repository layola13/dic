lumpiest:/ˈlʌmpiɪst/| adj.|最块状的，最笨重的|Most lumpy; having the most lumps.  Characterized by the greatest number or size of lumps.|The lumpiest mashed potatoes I've ever seen were served at that restaurant. 我在那个餐馆吃到了我见过最块状的土豆泥。|The batter was too thick and lumpiest, making it difficult to pour. 面糊太稠太块状了，很难倒出来。|近义词：bulkiest, heaviest, clumsiest|反义词: smoothest, flattest|词性:形容词

词源:
From lump + -y + -est.  "Lump" comes from Middle English *lumpe, of uncertain origin.
