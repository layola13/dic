hibernates:/ˈhaɪbərneɪts/| v. |冬眠，蛰伏|spends the winter in a dormant state|例句：The bear hibernates during the cold winter months. 熊在寒冷的冬季冬眠。

例句：Many insects hibernate to survive harsh weather conditions. 许多昆虫冬眠以度过恶劣的天气条件。|近义词：sleep, dormancy, overwinter|反义词:awaken, revive|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin hibernāre, from hibernum "winter."
