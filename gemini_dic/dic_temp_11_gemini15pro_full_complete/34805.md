climbing: /ˈklaɪmɪŋ/ | n. adj. | 攀登，攀爬；上升；（价格、温度等）上涨 | The activity of going up something, such as a mountain, using your feet and hands. Can also refer to the act of something increasing. |  He enjoys mountain climbing. 他喜欢爬山。| synonyms: ascending, scaling, mounting | antonyms: descending, lowering, falling | noun

climbing: /ˈklaɪmɪŋ/ | adj. | 攀缘的，攀登的；上升的 | Used to describe plants that grow upwards by clinging to walls or other supports, or to describe something that is increasing. | Climbing roses covered the wall. 攀缘玫瑰覆盖了墙壁。| synonyms: ascending, upward | antonyms: descending, downward | adjective
