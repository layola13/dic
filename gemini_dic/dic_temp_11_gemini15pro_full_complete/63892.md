epinastic:/ˌɛpɪˈnæstɪk/| adj.|向下弯曲的，偏上性的|Relating to or characterized by epinasty, the downward curvature of leaves or other plant parts, often caused by faster growth of the upper surface.|The epinastic leaves of the plant suggested it was suffering from waterlogging.  该植物向下弯曲的叶子表明它正遭受涝害。|近义词：downcurved, drooping|反义词:hyponastic|adjective
