gita:/ˈɡiːtɑː/| n.|《薄伽梵歌》|A Hindu scripture considered to be one of the most important texts in Hinduism.  It is part of the epic Mahabharata and presents a dialogue between Prince Arjuna and his charioteer and guide, Lord Krishna.|I read the Gita every morning for inspiration. 我每天早上读《薄伽梵歌》以获得灵感。|近义词:scripture, text|反义词:None|名词
