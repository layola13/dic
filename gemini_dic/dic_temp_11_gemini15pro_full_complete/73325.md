foredeem:/fɔːrˈdiːm/
verb
预先赎回；提前偿还
to redeem (something) in advance of the due date, especially of a mortgage or other loan.
He foredeemed the mortgage on his house. 他提前偿还了房屋抵押贷款。
Synonyms: prepay, redeem early, pay off early
Antonyms: default, fail to pay
词性: 动词
