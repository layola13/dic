dummying:/ˈdʌmiɪŋ/| v.|做假人；装傻；简化；用假人代替|The company was accused of dummying up the test results.  这家公司被指控伪造测试结果。|近义词: faking, simulating, simplifying|反义词: perfecting, elaborating|动词

词源:  From the noun "dummy" (meaning a mannequin or a stupid person) + "-ing" suffix to form a present participle/gerund.  Ultimately derived from the Dutch word "dom" meaning "stupid" or "mute."
