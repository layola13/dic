fabricative:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
捏造的，虚构的
Relating to, or characterized by fabrication.
The journalist was fired for publishing a fabricative story. 记者因发表一篇捏造的故事而被解雇。

He presented fabricative evidence in court. 他在法庭上出示了伪造的证据。

近义词:fabricated, invented, fictional, spurious, untrue, false
反义词:factual, true, genuine, real
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fabricativus, from Latin fabricatus (past participle of fabricari “to build, construct, fashion, manufacture; forge, invent”), from fabrica (“workshop; construction, fabric”), from faber (“craftsman, worker in hard materials”), of uncertain origin.
