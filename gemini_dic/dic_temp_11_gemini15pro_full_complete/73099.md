fopped:/fɑpt/ | v. | 炫耀，卖弄 | to behave in a way intended to attract attention and admiration, often in a way that is not sincere | He fopped about in his new suit, eager to impress everyone. 他穿着新西装炫耀，渴望给每个人留下深刻印象。 |  She fopped her expensive handbag around. 她炫耀着她昂贵的包包。 | 近义词：show off, flaunt, parade | 反义词：hide, conceal | 词性: verb

词源:  From the obsolete noun *fop* (a man who is too interested in his clothes and appearance), which comes from the Middle English *foppe*, of uncertain origin.
