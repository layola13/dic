bioplastic:/ˌbaɪəʊˈplæstɪk/ /ˌbaɪoʊˈplæstɪk/| n.|生物塑料|A type of plastic made from renewable biomass sources, such as vegetable fats and oils, corn starch, or microbiota. |Example 1:  The company is developing new bioplastics made from seaweed.  这家公司正在开发由海藻制成的新型生物塑料。

Example 2: Bioplastics are seen as a more sustainable alternative to traditional plastics. 生物塑料被视为比传统塑料更可持续的替代品。|近义词：biopolymer, eco-plastic|反义词: conventional plastic, petroleum-based plastic|词性:noun


biodegradable:/ˌbaɪəʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/ /ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/| adj.|可生物降解的|Capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms. |Example 1:  Biodegradable packaging helps reduce landfill waste. 可生物降解的包装有助于减少垃圾填埋场的浪费。

Example 2:  Many bioplastics are biodegradable, which is a major advantage. 许多生物塑料是可生物降解的，这是一个主要优势。|近义词：compostable, decomposable|反义词: non-biodegradable|词性:adjective
