hardcoded:/ˌhɑːrdˈkoʊdɪd/ | adj. | 硬编码的 | Embedded directly in the source code, rather than being obtained from external sources or calculated at runtime. |  The username and password are hardcoded into the application, which is a security risk.  用户名和密码硬编码在应用程序中，这是一个安全风险。 |  fixed, embedded, built-in | configurable, dynamic, flexible | adjective
hard-code:/ˌhɑːrdˈkoʊd/ | vt. | 硬编码 | To embed data or functionality directly into the source code, rather than obtaining it from external sources or calculating it at runtime. |  The developers decided to hard-code the server address to simplify the initial setup. 开发人员决定硬编码服务器地址以简化初始设置。 |  fix, embed, build in | configure, parameterize, externalize | verb
