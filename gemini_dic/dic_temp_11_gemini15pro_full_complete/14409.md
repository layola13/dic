autotoxemia:/ˌɔːtəʊtɒkˈsiːmiə/
noun
自体中毒
Poisoning by toxins produced within the body.
Autotoxemia can cause a variety of symptoms, including fatigue and headaches.
近义词: toxemia, blood poisoning
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "health," "wellness")
词性: noun
