nonpoet:/ˈnɒnˌpəʊɪt/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌpoʊət/| n.|非诗人；没有诗意的人|A person who is not a poet; someone lacking poetic sensibility or imagination. |He considered himself a nonpoet, more comfortable with prose than verse. 他认为自己不是诗人，写散文比写诗歌更自在。|Synonyms:prosaist, layman, non-artist|Antonyms:poet, versifier, artist|noun

nonpoet:/ˈnɒnˌpəʊɪt/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌpoʊət/| adj.|非诗人的；没有诗意的|Not characteristic of a poet; lacking poetic qualities. |His nonpoet friend found the metaphors confusing.  他没有诗意的朋友觉得这些比喻令人费解。|Synonyms:prosaic, unpoetic, unimaginative|Antonyms:poetic, lyrical, imaginative|adjective
