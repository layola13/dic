factitious:/fækˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
人为的；虚假的；不自然的
Artificially created or developed, not natural or genuine; contrived.
The demand for the product is entirely factitious, created by advertising.  这种产品的需求完全是人为制造的，是由广告创造出来的。
The painting was found to be a factitious Rembrandt, cleverly aged to resemble the real thing. 这幅画被发现是一幅伪造的伦勃朗作品，巧妙地做旧以模仿真迹。
近义词:artificial, contrived, bogus, sham, spurious, feigned
反义词:genuine, natural, authentic, real
词性:adjective

词源:From Latin facticius "made by art," from factus, past participle of facere "to make, do."
