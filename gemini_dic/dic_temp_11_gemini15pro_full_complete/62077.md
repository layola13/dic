encephalitic:/ɛnˌsɛfəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
脑炎的; 患脑炎的
Relating to or affected with encephalitis.
The encephalitic child was hospitalized immediately. 这个患脑炎的孩子立即被送往医院。
The virus can cause encephalitic complications. 这种病毒会导致脑炎并发症。
近义词:inflamed, infected
反义词:healthy, normal
词性:adjective

词源:
From encephalitis + -ic. Encephalitis comes from the Greek enképhalos (brain) + -itis (inflammation).
