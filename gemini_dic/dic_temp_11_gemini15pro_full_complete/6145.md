amicability:/ˌæmɪkəˈbɪləti/ | n. |友好, 和睦, 亲善|The state of being amicable; friendliness.|例句：The amicability between the two countries led to a peaceful resolution. 两国之间的友好关系促成了和平解决。

例句：The meeting ended with a surprising degree of amicability. 会议以令人惊讶的和睦程度结束。|近义词：friendliness, cordiality, amity, goodwill|反义词：hostility, animosity, enmity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *amicabilis* "friendly," from Latin *amicus* "friend" (from *amare* "to love") + *-abilis* "-able."
