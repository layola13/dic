overhandicap:/ˌoʊvərˈhændɪkæp/| vt.|过分让步；给予过高的让步|To give someone too much of an advantage in a competition or game, typically a golf handicap.|He overhandicapped his opponent in the golf match, giving him a much larger advantage than he deserved. 他在高尔夫比赛中对他的对手过分让步，给了他比他应得的更大的优势。|近义词：give too much of an advantage, overcompensate|反义词:underhandicap|v.

overhandicap:/ˌoʊvərˈhændɪkæp/| n.|过高的让步|A handicap that is too generous or advantageous.|The overhandicap he received allowed him to win the tournament easily.  他获得的过高让步使他轻易赢得了比赛。|近义词：excessive advantage, undue allowance|反义词: insufficient handicap|n.
