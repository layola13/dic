auditability:/ˌɔːdɪtəˈbɪləti/| n.|可审计性|The quality of being able to be audited.  The extent to which something can be checked or verified.|例句：The auditability of the company's financial records is crucial for investor confidence. 该公司财务记录的可审计性对于投资者信心至关重要。

例句：The system was designed with auditability in mind, ensuring that all transactions could be traced. 该系统的设计考虑到了可审计性，确保所有交易都可以追溯。|近义词：traceability, verifiability, accountability|反义词: obscurity, opacity|词性:noun
