construed:/kənˈstruːd/ | v. |解释，理解，领会|To interpret or understand the meaning of something, typically in a particular way.|例句：His silence was construed as a sign of guilt. 他的沉默被理解为承认有罪。|例句：The contract was construed in favor of the landlord.  这份合同被解释为有利于房东。|近义词：interpret, understand, perceive, take, see|反义词：misinterpret, misunderstand|词性:动词

词源:
From Latin *construere* "to pile up together, construct, arrange, build," from *com-* "together" + *struere* "to pile, build."  The meaning shifted from the literal sense of building to the figurative sense of building an interpretation.
