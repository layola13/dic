odelet:/ˈoʊdəlɛt/ | n. | 删除；删节；略去 | A deletion or omission, especially of a passage in a text. | The editor made several odelets to the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前对稿件做了几次删节。 |  近义词: deletion, omission, excision, elision | 反义词: addition, insertion, inclusion | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *odelette*, diminutive of *ode*, from Latin *oda*, from Greek *ōidḗ* (“song, ode”).  The sense development from "song" to "deletion" is unclear and likely influenced by words like "delete".
