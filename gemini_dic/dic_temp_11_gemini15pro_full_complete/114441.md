makeover's:/ˈmeɪkoʊvərz/
noun
改造，翻新，改头换面
A makeover is a complete transformation or renovation of someone's appearance, a place, or a thing.
She gave her bedroom a complete makeover. 她彻底改造了她的卧室。
renewal, renovation, revamp
decline, deterioration
