overpopularities:/ˌoʊvərˌpɑːpjʊˈlærətiz/
|n.|过度流行；过度的普及|The overpopularities of certain destinations can lead to overcrowding and environmental damage. 某些目的地的过度流行会导致过度拥挤和环境破坏。|
|英文解析:  The state of being excessively popular.  Formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning excessive) to "popularity," and then pluralizing it.|
|例句：The overpopularities of some social media trends can be fleeting. 一些社交媒体趋势的过度流行可能是短暂的。|
|例句：The overpopularities of fad diets often lead to health problems.  流行减肥法的过度流行经常导致健康问题。|
|近义词：fads, crazes, trends|
|反义词： unpopularity, obscurity|
|词性: Noun|
