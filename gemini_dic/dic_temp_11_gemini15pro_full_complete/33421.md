churchwardenism:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdənɪzəm/ | n. |教区执事式的管理; 保守的管理; |The excessive interference in minor details, often attributed to churchwardens.  A petty, interfering, and officious management of affairs, especially in a parish. |The villagers complained of the vicar's churchwardenism, as he dictated everything from the color of the altar cloths to the hymns sung. 村民们抱怨牧师的教区执事式管理，因为他对从祭坛布的颜色到唱的赞美诗都指手画脚。| The committee's churchwardenism stifled creativity and led to many talented members resigning. 委员会保守的管理扼杀了创造力，导致许多有才华的成员辞职。 |Near synonyms:  pettiness, officiousness, interference, meddling| Antonyms:  laissez-faire, non-interference, liberalism | noun
