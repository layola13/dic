full-term:/ˌfʊl ˈtɜːrm/ /ˌfʊl ˈtɝːm/| adj.|足月的，妊娠足月的|Reaching the end of the normal gestation period.|例句：She gave birth to a healthy full-term baby. 她生了一个健康的足月婴儿。|例句：The full-term pregnancy lasts about 40 weeks.  足月妊娠持续约40周。|近义词：mature, complete|反义词：premature, preterm|词性:形容词
词源:  From "full" + "term," meaning the complete or expected duration of a pregnancy.
