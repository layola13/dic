nonrespectable:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈspɛktəbl/ | adj. | 不受尊敬的，不体面的，名声不好的 | Not worthy of respect; disreputable. |  He was involved in some nonrespectable activities that tarnished his reputation. 他参与了一些不光彩的活动，损害了他的名誉。

|  Her nonrespectable behavior led to her dismissal from the company. 她不体面的行为导致她被公司解雇。 | 近义词：disreputable, shady, dishonorable, unseemly, unbecoming | 反义词：respectable, honorable, reputable, esteemed | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + respectable (adjective)  来源于拉丁语respectus,意思是“回头看，注意”。
