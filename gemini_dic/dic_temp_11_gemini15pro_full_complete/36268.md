cohabitant:/koʊˈhæbɪtənt/ | n. |同居者，同栖者|A person who lives with another, especially in a sexual relationship, without being married. |例句：The cohabitants decided to split the rent equally. 同居者决定平分房租。

|例句：Many young couples choose to be cohabitants before marriage. 很多年轻情侣选择在结婚前同居。

|近义词：roommate, housemate, partner, live-in partner |反义词：spouse, husband, wife|词性：名词

cohabit:/koʊˈhæbɪt/ | v. |同居，同栖|To live together in the same dwelling, especially as unmarried sexual partners. |例句：They cohabited for five years before getting married. 他们在结婚前同居了五年。

|例句：The study examined the financial implications of cohabiting. 这项研究调查了同居的经济影响。

|近义词：live together, shack up |反义词：live apart, separate|词性：动词
