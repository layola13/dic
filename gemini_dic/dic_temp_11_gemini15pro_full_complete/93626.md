imbizo:/ɪmˈbiːzoʊ/
noun
南非部落会议；部落集会
A Zulu word referring to a gathering called by a traditional leader or chief to discuss important matters with members of the community.
The chief called an imbizo to discuss the drought.  酋长召集了一次部落会议来讨论旱灾。
The imbizo lasted for several days, with much discussion and debate.  部落会议持续了几天，进行了许多讨论和辩论。
synonym: gathering, meeting, assembly, council
antonym: dispersal, scattering
n.
