erinite:/ˈɛrɪnaɪt/| n.|翠砷铜矿|A bright green basic copper arsenate mineral, Cu₃(AsO₄)₂·2Cu(OH)₂.  It is a secondary mineral found in the oxidized zone of copper deposits.|The museum displayed a beautiful sample of erinite.|Synonyms: emerald copper, olivenite (similar appearance but different composition)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
