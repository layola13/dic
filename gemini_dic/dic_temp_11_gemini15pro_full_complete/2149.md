adcock:/ˈædkɒk/| n.|艾德考克天线（一种测向天线）|A directional antenna system used in radio direction finding, consisting of two vertical aerials spaced less than half a wavelength apart.|The adcock antenna is highly effective in minimizing the effects of ground waves. 艾德考克天线能有效地减少地波的影响。|近义词:directional antenna, loop antenna|反义词:omnidirectional antenna|noun
