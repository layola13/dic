bowel:/ˈbaʊəl/ (US), /ˈbaʊəl/ (UK)| n.|肠；内脏；深处；同情心|The inner part of something, usually the intestines.  It can also refer to the deepest part of something, or feelings of compassion.|He had surgery on his bowel. 他做了肠道手术。|synonyms: intestines, guts, innards, entrails, viscera; depths, core, heart; compassion, pity, mercy|antonyms: exterior, surface; apathy, indifference|noun

bowel:/ˈbaʊəl/ (US), /ˈbaʊəl/ (UK)| n. (often bowels)|同情；怜悯|Feelings of compassion or pity.|His bowels of compassion were moved by the plight of the refugees.  他被难民的困境触动了恻隐之心。|synonyms: compassion, pity, mercy, sympathy|antonyms: apathy, indifference, cruelty|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
