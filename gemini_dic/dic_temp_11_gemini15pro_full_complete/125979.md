musketade:/ˌmʌskəˈteɪd/| n. |滑膛枪，火枪兵|A smoothbore long gun used by infantrymen, typically in the 17th and 18th centuries.  A soldier armed with such a gun. |例句：The musketeers formed a line and fired their muskets. 火枪兵排成一列，开火射击。

例句：The museum displayed a collection of antique muskets and musketades. 博物馆展出了一批古董滑膛枪和火枪。|近义词：musket, firearm, rifle (in later periods)|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly peace weapon, bow and arrow)|词性:noun
