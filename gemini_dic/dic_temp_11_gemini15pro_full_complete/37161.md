come-to-your-senses:/kʌm tə jər ˈsɛnsɪz/
verb phrase
清醒过来；恢复理智；回过神来
To begin thinking clearly and rationally again after a period of confusion or irrationality.
He finally came to his senses and realized he was making a huge mistake. 他终于清醒过来，意识到自己犯了一个巨大的错误。

The shock of the accident made her come to her senses. 事故的冲击让她回过神来。

近义词: regain consciousness, become aware, sober up, wise up
反义词: lose one's mind, go crazy, become irrational
词性: verb phrase
