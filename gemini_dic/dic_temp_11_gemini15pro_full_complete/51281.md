desynonymize:/diːsɪˈnɒnɪmaɪz/
verb
去同义化；消除同义词
To replace synonyms with a single preferred term.
The editor desynonymized the document to ensure consistent terminology. 编辑对文档进行了去同义化处理，以确保术语一致。
近义词: standardize, normalize, unify
反义词: synonymize (though less common)
词性:v.

Etymology:
"de-" (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "synonym" + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs)
