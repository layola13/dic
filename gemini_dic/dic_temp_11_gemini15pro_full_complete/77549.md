gdns:/ˈdʒiːˌdiːˌɛnˈɛs/| n. |谷歌公共域名系统|Google Public Domain Name System, a free and secure DNS service offered by Google. It resolves domain names into IP addresses, allowing users to access websites and other online resources.|Example: I switched to using Google Public DNS (GDNS) for faster internet browsing. 我为了更快的互联网浏览速度切换到使用谷歌公共域名系统 (GDNS)。|Example: GDNS helps improve online security by blocking known phishing and malware sites.  GDNS 通过阻止已知的钓鱼和恶意软件网站来帮助提高在线安全性。|近义词:DNS, Public DNS|反义词: (No direct antonym)|abbr.
