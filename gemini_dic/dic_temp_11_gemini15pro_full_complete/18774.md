bernard:/ˈbɜːrnərd/| n. |伯纳德; 圣伯纳德犬|A masculine given name, a French form of Bernhard, from Germanic elements *ber "bear" and *hard "hardy, brave." Also, a breed of large dog, originally bred to rescue travelers lost in the Swiss Alps.|My friend Bernard is a doctor. 我的朋友伯纳德是一名医生.|Bernard rescued a child from the burning building. 伯纳德从燃烧的建筑物中救出一个孩子.|Synonyms: (for the name) none specific, many other male names; (for the dog) Saint Bernard, alpine dog|Antonyms: N/A|noun
