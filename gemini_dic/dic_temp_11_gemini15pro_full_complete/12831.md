associate: /əˈsoʊʃiət/ (US), /əˈsəʊʃiət/ (UK) | n. | 同事，伙伴；合伙人；准会员 | A person united with another or others in an act, enterprise, or business; a companion, partner, or colleague.  |  例句：He is a business associate of mine. 他是我的生意伙伴。 | 近义词：colleague, partner, companion, friend | 反义词：enemy, opponent, rival | 词性: noun

associate: /əˈsoʊʃiˌeɪt/ (US), /əˈsəʊʃiˌeɪt/ (UK) | v. | 联系；联想；交往 | To connect or join together; combine. To connect in the mind or imagination. To be together as friends, companions, or business partners. | 例句：I associate summer with holidays. 我把夏天和假期联系起来。 | 近义词：connect, link, relate, combine | 反义词：separate, disconnect, disassociate | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *associatus, past participle of associare "to join with," from Latin ad- "to" + sociare "to join, unite," from socius "companion, ally."
