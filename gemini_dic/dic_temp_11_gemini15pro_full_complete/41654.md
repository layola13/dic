correlate:/ˈkɔːrəleɪt/ (US) /ˈkɒrəleɪt/ (UK)| v.  n.|使…相互关联；相关联；相关物；相关联的人|To have a mutual relationship or connection, in which one thing affects or depends on another.|Stress levels and heart disease are strongly correlated. 压力水平和心脏病密切相关。|近义词：relate, connect, correspond, link|反义词:differ, diverge|verb

correlate:/ˈkɔːrəleɪt/ (US) /ˈkɒrəleɪt/ (UK)| n.|相关物；相关联的人|Something that is correlated with something else.|The study found no clear correlate between obesity and intelligence. 这项研究没有发现肥胖和智力之间有明显的相关性。|近义词：relation, counterpart, parallel|反义词:opposite, contrast|noun
