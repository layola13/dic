heave-to:/hiːv ˈtuː/
verb
停下（尤指船隻）
To bring a ship to a standstill with the head turned slightly toward the wind.
The captain ordered the crew to heave-to. 船长命令船员停下船。
The ship hove-to in the storm. 船在暴风雨中停了下来。
近义词: stop, halt
反义词: proceed, continue
词性: verb (intransitive/transitive)

heave-to:/hiːv ˈtuː/
noun
停船
The act of bringing a ship to a standstill.
The heave-to allowed the crew to make repairs. 停船让船员得以进行修理。
The sudden heave-to surprised the passengers. 突然停船让乘客们感到惊讶。
近义词: stop, halt
反义词: proceeding, continuation
词性: noun


词源:  "Heave" comes from Old English *hebban "to lift, raise," related to Old High German heffen, Gothic hafjan. "To" in this context indicates direction or purpose.
