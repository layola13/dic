fibbers:/ˈfɪbərz/
noun
说谎者，惯于说谎的人
People who tell lies, especially habitually.
The child was one of the biggest fibbers I'd ever met, constantly making up stories.  这个孩子是我见过的最爱说谎的人之一，总是编故事。
He's such a fibber; you can't believe a word he says. 他真是个骗子；你不能相信他说的任何一个字。
synonyms: liars, prevaricators, fabricators
antonyms: truth-tellers, honest people
n.
