excites:/ɪkˈsaɪts/| vt.|使兴奋，使激动|to cause feelings of enthusiasm and eagerness in (someone)|The prospect of traveling excites me. 旅行的前景让我兴奋。|近义词：thrills, stimulates, arouses|反义词：bores, calms, soothes|动词

exciting:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ/ | adj.|令人兴奋的，刺激的|causing feelings of enthusiasm and eagerness|The roller coaster was an exciting ride. 过山车是一项刺激的游乐设施。|近义词：thrilling, stimulating, interesting|反义词：boring, dull, tedious|形容词

excited:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj.|兴奋的，激动的|feeling or showing enthusiasm and eagerness|I am excited about the upcoming concert. 我对即将到来的音乐会感到兴奋。|近义词：thrilled, enthusiastic|反义词：bored, apathetic|形容词

词源: From Latin excitare "to rouse, stir up," frequentative of exciere "to call forth, rouse," from ex- "out" + ciere "to set in motion."
