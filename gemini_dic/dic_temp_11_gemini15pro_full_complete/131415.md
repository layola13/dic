nonclerically:/nɒnˈklɛrɪkəli/
| adv. |非神职人员地，世俗地|Not in a clerical or priestly manner; as a layperson. |He lived nonclerically, though he retained his religious beliefs. 他过着世俗生活，尽管他保留着自己的宗教信仰。|  He dressed nonclerically in jeans and a t-shirt. 他穿着牛仔裤和T恤，不像个神职人员。|近义词：secularly, laically|反义词：clerically, ecclesiastically|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
clerical (adjective): relating to clergy or their work
-ly (suffix): adverbial suffix
