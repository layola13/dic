USAGE->

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 他们明天会在会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have finished by the time we arrive? = 我们到达时她将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

Interrogative Sentences:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。

what for = 为了什么？
no matter what = 无论如何

what about = ...怎么样？
