noncareer:/ˌnɑːnkəˈrɪr/
adjective
非职业的，非事业的
Not related to a career or profession; not intended as a long-term or professional pursuit.
She took a noncareer job during the summer.  她在夏天做了一份非职业性的工作。
He pursued noncareer interests like painting and hiking. 他追求非职业的兴趣，比如绘画和徒步旅行。
近义词:  avocational, unprofessional
反义词:  career, professional
词性: adjective


noncareer:/ˌnɑːnkəˈrɪr/
noun
非职业生涯
A path or period of time not focused on a specific career or profession.
She chose a noncareer to focus on family. 她选择了一种非职业生涯，专注于家庭。
Many people find fulfillment in a noncareer. 很多人在非职业生涯中找到了满足感。
近义词:  avocation, non-profession
反义词:  career, profession
词性: noun


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "career"
