half-confessed:/ˌhæf kənˈfest/| adj.|半坦白的，半招认的|Partially admitted or acknowledged, often implying reluctance or incompleteness.  The truth is often somewhere between fully concealed and openly declared, residing in the realm of the half-confessed.|His half-confessed love for her was evident in his hesitant glances. 他对她半遮半掩的爱意从他犹豫的眼神中显露出来。

She gave a half-confessed smile, acknowledging her part in the prank. 她露出一丝半招认的微笑，承认自己参与了恶作剧。|Near synonyms: partially admitted, implicitly acknowledged, hinted at, intimated|Near antonyms: fully confessed, openly admitted, completely denied|adjective
