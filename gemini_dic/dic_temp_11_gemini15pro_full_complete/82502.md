gryde:/ɡraɪd/ | n. | 大锅，煎锅 (古英语/中古英语) | A large pot or cauldron used for cooking, especially frying or boiling.  Derived from Middle English "gryde," from Old English "grīd," related to Old Norse "grǫd." |  They used a large gryde to cook the stew. 他们用一个大锅来炖菜。 | Synonyms: cauldron, pot, pan | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a cooking vessel) | noun
