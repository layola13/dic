overmournfully: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnfəli/ | adv. | 过度悲伤地，悲哀过度地 |  In a way that expresses excessive grief or sorrow.  It signifies mourning beyond what might be considered typical or appropriate. | She wept overmournfully at the funeral, drawing attention to herself. 她葬礼上哭得过度悲伤，引起了人们的注意。|  He sighed overmournfully, as if the weight of the world was on his shoulders.  他悲哀地叹了口气，仿佛肩负着整个世界的重担。 | 近义词: excessively sadly, too sorrowfully | 反义词: joyfully, happily | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
* over- (prefix):  meaning "excessive," "too much"
* mournfully (adverb): from mournful (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Mournful comes from mourn (verb) + -ful (adjective suffix).  The verb "mourn" ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *murnan, meaning "to care, remember, grieve."
