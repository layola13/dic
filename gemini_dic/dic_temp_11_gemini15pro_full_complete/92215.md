hypertension:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɛnʃən/ | n. | 高血压 | Abnormally high blood pressure. A condition in which the blood pressure in the arteries is persistently elevated. |  High blood pressure, also known as hypertension, is a common condition. 高血压，也称为高血压，是一种常见疾病。

Her doctor told her she had hypertension and needed to change her diet. 她的医生告诉她她患有高血压，需要改变饮食。 | 近义词: high blood pressure | 反义词: hypotension | 词性: noun
