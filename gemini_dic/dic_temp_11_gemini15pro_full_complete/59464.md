ease-out:/ˈiːzˈaʊt/| v. |逐渐减慢，逐渐停止|To gradually decrease or reduce the intensity or speed of something, especially at the end of a process or movement.|The animation eases out as it approaches the final frame. 动画在接近最后一帧时逐渐减慢。|近义词：slow down, decelerate, taper off|反义词: speed up, accelerate|动词

ease-out:/ˈiːzˈaʊt/| n. |(动画、运动等)逐渐减慢|The gradual decrease or reduction of intensity or speed, especially at the end of a process or movement.|The ease-out of the car's brakes made for a smooth stop. 汽车刹车的逐渐减慢使得停车很平稳。|近义词：deceleration, tapering off|反义词: acceleration|名词

词源：  Formed by combining "ease" (meaning to make or become less severe, difficult, or painful) and "out" (indicating a gradual decrease or cessation).
