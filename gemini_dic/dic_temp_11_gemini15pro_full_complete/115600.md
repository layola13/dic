marbles:/ˈmɑːrblz/ | n. | 大理石制品; 大理石 |A type of metamorphic rock that takes a high polish and is used in sculpture and architecture.  This meaning is usually singular (marble). |例句：The statue was carved from marble. 这座雕像是用大理石雕刻的。

例句：The palace was decorated with marble floors and columns. 宫殿用大理石地板和柱子装饰。|近义词：stone, rock |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
