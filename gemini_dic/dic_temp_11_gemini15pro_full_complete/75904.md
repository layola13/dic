fundraiser:/ˈfʌndˌreɪzər/| n.| 筹款人；筹款活动；|A person or event organized to collect money for a cause.|The school held a fundraiser to build a new library. 学校举办了一场筹款活动以建造一个新的图书馆。|Synonyms: donation drive, fundraising event, charity event|Antonyms: spending spree, expenditure|noun

fundraiser:/ˈfʌndˌreɪzər/| n.| 筹款活动；|An event organized to collect money for a cause.|The fundraiser was a huge success, raising over $10,000. 这次筹款活动非常成功，筹集了超过1万美元。|Synonyms: benefit, fundraising event, charity event|Antonyms: spending spree, expenditure|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fund + raiser.  "Fund" comes from the Latin "fundus" meaning "bottom, foundation." "Raiser" comes from the verb "raise," which has its origins in Old Norse "reisa" meaning "to raise, lift."
