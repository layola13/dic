curvet:/kərˈvɛt/ /ˈkɜːrvət/| n. vi. vt.|腾跃，跃马式，弯曲|A rising or rearing of a horse on its hind legs, or a leap in a playful or spirited manner.  A movement with graceful curves.|The horse performed a graceful curvet. 这匹马表演了一个优美的腾跃。|近义词：leap, bound, prance, caper|反义词:slump, drop|noun, verb
