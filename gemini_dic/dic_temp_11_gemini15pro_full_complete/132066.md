noncoverages:/nɒnˈkʌvərɪdʒɪz/
|n.|未承保范围，未覆盖范围|The policy lists several noncoverages, including flood damage.  该保单列出了几项未承保范围，包括洪水损害。|Absence of coverage; things or situations not covered by an insurance policy or other protective measure.  缺乏覆盖；保险单或其他保护措施未涵盖的事物或情况。|例句：Make sure you understand the noncoverages in your health insurance plan.  确保你了解你的健康保险计划中的未承保范围。

例句：The noncoverages included pre-existing conditions and cosmetic surgery. 未承保范围包括既往症和整容手术。|近义词：exclusions, exceptions, limitations|反义词：coverages, inclusions|词性：名词
