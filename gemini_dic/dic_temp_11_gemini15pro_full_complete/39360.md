conjugateness:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡɪtnɪs/ /ˈkɑːndʒʊɡɪtnɪs/ | n. | 共轭性 | The state or quality of being conjugate.  This term is frequently used in mathematics, chemistry, and linguistics. | The conjugateness of these two verbs allows for smoother transitions in the sentence. 这两个动词的共轭性使句子过渡更顺畅。 |  conjugacy, relatedness | unrelatedness | noun

conjugate:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪt/ /ˈkɑːndʒʊɡeɪt/ | adj. | 共轭的 | Joined together, especially in pairs; coupled.  Also, (of verbs) having the same derivation or inflectional forms. | The two parts of the molecule are conjugate. 分子的两部分是共轭的。 | connected, coupled, related |  unconnected, separate, unrelated | adjective

conjugate:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪt/ /ˈkɑːndʒʊɡeɪt/ | v. |  共轭,结合 | To join together, especially in pairs; to give the different forms of a verb in a specified order and person. |  He conjugated the verb "to be" for us. 他为我们讲解了动词“to be”的各种形式。 | combine, unite, join | separate, divide | verb
