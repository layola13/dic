centaurs:/ˈsɛntɔːrz/| n.|半人马|A mythical creature with the head, arms, and torso of a human and the body and legs of a horse.|例句：Centaurs are often depicted in Greek mythology. 半人马经常出现在希腊神话中。

例句：The centaur Chiron was known for his wisdom and skill in medicine. 半人马喀戎以其智慧和医术而闻名。|近义词：mythical creature, chimera|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "human" or "horse" as contrasting beings) |Noun
