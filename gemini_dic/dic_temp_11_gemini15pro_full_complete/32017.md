chequer:/ˈtʃɛkər/ (US) /ˈtʃɛkə/ (UK)| n. v.|格子，方格；棋盘；西洋跳棋；收银台；出纳员|A pattern of squares, typically alternating in color; a board for playing checkers or chess; a cashier or checkout counter.|The floor was laid with black and white chequers. 地板铺着黑白相间的方格。|Synonyms: checker (US), grid, lattice, squares|Antonyms: solid, plain|noun

chequer:/ˈtʃɛkər/ (US) /ˈtʃɛkə/ (UK)| v.|使成方格状；使多样化；控制；制止|To mark or decorate with a checkered pattern; to vary or diversify; to restrain or control.|The tablecloth was chequered with red and white squares. 桌布上布满了红白相间的方格。|Synonyms: variegate, diversify, check (US)|Antonyms: unify, homogenize|verb
