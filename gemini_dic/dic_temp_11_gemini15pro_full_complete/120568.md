mires:/maɪərz/ | n. | 泥潭; 沼泽;困境 |  A stretch of swampy or boggy ground. A situation that is difficult to escape from. |  The car became stuck in the mires. 汽车陷入了泥潭。 He found himself in a mire of debt. 他发现自己陷入了债务的泥潭。 | synonyms: bog, marsh, swamp, quagmire, morass | antonyms:  solid ground, bedrock | noun

mire:/maɪər/ | v. |  使陷入泥潭; 陷入困境 | To cause to become stuck in mire. To involve in difficult circumstances. |  The heavy rain mired the trucks.  暴雨使卡车陷入了泥潭。 The company was mired in legal disputes.  这家公司陷入了法律纠纷的泥潭。 | synonyms: entangle, embroil, bog down | antonyms: extricate, free, liberate | verb
