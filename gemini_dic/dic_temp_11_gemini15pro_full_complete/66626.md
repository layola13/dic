exinanite: /ɛksɪˈnænaɪt/
adjective
空的，空虚的，无生命的
Emptied; deprived of inhabitants or occupants; lifeless.
The exinanite landscape stretched before them, devoid of any sign of life.  空旷无垠的景色展现在他们面前，没有任何生命的迹象。
The once bustling city now stood exinanite, a ghost of its former self.  曾经熙熙攘攘的城市如今空无一人，成了昔日自身的幽灵。
synonym: empty, vacant, deserted, barren, desolate
antonym: full, occupied, inhabited, teeming, lively
词性: 形容词
