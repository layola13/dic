altitude:/ˈæltɪtjuːd/| n.|高度，海拔|The plane was flying at an altitude of 30,000 feet.  飞机正在30,000英尺的高度飞行。|例句：The mountain has an altitude of over 20,000 feet. 这座山海拔超过20,000英尺。|近义词：height, elevation|反义词:depth|词性:名词

词源：From Latin *altitūdō*, from *altus* (“high”)
