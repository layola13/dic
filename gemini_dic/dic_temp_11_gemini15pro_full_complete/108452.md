laxnesses:/ˈlæksnɪsɪz/
noun
懈怠，松懈，疏忽
Lack of strictness or care; the state of being lax.
The laxnesses in security allowed the thief to enter. 安全方面的疏忽让小偷有机可乘。
近义词:negligence, carelessness, remissness, slackness
反义词:diligence, carefulness, strictness
词性:n.


Etymology:
From laxness + -es (plural suffix).  Laxness itself comes from lax + -ness.  Lax ultimately derives from the Latin "laxus" meaning "loose, wide, slack."
