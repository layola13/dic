lapidate:/ˈlæpɪdeɪt/| vt.|用石头砸死；向…扔石头|To kill by throwing stones at. |The angry mob tried to lapidate the accused thief.  愤怒的暴徒试图用石头砸死被指控的小偷。|Synonyms: stone, pelt|Antonyms: protect, defend|verb
lapidate:/ˈlæpɪdeɪt/| adj.|镶嵌宝石的|Set with or covered with stones, especially precious stones.|The lapidary created a beautiful lapidate necklace. 宝石匠制作了一条漂亮的宝石项链。|Synonyms: jeweled, gem-encrusted|Antonyms: plain, unadorned|adjective
