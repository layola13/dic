cross-buttocker: /ˈkrɒsˈbʌtəkər/ (British English) /ˈkrɔːsˈbʌtəkər/ (American English)| n.|交叉臀部打击者；（尤指橄榄球中）用臀部撞击对方球员下体的人|A person who makes a cross-buttock, especially in rugby.  This refers to a move where a player uses their hip to knock an opponent off balance, often targeting the groin area. It's considered a foul in most sports.|例句：The referee penalized the player for a dangerous cross-buttocker. 裁判因为危险的交叉臀部打击判罚了这名球员。

例句：He was known for his infamous cross-buttocker, which often resulted in penalties. 他以臭名昭著的交叉臀部打击而闻名，这经常导致他被罚。|近义词：cheap shot, foul play|反义词: fair play, sportsmanship|词性: noun
