dandiest:/ˈdændiɪst/| adj.|最衣着讲究的，最时髦的|Superlative form of dandy: most dandy.  Meaning extremely fashionable and stylish, often in a slightly showy or flamboyant way. |He was the dandiest man in the room, with a silk scarf and a perfectly tailored suit. 他是房间里最衣着讲究的人，戴着丝巾，穿着剪裁完美的西装。|近义词：stylish, fashionable, dapper, chic, trendy|反义词：frumpy, dowdy, shabby, unfashionable|词性:形容词
