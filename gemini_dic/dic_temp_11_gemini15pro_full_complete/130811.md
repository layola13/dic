nonapostolic:/ˌnɒnˌæpəˈstɒlɪk/
adj.
非使徒的；非使徒传统的
Not relating to or derived from the apostles or their teachings.
The nonapostolic church developed its own distinct traditions.  这个非使徒教会发展了自己独特的传统。
The group's nonapostolic beliefs led to their expulsion from the mainstream church.  该组织的非使徒信仰导致他们被逐出主流教会。
Near Synonyms: unorthodox, heterodox, unconventional, dissenting
Antonyms: apostolic, orthodox, conventional, traditional
词性: 形容词
