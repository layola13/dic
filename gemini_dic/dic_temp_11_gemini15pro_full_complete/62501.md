endothermic:/ˌɛndoʊˈθɜːrmɪk/ | adj. |吸热的| (of a reaction or process) accompanied by or requiring the absorption of heat.|  The dissolution of ammonium nitrate in water is an endothermic process. 硝酸铵溶于水是一个吸热过程。| synonyms: heat-absorbing | antonyms: exothermic | adjective
