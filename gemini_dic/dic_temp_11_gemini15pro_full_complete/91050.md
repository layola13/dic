hussite:/ˈhʌsaɪt/| n. |胡赛特派教徒，胡斯信徒|A follower of Jan Hus, a Czech religious reformer who was burned at the stake for heresy in 1415.|例句：The Hussites fought for religious freedom in Bohemia. 胡斯信徒在波西米亚为宗教自由而战。

例句：The Hussite Wars were a series of religious conflicts in the 15th century. 十五世纪的胡斯战争是一系列宗教冲突。|近义词：reformer, Protestant, iconoclast|反义词：Catholic, orthodox|词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Jan Hus (c. 1369-1415), a Czech theologian and philosopher. His name means "goose" in Czech.  His followers became known as Hussites.
