brachydome:/ˈbrækɪdoʊm/| n. |短穹|A dome-like crystal form with low vertical development.  A crystal form consisting of a set of four similar, non-parallel faces that intersect to form a short, dome-like shape on a crystal.|The mineral calcite often exhibits brachydomes. 方解石经常呈现短穹。|Synonyms: dome (in some contexts)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly prism or pinacoid depending on the specific contrasting crystal form)|noun

brachydome:/ˈbrækɪdoʊm/| n. |短轴穹|A crystal form with a set of four faces parallel to a plane of symmetry and intersecting to form a dome-like shape shortened in the direction of the brachy axis (the shorter lateral axis).|The brachydome is a characteristic feature of some orthorhombic minerals. 短轴穹是某些斜方晶系矿物的一个特征。|Synonyms: dome (in a broader context)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly macrodome or prism depending on the specific contrasting crystal form)|noun
