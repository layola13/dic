hundredth:/ˈhʌndrədθ/| adj. n.|第一百的，百分之一；第一百个|Representing or relating to one part in a hundred equal parts.  Also, the ordinal number of one hundred in a series.|He celebrated his hundredth birthday. 他庆祝了他的百岁生日。|The hundredth customer will receive a prize.  第一百位顾客将获得一份奖品。|近义词:centennial, one percent|反义词:first|词性:形容词，名词
