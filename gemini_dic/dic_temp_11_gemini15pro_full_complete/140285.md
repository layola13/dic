oftens:/ˈɔːfənz/ /ˈɑːfənz/ | adv. | 经常，常常 | Often is used to describe something that happens frequently, but not always.  It indicates a regularity of occurrence. | She often goes to the gym after work. 她经常下班后去健身房。|  He often forgets his keys. 他经常忘记带钥匙。 | 近义词: frequently, regularly, commonly | 反义词: rarely, seldom, infrequently | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *often, *oftes, from Old English *oft, *ofte.

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He often walks to work. 他经常步行上班。
She often reads before bed. 她经常睡前读书。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is often complaining about the weather. 他经常抱怨天气。 (表示一种习惯性的抱怨)
She is often helping her mother with the chores. 她经常帮她妈妈做家务。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has often visited his grandparents. 他经常去看望他的祖父母。
She has often traveled abroad. 她经常出国旅行。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He has been often working late recently. 他最近经常工作到很晚。
She has been often practicing the piano. 她经常练习钢琴。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He often went to the library as a child. 他小时候经常去图书馆。
She often played with her dolls. 她经常玩她的洋娃娃。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was often complaining about the noise. 他经常抱怨噪音。
She was often helping her neighbors. 她经常帮助她的邻居。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had often visited that museum before he moved. 他搬家前经常去那个博物馆。
She had often thought about changing careers. 她经常考虑换工作。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been often working on that project before it was canceled.  项目取消前，他经常致力于那个项目。
She had been often practicing her speech before the big day.  在大日子之前，她经常练习她的演讲。

虚拟语气:
If I often went to the gym, I would be in better shape. 如果我经常去健身房，我的身材会更好。
I wish I often had time to read. 我希望我经常有时间读书。


被动语态:  (oftens 不能用于被动语态，因为它是一个副词，修饰动词，而不是一个可以被动的动词)


疑问句: (oftens 一般不直接用于疑问句, 通常使用 how often)
How often do you go to the gym? 你多久去一次健身房？
How often does she visit her family? 她多久去看望一次家人？

否定句: (oftens 的否定形式通常是 not often 或 rarely/seldom)
He doesn't often go out on weeknights.  他平日晚上不经常出去。
She is not often late for work. 她上班不经常迟到。



PHRASE-> (oftens 一般不构成短语)

quite often  相当经常
very often 非常经常
as often as possible 尽可能经常
