funnest:/ˈfʌnɪst/| adj.|最有趣的|Superlative form of fun.  Used informally, often considered non-standard English.  Means the most enjoyable or amusing.|例句：That was the funnest party I've ever been to! 那是我参加过的最有趣的派对！|近义词：most enjoyable, most amusing, best|反义词：least fun, most boring|词性:形容词
Etymology: From fun + -est (superlative suffix).  The regular superlative of "fun" is "most fun," but "funnest" is commonly used in informal speech, especially by children.
