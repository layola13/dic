dotage:/ˈdoʊtɪdʒ/| n. |老年昏聩；溺爱|A state of senility or mental decline associated with old age; excessive fondness or indulgence.|His grandfather, in his dotage, often mistook his grandson for his long-dead brother.|Synonyms: senility, feebleness, imbecility, second childhood; infatuation, partiality|Antonyms: lucidity, alertness, vigor; indifference, apathy|noun
