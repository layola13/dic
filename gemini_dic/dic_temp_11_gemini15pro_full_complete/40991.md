coordinators:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtərz/| n.|协调员，统筹者|A person who organizes people or groups so that they work together efficiently and effectively.  A coordinator is responsible for bringing different elements of a project or activity together in a smooth and organized way.|The event coordinators did a fantastic job ensuring everything ran smoothly. 活动协调员出色地完成了工作，确保一切顺利进行。|synonyms: organizer, planner, manager, facilitator, director|antonyms: disorganizer|noun
