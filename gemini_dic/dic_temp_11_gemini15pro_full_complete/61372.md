embitterers:/ɪmˈbɪtərərz/
noun
使人怨恨的事物；使情况恶化的事物
Things or people that cause someone to feel bitter or resentful; things that worsen a situation.
The constant criticism from her boss was one of the main embitterers in her life. 她老板不断的批评是她生活中主要的怨恨之源。
The economic downturn was a major embitterer for many families. 经济衰退是许多家庭苦恼的主要原因。
synonyms: aggravators, exacerbators, sources of bitterness, provocations
antonyms: soothers, mitigators, sources of joy, comforts
