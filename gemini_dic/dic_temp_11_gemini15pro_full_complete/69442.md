freeze:/friːz/| v. n.| 冻结，凝固，僵住|To change from a liquid to a solid because of a low temperature; to become very cold; to stop moving suddenly.|The lake froze over in winter. 冬天湖水结冰了。|近义词：chill, congeal, solidify|反义词:thaw, melt|verb
freeze:/friːz/| n.| 冻结，冰冻期|A period of very cold weather.|There was a hard freeze last night. 昨晚天气非常寒冷。|近义词：frost, cold snap|反义词:thaw|noun
