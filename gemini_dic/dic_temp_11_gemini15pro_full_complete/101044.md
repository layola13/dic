inveighs:/ɪnˈveɪz/| v.|痛斥，猛烈抨击|To attack (someone or something) verbally with strong or bitter criticism.|He inveighed against the government's new policies. 他猛烈抨击政府的新政策。|近义词：denounce, rail, fulminate, lambaste|反义词: praise, commend, laud |词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Latin *invehere, from Latin in- "in, into, on, upon, against" + vehere "to carry, convey".
