caryopsis:/ˌkæriˈɒpsɪs/ /ˌkɛriˈɑːpsɪs/| n. |颖果|A dry one-seeded fruit in which the ovary wall is united with the seed coat, typical of grasses and cereals.|The wheat caryopsis is the main source of flour.|近义词:grain, seed|反义词:None|词性:Noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin, from Greek *karyon* (nut) + *-opsis* (appearance)
