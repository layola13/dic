desolatenesses:/ˌdɛsəlɪtnɪsɪz/
noun
荒凉，凄凉
The state of being desolate; bleakness and emptiness.
The desolatenesses of the abandoned town were striking.  这座废弃城镇的荒凉景象令人震惊。
The photograph captured the utter desolatenesses of the landscape after the fire.  这张照片捕捉到了火灾后这片土地的极度荒凉。
近义词: bleakness, barrenness, desolation, emptiness
反义词: vibrancy, liveliness, lushness, fertility
词性: noun

desolate:/ˈdɛsəlɪt/
adjective
荒凉的，凄凉的
(of a place) deserted of people and in a state of bleak and dismal emptiness.
The landscape was desolate and windswept.  这片土地荒凉而多风。
She felt desolate and alone after her husband died.  丈夫去世后，她感到孤独和凄凉。
近义词: barren, bleak, deserted, empty
反义词: populated, lively, lush, fertile
词性: adjective
