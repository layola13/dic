apogees:/ˈæpədʒiːz/
n.
最高点；顶峰；巅峰；极点
the highest point in the development of something; a climax or culmination
例句：The empire reached its apogee in the mid-17th century.  该帝国在17世纪中叶达到顶峰。

例句：His career reached its apogee when he was elected president.  他的职业生涯在他当选总统时达到了顶峰。

近义词：climax, peak, summit, pinnacle, zenith
反义词：nadir, perigee
词性: 名词(plural)
