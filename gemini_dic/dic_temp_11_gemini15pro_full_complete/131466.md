noncoherent:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhɪərənt/ | adj. |不连贯的，不一致的|Lacking coherence or connection; disjointed.|Her speech was rambling and noncoherent. 她的讲话漫无边际，语无伦次。|incoherent, disjointed, disconnected|coherent, consistent, connected|adjective
