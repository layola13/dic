antifemale:/ˌæntiˈfiːmeɪl/
adj.
反对女性的，歧视女性的
Opposed to or hostile towards women; discriminatory against women.
His antifemale comments sparked outrage. 他歧视女性的言论引发了众怒。
近义词: misogynistic, sexist
反义词: profeminist, feminist
词性: 形容词

词源:  "anti-" (against) + "female" (woman).  The prefix "anti-" comes from Greek, meaning "against" or "opposite of." "Female" comes from Old French "femelle," ultimately from Latin "femella," diminutive of "femina" (woman).
