appearingly:/əˈpɪərɪŋli/
adverb
显然地；似乎；看起来
Seeming to be true but not necessarily so.
He was apparently much surprised at the news. 他对这个消息显然很惊讶。
Synonyms: seemingly, ostensibly, evidently, outwardly
Antonyms: genuinely, truly, actually, really
詞性: 副詞
