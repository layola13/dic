overpollinating:/ˌōvərˈpɑːləˌneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度授粉 | The act of pollinating a plant or crop excessively, often leading to negative consequences. | The farmer worried about overpollinating his apple orchard. 农民担心他的苹果园授粉过度。| Overpollination can lead to smaller fruit size. 过度授粉会导致果实变小。| synonyms: over-fertilizing, excessive pollination | antonyms: underpollinating, insufficient pollination | verb
