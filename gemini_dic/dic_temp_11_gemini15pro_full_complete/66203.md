excitability:/ɪkˌsaɪtəˈbɪləti/| n.|兴奋性，应激性|The excitability of nerve cells allows for rapid communication throughout the body. 神经细胞的兴奋性使得全身快速通讯成为可能。|例句：The excitability of the fans increased as the game went on.  随着比赛的进行，球迷们的兴奋性越来越高。

例句：The chemical increased the excitability of the neurons.  这种化学物质增加了神经元的兴奋性。|近义词：irritability, responsiveness, sensitivity|反义词：inertness, impassivity|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin excitabilitās, from Latin excitābilis (“easily roused”).  源自晚期拉丁语 excitabilitās，来自拉丁语 excitābilis（“容易被唤醒的”）。
