chalkstone:/ˈtʃɔːkˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈtʃɔːkˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |白垩岩，白垩石|A type of limestone formed largely from the skeletal remains of marine organisms, especially foraminifera, and consisting mainly of calcium carbonate.|The cliffs are made of chalkstone. 这些悬崖是由白垩岩构成的。|Synonyms: chalk, limestone|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider igneous rock or metamorphic rock)|noun
