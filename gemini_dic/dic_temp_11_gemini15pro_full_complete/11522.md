aril:/ˈærɪl/ | n. |假种皮|An outer covering of a seed, arising from the stalk or funiculus, as in the yew.|The yew’s aril is a fleshy, cup-shaped structure. 紫杉的假种皮是一个肉质的杯状结构。|Synonym: arillode|Antonym: N/A (lack of a specific antonym)|noun
arillode:/əˈrɪləʊd/ | n. |拟假种皮|A fleshy outgrowth from the base of a seed that partially or completely covers it, similar to an aril but developing from the micropyle (seed opening) rather than the stalk.|The spice mace is derived from the arillode of the nutmeg seed.  肉豆蔻香料来源于肉豆蔻种子的拟假种皮。|Synonym: false aril|Antonym: N/A (lack of a specific antonym)|noun
