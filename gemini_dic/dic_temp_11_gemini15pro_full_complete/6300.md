amort:/əˈmɔːrt/ | adj. | 熄灭的，无生命的，沮丧的 | Extinguished, lifeless, dispirited; no longer burning or active, either literally or figuratively.  |  The fire, once blazing fiercely, is now amort. 火曾经熊熊燃烧，现在已经熄灭了。 | 近义词：extinguished, dead, lifeless, listless, apathetic | 反义词：alive, vibrant, animated, enthusiastic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Old French *amort (modern French mort, “dead”), from Latin mortuus, past participle of morī (“to die”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mer- (“to die, disappear”).
