interflow:/ˈɪntərfloʊ/| n.|相互流动，渗流|The interflow of groundwater is an important part of the water cycle.  地下水的相互流动是水循环的重要组成部分。|例句：The interflow between the two rivers created a unique ecosystem.  两条河流之间的相互流动创造了一个独特的生态系统。

例句：The interflow of ideas between different cultures can be enriching. 不同文化之间思想的交流可以互相丰富。

近义词：exchange, interchange, interaction|反义词: stagnation, separation|词性:noun

Etymology:
From inter- +‎ flow.
