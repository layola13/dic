multiple-choice:/ˈmʌltɪpl ˈtʃɔɪs/ | adj. | 多项选择的 |  A type of question or test where you are given a number of possible answers and you have to choose the correct one.  |  The exam was multiple-choice, so it was quite easy. 考试是多项选择题，所以很简单。| synonyms:  multi-option, select-one, fixed-response | antonyms: open-ended, essay, free-response | adjective


multiple-choice:/ˈmʌltɪpl ˈtʃɔɪs/ | n. | 多项选择题 | A question or test with multiple-choice options. |  I prefer multiple-choice to essay questions. 我喜欢多项选择题胜过论述题。| synonyms: multiple-choice question, multiple-choice test | antonyms: open-ended question, essay question | noun
