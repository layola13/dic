alantoin:/ˈælənˌtɔɪn/ | n. |尿囊素| A crystalline oxidation product of uric acid, C₄H₆N₄O₃, present in many plants and in fetal allantoic fluid and mammalian urine. It promotes cell proliferation and wound healing and is used in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. | Alantoin is often used in skincare products to soothe irritated skin. 尿囊素常用于护肤品中，以舒缓受刺激的皮肤。 |Synonyms:  glyoxyldiureide| Antonyms:  N/A | Noun
