nonexaggeration:/ˌnɒnɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃn/
noun
不夸张;如实;不加渲染
The statement is a nonexaggeration of the facts.  这个陈述是对事实的如实描述。
This is nonexaggeration; it really happened. 这不是夸张，真的发生了。
Synonyms: truth, reality, fact, accuracy
Antonyms: exaggeration, hyperbole, overstatement
n.
