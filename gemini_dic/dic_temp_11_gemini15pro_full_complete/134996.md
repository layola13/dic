nonnebular:/ˌnɑːnˈnebjələr/
adjective
非星云的
Not pertaining to, resembling, or originating from a nebula.
The scientist argued that the phenomenon had a nonnebular origin.  这位科学家认为这种现象起源于非星云。
The formation of this celestial body is considered nonnebular.  这个天体的形成被认为是非星云的。
Near Antonyms: nebular
Antonym: nebular
