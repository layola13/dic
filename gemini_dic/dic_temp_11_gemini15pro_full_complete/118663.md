meta-ethical:/ˈmɛtəˈɛθɪkəl/
adjective
元伦理的
Relating to the study of the nature of ethics, including the meaning of ethical terms and judgments, the nature of moral reasoning, and the foundations of moral principles.  It explores the meaning of moral language and the logic of moral arguments, rather than addressing specific ethical dilemmas.
Meta-ethical discussions often revolve around whether morality is objective or subjective, relative or universal.
元伦理的讨论通常围绕道德是客观的还是主观的，相对的还是普遍的。
Meta-ethical theories don't tell us what to do, but they help us understand the nature of moral claims.
元伦理理论并没有告诉我们应该做什么，而是帮助我们理解道德主张的本质。
