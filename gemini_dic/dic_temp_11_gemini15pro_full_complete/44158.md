criticiser:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzər/ | n. | 批评家，批评者 | A person who expresses an unfavorable opinion of something. | He's a harsh criticiser of modern art. 他是一位严厉的现代艺术批评家。|  The criticiser slammed the new movie.  这位批评家猛烈抨击了这部新电影。 | synonym: critic, faultfinder, detractor | antonym: supporter, admirer, advocate | noun
