chicory: /ˈtʃɪkəri/ (US) /ˈtʃɪkəri/ (UK)| n.| 菊苣; 菊苣根;|A blue-flowered Mediterranean plant of the daisy family cultivated for its edible salad leaves and carrot-shaped root.|例句：I added some chicory to the salad for a bit of bitterness. 我在沙拉里加了一些菊苣，增添一些苦味。

例句：Chicory root is often roasted and ground as a coffee substitute. 菊苣根常被烤制和磨碎用作咖啡替代品。

近义词：endive (especially for the leafy variety), radicchio|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sweet herb" as a contrast in flavor)|词性:noun
