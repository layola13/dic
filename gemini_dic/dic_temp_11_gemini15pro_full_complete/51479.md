detoxified: /ˌdɪːˈtɑːksɪfaɪd/
adjective
已解毒的，已清除毒素的
freed from toxic substances.  The patient was detoxified after accidentally ingesting poison.  这位病人在意外摄入毒药后被解毒了。
近义词: cleansed, purified, purged
反义词: contaminated, poisoned, polluted
词性: adjective

detoxify: /ˌdɪːˈtɑːksɪfaɪ/
verb
解毒，清除毒素
to remove toxic substances or qualities. The clinic helps people detoxify from drug and alcohol addiction.  这家诊所帮助人们戒除毒瘾和酒瘾。
近义词: cleanse, purify, purge
反义词: contaminate, poison, pollute
词性: verb

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + toxic + -ify (suffix meaning "to make or cause to be")  来源于拉丁语 toxicum (poison)
