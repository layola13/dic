deputy:/ˈdepjʊti/ /ˈdɛpjʊti/| n. adj.|代理人，副手，代表|A person appointed or elected to act or speak for another or others; a substitute.  A person holding a secondary rank to a superior.|The deputy sheriff was in charge while the sheriff was away. 副警长在警长不在的时候负责。|近义词:assistant, substitute, representative, agent, delegate|反义词:chief, leader, head, principal|noun

deputy:/ˈdepjʊti/ /ˈdɛpjʊti/| adj.|代理的，副的|Acting or appointed as a substitute; second in rank or authority.|He served as deputy mayor. 他担任副市长。|近义词:acting, substitute, assistant|反义词:chief, principal|adjective
