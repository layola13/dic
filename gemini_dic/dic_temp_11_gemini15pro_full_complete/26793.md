caffeic:/kæˈfiːɪk/ | adj. | 咖啡的，咖啡酸的 | Relating to or derived from coffee; specifically designating caffeic acid, a yellowish crystalline acid obtained from coffee and other plants. |  Coffee beans contain caffeic acid, an antioxidant. 咖啡豆含有咖啡酸，一种抗氧化剂。|  Caffeic acid is found in many fruits and vegetables. 咖啡酸存在于许多水果和蔬菜中。 | 近义词: cinnamic (肉桂的，有关肉桂酸的)  | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词) | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *caféique*, from *café* (coffee) + *-ique* (adjectival suffix). Ultimately from Turkish *kahve*, from Arabic *qahwah* (coffee).
