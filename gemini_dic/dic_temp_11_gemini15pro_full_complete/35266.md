clustered:/ˈklʌstərd/| adj.|聚集的，成群的|Gathered or growing closely together in a group.|The houses were clustered around the village square.  这些房屋聚集在村庄广场周围。|The grapes clustered on the vine. 葡萄成串地挂在藤蔓上。|近义词：grouped, bunched, concentrated, agglomerated|反义词：scattered, dispersed, spread out|词性:形容词
词源：From the noun "cluster" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Cluster" comes from Old English "clyster" meaning "clod, lump."
