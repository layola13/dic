bedazzlement:/bɪˈdæzlmənt/| n.|眼花缭乱，炫目，迷惑|The sudden flash of light caused a moment of bedazzlement. 突然的闪光使人一时眼花缭乱。|例句：The sheer bedazzlement of the Las Vegas strip can be overwhelming.  拉斯维加斯大道的耀眼夺目让人难以招架。

The bedazzlement of the fireworks display left the children speechless.  烟花表演的绚丽景象让孩子们说不出话来。|近义词：amazement, astonishment, fascination, dazzlement|反义词:disinterest, boredom|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bedazzle + -ment.  Bedazzle itself comes from be- (making a verb causative) + dazzle.  Ultimately, dazzle has roots in a Scandinavian word meaning "to be fool."
