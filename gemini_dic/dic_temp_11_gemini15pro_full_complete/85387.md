harten:/ˈhɑːrtn/ (US) /ˈhɑːtn/ (UK)| v. |鼓励，激励，振奋|To make someone feel more cheerful or hopeful; to encourage.|The coach's words heartened the team after their defeat. 教练的话在球队战败后鼓舞了他们。|例句：
The good news heartened us.  好消息鼓舞了我们。

Seeing the support of the community heartened the firefighters. 看到社区的支持，消防队员们倍感振奋。

|近义词：encourage, cheer, inspire, uplift|反义词:discourage, deject, dispirit|词性:动词
