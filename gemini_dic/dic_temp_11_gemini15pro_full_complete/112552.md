lovelessly:/ˈlʌvləsli/| adv.|无爱地，冷漠地，缺乏感情地|Without love or affection; in a cold or uncaring manner.|She treated him lovelessly, never showing any warmth or tenderness. 她待他冷漠无情，从未流露出任何温暖或温柔。|例句：He spoke lovelessly, his voice devoid of emotion. 他说话毫无感情，声音里没有一丝情感。|近义词：coldly, heartlessly, indifferently, uncaringly|反义词：lovingly, affectionately, tenderly|词性:副词
词源:love (n.) + -less (suffix) + -ly (suffix).  From Old English lufu "love, affection, friendliness," of Germanic origin,  + -less "without" + -ly adverbial suffix.
