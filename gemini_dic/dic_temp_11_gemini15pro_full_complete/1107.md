accessibly:/əkˈsɛsəbli/ | adv. | 易接近地；可理解地；易懂地 | In a way that is easy to reach, enter, use, or understand. | The museum is now accessibly to people in wheelchairs.  博物馆现在方便轮椅使用者进入。| 近义词: easily, readily, conveniently | 反义词: inaccessibly, difficultly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From accessible + -ly.  "Accessible" comes from Late Latin accessibilis, from Latin accedere "to approach," from ad- "to" + cedere "to go."
