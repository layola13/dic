clinger's:/ˈklɪŋərz/| n.|粘着的人的（东西）|Something that belongs to a clinger, someone who is excessively attached or dependent on another person.  It is the possessive form of "clinger." |例句：That's the clinger's jacket. 那是那个粘人的人的夹克。|近义词：dependent person's, leech's|反义词: independent person's|词性:noun (possessive)
