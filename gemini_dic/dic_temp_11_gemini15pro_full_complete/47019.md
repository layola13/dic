dastard:/ˈdæstərd/
n.
懦夫；卑鄙的人
A cowardly and treacherous villain.
The dastard ran away as soon as the fighting started.  那个懦夫一开战就逃跑了。
近义词：coward, villain
反义词：hero
词性：名词


词源:
From Middle English *dastard, from Old French *dastart (“sluggard, coward”), perhaps from Old Norse *dastra (“to weary, exhaust”), from Proto-Germanic *dastrōną.
