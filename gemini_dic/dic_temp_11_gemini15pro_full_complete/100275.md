intersect:/ˌɪntərˈsekt/ | v. |相交，交叉| to divide (something) by passing or lying across it : to cut or cross each other | The roads intersect near the bridge.  这些道路在桥附近相交。| 近义词：cross, meet, converge | 反义词: diverge, separate |词性:动词

intersect:/ˈɪntərˌsekt/ | n. | 交点，相交点 | the point where two lines or roads meet or cross each other | The intersect of the two lines forms a right angle. 两条线的交点形成一个直角。 | 近义词：intersection, junction, crossing | 反义词: N/A |词性:名词


词源:  From Latin *intersectus*, past participle of intersecare "to cut into, cut apart, divide," from inter "between" + secare "to cut."
