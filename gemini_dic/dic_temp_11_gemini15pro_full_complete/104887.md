keeper:/ˈkiːpər/ (US); /ˈkiːpə/ (UK)| n.|守护者，饲养员，管理人|A person who takes care of something or someone.|The zoo keeper feeds the animals every morning. 动物园管理员每天早上喂动物。|
近义词：custodian, guardian, warden, caretaker|反义词:destroyer, demolisher|词性:noun

keeper:/ˈkiːpər/ (US); /ˈkiːpə/ (UK)| n.|(足球、曲棍球等)守门员|A player who defends the goal in sports like soccer or hockey.|The keeper made a fantastic save. 守门员做出了精彩的扑救。|近义词：goalie, goalkeeper|反义词:forward, striker|词性:noun
