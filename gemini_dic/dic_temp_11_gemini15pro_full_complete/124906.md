muddy:/ˈmʌdi/ (US), /ˈmʌdi/ (UK)| adj.|泥泞的；浑浊的；模糊不清的；(声音)沉闷的|Covered or filled with mud; not clear or bright; (of sound) dull and unclear.|The road was muddy after the rain. 雨后道路泥泞不堪。|近义词：dirty, murky, unclear, dull|反义词：clean, clear, bright, distinct|词性:形容词

muddy:/ˈmʌdi/ (US), /ˈmʌdi/ (UK)| v.|使…沾上泥；使…浑浊|To make something muddy.|Don't muddy your new shoes! 别把你的新鞋弄脏了！|近义词：soil, dirty|反义词：clean, clear|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mudig (unattested), from mud + -ig. Cognate with Dutch moddig, German muddig.
