abattis:/əˈbætiː/ /æbəˈtiː/
n.
障碍物，防御工事
An obstacle formed by felled trees with sharpened branches pointed outward, used as a defense against attack.
The soldiers constructed an abattis to slow the enemy's advance. 士兵们建造了障碍物来延缓敌人的进攻。

abattis:/əˈbætiː/ /æbəˈtiː/
n.
(尤指被砍伐树木形成的)防御工事
A defensive obstacle, especially one made of felled trees.
The abattis proved effective in deterring the enemy's cavalry. 防御工事有效地阻止了敌人的骑兵。


Etymology->
From French *abattis*, from *abattre* "to fell," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) "to" + *battre* "to beat."

Synonym->
barrier, barricade, obstacle, obstruction, rampart

Antonym->
opening, passage, access
