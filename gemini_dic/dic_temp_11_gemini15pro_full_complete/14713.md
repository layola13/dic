awarders:/əˈwɔːrdərz/
n.
授予者；颁奖者
People or organizations that give awards.
The awarders chose the best film of the year.  授予者评选出了年度最佳影片。
The awarders of the Nobel Prize are highly respected. 诺贝尔奖的颁奖者备受尊敬。
Synonyms:  givers, presenters, donors, grantors
Antonyms: recipients, receivers
