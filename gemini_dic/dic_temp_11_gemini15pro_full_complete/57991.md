drivellers:/ˈdrɪvələrz/| n.|胡言乱语者；流口水的人；傻瓜|A person who talks nonsense or drivels.  Someone who is foolish or inept.|The politician dismissed his critics as a pack of drivellers.|Synonyms: babblers, chatterboxes, fools, idiots, simpletons|Antonyms: geniuses, intellectuals, sages|Noun

drivel:/ˈdrɪvəl/| n. vi.|胡言乱语；口水；废话|Nonsense; saliva flowing from the mouth; to talk foolishly or incoherently.|He was talking such drivel that I just tuned him out.|Synonyms: nonsense, gibberish, balderdash, twaddle|Antonyms: sense, wisdom, truth|Noun, Verb
