intermixes:/ˌɪntərˈmɪksɪz/
verb
混合，掺杂
combines or mixes different things together.
The artist intermixes traditional techniques with modern digital art.  这位艺术家将传统技法与现代数字艺术混合在一起。
The DJ skillfully intermixes various musical genres.  DJ巧妙地融合了各种音乐流派。
Synonyms: blend, mingle, combine, mix, merge
Antonyms: separate, divide, sort, segregate
词性:v.
