nondecelerations:/ˌnɒndiːˌsɛləˈreɪʃənz/ /ˌnɑːndiːˌsɛləˈreɪʃənz/
noun
无减速
Absence of decelerations; typically used in the context of fetal heart rate monitoring during pregnancy, indicating the fetal heart rate doesn't slow down in response to uterine contractions.
During labor, the doctor monitored the fetal heart rate for any nondecelerations, which can be a positive sign.  在分娩期间，医生监测胎儿心率是否存在无减速，这可能是一个积极的迹象。
The nondecelerations on the fetal monitor were reassuring to the medical team. 胎心监护仪上的无减速现象让医疗团队感到安心。
Synonym: absence of decelerations
Antonym: decelerations
词性: 名词
