blazingly:/ˈbleɪzɪŋli/| adv.|炽烈地，闪耀地，极度地|in a blazing manner; intensely; extremely|例句：The sun was blazingly hot. 太阳炽热无比。|近义词：intensely, brightly, fiercely, extremely|反义词：dimly, faintly, mildly|词性：副词
词源：From blaze + -ing + -ly.  "Blaze" comes from Old English *blǣsan, from Proto-Germanic *blēsaną (“to blow, blast”).
