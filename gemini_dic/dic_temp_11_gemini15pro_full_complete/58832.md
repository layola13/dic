dupion:/ˈdjuːpiən/ /duːpiˈɒn/ | n. |双宫绸，绢丝|A strong, close-woven silk fabric with a slightly uneven surface, often used for upholstery and curtains.| The curtains were made of a luxurious dupion silk. 窗帘是用奢华的双宫绸制成的。|  The bride's dress was made of ivory dupion. 新娘的礼服是用象牙色的双宫绸做的。 |Synonyms: shantung, tussah | Antonyms: N/A | noun
