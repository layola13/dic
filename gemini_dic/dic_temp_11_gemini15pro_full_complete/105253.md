khanga:/ˈkɑːŋɡə/| n.|坎加|A brightly colored East African cotton cloth, traditionally worn by women, often bearing a printed proverb or message.|She wore a beautiful khanga to the market. 她穿着漂亮的坎加去市场了。|The khanga she wore had a proverb about the importance of family. 她穿的坎加上面有一句关于家庭重要性的谚语。|近义词:kanga, leso|反义词: (No direct antonym for a piece of clothing)|词性:noun
