opportuneness:/ˌɒpətjuːnˈnɛsnɪs/ /ˌɑːpərtjuːnˈnɛsnɪs/
n.
适时性；时机恰当
The quality of being opportune; suitability of time or circumstance.
His opportuneness in buying the stock just before it went up was remarkable. 他在股票上涨前买入股票的时机把握得非常好。
近义词：timeliness, appropriateness, suitability, fitness
反义词：inappropriateness, untimeliness, inopportuneness
词性: 名词
