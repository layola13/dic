antephialtic: /ˌæntiˈfaɪˈæltɪk/ | adj. | 解除噩梦的，防止噩梦的 |  Preventing or curing nightmares; effective against nightmares.  It comes from the Greek "anti-" (against) and "phialtes" (nightmare). |  He sought an antephialtic remedy for his recurring nightmares. 他寻找一种可以治疗他反复出现的噩梦的疗法。| Synonyms: nightmare-preventing, anti-nightmare | Antonyms: nightmare-inducing | Adjective
