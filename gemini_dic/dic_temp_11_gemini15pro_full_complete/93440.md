illuminations:/ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃənz/
noun
照明；彩灯；启发；阐明
the lighting of something, or decorations consisting of bright lights, or something that makes a subject or problem clearer.
The city's illuminations at Christmas are spectacular.  这个城市圣诞节的彩灯非常壮观。
The book provides valuable illuminations on the history of the period. 这本书对这一时期的历史提供了宝贵的启示。
Synonyms: lights, enlightenment, clarification, insight
Antonyms: darkness, obscurity, confusion
