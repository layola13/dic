epilogue:/ˈepɪˌlɒɡ/ (US) /ˈepɪˌlɔːɡ/ (UK)| n.|结语，后记|A section or speech at the end of a book or play that serves as a comment on or a conclusion to what has happened.|Example: The epilogue of the novel tied up loose ends and hinted at a sequel. 这部小说的结语把零散的线索串联起来，并暗示了续集的存在。

Example:  The play's epilogue showed the main characters ten years later, living happily ever after.  这部戏剧的尾声展现了十年后主要角色过着幸福快乐的生活。|近义词：afterword, postscript, conclusion|反义词:prologue, preface|noun
