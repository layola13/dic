all-embracing:/ɔːl-ɪmˈbreɪsɪŋ/
adjective
包罗万象的；无所不包的
Including everything; comprehensive.
例句：The book offers an all-embracing account of the artist's life and work. 这本书对这位艺术家的生平和作品进行了全面的描述。

例句：The new law is all-embracing in its scope.  这项新法律的范围包罗万象。

近义词：comprehensive, inclusive, all-inclusive, universal
反义词：limited, restricted, narrow, exclusive
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From all + embracing (present participle of embrace).  "Embrace" comes from Old French "embracier," from "en-" (in) + "brace" (arms).  Ultimately from Latin "bracchium" (arm).
