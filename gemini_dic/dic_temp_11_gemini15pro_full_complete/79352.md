gleire:/ɡlaɪər/| n.| 蛋白, 粘液|A viscous, albuminous substance, especially the white of an egg.  A glair.  Sometimes also used for other viscous fluids.|The gleire of the egg helped bind the ingredients together.  鸡蛋的蛋白帮助把配料粘合在一起。|近义词：albumen, white, mucus, viscosity|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thinness" in reference to consistency)|词性:noun
