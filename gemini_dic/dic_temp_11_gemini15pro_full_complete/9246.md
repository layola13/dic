antipathist:/ænˈtɪpəθɪst/
n.
憎恶者，厌恶者
One who feels antipathy. A person who strongly dislikes someone or something.
He is an antipathist of modern art. 他是现代艺术的憎恶者。

近义词:hater, detractor, antagonist, enemy
反义词:supporter, admirer, proponent, friend
词性:noun
