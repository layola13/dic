nebuly:/ˈnɛbjʊli/| adj.|云状的，星云状的|Resembling a cloud; cloudy or hazy in appearance. Often used in heraldry to describe a wavy line representing clouds.|The shield was nebuly argent and azure. 盾牌是银色和蓝色的云状图案。|近义词：cloudy, hazy, nebulous|反义词：clear, distinct|词性:形容词

nebuly:/ˈnɛbjʊli/| n.|星云状物|Something that resembles a cloud or nebula.|The artist painted a nebuly of colors across the canvas. 艺术家在画布上画了一团云状的色彩。|近义词：cloud, nebula|反义词：- (no clear opposite)|词性:名词
