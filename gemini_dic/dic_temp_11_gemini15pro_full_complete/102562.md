jaggedness:/ˈdʒæɡɪdnəs/

|近义词：roughness, unevenness, irregularity|
|反义词：smoothness, evenness, regularity|
|词性：名词|
|词源：From jagged + -ness. Jagged comes from the Middle English jaggen, of unknown origin.|
