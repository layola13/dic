caucus:/ˈkɔːkəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːkəs/ (UK)| n. vi.| 核心会议，预选会议|A meeting of members of a political party or movement, especially in the US, to choose candidates or decide policy.|例句：The Democratic caucus chose their nominee for president. 民主党核心会议选出了他们的总统候选人。

例句：The senators held a caucus to discuss the upcoming bill. 参议员们举行了一次核心会议来讨论即将出台的法案。|近义词：meeting, conference, assembly|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb


caucus:/ˈkɔːkəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːkəs/ (UK)| vi.| 参加核心会议|To hold or participate in a caucus.|例句：The party members caucused to select their candidate.  党员们召开核心会议来选择他们的候选人。
