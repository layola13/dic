nonsubscriber:/ˌnɑːnsəbˈskraɪbər/ /ˌnɒnsəbˈskraɪbər/
n.|非订阅者，非订户|A person who does not subscribe to something, such as a publication, service, or cause.|例句：The website offers limited content to nonsubscribers.  该网站为非订阅者提供有限的内容。|近义词：nonmember, outsider|反义词:subscriber, member|词性:名词
Etymology: non- + subscriber.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "subscriber" refers to someone who subscribes.
