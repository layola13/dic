over-the-counter:/ˌoʊvər ðə ˈkaʊntər/ | adj. | 非处方的，直接销售的 | Available without a prescription; bought directly by a customer. |  You can buy many effective painkillers over-the-counter. 你可以在非处方药柜台上买到许多有效的止痛药。|  Many allergy medications are available over-the-counter. 许多抗过敏药物是非处方药。| Synonyms: nonprescription, readily available | Antonyms: prescription, restricted | adjective

over-the-counter:/ˌoʊvər ðə ˈkaʊntər/ | n. | 场外交易市场 | A market for securities not listed on an organized exchange. | The company's shares are traded over-the-counter. 该公司的股票在场外交易市场交易。 |  Over-the-counter derivatives can be risky. 场外交易衍生品可能存在风险。 | Synonyms: OTC | Antonyms: exchange-traded | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"Over the counter" originally referred to goods sold directly to customers "over" a shop counter, as opposed to goods requiring special orders or prescriptions. This meaning expanded to include medications that could be purchased without a prescription.
