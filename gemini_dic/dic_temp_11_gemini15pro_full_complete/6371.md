amphibrach:/ˈæmfɪbræk/ | n. | 短长格，抑扬抑格 | A metrical foot consisting of a short syllable between two long syllables or an unstressed syllable between two stressed syllables. |  The word "amphibrach" itself is an amphibrach.  这个单词"amphibrach"本身就是一个短长格。
