outsource:/ˈaʊtsɔːrs/| v. |外包，外购|To obtain (goods or a service) from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source.|Many companies outsource their IT operations to specialized firms. 许多公司将他们的IT运营外包给专业公司。|The company outsources its customer service. 这家公司将其客户服务外包。|近义词:contract out, subcontract|反义词:insource|词性:动词

outspurn:/ˌaʊtˈspɜːrn/| v. |超过，胜过 (尤指速度或距离)|To surpass or outrun, especially in speed or distance, by spurring or urging forward.|The young athlete outspurned his rivals in the final stretch of the race.  这位年轻的运动员在比赛的最后阶段超过了他的对手。|He outspurned the other runners and won the race. 他超过了其他选手，赢得了比赛。|近义词:outrun, outstrip, surpass, exceed|反义词:lag behind, trail|词性:动词
