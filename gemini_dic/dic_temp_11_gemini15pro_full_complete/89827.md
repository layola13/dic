hornet:/ˈhɔːrnɪt/ (US), /ˈhɔːnɪt/ (UK)| n. |大黄蜂，马蜂|A large social wasp with a powerful sting.|例句：A hornet stung me in the garden.  一只大黄蜂在花园里蜇了我。

例句：Hornets build large paper nests in trees. 大黄蜂在树上筑造大型的纸巢。|近义词：wasp, yellow jacket|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
