crematoriums:/krˌiːməˈtɔːriəmz/
n.
火葬场；焚尸炉
A place where a dead person's body is burned to ashes.
The city's crematorium was overwhelmed during the pandemic.  这座城市的火葬场在大流行期间不堪重负。
Many people choose cremation over burial. 许多人选择火葬而不是土葬。
synonyms: crematory, incinerator
antonyms: burial ground, cemetery
noun
