flora:/ˈflɔːrə/ (US), /ˈflɔːrə/ (UK)| n. |植物群，植物区系|Plants considered as a group, especially the plants of a particular region or time.|例句：The flora of the Amazon rainforest is incredibly diverse. 亚马逊雨林的植物群非常多样。

例句：The flora of this island is unique.  这个岛的植物群很独特。


近义词：vegetation, plant life|反义词:fauna|词性:noun
