overcooled:/ˌoʊvərˈkuːld/ | adj. | 过冷的，冷却过度的 | Cooled below the optimum temperature. | The engine was overcooled due to a faulty thermostat. 发动机由于恒温器故障而过冷。| The overcooled metal became brittle. 过冷的金属变脆了。 | 近义词：supercooled, overchilled | 反义词：overheated, superheated | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix) + cooled (past participle of cool)
"over-" 表示 "过度"，"cooled" 表示 "冷却的"，因此 "overcooled" 表示 "过度冷却的"。
