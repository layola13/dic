anastrophe:/əˈnæstrəfi/ | n. | 倒装; 词序颠倒 |  A rhetorical device involving the inversion of the usual order of words.  It is a form of hyperbaton.  |Blessed are the meek. (Normal: The meek are blessed.)  蒙福的是温柔的人。（正常语序：温柔的人是蒙福的。）| Near Synonyms: inversion, hyperbaton | Antonyms:  natural word order | noun
