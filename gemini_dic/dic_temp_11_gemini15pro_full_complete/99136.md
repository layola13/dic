interceptive:/ˌɪntərˈsɛptɪv/| adj.|拦截的，截取的| Designed to intercept or interrupt the course of something.| The interceptive missile successfully destroyed the incoming threat. 拦截导弹成功摧毁了来袭的威胁。|Synonyms:  intercepting, obstructive, preventive|Antonyms:  permissive, allowing, facilitating|adjective

interceptive:/ˌɪntərˈsɛptɪv/| adj.|感知的，察觉的 (specifically relating to internal bodily sensations)|Relating to the perception of internal bodily sensations.|  Interceptive awareness allows us to recognize feelings of hunger or thirst. 感知意识使我们能够识别饥饿或口渴的感觉。|Synonyms:  visceral, sensory, internal|Antonyms:  external,  unaware|adjective
