broguery:/ˈbroʊɡəriː/
n.
仿古鞋；粗革皮鞋制造；（尤指）粗革皮鞋制造业
The manufacture or trade of brogues, especially thick-soled shoes.
The town was known for its broguery.  这个小镇以其粗革皮鞋制造业而闻名。
近义词: shoemaking, footwear manufacture
反义词: None (lack of a direct opposite concept)
词性: noun


词源: From "brogue" (a kind of shoe) + "-ery" (a suffix denoting a place of business, trade, or collection). "Brogue" itself comes from the Irish word "bróg" meaning "shoe".
