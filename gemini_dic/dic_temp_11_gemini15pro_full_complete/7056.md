anemology:/ˌænɪˈmɒlədʒi/ | n. |风学；风的学说|The study of winds, specifically their direction, force, and velocity.  It encompasses the causes of winds, their effects on weather and climate, and their measurement. |The professor of anemology explained the formation of hurricanes.  教授讲解了飓风的形成。|近义词：aerology|反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially consider stillness, calmness related to air.)| noun
