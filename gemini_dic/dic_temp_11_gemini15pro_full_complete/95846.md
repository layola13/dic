incorresponding:/ˌɪnkɔːrɪˈspɑːndɪŋ/
adjective
不一致的，不相应的
Not matching or agreeing; not consistent or equivalent.
The two versions of the story were incorresponding, with major differences in key details. 这两个版本的故事不一致，关键细节上有重大差异。
The data from the two studies were incorresponding, making it difficult to draw any firm conclusions.  两项研究的数据不一致，因此难以得出任何确凿的结论。
近义词: dissimilar, different, conflicting, divergent, disagreeing, inconsistent
反义词: corresponding, similar, consistent, matching, agreeing, equivalent
词性: adjective

词源:
"in-" (prefix meaning "not") + "corresponding" (from Latin "correspondere," meaning "to agree with")
