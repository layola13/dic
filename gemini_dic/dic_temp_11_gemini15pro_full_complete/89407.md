homotaxic:/ˌhɒməˈtæksɪk/ | adj. |同位的，同轴的|Having the same taxonomic arrangement or position.  Referring to geological formations or fossil assemblages that are found in the same stratigraphic position or level and are therefore considered to be of the same age.|The homotaxic nature of the two fossil deposits suggested they were from the same period. 这两个化石矿床的同位性表明它们来自同一时期。|Near synonyms: contemporaneous, coeval, synchronous|Antonym: heterotaxic|adjective
