mechanises:/ˈmɛkənaɪzɪz/
verb
使机械化;用机械装置
equips with machinery, especially to replace manual labor.
The factory mechanised its production line.  这家工厂将其生产线机械化了。
The army has mechanised its infantry.  军队已经机械化了它的步兵。
automate, motorize
manualize
v.
