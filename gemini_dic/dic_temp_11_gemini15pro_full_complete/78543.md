ghostlier:/ˈɡəʊstliər/ (US) /ˈɡoʊstliər/| adj.|更鬼魅的，更幽灵般的|More resembling or suggestive of a ghost; more unearthly or spectral.|The old house felt ghostlier at night.  这座老房子在晚上感觉更鬼魅了。|The abandoned amusement park had a ghostlier atmosphere than usual. 这个废弃的游乐园的气氛比平常更诡异。|Near synonyms: spookier, eerier, more spectral, more wraithlike|Near antonyms: less spooky, less eerie, more earthly, more tangible|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old English *gāstlic, from gāst "ghost, spirit" + -lic "-ly".  The comparative form "ghostlier" is formed by adding "-er" to the base adjective "ghostly".
