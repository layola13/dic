beggarly:/ˈbeɡərli/| adj.|贫穷的，乞丐似的，低劣的|Resembling or characteristic of a beggar; mean or contemptible.  Characterized by extreme poverty or scarcity.|He offered a beggarly sum for the antique vase. 他为那只古董花瓶出的价钱低得可怜。|Synonyms: paltry, meager, pitiful, despicable, contemptible|Antonyms: generous, lavish, opulent, substantial|adjective

beggar:/ˈbeɡər/| n. v.|乞丐，乞讨，使贫穷|A person who lives by asking for money or food. To reduce to beggary; impoverish.|The beggar asked for spare change. 乞丐讨要零钱。 |He was beggared by the lawsuit.  他被这场官司搞得倾家荡产。|Synonyms: mendicant, pauper, supplicant (n.), impoverish, ruin (v.)|Antonyms: millionaire, tycoon (n.), enrich (v.)|noun, verb
