keepable:/ˈkiːpəbl/| adj.|可保留的，值得保留的|Something that is keepable is worth keeping or remembering.|This information is not really keepable. 这条信息其实不值得保留。|近义词：retainable, preservable, worth keeping|反义词：disposable, dispensable|词性:形容词

词源: From "keep" (verb) + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").  "Keep" comes from Old English "cēpan" meaning "to observe, attend to, take care of."
