nonobsession:/ˌnɑːnəbˈseʃən/
n.
缺乏执念，不痴迷
The state of not being obsessed with something.
He demonstrated a healthy nonobsession with material possessions. 他表现出对物质财富的健康的不痴迷。
non-attachment, disinterest, indifference
obsession, fixation, preoccupation
noun
