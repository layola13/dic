bivolo:/baɪˈvoʊloʊ/
n.
双翼飞机，复翼机
A biplane.  An airplane with two main supporting surfaces, one above the other.
The bivolo soared through the air, a nostalgic reminder of early aviation. 这架双翼飞机翱翔在空中，让人怀念起早期的航空时代。
The pilot skillfully maneuvered the bivolo. 飞行员熟练地驾驶着双翼飞机。
synonyms: biplane
antonyms: monoplane
noun
