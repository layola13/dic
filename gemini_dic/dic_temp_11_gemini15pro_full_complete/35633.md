coatings:/ˈkoʊtɪŋz/
n.
涂层，覆盖层
A thin layer or covering spread over a surface.
The car has a new protective coating. 这辆车有一层新的保护涂层。

近义词:covering, layer, film, finish
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "substrate" or "base")
词性:Noun (plural)

coatings:/ˈkoʊtɪŋz/
n.
(布料的)涂层，覆层
A substance applied to fabric to make it waterproof, stain-resistant, etc.
This jacket has a waterproof coating. 这件夹克有防水涂层。

近义词:finish, treatment
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:Noun (plural)


词源: From the verb "coat," which comes from Old French "cote" meaning "tunic, coat of mail."  The suffix "-ing" creates the noun form, referring to the act of coating or the material used for coating.
