overdilating: /ˌoʊvərdaɪˈleɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度扩张；过度放大
expanding or widening excessively.
The ophthalmologist cautioned against overdilating the patient's pupils. 眼科医生告诫不要过度放大病人的瞳孔。
The drug can cause overdilating of the blood vessels. 这种药会导致血管过度扩张。

近义词: overexpanding, overstretching, overwidening
反义词: constricting, contracting, narrowing
词性: verb (present participle)

词源:
From over- + dilating, the present participle of dilate.
Dilate comes from the Latin word "dilatare," meaning "to spread out." The prefix "over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much."
