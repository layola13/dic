crocodilia:/ˌkrɒkəˈdɪliə/ | n. |鳄目动物；鳄鱼类 | An order of large reptiles, including crocodiles, alligators, caimans, and gharials, characterized by long snouts, powerful jaws, and a body covered in scales. | Crocodilia are found in tropical and subtropical regions. 鳄目动物分布在热带和亚热带地区。|  Crocodiles and alligators are both members of the Crocodilia order. 鳄鱼和短吻鳄都是鳄目动物。| synonyms: crocodylians | antonyms: N/A | noun
