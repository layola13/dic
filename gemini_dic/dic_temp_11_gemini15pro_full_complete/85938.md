headlong:/ˈhedlɒŋ/ | adj. adv. | 轻率的；慌慌张张的；头朝下的 |  Reckless; hasty; headfirst. |  She made a headlong dive into the pool. 她一头扎进了游泳池。 | 近义词：reckless, hasty, rashly, headfirst | 反义词: cautious, careful, deliberate | 词性: 形容词/副词


词源: From Middle English *hedlong, from Old English *hēafodlang "headlong," from hēafod "head" + lang "long."  The original sense was probably "with the head foremost," as in diving. Over time, the meaning extended to include recklessness and haste.
