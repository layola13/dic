mentorship:/ˈmentɔːrʃɪp/ |n.| 师徒关系；指导；导师制 |A relationship in which a more experienced or knowledgeable person helps to guide a less experienced or knowledgeable person.|  She benefited greatly from his mentorship. 她从他的指导中获益匪浅。| synonyms: guidance, tutelage, coaching | antonyms: neglect, abandonment | noun
