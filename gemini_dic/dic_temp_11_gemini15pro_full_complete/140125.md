off-the-air:/ˌɔːf ði ˈeər/ | adj. | 停播的，停止播送的 | Not being broadcast on television or radio. | The show went off-the-air after five seasons.  这个节目播了五季后停播了。 | Synonyms:  unbroadcast, not on the air | Antonyms: on-the-air, broadcasting | adjective
off-the-air:/ˌɔːf ði ˈeər/ | adv. | 私下地，非公开地 | In private; not publicly. |  He told me off-the-air that he was planning to resign. 他私下告诉我他打算辞职。| Synonyms: privately, confidentially, off the record | Antonyms: publicly, on the record | adverb
