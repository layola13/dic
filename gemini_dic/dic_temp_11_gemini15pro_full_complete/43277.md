craftsman:/ˈkræftsmən/ (US), /ˈkrɑːftsmən/ (UK)| n. |工匠；手艺人|A skilled worker who makes things by hand, especially decorative or artistic objects.|The craftsman carefully carved the wood into a beautiful statue.  这位工匠小心翼翼地把木头雕刻成一尊美丽的雕像。|近义词：artisan, artificer, maker, smith|反义词：amateur, novice|noun

craftsman:/ˈkræftsmən/ (US), /ˈkrɑːftsmən/ (UK)| n. |技工|A skilled worker in a particular craft or trade.|He was a skilled craftsman who could build anything out of wood. 他是一位技艺精湛的工匠，可以用木头建造任何东西。|近义词：technician, mechanic, journeyman|反义词：amateur, novice|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *craftesman*, equivalent to craft + -s- + man.
