lipless:/ˈlɪplɪs/| adj.|无唇的，没有嘴唇的|Lacking lips; having no lips.  Describes something or someone without lips or with lips that are not visible or functional.|The fish appeared lipless, its mouth a gaping hole. 这条鱼看起来没有嘴唇，它的嘴像个大洞。|The stroke left him lipless and unable to speak clearly. 中风使他失去了嘴唇，无法清晰地说话。|Synonym: lip-less|Antonym: full-lipped|adjective
