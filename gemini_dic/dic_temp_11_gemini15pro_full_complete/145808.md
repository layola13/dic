overdedicated:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度投入的，过度奉献的 | Excessively dedicated; devoted to a cause or task beyond what is reasonable or healthy.  Characterized by devoting too much time and energy to something. | She was so overdedicated to her work that she neglected her family. 她过度投入于工作，以至于忽略了她的家人。|  He's an overdedicated teacher, often working late into the night. 他是一位过度投入的老师，经常工作到深夜。 |近义词：overzealous, obsessive, fanatical | 反义词：apathetic, indifferent, uncommitted | 词性: 形容词
