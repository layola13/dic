multitudinary:/ˌmʌltɪˈtjuːdɪnəri/
adjective
大量的；群众的；由众多人组成的
Consisting of or containing a multitude; very numerous.
The multitudinary crowds at the concert made it difficult to move.  演唱会上人山人海，寸步难行。
Multitudinary stars filled the night sky. 无数的星星布满了夜空。
近义词: numerous, many, countless, innumerable, myriad
反义词: few, scarce, sparse
词性: adjective

multitudinary:/ˌmʌltɪˈtjuːdɪnəri/
noun
群众；大众
A multitude; a great number of people.
The multitudinary gathered in the square to protest. 群众聚集在广场上抗议。
He addressed the multitudinary with a rousing speech. 他向群众发表了激动人心的演讲。
近义词: crowd, throng, mass, host
反义词: few, handful
词性: noun


词源:
From Late Latin *multitudinarius, from Latin multitudo (“multitude”) + -arius.
