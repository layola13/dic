eunomian:/juːˈnoʊmiən/ | adj. | 合法的；井然有序的；符合法律的；受法律约束的 |  Of or relating to good order, especially good laws; lawful.  Derived from Greek *eunomia* "good order," from *eu-* "good" + *nomos* "law." | The eunomian government ensured stability and prosperity.  这个依法治理的政府确保了稳定和繁荣。 | Synonyms: lawful, legal, legitimate, orderly, regular | Antonyms: unlawful, illegal, illegitimate, disorderly, chaotic | adjective
