buller:/ˈbʊlər/| n.| 矿石筛选机; 筛选矿石的人;粗筛; 振动筛; |A machine or person that separates ore. The buller removed the unwanted rocks from the valuable minerals. 筛选机从有价值的矿物中去除了不需要的岩石。|近义词：screener, sifter|反义词: N/A |noun
buller:/ˈbʊlər/| n.| 筛选出的废物, 废石; |Waste material separated from ore. The buller was discarded after the valuable minerals were extracted. 筛选出的废料在提取了有价值的矿物后被丢弃了。|近义词：tailings, waste, refuse|反义词: ore |noun
