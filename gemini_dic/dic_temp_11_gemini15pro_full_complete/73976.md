fortitude:/ˈfɔːrtɪtjuːd/ (US) /ˈfɔːtɪtʃuːd/ (UK)| n. |勇气，坚韧，刚毅|Mental and emotional strength in facing difficulty, adversity, danger, or pain courageously.|例句：She endured the hardship with great fortitude. 她以极大的毅力忍受了苦难。|近义词：courage, bravery, resilience, resolve, strength, grit|反义词：cowardice, fear, weakness, timidity|词性:名词
