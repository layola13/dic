impel:/ɪmˈpel/ (US), /ɪmˈpɛl/ (UK)| v. |推进；驱使；激励|To drive, force, or urge (someone) to do something.|Curiosity impelled her to ask more questions. 好奇心促使她问更多的问题。|近义词：urge, compel, drive, prompt, motivate|反义词：deter, restrain, discourage|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *impellere*, from *in- "into, towards"* + *pellere "to push, drive."*
