discarded:/dɪˈskɑːrdɪd/
adjective
丢弃的，废弃的
Something that is discarded has been thrown away or rejected as useless or unwanted.
The street was littered with discarded newspapers. 街上到处都是丢弃的报纸。
Synonyms: abandoned, rejected, dumped, jettisoned
Antonyms: kept, saved, treasured, preserved
词性: 形容词


discarded:/dɪˈskɑːrd/
verb
丢弃，抛弃
To discard something is to throw it away or get rid of it because you no longer want or need it.
He discarded his old clothes. 他丢弃了他的旧衣服。
Synonyms: dispose of, throw away, get rid of, abandon
Antonyms: keep, save, retain, preserve
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old French word "descarter," meaning "to discard," which ultimately comes from the Latin "carta," meaning "card."  Originally, the word referred to discarding a playing card.
