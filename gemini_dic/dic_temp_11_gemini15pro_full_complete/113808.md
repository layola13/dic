mad:/mæd/
adjective
疯狂的；生气的；着迷的
insane; angry; enthusiastic
He was mad with grief. 他悲痛欲绝。
She's mad about horses. 她对马着迷。
近义词:crazy, insane, furious, angry, enthusiastic
反义词:sane, rational, calm, pleased

mad:/mæd/
adjective
发疯的；狂怒的；狂热的
mentally ill; extremely angry; very enthusiastic
He went mad after the accident. 事故发生后，他疯了。
She was mad at him for forgetting her birthday. 她因为他忘了她的生日而对他很生气。
近义词:insane, crazy, furious, irate, passionate
反义词:sane, calm, composed


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gemæded "made, created, constructed, composed; acted, done," past participle of *gemædan (related to mædan "to make"), from Proto-Germanic *gamadojan, from PIE root *ma- (4) "to measure, fit, fashion." Sense of "insane" is from c. 1300.
