hagged:/ˈhæɡɪd/| adj.|憔悴的，像巫婆的|Looking exhausted and worn, as if by hardship or age.  Appearing like a hag, especially in being thin, gaunt, and wrinkled.|例句：She looked haggard after her sleepless night. 她一夜没睡，看起来很憔悴。|例句：The haggard old woman hobbled down the street.  那个憔悴的老妇人蹒跚地走在街上。|近义词：worn, gaunt, drawn, exhausted, careworn|反义词：youthful, radiant, refreshed|词性:形容词
