nonmelodically:/ˌnɒn məˈlɒdɪkli/ /ˌnɑːn məˈlɑːdɪkli/
adverb
非旋律地；无旋律地
Without melody; in a way that is not melodic.
The piece was played nonmelodically, focusing on rhythm and texture. 这首曲子演奏得没有旋律，注重节奏和质感。
The avant-garde composer often used instruments nonmelodically, creating unusual soundscapes.  这位先锋派作曲家经常不按旋律使用乐器，创造出不寻常的声音景观。
Synonyms: atonally, unmelodically, dissonantly
Antonyms: melodically, tunefully, harmoniously
词性: 副词
