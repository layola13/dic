limitational:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
限制性的，局限性的
Relating to or imposing limitations.
His limitational beliefs prevented him from pursuing his dreams.  他的局限性信念阻碍了他追求梦想。
The study had several limitational factors, such as a small sample size. 这项研究有几个限制性因素，比如样本量小。
近义词: restrictive, confining, limiting
反义词: expansive, unlimited, unrestricted
词性: adjective

词源: From limitation + -al.  来自 limitation（限制） + -al（形容词后缀）。
