half-brother:/ˈhæf ˌbrʌðər/ |n.|同父异母或同母异父的兄弟|A male sibling with whom one shares only one parent.| My mother remarried, and I now have a half-brother. 我母亲再婚了，现在我有一个同母异父的兄弟。|
John and David are half-brothers; they share the same father but have different mothers. 约翰和大卫是同父异母的兄弟；他们有同一个父亲，但母亲不同。|stepbrother, half sibling|full brother|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"half" + "brother"
"half" (Old English healf) meaning "one of two equal parts"
"brother" (Old English broþor) meaning "male sibling"
