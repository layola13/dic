fudge:/fʌdʒ/| v. n.|捏造，篡改；敷衍；回避；(吃)软糖|To manipulate or falsify something, often data or figures; to present something inaccurately or deceptively. Also, a type of soft candy.|He fudged the numbers in the report to make the results look better. 他篡改了报告中的数字，让结果看起来更好。|Synonyms: fake, falsify, manipulate, doctor, cheat, fudge, cook,  |Antonyms: verify, authenticate, confirm|verb
fudge:/fʌdʒ/| n.|软糖；胡说；废话|A type of soft candy, typically made with sugar, butter, and milk. Also, nonsense; rubbish.|She made a batch of delicious chocolate fudge. 她做了一批美味的巧克力软糖。|Synonyms: nonsense, rubbish, balderdash|Antonyms: sense, truth|noun
