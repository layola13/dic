damn with faint praise:/ˌdæm wɪð feɪnt preɪz/
verb phrase
贬低；明褒实贬；用轻微的赞扬来贬低
To criticize someone or something indirectly by giving a compliment that is not enthusiastic or sincere.
He damned the play with faint praise, saying it was "not bad." 他用“还不错”这样轻描淡写的赞扬贬低了这部戏。
近义词: backhanded compliment, left-handed compliment, criticize subtly
反义词: praise enthusiastically, wholeheartedly endorse
词性: verb phrase
