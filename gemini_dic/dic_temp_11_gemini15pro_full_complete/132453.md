nondepravity:/ˌnɒndɪˈprævəti/ | n. |  纯洁，善良，正直 | The state or quality of not being depraved; lack of corruption or wickedness. |  His nondepravity was evident in his charitable works and honest dealings.  他的纯洁善良体现在他的慈善事业和诚实的行为中。 | 近义词: goodness, virtue, righteousness, morality, integrity, decency, probity | 反义词: depravity, wickedness, corruption, evil, immorality | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + depravity (from Latin *depravare* "to corrupt")
