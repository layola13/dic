ettle:/ˈɛtəl/| v.|安顿；定居；解决；决定|To establish oneself in a place; to settle down.  To resolve or decide something.|He finally settled in a small town in the countryside. 他最终在乡下一个小镇安顿下来。

She settled the dispute between her two children. 她解决了两个孩子之间的争端。|近义词：settle, resolve, decide, determine|反义词: unsettle, disturb|词性:动词
