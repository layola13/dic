disserviceable:/dɪsˈsɜːvɪsəbəl/
adjective
有害的，帮倒忙的
causing harm or damage; not helpful
Her comments were disserviceable to the cause, alienating potential supporters. 她的评论对这项事业有害，疏远了潜在的支持者。
The rumors spread about him were highly disserviceable to his reputation.  关于他的谣言对他的名誉非常不利。
近义词: harmful, detrimental, injurious, counterproductive, unhelpful
反义词: helpful, beneficial, advantageous, useful
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- + serviceable.  "Dis-" is a prefix indicating negation or reversal, and "serviceable" means useful or able to provide good service.
