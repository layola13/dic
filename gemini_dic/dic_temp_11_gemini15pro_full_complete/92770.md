ichthyosaurus:/ˌɪkθiəˈsɔːrəs/ | n. | 鱼龙 | An extinct marine reptile resembling a dolphin, with a long pointed head, four flippers, and a vertical tail fin.  It lived during the Mesozoic Era. | The ichthyosaurus fossil was remarkably well-preserved.  这块鱼龙化石保存得非常好。 |  The museum displayed a complete ichthyosaurus skeleton.  博物馆展出了一具完整的鱼龙骨架。 | synonym: fish-lizard, dolphin-like reptile (informal) | antonym: None (as it's an extinct species, there's no direct opposite) | noun
