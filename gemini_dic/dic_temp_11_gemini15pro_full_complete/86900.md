hemisystole:/ˌhɛmiˈsɪstəli/
noun
半收缩，半搏
Half a systole; specifically, a perceptible shortening of the interval between two pulse beats, sometimes alternating with a normal interval.
The doctor detected a hemisystole in the patient's heartbeat. 医生在病人的心跳中检测到半收缩。
近义词: partial systole
反义词: diastole
词性: n.


Etymology:
From hemi- + systole.  Hemi- comes from Greek hēmi- "half". Systole comes from Greek systolē "contraction".
