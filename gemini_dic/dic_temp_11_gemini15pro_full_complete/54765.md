dispathy:/dɪsˈpæθi/
n.
lack of sympathy; indifference
A lack of sympathy or interest; indifference.
He showed a dispathy towards her suffering. 他对她的痛苦漠不关心。
近义词:indifference, apathy, unconcern, aloofness
反义词:sympathy, empathy, compassion, concern
词性:名词

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + -pathy (feeling, suffering).  Ultimately from Greek *dyspátheia*, from *dys-* (bad, ill) + *páthos* (suffering, feeling).
