magnetic-inclination:/mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 磁倾角 | The angle between the direction of the Earth's magnetic field and the horizontal plane at a given location.  It's also known as the dip angle. | The magnetic inclination at the magnetic poles is 90 degrees. 地球磁极的磁倾角是90度。

The magnetic inclination is zero at the magnetic equator. 磁赤道的磁倾角为零。 | Synonyms: dip angle, magnetic dip | Antonyms: N/A | noun
