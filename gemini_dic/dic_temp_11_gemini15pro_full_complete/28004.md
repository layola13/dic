caoutchouc:/ˈkaʊtʃʊk/ | n. | 橡胶，天然橡胶 |  A tough, elastic substance obtained from the latex of certain tropical trees, especially Hevea brasiliensis (the Pará rubber tree).  It is the primary source of natural rubber. |  The indigenous people of South America used caoutchouc to make waterproof shoes. 南美洲的土著人用橡胶制作防水鞋。 |  The tires are made of caoutchouc. 这些轮胎是用橡胶做的。| synonyms: rubber, latex, gum elastic | antonyms: N/A | noun
