mourners:/ˈmɔːrnərz/| n.| 哀悼者，送葬者|People who mourn the death of someone. |例句：The mourners gathered at the graveside. 哀悼者聚集在墓旁。

例句：The mourners wore black clothes. 送葬者穿着黑色的衣服。|近义词：grievers, wailers|反义词:celebrators|Noun
