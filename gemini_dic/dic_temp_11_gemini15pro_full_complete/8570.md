anticipatory:/ænˈtɪsɪpətɔːri/
adjective
预期的，预料的
Characterized by anticipation; expecting something.
She gave an anticipatory smile. 她露出了一个期待的微笑。
anticipatory buying  预期性购买
synonyms: expectant, expected, foreseen, predictable
antonyms: unexpected, unforeseen, surprising
