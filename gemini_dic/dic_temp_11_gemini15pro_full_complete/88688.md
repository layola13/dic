hobber:/ˈhɒbə(r)/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbər/ (US)| n.|滚齿机；滚刀|A machine tool for cutting toothed gears using a hob.|The hobber is essential for producing gears with precise teeth.  滚齿机对于生产齿轮的精密齿至关重要。|The factory uses a high-precision hobber for gear manufacturing.  这家工厂使用高精度滚齿机制造齿轮。|Synonyms: gear cutting machine, gear generator|Antonyms: N/A|noun
