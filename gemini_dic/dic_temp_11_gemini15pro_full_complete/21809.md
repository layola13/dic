bobtail:/ˈbɒbteɪl/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbteɪl/ (US)| v.|截短尾巴|To cut short the tail of an animal.|They decided to bobtail their dog's tail. 他们决定剪短狗的尾巴。|近义词：dock|反义词: - |词性:动词
