inquest:/ˈɪŋkwɛst/| n.|调查，审讯(尤指死因)|An inquest is a formal inquiry, especially held before a jury, to determine the cause of a sudden, violent, or unexplained death.|The inquest into the death of the young woman returned a verdict of accidental death. 年轻女子的死因调查裁定为意外死亡。|Synonyms: inquiry, investigation, hearing, examination|Antonyms: cover-up, concealment|noun
