ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin eucharistia, from Ancient Greek εὐχαριστία (eukharistía, “gratitude, thanksgiving”), from εὐχάριστος (eukháristos, “grateful”), from εὖ (eû, “well”) + χάρις (kháris, “grace, favor”).
