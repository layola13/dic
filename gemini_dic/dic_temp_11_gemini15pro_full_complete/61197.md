eluxate: /ɪˈlʌkseɪt/ | v. |  使奢华，使豪华；洗脱（罪名） | To make luxurious or elegant; to clear from guilt or blame. | The company eluxated the hotel rooms with expensive furnishings. 这家公司用昂贵的家具使酒店房间变得奢华。  The lawyer worked tirelessly to eluxate his client. 律师不知疲倦地工作，为他客户洗脱罪名。 | 近义词: enrich, adorn, embellish, exonerate, acquit | 反义词: impoverish, strip, incriminate, condemn | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin *eluxatus*, past participle of *eluxare*, from *ex-* (out) + *lux-* (light, luxury).  最初的意思是“带出光明”，引申为“使奢华”，后来又引申为“洗脱（罪名），使清白”。
