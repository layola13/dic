jawless:/ˈdʒɔːləs/| adj.|无颌的，没有颌的|Lacking jaws.  Describing animals that do not have jaws, such as lampreys and hagfish.|Lampreys are jawless fish. 七鳃鳗是无颌鱼。|Jawless fish are primitive vertebrates. 无颌鱼是原始脊椎动物。|近义词:agnathan|反义词:jawed|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From jaw + -less.  "Jaw" comes from Old English "ceowan" (to chew).  "-less" is a suffix indicating absence or lack of something. 词源：来自 jaw + -less。“Jaw”来自古英语“ceowan”（咀嚼）。“-less”是一个后缀，表示没有或缺乏某物。
