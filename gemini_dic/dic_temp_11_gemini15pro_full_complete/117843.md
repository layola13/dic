mellon:/ˈmɛlən/| n.|甜瓜，蜜瓜|(Archaic or literary) A fruit, especially a watermelon or muskmelon, or the plant that bears it.  From the Greek μηλον (mēlon).|The knight brought forth a ripe mellon.|Synonyms: muskmelon, cantaloupe, watermelon|Antonyms: None (fruit doesn't typically have direct antonyms)|Noun
