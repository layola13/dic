bel:/bɛl/| n.|贝尔（响度单位）|A unit of measurement of sound intensity, equal to ten decibels.  It is a logarithmic unit expressing the ratio of two powers or two like quantities proportional to power, and equal to the common logarithm of this ratio.|The sound was measured at 10 bels.|近义词:decibel (dB) (more commonly used)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
