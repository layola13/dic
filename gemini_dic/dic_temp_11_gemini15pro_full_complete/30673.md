cerotene: /ˈsɛrəˌtiːn/ | n. |鲸蜡烯，二十六烷|A long-chain alkane hydrocarbon, C₂₆H₅₄, found in beeswax and other natural waxes. It's a white, waxy solid at room temperature.|Cerotene is a component of many waxes. 鲸蜡烯是许多蜡的成分。| Cerotene is often used in cosmetics. 鲸蜡烯常用于化妆品。|Synonyms: hexacosane |Antonyms: N/A|noun
