biriyani:/bɪrˈjɑːni/ /ˌbɪriˈɑːni/| n.| 印度香饭，手抓饭 |A mixed rice dish, originating from the Indian subcontinent, made with spices, meat (such as chicken, goat, mutton, prawn, or fish), eggs or vegetables such as potatoes in certain regional varieties.|I had a delicious chicken biriyani for lunch. 我午餐吃了一份美味的鸡肉香饭。|近义词：pulao, pilaf|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps plain rice) |词性:noun
