instantnesses:/ˈɪnstəntnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
瞬间性；即时性
The quality or state of being instantaneous; immediacy.
The instantnesses of modern communication allow news to travel around the world in seconds. 现代通讯的即时性使新闻能够在几秒钟内传遍世界。
Synonyms: immediacy, instantaneousness, promptness
Antonyms: delay, slowness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From instant + -ness.  "Instant" comes from Late Middle English, from Latin instant-, instans, present participle of instare 'to press upon, be at hand', from in- 'on, upon' + stare 'to stand'.
