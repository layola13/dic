in the wake of: /ɪn ðə weɪk əv/ | prepositional phrase | 在…之后，随着…而来 | following something, especially something unpleasant or disastrous |  例句：Many social problems arose in the wake of the war. 战争之后出现了许多社会问题。| 近义词：following, after, subsequent to, as a result of, in the aftermath of | 反义词: preceding, prior to | 词性: prepositional phrase
Etymology: "wake" originally referred to the track left by a ship in the water.  The phrase evolved to mean the effects or consequences that follow an event.
