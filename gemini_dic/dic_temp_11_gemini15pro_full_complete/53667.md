discing:/ˈdɪskɪŋ/
verb
耕作；用圆盘耙耕地
The act of tilling or cultivating soil using a disc harrow.
The farmer was discing the field in preparation for planting. 农民正在用圆盘耙耕地，准备播种。
cultivating, plowing, tilling
harrowing, raking
