deoxidate:/diːˈɒksɪdeɪt/
verb
脱氧，还原
to remove oxygen from a substance, especially metal
The process deoxidates the molten steel.  这个过程可以去除钢水中的氧。
近义词:reduce, deoxydize
反义词:oxidize, oxygenate
词性:v.

deoxidization:/diːˌɒksɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
脱氧，还原
the process of removing oxygen from a substance
Deoxidization is crucial in steelmaking. 脱氧在炼钢过程中至关重要。
近义词:reduction
反义词:oxidation
词性:n.

deoxidizer:/diːˈɒksɪdaɪzər/
noun
脱氧剂
a substance used to remove oxygen
Aluminum is a common deoxidizer in metallurgy. 铝是冶金中常用的脱氧剂。
近义词:reducing agent
反义词:oxidizing agent
词性:n.

词源:  "de-" (Latin prefix meaning "remove" or "from") + "oxide" (a compound of oxygen with another element) + "-ate" (verb suffix indicating "to cause to become") or "-ization" (noun suffix indicating "the process of").
