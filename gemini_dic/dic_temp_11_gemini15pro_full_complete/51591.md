devalues:/diːˈvæljuːz/
verb
贬值，降低…的价值
to reduce the value of something, especially a currency, in relation to other currencies or commodities.
The government's decision to increase interest rates devalued the currency. 政府提高利率的决定导致货币贬值。
Cheap imports devalue domestic products. 廉价的进口商品使国内产品贬值。
synonyms: depreciate, cheapen, undermine, downgrade, diminish, reduce, lower, decrease
antonyms: appreciate, enhance, increase, raise, upgrade, improve
词性: 动词
