incontested:/ˌɪnkənˈtestɪd/| adj.| 无争议的；无异议的；未经争辩的|Not disputed or challenged; agreed upon.|The election was largely incontested.  这次选举基本上没有争议。|The will was incontested. 这份遗嘱无人质疑。|近义词：undisputed, unchallenged, conceded|反义词：contested, disputed, challenged|词性:形容词
incontestably:/ˌɪnkənˈtestəbli/| adv.| 无可争辩地；无疑地|In a way that cannot be disputed or challenged; undoubtedly.|He is incontestably the best player on the team. 他无疑是队里最好的球员。|She is incontestably guilty. 她无疑是有罪的。|近义词：undeniably, undoubtedly, indisputably|反义词：disputably, arguably|词性:副词
