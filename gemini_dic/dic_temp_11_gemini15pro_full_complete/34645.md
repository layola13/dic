clenching:/ˈklɛntʃɪŋ/| n. v.|紧握，咬紧，攥紧|The act of gripping or holding something tightly, often with the hands or teeth.  It can also refer to a tightening sensation in the muscles.|He injured his hand while clenching his fist in frustration. 他因沮丧地握紧拳头而伤了手。|synonyms: gripping, grasping, clutching|antonyms: releasing, loosening|verb/noun

clenching:/ˈklɛntʃɪŋ/| adj.|紧握的，咬紧的|Characterized by gripping or holding tightly.|Her clenching jaw betrayed her anxiety. 她紧咬的下巴暴露了她的焦虑。|synonyms: tight, firm, gripped|antonyms: loose, relaxed|adjective
