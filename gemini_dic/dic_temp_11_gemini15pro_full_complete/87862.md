hideouts:/ˈhaɪdaʊts/| n. | 藏身处，隐匿处 |A place where someone hides, especially from the police or from other people who are looking for them. | The police raided several hideouts looking for the escaped convicts. 警方突袭了几个藏身处，寻找逃犯。|  The rebels used the cave as a hideout. 叛军用这个洞穴作为藏身处。 | synonyms: refuge, shelter, sanctuary, den | antonyms: exposure, open area | noun
