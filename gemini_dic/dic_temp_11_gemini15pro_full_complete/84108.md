half-joking:/ˌhɑːf ˈdʒoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 半开玩笑地 | Partly joking; not entirely serious. | He made a half-joking remark about her new hairstyle. 他半开玩笑地评论了她的新发型。|  She half-jokingly suggested they elope. 她半开玩笑地建议他们私奔。 | Near synonyms:  playful, teasing, tongue-in-cheek | Antonyms: serious, earnest | adjective

Etymology->
From "half" + "joking" (present participle of "joke").  "Half" indicates a partial or incomplete state, so "half-joking" signifies that the joking is not fully serious or committed.
