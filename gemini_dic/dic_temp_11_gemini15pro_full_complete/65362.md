eulogizes: /ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzɪz/
verb
赞扬；颂扬；称赞
praises; extols; speaks or writes in high praise of.
The mayor eulogized the firefighters for their bravery. 市长赞扬了消防员们的英勇行为。
synonyms: praises, commends, extols, lauds, celebrates
antonyms: criticizes, condemns, denounces, disparages
词性: verb (动词)

eulogy: /ˈjuːlədʒi/
noun
悼词；颂词；赞词
a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died.
He delivered a moving eulogy at his father's funeral. 他在父亲的葬礼上致了感人的悼词。
synonyms: tribute, panegyric, encomium, accolade
antonyms: criticism, condemnation, denunciation
词性: noun (名词)
