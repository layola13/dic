arch-rival:/ˈɑːrtʃ ˈraɪvl/ | n. | 主要竞争对手，宿敌 |  A principal rival; a chief opponent.  |  The two teams have been arch-rivals for decades. 这两支队伍几十年来一直是宿敌。 | Synonyms: nemesis, chief competitor, main opponent | Antonyms: ally, friend, partner | noun

arch-rival:/ˈɑːrtʃ ˈraɪvl/ | adj. | 主要竞争的，宿敌的 |  Being a principal rival; of a chief opponent. |  Their arch-rival company just released a similar product.  他们主要的竞争对手公司刚刚发布了一款类似的产品。 | Synonyms: principal, chief, main | Antonyms: allied, friendly, cooperative | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From *arch-* (chief, principal) + *rival* (competitor). The *arch-* prefix comes from the Greek *arkhos* (leader, ruler). *Rival* comes from the Latin *rivalis* (using the same stream, hence, competitor).
