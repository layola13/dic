ergaster:/ərˈɡæstər/ | n. |  匠人（一种已灭绝的早期人类）| An extinct hominin species (or subspecies of Homo erectus) that lived in eastern and southern Africa during the early Pleistocene.  They are characterized by a larger braincase and more human-like limb proportions compared to earlier hominins. |  Example: *Homo ergaster* is considered by some to be the direct ancestor of *Homo erectus*.  匠人被一些人认为是直立人的直接祖先。

Example: The discovery of *Homo ergaster* fossils provided valuable insights into human evolution. 匠人化石的发现为人类进化提供了宝贵的见解。| 近义词: *Homo erectus* (some consider *ergaster* a subspecies of *erectus*)| 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
