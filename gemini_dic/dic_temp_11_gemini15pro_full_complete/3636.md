agent-noun:/ˈeɪdʒənt naʊn/| n. | 代理名词；施事名词 |A noun that denotes the doer of an action, typically the subject of a transitive verb.  It indicates the entity that performs or initiates the action expressed by the verb. |The **baker** made a delicious cake. 面包师做了一个美味的蛋糕。|Synonyms: actor, doer, performer|Antonyms: patient, undergoer, recipient |noun
