nonpollutants:/ˌnɑːnˈpəˈluːtənts/| n.|无污染物|Substances that do not pollute the environment.|Electric cars are promoted as using nonpollutants for fuel.|近义词:clean energy sources, eco-friendly materials|反义词:pollutants, contaminants|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".
pollutant: from pollute + -ant.
pollute: from Latin polluere "to defile, soil, stain," figuratively "to corrupt morally".
