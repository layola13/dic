deferential:/ˌdɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 恭敬的，顺从的 | Showing respect and politeness; respectful. |  She addressed the judge in a deferential tone. 她以恭敬的语气对法官讲话。

The young assistant was always deferential to his superiors.  年轻的助手对他的上司总是毕恭毕敬。


近义词: respectful, courteous, polite, submissive, obeisant | 反义词: disrespectful, rude, impolite, assertive, defiant | 词性: adjective

词源: Late 16th century: from medieval Latin deferentialis, from Latin deferre ‘carry down, submit’ (see defer).
