adagio:/əˈdɑːdʒioʊ/ | /əˈdɑːdʒiːoʊ/
n. | 缓慢的乐章或速度; 柔板 | A slow tempo or movement in music; a piece of music played in a slow tempo. | The second movement of the concerto was a beautiful adagio.  协奏曲的第二乐章是一段优美的柔板。| slow movement, lento | fast movement, allegro
adj. | 缓慢的; 柔板的 | Performed or played slowly; relating to a slow tempo. | The adagio melody created a peaceful atmosphere.  缓慢的旋律营造了宁静的氛围。| slow, leisurely | fast, rapid
