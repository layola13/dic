intervener:/ˌɪntərˈviːnər/| n. |干预者，介入者|A person who intervenes, especially in a lawsuit or conflict.|例句：The intervener in the dispute helped the two sides reach an agreement.  干预者帮助争议双方达成了协议。

例句：The court allowed the environmental group to act as an intervener in the case. 法院允许环保组织作为干预者参与此案。|近义词：mediator, arbitrator, negotiator, intercessor|反义词：bystander, onlooker|noun

intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/| v. |干预，介入，插话；干扰，阻挠；发生，出现|To come between so as to prevent or alter a result or course of events.|例句：The teacher intervened in the fight between the two students. 老师介入了两名学生之间的打架。

例句：He intervened to stop the argument from escalating. 他介入以阻止争吵升级。|近义词：interfere, mediate, arbitrate, intercede|反义词：ignore, abstain, withdraw|verb
