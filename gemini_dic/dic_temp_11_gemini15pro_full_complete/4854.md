alienation: /ˌeɪliəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 疏离，异化，离间 | The state of being isolated or estranged. A feeling of powerlessness and estrangement from society or other people.  |  His alienation from his family grew over the years. 他与家人的疏离感逐年加深。 | synonyms: estrangement, isolation, detachment, disaffection | antonyms: connection, integration, belonging | noun

alienation: /ˌeɪliəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 财产转让，让渡 | The transfer of the ownership of property rights. | The alienation of the land to the government caused an uproar among the locals.  土地被转让给政府后在当地居民中引起了轩然大波。 | synonyms: transfer, conveyance | antonyms: acquisition, retention | noun
