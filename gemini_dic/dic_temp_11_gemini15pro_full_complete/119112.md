metronomes:/ˈmɛtrəˌnoʊmz/ | n. | 节拍器 | A device that produces regular, metrical ticks (beats) at an adjustable tempo, used by musicians to practice playing to a steady rhythm. | The pianist used a metronome to practice the difficult passage. 钢琴家用节拍器练习困难的段落。| synonyms: timekeeper, beat machine, rhythm box | antonyms: N/A | noun
