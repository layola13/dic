naphthyl:/ˈnæfθɪl/ | adj. n. |萘基；萘的|Relating to or derived from naphthalene, a crystalline aromatic hydrocarbon.|1-Naphthyl acetic acid is a plant growth regulator.  1-萘乙酸是一种植物生长调节剂。

Naphthyl groups are present in many dyes.  许多染料中都存在萘基。|Synonyms: naphthalene-based (adj.) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps alkyl could be considered a contrasting group in organic chemistry) | adjective, noun
