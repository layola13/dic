cordial:/ˈkɔːrdʒəl/ (US), /ˈkɔːdiəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|热情友好的；兴奋的；由水果制成的甜饮料|Warm and friendly; stimulating.|例句：We received a cordial welcome from our hosts. 我们受到了主人热情的欢迎。|例句：She gave me a cordial smile. 她给了我一个热情的微笑。|近义词：warm, friendly, affable, amiable, genial, hearty|反义词：cold, unfriendly, hostile, aloof|词性:形容词

cordial:/ˈkɔːrdʒəl/ (US), /ˈkɔːdiəl/ (UK)| n.|由水果制成的甜饮料|A sweet fruit-flavored drink.|例句：She offered me a glass of homemade cordial. 她给了我一杯自制的甜饮料。|例句：The elderflower cordial was refreshing. 接骨木花甜饮料令人神清气爽。|近义词：drink, beverage, juice|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *cordial*, from Old French *cordial*, from Medieval Latin *cordiālis* (“of or for the heart”),  from Latin *cor*, *cordis* (“heart”).
