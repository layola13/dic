hearties:/ˈhɑːrtiz/| n.|伙伴们，同志们|A term of address for comrades or friends, often used in a jovial or affectionate way.  It's a plural form of "hearty".|例句：Ahoy there, hearties! Let's set sail!  喂，伙伴们！我们启航吧！
