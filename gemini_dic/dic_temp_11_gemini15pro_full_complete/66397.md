executer's:/ˈɛksɪˌkjuːtərz/
|n.|执行者的，遗嘱执行人的
|belonging to or relating to an executor (a person or institution appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of their will).|
|The executor's duties include distributing the assets of the estate.|遗嘱执行人的职责包括分配遗产的资产。
|The executor's commission was 5% of the estate's value.|遗嘱执行人的佣金是遗产价值的5%。
|近义词: administrator, trustee|
|反义词: heir, beneficiary|
|词性: possessive noun (所有格名词)|
