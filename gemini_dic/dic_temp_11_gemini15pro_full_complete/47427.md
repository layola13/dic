deadpanner:/ˈdɛdˌpænər/ (US), /ˈdɛdˌpænə/ (UK)| n.|面无表情的人；不动声色的人|A person who delivers jokes or funny lines with a serious, impassive expression.|He's a real deadpanner; you never know if he's joking. 他真是个面瘫，你永远不知道他是不是在开玩笑。|Synonyms: poker face, straight face, unemotional person|Antonyms: expressive person, animated person|noun

deadpan:/ˈdɛdˌpæn/ (US), /ˈdɛdˌpæn/ (UK)| adj. adv.|面无表情的；不动声色的|Delivered with a serious, impassive expression.|He delivered the joke in a completely deadpan way. 他以一种完全面无表情的方式讲了这个笑话。|Synonyms: expressionless, impassive, blank|Antonyms: expressive, animated, emotional|adjective, adverb
