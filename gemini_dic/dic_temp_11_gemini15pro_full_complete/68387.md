fallower:/ˈfæləʊər/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊər/ (US)| n. |追随者，信徒，拥护者|A person who supports or admires a particular person, cause, or activity; a person who follows another on a social media platform. |例句：She has a large fallower on Twitter. 她在推特上有很多粉丝。

例句：He is a fallower of the teachings of Buddha. 他是佛陀教义的信徒。|近义词：follower, adherent, supporter, devotee, fan|反义词：opponent, critic, detractor|词性:名词

词源: From "follow" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).  Ultimately from Old English *folgian "to follow."
