ingurgitate:/ɪnˈɡɜːrdʒɪteɪt/
verb
吞食，狼吞虎咽
to swallow greedily or in great quantity.
The python ingurgitated the whole rabbit. 巨蟒吞下了整只兔子。
ingurgitate large quantities of food  大量吞食食物
synonyms: devour, gulp, gobble, consume
antonyms: regurgitate, vomit
verb
