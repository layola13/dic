jounce:/dʒaʊns/| n. v.|颠簸，摇晃；使颠簸，使摇晃|A sudden jolt or bump. To move abruptly and roughly.|The car jounced along the rough road. 汽车在崎岖不平的道路上颠簸。|近义词：bounce, jolt, bump, shake|反义词:smooth, glide|noun, verb
Etymology:  From earlier *jounse, probably a variant of *jolt, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old French jolter.
