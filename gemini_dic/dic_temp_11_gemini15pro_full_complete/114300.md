mainstay:/ˈmeɪnsteɪ/| n. |支柱，主要依靠，中流砥柱|a key element, person, or thing that provides support or strength to something|Tourism is the mainstay of the local economy. 旅游业是当地经济的支柱。|synonyms: cornerstone, pillar, anchor, support, linchpin|antonyms: weakness, vulnerability|noun


mainstay:/ˈmeɪnsteɪ/| n. |主要依靠，主要支持|the chief support|Her children are her mainstay. 她的孩子们是她的精神支柱。|synonyms: support, dependence, anchor|antonyms: weakness, vulnerability|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *mainstaegh, from main "might, strength" + staegh "stay." Originally a nautical term for a large rope supporting the main mast.
