composure:/kəmˈpoʊʒər/| n. |镇静；沉着；泰然自若|The state of being calm and in control of oneself, especially in a difficult situation. |She maintained her composure despite the intense pressure. 她即使在巨大压力下也保持镇静。|Near synonyms:  calmness, equilibrium, poise, self-possession|Antonyms: agitation, anxiety, distress, panic|noun
