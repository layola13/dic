downpour:/ˈdaʊnpɔːr/| n.|倾盆大雨；暴雨|A sudden and heavy fall of rain.|The unexpected downpour soaked us to the skin. 突如其来的倾盆大雨把我们淋得浑身湿透。| The game was cancelled due to the heavy downpour.  由于暴雨，比赛取消了。|近义词：deluge, torrent|反义词: drizzle, sprinkle|词性:名词

Etymology: From "down" + "pour". "Down" indicates the direction of the rain, and "pour" suggests a heavy flow.
