fumarole:/ˈfjuːməˌroʊl/ (US) /ˈfjuːməˌrɒl/ (UK)| n. |喷气孔；火山喷气孔|An opening in or near a volcano, through which hot sulfurous gases emerge.|Steam and other gases emerged from fumaroles on the side of the volcano. 火山的侧翼上的喷气孔喷出蒸汽和其他气体。|Synonyms: vent, opening, fissure, crater|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
