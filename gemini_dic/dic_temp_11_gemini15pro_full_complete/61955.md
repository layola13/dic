emulsifying:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪɪŋ/
| adj.|乳化的|Making or becoming an emulsion; capable of acting as an emulsifier.|The emulsifying agent helps to combine oil and water.|近义词: mixing, blending, homogenizing|反义词: separating, demulsifying|词性: adjective, present participle of emulsify
emulsify:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪ/
| v. |乳化|To combine two immiscible liquids, such as oil and water, into a stable mixture (an emulsion).|Lecithin is used to emulsify the ingredients in this recipe.|近义词: mix, blend, homogenize|反义词: separate, demulsify|词性: verb

词源: From French *émulsifier*, from Latin *emulsus* (past participle of *emulgere* "to milk out, drain out"), from *ex- "out" + *mulgere* "to milk".*
