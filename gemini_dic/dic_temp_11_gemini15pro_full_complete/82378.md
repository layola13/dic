groyning: /ˈɡrɔɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 呻吟; 抱怨 | To make a low, mournful sound expressing pain or suffering; to complain or grumble. | He lay on the floor, groyning in pain. 他躺在地板上，痛苦地呻吟着。|  She was constantly groyning about the workload. 她不停地抱怨工作量。| 近义词: moaning, complaining, grumbling | 反义词: rejoicing, celebrating | verb
