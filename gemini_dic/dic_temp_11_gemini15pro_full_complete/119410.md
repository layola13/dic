microfilm: /ˈmaɪkroʊfɪlm/ | n. |缩微胶卷；缩微胶片| A film on which materials are photographed at greatly reduced size. |  The library has a large collection of old newspapers on microfilm.  图书馆收藏了大量缩微胶卷形式的旧报纸。 | synonyms: microfiche, microphotograph | antonyms: enlargement | noun
