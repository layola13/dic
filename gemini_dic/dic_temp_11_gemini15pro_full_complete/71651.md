flattering:/ˈflætərɪŋ/| adj.|奉承的；使人显得更漂亮的；使人感到荣幸的|Making someone look or feel better than they actually are, often insincerely.  Making something appear more attractive or impressive than it really is.|That dress is very flattering on you. 那件衣服你穿起来很漂亮。|Synonyms: complimentary, approving, enhancing, favorable|Antonyms: unflattering, critical, detracting, unfavorable|adjective


flatterable:/ˈflætərəbl/| adj.|容易奉承的；可讨好的；可奉承的|Susceptible to flattery; easily pleased by compliments.  Also, capable of being made to appear more attractive or impressive.|He's a very flatterable young man. 他是一个很容易被奉承的年轻人。|Synonyms: susceptible, impressionable, vain, gullible|Antonyms: discerning, critical, skeptical, impervious|adjective
