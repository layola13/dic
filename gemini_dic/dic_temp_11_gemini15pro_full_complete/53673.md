discipleships:/dɪˈsaɪpəlʃɪps/
n.
门徒训练；门徒身份；门徒关系
The process of teaching and training someone to follow the teachings and example of a leader, especially a religious leader.  Also refers to the state of being a disciple.
Many churches offer discipleships programs for new members. 许多教会为新成员提供门徒训练课程。
The pastor emphasized the importance of discipleships in the growth of the church. 牧师强调了门徒训练在教会发展中的重要性。
近义词:apprenticeships, mentorships, training
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: leadership, independence)
词性:noun
