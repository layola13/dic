allegedly: /əˈledʒɪdli/ | adv. | 据称，据说 |  Used to convey that something is claimed to be the case or have taken place, although there is no proof. | He allegedly stole the car.  他据称偷了那辆车。| Synonyms: reportedly, supposedly, ostensibly | Antonyms: demonstrably, verifiably, undeniably | adverb
