ostracize: /ˈɑːstrəsaɪz/ (US) /ˈɒstrəsaɪz/ (UK)
|vt.|排斥，孤立，放逐|to exclude from a group or society by common consent.|He was ostracized by his colleagues for his unethical behavior. 他因为不道德的行为而被同事们排斥。|
| | |She was ostracized from the community after the scandal.  丑闻之后，她被社区孤立了。|
| | |synonyms: exclude, banish, shun, blackball, boycott|
| | |antonyms: include, welcome, embrace, accept|
|verb|
