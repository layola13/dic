dunking:/ˈdʌŋkɪŋ/
n.|扣篮，浸泡|The act of dipping something into a liquid, especially food.|He enjoys dunking his cookies in milk. 他喜欢把饼干泡在牛奶里吃。|近义词：dipping, immersing, submerging|反义词:removing, taking out|词性:名词

dunking:/ˈdʌŋkɪŋ/
v.|扣篮，浸泡|Present participle of "dunk".|He was dunking his donut in coffee. 他正把甜甜圈泡在咖啡里。|近义词：dipping, immersing, submerging|反义词:removing, taking out|词性:动词

词源：
From earlier *dunken, of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Middle Dutch dunken (“to dip, to duck”), Danish dyppe (“to dip”).  可能与中古荷兰语 dunken（“浸，压”）和丹麦语 dyppe（“浸”）有关。
