outshove: /ˌaʊtˈʃʌv/
verb
推出去; 比…推得更远
to shove something or someone further or with more force than someone else
He outshoved his opponent in the scrum. 他在混战中把对手推了出去。

outshove: /ˌaʊtˈʃʌv/
verb
胜过; 超越
to surpass or exceed in shoving
She outshoved all the other competitors in the shot put. 她在铅球比赛中推得比其他所有选手都远。

近义词: outpush, outthrust
反义词: yield, give way
词性: verb
