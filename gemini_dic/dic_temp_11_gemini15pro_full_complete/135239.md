nonpalatalizations:/ˌnɒnpælətəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非腭化 | The absence of palatalization; the process of a sound not becoming palatalized.  This refers to sounds, typically consonants, not shifting their articulation towards the palate. |  The nonpalatalization of /k/ before front vowels is a characteristic of some dialects. /k/在某些方言中前元音前不腭化是一个特征。|  The linguist studied the nonpalatalizations in the language's historical development. 语言学家研究了该语言历史发展中的非腭化现象。|  non-palatalisation (British spelling) | palatalizations | noun
