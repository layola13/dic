garnetlike:/ˈɡɑːrnɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像石榴石的；深红色的
Resembling a garnet, especially in color.  Typically a deep, rich red.
The wine had a garnetlike hue.  这葡萄酒有着像石榴石一样的色调。
Her dress was a beautiful garnetlike red. 她穿着一件美丽的石榴石红的连衣裙。
近义词:garnet-colored, ruby-colored, wine-colored, maroon
反义词:colorless, pale
词性:adjective
