multireligious:/ˌmʌlti rɪˈlɪdʒəs/
adjective
多宗教的，多种宗教的
Characterized by the presence or tolerance of multiple religions.
Malaysia is a multireligious country with a significant Muslim, Buddhist, Christian, and Hindu population. 马来西亚是一个多宗教国家，拥有大量的穆斯林、佛教徒、基督徒和印度教徒。
