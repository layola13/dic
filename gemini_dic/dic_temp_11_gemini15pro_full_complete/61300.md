embargoing: /ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊɪŋ/ /ɛmˈbɑːɡəʊɪŋ/
|v.|禁运；禁止|The act of placing an embargo on something, typically a country or good.|The US is embargoing all imports from that country. 美国正在禁止从该国进口所有商品。|近义词: banning, prohibiting, restricting|反义词: permitting, allowing|verb
