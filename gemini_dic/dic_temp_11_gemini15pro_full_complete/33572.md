ciliary:/ˈsɪliəri/ (US), /ˈsɪliəri/ (UK)| adj.| 睫状的，纤毛的|Relating to cilia, which are short, hairlike structures found on the surface of some cells.|The ciliary muscles control the shape of the lens in the eye.  睫状肌控制着眼睛晶状体的形状。|The doctor examined the patient's ciliary body. 医生检查了病人的睫状体。|近义词：ciliate, ciliated|反义词: None|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *ciliāris*, from *cilium* ("eyelid, eyelash").
