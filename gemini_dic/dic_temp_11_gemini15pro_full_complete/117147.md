meaninglessly:/ˈmiːnɪŋləsli/| adv.|毫无意义地，无谓地|Without meaning or purpose; in a way that does not achieve anything.|He spent his days meaninglessly wandering the streets. 他漫无目的地在街上游荡。|Synonyms: pointlessly, purposelessly, aimlessly, futilely, uselessly|Antonyms: meaningfully, purposefully, significantly|adverb

Etymology->
From meaning + -less + -ly. Meaning comes from Old English *mænan "to mean, intend, signify," from Proto-Germanic *mainijan "to have in mind, think" (source also of Old Frisian mena "to signify," Old Saxon menian "to intend, signify, commemorate," Old High German meinen "to mean, think, intend," Gothic munan "to think, have in mind").
