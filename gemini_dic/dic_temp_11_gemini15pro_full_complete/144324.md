outspurning:/ˌaʊtˈspɜːrnɪŋ/
verb
超过，胜过
surpassing, exceeding, especially in speed or scope.
The young athlete outspurned his rivals in the final stretch of the race.  年轻的运动员在比赛的最后阶段超过了他的对手。
surpass, exceed, outstrip, outrun
lag behind, fall behind
