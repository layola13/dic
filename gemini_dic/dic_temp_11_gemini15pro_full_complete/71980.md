flimsy:/ˈflɪmzi/| adj.|脆弱的；薄弱的；站不住脚的|Easily damaged or broken; not strongly made or held together; (of an excuse, argument, etc.) weak and unconvincing.|The evidence against him was pretty flimsy.  针对他的证据相当薄弱。|synonyms:fragile, weak, thin, slight, insubstantial, feeble, shaky, unconvincing|antonyms:strong, sturdy, robust, substantial, solid|adjective


flimsy:/ˈflɪmzi/| n.|薄纸; 复写纸|thin paper|She scribbled a note on a flimsy. 她在一张薄纸上草草地写了一张便条。|synonyms:tissue, paper|antonyms:cardboard, thick paper|noun
