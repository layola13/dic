neuropterous: /nʊˈrɒptərəs/ /nʊˈrɑːptərəs/
| adj. |神经翅目的；脉翅目的| Belonging or pertaining to the Neuroptera, an order of insects having four net-veined wings. |  Neuropterous insects, such as lacewings, are beneficial to gardens.  脉翅目昆虫，例如草蛉，对花园有益。 | Synonyms: net-winged | Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek νεῦρον (neûron, “sinew, tendon, nerve”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”)
