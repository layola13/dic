dogday:/ˈdɒɡ deɪz/ (British) /ˈdɑːɡ deɪz/ (American)| n. |三伏天；酷暑期；一年中最热的日子|The period between early July and early September when the weather is very hot. It is traditionally associated with the time when Sirius, the Dog Star, rises and sets with the sun.|The dog days of summer were upon us, and the city sweltered in the heat.  三伏天到了，城市在酷热中闷热难耐。|Synonyms: hottest days, summer heat, sweltering period|Antonyms: coldest days, winter chill|Noun
