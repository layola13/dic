octodentate:/ˌɒktəʊˈdɛnteɪt/ | adj. | 八齿的，八配位的 | Having eight teeth or toothlike projections. In chemistry, it describes a ligand that can bond to a central metal ion through eight donor atoms. |  EDTA is a well-known hexadentate ligand, while some larger molecules can act as octodentate ligands. 乙二胺四乙酸是一个著名的六齿配体，而一些更大的分子可以作为八齿配体。|  octadentate ligand, polydentate ligand | bidentate, tetradentate | adjective
