bayard:/ˈbeɪərd/ | n. |  骏马；骑士；勇武的男子 | A spirited horse; a knight; a chivalrous man.  A bayard is a traditional symbol of chivalry and courage. It often refers to a horse known for its spirit and strength, or a knight possessing those same qualities. |  He rode a magnificent bayard into battle. 他骑着一匹壮丽的骏马投入战斗。|  Synonyms: steed, charger, paladin, knight-errant | Antonyms: coward, craven | noun
