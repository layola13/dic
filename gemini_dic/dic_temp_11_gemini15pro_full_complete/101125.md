investment: /ɪnˈvɛstmənt/ | n. | 投资，投资额；投入 |  The act of putting money, effort, time, etc. into something to make a profit or get an advantage, or the money, effort, time, etc. used to do this. |  例句：He made a large investment in real estate. 他在房地产上进行了大量投资。| 近义词：expenditure, outlay, contribution | 反义词: divestment, withdrawal | 词性: noun
