outriggerless:/ˌaʊtˈrɪɡərlɪs/
adjective
无支架的，没有外伸支架的
lacking outriggers; without stabilizing floats.
The outriggerless canoe was difficult to balance.  这艘没有支架的独木舟很难保持平衡。
The design of the new boat is outriggerless, making it faster but less stable. 这艘新船的设计没有支架，使其速度更快，但稳定性较差。
近义词:unsupported, unstabilized
反义词:outriggered, stabilized
词性:adjective

词源:
"outriggerless" is formed by combining "outrigger" and the suffix "-less."  "Outrigger" itself comes from "out" + "rigger," with "rigger" referring to the equipment used to control and support sails on a boat. The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking."
