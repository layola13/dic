indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪndɪkeɪtə/ (UK)| n.|指示器，指标，标志|Something that shows or points to the state or level of something.|The unemployment rate is a key indicator of the health of the economy. 失业率是经济健康状况的关键指标。|Synonyms: sign, signal, measure, gauge|Antonyms: None|Noun

indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪndɪkeɪtə/ (UK)| adj.|指示的，标示的|Serving as a sign or indication of something.|The indicator light flashed red, warning of a problem. 指示灯闪烁红色，警告出现问题。|Synonyms: indicative, signifying, pointing|Antonyms: None|Adjective
