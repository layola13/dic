nonett:/nəʊˈnɛt/ | n. | 九重奏; 九人小组; 九个一组 | A group of nine musicians or singers who perform together; A group or set of nine. | The nonett played a beautiful piece by Beethoven.  九重奏演奏了一首贝多芬的优美作品。| The nonett of judges reached a unanimous verdict. 九名法官组成的审判团达成了一致的裁决。 | 近义词: ensemble, group | 反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以根据语境选择例如 solo, duo 等) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Italian *nonetto*, diminutive of *nove* ("nine"), from Latin *novem*. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*néwn̥*.
