outedged:/ˌaʊtˈɛdʒd/
verb
胜过，优于，略胜一筹
surpass in performance, especially by a small margin.
例句：The team was outedged by their rivals in the final minutes of the game.  该队在比赛的最后几分钟被对手略胜一筹。

例句：He outedged his opponent by a single vote. 他以一票的优势险胜对手。
近义词：outperform, surpass, beat, defeat, better, top, exceed
反义词：lose to, be defeated by, fall behind
词性：v.
