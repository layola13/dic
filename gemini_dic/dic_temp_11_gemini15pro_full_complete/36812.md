colonialism:/kəˈloʊniəlɪzəm/| n. |殖民主义|The policy or practice of acquiring full or partial political control over another country, occupying it with settlers, and exploiting it economically.|The history of Africa has been deeply affected by colonialism. 非洲的历史深受殖民主义的影响。| Colonialism led to the exploitation of resources in many countries. 殖民主义导致许多国家的资源被剥削。|imperialism, expansionism|anti-colonialism, independence|noun
