auto-infection:/ˌɔːtoʊɪnˈfɛkʃən/ | n. | 自体感染 | The process of becoming infected again with germs that are already present in or on one's body. | Example 1: The doctor warned her about the possibility of auto-infection if she didn't keep the wound clean. 医生警告她，如果她不保持伤口清洁，就有可能发生自体感染。

Example 2:  Auto-infection can occur when bacteria from one part of the body spreads to another. 当身体某个部位的细菌传播到另一个部位时，就会发生自体感染。 | Near Synonyms: reinfection, self-infection | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: recovery, healing) | noun
