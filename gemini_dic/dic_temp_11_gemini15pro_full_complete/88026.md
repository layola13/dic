highland:/ˈhaɪlənd/| n. adj.|高地，高原；苏格兰高地|A mountainous or elevated region.  Relating to or characteristic of a highland or highlands, especially the Highlands of Scotland.|例句：The Scottish Highlands are known for their rugged beauty. 苏格兰高地以其崎岖的美丽而闻名。|近义词：mountain, upland, plateau|反义词:lowland|词性：名词，形容词
