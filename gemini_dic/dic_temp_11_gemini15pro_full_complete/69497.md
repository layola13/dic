felicity:/fəˈlɪsɪti/ | n. | 幸福；幸运；福气 | Intense happiness; bliss.  |  She accepted the award with great felicity. 她非常幸福地接受了奖项。 |  happiness, bliss, joy, contentment |  unhappiness, sorrow, misery | noun

felicity:/fəˈlɪsɪti/ | n. | (措辞、风格等的)巧妙，贴切，恰当 |  Appropriateness of style or expression; aptness. | His felicity of expression impressed everyone. 他表达的巧妙给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|  aptness, appropriateness, suitability, fitness |  inappropriateness, unsuitability, awkwardness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *felicite, from Old French felicité, from Latin fēlīcitās (“happiness, fertility”), from fēlīx (“happy, fruitful, lucky”).
