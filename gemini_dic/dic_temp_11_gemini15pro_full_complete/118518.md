mesocoele:/ˈmiːzəˌsiːl/| n. |中腔，体腔中段|The mesocoele is the middle portion of the body cavity in a developing embryo, specifically between the procoele and metacoele.  它是发育中胚胎体腔的中间部分，特别是位于前腔和后腔之间。|The mesocoele develops into the pericardial cavity in vertebrates. 在脊椎动物中，中腔发育成围心腔。|Synonyms: mid-coelom, middle body cavity|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
