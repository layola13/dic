liage:/ˈliːɪdʒ/
noun
效忠，忠诚；臣服
The state of being bound by oath or feudal duty to a superior; allegiance.
The knight pledged his liage to the king.  这位骑士向国王宣誓效忠。
近义词：allegiance, loyalty, fidelity, fealty
反义词：treachery, disloyalty, betrayal
词性：n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *liege, from Frankish *letigi "free in respect of one's person as opposed to one's estate" or from Medieval Latin *laeticus.
