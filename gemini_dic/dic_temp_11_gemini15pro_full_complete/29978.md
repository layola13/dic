caviling:/ˈkævəlɪŋ/| adj.  n.|吹毛求疵的；挑剔；吹毛求疵|Making petty or unnecessary objections.|He's always caviling about the most trivial things. 他总是对最琐碎的事情吹毛求疵。|近义词：carping, nitpicking, quibbling|反义词：approving, praising|adjective
caviling:/ˈkævəlɪŋ/| n.|吹毛求疵；苛责|Petty or unnecessary objections.|The endless caviling over minor details held up the project.  对细枝末节无休止的吹毛求疵耽误了项目。|近义词：carping, nitpicking, quibbling|反义词：approval, praise|noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *caveillen*, from Anglo-Norman *caviller*, from Old North French *caviller* (“to mock, jest, cavil”), from Vulgar Latin *cavillārī*, frequentative of Latin *cavillī* (“jests, raillery”).
