goldsmiths: /ˈɡoʊldˌsmɪθs/| n.|金匠；金饰工匠；金器店|A person who makes or sells gold jewelry and other articles made of gold.  It can also refer to a shop that sells gold items.|例句：The goldsmiths in the city are known for their intricate designs. 城市里的金匠以其精巧的设计而闻名。

例句：My grandmother inherited a beautiful necklace from a renowned goldsmiths. 我祖母从一家著名的金匠店继承了一条漂亮的项链。|近义词：jeweler, silversmith|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *goldsmyth, from Old English goldsmiþ, from gold (“gold”) + smiþ (“smith”).  Cognate with German Goldschmied.
