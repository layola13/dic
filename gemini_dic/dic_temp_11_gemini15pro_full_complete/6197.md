ammodyte:/ˈæməˌdaɪt/ | n. | 沙潜鱼，沙蜥 | A small, slender fish of the family Ammodytidae, found in coastal waters.  Also, a type of lizard that lives in sand. |  The fisherman caught an ammodyte while fishing near the shore.  渔夫在岸边钓鱼时捕到了一条沙潜鱼。 | The ammodyte quickly burrowed into the sand. 沙蜥迅速钻入沙中。 | synonyms: sand lance, sand eel (for the fish); sand lizard (for the lizard) | antonyms: N/A | noun
