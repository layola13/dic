gandered:/ˈɡændərd/
verb
瞥了一眼，匆匆一看
took a quick look; glanced
He gandered at the newspaper headlines. 他瞥了一眼报纸上的标题。
近义词: glanced, glimpsed, peeked
反义词: stared, gazed, scrutinized
词性: 动词
