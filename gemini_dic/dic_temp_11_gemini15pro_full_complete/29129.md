caseation:/ˌkeɪsiˈeɪʃən/| n.|干酪化，干酪样变性|The process by which tissue is converted into a cheese-like substance, typically as a result of tuberculosis or other infections.|The doctor observed caseation in the patient's lung tissue. 医生观察到病人肺组织出现干酪化。|Synonyms: cheesy degeneration, caseous necrosis|Antonyms: healthy tissue, normal tissue|noun

caseation:/ˌkeɪsiˈeɪʃən/| n.|干酪样坏死|The death of tissue with a cheese-like appearance.|Caseation is a characteristic feature of tuberculosis. 干酪样坏死是肺结核的特征性表现。|Synonyms: caseous necrosis, cheesy degeneration|Antonyms: healthy tissue, normal tissue|noun
