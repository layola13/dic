nonfinancially:/ˌnɒnfaɪˈnænʃəli/
adverb
非财务地；非金钱地
Not related to financial matters; in a way that does not involve money.
Her success was measured nonfinancially, through the positive impact she had on the community. 她成功的衡量标准不是金钱，而是她对社区产生的积极影响。
Many people are motivated nonfinancially, finding fulfillment in helping others or pursuing their passions. 许多人的动力并非来自金钱，而是帮助他人或追求自己的热情中找到满足感。
近义词: non-monetarily, intangibly
反义词: financially, monetarily
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"
financial (adjective): relating to money
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs
