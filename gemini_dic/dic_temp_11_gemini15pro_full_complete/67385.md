exteriorly:/ɪkˈstɪəriərli/
adverb
外部地，外表上，从外部
On the outside; externally.
The house was painted white exteriorly.  房子外面漆成了白色。
近义词: externally, outwardly
反义词: interiorly, inwardly
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *exteriorius, from Latin exterior ("outer").  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "exterior" into an adverb.
