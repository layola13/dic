noncomplacently:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪs(ə)ntli/
adverb
不自满地，不懈怠地
in a way that shows you are not satisfied with the existing situation and want to do better.
The team worked noncomplacently, always striving for improvement.  团队不懈怠地工作，始终力求进步。
She pursued her goals noncomplacently, never resting on her laurels. 她不满足于现状地追求自己的目标，从不固步自封。
近义词: diligently, assiduously, tirelessly
反义词: complacently, passively, idly
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- (not) + complacent (satisfied, especially self-satisfied) + -ly (adverbial suffix). The word "complacent" comes from the Latin complacens, present participle of complacere (to please greatly), from com- (with) + placere (to please).
