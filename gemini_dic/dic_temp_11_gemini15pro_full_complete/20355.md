bismuths:/ˈbɪzməθs/
n.
铋（金属元素），复数形式
A brittle, crystalline, white metallic element with a pinkish tinge, used in alloys, medicine, and cosmetics.
Bismuth is a chemical element with the symbol Bi and atomic number 83.
例句：Bismuths are used in some pharmaceuticals. 铋被用于一些药物中。

Bismuths have a low melting point. 铋的熔点很低。

近义词：None (as it's a specific element)
反义词：None (as it's a specific element)
词性：Noun
