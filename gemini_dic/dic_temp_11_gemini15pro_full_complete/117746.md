melanomata:/ˌmɛləˈnəʊmətə/ | n. |黑色素瘤（复数）|A malignant tumor associated with skin cancer, typically a dark-colored lesion.  The plural form of melanoma. |Example 1:  Multiple melanomata were found on the patient's back. 病人的背上发现了多个黑色素瘤。

Example 2: The doctor biopsied several suspicious melanomata. 医生对几个可疑的黑色素瘤进行了活检。|Synonym: melanomas|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a disease, but one might consider "healthy skin tissue")|Noun
