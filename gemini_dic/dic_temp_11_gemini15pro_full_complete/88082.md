highmen:/ˈhaɪmən/| n. |爪牙；亲信；（尤指）打手|A group of followers or supporters, especially those hired to intimidate or cause trouble.|"The politician surrounded himself with highmen who would do his bidding, no matter how unethical." 这位政客身边围绕着一群爪牙，无论多么不道德，都会听从他的吩咐。|Synonyms: henchmen, thugs, goons, myrmidons|Antonyms: opponents, adversaries|noun
