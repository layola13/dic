overpublicized: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌblɪsaɪzd/
adjective
过度宣传的
Given excessive publicity; promoted or advertised too much.
The overpublicized event failed to live up to expectations.  这个被过度宣传的活动没有达到预期。
The movie was so overpublicized that I was already tired of it before it even came out.  这部电影被过度宣传了，以至于还没上映我就已经厌倦了。
近义词: overhyped, overexposed
反义词: underpublicized, unknown
词性: adjective

词源:
over- (prefix) + publicized (past participle of publicize)
"over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively."
"publicize" comes from "public" (from Latin "publicus") + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or cause to be").
