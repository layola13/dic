hummingbird:/ˈhʌmɪŋˌbɜːrd/ (US) /ˈhʌmɪŋbɜːd/ (UK)| n.|蜂鸟|A small, brightly colored bird with a long, thin beak and the ability to hover in the air by rapidly flapping its wings.|I saw a hummingbird flitting among the flowers. 我看到一只蜂鸟在花丛中飞舞。|近义词：hummer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From hum + -ing + bird, referring to the humming sound produced by the rapid beating of the bird's wings.
