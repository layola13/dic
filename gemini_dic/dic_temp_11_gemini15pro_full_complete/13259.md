atellan: /ætˈɛlən/
adjective|阿特兰的；阿特兰文化的|Relating to, or characteristic of, Attela, an ancient city in Campania, Italy, or its inhabitants.|The museum houses a collection of Atellan figurines.  博物馆收藏了一批阿特兰雕像。|Near Antonyms: Roman, Campanian (in the broader sense)|Far Antonyms: Greek, Etruscan|adjective
Etymology: From Latin *Atellanus*, relating to Atella.  The Atellan farces were a type of Roman comedy named after the town.
