microgranitic:/ˌmaɪkroʊɡrəˈnɪtɪk/
adjective
微花岗岩的；细粒花岗岩的
Composed of or resembling microgranite.  A rock texture similar to granite but with smaller crystals.
The thin section revealed a microgranitic texture.  薄切片显示出微花岗岩结构。
Synonyms: fine-grained, aphanitic (partially)
Antonyms: coarse-grained, phaneritic
词性: adjective
