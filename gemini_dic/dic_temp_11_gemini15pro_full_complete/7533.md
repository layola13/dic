annexationists:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃənɪsts/
noun
兼并主义者；吞并主义者
People who advocate or support annexation, the act of adding territory to one's own territory by appropriation.
The annexationists believed that acquiring neighboring lands would bring economic prosperity. 兼并主义者认为，吞并邻近的土地会带来经济繁荣。
The annexationists' aggressive policies led to war. 兼并主义者的侵略政策导致了战争。
Synonyms: expansionists, imperialists
Antonyms: anti-imperialists, pacifists
