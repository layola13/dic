botanic:/bəˈtænɪk/ /boʊˈtænɪk/| adj.|植物学的；植物的|Relating to plants; botanical. |例句：She visited the botanic gardens. 她参观了植物园。|例句：He has a keen interest in botanic research. 他对植物学研究有浓厚的兴趣。|近义词：botanical, plant, floral|反义词：zoological, faunal|词性:形容词
botany:/ˈbɒtəni/ /ˈbɑːtəni/| n.|植物学|The scientific study of plants, including their physiology, structure, genetics, ecology, distribution, classification, and economic importance. |例句：She is studying botany at university. 她在大学学习植物学。|例句：Botany is a fascinating subject. 植物学是一门引人入胜的学科。|近义词：plant science, phytology|反义词：zoology|词性:名词
