medicative:/ˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪv/| adj.|治疗的，有疗效的，药用的|Having healing or medicinal properties; tending to cure or relieve.|例句：The herbal tea has a medicative effect on sore throats.  这种凉茶对喉咙痛有疗效。

例句：The spa offered medicative mud baths.  这个水疗中心提供药用泥浴。|近义词：curative, healing, therapeutic, remedial|反义词：harmful, injurious|词性:形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *medicativus, from Latin medicatus (“treated medically”), past participle of medicari (“to treat medically”), from medicus (“physician”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *med- (“to take appropriate measures”).
