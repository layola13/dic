neogaean:/ˌniːoʊˈdʒiːən/ | adj. | 新生代的 | Relating to or denoting the Neogene period or system. | The neogaean period saw the rise of many modern mammal groups.  新生代时期见证了许多现代哺乳动物群体的兴起。| Example:  Neogaean fossils are often well-preserved. 新生代化石通常保存完好。| Synonyms: Neogene, Cenozoic (in part) | Antonyms:  Paleogene, Mesozoic, Paleozoic | Adjective

From Neo- (new) + Greek gaia (earth)
