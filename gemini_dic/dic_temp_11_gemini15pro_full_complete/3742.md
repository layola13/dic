agileness:/əˈdʒɪlnəs/| n. |敏捷性，灵活性|The quality or state of being agile; nimbleness; ability to move quickly and easily.|The cheetah's agileness is essential for hunting its prey. 猎豹的敏捷性对于捕猎猎物至关重要。|synonyms: quickness, swiftness, briskness|antonyms: slowness, sluggishness, inertia|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From agile + -ness.  Agile comes from the French word "agile," ultimately from the Latin "agilis," meaning "nimble, quick, active," derived from "agere," meaning "to do, to drive, to act."
