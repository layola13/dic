drainable:/ˈdreɪnəbl/
adjective
可排水的，可放干的
able to be drained.
The swimming pool is drainable for cleaning.  游泳池可以放干以便清洁。
The field is drainable, so it doesn't get waterlogged. 这块田地可以排水，所以不会积水。
近义词:emptyable, exhaustible
反义词:undrainable, non-drainable
词性:adjective

词源:
From drain + -able.  "drain" comes from Old English *drægnian, from Proto-Germanic *dregniz. "-able" comes from Old French -able and Latin -abilis.
