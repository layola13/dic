abecedary:/ˌeɪbiːˈsiːdəri/ | adj. | 按字母顺序排列的 | Arranged alphabetically. | The entries in the encyclopedia are abecedary. 百科全书的词条是按字母顺序排列的。 | synonyms: alphabetical | antonyms: disordered, random | adjective
