declotted:/diːˈklɒtɪd/ | adj. | 去除血栓的，溶栓的 | Having had a clot or clots removed.  The blood vessel was declotted using a specialized catheter. | 例句：The surgeon declotted the artery.  外科医生清除了动脉的血块。

例句：The declotted blood flowed freely again. 去除了血栓的血液再次自由流动。| 近义词：unclotted, thrombolysis,  | 反义词：clotted, thrombosed | 词性: 形容词


词源:  de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + clot + -ed (past participle suffix).  The prefix "de-" indicates the removal of something, in this case, a clot. "Clot" refers to a semi-solid mass of coagulated blood. "-ed" signifies that the action of declotting has been completed.
