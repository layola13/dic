multifaceted:/ˌmʌltiˈfæsɪtɪd/ | adj. | 多方面的，多层面的 | Having many different aspects or features. |  Her personality is multifaceted, encompassing both great kindness and fierce determination. 她的人格是多方面的，既有极大的善良，也有坚定的决心。| Example: The problem is multifaceted and requires a comprehensive solution. 这个问题是多方面的，需要一个全面的解决方案。 | Synonyms: complex, diverse, varied, many-sided | Antonyms: simple, one-dimensional, single-minded | adjective
