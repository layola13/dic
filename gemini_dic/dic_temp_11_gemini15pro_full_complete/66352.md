excursion:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/
n.
短途旅行，远足
A short journey or trip, especially one made for pleasure.
例句：We took a day excursion to the beach. 我们去海滩进行了一日游。

例句：The school organized an excursion to the museum. 学校组织了一次去博物馆的游览。

近义词：trip, outing, journey, expedition
反义词：stay, homebody
词性：名词

excursion:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/
n.
(短暂的)涉猎，(思想等的)离题
A deviation from a regular course or track.
例句：His excursion into politics was short-lived. 他短暂涉足政治。

例句：The book contains interesting excursions into related subjects. 这本书包含了对相关主题的有趣探讨。


近义词：deviation, digression
反义词：conformity, adherence
词性：名词



词源：From Late Latin excursio, from Latin excurrere “to run out,” from ex- “out” + currere “to run.”
