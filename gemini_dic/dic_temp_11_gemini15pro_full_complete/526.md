abominableness:/əˌbɒmɪnəbəlˈnɛs/| n.|可憎；令人憎恶|The abominableness of the crime shocked the nation.  这起罪行的可憎程度震惊了全国。|The sheer abominableness of the conditions in the prison was exposed by a journalist. 一名记者揭露了监狱里骇人听闻的恶劣环境。|近义词：atrocity, heinousness, odiousness, vileness|反义词：pleasantness, agreeableness|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French abhominablete, from late Latin abominabilitas, from abominari ‘deprecate as an ill omen’ (see abominate).
