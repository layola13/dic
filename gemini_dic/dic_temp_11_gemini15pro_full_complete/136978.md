Etymology->
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
spherical (adjective): From Late Latin sphēricus, from Greek sphairikos, from sphaira "sphere, ball."
-ity (suffix):  Forms nouns of quality or state, from French -ité or Latin -itas.
