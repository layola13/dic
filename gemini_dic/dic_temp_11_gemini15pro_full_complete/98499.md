instant-replays:/ˈɪnstənt ˈriːpleɪz/ | n. | 即时回放 |The instant replay showed the player was out of bounds. |  A recording of a segment of a game, match, or other event that is broadcast immediately after it occurs, often in slow motion. | Example: The referee checked the instant replay to confirm the goal. 裁判查看了即时回放以确认进球。
Example:  Instant replays are a crucial part of modern sports broadcasting. 即时回放是现代体育广播的重要组成部分。| Synonyms: replay, action replay | Antonyms: live broadcast | Noun
