hoovers:/ˈhuːvərz/ (British), /ˈhuːvɚz/ (American)| n. v.|吸尘器；用吸尘器打扫|A vacuum cleaner; to clean with a vacuum cleaner.  Hoovers is the plural form of Hoover, which can be both a noun and a verb, though primarily recognized as a proprietary eponym. |She hoovers the carpets every week. 她每周都用吸尘器清洁地毯。|近义词：vacuum cleaners, vacuums|反义词: (As a verb) mess up, dirty|词性: Noun, Verb


词源:
Derived from the name of the company, The Hoover Company, which popularized vacuum cleaners.  It became a genericized trademark, used to refer to any vacuum cleaner, particularly in British English.
