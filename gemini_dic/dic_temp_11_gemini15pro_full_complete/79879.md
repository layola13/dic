gnashing of teeth: 咬牙切齿 (idiomatic expression for expressing anger, frustration, or envy)

He listened to the news of his rival's success with a gnashing of teeth. 他咬牙切齿地听着对手成功的消息。

grit one's teeth:  咬紧牙关 (similar meaning, but emphasizes determination)

She gritted her teeth and pushed through the pain. 她咬紧牙关，克服了疼痛。

grind one's teeth: 磨牙 (more general term, can be due to stress, sleep, or dental issues)

He tends to grind his teeth when he's concentrating. 他集中注意力时容易磨牙。


clench one's teeth: 咬紧牙关 (similar to grit one's teeth, often in response to pain or effort)

He clenched his teeth as he lifted the heavy weight. 他举起重物时咬紧牙关。


gnash one's teeth in rage:  愤怒地咬牙切齿 (emphasizes the anger associated with the action)

She gnashed her teeth in rage when she heard the insult. 当她听到侮辱时，她愤怒地咬牙切齿。
