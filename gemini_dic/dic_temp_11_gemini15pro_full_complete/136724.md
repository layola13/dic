nonsensiblenesses:/ˌnɒnsɛnsɪblənəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnsɛnsɪblənəsɪz/
n.
胡说八道；愚蠢的行为；荒谬的事情
instances of being nonsensical; absurd or illogical actions or words.
His pronouncements on the subject were utter nonsensiblenesses. 他在这个问题上的声明完全是胡说八道。
近义词: absurdities, follies, silliness, inanities
反义词: sense, wisdom, rationality
词性: 名词
