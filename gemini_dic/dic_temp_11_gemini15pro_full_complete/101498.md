irised:/ˈaɪrɪst/ | adj. | 彩虹色的，虹彩的 | Having the colors of the rainbow, or exhibiting iridescence.  | The oil slick on the water created an irised effect. 水面上的油污产生了彩虹色的效果。 |  The butterfly's wings were irised, shimmering in the sunlight. 蝴蝶的翅膀闪着虹彩，在阳光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词: iridescent, rainbow-colored, prismatic | 反义词: dull, drab | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the noun "iris" (referring to the rainbow or the colored part of the eye) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Greek "iris" meaning "rainbow."
