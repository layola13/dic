overimmunized: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmjəˌnaɪzd/
adjective
过度免疫的
Having received more immunizations than are necessary or recommended, potentially leading to adverse effects.
The child was overimmunized and suffered some side effects.  这个孩子过度免疫，遭受了一些副作用。
The veterinarian warned against overimmunizing pets. 兽医警告不要给宠物过度免疫。
Near Synonyms:  overvaccinated
Antonyms:  underimmunized, undervaccinated
词性: adjective
