forelend:/fɔːrˈlɛnd/
verb
预支；预付；预借
To lend (money) beforehand; advance.
He forelent her the money she needed to start her business. 他预支了她创业所需的资金。

forelend:/fɔːrˈlɛnd/
verb
预示；预兆
To indicate or suggest beforehand; presage.
The dark clouds forelent a storm. 乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。


Etymology:
From fore- + lend.  "Fore-" is an Old English prefix meaning "before," "in front," or "beforehand." "Lend" comes from Old English *lænan, from Proto-Germanic *lainjanan, meaning "to lend."

Synonyms:
advance, prepay, loan

Antonyms:
borrow, owe
