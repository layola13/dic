dairies:/ˈdeəriːz/
n.
奶制品; 牛奶厂; 乳品店; 日记;
places where milk and cream are stored and milk products are made, or businesses that sell these products; a book in which you write down your personal experiences and thoughts each day.
We get fresh milk from the local dairies. 我们从当地的奶厂获得新鲜牛奶。
She kept dairies for most of her adult life.  她在成年后的大部分时间里都坚持写日记。
近义词:creamery, milk plant, journal, diary
反义词:None
词性:名词(复数)

dairy:/ˈdeəri/
n.
奶制品; 牛奶厂; 乳品店;
a place where milk and cream are stored and milk products are made, or a business that sells these products.
This dairy produces excellent cheese.  这家乳品店生产很棒的奶酪。
近义词:creamery, milk plant
反义词:None
