defensor:/dɪˈfɛnsər/ | n. | 防卫者，辩护者 | A person who defends someone or something. |  例句：He was a staunch defensor of human rights. 他是人权的坚定捍卫者。

例句：She acted as her brother's defensor in the dispute. 在这场争论中，她充当了她哥哥的辩护者。

近义词：protector, guardian, advocate, champion | 反义词：attacker, aggressor, opponent, prosecutor | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēfēnsōr, from Latin dēfēnsus, past participle of dēfendere (“to ward off, defend”), from dē (“down, away from”) + fendere (“to strike, push”).
