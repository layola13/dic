advocation:/ˌæd.vəˈkeɪ.ʃən/
n.
支持；拥护；辩护；倡导
The act of pleading for, supporting, or recommending; active espousal.
She is known for her advocation of women's rights. 她以倡导女权而闻名。
synonyms: support, championship, espousal, promotion, advocacy
antonyms: opposition, resistance, denouncement, condemnation
noun

advocation:/ˌæd.vəˈkeɪ.ʃən/
n.
(尤指律师的)职业，工作
A person's vocation or calling, especially one that is realized and pursued after retirement from one's regular occupation.
After retiring from teaching, he took up painting as his advocation.  从教职退休后，他把绘画作为自己的事业。
synonyms:  vocation, calling, occupation, profession, pursuit, hobby, pastime
antonyms: unemployment, idleness, leisure
