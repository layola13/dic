diffusivity:/ˌdɪfjuːˈsɪvɪti/| n.|扩散率，扩散系数|The diffusivity of a substance is a measure of how quickly it spreads or disperses through another substance.  一种物质的扩散率是衡量它在另一种物质中扩散或分散速度的指标。|The diffusivity of heat in copper is high. 铜的导热率很高。|Synonyms: diffusion coefficient, dispersion rate|Antonyms: impermeability, resistance|noun
