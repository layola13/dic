diphthongal:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡəl/ (UK), /dɪfˈθɑːŋɡəl/ (US)| adj.|双元音的|Relating to or consisting of a diphthong; characterized by diphthongs.  A diphthong is a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves towards another (as in coin, loud, and side).|The word "loud" is diphthongal, as the vowel sound changes from 'a' to 'ʊ'.  大声这个词是双元音的，因为元音从'a'变为'ʊ'.|The pronunciation of "fire" in some dialects is diphthongal.  在某些方言中，“fire”的发音是双元音的。|近义词:biphonemic|反义词:monophthongal|词性:形容词
