gneissy:/ˈnaɪsi/| adj.|片麻岩状的，似片麻岩的|Resembling gneiss; gneissic.  The rock has a gneissy appearance.|The cliffs were composed of a dark, gneissy rock. 悬崖由深色的、片麻岩状的岩石构成。|Synonyms: gneissic, foliated, laminated|Antonyms: massive, homogeneous|adjective

Etymology:  Derived from "gneiss" + "-y".  "Gneiss" comes from German "Gneis," probably related to Old High German "gneisto," meaning "spark," referring to the glittering appearance of some gneisses.
