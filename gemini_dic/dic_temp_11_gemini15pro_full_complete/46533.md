dalali:/dɑːˈlɑːliː/
n.|掮客，中间人，代理人|A broker or intermediary, especially in real estate transactions.|He worked as a dalali, connecting buyers and sellers of property. 他作为一名掮客，为买卖房产的人牵线搭桥。|近义词:broker, agent, intermediary|反义词:client, customer|词性:名词

词源: From Swahili *dalali*, from Arabic *dallāl* (دَلَّال) meaning "broker," "agent," or "auctioneer."
