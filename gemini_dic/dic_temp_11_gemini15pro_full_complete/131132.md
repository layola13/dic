nonbleeding:/ˌnɒnˈbliːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 不出血的，无出血的 | Not bleeding; not subject to bleeding.  | The surgeon opted for nonbleeding sutures. 外科医生选择了不出血的缝合线。|  The patient required nonbleeding bandages. 病人需要不出血的绷带。 | 近义词：bloodless, non-hemorrhagic | 反义词：bleeding, hemorrhagic | 词性：形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not". 表示否定
bleed (verb): from Old English *blēdan, meaning "to lose blood". 指的是出血
