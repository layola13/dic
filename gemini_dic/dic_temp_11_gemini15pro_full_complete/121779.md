misreceive:/ˌmɪsrɪˈsiːv/
verb
错误地收到；收错
To receive incorrectly or misunderstand.
The email was misreceived and ended up in the spam folder. 这封电子邮件被错误地接收并最终进入了垃圾邮件文件夹。
The instructions were misreceived, leading to confusion. 指令被误解了，导致了混乱。
misinterpret, misunderstand
receive correctly, understand correctly
