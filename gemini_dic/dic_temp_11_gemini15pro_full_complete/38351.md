concentrated:/ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|集中的，浓缩的，浓的|Existing in a high proportion relative to other substances; having had water or other diluting agent removed or reduced.|The company is making a concentrated effort to improve its customer service.  该公司正在集中精力改进其客户服务。|近义词：intense, strong, powerful, rich|反义词：dilute, weak, dispersed|adjective


concentrate:/ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt/ (US)| v.|集中（注意力），聚集，浓缩|Focus one's attention or mental effort on a particular object or activity.|I need to concentrate on my work. 我需要集中精力工作。|近义词：focus, attend, direct|反义词：distract, scatter|verb

concentrate:/ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt/ (US)| n.|浓缩物|A substance made by removing or reducing the diluting agent.|Orange juice concentrate is cheaper than fresh juice. 浓缩橙汁比鲜榨橙汁便宜。|近义词：extract, essence|反义词：dilution|noun
