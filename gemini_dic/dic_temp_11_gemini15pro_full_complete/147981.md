overrewarded:/ˌoʊvərɪˈwɔːrdɪd/
adjective
过度奖励的；报酬过高的
having received more reward than is deserved or appropriate.
The CEO was widely considered to be overrewarded, given the company's poor performance.  这位首席执行官被普遍认为报酬过高，考虑到公司的糟糕业绩。
He felt overrewarded for the small amount of work he had done. 他觉得自己因所做的少量工作而得到了过多的报酬。
overcompensated, overpaid
underrewarded, underpaid
adjective
