antiphthisic:/ˌæntiˈtɪzɪk/ /ˌæntiˈfɪzɪk/| adj. n.|抗痨的，抗结核的；抗痨药|A medicine or treatment that is effective against tuberculosis (TB).|The doctor prescribed an antiphthisic drug for the patient with tuberculosis.|近义词：antitubercular, antituberculous|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps pro-tubercular in a hypothetical sense but this is not a commonly used word)|adjective, noun
