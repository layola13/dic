aconitine:/əˈkɒnɪtiːn/
n.
乌头碱
A highly toxic alkaloid derived from plants of the genus Aconitum (monkshood or wolfsbane).
Aconitine is used in some traditional medicines but is extremely dangerous. 乌头碱用于一些传统药物，但非常危险。
Aconitine can cause paralysis and even death in small doses.  即使是小剂量的乌头碱也会导致瘫痪甚至死亡。
Synonym:
neurotoxin, poison
Antonym:
antidote, remedy
noun
