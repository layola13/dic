hp:/eɪtʃ piː/ |n.| 生命值，马力 |Hit Points (in gaming) or Horsepower (in mechanics).  A numerical value representing health in games, or a unit of power. |例句：My character only has 10 hp left. 我的角色只剩下10点生命值了。

The car has 200 hp. 这辆车有200马力。|近义词：(For HP as hit points) health, life; (For HP as horsepower) power, bhp |反义词: (For HP as hit points) damage, weakness |词性: noun (缩写)
