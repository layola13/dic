athletic: /æθˈlɛtɪk/ | adj. | 运动的；强壮的；活跃的 | Physically strong, fit, and active. | She’s very athletic. 她非常擅长运动。 | He has a tall, athletic build. 他身材高大健壮。 |  Synonyms: strong, muscular, fit, active, vigorous, energetic | Antonyms: weak, frail, unfit, inactive, sedentary | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *athleticus, from Latin athleta, from Ancient Greek ἀθλητής (athlētḗs, “contestant in games, competitor for a prize, prizefighter”), from ἆθλος (âthlos, “contest, struggle”).
