oppositional:/ˌɒpəˈzɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 对立的，反抗的 | Showing a tendency to resist or oppose; characterized by resistance or defiance. |  He displayed oppositional behavior throughout his childhood. 他整个童年时期都表现出反抗行为。

The teenager's oppositional defiance worried her parents.  这个青少年的反抗行为令她的父母担忧。

| 近义词: resistant, defiant, contrary, antagonistic | 反义词: compliant, cooperative, agreeable, obedient | 词性: adjective
