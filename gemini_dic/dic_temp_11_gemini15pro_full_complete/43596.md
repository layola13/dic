creamier:/ˈkriːmiər/ (US), /ˈkriːmiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更乳脂状的，更柔滑的|Comparative form of creamy: having a smooth, rich texture like cream.|The cheesecake was creamier than usual. 这款芝士蛋糕比平时更柔滑。|This brand of ice cream is creamier. 这个牌子的冰淇淋更乳脂状。|近义词：smoother, richer, silkier|反义词：thinner, runnier|词性:形容词
creamier:|词源: cream (n.) + -y (adj. suffix) + -er (comparative suffix). From Old French "cresme," ultimately from Latin "crassus" (thick). The "-y" suffix forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "having the qualities of," and "-er" is the standard comparative suffix.|
