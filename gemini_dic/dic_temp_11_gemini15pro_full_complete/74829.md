freneticism:/frɪˈnɛtɪsɪzəm/
n.
狂热；疯狂；激动
A state of intense activity and excitement.
The freneticism of the city can be overwhelming.  城市的喧嚣令人难以承受。
The freneticism of the stock market during the crash was palpable. 股市崩盘期间的疯狂气氛是显而易见的。
近义词：frenzy, mania, hysteria, agitation
反义词：calm, tranquility, peacefulness
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From frenetic + -ism.  Frenetic comes from the Greek phrenitis, meaning "inflammation of the brain," from phrēn, "mind."
