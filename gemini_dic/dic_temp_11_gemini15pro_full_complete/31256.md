chaoses:/ˈkeɪˌɑːsɪz/| n. |混乱，混沌|A state of utter confusion or disorder; a total lack of organization or order.|The city was in chaos after the earthquake. 地震过后，这座城市陷入了混乱。|Synonyms: disorder, turmoil, pandemonium, anarchy|Antonyms: order, harmony, peace, stability|Noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *chaos*, from Old French *chaos*, from Latin *chaos*, from Ancient Greek *χάος* (*kháos*) meaning "yawning void, vast chasm, abyss".
