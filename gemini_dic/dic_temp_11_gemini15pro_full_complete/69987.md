fetation:/fiːˈteɪʃən/
n.
怀孕；妊娠；胎儿的孕育；（植物的）结果实
The process of carrying a fetus in the womb during pregnancy.  The act or process of conceiving and developing offspring.
The doctor monitored the fetation carefully. 医生仔细监测了妊娠情况。

The fetation of this rare plant is a complex process.  这种稀有植物的结实是一个复杂的过程。

近义词: pregnancy, gestation, conception
反义词: abortion, miscarriage
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fetatio, from Latin fetus (“offspring, pregnancy”) + -ation.
