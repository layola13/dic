leveraging:/ˈliːvərɪdʒɪŋ/
v.
利用；运用
using something to maximum advantage.
The company is leveraging its resources to expand into new markets.  公司正在利用其资源拓展新市场。

leveraging:/ˈliːvərɪdʒɪŋ/
n.
杠杆作用; 利用
The act of using something to maximum advantage.
The leveraging of social media marketing helped the company gain more customers. 利用社交媒体营销帮助公司获得了更多客户。



Etymology->
From lever + -age.  Ultimately from Latin lēvāre (“to lift, lighten”).

Synonyms->
utilizing, capitalizing on, exploiting, applying

Antonyms->
wasting, neglecting, squandering
