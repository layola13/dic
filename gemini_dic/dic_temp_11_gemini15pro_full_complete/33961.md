cisatlantic:/ˌsɪsætˈlæntɪk/
adjective
大西洋此岸的；大西洋西岸的
Located on or relating to the side of the Atlantic Ocean nearer to the speaker, especially relating to the western side when speaking from Europe or the eastern side when speaking from the Americas.
The cisatlantic trade routes were heavily used during the colonial period.  大西洋此岸的贸易路线在殖民时期被大量使用。
The cisatlantic perspective on the war differed greatly from the European view.  大西洋此岸对战争的看法与欧洲的观点大相径庭。
Near Antonyms: transatlantic
Word Origin: From cis- + Atlantic.
