The mobocrat used fear-mongering tactics to consolidate his power. 煽动暴民的政客用散布恐惧的策略巩固了他的权力。 | Near synonyms: demagogue, rabble-rouser, agitator | Near antonyms: statesman, diplomat, conciliator | noun
