electorship: /ɪˈlɛktərʃɪp/
n.
选民资格；选民身份；选举人的职位
The position or status of an elector.  The right to vote in an election.
He held the electorship for ten years.  他担任选举人十年了。
synonyms: franchise, suffrage, right to vote
antonyms: disenfranchisement
