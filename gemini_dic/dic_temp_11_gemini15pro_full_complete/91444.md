hygrograph:/ˈhaɪɡrəɡræf/ /ˈhaɪɡroʊɡræf/
noun
湿度记录仪，湿度计
An instrument that records variations in atmospheric humidity.
The hygrograph showed a sharp rise in humidity just before the storm. 湿度记录仪显示，就在暴风雨来临之前，湿度急剧上升。
Synonyms: hygrometer, humidigraph
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a measuring instrument)
