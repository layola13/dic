auto:/ˈɔːtəʊ/ (UK) /ˈɔːtoʊ/ (US)| n. adj.|汽车；自动的|A car, short for automobile. Also used as a prefix meaning "self" or "automatic".|He drove his auto to work.|近义词：car, automobile, vehicle (n.); automatic, self- (adj.)|反义词:manual (adj.)|词性:名词, 形容词


Etymology: Shortened from "automobile," which comes from French, combining "auto-" (self) and "mobile" (moving).  The "auto-" prefix originates from Greek "autos" (self).
