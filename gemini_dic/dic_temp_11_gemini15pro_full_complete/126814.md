napalming:/ˈneɪpɑːmɪŋ/
n.
凝固汽油弹攻击，烧夷弹攻击
The act of attacking something or someone with napalm.
The village was subjected to relentless napalming. 这个村庄遭受了无情的凝固汽油弹攻击。
bombing, incineration, burning
extinguishing, dousing
noun
