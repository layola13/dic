decapitate: /dɪˈkæpɪteɪt/
verb
斩首，砍头
To cut off the head of; behead.
The executioner decapitated the prisoner.  刽子手将囚犯斩首。

The king was decapitated during the revolution. 国王在革命期间被斩首。
synonym: behead, execute, guillotine
antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider "spare," "pardon," or actions that preserve life)
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin decapitatus, past participle of decapitare (“to behead”), from Latin de- (“from, off”) + caput (“head”).
