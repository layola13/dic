doorbelling:/ˈdɔːrˌbɛlɪŋ/
n.
按门铃；按门铃的行为
the act of ringing a doorbell
The constant doorbelling was driving her crazy.  不断的门铃声让她快疯了。

近义词:ringing the doorbell
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "knocking" if considering alternatives to doorbells)
词性:noun
