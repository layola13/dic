communital: /kəˈmjuːnɪtəl/
adjective
公共的，社区的
Of or relating to a community or communion.
例句：Communital harmony is essential for a thriving society.  社区和谐对于繁荣的社会至关重要。

例句：The communital garden provides fresh produce for the neighborhood.  社区花园为邻里提供新鲜农产品。

近义词：community, collective, shared, common, public
反义词：private, individual, personal
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *communitalis, from Latin communis 'common'.
