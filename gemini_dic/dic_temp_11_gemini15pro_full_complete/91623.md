hyperbarbarously:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːrbərəsli/
|adv.|极其野蛮地，极端残酷地|In an extremely barbarous or cruel manner; with excessive savagery.|The prisoners were treated hyperbarbarously, subjected to torture and starvation.|近义词：savagely, brutally, inhumanly, cruelly|反义词：humanely, kindly, compassionately|词性:副词
词源:  From "hyper-" (meaning "excessive, over") + "barbarous" (meaning "uncivilized, cruel") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Greek "barbaros" (meaning "foreign, non-Greek").
