evanesce:/ˌɛvəˈnɛs/ | v.i. | 消失，消散 | To disappear gradually; vanish; fade away. | The mist evanesced as the sun rose.  太阳升起时，薄雾消散了。

The memory of the dream evanesced quickly. 梦的记忆很快就消失了。


近义词：disappear, vanish, fade, dissolve, evaporate | 反义词：appear, materialize, emerge, persist | 词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *evanescere, present active infinitive of evanesco (“I vanish, disappear, pass away”), from ex (“out of, from”) + vanesco (“I vanish”), inchoative of vānus (“empty, void, vain”).
