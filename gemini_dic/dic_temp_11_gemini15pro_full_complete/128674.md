nicotined: /ˈnɪkəˌtiːnd/ | adj. | 尼古丁化的；受尼古丁影响的 | Containing or saturated with nicotine; influenced by nicotine. |  The air in the bar was thick and nicotined. 酒吧里的空气又浓又充满尼古丁味。|  The singer's voice was raspy and nicotined. 歌手的声音沙哑，像是吸了很多尼古丁。 | Synonyms: nicotine-stained, nicotine-laden | Antonyms: nicotine-free | adjective


Etymology:

From nicotine + -ed.  Nicotine itself comes from  Jean Nicot, a French diplomat who introduced tobacco to France in the 16th century.
