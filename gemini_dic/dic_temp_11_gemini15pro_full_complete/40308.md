contortion:/kənˈtɔːrʃ(ə)n/
n.|扭曲，变形；扭歪的动作|the act of twisting or bending something out of its normal shape.
The contortion of the metal was caused by the intense heat.|扭曲，变形；弯曲|distortion, deformation, twisting, bending|straightening, unbending|noun

contort:/kənˈtɔːrt/
v.|扭曲，变形；歪曲|to twist or bend something out of its normal shape.
His face was contorted in agony.|扭曲，变形；歪曲|distort, deform, twist, bend|straighten, unbend|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contortionem, from Latin contortus, past participle of contorquēre "to twist violently," from com- "together" + torquēre "to twist."
