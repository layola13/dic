globbier:/ˈɡlɒbiər/ (UK) /ˈɡlɑːbiər/ (US)| adj.|更粘稠的，更糊状的|Comparative form of "globby," meaning more resembling a glob or globs; thicker and stickier.|The batter was much globbier than I expected, making it difficult to pour. 面糊比我预期的要粘稠得多，很难倒出来。|The oatmeal was too globbier for my liking. 这燕麦粥对我来说太粘稠了。|近义词：stickier, thicker, gooier, viscousier|反义词：thinner, runnier, smoother|词性:形容词

词源: From "glob" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Glob" likely originated as an imitative word.
