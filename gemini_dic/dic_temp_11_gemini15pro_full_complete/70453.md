figure-in:/ˈfɪɡjər ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 算入；包括；将…列入考虑；是…的一部分 | to include or consider something when making plans or calculations; to be a part of something |  We need to figure in the cost of travel. 我们需要把旅行费用算进去。| synonyms: include, factor in, take into account, consider | antonyms: exclude, omit, disregard | verb phrase

figure-in:/ˈfɪɡjər ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 弄懂；理解 | to understand something or someone | I can't figure in why he did that. 我不明白他为什么那样做。| synonyms: understand, comprehend, grasp, make sense of | antonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret | verb phrase

figure prominently in:  在…中起重要作用
