outjests:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɛst/
verb
胜过…的玩笑; 以玩笑胜过
surpass in jesting; outdo in wit or humor
He outjested all the other comedians at the festival. 他在节日的玩笑上胜过了所有其他的喜剧演员。
近义词: outwit, surpass, excel
反义词:  be outdone, lose to
