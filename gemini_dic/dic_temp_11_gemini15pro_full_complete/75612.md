fugal:/ˈfjuːɡəl/| adj.|赋格的，遁走曲的|Of or relating to a fugue, a musical form in which a theme is introduced by one voice or instrument and is successively taken up by others and developed by interweaving the parts.|The composer's later works often incorporated fugal elements.  作曲家后期的作品经常融入赋格元素。|The organist played a complex fugal piece.  风琴演奏家演奏了一首复杂的赋格曲。|Synonyms: contrapuntal, polyphonic|Antonyms: homophonic, monophonic|adjective

fugal:/ˈfjuːɡəl/| adj.|逃亡的，躲避的|Characterized by or resembling flight; fleeing.|He made a fugal attempt to escape the paparazzi. 他试图躲避狗仔队的追逐。|Her fugal behavior after the incident aroused suspicion.  事件发生后她躲躲闪闪的行为引起了怀疑。|Synonyms:  escapistic, elusive, fugitive|Antonyms: confronting, facing|adjective
