earthworm:/ˈɜːθwɜːrm/ (US) /ˈɜːθwɜːm/ (UK)| n.|蚯蚓|A burrowing annelid worm that lives in the soil. Earthworms play an important role in aerating and enriching the soil.|例句：The robin pulled an earthworm out of the ground. 知更鸟从地里拽出一条蚯蚓。|近义词：night crawler, angleworm|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From Old English *eorðwyrm, from eorðe "earth" + wyrm "worm, serpent."
