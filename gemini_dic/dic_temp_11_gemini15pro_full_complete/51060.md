desolatory:/ˈdesələtɔːri/ /ˈdɛsələˌtɔri/
adjective
荒凉的；凄凉的；使人沮丧的
Causing or marked by desolation; bleak and cheerless.  Making one feel sad and hopeless.
The desolatory landscape stretched for miles, with no sign of life.  这片荒凉的景象绵延数英里，没有任何生命的迹象。
The news of the factory closing had a desolatory effect on the town. 工厂倒闭的消息对这个小镇产生了令人沮丧的影响。
近义词: bleak, desolate, dreary, barren, depressing, disheartening
反义词: cheerful, comforting, heartening, lively, vibrant
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *dēsōlātōrius, from Latin dēsōlātus (past participle of dēsōlāre ‘abandon, lay waste’), from de- ‘thoroughly’ + sōlāre ‘make lonely’, from sōlus ‘alone’.
