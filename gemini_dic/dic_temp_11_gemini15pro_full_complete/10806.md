aqueduct:/ˈæk.wɪ.dʌkt/| n. |渡槽，导水管|A channel for conveying water, typically in the form of a bridge across a valley or other gap.|The Roman aqueducts are marvels of engineering. 罗马渡槽是工程学的奇迹。|
The city's water supply depends on a series of aqueducts.  这座城市的水供应依赖于一系列渡槽。|
Synonyms: channel, conduit, watercourse|
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps blockage, obstruction)|
