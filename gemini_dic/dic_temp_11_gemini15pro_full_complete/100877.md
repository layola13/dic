intrusion: /ɪnˈtruːʒən/ (US), /ɪnˈtruːʒn/ (UK)
n.|入侵，闯入，侵扰|The intrusion of the paparazzi into her private life was unbearable. 狗仔队对她私生活的入侵令人无法忍受。|例句：The constant intrusions of noise from the street made it difficult to concentrate.  街上不断传来的噪音让人难以集中注意力。|近义词：invasion, encroachment, trespass|反义词: retreat, withdrawal|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin intrusio(n-), from intrudere ‘thrust in’ (see intrude).
