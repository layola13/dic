incumbrancer:/ɪnˈkʌmbrənsər/
n.|妨碍者，累赘，债权人|A person or thing that encumbers or burdens something, especially a property with debt.|The property is free from any incumbrancers. 这处房产没有任何债权人。|synonyms: burden, hindrance, obstacle, impediment, obstruction |antonyms: advantage, aid, asset, benefit|Noun


incumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/
n.|阻碍，累赘，债务|A burden or impediment; a claim or lien on property.|The mortgage was an incumbrance on the property. 抵押贷款是这处房产的负担。|synonyms: burden, hindrance, obstacle, impediment, obstruction, lien, charge |antonyms: advantage, aid, asset, benefit|Noun
