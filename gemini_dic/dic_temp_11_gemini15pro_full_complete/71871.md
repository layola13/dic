fleshly:/ˈflɛʃli/| adj.|肉体的；肉欲的；世俗的|Relating to the body; carnal; worldly.  Pertaining to the physical and sensual, often with connotations of sin or earthly desires.|例句：He indulged in fleshly pleasures. 他沉溺于肉欲。|例句：Her paintings explored the fleshly form. 她的画作探索了肉体的形态。|近义词：carnal, bodily, physical, corporeal, sensual|反义词：spiritual, ethereal, incorporeal|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old English *flæsclīc (composed of flæsc "flesh" and -līc "-ly").
