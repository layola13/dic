nonubiquitary: /ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəri/ | adj. | 非普遍存在的，非无所不在的 | Not present everywhere; not ubiquitous. |  The nonubiquitary nature of this species makes it difficult to study.  这种物种的非普遍存在性使其难以研究。|  The plant is nonubiquitary, found only in specific regions.  这种植物并非无处不在，只在特定区域发现。 | 近义词: rare, scarce, localized, uncommon | 反义词: ubiquitous, omnipresent, widespread, universal | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "ubiquitary" (from Latin "ubique" meaning "everywhere").  由表示否定的前缀"non-"和"ubiquitary"(源自拉丁语"ubique"，意为“到处”)组成。
