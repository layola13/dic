corslet:/ˈkɔːrslɪt/| n.|胸甲，护胸甲|A piece of armor covering the torso, typically made of metal plates.|The knight polished his corslet until it gleamed. 骑士把他的胸甲擦得闪闪发光。|Synonyms: armor, breastplate, cuirass|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "clothing" in a broad sense)|noun


Etymology->
From Old French *corselet*, diminutive of *cors* "body," from Latin *corpus*.
