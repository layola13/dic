loungiest:/ˈlaʊndʒiɪst/| adj.|最闲适的，最懒散的|Superlative form of "loungy," meaning most relaxed and comfortable, often in a lazy way.|例句：He had the loungiest attitude I've ever seen. 他是我见过最懒散的人了。

例句：The cat found the loungiest spot on the sofa. 猫在沙发上找到了最舒服的地方。|近义词：most relaxed, laziest, most comfortable, idlest|反义词：busiest, most active, most energetic|词性:形容词

词源: From "lounge" (n. & v.) + "-y" (adjective suffix) + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Lounge" ultimately comes from French "longer," meaning "to linger."
