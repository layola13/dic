disrepair:/ˌdɪsrɪˈpɛər/| n.|失修，破损状态|The house was in a state of disrepair. 这座房子处于失修状态。|例句：The old building fell into disrepair after years of neglect.  这栋老建筑在多年的忽视后变得破败不堪。

例句：The bridge has fallen into disrepair and is no longer safe to cross.  这座桥已经失修，不再安全通行。|近义词： dilapidation, deterioration, neglect, ruin|反义词:repair, good condition, sound condition|词性:名词
