citrange:/ˈsɪtrɪndʒ/| n.|香橙|A hybrid between a sweet orange (Citrus sinensis) and a trifoliate orange (Poncirus trifoliata). It is a relatively small, tart fruit, often used for juice or marmalade.|The citrange is a hardy citrus fruit that can withstand colder temperatures than most oranges. 香橙是一种耐寒的柑橘类水果，比大多数橙子更耐寒。|Citranges are primarily grown for their rootstock, which is disease-resistant. 香橙主要种植是因为它们的根茎抗病。|近义词:orange, tangerine|反义词:|noun
