nextness:/ˈnɛkstnɪs/
n.
接下来的状态;下一个;紧接着
the state or quality of being next or nearest.
The nextness of the event made everyone anxious.  即将到来的事件让每个人都感到焦虑。
近义词: proximity, immediacy, closeness
反义词: remoteness, distance
词性: 名词

词源:  From "next" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). "Next" comes from Old English "nīehst," superlative of "nīeh" (near).
