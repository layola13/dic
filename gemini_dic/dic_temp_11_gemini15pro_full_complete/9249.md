antipathogen:/ˌæntiˈpæθədʒən/| n.|抗病原体|A substance or agent that inhibits or destroys pathogens (disease-causing organisms).|Researchers are developing new antipathogens to combat drug-resistant bacteria. 研究人员正在开发新的抗病原体来对抗耐药细菌。|Synonyms: antimicrobial, antibiotic, antiviral, antifungal|Antonyms: pathogen,  pro-pathogen|noun
