forsakers:/fərˈseɪkərz/
n.
放弃者，遗弃者
Those who forsake their responsibilities will face consequences.
那些放弃自己责任的人将会面临后果。
People who abandon or desert someone or something, especially in a time of need.
那些抛弃或遗弃某人或某事的人，尤其是在需要帮助的时候。
例句：The forsakers of their families were shunned by the community. 遗弃家庭的人被社区所唾弃。
The forsakers of their oaths were punished severely.  违背誓言的人受到了严厉的惩罚。

近义词：deserters, abandoners, renegades
反义词：loyalists, supporters, adherents
词性: 名词 (plural)
