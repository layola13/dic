ancillary:/ˈænsɪləri/ /ænˈsɪləri/| adj. n.|辅助的，附属的；辅助人员，附属设备|Providing necessary support to the primary activities or operation of an organization, institution, industry, or system.  Serving a supplementary or supporting function.|The company's ancillary businesses include catering and equipment rental. 该公司的辅助业务包括餐饮和设备租赁.|Synonyms: auxiliary, supplementary, subordinate, supporting|Antonyms: primary, main, principal|Adjective

ancillary:/ˈænsɪləri/ /ænˈsɪləri/| n.|辅助人员，附属设备|A person or thing providing necessary support to the primary activities or operation of an organization, institution, industry, or system.|The hospital has a large number of ancillary staff. 这家医院有大量的辅助人员.|Synonyms:  auxiliary, adjunct, appendage|Antonyms: core, center, primary|Noun
