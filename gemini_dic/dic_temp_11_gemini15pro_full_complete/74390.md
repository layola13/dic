framer's:/ˈfreɪmərz/| n.| 装裱师的；制作者的；设计者的 |  Possessive form of "framer," meaning belonging to or associated with a framer. A framer can be someone who makes frames (for pictures, etc.), someone who constructs something (like a house or a law), or someone who creates or devises something (like a plan). | The framer's tools were neatly arranged on his workbench. 装裱师的工具整齐地摆放在他的工作台上。|  Synonyms: builder's, designer's, maker's, architect's (depending on the context) | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, depends on the context, possibly) destroyer's, demolisher's |  noun (possessive)
