caricaturing:/ˈkærɪkətʃərɪŋ/ | n. |漫画化；滑稽模仿| The act of exaggerating the peculiarities or defects of a person or thing. |  He made a living caricaturing politicians. 他以漫画政治人物为生。| synonyms: lampooning, satirizing, mimicking, mocking | antonyms: flattering, idealizing | noun

caricaturing:/ˈkærɪkətʃərɪŋ/ | v. |漫画化；滑稽模仿|  Present participle of caricature. | He is caricaturing the president in his new play. 他在他的新剧中滑稽地模仿总统。 | synonyms: lampooning, satirizing, mimicking, mocking | antonyms: flattering, idealizing | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *caricaturer*, from Italian *caricare* "to load, exaggerate," from *carro* "car, cart" (from Latin *carrus*).  The original sense was "to overload," then figuratively "to exaggerate."
