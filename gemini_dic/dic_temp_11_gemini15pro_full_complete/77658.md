gelding: /ˈɡɛldɪŋ/ | n. | 阉割的马；太监 | A castrated male horse.  A male animal that has been castrated, especially a horse. |  例句：The farmer brought his gelding to the market.  农民把他的阉马带到市场上。

例句：The gelding was gentler than the stallion. 这匹阉割过的马比种马温顺。| 近义词：castrated horse, eunuch (for humans) | 反义词：stallion, colt | 词性：noun

gelding: /ˈɡɛldɪŋ/ | v. | 阉割 | To castrate a male animal, especially a horse. | 例句：They decided to gelding the young colt. 他们决定阉割这匹小马驹。

例句：Gelding a horse can make it calmer.  阉割马可以使它更平静。| 近义词：castrate, neuter | 反义词：breed | 词性：verb
