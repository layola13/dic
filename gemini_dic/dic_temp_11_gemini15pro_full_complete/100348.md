interstellar:/ˌɪntərˈstelər/ | adj. | 星际的 | Occurring or situated between stars. |  Example: Interstellar travel is a dream of many scientists. 星际旅行是许多科学家的梦想。

Example: The interstellar medium is filled with gas and dust. 星际介质充满了气体和尘埃。| 近义词: cosmic, galactic | 反义词: planetary, terrestrial | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From inter- (between) + stellar (relating to stars).  "Stellar" comes from Latin "stella" (star).
