citywide:/ˈsɪtiwaɪd/
adj.
全市的，遍及全市的
Extending throughout a city; affecting the whole of a city.
The citywide cleanup campaign was a great success. 全市清洁运动取得了巨大成功。
The mayor announced a citywide curfew. 市长宣布了全市宵禁。
近义词: city-wide, throughout the city, all over the city
反义词: local, neighborhood
