mood-altering:/ˈmuːdˌɔːltərɪŋ/
adjective
改变情绪的；影响心情的
affecting or changing one's mood.
Alcohol is a mood-altering substance. 酒精是一种可以改变情绪的物质。
This medication has mood-altering side effects. 这种药物有改变情绪的副作用。
synonyms: psychoactive, psychotropic
antonyms: mood-stabilizing
Etymology: mood (n.) + altering (adj.)
mood (n.): from Old English *mōd "mind, feeling, heart, spirit, courage"
altering (adj.): from alter (v.) "to change," from Old French alterer "to change, alter," from Latin alterare "to change, alter," from alter "other (of two)"
