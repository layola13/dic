diamylene:/daɪˈæmɪliːn/
n.
二戊烯，癸烯
A branched alkene with the molecular formula C10H20. It is used as a solvent and in the production of synthetic lubricants and resins.
Diamylene is often used in industrial applications. 二戊烯常用于工业应用。
近义词:decene (癸烯 - a broader term as diamylene is a specific type of decene)
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

词源:di- (two) + amylene (戊烯)  From Greek "penta-" (five)  + "-ene" (alkene suffix)
