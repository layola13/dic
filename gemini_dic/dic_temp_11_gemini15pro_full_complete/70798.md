fingermarked:/ˈfɪŋɡərmɑːrkt/
adjective
沾有指纹的，有指印的
Marked with fingerprints.
The glass was fingermarked. 玻璃上有指纹。
Synonyms: smudged, stained, marked
Antonyms: clean, pristine
词性: 形容词
