noncongestion:/ˌnɒnkənˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. |无拥堵，畅通|The absence of congestion; a state of free and easy movement or flow. | The city planners aimed for noncongestion by implementing a new traffic system. 城市规划者通过实施新的交通系统来实现畅通无阻。| The new highway system contributed to the noncongestion of traffic during rush hour. 新的高速公路系统有助于在高峰时段缓解交通拥堵。 | 近义词: free flow, uncongested state, smooth flow | 反义词: congestion, traffic jam | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," "opposite of," from Latin non "not."
congestion (noun): From Late Latin congestionem (nominative congestio) "accumulation, a collecting together," noun of action from past-participle stem of Latin congerere "to heap together, collect, amass," from com "together" (see com-) + gerere "to carry, bear" (from PIE root *ges- "to carry, convey").
