nonsacrifices:/ˌnɒnˈsækrɪfaɪsɪz/
noun
非牺牲；未牺牲的事物
Things that are not sacrifices; actions or objects not given up for a higher purpose.
The family vacation was one of the few nonsacrifices they made during the difficult economic times.  在经济困难时期，家庭度假是他们少数几个没有牺牲的事情之一。
The company's decision to invest in new technology was seen as a nonsacrifice, as it would ultimately benefit them. 公司决定投资新技术被视为一种非牺牲，因为它最终会使他们受益。
Near synonyms:  preservation, conservation, retention
Antonyms: sacrifices, offerings, immolations
