cruised:/kruːzd/
verb
巡航，漫游，闲逛
travelled at a moderate speed, especially in a leisurely way.
例句：We cruised along the coast. 我们沿着海岸航行。
例句：The taxi cruised past the hotel. 出租车缓缓地驶过旅馆。
近义词：sailed, toured, drifted, wandered, roamed
反义词：raced, sped, hurried
词性：v.

cruised:/kruːzd/
adjective (informal)
轻松的，不费力的
done with ease and confidence.
例句：He cruised through the exam. 他轻松地通过了考试。
例句：She cruised to victory in the election. 她在选举中轻松获胜。
近义词：easy, effortless, smooth
反义词：difficult, challenging, strenuous
词性：adj.
