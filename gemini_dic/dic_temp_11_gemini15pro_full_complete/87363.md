heroin:/ˈheroʊɪn/|n.|海洛因；吗啡|A highly addictive drug derived from opium.  It is a powerful opioid that can cause severe physical and psychological dependence. |He was addicted to heroin. 他对海洛因上瘾了。|Synonyms: opiate, narcotic, smack (slang), junk (slang) |Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific drug)|noun


heroins: /ˈheroʊɪnz/|n.|海洛因（复数，不常用）|Plural form of heroin, rarely used. Refers to multiple types or instances of heroin. |The police seized several different heroins in the raid.  警察在突袭中缴获了几种不同的海洛因。(This usage is less common; "types of heroin" is generally preferred.) |Synonyms: opiates, narcotics |Antonyms: N/A|noun
