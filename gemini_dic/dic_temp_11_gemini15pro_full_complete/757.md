absolutism: /ˈæbsəluːtɪzəm/ | n. | 专制主义；绝对主义 | A political system in which a ruler or government has unlimited power. |  The French Revolution was a reaction against the absolutism of the monarchy. 法国大革命是对君主专制的一种反抗。|  The king ruled by divine right, believing in the principle of absolutism. 国王凭借神授的权利进行统治，信仰绝对主义原则。 | synonyms: autocracy, despotism, tyranny | antonyms: democracy, liberalism | noun
