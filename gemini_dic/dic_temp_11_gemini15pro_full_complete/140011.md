oedema (US spelling: edema):/ɪˈdiːmə/ (UK) /ɪˈdimə/ (US)| n.|水肿|Swelling caused by excess fluid trapped in the body's tissues.|After the surgery, she experienced some oedema around the incision. 手术后，她的切口周围出现了一些水肿。|Synonym: swelling, puffiness|Antonym: dehydration|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek οἴδημα (oídēma, “swelling”), from οἰδέω (oidéō, “I swell”).
