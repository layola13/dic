ballproof:/ˈbɔːlˌpruːf/
adjective
防弹的; 防球的
Impervious to the impact of balls or projectiles.  Something designed to resist damage from balls.
The new backstop is ballproof and protects spectators from stray baseballs.  新的防护网是防弹的，可以保护观众免受流弹的伤害。
synonyms: impact-resistant, projectile-proof, impenetrable
antonyms: vulnerable, fragile
词性: 形容词
