nonimperative:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɛrətɪv/
adjective
非命令式的；非强制性的
Not imperative; not commanding.
This suggestion is nonimperative, it's entirely up to you.  这个建议不是强制性的，完全取决于你。
The guideline offers nonimperative advice for new parents.  该指南为新手父母提供非强制性建议。
non-mandatory, optional, discretionary
imperative, mandatory, compulsory
adjective
