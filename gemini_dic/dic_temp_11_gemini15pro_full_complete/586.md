about-turn:/əˈbaʊt tɜːrn/ | n. | 向后转，转变，彻底改变 | A sudden change of opinion, policy, or direction. | The government did an about-turn on its tax policy. 政府彻底改变了其税收政策。| The general ordered the troops to make an about-turn. 将军命令部队向后转。 |  近义词：U-turn, reversal, volte-face | 反义词: continuation, perseverance | 词性: 名词

about-turn:/əˈbaʊt tɜːrn/ | adj. | 转向的，反方向的 |  Turned in the opposite direction. (Less common usage) |  His about-turn movement surprised everyone. 他的转向动作让所有人感到惊讶。(较少使用) | The about-turn policy confused the public.  这项转向的政策让公众感到困惑。(较少使用) | 近义词：reversed, inverted | 反义词: forward, direct | 词性: 形容词
