naturopathy:/ˌneɪtʃəˈrɒpəθi/ | n. |自然疗法|A system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as control of diet, exercise, and massage.|She turned to naturopathy after conventional medicine failed to alleviate her symptoms. 她尝试了各种传统疗法后，病情仍未好转，于是转向了自然疗法。|Synonyms: natural medicine, holistic medicine, alternative medicine|Antonyms: conventional medicine, allopathy|noun
