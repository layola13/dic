anisyl:/ˈænɪsɪl/ | adj. n.|茴香基的；茴香基|Relating to, derived from, or containing anisole; the univalent radical CH3OC6H4 derived from anisole by removal of a hydrogen atom.|The anisyl group imparts a characteristic aroma to certain compounds.|近义词:methoxyphenyl|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology: From anis(e) + -yl.  Ultimately from Greek ἄνηθον (ánēthon, “anise”).
