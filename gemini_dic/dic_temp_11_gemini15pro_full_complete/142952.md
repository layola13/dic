outact:/ˌaʊtˈækt/
verb
胜过；比……演得好
to perform better than
例句：He outacted all the others in the play. 他在剧中的表演胜过所有其他人。
例句：She completely outacted her more famous co-star.  她完全胜过她更有名的搭档。
近义词：outperform, surpass, excel
反义词：underperform
词性：v.
