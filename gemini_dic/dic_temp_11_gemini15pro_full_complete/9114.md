antimonsoon:/ˌæntaɪmɒnˈsuːn/ | n. | 反季风 | A wind system that reverses seasonally, blowing onshore in summer and offshore in winter, especially in the Indian Ocean. |  The antimonsoon brought dry air and clear skies. 反季风带来了干燥的空气和晴朗的天空。| The fishermen rely on the antimonsoon winds for their livelihood. 渔民依靠反季风的风来维持生计。 | 近义词: land breeze | 反义词: monsoon |词性: 名词
