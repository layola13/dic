coal-tar:/ˈkoʊl ˌtɑːr/ | n. |煤焦油；煤焦油沥青|A thick, black, viscous liquid obtained from the destructive distillation of coal. It is a complex mixture of hydrocarbons and other organic compounds.|Coal-tar was once widely used in road construction. 煤焦油曾经被广泛用于道路建设。|Synonyms: bitumen, pitch |Antonyms: None | Noun

coal-tar:/ˈkoʊl ˌtɑːr/ | adj. |煤焦油的|Made from or relating to coal tar.|Coal-tar soap is used to treat skin conditions. 煤焦油肥皂用于治疗皮肤病。|Synonyms: bituminous |Antonyms: None | Adjective
