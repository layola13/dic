draw-line-in-the-sand:/drɔː laɪn ɪn ðə sænd/
n.|划清界限，表明立场|To establish a clear boundary or limit; to take a firm stand.  It implies a point of no return.|The company drew a line in the sand and refused to negotiate with the terrorists.|近义词: take a stand, set a limit, lay down the law|反义词: compromise, negotiate, give in
