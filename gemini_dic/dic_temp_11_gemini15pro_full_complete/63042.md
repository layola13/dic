enregister:/ɛnˈrɛdʒɪstər/ /ɪnˈrɛdʒɪstər/
verb
登记，注册
To record or register something, often officially.
The births must be enregistered within three months.  出生必须在三个月内登记。
The car was enregistered in his name. 这辆车登记在他的名下。

近义词: register, record, enroll
反义词: deregister

词性: v.
