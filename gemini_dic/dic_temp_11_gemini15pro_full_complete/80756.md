gossamer:/ˈɡɒsəmə(r)/| n. adj.|薄纱，蛛丝，轻薄的|1.(n.) A fine, filmy substance consisting of cobwebs spun by small spiders, seen especially in autumn. 2.(adj.) Used to describe things that are very light, thin, and delicate. |1.(n.) The dew-covered gossamer glistened in the morning sun. 沾满露水的蛛丝在晨曦中闪闪发光。 2.(adj.) She wore a gossamer veil. 她戴着轻薄的面纱。|近义词：(n.) cobweb, spiderweb; (adj.) diaphanous, ethereal, filmy, gauzy|反义词：(adj.) thick, heavy, dense|词性:名词，形容词
