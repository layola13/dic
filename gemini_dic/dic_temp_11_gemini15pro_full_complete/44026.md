crimpiness:/ˈkrɪmpɪnɪs/
n.
卷曲度，波纹度
The state or quality of being crimpy; having many small bends or waves.
The crimpiness of her hair made it difficult to style. 她的头发卷曲度太高，很难做造型。
waviness, curliness, kinkiness
straightness, smoothness
noun
