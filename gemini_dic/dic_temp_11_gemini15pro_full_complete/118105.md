menispermaceous:/ˌmɛnɪspɜːˈmeɪʃəs/
adjective
防己科的；属于防己科的
Belonging to or characteristic of the Menispermaceae, a family of chiefly tropical, climbing shrubs.
Menispermaceous plants are known for their diverse chemical compounds. 防己科植物以其多样化的化合物而闻名。
The curare used by South American tribes is derived from a menispermaceous plant. 南美部落使用的箭毒是从一种防己科植物中提取的。
近义词: (No direct synonyms, but can use descriptive phrases like "belonging to the moonseed family")
反义词:  (No antonyms)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Menispermaceus,  from Menispermum (the type genus) + -aceus (suffix meaning 'belonging to' or 'having the nature of'). Menispermum comes from Greek mēniskos (crescent) + sperma (seed), referring to the crescent-shaped seeds of some species.
