blancher:/ˈblɑːnʃər/ | n. | 烫漂机，热烫机 | A device used to blanch food, usually vegetables or fruits, by briefly immersing them in boiling water or steam. | The blancher quickly cooks the vegetables, preserving their color and nutrients.  烫漂机快速烹煮蔬菜，保留其颜色和营养。|  Synonym: blanching machine | Antonym: N/A | noun

blanchers:/ˈblɑːnʃərz/ | n. | 烫漂机（复数） | Multiple devices used to blanch food. | The factory uses several large blanchers to process tons of green beans every day. 这家工厂每天使用几台大型烫漂机来加工数吨的青豆。| Synonym: blanching machines | Antonym: N/A | noun


Etymology: From the French verb "blanchir," meaning "to whiten" or "to blanch."
