gasoscope: /ˈɡæsəˌskoʊp/
noun
气体检测仪，气体分析仪
An instrument used for detecting or analyzing gases.
The gasoscope alerted the miners to a dangerous buildup of methane. 气体检测仪提醒矿工甲烷的危险积聚。
The researchers used a gasoscope to analyze the composition of the air. 研究人员使用气体分析仪来分析空气的成分。
synonyms: gas analyzer, gas detector
antonyms: N/A
n.
