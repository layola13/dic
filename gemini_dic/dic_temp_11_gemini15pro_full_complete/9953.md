aphasy: /əˈfeɪʒə/ (US), /əˈfeɪziː/ (UK) | n. | 失语症 | Loss of ability to understand or express speech, caused by brain damage. |  Example: After the stroke, he suffered from aphasia and struggled to speak.  中风后，他患上了失语症，难以说话。

Example:  Her aphasia made communication difficult, but she could still understand some things. 她的失语症使沟通变得困难，但她仍然能理解一些事情。 | 近义词: dysphasia | 反义词: fluency | 词性: noun
