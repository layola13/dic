calcar:/ˈkælkɑːr/ (US), /ˈkælkɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |骨刺，距|A spur-like process on a bone or other anatomical part.  A projecting anatomical structure or part, especially a bony outgrowth.|The tibia's calcar creates a lever system for the attached tendons. 胫骨的骨刺为连接的肌腱创造了一个杠杆系统。|Synonyms: spur, process, projection, outgrowth|Antonyms: depression, hollow, concavity|noun


calcar:/ˈkælkɑːr/ (US), /ˈkælkɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |（某些昆虫的）距|A spur, especially on the leg of a bird or insect.|The calcar on the rooster's leg is used for fighting.  公鸡腿上的距是用来打架的。|Synonyms: spur, spine|Antonyms: N/A|noun
