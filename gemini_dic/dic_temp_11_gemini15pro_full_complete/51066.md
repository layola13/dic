desoxalic:/ˌdiːsɒkˈsælɪk/ | adj. | 去氧草酸的；脱氧草酸的 |  Relating to or derived from desoxalic acid, a crystalline acid formed by the action of sodium amalgam on diethyl oxalate. | Desoxalic acid is a reducing agent. 去氧草酸是一种还原剂。|  近义词: reductive (还原的) | 反义词: oxidative (氧化的) | 词性: adjective

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "removal of") + "oxalic" (referring to oxalic acid).  The term signifies a compound related to oxalic acid but with oxygen removed.
