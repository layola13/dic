nonretainment:/ˌnɒn rɪˈteɪnmənt/
n.|未保留，未挽留|The nonretainment of skilled employees can be detrimental to a company's success. 未能留住熟练的员工可能会对公司的成功造成不利影响。|The nonretainment of the data led to significant problems later on.  未能保留这些数据后来导致了严重的问题。|retention|dismissal|noun
