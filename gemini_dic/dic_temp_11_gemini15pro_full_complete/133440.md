nonfastidious: /ˌnɒnfæˈstɪdiəs/ /ˌnɑːnfæˈstɪdiəs/
adjective
不挑剔的；不苛求的
Not excessively particular, critical, or demanding; easy to please.
This bacterium is nonfastidious, able to grow on a variety of simple media.  这种细菌不挑剔，能够在各种简单的培养基上生长。
These plants are nonfastidious and can tolerate a wide range of soil conditions. 这些植物不苛求，可以耐受各种土壤条件。

近义词:undemanding, adaptable, tolerant, uncritical
反义词:fastidious, picky, choosy, demanding

词性:adjective
