barbershop: /ˈbɑːrbərʃɑːp/ (US) /ˈbɑːbəʃɒp/ (UK)| n. |理发店；理发厅|A place where men get their hair cut.  A barbershop is traditionally a place where men can also socialize.| I went to the barbershop to get a haircut. 我去理发店剪头发了。

He got a shave at the old-fashioned barbershop. 他在那家老式理发店刮了胡子。


近义词: barber's, hairdresser's (more general) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From barber + shop.  "Barber" comes from Old French "barbier," meaning "barber," ultimately from Latin "barba," meaning "beard."  "Shop" comes from Old English "scoppa," meaning "shed, booth."
