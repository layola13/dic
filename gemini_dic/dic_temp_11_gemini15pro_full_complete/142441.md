oscillate:/ˈɒsɪleɪt/ | v. | 振荡， колебаться; 犹豫，踌躇 | To move back and forth repeatedly between two positions or states. | The stock market oscillated wildly throughout the day. 股市一整天都在剧烈波动。| fluctuate, vibrate, waver | remain, stay | verb
