aldine:/ˈɔːldaɪn/ | adj. | 阿尔丁的; 印刷精美的; | Relating to Aldus Manutius, a Venetian printer of the late 15th and early 16th centuries, or to the books he printed, characterized by elegant type and fine paper. | This book is a beautiful example of aldine typography.  这本书是阿尔丁印刷术的一个精美范例。| Near synonyms: elegant, fine, exquisite | Antonyms: crude, coarse, inelegant | adjective
