absorbances:/əbˈzɔːbənsɪz/
n.
吸光度；吸收率
The measure of the amount of light absorbed by a substance.  This is often used in spectroscopy.
The absorbance of the sample was measured at 500 nm.  样品的吸光度在 500 纳米处测量。
absorption, optical density
transmission, reflectance
