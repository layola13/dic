greyer:/ˈɡreɪər/ (US), /ˈɡreɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更灰色的，更灰白的|Comparative form of grey: more grey.  Indicates a more intense or deeper shade of grey than something else being compared.|The sky grew greyer as the storm approached.  天空随着暴风雨的临近变得更加灰暗。|The old house looked greyer than I remembered. 这栋老房子看起来比我记忆中的更灰暗了。|Near synonyms: darker, duller, ashier|Near antonyms: brighter, lighter, whiter|adjective

grey:/ɡreɪ/ (US), /ɡreɪ/ (UK)| adj. n.|灰色的，灰白的；灰色|Of a colour intermediate between black and white, as of ashes or lead.|The cat is grey. 这只猫是灰色的。|He wore a grey suit. 他穿着一套灰色西装。|Near synonyms:  ashy, charcoal, dove, slate, smoky|Near antonyms: black, white, bright, colorful|adjective, noun
