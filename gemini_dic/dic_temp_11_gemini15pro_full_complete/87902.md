hierarchy's:/ˈhaɪərɑːrkiːz/| n.|等级制度的；等级森严的| possessive form of hierarchy, referring to something belonging to or associated with a hierarchy.  It indicates ownership or a close relationship. |The hierarchy's decision was final. 等级制度的决定是最终的。|synonyms: ranking's, order's, echelon's| antonyms: equality's, disorder's|
