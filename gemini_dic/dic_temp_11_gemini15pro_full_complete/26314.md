buy-in:/ˈbaɪɪn/ | n. |支持，同意；入股；买进|  The project needs buy-in from all stakeholders. 这个项目需要所有利益相关者的支持。| Example: We need to get buy-in from the team before implementing the new strategy. 我们需要在实施新策略之前获得团队的支持。
Example:  His buy-in was crucial for the success of the project. 他的支持对项目的成功至关重要。 |Synonyms: agreement, acceptance, approval, support, backing | Antonyms: opposition, disagreement, rejection | noun


buy-in:/ˈbaɪɪn/ | n. |初始投资，入门费| The buy-in for the poker game was $100.  扑克游戏的入场费是100美元。 | Example: The buy-in for this investment opportunity is quite high. 这个投资机会的初始投资相当高。
Example: What's the buy-in for joining this exclusive club? 加入这个高级俱乐部的费用是多少？ |Synonyms: investment, stake, ante | Antonyms: divestment, sale | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "buy" + "in," literally meaning to "buy into" something, both financially and metaphorically.
