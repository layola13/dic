misquoters:/ˌmɪsˈkwəʊtərz/
n.
错误引用者，歪曲原话的人
People who quote someone inaccurately.
The article was full of misquoters who twisted the politician's words. 这篇文章充斥着歪曲政客原话的错误引用者。
近义词: distorters, misinterpreters
反义词: accurate quoters
