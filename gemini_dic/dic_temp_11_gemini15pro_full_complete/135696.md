nonpreservable:/ˌnɒnprɪˈzɜːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可保存的，无法保存的 | Something that is nonpreservable cannot be preserved or saved for later use.  It is likely to decay or spoil. | These berries are nonpreservable and must be eaten fresh. 这些浆果无法保存，必须新鲜食用。| The chef explained that some types of fish are nonpreservable due to their high oil content.  厨师解释说，有些种类的鱼由于含油量高而无法保存。 | perishable, unstable | durable, preservable | adjective
