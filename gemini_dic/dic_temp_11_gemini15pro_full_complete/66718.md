exopodite:/ˈɛksoʊpəˌdaɪt/| n. |外肢; 外足; |The exopodite is the outer branch of a crustacean's limb, often adapted for swimming or walking.|The crab used its exopodite to propel itself through the water.螃蟹用它的外肢在水中推进自己。|近义词:outer ramus, outer branch|反义词:endopodite|noun
