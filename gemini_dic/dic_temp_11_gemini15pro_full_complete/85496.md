hasteless:/ˈheɪstləs/| adj.|从容不迫的，不慌不忙的|Without haste; leisurely; deliberate. Characterized by the absence of urgency or rush.|He took a hasteless stroll through the park. 他悠闲地在公园里散步。|近义词：unhurried, leisurely, deliberate, slow, measured|反义词：hasty, hurried, rushed, urgent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From haste + -less.  Haste itself comes from Middle English *hast*, from Old French *haste*, ultimately from Frankish *hast* (speed, haste).
