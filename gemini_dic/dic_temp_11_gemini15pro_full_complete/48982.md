degerminator:/diˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtər/ | n. |去胚芽器，去芽器|A machine or device used to remove germs, particularly the germ from a grain of cereal.|The degerminator separates the germ, bran, and endosperm of the wheat kernel.  去胚芽器将小麦粒的胚芽、麸皮和胚乳分离。|Synonyms: germ separator, huller (for some applications) |Antonyms: N/A |noun
