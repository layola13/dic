fertilises:/ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
使肥沃；使受精；施肥于
makes something fertile or productive; causes a female egg to develop a new individual by uniting with a male sperm cell
The farmer fertilises his crops with manure.  农民用粪便给作物施肥。
synonyms: enrich, nourish, inseminate, fecundate
antonyms: sterilize, barren
词性:动词
