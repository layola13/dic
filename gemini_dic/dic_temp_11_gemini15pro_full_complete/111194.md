lithologic:/ˌlɪθəˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˌlɪθəˈlɑːdʒɪk/
adjective
岩性的
Relating to the physical characteristics of rocks, such as their composition, texture, and structure.
The geologist studied the lithologic characteristics of the core samples. 地质学家研究了岩心样品的岩性特征。

近义词:petrographic, petrological
反义词: None

词性: adjective
