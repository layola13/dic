invitar:/imbiˈtar/ (Spanish)| vt.|邀请|To invite someone to something; to request the presence of someone.  From Latin invitāre "to invite," frequentative of invītāre "to summon, invite."|I invited her to the party. 我邀请她参加了派对。|Synonyms: summon, ask, request, bid|Antonyms: exclude, reject, uninvite|Verb

invitar:/imbiˈtar/ (Spanish)| vt.|招待，款待|To treat someone to something, especially food or drink.|He invited us to dinner at his house. 他邀请我们去他家吃晚饭。|Synonyms: treat, entertain, host|Antonyms: neglect, ignore|Verb
