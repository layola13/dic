nonascertainableness:/nɒnˌæsərˈteɪnəbəlnəs/
n.
不可确定性
The state or quality of being impossible to ascertain or determine with certainty.
The nonascertainableness of the damage made it difficult to settle the insurance claim.  由于损失难以确定，保险索赔难以解决。
近义词:indeterminacy, uncertainty,  undecidability
反义词:certainty,  determinability, ascertainability
词性:noun

词源:
From non- + ascertainable + -ness.  "Ascertain" comes from Old French "acertener"  (make certain), from "a-" (to) + "certain" (certain).
