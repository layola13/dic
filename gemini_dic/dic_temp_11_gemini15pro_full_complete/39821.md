conspicuously:/kənˈspɪkjuəsli/
adv.
显著地，明显地
in a manner that attracts attention; noticeably.
Her conspicuously large diamond ring attracted everyone's attention. 她那颗格外大的钻戒吸引了所有人的目光。
Synonyms: noticeably, obviously, prominently, strikingly, visibly
Antonyms: subtly, inconspicuously, discreetly, unobtrusively
词性: 副词
