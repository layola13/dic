gemara:/ɡəˈmɑːrə/| n. |《革马拉》|The Gemara is the rabbinic commentary on the Mishnah, forming the Talmud.|He spent years studying the Gemara. 他花了数年时间研究《革马拉》。|近义词:Talmud (sometimes)|反义词:None|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Aramaic גְּמָרָא (gəmārā), meaning "completion," "tradition," or "learning."  It ultimately derives from the Aramaic verb גְּמַר (gəmar), meaning "to complete" or "to finish."
