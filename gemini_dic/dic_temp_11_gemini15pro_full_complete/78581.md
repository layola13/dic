giantess:/ˈdʒaɪəntɪs/| n.|女巨人|A woman of extraordinary height; a female giant.|例句：The giantess towered over the trees. 女巨人高耸于树木之上。

例句：In the fairytale, the giantess captured the prince. 在童话故事中，女巨人俘虏了王子。|近义词：Amazon, colossus|反义词：dwarf, midget|词性：名词

Etymology: From Middle English *giauntesse, from Old French geantesse, feminine of geant (“giant”), from Vulgar Latin *gigantem, accusative of gigas (“giant”), from Ancient Greek γίγας (gígas).
