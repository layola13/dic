neocene:/ˈniː.əˌsiːn/ | n. | 新近纪 |  Relating to or denoting the present geological epoch, which is the second epoch of the Quaternary period and followed the Pleistocene. It is characterized by the significant impact of humans on Earth's geology and ecosystems. | The neocene epoch is marked by human-induced climate change.  新近纪的特点是人为引起的气候变化。|  Holocene, Anthropocene | Pleistocene | noun
