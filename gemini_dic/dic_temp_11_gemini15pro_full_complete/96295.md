indigency:/ˈɪndɪdʒənsi/| n.|贫困，穷困|The family lived in a state of indigency. 这家人生活贫困。|例句：The rise in indigency in the city is alarming. 城市贫困人口的增加令人担忧。

例句：Many families were forced into indigency by the economic downturn. 许多家庭因经济衰退而陷入贫困。|近义词：poverty, destitution, penury, want|反义词：wealth, affluence, prosperity|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French indigence, from Latin indigentia, from indigent- ‘needy’, from the verb indigere.
