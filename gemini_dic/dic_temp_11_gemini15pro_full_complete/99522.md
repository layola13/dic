intergalactically:/ˌɪntərɡəˈlæktɪkli/
| adv. |星系间的，跨星系的|Occurring or existing between galaxies; involving travel or communication between galaxies.|例句：Intergalactically renowned scientist. 星系间知名的科学家。|例句：The spaceship traveled intergalactically. 宇宙飞船进行了星际旅行。|近义词：cosmic, interstellar|反义词：local, terrestrial|词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + galactic (relating to a galaxy) + -ally (adverbial suffix).  "Inter-" comes from Latin, "galactic" ultimately derives from Greek "galaxias kyklos" (milky circle), and "-ally" also has Latin origins.
