overplays:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪz/ | v. | 过分表演；夸大；过分强调 | to act a part in a play too dramatically; to exaggerate the importance of something | He overplayed his hand and lost the deal. 他操之过急，失去了这笔交易。 | overemphasize, exaggerate, overact, overstate | underplay, downplay | verb

overplays:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪz/ | n. | 过分表演；夸大；过分强调（的行为）|  An instance of overacting or exaggerating. | The overplays in his performance were distracting.  他表演中夸张的部分让人分心。| exaggeration, overstatement, overacting | understatement, downplaying | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From over- + play.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Play" in this context refers to acting or performing.
