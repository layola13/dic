affret:/əˈfrɛt/ | n. |  (古)租船；(古)租船契约 |A contract for the hire of a ship, especially in the Mediterranean. | Example: The affret stipulated that the ship would carry a cargo of spices from Alexandria to Venice. 这份租船契约规定，该船将把一批香料从亚历山大港运到威尼斯。
Example:  The merchant secured an affret for a voyage to Constantinople. 这位商人获得了一份前往君士坦丁堡的租船契约。| Near Synonyms: charter, lease | Antonyms: N/A | noun
