hyacinthine:/ˈhaɪəsɪnθaɪn/| n.|紫蓝色|A purplish blue color.|The artist used a hyacinthine hue for the background.  这位艺术家用紫蓝色作为背景。|Synonyms: violet, azure, lavender|Antonyms:  yellow, orange, green|noun
