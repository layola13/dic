/ˈɪndɪk/ | n. | 指示剂，指标 | Something that serves to indicate; a sign. (This usage is less common) |  The sudden drop in sales was an indic of trouble ahead. 销售额的骤降预示着未来的麻烦。| Synonyms: indicator, sign, symptom | Antonyms: N/A | noun
