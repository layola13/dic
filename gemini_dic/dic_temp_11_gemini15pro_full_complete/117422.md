medievalism:/ˌmediˈiːvəlɪzəm/ | n. | 中世纪主义；中世纪风格；中世纪研究 | The revival or imitation of medieval customs, arts, or styles.  |  The castle's architecture is a striking example of medievalism. 这座城堡的建筑是中世纪主义的一个突出例子。 |  The author's use of archaic language contributed to the medievalism of the novel. 作者使用古语使得这部小说更具中世纪风格。 |近义词: archaism, antiquarianism |反义词: modernism, contemporaneity | 词性: noun

medievalist:/ˌmediˈiːvəlɪst/ | n. | 中世纪研究者；中世纪专家 | A scholar specializing in the Middle Ages. |  The medievalist lectured on the development of Gothic architecture.  这位中世纪研究者讲授了哥特式建筑的发展。 |  She consulted with a medievalist to ensure the historical accuracy of her novel. 她咨询了一位中世纪专家，以确保她的小说的历史准确性。 | 近义词: scholar, historian | 反义词: modernist | 词性: noun
