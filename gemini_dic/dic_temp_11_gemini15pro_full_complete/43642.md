creativeness:/ˌkriːeɪˈtɪvnəs/ |n.|创造性，创造力|The state or quality of being creative; the ability to create.|Her creativeness is evident in her artwork. 她的艺术作品展现了她的创造力。|Synonyms: ingenuity, originality, inventiveness, imagination|Antonyms: unoriginality, banality, staleness|noun

Etymology->
From creative + -ness.  Creative comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin creativus, from creare ‘create’.
