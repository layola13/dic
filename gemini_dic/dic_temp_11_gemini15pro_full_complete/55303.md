dissolutive:/dɪˈsɒl.jʊ.tɪv/
adjective
溶解的; 解散的; 终止的
Having the power to dissolve or annul something; tending to dissolve or break down.
The dissolutive effects of the solvent were readily apparent. 溶剂的溶解效果显而易见。
The judge issued a dissolutive order, terminating the contract. 法官发布了一项终止令，终止了合同。
近义词: dissolving, disintegrative, destructive
反义词: constructive, formative, binding
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *dissolutivus, from Latin dissolvere "to loosen, dissolve," from dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen."
