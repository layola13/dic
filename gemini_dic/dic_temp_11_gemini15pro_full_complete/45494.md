curdle:/ˈkɜːrdl/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrdl/ (US)| v. n.|凝结，变酸; 凝乳|To cause a liquid, especially milk, to separate into curds and whey; or to become thick and lumpy.|The milk curdled when we added the lemon juice. 我们加了柠檬汁后，牛奶就凝结了.|synonyms: clot, coagulate, thicken|antonyms: liquefy, thin, dissolve|verb
