gymnosomata:/ˌdʒɪmnoʊˈsoʊmətə/ | n. | 裸壳翼足类 |  A taxonomic order of pelagic sea slugs, also known as sea angels, characterized by their lack of a shell and wing-like parapodia used for swimming.  They are found in the world's oceans, primarily in cold waters. |  Example: Gymnosomata are fascinating creatures that play an important role in the marine ecosystem. 裸壳翼足类是迷人的生物，在海洋生态系统中扮演着重要的角色。 | Example:  Researchers are studying the unique swimming adaptations of gymnosomata. 研究人员正在研究裸壳翼足类独特的游泳适应性。 | 近义词: sea angels, pteropods | 反义词:  (No direct antonym; could consider shelled pteropods like Thecosomata) | 词性: noun

gymnosomatous:/ˌdʒɪmnoʊˈsoʊmətəs/ | adj. | 裸壳翼足类的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Gymnosomata order. | Example: Gymnosomatous sea slugs are known for their graceful movements in the water. 裸壳翼足类海蛞蝓以其在水中优雅的运动而闻名。 | Example: The study focused on the gymnosomatous adaptations to cold water environments.  这项研究的重点是裸壳翼足类对冷水环境的适应。 | 近义词:  (None) | 反义词:  (None) | 词性: adjective
