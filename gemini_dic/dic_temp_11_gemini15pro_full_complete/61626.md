emigration:/ˌemɪˈɡreɪʃn/ | n. | 移民，移居国外 | The act of leaving one's own country to settle permanently in another; moving abroad. |  Many Irish people faced poverty and starvation, leading to mass emigration to the United States. 许多爱尔兰人面临贫困和饥饿，导致大规模移民到美国。| synonyms: migration, exodus, departure | antonyms: immigration, repatriation, return | noun
