melancholically:/ˌmɛlənˈkɒlɪkli/ | adv. | 忧郁地；伤感地 | In a melancholic manner; with sadness or depression. | She walked melancholically along the beach, lost in thought. 她忧郁地走在沙滩上，陷入了沉思。|  He sighed melancholically and turned away. 他忧郁地叹了口气，转过身去。| 近义词: sadly, mournfully, wistfully, pensively | 反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *melancholicus + -ly.  From Greek melankholikós, from melan- 'black' + kholé 'bile'.  Originally referring to a supposed excess of black bile causing melancholy.
