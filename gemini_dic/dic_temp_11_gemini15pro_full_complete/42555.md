counternatural:/ˌkaʊntərˈnætʃrəl/ | adj. |违反自然的；反常的|Going against the natural order or what is considered normal or expected.|例句：His counternatural calmness in the face of danger was unnerving.  面对危险，他异常的冷静令人不安。

例句：The idea of time travel seems counternatural. 时间旅行的想法似乎违反自然规律。


近义词：unnatural, abnormal, perverse, aberrant |反义词: natural, normal, typical, usual |词性: adjective
