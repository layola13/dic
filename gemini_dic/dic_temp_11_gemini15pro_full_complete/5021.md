all-for-the-best:/ɔːl fɔːr ðə best/
|adj.|
|一切都会好起来的；最终是好事；塞翁失马焉知非福|
|Used to suggest that although a situation seems bad or unpleasant now, it will have a good result in the future.|
|例句：It's sad that he lost his job, but it might be all for the best. 他丢了工作很可惜，但也许最终是好事。|

|例句：Although the breakup was painful, she eventually realized it was all for the best. 虽然分手很痛苦，但她最终意识到这是最好的结果。|
|近义词：beneficial, advantageous, fortunate, ultimately good|
|反义词：disastrous, unfortunate, calamitous|
|词性：adjective phrase|
