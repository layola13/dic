accreditation: /əˌkrɛdɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 认证，鉴定；认可 | the official process that an educational program or institution goes through to ensure quality and meet established standards.  This process involves review by an external agency and results in official recognition. | The university lost its accreditation due to insufficient resources. 这所大学由于资源不足失去了认证。| The hospital received accreditation for its excellent patient care. 这家医院因其出色的病人护理获得了认证。 |certification, approval, recognition, validation | disapproval, disqualification | noun
