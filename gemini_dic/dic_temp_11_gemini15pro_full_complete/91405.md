hydrozoa:/haɪdrəˈzoʊə/ | n. | 水螅纲 |A class of Cnidaria that includes hydras, hydroids, siphonophores, and other related aquatic animals. These organisms are predominantly marine, though some freshwater species exist. They are characterized by their simple body plan, often with a polyp stage and a medusa stage in their life cycle.| The Portuguese man-of-war is a colony of hydrozoa. 葡萄牙僧帽水母是一种水螅纲动物的群体。|  The hydra, a common freshwater hydrozoan, reproduces asexually by budding.  水螅，一种常见的淡水水螅纲动物，通过出芽进行无性繁殖。 |近义词: cnidarians |反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
