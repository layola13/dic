词源:
From Old English *dernian, from Proto-Germanic *darnijaną (“to mend, conceal”). Cognate with Old Norse derna (“to hide, conceal”), Old High German tarnēn (“to hide, conceal”), Gothic 𐌳𐌰𐍂𐌽𐌾𐌰𐌽 (darnijan, “to hide”). Related to dreary.  The original sense was "to hide, conceal (a flaw)", hence "to mend".
