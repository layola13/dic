gosling:/ˈɡɒzlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːzlɪŋ/ (US)| n.|小鹅，幼鹅|A young goose, especially one that is still covered with down.|例句：The goslings followed their mother to the pond. 小鹅们跟着它们的妈妈去了池塘。|例句：We saw a family of goslings waddling along the riverbank. 我们看到一群小鹅沿着河岸蹒跚而行。|近义词：chick, fledgling|反义词:adult goose|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gōsling, diminutive of gōs (“goose”). Cognate with German Gänschen (“gosling”), West Frisian goesling (“gosling”).
