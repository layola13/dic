crusted:/ˈkrʌstɪd/ /ˈkrʌstəd/| adj.| покрытый коркой, с коркой; затвердевший|Covered with a hard outer layer; hardened.|The old bread had a thick, crusted top.  这块老面包的顶部有一层厚厚的硬皮。|The lake was crusted with ice. 湖面上结了一层冰。|近义词：hardened, encrusted, coated|反义词:soft, smooth|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From crust + -ed.  "Crust" comes from the Old French "crouste," ultimately from the Latin "crusta" meaning "shell, rind, bark."
