lightner:/ˈlaɪtnər/| n.| 淡化剂; 增亮剂; 亮化剂 |A substance used to lighten or brighten something, such as hair or skin. |She used a hair lightner to achieve a blonde look. 她用了一种头发增亮剂来达到金发的效果。| The gardener used a soil lightner to improve drainage. 园丁使用土壤改良剂来改善排水。 |synonyms: brightener, bleach, whitener| antonyms: darkener, shader| noun
