mannitol:/ˈmænɪˌtɒl/ (US) /ˈmænɪˌtɔːl/ (UK)| n. |甘露醇|A white crystalline sugar alcohol C6H14O6 found in many plants and used especially as a diuretic and a sweetening agent.|Mannitol is used to reduce swelling in the brain. 甘露醇用于减轻脑肿胀。|Synonyms: sugar alcohol, hexitol|Antonyms: None|noun

mannitose:/ˈmænɪˌtoʊs/ (US) /ˈmænɪˌtəʊs/ (UK)| n. |甘露糖|A simple sugar C6H12O6 that is a stereoisomer of glucose and occurs naturally in some plants and fruits.|Mannose is a sugar monomer of the aldohexose series of carbohydrates. 甘露糖是碳水化合物中己醛糖系列的一种单糖。|Synonyms: hexose, aldohexose|Antonyms: None|noun
