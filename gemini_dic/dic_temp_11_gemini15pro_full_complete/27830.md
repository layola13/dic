例句：Experienced canoers know how to handle strong currents.  有经验的划独木舟者知道如何应对强劲的水流。|近义词：paddler, oarsman, boatman|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps passenger if contrasting active vs. passive)|词性:noun
