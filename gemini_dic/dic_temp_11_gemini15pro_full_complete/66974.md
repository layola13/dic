experimenters:/ɪkˈspɛrɪməntərz/ | n. | 实验者，实验人员 | People who conduct experiments; researchers who systematically investigate phenomena. | The experimenters carefully recorded the results of each trial. 实验人员仔细记录了每次试验的结果。|  The experimenters manipulated the variables to observe the effects on the subjects. 实验人员操纵变量以观察其对受试者的影响。 | synonyms: researchers, investigators, scientists, testers | antonyms: subjects, participants | noun
