attest to:/əˈtɛst tuː/
verb
证明；证实
To provide evidence or confirm the truth of something.
例句：The numerous awards on his wall attest to his skill as a lawyer. 他墙上众多的奖项证明了他作为一名律师的技能。

例句：Several witnesses can attest to her presence at the scene. 几位目击者可以证明她在现场。

近义词：confirm, verify, corroborate, substantiate, testify to, bear witness to
反义词：deny, contradict, refute, disprove
词性：verb (phrasal verb)
