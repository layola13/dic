authotype:/ˈɔːθoʊˌtaɪp/ | n. | 自体 | A specimen of a species used by the author of its original description. It serves as the definitive reference for the species. | The authotype of this newly discovered beetle is kept in the Natural History Museum.  这种新发现的甲虫的自体保存在自然历史博物馆。| synonym: holotype, type specimen | antonym: N/A | noun
