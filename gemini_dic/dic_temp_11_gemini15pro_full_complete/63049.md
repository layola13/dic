enriching:/ɪnˈrɪtʃɪŋ/ /ɛnˈrɪtʃɪŋ/
adjective
使富裕的；充实的；有益的
Making something better or more valuable, often by adding something to it.
例句：Traveling is an enriching experience. 旅行是一次丰富的经历。

例句：Volunteering at the animal shelter was an enriching experience for her. 在动物收容所做志愿者对她来说是一次丰富的经历。

近义词：rewarding, fulfilling, valuable, improving, enhancing
反义词：impoverishing, depleting, draining, vapid
词性: adjective
