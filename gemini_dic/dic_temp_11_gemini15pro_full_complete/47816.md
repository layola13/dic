decarburize:/diːˈkɑːrbəraɪz/
verb
脱碳，除去…的碳
To remove carbon from (something, such as steel or iron).
The steel was decarburized in a furnace.  这种钢材是在熔炉中脱碳的。

The process decarburizes the iron. 这个过程除去了铁中的碳。

synonym: decarbonize, decarbonise
antonym: carburize, carburise
verb
