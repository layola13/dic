astatically:/eɪˈstætɪkli/ | adv. | 无定向地；不稳定地 | Without a fixed direction or position; unstably.  | The compass needle spun astatically, influenced by a nearby magnetic field. 指南针的指针受到附近磁场的影响，不稳定地旋转。 | Example: The particles moved astatically in the solution. 粒子在溶液中不稳定地运动。 | Synonyms: erratically, unsteadily, randomly, irregularly | Antonyms: stably, fixedly, steadily | 词性: 副词
