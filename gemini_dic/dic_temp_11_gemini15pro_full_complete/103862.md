ju-jitsu: /dʒuːˈdʒɪtsuː/ /dʒʊˈdʒɪtsuː/
n.
柔术
A Japanese martial art and method of close combat for defeating an armed and armored opponent in which one uses no weapon or only a short weapon.
She's a black belt in ju-jitsu. 她柔道是黑带。

近义词:jiu-jitsu, judo, aikido
反义词: (No direct antonym for a martial art, possibly consider concepts like pacifism or non-violence)
词性: noun
