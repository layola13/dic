encumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/| v. |阻碍，妨碍，拖累|To burden or weigh down; to impede or hinder the function or activity of.|例句：The hiker was encumbered by a heavy backpack.  徒步旅行者被沉重的背包拖累了。

例句：Debt encumbers many young people today. 债务拖累了今天的许多年轻人。|近义词：hinder, impede, obstruct, burden|反义词:aid, assist, help|词性:动词
