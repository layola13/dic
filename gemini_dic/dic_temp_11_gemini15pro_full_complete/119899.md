militarized:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/ | adj. |军事化的|Equipped with military forces and weapons, or adapted for military purposes; having a strong military presence or influence.|例句：The border region has become heavily militarized.  边境地区已经高度军事化了。

例句：The government militarized the police force. 政府将警察部队军事化了。|近义词：armed, fortified, weaponized|反义词：demilitarized, civilianized|词性:形容词


词源:
From militarize + -ed.  From military + -ize. Ultimately from Latin mīlitāris (“of a soldier”).
