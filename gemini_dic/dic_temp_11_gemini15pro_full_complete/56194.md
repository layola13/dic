dodgem:/ˈdɒdʒəm/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒəm/ (US)| n.|碰碰车|A small electric car driven at a fairground, amusement park, etc., where people deliberately bump into each other for fun.|We had a great time riding the dodgems at the fair. 我们在游乐场玩碰碰车玩得很开心。|Synonyms: bumper car|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

dodgem:/ˈdɒdʒəm/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒəm/ (US)| v.|驾驶碰碰车|To drive a dodgem car.|He dodged the other cars skillfully. 他熟练地躲避着其他车辆。|Synonyms: bump, crash (informal)|Antonyms: avoid|Verb

词源:  From *dodge* (verb) + *-em*, a suffix used to form nouns from verbs, often implying repeated or playful action (like *frolicsome*).  The verb *dodge* means "to avoid by a sudden shift of position."  Thus, a *dodgem* is a car used for playfully dodging other cars.
