fluidness:/ˈfluːɪdnəs/| n.|流畅性，流动性|The fluidness of her movements was captivating.  她的动作流畅，令人着迷。|例句：The artist admired the fluidness of the dancer's movements. 艺术家欣赏舞者动作的流畅性。

The fluidness of the prose made it easy to read. 这篇文章的流畅性使它易于阅读。|近义词：fluidity, smoothness, grace, effortlessnes|反义词:stiffness, rigidity, awkwardness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fluid + -ness.  Fluid (meaning "flowing") comes from the Latin word "fluidus," which in turn derives from "fluere," meaning "to flow." The suffix "-ness" transforms an adjective (fluid) into a noun denoting a state or quality.
