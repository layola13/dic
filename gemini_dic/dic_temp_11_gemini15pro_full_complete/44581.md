crouper:/ˈkruːpər/ (US), /ˈɡruːpər/ (UK)| n. |石斑鱼|A large sea fish of the Serranidae family, many species of which are caught for food. |例句：The fisherman caught a huge grouper. 渔夫钓到了一条巨大的石斑鱼。

例句：Grouper is a popular dish in many coastal regions. 石斑鱼是许多沿海地区的热门菜肴。|近义词：sea bass, rockfish|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of fish)|词性:noun
