favoress:/ˈfeɪvərɪs/
n.
女宠儿，受宠的女子
a woman who is treated with special favor by someone, especially a person of higher rank.
Elizabeth I was a great favoress of the arts. 伊丽莎白一世是艺术的伟大赞助人。
近义词: favorite, darling, pet
反义词: outcast, pariah
词性: 名词


词源:
From Anglo-Norman *favouresse, feminine of favouré (“favored, beloved”), past participle of favourer (“to favor”), from Latin favor (“favor, goodwill”).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *gʰewh₂- (“to like, love”).
