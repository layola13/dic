eskar:/ˈɛskər/ | n. |冰磧壟，蛇形丘|A long, winding ridge of stratified sand and gravel deposited by a stream flowing in or under a melting glacier.|  The hikers followed the eskar across the barren landscape.  徒步旅行者沿着冰碛垄穿过荒芜的景色。

Eskers are important features in understanding past glacial activity. 冰磧壟是了解过去冰川活动的重要特征。 | 近义词:ridge, mound | 反义词:valley | 词性:noun
