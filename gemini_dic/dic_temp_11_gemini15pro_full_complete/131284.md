noncelestial:/ˌnɒnsɪˈlɛstiəl/
adjective
非天体的，尘世的
Relating to earthly things as opposed to heavenly or spiritual things; not of the heavens.
The museum's collection includes both celestial and noncelestial globes.  这个博物馆的收藏包括天体和非天体地球仪。

近义词: earthly, terrestrial, mundane, worldly
反义词: celestial, heavenly, spiritual
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + celestial (from Latin caelestis, meaning "heavenly")
