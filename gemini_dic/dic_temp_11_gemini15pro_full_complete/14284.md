autoharp:/ˈɔːtəˌhɑːrp/ | n. | 自鸣竖琴 | A stringed musical instrument with buttons that, when depressed, mute all strings except those that produce the desired chord.  | She played a beautiful melody on the autoharp. 她用自鸣竖琴演奏了一曲优美的旋律。 | My grandfather loved to play the autoharp on the porch. 我祖父喜欢在门廊上弹奏自鸣竖琴。| 近义词: zither, chord zither | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific musical instrument) | noun
