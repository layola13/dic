noncredulousnesses:/ˌnɒnkrɪˈdʒuːləsnəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnkrɪˈdʒuːləsnəsɪz/
noun
轻信；容易受骗
The state of being noncredulous; skepticism.  Lack of credulity; unwillingness or inability to believe.
His noncredulousnesses about the supernatural led him to question the psychic's claims. 他对超自然现象的怀疑使他对通灵者的说法提出了质疑。
noncredulity, skepticism, disbelief, doubt
credulity, gullibility, naivety
noun
