allaying:/əˈleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 减轻，缓和，平息 |  reducing or diminishing the intensity of (something unpleasant, such as fear, suspicion, or pain). | The government is trying to allay public fears about the spread of the virus. 政府正在努力减轻公众对病毒传播的恐惧。| Synonyms: alleviate, mitigate, assuage, soothe, calm, pacify | Antonyms: aggravate, exacerbate, intensify, heighten, provoke, incite | verb

allaying:/əˈleɪɪŋ/ | adj. |  减轻的，缓和的 | serving to reduce or diminish the intensity of something unpleasant | The medicine had an allaying effect on his pain.  这药对他的疼痛有缓解作用。| Synonyms: soothing, calming, relieving, mitigating | Antonyms: aggravating, exacerbating, intensifying | adjective
