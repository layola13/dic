jaywalking:/ˈdʒeɪˌwɔːkɪŋ/
n.
乱穿马路
The act of crossing a street at a place other than a designated crosswalk, or without regard for traffic signals.
Jaywalking is illegal in many cities.  在许多城市，乱穿马路是违法的。
The police officer ticketed the pedestrian for jaywalking. 警察给那个行人开了乱穿马路的罚单。
crossing against the light, illegally crossing the street
obeying traffic laws
noun
