blogrolls:/ˈblɒɡrəʊlz/ (US) /ˈblɑːɡroʊlz/
noun
博客列表，博客卷
A list of blogs, often displayed on a blog or website, that the owner recommends or reads regularly.  This allows visitors to easily discover other blogs of interest.
I regularly update the blogrolls on my website to share new finds with my readers. 我定期更新我网站上的博客列表，与我的读者分享新的发现。
My blogrolls include sites about cooking, travel, and technology. 我的博客列表包含关于烹饪、旅行和技术的网站。
synonyms: blog list, link list, weblog roll
antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
