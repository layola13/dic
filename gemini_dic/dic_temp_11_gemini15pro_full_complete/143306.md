outduel: /ˌaʊtˈdjuːəl/ /ˌaʊtˈduːəl/
| vt. | 战胜，击败（尤指通过技巧或策略）| To defeat someone in a duel or contest, especially by using skill or cunning. | He outdueled his opponent in a fierce debate. 他在一场激烈的辩论中击败了对手。|
| | | The two knights outdueled each other for the princess's hand. 两位骑士为了公主的手而决斗。|
|近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, defeat, conquer, vanquish |
|反义词：lose to, be defeated by |
|词性：动词|
