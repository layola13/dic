demitone:/ˈdemɪˌtəʊn/ (UK), /ˈdemɪˌtoʊn/ (US)| n.|半音|A semitone is the smallest interval used in Western music. It is the difference in pitch between two adjacent notes on a piano keyboard, including both white and black keys.|The piece is written in the key of C sharp minor, with frequent use of demitones.|synonym: half step, half tone|antonym: whole tone, whole step|noun
