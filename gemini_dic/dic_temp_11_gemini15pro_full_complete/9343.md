antiproductive:/ˌæntɪprəˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
适得其反的，无益的
Having the opposite effect to that intended; tending to hinder the achievement of a goal.
Her constant complaining was antiproductive. 她不断的抱怨适得其反。
Excessive bureaucracy can be antiproductive. 过多的官僚主义可能适得其反。
近义词:counterproductive, unproductive, detrimental, harmful
反义词:productive, helpful, beneficial
词性:adjective

anti-/ænti-/
prefix
反对，抵抗
Against; opposed to.
antibiotic 抗生素
近义词:against, opposing
反义词:pro-, for
词性:prefix

productive:/prəˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
富有成效的
Producing or able to produce large amounts of something.
He had a very productive meeting. 他进行了一次非常富有成效的会议。
She is a productive writer. 她是一位多产的作家。
近义词:fruitful, effective, efficient
反义词:unproductive, barren
词性:adjective
