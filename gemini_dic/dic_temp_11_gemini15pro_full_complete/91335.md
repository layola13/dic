hydrophyte:/ˈhaɪdrəˌfaɪt/| n.|水生植物|A plant that grows only in or on water.|Water lilies are hydrophytes that thrive in ponds.  睡莲是在池塘中茁壮成长的水生植物。|近义词：aquatic plant|反义词:xerophyte|词性:noun

hydrophyte:/ˈhaɪdrəˌfaɪt/| n.|水生植物|A plant adapted to growing in aquatic environments, either completely or partially submerged.|Mangroves are hydrophytes adapted to salty coastal waters.  红树林是适应咸水沿海水域的水生植物。|近义词：water plant|反义词:desert plant|词性:noun
