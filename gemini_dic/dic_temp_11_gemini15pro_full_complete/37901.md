completely /kəmˈpliːtli/ | adv. | 完全地，彻底地 | Entirely; to the full extent; totally. |  I completely forgot about our appointment. 我完全忘记了我们的约会。 | completely finished the project. 我彻底完成了这个项目。| 近义词: totally, entirely, fully, absolutely | 反义词: partially, incompletely, slightly | 词性: 副词


词源: From Middle English *completlely, from Old French *completelment, from Latin complētementum (“completion”), from complēre (“to fill up”).
