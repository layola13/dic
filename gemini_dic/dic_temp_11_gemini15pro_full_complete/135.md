abandonments: /əˈbændənmənts/
n.
放弃，抛弃，遗弃
The act of abandoning something or someone.
The orphanage was filled with children of abandonments. 这家孤儿院里满是遭到遗弃的孩子。
The abandonments of the city after the hurricane was widespread. 飓风过后，这座城市的遗弃现象十分普遍。
Synonyms: desertion, relinquishment, forsaking
Antonyms: adoption, acceptance, reclaiming
noun
