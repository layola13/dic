overproduce:/ˌoʊvərprəˈduːs/ /ˌəʊvəprəˈdjuːs/| v. |过度生产，生产过剩|To produce more than is needed or wanted.|The factory overproduced goods, leading to a surplus in the warehouse. 工厂生产过剩，导致仓库积压。|近义词：overmanufacture, glut, saturate|反义词: underproduce|词性:动词

overproduction:/ˌoʊvərprəˈdʌkʃən/ /ˌəʊvəprəˈdʌkʃən/| n. |生产过剩，过度生产|The act of producing more than is needed or wanted.|The overproduction of oil caused prices to plummet. 石油生产过剩导致价格暴跌。|近义词：surplus, glut, excess|反义词: shortage, undersupply|词性:名词
