caffetannic: /ˌkæfiˈtænɪk/ | adj. | 咖啡鞣酸的，咖啡单宁酸的 |  Relating to or designating caffetannic acid, a tannin found in coffee.  It's a type of chlorogenic acid, not a true tannin. | Coffee's bitterness is partly attributed to its caffetannic acid content. 咖啡的苦味部分归因于其咖啡鞣酸含量。|  Caffetannic acid contributes to the antioxidant properties of coffee. 咖啡鞣酸有助于咖啡的抗氧化特性。 | 近义词: chlorogenic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms related to sweetness or lack of bitterness) | 词性: adjective

词源:
caffe- (from Italian caffè, from Arabic qahwah) + tannic (from French tannique, from tan)
