burrel:/ˈbʌrəl/| n.|粗麻布；粗麻布衣服；麻袋|A coarse, rough fabric made from jute, hemp, or other similar fibers, often used for making sacks or bags.  It can also refer to clothing made from this fabric.|The farmer stored the grain in a burrel. 农民把粮食储存在麻袋里。|Synonyms: sackcloth, hessian, burlap|Antonyms: silk, satin|noun
