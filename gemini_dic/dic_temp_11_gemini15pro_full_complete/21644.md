blurted:/ˈblɜːrtɪd/ (US) /ˈblɜːtɪd/ (UK)| v.|脱口而出，突然说出|To say something suddenly and without thinking, usually because you are excited or nervous.|He blurted out the secret before he could stop himself. 他还没来得及阻止自己就脱口说出了这个秘密。|synonyms: exclaimed, ejaculated, uttered, divulged|antonyms: concealed, suppressed|verb

blurting:/ˈblɜːrtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈblɜːtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|脱口而出，突然说出|The act of saying something suddenly and without thinking.|His blurting of the secret surprised everyone. 他脱口而出这个秘密让每个人都感到惊讶。|synonyms: outburst, exclamation, ejaculation|antonyms: concealment, suppression|noun
