kampylite:/kæmˈpɪlaɪt/
n.|硫砷铅矿|A mineral consisting of lead arsenide sulfide, Pb5(AsO4)3Cl.  It is a secondary mineral formed in the oxidized zones of lead deposits.|Kampylite is a rare mineral found in oxidized lead deposits.|近义词:mimetite (a similar lead arsenate mineral)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *kampylos* (curved), referring to the curved crystals sometimes formed.
