eldritch:/ˈeldrɪtʃ/ | adj. | 怪异的，可怕的，邪恶的 | Weird and sinister; uncanny.  Derived from Middle English *elriche, from Old English *ylfe + *rīċe 'powerful,' 'kingdom of elves.'  Implies a supernatural, otherworldly eeriness. | The house had an eldritch atmosphere, as if something unnatural lurked within. 这栋房子有一种怪异的气氛，就好像里面潜伏着什么不自然的东西。 |  例句：The forest gave off an eldritch glow at twilight.  暮色中，森林发出一种怪异的光芒。| 近义词：eerie, uncanny, weird, supernatural, ghostly | 反义词：normal, ordinary, mundane | 词性: 形容词
