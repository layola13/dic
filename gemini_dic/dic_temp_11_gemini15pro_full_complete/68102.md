faeces:/ˈfiːsiːz/ (BrE) /ˈfisiːz/ (AmE)| n. |粪便，排泄物|Solid waste discharged from the bowels; excrement.|The vet examined the dog's faeces for parasites. 兽医检查了狗的粪便，看看有没有寄生虫。

The analysis of faeces can reveal a lot about an animal's diet. 对粪便的分析可以揭示很多关于动物饮食的信息。|近义词：excrement, stool, bowel movement, dung|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
