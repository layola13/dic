ibn:/ɪbn/ | n. | 儿子，后裔 | Son, descendant (of), literally "son of" in Arabic. Used in names, especially in Muslim cultures, to indicate lineage. | Example: Ibn Sina (Avicenna), literally "son of Sina." 伊本·西纳（阿维森纳），字面意思是“西纳之子”。| Synonym: son, offspring, descendant | Antonym: father, ancestor | noun
