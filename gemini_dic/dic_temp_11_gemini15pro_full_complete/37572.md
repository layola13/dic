communalism: /kəˈmjuːnəlɪzəm/ | n. | 共产主义，社群主义，地方主义 |  A principle of political organization based on federated communes.  A theory or system of social organization based on small self-governing communities.  An allegiance to one's own ethnic group rather than to the wider society.  | Example 1: The country experimented with communalism in the 1960s.  这个国家在20世纪60年代尝试了社群主义。

Example 2:  Communalism can sometimes lead to social division.  地方主义有时会导致社会分裂。 | Synonym: collectivism, communism, localism | Antonym: individualism, nationalism, globalism | noun
