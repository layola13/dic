beautied:/ˈbjuːtɪfaɪd/
verb
美化了的，使变美的
past participle of beautify: made more beautiful.  The sunset beautified the landscape. 日落使风景更加美丽。
The flowers beautified the room.  花朵美化了房间。
Synonym: embellished, adorned, enhanced
Antonym: uglified, marred, spoiled
