mesoseme:/ˈmɛsoʊˌsiːm/
n.|中义词|A word or phrase whose meaning is intermediate between a more general and a more specific term.  It occupies a middle ground in a semantic hierarchy.|The word "animal" is a mesoseme, broader than "mammal" but narrower than "living thing".|近义词: mid-level term, intermediate term|反义词: hypernym (broader term), hyponym (narrower term)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek *mesos* "middle" + *sēma* "sign, mark"
