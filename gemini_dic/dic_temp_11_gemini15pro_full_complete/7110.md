aneurysmal:/ˌænjuˈrɪz.məl/ | adj. | 动脉瘤的；像动脉瘤的；膨大的；不稳定的 | Relating to or resembling an aneurysm; exhibiting a sudden or dramatic expansion or enlargement, often suggesting instability. |  The aneurysmal dilation of the aorta posed a serious threat to his health. 主动脉瘤样扩张对他的健康构成严重威胁。 |  The stock market experienced an aneurysmal surge before crashing. 股票市场在崩盘前经历了剧烈的飙升。 | 近义词：expanded, dilated, swollen, unstable, volatile | 反义词：contracted, stable, steady | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From aneurysm + -al.  Aneurysm comes from the Late Latin aneurysma, from Greek aneurysma "a widening," from aneurynanai "to widen," from ana- "up, throughout" + eurynan "to widen."
