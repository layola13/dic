nutritiously:/nʊˈtrɪʃəsli/
adverb
营养地；滋养地
in a way that provides the substances that people, animals, and plants need to live and grow healthily.
She tries to eat nutritiously. 她尽量吃得营养。
近义词:healthily, healthfully
反义词:unhealthily
词性:adverb

词源:
From nutritious + -ly.  Nutritious comes from Late Latin nūtrīcius (“nourishing”), from Latin nūtrīre (“to nourish”).
