blusterous: /ˈblʌstərəs/ (US), /ˈblʌstərəs/ (UK) | adj. | 狂暴的，喧闹的，咆哮的 | Characterized by strong winds and noise; loud and noisy; boastful and aggressive.  | Example: The blusterous wind howled through the trees. 狂风呼啸着穿过树林。 | Example: His blusterous personality intimidated many people. 他咋咋呼呼的性格吓到了很多人。 | Synonyms: boisterous, loud, noisy, tumultuous, stormy, wild, raging, fierce, violent, vehement, aggressive, swaggering | Antonyms: calm, peaceful, quiet, gentle, mild, serene, tranquil | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blustren*, from Old Norse *blystr*, meaning "blast, gust."
