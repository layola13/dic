absolute:/ˈæbsəluːt/ | adj. n.| 绝对的；完全的；专制的；绝对值；绝对事物 |  Free from imperfection; complete; perfect.  |  He ruled with absolute power.  他实行专制统治。 | synonyms: complete, total, perfect, utter, thorough, outright, downright, unqualified, unconditional, unlimited, unrestricted, unrestrained, unmitigated, sheer, pure, veritable, positive, definite, certain, unequivocal, unambiguous | antonyms: relative, qualified, conditional | 词性: 形容词, 名词

absolute:/ˈæbsəluːts/ | n. | 绝对值；绝对事物（absolute的复数） |  The magnitude of a real number without regard to its sign.  | The graph shows the absolutes of the differences between the two measurements.  图表显示了两个测量值之间差异的绝对值。| synonyms: magnitude, value | antonyms: relative values | 词性: 名词
