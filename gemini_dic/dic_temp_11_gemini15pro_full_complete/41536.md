coronership: /ˈkɔːrənərʃɪp/ /ˈkɑːrənərʃɪp/
n.
验尸官职位；验尸官任期
The office or tenure of a coroner.
The coronership is an elected position. 验尸官职位是选举产生的。
He held the coronership for over twenty years. 他担任验尸官的职位超过二十年。
Synonyms: coroner's office, coroner's position
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms. One might consider related roles in the legal system, such as judgeship or attorneyship, as conceptually distant opposites.)
词性: noun
