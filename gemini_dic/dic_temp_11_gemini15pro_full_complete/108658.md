leaflet: /ˈliːflɪt/ (US), /ˈliːflət/ (UK) | n. | 传单；小册子；散页印刷品 | A printed sheet of paper, sometimes folded, containing information or advertising and usually distributed free. |  They were handing out leaflets about the upcoming election. 他们在散发有关即将举行的选举的传单。

The travel agent gave me a leaflet about holidays in Greece. 旅行社给了我一本关于希腊度假的宣传册。


近义词: pamphlet, flyer, brochure, handbill, circular
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "book" for a longer, more substantial publication)
词性: noun
