cadre:/ˈkɑːdrə/ (US), /ˈkɑːdə/ (UK)| n.| 骨干，干部；核心小组|A small group of trained people who form the basic unit of a military, political, or business organization. |The party's cadre was made up of experienced and dedicated members. 该党的骨干由经验丰富、忠诚的党员组成。

The company sent a cadre of engineers to troubleshoot the problem. 公司派出一组工程师去解决这个问题。|近义词: core, nucleus, team, group|反义词: masses, rank and file|词性: noun
