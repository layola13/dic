indenization:/ɪnˌdɪdʒənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |本土化；本地化；归化|The process of adapting something to local conditions or making it locally owned and controlled.  | The government is pursuing a policy of indenization of industry. 政府正在推行产业本土化政策。|  Example: The indenization of the curriculum was necessary to make it relevant to the students' lives.  课程的本土化对于使其与学生的生活相关是必要的。 |Synonyms: localization, nationalization, domestication| Antonyms: globalization, internationalization | noun
