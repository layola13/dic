lunging:/ˈlʌndʒɪŋ/
v.
猛冲，突进
A sudden forward movement.
The boxer made a lunging attack. 拳击手做了一次猛冲攻击。

lunging:/ˈlʌndʒɪŋ/
n.
猛冲，突进
The act of making a sudden forward movement.
His lunging caught his opponent off guard. 他的猛冲让对手措手不及。

词源: From lunge + -ing.  "Lunge" originates from the French word "allonger," meaning "to lengthen."

近义词:  pouncing, thrusting, diving, darting
反义词: retreating, withdrawing, recoiling

词性:  Verb (present participle), Noun (gerund)
