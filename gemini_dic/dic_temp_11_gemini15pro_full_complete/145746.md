overcrowding:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪŋ/
n.
过度拥挤；人满为患
The state of having too many people or things in a space.
The overcrowding in the city makes it difficult to find affordable housing. 城市里人口过密，很难找到负担得起的住房。
Overcrowding in prisons is a serious problem. 监狱过度拥挤是一个严重的问题。
overpopulation, congestion
underpopulation, sparseness
