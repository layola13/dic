nonstimulants:/ˌnɒnˈstɪmjʊlənts/ /ˌnɑːnˈstɪmjələnts/| n. |非兴奋剂| Medications used to treat ADHD that do not contain stimulants.  They work differently than stimulants but can still improve focus, attention, and impulsivity. |Example: My doctor prescribed nonstimulants for my ADHD because stimulants caused too many side effects. 我的医生给我开了非兴奋剂治疗多动症，因为兴奋剂会引起太多副作用。

|Example:  Nonstimulants are often a good option for people who cannot tolerate stimulants. 对于不能耐受兴奋剂的人来说，非兴奋剂通常是一个不错的选择。|近义词: non-stimulant medications,  ADHD medications (broad), |反义词: stimulants, stimulant medications |词性: noun

Doctors often prescribe nonstimulants for ADHD. 医生经常开非兴奋剂治疗多动症。

Some people respond better to nonstimulants than stimulants. 有些人对非兴奋剂的反应比兴奋剂好。

Nonstimulants can improve focus and attention. 非兴奋剂可以提高注意力和集中力。

Nonstimulants are sometimes used in combination with behavioral therapy. 非兴奋剂有时与行为疗法结合使用。
