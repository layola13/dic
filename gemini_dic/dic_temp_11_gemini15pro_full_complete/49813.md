demonstrable:/dɪˈmɒnstrəbl/ /dɪˈmɑːnstrəbl/| adj.|可证明的，可论证的，显而易见的|clearly apparent or capable of being logically proved.|例句：There is demonstrable evidence that smoking causes cancer. 有确凿的证据表明吸烟会导致癌症。|近义词：provable, evident, apparent, obvious|反义词：undemonstrable, unprovable, dubious|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin demonstrabilis, from demonstrare ‘demonstrate’.
