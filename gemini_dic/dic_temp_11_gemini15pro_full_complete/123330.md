monography:/məˈnɒɡrəfi/ /məˈnɑːɡrəfi/| n. |专题著作；专论|A detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it.|例句：He is the author of a monograph on the butterflies of Africa. 他是非洲蝴蝶专论的作者。

例句：She's writing a monograph on the life of Emily Dickinson. 她正在写一本关于艾米莉·狄金森生平的专著。|近义词：treatise, dissertation, study, paper|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology->
From French *monographie*, from Ancient Greek *μονographia* (monographia, “writing on a single subject”), from *μόνος* (mónos, “single, alone”) + *γράφειν* (gráphein, “to write”).
