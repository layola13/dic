discontentive:/dɪskənˈtɛntɪv/
adjective
不满的；不满足的
Causing or showing discontent; dissatisfied.
The workers were discontentive with their low wages.  工人们对低工资不满。
He gave a discontentive sigh. 他不满地叹了口气。

近义词:discontented, dissatisfied, malcontent, disgruntled
反义词:content, satisfied, happy, pleased
词性:adjective
