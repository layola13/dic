factful:/ˈfæktfʊl/
adjective
基于事实的；尊重事实的；
Based on facts; concerned with what is actually the case.
Factful decision-making relies on data and evidence, not assumptions.  基于事实的决策依赖于数据和证据，而不是假设。
Governments should pursue factful policies. 政府应该奉行基于事实的政策。
Synonyms: evidence-based, data-driven, empirical, factual
Antonyms:  misinformed,  uninformed, biased, prejudiced
