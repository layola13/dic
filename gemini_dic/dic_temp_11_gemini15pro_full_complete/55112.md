disruly:/dɪˈsruːli/| adj.| unruly, disorderly, undisciplined| behaving in a way that does not obey rules or authority; difficult to control| The disruly children were shouting and running around. 这些不守规矩的孩子又喊又叫地跑来跑去。|Synonyms: unruly, boisterous, rowdy, rambunctious, turbulent, uncontrolled, wild, lawless|Antonyms: obedient, well-behaved, disciplined, orderly, compliant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + rule + -ly (forming adverbs and adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *disregula, from dis- + regula ("rule").
