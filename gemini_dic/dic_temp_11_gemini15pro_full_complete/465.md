ablution:/əˈbluːʃən/| n.| 洗礼，净礼；沐浴；洗涤|The act of washing oneself, especially as a religious ritual.|例句：Ritual ablutions are performed before prayer.  祷告前进行仪式性的洗礼。

例句：He performed his ablutions in the river. 他在河里进行了沐浴。|近义词：washing, cleansing, purification, bath|反义词：contamination, soiling|词性:名词
