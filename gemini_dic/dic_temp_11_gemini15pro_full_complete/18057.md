begrudgingly:/bɪˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli/
adverb
勉强地; 不情愿地
Reluctantly or resentfully, as if giving something up unwillingly.
He begrudgingly agreed to help. 他勉强同意帮忙。
Near Synonyms: reluctantly, unwillingly, resentfully, grudgingly
Antonyms: willingly, readily, gladly, cheerfully
词性: 副词
