nanoparticles:/ˈnænoʊˌpɑːrtɪkəlz/| n. |纳米颗粒，纳米粒子|Extremely small particles with dimensions between 1 and 100 nanometers.|Scientists are exploring the use of nanoparticles in medicine. 科学家们正在探索纳米颗粒在医学中的应用。|Synonyms: nanomaterials, ultrafine particles|Antonyms: bulk materials|noun
