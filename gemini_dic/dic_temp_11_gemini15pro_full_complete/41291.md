cordless:/ˈkɔːrdləs/ (US) /ˈkɔːdləs/ (UK)| adj.| 无绳的，无线的|Without a cord; operating without being connected by a cord.  Powered by batteries.| I prefer a cordless vacuum cleaner because it's so much easier to use. 我更喜欢无线吸尘器，因为它用起来方便多了。|

My new cordless headphones are great for working out. 我的新无线耳机非常适合锻炼时使用。
A cordless drill is a handy tool to have around the house.  一个无线电钻是家里必备的方便工具。

近义词：wireless, portable, mobile|
反义词：corded, wired|
