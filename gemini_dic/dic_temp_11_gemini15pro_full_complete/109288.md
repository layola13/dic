lemures:/ˈlɛmjʊriːz/
n.
shades, ghosts, spirits of the dead (especially harmful ones)
Ghosts or spirits of the dead, especially in Roman religion, often considered malevolent.
The ancient Romans placated the lemures with offerings during Lemuria, a festival to appease restless spirits.
specters, phantoms, wraiths
