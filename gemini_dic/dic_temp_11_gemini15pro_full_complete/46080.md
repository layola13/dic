cyclopedic:/ˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. | 百科全书式的；知识渊博的；包罗万象的 | encompassing a wide range of knowledge; like an encyclopedia |  He has a cyclopedic knowledge of baseball. 他对棒球的知识非常渊博。| encyclopedic, comprehensive, exhaustive | limited, narrow, specialized | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cyclopédique*, from *cyclo-*, ultimately from Greek *kúklos* "circle" + *paideía* "education, rearing of a child."  Meaning "encyclopedic" arose from the "circular" or comprehensive nature of encyclopedias.
