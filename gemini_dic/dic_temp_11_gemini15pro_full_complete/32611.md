chirpiest:/ˈtʃɜːrpiɪst/| adj.|最活泼的，最快乐的|Superlative form of chirpy: most chirpy.  It describes someone who is exceedingly cheerful and lively, often expressing this through their voice.|例句：She was the chirpiest person in the room, her laughter infectious. 她房间里最活泼的人，笑声很有感染力。

例句：Of all the birds in the forest, the robin was the chirpiest. 森林里所有的鸟中，知更鸟是最快乐的。|近义词：cheeriest, most cheerful, most upbeat, most lively|反义词：gloomiest, most depressed, most sullen|词性:形容词
