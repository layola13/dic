low-level:/ˈloʊˌlɛvəl/| adj.|低级的，底层的，基础的|Relating to the fundamental or basic operations of a computer or system, often involving direct interaction with hardware or operating at a less abstract level.|Example: Low-level programming languages are closer to machine code. 低级编程语言更接近机器代码。|Example: The error was caused by a low-level hardware malfunction. 这个错误是由底层硬件故障引起的。|近义词：basic, fundamental, elementary, simple|反义词:high-level, advanced, complex|词性:adjective
