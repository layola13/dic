nonimports:/ˌnɑːnˈɪmpɔːrts/ | n. | 非进口商品；未进口商品 | Goods or services that are not imported from another country. |  Example: The store focuses on selling nonimports to support local businesses. 这家商店专注于销售非进口商品以支持当地企业。

Example:  The government is encouraging the consumption of nonimports to boost the domestic economy. 政府鼓励消费非进口商品以促进国内经济。 | Synonym: domestic goods, local products | Antonym: imports, foreign goods | noun
