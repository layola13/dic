nonprofessorial:/ˌnɒnˌprəfɛˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
非教授的；非教授级别的
Not related to or characteristic of a professor or the position of a professor.
The nonprofessorial staff at the university play a crucial role in its smooth operation. 大学的非教授员工在其顺利运作中发挥着至关重要的作用。
The university is looking to fill several nonprofessorial administrative positions. 这所大学正在招聘几个非教授级别的行政职位。
Synonyms: non-academic, non-faculty
Antonyms: professorial, academic
词性: 形容词
