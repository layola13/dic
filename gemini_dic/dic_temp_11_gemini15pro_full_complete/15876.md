ballistics:/bəˈlɪstɪks/
n.
弹道学，射击学
The science of projectiles and firearms.
The police used ballistics to identify the murder weapon. 警方用弹道学鉴定凶器。
近义词:gunnery
反义词:None
词性:Noun

ballistics:/bəˈlɪstɪks/
n.
（导弹等的）发射学
The study of the flight of projectiles, especially bullets, shells, or bombs.
The missile's ballistics were carefully calculated. 导弹的发射轨迹经过了精密的计算。
近义词:rocketry, missile technology
反义词:None
词性:Noun
