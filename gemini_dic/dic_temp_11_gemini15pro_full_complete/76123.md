furuncle:/ˈfjʊərʌŋkəl/ (US), /ˈfɜːrʌŋkəl/ (UK)| n. |疖子，痈|A painful, pus-filled infection of a hair follicle, also known as a boil.|  I had a painful furuncle on my neck. 我的脖子上长了一个疼的疖子。| The doctor lanced the furuncle to drain the pus. 医生切开疖子排出脓液。|近义词：boil, abscess|反义词:|词性:noun
