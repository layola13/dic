overdeliberation:/ˌoʊvərdiˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən/
|n.|过度审议，过度考虑|The act of deliberating excessively or too long, often resulting in inaction or paralysis by analysis.|After weeks of overdeliberation, the committee still hadn't made a decision.  经过数周的过度审议，委员会仍然没有做出决定。|Overthinking, excessive analysis, rumination|Decisiveness, promptness|Noun
