colloquial:/kəˈloʊkwiəl/| adj.|口语的，会话的|Used in or characteristic of familiar and informal conversation; not formal or literary.|Example: Her writing style is colloquial and easy to understand. 她的写作风格口语化，易于理解。|Example:  "Wanna" is a colloquial form of "want to". "Wanna"是"want to"的口语形式。|近义词：informal, conversational, chatty|反义词: formal, literary|词性:adjective
Etymology:  Late 18th century: from Latin *colloquialis, from colloquium ‘conversation’ (from col- ‘together’ + loqui ‘to speak’).
