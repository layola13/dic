dreamy:/ˈdriːmi/| adj.|梦幻的，空想的，想入非非的|Having a pleasant, unreal quality; suggestive of dreams; given to daydreaming or fantasizing.|She had a dreamy look in her eyes. 她眼神迷离，像是在做梦。|近义词：fanciful, whimsical, romantic, ethereal|反义词：realistic, practical, down-to-earth|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dream + -y.  The suffix -y forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of".  The word "dream" comes from Old English *drēam, of Germanic origin.
