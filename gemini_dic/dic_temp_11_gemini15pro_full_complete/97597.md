inhabiter:/ɪnˈhæbɪtər/ | n. | 居民，居住者 |A person or animal that lives in or occupies a place. |  The city's inhabitants are proud of their history. 城市居民为他们的历史感到自豪。

The island's only inhabitants are a few species of birds.  这个岛上唯一的居民是几种鸟类。

| 近义词：resident, dweller, occupant, tenant | 反义词：migrant, nomad | 词性：noun

inhabit:/ɪnˈhæbɪt/ | v. | 居住于，栖息于 | To live in or occupy (a place). |  Many different species inhabit the rainforest.  许多不同的物种栖息在雨林中。

Polar bears inhabit the Arctic regions. 北极熊栖息在北极地区。

| 近义词：live in, reside in, occupy, dwell in | 反义词：vacate, leave, abandon | 词性：verb
