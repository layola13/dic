leglike:/ˈleɡlaɪk/
adjective
腿状的，像腿的
Resembling a leg in form or function; having the appearance or characteristics of a leg.
The robot had leglike appendages that allowed it to walk.  这个机器人有腿状的附件，可以让它行走。
The insect's antennae were thin and leglike.  这昆虫的触角又细又像腿。
