amphidisc:/ˈæmfɪdɪsk/ | n. | 双盘体，双盘骨针 | A sponge spicule with a star-like disc at each end.  A type of microscleres found in some sponges, characterized by having two similar, flattened, circular or elliptical expansions. |  The amphidiscs of this sponge species are unusually large.  这种海绵物种的双盘骨针异常大。 | synonyms:  microsclere (in a broader sense),  | antonyms:  megasclere | noun
