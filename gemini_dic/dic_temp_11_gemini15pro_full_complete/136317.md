nonrequirements:/ˌnɒn rɪˈkwaɪərmənts/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈkwaɪərmənts/
n.
非必要条件；非强制性要求
Things that are not required or mandatory.
The project manager identified several nonrequirements to streamline the process. 项目经理确定了几个非必要条件，以简化流程。
Removing the nonrequirements significantly reduced the project's cost. 去除非必要条件显著降低了项目的成本。
近义词: nonessentials, optional features
反义词: requirements, necessities
词性: 名词
