fucus:/ˈfjuːkəs/| n.|墨角藻；岩藻|A genus of brown algae, common along rocky seacoasts.  A type of seaweed found on rocks along the coast.|Example 1: The fucus was slippery and covered the rocks near the shore.  海岸附近的岩石上覆盖着滑溜溜的墨角藻。

Example 2:  Researchers studied the properties of fucus for potential medicinal uses. 研究人员研究了墨角藻的特性，以寻找潜在的药用价值。|近义词：seaweed, kelp, wrack, alga|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of seaweed)|Noun
