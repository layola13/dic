nonclient:/ˌnɑːnˈklaɪənt/ | n. | 非客户；非委托人 |  A person or organization that does not use the services of a particular lawyer, accountant, etc. | Example 1:  The software is also available to nonclients for a fee.  这款软件也提供给非客户使用，但需要付费。

Example 2: The firm offers consulting services to both clients and nonclients.  这家公司为客户和非客户都提供咨询服务。 | Synonyms: outsider, nonmember | Antonyms: client, customer | noun
