lunaville:/ˈluːnəvɪl/ | n. |月球村，幻想中的月球居住地| A fictional or imagined settlement on the moon.  This word is likely a neologism, combining "lunar" with "ville" (meaning town or city).  |  They dreamed of building a lunaville with all the amenities of Earth. 他们梦想在月球上建造一个拥有地球上所有便利设施的月球村。 |  lunar colony, moon base |  Earth-based settlement | noun
