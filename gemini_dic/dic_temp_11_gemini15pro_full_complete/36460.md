cold-turkey:/ˌkoʊld ˈtɜːrki/| adv. adj. n.|突然地；冷火鸡（戒毒方法）；冷火鸡肉|Abruptly and completely, especially in reference to stopping an addictive substance. It can also refer to the unpleasant symptoms experienced during withdrawal or to food, specifically cold cooked turkey.|He quit smoking cold turkey. 他突然戒烟了。|近义词：abruptly, suddenly, immediately|反义词：gradually|词性: adverb, adjective, noun

cold-turkey:/ˌkoʊld ˈtɜːrki/| n.|冷火鸡肉|Cooked turkey that is served cold.|We had cold turkey sandwiches for lunch.  我们午餐吃了冷火鸡三明治。|近义词：leftover turkey|反义词： (no direct antonym, could be "hot turkey")|词性: noun
