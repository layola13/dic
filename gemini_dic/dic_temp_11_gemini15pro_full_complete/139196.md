objectionable:/əbˈdʒekʃənəbl/| adj.|令人反感的，讨厌的|Something that is objectionable is unpleasant or offensive.|Her behavior was highly objectionable. 她的行为令人非常反感。|近义词：offensive, unpleasant, disagreeable, repugnant, reprehensible|反义词：acceptable, pleasant, agreeable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin objectus "thrown against, opposed," past participle of obicere "to throw against, oppose" (see object (n.)). The meaning "liable to objection" is from 1794.
