laureateships: /ˈlɔːriɪtˌʃɪps/ | n. | 桂冠；获奖者身份；获奖者职位 | The position or honor of being a laureate. |  The university boasts three Nobel laureateships in physics. 这所大学以拥有三位诺贝尔物理学奖获得者而自豪。| 近义词: awards, honors, accolades | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑disgrace, dishonor) | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From "laureate" + "-ship".  "Laureate" comes from Latin "laureatus," past participle of "laureare" meaning "to crown with laurel."  The laurel wreath was a symbol of victory and honor in ancient Greece and Rome.  "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
