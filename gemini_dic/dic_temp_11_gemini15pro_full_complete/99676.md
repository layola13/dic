interlingualism:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm/ | n. | 语际性，语际现象 | The phenomenon of transfer of linguistic features between different languages in the mind of a bilingual or multilingual individual.  This can include phonological, lexical, grammatical, and semantic influences. |  Example 1: Interlingualism can lead to code-switching in bilingual speakers. 语际现象会导致双语者进行语码转换。

Example 2:  Researchers are studying the effects of interlingualism on language acquisition. 研究人员正在研究语际现象对语言习得的影响。 | Near Synonyms: language contact, language transfer, cross-linguistic influence | Antonyms: monolingualism | noun
