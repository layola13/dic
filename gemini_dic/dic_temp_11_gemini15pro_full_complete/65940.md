exacter:/ɪɡˈzæktər/ | n. |  精确者; 要求严格的人 | A person who demands precision or correctness. | The exacter insisted on every detail being perfect.  精确者坚持每个细节都要完美。| 近义词: perfectionist, purist, stickler | 反义词: slob, approximator | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "exact" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons who perform the action of the verb).  "Exact" comes from the Latin "exactus," past participle of "exigere" (to demand, require, drive out).
