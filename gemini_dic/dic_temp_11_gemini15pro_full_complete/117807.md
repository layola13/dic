meliorism:/ˈmiːliərɪzəm/
n.
改良主义;乐观主义
The belief that the world can be made better by human effort.
Example:  Her meliorism inspired her to start a charity. 她对改良主义的信念激励她创办了一个慈善机构。

Example:  Despite the challenges, his meliorism never wavered. 尽管面临挑战，他的乐观主义从未动摇。

近义词:optimism, progressivism
反义词:pessimism, cynicism
词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *meliorismus, from Latin melior (“better”).
