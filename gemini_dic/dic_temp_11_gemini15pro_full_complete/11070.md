archaeologist's: /ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |考古学家的（所有格）|  The possessive form of "archaeologist," meaning something belonging to or associated with an archaeologist. |  The archaeologist's trowel was carefully placed in the dig bag. 考古学家的泥铲被小心地放在挖掘袋里。|  The archaeologist's discovery changed our understanding of the ancient civilization. 考古学家的发现改变了我们对古代文明的理解。| 近义词: (None - relates to possession) | 反义词: (None - relates to possession) | 词性: Possessive noun


Etymology:

From French *archéologue*, from Greek *arkhaiología* "antiquity study" + *-ist*. *arkhaiología* is from *arkhaîos* "ancient" + *-logía* "study".
