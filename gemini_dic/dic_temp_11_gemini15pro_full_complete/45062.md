cubesat:/ˈkjuːbsæt/| n.|立方星|A miniaturized satellite for space research that is made up of multiples of 10×10×10 cm cubic units.|Several universities are collaborating on a CubeSat project to study the Earth's atmosphere. 几所大学正在合作开展一个立方星项目，以研究地球大气层。|Synonyms: nanosatellite, microsatellite, small satellite|Antonyms: conventional satellite, large satellite|noun
