fusillading: /ˌfjuːsəˈleɪdɪŋ/ (US), /ˌfjuːsɪˈleɪdɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.| 射击，连珠炮似地攻击；大量提问 |A fusillade is a rapid and continuous discharge of firearms or outburst of criticism or questions.| The soldiers were subjected to a heavy fusillade from enemy troops. 士兵们遭到了敌军的猛烈射击。| synonyms: barrage, volley, bombardment, onslaught, deluge | antonyms: trickle, dribble | noun
fusillading: /ˌfjuːsəˈleɪdɪŋ/ (US), /ˌfjuːsɪˈleɪdɪŋ/ (UK)| v. | 射击，连珠炮似地攻击；大量提问 |The act of fusillading, firing a fusillade, or attacking with a barrage of something (like questions or criticism).|  The reporters were fusillading the president with questions. 记者们向总统连珠炮似地提问。| synonyms: bombarding, peppering, pelting | antonyms: withholding, suppressing | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fusillade*, from *fusiller* "to shoot," from *fusil* "musket," from Italian *focile* "steel for striking fire, flintlock," from Late Latin *focilis* "of fire, fiery," from Latin *focus* "hearth, fireplace."
