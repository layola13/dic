hailed:/heɪld/ | v. | 欢呼；称赞；招呼；下冰雹 | To greet or acclaim enthusiastically; to precipitate hail. | The crowd hailed the returning heroes.  人群欢呼迎接凯旋的英雄们。 | acclaim, praise, greet | criticize, condemn | verb

hailed:/heɪld/ | v. | 来自；出生于 | To come from or have originated in. | She hails from a small town in Iowa. 她来自爱荷华州的一个小镇。 | originate from, come from |  | verb

注意事项: 注意hailed作为动词的两种不同含义：欢呼/称赞 和 来自/出生于。上下文语境很重要。
