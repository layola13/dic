arachnophobic:/əˌræknəˈfəʊbɪk/| adj.|蜘蛛恐惧症的，患蜘蛛恐惧症的|Having an extreme or irrational fear of spiders.|例句：She is arachnophobic and can't even look at a picture of a spider. 她有蜘蛛恐惧症，甚至不能看蜘蛛的照片。

例句：He's so arachnophobic that he screams if he sees a cobweb. 他有严重的蜘蛛恐惧症，看到蜘蛛网都会尖叫。|近义词：spider-fearing|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "arachnophilic" - spider-loving, but not commonly used) |词性:adjective
