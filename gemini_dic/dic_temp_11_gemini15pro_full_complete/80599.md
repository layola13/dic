googs:/ɡuːɡz/ | n. |护目镜; 泳镜| Goggles, often used for swimming or other activities requiring eye protection. | He forgot his googs at home and had to squint in the pool. 他把泳镜忘在家里了，不得不在泳池里眯着眼睛。| She wore googs while skiing to protect her eyes from the sun and wind. 她滑雪时戴着护目镜，以保护眼睛免受阳光和风的伤害。| 近义词:goggles, eyewear | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


词源:  Shortened from "goggles." Likely influenced by the brand name "Google," due to a similar sound and the use of goggles in virtual reality applications.
