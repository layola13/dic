metrology:/mɪˈtrɒlədʒi/ /məˈtrɑːlədʒi/
n.
计量学
The science of measurement, embracing both experimental and theoretical determinations at any level of uncertainty in any field of science and technology.
The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) plays a key role in metrology in the United States.  美国国家标准与技术研究院 (NIST) 在美国的计量学中扮演着关键角色。
Metrology is essential for ensuring the quality of manufactured goods. 计量学对于确保制成品的质量至关重要。

近义词:measurement science
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps inaccuracy or imprecision in context)
词性:noun
