overinstructiveness:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪvnəs/
|n.| 过分指导；过分教导 |The overinstructiveness of the manual made it confusing rather than helpful.  该手册的过分指导使其令人困惑而不是有所帮助。|The teacher's overinstructiveness stifled the students' creativity.  老师的过分指导扼杀了学生的创造力。|近义词: overbearingness, officiousness |反义词: permissiveness, leniency |词性: noun

overinstruct:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkt/
|v.| 过分指导；过分教导|Don't overinstruct your children, let them learn from their mistakes. 不要过分指导你的孩子，让他们从错误中学习。|The coach overinstructed the team, leading to confusion and poor performance. 教练对球队进行了过分指导，导致了混乱和糟糕的表现。|近义词: overdirect, micromanage|反义词: underinstruct, neglect|词性: verb

instructive:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪv/
|adj.| 有教育意义的；有启发性的|The documentary was highly instructive about the history of the region. 这部纪录片对该地区的历史很有启发性。|The museum offers instructive exhibits on local wildlife.  博物馆提供有关当地野生动物的教育性展览。|近义词: educational, informative, enlightening|反义词: uninformative, useless|词性: adjective

词源:
From over- + instructive + -ness.  "Over-" 表示 "过度"，"instructive" 表示 "有教育意义的"，"-ness" 是名词后缀，表示状态或性质。
