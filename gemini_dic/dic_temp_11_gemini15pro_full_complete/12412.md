asininely:/ˌæsɪˈnaɪnli/ | adv. |愚蠢地，笨拙地 | In a manner resembling a donkey; stupidly, foolishly. | He reacted asininely to the news, throwing a tantrum like a child. 他对这个消息的反应愚蠢至极，像个孩子一样发脾气。 |  She asininely left her keys in the car. 她愚蠢地把钥匙忘在了车里。 | 近义词：stupidly, foolishly, idiotically, absurdly | 反义词：wisely, intelligently, sensibly | 词性：副词

词源: From asinine + -ly.  Asinine itself comes from the Latin *asinīnus*, from *asinus* "ass, donkey."
