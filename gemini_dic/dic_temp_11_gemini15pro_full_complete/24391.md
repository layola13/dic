brinkless:/ˈbrɪŋklɪs/
adj.
无边缘的，无限的
Having no brink or edge; boundless.
The poet described a brinkless expanse of ocean. 诗人描绘了一片无边无际的海洋。
近义词：boundless, limitless, infinite
反义词：finite, limited, bounded
词性：形容词

词源:
From brink + -less.  "Brink" comes from Middle English *brinke, from Old Norse *brekka "slope, edge."  "-less" is a common Old English suffix meaning "without."
