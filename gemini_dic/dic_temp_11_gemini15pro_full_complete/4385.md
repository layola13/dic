alalia:/əˈleɪliə/ | n. | 失语症，言语缺失| Loss or impairment of the power of speech, due to injury or disease of the brain. |  Example: The stroke resulted in alalia, making it difficult for him to communicate. 中风导致他失语，难以沟通。

Example:  Developmental alalia can affect a child's ability to learn language.  发育性失语症会影响儿童学习语言的能力。

| 近义词: aphasia, dysphasia | 反义词: eloquence, fluency | 词性: noun
