communicate:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ (US) /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ (UK)
verb
交流；沟通；传达；感染
To share or exchange information, news, or ideas.  To transmit (a disease).
We communicate mostly by email. 我们主要通过电子邮件沟通。
Measles is easily communicated. 麻疹很容易传染。
近义词: convey, transmit, share, impart
反义词: withhold, suppress
词性: v.


communicate:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ (US) /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ (UK)
noun
沟通；交流
The imparting or exchanging of information or news.
Effective communicate is essential in a good relationship. 良好的沟通在一段良好的关系中至关重要。
Regular communicate with clients is crucial.  与客户的定期沟通至关重要。
近义词: interaction, exchange, correspondence
反义词: isolation, silence
词性: n. (rare)
