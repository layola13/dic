minger:/ˈmɪŋə(r)/ (UK) | n. |令人讨厌的人；卑鄙的人；怪人|A very unpleasant or contemptible person.  Often used to describe someone considered unattractive,  odd, or socially inept.  | He called her a minger, which was completely uncalled for. 他骂她丑八怪，这完全是没必要的。| 近义词：creep, weirdo, loser, jerk | 反义词：pleasant person, attractive person | 词性: 名词

词源: British slang, possibly derived from "ming" meaning manure or unpleasant smell.
