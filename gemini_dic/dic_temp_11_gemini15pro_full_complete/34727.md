clickjacking:/ˈklɪkˌdʒækɪŋ/| n. |点击劫持|A malicious technique where an invisible or disguised link is placed over something a user intends to click. When the user clicks, they are unknowingly redirected to a different page or perform an unintended action.|Example: The attacker used clickjacking to trick users into liking a malicious Facebook page. 攻击者使用点击劫持诱骗用户点赞恶意Facebook页面。

Example:  Clickjacking can be used to steal sensitive information or spread malware. 点击劫持可以用来窃取敏感信息或传播恶意软件。|近义词:UI redressing, clickbaiting|反义词:|n.
