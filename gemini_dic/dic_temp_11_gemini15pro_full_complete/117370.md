mediating:/ˈmiːdieɪtɪŋ/| adj. v.|调解的，斡旋的; 调停，斡旋|acting as a mediator; intervening for the purpose of settling differences|The lawyer is mediating between the two parties in the dispute. 律师正在调解纠纷双方。|Synonyms: arbitrating, interceding, negotiating|Antonyms: aggravating, escalating, inciting|Verb/Adjective

mediating:/ˈmiːdieɪtɪŋ/| adj.|调解的，斡旋的|having the effect of intervening for the purpose of settling differences|The mediating role of the UN was crucial in the ceasefire. 联合国的调解作用在停火中至关重要。|Synonyms: intermediary, conciliatory, peacemaking|Antonyms: antagonistic, divisive, inflammatory|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mediatus, past participle of mediare "to be in the middle," from Latin medius "middle."
