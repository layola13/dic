lipochrin: /ˈlɪpəkrɪn/ /ˈlaɪpəkrɪn/
noun
脂褐素;老年色素
A pigment that accumulates in aging tissues, giving them a yellowish-brown color. It is composed of oxidized lipids and proteins.
Example: Lipochrin deposits are a common sign of aging in the skin.  脂褐素沉积是皮肤老化的常见迹象。

Example: The scientist studied the accumulation of lipochrin in the brain cells of mice.  科学家研究了小鼠脑细胞中脂褐素的积累。
Synonym: age pigment, lipofuscin
Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific substance.  One could consider "youthful pigment" conceptually, but there's no established term)
词性: noun
