outlabors:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbərz/
verb
胜过工作；比...更努力工作
To work harder or longer than someone else.
He outlabored his competitors and won the contract. 他比他的竞争对手工作更努力，赢得了合同。
The team outlabored their opponents and secured a victory.  团队比对手更加努力，确保了胜利。
surpass, outwork, outperform
slack off, underperform
