class:/klæs/ | n. | 班级；课；种类；等级 | A group of students who are taught together.  A period of teaching in a subject. A set of things that are similar. A level or rank in society. |  The English class is at 9 am. 英语课在上午9点。| synonyms: category, group, grade, rank | antonyms: individual, unique, singular | noun

class:/klæs/ | v. | 分类；归类 | To put people or things into groups according to type. | They classed him as a genius. 他们把他归类为天才。| synonyms: categorize, group, classify | antonyms: separate, divide, distinguish | verb
