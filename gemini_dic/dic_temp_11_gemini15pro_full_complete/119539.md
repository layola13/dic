microseism: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌsaɪzəm/ (US), /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsaɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |微震，微地震|A faint earth tremor caused by natural phenomena, such as waves hitting the shore, or by human activities, such as heavy traffic.|例句：The seismograph detected a series of microseisms. 地震仪检测到了一系列微震。

|例句：Microseisms can interfere with the detection of earthquakes. 微震会干扰地震的检测。|近义词：tremor, earth tremor|反义词: N/A |noun
