aristotype:/ˈærɪstəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 原始型，原型 |The original form of a species, from which varieties have arisen. | The botanist considered the specimen an aristotype, believing it represented the purest form of the species.  这个植物学家认为该标本是一个原始型，相信它代表了该物种最纯粹的形式。 | 近义词: prototype, archetype | 反义词:  variant, derivative | noun

aristotype:/ˈærɪstəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 优良品种，精英品种 | A superior or elite type of something, often a plant or animal. |  The breeder focused on developing an aristotype of rose, renowned for its fragrance and vibrant color.  育种家专注于培育一种优良的玫瑰品种，以其芬芳和鲜艳的色彩而闻名。 | 近义词:  elite, superior, prime | 反义词:  common, ordinary, inferior | noun
