myelins:/ˈmaɪəlɪnz/ | n. |髓磷脂（复数）|A mixture of proteins and phospholipids forming a whitish insulating sheath around many nerve fibers, increasing the speed at which impulses are conducted.|Multiple sclerosis is a disease that damages the myelins in the brain and spinal cord. 多发性硬化症是一种损害大脑和脊髓中髓鞘的疾病。| 近义词: myelin sheaths | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From myelin + -s (plural suffix). Myelin comes from Greek *muelos* meaning "marrow."
