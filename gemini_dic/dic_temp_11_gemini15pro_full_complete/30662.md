cerographist:/sɪˈrɒɡrəfɪst/ /səˈrɑːɡrəfɪst/
noun
蜡版画家，蜡画制作者
A person skilled in cerography (the art of writing or drawing on wax).
The cerographist carefully etched the design onto the wax tablet. 蜡版画家小心翼翼地在蜡板上蚀刻图案。
近义词: engraver, etcher, artist
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "destroyer of art" in a very broad sense)
词性: noun
