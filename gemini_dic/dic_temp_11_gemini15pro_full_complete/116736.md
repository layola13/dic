maul:/mɔːl/ | n. | 大锤 | A heavy hammer. | The blacksmith swung the maul to shape the hot iron. 铁匠挥舞着大锤来塑造烧红的铁块。 |  hammer, sledgehammer | - | noun
maul:/mɔːl/ | v. | 粗暴地对待；撕裂；伤害 | To handle roughly or clumsily; to injure by tearing and lacerating. | The bear mauled the hiker. 熊袭击了徒步旅行者。| injure, wound, mangle | heal, protect | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mallen*, from Old French *mail* "hammer," from Latin *malleus* "hammer."
