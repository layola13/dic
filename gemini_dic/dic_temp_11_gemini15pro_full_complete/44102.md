crispate:/ˈkrɪspeɪt/| v.|使卷曲，使起皱纹|To cause to become curled or wrinkled.|The heat crispated the leaves.  高温使叶子卷曲了。|近义词：curl, wrinkle, crimp|反义词:smooth, flatten|词性:动词
