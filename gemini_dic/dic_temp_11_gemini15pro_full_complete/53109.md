diphycercal:/daɪˈfɪsərkl/ | adj. | 双尾的，原尾的 | Having a tail that tapers to a point, with the vertebral column extending to the tip, as in the lungfish.  A tail fin in which the vertebral column extends to the tip, dividing the fin into symmetrical dorsal and ventral lobes. |  The lungfish has a diphycercal tail. 肺鱼有双尾。| Synonyms: protocercal | Antonyms: heterocercal, homocercal | adjective

diphycercal:/daɪˈfɪsərkl/ | n. | 双尾 | A type of tail fin found in certain fish, characterized by a symmetrical dorsal and ventral lobe with the vertebral column extending to the tip. |  The fossil revealed a diphycercal. 化石显示出一个双尾。| Synonyms: protocercal tail | Antonyms: heterocercal tail, homocercal tail | noun
