lygodium:/laɪˈɡoʊdiəm/| n. | 海金沙属植物；海金沙 |A genus of climbing ferns in the family Lygodiaceae, characterized by their twining rachis and small, often fan-shaped leaflets.|The lygodium climbed up the oak tree. 海金沙爬上了橡树。|近义词：climbing fern, Japanese climbing fern |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

词源: From Ancient Greek  λυγώδης (lugṓdēs, “flexible, pliant”)
