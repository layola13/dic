disavouch:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊtʃ/
|vt.|否认；拒绝对…承担责任；|To deny responsibility for or connection with.|He disavouched any knowledge of the plot. 他否认对该阴谋有任何了解。|近义词：deny, repudiate, disclaim|反义词：acknowledge, admit, avow|词性:动词
Etymology: From dis- (expressing negation) + avouch (to affirm). Ultimately from Latin advocare "to call to one's aid."
