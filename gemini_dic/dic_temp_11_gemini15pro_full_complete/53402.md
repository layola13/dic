disallows:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊz/
verb
不允许，禁止
To refuse to allow or permit; to prohibit.
The school disallows students from bringing cell phones to class. 学校不允许学生带手机上课。
forbids, prohibits, prevents
allows, permits, authorizes
