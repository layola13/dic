glaceing:/ˈɡleɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 上釉，糖衣，(糕点上的) 糖霜 | The act of covering something with a thin, smooth, often shiny layer, especially of sugar or icing. | The glaceing on the cake was beautifully smooth. 蛋糕上的糖霜非常光滑。 | 近义词: icing, frosting, glazing | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 uncovered, bare) | 词性: 名词

glaceing:/ˈɡleɪsɪŋ/ | adj. |  冰冷的，冷淡的 | Having a smooth, glassy surface, or appearing cold and unfriendly. | She gave him a glaceing stare. 她冷冷地瞪了他一眼。| 近义词: icy, frosty, frigid, glacial | 反义词: warm, friendly, cordial | 词性: 形容词
