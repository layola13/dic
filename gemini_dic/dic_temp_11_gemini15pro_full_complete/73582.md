foretack: /ˈfɔːrtæk/ | n. |前支索，迎风支索| A rope used to secure the lower forward corner of a foresail to the stem or bowsprit of a sailing vessel, especially when sailing close-hauled. |The foretack was tightened to bring the foresail closer to the wind. 前支索被拉紧以便使前帆更靠近风向。| The ship's foretack snapped in the strong winds.  这艘船的前支索在强风中断了。| 近义词: downhaul | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially the after leech of the sail) | 词性: noun
