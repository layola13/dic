nonfortuitous:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈtuːɪtəs/
adjective
非偶然的；非意外的；故意的
Not occurring by chance; deliberate or premeditated.
His seemingly nonfortuitous encounter with the CEO led to a job offer. 他与CEO看似并非偶然的相遇为他带来了一份工作。
The placement of the cameras was nonfortuitous, suggesting a planned surveillance operation. 摄像机的摆放并非偶然，暗示着这是一次有计划的监视行动。
近义词: deliberate, intentional, planned, premeditated
反义词: accidental, chance, fortuitous, random
词性: adjective
词源: From non- + fortuitous, from Latin fortuitus ‘happening by chance’.
