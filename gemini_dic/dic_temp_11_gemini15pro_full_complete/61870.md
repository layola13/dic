empowered:/ɪmˈpaʊərd/ /ɛmˈpaʊərd/| adj. |被授权的，被赋能的|Having been given power or authority to do something; made stronger and more confident, especially in controlling one's life and claiming one's rights.|例句：Empowered employees are more likely to take initiative.  被赋能的员工更有可能主动采取行动。

例句：The training program empowered the women to start their own businesses.  这个培训项目使这些妇女能够自己创业。|近义词：authorized, enabled, strengthened, confident|反义词：powerless, weak, helpless|词性:形容词
