outbrazen: /ˌaʊtˈbreɪzən/
verb
厚颜无耻地胜过; 比…更厚颜无耻
surpass in brazenness; behave more brazenly than.
He outbrazened his rivals. 他比他的对手更厚颜无耻。
Synonyms: outface, surpass,  exceed, outdo
Antonyms:  be shy, be timid, be humble
词性: 动词(verb)
