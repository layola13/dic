bang:/bæŋ/| n. v. int. adj.|猛击，砰一声，刘海|A sudden loud noise, as of an explosion. To hit or shut forcefully and noisily. A fringe of hair cut straight across the forehead.|例句：The door banged shut. 门砰地关上了。 He banged his fist on the table. 他用拳头猛击桌子。 She wears her hair in a bang. 她留着刘海。|近义词：slam, crash, thump, fringe (for hair)|反义词:whisper, tap (for sound), none (for hair)|词性:名词，动词，感叹词，形容词
