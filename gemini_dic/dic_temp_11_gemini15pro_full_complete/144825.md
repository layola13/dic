over-inflates:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪts/| v.|过度充气；过分夸大|To inflate something beyond a safe or recommended limit; to exaggerate greatly.|The salesman over-inflated the benefits of the product.  推销员过分夸大了产品的好处。|He over-inflated his importance to the company. 他过分夸大了他对公司的重要性。|近义词：overstate, exaggerate, pump up|反义词: understate, downplay|词性:动词
