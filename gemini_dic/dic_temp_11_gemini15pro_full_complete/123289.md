monogamously:/məˈnɑːɡəməsli/ | adv. | 一夫一妻地，单配偶地 | In a manner characterized by having only one spouse or sexual partner at a time. |  They lived monogamously for fifty years.  他们一夫一妻地生活了五十年。| synonym: faithfully, exclusively | antonym: polygamously, promiscuously | adverb

monogamy:/məˈnɑːɡəmi/ | n. | 一夫一妻制，单配偶制 | The practice or state of being married to one person at a time. | Monogamy is the most common form of marriage in Western cultures.  一夫一妻制是西方文化中最常见的婚姻形式。| synonym: single marriage | antonym: polygamy, polyandry | noun
