injust:/ɪnˈdʒʌst/
adjective
不公正的，不公平的
not fair; not based on what is morally right and fair
例句：The unjust law sparked widespread protests. 这项不公平的法律引发了广泛的抗议。

例句：It's unjust to blame her for everything. 把一切都怪在她身上是不公平的。
近义词：unfair, inequitable, biased, prejudiced
反义词：just, fair, equitable, impartial
词性: adjective
