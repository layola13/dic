nonfluorescent:/ˌnɒnˌflʊəˈrɛsənt/ | /ˌnɑːnˌflʊəˈrɛsənt/ | adj. | 非荧光的 | Not fluorescent; not emitting light when exposed to radiation. | The control group received nonfluorescent antibodies. 对照组接受了非荧光抗体。| The paint used was nonfluorescent to avoid interference with the laser measurements. 使用的油漆是非荧光的，以避免干扰激光测量。 | 近义词: nonluminescent, dull, dark | 反义词: fluorescent, luminescent, bright | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + fluorescent (from Latin fluere "to flow" + -escent (suffix forming adjectives meaning "beginning to be" or "somewhat"))
