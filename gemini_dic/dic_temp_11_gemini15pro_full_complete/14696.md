awakeningly:/əˈweɪkənɪŋli/
adv.
唤醒地，觉醒地
in a way that causes awakening or awareness.
She smiled awakeningly, as if suddenly realizing the truth. 她带着顿悟般的微笑，仿佛突然明白了真相。
近义词：revealingly, enlighteningly
反义词：drowsily, sleepily
词性: 副词

词源: From awaken + -ing + -ly.  "Awaken" comes from Old English *awæcnian, from *wacan "to wake."
