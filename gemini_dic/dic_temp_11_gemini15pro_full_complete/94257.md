impermeabilities:/ɪmˌpɜːrmiəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|不渗透性；不透水性； непроницаемость (Russian)|The quality of being impermeable; not allowing fluids or other substances to pass through.  This refers to the plural form, indicating multiple instances of impermeability.|The impermeabilities of certain rock formations make them ideal for containing groundwater.  某些岩层的防渗性使其成为储存地下水的理想选择。|The new material boasts high impermeabilities to both water and air.  这种新材料对水和空气都具有很高的不渗透性。|impenetrabilities, resistances, imperviousness|permeabilities, porosities
