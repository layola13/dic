editorial:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. n. | 编辑的，社论的；社论 |  Relating to the editor or editing of a publication; an article in a newspaper or other periodical presenting the opinion of the publisher or editors. |  The editorial board decided to endorse the candidate. 编辑委员会决定支持这位候选人。 | synonyms: leading article, commentary, opinion piece | antonyms: news report, objective article | adjective

editorial:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəl/ | n. | 社论 | An article in a newspaper or other periodical presenting the opinion of the publisher or editors.  | The newspaper's editorial criticized the government's handling of the crisis. 这家报纸的社论批评了政府处理危机的方式。 | synonyms: leading article, commentary, opinion piece | antonyms: news report, objective article | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *editorialis, from Latin editor (“publisher, editor”), from ēdō (“I publish, put forth, give out, exhibit”).
