moran:/ˈmɔːrɑːn/ (US), /ˈmɔːrən/ (UK)| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid or incompetent person.|例句：He's such a moran, he locked his keys in the car. 他真是个笨蛋，把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be a moran, put your coat on, it's cold outside. 别傻了，穿上外套，外面很冷。

近义词：idiot, fool, imbecile, dolt, nitwit|反义词:genius, intellectual|词性:noun
