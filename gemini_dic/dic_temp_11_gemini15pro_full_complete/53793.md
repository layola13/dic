discomposure:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊʒər/| n.|不安；慌张；失去镇静|A state of being flustered, agitated, or unsettled; lack of composure.|例句：His discomposure was evident in his trembling hands. 他的不安从他颤抖的双手显而易见。|例句：The sudden news caused her considerable discomposure.  突如其来的消息让她非常不安。|近义词：anxiety, agitation, perturbation, unease|反义词：composure, calmness, tranquility|词性：名词

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + composure (from com- "together" + poser "to place, put")
