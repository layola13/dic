elaidic:/ˌɛlɪˈɪdɪk/
adjective
反油酸的；反式十八烯酸的
Relating to or denoting elaidic acid.
Elaidic acid is a trans fatty acid found in partially hydrogenated vegetable oils. 反油酸是一种存在于部分氢化植物油中的反式脂肪酸。

Elaidic acid is the trans isomer of oleic acid. 反油酸是油酸的反式异构体。

近义词: trans, unsaturated
反义词: cis, saturated
词性: adjective
