clandestine:/klænˈdɛstɪn/ | adj. | 秘密的，私下的，暗中的 | Kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit. | They held a clandestine meeting in the library. 他们在图书馆举行了一次秘密会议。| The spies exchanged clandestine messages. 间谍们交换了秘密信息。 |  近义词: secret, covert, furtive, surreptitious | 反义词: open, public, overt | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *clandestin*, from Latin *clandestinus* "secret, hidden," perhaps from *clam* "secretly" (related to *celare* "to hide").
