nibble:/ˈnɪbl/| n. v. |一小口，轻咬；细咬|A small bite or nibble is a very small amount of food.  A nibble is also the act of eating something by taking very small bites.|She took a nibble of the cheese. 她尝了一小口奶酪。|近义词：bite, morsel, snack|反义词:gulp, mouthful|noun, verb
