cassette:/kəˈsɛt/ /kæˈsɛt/| n.|盒式磁带；卡式磁带；暗盒|A sealed plastic unit containing a length of magnetic tape, used for recording sound or pictures.|I found a box of old cassettes in the attic. 我在阁楼里发现了一盒旧磁带。|近义词:tape, cartridge|反义词:|noun

cassette:/kəˈsɛt/ /kæˈsɛt/| adj.|盒式的；卡式的|Relating to or using cassettes.|A cassette player. 一个盒式磁带播放机。|近义词:tape, cartridge|反义词:|adjective
