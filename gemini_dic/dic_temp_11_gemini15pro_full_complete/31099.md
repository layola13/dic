championless:/ˈtʃæmpiənˌləs/
adjective
无冠军的；无人拥护的
lacking a champion; having no champion
The championless team struggled to find motivation. 这支没有冠军的队伍很难找到动力。
The championless cause was eventually forgotten. 这项无人拥护的事业最终被遗忘了。
Synonyms: undefended, unsupported, friendless
Antonyms: championed, supported, defended
词性: 形容词
