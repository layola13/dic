hegemon:/ˈhedʒɪmən/ | n. |霸主，霸权国，领导者|A leading or dominant state, especially among a group of states; a leader, especially one who dominates others. |The United States has been considered a global hegemon since the end of the Cold War.  美国自冷战结束以来一直被认为是全球霸主。

|The company became the market hegemon after acquiring its main competitor.  该公司在收购其主要竞争对手后成为了市场霸主。|Synonyms: leader, ruler, dominant power, superpower |Antonyms: subordinate, follower, underling |noun
