adjudicate:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/
verb
裁决，判决，裁定
To formally judge and settle a dispute or contest; to act as judge.
The panel will adjudicate on the merits of each submission. 评审小组将根据每份提交材料的优劣进行评判。
The case was adjudicated in the High Court. 该案件由高等法院审理。
judge, decide, determine, rule, arbitrate
waver, hesitate, defer, postpone
