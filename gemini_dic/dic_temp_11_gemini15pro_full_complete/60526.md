elaeolite:/ɪˈliːəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 脂光石，霞石 | A glassy variety of nepheline, a feldspathoid mineral.  It is often massive and can exhibit a greasy luster. | Elaeolite is a common constituent of nepheline syenites. 脂光石是霞石正长岩的常见成分。| Elaeolite can sometimes be confused with quartz. 脂光石有时会被误认为是石英。 | Synonyms: nepheline | Antonyms: None | noun
