clusters:/ˈklʌstərz/ (US), /ˈklʌstəz/ (UK)| n.|簇，群，串，丛|A group of similar things or people positioned or occurring closely together.|例句：There was a cluster of grapes on the vine. 藤蔓上有一串葡萄。|例句：A cluster of fans gathered around the stage door. 一群粉丝聚集在舞台门口。|近义词：group, bunch, collection, agglomeration|反义词：scattering, dispersion|词性:noun
