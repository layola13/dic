causing:/ˈkɔːzɪŋ/| adj. |导致的，引起的|Present participle of "cause," meaning to make something happen.  It describes something that is the reason for something else happening.|The heavy rain causing floods in the city.  这场大雨造成了城市洪水。|Synonyms: creating, producing, generating, leading to, resulting in, bringing about|Antonyms: preventing, hindering, stopping|Verb (present participle)
