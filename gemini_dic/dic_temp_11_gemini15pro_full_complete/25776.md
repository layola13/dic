burdensomeness:/ˈbɜːrdənsəmnəs/
n.
沉重，繁重，累赘
The state or quality of being burdensome; onerousness.
The burdensomeness of his responsibilities made him consider resigning.  他责任的沉重让他考虑辞职。

The burdensomeness of the new regulations angered small business owners. 新规定的繁重激怒了小企业主。

近义词: onerousness, heaviness, weightiness
反义词: lightness, ease, simplicity
词性: noun


词源:  From burdensome + -ness.  Burdensome comes from burden (from Old French *burdon, of Germanic origin) + -some (Old English -sum).
