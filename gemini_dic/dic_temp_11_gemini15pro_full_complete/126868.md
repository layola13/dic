nappier:/ˈnæpiər/| adj.|更毛茸茸的，绒毛更密的|Covered with a dense, short, soft pile or fuzz.  More nappy than something else.|The peach was nappier than the nectarine.  这个桃子比油桃毛茸茸的。|近义词：fuzzier, hairier, downier|反义词: smoother, slicker|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From nap (n.) + -ier (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Old English *hnoppian "to pluck, nip off."
