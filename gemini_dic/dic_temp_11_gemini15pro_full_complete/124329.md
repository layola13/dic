mothers:/ˈmʌðərz/ | n. | 母亲们 | Plural form of "mother," referring to more than one female parent. | The mothers waited anxiously outside the school. 母亲们焦急地在学校外等待。| Mothers gathered at the park for a picnic. 母亲们聚集在公园里野餐。 | 近义词: moms | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a familial relationship) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "mōdor," from Proto-Germanic "*mōdēr," from Proto-Indo-European "*méh₂tēr."  Cognates include Dutch "moeder," German "Mutter," Latin "māter," Greek "mḗtēr," Sanskrit "mātṛ," and Persian "mādar."
