clinanthium:/klaɪˈnænθiəm/
n.
花托，花序托
The expanded apex of a peduncle bearing sessile flowers, as in the sunflower.
The sunflower's clinanthium is broad and flat, providing a platform for its numerous florets.  向日葵的花托宽而扁平，为其众多的花朵提供了一个平台。
clinanthium:/klaɪˈnænθiəm/
noun
花托，花序托
The part of the inflorescence that bears the flowers, especially in Asteraceae.
The clinanthium of the daisy is conical.  雏菊的花托是锥形的。
receptacle, torus
