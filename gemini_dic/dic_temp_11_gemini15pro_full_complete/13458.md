atrial:/ˈeɪtriəl/| adj.|心房的|Relating to the atria (the upper chambers) of the heart.|The atrial walls are thinner than the ventricular walls. 心房壁比心室壁薄。|Atrial fibrillation is a common heart rhythm disorder. 心房颤动是一种常见的心律失常。|近义词:cardiac|反义词:ventricular|词性:形容词
atria:/ˈeɪtriə/| n.|心房|The upper chambers of the heart that receive blood from the veins.|Blood enters the heart through the atria. 血液通过心房进入心脏。|The atria contract to push blood into the ventricles. 心房收缩将血液推入心室。|近义词:auricle|反义词:ventricle|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *ātrium*, meaning "entrance hall, main room,"  later specialized to the heart's atria.
