aerification:/ˌeərɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
通气；透气
The process of creating holes in the soil to improve air and water penetration.
The golf course undergoes annual aerification to improve drainage.  高尔夫球场每年进行一次通气以改善排水。
ventilation, aeration
compaction, suffocation
