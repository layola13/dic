maieutical:/maɪˈuːtɪkəl/
adjective
产婆术的；助产的；苏格拉底式的
Relating to the method of eliciting new ideas from another person by skillful questioning, resembling the way a midwife helps deliver a baby; Socratic.
Socrates' maieutical method of teaching is still used in classrooms today. 苏格拉底的助产式教学法至今仍在课堂上使用。
近义词:Socratic, heuristic
反义词:didactic, lecture-based
词性:adjective
