bone of contention: 争论的焦点, The price of the house was the main bone of contention between buyer and seller. 房子的价格是买方和卖方之间争论的焦点。
point of contention: 争论点,  The exact definition of the term became a major point of contention.  该术语的准确定义成为一个主要的争论点。
