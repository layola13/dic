democratizing: /dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使民主化；使大众化 | making something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions based on class, wealth, etc. | The internet has played a crucial role in democratizing information. 互联网在信息民主化方面发挥了至关重要的作用。|  synonyms: popularizing, spreading, making accessible | antonyms: restricting, limiting, monopolizing | Verb

democratization: /dɪˌmɒkrətɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 民主化；大众化 | the process of making something accessible to everyone | The democratization of education is essential for a just society. 教育的民主化对于一个公正的社会至关重要。| synonyms: popularization, diffusion | antonyms: restriction, limitation | Noun
