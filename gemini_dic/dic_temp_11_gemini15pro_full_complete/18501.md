bending:/ˈbɛndɪŋ/| n. adj.|弯曲，屈服|The bending of the metal rod demonstrated its flexibility. 金属杆的弯曲显示了它的灵活性。|例句：The constant bending of the pipe eventually caused it to break.  管道不断的弯曲最终导致它断裂。|近义词：curving, flexing, bowing|反义词:straightening, rigidifying|n.

bending:/ˈbɛndɪŋ/| adj.|弯曲的|The bending branches of the willow tree swayed in the wind. 垂柳弯曲的树枝在风中摇曳。|例句：The bending path led us through the forest. 弯曲的小路带领我们穿过森林。|近义词：curved, crooked|反义词:straight|adj.



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bendan "to bend, curve, make crooked," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse benda "to bend, stretch a bow," and Old High German biugan "to bend."
