outrogue:/ˌaʊtˈroʊɡ/
verb
超过，胜过（在恶行或无赖行为上）
to surpass or exceed someone in roguery or villainous behavior
例句：He outrogued even the most hardened criminals. 他甚至比最顽固的罪犯还要恶劣。

例句：She managed to outrogue her competitors in the business world, using unethical tactics to gain an advantage.  她在商界设法比她的竞争对手更无赖，用不道德的策略来获得优势。

近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
反义词：be outdone, be surpassed

词性：v.
