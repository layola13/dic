bagreef: /ˈbæɡriːf/
Noun
暗礁，礁石
A submerged ridge of rock or coral near the surface of the water.
The ship struck a bagreef and sank.  这艘船撞上了暗礁沉没了。

近义词: reef, shoal
反义词: deep water
