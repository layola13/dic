nonperseverant:/ˌnɒnpɜːrsɪˈvɪərənt/ | adj. | 不持之以恒的，缺乏毅力的 | Not persevering; lacking persistence; easily giving up. | He was nonperseverant in his studies and often switched majors. 他学习缺乏毅力，经常换专业。| She made a nonperseverant attempt to learn the piano, quitting after only a few lessons. 她尝试学习钢琴，但缺乏毅力，只上了几节课就放弃了。 | 近义词: fickle, inconstant, irresolute, unstable, unsteady | 反义词: perseverant, persistent, tenacious, resolute, steadfast | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (not) + perseverant (persevering)
