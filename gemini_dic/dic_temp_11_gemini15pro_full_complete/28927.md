carrigeen:/ˈkærɪɡiːn/| n.|角叉菜，爱尔兰苔藓|A type of red seaweed (Chondrus crispus) found on the coasts of the North Atlantic, used as a thickener and stabilizer in food products.|Carrigeen moss is used to make a soothing drink. 角叉菜可以用来做一杯舒缓的饮料。|Synonyms: Irish moss, carrageenan moss|Antonyms: None|noun

carrageen:/ˈkærɪɡiːn/| n.|角叉菜胶|A gelatinous substance extracted from carrigeen moss, used as a thickener and stabilizer in food products.|Carrageenan is used in many processed foods. 角叉菜胶用于许多加工食品中。|Synonyms: carrageenan, Irish moss extract|Antonyms: None|noun
