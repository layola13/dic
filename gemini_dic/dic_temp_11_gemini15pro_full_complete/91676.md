hyperclassicalities:/ˌhaɪpərˌklæsɪˈkælɪtiz/
|n.|超级古典主义|Exaggerated adherence to classical principles or styles, often to the point of artificiality or affectation.|His music is full of hyperclassicalities, so ornate it becomes almost parodic. 他的音乐充满了超级古典主义，如此华丽以至于近乎滑稽。|
|n.|超级古典主义|The composer's later works were marked by a return to hyperclassicalities, stripping away the experimental elements of his earlier compositions.  这位作曲家后期的作品以回归超级古典主义为标志，摒弃了他早期作品中的实验元素。|
|近义词:|formalism, traditionalism, purism|
|反义词:|modernism, avant-garde, experimentalism|
