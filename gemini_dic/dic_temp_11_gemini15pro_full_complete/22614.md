boozed:/buːzd/
| adj.| 喝醉的 |Having drunk too much alcohol; intoxicated. |He came home boozed last night. 他昨晚醉醺醺地回家了。|例句：They were boozed and disorderly. 他们喝醉了，行为不检点。| 近义词：drunk, intoxicated, inebriated, tipsy, plastered |反义词: sober, temperate|词性:形容词
boozed:/buːzd/
| v. |  (booze的过去式和过去分词) 喝酒 |To drink alcohol, especially to excess. |They boozed all night. 他们喝了一整夜的酒。|例句：He spent the weekend boozing with his friends. 他整个周末都和朋友们在一起喝酒。|近义词：drink, imbibe, guzzle |反义词: abstain |词性:动词
