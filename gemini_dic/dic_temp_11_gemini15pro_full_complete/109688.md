leukaemia: /luːˈkiːmiə/ (UK) /luːˈkimiə/ (US)| n.|白血病|A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leukocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anemia and other symptoms.|My uncle was diagnosed with leukaemia last year. 我叔叔去年被诊断出患有白血病。|Synonyms: cancer of the blood, blood cancer|Antonyms: health, wellness|Noun

leukeumia: /luːˈkiːmiə/ (US)| n.|白血病|Same as leukaemia (British spelling).| Leukemia is a serious blood disorder. 白血病是一种严重的血液疾病。|Synonyms: cancer of the blood, blood cancer|Antonyms: health, wellness|Noun
