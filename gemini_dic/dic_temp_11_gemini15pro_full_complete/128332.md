neutering:/ˈnjuːtərɪŋ/ /ˈnuːtərɪŋ/
n.
绝育；阉割
The surgical removal of reproductive organs, rendering an animal incapable of reproduction.
例句：Neutering your pet can help prevent unwanted litters.  给你的宠物绝育可以帮助防止意外怀孕。

例句：Neutering is a common procedure for cats and dogs. 绝育是猫狗的常见手术。


近义词：spaying (specifically for females), castration
反义词：none
词性：noun
