faugh:/fɔː/
interjection
呸；啐；表示厌恶或 disgust 的感叹词
An exclamation of disgust or contempt.
Faugh! This milk is sour! 呸！这牛奶馊了！
近义词: ugh, yuck,呸
反义词: None (感叹词通常没有反义词)
词性: interjection

词源:
From Middle English *fagh, *faugh, *faw, *fow, of imitative origin.
