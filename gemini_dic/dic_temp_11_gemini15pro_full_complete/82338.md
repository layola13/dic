groves:/ɡroʊvz/| n. |树丛，小树林|A small group of trees.|We walked through the shady groves. 我们穿过阴凉的小树林。|近义词：woods, thicket, copse, forest|反义词:plain, field|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grove*, from Old English *grāf*, from Proto-Germanic *grōbō* (“grove, thicket, brushwood”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghrebh- (“to dig, scratch, scrape”). Cognate with Dutch *groef* (“groove, trench, furrow”), German *Grube* (“pit, mine”), Old Norse *grǫf* (“ditch, grave”).
