coleus: /ˈkoʊliəs/ | n. | 彩叶草，锦紫苏 | A plant with brightly colored leaves, often grown as a houseplant or in gardens. |  例句：The coleus added a vibrant splash of color to the garden.  彩叶草给花园增添了一抹鲜艳的色彩。

ETYMOLOGY->
From the genus name *Coleus*, derived from the Greek *koleos* meaning "sheath", referring to the way the filaments of the stamens are united into a tube.
