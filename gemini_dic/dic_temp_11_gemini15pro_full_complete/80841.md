goulash:/ˈɡuːlæʃ/ /ˈɡuːlɑːʃ/ | n. |红烩牛肉(一种匈牙利菜)；大杂烩|A highly seasoned stew of meat and vegetables, especially beef, onions, and paprika, originating in Hungary.  A mixture or jumble of different things.|例句：We had a delicious goulash for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了美味的红烩牛肉。

例句：The conference was a goulash of different opinions. 这次会议汇集了各种不同的意见。|近义词：stew, ragout, hodgepodge, medley|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly a simple dish like "plain rice")|词性:noun
