ditzier:/ˈdɪt.zi.ər/
adj.
更轻佻的，更愚蠢的
more lightheaded and silly; more frivolous and scatterbrained.
She seemed ditzier than usual after that second cocktail.  她喝了第二杯鸡尾酒后，看起来比平时更傻乎乎的。
近义词:giddier, flightier, sillier, frivolous
反义词:serious, sensible, sober, grave
词性:形容词 (Comparative adjective)


ditzy:/ˈdɪt.si/
adj.
轻佻的，愚蠢的
lightheaded and silly; frivolous and scatterbrained.
She has a ditzy personality. 她性格轻佻。
近义词:giddy, flighty, silly, frivolous
反义词:serious, sensible, sober, grave
词性:形容词

词源:  "Ditzy" and "ditzier" are believed to be derived from "dizzy," referring to a state of lightheadedness and disorientation, which can be associated with silliness or flightiness.
