acupuncturing: /ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃərɪŋ/
n.|针灸，针刺|The practice of inserting thin needles into specific points on the body to relieve pain or treat various health conditions.|Acupuncturing has been used for centuries to treat pain. 针灸几个世纪以来一直被用来治疗疼痛。|近义词: acupuncture treatment|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps conventional medicine)|词性: noun (gerund or present participle)
