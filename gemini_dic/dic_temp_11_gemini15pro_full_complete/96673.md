industrywide:/ˈɪndəstriwaɪd/
adj.|全行业的，遍及整个行业的|Affecting or involving all or most parts of a particular industry.|The recession had an industrywide impact. 经济衰退对整个行业都产生了影响。|Synonyms: industry-wide, across-the-board, universal, global|Antonyms: localized, limited, specific|adjective
