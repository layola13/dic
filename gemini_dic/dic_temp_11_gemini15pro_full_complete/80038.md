go west:/ɡoʊ west/ | vi. | 向西走；死亡；衰落 | To travel westward; to die; to decline or deteriorate. |  The pioneers went west in search of a better life.  拓荒者向西迁移，寻找更好的生活。

The old actor's career went west after the scandal.  这位老演员的职业生涯在丑闻之后一落千丈。| 近义词：decline, deteriorate, perish, expire | 反义词: improve, flourish, thrive | 词性: 动词短语
