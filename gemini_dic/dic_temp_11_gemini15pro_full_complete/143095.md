outbreathing:/ˌaʊtˈbriːðɪŋ/
n.
呼气，呼出
The process of exhaling air from the lungs.
During outbreathing, carbon dioxide is released from the body.  在呼气过程中，二氧化碳从体内排出。
The yoga instructor emphasized slow, controlled outbreathing.  瑜伽教练强调缓慢、控制的呼气。
近义词: exhalation, expiration
反义词: inbreathing, inspiration
词性: noun

Etymology: From 'out-' (meaning outwards) + 'breathing' (the act of respiring).  源自“out-”（意为向外）+“breathing”（呼吸的动作）。
