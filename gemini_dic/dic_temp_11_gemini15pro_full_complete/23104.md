bouquet: /boʊˈkeɪ/ (US) /buːˈkeɪ/ (UK)| n. |花束；酒香|A bunch of flowers, typically given as a gift. Also, the characteristic aroma of wine.|She received a beautiful bouquet of roses for her birthday. 她生日那天收到了一束美丽的玫瑰花。 The wine has a complex bouquet. 这款酒香气复杂。|近义词: bunch, nosegay, posy|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps scattering or individual flowers)|noun
