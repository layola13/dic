nondebilitative:/ˌnɒndɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
非衰弱性的；不使人衰弱的
Not causing debility or weakness; not debilitating.
The illness, though unpleasant, was nondebilitative and he was able to continue working. 虽然这种病令人不快，但并不使人衰弱，他还能继续工作。
Near Synonyms:  non-incapacitating, mild, moderate, manageable
Antonyms: debilitating, weakening, incapacitating
词性: adjective
