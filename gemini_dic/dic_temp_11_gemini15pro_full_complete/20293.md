bise:/baɪz/| v. |平分，二等分|To divide into two equal parts.|例句：Bisect the line segment AB. 平分线段AB。|例句：The road bisects the town. 这条路把小镇一分为二。|近义词：halve, divide |反义词: join, unite |词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *bissectus*, past participle of *bissecare* "to cut in two," from *bi-* "two" + *secare* "to cut."
