excrementous:/ˌɛkskrɪˈmɛntəs/
adj.
排泄的；粪便似的；污秽的
Consisting of or resembling excrement; filthy.
The zookeeper had the unenviable task of cleaning the excrementous cage.  动物园管理员不得不做一件不怎么令人羡慕的工作——清理满是粪便的笼子。

The air in the stable was thick and excrementous. 马厩里的空气又闷又臭，充满着粪便的气味。

近义词:fecal, dungy, filthy, foul
反义词:clean, pure, pristine
词性:adjective


词源: From Late Latin *excrementosus, from Latin excrementum (“excrement”).
