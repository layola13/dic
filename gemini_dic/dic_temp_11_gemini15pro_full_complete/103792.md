jouster:/ˈdʒaʊstər/| n.| 比武骑士；马上长矛比武者 |A person who takes part in jousting.| The jouster lowered his lance and charged towards his opponent. 比武骑士放低了他的长矛，向他的对手冲去。| The jouster unseated his rival with a powerful blow. 比武骑士用强有力的一击把他的对手打下马。 | 近义词:knight, competitor, contestant | 反义词:spectator | 词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *jouster*, from Old French *jostere*, from *joster* "to joust," from Vulgar Latin *juxtare* "to approach, to join," frequentative of Latin *jungere* "to join."
