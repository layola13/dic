capsular:/ˈkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
胶囊的；荚膜的
Relating to a capsule, especially a biological capsule.
The bacteria has a distinct capsular layer. 这种细菌有一个明显的荚膜层。
近义词:capsulated, enclosed
反义词: acapsular
词性: adjective
