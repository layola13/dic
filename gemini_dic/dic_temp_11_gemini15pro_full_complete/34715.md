clicher:/ˈkliːʃeɪ/ /kliˈʃeɪ/ | n. | 陈词滥调；老生常谈 | A phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought. |  The plot of the movie was full of clichers.  这部电影的情节充满了陈词滥调。| synonyms: platitude, banality, truism, commonplace | antonyms: originality, novelty, freshness | noun

clicher:/kliˈʃeɪ/ /ˈkliːʃeɪ/ | adj. | 陈腐的；老套的 |  (Rarely used as an adjective) Overused and lacking originality.  | His writing style is rather clicher. 他的写作风格相当陈腐。 | synonyms: hackneyed, stale, trite, overused | antonyms: original, fresh, innovative | adjective
