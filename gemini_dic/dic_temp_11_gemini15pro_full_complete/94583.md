impracticability:/ɪmˌpræktɪkəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可行性，不切实际|The impracticability of the plan was evident from the start.  这个计划从一开始就显而易见是不可行的。|
Etymology: From Late Latin *impracticabilitas, from impracticābilis "impracticable," from in- "not" + practicābilis "practicable."
Synonyms: infeasibility, unworkability, impossibility, unattainability
Antonyms: feasibility, practicality, possibility, viability
Part of Speech: Noun
