codefendant:/ˌkəʊdɪˈfɛndənt/| n.|共同被告|A person who is charged with a crime along with another person or persons.|例句：The codefendant pleaded guilty to a lesser charge. 共同被告对较轻的指控认罪。|例句：She and her codefendant were tried together. 她和她的共同被告一起受审。|近义词：accomplice, associate|反义词:plaintiff, prosecutor|noun
