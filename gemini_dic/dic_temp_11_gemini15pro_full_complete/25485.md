bullfight:/ˈbʊlˌfaɪt/ | n. | 斗牛 | A public spectacle, especially in Spain, Portugal, and Latin America, in which a bullfighter (matador) confronts and usually kills a bull in an arena. |  The bullfight was a thrilling spectacle of skill and bravery. 斗牛是一场惊心动魄的技巧和勇气的展示。
