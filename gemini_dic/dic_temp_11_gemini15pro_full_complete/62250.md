encrypts:/ɪnˈkrɪpts/ | v. | 加密 | Converts (information or data) into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access. |  The software encrypts all data before sending it over the network.  该软件在通过网络发送所有数据之前对其进行加密。 | synonyms: codes, ciphers, scrambles | antonyms: decrypts, deciphers | verb

encrypts:/ɪnˈkrɪpts/ | v. | 加密 |  To transform information into a secret code so that it can only be understood by authorized individuals. | He encrypts his emails to protect them from hackers. 他对电子邮件进行加密以防止黑客入侵。 | synonyms:  conceals, protects, secures | antonyms: exposes, reveals, decrypts | verb
