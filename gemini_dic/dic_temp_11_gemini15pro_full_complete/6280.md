amorette:/ˌæməˈrɛt/ | n. | 爱情小事；爱情琐事 | A small love affair; a love trinket or token. | The antique shop specialized in amorettes and other romantic keepsakes. 这家古董店专门经营爱情小物件和其他浪漫的纪念品. | Synonyms: love token, keepsake, memento | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider items of hate or disdain) | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *amorette*, diminutive of *amour* (love), from Latin *amor*.
