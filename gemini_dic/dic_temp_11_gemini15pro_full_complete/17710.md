bedbug:/ˈbedbʌɡ/| n.|臭虫，床虱|A small, wingless, bloodsucking insect that infests houses, especially beds, and feeds on human blood.|例句：The hotel room was infested with bedbugs.  酒店房间里到处都是臭虫。|近义词：chimex,  bed louse|反义词: N/A|noun
