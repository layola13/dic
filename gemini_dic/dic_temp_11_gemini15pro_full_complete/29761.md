catstick:/ˈkætˌstɪk/| n. |逗猫棒，猫玩具|A toy consisting of a stick with feathers or other enticing objects attached to a string, used to play with a cat.|She dangled the catstick in front of the kitten. 她把逗猫棒在小猫面前晃来晃去。|近义词：cat toy, teaser|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
