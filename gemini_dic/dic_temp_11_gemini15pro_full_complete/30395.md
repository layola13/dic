centime: /ˈsɑːnˌtim/ /ˈsɛnˌtim/ | n. | 生丁（法国、比利时、瑞士等国的货币单位，等于百分之一法郎或百分之一瑞士法郎） | A monetary unit equal to one hundredth of a franc, used in several countries, including France, Belgium, and Switzerland. |  He found a few centimes in his pocket. 他在他的口袋里找到了几个生丁。

The price was 50 francs and 25 centimes. 价格是50法郎25生丁。
