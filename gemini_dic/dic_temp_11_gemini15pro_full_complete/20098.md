biospheric:/ˌbaɪəˈsfɪrɪk/ | adj. | 生物圈的 | Relating to the biosphere, the part of the Earth's crust, waters, and atmosphere that supports life. | The biospheric effects of climate change are far-reaching. 气候变化对生物圈的影响是深远的。| The Amazon rainforest plays a crucial role in the biospheric health of the planet. 亚马逊雨林在地球生物圈健康中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: ecological, environmental | 反义词: abiotic | 词性: adjective

Etymology: biosphere (n.) + -ic (adj. suffix)
From bio- + sphere. Bio- comes from Greek bios "life". Sphere comes from Greek sphaira "ball, globe".
