filtrate:/ˈfɪltreɪt/| n. v.|滤液，过滤；滤出|a liquid which has passed through a filter|The filtrate was then analyzed for contaminants. 滤液随后被分析了污染物含量。|近义词:solution, effluent|反义词:residue, precipitate|n.

filtrate:/ˈfɪltreɪt/| v.|过滤；滤出|to pass (a liquid, gas, light, etc.) through a filter in order to remove something unwanted|The kidneys filtrate waste products from the blood. 肾脏过滤血液中的废物。|近义词:filter, strain, sieve|反义词:contaminate, pollute|v.
