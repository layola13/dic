desktop: /ˈdɛskˌtɑːp/ (US) /ˈdɛskˌtɒp/ (UK)| adj.|台式的，桌面的|Relating to a desktop computer. |He prefers a desktop computer to a laptop. 他更喜欢台式电脑而不是笔记本电脑。|Synonyms: non-portable, stationary|Antonyms: mobile, portable|adjective
