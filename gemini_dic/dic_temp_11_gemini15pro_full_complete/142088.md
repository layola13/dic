orientability:/ˌɔːriɛntəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可定向性 | The property of a surface or manifold that allows a consistent choice of surface normal vector at every point.  |  The orientability of the Möbius strip is a classic problem in topology. 莫比乌斯带的可定向性是拓扑学中的一个经典问题。 |  orientable, directionality  | non-orientability | noun
