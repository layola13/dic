bipedal:/baɪˈpiːdl/
adjective
两足动物的；用两足行走的
(of an animal) using only two legs for walking.
Humans are bipedal. 人类是两足动物。
Bipedal locomotion is a defining characteristic of hominids.  双足运动是原始人类的定义特征。
近义词:two-footed
反义词:quadrupedal
词性:adjective
