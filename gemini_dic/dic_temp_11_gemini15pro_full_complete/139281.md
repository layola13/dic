obliterate: /əˈblɪtəˌreɪt/ | v. | 消灭，抹去，摧毁 | To destroy something completely so that nothing is left. | The bombing raid obliterated the entire city block. 轰炸袭击彻底摧毁了整个街区。| synonyms: destroy, eradicate, annihilate, wipe out, expunge | antonyms: create, build, establish, preserve | verb

obliterated: /əˈblɪtəˌreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被摧毁的，被抹去的 | Completely destroyed or erased. | The ancient city was obliterated by the volcano. 这座古城被火山摧毁了。| synonyms: destroyed, eradicated, annihilated, wiped out, expunged | antonyms: created, built, established, preserved | adjective

Etymology: From Latin obliteratus, past participle of obliterare "to erase, efface, blot out," from ob "over" + littera "letter."
