bags:/bæɡz/
n.|包，袋子|A flexible container with an opening at the top, used for carrying things.|I carried my groceries in two bags. 我用两个袋子装着我的食品杂货。|Synonyms: sacks, pouches, containers|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unpackaged goods")|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse baggi ("bag, bundle"), from Proto-Germanic *bag-, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰak- (“staff, stick”).  The original sense likely referred to a sack made of a rough material.
