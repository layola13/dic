adversely:/ədˈvɜːrsli/
adverb
不利地；有害地
in a way that prevents success or development; negatively or unfavorably
The cold weather has adversely affected the crops. 寒冷的天气对农作物造成了不利影响。
近义词: negatively, unfavorably, detrimentally, harmfully
反义词: favorably, positively, beneficially
词性: adverb

adverse:/ˈædvɜːrs/
adjective
不利的；有害的；相反的
preventing success or development; harmful; unfavorable
Adverse weather conditions forced the cancellation of the game.  恶劣的天气条件迫使比赛取消。
近义词: unfavorable, negative, detrimental, harmful
反义词: favorable, positive, beneficial, advantageous
词性: adjective

词源：From Latin *adversus, from ad- 'to' + versus 'turned'.
