grungiest:/ˈɡrʌndʒiɪst/ | adj. | 最邋遢的，最脏乱的 | superlative form of grungy: extremely dirty and unpleasant, especially in an unkempt way  | The grungiest bar in town was surprisingly popular with the college students. 城里最邋遢的酒吧却意外地受大学生欢迎。 | Example:  His apartment was the grungiest place I'd ever seen, with clothes piled everywhere and dirty dishes stacked in the sink. 他的公寓是我见过的最脏乱的地方，衣服堆得到处都是，脏盘子堆在水池里。| Synonyms:  filthiest, dirtiest, grubbiest, scruffiest | Antonyms:  cleanest, tidiest, neatest | word type: adjective
