jerry-built:/ˈdʒɛri bɪlt/
adjective
草率建造的，粗制滥造的
built cheaply and poorly, likely to break down
The jerry-built house collapsed in the first earthquake. 这栋粗制滥造的房子在第一次地震中就倒塌了。
近义词:shoddy, flimsy, poorly constructed
反义词:well-built, sturdy, solid
词性:adjective

词源:
From the proper name "Jerry," a derogatory nickname for Germans during World War I, used to describe things perceived as cheaply or poorly made.  Later generalized to refer to anything flimsy or poorly constructed.
