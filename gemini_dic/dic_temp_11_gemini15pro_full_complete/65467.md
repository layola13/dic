eurocentrism:/ˌjʊərəʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/
n.
欧洲中心主义
The belief that Europe is the most important part of the world and that European culture and values are superior to others.
Example:  The history textbook exhibited a strong eurocentrism, focusing primarily on European events and perspectives.
教科书展现出强烈的欧洲中心主义，主要关注欧洲事件和观点。

Example:  Her research challenges the eurocentrism prevalent in many academic disciplines.
她的研究挑战了许多学科中普遍存在的欧洲中心主义。
近义词: Western-centrism, Occidentalism
反义词: multiculturalism, Afrocentrism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From euro- (combining form meaning "Europe") + -centrism (suffix meaning "centered on," "focused on").  "Euro-" ultimately derives from Greek "Eurōpē" (Europe). "-centrism" derives from Latin "centrum" (center) and Greek "kentron" (sharp point, center).
