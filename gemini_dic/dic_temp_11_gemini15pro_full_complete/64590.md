eructation:/ɪˌrʌkˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 打嗝；喷出；爆发 | the act of expelling gas noisily from the stomach through the mouth |  After drinking the soda, he let out a loud eructation.  喝完汽水后，他大声地打了个嗝。 | synonyms: belch, burp | antonyms: none | noun

eructation:/ɪˌrʌkˈteɪʃən/ | n. | (火山、间歇泉)喷发 | a sudden violent ejection of something | The eructation of the volcano sent ash and debris miles into the air. 火山的喷发将火山灰和碎屑送入空中数英里。 | synonyms: eruption, outburst | antonyms: none | noun
