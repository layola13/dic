non-rival:/ˌnɒnˈraɪvl/ /ˌnɑːnˈraɪvl/| adj.|非竞争性的，非对抗性的|A good is non-rivalrous if one person's consumption of it does not diminish another's ability to consume it.|例句：Public parks are often cited as an example of a non-rival good. 公共公园通常被认为是非竞争性商品的一个例子。

例句：Knowledge is considered a non-rival good because one person learning something doesn't prevent another person from learning the same thing. 知识被认为是非竞争性商品，因为一个人学习某样东西并不妨碍另一个人学习同样东西。|近义词：non-excludable, public|反义词:rival, private|词性:adjective

non-rivalrous:/ˌnɒnˈraɪvlərəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈraɪvlrəs/| adj.|非竞争性的，非对抗性的|Same as non-rival.|例句：Clean air is a non-rivalrous resource until it becomes polluted.  干净的空气是一种非竞争性资源，直到它被污染。

例句：Street lighting is a non-rivalrous service. 路灯是一种非竞争性服务。|近义词：non-excludable, public|反义词:rival, private|词性:adjective
