dichromate: /daɪˈkroʊmeɪt/ | n. | 重铬酸盐 | a salt containing the divalent anion Cr₂O₇²⁻ | Potassium dichromate is a powerful oxidizing agent. 重铬酸钾是一种强氧化剂。 | 近义词: bichromate | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From di- + chromate.  "Di-" signifies two, and "chromate" refers to salts containing the chromate anion (CrO₄²⁻).  Ultimately from Greek "khrōma" meaning "color," due to the bright colors of chromium compounds.
