immanently:/ˈɪmənəntli/
adjective
内在地，固有地
Existing or operating within; inherent.
God's grace is immanently present in the world.  上帝的恩典内在地存在于世界上。
inherently, intrinsically, essentially
extrinsically, externally
词性:形容词
