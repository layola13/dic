castration:/kæˈstreɪʃən/ | n. |阉割; 去势 |The castration of male animals is a common farming practice.  雄性动物的阉割是一种常见的农业实践。| Removal of the testicles or ovaries; the act of castrating or the state of being castrated.  切除睾丸或卵巢；阉割的行为或被阉割的状态。|例句：The farmer performed castration on the piglets. 农民对小猪进行了阉割。

castration:/kæˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 摧毁; 毁坏(尤指创造力) |The censorship amounted to a castration of artistic expression. 审查制度无异于扼杀了艺术表达。|The act of damaging or destroying something, especially creativity. | 例句：He felt his creativity had been castrated by the strict rules. 他觉得严格的规定扼杀了他的创造力。


近义词：emasculation, neutering, gelding | 反义词: - | 词性: noun
