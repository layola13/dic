extine:/ˈɛkstɪn/
n.
外壁；花粉外壁；孢子外壁
The outer layer of the wall of a pollen grain or spore.
The extine of the pollen grain is highly ornamented.  花粉粒的外壁装饰精美。

The fossilized extine provides valuable information about ancient plant life.  花粉外壁的化石提供了关于古代植物生命的宝贵信息。

近义词:exospore, outer wall
反义词:intine
词性:noun
