goalmouths: /ˈɡoʊlˌmaʊðz/ | n. |球门|The area directly in front of a goal, especially in soccer.  The plural form of "goalmouth". |The striker blasted the ball wide of the goalmouths.  前锋将球射偏，远离了球门。| The ball rebounded off both goalmouths before finally going in. 球在两个球门之间反弹，最终进了球门。 | synonyms: goals, nets | antonyms: field, midfield | noun
