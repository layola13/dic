nobleman:/ˈnoʊblmən/| n.|贵族；高贵的人|A man who belongs to the nobility, a high-ranking social class historically associated with hereditary titles, land ownership, and privilege. |例句：The nobleman lived in a grand estate. 这位贵族住在一个宏伟的庄园里。

例句：He was raised as a nobleman and enjoyed a life of luxury. 他被抚养成为一名贵族，享受着奢华的生活。|近义词：aristocrat, peer, lord|反义词：commoner, peasant|Noun
