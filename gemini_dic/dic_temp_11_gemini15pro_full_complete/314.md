abets:/əˈbets/
v.
教唆；煽动；怂恿
encourages or assists (someone) to do something wrong, in particular, to commit a crime.
He abetted his friend in the robbery. 他教唆他的朋友抢劫。
近义词:encourages, assists, supports, aids, incites, instigates
反义词:dissuades, discourages, prevents, hinders, obstructs
词性:动词

abets:/əˈbets/
词源: From Old French abeter meaning "to bait" or "to harass with dogs," from a- "to" + beter "to bait."  The meaning shifted to "incite" and then to its current sense.
