overanimation:/ˌoʊvərˌænɪˈmeɪʃən/
noun
过度活跃；过分激动
Excessive animation or excitement.
Her overanimation made it difficult to understand what she was trying to say. 她过度激动，让人难以理解她想说什么。
The child's overanimation quickly tired him out.  这个孩子过度兴奋，很快就累了。

近义词:  excitability, exuberance, hyperactivity
反义词:  calmness, composure, tranquility
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + animation. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Animation" comes from the Latin word "anima," meaning "soul" or "breath," and refers to the state of being lively or full of spirit.
