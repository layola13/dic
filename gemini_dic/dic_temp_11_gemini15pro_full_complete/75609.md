fugaciousness:/fjuːˈɡeɪʃəsnəs/| n. |短暂，易逝|The quality of being fleeting or ephemeral; transience.|The fugaciousness of youth is a common theme in literature. 青春的短暂是文学中的常见主题。|Synonyms: transience, ephemerality, brevity, impermanence|Antonyms: permanence, eternity, longevity|noun


fugacious:/fjuːˈɡeɪʃəs/| adj. |短暂的，易逝的|Fleeting; lasting a short time.|The fugacious beauty of the cherry blossoms is breathtaking. 樱花短暂的美令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: fleeting, ephemeral, transient, short-lived|Antonyms: permanent, lasting, enduring, perpetual|adjective
