overcondensations:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːndenˈseɪʃənz/
noun
过度冷凝；过度浓缩
The process by which something becomes excessively condensed. This could refer to various substances, from gases turning into liquids to the compaction of information.
Overcondensations in the manuscript made it difficult to read.  手稿过度浓缩，难以阅读。
The overcondensations of the plot made the movie confusing.  情节过度浓缩，使电影令人费解。
synonyms: compaction, compression, condensation
antonyms: expansion, rarefaction, dilution
