cook-the-books:/kʊk ðə bʊks/
|v.|做假账|To falsify financial records, typically to make a company appear more profitable than it actually is.  This involves manipulating figures, hiding debts, or inflating income.|The accountant was caught cooking the books and sentenced to prison. 会计被发现做假账并被判入狱。|Synonyms: falsify, manipulate, embezzle, defraud|Antonyms: audit accurately, report honestly|verb
