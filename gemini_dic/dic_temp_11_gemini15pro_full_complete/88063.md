highland: /ˈhaɪlənd/ (US), /ˈhaɪlənd/ (UK)| n. adj.|高地，高原；苏格兰高地|A mountainous area or a region of elevated land.  It often refers specifically to the Scottish Highlands.|例句：The Scottish Highlands are known for their rugged beauty. 苏格兰高地以其崎岖的美丽而闻名。

|例句：He lives in the highlands of Tibet. 他住在西藏高原。|近义词：mountain, upland, plateau, moor|反义词:lowland|词性:名词/形容词


highland: /ˈhaɪlənd/ (US), /ˈhaɪlənd/ (UK)|adj.|高地的；高原的|Relating to or characteristic of a highland or highlands, particularly the Scottish Highlands.|例句：Highland cattle are a hardy breed. 高地牛是一种耐寒的品种。
