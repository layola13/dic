hermit:/ˈhɜːmɪt/ (US) /ˈhɜːmɪt/ (UK)| n.|隐士；遁世者|A person who lives a solitary life and avoids other people, especially for religious reasons.|The old man lived as a hermit in the mountains.  这位老人像隐士一样住在山里。|Synonyms: recluse, anchorite, solitary|Antonyms: socialite, extrovert|noun

hermitage:/ˈhɜːmɪtɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈhɜːmɪtɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|隐居处；修道院|The dwelling of a hermit; a secluded retreat.|The hermitage was a small cabin deep in the woods. 这座隐居处是位于树林深处的一个小木屋。|Synonyms: retreat, sanctuary, monastery|Antonyms: city, metropolis|noun
