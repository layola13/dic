christianize: /ˈkrɪstʃənaɪz/ /ˈkrɪstiənaɪz/
verb
使基督教化；使皈依基督教
To convert to Christianity; to make Christian.
The missionaries attempted to christianize the indigenous population.  传教士试图使土著居民皈依基督教。
The Roman Empire was christianized over several centuries. 罗马帝国在几个世纪的过程中逐渐基督教化。
近义词: evangelize, proselytize
反义词: secularize, paganize
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *christianizāre, from Latin Chrīstiānus (Christian) + -izāre (suffix forming verbs meaning ‘to make into,’ ‘to cause to be’).
