godfathering:/ˈɡɒdfɑːðərɪŋ/ | n. | 教父职责；精神指导；支持；赞助 | The act of serving as a godfather, offering guidance, support, or sponsorship, often in a context beyond a religious ceremony.  It can also refer to the act of supporting or initiating something. | He took his godfathering duties very seriously, guiding his godson through life's challenges. 他非常认真地对待教父的职责，引导他的教子应对生活中的挑战。 | The mayor's godfathering of the new arts program ensured its success. 市长对新艺术项目的支持确保了它的成功。 |  sponsorship, backing, mentoring, championing |  neglect, opposition | noun
