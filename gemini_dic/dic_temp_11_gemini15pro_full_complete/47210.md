daymare:/ˈdeɪmɛər/ | n. | 噩梦，白日梦 | A frightening or unpleasant dream experienced while awake.  A daytime version of a nightmare. |  I had a terrible daymare about failing my exam. 我做了一个可怕的白日梦，梦见我考试不及格。 |  Example: After the accident, she experienced frequent daymares. 事故发生后，她经常做噩梦。| 近义词: nightmare, bad dream, hallucination | 反义词: sweet dream, pleasant dream | 词性: noun

词源:  A blend of *day* and *nightmare*.
