criticizes: /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 批评，批判 | To express disapproval of someone or something, finding fault or highlighting shortcomings. | He criticizes her work ethic. 他批评她的职业道德。| The report criticizes the government for its handling of the crisis.  该报告批评了政府对危机的处理方式。| synonyms: condemns, censures, denounces, reproaches, upbraids | antonyms: praises, commends, lauds, applauds | verb (third-person singular simple present)


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *critiquer*, from Greek *kritikós* (κριτικός) meaning "able to discern, judge," from *krínō* (κρίνω) meaning "I separate, judge."
