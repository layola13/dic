counteractingly:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktɪŋli/ | adv. | 相反地，抵消地 | Acting in opposition to something; in a way that cancels out or reduces the effect of something. | The medicine, counteractingly, made his symptoms worse.  这种药反而使他的症状恶化了。 |  His efforts, counteractingly, only exacerbated the problem. 他的努力反而加剧了问题。 | 近义词: conversely, inversely, oppositely | 反义词: synergistically, complementarily | 词性: 副词
