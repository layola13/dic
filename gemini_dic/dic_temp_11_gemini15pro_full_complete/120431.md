minke: /ˈmɪŋki/ | n. |小须鲸|A relatively small baleen whale (Balaenoptera acutorostrata) with a sharply pointed snout, found in all oceans.|例句：The minke whale is the smallest of the rorquals. 小须鲸是须鲸中最小的。|例句：Minke whales are often hunted for their meat and oil. 小须鲸经常因为它们的肉和油而被捕猎。|近义词：rorqual, whale|反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal species)|词性:noun
