haulages:/ˈhɔːlɪdʒɪz/
n.
运输; 搬运; 拖运
The act of hauling or transporting something, especially goods, over a distance.
The company specializes in heavy haulages across the country.  这家公司专门从事全国各地的重型货物运输。
The haulages of coal from the mine to the power plant were frequent. 从煤矿到发电厂的煤炭运输很频繁。
近义词:transport, carriage, conveyance, freight
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "haul," which comes from the Old Norse "hala" meaning "to pull, drag."  The suffix "-age" forms nouns of action or process.
