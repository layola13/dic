logjams:/ˈlɒɡdʒæmz/ (US) /ˈlɒɡdʒæmz/ (UK)
n.| 木材阻塞；僵局；困境| A blockage in a river caused by an accumulation of logs and other debris, or any similar blockage or deadlock.|The heavy rains caused a logjam in the river.  暴雨导致河道堵塞。|近义词：blockage, deadlock, obstruction, gridlock, stalemate|反义词:flow, progress, solution
词性:名词
