cupidities:/ˌkjuːpɪˈdɪtiz/| n. |贪婪，贪欲|Intense desires, especially for wealth or possessions.  It refers to an excessive or inordinate longing, often associated with greed or avarice.|例句：His cupidities knew no bounds, driving him to amass a vast fortune through ruthless means. 他的贪婪无止境，驱使他通过无情的手段积累巨额财富。

例句：The company's downfall was attributed to the unchecked cupidities of its executives. 该公司的垮台归咎于其高管们不受约束的贪婪。|近义词：greed, avarice, covetousness, rapacity |反义词: generosity, contentment, altruism|词性:名词(plural noun)
