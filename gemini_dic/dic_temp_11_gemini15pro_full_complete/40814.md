convolvulaceous:/kənˌvɒlvjʊˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
旋花科的；像旋花科植物的
Relating to or resembling plants of the Convolvulaceae family, typically having trumpet-shaped flowers and twining stems.
The garden is filled with convolvulaceous blooms in shades of pink and purple. 花园里开满了粉色和紫色的旋花科植物。
Near synonyms: twining, climbing, viny
Antonyms: erect, upright
词性: adjective
