incinerated:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪtɪd/
verb
焚化的，烧毁的
reduced to ashes; burnt to ashes.
The documents were incinerated after the trial.  审判后文件被焚毁了。
The waste was incinerated in a special facility.  废物在一个专门的设施中被焚化了。
synonyms: burned, cremated, destroyed by fire
antonyms: preserved, protected, saved
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incineratus*, past participle of *incinerare* (“to reduce to ashes”), from *in-* (“in, into”) + *cinis*, *cineris* (“ashes”).
