liberates:/ˈlɪbəreɪts/
verb
解放，释放
sets free from confinement, restraint, or oppression.
The new law liberates small businesses from excessive regulations. 新法律将小型企业从过多的监管中解放出来。
frees, releases, emancipates
confines, restricts, imprisons
