air-traps:/ˈɛər træps/| n.|气阱；空气阻隔；空气滞留|A pocket of air trapped within a solid or liquid, often causing problems in pipes or other systems.  It can also refer to a situation where air is trapped in the lungs, such as in certain medical conditions.|The plumber found an air-trap in the pipe that was causing the gurgling sound. 水管工在管道中发现了一个气阱，导致了汩汩的声音。

The diver had to ascend slowly to avoid air-traps in his bloodstream. 潜水员必须缓慢上升以避免血液中的空气滞留。|近义词：air pockets, airlocks|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "continuous flow")|noun
