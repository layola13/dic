outvillain:/ˌaʊtˈvɪlən/
verb
胜过；在邪恶方面超过
to be more villainous than
例句：He outvillained all the other villains in the play. 他在剧中比所有其他反派角色都更邪恶。

例句：The emperor's cruelty outvillained even his most ruthless predecessors.  这位皇帝的残暴甚至超过了他最冷酷无情的几位前任。
近义词：surpass, outdo, exceed, eclipse
反义词：be outdone by, fall short of
词性：动词
