depressurizers:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzərz/ | n. | 减压器，卸压装置 |A device or system designed to reduce pressure, often in a controlled manner, such as in an aircraft cabin or a pressure vessel.| The aircraft's depressurizers activated during the descent. 飞机的减压器在下降过程中启动了。 | synonyms: pressure relief valves, pressure regulators, safety valves | antonyms: pressurizers | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning 'down, away from') + pressurize (verb meaning 'to subject to pressure') + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments).  Ultimately from Latin pressus, past participle of premere 'to press'.
