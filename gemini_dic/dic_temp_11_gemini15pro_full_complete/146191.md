overexertion:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrʃən/| n.|过度劳累，用力过度|The athlete collapsed due to overexertion.  这位运动员因过度劳累而晕倒了。|Overexertion can lead to serious health problems. 过度劳累会导致严重的健康问题。|近义词：strain, overwork, exhaustion|反义词:relaxation, rest|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + exertion.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Exertion" comes from the Latin "exertus," the past participle of "exercere," meaning "to exercise" or "to put forth."
