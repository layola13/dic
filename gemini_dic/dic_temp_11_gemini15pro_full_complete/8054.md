anthropological:/ˌænθrəpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 人类学的 | Relating to the study of humankind, particularly in its origins, development, customs, and beliefs. | Example:  Her research involves anthropological fieldwork in remote Amazonian tribes. 她的研究涉及在亚马逊偏远部落进行人类学实地考察。

Example:  The museum houses an impressive collection of anthropological artifacts.  博物馆收藏了令人印象深刻的人类学文物。 | Synonyms: ethnographic, ethnological | Antonyms: - | word type: adjective
