noninheritability:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɛrɪtəbɪləti/ | n. | 不可遗传性，非遗传性 | The noninheritability of acquired characteristics is a cornerstone of modern genetics.  |  The study demonstrated the noninheritability of the learned behavior.  这项研究证明了学习行为的不可遗传性。| near synonyms: non-heredity, acquired trait  | antonyms: heritability, heredity | noun

non-inheritable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可遗传的，非遗传的 |  Eye color is inheritable, but a learned skill like playing the piano is non-inheritable. |  Tattoos are non-inheritable modifications to the body. 纹身是对身体的不可遗传的修饰。| near synonyms: non-hereditary, acquired | antonyms: heritable, hereditary, genetic | adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
inherit: from Old French enheriter "to inherit," from Latin inhereditare "to appoint as heir," from in- "in" + heres (genitive heredis) "heir"
-ability (suffix): from Middle English -abilite, from Old French -ableté, from Latin -abilitatem, suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives ending in -abilis.
