encampments:/ɪnˈkæmpmənts/
n.
营地；扎营
A place where a group of people has camped, especially for a long time.
The refugees set up encampments along the border. 
难民们沿着边境搭建了营地。

encampments:/ɪnˈkæmpmənts/
n.
(军队)驻扎；宿营
The act of setting up a camp, especially by an army.
The general ordered the encampments of troops near the river.
将军下令部队在河边扎营。

encampments:/ɪnˈkæmpmənts/
n.
临时住所（群）
A collection of tents or other temporary shelters.
The city provided temporary encampments for the homeless.
这座城市为无家可归者提供了临时住所。



近义词:camps, settlements, bivouacs
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
