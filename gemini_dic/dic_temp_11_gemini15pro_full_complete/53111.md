diphyllous:/daɪˈfɪləs/
adj.
二叶的；有两片叶的
Having two leaves or leaflike structures.
The diphyllous plant produced a small, delicate flower.  这株二叶植物开出了一朵娇小精致的花。
The orchid is diphyllous, with two prominent leaves at its base.  这株兰花是二叶的，基部有两片明显的叶子。
近义词:bileaved, two-leaved
反义词:polyphyllous (多叶的)
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *di- "two" + *phyllon "leaf"
