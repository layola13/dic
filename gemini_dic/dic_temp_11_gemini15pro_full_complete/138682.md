nugify:/ˈnʌɡɪfaɪ/| v. |大麻化；将…制成大麻制品|To turn something into a cannabis product, often referring to converting cannabis flower into concentrates like hash or wax.  This is a slang term.|Example: They nugified the rest of their harvest into shatter. 他们把剩下的收获物制成了碎玻璃状的大麻浓缩物。

Example:  He's going to nugify that trim into some potent oil. 他要把那些修剪下来的大麻叶子制成一些强效的大麻油。|近义词:concentrate, extract|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "decarboxylate" in a specific chemical context)|词性:动词
