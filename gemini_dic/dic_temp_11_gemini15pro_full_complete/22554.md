boosters:/ˈbuːstərz/
n.
助推器；增强剂；支持者；加强针
Something that boosts or increases something else; a supporter or promoter.
The new boosters helped propel the rocket into space. 新的助推器帮助火箭推进到太空。
synonyms: amplifier, enhancer, promoter, supporter
antonyms: inhibitor, suppressant
词性:名词(plural)
