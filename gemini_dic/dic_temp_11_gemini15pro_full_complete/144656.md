outwrest:/ˌaʊtˈrest/| vt.| (用力)  wrest from; take by force| 夺取；用力取得 | He outwrested the sword from his opponent's grasp. 他从对手手中夺走了剑。| 近义词:wrench, wring, extract, seize, usurp| 反义词:yield, surrender, relinquish| 词性:动词

词源:
From out- + wrest.  "Out-" acts as an intensifier, suggesting a surpassing or exceeding in the action of wresting.  "Wrest" comes from Old English *wrǣstan, from Proto-Germanic *wraistjaną (“to twist, wring”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)wrey- (“to turn, twist, wind”).
