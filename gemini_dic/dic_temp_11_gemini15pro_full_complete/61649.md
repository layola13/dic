emissaries:/ˈemɪsəriz/ | n. | 使者，特使，密使 |  A person sent on a special mission, usually diplomatic. | The king sent his emissaries to negotiate a peace treaty. 国王派遣使者去谈判和平条约。| synonyms: envoy, ambassador, delegate, representative, agent | antonyms: receiver, recipient | noun
