mind-numbingly:/maɪndˈnʌmɪŋli/
| adv. | 令人麻木地，极其乏味地 |  Extremely boring and tedious, causing a loss of sensation or feeling.  | The lecture was mind-numbingly dull.  这讲座无聊得令人麻木。 | Synonyms:  tedious, boring, monotonous, dull, insipid | Antonyms: stimulating, exciting, engaging, interesting, captivating | 词性: 副词

mind-numbing:/maɪndˈnʌmɪŋ/
| adj. | 令人麻木的，极其乏味的 | Extremely boring and tedious, causing a loss of sensation or feeling. |  I had a mind-numbing job sorting through paperwork all day. 我整天做着整理文件的令人麻木的工作。 | Synonyms: tedious, boring, monotonous, dull, insipid | Antonyms: stimulating, exciting, engaging, interesting, captivating | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mind" (referring to mental processes) + "numbing" (causing a loss of sensation).  "Numb" originates from Old English "numen," meaning "taken,"  related to the idea of senses being taken away.
