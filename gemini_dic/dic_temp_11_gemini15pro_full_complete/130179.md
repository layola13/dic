non-romantic:/nɒn.rəʊˈmæntɪk/ | /nɑːn.roʊˈmæntɪk/ | adj. | 非浪漫的；不浪漫的；没有爱情的 | Not characterized by romance; lacking in romantic elements. |  They have a very non-romantic relationship, more like a business partnership. 他们之间没有浪漫的爱情，更像是商业伙伴。

They decided to have a non-romantic dinner together, just as friends. 他们决定一起吃一顿没有浪漫气氛的晚餐，就像朋友一样。 | 近义词: unromantic, platonic, practical | 反义词: romantic, passionate, sentimental | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".  Indicates negation or absence.
romantic (adjective): from French romantique, ultimately from Late Latin romanicus "of the Roman language," referring to vernacular tales of chivalry and adventure.
