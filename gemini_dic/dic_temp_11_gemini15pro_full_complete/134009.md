nonhospitals:/ˌnɒnˈhɒspɪtəlz/ | n. | 非医院机构 | Facilities that provide healthcare services outside of a traditional hospital setting.  These can include clinics, nursing homes, rehabilitation centers, and other similar facilities. | Many patients now receive care in nonhospitals due to lower costs and increased convenience. 许多病人现在在非医院机构接受治疗，因为成本更低，也更方便。| synonyms: outpatient facilities, community health centers, ambulatory care centers | antonyms: hospitals | noun

nonhospital:/ˌnɒnˈhɒspɪtəl/ | adj. | 非医院的 | Relating to or involving healthcare services provided outside of a traditional hospital setting. | Nonhospital care is becoming increasingly common. 非医院护理正变得越来越普遍。| synonyms: outpatient, community-based, ambulatory | antonyms: hospital, inpatient | adjective
