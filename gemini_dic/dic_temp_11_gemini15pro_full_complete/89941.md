horseless:/ˈhɔːslɪs/| adj.|无马的，不用马拉的|Without a horse; not drawn by a horse.  It describes something that operates or functions without the use of horses, particularly vehicles.|The horseless carriage was a novelty at the time. 那时，无马马车还是个新鲜事物。|近义词：automobile, car, motor-driven|反义词: horse-drawn|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From horse + -less.  The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking".
