overcuriosities:/ˌoʊvərˌkjʊəriˈɑːsətiz/
|n.|过度好奇; 过分好奇; 过度求知欲|An excessive or inappropriate interest in things, especially those considered private or taboo.  A tendency to pry into other people's affairs.  A preoccupation with obscure or trivial details.|Her overcuriosities led her to snoop through her neighbor's mail. 她的过度好奇让她偷看了邻居的邮件。|Near Synonyms: nosiness, inquisitiveness, prying, meddling, intrusiveness|Near Antonyms: indifference, apathy, detachment|noun
