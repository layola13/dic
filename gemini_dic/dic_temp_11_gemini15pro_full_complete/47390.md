deadener:/ˈdɛdnər/ (US) /ˈdɛdnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|减震器，消音器，抑制剂|Something that deadens or reduces the intensity or vibrancy of something else.  It can refer to a substance that absorbs sound or vibration, or something that reduces the intensity of a feeling or experience.|The thick carpet acted as a deadener for the footsteps upstairs. 厚厚的地毯减轻了楼上脚步声。|Synonyms: muffler, damper, suppressor, cushion, mute|Antonyms: amplifier, intensifier|noun

deadener:/ˈdɛdnər/ (US) /ˈdɛdnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|沉闷的人或事|Something or someone that makes things less lively or interesting.|The long meeting was a real deadener.  漫长的会议真是令人沉闷。|Synonyms: bore, drag|Antonyms: stimulant, energizer|noun
