mediator:/ˈmiːdieɪtər/| n.|调解员，调停人，仲裁者|A person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement; a go-between.|The mediator helped the two sides reach a compromise.  调解员帮助双方达成了妥协。|Synonyms: arbitrator, negotiator, conciliator, intermediary|Antonyms: agitator, instigator|noun
