nonfundamentalists:/ˌnɒnˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪsts/ | n. | 非原教旨主义者 | People who do not adhere to fundamentalist beliefs, especially in religion. |  Many nonfundamentalists find the literal interpretation of religious texts to be problematic. 许多非原教旨主义者认为宗教文本的字面解释是有问题的。 |  Nonfundamentalists often embrace more modern or liberal interpretations of scripture. 非原教旨主义者通常接受更现代或自由的经文解释。 | Synonyms: moderates, liberals, reformists | Antonyms: fundamentalists, traditionalists | Noun
