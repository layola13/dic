dispensationalism:/ˌdɪspɛnˈseɪʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
时代论
A theological system that divides history into distinct periods (dispensations) each marked by a different way God deals with humanity.
Dispensationalism is a popular belief system among some evangelical Christians.  时代论在一些福音派基督徒中是一个流行的信仰体系。
Dispensationalism emphasizes a literal interpretation of biblical prophecy. 时代论强调对圣经预言的字面解释。
synonym: covenant theology (partially), premillennialism (often)
antonym: amillennialism, postmillennialism
noun
