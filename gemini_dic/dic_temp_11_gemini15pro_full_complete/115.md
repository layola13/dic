abaist:/əˈbeɪst/
adjective
abashed; ashamed
Feeling or caused to feel uneasy and self-conscious.
例句：He felt abaist after realizing his mistake. 他意识到自己的错误后感到羞愧。

例句：She was abaist at being caught in a lie. 她因为撒谎被抓而感到难堪。

近义词：ashamed, embarrassed, humiliated, mortified
反义词：proud, confident, unashamed
词性：adjective


Etymology:
From Middle English *abaist, abashed, ashamed, from Old French *abaissier, to lower, abase, from a- (from Latin ad-, to) + baissier, to lower, from Vulgar Latin *bassiare, from Late Latin bassus, low.  The form abaist is archaic and rarely used.
