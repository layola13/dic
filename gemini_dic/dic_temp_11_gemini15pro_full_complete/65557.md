eutrophic:/juːˈtrɒfɪk/ |adj.|富营养化的|Characterized by excessive richness in nutrients, often leading to algal blooms and oxygen depletion in aquatic ecosystems.|The lake became eutrophic due to fertilizer runoff. 这个湖由于肥料径流而变得富营养化。|synonyms: over-fertilized, nutrient-rich|antonyms: oligotrophic|adjective

eutrophic:/juːˈtrɒfɪk/ |adj.|过度肥沃的|Characterized by excessive nutrients, typically in soil, leading to rapid plant growth.|The eutrophic soil produced a bumper crop. 肥沃的土壤带来了丰收。|synonyms: fertile, rich|antonyms: infertile, poor|adjective
