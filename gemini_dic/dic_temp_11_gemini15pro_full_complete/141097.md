onshore:/ˈɑːnʃɔːr/ /ˈɒnʃɔːr/
adjective
陆上的；近岸的；向岸的
Located or occurring on land; returning or coming to land from the water.
The onshore breeze cooled us down. 陆上的微风给我们降了温。

onshore:/ˈɑːnʃɔːr/ /ˈɒnʃɔːr/
adverb
在岸上；向岸上
Towards or onto the land from the water.
The wind blew onshore. 风吹向岸边。

Etymology:
From on + shore.  "Shore" comes from Old English "scora," meaning "shore, coast."


近义词:landward, coastal
反义词:offshore
词性:adjective, adverb
