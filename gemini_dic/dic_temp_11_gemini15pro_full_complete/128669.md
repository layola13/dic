nicotianine:/nɪˌkoʊʃəˈnaɪn/| n. |烟碱胺|An alkaloid found in tobacco plants, related to nicotine but less toxic. It is involved in the plant's defense against herbivores and pathogens.  It chelates metals, particularly nickel. |Nicotianine plays a role in nickel homeostasis in plants. 烟碱胺在植物体内镍的稳态中起作用。

Nicotianine levels are elevated in response to pathogen attack. 烟碱胺水平在应对病原体攻击时会升高。|近义词: None (highly specific compound)|反义词: None|词性:noun
