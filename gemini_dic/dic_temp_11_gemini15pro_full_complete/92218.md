hypertexts:/ˈhaɪpɚˌtɛksts/| n. |超文本，超链接文本|A system of interconnected documents accessed through hyperlinks.|Example: The internet is a vast network of hypertexts. 互联网是一个巨大的超文本网络.|Example:  Clicking on hypertexts allows you to jump to different parts of the document or to other related documents. 点击超文本允许你跳转到文档的不同部分或其他相关文档。|近义词：hypermedia|反义词: linear text|词性:noun
