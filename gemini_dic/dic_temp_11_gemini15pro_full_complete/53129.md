diploidal:/dɪˈplɔɪdəl/
adjective
二倍体的
Having two sets of chromosomes, one from each parent.
例句：Humans are diploid organisms.  人类是二倍体生物。

例句：The diploid number of chromosomes in humans is 46.  人类的二倍体染色体数是46。
近义词：euploid (specifically having the normal diploid number)
反义词：haploid, polyploid
词性：adjective
