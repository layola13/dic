enhedge:/ɪnˈhɛdʒ/| vt.|限制，约束，围住；|To enclose or surround with a hedge; to confine or restrict. |例句：The property is enhedged by a tall privet hedge. 这处房产被高大的水蜡树篱笆围了起来。|例句：Regulations enhedge the power of the president. 法规限制了总统的权力。|近义词：confine, restrict, limit, enclose, surround|反义词：liberate, free, release|动词
