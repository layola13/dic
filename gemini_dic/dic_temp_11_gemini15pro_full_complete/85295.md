harmony:/ˈhɑːrməni/ (US) /ˈhɑːməni/ (UK)| n.|和谐，协调，融洽|A pleasing arrangement of parts; agreement or concord.|The harmony of the colors in the painting was striking. 这幅画中色彩的和谐引人注目。|synonyms: agreement, concord, unity, accord, consistency, consonance, compatibility|antonyms: discord, conflict, disharmony, dissonance, friction, strife|noun

harmony:/ˈhɑːrməni/ (US) /ˈhɑːməni/ (UK)| n.|和声，和音|A combination of musical notes to form chords and chord progressions having a pleasing effect.|The barbershop quartet sang in perfect harmony.  这个理发店四重唱组的和声完美无瑕。|synonyms: consonance, euphony|antonyms: dissonance, cacophony|noun
