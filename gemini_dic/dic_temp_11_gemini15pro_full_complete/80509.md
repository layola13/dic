good-naturedness:/ˌɡʊdˈneɪtʃərdnəs/| n. |和蔼；温厚；性情好|The quality of being kind, friendly, and easygoing.  Having or showing a pleasant and tolerant disposition.| Her good-naturedness made her popular with everyone. 她的和蔼可亲使她受 everyone 欢迎。|Synonyms: amiability, affability, geniality, kindness, pleasantness|Antonyms: ill-naturedness, bad temper, grumpiness, churlishness|noun
