offloading:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪŋ/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdɪŋ/
n.
卸载，卸货，减负
the process of removing something from a vehicle, ship, etc.  or reducing a burden
例句：The offloading of the cargo took several hours.  卸货花了几个小时。

例句：The company is offloading some of its non-core businesses. 公司正在剥离一些非核心业务。
synonym: unloading, discharging, transferring
antonym: loading, charging
noun


offload:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/
v.
卸载，卸货，减负，转嫁（责任等）
to remove something from a vehicle, ship, etc., or to get rid of something unwanted, or to give some of your work, duties, or responsibilities to someone else
例句：They offloaded the grain from the ship. 他们从船上卸下了谷物。

例句：He offloaded his old car onto his brother. 他把他的旧车转给了他弟弟。
synonym: unload, discharge, transfer, dump
antonym: load, charge
verb
