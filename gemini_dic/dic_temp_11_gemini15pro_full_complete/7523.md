annelidous:/əˈnɛlɪdəs/
adjective
环节动物的; 属于环节动物门的
Relating to or belonging to the Annelida phylum, a large phylum of segmented worms, including earthworms, leeches, and marine bristle worms.
Earthworms are annelidous creatures that play a vital role in soil health. 蚯蚓是环节动物，对土壤健康起着至关重要的作用。
The scientist studied the annelidous nervous system.  这位科学家研究了环节动物的神经系统。

近义词:segmented, vermiform
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider non-segmented)
词性: adjective
