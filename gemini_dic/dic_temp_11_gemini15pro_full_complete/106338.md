kohlrabi:/ˈkoʊlˌrɑːbi/ /ˌkoʊlˈrɑːbi/| n. |苤蓝，球茎甘蓝|A biennial plant (Brassica oleracea Gongylodes Group) of the mustard family, closely related to the cabbage and grown for its enlarged, edible stem.|Kohlrabi is a delicious addition to salads. 苤蓝是沙拉中美味的添加物。|Synonyms: German turnip, turnip cabbage|Antonyms: None|noun

kohlrabi:|/ˈkoʊlˌrɑːbi/ /ˌkoʊlˈrɑːbi/|n.|苤蓝，球茎甘蓝|A biennial plant (Brassica oleracea Gongylodes Group) of the mustard family, whose edible stem base swells into a bulbous shape.|We added kohlrabi to the stir-fry. 我们在炒菜里加了苤蓝。|Synonyms: German turnip, turnip cabbage|Antonyms: None|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German Kohlrabi, from Kohl (cabbage) + Rübe (turnip). Ultimately from Latin caulis (stem, stalk) and Latin rapa (turnip).
