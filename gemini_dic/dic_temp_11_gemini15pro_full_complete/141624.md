optician:/ɒpˈtɪʃən/ /ɑːpˈtɪʃən/
n.
眼镜商；配镜师
A person who makes and sells eyeglasses.
例句：I went to the optician to get my eyes tested. 我去眼镜店检查视力。

例句：The optician adjusted the frames of my glasses. 配镜师调整了我的眼镜框。

近义词：eye doctor, optometrist
反义词: None

词性: Noun
