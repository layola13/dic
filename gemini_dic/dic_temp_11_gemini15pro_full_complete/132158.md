noncuriously:/ˌnɑːnˈkjʊəriəsli/
adv. |不带好奇地，漠不关心地|Without curiosity; indifferently. |He noncuriously accepted the news. 他漠然地接受了这个消息。|Synonyms: indifferently, apathetically, listlessly |Antonyms: curiously, inquisitively, eagerly
词性: 副词


词源:
non- (prefix) + curiously (adverb)
"non-"  表示“不，非”
"curiously" 源自拉丁词 "curiosus"，意为“careful, inquisitive”（仔细的，好奇的）。
