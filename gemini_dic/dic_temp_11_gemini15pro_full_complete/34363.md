classicalness: /ˈklæsɪkəlnəs/
n.
古典性；经典性；正统性
The quality of being classical, conforming to ancient Greek or Roman models in literature or art, or to later systems modeled upon them.
The building's classicalness is evident in its symmetrical design and Doric columns.  这座建筑的古典性体现在其对称的设计和多立克柱上。
Her playing has a remarkable classicalness and purity of style. 她的演奏有一种非凡的古典性和纯粹的风格。
synonyms: classicism, purity, formality, traditionalism
antonyms: modernity, informality, avant-garde
noun
