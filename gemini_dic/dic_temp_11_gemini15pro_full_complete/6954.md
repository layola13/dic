andarac:/ˌændəˈræk/ | n. | 晾衣架，晒衣架，烘衣架 | A framework for drying clothes, fish, etc. |  Example: The fisherman hung his nets on the and arac to dry. 渔夫把渔网挂在晾衣架上晾干。

Example:  She placed the laundry on the and arac to air out. 她把洗好的衣服放在晾衣架上晾干。 | Synonym: clothes horse, drying rack, clothes dryer | Antonym: N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Romanian *andărăc, from Turkish *andarık, possibly from Byzantine Greek *ανταράκιον (antarákion), diminutive of *αντάραξ (antárax). Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἀνθέριξ (anthérix, “prop, support”).
