outswindled:/ˌaʊtˈswɪnd(ə)ld/
verb
中文翻译：骗取，诈骗（比outswindle更厉害）
英文解析：Past participle of outswindle, meaning to cheat or defraud someone of something, especially money, by using clever but dishonest methods.  Implies surpassing or exceeding someone in swindling.
例句：The con artist outswindled the elderly couple out of their life savings. 骗子骗走了这对老夫妇的毕生积蓄。

例句：He was so cunning, he even outswindled the notorious gambler. 他太狡猾了，甚至连臭名昭著的赌徒都被他骗了。

近义词：defrauded, cheated, conned, tricked, duped
反义词： compensated, repaid, reimbursed
词性：Verb (past participle)
