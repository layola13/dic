fassaite:/ˈfæsəˌaɪt/| n.|透辉石|A dark green to black calcium-rich pyroxene mineral, Ca(Mg,Fe,Al)(Si,Al)2O6, found in metamorphic rocks.|Fassaite is a common mineral in many metamorphic rocks. 透辉石是许多变质岩中的常见矿物。|The geologist identified the green crystal as fassaite. 地质学家将绿色晶体鉴定为透辉石。|Synonyms: pyroxene, augite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
