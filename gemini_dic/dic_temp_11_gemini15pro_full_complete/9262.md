antipatriotism: /ˌæntiˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/ | n. | 反爱国主义，不爱国 | Lack of patriotism; opposition to patriotism.  The sentiment of opposing one's own country, especially in a time of war. |  His antipatriotism was evident in his refusal to salute the flag.  他的反爱国主义体现在他拒绝向国旗敬礼。

Antipatriotism during wartime can be dangerous.  战时的反爱国主义可能是危险的。 | 近义词: disloyalty, sedition, treason | 反义词: patriotism, loyalty, nationalism | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From anti- + patriotism.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from the Greek "anti." "Patriotism" comes from the French "patriotisme," ultimately from the Greek "patriōtēs" meaning "fellow countryman."
