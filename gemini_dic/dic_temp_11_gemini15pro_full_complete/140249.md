offs:/ɒfs/ | n. | 休息；休假；关闭；优势；开始 | a period of time when you are not working or studying; the act of turning off a piece of equipment; an advantage; the start of a race or other event |  Example: He took a few days off. 他休息了几天。| Synonyms: holiday, vacation, break, rest, advantage, start | Antonyms: on, working, disadvantage, end | noun

offs:/ɒfs/ | adv. | 离开；偏离；停止；中断；取消 | away from a place or position; not operating or connected; cancelled or discontinued | Example: The meeting's off. 会议取消了。| Synonyms: away, gone, disconnected, cancelled | Antonyms: on, connected, continued | adverb

offs:/ɒfs/ | prep. | 从…离开；偏离 | away from a place or position | Example: He fell off the ladder. 他从梯子上摔了下来。| Synonyms: from, away from | Antonyms: on, onto | preposition
