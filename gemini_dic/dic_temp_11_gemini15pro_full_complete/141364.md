operationally:/ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənəli/ /ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənəli/| adv.|操作上地；运营上地；实际上|Relating to the way in which something works or is performed.  It describes something in terms of its practical function or application, rather than its theoretical or ideal form.|The system is operationally sound, but it needs some cosmetic improvements.  该系统在操作上是完善的，但需要一些外观上的改进。|practically, functionally, in practice, effectively|theoretically, hypothetically|adverb
