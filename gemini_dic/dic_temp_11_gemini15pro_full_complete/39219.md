conglomeration:/kənˌɡlɒməˈreɪʃn/ (British) /kənˌɡlɑːməˈreɪʃn/ (American)| n. |聚集，混合物，企业集团|A collection of different things gathered together, especially businesses or organizations. |The company is a huge conglomerate with businesses in many different sectors. 这家公司是一个庞大的企业集团，业务遍及许多不同领域。|Synonyms:  amalgamation, aggregation, combination, cluster, mix, composite|Antonyms: separation, division, disaggregation|Noun

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (British) /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (American)| n. |砾岩|A rock consisting of rounded fragments cemented together.|The cliff face was composed of a conglomerate of various stones.  悬崖面由各种石头组成的砾岩构成。|Synonyms: composite, aggregate|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (British) /kənˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (American)| v. |聚集，凝聚|To gather together into a mass or coherent whole.|Several small businesses conglomerated to form a larger corporation. 几家小企业合并成一家更大的公司。|Synonyms:  amalgamate, merge, combine, consolidate|Antonyms: separate, divide, disperse|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin conglomeratus, past participle of conglomerare ("to roll together, heap up"), from con- ("together") + glomerare ("to wind into a ball"), from glomus ("ball").
