forswear:/fɔːrˈswɛːr/
verb
放弃；发誓戒除；作伪证
renounce or give up (something valued or pleasant); agree to give up or do without (something); swear falsely; commit perjury
He forswore all indulgence in alcohol. 他发誓戒酒。
She forswore her allegiance to the king. 她放弃了对国王的效忠。
synonym: renounce, abandon, reject, repudiate, disavow
antonym: affirm, uphold, maintain, embrace, adopt
词性: 动词

forswear:/fɔːrˈswɛːr/
verb
发誓不做；弃绝
To formally reject or disavow a formerly held belief, usually under oath.
The knight forswore his former life of violence and pledged to protect the innocent.  这位骑士发誓放弃以前的暴力生活，并承诺保护无辜者。
He forswore smoking after his doctor's warning.  在医生的警告后，他发誓戒烟。
synonym: renounce, abjure, recant, repudiate
antonym: affirm, uphold, maintain, embrace
词性: 动词
