overhumanized:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪzd/
adjective
过度人性化的
Having been given or attributed excessive human qualities, often to the point of being unrealistic or sentimental.  The portrayal of animals in children's books is often overhumanized.
The author's portrayal of the robot was criticized for being overhumanized.  作者对机器人的刻画被批评为过度人性化。
anthropomorphic, humanized
dehumanized, impersonal
adjective
