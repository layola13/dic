derrick:/ˈderɪk/| n.|起重机，井架|A derrick is a large machine used for lifting heavy objects, especially on ships or in oil fields.|The derrick lifted the heavy pipes into place. 起重机将沉重的管道吊到位。|The oil derrick stood tall against the desert sky.  石油井架高高耸立在沙漠的天空下。|近义词：crane, hoist|反义词:|noun
