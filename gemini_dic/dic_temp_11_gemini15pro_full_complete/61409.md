embodiers:/ɪmˈbɒdiərz/ /ɪmˈbɑːdiərz/| n.| 体现者，化身 |People who embody certain qualities or values are seen as living examples of them.  那些体现某些品质或价值观的人被视为它们的活生生的例子。| The dancers were embodiers of grace and elegance.  这些舞者是优雅和典雅的化身。|近义词：representatives, incarnations, epitomes|反义词: N/A|词性: noun (plural)

embody:/ɪmˈbɒdi/ /ɪmˈbɑːdi/| vt.| 体现，包含，使具体化 |To embody something means to represent it in a clear and obvious way, to be a very good example of it, or to include it.  体现某事物意味着以清晰明了的方式表现它，成为它的一个很好的例子，或者包含它。|  This painting embodies the spirit of the Renaissance. 这幅画体现了文艺复兴的精神。| 近义词: represent, personify, incorporate | 反义词: exclude, omit | 词性: verb


词源:
From Old French *embodier, from en- (“in”) + bodī (“body”), from Late Latin *bodī (“body”)
