noncommodity:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmɑːdəti/ |n.| 非商品，非标准化商品 |Something that is not a commodity; a good or service whose characteristics are significantly differentiated from other goods or services of a similar type, allowing it to command a price premium. |例句：Specialized software is a noncommodity, as its features and functionality vary widely. 专业软件是非商品，因为它的特性和功能差异很大。

例句：Consulting services are considered a noncommodity due to the personalized expertise involved. 咨询服务由于涉及个性化的专业知识，被认为是非商品。|近义词：specialty, differentiated good, service |反义词：commodity, standardized product |词性：名词
