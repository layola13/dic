fleecelike:/fliːslaɪk/
adjective
像羊毛的；似羊毛的
Resembling fleece; soft and woolly.
The blanket had a fleecelike texture. 这条毯子有像羊毛一样的质感。
The caterpillar was covered in fleecelike hair.  这只毛毛虫身上覆盖着像羊毛一样的毛。
近义词: woolly, fluffy, soft
反义词: coarse, rough, prickly
词性: adjective

词源:  From fleece + -like.  "Fleece" comes from Old English *flys (akin to Old High German flīz "fleece") from Proto-Germanic *fleusaz, from Proto-Indo-European *plew- "to flow, float, swim."  The suffix "-like" is of Old English origin.
