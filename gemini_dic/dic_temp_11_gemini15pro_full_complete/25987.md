bursiculate: /bɜːˈsɪkjʊleɪt/ | adj. |  囊状的；小囊的；有小囊的 | Having or resembling small pouches or burs. | The bursiculate surface of the plant's leaves trapped morning dew.  这种植物叶子的囊状表面会留住晨露。 |  pouch-like, saccate | smooth, flat | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *bursicula*, diminutive of *bursa* (purse, pouch).
