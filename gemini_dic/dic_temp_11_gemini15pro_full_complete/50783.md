descanting:/dɪˈskæntɪŋ/ | v. | 详述，评论；演唱 | speaking or writing at length on a subject; singing a melody with ornamentation | He was descanting on the merits of organic farming. 他正在详细阐述有机农业的优点。 | The choir was descanting beautifully on the hymn.  唱诗班正优美地演唱赞美诗。 | 近义词：elaborating, expounding, commenting, discoursing | 反义词：summarizing, condensing | 词性：动词

descant:/ˈdɛskænt/ | n. | 详述，评论；演唱；歌曲的附加旋律 | a detailed discussion or commentary on a particular subject; a melody or counterpoint sung or played above a basic melody | His descant on the current political situation was insightful. 他对当前政治局势的评论很有见地。 | The soprano's descant added a beautiful layer to the song. 女高音的附加旋律为歌曲增添了优美的层次。 | 近义词：commentary, discussion, exposition, discourse | 反义词：summary, précis | 词性：名词


Etymology:
From Old French "deschanter" (to sing, chant), from Latin "discantare" (to sing apart, sing an accompaniment), from "dis-" (apart) + "cantare" (to sing).
