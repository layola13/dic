autopoiesis:/ˌɔːtəʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs/ /ˌɑːtəʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs/| n.| 自创生，自我生成，自体生成| A system capable of reproducing and maintaining itself.  A system that is organized as a network of processes that produce its own components and thus continuously regenerate and realize the network of processes that produced them.|  Example:  The theory of autopoiesis describes how living organisms maintain themselves through self-production. 自创生理论描述了生物体如何通过自我生产来维持自身。

Example:  The concept of autopoiesis has been applied to social systems as well as biological ones. 自创生的概念不仅适用于生物系统，也适用于社会系统。|近义词：self-production, self-maintenance, self-organization|反义词:allopoiesis|词性:noun
