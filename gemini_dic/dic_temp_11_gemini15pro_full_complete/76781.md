gamier:/ˈɡeɪmiər/| adj.|更刺激的，更冒险的，更有风险的（通常指活动或情况）; (指肉类)味道更浓的，更野味的|Having a strong, pungent flavor or smell, as of game; more risky or adventurous.|The gamier the investment, the higher the potential return.  投资风险越大，潜在回报就越高。|Synonyms: riskier, more adventurous, more pungent, stronger (flavor)|Antonyms: blander, milder, safer, less risky|adjective


gamier:/ˈɡeɪmiər/| adj.|更低俗的，更粗俗的（指笑话、故事等）|More vulgar or risqué (referring to jokes, stories, etc.).|His jokes were getting gamier as the night went on. 随着夜幕降临，他的笑话变得越来越低俗。|Synonyms: racier, bawdier, more suggestive|Antonyms: cleaner, more proper, more decent|adjective
