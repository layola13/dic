cry:/kraɪ/
n.|哭泣，叫喊，呼喊|The baby's cry woke me up. 婴儿的哭声把我吵醒了。
cry:/kraɪ/
v.|哭，喊叫，叫卖|She cried when she heard the bad news. 她听到坏消息后哭了。


Etymology: From Middle English *crien*, from Old French *crier*, from Latin *quīrītāre* (to shriek, scream), frequentative of *querī* (to complain, lament).

Synonyms: weep, sob, wail, yell, shout, scream
Antonyms: laugh, giggle, chuckle, rejoice
