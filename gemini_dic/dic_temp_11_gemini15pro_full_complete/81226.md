ETYMOLOGY->

grandparent + -hood

grandparent (n.): from grand- + parent

grand- (prefix): from Old French grand, from Latin grandis "great"

parent (n.): from Old French parent, from Latin parentem (nominative parens), present participle of parere "to bring forth, give birth to"

-hood (suffix): from Old English -hād, denoting "state, condition, quality"
