babur:/ˈbɑːbər/| n.|巴布尔,  巴卑尔|Founder of the Mughal Empire in India. Babur was a descendant of Timur (Tamerlane) and Genghis Khan.  He is considered a great military leader and poet.|Babur wrote a detailed memoir of his life and campaigns. 巴布尔写了一本详细的回忆录，记录了他的生活和战役。|Synonym:  Mughal emperor, Zahir-ud-din Muhammad|Antonym: N/A|noun
Etymology->
From Persian بابر (bābur), meaning "tiger" or "lion."|
