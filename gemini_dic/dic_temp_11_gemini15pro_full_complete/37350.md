commendatory:/kəˈmɛndətɔːri/ | adj. | 称赞的，赞赏的 | expressing or conveying commendation; praising |  The teacher wrote a commendatory note on the student's essay. 老师在学生的作文上写了一段赞扬性的评语。 |  The speaker gave a commendatory speech about the organization's work. 演讲者就该组织的工作发表了赞扬性讲话。 | 近义词:laudatory, complimentary, approving, flattering | 反义词:critical, derogatory, disapproving | 词性:形容词

commendable:/kəˈmɛndəbl/ | adj. | 值得称赞的 | deserving commendation; praiseworthy | Her dedication to her work is highly commendable. 她对工作的奉献精神非常值得称赞。|  His bravery in the face of danger was commendable.  他在危险面前的勇敢值得称赞。| 近义词:praiseworthy, laudable, admirable, meritorious | 反义词:reprehensible, blameworthy, deplorable | 词性:形容词 (Note: This is a related word, not a different form of "commendatory")

commendation:/ˌkɒmenˈdeɪʃn/ | n. |  称赞，赞扬 | the act of commending; praise |  He received a commendation for his bravery. 他因勇敢而受到嘉奖。 | The project earned the commendation of the city council.  该项目赢得了市议会的赞扬。| 近义词:praise, approval, acclaim, applause | 反义词:criticism, condemnation, disapproval | 词性:名词

commend:/kəˈmɛnd/ | v. |  称赞，推荐 | praise formally or officially; present as suitable for approval or acceptance; entrust |  I commend you for your hard work. 我赞扬你的辛勤工作。 | I commend this book to anyone interested in history. 我向任何对历史感兴趣的人推荐这本书。 | 近义词:praise, applaud, endorse, recommend | 反义词:criticize, condemn, denounce | 词性:动词
