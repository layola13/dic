foot:/fʊt/ (US), /fʊt/ (UK)| n. | 脚；英尺；底部；音步 | The part of the leg below the ankle. A unit of length equal to 12 inches (30.48 centimeters). The lowest part; the base.  A metrical unit in poetry. | She hurt her foot playing soccer. 她踢足球时伤了脚。The table is three feet tall. 这张桌子有三英尺高。| synonyms: base, bottom, extremity | antonyms: head, top | noun

foot:/fʊt/ (US), /fʊt/ (UK)| v. | 支付；步行；跳舞；合计 | To pay a bill. To go on foot; walk.  To dance. To add up; total. |  We footed the bill for dinner. 我们付了晚餐的账单。They footed it back home. 他们步行回家。 | synonyms: pay, walk, dance, sum | antonyms:  | verb
