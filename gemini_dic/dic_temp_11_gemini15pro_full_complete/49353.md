delinquency:/dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi/| n.| 违法行为；拖欠；失职|A failure to do something that you legally have to do, especially by not paying a debt; behaviour, especially by young people, that is illegal or not acceptable. |例句：Juvenile delinquency is a serious problem in many urban areas.  青少年犯罪是许多城区的一个严重问题。

例句：The bank will charge a fee for any delinquency in payment.  任何拖欠付款，银行都会收取费用。|近义词：misbehavior, misconduct, offense, wrongdoing, default, negligence |反义词: obedience, compliance, responsibility|词性:noun
