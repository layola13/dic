bickered: /ˈbɪkərd/ | v. | 争吵，拌嘴 | Argued about petty and trivial matters. |  They bickered over who should do the dishes. 他们为了谁洗碗而拌嘴。

The children bickered constantly about whose turn it was to play the video game. 孩子们总是为了轮到谁玩电子游戏而争吵。 | 近义词: argued, squabbled, quarreled | 反义词: agreed, harmonized | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bikeren*, frequentative of *biken* (“to peck, stab”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to  Old Norse *pikka* (“to pick, prick”). The sense of “to quarrel” developed from the image of birds pecking at each other.
