imperatory:/ɪmˈpɛrətɔːri/ /ɪmˈpɛrətəri/
adjective
命令式的；必须服从的；专横的
Expressing a command or order; peremptory.
The teacher spoke in an imperatory tone. 老师用命令的语气说话。

The general gave an imperatory command to his troops. 将军向他的部队发出了必须服从的命令。
authoritative, commanding, dictatorial, peremptory
beseeching, requesting, submissive, pleading
词性: adjective
