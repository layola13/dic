binaries:/ˈbaɪnəriːz/
n.|二进制文件，二进制代码，二元对立|A system of numerical notation that uses only two digits, 0 and 1, to represent all numbers.  Also refers to files containing non-textual data, often executable programs or other non-human-readable data. Can also refer to a concept of two opposing elements.|The program is distributed as a set of binaries.  该程序以一组二进制文件的形式分发。|Synonyms: executable, binary file, machine code|Antonyms: source code, text file|noun


binaries:/ˈbaɪnəriːz/
n.|双星，双子星|Two stars that are gravitationally bound together and orbit a common center of mass.|Astronomers observed the binaries through the telescope. 天文学家通过望远镜观察了双星。|Synonyms: double star|Antonyms: single star|noun
