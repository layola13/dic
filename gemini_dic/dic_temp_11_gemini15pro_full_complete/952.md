academicals: /ˌækəˈdɛmɪkəlz/ | n. | 学者；大学教师；学究式人物 |  People associated with academia, especially scholars and teachers at a university.  It can sometimes have a slightly negative connotation, suggesting a person who is overly theoretical and out of touch with the practical world. | The academicals debated the merits of the new theory for hours.  学者们就新理论的价值争论了几个小时。| Synonyms: scholars, academics, intellectuals | Antonyms: practitioners, pragmatists | Noun
