conicoid:/ˈkɒnɪkɔɪd/ /ˈkɑːnɪkɔɪd/
n.
二次曲面；锥面体
a surface or solid generated by the rotation of a conic section around one of its axes
The equation represents a conicoid. 这个方程式代表一个二次曲面。

近义词: quadric, quadric surface
反义词: (无明显的反义词)
词性: noun
