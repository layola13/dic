gelidities: /dʒɛˈlɪdɪtiz/
n.
凝胶状; 胶凝性
The state or quality of being like a gel or jelly.
The gelidities of the substance made it difficult to handle.  这种物质的胶凝性使它难以处理。
The scientist studied the gelidities of various polymers. 科学家研究了各种聚合物的胶凝性。
近义词：gelatinousness, viscosity, jelly-like consistency
反义词：fluidity, liquidity
词性：名词
