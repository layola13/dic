cotangent:/ˌkoʊˈtænʤənt/| n.|余切|A trigonometric function that is equal to the ratio of the adjacent side to the opposite side of an angle in a right triangle.  It is also the reciprocal of the tangent. |The cotangent of 30 degrees is √3. 30度的余切是√3。

The cotangent of angle A is the length of side b divided by the length of side a.  A角的余切是b边的长度除以a边的长度。|近义词:None (as a specific trigonometric function)|反义词:None (as a specific trigonometric function)|词性:noun
