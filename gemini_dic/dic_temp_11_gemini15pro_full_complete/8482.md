ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + authoritarianism.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Authoritarianism" comes from "authority" (from Old French "auctorité," ultimately from Latin "auctoritas") + "-ian" + "-ism."
