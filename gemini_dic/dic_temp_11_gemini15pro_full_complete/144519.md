outtyrannize: /ˌaʊtˈtɪrənaɪz/
verb
压制；专横对待；比...更专横
to tyrannize more than another tyrant; to exceed in tyranny.
The dictator outtyrannized his predecessors, imposing even stricter curfews and limitations on free speech.  这位独裁者比他的前任更加专横，实施了更严格的宵禁和言论限制。
He outtyrannized all the other rulers in the region. 他比该地区所有其他统治者都更加专横。
近义词: oppress, domineer, subjugate, crush
反义词: liberate, free, empower
