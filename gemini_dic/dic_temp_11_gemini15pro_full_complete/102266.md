itinerantly:/aɪˈtɪnərəntli/
adverb
巡回地；流动地
in a manner that involves traveling from place to place, usually to work
The preacher traveled itinerantly, spreading his message to different towns.  这个传教士四处游历，向不同的城镇传播他的信息。
The band toured itinerantly for two years, performing in small venues across the country.  乐队巡回演出两年，在全国各地的小型场所演出。
近义词: nomadically, peripatetically, wanderingly
反义词:  sedentarily,  stationarily
词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *itinerant-, from Latin itinerant- (present participle of itinerārī ‘to journey’), from iter, itiner- ‘journey, road’.
