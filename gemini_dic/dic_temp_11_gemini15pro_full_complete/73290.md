forebrace: /ˈfɔːrbreɪs/ | n. |前括号，左花括号 | A character used in pairs to enclose a group of items or a block of text, especially in mathematical expressions or programming code.  Similar to "{". | Example: The forebrace indicates the start of a code block.  前括号表示代码块的开始。

Example: In mathematics, the forebrace is used to denote a set.  在数学中，前括号用来表示集合。 | 近义词: left brace, opening brace, curly bracket | 反义词: brace (when referring to the pair), right brace, closing brace | 词性: noun
