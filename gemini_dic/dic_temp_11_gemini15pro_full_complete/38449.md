concession's:/kənˈseʃənz/| n.|让步，妥协，优惠，特许权|Something granted or yielded, often in response to demands; a thing conceded.|The company made concessions to the workers' demands for higher wages.  公司对工人提出的加薪要求做出了让步。|Synonyms: compromise, allowance, privilege|Antonyms: resistance, refusal|noun

concession's:/kənˈseʃənz/| n.|（商业）特许经营权|A business operating within the premises of another business.|The airport has several fast-food concessions. 机场有几个快餐特许经营店。|Synonyms: franchise, license|Antonyms: prohibition, restriction|noun
