feeding-frenzies:/ˈfiːdɪŋ ˈfrɛnziːz/
|n.|疯狂进食；抢购狂潮|A period of intense and often uncontrolled activity, typically involving a large number of people competing for something.  A surge of excitement and activity, often driven by greed or fear of missing out.|例句：The announcement of the sale sparked a feeding frenzy among shoppers.  宣布打折促销引发了顾客们的抢购狂潮。

|例句：The sharks went into a feeding frenzy when they sensed the blood in the water.  鲨鱼闻到水里的血腥味后陷入了疯狂的进食状态。
|近义词：flurry, scramble, rush, free-for-all|反义词：lull, calm, quiet|词性:名词(plural)
