nourishing:/ˈnɜːrɪʃɪŋ/ /ˈnʌrɪʃɪŋ/| adj.|营养的，滋养的|Providing the substances needed for growth, health, and good condition.|例句：A nourishing meal is essential for good health. 一顿营养丰富的饭菜对健康至关重要。|近义词：nutritious, wholesome, healthy|反义词：unhealthy, detrimental, harmful|词性:形容词

nourish:/ˈnɜːrɪʃ/ /ˈnʌrɪʃ/| vt.|滋养，养育|To provide with the food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition.|例句：The soil nourishes the plants. 土壤滋养植物。|近义词：feed, sustain, nurture|反义词：starve, deprive, neglect|词性:动词
