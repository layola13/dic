overgarrisoned:/ˌoʊvərˈɡærɪsənd/
adjective
驻军过多的，兵力过剩的
Having more troops stationed in a place than are necessary or desirable.
The small island was overgarrisoned, with more soldiers than civilians.  这个小岛驻军过多，士兵比平民还多。
The fort, overgarrisoned and undersupplied, quickly fell to the enemy.  这个要塞驻军过多，补给不足，很快就落入敌人手中。
