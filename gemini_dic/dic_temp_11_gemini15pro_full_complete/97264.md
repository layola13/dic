inflight:/ˈɪnflaɪt/ | adj. | 飞行中的；在飞行期间的 | Happening or provided during a flight. |  Example: Inflight entertainment was excellent.  机上娱乐很精彩。

Example: Inflight meals are often served on long journeys. 长途旅行中通常会提供机上餐食。

近义词: airborne, in-flight | 反义词: grounded | 词性: 形容词

词源: in- (prefix meaning "in, within") + flight (noun)
