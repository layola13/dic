dishearten:/dɪsˈhɑːrtn/ /dɪsˈhɑːrtən/
verb
使沮丧，使灰心
To cause (someone) to lose determination or confidence : to discourage
The team was disheartened by the loss.  这支队伍因失败而感到沮丧。
The coach was disheartened by his team's performance. 教练对他的球队的表现感到失望。

近义词: discourage, deject, demoralize
反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire
词性: verb
