hyperlinks:/ˈhaɪpərˌlɪŋks/ | n. | 超链接 | A link from a hypertext file or document to another location or file, typically activated by clicking on a highlighted word or image on the screen. | Example: This webpage contains many hyperlinks to other relevant websites.  这个网页包含许多指向其他相关网站的超链接。

|Example: Clicking on the hyperlink will take you to a new page. 点击超链接会带你到一个新页面。| Synonym: link, web link, URL | Antonym: N/A | Noun


hyperlink:/ˈhaɪpərˌlɪŋk/ | vt. |  创建超链接 | To create a hyperlink in a document or webpage. | Example:  I hyperlinked the keywords in my article to relevant resources. 我把文章中的关键词超链接到相关的资源。

| Example:  Remember to hyperlink the image to the product page. 记住要把图片超链接到产品页面。 | Synonym: link | Antonym: N/A | Verb
