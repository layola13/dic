impulsion:/ɪmˈpʌlʃən/ | n. |冲动，推动，驱使|The impulsion to create is strong within her. 她内心深处有着强烈的创作冲动。|例句：He felt an impulsion to help the injured man. 他感到一种想帮助那个受伤男人的冲动。

例句：She acted on impulsion, without thinking about the consequences. 她一时冲动就采取了行动，没有考虑后果。

近义词：impulse, urge, drive, motivation |反义词: hesitation, reluctance | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impulsiōn-, from Latin impulsus, past participle of impellere "to push, drive on," from in- "in" + pellere "to push, drive."
