japa:/ˈdʒɑːpə/| n.|念诵，重复祈祷（尤指印度教的）; 离开（尤指为了更好的工作或生活方式）|A mantra or name of a deity repeated silently or aloud as a spiritual discipline, especially in Hinduism. Also, the act of leaving one's native country, often for better work or lifestyle opportunities.|例句：He practiced japa every morning. 他每天早上练习念诵。

例句：The increasing japa among young professionals is a concerning trend. 年轻专业人士中越来越多的离开是一个令人担忧的趋势。|近义词：mantra, repetition, emigration, departure|反义词:arrival, immigration|词性:名词
