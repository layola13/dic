barytes:/ˈbɛəraɪtiːz/ /ˈbɛərˌaɪtiːz/ | n. |重晶石|A heavy white mineral consisting of barium sulfate, used as a source of barium and in some paints and other products.  It is the primary ore of barium.|Barytes is used as a weighting agent in drilling mud. 重晶石在钻井泥浆中用作加重剂。|Synonyms: barite, heavy spar|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

barytum:/ˈbɛərɪtəm/ | n. |钡|An obsolete term for barium, a soft, silvery metallic element.| Barytum was once used in medicine, but it is now considered toxic. 钡曾经用于医学，但现在被认为是有毒的。|Synonyms: barium|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
