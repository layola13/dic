nothingly:/ˈnʌθɪŋli/
adv.
in a manner of no consequence; insignificantly; trivially.
中文翻译：无足轻重地；微不足道地；琐碎地
英文解析：in a way that is unimportant or insignificant.
例句：He dismissed the issue nothingly, as if it were of no importance. 他轻描淡写地驳回了这个问题，仿佛它无关紧要。

例句：She spoke nothingly of her accomplishments, preferring to remain humble. 她轻描淡写地谈论她的成就，宁愿保持谦虚。

近义词：insignificantly, trivially, slightly, negligibly
反义词：significantly, importantly, considerably
词性：副词
