hit-the-fan:/hɪt ðə fæn/|idiomatic expression|糟糕透顶；事情变得一团糟；公开化，爆发|When something unpleasant becomes widely known and causes trouble.|例句：If he doesn't get his way, the shit will hit the fan. 如果他不称心如意，就会天下大乱。

例句：When the news of the scandal hit the fan, the company's stock plummeted. 当丑闻曝光后，该公司的股票暴跌。|近义词：blow up, go south, go pear-shaped, turn sour|反义词：go smoothly, go according to plan|phrase
