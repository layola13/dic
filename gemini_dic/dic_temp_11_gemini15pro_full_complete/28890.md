carpophyte:/ˈkɑːrpəˌfaɪt/| n. |结实植物，果实植物|A plant whose reproductive structures are readily visible, like a fruit.  Specifically refers to seed plants (gymnosperms and angiosperms) and some pteridophytes (ferns) that form large megaspores.|例句：The apple tree is a common carpophyte, producing fleshy fruits. 苹果树是一种常见的结实植物，会结出肉质果实。

例句：Carpophytes, unlike simpler plants, have evolved complex mechanisms for seed dispersal. 与更简单的植物不同，结实植物进化出了复杂的种子传播机制。|近义词：seed plant, fruiting plant|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
