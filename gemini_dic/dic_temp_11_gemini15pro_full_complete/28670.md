carino:/kɑːˈriːnoʊ/ (US), /kəˈriːnəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|可爱的，亲爱的，讨人喜欢的|Sweet, dear, pleasant, nice, cute. Used especially as a term of endearment.|例句：He's such a carino boy! 他真是个可爱的小男孩！|近义词：cute, lovely, sweet, adorable, charming|反义词：unpleasant, nasty, horrible|词性:形容词

carino:/kɑːˈriːnoʊ/ (US), /kəˈriːnəʊ/ (UK)| n.|亲爱的，可爱的人|A dear or loved one (noun usage is less common).|例句：She called him her carino. 她叫他亲爱的。|近义词：dear, sweetheart, darling|反义词：(No direct antonym for a term of endearment)|词性:名词
