moistureless:/ˈmɔɪstʃərlɪs/
adjective
无水分的；干燥的
lacking moisture; dry
The desert air was hot and moistureless. 沙漠的空气炎热干燥。
近义词:arid, dry, parched
反义词:moist, damp, humid
词性:adjective

词源:
From moist + -ure + -less.  Moist ultimately comes from Latin *musteus,  "musty, moldy."  The suffix -ure forms nouns from verbs, and the suffix -less forms adjectives meaning "without."
