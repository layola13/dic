mix up: 弄混，混淆  Don't mix up the files. 不要把文件弄混了。
mix with: 与…交往  She mixes well with her colleagues. 她和同事相处得很好。
mix in:  加入，混合  Mix in the flour gradually. 逐渐加入面粉。
mix it up:  使…多样化  Let's mix it up a bit and try a new restaurant. 让我们换换口味，去一家新餐厅试试。
mix and match:  混合搭配  You can mix and match these clothes to create different outfits. 你可以混合搭配这些衣服来打造不同的造型。
