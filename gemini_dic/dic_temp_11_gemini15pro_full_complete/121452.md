misitemized: /ˌmɪsˈaɪtəmaɪzd/
|v. |错误列出的，错误逐条记载的| to itemize incorrectly, list with errors |The expenses were misitemized, leading to an inaccurate budget.  费用被错误列出，导致预算不准确。|例句：The invoice was misitemized and included charges for services we didn't receive. 发票项目列错了，包含了我们没有接受的服务的费用。| 近义词：miscalculated, misrepresented, incorrectly listed | 反义词：correctly itemized, accurately listed|词性: 动词

misitemized: /ˌmɪsˈaɪtəmaɪzd/
|adj. |错误列出的，错误逐条记载的|incorrectly itemized, listed with errors| The misitemized bill caused confusion and delayed payment. 错误列出的账单引起了混乱并延迟了付款。|例句：The misitemized report was returned to the accounting department for correction. 错误列出的报告被退回会计部门进行更正。| 近义词：inaccurate, erroneous, flawed | 反义词：accurate, correct, flawless|词性: 形容词
