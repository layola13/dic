ediacaran:/ˌiːdiˈækərən/| adj. n.|埃迪卡拉纪的，埃迪卡拉生物群|Relating to or denoting the Ediacaran period, typically about 635 to 541 million years ago. Also refers to the distinctive soft-bodied organisms that lived during this period.|例句：The Ediacaran fossils offer a glimpse into a strange, early world. 埃迪卡拉纪的化石让我们得以一窥一个奇特的早期世界。

例句：The Ediacaran biota represents a crucial stage in the evolution of life. 埃迪卡拉生物群代表了生命演化中的一个关键阶段。|近义词：Precambrian (更广泛的时期), Vendian (另一个名称)|反义词：Cambrian (后一个时期)|词性:形容词，名词
