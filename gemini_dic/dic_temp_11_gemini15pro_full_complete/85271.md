harmonious:/hɑːrˈmoʊniəs/ |/hɑːˈməʊniəs/| adj. |和谐的，协调的，和睦的|  Free from disagreement or dissent; characterized by agreement or concord.  Marked by accord in sentiment or action.  Having components pleasingly or appropriately combined.|The harmonious blend of voices in the choir was beautiful. 合唱团里和谐的嗓音交融在一起，十分优美。| synonyms:  concordant, agreeable, compatible, tuneful, balanced, peaceful, serene | antonyms:  discordant, inharmonious, conflicting, clashing, cacophonous, jarring |词性:形容词
