infiltrate: /ˈɪnfɪltreɪt/ | n. | 渗透物 | Something that infiltrates, especially a substance that permeates something else. | The infiltrate was identified as a toxic chemical.  渗透物被鉴定为一种有毒化学物质。 | synonyms:  permeant,  penetrant | antonyms:  exudate,  effluent | noun
