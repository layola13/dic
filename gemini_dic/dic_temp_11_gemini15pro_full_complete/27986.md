canvaslike:/ˈkænvəsˌlaɪk/
adjective
像帆布的; 粗糙的
Resembling canvas in texture or appearance; coarse.
The painting had a canvaslike texture. 这幅画具有像帆布一样的质感。
Her skin had a canvaslike texture from years of sun exposure.  由于多年的日晒，她的皮肤摸起来像帆布一样粗糙。
Synonyms: coarse, rough, textured, burlaplike
Antonyms: smooth, fine, soft
词性: adjective
