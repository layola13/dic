maturative:/ˈmætʃərətɪv/| adj.|成熟的，促进成熟的|Relating to or causing maturation; developmental.  The process of becoming mature; the emergence of personal and behavioral characteristics through growth processes.|The therapist focused on the maturative process of the adolescent.|近义词:ripening, developing, maturing|反义词:immature, undeveloped|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *maturativus*, from *maturare* "to ripen," from *maturus* "ripe."
