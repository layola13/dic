mandingo:/mænˈdɪŋɡoʊ/
noun|曼丁哥人，曼丁哥语|A member of a West African people inhabiting the region from the Gambia River eastward to Liberia. Also: the language of this people, belonging to the Mande group of the Niger-Congo family.|The Mandingo people have a rich oral tradition. 曼丁哥人有着丰富的口头传统。|Synonyms: Mandinka, Manding|Antonyms: N/A|n.

mandingo:/mænˈdɪŋɡoʊ/
adjective|曼丁哥人的，曼丁哥语的|Relating to the Mandingo people or their language.|Mandingo culture is known for its music and storytelling. 曼丁哥文化以其音乐和讲故事而闻名。|Synonyms: Mandinka, Manding|Antonyms: N/A|adj.
