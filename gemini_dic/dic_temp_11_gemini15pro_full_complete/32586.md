chiromancers:/ˈkaɪrəˌmænsərz/ | n. | 手相家，掌纹学家 | A person who practices chiromancy; a palm reader. |  Many chiromancers claim they can predict the future by reading the lines on your palm. 许多手相家声称他们可以通过解读你手掌上的纹路来预测未来。

Chiromancers were often consulted by royalty in ancient times.  在古代，皇室经常咨询手相家。| 近义词: palm reader, hand reader, fortune teller | 反义词: skeptic | 词性: noun


chiromancer:/ˈkaɪrəˌmænsər/ | n. | 手相家，掌纹学家 | A person who practices chiromancy; a palm reader. | The chiromancer studied my palm intently. 手相家专注地研究我的手掌。

She visited a chiromancer hoping to learn about her future. 她拜访了一位手相家，希望能了解自己的未来。| 近义词: palm reader, hand reader, fortune teller | 反义词: skeptic | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Middle English: chiromauncer, via Old French chiromance, from Greek kheir ‘hand’ + manteia ‘divination’.
