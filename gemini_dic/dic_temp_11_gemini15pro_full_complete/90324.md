housewife:/ˈhaʊsˌwaɪf/ (US) /ˈhaʊswɪf/ (UK)| n. |家庭主妇|A woman whose main occupation is caring for her family and home.|例句：She was a contented housewife and mother. 她是一位满足的家庭主妇和母亲。|例句：Being a housewife is a demanding job. 做家庭主妇是一项要求很高的工作。|近义词： homemaker, stay-at-home mom|反义词: working woman, career woman|词性: noun

词源:  From Old English *hūsƿīf (attested as húsƿifa), from hūs (“house”) + wīf (“woman, wife”).  Cognate with German Hausfrau.
