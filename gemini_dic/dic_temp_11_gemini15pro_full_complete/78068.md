geod:/ˈdʒiːɒd/ | n. |晶洞； geodes(复数)|A hollow rock containing crystals. A rounded, hollow rock that is often lined with crystals.|The geode was cracked open to reveal sparkling amethyst crystals. 晶洞被敲开，露出了闪闪发光的紫水晶晶体。|The children were excited to find a geode on their hike. 孩子们很高兴在徒步旅行中发现了一个晶洞。|Synonyms: druse, vug|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps solid rock)|noun
