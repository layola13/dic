millinery: /ˈmɪlɪnəri/ (US), /ˈmɪlɪnəri/ (UK) | n. | 女帽制造业; 女帽业 |The business or trade of making and selling hats for women. | Her passion for fashion led her to a career in millinery. 她对时尚的热情使她从事了女帽业。| Synonyms: hatmaking, hat trade | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *millenerie, from Old French millener, a dealer in Milan goods (especially silks, ribbons, and hats), from Milan, Italy.
