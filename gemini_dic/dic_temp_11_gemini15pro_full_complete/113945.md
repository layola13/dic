magazines:/ˈmæɡəˌziːnz/
n.
杂志；期刊
Periodical publications containing articles and illustrations, typically covering a particular subject or area of interest.
I subscribe to several fashion magazines. 我订阅了几本时尚杂志。

She writes articles for various magazines. 她为各种杂志撰写文章。

近义词:journals, periodicals, publications
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用单数形式 magazine 的反义词，例如 book 书)

词性:名词(plural)

词源: From French magasin "warehouse, depot, store," from Italian magazzino, from Arabic makhazin, plural of makhzan "storehouse."  The sense evolution was from "storehouse of information" to "periodical publication."
