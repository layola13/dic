moonlighters:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪtərz/
n.
兼职者；从事第二职业的人
People who have a second job in addition to their regular employment, often worked at night.
Many teachers are moonlighters, working extra jobs to supplement their income. 许多教师都兼职，做额外的工作来补充他们的收入。
synonyms: part-timers, second jobbers
antonyms: full-time employees, primary job holders
noun
