interambulacrum:/ˌɪntəræmˈbjʊləkrəm/ | n. |间步带|An area on the surface of a sea urchin or other echinoderm between the ambulacral areas (the ambulacra, which bear tube feet).|The spines on the interambulacrum are often different in shape and size from those on the ambulacra. 间步带上的棘刺通常在形状和大小上与步带上的棘刺不同。|The interambulacrum of this sea urchin species is characterized by large, club-shaped spines.  这种海胆的间步带特征是大而棒状的棘刺。|近义词:interambulacral area|反义词:ambulacrum|词性:noun
