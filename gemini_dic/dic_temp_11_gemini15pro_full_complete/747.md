absol:/ˈæbsɒl/ | n. | 绝对值；绝对事物 |  A value or quantity without regard to positive or negative character; magnitude.  In mathematics, the absolute value or modulus of a real number x, denoted |x|, is the non-negative value of x without regard to its sign. | The absolute value of -5 is 5. |-5的绝对值是5。 | magnitude, value | negativity | noun
