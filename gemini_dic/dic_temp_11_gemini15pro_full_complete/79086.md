glacially:/ˈɡleɪʃəli/ /ˈɡleɪʃəli/| adv.|极其缓慢地，冷冰冰地|Extremely slowly, like a glacier; also, in a cold or unfriendly manner. |The project progressed glacially, taking far longer than anticipated. 这个项目进展极其缓慢，比预期的要长得多。

The lawyer questioned the witness glacially, showing no emotion. 律师冷冰冰地询问证人，没有表现出任何情绪。|近义词：slowly, gradually, coldly, icily|反义词：quickly, rapidly, warmly, cordially|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *glacial* + *-ly*.  *Glacial* comes from French *glacial*, from Latin *glacialis* "icy, frozen," from *glacies* "ice."
