over-sparingly: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛrɪŋli/ | adv. | 过于节俭地，过于吝啬地 |  In a way that is excessively unwilling to spend or use resources. It combines "over," meaning excessively, and "sparingly," meaning using very little. | She used salt over-sparingly, and the food was bland. 她用盐过于吝啬，食物很淡。|  The philanthropist, known for his frugality, donated over-sparingly to the cause.  这位以节俭著称的慈善家，对这项事业的捐赠过于吝啬。 | 近义词: too frugally, excessively thriftily, parsimoniously | 反义词: lavishly, generously, extravagantly | 词性: 副词
