bmi:/ˌbiː ɛmˈaɪ/| n.|身体质量指数|A measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.|Example:  His BMI is within the healthy range. 他的BMI在健康范围内。

Example:  A high BMI can indicate an increased risk of health problems.  高BMI可能表明健康问题的风险增加。|近义词:Quetelet index|反义词: (BMI has no direct antonym)|词性:noun
