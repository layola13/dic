carunculated: /kəˈrʌŋkjʊleɪtɪd/

caruncle: /ˈkærʌŋkl/
| n. | 小肉瘤；肉垂 | A small, fleshy excrescence; a wattle. | The rooster had a bright red caruncle under its beak. 公鸡的喙下有一个鲜红色的肉垂。| Example 2:  Some breeds of dogs are prone to developing caruncles on their skin.  一些品种的狗容易在皮肤上长肉瘤。| 近义词: wattle, papilla, nodule | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "smooth area") | 词性: 名词
