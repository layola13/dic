californication:/kælɪˌfɔːrnɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
加州化；加利福尼亚化；加州梦的堕落
The process of becoming like California, often used negatively to imply a focus on superficiality, hedonism, and materialism.
The band Red Hot Chili Peppers released an album titled "Californication" which explores the darker side of the California dream. 红辣椒乐队发行了一张名为“加州化”的专辑，探索了加州梦的阴暗面。

The rapid development of the area led to a sense of californication among the residents.  该地区的快速发展导致居民中出现了一种加州化的感觉。

近义词: Americanization, commercialization, Hollywoodisation
反义词:  None (This word represents a unique sociocultural phenomenon)
词性: Noun
