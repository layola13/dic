interperceptual:/ˌɪntərpərˈsɛptʃuəl/
adjective
知觉间的，跨知觉的
Relating to or involving the interaction or integration of information from different senses.  It describes the relationship or interaction *between* different perceptual modalities.
The study investigated the interperceptual effects of visual and auditory stimuli. 这项研究调查了视觉和听觉刺激之间的相互作用。
The experiment demonstrated interperceptual bias between tactile and visual information. 实验展示了触觉和视觉信息之间的知觉偏差。
近义词: cross-modal, multisensory
反义词: unimodal
词性: adjective
