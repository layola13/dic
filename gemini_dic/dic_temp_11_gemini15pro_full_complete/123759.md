moorhen:/ˈmʊərhɛn/ | n. | 黑水鸡，骨顶鸡 | A small water bird of the rail family, with dark plumage and a red bill and frontal shield. |例句：The moorhen darted across the pond. 黑水鸡飞快地穿过池塘。
