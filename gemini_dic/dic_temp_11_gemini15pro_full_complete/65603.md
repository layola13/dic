evan:/ˈɛvən/| adj.|短暂的，瞬息的；易消散的；|Evanescent memories of childhood. 童年转瞬即逝的记忆。|近义词：fleeting, transient, ephemeral, vanishing|反义词：permanent, lasting, enduring|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *evanescentem*, present participle of *evanescere* (“to vanish, disappear”), from *ex-* (“out”) + *vanescere* (“to vanish”), from *vanus* (“empty, vain”).
