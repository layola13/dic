bulgier:/ˈbʌldʒiər/ | adj. | 更鼓胀的，更凸出的 | More bulging; having a more pronounced bulge or swelling. | The suitcase became bulgier as I added more clothes.  我的行李箱随着我不断往里加衣服而变得越来越鼓。 |  My backpack is bulgier today than yesterday because I have more books. 我的背包今天比昨天鼓，因为我带了更多的书。 | 近义词：rounder, fuller, more swollen | 反义词：flatter, thinner, less swollen | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From bulge + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Old French *bouge "leather bag, swelling," of uncertain origin.
