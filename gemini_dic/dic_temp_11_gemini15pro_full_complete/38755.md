condonable:/kənˈdoʊnəbl/
adjective
可原谅的，可宽恕的
That which can be condoned; excusable; forgivable.
His behavior, though inappropriate, was condonable given the circumstances. 考虑到当时的情况，他的行为虽然不恰当，但还是可以原谅的。
Synonyms: excusable, forgivable, pardonable, venial
Antonyms: inexcusable, unforgivable, unpardonable, blameworthy
词性: adjective
