cyborgs:/ˈsaɪbɔːrɡz/
noun
半机械人；电子人
A fictional or hypothetical person whose physical abilities are extended beyond normal human limitations by mechanical elements built into the body.
例句：In the movie, cyborgs are created to fight in wars. 在电影中，半机械人被创造出来是为了打仗。

例句：Many science fiction stories feature cyborgs with enhanced strength and speed. 许多科幻小说都以拥有增强力量和速度的半机械人为特色。

近义词：bionic humans, androids
反义词: humans, biological organisms
词性:n. (noun)
