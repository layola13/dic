noncontrollable:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrəʊləbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnkənˈtroʊləbl/

noncontrollable: n. |不可控制的事物| Something that cannot be controlled. |  Rising healthcare costs are a major noncontrollable for many families.  不断上涨的医疗保健费用是许多家庭无法控制的主要因素。 | uncontrollable factor, unmanageable element | controllable factor, manageable element
