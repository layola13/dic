mustee: /ˈmʌstiː/
n.
混血儿（带有贬义，尤指白人和黑人的混血后代）
A person of mixed white and Black ancestry (offensive term, especially of a person with one white and one Black parent).

例句：
The term "mustee" is now considered offensive and should not be used.
“mustee”这个词现在被认为是冒犯性的，不应该使用。

In historical documents, you might encounter the term "mustee," referring to people of mixed race.
在历史文献中，你可能会遇到“mustee”一词，指的是混血人种。
