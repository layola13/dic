catacoustic:/kætəˈkuːstɪk/ | adj. |  回音的，传音的 | Relating to the reflection of sound; designed to gather and project sound waves. | The ancient Greeks were masters of catacoustic design, building amphitheaters that maximized the projection of sound. 古希腊人是传音设计的 masters，他们建造的圆形剧场最大限度地提高了声音的传播。| The lecture hall's catacoustic properties were excellent, allowing even those in the back rows to hear the speaker clearly. 演讲厅的传音性能非常好，即使是坐在后排的人也能清晰地听到演讲者的声音。 | synonyms: resonant, echoing, amplifying | antonyms: sound-absorbing, dampening | Adjective
