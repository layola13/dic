gazebo:/ɡəˈziːboʊ/ (US) /ɡəˈziːbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|凉亭；观景亭|A freestanding, roofed, open-sided structure, often octagonal or turret-shaped, providing shade and a place to relax in a garden or park.|例句：We sat in the gazebo and watched the sunset. 我们坐在凉亭里看日落。

例句：The gazebo offered a welcome respite from the midday sun. 凉亭提供了一个躲避正午阳光的好去处。|近义词：pavilion, pergola, summerhouse|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "enclosed space")|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the 18th century, humorous formation perhaps based on "gaze" + Latin-style ending "-ebo," suggesting a place for gazing.  The suggestion of a Latin origin is entirely false.
