bcc:/ˌbiː siː ˈsiː/ | n. | 密送，暗送；副本抄送 | A blind carbon copy (abbreviated Bcc or bcc) is a way of sending emails to a number of people without revealing their addresses to each other. | I sent the email to John, with Mary and Peter in bcc. 我把邮件发给了约翰，并将玛丽和彼得暗送。 | 近义词:  blind copy | 反义词: cc (carbon copy) | 词性: noun
