cultist:/ˈkʌltɪst/| n. |信徒，狂热追随者|A member of a cult; a person who is excessively devoted to a particular person, idea, or activity. |例句：The cultists followed their leader without question. 这些信徒毫无疑问地追随着他们的领袖。

例句：The group was identified as a dangerous cult, and its cultists were known for their extreme beliefs.  这个团体被认定为危险的邪教，其信徒以其极端的信仰而闻名。|近义词：follower, devotee, fanatic, zealot|反义词:skeptic, critic, dissenter|词性:noun
