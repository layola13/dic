bulbel:/ˈbʌlbəl/ | n. | 小鳞茎; 球茎 |A small bulb; a bulblet.  A small, rounded, usually underground storage organ present in some plants, like a miniature bulb. It can develop into a new plant.|  The lily propagated through bulbels.  百合通过小鳞茎繁殖。 | synonyms: bulblet, bulbkin | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical part of a plant.  Could consider terms like "root", "rhizome" as contrasting plant parts.) | noun
