gibbousness:/ˈɡɪbəsnəs/ | n. |凸圆，隆起，（尤指月亮）凸月|The state of being gibbous, especially of the moon when it appears more than half full but less than full.|The gibbousness of the moon was clearly visible in the night sky. 月亮凸圆的形状在夜空中清晰可见。|近义词：convexity, protuberance|反义词：concavity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gibbosus, from Latin gibbus (“hump, hunch”), probably ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gebʰ- (“to bend, curve”).
