obstreperous: /əbˈstrɛpərəs/ /ɑːbˈstrɛpərəs/
adjective
喧闹的；吵闹的；不服管教的
Noisy and difficult to control.
例句：The obstreperous child was finally removed from the classroom. 喧闹的孩子最终被带出了教室。

例句：The obstreperous fans were cheering and chanting throughout the game. 吵闹的球迷在整个比赛过程中都在欢呼和高喊。

近义词：unruly, boisterous, rambunctious, disorderly, disruptive
反义词：quiet, calm, peaceful, obedient, docile
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin obstrepere ("to make a noise against, oppose noisily"), from ob- ("against") + strepere ("to make a noise").
