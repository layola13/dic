nonutopian:/ˌnɒnjuːˈtəʊpiən/ | adj. | 非乌托邦的，非理想化的 | Not utopian; realistic, practical, or acknowledging the flaws and imperfections of society. |  The novel presents a nonutopian vision of the future, focusing on the struggles of ordinary people. 这部小说展现了未来的一种非乌托邦景象，关注的是普通人的挣扎。| Synonyms: realistic, pragmatic, practical, down-to-earth | Antonyms: utopian, idealistic, visionary | adjective

nonutopian:/ˌnɒnjuːˈtoʊpiən/ | n. | 非乌托邦主义者 | A person who does not believe in or advocate for a utopian society. |  As a nonutopian, she believed that true progress came through incremental change, not radical revolution. 作为一名非乌托邦主义者，她相信真正的进步是通过渐进式的变革而不是激进的革命实现的。| Synonyms: realist, pragmatist | Antonyms: utopian, idealist, visionary | noun
