matrimonial: /ˌmætrɪˈmoʊniəl/ (US) /ˌmætrɪˈməʊniəl/ (UK)| adj.| 婚姻的，夫妇的|Relating to marriage or married life.|  例句：They sought matrimonial advice from a counselor. 他们向一位顾问寻求婚姻建议。

例句：Matrimonial bliss is not always easy to achieve. 婚姻幸福并非总是容易实现的。

|近义词：marital, nuptial, conjugal|反义词：single, unmarried, divorced|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *matrimonialis, from Latin mātrimōnium (“marriage”), from māter (“mother”).
