anti-cruelty:/ˌæntiˈkruːəlti/
| adj.| 反虐待的，防止虐待的 | Opposing or prohibiting the infliction of pain or suffering, especially on animals. | The anti-cruelty society works to protect animals from abuse.  反虐待协会致力于保护动物免受虐待。|  humane, compassionate | cruel, inhumane | adjective


anti-cruelty:/ˌæntiˈkruːəlti/
| n.| 反虐待 | The principle or practice of opposing cruelty. |  Anti-cruelty measures are essential for animal welfare. 反虐待措施对动物福利至关重要。| compassion, kindness | cruelty, inhumanity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti "against, opposite"
cruelty: from Old French crualte, from Latin crudelitas, from crudelis "cruel," related to crudus "raw, bloody"
