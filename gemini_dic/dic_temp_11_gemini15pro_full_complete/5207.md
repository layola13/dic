alligator-clamps:/ˈælɪˌɡeɪtər klæmps/ | n. |鳄鱼夹|A type of strong spring clamp, usually insulated, with toothed jaws, used for making temporary electrical connections.|I used alligator-clamps to connect the battery to the wires. 我用鳄鱼夹将电池连接到电线上。|Synonyms: crocodile clips, battery clips |Antonyms: N/A |noun

alligator-clip:/ˈælɪˌɡeɪtər klɪp/ | n. |鳄鱼夹|Singular form of alligator-clamps.  Refers to a single clip. |The alligator-clip held the wire securely in place.  这个鳄鱼夹牢牢地夹住了电线。|Synonyms: crocodile clip, battery clip |Antonyms: N/A |noun
