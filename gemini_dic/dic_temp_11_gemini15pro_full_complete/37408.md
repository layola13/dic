commercialities:/kəˌmɜːrʃiˈælɪtiz/
noun
商业性，商业化，商业气息
the aspects of something that make it suitable for buying and selling; the quality of being influenced by or used for commerce.
The increasing commercialities of the holiday season have diluted its true meaning.  圣诞节越来越商业化，冲淡了它真正的意义。
The film was criticized for its excessive commercialities. 这部电影因其过度的商业气息而受到批评。
synonyms: commercialism, mercantilism, marketability
antonyms: noncommercialism, unprofitability
