doggish:/ˈdɒɡɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡɪʃ/ (US)| adj.|像狗的；粗鲁的；顽固的|Resembling or characteristic of a dog, especially in a negative way; sullenly obstinate; churlish.|He gave me a doggish look, clearly unhappy with the interruption. 他给了我一个像狗一样的眼神，显然对被打扰很不高兴。|The old man was doggish in his refusal to leave his home.  这位老人顽固地拒绝离开他的家。|近义词：sullen, surly, churlish, grumpy, morose|反义词：amicable, friendly, pleasant, affable|词性:形容词
