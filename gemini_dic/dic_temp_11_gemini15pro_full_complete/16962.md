basswood:/ˈbæsˌwʊd/| n.|椴木；美国椴木|A North American tree with pale wood.  The wood of this tree, used for carving and making furniture.|The crafter preferred basswood for his intricate carvings.  这个工匠喜欢用椴木进行复杂的雕刻。|Synonyms: linden, lime (tree), whitewood|Antonyms: hardwood (generally)|noun
