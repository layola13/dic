hourglasses:/ˈaʊərˌɡlæsɪz/
n.
沙漏（hourglass 的复数形式）
instruments for measuring time, typically consisting of two connected glass bulbs containing sand that flows from the upper bulb to the lower one in a fixed time
The museum displayed several antique hourglasses.  博物馆展出了几个古董沙漏。
Time is running out, as symbolized by the hourglasses. 时间正在流逝，正如沙漏所象征的那样。
timers, clocks, watches
stopwatches
noun
