macropteres:/ˌmækrɒpˈtɪəriːz/ | n. | 长翅型，长翅目昆虫 | Insects with long or large wings, especially in reference to certain insects that have two forms, one with long wings and one with short or no wings. |  Long-winged aphids are called macropteres and can fly to new host plants. 长翅蚜虫被称为长翅型，可以飞到新的寄主植物上。| macropterous, long-winged | brachypterous, short-winged, apterous | noun
