overanalytic:/ˌoʊvərˌænəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
过度分析的
Given to or marked by excessive analysis.
She's so overanalytic that she can't enjoy anything without picking it apart. 她过度分析，以至于做什么事都吹毛求疵，无法享受其中。
He tends to be overanalytic, which can sometimes paralyze him from taking action. 他倾向于过度分析，这有时会让他无法采取行动。
近义词: overthinking, obsessive, hypercritical
反义词: intuitive, instinctive, uncritical
词性: adjective
