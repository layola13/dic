ethic: /ˈeθɪk/ | n. | 道德，伦理 | A system of moral principles. |  例句：Medical ethics prevent doctors from harming their patients.  医学伦理阻止医生伤害他们的病人。

例句：The company's business ethics are questionable. 这家公司的商业道德值得怀疑。

近义词：moral, principle, value | 反义词：immorality, corruption | 词性: noun
