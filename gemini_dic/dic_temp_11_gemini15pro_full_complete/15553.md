bagh: /bɑːɣ/ | n. | garden, park (especially in India, Pakistan, and Iran) |  From Persian باغ (bāğ) meaning "garden," "orchard," or "enclosure."  A bagh is a formal garden, often walled, featuring fountains, pools, and geometrically arranged plants. | The Shalimar Bagh in Lahore is a famous example of Mughal garden design.  Shalimar花园(巴格)在拉合尔是蒙兀儿花园设计的著名例子。| synonym: garden, park, orchard | antonym: wilderness, desert | noun
