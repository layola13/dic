ascendant:/əˈsɛndənt/ | adj. n. | 上升的，占优势的;  优势，支配地位 | rising in power or influence; a position of dominance or controlling influence |  The politician's star is ascendant.  这位政治家的影响力日渐上升。 | synonyms: rising, dominant, prevailing, influential | antonyms: declining, falling, subordinate, weak | adjective

ascendant:/əˈsɛndənt/ | n. |  上升星座，祖先 |  astrology the sign of the zodiac that is rising above the eastern horizon at the moment of someone's birth; an ancestor | My ascendant is in Leo. 我的上升星座是狮子座。| synonyms: ancestor, forebear, progenitor | antonyms: descendant | noun
