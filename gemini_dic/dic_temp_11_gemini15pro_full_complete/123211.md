monocistronic:/ˌmɒnəʊsɪˈstrɒnɪk/ | adj. |单顺反子的|Containing or being a cistron that codes for only one polypeptide chain.  A monocistronic mRNA molecule contains the genetic information to translate only a single protein chain, unlike polycistronic mRNA which codes for multiple proteins.|  Eukaryotic mRNAs are typically monocistronic. 真核生物的mRNA通常是单顺反子的。|  Most bacterial mRNAs are polycistronic, while eukaryotic mRNAs are almost always monocistronic. 大多数细菌mRNA是多顺反子的，而真核mRNA几乎总是单顺反子的。|  polycistronic | monogenic | adjective
