bubbliness:/ˈbʌblɪnəs/
noun
活泼，快乐，兴奋
The quality of being full of energy and enthusiasm.
Her bubbliness was infectious, and soon everyone was smiling. 她的活泼很有感染力，很快每个人都笑了。
