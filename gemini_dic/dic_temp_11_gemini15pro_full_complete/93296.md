ilks:/ɪlks/
noun
种类；同类；同类的人或物
a kind, class, or group.
例句：People of all ilks attended the festival. 各式各样的人都参加了节日。

例句：Birds of that ilk are rare in this region.  那一类的鸟在这个地区很罕见。

近义词：kinds, sorts, types, categories
反义词：None (The word "ilks" refers to a general grouping, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性：名词
