bemingle:/bɪˈmɪŋɡəl/
verb
混合，交往
to mix or mingle together
The scents of flowers and freshly cut grass bemingled in the air. 花香和新割的草的气味在空气中混合在一起。
synonyms: mix, blend, intermingle, commingle
antonyms: separate, divide, segregate
词性: 动词
