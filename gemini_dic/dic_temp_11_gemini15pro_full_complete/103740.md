jot:/dʒɑːt/ | n. vt. | 少量；略记；要点 | a very small amount of something |  I'll make a jot of that in my notebook. 我会在笔记本上记下这一点。| 近义词: bit, smidgen, speck, iota | 反义词: lot, abundance | 词性: 名词, 动词

jot:/dʒɑːt/ | vt. | 简要记下；草草记下 | to write something quickly |  Please jot down your name and address. 请写下你的姓名和地址。| 近义词: note, scribble, record | 反义词: elaborate, detail | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English jot, from Old French jote, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to *jut, of imitative origin.
