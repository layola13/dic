kerned:/kɜːrnd/ | adj. | 字距调整的 | Adjusted the spacing between letters or characters, especially to improve appearance. | The title of the book was kerned for a more elegant look. 这本书的标题经过了字距调整，看起来更优雅了。 | The logo was carefully kerned to create a balanced visual effect.  这个标志经过精心字距调整，以创造平衡的视觉效果。 | 近义词: adjusted, spaced | 反义词: unkerned | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From "kern" (a part of a typefounder's mold) + "-ed".  "Kern" itself comes from Middle French "carne" meaning "corner, angle."
