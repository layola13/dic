modem:/ˈmoʊdəm/ (US) /ˈməʊdɛm/ (UK)| n. |调制解调器|A combined modulator and demodulator that converts digital signals to analog signals for transmission over telephone lines and other communication media, and back to digital signals upon reception.|I need a new modem for faster internet speeds. 我需要一个新的调制解调器来提高网速。|Synonyms: modulator-demodulator |Antonyms: None | Noun
