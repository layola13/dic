flux:/flʌks/
n.|流; 流量; 变迁; 不断的变动|A continuous movement or flow.|The flux of information on the internet is overwhelming. |互联网上的信息流量巨大。
近义词:flow, change, fluctuation, instability, variance
反义词:stability, constancy, equilibrium

flux:/flʌks/
v.|熔化; 融合; 流出|To melt or become fluid.|The artist fluxed the metals together to create a sculpture. |艺术家将金属熔合在一起创作了一座雕塑。
近义词:melt, fuse, flow
反义词:solidify, harden
