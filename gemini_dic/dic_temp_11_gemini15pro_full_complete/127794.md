neighbourhoods:/ˈneɪbərˌhʊdz/ (US), /ˈneɪbəˌhʊdz/ (UK)| n.|街区，邻里，社区|A neighbourhood is a district, especially one forming a community within a town or city.  It refers to the area immediately surrounding someone's home.|I grew up in a friendly neighbourhood. 我在一个友好的街区长大。|近义词：community, district, area, vicinity, locale|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps consider "isolation," "remoteness")|Noun
