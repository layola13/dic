hariolation:/ˌhærɪəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 占卜;预言 | The act of predicting the future, often by interpreting omens or signs.  Derived from "hari" (holy, sacred) and the suffix "-ation" (action or process). | The fortune teller's hariolation predicted a long and prosperous life. 占卜师的预言预示着长寿和繁荣。 | 近义词: divination, prophecy, prediction, augury, forecasting | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "misinterpretation" or "miscalculation" as contrasting concepts.) | 词性: noun
