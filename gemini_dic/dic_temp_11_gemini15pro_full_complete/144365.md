outstep:/ˌaʊtˈstɛp/| v.|超越，超出…的限度|To go beyond a proper or usual limit; exceed.|He outstepped his authority when he fired the employee without consulting his superiors. 他在未咨询上级的情况下解雇了员工，超出了他的权限。|The company outstepped its competitors by developing a groundbreaking new technology. 这家公司通过开发突破性的新技术超越了竞争对手。|近义词：exceed, transgress, overstep|反义词: observe, respect|词性:动词

词源: out- (超出) + step (步子)
