cousin-hoods:/ˈkʌzənˌhʊdz/| n.|表兄弟姐妹关系；堂兄弟姐妹关系；亲戚关系|A group or collective of cousins. It refers to the state or quality of being cousins, or the relationship between cousins.  It can also refer to a group of people related by cousinhood, similar to a brotherhood or sisterhood.|My extended family has strong cousin-hoods, and we get together every summer.|近义词：kinship, family ties, relatives|反义词:estrangement, alienation|词性:noun
