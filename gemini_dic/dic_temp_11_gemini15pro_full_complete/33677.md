cinnabarine:/ˈsɪnəˌbɑːraɪn/ | adj. | 辰砂色的，朱红色的 | Of a brilliant red color, like cinnabar. | The cinnabarine robes of the monks were a striking sight against the gray walls.  僧侣们朱红色的长袍在灰色的墙壁映衬下格外醒目。 | 近义词：vermilion, scarlet, crimson | 反义词: azure, cerulean | 词性: 形容词

cinnabarine:/ˈsɪnəˌbɑːraɪn/ | n. | 辰砂 | A bright red mineral consisting of mercury sulfide. (This definition is less common than the adjective form) | The artist used natural cinnabarine to create the vibrant red hues in his painting.  这位艺术家使用天然辰砂来创作他画作中鲜艳的红色调。 | 近义词：cinnabar | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cinabarin*, from Late Latin *cinnabarinus*, from Latin *cinnabaris* ("cinnabar"). Ultimately from Ancient Greek *κιννάβαρι* (kinnábari).
