designates:/ˈdɛzɪɡneɪts/| v.|指定；指派；标示|Officially give a name or status to (someone or something).|The area was designated a national park. 这个地区被指定为国家公园。|appoints, nominates, selects, assigns|ignores, overlooks|verb
designates:/ˈdɛzɪɡneɪts/| n. (plural)|被指定的人或物|A person or thing that has been designated. |The designates for the award will be announced next week.  该奖项的指定人选将于下周公布。|nominees, appointees|N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *designatus, past participle of designare ("to mark out, designate"), from de- ("out, from") + signare ("to mark"), from signum ("mark, sign").
