last:/læst/
verb
持续，维持
The meeting lasted for two hours. 会议持续了两个小时。
例句：The battery lasts for about a week.  电池大约能用一个星期。
例句：How long will the concert last?  音乐会要持续多久？
近义词：continue, endure, persist
反义词：end, cease, stop
词性: 动词
词源：From Old English *læstan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *lastoną (“to follow after, continue”), from Proto-Indo-European *lā̆dʰ- (“to leave behind, stretch out”). Cognate with Old Saxon lāstan (“to follow, continue, last”), Old Frisian lesta (“to follow, last”), Dutch laatsen (“to abandon”), Old High German lāzan (“to leave behind”), German lassen (“to let, leave”).
