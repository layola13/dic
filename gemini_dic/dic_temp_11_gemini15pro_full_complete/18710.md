bereaveds:/bɪˈriːvdz/
|n.| 丧失亲友的人；死者家属 |People who have experienced the loss of a loved one, especially through death.  |The bereaveds gathered for a memorial service. |近义词：mourners, the grieving |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the past participle of *bereave*, from Old English *berēafian* "to deprive of, take away by violence," from *be-* (intensive prefix) + *rēafian* "to rob, plunder."
