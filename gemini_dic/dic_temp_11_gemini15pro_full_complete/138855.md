nursemaided:/ˈnɜːrsmeɪdɪd/ | v. | 照顾（尤指孩子）；像保姆一样照顾 |  To have cared for or guided someone, especially a child, in a way reminiscent of a nursemaid. |  He was nursemaided through his difficult first year at university by his older sister. 他大学第一年过得很艰难，多亏他姐姐像保姆一样照顾他。| 近义词: cared for, nurtured, looked after | 反义词: neglected, abandoned | 词性: 动词
