affeer:/əˈfɪər/
|v.|估价，确定（罚款、赔偿金）; 确认，批准|The sheriff was empowered to affeer the amercement. 郡长被授权确定罚款金额。|assess, fix, confirm| reject, disallow

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *afeurement, from afeurer “to fix, assess,” from a- “to” + feur “price, value,” from Latin forum “marketplace.”
