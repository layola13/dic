ethylin:/ˈɛθɪlɪn/ | n. | 乙基林；乙撑亚胺 |A cyclic amine with the formula C2H5N. It is a highly reactive and toxic compound used in various industrial applications, particularly as a chemical intermediate.  | Ethylin is used in the production of certain polymers. 乙基林用于生产某些聚合物。| Ethylin reacts readily with water. 乙基林很容易与水反应。|Synonyms: aziridine, ethyleneimine | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific chemical compound) | noun
