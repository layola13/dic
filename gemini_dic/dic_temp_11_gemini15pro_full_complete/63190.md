entangler:/ɪnˈtæŋɡlər/
n.|纠缠者，使卷入者|Someone or something that entangles or causes entanglement.|The vines acted as an entangler for the small animals.|近义词: snarer, trapper|反义词: liberator, rescuer|词性: 名词

entangle:/ɪnˈtæŋɡl/
v.|使纠缠，卷入，使复杂化|To cause to become twisted together with or caught in something.|The fishing line became entangled in the seaweed.|近义词: tangle, snarl, involve|反义词: disentangle, untangle, extricate|词性: 动词
