antinationalisms:/ˌæntiˈnæʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.|反民族主义|Sentiments opposed to nationalism, often advocating for international cooperation or global governance.  The belief that nationalism is harmful and should be opposed.|The rise of antinationalisms in the 20th century was a reaction to the horrors of two world wars. 20世纪反民族主义的兴起是对两次世界大战恐怖的反应。|近义词:cosmopolitanism, internationalism, globalism|反义词:nationalism, patriotism|词性:noun
