erration:/ɪˈreɪʃn/ | n. |  错误；不规则行为；古怪行为 | An instance of erratic behavior or an action that is unusual or unexpected. | The car's sudden erratic movement startled the driver. 汽车突然的古怪动作吓到了司机。| synonyms: aberration, anomaly, deviation, irregularity | antonyms: normalcy, regularity, stability | noun

erratic:/ɪˈrætɪk/ | adj. | 不稳定的；古怪的；飘忽不定的 |  Not even or regular in pattern or movement; unpredictable. | His erratic behavior made it difficult to work with him. 他飘忽不定的行为使人很难与他共事。| synonyms: unpredictable, inconsistent, unstable, capricious | antonyms: consistent, predictable, stable, steady | adjective
