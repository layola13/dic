everlastingly:/ˌevərˈlæstɪŋli/
adverb
永远地，永恒地
For ever; eternally.
The memory will remain everlastingly. 这段记忆将永远留存。
近义词: eternally, forever, perpetually, always, infinitely
反义词: temporarily, briefly, fleetingly
词性: 副词

词源:
From ever + lasting + -ly.  "Ever" traces back to Old English "æfre," meaning "always." "Lasting" comes from the Middle English "lasten," meaning "to endure, continue." The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "lasting" into an adverb.
