overintellectually: /ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəli/
adverb
过于理性地，过分理智地
Thinking or analyzing something in an excessively intellectual manner, often to the point of impracticality or losing sight of the bigger picture.
He approached the problem overintellectually, getting bogged down in minor details and missing the obvious solution. 他过于理性地处理这个问题，纠结于细枝末节，错过了显而易见的解决方案。
She analyzes movies overintellectually, focusing on obscure symbolism and ignoring the emotional impact. 她过度分析电影，专注于晦涩的象征意义而忽略了情感冲击。

近义词: cerebral, excessively analytical, abstractly
反义词: intuitively, emotionally, pragmatically
词性: adverb

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "intellectually" (adverb form of "intellectual," from Latin "intellectus," meaning "understanding, perception")
