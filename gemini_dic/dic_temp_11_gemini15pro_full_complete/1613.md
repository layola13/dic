acidifies:/əˈsɪdɪfaɪz/
verb
使酸化；使变酸
Makes something more acidic.
例句：
The burning of fossil fuels acidifies the oceans.
燃烧化石燃料会使海洋酸化。

Acid rain acidifies the soil, making it difficult for plants to grow.
酸雨使土壤酸化，使植物难以生长。

近义词：sours, acidulates
反义词: alkalizes, neutralizes
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From acid + -ify (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become").  Acid ultimately comes from Latin *acidus, meaning "sour," related to acēre "to be sour."
