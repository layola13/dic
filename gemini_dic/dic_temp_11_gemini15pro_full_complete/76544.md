gallantries: /ˈɡæləntriz/ | n. |殷勤，献殷勤；(尤指)对女性的殷勤；勇敢的行为；英勇事迹 |  Polite attention or respect given to women; acts of bravery or chivalry. | He performed many gallantries for the ladies. 他对女士们献殷勤。| 近义词: courtesies, chivalry, attentions, politeness | 反义词: rudeness, impoliteness, disrespect | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French "galanterie," meaning "gallantry, courtesy, bravery," from "galant," meaning "gallant, courtly, brave." Ultimately from Frankish "*galant," meaning "valiant, spirited."
