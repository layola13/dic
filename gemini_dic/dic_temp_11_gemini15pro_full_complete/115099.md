magnesium:/mæɡˈniːziəm/ | n. | 镁 | A silvery-white metallic element, the lightest structural metal, used in alloys, flares, and flashbulbs, and essential for plant and animal nutrition. | Magnesium is an important mineral for human health. 镁是人体健康的重要矿物质。|  Magnesium alloys are used in aerospace applications due to their lightweight nature.  镁合金因其轻质特性用于航空航天领域。| synonyms:  Mg (symbol), metal, element | antonyms: nonmetal | noun
