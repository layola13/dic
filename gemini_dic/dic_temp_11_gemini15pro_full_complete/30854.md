chadian: /ˈtʃædiən/ | adj. n. | 乍得的；乍得人 |  Relating to Chad, a country in north-central Africa, or its people. |  Chad is a landlocked country in North Central Africa. Its diverse geography ranges from arid deserts to savannas and lowlands. It is bordered by Libya to the north, Sudan to the east, the Central African Republic to the south, Cameroon to the southwest, Niger to the west, and Nigeria to the southwest.| 近义词: Chadian (as a noun, there are no direct synonyms, but one could use "person from Chad" or "inhabitant of Chad") | 反义词: (None -  nationality doesn't have antonyms) | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: From Chad + -ian.
