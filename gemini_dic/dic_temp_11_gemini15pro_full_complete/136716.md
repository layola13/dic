nonsensationalistic:/ˌnɒnsenˈseɪʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非耸人听闻的；不哗众取宠的
Avoiding sensationalism; not designed to produce a startling effect.
The documentary presented a nonsensationalistic account of the war.  这部纪录片对战争进行了非耸人听闻的描述。
Synonyms: factual, objective, unbiased, sober, measured
Antonyms: sensational, lurid, exaggerated, hyped, dramatic
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From non- + sensationalistic.  Sensationalistic from sensation (感觉) + -al (形容词后缀) + -istic (形容词后缀). Ultimately from Latin *sensātiō.
