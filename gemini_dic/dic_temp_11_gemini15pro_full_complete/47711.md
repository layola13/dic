decachordon:/ˈdɛkəkɔːrdən/| n.|十弦琴，十弦里拉琴|A ten-stringed instrument, specifically a kind of lyre or psaltery.  It can also refer to a treatise on music theory, particularly one dealing with the ten-stringed instrument.|Marsilio Ficino wrote a work called "De Decachordo," exploring the symbolism of the ten strings.  马尔西利奥·菲奇诺写了一部名为《十弦琴》的著作，探讨了十根琴弦的象征意义。

|Synonyms: lyre, psaltery|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "monochord" for a single-stringed instrument)|noun
