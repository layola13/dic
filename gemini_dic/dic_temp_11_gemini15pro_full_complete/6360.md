amphibiously:/æmˈfɪbiəsli/
adv.
两栖地；水陆两用地
in a way that relates to, involves, or is suited to both land and water.
The vehicle can travel amphibiously.  这辆车可以水陆两用。
The Marines landed amphibiously. 海军陆战队实施了两栖登陆。
近义词: both on land and in water
反义词: terrestrially, aquatically
词性: adverb

amphibious:/æmˈfɪbiəs/
adj.
两栖的；水陆两用的
able to live both on land and in water; relating to or adapted for both land and water.
Frogs are amphibious creatures. 青蛙是两栖动物。
The military launched an amphibious assault. 军方发动了两栖攻击。
近义词:  dual-habitat, bicolous
反义词: terrestrial, aquatic
词性: adjective
