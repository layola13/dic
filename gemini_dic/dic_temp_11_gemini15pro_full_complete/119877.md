militant:/ˈmɪlɪtənt/| adj. n.|好战的，激进的；斗士，激进分子|Engaging or ready to engage in warfare or combat; aggressively active, especially in support of a cause.|例句：The government is facing pressure from militant groups. 政府正面临来自激进组织的压力。|近义词：aggressive, assertive, combative, activist|反义词:peaceful, passive, dovish|词性:adjective, noun

militant:/ˈmɪlɪtənt/| n.|斗士，激进分子|A person engaged in warfare or combat; a person who is aggressively active, especially in support of a cause.|例句：The militants demanded the release of their leader.  激进分子要求释放他们的领导人。|近义词：activist, fighter, revolutionary|反义词:pacifist, moderate|词性:noun
