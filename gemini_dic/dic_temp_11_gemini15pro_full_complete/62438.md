endopleura:/ˈɛndəʊˈplʊərə/ | n. | 内种皮 | The innermost layer of the seed coat in flowering plants. | The endopleura is a thin, membranous layer that surrounds the embryo. 内种皮是环绕胚胎的薄膜层。 |  inner seed coat, tegmen | outer seed coat, testa | noun

endopleura:/ˈɛndəʊˈplʊərə/ | n. | 内膜 |  The inner lining of a membrane or an organ. (Less common usage) | The endopleura of the pericardium secretes lubricating fluid.  心包的内膜分泌润滑液。| inner lining, membrane | outer layer | noun
