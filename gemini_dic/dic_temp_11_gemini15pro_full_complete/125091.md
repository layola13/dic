mulligatawny:/ˌmʌlɪɡəˈtɔːni/ | n. | 木力卡坦尼汤（一种用鸡肉或羊肉做的、加有咖喱粉和其他调味品的浓汤） | A curry-flavored soup of East Indian origin, typically made with chicken or meat. |  例句：I had a delicious bowl of mulligatawny soup for lunch. 我午餐喝了一碗美味的木力卡坦尼汤。

| 近义词: curry soup, spiced soup | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of soup) | 词性: noun


mulligatawny:/ˌmʌlɪɡəˈtɔːni/ | adj. | 木力卡坦尼汤的；加有咖喱粉的 | Flavored with curry, like mulligatawny soup. | 例句：The mulligatawny aroma filled the kitchen. 厨房里充满了木力卡坦尼的香味。

| 近义词: curried, spiced | 反义词: bland, unspiced | 词性: adjective
