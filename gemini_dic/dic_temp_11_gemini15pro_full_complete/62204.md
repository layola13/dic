encourage: /ɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ (British) /ɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ (American) | vt. | 鼓励，激励 | To inspire with courage, spirit, or hope. To give support or confidence to someone. |  His teacher encouraged him to apply for the scholarship. 他的老师鼓励他申请奖学金。| synonyms: inspire, motivate, support, boost | antonyms: discourage, dissuade, hinder, deter | Verb

encouraging: /ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋ/ (British) /ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋ/ (American) | adj. | 令人鼓舞的，振奋人心的 | Giving someone support or confidence. Suggesting a positive and hopeful future. |  The results of the first test were encouraging. 第一次测试的结果令人鼓舞。| synonyms: promising, hopeful, positive, optimistic | antonyms: discouraging, disheartening, depressing, pessimistic | Adjective
