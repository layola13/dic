overimporting: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɔːrtɪŋ/
n.|过度进口|The act of importing goods in excess of domestic demand or production capacity.|例句：Overimporting can lead to trade imbalances and harm domestic industries. 过度进口会导致贸易失衡，损害国内产业。|近义词：excessive importing|反义词: underexporting |词性: 名词
