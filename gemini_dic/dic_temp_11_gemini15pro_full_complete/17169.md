battleworthy:/ˈbætlˌwɜːrði/
adjective
适于战斗的; 可作战的; 能征惯战的
Fit for battle; capable of fighting effectively.
The general inspected the troops to ensure they were battleworthy. 将军视察了部队，以确保他们有作战能力。
The newly repaired tank was deemed battleworthy and sent to the front.  新修理好的坦克被认为可以作战，并被送往前线。
近义词:combat-ready, fit for fight, fighting fit
反义词:unfit, unprepared, weak
词性:adjective
