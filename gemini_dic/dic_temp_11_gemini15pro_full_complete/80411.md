gombo:/ˈɡʌmboʊ/ (US), /ˈɡɒmbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|秋葵，秋葵浓汤|A vegetable with long seed pods, or a thick stew made with this vegetable.|例句：I added some okra, also known as gumbo, to the stew. 我在炖菜里加了一些秋葵，也叫秋葵。
