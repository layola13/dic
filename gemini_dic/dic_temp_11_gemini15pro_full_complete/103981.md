juggernautish:/ˌdʒʌɡərˈnɔːtɪʃ/
adjective
势不可挡的；庞大而笨重的
extremely large and powerful and therefore difficult to stop; like a juggernaut
The company became a juggernautish force in the market.  这家公司成为市场上势不可挡的力量。
The team's juggernautish defense crushed their opponents.  该队势不可挡的防守击溃了对手。
Synonyms: unstoppable, overwhelming, powerful, massive, immense
Antonyms: weak, feeble, insignificant, small
Etymology: From Juggernaut, the name of a Hindu deity whose image is carried in an annual procession on a large cart.  People who were accidentally crushed by the cart were considered blessed.
