deridingly:/dɪˈraɪdɪŋli/ | adv. | 嘲笑地，嘲弄地 |  In a manner that shows contempt or ridicule.  Expressing mockery or scorn. | He laughed deridingly at her attempt to sing. 他嘲笑地讥讽她唱歌的尝试。|  She spoke deridingly of his efforts to learn French. 她嘲弄地说他学习法语的努力。 |  sarcastically, mockingly, scornfully, contemptuously | respectfully, admiringly | adverb
