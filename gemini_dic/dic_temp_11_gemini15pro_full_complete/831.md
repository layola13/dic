abstinent: /ˈæbstɪnənt/ | adj. | 节制的，禁欲的 | Not indulging in something, typically alcohol or sex. | He has been abstinent from alcohol for five years. 他已经戒酒五年了。|  She decided to remain abstinent until marriage. 她决定婚前禁欲。 | 近义词: temperate, sober, moderate | 反义词: indulgent, intemperate, immoderate | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: From Latin abstinent- ‘refraining from,’ from the verb abstinere, from abs- ‘from’ + tenere ‘to hold.’
