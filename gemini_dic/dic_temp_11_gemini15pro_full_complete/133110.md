nonerudite:/ˌnɒnˈɛrʊˌdaɪt/
adjective
非博学的，不博学的
Not erudite; lacking in scholarly knowledge.
He gave a nonerudite, but heartfelt speech. 他发表了一场不博学但真诚的演讲。
non-scholarly, unscholarly, unlearned, uneducated, ignorant
erudite, learned, scholarly
