lenience: /ˈliːniənsi/ (US), /ˈliːniənsi/ (UK)| n. |宽大，仁慈，温和|The judge showed lenience in his sentencing, considering the defendant's difficult upbringing. 法官考虑到被告的艰难成长经历，在量刑时表现出了宽大处理。|例句：The professor was known for his lenience with late submissions.  这位教授以对迟交作业的宽容而闻名。

例句：He begged the court for lenience. 他恳求法庭宽大处理。

|近义词：mercy, clemency, forbearance, tolerance|反义词：severity, harshness, strictness|词性: noun
