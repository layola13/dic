nonfigurativenesses:/ˌnɒnˈfɪɡjərətɪvnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
非具象性；非比喻性
The quality or state of being nonfigurative; abstractness.
Her art is characterized by its nonfigurativenesses, focusing on shapes and colors rather than representing real objects. 她的艺术以非具象性为特征，专注于形状和颜色，而不是表现真实物体。
nonfiguration, abstraction
figuration, representationalism
noun
