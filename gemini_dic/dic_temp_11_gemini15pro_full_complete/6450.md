amplificatory:/æmˈplɪfɪkətɔːri/
adjective
放大的；扩充的；补充说明的
serving to amplify; enlarging; expanding.
The lawyer provided amplificatory evidence to support his client's claim.  律师提供了补充证据来支持其委托人的主张。
amplificatory information was added to the report.  报告中增加了补充说明的信息。
synonyms:  expansive, supplementary, augmentative,  enlarging, extending
antonyms:  reductive, concise, abridged,  diminishing, compressing
词性: 形容词
