apsidal:/ˈæpsɪdəl/| adj.|拱端的，拱形的；远拱点的|Relating to an apse, especially the apsidal line connecting the two extreme points in the orbit of a planet, satellite, etc. |The apsidal precession of Mercury is a well-known phenomenon. 水星的拱点进动是一个众所周知的现象。|Near Antonyms: perihelion, perigee|Far Antonyms: nadir|adjective
