delineator:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtər/ | n. | 分界线，界限标，轮廓描绘器 | something that clearly shows the limits of something else |  The white lines are delineators for the pedestrian crossing.  白色线条是人行横道的分界线。 | synonyms: marker, boundary, separator, divider | antonyms: connector, unifier | noun

delineator:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtər/ | n. | 描绘者，阐述者 | someone who describes or portrays something precisely | The author is a skilled delineator of character. 作者是一位善于刻画人物的描绘者。| synonyms: describer, portrayer, illustrator, expounder | antonyms: obscurer, confuser | noun
