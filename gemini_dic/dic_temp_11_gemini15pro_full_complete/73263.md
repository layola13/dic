fore-understanding:/fɔːr-ʌndərˈstændɪŋ/
noun
预先理解；事先了解
An understanding reached before something else happens or is done.  This involves having prior knowledge or a preliminary grasp of a concept or situation.
Before attending the lecture, it's helpful to have some fore-understanding of the topic.  在上课之前，对主题有所预先理解是很有帮助的。
Synonyms: pre-understanding, prior knowledge, preliminary grasp, previous comprehension
Antonyms: misunderstanding, ignorance, lack of knowledge
n.
