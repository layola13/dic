half-willfulnesses:/ˌhæfˈwɪlfʊlnɪsɪz/
|n.| 半任性；半固执；半随心所欲 | A state characterized by a mixture of willfulness and compliance, or by willfulness expressed or acted upon only partially or intermittently. |  Her actions were marked by a series of half-willfulnesses, sometimes obeying, sometimes rebelling. 她的行为表现出一系列半任性，有时服从，有时反抗。|Near synonyms: capriciousness, waywardness, fitfulness, half-heartedness. | Near antonyms: obedience, compliance, docility.
