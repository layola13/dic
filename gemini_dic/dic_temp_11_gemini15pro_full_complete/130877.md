nonaristocratic:/ˌnɒnˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk/
adjective
非贵族的，平民的
Not belonging to the aristocracy; not aristocratic.  Relating to or characteristic of people who are not members of the aristocracy.
The author explores the lives of nonaristocratic families in 19th-century England.  这位作者探索了19世纪英格兰非贵族家庭的生活。
The nonaristocratic movement gained momentum after the revolution.  革命后，非贵族运动获得了动力。
synonyms: common, plebeian, working-class, middle-class, lower-class
antonyms: aristocratic, noble, elite, upper-class
