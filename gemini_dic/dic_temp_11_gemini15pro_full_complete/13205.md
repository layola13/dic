at-the-latest:/æt ðə ˈleɪtɪst/ | adv. | 最迟；至迟 |  Meaning "no later than" a specified time. It emphasizes the deadline. | The project must be completed by Friday at the latest.  这个项目最迟必须在星期五完成。 | Synonyms: by the latest, no later than, at most | Antonyms: at the earliest, at least, as early as | adverb

at-the-latest:/æt ðə ˈleɪtɪst/ | prep. phr. | 最迟；至迟 | Functioning as a prepositional phrase indicating a deadline. | Applications must be submitted at the latest by October 31st. 申请必须最迟在10月31日之前提交。 | Synonyms: by the latest, no later than, at most | Antonyms: at the earliest, at least, as early as | prepositional phrase
