dystome: /ˈdɪstoʊm/ | n. | 吸虫 | A parasitic flatworm, especially a fluke. |  Example: The dystome infects the liver of sheep.  吸虫感染了绵羊的肝脏。 | Example:  Researchers are studying the life cycle of this particular dystome. 研究人员正在研究这种特殊吸虫的生命周期。 | Synonym: fluke, trematode | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a biological organism) | Noun

Etymology: From Greek *dys-* (bad, ill) + *-stoma* (mouth).
