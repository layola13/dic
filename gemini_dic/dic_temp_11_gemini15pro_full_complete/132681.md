nondispersive:/ˌnɒndɪˈspɜːsɪv/ | adj. | 非弥散的，非色散的 | Not dispersing or causing dispersion, especially of light or other radiation. |  A nondispersive infrared sensor is used to detect the presence of carbon dioxide. 一种非色散红外传感器被用来检测二氧化碳的存在。 | Near synonyms: non-scattering, monochromatic | Antonyms: dispersive, scattering | adjective
