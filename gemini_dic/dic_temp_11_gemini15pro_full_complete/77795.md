generalizability:/ˌdʒɛnərəlɪzəˈbɪləti/| n. |概括性，推广性，普遍性|The extent to which research findings can be applied to settings, populations, and conditions beyond the specific context of the original study.|  The study's small sample size limits its generalizability.  这项研究的小样本量限制了它的概括性。|近义词：applicability, universality, external validity|反义词: specificity, particularity|名词
