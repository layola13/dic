bricking:/ˈbrɪkɪŋ/| v.|使变砖；使无法使用；|To render a device (like a smartphone, tablet, etc.) unusable, typically by installing corrupted or incompatible software. This often results in the device being as useful as a brick.|My phone is bricking after I tried to install that custom ROM. 我的手机在尝试安装那个自定义ROM后变砖了。|近义词：ruining, disabling, breaking|反义词:fixing, repairing|动词

bricking:/ˈbrɪkɪŋ/| n.|变砖；失效；|The act of rendering a device unusable, as useful as a brick, often due to failed software installation.|There’s a risk of bricking your device if you don't follow the instructions carefully.  如果你不仔细按照说明操作，你的设备有变砖的风险。|近义词：failure, malfunction|反义词:recovery, repair|名词
