distinguishingly:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋli/

|词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From distinguish + -ing + -ly.  Distinguish comes from Old French distinguer, from Latin distinguere "to separate, divide, mark off, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + -stinguere "to prick, mark," related to stinguere "to extinguish."
