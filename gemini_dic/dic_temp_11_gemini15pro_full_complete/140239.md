offload:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/| v. n.|卸货，卸载；摆脱，倾诉；转嫁|To remove something from a vehicle or ship; to get rid of something unwanted by giving or selling it to someone else; to transfer work or responsibilities to someone else.|The truck driver offloaded the cargo at the warehouse. 卡车司机在仓库卸货。|Synonyms: unload, discharge, disburden, dump, transfer, delegate|Antonyms: load, burden, assume|verb

offload:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/| n.|卸货，卸载|The act of unloading something.|The offload of the goods took several hours. 卸货花了几个小时。|Synonyms: unloading, discharge|Antonyms: loading|noun
