cross-wind:/ˈkrɒsˌwɪnd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌwɪnd/ (US)| n. adj.|侧风，横风|A wind blowing across or at right angles to a particular line or path, especially the direction of travel of an aircraft or ship.|The pilot had to contend with a strong cross-wind during landing. 飞行员在着陆时不得不应对强劲的侧风。|近义词：side wind|反义词:headwind, tailwind|noun
cross-wind:/ˈkrɒsˌwɪnd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌwɪnd/ (US)| adj.|侧风的，横风的|Relating to or exposed to a wind blowing across or at right angles to a particular line or path.|The cross-wind landing was challenging for the pilot.  侧风着陆对飞行员来说很有挑战性。|近义词：side wind|反义词:headwind, tailwind|adjective
