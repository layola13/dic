nonrelevant:/nɒnˈrɛləvənt/| adj.|不相关的，不切题的|Not related or pertinent to the subject at hand; inapplicable.|例句：His comments were nonrelevant to the discussion. 他的评论与讨论无关。|例句：The information provided was nonrelevant to the case. 提供的信息与案件无关。|近义词：inapplicable, impertinent, immaterial, extraneous|反义词：relevant, pertinent, applicable, germane|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- (not) + relevant (related to the matter at hand).  "Relevant" comes from the Latin "relevare" meaning "to lift, relieve, raise."
