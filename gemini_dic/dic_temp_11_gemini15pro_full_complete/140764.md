omnivorously:/ɒmˈnɪvərəsli/| adv.|贪婪地；杂食地|In a manner that consumes a wide variety of food, both plant and animal; excessively and indiscriminately.|He read omnivorously, devouring books on every subject.|近义词：voraciously, indiscriminately, avidly|反义词：selectively, sparingly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Latin *omnivorus* (all-devouring), from *omni-* (all) + *vorare* (to devour).  The suffix *-ously* transforms the adjective *omnivorous* into an adverb.
