hyperactives:/ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvz/ | n. | 多动症患者，过度活跃的人 | People who are hyperactive have a lot of energy and find it difficult to stay still or quiet. |  The teacher found it challenging to manage the hyperactives in her classroom. 老师发现管理她班上的多动症儿童很 challenging。|Near synonyms: overactive individuals, restless people | Antonyms: calm individuals, placid people | noun

hyperactive:/ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 过度活跃的，多动的 |  Excessively active and restless. | The hyperactive puppy wouldn't stop jumping and running around. 这只过度活跃的小狗不停地跳来跳去。| Near synonyms: energetic, restless, fidgety | Antonyms: calm, quiet, still | adjective
