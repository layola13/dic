ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hyperbolicus*, from Ancient Greek ὑπερβολικός (huperbolikós, “exceeding, extravagant”), from ὑπερβολή (huperbolḗ, “excess, exaggeration, hyperbole”), from ὑπερβάλλω (huperballō, “I throw over or beyond”), from ὑπέρ (hupér, “over, beyond”) + βάλλω (ballō, “I throw”).
