herehence: /ˌhɪərˈhɛns/ | adv. | 从此以后，从现在起 | From this place and time onward. |  例句：Herehence, I shall devote myself to this cause. 从此以后，我将致力于这项事业。| 近义词：henceforth, from now on | 反义词: heretofore, previously | 词性: 副词

词源:  "here" + "hence." "Here" indicates this place or time, while "hence" means "from this place or time."  The combination emphasizes the starting point being the present moment.
