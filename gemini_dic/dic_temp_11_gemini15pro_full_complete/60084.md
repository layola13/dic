eeld:/iːld/ | n. | 鳗鱼(尤指欧洲鳗) | A snake-like fish with a smooth, slippery skin. |例句：The eeld wriggled out of my grasp.  鳗鱼从我的手中滑走了。|例句：Eels are catadromous, meaning they spawn in the sea and migrate to freshwater to grow. 鳗鱼是降河性洄游鱼类，这意味着它们在海里产卵，然后迁移到淡水中生长。| 近义词：eel, moray (for certain types) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | 词性: noun
