milestone:/ˈmaɪlstoʊn/ (US) /ˈmaɪlstəʊn/ (UK)| n. | 里程碑，重大事件，转折点 | A significant stage or event in the development of something. |  The company's 50th anniversary was a major milestone. 公司成立 50 周年是一个重要的里程碑。

The first successful flight was a milestone in aviation history.  首次成功飞行是航空史上的一个里程碑。 | synonyms: landmark, turning point, achievement, breakthrough | antonyms: setback, regression, failure | noun
