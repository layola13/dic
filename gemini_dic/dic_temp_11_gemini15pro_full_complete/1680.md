acolothist: /əˈkɒləθɪst/
noun
侍僧，助手
A person who assists a priest or minister in a religious service.
The acolothist lit the candles on the altar. 侍僧点燃了祭坛上的蜡烛。
Synonyms: assistant, helper, aide
Antonyms: leader, principal
