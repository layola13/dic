bestirred:/bɪˈstɜːrd/
verb
奋起；激励；唤醒
to rouse to action; stir up; agitate
例句：He finally bestirred himself to finish the project. 他终于激励自己完成了这个项目。

例句：She bestirred herself to make breakfast for her family. 她起来为家人做早餐。


近义词：rouse, stir, awaken, activate, energize
反义词：rest, relax, be still, remain inactive
词性：verb
