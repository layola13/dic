ETYMOLOGY->
From "chowder" (a type of thick soup or stew) + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns and verbs). The metaphorical sense of "scolding" likely arose from the idea of being "drenched" or "overwhelmed" with criticism, similar to being immersed in a thick chowder.
