globalists:/ˈɡloʊbəlɪsts/ | n. |全球主义者| People who advocate for globalism, the idea of increased interconnectedness and interdependence among countries through free trade, open borders, and international cooperation.  |The globalists believe that international cooperation is essential to solving global problems. 全球主义者认为国际合作对于解决全球问题至关重要。|  The forum attracted globalists from all corners of the world.  这个论坛吸引了来自世界各地的全球主义者。|Synonyms: cosmopolitans, internationalists, global citizens| Antonyms: nationalists, isolationists| noun
