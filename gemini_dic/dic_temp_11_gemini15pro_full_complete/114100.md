magniloquence:/ˌmæɡˈnɪləkwəns/ | n. | 夸夸其谈，浮夸的言辞 | The use of high-flown or bombastic language.  | His magniloquence impressed the audience, but it lacked substance.  他夸夸其谈给观众留下了深刻的印象，但缺乏实质内容。 | synonyms: bombast, grandiloquence, rhetoric, hyperbole | antonyms: understatement, simplicity, conciseness | noun


Etymology->
From Latin *magniloquentia*, from magniloquus (“speaking grandly, using lofty language”), from magnus (“great, large”) + loquor (“I speak”).
