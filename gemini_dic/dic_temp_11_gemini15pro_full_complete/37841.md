competes:/kəmˈpiːts/ | v. | 竞争，对抗 | strives to outdo another for acknowledgment, a prize, supremacy, profit, etc.; engages in a contest |  The company competes with several other firms for market share.  这家公司与其他几家公司竞争市场份额。 | synonyms: contends, rivals, contests, vies | antonyms: cooperates, yields, submits | verb

PHRASE->
competes with:  与...竞争  The new product competes with existing brands. 新产品与现有品牌竞争。
competes against: 与...对抗 He competes against his brother in everything. 他在所有事情上都与他哥哥竞争。
competes for: 为...而竞争  She competes for attention. 她争夺注意力。
competes in: 参加...比赛 He competes in swimming competitions. 他参加游泳比赛。
competes successfully: 成功地竞争  The company competes successfully in the global market.  该公司在全球市场上成功竞争。
