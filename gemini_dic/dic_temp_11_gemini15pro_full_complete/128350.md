neutralizer:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzər/ | n. | 中和剂；抵消因素 |A substance that neutralizes something, especially a chemical that neutralizes an acid or alkali.| The farmer used a soil neutralizer to reduce the acidity. 农民使用土壤中和剂来降低酸度。 | synonyms: counteragent, antidote, remedy | antonyms: intensifier, catalyst | noun

neutralizers:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzərz/ | n. | 中和剂；抵消因素（复数） |Substances that neutralize something, especially chemicals that neutralize acids or alkalis.| The lab stocked various neutralizers for different types of acids.  实验室储备了各种用于不同类型酸的中和剂。| synonyms: counteragents, antidotes, remedies | antonyms: intensifiers, catalysts | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From neutralize + -er.  Neutralize comes from the French neutraliser, from neutre (neutral) + -iser (to make).  Ultimately from Latin neuter (neither).
