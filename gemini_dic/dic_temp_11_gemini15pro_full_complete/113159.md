lupinine: /ˈluːpɪniːn/ /ˈluːpɪnaɪn/ | n. |羽扇豆碱；鲁冰碱| A quinolizidine alkaloid, C₁₀H₁₉NO, obtained from lupine and other plants of the genus *Lupinus*. It is a liquid, and is poisonous. |Lupinine is present in many species of lupine. 羽扇豆碱存在于许多种类的羽扇豆中。|Lupinine can be synthesized in the laboratory. 羽扇豆碱可以在实验室合成。| 近义词: sparteine (in terms of being a lupin alkaloid)| 反义词: None| 词性: noun

lupinine: /ˈluːpɪniːn/ /ˈluːpɪnaɪn/ | adj. |羽扇豆的；羽扇豆碱的| Relating to or derived from lupine. |Lupinine alkaloids are found in several plant species. 羽扇豆碱存在于几种植物中。|The lupinine content of the seeds can vary. 种子中羽扇豆碱的含量可能会有所不同。|近义词: lupin | 反义词: None| 词性: adjective
