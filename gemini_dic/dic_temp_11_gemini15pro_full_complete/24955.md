brushiness:/ˈbrʌʃɪnɪs/
n.
毛茸茸；浓密；粗糙
The quality of being like a brush; resembling the bristles of a brush.
The brushiness of the fabric made it unsuitable for a summer dress.  这种布料的毛茸茸质感使它不适合做夏装。
Near synonyms: hairiness, shagginess, fuzziness
Near antonyms: smoothness, sleekness
