now-and-again:/ˌnaʊ ən əˈɡen/ | adv. |偶尔，有时| happening occasionally; from time to time | We go to the cinema now and again. 我们偶尔会去看电影。|  She calls me now and again for a chat. 她偶尔会打电话给我聊天。 | 近义词: occasionally, sometimes, every now and then, from time to time, once in a while | 反义词: frequently, often, always | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "now and again," which is a simple combination of the adverbs "now" (meaning at the present time) and "again" (meaning once more).  The phrase implies actions or events that happen at the present time but not continuously, thus creating the sense of occasional occurrence.
