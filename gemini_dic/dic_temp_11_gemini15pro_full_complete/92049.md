hyperphysical:/ˌhaɪpərˈfɪzɪkəl/
adj.
超物理的; 超越物质的
Beyond the physical world; metaphysical, supernatural, or transcendental.
例句：
He described a hyperphysical realm beyond human comprehension.
他描述了一个超越人类理解的超物理领域。

The novel explores hyperphysical themes of consciousness and reality.
这部小说探讨了意识和现实的超物理主题。

近义词：metaphysical, supernatural, transcendental, otherworldly
反义词：physical, material, mundane
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + physical.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive," derived from Greek "hyper" (over, beyond). "Physical" comes from Late Latin "physica" (natural science), from Greek "physikē" (study of nature), from "physis" (nature).
