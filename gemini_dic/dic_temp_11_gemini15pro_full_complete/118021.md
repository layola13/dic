memoryless:/ˈmɛmərilɪs/| adj.|无记忆的，无记忆性的|Lacking memory; specifically referring to a process where the future behavior is independent of the past.|The exponential distribution is a commonly used memoryless distribution in probability. 指数分布是概率论中常用的无记忆分布。|Example 1:  In a memoryless system, the past history of events doesn't influence the probability of future events.  在一个无记忆系统中，过去的事件历史不会影响未来事件的概率。

Example 2: Markov chains are often used to model memoryless systems. 马尔可夫链常用于模拟无记忆系统。|近义词：Markovian, amnesiac, oblivious|反义词：retentive, mindful|词性: adjective
