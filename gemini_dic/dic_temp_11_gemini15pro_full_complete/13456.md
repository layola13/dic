atresia:/əˈtriːʒə/ /əˈtriːziə/| n. |闭锁，不通|Absence or abnormal closure of a passage or opening in the body.| Esophageal atresia is a birth defect in which the esophagus, the tube that connects the mouth to the stomach, doesn't develop properly. 食管闭锁是一种先天性缺陷，其中食管（连接口腔和胃的管道）发育不正常。

Atresia can affect various parts of the body, such as the ear canal or the intestines. 闭锁可以影响身体的各个部位，例如耳道或肠道。|近义词：closure, occlusion, obstruction|反义词: patency|词性: noun
