mellowness:/ˈmɛloʊnɪs/| n. |成熟；柔和；醇厚；圆润|The state of being mellow; softness; ripeness; smoothness. |The mellowness of the wine improved with age.  这葡萄酒随着年份的增长更加醇厚。|近义词：softness, ripeness, maturity, smoothness, gentleness|反义词：harshness, sharpness, immaturity, acidity|词性:名词

Etymology: From *mellow* + *-ness*.  *Mellow* comes from Old English *mearu* "soft, tender, ripe," related to Old High German *maro* "soft, tender," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mazwjaz*.
