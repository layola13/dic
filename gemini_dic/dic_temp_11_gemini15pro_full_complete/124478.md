mouldable:/ˈməʊldəbl/ (US) /ˈmoʊldəbl/| adj.|可塑的，易受影响的|Able to be shaped or influenced easily.|The clay was soft and mouldable. 这种黏土很软，可塑性强。|Example: Young children's minds are very mouldable.  小孩子的心灵很容易受影响。

|Synonyms: pliable, malleable, ductile, adaptable, flexible|Antonyms: rigid, inflexible, unyielding, stiff, intractable|adjective
