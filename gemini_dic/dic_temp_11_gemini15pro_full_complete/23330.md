noun
男孩气，孩子气

Her boyishnesses, like climbing trees and playing in the mud, charmed everyone. 她爬树玩泥巴的男孩气概让每个人都着迷。
Synonyms: boyishness, immaturity, childishness, youthfulness
Antonyms: maturity, sophistication, womanliness
词性: 名词

From boyish + -ness.  "Boyish" comes from "boy" (Middle English *boi, of uncertain origin) + "-ish" (Old English -isc, a suffix forming adjectives).  "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), -nis(s), -nyss) is a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition.
