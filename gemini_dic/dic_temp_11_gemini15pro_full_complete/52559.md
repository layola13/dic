disfranchise:/ˌdɪsˈfrænˌtʃaɪz/ | vt. | 剥夺公民权; 剥夺权利 | To deprive someone of the right to vote or other rights of citizenship. |  Many people were disfranchised because of their criminal records. 许多人因为犯罪记录而被剥夺了公民权. | deprive of rights, marginalize | empower, enfranchise | verb
