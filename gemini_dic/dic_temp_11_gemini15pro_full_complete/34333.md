clashing:/ˈklæʃɪŋ/| adj.|冲突的，不协调的|Meeting of opposing forces or ideas; disagreement or conflict.  Describes things that don't go well together, often in terms of style, color, or personality.|Her clashing prints made her look like a walking art disaster. 她不协调的印花让她看起来像一场行走的艺术灾难。|synonyms: conflicting, discordant, jarring, incompatible|antonyms: harmonious, matching, compatible, coordinating|adjective

clashing:/ˈklæʃɪŋ/| n.|冲突，不协调|The act or process of things conflicting or disagreeing.|The clashing of cymbals marked the end of the performance.  钹的碰撞标志着表演的结束。|synonyms: conflict, disagreement, discord, friction|antonyms: harmony, agreement, concord, accord|noun
