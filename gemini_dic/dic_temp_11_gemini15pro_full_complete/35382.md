co-op:/ˈkəʊɒp/ (UK), /ˈkoʊˌɑːp/ (US)| n.|合作社，合作商店，合作公寓|A business or organization owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits.|例句：I buy all my groceries at the local co-op. 我在当地的合作社买所有的食品杂货。

例句：They live in a co-op apartment building. 他们住在合作公寓楼里。|近义词：cooperative, collective|反义词：corporation|词性：名词


co-op:/ˈkəʊɒp/ (UK), /ˈkoʊˌɑːp/ (US)| adj.|合作的|Owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits.|例句：This is a co-op venture between two companies. 这是两家公司之间的合作项目。

例句：They decided to form a co-op farm. 他们决定成立一个合作农场。|近义词：cooperative, joint|反义词：private, individual|词性：形容词


词源: Shortening of cooperative.
