madras:/məˈdræs/, /ˈmædrəs/| n. adj.| 马德拉斯布, 马德拉斯(印度城市)|A light cotton fabric, typically patterned with brightly colored plaid or checks, used for clothing, especially summer wear. Also refers to the city of Chennai in India, formerly known as Madras. |She wore a madras shirt and khaki shorts. 她穿着一件马德拉斯格子衬衫和卡其色短裤。|近义词:gingham, plaid|反义词:None|noun, adjective
