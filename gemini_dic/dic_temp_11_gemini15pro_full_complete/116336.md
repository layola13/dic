masterminds:/ˈmæstərˌmaɪndz/
noun|策划者，主谋，智囊|A person who plans and directs a complex undertaking, especially one that is illegal or secretive. |例句：The mastermind behind the robbery was finally caught. 抢劫案的主谋终于被抓获了。|近义词：architect, planner, strategist, organizer, director|反义词：follower, subordinate|词性:n.

mastermind:/ˈmæstərˌmaɪnd/
verb|策划，主谋，幕后操纵|To plan and direct (a complex undertaking).|例句：He masterminded the whole operation from start to finish. 他从头到尾策划了整个行动。|近义词：orchestrate, engineer, devise, contrive|反义词：follow, obey|词性:v.
