overmournfulnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
过度悲伤，极度悲痛
Excessive mournfulness; the state of being excessively or unduly mournful.
His overmournfulnesses after his pet's death concerned his family. 他对宠物死后过度悲伤，让他的家人很担心。
Her constant overmournfulnesses became a burden to her friends. 她持续的极度悲痛成了她朋友们的负担。
Synonym: excessive grief, extreme sadness
Antonym: joy, happiness
noun
