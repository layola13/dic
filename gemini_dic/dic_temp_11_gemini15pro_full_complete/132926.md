nonelucidations:/nɒnɪˌlʊːsɪˈdeɪʃənz/
noun
无阐明;未解释;缺乏解释
Absence of explanations or clarifications.  A lack of elucidation.  The state of being not elucidated.
His nonelucidations on the matter only deepened the mystery. 他对这件事的未解释只是加深了谜团。
Near synonyms: non-explanation, lack of clarification, obfuscation, mystification
Antonyms: explanation, clarification, elucidation, illumination
词性: 名词

词源:
"Nonelucidations" is derived from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "elucidation" (meaning "explanation" or "clarification").  "Elucidation" itself comes from the Latin word "elucidare," meaning "to make clear" or "to explain."
