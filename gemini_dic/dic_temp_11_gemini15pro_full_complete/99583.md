interinsert:/ˌɪntərˈɪnsɜːrt/
verb
插入，嵌入
to insert between or among other things
He interinserted a comment into the conversation. 他在谈话中插入了一条评论。
近义词: insert, interpolate
反义词: extract, remove
词性:v.
