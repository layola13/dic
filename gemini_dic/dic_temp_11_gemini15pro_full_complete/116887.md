maybush:/ˈmeɪbʊʃ/| n. |山楂树，五月开花的灌木|A shrub that blossoms in May, often specifically the hawthorn. |例句：The maybush was covered in white blossoms. 山楂树上覆盖着白色的花朵。

例句：The scent of the maybush filled the air. 山楂花的香味弥漫在空气中。|近义词：hawthorn, whitethorn|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *maybusk, equivalent to may + bush.  The "may" refers to the month of May, when these shrubs typically bloom.
