gangrel:/ˈɡæŋɡrəl/| adj. n.|流浪的，无家可归的；流浪者，无家可归的人|A gangrel is a person who wanders from place to place, especially a homeless person or vagrant.|The old man lived a gangrel life, sleeping under bridges and begging for food.  这位老人过着流浪的生活，睡在桥下，乞讨食物。|例句：He was a gangrel, wandering from town to town. 他是一个流浪汉，从一个城镇流浪到另一个城镇。|近义词：vagrant, homeless, itinerant, nomadic|反义词：settled, resident|词性：形容词，名词

词源：From Middle English *gangrel, gangrell, from gang (“to go, walk”) +‎ -rel (diminutive suffix).  来自中古英语 *gangrel, gangrell，由 gang（“去，走”）+ -rel（指小后缀）组成。
