commers:/ˈkʌmərz/ | n. | 来者，顾客 |  People who come to a place, especially to buy or sell something. This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.  It is the plural form of "commer," which is itself obsolete. |  In days of yore, the commers to the market were numerous. (古时候，到市场来的人很多。) | Near synonyms: newcomers, arrivals, visitors, customers | Antonyms: leavers, departees | Noun
