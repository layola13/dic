dry-roasted:/ˌdraɪˈroʊstɪd/
adjective
干烤的
roasted without oil or fat.
例句：I prefer dry-roasted peanuts to oil-roasted ones. 我喜欢干烤花生胜过油烤花生。
例句：Dry-roasted nuts are a healthier snack option. 干烤坚果是更健康的零食选择。
近义词：roasted, toasted
反义词：oil-roasted, fried
词性：adjective


dry-roast:/ˌdraɪˈroʊst/
verb
干烤
to roast without oil or fat.
例句：They dry-roast the coffee beans to perfection. 他们把咖啡豆干烤到完美。
例句：You can dry-roast the nuts in a pan or in the oven. 你可以用平底锅或烤箱干烤坚果。
近义词：roast, toast
反义词：fry
词性：verb
