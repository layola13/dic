floras:/ˈflɔːrəz/
| n. |植物群；植物志|The floras of different regions vary greatly. 不同地区的植物群差异很大。|Floras refers to the plants of a particular region or period, listed in a book or other work. |The botanist is studying the floras of the Amazon rainforest.  这位植物学家正在研究亚马逊雨林的植物群。|Synonym: vegetation, plant life|Antonym: fauna|noun
