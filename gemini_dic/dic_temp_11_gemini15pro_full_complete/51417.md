deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/ /dɪˈtɜːrənt/
n.|威慑物，制止物；威慑因素|something that discourages or prevents a certain action|The high price of the tickets is a deterrent to many potential concertgoers.  高昂的票价让很多潜在的演唱会观众望而却步。|deterrents|
deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/ /dɪˈtɜːrənt/
adj.|威慑的，制止的|discouraging or preventing something|The new security measures are proving deterrent to shoplifters.  新的安全措施对商店扒手起到了威慑作用。|deterrent|
deterrents|名词复数|威慑物，制止物；威慑因素|Plural form of deterrent|Stricter laws can act as deterrents to crime.  更严格的法律可以起到威慑犯罪的作用。|deterrents|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēterrent- (stem of dēterrens), present participle of dēterrēre "to frighten from, deter," from dē- "away" + terrēre "to frighten."
