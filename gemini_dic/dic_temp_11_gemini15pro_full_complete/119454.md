micromanager:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌmænɪdʒər/| n. |微观管理者，事无巨细的管理者|A micromanager is someone who observes and/or controls the work of their subordinates or employees in a detailed and excessive way.|例句：My boss is a micromanager; he checks every single email I send. 我的老板是个事无巨细的管理者；他检查我发的每一封邮件。

例句：Working for a micromanager can be incredibly stressful and stifle creativity.  为一个事无巨细的管理者工作压力会非常大，而且会扼杀创造力。

近义词：control freak, nitpicker, overseer|反义词: delegator, macromanager |词性:noun
