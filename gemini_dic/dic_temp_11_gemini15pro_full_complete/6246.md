amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/
n.
变形虫
a single-celled animal that catches food and moves by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.  Amoebas are either free-living in damp environments or parasitic.
The scientist observed the amoeba under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下观察变形虫。
protozoan, microorganism
multicellular organism
noun
