kickshoe: /ˈkɪkʃuː/
n.
kickshoe; a type of shoe, especially one designed for kicking.  Sometimes used humorously or informally.
The child wore sturdy kickshoes for playing in the park.  这孩子穿着结实的踢鞋在公园里玩。
kickshoe; a shoe used in certain types of dance, particularly clogging or tap dancing.
The dancer's kickshoes created a rhythmic beat on the wooden floor. 舞者的踢鞋在木地板上创造了有节奏的节拍。
Synonyms: shoe, boot, footwear, clogs (if for dancing)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider bare feet or slipper)
