noncustodial:/ˌnɒn.kʌˈstəʊ.di.əl/ | adj. | 非监护权的 |  Relating to a parent who does not have legal custody of a child.  This parent typically still has visitation rights and responsibilities, such as paying child support. |  The noncustodial parent is required to pay child support.  非监护方父母需要支付子女抚养费。 | The noncustodial parent has visitation rights every other weekend. 非监护方父母每隔一周有探视权。 | Synonyms: non-custodial (variant spelling) | Antonyms: custodial | adjective
