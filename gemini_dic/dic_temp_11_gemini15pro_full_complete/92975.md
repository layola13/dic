idiocracy:/ˌɪdɪˈɑːkrəsi/
|n.|
|白痴统治；愚民政治|
|A form of government in which the most stupid people have the most power and influence.  It refers to a society where intelligence and critical thinking are devalued, leading to a decline in the quality of decision-making and overall societal function.|
|The movie "Idiocracy" depicts a future where society has become dumbed down and ruled by the unintelligent.|这部电影“蠢蛋进化论”描绘了一个未来社会，在这个社会里，人们变得愚蠢，被不聪明的人统治。|

|The current political climate feels like a slide towards idiocracy.|当前的政治气候感觉就像滑向愚民政治。|
|近义词：moronocracy, kakistocracy|
|反义词：meritocracy, technocracy|
|词性：noun|

Etymology:
|From idiocy (meaning "extreme stupidity") + -cracy (a suffix denoting a form of government or rule).| 由idiocy（意思是“极度愚蠢”）+ -cracy（表示政府或统治形式的后缀）组成。
