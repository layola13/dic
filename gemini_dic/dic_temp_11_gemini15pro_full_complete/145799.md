overdecorates: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəˌreɪts/
verb
过度装饰
To decorate excessively; to adorn with too many ornaments or embellishments.
The room was overdecorated, with too many knick-knacks and fussy details.  这个房间装饰过度，摆满了太多小摆设和繁琐的细节。
She overdecorated the Christmas tree, and it looked cluttered. 她把圣诞树装饰得太过头了，看起来很杂乱。
近义词: overembellish, overdo
反义词: underdecorate, simplify
词性: verb

over: /ˈoʊvər/
adverb, preposition, adjective
过度，在...之上，结束的
From Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above, beyond".
例句：The game is over. 游戏结束了。
He jumped over the fence. 他跳过了栅栏。
近义词: above, across, beyond
反义词: under, below
词性: adverb, preposition, adjective

decorate: /ˈdɛkəˌreɪt/
verb
装饰
From Latin decorare "to adorn, embellish, beautify," from decus "grace, ornament, dignity, honor".
例句:  They decorated the house for Christmas. 他们为了圣诞节装饰了房子。
She decorated the cake with flowers. 她用花装饰了蛋糕。
近义词: adorn, embellish, ornament
反义词: strip, bare
词性: verb
