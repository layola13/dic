molehill:/ˈmoʊlhɪl/ (US), /ˈməʊlhɪl/ (UK)| n. |鼹鼠丘，小土堆，小事|A small mound of earth thrown up by a mole burrowing underground.  Figuratively, it refers to a minor difficulty or problem.|
例句：
He stumbled over a molehill in the garden. 他在花园里被一个鼹鼠丘绊倒了。

例句：
Don't make a mountain out of a molehill. 不要小题大做。


近义词：mound, bump, trifle, triviality|
反义词：mountain, major issue|
词性:noun
