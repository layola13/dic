missample: /mɪsˈsɑːmpəl/
verb
错误地取样；取样不当
To take a sample that is not representative of the whole.
Researchers missampled the population, leading to inaccurate conclusions. 研究人员对人口进行了错误的抽样，导致结论不准确。
The survey missampled the target demographic, skewing the results.  这项调查错误地抽样了目标人群，导致结果出现偏差。
近义词: misrepresent, distort
反义词: represent accurately
词性: verb
