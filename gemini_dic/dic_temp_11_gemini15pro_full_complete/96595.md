inductional:/ɪnˈdʌkʃənəl/
|adj.|
|感应的；诱导的|
|Relating to or produced by induction (the process of producing an electric or magnetic effect by the influence of a nearby magnet, electric current, or changing magnetic field).|
|The inductional heating system is highly efficient. 感应加热系统效率很高。|

|The inductional charging technology allows for wireless powering of devices.  感应充电技术允许设备进行无线供电。|
|inductive, magnetic|
|non-inductive|
|adjective|
