counterponderate:/ˌkaʊntərˈpɑːndəreɪt/ | v. | 平衡；抵消；使平衡 | to weigh against; to act as a counterpoise to; to counterbalance | The advantages counterponderate the disadvantages. 优点抵消了缺点。 | 近义词: counterbalance, offset, compensate | 反义词: outweigh, overbalance | 词性: 动词

counterponderate:/ˌkaʊntərˈpɑːndəreɪt/ | adj. | 平衡的；抵消的 | counterbalancing |  His careful planning counterponderate the risks involved. 他谨慎的计划抵消了所涉及的风险。| 近义词: counterbalancing, offsetting, compensating | 反义词: outweighing, overbalancing | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contraponderatus, past participle of contraponderare (“to weigh against”), from Latin contra (“against”) + ponderare (“to weigh”), from pondus (“weight”).
