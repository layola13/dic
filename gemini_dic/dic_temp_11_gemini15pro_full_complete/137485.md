nontopographical:/ˌnɒntɒpəˈɡræfɪkəl/ |adj.|非地形学的，不基于地形的|Not related to or based on the physical features of a place.|例句：The painting was nontopographical, focusing more on emotion than accurate depiction of a landscape. 这幅画是非地形学的，更注重情感而不是对景色的准确描绘。

例句：Her novel takes place in a nontopographical dreamscape, a world unbound by physical laws. 她的小说是设定在一个非地形学上的梦境中，一个不受物理定律约束的世界。|近义词：abstract, conceptual, nonrepresentational|反义词：topographical, representational|词性: 形容词
