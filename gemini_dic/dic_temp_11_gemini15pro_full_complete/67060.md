explicator:/ˈɛksplɪˌkeɪtər/ | n. | 解释者，阐释者 |  A person who explains or interprets something, especially a text or work of art. | The literary critic acted as an explicator of the novel's complex themes. 文学评论家充当了这部小说复杂主题的阐释者。| synonyms: interpreter, analyst, commentator, elucidator | antonyms: obfuscator, confuser | noun

explicate:/ˈɛksplɪˌkeɪt/ | v. | 解释，阐释 | To analyze and develop (an idea or principle) in detail. | The professor explicated the poem line by line. 教授逐行解释了这首诗。| synonyms: explain, clarify, interpret, elucidate | antonyms: confuse, obscure, complicate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin explicator, from Latin explicare "to unfold, unravel, explain," from ex- "out" + plicare "to fold."
