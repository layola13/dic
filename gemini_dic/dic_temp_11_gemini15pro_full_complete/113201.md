lurker:/ˈlɜːrkər/ (US), /ˈlɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 潜伏者；窥探者 |Someone who reads discussions in an online forum or social media group but rarely or never participates.  A person who waits hidden in order to attack or do something harmful.| 例句：He's a lurker on several online forums, preferring to read others' posts rather than contribute himself. 他在几个在线论坛潜水，喜欢阅读别人的帖子而不是自己发帖。

例句：The police caught the lurker who had been hiding in the bushes. 警察抓住了躲在灌木丛中的潜伏者。| 近义词：observer, onlooker, reader, follower (in online context) | 反义词：participant, contributor, poster (in online context)| 词性: 名词
