hyperexaltation:/ˌhaɪpərˌɛɡzɔːlˈteɪʃən/ |n.| 过度兴奋，极度亢奋 |A state of extreme exaltation or excitement; excessive elation.| His hyperexaltation after winning the lottery was understandable. 他中了彩票后极度兴奋，这是可以理解的。| Near Synonyms: elation, euphoria, ecstasy, frenzy |Antonyms: depression, dejection, melancholy| noun

hyperexalt:/ˌhaɪpərɪɡˈzɔːlt/ |v.| 过度兴奋，极度 exalt |To exalt or praise excessively.|The media hyperexalted the young athlete after his victory. 媒体过度吹捧这位年轻的运动员在他的胜利之后。| Near Synonyms: glorify, extol, laud |Antonyms: disparage, denigrate, belittle | verb
