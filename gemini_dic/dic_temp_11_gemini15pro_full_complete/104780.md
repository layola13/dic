kazak:/kəˈzæk/, /ˈkɑːzæk/ | n. adj.| 哈萨克人，哈萨克族的；哈萨克语；哥萨克人；哥萨克的 |A member of a Turkic people of central Asia, or their language. Also, a member of a group of predominantly East Slavic-speaking people from Ukraine and Southern Russia, historically known for their horsemanship and military skill. | Example:  The kazak rode his horse across the steppe. 哈萨克人骑着马穿过草原。

Example: She speaks fluent Kazak. 她能说流利的哈萨克语。| 近义词: Cossack (for the East Slavic group) | 反义词:  None | 词性: 名词，形容词
