metalbumin:/ˌmetælˈbjuːmɪn/
n.
变性白蛋白，偏白蛋白
A derivative of albumin, especially one found in some pathological conditions.
Metalbumin is found in the blood of patients with certain liver diseases.  在某些肝病患者的血液中发现了偏白蛋白。
Metalbumin can be detected through specific laboratory tests. 偏白蛋白可以通过特定的实验室测试检测到。
Synonym:  modified albumin, denatured albumin
Antonym:  native albumin (though not a perfect antonym, it represents the unaltered form)
noun
