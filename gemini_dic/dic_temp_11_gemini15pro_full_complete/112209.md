loopholing:/ˈluːphəʊlɪŋ/ | n. |漏洞利用，钻空子|The act of exploiting loopholes, which are ambiguities or omissions in a law or contract, allowing one to evade a rule or obligation without technically violating it.|Tax loopholes are a common target of government reform. 钻税法的空子是政府改革的常见目标。|Synonyms: exploitation, manipulation, circumvention|Antonyms: compliance, adherence|noun

loophole:/ˈluːphəʊl/ | n. |漏洞，空子|An ambiguity or omission in a law or contract, allowing one to evade a rule or obligation without technically violating it.|The lawyer found a loophole in the contract that allowed his client to walk away from the deal. 律师在合同中发现了一个漏洞，使他的客户能够退出交易。|Synonyms: opening, gap, ambiguity|Antonyms: closure, blockage, clarity|noun
