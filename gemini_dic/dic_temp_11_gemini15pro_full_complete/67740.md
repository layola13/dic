eye-watering:/ˈaɪˌwɔːtərɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人瞠目结舌的，贵得吓人的 | Extremely expensive; so high as to cause astonishment or disbelief. | The price of the new sports car was eye-watering. 这辆新跑车的价格贵得吓人。 |  Synonyms: exorbitant, extortionate, prohibitive, steep, astronomical | Antonyms: cheap, affordable, reasonable, inexpensive | adjective

eye-watering:/ˈaɪˌwɔːtərɪŋ/ | adj. | 催人泪下的 | Causing tears to come to the eyes, often because of strong emotion.  | The reunion with her long-lost brother was an eye-watering moment.  与她失散多年的兄弟重逢是一个催人泪下的时刻。 | Synonyms: moving, touching, poignant, tearful, tear-jerking | Antonyms: unemotional, unmoving, indifferent | adjective
