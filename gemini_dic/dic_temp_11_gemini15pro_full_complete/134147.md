nonimpregnated: /ˌnɒnɪmˈprɛɡneɪtɪd/
adjective
未浸渍的，未渗透的
Not impregnated; not saturated or permeated with a substance.
The wood was nonimpregnated and therefore susceptible to rot.  未浸渍的木材因此容易腐烂。
The fabric is nonimpregnated and thus breathable.  这种织物未经浸渍，因此透气。
Synonyms: untreated, unprocessed, raw, pure, virgin
Antonyms: impregnated, saturated, permeated, treated, processed
词性: 形容词
