词源:  This phrase originates from the practice of speakers jotting down notes on their shirt cuffs if they didn't have a notepad handy. Speaking "off the cuff" then came to mean speaking without prepared notes or a script.  这个短语源于演讲者如果没有笔记本，就会在衬衫袖口上草草记下笔记的做法。“off the cuff”的意思就是不用准备好的笔记或讲稿进行演讲。
