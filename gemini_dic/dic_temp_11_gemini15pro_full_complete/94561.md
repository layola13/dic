impotent:/ˈɪmpətənt/| adj.|无能的；阳痿的|Lacking power or ability to act; unable to achieve an erection.|He felt impotent in the face of such overwhelming odds. 他在如此巨大的困难面前感到无能为力。|The medication had a side effect of making him impotent. 这种药物的副作用是让他阳痿。|近义词:powerless, ineffective, incapable, infertile (in males)|反义词:potent, powerful, capable, virile|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *impotent-em,  from Latin *impotens “powerless,” from in- “not” + potens “powerful”
