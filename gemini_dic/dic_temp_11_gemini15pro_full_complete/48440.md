deductor:/dɪˈdʌktər/
n.
扣除者，减除者，演绎者
Someone who deducts something, especially an amount of money.
The deductor claimed a large sum of money from the total.  扣除者从总额中扣除了一大笔钱。
The deductor carefully analyzed the evidence. 演绎者仔细分析了证据。
synonyms: subtractor, inferer, reasoner
antonyms: adder, contributor
noun
