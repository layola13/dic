frequently: /ˈfriːkwəntli/ (US) /ˈfriːkwəntli/ (UK)| adv.|频繁地，经常地|Happening often; at short intervals.|She frequently visits her parents. 她经常去看望她的父母。|近义词: often, regularly, commonly, habitually|反义词: rarely, seldom, infrequently, occasionally|词性:副词

词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French frequent, from Latin frequens, frequent- ‘crowded, numerous, recurring often’, from frequentare (see frequentate).
