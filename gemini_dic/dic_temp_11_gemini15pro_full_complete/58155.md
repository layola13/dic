droughtiness:/ˈdraʊtinəs/| n.|干旱；干燥|The droughtiness of the region has made farming difficult. 这个地区的干旱使得耕作困难。|The prolonged droughtiness caused widespread crop failure. 长期干旱导致了大面积的农作物歉收。|近义词: aridity, dryness|反义词: wetness, humidity|词性:名词

Etymology:  From drought + -iness.  Drought comes from Middle English *droughte*, from Old English *drugaþ* "dryness, drought," related to *dryge* "dry." The suffix *-iness* forms nouns meaning "state or quality of being."
