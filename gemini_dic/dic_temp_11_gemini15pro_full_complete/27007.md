calculatingly:/ˈkælkjuleɪtɪŋli/
adverb
精明地；有算计地；工于心计地
in a way that is carefully planned in order to get an advantage
She smiled calculatingly. 她工于心计地笑了笑。
近义词: shrewdly, craftily, artfully, cunningly
反义词: artlessly, ingenuously, naively
词性: adverb
词源: From calculate + -ing + -ly.  Calculate comes from Latin *calculatus, past participle of calculare "to reckon, compute," from calculus "pebble" (used in reckoning).
