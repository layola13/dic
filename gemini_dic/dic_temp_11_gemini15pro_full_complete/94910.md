in-ground:/ˈɪnˌɡraʊnd/
adjective
地下的；埋地的；嵌入地面的
Installed or placed below ground level.
We have an in-ground swimming pool. 我们有一个地下游泳池。
My parents recently had an in-ground sprinkler system installed. 我父母最近安装了一个地下洒水系统。
近义词:buried, subterranean, underground
反义词:above-ground
词性:adjective

in-ground:/ˈɪnˌɡraʊnd/
noun
地下装置；地埋式装置
Something that is installed or placed below ground level.
The in-ground is perfect for keeping the roots cool.  这种地下装置非常适合保持根部凉爽。
This in-ground is easier to maintain than an above-ground one.  这种地埋式装置比地上装置更容易维护。
近义词:installation, fixture
反义词:above-ground structure
词性:noun
