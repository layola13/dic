heredities: /həˈrɛdətiz/
n.
遗传, 遗传特征
the transmission of traits from one generation to the next.
The study of heredity helps us understand why children resemble their parents.  遗传学研究帮助我们理解为什么孩子像他们的父母。
Heredities can influence a person's susceptibility to certain diseases. 遗传因素会影响一个人对某些疾病的易感性。
genetics, inheritance, legacy
acquired characteristics

词性: 名词 (Noun)
