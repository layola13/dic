cross-platform:/ˈkrɒsˈplætˌfɔːrm/ (US) /ˈkrɑːsˈplætˌfɔːrm/| adj.|跨平台的|Designed to work on different operating systems or devices.| This game is cross-platform, so you can play it on your PC, phone, and console.  这款游戏是跨平台的，你可以在你的电脑、手机和游戏机上玩。|近义词：multi-platform, platform-agnostic, portable|反义词: platform-specific, platform-dependent|词性:形容词

cross-platform:/ˈkrɒsˈplætˌfɔːrm/ (US) /ˈkrɑːsˈplætˌfɔːrm/| n.|跨平台|Software or hardware that works on multiple platforms.|  Cross-platform development is becoming increasingly important in the tech industry.  跨平台开发在科技行业变得越来越重要。|近义词：multi-platform|反义词: single-platform|词性:名词
