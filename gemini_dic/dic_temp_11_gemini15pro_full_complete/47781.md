decanter:/dɪˈkæntər/| n.|醒酒器; 倾注器|A glass container used to serve wine, especially after it has been decanted.  A vessel, usually an ornamental glass bottle, into which wine or other alcoholic beverages are decanted for serving.|He poured the wine into the decanter. 他把酒倒进了醒酒器。|近义词：carafe, flagon|反义词: N/A|noun


decanter:/dɪˈkæntər/| vt.|滗; 倾析|Gradually pour (wine or other liquid) from one container to another, typically in order to separate out sediment.|He carefully decanted the wine into a carafe. 他小心地把酒倒进玻璃水瓶里。|近义词：pour off, siphon|反义词: N/A|verb
