cowbane:/ˈkaʊbeɪn/ | n. |毒芹，水毒芹|A highly poisonous plant (Cicuta maculata) of the parsley family, also known as water hemlock.|例句：Cowbane is one of the most toxic plants in North America. 水毒芹是北美毒性最强的植物之一。

例句：The farmer warned us to keep our livestock away from the cowbane growing near the stream. 农民警告我们要让牲畜远离生长在小溪边的水毒芹。|近义词：water hemlock, poison hemlock, spotted water hemlock|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
