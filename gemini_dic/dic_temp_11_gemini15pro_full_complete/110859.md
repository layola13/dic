lion:/ˈlaɪən/ (US), /ˈlaɪən/ (UK)| n.|狮子|A large, powerfully built cat-like animal with a flowing mane (in males) that is native to Africa and India.|The lion roared loudly. 狮子大声咆哮。|Synonyms: big cat, king of the jungle, predator|Antonyms: prey|noun


lion:/ˈlaɪən/ (US), /ˈlaɪən/ (UK)| adj.|像狮子的; 勇敢的|Resembling or characteristic of a lion; courageous.|He fought with lion-like bravery. 他以像狮子一样的勇敢战斗。|Synonyms: brave, courageous, valiant|Antonyms: cowardly, timid|adjective
