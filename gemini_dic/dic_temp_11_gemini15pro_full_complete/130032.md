non-parametric:/ˌnɒnˌpærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 非参数的 |  Not assuming any specific distribution for the underlying population.  Relating to or involving statistical methods that do not make assumptions about the probability distributions of the variables being assessed. |  We used a non-parametric test because the data was not normally distributed. 我们使用非参数检验，因为数据不是正态分布的。|  Non-parametric methods are often used when the sample size is small. 当样本量较小时，通常使用非参数方法。|近义词: distribution-free | 反义词: parametric | 词性: 形容词
