outjumps:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmps/
verb
超越，跳得比...更远
jumps further or higher than
The athlete outjumps his competitors.  这位运动员跳得比他的竞争对手更远。
surpasses, exceeds, overleaps
falls short of, lags behind
