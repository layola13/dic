inopportunism:/ɪnˌɒpətjuːˈnɪzəm/
n.
不适时；乘人之危；投机取巧
The exploitation of opportunities without regard for moral principles.
He showed his inopportunism by asking for a raise right after the company announced layoffs.
他刚好在公司宣布裁员后要求加薪，这表明了他的投机取巧。

opportunism, exploitation
principle, appropriateness
noun
