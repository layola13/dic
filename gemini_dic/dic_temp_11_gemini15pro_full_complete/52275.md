dichlamydeous:/ˌdaɪkləˈmɪdiəs/
| adj. | 双被的，具有双层花被的 | Having two whorls of floral envelopes in the perianth, namely the calyx (sepals) and the corolla (petals). | The rose is a dichlamydeous flower, displaying both sepals and petals. 玫瑰是一种双被花，同时具有萼片和花瓣。| Example 2: Dichlamydeous flowers are common in many angiosperm families. 双被花在许多被子植物科中很常见。| 近义词: dichlamydeate | 反义词: monochlamydeous, achlamydeous | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *di- (two) + *chlamys* (cloak, mantle) + *-ous* (adjectival suffix).
