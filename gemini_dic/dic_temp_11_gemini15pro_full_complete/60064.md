edvard: /ˈɛdvɑːrd/ | n. |爱德华（男子名）| A male given name, a Scandinavian form of Edward. From the Old English name Eadweard, meaning "wealthy guardian" or "rich protector". It is composed of the elements ead "wealth, prosperity" and weard "guardian, protector".| Example: Edvard Munch was a famous Norwegian painter. 爱德华·蒙克是一位著名的挪威画家。
