depasture:/dɪˈpæstʃər/ | v.  n. | 放牧; 牧场 | To graze (livestock); to feed on growing grass.  A pasture. | The farmer depastured his cattle in the meadow. 农民在草地上放牧他的牛。| The sheep depastured the hillside. 羊群在山坡上吃草。 | 近义词: graze, pasture, feed | 反义词: starve | 词性: 动词, 名词
