cornic:/ˈkɔːrnɪk/ | adj. | 角状的；角的；含角质的 | Resembling a horn; horny. | The cornic layer of the skin is tough.  皮肤的角质层很坚韧。|  The rhino has a cornic horn.  犀牛有角质的角。 | 近义词: horny, keratinous, callous | 反义词: soft, smooth | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *cornīceus, from cornū (“horn”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- (“horn; head”).
