aghastness:/əˈɡæstnɪs/
noun
惊骇；惊愕
The state of being aghast; overwhelming amazement, terror, or horror.
She stared at the scene with utter aghastness. 她惊恐地盯着眼前的景象。
近义词：horror, terror, shock, dismay, amazement
反义词：calmness, composure, tranquility
词性:n.

aghast:/əˈɡæst/
adjective
惊骇的；吓呆的
Filled with sudden fright or horror; shocked.
He was aghast at the news of the accident. 他听到事故的消息吓呆了。
近义词：horrified, shocked, stunned, appalled
反义词：calm, composed, unfazed
词性:adj.
