blazer:/ˈbleɪzər/ (US), /ˈbleɪzə/ (UK)| n.| 运动夹克，正式外套，颜色鲜艳的夹克|A type of jacket, often worn as part of a uniform, or as a smart casual item. It typically features metal buttons and may have a club or school badge.|He always wears a blazer to work. 他总是穿西装外套上班。|Synonyms: jacket, coat, sports jacket|Antonyms: N/A|noun

blazer:/ˈbleɪzər/ (US), /ˈbleɪzə/ (UK)| v.| 熊熊燃烧，闪耀|To burn brightly or intensely.|The fire blazed in the hearth. 火炉里的火熊熊燃烧。|Synonyms: flame, flare, burn|Antonyms: smolder, extinguish|verb
