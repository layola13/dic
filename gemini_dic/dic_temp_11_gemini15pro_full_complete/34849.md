clinked: /klɪŋkt/
verb
发出叮当声；碰杯
made a short ringing sound, typically of glass or metal objects striking each other lightly.
The glasses clinked as we toasted the happy couple. 我们碰杯祝贺这对幸福的夫妇。
近义词: jingled, tinkled, rang
反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound, but perhaps "silenced")
词性: verb


clinked: /klɪŋkt/
adjective
(rarely used)
叮当作响的
having made a clinking sound
(This usage is uncommon.  "Clinking" is more commonly used as a present participle.)
The clinked glasses lay scattered on the table.  叮当作响的玻璃杯散落在桌子上。(This sounds unnatural.  Better: "The clinking glasses...")
近义词: jingling, tinkling
反义词: silent
词性: adjective (rare)
