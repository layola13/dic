mistranscribe:/ˌmɪstrænˈskraɪb/ | v. | 抄写错误；听写错误 | To write or type something incorrectly, especially when copying from another source; to hear and write down something incorrectly. |  He mistranscribed the date of the meeting in his notes. 他在他的笔记中抄错了会议日期。

The court reporter mistranscribed the witness's testimony. 法庭记录员把证人的证词写错了。 | 近义词: miscopy, miswrite, err, blunder | 反义词: transcribe correctly, accurately record | 词性: 动词
