heathendom:/ˈhiːðəndəm/| n.|异教；异教世界；未开化地区|A term used to refer to the lands or people who do not practice Christianity, often with negative connotations of barbarity or savagery. |The missionaries ventured into the heathendom to spread the word of God.  传教士冒险进入异教世界传播上帝的话语。|The Vikings, before their conversion, were considered to be part of the northern heathendom.  维京人在皈依基督教之前被认为是北方异教世界的一部分。|近义词：paganism, idolatry|反义词：Christianity, Christendom|词性: noun
