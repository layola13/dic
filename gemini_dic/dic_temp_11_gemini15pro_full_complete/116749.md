maungy:/ˈmɔːndʒi/ | adj. | 闷闷不乐的，脾气暴躁的；发霉的，有霉味的 | Characterized by a sullen and ill-tempered mood; having a stale, moldy, or musty smell. | The maungy old man yelled at the children playing in his yard. 那个脾气暴躁的老人对着在他院子里玩耍的孩子们大吼大叫。 | The basement had a maungy smell after the flood. 洪水过后，地下室有一股霉味。 | 近义词：sulky, grumpy, musty, moldy | 反义词：cheerful, pleasant, fresh | 词性: 形容词

词源：Probably from *mangy (now usually spelled mangey), influenced by maunder (to grumble). 可能源自 *mangy（现在通常拼写为 mangey），受 maunder（抱怨）的影响。
