neomorph:/ˈniːoʊmɔːrf/| n.|新形态，新形式；（尤指）新表型，新性状|A neomorph is a structure that arises de novo during development and assumes a morphology not previously found in that lineage.|The researchers discovered a neomorph in the fruit fly, a wing structure not present in its ancestors.|近义词:novelty, innovation, mutation|反义词:archetype, ancestral form|noun
