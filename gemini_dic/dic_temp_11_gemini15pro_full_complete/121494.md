misleadingly:/mɪsˈliːdɪŋli/ | adv. | 误导地，迷惑地 | In a way that causes someone to believe something that is not true. | The advertisement was misleadingly worded to make people think the product was much better than it actually was. 该广告的措辞具有误导性，让人们误以为产品比实际好得多。|  The map was misleadingly drawn, making the distance seem shorter than it was. 这张地图画得有误导性，使距离看起来比实际的短。 | synonym: deceptively, falsely, erroneously | antonym: truthfully, accurately, honestly | 词性: 副词
