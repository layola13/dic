meander:/miˈændər/| n. v.| 漫步；曲流；迂回曲折的路线；|A meander is a winding curve or bend in a river.  (曲流是指河流中蜿蜒的曲线或弯道。) |The path meanders through the woods. (这条小路蜿蜒穿过树林。) |近义词：wander, roam, wind, snake, curve|反义词:straighten, rush|词性:名词, 动词
