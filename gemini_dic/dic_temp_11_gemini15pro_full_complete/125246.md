multidimensionalities:/ˌmʌltiˌdaɪmɛnʃəˈnælɪtiz/ | n. | 多维度，多维性 |  The state or quality of having multiple dimensions or aspects. It refers to something that exists in or can be considered from multiple perspectives or levels. | The artist explored the multidimensionalities of human experience in her work.  这位艺术家在她的作品中探索了人类经验的多维性。|  dimensions, complexities, facets, aspects | unidimensionality, simplicity | noun
