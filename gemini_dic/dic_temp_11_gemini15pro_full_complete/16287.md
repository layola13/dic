baptize:/ˈbæptaɪz/ | v. | 施洗，(为...)举行洗礼；命名，称呼 | To perform the religious rite of baptism; to christen; to give a name to. |  例句：The priest baptized the baby with holy water. 牧师用圣水为婴儿施洗。

例句：They baptized the ship "Queen Mary".  他们将这艘船命名为“玛丽皇后”。 | 近义词：christen, name, dedicate | 反义词：N/A | 词性：动词
