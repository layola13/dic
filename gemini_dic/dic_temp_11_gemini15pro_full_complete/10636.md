appropriable:/əˈproʊpriːəbl/
adjective
可挪用的，可占用的
Suitable or available to be taken and used for one's own benefit, typically without permission.
The company's profits were appropriable by the shareholders.  公司利润可由股东挪用。
The abandoned building was appropriable by squatters.  这栋废弃的建筑物可以被擅自占用者占用。
近义词:seizable, confiscatable, usable, available
反义词:unavailable, inappropriate, unsuitable
词性:adjective


词源:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin appropriabilis, from appropriare ‘to make one’s own’, from ad- ‘to’ + proprius ‘own’.
