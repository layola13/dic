garble:/ˈɡɑːrbl/ (US) /ˈɡɑːbl/ (UK)| vt. n.|曲解，歪曲；混淆；（电子信号）失真|to reproduce (a message, sound, or transmission) in a confused and distorted way.|The message was garbled in transmission. 信息在传输过程中失真了。|近义词：distort, confuse, muddle, scramble, jumble|反义词：clarify, elucidate|verb

garble:/ˈɡɑːrbl/ (US) /ˈɡɑːbl/ (UK)| n.|混乱，含混不清的信息|a confused or distorted version of something.|He told a garble of lies. 他说了一堆谎话。|近义词：gibberish, nonsense|反义词：clarity|noun
