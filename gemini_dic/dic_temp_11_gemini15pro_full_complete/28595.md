caregiving:/ˈkɛrˌɡɪvɪŋ/| n.|照护；护理；照顾|The act of providing care for someone who is unable to care for themselves, such as a child, an elderly person, or someone with a disability.|She found caregiving for her elderly parents to be both rewarding and challenging. 她发现照顾年迈的父母既有回报又有挑战性。|近义词：care, nursing, assistance, support|反义词：neglect, abandonment|noun
