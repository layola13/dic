doorstepper:/ˈdɔːrˌstɛpər/| n.|挨家挨户上门推销或宣传的人；上门采访的记者|A person who goes from door to door selling or promoting something, or a journalist who conducts doorstep interviews.|The politician was harassed by doorsteppers throughout his campaign.  这位政治家在整个竞选期间一直受到上门采访记者的骚扰。|
例句：The charity employed doorsteppers to collect donations.  这家慈善机构雇用挨家挨户上门募捐的人。|近义词：canvasser, solicitor|反义词:|noun
