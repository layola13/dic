linse:/ˈlɪnzə/ (US), /ˈlɪnzə/ (UK)| n. |透镜; 晶状体; 小扁豆|A piece of glass or other transparent substance with curved sides for concentrating or dispersing light rays, used singly (as in a magnifying glass) or with other lenses (as in a telescope);  the transparent, biconvex body separating the posterior chamber and the vitreous body of the eye.|例句：The camera's linse was scratched. 相机的镜头被刮花了。

例句：The doctor examined the patient's linse. 医生检查了病人的晶状体。

|近义词：lens (English equivalent),  objective (for camera lenses)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
