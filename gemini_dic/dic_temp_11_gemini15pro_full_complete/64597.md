eruditional:/ˌɛrʊˈdɪʃənl/| adj.|博学的，学识渊博的|Having or showing great knowledge or learning.|例句：She is an erudite and sophisticated woman. 她是一位博学而 sophisticated 的女性。|近义词：learned, scholarly, knowledgeable, wise|反义词：ignorant, uneducated, unlearned|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ērūdītus*, past participle of *ērudīre* "to educate, train, instruct," from *ē-* "out, from" + *rudis* "rough, untrained, unskilled."  Literally meaning "trained out of roughness."
