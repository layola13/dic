/ˈleɪbl/ | n. vt. | 标签，标记；称号，污名 | A small piece of paper, fabric, plastic, or similar material attached to an object and giving information about it.  To attach a label to. |  This wine is of a good label. 这种酒的牌子不错。| synonym: tag, sticker, brand, designation | antonym: unlabeled, unmarked | noun

/ˈleɪbl/ | vt. | 标注，贴标签于；把…称为 | To attach a label to something; to describe someone or something in a particular way, especially unfairly |  He labeled the bottle of medicine. 他给药瓶贴上标签。| synonym: tag, categorize, classify, brand | antonym: mislabel | verb
