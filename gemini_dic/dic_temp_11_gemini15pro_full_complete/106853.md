labourer: /ˈleɪbərər/ (UK) /ˈleɪbərər/ (US)| n. |劳动者，工人|A person who does physical work, especially outdoors. |例句：The labourers worked tirelessly in the fields. 工人们不知疲倦地在田里劳作。

例句：The construction site employed hundreds of labourers. 建筑工地雇佣了数百名工人。|近义词：worker, employee, workman|反义词：employer, boss, manager|词性: noun
