mannequin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ (US), /ˈmænɪkɪn/ (UK)|n.|人体模型；时装模特儿|A jointed model of the human body, used for displaying clothes in shop windows or for drawing or painting.  A person who models clothes professionally.|The mannequins in the store window were dressed in the latest fashions.  商店橱窗里的人体模型穿着最新的时装。|synonyms: dummy, model, lay figure|antonyms: human, person|noun

mannequin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ (US), /ˈmænɪkɪn/ (UK)|n.|时装模特儿|Someone employed to model clothes.|She worked as a mannequin in Paris. 她在巴黎当过时装模特儿。|synonyms: model|antonyms: customer, shopper|noun
