noctambulism: /nɒkˈtæmbjʊlɪzəm/ /nɑːkˈtæmbjəlɪzəm/
n.
梦游症
The act or state of walking or performing other motor acts while asleep.
She suffered from noctambulism as a child and would often wake up in different parts of the house.  她小时候患有梦游症，经常会在房子的不同地方醒来。
He experienced an episode of noctambulism and woke up in the garden. 他经历了一次梦游，在花园里醒来。
sleepwalking, somnambulism
