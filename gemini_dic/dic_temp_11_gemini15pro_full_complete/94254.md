impermanency: /ɪmˈpɜːrmənənsi/ /ɪmˈpərmənənsi/
noun
无常，短暂，易变性
The state or quality of not being permanent; transience.
Everything in life is marked by impermanency.  人生的一切都带有无常的印记。
The impermanency of worldly possessions. 世俗财产的短暂性。
Synonyms: transience, ephemerality, brevity, instability, mutability
Antonyms: permanence, eternity, stability, immutability
词性: 名词
