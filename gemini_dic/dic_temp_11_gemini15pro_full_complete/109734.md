leveraged: /ˈliːvərɪdʒd/ /ˈlɛvərɪdʒd/
| adj. | 利用杠杆的；举债经营的 |  Using borrowed capital for (an investment), expecting the profits made to be greater than the interest payable. |  The company is highly leveraged and vulnerable to interest rate rises.  这家公司负债很高，很容易受到利率上升的影响。 | 近义词: geared, indebted | 反义词: unleveraged, debt-free | 词性: adjective

词源: From the noun "leverage" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Leverage" itself comes from "lever" (a rigid bar used to exert a force) + "-age" (suffix forming nouns). Ultimately from Old French "levier" (to raise).
