hieroglyphist:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪst/ | n. | 象形文字学家；解读象形文字的人 | A person who studies or interprets hieroglyphs. |  Example: The hieroglyphist spent years deciphering the ancient inscription.  这位象形文字学家花了数年时间破译古代铭文。

Example:  The hieroglyphist was able to translate the text on the Rosetta Stone.  这位象形文字学家能够翻译罗塞塔石碑上的文字。 | Synonym:  epigraphist,  paleographer | Antonym: N/A | noun

hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 象形文字的 | Relating to or written in hieroglyphs. | Example:  The hieroglyphic writing on the tomb told the story of the pharaoh's life.  墓穴上的象形文字讲述了法老一生的故事。

Example:  The hieroglyphic symbols were difficult to understand. 这些象形文字符号很难理解。 | Synonym: pictorial, symbolic | Antonym: N/A | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hieroglyphicus, from Greek hieroglyphikos, from hieroglyphein (to inscribe sacred characters) + -ikos (adjectival suffix). Hieroglyphein is from hieros (sacred) + glyphein (to carve).
