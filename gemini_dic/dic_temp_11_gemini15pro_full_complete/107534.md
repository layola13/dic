landgravine: /ˈlændɡreɪviːn/
noun
女伯爵；伯爵夫人
The wife or widow of a landgrave.
The landgravine presided over the festivities. 女伯爵主持了庆祝活动。

The landgravine donated generously to the local orphanage. 女伯爵慷慨地向当地孤儿院捐款。

近义词: countess
反义词: (No direct antonym.  Consider commoner, or a lower-ranking noble title.)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Landgräfin*, feminine form of *Landgraf* ("landgrave"). *Land* means "land" and *Graf* means "count".
