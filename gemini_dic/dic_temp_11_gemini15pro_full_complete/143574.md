outhouse:/ˈaʊtˌhaʊs/| n.|户外厕所，茅厕|A small building, typically located outside a main building, that is used as a toilet.|The old outhouse behind the farmhouse smelled terrible.  农舍后面的老户外厕所气味难闻。|例句：The outhouse was a small wooden structure.  这个户外厕所是一个小木结构。|近义词：latrine, privy, restroom (more modern)|反义词: bathroom (indoor), toilet (indoor)|词性:noun

outhouse:/ˈaʊtˌhaʊs/| n.|附属建筑|A small building separate from but subordinate to a main building, used for storage or some other purpose.|They kept gardening tools in the outhouse. 他们把园艺工具放在附属建筑里。|例句：The outhouse served as a workshop for the carpenter.  这个附属建筑用作木匠的工作室。|近义词：shed, annex, outbuilding|反义词: main building, main house|词性:noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ūt-hūs "out-house," literally "outside house."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *ūz "out" and *hūsan "house."
