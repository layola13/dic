irresponsive:/ˌɪrɪˈspɑːnsɪv/ | adj. | 无反应的，无应答的，不敏感的 | Not responding or reacting to something; insensitive. |  The patient was irresponsive to all stimuli. 病人对所有刺激都没有反应。|  He seemed irresponsive to her pleas for help. 他似乎对她的求助无动于衷。 | 近义词: unresponsive, unreacting, insensitive, indifferent, apathetic | 反义词: responsive, reactive, sensitive, sympathetic | 词性: 形容词

词源: ir- (not) + responsive (reacting quickly or in a positive way)  来自拉丁语 respondere, 意思是 "to answer."
