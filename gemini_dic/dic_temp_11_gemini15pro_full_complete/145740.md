overcrowd:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊd/| v. |过度拥挤，使…过于拥挤|To fill a space with too many people or things.|The classroom was overcrowded with students. 教室里挤满了学生。|近义词：overfill, congest, pack|反义词：desolate, empty|词性:动词
overcrowded:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪd/| adj. |过度拥挤的，过于拥挤|Filled with too many people or things.|The overcrowded train was delayed. 挤满人的火车晚点了。|近义词：overfilled, congested, packed|反义词：desolate, empty|词性:形容词

词源：
From over- + crowd.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "crowd" refers to a large number of people gathered closely together.
