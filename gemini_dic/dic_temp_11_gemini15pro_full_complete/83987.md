half-dazed:/ˌhɑːfˈdeɪzd/ | adj. | 半昏迷的；茫然的 |  Being in a state of partial daze, feeling stunned or confused. |  He wandered around in a half-dazed state after the accident. 事故发生后，他茫然地四处游荡。 |  The medication left her feeling half-dazed and sleepy. 药物让她感到半昏迷，昏昏欲睡。| dazed, stunned, bewildered, confused, disoriented | alert, aware, conscious, focused | adjective
