intercomparison:/ˌɪntərkəmˈpærɪsn/ | n. |相互比较，比对|The intercomparison of different models helps to identify their strengths and weaknesses.  不同模型的相互比较有助于识别它们的优缺点。|例句：The intercomparison of the two datasets revealed significant discrepancies. 两个数据集的比对揭示了显著的差异。

例句：An intercomparison of national education systems can be very informative. 对各国教育体系的比较研究可以提供很多信息。|近义词：comparison, contrast, juxtaposition|反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + comparison.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "comparison" refers to the act of comparing.
