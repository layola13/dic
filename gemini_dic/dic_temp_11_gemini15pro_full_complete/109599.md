letheon: /ˈliːθiɒn/ | n. | 忘忧药，忘却之药 | A mythical potion that induces oblivion or forgetfulness of sorrow.  It is often mentioned in Greek literature. | In the Odyssey, Helen gives Telemachus a drug called nepenthe, often identified with letheon, to soothe his grief.  赫莲娜在《奥德赛》中给忒勒玛科斯一种名为忘忧药的药物，通常被认为是letheon，以抚慰他的悲伤。 |  She hoped the letheon would erase the painful memories. 她希望忘忧药能抹去痛苦的回忆。 | synonyms: nepenthe, oblivion | antonyms: remembrance, memory | noun
