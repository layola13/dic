excubitorium:/ˌɛkskjʊbɪˈtɔːriəm/| n. | 卫兵室，哨所 | A guardhouse or watchtower, especially in a Roman fort.  A place where guards or sentinels kept watch. | The excubitorium offered a panoramic view of the surrounding countryside, allowing the soldiers to spot approaching enemies. 卫兵室提供了周围乡村的全景，让士兵们能够发现逼近的敌人。 The archaeologists unearthed the remains of the excubitorium near the fort's main gate. 考古学家在堡垒正门附近挖掘出了卫兵室的遗迹。 | 近义词：guardhouse, watchtower, sentry post | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like "headquarters" or "barracks" which represent different functional areas within a fort) | 词性: noun
