non-mutual:/ˌnɒn ˈmjuːtʃuəl/ | adj. | 非相互的，单方面的 | Not shared or felt by both or all parties involved. |  Their dislike was non-mutual; she adored him, but he found her annoying. 他们的厌恶并非相互的；她崇拜他，但他觉得她很烦人。 |  The feeling was non-mutual; I was deeply in love, but he felt nothing for me.  这种感觉不是相互的；我深深地爱着他，但他对我毫无感觉。 | 近义词: one-sided, unilateral | 反义词: mutual, reciprocal | 词性: 形容词
