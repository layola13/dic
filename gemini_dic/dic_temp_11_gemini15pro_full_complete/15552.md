baggy:/ˈbæɡi/| adj.|宽松的，袋状的|Loose and hanging in folds.|Her baggy jeans looked like they were about to fall off. 她那条宽松的牛仔裤看起来像是要掉下来了。|The clown wore baggy pants. 小丑穿着宽松的裤子。|近义词：loose, oversized, roomy|反义词：tight, fitted, snug|词性:形容词
baggy:/ˈbæɡi/| n.|宽松的衣服|Loose-fitting clothing.|He prefers baggies for skateboarding. 他喜欢穿宽松的衣服滑板。|She pulled on a baggy and headed to the gym. 她穿上一件宽松的衣服去了健身房。|近义词：sweats, loungewear|反义词：formal wear|词性:名词
