kinetophone:/ˌkaɪˈnɛtəfoʊn/ /ˌkɪˈniːtəfəʊn/
noun|活动电影放映机，早期有声电影|A device combining a motion-picture projector and a phonograph, used for showing sound films.|Edison's kinetophone synchronized moving pictures with sound.|Synonyms: sound projector, talkie machine|Antonyms: silent film projector|n.
