cheered:/tʃɪrd/| v. |欢呼；喝彩；感到高兴|to shout approval or praise; to become more cheerful|The crowd cheered when the team scored.  观众在球队得分时欢呼起来。|近义词：applauded, acclaimed, encouraged, heartened|反义词:booed, jeered, discouraged, disheartened|verb

词源:
From Middle English *cheren, from Anglo-Norman cherir (“to cherish, hold dear, treat with affection, console, comfort, gladden, encourage”). More at cherish.
