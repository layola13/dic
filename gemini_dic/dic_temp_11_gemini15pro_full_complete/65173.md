ethnolinguist:/ˌɛθnoʊˈlɪŋɡwɪst/ | n. | 民族语言学家 | A specialist who studies the relationship between language and culture. | An ethnolinguist might study how the vocabulary of a culture reflects its values. 民族语言学家可能会研究一种文化的词汇如何反映其价值观。

| Synonym: anthropological linguist, linguistic anthropologist | Antonym: N/A | noun
