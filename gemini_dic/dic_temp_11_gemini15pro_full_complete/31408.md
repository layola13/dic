chargesheet:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒʃiːt/ /ˈtʃɑːrdʒˌʃiːt/| n.|控告书，起诉书，告发书|A formal document prepared by the police and submitted to a court, listing the crimes that a person is accused of.|The police filed a chargesheet against the suspect. 警方对嫌疑人提交了控告书。|近义词：indictment, accusation|反义词:acquittal|词性:名词

词源:  From "charge" (accusation) + "sheet" (document).  Essentially a "sheet of charges."
