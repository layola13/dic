hydrophora:/haɪˈdrɒfərə/
n.
水囊,积水,水泡
A medical term referring to the collection of watery fluid in a cavity or sac.  Specifically, it can refer to hydrocele (fluid in the scrotum), hydrocephalus (fluid in the brain), or hydrosalpinx (fluid in the fallopian tube).
Hydrophora can be a serious condition requiring medical attention. 水囊可能是一种需要就医的严重疾病。
The doctor diagnosed the patient with hydrophora. 医生诊断病人患有积水。
Synonyms: edema, swelling, effusion
Antonyms: dehydration, dryness
