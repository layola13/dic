makeshiftness:/ˈmeɪkʃɪftnəs/
n.
临时凑合；权宜之计
The quality of being makeshift; temporary and usually of poor quality.
Due to the unexpected rain, they had to resort to the makeshiftness of using a tarp as a tent.  由于突如其来的大雨，他们不得不临时用防水布当作帐篷。
The makeshiftness of the shelter was evident in its flimsy construction.  这个避难所的临时搭建性在其脆弱的结构中显而易见。
近义词:temporariness, impermanence, improvisation
反义词:permanence, stability, durability
词性:noun

Etymology:  From makeshift + -ness. Makeshift itself comes from the verb phrase "make shift," meaning "to manage with the means at hand."
