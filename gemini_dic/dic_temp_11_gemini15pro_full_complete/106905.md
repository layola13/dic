lacerability:/ˌlæsərəˈbɪləti/
n.
易撕裂性; 易破损性
The quality of being easily torn or ruptured.
The old parchment had a high degree of lacerability. 这张古老的羊皮纸很容易撕裂。
近义词:fragility, tearability, vulnerability
反义词:durability, toughness, strength
词性:noun

lacerable:/ˈlæsərəbl/
adj.
易撕裂的; 易破损的
Capable of being torn or ruptured.
This material is extremely lacerable, so handle it with care. 这种材料极易撕裂，所以要小心处理。
近义词:fragile, tearable, breakable
反义词:durable, tough, strong
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lacerabilis, from Latin lacerare (“to tear, mangle”), from lacer (“torn, mangled”).
