microfilmable:/ˌmaɪkrəʊˈfɪlməbl/ |adj.|可以缩微拍摄的|Suitable for being photographed or recorded on microfilm.|This document is microfilmable due to its small size and clear print. 这份文件由于其尺寸小且印刷清晰，可以进行缩微拍摄。|The library's collection of old newspapers is microfilmable to preserve them. 图书馆的旧报纸收藏可以缩微拍摄以便保存。|photographable, recordable|non-microfilmable|adjective
