interrogates:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪts/| v.|审问，质问，讯问|To ask someone a lot of questions for a long time in order to get information, sometimes in an aggressive way.|The police interrogated the suspect for hours. 警方讯问了嫌疑人几个小时。|近义词：question, examine, grill, probe|反义词:answer, reply|verb (动词)

interrogate:/ɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪt/| v.|询问，查询（计算机或程序）|To get information from a computer or other device.|The program interrogates the database to find the required information. 程序查询数据库以找到所需的信息。|近义词：query, access|反义词:N/A|verb (动词)
