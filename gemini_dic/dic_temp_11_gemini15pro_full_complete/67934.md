facetious:/fəˈsiːʃəs/ /fɪˈsiːʃəs/| adj.|爱开玩笑的，滑稽的，轻浮的|Treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant.|例句：He made a facetious remark about her weight, which she didn't find funny. 他对她的体重开了个轻浮的玩笑，她并不觉得好笑。

例句：His facetious comments were out of place at the funeral. 他在葬礼上的轻浮言论很不合时宜。|近义词：flippant, frivolous, waggish, playful, joking, jocular|反义词：serious, sincere, earnest, solemn|词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *facetiosus*, from facetia (“wit, humor, jest; elegance, refinement”), from facetus (“elegant, fine, courteous; witty, facetious, pleasant”), related to facere (“to do, make”).
