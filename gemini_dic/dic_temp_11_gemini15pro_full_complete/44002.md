criminalized: /ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzd/
verb
使…成为犯罪；宣布…为非法
to make something illegal or treat something as a crime
例句：
The government criminalized drug use. 政府将吸毒定为犯罪。

They criminalized certain forms of political protest. 他们将某些形式的政治抗议定为非法。

近义词：proscribe, outlaw, ban, prohibit
反义词：legalize, decriminalize, permit, allow
词性：v.
