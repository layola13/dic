asphyxiated: /əsˈfɪksieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 窒息的，闷死的 |  Deprived of oxygen, usually to the point of unconsciousness or death.  Caused by an interruption of breathing due to suffocation (such as from drowning or being buried alive), strangulation, or chemical asphyxiants such as carbon monoxide. | The firefighters rescued the asphyxiated family from the smoke-filled house. 消防队员从充满烟雾的房子里救出了窒息的一家人。| The victim was found asphyxiated in the garage with the car engine running. 受害者被发现死在车库里，汽车引擎还在运转。 | 近义词：suffocated, choked, smothered | 反义词: resuscitated, revived | 词性: 形容词


asphyxiate: /əsˈfɪksieɪt/ | v. | (使)窒息，(使)闷死 | To cause someone to become unconscious or die due to lack of oxygen. |  The fumes from the fire asphyxiated several people. 火灾产生的烟雾使几个人窒息。| Don't let the baby play with the plastic bag; they could asphyxiate themselves. 不要让婴儿玩塑料袋；他们可能会窒息而死。 | 近义词：suffocate, choke, smother | 反义词: resuscitate, revive | 词性: 动词


词源: From Late Latin *asphyxiatus, past participle of asphyxiare, from Greek asphyxia "stopping of the pulse," from a- "without" + sphyxis "pulse."
