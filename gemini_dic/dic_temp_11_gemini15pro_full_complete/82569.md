guaranty:/ˈɡærənti/ | n. vt.| 保证，担保；保证书，担保物 | a formal promise that something will be done, especially that a product will be repaired or replaced if it does not work properly | The store gave a guaranty that the television would work for at least five years.  这家商店保证这台电视至少能用五年。 |近义词：warranty, assurance, pledge |反义词：breach, denial| noun, verb
