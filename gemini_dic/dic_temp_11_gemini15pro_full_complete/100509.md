intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/
verb
干预，介入
come between so as to prevent or alter a result or course of events.
例句：The United Nations intervened to stop the war. 联合国介入以阻止战争。

例句：She intervened in the argument between her two sons. 她介入到两个儿子之间的争论中。

近义词：interfere, mediate, arbitrate
反义词：ignore, abstain, withdraw
词性：v.


intervened:/ˌɪntərˈviːnd/
verb
(intervene的过去式和过去分词) 干预，介入
past tense and past participle of intervene.
例句：The teacher intervened when the two students began to fight. 当两个学生开始打架时，老师介入了。

例句：He intervened on behalf of his friend. 他为了朋友的利益进行了干预。

近义词：interfered, mediated, arbitrated
反义词：ignored, abstained, withdrawn
词性：v.
