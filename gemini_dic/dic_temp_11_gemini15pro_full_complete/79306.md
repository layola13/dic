glazy:/ˈɡleɪzi/| adj.|呆滞的，无神的，光滑的， покрытый глазурью|Covered with a smooth, shiny surface; having a dull or unfocused look in the eyes.  Lacking liveliness or expression.|The donuts were covered in a glazy icing.  甜甜圈上覆盖着光滑的糖霜。

His eyes were glazy with exhaustion. 他的眼神因疲惫而呆滞。|近义词：glossy, shiny, smooth, dull, unfocused, lifeless|反义词：rough, matte, alert, lively|词性:adjective
