outing:/ˈaʊtɪŋ/ | n. | 外出，旅行; 短途旅游; | An excursion, especially for pleasure or recreation. | We went on a family outing to the beach. 我们全家去海滩玩了一趟。| synonyms: excursion, trip, expedition, jaunt, picnic | antonyms: stay, homebody | noun

outing:/ˈaʊtɪŋ/ | n. | 公开; 揭露 | The act of publicly revealing a secret, typically about someone's sexuality or gender identity. (This usage can be considered controversial.) | The outing of the celebrity caused a media frenzy.  这位名人的公开身份引起了媒体的狂热。| synonyms:  disclosure, revelation, exposure | antonyms: concealment, hiding | noun
