khakilike:/ˈkɑːkiˌlaɪk/| adj.|卡其色的，像卡其布的|Resembling khaki in color or appearance.  Having a dull brownish-yellow color similar to khaki fabric.|The walls were painted a khakilike color. 墙壁被漆成了卡其色。|近义词：khaki-colored, beige, tan, sand-colored|反义词: (No direct antonym for a color, but could use contrasting colors like) black, white, blue|词性: adjective
