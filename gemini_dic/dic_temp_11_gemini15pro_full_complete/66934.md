expensed:/ɪkˈspɛnst/| v. |报销，支出|To record a cost or payment as an expense in accounting.  To charge (something) as an expense.|The company expensed the cost of the new computers. 公司将新电脑的费用报销了。|Synonyms: charged, paid out, disbursed|Antonyms: credited, deposited, saved|verb

expensed:/ɪkˈspɛnst/| adj. |已报销的，已支出的| Treated as an expense. |The expensed items were listed in the financial report. 已报销的项目列在财务报告中。|Synonyms: paid, covered, reimbursed|Antonyms: unpaid, outstanding, due|adjective
