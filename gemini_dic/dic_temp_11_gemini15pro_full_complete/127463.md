necessary:/ˈnesəseri/ | adj. | 必要的，必需的 | Essential, indispensable, required; something that must be done or present |例句：Food is necessary for survival.  食物是生存所必需的。

例句：A valid passport is necessary for international travel.  有效的护照是国际旅行所必需的。| 近义词：essential, indispensable, required, vital, crucial | 反义词：unnecessary, optional, superfluous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old French *necessarie (Modern French nécessaire), from Latin necessarius (“unavoidable, indispensable, necessary”), from necesse (“inevitable, necessary”).
