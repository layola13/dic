auto-rickshaw:/ˈɔːtəʊ ˈrɪkʃɔː/ (UK) /ˈɔːtoʊ ˈrɪkʃɑː/ (US)| n. |自动人力车，三轮摩托车|A small, three-wheeled motorized vehicle used as a taxi in many South Asian and Southeast Asian countries.|
例句：I took an auto-rickshaw to the market. 我坐了一辆自动人力车去市场。

例句：Auto-rickshaws are a common form of transportation in India. 在印度，自动人力车是一种常见的交通工具。

近义词：tuk-tuk, three-wheeler|反义词: car, taxi, bus |词性: noun
