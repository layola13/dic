deprovincialize:/diːprəˈvɪnʃəlaɪz/
verb
使不狭隘；使开阔眼界；去除地方偏见
To free from provincialism; to broaden the outlook or experience of.
The university experience deprovincialized her, opening her eyes to different cultures and ways of life.  大学的经历让她开阔了眼界，接触了不同的文化和生活方式。
Travel can deprovincialize a person by exposing them to new ideas and perspectives.  旅行可以让一个人接触到新的想法和观点，从而摆脱狭隘。
Synonyms: broaden, enlighten, liberalize, cosmopolitanize
Antonyms: narrow, restrict, confine, insulate
词性: 动词
