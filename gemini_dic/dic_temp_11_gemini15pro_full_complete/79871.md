gnarliness:/ˈnɑːrlɪnəs/| n. |粗糙；扭曲；困难；棘手|The gnarliness of the old tree's branches was impressive.  这棵老树枝干的粗糙扭曲令人印象深刻。| English Explanation: The quality or state of being gnarled; twisted, knotty, and rough.  Often used figuratively to describe something complex, difficult, or challenging. |例句：The gnarliness of the problem made it difficult to solve. 这个问题的棘手性使得它难以解决。

例句：The surfer admired the gnarliness of the waves.  冲浪者赞叹海浪的汹涌澎湃。|近义词：complexity, difficulty, roughness, twistedness|反义词:simplicity, smoothness, ease|词性:noun
