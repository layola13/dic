histogram:/ˈhɪstəɡræm/| n. |直方图，柱状图|A diagram consisting of rectangles whose area is proportional to the frequency of a variable and whose width is equal to the class interval.|The histogram clearly shows the distribution of student scores.|近义词：bar graph, bar chart|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *histos* (μαστός, "mast, beam, loom") + *gramma* (γράμμα, "letter, writing, drawing")
