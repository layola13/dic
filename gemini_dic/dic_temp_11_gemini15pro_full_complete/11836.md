arrested:/əˈrɛstɪd/| adj. v. (past participle of arrest)|逮捕的，被捕的；停止的，停滞的|Stopped or brought to a standstill; seized by legal authority.|The police arrested the suspect. 警察逮捕了嫌疑人。|Synonyms: apprehended, captured, detained, seized|Antonyms: released, freed|verb (past participle)

arrested:/əˈrɛstɪd/| adj. |被阻止的，抑制的|Slowed down or stopped in its progress or course; attracting attention.|The disease caused arrested development in the child. 这种疾病导致了孩子的发育停滞。|Synonyms: hindered, impeded, obstructed, checked|Antonyms: promoted, advanced, accelerated|adjective
