idealizers:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzərz/
n.
理想化者；完美主义者
People who idealize others often overlook their flaws. 理想化别人的人常常忽视他们的缺点。
She saw him as a hero, one of her many idealizers. 她把他视为英雄，她众多理想化者之一。
synonyms: romanticizers, admirers, idolizers
antonyms: critics, detractors, realists
noun

idealize:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪz/
v.
理想化；将…理想化
He tends to idealize his childhood memories. 他倾向于理想化他的童年记忆。
The movie idealizes the lives of gangsters. 这部电影美化了黑帮的生活。
synonyms: romanticize, glorify, exalt
antonyms: vilify, denigrate, criticize
verb

idealized:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzd/
adj.
理想化的
He has a very idealized view of marriage. 他对婚姻抱有非常理想化的看法。
The painting presented an idealized version of the countryside. 这幅画呈现了乡村的理想化版本。
synonyms: romanticized, glorified, unrealistic
antonyms: realistic, pragmatic, down-to-earth
adjective

idealization:/aɪˌdiːələˈzeɪʃən/
n.
理想化
The idealization of rural life is a common theme in literature.  乡村生活的理想化是文学中的一个常见主题。
Her idealization of him blinded her to his faults. 她对他的理想化使她对他的缺点视而不见。
synonyms: romanticization, glorification
antonyms: realism, pragmatism
noun
