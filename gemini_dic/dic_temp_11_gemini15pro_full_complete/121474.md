miskin:/ˈmɪskɪn/| adj.|可怜的，不幸的；贫穷的|Poor or wretched; lacking the necessities of life.  Derived from Middle English *meskeen, from Old French meschin, ultimately from Late Latin miskinus, of uncertain origin, possibly from Arabic miskīn "poor, humble."|He looked so miskin standing there in the rain. 他站在雨里，看起来真可怜。|近义词：poor, needy, destitute, impoverished|反义词：rich, wealthy, affluent|词性:形容词
