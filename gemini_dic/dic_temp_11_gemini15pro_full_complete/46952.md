darrain:/dəˈreɪn/
verb
中文翻译:决斗；对决；放干（沼泽等的水）
英文解析: (archaic) To settle an issue or contest by duel or battle; to determine or decide by combat. Also, to drain or empty (as a marsh).
例句：They agreed to darrain the matter on the field of honor. 他们在荣誉的战场上同意决斗来解决此事。
例句：The engineers worked to darrain the swamp. 工程师们努力将沼泽的水排干。
近义词：contest, battle, fight, drain, empty
反义词：fill, flood, avoid, evade
词性:v.
