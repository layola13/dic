co-determinant:/ˌkoʊdɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/ | n. |共同决定因素；共决因素|A factor that jointly determines or influences an outcome, along with other factors.  It refers to a system of joint workplace decision-making by management and workers, particularly in Germany.|  Germany's system of co-determination gives workers significant influence over company decisions. 德国的共同决定制度赋予工人对公司决策的重大影响力。|Synonyms: contributing factor, influential factor, joint factor|Antonyms: independent factor, sole determinant|noun


co-determination:/ˌkoʊdɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |共同决定；共决|The practice or principle of joint decision-making by management and employees, especially as practiced in Germany.| Co-determination ensures that employees' voices are heard in company strategy. 共同决定制度确保员工的声音在公司战略中得到倾听。|Synonyms: joint decision-making, shared governance, worker participation|Antonyms: autocracy, unilateral decision-making|noun
