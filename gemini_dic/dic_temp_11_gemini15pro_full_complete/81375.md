graptolites: /ˈɡræptəˌlaɪts/ | n. | 笔石 | Extinct marine colonial animals of the Paleozoic era, forming branching, fan-shaped or other colonies of tiny individuals.| Graptolites are important index fossils for dating Paleozoic rocks. 笔石是测定古生代岩石年代的重要标准化石。| synonyms: fossils, index fossils | antonyms: N/A | noun

graptolite: /ˈɡræptəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 笔石 | A single organism or zooid of a graptolite colony. | Each graptolite in the colony had a small cup-like structure.  群体中的每个笔石都有一个小的杯状结构。| synonyms: zooid, fossil | antonyms: N/A | noun
