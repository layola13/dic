honeyednesses:/ˈhʌnidnɪsɪz/
n.
甜言蜜语；甜蜜；奉承
Excessive sweetness or flattery.
Her honeyednesses eventually became tiresome. 她甜腻的奉承最终变得令人厌烦。
Synonyms: sweetness, flattery, blandishments, compliments
Antonyms: bitterness, harshness, criticism, rebuke
Noun

Etymology:
From honey + -ed (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns) + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Old English hunig.
