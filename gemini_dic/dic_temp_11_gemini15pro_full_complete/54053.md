disdainfulness:/dɪsˈdeɪnfʊlnəs/
n.
轻蔑，鄙夷
The state of being disdainful; a feeling or show of contempt or scorn.
Her disdainfulness towards the proposal was evident in her tone. 她对这个提议的轻蔑之情从她的语气中就能明显听出来。
近义词：contempt, scorn, arrogance, haughtiness, superciliousness
反义词：respect, admiration, humility, modesty
词性: 名词
词源: From disdainful + -ness.  Disdainful from disdain + -ful. Disdain from Old French desdeignier, from des- (expressing negation) + deignier "to deign," from Latin dignari "to deem worthy."
