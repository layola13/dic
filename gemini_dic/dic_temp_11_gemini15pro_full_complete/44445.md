cross-sectioning:/ˌkrɒsˈsɛkʃənɪŋ/ /ˌkrɔːsˈsɛkʃənɪŋ/
|n.| 横截面分析，横截面化，制作横截面|The process of cutting something in half to view its internal structure.| Geologists performed cross-sectioning on the rock sample to analyze its composition. 地质学家对岩石样本进行了横截面分析，以分析其成分。| cross section, sectioning |synthesis, reconstruction|noun
cross-section:/ˈkrɒsˌsɛkʃən/ /ˈkrɔːsˌsɛkʃən/
|n.|横截面，截面；典型；代表|A surface or shape that is revealed when something is cut through; a representative sample of a larger group.|The cross-section of the tree trunk showed its growth rings. 树干的横截面显示了它的年轮。 The survey provided a cross-section of public opinion. 这项调查提供了一个公众舆论的典型。| section, profile, sample| longitudinal section, exterior|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cross-" + "sectioning". "Cross-" meaning across or transverse, from Old Norse "kross," ultimately from Latin "crux" (cross). "Sectioning" is the present participle of "section," from Late Latin "sectionem," meaning "a cutting," from "secare" (to cut).
