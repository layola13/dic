halloweens:/ˌhæləˈwiːnz/ | n. | 万圣节节日(复数形式，通常不使用) |  The word "halloweens" is grammatically correct as the plural of "Halloween," but it's uncommonly used. Most people simply refer to multiple instances of the holiday as "Halloweens" only when discussing specific dates across multiple years, like "the Halloweens of 2020 and 2021."  | Example: We had great costumes for the Halloweens of our childhood. 我们童年时期的万圣节都有很棒的服装。 | Synonym: (Singular) Halloween | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
