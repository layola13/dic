clawing:/ˈklɔːɪŋ/ (US) /ˈklɔːɪŋ/ (UK)| v. n. adj.| 抓，搔，爬，挣扎，拼命争取 |The cat was clawing at the door. 猫在抓门。|近义词：scratching, scraping, grabbing, clutching, fighting|反义词：releasing, letting go|词性: 动词现在分词/名词/形容词

词源: From Middle English *clawen, from Old English clawian (“to claw, scratch, scrape”), from Proto-Germanic *klawōną (“to claw”). Cognate with Dutch klauwen (“to claw”), German klauen (“to claw, steal”), Swedish klå (“to claw, scratch”).  The noun form developed later.
