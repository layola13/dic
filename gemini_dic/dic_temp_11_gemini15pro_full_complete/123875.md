moratory:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ /ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːri/ | n. | 暂停，中止，延期 | A temporary suspension of an activity. |  The government imposed a moratorium on nuclear testing. 政府宣布暂停核试验。

The bank declared a moratorium on loan repayments. 银行宣布暂停偿还贷款。

近义词: suspension, postponement, halt, freeze, ban

反义词: continuation, resumption, commencement

词性: noun
