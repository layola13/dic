involucel:/ˌɪnvəˈluːsəl/| n. |小总苞；次级总苞|A secondary involucre, as one surrounding a partial umbel in the Umbelliferae.|The involucel of this plant has narrow bracts.|近义词：bracteole, bractlet|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly the main involucre)|noun
