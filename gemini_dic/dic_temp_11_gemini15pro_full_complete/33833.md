circumlocutional:/ˌsɜːrkəmləˈkjuːʃənl/ /ˌsɜːrkəˌloʊˈkjuːʃənl/
adjective
迂回的，绕圈子的
Expressed in a roundabout way; wordy and indirect.  Avoiding getting straight to the point.
He gave a circumlocutional explanation of his lateness, blaming traffic, a flat tire, and road closures, when the truth was he simply overslept. 他拐弯抹角地解释他迟到的原因，怪罪交通、爆胎和道路封闭，而事实是他只是睡过头了。
The politician's circumlocutional response avoided directly answering the reporter's question.  这位政治家的迂回回应避免了直接回答记者的问题。

近义词:wordy, verbose, periphrastic, indirect, evasive, rambling
反义词:concise, succinct, direct, straightforward
词性:adjective
