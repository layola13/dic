ek:/ɛk/ | n. | eke (古语, 很少使用) | A small addition or supplement. | He managed to eke out a living by selling vegetables. 他靠卖菜勉强维持生计。 | synonyms: supplement, addition, bit, scrap | antonyms:  deduction, subtraction | noun

ek:/ik/ (dialectal, chiefly Scotland) | v. |  增加，补充(尤指少量地) |  She eked out her meager income by taking in laundry. 她靠帮人洗衣服来补充她微薄的收入。 | synonyms: supplement, augment, stretch, extend | antonyms: decrease, diminish, reduce | verb
