hang-glider:/ˈhæŋ ɡlaɪdər/| n.|悬挂式滑翔机|A light aircraft designed to be flown while hanging suspended from it.|He went hang-gliding over the cliffs. 他在悬崖上玩悬挂式滑翔。|近义词：glider|反义词:|名词

hang-glide:/ˈhæŋ ɡlaɪd/| v.|玩悬挂式滑翔|To fly using a hang-glider.|She loves to hang-glide on weekends. 她喜欢在周末玩悬挂式滑翔。|近义词：glide|反义词:|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hang" + "glider".  "Hang" in this context refers to the pilot's suspended position. "Glider" refers to the unpowered aircraft.
