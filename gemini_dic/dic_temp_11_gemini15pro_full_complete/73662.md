forgeman:/ˈfɔːrdʒmən/ | n. | 锻工；铁匠 | A worker in a forge. | The forgeman hammered the red-hot metal into shape. 锻工把烧红的金属锤打成形。|  Synonyms: blacksmith, smith, farrier | Antonyms: None | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fyrgesmið (attested only as fyrgesmiþþe, dative), from fyrg "forge" + smið "smith."
