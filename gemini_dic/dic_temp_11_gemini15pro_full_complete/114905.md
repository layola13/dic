man-up:/ˌmæn ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb | 振作起来；拿出男子气概；勇敢面对 | To behave in a traditionally masculine and brave way, often in the face of adversity or challenge.  | Example: He needs to man-up and take responsibility for his actions. 他需要振作起来，对自己的行为负责。

Example:  "Stop whining and man-up!" she told him. “别抱怨了，拿出点男子气概！” | 近义词: toughen up, be brave, face the music, take responsibility, grow up | 反义词: wimp out, chicken out | 词性: 短语动词
