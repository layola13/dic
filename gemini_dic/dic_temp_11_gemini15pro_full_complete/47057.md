dateableness:/ˈdeɪtəbəlnəs/
n.
可约会性，适合约会程度
The quality of being suitable for a date; attractiveness as a potential romantic partner.
Her dateableness increased after she got a new haircut.  她剪了新发型后，她的可约会性提高了。
He questioned his own dateableness after a series of unsuccessful dates.  在一系列约会失败后，他开始质疑自己的可约会性。
近义词：attractiveness, desirability, eligibility
反义词：undesirability, unattractiveness
