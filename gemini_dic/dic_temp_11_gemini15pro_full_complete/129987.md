non-mutable:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可变的 | Not able to be changed after creation.  |  Example: Strings in Python are non-mutable, meaning you cannot change individual characters after the string is created. Python中的字符串是不可变的，意味着字符串创建后您无法更改单个字符。

Example:  Tuples are non-mutable data structures in Python.  Python中的元组是不可变的数据结构。 | Synonyms: immutable, unchangeable, fixed, constant | Antonyms: mutable, changeable, variable | Adjective
