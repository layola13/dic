natives:/ˈneɪtɪvz/| n.|本地人，土著人|People who were born in a particular place are natives of that place.  出生在特定地方的人是那个地方的本地人。|The natives were friendly and welcoming. 当地人很友好，热情好客。|近义词：inhabitants, residents, locals, aborigines|反义词：foreigners, immigrants, aliens|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *natif*, from Old French *natif* (masculine), *native* (feminine), from Latin *nātīvus* (“born, native”), from *nātus*, perfect passive participle of *nāscor* (“to be born”).
