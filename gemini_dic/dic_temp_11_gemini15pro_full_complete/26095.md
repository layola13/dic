businesslike:/ˈbɪznəsˌlaɪk/| adj.|务实的；有效率的；公事公办的|Efficient and practical, like a person dealing with business.|She adopted a businesslike approach to the problem. 她用务实的方法处理这个问题。|近义词：efficient, practical, professional, methodical, systematic|反义词：inefficient, unprofessional, casual, disorganized|词性:形容词

Etymology:
business + like.  "Business" comes from the Old English "bisignes" meaning "care, anxiety, occupation." "Like" comes from the Old English "gelic" meaning "similar, resembling."
