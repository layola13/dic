buffeting:/ˈbʌfɪtɪŋ/
n.|冲击，打击，震动|The small boat was subjected to a severe buffeting from the waves. 小船受到海浪的猛烈冲击。|
近义词：battering, pounding, thrashing
反义词：protecting, shielding
词性:名词

buffeting:/ˈbʌfɪtɪŋ/
v.|冲击，打击，反复拍打|(present participle of buffet)  The wind was buffeting the trees. 风猛烈地吹打着树木。|
近义词：battering, pounding, thrashing
反义词：protecting, shielding
词性:动词

词源: From Old French buffeter "to strike, slap," of imitative origin.
