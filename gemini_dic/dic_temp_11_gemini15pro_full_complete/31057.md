chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/ (US), /ˈtʃæmfə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |倒角，斜切；|A chamfer is a small angled surface formed by cutting off the edge or corner of something. As a verb, it means to create such a surface. |The carpenter chamfered the edges of the table. 木匠将桌子的边缘倒角。|近义词：bevel, slope, cant|反义词：round, curve|noun, verb

chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/ (US), /ˈtʃæmfə(r)/ (UK)| v. |倒角，斜切；|To cut away a corner or edge of something at an angle. |He chamfered the legs of the chair to give it a more elegant look. 他把椅子的腿倒了角，使它看起来更优雅。|近义词：bevel, slope, cant|反义词：round, curve|verb
