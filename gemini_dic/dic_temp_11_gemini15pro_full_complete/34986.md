clogging: /ˈklɒɡɪŋ/ (US) /ˈklɒɡɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |阻塞；堵塞；障碍物|The act of becoming blocked or filled with something that prevents movement or flow.  The clogging of the arteries can lead to heart disease. 动脉阻塞会导致心脏病。|近义词：blockage, obstruction, obstruction|反义词: clearing, unclogging|noun

clog: /klɒɡ/ (US) /klɒɡ/ (UK)| v. |阻塞；堵塞|To block or become blocked with something that prevents movement or flow.  The drain is clogged with hair.  排水管被头发堵住了。|近义词：block, obstruct, jam|反义词: clear, unclog|verb

clogged: /klɒɡd/ (US) /klɒɡd/ (UK)| adj. |阻塞的；堵塞的|Blocked or filled with something that prevents movement or flow. The pipes are clogged. 管道堵塞了。|近义词：blocked, obstructed, jammed|反义词: clear, unclogged|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cloggen*, from Old Norse *klakki* ("lump, block of wood")
