noncosmically:/nɒnˈkɒzmɪkli/
adverb
非宇宙地； 世俗地； 局部地
Relating to earthly or human matters rather than the universe or cosmos; not cosmic.
The problem, viewed noncosmically, was simply one of logistics. 从世俗的角度来看，这个问题只是一个后勤问题。
Near synonyms: earthly, worldly, mundane, secular, terrestrial, temporal, sublunary
Antonyms: cosmic, universal, global
词性: 副词
