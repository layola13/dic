overscrupulosity:/ˌoʊvərˌskruːpjəˈlɒsɪti/
n.
过度谨慎；过分小心；过于拘泥
excessive scrupulousness; extreme conscientiousness and concern with moral correctness.
He suffered from overscrupulousity about hygiene. 他对卫生过于谨慎。
近义词：conscientiousness, meticulousness, punctiliousness
反义词：carelessness, negligence, recklessness
词性：noun

overscrupulous:/ˌoʊvərˈskruːpjələs/
adj.
过度谨慎的；过分小心的；过于拘泥的
excessively scrupulous; extremely conscientious and concerned with moral correctness.
She is overscrupulous in her attention to detail. 她对细节过于拘泥。
近义词：conscientious, meticulous, punctilious
反义词：careless, negligent, reckless
词性：adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *overscrupulosus, from Latin over- + scrupulosus (“sharp, pointed, rough, prickly; critical, precise, conscientious, scrupulous”), from scrupulus (“small sharp stone; uneasiness, anxiety, scruple”). Equivalent to over- +‎ scrupulous.
