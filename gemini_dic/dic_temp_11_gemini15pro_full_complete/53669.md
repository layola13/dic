discipled:/ˈdɪsəplɪnd/| adj.|自律的，受过训练的|Showing control and order in one's behavior and way of life; adhering to a strict code of conduct.|Example: She is a disciplined athlete, adhering to a rigorous training schedule. 她是一位自律的运动员，坚持严格的训练计划。|Example: The disciplined student consistently completed all of his homework assignments.  这个自律的学生总是完成所有的家庭作业。|近义词：controlled, self-controlled, rigorous, strict, orderly, methodical|反义词:undisciplined, uncontrolled, lax, chaotic|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "disciple" (pupil, follower), from Old French "disciple," from Latin "discipulus," meaning "pupil, learner."  The "-ed" suffix transforms the noun into an adjective indicating the possession of the quality of being a disciple, hence disciplined, meaning trained, controlled, or exhibiting self-control.
