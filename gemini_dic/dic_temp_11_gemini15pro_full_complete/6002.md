amd:/ˈeɪ ɛm diː/| n. |高级微设备（Advanced Micro Devices）|A semiconductor company that develops computer processors and related technologies.  It is a major competitor of Intel. |例句：I just built a new computer with an AMD Ryzen processor. 我刚用AMD Ryzen处理器组装了一台新电脑。

例句：AMD's graphics cards are becoming increasingly popular. AMD的显卡越来越受欢迎了。|近义词：competitor, rival (in the context of Intel) |反义词: Intel (primarily)|词性:noun
