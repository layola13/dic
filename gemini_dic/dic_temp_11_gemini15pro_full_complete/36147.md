coexistences:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstənsɪz/
n.
共存；共处；和平相处
The state or fact of existing at the same time or in the same place.
例句：The coexistence of different cultures in a society can enrich its development.  一个社会中不同文化的共存可以丰富其发展。

例句：Peaceful coexistence between nations is essential for global stability. 国家间的和平共存对全球稳定至关重要。

近义词：cohabitation, living together, existing together
反义词：conflict, antagonism, incompatibility
词性: 名词
