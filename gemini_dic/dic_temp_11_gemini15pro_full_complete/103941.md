judicable:/ˈdʒuːdɪkəbl/ | adj. | 可判决的；可裁定的；可审理的 | Capable of being judged or settled by a court of law or other tribunal.  Relating to a matter that falls within the jurisdiction of a court. |  This is a judicable issue and should be brought before the courts.  这是一个可裁决的问题，应该提交法院审理。 |  justiciable, actionable | nonjudicable, unjusticiable | adjective
