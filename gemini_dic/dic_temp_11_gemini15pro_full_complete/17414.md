beaminess:/ˈbɪːminəs/| n.| 光辉；容光焕发；喜气洋洋|The quality of being radiant, as with happiness or good health.|Her face was full of beaminess after she received the good news. 她收到好消息后，脸上充满了喜悦的光芒。|Synonyms: radiance, luminosity, glow, brilliance|Antonyms: dullness, gloominess|noun
Etymology:  From beam + -i- + -ness.  Beam meaning a ray of light, extended to mean a radiant smile, and with -ness forming the abstract noun for the quality of being beaming.|
