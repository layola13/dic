drawbolt:/ˈdrɔːboʊlt/ | n. | 拉杆,栓杆 | A bolt, typically threaded at both ends, used to clamp two parts together by drawing them towards each other. | The drawbolt secured the two halves of the mold firmly. 拉杆将模具的两半牢牢固定住。| The drawbolt on the trailer hitch failed, causing the trailer to detach. 拖车挂钩上的拉杆坏了，导致拖车脱落。 | synonym: tie rod, clamping bolt, tension bolt | antonym: N/A | noun
