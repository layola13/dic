de-integrations:/diːˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənz/ | n. | 分解，瓦解，崩溃 | The process of breaking down or disintegrating something that was previously integrated.  The act of separating something into its component parts, especially in a social or political context. | Example 1: The de-integrations of the Soviet Union led to the emergence of several independent states. 苏联的解体导致了几个独立国家的出现。

Example 2: The social de-integrations caused by the war were devastating. 战争造成的社会崩溃是毁灭性的。 | Near Synonyms: disintegration, breakdown, collapse, fragmentation, separation, division | Antonyms: integration, unification, consolidation, fusion, synthesis | noun
