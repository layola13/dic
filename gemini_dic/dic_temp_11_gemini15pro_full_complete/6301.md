amortisable:/əˈmɔːrtaɪzəbl/ /əˈmɔːrtəzəbl/
adjective
可摊销的
Able to be amortized; (of an asset)  gradually written off the value of (an intangible asset) over a period of time.
The cost of the new equipment is amortisable over five years.  这台新设备的成本可以在五年内摊销。
Synonyms: depreciable, deductible, write-off-able
Antonyms: non-amortisable, non-depreciable
