caving:/ˈkeɪvɪŋ/| n.|洞穴探险；洞穴探险运动|The exploration or sport of exploring caves. |例句：They went caving in the mountains last weekend.  他们上周末去山里探洞了。

例句：Caving can be a dangerous activity if not done properly. 如果操作不当，洞穴探险可能是一项危险的活动。|近义词：spelunking, potholing|反义词:|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "cave," which means to collapse or form a cave.  Ultimately from the Latin "cavus," meaning hollow.
