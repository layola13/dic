dugouts:/ˈdʌɡaʊts/
noun
防空洞，掩体；（棒球场上的）球员休息区
A shelter dug in the ground, especially one used by troops; a sheltered bench for baseball players.
The soldiers huddled in the dugouts, seeking protection from the shelling. 士兵们挤在防空洞里，寻求躲避炮击的保护。
The baseball team retreated to their dugout between innings. 棒球队在局间退回到他们的休息区。
synonyms: shelter, trench, bunker, dugout (singular), benches
antonyms:  exposed area, open field
词性: 名词

dugout:/ˈdʌɡaʊt/
noun
独木舟
A canoe made from a hollowed-out log.
The fisherman paddled his dugout across the calm lake. 渔夫划着他的独木舟穿过平静的湖面。
They used a dugout to transport goods along the river. 他们用独木舟沿着河流运送货物。
synonyms: canoe, pirogue
antonyms:  ship, yacht
词性: 名词


词源：From "dug out," past participle of "dig out."  来自于“dig out”的过去分词，意思是挖出来的。
