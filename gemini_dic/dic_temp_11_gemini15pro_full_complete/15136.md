bacilluses:/bəˈsɪləsɪz/
noun
杆菌，细菌（bacillus的复数）
Rod-shaped bacteria; plural of bacillus.
Bacilluses are single-celled microorganisms. 杆菌是单细胞微生物。
Many types of bacilluses are found in soil. 许多类型的杆菌存在于土壤中。
Synonyms: bacteria, germs, microbes
Antonyms: viruses (not a perfect antonym, but a different type of microorganism)
