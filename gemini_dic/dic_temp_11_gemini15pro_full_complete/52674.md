diglossia:/daɪˈɡlɒsiə/ /daɪˈɡlɑːsiə/| n.|双语制，双语现象|A situation in which two distinct varieties of a language are used in a community, one regarded as high (or H) variety and the other as low (or L) variety.  H is typically used for formal or official purposes, while L is used for everyday communication.|In many Arabic-speaking countries, diglossia exists with Modern Standard Arabic (MSA) as the H variety and various local dialects as L varieties. 在很多阿拉伯语国家，存在着以现代标准阿拉伯语(MSA)作为高位变体，以各种地方方言作为低位变体的双语现象。

Diglossia is common in German-speaking Switzerland, where Standard German is the H variety and Swiss German is the L variety. 在德语区的瑞士，双语现象很普遍，标准德语是高位变体，瑞士德语是低位变体。|近义词：bilingualism|反义词:|词性:noun
