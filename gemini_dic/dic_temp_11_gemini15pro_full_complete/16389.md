barbiturate: /bɑːrˈbɪtʃərɪt/ /bɑːrˈbɪtʃʊreɪt/ | n. | 巴比妥酸盐 | Any of a class of sedative and sleep-inducing drugs derived from barbituric acid.  They depress the central nervous system and were formerly widely used as hypnotics, sedatives, and anesthetics.  Overdoses can be fatal. |  She was prescribed barbiturates for insomnia. 她被开了巴比妥酸盐治疗失眠。| synonyms: sleeping pill, downer, sedative | antonyms: stimulant | noun

barbiturate: /bɑːrˈbɪtʃərɪt/ /bɑːrˈbɪtʃʊreɪt/ | adj. | 巴比妥的 | Relating to or containing barbiturates. | Barbiturate abuse can be dangerous.  滥用巴比妥类药物很危险。| synonyms: hypnotic, soporific | antonyms: stimulating | adjective
