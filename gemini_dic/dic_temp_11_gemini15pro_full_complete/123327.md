monographists: /məˈnɒɡrəfɪsts/
noun
专著作者；专题论文作者
Writers of monographs, which are detailed written studies of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it.
The monographists meticulously documented the history of the region.  这些专著作者一丝不苟地记录了该地区的历史。
The conference attracted leading monographists in the field of astrophysics.  这次会议吸引了天体物理学领域的顶尖专著作者。
近义词:  scholars, researchers, specialists, writers, authors
反义词:  generalists, amateurs
词性:  noun (plural)
