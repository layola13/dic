lauraceous:/lɔːˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
月桂科的；似月桂的
belonging to the laurel family, Lauraceae, or resembling a laurel
The lauraceous forests of the cloud forest are home to many unique species. 云雾森林中的月桂科森林是许多独特物种的家园。
The tree's lauraceous leaves gave off a spicy aroma. 这棵树的月桂状叶子散发出辛辣的香味。
Synonyms: laurel-like
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider using terms describing dissimilar plant families, like "coniferous" or "deciduous" depending on the context)
词性: adjective
