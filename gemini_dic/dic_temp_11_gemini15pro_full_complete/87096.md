heracleonite:/hɛrəˈkliːəˌnaɪt/
n.|赫拉克利特信徒；赫拉克利特哲学的追随者|A follower of the Greek philosopher Heraclitus, known for his doctrines of universal flux and the unity of opposites.|Heracleonites believed that the world was in constant change.|Synonym: Heraclitean|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a follower of a philosopher with opposing views, such as Parmenides)|noun
