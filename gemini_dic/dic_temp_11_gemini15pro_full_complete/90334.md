housling:/ˈhaʊzlɪŋ/| n.| 圣餐；圣餐礼；（尤指临终前的）圣餐；（天主教的）临终圣事|The act of administering or receiving the Eucharist, especially to a dying person.  It can also refer to the Eucharist itself.|He received housling before he died. 他在去世前领了圣餐。|近义词：Eucharist, communion, sacrament, viaticum|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *houseling, *husseling, *hosling, from Old English *hūsl, *hūsel "Eucharist, sacrifice," from Proto-Germanic *hunslą (“sacrifice”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱwen- (“holy, sacred”). Cognate with Old Frisian hūsel (“sacrifice, Eucharist”), Old Saxon hūsil, hūsli (“sacrifice, Eucharist”), Old High German huosal, huosl (“Eucharist”), Old Norse húsl (“sacrifice, Eucharist”), Gothic 𐌷𐌿𐍃𐌻 (husl, “sacrifice, offering”).
