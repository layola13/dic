cucumiform:/kjuːˈkjuːmɪfɔːrm/
adjective
黄瓜形的；瓜形的
shaped like a cucumber.
The virus has a cucumiform shape. 这种病毒呈黄瓜状。
近义词:cucumber-shaped, oblong, elongated
反义词:round, spherical
词性:adjective
