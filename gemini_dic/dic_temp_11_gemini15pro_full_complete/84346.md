half-section:/ˈhæfˌsɛkʃən/| n.|半段；半截；(尤指土地的)一半|A half-section is one half of a section, typically referring to a parcel of land.|The farmer sold a half-section of his land last year. 去年这位农民卖掉了他的半块地。|synonyms: half, moiety|antonyms: whole, entirety|noun

half-section:/ˈhæfˌsɛkʃən/| n.|半份；半部分|A half-section can also refer to half of something that is normally considered a whole.|She ate a half-section of the grapefruit. 她吃了一半柚子。|synonyms: half, portion|antonyms: whole, all|noun
