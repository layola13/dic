darg:/dɑːrɡ/
n.
a day's work; a task, especially a difficult or tedious one
一天的工作；一项任务，尤指困难或乏味的任务
He considered mowing the lawn a real darg. 他认为修剪草坪是一项真正的苦差事。
近义词：chore, task, job, assignment
反义词：leisure, pastime, recreation
词性：名词

词源：
From Scots *dark, from Old Norse *døgrk "day's work," from dagr "day".  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dagaz "day".  Related to day.  The same root gives German Tagwerk "day's work".
来自苏格兰语 *dark，源自古诺尔斯语 *døgrk “一天的工作”，来自 dagr “一天”。最终来自原始日耳曼语 *dagaz “一天”。 与 day 相关。相同的词根产生了德语 Tagwerk “一天的工作”。
