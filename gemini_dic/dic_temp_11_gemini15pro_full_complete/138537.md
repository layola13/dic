noxiously:/ˈnɒkʃəsli/ /ˈnɑːkʃəsli/| adv.|有害地；讨厌地；不愉快地|In a way that is harmful, unpleasant, or offensive.|The factory fumes spread noxiously across the town. 工厂的烟雾有害地弥漫在整个城镇。|The rotting meat smelled noxiously. 腐烂的肉发出令人作呕的恶臭。|Synonyms: harmfully, offensively, deleteriously, perniciously, virulently|Antonyms: harmlessly, beneficially, healthily|词性:副词
