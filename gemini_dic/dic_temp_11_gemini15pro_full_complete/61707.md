emote:/ɪˈmoʊt/ (US), /ɪˈməʊt/ (UK)| v. |表达感情；流露感情|To express emotion, especially in an excessive or theatrical way.|She emoted her joy by jumping up and down. 她通过上蹿下跳来表达她的喜悦。|

例句：
He emoted his anger by slamming the door. 他通过摔门来表达他的愤怒。

例句：
The actor emoted beautifully in the final scene. 这位演员在最后一幕中出色地表达了情感。


近义词：express, display, show, convey
反义词：conceal, repress, suppress
词性：verb (动词)
