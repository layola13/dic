expostulate:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊleɪt/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəleɪt/| v. | 劝诫；告诫；抗议；反对 | To reason earnestly with someone against something that person intends to do or has done; remonstrate. |  I expostulated with him about the risk involved, but he wouldn't listen. 我劝诫他要注意其中的风险，但他不听。 |  He expostulated with the referee about the decision. 他向裁判抗议判罚。 | 近义词：remonstrate, protest, object, argue, reason | 反义词：agree, consent, approve | 词性: 动词
