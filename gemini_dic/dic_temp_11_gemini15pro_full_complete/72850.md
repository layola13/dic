folkest:/ˈfoʊkɪst/| adj.|最民间的，最具民族特色的 (Superlative of "folky")|This is the folkest music I've ever heard. 这是我听过的最具民族特色的音乐。|近义词：most traditional, most rustic|反义词: most modern, most contemporary|词性:形容词

folkest is the superlative form of "folky".  "Folky" itself is an adjective derived from the noun "folk." It describes something characteristic of folk music, traditions, or culture.  "Folkest," therefore, means having the most qualities associated with folk culture. While grammatically correct, it's not frequently used in modern English.  "Most folky" is generally preferred.
