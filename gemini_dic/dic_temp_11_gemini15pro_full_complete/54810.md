dispersability:/dɪˌspɜːrsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 分散性，分散能力 | The dispersability of the powder in water is excellent.  This refers to how easily and uniformly a substance can be distributed or spread throughout another medium. | The dispersability of the pigment in the paint affects the final color.  颜料在油漆中的分散性会影响最终的颜色。| synonyms: dispersibility, spreadability, distributability | antonyms: insolubility, immiscibility, agglomeration | noun

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ | v. | 分散，散开 | The crowd dispersed after the concert. This means to scatter or spread out in different directions. | The police dispersed the protesters. 警察驱散了抗议者。| synonyms: scatter, disseminate, distribute | antonyms: gather, collect, assemble | verb
