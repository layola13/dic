noncomprehendingly: /ˌnɒnkɒmprɪˈhɛndɪŋli/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnkɑːmprɪˈhɛndɪŋli/ (US)
adverb
不理解地，茫然地
in a way that shows you do not understand something
She stared at him noncomprehendingly. 她茫然地盯着他。
Synonyms: blankly, vacantly, uncomprehendingly, bewilderedly
Antonyms: understandingly, knowingly, intelligently
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- + comprehending + -ly.  "Comprehend" comes from the Latin "comprehendere" (to grasp, seize, understand), from "com-" (together) + "prehendere" (to grasp).
