lopsidednesses:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪdnəsɪz/ | n. | 不平衡，不对称 |The state of being lopsided; lack of balance or symmetry.  A collection or a number of instances of lopsidedness.  |  The lopsidednesses in the election results were shocking. 选举结果的不平衡令人震惊。 The painting's lopsidednesses were intentional, adding to its quirky charm.  这幅画的不对称是故意的，增加了它古怪的魅力。 | 近义词: imbalances, asymmetries, inequalities | 反义词: balances, symmetries, equalities | 词性: 名词

词源:  From lopsided + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality)  最终来源于 lop (倾斜) + sided (有边的).
