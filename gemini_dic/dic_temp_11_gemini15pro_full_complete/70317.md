fiefdoms:/ˈfiːfdəmz/
n.
封地；领地；势力范围
Areas controlled by a particular person or group, often seen as their exclusive domain.
The company is divided into several fiefdoms, each with its own manager.  这家公司被划分为几个独立的部门，每个部门都有自己的经理。
The internet has broken down the traditional fiefdoms of the media. 互联网打破了传统媒体的势力范围。
domains, territories, bailiwicks, spheres of influence, provinces
