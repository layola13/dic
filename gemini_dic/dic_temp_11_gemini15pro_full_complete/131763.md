nonconjugalities:/nɒnkɒndʒʊˈɡælɪtiz/ | n. |非共轭性；非共轭关系|The absence of a conjugacy relationship between mathematical objects, often referring to transformations or groups. This implies that they cannot be transformed into each other by a specific type of operation.| The nonconjugalities of these two subgroups complicate the analysis of the group structure. 这两个子群的非共轭性使群结构的分析变得复杂。|Near synonyms: dissimilarity, difference, non-equivalence | Antonyms: conjugacy, similarity, equivalence | noun
