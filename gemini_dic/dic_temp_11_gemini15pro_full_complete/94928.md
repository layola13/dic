in-manner:/ɪnˈmænər/ | adv. | 以...的方式 | in a particular way or style | She spoke in a calm and measured manner. 她以平静而有分寸的方式说话。 | Examples: He greeted me in a friendly manner. 他以友好的方式问候我.  She dressed in a rather flamboyant manner. 她穿着相当艳丽。 | Synonyms: method, way, fashion, style, mode | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for manner itself, but antonyms could apply to specific manners, e.g., rudely vs. politely) | adverb
