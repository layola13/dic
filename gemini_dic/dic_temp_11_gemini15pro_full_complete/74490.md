frankly:/ˈfræŋkli/| adv.|坦白地，坦率地|In a frank and honest manner; openly and truthfully.  It means "to be honest," and is used to introduce an honest, and sometimes blunt, statement.|Frankly, I don't think you're qualified for this job. 坦白地说，我认为你不适合这份工作。|Frankly, I'm tired of all the drama. 坦率地说，我厌倦了所有的戏剧性事件。|Synonyms: honestly, truthfully, candidly, bluntly, openly|Antonyms: evasively, indirectly, deceptively|adverb
