hoopla:/ˈhuːplɑː/| n.|喧闹，热闹，炒作|A great deal of fuss or excitement.  |例句：There was a lot of hoopla surrounding the opening of the new stadium. 新体育场的开幕式周围有很多喧闹。

例句：They generated a lot of hoopla for their new product launch. 他们为他们的新产品发布制造了很多宣传。|近义词：fuss, commotion, excitement, buzz, fanfare, hype|反义词：calm, quiet, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

词源:
Originating in the late 19th century, "hoopla" likely comes from the French exclamation "houp-là," used when tossing objects, particularly in games or performances. This connection suggests the word's initial meaning related to the excitement and commotion surrounding such activities.  起源于 19 世纪后期，“hoopla”可能来自法语感叹词“houp-là”，用于投掷物体时，尤其是在游戏或表演中。这种联系表明该词的最初含义与围绕此类活动的兴奋和骚动有关。
