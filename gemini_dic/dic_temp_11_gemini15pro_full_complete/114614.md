maleficence:/məˈlɛfɪsəns/| n.|恶意；罪恶；坏事|The act of committing harm or evil; the state of being harmful or evil.  It implies a conscious intent to do evil or cause harm.|His acts of maleficence shocked the community. 他的恶意行为震惊了整个社区。|近义词：evil, wickedness, malevolence, malignity|反义词：benevolence, beneficence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *maleficentia, from Latin maleficens (present participle of maleficere “to do evil”), from male (ill) + -ficens (present participle of facere “to do, make”).
