guides:/ɡaɪdz/| n. v.|指导，指南，导游，向导|A person who leads or directs others on a journey, course of action, etc. or something that assists in achieving a specific result.|The museum offers guided tours. 博物馆提供导览服务。|synonyms: leaders, mentors, instructors, manuals, handbooks|antonyms: followers, students|noun

guides:/ɡaɪdz/| v.|指导，引导，操纵，影响|To lead or direct someone or something.|He guided the tourists through the ancient ruins. 他引导游客穿过古代遗址。|synonyms: lead, direct, steer, pilot, influence|antonyms: follow, mislead|verb
