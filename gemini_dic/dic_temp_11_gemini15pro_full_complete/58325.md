dryness:/ˈdraɪnɪs/ | n. | 干燥，干旱 |The dryness of the desert made it difficult to survive. 沙漠的干燥使得生存变得困难。|例句：The dryness of his throat made it hard to swallow. 他的喉咙很干，难以吞咽。|例句：The dryness of the climate led to frequent wildfires.  干燥的气候导致频繁的野火。|近义词：aridness, dehydration, desiccation |反义词：wetness, humidity, moisture |词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *drīnes*, equivalent to dry +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drusiz.
