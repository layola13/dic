evangile:/ɪˈvæn(d)ʒɪl/ /ɪˈvæn(d)ʒəl/| n.|福音，福音书|A gospel or a book containing one.|例句：The evangile of Matthew is the first book of the New Testament. 马太福音是新约的第一卷书。

例句：He preached the evangile to the poor. 他向穷人传福音。|近义词：gospel, good news, glad tidings|反义词:bad news|词性:noun
