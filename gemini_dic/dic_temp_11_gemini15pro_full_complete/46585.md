damages:/ˈdæmɪdʒɪz/
n.
损害赔偿金; 损失; 赔偿金
A sum of money claimed or awarded in compensation for a loss or an injury.
The jury awarded her $5 million in damages. 陪审团判给她500万美元赔偿金。

近义词: compensation, restitution, recompense, indemnity
反义词: repairs, restoration


词性: noun (plural)
词源: From Anglo-French *damages, plural of damage, from Old French damage, from dam.
