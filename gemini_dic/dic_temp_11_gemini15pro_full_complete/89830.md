hornfoot:/ˈhɔːrnfʊt/| n.|角足动物；有蹄类动物|An animal with hooves, especially an ungulate.|Example 1:  Ancient hornfoots roamed these plains. 古代有蹄类动物漫游在这些平原上。

Example 2: The museum displayed fossils of various hornfoots.  博物馆展出了各种有蹄类动物的化石。|近义词：ungulate, hoofed animal|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "soft-footed animal")|词性: noun
