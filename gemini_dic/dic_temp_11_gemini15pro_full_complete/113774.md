macroscopic:/ˌmækrəˈskɒpɪk/ |adj.|宏观的；肉眼可见的|Large enough to be visible with the naked eye; relating to large-scale or overall properties or patterns.|例句：Macroscopic changes in the landscape were observed after the flood. 洪水过后，地貌发生了肉眼可见的变化。

例句：The macroscopic properties of the material, such as its color and texture, were carefully analyzed. 该材料的宏观特性，例如颜色和质地，都经过了仔细的分析。|近义词：large-scale, visible, observable|反义词：microscopic, invisible|词性：adjective
