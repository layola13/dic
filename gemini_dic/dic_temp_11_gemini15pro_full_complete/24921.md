brunches:/ˈbrʌntʃɪz/
noun
早午餐（brunch 的复数形式）
A meal eaten in the late morning that combines breakfast and lunch.
We often have brunches on Sundays. 我们经常在星期天吃早午餐。
近义词:late breakfast, combined breakfast and lunch
反义词:breakfast, lunch
词性:n.

brunch:/brʌntʃ/
noun
早午餐
A meal eaten in the late morning that combines breakfast and lunch.
We had a delicious brunch at the hotel. 我们在酒店吃了一顿美味的早午餐。
近义词:late breakfast, combined breakfast and lunch
反义词:breakfast, lunch
词性:n.


Etymology:  A portmanteau of breakfast and lunch, coined in Britain in the late 19th century.
