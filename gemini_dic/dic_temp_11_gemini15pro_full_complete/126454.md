mytacism: /ˈmaɪtəsɪzəm/ | n. | 口齿不清，m, b不分 | Excessive or incorrect use of the letter M, or the sound of M.  It can also refer to the mispronunciation of the sounds /m/ and /b/, often substituting one for the other. |  His mytacism made it difficult to understand him when he said words like "bomb." 他说话口齿不清，说“bomb”之类的词时很难听懂。

His mytacism was so severe that "mom" sounded like "bob." 他的口齿不清非常严重，“mom”听起来像“bob”。 | 近义词: lisp, slurring, mispronunciation | 反义词: clear articulation, clear speech | 词性: noun
