deterrer:/dɪˈtɜːrər/ (US) /dɪˈtɛrər/ (UK)| n. |威慑力量；制止因素；妨碍物|Something that discourages or prevents someone from doing something.|The high price of the product acted as a deterrent to potential buyers. 该产品的高价格对潜在买家起到了威慑作用。| The presence of police is a deterrent to crime. 警察的存在对犯罪具有威慑作用。| synonyms: discouragement, obstacle, impediment, restraint, curb, check, hindrance | antonyms: encouragement, incentive, inducement | noun
