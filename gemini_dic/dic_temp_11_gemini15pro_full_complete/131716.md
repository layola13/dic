nonconfederates:/ˌnɒnkənˈfɛdərəts/
noun
非同盟者；未加入联盟者
People or groups who are not part of a confederation or alliance.
During the American Civil War, the term could refer to those loyal to the Union.  在美国内战期间，这个词可以指那些忠于联邦的人。
The nonconfederates fought to preserve the Union. 非同盟者为维护联邦而战。
synonyms: non-allies, independents
antonyms: confederates, allies
词性:n.
