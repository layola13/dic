bowing-out:/ˈbaʊɪŋ aʊt/
noun
告别，退场，退出
the act of leaving a job or a position of power, especially after a long time
After 30 years in the company, he made a graceful bowing-out.  在公司工作了30年后，他优雅地告别了。
retirement, resignation, departure, withdrawal
continuation, persistence
