nonpalatable:/nɒnˈpælətəbl/ /nɑːnˈpælətəbl/| adj. |令人不快的，讨厌的；难吃的|Not pleasant to taste or accept.  Something that is difficult to accept or tolerate, often referring to ideas or situations, but also to food or drink.|The medicine had a nonpalatable, bitter taste.  这药有一种令人不快的苦味。|Synonyms: unpalatable, disagreeable, unpleasant, distasteful, objectionable, offensive|Antonyms: palatable, agreeable, pleasant, tasty, delicious|adjective
