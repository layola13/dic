dengues:/ˈdɛŋɡiz/| n.|登革热|A mosquito-borne tropical disease caused by the dengue virus.  Characterized by fever, headache, muscle and joint pains, and a characteristic skin rash.|She contracted dengue fever while traveling in Southeast Asia. 她去东南亚旅行时感染了登革热。|近义词: breakbone fever (非正式)|反义词: None (登革热是一种具体的疾病，没有直接的反义词)|Noun
