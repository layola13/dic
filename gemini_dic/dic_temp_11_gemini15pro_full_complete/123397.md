monopathy: /məˈnɒpəθi/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpəθi/ (US) | n. | 单一病症; 局部病变 | A disease affecting only one part or organ of the body; suffering from only one kind of disease. |  He was diagnosed with a monopathy of the liver.  他被诊断出患有肝脏局部病变。| Synonym: localized disease | Antonym: systemic disease | noun


monopathy: /məˈnɒpəθi/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpəθi/ (US) | n. | 专一情感; 单一情感 |  Concentration of interest or feeling on one particular object or subject. | His monopathy for his work excluded all other interests. 他对工作的专一让他对其他任何事情都失去了兴趣。| Synonym: obsession, fixation | Antonym: diverse interests | noun
