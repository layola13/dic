confronted:/kənˈfrʌntɪd/ | v. | 面对，遭遇，对抗 | To meet face-to-face, especially as a challenge; come to grips with.|  I was confronted with a difficult decision. 我面临着一个艰难的决定。|Synonyms: face, encounter, tackle, address| Antonyms: avoid, evade, dodge |verb

confronted:/kənˈfrʌntɪd/ | adj. | 面临的，遭遇的 |  (Usually used in a passive sense) Faced with or experiencing something, especially something difficult or unpleasant. | He was confronted with evidence of his guilt. 他面临着证明他有罪的证据。| Synonyms: facing, experiencing, undergoing | Antonyms: avoiding, evading | adjective
