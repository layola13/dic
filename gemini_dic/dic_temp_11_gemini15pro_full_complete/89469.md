honeycomb:/ˈhʌnikəʊm/ (UK) /ˈhʌnikom/ (US)| n. adj.|蜂窝，蜂巢; 蜂窝状的|A structure of hexagonal wax cells built by honeybees to store honey and eggs.  Also used to describe things resembling this structure.|The beekeeper harvested honey from the honeycomb. 养蜂人从蜂巢中采集蜂蜜。|The chocolate bar had a honeycomb texture. 这块巧克力有着蜂窝状的纹理。|近义词：beehive, network, lattice|反义词: solid|noun, adjective
