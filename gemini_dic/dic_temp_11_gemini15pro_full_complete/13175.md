at-cost:/æt kɔːst/
| adj.| 按成本的，无利润的 | Selling goods at-cost means selling them for the same price as they cost to produce or acquire, without adding any markup for profit. | The company decided to sell its remaining inventory at-cost to clear space for new products. 公司决定按成本价出售剩余库存，以便为新产品腾出空间。 | The bookstore offers some books at-cost to students. 书店以成本价向学生提供一些书籍。 | 近义词: at prime cost, at cost price | 反义词: at a profit | 词性: 形容词

at-cost:/æt kɔːst/
| adv.| 按成本地，无利润地 | Selling at-cost means selling for the same price as the cost of production or acquisition. | They sold the damaged goods at-cost to avoid a total loss. 他们以成本价出售了受损的商品，以避免全部损失。 | The charity shop sells donated clothes at-cost to those in need.  慈善商店以成本价向需要的人出售捐赠的衣服。| 近义词: at prime cost, at cost price | 反义词: at a profit | 词性: 副词

词源: "at" (表示位置或状态) + "cost" (成本)
