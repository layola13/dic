multiseries:/ˈmʌltiˌsɪəriːz/ |adj.|多系列的，多层的|Consisting of or involving multiple series; relating to or involving several distinct groups or sets arranged in a sequence. |This multiseries study followed participants for over a decade. 这项多系列研究对参与者进行了超过十年的跟踪。|The graph displays the multiseries data from different geographical regions.  该图表显示了来自不同地理区域的多系列数据。|近义词:multi-part, multiple, serial|反义词:single, singular|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"multi-" (prefix): from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much"
"-series": from Latin "series" meaning "a row, chain, sequence"
