impanelment:/ɪmˈpænəlmənt/
n.
登记; 编入陪审团名单; 陪审团的选任;
The act of selecting and swearing in a jury.  The list of jurors so selected.
The impanelment of the jury took several hours. 陪审团的选任花了几个小时。
The judge ordered the impanelment of a new jury. 法官下令组成新的陪审团。
近义词:enrollment, listing, selection
反义词:discharge, dismissal
词性:名词

impanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/
vt.
把…列入陪审团名单; 登记;
To enroll or list in a panel or list of jurors.
The court impaneled a jury for the trial. 法院为审判挑选了陪审团。
Twelve citizens were impaneled to hear the case. 十二名公民被选入陪审团听取案件。
近义词:enlist, enroll
反义词:discharge, dismiss
词性:动词

词源:
From Old French empaneller "to put on a panel," from en- "in" + pannel "panel, piece of cloth, parchment."
