bencher:/ˈbɛntʃər/| n.|长椅上的人，法律专业学生，资深律师|A person who sits on a bench; especially a senior member of the legal profession, or a law student.|The retired bencher offered sage advice to the aspiring lawyer.|近义词:senior counsel, barrister, lawyer, student|反义词:junior, layman|词性:noun

Etymology: From "bench" (Old English *benc, from Proto-Germanic *bankiz), meaning a long seat.
