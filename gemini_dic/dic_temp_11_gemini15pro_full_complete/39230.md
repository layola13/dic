congos:/ˈkɒŋɡəʊz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡoʊz/ (US)| n. |刚果（指刚果共和国或刚果民主共和国）; 庆贺，祝贺（常用复数形式）|A central African republic; a form of congratulations, often used in the plural. |例句：He sent his congos on their wedding. 他向他们表达了结婚的祝贺。

例句：The Congos gained independence in 1960. 刚果在1960年获得独立。|近义词：congratulations, felicitations|反义词: condolences|词性:noun
