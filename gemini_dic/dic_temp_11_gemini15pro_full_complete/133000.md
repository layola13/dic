nonengineering:/ˌnɑːnˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
adjective
非工程的；非工程学的
Not related to or involved in engineering.
This position requires strong analytical skills, but is a nonengineering role.  这个职位需要强大的分析能力，但不是工程职位。
The university also offers various nonengineering degrees.  这所大学也提供各种非工程学位。
non-technical, non-scientific
