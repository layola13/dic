dreamily:/ˈdriːmɪli/| adv.|梦幻地，恍惚地，若有所思地|In a dreamy way; as if lost in thought or reverie.|She gazed dreamily out the window. 她梦幻般地凝视着窗外。|He smiled dreamily, remembering his childhood. 他梦幻般地笑着，回忆着他的童年。|近义词：absentmindedly, wistfully, pensively|反义词：alertly, realistically, practically|词性: 副词

词源:  From "dreamy" + "-ly". "Dreamy" comes from Old English "drēam" + "-y".
