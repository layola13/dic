fraternities:/frəˈtɜːrnətiz/
n.
兄弟会；大学生联谊会
A chiefly North American social organization for male students at a college or university.
例句：He joined one of the fraternities on campus. 他加入了校园里的一个兄弟会。

例句：Fraternities are known for their social events and parties. 兄弟会以其社交活动和派对而闻名。

近义词：brotherhoods, societies, associations
反义词：sororities
词性：名词
