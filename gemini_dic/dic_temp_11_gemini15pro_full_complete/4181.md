airdroppable:/ˈɛərdɹɒpəbəl/ | adj. | 可空投的 |  Something that is airdroppable can be delivered or supplied by airdrop (dropping from an aircraft, usually by parachute). | The humanitarian aid packages were airdroppable, allowing them to reach remote villages cut off by the floods.  人道主义援助包裹是可空投的，使它们能够到达被洪水切断的偏远村庄。|  The equipment was lightweight and airdroppable, essential for the mission's success.  该设备重量轻且可空投，这对任务的成功至关重要。 | Synonyms: deliverable by air, parachute-deliverable | Antonyms: non-airdroppable, undeliverable by air | adjective
