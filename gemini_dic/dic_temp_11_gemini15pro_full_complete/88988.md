holistically:/həʊˈlɪstɪkli/ /hoʊˈlɪstɪkli/| adv.|整体地；全面地；从整体上|Considering something holistically means considering all its parts and their relations rather than focusing on a single part.|例句：We need to approach this problem holistically. 我们需要全面地处理这个问题。|例句：She treated her patients holistically, considering their physical and emotional well-being. 她全面地治疗她的病人，同时考虑到他们的身心健康。|近义词：comprehensively, thoroughly, entirely|反义词：partially, narrowly, superficially|词性:副词

词源:
From holistic + -ally.  Holistic from Greek ὅλος (hólos, “whole”) + -istic.
