lithophyll: /ˈlɪθəˌfaɪl/| n. |石生植物；岩生植物|A plant that grows on or in rocks.|Example:  Lithophylls are adapted to survive in harsh, rocky environments. 石生植物适应在恶劣的岩石环境中生存。

Example: Many lithophylls can be found growing on cliffs near the ocean. 许多石生植物可以在靠近海洋的悬崖上找到。|近义词: rock plant, rupestral plant|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider aquatic plant or terrestrial plant)|词性: noun
