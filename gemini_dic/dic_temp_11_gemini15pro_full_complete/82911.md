gumlike:/ˈɡʌmlaɪk/
adjective
像口香糖的；胶状的
Resembling gum in texture or consistency; sticky and adhesive.
The substance had a gumlike consistency, making it difficult to remove. 这种物质具有胶状稠度，难以去除。
The alien's skin was gumlike and shimmered in the dim light. 外星人的皮肤像口香糖一样，在昏暗的光线下闪闪发光。
近义词：sticky, adhesive, gummy, resinous, viscous
反义词：dry, brittle, hard, non-adhesive
