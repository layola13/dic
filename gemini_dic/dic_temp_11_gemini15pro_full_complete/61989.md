enacts:/ɪˈnækts/
verb
制定，颁布，扮演
to make a bill or other proposal into law; to perform a role in a play, film, etc.
Congress enacted a new tax law. 国会颁布了一项新的税法。
The actors enacted the play with great skill.  演员们以精湛的技艺演绎了这部戏剧。

synonym:legislate, perform, decree, ordain, stage
antonym:repeal, abolish, revoke, rescind
