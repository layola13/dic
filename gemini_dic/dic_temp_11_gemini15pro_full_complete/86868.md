hemimorphic:/ˌhɛmiˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|半形的，异极的|Developing or occurring on only one side of an organism; developing different forms at opposite ends of an axis, as in certain crystals.|The hemimorphic flowers of the plant attracted the botanist's attention.|近义词：anisotropic, asymmetric|反义词:isotropic, symmetric|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek "hēmi-" (half) + "-morph" (form) + "-ic" (adjectival suffix).
