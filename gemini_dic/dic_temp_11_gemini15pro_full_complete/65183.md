ethnology:/ɛθˈnɑːlədʒi/ /ɛθˈnɑlədʒi/
n.
民族学
The study of the characteristics of different peoples and the differences and relationships between them.
Ethnology helps us understand the diversity of human cultures. 民族学帮助我们理解人类文化的多样性。

Ethnology researchers often conduct fieldwork to study different cultures. 民族学研究人员经常进行实地考察来研究不同的文化。
