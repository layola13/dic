gain:/ɡeɪn/| v. n.|获得，赢得；增加；(钟表)走快|To obtain something desirable; to increase in amount, size, or strength; (of a clock or watch) to show a time ahead of the correct time.|He gained experience by working abroad. 他通过在国外工作获得了经验。

She gained weight after the holidays. 假期过后她体重增加了。

My watch gains five minutes a day. 我的表每天快五分钟。|近义词：obtain, acquire, earn, increase, profit|反义词：lose, forfeit, decrease|词性:动词、名词


pain:/peɪn/| n. v. adj.|疼痛；痛苦；努力；辛苦|A physical sensation of discomfort or suffering; mental or emotional distress; trouble or effort.|She felt a sharp pain in her back. 她感到背部一阵剧痛。

He caused his parents a lot of pain. 他给父母带来了很多痛苦。

It was a pain to get the car fixed.  修车真费劲。|近义词：ache, suffering, distress, agony, discomfort|反义词：pleasure, comfort, relief|词性:名词、动词、形容词 (古语用法，现已少见)
