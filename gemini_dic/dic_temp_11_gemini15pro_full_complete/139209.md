objectivities:/ˌɒbdʒekˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.|客观性；客观事实；目标|The report aims for balance and objectivity.  这份报告力求平衡和客观。|例句：The scientist maintained objectivity throughout the experiment. 科学家在整个实验过程中保持客观性。|近义词：impartiality, neutrality, fairness|反义词：subjectivity, bias, prejudice|词性:名词

objectivity:/ˌɒbdʒekˈtɪvɪti/
n.|客观性；客观事实；目标|Her objectivity is questionable given her personal involvement. 考虑到她的个人参与，她的客观性值得怀疑。|例句：Journalists should strive for objectivity in their reporting. 记者应该在报道中力求客观。|近义词：detachment, disinterest, impartiality|反义词：partiality, subjectivity, bias|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English objectivite, from Late Latin objectivitas ("objectivity"), from Latin objectus ("thrown before, presented," past participle of obicere), from ob- ("toward, against") + iacere ("to throw").  The sense of "lack of bias" developed in the 19th century.
