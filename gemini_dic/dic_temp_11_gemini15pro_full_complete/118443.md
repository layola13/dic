mesas:/ˈmeɪsəz/| n. |台地，平顶山|A broad, flat-topped elevation with one or more clifflike sides, common in the southwestern US.  |例句：The mesas of the Southwest are stunning. 西南部的台地景色令人叹为观止。

|例句：We hiked to the top of the mesa for a panoramic view. 我们徒步登上台地顶部，欣赏全景。|近义词：plateau, butte, tableland |反义词:valley, canyon|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *mesa*, meaning "table," ultimately from Latin *mensa*, also meaning "table."
