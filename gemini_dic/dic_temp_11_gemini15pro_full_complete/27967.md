cantred:/ˈkæntrɛd/| n. | 百户区，郡区 (威尔士)|A cantred is a traditional Welsh land division, similar to a hundred or a wapentake in England. It was historically an area of land notionally containing 100 townships.|The cantred of Mawr was a powerful region in medieval Wales.| 近义词：hundred, wapentake, district| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "city" in a very broad sense as an opposite of a rural division)| 词性: noun
