numismatical:/ˌnjuːmɪzˈmætɪkl/ | adj. |钱币学的；钱币收藏的 | Relating to the study or collection of coins, medals, and paper money. | He inherited his grandfather's numismatic collection, which included rare Roman coins. 他继承了祖父的钱币收藏，其中包括稀有的罗马硬币。 | Example: She has a numismatic interest in ancient Greek coins. 她对古希腊钱币有收藏兴趣。 | Synonyms: numismatic, coin-collecting | Antonyms: None | adjective
