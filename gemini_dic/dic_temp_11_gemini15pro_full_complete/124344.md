motile:/ˈmoʊtaɪl/ (US) /ˈməʊtaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|能动的，运动的|Capable of spontaneous movement.|例句：Motile sperm are essential for fertilization.  能动的精子对受精至关重要。

例句：Amoebas are motile single-celled organisms.  变形虫是能动的单细胞生物。|近义词：mobile, moving, migratory|反义词：immobile, sessile, nonmotile|词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *mōtilis, from Latin mōtus (“moved”), past participle of movēre (“to move”).
