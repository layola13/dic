dolomite:/ˈdɑːləˌmaɪt/ /ˈdɒləˌmaɪt/| n.|白云石，苦灰岩|A common mineral consisting of calcium magnesium carbonate, CaMg(CO3)2, found in extensive beds as a compact limestone or marble.|The Dolomites are a mountain range located in northeastern Italy. 白云石山脉位于意大利东北部。|Synonyms: dolostone, magnesian limestone|Antonyms: N/A|noun

dolomite:/ˈdɑːləˌmaɪt/ /ˈdɒləˌmaɪt/| adj.|白云石的，含白云石的|Made of or containing dolomite.|Dolomitic limestone is used in construction. 白云质灰岩用于建筑。|Synonyms: dolomitic|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
