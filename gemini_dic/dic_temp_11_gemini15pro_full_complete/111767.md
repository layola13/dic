lodger:/ˈlɒdʒə(r)/ (US: /ˈlɑːdʒər/)| n.|房客；寄宿者|A person who pays rent to live in someone's house.|例句：The landlady had three lodgers in her house. 女房东家里有三个房客。

例句：She found a lodger to share the rent. 她找了个房客分担房租。|近义词：tenant, roomer, boarder|反义词:landlord, landlady|词性:noun
