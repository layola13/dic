conceptuality:/kənˌsɛptʃuˈælɪti/ | n. | 概念性；观念性 | The quality of being conceptual; dependence on or relation to concepts or mental representation.  It refers to the extent to which something is based on or expressed through abstract ideas and concepts, rather than concrete facts or experiences. |  The artist explored the conceptuality of time in his latest work.  这位艺术家在他最新的作品中探索了时间的概念性。 | abstractness, idealism | concreteness, practicality | noun
