custom's:/ˈkʌstəmz/| n. |海关；关税|A government agency responsible for collecting duties levied by a country on imports and exports, and for controlling the flow of goods into and out of a country.  The duties or taxes themselves. |例句：I had to go through customs at the airport. 我必须在机场过海关。

例句：The customs on imported cars are very high. 进口汽车的关税很高。|近义词：duty, tariff, excise|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
