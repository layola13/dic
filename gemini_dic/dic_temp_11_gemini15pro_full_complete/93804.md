immersively:/ɪˈmɜːsɪvli/
adverb|沉浸式地，身临其境地|In a way that completely involves someone, making them feel included.|The game is immersively realistic, transporting players to another world.|近义词：deeply, intensely, completely, engagingly|反义词: superficially, distantly, shallowly|词性:副词
Etymology: From immerse + -ive + -ly.  "Immerse" comes from the Latin "immersus," the past participle of "immergere," meaning "to plunge into," from "in-" (in, into) + "mergere" (to dip, plunge).
