nonintrospective:/ˌnɒnɪntrəˈspɛktɪv/
adjective
非内省的，不反省的
Not given to or characterized by introspection; not inclined to examine one's own thoughts or feelings.
He was a nonintrospective man, rarely dwelling on his own emotions. 他是一个不内省的人，很少思考自己的情绪。
She lived a nonintrospective life, focused on external events rather than internal reflection.  她过着一种不内省的生活，专注于外部事件而不是内省。
近义词: unreflective, unanalytical, outward-looking
反义词: introspective, reflective, self-analyzing
词性: adjective
