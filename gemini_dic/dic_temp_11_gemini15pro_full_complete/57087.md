doughnutlike:/ˈdoʊnʌtˌlaɪk/
adj.
像甜甜圈的，环状的
Resembling a doughnut, especially in shape; ring-shaped.
The bacteria formed a doughnutlike structure under the microscope. 细菌在显微镜下形成了一个像甜甜圈一样的结构。
The planet's rings are doughnutlike. 这个星球的光环是环状的。
近义词:ring-shaped, annular, toroidal
反义词:solid, spherical
词性:adjective
