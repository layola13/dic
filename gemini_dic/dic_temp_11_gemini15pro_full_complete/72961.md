foodless:/ˈfuːdlɪs/
adj.
无食物的，缺乏食物的
Lacking food; without food.
The refugees were stranded in a foodless desert. 难民被困在没有食物的沙漠中。
近义词:hungry, starving, famished
反义词:fed, full, nourished
词性:形容词

词源: "food" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  "Food" comes from Old English "fōda", meaning "food, fodder".  "-less" comes from Old English "-lēas".
