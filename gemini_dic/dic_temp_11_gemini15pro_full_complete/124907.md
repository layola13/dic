muddying: /ˈmʌdiɪŋ/ | v. | 使浑浊；使混乱；玷污 | making something muddy, either literally (like water) or figuratively (like a reputation) | The children were muddying the water by throwing rocks into the stream. 孩子们往小溪里扔石头，把水搅浑了。 | synonyms: dirtying, soiling, sullying, obscuring, confusing | antonyms: clarifying, cleaning, purifying | verb

muddying: /ˈmʌdiɪŋ/ | adj. |  (rarely used) 变得浑浊; 变得混乱 | becoming muddy | The previously clear stream is now muddying after the storm.  先前清澈的小溪在暴风雨后变得浑浊了。 | synonyms: clouding, darkening, confusing | antonyms: clearing, brightening | adjective
