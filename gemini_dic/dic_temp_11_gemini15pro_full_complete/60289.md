eggier:/ˈeɡ.i.ər/
adjective
更像鸡蛋的；更腥的
more resembling an egg; more eggy.
The custard was much eggier than I expected.  这蛋奶沙司比我预想的要腥很多。
The yolk of this duck egg is eggier than a chicken egg. 这个鸭蛋的蛋黄比鸡蛋的蛋黄更腥。
近义词:eggy
反义词:bland

词性:adjective
