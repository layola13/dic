major-leaguers:/ˈmeɪdʒər ˈliːɡərz/| n. |大联盟球员|Players in a major professional sports league, typically Major League Baseball (MLB).  Refers to athletes competing at the highest level of their sport.|例句：The major-leaguers showed their skills on the field. 大联盟球员在场上展现了他们的技术。

例句：He dreams of becoming one of the major-leaguers. 他梦想成为一名大联盟球员。|近义词：professional athletes, top athletes|反义词：minor-leaguers, amateur athletes|词性:Noun (plural)
