organistic:/ɔːrˈɡænɪstɪk/
adjective
有机体的；有机的；器官的；组织的
relating to or characterized by the use of, or the belief in, organic principles of form and design.  Also relating to living organisms.
例句：The architect favored an organistic approach, letting the building's form grow naturally from the site.  这位建筑师喜欢有机的方法，让建筑的形状从场地自然生长。

例句：Organistic farming methods avoid the use of synthetic pesticides and fertilizers. 有机耕作方法避免使用合成杀虫剂和肥料。
近义词：organic, biological, living
反义词：inorganic, synthetic, non-living
词性: adjective


词源: From "organic" + "-istic"  "Organic" comes from Late Latin *organicus, from Latin organum "instrument, tool; bodily organ," from Greek organon "instrument, tool, organ," literally "that with which one works."
