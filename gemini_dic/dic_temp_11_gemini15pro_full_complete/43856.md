crescendoing:/krɪˈʃendoʊɪŋ/
verb
逐渐增强,渐强
gradually increasing in loudness or intensity
The music crescendoed to a dramatic climax.  音乐逐渐增强到一个戏剧性的高潮。
近义词:intensifying, escalating, mounting
反义词:decreasing, diminishing, subsiding
词性:v.

crescendo:/krɪˈʃendoʊ/
noun
渐强, 高潮
a gradual increase in loudness, or the highest point reached in a gradual increase of anything.
The applause reached a crescendo.  掌声达到了高潮。
近义词:climax, peak, culmination
反义词: diminuendo, decline, bathos
词性:n.

Etymology: From Italian crescendo, present participle of crescere "to grow," from Latin crescere.
