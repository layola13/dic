gaddingly:/ˈɡædɪŋli/| adv.|游荡地，闲逛地|In a manner characterized by frequent, frivolous outings or excursions; restlessly and aimlessly.|She spent her gap year gaddingly about Europe.|近义词：restlessly, aimlessly, frivolously|反义词: purposefully, diligently|词性:副词
Etymology: From the verb "gad" (to roam about idly) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The verb "gad" comes from Middle English "gadden," probably of Scandinavian origin.
