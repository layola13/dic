cardinalatial: /kɑːrdɪˈnælɪtiəl/
adjective
基数的；与基数有关的
relating to or denoting a cardinal number (a number denoting quantity rather than order).
The cardinalatial property of a set refers to its size, or the number of elements it contains.  集合的基数性质指的是它的大小，或者它包含的元素的数量。
Cantor explored the cardinalatial aspects of infinite sets. 康托尔探索了无限集的基数方面。
近义词: numerical, quantitative
反义词: ordinal
词性: adjective
