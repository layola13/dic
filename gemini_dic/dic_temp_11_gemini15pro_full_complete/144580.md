outwatched:/ˌaʊtˈwɒtʃt/ | v. | 比...看得更多，比...看得更久 |  To have watched something for a longer period or more frequently than someone else. | I outwatched my brother during the movie marathon – he fell asleep after the third film. 我在电影马拉松中比我弟弟看得更久——他在第三部电影后就睡着了。| synonyms: outviewed, surpassed (in viewing) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "outwatched by") | verb
