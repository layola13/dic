boutiquey: /buːˈtiːki/ | adj. |精品店的，有精品店风格的，小而精致的 |Resembling or characteristic of a boutique; stylish in a way reminiscent of a small, fashionable shop. |The hotel had a boutiquey feel with its chic decor and personalized service.  这家酒店有一种精品店的感觉，装饰别致，服务个性化。|例句：The cafe had a very boutiquey atmosphere. 这家咖啡馆有一种精品店般的氛围。|近义词：chic, stylish, trendy, fashionable|反义词：mass-produced, generic, commonplace|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From boutique + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  Boutique itself comes from the French word "boutique," meaning "shop."
