incompatibles:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəblz/
| n. |不相容的人或事物|People or things that are incompatible cannot exist or be used together successfully.|These two blood types are incompatibles and cannot be mixed.|近义词：conflicts, clashes, antagonists, inconsistencies|反义词：compatibles, harmonies, consistencies|词性:名词(复数)
incompatible:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbl/
| adj. |不相容的，不兼容的，合不来的|Not able to exist or work together successfully.|This software is incompatible with my operating system.|近义词：conflicting, clashing, antagonistic, inconsistent|反义词：compatible, harmonious, consistent|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incompatibilis, from in- "not" + compatibilis "compatible," from com- "together" + pati "to suffer, endure."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *peH2- "to try, risk, endure."
