backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| n.|强烈反对，反冲，反作用|A strong negative reaction by a large number of people, especially to a social or political change.|The government is facing a backlash over its new tax proposals. 政府正面临着对其新税收提案的强烈反对。|synonyms: reaction, resistance, opposition|antonyms: support, approval|noun

backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| n.|机械齿轮等的齿隙，空隙|Play between adjacent parts of a mechanism.|The backlash in the steering made it difficult to control the car.  方向盘的齿隙使得汽车难以控制。|synonyms: play, slack, looseness|antonyms: tightness, rigidity|noun
