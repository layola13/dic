garnishable:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃəbl/| adj.|可装饰的|Something that is garnishable can be decorated with a garnish.  It refers to food or drink that can be enhanced or made more visually appealing with an edible decoration.|The soup was garnishable with fresh herbs. 这汤可以用新鲜香草装饰。|近义词：decorable, embellishable|反义词：plain, undecorated|词性:形容词
garnish:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃ/| n. vt.|装饰物，装饰；|A garnish is something that is added to food or drink to improve its appearance or taste. As a verb, it means to add such a decoration.  It comes from Old French "garnir" meaning "to furnish" or "to equip."|The chef garnished the dish with a sprig of parsley. 厨师用一小枝欧芹装饰这道菜。|近义词：decoration, embellishment|反义词：--|词性:名词,动词
