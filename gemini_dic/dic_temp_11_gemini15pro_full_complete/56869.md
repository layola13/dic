doryphora:/ˌdɔːrɪˈfɔːrə/ | n. | 马铃薯甲虫 |  A beetle (Leptinotarsa decemlineata) that feeds on potato plants, also known as the Colorado potato beetle. | The doryphora is a major pest for potato farmers. 马铃薯甲虫是马铃薯种植者的主要害虫。|  Colorado potato beetle, potato bug |  None | Noun
