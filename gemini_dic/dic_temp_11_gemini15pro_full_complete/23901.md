breakbone:/ˈbreɪkbəʊn/ (UK) /ˈbreɪkboʊn/ (US)| adj. n.|破骨的；登革热；一种北美灌木|A severe form of dengue fever characterized by severe muscle and joint pain, as if the bones were breaking.  Also, a North American shrub (Ceanothus americanus) with tough, flexible branches.|例句：He was diagnosed with breakbone fever after returning from his trip. 他旅行回来后被诊断出患有登革热。

例句：The breakbone shrub is known for its medicinal properties. 破骨灌木以其药用价值而闻名。|近义词：dengue, break-bone fever (for the illness), New Jersey tea (for the shrub)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the illness or shrub)|adjective, noun
