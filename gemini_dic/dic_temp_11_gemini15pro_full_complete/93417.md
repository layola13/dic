illogicality:/ˌɪˌlɑːdʒɪˈkælɪti/
noun
不合逻辑； illogicality
The illogicality of his argument was apparent to everyone. 他的论证不合逻辑，每个人都显而易见。
His statement was full of illogicality. 他的陈述充满了不合逻辑之处。
irrationality, absurdity, unreasonableness
logic, rationality, reasonableness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From illogical + -ity.  Illogical comes from in- (not) + logical (from Greek logos, meaning "word, reason").
