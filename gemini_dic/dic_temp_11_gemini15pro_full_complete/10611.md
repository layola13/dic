approachability:/əˌproʊtʃəˈbɪləti/| n.|可接近性；平易近人；亲切感|The quality of being easy to meet or deal with; friendliness.|Her approachability makes her a popular teacher.  她的平易近人使她成为受欢迎的老师。|Synonyms: accessibility, friendliness, affability, open-mindedness|Antonyms: aloofness, coldness, unapproachability, inaccessibility|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From approachable + -ity.  Approachable comes from approach (v.) + -able. Approach ultimately derives from the Latin "appropiare," meaning "to come nearer to."
