misdiagnosis:/ˌmɪsdaɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs/
|n.|误诊|An incorrect diagnosis of an illness or other condition.|The patient suffered from a misdiagnosis which delayed his treatment. 病人遭受了误诊，延误了他的治疗。|mislabeling, misidentification|correct diagnosis|noun


Etymology:
"mis-" (Old English) meaning "wrongly, badly" + "diagnosis" (from Greek "dia-" meaning "through, across" + "gnosis" meaning "knowledge")
