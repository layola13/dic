overbrilliantly:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɪliəntli/
adverb|极其出色地，非常辉煌地|In an excessively brilliant manner; with extreme brilliance.|She played the piano overbrilliantly, almost to the point of showing off. 她弹钢琴弹得极其出色，几乎到了炫耀的地步。|The fireworks display was overbrilliantly executed, leaving the audience breathless. 烟花表演执行得非常辉煌，让观众屏息凝神。|近义词：exceptionally, superbly, magnificently, dazzlingly|反义词：poorly, badly, inadequately|词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "brilliant" (from French brillant, present participle of briller "to shine," from Italian brillare, of uncertain origin) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
