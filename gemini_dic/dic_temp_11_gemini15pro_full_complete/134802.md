nonmigrants:/ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrənts/ | /ˌnɑːnˈmaɪɡrənts/ | n. | 非移民；不迁徙者 | People or animals who do not migrate.  Those who stay in one place rather than moving seasonally or for other reasons. |  Many nonmigrants enjoy the stability of living in one place year-round. 许多非移民喜欢常年居住在一个地方的稳定性。

The study compared the health of migrants and nonmigrants. 这项研究比较了移民和非移民的健康状况。| 近义词: residents, settlers, natives | 反义词: migrants, immigrants, nomads | 词性: noun
