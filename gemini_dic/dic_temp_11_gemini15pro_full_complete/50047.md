denouement:/ˌdeɪnuˈmɑːn/ (US), /deɪˈnuːmɒ̃/ (UK)| n. |结局，收场|The final resolution of the plot in a literary work, such as a novel, play, or film.  It is the point at which all loose ends are tied up and the story reaches its conclusion.|The denouement of the film was predictable and rather disappointing. 这部电影的结局在意料之中，而且相当令人失望。|Synonyms: conclusion, resolution, outcome, ending, climax, culmination|Antonyms: beginning, start, introduction, exposition|noun
