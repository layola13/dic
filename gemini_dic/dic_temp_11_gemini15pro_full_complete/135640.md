nonpreciously:/nɒnˈprɛʃəsli/
adverb
非贵重地，不珍贵地
Not preciously; in a way that is not of great value.
She wrapped the gift nonpreciously in newspaper. 她用报纸随便地/不贵重地包了一下礼物。
nonpreciously stored the everyday dishes.  她把日常用的盘子很随意地存放起来。
Synonym: commonly, ordinarily, plainly
Antonym: preciously, expensively
词性: 副词
