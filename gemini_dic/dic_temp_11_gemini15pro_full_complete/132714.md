nondistributions:/ˌnɒndɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənz/ | n. | 非分配; 不分配 |  The act of not distributing something, especially profits or assets.  This often refers to a situation where a company retains earnings instead of paying them out as dividends. | The company's nondistributions of profits led to increased investment in research and development. 公司不分配利润导致研发投入增加。| The nondistributions policy angered some shareholders. 不分配政策激怒了一些股东。 |  nonpayment, retention, withholding | distribution, allocation | noun
