adduction:/əˈdʌkʃən/
n.
内收, 加合
The movement of a limb toward the midline of the body.
The doctor tested the adduction of her hip. 医生检查了她髋关节的内收情况。
近义词: approximation, drawing together
反义词: abduction, protraction
词性: 名词
